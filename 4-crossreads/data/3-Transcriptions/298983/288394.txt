<p>Monday 11th March<br/>Went out to the job at midnight a very quiet night</p>
<p>Tuesday 12th March<br/>Went out at 2 AM until daylight</p>
<p>Wednesday 13th March<br/>Out 12 till 5 foggie &amp; dark</p>
<p>Thursday 14th March<br/>Steady rain all morning Night shift as usual</p>
<p>Friday 15th March<br/>Worked in afternoon for a change getting stuff for job</p>
