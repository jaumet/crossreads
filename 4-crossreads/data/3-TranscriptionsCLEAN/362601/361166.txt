  
 28 February, Friday 
 F. Lovely Day. I go to meeting Place & meet Jack the N.Z. pianist & L/C Nuttell from Dampreis show. Friend of Maud Hughes. We all get scaled & I come back to Mess & talk to Nuttell & go through wards. 
 Recd letter from M.R.L. 
 I dream about all Skeletons. 
 1 March, Saturday 
 F. I am bit better & get washing & go to Dance. See Alice & we have chat about scaling one another I am very lively & play up considerably. 
 Recd letter from Jennie.
