
June, 1915Tuesday 22Court martial today I am Prosecutor.Received letter from Laura & a few cards from the front some are very funny I have some good snap shots - of dead Turks etc - but will not trust them to the tender mercy of a Censor, who no doubt would like them himself .Some quaint Post Cards come back from Dardanelles written on Cardboard or Brown Paper - one from one of my men was just addressedMr Weir.Heliopolis & I got it send it on to you with this to keep for me.
