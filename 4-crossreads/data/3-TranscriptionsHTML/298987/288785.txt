<p>July<br/>training camp &amp; went with Hall to some very good pictures</p>
<p>Friday 19th<br/>Had a bonnie bath &amp; turned out of Isolation Drew Oversee Equipment &amp; was put into C Co for the Draft next Tuesday</p>
<p>Saturday 20th<br/>Fell in with full packs for inspection our Dead Meal Ticket were the only thing inspected to see they were stamped properly. Had another hot bath to make sure all the grease was off. Went to Training Batn &amp; saw quite a good Concert at Greenhills the new YM there</p>
<p>Sunday 21st<br/>Was on Church Parade first time since I don't know when not that I try to miss it but just happen to have been on something different for Sunday mornings<br/>Went for a walk through Longhedge Devial a wee place near here It is pretty country</p>
<p>Monday 22nd<br/>Went through the Platoon in Atack</p>
