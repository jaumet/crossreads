Last night I was on a  different working party" 3 of us had 40 men earthing up one of the avenue's leading to the 4th Battalion, it was a fairly dangerous job, for every now &amp; again a couple of German machine guns would sweep the ground we were working on from end &amp; end, at the first crack of the bullets you should see the boy's duck, &amp; I can tell you I was never far behind we would all lay flat on our stomachs &amp; as quiet as a mouse, you cant help laughing when you look up &amp; see all the silent form's, like a lot of Moslems at prayer.
