Jan. 29th
Leave Jeumont at 9 a.m. with Rupert Brown for London.
First stage of journey in motor-lorry to Charleroi - where we spend afternoon in looking round town before entraining in the Evening.
Meet Sid Alder, Capt. Potts etc.&nbsp; Entrain in Calais bound troop train at 10 p.m. and leave Charleroi at midnight.
