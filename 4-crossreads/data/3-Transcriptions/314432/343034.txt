<p>Statement made in the field of Mont Quentin by Private Mark Lowndes "DCoy" 2nd Battalion to Pte F J Brewer who took same down in Longhand. The following is the original statement</p>
<p><u>Pte M Lowndes states:-</u></p>
<p>On the 29th Aug we left Corbie, our objective being the capture of Mont St Quentin.
