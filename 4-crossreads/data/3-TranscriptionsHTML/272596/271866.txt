<p>The W.A.s have been parading 2 or 3 times today for roll calls &amp; etc. They are going off at Albany now instead of Fremantle</p>
<p>Bill May went &amp; saw the Adjutant this morning &amp; tried to find out definitely what they are going to do with us on arrival in Melbourne   he promised him we would be let get away as far as he was concerned with the rest, but I place very little faith in this for the Port Authorities are in charge then &amp; we will be at their disposal, so I am quite prepared for all eventualities &amp; am posting my letters advising friends of what has happened, I am quite resigned to the inevitable now &amp; the sooner it is over the better</p>
