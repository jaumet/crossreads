 8 July 1918 Monday Arrived at Weymouth at midnight. Got to London about midday &amp; in the afternoon went out to see some friends at Leytonstone. Left London by the 7 P.M. train for Weymouth. 
 1918 July 9 Tuesday Went up for the classification this morning &amp; was again marked C.I. Waiting now for a final Board. Had a look round the town in the evening which appears to be a nice place.
