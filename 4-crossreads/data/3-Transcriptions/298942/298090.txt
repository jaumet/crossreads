<p>On the way I found a piece of shell &amp; put it in my pocket as a souvenir.</p>
<p>Since then he has been shelling round there pretty consistently at intervals, but we have had no more cases so far. I notice a cluster of houses in hollows some distance from here have been set on fire.  Metoren &amp; Bailleul are still burning.  The latter shows as a reflected glow every night &amp; the former burns brightly in full view</p>
