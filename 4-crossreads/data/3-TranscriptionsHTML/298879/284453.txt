<p>October 1915<br/>Sunday 17<br/>Dull. A little rain fell. C. of  E. church service . Very poor sermon although a good text was used: "I have fought the good fight I have kept the faith." Flies are very troublesome still, though the weather is cool.</p>
<p>Monday 18<br/>Windy&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fine service in the YMCA tent by a Methodist preacher. Am Bgde Orderly again.</p>
