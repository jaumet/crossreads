<p>9th April Friday 1915</p>
<p>Been in the hold aft shifting railway sleepers in order to get to some boxes of ammunition.</p>
<p><strike>9</strike> 10 April Saturday 1915</p>
<p>Islands just visible on both sides of us at reveille.  200 rnds. of ammunition served out per man.  Passed isles all day.</p>
<p><strike>10</strike> 11th April Sunday 1915</p>
<p>6 a.m. sailed up between hilly country.  Water about as wide as the Humber.  At 8 a.m. anchored after passing heaps of battleships &amp; transports.  3 or 4 transports arrived just ahead of us.  Cool weather.</p>
