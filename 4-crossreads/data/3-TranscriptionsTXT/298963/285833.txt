 5th The day passed uneventfully.  In evening went along railway line, saw damaged tank &amp; went along another battle front. 
 6th Marched to Byzantin with heavy packs, this the old Posieres battle field.  Here the blasted scene of desolation was very noticeable.  Debris &amp; salvage lay everywhere &ndash; forests were laid bare to the ground.
