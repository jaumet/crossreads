After the row I went back & met a Lady Friend & went round to Her Home for a chat & some music.  Her father is one of the Deacons in the Cathedral & they live on the Cathedral Lawn.  They have a lovely quaint old English Home & are good people.  I've another Home from Home now.  Both girls are over 30 years of age & are only Pals.  These are my only Girl acquaintances & they are all I need.  I can go when I like whether they are at Home or not & therefore I can always have some Home Comforts.
