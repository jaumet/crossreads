<p>[The following paragraph has been added to the diary in or after 1969 by a son or daughter of Arthur Burrowes]<br/>My father, Sgt Arthur J. Burrowes No. 7008 18th Battalion, was wounded in the advance in the early hours of 8-8-1918 and returned to various hospitals in England, before returning home to Sydney NSW in Feb/March 1919. He was in Randwick Military Hospital until the end of 1919, and lived until 1969.</p>
