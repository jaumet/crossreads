 5-3-16 Sun 5th It is reported that we leave here (Bahnasa) next Tuesday or Wed. &amp; are to go to Minia &amp; that English troops are coming here to take our place, nothing out of the ordinary happens here the days &amp; [are] rather warm to be comfortable but the nights are quite chilly, I brought the set of boxing gloves &amp; we make use of them pretty well every evening 
 6-3-16 Mon 6th I heard yesterday that last week an order came to the Regt. asking for applications for Signalling Officers, Nelson was rcommended, also Traill by the Colonel.
