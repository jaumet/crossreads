<p>November 1916<br/>16th   This morning there was a very heavy frost &amp; the water was covered with ice &frac14;" thick<br/>Four of Fritz's planes flew over this morning &amp; dropped two bombs near the railway ammunition dump.  One did no damage but the other fell on a wagon, killing 6 horses &amp; a man, &amp; wounding two of the drivers.<br/>17th   There was another heavy frost this morning &amp; last night was very cold.  The ice on the water is now about &frac12; an inch thick &amp; although the sun has been shining during the day it has been very cold.  I am still on the drain &amp; have started on a culvert under a road near the dump.</p>
