<p>Friday 28th September 1917<br/>Painting wagons.<br/>Lovely moonlight the last few nights and Fritz has taken full advantage of it, for dropping bombs. He has missed our camp so far but we get a lot of shrapnel from the anti-aircraft guns, one piece struck our tent but did not go through.</p>
