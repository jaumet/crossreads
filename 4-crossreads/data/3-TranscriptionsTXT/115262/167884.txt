 Friday 27 April 1917. 
 &quot;The&quot; day - a big one for Seyr &amp; district. &nbsp; 
 The Ceremonial Parade in the reserve near Seyr at 10.15 am. was a &quot;grand sight&quot; &amp; a &quot;success&quot; &nbsp; 
 Bivouac carried out for lunch after the review&nbsp; order&nbsp;march. &nbsp;&nbsp; 
 4000 troops took part. Colonel Semmens took&nbsp; part&nbsp; &nbsp;salute. &nbsp; Aust. Camel,&nbsp; Bgde&nbsp; &nbsp;Fd.&nbsp;Amb. headed the march through Seymour at 1pm. &nbsp; 
 Sports program carried out in Kings Park in afternoon. A.C. B .F.A&nbsp;won relay race &amp;&nbsp;stretcher competition.. &nbsp; 
 Caught the 5 min&nbsp;to 5 pm&nbsp;train &nbsp; from Seyr. to Melbne on leave. &nbsp; 
 Stayed at Toorak&nbsp; &amp; then into&nbsp;city in M morn
