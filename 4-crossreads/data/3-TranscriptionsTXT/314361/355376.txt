
doingSunday Sept. 17th 16Have been in the trenches one four weeks to night. This place is now livlier than ever, last night there was a very heavy bombardment, a little to our right, think Fritz is getting more than what he expected. I could not sleep, so got up & stood outside against the side of the house listening to the bullets, the air was thick with them.We get plenty of good rumours about things are going on the SommeSept 20thYesterday received letter from Lou dated July 30th & one from Mother.It is raining again, we have had a very quite night.Sunday 24th SeptOut in the billet once again Yesterday afternoon I spent in Armentiers had a good look round the place. This town is occasionally shelled, for this reason the gratings over the cellars are blocked up with sandbags. They have a large church there which has been knocked about considerably, churches appear to get the most knocking about.Tuesday 26th 9. 16Still in billet & having
