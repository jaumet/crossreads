<p>5.<br/>little woman, hurts me dreadfully &amp; adds greatly to my grief &amp; infinite regrets. How cruel circumstances have been. Had Mother received my first letters, full of loving thoughts &amp; my regret for the pain I'd caused her to suffer <strike>it</strike> they might have helped her to survive the illness &amp; then my letters &amp; parcels would have followed quickly to interest &amp; comfort her.</p>
<p>Mum struggled bravely but it was the first two newsless dreadful months that crushed her, &amp; within a little week of my first letter. As you say, you knew quite well the great love there was between us, &amp; you will understand how it causes me unutterable grief</p>
