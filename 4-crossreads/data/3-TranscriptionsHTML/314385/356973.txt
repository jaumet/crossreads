<p>The weather is fine &amp; sunny. Took a walk to Contay a village adjoining this as at all others it is full of troops</p>
<p>Tues Aug 29th<br/>On same duties.</p>
<p>Weather broken up with heavy thunder storms recd letters from Australia.</p>
<p>The casualties are still comming in freely some cases very distressing as shell shock &amp; all unrecognisable with mud &amp; clay.</p>
<p>I met some old acquaintances of the "Demothsenes" who were among the wounded they were quite glad to see one.</p>
<p>The Canadians have arrived here &amp; are releiving <strike>us</strike> the 2nd divisions &amp; their A M C are staying here for the night a hardy looking lot &amp; speak French mostly.</p>
<p>Wed Aug 20th<br/>Duties as usual, letter from C (20/4/16)</p>
<p>News to hand that Roumania has entered the war had a cheering effect &amp; we hope will tend to hastend the end. The cheif topic of conversation with is seem to be discussions on the general situation</p>
