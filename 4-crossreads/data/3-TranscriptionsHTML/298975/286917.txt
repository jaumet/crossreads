<p>Wed 4/7/17<br/>Nothing out of the way</p>
<p>Tursday 5/7/17<br/>On Guard. Absolutely calm</p>
<p>Friday 6th<br/>Had a good night. Haveing no lights made very little difference as it is full Moon. Not being able to read was the only draw back.</p>
<p>75 Men have been picked today for a perminunt Guard to look out for Subs &amp; Raiders Boat drill this <strike>en</strike> evening. A big whale showed up just after we fell in. The first excitiment for some time.</p>
<p>Sat 7th<br/>Nothing</p>
