
<p>1918 &nbsp;&nbsp;February</p>
<p>7 THUR &nbsp;Little rain. Fixing up</p>
<p>billet. Went to Au Progr&eacute;s</p>
<p>at night. had a good</p>
<p>time. Rec. letter from Tense.</p>
<p>8 FRI &nbsp;Raining on &amp; off all day.</p>
<p>Home 6.30. Rec. pay 40. Francs</p>
<p>7. passes through for Blighty</p>
<p>Writing at night. Warm.</p>
<p>9 SAT &nbsp;Fine day. Sports in</p>
<p>aft. Very quiet.</p>
