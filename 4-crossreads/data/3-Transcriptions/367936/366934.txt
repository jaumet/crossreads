<p>Wednesday 16 February 1916<br/>Out on verandah all day.  Dr. Nicholl called &amp; was supprised to see me up.  He &amp; Mrs. Nicholl &amp; Joan are going over to Tassy for a holiday.<br/>Mother went to town in afternoon with Hal.</p>
<p>Thursday 17 February 1916<br/>Walked about a bit and then wrote letters.<br/>Roy &amp; Cisla started for England per "S.S. Niagara" to make munitions.<br/>Mother &amp; I went round to Sam's in evening, saw Dora there.</p>
