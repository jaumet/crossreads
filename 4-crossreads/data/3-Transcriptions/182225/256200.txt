<p>FEB. MONDAY 14 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine. On the guns in morning for the second time since being in camp. We were taught about the sights of a gun. Foot Slogging in Park in afternoon. The Casula &amp; Liverpool men came out &amp; started to riot in morning. At 4 oclock they called for volunteers to go - if necessary) to help quel the riot. We were not to have any arms only our fists. I volunteered, but we were not called out at all &nbsp;There was one man shot at the Central station &amp; many others wounded; lots of property was broken in the city.</p>
