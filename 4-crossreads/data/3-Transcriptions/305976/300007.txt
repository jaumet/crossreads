<p>The people seem superior to average Jerusalem people. We are camped among Olive trees and amid good surroundings. The weather is cool and at present we are having a good spell.<br/>Tuesday June 4<br/>This morning we were oiling wells &amp; in afternoon examined Aquaduct which brings supply of water from Solomon's wells to Bethlehem.<br/>Wednesday June 5<br/>visited Bethlehem today. Very much struck with this place will write my impressions later.</p>
