 Preparations for furlough.&nbsp; For first time we have to pay for Railway Tickets.&nbsp; &quot;An injustice or not&quot;- Army masqueraders - and a &quot;camp robbery&quot;.&nbsp; Meet Mrs Wighram of Manor House and give a few items.&nbsp; The village of Sutton and the Cemetery. 
  April 11th  
 Reveille at 5 a.m.&nbsp; Leave camp at 7 a.m. for Warminster
