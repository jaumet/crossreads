<p>1916 March 6<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Trio go down town at 4.10 pm. I go out to Brodies to tea &amp; say Goodbye. Mary gives me pair socks. Walk over to Petersham &amp; go to Dentists. Have two teeth to get filled. Supper at Macs' &amp; home to camp at 11.30</p>
