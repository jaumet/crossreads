<p>into bed last night, but a few of us determined to run the risk of sleeping on top, and as a consequence met our "Waterloo" exactly at midnight. This time it was not the rain merely, or even spray, but a veritable torrent of salt water that nearly drowned us. Out beds were so soaked that we simply grabbed shoes etc &amp; fled leaving blankets &amp; all behind, and sought shelter below. The chap next me was so wet that he had to dry himself with a towel after taking his pyjama-shirt off. But the fun was fine &amp; well worth the wetting, which ran off like water from a duck's back!</p>
