
<p><u>Note</u></p>
<p>The following lines were inspired by the sight of a baby&#39;s shoe lying <sup>forlorn</sup> among the debris &amp; shattered remnants of what was once the prosperous village of &quot;Longueville&quot; abutting the sinister &amp; illomened &quot;Devils Wood&quot; (Delville Wood) on the road to Bapaume.&nbsp; In this particular sector the havoc wrought by modern war has been most complete.&nbsp; One must have seen to&nbsp; realise the awful desolation</p>
