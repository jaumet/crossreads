 The Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers &ndash; August 18th 1916 
 Dear Mr. Raymond &ndash; The bearer Mrs. Hodge is in need of assistance for some necessary dental trouble &amp; is unable to manage the expense without help &ndash; 
 Could you assist her. She will tell you what it is she needs &ndash; 
 Yours faithfully B. Booth. Hon. Sec.
