We then went to the War Office in Whitehall where we saw a Mr Pletts a prominent Marconi man who is attached to the War Office.  After having tea we returned to the Ship.  Weather threatening rain.<br/><u>Thursday 8th July 1915.</u>  Day dismal &amp; threatening.  Cleared up towards nightfall, went for a stroll around the district.  Called in at RC Presby where I was made very welcome by the Priest.  After a while we went over to the Church.  Standing at the door was a soldier &amp; his wife.
