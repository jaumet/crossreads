<p>3rd.  Saw Jack Johnson this morning for the first time since leaving [indecipherable] in April 1915. he has 2 stars up &amp; is in the heavy mortars. we had quite a long yarn for he is a good fellow<br/>Had to attend Orderly Room to give evidence on Stokes case  the C.O. blew Bellchambers &amp; Graham up for nothing at all.<br/>Last night we tried a new preparation for the feet instead of whale oil. they got it from the French who swear by it. First of all they wash their feet in warm water &amp; rub this certain soap on, afterwards well powdering the feet with a whitish looking stuff which is supplied to us<br/>Len took a party of men away today on a Divisional job</p>
