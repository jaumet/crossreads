<p>Well within reach of Machine Guns and Whiz Bangs, we worked silently like so many ghosts, and all that could be heard was the thud of the picks and the deep breathing of the men and now and again the sharp whistle of machine gun bullets as some alert Fritz thought he saw or heard something. And where a flare went up every man stayed as he was like so many tree stumps, as a move not only meant death to him but to his cobbers.</p>
