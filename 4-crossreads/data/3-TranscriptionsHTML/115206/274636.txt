<p>March 1918<br/>Thursday 7</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Spent a fairly quiet day at P., with Smithy. Went along for birds.<br/>Marcolm Mills, drifted to Blighty for a spell and also to obtain his transfer to Dental Corps.<br/>Have not received any letters for some time. Drifted to L'aison with Mr Mason at 6.10 pm.<br/>Fritz tried at raid around Menin Road. 2 Battalions of 31st Prussian Div, went over. <br/>Beautifully clear day. Tower at Wewique brought down by 15" How, also a Bosche plane.</p>
<p>Friday 8</p>
