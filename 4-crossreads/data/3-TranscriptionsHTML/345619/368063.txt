<p>[Reverse of the above postcard]<br/>Mrs A. J. Burrowes (Mum)<br/>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>N.S.W.<br/>Australia.</p>
<p>Dearest &amp; Best<br/>We are just of back to our camp after a real fine time, I have been wanting to write a letter to you here, but there has not been a chance, I hope to fill a long one between now &amp; Tuesday. from your husband.</p>
