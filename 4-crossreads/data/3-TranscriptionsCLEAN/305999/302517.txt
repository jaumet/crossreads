  
 18/6/16 then next morning I was examined by our Bt doctor and he said I had a touch of bronchitis  I had been very bad for the parss week or more 
 18/6/16 I was sent to the second Fld Ambulance and was taken in with above 
 19/6/16 I was sent from above to Balleul to the divisional rest camp  we where well looked after
