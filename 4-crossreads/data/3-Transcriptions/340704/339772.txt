<p>May 1917</p>
<p>Saturday 5<br/>Left Regt near Gamli for bomb school Biltoun. 5 hours ride before reaching train Khan Zunus<br/>Travelling all night in the train</p>
<p>Sunday 6<br/>Arrived Kantara 5.30 am. Had breakfast saw Olsson. Arrived Zeitoun 4 pm.</p>
<p>Mrs Chisholm and Miss Mc Phillamy are doing great work for the boys at Kantara in their refreshment room</p>
