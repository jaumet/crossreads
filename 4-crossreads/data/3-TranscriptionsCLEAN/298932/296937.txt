  
 WIZERNES Friday 27 Sunrise on the western front this morning is beautiful, the sky is one blaze of color's. "Glorious Gully adeiu" Saturday 28 Now camped between between Thiepval & Beaumont-Hamel. Had a swim in the river Ancre at dawn the coldest thing I've ever struck Sunday 29 A.D.C Co's entrained for the north yesterday, B Co staying behind on railway work weather very hot and the boys dead beat Now in the train bound for goodness knows where passed through St Pol Doullens eventually arriving at St Omer and marched to Arques
