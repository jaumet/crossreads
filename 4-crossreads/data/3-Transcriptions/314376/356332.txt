<p>21<br/>tropics through which we were then proceeding. I may say that at this portion of the narrative that the clippers for the hair and also the implements for the Sports were supplied to us, I think, from the funds of the Y.M.C.A. or some similar organisation.</p>
<p>When we reached Colombo we were allowed ashore and marched across to the barracks, about half a mile or so inland. The weather was intensely hot and we were very glad indeed when the march culminated at the Barracks where we spent the rest of the day.</p>
<p>We were given a ticket with which it was possible to buy yourself a glass of beer or soft drink as the case might be.</p>
