
<p>&quot;white Coolies&quot;. These black Jamacian troops are dinkum soldiers, all speak English, are very polite and dignified and are loyal because they think they hate the Huns a degree worse than the British &amp; Portugese. The local nigs are a semi-starved race of bandits &amp; would cut a throat for a 1<sup>d </sup>as cheerfully as they will adopt any named creed for 2&frac12;<sup>d </sup>. They dont like silver money (wise people!) knowing that copper does not pay the counterfeiter The men will undertake to do any work named &amp; prefer that you dont state a figure --- the women then are sent out to do the work while the nig sits back &amp; makes out a bill of costs that would shame a Pitt St shark to death.</p>
