<p>I can see is most thrilling &amp; interesting. I am going to try and get this posted before I leave the ship.<br/>We have had glorious weather and have passed many islands. One remembers St Pauls journeyings in these parts. I am very well. Most of the sisters have had stomach attacks from food eaten at Alexandria and have been in bed and had to be dosed. I have a little snapshot of the house I stayed in in Alexandria &ndash; but I am afraid to post it with this as the letter may be opened.
