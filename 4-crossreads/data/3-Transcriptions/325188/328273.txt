<p>March 30th<br/>After midnight tomorrow (Easter Sunday) anyone of Greek religion or Russian can kiss any pretty girl he meets until mid night on Monday as long as you say <u>"Christ has risen"</u> our soldiers are looking forward to it &ndash; Enclose a [indecipherable] from Rachel's tomb &ndash;</p>
