<p>Dave Williams was up at the mess on Wednesday.  We were told that He had sailed for Australia 2 months ago.  He will get away soon now, I should think.  My second inoculation was very successful from a medical point of view &amp; I had a lovely arm &amp; head on the second day.  But I am quite O.K. again now.</p>
