 1 November 1918 Friday Villa Montrose Heliopolis  Dinner off Hughes  Armistice  with Turks today- Finish Fight-&amp; demobilisation is the great work ahead- I enclose Announcement out of Manchester Guardian sent by Mrs Ish. After all I won't want any more Diary now. Am spending a lot of money but as I hope to go into the Bush again &ndash; it is the only place for a poor man to live.
