 I sleep upstairs &amp; have my trunk on the Balcony, so do not think the  brutes  would dare go upstairs.  Now the damage is done the door is locked every night &amp; the M. Police are told to watch our premises.  I shall try &amp; write a few lines to my friends next week. 
 Sorry I could not write on some of the photos but thought Mr. Censor may get them.
