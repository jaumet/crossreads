<p>Thursday 18<br/>Only one dose of greese today Went over to Training Ball in evening &amp; went to Pictures with Hall. They were good.</p>
<p>Friday 19th<br/>Had a bonnie bath &amp; was turned out of isolation. Equiped for over sea &amp; put into C. Co for next Tuesdays Draft</p>
<p>Saturday 20th<br/>Fell in with full packs for kit inspection It consisted of seeing we all had our disks stamped properly</p>
