
July 1918Monday 1
Vaux sur Somme.1st Bty Posit.Arrived with guns at 2.30 am, after a fairly long march.There seems to be a big concentration of artillery heavy and otherwise all along the front . Rumored a big stunt to come off on the 4th.Weather very warm and fine.Walked to W.L. with despatch and walked home again - 18 kilos altogether. Ken and I are making dugout for two - rotten position.
