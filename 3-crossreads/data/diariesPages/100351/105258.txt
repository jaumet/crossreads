
Frid. 21-7-’16
last May to remainder of Civilians who fled town. During A’noon Anderson & self again in Town. We went thru ruins of a [indecipherable]) Sugar factory also Schneiders Sewing Machine factory & Foundary. Several private dwellings entered two. One evidently Resid Flats, top milliner, Next Chemist & Clergyman Found remains of Laboratory & a library. Had to leave some valuable busts here. Small china jug beautifully made a big temptation. We were all ready & wained at 9.30 p.m. to march to our Enemy. Nothing doing saw Lt. Barker M.G. Cry.
