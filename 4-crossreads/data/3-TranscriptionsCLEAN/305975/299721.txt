  
 Saturday. Oct 12.10.18 am still in bed and taking quinine  I am felling quite sick from it. Sunday. Oct. 13.10.18 am still in bed and feeling anyhow to day. Monday Oct. 14.10.18 am still in bed and feeling quite miserable all day  had a letter from maud to day. Tuesday Oct. 15.10.18 am still in bed and feeling a little bit better to day.
