 He writes such interesting letters, and keeps me well posted with all the happenings in old No 12 O'Connell St &ndash; Must try and drop him a line this mail if possible Mr Trebeck also wrote me from Orange &ndash; 
 Now comes the rotten part of this letter &ndash; Today being the 19th, another week should in the ordinary course of events see me going on leave to visit the Sis. Instead of that I am the unhappy possessor of a badly sprained ankle, and although I am not going to Hospital, the wretched thing will keep me on my back for at least ten days &ndash; Have had it four days
