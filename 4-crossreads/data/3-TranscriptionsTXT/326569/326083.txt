 to Fovant 3.30 p.m. with laundry then went to Tipperary Hut &amp; had some pastry.  At 8.30 went to C.E. Hut with Taylor &amp; had some supper. 
 Monday, 23/7/17 On Lewis Gun all day out on range.  After tea went with Palmer &amp; James to Tipperary Hut &amp; had fruit salad, etc.  Received parcel pastry from Uncle Fred. 
 Tuesday, 24/7/17 Lewis Gun all day &ndash; watching stoppages with live cartridges.  After tea went to Tipperary Hut with Palmer &amp; James for supper, then had game chess.   Wednesday  Put up battalion colours. 
 Wednesday, 25/7/17 Lewis Gun in morning &amp; gun drill afternoon.  Rained before tea.  After tea went to Y.M.C.A.  Wrote to Uncle Fred &amp; Reg. Nancarrow. 
 Thursday, 26/7/17 Firing Lewis Gun all day.  After tea had dental inspection.
