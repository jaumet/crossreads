Altogether he says he is doing well. We had a very jolly evening there last Thursday &amp; Uncle Will &amp; Helen were there on Friday. Uncle Will is wishing he could go, &amp; indeed everyone seems to feel that the Allies will need as many as can go.</p>
<p>We wonder if you hear the news, but I suppose you do.</p>
<p>Each day now the paper has lists of our own killed &amp; wounded &amp; so many homes are sad.</p>
<p>Belgian Day was a great success, over &pound;90000 was collected &amp; the greatest enthusiasm prevailed.</p>
<p>Every one asks about you &amp; send kind messages. Ollie is here today &ndash; he will miss you &amp; Dene too now.</p>
<p>I am at last having my garden done up &amp; when you come home, we will be able to decorate with our own flowers.</p>
