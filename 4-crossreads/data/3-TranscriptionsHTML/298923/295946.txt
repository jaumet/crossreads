<p>7 October 1918 Monday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Well the Parcel arrived &amp; I was so thankful Coal Tar Soap especially good Paper  mail also on the way X I would not bother Mother about trying to get my Diary printed too many already &amp; its for you-  mostly I do it- X<br/>Fever continues men 50% lying around our Medical Cart like wounded after a scrap-  Its one long line of Ambulances to the rear &amp; another line of reinforcements coming up but the latter is never enough   The men have only overcoat &amp; Mosquitoe nets (no blanket) so as to travel light &amp; the nights are cold now.</p>
