<p>449<br/>My time in bed and put on weight every day &ndash; Last Saturday when I weighed I had gained 7&frac34; pounds on the week and was 13 stone 7&frac34; pounds in my pyjamas which means 14 stone when I get my clothes on &ndash;</p>
<p>There hasn't been any news from either the Mater or yourself for the last few weeks. The Australian mail seems to be hung up some where &ndash;By the time this letter reaches you it will be about New Years Day &ndash; may the New Year be a happy one for you. There can't be real happiness till this rotten war is over &ndash; One can't realize this misery I don't think unless actually having ones home in the country where</p>
