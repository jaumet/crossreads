<p>damned upstart of an Officer gave me 3 days CB, &amp; said he had let me down light  Talk about injustice, I felt like slathering him out  First they mess a mans papers up &amp; he misses the boat home" &amp; then they drag him up for practically nothing.  Just drew a quidlet &amp; am now 26/- overdrawn. Drill &amp; miserable sort of a day</p>
