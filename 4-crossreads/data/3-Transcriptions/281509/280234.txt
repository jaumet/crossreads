<p>11 January 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Tony bad with sore leg.  Most horrible hot dusty day.</p>
<p>Lecture in Rec. Tent by Capt. Willis --- Fractures.</p>
<p>Bob &amp; self go down to Petersham to tea.  Trilby Doyle met on train.  After tea go with Macs to Summer Hill  Dr &amp; Mrs Grant etc.  Evening &amp; supper.  Hamper heavy &ndash; into town to catch train for camp 11.15 pm.</p>
