<p>1915<br/>Sep. 20 Mon.<br/>MY BIRTHDAY (now 19)<br/>Feel awfully ancient.<br/>Spent the day swimming, walking &amp; reading.<br/>A batch of about 20 patients arrived &ndash; transferred from Gezira. There are about 500 patients here.<br/>The tucker is very good &ndash; much better than Gezira.</p>
