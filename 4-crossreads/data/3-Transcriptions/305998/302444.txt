<p>&amp; stumble down &amp; often thats the end of them as every strugle to rise sends them deeper.</p>
<p>We have to pull the guns out of Flers &amp; go to Ginchy a better position with a little less mud. The sickness has started amongst the boy's many have gone away sick, it makes more work for those left. I have felt out of sorts, temp 102 &amp; the Dr gave me medicine &amp; duty so I will not go to him again till I die. We shifted our wagon line a little nearer up, so had to leave our dear home with laboar</p>
