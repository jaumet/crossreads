
Monday 25 October 1915 Steaming South in the first class Steam Ship Demosthenes, of the Aberdeen line. 11. A.M. A submarine scare, ship canted by the sudden putting over of the helm. 11-30am. A water spout on the port bow, reaching from the clouds to the water.Rest of the day uneventful.Passed close by & in the distance many islands. Each with forbidding precipitous sides rising  from the sea. Villages. Cultivated areas.Sea Calm.
Tuesday 26 October 1915 Night without incident. Morning none more perfect for atmosphere & Sea. Steaming in S.S. Demosthenes a zig zag course down the Aegean sea.Absence of Seabirds remarked. A few species of different kinds of land birds came on to the ship.
Wednesday 27 October 1915 Morning perfect atmospherically & Marinely. In Alexandria a Harbour at 9-30 a.m. At wharf at 12-45 p.m. Landed on wharf at 1-45 p.m. At Majestic Hotel 2-30 p.m. Reported Head Quarters Mutapha [Mustapha] D.A.A.S. & A.D.M.S. at 5-30 p.m. - Took Jerrom to Variety show at 6 to 7-45 p.m.Dinner Hotel Majestic 8-15 p.m. Saw Lieut. Col. Christian in his bed-room somewhat sick.  Bed. 10 p.m.
