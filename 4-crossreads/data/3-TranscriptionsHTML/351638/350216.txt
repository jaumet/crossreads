To them the sea and the sun bath are as the breath of life. From earliest childhood they have been accustomed to live in the water. The bathing at Sydney where as many as fifty thousand men women and children take<br/>the ater at the same time is world famous. <p>Straight from the trenches this endless processlon of naked warriors covered with sand and dirt never ceases from sunrise to sunset. No sooner is a Colonial released from duty than he makes for the water no matter the snipers and the bursting shrapnel.
