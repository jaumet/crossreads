<p>Saturday May 25th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : nice stroll in morn to Rosendale &amp; afterwards through the market square : worked 2 &ndash; 10 pm :</p>
<p>Sunday May 26th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : on from 6 &ndash; 2 &amp; then went for a stroll : met a couple of Frenchies from the depot so strolled round with them for a few hours : had a good time :</p>
<p>Monday May 27th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : on from 6 &ndash; 2 : Fritz' plane over in afternoon but very high up : stroll after tea :</p>
<p>Tuesday May 28th</p>
<p>Dunkirk :</p>
<p>Wednesday May 29th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : on duty in afternoon &amp; night :</p>
