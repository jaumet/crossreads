  
 On the way out to the Pyramids we used to pass the home of the man with 4 wives.  Several sails on the Nile by moonlight.  One of the first things to strike in Cairo is the smartness of the native police. Also the funerals. Our men buried with Chaplain & firing party in the English Cemetaries in Cairo or Alex.  Several sails on the Nile by moonlight.  Carry on!
