<p>Monday 30th<br/>Drill all day night time went to concert given by 5 Division Concert Party and Band very good<br/>May 1st<br/>Went through ceremonial parade in morning and afternoon expecting Army Corps Commander polished up everything<br/>Wednesday May 2nd<br/>Ceremonial parade and inspection by Brigadier General big General never turned up after parade got inoculated afternoon off.</p>
