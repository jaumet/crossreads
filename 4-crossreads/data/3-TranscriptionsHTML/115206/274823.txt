<p>September 1918.<br/>Tuesday 10</p>
<p>In Reserve. 4 miles from Peronne.<br/>Spent a very quiet day, writing letters, Home and abroard.<br/>Weather very miserable all day. Heavy rain made our gully like a drain pipe.<br/>Having quite a spell and now doing nothing else but sleep and eat all day<br/>Mr Mays censored my letters. Rumors of going out very shortly for a little spell, near Amiens.<br/>Read despatch in the London Times.</p>
