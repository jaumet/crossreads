 Tuesday 26 June 1917. 
 The parades this morning were under Lt Col. Croll. our O.C. (orig 2nd L.H.F.A.) &amp; he complimented us on our marching etc. He said it was best of any Fd Amb. that had come to Egypt. Allotted to diffeent  divizions  sections&nbsp;for active service in the field. I was allotted as Stretcher Bearer. George Cameron &amp; I are now separated in this respect. 
 Issued with plate &amp; pannikin &amp; khaki shorts during afternoon. Up to this date since disembarkation we were compelled to use our mess tin for all meals. 
 Mess orderly for B mess.
