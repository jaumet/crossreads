  
 September 1915 
 Saturday 11 
 Got pay from paymaster in town & visited dentist at Raz - el  his hospital to get that offending molar out. 
 It was rather sore but it has caused me a lot of trouble. 
 Any amount of French people in town. 
 French & Italian soldiers 
 Cafes after French style, & Cafes Chantant or music halls open all days [indecipherable] for payment of stiff prices for drinks.
