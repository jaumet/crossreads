 Monday 2.10.16 Rained hard during the day.  Went to picture show; third attempt; each previous time being shelled away from the place.  Received parcel from home. 
 Tuesday 3.10.16 Still raining.  Have bad cold in head, probably result of trip to cellar a few days back when we were compelled to seek shelter. Still continue on the piquet stunt at Headquarters.
