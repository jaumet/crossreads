<p>Monday 28 August 1916<br/>Nothing much doing.<br/>Put up notice at Canteen offering 10/- reward for camera.<br/>Wrote to Mother (14).</p>
<p>Tuesday 29 August 1916<br/>Taube round and was heavilly bombarded &amp; reported to be brought down at Ogratina.<br/>All Section down Mediterranian for swim.  Got 10 letters and 7 papers.  Letter No. 8 July 17th from home.<br/>Put on horse picquet in evening.</p>
