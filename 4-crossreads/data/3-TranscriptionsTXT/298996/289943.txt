 29-8-15 I am still in 2 Field H. can scarcely log anything Doctor saw me this morning  sick in hosp  says I will be right here tomorrow. 
 30-8-15 Staff on horses shrapnel fired at them by Turks. I left hospital about 12 AM &amp; arrived back with 6th 2 PM our Bat cut to pieces awful affair. I am still stiff backed. Earlier stores issued to men this morning entered up in their pay books. 
 31-8-15 No sleep last night back terrible. We were relieved by Tommies T 6 essex We are going back to the right again. Rations today plentiful mules scarce flour, Lime juice jam Biscuits, Bacon Rice Onions.
