<p>Sunday 29<br/>Church parade. Wet weather</p>
<p>Monday 30<br/>Wet day &amp; cold.</p>
<p>Tuesday 31<br/>Tonight I spent very pleasantly, in a old Pozieres gathering Docker &amp; Barnby &amp; also James &amp; Robby were there &amp; we had a good talk over old times.<br/>I don't know, but in spite of paper talk etc I think peace movements are getting a hold.</p>
<p>August, 1917<br/>Wednesday 1<br/>If this country &amp; the people are realizing the utter absurdity of driving Germany out of Belgium &amp; France. Soon too, think we will compromise with Germany perhaps this year, as an outcome of this peace feeling.<br/>Wattle day at home<br/>Third battle of Ypres began yesterday &amp; has been made out to be a huge success. <br/>What are our casualties in these stunts?</p>
<p>Thursday 2<br/>Change in the weather which up to yesterday was beautiful is now wet cold &amp; windy.<br/>Three very interesting lectures this morning on "Economy in War Time" principally. Also on the eternal topics.</p>
<p>Friday 3<br/>To go by coach with Dock &amp; Barnby to Salisbury tomorrow.</p>
<p>Saturday 4<br/>Salisbury trip cried off on account of bad weather &amp; Barnby not being able to come.</p>
