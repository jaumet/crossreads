<p>Jan 13<br/>3 Destroyers arrive, they say they are to take us back to sydney, everybody is fed up as fever is bad now, &amp; the Hospitals are full.  it is not noticed when you have something else on ones mind, such as our experiences at Herbertshohe, but this continual waiting  &amp; not going gets on ones nerves, I was told to Pack up at once &amp; soon had everything done up &amp; was just getting the last box out to put on the cart when we were told G were not going only the ones who came back from Wilmshaven are to go first</p>
<p>Jan 14<br/>still Packed up we might stand a chance of going yet</p>
<p>Jan 15<br/>Unpack</p>
<p>Jan 16<br/>Discontent amongst troops at being kept here doing nothing as the Tropical Force had arrived &amp; were ready to take over all guards etc &amp; other duties</p>
<p>Jan 17<br/>nothing doing</p>
<p>Jan 18<br/>no got</p>
<p>Jan 19<br/>same, only rumours that different ships are coming, first the Berrima is being fitted out to come &amp; take us back to Sydney</p>
