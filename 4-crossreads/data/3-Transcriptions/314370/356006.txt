<p>April, 1916<br/>Monday 3<br/>We have got a motly crowd in this time.<br/>It took us two hours to sort them into squads<br/>I am given 19 men &amp; a gun from the 13th Bde M.G. Coy.<br/>See Theo Perry down for bombing course.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4<br/>Continue the good work.<br/>Most of my lot are working men &amp; object to so much writing.<br/>We are working on the summer programme<br/>6 am to 8 am<br/>9.30 am to 12.30 pm<br/>4.30 pm to 6.30 pm</p>
