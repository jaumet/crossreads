<p>June 1918<br/>Wednesday 19</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather wet, heavy rain throughout day. Gun came<br/>across in evening, we went for a long stroll and<br/>managed to crib some eggs, quite a very pleasant<br/>supper.<br/>Percy Grayling also visited canteen and purchased syrup<br/>Quaker oats, milk and one or two other necessities.<br/>Weather still raining .<br/>Expect to leave at any time now. Officers of the 42nd<br/>Battery were<br/>across here this morning.</p>
