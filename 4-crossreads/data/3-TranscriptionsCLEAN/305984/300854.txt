
26/12/17until 12 oclock plenty of fun amongst ourselves
27/12/17Snow again today  not nice  it was very cold all day
28/12/17It is hard to walk around the compound because of the frozen snow.  The boys sliding on the ice
29/12/17Nothing to note  the ice still in all water holes
30/12/17The old Wabbler & young on leave the Q Master is in charge. a beauty he is watchmaker would stop until one out paraded three times today hellish cold
31/12/17More left for holland exchange today.
1/1/18Midnight service last night  still cold  Boys throw bucket of water on the ground & have made a fine slide  they were at it all day  80 more men for holland
