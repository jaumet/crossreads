<p>does not succumb to beer &amp; women.  But that is not likely.  None of the Nicholls Family have come to that yet.  The Photos of the two Houses, came to hand O.K.  The "auld hoose" looks quite grand &amp; the fruit trees have grown considerably also the cannas that cover the Rocks.  I am glad that your screws are better.  I hurt a muscle playing Hockey &amp; I have been doing a little training for Anzac Day Sports.  It aches a little to-night.  I shall do no good in the Sports as I've only had one run.  We will go as lookers on.  I am also writing to Mum.</p>
<p>Best Love<br/>From Harry</p>
