 [Letter written on 1912 diary pages.] 10-6-15 
 Dear Mum &amp; Dad, 
 Well I am still well &amp; have a whole hide no holes through it as yet, it seems just to be a chap's luck if he gets hit or  if  not &amp; luck again if it's a bad hit or only a minor hit 
 We have just come out of the trenches after having a week in them we now have another fortnights rest as it is called but the rest consists of labouring road making, trench digging etc, etc, but for all
