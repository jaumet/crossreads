<p>20th June 19[indecipherable]<br/>Tell [indecipherable] that I heard from McKay yesterday that he is all right. I have not seen him yet.<br/>J [indecipherable] B</p>
<p>Dear old Weir<br/>Thanks muchly for your note per Franklyn I have been meaning to write for some time now but I am a poor correspondent and conditions here don't lend themselves to letter writing. I am afraid I cant give you much information except that the regt is holding its own with the [best. H.V.V. is doing very well but is a little fussy over small things. I really don't have much to do with him as I have been more or less detached from the Rgt from the start. [indecipherable] is well for that matter so is every one here except for a few colds. Old Gran was the surprise packet. The night the black b--- attacked us he had the hot corner &amp; he handled his men like a good one. He was up on the back of the parapet, how he did not get hit was a marvel [indecipherable]. Hor is much the same as ever you cant get much out of him.
