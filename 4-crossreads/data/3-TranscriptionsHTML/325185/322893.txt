<p>Le Rouise<br/>Belgiam<br/>15.1.19</p>
<p>Dear Father<br/>Well here goes to write a short note I feel rather ashamed of myself for not writing oftener lately but I cant settle to it some how. Dont know what it is but all appear to be the same They all say they cant set to write now. Well I am just waiting word to leave for England prior to sailing for Aust. It seems funny to be writing to say I am returning. I don't mind saying now I never expected writing from this turn out but have scraped through somehow I had a couple of letters from Bert while he was on leave in England. I would have given a good deal to have been abel to have gone across while he was there but it couldent be worked. I have written him &amp;amp: asked him to try &amp;amp:</p>
