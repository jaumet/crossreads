<p>Sunday Nov 11th</p>
<p>Leicester : met my friends &amp; seen most things worth seeing in the town : went to church at St Marys which is a fine old church : old Roman wall &amp; other sights very interesting : walk to Western Park after dinner &amp; talk after tea : slept at Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Monday Nov 12th</p>
<p>Leicester : caught 1 pm train to London : strolled about &amp; went to the Coliseum at night : excellent programme &amp; Ellen Terry came in for a fine applause : marvellous actress in spite of her age</p>
