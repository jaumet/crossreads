
15 May Wednesday
Yesterday changed leather equipment for web leather & doing a do latrine in morning.
Warned for guard in afternoon, & as the best dressed man gets off, spent the hours after tea in cleaning up.  Letter & diary in afternoon.
Wainwright of my squad, who is a short tempered crotchety, eccentric slightly unhinged chap, untidy & against most in authority caused great amusement by scoring a new pair
