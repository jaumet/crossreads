<p>Jan 18th  Another Ruse of the Germans.  We sent a patrol out into No Man's Land and <br/>the Germans sent up one of our Artillery Rockets, or a clever imitation.  Our Artillery <br/>opened fire killing 1 and wounding 4 of our patrol.</p>
<p>Jan 19th  My Birthday.  We went into the trenches again on  a new section, at Houplines.  <br/>I went up to the front lines and found our new dug-out, then went back to the Subsidiary <br/>Line for our stretchers and</p>
