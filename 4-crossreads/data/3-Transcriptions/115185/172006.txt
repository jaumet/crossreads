<p>12 &ndash; morrow &ndash; who knows I may be sent &ndash; quite soon. But as long as I'm strong and you &amp; Father are well I'll stay where I am, till I'm sent elsewhere.<br/>Much love precious Mother. Thank you Father for you lovely letters. I am looking forward to having the shoes from Callaghan. I got the note from them this mail.<br/>Very much love to Constance &amp; Jim Cousin Lily &amp; Percy and to Miss Hungerford and Mrs Ed &amp; Maggie.<br/>Love to Bill &amp; Edith &amp; family. I long to see them all.<br/>Ever dearest Mother &amp; Father.<br/>Your loving daughter<br/>Betha McMillan<br/>My love to kind Mr Arthur Allen. I'm sure he must go through hell of anxiety about dear Dennis &ndash; such a precious boy.<br/>I do hope he will be spared.<br/>I have sent you a letter from him to me amongst others to read &amp; keep &ndash; Sister Thompson</p>
