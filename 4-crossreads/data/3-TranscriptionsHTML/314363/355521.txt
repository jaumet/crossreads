<p>Feb. 1st 19. SS Takada.<br/>Early this morning we arrived at Freemantle during the morning we anchored outside untill the medical officer gave the ship a thorough overhauling, but the boat was given a free bill.<br/>We pulled in at midday &amp; went ashore, where we were given a fine reception, &amp; plenty to eat, it was very good of them.<br/>Fruit here is surprisingly cheap here after England, Grapes there are 2/6 lb. &amp; only 4d in Freemantle.</p>
<p>Feb. 9th. 19.<br/>We arrived at Adelaide Thursday, &amp; got ashore about 6 pm. We had a great welcome, &amp; had a royal time the two days we were there.<br/>Friday we went ashore early &amp; had breakfast at the Cheer Up Hut, which provided meals free to all visiting soldiers &amp; very fine meals.<br/>During the morning we visited the Art Gallery Museum &amp; Gardens.</p>
