<p>1915<br/>July 15 Thu.<br/>An order received from Headquarters to have the following personnel ready for duty between Alexandria &amp; the Dardanelles &ndash; 6 Officers, 10 Nurse, &amp; 50 N.C.Os. &amp; men. One clerk is to accompany the contingent &amp; I with several others would rather like the idea of going.</p>
