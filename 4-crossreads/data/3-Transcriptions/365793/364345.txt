<p>Aug 31 along the wharf, fishing for their tea, &amp; they seemed to be catching some too.<br/>Lost sight of Table Mtn 5 hrs out (60 miles). &ndash;<br/>Rec'd 2 to 4 oranges each which means finish buksheesh I suppose. Still in sight of land at 10 oclock<br/>Several Hospital cases came aboard this mn'g - chaps who, enroute to England were put off at the Cape with Meninghitis, brain-fever etc.</p>
<p>Sptr 1 Dull &amp; cold. Going fairly well, but believe the coal isn't much chop. Did 302 yesterday &amp; both sea &amp; wind are with us today. Bough a 25</p>
