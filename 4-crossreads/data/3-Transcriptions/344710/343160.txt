<p>18.12.17<br/>Well, we got out safe at Last.  The Bat. came out the next night, and had bad Luck.  A dozen or so killed and as many wounded, poor old C Coy getting most of it.<br/>We are billeted in a big factory and are just getting settled down;  but its great to be out, &amp; we'll all have a bath tomorrow.</p>
