<p>March, 1916<br/>Wednesday 22<br/>Forenoon on the written paper which towels 'em up  The questions set are far more severe than last exam.<br/>Afternoon on Drill &amp; stoppages<br/>Spent evening giving dad a lesson on the M.G. he was an apt pupil</p>
<p>Thursday 23<br/>Exam Drill<br/>My boys do very well, as also on their note Books, all but 3 got 100.<br/>Afternoon on stoppages.</p>
