<p>Monday 4 December 1916<br/>Cleaned up camp and moved off to new wells about 3 miles North.  Regt. now detached from brigade.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5 December 1916<br/>Dick &amp; I rigged up "bivey" with piece of canvas.  Taube over, dropped bombs on 2nd Regt. looked at us but we scattered with horses &amp; it imskied.<br/>On telephone from 6 till 10 p.m.</p>
