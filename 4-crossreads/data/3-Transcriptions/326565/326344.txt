<p>parade 8.45 Am, conducted by Captain Chaplain Cranston.  Played in a scratch football match during the afternoon; rather heavy playing in sand.</p>
<p>Monday 10/4/16<br/>Water fatigue for the day.  Lucky enough to get sufficient water to do my washing.  Issued with our colours.  Developed a few films at night, which turned out fairly successfully.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11/4/16<br/>Went in to railhead again &amp; caught desert train to Ferry Post; crossed canal without pass to Ismailia; had dinner at Hotel &ndash; de &ndash; Voyagers.  Caught</p>
