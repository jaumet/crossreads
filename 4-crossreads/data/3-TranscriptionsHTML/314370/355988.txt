<p>February, 1916<br/>Sunday 27<br/>After breakfast I wrote a letter home but forgot to post it<br/>After Dinner Dad &amp; I &amp; Dad's boy went for a run into Cairo &amp; out to Rod-el- farag the Nile docks.<br/>After Tea we went to the American Cosmograph</p>
<p>Monday 28<br/>Monday morning was spent making the dugout more like a home &amp; less like a dump.<br/>I also received a welcome surprise got a parcel from home sox etc.  God knows how long it has been coming.<br/>Spent evening w/- dad</p>
