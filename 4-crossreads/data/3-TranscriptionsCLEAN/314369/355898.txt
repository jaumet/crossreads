  
 2 October 1915 Saturday 
 Get leave   &  go out to Zeitoun to see the old man. Clean   &  oil my pistol which is getting quite lonely it having never been    being   off my belt for 4 mths Have tea w/- him  & return via Heliopolis 
 1915 October 3 Sunday 18th After Trinity 
 I have a restful morning  & go to church Doctor thinks me fit to go to Alexandria so expect to leave here shortly Very good concert this afternoon
