<p>There you have the Complete Stunter by one who is heartily tired of it.<br/>22nd Sept.<br/>Sand,  Sand, Sand Everlasting shifting sand that gets in  your bed o'nights  &amp;   your food by day; that gives off a dazzling glare from the blazing sun that makes one  pucker up one's face  &amp;  shield the eyes with the hand &ndash; I am so deadly sick of it all.</p>
