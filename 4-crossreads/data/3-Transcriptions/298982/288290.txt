<p>Friday morn 21st</p>
<p>We had a night of Qui Vive &amp; the fire was pretty hot at times also the fighting on our right which was kept up well &amp; the machine gun had a hot time &amp; I hear did great damage We did enjoy our tot of rum in the morn &amp; it is most welcome.
