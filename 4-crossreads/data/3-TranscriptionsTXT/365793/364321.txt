 Aug 10 about 10 oclock, &amp; proceeded to give sundry selections on a Concertina while his  mate  attempted to perform the Highland fling &amp; the Irish Jig.  He  managed the shouting part alright, but his legs got very wobbly &amp; eventually tangled him up amongst the deck chairs &amp; foot rests. Discovered the bath (with the aid of a kindly Chaplain) &amp; had a good hot swim. Soap mafeesh as the water was salt. Shoal of porpoises sporting on the Port Side.
