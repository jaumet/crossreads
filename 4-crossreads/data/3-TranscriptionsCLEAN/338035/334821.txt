 Thursday 14th Route march to Winterbourne & Porton in very cold weather.  In Winterbourne we inspected a Church the windows of which date back to 13th Century.  The Vicar shewed us some manuscripts which were dated in 1579.  Henry was spelt "Hinneary".  Another item read, "It is said that Charles II is King in name & not in fact since his abdication.  Whether this is or is not not so I do not care a button". Saw in a Casualty List last week that Cpl. A.H. Vidler (Fan's husband)  was  died of wounds & I  thereby  therefore sent her a message of sympathy. 
 Friday 15th Another field Day collecting wounded & establishing Dressing Stations.  At night Ernie Wightwood popped in & we chatted over old times.  Received  Letter from Ernie. Saw  hypo  hypnotic display in Y.M.C.A.  Very good.
