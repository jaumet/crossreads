<p>SATURDAY, JANUARY 11, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; cold. Knocking round</p>
<p>with Alan all day. Tommy</p>
<p>Fenwicke gave a party at night</p>
<p>and about a dozen of us</p>
<p>had dinner at the Pic &amp; went</p>
<p>on to &quot;Soldier Boy&quot; after. We</p>
<p>had a box there, &amp; we livened</p>
<p>the show up a bit.</p>
<p>Poss Nivison arrived over</p>
<p>on his way home to Aussie.</p>
