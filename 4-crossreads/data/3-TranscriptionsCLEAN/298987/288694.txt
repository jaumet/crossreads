 Wednesday 29th Very little doing today Rained most of the time. Received a letter from Leo W from France Went for a walk this evening through rustic lanes & Ye olden Village (Fornak) It is all very pretty - but so old Our camp is the only new looking thing I have seen I wonder people have gone to so much trouble in this country if the last few days are a fair sample of the climate. But things seem to grow well & that is the main thing 
 Thursday 30th  A fine day so started work in earnest The methods are better than they were at Liverpool must not say too much untill I have seen more of them One thing the man are all keen to do there list.
