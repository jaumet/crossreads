It was an awe inspiring sight to note all the dark shapes ploughing steadily through the water, so that when the moon was obscured the little fleet could not be observed unless at close range. We kept at an even pace bunched together within easy hailing distance of each other, the naval escort leading &amp; each transport having at the stern an 18 pounder gun &amp; crew which had been fitted up at Capetown.
