 They took a blanket off each man today, leaving us with two. 
 It has been decided to have a book printed of our trip when we get to Melbourne, the Y.M.C.A are undertaking the job,  5/- is being asked for each copy, I put my name down for one & chance what it turns out like 
 Have been passing through lots of flying fish today, I like watching them dodging the boat 
 Covered 266 miles yesterday a vast improvement on the last week 
 We are getting wireless news from Perth now, it has been decided that the W.A get off at Fremantle instead of going on to Melbourne.
