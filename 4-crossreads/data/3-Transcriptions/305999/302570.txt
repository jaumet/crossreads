<p>5 30 Tea<br/>7.30  Consert</p>
<p>Same day put in for leave to London</p>
<p>27/4/17<br/>At 12/17 PM I left Luggershall for London  I arrived at London 2/30 PM  I was not sorry to loose sight of the camps for a while</p>
<p>30/4/17<br/>I returned to camp on the 1 PM train  When I was just ready to leave my friends they receaved news that there son had been wounded</p>
<p>24/5/17<br/>I was given 7 days CB for being absent</p>
