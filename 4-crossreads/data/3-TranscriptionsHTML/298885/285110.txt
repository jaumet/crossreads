<p>Mon Nov 27th &ndash; 16<br/>Today one of the linesmen being ill I went out with 2 others to patrol our lines &amp; a time we did have.</p>
<p>First was the cold wind and sleet, then we had our old friend "MUD".</p>
<p>Well we had to cross the roads in this mud and to do this we had to take in turns in pulling each other out from where he stood.</p>
