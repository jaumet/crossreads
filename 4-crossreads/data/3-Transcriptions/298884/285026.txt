<p>&amp; I managed to get a shilling out of the Captain. He is the best bridge player I have ever seen though &amp; I am inclined to think that he let me off lightly.</p>
<p><u>20th</u>  Sydney heads at 6 A.M. we anchored just inside the heads. Ferries from Manly &amp; Circular Quay are dashing by all the time. The quarantine station is just across at the North Head &amp; looks anything but homely. All the people on the ferries are wearing masks as a protection against the flu.</p>
