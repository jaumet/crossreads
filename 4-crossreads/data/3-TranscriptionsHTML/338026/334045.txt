<p>Rouen</p>
<p>[15 Th]<br/><strike>D.A.H.Q</strike></p>
<p>Arm examined by Major  [Warned?]  for "Blighty" in the afternoon and issued with uniform for the trip over. Fritz again [boming?] Rouen.</p>
<p>[16 Fri]<br/>Left No. 5. Hospital by motor at 12 noon. Entrained &amp; left Rouen about 2-30pm. Arr. Le Hauve 6-30 pm. then taken to the Casino Hospital on the Prom for the night  AIR Raid warning  Had to get into cellars</p>
<p>[17 Sat]<br/><u>LE HAUVE</u></p>
<p>Left the Casino 3-30pm &amp; boarded [the?] <strike>Draughtsman returned from Blighty leave so had to return to S.A.A. Section</strike></p>
<p>Gloucester Castle at 3-45pm  Pulled out at 5-30pm &amp; lay off till 10 pm  Great number of wounded Germans on board. Pipe &amp; tobacco &amp; cigarettes from Shipping Coy.</p>
