We finished up at about 6 oclock in the morning having passed through 70 wounded including the Germans.<br/>The raid I fancy was a success although the left wing was held up by German bombers whose trench had escaped the strafe.  The German trenches were battered to pieces &amp; their losses must have been very heavy.  Our killed are not yet counted up but I expect it will be about 30.<br/>An aeroplane was up &amp; dropped parachute lights indicating the gun positions of the Bosch.<br/>The whole front was like a scene from fairyland with its star shells &amp; red &amp; green lights except for the screech of devilish shells &amp; the roar of artillery.<br/>Went home at 7 in the morning.
