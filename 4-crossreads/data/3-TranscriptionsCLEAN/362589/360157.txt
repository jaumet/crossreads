
The women of England and Scotland are simply splendid & one must take off their hats to them & no mistake. Women train conductors, train & motor drivers, munition workers; women farm labourers; women collecting tickets in the trains & railway stations - I saw one girl last night at midnight at Paddington; she collected my ticket - tired & heavy lidded but quite cheerful. They are even in dungarees cleaning out the railway engines in the workshops. We fight to win with a backing like that. Then I was across the Clyde & without membership details of what one hears & sees of new warships & destroyers galore, new guns; munition factories covering miles & miles of ground;
