
Australian Red CrossBulford22/8/18
Dear Dad,
I'm answering your letters of the same Dates as Mum's.  I was pleased to hear of Dave's arrival & of all the ceremony in connection with the Ring.  I guess I'll have to bring my wife with me for all my old Girls will be married or engaged before I get Home again.
