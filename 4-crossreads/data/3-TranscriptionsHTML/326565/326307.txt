<p>with treatment of fractures  Pte Phil Stone, B Sec, transferred from 8th Field Amb. to No 1 Casualty Clearing Hospital, on account of being medically unfit for field duties.<br/>Piquet duty from 6.30 to 10.30. piquet mounted to prevent gambling parties from assembling in old building within camp bounds.<br/>Censorship rules, revised, &amp; now much more strict than they previously were.</p>
