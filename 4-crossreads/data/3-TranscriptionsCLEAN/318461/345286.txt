  
 Wednesday 30/5/17 Cool & cloudy but fine, Just heard that Viv has been boarded by the Drs & sent home to Ausy. hope its true & good luck to him. Hear that Austria is seeking a separate Peace, hope so. Fenton & Hankinson the new men began yesterday on duty. Pay. 15/5 26.5.0. had a bath & clean change yesterday & what a treat after the vermin & sweat covered shirts & how they did Pong. its a great life. A little rain this afternoon thundery weather. Cant get a late Paper so don't know how the war is going. Going on a 90 mile march in a day or so to Alberville can see a lot not finishing. 
 Thursday 31/5/17 lovely morning cool & nice Breeze. A terrific Barrage put up last night all along the line & another this morning early must have been attack & counter. lots of Parties going away to school Pay. 16/5 def. 26.6.0. Been a lovely day, no letters. 
 Friday. 1st June 1917 A glorious morning, France at her best marred by the Presence of two Taubes cruising overhead. Boys just gone but some on fatigue & some to drill leaving here either tomorrow or Sunday. Pay 17/5 26.7.0 
 Saturday 2nd June 1917 another fine morning warm means Hot day heavy gun fire on our left Pay 18/5. 26.8.0. Been Raining this afternoon now cold enough for a fire Boxing on at 54th lines cricket & football here. 
 Sunday 3 June 1917 A very cold night but a beautiful morning, church Parade, Heavy fighting all night rumours that Tommie's took Quent. Fritzs planes over head this morning. Shelling Bapaume Pay 19/5 26.9.0. no word of leave yet & no letters. Seems rummy to be called Corp now. Had an exciting time exactly at 1/4  to 10 PM.
