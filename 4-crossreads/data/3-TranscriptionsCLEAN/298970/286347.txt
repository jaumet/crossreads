 The Advance 
 " - Proceeding in single file along the hedge side of the country roads, with long intervals between the companies to prevent observation from the air we soon came to the communication trenches - deep saps zigzagging to the front - and we took up our position. "Punctually at 6 p.m. the first wave went over the parapet, being at once subjected to a tremendous cannon fire from the Germans;  high explosives shattering our works and doing much damage.
