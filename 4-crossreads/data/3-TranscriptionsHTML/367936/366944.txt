<p>Tuesday 7 March 1916<br/>Little rain in morning.  We made clothes to cover Drill &amp; chaff cutter out of wheat bags.  Cut 25 bags of chaff.</p>
<p>Wednesday 8 March 1916<br/>Father went to Mudgee.  Mother &amp; <strike>Geoff</strike> I rode round Wonga &amp; School pdks.  Dick &amp; Geoff plowing.  I got Galina in from Castle Rock.</p>
