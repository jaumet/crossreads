<p>29-7-16<br/>died before they reached Romani.  No enemy 'planes over today for a change.</p>
<p>29-7-16<br/>Sat 29th<br/>Left camp at 2 oclock this morning, &amp; only found one of the 2nd Bde patrols instead of two at Qatia, we found the Turks had entrenched on the hills two miles east of Qatia &amp; we could not get to Bir Ayish.  We were kept busy as usual with the helio all day.<br/>One of A Sqd Sigs found Lieut Pearce shot through the heart at Bir-El-Hamisah;  but there were no sign of his three men, these turned up tonight at Hill 70;<br/>The Turks had machine guns laid everywhere today to trap us. &amp; they had some good targets but do not seem to be able to hit mounted men;  Fred</p>
<p>[Correctly, Capt. N. M. Pearce], 6th L.H.Regt]</p>
