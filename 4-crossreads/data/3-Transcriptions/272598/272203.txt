<p>Sunday 1.7.17<br/>Presentation of medals by General Birdwood &amp; Mr Holman [Australian<br/>politician] to men of the 8th Brigade.</p>
<p>Mr Holman reviewed the Brigade after the ceremony.  Few bombs &amp; trench mortars fired shortly after for the special benefit of Billy -  Mr Holman under fire, 20 miles behind the line &ndash;<br/>More reinforcements coming here, chiefly those who were weeded out during the winter including McCabe &amp; Smaile.</p>
<p>Monday 2.7.17<br/>Played A Sec cricket won by 6 wickets.  Brawl among the Sergeants.  Reunion of the Ambs of the Div,  Orgie among the officers at night in the form of a dinner.</p>
