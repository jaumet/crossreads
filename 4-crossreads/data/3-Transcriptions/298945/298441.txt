During the night eight<br/>horses were hit, Joe Dobbins got a piece in the head (slight), &amp; another shell (a dud) landed beside Tom Pollacks dug out again. He &amp; Roberts had shifted.</p>
<p>This morning the transport &amp; bearers are to move further back, &amp; there is a possibility that the A.D.S., also, will be shifted. At the present moment Major Watch is remarking that he thinks this place stands more than a fair chance of being bombed. What a nice comforting reflection.
