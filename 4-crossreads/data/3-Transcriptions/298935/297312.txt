I now wrapped it round my arm and a reinforcement passing by me shortly afterwards tied it tightly and also removed my Webb equipment, a feat I was unable to accomplish with one hand.  The sun seemed a terrible long time in sinking and I half expected to be hit again by some of the bullets that kept falling around.  Several more men were lying near groaning and writhing in their pain.<br/>At last it was dark and I rose to my feet.  I found the loss of blood had made me so weak that after a very few steps I had to sink down and rest.  Once I dropped near a man wounded in the shoulder propping himself up against a dead man.
