<p>Saturday 30 March<br/>Away early via Dundee Tay &amp; Forth Bridges to Edinburgh. <u>Enjoyable</u> trip. Found Will at the Hotel. Very wet. Afternoon in rain to Nelson Monument. Met friends at 6 p.m. Visited Royal. Another good day though weather bad.</p>
