 Thursday 13 June Rifle inspection by Capt Buckland. Satisfactory. 
 With Spr Hewett told off to go to Corps. Field Punishment Compound near [Bourlainville?]. Went there with good ride picked up Dr. Huston &amp; escorted him back to Orderly Room. Very pleasant ride. Xmas book fixed in pay book.
