
February 1917work to do as Genl Chetwode Commander of the Desert Column inspected at 10 am  An enemy aeroplane came over in the morning & dropped a couple of bombs.  Our Anti's covered the face of the sky with white puffs of smoke & the plane soon cleared back.  The Regiment played the NZealanders a drawn game at Rugby.  It was a good match.  Several showers of rain fell during the day.  We also got some mail.  I got 3 or 4 papers only.
Febry 13thUp at 3 am as I had to get several men away who were going on leave.  They will have 2 full days in Port Said.  The regiment parade at 9 oclock, mounted.  They are practicing galloping through barrages of artillery fire.  There was another football match in the afternoon.
