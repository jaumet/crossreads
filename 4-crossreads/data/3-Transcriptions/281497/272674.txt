<p>While the service was being held we could hear our boys practicing with the machine guns, their sharp &amp; snappy bark can be heard for miles &amp; aeroplanes were humming &amp; droning over us, like a well oiled sewing machine while the church bells were mingling their mellow tones with the rest, &amp; over all a great peace &amp; beautiful sun was shining, the padre's sermon was chiefly on the new phase the war has entered upon &amp; the great name &amp; fighting qualities of the Australians &amp; especially our old 1st Division, he urged us to keep it going</p>
