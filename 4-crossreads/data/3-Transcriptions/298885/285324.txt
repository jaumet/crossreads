<p>As we go further we can feel the warm weather more. Even today our 4th day out we find the sun's rays warmer than at any other time since arrival in France. Still we are to have it much hotter as crossing the Line we will have the sun at its hottest and then we shall gradually come into the Winter of Aussie</p>
