<p>August, 1915<br/>Tuesday 24<br/>Had another dream last night &ndash;<br/>Joe will remember Alma &ndash; well the Germans were shelling the Homestead from Trial Shaft &amp; I made the men make the underground Tank into a Bombproof dug out &ndash;<br/>Let old Panic the stallion loose so he would not get burnt &amp; undid all the dogs &ndash;<br/>Funny what brain cells lie hidden all these years &ndash; Poor Germans fancy them wanting DL</p>
