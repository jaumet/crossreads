
Tuesday 22 April 1919Beautiful day today. WAs on stand by but Lawo is doing it cos he is not too well. Am off work. Do me. Met Bill Axtell. He is going today. No trains again. Strike still on. Caught 8.30 pm train home.
Wednesday  23 April 1919Bonny day. Went into town & wandered around. Still no train. Caught the 8.30 train home. Got word of going to Blighty on N.M.E. tomorrow. Do me.
