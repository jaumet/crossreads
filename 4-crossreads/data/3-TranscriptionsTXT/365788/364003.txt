
3I am sending you from London a little Xmas Present with my best love & hope you will like it.I don't think I would send any more books with in parcels for say a couple of months old girl as I am too busy.Saw young Eric Shelly in Boulogne on his way back to join his Battalion after recovering from his wound received at Posieres. He looked very well. Nancy Norris had a letter from Mick Slade from some Camp in England. He seemed quite well when he wrote.
