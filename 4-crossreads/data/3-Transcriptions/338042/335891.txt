<p>June 11th<br/>Early Rise &ndash; Rehearsal &ndash; An afternoon walk to Flesselles where I pay a call on Harold Williams and spend a very pleasant time conversing about the English Tour &amp; "Little" personal items &ndash; Have tea with my "Brother Wag" &amp; return to the Aerodrome for the Show &ndash; We are patronised by Gen. Monash &ndash; Gen. Featherston &ndash; Surgeon Gen Howse &ndash; Major Berry &ndash; After the show I again meet Harold Love who is returning from the Base to his Unit.</p>
