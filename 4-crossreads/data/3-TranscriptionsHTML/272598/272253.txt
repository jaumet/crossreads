<p>Clymas Hotel.  Only Australian in the town.</p>
<p>Wednesday 9.1.18<br/>Very little to see or do in Truro so went on to Falmouth &amp; returned by boat up the River Fal to Truro.  Snow thawing, very wet under foot.</p>
<p>Thursday 10.1.18<br/>Visited Redruth &amp; spent the day at Lady Carbeck's home where my nurse is staying.  Caught 5.12 train back for Bristol arriving Bristol 12 midnight.</p>
<p>Friday 11.1.18<br/>Caught 9.10 Am Midland train from Bristol for Gloucester where I arrived 10.5 Am, arranged accommodation at Bell Hotel for Noakes</p>
