<p>1917 August</p>
<p>15 Wednesday</p>
<p>Guns had ceased thank God. The Adjutant Delouvrier &amp; Dr Petit from French camp came for us at 11 a.m. &amp; took us in ammunition cart to dine. We went walking to a Roumanian Village called &amp; back to evening meal &amp; home at 10.10 p.m. Capt. E. Pujol 2eme Regt. Dn 9eme Compagne y.4 Armie d'orient.</p>
