<p>Sunday 16 December 1917.</p>
<p>Things as usual. Rumors of 24 hrs. armistice on all fronts.</p>
<p>Had all our clothes fumigated. Wrote letters. Everyone in the unit is busy at this. Received 2 letters by mail today.</p>
<p>Moving orders for 20th inst. to Rafa are to hand for our Bgde.</p>
<p>Pay day. I drew 250 P.T.</p>
