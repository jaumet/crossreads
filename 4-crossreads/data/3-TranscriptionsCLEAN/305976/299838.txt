  
 Wednesday Feb 21 Raining like               Woke up in water.  Sham fight on today. Plenty of fun Thursday Feb 22 Final leave through. Left for Sydney 6pm. Had good trip over Friday Feb 23 Arrived Sydney 12 noon Saw Mother, Fred & Glad again. How pleased they were to see me. I really think those who love us feel it more than we do on parting. Spent  quite evening at Home & enjoyed it. Saturday Feb 24 Visited Garrison Hospital in morning surprised to hear poor old Jack Smith dead. Buried today, went to  funo  funeral
