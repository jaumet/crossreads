<p>Tuesday 18th July</p>
<p>Since last date written up I have received an order to get a supply of drugs to last six weeks, as the medical stores are moving.<br/>These arrived last night, but we have instructions from the C.O. to pack up as much as we can spare as, when we get moving orders (probably this week, he says) we shall only have 3 hours to get ready in.<br/>To day a full dress parade &amp; inspection is to be held, preparatory to moving off.<br/>Shall start packing up today.<br/>Treated a man successfully for ptomaine poisoning the other night, &amp; last night had a similar</p>
