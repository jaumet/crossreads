  
 11 January, Saturday 
 F Ellis & I go down to Base & meet Roy. We do nothing until after Tea & then change Breeches & Hat. We all go to Pictures but Ellis does not see it all out. Meet the Q.M. who was once a Manlyite. We have good run back to Graville & meet a very nice W.A.A.C. & nearly get overcarried. We walk up the Hill. 
 12 January, Sunday 
 F Go down Town. Meet Bert Brian & Horniball. We go & have Photos taken & then to YMCA Service. I go to Gare[Railway Station] with them & have a French Roll Sandwich. Come Home & write. Recd no letters. I am very emotional & lonely & tell MRL. Go. I work hard returning Gear. 
 Wrote M.R.L.
