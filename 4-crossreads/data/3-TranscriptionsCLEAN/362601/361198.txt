
3 May Saturday
F & Showery
I take Marie to Ramsay. We go to Picture & shopping. I lose her & get wind up about coming home alone. Arrive home & very tired. I am off my tucker & quiet. All others play cards while I watch puzzle. We go to bed & I get my face painted & tucked in. a lovely good night by -----, while all others look for Venus & the moon.
4 May, Sunday
F.We work at Puzzle especially Marie. I go up to make my bed & M. comes to fix my blind. In afternoon M. sews while I puzzle.
We all sit & try puzzle at night. I on a buffet in front of fire.
Auntie ticks me in this night, elddoc.
