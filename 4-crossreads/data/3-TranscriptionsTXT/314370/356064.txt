 August, 1916 Thursday 3 We sailed shortly after breakfast.  Accompanied by a destroyer. Franconia armed with 6" gum mounted right aft. Told off to life boat stations &amp; have to carry life belts wherever we go. 
 Friday 4 continue to move on a Zig Zag course to Malta which we expect to reach Saturday morning. Boat stations at 10.30 am. spend a couple of hours on the signal bridge.
