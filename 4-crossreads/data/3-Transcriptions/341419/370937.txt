<p>nice, the sun shining &amp; a cool breeze &amp; I had plenty to eat.  Beautiful rolling rich country out there, would be extremely rich if properly cultivated.  In fact a very good snap for some "shrewd head" with plenty of money after the war.</p>
<p>We loaded up with wounded &amp; sick prisoners the next morning &amp; came back here.</p>
<p>I was sorry for the poor devils, some were in a frightful state &amp; an 18 mile ride in a springless G.S. waggon over a bad road is pure murder to a sick man.  We buried five altogether on the road, I suppose which was pretty good all things considered.  I thought more would die myself, about 1&percnt; as we must have taken nearly 500 back.
