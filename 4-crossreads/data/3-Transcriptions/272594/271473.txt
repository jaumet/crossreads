<p>22nd   Beautiful this morning &amp; the sun is shining brightly.  Had 48 hours rations issued to us biscuits &amp; bully form the greater part of it.</p>
<p>Left camp about 10 oclock &amp; after a fair march pulled up at Bazentin Le Petit, a place I have been in before, on the way up we passed fairly close to "Pozieres" what a difference there is now to when I first knew "Pozzy"</p>
