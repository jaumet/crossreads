January   1918
13 SUN  Fine day.
Received letter from Amy
Writing at night
14 MON  Snow during night
Posted letter to Amy & encs.
to Myer's Ltd & Else Parker.
Quiet. Rec. letter & photo from
Gladys Cav. QMS Cable went away.
New QMS arrived.
15 TUES  Raining & blowing
hard. Rec. letters from Maggie
Else P. & Gladys Cav.
Writing at night
16 WED  Blowing hard & raining
Rec. letter & Card from W.T.B. L Berry
& papers Gladys Cav. Posted letters
to Maggie & encs. for Else P.
& Gladys Cavanagh
