
March& 3 of us stayed untill 3 PM  putting in the iron rods to reinforce the concrete a n bit dificult in the dark. A cake & sox from Aunt Mima. Much apreciated but am getting a bit overloaded with sox
Monday 11thWent out at midnight a very quiet night
Tuesday 12thWorking from 2 AM untill daylight
Wednesday 13thWorking midnight untill 5 AM Foggie & dark There is a Comfort Fund depot close to us & we can slip over at any time up to midnight & get a hot drink. Some of the chaps went one night lately & as there was no one in charge they pinched several tins of cocoa & milk & some chocolate. It was hardely a fair deal but as I have been drinking it am as much in it as anyone
Thursday 14thSteady rain all morning Night shift as usual
