<p>Tuesday 26th<br/>Fell in morning muster parade dismissed straight away visited villages of PPende and Drancourt and St Valery. Wednesday 27-<br/>Visited Drancourt again had dinner then returned to St. Valery<br/>Thursday 28th<br/>After parade went across to village of Le <strike>Crostnay</strike> Crotnay poured and had to remain there till tide went out again went to Cinema returned to camp one o'clock in morning</p>
