
<p>MONDAY, AUGUST 25, 1919.</p>
<p>Showery most of the day &nbsp;had 29</p>
<p>points altogether. Started shearing</p>
<p>at breakfast time &amp; had a good run</p>
<p>all day. Only four men turned</p>
<p>up but all are pretty good. Got</p>
<p>through 368 for the day.</p>
<p>Tickle lopped in<strike>&nbsp;point</strike>&nbsp;4D</p>
<p>&amp; was round Cow paddock after.</p>
<p>put shorn sheep in Middle paddock</p>
