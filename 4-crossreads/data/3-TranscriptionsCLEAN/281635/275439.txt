Temperature much milder & all snow thawed away making place very sloppy. Met Bob Clarke who came down from No 59 2 days ago. Had supper with him.11/1/18 Saw French airship. Milder temperature. Had couple route marches. Had supper with Bob Clarke.12/1/18 Read letter from Mr Randall. Raining all morning. After dinner went down to Boulogne for 4 or 5 hours & had tea down there - steak, chips & coffee costing 2fr 21/2.
