<p>August 1917<br/>Saturday 18th<br/>Fritz planes in evidence this morning one brought down in flames also one this evening.  There was another successfull advance by the English &amp; French in Ypres sector this morning   I am still having a holiday.<br/>Sunday 19th<br/>Another batch of C men left this morning en route for N.Z.   Fritz still carrying on with the bombing he got 8 horses &amp; 30 men this morning early.    Monday 20th<br/>Got paid today &amp; got word that we are going back any time two Hun airmen brought in   they were driven down in our lines<br/>Tuesday 21st<br/>Planes active through the night &amp; early morning another brought down</p>
