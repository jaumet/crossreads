<p>8 December 1916 Fri<br/>Raining some to-day. Very cloudy skies of late no aeroplanes. Poker again lost 12 francs.</p>
<p>9 December 1916 Sat<br/>Took up our quarters in the newly made huts. Played poker till lights out. Dick Powter placed under arrest for having light by Sgt Smith. Poker school won 20.</p>
