<p>We saw no enemy in the locality, we got home for tea &amp; I went on picquet. We had a few presents given out, that had been sent over for Xmas, a pudding for a section &amp; 2 ozs. of tobacco. the pudding went down well but it was only a taste &amp; it really upsets the palate for beef &amp; biscuits.</p>
<p>Jan 2/Sunday Went down to church service in the Yeomanys lines &amp; after wrote a few letters.</p>
<p>Jan 4/Raining very hard all day, eight of us went out as mounted outpost got home at 7 pm, We are duty Regt:</p>
<p>5/Up at 4.30 am, mounted Hdqts orderly, still raining</p>
