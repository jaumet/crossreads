<p>Friday Aug. 31.8.17<br/>turned out 5 A.M. saddled up and returned to camp 7 A.M. fed up off saddled had breakfast rested till 12.30 had dinner.  very hot day.  wrote to alma and win rested till 5 P.M. fed up had tea and turned in.  wrote to maud.</p>
