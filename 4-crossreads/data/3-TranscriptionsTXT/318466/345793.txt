 Sunday 19 May 1918 Major Lucas &amp; Anslow, Auntie Lu, Aunty Ema &amp; Rose, Ede &amp; Dorothy came to aid. Air Raid 11 p.m. 
 Monday 20 May 1918 Reached Hospital at 3 p.m.  The Air Raid last night caused quite a stir here.  No damage done though.
