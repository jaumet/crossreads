<p>W.  &amp;  commenced to drive the now repulsed 'enemy in a southerly direction. We caught up the regiments at about 8 oclock  &amp; had a sleep until 3 am  &amp; then started again in pursuit. The desert to the W  &amp; South of the camp was strewn with our shrapnel shell  &amp; cases  &amp;  clothing, boots, ammunition  &amp;  camels, abandoned by the Turks. About 2600 turks were captured including a German M.G.S.  &amp;   FA. complete. At about 10.30 we met the 3rd Brigade LH. advancing from Duweidar.<br/><strike>at</strike>  Everywhere the eye could see over the desert there were mounted</p>
