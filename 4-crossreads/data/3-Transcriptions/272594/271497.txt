<p>30th  Hot &amp; sultry today &amp; feels like rain.</p>
<p>Today we have been doing Coy. drill &amp;etc. &amp; yet in the morning &amp; evening we have to "stand to" it does seem ridiculous we are easily 5 miles behind the firing line.  Got a bit of a shock the Capt told me that I would be going to England in a few days, but I am not</p>
