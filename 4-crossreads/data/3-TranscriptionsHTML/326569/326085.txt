<p>Wednesday, 1/8/17<br/>All day stunt between Wyley [Wylye] &amp; Barford.  Commenced raining 1 p.m. &amp; rained all day.  Left 3 p.m. &amp; marching via Barford &amp; Hurdcott arrived back at camp 4.30 p.m.</p>
<p>Thursday, 2/8/17<br/>Rained all night.  Lewis Gun all morning &amp; afternoon.  After tea went to Tipperary Hut with Palmer for supper.  Still looking like rain.</p>
<p>Friday, 3/8/17<br/>Parade ground all morning.  Had early dinner &amp; went through gas chamber at Hurdcott.  At 6 p.m. went on as battalion runner.</p>
<p>Saturday 4/8/17<br/>Reveille 7.30 a.m. as men out late skirmishing night before.  On running all day finishing 6 p.m.  Had bath then went down to Tipperary Hut for supper.</p>
