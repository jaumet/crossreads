<p>divisions in, get good mention, Salonika stunt also still going. Saw confirmation in papers of the bombers brought down the other night. The notice stated that the 3 of them were huge bombing planes and carried a crew of 8 men &amp; bombs of 13 ft length with explosive weighing 1 ton, they were shot down from the air. 10 more passes through for 1914 men to Australia to go on Monday next.<br/>Sunday 22nd Sept<br/> Yesterday Mr Norwood of Adelaide, Y.M.C.A. gave us a very decent lecture on Australia, an excellent &amp; humorous speaker. weather cold. Went along to Div. Baths &amp; had a hot shower during the morning &amp; wrote letters &amp; read Charles Lamb the remainder of day. Rumours of success in Palestine.<br/>;Monday 23rd.Sept<br/> Our leave men for Australia went this morning. Mr.Norwood gave another lecture on possibilities of Australia, excellent. Colonel arrived back tonight from a months leave in England. Did my washing also wrote a letter &amp; did some reading.</p>
