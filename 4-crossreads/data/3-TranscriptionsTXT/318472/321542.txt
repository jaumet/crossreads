
12 October 1918SaturdayA Welcome home - also a wedding breakfast at Selwood - by relations & friends of Ruby's. A very nice and thoughtful little affair. Good news comes in from all Fronts. Nice bright weather.
1918 October 13Sunday20 After Trinity
Left Nowra by the 4.30 train got to Sydney about 9 P.M. & so ended a very enjoyable holiday. Special editions with "Right across" headings about Germany accepting the Allies terms of Peace.
14 October 1918MondayReported back to 21st A.A.H.Mosman this morning. Got out door patients pass. Went to Randwick. Saw Eye specialist. Report back to him this day week. Good news continues to come through from all Fronts.
1918 October 15TuesdayDid not much in particular. In the afternoon Ruby & I went down to the City & did a little business also went out to Newtown & saw Sis and the boys. Good war news continuing to come through.
