<p>Saturday Jan 12.1.18<br/>turned out 5.30 fed up Breakfast Packed up moved off 7.30 halted 11.30 had dinner moved off again 2.P.M. went till 5 P.M and camped for the night on line guard to night rained heavy  on 3 shift 12 till 3 done it in Bed<br/>Sunday Jan 13.1.18<br/>turned out 5 P.M. fed up Breakfast Packed up moved 7.30 arrived mazar 12.  off saddled got some wood dinner very windy issued with rations had tea 5.P.M turned in<br/>Monday Jan.14.1.18<br/>turned out 5.30 Breakfast moved off 7.30 halted 11.30 dinner moved again 2.P.M. trecked till 5 and camped had tea turned in<br/>Tuesday Jan.15.1.18<br/>turned out 5.30 fed up Breakfast cleaned up shaved etc moved off 8 A.M. arrived Raffa our destination 12 had dinner all nice and green here I have been</p>
