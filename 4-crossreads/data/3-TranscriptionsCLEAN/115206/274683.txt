  
 April 1918 Tuesday 23 
 Bussy - la - Daours.   D.R. Inspection by Capt. Muller.  German spy caught clad in 5th Divvie Pioneer rig. Weather beautiful  Made some souveners during spare time, also a bush vice. Rode across and saw Allan, Penner, Bob and Barnay at 5th M.G.s Coy camp Some of the Batteries have been slightly gassed. 7.50 pm. Three bombing planes turned up the [indecipherable] en route to Amiens.
