
28.11.15Sun 28thI was on duty from 10 PM till 2 AM this morning & received quite a surprise on going outside to go off duty to find snow was falling, the ground is very slippery & muddy from the rain & we are having a rather rough time just at present fortunately we are getting good food & plenty of warm clothing, a consignment of sheep skin waistcoats arrived yesterday otherwise this weather would be unbearable.  It is a funny thing that the rougher the life is the happier the men seem & they are in great spirits today singing & whistling everywhere; the Turks are expected to attackCamel's Hump tonight & every available man has to be in the trenches that also may have something to do with the good spirits of the lads & the trenches are not an inviting place to
