<p>I remember one night the 8th Brigade Head Qrs had a ball and one half dressed up as Ladies for the occasion It was a shame to see all the good clothes that went to waste that night but it was all in as Fritz only blew them up once he started.</p>
<p>On my arrival at the Dressing Station I was examined by a Doctor who ordered me at once onto a stretcher and by no means was I to leave it. About 3 pm I left the Dressing station at Aubigny for the Field Ambulance (14th) at Daours where I had a bath discarded my khaki for pajamas and at 4.30 pm we were on a 43 mile Ride by ambulance to Abbeville No 1 stationery Hospital.</p>
