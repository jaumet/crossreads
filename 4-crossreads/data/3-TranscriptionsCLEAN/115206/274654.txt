
March 1918Monday 25
Oosterhaven Wood - Hollebeke. Had a rather strenuous night. And early morning.2am guns very quiet on both sides.At &am . Fritz put down a barrage of 5.9 on Zero road which is a very close proximity to the campWeather fairly cool. Walked across to old Bty position. More casualties at W.L. Bob Elyod and his brother both killed. Ardey kolar,[klar?] both legs off. Charlie Smith severely wonded. Davis, a Blighty, Pat Crowe one leg taken off at knee, Boys were all out grazing when it happened
