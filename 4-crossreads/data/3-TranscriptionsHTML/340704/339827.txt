<p>[Pages 136 and 137 read "Abbassan"]<br/>August 1917<br/>Thursday 23<br/>Left Abbassan el Kebir 2 pm halted Tel el Fara and camped the night</p>
<p>Friday 24<br/>Up and away at 4 am on a reconnaissance south of Beersheba.<br/>Bombed by three taubes nobody hit. One plane flew low we drove it off with rifles.<br/>Returned to Fara 10 pm camped for the night</p>
