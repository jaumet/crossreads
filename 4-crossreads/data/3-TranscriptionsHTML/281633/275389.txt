<p>we entered the heads at sunrise, the sun showing half his face over the <strike>sea level</strike> Horizon just as the North Head blocked him from our view.  It was a striking coincidence the Badge of the A.I.F. welcoming us "in propria personae".<br/>We landed about 10 A.M.<br/>Finis.</p>
