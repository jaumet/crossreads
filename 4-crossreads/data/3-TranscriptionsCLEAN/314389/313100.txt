  
 Thursday, May 4th: Rise about 4.45 a.m.  Ride out on wagon to job.  Return to Camp about 5.30.  1st Battalion shelled in their billets to-day - a few casualties.  Fine day.  Retire about 8.30 p.m. 
 May 5th: Rise 4.30.  Ride out to work.  Fine day.  Return to Camp about 5 p.m.  Gas alarm at night. 
 May 6th: Rise 5 a.m.  Ride to work.  Work all day.  Return 6.30 p.m. 
 Sunday, May 7th: Rise 5.30 a.m.  Work all day. Billet in bombing school quarters.  Retire about 9 p.m. 
 Monday, May 8th: Rise 6.30.  Work all day up till 11 at night.  Cold and drizzling rain.  Retire about 11.30 p.m. 
 Tuesday, May 9th: Rise 7 a.m.  Work all day till 4 p.m.  Rainy day.  Retire at 8 p.m. 
 Wednesday, May 10th: Fine day.  Work all day building new huts.  Return to camp 4.30 p.m.  Retire 8 p.m.  Write to Mother.
