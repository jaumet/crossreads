 August, 1915 Friday 6 We Tranship into row boats from the Destroyer, under shell fire but at night so no damage is done &ndash; we then March 1 &frac12; miles up a Gully to [indecipherable] &amp; join the Regiment &ndash; It is hard work to get the c/s of Groceries along over by  Tips  of good gold can be done but I have it still including the insecticide &amp; Sand soap. All my love to you little mother. Boats here now, I write this from the Knight Templar.
