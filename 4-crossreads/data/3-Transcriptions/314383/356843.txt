Horsemen and [indecipherable] rush here there and everywhere, the drivers and riders scattering the people by their hoarse cries.<br/>We went to a picture theatre of all places in the East.  The hall was a fair size and packed with Arabs.  We go into the Reserve on the balcony at the rear of the place and see the show, drinking innumerable cups or rather glasses of coffee.</p>
