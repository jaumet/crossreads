<p>&amp;  is supposed to have shot himself, which is at any rate the most charitable thing to say. On the 17th I had to go to Weli Sheikh Wuran for new cacolets for my ten camels. It was a bilely hot day  &amp;  clouds of dust  &amp;  when I got there about lunch time Bernard shied at an  Engine  &amp;  got caught in a wire  &amp;  came over the top of me. The first spill I have had in the army. I was thrown quite clear fortunately  &amp;  caught him</p>
