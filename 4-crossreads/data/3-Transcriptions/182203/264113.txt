<p>3rd A.C.C.S.                                                                                                                                                   29th C.C.S.                                                                                                                                                B.E.F.                                                                                                                                                  France.                                                                                                                                           12. 11. 16.<br/>My Dearest Moth. &amp; Sisters,<br/>No Aust. mail last week, hope I get one at end of this week, no doubt some of mine have gone down in the ship that was submarined the other day.<br/>Our enemy has been very busy about here lately but we are quite safe &amp; I dont feel at all nervous. Only worried at the thought of our brave boys having to go through such dreadful work. How I wish this wicked war was ended, especially this cold wet miserable weather.</p>
