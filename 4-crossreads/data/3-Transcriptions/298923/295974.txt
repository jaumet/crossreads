<p>23 December 1918 not 1919 Monday<br/>Raining still but we have a Tent- G.O.C. Allenby addressed the Division at Richon &amp; told them they were murderers &amp; cowards &amp; by killing the Bedouins had taken away the good name of ANZAC-  in fact a worse atrocity than any the Turks had counselled etc etc-<br/>Comforts Fund sent each man a parcel of thick Soup but Firewood is the biggest want -   All sorts of rumours as to how long we are to be here as prayer goes up "not long"</p>
