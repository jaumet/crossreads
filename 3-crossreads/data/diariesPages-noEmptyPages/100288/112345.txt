Saturday 30/10/15
Signalling Competition during morning, did fairly well.
Went to Flemington in afternoon to see Derby run, had a very pleasant afternoon. Flemington course very fine, railway accommodation very poor. Returned to city for tea at Boulevard Caf.
Went to Tivoli fairly good programme. Had supper at Boulevard Caf returned to camp 12 pm.
