
<p>SATURDAY, JULY 26, 1919.</p>
<p>Rained solidly till midday. Had</p>
<p>another 35 points, making 62 for the fall</p>
<p>I was round Boundary &amp; Mountain</p>
<p>in morning. Peter drove me into</p>
<p>Quirindi after&nbsp;<s>dinner</s>&nbsp;<sup>lunch</sup> in little</p>
<p>car. Had afternoon tea with Eric</p>
<p>and dinner at [Narontal?]. Got the</p>
<p>9.30 train to Sydney.</p>
