Monday 23/4/17Had good look around things normal.Tuesday 24/4/17Letters from Doff enclosing 4 lovely snaps of Billie &ndash; Normal.Wednesday 25/4/17Orders to relieve Norrie half tonight &amp; half tomorrow night. Wrote to Doff &amp; General.Thursday 26.4.17Took over line from Norrie, usual shelling.Friday 27.4.17Usual shelling by both sides otherwise normal.Saturday 28/4/17Stapylton wounded, heavy shelling by huns and us. Enemy bombarded our trenches after Gas.Sunday 29/4/17Quiet by day and usual shelling by night. Cable from Doff
