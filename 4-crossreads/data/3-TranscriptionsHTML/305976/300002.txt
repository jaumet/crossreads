<p>At the Lena Ashwell Concert party I met some of the old boys who I had not seen since leaving Sydney School finished to day &amp; my leave commences Monday to Alexandria. C. Speering D Harold &amp; myself going together. The school has been a pleasant break from the Field but became very monotonous towards the finish I hope this will be my last school<br/>Sunday May 26<br/>Hot day did not go out.<br/>Wrote Mail.<br/>Monday May 27<br/>Left for Alexandria by 9.30AM train from Moascar. It is now harvesting season &amp; the natives</p>
