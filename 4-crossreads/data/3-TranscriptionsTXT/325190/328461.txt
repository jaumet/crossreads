Wednesday 31st OctStill in the rest camp, &amp; except for a few fatigues such as unloading waggons, have done nothing.Have been into Caestre several times.  It is crowded with soldiers every night, &amp; the dozen or so shops (estaminets &amp; half refreshment &amp; half novelty &amp; produce stores) seem to do a great trade.
