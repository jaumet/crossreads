<p>8.10.16<br/>dismissed the car with orders to meet us at 10.30 at the base, &amp; then went to the Windsor Hotel played Baines a game of billiards went to the American Kursaal till 10.30 &amp; found it very good. then caught our car &amp; had a glorious ride out along perfect roads</p>
<p>9.10.16<br/>Mon 9th<br/>Caught a cold &amp; sore throat yesterday &amp; do not feel too bright this morning slept till dinner time; the car turned up at 1 oclock went to the French Restaurant for lunch done some shopping till it was time to go to a Cinema Show. arranged for the car to meet us</p>
