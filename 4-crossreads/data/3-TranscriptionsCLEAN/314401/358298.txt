
Special prayer used at memorial service over Good Hope & Monmouth off Coronel Dec 28/14.Almighty Father whom it hath pleased to take unto Thyself the Son of our comrades lately departed, in They care we leave them sleeping, waiting in sure and certain hope of the Resurrection to eternal life thro' our Lord Jesus Christ   Our Lord , who shall change our vile body that it may be like unto his glorious body according to the mighty working, whereby he is able to subdue all things to Himself. And to beseech thee to give us grace so to follow their good examples that with them we may be partakers of thy heavenly kingdom Thro'
