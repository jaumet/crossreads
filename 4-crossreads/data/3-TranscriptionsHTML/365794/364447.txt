<p>Thursday 15 February 1917 [14 February 1918]<br/>Life goes on as usual.  9 pts. adm. to ward though all convalescent more or less.  Contacts most of them from up the line &amp; red tape saith they must pass through a "general" not from a stationary hosp. to a convalescent home.<br/>We are a staff of 95.</p>
