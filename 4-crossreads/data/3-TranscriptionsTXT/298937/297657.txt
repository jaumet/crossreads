Here the scenery is flat relieved by beautiful groupings of trees, cottages &amp; avenues, while again it is hilly with an old stone bridge here &amp; there &amp; lichen covered rocks &amp; boulders strewn everywhere.  What a relief after Egypt.  After Avignon we stopped at Orange for tea &ndash; one hour. Cheers, hand waves &amp; smiles wherever we pass, either station or lonely country crossing.
