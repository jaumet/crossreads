26th. Wednesday.Got to work early cleaning up trenches, collecting bombs &amp; ammunition &amp; well we did for about 3pm. he attacked our right flank with hand bombs &amp; kept up all night, We were loosing heavily by casualties &amp; 50 &amp;percnt; gone by 11pm. so called on 18th &amp; 19th. Battns. to reinforce us &amp; we quietened him down, but he came at it again next day.
27th. Thursday.Our boys did great work and pushed Fritz back a little. D.Coy 17th. relieved us about noon &amp; we fell back along Communications T till dark.
