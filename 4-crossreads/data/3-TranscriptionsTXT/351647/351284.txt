 France 26 December 
 Dear Mrs. Jack, 
 Of course I have been a pig &amp; haven't written for two or three weeks but I do hope you'll be gracious &amp; merciful &amp; forgive me.  I have been so jolly busy, my old assistant left me &amp; the new one came &amp; I didn't think I'd keep him &amp; so didn't bother to break him in for a few days, so its been a long process.  Also it has been so wretchedly cold &amp; its hard enough to get through my work. 
 I can keep warm in bed or if I walk a lot or cycle but sitting &amp; writing I simply freeze &amp; am helpless. 
 I have not much news at all, no letters from you or anyone since I wrote last, there has been no Australian mail.  It seems so long between mails these days.  I get a few Blighty letters but it's the home ones I hanker for. 
 First of all, thanks so much for having the parcel sent from London for me.  It had very nice things in &amp; we all enjoyed them so much.  It was a very happy thought of yours.  I did appreciate it. 
 Miss Arn sent me a bonzer parcel too so I have been very lucky.
