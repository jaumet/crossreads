
Thursday 30th Dec.A calm day : ship steady again: The weather began to be tropical & very hot & oppressive : the least exertion making one perspire a lot. Revellie 6 am physical drill 10 am (tug of war, skipping etc, followed by showers which seem the only way to keep cool. Our first active pay received (10/-) : Vic Berghoffer admitted to the hospital with influenza : A lot of flying fish have been about lately & Tom and I went to the bows after tea  & watched them.
