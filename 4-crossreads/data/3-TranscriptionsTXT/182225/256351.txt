 AUGUST&nbsp;&nbsp; SUNDAY 20&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916 
 Fine &amp; windy. The 27 th  Bat was on duty so there was no church parade&nbsp; I made a Signalling flag for myself in morning and read the Quirindi papers in afternoon &amp; wrote some letters in the evening. 
 &nbsp; 
 One of the sweetest enjoyments of life is true friendship &mdash; it divides sorrows, couples and doubles pleasures.
