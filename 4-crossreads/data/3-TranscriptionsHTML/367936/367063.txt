<p>Tuesday 31 October 1916<br/>Got tooth pulled out.<br/>Inspected by General Murray the G.O.C. in the afternoon.<br/>Sent registered parcel of photos home.</p>
<p>1st November 1916<br/>Carried on as per usual.</p>
<p>Wednesday [1] 2 November 1916<br/>All hands up early and saddled up to start for desert.  Lot of busters as owing to remounts being flash.<br/>Crossed canal.  Saw lot of Bedouin prisoners in Kantara E.<br/>Hill 70 for dinner.<br/>Camped at Doudiah [Dueidar].<br/>Camels carrying overcoats and blankets.</p>
