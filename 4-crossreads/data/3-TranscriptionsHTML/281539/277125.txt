<p>29/8/15 The operation consists of first spreading a garment or mat on the ground facing East and then standing with bowed head &amp; clasped hands, meditating. Then down on knees and touch ground with forehead muttering prayers. Repeated 2 or 3 times and then up.</p>
<p>Train then to Cairo and switched on to Alexandria line. It is now 9.35 am we may be on board a transport 120 miles away in a few hours before dawn any way. Looking at Cairo passing through the mind runs back to the Mouska &amp; the Arabian Nights. Slept two hours. At midnight we are passing at great speed through irrigated country, the plains stretching on either side a canal running all the way along the right hand side. The area full of a dank smell like [indecipherable] and we rushing along through the [indecipherable] at express rate</p>
