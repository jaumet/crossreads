Tues.Wed.A few more letters from Ausy.Thurs.A bit sunny.Fri. Sat.Usual Camp life.Sun. Another little walk. Came in about 9 amp few minutes after Bill Ern Davo Tom Woods came in the hut. It was some surprise. Had a good chat for a time.Mon. 9th AprilFairly cold again. Saw Ern Tom again that night they gave us all the latest of home, Coopers etc. Gave them a couple of papers letters to read as they hadn't received any since lobbing.10thOn the same old job of clearing up the mess table. On nippy morning it makes your fingers very cold handling cold plates. Still more of our lads going over.
