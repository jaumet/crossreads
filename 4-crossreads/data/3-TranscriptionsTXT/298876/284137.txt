 Land again lost to sight &ndash; Saw some very large whales. 
 Thursday 24-2-16 
 A nice day &amp; very calm. Saw a light about daylight, it must have been some part of Indian Coast &ndash; Saw a lot of sea snakes &ndash; very quiet on board. 
 Friday 25th 
 Fine day &amp; very calm. It is a treat to watch the sun set &ndash; more especially when the sea is calm. Most of the Boys have converted their trousers into shorteys. Kept very busy of an night &ndash; Having the 2 Hospitals &ndash; Mumps &ndash; Measles and Isolation to attend to. Boxing Tournament has started quite a number of willing bouts already &ndash; AMC are being represented.
