Egypt13 Feb 1916
My dear Mother
You might be interested to know what I am occupied with just now. Well I'll try to explain. I've just paid my Company &amp; am trying to make the books balance. I claimed for &pound;600 which I received in Egyptian pound notes. Now one English pound is equal to 97&frac12; piastres, &amp; an Egyptian pound is worth 100 piastres, &amp; consequently I received 58 500 pt. so far so good, but all payments had to be entered in English money &amp; that's where the fun began.
