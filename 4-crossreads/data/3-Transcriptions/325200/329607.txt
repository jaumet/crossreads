<p>was so many miles distant &ndash; written in German  &amp;    <strike>Arabic</strike>  Turkish.<br/>We had just drunk some water out of the best well there which is hardly brackish at all. When a doctor came up  &amp;  told us that Cholera had broken out  &amp;  cautioned us against the water. You can imagine our feelings!<br/>We then returned to Hod el Kirba to find the brigade starting for home, so we went with them to Katia.<br/>A long day, - we had started a 4 am  &amp; reached Katia at 11 pm</p>
