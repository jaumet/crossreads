<p>it was fairly late in the afternoon when we pulled up at the Transport utterly knocked out, we threw ourselves down on the grass until we recovered then had a good wash &amp; the cooks gave us a good dinner After this we drew our packs &amp; got some clean clothes &amp; went to bed early for we had had practically no sleep for the last 3 nights</p>
<p>6th This morning we pushed off for Bde Hqrs &amp; found it after walking a mile</p>
