<p>3.  of him as "Small pox" &amp; I begged him to find another name in case he might overhear some time and it hurt his feelings, so John suggested "Violet" as he is so shy &amp; modest, and Violet it has been ever since: it is so unspeakably appropriate!  Do you know Violet always brings my two ice-creams on the one plate now: last night they were strawberry ices &amp; my two were a really deep pink not only a blush pink like most of the others, and the others declared that Violet had meant them as a special honour, though I think possibly they happened to be the scrapings off the bottom where the colouring was not properly mixed through.<br/>Gwen Bateman is an absolute darling &amp; the memory she has for poetry &amp; entertaining anecdotes, or more solid matter, is wonderful &amp; she keeps us entertained by the hour.  I wish I had either her knowledge of literature or her memory, it would be invaluable, but I'm not going to despair I'm going to cultivate.<br/>Strange to say Father, with you, departed any liking for novel-reading on board, &amp; I haven't even opened that book I got out of the library while you were here.<br/>Have read a good bit of that Guide Book &amp; "The Physiology of Faith &amp; Fear," &amp; have been knitting a bit.</p>
