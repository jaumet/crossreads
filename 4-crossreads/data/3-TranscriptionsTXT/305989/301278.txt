Sunday 14 October 1917Quite day, hands very sore.
Monday 15 October 1917Had snap taken of the Dumpites:  nothing doing.
Tuesday 16 October 1917Taube over:  one brought down in Wadi, nothing doing in particular.
Wednesday 17 October 1917Got two parcels from Aunt Rose &amp; Dorothy.  Saw a good concert given by The Tanks at Y.M.C.A. had good supper.
Thursday 18 October 1917Quiet day, got a lot of new gramophone records, quiet night, read after supper.
Friday 19 October 19171st Anzac Mtd. Dump moving into Shellal Crossing;  helping the lads to pack up.
Saturday 20 October 1917Quiet day.  Matt Ward came back from Port Said.
