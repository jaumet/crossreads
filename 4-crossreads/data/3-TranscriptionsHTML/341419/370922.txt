<p>again &amp; of course the tale is that our 60 pdrs. have knocked him out.</p>
<p>It is very circumstantial too.  For they say that some of the 1st Bgde. saw the flash of the gun at Shunet Nimrin &amp; laid information &amp; hence the battery opened &amp; got him.  Certain it is that they engaged him all right, that was obvious but if they succeeded in knocking him out well &ndash; they were lucky;  one gun is not a large target at about seven miles.</p>
<p>Of other news there is not much.  The Battle section of Des. Corps H.Q.s &amp; the big evacuating motor Ambulances from the Anzac Clearing Station have all gone to Jaffa, which points to something big happening there.</p>
