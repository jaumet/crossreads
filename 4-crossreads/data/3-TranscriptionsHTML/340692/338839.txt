<p>After leaving Etaples, &amp; arriving at a little station named Abeele, somewhere near the Belgium frontier, I spent a couple of days at a re-inforcement camp.  From here I marched to Steenvoorde, where I became attached to the 7th Aus. Field Ambulance.  We then marched in stages, stopping for a night at Noordpreene; [Noordpeene] two nights at Tilques, another good days march &amp; we entrained at Arques, &amp; arriving at St we moved on to where we billeted for another day.
