<p>Monday 25th June Fovant<br/>We arrived in Fovant Camp at midday on Friday &amp; found everything ready for us &ndash; even beds made<br/>The position is not nearly so bad as we had been led to expect, &amp; our actual camp is rather good.<br/>Lord Werley [?] reviewed the 16th Brigade on Saturday &amp; the Ambulance was adjudged the smartest on parade</p>
