Saturday 8 March 1919In town arranging Permits to travel, money matters, etc.  As I told you, have sent Dolly's money to Commonwealth Savings Bank, Melbourne, with specimen signature so she can draw on arrival.  With her I am sending the following kit,
1 New Steel Uniform Box 4" x 2"1 old ditto1 Wooden Box about 4&frac12;" x 2&frac12;"1 Big leather Trunk2 Black Tin Trunks1 Gladstone Bag1 Brown leather Trunk 4&frac12; x 2&frac12;1 Hat Box1 Suit Case1 Dressing Case1 Cradle
The first 3 mine.
