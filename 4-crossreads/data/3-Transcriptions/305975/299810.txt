<p>Saturday May- 24.5.19.<br/>went to sydney to day  was out at wins place  raining heavy<br/>Sunday May.25.5.19<br/>am having a day with tom and annie very wet  stayed in bed till 12.30 A.M.<br/>Monday May.26.5.19<br/>am still with tom and annie  raining again  went into town  left my razors to be done up   measured for a suit.</p>
