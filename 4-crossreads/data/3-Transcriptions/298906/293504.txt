<p>at us, he looked to be coming  fair in among the mob, you would have laughed had you seen them stepping off the forms &amp; chairs in their hurry some of them fell head over heels, they made sure he had lost control of his machine, but the roar of his engines should have told them different, they look a terrible affair flying straight at you puts one in mind of some huge horse or dragon fly, this plane just skimmed our heads, &amp; we all gave him a cheer as he passed over, as he turned his machine he waved his hand to us, &amp; steered the plane with the other its simply marvellous what they can do nowadays with the flying machines</p>
