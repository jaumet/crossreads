<p>9<br/>Later, a batch of German red-cross prisoners, in their green-grey uniforms and red handed caps.</p>
<p>Did those lads notice as I did? How distinctive is the German type? The hard bullet shaped head, with the rear part of the crown flattened on an angle.</p>
<p>The cruel unkindly eyes, lacking the shadow depth of fancy and gleam of imagination.</p>
<p>Did those lads think how many are the points in the modern German, which are characteristic of soul-less dumb driven cattle?</p>
