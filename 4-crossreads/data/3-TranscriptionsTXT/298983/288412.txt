
Saturday 20th AprilQuite a bussy day went to the Church to the christening of Christopher Powel - Cotton came back Had cake & champain & planted a tree. Evening the Major showed us some of his stuffed beasts & later lantern slides of one of his African trips giving a lecture each time all very interesting
