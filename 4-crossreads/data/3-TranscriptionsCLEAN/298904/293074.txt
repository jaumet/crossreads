  
 We were not long in embarking & soon we were detailed off to our respective portion of the ship as we went aboard a chap handed each man a meal ticket, for we had to have 2 sittings, as there was not enough room for them all at once, she had been an immigrant ship & had bunks fitted out in her & we had these & very  good they were, I know I slept sound in them.
