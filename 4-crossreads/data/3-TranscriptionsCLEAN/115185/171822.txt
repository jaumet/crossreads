  
 P.S. I suppose you wonder why we didn't go on Monday 12th. There was not room on the transport and all the rest of the hospital have gone except Colonel Dick  the  who has been left to look after us. He such a nice sensible man. I am glad he is still here. His wife a comfortable lady - with kind heart is here too - she will miss him terribly - as none of the doctors wives can go with them. Mrs Fraschi - is separated
