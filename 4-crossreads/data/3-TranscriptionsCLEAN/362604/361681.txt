 We are both well & having as good a time as we can. 
 Give my Best Wishes to all the neighbours.  I hope that you are all keeping well & Happy.  What did Nettie do when she watered the Garden.  Did she splash Herself.  She says that she had a feed of guavas.  I did not think there would be enough for anyone to have a feed. 
 Fond Love from Harry.
