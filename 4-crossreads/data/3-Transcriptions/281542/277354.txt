<p>The Turk has become very saucy in his trench fighting wh game he plays "par excellence".  In close work our men use only periscope rifles.  The sharpshooting Turk is a considerable item &amp; it is very unwise to show a head above the trenches.</p>
<p>The enemy are using a captured French 75 to great advantage.  We receive a peppering morning &amp; evening from this big gun.  It creates great havoc wherever its shells lodge &amp; blows trenches sky high.  Today it has been particularly active &amp; my dug-out is precariously within</p>
