<p>Spent most of the day getting settled in K. Co.  Was asked out by a lady in the Y.M. to see an ancient Church but could not manage it.</p>
<p>Thursday 3rd</p>
<p>Saw Dr. am moveing again tomorrow.  Marched to Tivoli, Rouen, good veriaty show.</p>
<p>Friday 4th</p>
<p>Left per ambulance at 9 for Rouen entrained at 11 reached Hafleur &ndash; 5 miles from Le Havre &ndash; about 15 marched to Con. Camp 1 mile.  Had dinner also ate more than was good for me on the train.  Have a bow Hut</p>
