<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dec 21<sup><u>st</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Very cold but fine - Had to be towed again to start the engine, I drew the supplies &amp; went up to the workshop when I had finished &amp; stayed the night. Roy &amp; Dick were also in the workshop &amp; they spent the evening with me -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dec 22<sup><u>nd</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Not so cold to-day - I left the engine for the Staff Sergt to try &amp; start, but he could&#39;nt get her going. took carburettor off &amp; worked on it &amp; eventually got her started - I did&#39;nt go on supplies to-day as I could&#39;nt get out in time - When I got out I went back to the Park - had a hot bath in Bailleul -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dec 23<sup><u>rd</u></sup>&nbsp; Very cold again - Had to be towed again to start the engine - drew supplies for Brigade &amp; got back to Park at 2.30 P.M.</p>
