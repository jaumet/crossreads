 Juillet 17 No 2 Duty Section. Other Sections on Drill &amp; Route March. Showery weather. Nothing of note on our Front. Russians still doing well &amp; French more than holding their own. Had swim in lake off Somme Canal this afternoon. 8th Brigade camped in Corbie for the night, &amp; a good many [indecipherable] among them making a general nuisance of themselves. 
 Juillet 18 14th Brigade marched into Corbie today &amp; will camp here tonight. A good many zig zagging about the streets. Nothing fresh to report. We are carrying on with general training.
