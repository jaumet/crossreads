8th Oct. - SundayRaining & cold, but fine afternoon.  Church at St. Sever at 8 a.m.  After work at 12-30, start on a most interesting tour.  Meet our gallant young Belgian friend & take a tram to Maromme.  Here a fine monument to the French soldiers of 1870-1.  This & a walk right into the country along the hills to a little village St. Jean du Cardonnay a distance of 41/2 kilometres
