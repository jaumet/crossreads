<p>3/4/15<br/>We marched from Mena Camp to Cairo and embarked for alexanderia there we embarked on to the troop ship Galeka</p>
<p>4/4/15<br/>We left Alexanderia for Lemnos ireland arriving there on the 7/4/15</p>
<p>7/4/15<br/>We arrived at Lemnos ireland it was a Greek ireland till the war started then England took it over it was about 20 miles in with and about the same in length it was a rum old spot</p>
