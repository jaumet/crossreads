<p>Wednesday 12th December 1917<br/>Steenwerck<br/>Harnessed up and left lines by 11am. 10.30 inspection by General Smyth. Went to dump for load of coal and timber for cooks.<br/>Got back to lines by 12noon. Half holiday. Our section beat the 3rd D.A.C. at football 61 to 28.<br/>Fritz dropping bombs</p>
