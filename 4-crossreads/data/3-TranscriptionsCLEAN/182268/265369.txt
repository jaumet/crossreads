  
 Friday 16/11/17  Marched to Berthen via Mont Des Cats [Mont des Cats]  I walked- orders received to report to Reinf Camp. 
 Saturday 17/11/17  Bn moved to Neuve Eglise- I to Fletre. after a walk of 5 miles  Saw Div at Eche 
 Sunday 18/11/17  walked to Neuve Eglise & had breakfast with Battalion- walked home in afternoon 19 miles 
 Monday 19/11/17  Walked to Mont Des Cats 5 miles  Inspected Camp which is in a fearful condition.
