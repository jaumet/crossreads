
August,1918Sunday 11
In front of Harboniers, Bty. Pos. [indecipherable]Weather warm and bright. Battery had a duty shoot at 9 am this morning  - preparation for 1st Bdgs attack.Left with Smithy for the Waggon Lines and picked up atransportat Bayonville [indecipherable] as far as VillersBrettoneaux and thence lorry to Daours eventuallyarriving at Bussy when we had an excellent lunchconsisting of skats and eggs. Returning to our W.L. which were fairly close to Home we had a sumptuous supper of bread and jam.Had so few visits from the [indecipherable] little Fritz.
