 20 October 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out) Still here- Kantara East No.24 Stationery Hospital This place has grown into an immense Canvas town since the days we first crossed the Canal- No comforts here, Bully beef for lunch!! &amp;  black bread the  best  food goes to the front line which is right- Our Tummies are upset from Quinine- but we are all much better. X An orderly put the wrong end of the Thermometer in my mouth &amp; was quite satisfied, another took mine this morning instead of another officer who was being discharged &amp; who occupied the bed along side.
