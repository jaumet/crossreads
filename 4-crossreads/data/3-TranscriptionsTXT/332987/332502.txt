 2-10-1918 Still in train, making for the town of Taranto, which is at the gulf of Taranto.  Weather changed, raining. 
 3-10-1918 Nearing train journey's end.  Arrived at Taranto rest camp 4 p.m. after 8 days and 7 nights on train.  8 p.m. warned for baggage guard, up all night. 
 4-10-1918 On board boat Ormonde, 15,000 tons.  All Sgts. in cabins. 
 5-10-1918 Raining.  No word of sailing. 
 6-10-1918 Taking on board 250 English Officers, bound for India.  Now on board approximately 2,000 troops.  Boat stations and life belt drill this morning.
