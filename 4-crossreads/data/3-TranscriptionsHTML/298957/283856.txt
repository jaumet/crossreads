<p>Sunday 21st Nov. Church Service by Corp (Rev.) Cosier of our unit. His services are always worth attending. An albatross one day flew over the ship and the captain remarked that it was a good omen. On previous trips the "Ascanius" had been chased by submarines in the Mediterranean and also by the "Emden".<br/>Hospital patient<strike>s</strike> died of pneumonia and the burial service was a very impressive one.
