 7/4/18 &ndash; Albert-Dernacourt Line The artillery activity of the enemy is very marked and fierce shelling continued all night.  Round Bn. H.Q. we had a rough time and some shells landed very close.  Fortunately there were no casualties.  Dawn passed uneventfully and there was no attack.  Mud is deep abround [abound, abroad, or around ?].  Walked round the men's bivvies and some of the sights would make an angel weep.  Poor dirty tired lads sleeping crouched in filthy wet holes.
