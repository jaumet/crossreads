<p>4th Aus Div, 15th May 17<br/>My dear Ferguson, Have just received your note of 10th inst I cannot tell you how very pleased I am at your good progress. You seem also to be making hay while the Sun shines as far as the ladies are concerned from all I can hear. I quite envy you. Do</p>
