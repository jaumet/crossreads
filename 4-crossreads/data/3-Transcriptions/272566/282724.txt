<p>Saturday 8 December</p>
<p>Fine, but roads very greasy.  Cold outside.<br/>Run to A.D.M.S. Dranoutre thence to 1st Anzac Corps at Flete.<br/>Night casualty wire at 6 p.m.<br/>Our unit today played the 14th Fld. Ambce, at Locre &ndash; Rugby League.  Resulted in a win for us as usual  20 to Nil.<br/>Lt. Col. Makin reported back from sick leave to England.  He is our new O.C. &amp; not a bad sort.</p>
