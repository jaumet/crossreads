<p>But we don't want them cured, we can put up with the rain and treat it as a joke.</p>
<p>Sept 14th &ndash; 17<br/>Here we are back again in the same possie which we vacated on the 4th Inst</p>
<p>On our arrival here we were accorded a warm reception which goes to point out Fritz's feelings towards the Australians  He certainly does not love them, nor they him. Anyhow coming events will certify matters.</p>
