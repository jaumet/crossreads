 6 September 1918 Friday A bright sunny day and nothing of importance happening on the boat. Good news continues to come through from the Western Front where all troops of the Allies appear to be advancing on all Fronts. 
 1918 September 7 Saturday Good news continues to come through principally from the French  and  Front where the enemy are reported in full flight. British doing well also. Nice fine weather &amp; things going well.
