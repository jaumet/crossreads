 My Colonel has just left us to go back to Australia. His name is Colonel Stevens CMG. DSO. and is a very good man. There is also some of my friends out of the Battn. gone with him and I am sure they fully deserve it. I suppose Miss King my time will come next so I will have to keep my head down for a while.
