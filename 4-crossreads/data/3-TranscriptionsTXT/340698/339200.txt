
29/5/17Bde sigs. School started today 10 days course.
24/6/17Bde church parade today. Gen. Birdwood and Walker were present issuing out decorations. I received my M.M.
29/6/17Marched to Englebermer from Lavieville via Hellencourt and Senlis. We expect to remain here about 10 days for a course of field training.
