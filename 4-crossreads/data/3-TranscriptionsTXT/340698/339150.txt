 23/10/16 Went per motor lorry to a position about 8 miles from Fricourt and marched to that place. 
 24/10/16 Camped in tents and [bivouacs?] about a mile from Fricourt last night and moved on to Hametz Woods today. 
 We expect to go into the line in a few days. 
 25/10/16 We are camped in all kinds of rough biveys here.
