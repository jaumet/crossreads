<p>His last letter</p>
<p>83  France<br/>29/3/18</p>
<p>My Dear Father &amp; Mother<br/>Very many thanks for your letters of 27th Jan &ndash; I was passing thro London and got them at Berners Hotel where I stay, forwarded on from Sis &ndash;<br/>Was recalled to France yesterday on account of doings up the front &ndash;<br/>Very unsatisfactory as we are held up at a Base</p>
