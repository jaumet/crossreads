<p>Tuesday 29 May 1917.</p>
<p>Flying fish seen today by the hundred. Another very hot day&nbsp;and sea a little rougher today.</p>
<p>1<sup>st</sup>&nbsp;sports meeting this afternoon.</p>
<p>Physical jerks during morning&nbsp;&ndash; a practice for the competition in the sports. &nbsp;One of the 15. Japanese Cruiser moving very quickly into various positions yesterday &amp; today.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sports caused amusement&nbsp;among the boys.</p>
<p>Wrote 2 letters.</p>
<p>Detailed for guard duty tomorrow to mount at 9 am.</p>
<p>Sleeping on deck again tonight.</p>
<p>G.S.S. Cmn&nbsp;&amp; I&nbsp;went down below into stokehold &amp; engine room, &amp; right along to end of propeller shaft.</p>
<p>Speed now&nbsp;Full&nbsp;as indicated on dial above log book below.</p>
