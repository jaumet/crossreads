<p>Fri 6th Some bombardment at night &amp; action on left</p>
<p>Sat 7th Things very lively early Heavy musketry rifle gun &amp; battleship fire.<br/>Many dead brought down to Morgue a false attack early to screen operations on left cost the 1st &amp; 2nd Light Horse dear on Popes &amp; Quinns also the Welsh Fulisiers up Pope's gully &amp; the Light Horse on the left Walkers. Our little corpl did not turn up all day &amp; it is said he has gone over parapet with his brother who was a bomb thrower. The losses are pretty heavy on this front at least 26 in Mortuary &amp; 8 or more</p>
