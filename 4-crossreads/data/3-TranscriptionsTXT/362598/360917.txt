 Sunday 19 March 1916 Bill, Ellis &amp; I went to Mary's well &amp; Virgin's Tree &amp; to Kursaal after tea. 
 Monday 20 March 1916 Dave Niness took Photo E. R. I.  Roy &amp; I went to Kursaal together.  Roy came out to dinner but got very little.  Came down to my ward to talk to Corbett. 
 Tuesday 21 March 1916 Ward-master. Home, Nettie, Bess, Laura, Nell &amp; Pa, Ma, Cock &amp; Ida. Went to Pyramids &amp; round the Houses of Cairo.  Bill, Dave, Charlie &amp; E. &amp; I.  Rode from Mena in motor car. 
 Wednesday 22 March 1916 Met Johhno &amp; went to Pictures.  Special leave from 5 to 10. 
 Thursday 23 March 1916 Wrote to Mem &amp; Cohens. 
 Friday 24 March 1916 Went to Blue Mosque.  Met Roy in Gardens, Tea at Caf&eacute; de la Paix.  Tea Misrquash.  Roy, Dave Niness &amp; I went to Pictures.  Pictures Misrquash.  Pictures Colosse. 
 Saturday 25 March 1916 Our Theatre Experts gave concert in Esbekiah Gardens.
