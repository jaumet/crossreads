<p>Thursday 22 May 1919<br/>Hot again. Went to Brixton for tea. Missed lecture again. Had some ices with Bill at Arding &amp; Hobbs.</p>
<p>Friday 23 May 1919<br/>As usual hot again. Went to the motor class &amp; from there out to Marie's to tea. Bill was there but left at 6 pm. Marie &amp; I took him to the bus &amp; then went visiting but the people weren't home. Went back to the flat &amp; played Rummy &amp; Crib. Moved into new room at the flat. 40/- per week.</p>
