<p>April 1916<br/>Sunday 2<br/>Visited sights of Colombo in rickshaw. Temple,Galle Face. Park Gardens. Bought silk at native shops. Spent &pound;2-5-0.<br/>Thoroughly enjoyed stay. Cost 15/- for 2 days, very cheap<br/>Left Colombo 10.30 pm</p>
<p>Monday 3<br/>Wrote long letters about Colombo . Small monkey brought aboard from Colombo. Pet of the ship</p>
