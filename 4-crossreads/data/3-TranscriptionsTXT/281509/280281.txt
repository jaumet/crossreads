 28 February 1916 Monday 
 Go down lines &amp; get my tunic changed by Phil Prott. Do nothing all morning &amp; not much more in afternoon.  Rain at intervals. After tea go down lines with T &amp; B. meet Francis Bros. at Methodist Club. Cigar &amp; concert. former to strong dizzy &amp; sick, latter to tame. Transfer our patronage to Y.M.C.A tent. I go to sleep on pile of bags. Home to bed at 10 pm.
