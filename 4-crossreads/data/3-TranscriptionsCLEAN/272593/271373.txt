Had 3 of my platoon go to hospital this morning.15th  Marched out to a new parade ground this morning & practised our new formation in battle order the platoon shaped very well & are coming on fast.While we were out there a lot of Lancers came cantering across the ridge with their sabres drawn & flashing in the bright sunlight.
