<p>We saw the Sgt stop him &amp; make enquiries &amp; not evidently being satisfied brought him back</p>
<p>in the meantime one of our staff cars came to the X Rds &amp; I baled it up &amp; told them we had got a suspected spy So they waited for him, asked him a few questions &amp; then put him into the car &amp; took him off as he got in he gave the Sgt the bitterest glance imaginable &amp; then we heard about an hour after that he was a genuine officer! And now I wonder which is the most 'cut up' the Sgt or the 'Spy'?</p>
<p>We <strike>came</strike> did not stop at Sercus but made our way back to Wallon-</p>
