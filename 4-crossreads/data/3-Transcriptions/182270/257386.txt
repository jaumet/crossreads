
<p>SATURDAY, MARCH 15, 1919.</p>
<p>1025</p>
<p>15</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. A perfect day. On deck</p>
<p>playing games &amp; reading all day.</p>
<p>Had a boxing tournament in</p>
<p>afternoon; and a pierrot concert</p>
<p>at night &amp; both were very good</p>
<p>indeed.</p>
<p>I got second in the sweep.</p>
<p>Days run 333 miles.</p>
