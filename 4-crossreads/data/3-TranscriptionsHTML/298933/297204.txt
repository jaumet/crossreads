<p>Vignacourt<br/>[November 1918]<br/>14 Th<br/>No bread jam or butter on issue for our rations as gone to feed the hungry huns<br/>15 Fri<br/>The French are admirable, I shall never forget them.They love the Aussies<br/>16 Sat<br/>Spoken at an Aust Cemetry by the Mayor of Amiens<br/>People of France you can never repay the debt you owe to Aust All women should be as mothers sisters wifes &amp; sweethearts to the full by caring for their graves for all times</p>
