<p>1916 January 14<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Inoculated 3rd &amp; final time this morning.</p>
<p>Shirk parade in afternoon on acc. of inoculation.  Feel rotten. Tea &amp; sleep.  Get up for lecture by Capt. Willis.  Apierients.  Tumble into bed feeling pretty knocked out.  Day hot &amp; dusty.</p>
