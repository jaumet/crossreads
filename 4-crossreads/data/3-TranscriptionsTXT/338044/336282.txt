 26th Sept; Tuesday. Raining again this morning &amp; afterwards a beautiful day &amp; very warm. Our boys doing great work &amp; cut off Combles. No more re the transfer. After tea return home &amp; study French until 10-30 pm. Expenses 5c (3c for papers 2c for Tram fares) 
 27th Sept; Wednesday A fine day, &amp; pleasantly cool. Great news from the front. Combles taken    &amp; great advance by French &amp; British. 5000 prisoners in two days. Thiepval taken at last.
