<p>178<br/>offered me my pick. I selected one and have used it ever since. It was a "King George" and had imprinted on it "Made in Germany". We only had one fatigue job that was to take the "Cookers" back to the cooks. I was by this time much more accustomed to fatigues and mule like work.</p>
<p>Whilst here we had heard our artillery &ndash; which was close by had orders to shell the Germans with Gas Shells.</p>
<p>Lying round were plenty of rifles and equipment belonging to soldiers who had been killed or wounded in the taking of Hermies and Doignies.</p>
