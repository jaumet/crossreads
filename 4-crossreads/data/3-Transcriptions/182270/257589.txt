
<p>SATURDAY, OCTOBER 4, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine with cold Southerly gale blowing</p>
<p>Lopped in Top paddock in morning</p>
<p>&amp; burning off track in Wheelers</p>
<p>in Afternoon.</p>
<p>Darby&#39;s bore fell in &amp; he had to</p>
<p>punch it all out again &amp; case it</p>
<p>as he went down. Down to</p>
<p>about 106 ft in rotten &quot;mush&quot; &amp;</p>
<p>no water yet. Tickle drawing</p>
<p>water to him all day</p>
