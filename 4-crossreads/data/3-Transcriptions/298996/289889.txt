<p>27.4.15<br/>Nothing doing to day</p>
<p>28.4.15<br/>Very hot to day. We were moving the Pay [indecipherable] tent to day which was not pitched properly well the table was <strike>standing some distance from the tent</strike> just put out side of same with paper on it when a whist wind came along and lifted some of the papers from same the men standing about at the time caught all but one that one went very high <strike>as Dov</strike> there were some <strike>ha</strike> Crows or some mind of bird flying about and two of them got hold of same and had a fight for same One fellow chased same</p>
