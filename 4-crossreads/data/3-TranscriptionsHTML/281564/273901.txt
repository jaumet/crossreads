<p>The Company possesses a large herd of Chinese cattle which, tended by Malays, rove through the plantation &amp; supply milk &amp; beef to the people of the island. Received more war news.</p>
<p>Thursday 29th Oct<br/>Went to the hospital in the morning. The place is now usually packed with patients in door &amp; out, clamouring for admission.  Some have the fever but the majority come to have their leg bandaged, for any small wound even a puncture caused by a mosquito fester &amp; spreads often taking poison &amp; causing great irritation. The usual treatment at the hospital is to paint the wound with iodine of which they seem to have an unlimited supply on hand.</p>
<p>The definition of one of the heads of our army :- One half officers &amp; non coms, &amp; the other made up of prisoners , spies and hospital patients is fairly correct.</p>
<p>In the evening we had an exciting pig chase but although we had three shots we did not get anyway near them</p>
