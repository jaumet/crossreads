
<p>built in the 12<sup>th</sup> Century.&nbsp; There is no modern restoration to mar the work of the old masters.&nbsp; Tis a wonderful monument to the exceeding worth of those long dead craftsmen.&nbsp; Don &amp; I explored the interior.&nbsp; Priceless relics are strewn around.&nbsp; The High Altar is a gaping ruin.&nbsp; Wonderful examples of the woodcarvers art are scattered everywhere; An illuminated vellum missal is lying forlorn at the Altar Steps</p>
