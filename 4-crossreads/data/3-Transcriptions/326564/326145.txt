<p>So I reckoned I did not do too bad<br/>The French people must have thought us bad, they were all out to see us pass through the villages, it was funny to see the mob rolling home dead beat &amp; done to the world &amp; we were all in the same boat, after I cooled down I went and had a cold bath, &amp; then after tea there was a lecture on aerial photography, after this was over I walked down to Merckenghem to see about my watches if they were done or not, here I struck Fatty Gill &amp; had a bit of a yarn with him, then I walked home in the moonlight.</p>
<p>21st. Lovely weather again &amp; thing are looking beautiful. Last night old Fritzs planes were over bombing &amp; all our lights were put out</p>
