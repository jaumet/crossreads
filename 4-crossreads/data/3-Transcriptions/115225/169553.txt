
<p>Apl 15<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp; We left park at 7.30 A.M. &amp; loaded ammo at Poulainville &amp; took it to corps reserve dump on the <s>allon</s> Amiens Contay road <sup>near S<u>t</u> Gratien</sup>. got back to park at 4.30 P.M. &amp; received some letters from home &amp; a parcel. Cold day but no rain &mdash;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Apl 16<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp; We went to Flesselles amunition dump at 8.15 A.M. &amp; hauled to Corps reserve dump. heavy mist &amp; cold wind all day. We heard that the Germans have captured Bailleul, &amp; Meteren is <s>the front</s> no mans land</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Apl 17<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp; We stayed in to-day to work on the lorry - it was a dull rainy day - I lost 15 francs at a two up school in the evening there was a very big school &amp; up for hundreds of francs.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Apl 18<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp; Five of the lorries are out on supplies every day for the Brigade &amp; when they have finished they return to Amiens &amp; load up with furniture etc &amp; haul for the civies - We got ready for the road. Cold bleak day &amp; some rain.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
