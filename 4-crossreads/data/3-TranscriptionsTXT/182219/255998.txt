Dec 23 1917 Xmas Eve
Started on our journey once again had a very unpleasant ride last night on the goods as we were compelled to camp under a number of water carts & the weather blew up very cold & windy, we had alternate snatches of sleep & nightmares owing to the innumerable bumpings buffeting & stoppages. We finally succeeded in reaching Dier Sienade about 2 am & were bumped into a siding to await the pleasure of the unloading gangs, this enabled us to get a little sleep but it was bitterly cold
