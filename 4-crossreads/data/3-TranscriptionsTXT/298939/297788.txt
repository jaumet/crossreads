Thursday June 21stWe started yesterday morning at 5.30 from Wareham.  I had goodbye to Mrs Brooks [?] at the School overnight, & on our way through town most of the population waited to see the last of us.We marched 10 miles before dinner & finished at Blandford (17 miles) at 3 p.m.  Here we camp in a big private park on the outskirts of the town
