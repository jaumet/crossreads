<p>to the Clearing Station in the morning &amp; now here I am. It is a good job. I have sole command of a contingent of some 14 camels &amp; their attendant native drivers. I is I think though, the beginning of the end. I am on the shelf, shop soiled. Well, I have seen Sari Bair, Romani, Katia, Bir el Abd, El Arish, Magdhaba, Rafa and they will do me for the rest of my life quite well.<br/>Vance must have changed him mind, it was quite a surprise for me to come here.</p>
