<p>December, 1915</p>
<p>Friday 10</p>
<p>Anglo Indian doctors, R.A.M.C. orderlies &amp; nurses of Queen Alexander's nursing reserve on board.</p>
<p>Treated splendidly.</p>
<p>Leg quite straightforward &amp; progressing wonderfully.</p>
<p>Wrote field service cards for about 8 people.</p>
<p>Getting fit again onboard with the good food.</p>
