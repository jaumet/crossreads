<p>19 August 1915<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Sky is overcast this morning but it may clear up<br/>Our packs arrive to day for which we are truly thankful for after 13 days our shirts   &amp;  sox are pretty ripe.<br/>The Fifth Brigade has landed at last. Now that they are here we wonder which Bde they are going to releive or assist.</p>
<p>1915 August 20<br/>Friday</p>
<p>During the morning Brigadier General Monash  &amp;  his staff make a tour of our trenches   &amp;  decide that an advance will have to be made  &amp;  the ridge opposite cleared of the Turks.<br/>The time set is 3 pm to morrow   &amp;  we are to have the place of honor which is first out of our trench.<br/>The navy fire several sighting shots to get the range   &amp;  the bombardment is fixed for 2 pm on 21st.</p>
