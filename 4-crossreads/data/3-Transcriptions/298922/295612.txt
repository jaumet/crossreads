<p>Friday 8th November 1917</p>
<p>Today I took the Isherwoods to the Australian Club for Lunch &amp; afterwards to the Australia House in the Strand &amp; saw the Lord Mayors Procession, had afternoon tea with the High Commissioner, Andy Fisher!!! in the evening dinner at the Carlton &amp; went to see CHU CHIN CHOW.</p>
<p>It worries me spending all this money but it cannot be helped at present.</p>
<p>Good news from GAZA in the Papers but ITALY!!!!</p>
