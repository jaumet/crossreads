 My Darling Mother, No letters to hand from home since arriving in France am anxiously looking forward for Some.    Hope you are all well and in the best of health &amp; that business is keeping up.   I see Malcolm McPherson nearly every day, he is camped about &frac12; mile away from us &amp; we drift together of an evening &amp; swop news.    he is in the 3rd Engineers, wrote to you a few days ago.
