One has no sense of speed or movement when well up in the air. All you get is the rush of wind past your head &amp; the roar of your engine. You get plenty of sensation when you shut your engine off, put her nose down to glide to earth, or when you stunt up at a good height. (Switch-back, &amp; steep banking)
