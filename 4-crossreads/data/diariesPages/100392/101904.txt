Jan 19 1918
F2 Ward 14
Kitchener Hospital 
Brighton Eng
Dear linda
You will see by the above address that I am back in dear old Blighty again on the sick list. I was very badly gassed in action on Nov 17 behind passhendaele ridge while firing on 18 pounders Batteries which were holding our front line on that date but I would not give in until the battery (the 15) was relieved 3 days after and after after dragging
