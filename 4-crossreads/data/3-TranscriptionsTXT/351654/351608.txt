Neither of us have had the luck to get a trip to England yet &amp; as there is no leave to be granted until this offensive ends that will not be before the winter sets in, we have a while to wait yet, though.  We hear from Mother regularly.
Well I think I will now turn in for a while.  I don't suppose it will be for long.
With kind regardsYours very sincerelyBertie Macnamara.
