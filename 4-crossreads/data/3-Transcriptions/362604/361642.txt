<p>Australian Red Cross</p>
<p>Bulford<br/>20/4/18</p>
<p>Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George,</p>
<p>We have had several letters from you all but Ellis has taken them all away to answer &amp; He's out to-night.  I'll get them all &amp; answer later in the week.  We have had a horrid week of rain, hail, snow &amp; ice.  I hope it clears up soon because we want to play cricket &amp; Tennis.
