I asked the question what time is allowed to make my private arrangements, I was told the maximum was 1 month so I decided to avail myself of the limit &amp; was then given a Card, notifying me to report myself &amp; at the Drill Hall on October 30th 1917. I then took my Departure feeling more contented &amp; satisfied that I was a true Soldier &amp; more determined than ever to keep our Dear Old Flag Flying.
