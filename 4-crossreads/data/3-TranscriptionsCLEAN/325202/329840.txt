 Nov 26 (Sunday) Went into Amiens 10 miles away yesterday - A very beautiful Cathedral but the facade & the interior woodwork is all sandbagged up as a precaution against air-raids nothing else of interest. 
 Just a good provincial town of 100,000 inhabitants at present filled with British soldiers - specially officers - Prices exhorbitant was charged 1 franc for cup of cafe au lait at a middle class confectioners.
