<p>finished up through Calais to Audruicq : into huts &amp; cleaned ourselves up :</p>
<p>Saturday March 30th</p>
<p>Audruicq : cleaned up, washing etc during day : had a walk round the town after tea &amp; it is a decent joint :</p>
<p>Sunday March 31st</p>
<p>Audruicq : H.Comm in morn &amp; very nice : stroll round in afternoon &amp; after tea &amp; enjoyed it</p>
<p>Monday April 1st</p>
<p>Audruicq : nothing much doing : paid 20 francs in afternoon : went tom theatre with Gill after tea &amp; it was a good show too : lady impersonaters very good &amp; one had a splendid voice into the bargain : air raid at night :</p>
