 Quite a treat to get somewhere where it is a bit quiet 
 Wednesday 20.6.17 Slight disturbance between a couple of our Sergeants.  Parade preparatory to inspection by G.O.C. to-morrow.  Two N.C.Os &amp; 22 men went on leave to Amiens.  One of our men placed under mental observation; general opinion is that he is working his ticket. 
 Thursday 21.6.17 Inspection postponed owing to rain till to-morrow.  Acting postman for the day.  Played Baseball in the evening.
