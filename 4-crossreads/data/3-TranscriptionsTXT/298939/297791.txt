 Monday 25th June Fovant We arrived in Fovant Camp at midday on Friday &amp; found everything ready for us &ndash; even beds made The position is not nearly so bad as we had been led to expect, &amp; our actual camp is rather good. Lord Werley [?] reviewed the 16th Brigade on Saturday &amp; the Ambulance was adjudged the smartest on parade
