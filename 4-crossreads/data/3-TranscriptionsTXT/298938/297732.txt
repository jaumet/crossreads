 getting back to the 3rd &amp; incidentally the dispensary job, is not great. Never mind, keep a good heart &amp; all will be well.  I've left for the front again &amp; am quite prepared for any misfortune that may come my way.  My good luck in living so long counteracts any hard luck I have had in not getting promotion A la victoire. 
 23rd March Unexpectedly put on draft yesterday. Handed in all spare kit today. On guard from 5 p.m. yesterday till 5 p.m. today.
