  
 Jan 13th The rain came down in torrents all night & has continued to day accompanied by heavy lightening The Bdge in Bivouacs (Bivvys) were washed out & are having a miserble time. We are now attached to desert Coys again 
 Jan 14th Heavy rains & thunderstorms continued through the night but cleared into glorious sun shine during the morning Cpt H___ secured two rooms to day for  the use of del  his photographic requirements close to hand & we spent part of the day fitting them up The new workrooms are part of a house occupied by a Yiddish family
