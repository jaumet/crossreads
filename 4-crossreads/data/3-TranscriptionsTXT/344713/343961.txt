Sun. Aug. 12th: -Out to Sec-Bois near Vieux-Berquin.
Fri. Aug 17th: -The 3rd Anniversary of formation of the 4th Bn.  Had a bit of a sports meeting.
Sun. Sept. 9th: -With Lt. Leich left the Bn. & got train to Hazebrouch to Etaples & out up at the rest Camp there.We are going to a Signal School near Albert.Big riots are going on here over a "Redcap" shooting a Scotchman.They are making a start in killing off all the "Redcaps" they can lay their hands on.
Mon. Sept. 10th : -Left by train to Albert & went a few Kilometres out to Aveluy & joined the 1st Anzac Signal School. Here for a few weeks.
Sun. Sept. 16th.Down to Amiens for a trip
