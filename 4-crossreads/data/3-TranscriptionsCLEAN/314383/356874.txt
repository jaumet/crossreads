May 19Rested all day.  Have absolutely gone in the hip.  The march proved extremely severe owing to the heat & bad track. Food during the first stage to Kirmanshah, from there onwards we dispensed with the Gen ration of bully, biscuits & cheese & lived on the country. 1 a day is allowed to officers & 10/- to Sgts.  Capt Hooper was appointed Q.M. & his work in procuring food is of the highest order.
