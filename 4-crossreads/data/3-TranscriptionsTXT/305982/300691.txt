21-5-16Usual work this afternoon today  Beautiful day cool breeze blowing
22.5.16Last last night Co order to parade 6 oclock full marching order which we dida good turn out morning parade 9.00 in mess shed musketry instruction.  afternoon parade Batt full marching order going through drill etc for inspection day hellish dusty now at Batt parade. beautiful sunset. [indecipherable]23.5.16received order last night that Batt would be inspected by B. General Monash men look great in full marching order with Aust clothing & helmets.  General very pleased at the way same was turned out.  Gave the men a good speech about discplin disciplline. when they get to France which will be very soon. 6 oclock Parade 10 oclock we fell in & had a swimming Parade  it was beautiful in.
