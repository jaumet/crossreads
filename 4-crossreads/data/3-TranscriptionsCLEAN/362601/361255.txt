  
 22 August, Friday 
 Catch 10 pm boat & do not feel as tired as expected. 
 23 August, Saturday 
 Elizabeth Dee Why at 7.30 pm. 
 Work in garden all afternoon. Go to Manly with M Paul & ring Mrs Preddy. Meet two Miss Thomas'. Go to evening. Meet nice people. Play. Meet George ventriloquist. Come home with Mrs Moran, only two in tram. Meet Mr Wiltshire.
