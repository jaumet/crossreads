<p>wants a little spare money, nuf sed. I am above worrying over trifles like that now as I said before life is short. Now go back to the beginning of this paragraph re you being right in a certain supposition.<br/>In a few days there is to be a big upheaval in the "orientation department" &amp; a couple of stars are going to fall my way, so if the unforseen doesnt happen I will be a full blown Lieut, by the time this reaches you, dont mention the fact to the other members of the family, for in case it misses I dont want to be made a fool of by having it spread over the country &amp; then not come off, I will cable &amp; let you know.<br/>I am in a very queer position now "acting as officer" still a Sergeant but messing &amp; living with the other officers of Company, the promotion itself has only to come through Divisional Orders &amp; the thing is done.<br/>My career through this Battalion is varied &amp; slightly interesting:- volunteered as a private soldier then Corporal &amp; during the probationary stages in Kensington, I was reduced to Lance Corporal &amp; next day again made Corporal, wounded three times, rejoined &amp; promoted Lance-Sergeant</p>
