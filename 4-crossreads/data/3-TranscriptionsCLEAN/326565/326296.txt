Sunday 30/1/16Church parade conducted by Capt Cranston, (Chaplin 29th Btn)Caught ration boat & again went to Ismailia, about 11 miles distance up Canal.  Very pleasant trip; Ismailia a very pretty place.  Caught train leaving Ismailia about 10 pm, arrived back in camp 12 pm.
