  
 February 1918 Tuesday 5 
 On the road - and Messines. Left our billets at 8.30am and arrived at Daylight Corner via Dranoutie and Bailleul at 12.30pm. Warned to leave for guns immediately on arrival. Packed my kit and went up with gun and Kempe . Arrived at S.O.S. gun at 2 pm and at pill box at 5 pm. Took up the 10 till 12 watch. Weather wet Have a fine dug-out almost 5.9 proof.
