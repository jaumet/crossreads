The weather is still holding fine, it was a little cloudy today but nice &amp; warm, just right for us. 24th. Nothing much doing today except for the usual bayonet &amp; rifle drill, &amp; physical exercise first thing in the morning. Mr Graham our officer wants me to take the sec. of our Platoon.
