  
 September 1917 Mon 3  This is a very decent place on the top of a strongly fortified hill it was to have been the last line of defence before Calias had the Germans broken through at Ypres Tuesday 4th There is plenty of work to do here we have to be ready for 1000 patients in 6 weeks.  The big town of St Homer [Saint- Omer] is about 5 miles from here  Fritz raided there last night Wednesday 5th Still toiling Thursday 6th Took a stroll to one of the numerous villages this evening Friday 7th All P.B's went back to Hazebrouck today to go before another Medical Board Saturday 8th I landed the long looked for C. today so will be going to England in a fortnight & then on to N.Z.    went back to the farm this evening
