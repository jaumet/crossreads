<p>27th June  Tuesday:-<br/>From letters received from London it seems rather certain that we are all going to be packed off to the front when we reach there. This will do me. A few of those originally chosen seem <strike>rath</strike> inclined to prefer staying in Egypt on strength of this, so there is a chance for me, after all. To-day is cool, only 98 &deg; . After a short stroll &amp; writing a few letters, retire.</p>
<p>28th June:- Wednesday<br/>More rumours, but it is evident we are off to the front after a short stay in England. This only serves to show what a mistake the general idea of the inexperienced in Australia make. One never knows the time &amp;  we just do as we are told &amp;  go</p>
