
1915Jan. 30 Sat.Number of patients in Hospital up to this morning was 630. The number of patients to be taken in as laid down in Military regulations is 520.Paid 12/- in piastres.Worked until 10.45 p.m.Deputation of 3 men paraded before the O.C. in connection with the supply of tucker to the camp - The food is poor & scanty & it is quite likely that the Quartermaster (Capt. Gray) & his storemen are rooking us - Wait for developments - an inquiry will be held.
