
October 1915
Wednesday 6
goes round the hill parallel with the trenches then leaving the road turns to the left towards the sea, & sharply down. Thence it is downhill all the way to a road which leads to the sap before mentioned.
Total distance about 1 mile.
A bearer has to keep going on either of these tracks under heavy shrapnel fire for 24 hours or more when a charge is on, dark or light.
Thus he does his bit.
