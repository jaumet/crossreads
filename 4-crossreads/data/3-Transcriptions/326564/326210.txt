An odd girl or two of questionable character, old Jock was narked  because they had no beer for dinner only wine, the service was very slow &amp; it took us about 3/4 of an hour to get through 3 courses, we left then for it was getting late, &amp; we had to get back to call our rolls, 5 francs each was the damage &amp; Graham stood the lot, the streets were full of drunks &amp; we meet the noble Joe Brisset being dragged along as drunk as a lord, quite a number of houses are smashed in the city,</p>
