 27 August 1918 Tuesday Just the same old ship routine. Reported things going well on all Fronts "Albert" re-captured &amp; British troops advanced well well forward of that position &ndash; taking fair number prisoners. 
 1918 August 28 Wednesday Making good progress in fine weather &amp; favourable Sea Good news still coming through from all Fronts Good advances and many prisoners. Concert at night.
