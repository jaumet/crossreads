<p>Sunday 22nd<br/>Marched up Town at 10 A.M.<br/>Wandered about untill lunch &amp; then went to Camp Bay via the Kloop.  This is the better way to go as far as sceniery is conserned for while the Town &amp; in sight the Tram is ascending &amp; travelling comparitively slowly. Traveling the opposite way one goes to fast to take it all in. Found a free Buffet &amp; of course ait Went on the Pier in evening it seems t be cheefely for amusemet purposes</p>
<p>Monday 23rd<br/>Went through gardens rather small &amp; not particularly good also through Museum a fine colection of stuffed animals especially Deer of all sorts &amp; sizes never thought there were so many different kinds also good show of minerals. The plaster figures of native Bushmen are most life like &amp; very interesting an interesting place but did not take time to</p>
