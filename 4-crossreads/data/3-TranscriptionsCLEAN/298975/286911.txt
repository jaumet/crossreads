  
 Tuesday 19/6/17 Wether still bad. This Boat is very fond of shipping water in a small way & many have got good wettings to day Canteen opened 
 Wed 20 The rougest night we have had so far waves comeing right over the bows. I am on guard today 
 Thursday  21 Came of guard this morning. Just the same old thing as Liverpool. Only not quite as much style. No arms are carried & the relief cannot march very well up & down gang ways
