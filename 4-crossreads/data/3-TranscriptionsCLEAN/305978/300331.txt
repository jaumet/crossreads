  
 19th/ A lovely day, poor fellow of the 4th Reinft 19 Batt fell down a hold & was picked up badly injured he died at 8pm. innoculated for the 4th time life boat drill. 
 23/ Boxing tournament opened cleaned up for gaurd 
 24/ Saw more sharks & whales. Sighted two vessels at four oclock one looked like transport & the other a torpedo boat destroyer four-thirty sighted land passed the two brothers & one large Island; two vessels travelling behind us about about four miles distance
