<p>Now I'll finish my story about my stay at Grimsby the first part of which I wrote in a letter to Mum.  I did not get up until 10 a.m. every morning.  It is the first time in my life that I have stayed in bed so late on two mornings in succession.  One morning I did not get up till 11 a.m. &amp; had my Breakfast &amp; dinner together.  It was very cold on Sunday &amp; J. &amp; I did not leave the Fireside.  On Monday I went up to see Mrs. Melhuish, who is the sister of Mrs. Horner, Ida's next door neighbour.  I went alone as Jinnie had work to do.  They gave me a lovely time.  The</p>
