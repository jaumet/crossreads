  
 F 9 Sun Accept for one [indecipherable] 
 Recd letters from Maisie. Mem & Dulce. Develop prints in afternoon. Go on Bike all around Tank Hill & Amesbury with Tam. We both get very wet. 
 F 10 Mon Go to [Hurdeoh?] return to opened evening with me & Mrs Edmonds Home a nice time but train journey Home is very distracting. Recd letter from Esme. 
 F 11 Tues. Go to see Char. Chap. In 'Tillies Punctured Romance'. It is very good. Read all remainder of night Find Red Cross Tobacco which Mrs Mills sent Lawson [Creek's?] F 12 Wed Played Cricket. We win by 3 runs. A disgusting affair and foolishly throw my wicket away for 3 runs. Go to Waac's concert which is not held. No more Waacs concerts for me. [Mudgee?]. 
 F 13 Th Catch first Train. Arr Grantham 6pm & chase RTO all around town. Reach [Gyal?] 9.10pm. Go to bed at 1am. I have 8 changes of trains on way up. 
 F 14 Fri Lovely Day. Stay Home all morning & afternoon. Go to Cleethorpes on   [indecipherable] [Comet?] get a partner to [indecipherable]. Go to theatre afterwards. See Parker's appeal. 
 F 15 Sat Leave Gy at 12 arr London 5.10. Catch 5.50 for B'ford  & reach it at 9 pm. Have nice Travelling companions all the way. On arrival Home recd letter from Dad. Doris. Marie & Marion Clarke. Go to bed [indecipherable]  11 pm do supper. I learn that [indecipherable] choc. & Sweets  &  Book O.K.
