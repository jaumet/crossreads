<p>Royal Herbert Hospital<br/>Woolwich  19 June 1917</p>
<p>Dear Mr Ferguson</p>
<p>Your two letters duly arrived &amp; I am glad to hear you are all well at your end. Am sorry to say my work in France came to rather a premature end &amp; I am now at above military hospital.
