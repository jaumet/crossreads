Numerous collections of perpendicular poles, denotes the presence of hopfields.  The hill of Mont de Katz is practically the only large hill to be seen on the landscape & that is slightly wooded & very pretty  --  some 8 miles from here I should think. The guns can be heard in a desultory fashion all day, & at night the horizon is lit up from time to time by flashes.  I have not yet been able to get my bearings.
