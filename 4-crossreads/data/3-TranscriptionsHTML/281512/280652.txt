<p>10th September  Monday<br/>I cannot help writing of the beautiful evening of yesterday. Lately we have had a few good days, but their aesthetic charm has been lost by hostile bombing. Yesterday evening was unusually calm, &amp; the sun set like a great golden halo. From my hut there is a charming view up the hill, through a long avenue of fine trees &amp; on the hill brow stands the mill of which there are great numbers in this locality.</p>
<p>The surrounding lands are in full cultivation &amp; the gathered in wheat is standing in long rows ready for ricking. For some unusual reason the guns are quiet this evening, &amp; the whole outlook is one of serene peace.
