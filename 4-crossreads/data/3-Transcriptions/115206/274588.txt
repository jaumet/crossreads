<p>January 1918<br/>Friday 18</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Heavy fall of snow during the night.<br/>Smith and Thompson arrived home at 5 am from Paris and slept until 5 pm.<br/>On Battery Orderly today . and in spare time wrote "Sounds of the Night " <br/>Bomdr Bennet went to Hdqs and Dr Fitzpatrick took his place in the office.<br/>Had several long runs during the night.</p>
