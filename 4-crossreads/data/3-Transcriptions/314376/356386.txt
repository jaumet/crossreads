<p>75<br/>with wire mats with which to effect a passage over the wire and, covered with a bombardment, left our trenches and started for the German line. We had a big number of guns quartered round and the noise was something terrific especially when Fritz started to reply.</p>
<p>We had been hard at work piling sandbags and reinforcing the dugout and it was well for us that we did this as they were a great protection to us from the German grenades which were thrown across with the aid of rifles.</p>
<p>The raiders eventually came back, I think they were only away for a matter of 10 mints.</p>
