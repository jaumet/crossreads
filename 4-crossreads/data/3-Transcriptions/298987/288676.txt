<p>from the Town &amp; Hillside Formed into double line with Apric leading this afternoon. We are the center boat in the left hand line</p>
<p>Saturday 28th<br/>Am ships Orderly C/pl today</p>
<p>Sunday 29th<br/>Usual programme. The sea is like glass &amp; the six big boats just seem to be at anchor so well do they keep in line<br/>The lines are about &frac12; a mile apart &amp; half that distance between boats in line. Only one very small light shows on the stern of each boat No smoking is allowed on deck &ndash; a very necessary precaution as it is suppriseing how clearly one can see a lighted match or cigarette on any of the other boats</p>
