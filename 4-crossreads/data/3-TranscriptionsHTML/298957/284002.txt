<p>30th April (Sunday)<br/>Lovely morning. Church Parade</p>
<p>1st May (Monday)<br/>Fine Day. Rail Head on Dental Leave &amp; finishing off the Mess Shed</p>
<p>2nd May (Tuesday) Fine.<br/>Orders read out about Gen. Townshend surrendering with 2900 British &amp; 6000 Indians. Squad &amp; stretcher Drill and practise at carrying patients by the "Fireman's Lift". March over desert for sandbags. Infantry having a bombing attack on trenches close by</p>
