<p>[Pages 54 to 62 blank]<br/>March 1917<br/>Monday 26<br/>Very foggy morning did not lift until 9am<br/>First operations at Gaza 10th LH in reserve &ndash; going in-<br/>Standing to arms all night moved at 2 am .10th as rearguard to the brigade. Major Dangar does some good work</p>
<p>Tuesday 27<br/>Retired from Gaza shelled by the Turks also fired upon with rifles. One of our planes came down in flames. Almost surrounded and captured.<br/>Got back safely thanks to Genl Roystons leadership. Total British losses &ndash; 13000 and Gaza not taken.</p>
