<p>I bought a pretty tray-cloth &ndash; but did not have time to buy anymore. Thought it may be useful if not for myself for home &ndash; We then went to see St John's Church &ndash; The most wonderful church I have ever been in. The paintings are some of the oldest    &amp; best in the world &amp; the marble &amp; other work most interesting.</p>
