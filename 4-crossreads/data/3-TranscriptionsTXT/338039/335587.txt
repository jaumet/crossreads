 Mar.22nd. No reveille necessary.  Spend all day in train (1/4 mile long) &ndash; Pass through Lyons. Dijon. Avignon, and other large towns- fine pastures- villages- orchards. farms. vineyards   Regularity &amp; beauty of trees &amp; hedges- Apparent absence of all menfolk.  Women &amp; girls on farms &amp; in offices-   Enthusiastic welcomes by French. Mar 23rd. B &amp; B- Very cold- no sleep- Now in Nth. of France- agricultural country- Souvenirs requested by French-
