<p>Tuesday Oct 9th</p>
<p>Dartford : helped to clean up the ward : cold day : inspected by Doctor &amp; marked for Weymouth at the end of the week : paid 3/6 after dinner</p>
<p>Wednesday Oct 10th</p>
<p>Dartford : helped to clean up : stroll to village with Monty after dinner &amp; watched an entertainment in the Y.M.C.A. by a sailor from Holland (interned) : pretty fair : met Snowy Pearson, Bill Richardson &amp; Morrie who have come in during the day : good talk to Morrie after tea</p>
<p>Thursday Oct 11th</p>
<p>Dartford : drew my khaki in afternoon : concert in Hall after tea &amp; was very good indeed</p>
