<p>&amp; they put me snug into one of them, &amp; there I stopped until the train reached  Rouen, hanged if it was just snowing again when we were taken out &amp; driven to the hospital in motors again.  I finally landed in No. 6 an English hospital &amp; I never wish to get in one again they are absolutely rotten &amp; the tucker is worse I nearly starved during my few days there &amp; if they hadn't let me out when they did I would have bolted &amp;</p>
