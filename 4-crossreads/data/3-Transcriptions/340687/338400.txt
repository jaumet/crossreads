<p>April, 1917. Good Friday. Friday 6<br/>Usual sort of a day. Raining and miserable. Engine not working well. Pumped twice &amp; saw football in afternoon. Hodgson spent night with me on way back to company from applying in A.F.C. Spent pleasant evening &amp; he lent me 10f &amp; some bread.</p>
