  
 16 Nov 16 Went out & done sub-calibre firing. H.M.A.S. Sydney came in today to join up one of the squadrons. 
 17 Nov 16 Coaled ship. Took in 230 tons average 181.5 tons 
 18 Nov 16 Anchored at Rosyth 
 19 Nov 16 Anchored at Rosyth. Admiral Goodenough went back to his own ship the H.M.S. Southhampton. Melbourne was flagship a fortnight. 
 20 Nov 16 Anchored at Rosyth 
 21 Nov 16 Heaved anchor at 4 pm & accompanied by H.M.S. Dublin & some Destroyers proceeded to sea.
