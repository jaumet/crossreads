 Monday 9/7/17 Umpiring for Div Scheme, going all day, very tired. Tuesday 10/7/17 Field Firing, Pow Wow of Umpires during the afternoon. Wednesday 11/7/17 Training continued, saw Bas, went to 28th Bn concert. Thursday 12/7/17 Reconnoitred ground for scheme tomorrow with all officers. Friday 13/7/17 Brigade Scheme, General sent us to repeat where ignorance is bliss etc &ndash; Saturday 14/7/17 Corps School near Albert for demonstration very good &ndash; won a rifle match.
