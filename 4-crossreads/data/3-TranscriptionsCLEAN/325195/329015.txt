
20.11.15Sat 20thWrote a letter this morning & had a busy time this afternoon while on duty, had to take a message to the beach master: also run down the O.C. of the Sqds to show them routine orders & then Post them up on the board; Everything same as usual & the sea calm again today: Today has been a little chilly
21.11.15Sun 21stThe German aviator made a little mistake today he dropped a bomb meant for one of our trenches on Walkers, the bomb was badly aimed & dropped neatly in the Turks; Francis who has been in  England turned up here today, he says he had the time of his life in England; Today has been very cold
Mon 22nd
