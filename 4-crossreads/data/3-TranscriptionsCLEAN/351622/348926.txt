  
 22 November 1916 Wed The [indecipherable] evacuate sick with [indecipherable]. Finished our game of [indecipherable] 6 am. after all night sitting lost 5 francs. Doug Hall & Smith leave - wrote to Mother & Ossie. Terribly cold, busy night. Bearers return. 
 23 November 1916 Thur Leave to Amiens starts about sixteen men a day. Have all day there. Put about 200 sick & wounded through & then sat down to the poker school in the dressing tent. Paid this morning. Very foggy night.
