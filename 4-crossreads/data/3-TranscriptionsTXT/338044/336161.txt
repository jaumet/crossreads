 We are continually having scraps here with advanced parties of Turks, whom we have expected to arrive for months &amp; even to-day, nine Turks    &amp; one Bedouin Arab were captured &amp; I was accompanied by them to Cairo. After tramping round the sand for miles in search of 5th Division Hdqus, I eventually found it way down on the banks of the Canal, near the light railway run out to the front line of trenches. Whilst here I inspected the trenches well walking through &amp; over them  The net result of my trouble was to get small satisfaction having delayed so long &amp; on return to Cairo, my application was returned to me definitely refused
