 Naval Signal 
 To - Flag&nbsp; 
 From - Captain Travers 
 Date - 24 9 14 
 Capt Travers has returned with the Chief Official of the place with his staff. &nbsp;The governor is inland&nbsp;&amp; cannot be communicated with at present. &nbsp;This chief official assures the Staff Officer that the place is undefended&nbsp;&amp; there are&nbsp;no mines. &nbsp;He wishes to see the Administrator and original of terms of capitulation. &nbsp;I am sending him to Berrima now.
