<p>Fri Dec 22nd &ndash; 16<br/>I am sure I wont sit down and cry when I crawl out of this possie. Today it was my turn to drag the water and it was only fate which helped me across the road, a matter of seconds before a beautiful "iron-jelloid" landed killing 6 horses and blowing a wagon to bits Some hot place this believe me.</p>
