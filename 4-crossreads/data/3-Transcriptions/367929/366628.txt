<p>May 1915<br/>Wore our equipment. Few Australians and NZ all past recognition and removal.</p>
<p>25 Tuesday<br/>Down to Anzac Cove with Clark for the swim I have daily. Had b</p>
<p>reakfast after coming out, on the beach. HMS Triumph blown up by a torpedo off the shore.</p>
<p>26 Wed<br/>Oceassional stands to arms. Swim under bullet fire. Had 1 killed and 2 wounded by shots down Monash Valley.</p>
