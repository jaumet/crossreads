<p>The other lot of snaps of Anzac have been despatched some time ago.</p>
<p>Later 10.3.16  Have just quarter of an hour to lunch so will finish this off. No home mails arrived yet.</p>
<p>Things are settled now as regards postings in this Division. I am to remain as Adjutant of the 11th Bde, so I suppose it wont be long before you hve to address me "Captain T.W.G." The three Brigadiers in this Division are Majors Hughes, King &amp; Rabett, with a commander for the Howitzer Bde still to be appointed.</p>
<p>Address me in   <strike>future</strike> until further notice .</p>
<p>Lieut T.W.Garling<br/>Adjutant   11th Field Arty Brigade<br/>4th Aust Divn<br/>Intermediate Base Depot<br/>Egypt</p>
<p>Must knock off now. Fondest love to yourselves &amp; Pat from your loving son Terence.</p>
