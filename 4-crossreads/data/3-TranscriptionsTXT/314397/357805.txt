Saturday 10.8.18Started work on the main road at 1.45pm &amp; knocked off at 8pm. Had tons of work to do as the traffic now is tremendous &amp; old shellholes &amp; new ones all require a lot of filling in. Just before going out Bill Cowper came along to see me. He looks well. Saw the tanks go over, just north of us, also saw the infantry. Gee it looked well. They got all their objectives too. Saw Les Dean &amp; Chas.
