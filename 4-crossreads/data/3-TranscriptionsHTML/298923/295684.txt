<p>18 January 1918 Friday<br/>Speaking about Venus I was watching our Shells bursting away two Taubes when the star caught my eye- at midday!!!<br/><strike> I hope you are all in Melbourne now at Mrs Jack Austins</strike><br/>Moved on to <strike><u>RICHON-LE-ZION</u></strike> which means the new Zion- not far from <strike>Jaffa.</strike><br/>This town the Jews are shorter for food- NO SUGAR, TEA or COFFEE- Tea is made out of dried figs &amp; sweetened with JAM-   Coffee-burnt MAIZE.</p>
