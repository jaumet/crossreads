
<p>Jan. Friday 21 1916</p>
<p>Got final leave from the camp about 2 pm. I&nbsp;got two berths to Quirindi in 8.30 pm train &amp; Linda &amp; self came home together. My word, I felt proud of myself taking Linda about &amp; getting things ect&nbsp;for her. M<sup>r&nbsp;</sup>Ronald came up in the same train.</p>
