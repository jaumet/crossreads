<p>February, 1915<br/>Sunday 14<br/>Birthday in Camp, branded all Horse Rugs<br/>In the afternoon went to an old part of the Desert where Napoleon buried many slain &amp; the Tommies were digging up the bones to get earrings, rings, etc:- bones skulls were very plentiful but ghoulish work especially as Smallpox Kills a good many.<br/>An Australasian would be acceptable now &amp; again if you would post me one I'll let you know if they arrive.</p>
