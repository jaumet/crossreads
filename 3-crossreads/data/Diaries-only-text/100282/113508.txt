22nd. Very windy this morning a light misty rain started to fall but not heavy enough to drive us in off parade worse luck, after the usual stunts on the parade ground, we marched down to Meteren done some more wiring got our hands gloves tore about in the process. Howard came back from school this morning so we are well stocked with Sgts once again.
Pay day so we can look out for a few lively nights for there is bound to be some pretty stunting
