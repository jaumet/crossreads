 Thursday 27 July 1916 Trench digging again in morning. Lecture &amp; bomb throwing in evening. Had to parade in evening and go to station to unload horses and bring to camp. 
 Friday 28 July 1916 Bomb throwing.  Lecture &amp; trench attacks.  On guard for 1 hour &amp; a half in evening as Infantry were rioting prior to leaving for France.  Voulenteered for Machine Gun section.
