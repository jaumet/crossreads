<p>28 November, Friday</p>
<p>We buy more films. Very busy all day[?] O &amp; I are real ill.</p>
<p>29 November, Saturday</p>
<p>Go down meet Gibby. Man falls overboard &amp; I break V. Roxy plate. Dig in garden in the wet &amp; dress to visit Ellis in the evening. Meet Mrs Hancock. Also visit Eunice &amp; Muriel. Hilda comes Home but I miss her.</p>
