<p>Tuesday Jan 11th My  Birthday (23rd)<br/>a very calm day : hot : Revellie 6 am : congratulations from my cobbers (Birthday) &amp; Just make a small sailing boat we had passed early in the morning : parade for physical drill 10 am : attended physical class after tea : slept on deck : no land sighted &amp; only the one boat as above :</p>
<p>Wednesday Jan 12th<br/>A hot windy day : Hazy &amp; a big swell on : We were supposed to have passed through the straits of Babel Mandeb early hours of morning. All day we kept<br/>Revellie 6 am</p>
