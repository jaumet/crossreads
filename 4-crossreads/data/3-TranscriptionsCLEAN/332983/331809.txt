A terrific bombardment from the Asiatic side 6in shells buzzing about. One shell in particular bounced over 3rd Btys lines. A guard is mounted over it at present, waiting for the Naval authorities to destroy it.A Naval pennice was towing a barge when Asia opened fire on her. They put one shot into the River Clyde. It was hot and sweet while it lasted I thought we were done for.
