<p>Several paragraphs have recently appeared in English papers, re the original men going home on Australian furlough.  It is stated that Pearce has already promised it on condition he gets 5000 recruits to fill the places.  We regard it with grave suspicion and fancy it is only an underhand way of obtaining recruits.  Anyway there are not 5000 originals left of the first division now, they are slowly becoming extinct as they must certainly do in time.</p>
