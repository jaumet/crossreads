<p>some shells droped close &amp; we ducked for a <strike>for a</strike> Tunnel near by started again &amp; once more He put his shells in Us for the Tunnell again <strike>Got at</strike> it again &amp; finished of quick &amp; lively. Another chap &amp; I were last to leave &amp; just as we got on the Tram track a gass shell droped plomp just close so we ran for it &amp; the next one dropped just behind my mate. It kept us moveing, but they started to drop pretty thick in front so we stoped a bit &amp; then went on</p>
