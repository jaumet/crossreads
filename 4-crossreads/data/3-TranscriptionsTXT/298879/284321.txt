 January 1915 Tuesday 26 Mounted Canal Guard 8.45 A.M. Inspected by W.O. Lowe. Marched to our post. Natives to cook our meals, Engineering party practising bridge building. 
 Wednesday 27 Dismounted from Canal Guard. Had a holiday for the rest of the day. Very windy. Clouds of sand blowing everywhere.
