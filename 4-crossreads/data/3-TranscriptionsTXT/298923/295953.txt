
14 October 1918 MondayJerusalem   66 Casualty Clearing Hqs arrived here midday-   "Tummy" still on strike but feel better goodness knows where we go next-Great rumours of peace but I believe nothing-   All Hospitals & trains full so stop here -for present-Heard our Brigade is coming back to Richon way soon to reorganise so expect will join up there later on-This Hos. overlooks the Walls of old Jerusalem & was a Monastery-   SISTERS here.
