<p>Monday 3rd [Dec]<br/>Very heavy frosts.  All pools frozen this morning &amp;  trying to snow.  It did a little.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4th<br/>Another heavy frost this morning.  At football the A Team defeated B Team by 16 &ndash; 0.  The B Team were outclassed   I played my first game today with B Team &amp; didn't have anything to do.  Saw Les Powers this afternoon. He is with 15 [?] Fld Amb &amp; also Sammy Doyle.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5th<br/>Concert by our own boys.  Very good.  First appearance of our orchestra.</p>
<p>Thursday 6th<br/>Football practice &amp; at night Cribbage tournament.  Weather very cold &amp; everything frozen pools [indecipherable] etc.</p>
<p>BOMBING RAIDS BY GOTHAS</p>
<p>Friday 7th  Saturday 8th<br/>Played football this afternoon against ASC Team (D Cunningham was playing) &amp; won by 6 &ndash; 5.  Two goals by Billy Brown against a converted try.  I played but did not shape at all well at full back.</p>
<p>Sunday 9th<br/> Monday 10th<br/>Fritz was over us bombing with his Gothas this afternoon &ndash; five or six of them &amp; he distributed a few papers.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11th<br/>Polling day.  Reinforcements Referendum.  I voted  "yes"</p>
<p>Wednesday 12th<br/>A bad day</p>
