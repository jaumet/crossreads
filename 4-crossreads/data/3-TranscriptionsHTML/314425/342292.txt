We do not expect to leave the ship until late in the afternoon for there are 5 hospital ships ahead of us, &amp; it will take some time for them to unload.<br/>We had a first class breakfast this morning at 8. am.<br/>We were ordered to sleep in our life belts last night. Yesterday's Daily Mail prints the the wireless telegrams that passed between  the German General staff &amp; Marshall Foch on Thursday,</p>
