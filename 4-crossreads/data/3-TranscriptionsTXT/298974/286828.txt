 Well mother dear, the mail closes immediately and I must wrap this up and post it at once. I'm always thinking of you all, and have been getting some lovely letters from you all &ndash; even Bill. Fathers papers come regularly &amp; I thing all the letters too. Do write regularly to Pvt. Fry, 4992 a D Coy. 3rd Btn. A.I.F. Abroad.
