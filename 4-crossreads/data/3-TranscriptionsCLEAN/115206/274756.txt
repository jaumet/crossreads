  
 July 1918 Friday 5 
 Vaux sur Somme.1st Battery Position At 1am this morning Villery Brettoneaux and Hamel were burning. Captures in this Sector now mount to 100 M.E. and 42 Officers, as well as the prisoners and material captured yesterday. Very quiet throughout the day, had a few minutes fishing, received a letter from Toots. Weather glorious.at 10.40 pm Fritz counter attacked and we received the S.O.S. the attack failed. At 2.35 am Fritz again counter attacked, we received another S.O.S.  Attack failed
