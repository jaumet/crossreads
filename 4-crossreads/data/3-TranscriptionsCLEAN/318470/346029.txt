  
 Sunday 27 August 1916 A quiet day - only a little bombardment on each side.  Usual work strengthening positions. 
 Monday 28 August 1916 Heavy Artillery Bombardment of enemy line.  Eighth Brigade made a Raid at night.  No success.  One casualty. 
 Tuesday 29 August 1916 Very heavy rain.  Usual work on Dugouts & generally strengthening positions.  Rain impairing work. 
 Wednesday 30 August 1916 Nothing unusual.  Fair amount of Artillery Fire on both side. 
 Thursday 31 August 1916 Still on Revetting work & strengthening positions generally.  Nothing unusual. 
 Friday 1 September 1916 Uneventful, a few Bombs thrown from both sides. 
 Saturday 2 September 1916 General bombardment on both side.  Usual revetting & strengthening work. 
 Sunday 3 September 1916 Large Aerial Fleet fleeting to be seen over Enemy position.  Said to have bombarded Line near Lille. 
 Monday 4 September 1916 A good deal of Artillery preparation on both side.  Work going on as usual.  Showery droopy weather. 
 Tuesday 5 September 1916 Very heavy showers making work very difficult.  Fairly heavy bombardment both sides. 
 Wednesday 6 September 1916 Still very showery.  Continuing work revetting & strengthening generally.  Fairly heavy Artillery Firing. 
 Thursday 7 September 1916 Heavy bombardment by our Artillery & trench Howitzers.  Fairly heavy retaliation by Enemy Guns. 
 Friday 8 September 1916 Short heavy bombardment by our Guns about 5 p.m.  Nice fine day.  Work as usual strengthening. 
 Saturday 9 September 1916 Usual Artillery activity & stokes Howitzers.  Still revetting & strengthening.
