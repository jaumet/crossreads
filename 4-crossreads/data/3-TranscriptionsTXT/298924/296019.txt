 Thursday 6 February 1919 Rafa Major Irwin our C.O. promoted Lt. Col. vice Granville to England. Heavy sandstorm preceded rain &ndash; Y.M.C.A. blown down.  Busy writing Regimental History &amp; arranging try-outs for Gymkhana.  Cold nights &amp; my cold bad. Just got word cannot travel with wife &amp; Regt. sails about 21st Feby. &ndash; but still nothing definite - &amp; wife may be allowed to sail with me. Love to all.
