
War Diary
[Sketch map of Turk positions around Canterbury Slope by J.M. Antill dated 24.9.1915]
1915  24th Sept.Inspected Trenches with C.O.R.E.  again shelled by our own 18 prs. - third time in last 4 days - also by enemy at 1730, both Bivouacs and Trenches - One man killed and 3 wounded - former by our own guns - Working incessantly to improve our trenches and saps but are very short-handed and men dropping out each day.
