<p>Our raiding party is off  the 4 th Batt are going to do it.<br/>There was a rather pretty sunrise on the frost bitten fields of Picardy this morning.<br/>9th  Last night we left our comfortable dugouts &amp; tramped off across the snow covered ground to another lot of trenches which we were going to occupy for our next stage which would be "close supports", the Bombers &amp; No. 12 had their dugouts in &amp; along an old sunken road, as soon as we got settled down out they hauled us for "ration fatigue"  went down to the dump, waited an hour then it struck someone that it would be a good hour before the trucks would be here so off we had to go, how we swore, while waiting down comes the Capt.
