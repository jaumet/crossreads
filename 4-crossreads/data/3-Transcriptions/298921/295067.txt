<p>April, 1915<br/>Sunday 4<br/>Went to the oldest obelisk in the world at old Heliopolis created by rameses II &amp; brought from Luxor, one solid piece of Granite 3000 B.C.<br/>Near here is the Virgins Tree where she rested with Joseph &amp; also the old Church with Dado showing Josephs flight x Egypt-<br/>Terrific Dust storm.</p>
