<p>Dear Mr. Layton &ndash;<br/>I am sending on Mrs. Carey wife of Jack Tarbut Carey 1857 15 Batt. returned wounded &amp; left again for the front 9th R. 14 Batt.</p>
<p>She will explain her case to you as it is somewhat unusual.</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>B. Booth.</p>
