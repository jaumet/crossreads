 February, 1916 Saturday 5 The men for next school march in at 10 to 12 am. Write letter home. 
 Sunday 6 have a muster parade this morning &amp; try to sort the mob out a bit At 9 pm several hundred men march in from Tel-el-Kebir &amp; Ismalia Meet several; old cobbers two are now officers.
