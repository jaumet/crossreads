 SUNDAY, JANUARY 12, 1919. 
 Fine. Poss &amp; I &amp; a few more 
 went for a walk round the 
 &quot;Row&quot; in the morning. I was 
 at the flat all the afternoon 
 and evening. Alan got 
 word to return &amp; motored 
 up to Upavon in the morning. 
 Had dinner at Hatchetts 
 at night.
