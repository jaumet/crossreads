
Saturday 12 August 1916Still waiting.Took my boots off for first time & found my feet still there.6th & 7th Regts. passed in evening going back to camp.
Sunday 13 August 1916Left Ogratina in morning and came back to camp.  Lovely to change & have a wash again.Colin Sams came over in evening.  Heard that we are to be sent back for a rest.
