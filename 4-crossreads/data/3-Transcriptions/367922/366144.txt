<p>Mch. 1 1890</p>
<p>Dear Arthur,<br/>I am organising another trip to Caddily for the coming Easter and shall be glad if Miss Macmanamy &amp; yourself would form a part of the company, which will be of the usual number say, about six ladies &amp; eight gentlemen. The arrangements will be the same as on the last visit when you were present, the ladies only exs. being the train fare. If you</p>
