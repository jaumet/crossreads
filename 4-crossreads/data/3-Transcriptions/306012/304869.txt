<p>23rd <strike>April</strike> March 1918 (Sat.)<br/>We occupied several old houses on the outskirts of the village about 3 or 4 hundred yards from the old spot.<br/>To-day we evacuated the remainder of the patients and removed equipment from the shelled camp.</p>
<p>24th March 1918 (Sund.)<br/>Fritz kept putting stuff over but none on us, although it meant work for all.<br/>News has come through that the Germans have commenced a big attack on the Somme and are forcing the British back.  We are getting ready for an early move.</p>
