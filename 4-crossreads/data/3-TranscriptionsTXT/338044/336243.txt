 an hour of cross-examining, whilst I stuck to my point &amp; eventually the Colonel agreed to send on my application &amp; recommend it to Lieut-Col. McGlynn of 5th Division at the front. It now remains for him to sanction it    &amp; I shall have my chance. Colonel Griffiths I consider, treated me most considerately in this matter &amp; I am really indebted to him  A few weeks more &amp; I shall probably be at the front. We, being unsettled, (Legge    &amp; I) dine at another nuisance of a restaurant &amp; after a very poor meal (French customs as usual) we pay 2 francs, 30 centimes for it. Have got home early &amp; now off to bed 9-30 pm.
