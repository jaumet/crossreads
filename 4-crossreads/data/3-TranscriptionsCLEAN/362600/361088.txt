
F 2 SunWalk Durrington to church. It is very hot. Home drink with Mrs Seawood.Come home have bath & write & read all nightWrote MumF 3 MonWe have a long talk at night about my ["unnaturalness"?] I go & watch cricket match & read all night. Dream all night SW - [sketch of heart] -ING [indecipherable]
F 4 Tues.Ride to Sarum. Fix bike go to Close.  & go all over Cathedral with Mr F & Romsey Vicar.Go up to Mrs E & M, W.F & Dad, go on River with me
F 5 WedPlay cricket at night Tommies all out 126. We get 6 for 140 odd. [indecipherable] 100 r 7.32.Write & read all night. D & E goes to Waac's concert at night not Mrs Hunt. Wrote M.R.L
F 6 ThSit in room & read all night. E comes home at 11.30 pm very late. Have supper of Pears & cream.
F 7 FriGo to bed at 1 pm & read all time. Tap. Comes down to write all night. I am very dopey rashes [indecipherable] all over.Finish Dicken's American Notes.
F 8 SatUp 6 times & feel dizzy in the head otherwise OK. Ride to W'ton but gnats & motor dust hasten me home again. Pint photo & write letter to Peg.  & Nette. D & E especially E scrub out room & clean up generally.
