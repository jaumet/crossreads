<p>hooting &amp; noise of the many steamers &amp; ferry boats in the harbour; the "Capt. Cook", the pilot boat, left us at the heads .<br/>All being tired after the day of excitement we turned in to our new kind of bed, the hammock, I prefered to lay on the decking after the first night.</p>
