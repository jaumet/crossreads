<p>July 1918<br/>Monday 8</p>
<p>Vaux sur Somme.2nd Bty. Position<br/>At 1am Fritz was shelling on a duty shoot, all round district. At 12.20 am all the guns opened for a stunt, of what I do not know. <br/>Smithy came up to position and I was very glad to see him looking so well and happy. <br/>Halligan went out to L'aison at Hamel with Mr. Kerr.<br/>Wrote several letters for Smithy and myself. Had a feed of chips made by Tom.<br/>Had a heavy thunderstorm</p>
