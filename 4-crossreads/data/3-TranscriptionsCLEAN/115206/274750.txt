
June 1918Saturday 29
Bourdon. On the roadPulled into our W. Lines at 2.30 am, feeling pretty tired too. Ken Gunn not looking very well and does not profess to be too well. Billeting party left at 11.30 am.Weather splendid walked into Hanzest in the evening with Ton Code, had a chat with one or two folk there and a snack.Pulled out at 9 pm for [indecipherable]. Had a spendid trip all night and arrived at Poulonville at 3.50 am. On Sunday morning.French advanced 7 kilos at Montdider.
