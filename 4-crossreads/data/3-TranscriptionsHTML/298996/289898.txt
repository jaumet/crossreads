<p>was a slave market before he commandured same it looks more like a prison than a bake house when we went into same all one could see was bread all over the floors it was a Great sight After we had drawn all the bread necessary for us we went to Cairo railway station then the fun began our driver waited about half an hour for the Carriers to shift their wagons out of the way but they did not move he then started his motor and he gently put his front wheel against the narrow gutted wagon nearest him &amp; lifted it up a little the Native began to scream the driver drove backward</p>
