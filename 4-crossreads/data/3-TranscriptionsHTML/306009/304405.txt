<p>1915<br/>Aug. 9 Mon.<br/>Our office has been somewhat rearranged. All hospital work will in future be done in the Secretary &amp; Registrar's Office &amp; in our office we shall only do regimental &amp; Colonel's work. To do this work there is a Sergt. &amp; myself. The O.C. has a Staff Sgt. As his Sec.</p>
