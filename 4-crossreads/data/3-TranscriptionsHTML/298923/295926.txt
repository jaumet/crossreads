<p>17 September 1918 Tuesday<br/>You will hear of great doings in a few days time in these parts-  We are on the Flank &amp; today I rode along a bridle track &amp; could see Jerusalem away in the setting sun &amp; miles in the opposite direction across the Jordan.<br/>Joe writes that Laura <u>looks well</u> I think sometimes she must be a bit of a Hypochondriack   Can't spell it, anyway very glad to hear she is well-<br/>Huns over but no <strike>plan</strike> bombs dropped-</p>
