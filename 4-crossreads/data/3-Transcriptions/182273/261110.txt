
<p>March &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>10 SUN &nbsp;Fine morning. rain</p>
<p>at night. Emmie &amp; Sally came</p>
<p>in aft. Pollie in morning. Ted</p>
<p>Murray at night. Went to Burnetts</p>
<p>&amp; after around the village.</p>
<p>Posted letters to Gladys. &amp; Peggy. u s u</p>
<p>Photos</p>
<p>11 MON &nbsp;Left for Wigan 2.58. met</p>
<p>Betty at School. Tea in town &amp;</p>
<p>after to Plant. had a glorious</p>
<p>evening. Said Goodbye to Bet</p>
<p>Stayed at Victoria.&nbsp;<sup>Wired to</sup>&nbsp;Webbs.</p>
<p>12 TUES &nbsp;Fine day. Left Wigan 10.am</p>
<p>Posted 2 letters &amp; parcel to Betty.</p>
<p>Lunched in town. Went to &#39;Court&#39;</p>
<p>Theatre at night &#39;Bing Bhoys&#39;. took</p>
<p>girls. tr&egrave;s bon evening.</p>
<p>13 WED &nbsp;11.45 PM Left W.Ton 10 am</p>
<p>LPool 11.30. Went to Bibbys &amp; Grayson&#39;s in</p>
<p>aft also Blackledges.&nbsp;called at Mac&#39;s</p>
<p>Capt. Mooney&#39;s etc said Goodbye to</p>
<p>all. Left home 11 PM. &amp; Lime St. 11.50&nbsp;<sup>PM.</sup></p>
<p>Hard parting. Tram jumped rails</p>
