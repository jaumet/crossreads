<p>February, 1916<br/>Tuesday 29<br/>I took a long walk out on the desert &amp; fired a few shots w/- my pistol to see if it would still go off (it did)<br/>After dinner I went into Town for a ramble returning about 10 pm tired out.</p>
<p>March, 1916<br/>Wednesday 1<br/>Poke about all morning &amp; after dinner went over to Dad's &amp; played cards till nine then went home to bed &amp; read a book till 1 am.</p>
