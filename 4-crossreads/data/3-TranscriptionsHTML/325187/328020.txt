<p>[Wednesday 4 October 1916]</p>
<p>Went &amp; saw Laura &amp; also a Doctor to get a certificate re Bronchitis &amp; so help for extension of leave.<br/>Mr Neill called &amp; asked us to Dinner on Friday next-<br/>We have seats tonight for "Her Husband's Wife" at the New Theatre.<br/>London is very dark at night time.</p>
