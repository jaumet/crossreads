<p>Friday 13<br/>We are in little wooden huts, and back in Belgium, cold winds and rain. We had a long march yesterday<br/>Saturday 14<br/>Feeling sore and stiff after playing football N S Wales versus Victoria 5th Brigade &amp; 6th Brig<br/>Sunday 15<br/>On the track again this time to an officers school near Calais A.1<br/>So far so good. I have been in some nasty corners, but not with standing all that Ive had a good time!</p>
