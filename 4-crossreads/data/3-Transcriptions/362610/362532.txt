<p>1917<br/>14 Jan. Left Wandsworth, travelled to Southampton &amp; sailed per HMAHS "Kanonna".<br/>24 Jan. Reached Sierra Leone.<br/>26 Jan. Left Sierra Leone.<br/>11 Feb. Reached Durban.<br/>12 Feb. Left Durban.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Feb. Passed island of St. Paul.<br/>28 Feb. Reached Fremantle.<br/>1 Mch.  Left Fremantle.<br/>7 Mch.  Reached Port Melbourne.<br/>8 Mch.  Left Melbourne.<br/>11 Mch. Reached Sydney.</p>
