 Wednesday 14 March 1917. 
 In camp. 
 At Lt. Horse Fd Amb. bivouac in the morning and in afternoon practised for sham battle with the 23/7th Lt Horse to take place tomorrow all day. 
 Latest furphy &quot;Sailing&nbsp; before  about Monday 19th inst&quot;. 
 &quot;Euripides&quot; Artillery move out for, nobody knows where. 
 No lecture tonight.
