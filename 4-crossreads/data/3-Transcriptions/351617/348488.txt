<p>After we had been two days at Walcourt we (the Wireless section) came to the conclusion we had to walk too far for our meals and we decided to start a cook house of our own.</p>
<p>As soon as I heard of this suggestion the though struck me that the job of cook would not be such a bad game, as dixies are not likely to fly up and hit one like donkeys hoofs are so straight away I went and had a conference with my mate Bill Rigby.
