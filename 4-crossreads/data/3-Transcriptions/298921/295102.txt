<p>May, 1915<br/>Sunday 9<br/>Letters I expect will be erratic now as many May be sent on to the Regiment &ndash; Posted some Snapshots of Luxor &amp; one of the L.H. Marching through the streets of Cairo &ndash; British Citizens are opening their houses to billet wounded soldiers &amp; officers at Maidi a pretty suburb.</p>
