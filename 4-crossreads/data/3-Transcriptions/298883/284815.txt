<p>Our Bde. has been ordered to attempt a crossing of the Somme and push the germans off the high ground beyond.</p>
<p>5.30 p.m.  Attack cancelled and the Bde. ordered to stand fast until further orders.</p>
<p>30th<br/>Raining again.</p>
<p>4.30 a.m.  Orders have been received for the Bn. to move at 6 A.M.  The men had a good hot meal and we started off.  We are to follow up the 6th Aust. Bde. who are to make an attempt at crossing instead of us.</p>
<p>11 a.m.  Message has been received saying the 6th Bde. attempt to cross has failed.  We moved back to a position in rear of the town of Barleux on receipt of this news.</p>
