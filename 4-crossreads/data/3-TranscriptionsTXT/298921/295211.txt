 August, 1915 Saturday 28 Nile is now flooded, leaving its silt over the valley &amp; fertilizing it for successive crops &ndash; We get so little reliable news except from the wounded &ndash; Even the graphic [indecipherable] illustration of the Aust: L.H. charging  "on horseback  at Gallipoli &amp; with rifles slung &ndash; most absurd  no  horses being over there &ndash; I hope the seasons are better for Graziers all round the ram sales were not very brilliant this year. 500 more men came in yesterday.
