THURSDAY, JULY 3, 1919.
Fine & warm in the sun. We all went
into Quirindi in the big car in
the morning Father & Mother & Peter
went on out to Wallabadah & Jo
& I drove her car home.
I fixed up with Bank N.S.W for a
loan up to 6000 for building
stocking ect, Drove old Cy down
to Middle in afternoon.
Tickle round ewes in morning
& cutting logs for mill stand.
