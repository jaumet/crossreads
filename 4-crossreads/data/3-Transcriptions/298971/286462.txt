<p>13th November (Monday)</p>
<p>In morning all A Section went to the baths and in afternoon we were down at the quarry getting stones.</p>
<p>14th November (Tuesday)</p>
<p>Rushed off as attendant on Motor Car to Thistle Dump.  12 went in all.  Took 7 hours to get there, such was the bad traffic management.  Wounded were being brought in in large numbers and we were taking them off to the railway</p>
