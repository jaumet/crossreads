By Jove I was glad when morning came I was that cold that I could scarcely talk. everything I had on was sopping wet my dugout which was occupied by 4 of us was more like a pond than anything else I could compare it too, my feet were as cold as cold could be. Heavens how we suffer with our feet they are almost dead for half the night give us About 6.30 our rations breakfast came up hot stew tea best of all a pair of dry socks for each man. We get these every morning they are a God send
