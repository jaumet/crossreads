<p>Monday 6.11.16<br/>Kept going continuously the whole of the night; rained about midnight &amp; made work much harder. Practically the whole of us dead beat, but had to continue carrying till 12 noon when we were relieved by 6 F Amb, 5th F Amb took over advanced posts.  Feel the strain of short rations.</p>
