<p>22 November 1916 Wed<br/>The [indecipherable] evacuate sick with [indecipherable]. Finished our game of [indecipherable] 6 am. after all night sitting lost 5 francs. Doug Hall &amp; Smith leave &ndash; wrote to Mother &amp; Ossie. Terribly cold, busy night. Bearers return.</p>
<p>23 November 1916 Thur<br/>Leave to Amiens starts about sixteen men a day. Have all day there. Put about 200 sick &amp; wounded through &amp; then sat down to the poker school in the dressing tent. Paid this morning. Very foggy night.</p>
