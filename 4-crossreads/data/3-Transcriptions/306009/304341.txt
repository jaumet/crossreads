<p>1915<br/>June 4 Fri.<br/>Nothing out of the ordinary doing to-day.<br/>Pretty busy most of the day but found time to write a couple of letters home.<br/>Yesterday received a very decent letter from the Chief Scout in connection with our Australasian Scouts Association, most of the 84 members of which are now scattered far &amp; wide &ndash; for all I know</p>
