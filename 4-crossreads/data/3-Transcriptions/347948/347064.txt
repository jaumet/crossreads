<p>14th  Another death to-day from the artillery this makes 7 in 30 days.  Sick men taken ashore and another is said to have died on the way.<br/>Major Cooke Russell announced that owing to the measles and mumps cases the Cape Town medical authorities had quarantined the ship until fumigated, on account of a death owing to Spinal Meningitis.<br/>The order for quarantine has now been revoked and instead the ship is to be thoroughly fumigated to-morrow.</p>
<p>15th  Reveille at 5 o'clock this morning and all hammocks &amp; blankets are taken and hung up in "C" Deck ready for fumigation.</p>
