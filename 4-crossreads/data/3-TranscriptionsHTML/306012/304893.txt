This suddenly brought back to me the fact that we were at war and that an enemy plane had dropped these two bombs a few days ago.  Better that he should drop them here in this little nature's paradise than in a camp full of men in the prime of life.  Providence!<br/>The Australians are barring the Germans' way to Amiens. The papers, both French &amp; English, have been dealing out plenty of praise to the Australians during the past few days.  The Americans are beginning to arrive slowly but surely.
