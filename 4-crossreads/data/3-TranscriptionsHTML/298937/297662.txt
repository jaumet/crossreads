<p>landed &amp; waited in the Shed for some time.<br/>Got a carpenter to send a note to Dad for me.<br/>3 Trains for our crowd &amp; I was in the 2nd train.<br/>Felt delighted to once again behold the green fields &amp; lovely landscapes of England.<br/>After a splendid journey arrived at Amesbury where we disembarked &amp; marched 6 miles to Rollestone Camp, passing near Stonehenge.  Most of the march was over a road I cycled on over four years ago.<br/>We found at Rollestone</p>
