  
 7. Sunday Heavy showers.  Finished Macedonia sketches.  Sig. Siga. Signora Bambacionis called for tea - Read History. 
 8. Monday Sunshine now & again.  Went to Miss Hodges Indian tea at Minerva.  Terribly boring.  Home & washed hair.  Dr & Mrs Clark called 
 9. Tuesday Such a dense fog, lights necessary at 10 a.m.  Wrote article for S.M.H.  Pottered had a snooze etc. 
 10. Wednesday Showery &  grey.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10 returned home at 4 &  pottered on odds &  ends.
