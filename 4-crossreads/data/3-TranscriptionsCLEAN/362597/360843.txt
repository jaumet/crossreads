 Friday 31.12.15 (9) Arose at 6.30 & had a lovely hot bath. Dave & I went for a walk down to the Terrace & then met 3 of the Pros [Probationers] & went for another walk. Breakfast at 8. Tremendous breakfast attended by a French waitress. After Breakfast took a walk round the town & sent some P.C. views Home. Wrote a letter Home in the Grand Writing Room. 
 Ellis, Dev & I with 3 of the Probationers left the Hotel by Motor at 10.45. The road is like a billiard table.
