
chap from 31st btn & he told me Reg N had been killed at Polygon Wood on about Sep 27th. He was first of all wounded then a shell came along & blew him to pieces. Last Sunday received letters from Muriel, Mrs Nancarrow, Minnie Lusc[ombe], Guild, Uncle Harry & Amanda.18/10/17 Could not get to sleep for a long time on account of the vermin crawling about & going to bed too early viz 5.30pm. Received change underclothes & sox so had cold bath in creek alongside camp & changed. Went to canteen in afternoon & bought some chocolate. Some of the chaps arrived
