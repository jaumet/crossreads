 To Mrs GO Hawkins 10 Norman Av Hawkburn Australia &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No1 Victoria General France Australia 
 1654 Sapper Hawkins G.O. 7th Field Cop Aust Engineers 
 [In right margin] Much love to all 
 Dear Beatrice 
 Am in one of our own hospitals near or rather at [sensored] Left the trenches, two days ago. Nothing serious only 'trench feet' 
 Am booked for Blighty I believe 
 Military movement are so uncertain that postal arrangements keep astray as usual, preventing me getting letters
