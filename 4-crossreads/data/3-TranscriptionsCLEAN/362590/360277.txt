
In BilletsFrance10.1.16Dear Judge
Have just seen Keith who was driving round in a beautiful car. He took me for a drive & we had a great yarn. He's looking awfully fit.
I know you have heard thousands of fellows talk about Arthur but I thought you might like to know this.
I was on a piquet in a town here & one of the 20th pretty well on his ear strolled up to my post & started yarning
