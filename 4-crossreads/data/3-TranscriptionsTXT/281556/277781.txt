 6.30 to 7.30 beautifull 7.30 to 10pm Battn Concert in Town Hall very tiring. Weather fine. 
 Sep 3rd. Sunday. Church parade 10.30 Bgde. Afternoon looked through [Indecipherable] &amp; then short while in Cathedral listening to organ. Took several snaps while out. light showers &amp; rather cloudy. Church at Soldiers Home evening just in before exceedingly heavy showers came. Did little shoping after service &amp; then home to beg (Bed ?).
