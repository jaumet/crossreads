<p>1917 September</p>
<p>21 Friday</p>
<p>Feeling better but sick from so much quinine.  Long day as the pea-nut stand opens early and closes late &ndash; no possibility of quiet.  Sister Russell blew in during afternoon &ndash; joyous surprise.  Horrors found I was paralysed by the injection of Hansell &ndash; wondered why pain had ceased.</p>
