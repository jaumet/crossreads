<p>October 1915</p>
<p>Saturday 30</p>
<p>A.M.'s fatigue</p>
<p>Arrival of 30 odd reinforcements  from [indecipherable]  A.G.H. &amp; belonging to other ambulances especially the 7th. Among them was the chap I passed at Brisbane prominent in the Daily Mail picture. Coincidence No.1 for he first went in to Infantry.</p>
<p>Coin. No.2. Met Jack Buckle of [indecipherable] days, down in Army Service Corps.</p>
