  Monday 18th Feb.  Committee meeting of concert party yesterday decided to attempt concert very soon. Went be Osteasteene, where the rest of the Ambulance is, today &amp; fixed up for the chaps to prepare their stuff up there. Meeting of literary men tonight &amp; was proposed as Pres. But had to refuse on account of concert party work &ndash; put on the committee. If piano arrives soon, will be able to give a pretty good show in a few days. Cold, clean &amp; very fine.
