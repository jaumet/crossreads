<p>Wednesday 17 January 1917 [1918]<br/>&frac12; day &amp; went with S. Steel to Mosque &amp; Dead City.  Both places very impressive.  Mahomid [Muhammad or Mohammad] Ali was 2nd Khedive &amp; was the one who gave a dinner to the &hellip; &amp; then slew all save one who sprang on his horse &amp; jumped a great height to the open space below.  All this was 120 years ago in 1811.  Mahomid Ali then built for &pound;25,000-0-0 this beautiful mosque &ndash; 2000 electric lights put by Kitchener, splendid carpets, &amp; lovely alabaster pillars &amp; concave nitche in wall.</p>
