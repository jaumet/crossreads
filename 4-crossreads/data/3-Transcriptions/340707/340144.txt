<p>At first the betting system worries me [indecipherable] how the prices [indecipherable] 5 fr 10 fr 50 fr at each bets are made in these prices You can bet that horse wins or that it gets a place &amp; the prices are determined afterwards. You are ignorant at the time. Horses names are not used only the numbers. Horses running &amp; results of last race &amp; prices paid are shown on an indicator. I do fairly well ending up losing about 16/- not as bad considering I knew absolutely nothing of the horses &amp; no one on the ground.</p>
<p>Leaving course with friend I had found I went to Rue des Paradis &amp; thence to Restaurant Rabbie 69 Boiel Magenta en face de la Gare de L'Est where I met M &amp; Mde Cayer &amp; Oliver a retired French captain &amp; Lucille. They give me a pleasant evening. The freedom from restraint &amp; other peoples opinion on their own innocent enjoyment is very refreshing after the staidness of England.</p>
