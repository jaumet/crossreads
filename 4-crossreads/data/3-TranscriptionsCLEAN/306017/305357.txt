The coolness of everybody was inspiring.  We held on till dark & then we returned to Oghratina hungry thirsty & tired.  We got rations & water & went back next morning.
The N Zealanders occupied Bir-el-Abd the Turks retreating through the night.  We did nothing next day, but our troops occupied Salmana.  Jacko was well back on his own sand hill & was retreating S.E.  Our aeroplanes were heavily shelled each time they appeared over Jacko's lines we counted 140 shrapnel bursts after 1 poor aeroplane & then it sailed quietly away.We stayed out till Saturday night & returned to Katia.
