<p>[The following three items listed crossed through.]<br/>To Strip Lewis Gun<br/>1.  Take off butt stock <strike>first</strike> by inserting point of bullet.<br/>2.  Trigger group &ndash; press trig. &amp; draw to rear.<br/>3.  Body Cover &ndash; slide to rear &amp; lift up.</p>
