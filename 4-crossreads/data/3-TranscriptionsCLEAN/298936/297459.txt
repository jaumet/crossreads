  
 October 1915 
 Saturday 30 
 A.M.'s fatigue 
 Arrival of 30 odd reinforcements  from [indecipherable]  A.G.H. & belonging to other ambulances especially the 7th. Among them was the chap I passed at Brisbane prominent in the Daily Mail picture. Coincidence No.1 for he first went in to Infantry. 
 Coin. No.2. Met Jack Buckle of [indecipherable] days, down in Army Service Corps.
