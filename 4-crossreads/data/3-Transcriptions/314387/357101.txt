<p>Friday 31st May 1918<br/>In trenches all day nightime patrol /  Sunday June 1st<br/>Same old thing relieved &amp; night by 50th Battalion Split marched all night<br/>Sunday 2nd<br/>Marched till dinner time then billeted in big empty Hospital outside Amiens suburb Privery<br/>Monday 3<br/>Parade in morning afternoon off -   Tuesday May 4th<br/>Parade and two hours fatigue in morning paid thirty Francs afternoon</p>
