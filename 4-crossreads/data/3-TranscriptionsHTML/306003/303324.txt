<p>19-4-16<br/>Wed 19th<br/>A good many men applied today for transfer to 1st Field Sqd. Div. Engineers.  The officers came [indecipherable] this afternoon with a cricket bat &amp; ball but the ball did not last long as it was a cheap one &amp; soon went to pieces.<br/>had cocoa tonight</p>
<p>20-4-16<br/>Thurs 20th<br/>The transport drivers brought out a bat &amp; ball from Minia for us yesterday, we had a few strikes this morning &amp; finished the ball off this afternoon</p>
<p>The South Western Mounted Bde Field Amb. was replaced  by 4th Dismounted Field Amb. at Minia today;  We received a paper mail this morning  Tonight we had a concert<strike>tonight</strike> which went off very well</p>
