  
 Greatly enjoy a French Xmas Breakfast, - included in the many dishes is an English pudding for the two guests.  - A pleasant afternoon and a Xmas Dinner at 8 p.m. given to a dozen Australians and a Canadian by our dear friend Miss Butler - A very merry and light hearted Party. - Midnight celebrations in the Boulevards - The end of a very happy Xmas Day -
