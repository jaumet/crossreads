<p>BUZINCOURT<br/>Friday 15<br/>Awakend from a sound sleep by a gas alarm early this morn<br/>Camped between two hills "Dolce Far Niente"<br/>Saturday 16<br/>We are the reserve division. Summer Sun good rations, &amp; beautiful nights. Lifes worth living. Plenty of "baloons about<br/>Sunday 17<br/>Not a single shot fired on this front for 48 hours there must be something in the air for tis very unusual, either a calm before the next storm or peace terms in view</p>
