<p>Sunday 10th February<br/>As usual Pross &amp; I went salvaging this afternoon &amp; got a full tin on Biscuites 8 tins Butter 3 of Jam besides other odds &amp; ends useful about the humpy. One can get almost anything about here &ndash; The waste is terrible.</p>
<p>Monday 11th February<br/>The daily round as usual Go out at 6 AM &ndash; back at noon</p>
