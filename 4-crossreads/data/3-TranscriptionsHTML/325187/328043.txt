<p>[Friday 27 October 1916]</p>
<p>Polling Booth.<br/>London is not a place of pleasure in the dark we come back here each night till 10.30.<br/>Don't mention how the votes go as strict secrecy must be observed.<br/>Laura dined with me last night she is very thin but glad to get away from Walsall.<br/>Colonel Ryan Colonel Manderby were on the Medical Board- English Hospital vote YES<br/>Trench Field Force NO   Egyptian not yet here but think they will vote YES- it will be a close go</p>
