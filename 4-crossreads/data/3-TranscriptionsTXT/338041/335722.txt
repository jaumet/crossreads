 July 29th Return of Pte Stock (Wounded) &amp; news of Bert Mallet's death etc. 
 July 30th Weather hot &amp; depressing &ndash; The Anzacs continue their excellent work round Pozieres &ndash; I pay a visit to Eddie Cornish 45th Batt and have a pleasant evening &ndash; More casualties among our Ambulance. 
 July 31st Australia Day &ndash; First hand news from the trenches &ndash; News of Harry &amp; others &ndash; A Hell on Earth &ndash; "Gallipoli insignificant" &ndash; Another visit to E.C. [Eddie Cornish] wherein I meet Capt. Perry &amp; Lieuts Murray. Fergusson. McIntyre Storer. 
 Aug. 1st Mail from home &ndash; Presence of numerous bomb-dropping Taubes. Quiet night on Duty.
