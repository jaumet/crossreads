 Monday Apl. 2nd Went to Baths this morning.  Had Church parade this afternoon.  There was a splendid crowd of men, "Who sh. separate us from Love of God wh. is in Xt Jesus". 
 Tuesday 3 Snowing heavily all night &amp; this morning.  Strong wind.  Everything white.  I went to Houplines this morning to the cocoa stall.  2 letters from Home. 
 Wednesday 4th Houplines again today.  British &amp; French pushing in on the Somme &amp; lower down. Class this evening, 20 present.  We had the sacrament at the class.  A splendid meeting. 
 Thursday 5th Beautiful fine day.  Wrote letters Home in morning.  Went to Batt. H.Q. afternoon. Crackenthorp &amp; his friend came in &amp; we had some games of chess.
