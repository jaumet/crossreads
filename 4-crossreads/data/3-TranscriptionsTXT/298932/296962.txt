 CASSELL Monday 22 An Australian mail in, I got 2 one from Muriel a good letter &amp; one from the bank good also Tuesday 23 The weather is getting colder every day, but never the less it as commenced more favourably than last year Wednesday 24 I think we are winning good news in today Down on the Somme we knocked Fritz pretty hard Thursday 25 Birdwood, General of the A.I.F was here to day and gave the boys their medal's
