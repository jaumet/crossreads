 Jan 24/16 (Tuesday) The Column came back this afternoon, from all accounts the honours seem to be even between us & the enemy Our total casualties of killed & wounded being just under 300. The bulk of these are with the Sikhs & the S Africans 
 I saw the latter march in. - There were hardly half the battalion in the body the rest came straggling in leg weary  footsore  & foot & blistered, or were riding in on the A.S.C. Waggons dead beat
