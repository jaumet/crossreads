 May 31 The Colonel said I would be able to go back to my job at home, but he didn't say when! &amp; I don't want a job on the staff. Went to the Dentist this morning &amp; suffering a few more twinges, he's not finished yet either by a long way. Played several games of croquet &amp; then went to listen to &amp; watch the Concert which was given by several tip top artists &ndash; mostly juveniles, and it was good too &ndash; real good &amp; the surroundings perfect. You bet the boys appreciate it all - Being wounded &amp; sick isn't half bad I can tell you. Our suits (Hospital) are black &amp; look better &amp; feel more comfortable than the blue's we had at No 3. London.
