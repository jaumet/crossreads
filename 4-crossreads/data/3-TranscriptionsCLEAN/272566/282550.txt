
Sunday 15 April
Gas helmet parade before breakfast in lieu of route march.Orders have come through from the A.D.M.S. saying that all bearers are to be held in readiness for the line at a few minutes notice.  The Australians have been pushed back at Lagnicourt but after a fierce counter-attack, retook the positions, inflicting a heavy hiding on Fritz.Still on Incinerator.Put through tear gas to test our box respirators.Showery all day.
