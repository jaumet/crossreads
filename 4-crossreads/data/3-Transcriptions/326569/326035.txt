<p>In afternoon went with Smith, Strahan &amp; Sowter to the church at Barford.  It is 700 years old &amp; has fine carvings on the altar.  Has memorials engraved dated 1584, 1618 &amp; numbers in the 1700s &amp; early 1800s.  Altogether was very interesting.</p>
<p>Saturday, 7/4/17<br/>Funniest Easter Saturday that I ever had.  Paraded with full equipment, blankets, all belongings &amp; changed our quarters to hut 20.  Messed about on parade ground for about 2 hours before finally fixed up &amp; during the process lumped our belongings all over the place.
