<p>11th. There has been great ructious today. I was supposed to take a party of 6 men to join the other Coy's in a route march, I had an early breakfast &amp; as I was coming up past B.O.R. old Frith the RSM. was standing on the road, he stopped me &amp; asked me what "orders" I had received, I told him, so he said when you go up tell your S.M. that everyone will be on parade I turned round to him &amp; said in a surprised tone, "everyone" he never answered me but walked up the steps, &amp; as I walked away the clock struck 7, &amp; the men were supposed to be ready at, 7.10. so you can see what kind of a chance they had, for they had never</p>
