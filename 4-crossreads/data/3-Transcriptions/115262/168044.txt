<p>Thursday 4 October 1917.</p>
<p>Jacko never put in an appearance today until late. (As he comes every day now I&#39;ve decided not to note every time he comes.)</p>
<p>Australian mail 6<sup>th</sup> since arriving in Egypt. Small mail which closed in Australia on July 19<sup>th</sup> week later that previous mail but took 7 weeks longer to reach here.&nbsp;</p>
<p>(One letter that I expected never arrived.)&nbsp;</p>
<p>Received 6 letters and packet of local papers.</p>
