 July wanted. The    fi  First Battalion took the two first lines &amp; "C" &amp;  "D" Coys of the third took the third line &amp; dug in at the edge of the village of Poziers on the morning of the 23rd. Things were very quiet all day sunday &amp;  half of monday &amp;  so gave us a chance to dig in but during the afternoon of monday Fritz opened up a bombardment which lasted all that night till about nine o'clock next morning when it lifted &amp; it was then that they gave a half hearted  bombardment
