<p>August, 1915<br/>Sunday 8<br/>S.S. Caledonian Hospital ship &ndash;<br/>First towed out in Barge full of dead &amp; wounded to R.V.S. "Liberty" &ndash; she took us to Imbros island &amp; we transhipped to Caledonian &amp; now are steaming for Alexandria. I lost all my kit &amp; a lot of blood &ndash;<br/>Shot one Turk dead with Revolver &amp; used 20 of their Bombs out of Captured trench but we were driven back. 1200 on this boat mangled humanity in only 4 shirts &amp; no pyjamas or breeches mine were cut off at dressing station all blood</p>
