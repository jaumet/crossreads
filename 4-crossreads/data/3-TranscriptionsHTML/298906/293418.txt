<p>I see by last nights "orders" that they are going to send me to Candahar Barracks to a School my luck must be in surely.<br/>To-night I am Canteen Sergt. my duty is to preserve order &amp; see that it is closed at the proper time, &amp; then I have to report on the Staff parade. I have a Corporal &amp; 6 men with me, they patrol the joint &amp; report anything wrong to me.<br/>26th.
