 Febry 10th The day was cloudy &amp; cool.  The Squadron did an hours exercise in the morning.  We got tents &amp; pitched them in the afternoon.  The 1st Brigade marched through on their way back to Bayoud &amp; Mageibra. I saw Douglas Cooper &amp; had a yarn with him for about an hour 
 Febry 11th "B" Squadron was duty squadron for the Brigade.  The duty squadron finds the patrols, camel escorts &amp; pumping fatigues.  "B" Troop found 19 other ranks to El Arish on camel escort.  There was a lot of aerial activity on our part during the day, but don't know whether they were out on duty or pleasure.  Wrote several letters in the afternoon &amp; have got my correspondence about squared up.  We got our colours issued green &amp; red  [sketch of patch] are our regimental colours.  A thunderstorm worked up at night.  I took over orderly sergeant for a week.  Several tents got blown down at night &amp; we also had some rain.
