<p>August 1915<br/>here since May all look pale &amp; there is no doubt a spell would do them a lot of good.</p>
<p>Wednesday 18th<br/>The day was very hot &amp; the flies troublesome.  Didn't get any sleep in all day.  At night there was very heavy firing near Achi Baba.  The rest of the line was very quiet.</p>
<p>Thursday 19th<br/>There was a cool change &amp; it was very acceptable.  The day was very quiet.  Wrote a letter to Cousin Meg.  The destroyer patrolling our right flank put in a lot of shells during the night.  It was quite impressive seeing them burst &amp; the search light illumine the smoke.</p>
<p>Friday 20th<br/>The English mail came in to day.  I got 2 letters from mother dated 7th &amp; 15th July.  Things were very quiet all day on our front.  There was some fighting</p>
