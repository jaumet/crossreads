<p>The dugout we occupied up to the night before last has been hit by a Minnie and no dug <br/>out can be seen, in fact nothing but a hole about 20 ft across and 12 ft deep.  Our SB <br/>squad was called out and we picked up one man -- But I will describe no more <br/>awful casualties, it would only make my diary a horror instead of an interesting book&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   <br/>However, I thought my first sight of death would horrify me, but we take it as part of our <br/>work, and the scene of action, the whirring of the flying shells and shrapnel, chase all <br/>thoughts of the gruesomeness of our task</p>
