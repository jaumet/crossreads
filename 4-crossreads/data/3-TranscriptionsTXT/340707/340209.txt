 79 added to the bill as a supplement. I visited Nostre Logis but found no one there whom I knew. 
 28-7-19 Went to visit Harrison &amp; then to Canteen where I made purchase amount to 27 fr then to see Shackleton but he will not be there an address is given 13 Rue Marche du St Honore &amp; I pick up driver looking for him &amp; ride along with him. 
 Meet Shackleton &amp; arrange to see him to morrow at Pepiniere Barracks Place St Augustin at 10 am rather early for me. Then dine with Brise &amp; take her to No 2 Rue Brown Sequard &amp; we pass a pleasant evening with the Gamels &amp; the Huesson who drops in. 
 29-7-19 Reach home at midnight &amp; find parcel Brise has left for me contains sweetmeats as a birthday present.
