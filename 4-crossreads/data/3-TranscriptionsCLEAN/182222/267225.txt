Feed at Moss's Garden Cafe  4 courses followed by a few beers (200 fine for Moss if caught) all for normal sum of 1/- & invited to come back for 5 course dinner which we later did. Imagine one of our home cafes opening out that way! YMCA post letters & feed of fruit, Rickshaw round the village. Met a heap of nice people.
