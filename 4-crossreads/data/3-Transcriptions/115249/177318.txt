<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 11 9 4</p>
<p>Time - 5.15</p>
<p>Commander Stevenson considers it inadvisable to go to Kaba Kaul tonight, as could not return here before dark. &nbsp;Propose leaving here 5.15 tomorrow&nbsp;&amp; will commence attack from both Herbertshoe and Kaba Kaul immediately you have shelled as arranged . &nbsp;Propose landing stores tonight for Herbertshohe garrison</p>
