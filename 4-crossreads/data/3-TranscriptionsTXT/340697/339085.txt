 The evacuation of the Peninsular about 50 casualties being the total for the whole scheme and the majority of these were at Suvla. We embarked at Watsons Pier in shrapnil proof pontoons at 9.30 pm. All parties left about half an hour earlier than had been previously arranged. Were taken aboard the El Kihira. She moved off at midnight and we arrived at Mudros
