<p><br/>21 January 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Field Day  Todays A.M.C. march out about 4 miles into bush.  Do Ambulance work all afternoon.  Tea.  brook O.C swims.  Boys raid vineyard.  pay damages. Rob, Tony, Willy White &amp; self pinch off home strike peach orchard.  Have fill.</p>
<p>Willy receives appointment to P.M.O. office. Cancelled.<br/>To bed early.</p>
