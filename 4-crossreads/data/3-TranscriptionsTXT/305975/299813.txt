
Monday June. 2.6.19went to sydney to day  was up at Barracks  went and saw win  she was in bed sick.   came home by 10.6.6.tuesday June. 2.6.19up at home to day  looks like more rain  up at mrs ushers to night Playing CardsWednesday June. 3.6.19at home to day  nice day  went down to kemps place to night  Played cards  stayed till 11.30.
