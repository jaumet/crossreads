<p>We had to carry a lot of duck boards &amp; hurdles up with us for we were going to put down a floor in the portion of trenches we had completed &amp; a nice job it was for there was easily a foot of water &amp; slush in the bottom &amp; the men had to work in this, we finished at 2 oclock &amp; come home then.</p>
<p>The 2nd Battalion got a nasty dishing up last night had 18 men Killed including 2 Capt. &amp; 4 Lieut's  "Aerial torpedoes" got them I believe.</p>
<p>Saw another Taube brought down this morning by one of our planes he come a beautiful buster.</p>
