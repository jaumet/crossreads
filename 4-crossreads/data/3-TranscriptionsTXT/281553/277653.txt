
16th TuesdayWeather very bright & hot.  Naval Guns directed toward Beachy Bill with heavy shells during afternoon.
27th WednesdaySky clouded at times but very windy.  Combined Naval & Artillery bombardment all along line for about 1 hour midday.
28th ThursdayWeather Bright & hot.  Fairly quiet along front.  Evening commenced early with bomb throwing & kept up very furiously all night.  390 Bombs in all used on whole Post during the night.  Small English Mail.
