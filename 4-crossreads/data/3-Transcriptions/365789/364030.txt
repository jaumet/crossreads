<p>Base Records Office, A.I.F.<br/>25th April, 1917.</p>
<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>I have much pleasure in forwarding hereunder copy of extract from Fifth Supplement No. 29827 to the London Gazette of 16th November 1916, relating to the conspicuous services rendered by your brother, No. 729 Private F. S. Phillips, 1st battalion (late 1st Field Ambulance A.M.C.)</p>
<p>Awarded the Military Medal.</p>
<p>"His Majesty the King has been graciously pleased to award the Military Medal for bravery in the Field to the undermentioned soldier:-</p>
<p>No. 729, Private Frederick Stobo Phillips"</p>
<p>The above has been promulgated in Commonwealth of Australia gazette, No. 62 of 19th April, 1917.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>J.M. Lean Major.<br/>Officer i/c Base Records</p>
<p>Mrs. W. H. Reid<br/>C/o A.D. Stobo<br/>Alliance Assurance Company<br/>Pitt Street<br/>Sydney.  N.S.W.</p>
