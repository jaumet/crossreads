<p>Sat. 8-7-'16<br/>Slept till 9 a.m. Bath afternoon. Full Mching Order 9.30 P.M. Marched to Aldershot Camp Neuve Eglise<br/>Slept between Bivouacs from 2 a.m.<br/>Sunday 9-7-'16<br/>Sports during Afternoon. Packed up &amp; marched to Bailleul 8.30 pm. &ndash; 11.30 P.M. Billetted in large School 12 Midnight<br/>Sunday 9-7-'16<br/>Above<br/>Mon. 10-7-'16<br/>Awake 7-30 am. Bk'fast. Wained for Guard 9-35 a.m. Mounted same 10 a.m. Little girls of School Sang danced &amp; played for us in exchange for pennies. Nearly all Orphans here. Y.M.C.A tent here very good &amp; cheap.
