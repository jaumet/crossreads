When they brought the Germans in the other night, our Colonel poured out a cup of coffee &amp; gave it to them I wonder if they would do the same by us. 30th. Last night I was on the Dead dog" fatigue again, &amp; it was another rotten job, we had 6 truck loads of sand bags timber barbwire, stakes, concrete slabs, cement, iron &amp; goodness only knows what else, we got home dog tired at just on 3 oclock in the morning.
