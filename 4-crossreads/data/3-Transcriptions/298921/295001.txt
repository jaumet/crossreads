<p>January, 1915<br/>Thursday 28<br/>At "Suez", [indecipherable] barracading the Bridge &amp; wheel house with Bags of Oats as "snipers" line the Canal to have a pot shot at Steamers as they pass if conveying Troops.<br/>Will endeavour to post at Alexandria on arrival.<br/>Saturday.<br/>Among my Pictures at [indecipherable] was a colored photo of a steamer 'in' the Canal I think both banks look like the backcountry after a drought!!<br/>As the boats go along, from the harbour, they have the appearance of steaming across a sandy plain the entrance not being visible from where we lie at anchor.</p>
