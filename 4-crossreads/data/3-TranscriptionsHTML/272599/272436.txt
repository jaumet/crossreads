<p>1917 Friday 15 June</p>
<p>Of at 7.30 a.m. Breakfast. Bed. Busy night as usual. Some were evacuated yesterday to Blighty They all from this ward are sent across as they are able to stand journey. <br/>Supper each night at 11. to 11.30. draw from cook house. Cocoa 4.30 a.m. Relieved just on breakfast time. Convalescents do all washing up in kitchen etc.</p>
