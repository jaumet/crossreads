  
 Thursday Feb 1 sailing postponed on account of Raider out. Troops from all states banking up in Seymour. Friday Feb 2 Weekly route march, across fields to Goulbourn River, across Railway Line &  back through old Seymour Town. Route marches very enjoyable. Saturday Feb 3 On duty, off in afternoon. Went fishing to Goulbourn River with some of the Boys. Change from Camp Sunday Feb 4 Washing day (nuff said) Monday Feb 5 Waggon Drill & Lecture Tuesday Feb 6 Signalling, Evening wrote letters
