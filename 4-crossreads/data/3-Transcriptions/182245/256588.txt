<p>MARCH 1917</p>
<p>14 WEDNESDAY</p>
<p>Fine most of the day, but rained</p>
<p>a little during the day. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness all the morning and</p>
<p>we went up &amp; brought out our</p>
<p>guns from the old pits, &amp; brought</p>
<p>them back to the waggon lines</p>
<p>for a couple of days spell before</p>
<p>going to Ploegsteert (Plug Street)</p>
<p>The 31<sup>st</sup> Battery relieved us at</p>
<p>our pits.</p>
