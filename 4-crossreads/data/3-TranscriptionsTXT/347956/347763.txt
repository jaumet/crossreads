 Nov 1 &ndash; 2 &ndash; 3 
 Sun 1st Left King George's Sound at 6-30 A.M.  saw two sharks as we left the heads.  ships came out in single file, &amp; then steamed into position 2 abreast (3/4 of as mile separating them) &amp; a mile apart lengthways.  Major Vernon conducted the Church Service.  another horse died (Lieu. Wordsworth's) 
 Mon 2nd Idleboy has touch of influenza: attending to horses all the morning.  there are 35 Troop ships &amp; they make a very pretty picture steaming along all in line. can only see 3 cruisers.  another horse died
