 128. It was at BOHAIN I left the Unit after a very trying time including the taking of the Hindenburg Line. Our Artillery were covering 2 Divisions of Yanks the Infantry were back resting after the  severest  capture of the LINE. 
 The change to England was very welcome although it hurt a little leaving all my pals.
