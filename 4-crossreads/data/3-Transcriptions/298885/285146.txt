<p>June 13th &ndash; 17<br/>Caught 10.10AM train for Grantham and arrived at 12.30 after a through run of 105 miles which was very much enjoyed as I was extremely lucky in getting my leave in the best part of the year and so I saw all the country at its best.</p>
