1916   THURSDAY 28   SEPT.
Fine & warm. Out with the signallers all day. We had a test in the morning & I passed all right. The battery went on duty in the evening. Capt Campbell took some of the headquarters in a gun laying class in the evening. Old George Curry came over to see us in the evening & he & Poss & Self went to the pictures in the evening.
