<p>[Wednesday 12 April 1916]</p>
<p>Thousands of Armenians are in this country, the Turks have murdered about one million already &amp; Poor devils they are very destitute &amp; their future a blank- we have one as Cooks offsider for the Mess.<br/>Today a race between a Camel &amp; a troop horse over 6 miles, I'm backing the Camel.<br/><u>Later</u> The horse won, to our surprise the Camel could not last.<br/>Prince of Wales went thro' in train tonight from Luxor, all Egyptian Notables crowded the train to see him, his rank is Staff Captain, &amp; keep out of the road.  (please thank May many thanks for MITS.) omit</p>
