After a little delay the Train started backwards on to another line when I had a last shake hand with my Wife,  who  after a little shouting we arrived in front of a large Shed & the orders given to detrain, we all filed out into the Shed & formed up in our original platoons, the Weather being fairly hot & the doors being closed the atmosphere was very unpleasant & we had to put up with it for some considerable time why we were kept there so long it was hard to say, we were told when we went on board to stop at our mess tables until further orders.
