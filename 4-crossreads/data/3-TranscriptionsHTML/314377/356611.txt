<p>This was "finish" for me as far as "Active Service" was concerned. Immediately I felt I was hit I was put onto a stretcher and turned along back to the dressing station</p>
<p>There were quite a number of chaps came to my assistance. I was dropped twice and it was a great difficulty to find the dressing station. Owing to intense blackness I asked the chaps to hurry and reach the aid-post as I felt myself to be losing a great deal of blood.</p>
