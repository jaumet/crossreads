<p>Friday 14.6.18&nbsp; Very sick, saw M.D.&nbsp; walked to St. Ouen&nbsp; turned in early</p>
<p>Saturday 15.6.18&nbsp; Very sick stayed in Bed, Bn. relieved</p>
<p>Sunday 16.6.18&nbsp; Better, joined Bn at Frechencourt&nbsp; still a bit seedy</p>
<p>Monday 17.6.18&nbsp; stayed in bed all day&nbsp; made appln to be returned to Australia</p>
<p>Tuesday 18.6.18. Saw Wisdom, walked down to Transport lines, had a game of Bridge.</p>
<p>Wednesday 19.6.18&nbsp; Had a few strikes at cricket- visited each Coy. Letter from Doff</p>
