<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 21 9 14</p>
<p>Time - 6.50 PM</p>
<p>From Brigadier. &nbsp;Governor surrendered with four Officers fifteen Officials Thirty Seven White Non Commissioned Officers and men and one hundred and twenty natives <s>all of whom are at Rabaul</s>. &nbsp;Governor staying night at Herbertshohe reports here early tomorrow. &nbsp;Half Company Infantry at Toma to receive reinforcements sent from Wilhelmshafen</p>
