 (21 &ndash; 23)  12-14 
 containing Alexanders Hymns &amp; we had a very enjoyable evening 
 Mon 21st Signalling all day.  nothing particular happened only a few spills to break the monotony of the day.  A concert was arranged by the residents last night &amp; was a huge success.  a good many ladies giving items which was well received.  Yesterday I received a letter from Edie Tomlins. 
 Tue 22nd Sig. morning.  were busy all the afternoon cleaning our gear, as we are to march through Cairo to-morrow &amp; have to get all the rust off our stirrups &amp; spurs.  went nowhere after tea. 
 Wed 23rd Revalie went at 4.45 AM &amp; was soon on our march to Cairo we went through the principal streets, received a good reception.
