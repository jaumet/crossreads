 11.10.16 a very quiet time this afternoon &amp; at 9 oclock tonight we caught the motor car &amp; came back to Camp   A few American sailors are knocking around Alexandria &amp; they have no trouble to buy an argument but the lads have behaved very well here &amp; I have seen no more than half a dozen lads the worse off for liquor
