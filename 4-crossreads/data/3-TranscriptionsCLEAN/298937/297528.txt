 Tuesday 15th Feb. 
 Am making headway with French under tuition of Dr. Cochenos. Latin I am also going on with in order to get up to matriculation standard as soon as possible.  Dr. Boutazy thinks I should stay in Egypt after the war & go to Brerut College to take medical degree.  He is giving me all the practice he can here.
