Thursday 14 February
A football match to-day between 1st Field Ambulance 1st Div. M.G. resulted in a victory for us. The match was very exciting played on a muddy ground. The 2nd match we have won in the Division Comp. A German balloon small floated over our lines came down in our camp, had phamplets addressed to French inhabitants.
Friday 15 February
The Fancy Dress Carnival came off to-night and was a huge success. I went as a Staff Captain red facings colours on Mr. Neave's tunic was adjudged very good. All were present, officers included all costumes very good. Prizes were awarded for most comical dress best couple. H. Long as a sister was great. Refreshments a flashlight photo. Of course many were rather inked on whisky etc.
