<p>Tuesday 27 August<br/>Put wagon in order &amp; knocked about getting stores etc.</p>
<p>Afternoon out to Chipplly Ex., SAA, MDS, Div Train. Run out of cable 400 yds from Div Train. Returned Chipplly but could not obtain cable. Returned about 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Tired once more.</p>
