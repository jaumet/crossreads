<p>19 May, Monday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Billy &amp; I carry swag to Dinton &amp; have refreshments there. After dinner we go for sleep etc. after tea get films. Sat &amp; see Picture on "Life of Nelson". 7 reels very good.</p>
<p>2 Boat rolls I miss.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L. &amp; Home.</p>
<p>20 May, Tuesday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Print photos all day. Very long job. Go for a walk with Billy after tea, all around Dinton. We view some magnificent views, some magnificent views from 'Hill Top'. Meet Ted "Ryde", Lyall Walker.</p>
<p>Wrote Lottie, Miss Chapman, Mrs McKinnon</p>
