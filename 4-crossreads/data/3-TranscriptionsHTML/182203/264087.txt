As we walked towards the main entrance, some of the patients were sitting out on the Lawn &amp; as I looked across, saw an officer stand up &amp; walk <strike>on tw</strike> with the aid of two sticks towards me &amp; who should it be but Jackson's great pal Capt. Hay-Lawson. He was so delighted to see me &amp; helped me find out if George was in &ndash; so I sat out on the lawn with him &amp; had a great old yarn. He is a charming man, most interesting &amp;  very entertaining. He poor chap was wounded in the L. leg by a gunshot &ndash; He has had an awfully bad time, been there over a month but is getting on alright. He looks as if he had been very ill too.
