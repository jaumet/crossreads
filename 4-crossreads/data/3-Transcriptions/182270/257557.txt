
<p>TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 2, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot with very strong N.W</p>
<p>wind. I was lopping in 4D,</p>
<p>Top, &amp; Mountain. Linda came</p>
<p>out &amp; met me at lunch time</p>
<p>&amp; was round with me the rest</p>
<p>of the time.</p>
<p>Tickle poisoning in the</p>
<p>Boundary &amp; Cow paddocks.</p>
