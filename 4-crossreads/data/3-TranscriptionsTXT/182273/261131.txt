23 THURS &nbsp;Change. Weather cool
&amp; heavy wind. Posted
letters to Amy &amp; (Maggie)
Julie &amp; Tense. Very quiet.
Cricket match. &amp; two
bands playing. Writing.

Very quiet day &amp; night
Writing.

Mrs Mooney. Amm. going
up. Capt. Malley paid
