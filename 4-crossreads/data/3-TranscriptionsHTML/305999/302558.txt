<p>10/12/16<br/>At 10/30 we embarked on a small steam boat  At 12/5 we left leharve  it was very calm at sea  pretty crouded</p>
<p>11/12/16<br/>At 7 AM we disembarked at South hampton got into a train and went to Waterloo station London  We marched to horse ferry road  got new uniform and went to the bank and drew my money  very pleased<br/>during my stay in London I visited the St Pauls the museum</p>
