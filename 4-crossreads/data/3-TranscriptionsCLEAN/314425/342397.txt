If we assume a net funded indemnity of 25,000,000,000 with interest at 3 per cent and a sinking fund of 1/3 per cent it would be liquidated in 66 years, & would require an annual charge of 875,000,000 of 5.16.3 per head of the present population of enemy countries.  With a rapidly increasing population and productivity it is sheer nonsense to suggest that such a liability is beyond their power especially if for say, three years the interest were funded (added to the debt) and only the sinking fund, a trifle of 12,500,000 exacted.
