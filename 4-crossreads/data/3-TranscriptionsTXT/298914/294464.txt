Johnston thrown & kicked so placed in limber.  Stumbling over waddis and rough country.11thMatch famine. Arrive Hill 331 at 9 am.  120 enemy brought in.  Terrific thunderstorm at night - gloriously wet & no shelter.  Sig struck dead by lightning.12thHeavy shelling 16 allied planes bomb Jacko returning.  13th Big attack at dusk.13thWater Medjel.  6 am leave 331 11.30 am through Juli's and erect at Suffrie Gabri - Hill 154 at 2.30 PM stay night.14thLeave 1 PM and gallop through Beit Duras, Esdud Burkah, Beshit and arrive Yebna 6 PM as fresh as a bad oyster.  After a hell of a rough ride. Engine peters out with the rough trip.15thCamel W/T take over while we repair the engine.16th Leave Yeknah 10.30 am and Reg & I gallop to Devan and meet Freda.  Give kiddies rides up the street.  Change engines with W8.  Engine peters out.17th 300 enemy arrive.18thLondon W/T arrive terrific thunderstorm at night.  Done up and wet through.
