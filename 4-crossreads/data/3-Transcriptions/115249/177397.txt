<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Flag</p>
<p>To - Berrima</p>
<p>Date - 11 9 4</p>
<p>Time - 2 45</p>
<p>Brigadier, Boat is being sent for you now as I should like to see you, I have sent a message to Cr Beresford to say that if he cannot keep open his communications he had better retire and wait reinforcements.</p>
