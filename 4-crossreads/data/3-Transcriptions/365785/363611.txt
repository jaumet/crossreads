<p>With love<br/>To All at "The Wurley"</p>
<p>From Mick</p>
<p>7th F.A. Brigade</p>
<p>Christmas Greetings</p>
<p>This Xmastide<br/>we're far away,<br/>Yet memories ever roam;<br/>A Merry Xmas &ndash;<br/>bright New Year<br/>To all at Home<br/>Sweet Home.</p>
<p>W.S.H.</p>
<p>1916<br/>Lark Hill Camp<br/>Salisbury Plain<br/>England.</p>
<p>Gnr. George W. Bleach, 26th Bty. 7th F.A.B.</p>
