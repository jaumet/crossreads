<p>As per usual I picked up a good many practical hints &ndash; The men lie on ambulance stretchers on the floor &ndash; The sooner the authorities make a base at Cairns or Townsville &amp; transmit the sick there out of this fever den the better I should have thought &ndash;<br/>I talked a good bit to Dr Fitzherbert (Captain) S. is Major &ndash; He was a jolly young chap but didn't seem to relish his job - I advised him to keep right up to his work &amp; learn all he could &amp; not to worry &ndash; It's the humid heat that takes the energy out of the men &ndash; Water 80 &ndash; air 84 &ndash; wet bulb 81 &ndash; A lot of moisture &ndash; If your fan don't act in your cabin you've got to leave &ndash; The juice failed at 2 am this morning &amp; I had to take my bed &amp; clear &ndash; I couldn't stick it at all &ndash; After seeing &amp; hearing all I could &ndash; I joined<strike>with</strike> Gordon &amp; Gauntlet &amp; we walked to the Gardens &amp; the native Hospital which seemed filthier than ever &ndash; then we went</p>
