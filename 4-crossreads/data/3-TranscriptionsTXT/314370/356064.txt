
August, 1916Thursday 3We sailed shortly after breakfast.  Accompanied by a destroyer.Franconia armed with 6" gum mounted right aft.Told off to life boat stations & have to carry life belts wherever we go.
Friday 4continue to move on a Zig Zag course to Malta which we expect to reach Saturday morning.Boat stations at 10.30 am.spend a couple of hours on the signal bridge.
