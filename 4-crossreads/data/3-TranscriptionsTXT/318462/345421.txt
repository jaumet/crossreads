
thank goodness glad to see the last of the malingerers. Pat going to Amiens for another Blighter named Price who is under sentence of 5 years but escaped.Moving today but do not know where Staff & self are going.The ground is getting a little firmer & would soon dry if the weather lasted.deferred Pay 36-9-0 - 60/3. 84 francs. funny game this war here we are just near Pashendale Ridge & the guns roaring night & day & men getting killed every minute or so or wounded & just across from us is a Band Practising.shifted to Day light Corner near Messines Ridge now or what is left of it nothing but shifting lately another one Tomorrow but not far.
Wednesday 14/17dense fog this morning the beautiful weather of the last two days couldn't continue especially as the guns have been firing like mad for 4 or 5 days now if the hun is wining in Italy he's certainly not doing so here. Moving again to day about 500 yds only. deferred pay. 36-10-6. - 64/9 fritz dropped two of his large bombs here the other morning killing one man & twelve horses just been looking at the holes about 10 ft deep & 30 ft Round "some bomb".  Letter from Lucy also Syd.fog lifted in the Afternoon. Am on night duty with my men 12 hours. Cookers stores water etc.
