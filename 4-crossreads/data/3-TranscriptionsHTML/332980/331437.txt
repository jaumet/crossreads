<p>Wagons, she Steamed well also pulled &amp; soon we reached the top &amp; down hill we went passing several Stations &amp; eventually arrived at Hesdignel &amp; after a delay of about 1 hour, we continued on &amp; into Bolougne arriving there at 8.30 &amp; after stowing our Train went into the Outreau Depot &amp; signed off at 9.30, As the hut I had previously occupied had been pulled down we all had to sleep in a Bell Tent, as I had 2 Blankets &amp; a Waterproof Sheet &amp; a big sack I was OK for the night.
