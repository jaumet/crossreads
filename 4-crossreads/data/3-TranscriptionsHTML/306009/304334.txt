<p>1915<br/>May. 28 Fri<br/>Another mighty warm day to-day - must have been fully 110&deg; but Cairo heat is very dry &amp; therefore bearable.<br/>Another chap - Birch Roberts - came into the Office to-day from Mena- now 3 of us.<br/>Our daily state shows 434 in Hospital.<br/>Egypt contains a wonderful variety of nationalities &amp; many people can speak English, French, Arabic, Greek, &amp; Italian which are the languages mostly in use.</p>
