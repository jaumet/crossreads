I spent the night there & set off home about 9 A.M. walking through Millencourt near where I picked up a lorry which took me to Albert from there I managed to get to Le Sars where I located Will Harris's grave, a great friend of Morris's   Near here I picked up a lorry to Bapaume & later an ambulance in which I finished my journey having covered in all about 36 miles.  Now I have just finished a letter to Will which I will send over to him tomorrow Saturday 19th May     Just an ordinary day with Fritz sending a few over near by.
