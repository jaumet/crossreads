<p>Time's pendulum is on the swing<br/>And Autumn dulls the Summer sky<br/>Like Summer birds our thoughts take wing<br/>And joyfully, they homeward fly.</p>
<p>These birds of thoughts and mem'ries dear,<br/>Our greetings take o'er leagues of foam:<br/>A merry Xmas, bright New Year,<br/>To all our folks and friends at home.</p>
<p>S.W.H.</p>
