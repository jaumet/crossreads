 Wednesday 18 July 1917. 
 On the mules again this morning. Thrown straight away but tackled another &amp; got through the morning with him. They are obstinate &quot;pig headed&quot; animals. Handed in my name at 11 a.m. for leave to Moascar this evening. 
 Tent orderly again today. Wrote up some post card views of Ismailia. 
 Went on leave to Moascar main camp reported back at 9.15 pm. All troops in Isolation area being inoculated against Cholera &amp; Para Typhoid tonight up till 1 a.m. tomorrow morning. 
 Guard taken off with exception of real Iso. guard. Quarantine flags taken away also from main camp.
