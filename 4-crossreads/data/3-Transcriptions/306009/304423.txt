<p>1915<br/>Aug. 27 Fri.<br/>Dropped in at the Gezira Skating Rink to-night for a bit &ndash; think I must start &amp; learn sooner or later.<br/>In the afternoon borrowed Sergeant Middleton's camera and took some photos round the gardens. Hope they come out alright!</p>
