<p>the 7th &amp; promised to return to lunch.  Went by tram into town &amp; left a note for any of our cars which might visit the C.C.S.  Returned to lunch &amp; after, my pal returned to town with me and I managed to draw 40 francs at the pay office, so that I was safe once more.  Booked seats at a theatre to see Mignon, comic opera, best seats were 3.50 &amp; ranged down to 0.85 &amp; went out again to the 7th for tea &amp; as no car had arrived to take us home, was forced to stay the night, so went to our old friends.  They were pleased to see us again &amp; said they would be up at 10.30 when I expected to be back from the theatre.  Took along some biscuits &amp; lollies for the youngsters as a thank offering.  Mignon was very good indeed &amp; it turned out a first rate evening, I eventually returning</p>
