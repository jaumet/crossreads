<p>Sunday 21 May 1916<br/>Very little doing today.  Had most of day off.  Nothing of importance going.</p>
<p>Monday 22 May 1916<br/>Working on water tanks &amp; Drinking troughs.  Still nice and cool.</p>
<p>Tuesday 23 May 1916<br/>General work about camp &ndash; building &ndash; waterworks etc.</p>
<p>Wednesday 24 May 1916<br/>Nothing fresh going.  Coy. still carrying out building &ndash; water works, work in trenches etc.</p>
<p>Thursday 25 May 1916<br/>Same work as usual &ndash; building etc.  Nothing fresh going.  Still nice and cool.</p>
<p>Friday 26 May 1916<br/>Working on buildings, water supply etc.  Nothing fresh going.</p>
<p>Saturday 27 May 1916<br/>Work as usual on buildings, etc.  Had Kit Inspection, expect to be going away.</p>
<p>Sunday 28 May 1916<br/>Welsh R.Es came today to relieve us.  Packed up ready to leave.  Expect to leave tomorrow.</p>
<p>Monday 29 May 1916<br/>Marched to Rail Head today &amp; from there on to "Ferry Post" where we'll stay until going to Moaskar.</p>
<p>Tuesday 30 May 1916<br/>Traing [Training] in usual work today besides fixing up camp.  Nothing fresh going.</p>
<p>Wednesday 31 May 1916<br/>Bridging Instruction &amp; other Field work.</p>
<p>Thursday 1 June 1916<br/>Pontooning &amp; bridging on "Suez Canal".  Constructed [indecipherable] bridge.</p>
<p>Friday 2 June 1916<br/>Demolition today.  Fired all surplus "Training" gun cotton away.</p>
<p>Saturday 3 June 1916<br/>Marched to Moaskar, distance about 6 miles.</p>
