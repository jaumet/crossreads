 Thursday 6 April 1916 Swim in morning. An issue of one blanket all round. Bayonet drill. Advance guard action in afternoon. 
 Friday 7 April 1916 Church parade before breakfast. Took my turn as tent orderly. Had a small route march of about 8 miles. Was enoculated in afternoon then had a lecture from a Sergeant who had served in South Africa.
