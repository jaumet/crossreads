 5. I have had an attack of enteritis but am feeling much better just now. It may have been due to bad water or so much mud in the food last month. 
 The Huns have been going in for some night enterprises lately with their 'planes &amp; we have been getting a few bombs dropped in the locality. There are plenty of good targets in the vicinity but it is a hard job to drop bombs accurately &amp; the Huns' recent efforts were attended with very little success. None came over last night &ndash; due to the fact that it was snowing.
