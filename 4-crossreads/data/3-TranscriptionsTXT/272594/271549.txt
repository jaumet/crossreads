 got a crack down here 
 7th Left here for Albert we were lucky for we struck cars &amp; got a ride right into Albert &amp; so saved us a long weary walk. 
 Jock &amp; I went over to Meaulte to see Len, we soon found him he was checking loads of trucks as they went through he was surprised to see Jock &amp; I, we had tea here &amp; I got some souvenirs from Len to take over, after tea we all walked back to our camp.
