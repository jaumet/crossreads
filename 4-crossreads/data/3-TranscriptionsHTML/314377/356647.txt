<p>About the 8th November word came through that I was put on a boat-roll. This meant that I would expect to soon be back home again. I had a farewell feast at the home of one of the Salvationists and was presented with a little book "Soldier's Guide" and thanked for (it) my services In reply I made a speech</p>
<p>I was a good deal out in my estimate of the time of leaving however left on Jany 19th!</p>
