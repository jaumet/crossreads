<p>useless, &amp; what a weary walk we had nothing but mud, mud, &amp; still more mud we arrived at the camping ground thoroughly knocked out, &amp; to make matters worse most of the boys boots are ruined &amp; I know the nails stuck into my feet until they bled, but still I plodded on, we will be reequipped in a few days or so for our clothes are absolutely ruined &amp; only &frac12; the boys have rifles.<br/>7th.  One of the coldest nights I ever experienced was last night &amp; I never had a wink of sleep, &amp; our big guns kept up a perfect roar.<br/>About 11 oclock this morning we shifted out in a heavy storm of rain &amp; we had to plough our way through many feet of slimy mud, the 9th Bn are taking our place in the line</p>
