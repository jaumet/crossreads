  
 Wed. 24th May 
 Foot healthy & nearly healed now. Yesterday afternoon Dr. Cockinos paid the second visit & Mrs. Nyland also visited me. She is very kind to me & it was good of her. I can't really understand her.  I never feel perfectly at ease when talking to her & always have an irresistible tendency to smile, & she seems to half smile now & again.
