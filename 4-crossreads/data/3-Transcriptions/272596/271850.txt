<p>all I wish for now is to be allowed to settle down quietly on the land somewhere &amp; work towards my goal for I have a certain thing in view &amp; I shall not be satisfied until I get that far.</p>
<p>Got caught on the boat deck this afternoon in a heavy shower, I and a few more were laying under a lifeboat &amp; before we scarcely knew what had happened we were flooded out by this tropical storm &amp; had to beat it for down below</p>
<p>A big bird has been circling round our ship for hours, it settled at last "forrard" &amp; one of the boys caught it,  by the look of things it had got out of its track &amp; was just about exhausted they gave it some food &amp; after it had had a good rest it flew away</p>
