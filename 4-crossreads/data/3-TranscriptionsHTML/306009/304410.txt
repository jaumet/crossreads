<p>1915<br/>Aug. 14 Sat.<br/>A batch of nearly 200 British wounded admitted about midnight. Another landing has been affected on the Peninsula &ndash; this time by Kitchener's Army &ndash; heavy losses.<br/>Heard that Ben Champion &amp; Art. Felton have arrived in Egypt with the  7th  Reinforcements of the 1st Inf.</p>
