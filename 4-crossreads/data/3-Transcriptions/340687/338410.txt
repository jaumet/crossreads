<p>April, 1917. Monday 16<br/>Up at 9.15. Pumped in morning &amp; again afternoon, not going well. Overheating. Probably circulation bad. In evening went to pictures &ndash; fair &ndash; close by and afterwards to Water point. Day fairly fine &amp; sunny but squally rain in evening.</p>
