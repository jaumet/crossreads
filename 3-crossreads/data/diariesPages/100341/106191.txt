May 20th
Up 5-30 a.m. Past Cape Leeuin 11 p.m., 19/5/15, last of Australian coast. Batt. Ord. Cpl. for day. Beautiful calm weather sea. Bright sun all day, lovely sunset evening. Course N.N.W., 316 miles, 24 hrs. to noon today, position just below Freemantle. Had large double tooth taken, sitting on jam case during action, lovely ? reading during evening.
Covering ship with awning for Tropical climate.
