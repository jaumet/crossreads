 Fri. Feb. 4th -16 Horses on our guns shied at camels and bolted across desert. 
 Sat. Feb. 5th -16 Visited Cairo Zoo a very pretty and well laid out.  Animals and birds of every description from an elephant to a flea. 
 Sun. Feb. 6th -16 Church Service 8.15 A.M. 
 Mon. Feb. 7th -16 8 men from 13/1st F.A.B. sent to Tel-el-kiber for patrol duty. 
 Tues. Feb. 8th -16 On guard, weather very cold, wet and windy. 
 Wed. Feb. 9th -16 Still raining very hard everyone and everything wet.
