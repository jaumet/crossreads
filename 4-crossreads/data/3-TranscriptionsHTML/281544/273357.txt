<p>Thursday 8/11/17.<br/>All day long the aerial work of our planes was very exciting, just before lunch a Fritz Machine came over St Jean for Observation purposes, he was sailing around having things all his own way when out from a Cloud came one of ours &amp; made straight for Fritz. as our Machine was up above him.
