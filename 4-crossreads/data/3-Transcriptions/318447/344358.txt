<p>turned in in the YMCA tent &ndash; which is a sort of free hotel to all travellers on the line.</p>
<p>Next morning I found the place, got rid of the natives &amp; having lazed away the morning in the sun &amp; canteen I got the new natives &amp; caught the five oclock train which unfortunately refused to go any further than Deir sinneid so I spent the night there &amp;, having sent a wire in the morning to announce my arrival caught the seven train and arrived up here about lunch time</p>
