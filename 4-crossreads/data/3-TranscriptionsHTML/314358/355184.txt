<p>Dry as dust, rootless of the growth of better things made desolate by the curse of strained intelligence<br/>Empty of heart and soul, of reverence of love</p>
<p>Most of the lads in this draft are what we call new men; those who have not previously been to the front.</p>
<p>Of course they are greatly interested in the German prisoners, their general appearance, their uniforms caps etc.</p>
<p>Yes, as I have already said, all that the Australian cares to see has been seen</p>
