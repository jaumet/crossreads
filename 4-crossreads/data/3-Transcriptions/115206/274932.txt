<p>December  1918.<br/>Monday 30</p>
<p>At Sea. Red Sea. Indian Ocean.<br/>Weather warmer by a great deal, and the climate is beginning to swelter along a fair amount. Ran into a heavy ground swell.<br/>Passed the twelve apostles early in the afternoon. Played in the Bridge Tournament and lost in the first round by 28 points. Played cribbage for about three hours and lost 2/6 on the deal.<br/>Days run very poor. 254 miles only.</p>
