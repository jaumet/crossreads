<p>May 14th<br/>Now that time is drawing near for return to France I am feeling very loathe to depart from all that is so dear to me in England. Marl returns from Manchester &amp; makes the acquaintance of Mrs.Scales.  Marl &amp; I enjoy a sumptuous lunch &amp; then journey to Town where we spend afternoon at the "Palladium" which has on its Bill Harry Weldon. J.N. Rickaby. Ruth Vincent. Chas Austin etc.- Marl reluctantly returns to Wiltshire and I return to Elm Bank accompanied by Anita - Marland by the way is both</p>
