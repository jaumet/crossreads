
30 March 1915Tuesday
Have the pleasure of meeting several old friends namely Leuit Stan Perry   &  Sgt Jack Holman   &  Cpl Nelson all from Auburn   &  In our Bat. All very glad to see us.
1915 March 31Wednesday
13th Bat out field firing   &  Outpost work so have the camp to ourselves.do a bit of drill   &  have a half day offMake first trip to Cairo. Find it the dirtiest   &  most immoral place on the Globe.Have a delightful dinner of Soup, Roast, Fish, chicken, salad,   &  dessertReturn to camp 9.30pm
