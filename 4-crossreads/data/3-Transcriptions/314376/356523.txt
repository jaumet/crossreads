<p>212<br/>We were a very small portion of the batallion in comparison to what had set out. I think our strength was 649 going in : coming out 50!</p>
<p>After a battle like Bullecourt it is no wonder one's nerves were frayed. We had to pass some 60 pounders on the way back and the discharge made us feel very jumpy.</p>
<p>There was, so I heard, here abouts, a dressing station in close proximity to the Artillery!</p>
<p>Arriving at Beaullencort how thankful I would feel to God for his preservation of me.</p>
<p>I was to settle down once more for a time anyway.</p>
