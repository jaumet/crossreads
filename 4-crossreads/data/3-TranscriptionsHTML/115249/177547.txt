<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - O/C Inf.</p>
<p>To - Brigadier</p>
<p>Date - 12 9 4</p>
<p>Time - 12.25am</p>
<p>Would like beef &amp; biscuit rations sent before I move off</p>
<p><u>Reply</u>&nbsp;Cannot send rations before you move. You can draw for biscuit from Herbertshohe naval supply. which was landed from Berrima on Friday afternoon.</p>
