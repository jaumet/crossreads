<p>Thursday 27 December 1917</p>
<p>BACK at Moascar owing to Railway line being washed away by rain have to wait another few days <strike>&amp; could have been with my little wife all this time.</strike>  Having dinner with Col. <strike>McLeish</strike> tonight in his MESS. Did I mention seeing STANLEY BRAIM he has improved a lot &amp; is now a Cadet in the Flying Corps &amp; may get his commission, he is not drinking now. Letter from Laura &amp; redirected one from May, please I did not get photo of Jack &amp; <u>Douglas &amp; I am</u> a Major not a Captain now for a year.</p>
