<p>17 April 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Make a short excursion to Cairo through the native Quarter   &amp;  Moslem bazaars.<br/>Our men build a barbed wire enclosure round the Quarter Guard to give the sentries a better chance of keeping the prisoners in.</p>
<p>1915 April 18<br/>Sunday<br/>2nd After Easter</p>
<p>I miss Church parade having to make out a whole lot of lists of names   &amp;  numbers.</p>
