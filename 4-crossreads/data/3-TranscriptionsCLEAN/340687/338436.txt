
May, 1917. Saturday 12Very warm day. Up at 8 & helped Burnop to clean up - am waiting ready to return to Coy if not coming out. Coy not coming out yet so set out at 10.30 after some beer and caught a lorry but had a wait on road for some time. Walked from Bapaume to Bancourt & on to Coy near aviation Gd. about 3 miles after passing 1st D.H.Q. Price M.C. Dare & Jacobs M.M. & me promoted l-c don't know why. Got tea - letters and read till dark. No sign of moving. Our gramophone in full swing.
