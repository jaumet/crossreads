 Monday 17 April 1916 A dull grey day with a light drizzle of rain in the morning &amp; a heavy rainstorm in the evening. The wind was of course cold &amp; biting. At the training ground during the morning until 1.30 &amp; during the afternoon censoring letters &amp; fixing up company matters, the men getting their first pay of 15 francs since they left Egypt.
