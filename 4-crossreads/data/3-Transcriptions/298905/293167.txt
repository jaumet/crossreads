<p>happened except perhaps it was quite rough enough for me, &amp; the great swag of officers on leave compared to the men it was very noticeable, &amp; they in their usual selfish style took up about half the accommodation of the ship.<br/>We were escorted nearly all the way over by a destroyer, the first sight I had of old Englands shore's were the white chalk cliffs which we have all read &amp; heard about so often, who would have thought a short 2 years ago that I would be crossing the English Channel on my way for a well earned holiday.<br/>Folkestone where we got off at is a pretty big place, &amp; very neat &amp; clean all the houses seem to be built the same way, you can easily see that it is a very ancient place, after getting off the boat, we slipped straight along</p>
