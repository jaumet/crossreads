<p>June 23/17 Peronne Rd near Bapaume<br/>pulled in here again with the Company <strike>travelling</strike> from Behencourt in one long days trek travelling through Albert Pozieres, Le Sars. all last years battlefield is now a blaze of colour &ndash; covered with wildflowers <strike>where</strike> last winter one would have thought nothing would ever grow again &ndash;</p>
<p>Albert itself is being cleaned up, the least damaged houses being repaired and the inhabitants are beginning slowly to come back &ndash; a dreary homecoming it must be.</p>
