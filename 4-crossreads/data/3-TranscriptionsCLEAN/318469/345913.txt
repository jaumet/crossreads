8.4.15First Field Coy boarded Ionian
9thNothing important  [indecipherable].
10Preparation for landing Dardanelles - forming barrel Piers etc.
11thLittle work - barrel Piers etc.
12thNothing interest - Piers - All going well.
13thSame as yesterday
14thDid a little barrel Piering. Things quiet.
15thStill on Ionian.  Nothing fresh.
16thStill doing a little work. Nothing of interest.
17thAwaiting orders, nothing fresh.
18Usual routine.  Tired waiting
19thTransferred to "Malda".  Got Webb equipment.  Heard that a Torpedo boat "Turkish" attempted to torpedo a transport coming in a few nights ago but missed & was in turn beached. Panic on transport resulted in a good many being drowned
20thNothing of much consequence today. Was told that we are to form part of first landing party.
