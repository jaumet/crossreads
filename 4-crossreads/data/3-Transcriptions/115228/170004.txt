<p><u>Nov 4th</u>:&nbsp; Had a great deal of trouble in getting water for our horses, but were eventually successful.</p>
<p>News from the remainder of the Front is good especially around Gaza where most of the enemy&#39;s strong points are gradually falling into our hands.&nbsp; Our air service seems to have established complete ascendancy over the enemy planes lately. &amp; a number of German machines have recently been brought down.&nbsp;</p>
