<p>Dec 25<br/>Xmas Day<br/>I know the little Mother will be at Church &amp; the lines she has written me 'Till we meet again' I have just re-read. Capt. White is O/C &amp; there are only the Q'land Reinforcements &amp; my own on board.<br/>Our boat is a German Prize boat originally the Pfalz &amp; had only made one voyage when we boarded her &ndash;<br/>Wool, Ores, frozen Meat Crew are all strangers to each other, the Skipper a good man<br/>concert in the evening</p>
