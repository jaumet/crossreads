<p>H.M.A.T. Ulimaroa<br/>18/4/19</p>
<p>The Secretary<br/>1st Light Horse Comfort Fund<br/>Sydney</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Vernon<br/>On behalf of members of 1st L.H. Regt. I wish to thank you for your hearty greetings, we all appreciated very highly your messages of welcome and for the many kindnesses extended to us whilst on service by your Committee.</p>
<p>Again thanking you all and hoping that we may have the opportunity within a few days of meeting you all again<br/>Kindest regards.<br/>Yours Sincerely<br/>TEW. Irwin Lt Col.<br/>1st L.H.Regt.</p>
