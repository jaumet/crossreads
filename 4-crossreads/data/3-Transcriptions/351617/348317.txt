<p>2 days before, suffering 9000 casualties in 11 hours fighting, and as we were their first Reinforcements we were soon to get a move on &amp; fill the gaps in their ranks. Then followed strenuous days of training, Bombing, Practice Shooting, &amp; Bayonet Drill, &amp; every one knew that his life depended on these things, besides the ones he had left behind so we settled down to do our best.</p>
<p>About the 21st of August the 7th of the 30th, &amp; 1st of 53rd, 54th, 55th, &amp; 56th, Battalions marched into camp &amp; among them were several of the old boys I had known at home. By now the draft list was out &amp; we were soon to get a move on so everything was hurry getting all deficiencies in kit &amp; equipment Etc, &amp; in our spare time writing home &amp; going to see any of the boys who had just arrived with the latest news of our own home towns.</p>
