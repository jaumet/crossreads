 March 3rd Harry Elliot &amp; self have a pleasant morning walk down &amp; under the Dean Bridge &amp; Gardens &ndash; In the afternoon an Australian (in company with a Lassie) set out for Crammond &ndash; a grand walk in which much picturesque scenery is encountered &ndash; The [indecipherable] &amp; the Ocean. Returned Edinburgh. March 4th Letters written &amp; received &ndash; "Poems of Sentiment" (Wilcox) &ndash; O. Henry's &ndash; Last day in Edinburgh &ndash; Evening spent at "59".
