
[Thursday 4 May 1916]
Court again.  Finished CM at noon & came Cairo with Judge Advocate worked it well to see Nowell & stay 2 days, till Court is Dissolved-Nowell is thin but well has 3 months Holiday & goes to England lucky beggar he is a Colonel very like his old Father & a great row of Ribbons for Gippy decorations.He wishes to be remembered to you all-I have taken a couple of Photos which I will send you-We have had a great yarn, but he tires easily-
