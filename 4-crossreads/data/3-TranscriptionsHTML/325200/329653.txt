<p>Dueidar<br/>2nd November<br/>We reached this delectable spot this evening about 5.30 pm. And now I am in bed writing by the light of a very inadequate moon. Used as I am to this business now of making camp still even now I am influenced by the sort of glamour which is undoubtedly attendant upon a brigade of cavalry   <strike>entering</strike>   advancing . The noises both of men  &amp;  horses  &amp;  the place lit up like a gin palace by countless twinkling fires &ndash; the whole effect is decidedly picturesque and &ndash; to use an Americanism &ndash; it "gets" you sometimes.</p>
