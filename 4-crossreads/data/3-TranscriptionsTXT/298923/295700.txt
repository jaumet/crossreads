3 February 1918 SundayWarm &amp; fine.  Field Officer today &amp; instead of Church attended a lecture on Jerusalem-Good news tonight re Berlin strikers hope it is all true.Recd. parcel from the wife she has gone Port Said.Have been busy fixing up mounted sports for the men.  Am attending a Topographical course under Captain McLean (who is a Licensed Surveyor) tomorrow
