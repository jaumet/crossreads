<p>Today, Tuesday,. It has been a lonely day. We had the bosch planes over again last night. They came over Sunday evening &amp; for an hour or so the night was wild with shot &amp; shell &amp; dropping bombs last night, it was also very lively.<br/>This evening we had a Concert at C Coy Billet which was a real good show.</p>
<p>Wednesday Sept 5<br/>The weather has been beautifully free &amp; clean Nothing of importance today. Service at the YMCA Hall this evening. Mjr Mackenzie gave address I led singing</p>
<p>Thursday 6th<br/>Warm &amp; sultry culminating with a thunderstorm this evening A concert which we were to hold this evening had to be abandoned</p>
