<p>Thur June 15th &ndash; 16<br/>Received my 1st parcel.<br/>Met Ossie Whaites who is looking A1.</p>
<p>Fri June 16th &ndash; 16<br/>Last night at 11.30 the gas alarm was sounded and after waiting for 1 &frac12; hours we were told to get to bed again and then at 2AM the alarm came again and after another 2 hours wait we were ordered to bed again. I was fed up as I had to come on duty at 4AM.</p>
