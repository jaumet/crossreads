21/4/18
One indeed did fall short and wounded our cook Robson in the arm. It burst in the roof and splinters came through. Our 5th Divn. Lewis Gunners dispute with the Corps Aust. Air squadron the honour of bringing down Baron Richtofen the German crack flier. Towards midnight a fair amount of shelling went on and some gas was mixed in but nothing much. His activity in the way of gunning has shown an increase the last few days. A few wounded men came through here during the night.
