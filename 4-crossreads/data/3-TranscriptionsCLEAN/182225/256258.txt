 MAY   SATURDAY 20   1916 
 Very windy & cold. Still they keep me in hospital but may get out tomorrow  It seems ages since I came in here. Read all day. The time we took writing letters yesterday was wasted as we are not calling at Freemantle but making straight through to Durban.
