
I came across an old Varsity friend to day. He is a barrister from Brisbane & is acting as batman to one of the Queensland officers. We have plenty of deck games including cricket & deck hockey the latter is a bit too strenuous for me but is a jolly good game. We had a dance last night and as a result I am a bit stiff to day The moustache is progressing most satisfactorily - I did not want to grow one, knowing how you hate them, but it is laid down in Kings Regulations that officers should grow them & it is necessary also to shave clean the terminal half inch on each side.
