June 9th  Sunday. We started work at 8.A.M. & knocked off at 12.30 & had dinner - I went down to the pool directly after dinner & repaired the spring board & the others came down later with a boat they brought home from the ^Somme canal at Amiens. we had a swim & took the boat back to the cache. there was a cold wind blowing - We walked to Picquigny with Zita, Marie & a friend of theirs, & went up to the old castle, bought some fruit etc. & returned to Belloy at 8.P.M. stayed with them till 10.P.M.
 
June 10th  It rained a bit in the morning - we had the engine running & pretty well finished the other work. putting two re-tyred rear wheels on & one front. we did'nt go for a swim to-day as it was too cold. We attended our French lesson in the evening as usual
 
June 11th  Finished the job & took the bus out for a test run in the afternoon
