
He came at 4. p.m. & motored me out to Ras-El-Tin Convalescent Home - Sisters Ross  &  Finlay are there also Captain McLarin (Cris Murray's beloved). I had letters & parcels for them from different ones at The Palace. It is a lovely spin out there. Had afternoon tea there, saw over the hospital & had a beautiful view of the Harbour from the Flat-roof.
