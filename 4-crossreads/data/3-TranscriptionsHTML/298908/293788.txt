This joint is always crowded with society belles big heads officers &amp; etc. &amp; I was the only soldier under an officers rank in there, nearly everyone is scared of going into these high class places but it has no effect on me.  I rather like the experience, &amp; it gives you confidence in yourself.<br/>After coming out I strolled down Picadilly &amp; eventually into the Hippodrome to see the revised edition of "Box o Tricks" for I had a few hours to spare before my train left.
