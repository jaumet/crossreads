Feb. 15th
Reading & Writing.
Matinee at the Strand which features Arthur Bourchier.  Noel Coward.  Alex Scott Gatty & Kyrle Bellew - A poor play with no decent situation or dialogue and certainly no oc-cup opportunities for good acting.
Return to Elm Bank and have very pleasant evening.  Read "Nonsense Novels" & "De Omnibus".

Morning of writing and preparation
