<p>7<br/>laughable. &ndash; "An Egyptian Fantasia" was the title given to a rather accurate imitation of the adventures of two [indecipherable] who came to Egypt with the Aus 2nd Division.<br/>Really most [indecipherable] done in parts &amp; caused shrieks of laughter. &ndash; Some of those who were supposed to be Arab Guides, street sellers etc were really excellent. &ndash; The Stage was got up almost as a replica of a scene in one Egyptian Street I have seen. &ndash;<br/>Another item on the programme consisted of Mouth Organ Selections (really well played) &amp; Concertina Selections (also really well played)<br/>There was another Farce, but I left before it took place.<br/>Just received a nice little note from May Reid &ndash; that was.<br/>Au-revoir &ndash; Much love from<br/>Always your loving brother,<br/>Fred.<br/>Love to Mrs Read &amp; the old Doctor<br/>Remembrance to Mrs Garland &amp; Miss Jean. Fred</p>
