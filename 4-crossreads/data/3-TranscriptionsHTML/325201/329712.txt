<p>And Jimmie Mcgowen  &amp;  wounded Stanger Leathes &amp;  Grey amongst others. That was during Sari Bair on the "Dards, a regular nightmare from the 7th - 15th. In Aug 16 at about 6.30 am at Romani I had to galop over a ridge into what I firmly believed was the end of all things. Enough to make anyone superstitious.<br/>As I say I was  going to write in a sparkling etc manner, when they decided to inoculate me for all kinds colours &amp;  species</p>
