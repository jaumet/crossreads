<p>Wednesday 8 August 1917.</p>
<p>Saddled up straight away in marching order and went for an hours jaunt.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Washing &amp; grooming parade then to water &amp; back to feed.</p>
<p>Arranged to have the name of Regtl&nbsp;paper changed from Stretcher to &quot;Cacelot&quot; [&quot;The Cacolet&quot; was the journal of the Camel Field Ambulance], &nbsp;and a concert with outside &amp; 14 A.G.H. artists is being arranged in our huts for Friday evening.</p>
<p>Another attempt to finish a letter to D&#39;nong and finished it too.</p>
<p>Went on leave to Cairo &amp; regd&nbsp;three envelopes with handkerchiefs enclosed.</p>
<p>Met Mal Jamieson 3rd L.H.F.A. who is down from <s>Gaza</s> the line for 7 days leave.&nbsp;</p>
