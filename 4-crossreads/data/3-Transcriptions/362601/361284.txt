<p>19 October, Sunday</p>
<p>Catch 9.15 boat with Otto &amp; Amy. Go to beach with N in afternoon. We go to Melville at night, arrive Home minuit. Up at daybreak almost because of roosters etc, next morning.</p>
<p>20 October, Monday</p>
<p>Meet Dave's Girl in tram. Get in the wrong train too. Home again &amp; early to bed.</p>
