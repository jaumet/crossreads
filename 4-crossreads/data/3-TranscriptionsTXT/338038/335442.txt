
82  France  26th Feby 1918Base Records
My Dear Father & MotherYour most welcome letters of 28th Dec & 7th Jany received here today.  Two from Father & two from Mother.  The Jany letters seem to have come very speedily & needless to say were eagerly devoured for news of you - While on the subject, I'm now told my cable for Fathers birthday was duly despatched so trust it reached its destination.Your letters as usual were full of interest, and I only regret my own fall so far short of the mark in that respect.I am afraid one of my previous letters built up false hopes about the possibility of my obtaining furlough - I was I must admit rather hopeful they
