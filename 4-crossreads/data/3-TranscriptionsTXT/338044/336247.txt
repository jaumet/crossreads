Work until 5.30 pm    & after dinner go to the Cinema              , which is rather good. Having missed the last tram home, take a brisk walk of half an hour & arrive at 11-10 pm    Cinema price, first class seats 1franc for soldiers and 11/2 f. for civilians
20th August:- SundayBeing tired, did not wake this morning until 8 am, too late for Church & got off to work: 9am  The casualties show no signs of decreasing & we put through until 12-30, nearly 300 for 2nd Division   A nice hot dinner, well prepared in English style, awaits us, at our new found English lady's place    & I  very much appreciate it, as the weather is rather cool & looks
