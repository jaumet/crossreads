<p>There was a diving board in one of the Somme Pools&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    an inviting place on a hot day &ndash; The first man dived &amp; never came up &ndash; a second man went in after him &ndash; he never came up The rest investigated &amp; found them both impaled on bayonets under the water.</p>
