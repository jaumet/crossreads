<p>Tuesday 28<br/>Letter from Dock that everything has gone wrong &amp; that I cannot get into the offices.</p>
<p>Friday 31<br/>Up till late the weather has been very unsettled raining off &amp; on every day but now we are having beautifully warm days.</p>
<p>Sept., 1917<br/>Saturday 1<br/>Walk all over the country to Wallop on the Southampton road. The country &amp; villages &amp; especially the trees about here are beautiful &amp; this is Salisbury Plain</p>
