<p>&amp; also went through one of the silk weaving mills, all the work in this mill was being done with old hand spinning wheels. At one of the native bazaars they tried very hard to persuade us to buy a lot of Amber cigarettes (10'- for box of 50), &amp; showed us numerous letters of recommendation from people of note, including one from Mrs Asquith, wife of the recent British premier. We have much appreciated the good meals, hot baths etc of the past couple of days, truly great luxuries</p>
