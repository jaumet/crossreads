  
 3rd August Fine day. Our aeroplanes very active. The germans get terribly annoyed with them (there is nearly always a half a dozen up) and they fire their antiaircraft guns at them nearly all day. 5th August Everything ready to move back to the trenches. We are to relieve the 56th Bn who will then take over our billets here in Fleurbaix. The Bn left Fleurbaix for the trenches about 9.30 PM. I saw the boys off and then got my gear and went off myself in the opposite direction I slept at the QMasters. 6th August Today is the anniversary of the Lone Pine charge of
