<p>to hurry back to our ship &amp; just returned in time. Steamed out of Harbour at 5. p.m. Oh, it was a beautiful  &amp; wonderful sight. Saw mine-sweepers, monitors etc. We were fearfully tired, so soon after dinner turned in.<br/>Friday &amp; Saturday were very disagreeable days, a heavy swell on. I was not sick but felt very heavy &amp;  heady</p>
