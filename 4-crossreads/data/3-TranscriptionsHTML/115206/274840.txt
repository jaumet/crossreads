<p>September 1918.<br/>Friday 27</p>
<p>Tincourt and we are still here.<br/>Weather wet during the morning, having a farily busy time with wire and etc.spent a fair amount of time on different work.<br/>Georgie Baker returned from hospital<br/>Am inclined to believe that the big stunt comes off on Sunday morning, but could not say with any reliability<br/>Met Les Fuller, and one or two other boys.<br/>Spent a good night under the new blankets and dugouts.</p>
