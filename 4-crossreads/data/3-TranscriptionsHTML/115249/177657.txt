<p>From - Brigadier.</p>
<p>To - Rear Admiral C.</p>
<p><u>12th Septr.14:</u></p>
<p>(6-5 a.m.)</p>
<p>My only object suggesting retention wireless station was to effect repairs and turn to our own use. &nbsp;If, however, not so required, recommend abandonment of same and withdrawal of all troops. from there to Herbertshohe, and carry out original programme of Naval Reserve garrisoning Herbertshohe , balance of force direct to Rabaul.</p>
