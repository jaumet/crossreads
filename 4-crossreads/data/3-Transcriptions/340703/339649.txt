<p>October 1916<br/>Monday 9<br/>Sherika is an oasis in Libyan desert &ndash; very fertile on the oasis but bare and barren away from it.<br/>Settling down to camp routine.</p>
<p>Tuesday 10<br/>Capt Wright (an Englishman) is O.C. and is a peculiar man. Has several favourites in the Coy. He does not seem to like Australians. Lt. Grime one of the officers is a most useless fool. Lt Mills seems a decent chap and capable.</p>
