 Friday 30th 1917. Up again at 7 am &amp; paraded at 9 am &amp;  was  all was marched up to the Parade Ground &amp; had Company Drill until 12.15 Pm when we were dismissed for Dinner at 2 Pm we were again paraded for Roll Call &amp; then dismissed for the Day.
