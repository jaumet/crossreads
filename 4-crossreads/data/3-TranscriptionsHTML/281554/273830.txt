<p>Jan. 1st 1916<br/>Awoke to hear rain early morning.  No parade <strike>befor</strike> all day.  Spent from 10 a.m. till 3 p.m. at villages.  No rain falling all day, strong wind sprung up &amp; continued till after Lights out.</p>
<p>Jan. 2nd Sunday<br/>B. Coy. for duty, did none all day.  Afternoon went taking photos.</p>
<p>Jan. 3rd Monday<br/>Weather Fair but Strong bitterly cold wind.  Inspection Parade 9.30 a.m. till 11 a.m. by Brigadier &amp; English Staff Officer.  Warned for embarkation.</p>
