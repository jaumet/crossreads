<p>12 August 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Up for breakfast, -- difficulty of getting a wash. Resort to cupful of water in dixie.<br/>Parade &amp; knock round all day. meet Hulort Brown &amp; have good yarn. Mac. B &amp; Stewart &amp; self. cafe au lait. Disgusting soldiers. To bed, &amp; shickered mates. Murphy vomit.</p>
