  
 Monday 6/5/18 dull morning, like more rain, muster Parade. deferred Pay 49-9-6 Owe me 29/7. wrote to Syd & Viv. nothing doing much. 
 Tuesday. 7/5/18 Rained all night & is doing so now, came through the tiles & dropped all over us, heavy Bombardment of fritz's lines with gas etc guess he got a sweet dose & suppose he'll return it. The Yanks are arriving on this front at last. deferred Pay. 49-11-0 Owe me 31/7. wrote to Maude Been charged 10/1 for cable sent from Egypt 11/4/16 when will the B[dash] finish charging me with something anyway it's not me but Viv who sent Cable. leaves me 20/6 now. Turned out fine & hot Rumours that the hun is coming over Tomorrow morning, hope its not a repetition of Aubigny. 
 Wednesday. 8/5/18 fritz did not come over this morning as anticipated another one of his dates gone agly.  did not rain last night but a lot of gun fire all night. cloudy but fine today. deferred Pay. 49-12-6. owe me 22/6. Wrote to Mrs Parsons turned out fine & clear, cold wind blowing, guns very busy. 
 Thursday. 9/5/18 Beautiful Morning, old Frenchman here says Bon weather now, Yes for a couple of hours I suppose, getting very hot. guns kept at it nearly all night its to prevent fritz massing I think anyway prevention is better than cure. deferred Pay 49-14-0 Owe me 24/6. wrote to Loo Posted Parcel to Nell & letters to Nell, Syd, Viv, Maude, Loo, Mrs Parsons & Amos Williams. thank goodness that is done been carrying them for weeks.
