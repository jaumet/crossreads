<p>But other C.C. Stations have had to do all the work , so out M.O &ndash; (surgeons)   &amp;  theatre Sisters have gone to help there. There is to be a great Stunt soon, so shall have plenty to do then. We are very busy getting our wards etc fitted up.</p>
<p>Have not seen much of the new Colonel, but he is a very  busy man. Am sure we will like him very much. Dorothy did not get her leave, so we may be  able to have it same time.</p>
