<p>Sunday 30th<br/>Final alterations to Canberra Redoubt  thence to Lithgow Post.  Coy shifted camp N.W. some 300 yds. into deep hollow nearer Canal.</p>
<p>Monday 31st<br/>Siting different position for Lithgow Redoubt all day.  Very cold wind.</p>
<p>February commenced in check covered Book.</p>
<p>Diary<br/>May 11th 1915 &ndash; Jan. 31st /16</p>
