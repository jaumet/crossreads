<p>at least as long as we remained in this place it was very quiet.  Our work in the day was to fill sand bag's and do other useful work about the trench.</p>
<p>At night there were parties told off to build up the parapet, others to go out and put barbed wire entanglement's out and numerous other jobs were found for us.</p>
<p>While at this place I saw plenty of Aeroel fights and some real good ones at that.</p>
<p>Of course all this line along this part is only barrackades made of sand-bags.</p>
