<p>Shells landed on next billet to ours about 400 yards away</p>
<p>Thur Apr 27th &ndash; 16<br/>Our lads caught a spy &ndash; (of which there are several) a woman cutting the telephone wires.  Reported infantry attack tonight</p>
<p>Fri Apr 28th &ndash; 16<br/>At 11PM last night we had our first experience of a gas attack. What with everyone excited and rifles, mortars and guns firing, there was something doing.</p>
<p>At 12.30AM all quiet again</p>
<p>Sat Apr 29th &ndash; 16<br/>Our boys caught 2 more spies, both farmers, one</p>
