<p>his chair on one leg thrust his hands deeply into his pockets writhed and wriggled and finally jumped up and in a petulant voice refused to discuss the subject further. Apology was instantly tendered for the unintentional hurt by Mr. McKenzie which was gradually accepted by the unhurt one who finally agreed that the church had failed but had not failed and all ended happily.
