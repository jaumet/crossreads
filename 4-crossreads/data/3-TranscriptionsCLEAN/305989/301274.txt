
Sunday 16 September 1917On Day Stableman tied up 30 horses:  played bridge at night.  Wrote Nos. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 4e].
Monday 17 September 1917On fatigue on Officers Cookhouse.  Saw Guthrie.  Preparing to move to Kilo 9.
Tuesday 18 September 1917Left at 6.30 for Kilo 9, arriving about 11.30 & struck camp, picquet at night.  Awfully dusty & windy.
Wednesday 19 September 1917Making ourselves comfortable as possible.  Windy & Dusty.
Thursday 20 September 1917On Q.M. fatigue, bought some canteen stuff at Tommies canteen, windy & dusty.
Friday 21 September 1917Early morning parade & quiet day.  Wind lesser.
Saturday 22 September 1917Quiet day.
