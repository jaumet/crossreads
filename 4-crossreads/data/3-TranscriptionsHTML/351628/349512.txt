<p>Dear Eric<br/>We got word that Frank is in Hospital in the Reading Hospital, England &ndash; wounded in the arm &ndash; he is lucky to have escaped so long &ndash; almost 3 years over there &ndash; &amp; this is the first time he has been hit; no word of [indecipherable] yet &ndash; over 6 months now &ndash; should soon hear from Katie &ndash;</p>
