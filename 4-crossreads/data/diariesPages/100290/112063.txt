29 October 1916
Sunday 
One more Sunday at Front. The travesty of a peaceful Sabbath. Short tucker, Augment supply from canteen. Nothing doing today. Improve our dugout. Rain as usual. Intermittent bombardment all day night. our artillery do not give Fritz much rest Another great bundle of letters. ? 19. 50 letters in 3 days ? a record oh.! how welcome each one is
