<p>Friday 16th July<br/>Fresh meat for breakfast. How delightful it was and what a break in the monotony of our daily fare. I noticed that the Light Horse Brigades were getting Travelling Kitchens from funds collected in Brisbane and I thought that we should have one for our 7th Battery &ndash; and wrote home to that effect.</p>
<p>Each day to fill in moments when not reading or shelling the unspeakable Turks there were many odd jobs we did such as [Indecipherable] etc. We always kept ourselves shaved even while bullets or shells played merrily over the dug outs and trenches.  We got an issue of English War notes to-day. Small pieces of paper which hardly looked genuine</p>
