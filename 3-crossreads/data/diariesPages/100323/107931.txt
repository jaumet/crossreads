Our pilot brought us out a very winding path and we passed several ungainly monitors manned in our honour. A "U" boat (86) with English submarine experts on board was doing a fast trip steaming past us and giving up her secrets to Naval engineers. From the open sea before reaching the breakwater one could get a splendid view of the three Devon cities. Steering well out to sea we soon left England behind and the famous Eddystone lighthouse came into sight perched on a small rock ? lonely in the middle of the sea.
