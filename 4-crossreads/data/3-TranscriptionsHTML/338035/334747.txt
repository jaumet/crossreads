<p>No. 12039<br/>Priv. W. Pearson<br/>9th Field Ambulance<br/>3rd Aust. Division<br/>(9th Brigade)<br/>1st May 1916</p>
<p>2 Short St.<br/>Leichhardt.</p>
<p>May 1916</p>
<p>Leaving Sydney</p>
<p>Monday 1st<br/>Arriving at the Agricult. Ground after leave at 2 <strike>p.m.</strike> a.m. &amp; slept for about half an hour.<br/>Reveille at 3 a.m. when it was fairly cold.<br/>After a roll call &amp; a tedious wait we finally commenced to move at 5 a.m. &amp; friends &amp; relations joined us all along the route which was along Randwick Rd., Oxford St. to Darlinghurst thence Bourke St. to Wolloomoloo [Woolloomooloo] Mother, father, Harry, May (sis), W.B., Arch Flack.  Tom Jones joined us at Darlinghurst.  Arrived at Wharf at 6 a.m. &amp; I said good bye to all.  On Board the transport S.S. Benalla &amp; <strike>she</strike> after</p>
