<p>Febuary<br/>23rd   I got my discharge from hospital to-day &amp; am leaving for the company to-morrow.  I went to the reinforcements camp in Albert this afternoon &amp; was given permission to proceed straight on, otherwise I should have had to wait there until the next batch of reinforcements were leaving for the Engineers.<br/>24th   I left early this morning &amp; succeeded in catching an ammunition train which took me as far as Bazentin le Grand near Longueval, &amp; from there, had to walk to Waterloff Farm where the Company is "billited" arriving about 9 A.M. &amp; settled down in a fairly comfortable</p>
