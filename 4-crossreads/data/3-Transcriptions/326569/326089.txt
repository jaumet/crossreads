<p>Out on stunt over big hill all day.  Had feed blackberries.  After tea went to Tipperary Hut.</p>
<p>Thursday, 16/8/17<br/>Fine day.  On Q.M. fatigue all day so missed all night stunt.  Issued with new tunic &amp; w/proof sheet.  Went to Y.M.C.A. after tea.</p>
<p>Friday, 17/8/17<br/>Had easy day &ndash; men returned at dinner time.  Heard today leaving for France next week.  Recd. &pound;10 from Bank through Paymaster.</p>
<p>Saturday, 18/8/17<br/>Muster parade this morning &amp; detailed off for draft for France &ndash; 400 going from this btn.  After dinner had hot bath &amp; fixed up some of my clothes for France.  Sent &pound;12 to C/Wealth Bank per registered post &amp; posted</p>
