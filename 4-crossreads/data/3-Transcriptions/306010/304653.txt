<p>there was a big raid on the English coast. In this part of France no civilians are allowed to show lights in their houses after 9 p.m. As you know, we have a daylight saving bill in force &amp; although the days are getting shorter, it is yet hardly dark at 9. The winter is going to be pretty severe I guess. The last few days have been windy but today it has  rained like "billyho" &amp; half our camp has nearly been flooded out.<br/>Great satisfaction was evinced yesterday when it became known that Romania had declared war against Austria-Hungary. The French are immensely pleased.<br/>The work of getting in the crops seems to be progressing satisfactorily and</p>
