
Our halt for lunch was situated about one mile from a small village called El Kubat. We arrived at Latron which is situated at foot of Judean hill about 4 pm. Some 15 miles Jerusalem.Thursday March 14Anzac div left Latron 7.30 AM for Jerusalem. We started to climb Judean Hills Country is very mountainous & barren. Passed through Kuryet[?] El Enab or village of Grapes. French had possession of this place in pre war days. Beautiful stone church to be seen here.
