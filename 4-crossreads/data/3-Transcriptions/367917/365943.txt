<p>Pry.<br/>Belgium<br/>December 26th 1918</p>
<p>Mr. Justice Ferguson<br/>Supreme Court Chambers<br/>Sydney N.S.W.</p>
<p>Dear Sir,<br/>I yesterday (Christmas day) received your very valuable Xmas parcel containing cigarettes, tobacco, lollies &amp; etc, together with sprig of wattle, for which please accept my very best thanks &amp; I must say it is very kind &amp; thoughtful of you in thinking of us out here &ndash;</p>
<p>On Xmas eve I received notice to be in readiness to leave here on 28th inst I proceed to England thence on furlough to Australia, this notice together with Your parcel, I look upon as receiving two valuable gift, &amp; possibly</p>
