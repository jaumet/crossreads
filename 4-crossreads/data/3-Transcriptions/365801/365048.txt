<p>because if there is one man who deserves to get on in military circles it is he.<br/>In this lot of letters you were all talking about the 20th Bn fete.  How did it turn out?  Probably that question will be answered long before you get this.</p>
<p>There are all sorts of rumours travelling round at present, mostly good.  We know for certain about the Russian successes, &amp; about the fleet's victory, we also have most cheerful rumours about Verdun, which I hope are true.  It was jolly hard luck losing Kitchener, but I suppose we have plenty of men to take his place.</p>
<p>Remember me to all, &amp; give my love to Uncle &amp; Auntie<br/>Your affectionate son<br/>Arthur</p>
