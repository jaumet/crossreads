Saturday 25.8.17Arrived London 10.20 Am after a very comfortable trip down, passed through Newcastle & York en route. Took bus to Shaftesbury & again booked a room, then proceeded to Victoria Station to report to R.T.O. as we should have returned to France this morning, had no trouble with the aid of a few "bob" in fixing that up.
