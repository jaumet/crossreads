<p>Thursday 24 [23] May 1917 [1918]<br/>Town 3 p.m. &amp; Kheidivial [Khedivial] hotel &amp; met S. Roberts, drove through Noosah gardens &amp; lovely it all is, masses of flowers &amp; pretty drives.<br/>Tea at Groppi's 4 p.m., shopping then drive as aforesaid.<br/>Big fuss at 9 p.m. with poor little Sister Haggis &amp; Miss H.</p>
