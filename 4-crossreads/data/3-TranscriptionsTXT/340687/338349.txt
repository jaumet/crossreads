
February, 1917. Wednesday 14Cloudy and thawed a bit but froze hard again at night and pegging difficult. Out at 5 with inclusion of 8 reinforcements who badly got on our nerves and couldn't work. 2 [indecipherable] out coming home. Carried a bit and spent an unhappy night home at 1 Cold very bad. Stunt on night in front last to-night
