In accordance with the advance between Bray &amp; Cappy the 9th Batt, to whom I was attached on the morning of the 25th advanced their line to the sunken road running South of Cappy so that I had to advance my two rear guns along the canal bank  &amp;  get them into position to watch the Cappy road  &amp;  the spur towards Orchard Wood. He shelled heavily as we moved up &amp;  one of his machine guns (afterwards located in a tree) warmed us up as we were getting into position, which we nevertheless did without casualties.
