<p>May 29th<br/>It seems that our caravan has rested here long enough so have to move to Khan Yunis tomorrow morning at 7 a.m.<br/>I shall be very sorry to leave this little spot as it is in some ways ideal.  We have a good home with fig trees all around &amp; clean sand under foot so we get no dust.  The next place is sure to be dusty.</p>
<p>June 3rd<br/>We had no sooner reached the new camp than two fellows came to take us back to our units as the Division is now resting &amp; hence there is no use for the convoy.<br/>I have decided to try for a transfer to the C.T.C., it is the best job I have met with yet.  I don't</p>
