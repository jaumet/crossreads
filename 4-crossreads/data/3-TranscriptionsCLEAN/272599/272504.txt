  
 1917 Tuesday 28 August 
 Afternoon off. Take it easy read write & sleep a little   Terrific gale of wind & rain raged all day. Great excitement about tea time. A sailing vessel blown ashore on rocks below cliff. Thousands watch her being pounded to pieces. 4 of the crew rescued after great difficulty. Rockets & life line. Wind blowing hurricane force.  Chat at YM.C.A. Win to Miss P--
