<p>Saturday 16 March<br/>Moved off to Nielles for Boulogne but was advised that I was not to go to B. but was due for Blighty tomorrow.</p>
<p>Returned to Vaudringhem &amp; arranged things but on returning to Nielles was advised that I would not go until day after tomorrow.</p>
<p>Writing evening [Chanc?] Jack King in Boulogne.</p>
