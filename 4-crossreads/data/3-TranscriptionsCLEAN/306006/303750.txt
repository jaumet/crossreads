  
 25-2-17 have to wait till 3 oclock fo it. So I did not wait & went to Heliopolis at 1.30 had a general clean up & a shave & drove to Abbassia arriving there at 3 oclock put in till 4 yarning with some of the boys in Hospital & then took Hills for a garry drive around Heliopolis & Zeitoun arriving back at 5.30; I then drove back to Heliopolis for dinner & met Jessie at the Hospital at 8 oclock & drove into Cairo with her, arriving back at Abbassia at 10 P.M.
