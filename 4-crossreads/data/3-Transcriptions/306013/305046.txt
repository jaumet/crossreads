<p>Jan. 24. We had to dress up in our full equipment this morning in order to see how well dressed we could be for going ashore in England. The best-dressed man received &pound;1 prize. Sailing boat with three masts sailed fairly close to us to-day.</p>
<p>Jan. 25. Heavy roll &amp; cold wind. It Being too rough to drill we were given a lecture on trench warfare by our Officer. We all wished we were right there to have a hand in the game.</p>
<p>Jan 26. On guard again. Very cold, especially on night shifts. Alan &amp; I &amp; another lad found a warm steam-pipe in a corner of the deck &amp; there we spent the late hours of the night &amp; the early hours of next morning.</p>
<p>Jan. 27. We had a thorough clean-up today, and packed away all our belongings preparatory to disembarkation.</p>
