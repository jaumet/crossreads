
<p>was a pair of nearly new &amp; little worn Corsets&nbsp; Evidently the lady kept them for feast days &amp; holidays.&nbsp; Now I am no connoisuer of these aids to the female form divine.&nbsp; But this pair drew our eyes like a magnet.&nbsp; Solemnly we trooped up the rickety stairs &amp; surrounded the inoffending garment.&nbsp; Poor Jack Warboys (shortly afterwards killed in a counter attack at Morchise)</p>
