<p>Novembre 4<br/>Left "Martins Camp" at about 8 a.m. &amp; marched to wharf but did not get away on the first boat. Left about 4 p.m. &amp; reached England about 6 p.m. Didn't finish at Horseferry Rd till about 10 p.m.</p>
<p>Novembre 5<br/>Poking about London most of the day doing nothing in particular. Went down to Stratford where I am staying with Mr &amp; Mrs Smith or rather I am staying at Leytonstone near Stratford.</p>
