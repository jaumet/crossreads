<p>Enemy's Artillery was very quiet during the day and night.  Machine gun fire showed little activity.<br/>One enemy plane was noticed flying over white House about C.5.d.<br/>Weather dull and misty, poor visability.</p>
<p>16.2.18<br/>C.O. (Capt. D.A. Whitehead) visited line at midnight and found Sentries quite alert.<br/>At 12 noon a concentration was put down on Junction of Tracks and Tramline near Duriez Fme., C.5.d.35.85., two hundred rounds being fired.  This target was the centre of recent enemy activity.</p>
