
<p>MONDAY, OCTOBER 27, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; windy. Lopping in Middle</p>
<p>paddock in morning and put sheep</p>
<p>out of 4D into Boundary in afternoon.</p>
<p>Tickle lopping in morning and</p>
<p>getting wood &amp; water for himself</p>
<p>in afternoon</p>
<p>Tuesday Oct. 28.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
