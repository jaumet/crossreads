 Thursday May 24th 
 Havre : good day : this camp is in a very pretty place for scenery all round are beautiful green slopes : issued with rifle etc in morn &amp; also another V.D. &amp; scabie inspection : went to "bull ring" in afternoon for gas test " very pretty walk through a beautiful park : open air "fun" by the band &amp; the Padre after  tea : he is some Padre &amp; just the sort to get on with the boys 
 Friday May 25th 
 Havre : good day : reveille 5.30 am : fell in 7.30 am in full order &amp; marched to "bull ring" : interesting training but too much messing about before &amp; after going : the fools over us don't seem to realize that we have got heavy packs on all the time : concert in Y.M.C.A. after tea &amp; it was a splendid affair worthy to be on any stage at all:
