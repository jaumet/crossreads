 29  th  &nbsp; Off again at 1 pm.&nbsp; The enemy are in force some where ahead as our armoured car patrol has been machine gunned &amp; shelled.&nbsp; We jog along until dark &amp; we halt at the sound of gun fire ahead.&nbsp; All night along we dismount &amp; mount &amp; forge ahead a little every half hour or so.&nbsp; It is a night of misery &amp; we would feel better if we could keep going.&nbsp; The firing is intermittent all the night.&nbsp;  ^  30th &nbsp; At daybreak we get  a go  a move on &amp; pass captured guns &amp; dead Jacko&#39;s.&nbsp; Just near Sasa we come on to about 400 prisoners both Germans &amp; Turks.&nbsp; They have held us up all night,  but  they retreated in a hurry at daybreak but were soon overtaken.&nbsp; The road  was   is  strewn along here with discarded packs overcoats
