<p>Thursday 11/10/17. In billets near Steenvoorde, fixed up all shortage for 28<sup>th</sup>.</p>
<p>Friday 12/10/17. Collett returned &amp; took over, I went back to 26<sup>th</sup> Bn. Currie to 28<sup>th</sup>.</p>
<p>Saturday 13/10/17. Stirring up things&nbsp; saw Bridges at Div re. &quot;Dud&quot; Camp</p>
<p>Sunday 14/10/17. Finished letter to Doff&nbsp; saw Bas who has done well.</p>
<p>Monday 15/10/17&nbsp; Visited all billets&nbsp; went to STEENVOORDE to dinner with Lloyd&nbsp; applied for leave to England.</p>
