 23. Thursday Grey cold showery &amp; windy.  Stayed in house &amp; wrote some articles.  Went to bed a little after 10. 
 24. Friday Cold and grey.  Went to Miss N. at 10 &amp; left at 2.  Mrs. Nelson Smith left after tea. 
 25 Saturday Cold dull miserable profitless day.  Alone in house so slept in afternoon. Weak for want of nourishing food.
