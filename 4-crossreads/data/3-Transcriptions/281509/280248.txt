<p>25 January 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Duty as usual.  Tony is put on our reinforcements.  Great delight.  Exam. on lectures in afternoon.  Bob Tony &amp; self go to Macs to tea.  Tony lame foot. <br/>Go up to town first, see proof of photo. No good, get taken again. Good tea at Mac's. &amp; pleasant evening. Southerly buster.</p>
