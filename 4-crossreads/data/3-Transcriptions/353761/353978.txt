<p>(47)<br/>Is it wrong if you feel more sorry for the gentle Mary with her grief, than for the brave and patient Jesus with his everlasting peace?<br/>Maybe not, for the Mary is ever with us with her loss; while the mighty Jesus is afar off and as distant and as doubtful as the giants of our childhood.<br/>Come down from the little attic<br/>Descend the splintered stairs<br/>Pass through the tangled wreckage of the shop below.<br/>Leap across the break in its vaulted cellar, which gapes like a tomb<br/>And enter the cobbled street once more</p>
