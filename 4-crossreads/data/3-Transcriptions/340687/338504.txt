<p>July, 1917. Thursday 19<br/>Dull all day. Usual parades. 8.30. Marched to Froissy in charge of King and sat down till time to come home. Fell in at 2.30 and were dismissed. Fell in at 3 and blankets were fumigated at 1st company. Did some washing in evening.</p>
