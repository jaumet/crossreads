 Apr 21st &ndash; 18 
 Great excitement has held sway all day caused by numerous air duels between Fritz's planes and ours which have come out in great numbers to make the best of a fine day. 
 Shells also continue to pick out cottages &amp; the villiage church. 
 May 3th &ndash; 18 Today has brought me the unpleasant news of my brother Stan being wounded yesterday
