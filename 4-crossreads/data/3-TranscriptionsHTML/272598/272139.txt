<p>I have to take charge of section being only N.C.O. left.</p>
<p>Friday 15.12.16<br/>Cleaning up in preparation for moving away in a few days.  Met A. Gordon from Ryde.</p>
<p>Saturday 16.12.16<br/>Relief took over posts.  Went on sick parade with sore feet; very troublesome of late; put off duty, but carried on as usual.  Took charge of fatigue to A.D.S.</p>
<p>Sunday 17.12.16<br/>Again at A.D.S.  Number of the lads went on leave to "Blighty".  Still no news of relief.</p>
<p>Monday 18.12.16<br/>Feet very sore.  Very quite on front out from us.  Reports of good news from Verdun.</p>
<p>Tuesday 19.12.16<br/>Went on morning sick parade. Relief</p>
