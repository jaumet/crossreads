Paris on the 12th October
My dear Sir:
I have received your kind letter there is a few days, and I am glad to know you in good health.
At the beginning of October, I have gone in at school again.  I study chemistry, physics, natural history and principaly philosophy.  Ths last matter, at the beginning, is very difficult to understand and to read.
