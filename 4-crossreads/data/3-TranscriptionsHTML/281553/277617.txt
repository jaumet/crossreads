<p>Sunday 23rd Jan.<br/>Struck tents afternoon, had to sleep out all night that that transports may be loaded, were to leave 2-30 a.m. but postponed till 9-30 a.m.</p>
<p>Monday 24th<br/>entrained 9-30 a.m. steamed out nearer 10-30 in open trucks reaching Ismalia 11-30;  later marched through same along canal, Scenery just charming with its Canal &amp; tropical growth.  Had midday meal at flood gates opening into Suez Canal &amp; after another hour's tramp reached Punts where we all put hands  [See Page 30 for continuation of this entry.]</p>
