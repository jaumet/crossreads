<p>October 1915<br/>Friday 1<br/>Fine Heavy fog and dew last night. Tried to get into communication with the Sixth Bn. On the Heliograph, but were not successful.</p>
<p>Saturday 2<br/>Fine Saw the doctor this morning. Exempt from duty. Airship can be seen moving slowly in the air.</p>
