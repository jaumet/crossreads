<p>Tuesday 11 December 1917.</p>
<p>In a standing camp as it is termed. A few parades which bore everyone but most Corporals &amp; officers that always glory in same, after some weeks in the firing line.</p>
<p>On a fatigue that will last a day or so. All our sick camels have been taken to Beelah.</p>
<p>[&quot;Camel itch&quot; is being fought against now]</p>
