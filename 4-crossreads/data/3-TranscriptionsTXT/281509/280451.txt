 16 August 1916 Wednesday 
 Janet's birthday!!! Out in the battlefield. Return to Casualty Corner at 7 a.m. breakfast(?) short rations Seek "possie" in dugout &amp; rest all day. Dugout chats &amp; billet chats wage war on my back. Can't sleep for guns continually bombarding overhead. Incident of boy dying at Casualty Corner wound in chest.  Aeroplanes.  Fritzy has 8 balloons &amp; 3 taubes up. We have 20 odd &amp; aeroplanes by day. Incident of boy dying at Casualty Corner. Poor lad so quickly he went out. stayed in dugout all night. Slept fairly well.
