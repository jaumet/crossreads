 Australian Imperial Force 
 Base Records Office, Victoria Barracks 
 Melbourne 6th September 1919. 
 Dear Madam, 
 Forwarded herewith are two photographs and description of the locality of the grave of your bother, the late 2nd Lieutenant F.S. Phillips, 1st Battalion, which I trust come safely to hand. 
 Yours faithfully, J.M. Lean Major, Officer i/c Base Records. 
 Mrs. W. H. Read, C/- A. D. Stobo, Alliance Association, Pitt Street, Sydney, New South Wales.
