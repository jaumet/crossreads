  
 Tuesday 5/6/17 Quiet day, feeling tired - musketry. Wednesday 6/6/17 Field day towards Hamel very useful work done. Thursday 7/6/17 Visited by Genl Smythe & Wisdom - Won final of Rugby football against 25th. Friday 8/6/17 Musketry in morning. Close order drill in afternoon - lecture by Close at Senlis, wrote to Doff and General. Saturday 9/6/17 Went by car to Amiens with Lloyd and Close of the Field Artillery.
