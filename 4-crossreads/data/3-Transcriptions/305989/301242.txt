<p>Sunday 4 February 1917<br/>Paraded sick to get out of "Pilot parade" heard the Turko mine blown up.</p>
<p>Monday 5 February 1917<br/>Quiet day:  picquet at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 6 February 1917<br/>On field firing all day over at Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7 February 1917<br/>Read Ginger Mick:  had concert at Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Thursday 8 February 1917<br/>Gordon Bourkebek [Birkbeck] went on leave to Cairo.  Went to Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Friday 9 February 1917<br/>Got news of move, got things ready for "trek".  Bought a few things at the Canteen.</p>
<p>Saturday 10 February 1917<br/>Left El Arish:  trekking west on trough fatigue, stopped at 3rd Brigade.  Saw Sgt. Moore 303.  Camped the night at "Kilo 139".  Saw Billy Campbell.</p>
