 Notes For 1916 
 A Soldier's Prayer 
 Sing me to sleep where bullets fall Let me forget the war &amp; all Lousy my clothes are &amp; dirty my feet Nothing but bully &amp; biscuit to eat Sing me to sleep in some old trench My only cobbers ground lice &amp; stench Stretched out on my old waterproof Dodging the raindrops from the roof
