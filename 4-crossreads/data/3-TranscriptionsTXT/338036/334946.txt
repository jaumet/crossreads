
[9th Sept. contd]now in full swing.  Fritz planes very active behind our lines and St Omer & other towns is visited almost nightly.
Monday 10thWater carrying. At night - swim & run over championship course on the sports ground.
Tuesday 11thWater carrying again.  In 100 yds handicap I am on 7 yds mark, betting 8 - 1.  Met Ossy Wood just had a swim.
Wednesday 12thSports Day.  Had a very successful & enjoyable day in good weather.  In the morning the novelty events & heaps were run off leaving the more important events for the afternoon.  In 100 yds handicap I was on 7 yds & was beaten by Druitt by a hair.  He got a good lead in first 50 yds & in a sprint towards the 100 yds I almost caught him.  I started in other events - Wheelbarrow race, sack race Siamese race Stretcher race with carrying success.  In Tug of war our section were drawn against 'A' & was beaten after a good pull.  A eventually won the final.  The 100 yds handicap was won by Tommy Mahon 100yds champ.  200 yds champ & 1/4 mile by Harold Williams.  The mile was won by Sammy Watson with Tom Temperley [?]
