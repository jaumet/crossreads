
<p><u>2<sup>nd</sup></u>&nbsp; The noises of the Jungle at night are not conducive to sweet sleep.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Going aboard the &quot;wreck&#39; to shift cargo or some such stunt. Have to ge<u>t all </u>our baggage&nbsp; Leopards prowling round the barracks last night &amp; other things. There is a common pest here known as the Coli-snake, a Green &amp; brown devil 8&#39; long &amp; poisonous as all get out. Also the black mamba, a deadly tree snake and the old Boa. They tell that one was killed yest. over 40 feet long &amp; is lying on the hill behind the Bks. Am going to see it if I get a chance.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; On P L&nbsp;&nbsp; half full of water &amp; to be abonded Got apptd. hon. navel job watching no/one got caught in lenght of cable near companion way. Lasted all day &amp; no work (90 ft copper cable</p>
