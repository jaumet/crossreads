
4 March, Tuesday
F & WI go to Y.W.C.A. Dance & play an extra which is well received.
I dance Canadian Barn Dance & Brooklyn Schottische. I chat all about Plymouth to the Devon Lass & have supper with the N.Z. Sergts. Kilties (N.Z.'s) are in abundance.
Wrote Mum.
5 March, Wednesday
W.I go to pictures which are rotten. The two Dicks are there & I go to supper at Y.W.C.A.'s canteen apres. I am unable to get Boots or Breeches.
