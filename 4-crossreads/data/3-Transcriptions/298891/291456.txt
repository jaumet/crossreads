<p>Sunday 10 &ndash; got into Toulon where we had lunch &amp; left for Marseilles at 2.15 Dinner there &amp; left for Paris on slow train at 11. All seats gone on "Rapide".</p>
<p>Monday 11</p>
<p>Arrived in Paris at 6.30 taken by R.C.C. to Hotel St James &amp; went to bed. Dinner at [indecipherable]</p>
<p>Thurs 12</p>
<p>In Paris. Morning [indecipherable] 15 Passports &amp; [indecipherable] St Jacques. Then we went to a revue.</p>
<p>Wed. 13</p>
<p>Left at 8 a.m. for Havre. Arrived about 12.30 went to B.R.C. Hostel went to the Sg &amp; saw some one decorated &amp; a parade. Went on m.v. Mandie at 10 Channel smooth as [indecipherable]</p>
<p>Thurs.</p>
<p>Arrived at Waterloo at 12.00. Went to Minerva &amp; found Miss Hodge who took me in for night.</p>
