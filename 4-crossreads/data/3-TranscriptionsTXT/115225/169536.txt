 Mar 2  nd  &nbsp;&nbsp;I went out at 8.A.M. &amp; took a fatigue party of 45  th  &nbsp;Battalion up to Vierstradt. it was very cold &amp; windy &amp; snowed a bit in the afternoon - We slept well on our new beds last night. the night was cold. 
 &nbsp; 
  Memo . &nbsp;Weather mostly cold &amp; windy. with spells of bright sun, this week - Fritz raided our lines on Friday night whilst our 13  th  &nbsp;battalion was changing over but he came a thud. 
 &nbsp;
