<p>June 1918<br/>Saturday 22</p>
<p>On the Road. Bourdon.<br/>Weather wet and miserably cool. Cleaned harness in the<br/>morning generally swanking up for the road tomorrow.<br/>Walked to a neighboring village, where we spent a fairly<br/>Convivial evening in the company of Smithy and Gunn.<br/>Believe we pull out at [indecipherable] can't say with any<br/>certainty.<br/>Feel fairly happy.</p>
