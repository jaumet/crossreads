<p>The ship is a beautiful vessel of 47000 tons, and can steam 25 knots an hour.  There are hot and cold running baths, ballroom in fact everything, and it is quite easy for a newcomer on board to get lost for a time.  The ballroom is surrounded by a gallery supported by white pillars.  In peace time the boat carries 300 stokers and firemen, and it is said burns 1000 tons of coal a day.
