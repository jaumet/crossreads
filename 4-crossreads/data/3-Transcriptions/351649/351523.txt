<p>Sunday 28 Nov. 15</p>
<p>The Royal Automobile Club,<br/>Pall Mall,<br/>London, S.W.</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Robertson</p>
<p>I just got a letter from you and have been thinking about you all at Toganmain:  so although I said I was going to be a bad correspondant, I feel I must sit down and tell you what I'm doing, <strike>even</strike> besides your letter was a very nice one and it was sweet of you to write it.</p>
<p>Do you know I miss you all very much, its when you leave your friends that you find how much you like them:  so here goes to tell you all the news.</p>
<p>First of all the Osterley arrived with out any mishap.  We mounted a 4.7 gun at Port</p>
