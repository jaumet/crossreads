  
 Leave at noon. Calm sea. Lovely night. 4th Bde. Band plays 16/3/19 Arrive Port Said at 9am disembark at 12 noon. Leave by train 2pm and arrive Moascar 5pm  & move to Sig Training Unit. 17/3/19 NIL  Meet Leo & others. Wind of Gippos creating disturbance in Ismailia. 29-3-19 - To Kantara per motor for sets again - Khalass alashan 2/4/19 To Kantara per rail for sets. 3/4/19 Looking under tons of rubbish at Kantara for sets. 4/4/19  to Moascar - Mail in. 23/4/19 Kamseen all day - hellish hot and choking dust.
