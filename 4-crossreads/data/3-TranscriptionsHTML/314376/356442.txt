<p>The balance would go towards providing costumes and effects in the case of any concert or gathering arranged. It was our duty as mess orderly to clean out the stew dizzies and afterwards to fill same with water. At this camp we had to trudge along for about &frac34; of a mile before we reached the water supply. There was a big hill to surmount as well.</p>
