  
 May 30th Still at Flixecourt - bad news from French & British Fronts - Fall of Soissons - Australians upholding their great rep.,  Show as usual to a packed house. May 31st Continuation of fine "Home" weather -  Write poem "The Woodland" Long Rehearsal - Bob Bower takes a run to Amiens & returns with some necessaries - I make debut with my own monologue, entitled "The Opium Den" - Most Successful.
