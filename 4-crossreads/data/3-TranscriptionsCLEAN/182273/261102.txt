 February   1918 
 10 SUN  Fine day. 
 Football in aft. received 
 letter from Amy. Writing 
 at night. 
 11 MON  Posted letter to Amy & enc 
 for Myers L td . Fine day. 
 Touch of trench fever. 
 not feeling too good. 
 Bed early. 
 12 SHROVE TUES  Went into Ballieul 
 for stores. Rec. 2 letters & photos from  
 Gladys. card E.P. letter Maggie. 
 Had a merry evening at night. 
 13 ASH WED  Raining on & off. Horses 
 dipped. Rec. reg. letter from Amy & 5. 
 Football in aft. Fixing up  
 phone at night.
