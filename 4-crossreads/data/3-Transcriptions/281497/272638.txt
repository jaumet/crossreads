<p>In this battle I had more narrow escapes than all the others put together one bullet cut my puttee nearly in half &amp; just grazed the skin I was saved 3 times by my steel helmet &amp; had 2 holes in my equipment good enough wasn't it.</p>
<p>We moved from our billets this <strike>morning</strike> evening, &amp; had a stiff march of 11 miles, down a hard metal road didn't our feet ache, &amp; when we reached our destination we were dog tired.</p>
<p>29th. This morning we moved off again, it was very hot &amp; the sun fairly beamed down</p>
