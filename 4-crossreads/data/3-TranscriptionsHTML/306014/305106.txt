<p>March 9</p>
<p>Snow fell fairly thick last night &amp; it was also falling during our morning's parade &amp; as a result we had to resort to the "Dining Hall" &amp; there had a lecture on the service bomb known as the "Mills' Hand Grenade". We then did an hours Physical Training &amp; this was followed by exercises in which we used rifles as dumb-bells.</p>
