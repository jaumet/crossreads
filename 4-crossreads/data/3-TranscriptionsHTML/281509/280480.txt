<p>1916 September 14<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Duties per usual.  Cutting tent pegs. Very cold dull &amp; raining. Trys out  for sports meeting on Sat. 440 yds. Cock-fights [indecipherable] etc. rain interferes Bob &amp; self take stroll down road, pick up Byrne Johnston. Expeditionary Canteen. Flour mill. Stewart.</p>
