  
 in Tatts. You maybe lucky or otherwise The Turks are constantly trying to shell the watering area. To get to water you are compelled to cross open country Thus the excitement. The 1ST Reg are camped at Mersalarba[?]. 2ND Reg in between two hills to the left & 3RD reg with Headquarters behind the hills. Machine Gunners are down in the Wadi bed which is quite dry & not a bad possy. The sick & wounded unless urgent are brought in by night & one needs to be very careful as direction is easily misjudged.
