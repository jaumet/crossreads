 The bombardments by the warships on Archi Babi lasted at times for 24 hours without ceasing &amp; it is marvellous how any Turk could remain alive.  But Achi Babi was of solid rock &amp; was built up by masses of solid  concrete,  &amp; so the shells seemed to have little effect. They took the warning  &amp;  buried themselves in the specially constructed refuges far below the bowls of the earth.
