
22. SundayMiss Smith & I at home.  Many Indians.  Ethel & K. Adams. F. Post. Mr. & Mrs. Carpenter & others.  Wet dull day.
23. MondayWorked in office & went home to Miss Hodge at night.  Miss Wren had nice dinner waiting.
24. TuesdayWorked in office & went home to Miss Hodge.
25. WednesdayLovely frosty morning but grey later.  Dinner with Miss H & Miss W.  Supper alone & went to bed.
