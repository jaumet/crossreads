 Thursday 9th Dec I apportioned the items overordered by the different Units &amp; in the afternoon issued the Headquarters stores. There was a good deal of shelling to-day. 
 Friday 10th December I had to serve the 7th &amp; 9th Batteries to-day &amp; it kept me very busy indeed. There was a terrific Bombardment to-day by the Warships. It was good to hear the guns booming out once again. How they echoed &amp; re-echoed across the Peninsula when their broadsides went forth.
