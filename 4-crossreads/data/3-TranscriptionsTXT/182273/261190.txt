December   1918
15 SUN  Raining on & off all day
Went to Halsall's in aft
& Parker's at night. spent
a pleasant evening
16 MON  Fine day. Writing. Posted
letter to London A.I.F. & May.
Went to Rawlings at night
Spent a pleasant evening.
17 TUES  Raining. Called at
Grayson's in
aft. went home with Capt
Mooney. for musical evening
Rec. wire from London.
Dentist 10 am. Went to
18 WED  Stockton Heath. Met May.
Went to Pictures at night.
Posted letter & wire to St.
Budeaux Camp.
