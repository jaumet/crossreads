 Mar.9th Return from Blackheath to Town &ndash; I view the interior &amp; exterior of Houses of Parliament. Most interesting (see "Guide") &ndash; Luncheon &ndash; In the afternoon Mrs. Scales &amp; myself witness the finest show I have yet been fortunate enough to see in my life &ndash; It is "Dear Brutus" &ndash; written by J.M. Barrie &amp; played by Gerald du Maurier &amp; Co.- Mr. du Maurier becomes (in one play) my favourite Actor &ndash; God Bless Him! &ndash; Still stage struck &ndash; Evening spent Mr &amp; Mrs Wm Scales
