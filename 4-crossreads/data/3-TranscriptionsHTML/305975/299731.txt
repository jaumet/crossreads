<p>Thursday Nov. 7.11.18<br/>am in bed to day feel stiff in the hip  am feeling much better since the injection.   had letter from Josie and Bell.<br/>Friday Nov. 8.11.18<br/>got up to day and had to get in again  got another injection.   Raining here very heavy   Place all wet<br/>Saturday Nov. 9.11.18<br/>in bed in bed all day to day  still raining  Place is all water as well.</p>
