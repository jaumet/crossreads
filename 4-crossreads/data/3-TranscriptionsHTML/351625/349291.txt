<p>France<br/>9 June 18</p>
<p>Dear Mum</p>
<p>As I have time on my hands at present I will drop you a line.  You will be surprised to hear I am away from the Batt. at last but I don't think it will be for long, a week or so most likely.  I developed very sore eyes after coming out of the line last time &amp; as they seemed to be getting worse after a week or so I went &amp; saw the doctor &amp; was sent away immediately to the Casualty Clearing Station where I have anchored.  Here I have nothing to do &amp; plenty of</p>
