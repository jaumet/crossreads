 6 July 1918 Saturday Things as usual in the Depot. In the afternoon Sgt Connell &amp; I rode bikes into Colchester &amp; had a nice afternoon. A fete in aid of the Lord Roberts Memorial Fund was the chief attraction &amp; it was well patronized Arrived back in Depot about midnight 
 1918 July 7 Sunday 6 After Trinity 
 The usual quiet day in camp. The camp is open on Sunday afternoons &amp; a good many townspeople pay us a visit &amp; listen to the Band. Expect to go to Weymouth tomorrow.
