<p>Germ Concentr. Camp<br/>Holdsworthy</p>
<p>Esteemed Sir.</p>
<p>As I hawe been a resident of Australia for the past eighteen years, though at present interneed in the Commonwealth, I forward you a parcel containing my eight days allowance of Margarine?  I humbly aske of you, to hawe "this stuff analysed, to see if it is fitt for human consumption.  As I am still a resident of the Commonwealth, I think it only human &amp; right, that I still come also under the Law of "The pure food &amp; drug act.  Trusting you see your way clear to grant my request.</p>
<p>I remain Yours respektfully</p>
<p>F Bungardy 41 Comp10 M</p>
