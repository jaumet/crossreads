They seem so bright that one is inclined to forget that many of them will be months in hospital &amp; that others will never be able to fight again. What most disturbs the peace-of-mind of a great number of the wounded men is the fact that they are treated in French hospitals &amp; do not get their trip to "Blightey".</p>
<p>May 4</p>
<p>"Bull-Ring" once more! A lecture on trench discipline &amp; tactics, and a tour over the model trenches here filled in the morning's programme; Two dog biscuits &amp; a drop of tea filled <u>our own</u> programmes at lunch hour; and a spell in the trenches from 4 to 9 o'clock completed the day's programme.</p>
