She is equipped with quadruple expansion engines and can develop a speed of over 13 knots, though her normal speed is 11 knots.  There is electric light throughout, and she has a fumigating machine.  There is a small well deck forward, the remainder being flush.  The bridge and the officers quarters are a little forward of midships, and the engineers & Petty officers alongside the engine room.
