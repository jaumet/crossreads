  
 Sunday 11 February 1917 Left "139" early & "trekked" to Mazaar, stayed the night & left the 2nd Regt. there. 
 Monday 12 February 1917 Short days "trek" from Mazaar to Tilul & stayed the rest of day, heavy shower of rain, on picquet. 
 Tuesday 13 February 1917 Left Tilul early & proceeded to Bir-el-Abd.  Saw some pictures of Charlie Chaplain, bought some stores. 
 Wednesday 14 February 1917 Left Bir-el-Abd for Bayud arriving there about 2.30.  Relieved the Suffolk M.G. Section:  they went back to Bir-el-Abd. 
 Thursday 15 February 1917 Quiet day:  a bit windy.  Got good mail:  this wallet from dear old Jess : Saw Guth & Charlie Wright. (Mail  from ?) Mary, Mother 2, Theo, Elsie, Dulcie, Grace, Lucie, Miss P., Enid, Norma, Jess. 
 Friday 16 February 1917 Quiet day:  just getting settling down. 
 Saturday 17 February 1917 Very quiet day:  ordinary fatigues:  wrote Enid, Elsie.
