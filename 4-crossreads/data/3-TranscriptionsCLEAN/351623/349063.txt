26 Feb 19In Quarantine Camp. Conditions are improving, which the threat to march out has had a lot to do with I suppose. We have plenty to eat & also plenty of fruit from Red Cross also 2 pints of beer a day per man. There is a nice beach to swim, & plenty of fishing. It was good to go in for a swim after being in a cold climate so long.
27 Feb 19In Quarantine.
28 Feb 19In Quarantine. We all have our temperatures taken every morning & also our mouths sprayed.
1 Mar 19 ) In Quarantine.2 Mar 19 ) There has been some snakes killed here & one seen close to my tent. I feel a bit nervous some nights trying to go to sleep
