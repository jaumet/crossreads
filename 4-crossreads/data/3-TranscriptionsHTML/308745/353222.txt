<p>And the fierce thirst, and the strong pain.<br/>3<br/>Forget? God smiles to see them merry<br/>For his own son was once a boy<br/>They never shall be old and weary<br/>But of their youth shall have great joy<br/>But in the playing fields of heaven<br/>Shall run and leap new washed new shriven<br/>4<br/>Now heaven by golden boys invaded<br/>Scaped from the winters and the storm<br/>Stainless and simple as he made it<br/>God keeps the boy's heart out of harm<br/>The wise old saints look down and smile<br/>They are so young and without guile</p>
