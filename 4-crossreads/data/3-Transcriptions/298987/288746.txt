<p>change taking stuff &ndash; sand cement etc &ndash; up to the job. We put 2 or 3 trucks on behind a Ford motor fitted with railway wheels &amp; take it up fairly close to the job &amp; the shift going out to work push them the rest of the way. It is hard work especially the last few hundred yds where it is a branch line &amp; very badly laid</p>
<p>Sunday 16th<br/>Another day shift getting material</p>
<p>Sunday 17th<br/>All hands went out in evening &amp; finished the main part of the job by 10 PM. Had to stop work twice on account of Gas shells. As things were a bit lively were sent home in pairs. My mate &amp; I were about &frac12; way back when he started putting over a lot of mixed stuff. We were just nicely in front of it only ducked once But things livened up at the camp after we got there mostely gas some H E one 6 in burst about 6 yds from our door. Had just made some cocoa &amp; started feeding</p>
