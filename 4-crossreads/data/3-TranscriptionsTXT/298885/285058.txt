Thur. Apr. 6th -16Arrived at Hazebrouck at 8 P.M. and proceeded to Castre a distance of 4 miles and after losing our way several times we arrived at our destination at 4 A.M. where we are billeted in a large barn in the villiage.  Can hear big guns firing from here, a noise which gives you a funny feeling in the bread-basket.
