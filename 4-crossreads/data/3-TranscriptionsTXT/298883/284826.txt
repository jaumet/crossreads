 7.45 p.m.  The enemy heavily gas shelled this area giving our transport a bad time coming up with rations. 
 4th 4 AM.  Reports from Captain Roberts in charge of last nights operation states that the advance was carried out  by  successfully by "B" and "C" Companies under Capt. Cahill and Lieut. Thorburn in the direction of St. Denis Wood. 
 12 noon.  I was ordered to report to Staff Captain 15th Bde. at 2 p.m.  I took L/Cpl. Grinter with me again and went off.  We found the Bde. at lunch and was given a good meal by one of the menials there.
