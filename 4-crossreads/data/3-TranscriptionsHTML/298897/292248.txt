My first trip into Cairo from the hospital was a notable one being after an absence of 10 months &amp; the stink of the gippies  etc was most noticeable.  I soon became accustomed to it however.</p>
<p>On the 14th February I was discharged &amp; sent to the Montazah Convalescent Hospital in Alexandria.  Formerly it was the residence of the El(?) Khedive being several buildings standing in a few hundred acres on the shores of the Mediterranean 9 miles from Alexa.  The grounds are well laid out being planted with pine, eucalyptus, fruit trees etc a harbour formed by a breakwall, boat house etc, the whole being an ideal place for the purpose.   Food is plentiful &amp; good the catering being paid for at the rate of 2/9 per head.  On the 22nd February myself &amp; another obtained leave to go to Alexandria where I had a very good time sightseeing &amp; Garry rides, music Hall &amp; a good caf&eacute; &amp; the making of a few purchases, filling in the day.</p>
