  
 R.A.S. Grounds 26/1/16 Dear Capt Bilton, re Triangle of Error proposition this causes me much interest, & I would be glad if you would explain to me how you would find your true position from the following data :- You have correctly plotted on your map Points A, B & C. Scale 2" = 1 mile From A :- B is 45 &deg & 600 yds C is 69 &deg & 1150 yds You are somewhere to the South of these points &  their magnetic bearings are A 340 &deg B 10 &deg C 40 &deg  1. How would you find your correct position , & 2. What is the variation of your compass? K. Ferguson. Lieut.
