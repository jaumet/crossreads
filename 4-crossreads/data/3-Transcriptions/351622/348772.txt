<p>27 January 1916 Thur<br/>Semaphore-drill before breakfast finishing up with an extra big double across the sand. Major Moseley annoyed threatens more drill besides using awful language. Company drill before dinner. Scotty Thompson &amp; Murphy have big argument as usual at dinner. Fred Jeffes suffers with sweaty feet. All heads under blanket.</p>
<p>28 January 1916 Fri<br/>Day &ndash; picquet. Very warm walking up &amp; down the lines. Struck tents. Issued with new riding breeches being that Xlarge they were up to my armpits. Herbies squad salutes as the parade goes by.</p>
