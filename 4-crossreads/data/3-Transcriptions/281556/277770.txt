<p>13th. &ndash; Sunday. &ndash; Aug. &ndash;<br/>Church Parade 10.15am Writing afternoon. Church Service in school room after tea. very nice tea.</p>
<p>14th. Monday.<br/>Continued school, coming home to midday meals leaving billet 8.30am &amp; returning 4.30.pm.</p>
<p>15th Tuesday.<br/>As on 14th. little more advanced.</p>
<p>16th. Wednesday.<br/>Left with Cycles at 9am for La Vicogne to the old orchard again.</p>
