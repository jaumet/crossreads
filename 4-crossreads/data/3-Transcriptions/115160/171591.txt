<p>7</p>
<p>Auntie Maggie from which you will see she has had another turn in bed.</p>
<p>I gather from Dad&#39;s letter, that Nillie Ness is married &amp; has a family now too.</p>
<p>I see the tram runs down Campbell Street now. &nbsp;Do you miss the bus at all now that it has been discontinued?</p>
<p>The old fiddle has gone crook again with the same complaint. &nbsp;I will try and get to Charleroi and have it attended to. &nbsp;I have another case that I souveniered at Fresney-le-Grand &amp; I got rid&nbsp;of the old one. &nbsp;I think it has been the case that has done the damage.</p>
