<p>17<br/>Party pass on.  Not many yards from where the shell burst there is a gun emplacement, one of the gunners there spreads his fingers from his nose to the evil smoke and sneers "missed again Fritz"</p>
<p>But down there in the valley where a crowd of soldiers have gathered round the cookers for their breakfast a shell falls, "Good God" But the party have to turn off the track and make their way through a trench to the sap which leads to the front, thats where their work lies.</p>
