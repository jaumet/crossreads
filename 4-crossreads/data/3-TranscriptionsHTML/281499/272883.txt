They were on the move at the same time We reached our destination after wading through acres of mud &amp; rivers of water, we had to go into an old trench half full of water &amp; these are our quarters for the present.<br/>30th. Last night a company of the 5th Batt got lost &amp; ended up by stopping for the night in my queen of a trench, where we are camped is right opposite Flers, &amp; this morning Len &amp; a few more went over &amp; after rummaging round brought back some carrots potatoes turnips &amp; etc. they got them out of the gardens in her, for you know before the British offensive people lived in her but now she is smashed to pieces.<br/>This morning the Capt &amp; I went to the firing line &amp; on the way up we passed 3 big "Tanks" that had been put out of action by shells, I was very anxious to see one of these famous</p>
