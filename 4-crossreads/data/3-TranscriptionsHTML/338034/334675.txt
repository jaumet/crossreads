<p>Sept 30th :- Patrolled out to El Eras which is about 10 miles East of Bally Bunion. There is a very fine Hod here in which we had breakfast &amp; many dates, &amp; remained till mid-day. After lunch we patrolled to Hill 300 &amp; then home.<br/>Oct 1st : O'Connor, Hind, <strike>Martin</strike> &amp; myself went down to Ballah by the light railway &amp;  had a couple of hours very fine swimming in the Suez Canal.<br/>Oct 2nd :- In the afternoon went swimming again in the Canal at Ballah. One Troop from the 2nd Brigade arrived to relieve us.</p>
