 Sunday, 17/6/17 Hot day &ndash; morning went up valley &amp; picked wild flowers.  On way back called at farm house &amp; the 5 of us had 3 drinks milk each.  In afternoon scrubbed breeches.  After tea strolled down town. 
 Monday, 18/6/17 Rained heavily in morning &amp; heavy thunder storm.  Started with Lewis Guns.  Musketry all afternoon in drill shed.  Went down town after tea. 
 Tuesday, 19/6/17 Rained heavily.  Great cleaning out on a/c of removing but had no parades.  After tea went down town. 
 Wednesday, 20/6/17 Reveille 3.45 a.m., breakfast 4.30 &amp; moved off 5.30 a.m. en route
