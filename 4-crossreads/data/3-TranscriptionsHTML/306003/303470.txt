<p>sank well into the sand before exploding they made big holes &amp; knocked scores of men down from the shock but hit no one   about midday they were falling within a few feet of us &amp; my body was paralysed at times from the explosions;  The Turks were bringing up large bodies of reinforcements all day &amp; at 4 oclock we commenced to retire, &amp; it was a case of every man mount &amp; get away as fast as possible from the shell fire which was deadly</p>
<p>as soon as we got out of the danger zone we rallied together &amp; took up a position on a ridge &amp; covered the retreat of the last men to leave the front ridge, &amp; we were a hungry, thirsty lot when we arrived at Debabis at 8 oclock, we watered our horses, &amp; drank a good deal of salt water ourselves which made us worse than ever, &amp; at 10</p>
