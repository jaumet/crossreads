<p>19th Batt<br/>Popes Post<br/>6-12-15</p>
<p>O.C.Ordnance<br/>Can you possibly manage to supply me with the following for the trench Catapult.<br/>16 New Rubber Springs with Cords for Same<br/>1 Pocket for Catapult<br/>These items are urgently needed or the Catapult will be out of use.</p>
