<p>1 March, 1916<br/>Serapeum<br/>We cleaned about six inches of sand out of the tent<br/> &amp; got down to a more solid bottom, we will have to buy some mats for it. Sand &amp; dust gets in everything   This afternoon we rode out to the canal &amp; had a bonza swim.  its fine to ride out in a free &amp; easy way.</p>
