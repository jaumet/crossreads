<p>August, 1915<br/>Thursday 12<br/>A restless night, &amp; 3 more train loads arrived of what our men call "Navvies who can shoot a bit" -<br/>This being the definition of a Gallipoli soldier after the immense amount of Trenching &amp; Sapping done there by the Aust.<br/>Give the Turk his due they fight fair &amp; [indecipherable] compared to the Germans. When we charged they yelled ALLA ALLA &amp; some English [indecipherable]</p>
