<p>19th<br/>Seven of us officers were <strike>driven</strike> went into Derby to see the Rolls Royce works and were shown over the whole plant.  It was a very interesting day, also a little bit exciting.</p>
<p>21st<br/>Arose a little earlier and went into Coalville with Miss Smith and a Canadian flying corps officer who had crashed his machine whilst on a visit to Cole Orton Hall about 3 weeks ago and narrowly escaped losing his life.</p>
