 Sunday 2 Sept. 1917 
 Leave granted 3 weeks in England &ndash; I will cable from Cairo . "Convalescent 6 weeks leave England" it will take 3 travelling. I am so thankful &ndash; don't know boat till I report. I go ITALY &amp; FRANCE so if not torpedoed a splendid trip. I am certainly a very lucky man &amp; will Cable you my arrival in England. 
 It just happened our Brigade is doing nothing hence I got leave &amp; a lot may happen in 6 weeks. Love to you. I'm saddling up now!!!
