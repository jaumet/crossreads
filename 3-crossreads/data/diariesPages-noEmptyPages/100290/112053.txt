19 October 1916
Thursday
Heavy rain last night amp this morning. Parade dismissed at 9.30. on acc. of inclemency of weather. Smart route march in afternoon led by Major Wilson along Calais road. Meet Tony in afternoon take 3 cafes with him. Jock Heylin MacB. call for me we go 2 k.m. to Tournhiem. A quaint old fashioned village. Stone gate 600 yrs old. Water wheel electric light. So in cafe. Tea walk home ? pitch dark. Mirage of lake trees.
