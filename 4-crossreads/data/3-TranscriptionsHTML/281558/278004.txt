<p>Monday June 25th</p>
<p>Bapaume : cloudy : reveille at 4 am : parades 5 &ndash; 6.30 am, 8 &ndash; 11 am, &amp; 9 &ndash; 9.15 pm : we S.B.s went for wood :</p>
<p>Tuesday June 26th</p>
<p>Bapaume : still thundery : reveille 4 am usual parades : went &amp; looked over a cemetery &amp; seen some of our lads (450) Harry Bolt is buried there : got a "P" of Tim Lawler's &amp; Harry Bolt's :</p>
<p>Wednesday June 27th</p>
<p>Bapaume : thunder showers : revei 4 am : usual parades : after tea went into the town with Alf for a stroll round :</p>
<p>Thursday June 28th</p>
<p>Bapaume : thundery : reveille 4 am : usual parades : fell in at 4 pm in battle order &amp; marched off for our field "stunt" : after getting there &amp; settling down in a tent a very severe</p>
