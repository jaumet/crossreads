<p>[Messages and Signals - Form]</p>
<p>Recd at 11 p.m.</p>
<p>Date 25.4.15</p>
<p>From Minnewaska</p>
<p>By C.S.</p>
<p>To MASHOBRA</p>
<p>25/4/15</p>
<p>Five men killed six wounded in first boat load and one of the tars was killed. Our boys have taken two trenches and two field guns. The wounded were taken to Clan Macgillivary.</p>
<p>Place Minnewaska</p>
