<p>Monday 6 November 1916<br/>Taube over in morning;  droped four bombs on Bir-el-abd, killed 5 Tommies, wounded 7.  Fixed up horse lines etc.  Good place this for horse under palms.<br/>Fixed up shelters to sleep under.  No tents at all.</p>
<p>Tuesday 7 November 1916<br/>Took horses out to graze on the tufts of camel grass round here but they would not eat it much.<br/>Mail in in evening.  Got 12 letters.</p>
