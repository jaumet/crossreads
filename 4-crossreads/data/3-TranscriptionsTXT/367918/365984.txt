 10.4.19 
 My dear Judge, I am afraid that my letter writing has been very slack lately. Now that the war is over and the return home is getting nearer it is hard to realise that people at home still appreciate letters. 
 The school is running along smoothly. Some very good work is being done and I personally am having a very easy time. We have had most glorious weather.
