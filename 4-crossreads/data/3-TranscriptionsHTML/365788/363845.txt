<p>1st Field Amb<br/>Tek-el-Keber<br/>16 Jan 16<br/>After reading end, see <strike>page 5</strike><br/>My dear Irene,<br/>Just received two letters from you which was very nice.<br/>Thank you very much for letting people know my new address.<br/>I am sure any letters addressed to me Cp. Ghezirch will be delivered. Thank goodness I am not on the Richmond just now.<br/>None of your presents have come to hand yet, but they will no doubt soon.<br/>Went &amp; had a look at the Cemetery here today, Sunday.<br/>One saw there the names of many heroes.</p>
