<p>relieved by a Troop of "B" Squadron. We had rather a "cushy" job <strike>at</strike> &amp; were rather disappointed at having to rejoin the Regt. We had a couple of trips on escort to Bir-el-abd during the week &amp; on each occasion stayed the night there. That place, which six months ago was absolute desert is now transformed into quite a busy centre, traffic on the railway especially seems to be particularly heavy. O'Connor &amp; I one day inspected the water scheme there. The storage tanks &amp; pumping</p>
