 At 11 a.m. Roy & I leave the Rio Pardo and walk into Cape Town.  After a stroll along the main thoroughfare (Adderley St) and a spell in the Anzac Lounge (an institution which is under Y.U.C.A. supervision) we spend an hour of observation. 
 -  Cape Town and its inhabitants.  The Black & White Races and their divisions.  The colour distinction as applied to the Kaffins.
