 Pentaloupe a French penal settlement.  650 patients on board, 7 doctors &amp; 80 on the staff.  French battleship passed us.  It was very hot during the evening &amp; the ship was put about to try &amp; get a breeze. 
 Friday 25th June 
 Up to now sea has been dead calm but this morning a breeze has sprung up &amp; it is nice &amp; fresh.  At breakfast time 2 submarines &amp; a destroyer passed us. 
 Saturday 26th June
