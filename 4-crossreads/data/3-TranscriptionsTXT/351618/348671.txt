 and more than likely I will purchase a good deal of Oranges from him. I spent a very miserable day. The wind was simply awful &amp; chilled one to the bones. Luckily I had my Balaclava cap &amp; it greatly amused the Greeks. In my tent was Sergeant W.R. Smith 32 Avenue Rd Forrest gate Essex England and he and I chummed up. That evening we made ourselves a little bit more comfortable. I secured 2 other blankets under me, 4 over me &ndash; my mits on also my Balaclava cap &amp; my Canadian Jacket &amp; yet I was a little chilly. The wind found its way under the gaps of the tent &amp; in at the door but I must say I slept a little better. The clouds are very heavy &amp; the sea is roaring outside.
