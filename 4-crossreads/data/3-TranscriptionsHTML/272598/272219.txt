<p>(beef eaters) saw practically all of interest that was to be seen.  During afternoon paid a visit to Houses of Parliament, was unlucky after waiting not to hear Lloyd George speak.  Also went to Westminster Abbey &amp; saw practically the whole of it.  At night went to the Gaiety Theatre to "Theodore &amp; Co"  Wrote home before retiring.  Passed over the Tower Bridge going to the Tower in the morning</p>
<p>Friday 17.8.17<br/>Arose, had breakfast &amp; afterwards walked down Picadilly &amp; Oxford St; Paid a visit to Madam Tussauds wax works &amp; National Gallery.  Wended my way to Colleseum Theatre at night (Vaudeville) which is by far the best theatre in London from all accounts</p>
