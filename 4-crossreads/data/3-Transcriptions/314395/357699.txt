<p>Army &ndash; S No. 564667</p>
<p>Sugar Permit</p>
<p>This Permit authorizes sugar to be bought by or on behalf of (Name and Rank) 283 Sgt. Horan in one amount at a rate not exceeding 2 oz. per head per day for a period of 4 days ending 1 Apr. 1918.</p>
<p>I certify that no rations of sugar are being drawn by the abovementioned during the above period.</p>
<p>G. Light, 28 Mar. 1918<br/>Lieut. &amp; Adj.<br/>Reserve Brigade Australian Artillery</p>
<p>Signature of abovementioned or person purchasing on behalf of mess.</p>
