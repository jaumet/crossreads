<p>4 March, Tuesday</p>
<p>F &amp; W<br/>I go to Y.W.C.A. Dance &amp; play an extra which is well received.</p>
<p>I dance Canadian Barn Dance &amp; Brooklyn Schottische. I chat all about Plymouth to the Devon Lass &amp; have supper with the N.Z. Sergts. Kilties (N.Z.'s) are in abundance.</p>
<p>Wrote Mum.</p>
<p>5 March, Wednesday</p>
<p>W.<br/>I go to pictures which are rotten. The two Dicks are there &amp; I go to supper at Y.W.C.A.'s canteen apr&egrave;s. I am unable to get Boots or Breeches.</p>
