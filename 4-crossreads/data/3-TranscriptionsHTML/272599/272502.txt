<p>1917 Sunday 26 August</p>
<p>Usual work. Receive parcel of pr. socks yesterday from Joan &amp; Kathleen Doherty. Threatened rain at 5 30. still we went met -------- walk Column. Rain set in. Refreshments under <indecipherable/>. plumpudding  cream etc parcel from May. <br/>Heavy rain get soaked. Home early.</p>
