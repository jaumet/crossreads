<p>19th Batt<br/>Popes Post<br/>4-2-15</p>
<p>Capt Hale,<br/>Will you kindly let me know if the orders for bombs for this Batt will be procurable tonight.<br/>If 6s bombs are not available 4s Bombs will do. We are 110 Garlands short of number required by Brigade order.<br/>A Gray<br/>RSM</p>
