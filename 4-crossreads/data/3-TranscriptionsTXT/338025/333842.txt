 Saturday 10th November 1917 Caf&eacute; Hendrick Painting notice board for drying room. 7.30pm ordered to harness up by 8pm, take [Blank]ets and 24 hour rations. Kept waiting for over 1 hour at 2nd station. No idea where we are going to. Very wild night,12.30pm  arr caf&eacute; Hendrick (between Dickebusch and Ypres). Had good billet to sleep in. 
 Sunday 11th Piquet fed mules so did not get out of bed till near 9am. Done nothing all day except attend to mules. Kennedy, Smallwood and I had small room to ourselves.
