<p>1915<br/>Jan. 19 Tue.<br/>Still very busy on disembarkation work.<br/>Second batch left for camp during morning (20 men).<br/>Did Mess orderly.<br/>Russian Battleship observed at anchor in the harbour.<br/>After tea worked hard until 11 p.m. getting ready for an early departure next morning.<br/>Sunset is always very pretty.<br/>Mornings dark and a bit chilly.</p>
