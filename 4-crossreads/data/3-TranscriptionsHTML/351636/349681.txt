<p>The Centre<br/>4-1-16<br/>4-1-16</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton &ndash;<br/>Mrs. Main the bearer is seeking assistance for the payment of debts. She owes &pound;7 rent Grocer &pound;2 Milk 10/- She can manage on her pay if these are paid.</p>
<p>Perhaps you can help She will explain her case to you.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>B. Booth.</p>
