<p>February, 1915<br/>Friday 19<br/>Brigade training for outpost work towards the Canal. All sorts of Rumours float round that we go to Marseilles in a month but nothing is known definitely the youngest sub usually having the latest news.<br/>Posted an Egyptian Paper home today. Censor less strict here.<br/>Sore eyes, measles &amp; cold seem to be the chief troubles among the men.</p>
