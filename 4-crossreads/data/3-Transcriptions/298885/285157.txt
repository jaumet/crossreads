<p>July 26th -17<br/>Moved up to our position at Dickebusch for duty.  Here it is certainly more lively even when it is quiet, as at night our first course we are served with bombs, then follows a few heavies, these being backed up by gas shells.</p>
<p>Here we are dossing in the top floor of a house</p>
