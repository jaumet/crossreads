<p>May 22/17 Tuesday<br/>Detailed to take over Adeney's team with the C.R.E while he was on leave. I pulled out on Sunday carrying some of their Baggage &amp; also the packs &amp; blankets of the Infantry Fatigue Party &ndash; travelling with the 1st Field Coy Engrs. We camped that night at Contalmaison &amp; arrived here at Lavieville <strike>las</strike> yesterday midday.</p>
<p>The last years Somme Battlefield is well cleared up by the Salvage Parties stacks of shells &amp; empty shell cases, old clothing &amp; equipment being gathered into Stacks at the road side ready to be carted away.</p>
