Am still at the Battery, but have brought my senior subaltern up, and intend to go back to the wagon line tonight myself, as I am only in the way here &ndash; Now, am I not a Jonah? Of course it doesn't make an awful difference to me as after 2 years one gets sort of callous, and a week or two is neither here nor there, but poor old Sis will be disappointed as I wrote and told them I was coming.
