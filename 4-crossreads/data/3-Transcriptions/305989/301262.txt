<p>Sunday 24 June 1917<br/>On Sanitary fatigue &amp; Mess orderly.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic number for 7 and 6].</p>
<p>Monday 25 June 1917<br/>Billy came back from Cairo.  On picquet at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 26 June 1917<br/>Quiet day, cleaned up bits.  Mock trial by jury at Y.M.C.A. at night.</p>
<p>Wednesday 27 June 1917<br/>Mainly swimming all day &amp; good concert at night.</p>
<p>Thursday 28 June 1917<br/>Sports in afternoon &amp; concert at night quite a good turn out.  On day Stableman all day.</p>
<p>Friday 29 June 1917<br/>Left the Beach at 6.30 &amp; proceeded to Abasan El Kebir : Leminberg came, had to ride Ahmed the mule &amp; lead him ; saw a great air duel in Mess 6.</p>
<p>Saturday 30 June 1917<br/>Quiet warm day, on picquet at night.  Mail in.</p>
