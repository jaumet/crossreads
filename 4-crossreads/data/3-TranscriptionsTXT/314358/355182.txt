 9 Later, a batch of German red-cross prisoners, in their green-grey uniforms and red handed caps. 
 Did those lads notice as I did? How distinctive is the German type? The hard bullet shaped head, with the rear part of the crown flattened on an angle. 
 The cruel unkindly eyes, lacking the shadow depth of fancy and gleam of imagination. 
 Did those lads think how many are the points in the modern German, which are characteristic of soul-less dumb driven cattle?
