He is being returned to Australia shortly and we are wondering whose boat he is to go on. The 25th Aust Division has never forgiven him for the frightful stunt we did on the19th July 1916 our first stunt in France when we had 8000 casualties in as many hours with over 50% of killed. 480 men were taken prisoners mostly wounded who were unable to help themselves and had noone left to render them any assistance.
