
Go into Rest Station.
Jan 31Entrained, off 2 pm.  The day clear & fine.  Hitchock, Turner Stableberg and I share a 4th or 5th class carriage.
Feb 1st.Still travelling in this confounded dog-box - bitterly cold.  Able to get a few extras at various canteens en route which adds to the usual ration.
Feb 2Arrived St. Germain 8 a.m.  Go into camp, Lyons not far but out of bounds, anyhow too tired to try any tricks.
