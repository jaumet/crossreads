
Friday Aug 31stUsual lazy day, on duty, swimming & reading.Saturday Aug 31 Sept 1Bombardment going on all day & night. Monitors & Warships must be shelling hell out of GazaSunday Sept 2Flies very bad today. Hot day usual routine.Monday Sept 3Had the wind put up today. We were lazing in bivvy half asleep when bomb dropped too close to be pleasant. One wishes at these times that he could fade away until excitement over.Tuesday Sept 4Wrote mail sent home snaps.
