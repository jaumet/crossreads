<p>Have been able to Post letters for the first time since we have been on the move (Near Corbie)</p>
<p>Thursday 15th<br/>Squared a shell hole out about 6" deep &amp; made a cover with our Waterproof Caps last evening Got some dry straw &amp; had a good bed for the night. As usual bombs were dropped  Some machine bullets came fairly close to us  An observasion Baloon was towed up the road this morning about 100 ft up. There were 5 motors &amp; about 60 men in the turn out. The day was hot &amp; as we had nothing to do spent most of the time lying in our humpie</p>
