 Friday 25th August 
 Showery weather. We  are  commenced our (AI) assistant instructor exam. 
 Sunday 27th August 
 We finished our reading and  seld  sending test and heliograph setting yesterday. 
 Sat. 26TH. We had lamp reading last night. I am confident of passing. 
 28th August
