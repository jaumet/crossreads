 Egypt 21-4-16 
 My dear Judge 
 Today is Good Friday.  We had a Church Service this morning &amp; have the rest of the day off duty.  I intend going into Ismailia to dinner tonight (C.O. willing).  There is a splendid little Club there and all officers are hon. members.  They serve an excellent meal and it is very nice to get away from Mess tucker occasionally.
