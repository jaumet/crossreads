 May, 1915 Thursday 6 A thunderbolt fell today when I heard that Col. Meredith remains here o/c Camp &amp; I am detailed to remain with him &ndash; The Reg. leave their horses &amp; go as Infantry &ndash; Both the c/o &amp; I feel it very much &amp; he is selfish in keeping me, when he was going I was one of his Officers that would go at any price, now that he stops he keeps me as his aide - Letters from Laura   (23/4/15 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(10/5/15   "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   12/5/15 Also from Lorna &amp; Ralph I shall probably see him now, my job is to come on with the Brigade Horses &amp; 600 men.
