
1919May 25 [24]fell heavily & woke me up.  The burgler entered & turned out to be the Janitor.  He said that a lady had called to see me about an attache case that she had lost.  She had last seen it at Bobbies where I was carrying it.
May 25I called at Mrs McKenzies lodgings to see her but she was out.  Went to the Aldwych, but found that she had gone to St Albans, in pursuit of the case.  I went to the Temple to church.  The church was full, so took a seat in the old circular part near the entrance.  Just before the sermon, a crowd got up & went out.  The preacher, a very old man, gave out his text, & another crowd got up & walked out.  He had a thin piping voice.  I couldn't hear a word he said, as every few minutes someone else would get up & stump out heavily over the tiled floor.
