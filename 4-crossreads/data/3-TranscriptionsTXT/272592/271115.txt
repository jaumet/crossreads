 It is good job for me for I do nothing only make arrangements for the mens food &amp; etc. &amp; detail the parties off as wanted. 
 It was freezing cold last night but this morning is a beauty nice &amp; sunny. 
 Through some mistake of the Q.M. we were not taken on to-days rations strength &amp; so that meant that we had no tucker to go on with but I done a bit of scouting &amp; got bread, tea, sugar, salt, jam &amp; a box of "pork &amp; beans &amp; the cook bandicooted some potatoes from a neighbouring field so we did very well.
