June.
1st. After a run of varied weather across Indian Ocean, we reached Fremantle this morning experienced (some on board) the welcome home.
After disembarking W.A. troops, we had leave I went up to Perth.
Fine Sunday afternoon.
6th. Arrive off Outer Harbour, Adelaide at 6 a.m. disembarked troops at 1 p.m. left at same hour. Glorious day.
