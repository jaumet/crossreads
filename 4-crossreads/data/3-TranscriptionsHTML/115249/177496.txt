<p>W.T.Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship - Berrima</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship - Warrego</p>
<p>Date - 12 Sep</p>
<p>Time of Despatch or Receipt - 8.4 pm</p>
<p>To Brigadier from Commander D</p>
<p>Following received from Flag</p>
<p>When all your men on board and you can be of no further use to Brigadier return into harbour with Yarra. &nbsp;Please say if you wish me to remain.</p>
