 June, 1915 Wednesday 2 Saw the IsherwoodS off to England today &ndash; Father daughter &amp; daughter in law whose Husband is in the Lancashire  B  F. Artillery &ndash; I have known these people &amp; had many pleasant nights together &ndash; I am writing separately to tell you in confidence more about the above which will be better to keep in the family for the present, but I hope to meet them again some day.
