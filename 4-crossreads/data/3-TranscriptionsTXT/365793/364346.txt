 Spt 1 box of cigars for 10/-, &amp; they are a tip top smoke. Patiently waiting for pay, expect to get 1 quidlet. Still plenty of birds keeping us Company, some of 'em only little uns too. Boat parade at 2 pm &amp; of course I fell in on the wrong boat. Recd the large sum of ten bob.
