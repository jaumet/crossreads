<p>Le Bizet<br/>Between 9 p.m. and 3 a.m. 1500 rounds were fired on the following targets, viz.,<br/>(1)  Duriez Farm, C.5.d.30.95<br/>(2)  Screened Farm, C.5.b.6.4<br/>(3)  Tramline, C.5.b.70.25<br/>(4)  Road, C.5.d.40.85<br/>(5)  Junction of Road &amp; Tramline, C.5.b.95.15<br/>Enemy activity normal.  Weather clear during morning, poor visability in the afternoon.</p>
<p>3.3.18<br/>1500 rounds were fired on the following targets, viz.,<br/>(1)  Duriez Farm, C.5.d.30.93<br/>(2)  Road in C.5.d. &amp; C.5.b. between Duriez Farm and Screened Farm<br/>(3)  Cemetery Drive, C.11.c.90.80<br/>(4)  Celtic Avenue, C.11.c.40.07<br/>(5)  Junction of Tracks &amp; Trenches, C.11.b.85.13<br/>Time occupied 4 hours.<br/>Very little Artillery and Machine gun activity on our front throughout the period.  Weather dull with wind dropping to dead calm at midnight.  Visability fair.</p>
