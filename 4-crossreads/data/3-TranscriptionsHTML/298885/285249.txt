<p>Aug 28th &ndash; 18<br/>Off again this morning for another promised visit and now am at Cardiff and made to feel at Home right away.</p>
<p>Sept 2nd &ndash; 18<br/>Back again in London after a tip top time in Wales. Met Wal who informed me Geoff was in London and had made an appointment for me, so met Geoff at midday and had a real good fly around.</p>
