<p>At 2.30 P.M. we filed off the ship &amp; marched in column of route to the Town Hall, a distance of 2&frac12; miles &amp; were there dismissed. Alan &amp; I wandered through the city ,bought our Post-cards &amp; addressed them from the Soldiers Institute; we then had tea in one of the finest restaurants I have ever been in or seen. Strawberries &amp; cream, toast &amp; coffee, eggs &amp; bacon &amp; more coffee &amp; meat rolls were a few of the articles which did the disappearing trick.<br/>We visited the Y.M.C.A. which was thrown open to soldiers, we had a round trip in the tram, which is free to soldiers, visited Wesley Hall, where free feeds &amp; free concerts never end &amp; from which the badge I am sending out, came .Later we had more tram &amp; then marched</p>
