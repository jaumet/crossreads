<p>14 August 1918 Wednesday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Usual training, Posted notes yesterday-   Just received word we move on Friday so once more a Mobile force.<br/>X Had letters from Mrs Cotton &amp; Ferrer-   Nowell is lending me "after many days" to read.  He is now with an English Regt-   Australian Flying Corps entertained us with a Travelling X Pierrot Show, very good performance.<br/>X Parcel arrived safely thank you for all good things.  Oleac has cured my Skin but it recurs after some months-   I have plenty for a long time yet.X</p>
