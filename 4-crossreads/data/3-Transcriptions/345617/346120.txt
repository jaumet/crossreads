<p>Juillet 17<br/>No 2 Duty Section. Other Sections on Drill &amp; Route March. Showery weather. Nothing of note on our Front. Russians still doing well &amp; French more than holding their own.<br/>Had swim in lake off Somme Canal this afternoon.<br/>8th Brigade camped in Corbie for the night, &amp; a good many [indecipherable] among them making a general nuisance of themselves.</p>
<p>Juillet 18<br/>14th Brigade marched into Corbie today &amp; will camp here tonight. A good many zig zagging about the streets. Nothing fresh to report. We are carrying on with general training.</p>
