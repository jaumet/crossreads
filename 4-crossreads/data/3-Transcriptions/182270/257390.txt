
<p>WEDNESDAY, MARCH 19, 1919.</p>
<p>19 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;1029</p>
<p>Fine &amp; calm. An ideal day in fact.</p>
<p>Playing games on deck all day.</p>
<p>We sighted land about midday</p>
<p>and steamed along the coast</p>
<p>&amp; got into Table Bay and dropped</p>
<p>anchor there about 8 pm.</p>
<p>Days run 310 miles</p>
