<p>March, 1915<br/>Saturday 27<br/>IMPORTANT<br/>I wish you would look up from S.A, Diary, dates, as follows &ndash;<br/>When I left Africa in Hospital ship for England date I left Folkstone &amp; got my discharge. Date I left Capetown in <u>Sherie</u> with 1st Aust.Com Horse, on my return home the second time.</p>
