 20th. Sat May After breakfast visited Manchester city, had run round &amp; took several &quot;snaps&quot; returning to Deane with Mr Andersoon. After dinner went with same to Links where we met 2 other friends of his &amp; played Golf. till about 7pm. when we had nice light tea &amp; continued play after till dark. Returning home had bath &amp; dressed for wives of golf mates who did not come when expected. Had dinner about 11pm &amp; to bed about 1am.
