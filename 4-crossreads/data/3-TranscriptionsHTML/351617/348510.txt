<p>Next morning we heard "No Leave boat Sailing 12 noon" so we just settled down to another 3 weeks of agony. At 1 pm the word was received that the sailing was postponed till next morning and at 3 pm the troops "Broke Ship" and off ashore we went once more. We had a good day and at 7.30 am on 21st June we left Cape town astern and off we went on the final stage of our sea journey.</p>
