We hear rumours of some of our people having got a knockback near Perrone . The 3rd Div. are said to have sent back 200 prisoners this morning. Called suddenly to Bde H.Q. and there saw the General who said that Mont St Quentin is in our hands and we are working Peronne. Our 5th Division unable to cross in front here is coming round and crossing it behind our line then working round across us South.
