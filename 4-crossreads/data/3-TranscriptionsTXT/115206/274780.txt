 July 1918 Monday 29 
 Ribemont. Bty Position At 12.30 am the stunt opened and the barrage went over for 20 minutes then lulled for ten. After 10 minutes it opened again and continued until 20 minutes had passed again.  At 4.30 am another heavy barrage was laid down for 10 min. During the stunt Bdge went twice and guns 5 times. Ran a new piece of line out with Smithy to the O.P. line. Fritz shelling fairly consistently all day. scored a direct hit on the Canal Bank and nearly did some seroous damage.
