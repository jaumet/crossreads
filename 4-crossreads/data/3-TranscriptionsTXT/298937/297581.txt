 Wed. 24th May 
 Foot healthy &amp; nearly healed now. Yesterday afternoon Dr. Cockinos paid the second visit &amp; Mrs. Nyland also visited me. She is very kind to me &amp; it was good of her. I can't really understand her.  I never feel perfectly at ease when talking to her &amp; always have an irresistible tendency to smile, &amp; she seems to half smile now &amp; again.
