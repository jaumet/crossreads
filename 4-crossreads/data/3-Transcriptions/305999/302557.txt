<p>I arrived at Albert at 8 PM.  I was supposed to catch the train the next morning but I was asleep and missed it and had to wait till the next morning</p>
<p>8/12/16<br/>I entrained at Albert for leharve we where in the train 25 hours. very cold</p>
<p>9/12/16<br/>We disembarked at leharve and slept in stables on concreat floors</p>
