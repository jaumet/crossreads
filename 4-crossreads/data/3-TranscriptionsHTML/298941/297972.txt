<p>the imagination conjures up of the orphans &amp; nuns at the convent, &amp; the civilians of Locre &amp; Dranoutre, under shell fire, &amp; in the confusion of evacuation is not pleasant.<br/>Then there is Caestre, which now comes very close to the line: that will be shelled no 1. It is to be hoped that all the civilians were able to get away in time but one fears it was too sudden. I am wondering what has become of  Mdme Van Uxems<br/>Our last resting village</p>
