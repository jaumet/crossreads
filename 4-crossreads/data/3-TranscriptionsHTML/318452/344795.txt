<p>The trees have all lost their leaves &amp; it does not look its best. I have a very nice room &amp; the owner is very hospitable<br/>It is a great change after the noise of war no sound of it ever reaches us. The position is South of Boulogne &amp; between it &amp; Etaples about 43 miles from Etaples off the railway &amp; about 6 miles from Samer.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20th<br/>Went round the grounds for a walk this morning &amp; then wrote letters</p>
<p>Wed 21st<br/>Have not been out. British made a push near St Quentin  Successful</p>
