<p>11.1.16<br/>Nothing of any importance  never went out.  great Aeroplane activity.</p>
<p>12.1.16<br/>Sent Parcel of Cushion Covers to you all.<br/>Mrs Major Charlton arrived in Heliopolis having come over on the Mooltan.<br/>Heard that there were tons of letters stacked down at Suez &amp; that we will have same in a weeks time.<br/>No 1 Section played the rest of the Company football this afternoon &amp; ended in a win for the rest by 9 to nil.</p>
<p>13.1.16<br/>A Dull day &amp; Sand storm blowing.</p>
