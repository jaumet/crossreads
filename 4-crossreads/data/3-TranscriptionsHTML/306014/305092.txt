<p>In the afternoon, Fred Heap, Alan's friend, took us to the Shrewton Manor House for the evening. We arrived there in time for tea &amp; after doing it justice spent the evening in front of a fine "Olde Englishe" fire, with soft sofas cushions &amp; music "to taste". Mrs Pratt is the hostess &amp; seems to be only too glad for any of the lads to come to her home. She is a grand old lady, with snow-white hair and a bright, beaming smile &amp; is a mother to every wanderer who comes her way.</p>
