<p>Oct. 26th &ndash; Thursday<br/>Raining most of the day.  No parades as we (AM.C.) are duty Company for the camp.  Plenty of fatigues &amp; I have charge of several parties.  Usual duties on lines.  Draft for the front miss the train.  Expenses 5f.  News of French great advance from Verdun to Douamont [also spelt Douaumont].  Roumanians still retreating.  Sgts' mess meeting.</p>
