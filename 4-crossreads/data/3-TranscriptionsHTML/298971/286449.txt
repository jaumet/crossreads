<p>1st November (Wednesday)</p>
<p>Reported at Thistle Dump 9.30 am for fatigue or other work and we had to erect tents.  The earth is covered with mud feet deep in places and it was raining most of the day.  Cold and dull day.  2 German planes brought down.  There is excitement in the air almost every day.  I counted 30 of our aeroplanes up today and 17 observation balloons.
