 1916 August 3 Thursday 
 Heavy bombardment recommenced last night. We have been warned that we may shift off today. So far nothing eventuated. Wrote more letters &amp; read. "Knight on Wheels". Drifting parachute 
 Order to stand by as we will probably get orders to move off. No order came through. Go up to see Bob before going away. Walk into Contag. Eggs &amp; coffee. See freshwater spring. nettles
