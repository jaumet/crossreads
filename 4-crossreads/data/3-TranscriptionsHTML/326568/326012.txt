<p>April<br/>14th.  Move to-day en route to A.M.C. Depot at Fovant via Salisbury, but on arriving find that camp is full of 1916 men &amp; we had to go on to Hurdcott.<br/>15th.  Demob. of A.M.C. men is not satisfactory since we 1915 men are not being fairly treated here, not being on any precedence roll.  Existence is awfully monotonous here &amp; weather unpleasant.<br/>17th.  Walk to Wilton this evening after tea, which is a town</p>
