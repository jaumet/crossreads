
<p>MONDAY, AUGUST 11, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, but cloudy and hot. I lopped</p>
<p>in 4D &amp; then went down &amp; was</p>
<p>working at Boundary troughs</p>
<p>for rest of day putting up a</p>
<p>batter to them.</p>
<p>Tickle put a couple of posts in</p>
<p>Cow paddock fence &amp; then he helped</p>
<p>me.</p>
<p>Al &amp; Phil Charley went down</p>
<p>to Sydney by the 9.30 train</p>
