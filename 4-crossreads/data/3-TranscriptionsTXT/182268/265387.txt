
Wednesday 6/2/18. Good clean up at the Hotel, cleaned boots, belt etc. went to [Dear Brutus?]
Thursday 7/2/18  Took Mrs Bamford to The Yellow Ticket  very good play
Friday 8/2/18  Went with Bamford to Shell factory  spent whole day
Saturday 9/2/18  Stayed at Bamfords all day & real rest
Sunday 10/2/18  Had another good easy day- read a book at Bamfords
