17th Sept TuesdayA cool breeze blowing all day. Like rain again. Every one wants blankets etc. Wrote letters to-day.
Saturday 18th SeptQuiet
Sunday 19thWent to Ordnance again. My gang fitted themselves out - ! - a good deal of artillery firing to-day. last night our infantry attacked the Turks & captured several trenches near lone Pine
Monday 20th SeptA Blowy day & keen
Tuesday 21st SeptThe same. We had a lot of shelling to-day.
Wednesday 22ndVery keen again. Winds just like our Westerly. Busy on fresh indents etc. Ordnance & Ground
