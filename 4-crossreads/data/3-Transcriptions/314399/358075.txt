<p>Wednesday 19 March 1919<br/>Rained all day &amp; night. Went to dancing class at the gym &amp; learnt the foxtrot &amp; Missouri waltz. Went to Mountvilliers. Went to dentist again. Becoming quite a permanent thing now.</p>
<p>Thursday 20 March 1919<br/>Nice &amp; sunny all day. Have train picquet tonight. Wrote 5 letters. Bill Cowper left for Huppiy. Can't speak at all today. lost my voice.</p>
