<p>1917 Thursday 11 October</p>
<p>Afternoon off.   Jock Mac B Wilson &amp; self into B &ndash; walk   Jock late as usual.  Call at D.D.M.S. office &amp; find I am next for leave probably Sat or Sunday.  Buy Xmas cards.<br/>Payne, the gassed case evacuated in Blighty &ndash; a good save.</p>
