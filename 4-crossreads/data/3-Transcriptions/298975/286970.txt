<p>Picquet Start at 8.45 PM &amp; walk the Warminster Road untill about 11 To bring the drunks home</p>
<p>Monday 29th<br/>Still toiling on the Clink</p>
<p>Tuesday 30th<br/>Finished Clink</p>
<p>Wednesday 31st<br/>The Clink was not just a success as the Birds pulled some of it down last night We are working on an [indecipherable] Coupse in the same Camp</p>
<p>Thursday  1st Nov<br/>On working party. Quite a hot day</p>
