
Ice [11/2/17 Sun] Recd. letter from M.R.L. Played for Kirk & for service in Prison Ward. Wrote to M.R.L.
Thaw [12/2/17 Mon] Recd. Comforts from Sydney. Wrote Jennie & Aunt Annie Overflow of Patients
F [13/2/17 Tues] Nothing doing Bed at 8p.m.
F [14/2/17 Wed] Played for Lantern ServiceNothing Doing Went to see "Some Girls"
F [15/2/17 Thurs] Opening of Billiard Table. Everything very nice
F [16/2/17 Fri] Recd. letter from Jennie. Met Reg. Keirle from Perham on Escort Duty. Billy Ali was over too. Dreamt about 2 of M [code for Marge Miller perhaps - see 23/1/17] & Shipwrecked again. Rained at 5pm.
F [17/2/17] Misty & I drill defaulters Walk to Amesbury alone & dine. Meet Reub King & Bricky & dine again at Amesbury. Walk Home & tell about Factory life I dreamed all about it tonight. Go to Pictures in Amesbury.
