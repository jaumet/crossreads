 Thursday 8 March 1917 [7 March 1918] &frac12; day &amp; went town, met Sisters Henry &amp; O'B., tea Liptons &amp; we air our little woes. At 9 a.m. went to Cairo cemetery with S. Witherington &amp; took a photo of "F. Green" grave, also Norman Mowbray.  Capts. Grant &amp; Collins came in with us to Cairo. Took snap of our lettuce being washed in the old Nile on the way home &amp; very picturesque &amp; dirty it was.
