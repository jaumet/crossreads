When relieved at 8pm, have a pace along the deck for some fresh air before turning in at 8.30.  During my watch I read continuously, so you see time does not drag, and each hour is provided for.  By 9am the sea was very much rougher than it has been for some time, and a strong gale was blowing.  The wind whistled through the rigging with a very wailing note.
