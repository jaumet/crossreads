<p>order : home about 3 pm : wrote after tea</p>
<p>Tuesday Aug 28th</p>
<p>Monasterie : still showery : reveille 7 am : "brasso" issued &amp; we had to doll up our equipments : rations have been very short lately so the boys have lodged a complaint : but for what we buy we would by no means grow fat</p>
<p>Wednesday Aug 29th</p>
<p>Monasterie : showery : reveille 4 am : fell in &amp; inspected " our Coy were overstrength so we were left behind much to our joy : cleaned up the billet &amp; wrote : Morrie &amp; self went to St Omer after dinner for bread &amp; got eight loaves so the boys gave us a welcome : 2 letters from Lithgow :</p>
