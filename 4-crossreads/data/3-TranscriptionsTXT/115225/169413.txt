 Jan 6  th  .&nbsp; We pulled into Durban first thing in the morning &amp; were welcomed by a lady whom we saw signalling to us with flags - We had a route march through Durban in the morning &amp;  at  were then given leave from 2 till 10.30 P.M. The trams were free to troops &amp; we had a ride round. The day was a little bit showery but the sun was very hot. Had a hot bath &amp; went to the Y.M.C.A. concert in the evening. Like Durban very much.  Had  Had a ride in a ricksha 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 7  th  &nbsp; Sunday &amp; we had a church parade ashore. it was raining all day, &amp;  we  were given leave 11.30 A.M. till 10 P.M. 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 8  th  &nbsp; Went for a five mile route march in the morning. lovely day - given leave from 2 P.M. till 10.P.M. Went to museum &amp; art gallery. didn&#39;t think much of them - Had a swim at the Marine Parade baths, &amp; had tea on the Parade - Went to Y.M.C.A. concert in the evening. 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 9  th  &nbsp; We put out into the bay &amp; left Durban at 12.30 mid-day three hours behind the &quot;Berimar&quot;. we were in sight of land during the rest of the day - lovely day &amp; smooth sea.
