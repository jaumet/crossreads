  
 13.4.15 Tue. 13th.  B Troop returned from Cairo & arrived in Camp at 1 o'clock this morning, reville went at 5.30 this morning, as the days are getting longer.  Stan & I went on parade with B Troop & were getting squadron drill all the morning, arrived back in Camp for dinner at 2 o'clock;  had nothing to do between dinner & stables;  boxing with Conway after tea, had a game of billiards, & put the rest of the evening in writing.  Stan went into Heliopolis to-night.
