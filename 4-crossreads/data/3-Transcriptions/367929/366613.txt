<p>April 1915<br/>25 Sun<br/>Message to report to Board to sit on the damage done in the Wazir area of good Friday. So took the three horses and Gwillian and rode home  from Helouan,had lunch with Col Cox at Meadi en route. This was the day of A and NZ landing.</p>
<p>26 Mon<br/>Reported and found myself next senior to the President Lt Col Fallows T.D.<br/>Gallipoli doings of others.
