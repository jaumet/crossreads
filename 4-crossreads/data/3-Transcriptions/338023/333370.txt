<p>[Letter written on paper from the Australian Red Cross]<br/>Cty of Middlesex War Hospital<br/>9/10/17</p>
<p>Dear little Mother</p>
<p>Because you see the address as above you must not let yourself imagine heaps of horrors &amp; gallons of gore &ndash; as a matter of fact your hopeful wandering boy is quite alright with the small exception of a game leg that has to be rested and occasional 'Verey Lights' in the head. These things are to be expected &amp; don't last, thank the Lord!</p>
<p>I did not stop an iron ration &ndash; bar a few little splinters that  did not stop but just scratched as they passed. It was really the 9.2 that stopped me when I was going back to HQ with</p>
