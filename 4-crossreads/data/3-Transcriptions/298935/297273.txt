<p>On the 11th April 1914, I arrived at Melbourne from England and until the end of October I worked in the South &ndash; West of Victoria.  Returning to Melbourne I enlisted on the 9th November 1914 at the St. Kilda Rd. Barracks as an infantryman.<br/>The following day a batch of recruits was sent to the Broadmeadows camp and here most of us spent a happy, strenuous three months training in the 'gentle' arts of warfare.  I was then included as a signaler in the 2nd  reinforcements to the 6th Battalion.<br/>After many false alarms we received our embarkation orders and at 4 a.m. on the 1st February 1915 we were all hard at <strike>h</strike> work getting ready to leave the camp.
