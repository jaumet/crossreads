<p>"The Kaiser's Prayer" (the Latest Ultimatum)</p>
<p>Gott,Gott, dear Gott, attention please,<br/>Your partner &ndash; Vilhelm's here<br/>Und has a vord or two to say<br/>INTO YOUR PRIVATE ear.<br/>So durn avay all udders now<br/>Uns listen vell to me.<br/>For vat I say concerns me much,<br/>Meinself  und Shermany</p>
<p>You know dest Gott. I am your friend,<br/>Und from mein hour of birth<br/>I quietly let you der Heffen<br/>While I ruled o'er der earth;<br/>  Und ven I toldt  mein soldiers<br/>Of by-gone battle days<br/>I gladly split der glory<br/>Und gafe you half der braise.</p>
<p>In every vay I tried to brove<br/>Mein heart to you was true.<br/>Und only claimed mine honest share<br/>In great deeds vat ve do;<br/>  You could not haf a better friend.<br/>Knsky or land or sea<br/>Dan Kaiser Vilhelm number two<br/>Der Lord of Shermany.</p>
<p>So vat I say, dear Gott, ist this<br/>Dat ve should still be friends<br/>Und you should help to send my foes<br/>To meet their bitter endts.<br/>If you, dear Gott, vill this me do,<br/>I'll nothing ask again<br/>Und you and I will pardners be<br/>For evermore- Amen.</p>
<p>But listen ,Gott. It must be quick<br/>Your help to me you send<br/>Or else I haf to stop attack<br/>Und only blay defend.<br/>So four and twenty hours I gif<br/> To make der Allies run<br/>Und put me safe into mein blace-<br/> Der middle of der sun.</p>
<p>If you do this, I'll do my part<br/>I'll tell der vorld der fact-<br/>But if you don't den I must tink<br/>It is an hostile act.<br/>Den var at once I will declare<br/>Und in main anger rise<br/>Und send mein Zepplin ships to wage<br/>A fight up in der skies.</p>
<p>Dis ultimatum now, dear Gott,<br/>Is one of many more<br/>Mein mind is settled up to clear<br/>Der whole world off der floor<br/>Because you vas mein pardner, Gott.<br/>An extra  schance is giffen,<br/> So help at once, or else I'll be<br/>Der Emperor of Heffen</p>
