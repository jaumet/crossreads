 In Sept 1914 he enlisted &amp; on Oct 18/1914 sailed on the S.S "Afric" for World War 1. returning to Sydney for Anzac furlough on Dec. 9th/1918. 
 On Sept 13/1920 he married Alice Edith Mallott, of Sydney. They had four children &ndash; 3 sons &amp; 1 daughter &ndash; Thomas Cecil, enlisted R. A.
