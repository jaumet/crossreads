<p>Jan 22nd<br/>Word has come through which I did not welcome with any enthusiasm  It was to the effect that the 1915 men do not leave on the 29th as was first anticipated and now we are told our date of leaving is indefinite. Consequently we can do nothing but sit back and take notice.</p>
