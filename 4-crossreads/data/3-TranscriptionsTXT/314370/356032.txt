 May, 1916 Thursday 25 Mopped about camp all morning &amp; after dinner went out to Pattis' Guiseppe had gone to Port Said for a few days to visit some relatives. After supper several more people came in &amp; we had a glorious time till 11.30 pm 
 Friday 26 very warm again very little to do, so do nothing but read &amp; smoke &amp; smoke &amp; read. After tea I went to a picture show in Cairo.
