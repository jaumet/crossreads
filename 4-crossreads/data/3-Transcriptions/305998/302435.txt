<p>&amp; trucked horses for our three days journey to Le Harve. We passed through some lovely green country en route</p>
<p>26/ Arrived at Le Harve at 6 pm raining hard, marched five miles to a camp. We got more horses &amp; guns from the store depot here &amp; left for Armentiers district.</p>
<p>We have been here in action for five months now &amp; our battery has not suffered a causility so what luck we have so far. The 19th of July was the biggest here &amp; we fired about 5,000 rounds each battery a day so, we were kept</p>
