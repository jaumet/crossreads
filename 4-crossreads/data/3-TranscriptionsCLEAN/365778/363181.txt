  
 Friday 16/3/16 
 More training, inspection by G.O.C. Bde tomorrow. 
 Saturday 17/3/17 
 Inspection of Battalion by G.O.C. Very Goof turn out - played football against 25th - Meeting of C.O's. 
 Sunday 18/3/17 
 Quiet Day generally - very stiff after Football - experiments with Bombs. Wrote to Doff - orders to march forward tomorrow night. 
 Monday 19/3/17 
 Moved at 9 am, had mid-day meal at The BUTTE moved to BAPAUME and billeted for night, reconnoitre towards VAULX.
