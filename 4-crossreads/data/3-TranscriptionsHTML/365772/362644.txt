<p>Cairo<br/>20.8.15<br/>My dearest Sister,<br/>I am off to the front again tomorrow with 30 of our old boys who have been wounded &amp; going back so will have another go at our friend the Turk &amp; have a chance of getting my own back for the last three hits they gave me.<br/>I am pretty busy fitting the boys up etc., so will have to write a brief note this time dear. I will drop you a line from the boat.<br/>Give my love to Ralph &amp; Beres &amp; I hope I pull through this &amp; go back with you again, it is about time my luck changed isnt it.<br/>Well dear I will close now with fondest love from<br/>your loving brother<br/>Ken</p>
