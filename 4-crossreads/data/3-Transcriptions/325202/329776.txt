<p>we have been here we have had a sample of every variety of weather Snow, sleet, hail, wind, warm sunshine in turn &ndash; today it is drizzling rain.</p>
<p>All our 1st Division is billetted round this neighbourhood at present being in Reserve. We are about 15 miles from the front line &amp; expect in a few days our Division will go up &amp; relieve the 2nd Division which is holding a share of the trenches. We are kept pretty busy taking supplies round to where various units are billetted.</p>
