  
 Egyptian words 
 Egyptian - Answer Impshee - Clear out Heggory - Hurry up Saheeda - Greeting Quaiso - Good Quaiso Rita - Very Good Mush Quaiso - No Good Bookara - Tomorrow Ismah - Hey Buck Sheesh - Tip Feloosh - Money Mahfeesh - There is none. Finished. Mensh - Keep to the right.
