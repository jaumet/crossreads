 May 4 1918 at sea 
 coaling getting water &amp; food on board we haeve anchor for panama steaming down cheaspeak bay we salute the US Battleships while standing to attention it is a smooth sea and warm weather and after dropping the pilot and waving adieu to the pilot ship we stand out to sea once more on the atlantic pass Jamestown where the first English landed in America was it the mayflower
