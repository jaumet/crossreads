<p>Friday, 18/5/17<br/>Up at 3.45 a.m., had light breakfast &amp; caught 4.55 a.m. train for York &ndash; stopped at Peterboro', [Peterborough] Grantham, Newark, Doncaster, Retford &amp; Selby, arrived York about 10 a.m.  Caught Scarboro [Scarborough] train &amp; changed at Seamer Junct. 3 miles from Scarboro &amp; arrived Filey about 11.30 a.m.  Walked to Muston about 2 miles &amp; met some of the family.  Uncle Will arrived home about 5.15 p.m. &amp; after</p>
