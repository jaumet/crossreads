 [Janvier 31 Vendredi] Receive letter from mother about Uncle Willy's death, a beautiful touching letter &amp; a fine tribute to Uncle he had a great heart always giving, living up to his motto always. 
 Also letter from May G. very touching. 
 I am immensely anxious to be home at once, I am very anxious about the ones at Home. 
 Finlay crashes doing the falling leaf stunt, not hurt. 
 [Fevrier 1 Samedi] No flying very stormy.  Day in town with Beaton playing billiards.  Home today felt Uncle Willy's loss very much very sad. 
 To be launched on the Pup on Monday hope it is fine.
