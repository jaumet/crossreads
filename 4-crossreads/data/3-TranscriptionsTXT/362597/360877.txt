Wednesday 19.1.16 (28)We are steaming through a glassy smooth sea & it is very warn 'tween decks. Full Dress Parade & the Colonel spoke of our tidyness & good conduct & hoped it would continue.
Semi-finals of Boxing Tournaments held. Some very good fights.
