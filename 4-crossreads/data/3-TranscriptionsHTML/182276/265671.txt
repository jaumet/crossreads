<p>Intelligence Report No II<br/>from 6 am <strike>30</strike> 1st to 6 am 2nd<br/>Right Sector:- No 5 gun fired indirect on I 18 Central Roads &amp; trenches around Ruelle de la Noix<br/>1.30 &ndash; 2 pm 102nd Brigade bombarded enemy lines with Trench Mortars. Between "Stand to" and midnight our Lewis guns &amp; Snipers were active. The enemy M.G's &amp; Snipers retaliated. After midnight very little firing took place<br/>Left Sector:- No 7 position fired indirect on I 6 A 5/0, Road and Dump at Intact Farm. No 8 &ndash; on I 12 A 4/9 Trenches &amp; buildings round Retaliation Farm.</p>
<p>B.J Carey Capt<br/>9th M.G. Coy<br/>2-12.16</p>
