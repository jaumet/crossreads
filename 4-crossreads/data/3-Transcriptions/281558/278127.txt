<p>Pal of Mine &ndash; continued</p>
<p>A rough hewn bit of limestone on the spot where Ginger lies<br/>Bears a proper soldiers tribute &ndash; "a brave man never dies"<br/>&amp; I hear the sentnel saying when the judgment story's told<br/>All's Well Pass little comrade to your dugout made of gold</p>
<p>Where the wailing croon of Shrapnel plays a requiem o'er you head<br/>In the slime of muddy Flanders where the bravest's  fought &amp; bled<br/>I'll go looking for you Ginger when I've crossed lifes firing line<br/>&amp; wether hell or heaven may I meet you Pal o mine.</p>
<p>Mdme Gressier Gressier<br/>&agrave;Westbecourt par Lumbres Pasd Cal</p>
<p>Monsieur Jules Devuldes<br/>Cultivateur<br/>Claimarais</p>
<p>Mr Albert Rowe<br/>Hollowell's Dairy<br/>Cricket St Thomas<br/>Nr Chard<br/>Somerset</p>
