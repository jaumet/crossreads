<p>recent victories &amp; the French are greatly excited over the recapture of their big towns : Fritz must be getting it hot &amp; strong &amp; it seems funny to get good nights &amp; have no air raids on the menu.</p>
<p>I hear the Belgies have got the big gun he used to shell this place with so we are done with its shells for good now :</p>
<p>Tuesday Oct 22nd to Oct 31st</p>
<p>Still on 12 hours so haven't had a chance to go out at all</p>
<p>Friday November 1st Mardyk</p>
<p>had a stroll down town for a couple of hours &amp; the place is fast livening up &amp; new shops are opening up here &amp; there.</p>
<p>Sat Nov 2nd to Tuesday 5th Nov Mardyk :</p>
<p>went to pictures with Gil on Sat night &amp; they were pretty good too : A Fritz plane was over yesterday morn but the anti-aircraft guns soon made the place too hot for them &amp; off he went :</p>
<p>Wednesday Nov 6th to Sunday 10th Mardyk :</p>
<p>nothing much doing : we are on 8 hours again from today &amp; it will be very welcome :</p>
<p>Monday Nov 11th Mardyk</p>
<p>Signing of Armistice came through &amp; there was a lot of whistle blowing etc. "Mournful Mary" blew &amp; the anti-aircraft fired a bit of a salute :</p>
