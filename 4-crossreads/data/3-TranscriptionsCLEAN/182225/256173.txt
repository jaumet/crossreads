 1916   TUESDAY 18   JAN. 
 Spes Wharton & Jack Keep are in 15/1 st  Brigade & they have a good lot of fellows there & get away 9 th  February so I applied for a transfer to their lot & to their tent & got it  There are 11 in the tent but they are all real good coves & mates. Shifted my duds into my new quarters this morning
