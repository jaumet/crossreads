<p>1917 Friday 27 July</p>
<p>Afternoon off. According to arrangement Jock &amp; self meet Miss S &ndash; at gate 2 p.m. J. late as usual. Miss tram catch next. Land Le Portel by tram. Incident of collision cart &amp; tram shaft narrowly missing Miss S &ndash; head smash window behind. Roam over fields knee deep in grass, a regular summer idyll. <br/>Reach Equiham camp about 6 p.m. - tired but happy. Meet Miss Smith. Tea at chalet. Walk home cool of evening  My discomfort. Gharry &ndash; Horse falls. more excitement. &ndash; Late home.</p>
