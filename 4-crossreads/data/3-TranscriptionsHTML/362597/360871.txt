<p>appeared in the ring. The tall cove was rigged up in neck to knee costume but the little cove was rigged up like a prize fighter with a pair of V's only made out of the Union Jack. Then the fight commenced. You never saw such a funny thing in your life.</p>
<p>In the 4th round both of the men were too weak to put their hands up. After each round the seconds used to take in a mouthful of water &amp; spit all over the faces of the boxers &amp; rubbed their backs &amp; arms down with a dirty wet mop &amp; rubbed flour on their faces instead</p>
