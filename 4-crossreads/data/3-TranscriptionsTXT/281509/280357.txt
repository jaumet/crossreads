 14 May 1916 Sunday 
 Sunday in  mid-Mediterranean  Think of it!!   Another night passed without incident. Food pretty rotten.  Indigestion rife among the troops &ndash; Bread poison. Church parade on promenade deck at 9. a.m.  Pres. Chap. Hutchison  No singing. Bob, Tony &amp; self take communion in company of about 30 others in Sergeants Mess Room at 10 a.m. Rev. Hutchison [indecipherable] officiating Cold wind rising. &amp; sea with it. Sailing ship at pitch &amp; toss.
