 January 16th Early in the morning we marched to Wilton &amp; entrained for London, noting as we steamed through, the great utilisation of formerly idle land for producing vegetables. It was fairly open country till we reached the environs of London &amp; then with a sudden crash we right in waterloo station. We were lined up &amp; marched over Westminster bridge to Horseferry Road. In the big yard of the A.I.F.
