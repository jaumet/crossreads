<p>&amp; had dinner in Holborn.  I visited the British Museum &amp; the National Art Gallery.  All the best things are not for show being safely stored but I have seen them.  I saw the imitation of a ruined Belgian Village in Trafalgar Square which was an advert. for "Feed the Guns" &amp; then went to Lyon's Corner House, Dave &amp; Cyril both know the spot, first floor at far end on the right, against the Balcony rail looking</p>
