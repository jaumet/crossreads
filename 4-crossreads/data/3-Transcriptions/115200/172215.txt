<p>22nd/9/16</p>
<p>Cairo</p>
<p>to Day I am going to the zooligical gardens measeum and citadal</p>
<p>23/9/16/</p>
<p>Yesterday Seargent Major Roy(?) and myself went out to the Cairo Zooligical gardens and we had a most interesting time</p>
<p>These is in this zoo. Various Virities of Both animals and Birds. in one place there are about Eight Kangaroos, Presented By the 3<sup>rd</sup> A.L.H regiment and a monkey presented By Pte Warren all there were OK. The grounds are most Beautyful.</p>
