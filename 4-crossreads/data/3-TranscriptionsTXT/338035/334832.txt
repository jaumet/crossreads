 Monday 23rd At 7 a.m. left Camp 3 to relieve "B" Section at the 9th Brigade Hosp. No. 2 Camp 19 (Artillery). I was clerk there and was kept going. 
 Tuesday 24th  Keep  Kept going all day.  Received letters &amp; parcel of socks. 
 Wednesday 25th Was relieved at 10 a.m. by "A" Section and we proceeded to Camp 3 again. Cleaning harness afternoon.  Mail from home. 
 Thursday 26th Clean up in anticipation of leave tomorrow. 
 Friday 27th Although wet &amp; rain was about we left Camp at 9 a.m. and arrived in London about 1 p.m.  Thence round to Aldwych Hut with Corp. Macaulay and booked our beds etc. then dinner.  Walk with Mac down Strand and I had a photo taken at Chesneys Ltd. and we purchased several necessities also Christmas Cards.  Back to Aldwych for tea. Leaving Mac I went to Charing Cross Stn. but rain prevented me from walking about much. Had supper in the "Corner House" about 9 and then went for a run by tube to Upton Park &amp; back arriving Aldwych at midnight. 
 Saturday 28th Referendum Polling Day in Australia. Morning.  Stroll along Embankment with Mac, Harold Lees &amp; Ron Ponton and inspecting Cleopatra's Needle we pushed on to Parliament House.
