<p>April.<br/>6th.  Cloudy to-day, but still enjoyable along the coast etc.<br/>7th.  Gloriously fine &amp; I did nothing but laze on the front.<br/>8th.  Return to dirty London on a dirty day.<br/>9th.  British Museum &amp; Zoological Gardens.  The latter are very poor indeed.</p>
