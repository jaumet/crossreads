
Colonel 10.30  asked a few questions and Humed and hared not a Bad old fellow at all  asked if I was fit  I said yesMonday 26.8.18feeling much Better to day was discharged from hospital went to Boulac Convalescent homeTuesday Aug.27.8.18feeling Better to day there is a fair amount of fat here too and Plenty of medicene and BeerWednesday Aug 28.8.18feeling much Better to day went for a route march till 11.AM returned to camp 11.30.Thursday Aug 29.8.18had a good drink of Beer and had dinner slept all afternoonfriday Aug 30.8.18had tea and turned in cool night.  I am feeling wellJoe.M.Bolger
