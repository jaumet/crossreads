  
 The foregoing rubbish was written on April 3rd. It is now April 9th  & - God help us we are still here. The other night all amenities of the Scotch brigade caught fire or was set afire and at about midnight there was a noise like a demonstration at Pope's. It was a Sohag a few days ago that I had my first introduction to "Habhan". That is the Arabic for it; what the English is I don't know, it is a small seed or grain that they put into the coffee which makes it delicious it is just as if a drop of Benedictine had been put in it.
