
1915June14 Mon.Parade of the Unit held at 2 p.m. for inspection by the O.C.- most of the chaps turned out in their new uniforms - jolly warm in the sun - must have been over 100 in the shade.Yester an Officer & a dozen men arrived from Sydney - advance party of the 5 Field Ambulance - discovered G.P. Stock, Scout Commissioner for N.S.W., among them as a Pte. & also Banks Smith, an old Wahroonga chap.
