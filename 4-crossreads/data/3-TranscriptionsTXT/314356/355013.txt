 Luncheon was a funeral affair.  The cat, a lovely Persian tabby in a highly interesting condition, clawed for food!  Said the 'girl' &ndash; "He's  wounderfully  well fed, isn't he?", and the seeded satyr "Naughty!" - wether to the girl or cat is not certain.
