 Thursday 29th March Very cold & wet, steady work during the day & very few casualties. Rumours of our going out getting stronger daily especially as Canadians have again appeared on the scene. I have yet to learn that they have done as much as the Australians in this war. 
 Friday 30th March Mud & yet more mud, the road gangs not yet showing up in the town. Went to see the local cemetary rather a fine one & must contain over a 1000 Germans. The men are buried in one section, well laid out, graves nicely kept & surrounding a French memorial built to commemorate the Franco-Prussian War. What Irony!!!
