 The dynamo is here somewhere, hidden away in the passages, but the one disadvantage is that it fails at 9.30 pm &  ^  does not  come s  on until about 6 am so I am writing this missive by candle light. 
   While out here we are doing Working Parties marching to jobs usually to the trenches doing the day's or the nights work & back to our caverned home. 
    'Tis a curious existence this military one & each chap in the A.I.F. plays many parts in doing his daily work. 
    Oh yes & there are baths just across the road where A Coy will bathe tomorrow.
