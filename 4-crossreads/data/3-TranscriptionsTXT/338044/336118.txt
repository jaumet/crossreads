
13th Nov.Usual hospital routine  At 6pm "Argyllshire" friend "Scottie" called & together we go to Cairo. After concert  & Pictures in Esbekia Gardens we have a royal supper, then a race on donkeys, which I win by fifteen lengths    & we finish up with a Garry drive home.
14th Nov:-Very cold day &  I have a rather bad cold in the head. Usual duties    &  I go to bed at 8 pm. Feel  knocked up  & can do with a good sleep.
15th Nov:-After long rest wake up refreshed  &  fit for work &  there is plenty to do. A trip to Kasir-el-aini Hospital &  there meet an English Field Artilleryman who was on the transport torpedoed off
