This is a most dreary some God forsaken country, charred trees & ploughed up ground, before the war it must have been a beautiful part of France.Too dark to write more. We have just heard that Peronne has fallen the 8th Bde I believe has crossed the Somme.This rumour turned out to be incorrect.Sept 10th 18The Beaufort War Hospital Fishponds. Bristol England.Have been wounded & am now in hospital, this is the third day in England.This is a fine large place & am very comfortable, but the foot is not good, nor not enough. Am progressing fine & think that I shall get up this afternoon & have a walk about.On the night of the 29th we did not move, we lay down were we were & tried to get a few hours sleep.
