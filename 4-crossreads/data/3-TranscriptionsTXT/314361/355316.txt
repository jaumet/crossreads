
Lou yet.Thursday. Dec. 30th 15This school breaks up on Saturday just now I am going through an exam.Yesterday I received a parcel from Louie. It was first rate, just a few things I needed.1st January 1916Saturday New Years DayWe leave school today. return to Coy. at  Abbassia. Last night I registered parcel with three silk [indecipherable]. Louie.This morning at  mea breakfast we each received a parcel from the Sydney War chest. In mine was a tin of boiled lollies, & a tin of (50) shamrock cigarettes. Have a full supply of cigarettes just now, a tin of 50 from home & a tin of shamrock.Have received no word from Lou yet, & so far only two from Louie.New Years NightReceived a letter from Lou tonight. & from Home. This afternoon I spent in Cairo.Am now back in Abbassia Camp. Sent Pos. off to Lou Home & NellSunday Jan 2nd 16This afternoon I & some friends went to the dead
