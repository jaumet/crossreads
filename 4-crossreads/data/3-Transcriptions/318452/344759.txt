<p>one was telling how he was buried by a shell another how his clothes were cut by shrapnel others how their comrades fell &amp; so on far into the evening.<br/>I told them I was having a service and quite a good many gathered round as we sang &amp; read and had a short address. &amp; the day closed in.<br/>Every night we were treated to a visit from the Hun planes dropping bombs &amp; sometimes flying low and firing the machine guns at the horse lines &amp; into groups where the <strike>were</strike> men were living, but fortunately we escaped<br/>I was sleeping out under a mess cart cover, but it was fine weather &amp; I fared pretty well.</p>
