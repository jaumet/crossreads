 April, 1916 Monday 17 Were treated to a splendid aviation display this afternoon, a small tractor Biplane looped the loop no less than 6 times in about 20 mins. Went for a long walk in the cool evening. 
 Tuesday 18 This morning my chum was sent to Hosp to have his ingrowing toenail cut out.
