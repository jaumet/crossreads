  
 [The next paragraph is written in a different hand] 15th July  Brek 7 45  Sort of a greasy potato puff puff - slop tea & bread. Overhauled & passed two tramp steams bound west about 10 0. Sardinia in sight at 8-9 this morn.  Parade 10.0. Exs Jam  18 1/  chocolate 1/- 
 [The next sentences are in Hurley's handwriting] Alexandria & we may go.  Home early & learn French. Then to bed.
