
Thursday 13th Sept. lovely day, went for a stroll, I received some Ausie letters last night. Friday fine day, went for walk, head still bad, ever since I left Weymouth.
Saturday Ada & Lily went Home, fine day
Sunday fine, all the family went for a walk after tea, round to Lancing where I seen Thomas A' Becketts place, I went to Aunt Sally's in the morning & met Mr Pelley I also met some Cousins name d of same as ours.
Monday 17th Sept changeable weather. I received my photo proofs, they are not bad, I went & bought the girls some presents. I met Mr Chipper an old friend of Dad's last night.
