<p>Before we set out we were supplied with an empty petrol tin each. It was not long before I had to discard this however. We came to a sunken road with high sloping banks and a gutter at the bottom. To make matters more difficult there was a telephone wire stretched across the bottom which had to be surmounted as well.</p>
