<p>received no casualty list of the engagement yet, so don't know if any of our friends were passed out or hurt.  This is a lovely place &amp; when the grounds were all kept up must have looked very much better.  I will have been away from my Regt. nearly a month by the time I get back to it.  I now possess a camera.  It is very interesting but expensive &amp; I am always running myself a bit short.  Most of the convalescents here are Tommies &amp; as fast as they get well they are sent back to England.  I don't think any but the Light Horse will be left in Egypt very soon.  Have met</p>
