
Wednesday 11th July 1917
Foufflin-RicametzReveille 4.30, Breakfast 5,, stables 5.30am and harnesses up and ready to move off at 6.30am. Arr. Foufflin-Ricametz about 1pm. Watered and fed mules.The country we came through was all cultivated and with the trees all scatted about it looked A1.Arr. at our billet about noon, which was a large house of about 12 rooms, lovely avenue leading up to the house used avenue for mule lines.
