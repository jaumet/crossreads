Fairly cold after tea. 5/6/18 After dinner (2pm) went to St Omer &amp; looked around the place. Went &amp; saw Notre Dame &amp; the old ruins. Is a very pretty walk in &amp; back. Arrived back again 7.30pm. 6/6/18 Wrote home 11/6/18 After tea went to aerodrome &amp; inspected several aeroplanes at close quarters.
