 July 27th Rehearsal - Entertain patients at varius wards of A.G.H. - Show in th Evening to "Engineers" at St, Etienne July 28th (Sunday) A day of days! - at 10 a.m. we leave camp for Rouen & there take Excursion Launch up "River Seine" - Our 27 Batt. Band is also on board
