8th October 1917Left Park House today for AIF Headquarters London to work on a special job. Dont know how it will last or the outcome of the affair.
9th October 1917Fixed up at Horseferry Road to start work tomorrow. Also got diggings close handy.
5/1/18 to 14/1/18On 10 days leave to Scotland &amp; to Carlisle &amp; Manchester. Had a fairly good time.
1/2/18Left London for Plymouth to get on the boat for home.
3/2/18Left Plymouth at 12 noon on the Balmoral Castle for home. Our Convoy consisted of the "Morea" as escort with 12 destroyers. The Argylleshire Euripides with troops. The Ionic with soldiers' wives. The Khyber &amp; a Holt boat completed the fleet.
15/2/18Arrived at Sierra Leone (Freetown) at 7 oclock this morning,  after rather a pleasant trip from Plymouth. Its terribly hot here, though Freetown looks a decent sort of town.
