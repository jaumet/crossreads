<p>July 1917<br/>bridges to allow traffic to pass through, &amp; we also had to "break" on an average of 3 times a day.<br/>20th   I went to Senlis this morning to have my teeth attended to, &amp; will probably be staying a couple of days.<br/>The "Divisional Train" arrived in Corbie this morning &amp; passed through the same procedure as the three brigades.   General Birdwood also, was here to-day, but was unable to attend the inspection on the previous days.<br/>21st   I am at Senlis now in the 8th Field Hospital &amp; the dentist has made a start on my teeth<br/>We had very heavy rain during the afternoon</p>
