 Saturday 31/3/17 Presentation of Medals, Lloyd, Cherry, Ward &amp; Wood got M.C.'s for Malt French, going on leave tomorrow. Sunday 1/4/17 Left by car with Gen Smith &amp; Slane, arrived Bologne [Boulogne] at 1 pm caught boat at 5.30 pm, in London at 9, staying at Morleys. Monday 2/4/17 Saw Genl House, &amp; Col Buckley, also went to Bank re money, &amp; Tailors, went to Suzettes at night, wired Doff saying on leave ten days, all three well, wire me care Bank.
