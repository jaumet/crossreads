<p>I shall cable but do not write any more letters after this until yhou hear from me.</p>
<p>Ten of us have been told to be ready. We do not know what route but shall cable as soon as I reach Australia.</p>
<p>Have not received any letters from Ethel since she went to Talbot.</p>
<p>It was very bright   &amp;  gay fro you all having Annie   &amp;  Phyllis staying so long. You seem to have plenty of visitors   &amp;  it is good for you.</p>
<p>Had nice letters from Chanty   &amp;  Gers, Mollie. Alice Bower, Marion, Annie Thomas, Mrs Baynes   &amp;  Allen.</p>
