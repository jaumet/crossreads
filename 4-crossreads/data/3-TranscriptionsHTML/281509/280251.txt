<p>1916 January 28<br/>Friday</p>
<p>A regular mix up.  Camp commandant up this morning &amp; stayed all day conferring with officers.  Result. Afternoon parade &amp; reorganising of whole camp.  Divided into 3 sections. A. B &amp; C. Tony &amp; self &amp; most of E ward in C section, Bob in B.</p>
<p>Miss Palser sends up hamper.  Have good tea of dainties.  Thunder storm breaks after tea, intermittent rain.  [F]ive play cards &amp; read in tent.  9 pm deluge falls &amp; floods tent.  Tony &amp; hoppy leg looking for dry spot.<br/>See proofs of photo taken yesterday. very good.</p>
