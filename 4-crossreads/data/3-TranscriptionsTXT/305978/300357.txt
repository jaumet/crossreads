 Dec 16th/ Out pattroling, found enemy near camp, so we had a cut in at them. They must have thought they had us nearly under. Seeing us retreat in disorder so got much more game &amp; came closer, they soon bolted back to the hills when they found us with plenty of bullets for them We cannot do much till reinforcements arrive from Alexandria.
