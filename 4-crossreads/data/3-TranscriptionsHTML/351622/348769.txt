<p>21 January 1916 Fri<br/>Steve Jeffes receives billy from Ivy James of Walcha. Went to see Vere &amp; Cecil Johnson &amp; then called on Jimmy [Youman]. It is so much like old times when we get together to have a little chat. I wonder if ever the time will see us altogether again in [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>22 January 1916 Sat<br/>Ken leaves for Cairo. Paid a visit to Lieut Roger Mogue drank his health with a couple of the old familiar brand of black-white. Went to see Jim Frank &amp; Taplin in the night &amp; gave the Crown &amp; Anchor a few bob after I had left them. Waggon drill all the morning. Afternoon off.</p>
