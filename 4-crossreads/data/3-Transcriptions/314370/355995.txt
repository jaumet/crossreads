<p>March, 1916<br/>Sunday 12<br/>As soon as dinner was over Dad &amp; his boy came down &amp; had a look at my "dugout" which is plentifully decorated w/- pictures etc.<br/>Going into Cairo we went on the tram out to "Vieux Caire" (old Cairo) to have a look round<br/>Finished up by going to the Kursaal</p>
<p>Monday 13<br/>Start seconds weeks work<br/>Put the class through a final run over ready for tomorrows exam.  All seem ready for it.</p>
