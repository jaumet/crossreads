  
 1 May, Thursday 
 F. I am up [?]times & very hungry. I make a hard confession quite easily. M & I go for a walk. We clamber over the hedge & have to get over the water ditch. We sit by the fire & talk all evening. I do ironing. 
 2 May Friday 
 F. Hot shower in afternoon. I get up early & light fire etc. I print P.Cto M. do ironing & Auntie & I go for a walk all around by back road. We get caught in the rains. I sit by the fire & write. I have my nails manicured & thank manicurist. 
 Wrote Dick & D. Op
