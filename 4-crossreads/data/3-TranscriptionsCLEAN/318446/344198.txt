It has rained off & on every day & night during the past week & as we are spending the night out here in the open with no bivouac, I shall certainly be very cold & probably wet through.  This place is a big palm grove rather scattered with a mud village in the middle & a big sized swamp - now a lake - alongside. 
 El Arish Jan. 1st 1917 Arrived back from the stunt yesterday without mishap. We have had a very promising New Years day, a tremendous sandstorm raging all day and even now (8 p.m.) the wind, though abated, is still violent.  This morning three enemy 'planes over - for the first time for a week - dropped about 12 bombs on the pier here & railhead at Kilo 139.
