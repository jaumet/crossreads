  
 16 January 1918 Wednesday (X to X crossed out) X This is Rail head at present &  Hors  Camels are loaded every morning with Forage & stores for the Trenches- Your letter Nov 25th & Days & one for Jim Ayre came today, am glad mine turned up safely-  I note all your news-  I do hope you are able to accept Mary Austins offer & get a trip to Melbourne   it does the little Mother good  X This afternoon the Naval guns have been banging away at the Turks line from the sea-   In the distance the 800 lbs Shells can be seen bursting from a hill with glasses
