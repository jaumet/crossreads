  
 July 1918 Tuesday 16 
 Bresle. F.O.P. and Bty Position. Weather perfect though a little warm if anything.  Went on duty from 2 am till 8 am. Very light shelling of Ribemont by the Bosche. Parson's arrived on scene demanding water but I made him some tea. About 2 hours later he wanted more tea - he did not get it. Arrived home at 7pm with Percy. Old Mick Sheedy came up to Battery. Had a heavy thunderstorm in early part of evening drenched everything. Fritz attack commenced on a 40 mile front
