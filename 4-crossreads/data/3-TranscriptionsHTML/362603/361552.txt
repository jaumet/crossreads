<p>Church Army Recreation Hut letterhead<br/>25/11/17<br/>Dear Mum,<br/>Another week has gone past &amp; there is not much news to relate.  I went to Sarum on Tuesday &amp; yesterday.  I visited my Friends as usual.  I did not ask You to write to my other &amp; first Friends in Salisbury.  They are also Ellis' Friends.  They are homely people just the same as we are &amp; I always visit them in the afternoon's, &amp; spend the evenings with My Friends in the Close.  Mr Edmonds <u>13</u>   Wyndham Rd Salisbury is His address.  They are just lovely to Us &amp; they are not wealthy either.  Just hard working people the same as ourselves.  They would be delighted to hear from You &amp; to receive a few 6d. Books of Views.  Our Friends can never do enough for Us.  Do not ever think that we are homeless waifs &amp; then we have our lovely Manx Auntie.  I'll never rest content now until I get Her out to Australia.  She is as lively as a Girl although She often has sleepless nights.  She is a very light sleeper &amp; when once wakened, rarely gets to sleep again.<br/>I am enclosing a few leaves from my old album.
