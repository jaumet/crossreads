<p>Wednesday 15th<br/>Sent all my Xmas cards away, big mail came in.  I got no less than 18 letters my father is very ill and it is for that reason I want to get home, all other letters brought good news.  Big move on, troops are leaving &amp; exchanging here, gave farewell night to Welsh Sgts. at our mess.</p>
<p>Thursday 16th<br/>Anxiously awaiting answer from Cairo, wrote home and two Ev.  Rec. some lovely Xmas presents to-day including a beautiful wallet from Ev.</p>
<p>Friday 17th<br/>Spent day writing, time hangs very heavily now, camp very quiet.</p>
<p>Saturday 18th<br/>My mare is in great nick.  I gallop her every morning.  Send 10 men away to [indecipherable] each day for Dental treatment.</p>
<p>Sunday 19th<br/>Monaster has fallen, good news from all fronts we are disgusted with Conscription failing</p>
