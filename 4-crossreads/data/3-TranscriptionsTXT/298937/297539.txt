 Thursday 9th Mar. 
 Yesterday afternoon Molloy, Dr. Cockinos &amp; I went to the Pyramids. The Dr. insisted on paying all expenses. Arrived at the face of Cheops, we elected to go inside, but when we started Dr. Cockinos drew back, for it looked like being very strenuous for such a hot afternoon.
