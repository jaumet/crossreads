
No 3 HospitalAbbassiaNear Cairo7th June 1916.
My dear MotherAfter all said and done I have gone and got mumps, and am up here in hospital, which is a jolly side nicer place than in the contact camp out in the desert at Tel el Kebir.  I started to get them last Sunday; was sent to hospital in camp on Monday and came up here to day.Yesterday in camp I received twelve letters and My Word! I was pleased.Two were from you, one from Aunt Katie, and the rest from Telarah, none
