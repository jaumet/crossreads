<p>respects<br/>The large number of bays and inlets, in the coast are very entrancing. A large lighthouse stood on the edge of this coast "(a sentinel of the sea)".</p>
<p>The next day Sunday, 3rd Dec 1916 was the hottest day I have ever experienced in my life, awnings were erected everywhere for our benefit</p>
<p>There are a number of other Australian transports, also warships in the bay alongside us, evidently awaiting further orders from the Admiralty.</p>
<p>The dark people were not slow in looking for trade; every morning and afternoon found them constant visitors at our ship's side endeavouring to sell their wares; fruit of all description. They did not experience much trouble in the disposing of them!</p>
