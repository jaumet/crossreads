<p>Jan 20th :- the 1st Regt. moved out of El Arish at * A.M. to do escort <strike>duty</strike> &amp; guard duty to a party of engineers camped 8 miles East of El Arish at a place named Bir el Burj.<br/>Jan 23rd :-  When we left El Arish we expected to be away only 48 hours, &amp; consequently we brought only a couple of blankets &amp; no tents or oilsheets. It rained heavily last night &amp; we received a good soaking. It now appears that we are to be out here for a week or ten days, &amp; rumour has it that when we return we are to receive a few days leave.</p>
