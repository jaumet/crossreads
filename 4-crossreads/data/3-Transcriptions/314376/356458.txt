<p>147<br/>Another find was a Klufs. German steel is well tempered and an article of this description well prized by the finder. In the Head Quarters dugout was a German stove. We thought this might be a trap and for a time would have nothing to do with it.</p>
<p>You can rest assured this class of order was obeyed by the Australians.</p>
<p>There were not many guns round these parts due in our case to difficulty of getting same over the mud. The Germans had left a few and the morning of the attack they let us have a fair supply.
