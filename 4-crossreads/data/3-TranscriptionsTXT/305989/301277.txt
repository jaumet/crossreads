 Sunday 7 October 1917 Scored some Arrowroot from some of the lads at Camp.  Wrote No. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 7]. 
 Monday 8 October 1917 Changed guard, got a few letters, very little doing. 
 Tuesday 9 October 1917 Quiet but dusty day, went over to Y.M.C.A. &amp; pinched some boxes. 
 Wednesday 10 October 1917 Quiet day spent most of the afternoon preparing for a Big feed up with Sgts. of the 10th Irish Division real good night. 
 Thursday 11 October 1917 Very dusty &amp; windy, got a couple of parcels.  B.W.I. band played at Y.M.C.A. quite a good turnout. 
 Friday 12 October 1917 Nothing Doing. 
 Saturday 13 October 1917 Nothing Doing, band played near our dump.
