 16/10/17  More  Another beautiful day Roosevelt paying visit to Italy  Royal honours  More squabbling in Germany.  Socialists making a noise. Russian war minister appeal to Russia saying the hour of trial has come &amp; to make all sacrifices to attain their object.  Squirrel on pole.
