<p>1915<br/>Nov. 3 Wed.<br/>Stacks of work in the new position. Col. Martin is such a fussy old chap, unmethodical in his work, forgetful memory at times &amp; needing constant attention.<br/>Stacks of correspondence awaiting attention &ndash; Busy mastering the contents of it all &amp; getting things in something like order.</p>
