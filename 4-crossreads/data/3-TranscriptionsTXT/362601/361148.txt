
23 January, Thursday
FLong busy worrying day. We straighten our room up & then the lights go out. We go to Tops room for an hour then I find Hurricane lamp & we get ready for bed after a lovely Hot bath. Our troubles never cease but we are set at last in a new room.
24 January, Friday
F & iceE & I go to Pictures & they are bon. Come Home for supper at Top's flat & then off to bed later on. Recd 2 letters from Auntie Annie. I sleep in spring bed & rather don't sleep. Dream of hurricanes & volcanoes etc on Esplanade.
