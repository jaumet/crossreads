<p>Tuesday 12 June 1917</p>
<p>Now that stirrups IRONS &amp; BITS have to be brightened, it is no easy job after 3 years of rusting. However the boys rose to the occasion &amp; found that the SODA Tablets issued to CHLORINATE the drinking water were excellent, the M.O. wonders at the demand!! so suddenly. As a rule the men drink the first water they find if dry &amp; worry not about germs.</p>
<p>It is an exploded idea the deadliness of SCORPIONS, men get bitten almost daily &amp; beyond a big swelling for the whole of the time &amp; a sick feeling, they carry on &ndash; not pleasant I'll admit but we thought at first would be more serious.</p>
