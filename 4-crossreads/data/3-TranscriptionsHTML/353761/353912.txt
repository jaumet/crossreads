<p>4<br/>rather had been such<br/>We used one of the large ground floor rooms for a kitchen and the half wrecked first floor rooms to sleep in<br/>The back portion of the building had been blown to glory and all the passageways to our bed rooms were littered with wreckage.<br/>Some large rooms upstairs were just a mass of broken roof and books.<br/>These books, &ndash; I did want to have time to rummage amongst them for there were many rare old works to be found.<br/>Strange old books in French with quaint illustrations.<br/>The whole place was such a wreck and the rats which ran over us at night were so hungry and large<br/>All this while there is the noise of our guns, and every now and then shells whizzing into town from the enemy smashing up to more complete ruin the ruin already made.<br/>Good night Love to all<br/>Still waiting for furlough<br/>Am well Mother &amp; children<br/>More when not so tired</p>
