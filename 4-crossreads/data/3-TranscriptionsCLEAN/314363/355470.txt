 The following has been crossed out: 
 This morning we moved out to this village, HQ only are in village the coys, out in the trenches, which are every where outside. June 2nd 18 Pont Noyelles To day have been laying lines all over the country as the coys. are spread about so much. To night a few shells have come over, but none have fallen in the villaghe. June 7th 18 We have been for the last few days camped in a pretty wood outside the village (Pont Noyelles). We are having a very nice time, there is plenty of swimming, as there is water all round us. Yesterday the the Bn. held aquatic sports, & our section pulled off two firsts. This afternoon Tom paid me a visit, had tea together. Was inoculated this evening.
