However, this bed although hard was very acceptable considering the hours we spent out of it as will be explained later.<br/>At 6.30am reveille sounded &amp; was welcomed with out any loud exclamations. Every tent however buzzed with subdued sleepy mutterings except ours in which but two voices sounded &ndash; those of Hop &amp; Pierce. Hop tried to coax Eric &amp; I out, &amp; Pierce naturally got out to "work"   as though we went into camp for that purpose. Anyhow even though there were but 4 in our tent [thanks to our own "head work" Hop got little thanks for it., occasionally owing to the presence of the sergeant major it was absolutely a case of necessity that we got out (via the back ) to avoid punishment. Hop did get caught once &amp; was fined 5/-</p>
