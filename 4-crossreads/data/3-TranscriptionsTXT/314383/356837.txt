Feb 26Trip fairly monotonous.  Arrived  here 1am for coal apparantly stay here all night.
Feb 27Left 5pm.  All the coaling is done at sea some distance from the land.  Huge barges convey the coal to the ships side & natives carry it aboard in baskets carried on their heads.
