 The Hardships of Gallipoli 
 Gallipoli like Mesopotamia was full of hardships  &amp; general inconveniences.  Nearly every trench was located on the summit of tremendous steep mountains, &amp; the continual going up &amp;  down their steep inclines required great stamina &amp; determination especially as we had to transport by hand all the heavy boxes of bully beef &amp; biscuits.  We were short of reinforcements &amp; so had to do double our share in the trenches &amp; when liberated for rest we were invariably called upon for fatigue which was always heavy  &amp; most tiring.
