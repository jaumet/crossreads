<p>Our party return to our base at Ft. Rompu. Gooseberry bush.<br/>May 27th<br/>Take up my duties in Dressing Room. Return of less appreciated food. Very busy day.<br/>May 28th<br/>Dressing room inspection of our Hospital and quarters by the A.D.M.O. and Surgeon Generals Porter and House. "All well pleased with our (self and pal) dressing room.<br/>Sabbath Church and communion.</p>
