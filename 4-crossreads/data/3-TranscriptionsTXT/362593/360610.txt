
Saturday 5 October 1918Photographic recco to Rayak.  Turks have completely gone.  Dropped 8 bombs on Bedouins looting.Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 2 h. 40 m.Went shooting partridge on Mt. Carmel, quite fine spot.  Ginty & self got 5 lovely birds.
Sunday 6 October 1918Shooting again, very bad shooting, only got 1 bird.Hours flown on active service, 469 h. 27 m.
