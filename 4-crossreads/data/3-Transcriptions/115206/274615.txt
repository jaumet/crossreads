<p>February 1918<br/>Thursday 14</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Made two trips to F.O.O. today, gee but it's a long step.<br/>Made enquiries about E.G.F. and find he is at Spy Farm<br/>Weather has been very unsettled.<br/>Received a wire that Fritz was massing at Lake Farm . <br/>Our Bty put 20 rounds in. <br/>Had the 2 to 4 this morning.<br/>Feeling fairly tired after so much travelling today, so I must drink my run and pop into bed.</p>
