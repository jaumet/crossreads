Arrived there I found that there were seven or eight cases to be dealt with. My case did not come on until about 4 PM.It was a wretched business from start to finish.My Accused was a tall muscular Youth of about 23 years, fair complexioned, fair hair, blue eyes and Roman Nose. He had a striking personality. Inflexibility of purpose could be read in that face.
