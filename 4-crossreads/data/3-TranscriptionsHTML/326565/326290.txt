<p>Dhows on canal carrying metal for railway &amp; road.  A raft constructed by 7th Aust Engineers camping here, sank while being given a trial by 16 men; one was drowned &amp; several others much the worse for their experience.  More A.M.C comforts distributed.</p>
<p>18/1/16. Tuesday<br/>General Godley &amp; Staff of Imperial forces inspected all troops in vicinity this morning.  Victim of drowning fatality buried at Ismailia this morning.
