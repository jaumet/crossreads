<p>June, 1916<br/>Friday 16<br/>final exam &amp; packing.<br/>officers behaved very well to me.<br/>Two got specials &amp; all were over 95 &percnt;<br/>Damn good.</p>
<p>Saturday 17<br/>March out of camp &amp; go by special train to Tel-el-Kebir<br/>After being shunted from one place to another we eventually landed in the 14th T.B.<br/>perched on a great sand hill miles away from anywhere</p>
