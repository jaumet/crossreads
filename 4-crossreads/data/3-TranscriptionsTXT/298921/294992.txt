 January 1915 Tuesday 19 The days are becoming shorter &amp; the nights colder as we move away from the Equator it is winter in Egypt &amp; I expect cold enough at night. To date 7 horses have died, mostly from Pneumonia &ndash; Day follows day, stables routine broken by Rifle inspection, we are able to walk the horses all the way around the ship. Orderly officer today.
