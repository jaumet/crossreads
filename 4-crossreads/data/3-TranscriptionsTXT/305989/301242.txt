Sunday 4 February 1917Paraded sick to get out of "Pilot parade" heard the Turko mine blown up.
Monday 5 February 1917Quiet day:  picquet at night.
Tuesday 6 February 1917On field firing all day over at Y.M.C.A.
Wednesday 7 February 1917Read Ginger Mick:  had concert at Y.M.C.A.
Thursday 8 February 1917Gordon Bourkebek [Birkbeck] went on leave to Cairo.  Went to Y.M.C.A.
Friday 9 February 1917Got news of move, got things ready for "trek".  Bought a few things at the Canteen.
Saturday 10 February 1917Left El Arish:  trekking west on trough fatigue, stopped at 3rd Brigade.  Saw Sgt. Moore 303.  Camped the night at "Kilo 139".  Saw Billy Campbell.
