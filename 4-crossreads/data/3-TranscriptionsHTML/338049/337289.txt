<p>Wednesday 20 March<br/>Visited hairdressers, visited bank &amp; looked around in morning. Visited Opera Marriage of Figaro. Very good. Had company of elderly Capt. Very good.</p>
<p>Met Will had tea &amp; then to the Better 'Ole. Met Roy Ewart &amp; Nick there. Show not too bad. Slept at Horseferry Rd.</p>
<p>Arranged to go to Scotland with Will tomorrow night.</p>
