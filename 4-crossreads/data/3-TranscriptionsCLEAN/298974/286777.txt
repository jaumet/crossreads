 How is the allotment? 
 Cape Town 21/9/16 
 Dear Mother We had a few hours leave here but it was at night & we could not see the town. We just strolled about & had two big feeds. Some of the buildings are very fine & the table Mountain is wonderful. Am feeling very well. Love to all Dene
