<p>Sun. Apr. 15th  [no entry]<br/>Mon. Ap. 16th <br/>Today I packed up some souvenirs &amp; sent to ArThur.  Had a good rehearsal tonight.  Things going good.</p>
<p>Tues. Apr. 17.  Got Letter from Aunt Nell.</p>
<p>Wed. Ap. 18th   Our Concert came off at 7 p.m.  The Mess Hut was packed about 600 or 700 there.  The lady on the Farm lent Payney some clothes to wear in the Sketch; &amp; she &amp; her little girl Susan came to the Show.  They laughed very much &amp; enjoyed themselves, although they wouldn't understand a word we said.  The Anzac Concert Party were there &amp; said it was a very good show.</p>
