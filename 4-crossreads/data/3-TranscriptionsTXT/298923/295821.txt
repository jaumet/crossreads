
4 June 1918 TuesdayYes I remember Harry Watton carrying me on his horse to Correagh & you greeting me there.  A.W. Pearce letter speaks for itself-   Thank Day & K for Parcel not yet received- but coming-Note all your news-   Nothing doing here yet so no need to be anxious-   Papers coming later on-Dolly well & happy.  Tell me what name you would like for the Trumpeter-All my loveYour son F W
