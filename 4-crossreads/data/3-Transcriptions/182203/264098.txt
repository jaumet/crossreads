<p>homesick. If this weather would only last but we must expect the winter now, so shall have to make the most of it.<br/>Our tent is must warmer than we expected it to be but oh, shall never get used to tent-life. We are very busy, only feel very disgusted at feeling tired before our work is done, still we are surprised at our energy, not that ever lasting weary feeling we had in Egypt.<br/>I'm feeling splendid &amp; eating well. Last night the Unit gave a War-Bridge Evening, we were all invited &amp; a number of us went &amp; had a most enjoyable time. Our first gaiety in France. <br/>Do you still play <u>Bridge</u> &amp; <u>500</u>? I often think of you all, having your regular game with dear old Mother. When I think of your nice cosy fire</p>
