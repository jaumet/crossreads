<p>Thursday 24/2/16<br/>Issued with tin of tobacco, gift from Victorian Racing club.  Fatigue work practically all day.  Scratch concert arranged, <strike>at night</strike> which passed a very pleasant evening.</p>
<p>Friday 25/2/16<br/>Fell in on parade at 10 AM. in full marching order with blankets rolled &amp; kit bags.  Parade dismissed &amp; camp thoroughly cleaned up.<br/>All goods &amp; equipment loaded on to 20th A.S.C. waggons &amp; transported to railhead Serapeum West.  Issued with 24 hrs rations (1 tin</p>
