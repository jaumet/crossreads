Nov. 25th&nbsp;&nbsp;We went to Church in the morning &amp; for a walk in the afternoon. it was a fine day - &amp; some friends came in in the evening &amp; we had some music
&nbsp;
Nov. 26th&nbsp;&nbsp;I had made an appointment with the dentist for 9.15 A.M. which I kept. he did all the work necessary in 1&frac34; hours (filled seven teeth) &amp; made a very fine job of them for&nbsp;&pound;2.12.6. Mr&nbsp;&amp; Mrs&nbsp;S &amp; I went to the theatre in the evening &amp; saw &quot;To-night&#39;s the night&quot; (the second time I have seen it -&nbsp;
Nov. 27th&nbsp;&nbsp;I slept in this morning &nbsp;got up in time to have a good hot bath before lunch (which we had early) &amp; I caught 12.46 train to Paddington - Left my haversack at Waterloo &nbsp;&amp; went to tea in Oxford Street with &amp; friend &amp; went back to Kingston at 8.30 P.M.
&nbsp;
Nov 28th&nbsp;&nbsp;I went to Victoria &amp; booked a bed for to-night as I am going back to France to-morrow morning a day early as I wish to visit my Sister at her Unit. No 1. Canadian Gen. Hospital.
