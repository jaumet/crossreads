I see that beast Wall has been getting a lot more space in the papers than he deserves; the whole transaction was open &amp; above board &amp; I don't worry about it, only I don't see his object in telling deliberate untruths to make up a case. I think someone unknown must be behind him, although I have no enemies so far as I know of &ndash; I made out a fair valuation of the place &amp; offered it to the Red Cross at &pound;500 under that amount at which they closed.</p>
