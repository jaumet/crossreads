 April 5th Sheikh Zowaiid, Good Friday. Tonight we are at this place on our way to Khan Yunis a spot about 6 miles N.E. of Rafa.  Here we shall be in the front line with all its attendant discomforts & a certainty of taking a turn in the scrap at Gaza before long. News tonight came that a Taube bombed Romani the other day, our informant telling us that he came so low that "you could 'it 'im with a stick".
