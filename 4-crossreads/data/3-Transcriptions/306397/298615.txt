<p>French warships were in these waters and indeed in the twilight 2 French cruisers convoying 2 mail boats coming from Nagasiki were reported from the crows nest (look out) in which signal Lance Corporal Metzing was look out.  With some effort 2 ships and 2 torpedo boats could be distinguished in newtral waters. Signal Lance Corporal M. was afterwards promoted to 1st Sig Lance Corporal by the commander.</p>
<p>We took course for Japan for bluff and later altered it for Tsingtau. The spirit on board was splendid and in the mess our prize was celebrated with champagne.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5/8/14</p>
<p>The journey to Tsingtau is continued without anything unusual occurs. In the afternoon we get the wireless information of the declaration</p>
