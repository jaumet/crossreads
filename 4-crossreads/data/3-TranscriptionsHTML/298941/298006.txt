<p>recaptured  Bailleul It seems to be official for one officer told me, &amp; if it is, things are on the mend surely. The very light firing from the line seems to show that there has been a lull today. It freshened up at sunset, but  has now died down again. It is moonlight &amp; clear, but no German planes have come yet. Plenty of time I suppose.</p>
