<p>W.T.Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship - Berrima</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship - Warrego</p>
<p>Date - 11.9.14</p>
<p>Time of Despatch - [illegible, possibly 5.32pm]</p>
<p>Comdr to Brigadier</p>
<p>My information was previous to Comdr Beresford.</p>
<p>50 men with a flag of truce conducted by a captured German have proceeded to the WT station with the view&nbsp;for negotiation with the officers there for an unconditional surrender.</p>
<p>I am sending about 30 black prisoners&nbsp;&amp; 2 white under guard to Australia now.</p>
<p>Comdr Beresford proposes camping about three quarters of a mile inland from the pier awaiting result of negotiations @ 1715</p>
