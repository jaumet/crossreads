Of course the two-up kings have their little game thought the game is not played nearly so promiscuously amongst these chaps as is generally the rule with a crowd of our men. We seem to have a specially select group of men. It is a pleasure to talk to any of them. Their language of course continues to be rather livid but it is a habit which is hard to drop.</p>
