<p>There are 4 holds on the ship for the troops. There are <strike>Sixteen</strike> Twelve tables in each <strike>an</strike> set at same. The men sleep in hammocks, which are swinng from beams especially erected for same.<br/>It is a great sigh to see them all erected but it is a hard job to get the men out of them.</p>
