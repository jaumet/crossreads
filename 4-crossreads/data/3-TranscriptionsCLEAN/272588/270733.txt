
Friday 5 JulyOn Thursday morning the Boshe raided 10th Battn. but he came a gutzer.Very quiet all day & an Australian mail to-day. To be relieved to-morrow.  Glorious weather continues.
Saturday 6 JulyAbsolutely quiet all day & not a shot fired by Fritz.  Glorious day.An American Sergeant was attached to this post & he is good to talk to.The Australian ideas as to the American words of command were very amusing to him.They wereAust - Slope armsAmerican - Guys muskets on your shoulders put.Aust - Eyes right or leftAmerican - Guys look at 'emAust - AttentionAmerican - Guys stiffenAust - Stand at EaseAmerican - Guys SlackenHowever, the Sergeant exploded all these ideas & was much amused.Their words of command are similar to ours.
