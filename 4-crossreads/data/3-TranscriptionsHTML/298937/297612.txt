<p>Within a mile of the Cinema is the graveyard where many heroes of that battle of Tel El-Kebir (the site of which is about 3 miles away) which was fought when this place was just desert &amp; oases, <strike>by</strike> lie buried.<br/>There seems to be a great movement of troops.<br/>Train after train goes by every evening, with Australians, Tommies, guns, horses &amp; waggons.</p>
