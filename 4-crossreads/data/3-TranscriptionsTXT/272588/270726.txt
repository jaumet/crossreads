
Saturday 22 JuneMove up to A.D.S. at Borre. A stunt is coming off to-night or to-morrow night.Still fine weather.
Sunday 23 JuneGlorious weather & to-night a stunt is to come off to the left of Meteren as far as the 1st Aust. Division is concerned.  Some Americans, with full packs have gone past here to-day, up to the line.
