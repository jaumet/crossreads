  
 Of leave as well as witnessing the big riot of March we paraded for review in the Domain on April 8th 1915. After marching to the show ground we were at liberty to spend our last night at home prior to embarking. I shall never forget my farewell to those I love which took place late that night. Very early next morning we marched to Woolloomaloo whares and went abroad the troopship S.S. Nestor, a boat of 14.500 tons.
