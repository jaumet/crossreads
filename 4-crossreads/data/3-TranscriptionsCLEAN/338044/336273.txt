 18th September:- Monday A dirty day & raining all day (a truly unpleasant sort of rain). Hundreds of wounded coming down the line & all the hospitals are being cleared out to receive them. The numbers of wounded coming down & the subsequent reports of the newspaper, carry out the rumour we had last week of the contemplated new offensive at Flers  Ginchy, Thiepval & our success is now well known, having taken the 3rd German line of trenches.
