<p>I had a most dangerous piece of work to do with a party this morning it was cleaning out the communication trench for the Germans were still playing on it &amp; every here &amp; there it was blown right in &amp; traffic was getting dangerous, however we got it finished in about 3 hours all under heavy shell fire.</p>
