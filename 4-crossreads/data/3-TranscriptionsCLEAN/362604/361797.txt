  
 Sanvic Le Havre France 29.11.18 
 Dear Dad, Mum & George, 
 Ellis has all the letters that we have just received so I cannot answer them but I'll tell you such of the events of the week that I think will interest you.  Firstly, we received your X'mas Parcels, 1 each, & they have just come in nice & handy.  The cakes are lovely & have kept beautifully.  The other niceties are lovely also.  Ellis & Len Valentine have gone to the Theatre but I have been out in the wet all day & prefer to sit by the Fire.
