15/12/16We are in mud ankle deep here  Heard the shells dropping on Albert last night  we moved off from rest camp in motor lorries fore for Rebemont where our Batt is & joined them up.  What a change there is in  the Batt officers etc  makes one cry to see same.  I am in B Company now.transferred  no books nominal rolls etc  beautiful mess, things are in a muddle all over the place.
16/12/16Parade to day Brigade parade a band playing etc only band in sameBr. General gave us a talking to about Amiens & the evils thereof also about the good work about of the Brigade also about Officers & maps getting into the Germans hands turned out good job for a wonder.  went to concert last night in a village near by.  very good indeedmoney from Commonwealth Post 15/10/0
17/12/16We moved off the whole Brigade at 9 oclock this morning full packs up for our different village to billet until early in January 1917.  we arrived at                   our village at 4 oclock after an awful march in mud ankle deep in many places
