<p>this is only the second day since reaching here that it has been fine &amp; only at night when between our blankets at the billet have we had dry feet &amp; we are just slathered in mud &amp; it is impossible to keep clean when you are working amongst horses this weather. But everyone is fit &amp; there is hardly a case for the sick parade except sores and bruises.</p>
