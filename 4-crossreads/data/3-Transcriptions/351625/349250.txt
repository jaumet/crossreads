<p>Our tucker has not been too good lately, the infantry seems to get the worst tucker of any part of the Army, but we will be able to buy good feeds again soon &amp; get in good form again.  I have seen John Hosier frequently of late, he is in B Coy &amp; seems pretty fed up of the game but he is a good soldier.  It is just about time for me to nock off &amp; turn in &amp; make the most of a night's rest as I may not get</p>
