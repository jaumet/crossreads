<p><u>Saturday 23rd March '18.</u><br/>Yesterday I asked to be sent out; not from dislike of staying here, but from intense desire to get out into a little civilisation again before returning to the front. Today the doctor marked me for convalescent camp.<br/>Another little group of acquaintanceships gone west, as is the way of everything in this war. Walton, of course,</p>
