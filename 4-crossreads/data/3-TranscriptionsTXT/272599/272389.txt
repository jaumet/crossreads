 1917 Saturday 28 April 
 Duties during morning.  Inoculation T.R.B.  Result everyone has stiff arms &amp; sore. A.C. Johno &amp; self visit La Boiselle crater. &ndash; a huge gaping hole like an extinct volcano. "Tis hardly credible that it was caused by man's agency. fully 100 yards across by 200 ft deep.  Papers from Jan Mac. &amp; bundle of locals from Harry &ndash; Show doings   Good old read for rest of evening.
