<p>laughed &amp; called me a fool &amp; said it was only a fancy but he took them, &amp; promised to do as I wished should I go under, Jacks a fine chap &amp; a great cobber of mine, theres no doubt we have some splendid chaps in our little circle &amp; they would stick by one another till the last, mates in every sense of the word, as we drove away in the car I felt sorry in one way to be leaving them but all the same I was very glad to be leaving France &amp; the horrors of the battlefield for a while &amp; I could see a vision of old England in the distance, &amp; a nice spell which would do me the world of good after an hour or so in the car we pulled up at the 15th Clearing Station, here we were examined</p>
