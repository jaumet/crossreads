<p>June, 1915<br/>Tuesday 1<br/>Joes' letter of 3/5/15<br/>Days    "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;25/4/15<br/>Jack Harrison 2/5/15<br/>MOTHER 2/5/15<br/>All of which I will answer by letter as soon as possible.<br/>Very busy with much [indecipherable] work.<br/>Ran over a Nigger today in Brigade Car &amp; killed him they are like ants on the roads &amp; life not of much consequence apparently &ndash; still I was sorry, but they do not like the English.</p>
