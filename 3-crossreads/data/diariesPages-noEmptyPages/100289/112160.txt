Monday 7.8.16
Went on duty in motor to Estaires Bac St Maur, returning to Estaires where I spent a couple of hours before returning. Walked back, arriving at D.R.S. about 6 pm.
Tuesday 8.8.16
In evening walked a mile or so along a country lane in Doulieu to look over an old windmill; very interesting.
Wednesday 9.8.16
No variation in daily routine. Taking advantage of opportunities living very high; good news coming from all fronts; on the whole, in good spirits.
