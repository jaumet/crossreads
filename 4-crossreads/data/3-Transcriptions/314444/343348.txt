The barreness of the country is pitiable.<br/>We anchored in the suez harbour at about 11 am &amp; at 12.30 we entrained for Cairo. I was one of a guard party to prevent natives from coming near the ship. Was greatly amused with  them. The same scenes The same style of dress, the skirt. The native police look very smart.
