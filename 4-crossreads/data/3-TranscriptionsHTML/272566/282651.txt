<p>Sunday 23 September</p>
<p>To A.D.M.S. Reninghelst.<br/>Head Quarters later in the day moved to Dickebush so my next run was to this place via Westoutre La Clytte and return via Ouderdom &amp; Busseboom<br/>To 29th Battn.   L29 Sheet 27.<br/>More Bombs from Fritz &amp; shells into Poperinghe. </p>
