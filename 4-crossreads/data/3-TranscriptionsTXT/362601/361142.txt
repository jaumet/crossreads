 11 January, Saturday 
 F Ellis &amp; I go down to Base &amp; meet Roy. We do nothing until after Tea &amp; then change Breeches &amp; Hat. We all go to Pictures but Ellis does not see it all out. Meet the Q.M. who was once a Manlyite. We have good run back to Graville &amp; meet a very nice W.A.A.C. &amp; nearly get overcarried. We walk up the Hill. 
 12 January, Sunday 
 F Go down Town. Meet Bert Brian &amp; Horniball. We go &amp; have Photos taken &amp; then to YMCA Service. I go to Gare[Railway Station] with them &amp; have a French Roll Sandwich. Come Home &amp; write. Recd no letters. I am very emotional &amp; lonely &amp; tell MRL. Go. I work hard returning Gear. 
 Wrote M.R.L.
