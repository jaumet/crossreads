12 April 1916 WedAs usual coffee at 6.30 am. After breakfast Ken & self turn in together enjoying a good rest. Raining some to-day. Had a hot bath which was great. At 2 p.m. made our way to the estaminate having coffee half a dozen times with Mrs. Weillaert. These [text continued at top of page]
13 April 1916 Thur17th Battalion sustains their first causalities 2 wounded & one killed. Very cold to-day. Met Lieutenant Tims who told me of Jimmy & went around to our friends place & sipped coffee all day nearly. In the wards Marsh & I are looking after are mostly highlanders.
