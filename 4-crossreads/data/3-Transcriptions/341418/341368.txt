<p>my recitation of events. After writing the above I went over to this ridge  &amp;  there discovered that half an hour before I arrived the New Zealanders had come upon a camp of about 2-300 turks  &amp;  "shots were exchanged" but owing doubtless to the   <strike>sun being</strike>  dawn having already broken, the enemy had almost all got away. The aeroplanes of ours did some very pretty work as they were getting away diving   <strike>go</strike> down to about 200 feet, dropping bombs,  &amp;  as she rose again letting go with the machine gun in her tail. The whole scrap gave me the impression of a little comedy perfectly staged with us in the boxes</p>
