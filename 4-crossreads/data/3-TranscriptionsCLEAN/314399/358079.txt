  
 Sunday 23 March 1919 Cold on the improve. Had brekker in bed. Have a poisoned leg now. It is some sore too. Went in & did Rue de Paris picquet tho. It was icy cold & rained some. 
 Monday 24 March 1919 In bed all day with my leg. Read a couple of books. Very cold. Rained all day &   night & also snowed to some order.
