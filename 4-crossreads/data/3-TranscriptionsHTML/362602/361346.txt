<p>7th day out<br/>Have been orderly corporal to-day &amp; I have been buzzing round everywhere.  I have been writing to Bess &amp; Ida Nelson &amp; I will write a few lines to you before I go to bed.  We sighted land again to-day, the first time for 3 days &amp; we are now steaming along the coast.  We expect to be in port tomorrow so will drop you another line while I am ashore.  We are having a glorious time, but are very anxious to have a run ashore.  We enjoyed our Xmas dinner very much &amp; the nuts &amp; ginger best</p>
