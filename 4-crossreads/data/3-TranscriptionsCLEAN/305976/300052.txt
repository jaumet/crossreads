There are any amount of Souvenir's to be gathered but we have no means of carrying themFriday Sept 27Our present position is at Whalem a Sarcassian  village of white houses. The Sarcassians are undoubtedly favourable to the Turks and view us no friendly eyes. Yesterday they fired on small parties of out chaps. German machine Gunners at Amman  fought at their guns practically to the last man. Estimated number of prisoners is now 35000
