<p>Monday 30 April</p>
<p>Beautiful Day.<br/>On Incinerator.<br/>Notice posted up re Horse sports which are to take place next Saturday.<br/>Aust. Rules Football Meeting.<br/>Went to "Anzac Coves" concert in Albert at 7 p.m.<br/>Late this evening a flock of aeroplanes flew over in Fritzs direction possibly on a bombing expedition.  It was a pretty sight &ndash; the planes being detected only by the buzz of the engine and the moving lights in the sky.</p>
