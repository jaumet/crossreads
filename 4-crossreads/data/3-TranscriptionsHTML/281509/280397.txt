<p>23 June 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Dispensary again. Taken off all parades form henceforth. &amp; put on permanent staff.<br/>Nettleton &amp; Cartwright endeavour to get into Aerial Corps. prospects good.<br/>Talk of former patients of Harve hosp. 4,000 men. 200 officers. 4 parsons. Man &amp; his sister.<br/>Camp hospital started at Ditails Camp. Tony, Bob &amp; I chosen by Staff Smith as orderlies.</p>
