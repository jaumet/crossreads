 sight, &amp; everyhere &amp; there you would see the inevitable dead mule laying on the edge of the water or else floating close, there were mules killed nearly every night on the beach for Beachy played on it nearly all the time &amp; rations had to be got up somehow or other  we could see from this gale what kind of a job we were going to have when the real winter set in.
