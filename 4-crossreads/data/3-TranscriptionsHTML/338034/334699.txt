<p>&amp; on the Sinai Peninsula <strike>Syria</strike> Palestine frontier. After a sleepy night march, daylight on the 9th inst found us once again in fertile country, about 5 miles South of Rafa &amp; two miles from the Turkish entrenchments. our aeroplanes arriving soon after daylight reconnoitred all the surrounding country &amp; soon after their reports being received by the Commanding General we <strike>commenced to</strike> deployed and encircled the Turkish positions which <strike>were</strike> commenced about 1 &frac12; miles in from the coast.</p>
