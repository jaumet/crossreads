<p>Friday Mch. 9th<br/>Nothing much doing today.<br/>Weather still cold.  Snow falling slightly.</p>
<p>Saturday Mch. 10th<br/>Went to Trenches this afternoon &amp; made arrangements to go down tomorrow to line.
