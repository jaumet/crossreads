<p>March 2/3/16  Thursday<br><br />
Bacon for breakfast, sure indication of route march.  Morning. Route march across the old field, about 3 miles, to the old trenches used in 1882.  Human remains lying about on the ground in proximity to the trenches.  Pte A. Fernandez, Batman, being medically unfit, left camp to return to Australia.</p>
