
3 January, Friday
F & WMy day off. I go down to flat via long route. Have afternoon tea. Go to Nice Harrais & back to YMCA for Tea. Am bossed about & made to sit where I don't want to sit. Meet all the Girls. Ellis & S/S Lilley come down & we watch rotten concert.
The "Ceramic" is in the Harbour. Receive letters from Lottie, Home & P.C's from Os Merritt & Lily. Monkey has not left yet. Supper & bed early.
4 January, Saturday
WRecd letter from M.R.L. Go to YMCA alone & see good concert by Crocks. Kissing, Les Holmes old song is a reminder of old days. Come Home tout[?] Suite & write. Monkey goes away.
Wrote Dad & M.R.L.
