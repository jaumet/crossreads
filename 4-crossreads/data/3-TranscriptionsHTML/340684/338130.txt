<p>441<br/>5<br/>have been mental for several years of my life &ndash; I seem to be a different being altogether &ndash; I don't know why it is I looks so young &ndash; some old ladies the other day seriously thought I was under twenty andthought  it  was a pity such a yong boy should be sent to the war &ndash; it was probably my pyjamas that did it</p>
<p>I hope you are abale to read this through &ndash; your'e going to get one once a week whether you read it or not I am writing to the Mater this week because I think by her</p>
