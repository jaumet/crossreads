<p>getting rid of the waster. Have been doing musketry all the afternoon all the N.C.O.s. are on it, but it is only a farce for the Fragments will pay no attention whatsoever.<br/>Yesterday afternoon we were not on parade (NCOs) for there was not a single private left in the lines they were all at the range.<br/>12th. Had a rotten time today we have been doing musketry all the time, &amp; its dead slow<br/>Fairly hot &amp; moist but much cooler tonight<br/>13th. Had to go through the gas test this morning, it seems the height of nonsense don't it putting the NCOs from overseas through the gas chamber, after us living</p>
