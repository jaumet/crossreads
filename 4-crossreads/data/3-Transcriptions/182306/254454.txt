<p>she kept circling round us till we got nearly to Eng. Where we were met by the mosquito fleet. (Torpedo boats) She looked just like <s>f</s> Life boats beside us. We experienced Some very cold weather before ariving Devonport proved a very welcome sight after being on the water so Long. Landed on the 30th Jan Taken ashore on lighters to the wharf and caught train for Amesbury. Marched from there to our camp about seven mile</p>
