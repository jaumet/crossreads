<p>December  1918.<br/>Tuesday 17</p>
<p>At Sea. H.S."Karoola."<br/>Weather fine and clear averaged 14.2 miles and for 24 hours had travelled 335 miles.<br/>Played Auction Bridge and one.<br/>Met Bill Hart and Don Cameron.<br/>Passed through the Straights of Gibraltar at 8 pm.<br/>The town and rock were a blaze of light as also was Algiers.<br/>Wrote to Ken Gunn. Climbed up to the yard arm of the mainmast.</p>
