<p>February 1918<br/>Friday 15</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Had the 4 to 6 shift on the phone and this morning.<br/>Walked to Spy Farm and met old Les Fuller and Duncan.<br/>Had tea with Les and proceeded on to Daylight Corner, saw Thompson and James. Had a pow pow. Believe that Ike Steinthal is in the 10th Field Coy, also some of the others.<br/>Weather wet, altho a little better today.<br/>Fritz shelled for a couple of hours. Garford and Mills on L'aison.</p>
