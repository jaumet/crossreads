<p>29th.  Went down the city early yesterday evening mainly to collect my change from "Moons" before they closed, they recognized me as soon as I set foot inside the door &amp; brought my change right away.  After leaving here I went along to "Genom's" for dinner, had my usual 3 courses, a good thick soup, such as oxtail, oyster or, tomato, fish &amp; the usual trimmings, a sweet, bread &amp; butter &amp; coffee.  This generally fixes me up &amp; costs between 5 &amp; 6/- according to the quality of the fish you order.  This is of course a flash restaurant &amp; it can be got cheaper at other places, but I would rather pay a little more &amp; have it well cooked &amp; nicely served than go to the cheaper places where it is plentiful &amp; coarse.<br/>After having dinner I took the tram to Devonport &amp; there met my friend &amp; back we went to Plymouth for we were going to the big concert at the Guildhall.
