
29th FridayWeather still good.  Heavy Gun fire on both flanks also some battery work on & about our front.  Turks began fierce Bomb throwing again 4-30 to 5 p.m., remainder of night very quiet.
30th SaturdayWeather good & bright.  Bread & meat been off since 27th.  Gale.
31st SundayWeather continues good.  Bread & Meat issued.  Front quiet.  Used Japs Mortar 9-30 p.m. to quieten Turks with their Broom Stick Bombs playing on Pope's.  Out for same reason again 1-30 a.m.
