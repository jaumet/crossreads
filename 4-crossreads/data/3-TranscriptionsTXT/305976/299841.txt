 Wednesday March 7th Date of sailing indefinitely postponed. No reason given, seems to be some danger. Troops from all states banking up. When will we leave this hole with its bread &amp; jam (Plum) and stew. Thursday March 8th Usual drill all hands fed up at turn of events. Friday March 9th Our weekly route march this week to rifle butts then cut across country striking Goulbourn river &amp; returning home by Trawool Rd distance appro  20 miles. Saturday March 10th On duty week end.  &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; .
