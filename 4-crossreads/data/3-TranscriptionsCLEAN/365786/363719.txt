  
  The Pyramids.  They face the cardinal points.  This huge mass of stone covers an area of nearly 13 acres.  It is constructed of a yellowish limestone quarried in the vicinity.  The space on top is about 12 yards square.  The pyramids face the cardinal points. Kephren looks higher but is a few feet lower & about 100 feet less at the base.  It was covered with limestone slabs & partially polished granite. Mykerinos is only 204 ft. high & its sides about 350 ft. long.  Near is the Sphinx hewn out of the natural rock augmented by blocks of stone.
