<p>Proceeding up the channel of the river which is marked by buoys we passed several foundries &amp; ship building yards.  At one of the latter were 12 trawlers on the slips specially built with solid steel stems hardened for ramming submarines.  Behind the breakwater but out of sight is an aerodrome with 12 aeroplanes which make reconnaissances every night.  There are also 8 submarines here for defence work.  Our North Sea pilot told us that we have captured about 40 u boats up to date.
