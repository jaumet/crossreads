<p>LYNDE<br/>Friday 3<br/>A long wet weary walk to the market town of "Lumbres" which is a nice place, pretty girls in pretty robes, make you wish<br/>Saturday 4<br/>A heavy bombardment is now raging, telling the hun that we are stronger than 4.8.14<br/>Sunday 5<br/>It is a hard life but somehow or another you get to like it for it is so changeable<br/>Two years a soldier and two years since I gave the girl a ring 2 years of queer times</p>
