
Friday 18 OctoberIn London in the thickest fog I have yet been in.  Shopping etc. all day.  Dinner at troc. & to the "Maid of the Mountains" in the evening. Return to France to-morrow.
Saturday 19 OctoberLeave Victoria St. at 9 a.m. & cross to Calais from Dover.  Crossing fine.Now at rest camp at Calais.
