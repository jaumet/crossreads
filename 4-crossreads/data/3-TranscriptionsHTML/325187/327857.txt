<p>[Easter Day 23 April 1916]</p>
<p>CAIRO<br/><strike>Rused</strike> Rushed up here from Assuit got some clothes; leave again 11 o'clock Express  Sitting in train was a man I last met in Portugese East Africa at <strike>Beira</strike> BEIRA.<br/>Had only a few hours to get a few nessesaries and in my hurry left Watch in lavatory-<br/>You'll think I'm losing my head, Belt at Lemnos &amp; now Watch here.<br/>I may get watch but the Native servants steal anything <strike>of</strike> from "<u>FOOL SAHIBS</u>"</p>
