Harris could not get away tonight, but Jessie, Hills &amp; High met me at the camp at 7.30 (after lamp parade) in a car, we went to the National for dinner. then went for a spin to the pyramids &amp; Maadi they then came back &amp; dropped me at Zeitoun at 11.30, before going to the 14th (no pass)</p>
<p>10-3-17<br/>Sat 10th<br/>Had a Theory test this morning I hurried through it &amp; got ready to go out but could not get a pass till 2 oclock.
