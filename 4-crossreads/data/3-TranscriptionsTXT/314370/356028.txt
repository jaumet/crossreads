 May, 1916 Wednesday 17 In the early morning parade we practiced standard tests. After breakfast the N.C.Os had their written Exam &amp; the Instructors corrected &amp; marked their books. A number of "Stokes Trench Howitzers" arrive Night Operations Temp&deg; 
 Thursday 18 Put in all day at stoppages &amp; Standard tests. Weather much cooler only 99&deg; Am now going to the usual concert.
