  
 Sunday 23 December 
 Frost and snow - a continuation of what we have been having for some days past.  Sunny & pleasant. 3 trips to Samer, visiting A.D.M.S. Sigs. 5th Div. Workshops, A.P.M., Canteen etc. Another run (Joyride) to the place we were at last night.  French Officers here from Calais on a shooting expedition.  Saw the Deer being skinned & Dressed
