 February 1918 Sunday 3 
 On the road Reveille at 6.30 am no parade until Boot and Saddle. Left at 11am. passed through Hazebrouck at 12.30 Arrived at billets, three kilos from Bailleul, at 5.30pm Am feeling in very high spirits, received letter from Mrs. Adam. Warned off as horseholder for party going to the guns tomorrow morning Had a feed of eggs and chips at  a little Estaminet facing the billet.
