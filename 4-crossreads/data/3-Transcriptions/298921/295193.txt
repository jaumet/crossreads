<p>August, 1915<br/>Tuesday 10<br/>Not far from Alexandria beautiful weather to be killing men &ndash; The Turks fought fair but shelled us going out to Hospital ships, probably could not tell we were only for the meat ships, they hit 2 with Shrapnel.<br/>Out of the Boat into Hospital Train.<br/>No.1 Australian General Hospital Heliopolis the same place I used to visit the sick ere I was one &ndash;<br/>SIKHS attended us on the Train &amp; nurses were very good &ndash;<br/>A good many died coming over.</p>
