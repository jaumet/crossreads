<p>Tuesday 21 August 1917<br/>My birthday.</p>
<p>Aug. 20th 1918<br/>Town 4.30 p.m. in Heliopolis tram met Mr. Laing &amp; his American friend &amp; I hurt her I fear by referring to the Yanks in the war!<br/>Head washed &amp; 1st treatment.<br/>Hole in bellows of camera, spoil 6 photos.</p>
