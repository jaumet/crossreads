<p>Had another bath, to make sure I was quite clean. Went over to T. B in evening &amp; saw a decent concert at Greenhills Y.M.</p>
<p>Sunday 21st<br/>Went on Church parade the first time for a very long time Allways seem to miss it without trying too. A very good little address Took a walk around this evening through Longbridge Deneril. Just a wee place near here</p>
<p>Monday 22<br/>Went through the Platoon in attack</p>
