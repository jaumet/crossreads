 Thursday 25/1/17 have put in an application for leave to Paris &ndash; work finished. 
 Friday 26/1/17 
 Leave for Paris for 7 days approved &ndash; General report on Training. 
 Saturday 27/1/17 
 Men marched out at 10 am. I remain &amp; had Luncheon with M Domart who drove me almost to Amiens. Arrived Paris 8.30 pm had dinner &amp; went to Follies Bergere.
