<p>Sunken or fly in Col. Trench<br/>Dig trench 9" wide 18" deep at mouth  18" inside  slope 4" deep at chimney end. Lay across strips of tin or sods to support pug place on camp Kettles &amp; pug all over  Trench in front to work in.  By making splay mouth for trenches &amp; placing piece of tin across made good hot plate or frying tin</p>
