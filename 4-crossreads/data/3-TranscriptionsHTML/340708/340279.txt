<p>August 1917<br/>Wednesday 22nd<br/>Bad weather as set in again so things are very quiet<br/>Thursday 23rd<br/>Weather is still bad Fritz paid us a visit this evening<br/>Friday 24th<br/>Put in the Hospital today   [indecipherable] a least the place they call a hospital it is not fit to house a good dog much less men who are crook   [indecipherable]   have been risking there lives for months the British army is the rottenest organization in the world<br/>Saturday 25th<br/>Still having a crook time will most likely have to be operated on<br/>Sunday 26th<br/>It as been raining &amp; blowing a gale today there was to be a big offensive tomorra  this will just about knocked in the head</p>
