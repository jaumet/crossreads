<p>It flew absolutely directly towards us, &amp; must have seen our huts &amp; tents with the long line of waiting motors. We held breath &amp; waited for the bombs we felt sure would be ours for no asking, but he flew straight over. He may have seen that it was a dressing station. A moment later he dropped five bombs to the right &amp; behind us, apparently on the main St Quentin road</p>
