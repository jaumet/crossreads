<p>crops, chiefly barley &amp; wheat are now only a few inches above ground. We met a tribe of Bedouins displaying the flag of truce, &amp; marching in to give themselves up to us, as per instructions sent out to the various tribes by the military authorities.<br/>Jan 1st '17 :- Two Taubes bombed the artillery &amp; Infantry camps here today, however they did poor work, killing only 1 engineer &amp; wounding 4 natives, also a couple of horses.<br/>Jan 7th '17. Two taubes bombed the various camps here today, they killed about 25 niggers</p>
