 MONDAY, APRIL 28, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; warm. Went round the 
 bottom end in morning, and 
 round the Hill &amp; Ben Ledi with 
 Jo in the afternoon. Jo &amp; Peter &amp; I 
 went out in the car after dinner 
 shooting rabbits ect in the 
 head lights. We got a few hare&nbsp; 
 &amp; 4 rabbits.
