 Tuesday 25 June 1918 Total Hours flown, Over lines &ndash; 305 h. 17 m. Tests, etc. &ndash; 11 h. 35 m. Total &ndash; 316 h. 52 m. 
 Wednesday 26 June 1918 H.A.P.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 2 h. 50 m., no excitement. Murphy &amp; Farquhar scrap a Hun over aerodrome.  Hun escaped but was on the point of landing.  Farquhar killed.  A very fine chap and a good sport.
