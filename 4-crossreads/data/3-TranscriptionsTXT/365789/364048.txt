Eventually I ascertained Fred was last seen bandaging one of wounded men named Burns & this was verified when they found him, for he was in the act of bandaging Burns when he was, poor fellow, shot through the head & fell forward.  Death having been instantaneous so that he did not suffer any pain, & he was found in that position when we made this last advance (March 1917)
