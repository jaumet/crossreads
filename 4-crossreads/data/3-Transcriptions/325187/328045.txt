<p>[Date 28th has been crossed out &amp; replaced by 29th October and from here all dates have been altered and the diary is a 1917 diary with date changed to 1916]</p>
<p>[Sunday 29 October 1916]</p>
<p>Went Church with Dolly   Laura <strike>&amp; self</strike> came to dinner<br/>Posted photos to both families of wee wife &amp; self.<br/>Returning to AIF office continue vote recording NOs seem to have it.<br/>Rang up Sir Geo Reid &amp; made an appointment for Tuesday afternoon at House of Commons.<br/>Thank you for goggles etc &amp; also Mays parcel which no doubt are in Egypt waiting for me.<br/>Can only find re Sergt Walter Hood is that he is well &amp; has not been injured or sick- he is not in England-<br/>How much LSD have I in Deniliquin Savings Bank please</p>
