<p>Going away, as I said good bye &ndash; "Spirits all right Keith?" He beamed &amp; made a delightful upward spirit movement with his fore finger &ndash; cannot you see him? &amp; does your mind flash now &ndash; as mine did at the moment &ndash; to that particular Keith, with hands clasped round his knees, his head on one side, &amp; &ndash; will Mrs Ferguson ever forgive me! &ndash; a look of [indecipherable] humour &amp; spectachure wisdom this can only be described as Kook a burr a ish!</p>
<p>This, I hope, will find you in lovely Tasmania &ndash; may you spiritual  hump was full &amp; fat with beautiful memories to nourish you across the desert spaces of the year.</p>
