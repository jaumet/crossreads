
25:XI:16Poor old Jack died on the 10th of this month.In October he collapsed at Boulogne through ear trouble & after four operations at the Queen Alexandra Hosp. Malo-les-Bains, he developed pneumonia & bronchitis,The cause of death was septic pneumonia.Went to London for three days (14th to 17th) & had a few hours with Dad & Lily.Everything here is as it was before.
