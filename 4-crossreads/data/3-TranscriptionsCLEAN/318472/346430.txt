
24 July 1918WednesdayProceeding well - so far no further mishap. This afternoon after wishing us luck our Escort left us. Sea rough all day with heavy wind & rain squals  A good many of the boys sea. sick. We appear to be off the usual track as we have only sighted one boat since leaving & that a Destroyer.
1918 July 25ThursdayNothing unusual happening. The weather has cleared up considerably the sea is much more smooth. Reported by Wireless that Submarines have been sighted about a days sail ahead. A Large ships passed close about 10.30 P.M.
