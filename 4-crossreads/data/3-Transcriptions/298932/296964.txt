<p>Zillebeke<br/>[OCTOBER &ndash; NOVEMBER]<br/>Monday 29<br/>Bombs falling all around us was bad enough but when a big shell lifted us out of our beds, 'Oh it was topping<br/>Tuesday 30<br/>Buggar the army and buggar the war, that is the spirit of the British Army right along the battle front<br/>Wednesday 31<br/>Got mixed up with men from the U.S.A. and of course I am guessing and kalkerlating all kinds of things<br/>NOVEMBER Thursday 1st<br/>A bomb fell outside my tent, made a big row, woke me up and made a seive of my tent "Lucky Jack"</p>
