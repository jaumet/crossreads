<p>I &amp; another fellow who shares the room with me sit outside the estaminet with the Frenchie &amp; wife &amp; their family. At the first warning the wife usually takes here her kiddies below into the beer cellar</p>
<p>Punctually enough the last 4 nights at early dark about 9.30 p.m. we have heard their engines and then our searchlights have lit up and the Archies &amp; quick firing guns started and then <strike>one hear</strike> after a few minutes one <strike>hears the</strike> sees the flash &amp; hears the crash of the bomb.</p>
