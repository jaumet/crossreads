13.4.17  FridayFatigues again.  Plenty of sick coming through, can hardly expect anything else considering the hard work the boys have been through lately.
Saturday 14.3.17 [correct transcription of date]Reveille 6.15 Am.  Route march 6.45 AM. A bit of a strain considering we have only been out of the line a few days.  Few of the boys on leave to Amiens overstepped the mark & were put in the "boob" by the Military Police.
