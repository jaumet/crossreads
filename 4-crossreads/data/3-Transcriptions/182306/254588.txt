
<p>March 12th</p>
<p>Delta arived in the harbour about - 6-30. and we were sent round to Quarentine waters Just of Manly But we all refused to go ashore &amp; stayed on board for three days then decided to let the Queenslanders get a move on so finished up with 7 days at the station.
