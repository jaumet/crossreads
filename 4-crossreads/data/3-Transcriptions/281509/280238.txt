<p>15 January 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Morning shift. Pay morning &amp;pound 3.15.</p>
<p>Prepare weekend leave.  Leave 11.30 am train.  Crowded train 20 in box carriage  3 on rack  2 in lavatory. Civilian broke window getting through at Fairfield.  Sanitary paper streamers.  <br/>Dinner on pot at Marine Caf&eacute; Gardens &amp; sleep. Meet Mary at Quay go to Central tea.  see Janet &amp; [indecipherable] off by Nowra train  Essie Mr Lang.  Meet Jack, Ethel Evelyn &amp; Essie.  Say goodbye to the former. Home to Ashfield. Take Ess to pictures, stay night &ndash; Bed 11 pm.</p>
