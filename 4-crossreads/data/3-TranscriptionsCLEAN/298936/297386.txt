  
 July 1915 
  Saturday 31  
 the men along the wharf officers powerless to quieten men.  Mutiny the order of the day. 
 Two casualties brought to me for first aid. 
 Gillam pierced in lung & butted in stomach. 
 Somerville fell in water but rescued. 
  Saturday  
 Men refused to fall in 
 [indecipherable], minister for defence arrived & gave a speech to men. I went to Base Hosp. with sick men & on return after sending wires to Rita & Grafton
