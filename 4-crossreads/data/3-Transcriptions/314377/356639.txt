<p>One day Sister came to me saying I could go down for my Kit. This meant I was transferred to another hospital which was Southall Middlesex.</p>
<p>This place was run entirely by Australian M.O. Sisters and wardsmen &ndash; all Australians. A Mrs. Macpherson was in charge of the Canteen arrangements She was very kindly disposed amongst the soldiers</p>
<p>To go back. Leaving Woolwich I went on to a Motor to Lewisham Station The M.O. of the ward had sent me to walk on crutches. It was particularly an impossibility for me to do so as I was not at all sure of myself in my opinion</p>
<p>It was quite a long order</p>
