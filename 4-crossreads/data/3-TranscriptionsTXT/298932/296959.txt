
KIT AND KATFriday 12Shifting out of Ypres left by train got off at Abelle & marched to the frontier of France & Belgium, resting at a farmSaturday 13Drinking the blue's away "Vin Rouge Vin Blange Beer ect. Had a wash & shave the first for ten day'sSunday 14Still drinking. Old Nutty is a complete wreck. All the boys are "fou the nou but it is a case of drink and forget The sun is shining and life is a good deal better.
