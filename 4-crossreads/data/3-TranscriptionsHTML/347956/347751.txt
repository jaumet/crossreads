<p>Aug &amp; Sep 31st &ndash; 4th</p>
<p>Mon 31st<br/>Was sent to Randwick Race Course for a load of boots etc<br/>Aunt Bessie came to Rosebery afternoon,  on picket duty night.</p>
<p>Tue 1st Sep.<br/>Was vaccinated morning &amp; foot drill  Stan &amp; I went to the Tivoli evening</p>
<p>Wed 2nd<br/>Foot drill, received leggings, boots pants etc.  Stan &amp; I went to the Pens for tea &amp; met Aunt Agnes, Beat &amp; Olive &amp; Mrs Griffiths saw Jessie to Moorcliff.  got to camp at 12.30 midnight</p>
<p>Thurs 3rd<br/>Fatigue duty morning.  received a pass to see dentist afternoon &amp; went to Manly instead, had tea at railway with Beatrice &amp; Olive Griffiths, Criterion theatre &amp; met Mrs McLaren &amp; Vera Griffiths there.</p>
<p>Fri 4th<br/>Foot drill morning, starting with 2 laps around the track at the double before breakfast as an appetiser, I decided not to go to town at night, as I was too tired, was hauled out for fatigue work at 6.30 pm &amp; was kept at it constant till after midnight unloading saddles, will never stop in again [indecipherable] bed early, as I was last in.</p>
