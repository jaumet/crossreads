
3.4.15who got wet also stripped;  & it ended up with 15 or 20 in the water by the time we had finished;  we got the whole of the horses back in 15 minutes, & finished up by one of the lads pushing two of the natives off the boat into the water.  We had the afternoon off & had a look at the Weir & Bridges, also a walk through the gardens, & finished up by a look through the Museum which is full of engineering models.  We heard a rumor to-day of a riot in Cairo last night;  in which 3 Aus. were killed by the Red Caps who fired on them.  To bed early.
