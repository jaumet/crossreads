<p>8 February 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>After breakfast Bob &amp; self drive down to Appletree.  Find all O.K. stay to dinner. Back home at  4 pm call en route at Alec's, Violet Bank &amp; Orange Grove.<br/>Mary very sick with bad knee.  Mary Jane coming out from Nowra to look after them.<br/>We go to Robson's after tea &amp; tea &amp; talk fill in the evening.</p>
