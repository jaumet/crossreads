 In these times of war, when 'tis sought to transform the ordinary civilian who has no special inclination urging him to be a soldier, what is essential to success? 
 Much more continuous and harder work on the part of both the instructor and the instructed, than is necessary during peace. 
 Every moment in the day while sleep is not essential should be devoted to the business.
