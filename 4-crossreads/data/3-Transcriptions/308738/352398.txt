<p>Feb. 23 Tuesday 1915<br/>Major Fuller out at Rifle Butts shooting practice.<br/>Col Cox eye much better.</p>
<p>Feb. 24 Wednesday 1915<br/>Major Fuller at Rifle Butts all day<br/>Went out at 10 AM for Rifle Practice my shooting was only passable very erratic at times.</p>
<p>Feb. 25 Thursday 1915<br/>Major Fuller at Rifle Butts all day.<br/>Meeting of Canteen Committee 4. 30 PM to arrange advance in price of certain articles.<br/>We are likely to leave in about 2 weeks Disinfected tent.<br/>Concert in Recreation tent given by the Maadi Residents &amp; others.</p>
<p>Feb. 26 Friday 1915<br/>Major Fuller &amp; Col Cox out to Dinner tonight.   I completed musketry course this morning. The whole Brigade on Parade today</p>
