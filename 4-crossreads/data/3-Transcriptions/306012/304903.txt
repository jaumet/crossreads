<p>Bordeaux has gone from 262,000 to 325,000 &amp; neighbouring districts from 89,000 to 121,000.<br/>The pop. of Nantes was 171,000 and now it is 20,000 more not including troops in the town.  Included in the 20,000 are 10,000 refugees from invaded districts &amp; munition workers.<br/>Toulouse had 150,000 in 1914.  Now there are 210,000 as well as 10,000 refugees &amp; foreign workmen.<br/>Nice has now 180,000 (not including numerous foreigners).  Commencement of the war the total was 168,000.<br/>Le Havre has a pop. of 160,000 not including the garrison, and 30,000 foreigners (mostly British).  There are 80,000 refugees in this town which by the way is the Australian Base Depot in France.<br/>Toulon has 120,000, Bourges 110,000, Poitiers 51,000, Brest 100,000, Dijon 90,000,</p>
