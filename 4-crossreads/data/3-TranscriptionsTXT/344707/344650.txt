 I shall return to Australia when the War ends &ndash; No word of going back yet. Collected 214 Bedouins today. They live in caves, Donkeys &amp; all together, &amp; are very poor &ndash; See Diary March 19th I do love to get home news &amp; to hear All's well &ndash; Can't understand my cable not reaching you I sent it Dec 15th &ndash; 20th Move postponed owing to Jordan being in flood anyway I am staying here &ndash; with details &ndash; the regiment will be away some days &ndash; Still wet &ndash; Rain saved the men a lot during the last fortnight.
