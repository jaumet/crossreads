
11 October 1916 WedDid not get to bed till 4.30 am this morning. Slept till dinner time. Just outside laying on stretchers are three dead men. Poor fellows they are just going to bury them. [indecipherable] Went broke [indecipherable]
12 October 1916 ThurHad a game of two-up & did my wages in less than five minutes. Terrific bombardment followed by a raiding party of 7th Brigade gpomg over 2 prisoners wounded Germans. Found the German lines empty.
