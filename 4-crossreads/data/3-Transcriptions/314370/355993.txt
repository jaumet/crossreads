<p>March, 1916<br/>Wednesday 8<br/>Morning work as per usual.  Afternoon half holiday.<br/>I put in a couple of hours extra with a couple of new hands<br/>Have tea w/- dad play cards</p>
<p>Thursday 9<br/>Men are showing a bit of improvement in the work.<br/>We had a rather easy day two long lectures taking up about 3 &frac12; hours.<br/>Tonight I made another little table.</p>
