 Monday 20/3/16 Again inoculated &amp; relieved from piquet 12 noon.  Walked out about 1 mile during dinner time &amp; spent a very interesting half hour watching the Artillery in action. First experience of artillery fire; firing live shells; their shooting appeared to be very accurate.  Off duty during afternoon. 
 Tuesday 21/3/16 Had an unexpected visit from Allan Rowling during dinner hour.  Very pleased to see him &amp; had quite a pleasant yarn with him. Issued with helmets at afternoon parade; spent remainder of afternoon
