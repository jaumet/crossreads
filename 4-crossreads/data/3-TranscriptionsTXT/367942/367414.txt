
3rd Dec.Round the Cape & proceeding about due West6th Dec.Met the "Igotz - Mendi" again & gave her some stores7th Dec.Spaniard left us going to Westrd "Wolf" steering about W.N.W. average speed 9 knots, evidently making for Trinidad14th Dec.Sighted Barque just before sundown & followed her all night.15th Dec.5 A.M. Captured French Barque "Marshall Daveout" of Nantes, from Geelong to Dakar with Cargo of wheat. Wireless & 2 3in guns. Took off crew & provisions. 1 pm sunk her & proceeded.16th Dec.Seaplane assembled again after recovering & repairs made flight at 5 pm.
