<p>Thiloy, cleaned horses of a little mud.</p>
<p>29th Pulled out for spell. Stayed two days near Albert, then had sudden order to go into action at Ervillers between Baupaume &amp; Arrass. The guns were pulled into St Leger &amp; we were there till the big stunt came successfull,  then went forward a few miles. The weather very cold,  keeps snowing hard, being near the old ruins of Urvillers we have plenty of fire wood which is  needed.
