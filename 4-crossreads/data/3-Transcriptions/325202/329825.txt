<p>We all prefer the Belgium country people to the French they seem much more hospitable &amp; ready to "put themselves about" in doing little things for us although they are just as keen over the "bawbees". More enterprising in fact every little cottage seems to be a small store.</p>
<p>No leave to England yet The officers, Batmen, clerks &amp; a <strike>fair</strike> good number of NCO's have been but the poor Driver who is bearing the brunt of the hard work in this Corps has had none. It is a damned scandal many of us with over</p>
