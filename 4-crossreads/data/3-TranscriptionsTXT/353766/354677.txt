
Captured small NDL steamer "Sumatra". Quite thrilling. Hodgkinson - boarding officer. Various other small craft takenSept. 13 Coaled & oil.Sunday. Landed 3 p.m. for annexation & hoisting of colours. Never expected to see anything like this. Wonderful.
