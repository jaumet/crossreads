<p>rank of corporal confirmed from the 1st June &amp; now on the 25th July they make me a Lance Sgt which is another stripe backshish, no earthly good to us here except it looks well on ones coat.<br/>I attended a convivial evening in the A.I.F. canteen the other night given by the canteen Sgt. who is a bit of a celebrity in his way, claiming as he does, to be the only man in the AIF. who has achieved the distinction</p>
