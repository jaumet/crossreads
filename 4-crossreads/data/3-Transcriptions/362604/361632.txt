<p>Bulford<br/>24.3.18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>I have received your letter of 13.1.18.  It has been a frightful hot day &amp; I went out for a long walk.  I returned Home late &amp; Ellis, Dick Toppin our mate or I have had no tea yet.  But you just wait we'll make it up when one of my Orderlies returns.  We walked to Amesbury &amp; all around the river banks.  We could only get tea &amp; war cakes.
