 SATURDAY, AUGUST 16, 1919. 
 Fine. Car met us in Quirindi 
 &amp; we got home for breakfast. 
 I was round bottom end all 
 day. Tickle got a big white 
 goat in cultivation while I was 
 away. Doolan &amp; Wardell started 
 trapping in Wheelers &amp; got about 
 300 in two nights.
