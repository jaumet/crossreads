<p>Saturday 23/2/18&nbsp; Meeting of CO&#39;s at Bde.HQrs. Robinson went on leave- went for a ride</p>
<p>Sunday 24/2/18. Church parade, went for a long ride with Lloyd</p>
<p>Monday 25/2/18. Went to Boulogne to play football- match off- returned at 9.45 p.m.</p>
<p>Tuesday 26/2/18. Route March 15 miles walked all the way. wrote to Doff.</p>
<p>Wednesday 27/2/18&nbsp; Div Boxing Competitions&nbsp; stadium in this area&nbsp; I judged.</p>
<p>Thursday 28/2/18&nbsp; Final of boxing- 26<sup>th</sup> won by two firsts &amp; one second&nbsp; rained in afternoon.</p>
