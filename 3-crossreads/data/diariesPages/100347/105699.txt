Monday May 12th at Sea
up anchor away about noon to the strains of our band : foggy misty rain so couldn't get a photo of anything : struck a decent roll on : slept on deck it was nice : 
Tuesday May 13th at Sea
decent roll on plenty of the lads were sick : I felt a bit squeamish but didn't miss any meals managed to keep them all down : seen a couple of small whales :
Wednesday May 14th at Sea
usual routine : calmer sea so we settled ourselves down to things : good library opened
