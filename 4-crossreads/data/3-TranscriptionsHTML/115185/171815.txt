<p>28 - Jeanne &ndash; Neville Rich sent them a German shell picked up on battlefield. They are devoted to him. He's signalling now they say.<br/>I also saw Aunt Jessie &amp; Uncle Charles en route to Harrowgate from Portishead &amp; Gibson McMillan &amp; Cecily. Denis is a dear &amp; brought Kitty Maxwell to see me at the [indecipherable] flat. I've promised to go to Mrs Maxwell's to see the baby if I have time. But the getting about is the trouble.<br/>B.</p>
