
Things have cleared up & it is a glorious & sunny morning like a Spring morning in Tasmania not a cloud in the sky & birds singing everywhere, you would scarce know that a war was on save for the incessant & never ending growl & rumble of the guns in the distance, & the stream of traffic on the roads & the steady hum of the planes overhead, everything else seems so peaceful calm & quiet. I got the full strength of the raid the Germans made on the 10th Battn. a few nights back, it appears that the 13th Battn. knew it was coming off but they did not know when, & they had just been relieved when over Fritz came, 9 officers & 200 strong
