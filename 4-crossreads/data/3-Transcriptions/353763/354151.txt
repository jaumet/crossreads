<p>evidently Native police &amp; looked very  important &ndash; rifles &amp; caps &amp; uniform but the vast crowd of native<strike>s</strike> helpers showed that their allegiance was duly secured &amp; if reports are correct the change from German to British rule is greatly to their advantage  -  A white officer marched with the rear guard &amp; then we all followed to what was evidently Head quarters &amp; then the Show was over  -  The Tommies were marched back to the troop ship where the naval men had already gone &amp; then we saw the niggers lined up  -  They were evidently harangued for presently we heard them cheering tremendously &amp; then they were dismissed &amp; ran off all over the show but mostly down to the Troop Ship  -</p>
