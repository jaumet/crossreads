<p>Bill's head appeared in the door of dugout with the precious article in his hand.  His expletives during the search had both John Adams and myself in fits of laughter.  His English is very imperfect and some of his sayings are screamingly funny.  They are composed chiefly of English slang, real curses and swear words, besides his own lingo mixed up in it.</p>
