
Monday 11/3/18  Two Coys working two training, had a game of cricket- Two letters from Doff- Billie has been sick.
Tuesday 12/3/18. Robinson back from leave took over raiders. Going to musketry demonstration tomorrow. Wrote to Doff
Wednesday 13/3/18  Left by car to Bailleul then by train St. Omer & bus to Musketry School at Nordbecourt arrived about 5.30 p.m
Thursday 14/3/18  Saw students at work and some demonstrations  dined at night with Thelander who is at the school
