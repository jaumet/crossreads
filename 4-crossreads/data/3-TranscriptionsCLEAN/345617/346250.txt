  
 Novembre 24 Part of the Section working in Camp & part in the Reserve Line. I had a good look round our defence work to pick locality. Good news still coming in from our advance near Cambrai. 
 Novembre 25 Out with Mr Crawford taking map locations of dugouts & pill boxes. Most of Section working on strong points in Reserve & Support lines. Still getting good news from the Cambrai Front. Over 100 guns have been captured & a large number prisoners beside a lot of civilians released.
