 June 25th Nothing unusual. Mass 7.30a.m. 
 June 26 First view of German soldiers. Three pass through our base. Our Boys successful raid. 
 June 27th Letter to E.C. 
 June 28th "The White Company" 
 June 29th I again meet Roger Morgan &ndash; Capt. Strachan annoyed by the Boys. 
 June 30th Preparations for evacuation of Ft Rompu. 
 July 1st We leave our "Good old home" at 10.30am for Bailleul &ndash; March not enjoyable owing to rapid pace heat of day etc &ndash; We arrive at our new quarters &ndash; very tired.
