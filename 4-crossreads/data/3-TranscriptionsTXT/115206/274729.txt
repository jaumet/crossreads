 June 1918 Saturday 8 
 Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles. Weather very fine. Visited Jack Harvey, had some very  refreshing drinks Rode across to Bonnay for a feed and certainly had one sang at Concert in  Y.M.C.A. pretty successful turn out. Had a very enjoyable swim with Don Ack, P.E and Trail Red Palmer worries about something. Inspected a programme of events for tomorrow's sports. Met North and Robbie at 113th Lines.
