<p>cost R2 (2/8) &amp; was first class.  The sights such as the oriental shops are very interesting &amp; the natives vary so.  Some are most serious others smile &amp; laugh without provocation according to their cast &amp; race.  The men mostly wear a waist cloth &amp; the women have in addition a blouse  when young they are extremely interesting &amp; sometimes pretty but they excel with their eyes both them &amp; the young men &amp; children having beautiful eyes.  The carts are old world affairs having mostly thatched covers &amp; are  drawn by oxen of various sizes &amp; colours.  Motor Cars &amp; carriages are also common.  A Rickshaw cost is 8d per hour or 50 cents although they generally get more from visitors   Black crows abound everywhere &amp; are quiet a feature.  Cocoanut palms gives the scene a touch needful &amp; are in large clumps everywhere especially along the beach.  The moment a ship arrives it is surrounded by hundreds of  boats of different kinds, catamarans dart in &amp; out &amp; the coloured waistcloths of the men the quaint boats &amp; the white coverings of the fuelling boats all makes a picture   Begging is second nature.  As soon as a child can move it is instructed in the art. We returned to the ship &amp; found our Doctor awaiting our arrival.  Our boatmen attempted to overcharge us but we compromised</p>
