These men are reckoned up as 150,000 fit men & as reinforcements will supply the A.I.F. till September next at least. What about Australia's Industries? Some say Australia's trade will be ruined by withdrawing so many men from production.  Are France & Britain ruined by withdrawing twice our percentage & using as many again for munitions?
