  
 February, 1916 Friday 25 Early morning we do a little practice in Gun mounting & get our gun & Tripod ready for the standard tests After breakfast we examine all squads at Drill After dinner inspection & in the evening I went into Heliopolis for a walk. 
 Saturday 26 First thing I heard this morning was "Cookhouse" at 8 am. After breakfast we had a muster parade & issue of certificates. After dinner all men for Ismalia & Tel-el-Kebir march out At night I went to pictures w/- dad
