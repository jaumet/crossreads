  
 Tuesday 28th Rained most of the night & blew some of the tents over - not ours fortunately - Had a very bad night as I was sore from lying on the hard boards so long. Still she rains. Moved into huts this evening a great improvement Are supposed to be in Isolation. It is the rule when you first land Am feeling fairly well again. The isolation is a fraud as we go about much as we like.
