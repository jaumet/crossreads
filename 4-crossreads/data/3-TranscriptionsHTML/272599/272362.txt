<p>April Sunday 1 1917</p>
<p>"Camp" in a ferment this morning  Bugle blew reveille at 5.30 a.m. parade quarter-hour later. Orders out to strike camp &amp; remove it to Aveluy other side of Albert 6 miles distant. <br/>Such a removal means a prodigeous amount of work. All patients (scabies) had to be shifted, tents &amp; marquees pulled down &amp; much heavy loading. Work hard till tea time. Turn in early.</p>
