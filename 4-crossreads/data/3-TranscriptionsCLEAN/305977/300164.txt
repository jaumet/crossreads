 Monday 25th  [Jan] Was on forage fatigue in the morning.  Very sultry all day.  Got news of another aerial raid on the English coast also that German Cruiser was sunk & 2 battleships damaged.  Heard of further British successes at the front.  We are all pretty tired of the boat, have been at sea 5 weeks now but a few more days ought to see through.  Was paid 10/-.
