<p>1917 July</p>
<p>30 Monday</p>
<p>Was made staff cook against my will. Then was told that 3 of us wd. take it each in turn. Then was told this wd. not do, I must be head. Then Miss Brown made a row with everyone &amp; insisted on being head. I just let 'em muddle along and take no notice as I've had a year's training in London of English ways. Will think my own thoughts and write a book if the plot comes into my head. Lindsay already contributed some paper. Had a swim</p>
