<p>Saturday 6 April 1918<br/>Trip to Keikit [?] Deran in our 2E <strike>Pilot</strike> to drop a message, pilot Lt. Murphy, in air 40 m.</p>
<p>Sunday 7 April 1918<br/>Letters from Home.<br/>On photos, with Addie.  In air 2 h. 5 m., did not make a very good job of them, my first trip with large camera, am going out again tomorrow and will make a better job of it.</p>
