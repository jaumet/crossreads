 Feb. 13 Saturday 1915 Half day Parade for Regiment Major Fuller &amp; Col Cox out together in afternoon. I went to Cairo by 2. 30 PM train got tents paid balance. Met Mr. Victor Convas who took me around to see the sights. Back camp 11PM Ordered lime to be sent to Maadi Station Monday 
 Feb. 14 Sunday 1915 Major Fuller &amp; other Officers went to Pyrimads Church Parade General Walker visiting Mr Victor Convas visited camp Went to church tonight with Jack Lister. 
 Feb. 15 Monday 1915 Regiment foot drill all day White washing stones  6/- worth lime received from Cairo
