25-3-17Williamson B Sqdn rolled his blankets &amp; started down the road towards Cairo, arriving at the Flying Corps hangars (Bardawil) 4 miles West of us; he told them he was going to England to fix up his affairs there &amp; wanted an aeroplane to fly over to England they offered him a Bristol but he said he wanted a Taube they humoured him &amp; put a guard over him; till a guard from here who had been sent to catch him arrived they brought him back &amp; put him in the Field Ambulance, he has been very strange for some time &amp; is quite dotty; but mad enough not to go East towards the enemy
