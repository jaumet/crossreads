<p>7th Australian Infantry Brigade.</p>
<p>6th July 1918.</p>
<p>Dear Col. Travers,</p>
<p>I have just received advice that your application for return to Australia for urgent family reasons has been granted by the G.O.C., A.I.F., as a special case.</p>
<p>Before you go I want to say how sorry I am to lose you both personally and in the interests of the service [indecipherable]&nbsp; I want also to thank you for the excellent work you [indecipherable] while under my command, and to express the hope that you [indecipherable] find matters all right on your return to Australia, [indecipherable] may be able to return to us in the future.</p>
<p>With Best Wishes and Regards,</p>
