 up any old Weymouth boys meet youn. Roberts &amp;  Escott. Packed saddles to leave on the 12th. 
 12th/Left Mera Matruh at 9 am., arrived at first well at 2pm. put down horse lines &amp; camped for night, looking like rain plenty of good tucker some the S.M. has stored up &amp; now wants to be rid of. not much spare rum come to light yet. I gues he knows where that went. 
 13th/ Travelled 17 miles to next well &amp; camped for night got in at six pm
