 Stretcher drill.  All No 4s of squad brought out in turn to drill the section, my first opportunity of drilling the section. Excitement at night; gambling school composed of men from the Infantry raided within our lines.  Some of the men arrested. 
 Saturday  12/2/16 Went on dental parade &amp; had tooth refilled.  Large mail from home after a fortnights interval. 
 Sunday 13/2/16 Church parade 9 AM.  Two British Monitors, carrying 2 large guns, one fore &amp; one aft &amp; two ante aircraft guns passed
