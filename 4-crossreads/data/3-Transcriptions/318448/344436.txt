<p>at Xmas time. There has been no row about it as yet anyhow &amp; I hope to have many pleasant rides around the place on him. I have decided to call him 'Henry'. He is not nearly as good a beast as Bernard nor so pleasant to ride but still I dont intend to look (or is it smack?) him in the mouth for he is a gift horse. He stands about 15 hands &amp; is a very dark chessnut almost a brown. I have only ridden him once as yet but so far shying seems to be his only fault. This</p>
