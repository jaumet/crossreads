<p>November 1915<br/>Wednesday 10<br/>Fine   Strong breeze blowing. One handkerchief issued per man. More soldiers gifts I suppose.</p>
<p>Thursday 11<br/>Fine   Calm sea.  Warship busy today shelling across the Peninsular. Second Bde. Light Horse did another stunt on the right &amp; successfully occupied an old Turkish trench.</p>
