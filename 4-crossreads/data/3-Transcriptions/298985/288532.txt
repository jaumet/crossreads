<p>Tuesday 13th<br/>Got orders to pack &amp; be ready to move at 12 noon But did not go untill 4 which was well as the day was hot. Went back to Villiers Bretteneaux &amp; got the same billit as before. Heard the usual nightly bombs drop mostely</p>
