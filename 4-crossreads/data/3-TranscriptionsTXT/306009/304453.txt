 1915 Sep. 28 Tue. Met Mildred Brown at 3.15 p.m. &amp; after calls at a photo. shop &amp; post office, went off to the Native Bazaars where all kinds of shoes, mats, rugs, jewelry, tinware, etc, etc, can be seen in the making &amp; all sorts of curios &amp; valuables are on sale. Finished up with a visit to No. 1 Gen. Hospital, Heliopolis.
