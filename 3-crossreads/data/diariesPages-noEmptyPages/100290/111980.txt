1916 August 7
Monday Bank Holiday
Resume duty at 8 a.m. Help carry patients from dressing room to ward all day.
Lieut. Jacka thro? wounded. Another Lieut's steel helmet pierced by bullet. narrow shave. Stream of wounded continue all day. What a price we pay for a few yards of ground. The way to Berlin is literally paved with the bodies of our gallant dead. The horror awfulness of it all.
