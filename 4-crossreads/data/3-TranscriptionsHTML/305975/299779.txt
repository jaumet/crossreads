<p>tuesday feb. 18.2.29<br/>turned out 7. AM  had my Breakfast  Packed up our saddles  had look round  caught train 2. P.M.  arrived back Kantara 3.30  had tea walked to east Kantara no room in train  seems to be all messed up here  went to bed cant get on train<br/>Wednesday feb. 19.2.19<br/>turned out 6.30  had a wash  got on train and arrived Raffa 2.30  had dinner and walked to camp had tea  turned in</p>
