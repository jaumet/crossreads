<p>October 1916<br/>18th Continued.  A band Concert was held in the mess hut to-night &amp; proved a great success.<br/>19th   Heavy rain has fallen since last night.  I had to go out to the Kangaroo Rifle range this morning with reference number 104 to go through another musketry test.  Kangaroo Range is about 4 miles from here &amp; on the other side of the Bull Ring<br/>21st   The weather has been very cold to day &amp; yesterday with thick frosts in the morning.  I have had a very bad cold for the last two or three days &amp; am not feeling up to the mark</p>
