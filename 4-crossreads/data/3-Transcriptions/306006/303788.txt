<p>23-3-17<br/>Fri 23rd<br/>Rose after 8 oclock this morning &amp; found Dev hard at work on the type-writer had breakfast &amp; put the morning in lying on the bed smoking &amp; reading, had dinner at 12 oclock &amp; then Dev came with me to the station at Kantara East &amp; I just managed to catch the 12.40 train to El Arish, had a cruel journey the trucks bumping &amp; jolting all the way, arrived at El Arish at midnight &amp; got off the train only a few hundred yards from where the Regt is camped; 3 lads from the 3rd Regt came down with me &amp; knew where we are camped</p>
