<p>Whilst here word came to me that I was to have my leave to England &ndash; As will be expected I was filled with anticipation as to the change.</p>
<p>We were whilst on these billets employed on fatigue work at or near a place called Zonnebeke We would be taken up to this in a light railway wearing tin helmets as a protection from shrapnel. It was a dangerous job. Every day there were causalities whilst I was there</p>
<p>One time a German aeroplane flew over the gun pit and dropped 3 Bombs quickly one</p>
