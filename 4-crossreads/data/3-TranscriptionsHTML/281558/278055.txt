<p>Tuesday Oct 30th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : showery : parade in morn &amp; paid up : parade in afternoon &amp; had a V.D. &amp; scabie inspection : got a few letters : read in army hut after tea</p>
<p>Wednesday Oct 31st</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : reveille 5.30 am : fell in got our passes &amp; off to London : got fixed up at H.Q. so Griff &amp; self roamed round had a good time : air raid about 10 pm &amp; great excitement : had some fun comforting the girls : no bombs near us : fair bit of gunfire</p>
<p>Thursday Nov 1st</p>
<p>Raid about 4 am but I went to bed long before that : roamed round London &amp; went to "Tussauds" which is very good : went &amp; seen "A little bit of fluff" at night &amp; then got lost going home : plenty of women etc sleeping in the door</p>
