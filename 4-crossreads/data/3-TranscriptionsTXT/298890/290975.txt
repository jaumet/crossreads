 Miss Hodge &amp; I went to B. Museum for day.  Lunch &amp; tea at Minerva. 7.Thursday. Air raid started about 11.30 p.m. "All clear" about 1.45 a.m. could hear every plane buzzing above us. 
 8.Friday B.M. in morning.   Miss H. took me to on "The Better 'Ole' at Oxford in the afternoon. A. Bourchier as 'Old Bill'. 
 9. Saturday. Stayed in all day &ndash; tried to do a little writing. Miss Hodge came home late with tales of bomb damage at Warwick Ave.
