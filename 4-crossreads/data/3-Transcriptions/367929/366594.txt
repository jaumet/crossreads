<p>February 1915<br/>8 Mon<br/>Saddled and left at 3 - for outpost line from hut in NE direction. Dug trenches till 12 midnight &ndash; returned camp 2.15 am.</p>
<p>9 Tuesday<br/>Out with NZ Mtd Rifle Bde and inspect the trenches N of old Suez railroad.</p>
