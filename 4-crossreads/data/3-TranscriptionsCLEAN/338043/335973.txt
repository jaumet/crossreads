  
 Our Engineer Dvr. Smith leaves for Long Service Furlough in Australia - After the show I write a new dramatic sketch for what is called "The Burglar". 
 Sept. 15th Sabbath - Further decoration to the Blue Room. - Wash clothes - write letters. Weather warm & oppressive. 
 Sept. 16th A never to be forgotten storm - Thunder & lightening as I have never seen before heard or witnessed it. Show as usual.
