24 February, Monday
F.I go walk around block then back to mess. I am in a very bad skin &amp; tell Slogger Jones at the mol[?] what I think about the Place. I play Billiards &amp; go to bed about 9 pm.
25 February, Tuesday
F.Neary 300 admissions. I go to bed at 2 pm but have to get up again. Carry on until 7 pm &amp; then go to bed. Can't go to Dance too sick. We discharge 130 men also.
We are a mess 'cos we don't know where all the Patients have been put.
Wrote Marion Edmonds.
