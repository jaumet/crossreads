 16 November, Sunday 
 Dig &amp; make wall around top garden. Go to Dee Why &amp; fix ashes on front of house. Swim &amp; Home again. E &amp; E out to dinner. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie. 
 17 November, Monday 
 Go to E &amp; E for Tea &amp; meet N at 8 pm. Meet R Charles &amp; go walk along Lovers Walk to Condamine St.
