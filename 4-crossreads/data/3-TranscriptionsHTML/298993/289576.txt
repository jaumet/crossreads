<p>Wednesday Jan 10.1.17. stand to 6.30 had breakfast bully and biscuits saddled up and left 7AM. trecked Back to elaush which place we reached 3.PM unloaded and boiled the billy first cup of tea for 2 days rations short. Thursday Jan 11.1.17 turned out 7.A.M had breakfast and delivered a few wires afternoon had bath and done some washing. Friday 12.1.17. turned out 6.A.M had breakfast and cleaned up. afternoon 2.P.M general parade formed up in a hollow.
