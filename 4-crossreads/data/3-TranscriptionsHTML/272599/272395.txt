<p>1917 Friday 4 May</p>
<p>Put on Mess Room staff &ndash; light duties. Parade to Dr. with wrist  Feeding the multitude. <br/>Down the village with Jock &amp; Cart. Coiffeur. Chocolate &amp; pastry. Mac. Coal transport. <br/>2nd Div. patients in hospital this morning.</p>
