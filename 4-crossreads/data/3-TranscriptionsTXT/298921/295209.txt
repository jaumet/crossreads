
August, 1915Thursday 26I have found out the size of the Building the main passage is 232 yards long!!! &   there are 1200 rooms & hundreds of Bathrooms - each room has its own telephone & electric Fans in most -Alabaster & marble for staircase & stain glass Dome - Had a good sleep last night quite a fog this morning - Laura sent me a Fly Killer & soks & Handkerchief - just heard an officer is off to Australia so will give him these notes to post on Board the Euripedes -Love to all, getting on well, but will be some weeks yet - FW
