<p>Wed Aug 16th &ndash; 16<br/>I am detailed as orderly for an indefinite time between Bde HQ Qtrs and Div Hd Qtrs.</p>
<p>Thurs  Aug 17th &ndash; 16<br/>Great excitement this morning when a taube set on fire by one of our planes, came down about 500 yards away.</p>
<p>Fri Aug 18th &ndash; 16<br/>Last night our boys made another raid and were very successful bringing back a good number of prisoners.</p>
