<p>28 April 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Picked for guard duty for next 24 hours. Howells, Parsons, Tony &amp; Jock too. 2 hrs on 6 off. sleep down in guard tent.<br/>Bob goes over for my watch at Bazaar. found shop wrecked &amp; owner gone.<br/>Dave Watts comes down from Light Horse lines &amp; finds me. Glad to see him, have a good yarn. D Waddell &amp; Ern Noble in same regiment.</p>
