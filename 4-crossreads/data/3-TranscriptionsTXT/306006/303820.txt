 13-4-17 have a superiority of miles per hour over the fastest planes we have on this front which are the Martinsyde &amp; Bristol; arriving in the village which consisted of only a few huts &amp; orchards we found it deserted of natives excepting 2 men one old woman a "bint" about 18 years old &amp; 15 young children, we found Upham &amp; MacNamara on top of a roof &amp; they advised us not to look through the houses as they did &amp; only found fleas to their sorrow; Our horses have had no water since leaving Khan Yunus yesterday morning at 10 oclock so we mustered what little Arabic we knew &amp; paid the native family a visit &amp; told them we wanted water they offered us an earthernware
