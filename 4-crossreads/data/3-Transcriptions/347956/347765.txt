<p>Nov 5 &ndash; 6 &ndash; 7</p>
<p>the same route as we are)  at 6.30 P.M. was speaking to her by semaphore &amp; found out that Kingsborough won the Cup &amp; that Turkey had declared war with Germany; we are doubtful whether they meant for or against Germany.  we are inclined to think it is for Germany.  quite a change to see some ladies still on same course.  have got a touch of influenza, &amp; coughed a set of teeth overboard, just before tea time too; another lecture.</p>
<p>Fri 6th<br/>Sleeping all morning.  teaching troops semaphore afternoon.<br/>not feeling too bright.  a lecture after tea.  rather warm</p>
<p>Sat 7th<br/>Saw the Dr &amp; was ordered off duty for the day.  tug of war was pulled off afternoon.  C Squad<br/>(Rigged up hammock over [indecipherable] room)</p>
