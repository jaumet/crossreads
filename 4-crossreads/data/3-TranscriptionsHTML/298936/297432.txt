<p>October 1915</p>
<p>Sunday 3</p>
<p>Light duty today for a change.</p>
<p>Got some firewood from the hill during day &amp; did little else except write some letters &amp; read "Vanity Fair".</p>
<p>Heard that the firing on the left a couple of days ago resulted in 1000 yds gain.</p>
<p>Three hospital ships in the bay &amp; several visits from our aeroplanes overhead.</p>
