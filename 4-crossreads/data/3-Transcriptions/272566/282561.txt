<p>Thursday 26 April</p>
<p>Lovely Day.<br/>Incinerator.<br/>After tea practising football for tomorrow's match V Reinforcement Camp.  3 hours football so am feeling pretty stiff.<br/>Supper tonight of cocoa toast &amp; eggs &ndash; fruit &amp; cream.</p>
