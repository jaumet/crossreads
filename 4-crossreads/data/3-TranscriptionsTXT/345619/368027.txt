
[Reverse of the above postcard]No. 7008Sgt. Burrowes A.J.21st Rft18th Batt.Australian Imperial Forces
26th June 1918Dear ManWe are hoping you will get these for your B.Day. There is a mail going out today which they will catch. D.V.W.P. I would have liked to have written a long letter but came home late from Sydney last night & Have to go to St Mary's tonight so will write tomorrow. Much love & many kind thoughts. Annie
