 Thursday 23&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; About hospital&nbsp; 650 patients&nbsp; Had dinner with Lieut Col McGlynn ar  Shep  the Continental Hotel.&nbsp; [Major (Honorary Lieutenant Colonel), later Brigadier General, John Patrick McGlinn CBE VD CMG OBE (1869-1946)] 
 Friday 24&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; Saw Lt Col McGlynn, he had come from Galipoli, where he has been since April.&nbsp; He looks very well.&nbsp; Lunched with Col. Manifold &amp; Col. Hunter at the Continental Hotel.&nbsp; 
 Saturday 25 September 1915&nbsp; In Cairo during the morning.&nbsp; Sent 128 men to Australia, all correct.&nbsp; 
 Sunday 26&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; At Basilica at 9-30 a.m.&nbsp; At Matarieh with Major Young&nbsp;at 6 p.m.&nbsp; Letters from home this morning.&nbsp;
