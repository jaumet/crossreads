<p>was full of shell holes filled in with slush. &amp; before you knew it you would be in one &amp; strugle out to get in another.</p>
<p>27/. Teams took up shells &amp; Fritz gaves us hell for a while, B sub gun team got blown up. Dad Harbor was killed outright, Hayward wounded in back &amp; Williamson was shell schocked.</p>
<p>28/. I rode to battery with Thompson., Fritz was qt. Three teams going to Flers to stay for three weeks when they will be relieved.  Moved up to near Baupaume  to a place called Lingy</p>
