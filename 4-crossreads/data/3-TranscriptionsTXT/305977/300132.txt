 November 1914 Sunday 15th Very hot &amp; thundery.  Church parade in the morning.  Remarkable numbers of Presbyterians &amp; Methodists.  Service in the YMCA.  Bath parade &amp; medical inspection.  Full uniform parade in the afternoon.  4 men taken out of B troop &amp; transferred to 2nd Reinforcements.  Detailed for main guard tonight. 
 Monday 16th On main guard.  Very hot day.  Thunderstorm in afternoon.  Had tea in Liverpool &amp; went to picture at night. 
 Tuesday Mounted drill in the morning, 2 or 3 miles along the road.  Very hot in morning.  Heavy rain about noon &amp; showery thereafter.  Pay day, drew &pound;3/15/-.  Went to Fergusons, catching the 3 to 6 train.
