<p>1917 Sunday 15 July</p>
<p>A brilliant morning. Would like much better to go out instead of going on graft. <br/>Half afternoon off from 4.30 p.m. Carty &amp; self walk to Column. C's confidences. Back to church at Y.M.C.A. Eng. Ser. Major. ordinary. Walk out to cliffs after.</p>
