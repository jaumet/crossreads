<p>LE BARQUE<br/>Friday 9<br/>By jove the battalion are working hard for six bob a day<br/>Saturday 10<br/>Riding with the Colonel to the front line and got lost, nothing extraordinary<br/>Sunday 11<br/>Le Sars smashed to dust &amp; match wood<br/>Wading through mud knee deep and deeper</p>
