
11 SundayMr & Mrs Arncliffe Sennet, Lt. Ross. the Banbacionis & young Brown came to tea.  After which I met Claude at Marble Arch & to movies & tea.  Fine day.
12 MondayLovely day.  Left London for Hitchin at 1.35 to rest at Fairfield.  Pottered & read a book of E.M. Dell's
13 TuesdayPottered about garden & had a sleep & tried a little short hand.  Lovely day
14 WednesdayLovely weather.  Same routine.  Miserably depressed.
