<p>Saturday May 18th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : off duty 6 am : stroll with Gill after tea &amp; watched the boats in the dock :</p>
<p>Sunday May 19th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : on duty 2 &ndash; 10 pm : plenty of air raids lately :</p>
<p>Monday May 20th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : went to Malo in morn &amp; had a stroll round : hot day :</p>
<p>Tuesday May 21st</p>
<p>Dunkirk : yarn with my French friend &amp; stroll through the town : air raid alarm :</p>
<p>Wednesday May 22nd</p>
<p>Dunkirk : stroll round the docks in morn : another hot day : raid alarm at night :</p>
<p>Thursday May 23rd</p>
<p>Dunkirk : wrote in morn : weather inclined to be stormy : on duty 2 pm :</p>
<p>Friday May 24th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : stroll round the docks in morn &amp; worked 2 &ndash; 10 pm :</p>
