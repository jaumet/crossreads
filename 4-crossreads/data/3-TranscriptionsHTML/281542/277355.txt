<p>They also pump shrapnel into us about twice a day &ndash; but as with the 75 it is evident that the ammunition is very limited, otherwise our position would be certainly untenable.  Their shrapnel, what they have of it, is very effective - forward 200 yds. by about 100 yds. wide.</p>
<p>Our fellows in the front trenches are suffering &amp; undergoing great trials.  <strike>Their</strike> Perhaps their worst enemy is the unendurable stench from the Turkish trenches where <strike>our own</strike> the bodies of our heroes lie rotting side by side with those of the enemy.  Many are in a state of utter putrefaction &amp; are swollen like poisoned pups</p>
