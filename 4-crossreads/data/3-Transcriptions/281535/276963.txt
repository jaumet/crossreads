<p>19/5/19<br/>Sutton-Veny<br/>Hot but pleasant day. Men were paid and handed in certain articles of kit. Held a conference of company commanders and rubbed home a few points. Sacked my batman Scotty Watson. Rumour that Hawker has flown Atlantic announced at cinema tonight.</p>
<p>20/5/19<br/>First thing met James and in a car went about 5 miles with him along narrow winding lanes fringed with beautiful trees. The beauty of this country side is a regular gardenlike beauty. We passed through the small hamlet Longbridge Deverill and drove up to a plain kind of manor house with several gardeners working in front of it.
