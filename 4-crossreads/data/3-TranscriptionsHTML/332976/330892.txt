<p>July 1917<br/>from a tree next door, the old lady having invited another chap &amp; myself to have some.<br/>It has been raining all the morning but cleared up after dinner.<br/>9th   This morning the section went down to the lock &amp; sorted out a lot more of the gear for pontooning.   Some of it is to be taken further down stream together with material for the pile driving which we will be doing in a few days.<br/>10th   Nos 3 &amp; 4 section went for a route march this morning for about 5 Km along the river towards Perone.   In the afternoon the whole Coy. was photographed by the French photographer in the town.</p>
