 19 October, Sunday 
 Catch 9.15 boat with Otto &amp; Amy. Go to beach with N in afternoon. We go to Melville at night, arrive Home minuit. Up at daybreak almost because of roosters etc, next morning. 
 20 October, Monday 
 Meet Dave's Girl in tram. Get in the wrong train too. Home again &amp; early to bed.
