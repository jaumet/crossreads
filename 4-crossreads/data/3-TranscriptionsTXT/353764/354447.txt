12.4.16 WednesdayToday we had the beginning of a Sand Storm which I understand last about 3 weeks at this time of the Year.  It is the [most] uncomfortable & wretched thing ever one had to put up with.  There are times when you cannot see your hand in front of you & everything gets smothered in fine Sand.  It gets in your food & everywhere.  I am also on a big D.C.M. & things are only middling.
