1.8.11th of July. Also parcel 20 June. Few of the boys transferred to 3rd A.G.H.
Friday 14.9.17
Orderly Corporal. Walked to Wardriques at night. Brawl in camp late.
Saturday 15.9.17
Had to give evidence in connection with last nights disturbance. Camp struck all patients evacuated; expect to move on Monday.
Sunday 17.9.17
General fatigues, preparing for to-morrows move.
Monday 18.9.17
Early reveille. Moved away 9 Am. early part of march very nasty choppy, probably owing to bad arrangements; improved after passing saluting base at about 10 Am. Trouble among transport, one wagon
