 Tuesday. We went back to Belah to water &amp; feed  Infantry still attacking town with heavy losses  a great assault made by NZ and 2nd Brigade on Eastern end  captured 2 guns and 300 prisoners Wednesday  Very quiet so far  a big offensive coming off  40,000 Turks on our front
