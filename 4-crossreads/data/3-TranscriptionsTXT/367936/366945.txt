 Thursday 9 March 1916 Dick &amp; Geoff finished Front pdk. Father, Mother &amp; I mustered sheep from Long pdk. down here.  Draughted them in afternoon and took 8 &amp; Leis ewes to Wonga. 
 Friday 10 March 1916 Dick &amp; I plowed up front road. Father &amp; Geoff riding round.  Dick started discing 40 acres. Mother &amp; Father drove over to Barham. Geoff &amp; I mustered Front pdk.
