 August,1918 Wednesday 21 
 In front of Morcourt, Bty Pos. Weather fine and clear. Was informed that Ken Gunn, Smithy, Ped,Tom and myself were recommended for a decoration About 11.30 Fritz found Bty position with 5.9s and 8 inch howitzers, blew up [indecipherable] gun, also Lewis m.g. and wounded Les Alymer "B" sub Catling and Hannermay were also wounded, at forward W.L. Repeated barrage and caught Tommy Pattey in rear. Cut up all our lines. Had a fairly rough day, all through 2 of our guns to 55th &ndash; 2 of them knocked out
