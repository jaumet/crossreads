
just detrained & are in a rest camp overlooking the harbour The town in the distance looks an insignificant a very ordinary little place - before the war just a port of call for coastal trade but now it is an important base for both naval & military for the Macedonian Front & Turkish Campaign & I don't suppose there was ever so much work & money in the place before.
The country has been very monotonous these last two days & we are all glad to have got this stage of the journey over - 8 days in the train
Oct 3. S.S. H.M.T. Ormonde Glasgowwas on
