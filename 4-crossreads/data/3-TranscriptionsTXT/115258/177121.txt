 Cairo 
 22nd December 1918 
 My dear Father, 
 I sent you a short note last week to thank you for sending the tenner, so now as I&#39;m up in Cairo once more I tell you about the trip up here. This is Sunday morning, a beautiful cool sunny day; - just the ideal weather for a sight-seeing holiday.
