  
 February 1918 Friday 15 
 Messines Had the 4 to 6 shift on the phone and this morning. Walked to Spy Farm and met old Les Fuller and Duncan. Had tea with Les and proceeded on to Daylight Corner, saw Thompson and James. Had a pow pow. Believe that Ike Steinthal is in the 10th Field Coy, also some of the others. Weather wet, altho a little better today. Fritz shelled for a couple of hours. Garford and Mills on L'aison.
