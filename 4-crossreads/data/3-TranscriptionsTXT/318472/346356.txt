 11 May 1918 Saturday A beautiful fine day Nothing special doing. Went for a stroll down to Barford in the afternoon. 
 1918 May 12 Sunday After Ascension 
 Just the same old Camp Sunday Had a stroll through the woods between our camp and the Dinton Rd in the afternoon Went to a Y.M.C.A. Service in the evening Nice fine weather.
