<p>drill. The sergeant took us into the trees, gave us slope arms, fix &amp; unfix bayonets, then pile arms, &amp; we sat down &amp; yarned till we came back. Had a swim in the Somme afterwards; a Fritz 'plane came right over, &amp; our anti-aircraft &amp; Lewis guns opened out on all sides from the trees, he turned &amp; there were several of our chaps undressed on the bank, wouldn't their white bodies be a plain target from above, eh! However Fritz was travelling too fast to get away from the shrap, to fire his machine gun at a few of us. The battalion was paid today, &amp; the gambling soon started, two up being first favorite, &amp; there were groups in a circle, &amp; shouts of "some money tails", "I want 200", &amp; c. Saw Boo again today, he was playing cards with some of his company, &amp; the francs &amp; franc notes lay on the waterproof sheet in the centre. Saty. 29 June  Had a practice at platoon in attack this morning, then we went along the Somme Canal into Daours, &amp; all had hot showers at the military baths, &amp; handed in our dirty clothes, &amp; got washed ones. It was bonza to feel thoroughly clean again but one soon gets chatty. Everything looked a picture</p>
