<p>October, 1915<br/>Tuesday 19<br/>Embarked on S.S. Franconia a 15000 Ton Cunard Liner a beautiful boat &amp; most luxurious it is always such a contrast a soldiers life.<br/>We have about 2500 soldiers on board &amp; lots of officers &ndash; about 90 I was surprised to meet among the 1st  [indecipherable] Reinf:- Shannon from Jerilderie who was with me in the Bushman the day I was shot. At the stern of the boat we have a 6" gun already for any submarine &amp; we steer a Zig Zag course, &amp; all hands are "told off to their respective boats"</p>
