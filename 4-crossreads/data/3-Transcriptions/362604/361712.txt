<p>who are convalescent have to go on Parade &amp; it means a lot of extra messing about.  I have just been up to the mess for supper &amp; a game of cards.  Would you believe Ellis' delight for the last week or more has been whist &amp; He enjoys a game as much as any old card player.  I generally read or write when I am not out on the Bike.  I may go to the Theatre to-morrow evening.  We have a nice Billiard Table in the mess but I rarely have a game as the table is generally in use &amp; I would not bother waiting away.  I am <u>the</u> mug of the mess at Billiards but of course it is only from no practise because there is hardly a minute in "off</p>
