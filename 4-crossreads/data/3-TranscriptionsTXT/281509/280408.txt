 1916 July 4 Tuesday 
 Up at 5.30 &amp; to work. moving. Parade at 2 p. m. &amp; set out on our march of 8 miles. Sent on black bag &amp; blankets for transport. Carry equipment &amp; overcoat. Thunderstorm breaks as we leave. Rained all the way. Do a forced march all the way. Thoroughly fagged out at termination. Cross the frontier between France &amp; Belgium.  Now on Belgian soil at Neuve Eglise. Crowd of 20 in tent wet clothes &amp; mud
