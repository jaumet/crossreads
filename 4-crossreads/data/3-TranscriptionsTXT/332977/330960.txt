 Battleship plays the National Anthem as we pull out to stream 
 March 3rd Passing Athens conditions rotten, cannot even get drinking water, nothing to wash our mess tins with, no forks plates or mugs issued us 
 March 4th Arrived Lemnos Island, beautiful harbour scores of Warships, transports, Destroyers and submarines, 
 March 5 Lying in Harbour awaiting orders 
 6th we row Queenslanders ashore to make room 
 7th Admiral of the fleet comes aboard We form a boat crew, to row men ashore 
 8th Rowed 10 men ashore back in evening, buy nick nacks, good feed 
 9th Long route march ashore, pulled ashore in boats, have a good day 10th great rejoicing cant go ashore to rough 
 11th On prison guard, Warships &amp; Transports arrive 
 12th French General visit our ships 
 13th Rowing men ashore, 1&frac34; hrs to do a trip, to rough to handle boats
