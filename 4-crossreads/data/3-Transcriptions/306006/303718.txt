<p>24-1-17<br/>Wed 24th<br/>I have a badly swollen throat &amp; the Dr was thinking seriously of sending me off to Field Ambulance this morning but decided to give me till tomorrow to show an improvement; Two English Officers were out looking over the country today, a Taube flew over us today very high &amp; received a warm welcome at El Arish from the Anti Air Craft guns.</p>
<p>A few more Turkish deserters came in today</p>
