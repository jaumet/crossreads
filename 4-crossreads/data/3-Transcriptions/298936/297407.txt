<p>September 1915<br/>Wednesday 8</p>
<p>Received instructions to prepare to leave tomorrow.</p>
<p>Paid in the morning.  Complemented by the Captain for good work in [indecipherable]. Sorry to loose me.</p>
<p>Had a flare up in the evening with Hunter with whom I am very good friends now.</p>
<p>Rather a pity I should [indecipherable].</p>
<p>Met Colonel on return &amp; he gave me good wishes &amp; wants me to write how many hundreds of letters</p>
