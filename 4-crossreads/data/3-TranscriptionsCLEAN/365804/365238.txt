 As you have perhaps heard I have received a commission in the Royal Artillery and I am in the anti-aircraft section. We are at Woolwich for the purpose of mobilization and we expect to have everything in readiness to leave for France at the end of this week. 
 With renewed expressions of my sympathy in which my wife joins I am Yours sincerely Victor Haigh.
