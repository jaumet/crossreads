<p>Same as yesterday</p>
<p>31st July Wednesday</p>
<p>Went to P. &amp; 0. and inquired sailing dates, and discover a boat is due to sail for Bombay via Port Said next Saturday. The next ship will be more than 3 later. I have decided if at all possible, to leave by the former boat. Book passage during afternoon.</p>
