  
 Christmas card  dated 8.12.18 GREETINGS AND BEST WISHES FROM Handwritten:-.  Clyde,  Beaumeuse, Belgian Border 
 Text:-  CHRISTMAS 1918 We greet you friend, where'er your distant home, In Britain, France, 'neath Austral's sunny dome; Now there is peace, we'll see you all again- For soon we hope to leave this cold Bohain, To safely dodge the Hun slow-action mine And spend our last war Christmas on the Rhine
