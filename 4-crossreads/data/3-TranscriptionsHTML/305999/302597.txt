<p>23/2/18<br/>4.30 the manawar came along side again for more rations<br/>fine weather</p>
<p>28/2/18<br/>at 11 30 AM we entered Cape town and pulled along side the peir and had dinner at 2 PM  we fell in on the pier  we marched up to the dock gates and we where dismissed<br/>I had a good time reported on board again at 11 PM  lovely weather  Cool night</p>
