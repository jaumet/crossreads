  
 Sunday 5 August 1917. 
 7 a.m. Reveille. No riding this morning on the camels. At 2 pm a party of 15 of us went for the afternoon to see the Pyramids. The tram ride (electric) was great & the views across the intensely cultivated fields were interesting. Saw Egyptians ploughg with the old wooden plough and 2 oxen. On arrival at the Pyramids we hired a donkey each & with a guide saw all that there is to be seen; visiting all the pyramids sphinx, old & new tombs & seeing through the temples of sphinx & interior of the largest pyramid s . Had tea at Y.M.C.A. in Cairo & reached Abbassia Camp at 9.30 pm.
