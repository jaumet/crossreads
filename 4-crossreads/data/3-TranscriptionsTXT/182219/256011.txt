 30 Dec 
 We proceeded by motor to Sector &amp; from their by car to our front line position called Khansbetha Ibn-Harith. 
 We stayed with the 3rd LH Bdge Hdqrs now &amp; again the chatter of a Gun echoed down the valley &amp; an occasional snipers rifle rang out but this was all.
