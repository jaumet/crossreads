<p>30th Sept. '17<br/>Tonight  there is a beautiful full moon.<br/>I wonder how many people are watching it rise &amp;  saying the usual inanities concerning it?<br/>A few days ago we were issued with six donkeys! We are becoming more  &amp;  more a cross between a farm &amp;   a circus. We have now some 80 white men, 30 natives, 100 horses, 35 camels, six donkeys, four fowls, two pigeons &amp;   two dogs to say nothing of the usual chorus</p>
