23-9-1918On the road, making for Bray.  Arrived there 12 noon, after an 18 Kilometre march.
24-9-1918Still at Bray, Billeted in a concert hall.  Nothing doing, filling in time until the 27 inst.  We then entrain for an 8 days train journey.
25-9-1918Farewell by General Birdwood.  We move off tomorrow.  New pay books issued.
26-9-1918, In trainLeft Bray 10 a.m.  Passed through Amiens.  27 men in horse truck.
27-9-1918Going strong, passed through Versailles and Troyes.
