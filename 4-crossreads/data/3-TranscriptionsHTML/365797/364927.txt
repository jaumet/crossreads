<p>the week end with her mother-inlaw at Glenbrook. Alec writes that he is in Egypt &amp; finds it a dirty place wants a good sweeping. He found Roll [indecipherable] &amp; so is happy.</p>
<p>We have had no word from Oliver so he must have gone round the Cape.</p>
<p>We are very quiet nothing but Red Cross, Six o"clock" Closing Liberal Meetings &amp; such good works going on. The weather is just lovely spring &amp; the birds are very busy. What a treat it must be to you all to be in France(?) &amp; see green fields &amp; flowers instead of sand.</p>
<p>In the Sunday Times today there is a group of photos &ndash; the Howell-Price family, fine sons in khaki four in the D.S.O. &amp; the eldest is a Lieut Col. at 26</p>
