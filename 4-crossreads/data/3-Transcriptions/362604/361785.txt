<p>faring.  I opened my Birthday cake last Friday &amp; we three &amp; Jim had a royal feed.  It is an epoch to have a large meal in this Country.  I had to leave some of my dinner to-day as there was such a lot.  Just fancy.  It is the first time for months &amp; months that it has happened but I have had "elegant sufficiency" many times of course.  Jim was one of the original 1914 Boys &amp; is ever so much steadier</p>
