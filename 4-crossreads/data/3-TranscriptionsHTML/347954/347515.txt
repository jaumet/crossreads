<p>we had lost six men &amp; a number wounded the ultimate result is unknown to me.  Our three destroyers continued to keep up the fire on the fort for quite a long while &amp; the earth in the immediate vicinity was not visible for quite a period.  Possibly the enemy here have subterranean <strike>change</strike> passages into which to get during the onslaught of our guns as we don't appear to lessen their numbers.</p>
<p>We advanced during the night &amp; captured the lagoon which is part of our way towards 981 but at a great loss.  The Naval Corp don't appear to be up to our standard as far as physique &amp; stamina is concerned &amp; possibly they are in reality raw recruits with broken hearts from heavy training undergone in Egypt.</p>
<p>We have had rather a set back on the left wing through the finding of a spy in our midst.  He occupied the rank of Corporal in the &ndash; battalion &amp; was found with incriminating evidence &amp; a secret code book in his possession.  Needless to say his exit was rather hurried.</p>
