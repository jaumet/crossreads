<p>Tuesday 25/12/17&nbsp; Wrote to Doff wishing her a Merry Xmas&nbsp; Snowing- men on fatigues.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26/12/17. Quiet Day&nbsp; wrote to Mother &amp; Bas&nbsp; Men on fatigue</p>
<p>Thursday 27/12/17&nbsp; Still snowing &amp; cold&nbsp; saw [Bundock?] of [R.A.A.?]&nbsp; men fatigues.</p>
<p>Friday 28/12/17. Quiet Day but very cold, reconnoitred front near La Basse Ville</p>
<p>Saturday 29/12/17&nbsp; Received cable from Doff saying all well &amp; wishing me Xmas Greetings.</p>
