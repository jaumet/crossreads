<p>8/8/16<br/>we marched back to warloy 12 miles  we where revewed by ther King and general Bird ward  the later presented some of our 5B  with M. Medal</p>
<p>14/8/16<br/>At 8 AM we left and marched to Albert we had dinner and then marched to reserve trenches where we remained for the night prity cold</p>
<p>15/8/16<br/>We went to the line 1 platoon in firing line</p>
