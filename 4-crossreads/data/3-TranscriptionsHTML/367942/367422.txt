<p>here fairly easy. Roll call at 10.30 AM. &amp; again at 4.30pm. Everyone must be in their own rooms by 9.30pm when guard come round &amp; see that everyone is there, then lights out.<br/>At 5A.M. guards again come round to see no one has escaped during the night. Rise at any time you please after the guard round.<br/>Daily routine &ndash;: Rise 7.30 AM. Have shower, shave, dress &amp; then go over to cook house &amp; make breakfast, all that is supplied by Germans is a substitute for coffee. Then have a walk or game of tennis.<br/>12 noon Dinner which consists of a plate of soup, god knows what it is composed of, &amp; vegetables.<br/> 230 Make afternoon tea, then go &amp; look at war news &amp; walk round till 6pm when we have dinner. Made by ourselves, that being supplied being merely a plate of soup. Then another stroll till dark &amp; then bridge &amp; bed. Twice a week we are allowed out for a walk on parole about 40 at a time.</p>
