
Cape Town. I shall post this to Grandmother because if you have not left Sydney by any chance - you will read my scribble to Father. I can't write interesting letters because I'm not allowed to tell anything about what I see or what we do. This is really only to say I am well & as happy as anyone could be during this terrible war - & being away from all I love especially Lawrence - though I suppose considering the uncertainty of his future it is just as well that I am occupied - & have
