<p>27 Sun May<br/>A day of rest and beautiful weather</p>
<p>28 Mon May<br/>Anzac Div relieved by IMD  camped near Khan Yunus  Great air duel in evening  Taube drove one of our machines to earth</p>
<p>29 Tue May<br/>Very quiet and very dusty  nothing doing on the front  Aircraft very busy</p>
<p>30 Wed May<br/>Usual quietness  Aeroplanes very busy.  Feeling very seedy want a change</p>
