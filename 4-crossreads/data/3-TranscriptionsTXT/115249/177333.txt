 Naval Signal 
 From - Berrima 
 To - Sydney 
 Date - 7 9 14 
 Your 1720. &nbsp;From Brigadier. &nbsp;I consider Kanowna detachment as&nbsp;at present constituted and equipped unfit for immediate service and&nbsp;in view of todays  action  events and your action in ordering ship back&nbsp;to Townsville recommend disbandment and reorganisation if Admiral&nbsp;considers further troops necessary. Request that arrangements be made for Captain Fry loaned from  the  Berrima to Kanowna as adjutant return to this Force earliest opportunity
