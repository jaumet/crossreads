<p>Generally one or more of mothers bright and newsey letters await me when I return from the trenches for a few days and they form a regular welcome.<br/>I must close now in order to pack for another move "&agrave; bonne heure" tomorrow.<br/>With fond love to all at home &amp; next door<br/>Your loving son<br/>Eugene<br/>7th October</p>
