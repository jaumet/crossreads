<p>arrive at your next post, <strike>you</strike> but not till you have run into the flaming mouth of a battery or two, or having had to double back on your tracks, owing to your coming into contact with an impassable trench, &amp; all the while the stretcher cutting into your sholder &amp; worse than all this, having serverly shaken the patient whos suffering &amp; concern has been far more excited than your own.</p>
