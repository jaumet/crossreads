<p>cropped from the irrigated gardens which border the sweet water canal from Ismalia to Cairo &amp; make those striking patches of lined green against the dry light <strike>sand of the</strike> desert sand beyond.  <strike>Most of you possibly know Ismalia</strike> Ismalia is of course well known to the tourist and I feel I am becoming Kalaidoscopic if not Virginal.
