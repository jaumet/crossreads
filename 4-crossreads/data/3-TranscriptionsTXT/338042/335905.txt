 July 1918 
 July 1st Pack up costumes, properties etc. &amp; leave for Bertangles &ndash; We have had a very decent week at Vignacourt &ndash; Back to the bell tents in the woods &ndash; Bed made under the trees &ndash; July 2nd Rehearsal &amp; re-opening show in the "hangar" of 48th Squadron R.F.C. &ndash; At present very busy on long, new sketch &ndash; Heavy bombardments along the Front.
