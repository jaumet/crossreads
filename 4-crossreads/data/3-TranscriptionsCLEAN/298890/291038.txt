  
 13 Sunday At Miss Hodges till 8.30 then returned to 22 Harley Rd.  Typed all day. 
 14 Monday Wet drizzling day.  Went to dinner with Nancy at night at Lyceum Club as Mrs Bagis guest.  Literally wept with boredom  escaped at 9.30 
 15 Tuesday Office routine.   Did some of my own typing.  Matron Nye came & I took her to lunch at Minerva. 
 16 Wednesday Office routine.  Lunch at Minerva.
