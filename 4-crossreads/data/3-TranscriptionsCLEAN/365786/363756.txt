 1st October 1916. 
 Mrs Read, Wahroonga. 
 Dear Madam, The Council of the National Referendum Campaign desire to thank you very sincerely for your kind help in the strenuous Campaign in which you have so ably assisted.  They feel that the work done by the women interested in the movement is worthy of all praise. 
 Yours faithfully, H. Lamond. Joint Hon. Secretary.
