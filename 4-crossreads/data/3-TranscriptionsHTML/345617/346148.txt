<p>Aout 14<br/>Had a short march in the morning &amp; had a lecture by D.I. Lecturer on the new gas the Germans are using. Nothing else of importance.</p>
<p>Aout 15<br/>Had Coy route march today &ndash; distance about 14 miles. Got back about 3p.m. every body feeling very tired.</p>
