I don't wonder at the 3rd Brig sticking to their trenches so long, they had a Paradise" compared to what we had battled through in. We were not long in settling down to the old trench life again, it was noticeably colder now though the sun still continued to shine. On the 1st Nov there was a heavy gale which lasted 24 hours during which time it made a mess of Anzac beach, cases of biscuits bully potatoes were piled all over the place
