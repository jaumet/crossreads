<p>Sunday 31st. October<br/>Church parade again. Mock funeral of the chaplain afterwards. His preaching is vile, and he has been heard talking about the contents of letters posted. (He was appointed censor).</p>
<p>Everybody hung over the side nearly all the afternoon watching 'with bated eyes and bulging breath' (or some other such thing) another ship which kept in sight for a couple of hours.</p>
