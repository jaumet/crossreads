
March 1918Thursday 14
MessinesWent down forward, with Charlie Adey, and had a little while with Ken Gunn.By appearance of country around about the F.O.O. Fritz has been very busy with 4.2" and whizz bangs.Weather wet and indeed very sloppy..60 hopped the bags at 3.20am. raid very successful. 4 prisoners and 2 casualties.Came home from L'aison with Kerr, at 9.30 am. after a very quite night.Things in general fairly quiet.
