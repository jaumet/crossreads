<p>10 May 1916 Wed<br/>Saw Major Aidy who told me to rest for the day on account of my bad throat. Mr. Pearce packs up &amp; rejoins the 21st Battalion. Paid to-day &amp; won at few francs at the school. Clive Catt gets 7 Days field Punishment for smoking in billet. Feeling much better &amp; able to sit [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>11 May 1916 Thur<br/>Captain Lonnen &amp; Mr. Carrol leave the hospital &amp; rejoin unit extending me an open welcome to come &amp; see them at any time. Played banker went broke again. Received letters from Mater, Fred Cass, [Roy] Craig &amp; also book of verse from Dad. Mac leaves me to take charge of the Mumps wards. Took a walk into Steenwerck making several new acquaintances.</p>
