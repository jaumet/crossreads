<p>DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS</p>
<p>12 &ndash; Sircus &ndash; Bgde. sports 10th won Challenge cup marks were 10th 49, 12th 26, 9th 22, 11th 11.</p>
<p>15 &ndash; Sircus &ndash; Precautionary stage adopted, moved to La Krule and bivouacked.</p>
<p>16 &ndash; La Crule &ndash; Nothing happened.\</p>
<p>17 &ndash; Borre &ndash; Brgde. in line Btn. in Reserve T.L. in Hazebrouck.</p>
<p>15/25 &ndash; Borre &ndash; Training &amp; Fatigues.</p>
<p>25/26 &ndash; Merris &ndash; Bn. releived 11th Bn. casualties for month 204.</p>
<p>28 &ndash; Merris &ndash; Minor operations.  Transport first delivered rations to Coy. Head Quarters.  Congrats. from Army &amp; Corps Commanders.</p>
<p>July 1/5 &ndash; Merris &ndash; Still in line.</p>
<p>4/5 &ndash; Raid on our lines attempted and beaten back.</p>
<p>5/6 &ndash; Hondeghem &ndash; Bn. releived by 4th moved to camp near Hondegham.</p>
<p>8 &ndash; Hondeghem &ndash; Review parade in Honour of G.O.C.  3rd AI Brigde. &amp; Transport present.</p>
<p>21/22 &ndash; Merris &ndash; Bn. moved to line and releived 11th Bn.</p>
<p>29/30 &ndash; Merris captured, casualties light 187 prisoners.</p>
<p>30/31 &ndash; Hondeghem &ndash; Bn. releived by 8th Bn. moved to Hondeghem.</p>
<p>Aug. 1 &ndash; Hondeghem &ndash; Strength 45 officers 715 other ranks.</p>
<p>Heurighem &ndash; Left Hazebrouck 8.30.  Bn. left in Buss arrived 11 P.M.</p>
<p>6 &ndash; 8.15 &ndash; Wizernes &ndash; Marched to Wizernes and entrained for Somme.</p>
<p>7 &ndash; Pt. Remy &ndash; Detrained Pont Remy marched to Cocquerel.  Bn. left Cocquerel by bus transport by road.</p>
<p>Poulenville &ndash; Arrived Poulenville 1.00.</p>
<p>8 &ndash; Hammel &ndash; Left Poulenville 14.30 arrived at trenches near Hammel about 22.00.</p>
