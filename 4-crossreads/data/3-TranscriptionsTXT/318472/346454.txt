
17 August 1918SaturdayNothing of special import. Beautiful weather & everything going well. Expect to reach the Cape about Monday. A little war news but nothing of great importance We are making a little progress.
1918 August 18Sunday12 After Trinity
Nothing exciting happening. Usual Church Services in morning & "sing song" Service at night & afterward a discussion of ways & means of getting a living after returning home. O.C. Major Ross prisided & a few legislators on "would bes" spoke.
