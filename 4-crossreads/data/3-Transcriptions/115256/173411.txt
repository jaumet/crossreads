<p>Monday 7 June 1915</p>
<p>Xmas Eve &ndash; everything very quiet &ndash; no excitement &ndash; time camp fire concert.</p>
<p>Xmas Day &ndash; Holy Communion &ndash; prayers for my dear ones at home &ndash; very quiet Christmas &ndash; all our Xmas presents turn up at 7.30 in the evening.</p>
<p>29th &ndash; Embark on "Tunisian" for Egypt.  Hurrah!</p>
<p>New Year's Eve &ndash; everything quiet, <br/>New Year's Day, everything quite.<br/>Ismalia &ndash; Moascar.</p>
<p>Jan. 16 &ndash; Review by Gen. Sir Archibald Murray.</p>
