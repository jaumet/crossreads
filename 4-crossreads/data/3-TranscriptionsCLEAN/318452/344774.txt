 Wednesday 24th On Sunday last we had a parade at 10. I buried one of our men at 3, & at 5.30 I had a service in a hut. It was a splendid time & the singing was very fine. The hut was well filled During the service the 3 blasts of the whistle were sounded & all lights out, but, we had ours well screened & carried on   6 large bombs fell quite close it seemed, but we carried on.
