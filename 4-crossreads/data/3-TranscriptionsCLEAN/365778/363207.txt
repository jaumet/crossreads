  
 Sunday 15/7/17 Church Parade - Bas had cable from Doff - went for ride with Bas & Martin. Monday 16/7/17 Field Firing, new horse arrived named him "Banjo" - meeting of C.O.'s. Tuesday 17/7/17 Wrote to Doff. Company Training. Wednesday 18/7/17 Route March - Full order 111/2 miles - very good work Via Transloy - Hapincourt. Thursday 19/7/17 Attack practice - Trench to Trench conducted by Currie. Friday 20/7/17 Field Firing morning, night stunt meeting of C.O.s.
