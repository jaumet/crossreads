<p>Monday 13&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />Prof Watson called about 6 pm&nbsp;&nbsp;<br />Went with him to Sheppheards hotel &amp; dined with Barrett at 8.30 p.m. Home at 11-30 p.m.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Tuesday 14&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo this morning for two hours.&nbsp;<br />Letters from all at home&nbsp; Answd these letters by 2 A.M. 15-9-15&nbsp;</p>
<p>Wednesday 15&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />About the hospital all day&nbsp;<br />Work not being done to my satisfaction by my medical staff.&nbsp;</p>
