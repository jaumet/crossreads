<p>July 1918<br/>Friday 26</p>
<p>Ribemont. Bty Position<br/>Weather putrid very damp. Received a letter from Roy, also a phot of his highness.<br/>At 3.30 pm orders came to hand to proceed to a [indecipherable] 1800 yds behind the line. We were well established in a little trench dugout at [indecipherable] <br/>Met Harry Offer now a W.O. in the 106th Mobile Bdge of Artillery, also Con Tester and Simpson. Had a good old yap.</p>
