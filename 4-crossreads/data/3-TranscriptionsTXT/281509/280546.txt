 1916 November 19 Sunday 
 Rained all night, not so cold  yet bad enough.  B &amp; self go into Albert 2 &frac12; miles, in aft.. there being no parade.  Poke round, wrecked railway station cathedral etc.  Buy souvenir salt spoons. Band, drum &amp; fife in courtyard. Tommies give us tea &amp; we have tin pineapple &amp; condensed milk.  Home &amp; bed.  We get leave to Armiens tomorrow
