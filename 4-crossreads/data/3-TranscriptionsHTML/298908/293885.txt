<p>Saw in the papers today where the first lot of 1914 men have arrived home &amp; got a splendid reception &amp; here we are stuck in England yet.<br/>The "Advance party" went to London this morning to bring our ship to here she is now at "Tilbury Docks".<br/>Am on duty today, Coy. Orderly Sgt. so have to stop in Camp tonight.</p>
