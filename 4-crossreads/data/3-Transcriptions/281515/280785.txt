<p>Dec 26th<br/>a Holiday  nothing much doing only some of the boys got into the Guard Tent for Drunks</p>
<p>Dec 28<br/>Japanese Fleet of two cruisers two destroyers &amp; one Collier arrive, the officers come ashore &amp; are received with a guard of Honour,  The Japanese officers came &amp; had a look round Rabaul.  they seemed to be interested in everything they saw</p>
<p>Dec 29<br/>Japanese Fleet leaves quietly as they came in. I have often heard that they creep in here at night time now &amp; again to see that all is safe since our fleet went away.</p>
<p>Dec 30<br/>We have sports on the Parade ground G Coy win most of the events</p>
<p>Dec 31st   New years eve<br/>Thought of Home.  went to sleep about 10 pm, but was awakened by steamers in the Harbour blowing off their Whistles &amp; rifles &amp; revolvers being fired, Troops cheering &amp; niggers singing</p>
<p>Jan 1st 1915<br/>See over</p>
