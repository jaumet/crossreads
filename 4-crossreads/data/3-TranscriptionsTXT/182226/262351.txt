 Thursday 25 November, 1915 
 Sid had to meet Carrie &amp; take her out rowing. I went out to see Mac at Cattigera [?]. He &amp; Bernie both expect to be sent to convelescent camp shortly. 
 I played billiards after tea &amp; met Sid after he had left his club. 
 We slept at the Hotel as usual.
