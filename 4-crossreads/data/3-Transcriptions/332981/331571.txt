During the afternoon the Band gave a splendid performance, which reflects great credit on the Bandmaster, H. Cornish, who handled the players splendid. The Loco &amp; Traffic out victorious by several goals, and Corp. J. Fisher gave a good exhibition as a central umpire.</p>
<p>At 4.30 the match concluded with the usual cheers for both teams, and all hands returned to the Camp and at 5 pm, the bugle again rang out &amp; all adjourned into the Tents for a Tea, where another good time the Boys had.</p>
<p>At 6 pm the Evening Concert started, and the tents were crowded and each item was cheered, also the Band gave several good selections during the evening. Bread &amp; cheese, cakes, beer &amp; other drinks were served all round.</p>
