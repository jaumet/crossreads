<p>Saw Henry VIII's  armour &amp;  the spot where Lady Jane Gray  &amp;  all those poor creatures were beheaded also the Block  &amp; axe. Scarry The window marked 2 on No 1 Card is the one here open One of the Yeoman of the Guard gave us all the details about the various noted Prisoners. We next saw the Crown Jewels. My, they are just lovely as indeed they ought to be. In Beauchamp Tower we saw all the old armour etc right down to poor old Kitchener's sword. I saw some of the funniest guns   &amp; cannons imaginable. Some very old 1000 by date  1 found in Chine in 1841.</p>
