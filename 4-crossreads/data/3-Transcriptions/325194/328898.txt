<p>1.6.15<br/>been here in billets about 8 days, the trenches were fairly quiet a few left their beanis on the parapets a few wounded  Capt Judge, one of the old boys got his.<br/>Some bombardment last night expected to go in but did not don't feel up to much myself, got that way I don't much care which way she blows<br/>Billy Hughes visited us</p>
<p>2.6.16<br/>Nothing much doing went to tea at Sidonies wasn't very exciting</p>
<p>4.6.16<br/>Heavy bombardment on the line thought we would be called out but was not</p>
