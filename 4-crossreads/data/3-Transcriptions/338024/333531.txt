<p>27739<br/>Driver T. D.  Munro<br/>6th. f T. A. B. A. T. A.<br/>Abroad.</p>
<p>Nov.  20th. 1916. Maribyrnong Camp<br/>Reveille 3. 30 am.<br/>Left camp at 6am.  and marched to a scot Vale station.  Arrived there about 7 am and got special train to Port.  Melb.  (arr.  P. M.  about 8 am).  Embarked on S. S.  "Borda" (A30) at 10 am.<br/>Friends not allowed on pier till 12. 50 pm.<br/>Sailed at 1 pm</p>
