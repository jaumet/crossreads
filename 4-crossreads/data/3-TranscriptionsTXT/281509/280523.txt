 27 October 1916 Friday 
 Usual muddy duties,  Put in day, slithering up &amp; down road for our tucker, trying to keep warm round fire, gathering wood &amp; generally mooching round. A great batch of letters arrived &ndash; 17. all  redirected A.D.B.D. such a read &amp; how welcome they were. They are our chief delight
