
<p>May &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>12 SUN &nbsp;Dull day. Washing</p>
<p>3 Sergts came</p>
<p>down from guns. Fairly&nbsp;</p>
<p>quiet a few bombs at</p>
<p>night</p>
<p>13 MON &nbsp;Raining on &amp; off all</p>
<p>day &nbsp;Rec. letters from Tom</p>
<p>&amp; Peggy. Writing at night</p>
<p>Capt. Malley<sup>M.C.</sup> paid us a</p>
<p>visit. Very quiet.&nbsp;</p>
<p>14 TUES &nbsp;Posted letter &amp; photo</p>
<p>to Peggy. Fine day. Very</p>
<p>quiet. Planes active</p>
<p>Rumour out re pulling</p>
<p>out on 20<sup><u>th</u></sup>. Bombs at night</p>
<p>15 WED &nbsp;Fine hot day. Very</p>
<p>warm. Guns very active</p>
<p>Amm: going up.</p>
<p>Few bombs at night.</p>
<p>Quiet. Played 3<sup><u>rd</u></sup> Bty.</p>
