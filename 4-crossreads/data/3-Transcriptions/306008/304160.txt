<p>1914<br/>Dec 24 Th<br/>Christmas Eve<br/>Received 14/- in pay<br/>Crossed the Line<br/>Father Neptunes Ceremony</p>
<p>Dec 25 Fri<br/>Communio Service at 7 a.m.<br/>Church Parade at 11 a.m.<br/>Good tucker &ndash; plum pudding &amp; fowl<br/>Wrote letters<br/>First Christmas Day away from Home</p>
<p>Dec 26 Sat<br/>Sailed up coast of Ceylon in afternoon.<br/>Arrived in Colombo Harbour at 6 p.m.<br/>Ceylon coinage : Cents &amp; Rupees x 100 cents = 1 rupee.<br/>Postage on letter to Aust. 6 cents</p>
