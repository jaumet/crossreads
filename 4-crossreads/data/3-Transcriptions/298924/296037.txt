<p>Monday 24 February 1919<br/>Gen. Chauvel inspected the Division &amp; said the usual things &ndash; but still here &amp; the latest definite news is we sail on March 18 or 19 &ndash; that means arriving Australia end of April.  The wife goes in the first good boat available next month.<br/>I was Divisional Field Officer &ndash; cold much better now that the warm weather is here.<br/>Dolly &amp; child are well, love to all.  Your son, F.W.</p>
