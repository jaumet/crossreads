<p>[Transcriber's note: this is a collection presumably by Bungardy, of evidence and complaints including letters written by German internees and internee committees.  The documents were to be used to prompt an investigation into the treatment of the internees by Captain Hawkes who was in charge of the internees while they were at the Torrens Island camp in South Australia.  At the time of writing, the internees had been moved to the Holdsworthy camp in Sydney.]
