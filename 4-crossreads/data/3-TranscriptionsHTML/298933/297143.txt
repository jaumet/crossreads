<p>Centelles<br/>[April 1918]<br/>14 Sun<br/>Drinking Rum<br/>15 Mon<br/>After 4 hours heavy shelling we were forced to quit our quarters<br/>16 Tues<br/>B H Q in a chalk pit.General sent me back to Boves, living in a chateau<br/>17 Wed<br/>My home is a musty old cellar my mate is a good sort, Jack Trenbeth.The boys are having a hard time.</p>
