  
 July 18 -1918 
 Things are going along Smoothly  Just now at the present time we are out of the line with the 5 th  Brigade [indecipherable] While the main Body are in the front line  We are Camped on the Banks of the [Seine?] just outside Amiens and [it?] is an Ideal Camping Ground under very tall Trees  plenty of swimming & Boating on this [Bonney like?] stream. Went to field ambulance this morning for Drugs.
