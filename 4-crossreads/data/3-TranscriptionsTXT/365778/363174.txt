
Had sinner with Wisdom and went to a concert in Y.M.C.A. hut at night.
Sunday 11/2/17
Church Parade, re-allotment of Officers to Coy's, sore throat - wrote to Doff - had parcel from Doff Billies cake.
Monday 12/2/17
Meeting of C.O's at Bde H.Q. had letters from Doff & Nellie.
Tuesday 13/2/17
Orders received to move to SEVERN ELMS as Support Battn took over from 23rd relief finished at 8.30 pm.
Wednesday 14/2/17
Quiet day generally - had a game of cards at night - Battalion mostly on fatigues.
