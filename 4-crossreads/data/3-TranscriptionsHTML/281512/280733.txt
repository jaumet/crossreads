<p>18th October. Thursday.<br/>Goods accumulated &amp; have decided to return by the Staff train leaving on Saturday.</p>
<p>Had a look over the various places which have suffered the effects of the recent bombing. There is great talk of reprisals at present &amp; it really does appear as if our phlegmatic Govt are going to make an effort to satiate the strong public opinion.</p>
<p>19th October<br/>Lunched with Sir Douglas Mawson &amp; Lady Mawson at their flat &amp; had slack afternoon, having a look around. During the evening a raid warning was given &amp; most of the folk made for the tubes or basements. With a large party of visitors, I remained in the dining room of the Imperial Hotel &amp; had a very pleasant concert. I subsequently</p>
