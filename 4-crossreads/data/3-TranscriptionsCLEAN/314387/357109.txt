  
 July 24th Same as day before patrol & night. July 25th Same again went out in No Mans Land looking for body of dead German for indentification, relieved midnight by 2nd London Fusiliers. Marched back to Ballarat line.      July 26th In trenches all day nightime went up to front line carrying stoles mortars bombs for hop over 8th - 14th Brigades hopped over midnight look 600 yards casualities reported light - best mate killed.
