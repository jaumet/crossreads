  
 20 October 1915 Wednesday 
 Leave Helouan at 9 am  &  reach Zeitoun at 12. After dinner I am installed in a sleeping Hut with some 50 more of the 13th Bn ready to return to the front At 2 pm put in the time till 4.30 on the miniature Range. Very glad to be back at work again 
 1915 October 21 Thursday 
 Am orderly Sgt not much to be done. The 9/13 are in camp here but at present on a 3 day musketry course at Abbasia
