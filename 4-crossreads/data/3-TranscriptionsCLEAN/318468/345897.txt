News through today which appears to be correct that 3 British boats have been lost in the Dardanelles the "Indomitable" Invincible (Battle Cruisers) & Ocean - an old boat - beside a French boat.   Carried on with work as usual although it is Sunday (written in bed listening to the wind & rain driving onto the tent)
22-3-15   Nothing unusual today.   Work going on as usual.   Sapper Sutherland & I went with Col: Liverick & [indecipherable] Mather to inspect water supply   We expect to lay about 1/2 mile piping for supply shortly.
23-3-15.   No. 1 Sec:  had leave today   Cpl Bird Spr Cridland Gatty-Smith & I, had a stroll across the Island to the sea board in an Easterly direction.   Saw a couple of villages we had not seen before.    We also saw the wreck of the Destroyer that was lost a couple of nights ago.   R.E.s leaving to-morrow.  No. 3 back again but did no work.
