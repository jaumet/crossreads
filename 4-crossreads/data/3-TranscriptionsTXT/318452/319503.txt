
January 22 TuesdayDuring Last week Col Holden held a conference with the Chaplains at Salisbury (Thursday). It had snowed very heavily during the night before & it was very heavy walking to the station.We had lunch at the Nelson Temperance Hotel & engaged a room for the conference We discussed a scheme for meeting the needs of the Aust. at Horseferry Road by taking over the Wesleyan Church near by & fitting it out as a social room during the week & having evangelistic services on
