 9.7.18 Fine.  On job as usual and carried on filling holes till in some clod fights.  Remains of artillery dinner.  On guard at 5.  Match with drivers won by us, 20 runs on 1st innings.  Quiet guard and did a small amount of letter writing.  L. 
 10.7.18 On guard, some showers during day.  Off at 5 and down to drivers on bike.  Back and to canteen &amp; to cricket match v A.S.C. [Army Service Corps] drawn.  In evening heavy showers.  Moffitt back. 
 11.7.18 On to job as usual and filled in light railway till 1.  In aftn. cleaned up and prepared to go to St. Omer to-morrow.  Did a good deal of washing.  Saw finish of football against A.S.C., won by Artillery and had several games of tennis.  Read before going to bed. 
 12.7.18 Heavy rain in morning and heavy showers all day and sunny with a strong wind the rest of day.  Decided not to go to St. Omer and didn't go out to work.  Spent day reading and wrote two letters and dried clothes.  Moving to Light Brigade front to-morrow. 
 13.7.18 Out to work as usual at 8 and only No. 2 going out.  Worked till 11.30 filling in line and home to dinner and packed up and moved about 1.30.  Only 3 pontoons.  I took 9 men on to one pontoon and we moved to new place on left of Borre and off parallel road.  Living in fairly good dugouts with Edmonds.  Nothing doing for us but No. 1 out on supports &amp; lost coming home.  No. 4 on mines.  Read till bed time.  Fine but cloudy.
