 Out on station work today. Expect to push off tomorrow morning. We hear it is a route march to the firing line a distance of about 21 miles. We expect to do the journey in three days. 9.4.16 
 Left Wallon Cappell for Outtersteene, with full pack up. It was one of the heaviest marches we have had. A distance of about 12 miles. Arrived here about 12.p.m. 10.4.16 
 Billeted in small outhouse. Walked into Baileull
