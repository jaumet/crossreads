<p>13th Aug<br/>1 pm Entered a well sheltered harbour &amp; anchored[symbol] Presumed it is somewhere on N.W. end of Dutch New Guinea. Matunga came alongside &amp; they started taking all her cargo &amp; coal onboard. Crew of Matunga came onboard at 5 p.m.</p>
