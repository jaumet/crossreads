
[4th Sept 1916]I fulfilled the first requirements & six others were selected including Jimmy Hyde, both of whom were in the dispensary at Marseilles.Given charge of party by seniority under Capt Rhodes also travelling with us to No 2 Command Depot, Weymouth.Went by 12-10 pm train via Andover & Southampton West, at both of which places we changed.Arrived at Weymouth at 4 pm & after 21/2 miles march, reached the camp which is at Monte Video out near the sea west of Portland Bill, at tea time.
