<p>(40)<br/>Could you peep into that empty room there at rear of the narrow shop over the way, you could see the skeleton of a swinging cot, and by a touch you would find it yet able to rock on its pivots. Almost beneath it, amongst the scattered brickbats and powdered plaster on the floor, lies a great iron shell; unexploded.<br/>A dud<br/>Its copper band bears the groove marks of the gun which fired it. Its brazen nose, almost undamaged, is red with brick dust.<br/>You wonder why it did'nt explode.<br/>I wondered at that.<br/>We often wonder and marvel at such things</p>
