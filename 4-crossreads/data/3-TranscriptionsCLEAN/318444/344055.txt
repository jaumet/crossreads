
Mon Aug 11th:-Back again to Warminister & Sutton Veny camp.
Fri 22nd Aug:-London again. This time on marriage leave. So I'm going to do the trick at last & to a girl whom I've known since my first furlough in 1916 so I've had plenty of time for reflection. "Here's Luck".
Tues Sept 2nd 1919:-"Hitched up" well & truly today at St Barnabas' Church East Dulwich, London. "The deed is done".
Sun Sept 21st:-Left by the 2.40 pm from Paddington station to Warminister.After my kits &  mail as from now I'm finished with camps & will be on indefinite leave till "me & the missus" are put on to a boat.
Mon Sept 22nd:-Returned to London. Domiciled at 3 Daneville Road Camberwell S.E.
