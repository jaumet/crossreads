 Fri. Nov. 9th: - Relieved by 1st Bn. &amp; went to Anzac House on Anzac Ridge, a concrete "Fritz" observation point &amp; stayed night. S.M. Bock &amp; Corp Gillam of "D" Coy were "blown-out" at back of the "House" &amp; Corp Searle badly wounded. 
 Sat. Nov. 10th. Relieved here by "Tommies" &amp; went back to old "pill-box" on Westhoek ridge &amp; stayed night. 
 Sun. No. 11th: - Left Westhoek &amp; passed through Ypres to "Vancouver Camp" near Vlamertinghe. This is the finish of our part in the 3rd Battle of Ypres. We are starting tomorrow on a 3 day's march back "somewhere". 
 Tues. Nov. 13th. Left "Vancouver Camp" by motor &ndash; Bn. marched - &amp; passed through Poperinghe, Watou-Abeele, Boeschepe &amp; Berthen &amp; billeted for night outside of Shaexken.
