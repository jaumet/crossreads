<p>October 1918.<br/>Tuesday 1</p>
<p>In rear of Hargicourt (advanced to rgt. of Bellicourt)<br/>Weather fine and clear had a little trouble with lines and did 5 to 8 am shift.<br/>French service time came into force today.<br/>Battery moved forward to right flank of Bellicourt, came through village at the "tuite".<br/>Established in a pill box, but Peter objected so I suppose we shall have to move.<br/>Had the 10 to midnight. Feel very tired "sissoir". Fritz shelling pretty regularly.</p>
