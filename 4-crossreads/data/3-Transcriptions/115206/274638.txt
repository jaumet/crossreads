<p>March 1918<br/>Saturday 9</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Spent the morning from 4 to 6 am on watch and from that hour until noon in bed. Fritz shelled O.P. and front line fairly heavily all day.<br/>Smith nearly sniped with a 4.2 H.V. whilst enroute to the line.<br/>Paid a visit to Pip's crowd and had a fairly decent time.<br/>9.45 pm Fritz doing a fair amount of shelling on dump.<br/>11 pm.Clocks advanced 1 hour. Fritz still having a shoot. And is expected to come over in lump tonight.</p>
