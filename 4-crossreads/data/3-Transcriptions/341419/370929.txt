<p>Jericho Jane did not fire this morning and I think that it is gone.  The turks are now evacuating Shunet Nimrin &amp; Es Salt &amp; there is a rumour.</p>
<p>25.9.18<br/>10 p.m.<br/>I was interrupted just there by the news that we were going that same evening &amp; thus I had no more time to write.</p>
<p>We set out that evening &amp; reached Shunet Nimrin at about 3 a.m. next morning, here we stayed until 11 a.m. when we entered the hills.  The fires lit by the evacuating Enemy were still burning &amp; on turning a corner in the wadi we were delighted to see Jericho Jane lying on her side in the stream with a dead Turk alongside her.</p>
