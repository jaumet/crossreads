<p>Cup of Tea &amp; had my rations &amp; then a quiet stroll until 2.40 Pm when the Train left for St Omer via Les Fontinettes &amp; Audruicq arriving at St Omer at 4.15 Pm when I signed off for the Day.</p>
<p>Sunday 14th.<br/>I obtained permission for a day at home, so after Breakfast I did some Washing &amp; then went out to the A.V.C. Barracks for a Hot Shower Bath &amp; then into the Estaminet for a Drink &amp; a chat for an hour, I was invited by my Lady Friend if it was fine to promenade with her &amp; the Children after Dinner to Clairmaire where they intended visiting friends, but it came on to Rain just after Dinner so I did not venture out but spent the Afternoon Reading &amp; Writing until after Tea when I went for a Stroll with a chum up the Town &amp; into the Park for a while returning later on down past the Building which was used a No.10 Stationary Hospital which was Bombed some few weeks back, This Building is very large &amp; is 5 storey high &amp; were the Bomb or</p>
