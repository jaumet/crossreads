<p>17:5:18<br/>On the last day on which I wrote we reached Talut ed Dune, a spot -  &amp; a very dirty one at that &ndash; in the hills about a mile or two in the hills towards Jerusalem.<br/>Here we languished &amp; Hope nearly died but finally on the 15th we left for Jerusalem which we reached just as it was breaking dawn after a very tedious &amp; slow trek. The next evening we started down the Bethlehem Road passed through Bethlehem &ndash; the modern Brit Lahm</p>
