<p>29 September, Monday</p>
<p>Go to meet Doris &amp; Maisie. Wait 35 minutes. Have no Tea. We go to theatre. See unfrequented love of Japanee boy who dies looking after child of his lost love. See Annette Kellerman in Queen of the Sea. Just catch 10 pm boat.</p>
<p>30 September, Tuesday</p>
<p>Further treatment met George Fernif. See N but I have to leave my seat for an autie Digger on boat. Music for Dad &amp; Mum &amp; write letters.</p>
<p>M 26</p>
