<p>103<br/>to carry the additional weight, I shared the contents out with a number of comrades-in-arms from 5 and 6 platoons; for which they tanked me.</p>
<p>Proceeding on our way by the aid of motor buses, we eventually reached the billetting area Fleuselles.</p>
<p>It was just the usual style of French village, a church and a main street although with side streets slightly larger than others that I have been billetted in afterwards. Our quarters were the outhouses of a farm house which were lined with bunks with were bottoms badly in need of repair.</p>
