<p>SATURDAY, AUGUST 23, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine but looked as if it may come</p>
<p>up rain later. Went round my</p>
<p>place with Clyde &amp; Olga &amp; Linda &amp; Al</p>
<p>in big car in morning and I</p>
<p>rode round in afternoon. All</p>
<p>the people got away home safely.</p>
