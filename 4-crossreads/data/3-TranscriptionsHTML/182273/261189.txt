<p>1918&nbsp;&nbsp; December</p>
<p>12 THUR&nbsp; Put in applications for leave.</p>
<p>Raining on &amp; off all</p>
<p>day. Half passes finished by 8 PM</p>
<p>Rec. mine 10.15 caught</p>
<p>10.32 PM from St Budeaux &amp; the 12.5</p>
<p>from Plymouth to London. went</p>
<p>with J.W.C. &amp; H Cunningham</p>
<p>13 FRI&nbsp; Arr Paddington 6.45. Left Euston</p>
<p>9.10 am home 3. PM. Had walk</p>
<p>round at night. Went to</p>
<p>Waterloo.</p>
<p>14 SAT&nbsp; Fine day. Out with the</p>
<p>boys&nbsp; Went to &#39;Metropole&#39;</p>
<p>at night to see &#39;Tainted Goods&#39;</p>
<p>very good show.</p>
