
Sunday 1 October 1916Church Parade & General inspection by Gen. Moore.  D., B., E. & I go to Shipton but fail to get to Salisbury.  We motor to Tidsworth for Supper & walk Home.  We tour village for grub.
Monday 2 October 1916No parade morning & route march in afternoon.  Wrote Dad, Mum & George, Marg., Maynie, Marie, Jennie.  I get a change of Boots.  Recd. letter Marie 17/9/16.
Tuesday 3 October 1916We route march to Cholderton & see a 50 Dennis Motor Transport.  Heaps of drill all afternoon.  Go to bed very early as we are all tres fatigue.
Wednesday 4 October 1916I am on Y.M.C.A. Fatigue.  I meet Dougall Carroll & mud-guts.  Route march in afternoon, go to bed early.  Write to Bess.
Thursday 5 October 19163 route marches.  We get leave.  I wash clothes.  See Tocooloo Roberts at Cholderton & visit old church.  Wrote Jennie & Roy.
Friday 6 October 1916No route march after 9 till 2 p.m.  Very rainy.
Saturday 7 October 1916Ern goes on Guard.  D., B., E. & I go to Shipton & return to Y.M.C.A.  We have supper on bacon & eggs.  Mick Barr goes to France & bids Bill Good-Bye.
