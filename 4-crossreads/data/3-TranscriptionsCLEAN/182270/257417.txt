 TUESDAY, APRIL 15, 1919. 
 46 
 Fine & warm we got inside the 
 Heads about 6.30 AM & anchored 
 off Clifton Gardens. Got all sorts 
 of roumers about quarintine. We 
 moved to quarintine bay about 
 6.30 pm & stayed there for the 
 night. It was not a very warm 
 welcome home, but still it's 
 good to be home. 
 Days run 214 miles making 
 a total distance of 12761 miles 
 traveled in 46 days.
