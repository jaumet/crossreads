<p>June 1918<br/>Saturday 15</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather wet, during morning fine and clear, in afternoon. <br/>Had a very enjoyable concert at W.L. a very good tenor,<br/>and ebcontionist.  The Star items. O'Kelly the tenor,<br/>sang several very sweet songs. Polished up old<br/>"Acks" bit and irons "Smithy  still feeling bad.<br/>Had a run to Bussy; and saw folks there.<br/>Second divvie pioneers and 5th Bdge came out. Met<br/>Harry Rich now Lt  Rich, - congratulated him on his<br/>progress. <br/>Received 3 letters from Australia  20/4/18</p>
