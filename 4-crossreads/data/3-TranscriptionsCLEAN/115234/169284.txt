 Prospect by no means alluring. 
  12 th    Dec . 
 Had a hell of a job last night.  Was one of a fatigue party carrying Stokes Shells to a trench mortar position behind the front line.  They weighed 54 lbs each & we had to carry em a mile over shell holed & muddy ground.  To add to the enjoyment Fritz used to periodically play a tune on his machine gun & the bullets hummed over our heads like bees
