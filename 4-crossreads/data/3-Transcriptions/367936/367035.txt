<p>Tuesday 5 September 1916<br/>All hands down swimming bar Bill West, he on day stableman.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5 September 1916<br/>Wrote to Mother (15).<br/>Got a parcel from Telarah.  Back scratcher in it caused great excitement.<br/>Went over to 6th in evening to see Colin but he was at Cantarah;  heard that Leycester's wound was alright.</p>
