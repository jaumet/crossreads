  
 At  Sea   3rd Nov. 1917 
 From. No 3495 Trooper F  J W Smith 30th Reinforcements 1st L H Regiment, Australian Imperial Forces on Active Service Abroad. 
 Thanks very kindly for the comforts so kindly forwarded by you through Sgt Kitching. They are comforts I needed very much being a married man with family of 3 children I was unable to purchase myself  again I thank your Committee for their kindness Frank Smith
