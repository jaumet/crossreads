<p>Friday 2 November 1917<br/>T. 99.8, P. 76, T. 99.6<br/>To have gr. I cal., no sleep but drowsy.<br/>Had P. of brom &amp; chloral. hydrate 10 p.m. &amp; got about 4 hrs. sleep but oh, for a natural one again.<br/>Day like all other days now.  Everyone kind but I feel more than ever the "thin cold wall" which I believe will forever separate us from the English &amp; visitors are forbidden me so except for Sisters Lancer &amp; Rogerson the day is a lonely one.<br/>Marge is sending a cable for me in case the Military have sent one to my dear little Mith &amp; she might be worried.</p>
