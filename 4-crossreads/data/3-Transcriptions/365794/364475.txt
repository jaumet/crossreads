<p>Thursday 14 March 1917 [1918]<br/>Letter from Marge, veils received &amp; ink not Kodak paste.<br/>Cobs is now a "sister".<br/>Miss Conyers is in Egypt, came on last boat.<br/>Life is very trying these days, worry from home, no work in the wards &amp; except for C. Grant not a congenial soul to work through the long day with.</p>
