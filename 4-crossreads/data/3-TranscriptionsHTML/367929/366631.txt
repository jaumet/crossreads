<p>May and June 1915<br/>31 Mon<br/>Yesterday quieter. I placed C Sqd on Popes Post in right section.<br/>Swim in morning. Met Clarence Pyke.</p>
<p>1 June Tuesday<br/>Quiet last night but we were to have made an attack on Quinns and Popes. Found 1 off 1 sgt 2 cpl and 24 men as picquet in NZ Valley. 4 wounded</p>
