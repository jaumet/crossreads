<p>We have had a concert tonight, it appears to be a very difficult thing to get a party together good enough to amuse the boys &amp; this one appears to be very little more successful than its predecessors, but our orchestra is improving rapidly &amp; they keep things moving</p>
<p>There was some rare fun today with the pillow fighting, if it wasn't for these welcome little; breaks we should all be bored to death with a vengeance</p>
