
F 4 SunWrote Nette. P.C. Clean bike all afternoon.  T & I walk to D'ton. We try to meet [Q ins?] wife my looks are beautiful & we sit & rest. Flo. Looks like a Picture when we see her in the lane. We talk to the old Washer woman in Water St.
W 5 Mon[indecipherable]. I go to Sarum, to Baseball Match & Sports after Home to No. 13 to Tea. [do?] 6 rg are ok. We play all sorts of games & have a real jolly High time the like of which we have not seen since the war-
F 6 Tues.Nothing doing. Come Home & generally tidy up. Tap is nauseous. Tom & all of us talk until lights out after Top gets it off His chest.
F 7 WedReal Hot dopey day. Send write to Aunt Annie. Recd. Letter from M.R.L. Write to Dad Maisie
F 8 ThRecd letter from Dulce Butell. Go to Pictures & intend going for a run but go off to sleep & am only awakened at 10 pm.
Erye & Top. Very Dull & dopey.
F 9 FriRecd 2 letters from Home. Marion Clarke & Ida Walter. Sew & go for a run around oval. Lovely Day
F 10 SatPack Bag & sew Breeches. Take sewing out onto tennis Lawn while E & T. play Tennis. Go for a run afterwards. Recd letter from Strobmugh re song. Get Tunic from Tommies at 2/6
