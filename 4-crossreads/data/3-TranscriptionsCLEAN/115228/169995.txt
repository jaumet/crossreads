Oct 28th:  The Brigade moved out at 4.30 p.m. & arrived at Kalassa about 9 p.m. where we camped the night.
Oct 29th:  Moved out to Asluj a further 8 miles where three mounted divisions were encamped ready to "kick off" for the attack on Beersheba.
