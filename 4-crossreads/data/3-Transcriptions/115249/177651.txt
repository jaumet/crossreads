<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Flag</p>
<p>To - Brigadier</p>
<p>Date - 20.9.14</p>
<p>Time - 9.15</p>
<p>I should like Armourer Johnson now doing duty at Herbertshohe to rejoin his ship the Encounter. &nbsp;This rating is much needed in Encounter. &nbsp;An armourers crew could be sent to relieve him and is quite reliable to do the&nbsp;duty required of him onshore (2115)</p>
