 July 1918 Saturday 20 
 Frechencourt. Major asked for volunteers from gunners as spooks and received no reply to his most enticing question. He was slightly disgusted.  Ran round to Bussey - la Daours  and saw friends had a bon spread in honor of my return. Weather very damp, paid 40 francs and had a little flutter on the cards. Received a new tunic from Q.M. and won a fine example of an Army bycyle.
