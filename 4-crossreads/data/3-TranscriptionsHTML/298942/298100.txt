<p>Sunday 5 May</p>
<p>This <strike>morning</strike> afternoon Cecil Rogers (who has acquired a reputation for "saloaping") &amp; I went into Fletre to see what there was to get.  Fritz shells the village with heavy stuff every day, but he had a good smack at it in the morning so we decided to chance it.</p>
