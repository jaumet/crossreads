<p>later. Dinner at night is at &frac14; to 6 &amp; then back to office at 6.30, where I stay always till 8 &amp; often to 9 or 9.30. Every day is about the same except Tuesdays &amp; Fridays when I am on leave from 2 p.m. for practically the rest of the day. The work is not heavy nor laborious and often there is practically nothing to do except <u>to be there on the spot</u> &amp; fag French with plenty of interruptions.<br/>I believe I am getting homesick. I don't see much of the other chaps &amp; the lack of civilian social life is rotten especially when you can see it going on around you and are prevented from participating o account of the uniform. Fancy being a permanent soldier!<br/>Jan 24"  (Monday)<br/>Number of patients in Hospital to-day</p>
