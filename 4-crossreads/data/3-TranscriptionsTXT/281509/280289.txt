
7 March 1916Shrove Tuesday
Heavy continuous rain up till eleven o'clock. Bob & self detailed for stretcher party for day. Tony for washing party. Send Mary & Ess wires cancelling parade. 10th of 6th & 4th of 8th Reinforce. leave for parade & embarkation next day. Down town again in aft. Tony & self have tea at Majestic & see the waitress again. Buy present for H's. I go to Hewletts to tea. Ess sick. After call at Palser's get this pen presented to me. Home at 11.30. Meet Alexander at Strathfield station home with him
