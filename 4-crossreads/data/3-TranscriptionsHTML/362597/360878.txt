<p>We came through Hell's Gate at 7 pm. There are a string of Islands strung out across the water from Arabia to Africa &amp; we passed on the southern side of the Island of Perim. There is a naval station on Perim &amp; our wireless sent a message &amp; received one in answer.</p>
