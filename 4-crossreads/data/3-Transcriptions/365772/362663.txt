<p>Lemnos<br/>23.9.15<br/>My dearest Sister,<br/>Your very welcome letter of the 8.8.15 to hand yesterday also one a few days ago telling me about Nurse Herbert &amp; the sheepskin vest. I am waiting for it to come along now, it will be just the thing for the winter at Gallipolli, we are in for an awful time when the winter starts properly we are getting a taste of what is to come now, bitterly cold winds &amp; cold rain.<br/>You know it snows when things are cooled up properly. I never did like</p>
