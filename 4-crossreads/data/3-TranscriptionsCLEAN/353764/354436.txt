Needless to say they got the worst on the lines & there was a good deal of merriment over it, as they handed them to us a month ago.I nearly met with an accident this evening whilst riding to Zeitoun.  I was going along quietly when a Motor Despatch rider dashed past & my horse took fright & bolted.  Luckily he made for the Desert & I was able to pull him up after a while.  This is the second time he has cleared out with me & in future he is going to get a liberal dose of "Spurs".
All the first Year Medical graduates have been offered their discharge & it makes us feel homesick to see them getting ready to sail for Bonny N.S.W.Now & again we get very homesick & would give a lot to be able to go home.  The weather has suddenly cooled off again which means more sickness.
