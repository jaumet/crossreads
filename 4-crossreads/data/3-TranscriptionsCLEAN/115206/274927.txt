  
 December  1918. Wednesday 25 
 Port Said. H.S."Karoola." Weather fairly decent. Berthed and coaling still. Had a fine xmas dinner, pork and Duck with plum duff to follow. For tea we had salmon, green peas, potatoes with jelly and tinned peaches. Left Port Said at 6.30 pm. Passed Kantara at 9.55 and Fedan at 11pm. Passed Ismailia at 11.30
