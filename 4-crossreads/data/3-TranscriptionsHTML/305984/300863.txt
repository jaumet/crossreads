<p>10/1/18<br/>Raining today  flea inspection  (Boss  promoted</p>
<p>11/2/18<br/>Raining nothing to note plenty of fleas</p>
<p>12/2/18<br/>Still raining off &amp; on   News of German at peace with Ucrean of Russia  promises of more food to the public.  no news of the strike movement.</p>
<p>13/2/18<br/>Still raining &amp; the fleas worse  fire parade to this afternoon  no news of any description</p>
<p>14/2/18<br/>Nothing of interest today</p>
<p>15/2/18<br/>Letter from dear old Nellie Farrell.  Jack Aston card from Mother  Xmas.</p>
<p>16/2/18<br/>Very sharp frost again to day &amp; [indecipherable] beautiful walk</p>
