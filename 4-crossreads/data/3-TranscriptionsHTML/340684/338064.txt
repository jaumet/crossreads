<p>337<br/>Royal Manchester Eye Hospital<br/>Manchester 6th July 1915</p>
<p>Dearest Joker and Bobbie<br/>Many thanks to you both for your kind letters &ndash; Perky will have given you all my news &ndash; I was awfully pleased to see her. We had a great time together &ndash; I enjoyed my lunch last Sunday more than I can ever remember  having enjoyed a meal before &ndash;</p>
