18th. Got away at 8 oclock this morning Eve Alice came down to the station with me. I arrived at Charing Cross to time went by tube to Paddington got my parcel out into the train which was a very fast one the Penzance express a non stop to Westbury 120 minutes run good going, the country looks splendid on the way down, we also passed hundreds of Tanks at some of the great munition works as for shells well there were miles of them aeroplanes racing the train all the way down, caught the Warminster train soon was there into a taxi with some officers who were going to the Camp so I just arrived back in time for dinner after a pleasant little 48 hours here I am now at a little table thinking over my experiences.
