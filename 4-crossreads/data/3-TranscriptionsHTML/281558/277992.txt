<p>Thursday May 17th</p>
<p>Perham Downs : rainy : reveille 6 am march 6.30 am : cookhouse fatigue all day : got a pair of sox from Bel &amp; a few letters : wrote at night</p>
<p>Friday May 18th</p>
<p>Perham Downs : dull : reveille 6 am : march 6.30 am : fell in 8.30 in full marching order for a route march : almost to Andover &amp; back about 12 miles : fairly hard : went to Ludgershall with Clarrie after tea</p>
