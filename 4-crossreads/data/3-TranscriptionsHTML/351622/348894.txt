<p>23 September 1916 Sat<br/>Went up to Valmertinghe to get two or three general lumber loads of brick from the old ruins of the town. Belgium who spoke seven languages Sgt Christmas treats all hands. Played soccer in the afternoon.</p>
<p>24 September 1916 Sun<br/>Started work with Mat Baty in a very easy job not much to do. In the afternoon went with a despatch down to head Quarters at Poperhinge</p>
