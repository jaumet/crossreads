30th Continued 217M.
We 3rd party re-embarked at 5-30pm., all on ship at 7pm., sailed out of Barrier at 9-30, slept on after deck on duty. Weather cloudy but fine, extremely hot on march. Lights out after 10pm.
May 31st N.N.W. M.303
Relieved from Guard at 9-30. Muster Parade called same time, remained on deck but not on duty till 11am. Retire. Sighted high mountains on starboard at 8am. 9-30 natives in Cat - ? in all directions about 5 miles off shore, left them behind after noon, land in sight all day. Lecture Sec. Cmdrs. Good Breeze cool deck, hot below. 	,
