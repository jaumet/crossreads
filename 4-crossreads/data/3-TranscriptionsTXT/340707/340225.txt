It is this system of fleecing by ones own countryman I cannot understand. A foreigner has some right in his own country. Just imagine 875 f to verify a signature the work of 1 minute & work which any fool could do. The good that these institution do is annulled by the extortions they make.
I then make my way to rue Grenelle where M. le Ministre has his bureau at 110. The building is absolutely destitute of human beings. Knowing where to go I enter a large room which a flunkey possesses to himself & leave my papers & call again in 15 days.
