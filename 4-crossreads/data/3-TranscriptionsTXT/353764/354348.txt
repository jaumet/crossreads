 Monday 6th.12. 15. Still hot.  Passed "Line" 12.30 pm Sighted Ship but too far away to see who she was. We are now right in Eastern Waters &amp; consequently our danger of Submarines commences. Name of Ship painted out &amp; also Signs on Port &amp; Starboard sides of Boat Bridge covered over (A34).  Raining hard off &amp; on Tropical Showers. 
 Tuesday 7. 12. 15 Nothing much doing.  Weather very hot, &amp; playing up with our fellows. 10 in Hospital with Sunstroke. Squall rising this afternoon accompanied by terrific rain &amp; wind. 
 Wednesday 8.12.15 Still very hot.  Held Sports &amp; Concert.  Two Reps.  A.S.C. again successful. Turner won on knock-out from Woods F.A. Charlsworth won on points from Phillpots B.A.C. Concert again held in the evening &amp; proved very attractive.
