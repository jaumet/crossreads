 1917 Wednesday 21 Feb. 
 Not much improvement last night &amp; today. Its a crook place to be crook in. This is when one misses home  The boys attention is rough but kindly. My bingy rejects all food.  The Doc. has a look at me on the afternoon orders me back to Edward's Post. Ticketed Gastric Enteritis. Arrive back at Edward's Post about 4 p m found Ridgway &amp; Sergt Clifton had been sent down to Bazentin.  Hunt keeps me here &amp; doses me up.  Turn in to bunk in dugout &amp; put in most joy.
