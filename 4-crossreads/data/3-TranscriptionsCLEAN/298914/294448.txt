
3-1-17Cold bleak day with rain.4-1-17Move to El Arish-Birzekari and erect again.  Cold day and very bleak.5-1-17Sun actually shines all day.  Heated arguments over cooking in which AEH shows his point to his disadvantage by falling in the estimation of others.  Missen recalled.  Apply for transfer to R.F.C.6-1-17Bright day.  Missen called to W2 leaving us short handed.  Taube over.7-1-17Two Taubes bomb rail head & kill 26 natives & wound 38, Harold Menzies visits me. [indecipherable] Taubes come over at 9.15 pm and bomb the El Arish Wadi, thinking that the troops were on their way out to Rafa as had been arranged but cancelled later.  Taubes use their machine guns & drop 25 bombs, it being bright full moon.  Not one hit.  Anti aircraft guns try to hit him.8-1-17Leave Zekari for W2.  Taube over and turned away.  W2 leave for Rafa on horses taking a pack set and a trench set.9-1-17Taube bombs El Arish with 5 bombs.10-1-17W2 returns, trench set broken by horse knocking key off.11-1-17Nothing doing.12/1/17Go to El Arish Wadi to see the Rafa Prisoners.  Two Taubes bomb Arish during the afternoon.13-1-17Two Taubes over but kept well off14-1-17Hostile planes over but kept well back by anti guns.  Mail day
