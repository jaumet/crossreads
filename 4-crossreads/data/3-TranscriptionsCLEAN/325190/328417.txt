The weather here has, on the whole, been rather wet since I arrived.  Now & again a couple of days running have been fine, & on account of the tremendous contrast between such weather, & the other awful dull heavy wet leaden morbidly miserable stuff called weather,  was  made me quite flamboyantly light hearted.
