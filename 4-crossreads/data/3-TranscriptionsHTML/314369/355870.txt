<p>7 August 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Improve position taken up  &amp;  see Gurkas at work doing same on our right<br/>Main position appears to be far in advance of us  &amp;  we can see the Turks on the run from N.Zs   &amp;  Tommies<br/>Move into Bivouac in a sheltered Gully &amp;  settle down for the day</p>
<p>1915 August 8<br/>Sunday<br/>10th After Trinity</p>
<p>Pulled up about 2 am   &amp;  get into a position to prevent the Turks making a turning movement<br/>About 6 am the 15th   &amp;  16th Bats make a charge on Hill 971 but lose hundreds &amp;  have to retire<br/>We keep busy improving our trench  &amp;  they put a M.G. in right near us.<br/>We have to relieve 'D' Coy some distance further along the firing line<br/>Work watches all night</p>
