<p>16.4.18<br/>O.C. in company with C.O. M.G. Batt. visited Bde. Hdqrs. with reference to dispositions of guns.  Final dispositions act as follows:-<br/>Six (6) guns for direct fire in support of front line.  Two (2) groups of 4 guns each in J.11.c. and J.5.c. with barrage tasks for protection of Marett Wood Spur, and direct tasks in the event of enemy penetration.<br/>Two (2) guns held as fire reserve in J.10.d.  19 Other Ranks reported for duty in the line.<br/>The following targets were engaged at irregular intervals throughout the night &amp; morning, 1600 rounds were expended:-<br/>(1)  Battery Position K.7.b.2.  <br/>(2)  Streets, Ville-Sur-Ancre.  <br/>(3)  Sunken Road K.1.b.05.40.  <br/>(4)  Cemetery Ville-Sur-Ancre K.1.a.90.70.  <br/>(5)  Sunken Road K.1.a. &amp;amp. c.  <br/>(6)  Trenches K.7.b.10.75.  <br/>(7)  Area E. of Ville-Sur-Ancre.<br/>Enemy artillery opened harassing fire about 8.30 p.m. on the road round Treux and Buire, about 20 rounds 5.9.  Enemy quiet remainder of day.  Enemy Machine Guns more active than usual.  Enemy Aircraft, Nil.</p>
