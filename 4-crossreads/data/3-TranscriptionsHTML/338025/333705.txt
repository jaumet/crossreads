<p>Tuesday 5th June 1917<br/>Aveluy nr Bouzincourt  .<br/>Inspections of 2nd Div. Artillery by General Birdwood this morning.<br/>Our O.C. (Leut.Gouldin) thanked the boys the way they worked on their harness etc. and the splendid way they carried out their work during the Review.</p>
