 Sept 26th There is a sudden rumour afloat that the Brigade is going to Kantara next thursday  &amp;  from there to &amp;  Maadi to Heliopolis y Zeitun S upper Egypt to try conclusions with the Senussi once again. It seems to have a sort of  indefineable sense of being right somehow but as yet I can get no reliable information concerning it.
