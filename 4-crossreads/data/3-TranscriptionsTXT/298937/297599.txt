 Wednesday 14th June 1916 
  To  Yesterday we were paraded in front of a clothing board &amp; there a note was made of any kit required. This morning early we went on the usual route march into the desert, &amp; were inspected there by the camp commandant.  He told us that we should be moving off this week.
