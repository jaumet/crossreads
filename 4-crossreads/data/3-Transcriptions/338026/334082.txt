<p><u>On board. H.M.T.S. "NESTOR."</u><br/>Hospital Ship.</p>
<p>[15 Sun]<br/><u>very heavy showers of rain</u></p>
<p>Issued with 2 suits pajamas, 1 flannel undershirt &amp; underpants &amp; 1 shirt, pair canvas shoes, 2 cakes soap, tooth soap, &amp; HAT. writing paper, socks (1 pr) shaving soap &amp; handkerchief from Red + in pillow cover.</p>
<p>16 Mon. 7am  Mine sweepers put out. (Gibralta)</p>
<p>17 Tues  9am. Called in at Gibralta &amp; left again at 10-30am.<br/>"Nestor" doing 14 &frac12; [knots?] 1,500 troops aboard.</p>
<p>[18 Wed]<br/>Boxing tournament and Concert at night.<br/>Lovely weather and sea very calm<br/>Issue of chocolate from Australian Red Cross<br/>10pm Cable of mine sweeper broke so brought on deck</p>
