
3rd Aust C.C.S.B.E.F.France20.1.17
My dear Mother   &  Sisters,
Last night I got two letters from home. Alice's 20th Nov   &  Belle's 27th, You cannot imagine how upset I was to hear of poor little Eddy's illness. I do hope the darling was not very ill   &  has quite recovered. What an anxious time it must have been for  you all.
