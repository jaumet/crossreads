<p>1915<br/>June 13 Sun.<br/>Spent the afternoon writing letters home in the Y.M.C.A. tent here.<br/>Together with Pte. Lumsdaine, who works in the Office, rigged up beds out in the garden close up beside a Statute of some female surrounded by lions or other insects &ndash; fine sleeping with the star-lit sky as a canopy in this land of the Pharaohs.<br/>In the evening attended service conducted by 2 Y.M.C.A. men.</p>
