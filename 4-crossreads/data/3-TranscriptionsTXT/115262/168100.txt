 Thursday 29 November 1917. 
 On picquet till 6 pm. 
 More parcels arrive  again . this time 2 for me. One from &quot;home&quot; &amp; one from Joy. Contents very appreciable. 
 Reinforcements 15 for our unit arrive. 
 A.S.C. dump shelled again. 
 Taubes visit us but no bombs dropped. 
 Watered camels &amp; filled fantasies (in 22 minutes.)
