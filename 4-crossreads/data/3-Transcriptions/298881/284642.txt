About 9.30 a.m. after a little trouble I presented my list of articles to the waiter in the canteen, collected my dry goods there and then proceeded to the market square to buy some vegetables.  The square presented a great sight.  On one side in a row stood carts, containing white pigs, squealing and kicking up a great row on their own.
