<p>Friday 26 July<br/>Fair day, couple of showers.</p>
<p>Horses out again but in driving out got thrown clean over the horses head. No damage done. Rode in with them evening.</p>
<p>Later to Cooees. Extremely good. Had Cooee visitor to tent after show. Good.</p>
