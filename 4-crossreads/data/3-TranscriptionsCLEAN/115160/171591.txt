Auntie Maggie from which you will see she has had another turn in bed.
I gather from Dad's letter, that Nillie Ness is married & has a family now too.
I see the tram runs down Campbell Street now.  Do you miss the bus at all now that it has been discontinued?
The old fiddle has gone crook again with the same complaint.  I will try and get to Charleroi and have it attended to.  I have another case that I souveniered at Fresney-le-Grand & I got rid of the old one.  I think it has been the case that has done the damage.
