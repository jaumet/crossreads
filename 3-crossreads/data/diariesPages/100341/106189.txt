May 18th
Physical drill before breakfast; after drill order with rifles, musketry, triger pressing, aiming all positions. Afternoon given for mending clothes. I spent it reading, writing and sleeping. After tea spent time on deck till 7.30 then writing. Letters Kitty Eva, Cards to. Mother, Sanders, letter to solicitors. Weather much better sun all morning cloudy afternoon, evening clear mild. Speed full, Course W.N.W. 290 Miles.
