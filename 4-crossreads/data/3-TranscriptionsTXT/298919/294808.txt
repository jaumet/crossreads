The show was good.Thursday 15th July 1915.  Left dry dock 9.30am back to mrg[?]  About 4p Purser and Self went ashore.  I called in at Harveys & had Wireless gold cuffs fitted on my uniform.  It is rather embarrassing wearing uniform in the City especially Transport officers.  We wear RN buttons & a crown on our Commonwealth flag badge on the Cap.
