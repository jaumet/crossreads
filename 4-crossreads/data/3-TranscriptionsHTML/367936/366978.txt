<p>Sunday 14 May 1916<br/>Mother's day!!<br/>Had half day off and did some washing.  Started letter to Mother in evening.  Very hot!  Saw a lot of flying fish, also 2 sea-gulls must be near some land.</p>
<p>Monday 15 May 1916<br/>Very calm day.  No wind and very hot.<br/>102&deg; in stables near engine room.<br/>On picquet from 10 p.m. till 2 a.m.<br/>Slight shower in evening.</p>
