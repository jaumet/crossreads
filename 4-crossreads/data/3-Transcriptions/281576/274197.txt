<p>Tue. 20.  Our well has gone dry, so the Baths had to close down, as we had no water.  We are rehearsing for a Concert on Sat. night.</p>
<p>Wed. 21 Mar.  Put on to assist Geo. Hand in the Convalescent Hut.  Kept busy supplying men for the different Fatigues about the camp.</p>
<p>Thur. 22.  Weather getting good again.  Quiet a treat to be alive.  Busy getting ready for the Concert.  Johnnie Moore got &pound;5 from the Comforts Fund to buy a new violin.</p>
<p>Friday 23.  The Nightbirds Orchestra is getting some class now.  We have 2 violins, Piano &amp; Cornet.  Our Concert is put off till Monday night, as the "Anzac Coves  want to see it.</p>
