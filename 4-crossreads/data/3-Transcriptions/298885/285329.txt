<p>steamed out and have now covered 300 miles of the Red Sea<br/>Not being able to coal at Port Said we are to call at Aden.</p>
<p>The weather now is very hot indeed being the hottest time of the year for this part of the world. We are only not very far from the Equator.</p>
<p>May 29th &ndash; 19<br/>At 4AM we passed the Isle of JABEL TIER an uninhabited volcano isle 800 ft high. It has a lighthouse and was in pre war times</p>
