<p>Thursday 8th November 1917</p>
<p>Went Bank &amp; then Red X:  nothing definite yet re going, bad news still from Italy &ndash; still good news of GAZA this morning a fight I have missed &amp; I'm not sorry.</p>
<p>Capt. ISHERWOOD &amp; his wife arrived from Norwich, the former is a little man, the wife big, we went to Trocadero for Dinner &amp; on to see "General Post" at the Haymarket.</p>
<p>Had Laura to afternoon tea to meet the Isherwoods.</p>
