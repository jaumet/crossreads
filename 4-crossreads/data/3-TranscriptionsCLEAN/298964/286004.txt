  
 19 Th April Travelled all night in Position at dawn terrible bombardment with B. Guns  tanks & Gas used  Fokkers doing damage with bombs  no advance made 
 20 Fri April travelled until 1 am to 3 miles W of [indecipherable]  dog tired and very sleepy 
 21 Sat April moved to new camp about 3 miles W of J.  water very scarce  General Chauvel left  Anzac Div for Desert  Column Headqtrs  also sent with him
