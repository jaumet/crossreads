<p>[News cutting inserted in diary]<br/>14.12.1918</p>
<p>SOLDIERS' DIARIES</p>
<p>The MITCHELL LIBRARY is desirous of Purchasing for the State Archives, ORIGINAL DIARIES of Australian Soldiers taking part in the Great War.  Good prices will be paid for good material.</p>
<p>Address, Mitchell Library, Macquarie-street, Sydney.</p>
<p>For the Trustees,<br/>W.H. Ifould,<br/>Principal Librarian and Secretary</p>
