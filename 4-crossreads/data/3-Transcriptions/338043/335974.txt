<p>Sept. 17th<br/>More Australian &amp; English Letters, all of which contain good bright news &ndash; "Non-Suited" still going strong. Rehearsal of "Eastern Romance".</p>
<p>Sept. 18th<br/>Rehearsal &amp; Show &ndash; Change of programme &ndash; Letter writing etc. &ndash; My memory plays me false for the first time in the History of "The Coves" &ndash; I recite part of Xmas Carol, and then suddenly came to an abrupt stop.</p>
