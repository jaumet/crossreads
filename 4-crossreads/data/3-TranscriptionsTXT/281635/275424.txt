
15/10/17 All fit men in camp sent to front line but 28 of us went on sick parade into Ypres. Away all morning & saw the ruins of the place including the Cloth Hall. After dinner issued with old rusty rifles & putting an afternoon cleaning them up. Went to bed about 6pm.16/10/17 Were busy cleaning rifles ready for an inspection when Fritz started shelling the camp. In 33rd btn 2 killed & 2 wounded & in 35th (our btn) the pay Sergt was blown to pieces. All men were ordered to scatter & at 10.30am orders came
