They dig with energy and pleasure into the ancient civilized tombs & graves around about Zeitoun & Heliopolis. They bring into Camp Bone & Skulls, which their entrenching tools have unearthed. Saturday11/3/16
Very cold last night - windy & sand blowing today. Went for a Route march to Matarieh - Visited the well & the tree where the Virgin Mary rested & drank from the well - got a few chips of a tree - a Sycamore Fig - partly alive & dead - The Virgin rested here on her flight from Jerusalem into Egypt.
