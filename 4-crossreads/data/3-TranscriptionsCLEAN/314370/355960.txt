  
 January, 1916 Sunday 2 Went to church parade & then had dinner w/- dad We went into Heliopolis Music Hall Had supper at 9.30 & turned in about 10.30 pm. 
 Monday 3 All men returned from M.E.F. are paraded with a view of returning to our battalion. But hear no further news except that the 4th Bde is at Tel-el-Kebir
