<p>Sun. Jan. 23rd -16<br/>Entrained for Zeitoun about 160 miles.  Splendid views from train.  Low lying lands with more vegetation than one would expect to see in such a dry country.</p>
<p>Mon. Jan. 24th -16<br/>Visited Heliopolis at night splendid buildings especially No. 1 General Hospital.</p>
<p>Tues. Jan. 25th -16<br/>Attached to 1st D.A.C. 3rd Section.  Days routine, water, groom and feed horses in morning and drill in afternoon.</p>
<p>Wed. Jan. 26th -16<br/>Visited Cairo at night &amp; found the city very interesting.</p>
