<p>30 July 1918<br/>Tuesday<br/>Making a good trip but nothing very interesting happening No war news through So do not know how things are going. Still on Submarine lookout on Bridge.</p>
<p>1918 July 31<br/>Wednesday<br/>Nothing very exciting Sighted a convoy of four boats bound Northward from Sierra Leone. Sighted a few whales &ndash; porpoise Weather unpleasantly warm.</p>
