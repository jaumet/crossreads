<p>Monday 13<br/>In the city of Abbeyville now after cash hope we succeed Got [indecipherable] 1st, 17th 18th &amp; 28th Batt.<br/>Tuesday 14<br/>A tourist our 4th day in the train and the end not yet in sight now at Romescampis<br/>Wednesday 15<br/>Now at Albert, arrived here last night and the huns greeted us with bombs &amp; machine G fire<br/>Thursday 16<br/>I am as Cold as ice is stranded on the Albert station living on biscuits</p>
