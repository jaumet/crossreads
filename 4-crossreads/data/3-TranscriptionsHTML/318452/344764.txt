<p>Thursday 4th Oct<br/>The advance began this morning at Daybreak.<br/>A detailed account will be added later. I went to several hospitals during the day. Prisoners were coming down in hundreds &amp; wounded were packing the hospitals. I hear the objectives were all taken. M Clark died today Called at 35th Transport &amp; saw some of the Old Batt.</p>
<p>Friday<br/>Today I went up to the hospital on the Menin Rd I passed through Ypres</p>
