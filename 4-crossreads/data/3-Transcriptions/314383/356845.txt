<p>An enormous amount of energy is expended on the Arabs as they load our kit on the tub.</p>
<p>These people are remarkable for their strength &amp; laziness.  The loads  <strike>they</strike>one carries would frighten two white men &amp; on every opportunity they lose themselves in order to dodge work.</p>
<p>The river still continues to be wide &amp; we pass innumerable depots and workshops on the banks.</p>
