<p>In the last round, the limit was reached. The little chap, little Scotty we call him was hanging onto the ropes with one hand &amp; swinging the other one round like a windmill. About halfway through the round neither could see the other &amp; they were rolling all round the hatch till the gong went.</p>
<p>Some of the other fights were very good &amp; we had a good afternoon.</p>
