<p>19th Sept. 1914<br/>Last night an English warship betrayed herself by her wireless. It was as follows:- The English warship gave her secret name e.g. "Colonia" through the wireless, to a coastal station. The station called up did not know this name, and made enquiries by wireless to another station, asking what the name "Colonia" meant. The latter replied "Colonia" is the secret name of the British warship "Hampshire" so we had to thank our excellent and useful wireless station for the news that "Emden" was searched for by "Hampshire" in the Gulf of Bengal.</p>
