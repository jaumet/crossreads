The [indecipherable] having gone to England to discuss the best place for our Base with Mr Hughes. It is a very serious and difficult question for some one to decide &ndash; for it may entail a great deal of comfort and quick recovery for our troops from wounds &amp; sickness or vice-versa and so many things for and against all the convenient places.<br/>It is strange how we deliver over our destinies to men who up till now have been unimportant people. But even in the old days prophets and leaders "rose up" as it were from oblivion.
