<p>[Tuesday 29 February 1916]</p>
<p>I had to go to <strike>Samalut</strike> &amp; see the local Magistrate (Egyptian) re some shots fired from a Bedouin Village.<br/>I found him in the CLUB!!! &amp; was soon surrounded by all the colored Notables &amp; swallowed 2 Whiskies &amp; sodas with great skill-<br/>Thro' an Interpreter (after much Salaaming etc) I tried to put them right about a few natives ie Maoris do not come from Australia- not any Blackfellows are in our Parliament- etc.<br/><u>All Classes</u> here are much affected by Sedition papers now being circulated, they want to govern themselves they say &ndash;<br/>Tried to buy English Paper but only got an Arabic &amp; French one which I send you</p>
