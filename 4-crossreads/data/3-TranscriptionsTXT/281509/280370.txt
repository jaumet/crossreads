 1916 May 27  Saturday 
 Reveille 5.30. a.m. Tony Bob self &amp; Cartwright told off for stretcher party  again.  5 aeroplanes up scouting round at breakfast. Cold night. Paid 20 francs. in forenoon. "In the field." entered in pay book. Do no work all day.   Trio go for long walk to beach after tea. 3 miles. Bob &amp; his bog. Back to tea at Y.M.C.A. joined by Sergt. Barr. To bed &amp; more "letters" read by Barr. "There is one corner in this foreign land, that is forever England."
