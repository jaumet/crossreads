<p>the Muller [transcribers note: correct spelling Mullah]  &amp;  then proceeded "I am very pleased that you are having a concert. I should like to see more of them; they keep the men together  &amp;  gives them something to think about of a night. I wish you every success." Illuminating, wasn't it? needless to say as all beer  &amp;  spirits are now taboo in Sohag the concert was just about a very poor success.<br/>I understand that the  Mudir is going to give the troops a days sports, on the 16th May.<br/> God grant we shal'et be here!</p>
