 October, 1915 Sunday 3 Holy Carpet starts for Mecca today &ndash; pity so many soldiers persist in carving their names wherever they go &ndash; Here the chalk rocks around the Garden &amp; buildings are all disfigured by Tom JONES WOOLOOMOLOO. etc etc the same at Pyramids &amp; other places.   Carbuncle will block me now for a day or two   (DID you get Medal I posted Registered ere I went to Gallipoli &amp; have Receipt let me know please? Also Pictures from HAY.!
