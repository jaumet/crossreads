<p>Monday 23 September 1918<br/>Rounds fired 1700, bombs dropped 16, trips 2, 2 h. 5, 2 h. 0.</p>
<p>Tuesday 24 September 1918<br/>Recco, roads.  Pilots Lt. McGuinness &amp; Tonkin.<br/>In air 3 h. 35 m.<br/>Landed at Um es Senab with Arab forces, had an interesting time.  Landed at A Fuleh, gone in report, then home.<br/>A Fuleh was old Hun aerodrome.<br/>4 machines intact there.</p>
