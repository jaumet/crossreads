[See image for drawing of the plan.]The day following our return to camp we had a race meeting on the desert which was very exciting.  On the 9th of Aug. a dozen of us went to Abassia, about two miles distant for horses.  There are some thousands kept there and we led 50 back.  On the 11th we marched to Kasr-el-Nil Barracks to relieve the East Lancashire Regiment as garrison, but after staying one night and a day, were in turn relieved by the 6th Aust. Brigade.
