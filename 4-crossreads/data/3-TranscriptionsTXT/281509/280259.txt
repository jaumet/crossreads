 6 February 1916 Sunday 
 We go up to church in morning.  Meet old friends Mr. Kay Bannot preached.  Mary &amp; Stan with us. Stan's to dinner. Go to Cambewarra in afternoon.  Church at night again.  Visit Miss Glanville's &amp; meet Miss Barbara on way to church.  Sit in choir for last time. Bob meets old Manse friend in Miss Taylor. Walk home with Miss Tottie. Supper &amp; bed.
