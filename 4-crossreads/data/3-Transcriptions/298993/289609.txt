<p>Monday Mar 26.3.17  am having a bit of a snack now it is terrible hot the country here is beautiful all green grass and barley acres of it and I riding over it  can hear firing in the distance I could do with a cup of tea I think we are here for some time artillery and L H  and L G have just past  the name of this place is enterance to Palestine I have a splitting head ache I boiled the billy and made some tea it was first rate I am a lot better now.
