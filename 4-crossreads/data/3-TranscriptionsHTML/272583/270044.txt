They some time wear short trousers just above the knee and puttees.<br/>They cant come up to the Australian Soldier for neatness though. They are left far behind. <br/>My opinion of the place is not overwhelming as far as I have seen. We are landing to-night at 6.30<br/>Ball ammunition is to be issued as soon as we land on account of the Union Jack being hoisted to-morrow.<br/>If the rain continues we shall have it wet for the disembarkation as we did when we embarked.
