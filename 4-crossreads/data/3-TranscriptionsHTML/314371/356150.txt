<p>Nov. 26th<br/>Still going on O.K., am feeling well.  Looking well &amp; am well.  Hope to get away shortly.</p>
<p>Dec. 2nd<br/>Left Hosp. 7.30 a.m., A.T. to Calais, got on board ship same day.</p>
<p>De. 3rd<br/>Too rough to cross, slept well, plenty to eat.</p>
<p>Dec. 4th<br/>Moved out at 9.30, arrived Dover 10.50, A.T. again, taken to Aux. Hosp. at Sholden Lodge, Deal, Kent.  Very good home.</p>
<p>Dec. 5th<br/>Lovely weather, cold.  Feeling very well &amp; want to get up.  Wrote letters.</p>
<p>From Dec. 5th I made good progress until Dec. 16th when I had to be opened up again on account of a collection of puss.</p>
