16/2/19 Cologne
When we came out it was dark and Lochstrasse was packed with Hun civilians and soldiers promenading and electric signs actively indicated picture shows. Walking up the street three little girls behind us made audible coquetting remarks in English while one good looker in front after a scowl repeatedly smiled and eventually turned (with a smile of invitation) down a side street. We returned to the crowded Simplicimus to a tea room and there sat at a table and drank Rhine wine while a man sang songs of the Rhine and an orchestra played.
