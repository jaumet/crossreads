<p>17 June 1916 Sat<br/>At midnight last night I was awakened by the gas &ndash; gongs sirens &amp; everything that denotes an enemy gas attack. We we all lined up outside the billet on the road. It was very cold there &amp; great deal of excitement. The chlorine gas came over &amp; I soon got a whiff of it immediately putting the helmet on. Good results came from the helmets as no cases [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>18 June 1916 Sun<br/>Dressed Lieut Barlows wounds to start off with. During the afternoon a sports meeting was held. The cock-fights were very amusing. Clive Catt &amp; I took part in the wheelbarrow race. I collapsed after going about 20 yds. After tea 14 of the boys adjourned to the estaminet to give Tiny a send off as he was leaving for the sanitary party. 14,000</p>
