  
 12/4/18 - Lavieville A lovely sunny day.  The Boche shelling very close to here and sending up debris & smoke.  Found the dead bodies of a Scotch officer and a private at the local church.  They had evidently been there some days and had bad wounds.  Met a Major of the Tanks and with him made a reconnaisance of a new line forward.  The weather was so clear that the observation from Albert was absolutely wonderful.  After lunch met Rodda and Kennedy & made yet another reconnaisance, this time of the right flank.
