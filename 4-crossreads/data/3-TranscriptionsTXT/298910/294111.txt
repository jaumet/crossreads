 Thursday  8/3/17 
 Strong winds blowing &ndash; bitterly cold, snow fell at intervals during night &amp; day. Light artillery very busy. Very few casualtys through. 
 Friday  9/3/17 
 Snowing &ndash; foggy etc &ndash; Very quiet. A few trench feet came through. 
 Saturday  10/3/17 
 Dirty foggy day &ndash; Snow thawing. Excepting for local artillery duels very quiet. Very few casualtys. Wrote to Mrs O'Kelly giving more particulars of Hubert. 
 Sunday  11/3/17 
 Miserable sort of a dull day. Busy ay night. Fritz's artillery giving us extra special attention &ndash; also throwing over great numbers of Minniewwurfers etc. 
 Monday  12/3/17 
 Dull to drizzling rain &ndash; Got letters from G. Nock &amp; C. Hagan. Very busy all night, nearly all cases, were very badly wounded. Performed several urgent operations at M.D.S.
