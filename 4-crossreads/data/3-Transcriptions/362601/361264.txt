<p>9 September, Tuesday</p>
<p>Go to see Charlie in take Bess &amp; Laura. Very rainy day. Stay at Stanmore meet Horrie Norton.</p>
<p>10 September, Wednesday</p>
<p>Go to North Steyne. Welcome in Tyke's Hall.</p>
<p>Meet lots but not Peg. Sit with G. H. Taubman &amp; Mrs T. Dance with a nice ma-in-law.</p>
