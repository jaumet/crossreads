 Sunday 29 September Visit Abbeville to-day by train &amp; walk home along the road.  Abbeville has been fairly extensively bombed &amp; knocked about but we found a fine hotel for dinner &amp; visited 3rd A.G.H. having the d'apres midi with Miss Murdoch, Eric Grenville's friend. 
 Monday 30 September Usual routine at Pont Remy &amp; walks around the village.
