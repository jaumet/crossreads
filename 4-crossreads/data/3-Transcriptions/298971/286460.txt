<p>10th November (Friday)</p>
<p>Zep. brought down during the night &amp; it landed on French Amm. dump near us.  Getting stones for road mending all day.  Visited <strike>Becourt</strike> Meaulte in evening.  Shells dropped round our camp during the night.</p>
