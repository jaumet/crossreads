<p>March, 1915<br/>Wednesday 24<br/>Camped tonight at Helonan great Tourists resort an Oasis of Sulphur springs, Camels Donkeys more numerous than elsewhere.<br/>We were awakened at 3 Am by a devout worshipper singing at the top of his voice the Koran from one of the house tops.</p>
