JANUARY 1917
23 TUESDAY
Fine & Sunny but very frosty
the heaviest & coldest frost I
have seen. The sun shone all
day, but no warmth in it.
Cleaning harness all morning
& exercising horses in afternoon.
heard the 27th got a gun put out
of action by a direct hit & one
man killed & a couple wounded
