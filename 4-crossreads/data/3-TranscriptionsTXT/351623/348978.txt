There are also oil wells at Pitch Lakes. The output of pitch is about 400 tons per day.Our concert party & band gave an entertainment ashore in aid of Red Cross. It was well attended by a large & appreciative audience.
19 April 16Anchored at Port of Spain
20 April 16Heaved anchor & steamed slowly down the Bay. Done sub-calibre firing.Anchored in afternoon a few miles out of Port of Spain.
21 April 16Got under weigh in morning watch & run two torpedoes, while doing about 18 knots speed. Came up closer to Port of Spain & anchored.
