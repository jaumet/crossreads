  
 A long & miserable march under wretched conditions - We pass through Meaulte, Becordel. Fricourt. Mametz, and pass the night in the last named. 
 Dec. 23rd. Reveille 3.45a.m - We march off in the dark through rain, mud, slush on a road half under water & stray mettle - After a solid march we arrive at a miserable spot in Bannefay Wood - Take up duties in A.Div. Much improved service directly behind the Line light railways. 
 Dec. 24th. Xmas Eve - A fine day but cold - distribution of Gifts - Taubes once more - Application for new position - News of Eric Gordon. A midnight meal.
