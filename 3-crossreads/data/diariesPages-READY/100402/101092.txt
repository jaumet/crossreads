Saturday 22 June
Move up to A.D.S. at Borre. A stunt is coming off to-night or to-morrow night.
Still fine weather.
Sunday 23 June
Glorious weather to-night a stunt is to come off to the left of Meteren as far as the 1st Aust. Division is concerned. Some Americans, with full packs have gone past here to-day, up to the line.
