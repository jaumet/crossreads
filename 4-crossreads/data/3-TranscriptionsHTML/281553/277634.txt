<p>June 23<br/>Tried all town for Frame, got one &amp; did some printing late evening, all well.  Writing night.</p>
<p>June 24<br/>Printing negatives, etc., took several photos.  Went for watch after tea.  Writing afternoon.  Washing Hdkfs.</p>
<p>June 25<br/>Photography most of day, out for while in evening back about 9 p.m.</p>
<p>June 26<br/>Letter writing &amp; reading, out 2 hrs. after dinner.</p>
<p>June 27<br/>Relieved 10-30 a.m., marched back leaving kits for wagon following.</p>
