 FRIDAY, AUGUST 8, 1919. 
 Fine & warm. Round Cow paddock 
 & Mountain in morning & lopping 
 in 4D in evening. Tickle poisoning 
 in Mountain & Top paddocks. 
 Darby bust both his bits & sinker 
 bars & has to stop work while he 
 has them sent to Sydney & fixed up. 
 He is down 150 ft [bi?].
