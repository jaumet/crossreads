<p>December 1916<br/>(28th continued)   There are only two sections of the company left, the other two having gone away to another town, but where to or what for, I do not know.  No.3 section is working in the other end of the town about two miles away on similar work to us, &amp; the remainder of <strike>number</strike> No.4 section have been divided up, some having gone to other towns in the vicinity on special work &amp; others remaining here for odd jobs in connection with the company.<br/>30th   We are still on the same work &amp; are making considerable progress.  The weather has been rather wet for the last two days &amp; fairly cold with heavy frosts.</p>
