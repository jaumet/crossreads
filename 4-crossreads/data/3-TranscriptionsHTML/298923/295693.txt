<p>27 January 1918 Septua-gesima Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Raining again Church service this morning, <strike>but men wont sing when cold blooded.</strike><br/>Busy clipping horses mending saddlery etc &amp; getting ready for our next push.<br/>X Post this with love to you all- &amp; don't forget to tell May I often think of her but do not write.  FW  X</p>
