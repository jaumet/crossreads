 This bomb of ours is a beauty, when they drop it, it bursts into 4 pieces &amp; a little lower down these 4 pieces burst into 8 &amp; lower down still they become sixteen &amp; any one of these portions is sufficient to bring any balloon down, they are what they call incendinary bombs &amp; are used sometimes to destroy ripening crops.
