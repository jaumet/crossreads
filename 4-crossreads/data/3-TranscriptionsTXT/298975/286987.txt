 Jan 1918 
 to the Butts with a markinh party 
 Thursday  3 Still at Butts Have Hegarty for a mate. Hall on next target. Backed our score against his for suppers. We won 
 Friday 4th Got another cheap supper 
 Saturday On the Butts Won again Went to Warminster in afternoon, Banked &pound; 5 Saw the Pictures &amp; had 2 fair meals
