Just before Xmas I believe the French Authorities issued an "order" to the effect that if any girl between the age of 16 18 was caught walking out with a soldier she would be sent away to work on munitions also any female caught sitting with Soldiers in an estaminet or caf or doing anything of this sort, she also would be dealt with similarily, in one way this is a good thing but they should have enforced it long ago, from what I can see of things they are a crook lot of women round here as far as morals are concerned anyhow.
