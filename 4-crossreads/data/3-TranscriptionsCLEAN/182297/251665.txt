  
 Narrative up to 12 noon, 6/2/15 
 Yesterday morning, a small mixed detachment from EL KUBRI, reconnoiting GEBE MURR came in contact with a small hostile force. We lost 1 Sowar, killed. The night passed without incident. 
 Hostile force behind GEBEL HABEITA was unchanged this morning. 
 The enemy's losses south of TUSSUM on the morning of 4 th  inst, were:- 59 killed, 58 wounded, 190 prisoners, and 3 maxims.
