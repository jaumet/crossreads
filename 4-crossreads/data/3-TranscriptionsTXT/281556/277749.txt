16th. Sunday.Fell in for Church parade but orders came for moving & at noon we set off for "Cardinette" about 6 miles of. These billets were small and dirty and the people very unsociable, took their pumps to pieces etc & made things unpleasant for us.
17th. Monday.Drill as usual before bkfst at 9 to 12 & 2 till 4.30pm.
Mail recd. Slight rainfall.
