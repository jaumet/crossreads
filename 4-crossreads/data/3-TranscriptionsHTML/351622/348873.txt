<p>12 August 1916 Sat<br/>Very busy all day have put on fifty patients to look after. Plenty of dressings &amp; Captain Davenport performed a couple of operations during the afternoon on septic wounds. Dick &amp; I assisted him. Some 100 more patients arrive. Got to bed about 9 pm. &amp; was awakened by Geoff Wood who had [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>13 August 1916 Sun<br/>Kept going again all day. Mr. Sutton &amp; Tom Buck joins up again. Harry Frankland has dinner with us. Running a mess therefore we are enjoying good tucker which we procure in Talmas. Wrote several post cards &amp; letters before turning in onto the old best of straw in a decent barn</p>
