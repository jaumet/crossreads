Orders to-day where Wal McMullen was killed in action.26-10-17 Left HIS C.5.8 our fortified homestead near YPRES per motor Lorry at 9 a.m. Dinner at AIRE & arrived at this dreamy village ERNY ST JULIEN about 7-30 p.m. This is our second stay here & old madam where we were billeted last time was very pleased to see us. We have the same room & boska Double bed & enjoy a good nights rest between the sheets. Coffee is laid on night & morning so things are bon.4-11-17 Last night Wally Watson rang up from Division & invited Mac & I to the first performance of the SMART SET (4th Div.
