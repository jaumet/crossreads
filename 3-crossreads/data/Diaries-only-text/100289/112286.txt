Passed over the Tower Bridge going to the Tower in the morning
Friday 17.8.17
Arose, had breakfast afterwards walked down Picadilly Oxford St; Paid a visit to Madam Tussauds wax works National Gallery. Wended my way to Colleseum Theatre at night (Vaudeville) which is by far the best theatre in London from all accounts
