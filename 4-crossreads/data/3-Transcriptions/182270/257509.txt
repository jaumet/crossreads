<p>WEDNESDAY, JULY 16, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Drove over to Coomoo Coomoo</p>
<p>with Jo in little car for lunch then</p>
<p>on to Oakey Creek for afternoon</p>
<p>tea. Got changed at Oakey &amp; got</p>
<p>to Binnia about 7 pm. Wedding</p>
<p>started at 7.45 and everything</p>
<p>went off O.K. Bride &amp; &#39;groom</p>
<p>went to Colin Cameron&#39;s place</p>
<p>We danced till 4AM, then got</p>
<p>home to Oakey. It was a great</p>
<p>show, just like pre war days.</p>
