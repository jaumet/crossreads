 [Saturday 30 September 1916] 
 Cold much better .  Posted notes yesterday. Leave by 9.30 pm Boat from Belfast for Fleetwood arrive in the morning &amp; to meet Laura. All the Neill family have been so nice too me &amp; gave me Wedding presents Silver Cigarette Box &ndash; Gold Pin "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Flask &amp; Ink Pot Leather Despatch Box. They are all Rich  nice  people &amp; seemed grateful for what I did at Mulberrygong &ndash; one Reggie Neill has his only son posted as missing- Spent the day walking thro' a pretty fern glen, full of Pheasants &amp; Rabbits &amp; am sorry to leave old Ireland   I am taking a wee bit of  real  Shamrock with me.
