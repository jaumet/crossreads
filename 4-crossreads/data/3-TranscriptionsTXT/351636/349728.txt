 Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers 13-7.16. 
 Dear Mr. Layton, Mrs. Maxwell who I am sending up has filled in a form &amp; her case is being considered by you. but she is in very distressed circumstances &amp; has no money for herself &amp; child. 
 Can you kindly give her some immediate assistance till things can be arranged for her 
 Yours truly E. H. Bennie.
