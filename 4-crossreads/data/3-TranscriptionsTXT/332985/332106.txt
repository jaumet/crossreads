 Glorious day, St. Patrick day.  Half holiday.  First pay in new pay books.  General Birdwood here.  Bapaume has fallen 4-30 a.m. 
 18-3-1917, Sunday Weather improving.  Football match.  1st Bty. v 2nd Bty.  Men granted half holiday.  Sgt. McNamara gone to D.A. to get his Military Medal. 
 19-3-1917 Raining.  Good progress made on our front.  32 Kilometres in all.  The town of Bapaume dates back 864 A.D.  It was set on fire by the Germans. 
 20-3-1917 Snowing, sleet and rain, very disagreeable day.  Struck a snag in the remounts, had to rope him. 
 21-3-1917 Colder than usual.  A move rumoured on the 26 inst. 
 22-3-1917 Supposed to be going North, nothing doing.
