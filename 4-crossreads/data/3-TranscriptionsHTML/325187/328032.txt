<p>[Monday 16 October 1916]</p>
<p>Got myself photographed for you all at Home &amp; had afternoon Tea with Nowell at the Junior Army &amp; Navy Club.<br/>Lunched at the Criterion &amp; dinner at Frascatis &amp; then went to Daddy-Long-Legs.  Nowell asked my wife to tea tomorrow when Im away.</p>
