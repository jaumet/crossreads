<p>1 October 1916<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Great general clean up at camp in anticipation of visit from G.O.C.<br/>Gen. Walker. Sweep duckboards, a noble work!<br/>Capt. Guthrie holds service in Mess room as usual --- Eternity.<br/>General Parade at 11 a.m. Stay on parade for 1.40 hrs Gen. late. arrives ultimately cursory inspection &amp; dismiss. Poof!<br/>Bob &amp; Frank into Pop on bikes to get photos. disappointed. <br/>Collared a couple of boards [indecipherable]. Service at C.C.C.S. in evening. 2nd address on St. Paul.  Begin to read "Little Minister". Barrie.</p>
