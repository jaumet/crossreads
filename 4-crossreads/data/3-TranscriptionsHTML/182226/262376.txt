<p>Monday 20 December, 1915&nbsp;</p>
<p>Left Gozo for All Saints, I was not in the draft but I went &amp; got put on.</p>
<p>We left Gozo at 2 p.m. arrived at Malta at 4.30, put in No 1 Section.</p>
<p>Mac had lef[t] All Saints today. I said things.</p>
<p>The rules have been made more springent here now but I hope to find a way of getting over the difficulty.</p>
