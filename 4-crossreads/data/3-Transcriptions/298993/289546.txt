<p>Saturday Sept 23.9.16<br/>Ticking and exercising camels cleaning up for inspection was inspected had dinner, rested in afternoon<br/>Sunday Sept 24.9.16<br/>Went to helipolis to church a long walk. Warm day returned to camp for dinner.<br/>Monday Sept. 25.9.16<br/>On camel picquet to night , nothing doing<br/>Tuesday Sept 26.9.16<br/>Dipping camels today. Had two letters from Jose arrived then wrote to mother<br/>Wednesday Sept 27.9.16<br/>Drilling to day am not too well went into cairo got a bottle of Physic.</p>
