<p>rifle &amp; all my gear were blown to pieces.  One thing we had plenty of tucker, a hot stew every night, bacon for breakfast &amp; hot tea three times a day with more bread than we could eat.  I was never more glad in all my life to get away from a place as I was from that place.  Three days before this hop we were in the line for five days &amp; did a little hop then, advanced about 700 yds, 2 platoons of us knocked Fritz out &amp; held it.  Before that we had 10 days spell, the best rest we have</p>
