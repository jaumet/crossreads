<p>September 1918.<br/>Monday 16</p>
<p>In Reserve  (Hebecourt) and Bouvincourt.<br/>Weather very warm, quite a midsummers day.<br/>Left for line at 10.45 am. and headed to a cluster of bushes near Bouvincourt. Travelled via Braches, Peronne, Doingt and Cartigny to a little position near to the village I mentioned.<br/>Ammo wagons proceeded to Bty position.<br/>About midnight a storm sprang up, and the boys received a very unpleasant  drenching.<br/>Smithy and Doc came a beautiful thud.<br/>Wrote D.B.F. and T.L.</p>
