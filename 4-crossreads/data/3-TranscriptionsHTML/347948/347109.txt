<p>Comm Off.<br/>Essex, Prisoner, Persian Gulf, India, France.</p>
<p>11th  Leave is granted from from 5 to 10 and the bounds are 3 mile radius from camp.  Hats are to be worn to distinguish the Australian from the English Soldier.<br/>Equipment belts must also be worn, men properly shaved and boots cleaned and white band on shoulder strap clean.<br/>Strawberries are very cheap we got 4 lb baskets for 2/- and at the canteen you can get a saucer of strawberries &amp; cream for 2d</p>
