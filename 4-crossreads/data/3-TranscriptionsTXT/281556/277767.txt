 List &amp; rec'd about 40 reinforcements to the Coy. Moved at 1pm. for Warloy which we reached &amp; were billeted in about 5pm. I was on the Billeting party and went through on Transport motors. 
 7th. Wednesday. Up 7am muster parade later. Recd mail. 
 Requisitions made out, complete ly  Nominal rolls also completed &amp; nominations for promotions rec'd. Shoping &amp; to bed early. 
 8th. Tuesday. Orders for moving at 9am. Left on bike 8.30
