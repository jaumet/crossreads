  
 Sunday 10 February Still at Ravelsberg D.R.'s in the pack store. Expect leave to Paris shortly. 1st Australian mail for ages. 
 Monday 11 February Football match 1st F. Amb. v. 1st F. Co. Engineers.  Victory for Ambulance 16-3. About 25 German prisoners passed along the road this morning.  Captured last night in raid.
