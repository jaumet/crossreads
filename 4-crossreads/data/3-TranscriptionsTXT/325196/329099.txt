 29-1-16 Sat 29th Had a kit inspection this morning &amp; had to put in for any shortages, as my black kit bag is still missing I had to put in for a new rig-out;This afternoon the 1st Regt. played the 2nd Regt. Football, &amp; we won by 11 points to nil. After tea we commenced boxing &amp; some good bouts were put up till it got too dark to see.
