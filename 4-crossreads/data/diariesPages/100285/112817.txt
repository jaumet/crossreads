Got my pass this morning which I applied for to go to Frome with Len to see some people he knew down there so after dinner I went across to the O.T.B so after some delay Len I got away on bicycle they were bike's I can tell you one took his life in his hands riding or rather pushing them for they resembled wheelbarrows in this respect neither had brakes of any description to steady them, cost us 3/6 each for them, on our way we had to pass through Warminster so I slipped in had a look at my negatives which I was having developed.
