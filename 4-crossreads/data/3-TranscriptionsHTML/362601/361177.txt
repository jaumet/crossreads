<p>22 March, Saturday</p>
<p>F.<br/>We go to look for Venus but we can't fix the telescope only see bright lights.</p>
<p>We go to Ramsey &amp; to Pictures. See a Lone drama. Tea at Angalie go shopping &amp; pay debts. Then we go to Mr &amp; Mrs Gibsons. Home by Lezarpe Road meal nice &amp; his bit &amp; chatty all the way. I rush to Glen &amp; meet M at P.O. We walk from the Bridge home &amp; I relate incidents of my own Home life. Supper &amp; cards until bed-time &amp; we go to Room together &amp; I see N.C.L.</p>
<p>23 March, Sunday</p>
<p>F. Lovely Day.</p>
<p>M &amp; I sit by fire &amp; talk etc. we are both very lively &amp; happy especially at Tea. We sit &amp; yarn by the fire at night &amp; I am very tired. We have music &amp; song &amp; all go to bed at 11 pm. We look over spare stuff in my room. Auntie gives me a ring, also one for Ellis. I engage myself to be faithful for ever &amp; [indecipherable].</p>
<p>I pack my bag &amp; give M my old Pocket Book.</p>
