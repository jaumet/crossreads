<p>Side by side at the entrance of the Mosque are two pillars also barred off.  <strike>It</strike> These were placed there by Amr, who stated that anyone who could pass through between the pillars would be assured of everlasting life in the hereafter.  Thin &amp; normal people could get through easily, but among the many thousands that pressed forward to gain paradise, came many of the more ample Mohammedans.
