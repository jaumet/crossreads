<p>3rdJan  Shifted back to Merris for a rest. <br/>A few words about the snow. It is damnable stuff.  It freezes the <br/>ground and makes it hard enough to turn a pick. And if you fall !!   <br/>We made a slide over a frozen pond and if anyone is tired of life <br/>I advise them to take on sliding.</p>
