<p>Monday, 21/5/17<br/>Up at 6 a.m. &amp; no early morning parade.  Practically no parades at all during the day.  Received letter from Miss P. dated Feby. 19th &amp; a parcel of eatables from Uncle Fred.  After tea went to Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 22/5/17<br/>Platoon drill in morning &amp; route march in afternoon passing through Wareham.  After tea looked around Wareham with Skelsey.</p>
<p>Wednesday, 23/5/17<br/>Fairly cold early in the morning.  Heavy day of drill at the "Bull-ring".  A bit of a strike at dinner time on a/c of scarcity of food.  Officers promised to have quantity &amp; quality improved.  After tea took stroll down to Y.M.C.A. &amp; got caught in heavy rain.  It is now raining heavily.  Handed in our waterproof capes today.</p>
<p>Thursday, 24/5/17<br/>Inclined to be windy today although fine.  Had little bayonet drill in morning &amp; in afternoon went for route march.  Saw train containing 10 "Tanks" passing by.  Went to Wareham after tea &amp; then to Y.M.C.A. &amp; went to bed.</p>
<p>Friday, 25/5/17<br/>Much warmer today &amp; did a hard day's work viz. Musketry, Bayonet fighting, Physical Training, Squad drill, and Bombing.  After tea</p>
