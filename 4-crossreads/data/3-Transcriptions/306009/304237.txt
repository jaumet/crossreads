<p>1915<br/>Feb. 20 Sat.<br/>Plenty of work to do &ndash; Number in Hospital now up to 896.<br/>Am picking up words of Arabic lingo slowly - it is astonishing how well the natives speak English (including swear words of course).<br/>Had a chat with Charlie Wade &ndash; he is a Lance-Corpl in the 2nd Infantry.</p>
