<p>3.<br/>my Battn &amp; I have been put to 'A' Coy. of course my stripes came off &amp; from what I can see I have no chance of a star. This is quite right as the chaps who deserve them are the men who have been with the Bn from the start &amp; they're a fine lot too.</p>
<p>Ink run out.</p>
<p>We're in a really typical French farm &amp; very comfy to.</p>
<p>I haven't seen the firing line yet &amp; it is much to my shame that I say it as I had over 4&frac12; months in</p>
