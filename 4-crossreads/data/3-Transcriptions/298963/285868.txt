<p>25th Aug.<br/>Much has taken place since I last wrote in this diary.  Today nurse gave me this, &amp; asked me if I wanted it.  I am in Hospital (Brit.) No. 2 Stationary at Abbeville.  It's worse than in the line &ndash; for 3 days I've lain here to my recollection &amp; had not even a dressing on my wound.  The doctor a real flash sort comes in sometimes, looks at one or two &amp; goes to play tennis.  All that I see done in the ward is "red tape".  If a patient dares</p>
