Lyddite is apt to explode on concussion &ndash; Our magazine temperature is kept at 70 deg F by means of ice &ndash; made on the ship &amp; a forced draught &ndash; You don't want high temps in a magazine &ndash; They repeatedly take the temperature &amp; watch it closely &ndash;We dont do 'Rounds' save on Sunday when the Head of each Department accompanies the Commander who formally inspects the Ship &ndash; I don't know that there's much more to add to my tale &ndash; My SBA is not popular &ndash; He's reported to have swelled head &amp; to be above his station &ndash; He told me a sailor kicked him to day &ndash; playfully &ndash; but he wasn't going to stand it &amp; wanted that sort of thing put an end to &ndash; I fear I didn't console him much &ndash; Sailor men don't care for family pride even in an S.B.A &ndash; By the way Garden Island &amp; the Encounter spoke to us in the eveg &ndash; We all of us hope very much that the Encounter is not coming up this way &ndash; Charles Lewin is not a popular officer - &amp; he'll be senior to Commander D. of the Warrego or Fitzgerald &ndash;
