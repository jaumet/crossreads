<p>Jerry came over to see me but Joe got hold of him while I was fixing up the cricketing material.  The light is bad &amp; I can hardly see to write now.  He often comes over now with news of Alf &amp; Harry.  Poor old Alf he got his wish after all &ndash; a boy from a good home who never wanted for anything.  I knew him when in Hospital, he was anxious then to come up with the boys.  He got his wish, &amp; now he lies in hospital &ndash; dying they say &ndash;</p>
