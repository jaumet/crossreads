
Tuesday 20/11/17  Complained to [Jess?] about Waters- posted picquets, Morning Walk
Wednesday 21/11/17. Gen'l White visited the camp. Rode to Steenvoorde & had dinner there. Walk
Thursday 22/11/17  Orders received to close camp, general clean up.
Friday 23/11/17. Left for Division reported through Bde & joined up Battalion. Walk
Saturday 24/11/17  Had a good look around- Letter from Doff- Had game of H ball against 25th.
