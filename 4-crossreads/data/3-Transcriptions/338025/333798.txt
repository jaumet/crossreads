<p>Thursday 29th September1917<br/>Harness up moved out of camp by 12 noon. Went to ordanance (nr Poperinghi) with a load of damaged waggon wheels. Arr&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  ordanance about 3pm where we were unloaded by china men. Passed through some beautiful country civilians going on with their work in the fields as if there was no war on. Got back to camp about 4.30pm. Our guns opened up about 8pm. Skyline lit up by the flashes</p>
