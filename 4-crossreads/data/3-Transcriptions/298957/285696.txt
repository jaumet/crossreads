<p>made regarding the death of Lord Kitchener<br/>12th June (Monday) Marched down to Lake at Ismailia and stayed all the morning. Some were in for a swim while we remainder were lying down in the Palm Groves eating water melons. It is a beautiful spot just here. Some of the rough class amongst the Australians were upsetting natives and "pinching" their goods. In the afternoon we were packing up &amp; loading waggons.</p>
