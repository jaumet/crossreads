<p>for Nugget. Of course I found him where I expected, asleep in bed, &amp; if I had left him there he'd have slept all day, but I just pulled him out &amp; we went &amp; had Breakfast.</p>
<p>All that day we put in looking round London, The National Art Gallery, Westminster Abbey, The British War Museum Etc, &amp; numerous other sights that abounds in London. At 4.30 pm we again met Jim &amp; we went down to Hyde Park to Listen to the Grenadier Guards Band &amp; to lie on the Grass &amp; talk of home.</p>
<p>That night we all went to the pictures for a change, &amp; after Supper went home again. The next day being Sunday; we did not get up until Late and then went for a stroll before meeting Jim at 2 pm, when we went for a run round in a Taxi. And so we spent the first leave in London &amp; were very sorry when the time came to go back to the Dull</p>
