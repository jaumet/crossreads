<p>which date we arrived at Suez.  The letters which I had written aboard were supposed to go off then, but as a matter of fact they were taken on to Egypt &amp; did not leave till about the end of July.  Mab would also have to wait the 4 or 5 weeks it would take for the letters to reach home.</p>
<p>Arthur called in again on me at 6.30 on his way to Quinns Post.  <strike>He was surprised</strike> He was very anxious as to whether I had received any mail &amp; when I answered in the affirmative he looked pretty glum because he had not received any &amp; he was hoping that something had gone amiss.  I cheered him up however &amp; told him a lot of my mail had not arrived.  I also gave him some more cigarettes.  He promised to call in again when he was passing.</p>
