<p>27/3/18 &ndash; La Basse Ville<br/>The cross country between James and my H.Q. runs along the old No Mans Land that existed before the Battle of Messines.  It is now very free from shell holes and scattered across it are a fair number of vegetable plots.  A broad guage peace railway line runs across here and there are remnants of flower and other gardens to be seen.
