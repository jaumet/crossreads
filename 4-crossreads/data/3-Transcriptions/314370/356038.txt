<p>June, 1916<br/>Tuesday 6<br/>Put in the early morning on the minature range but had a lot of trouble w/- the gun.<br/>Work as usual during the day.<br/>Am beginning to get worried, have had no letter from home since March.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7<br/>Working hard on Mechanism now for next Sat.<br/>Class did very well yesterday, even better than Sat last</p>
