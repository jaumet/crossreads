 Devons were called out &amp; half a dozen Egyptians were killed &amp; a number wounded.  A sign of the times. C. arrived home dead drunk to-night &amp; caused trouble. 
 Tuesday 15th Feb. 
 Am making headway with French under tuition of Dr. Cochenos. Latin I am also going on with in order to get up to matriculation standard as soon as possible.  Dr. Boutazy thinks I should stay in Egypt after the war &amp; go to Brerut College to take medical degree.  He is giving me all the practice he can here.
