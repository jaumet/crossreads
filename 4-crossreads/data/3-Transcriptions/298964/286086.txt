<p>We were camped on the Beach for 31 days  during that time the officers &amp; Sergs done nothing but drink  It was disgraceful the way both lots carried on.  The troopers could not get a taste and one officer refused to sign an order for the men to get a drop of alcohol when it was obtainable at A.I.F Canteen</p>
