
1916
35 new men from Alexandria arrive this morning & the likelihood is now of our being sent back to London.
1st October - SundayA memorable day of 25 hours.  Rather cold but no rain.  After work at 12-30 p.m., take tram home for lunch with our two Collegian young friends & Madame & Madamoiselle, as per their invitation.  After a very pleasant lunch & talk, the two leave (one to a College - M. Louis Leudet & other to for teaching having finished his studies & the other to the University M. Roger Hautemer).
