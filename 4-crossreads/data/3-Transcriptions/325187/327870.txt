<p>6 May 1916 Saturday<br/>Got some Decent Uniforms again &amp; the Head Waiters will not now put me where I cannot hear the Band or give many Flowers on the TABLE, as appearances go a long way with this type of man both in Egypt as in any other place.<br/>(Nowell I thought would have gone to Australia but he says like everyone &amp; everything else it is apr&egrave;s la guerre.) omit<br/>Met a sgt of the old Bushman a man called Keenan, who has come over to Cairo to open a branch of the <strike>G</strike> Commonwealth Bank in Cairo.</p>
