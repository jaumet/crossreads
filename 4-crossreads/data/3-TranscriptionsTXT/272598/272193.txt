 Monday 4.6.17 Reveille 7 Am.  Breakfast 8, Parade 9 Am  Lecture "Gas" 9-12 noon.  Dinner 12 to 2 pm.  to  Lecture &amp; Helmet practice 2 pm to 4 pm.  Walked to Albert &amp; went to 5th Army Cinema show. 
 Tuesday 5.6.17 Lectures &amp; Gas Helmet drill as yesterday; fairly interesting but very hot &amp; oppressive in hut where lectures are given. 
 Wednesday 6.6.17 Last day of the School.  Put through Smoke clouds, Tear Gas &amp; Cloud Gas.  Have benefited by the 3 days instruction.  Ford car called to take us back to Bapaume at 3.45 pm; had 3 blow outs &amp; 1 puncture on way back.  Sent home saw edged bayonet to Father.
