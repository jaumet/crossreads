 Charleroi - a journey which knows no joys.&nbsp; Night on the train. 
  Feb. 19th  
 Pass through Bailleul.&nbsp; Steenwench.&nbsp; Armentieres.&nbsp; Lille.&nbsp; Tournai &amp; Charleroi.&nbsp; Walk with pack from Charleroi to Beaumont - a distance of 23 kilo-metres (15 miles). 
 Billet for night at Beaumont. 
  Feb. 20th  
 Set out from Beaumont and arrive at Jeumont.
