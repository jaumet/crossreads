<p>6th July:- Thursday   94&deg;<br/>To-day is rather cool again  We were called up this afternoon &amp; full instructions given for departure. We are to pack all on Saturday, receive ground sheets &amp; all necessaries &amp;  to be ready to move at 24 hours notice</p>
<p>7th July:- Friday<br/>We believe this week-end will see us off. Hot to-day 99&deg;  With an old Sydney pal, G. Amaurin go for a stroll until 9.30 pm &amp;  after a cup of tea at Anzac Hostel, get to sleep</p>
