 The car goes up Adderley St. over the Kloof Nek, along The Twelve Apostles, rising 700 ft. above sea level, and down into Camp's Bay. The fare for the circular journey with break at Camp's Bay is one shilling for Australians and New Zealanders in khaki. The actual time taken is one hour twenty minutes.
