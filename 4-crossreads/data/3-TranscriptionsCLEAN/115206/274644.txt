  
 March 1918 Friday 15 
 Messines Another fairly quiet day. Weather splendid, very little hostile artillery. Hun got 1 target from 40 rds of 5.9 down the 51 way. Battery did an aeroplane shoot.  Mr Mays parti to the A.T.P. 6.35pm.Fritz sent a few decent ones across. Received balance of articles from customs department. Knipe went back to W. Lines.  Finished my Australia, and engrave same with penknife. Received paper form Stella. 11.30 pm Fritz shelling fairly regularly.
