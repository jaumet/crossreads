<p>29 October 1916<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>One more Sunday at [indecipherable] Front. The travesty of a peaceful Sabbath. Short tucker, Augment supply from canteen. Nothing doing today. Improve our dugout. Rain as usual. Intermittent bombardment all day &amp; night. our artillery do not give Fritz much rest  Another great bundle of letters. &ndash; 19. 50 letters in 3 days &ndash; a record &amp; oh.! how welcome each one is</p>
