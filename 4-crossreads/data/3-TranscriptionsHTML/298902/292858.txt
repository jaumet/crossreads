Reported in at the orderly room &amp; then returned to where we had left our gear &amp; had dinner after which we visited a friend at D.A.D.O.S. &amp; yarned till 3 P.M. when we decided to return to our unit for the night it being bitterly cold.  Walked part of the way &amp; part by lorry arrived for tea &amp; spent a good night &amp; collected some reading matter.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4th Feb  Returned to Beaumont by car about 10.30 &amp; spent the remainder of the day reading before the fire, very cold &amp; snowing again.  Had a very fair night with three blankets on the floor.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5th Feb  After breakfast we went down to camp to collect some rations, then had lunch at 12, after which we went to the station, a lorry giving us a lift.  Here we had a long wait in a bitter cold with snow falling, part of which time we spent in a caf&eacute;.
