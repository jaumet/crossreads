They went at the picket armed with all sorts of outlandish weapons but were driven back one being captured, together with all their kits.</p>
<p>Sunday 27th September<br/>On guard all day no excitement.</p>
<p>Monday 28th September<br/>Loafed all day</p>
<p>Tuesday 29th September<br/>Ceremonial parade in the afternoon. About 30 native police who had been captured were taken into our service.<br/>Menu slightly strengthened by the introduction of fish &amp; lime juice.</p>
<p>Wednesday 30th September<br/>One of the "Starvation Six" caught a fish.</p>
<p>In the afternoon we hoisted the wireless pole &amp; dug trenches.</p>
