
Thursday 1/2/17
Visited the General who is at present in the line, he is very fit, had 3 lovely letters from Doff.
Friday 2/2/17
Moved to Acid Drop Camp. Very cold indeed, wrote to Doff.
Saturday 3/2/17
Went up the line & had a good look around, saw General Wisdom, returned about midnight.
Sunday 4/2/17
Not feeling too good, remained in bed all day, bitterly cold.
Monday 5/2/17
Visited General Wisdom, still very cold, going into the line tomorrow night.
