<p>Monday 26 March 1917.</p>
<p>On leave. Caught 10.45 a.m. train from Lyndhurst. Spent day very nicely at Dandenong &amp; caught 4.48 pm. train to City, and 6.35 pm train to &quot;<u>dear old</u>&quot; Seymour camp. In camp 9.45. Bed 10.15 pm.&nbsp;</p>
