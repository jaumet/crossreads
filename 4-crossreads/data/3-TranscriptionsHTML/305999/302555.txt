<p>2/12/16<br/>At 5.30 AM my platoon was aroused and we had a hasty breakfast.  Packed up and marched to Bde Hqrs  we got into a motor bus which took us to Frecourt.  Then we had to walk to Longerval wood  we camped there in a hut and went on fatigue.  remained a few days</p>
<p>5/12/16<br/>At 4 PM</p>
