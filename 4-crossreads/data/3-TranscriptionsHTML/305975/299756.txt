<p>on. 6.till 8.   Plenty of Beer about  all the boys are all drunk more or less  relieved 8.P.M.  turned in  went on again 12.till 2.   some of them are still singing and making a row.   turned in again 2.30.  very cold and raining<br/>Wednesday Dec. 25.12.18<br/>Turned out 6 AM very cold dissmissed from line guard  Breakfast  nice warm day  all the boys are happy and fighting  Plenty of free fights here.   had Papers from maud.</p>
