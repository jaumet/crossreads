<p>and all other fatigue duties &amp; there will be plenty of work now sorting our luggage from the hold ready to disembark at Suez if we are to embark.</p>
<p>Had another lecture on flies, bugs etc. I dodged down the stairs &amp; had a sleep. I have heard enough about these things in other lectures.</p>
<p>I have put my watch away. Every morning the time changes &amp; you will find your watch about 10 minutes or so fast. Then at 12 noon each day it is altered again so you are continually putting your watch back. I only take it out when on Orderly Corporal.</p>
