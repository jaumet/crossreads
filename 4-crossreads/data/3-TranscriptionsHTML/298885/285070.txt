<p>Tues May 30th &ndash; 16<br/>About 1AM every battery of ours, both big and small guns, opened up a bombardment for 1 &frac12; hours. It was a good representation of a wild stormy night with flashes here, there, and everywhere.</p>
<p>Wed May 31st  &ndash; 16<br/>About 3PM the enemy opened up on us and after about an hour found out position which resulted in 2 men being wounded with shrapnel. As I was coming off duty a shell landed</p>
