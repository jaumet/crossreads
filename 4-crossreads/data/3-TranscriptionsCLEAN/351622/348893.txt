  
 21 September 1916 Thur Went into Poperinghe Ambulance. On the road met Northumberland Fusiliers Machine Section who made me finish their water bottle of Beer. Letter from Mrs. Johnson. Colonel Geoffrey Hughes requires 4 bottles of whisky at canteen. Wrote to Aus. 
 22 September 1916 Fri Went into Ypres for Drinks to Asylum. Afterwards going to have a look over the town impressed by the damage done. Cathedral & Cloth Hall. Shells on Road coming home. Good game of soccer. Johnny Summers evacuated. Letter from Norm Burgess. [indecipherable] Capt Needham sick. German in shell hole
