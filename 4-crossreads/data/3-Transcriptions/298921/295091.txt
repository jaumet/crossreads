<p>April, 1915<br/>Wednesday 28<br/>Field day on the Desert &amp; in the evening went to see Tommy Redford of Zeitoun he told me he had seen you ere leaving Australia. Hospital train arrived last night from &ndash;<br/>Fleas bad now everywhere &amp; flies in millions.</p>
