<p>THURSDAY, APRIL 10, 1919.</p>
<p>41</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. On deck reading &amp;</p>
<p>playing games all day. Played</p>
<p>euchre with the Sisters in their</p>
<p>lounge at night. We sighted</p>
<p>land about noon &amp; the lights</p>
<p>of Adelaide about 9 pm.</p>
<p>Days run 324 miles</p>
