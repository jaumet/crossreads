Nov. 30 Tuesday
Bright clear but very cold, doing little pick shovel work to get warm. Walked to Beach Sap for rain water lying in hollows covered with thick ice dirty but lovely after long fast, got back 3-30 p.m., made up diary. Collected Mess tin full of snow to melt down for water. Lone Pine bombarded terribly by Turks gun fire reported 150 Casualties on Tuesday. Today's rations bacon tea for breakfast. Bully Bisc. for dinner. Tea same as dinner.
Dec. 1st Wednesday
Day off duty, collected wood rain water for trench use. Cooking eating most of afternoon. Evening very keen, day glorious sun. Fired [rocket ?[ opp No. 2 Bomb Pit.
