<p>January 1915<br/>Wednesday 20<br/>Hard days work. Skirmishing. Band marched on at the Head of Batt. Men paid. Received a letter from Sydney dated 16/12/14. One of our scouts disrated for making an ass of himself. Batt in pretty good state of efficiency.</p>
<p>Thursday 21<br/>Skirmishing again</p>
