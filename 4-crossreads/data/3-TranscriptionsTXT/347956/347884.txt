 10.2.15 
 General Godley inspected us while we were out; we arrived back in camp shortly after 3 oclock this afternoon &amp; did not feel inclined to go anywhere after &amp; turned into bed early 
 Wed 10th The bugle had us up at 5.15 this morning &amp; had to bustle about &amp; feed our horses &amp; ourselves &amp; get out on parade at 7 o'clock;  We were then marched into Cairo &amp; formed up with  the infantry, a whole division (17,000) taking part in the parade;  we did not have a very hard day &amp; returned to camp about dark, a big mail is to be issued tonight
