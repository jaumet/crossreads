<p>one side of the road is French &amp; the other Belgian but you would never know it for there is practically nothing to tell you at present.<br/>We finished up in "Steenvoorde" &amp; in the centre of the town, people were selling great rolls of cloth they had them placed on stands, all French or Belgian towns that I have seen have a fair sized square in the middle of their town &amp; all roads lead into this.<br/>After we were billeted we bought some coffee off the old dame at the farm house &amp; had a bit of dinner for we were fairly hungry, &amp; our "cooker's" had got lost on the road  About 3 oclock Len came up &amp; we went into the town with 2 or 3 more for a good feed for we were</p>
