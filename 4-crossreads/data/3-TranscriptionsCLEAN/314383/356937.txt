August 10thWilson sent a message in stating that 3-400 Tribesman were raiding the people.  Took 6 Sgts 2 Guns &  Petros's 50 men out to them.  Rode 20 miles &  sighted the enemy who not seeing our full force on a/c of the dust hiding the rear files, galloped to a ridge 4 miles east of the road.  Too late to fight as it was falling dusk so came to the top of a ridge 1/2 mile west of the people & there extended to the neighbouring hillocks.
