 1/21/2 
 Tuesday 30 October 1917. 
 Posted letter. 
 Watered camels. 
 Moved out at 5 pm sharp & commenced a trek till midnight. 
 (Wed 31 st  Oct continued.) Beersheebah by our guns. 
 2 redoubts have fallen and at 2 pm B-h may fall as our artillery fire is very effective. 
 Taweil-el-Habair is the hill from which we watched the bombardment and behind which the collecting station was. 
 H Qrs also dug in there.
