<p>Sunday also, &amp; the work of chaplains came under review &amp; the question of Supernumeray levy (Current) was discussed and a unanimous vote that the Conference should be appealed to to pay it.<br/>On Friday it rained all day, &amp; blew, the creeks and all flooded &amp; in some parts damage has been done.</p>
<p>Saturday last I had a funeral at Salisbury, a death from Meningitis.</p>
<p>Sunday I had 2 services one at 9.15 &amp; the other at 10.30 the latter was a very fine service.<br/>On Sunday night I got instructions to be ready</p>
