<p>There is not one building that has a roof to it let alone 4 walls, even the cellars have all been blown in and all wells and watering places poisoned</p>
<p>All intersections and crossroads blown up by mines without leaving room at either side for the smallest of traffic. The tall trees planted at equal distance apart years ago on both sides of the streets would prove a very pretty picture in Spring. But all these have been cut down 2 feet from the ground and left</p>
