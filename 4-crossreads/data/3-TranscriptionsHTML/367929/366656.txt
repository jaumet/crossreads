<p>July 1915<br/>24 Sat<br/>Went on destroyer Colne with Col Stoddart and 2 NZ officers to view the coast towards Suvla Point &ndash; ES Sutton and Sgt Gregory and 1 or 2 others wounded swimming</p>
<p>25 Sun<br/>A chill from being out at sea yesterday</p>
