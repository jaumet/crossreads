
<p>JAN. &nbsp; MONDAY 31 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Had physical drill before breakfast &amp; Lieut Rorke took us for Morse signaling after breakfast. Paid in afternoon so did no drill. Got the 20 past 4 train &amp; got to Lisheen early. Linda &amp; Self out on tennis court after tea, had a long talk over things &amp; promised ourselves another before I left. Late leave was cut down to 4 per subsection so did not get late leave</p>
