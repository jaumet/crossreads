Tuesday 5/6/17Quiet day, feeling tired &ndash; musketry.Wednesday 6/6/17Field day towards Hamel very useful work done.Thursday 7/6/17Visited by Genl Smythe &amp; Wisdom &ndash; Won final of Rugby football against 25th.Friday 8/6/17Musketry in morning. Close order drill in afternoon &ndash; lecture by Close at Senlis, wrote to Doff and General.Saturday 9/6/17Went by car to Amiens with Lloyd and Close of the Field Artillery.
