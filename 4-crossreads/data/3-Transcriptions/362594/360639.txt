<p>[Janvier 31 Vendredi]<br/>Receive letter from mother about Uncle Willy's death, a beautiful touching letter &amp; a fine tribute to Uncle he had a great heart always giving, living up to his motto always.</p>
<p>Also letter from May G. very touching.</p>
<p>I am immensely anxious to be home at once, I am very anxious about the ones at Home.</p>
<p>Finlay crashes doing the falling leaf stunt, not hurt.</p>
<p>[Fevrier<br/>1 Samedi]<br/>No flying very stormy.  Day in town with Beaton playing billiards.  Home today felt Uncle Willy's loss very much very sad.</p>
<p>To be launched on the Pup on Monday hope it is fine.</p>
