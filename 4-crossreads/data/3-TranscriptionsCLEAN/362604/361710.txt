  
 Bulford 15.7.18 
 Dear Dad, Mum, & George, 
 It was letter day yesterday but I went for a bike spin last night & Tommy & I tried to coax a nice mastiff dog Home for a mascot & we got lost in the process & when we eventually got back to the Mess it was late.  So I had supper on Cold Pudding, you know my old delight, & lots of cream & it was "Lights Out" before I got Home to bed.  Ellis & T. have gone out for a spin to-night while I stay Home to write.
