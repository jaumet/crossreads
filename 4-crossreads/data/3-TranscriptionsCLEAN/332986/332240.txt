 28-8-1917 Blowing a gale all day. Showery on and off. 7-45.P.M. now calm and fine. 3rd Bty knocked about a bit, 8 horses killed and 2 men wounded 2.A.M. Nothing doing weather too rough. Farrier of third Bty and Q M Sgt of 1st Bty gone on leave to England 
 29-8-1917 Showery and cold, I am supposed to go on leave tonight, Front quiet 
 30-8-1917 Cloudy, an ammunition party belonging to us, cut up. One man Baldwin blown to atoms, both horses missing, another horse had a leg blown off, another with a great wound in thigh. Drivers coming back in two and threes. Should have gone on leave today, but wire recieved, Channel blocked, owing to rough sea. The man killed was with us only three days
