<p>December  1918.<br/>Monday 16</p>
<p>At Sea. H.S."Karoola."<br/>Weather fine and clear.<br/>Nice smooth sea and a few newcomers are noticeable on deck.<br/>Up till non today we had steamed another 326 miles for 24 hours.<br/>Played poker and lost &pound;1. Passed five ships going northl.<br/>We are at present off the coast of Spain and should reach Gibraltar by 5 pm tomorrow.<br/>George better today.</p>
