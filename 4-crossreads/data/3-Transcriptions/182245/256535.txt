
<p>254</p>
<p>1917 JANUARY</p>
<p>20 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; cool wind blowing. Fixing</p>
<p>up horselines, harness hooks ect</p>
<p>&amp; exercising horses in the morning</p>
<p>&amp; cleaning harness in afternoon.</p>
<p>They are having one section</p>
<p>on duty per day to take up</p>
<p>amunition ect so we will</p>
<p>only be going up one day in</p>
<p>three. There was a good bit</p>
<p>of firing done during the day</p>
<p>but no shells came near us.</p>
