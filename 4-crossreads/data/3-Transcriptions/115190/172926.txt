<p>1<sup>st</sup> Nov.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; I leave per Motor Lorry for Beyroute at 10 am.&nbsp; Our route lies through the Lebanon Hills &amp; it is a picturesque journey all the way.&nbsp; The Turks have blown up a Viaduct &amp; we see the Canadian Bridging Corp at work repairing it.
