 February 1918 Monday 18 
 Messines Well our friend the Bosche was giving a fine exhibition of daring flying to-day. Went down to canteen and "Pip" having some lunch in Gunn's company. Weather frosty and clear, very piercing wind from S.E. Garfoot out on O.P. all day. Chas Adey, on a visit to W.Lines. Had a shot at old Fritz with the Lewis gun.  Went on duty at 12 to 2am during the night.
