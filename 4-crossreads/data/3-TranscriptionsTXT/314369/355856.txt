 10 July 1915 Saturday 
 Duty Company We have to find about a dozen guards   &amp;  fatigue parties 14th leave during the night so as not to run any risk of shrapnel like the unfortunate 16th 
 1915 July 11 Sunday 6th After Trinity 
 Church parade this morning Turks make a rally  &amp;  make a devil of a row but do no damage The Monitor fires about a dozen large shells (10") over our heads during the afternoon.
