
<p>326</p>
<p>1917 APRIL</p>
<p>2 MONDAY</p>
<p>A fair cow of a day. The ground</p>
<p>was covered with snow in</p>
<p>the morning &amp; it was blowing</p>
<p>a cold gale all day &amp; snowing</p>
<p>as well. Cleaning &amp; fixing up</p>
<p>a loose box for my horse in</p>
<p>morning &amp; lay &amp; read all afternoon</p>
<p>Had a letter from Alan tonight</p>
<p>&amp; he has his full lieutenancy</p>
<p>now &amp; is a flight commander</p>
<p>as well.</p>
