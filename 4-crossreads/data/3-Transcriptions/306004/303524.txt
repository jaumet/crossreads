<p>13.9.16<br/>Known as the "neutrals" &amp; are a fine collection of "take downs" one lad there a few months ago discovered one of the Neutrals using a double headed penny &amp; was kicked about so badly for making the discovery that he died next day;<br/>The Dr has been giving demonstrations today of how to carry a wounded man on horseback &amp; we had the usual parade</p>
<p>14.9.16</p>
<p>Thurs 14th<br/>Yesterday afternoon an Austrian Officer was brought from Bir-El-Abd, he had given himself up to our patrols, Turks are coming in daily &amp; the Austrian Officer say the Turks will all surrender as he does not believe any of them will fight but the majority of the troops</p>
