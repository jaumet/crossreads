<p>Sunday 12-11-16</p>
<p>Reached Southampton about 7.30 AM this morning and went straight aboard a train which was waiting to take the leave party. Went to Horseferry Road and got a new change of clothes from here I went to the Shaftesbury Hotel.</p>
