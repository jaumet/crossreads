  
 No 3 London General Hospital Wandsworth London SW 8th Aug 1916 My dear Sister, As you will see by the above, I am now again in England, only this time under different circumstances, as you know the last time I was on leave. I was sent over a couple of days ago, they dont keep you in France unless you are bad, as cases come in continually from the trenches & room must be kept for them.
