 1916 September 6 Wednesday 
 Have job of help put up meat room in cook house &ndash; Mitchell &amp; self drift over to Y M for cup tea &amp; biscuit at 11 a.m. strenuous work, needs nourishment. Great &amp; pleasant surprise. Bob arrives having had his transfer fixed up at last &ndash; Johnston leaves for England to see wife. Fysh sick. Bronchitis. Bob, Cart &amp; go for walk. visit cemetery. then hop pickers. Talk to Belgian girl. Daughter of farm.  help pick hops . Y M for supper.
