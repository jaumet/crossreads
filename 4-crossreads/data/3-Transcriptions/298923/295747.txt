<p>22 March 1918 Friday (Whole page crossed out)<br/>Bethlehem    Also Australasians-3 more letters  Mother &amp; Day Feb 3rd &amp; one from May addressed as usual to <u>Captain</u> Weir.  I did Cable sorry never delivered.<br/>You would enjoy all these old Holy places, am afraid the average soldier then is past much fervour now here-<br/>One soldier blew an oil lamp out in the Holy Sepulchre that had been burning 2000 years or more.  Such is notoriety-   The poor give oil to keep the lamps burning in the Tabernacles-</p>
