26th. Wednesday.
Got to work early cleaning up trenches, collecting bombs ammunition well we did for about 3pm. he attacked our right flank with hand bombs kept up all night, We were loosing heavily by casualties 50 percnt; gone by 11pm. so called on 18th 19th. Battns. to reinforce us we quietened him down, but he came at it again next day.
27th. Thursday.
Our boys did great work and pushed Fritz back a little. D.Coy 17th. relieved us about noon we fell back along Communications T till dark.
