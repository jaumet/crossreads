<p>is going to get some splendid assistance from England, who when all said &amp; done will be the deciding factor in the war. News has just come through of a wonderfully successful raid by the 9th. Battalion, they killed 40 odd Germans took prisoners 26 men &amp; 2 officers &amp; brought back 2 machine guns &amp; a trench mortar, that will take some beating<br/>Our Battalion received to-day a congratulatory letter from the 2nd Army Corp on the successful raid we made though we did not get so many prisoners as some of the others, we got a lot of valuable information.<br/>A few days ago a large fleet of our aeroplanes crossed well over the German lines, they carried liquid fire with them &amp; when they came to any crops they shot this stuff over them</p>
