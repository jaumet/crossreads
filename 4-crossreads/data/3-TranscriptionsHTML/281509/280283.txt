<p>1 March 1916<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Sleep all forenoon after guard duty.  Draw fortnightly pay <br/>at 11.30 am.  Dull showery afternoon, clears later.  Party including, Mary, Janet &amp; Essie, Jean Aberdeen, Nurse S[indecipherable] &amp; Miss Cameron come up. Have <br/>boska tea in tent. T. Temperly &amp; friend come too. Show ladies round &amp;; hear concert at night. Farewell to Reinforcements &amp; 10th Field Amb. Good. Scurry in getting guests off in motor.</p>
