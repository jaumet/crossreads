<p>Our Ambulance is at present split up into several sections. A Section complete is over near Estaires somewhere with the Brigade, B Sect tent sub-div is attached to 3rd Canadian C.C.S., &amp; C Sect tent to 17th Brit. C.C.S., while the bearer sub-divs of B &amp; C Sects are over near Reninghelst? I don't yet know the exact number of casualties during the recent stunts but I believe we lost between 70 &amp; 80 killed, wounded &amp; gassed. No less than 3 Dist. Con. Medals &amp; 13 Mil. Medals were awarded to 14th Field Amb.</p>
<p>Thursday 8th Nov 1917 (7 pm):-</p>
<p>On Tuesday evening went into Poperinghe &amp; saw the "Duds" &ndash; an English military concert party. The costumes &amp; items were good &amp; the big extemporary theatre was well filled. One forgot for the time that he was anywhere near the line and in range of German big guns. Poperinghe has been bombed &amp; shelled on numerous</p>
