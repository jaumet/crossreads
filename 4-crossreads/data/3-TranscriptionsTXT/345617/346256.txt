
Novembre 30Nothing of much account to report today. Some of my Sec. were working in camp & some have gone out tonight. I am in camp owing to having bad feet. I went to [indecipherable] today to give evidence in a case for Court Martiall but the case had been postponed for a week.
Decembre 1Out today with Captain Fatrell siting positions for strong points & barbed wire on Corps Ridge Defence line. Section engaged on strong points at Support line & taking wiring material to Corps line defences.
