  
 Arrived out of way Station before daylight marched over to Bouchon. 
 25 October 1916 Wed Griffin is turned down with the two franks. Appletrees Scotty & McGee make themselves a nuisance. Issue of rum. Blacksmiths shop. Awful billet. A steep old village lying in the hills. 
 26 October 1916 Thur Inspection of helmet. Griff gets his water bottle full of cognac. Lettuces [eschats] [indecipherable] Walk over the fields finishing up in the church yard. All in bed 6 pm. Moving again tomorrow.
