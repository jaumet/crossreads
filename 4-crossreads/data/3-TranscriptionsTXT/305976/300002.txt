 At the Lena Ashwell Concert party I met some of the old boys who I had not seen since leaving Sydney School finished to day &amp; my leave commences Monday to Alexandria. C. Speering D Harold &amp; myself going together. The school has been a pleasant break from the Field but became very monotonous towards the finish I hope this will be my last school Sunday May 26 Hot day did not go out. Wrote Mail.
