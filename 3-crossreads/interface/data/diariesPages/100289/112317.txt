Sunday 30th to Tuesday 1.1.18
Sunday very quiet, papers came through to proceed to Headquarters
Monday 1st Nursing staff entertained Patients Pantry staff on New Years Night, the night of the 1st Jan; fine concert given supper during interval. Fine opportunity to say good-by to all staff as practically the whole of them were there.
2.1.18 (Wednesday)
Feel very miserable having to leave hospital. Caught 9.30 Am
