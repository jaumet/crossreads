 Ministry of Munitions of War, Whitehall Place, London, S.W. 1. 
 17.xii.17 
 Dear Judge, 
 Just a line to wish you all the best possible in this so called festive season of Yuletide. 
 As you will see from the above, I am still engaged on War work, though not of a desperately dangerous character.  I like the work, it is infinitely preferable to the Law &ndash; but the hours are rather long, and I come here on Sundays.
