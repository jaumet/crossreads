<p>September</p>
<p>4:  Inspected by Gen. Cox who has returned from England &amp; taken over the Bde.from Bob Meredith.</p>
<p>10:  Aust. Imp. Camel Corps arrived today, 1000 strong &amp; presented a fine sight.</p>
<p>17:  Left Romani at 6 p.m. reaching Abd on the 18th, pushing on to Salmana at 5 p.m., camping for the night with our Bde.  2nd &amp; 3rd Bdes. rode on to Mazar.</p>
<p>19:  Mazar attacked by light horse at daybreak, but our force was too weak &amp; had to retire at noon; returning to Salmana , leaving here with wounded at 11 p.m.  17 killed &amp; 43 wounded.</p>
<p>20:  Reached Kilo 47 at 6 a.m., handing wounded to clearing station. These had been transported across 40 miles of desert &amp; were under canvas in 14 hours.  Left here at midnight,</p>
<p>21:  Arrived in camp at noon &amp; glad to have a few hours rest.</p>
<p>27:  Presentation of decorations won on Gallipoli.  Cpl. McRae received D.C..M. &amp; received a fine ovation from the boys.</p>
