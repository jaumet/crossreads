<p>&amp; it won't affect us much longer</p>
<p>they caught "Flash Jack" this morning this is the rotter that got aboard at Plymouth somehow &amp; managed to keep clear till the present, they say that this gentleman has never been to France but has been in Blighty the whole time malingering &amp; robbing his mates, hes a proper master &amp; looks it</p>
<p>Austin is his name, they now have him confined in the barbers shop &amp; the Guard looking after him.</p>
<p>The whole of us paraded in our Military Districts this afternoon we all had out "aeroplane colors" on, the rolls were called, my name being among the Tasmanians.</p>
<p>After this we were all supposed to go through the fumigator but it was just as big a farce as the clothing stunt] was, no names were taken, so you can guess what it was like.</p>
