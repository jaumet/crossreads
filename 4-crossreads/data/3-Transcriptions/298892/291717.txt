<p>I am afraid my letters will be considerably curtailed in future as we have some strenuous work ahead and probably I will be unable to get time to write more than a few lines. At any rate I will keep you constantly informed of my welfare even if it is only by the Field Service Card.<br/>I must now close as our batch of men for evening dressings have now arrived.<br/>Your loving son<br/>Eugene.</p>
