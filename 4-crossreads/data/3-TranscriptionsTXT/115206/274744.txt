
June 1918Sunday 23
On the road, Flexicourt.Weather fine, with no rain whatever. Plenty of dust though passed through several decent villages.Pulled in to a nice camp at 2 pm.Smithy on police duty .Walked with [indecipherable] Smithy and Gunn to [indecipherable] ,had some very decent eufs, viewed some bon mamselles .Fritz aeroplane bombed village near by,, rather large bombs.
