  
 Sunday 27th:  Left School. Rejoined Bn at Cantun Corner near Neuve Eglise Thursday 31st:  Entrained at Neuve Eglise for Lumbres. Marched from Lumbres to Coulomby. Saturday Feb 2nd:  School re-assembled at Bayenghem. Sat 16th:  Rejoined Bn. Thursday 28th:  Escort to Boulogne. Friday 1st March:  Returned. Monday 4th:  Entrained at Lumbres; detrained at Steenwerck. Marched to Kortepyp Camp near Neuve Eglise. Tuesday 5th:  Marched to le Rossignol camp.
