<p>October Sunday 14 1917</p>
<p>Parker sent on leave today.   My turn it should have been.   Am wondering why it was altered.<br/>Afternoon off.   MacB &amp; I out for walk.  tea in Wimereux.   Met Jock.  Malaga &amp; Porto.  Back church.   [indecipherable]  Billy Leeming in I ward.   Marked up for Blighty tomorrow.</p>
