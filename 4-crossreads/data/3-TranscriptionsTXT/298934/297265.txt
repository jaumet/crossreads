 27th June Sunday 1915 
 At midnight last night we passed Gib.  It was a bright moonlight night so we were able to see it clearly.  A searchlight shone on us several times.  The Spanish coast was quite near &amp; Africa also showed up plainly.  It has grown much cooler.  Passed very close to Cape St. Vincent. 
 28th June Monday 1915 
 Passed the Finisterre light at 9 p.m. &amp; entered the Bay.  Very calm. 
 29th June Tuesday 
 Buried a sailor died of dysentery.
