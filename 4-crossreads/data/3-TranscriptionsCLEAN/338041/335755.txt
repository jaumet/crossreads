  
 Nov. 21st. Return of Bearer to the Quarry - Preparations for departure - A miserable train trip followed by a more miserable march - "Where are we? - Somewhere in France". The thoroughness of Fritz's positions "avant l'offensive"! (Dugouts etc) - Back to the good old Huts. 
 Nov. 22nd. We lose our O.C. much to our regret. We are all decidedly pleased to be away from the war & its Horrors - A trip to a neighbouring village - "Vaudeville". 
 Nov. 23rd. General duties - News of "Britannic" - An expensive bath in Albert. - Promotion of Capt. Donald - Our air-service. 
 Nov. 24th. Mail from London & Home - Our recent camp & its surroundings
