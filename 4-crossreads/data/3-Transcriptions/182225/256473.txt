
<p>224</p>
<p>DEC.&nbsp;&nbsp; WEDNESDAY 20&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Raining most of day. We all went under gas in the morning the other side of No 10 Camp. We had it 20 times stronger than we will get it at the front, &amp; we never even got a whiff of it through our helmets. Out exercising horses in the afternoon.</p>
