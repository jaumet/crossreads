Still as sick as ever &amp; fed up with the place, the weather is very hot &amp; this makes it worse,  I managed to crawl out of bed &amp; go along to the canteen &amp; get a cup of tea, while there I strikes old Bandy a little bow legged chap who knocks about the wards doing any little odd jobs for the boys, by a marvellous piece of luck I got 3 rolls of films from him, I had been trying to get some everywhere but had no luck &amp; here I went &amp; picked a bunch up in one of the last places I expected as they are very scarce.</p>
