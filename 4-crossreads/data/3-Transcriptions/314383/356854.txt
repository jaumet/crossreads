<p>April 18<br/>The time has passed pretty monotonously <strike>the</strike>We have been fully employed at drill &amp; languages with an occasional trip to Baghdad.  Three days ago a big bunch of Turkish prisoners were marched through on their way to the embarking stage en route to India.  They were a weary, half starved ill clad bunch.</p>
