<p>12 May 1918 Sunday<br/>Travelled all night &ndash; Nowell Forth met me on the Platform at Ishmalia &amp; told me of Mardi Ritchie's engagement   He was well-  Breakfast at Rest Camp Gaza &amp; change trains at Ludd for Jerusalem- expect it will be 3 months ere I go Cairo again-<br/>Got letters Mother 13/3/18<br/> Mrs Strange   Day&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; "<br/>&amp;Mr Robertson who manages Cocketchedone now, also Papers &amp; Diary thank you for sending all-   My fountain pen bad</p>
