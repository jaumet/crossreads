<p>59</p>
<p>Left Suez about 5 pm The sea is freshening up a bit.</p>
<p>6th Feb. Thursday</p>
<p>Fairly breezy but fine On of the boys died last night. &amp; I had to carry him out of the Hosp to the place where they sew them up before going over the side Held the buriel Service this afternoon Sounded the Last Post and fired three volleys <s>of</s> from the firing party Very Sultry weather passed a few boats today.</p>
