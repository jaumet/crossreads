 &amp; on the Sinai Peninsula  Syria  Palestine frontier. After a sleepy night march, daylight on the 9th inst found us once again in fertile country, about 5 miles South of Rafa &amp; two miles from the Turkish entrenchments. our aeroplanes arriving soon after daylight reconnoitred all the surrounding country &amp; soon after their reports being received by the Commanding General we  commenced to  deployed and encircled the Turkish positions which  were  commenced about 1 &frac12; miles in from the coast.
