  
 Thur June 15th - 16 Received my 1st parcel. Met Ossie Whaites who is looking A1. 
 Fri June 16th - 16 Last night at 11.30 the gas alarm was sounded and after waiting for 1 1/2 hours we were told to get to bed again and then at 2AM the alarm came again and after another 2 hours wait we were ordered to bed again. I was fed up as I had to come on duty at 4AM.
