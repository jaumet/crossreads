<p>31st August<br/>I am still in hospital, the operation is over &ndash; i.e., a small one, the doctors have not agreed about me &amp; so I am off to dear old Blighty to be returned to Australia, more than likely, what feelings raise in the heart as we think of that land of our birth.</p>
