  
 inland Passed East London yesterday evening 
 Thursday 19th Table Mountain sighted about 11 AM Anchored in Table Bay in the evening The A29 (Sunic) just beating us 
 Friday 20th Nothing doing all day except to look at the Town. It looks well between the Mountain or rather Mountains as there are 2 of them & the sea. Hope we have a closer acquaintance. Quite a lot of small fish have been caught The cooks are makeing quite a god thing by charging for cooking them. Fishing is evidently quite a business as I have counted 97 small boats all around us this morning 
 Saturday 21st Went into the Wharf this morning & Marched to the Town Hall. Were given strict orders to go about in parties of not less than 4. The Hotels
