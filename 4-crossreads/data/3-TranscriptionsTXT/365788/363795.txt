 [YMCA Letterhead] No 2 Gen Hospital Cairo 7 Sept 15 My darling Sister, I leave with 9 others this morning for destination &amp; duty unknown, but we are told on good authority we are going to the Dardanelles as Stretcher bearers. I am afraid my chance of a Commission ends here. I got the &pound; 5 from the Anglo Egyptian Bank &amp; thank you very much I am very grateful.
