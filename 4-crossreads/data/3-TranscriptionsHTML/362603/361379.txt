<p>It has been snowing all day to day   &amp;   there is a covering of about 4 or 5 inches.  Lots of the Boys were snowballing   &amp;   had great fun.  There are Huge snow-balls   &amp;   snow men built everywhere.  It is such a pretty sight   &amp;   is quite warm.  But when she starts to melt lor, the muck   &amp;   isn't it cold too.<br/>I'll put on my Rubber Top Boots to-morrow<br/>Harry</p>
