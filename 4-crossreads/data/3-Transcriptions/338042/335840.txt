<p>with Mrs Wheeley &ndash; Preparations for departure from London &ndash; Battle still in German favour.<br/>Mar.31st<br/>Leave Elm Bank 8 a.m. Take train from Euston to Liverpool &ndash; Interesting journey through Clapham, Northampton, Rugby Crewe etc &ndash; Arrive Liverpool 5 p.m &ndash; Lodgings at 39 Seymour St.  An evening trip by Ferry to New Brighton.</p>
