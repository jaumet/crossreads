
Sept 1917Saturday 8thWas put on the Sanitary staff today with 2 others Started digging a pit for rubbish Rambled down the country lanes in the afternoon & bought about 18 very nice plums for 6d
Sunday 9thRained gently most of the day Went for a walk in the evening
Monday 10thA very fine warm day. On the sanitary job all day.
Tuesday 11thQuite hot today Things as usual
Wednesday 12thGave up the Sanitary Job & went on pariade. Went through Miniture Range Score 38 Went over to next camp
