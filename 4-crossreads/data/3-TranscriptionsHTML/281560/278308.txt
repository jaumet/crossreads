<p>position. It is knowledge that sort of makes ones toes tingle. We have our extra hour's "stand-to" night and morning in case he fires it off and tries to rush us. It is a very unpleasant prospect no matter how I look at it.<br/>12/10/16.<br/>Our pet mine has not exploded yet; and I still manage to sleep sweetly on top of it. Have been building a dressing station. Near Batt H.Q. I feel better down there, am not afraid to tread heavily. Hope "Hinie" nurses her a little longer for:- Hush!<br/>13/10/16.<br/>Went to No Mans land and assisted in a "Trench Raid."
