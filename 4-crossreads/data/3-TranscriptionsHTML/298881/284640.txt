<p>and has asked us on several occasions if we could have the band down here.  But it is a job to get the band at all regularly as the Brig. only allows one in the Brigade and they cannot stay with one Bn. all the time but visit each in turn.  Nothing cheers troops more than a good brass band.  It's grand.  War news is still very good, Italians and French both being very successful so far.  Enemy have been very busy about here lately bombing Hazebrouck a large and important town close by.  Last night bombs were dropped on St. Omer.  10 civilians, mostly women and children being killed.</p>
