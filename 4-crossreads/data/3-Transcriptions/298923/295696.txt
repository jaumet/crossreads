<p>30 January 1918 Wednesday(X to X crossed out)<br/>Finer, but very cold only another month of Winter.<br/>Boils still bad under arm otherwise fit.<br/>X Rather sorry to hear from wife that No 14 A.G.H. is being moved to Port Said so she will have to go there.<br/>No immediate chance of me getting a Base job at present, but we are spelling for a month here yet &amp; much may happen in the meantime. X<br/>Mails from England take much longer than mails from Australia at present.<br/>We see no Turks here <strike>at present</strike> but can hear the Artillery mainly.</p>
