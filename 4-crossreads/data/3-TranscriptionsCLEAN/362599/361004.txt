  
 F [6/5/17 Sun] Open air service & I don't play. Go to Cholderton to Kirk. Lovely old church. See Nelson's old home & where Prince of O. was entertained on way to London. 
 F [7/5/17 Mon] Again Practise Cricket. Go to bed early. Nin comes up to room & we talk. Wrote to Cyl. 7pm parades start. 
 F [8/5/17 Tues] Recd. letter from Jennie. Wrote. Doris (16) Mem. Mrs Trotter. E & I went to Albany Wards. Weather much cooler again. 
 F [9/5/17 Wed] E. go to S'bury. Nin makes 54. Our tem win easily by an innings I darn socks & mend white drawers. 
 W till 10am & then a lovely day [10/5/17 Thurs] Wrote to Mrs Stephen. Recd letters from Clare. Frances. Nette. 4 from Home. Bertha. Bess. Nell. Hilda. Dulce & Maisie Carter. Watched Cricket H.M.AT 8-120 Declared A.D.H 5-109. Drawn game. 
 F & Hot [11/5/17 Fri] Letter from Marge & Paper from Al Methven. Frank goes to Wmouth. Wrote to Jennie. Bess. Home. Marge. Dulce. Hilda. Maisie. Clare. The ward is very quiet to night. 
 F [12/5/17 Sat] A bit of a thunderstorm & a few points of Rain. D. Nett. & I go to S'bury. I am very hungry & have 4 meals in S'bury. Meet Charlie Wild in Cathedral & Bill Baker in Street, also Saunders from fire Station. We all go to Town Hall for Tucker but Chas & I only dine. We stay to hear the Pipers. We talk to bonnie English Lady. Go to Guest Home to eat & also to Congregational Hall. Wrote to Bert. & Mrs Stephens & Nette
