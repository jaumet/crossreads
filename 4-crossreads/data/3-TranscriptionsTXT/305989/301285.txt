
Sunday 2 December 1917Grazing in morning.  Nothing doing.  Picquet at night.  Wrote some letters.
Monday 3 December 1917Grazing in morning, quiet & windy day.
Tuesday 4 December 1917No grazing, dubbined up saddles instead.  Got news of move.  Left camp at 12 & proceeded to Jaffa.
Wednesday 5 December 1917Arrived at Jaffa 2.30 a.m. & stood to arms 4 & changed position of camp at daylight, stayed the day under easy gaze of Jacko.  Good orchestral concert at night.
Thursday 6 December 1917Still at Jaffa, usual routine, camped in orange orchard.
Friday 7 December 1917Rained after "stand to" and kept going all day off & on;  left Jaffa at 5 proceeded back to Ayunkara, arriving about 9.30 raining.
Saturday 8 November 1917Raining in early morning and continuing through the morning.  Jerusalem fell.
