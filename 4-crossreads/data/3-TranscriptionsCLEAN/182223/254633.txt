  
 Monday 1 February 1915 Six minor operations today. Varicocele. Axillary Abscess. Circumcissions (2), tonsils. Stricture. Dined with Col. Braund - 2 nd  Infty Battallion at 6pm. Walked to pyramids & Sphynx with Sister ? between 9 & 10-15 pm. Moon  not  behind the clouds. Sat under jaw of Sphynx for ten minutes. Posted letters home & many postcards. 
 Tuesday 2 February 1915 Some operations.  Reading & writing all day.  Some reading  
 Wednesday 3 February 1915 Operations this morning. 2 hernias. 1 appendix. 1 Mastoid 1 Empyema. Asked Dr Bird to be present, he came.  At 4-15 pm. set out for the great Pyramid. Climbed it down at 5-45 p.m. Name carved in the Stone as usual.  Visited Dr Adam Scheuber, of Cairo, at his Chalet above Mena House, at 6 p.m.
