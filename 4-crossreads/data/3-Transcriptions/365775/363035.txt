<p>French coast.  [first glimpse was a rugged chalklike appearance. 3.30 finds us in the harbour at Marseilles safe &amp; sound. I think we all offered up a prayer that we were over.  9 p.m. we are anchored at the wharf ready to disembark. Thus ends our journey across from Egypt to France]<br/>[Additional information transcribed from typescript at MC MSS 1025]<br/>Next day the 24th Saturday we are busy unloading guns &amp; horses taking them straight on to the train. 5 p.m. finds the last train pulling out for our trip across France, being now in the South &amp; we are to go straight across to the North &amp; into action.</p>
<p>Tis a beautiful sunny afternoon</p>
