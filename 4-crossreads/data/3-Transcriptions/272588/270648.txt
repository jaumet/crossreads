<p>Tuesday 22 January<br/>Weather wet though warm.  Day still spent in fatigue reading &amp; playing cards &amp; occasionally going to the pictures or a concert.</p>
<p>Wednesday 23 January<br/>Weather still dull but warm.  Ordinary routine of camp life.  A lecture on Armegeddon developed into a very dry sermon &amp; bored me to death.</p>
