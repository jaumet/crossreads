<p>It was read out on Parade tonight that all leave to England is stopped One knows absolutely nothing but there is a feeling of anticipation that we are on the eve of the biggest British effort yet attempted. Things have been too quiet this last month</p>
<p>But the artillery has been increasingly active these last few days. Last night was the heaviest bombardment I have heard on our immediate front I was on horse picket from 10 pm till 2 a.m and the roar of the guns big &amp; little never ceased for a minute while their flashes lit up the sky like an aurora</p>
