<p>April 1916<br/>Indian Ocean<br/>9th   Church Parade was held aboard at about 11 a.m. &amp; a few words were spoken by the C.O. about landing at Colombo where we expect to arrive on Wednesday week.  The sea is getting very calm &amp; we are experiencing good weather.<br/>10th   The weather is beautiful now &amp; the ship is hardly rolling.  We are well in the Indian Ocean now &amp; travelling N.N.W.<br/>12th   A big concert was held aboard to-night &amp; it proved a great success</p>
