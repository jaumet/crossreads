<p>of the camp.  We are "well in" with the Malays &amp; during conversation with one with a surprising amount of intelligence he asked us out to his place at Alexishafen, to shoot the coomool &amp; mooroe.</p>
<p>There are large numbers of Malays here (large that is in proportion to the white population) They have ever treated us well &amp; seem far in advance of the Chinese although the latter have their brother celestials in Australia far behind<br/>In the morning we were recalled to camp for purposes of identifycation. Ye Gods for identycation! We were lined up &amp; a gin who had been insulted stood grimacing on the steps before us. It reminded me of a Zulu witch smelling out a warrior for the sacrifice. Added to the humility of being "smelt out" a German (I will not say what) stood hounding the diseased looking female on to her work. But we passed through &amp; returned to the outpost.</p>
<p>Wednesday 28th Oct<br/>Continued with oupost duty, &amp; during the morning went down to the company's Farm where we had rather an exciting morning helping the Malays to lassoo</p>
