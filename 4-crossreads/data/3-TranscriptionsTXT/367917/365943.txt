 Pry. Belgium December 26th 1918 
 Mr. Justice Ferguson Supreme Court Chambers Sydney N.S.W. 
 Dear Sir, I yesterday (Christmas day) received your very valuable Xmas parcel containing cigarettes, tobacco, lollies &amp; etc, together with sprig of wattle, for which please accept my very best thanks &amp; I must say it is very kind &amp; thoughtful of you in thinking of us out here &ndash;
