<p>Monday 24 September</p>
<p>3 trips to Dickebush via Reninghelst and Ouderdom, and one trip to 29th Battn. Hd. Q.<br/>A repetition of Bombs from Fritz.<br/>The bearers are having a very rough time in the line, the casualties being heavy in all ambulances</p>
