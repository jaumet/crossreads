<p>[Letterhead Pilrig, Burwood Road, Hawthorn. (Auburn Station).]<br/>November 2. 1914.<br/>I have much pleasure in stating that Miss Thistlethwaite has nursed many cases for me. She has always given the greatest satisfaction to me &amp; to her patients, &amp; I consider her well qualified for any nursing position she may seek.<br/>Lewis J. Balfour M.B.B.S.B.A.</p>
