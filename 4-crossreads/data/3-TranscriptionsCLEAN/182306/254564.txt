 Feb 12th Wed 
 nice day with Calm sea Just passed a lot of flying fish. had another concert to night which was very good. another one of the boys died to night and is to be buried to morrow. 
 Feb 13th Thursday 
 Chap buried at ten oclock this morning. Nice day but fairly rough. 
 Feb 14th 
 Hardly a ripple on the water Ships slowed down so as we will not reach Colombo before our time
