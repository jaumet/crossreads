<p>Friday 14/8/14<br/>Two colliers are coming along side and coaling begins. I receive instructions from 1st Paymaster to supervise the loading of provisions. I board "Scharnhorst" where I meet my comrade Hoennecke.<br/>6 p.m. we go to sea again. Destination unknown.</p>
<p>Saturday 15/8/14<br/>The emden separates itself from the squadron and is going south accompanied by the collier "Markomania". The other ships are probably going to India. As I understand "Emden" has a very important problem to solve. What it is will be seen later.</p>
