  
 Wed. Jan. 19th -16 In the red Sea, coast very pretty passed 18 boats during the day.  Searchlights played on us last night by 4 battleships. 
 Thur. Jan. 10th -16 Last night it blew a gale result a heavy sea.  Thorough inspection of whole ship also kit.  Passed Dutch East Africa trading boat. 
 Fri. Jan. 21st -16 Blowing gale all day but quietened at night.  Very cold, passed two islands called "The Brothers," on one stands a big light house.
