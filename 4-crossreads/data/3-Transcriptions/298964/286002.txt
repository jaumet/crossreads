<p>12 Th April<br/>Captured one of the Turkish Lancers  usual Artillery bombardment  Fokkers very busy.  One of our Airmen killed in duel  Battle coming off in a few days anxiously waiting</p>
<p>13 Fri April<br/>Very quiet  Heavy artillery bombardment and Fokkers very busy  Received Gas helmets  we are going to use Gas shells</p>
<p>14 Sat April<br/>Turks bombarded our Hospital with 6 in gun for 2 hours  They gave notice to [indecipherable] result was they mean to shift it<br/>Heavy Artillery duel all day  Fokkers very busy dropping bombs  fine weather having plenty of sleep I will need it too</p>
