  
 5/5/17 Go for railway sleepers for firewood.  Reinforcements growling about hard times. 6/5/17 Another night air raid on hospital again, setting fire to the dispensary. 7/5/17 Another air raid & a second lot at 2 am. 8th Dropping them at the monitor Ladybird. 9th Finished cooking & made a 2nd Cpt [indecipherable] C troop. 10th Begin NCO'S work with the water parade. 11th At dentists all day.  16th 17th  19th Hell of a day. Sand dust and heat.  Half buried in dugout. 20th awake half buried.  Have to dig out. 21st  Heavy sea & calm day.  [indecipherable] of a [indecipherable] move. 22nd Orderly N.C.O.  Prepare to move. Dirty Dick (Hun) appears again low down. 23rd Leave 3am for El Farra.  Taylor gets thrown & I have to go back for ambulance.  5, 3rd  FA cannot receive him unless chits are obtained so go to 2nd L.H. Bde.  Simper & 5 proceed to El Abu Sitta & thence to Shellal & on to Farra.  Get thrown from mule and have job to locate camp.  Arrive camp 6 pm 24th Take charge water parade.  Squadron arrives. 25th On water parade - 2 miles to Wadi & 2 miles back.  Excellent shooting by anti guns.  In charge of picquet & have a busy time. 28th  Leave Farra for new camp, Abasan-el-Kabeer.
