<p>The war position looks very favourable &amp; everything points to it finishing this year.</p>
<p>9.5.16 Tuesday<br/>We received orders to-day to entrain at 2.30 for Serapeum &amp; I can tell you we had to work like fury to do same, as we only had a couple of hours notice.<br/>However we got the Horses &amp; Wagons down to Abbassia Siding &amp; commenced to load the first train.  This left at 4 pm &amp; arrived at 11 pm that night.  We got the second Train away at 6 pm &amp; the last on which I travelled at 10.15 pm.<br/>This was the most wretched journey ever I had, as we had to perch up on the top of the Wagons &amp; the air was very keen.<br/>We finally arrived at Serapeum at 4 a.m. next morning &amp; then commenced the most Strenuous days work that I have ever experienced</p>
