<p>Mountains make a beautiful View from the Sea, as the Weather was fine &amp; Sea smooth the Passage in was done in good time. Capetown Lies at the foot of the Table Mountain which is protected on the right by smaller mountain called the Lions Head which no doubt is true to name. I noticed that if there were any clouds about they were always hanging over the Table Mountain making what is commonly called a Table cloth &amp; as the Atmosphere is warmer on the Town side the clouds as they come over the Top they seem to evaporate. We dropped Anchor in Table Bay at about 5 Pm &amp; remained for the night. After dark when the Town was lit up it was a magnificient Light from the Boat, &amp; was admired by all.
