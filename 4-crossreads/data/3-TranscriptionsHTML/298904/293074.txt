<p>We were not long in embarking &amp; soon we were detailed off to our respective portion of the ship as we went aboard a chap handed each man a meal ticket, for we had to have 2 sittings, as there was not enough room for them all at once, she had been an immigrant ship &amp; had bunks fitted out in her &amp; we had these &amp; very  good they were, I know I slept sound in them.<br/>About 4 oclock that afternoon we set sail from Alexandria &amp; we passed a few of the old Gallopoli hospital ships on the way out, there were a lot</p>
