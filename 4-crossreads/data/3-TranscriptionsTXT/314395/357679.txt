The result of the referendum disgusted most of us. 
 I don't quite know how to face this job of mine.  I'm certainly glad to be away from the front for a while, but the atmosphere here is not congenial.  I feel that after 3&frac12; years, I have done my cut and am entitled to a base job, but still, I don't like camp life in England, and will not be content till I get back to France.
