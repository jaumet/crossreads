<p>Fancy dear old Major McLean of Williamstown has returned safe &amp; sound from the Peninsular &ndash; we heard such dreadful rumours about the dear old chap.</p>
<p>It has been a perfect day.  I came back to hospital in time for early dinner, so scribbling as fast as I can.</p>
<p>The patients all had a very happy time this morning extra cigarettes, tobacco, pipes and sweets, also cushions &amp; a few other comforts.  I had quite a merry morning with them all, my lot seemed better to-day and we were not nearly so busy as Xmas Day.  Some of mine are leaving for Australia in the morning.  I shall miss them very much, they are such <u>hard cases</u>.</p>
