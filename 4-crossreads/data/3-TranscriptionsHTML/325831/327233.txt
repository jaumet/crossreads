<p>Officers' Ward<br/>1st Anzac Rest Camp<br/>France<br/>31st January 1917</p>
<p>My dear Mr Justice Ferguson,</p>
<p>Many thanks for the two letters I received from you last week. I met Keith while I was on hospital leave in England this time. That was just before he joined the 20th battn. I am glad to hear that he is now on Staff work. Young Jack Mant met him the other day in one of the towns. Mant recently joined my battalion &amp; has a good chance of getting a Commission shortly. I spoke to the C.O. about him.</p>
