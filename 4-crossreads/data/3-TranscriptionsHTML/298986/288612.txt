<p>train &amp; marched to the Wharf.  Had to put in about 2 hours there severall showers of rain.  Got on board about 4.30.  Have just about enough room to sit down now, 7 oclock.  One thing on the wharf there was plenty to intrest one.  Chows loading bullie &amp; Biscuits.  Yank English &amp; our own troops Lorries trains Dirty French wemen &amp; dirtier kids selling us chocolates oranges etc. the latter</p>
