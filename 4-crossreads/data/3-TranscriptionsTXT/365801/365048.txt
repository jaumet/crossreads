 There are all sorts of rumours travelling round at present, mostly good.  We know for certain about the Russian successes, &amp; about the fleet's victory, we also have most cheerful rumours about Verdun, which I hope are true.  It was jolly hard luck losing Kitchener, but I suppose we have plenty of men to take his place.
