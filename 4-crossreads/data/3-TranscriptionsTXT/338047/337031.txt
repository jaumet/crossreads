 May 20 May 20 I went to Shepherds Bush to where Harold Brackpool was boarding with his newly married wife.  I found the place easily.  He was in the throes of composition, as he had decided to be a journalist.  Went down the street with him to order a suit of "civviles" for himself, had a game of billiards in a YM hut, &amp; at night met his wife Nance.  Went to the pictures. 
 May 21 Went to South Kensington Science Museum in the morning.  Saw the "Rocket" locomotive &amp; many old cars, &amp; models of machinery.  After dinner went to the South Kensington Hotel to a farewell concert by Miss Fairfax.   Four hundred were invited but nearly six hundred turned up.  We had a good feed.
