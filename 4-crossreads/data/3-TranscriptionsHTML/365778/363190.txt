<p>Wednesday 18/4/17<br/>Wrote to Doff. Still raining &amp; cold, went to Bde.<br/>Thursday 19/4/17<br/>Saw wire cutting experiment. Took over Support position at Noreuil, nasty trip through mud.<br/>Friday 20/4/17<br/>Men salvaging by day and working during night.<br/>Saturday 21/4/17<br/>Birthday heralded by enemy Gas shells barrage, otherwise quiet.<br/>Sunday 22/4/17<br/>Quiet Day, one shell fell on doorstep during night, otherwise things normal. Saw Heritage.</p>
