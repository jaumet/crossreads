<p>I believe our John Storey attached to 5th regiment is engaged to a Miss Chisholm doing Red Cross work.  Fancy old John being stung by the love dart.  A good job he hasn't had an arrow a few inches lower.</p>
<p>McLaren has just come from Sinai to England to get married.  I haven't seen him since I left for the peninsula.</p>
