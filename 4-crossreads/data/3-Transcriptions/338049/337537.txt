<p>Saturday 23 Nov.<br/>Lieut Howie on stables.</p>
<p>Phillips returned from hospital &amp; told us of Frank Maroney being in Abbeville. With Mac, O'Brien visited Abbeville. Dug Frank up &amp; had pleasant afternoon &amp; evening. Home very late &amp; hungry. Attached bull on return.</p>
