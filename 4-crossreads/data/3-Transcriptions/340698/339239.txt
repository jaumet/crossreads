<p>American hospital about 6 p.m. and was put to bed my arm feels pretty crook. Had the arm bandaged and dressed this morning again.</p>
<p>Expect to get across to England in the course of a few days.</p>
<p>I hear our lads captured their objective O.K. but that the casualties were very heavy.</p>
<p>7/10/17<br/>Had the X rays on my arm today.</p>
