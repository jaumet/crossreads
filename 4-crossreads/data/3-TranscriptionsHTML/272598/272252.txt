<p>Sunday 6.1.18<br/>Walked to Newlands &amp; Mousehole, raining all day, returned very early.</p>
<p>Monday 7.1.18<br/>Went to St Ives, snowed &amp; rained, very miserable.  Returning had to wait 1&frac12;  hours at St Erth junction so went on to Redruth instead of waiting &amp; caught train back from there to Penzance.</p>
