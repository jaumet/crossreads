<p>17<br/>Capt. Will. Med Off. Leaves for 22nd Howitzer Battry. 2nd Div.<br/>Brought out in orders that 1st Res. Regt of LH would not be granted by Australia, on the score of expense.<br/>18<br/>Saturday<br/>Instructed to send 6 officers &amp; 54 of 1st LH &amp;amp 3 off &amp; [indecipherable] of 2nd &amp; 77 of 3rd LH South to their Service Regts.<br/>To Maadi: McLean to K &amp; St V. Telegram late that Genl Cox would be down. Major Goncher returns next morning from 3rd Bde.<br/>19th<br/>Left Camp 7.30 Am: to railway stn to see the 170 old [Sohag, Geeigh, &amp; Tuck el Kheil:?] met Gen Cox He came to camp for few minutes. To town with him &amp; Maadi all afternoon calling: dinner at night: saw Col Tunbridge together.<br/>20<br/>Genl Cox goes to Ishmalia to see Hdqrts &amp;amp Genl. CBB White.<br/>For him in evening I entertain Col Royston. Genl returns 9.30 PM.<br/>21<br/>Tuesday<br/>Interviewed by Col Tunbridge: <strike>with Genl Cox</strike> lunch with the General: dinner NSW Lancers at Continental.<br/>22<br/>General returns to Sohag.<br/>23<br/>To Barrage with Walsh. Lt Morris &amp; Trickett return.</p>
