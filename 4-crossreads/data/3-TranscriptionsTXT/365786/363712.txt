It may blow for three days at a time.Cairo is the largest city in Africa contains the residence of the Khedive, it is a mixture of old & new.  The Romans under Agustus fortified the citadel & it became the headquarters of one of the 3 legions stationed in Egypt.  (Saladin built parts of it.)  Napoleon Bonaparte occupied it for several months & wherever one turns something historical can be seen.
