<p>battle of all The charge on "Pozeries" which belong to the 1st Division entirely, &amp; our Battalion was one of the Batts to lead the charge &amp; my humble self was one of the first over the parapet &amp; into "No mans land"<br/>After us came the other Divisions &amp; they all done very fine work but the honor of the charge belongs to the good old 1st Division, &amp; they have never failed yet in anything they had to do, nor lost an inch of ground once gained, what made the capture of Pozeries" all the more creditable to the Australians was the fact that the English had 3 times before assaulted this stronghold &amp; the longest they held it was 2 hours &amp; they were then driven out, when we went over there were any</p>
