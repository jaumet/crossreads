<p>30 November, Sunday</p>
<p>Out on the rocks in garden all morning. Go on late patrol in afternoon. Go for walk to Ellis place. Go up to see H Hickson after Tea. Stay late &amp; miss last tram but get Freshy bus.</p>
<p>1 December, Monday</p>
<p>Go to Bessies &amp; Jack's place at Haby, ville. He has nice garden which we all inspect. Spend pleasant evening.</p>
