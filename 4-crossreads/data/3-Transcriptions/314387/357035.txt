<p>Tuesday 31st<br/>In train all night passed through Amiens Abbeville Bordogno, Calais arrived and detrained Saint-Omer then marched to Ragueniem arrived afternoon<br/>Wednesday August 1st<br/>Had a teeth inspection morning paid forty francs afternoon rained all day<br/>Thursday August 2nd<br/>Route march in Morning afternoon went to Aire<br/>Friday August 3rd<br/>Lecture in morning rained nearly all day.</p>
