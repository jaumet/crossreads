<p>Our H.Q moved up at Midday at an hours notice but I am left behind with my limber until I get further orders All the 4 GS. waggons have gone up &amp; I am here on my own</p>
<p>Aug 13/14<br/>I moved up the following day the 9th to a little place  called Blangy-Trouville a few kilos behind Villers Brettoneux which was about our line on the 8th  The line is many miles  ahead</p>
