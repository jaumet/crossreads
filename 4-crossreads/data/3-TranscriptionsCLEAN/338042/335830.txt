  
 Mar.16th Breakfast 11.30 a.m. - In the afternoon Mrs Scales & myself witness the very popular play "General Post" (Lillian Braithwaite Norman Mc.Kinnell, Geo Tully, Gertrude Lang) at Theatre Royal, Haymarket - Evening spent at Elm [indecipherable]  - Doctor & Mrs. Massey (Belgian Refugees) - Songs Music & Recitations.
