<p>March 1918<br/>Monday 4</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Had a big stunt at 2 am. this morning. Weather wet. Spent the usual day. Am for O.P. tomorrow. Received Customs memo.<br/>At 12.5 midnight, we were barraging for a raid. Still raining. <br/>Roussell out at O.P.  Walker went for pigeons. Loaned A.G. 151.<br/>Formed a picture Gallery in dugout.<br/>Rumoured that Eggs-a-cook stunt of 2nd was a failure that's why they  <br/>"hopped over" at midnight.</p>
