<p>Return to Rest Camp. I make a sketch of the position for (I think) General Birdwood. 6 p.m. return to trenches .<br/>Turks bombing heavily  - we have had a spot of rain which has made it extremely difficult to gain a foothold.<br/>I asked Captain Margolin if he could spare a little of his jam &ndash; "It isnt my jam," he exclaimed, "It is our jam; help yourselves."&percnt;&percnt;&percnt;&percnt;&percnt;&percnt;&percnt;<br/>The stench from the corpses is appalling &ndash; I offer Captain Margolin a cigarette; though he does not smoke I think he ought to try &ndash; "All right Silas," he said,"  Ill see how I get on."
