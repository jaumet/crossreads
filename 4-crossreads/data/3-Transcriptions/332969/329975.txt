<p>11.4.15   Today is a beautiful day nothing at all doing most of us laying around on the decks.</p>
<p>12.4.15  Nothing doing</p>
<p>13.4.15  It is raining very heavy.</p>
<p>14.4.15  About 10 am we were all put into rowing boats &amp; tugged ashore to have a dumy landing it was great excitement when we landed we marched up on one of the hills close by which was covered with beautiful grass &amp; wild Poppies which were all out.</p>
<p>15  Nothing doing</p>
<p>16.4.15  Its a beautiful morning most of the troops went ashore but our Coy. did not go.  During the afternoon a lot of the boys went in for a swim.</p>
<p>17th &amp; 18th  There was nothing doing yesterday &amp; today to write about</p>
<p>19.4.15  I got up this morning not feeling to well had a very sore throat so went to the Dr. &amp; he gave me some tablets &amp; the day off duty.</p>
<p>20.4.15  Today There was nothing doing</p>
<p>21.4.15  When we go up it was very rough looking &amp; started to rain it has continued all day its terrible miserable everything being so wet.</p>
<p>Thursday 22  Nothing.</p>
<p>23.4.15 Its a beautiful day during the after noon</p>
