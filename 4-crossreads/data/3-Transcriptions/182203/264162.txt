<p>[Letterhead Perth Public Hospital.]<br/>November 9th 1914.<br/>Miss E. S. Thistlethwaite was a trainee in the Melbourne Hospital, and worked under me as a nurse on several occasions, when I was a Sister in the Institution  Her work as a nurse was exceedingly good. She was observant, and careful of detail, and showed tact in the management of her patients.<br/>Her general deportment was always good.<br/>C.Gray,                                                                                                                                                                          Matron,                                                                                                                                                                                Perth Public Hospital.</p>
