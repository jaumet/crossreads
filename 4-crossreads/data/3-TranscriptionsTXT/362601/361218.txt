 12 June, Thursday 
 Wet &amp; Hot 
 Fine after breakfast. Very strong wind a bit choppy. I read after dinner outside starboard Port holes but have to come inside it is so cool. Use last of scented soap. Read &amp; write after 8 pm. Go up aloft to sleep &amp; have nice cool wind. Have very good meals. Pork, Vermi[celi] &amp; Har[icot] Beans, Sago &amp; Peaches. See flying fish again. 
 13 June, Thursday 
 F Cool Wind 
 Work hard in morn &amp; go to Book-keeping in afternoon. Partnership makes me very puzzled. I am very forgetful &amp; do strange things. Jack &amp; I sit up reading until late. We cross the line but it is a lovely cool day with a strong wind. I dream that this is in Aussie going about everywhere. We cross the line. I sleep on seat in Hosp.
