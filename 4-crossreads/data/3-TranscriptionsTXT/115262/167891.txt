 Friday 4 May 1917. 
 Every one body&nbsp;tired of general breakups. &nbsp; 
  Reveille 7 p.m.&nbsp; for our unit only. &nbsp; 1 st&nbsp; parade at 9 p.m. &nbsp; 
 Made out &quot;Will&quot; forms, &amp; did nothing in &nbsp;general. &nbsp; 
 Official word that we are to sail next week, &amp; everyone in A.C. B .F. Amb. granted leave for week-end. &nbsp; 
 Bed 10 pm. after getting some washing done.
