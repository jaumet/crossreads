 Monday 7 May 1917. 
 Up at 6&nbsp;a.m. Caught 7 pm [a.m.] troop train to Seymour. At camp at 11.30 a.m. 
 Very little doing but we were drilled in cold wind &amp; misty rain. 
 Expect to go on Thursday 
 Advance guard for&nbsp;Lt. Horse on A42 leave camp at 6.15 a.m. 
 A.M.C. (re-allotted men) about 30 arrive from No 5 &amp; No 11. A.G.H. at 9.30 p.m.
