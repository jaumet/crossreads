<p>After being at Abassieh a few days I joined up with the troop in camp at Maadi.<br/>Maadi camp is situated on the fringe of the desert. The Suburb is a European quarter.<br/>I had the pleasure of meeting Roy here &amp; while in camp together had many good walks &amp; meals together. The work consisted chiefly grooming &amp; looking after the horses<br/>Had many very interesting rides over the desert<br/>The dead or placed in Tombs A native funeral is comical Professional mourners head the procession with songs.<br/>Saw ruins of ancient places  Old Cairo is an eye opener</p>
