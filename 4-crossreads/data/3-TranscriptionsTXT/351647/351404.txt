 I think I'll try &amp; arrange to go over there &amp; live &amp; work.  This open air stunt in this climate is a bit much for me. 
 I am living in a tent with Alleyne Edols (one of the Burrawang clan), he belongs to my old battery &amp; they are next door to us.  H.Q. sleeping quarters are rather crowded &amp; a bit off so when Alleyne who is rather an old pal of mine offered me the shelter of his domicile I rushed it.
