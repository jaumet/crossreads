  
 We parted with our natives at the hotel for a very small amount about half a rupee each (ninepence in English money). We had a cool drink each a splendid cup of tea with cakes and some  bananas & paw-paws. This latter fruit I had never tasted before & I must say that with the lime & a little sugar they were very nice indeed.
