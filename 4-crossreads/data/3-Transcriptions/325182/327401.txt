<p>4th May<br/>Since the last entry herein I have had plenty of time for very serious and deep reflection.  At times I have reflected very angrily on things which are <u>not</u>, and which in my exalted opinion <u>should be</u>, in a hospital at least.  But the all absorbing item of interest is the war which has been raging during the last month and which has now temporarily subsided slightly.  Perhaps we will start the ball rolling next time</p>
