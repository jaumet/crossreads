 April, 1915 Tuesday 27  My man  Our got thrown off the Buck jumper making the 28th success for the Horse. Came on to Heliopolis today &amp; found  mothers  letter  of 21/3/15 &amp; one from Tommy Redford who comes near us now to Zeitoun. Glad you have at last got a letter &ndash; Re Bucket &ndash; all I got was an Air Pillow &ndash; Sorry no rain &ndash; I note Ralph Arnolds name, &amp; have written Charlie. Did you hear ought of J.A.C. in Melbourne, [indecipherable] was [indecipherable] by 5" of rain at Xmas &amp; there is grass to burn.
