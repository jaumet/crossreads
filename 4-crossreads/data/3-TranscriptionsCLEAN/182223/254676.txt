  
 Thursday 1 July 1915 Joseph's Birth anniversary [Josephine Nash] Sent her a cable last night  Very busy all day  Admissions about 80 patients. 
 Friday 2 July 1915 Busy all day  Temperature comfortable   3   450 odd patients 
 Saturday 3 July 1915 Horse at 11 a.m.  Sent off to Australia 131 patients All went well  Captain Plant went to Alexandria 
 Sunday 4 July 1915  Not at Mass today  Colonel Manifold here on inspection All was correct.
