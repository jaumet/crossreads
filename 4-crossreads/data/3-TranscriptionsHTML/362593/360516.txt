<p>Recco Tul Keram.<br/>Pilot Capt. Addison, a B.F.<br/>In air 2 h. 15 m.  A good trip.<br/>Engine cut out near Jaffa &amp; nearly let us down in the sea.  Did a good recco.  Tracer shell fire from Ramin station.  Feel awfully well, glad to be at work again.</p>
<p>Monday 18 February 1918</p>
