<p>Shopping in town.</p>
<p>Return to Elm-Bank.</p>
<p>Evening spent in making up parcels, writing letters etc.</p>
<p><u>April 25th</u></p>
<p>&quot;Anzac Day&quot; - Anniversary of the Glorious Gallipoli Landing - Australians march in the City.&nbsp; Decorations, speeches etc.&nbsp; Shopping.&nbsp; Final business.</p>
<p>Farewell to Mr &amp; Mrs Harold Scales &amp; the children.</p>
