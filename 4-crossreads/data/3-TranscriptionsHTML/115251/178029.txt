<p>At 8-10 p.m. on the 9th October the following message was received from Thursday Island.</p>
<p>At 8-10 p.m. on 9th October following message was received from Navy, Melbourne:-</p>
<p>&quot;Thursday Island heard signals in German plain language, 4-45 am, 7th Octr., reporting that &quot;Australia&quot; and all large Men-o&#39;-war had left Rabaul going Eastward.&nbsp; Did you hear this signal and can you estimate where it was made from?.</p>
<p>Reply despatched at 9-30 p.m.</p>
<p>&quot;Message not intercepted here;&nbsp; Station believed to be working Tawanakus Bay.&nbsp; Expedition has been despatched.&quot;</p>
