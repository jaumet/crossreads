<p>Thurs. 9 Nov.  1916<br/>Sighted land at 10-15 am.  Arrived Durban about 12 noon.  Allowed on shore 3. 45 pm and marched into Durban (1. 75 miles) and granted leave till 11pm</p>
<p>Frid. 10 Nov. 1916<br/>Not finished coaling.  Granted leave from 10 am till 2 pm.  Sailed at 5-15 pm.</p>
<p>Sat 11 Nov. 1916<br/>Very rough and keeping in sight of land.</p>
