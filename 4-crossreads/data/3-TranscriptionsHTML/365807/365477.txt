<p>183 Belvedere St.<br/>San Francisco<br/>6. July 1916</p>
<p>My dear Dave &amp; Alice,</p>
<p>I cannot comfort you, I can only send you our love &amp; deepest sympathy. We still feel very sad about Arthur, &amp; wish we could help you to bear this trial. I did hope you would be spared this blow &ndash; Strange that his call should come in France!</p>
<p>Enclosed is a letter which John received yesterday. We feel sure you will love to</p>
