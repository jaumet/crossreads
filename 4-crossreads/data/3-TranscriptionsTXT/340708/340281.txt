
September 1917Mon 3  This is a very decent place on the top of a strongly fortified hill it was to have been the last line of defence before Calias had the Germans broken through at YpresTuesday 4thThere is plenty of work to do here we have to be ready for 1000 patients in 6 weeks.  The big town of St Homer [Saint- Omer] is about 5 miles from here  Fritz raided there last nightWednesday 5thStill toilingThursday 6thTook a stroll to one of the numerous villages this eveningFriday 7thAll P.B's went back to Hazebrouck today to go before another Medical BoardSaturday 8thI landed the long looked for C. today so will be going to England in a fortnight & then on to N.Z.    went back to the farm this evening
