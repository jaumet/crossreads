<p>Address all letters to the Battalion as before (until youhear from me again)</p>
<p>Bingham Hotel<br/>5, Southampton Buildings,<br/>Chancery Lane, Holborn,<br/>London. W.C.<br/>30-6-18</p>
<p>Dear Dad,</p>
<p>I am over in England again as you see, and just now am spending a few days on leave before going into the Officers Cadet College to obtain a Commission. I am afraid it is a good while since I wrote my last letter home, things were in a deuce of a mix up in France for a while and I did not get my usual letters away, but I trust my field cards reached you O.K. I have been expecting to get across here for some time and have been putting off my letters in consequence of being able to write in detail as soon as I got across here, I am</p>
