1st Nov.
   I leave per Motor Lorry for Beyroute at 10 am.  Our route lies through the Lebanon Hills & it is a picturesque journey all the way.  The Turks have blown up a Viaduct & we see the Canadian Bridging Corp at work repairing it. Our trip is spoilt by a loquacious & semi intoxicated Australian Askari who climbs over the tailboard at the last moment.
