 1917 APRIL 
 2 MONDAY 
 A fair cow of a day. The ground 
 was covered with snow in 
 the morning &amp; it was blowing 
 a cold gale all day &amp; snowing 
 as well. Cleaning &amp; fixing up 
 a loose box for my horse in 
 morning &amp; lay &amp; read all afternoon 
 Had a letter from Alan tonight 
 &amp; he has his full lieutenancy 
 now &amp; is a flight commander 
 as well.
