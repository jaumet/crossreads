I was jolly glad when I got out of it, and back to the collier.<br/>At 6am we finished our shift and then there was a great rush for the baths and we had to use a scrubbing brush to get the black off.<br/>After getting nice and clean, one of the Boys, who had not washed, would either hug, or brush up against you, and you had to start again</p>
