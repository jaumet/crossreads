The grasses growing round Rafa are chiefly rye grass & Crow-foot, two very excellent both of which are well known in Australia & excellent feeds for all classes of stock. After leaving Rafa we pushed on a further seven or eight miles to Khan Yunis, & camped near the village for the night, & we shall probably be a week or so few days here.
