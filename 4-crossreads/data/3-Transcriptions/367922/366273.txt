<p>12.<br/>well &amp; taking all reasonable care of myself so you Dear Ones' mustn't worry about me.</p>
<p>Yes! I knew that the hearts of you all <strike>it</strike> in that dear home, that was as my own, were with me in my grief &amp; are still. Dear Dene would have mourned her too for Mum-dear was very fond of him &amp; I can see her now as she put her arms round me &amp; we wept for the dear lads going &amp; for your families grief.</p>
<p>Every line of your letter proves the sympathy our losses have brought us &amp; if my letter fails to express it, it is my want of words adequate to do so. My loving thoughts are with you all. Will you tell all Dear Ones</p>
