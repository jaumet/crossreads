
(full page sketch of a plant with note at side as follows):Sketch of Cultivated plant found in the Sinai peninsular. Cultivated by the Bedouin Arabs. Has lily-like leaves and has a tuber like an onion sometimes as large as 8 inches in diameter. Cannot find out as yet what its uses are. It is not edible and cattle will not touch it, perhaps it has medicinal properties. 2.1.1917
