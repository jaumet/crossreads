<p>3 &ndash; I went to Cousin Gordon to dinner at Linden Gardens. I like them all very much. They asked the girls &amp; me next Saturday night &ndash; but the girls say they cant go. Cousin Gordon talked about J's career and is keen on helping her to find out what is the best way to get the lectures she wants. Wants them to go there and feel at home and get to know them he told me and Maud too. Marguerite is such an interesting girl &ndash; is translating german</p>
