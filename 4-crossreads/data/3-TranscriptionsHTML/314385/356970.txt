<p>A party of the 7th FA have also arrived here &amp; like ourselves are waiting developments.</p>
<p>About 30 yards away there is a 12 in pea shooter going off about every 15 minutes &amp; when he fires we experience an earthquake &amp; being in the upper story we expect to come flop to terra firma with any of these reports from this infant. I thought I would go &amp; see it fire &amp; the 12 in shell could be plainly traced through the air for half the distance.</p>
<p>Friday 25th Aug<br/>Our artillary friend still distracts us, however we got some fatigue to do that releived us of waiting for the next crashing report of the gun in the afternoon.</p>
<p>All hands are making splints &amp; getting bandages ready as we expect an overflow rush through the night as there is a battle in progress.</p>
<p>We finished about 8.30 pm &amp; then retired with the rats but at 10 that night we were all called out &amp; told to get dressed and ready to move (only us not the 7th</p>
