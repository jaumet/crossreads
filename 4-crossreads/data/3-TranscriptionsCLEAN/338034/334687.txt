  
 Later in the day, the  1st  3rd L.H. Brigade, a Yeomanry Brigade, a Brigade of New Zealanders, & the Australian Camel Corps arrived here. The beach has been found to be infested with mines &  today our Squadron Serg. Major (Geo. Shepheard) & Corp Struthers were blown to pieces by one which had been blown up on the beach. Dec 22nd A Cruiser , a couple of Monitors & about 5 mine sweepers have been traveling all day & fished up a number of mines.
