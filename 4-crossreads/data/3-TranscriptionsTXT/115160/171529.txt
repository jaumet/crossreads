 Gee, its nearly a week since I got the foregoing written - and none since. &nbsp;Not much has happened during the week to mention except that we didn&#39;t feel too comfy one morning at work. &nbsp;Happily the shells passed over our heads but there were some close enough.

 &quot;New Invoicing Regulation. 
 Importers of goods dutiable ad valorem from all countries other than China and Japan will, after 1st January next, be obliged to submit invoices showing the value for home consumption in the country of export of the goods at the date of the invoices and to make declaration as to the correctness of the values shown.&quot; 
 This will be a bit of a nuisance for I remember the same practice as existed when I was in the B.G.E.

 &quot;His Understudy. 
 A benevolent old gentlemen got into a motor-&#39;bus, and sat down opposite a nice, little, round-eyed boy who stared at him uncompromisingly. &nbsp;The old gentleman grew a little uncomforable under the child&#39;s fixed gaze, and, seeking relief in action, he winked at the youngster. 
 The small&nbsp;boy tried to return his salute, but both eyes persisted in shutting. &nbsp;The old gentleman found he had made things even more embarrassing, for the child piped to the young woman at his side: &#39;Mamma&#39;, he said &#39;wink at that man.&quot;
