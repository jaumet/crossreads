  
 2. Sunday Glorious day.  Met F.P. at Marble Arch at 12 & went to Hampton Court returning by 9 p.m. very tired  Had a very pleasant day.  Discovered a lot of philosophy & self control in F.P. 
 3. Monday Lovely day  Went to Cafe &  then to Caxton Hall to serve tea to B.D.W.S.U. "At Home"  crawled home & had attack of malaria 
 4. Tuesday 101  Temp. at night cooked at Minerva &  home &  to bed exhausted. F. P. called up about am. mail. 
 5. Wednesday Feeling v. ill & tired.  F.P. called but left earlier owing to my indisposition. I dressed up in pink rose dress  In bed in forenoon, served tea at Caxton Hall afternoon.
