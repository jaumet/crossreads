
8 November, Saturday
Go to M. No Din, sit on perch on promenade. Sports. I save O King. Go to E. Furniture Home. Down Corso. Lights, fireworks, confetti. Meet Dorrie, Roch, Ray, Alty & Mamie & Nancy. Sit on beach & along Corso. Home at 11 pm. Meet Errol Lovell & Scottish band & Nurse Lascelles. Recd letters from R Pine & M at Carnival.
9 November, Sunday
Gardening all morning. Dad & I go to Dee Why. Lovely in water but sharky. Snake in grass at Dee Why. Ellan Van[?]. Music after Tea, a lovely southerly at 8 pm.
