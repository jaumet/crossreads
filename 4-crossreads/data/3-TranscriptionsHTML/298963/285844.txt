<p>1st May<br/>Nothing happened worth recording.</p>
<p>2nd<br/>Also uneventful, "C" section proceeded to Beecourt Chateau.</p>
<p>3rd<br/>Played cricket against 1st Amb. Supply Train, won by 100 runs at Aliert. Our opponents treated us royally, supper supplied after the match &amp; a lorry brought us home to Pozieres.</p>
<p>4th<br/>Got orders at 4 a.m. to prepare to go into the line. <strike>to</strike> Left at 7 a.m. for main dressing station, loafed about all day, met a lot of old boys at night went <strike>to</strike> into line, encountered two or three duds of tear gass, &amp; sundry other unpleasant experiences but reached the Regimental aid post without casualties,</p>
