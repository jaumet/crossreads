The sports terminated abruptly when it was discovered that 150 men in the charge of a bogus non-com had marched past the guard at the gate and so secured liberty to visit the town.  The remainder of us were marched to the Feather Market where tea was provided.  The following day we were again taken to the Sports Ground.  Before entering it we were formed up and the 'Officers call' was sounded.  Now this had been arranged among us to be the signal for a dash for liberty so accordingly the 2,000 of us bolted.
