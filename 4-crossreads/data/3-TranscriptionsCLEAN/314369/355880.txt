
27 August 1915Friday
Find out this morning I am for Alexandria by the "Assaye"A whole lot of slightly wounded are taken ashore during afternoon in lightersAbout 7 pm all the men for the Assaye were transferred   &put into wards.Put in a very comfortable nightI hear today that through some mistake the 18th Bat has been terribly cut up only two days after they had landed!
1915 August 28Saturday
First thing I knew this morning was my mate waking me to come for breakfast.We are given clean shirts   &  Pajamas   &  so have a chance to get clean againMove out of Mudros at 8 pm.Retire to bed at 8.30 pm. but am unable to sleep so go on deck   &  make a bed there w/- better luck   &  do not wake until about 6 next morning
