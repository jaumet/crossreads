<p>6 July 1915<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Inlying piquet.<br/>Our new commanding officer takes over this morning he is a 16th Bat officer by name Col. Tilney V.D.<br/>Stand to arms 3 am to 4.30  &amp;  then sleep till about 8 am (Wednesday)</p>
<p>1915 July 7<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Write a letter home<br/>Very quiet all morning but get a few shells over during the afternoon.<br/>Very hot.<br/>Hear that 16th lost four killed   &amp; about 30 wounded in crossing on Monday.</p>
