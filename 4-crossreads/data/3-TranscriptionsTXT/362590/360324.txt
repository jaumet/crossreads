
R.A.S.G.
6.2.16My dear Father
I am glad you are enjoying your trip so well; & when you come back I expect to hear great tales of men falling overboard, millions of cheering troops, exciting adventures in gold mines etc - especially from Dorothy. As I have nothing to the contrary, I conclude that you are all well, & have no idea of returning yet a while.
