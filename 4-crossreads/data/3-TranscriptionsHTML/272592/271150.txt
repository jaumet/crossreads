<p>singlets underpants &amp; socks &amp; hand them in at a window with their towels receiving a ticket for each article, at another window these tickets are exchanged for clean things which they place on a form ready, &amp; then have a good hot bath &amp; put on the fresh underclothes, by this time their tunics &amp; breeches which have been dusted with Oxford powder &amp; ironed, a process which the occupants of the seams are said not to appreciate. The soiled underclothes &amp; towels which they have handed in, are soaked for 24 hours in large vats of cresol solution, &amp; then washed &amp; boiled. The socks are darned the singlets &amp; etc. mended &amp; buttons sewn on</p>
