Sun.Church parade usual reading chatting. Could see land. Jim out now. Got to Capetown before we were out of bed. From the boat you could see the Table Mountain well a good view. Flat on top sloping down to Capetown at very steep angle. In the morning it was covered with clouds but as day wore on cleared off. The place doesn't seem very high but still the clouds are there. After breakfast marched to sports ground quarinteened there, but a lot scaled out. We were served with lunch by a few ladies. In the afternoon marched to a big place where they entertain oversea troops. We had a nice tea, tea, meat-pies different assortment of cakes. Post cards handed out to write home on. Sent home some views. Town full of niggers same as Durban. Boarded again at sundown.
