 11th I have been appointed Sports Representative for this Battalion.  The weather at present is decidely unfavourable for sports of any  sp  sort.  It may fine up a bit, later.  I attended a meeting of Battalion Sports Representatives at 53rd Bn. H.qrs. at 5 oclock this afternoon.  Major Lucas of the 53rd presided.  He was commanding officer of this Bn. for a time at Villers Bretonneux in April last when a crowd of us were badly gassed.
