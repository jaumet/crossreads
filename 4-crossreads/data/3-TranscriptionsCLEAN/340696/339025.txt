 23/3/18 Word came through at issue time this afternoon to move to-morrow it will hurt leaving this good billet I have had a splendid time here. 
 24/3/18. Moved to Haverskerque & billeted with Blue Carter in a lovely room it was goodo. Fell in love with one  the  daughter Marie Rose & Blue fell in love with the other. Milk was on tap & we made a few pints look silly. They were sorry to lose us. 
 25/3/18 Moved to Houdain a fairly large town & fell in with nice people for the night
