<p>Spt 14 ever mixes with the boys &ndash; or makes inquiries of any description.<br/>All they think of is eating, drinking, sleeping &amp; playing games with the Nurses &amp; Officers. The "Major" Devil Dodger we call the "Sea Lion", but a private who sports the nickname of "The Broody (ca? Hen" is about the queerest looking chap yet!</p>
