<p>May 1918<br/>Thursday 16<br/>Bussy &ndash;la-Daours<br/>Went for a swim during the morning.<br/>Weather very warm, with no breeze. <br/>Had a morning run to Fuilloy at 7 am<br/>At 7 pm Fritz commenced the first aerial bombardment  since we have been in the village. <br/>We had a frisky time under the strain, a very big pool down by the Daours church greatly patronised  during the heat of the day.</p>
