<p>suppose as long as it anwers its purpose no one is annoyed. &nbsp;I saw where the Fund had totted&nbsp;&pound;43000 odd in those cuttings you sent me.</p>
<p>We are just near a big city that is rapidly refilling with its former inhabitants, but it presents a very sorry appearance in some places.</p>
<p>There war should not drag on too long, eh, with the goodness of the news? And we should soon have visions of Home and Beauty.</p>
<p>Well, Lock, keep your end up. I hope the butter shortage is over and that things are not so stringent as they appear on paper.</p>
<p>I hope you are in the best of nick and doing plenty on the toe.</p>
<p>Fondest regards,</p>
<p>Your affectionate cousin</p>
