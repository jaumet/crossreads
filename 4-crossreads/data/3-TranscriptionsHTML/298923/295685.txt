<p>19 January 1918 Saturday<br/>Richon-le- Zion   We can see the "Minarets of <strike>Jaffa</strike> from here, the country between all vineyards &amp; orange groves-<br/>Weather fine again &amp; the POPPIES are in bloom my Pen wont write so I give it best.<br/><strike>Sorry to hear of Mrs Bloomfields death</strike><br/>I enclose you a card from local Jews Hotel.<br/>All nations have settlements here, decent houses, &amp; <strike>Jaffa</strike> is a modern town but the Turks have pulled up the Railway line-   <strike>Post this with my love.</strike></p>
