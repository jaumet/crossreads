<p>Wednesday 8 November 1916<br/>Wrote to Mother (24).<br/>Started as Supernumery Signaller.<br/>Cpl. J. Lesk instructor.  Shown lamp Helio &amp; buzzer.</p>
<p>Thursday 9 November 1916<br/>Flag drill in morning and buzzer in afternoon.  Taube over but never did anything.</p>
