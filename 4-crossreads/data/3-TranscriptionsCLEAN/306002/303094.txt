  
 1.4.15 This morning Stan took our horses out with the Sqd. for exercise;  Nelson, Smithers & I was practising the setting up of the Helio. all morning to kill time, no parade after dinner & we dozed in the tent all the afternoon.  After tea Cork & I had a game of billiards & then went to the Y.M.C.A. as a concert was on & it was very good indeed, we stayed there till it was time to turn in.
