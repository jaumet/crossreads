<p>Thursday 8th<br/>Found this morning we were in a large hospital at Amiens  Did not have to turn out untill after 10 so had a good sleep. Washed shaved &amp; had a good meal.<br/>There is a clock tower here where one can get an excelent view of the Town not a very large place &amp; does not seem to have suffered from shell fire The only signs of war being plenty of planes going up &amp; down &amp; a some of the walls have been</p>
