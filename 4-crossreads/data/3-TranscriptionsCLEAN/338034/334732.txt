We now have a great number of guns on this front, both large & small howitzers & field guns. We now have an army of something like 100,000 men here in Palestine composed of three divisions of British & Scottish infantry, four Brigades of Australian Light Horse, one Brigade of New Zealand Mounted Rifles, two Brigades of English Yeomanry, &  a great deal of Artillery.
