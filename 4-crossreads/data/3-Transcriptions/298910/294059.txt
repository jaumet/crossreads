<p>12/9/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Dull day &ndash; A couple of our men suffering from <u>nerves</u> &amp; insomnia &ndash; so had to go &amp; relieve them at Foray House &ndash; Very busy during the night &ndash; had some bad cases &ndash; The Boys gave Fritz particular &ndash; Made some complaints about some inconveniences we had to put up with. Have been promised they will be rectified &amp; the wounded will benefit accordingly.</p>
<p>13/9/16  Wednesday</p>
<p>Raining &ndash; Mud &amp; slush everywhere &ndash; Back again at Convent Avenue. Fritz putting over a lot of big stuff to no advantage &ndash; one dud dangerously near our 6 x 8 &ndash; made it rock some.</p>
<p>The Boys made a mess of "Fritz's" parapet with the pudding bombs &ndash; One call at night.</p>
<p>14/9/16  Thursday</p>
<p>Dull &amp; raining &amp; very cold, appreciated our rum issue &ndash; Artillery duels night &amp; day &ndash; Fritz went mad again &amp; sent over some of all sorts &ndash; Pineapple Bombs &ndash; Pigeons etc &amp; as usual did no harm</p>
