<p>August 1915<br/>5 Thursday<br/>Not well enough to get up but had wanted to get ashore for impending advance</p>
<p>6 Fri<br/>Sicilia<br/>During night English troops landed at Suvla Burnu and there was severe fighting all day. Saw our troops on part of 971 west side.</p>
