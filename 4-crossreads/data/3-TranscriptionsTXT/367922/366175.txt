 Public Service Commr's Office, Customs House, Melbourne, 14th May, 1907. 
 Dear Mr. Fry, In the next exam. for apptmt. to the Clerical Division of the C'wlth Service "Shorthand and Typewriting" is to be included, for the first time, as an optional subject. The Commr. has asked me to undertake the setting &amp; making of the papers, and as you have had a good deal to do with that sort of work it would be of great assistance to me if you would give me a few hints.
