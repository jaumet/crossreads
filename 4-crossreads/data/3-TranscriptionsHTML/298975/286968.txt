<p>Thursday  18th<br/>Unloading coal all day concequently the hut is not short of fuel tonight</p>
<p>Friday 19th<br/>Working party Building a Clink at No 13 camp Mostly barb wire work am Wire expert. Pared 15/- event to Warminister</p>
<p>Saturday 20th<br/>Working party Warminister in afternoon payed 8d to see a very roton play</p>
<p>Sunday 21st<br/> As usual walked around Sutton Viny in evening. Had 3 drinks of stout.</p>
<p>Monday 22nd<br/>Still wire expert on the Clink</p>
