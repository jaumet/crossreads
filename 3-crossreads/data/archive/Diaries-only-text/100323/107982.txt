6/9/19 Chemnitz
Passed Wilsons Promontory early but I did not see it completely. Saw high cliffs and some stray islands. A lovely sunny day with a sea absolutely unruffled. Ordered all men up on deck and checked kits and cleaned decks. In the distance low blue land in sight.
7/9/19 Another fine day. Passed the "Themistocles" early looking very beautiful and trim. Passed Gabo about 7 with a slight roll. All day ran along in sight of low coast and blue distant hills. Paid all on board.
