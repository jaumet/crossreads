<p>Under cover of darkness all the mounted troops &amp; artillery retired to Tel el Gemme where we arrived just after mid-night &amp; camped till morning. Our casualties (the mounted troops operating on the flank) for the day would amount to something like 450 in killed &amp; wounded, but our attack was considered quite successful in its object of helping in the main attack at Gaza.<br/>Apl 21st :- Our Brigade was on outpost duty last night.
