 Saturday 9th Oct It was a delightful morning after the rain. Of course there was plenty of mud about &amp; you could see traces of the storm which had just abated. In the afternoon I went to Ordnance &amp; on the beach there was a fearful muddle. The various jetties &amp; landing stages were carried away. Barges &amp; small vessels were high &amp; dry on the sand. Biscuit boxes &amp; others were strewn all over the place. The debacle could not have been better completed by the heaviest of bombardments.
