<p>I being the only one in Battery to receive mail.</p>
<p>Wed. Mar. 1st -16<br/>Usual routine things very slow.</p>
<p>Thur. Mar. 2nd &ndash; 16<br/>Horses stampede causing great excitement.</p>
<p>Fri. Mar. 3rd -16<br/>Drivers put through test with hurdle.</p>
<p>Sat. Mar. 4th -16<br/>Had an enjoyable evening at YMCA Cairo</p>
<p>Tues Mar 7th &ndash; 16<br/>All reinforcements warned at 6 P.M. to be ready to leave next day for Tel-el-kiber.</p>
<p>Wed. Mar. 8th -16<br/>Left Zeitoun for Tel-el-kiber and every company</p>
