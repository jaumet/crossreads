<p>Wednesday 21 June 1916<br/>Nothin doin.</p>
<p>Thursday 22 June 1916<br/>Up early:  drew kit etc.  Taken by motor to Cairo Station, caught train Alexandria.  Beautiful fields all the way along the line.<br/>Arrived Alex 1 p.m. taken about 3 miles by car to Ras-el-tin.  Were put in isolation.</p>
