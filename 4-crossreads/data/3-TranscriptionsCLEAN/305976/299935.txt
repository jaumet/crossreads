  
 A good find with rations so low  Friday  Tuesday Nov 13 Bedouin Tribe came hanging round today. Buzzed them off. Found Capt Travers an English officer dead & stripped. What dogs these Bedouins are.  Saturday  Wednesday Nov 14 Abundance of dogs here. Flies so bad during day that it is impossible to eat. Stench from dead bodies of animals unbearable. Salvage corps busy burying dead. Tom bad with septic sores. Cannot at present locate division Sunday Nov 18th Have been down three days Dysentry. We moved today to Huj. Owing
