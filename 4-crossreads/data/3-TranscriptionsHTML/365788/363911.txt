<p>Serapenne</p>
<p>22 Mch 16<br/>My own dear Sister,</p>
<p>I am writing this as I sit on the bank of the Canal on the Cairo side.<br/>The day before yesterday 10 of us were detailed as an advance party to cross over to the R'way siding at Serapenne &amp; unload &amp; stack the baggage stores of the unit preparatory to leaving for a destination which is officially withdrawn as far</p>
