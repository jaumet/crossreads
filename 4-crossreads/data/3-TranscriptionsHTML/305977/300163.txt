<p>Saturday 23rd   [Jan]<br/>The other transports came up in the morning with the exception of the Ayrshire which didn't turn up till 2 in the afternoon.  Some of the chaps jumped overboard &amp; swam ashore at Colombo &amp; the guard that went ashore also caused a nuisance.  They were infantry off the Ulysses.  Was on stable piquet.  We left Aden about 5 oclock in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Sunday 24th<br/>Mess orderly.  Had a fairly busy day.  Usual boat &amp; fire station parade.  Passed several steamers including 2 P &amp; O mail boats, one was the Moldavia.  Passed an Orient boat during the night.  The Empress of Russia left us</p>
