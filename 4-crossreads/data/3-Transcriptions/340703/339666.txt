<p>November 1916<br/>Tuesday 28<br/>Arrived near the oasis of Dahkla at dark. Halted for night. Will go in to the village of Tenida in the morning.</p>
<p>Wednesday 29<br/>Arrived in Tenida 7am Went down the village bought eggs. Residents very poor. Senussis robbed of everything.</p>
