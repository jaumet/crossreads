<p>T Winter, Rabaul</p>
<p>Monday 21st September 14<br/>We were given the most honourable position as guard of honor to the German prisoners (damn them too!)<br/>At nine oclock they marched in. First came the 30 German prisoners &amp; these after a lot of useless ceremony piled their arms. They all wore kharki uniforms with cartridge belts , &amp; slouch hats.
