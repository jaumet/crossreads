<p>THURSDAY, MARCH 20, 1919.</p>
<p>20</p>
<p>Very misty till midday then</p>
<p>it cleared up. The port authorities</p>
<p>would not let us land, &amp; and</p>
<p>quarantined us; so we lay</p>
<p>in the Bay all day.</p>
<p>Reading and playing games</p>
<p>all day.</p>
