
8 June 1918SaturdayArrived in London 8.5 a.m. Did a little business & then went to Leytonstone to see my friends & left Waterloo 6.P.M. arriving back in camp about 9.30 P.M. feeling fairly tired after a good enjoyable holiday.
1918 June 9Sunday2 after Trinity
Not doing very much today takings things easy to get over the tired feeling after coming back from Leave. In the afternoon Sgt Gunner & I went for a stroll through Dinton  & Barford. Raining fairly heavy in the forenoon.
