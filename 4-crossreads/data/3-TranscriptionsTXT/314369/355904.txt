 14 October 1915 Thursday 
 Things as usual am going along steadily but getting as lazy as a Turk. Very good Pictures at night. Nights very cool. making use of blankets again. 
 1915 October 15 Friday 
 First interview with dentist. He filled one tooth very well   &amp;  I have to go again in a couple of days. A big draft of Reinforcements   &amp;  men returning is being got ready to leave for the front next week.
