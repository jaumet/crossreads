<p>8.1.15</p>
<p>fortnight, belonging to the Ammunition Column (3 of them) &amp; they were arrested &amp; quite a number from the Infantry.</p>
<p>Yesterday afternoon, a party of New Zealanders on a route match camped about a half mile from here; just about dark a scouting party (also New Zealanders but belonging to a different Regt.) lost their way &amp; came upon the New Zealanders who camped here &amp; asked them for food for the night &amp; were refused;  They then came here &amp; we found tea &amp; breakfast for them, they left here this morning after calling their New Zealand brothers some choice names;  A party</p>
