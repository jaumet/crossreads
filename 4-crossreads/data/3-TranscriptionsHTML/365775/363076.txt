<p>off the map, just here and there a few cellars remaining intact affording good dugouts. our horse lines later were made on top of the bricks &amp; mortars of these buildings &amp; here we put in the winter from November 18th to 31 Jan. 1917, when we pull out for a spell, the first since coming in. after about a week here seven of us got hold of a splendid dugout, each of us have a bunk &amp; we have a good fireplace at the end. we are very cosy here, having table &amp; all, we live pretty well, running a mess on our own.</p>
<p>I do not think there was one night we did not miss our rum &amp; milk condensed milk</p>
