  
 Tuesday 23 October 
 Wet and dull weather. 2 trips to A.D.M.S. Ypres  via Busseboom, Ouderdom & Vlamertinghe on each occasion The roads today are very muddy making motor-cycling an unpleasant job.  I'm splashed from head to foot with mud to say nothing of the state of the bike Cards after tea.
