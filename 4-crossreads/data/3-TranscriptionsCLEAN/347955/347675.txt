  
 left Raffa 9 A.M. we had to walk and lead our camels to Shelall twelve miles we got to there 3.30 PM a lot more messing about had dinner.  I am so tired as blases had to lay down tea 6.30 turned in. Sunday March 3.3.18. turned out 7 AM.  Camels out to graze Breakfast fell in 9A.M shifted the camel lines they were down the wrong way dug latrines had dinner went to the waddy done my washing had a good wash had tea turned in.
