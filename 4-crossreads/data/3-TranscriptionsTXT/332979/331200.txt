After every one was satisfied we were marched back to our tent to put away our Kit &amp; then we had a little more Drill &amp; then was dismissed for the Day. This Drill was continued daily until 7 days when I was transferred into the Army Medical Corps having applied for a Transfer a few days previous,  I as Info  I found the work in the A.M. C. lives very different altogether to  to  what I had in the other lives, we had Stretcher Drill which  was  taught us how to handle the Stretchers also how to lift patients also tendering First Aid such as stopping bleeding &amp; bandageing imaginary Wounds &amp; broken limbs.
