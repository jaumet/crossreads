<p>Thursday 24 May</p>
<p>Am on guard to night, &amp; although I cleaned up with great display of energy, failed to get off.</p>
<p>Kneeling in the grass at the cross roads writing this up by the light of the lantern I use to signal traffic along the by- road leading past our farm.</p>
<p>Very cool wind blowing in great contrast to the heat of late.</p>
<p>All quiet &amp; calm.</p>
