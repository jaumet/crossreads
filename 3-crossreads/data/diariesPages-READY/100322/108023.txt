14/4/19
Marcinelle
Up betimes and to the Station to see off a small draft of about 60 all told and including Charlie Hutton. Generals Hobbs Tivey and Martin were all down at the station. Travelling is done in German trucks marked ?Stettin" ?Breslau" etc and all comforts and other gear is put aboard by Hun prisoners. Such is the irony of War! At 9.30 the trains teamed out somewhat rapidly and it took a lot of scrambling before the troops got aboard. Farewells were exchanged from the moving trucks and one chap passing a group of Hun prisoners called out ?Au Revoir, Fritz, you bastard!" in cheerful pleasantry. Met Colonel Forbes later in the morning and with him saw General Martin about several matters. Lunched and then
