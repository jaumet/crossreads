<p>Monday 8 October 1917.</p>
<p>Bringing up supplies from Rafa. Got a little bushed as night came on before we got in sight of Aerodrome on return. Struck an outpost &amp; he put us on our track &amp; supplies were got through safely.</p>
<p>On return delighted to find another Australian mail (direct) 8 letters &amp; packet of local papers.</p>
<p>One of our battle planes brought down a Taube this morning.</p>
<p>Geo. S.S. Cameron back from Marakeb beach with Archie Scott.</p>
