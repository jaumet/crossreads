<p>May 1918<br/>Sunday 19</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash;la-Daours<br/>3 am Was aroused by the detonations of bombs and gas shells, now damage whatever.<br/>Had a midnight toot to Villers Bretnoeaux.<br/>Weather beautiful. Swam three horses during day. had old Ginger dressed. Ken Gunn paid me a little visit and had the usual eufs.<br/>Won a few francs at the old game.</p>
