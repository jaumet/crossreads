 Sunday also, &amp; the work of chaplains came under review &amp; the question of Supernumeray levy (Current) was discussed and a unanimous vote that the Conference should be appealed to to pay it. On Friday it rained all day, &amp; blew, the creeks and all flooded &amp; in some parts damage has been done. 
 Saturday last I had a funeral at Salisbury, a death from Meningitis.
