At 6 oclock we were relieved by the new Guard & we dismounted & so ended my first Guard at Lark Hill & the 2nd one I had ever done. 18th Saw 3 good fights at our Stadium last night, there must have been a good [indecipherable] sitting around enjoying the fun & we had a band playing.
