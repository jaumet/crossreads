 17 Oct 17 At sea patrolling off Horns Reef. Keeping a sharp look out for enemy ships. Two Suman ships sink convoy off the Shetlands. 
 18 Oct At sea patrolling off Horns Reef weather foggy at 9 am. 
 19 Oct 17 Arrived back in Rosyth at 4 am, started coaling at 8.30 finished at 11.30. Average 179 tons an hour. 
 20 Oct 17 In Rosyth. Weather cold. 
 21 Oct 17 Steamed to sea at 7.30 pm.
