I found the Colonel had left for Australia (via England) whilst I was away & I was very glad for the old gentleman has been out since August 1914- right through Gallipoli- & so he fully deserves his spell.  I was very sorry I wasn't able to say Good bye to him, & he expressed the same to one of the other officers, & stated how pleased he had been with my care of him.
