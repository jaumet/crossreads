<p>1915<br/>May. 23 Sun.<br/>1st Cairo Boy Scouts (English) are supplying boys for <strike>duty</strike> orderly duty daily both at Anglo-American Hospital (for Officers) &amp; Gezira Palace &ndash; where they are doing work that would otherwise have to be done by men of the unit.<br/>A number of English &amp; French ladies are helping with the nursing in the wards . They are all amateurs but can help of course.<br/>Service conducted by a C of E Clergyman this morning at 10 o'clock.<br/>Before turning in went for a walk with "Snow" Freeman</p>
