<p>Saturday 20 April 1918<br/>Photos.  Pilot Lt. Lewis, in air 3 h. 5, all photos spoilt at the wing photo section.<br/>Photos.  Pilot Lt. Mc, in air 2 h.<br/>Films auto. camera jammed.<br/>On first photo trip we (2 machines) met 4 Huns 1000 ft. above us.<br/>Very [from opposite page] disquieted and dissapointed that our pilots would not engage, though I urged mine to do so.<br/>fired 50 rounds at Huns.</p>
<p>Sunday 21 April 1918<br/>No photos, too cloudy, spent day digging in tent.  First hot day.</p>
