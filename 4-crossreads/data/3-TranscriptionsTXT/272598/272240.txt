 Thursday 4.10.17  - Friday 5.10.17 Both days have been up for a while, gradually becoming stronger.  Received a fine bunch of mail from home 22-29 July. 
 Saturday 6.10.17 Break in the weather.  September was a beautiful month as far as weather was concerned.  Convoy of "Huns" taken into hospital here. 
 Sunday 7.10.17 to Tuesday 9.10.17 Found my legs again
