<p>24-2-17<br/>Sat 24th<br/>Left Bayud at 5.30 after rising at 4 feeding our horses &amp; having breakfast, made the pace warm &amp; arrived at Abd a few minutes after 7 caught the 8.20 train &amp; played bridge on the way to Kantara arriving there at midday; Arrangements were made to stay at Kantara tonight but I slipped away with several others &amp; caught a train about 1 oclock for Cairo arriving there at 5.30  Went to the Anzac Hostel &amp; left my gear there &amp; arranged for a bed then had dinner &amp; a shave &amp; drove around to the National to see Frost &amp; Leask but found they had gone so rang up the 14th</p>
