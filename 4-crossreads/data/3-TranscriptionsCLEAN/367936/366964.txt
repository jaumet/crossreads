  
 Sunday 16 April 1916 Beautiful day. Father, Mother and I drove over to Wonga and took 4 Rams.  Mustered sheep into bottom paddock. Went over to Linburn in evening to "send off".  Was presented with note book & pen.  Large crowd present. 
 Monday 17 April 1916 Dick and I cut chaff.  Father rode round.  Mother & Miss Stirling went for clothes. Tellegram came from Bill Graham saying not sailing till Wednesday week. Father drew off honey in evening.
