 Dec 14  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;Very warm to-day. but a fine misty rain was falling all day - went on supplies as usual. I swopped batteries with one of the other lorries, I now serve 18  th  &nbsp;batt. instead of 17  th  &nbsp;batt. Fred left for Boulogne with his lorry. Fritz shelled Hazebrouck heavily - 
 &nbsp; 
 Dec 15  th  &nbsp; Beautiful day. but still wet underfoot - We did our supply trip - Jim had a liberal lot of rum up at the battery &amp; kept it up in the evening - The 5&nbsp; A.S.P.  D.S.C. &nbsp;have had several lorries catch fire during this month. some of them burnt pretty badly - 
 &nbsp; 
 Dec 16  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;Cold &amp; windy. I had trouble starting my engine &amp; had to get a tow to start, the magneto is crook (Dixie) &nbsp;I carried on with the supplies the rest of the 5  th  &nbsp;D.S.C moved off to Boulogne to-day -
