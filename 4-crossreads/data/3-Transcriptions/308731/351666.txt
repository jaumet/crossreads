<p>Mum told me to put that in it and. I thing that you think that you where at home having figs and fruit. and. I wish I cold find a sily pitsher wat I did one was the march past by and one was off to war and one was the fiendly viset.  I have sum of the pictures this is the only picture.  I could find.  good bye.<br/>Much love from<br/>frank Burrowes.<br/>xxx etc.</p>
<p>[Note from Frank's mother:  This was written a good while ago]</p>
