Peterson broke down in health after the Battle of Rafa, and was invalided home suffering from shell shock and neurasthenia, after receiving a most flattering letter from the Colonel of the Regiment (Lieut.-Colonel G. H. Bourne, D.S.O.). Sergt. Peterson's old British regiment, the Fourth Dragoon Guards, were included in the original British Expeditionary Force which crossed to France, and were with the De Lisle's Brigade in the fearful retreat from Mons, and after the Germans were driven back over the Marne. It is pathetic to note that the original regiment is to-day practically non-existent. Sergt. Peterson is now in business in Nerang.</p>
