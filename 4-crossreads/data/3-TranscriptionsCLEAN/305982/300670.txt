  
 18-3-16 Out on parade this morning these fellows the majority of same rather are men not wanted in their old Batt therefore out they go. they are a bad look our officer is a dream  This afternoon out again about 3.20 a storm put in an appearance it did rain & blow. of course we had some sand before hand which of course means back to camp parculiar Dusty appearance afterwards. 
 19-3-16 It rained heavily early this morn our tent was wet some rather fresh  abo  when we got out of bed church parade. this morning I saw CO about my transfer but he said he would look into it.  I saw Capt Barton whom said he would try & get me back to 13th Btt. other Sgt paraded about seniority but nothing doing much length of service. 
 20.3.16 Parades as usual today this morning squad & Platoon drill no rifles. this afternoon out for a march another Sgt & I. Artillery practicing gunnery  saat  sat down at the old Battle ground. very hot today. 
 21-3-16 same old parades just the same as yesterday beautiful moonlight night. last night glorious [indecipherable]
