
24 - how I will spend the other &pound 1 a week at Lemnos besides my mess allowance and Aunt Maria says I can send any I save home to her and she will put it in her savings bank. I got two new blouses till my own were washed but otherwise no clothes. I gave the black coat & skirt I got a Farmers for &pound 3 odd to a woman, a sergeants wife in the 2nd class, whose husband died on the ship, and she had 6 children with her. I went to see Mrs Hilda Owen at her hotel in South Kensington and saw Irene. Colonel Bob was wounded. She only saw it in the paper and
