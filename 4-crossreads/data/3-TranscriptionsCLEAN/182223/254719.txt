  
 Monday 29 November 1915  In Cairo last night with Dr Henry & others at a picture show, too sleep to enjoy it, home about 1 A.M.  In Cairo at 10 am. saw Col. Featherstone.  Writing & reading during the rest of the day 
 Tuesday 30 November 1915  Reading & writing all day.  The coldest day I have felt in Egypt. Wind from the North  Report says that snow is falling at Galipoli. 
 Wednesday 1 December 1915 No wind this morning Put on winter clothing today. Too heavy.  In Cairo during the afternoon. Had a chat with Knox who is managing the Red Cross work. He told me 3000 to 4000 Australians in hospital here today as against 7 or 8 thousand some time ago.
