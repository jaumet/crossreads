<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>In reply to your letter of 16th October 1917, I beg to inform you that a photograph of the grave of Corporal A.K. Gordon has been taken and a copy will be sent to you free of <strike>cost as soon</strike> when as possible.  I cannot trace any previous application having been received in this office.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>[indecipherable]<br/>Captain, Staff Capt.,<br/><strike>D.A.A.G.</strike> for<br/>Brigadier-General,<br/>Director of Graves Registration and Enquiries.</p>
<p>Miss N.E. Clark,<br/>"Elcho",<br/>Stanhope Road,<br/>Killara,<br/>New South Wales,<br/>Australia.</p>
