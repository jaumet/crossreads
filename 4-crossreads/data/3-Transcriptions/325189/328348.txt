<p>13th March 1915 Saturday</p>
<p>Medically examined this morning &amp; paraded this afternoon, the parade ground being on the desert.  It is jolly hard work walking on the sand &amp; the dust we make nearly chokes one.  The air is very clear, very cold in the early morning &amp; nice &amp; warm in the afternoon.  We get our meals in Mess Sheds.  The Egyptian Gov. are giving the English Tommies 6d a day.
