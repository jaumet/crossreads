<p>tried to make them beleive I was born in Belfast &amp; had crossed the "line" on the way out, but was to no purpose, I was ordered a bath &amp; they just did lather me with that soot &amp; ducked me well too.  I got more than anyone else I think.  Suppose it was to be my just reward for having spent the whole day in arranging the affair.</p>
<p>We had electric lights all over the No. 3 hatch &amp; round my room, where the ceremony was held.  I thoroughly enjoyed being dipped &amp; would have been disappointed had I not been.  The following is the Programme.</p>
