  
 27.7.18 
 hear on the Somme.  We came down from up north near Hazebrook [Hazebrouck] about three weeks ago.  So far our Batt. have not been in the front of the hop overs but have been following up as reserves & have been rushing from one part to another taking up positions in the line & have a pretty rough spin from Fritz's shells.
