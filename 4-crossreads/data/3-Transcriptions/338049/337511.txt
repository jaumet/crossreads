<p>Monday 28 October<br/>Very fine day.</p>
<p>As usual in morning Major inspected the crowd.</p>
<p>Afternoon journeyed to [indecipherable] &amp; saw Geoff Stillman. Then to Marieul saw Bill Morgan &amp; Korah Wills. Had tea with Bill &amp; a long yarn with Korah.</p>
<p>Came home in the dark but managed well.</p>
