21 Feb 18to 4th March Arrived Rosyth, coaled & went alongside the dockyard pier to have aeroplane platform completed. Also camaflouged the ship all colours of the rainbow.
4th March 18Left dockyard & anchored in stream off Charlestowe plenty of leave breaking.
6th March 18Went out past May Id to do firing & returned to Rosyth.
