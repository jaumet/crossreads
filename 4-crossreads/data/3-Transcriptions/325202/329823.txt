<p>Aug 26 (Sunday)<br/>Our 1st Division has done its bit on the Somme &amp; has pulled out.</p>
<p>Thursday we trekked back to Rubempre, Friday on to within two miles of Doullens where we entrained yesterday morning &amp; came North again. We detrained last night at Hopontre Siding near Poperinghe &amp; are bivouaced close by waiting to march to our billets on this sector of the Ypres front.</p>
<p>Oct 9/16<br/>We have been stationed since arriving at Hoograf</p>
