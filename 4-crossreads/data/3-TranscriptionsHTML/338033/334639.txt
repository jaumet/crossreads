<p>farmer living in their farms as they all build<br/>together making a village which is a proper little township on its own, having all the necessary shops. Most of the grass is used as hay, for there is not a great quantity of wheat or oats grown.<br/>Haystacks are well made, and always thatched. Being only small farms, you see none of the latest implements, single furrow ploughs and the very old style implements being in use.<br/>A lot of the wood and shutters are cultivated forests in order to keep up the supply</p>
