Jan 26th Route march to Goulbourn River along road leading to Trawool .Rations on Route, Bully, Biscuits &amp; Tea. Distance about 16 miles by road. Jan 27th Leave to Melbourne. Marched to Seymour station catching train about 12 pm Arrived Melbourne 3pm.
