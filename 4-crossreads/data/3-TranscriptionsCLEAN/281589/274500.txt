We we being issued with our furlough passes & tram passes (We have the option of a tram or train pass, men wanting Ferry or Coach pass being dealt with later on, we are mooring alongside the wharf, it is lovely being so near Home, we are all very calm now. How the whistles tootle & the kids yell from the Domain Baths, after being so long on the boat I must have been sorry to leave, as I waited for my mates, 
 at last we went down the gangway, in a sort of dream. V.A.D.s cheered & stuck flowers
