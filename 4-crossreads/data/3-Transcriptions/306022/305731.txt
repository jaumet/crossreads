<p>Tuesday 24th<br/>Hottest day we have had so far. Land sighted about 8.30 am and we put into Dakka, in French Senegal about 9.30, having to go in by a very circuitious channel, going around a rock which was almost a miniature of Gibraltar in appearance, also being a fort.</p>
