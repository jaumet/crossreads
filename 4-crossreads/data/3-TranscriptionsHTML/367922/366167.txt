<p>7. 2. 1904</p>
<p>My Dear Fry<br/>Many thanks for the notes you sent me: they are just what I require, and I shall not fail to make full use of them as occasion requires.</p>
<p>They are so ample that I shall be able to cut them into two &ndash; so you have given me material for two speeches. The charm about them is they will strike new chords in "Postal [indecipherable] Speechifying" and you know it is something these times to strike out on new lines.</p>
<p>As I told you I had</p>
