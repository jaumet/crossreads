<p>44 August 22nd<br/>Recd 8 letters, 3 from Army &amp; Navy Stores (accounts) 1 aunt Sarah, Mary Mrs Watts Ditchfield no 5 from Kate &ndash; made a very hot curry tonight &ndash; Played Bridge with Robinson tonight &ndash; nothing startling today &ndash;</p>
<p>August 23rd<br/>Wrote PC to Mother &amp; Fanny, Recd PC from Eileen &ndash; Feeling very much off colour tonight &ndash; Nearly every body in our mess is likewise, I attribute the epidemic to the curry. Recd parcel of biscuits from Berne. Played Bridge - <strike>Recd</strike></p>
<p>August 24th<br/>Feeling very sick &ndash; Doctor called see me &amp; instructed me to lay in bed &amp; not to eat anything &ndash; I have slight constipation of the bowels.</p>
<p>Recd 2 parcels one from Mary (Books) &amp; 1 from Army &amp; Navy Stores &ndash; also 1 letter from A. &amp; N. Stores &ndash;</p>
<p>My foot is deriving the benefit of my confinement to bed, <strike>as well as myself</strike> &ndash; Read most of the day.</p>
<p><strike>In</strike> todays Communique conveyed the news that the ships to transport officers to be exchanged, are now waiting in Rotterdam Harbour, &amp; that the wounded will be put on board in a ve[ry] short time &ndash; so we should soon hear good news &ndash;</p>
<p>This is first illness I have had since being a "Krieggefangenier</p>
<p>August 25th<br/>Best sleep I have had for a long time last night &ndash; feeling much better this mng &ndash; Dr called to see me. I got up at 11 am. Recd letter from Gladys also P.C. from Van der zeyde.</p>
<p>Meeting somewhat heated one of a[ll] [indecipherable] officers was held at 1 pm to elect [indecipherable] &amp; to discuss other business</p>
