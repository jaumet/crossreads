 14 &ndash; Sea journey. This letter has to be posted today. There is no more news to tell you. It's the 21st July and the weather is very calm &amp; sunshiny. Much love to you both dear parents. I hope you are both well and trying to get some peace and quiet. I hope Bill is well and that he and Edith have a comfortable house &ndash; I tried to see Mrs Myers but I didn't get an answer to my letter.
