
Monday 7 August 1916Summer again, but much cooler at night. Lecture by Div. Gas Officer to reinforcements in the morning, & during the day I visited our Engineers & other units to see about helmets &c.In the afternoon rode out on bike to Fresselles to try & get a refill for my electric torch from the canteen there. A hot & dusty ride over a pretty bad road. Demonstration in use of SOS rockets at night.
