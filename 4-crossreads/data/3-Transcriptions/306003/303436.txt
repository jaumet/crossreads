<p>20-7-16<br/>up again &amp; out scouting;  This afternoon the 2nd Bde received orders to come back within 1&frac12;  miles of our strong posts &amp; bivouac for the night;  One lad of the 2nd Brigade was shot dead today (through the head) &amp; 10 others wounded;  Two Turks were captured today &amp; they say there is 7 Battalion of Turkish troops under 5 German officers &amp; they intend to attack Armadier which is the name of the camp at Railhead right on the coast &amp; which is one of our strongest positions, so we are looking forward to the attack;</p>
<p>It is asserted that 8000 Troops arrived at Railhead last night &amp; 10,000 more will arrive tonight.  B Sqd of the 1st escorted some artillery out this morning but they did not go far  Hill 110 I believe, but the planes report that they could not locate the enemy's main body.  We sleep with clothes on tonight &amp; ready to move out at half an hours notice.</p>
