
<p>192</p>
<p>NOV. &nbsp; SATURDAY 18 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Snowing nearly all day lightly &amp; the ground was white at 10 pm. Cleaning harness &amp; stables ect in morning &amp; the colonel inspected it again. Went over to YMCA in afternoon &amp; played Poss billiards. Poss &amp; I went to the pictures at night &amp; it was the usual rotten show.</p>
