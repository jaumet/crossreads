  
 May, 1915 May 21-22  missing  Sunday 23 Spent the day in orderly room busy with Pay Sheets etc:- Very hot & our horses will be glad of the reed roof shelters being built - Rumour that 500 of our Brigade are back at Alexandria having had a tough time in the trenches but always successful. Here with the Horses we all endeavouring by Lectures in the heat of the day to instruct the Reinforcements as well as possible.
