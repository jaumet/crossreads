 1 September 1916 Fri Awoke &amp; very sick. 9.30 am left Contay marching about nine miles to Beauval. On arriving went around the town. Saw Tony Matterson Clary Loten &amp; Doc Grey who's has been awarded the D.C.M. They are all well &amp; look none the worse for [text continued at top of page] 
 [Note in margin] Village Church. 
 2 September 1916 Sat [indecipherable] On water &ndash; fatigue in the morning. After dinner I went round to Jim's billet. Coming back home on the road I ran up against Clarry Walters. In the evening I went to the town-hall to a concert rendered by the 17th Batt. It was very funny many of the performers being well tanked up. Especially Spud Murphy the stretcher bearer D.C.M. 
 [Note in margin] Drawing water 200 feet.
