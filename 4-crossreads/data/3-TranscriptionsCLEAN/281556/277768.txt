 9th. Wednesday. With cycles left at 6am for Halloy about 8 miles off. Grand scenery here passed aerodome early in journey. Reached Halloy about 8am & got billets alloted early. 17th. in last about 3pm. very quiet little village. Slept out under hedge in orchard close
