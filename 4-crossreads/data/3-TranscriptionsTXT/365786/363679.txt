
When fresh wounded were expected all able to be removed were sent to the different Convalescent Hospitals in Egypt.  Some were sent to England & others were returned to Australia.  (10 minutes)When our fresh troops arrive from Australia they are some days settling themselves in their various camps.  Then they set to work to drill & train.  They have long route marches in the desert, no joke during the summer months - & they practise trench digging, etc. & in their spare time they visit the city of Cairo with its fascinating shops & bazaars, its quaint & beautiful mosques, the remains of the aqueduct built by the Romans or they take the tram & visit the Pyramids of Gizeh [Giza] & the Sphinx.
