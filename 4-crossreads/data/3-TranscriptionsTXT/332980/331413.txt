Police requires to examine all Passes, after my was examined I continued on through the Streets, this Place is very busy & large crowds of Civilians, Soldiers & W.A.A.C. Girls were walking about everywhere. The Trams running to & fro were always full, I followed a Tram line until I came to the Beach & as it was a glorius day there were a large number of People promenading the Beach & Bathing, just near the Beach is a large convalescent Soldiers Home & they were all dressed in Blue Suits of a good cut & they all looked quite
