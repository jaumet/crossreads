 Afterwards we flew the Union Jack at half-mast but were made take it down as flags are not allowed on troopships at sea. 
 We had a little more Lifebelt &amp; Boat Drill to-day 
 10th of November Friday Received Wireless news that the R.M.S. Arabic was torpedoed in the Mediterranean Sea on November 6th at Noon. Kit inspection to-day again.
