<p>1915<br/>Oct. 27 Wed.<br/>Something wrong with the Australian mails. Only a few odd letters have arrived during the past 3 or 4 weeks.<br/>A Hospital ship is leaving for Australia to-morrow &amp; 9 orderlies had to be detailed from No. 2 G.H. Not feeling extra keen at the moment on going back for Christmas. I did not apply.</p>
