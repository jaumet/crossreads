<p>Sunday 19<br/>Met Bob Goldrick now Capt in this camp. Leaves for France on Tuesday.</p>
<p>Monday 20<br/>The troops about here, including O.T.B. have been inspected by H.R.H. Duke of Connaught. <br/>The review was a success<br/>On account of my 6'2" of length I was given a rifle &amp; put in front line of Battalion.</p>
<p>Wednesday 22<br/>Warned for draft to Park House.</p>
<p>Thursday 23<br/>Letter from Dock that he can get me in the AQMGs office. H.Q.</p>
<p>Friday 24<br/>Move to Park House, A.M.C. Depot. The camp is very good.<br/>Dock says I will be 'phoned for tomorrow to take up duties at A.Q.M.Gs office Tidworth</p>
<p>Saturday 25<br/>Interview with officer at AQMGs office &amp; I am to be taken on the staff.</p>
