Australia &amp;  whoever will may go to worse places, before I enlist as a    volunteer  to serve my country again after this &amp; the various rebuffs I have had in trying to get to the front. Meer  dirty  opposition    &amp; dislike is influencing this  fellow  Parker in his actions towards me. Cold footed as he is &amp; no desire to go to the front &amp; he stops others. Legge &amp; I hold a council of war, with Palliser &amp; are going to see the thing out, will fight this against his ignorant worship Parker, as I know the letter he has in hand is granting my application for transfer &amp; asking for me to be sent without delay.
