<p>up at Debabis yesterday afternoon as he made a line for Debabis soon after he landed, his engine had gone wrong which compelled him to land &amp; his propellor broke when the plane hit  the ground but he was not hurt<br/>At 6 oclock this evening the 1st (?) &amp; 2nd Regt left Oghratina &amp; started home, when we first came here no smoking was allowed coming home from Oghratina during the night time but no objections had been raised on the last 2 or 3 trips &amp; tonight the boys commenced singing soon after leaving Oghratina  &amp; popular songs were kept going till we arrived at Romani at 10 oclock</p>
<p>tonight the boys were in good spirits all the way &amp; finished up a very trying trip without a grumble from anyone;  The 2nd  3rd Regts' horses are much worse that our &amp; they lost a good many on the trip, we managed to go through without losing any, although practically every horse was knocked up when we got into Oghratina this morning;  the weather has been exceptionally hot during the last few days</p>
