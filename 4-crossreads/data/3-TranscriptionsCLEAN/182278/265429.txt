  
 Monday 22.7.18  More Haymaking  wrote some letters 
 Tuesday 23.7.18  Raining heavily, Wrote to Doff telling her of my intended return. 
 Wednesday 24.7.18  Orders received to report for duty at H.Qrs London, arrived at 6pm from Godalming. Went to the Alhambra 
 Thursday 25.7.18  President of D. [C.in's?] on 9 cases. Went to National Sporting Club at night. 
 Friday 26.7.18  Took Pattie Bamford to tea & afterwards spent the night at Malden
