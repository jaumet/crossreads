 Sunday June 24th Church parade in morning. settling down in camp. Feel the heat very much. Am not at present very much impressed by this Country. Monday June 28 Inspected by OC &amp; Camp Commandant. Troops pouring in at main camp. Tuesday June 26  Moved camp about &frac12; mile Suppose they wanted to give us something to do Saw what they termed a mirage to day. Caused I am told by the heat on sand. It is hoped that the promised land Palestine is superior to this. Wednesday June 27 What beautiful Sunsets &amp; Moonlight nights they have in this country Makes one think of home.
