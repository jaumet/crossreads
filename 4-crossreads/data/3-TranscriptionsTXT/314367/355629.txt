 I wrote in the forenoon but I have more to add &ndash; I heard an epigram in the smoke room which is good even if not original &ndash; 'Work is the curse of the drinking man'!! said a 30 year old philosopher &ndash; Well now the 'juice' is on &amp; all is serene &amp; good &ndash; About 2 pm &ndash; Skeete of the Una &amp; Lts Patrick &amp; Powell came off in a motor launch towing a boat full of niggers &amp; the C &amp; I joined up &amp; off we went &ndash; We went down harbor about 3 miles &amp; got between the island Matupi &amp; the main land &ndash; We traveled to a certain corner where we cd see steam coming up off the surface of the sea &ndash; It was a hot spring &ndash; I cd see the water boiling in it &ndash; We got up close &ndash; tried the temp &amp; in we hopped &ndash; Tell R &ndash; though &ndash; we had sounded &amp; got 10 feet I struck my chest at 5 feet &amp; scraped the skin off &ndash; A rock came up &ndash; no great harm &amp; we swam in the hot water &amp; now &amp; again scuttled like anything from a boiling patch &ndash; It was an unique experience for most of us &ndash; One saw steam bubbles coming up in certain parts &amp; those spots were hot spots &ndash; Leakage from the cauldron &ndash;
