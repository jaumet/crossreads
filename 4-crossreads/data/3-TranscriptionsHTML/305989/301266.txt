<p>Sunday 22 July 1917<br/>On mess orderly, stunt at 10.30 went out &amp; travelled all night &amp; got bushed in fog, met rest of Div. at El Bugga.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 7 and 6].</p>
<p>Monday 23 July 1917<br/>Took gun into position and saw a good many cavalry about:  arrived back at camp about 4.30 dead beat.  Picquet at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 24 July 1917<br/>General routine, very hot &amp; dusty.  Got letter with &pound;4.</p>
<p>Wednesday 25 July 1917<br/>Clarrie went to Rest Camp on beach.</p>
<p>Thursday 26 July 1917<br/>Stunt with 1st Regt. 1 &amp; 2 sectn. rode all night from 9.30 &amp; captured prisoners &amp; killed 1 Turco officer, got home late.</p>
<p>Friday 27 July 1917<br/>Quiet day, very hot but not very windy.</p>
<p>Saturday 28 July 1917<br/>Hot and windy nothing doing, same routine.  On Picquet at night.</p>
