<p>3<br/>We ware our uniforms always now, only put on dungarees for meals and any dirty work.<br/>I think I am going to get my pay tomorrow, that sergt has been awfully nice and has done all he could for me.<br/>Now I am in A Squadron I ought to get away with the 16th reinforcements, (which should have gone last week but did not) but as we are 80 over strength and they are giving preference to men who enlisted last year I dont think any of our lot will go so that means I will wont go for another month yet at least and perhaps much longer.<br/>I have to be enoculated 3 times yet and that takes 3 weeks to do.<br/>We were inspected by the Governor General yesterday; he has a jolly nice</p>
