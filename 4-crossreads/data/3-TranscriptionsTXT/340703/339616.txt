 April 1916 Sunday 2 Visited sights of Colombo in rickshaw. Temple,Galle Face. Park Gardens. Bought silk at native shops. Spent &pound;2-5-0. Thoroughly enjoyed stay. Cost 15/- for 2 days, very cheap Left Colombo 10.30 pm 
 Monday 3 Wrote long letters about Colombo . Small monkey brought aboard from Colombo. Pet of the ship
