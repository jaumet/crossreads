
May, 1915Sunday 30I will enclose a letter from one of our Sergeants - Poor old Man a D.C.M. man was one of my best reinforcements & gave me his medals & papers the night he left - I hope you are all well & this goes along safelyLove to allFrankAs Sgt Lovetts letter has passed Censor it should go on to you it & I cannot be blamed for writing War news "comprenez"
