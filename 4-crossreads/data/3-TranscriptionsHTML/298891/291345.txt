<p>1917 September</p>
<p>19 Wednesday</p>
<p>Sister Hansell none too kind gave me quinine injection.  Adam remarked that the arm was full of frothy stuff but she rammed it in nevertheless.  A lump arose in about an hour after.  Hope it has no serious results.  Feeling more comfortable otherwise.</p>
