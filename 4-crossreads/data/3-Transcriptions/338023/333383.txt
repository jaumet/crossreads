<p>Also a Miss Cooper (of Brisbane), who visits here twice weekly, keeps me well supplied with decent cigarettes. By the way Miss C. was over here getting a 'voice' put thro squad drill when war broke out &amp; un-dutifully refused to return to the aged official parent &amp; Brisbane Govt Hse 'shoshuls', preferring to live here on her own cash &amp; send motors from Silvermere to take the boys out joy-riding. Also received a visit from Abbe'   <strike>[Duposs]</strike>  Ducants late of parish of Lille &ndash; Armentieres who had  been asked to call by friend Bucamp of [indecipherable]. Very nice of him.</p>
<p>Have had various letters from the unit &amp; hear that good boys have gone under since I left &ndash; you see I was the first, the very first casualty in action! Luckily for myself.</p>
<p>Mrs H. writes to let me know Chas. is waiting to rejoin his Batt. The cash comes in handy, am holding enough &amp; the rest will be used at  overstrand as I have directed for Xmas presents for Harold &amp; Charlie &amp; the youngsters.</p>
<p>Love from Harry.</p>
