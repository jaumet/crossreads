 Apl 27  th  &nbsp; Fine day &nbsp;we got up at 9 A.M. &nbsp;left park at 11.30 A.M. &amp; took small arms ammunition from Corps reserve dump to Amiens (for machine guns as anti aircraft arms)  on Boulevard Alsace   Loraine.  We got home at 4.15 P.M. it rained a bit in the evening 
 Blossom is coming on the fruit trees. Fritz is shelling Amiens all the time and knocking the centre of the town about badly&mdash;
