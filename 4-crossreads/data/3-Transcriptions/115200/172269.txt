<p>56</p>
<p>These are the rations that [we?] get issued to us at the front</p>
<p>Jam &frac14; of a tin per Day.</p>
<p>Sugar. One [ounce?] per Day&nbsp; Perhaps it may be two</p>
<p>Bacon 2 Slices per Day.</p>
<p>Bully Beef you can get as much of this tack as you want&nbsp; tea 2 ounces per Day. Cheese about 3 ounces and it is not to good at that</p>
<p>Salt very little. Biscuits of these there was an unlimited Supply. Rum about &frac14; of a pint a week.</p>
<p>This ration is Supposed to last us 24 hours</p>
