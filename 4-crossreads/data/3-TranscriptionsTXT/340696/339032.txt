 130 "roaring forties" &amp; we struck boisterous weather for a week including a Snow Storm. Had to be screwed down so things weren't too comfortable. 
 Plenty of entertainment on board orchestra &amp; local concert talent also a miniature Picture Show which drew large "houses" &amp; the remarks from the Diggers was always the Star turn. 
 Spent 2 &frac12; days in Cape Town &amp; saw the place- very nice too. Secured a car one day &amp; drove round Table Mountain a gorgeous trip. Plenty of Fruit 
 Touched Albany- more fruit. Touched Adelaide- more fruit &amp; said goodbye to our S.A. pals. Great excitement on the Wharf. 
 Arrived Melbourne 20th Aug 1919
