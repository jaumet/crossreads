
<p>256</p>
<p>1917 JANUARY</p>
<p>22 MONDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; cold. Saw the sun</p>
<p>for a few minutes in the</p>
<p>afternoon, but no warmth</p>
<p>in it at all. Went for a bath</p>
<p>in the morning &amp; we had</p>
<p>a good hot swim in some</p>
<p>old wine vats about two</p>
<p>miles away. Exercising</p>
<p>horses all the afternoon.</p>
<p>Saw Joe Clonan in morning</p>
<p>&amp; he looks A1. Got a letter</p>
<p>from Linda &amp; Marj.</p>
