  
 Tuesday 16.10.17 Arrived at le Havre about 9.30 Am, detrained & were taken in huts fitted up for the temporary detention of patients waiting to cross Channel.  Went on board Hospital ship "Western Australia" about 3 pm & was detailed to a bed in E ward  Moved out from Le Havre with the old "Warilda" under escort at 8 pm. Fairly rough trip across but felt "A1", slept fairly well.
