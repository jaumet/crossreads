<p>340<br/>The rules are very strict here we get no leave whatever &ndash; up to a few days ago we could go out from 2-6 in the afternoon but a couple of fellows played up and were missing so the whole of the rest of us have had to suffer. It is a most miserable place to be shut up in &ndash; There are no grounds &ndash; just one</p>
