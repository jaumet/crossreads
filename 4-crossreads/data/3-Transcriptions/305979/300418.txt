<p>upper deck from which we could see both the officer on the "stage" and the man in the "pit"<br/>After a heavy tea of fresh bread &amp; butter, pickled onions &amp; cheese, which were issued to us, and tinned herrings &amp; more bread, and fancy biscuits, which we bought, and after the heroic efforts at making a noise at the concert, we all turned into our hammocks, tired out but happy, ad unium.</p>
<p>After dolling ourselves up in out tunics and khaki trousers, and polishing up our boots (but not brushing our hair, for we can't) we attended church parade this morning but I was a little disappointed for the chaplain did not seem to make the best of the good opportunity which has been placed</p>
