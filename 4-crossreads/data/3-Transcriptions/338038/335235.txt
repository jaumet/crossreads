<p>Marseille we expect to reach tomorrow evening, all being well. Nothing eventful has happened on the trip, which has been a great relaxation after the toil in Egypt. There is a good deal of care to be taken of course regarding protection from the Kaiser's "tinned fish," but all the same our voyage has been very pleasant. One can quite understand the charm of those yachting cruises in the Mediterranean after having come through here. This is absolutely the best time of year of course and the climate is delightful.
