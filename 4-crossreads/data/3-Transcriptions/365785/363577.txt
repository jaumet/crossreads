<p>From No. 2071, 3rd L.H.F.A.<br/>30/1/16</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Read<br/>Thank you very much indeed for your Xmas card in the shape of a boot which was sent on to me by Jack.  It was good of you to remember me.  I hope that you are all well and that you had a Merry Xmas and that this New Year will bring you every happiness &amp; comfort.</p>
<p>Kind regards from<br/>E.C. Bartels</p>
