 Monday 17 March 1919 Very dull all day today. Hotel De Ville picquet tonight. Went for a stroll &amp; did the usual work. Things very quiet tonight. Bus called early so we were home in camp at 10 pm. 
 Tuesday 18 March 1919 Special patrol today. Bill, George &amp; I went to Avelings &amp; stayed there till midnight dancing &amp; singing. Walked home via Rouelles &amp; Les Acacias. Got home at 1.45. Good going. Do me. Have a bonny cold.
