<p>Then we saw the S.M. &amp; we saw the S. Cook<br/>And if they could have done it they'd have killed us with a look<br/>The cook was looking healthy and the S.M. well<br/>He asked me if I'd like a little C.B. for a spell</p>
<p>So we went round to the Q.M. and he wasn't quite polite<br/>So we stole round to the Q.M. Store when he was out of sight<br/>We looked in every corner until night began to fall<br/>And vainly tried to find a secret panel in the wall</p>
