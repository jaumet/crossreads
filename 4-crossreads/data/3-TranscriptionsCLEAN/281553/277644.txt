  
 16th Thursday Mails distributed 9-30 a.m. then on to Beach, swim & scouring dixies, home 12-30, no duties all day & night, showers.  Went up to Mortar with other Cpl. & Sgt. during demonstration 10-30 p.m. when our second bomb failed & through accident exploded hitting 4 of us.  One died on way to foot of hill, one other cut up very much, Sgt. & myself merely got splinters slight.  Had my knee dressed at Stn. & returned to Shed.  Bed 12 p.m. 
 17th Friday Leg much swolen & stiff, redressed.  Slept, quiet all day, our section given
