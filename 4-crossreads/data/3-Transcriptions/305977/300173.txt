<p>tea &amp; had a good look around Cairo.  Caught the 10.30pm train back.  We rode donkeys down to the Station from the Central Square.  2 piastres each.</p>
<p>Monday 8th  [Feb]<br/>Cold morning.  Was put on forage fatigue &amp; had a fairly easy morning.  Regiment started drill again in earnest.  We drill in the streets of Maadi.  In the afternoon had to shift some forage about.  Was put on stable picket.  Horses were fairly quiet.  Drew middle shift with Dalley &amp; Blackwell.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9th<br/>Got a course of bayonet &amp; rifle drill.  Was put into us fairly strong.  Posted several letters as Australian mail closed at 11am.  Received a letter from home.</p>
<p>Wednesday 10th<br/>Our troop went on fatigue work improving our horse line.  Rest of regiment went on rifle drill.</p>
