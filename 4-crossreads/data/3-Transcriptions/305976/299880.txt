<p>Monday July 9<br/>Inspection &amp; address given by General Royston. Some 3000 men in Isolation camp Riding exercise in afternoon. Smash of two planes to day. Both Pilots killed<br/>Tuesday July 10<br/>Riding again today. Was thrown &amp; dragged across sand with foot in stirrup. Lucky was not hurt. Concert &amp; sing song in evening. Getting more accustomed to hot weather.<br/>Wednesday July 11<br/>First pay day drew 350 Piastres 1 Piastre = 2&frac12; in English money Usual camp routine<br/>Thursday July 12<br/>Very funny today. Went over to remount &amp; we had to saddle up &amp; ride Mules. Of all the obstinate</p>
