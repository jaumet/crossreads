
Saturday 4 May 1918The L.H. who were in a bad position at Es Salt have withdrawn safely.H.A. Bombing and machine gunning.Bombs were not very successful but fired 800 rounds at troops and did good work.  Pilot Lt. Cameron, in air 2 h. 35 m.Could not get Cameron down low enough, fired 800 rounds.
Sunday 5 May 1918Pilot Lt. Watt, in air 35 m., test engine, test camera and two guns.
