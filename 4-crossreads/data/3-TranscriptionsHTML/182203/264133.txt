<p>3rd A.C.C.S.                                                                                                                                                                c/o 29th C.C.S.                                                                                                                                                       B.E.F.                                                                                                                                                               France                                                                                                                                                                   26.12.16<br/>My dear Moth. &amp; Sisters,<br/>Cannot remember if I wrote to you on Sunday but have a few spare moments, so shall scribble a few lines to you.<br/>So sorry I did not receive any letters from home or the boys in the firing line, for Xmas, it would have added greatly to my happiness. Well, I just dreaded the thought of this Xmas but really it was one of the joyous ones I've ever spent.<br/>We have quite a number of patients but expect to evacuate in a day or so, but it was great</p>
