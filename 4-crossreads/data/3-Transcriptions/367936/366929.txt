<p>Sunday 6 February 1916<br/>Did not feel too well in morning.<br/>Afternoon got shivers.  Getting ready to go back to camp found had tempreture of 105&deg;.  Got in Dr. Mason.  Have Bronchitis and Congested Lung.</p>
<p>Monday 7 February 1916<br/>Off my onion most of day.  Telegram &amp; Dr's certificate sent to camp for me.  Telegram sent home.  Dr afraid of pneumonia.  Aunt Min sat up all Monday night with me.</p>
