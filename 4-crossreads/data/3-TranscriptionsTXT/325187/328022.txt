 [Friday 6 October 1916] 
 Dinner  Ferrars  Neill Made an allotment of 10/- per day to my wife as long as I'm in the service- We dine with Mr W  Ferr  Neill tonight Theatre very amusing last night, but Im not keen on it really the air is too bad. Recd. long letter from Day dated Aug 6 (I was wounded then) &amp; Mother   Sorry you sent parcel but some of the L.H. Officers will get it- Paper cuttings also up to June 19th- Answering your Question Mother General Chauvel is O/C Anzac Mounted Division.
