<p>Thursday 5 Sept.<br/>Cleaning up though warned about very rapid work. Congratulated etc by Capt Buckland on work.</p>
<p>Called out about 3 p.m. To Curlu laid line to Clery then to Ommiecourt les Clery. To lay another to 10th Bde but wagon missed me.</p>
<p>So home about 10.30. Camp swamped by rain.</p>
