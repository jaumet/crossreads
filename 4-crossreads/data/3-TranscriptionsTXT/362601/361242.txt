 25 July, Friday 
 Go to Town, meet Os Young, Dave Vernon, Frank Falls. Buy boots. First time in Civvies. Go to Pictures with Dave. Buy fruit &amp; peanuts. Go out to Glebee. Come Home with Bert Cocks. Go to Manly with Mum about Brook. See Ray o'Grady's people. 
 Received several letters that I wrote in England. 
 26 July, Saturday 
 Recd Discharge &amp; medal. I do gardening &amp; clean up yard &amp; act as executioner of the Ducks. Look out old books &amp; photos. 
 Ring Mary &amp; Mrs Hoper 529K or to apply my job. I go to Dobbers, forget shirt, get dressed in dark mum's overcoat &amp; forget mungaree. Play cards &amp; win easily.
