<p>they reckon it's the best Mess on the Plains &amp; I believe it for it is really first class.  There is a draft going way to France from here tonight.</p>
<p>14th Had us out on an early morning parade doing platoon &amp; Company drill, after this breakfast &amp; an interview with Capt. Foster about our future work &amp; when we start.  I think we will go on General Instruction work.  Hot &amp; muggy again today wants a good fall of rain to clear the atmosphere.</p>
