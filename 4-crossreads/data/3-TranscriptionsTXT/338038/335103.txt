Walking through the township in the evenings on sees all the villagers sitting outside their cottages at little tables, drinking the wine of the island, a sweet, sickly sort of mixture and very intoxicating. The industries are winegrowing and fishing. The Greeks there are past masters in the cooking of fish. We had tea ashore one evening at a little caf&eacute; place where they gave us some fried fish - the finest I have ever tasted. Well Mother, I think I have come to about the end of my news, such as it is. I do hope some letters from home will come along shortly as the time would not seem half as long with some news of how you all are etc. With much love to yourself, Father &amp; Pat I am Your loving Son Terence
