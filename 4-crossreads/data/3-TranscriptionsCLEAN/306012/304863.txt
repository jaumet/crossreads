Rome appeared to be full of officers clad in smart "avant guerre" uniforms & wearing many decorations.  Saluting is carried out very strictly, 2nd Lieuts. saluting Lieutenants, Lieutenants Captains & so on.The Italian language much resembles French and would, I feel sure, be very easy to pick up if one were living in the country for a short space of time.  I managed to get hold of a few words and could catch fragments of conversation.  French is spoken a great deal and is extremely useful in hotels, restaurants, shops & theatres as well as in social circles.
