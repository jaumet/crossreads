<p>along the wet pitch dark road I heard an officer reading out orders <strike>for</strike> to a draft of men waiting to march to the train to proceed to the front.  He was impressing on them the <strike>list</strike> various punishments for breaches of discipline on the journey.  Poor chaps.  But this is war.</p>
<p>Rouen 18th Dec.</p>
<p>I arrived here this morning about 6.30 A.M. in company with a big draft of other</p>
