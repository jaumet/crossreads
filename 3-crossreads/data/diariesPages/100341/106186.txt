May 15th
Woke up to find it very dark and rainy windy sea about same as 14th. Running on deck other physical drill till breakfast. Sausages Porridge etc. passed sailingship on starboard side about mile off same time going opposite direction.
9 a.m. Parade Musketry between showers. sea rising. Dinner stewed rabit vegetables. To wet rough for drill afternoon. wind sea high by tea.-time. all temporary structure on fore deck lashed down for weather. music after tea. Bed 7.30 pm.
