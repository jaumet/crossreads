
May 1918Wednesday 29
Battery W.Lines.Weather beautiful, very warm, had the usual dip in lake avec Ernest. Walked across to old billet and stayed with the boys.Purchased a dozen eggs at 4d each, broke nine as the result of a fall from a bicycle. Received four parcels. Of small dimensions. One from O.J. one from Mary, encore from L.O.L. and again from Miss Hawker. No bombs tonight; noticed a heavy fall on Amiens
