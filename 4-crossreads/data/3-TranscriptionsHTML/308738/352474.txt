<p>July 6 Tuesday 1915<br/>Spoke to Dr re getting light work somewhere. He said I was not well enough yet<br/>Went to Cairo &amp; Maadi saw Victor, went &amp; saw Capt Obrien<br/>Returned to Hospital 8. 30 PM<br/>Rode from Bab el Douk in Ambulance waggon</p>
<p>July 7 Wednesday 1915<br/>Lying quiet in bed today feeling unwell.</p>
<p>July 8 Thursday 1915<br/>Feeling better today went into Cairo saw Staff Officer re position in Post Office. Was booked to start work on Saturday morning<br/>Sent note to Major C. D. Fuller by W. J. Wecker A. M. C. who is leaving for Dardinelles tomorrow</p>
<p>July 9 Friday 1915<br/>Left hospital at 3. 30 PM after getting my clothes, &amp; discharge. Have arranged to go for a few days to Post Office to assist with work there. Have to report myself to Luna Park Hospital once a week.</p>
