 APRIL 1917 
 23 MONDAY 
 A beautiful warm sunny day. 
 I was at a gas lecture in 
 morning &amp; we had a half 
 holiday in afternoon. Bill 
 Hunter &amp; self went up a hill 
 near by &amp; lay in the sun 
 all afternoon yarning &amp; 
 writing letters. We had a 
 beautiful view of the country 
 &amp; it looks gloriously pretty. 
 Campbell recomended me 
 for S t &nbsp;John&#39;s Wood Officers 
 school &amp; filled in particulars 
 for same Had no more 
 word about Alan.
