<p>Sunday 7 January 1917 [1918]<br/>Day set apart for prayers for peace &amp; thanksgiving for mercies already received.  &frac12; day &amp; went to 14 A.G.H. first then to 70th G.H. &amp; Heliopolis for 4 p.m. tea &ndash; then Cairo &amp; tried shopping but no decent places open.  Club for letter writing then "All Saints" &amp; a crowded church with a good sermon &amp; hymns.</p>
