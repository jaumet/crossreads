<p>Mother has not to use her own birthday money.</p>
<p>Pleased she liked the present.</p>
<p>How awfully sad about Miss Cameron's death. Poor Mrs Trangmar   &amp;  Ada will find it very lonely. Am writing a few lines to Mrs Trangmar by this mail.</p>
<p>My special pal (man) wrote such a sad letter this week telling me of his brother's death &ndash; was killed in action. The dreadful thing is that he got him transfered.</p>
