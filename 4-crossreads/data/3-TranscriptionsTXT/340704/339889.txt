 December 1917 Tuesday 25 Xmas day  wet and cold Had good Xmas dinner. Only on e in our troop to have a fire. These olive trees burn well. Has the only wood here &ndash; so most have fires. 
 Wednesday 26 Fine day in patches. Dried some clothes. Moved out at 8 pm to attack at daybreak. Awful journey rough. Very cold on outpost at night.
