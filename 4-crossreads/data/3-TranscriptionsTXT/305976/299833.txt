
Thursday Feb 1sailing postponed on account of Raider out. Troops from all states banking up in Seymour.Friday Feb 2Weekly route march, across fields to Goulbourn River, across Railway Line &  back through old Seymour Town. Route marches very enjoyable.Saturday Feb 3On duty, off in afternoon. Went fishing to Goulbourn River with some of the Boys. Change from CampSunday Feb 4Washing day (nuff said)Monday Feb 5Waggon Drill & LectureTuesday Feb 6Signalling, Evening wrote letters
