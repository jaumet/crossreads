 1st A.G.H.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Egypt.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              June 18th  1916. My dear Home Folk., We are still in Egypt &amp; feel very disgusted, were told we were to leave last Friday &ndash; then for  certain Tuesday &amp; Wednesday next &ndash; but now may not move till next month.
