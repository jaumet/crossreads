 1917 June 
 14 Thursday 
 Waited &amp; cooked at Minerva &amp; at 4.30 was inoculated at St.Mary's Paddington &amp; went to Miss Hodge &amp; went to bed. Ned.Raine there. Lovely warm day. Miss Hodge gave me her bed &amp; took a room out. The Dr inoculating me had lost both legs in the war.
