<p>9.<br/>and getting your dungarees on. I should expect that it would mean a commission. He will be lucky if he sees any fighting.</p>
<p>I see that Sid Mack has taken silk and trust that he will remember me sometimes when asked to commit a breach of etiquette by mentioning his preference in juniors. I am somewhat surprised that Broomfield &amp; Kelynock have not had silk before now. It is to be hoped that Lingen will not suffer financially by his reckless precocity, but I assume that he has ambitions directed towards the S.C. Bench.</p>
