<p>April 1918<br/>Thursday 25</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours.    D.R.<br/>Had a tour to Bonnay after inspection, - received new gas bag.<br/>1200 Fritz's captured at Villers Brettonvibne. Some fine big fellows amongst them also some youngsters very heavy fighting for village of Villers Brettonvibne.<br/>Fifteenth Bdge did the damage.<br/>Rode up to W.L. at Franvillers and had a yap with boys. James knocked in thigh.  Fleming, Bill Aldred, Davies and three others killed.<br/>Weather mists.</p>
