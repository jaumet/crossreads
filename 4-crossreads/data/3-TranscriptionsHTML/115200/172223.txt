<p>trench I jumped in and asked him for the makeings of a cigarette wich he gave me. I had Been smoking it a few minutes when Crash. I got one in the left arm in a line with the heart. the Bullet remained in my arm. But I could just see the end of it - So i pulled it out and Droped it in the Trench. I left then and went to get my arm Dressed at the Dressing Station&nbsp; They sent me from there to the Beach where we were put on lighters and sent to the Hospital Ship Gascon.
