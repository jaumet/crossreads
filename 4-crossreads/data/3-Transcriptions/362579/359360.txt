<p>Belgium<br/>4.8.17</p>
<p>Hope you are all well. In Haste</p>
<p>My dearest Moth   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>I've done some travelling this week, came by car all the way from last C.C.S. on Thursday &ndash; Jolly glad to be with our own Unit again. We are twice as many sisters   &amp;  ever so many more Medical Officers. But it has rained so   &amp;  this place so flat that we cannot get on with the camp too quickly, consequently have to await for orders to open. There has been a good deal of fighting about</p>
