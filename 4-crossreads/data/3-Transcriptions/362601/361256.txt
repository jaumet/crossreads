<p>24 August, Sunday</p>
<p>O go out to Cock's for dinner &amp; tea. We go looking for land at North Harbour &amp; rate several new houses. Cocky that sneezes. I go on South Steyne beach for first time music &amp; chatter all the time.</p>
<p>25 August, Monday</p>
<p>Painter at shop &amp; is a nuisance. Meet Les Merrett &amp; go Home.</p>
