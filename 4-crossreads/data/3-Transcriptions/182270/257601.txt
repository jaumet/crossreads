
<p>THURSDAY, OCTOBER 16, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; S. Wind. Lopping in Mountain</p>
<p>in morning and ringing in the</p>
<p>Middle in afternoon.</p>
<p>Darby down 140 ft in the new</p>
<p>bore and has got rotten rock &amp; clay</p>
<p>all the way. Tickle drawing</p>
<p>water to him. The 200 ft of casing</p>
<p>came along &amp; was dropped at</p>
<p>Tickle&#39;s.&nbsp;</p>
<p>I got my new sheep dog. &quot;Wallace&quot;</p>
<p>from Tickle&#39;s &amp; brought him home</p>
<p>Dad came back in the mail car.</p>
