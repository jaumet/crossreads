<p>Saturday 14 April</p>
<p>Glorious day.  All leave to bearers from this camp has been stopped thanks to two men getting drunk in <strike>Armiens</strike> Amiens yesterday.  They were run in by the Military Police.<br/>On incinerator.<br/>Excitement caused by news of the Arras Offensive.  13,000 prisoners reported in papers.<br/>Our little hut it a picture of comfort.  The fire seldom goes out.<br/>Route march this morning before breakfast, as yesterday.</p>
