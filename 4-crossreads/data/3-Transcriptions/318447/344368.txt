<p>I rise at 6.30 Groom &amp; feed my pony breakfast at 8 at 10 the horses have to be watered &amp; after that besides drawing the horsefeed nothing is done till Lunchtime at 12 when we feed the horses &amp; ourselves again</p>
<p>After lunch I have a pipe &amp; then usually go out for a ride &ndash; there are one or two quite pretty corners around here &ndash; at 4 oclock we water feed etc again tea at 5. After tea we usually make a fire &amp; sit around it until about 8 or 9 &amp; Then I retire to my home &amp;</p>
