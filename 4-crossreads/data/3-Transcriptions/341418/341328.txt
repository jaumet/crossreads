<p>addition to a few of my usual bedfellows, to wit, fleas, I had another friend. I woke up in the morning with a large tick on the back of my  <strike>right</strike>  neck. Truly this is a charming country. It must have been very unpleasant here during the plagues, that is all I can say!<br/>April 19th<br/>Last Sunday we were stung again "Stinging" by the way is the vernacular for innoculation. On Monday we were all looking at the world  &amp;  the horse lines in particular with a very jaundiced eye.</p>
