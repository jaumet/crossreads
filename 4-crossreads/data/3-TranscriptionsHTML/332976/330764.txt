<p>8th continued<br/>  blankets &amp; gave me a slight scratch on the left hand, but I fixed it up with a piece of sticking plaster &amp; have said nothing about it.<br/>9th   I have had influenza for the last two days &amp; have been "off duty" to-day.  I got a slight "shell shock" last night &amp; have not been feeling too "fit."  Grace got it bad &amp; was sent off to hospital with the others.  Fritz has been sending over a few more of his H.Es. to-night &amp; has got some of the pioneers who are about 100 yds. behind us.</p>
