It is surprising how near the battle area some of the civilians still remain.  Every night without fail the enemy comes over, as soon as the sun goes down, bombing our back areas indiscriminately and many of the civilian homes have been destroyed, and many of the people wounded.  Some of course will cling to their old homes until they are absolutely driven from them, while others have actually moved up closer into the active areas, to open little ramshackle shanties bearing the magic sign "Beer".  The commercial instinct appears to be very highly developed in these people (as in the French).
