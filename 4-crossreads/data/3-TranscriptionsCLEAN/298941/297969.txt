
Saturday 13th April 1918My teeth have been are supposed to have been fixed up, & so any day may find me off. As a matter of fact Walter & I were both put on draft a couple of days ago, & after we had been paid on the strength of it, the discovery was made that no 1st Division men were needed.Whilst waiting for the train at Boulogne, we saw the transport of the 1st Field Ambulance go by
