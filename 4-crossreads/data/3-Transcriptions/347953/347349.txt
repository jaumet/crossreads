<p>On Thursday 27th Sept 1914 I enrolled at Victoria Barracks, and marched from there to Sydney Station en route to Rosehill only to arrive there to find no accommodation. We went back home &amp; arrived back the next morning. The preliminary &amp; [indecipherable] [elementary?] became monotonous, the heat intolerable but the early dawn was quite cold.
