
Some of the Boys had falls. Day passed quietly, settling down to new quarters. Still anchoredSaturday May 12thSpent morning on Deck games, Weather very cold & boisterous. Dropped anchor about 5pm Passed through rip & heads of Pt Lonsdale & Nepean.Sunday May 13Very rough to day, too rough for me, "Seasick." Feel as if I do not care what happens.Monday May 14Feel a bit better to day, still rough & cold. What a night. Soldiers lying everywhere.Tuesday May 15thNot quite so bad today. Transports keeping close together escorted by Destroyers Brisbane & Melbourne. Boat Drill all
