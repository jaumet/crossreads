
F [15/4/17 Sun] Glorious day. Play for Kirk & go for walk to see Marjorie. Write to Mum and OS Merrett. Jess MatherRecd. letters from Edie Taubman. French English
W [16/4/17 Mon] Nasty rainy day. We go to the Pictures stand in the rain. Show very good. See good girl dance & Pipe player
F [17/4/17 Tues] King George Review. Ellis goes over to see it. Recd. letter from Cyl. Wrote to H. Hey & Cyl. Bill H. & I walk to Stonehenge & go to Pictures at Amesbury.
F & Wet [18/4/17 Wed] Recd. letter from Jennie & letter & Parcel from Mrs Trotter. All inoculated again Ellis goes to Harefield. Dave & I go to Palladium. Rotten Show. Wrote to Mrs Trotter.
F & very warm [19/4/17 Thurs] Very sick with Inoculation. Go to bed very early. Wrote to Marge. Bess. Doris. Maisie. Dave & Frank's cobber comeWrote to Jennie.
F & HOT [20/4/17 Fri] recd. comforts from Mrs Stephens & New Hat from Q.M I play cricket & get very hot. Have shower apres & go straight to Bed
Dull [21/4/17 Sat] Nothing doing. E & I go for walk round Block. I am transferred to No4 Spitoon in lieu of No2 apres 17 months Lt Col. Grigor in lieu of major. My arm is very sore after playing cricket.
