 28-2-1917 Fine day, Guns gone forward, in action at a place called Seven Elms.  Enemy still retreating, sniping our men with machine guns, loosing a lot of men.  This is the second night our men has been out.  Had a terrible time last night, only got one gun out of action, one fell into a shell hole.  Having a hell of a time getting teams and packs out, day and night at it.  Mud over your boot, and feet sore.  1&frac12; hours to turn out two 8 horse teams.
