
August, 1916Monday 7Made good time all dayMaking in direction of Marseilles, so our destination is now certain
Tuesday 8In sight of the harbour about 2.30 pm.Pulled into wharf by tug at 5 pm.First to disembark when all were off we marched behind the band to rest camp about 2 miles from the town.got into tents about 10 pm damned tired
