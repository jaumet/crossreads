
24.2.15
Wed 24thWas with C Sq to-day, we were out with the troops & they were trench digging, while we laid down in the shade of our horses & watched them.  it was very warm during the middle of the day & it was rather late when we arrived back in camp in fact it was too late to get paid,  wrote home after tea & told plenty of lies amongst ourselves
Thur 25thHad a very easy day, went out to the pumping station practising signalling with the HQ signallers & returned to camp as soon as we
