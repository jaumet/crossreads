 Monday 5&nbsp;April 1915 A severe dust storm today. The wind began about 2 a.m and continued till about 6 p.m.&nbsp; In Cairo for an hour. Spoke with General Williams at Shepheards hotel&nbsp; Writing &amp; reading all day. 
 Tuesday 6&nbsp;April 1915 Acting O.C. today Colonel Martin away.&nbsp; Letters from Car, Joe &amp; Kitty. 
 Wednesday 7&nbsp;April 1915 Acting O.C. today Colonel Martin at Alexandria&nbsp; Letters from Mrs Fraser of New Port. Isle of Wight.
