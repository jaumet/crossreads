<p>Wednesday 10 April 1918<br/>Hun Aircraft Patrol.  Pilot Eustace Headlam, in air 2 h. 55 m.<br/>We met no Huns.<br/>An R.E. 8 was shot down by a Hun today.<br/>Our first success for many weeks.</p>
<p>Thursday 11 April 1918<br/>Hun Aircraft Patrol.  Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 2 h. 50 m.<br/>No excitement, reported new camp of 225 T.S. &amp; 3 H.L.</p>
