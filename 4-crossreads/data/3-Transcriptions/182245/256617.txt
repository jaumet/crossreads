
<p>339</p>
<p>1917 APRIL</p>
<p>12 THURSDAY</p>
<p>Fine, but a very cold wind</p>
<p>blowing all day. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness all day. Lieut</p>
<p>Hamilton made an inspection</p>
<p>today, &amp; he said our section</p>
<p>was the best. We had a bit</p>
<p>of a &quot;500&quot; tournament at</p>
<p>night, 2 francs per pair entrance.</p>
<p>Les McCarthy &amp; I were second</p>
