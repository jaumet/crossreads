The Bn. came in about 10 p.m and after seeing them all O.K. reported to the C.O. and crawled into a hole and had a sleep or rather I tried to.  The sky had cleared by this time and the night turned out to be one of the coldest I have ever experienced.
29thBreakfast was issued at 5 A.M. and all ranks prepared to move by 9 oclock.  Information has been received from the front line to the effect that the huns continue to retire.
