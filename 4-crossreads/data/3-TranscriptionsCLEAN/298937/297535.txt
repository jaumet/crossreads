That of course led to words & when he threatened to report me to the guard room, I invited him to do so by all means. This was not very good for my friend, but it was not to be avoided & I certainly gave the officious customer my opinion of him. The following morning I expected to be paraded at Orderly Room, but I was soon informed that it had been hushed up ostensibly on my account.
