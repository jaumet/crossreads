 8th Oct. &ndash; Sunday Raining &amp; cold, but fine afternoon.  Church at St. Sever at 8 a.m.  After work at 12-30, start on a most interesting tour.  Meet our gallant young Belgian friend &amp; take a tram to Maromme.  Here a fine monument to the French soldiers of 1870-1.
