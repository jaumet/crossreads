Sylvan FellsTurramurra
26th March
Dear Miss Small
I have ready my contribution for the book.  It is rather a large page to send through the post.  Will you be good enough to call or send for it, to Mr. Smith's office, it will be there on Tuesday next &ndash;
Messers MacNamara &amp; Smith15 Belmont BuildingsCastlereagh Street
(Next to Brook's the printer.)
Wishing you every success with the book,
Yours sincerely
G. Cossington-Smith
Please ask for Mr. Ernest Smith.
