<p>night of 14th &ndash; 15th May when the Germans attacked our portion of the Hindenburg Line (just to the right of Bullecourt and facing Reincourtz and <strike>when</strike> were repulsed by our men with very heavy losses.  Last night therefore was a bumper night and those of the recipients who are with us now were congratulated and honoured in the approved manner.</p>
<p>Warloy-Baillon 25/6/17<br/>Four other officers and myself promoted to Lieut. In last night's orders.</p>
