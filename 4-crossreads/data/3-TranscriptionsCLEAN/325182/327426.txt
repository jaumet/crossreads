
I cannot speak too highly of the kindness which I have received at the hands of Mrs. Abel Smith and her family.
7thLeft Cole Orton Hall and reached St. Pancras Station about 10.15 p.m.  Went to the Club in Piccadilly.
8thReceived my move order from Hqrs. and went down to Warminster to the officers training Battalion where I am now installed.
9thProceeded with a draft over France as Conducting Officer.
10thLeft Bologne Folkestone with the draft and arrived at Bologne about 12 noon after about 2 hours across the Channel.  Marched to a rest camp above the town where we stayed
