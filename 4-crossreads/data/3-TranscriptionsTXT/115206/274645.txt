 March 1918 Saturday 16 
 Messines 2 am. Fritz still dropping them on road. Another glorious day, weather simply beautiful. Received letters from Roy, Mum, Miss Carmichael and "Toots L."  Paid by Redmond at 5 pm. Went across to 53rd and delivered pigeon book. To- Rhodes. Had a yarn to him also Tommy Barr. 9.10 pm. Fritz plastered roads and put a few gas shells over, sneezing gas. Ivan on O.P. all day, came in at 7pm. Russell on l'aison.
