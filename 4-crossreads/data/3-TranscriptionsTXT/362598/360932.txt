 Sunday 2 July 1916 Wrote Home (49).  Spent a quiet evening outside our tent listening to Phonograph.  Night Matron again falls to my lot.  Hooted out of Bed at 10.45 p.m. to go to Billy's Ward. 
 Monday 3 July 1916 Ward Master all day.  Wrote Cards to Mem, Edie Tory, Ada Cohen, I. Westaway, Jess Mather. 
 Tuesday 4 July 1916 Bill, Dave &amp; I go to Cairo for a run.  Visit all the Popular places. 
 Wednesday 5 July 1916 Ellis, Bill &amp; I go to Heliopolis to meet Dick Rowe.  Also meet McKenzie, Dinkum Soldier McKenzie.  All Patients come inside the Ward.  A kid of 5 talks French &amp; English with equal ease. 
 Thursday 6 July 1916 Everything as usual, had a very lazy day.  Lay in bed at evening &amp; thought of Home &amp; Friends. 
 Friday 7 July 1916 Late dinner day.  Wrote Harry Taubman &amp; Edie Taubman.  Tried hard to rain.  Staff gives a concert in Y.M.C.A. Hut.  Very hot day &amp; Earn malingers. 
 Saturday 8 July 1916 The 4 Jews go out to Y.M.C.A.  I met Roy Ingram.  Ellis wins 3 games Billiards.  Dave &amp; I beat Billy &amp; Ellis 105 to 50.  We dine at Y.M.C.A. &amp; have a sing-song with McKenzie from Manly.  Went to Anzac Hotel for Tea &amp; concert afterwards.
