At 9.30 am a pigeon was liberated from off the Boat Deck, where it was to go to was hard to say. At 9.45 am the General Alarm was sounded & all hands had to parade with Life Belts on at the Respective Stations, it was carried out in quick time. At 4 Pm our Ship altered her Course & steamed away from her position for Gun Practice.
