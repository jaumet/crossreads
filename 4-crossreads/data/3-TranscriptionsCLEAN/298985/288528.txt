Sunday 11thSome bombs & shells were dropped about last night. None close to us Had dinner & moved of at 1.20. It was very hot & the road thick with dust & there was a good deal of traffic to stir it up. Flat uninteresting country. The fighting was here about 4 days ago Passed a few dead horses only noticed one dead man. The losses are said to have been light about here. Marched
