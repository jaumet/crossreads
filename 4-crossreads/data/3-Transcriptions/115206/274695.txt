<p>May 1918<br/>Sunday 5</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daours<br/>Weather clear, but very muggy.<br/>Cleaned up all my gear for saddle and my equipment.<br/>Boys are all very well and Ginger looks a little better. Paraded to M.O. for his inspection and had his legs dressed.<br/>Paraded before Muller in evening and he dished out the usual yap.<br/>Received a letter from Cousin and certainly liked the news</p>
