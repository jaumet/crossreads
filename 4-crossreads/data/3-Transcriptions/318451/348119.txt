<p>&amp; on the road I passed about 20 great guns drawn by tractors with caterpillar wheels, 6 in., 9 in., 12 in. &amp; field guns.  Great huge things which will soon speak out death &amp; destruction in the new offensive.<br/>New Railways cross the road &amp; run away up towards the front line.  Trains are emptying their shells, great huge things from morning till night &amp; stacks upon stacks of ammunition are piling up.  Hundreds of Motor Lorries following at intervals of a few yards are going night &amp; day loaded with shells to the dumps behind the lines.  Aeroplanes like flocks of birds patrol all day &amp; some at night.  Troops marching in by thousands.  Buildings going up like mushrooms &amp; thousands of horses &amp; mules in the lines.  It will be a tremendous thrust.  A Col. told me 25&percnt; more</p>
