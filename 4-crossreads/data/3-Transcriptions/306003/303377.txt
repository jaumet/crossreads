<p>heard the wireless had been put out of action &amp; our casualties were officers killed 1, wounded 3, other ranks killed 7, wounded 30, horses killed 37;  Some of the horses that got away returned soon after breakfast &amp; some men were sent out to wheel them back again;  The bomb dropping was the best I have ever seen for accuracy &amp; was from a great height, every bomb exploded but they sank well into the soft sand before exploding which affected their destructive power a lot as there were hardly any splinters flying &amp; anyone ten yards from where the bomb dropped were pretty safe;</p>
