 26 February 1915 Friday 
 More rain last night Everybody sleeping on deck come down about midnight Weather continues cool with light wind 
 1915 February 27 Saturday 
 A lot of potatoes   &amp;  onions stowed in the Aft hold right under our quarters begin to rot  &amp;  stink (horrid.) A gang of men are put on hauling them out   &amp;  dumping them overboard. The work is very slow   &amp;  dirty.
