<p>Mar 18th  Hyde Park  Buckingham Palace &amp; Rotten Row &amp; Richmond in afternoon  Glorious on River in evening<br/>Mar 19th.  Leave for Folkestone by Pullman (Dining) &amp; after a rough crossing get back to France again.  Rest Billet for night at Bolougne.<br/>Mar 20th  At Bolougne in rest billet.</p>
