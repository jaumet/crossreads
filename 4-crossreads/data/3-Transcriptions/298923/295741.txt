<p>16 March 1918 Saturday<br/>Left Wadi <strike>Auja</strike> &amp; marched in the rain to <strike>Rummon</strike> drew rations &amp; on in the dark to Beitin 4 horses died of cold &amp; the infantry plodded along in the mud.<br/>We could light no fires &amp; had no tucker since yesterday mornings breakfast.  8 Nigger camel drivers died with the cold &amp; wet.  They cannot stand the cold like our men.</p>
