<p>Sunday 6 January 18<br/>Went to Church &amp; a walk in afternoon when rain started first for some time. Have received 18 Australian letters this week.</p>
<p>Monday 7<br/>Marking at Butts again. Hall won the supper</p>
<p>Tuesday 8<br/>Butts again another free feed for Hall  A Rought <strike>Wednesday 9</strike> march at night about 5 miles</p>
<p>Wednesday 9<br/>Finished marking today Drill in afternoon A cold day. Have brought a pair of rough leather gloves big enough to pull over the woollen ones they are just the thing. Have found them most useful on the morning job</p>
<p>Thursday 10<br/>Bull ring same old stunts</p>
<p>Friday 11<br/>Got on to wire entanglement &amp; knotting ropes <strike>tod</strike> It is really the first instruction I have had in that line altthough it is supposed to be our special job</p>
<p>Saturday 12 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Rought March about 12 miles</p>
