  
 401 my eye got very inflamed so they stopped putting the stuff in it and it is just being bathed with boracic - it is quite clean. The specimen of my tears sent to London to be analysed showed no signs of any foreign germs so it is all ready for the operation - I hope I get it all over before you come back and am quite respectable to look at - I got the letter from Grandmere you sent on - I'll write to her - I have written Aunt Isabel and Aunt Maria - The Australian mail goes on Friday and I hope to write by it - I was amused with Dowell O'Reilly's letter and return it to you - He revives scenes of the past in a most vivid manner - I wonder if we'll ever again all be together at the farm ! We'd have a jolly time wouldn't we ! We'd hold a memorial service to old "Charlie: - 
 My love to aunt and Uncle and Cousin Lea - and kind regards to the Railtons - Hoping you are all as happy as can be Fondest love to you two selves from Ever your devoted brother Gib
