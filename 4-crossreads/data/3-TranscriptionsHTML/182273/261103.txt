<p>1918 &nbsp;&nbsp;February</p>
<p>14 THUR &nbsp;Rec. letter from Else Parker</p>
<p>Dull day. Went to</p>
<p>Au Progr&egrave;s after tea, had</p>
<p>a good time. Heavy firing</p>
<p>at night.</p>
<p>15 FRI &nbsp;Fine day. home 6 am.</p>
<p>Bath parade. Football in aft.</p>
<p>Writing at night.</p>
<p>16 SAT &nbsp;Fine day Posted letters</p>
<p>to Amy (&amp; Tom &amp; Maggie).</p>
<p>Football in aft.</p>
