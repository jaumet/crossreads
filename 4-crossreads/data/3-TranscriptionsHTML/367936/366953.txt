<p>Saturday 25 March 1916<br/>I took old ewes over to Wonga then went and saw Jim Oakley.<br/>Father &amp; Dick working at fence round Stack.<br/>Made rabbit traps in afternoon.  Helped Father shift studd ewe lambs.</p>
<p>Sunday 26 March 1916<br/>Fed 12 horses while Father milked.  Father and I mustered Front pdk. into Cliff and brought Studds into 18 acre, and put young rams on lucerne.  Jim Large came down and got his ewe.<br/>Eddie Blackman came over &amp; shot his dog "Spider".<br/>Mother, Father &amp; I burning off up gully.</p>
