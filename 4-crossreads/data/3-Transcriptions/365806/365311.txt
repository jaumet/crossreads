<p>Since being in France I have seen only four broken down lorries and <strike>they</strike> two of them were due to the side of the road falling away &amp; letting them down.  They really do wonderful work.</p>
<p>This is a splendid Canteen (British) in this town.  There is a room set apart <strike>from</strike> for officers where one can get a very nice meal &amp; sit around a real coal fire.  It is very enjoyable to get in there &amp; have a yarn.   My present billet is a vile place but is at least dry &amp; fairly warm.</p>
<p>It is very hard to make a letter anything like interesting. I seem to have exhausted all the topics which do not offend censorship Regs.</p>
<p>Alroy Cohen is in hospital with influenza.</p>
<p>Pitt is quite well &amp; is now my 2nd-in-command.  I think I told you he got a Military Cross &amp; that I got a Mention in Dispatches for a Raiding job.</p>
<p>Best of love to you all,<br/>Yours affectionately<br/>Caesar</p>
