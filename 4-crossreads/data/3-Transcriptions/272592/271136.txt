<p>They are now issuing daily rum to the troops &amp; how the boys like it.</p>
<p>I saw to-day in a French paper that when we took Pozeries the French cheered along the whole of their lines from one end to the other thats something for Australia.</p>
<p>22nd.   We are holding our Sports this afternoon, &amp; for a wonder the sun is shining brightly &amp; things are drying fast, all those with the exception of the rifle teams marched down with their belts on</p>
<p>I am now penning this laying on the green grass, &amp; at the present time I am watching the rifle squads in the competition drill A. &amp; B. Coy have just gone off</p>
