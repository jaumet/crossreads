<p>I sent a man down to <strike>my</strike> where I had left my piquet to make sure of their being recalled, got in a rickshaw &amp; went down to the wharf. I then jumped into a rowing boat with some of our N.C.Os &amp; away we went for the Derbyshire with the tug pulling along her ungainly barges full of our chaps just in rear of us.
