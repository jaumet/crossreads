  
 3.A.G.H.A.I.F. Abbassieh barracks, Cairo St Patrick's day. 17.3.16. 
 My beloved Mother & Father I send you this letter by Sister Dowe. She has been recalled, so I expect is wanted at P.A.H. She will give you all latest news of me - and our life here. I shall miss her very much. I enclose a group of "some" of the R P.A.H. sisters & nurses in the army nursing service - out here - taken at our quarters here. I hope the &pound 20 arrived safely. I am very busy now. I have a very large ward with 80 beds. Plenty of room in the ward and all very comfortable. I am working for a Major Lochardt Gibson of Brisbane - a specialist oculist and Captain Anderson of Melbourne Ear nose & throat cases. I like my work - and have a great deal to do.
