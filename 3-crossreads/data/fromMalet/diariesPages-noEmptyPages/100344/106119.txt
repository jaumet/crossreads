11th. Monday ? Sept ? 
Day rather dull but not bad. Letter from Mrs Walters day quiet easily the quietest front we have yet been on. very thankful too after Pozieres hope we stay its some home.
12th. Tuesday. ? Sept ?
Dull day with showers. little more shelling today only one on the trench not casualties slight damage to parapet.fixed up later after dark. 
16th month away "Ausy"
