<p>Sunday 26 May 1918</p>
<p>Monday 27 May 1918<br/>Protested about the food they are giving us here.  Worst hospital I have ever been in for grub.  Intend to have all my meals outside in future.</p>
<p>Tuesday 28 May to Saturday 1 June 1918<br/>[No comments recorded on these days.]</p>
