<p>July 19th<br/>Birthday still in trenches very quiet.<br/>July 20th<br/>Same as usual doing nothing<br/>July 21st<br/>Nothing during day nightime short water fatigue C Coy raided by English And Australians one killed our side six on theirs several casualities  - our trench mistaken for enemy.<br/>July 22nd<br/>Nothing during day nightime went up to front line then on patrol &ndash;<br/>July 23rd<br/>Ten o'clock A.M. raid by Yanks and Tommies in daylight stood too for it. Nightime on listeningson</p>
