
Sunday 28 January 1917Took horses down to "Kilo 155" for reinforcements but missed them.
Monday 29 January 1917No. 1 Sectn. on shooting practice firing a belt of ammunition.
Tuesday 30 January 1917On a muster parade to 155 for "Delousing", pretty chilly while clothes were being fumigated.
Wednesday 31 January 1917Foggy in morning.  Two Taubes over:  dropped several bombs.
Thursday 1 February 1917Quiet day:  saw Guth & got my disk made.  Outpost at night.
Friday 2 February 1917Windy, dusty day.
Saturday 3 February 1917Found a Turko mine on the coast, pulled it in & put a guard round it.
