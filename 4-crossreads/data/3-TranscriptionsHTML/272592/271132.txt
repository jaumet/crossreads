<p>Just here on the edge of the moat theres lies an enomorous bell taken by the Belgians from the Catherdal &amp; placed here for some reason or other, the bell is engraved all over &amp; looks fine</p>
<p>After a while we left &amp; struck out for Woodcote House here we picked up our guides &amp; they led us to the 6th Batt Hqrs, we had to wade through slush &amp; mud almost to one's knees &amp; the duckboard's having no netting</p>
