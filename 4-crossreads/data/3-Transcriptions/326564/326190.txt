<p>were set &amp; they soon had a great plate of eggs &amp; bread &amp; butter in front of us also plenty of beer &amp; etc, they could speak very good English &amp; we could easily see that these people were a far better class than we usually come in contact with, I quite enjoyed being amongst them &amp; when we left we came away with a big loaf of bread for use later on, but we wanted some more so we walked down to an estaminet &amp; induced the girl there to sell us a loaf of bread, we pitched her a pitiful tale &amp; it succedded admirably, you wouldn't believe what a job it is to get bread now for all the people are on rations &amp; though they get plenty for themselves they are not supposed to sell it   bread is always our chief trouble for we get plenty of other tucker</p>
