  
 23rd Nov:- Quiet day in Hospital  Two hospital trains arrive, bringing one hundred stretcher cases (wounded) & the hospital is full. Seen by Capt Kay (x-ray) who expresses the opinion from x-ray picture that my shoulder was broken &  not properly set. 
 24th Nov:- Very nice & cool day. Doctor reports on shoulder as opinion of last night. Go out for a motor drive this afternoon round Mataria &  Hellmich to the residence of an English gentleman who is in a high position in Cairo.
