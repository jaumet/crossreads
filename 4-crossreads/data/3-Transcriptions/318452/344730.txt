<p>was driven through a hose, with the oil &amp; petrol, &amp; ignited at the nozzle. This liquid flame shot out about 70 feet a terrific sheet of awful flame most terrifying but less effective. It was a great sight.<br/>On Thursday the King passed along the road &amp; our troops lined up to cheer him. At night we had a concert &amp; it was a great success The General was in to mess &amp; came to the concert.<br/>I visited the Hospital where there were some men of our Batt. who had met with an accident with a bomb.<br/>On Friday morning the Batt. moved out from Buire to a camp about 9 miles &amp; we are billeted in Huts. Last night it rained heavily &amp; came through the roof pretty freely, swamping us all out</p>
