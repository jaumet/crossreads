<p>DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS</p>
<p>14 &ndash; Remilly &ndash; Marched to Remilly transport to Staples.</p>
<p>15 &ndash; Wizernes &ndash; Marched to Wizernes entrained to Lindehoek Transport to Bleu.</p>
<p>16 &ndash; Line Messines &ndash; Releived 31st Batt.  Quiet Sector.  Transport arrived 12.00 T.L. Neuve Eglise.</p>
<p>24/25 &ndash; Wulverghem Camp &ndash; Releived by 12th Bn. came out to Wulverghem.</p>
<p>25 - Chrismas day, very cold.</p>
<p>26/31 &ndash; Fatigue, very cold strength of unit 33 officers <strike>559</strike> other Rnks. 637.</p>
<p>1918 - <strike>Releived by 12th Bn.</strike></p>
<p>Jan. 1/2 - Line Messines &ndash; Releived Batt. in line in wings.  Strength with unit 33 officers 559 O. Rnks.</p>
<p>9/10 &ndash; Wulverghem &ndash; Releived by 12th Battalion.</p>
<p>16 &ndash; Rossignol Camp &ndash; Left Wulverghem for Rossignol camp, Kemmel.</p>
<p>16/22 &ndash; Working Parties.</p>
<p>23/24 &ndash; Wytschaete &ndash; Bn. went into line in front of Wytschaete, very quiet.</p>
<p>31/1 &ndash; Aldershot Cmp. &ndash; Bn. releived by 31st Bn.  Moved to Aldershot Camp strength 39 officers 741 other ranks.</p>
<p>Feb. 1 &ndash; Fatigues training T.L. Nueve Eglise.</p>
<p>24 &ndash; Alarm given, 20 minutes everything ready for action.</p>
<p>28 &ndash; Tournal Camp &ndash; Bn. moved to Tournal camp.  During month 10th Bn. won Bgde. football cup.</p>
