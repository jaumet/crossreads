<p>1917 MAY</p>
<p>24 THURSDAY</p>
<p>Fine. I went up to the battery</p>
<p>with a working party all day</p>
<p>&amp; was moving amunition</p>
<p>all day. Old Fritz sent a few</p>
<p>over &amp; we sent about ten</p>
<p>times the number back. I</p>
<p>walked home in evening.</p>
<p>I heard the big stunt was</p>
<p>starting a week from today</p>
<p>&amp; is to be the biggest of the war</p>
<p>&amp; there is to be 9000 guns under</p>
<p>the one G.O.C. The Tommies are</p>
<p>going over first then 4<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;Div</p>
<p>then 3<sup>rd</sup>&nbsp;Div. That is the</p>
<p>roumer &amp; lets see how true</p>
<p>it comes out.</p>
