<p>Monday 1 July 1918<br/>Recco Amman.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 2 h. 50 m.<br/>H.A.P.  Pilot Lt. Tonkin.  In air 2 h. 50 m., no excitement.<br/>We are getting a great deal of work now.  Find I can only stick a trip every other day &amp; remain good.</p>
<p>Tuesday 2 July 1918<br/>Escort bomb raid on [indecipherable].  Pilot Lt. Maughan.  In air 2 h. 30 m., bombing good.</p>
