 SENIOR OFFICERS' SCHOOL, MALPLAQUET BARRACKS, ALDERSHOT. 9. 7. 17. 
 My dear Judge. I am attending the above school which lasts ten weeks. I can well imagine the delight of my people on receiving my cable to that effect. I have seen Keith. We had Lunch Dinner &amp; a theatre together on Saturday. I was greatly relieved to see how well he was looking. Really the only trace of rough handling noticeable is that the pronunciation of a complicated word like "Buckingham Palace" causes him some difficulty.
