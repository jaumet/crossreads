<p>At Sea</p>
<p>Dear Mother</p>
<p>We have left our first port of call in Springbokland and are nearing our second. The weather bids fair to be calm round the Cape &amp; I hope it is. So far I'm feeling very well indeed.</p>
<p>Give my love to all</p>
<p>Your loving son</p>
<p>Dene</p>
<p>Mrs. Arthur Fry<br/> Northcote Rd<br/>Lindfield<br/>New South Wales<br/>Australia</p>
