<p>[Sunday 22 October 1916]</p>
<p>Went to <strike>Br</strike> White's Home at Accrington &amp; then on to another friends of Dollies named Lanton-<br/>Lunched at Midland Hotel a lovely place <strike>whe</strike> in Manchester where Dolly had her 1st Coming out dance.<br/>Met Sassoon there the great Bagdad Jent who used to lend money to the King.<br/>Mr Isherwood is now content for Dotty to come to Australia now that his wife has come back to him- it was <u>her</u> fault.</p>
