<p>13 Mar.  Still at sea  very calm ship going very slow  got paid disembarkation pay</p>
<p>14 Mar. Arrived Sydney  Wooloomooloo wharf 2.30 drove in cars to Anzac Buffet Domain. then in Taxi out to relatives at Drummoyne had great time.<br/>Finish.</p>
