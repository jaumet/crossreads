From Colombo to Fremantle we had good weather. What a difference it was coming home to what it was going.Going there was plenty of work & coming home we had practically none. Between Colombo & Fremantle we were inoculated against Influenza. We were fortunate to have the Flying Corp Concert party on board & they gave some good Concerts.
