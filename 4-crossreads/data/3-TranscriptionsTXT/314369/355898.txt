
2 October 1915Saturday
Get leave   &  go out to Zeitoun to see the old man.Clean   &  oil my pistol which is getting quite lonely it having never been   being  off my belt for 4 mthsHave tea w/- him  & return via Heliopolis
1915 October 3Sunday18th After Trinity
I have a restful morning  & go to churchDoctor thinks me fit to go to Alexandria so expect to leave here shortlyVery good concert this afternoon
