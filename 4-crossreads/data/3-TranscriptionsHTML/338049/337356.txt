<p>Sunday 26 May<br/>Grazing along canal bank morning.</p>
<p>Afternoon races on flat up the hill. Very successful day. Arrangements good. Bookies in great form. Waacs nurses brass hats in good numbers. Had Millie in. Ran 6th. No luck.</p>
<p>Evening reading.</p>
