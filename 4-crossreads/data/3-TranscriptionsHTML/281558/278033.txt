<p>B coy come 2nd : we S.B.s had an M.O. parade &amp; lecture : nothing doing in afternoon : wrote after tea</p>
<p>Saturday Sept 8th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 6.30 am : C coy went out to finish the competition against the picked coy's of our Bgde &amp; won comfortably : we went on bath parade in morn &amp; drill for boys in afternoon</p>
<p>Sunday Sept 9th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 7 am : Holy Com 9.30 am : Alf, Morrie &amp; self in St Omer after dinner : had a good time : the Museum is a good one : went to church in Lord R'b't's Home after tea &amp; it was tres bien : band in park also good : seen some Aust munition workers &amp; civvies seem out of place alright</p>
