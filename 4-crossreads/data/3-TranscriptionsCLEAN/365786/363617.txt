  
 No. 2 A.G.H. on the island of Gezireh in the Nile - another suburb, connected by 2 fine bridges & with a good electric tram service. Island has sporting Club, Palace Hotel, Public Gardens, Aquarium & homes of wealthy native & Europeans. Accommodation for 1000 patients. Will Senior Major, 60 men in 6 wards. People in Cairo provided concerts & comforts, papers etc. When men are wounded they are first attended to in the field by a Dr. if one is handy or by A.M.C. orderlies & hurried back to a Clearing Station or Field Ambulance.  These are like small base hospitals.  They are staffed by Drs. & orderlies
