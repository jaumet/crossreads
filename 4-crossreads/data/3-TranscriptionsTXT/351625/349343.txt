 Y.M.C.A. 
 France 27 Aug. 18 
 Dear Mum, 
 It is just about time for another letter to you so as I have a bit of time on my hands I will at least get a start on.  One of the boys is expecting to go on Blighty leave soon so I will most likely give it to him to post over there.  We have just had  a pretty  another pretty stiff week in the line.
