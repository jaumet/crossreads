<p>Diary - letters &amp; preparation for to-morrow&#39;s departure.</p>
<p><u>April 26th</u></p>
<p>Most reluctantly and with a sad heart I leave &quot;Elm Bank&quot; - and later in town I bid farewell to Mr Scales.</p>
<p>Take 10.15 a.m. train from Paddington to Warminster.&nbsp; Return to Sutton Veny Camp.</p>
<p>Report to Orderly Room &amp; take up new quarters.&nbsp; Blanket</p>
