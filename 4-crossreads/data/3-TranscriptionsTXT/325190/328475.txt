 Although I had been absent from the unit for nearly two years I was welcomed openly, &amp; gained great delight by the way they addressed me as the Bishop of London,  -  the name I had been known by at Gallipoli. Meanwhile we continue our lazy life in the camp. One gets up in time for breakfast, makes the fire in a can outside the tent; messes about &amp; gets very dirty &amp; then does a little fatigue.  Before dinner one reads &amp; washes, varying the monotony by chasing pigs under the hop poles.
