P.M. feeling very tired.<br/>Thursday may 3.5.17. stand to 6.AM  fed up and had breakfast had shave and wash taube over can hear heavy gun fire warm morning done some sewing had dinner 12rested all afternoon had two letters one from Josien.11. one from maud also S mail and two papers.
