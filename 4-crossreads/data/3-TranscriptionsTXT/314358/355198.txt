 25 Military life has become quite the accepted thing now 
 Everything goes with the smoothness (of a business) long established. 
 For us the moment came at last.  We were ordered to embark 
 There was the necessary movement and bustle of getting everybody and everything on board, but no fuss or excitement as of commencement of the war. 
 The hungary grey troop ships swallowed a great mass of animated kahki which trailed slowly up its gangways, and gobbled up everything which the cranes dangled near
