<p>1916 February 13<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Up at 8.30.  had good shower. Wrote letters during morning. lunch &amp; back to camp at 2 pm. Met Fairfield friends at Liverpool Station. Walked up to A.M.C. took some of girls to trenches. Mud. Tea.&mdash; Adams &amp; Greensell. Pres Tent after tea. Wrote letters to Jack &amp; Wall. Service. Nurses. Rev Paton good. Got Margot's late letter.</p>
