<p>Thursday 1st August<br/>On Tuesday evening we held a committee meeting <strike>to</strike> to decide, among other things; how we would carry on up the line. While the meeting was in progress, news arrived that the orders for proceeding up the line were cancelled. It seemed that a divisional move was imminent: Accordingly, classes were not discontinued.</p>
<p>I spent most of Wednesday in writing up </u>and</strike> articles &amp; reports of our scheme</p>
