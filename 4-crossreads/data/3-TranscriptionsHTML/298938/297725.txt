<p>12/3/17<br/>Rather a long gap since I last wrote in here, &amp; plenty to write too.<br/>The sisters arrived &amp; turned out to be splendid.<br/>Work increased &amp; the dispensary hut was cleaned out to be renovated in common with some dozen others; the others to form a new hospital here</p>
<p>A partitioned off dispensary was to form part of the improvement<br/>When work had just started Sgt Clay returned to the 3rd, &amp; on leaving promised to see about what chances I would have of getting job of dispenser with them</p>
