  
 3.10.15 Sun 3rd Fox the batman here had a quart of Cocoa ready as we came off duty this morning at 6 o'clock, & as soon as we had finished breakfast Les & I went down to investigate the wreck, we swam out to it & were exploring it & found all the canvas that remained on her rotten  while investigating a naval officer sang out from the staging to "get off that wreck" we took no notice, after he had repeated his order several times he came out himself on a pinnace & said didn't you hear me sing out to get off the wreck; I said Yes Sir; he said why didn't you leave it.
