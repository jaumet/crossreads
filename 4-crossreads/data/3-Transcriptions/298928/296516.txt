<p>25th Dec.  Xmas.  Issued with plum puddings cigarettes raisins <br/>etc. and with what we bought ourselves we had a fine time.  A <br/>Concert all the afternoon.</p>
<p>26th  Went to Panto.  Dick Whittington &amp; his cat at <br/>Steenwork by Anzac Coves.  The roads are frozen hard and you <br/>are risking your neck walking on it.  The ground is so hard</p>
