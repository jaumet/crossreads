
Mar April. 2nd 18 Near Fouilloy.We moved into this front relieving a cavalry bde. it was a very dark night when we took over & very muddy. These Tommies appear to be a shiftless lot, there only a couple of posies they seem to have just hung about with their horses.We did four days in, then were relieved by the 56th & came back to the road, there we were much better off & made ourselves very comfortable.We went in again & were to have been relieved again by the 56th, but the night before, the enemy heavily gassed the village Villers Brettonoux   the wind changing blew on to the
