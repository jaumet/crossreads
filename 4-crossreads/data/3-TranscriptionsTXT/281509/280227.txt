 1916 4 January 4 Tuesday 
 Slept till 11.30 am.  Get up have shower, dinner &amp; dress for parade.  Governor General reviews troops in afternoon. Rob &amp; self go to English Hall to concert. The lassie with the goo-goo eyes. Negro. Confidences. Night duty  Mosquitoes bad.  Write to Geizer. Receive notes from Miss Palser &amp; Janet.
