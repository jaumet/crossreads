
"Who's the Lady" & the "Glad Eye" both Farces.  Ellis & I have both put in for 7 days leave to go to-gether to see Auntie & Grandma.  It will be our first trip together since October 1916.  Do you ever receive any Books of Pictures "With the Australian Forces" in France.  I ordered them to be sent to you.  Probably your letters acknowledging same have gone to the bottom.  We will be anxiously waiting now to hear how your fruit crop gets on.  We hope that you will make the place at Dee Why comfortable.  We are also anxiously waiting to hear of Dave's first visit.  I can hear the "good oil" flowing on that occasion.
29.7.18
Dear Dad, Mother & George,
I went for a ride with Tom last night after Tea & by the time I got back & went for a walk
