<p>I see him seeking glory with the throng,<br/>And the fury of unfettered hell defy,<br/>I see him dashing madly with a song -<br/>A crash!  My God, I see him fall and die.</p>
<p>Ah, boast thee death, of sorrow take your fill,<br/>Your victory lies underneath the sod,<br/>But, full of hope, the soul triumphant still,<br/>Is speeding to the Paradise of God</p>
