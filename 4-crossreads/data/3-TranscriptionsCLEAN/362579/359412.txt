
On Friday last, Sister Kelly   &  I went for a lovely trip to Cayeux. There is a Convalescent Home for the patients from here   &  they are taken by Car nearly every day. If there is room we sisters are allowed to go. We left here about 2. p.m.   &  returned about 7.30 p.m. Had a glorious day   &  it is a very pretty drive - right to the Mouth of the Somme   &  the beach is very differed to ours.
