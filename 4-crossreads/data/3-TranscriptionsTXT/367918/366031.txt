
[Ailly Le Haut Clocher]30/10/1919
To Sir Chief Justice FergusionSydneyNew South WalesAustralia
I ask of you Sir for a form of Permission for my Marriage in France the reason I ask of you Sir are these
No. 1 I stated my intentions to My Mother some 15 months ago of My Marriage in France Giving full Particulars of Every thing necessary also of My intended Wife and on 3 other occasions I have written but as yet I have received no answer to My letters My fiance as also written to My Mother but as not received an answer.
