<p>You will notice that the River is very narrow here but I suppose further up it is much wider. We crossed near this place in the train. We have no River near us. We are away in the Desert but have a big wall round us so we will miss a lot of the sand. We have no flies or mosquitoes yet but we will have enough in the Sun</p>
