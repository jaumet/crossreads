8thLt Upfold and I went across to Vraignes to find our Y.M.C.A. which we heard was over there.  We found it.  Our Y.M.C.A. men are pearls on their jobs.  The man in charge of the 14th Bde Y.M.C.A. Edwards by name is a very energetic chap and pushes well forward with the unit.
