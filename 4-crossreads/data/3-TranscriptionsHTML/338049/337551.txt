<p>Saturday 7 Dec.<br/>Into town early &amp; got paid, fixed up with clothing &amp; interview Education Bn. Afternoon touring Albert Halls heard Clara Butt &amp; [Bachman?] Evening to a Cantata given at a church by some friends of Wills &amp; put in a pleasant social evening afterwards at Floyds.</p>
