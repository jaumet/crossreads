 Nov 1 (Wednesday) Pommies Redoubt arrived here on Monday afternoon after being 6 hours on the road doing 4 miles. The road being blocked with traffic as the 1st Aust Div was going in  the  &amp; the 29th Div coming out. 
 Passed their Inf. on the road Munsters &ndash; R.IR. &ndash; Essex, Lancs Hants. They looked a pitiable sight &ndash; slathered in mud &amp; shaken with the strain &amp; hardships.
