 8/1/18 Spandish embassy here today in interest of the French not to see us Heavy snow  Packets  overcoats 
 9/1/18 Heavy snow again this morning.  shovelled up again  Letter &amp; Xmas card from Coombs  card from Mrs [indecipherable] &amp; letter from Mother Flo &amp; [indecipherable] dated 21/9/17
