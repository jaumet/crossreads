 August, 1915 Monday 16 Shearing will be in full swing now- Here "After Shearing" is never heard &ndash; generally &ndash; everything "Apres la guerre" The word Anzac made from the first letters of Aust &amp; N.Zealand Army Corps happens to be a real Turkish word meaning "Only just" &amp; that is about all it was for a start. Letter from Laura 29 July.
