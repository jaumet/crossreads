 9 October 1916 Mon From my dugout door there lies Ypres absolutely blown to  pieces . Shell in the old cookhouse didnt do any damage. 8.30.pm. A couple of our planes are directly over my dugout &amp; Fritz is shelling them for all he is worth. I saw one of our aeroplanes  land  brought down this morning on our left. Another bombardment to-night. 
 10 October 1916 Tues A very quiet day. I spent most of the day making a knife from a German shell case &amp; cartridge to send to Claude for Xmas. Mr. James came &amp; paid us late at night. Very busy at midnight some bad cases passing through one a big policeman from Melbourne a Sniper but got him [chest].
