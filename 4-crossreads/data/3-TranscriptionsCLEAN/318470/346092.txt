  
 29/5/17 Coy. played 20th Div. Gloucesters A.S.C. Rugby League & won 3 to nil.  Rough game.  3 of Gloucesters had to leave field. 
 30th Sec. digging trenches near Villa de Flos.  Other Secs. doing the same. 
 31st Still on "Strong points".  Doing a little training. 
  1st  1/5 [17] Nothing fresh going.  Training & Strong. 
 2nd Training & diging trenches but having easy time. 
 3rd Same as yesterday. 
 4th Nothing fresh going.  Having easy time. 
 5th No. 2 Sec. played No. 3 & won with two wickets.  Nothing fresh. 
 6th Coy. had route march today about 5 miles.  Played 8th F. Coy. Soccer & won. 
 7th Working on Sports ground & doing camp work & a little training.  Played a Tommy A.S.C. & won easily. 
 9/6/17 Had Div. sports today & 14th F. Coy. came out on top with 47 points to 15th Coys. 36.  8th F. got 23 & Sigs. 22, got Silver Cup. 
 10th A little squad drill & Coy. Fatigues.  Nothing of importance to note. 
 11th Good news from North  British - including our 3rd & 4th Divs. & N. Zealanders advanced on a 9 mile front taking a lot of prisoners & a good many guns. 
 12th Still good news from Front North of Armentiers.  All Enemy counter attacks beaten off.  Lost Soccer to 15th yesterday. 
 13th Having general clean-up today preparatory to moving out. 
 14th Getting ready to move out tomorrow.  Played 15th Rugby today & won 14 to 9.  Some more ground won near Arras.
