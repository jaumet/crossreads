
[Saturday 30 September 1916]
Cold much better .  Posted notes yesterday.Leave by 9.30 pm Boat from Belfast for Fleetwood arrive in the morning & to meet Laura.All the Neill family have been so nice too me & gave me Wedding presentsSilver Cigarette Box - Gold Pin"     Flask & Ink PotLeather Despatch Box.They are all Rich nice people & seemed grateful for what I did at Mulberrygong - one Reggie Neill has his only son posted as missing-Spent the day walking thro' a pretty fern glen, full of Pheasants & Rabbits & am sorry to leave old Ireland   I am taking a wee bit of real Shamrock with me.
