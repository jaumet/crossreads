<p>On Sat. I went to see Frances she was the only one at home, except Mrs A but was expecting Violet at any moment, in response to my card    Arranged for following night.<br/>Dad &amp; I went to [indecipherable] &amp; found Bowles manager.  Thence to Lily at Prudential &amp; lunch at Slater's.<br/>We saw Mr Manhattan at the Prince of Wales' Theatre &amp; in the evening brought a very pleasant day to a close,  Am seeing "The Battle of the Somme" on the Pictures. On Sunday I saw Mr &amp; Mrs Upjohn &amp; Hilda &amp; the youngsters</p>
