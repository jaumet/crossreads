 21st Raided Village &ndash; Women gave me a bad time with their yells &amp; clamour &ndash; Bought 8 eggs &amp; Four [indecipherable]  2 Bundles Onions some wood for 15/-  - This village is in Arub Wadi where Pontius Pilate made an aqueduct from the spring to Jerusalem &amp; was sent back to Rome for spending too much money about 18 Centuries ago &ndash; This water channel is now being unearthed by British for a new supply to Jerusalem
