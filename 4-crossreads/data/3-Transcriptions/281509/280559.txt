<p>2 December 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>A hard hoar frost set in at sundown last night. This morning the mud is as hard as stone. --  a treat to have clean dry boots at last first time  for six weeks. Usual duties all day. Issued with bonsor snow boots laced to knee. Not before time.</p>
