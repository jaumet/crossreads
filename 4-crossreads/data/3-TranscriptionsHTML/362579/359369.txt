<p>We are all wanting to get back to our own unit. Get fearfully fed-up of so many changes.</p>
<p>This is a very interesting spot &ndash; nothing but ugly tents, painted most hideous colours to look like the ground etc.</p>
<p>Hope to receive another Aust. mail soon, but heard that another had been lost.</p>
<p>Suppose you will be thinking of Summer now   &amp;  we hate the thought of the winter. Hope to be</p>
