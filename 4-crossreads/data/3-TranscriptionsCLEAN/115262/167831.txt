
Monday 5 March 1917.
Arrived back off leave.
News just come through that embarkation on Wednesday has been deferred till Friday.
Passed final medical exam for embarkation and had hair close cropped.
In afternoon our unit was taken for a swimming parade.
Interstate Artilelry, T.M.B. M. Grs, Cyclists, & A.S.C, units marched into camp at Seyr [Seymour] for embarkation this week.
