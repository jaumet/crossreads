8/8/19 Durban
Issued a half pay to the troops today. Many cases of petty thieving going on. We expect to move into a new berth today and coal getting away on Sunday morning all being well. A hot muggy steamy day today. After lunch our boat had berthed in a fresh place alongside a train full of coal. With Dr Marshall went up the town to meet Padre Fox. Then had afternoon tea on one of the alcove verandah places so suitable to this climate. We now went down to the native markets ? a most interesting place. Passed many Indian shops where crosslegged tailors bootmakers were at work and where there were tawdry jewellers shops full of their brass ornaments.
