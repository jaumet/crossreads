 Breakfast Verre. Dinner Melun. Tea Epoloun. We travelled around the outskirts of Paris 30.3.16 
 Breakfast at Abbaville. Had a slight mishap here train went on leaving about 40 of us behind. Had to catch another troop train an hour later. We disentrained at Hazebrouck and marched about 5 miles to a village named Wallen Cappel. We billeted in barns and houses here 31.3.16.
