They are to be shipped to France being required by the Army almost as much as human beings. They are wanted for the guns, for general service, for the Red Cross, for [indecipherable] transport and for officers. Without them it would be impossible for the army to maintain its most vital communications in the danger zone. Many of them will replace others that have done their duty nobly and have gone west.
