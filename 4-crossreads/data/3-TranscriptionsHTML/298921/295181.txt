<p>July, 1915<br/>Thursday 29<br/>All my Kit I leave at <strike> Thomas COOK &amp; Co Cairo, my medal at the Anglo Egypt Bank also about 20/-/- there 200/-/- is in England</strike> If anything happens, Kit will be sent Australian &ndash; <strike>it is a elaborate one, too much-</strike><br/>Rifle can always be picked up if necessary - &amp; one wants to be free to lose everything without worrying &ndash; one soon gets used to the ground I prefer always you remember a hard bed so have one ready for me <u>when I come home</u></p>
