April 9th  Big bombardment at La Basse in the early morning. there was a heavy mist - We started at 9.A.M. on the road for the Somme, we went like the hammers of Hell all day & reached our destination "Allonville" (a town about seven kilo's from Amiens) at 10.15 P.M.  we passed through the outskirts of Amiens (we got split up at  Poullainville in the dark & some of us went through Amiens & the rest by a bye road - the 4th M.T. Coy were parked at Poullaineville) we made a feed & went to bed, the place we parked was very muddy & it was raining when we pulled in, on the whole we made a quick & successful trip. all the lorries arrived safely.
