Saturday 30.9.16
Relieved from Piquet at 4 pm. This afternoon shells lobbed directly in front just to the rear of our billet; the concussion breaking a number of the windows. Fortunately there were no casualties.
Sunday 1.10.16
Outskirts of the town bombarded practically the whole of the day. Very interesting to watch the result of the shelling from the top story of our billet. Church service at night.
Monday 2.10.16
Fritz bombarded La Bizet over the canal from Armentieres practically the whole of the day. Very interesting
