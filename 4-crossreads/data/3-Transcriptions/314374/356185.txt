<p>No. 2639<br/>Private Verdi George Schwinghammer,<br/>"C" Company,<br/>42nd Battalion, A.I.F.</p>
<p>Enlisted (after having been previously rejected) 6th May 1916.</p>
<p>Sailed from Brisbane in "Kyarra" (afterwards torpedoed) 17/11/16.</p>
<p>Arrived in England (Plymouth) 30th January 1917.</p>
<p>Left England for France on 23rd June 1917 in "La Maguerite" and arrived in France (Le Harvre) 24th June 1917.</p>
<p>Left France finally for England in Hospital ship "St. Andrew" on 18th April 1919.</p>
<p>Arrived back in Australia (Sydney) 4th August 1919.</p>
<p>At Randwick Hospital and finally discharged on 26th Septr., 1919.</p>
<p>The following places were visited by me during my period of active service:-</p>
<p>Australia:-  Brisbane, Fremantle, Perth.</p>
<p>South Africa:-  Durban, Capetown, Sierra Leonne, St Helena.</p>
<p>England:-  London, Dinton, Sutton Mandeville, Fovant, Tisbury, Swallowcliffe, Larkhill, Amesbury, Durrington, Shrewton, Netheravon, Figheldean, Salisbury, Plymouth, Stratford-on-Avon, Exeter, Stonehenge, Southwark, Holyhead, Isle of Wight, Manchester, Andover, Dozer, Southampton, Peterborough, York, Canterbury, Dartford, Liverpool, Winchester, Sheerness, St Albans, Folkstone, Weymouth, Newcastle-on-Tyne, Wilston, [Wilton ?] Chester, Portsmouth, Birmingham, Carlisle, Oxford, Rochester, Chichester, Warminster, Sutton Veny, Crewe, Reading.</p>
<p>Scotland:-  Edinburgh, Glasgow, Leith, Perth.</p>
<p>Ireland:-  Kingstown, Dublin, Killarney, Thurles, Dundalk, Belfast.</p>
<p>France:-  Paris, Le Harvre, [Le Havre] Steenwerck, Jesus Farm, Hazebrouck, Messines, Neuve Eglise, Strazeele, Baillieul, Remilly, Lumbres, Wizurnes [Wizernes], St Omer, Popperinghe, [also spelt Poperinge] Ypres, Boulogne, Calais, Wimereux, Caestre, Sailly-le-Sec, Sailly-Laurette, Doullens,  Amiens, Locre, Tilques, Bois Grenier, Armentierres, Kortepyp, Nieppe, Erquinheim, Waterlands, Romarin, Ploegg Steert, Eccke [Eecke], Albert, Heilly, Bonnay,</p>
