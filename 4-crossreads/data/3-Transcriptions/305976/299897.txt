<p>I saw at one Mosque a pit connected to river Nile. Its sides were at one time covered with knives so that when persons were thrown in the pit their bodies would be <strike>thrown in the Nile</strike> be cut into small pieces on reaching bottom<br/>Friday August 3<br/>Camel riding to day with better success than last time. Visited Luna Park in evening. Luna Park situated at Heliopolis the cleanest &amp; most beautiful place I have seen in this country. Wide, well made streets &amp; big substantial stone building. Was I am told built for a second Monte Carlo but authorities would not permit it. Luna Park is a pleasure</p>
