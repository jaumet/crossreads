<p>Friday March 2. 1917<br/>A dull cloudy day.  The street is wet this morning, there being a little rain last night.  The 10th Field Ambulance is moving out to day and this Hospital will be taken over by the 11th Field Ambulance.  Some of there doctors are already here and are being shown around the different wards, also the orderlies.</p>
