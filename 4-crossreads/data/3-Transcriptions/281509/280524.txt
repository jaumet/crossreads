<p>1916 October 28<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Rain as usual. The endless mud &amp; slush. Nothing special doing. Stand 1 &frac12; hours in queue at carhon to get served with a few biscuits 2 letters from Bill &amp; Flo MacB. The lights of surrounding hills like a great city thousands encamped in the mud.  Operations are greatly hampered. well nigh impossible to carry on.</p>
