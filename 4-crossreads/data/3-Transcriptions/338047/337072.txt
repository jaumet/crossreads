<p>1919<br/>July 27<br/>passed by Groote Shuur, once Rhodes residence, at this time General Botha's residence, who had arrived home a few days previously.  On this account we were unable to inspect it.  We caught the train at Rondebosch &amp; returned to Capetown.  I went with Jim Copland at night to a concert in the Grand theatre (Buckshee).  I went to all the buckshee shows I could on account of the spondulicks running very low.  We arrived back at the boat at about</p>
