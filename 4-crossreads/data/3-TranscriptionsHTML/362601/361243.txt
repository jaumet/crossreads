<p>27 July, Sunday</p>
<p>Drive to Dee Why. Go walk on hill. Duck for dinner. George to Marathon at Long Reef. Cold &amp; windy.</p>
<p>I look out all letters &amp; P.C's &amp; we sort them up &amp; sit to talk after tea.</p>
<p>Wrote Auntie Annie</p>
<p>28 July, Monday</p>
<p>Mum &amp; I go out in rain to H'ville, meet Dick's people &amp; then go to Nelsons. Arrive Home at 9 pm.</p>
