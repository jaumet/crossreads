<p>As resting. The infantry are working rather harder than usual, and so are most of the Division.  As for me, a move always means an increase of work for a while, and I have no doubt, that we shant be in rest very long. However it is a blessing to get away from planes. The blighters used to come over every night, generally on their way to some town back of the front. They used either to drop a couple for practice on the way over, or unload coming back.</p>
