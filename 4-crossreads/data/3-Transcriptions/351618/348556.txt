<p>Troops at Achi Baba is described as splendid and even better than had been hoped for.</p>
<p>Wednesday 30th June [1915]<br/>Last night it was very windy dusty &amp; rain threatened and the Turks took advantage to make a small attack. I think what chivied them up was the fact that we had sent out several patrol parties to bring in the <strike>dead</strike> comrades who fell in the attack made on Monday last.<br/>To-day was quiet and in the evening we had a thunderstorm accompanied by heavy rain. This is the first storm I have experienced since leaving Qld in Sept last and it brought back memories as you can understand. Most of us sat up wrapped in oil sheets while the storm lasted.</p>
