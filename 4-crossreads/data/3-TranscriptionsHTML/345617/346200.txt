<p>Octobre 5<br/>Coy still resting. Good news continues to come to hand. It is now reported that the N. Zealanders &amp; 3rd Div have done very well &amp; have captured large numbers of prisoners. About 2000 prisoners passed here today. Germans are putting up very little resistance.</p>
<p>Octobre 6<br/>Good news continues to come from Front. Between 4,000 &amp; 5,000 prisoners have been taken so far. We are still resting near Reningheltz. The weather is now very cold &amp; windy - having rain also.</p>
