 18 June 1918 Tuesday Haven't been given a job yet. Nothing of importance to note. 
 1918 June 19 Wednesday Squally wet weather &ndash; sometimes fine &ndash; sometimes raining My feet are treating me fairly badly again &amp; I am having them massaged again. The Disease that has been raging in the Depot appears to have almost died out.
