<p>Land again lost to sight &ndash;<br/>Saw some very large whales.</p>
<p>Thursday<br/>24-2-16</p>
<p>A nice day &amp; very calm.<br/>Saw a light about daylight, it must have been some part of Indian Coast &ndash; Saw a lot of sea snakes &ndash; very quiet on board.</p>
<p>Friday<br/>25th</p>
<p>Fine day &amp; very calm.<br/>It is a treat to watch the sun set &ndash; more especially when the sea is calm. Most of the Boys have converted their trousers into shorteys. Kept very busy of an night &ndash; Having the 2 Hospitals &ndash; Mumps &ndash; Measles and Isolation to attend to.<br/>Boxing Tournament has started quite a number of willing bouts already &ndash; AMC are being represented.</p>
