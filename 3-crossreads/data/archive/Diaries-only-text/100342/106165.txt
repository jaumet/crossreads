22nd
Off Guard 4.30. Tea then to Canteen but could not get to counter before closing.
23rd
Parade all day 9.15 a.m. to 11, 1.30 to 4 p.m. Weather Cloudy cold. Parcel Miss Purcell.
24th
Parades as yesterday, N.C.O.s special parade during afternoons drill. Y.M.C.A. Canteen but could not get served before closing. Weather still dull damp.
25th
Weather Beautifull, Bright warm. On Water Fatigue all day. Canteen after tea, most of which closed. (Xmas)
