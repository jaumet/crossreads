<p>February, 1916<br/>Friday 25<br/>Early morning we do a little practice in Gun mounting &amp; get our gun &amp; Tripod ready for the standard tests<br/>After breakfast we examine all squads at Drill<br/>After dinner inspection &amp; in the evening I went into Heliopolis for a walk.</p>
<p>Saturday 26<br/>First thing I heard this morning was "Cookhouse" at 8 am.<br/>After breakfast we had a muster parade &amp; issue of certificates.<br/>After dinner all men for Ismalia &amp; Tel-el-Kebir march out<br/>At night I went to pictures w/- dad</p>
