<p>We also passed through several similar towns on our way: Aire, Hazebrouck, Cassel, Castre and Bailleul</p>
<p>On our trip from Staples to Reninghelst we passed by our first billet which we had when we came to France 16 months previously. Also passed over the Frontier of France and Belgium at Abeele. One cannot help noticing the difference between the civilians of France and Belgium. The latter are very obliging and will do any mortal thing for us which is very much</p>
