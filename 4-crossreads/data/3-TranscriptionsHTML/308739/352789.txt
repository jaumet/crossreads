<p>Sunday 1 October 1916<br/>The Brigade struck Camp &amp;amp; stacked all tents by 8 a.m.<br/>I left at 9 a.m. with Officers luggage for Hill 70.  Arrived at 11 a.m.  Lieu. Britton represented Brigade &amp;amp; secured Camels to get our Kits over to Camp.  Dick Phillips &amp;amp; I went into Kantara for a swim returning to Camp 9 p.m.<br/>At Romani to-day at 2 p.m. Gen. Chavel decorated certain men of our Brigade who won distinction at the recent Scrap;  afterwards meeting Reg. Commanders &amp;amp; Officers of Squadrons.<br/>C.O. of Regts. were warned against lightly dealing with men at Court Martials.</p>
<p>Monday 2 October 1916<br/>The Brigade left Romani at 8 a.m. under General Ryrie for 70 Hill arriving at 2 p.m.<br/>The 1st L.H. Brigade under Gen. Cox left Hill 70 at 11 a.m. for Kantara where they camp near Transport Depot.  Gen. Cox called on Col. Fuller &amp;amp; took Him to Kantara for Dinner.  The Col. returned 9-30 p.m.<br/>All Details from El Maler came by train &amp;amp; camped at Hill 70 Rail to-night.<br/>A Meeting of Officers to-night decided on Reg. Mess with S. Reed in Charge.</p>
<p>Tuesday 3 October 1916<br/>Details &amp;amp; Q. Masters Stores arrived in Camp this morning, the men being allotted to Squadrons.<br/>Lieu. Walker &amp;amp; Serg. Dickson went to Pt. Said to Buy Canteen Goods for Officers &amp;amp; Men.  Lieu. Hardy &amp;amp; Reed went to Kantara &amp;amp. purchased Mess requirements.  Col. Fuller &amp;amp; Gen. Ryrie went to Hill 40 &amp;amp; met the General commanding this section, after which they inspected Camp.<br/>All Squadron Leaders are preparing to leave for Cairo to attend Instruction Class commencing next Monday including Major Bruxner.</p>
<p>Wednesday 4 October 1916<br/>Reveille at 0545, drill 0630 to 0700, Stables till 0800, Parade at 0930 to 1100, Stables till 1200, Dinnertime till 1400, stables again at 1530.<br/>From to-day a Sylibus of Training for Regiment comes into force.<br/>Brig. Ryrie went to Cairo to-day.<br/>Col. Fuller inspecting Squadron Operations.  During Afternoon both the Col. &amp;amp. Lieu. Hardy rode over to ---- Post some ---- to inspect one of our Troops who are stationed there.<br/>I went to Kantara to get Tobacco etc. also had swim.<br/>Capt. Thompson gave a Lecture on Map Reading to-night.</p>
