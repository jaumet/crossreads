<p>Le Bizet<br/>Road, C.11.d.65.50 to 12.c.25.45, 100 rounds Box Barrage, C.18.d.00.60 to 18.C.89.30.<br/>Enemy's Artillery and Machine guns showed comparatively little activity throughout day and night.  Greater activity was shown by enemy aircraft.  Weather bright, visability indifferent.</p>
