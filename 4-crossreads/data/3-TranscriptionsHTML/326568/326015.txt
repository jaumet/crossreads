<p>April.<br/>30th.  After a calm voyage of twenty hours we entered Plymouth Sound leaving Plymouth Hoe on the right &amp; went up to the naval dockyard of Devonport.  Quite a large number of men 'o' warsmen are in here, including the biggest boats.<br/>Leave for the evening to Plymouth, to where we went on a lorry much to the amusement of the populace.<br/>Theatre Royal for a show.</p>
