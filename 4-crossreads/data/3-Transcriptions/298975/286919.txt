<p>Some birds about to day. Heavy showers this evening</p>
<p>Wed 11th<br/>Pioneers won the Prize for the cleanest Deck &amp; <strike>Tab</strike> second prize for cleanest table also won OGradie Drill. These are the first Prizes we have won. Sighted <strike>th</strike> a sailing Ship this morning a long way off. The water is not really as Blue as it has been</p>
<p>Thursday  12th Guard<br/>An extra dose of yarns today as to when we would reach Durban</p>
<p>Friday 13th<br/>All on the look out for land, Sighted about 12.30 P.M. The first Official</p>
