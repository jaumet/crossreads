Well Dad, It is well on in the afternoon & as I have many things to do before I go to Grimsby to-morrow I will have to go & make a start.  Dave & I are going to London by the mid-day train & I am to meet an English Lady who is doing Voluntary Nursing Work in a London Hospital.  Her Husband is a Major in the R.A.M.C. & is in France.
