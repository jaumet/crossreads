<p>Thursday 23 March 1916<br/>Dick started off early riding Gaida, leading two draughts.<br/>Father rode home in evening.  Dick brought sheep home in lorry.<br/>I went up &amp; got 5 horses out of Long pdk. and took them to Wonga.</p>
<p>Friday 24 March 1916<br/>Father &amp; I rode up round Store &amp; Long pdk. and down to Wonga.  Terribly dry every where &amp; dams getting very low.<br/>Mustered sheep down out of School pdk. and draughted off all old ones that were not going to water.</p>
