<p>24) The Landing</p>
<p>land. while we were going ashore Some of our Boys were Killed from Bullets and Schrapnel on chap was hit on his collar badge. The Badge was Bent but no injury to the wearer he was lucky indeed&nbsp; Well after a time we reached the Shore and then we were marched along the Beach and took our packs off. I might mention that some of them never seen their packs again.Those that Did find them. also found that they had all Been gone throug Before they found them again</p>
