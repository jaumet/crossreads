
Rossmore HouseChareli El MadabeghCairo, Egypt5.9.15
My dear Cairo FriendI quite intended this letter to have been posted last week but have been very busy and the mail closed before it got written.I was so pleased to get your P.C. from Suez.  What a slow time you had through the Canal.  I cannot tell you how much Mervyn & I miss you & The Major and your poor brother is quite lost without the flat to go to.  He & Mer. have been coming here for dinner one night for the last week but Mer. has been so busy ever since you left - I've hardly seen him beyond a glimpse in the Hospital.
