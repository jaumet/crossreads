<p>22 December, Monday</p>
<p>Go to Lodge for my Ring. Sit on beach. Call at Dobboe's about 8 pm for Tea. Home at 9.30 &amp; address X'mas Cards to Nettie, Mrs Mel, M Cocks, Clarke, Cuneo, Aunt G, &amp; F, Granma, Mrs Lawrence, Jack, Preddys, M Harper.</p>
<p>23 December, Tuesday</p>
<p>To I.O.O.F. Lodge. We have music, singing, dancing. I play for dances etc. Catch 11 pm tram Home.</p>
