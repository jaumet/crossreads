 [[March-April, 1916.] Friday 31 This trip is good, seems as if the weather was made to order, we are fast approaching Aden, mumps fashionable I am in the fashion, thank you Saturday April 1 Sitting here on the hatchway smoking my G. B. D Thinking of friends in the southland far across the sea Sunday 2 Holy Communion aboard ship, sighted a speck of land after dinner Fought a big chap from the 18th on Saturday never got hurt
