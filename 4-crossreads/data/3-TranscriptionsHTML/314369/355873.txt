<p>13 August 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>After a nights sleep we start a long continuous round of fatigues<br/>Water carrying, sapping, digging   &amp;  carrying provisions   &amp;  ammunition all day long<br/>Manage to get a few gallons of water put by for next day.<br/>go into trenches at 7 pm<br/>Very quiet all night hardly a shot fired<br/>Bury two men who have been lying in front of trench since 6/8/15<br/>Awfully gruesome job.   X</p>
<p>1915 August 14<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Put in some good work improving firing line   &amp;  cleaning up.   &amp;  making snug.<br/>are relieved at 7 pm.<br/>Get mum's two cards.<br/>Two Faube aeroplanes had been hovering about our position all afternoon but make off on the approach of a British Biplane</p>
