<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Sydney</p>
<p>Date - 7 9 14</p>
<p>Time - 7.5 pm</p>
<p>From Brigadier. &nbsp;My orders are to seize all German wireless stations in Pacific and occupy German Territory&nbsp;soon as possible under Admiral&#39;s instructions&nbsp;or <strike>failing an emergency</strike> failing such instructions&nbsp;in any emergency carry out objects of expedition. &nbsp;Further hoist British flag in all territory occupied with success and make suitable arrangements for temporary administration. &nbsp;I have plans prepared for giving effect to this</p>
