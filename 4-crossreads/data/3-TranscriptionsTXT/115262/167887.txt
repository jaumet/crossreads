 Monday 30 April 1917. 
 On leave. &nbsp; 
 Caught 10.45 am train and went as far as Dandenong. &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp; 
 In afternoon went along Melbne road for walk for a mile or so. Came back at 5 pm. &nbsp;&nbsp; 
 An enjoyable walk too&nbsp;and a fine day. &nbsp; 
 7.46&nbsp;pm&nbsp;train to City. &nbsp; 
 Stayed at Federal Palace Hotel &nbsp;&nbsp; 
 Crook during night.
