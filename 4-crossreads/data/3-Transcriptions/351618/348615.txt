<p>Wednesday 8th<br/>Nice &amp; crisp still. Fairly quiet. Still building up my store for Winter etc.</p>
<p>Thursday 9th Sept<br/>Busy finishing off roof of store with old blankets W.P [water proof?] Sheets etc. It is now twice the size it was and fairly comfortable except that it really requires Galvanised iron roof. None of that material is yet available. A quantity is on the water we are told &amp; will arrive in about 5 weeks time.</p>
<p>Friday 10th Sept<br/>A windy day but cool. My store had a calamity the side wall fell over. It was heaped up sand bags &ndash; luckily no one was near it.
