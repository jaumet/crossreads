<p>Thurs Jan 2nd:-<br/>Out to Acton for the day.</p>
<p>Fri Jan 10th:-<br/>Empire Theatre at night.</p>
<p>Sun Jan 12th:-<br/>Left London from Victoria Station &amp; crossed to Calais. Spent night at Rest camp.</p>
<p>Mon Jan 13th:-<br/>Entrained at Calais &amp; today passed through Etaples, St Pol &amp; Arras.</p>
<p>Tues Jan 14th:-<br/>Passed through Douai, Mons &amp; St Ghislain &amp; reached Charleroi at 11pm put up at 1st Div Rfts billets.</p>
<p>Wed Jan 15th:-<br/>Left Charleroi by tram to Chatelet &amp; by legs to Bn at Acoz.</p>
<p>Thurs Jan 16th:-<br/>Aussie Xmas pcls arrive.</p>
