Winter is coming fast &amp; already the fine leafy avenues are shedding their leaves &amp; gloomy skies be dismal the landscape.  I decided that we should visit our 69th Squadron at Savy aerodrome &amp; spend a few days photographing amongst them. As our Car began to give trouble, I willingly took advantage of a proffered passage in Capt. Castle's Cadillac (whom I met at the censors) &amp; made for St Pol.   Castle very kindly found Wilkins &amp; I billets &amp; here I intend to stay until the weather is sufficiently good to enable me to photograph the flyers.</p>
