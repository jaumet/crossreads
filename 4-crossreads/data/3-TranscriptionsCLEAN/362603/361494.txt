  
 Bulford 23.7.17 Dear Dad, Mum & George, I am Orderly Corporal To-night & have to get all the Defaulters to work but I have left them to carry on while I write to You.  I also sneaked away & had an Hour at the Cricket Nets.  On Friday last I had to give evidence at a D.C.M. at Hurdcott.  I had left my Bike in Salisbury so I got the Train there & rode the 7 miles to Hurdcott & back.  I was pretty sweaty when I arrived back at The Close but after a wash and a cup of Tea I was right.  I was on Duty then I sat down & made the Two girls mow the Lawn while I overseered.  I rode Home at 10.15 pm & some coon took me a short cut which turned out to be 2 1/2 miles further.  It was a nice ride though.  I have a nice lamp on my Bike & The trees melt over-head & the Hedges are 20 feet high most of the way Home, so It was just like riding through a dark cavern nearly all the Time.  It only took me 70 minutes to ride the 12 1/2 or 13 miles.  I was unlucky on Friday though, Eric Hynde one of our Club mates was over & He asked Ellis a question while His back was Turned & when Ellis turned round He nearly dropped dead.  Reg.
