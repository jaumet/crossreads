 Sep 24 &ndash; 30 
 Thur 24th Was paid for Idleboy.  Packing up all day, &amp; went to the Crown Studios to sit for P.C Photos.  then to the Sydney Hospital &amp; then to M Edwards &amp; camp 
 Fri 25th Called out at 4.45 AM to embark about 60 horses sent on board, marking kits &amp; rugs all day; the Pen family brought out tea, Mr Pen arrived here from N'mine. 
 Sat 26th Received orders that the Star of Victoria is not ready &amp; cannot embark.  could not get passes  Stan &amp; I scaled the fence.  saw boxing contest between McCoy &amp; Griffith, a draw 
 Sun 27th Reg. picking up scraps of paper morning, no Parade afternoon.  got passes evening &amp; went to Felicia, then to S.H. with Jess 
 Mon 28th Flag drill morning.  mounted station work afternoon.  scaled out &amp; went to Manly &amp; to Sydney Hospital evening 
 Tue 29th Flag drill all day lantern drill at 7.30 PM  Stan got leave &amp; went to Felicia 
 Wed 30th Flag drill morning  mounted station work afternoon, went to the city &amp; had my teeth filled  [indecipherable] Stan &amp; Jess went to Felicia
