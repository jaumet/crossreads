 Gun drill &ndash; Quarters &ndash; Fire drill &ndash; Hoses run out &ndash; fore &amp; aft &ndash; Pumps manned &ndash; Every man at his post &ndash; Buckets handed down &amp; the hoarse voice of the assistant (indecipherable) &amp; the sharp orders of the gunners &ndash; We are simply a fighting unit &amp; so far as we go fairly efficient but we couldn't go very far &ndash; Our blue jackets come from all kinds of ships &ndash; Minotaur &ndash; Cerberus &ndash; Australia, Penguin, Protector. I went 'rounds' last night with the Executive officer &ndash; I had to crawl almost under hammocks which are slung everywhere &ndash; All the blue jackets stand to attention when 'rounds' goes by &ndash; My own stunt is-one-sick &ndash; I've made the S.B.A. clean up stretchers &amp; put things in line &ndash; Tell Rawdon a flying fish came thro my port last night &amp; fell on the floor &ndash; We play ordinary Bridge &ndash; for drinks mostly &ndash; but I wont drink before 7 pm  &amp; this example is being followed &ndash; 2 or 3 in the eveg. wont hurt anyone &ndash; These officers are a clean minded lot &amp; that's how they should be &ndash; We have 3 men of Devon &amp; 2 of Hampshire besides me of Norfolk in the Wardroom -
