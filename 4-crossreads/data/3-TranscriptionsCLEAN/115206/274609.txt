  
 February 1918 Friday 8 
 Messines There is a scene of desolation round these parts; on our left and in front of the old pill box, is a mass of ruined houses once a small village. It is known as Guy Farm On clear days one can see old Fritzies villages, Comines. And Werweick, as marked by a cluster of steeples and church spires. Weather unsettled Was on 12 to 2 last night.
