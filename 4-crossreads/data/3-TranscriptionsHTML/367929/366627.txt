<p>May 1915<br/>23 Sun<br/>Fairly quiet day. No casualties from sniping a record &ndash; but the usual 1 or 2 sick sent back</p>
<p>24 Mon<br/>Armistice from 7.30 am to 4.30pm for enemy to bury their dead. Each side buried those half way between ea others trenches.<br/>Counted 51  dead opp [indecipherable] of post that C Sqd was at in various stages. Saw Germans Saw sev curios. Many of enemy</p>
