<p>Tuesday 27/3/17.<br/>We were all awake at 5 am &amp; hoped to see Land but our luck is out &amp; we only have the Sea to gaze on. We were called on to supply another Submarine Guard &amp; as I had a nasty Cold I did not volunteer.
