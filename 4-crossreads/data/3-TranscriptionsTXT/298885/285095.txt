 Thur Sep 21st &ndash; 16 Nothing startling to report for the last 6 days as it has been exceptionally quiet 
 Fri Sep 22nd &ndash; 16 Just as we were reading one of todays English papers of Fritz being short of guns and ammunition, he put about 20 quite close, and one blew then end of a dug out in one man wounded.
