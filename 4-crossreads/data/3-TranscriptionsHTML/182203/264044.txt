<p>afternoon . He has got very thin but is much better, had diarrhoea badly. Fred has not been at all  well  &amp; I have not seen him for a long time   Rang &ndash; up the other day. He is very busy too, there is great preparations in the camps for moving on.<br/>Hope you are all well  &amp; happy &ndash; I shall write soon again.
