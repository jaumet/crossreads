<p>16. 16.6.15- Gallipoli- Reference to General Rosenthals [indecipherable] for T.W.G's transference to his (Rosenthal's) Brigade- Description of Lemnos-</p>
<p>17. 25.6.15- Gallipoli- Saw landing of Forces  Returned to Alexandria- then ordered to report at front and took up duty [indecipherable] Battery on 1st June 1815- trenches 30 to 40 yds. apart. [Indecipherable] messages-</p>
<p>18. 7th-7th.-15- Severe action- Shell burst kills Corporal [indecipherable]- serving in 8th (W.A. Battery)  Met Neil Brodie- and Barnes- Promoted 1st Lieut. 6th. July/15. Mention of Burley's death</p>
<p>19- Turks fix bayonets- Viv Benjafield &amp; Ken Smith.</p>
<p>20- 12.8.15. Battle raging- Cable sent reassuring action satisfactory- appreciation of Australian Infantry- no sleep for 4 days. General impression of Early Victory-</p>
