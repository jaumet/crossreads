<p>I arrived at hospital Lubeck at 2 p.m.  I was given hospital clothing and my own were taken away.  I was then told to go to bed which I did.</p>
<p>It was not until the following morning that a doctor looked at me.  My bandages were then stiff and sticking to my face.  I was taken into the operating room and my bandages were wet and then almost dragged off my face then my face was covered with boracic powder.</p>
<p>For ten days I could not see out of my left eye and <strike>I could</strike> anything I ate had to be broken up and pushed between my lips.</p>
