<p>28 March 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Visited a Monastery called Kremezan away on the Hills for Austrian orphans, winery attached (there always is plenty of grog in these Monasteries)   Took Capt. McLean's gun &amp; had a shot at some partridge but only got 1 they are shy &amp; fast flyers.<br/>X In the evening paid a visit to another monastery supported by the Argentine people <strike>&amp; called</strike> &amp; close to King Solomons Pools-<br/>These places are mostly deserted now, but the pools are a wonderful bit of engineering, the Pipe line being made of stones to Jerusalem. X</p>
