<p>III<br/>catch them at Port Said.<br/>I note Pat had an offer for a move to a mill and quite agree with him in turning down a position of the kind if it was going to be permanent.<br/>I met Charlie Lecky here quite unexpectedly yesterday. He has just arrived. Told me that he thought his brother Joe would be coming over.<br/><u>26th June </u>  Mail closes tonight but I have nothing much more to add. It is rather funny, the Turk's attempt at "bluff". They are in the habit of sending messages warning us to surrender. Yesterday they dropped a message for us from an aeroplane, but it drifted back into their own trenches.
