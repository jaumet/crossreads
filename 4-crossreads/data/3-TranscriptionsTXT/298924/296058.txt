Monday 17 March 1919Red SeaBoat drill &amp; education classes but as the latter are voluntary, the average soldier thinks he has learnt enough.Fresh Breeze &amp; cooler.  We get up at 0530 &amp; have half an hour physical jerks.  It is a treat to eat good meat again -all N.Z. frozen mutton &amp; Beef as the Ulimaroa was last there.
