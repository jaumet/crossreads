 Tuesday 7/8/17 Coy training &amp; route march, weather now good &ndash; ground drying up. Wednesday 8/8/17 Wrote to Doff, rode down to see Corlette &amp; Slane. Thursday 9/8/17 Took the Battalion for a 15 mile route march, not a single man fell out of the march. Friday 10/8/17 Seconded from Battalion now to command 2nd Aust Div Reinf Camp (a Dud). Saturday 11/8/17 Went to St Omer, also Renescure, had a bad headache all day. Sunday 12/8/17 Inspected site of proposed Camp at Bandinghem with Corlette inspected Bn Billets during evening.
