<p>Saturday 3 March 1917 [2 March 1918]<br/>A case of thypus in 27th G.H.  Removed to infectious hosp. on 2nd &amp; today 4 sisters are isolated in a tent &amp; no pts., friends allowed to visit 27th.  Off duty 2-5, went &amp; shopped for Marge &amp; met Mrs. Davies &amp; tea with her at Groppi's &ndash; friendly woman.  Spent Pt. 75 for M. Woods, Pt. 25 remaining.</p>
