<p>Nothing said about me being overdue.</p>
<p>Tuesday 19th Nov  Set about fixing up things for the move, making up lists and sending back to salvage, all we could possibly do without as we expect to do a lot of marching.</p>
<p>Wednesday 20th Nov.  Very little doing as we are still marking time in connection with the move.  In the afternoon had a kit inspection in order to see that all the men had all their requirements.  Did some writing at night having received a huge mail on my return.</p>
<p>Thursday 21st Nov:  Spent the day issuing clothing etc &amp; making arrangements for the move.</p>
<p>Friday 22nd Nov  Made up several indents for things &amp; then after dinner took a car &amp; went to D.A.D.O.S at Oisement with them &amp; also collected some gear.  From here I went on to the Red + Depot at Longpre where</p>
