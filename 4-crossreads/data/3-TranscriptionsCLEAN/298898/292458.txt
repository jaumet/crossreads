We have several huts to work in with a few dugouts inhabited by a few of the personnel. Found the roads very fair altho' very soupy, still the 3 days rest from traffic insisted upon from commencement of the thaw saves them from being cut up. 
 Sunday 25th Feb. Anniversary of this units formation at Tel-el-Kebir and we had arranged festivities to commemorate the event at "Buire" which however was knocked on the head by this move Menus & programmes had been printed Guard's band engaged to play during the afternoon & a good dinner prepared all of which had to be given up.
