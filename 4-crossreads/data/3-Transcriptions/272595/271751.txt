<p>the wire which was sent by the sister he granted me 48 hours straight off &amp; in a few minutes I had my pass &amp; was off to Warminster where I was just lucky enough to catch a fast train which landed me in London at 1.55.  As soon as she pulled in to Paddington I hopped out, slipped round to the cloak room, deposited my coat &amp; bag &amp; then down in to the tubes &amp; in a few minutes I was at Marleybone Station &amp; just had time to rush a hasty dinner &amp; make a dive for the train to Denham so well did I judge it that I had just about 30 secs. to spare before she pulled out, on arrival at Denham I got a ride out to the Hos. in the Red Cross Cars which meet all trains &amp; once there I soon found 29 Ward where Bill was, he was in bed &amp; looked very thin &amp; white on it, his knee</p>
