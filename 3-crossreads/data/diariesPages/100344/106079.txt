Paid after dinner. Bathing Parade to Erquinghem Baths Gass alarm test on Parade ground with helmets on roll call.
8th. Saturday.
Had some of the new bayonet fighting exercises to day by Capt. F. Letterwriting after Parade.
9th Sunday.
Packed up Paraded 9am ready for marching. Huts cleaned up left 11am on good roads with lovely weather. Dinner on the roadside about 2pm. reached our new billets at Caestres 15 miles distance about 6pm. very tired slept in shed alongside windmill ( P.Cards of same sent to K.C.)
10th. Monday.
Up 7am had look through mill which is still used.
