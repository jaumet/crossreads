
Tuesday 19th July 1917RebruevietteReveille 4.30 am stables, breakfast, first parade 5.30am harnessed up and moved about 7am. Had a very stiff hill to pull up for a start. Passed through some lovely country.Arr. Rebrueviette about 2pm. Unharnessed and took donks to water by the time we came back the gunners had the lines ready so fed up and were dismissed till 3pm. Billet very poor.
