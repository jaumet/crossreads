 1915 Nov.28 Sun. Went for a walk in the afternoon through Kasr-el Nil past the Residency,  &amp; over Mohamed Ali bridge on to Rhoda Island where we endeavoured to find the Nile gauge there, but without success. After watching some feluccas pass through the opened Abbas II bridge, walked off the island by this bridge, passed the Health Department Hospitals just opened, &amp; near the Zoo engaged a garry &amp; drove back to Gezira.
