The state of affairs at present existing proved conclusively that the methods used in the Valley &amp; elsewhere viz Canilisation &amp; oiling stagnant pools with Kerosene did to a very great extent keep it down. Tuesday Oct 8 We have received good news from all fronts &amp; we all think the war will end this year. It is rumoured that all 3 yr &amp; over service will shortly be going home.
