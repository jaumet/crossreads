<p>them to the Gare St Lazare  Leaving our kits there we took Taxi to Place de la Republic and had dinner with B.E.E.</p>
<p>Went into one of the R.C. churches to have a look round</p>
<p>Made our way back to the Madeleine.- Bought the photos which we had taken. at Versailles and Fountainbleau  We almost missed the train going to Rouen.</p>
<p>After a long monotonous journey we reached Rouen about two o'clock in the morning</p>
<p>Aug 20th. Tues.</p>
<p>Rain fell in the drizzle. It is the first rain that we have had since we came on our trip.</p>
<p>Left the receiving camp at seven a.m. and after a long march we reached the Rest Camp. We were paraded after breakfast and told how to get to our various stations.</p>
<p>Reports in the newspapers of successes at Bailleul. I expect our C.C.S. is busy now.</p>
<p>We were like caged birds in this place and slipping out of camp B &amp; I went into the city of Rouen. Met Col D &amp; S.M.M. Spent a pleasant evening strolling about.</p>
<p>On our way back to camp we passed through No 1 A.G.H. There we met Capt B.</p>
<p>Find the air very relaxing in these parts</p>
<p>Aug 21st Wed.</p>
