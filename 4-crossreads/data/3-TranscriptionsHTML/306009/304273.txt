<p>1915<br/>Mar. 28 Sun.<br/>Great sandstorm blowing all day &ndash; dirt &amp; dust in everything.<br/>Tucker improving greatly &ndash; probably the inquiry is shaking up the Q.M.<br/>After tea was visited by Doyle &amp; H. Henderson &ndash; the latter a Trumpeter in the 7th Light Horse, Maadi.</p>
