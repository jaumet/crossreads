 2nd. Wednesday. Had lovely shave of 15 days growth & sponge bath. how refreshing too, more than words can tell. Went with party of 100 to trenches, being in rear we lost touch with those ahead & messed up so not knowing where we were to go or do I sent message to C.O. 17th.
