
& of course the sea was rough. & got worse as the night came on   We rounded the Lewin that night & I thought we would be swamped.  The heavy seas broke the ships hospital in & carried one of the lifeboats away.  However things were calm again the following morningOn Saturday 13th May a boxing tournament was started & lasted for some time some good bouts were witnessed & where ever it p was possible the chaps climbed to get a good view of the fights  We struck some heavy weather  again about half-way across the Indian Ocean. But as we got closer to Colombo the weather & seas were good & seas were good & began to feel the heat of the tropics
