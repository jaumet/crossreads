 July Monday 30 1917 
 Ward duties till 5 p.m.   Eye cases transferred to No. 25.  Hardelot.   Still raining. Out with Carty &amp; Buckley.   "Gukes" Kharki girls.   Look up Abernethy in his hospital.   Yarn till first-post.    He away to con. camp in morning.   Exchange addresses.   Wonder where &amp; if we'll meet again.   He's a good sort.
