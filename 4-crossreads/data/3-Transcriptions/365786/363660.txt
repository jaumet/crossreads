<p><strike>not listen to the cry of those who say "go slow" &ndash; no indeed, not till victory is ours must we slacken our efforts.</strike>  Sooner of later our men will again be in the forefront of the fight &ndash; they won't fail us &amp; we must see that we don't fail them.  Let us not pause in our good work, &amp; then when the poor wounded &amp; sick soldiers need our gifts we will be able to supply their <strike>needs</strike wants &amp; thus do our part in winning the fight.</p>
