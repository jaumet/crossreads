<p>scenes &amp; a picture of the Wattle &amp; Red Gum, awfully good of her to think of me.<br/>Very pretty card with lovely words on it, from The Osburnes. A real Aust. Kookaburra card form "Mount View" (The Trangmais) also a letter from Mrs Balfour saying she was sending a cake, so you see my friends are thinking of me.
