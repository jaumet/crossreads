
27 September, Saturday
Miss boat by seconds. We start to paint. I go in for a swim & the cold nearly freezes me but lovely afterwards. Terrific hot day & we all sleep out. M & I on floor & we get no sleep. Until southerly at minuit.
Recd letters from Dot & Ida.
28 September, Sunday
I am tres malade. We paint House. Pony goes mad wants to sit down. Mum is scared. E & Pad drive Home & we come Home by tram. I with Sandy in the back. Ring Doris, feeling lonely. Go to bed at 6.30 pm.
