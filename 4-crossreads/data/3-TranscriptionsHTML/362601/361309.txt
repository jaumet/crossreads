<p>8 December, Monday</p>
<p>Feel well &amp; fit up after so many years. Mrs Doyle to visit in Home. Write after 9 pm to Auntie.</p>
<p>Wrote M &amp; Auntie Annie.</p>
<p>9 December, Tuesday</p>
<p>Pick Gum leaves &amp; post letter to Auntie. E on boat. We go to A[nthony]. H[orderns]. &amp; Sons see Brultes, syce &amp; beetles &amp; butterflies.</p>
<p>Home &amp; chatter.</p>
<p>Recd letter from MRL.</p>
<p>Wrote until bed time.</p>
<p>Wrote (M &amp; Aunt Annie)</p>
