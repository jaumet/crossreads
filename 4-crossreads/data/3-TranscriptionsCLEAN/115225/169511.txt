Dec 21st  Very cold but fine - Had to be towed again to start the engine, I drew the supplies & went up to the workshop when I had finished & stayed the night. Roy & Dick were also in the workshop & they spent the evening with me -
 
Dec 22nd  Not so cold to-day - I left the engine for the Staff Sergt to try & start, but he could'nt get her going. took carburettor off & worked on it & eventually got her started - I did'nt go on supplies to-day as I could'nt get out in time - When I got out I went back to the Park - had a hot bath in Bailleul -
 
Dec 23rd  Very cold again - Had to be towed again to start the engine - drew supplies for Brigade & got back to Park at 2.30 P.M.
