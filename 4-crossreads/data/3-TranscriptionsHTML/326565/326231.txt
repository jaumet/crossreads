<p>at 2 pm back 4.30 pm, (very poor)  Had tea in refreshment shop next door to hall.  Left Healsville 7 pm arrived camp 10 pm.</p>
<p>Monday 25/10/15<br/>Usual parades  Selected by Captain North to go to Sydney with 17 others to Embark by Beltana on 9/11/15.<br/>Visited friends place Albert Park, played billiards &amp; ping pong, returned to camp 10 pm.</p>
<p>Tuesday 26/10/15<br/>Usual parades,  Reg returned from Ascot isolation hospital.  Went Concert given by St Lukes Church, Albert Park.  Left Concert &amp; went to tea at Caf&eacute; Francatelli</p>
