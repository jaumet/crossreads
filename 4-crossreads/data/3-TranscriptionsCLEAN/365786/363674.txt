
At Heliopolis also is now the big Australian Camp where the new Regiments & the Reinforcements are stationed.  Right out on the Desert, with it's awful glare, heat, dust & flies.  Here our men drill & train so that they may be more fit to fight for us.The No. 2 General Hospital is beautifully situated on the Island of Gezireh which is one of the suburbs of Cairo & is connected with the city by two fine bridges & has a good electric tram service.  The island consists of the Sporting Club, the Palace Hotel, Aquarium, Public Gardens & residences of wealthy inhabitants with native & european.  The fine Palace Hotel is used to house the No. 2 Aust. Gen. Hosp.  It is a huge building & has accomodation for 1000 patients.  It is fully staffed with Doctors, nurses & orderlies & thanks to the generosity & work
