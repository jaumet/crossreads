<p>Novembre 26<br/>Taking map locations of Pill Boxes &amp; Dugouts in our Supports &amp; Reserve line. Most of No. 2 Section working on strong points. Good news continues to come in regarding the advance near Cambrai.</p>
<p>Novembre 27<br/>Taking map location of servicable Dugouts and Pill Boxes along our Reserve &amp; Support lines. Most of Section employed putting in strong points in the Support line. Miserable wet weather today.</p>
