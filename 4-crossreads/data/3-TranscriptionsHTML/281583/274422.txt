<p>There was evidently a big stunt on, the news of which has not yet come through. It is still proceeding &amp; the fire is growing in intensity. There was a serious raid by Aeroplanes &amp; we were kept on the hop. They dropped bombs all over the place &amp; a few fell on a Field Hospital about 700 yards from us &amp; killed 8 AMC men &amp; wounded 45 others including a lot of patients. Guns were screeching all round us, while the rattle of machines guns on the Aeroplanes could be distinctly heard as they engaged one another.</p>
<p>One Taube was seen to fall in flames &amp; explode when it hit the ground. On the whole it has been a terrible night, &amp; I would be glad to see the clouds over the moon &amp; put an end to midnight raids. One can stand them in daylight, but they are uncanny at night.  Meanwhile the fighting still proceeds &amp; before the next 14 days are over, Baupaume should be ours,.</p>
<p>My cold is still very troublesome &amp; does not seems to mend, but it is the constant trudging about in the wet which does. It.</p>
<p>12.11.16  Sunday</p>
<p>We moved off for the front at 9am &amp; catching a Motor Lorry got a lift as far as Albert</p>
