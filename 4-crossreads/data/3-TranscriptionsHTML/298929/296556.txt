<p>7th  Preparations for the biggest stunt of all so far is marvellous  12 inch Hows have been put in &amp; the miles of transport.<br/>The attack has started on a 12 mile front.  Led by 3000 tanks and covered by the biggest barrage of the war.  We were up very close to the hopping off point &amp; last night was shelled &amp; gassed</p>
<p>8th  Prisoners by the hundred were brought back.  Some were from Stuttgart. Westphalia Berlin &amp; were of the 13th Reg Herwarth von Bittenfelds &amp; [indecipherable]<br/>There were also a good few</p>
