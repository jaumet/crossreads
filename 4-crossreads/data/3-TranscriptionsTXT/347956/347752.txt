 Sep 5-10 
 Sat 5th Joined the signallers, &amp; was drilling with them all day under Sergeant Major Coleman,  went to Stadium with Mylie Edwards &amp; saw a good contest between Kay &amp; Shugrue, Kay the winner. 
 Sun 6th On dismounted orderly duty, went to Felicia for tea, &amp; met Nowland &amp; Jess there 
 Mon 7th Drill with flag morning.  father came out at dinner time . were issued with saddles evening 
 Tue 8th Rifles issued to us morning  received 48 hours leave to go home &amp; say good-bye before we sail.  went to Pen's for tea, &amp; left Sydney for N'mine was a bad arm from vaccination 
 Wed 9th  Arrived N'mine after a good journey went to Backwater picnic to see George  left N.mine for Sydney on mail. 
 Thurs 10th Arrived in Sydney &amp; went to Felicia for breakfast, &amp; then to La Perouse for picnic had a lovely time &amp; met Hayley at Felicia made a will, my arm very sore
