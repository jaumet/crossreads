pushed onto trenches but fooled about till about 3am. in overcrowded and narrow sap which was under heavy shell fire. then with great difficulty pushed through in front of Pozieres to 1st new line where we dumped our tools returned to sunken Rd trench. Before returning we passed many prisoners wounded.
Had about 2 hours rest in sunken valley there with remaining B.Coy. men was ordered to report as diging party, to 18th Colonel in front line
