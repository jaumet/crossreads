At 8 o'clock last night, all the artillery on this section opened a tremendously heavy bombardment, the heaviest I have witnessed, which lasted till about 3.30 a.m. & at daylight the Infantry made a very successful advance on the left flank near the coast, & crossed by means of pontoon bridges, the small river which up to the present had been dividing our line & the enemy's. The Infantry captures 250 prisoners this morning.
