<p>1916 May 15<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Still cold. Breakfast consisted of 3 spoonfulls of porridge without milk or sugar. tea &amp; musty bread &amp; marmalade, Ugh! Bought steak &amp; egg.<br/>Posted letters to home &amp; Ruby on board. Nothing else doing all day.</p>
