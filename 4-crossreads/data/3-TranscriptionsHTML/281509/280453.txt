<p><br/>18 August 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Carry 3 times to Lee's Post for Pozieres. Knocked clear out. thoroughly exhaust <br/>Get budget of letters in dugout last night. What a read, &amp; what a brief respite from the awful surroundings. Flies in dugout 3&ndash; black, sleepy crawly horrors satiated with offal, seek comparative quiet in the bowels of the earth. Concussion blows candle out repeatedly. Too tired to rest. Meet Tony in afternoon &amp; have a good old yarn. Warwicks hop over this evening. Drizzly rain sets in again it makes the roads &amp; saps well-nigh impassable. 6th F. Amb bearers come up to reinforce. a big stunt anticipated to night by our boys.</p>
