<p>29/12/16 Dar E. Salaam Another attempt to get away from here at 6.30 AM no luck.</p>
<p>Got parcel from Albert in afternoon Pipe Soap Tea Tobacco Cigarettes etc</p>
<p>30/12/16 No attempt this morning to get away. abandoned apparently for the present.</p>
<p>31/12/16 Dar E. S No word of moving to day &ndash;</p>
<p>1-1-17 Dar. Es Salaam no word of shifting</p>
<p>2-1-17. D. E Salaam. Rumours of pay to day but no word of ship for home Paid &pound;2.0.0.</p>
<p>3/1/17 No word of moving yet &ndash; Kit Bags brought back to camp after being on  lighter for a week.</p>
<p>4/1/17 Still in Camp Daressalaam.</p>
<p>5-1-16 Ditto Feed of oysters</p>
<p>2 letters from "Aunt" dated 18th Dec.</p>
<p>6 Jan 17 D. E Salaam. Very bad last night vomiting pains in stomach fish poison</p>
<p>7 Jan D. E Salaam</p>
<p>8th Dar Es Salaam.</p>
<p>9th Rumour of our leaving here tomorrow per Professor Noerman Roll call at 4 p.m. to day for all unfit men</p>
<p>10-1-17. Roll Call 6 am no leaving to day</p>
<p>Still feeling rotten stomach bad. Wrote to Albert an Vi 8/1/17</p>
<p>11-1 17 No sailing to day feeling much better, still no mail</p>
<p>12/1/17 No Ship feeling OK. for a change. Wrote to Jinmy "Fraser"</p>
