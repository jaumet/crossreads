 25 March 1918 Monday Bethlehem   Went again to Church of Nativity &amp; the Milk Grotto the latter place crowded with pilgrims with BABIES, you know the legend i.e. no need for Foster Mothers if the Mother goes to Marys Milk Grotto. Later went to Typhus Hospital run by Armenians the Doctor was at QUINNS POST GALLIPOLI opposite me Aug 7th 1915.   His saddle was lost after we evacuated -   Pretty Syrian nurses here who had nursed our prisoners in Jerusalem before we came.
