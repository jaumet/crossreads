Sunday 3rd  [Jan]Church parade at 9.30.  Boat stations & fire stations afterwards.  Like any other week day on board ship.  Selected a good spot to sleep on upper deck.
Monday 4thThe weather is getting warmer as we near the tropics.  We are steering N.N.W.  Must be going to Colombo.  A lot of boxing was done tonight.  They are having a tournament starting from tomorrow night.  Entered my name.
