<p>48</p>
<p>St Patricks Hospital</p>
<p>Malta</p>
<p>27/12/15</p>
<p>We came into this hospital last night from mudros wich we left on Thursday December the 23<sup>rd</sup> and arrived in Malta harbour on Saturday the 25<sup>th</sup> about 2pm. Christmas Day we spent on Board Ship (Soudan)&nbsp; for Dinner we had roast Beef potatoes - pumpkin - and Plum Pudding and turkey. (Oh turkey) But we we were Sick of turkey as we were having lumps of it Since april the 26<sup>th&nbsp; </sup>So we had been haveing it for Eight months and then we had to withdraw&nbsp; Because Some turkeys are to hard to pluck&nbsp; So lets have Chicken next Xmas</p>
