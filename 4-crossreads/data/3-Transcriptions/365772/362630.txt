<p>Kasr-el Amy-Hosp Cairo<br/>23.5.15<br/>My dear Lizzie<br/>As you have heard by this from mother the turks got home on me to the tune of 3 hits but not a bullseye in the three.<br/>No 1 I received on the right thigh, 2 on the left thigh which was a dum-dum &amp; No. 3 through the left forearm, two are alright only the dum-dum keeps me down.<br/>Well dear I will have</p>
