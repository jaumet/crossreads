 Went to Cairo to night did not think much of what I saw I had a cold but could still smell. 
 19.4.15 I was very cold here last night very sandy about here sand for breakfast Dinner &amp; Tea,  Nigger  native looke very dirty 
 20.4.18 Last night  we  I  dr  another Sgt were going on a message we saw a soldier fall into a dirty water hole &amp; I never heard a man swear like he did in my life. The weather here is perculiar such a being hot in the day time &amp; cold in the nightime We were drilling to day on the Ancient City of On which is buried in sand.
