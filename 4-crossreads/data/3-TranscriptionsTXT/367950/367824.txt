
3rd May 1917 Victoria Coffee Palace Stanley Street Brisbane.
"The shouting and the tumult diesThe Captains and the Kings depart."The roses have faded, the long journey ended - and we are indeed home at last! What a long journey, what suspense at times!We landed at Melbourne on Anzac Day - a most appropriate time I thought - and we had a magnificant reception from the citizens of that city. We were cheered and hurray-ed from Port Melbourne to the Red Cross home just over the Flinders Bridge. We were granted leave immediately up till 11 pm.
