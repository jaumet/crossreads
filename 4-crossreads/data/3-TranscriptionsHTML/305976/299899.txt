<p>In evening had walk round Native Quarters &amp; took tram ride to Rod El Faraq situated on banks of River Nile. We hired Felucca &amp; sailed over to where there was a very large Melon patch. Sunset on the Nile sets one dreaming of Ausy.<br/>Monday Aug 6<br/>Back to our old friends the Camels. Riding, Grooming Feeding etc. Wrote mail evening.<br/>Tuesday August 7<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   Went into Cairo. Saw Egyptian Wedding. All concerned seemed wildly excited over the event &amp; dressed in Gaudy clothing,<br/>Might add that higher class Egyptian is entirely Westernised in his mode of <strike>address</strike> dress. My remarks</p>
