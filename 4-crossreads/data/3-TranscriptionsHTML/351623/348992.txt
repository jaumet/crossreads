<p>29 Aug 16 )<br/>30 Aug 16 )<br/>31 Aug 16 )<br/>1 Sept 16 )  At sea bound for Devonport England<br/>2 Sept 16 )<br/>3 Sept 16 )<br/>4 Sept 16 )<br/>5 Sept 16 )<br/>6 Sept 16 )</p>
<p>7 Sept 16<br/>Done high speed this morning, going a zig zag course. A Destroyer came out &amp; escorted us into Devonport. Passed Eddystone lighthouse. Arrived &amp; tied up alongside one of dockyard piers at 1 pm.</p>
<p>8 Sept 16<br/>All ships company went on 21 days leaf. Party of sailors off the Essex looking after Melbourne while we are away. Came up to London. Arrived in London &amp;</p>
