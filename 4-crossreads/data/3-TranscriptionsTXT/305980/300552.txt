26:  Gas demonstration, going through fumes &amp; firing rifles with helmets on.
28:  Left Shellal at 9 a.m. going as reserve Bde to Division at Abbassan el Kebir.
June
2:  Transferred to gun squadron.  Taubes bombed today, without doing any damage.
9:  1st Bde. &amp; Camel Corps made a mock attack on Raffa field for benefit of French general.  Galloped over the flat 2 miles before dismounting &amp; paraded a fine sight during the March past.
17: -  Left Abbassan 8 a.m. reaching Tel el Makeab-Raffa beach for a spell.  Good surfing &amp; plenty of inspections &amp; cleaning rusty bits &amp; irons with wet sand.
20:  Witnessed a duel between our monoplane &amp; a taube, both coming to grief with Hun pilot killed.
