<p>11-3-17<br/>Sun 11th<br/>Church parade at 8 oclock this morning but I went over to the Remount Depot instead &amp; had a yarn with Regan &amp; left my legging with him as they want some repairs &amp; wore his away in exchange. Received afternoon pass at 2 oclock &amp; Harris &amp; I went to the hospital &amp; met Dev High there, put an hour or two yarning with some of the lads &amp; then went to the Anzac Hostel for dinner had a hot bath &amp; was back at Abassia by 8.30 Cobb could not come out tonight &amp; Thompson came instead we drove in gharries to the Anzac Hostel for dinner had a hot bath &amp; was back at Abbassia by 8.30 Cobb could not come out tonight &amp; Thompson came instead we drove in gharries to the Anzac Hostel &amp; back to Abbassia  Our driver was slightly inebriated &amp; gave us a lively spin till his near sider fell, but did no damage, Harris &amp; I arrived back in camp at 11.30</p>
