<p>Thursday Nov 2nd:  Marched to Flers 15 miles. On Light Railway Fatigue<br/>Wednesday 8th:  Attached to 9.2 Battery. Running Ammunition<br/>Sunday 12th:  Relieved. Came back to Mametz<br/>Wednesday 15th:  Shifted to Montauban<br/>Saturday 18th:  In front line &ndash; Flers.<br/>Tuesday 21st:  Relieved by "Tommies" 9 p.m.  Came back to Carlton Camp.<br/>Wednesday 22nd:  Back to Ribemont<br/>Thursday 30th:  Marched 10 miles to Cardonette</p>
