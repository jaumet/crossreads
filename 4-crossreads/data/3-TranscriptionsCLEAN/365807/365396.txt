  
 [Letterhead: New South Wales  Coat of Arms.] Premier's Department Sydney, 3rd May,1917 
 A.17/2707 
 Dear Judge Ferguson, 
 I have the honor to inform you that a further message has been received from the Agent-General for New South Wales in London regarding the health of your son, Lt. Ferguson, viz:- 
 "Referring to your telegram of 24th April. Advised to-day Ferguson progressing very well." 
 The cost of the above cablegram was 6/6d. 
 Yours faithfully, 
 F.C.G. Tremlett Acting Secretary per W.H.T. 
 The Honorable Mr. Justice Ferguson, Supreme Court, Sydney.
