 2 December 1916 Thursday Col.  Cox CB  is now our Brigadier [indecipherable] returned to our Regiment &ndash;  Did you ever get my Pictures from HAY yet?  Quite a lot of Murrumbidgee men are here &amp; they are all wanted. Two mules have just pulled away &amp; are standing in the open exposed to theTurk fire, ere I finish these lines unless someone can catch or harness them under cover they will be killed &ndash; Then it means a burying party at night as wood is scarce. One mule's leg broken the other broke loose &amp; reached the Beach road &amp; was caught &ndash;
