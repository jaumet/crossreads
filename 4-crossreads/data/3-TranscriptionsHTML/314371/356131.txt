<p>Saturday 12th May<br/>Wounded &amp; prisoners started passing our position at about 5 a.m.  The whole operation was a success but at an awful cost.<br/>Some Battalions have hardly an officer left.  Altogether it was one of the worst stunts this year.<br/>We buried a cove out of the 7th Bn. who had been lying in front of our posy &amp; stinking awful.</p>
<p>Sunday 13th May<br/>Fritz bombarded all day &amp; nearly all night w/- shrapnel &amp; 5/9's.  Had a thunder storm during night &amp; a heavy fall of rain.</p>
