<p>March 1918<br/>Monday 25</p>
<p>Oosterhaven Wood &ndash; Hollebeke. Had a rather strenuous night. And early morning.<br/>2am guns very quiet on both sides.<br/>At &amp;am . Fritz put down a barrage of 5.9 on Zero road which is a very close proximity to the camp<br/>Weather fairly cool. Walked across to old Bty position. <br/>More casualties at W.L. Bob Elyod and his brother both killed. Ardey kolar,[klar?] both legs off. Charlie Smith severely wonded. Davis, a Blighty, Pat Crowe one leg taken off at knee, Boys were all out grazing when it happened</p>
