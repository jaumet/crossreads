<p>Monday 5 March 1917</p>
<p>Got 75 out of possible 80 for Exam, very interesting lecture on methods in France. We get enough mounted work AT THE TROT to keep us healthy.</p>
<p>Had dinner last night with General MEREDITH D.S.O. now C.O. 4th Brigade once my C.O. 1st L.H. Reg. which is now commanded by Col. Granville.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>Am sending by Hospital ship sailing Wednesday for Australia a Revolver I took from a TURK at El Arish with a Camel Hair cord attached, per a Lieut MILLS who had his arm shot when observing from aeroplane, a Sydney DENTIST.</p>
