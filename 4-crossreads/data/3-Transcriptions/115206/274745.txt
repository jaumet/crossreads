<p>June 1918<br/>Minday 24</p>
<p>On the road, Ebevnville.<br/>Weather very dusty, cold winds. Left billets at 7.30 am. And pulled into final camp at 12.50 pm.<br/>Walked into Pont Remf with Smithy, Gunn and [indecipherable]. Had a very decent snack in a farm close to railway station. Walked home through Friedcourt.<br/>Reg Harris and a few others returned to Battery. Teddie Dunn also.</p>
