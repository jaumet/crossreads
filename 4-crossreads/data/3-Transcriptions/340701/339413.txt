<p>Wednesday 23-8-16<br/>We are attached (A Company) to the R.E.s [Royal Engineers] alright.<br/>Letters to Mollie Harry &amp; Kids.  Back on the old tramway job now running a branch line between La Boiselle &amp; Pozieres.  Fritz caught the 9th Brigade relieving the 1st yesterday &amp; shelled the hell out of them both   incidentally he shelled us out of our job about 5.0 PM for about an hour.  I received a box of nice things from Mollie last night.  There were about 6 or 8 Taubes floating over us all yesterday afternoon &amp; our planes didn't seem to be able to get near them.  One of our own planes kept sailing across our Job the Germans shelling him all the time &amp; some of the shell fragments fell rather close to us.  We swore at him freely.  Seen a</p>
