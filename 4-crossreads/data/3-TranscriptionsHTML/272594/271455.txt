<p>After I had finished I had a jolly good wash &amp; this freshened me up considerably</p>
<p>17th  This morning we were all classified, I was given 2 days "light duty" &amp; then marked Dental" &amp; after that A class.  Have seen a good number of Portuguese round here, they are of a dark brown complexion, &amp; are all of an average size not as fine made men in my opinion as the French, though they seem very active &amp; smart &amp; move about quickly, their uniform is a color between</p>
