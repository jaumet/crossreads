<p>Sunday 6 August 1916<br/>Played Piano for Church.  Played too loud, gave no breathing time &amp; no expression.  Malish.</p>
<p>Monday 7 August 1916<br/>Sick Parade.  I get a cold from Playing Football.</p>
<p>Tuesday 8 August 1916<br/>One letter from Bess.  Went next door &amp; watched arrival of 130 Turkish Prisoners.</p>
<p>Wednesday 9 August 1916<br/>Leave all day.  Played Billiards, saw Australian wounded come in to Cairo.  Dinner Y.M.C.A., Tea at Groppi's.</p>
<p>Thursday 10 August 1916<br/>I go on S.P. 2 days, no drill.  Cois Cateer.  Play Cards with neighbours W.D. &amp; Tommy W.</p>
<p>Friday 11 August 1916<br/>Ward Master.  Finish top floor of Mel. Pav.  Work finishes.</p>
<p>Saturday 12 August 1916<br/>Route March.  I am well away.  I am O. Corp. &amp; Postman.  I help sort letters.  I get 2 from Home, one Hilda &amp; one May Coppin.  Parade at 4 p.m. &amp; I don't hear the Bugle.  Shepherd's Band plays Y.M.C.A.  Dick Rowe comes to Tea.</p>
