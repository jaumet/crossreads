  
 29/10/16 Sunday to day did not go to church  Notice to be prepared to leave Weymouth for Wool Tuesday next.  on our way back to France.  Letter from Mother. 
 30/10/16 Girl in taxi  Pavillion [indecipherable] Nothing of interest today  Hellish windy & needless to say cold.  rain also 
 31/10/16 We left Weymouth for Wool 2 PM, arrived in to new camp 4 oclock rather a well laid out camp  Raining like Billy OH  Marched through Weymouth with band 
 1/11/16 Rained all night rather cold into the bargain  2 blankets each  before medical officer passed OK etc  usual parade during the day morning noon cabled for 15/10/0 
 2/11/16 Rain again today  placed in charge of a draft for the 4th Div a hopeless mix up it is.  plenty of work & no instruction. 
 3/11/16 No leave granted here so broke camp went to Weymouth in motor car a fine beano.  we arrived back in camp about 12.30 it was glorious trip 6 of us same old messing about today
