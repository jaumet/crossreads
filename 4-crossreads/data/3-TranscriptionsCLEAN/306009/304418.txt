  
 1915 Aug. 22 Sun. Ben Champion, Art. Felton & another Sergt. from 7th Reinforcements, 1st. Infantry dropped in at 1 p.m. & we went out to Mena - had a look at Mena House, Pyramids, Sphinx & Temple, your 'umble acting as guide. We had a photo. taken on camels with Sphinx & Pyramids in the Background. Went back to Cairo & had tea at British Soldiers' Cafe - then went for a walk round the city (seamy side included).  Left the other chaps at the Heliopolis Tram & arrived back at Gezira at 8.15. p.m.
