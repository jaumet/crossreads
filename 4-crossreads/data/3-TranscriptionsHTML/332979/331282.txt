<p>and 62 Privates. After being inspected by the O.C. 15 Privates &amp; 2 Corporals were marched away to the Aft Boar Deck. As there are 5 Positions to guard it is done in 3 Shifts. The remainder was then marched upon the top Boat Deck above the Promenade Deck. As <strike>I was the</strike> the Unit forming the Guard was short of its strength ten of us was required to make up the Number &amp; as I was the Senior Non Com I was given the position of Corporal of the Guard in Charge of our number although we were scattered amongst the others I was given the post on the Port Side. As there are 3 Corporals on the Port Side, one <strike>in</strike> as a Stand By. The other two work 4 hour shifts. There are 9 positions made up as 3 on the <strike>[indecipherable]</strike> Forecastle Head &amp; 6 on the Port side of the Boat Deck. The Guard <strike>issued</strike> is on for 2 hours &amp; four Hours off. Their Duty is to stand to Attention with their Rifle which has 5 Rounds of Balls</p>
