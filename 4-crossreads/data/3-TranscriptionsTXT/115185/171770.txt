 2/ I was on duty &ndash; we leave here today. I don't suppose it matters in telling that for when you get this all importance of the news will be over - I don't suppose that letters to England will be censured. I have seen quite a lot of people &ndash; they come on board from time to time. I have not seen Julian as he is at Williams Haven.
