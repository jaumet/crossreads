The bty. has suffered heavy casualties and has been reinforced 19 times.  Sometimes almost complete renewal was necessary and not more than a dozen of the old hands could be mustered now.
In the first Somme attacks we advanced through La Boiselle and took a position in Bailif Wood - a few hundred yards in rear of the Infantry.  In about an hour the Huns found us and, turning several btys. on to ours shelled us so heavily that we were forced to retire (in the night) 500 yards to a more covered position.
