  June 1st  
 Sunday.&nbsp; Church Service on the &quot;Rio Pardo&quot;.&nbsp; Our Padre a very popular man. 
 The boat shows a daily increase in speed.&nbsp; We are now approaching the Canary Islands.&nbsp; The first instalment of my long &quot;English&quot; letter is written and it will be continued on subsequent Sundays.
