<p>Octobre 9<br/>Marched to Ypres a distance of about 6 miles. Had trouble finding places to camp in. Expect to start forward work tomorrow. Nasty cold weather with squally rain.</p>
<p>Octobre 10<br/>Working at West Hoek putting in shelters for the Infty. Heavy shelling but no casualties in Coy. Cold wet weather.</p>
