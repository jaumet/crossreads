Spent the day in packing up the remainder of our drugs &amp; going off to the Depot for a few odds &amp; ends.  Had a good drive on the old Ford.  At night had a final dinner at our late billet in the village as a send off.  The madame excelled herself in her cooking especially in preparing potatoes, specially mashed with egg etc &amp; baked in boiling fat.  Perfect. Monday 17th Sept.   Reveille 5.30, an early start in order to clean up camp.  Marched out at 9.30 passing through Wallen-cappell, St Sylvestre-cappell &amp; so on to "Steenvoorde" a very fair sized place, the march being about 9 miles &amp; very trying in parts as we  met roads with cobbles.
