<p>Sunday 10 June 1917.</p>
<p>On Isolation and have now charge of No 1 Troop Deck &amp; 14 C.B.F.A. boys.</p>
<p>Sea rather rough &amp; tonight rough weather is expected. Duties kept me from church parade.</p>
<p>Infectious cases still increasing</p>
<p>Wrote 1 letter this afternoon. Mail closes 6 p.m. Friday next.</p>
