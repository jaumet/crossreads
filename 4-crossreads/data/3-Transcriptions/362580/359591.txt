<p>No 3. Australian C.C<br/>[indecipherable] B.E.F<br/>France<br/>Jan 15th [1917]<br/>My dear Mrs. Thistlethwaite</p>
<p>Very many thanks for your card of remembrance for Xmas &ndash; it is so nice when so far from home to get these little things &ndash; You will see that Thistles &amp; I are together once more it is so nice after all this parting it is nearly twelve months since we last met this is</p>
