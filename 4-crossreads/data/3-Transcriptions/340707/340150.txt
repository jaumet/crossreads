<p>Here angel Gabriel appears at monks call or rather does not appear &amp; he has to go behind the scenes to find her. At second call she appears &amp; is more like a actress or tight rope dancer than angel. She revolves upside down &amp; in all positions &amp; then anyone in the audience is asked to represent Gabriel. One girl volunteer &amp; as she passes in the monk declares that there goes an angel almost [indecipherable] Everyone laughs &amp; badinage is rampant. This girl is given a pair of wings &amp; revolves around. Then two other tight clad angels balance on the moon.</p>
<p>Then applicants for job of angel or [indecipherable] monk are called for &amp; a soldier offers. The monk says he will not be able to work tomorrow &amp; his girl answers it does not matter as he is on strike (tho he is a soldier) &amp; so an angel appear alongside of him emptying gold into a basin at his elbow &amp; of being a reflection he is ignorant &amp; disregards. The monk [indecipherable] him for the loss of good opportunity to enrich himself. Then a group of beautiful angels appear &amp; then in same way he ignores [indecipherable] from outside appear a large pair of [indecipherable] to finish the performance. All laugh &amp; soldier return to his sweetheart</p>
