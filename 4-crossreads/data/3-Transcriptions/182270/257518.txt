
<p>FRIDAY, JULY 25, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine but cloudy and came on to</p>
<p>rain late at night. Had 27 points</p>
<p>Helped Peter mark the Strand lambs</p>
<p>in morning. They went 45%. Tickle</p>
<p>&amp; I took the Middle paddock mill down</p>
<p>&amp; the stand to pieces.</p>
<p>Darby down about 60 ft in the</p>
<p>bore still in hard sandstone.</p>
