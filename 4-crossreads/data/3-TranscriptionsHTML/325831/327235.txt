<p>3.<br/>Colonel Abbott had offered me a position on his brigade staff at Salisbury Plains but I was keen on getting on the Staff at the Front &amp; I did not take it on.</p>
<p>The Australians had a rough time down here during Xmas &ndash; They were working under great difficulties &ndash; The whole place was an ocean of mud &amp; travelling was very difficult. Of course it tended to break up the horses very much too.</p>
<p>Visiting the line on one occasion I slipped off the duck-board track &amp; got stuck fast in the mud unable to reach the duck boards again. I was fortunately rescued half an hour later by two engineers who happened to be passing.
