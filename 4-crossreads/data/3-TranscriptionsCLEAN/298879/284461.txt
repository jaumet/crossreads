  
 November 1915 Tuesday 2 Cold wind. artillery duel this morning & evening. Bombardment going on at Cape Helles 
 Wednesday 3 Fine   Grand day. Wrote a few letters. Had a look round the firing line. One man in B. Coy of our Bn. hit with a piece of a shell from a high explosive. He was taken to the Beach Hospital. Had a game of cards with some friends in the M. Gun Section.
