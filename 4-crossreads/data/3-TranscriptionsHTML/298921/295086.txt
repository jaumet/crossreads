<p>April, 1915<br/>Friday 23<br/>Crossed the Nile &amp; Canal twice 500 horses, saw &amp; took photos of Memphis Tombs of Osiris<br/>    "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   "  Ti<br/>    "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   "  Sacred [indecipherable]<br/>Statues of ramases II<br/>The horse jumped off Darapeyeta.<br/>The blind Mohommaden in the Temple opposite awaked us at dawn as normal calling the faithful to prayer.<br/>Tomb of Osiris is 150 feet in the earth &amp; the Paintings like at Luxor quite vivid &amp; not a bit faded tho' 3500 yrs B.C. The Sacred Bulls are in Black Granite Sarcophagi, of immense dimensions, floated from the Quarries of Asswan per Nile when in flood.</p>
