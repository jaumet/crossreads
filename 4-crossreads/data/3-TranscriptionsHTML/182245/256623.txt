<p>1917 APRIL</p>
<p>18 WEDNESDAY</p>
<p>Snowing in morning &amp;</p>
<p>raining the rest of the day. We</p>
<p>left Longuenesse at 10 AM</p>
<p>&amp; after passing through</p>
<p>&quot;Lumbres&quot; we arrived at</p>
<p>Quesques [(Kirk)?] about 7 pm</p>
<p>&amp; camped in a barn again</p>
<p>The country has altered</p>
<p>a lot here, has a lot of hills</p>
<p>&amp; is not very thickly populated</p>
<p>You cannot buy things</p>
<p>either like you can near</p>
<p>the line.</p>
