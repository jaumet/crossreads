<p>2PM trecked all night very tired<br/>Sunday Dec24.12.16 returned to alarach from the scene of battle 11AM had breakfast .very tired slept the remainder of the day<br/>Monday Dec 25.12.16 stand to 5AM very cold no firewood rations very short. Also camel food saddled up 10AM and went to beach about 2 miles away arrived 12AM had christmas dinner on beach at olrich returned to camp 4PM on picquet  very cold tonight<br/>Tuesday Dec 26.12.16 stand to 5AM very cold on [indecipherable] guard all day returned to camp 4PM raining to night every thing wet on Picquet to night wrote to Josie</p>
