5/2/19 Gourdinne
Carried out an inspection of H.Q. billets and was thereon engaged when the General arrived. With him discussed a lot of details it was decided that I assume command of the 23rd. too in about 3 days time. The Brigade will probably move to Marcinelle in about a fortnight. I had a good go in with the General on the question of getting our 1915 officers home he argued in a circle but eventually came round to our idea on the matter. After lunch I went over to the 6 FieldAm at Nalinnes walking through the woods. There I had a talk with Colonel Savage and arranged for Millett to be fixed. Snow was falling rather heavily and there was a cold raw wind that went right through clothes and everything. Coming home all
