
1915Oct. 17 Sun.Trotted off to the Armenian Scouts at 2 p.m. The attendance was 40 in 6 Patrols, 2 of which are ambulance, 1 signaller, 1 pioneer, 1 band & 1 ?Took the 2 amb. Patrols for first aid & we got along first rate. The Band consists of 6 buglers & 3 drummers & does well.This troop is run more on B.S.A. lines than any other Scouts I have seen in Cairo, English included.Went to Church (All Saints') at 6 p.m. with Mild. Brown. "Full House"
