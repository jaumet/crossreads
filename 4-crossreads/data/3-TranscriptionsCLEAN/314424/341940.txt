 Wednesday march 27. 1918. 1332 Revellie 2.20. Cold & frosty. 
 After a scanty breakfast consisting of tea & bully beef we pulled out of Authie at 4.20 and reached a place called Leicester Square in a forest, just near a village. Here we halted for 3 hours and had some hot tea & bread & jam. At 9.20 we were on the move again & passed through several villages travelling mostly in a Southern direction, through Puchevillers, Herissart & Contay & halted on the roadside at 2.p.m for dinner staying there until after 4 for the road was blocked.
