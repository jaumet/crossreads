<p>Starting out one morning about 2 o'clock we boarded troop trains that took us far north, disembarking us at Cassell. It is a very historic town in France. On the top of the Mount is a monument recording the fact of a battle that took place somewhere about the 14th century.</p>
<p>Right at the top too there is a pleasant tea shop patronised in peace by tourists and largely renowned. It is very ancient We were served with different foods The lady proprietor I think took account of the fact that we were soldiers</p>
<p>Making our way to the (top) town we were informed that some of the troops had run amok</p>
<p>Like most of the churches in France this one as very old</p>
