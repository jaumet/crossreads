 In the last round, the limit was reached. The little chap, little Scotty we call him was hanging onto the ropes with one hand & swinging the other one round like a windmill. About halfway through the round neither could see the other & they were rolling all round the hatch till the gong went. 
 Some of the other fights were very good & we had a good afternoon.
