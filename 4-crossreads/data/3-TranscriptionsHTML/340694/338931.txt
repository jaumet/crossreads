By this time the moon was well up &amp; the picture we cut would be more easily imagined than described. We had Coffee &amp; milk &amp; it was great also French bread &amp; issue butter &amp; biscuits, I don't remember ever enjoying a supper so much before. Jimmy Lean sprang a cigar each coming back &amp; the three of us seated in the front looked the real thing. We arrived back about 10 pm after a very enjoyable evening.</p>
