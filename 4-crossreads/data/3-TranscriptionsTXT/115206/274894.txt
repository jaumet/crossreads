 November 1918. Wednesday 20 
 Hosptl. Sthall Afta examination by Capt Pigott, I made a tour of workshops and hosptl surroundings with Roy. Ran out to see Eve and stayed all night with a Mr Harris &ndash; some friend of Roys. I am agreeably surprised to see agile manner in which Roy gets about despite his physical incapacity.
