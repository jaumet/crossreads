 Monday 2&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; About Hospital all day.&nbsp; Getting ready to send away a lot of men to Australia.&nbsp; Letters posted to Girls &amp; Mollie&nbsp; 689 patients here tonight&nbsp; 
 Tuesday 3&nbsp;August 1915 Sent 369 patients to Australia this morning, they were amenable to discipline and gave no trouble.&nbsp; Was in Cairo at 12 noon, interview with D.M.S. &amp; General Maxwell. Martin (L.Col.) had sent on my personal letter to Gen. Maxwell.&nbsp; Mainfold here during the afternoon.&nbsp; 
 Wednesday 4 August 1915&nbsp; In Cairo during the morning&nbsp; 116 patients admitted, from Alexandria and locally&nbsp;
