  
 June 1st  N.N.W.  M.301 Drill as usual.  Afternoon writing, slept over tea time, awoke at 'Sunset'.  Study, Band, Bath, Bed 8-30.  Weather fine & clear & hot.  Position noon N. of Laccadive Island.  Payday. 1 received.

 June 3rd  West.  323M. Physical Drill.  Parade Bt. Deck at 8am.  Celebration.
