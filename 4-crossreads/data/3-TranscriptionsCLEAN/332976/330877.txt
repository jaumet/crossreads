  
 June 1917 
 We paraded this morning in "fatigue dress" & marched to Aveloy, a small village about 6 Kms away, where we all had a swim, which was very welcome & well appreciated.   A large lagoon on the side of the River Anore affords a very suitable swimming basin & has been improved for that purpose by some of the lads who were in this section before us.
