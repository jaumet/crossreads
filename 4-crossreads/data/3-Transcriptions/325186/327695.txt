<p>moved out to a camp at Jesus Farm, named after a Statue of Christ that was close by. I still remained at Brigade Hd Qrs as a runner Christmas came round &amp; we had a big fall of snow in the Christmas morn The Battn. had a very rough Christmas dinner but at Brig. Hd Qrs we had a good one  The roads all round were covered in mud &amp; slush &amp; pushing a bike around with messages was no easy task  The period of spell being over we moved back to Armentiers to go into a sector of the line known as Houplines  The 36th Battn had relieved the <strike>38th</strike> 40th Battn</p>
