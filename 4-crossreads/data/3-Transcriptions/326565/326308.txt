<p>storm arose about 3.15 pm. &amp; spoilt remainder of the afternoon.<br/>Result of tennis &ndash; Large blisters on soles of feet, through having to play in socks; ruined pair of socks &amp; lost 5 piastre on game; nevertheless very enjoyable.<br/>Heavy rainstorm about 5 pm. Pay Day; Paid &pound; 1-10-9  - 150 piastre<br/>Third issue of tobacco from military authorities which I found very little difficulty to dispose of.</p>
