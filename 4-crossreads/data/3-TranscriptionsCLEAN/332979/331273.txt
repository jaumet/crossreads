There is a Railway of 400 Miles which runs through the Rubber & Coconut plantations, it is of a 2 ft 9" gauge & the Engines & Carriages are of a Small type. The rainfall averages about 130 inches per annum & the Rainy Season starts about April for 6 months & the Temperature very seldom drops below 86 degrees in the Winter.
