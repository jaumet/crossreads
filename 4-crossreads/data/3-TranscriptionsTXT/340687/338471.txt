 June, 1917. Saturday 16 Day hot &ndash; 6.30 parade &amp; fell in again at 9. And practised rafting all the morning. Until 1. In afternoon swimming parade &amp; I afterwards got a paper and read ME all evening. Concert in evening by D.E. disgraceful &amp; seen Nolan &amp; all officers drunk in front of French civilians &amp; Tommies
