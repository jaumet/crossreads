<p>2<br/>honour not to give any particulars of the troops here so cant tell any more.<br/>Saw Lynn Palmer with the 8th Field Amb yesterday, also Lance Addison from Hunter's Hill.<br/>When I see the Mail Boats pass this the Canal it makes me wish I were on one of them.<br/>Unless we were to be moved more into the desert we will be able to swim in the Canal &amp; although salt water is never as nice as fresh</p>
