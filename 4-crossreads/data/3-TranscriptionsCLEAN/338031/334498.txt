
January 16thEarly in the morning we marched to Wilton & entrained for London, noting as we steamed through, the great utilisation of formerly idle land for producing vegetables. It was fairly open country till we reached the environs of London & then with a sudden crash we right in waterloo station.We were lined up & marched over Westminster bridge to Horseferry Road.
