<p>Monday 23rd<br/>At 7 a.m. left Camp 3 to relieve "B" Section at the 9th Brigade Hosp. No. 2 Camp 19 (Artillery).<br/>I was clerk there and was kept going.</p>
<p>Tuesday 24th<br/><strike>Keep</strike> Kept going all day.  Received letters &amp; parcel of socks.</p>
<p>Wednesday 25th<br/>Was relieved at 10 a.m. by "A" Section and we proceeded to Camp 3 again.<br/>Cleaning harness afternoon.  Mail from home.</p>
<p>Thursday 26th<br/>Clean up in anticipation of leave tomorrow.</p>
<p>Friday 27th<br/>Although wet &amp; rain was about we left Camp at 9 a.m. and arrived in London about 1 p.m.  Thence round to Aldwych Hut with Corp. Macaulay and booked our beds etc. then dinner.  Walk with Mac down Strand and I had a photo taken at Chesneys Ltd. and we purchased several necessities also Christmas Cards.  Back to Aldwych for tea.<br/>Leaving Mac I went to Charing Cross Stn. but rain prevented me from walking about much.<br/>Had supper in the "Corner House" about 9 and then went for a run by tube to Upton Park &amp; back arriving Aldwych at midnight.</p>
<p>Saturday 28th<br/>Referendum Polling Day in Australia.<br/>Morning.  Stroll along Embankment with Mac, Harold Lees &amp; Ron Ponton and inspecting Cleopatra's Needle we pushed on to Parliament House.
