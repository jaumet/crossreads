<p>August,1918<br/>Thursday 8</p>
<p>Villers Brettoneaux and 1st Objective.<br/>Weather fair and slightly misty.<br/>Bombardment opened at 4.20 am and we left for an advanced position at 7.20 am<br/>The stunt was well expected and the final objective of the infantry was in their hands at midday. Plenty of work here today. Salvaged some good switchboards and phones. Also a clean shirt.<br/>Fritz downed a couple of our planes but we dropped 6 of his. Some very spectacular sights.<br/>Felt fairly tired at 4.45 pm when we turned in. expect a counter attack tonight.</p>
