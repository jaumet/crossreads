<p>Tuesday 27 February 1917</p>
<p>More Cavalry drill trotting about, &amp; what we should do if we had lances etc. &ndash; the old saying "if you want to learn how not to make money out of the Land go to an Agricultural College".</p>
<p>[The following sentence crossed through.]<br/>Letter card from May &amp; just heard of the evacuation of KUT by TURKS.</p>
<p>The S.S. "HUNTSEND" boat I came from England in has been torpedoed [indecipherable] in the Aegean Sea so I was lucky to arrive here safely &ndash; 2 LECTURES a day &amp; much Military LAW, this is soldering!!!  We've only been fighting.</p>
