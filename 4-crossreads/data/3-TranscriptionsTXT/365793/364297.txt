 Jly 16 A fellow cant work &amp; then listen to a Parson. Between the hours of 8 &amp; 11 pm this hut is the funniest I know. &ndash; an endless stream of wit &amp; humour, &amp; roars of laughter.  Somewhere about midnight a lonely cat curled up on my bed &amp; I got the shock of my life. Gave a devil of a heave;  that  cat hit the floor rather suddenly &amp; the old Doc' next door nearly fell out of bed with fright, and then a 'Lost-horseman' heaved puss out the back door &amp; all was peace and quietness again (JC.
