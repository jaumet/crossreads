<p>Sunday 24th March<br/>Holiday as usual got orders to get iron rations for a move saw relation in 17th Batt<br/>Monday 25th March<br/>Moved out got into Motor Lorries passed through several villages including Abelle got shelled got out of bus then marched to Metian<br/>Wednesday 27<br/>Got in train &amp; Gode Wareneselde left there &amp; night</p>
