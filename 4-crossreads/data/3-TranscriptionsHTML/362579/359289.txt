<p>Now for some news &ndash; We are promoted to Sisters now, so Dorothy, Kelly   &amp;  I are very delighted. Believe it begins from April 4th, so shall get 2/6 a day extra &ndash; not too bad.</p>
<p>I think I shall try   &amp;  put it into a Bank in London in case I need any while there.</p>
<p>Dorothy is awfully excited about it; of course it was time we got it, 2 years now On Active Service.</p>
<p>We have been a bit quieter for a few days, so I've had two days off-duty &ndash; had a sore throat,   &amp;  Col. C. did not want to let me go, so managed to give me a rest   &amp;  I'm quite alright now. Shall be on duty to-morrow.</p>
<p>24.4.17</p>
<p>Could not finish this yesterday, some visitors arrived   &amp;  I had to help entertain. Am on duty to-day   &amp;  feel splendid, my voice is better. These last 2 days have been beautiful sunshine   &amp;  much warmer. The Spring will be</p>
