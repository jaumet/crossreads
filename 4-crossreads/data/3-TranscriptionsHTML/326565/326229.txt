<p>Thursday 21/10/15<br/>General inspection by Col Tibbey.  Late leave at night, went to "The Dancing Mistress", Her Majesty's Theatre.  Had tea &amp; Supper at Boulevard Caf&eacute;.  Bob sang during tea.</p>
<p>Friday 21/10/15<br/>Left camp, 8 am, on Route March.  Marched to Flinders St, Caught train to Ringwood.  Arrived Warrandyte 8 miles for dinner,  Dined with Bob Roberts at Hotel.  Left Warrandyte about 2 pm in rain, arrived Kangaroo Gd about 3 miles further on &amp; Camped in hall for night.  Had a pleasant night in hall singing &amp; dancing, turned in about 10 pm</p>
