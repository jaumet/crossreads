<p>24.4.18<br/>Enemy artillery directed light harassing fire on roads in vicinity of Treux about 12 4.5's were dropped in valley in D.23.b. &amp; d. between 10.45 &amp; 11 p.m.  At 4.30 a.m. about twenty 4.5's dropped in same locality.  About 20 gas shells fell in T.5.b.60.70 between 3.30 &amp; 3.40 a.m.<br/>Enemy Machine Guns fired short bursts throughout the night.  Enemy Aircraft, Nil.</p>

<p>25.4.18<br/>Guns in new positions and improvement to positions proceeded with.<br/>The following targets were engaged at irregular intervals throughout the night &amp; morning, 1500 rounds being expended.<br/>(1)  Streets, Ville-Sur-Ancre.  <br/>(2)  Cross Roads K.1.b.48.55.  <br/>(3)  Hollow K.7.c.10.75.  <br/>(4)  Trenches K.7.c.10.75.  <br/>(5)  Sunken Road K.1.b. &amp; d.  <br/>(6)  Battery Position K.7.b.7.7.<br/>Enemy Artillery carried out harassing fire throughout night, more especially on roads</p>
