<p>Thursday, 18 January 1917</p>
<p>Men by Troops go to the Beach &amp; are <u>Deloused</u>!!! by the Army Medical detail. PANTS turned inside out brushed with Horse brush &amp; powdered. My Face is like the fat boy from Manly gone Septic but soon alright.</p>
<p>Surf here not too safe. A Doctor was drowned yesterday &amp; several Tommies. Nearly 2 years since I landed in Egypt first.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through]  Much GRAFT exists in the way of Promotions &amp; Decorations &amp; I am satisfied unless a man has someone behind him either wealthy or influential he sees not much chance altho' the Army is supposed to be so very democratic now. The Democrat dearly loves a [indecipherable].</p>
