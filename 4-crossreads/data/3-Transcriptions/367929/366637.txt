<p>June 1915<br/>12 Sat<br/>In valley bivouac. Penetrated gully up hill found a night outpost of the Turks &ndash; brought back some equipment curios.</p>
<p>13 Sun<br/>In valley bivouac.<br/>Padre Merrington and also padre Green held communion services opposite Regt Hdqu dugout. Latter was the 1st  had seen here &ndash; about 43 communicants.</p>
