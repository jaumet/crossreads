  
 And anyway it has not been offered me yet! 
 I should be sorry to miss seeing Damascus which I hear fell on the 3rd Oct. yielding 7000 prisoners which must be just about all now.  I don't see what is to stop them going right ahead until Turkey  throughs  throws it in. 
 Sept. 10th Still here & from all accounts & appearances likely to remain here for some two or three weeks. 
 We have moved camp up to the station about a mile east of Ammon. 
 This is an awful place.  It has been the Turkish camp, there are several dead turks buried - or I should say covered
