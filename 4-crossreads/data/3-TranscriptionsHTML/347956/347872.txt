<p>29.1.15</p>
<p>quantity of dust &amp; found that leading horses is not as easy as signalling<br/>had nothing to do this afternoon only get ready to move off in the morning for Heliopolis, went to the pictures after tea &amp; they were very good.<br/>Chasper Moore was told to-day that he has to go back to Aus. with a dozen others, as they are no good to the army.  27 men are being sent back from the Queenslanders.</p>
<p>Sat 30th<br/>Early revallie &amp; left Maadi at 9.30 &amp; had a very dusty ride through the dusty ruins until we came to the made roads near the Citadel &amp; there we had a fine trip from there on, we did not go through the principal streets</p>
