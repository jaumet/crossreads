<p>French Colonial troops here &amp; they have showy &amp; peculiar uniforms.</p>
<p>The country on the way down looked lovely for we had just had a heavy shower of rain &amp; it had freshened things up a lot Arrived at Bologne about 11.30 &amp; marched round to a big rest billet where we stopped the night.</p>
<p>9th Jock &amp; I had a good hot bath this morning &amp; just as we were enjoying it, an order came round</p>
