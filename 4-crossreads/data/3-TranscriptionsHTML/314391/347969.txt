<p>26/10/17 contd<br/>Observed all country N of Abda &ndash; no signs of enemy &ndash; many natives seen, one of whom reported twenty Turkish lancers at Ain Murra two days ago. Route very stony and severe on horses &ndash; Good plentiful water and easily developed at Ain Murra.</p>
