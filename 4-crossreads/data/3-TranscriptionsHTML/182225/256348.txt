<p>1916&nbsp;&nbsp; THURSDAY 17&nbsp;&nbsp; AUGUST</p>
<p>Showery all day. In some old sheds &amp; stables practising with the buzzer and disc signals all day. There was a little mail in from Australia, but I did not get any. We had a great old thunder storm at night. We had a lamp parade at night.</p>
