 Last night I could hear the trains whistling quite plain, well into Belgium they would be the German supply trains for this front 
 A very funny little incident occured last night quite close to my post, one of my sentries saw a man wandering about in "No mans land" & he immediately called me I spotted him at once & thought he was a German who had lost his way & in the peculiar light he looked quite close to us;  for it had been raining heavily & by this time it had ceased but a low lying thick mist
