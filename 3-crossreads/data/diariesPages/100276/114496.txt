27th. Dave Mason took a small patrol out last night but never saw any of the Hun's.
This morning I took an inventory of all our Posts bombs 303 ammunition. Our mortar battery landed 2 bombs almost in our trench this morning. I reported the matter to the Capt.
From where I am writing this I can see "Messines" hill quite plain it is a fine position for it overlooks the country for miles around. I think the "London Scottish" took it last year, about 200 yards behind me is the famous International trench so called on account of its being taken lost so many times by the British
