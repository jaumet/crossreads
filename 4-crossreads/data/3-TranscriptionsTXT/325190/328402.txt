 Wednesday 26th Sept. 17 Parkhouse again!! 
 I shall have to review the last six weeks rather briefly for the detailed diary of that period has been unfortunately lost. Left the 16th Field Amb on the morning after the successful concert about the beginning of Aug. &ndash; a Saturday - &amp; arrived at Perham Down.
