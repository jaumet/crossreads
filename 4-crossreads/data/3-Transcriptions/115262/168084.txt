<p>Tuesday 13 November 1917.</p>
<p>Moved off at dawn in a Nth-Nth Westerly direction till 10.30 am. Halted for a rest for camels &amp; a snack for ourselves.</p>
<p>3 miles now from line and our guns &amp; boys are making it hot for &quot;Jacko&quot; on this new front.</p>
<p>Camels watered &amp; fantasies filled. Turks held this position Esdud yesterday morning. <s>Left</s></p>
<p>We left at 3 p.m. and travelled till <s>dawn</s> dusk almost in sight of the village of Yebna.</p>
<p>&quot;Stand to&quot; all night.</p>
