 April 1918 Saturday 13 
 Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Dours.  D.R. Spent a farily quiet day.  Heard the bombardment for a Fritz hop over, and witnessed the usual fireworks. Weather wet and miserable. Fritz did a fair amount of bombing on Daours. He also shelled that region fairly heavily. Inspection and instruction on map reading. Reconnoitred bridges during afternoon and instructed the Bombadier in his alternative routes for necessitity
