<p>1917 Saturday 7 July</p>
<p>On duty all day. Patients much the same. Farrows people go home. He will get better I think. Taylor our worst case now bad wound in back &amp; displaced heart. Wife over to see him.  <br/>More air strikes on London. When will our men wake up</p>
