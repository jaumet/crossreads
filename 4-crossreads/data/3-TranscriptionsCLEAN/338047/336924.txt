For a hour or so we chiaked a French boy & some girls who came to have a look at us, but the troops finally got restless & toddled out to see what was in some of the trucks around us.  They soon returned with some vin rouge (the real mackay cobwebs on the bottle & all)  We had 24 bottles in our truck.  The next truck had a cask of cognac & the occupants soon became very merry.  Sunlight soap was the next article found, which was distributed.  The officers now began to take a hand, & rushed up & down the trucks trying to stop the thieving.  Our little fellow picked on me, & said "Guard this door" & rushed on.
