<p>16.2.15</p>
<p>&amp; go out with the squadron.  we had a fairly good day, with the exception of a charge we made, over sand, for about a mile &amp; we were all about knocked up, when we had the bayonet charge &amp; arrived back in camp at 4 oclock  On feeding party tonight  The military here, were paid this afternoon &amp; the beer is flowing free at the Canteen, also a bit of a scrap or two</p>
