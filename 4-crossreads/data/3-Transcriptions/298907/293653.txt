<p>where we were joined by 6 more men, &amp; we had a jolly fine time picknicing out on the open hillside on the green grass &amp; in the sun, the worst of it is though that we have to come in too early &amp; the girls had to do the same, they were not bad sorts &amp; bought everything themselves &amp; we spent rather a pleasant afternoon</p>
