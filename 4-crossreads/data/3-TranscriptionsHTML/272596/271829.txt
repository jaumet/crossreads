<p>Got my legs &amp; arms burnt dreadfully yesterday, &amp; tonight they are giving me something to think about I rubbed some "Hazeline Snow" into them hoping to steady the pain but so far little impression has been made</p>
<p>31st.  Have been very busy all the afternoon, took my tunic which I had washed a few days ago up on top &amp; sat in a nice breezy place &amp; sewed all sorts of things on.</p>
<p>There was a debate this afternoon on Prohibition or otherwise.  I never heard it but I believe it was very funny &amp; so were a lot of the argument they brought forth.</p>
