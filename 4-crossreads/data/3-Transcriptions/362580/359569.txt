<p>We are still having very delightful weather.  I'm feeling splendid but not going out very much now, resting a good deal &amp; have been busy trying to get my Xmas cards to Australia posted.</p>
<p>Our ward is getting busy now but still we are well staffed so I'm not working too hard.  Did I tell you we went to "The Kursaal" a vaudeville show, the other night &amp; quite enjoyed it.</p>
<p>On Saturday a party of us went out to see The Pyramids at Mena.  It was a beautiful afternoon and we all rode donkeys, right around the Sphinx &amp; Pyramids.  We saw the sunset, the picture was simply splendid and reflection of the Pyramids in the flooded water is the grandest thing you could imagine.
