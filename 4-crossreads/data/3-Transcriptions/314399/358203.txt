<p>Friday 25 July 1919<br/>Claires' Birthday</p>
<p>Many happy returns Kiddie. Had to wait at Swindon 3 hrs but got into the train &amp; slept. Arrived at Warminister at 7.30. Got a car to camp. At morning tea I met quite a crowd I knew. Was given a platoon this morning.<br/>Drew &pound;12  for voyage</p>
<p>Saturday 26 July 1919<br/>Parade as usual this morning. Nothing else of note happened all day 'cept in the evening some of the crowd got drunk &amp; kicked up a noise. Sent a cable home. Played bridge &amp; won 1/6.</p>
