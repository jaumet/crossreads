Had a little affair on Sunday which does not improve my comfort in the "Imperial" surroundings. Taff Jones of the 3rd came to see me &amp; was brought in to me by Jones of the "Mil. Police".  I volunteered to be responsible for him. When ready to go I took him out &amp; on the way show him the cells in which was a Turkish "Major", telling him his history, how he escaped from the Citadel &amp; had masqueraded previously as a Major, being only a Lieutenant.
