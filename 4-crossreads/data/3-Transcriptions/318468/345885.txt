<p>5-3-15.   An uneventful day   A few of our boys went ashore to have a look at the water supply.   A couple more transports came into the Harbor.   Things quiet.</p>
<p>6-3-15.   Same quiet &ndash; ship life   Tomorrow No. 1 Section is to go ashore &amp; camp for a week &ndash; fix up water supply troughs, etc:  for 9th Infantry Battln who go ashore today</p>
<p>7-3-15   We came ashore today bringing 7 days supply &amp; had a lot of work getting things ashore &amp; to our Camping ground.   It was quite dark before we had our Camp fixed up &amp; by the time we had our tea we were quite tired enough to turn in.<br/>No. 3 Section left at short notice for the Dardenelles to do some Demolition work   We all envy them getting away first but our turn will soon come</p>
<p>8-3-15   Last night was a wild night &ndash; or rather this morning    About 3 am a heavy thunder storm came up &amp; we had turn out &amp; dig trenches around our tents.   Some of us we sleeping</p>
