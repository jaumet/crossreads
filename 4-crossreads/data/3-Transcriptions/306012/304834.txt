<p>Sat. 2nd Feb. 1918<br/>We stayed six weeks at Cormont and had quite a decent time there.  For the first three weeks I was route marching, drilling etc., with the bearers but during the latter half of our rest I was collecting sick from various Units of the Division in surrounding villages and evacuating them daily to Etaples, Camiers and Boulogne.<br/>On Tuesday 29th Jan. we had reveille at 1 a.m. and marched out at 2.15 a.m.  After a ten kilometre march we arrived at Samer and boarded a troop train soon after.  We ran through Desvres, Hazebrouck &amp; Bailleul and detrained at Dekennabek Siding in Belgium.
