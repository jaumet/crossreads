 Yes! I knew that the hearts of you all  it  in that dear home, that was as my own, were with me in my grief &amp; are still. Dear Dene would have mourned her too for Mum-dear was very fond of him &amp; I can see her now as she put her arms round me &amp; we wept for the dear lads going &amp; for your families grief.
