
Oct. 11thI make a start to complete "The Xmas Carol" - Mail closed for several days - Hospital
Oct. 12th.Still a patient - Wild rumours concerning A.I.F.  - "Salonica" England etc. Letters & papers from Home.
Oct 13thMy eye is slowly improving - Gramophone in the Hospital. Pleasant memories awakened by "Humoreske" - "Cornish Floral Song". - More Home letters
