<p>stepping &amp; music, after this the whole crowd dispersed &amp; went to bed thoroughly tired out.<br/>Put the morning in drilling &amp; etc &amp; in the afternoon the Coy. were marched down to Hopoutre to get a bath but only cold water was on so very few had a wash, as soon as the men were out we collected them &amp; marched them home without our officers we knew this would not go unnoticed &amp; so it proved for this evening the Capt came round &amp; wanted to know why we did it &amp; etc, we told him because we thought it was the best thing to do rather than let the men stand about in the rain &amp; we had a good 5 miles to walk</p>
