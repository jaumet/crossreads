<p>On a hill opposite the camp is a badge worked by some Tommies. It won't be long before I start on one for us.</p>
<p>Friday 10th<br/>Weather still bad, &amp; wet. Slack day again, as all have sore arms. Huts fixed up and made cosy. All have a sort of trestle bed which is very springy and comfortable. There is also a stove in each hut, and electric light.</p>
