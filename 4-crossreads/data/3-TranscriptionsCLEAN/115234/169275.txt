 Start of Diary. - 1917 
 _____  "  _____  ____ 
 Arrived in Havre on Dec 6  th   - Humped our packs to Base, five miles out.  That night went into City & dipped our nose in foaming beakers & gazed on the wine while it was red:  Everything gay & bustling  The French, unlike the English, do no take their pleasures sadly.
