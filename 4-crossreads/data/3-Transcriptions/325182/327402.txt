<p>Mr. Farleigh continues to be bright and cheerful.</p>
<p>Mr. Farleigh is in a bed opposite to mine and recently had a relapse which sent him to bed with a temperature.  He is very chirpy notwithstanding and talks about the last man and the last shilling and mixes it up with "C" Companies cookers in his sleep.</p>
<p>Our nurses and sisters are all bright and cheery.  I cannot longer delay in mentioning the loss of our night nurse.  She failed to turn up on duty about three nights ago and I concluded that it was her night off, but she didn't</p>
