<p>It is fairly obvious that we shall strike the big advance right in the middle<br/>I don't feel particularly down in the dumps about the war, but I must admit that the Army has no attractions for me.<br/>I have not heard from Rita for weeks, but the U boats probably account for some letters from her.  Nevertheless I feel a little anxious.<br/>Weather improving just now &amp; the real Spring is apparently nearly on us</p>
