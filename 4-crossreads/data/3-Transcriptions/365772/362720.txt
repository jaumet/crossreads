<p>Southern Cross in fact half the firmament dropped down a couple of days after I wrote you, two of my oldest chums in the 2nd came out the same time, the three of us were in "G" Company together before.<br/>I tell you dear it makes a big difference you cannot realise the big change, in one thing, you have a batman to look after your welfare no worry &amp; trouble about food clothes etc a man</p>
