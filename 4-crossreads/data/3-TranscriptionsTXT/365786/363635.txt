We had  dinner  lunch in the dining car on the train &amp; arrived in Cairo about 5 p.m.  The distance is 148 miles &amp; the train takes 5 hours.   At Ismailiyeh the line branches off to Suez branches off.  In Cairo we went first to the Hotel Continental &amp; here we stayed for 4 weeks, but after that we were lucky to get a nice flat  with attendance &amp; food provided inclusive  &amp; in this we were freer &amp; more comfortable than at the Hotel besides being about &frac12; the price.
