Sure to be plenty of stoush anyway.<br/>The railway embankment has now reached El Arish, where it will probably stop until they bridge the wadi.<br/>A trawler ran ashore the other night &amp; is now lying on her side in a big surf.  Would make an excellent photo if the sun would come out.  The wind has been cold as charity lately &amp; I am seriously considering the advisability of two shirts.</p>
<p>8 p.m., Jan. 7th<br/>Thank goodness, the weather has broken &amp; now we have had two perfect days with sunshine &ndash; and Taubes.
