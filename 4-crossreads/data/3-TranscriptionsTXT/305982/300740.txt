 Piccadilly Circus etc. 
 20/10/16 Queens Hall last night  Glorious music.  am having a look around again to Buckingham palace saw guards change there very poor performance.  Grenadier Guards Band etc  Hyde Park.  Horses there after Trocodero Grill Room for dinner.  very chilly today here 
 21.10.16 Trafalgar day to day.  Nelsons monument great turnout  Paid a visit to National Art Gallery St Pauls Cathedral. Tower of London  Entrance to the city Lord Mayor of London St Michaels.  roamed about the strand all afternoon. 
 22/10/16 Went to Pedecoat lane this morning &amp; funny sights  got the 12 oclock train for Weymouth arrived there 5 oclock after a slow journey  raining like so &amp; so 
 23/10/16 Parcel from Mrs Lillie  Fred Matthews (Les Bowler) Fullerstons lollies &amp; whole lot of letters. Photo from home Parades as usual 
 24/10/16 Doctor called me out from the ranks &amp; had a good look at my shoulders
