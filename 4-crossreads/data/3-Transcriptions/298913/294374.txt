<p>The men are remarkably well behaved &amp; yet this Kultur is enforced, just for the sake of something to do. There are too many officers that are absolutely incompetent. Rifles are issued &amp; then retaken. Spurs to be worn and such &amp; such a parade &ndash; dismounted parade too, that shows the absurdity.<br/>So on goes the marvellous military machine, that is skited about by all the Australia ministers, poets, cricketers, MPS &amp; so on. Each one gives his opinion &amp; it is published in the press, as if they knew anything about it, I guarantee that if they enlisted for a while, their opinions would alter.
