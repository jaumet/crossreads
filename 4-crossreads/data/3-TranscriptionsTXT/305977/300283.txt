
November 1915to their own trenches than ours.  I received a letter from mother also letters from Kate, Roy, Will Douglas & Dorothy Blair during the week.  On Saturday Jean McCarthy & I went to see if we could find Capt Edgley but found he had gone away sick, broken down & they said he would be invalided back to Australia.  We were very sorry to hear it  However by good fortune we managed to meet Arthur Wright, but only had about 1/2 an hour to yarn with him.  He looks well & I was glad to see he had come through alright so far.  Alf Outridge, he informed me had been sent to England.
