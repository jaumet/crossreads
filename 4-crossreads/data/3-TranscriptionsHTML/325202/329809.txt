<p>close by &amp; we are billetted in some empty barns.</p>
<p>Have just walked down to see a batch of German Prisoners who have just been brought in &ndash; about 200 behind barbed wire of course. A smattering of old men &amp; boys but the majority are solid lumps of young men.</p>
<p>July 19 Wednesday<br/>We got immediate marching orders at 4 p.m. and within an hour were on the road we soon struck the main road &amp; joined the mass of traffic proceeding towards Albert about 5 miles away.</p>
<p>I have never seen such a mass of material &amp; men, either side of the road was just one series of bivouacs</p>
