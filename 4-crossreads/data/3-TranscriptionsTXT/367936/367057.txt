
Thursday 19 October 1916Wrote to Mother (21).Got parcel censored to send home.Went into town with Aire and West and out to Mex in tram for a drive.  Belview in evening.  Spoke to British Consul.
Friday 20 October 1916Fell in early and left Sidi Bishr for Kantara.Corn crops look lovely all the way.Got to camp about 6 o'clock in evening.
