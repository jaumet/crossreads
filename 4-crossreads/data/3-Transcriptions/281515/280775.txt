<p>Oct 10th<br/>There is talk of a mail coming soon.  we now &amp; again hear some war news which is very soon devoured as we are hungry for news have had no letters from England as yet, perhaps all letters stopped by censor</p>
<p>Oct 11th<br/>Fever breaks out &amp; a lot of men get sick  Malaria is the fever which I have had before in Crete not very pleasant either</p>
<p>Oct 12th<br/> Feel all right myself although lots look seedy</p>
<p>Oct 13th<br/>Mail arrives get loan of some newspapers &amp; have a good read of all the news</p>
