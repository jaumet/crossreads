 17 January, Friday 
 F Go to Bulford by Car &amp; go to Dick's Room all night. We yarn &amp; sup &amp; go to mess. Afterwards I play Billiards a bit yarn with all the old Boys. We have a lovely walk Home in the moonlight. Recd letter from Manly Predday. 
 Send telegram to Auntie. 
 18 January Saturday 
 F Lovely day, we got to Bulford by Car so to mess &amp; Dick's room. E goes to Gibbys with Tap. I go to YWCA &amp; Post Office. So to meet Hilda &amp; Corrie &amp; get scaled. So back to Dance &amp; Dance with Dick Dolly Moore who I squeez &amp; also Dorrie Webster. We set out &amp; walk home. I laugh at Billy &amp; Ellis sliding on the frosty hill side.
