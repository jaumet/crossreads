<p>1915<br/>Mar. 14 Sun.<br/>Attended Communion Service in Mena House Chapel at 6.45 a.m.<br/>In the afternoon attended gathering of 29 present &amp; intending members of the Aust. Sc. Assn. when a group photo was taken at the Sphinx.<br/>After tea did A.S.A. Secretarial work.</p>
