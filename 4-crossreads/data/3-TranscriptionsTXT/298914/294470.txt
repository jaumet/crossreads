Very cold here and misty.24th1am draw up in valley and draw rations.  2am proceed and arrive Jerus as the bells strike 3 am.  Bitterly cold wet & muddy.  Everyone frozen stiff.  Making the pace - a welter passing.  Calvary Damascus Gate & thence via Jaffa gate.  Arrive Bethlehem 5 am wet and muddy.  Have a sup of tea and at 8 am turn in.25thPack up ready to leave and at last min orders cancelled.26th leave (Jerusalem via) Bethlehem 10 am and take a stony road via Hebron arriving Zacharias 4 PM Camp overnight.27thLeave 9 am and continue past Junction Station and through AKIR and on to Deiran.5/3/18JC Tetson to Cairo on leave.8/3/18Practice stunt to Yebra, all aboard the carts.  A bum turn out.11/3/189 CS backsheesh arrive.  W5 move out to NZMR.  HQ & I move over to AIF cable.12/3/18Move out and camp at Lavron.3/3/18On to Jerusalem arriving 1700 and camp in Section Camp.14/3/18Cold bleak day.15/3/18Blowing like hell and cold.
