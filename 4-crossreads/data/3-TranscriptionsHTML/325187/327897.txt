<p>[Saturday 3 June 1916]</p>
<p>No Bombs<br/>We are getting fresh frozen Australian mutton now, but packed on a Camel, in the sun &amp; very often a Camel with skin disease does not make it more enticing, but a change from Bully.<br/>The YMCA is still with us a great institution but their Tent is a rather good sighter for aeroplanes.<br/>Being near the sea, everything steel rusts &amp; the sand knocks the devil out of Razors etc:-<br/>40 Horses still missing but of 1st Regiment.<br/><strike>Will post this with love, How the weeks go bye-</strike></p>
