 Jan 16.  Reported Tower.  Wasted morning, but got lists of gear to bring, both Artic &amp; Tropical outfits included.  Where on earth are we off to with summer &amp;  artic outfits and told to bring a two years supply.  Got us all thinking Russia, East Africa or North India. 
 Went to theatre with Grace tonight.  Dear  [indecipherable] Jan17.  Saw my old boss Mr. Maclellan today.  Took me to Simpsons for dinner, Drury Lane at night, a good day.
