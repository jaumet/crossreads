25th Tuesday
Put tents up had drill all day; several heavy rain storms; slept in tents.
26th Wednesday
Struck tents about 7 a.m. and with full pack etc. set off for 1st Line of Defence some 8 miles east of Canal. On journey many dropped out so had to remain with them followed later as able on Donkeys commandeered
