<p>23-12-16<br/>stopped firing in the direction of the Ambulance. The Turkish Artillery was in positions in the steep hills at the South of the Camp &amp; out of range from where we were they kept up a constant fire on the troops attacking the redoubts but did little or no damage. At 3 oclock C Sqdn was sent out to attack the Redoubts from the back, but they did not fire a shot as both Redoubts surrended as they came into position. C Sqdn with both Col Granville &amp; Major Smith pushed on South, with a Sqdn from each of the other Regts to secure the guns in the hills,  these they captured trying to get away; Prisoners (176) &amp; 3 officers) came up <strike>to</strike> under escort at 4 oclock &amp; at 6 oclock the remainder</p>
