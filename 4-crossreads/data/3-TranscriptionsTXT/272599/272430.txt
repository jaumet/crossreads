 1917 Saturday 9 June 
 Stretcher bearing today. Evacuations to Blighty.  After tea go into Boulogne. Visit Eng. tea shop. long yarn to [blank]   Home by convoy of Fritz wounded  Some of mid-age boys making themselves nuisances, they are not worth their salt here or up the line  Got 2nd Field punishments meted out to them
