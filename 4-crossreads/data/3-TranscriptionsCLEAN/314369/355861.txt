  
 20 July 1915 Tuesday 
 At work today on the water works splendid advancements being made Weather very hot   &  Sultry We "Stand to arms" at 3 a.m. The period of the Mohamedan Ramadan is at present in force   &  our people are expecting trouble. 
 1915 July 21 Wednesday 
 Terracing again The valley is nearly finished now   & we expect to start in another part next shift Turks fire twenty shells at our aeroplane without getting near it
