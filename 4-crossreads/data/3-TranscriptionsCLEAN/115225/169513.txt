Dec 26th  We went out on supplies as usual the snow from last night stayed on the ground & it was squally again to-day, with sunshine in between whiles. Jim stayed the night with his brother in the amunition section at La Creche
 
Dec 27th  I picked Jim up on the way to Steenwerck for supplies - We went back for coal for the brigade & delivered same  We had some whisky at the 18th canteen & went back to Park feeling very pleased with ourselves & looking thro at the world through rose-coloured glasses - We stayed in the lorry all the evening & with the primus kept good & warm. We have an issue of rum every night which we use as a night cap -
