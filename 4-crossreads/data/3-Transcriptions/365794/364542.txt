<p>Monday 20th May 1917 [1918]<br/>Rose 5.15 a.m.  Went H.C. in chapel 6.15 a.m., only sister present &amp; 5 Tommies &ndash; quiet &amp; peaceful service.<br/>No further information re Salonica save that Capt. Walker on being asked if will pass me says, Oh yes, you will be fit to go in 3 wks.<br/>Last day at 19th G.H. &amp; we are well cared for.<br/>Hosp. over 2000 beds run in 2 sections, this German building surgical &amp; officers &amp; sisters, "schools" medical.</p>
