<p>We are billetted in a French village at present in fairly good quarters, good for billets anyway.  This country is something like England only that it is very much more delapidated in fact all the villages seem to be more or less falling to pieces for want of repair &amp; the only industry is a bit of farming done by woman, old men &amp; men unfit for war.
