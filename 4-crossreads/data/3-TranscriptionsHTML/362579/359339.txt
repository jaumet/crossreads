<p>C/O Major Cunningham.<br/>Aust. Base. P. Office.<br/>Horseferry Rd.<br/>London.<br/>8.7.17</p>
<p>My dearest Moth.   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>Have not received any Aust. Mail this week, but suppose shall get it in due time.</p>
<p>Well, we have been very bush packing &ndash; all our patients have gone   &amp;   Tents down, the place looks miserable but for a change are not rushing it. Waiting now for moving orders, which may come through any moment now.</p>
