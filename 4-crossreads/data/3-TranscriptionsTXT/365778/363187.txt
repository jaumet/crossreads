Friday 6.4.17Good Friday snowing heavily, went to Eltham with Mr Gray &amp; played Billiards.Saturday 7/4/17Lovely spring day, went again to Eltham &amp; then returned to London at about 6 pm. Had dinner with Sir Thomas &amp; Lady Robinson from Qld.Sunday 8/4/17Met "George" Smith went to The Tower with him, had Albert Hall concert, afternoon tea &amp; Trocadero &amp; went to picture show.Monday 9/4/17Went to Horseferry Rd for 1 day's extra leave, went to Cheereo with George in afternoon. Zig Zag at night. [At Hippodrome Theatre]
