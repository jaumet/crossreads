
Thursday  3/5/17
Nice day - fairly warm. Drilling all day. Had a tug of war and a game of football at night. We hear our Boys are being counter attacked very fiercely by Fritz and nearly all open fighting - Got letters from Aunty Lyons - Gert & Kit Nock.
Friday  4/5/17
Nice sunny day - Semi finals of the Divisional sports - 15th F. Amb. Well represented & scoring well. On Picquet all day.
Saturday  5/5/17
Hot - dusty day. Divisional Sports continued some very fine performances put up by the Boys - running & jumping. Turned very windy and cold after tea - Got orders to march out at 10/30 pm - motor cars picked us up, run us over to "Veaux", where we arrived about 2-30 am - were told that we would hardly be called out till daylight, so we tried to have a camp under an awning, but it was that dusty windy & cold - one couldn't sleep.
