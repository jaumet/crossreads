<p>19 April 1918<br/>Friday<br/>Just the same old tale of camp life.</p>
<p>Went to concert at night &ndash; which was very good. The Troupe was called <strike>&amp; 3 all</strike></p>
<p>A nice bright show weather still very cold</p>
<p>1918 April 20<br/>Saturday<br/>Took over No 8 Canteen again today. Things very quiet no news of much importance from France.</p>
