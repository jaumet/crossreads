The show was good.<br/><u>Thursday 15th July 1915.</u>  Left dry dock 9.30am back to mrg[?]  About 4p Purser and Self went ashore.  I called in at Harveys &amp; had Wireless gold cuffs fitted on my uniform.  It is rather embarrassing wearing uniform in the City especially Transport officers.  We wear RN buttons &amp; a crown on our Commonwealth flag badge on the Cap.  The Wireless Cuffs are the same as RNR cuffs &amp; carrying a stick one is often mistaken for a Naval Officer &amp; soldiers and sailors draw themselves to</p>
