<p>4<br/>Didn't Elsie expect to present you with a niece or nephew by Xmas.</p>
<p>Poor old Ol she will scratch Elsie's eyes out.</p>
<p>Heaps of good wishes Waggy. Look after yourself and let some chaps who haven't been out at all yet go to Adjutant or Q.M. or the like.</p>
<p>Lovingly yrs<br/>Rid</p>
