  
 Spt 5  several games of deck quoits. Plenty of birds still following us. Fairly heavy rain this morning & last night The Sisters are playing the boys cricket, & judging by the laughter it's a dead funny game.  One  Sister holds the bat in the appropriate style, & puts in some pretty work too, but most of them get well away from the bat, & make wild hits at the atmosphere. Only 295 yesty; think the skipper must be economising in coal. Now & again an extra large wave heaves the propeller out
