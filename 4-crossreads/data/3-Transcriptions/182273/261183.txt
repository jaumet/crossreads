
<p>1918&nbsp;&nbsp; November</p>
<p>21 THUR&nbsp; Moved off 8 am to Boutillerie.</p>
<p>went through Amiens</p>
<p>City tr&egrave;s bon resuming pre-evac</p>
<p>conditions. saw many</p>
<p>repatriated French prisoners.</p>
<p>condition very bad. camped</p>
<p>in Barracks. Btys arr in</p>
<p>the dark. lost. Very cold.</p>
<p>All in Airaines took our dep. very hard</p>
<p>22 FRI&nbsp; Moved off at 8.am. Frost. went</p>
<p>via Amiens to Behencourt. had a</p>
<p>good time. went on to Cappy via</p>
<p>Bray arr: in dark. very cold.</p>
<p>all hands growling.</p>
<p>23 SAT&nbsp; Heavy frost. Moved off 9 am</p>
<p>via Peronne to Hamelet. no</p>
<p>billets slept out in the</p>
<p>open. very cold. arrived in</p>
<p>camp 3.30 PM</p>
