<p>1919<br/>May 29<br/>dinner we went across the river to Eton College, founded in 1449 by Henry VI.  We were shown into some of the ancient class rooms with (apparently) the original desks &ndash; Talk about T.K.S. desks being carved about.  500 years of boys knives had left precious little of these desks to write on, the tops of them were so scored.  We went upstairs to a place where every boy at the school is allowed to have his name cut for him on a panel.
