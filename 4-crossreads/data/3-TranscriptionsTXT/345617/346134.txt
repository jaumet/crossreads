
Juillet 31Arrived at 'Arques' at about 4a.m. and left the station about 6a.m. Marched to Ebblinghem about 6 miles arriving there about 8a.m.Arques is a fair sized village & appears to be mostly formed by Estaminets & Cafes. Ebblinghem is a very small place. "We are billeted at a farm"
Aout 1No Coy parade as the waether is very wet. Sec. [Section] Parades in billets. There is a great scarcity of firewood & we have trouble to find enough to cook the rations which are not too plentiful either.
