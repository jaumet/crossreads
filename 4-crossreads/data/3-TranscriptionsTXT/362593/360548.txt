 Thursday 2 May 1918 Recco, Lt. Maugham, in air 3 h. Had a splendid scrap with 3 Huns, drove two of them down but could not see definite results owing to low clouds, fired 450 rounds. 
 Friday 3 May 1918 [indecipherable] Walker killed &amp; Williams from 142 Sqdn. Recco.  Pilot Lt. Stooke.  In air 2 h. 35 m., great doings since the stunt is on, the L.H. in bad position. Machine gunned a regiment of Turks and gave them Hell from 1000 ft., fired 600 rounds.
