 Dunday 6 February, 1916 
 Tucker getting very scarce. 1/4 th  tins of jam &amp; 1/6 th  of a tin of bully per man with biscuits &amp; about 1/4 lb of meat for one day. 
 An aeroplane flew over the camp this morning. She was a big British machine &amp; was flying very low, evidently scouting for Turks.
