  
 Dec 24th Xmas eve. It started to rain to-day after lunch & kept up during the night. What a miserable hole of a country, where it rains about three times a year &  must spoil our small chance of enjoyment at this time of year. It did not deter us however from a good time. Such a night I will not soon forget. I was at the Postal Corps mess, where, tables were spread &  we ate, drank &  sang till 12 pm. Had some fine musicians, English Tommies, one French soldier & two French sailors  So, kept up the old traditions in Royal Style.
