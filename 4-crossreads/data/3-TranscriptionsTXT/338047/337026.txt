 1919 May very excited, but calmed down when I assured him that all Australians could swim.  They were pulled in to shore, &amp; one who had been hanging on to the bottom of the boat explained that he could not swim a stroke.  I walked away from my Sheffield  friend.  Jim Copeland became very interested in tennis.  There were public Courts at the end of the promenade, &amp; we used to go there when cash was short to watch the play.
