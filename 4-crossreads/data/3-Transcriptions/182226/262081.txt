<p>Monday 28 February, 1916</p>
<p>We are to have the machine guns of the Brigade, are going to be formed into one company. Lewis guns will take the place of the maxims in the Battalions [indecipherable] &amp;[?]&nbsp;&nbsp;have taken on the job of scouts. We will be on Headquarters staff &amp; big kits[?]. This change takes place from the 1<sup>st</sup> March.</p>
