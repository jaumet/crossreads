
B Troop A Squadron 1st A.L.H. Regt.Romani24th Sept.16
Mrs. VernonHon.Sec.1st. Light Horse Comforts FundMoore StreetSydney
Dear Mrs VernonI am writing to acknowledge with thanks the receipt of a parcel of 21 shirts, addressed to me. I distributed them among the boys and I can assure you they were highly appreciated. most of the other troops received similar parcels a few days after mine. Thanking you and your fellow workersWith kind regardsYours TrulyNJMEdwards. Lt.O.C. "B" Troop
