<p>28.5.15<br/>Nice &amp; fresh this morning makes one feel like being in Sydney again or the Harbours we passed &amp; Island about five oclock &amp; cloud was incircles around same it was very pretty. We are still at sea. There was a Glorious sunset this afternoon we were travelling due north. We <strike>sha</strike> saw a Grecan Destroyer this evening travelling full speed we were escorted by sea to Lemnos Island where we are going to land from what I hear</p>
