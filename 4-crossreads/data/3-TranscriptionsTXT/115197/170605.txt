 Shopping in town. 
 Return to Elm-Bank. 
 Evening spent in making up parcels, writing letters etc. 
  April 25th  
 &quot;Anzac Day&quot; - Anniversary of the Glorious Gallipoli Landing - Australians march in the City.&nbsp; Decorations, speeches etc.&nbsp; Shopping.&nbsp; Final business. 
 Farewell to Mr &amp; Mrs Harold Scales &amp; the children.
