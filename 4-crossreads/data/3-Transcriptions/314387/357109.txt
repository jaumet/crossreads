<p>July 24th<br/>Same as day before patrol &amp; night.<br/>July 25th<br/>Same again went out in No Mans Land looking for body of dead German for indentification, relieved midnight by 2nd London Fusiliers. Marched back to Ballarat line. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;July 26th<br/>In trenches all day nightime went up to front line carrying stoles mortars bombs for hop over 8th - 14th Brigades hopped over midnight look 600 yards casualities reported light - best mate killed.</p>
