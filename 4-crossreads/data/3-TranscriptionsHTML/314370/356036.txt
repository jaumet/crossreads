<p>June, 1916<br/>Friday 2<br/>Final run over stripping &amp; description &amp; getting exam papers ready.<br/>Go for a walk into Zeitoun after tea.  The cool breeze is very refreshing after the hot sweaty day.</p>
<p>Saturday 3<br/>Description Exam takes up all morning, my little class do very well.<br/>After dinner I went out to Patti's spending a very pleasant afternoon &amp; evening, returning at 12 pm.</p>
