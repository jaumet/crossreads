<p>[Saturday 17 June 1916]<br/>We have been turning night into Day so much lately I've rather lost the thread of things anyway we are back in Camp once more very tired &amp; very sleepy<br/><strike>Letter from May dated April 30th telling me of the Insurance work, poor old girl I'm sorry can't help her more. </strike> Among some Tobacco &amp; Cigarettes issued to me I found a Card from W Kilpatrick  Cornallas  Peter Pan Cigarettes No doubt about it the folk at home are good to Thomas Atkins &amp; it is appreciated-</p>
