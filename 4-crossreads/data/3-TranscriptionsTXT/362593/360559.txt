 Wednesday 5 June 1918 
 Thursday 6 June 1918 H.A.P.  Pilot Lt. Addison, in air 2 h. 10 m., forced landing at Jericho.  103rd A.A. Section took us in for the night, a terrible hot night.  Jordan valley is Hell. Took off with Lt. Burton in the 2E, had to return to Jericho.  In air 35 m.  One of the worst trips I have ever experienced, very releaved to get down.  Addie arrives back here.
