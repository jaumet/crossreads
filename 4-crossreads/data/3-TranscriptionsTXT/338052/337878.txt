 [4 March] 7.am.  Arrived on Island of Lemnos.  Anchored in small Harbour off PORT MUDROS NAVAL BASE in company with British    &amp;  French fleet M.S  Blenheim, Grafton, Triumph, Prince George, "Beure FRENCH, Dartmouth, ALBION with Submarines    &amp;  Destroyers Very fine weather    &amp;  beautiful panorama. 
 [5 March] Remainder of convoy, "detached force" arrived during afternoon. awaiting orders for disembarking 
 [6 March] 2 "Castle line" transports with R.M.L.I.arrive Went on board "IONIAN" for ORDERS. posted . A.S.P.C. to Win    &amp;  home also letter to Win. 
 [7 March] CHURCH.PARADE. afternoon. landed with C.Coy ashore for exercise.  Marched thro' [Talikra]
