<p>31 Th May<br/>Nothing doing on the front.  Aerial activity  no sign of a move  must be waiting for Jacko to attack</p>
<p>1 Fri June<br/>General visited line 15 South of Fara in sight of Beersheba  great activity in air one Taube forced down</p>
<p>2 Sat June<br/>Artillery duel through out the day.  Taube forced down at Rafa  machine intact also pilot &amp; observer  our chaps after Jacko when he shows out</p>
