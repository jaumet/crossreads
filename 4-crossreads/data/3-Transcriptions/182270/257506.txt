
<p>SUNDAY, JULY 13, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Went down to the Rocks</p>
<p>with Tickle at day light. He got two</p>
<p>foxes in Mountain &amp; one in the Rocks</p>
<p>&amp; I got one in the Rocks.</p>
<p>Took delivery of 269 X bred ewes</p>
<p>and 160 X bred wethers from A Jones</p>
<p>on a/c&nbsp;Barney Perkins. Put ewes</p>
<p>in Cow paddock &amp; wethers in 4D.</p>
