<p>Yesterday afternoon 8 of our chaps were wounded by the premature explosion of a bomb, but luckily no-one was killed, they were practicing at the bombing school<br/>They are feeding us fairly well here a sample of the rations would be<br/>1 loaf of bread between 3 men 1 tin jam for 5 men every other morning<br/>1 lb butter between 12 men a fair slice of cheese &amp; sometimes a few tins of bully, while 3 times a week we have porridge &amp; bacon, for breakfast one of these every other morning<br/>for dinner we normally have a pretty good stew with plenty of vegetables in it, or rice &amp; rasins or prune's we have plenty of tea twice daily that is for breakfast &amp; tea, &amp; now &amp; again sauce &amp; pickles.</p>
