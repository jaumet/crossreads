 June. 11th.  After slow voyage we arrived inside Sydney heads at 7 a.m. &amp; there I saw the best sight of my travells.  The beauty of the harbour improves after nearly four years absence.  Disembark at Wooloomooloo &amp; conveyed to Anzac Buffet Domain, where I met friends &amp; at 2.30 p.m. was in possession of my discharge, dated Aug 3.
