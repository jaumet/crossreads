<p>18/11/16<br/>Marched from Frimont to St Saveur. This is the best town we have ever been billetted in.</p>
<p>1/12/16<br/>Left St Saveur at 2 a.m. and marched to Vignacourt. Entrained here and proceeded to Buire. Marched back to Ribemont about four kilos. We expect to remain here several days.</p>
