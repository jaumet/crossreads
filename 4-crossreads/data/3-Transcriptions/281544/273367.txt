<p>stove, &amp; then off we went through new Calai, as it was after Six a large number of People were walking about we kept going until we went over the Canal when I first met a Military Police &amp; asked him to direct us to where we could obtain Breakfast. After showing him our Pass he directed us &amp; soon we were inside a Restaurant, which was nice &amp; warm, &amp; after obtaining a Wash we sat down at a Table &amp; we both had Fried Eggs &amp; Coffee &amp; Rum, which was very acceptable &amp; after paying 4&frac12; Franc's off we went to have a look around, we walked up to New Calai &amp; back again when we discovered the Church Army Hut &amp; in we went for a rest &amp; a hot drink of Coffee, I was asked by the Officer there to give him a hand to unload some Cases into a storeroom &amp; after assisting him I came back &amp; stopped there until lunch time when we both went up the street looking for a Caf&eacute;, we came across a likely one &amp; soon was seated inside &amp; ordered steak &amp; Onions &amp; Chip Potatoes for 2&frac14; Francs <strike>the</strike> each.
