 Wednesday 16 January Weather very boisterous this morning but died down this afternoon &amp; turned out sunny. A fatigue trip to Lindenhoek near Kemmel. 
 Thursday 17 January An "evening in Egypt" with a lecturer at Y.M.C.A. who gave us a lantern lecture on Egypt, Cairo &amp; surroundings prominent. Weather still bad, wet and snowing.
