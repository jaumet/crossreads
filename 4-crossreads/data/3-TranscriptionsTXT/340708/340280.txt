
August 1917Monday 27thStill wet & rough.Tuesday 28thIt cleared up for a bit this morning but came on worse this afternoon.I am getting better now   I don't think I will have to go under an operation after allThursday 30thWeather still rough.Friday 31Fritz paid us the first visit for a week owing to bad weather.Saturday Sept. 1stI am pretty right again did a bit of work the first for 3 weeksSunday 2ndShifted back to Hazebrouck this morning & then on to Wiskque this afternoon they are building it is well out in the country so we should be safe from airraids.
