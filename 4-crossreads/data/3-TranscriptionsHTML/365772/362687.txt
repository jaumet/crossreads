<p>Tel el Kebir, Egypt<br/>4.1.16<br/>My dearest Sister,<br/>As you will notice by the heading I am again in the desert land, this time as far away from Cairo as they can get us &amp; only a native village near by. The name is no doubt familiar to you on account of the battle of "Tel-el-Kebir" which Wolsely fought with the arabs here in the "eighties". The only thing of interest at all is the graveyard where the British troops were buried &amp; that is a pretty gloomy thing to go sight seeing.<br/>I received a couple of letters from you &amp; some more from the rest of the family yesterday, which has been waiting for us for a few weeks, your "overflow" parcel turned up alright &amp; will</p>
