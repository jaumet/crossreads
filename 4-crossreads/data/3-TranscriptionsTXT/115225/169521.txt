Jan 21st  I did'nt like too well the place I was staying & I met a couple of fellows out of 31st Canadian Battalion in the same way so I went with them to Hotel D'Egypte, they took a double room between them & I took a single room. they were nice rooms & nicely furnished in the most central position two hundred yards from the opera. We went & saw the Eifle Tower & went up in the great wheel in the afternoon & went to the Olimpic theatre in the evening
 
Jan 22nd  We got up at 9.A.M. & had breakfast at our hotel. We went & saw Napoleons tomb & Hotel d'Invalides in the morning & in the afternoon to "Musee Gravin" which consists of waxwork tableaux of historical interest. cabinette fantastique. conjouring etc & Palace des Mirages - We had tea at the Soldiers & Sailors club & stayed to the concert in the evening. it was splendid & consisted of 1st class talent only -
