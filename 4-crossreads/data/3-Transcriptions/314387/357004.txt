<p>1917<br/>March 19/ Went into Bapaume sightseeing afternoon went in again on fatigue work filling into waters made by enemy.<br/>March 20<br/>Moved up next line camping in Big underground dugout.<br/>Wednesday 21<br/>Moved up to Bapaume camped in cellar.<br/>Thursday 22    [indecipherable rubber stamp]<br/>Fatigue work in street filling up mine water.<br/>Friday 23<br/>Same as previous day late afternoon tried out of Bapaume and did [indecipherable] sentry.</p>
