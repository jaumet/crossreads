Reported in at the orderly room & then returned to where we had left our gear & had dinner after which we visited a friend at D.A.D.O.S. & yarned till 3 P.M. when we decided to return to our unit for the night it being bitterly cold.  Walked part of the way & part by lorry arrived for tea & spent a good night & collected some reading matter.
Tuesday 4th Feb  Returned to Beaumont by car about 10.30 & spent the remainder of the day reading before the fire, very cold & snowing again.  Had a very fair night with three blankets on the floor.
Wednesday 5th Feb  After breakfast we went down to camp to collect some rations, then had lunch at 12, after which we went to the station, a lorry giving us a lift.
