<p>9-4-17<br/>No. 2 Section again on road repairs at Grevillers.  Very bad weather.  Still doing well on all Fronts.</p>
<p>10th<br/>No. 2 Sec. engaged building huts for Coy.  Good news through of big advance by British near Arras, 10,000 prisoners taken &amp; about 100 Guns includ. 8" &amp; 4.9 Guns.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Still good news from British Front where the big advance started on the 9th.  Still working on Rds.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Still working on Rds.  It is now said that British near Arras have captured 200 Guns.</p>
<p>13th<br/>Still working about Bapaum.  1st &amp; 4th Divs. got a rough handling this morning near Hernicourt but eventually turned defeat into victory on being reinforced by the 2nd Div.</p>
<p>14th<br/>Still on roads etc. about Bapaum.  On our Front yesterday the Hun captured 16 of our 18 [indecipherable] destroying 2 of them but they were all recaptured.</p>
<p>15th<br/>It is now reported that the British have captured 14,000 prisoners &amp; <strike>194,000</strike> 194 guns.</p>
<p>16th<br/>In the engagement on our Front we captured 300 prisoners &amp; it is estimated that at least 1500 of the enemy were killed.</p>
<p>17th<br/>Big French success in the Champagne district on 25 mile Front.  Things still going well on the whole British Front.</p>
