<p>I was lying and I believe numbers of men would have been down to see me if they could</p>
<p>There was a Scotch sister in the ward whose name was [indecipherable] Owing to the inhalation of the eather I was not able to eat very much no appetite at all I was given chicken but did not enjoy it</p>
