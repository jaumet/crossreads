
27 Jan to 31st JanIn Indian Ocean
1 Feb 19Arrived Colombo, Ceylon. No leave granted, but the majority of the soldiers & sailors went ashore on their own account. I climbed through the porthole & down into an empty lighter & went myself. The lighters were coming over to the ship full of coal & going back full of soldiers.Needless to say we were not particular about our dress, most of the soldiers had no tunics & our chaps were in overalls or anything they could get. The first thing that greeted us when we got ashore were the rickshaw men, all in a group outside the shippingyard gates. They were all clamouring after us to ride in their particular rickshaw.
