<p>3 March 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>X Just got orders to go to 5th Wing Australian Flying Corps for 3 days instruction- not to learn flying but intercommunication, soon all C.O. will have [to] make their Reconnaissance by air.X<br/>Weather warmer &amp; we are off on another stunt X over the Judean Hills  I join the Regiment as they follow me up-  No chance of leave at present-<br/>Enclose Dolly's Granny's letter an old bed-ridden woman of 75.<br/>All my love to you   I send some Gum leaves from the Jordan valley also in box- &amp; grown around a Monastery there.X</p>
