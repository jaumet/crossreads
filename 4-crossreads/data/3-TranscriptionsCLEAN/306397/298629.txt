A change at last. Presently everyone is quiet and are waiting with tension for what is to come. First we see just a dark spot which is gradually coming nearer and is soon seen to be a steamer with one funnel. Already the B.G. has the aim and the distance is being taken. Our tension is increasing. We are already a few hundred metres nearer and "Emden" gives the first challange and puts on speed. The steamer begins to morse, but it can't be made out what she wants.
