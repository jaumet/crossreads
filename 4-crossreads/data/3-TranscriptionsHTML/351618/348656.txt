A large wagon of the 8th Battery came down to take our cases about 8 oclock &amp; we soon had everything in it &amp; started our journey to Headquarters. It was a very rough trip &amp; I felt tired when we arrived there &ndash; having had little to eat since the day previous. I slept at Headquarters making a bunk in the underground store wh had been dug.</p>
<p>Sunday 7th Nov<br/>To-day we fixed prices to allow of a little profit for breakages etc &amp; then called for orders from the Batterys. Needless to say the orders far exceeded the stock I had secured. In the evening I made up the orders working late to have them ready early.</p>
