<p>evening Martin came in to say he had seen my name go through to leave today.  Looked them up at the Orderly Room about 11 this morning &amp; they sayed yes be ready at 1 oclock.  Was struggling intoo a pair of pants Pat had given me they are very tight about the knees looked through the window &amp; saw the draft falling in.  Had to bustle some but got away allright.  Went to Havre by</p>
