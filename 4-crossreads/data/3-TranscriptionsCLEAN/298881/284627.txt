
From my window I can see a large row of tall bushy trees, portion of the street and several tiled roofs - I also have a per good view of the back yard.  This window like most windows in France is made in two parts & each opens on hinges "doorwise".  This room has four walls which is usual in a room.  On the left hand wall as I enter the room there hangs a plaster crucifix with a plaster saviour hanging thereon and the plaster figure of a woman kneeling at the foot of the crucifix.
