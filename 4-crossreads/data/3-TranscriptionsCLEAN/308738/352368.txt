
Jan. 2 Saturday 1915Troopship Suevic changed position falling in rear of Thermosticles
Submarine cast off from Berrima accompanying the Squadron as protector. Orders issued that all lights out as far as possible.
Jan. 3 Sunday 19152 Horses died. Weather getting warmer smooth sea. Averaging over 11 knots per hour. "Ayrshire"falling back.Examination of riflesFire drill & boat parade.Berrima Hauled to for the purpose of replenishing submarine with oil.
Jan. 4 Monday 1915Weather calm & fine9th day vaccanated, feverish, off tucker, went to bed early. Temperature 101Washing day
