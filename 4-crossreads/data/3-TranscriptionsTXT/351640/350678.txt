 Good night.  Sometimes think of me.  Judging by rumours and from appearances we are stuck in Egypt for an indefinite period.  Britains plans must have miscarried grieviously to result in keeping divisions of Australians in this land straining at the leash, doing but exercise, work, &amp; costing to the empire such great extent.  However here we are but to obey, &amp; when acts are required to put forth every effort to make our side ride the storm to safety and victory. 
 Again Good night.  [Lines of Xs and Os.] 
 Heaps of letters and papers from Australia yesterday and today not one for me.  Am I angry?  No.  Resigned.  The responsibility is not mine, but resting on the shoulders of the other fellow, his is the blame.
