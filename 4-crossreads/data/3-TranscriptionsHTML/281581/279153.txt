<p>The passengers all returned as usual laden with parcels and this evening an atmosphere of a fair reigns over the ship. A gramophone - an especially tinny one croaks away on the Bridge.  Another blares away aft and amidships a mandolin tinkles away amateurishly. Oh! to be at sea, and hear even the clanking engines to this bazaar euphony.</p>
