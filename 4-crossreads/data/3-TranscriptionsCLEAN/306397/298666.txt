 16th October 1914 Our transfer to Singapore commenced at 6 a.m. We traveled 1st class the N.C.O. 2nd and the men 3rd. An officer of P.V.C. and an escort of about 20 men accompanied us to the station. After having been 38 hours without food we at last got some thing to eat.
