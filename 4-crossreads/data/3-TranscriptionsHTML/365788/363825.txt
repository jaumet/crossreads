<p>Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>H.M.FORCES IN EGYPT<br/>8 Dec 1915<br/>1st Field Ambulance<br/>pans Division .</p>
<p>My dearest Irene,<br/>Here we are still at Lemnos Island &amp; may be here some time. I think I told you we were actually on the transport to be taken to the Dardanelles when we got orders to disembark. We are <strike>actu</strike> officially taken on the strength of the 1st Field Ambulance  So if you address your letters here you will be quite in order &amp; I will get them about two weeks earlier than when they are sent to indecipherable] first.<br/>No news worthy of the name &ndash; I do hope I shall be able to write as well with a pen when I get</p>
