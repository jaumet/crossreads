<p>It was 10 p.m before be finally halted &amp; by the time we had unharnessed &ndash; stretched our drag ropes from waggon wheel to waggon wheel (to tie the horses on to) watered &amp; fed our horses ditto ourselves &ndash; It was midnight &amp; we were quite ready for our blankets &ndash; one of the longest days I ever remember on the go for a full 23 hours.</p>
