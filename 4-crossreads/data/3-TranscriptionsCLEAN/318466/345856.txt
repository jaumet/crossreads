
Sunday 29 December 1918Arrived Auckland Harbour at noon.  Pulled alongside in eveng.  We went ashore, seems very slow place.  Bridge with Sturdees later.
Monday 30 December 1918Went ashore early & up to Mt. Eden.  Pretty suburbs of Auckland.  To Pictures in eveng, Charlie Chaplin.  First I've seen for years.  Very cold for mid-Summer.
Tuesday 31 December 1918To the Gardens - Albert Park - Museum & Takapuna in morning., none of which were anything to cable home about.  Sailed at 5 p.m. for Sydney.
Wednesday, 1 January 1919Was impressed by the signs of 'flu in Auckland.  The place looks more dead than alive.  Hear the deaths ran into thousands.
