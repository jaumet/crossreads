<p>27<br/>12/7/16<br/>Cycled back to Merris Visited our old friends who welcomed us with open arms. Gave us cherries &amp; opened a bottle of cider.</p>
<p>13/6/16.<br/>Stayed the night at Merris &amp; cycled to Bailleul where we entrained for Candas at the time not knowing where we were going. We travelled in an empty wagon on a truck &amp; made ourselves comfortable. There were 3 of us together &amp; we sang nearly all the way. Cleared the floor of the wagon &amp; had a dance what! how the people at the Village did laugh. We passed through lovely country very hilly &amp; picturesque.</p>
