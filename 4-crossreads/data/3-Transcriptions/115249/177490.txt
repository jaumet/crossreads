<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Administrator F/Wilhelmshafen</p>
<p>To - VAC</p>
<p>Date&nbsp;- 24 9 4</p>
<p>Time&nbsp;- 3.30 pm</p>
<p>Garrisons posted. &nbsp;All in suitable quarters. &nbsp;Flag hoisted proclamation read - Seventeen Germans surrendered - Four of&nbsp;these are officials who will probably be employed. &nbsp;The others are Planters Missionaries and business men all of whom have taken oath of neutrality - Chief official not yet in but has been sent for</p>
