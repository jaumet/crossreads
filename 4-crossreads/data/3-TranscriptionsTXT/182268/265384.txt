Saturday 26/1/18&nbsp; Went out to Malden &amp; took Hetty to matinee Valentine, returned about 6.p.m &amp; took Pattie to local Pictures
Sunday 27/1/18&nbsp; Had a good loaf around &amp; read a book at Malden
Monday 28/1/18&nbsp; lunched with Hardie&nbsp; had an air raid&nbsp; went to the Palladium.
Tuesday 29/1/18&nbsp; Another air raid&nbsp; got a pair of new shoes- had tea with Mrs Gordon Vesche. Wrote to Doff
