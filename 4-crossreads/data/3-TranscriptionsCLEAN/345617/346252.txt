  
 Novembre 26 Taking map locations of Pill Boxes & Dugouts in our Supports & Reserve line. Most of No. 2 Section working on strong points. Good news continues to come in regarding the advance near Cambrai. 
 Novembre 27 Taking map location of servicable Dugouts and Pill Boxes along our Reserve & Support lines. Most of Section employed putting in strong points in the Support line. Miserable wet weather today.
