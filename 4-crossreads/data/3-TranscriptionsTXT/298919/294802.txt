
our boat is docked, amongst the collection.  Tea in town, returned to Ship & then went to Confession.Sunday 11th July 1915.  Went to 9am Mass & Comm.  Went to town at noon with Purser.  He took some of his belongings with him & intends staying in town during our stay here.  Taxi'd from Fenchurch Street to Imperial Hotel Russel Sq where he engaged a room.  Washed & then had lunch.  Taxi'd to the Zoo at Regents Park.  People only admitted on Fellows tickets on Sundays.  Fr Davidson having given me 2 tickets we were alright.  Gardens very nice.  Some very interesting specimens.  Walked about for a couple of hours, had afternoon tea & motored back to Imperial.  Had dinner & strolled down to Leicester Square.
