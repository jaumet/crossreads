The tucker in these base camps is not good nor bad as far as that goes, but just a I remarked in the diary I kept at Gallipoli, it is better to get to ones unit where one is looked after well, & at least feeds are certain during the average time.  Under these conditions feeds are by no means certain always, for rations get lost or wet or damaged, & tea is unheard of except at actual stoppages.
