<p>March 1918<br/>Friday 22</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Spent the day at F.O.P. Did a shoot during afternoon. Weather fine but misty.<br/>Fritz put down heavy barrage on the Warncton Sector.<br/>News came to hand per Major to the effect that Fritz had recaptured Bulle and Lagnicourt<br/>Had tea and sujpper with some fellows of the Wireless section at right Battaln.<br/>I did L'aison with "Dope"<br/>Uneventful night. Created a record for duckboard trotting.</p>
