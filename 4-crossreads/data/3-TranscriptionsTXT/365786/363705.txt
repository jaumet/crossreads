Long ago water was only supplied to the land by the inundation &ndash; now however thanks to the huge dam at Assuan &amp; the barrages at Assiut &amp; the Delta, perennial irrigation is possible &amp; no water escapes to the sea unless by wish of those in charge of the irrigation works.  Long ago the ancients speak of 7 mouths to the Nile, but now only the East &amp; West branches are used, viz, the Rosetta &amp; Damietta branches.
