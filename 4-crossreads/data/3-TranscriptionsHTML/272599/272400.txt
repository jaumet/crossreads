<p>May Wednesday 9 1917</p>
<p>Duties till 2 p m. Bath, tog up &amp; walk into Boulogne. Promenade Esplanade. Hospital boat St Andrew  Met MacB. Tea 2nd story, Waitress tres triste. Trams crowded. Walk home. Buy wristlet watch holder &amp; bow. souvenir.</p>
