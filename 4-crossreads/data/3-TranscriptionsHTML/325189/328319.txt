<p>we were joined by two other transports.  We then resumed our position westward.  The next day seasickness held sway.  I fell a victim to a slight attack after tea.  I slung my hammock on deck &amp; was quite recovered the next day.  Stan Cocking &amp; I undertook to be orderlies for our mess throughout the voyage.  We are exempt from all other duties.</p>
