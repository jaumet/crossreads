25) The Landing
By Somebody that could not find his own. I was very lucky in mine the only things that were missing were cigarettes towel and one Bag of iron rations&nbsp; iron rations Conist Consist of one pound tin of Bulley Beef two oxo [tubes?] for makeing [Bovril?] with and tea Sugar and Biscuits&nbsp; we landed with 2 of these rations and with that and Bottle of water had to last us 3 Days&nbsp; on the 2nd of many however I lost my pack while we were attacking the turks. Of this attack I will give an account. later we [now?] leave the Beach and go up to the trenches
