I myself was arrested for putting an Overcoat on.  38 Prisoners wher placed in a little Cell on the Island, wich we used to call the sardine tin.  I don't know its exceat dimensiones but I think about 8 feet by 6 feet.  It was build of galvanised Iron.  Whe wher singing in the Cell.  I stood near the doer, and could see the commandant approach holding a revolver behind him.  I warned my fellow prisoner, to keep quit wich they than dit.  The Captain remained within 6 paces and waited.  Their wher holes in the Iron, and while we wher looking through, the soldiers used to poke Barb Wire in, and scratch our faces.  When the small compound was completed, we wher ordered, to come out of the cell. We wher ordered to take off my <strike>our</strike> overcoats and wher place in the small compound.
