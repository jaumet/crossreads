<p>August,1918<br/>Wednesday 14</p>
<p>Cayeau and,<br/>Left for Bty position forward of Rosieres, with a coil of wire, in company of Gunn.<br/>Received a couple of letters of interest from people at home. We returned to Cayeau again at night.<br/>Had word from the 8th Casualty Clearing Station that Roy Lewis and Jack Gorrick had died of wounds. He is one of the very best, and I'm very sorry. Had a few visits around the districts but I could find no evidences at hand. Burglar Laurence returned.</p>
