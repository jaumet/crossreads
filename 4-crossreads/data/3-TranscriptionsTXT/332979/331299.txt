 The Little Boys &amp; Girls waved their hats &amp; shouted out to us. We in turn gave them the (Australian Cooee.) The Seenery through the City was beautiful the Long Rows of Houses &amp; Narrow Streets &amp; the people at the Windows gave us a Welcome by waving  their  all sorts of Articles. As we left Devenport at 6 Pm &amp; the Sun  was at mo  had almost disappeared, we could only get a partial glimpse of the Country en route, but still what we did see it looked beautiful &amp; Green, the Ivy growing all along the Embankment &amp; up the buildings.
