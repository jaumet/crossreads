Ben Henry is doing munition works at a Ship building yard in Gy [Grimsby].  I went to Cleethorpes on the Pier with Jennie one night but could not get a partner so I did not dance.  We only stayed an Hour &amp; then went to the Theatre.  On the journey up to Gy. I had 8 changes to make &amp; it took me 12&frac12; Hours to do the journey about 300 odd miles.
