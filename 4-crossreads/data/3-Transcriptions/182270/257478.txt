<p>SUNDAY, JUNE 15, 1919.</p>
<p>Came on to rain about 8 AM &amp; was raining</p>
<p>most of the day. Had 37 points.</p>
<p>About the office all the morning</p>
<p>and round the place all afternoon</p>
<p>We went round Hut paddock</p>
<p>in afternoon &amp; got a couple of</p>
<p>bunnies.</p>
