 May 1/16 We moved our camp yesterday to a spot near the "Supply Depot" I was only thinking that in happier times it would be the ideal spot for a week end camp. In a  farm  field adjoining the farm buildings we have pitched tents near the hawthorn hedge & under some high trees. There is the farm pond close by & at the farm we can buy milk
