  
 On the morning of June the 7th we entered Plymouth Sounds after having a very calm trip. Not with standing this fact, I managed to get sea-sick on two separate days. Our landing was saddened by the news of Kitcheners death which occurred on that day. We were the first complete Australian unit to reach England and all the way to our barracks we were cheered by the people on either side of the line.
