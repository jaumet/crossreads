I had a letter from Jennie last week in which she stated that Her Sailor Boy, Jack, a  submarine  mine-sweeper officer had asked Her to marry Him.  She has consented but will have to wait until after the war.  Marie &amp; Auntie have both written also &amp; they have both been a bit off colour but are improving now.  The heat has been very pronounced lately &amp; they have been busy spring cleaning. 
 I went to Sarum by train on Tuesday &amp; went for a row on the river.  It was very nice.  It is a long time since I went on the river.  I may go in on the bike on Tuesday.
