<p>From what Mother writes you have not been yourself dear. Do take care for the childrens and my sake,</p>
<p>I am delighted you are signing again.</p>
<p>Do not think I fail to know you are all struggling over there for I know the money coming in is not sufficient thats why I am so anxious for the new allotment to reach you. You will then be able to manage fairly well would you Bee?</p>
<p>Well you good people never say one word about hardships you endure. Ah you are really noble hearted in that way and I trust to live in a way well deserving your goodness, and I shall my darling.</p>
<p>What fine letters our darlings write. They are spendid</p>
