 I was on Gas guard last night till 11 oclock, &amp; so  slept in this morning till dinner time. This was the first night's sleep I had had since Monday. Now I am feeling fresh again &amp; ready for the stunt we understand is to take place the day after tomorrow. If it is true it can only be an attempt to smash the Hindenburg line up.
