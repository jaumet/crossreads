 11 July, Friday 
 F. Very rough sea &amp; strong gale. Sea roughest yet. Jack Cramer gets a WAC Conigrams. I I read about H &amp; M become separated though married &amp; return to one another. Read. 
 Mar. wom. 28 falls 1 &amp; L with single M of 04. Reads [indecipherable]. 
 Read a Bachelors love Story Collects. Single girl 82 m. grey haired bandman of 04[?] go to bed in hammock. Just as well as there is another flood. Distribution on music &amp; wardrobe. 
 12 July, Saturday 
 F. Spend whole day reading. Lectures on Fruit pests &amp; [indecipherable] to Jerusalem, Allenby means "Bearer of the Law". 
 Description of Kaiser &amp; Allenby's entrances to Jerusalem the blessed. Read at night. Awful list on boat &amp; swell still troublesome. I am hungry but can't eat ships munfaces[?]. We have another kit inspection for dutiable goods.
