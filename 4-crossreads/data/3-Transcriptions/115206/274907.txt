<p>December  1918.<br/>Tuesday 3</p>
<p>Wembley. On furlough<br/>Weather wet and miserable<br/>As I received no answer from Eve I presume that she has either not had my letter or parcel.<br/>I sincerely hope she is not too ill. However I trust that she will not be disappointed.</p>
