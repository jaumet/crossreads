<p>I saw Bertie Macnamara today, just the same old stick.</p>
<p>I wonder what you did this afternoon for of course its 3 a.m. Monday with you now.  I'd like to have had a wander round the garden, and a yarn, my goodness me if I can remember it all.  I'll not live long enough to tell you all I'd like to.  I rather hope its the autumn when I get back to Tog, I like the late autumn best, the lovely days &amp; cold nights.</p>
