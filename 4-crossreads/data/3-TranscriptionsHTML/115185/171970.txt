<p>11 &ndash; Sunday 20 Feb. I went to early service in the Garrison Church and to Church Parade at 9.30 am.<br/>Then in the afternoon Alma &amp; I went to see the Arabic Museum and the Egyptian Museum. We did not see much and are going again. Often we took Pte Stuart Welsh as escort &ndash; Capt Stuart's young Cousin. We saw some wonderful carving &amp; inlaid wood work and old mosaic designs made with tiles &amp; old pottery taken mostly from mosques. I got some P.C.s to send you. At the Egyptian Museum we were facinated &ndash; I saw a head of the Goddess Hathor perfectly beautiful &ndash; with cows ears &ndash; one lovely sarcophacus &ndash; we specially liked &ndash; with figures of goddesses each corner carved in the granite with beautiful wings &ndash; a wonderful conception. There is a spiritual meaning in the wings each King &amp; Queen having special wings. I have not learnt or read yet what they mean or what they are for &ndash; but I should imagine that they meant to bear the soul away when the body dies. These wings are painted on</p>
