
February 1918Saturday 2
On the roadReveille at 5.30 am very cold but finePassed through St Omer and Wizernes at a fairly early hourArrived at our billets a big shed, at 4 pm.Watered and fed. Short speech by Major congratulating Bty on their smart turn out.Went into Staples with Jamie, Smith or orderly Sgt. Had a feed of eggs and chips, also some fun in an Estaminet.Lost ourselves coming home.
