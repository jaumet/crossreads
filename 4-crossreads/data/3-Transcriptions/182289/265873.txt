Harassing fire in the early part of the night round village of Treux.  Enemy reply to our shoots light.<br/>Enemy Machine Guns.  Normal activity.<br/>Enemy planes were active generally over their own lines, several attempts to move to our territory were frustrated by our patrols.</p>
<p>24.4.18<br/>Under approval of 10th A.I. Bde. guns were placed as follows:-<br/>3 guns in E.19.d., remainder to move so as to give improved fields of fire.  Three guns placed in Pioneer Line in D.24.d. and D.30.b. with direct fire tasks and also barrage tasks in front of Dernancourt.  Two guns in reserve line west of Buire in D.28.b. with direct fire task.<br/>The following targets were engaged at irregular intervals throughout night &amp; morning, 1500 rounds expended:-<br/>(1)  Streets, Ville-Sur-Ancre.  <br/>(2)  Hollow K.7.b.  <br/>(3)  Sunken Road K.1.b. &amp; d.  <br/>(4)  Trenches K.7.b.10.75.  <br/>(5)  Cemetery, Ville-Sur-Ancre.  <br/>(6)  Road &amp; Trench E.26.c.00.25.</p>
