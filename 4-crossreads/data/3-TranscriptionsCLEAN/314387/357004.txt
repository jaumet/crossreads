  
 1917 March 19/ Went into Bapaume sightseeing afternoon went in again on fatigue work filling into waters made by enemy. March 20 Moved up next line camping in Big underground dugout. Wednesday 21 Moved up to Bapaume camped in cellar. Thursday 22    [indecipherable rubber stamp] Fatigue work in street filling up mine water. Friday 23 Same as previous day late afternoon tried out of Bapaume and did [indecipherable] sentry.
