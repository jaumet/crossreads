<p>April, 1916<br/>Monday 17<br/>Were treated to a splendid aviation display this afternoon, a small tractor Biplane looped the loop no less than 6 times in about 20 mins.<br/>Went for a long walk in the cool evening.</p>
<p>Tuesday 18<br/>This morning my chum was sent to Hosp to have his ingrowing toenail cut out.</p>
