 Tuesday 21 September, 1915 
 Still off Duty. 
 Received a letter from the mater. 
 Artillery is very active lately. Fleet as well as land batteries. 
 Rum was issued. 
 The Island of Cinbros[?] is a beautiful sight just before dark. The sun goes down just to the right of it &amp; it looks a very dark blue &amp; wventually fades away, growin[g] the same colour as the sea.
