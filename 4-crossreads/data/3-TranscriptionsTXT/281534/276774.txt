 4/1/19 Gourdinne Made an inspection of A Coy's billets with the doctor and then came round through Headquarters. Near the band billet was a little old shop where two men were busy manufacturing sabots. With a piece of green elm timber and a few sharp augurlike instruments they soon turn out these simple but very unsightly wooden shoes. At the schoolmasters billet I met a man just back from Russia and who tells awful tales of misery absolute among the unhappy Russians and Poles. He was in Berlin just about Xmas. and speaks of great misery there. How War drags hundreds of unfortunates into its clutches.
