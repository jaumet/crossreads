The other night the officers had a great spread, to celebrate Captain Jacobs "crown" he is a Major now, his promotion was very popular in the Battalion for he is well liked.
Had to give evidence this evening on the Stokes case at the "Orderly room". McKillop went on leave today I sent one of my completed diaries with him.
