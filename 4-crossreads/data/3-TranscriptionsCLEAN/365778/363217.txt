
Tuesday 4/9/17left at 9 am arrived Calais at 6 pm missed Train, Calais air raid 100 casualties, to Hotel Sauvage for night.Wednesday 5/9/17Returned by car arriving at 10 am. Battalion at Bombing.Thursday 6/9/17Battalion practised in Divisional Stunt clearing Clairmarias Wood. Saw Genl Plumer.Friday 7/9/17Inspection of Battalion. Night stunt. 28th Sports.Saturday 8/9/17Quiet Day - half holiday. Bean came along for account of Malt Trench.
