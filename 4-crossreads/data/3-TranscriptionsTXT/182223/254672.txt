 Thursday 17 June 1915 Busy all day. Patients in and out. &nbsp;Examining those to be sent to Australia.&nbsp; Not so hot today. 
 Friday 18&nbsp;June 1915 Dr Hollywood of W. Maitland brought a parcel &amp; letters from Mollie for me.&nbsp; Dinner,&nbsp;at 10-30 pm. at Borrills Cafe at Heliopolis &ndash; Hollywood, Garde, Leary, Plant &amp; others&nbsp; 
 Saturday 19&nbsp;June 1915 Heaps of patients today&nbsp; Over 350 in hospital&nbsp; Letters from Macquarie&nbsp;St, Maitland, &amp; other places. Dates Febry &amp; up to 18 th  May 
 Sunday 20&nbsp;June 1915 Mass &nbsp;at Basilica (Byzantine Church) at 9-30 a.m. Working hard all day.&nbsp; Had dinner with Dr Schuber at the Chalet Mena at 7.30 pm.
