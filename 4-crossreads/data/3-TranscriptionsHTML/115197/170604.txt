<p><u>April 24th</u></p>
<p>Early breakfast.</p>
<p>Make preparations and set out for the village.&nbsp; My brave &quot;English Mother&quot; - Our sad &amp; painful farewell.</p>
<p>Bid &quot;good bye&quot; to Mr &amp; Mrs Matthews, Honor &amp; Joyce whose great kindness is beyond my poor expression.</p>
<p>Motor bus to Lymington - Train to Paddington (changing at Brockenhurst)</p>
