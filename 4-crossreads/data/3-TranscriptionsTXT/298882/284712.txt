Al Aneluy 4.11.17
I left Poperinghe on  2nd of this month and after a tedious train journey arrived at Romescamp on the 3rd about 6 a.m.  Walked out to Aumale a very large town about 8 kilos distant stayed for a couple of hours and returned to Romescamp.
