<p>As to how I like the life I like it very well. We are like a lot of school boys  only that we are better friends and it is very seldom indeed that there is a row even amongst the whole battalion. It is very funny to see all the different squads at the morning physical culture exercises.<br/>One section will be hopping up and down flapping their arms and legs for all the world like those figures on strings which work when you give a pull. Others will be squatted down on their heels and waddling along in that position, others racing on hands and knees, others doing the goose step, others leaping over one anothers backs, in fact looking around one can see groups in almost every imaginable</p>
