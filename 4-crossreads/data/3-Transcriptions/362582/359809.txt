<p>&amp; was sound asleep in a very few moments. Went out at 4 p.m. for a lovely walk &amp; had dinner at Belgian Restaurant with my <u>love</u>. Most likely I shall be off-duty on Friday &amp;  go to Cairo in the afternoon with another sister, who is due off. We stay at a Nurse's Convalescence Home near Cairo. Returning on Saturday-night. I shall try &amp;  buy a brass ornament like the one I sent Mrs Osburne while there &amp;  send it home. Had a very interesting letter from Consie Murray from France the other day.
