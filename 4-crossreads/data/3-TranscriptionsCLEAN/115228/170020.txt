The 3rd L.H.Regt. supported by the 2nd Regt. & in conjunction with the Infantry attacked positions on our right (Bald Hills) at 1o'clock this morning, gaining all their objectives with few casualties. At 8 a.m. after more heavy artillery prepartion the Infantry attacked on a four mile front running from the coast - inland, and advanced something lke five miles in depth during the day, the Turks retiring without offering any serious resistance. We now have our line straightened & also Jaffa well out of range of the enemy's big guns. In all the recent engagements
