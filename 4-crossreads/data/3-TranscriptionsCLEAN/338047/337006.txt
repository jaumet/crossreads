It was held in the town square.  There was the usual run of merry go rounds playing the old familiar tunes & giving a very short ride for the money.  Swing boats, ice cream, & chipped potato booths, & various side shows - strong men flying women, fortune tellers etc & punch & judy shows.  The shows luckily were cheap. -- We spent 75 centimes each, both of us being short of cash & saw two or three of them.  There were also spinning jennies & Aunt Sallies.  Nearly all the Aunt Sallies consisted of figures of German soldiers & the Kaiser.  The froggies did leather into them too.
