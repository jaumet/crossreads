<p>Extensive help was given by the tanks, armoured cars and cavalry, each doing marvellous and almost impossible work. Now &amp; then you could hear a dainty piece of 15 inch "iron-jelloid" whistling its way onward through the slight fog, being coughed up by a large French gun stationed on the railway a few hundred yards from our billit.</p>
