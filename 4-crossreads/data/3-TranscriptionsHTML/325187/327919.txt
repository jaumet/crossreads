<p>[Sunday 25 June 1916]</p>
<p>Church Parade but did not go.<br/>Hear most of our Mail has gone to FRANCE in error, so may miss a few letters for a while-<br/>Sunday Dinner Fray Bentos, Bully Beef- We all swear by the Queensland stuff if we can get it it is much the best Army Beef issued.  As for Davies the American it was the brand that had the Fish hooks in it.<br/>Australasian April 29 from Mother just received - &amp; Independent.</p>
