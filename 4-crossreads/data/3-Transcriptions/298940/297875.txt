Then I carried with Mick.<br/>At the next relay two things caused Mick Stafford to indulge in good Australian language: two infantry men &ndash; lost &ndash; flashing a torch to find the way, on a fairly good track with direct observation from Fritz. Their action was liable to get both us &amp; any wounded we might have to carry severely strafed, not to mention a damaged truck and all the other dangers. He told them all about it<br/>Then the relay bearers, Barlow &amp; Haynes particularly, were a long time getting ready</p>
