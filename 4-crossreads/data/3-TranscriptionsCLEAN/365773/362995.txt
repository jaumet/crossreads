
[Previous pages are postcards]Weymouth2.1.19With any luck at all I should be home about the end of March or early in April.I couldn't get into the Indian army as they are not at present accepting any more transfers, it is a pity I didnt push it about 18 months ago & then I would have got in flying - but then I didn't like leaving the war to go to a soft job & no war, it wouldn't have looked at all well.I will have to buck in when I get back & do something for myself - you know these last five years have been wasted as far as civilian life is concerned.I got on to Owen's trail he is clear of Germany
