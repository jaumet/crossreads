<p>Our room contains a dressing table and wardrobe en suite in light oak, but the remainder of the pre-war furnishings have given place to two single bedsteads, two camp-beds, two card tables, and, alas!, two Welshmen.   They are G. Williams (C.B. "Lestris") and O. Richards (C.E. "Bulgarian").</p>
<p>A late lunch was served us in a beautiful dining hall.</p>
<p>A genuine lunch, too, of cold tongue, beef, potatoes, butter, tea, jam, etc.    (<u>How</u> we wolfed it!)</p>
