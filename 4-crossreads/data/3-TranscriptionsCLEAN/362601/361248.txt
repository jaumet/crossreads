
6 August, Wednesday
Stop Home. Gardening & House maid. Ring Nette, Mrs Clarke. Speak Nette 35 minutes or more. Sandy nearly eats a child.
Do a few hours gardening & read & write at night.
Receive notification about deferred pay.
7 August, Thursday
Mum & I sort out trophies & boxes, clothes, etc nearly all day. Ring Mrs Clarke. Write at night.
Wrote Auntie Annie.
