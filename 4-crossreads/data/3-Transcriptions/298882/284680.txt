<p>We had had a gas alarm previous to this so that by the time the shell burst and left us in darkness <strike>and</strike> smoke and dust everybody had the "wind up" pretty badly.  All night and all day today the enemy has shelled our pill box incessantly <strike>and</strike> with the result that all heads are aching a treat but otherwise OK.  A more hellish time I have never experienced.</p>
