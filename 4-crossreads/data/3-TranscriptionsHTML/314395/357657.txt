<p>turn out some really beautiful work.  To see the way they flick the bobbins to &amp; fro, in &amp; out, is astonishing.  It takes about two days to make a yard of 4" Torchon, and the price is 5 fcs. a yard.  Some of course charge more.  Valencienne &amp; Bruges lace will go as much as 50 fcs. a yard.  They are also very clever at embroidery work, and turn their skill to some account, for these flimsy nick-nacks sell like hot cakes to our boys, who want to send some souvenir home &amp; having no knowledge of their value, will give anything asked.</p>
