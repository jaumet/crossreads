 heavy marching.  Complaints from some of men, regarding insufficient food.  Making railway from main line to canal, where it will connect with metal road on opposite side prepared for motor traffic.  Few skirmishes reported between Turkish outpost &amp; ours, a few miles inland on opposite side of Canal. Piquet from 4.30 pm till 9 pm.  S.S. Ceramic passed through from Australia with very large number of troops on board. 
 Friday 17/12/15 Reveille 6 A.M.  No duty owing to being on piquet last night. Spent majority of day letter writing.
