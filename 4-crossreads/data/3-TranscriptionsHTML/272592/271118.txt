<p>The 1st. Battalion were specially recommended in Army Order's for the fine engineering work done by us in Ypres, I should think so for we worked day &amp; night &amp; we have some good men that know their work thoroughly</p>
<p>16th.   Another nice morning &amp; the sun shining brightly but not too much warmth in her, I took the men myself this morning to "Quintons Siding" &amp; gave the other Lcpl a spell we have a convoy of 12 motors on this trip &amp; they carry, timber, logs, patent roofings, sleepers, sheet iron, &amp; etc. to the C.R.E. dump, here it is all sawn up into suitable sizes &amp; distrubited amongst the</p>
