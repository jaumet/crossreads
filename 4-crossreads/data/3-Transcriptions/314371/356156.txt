<p>Apr. 17th Wed.<br/>I went on my own to Tea w/- Alton's &amp; spent a very happy evening with much talk &amp; music.</p>
<p>Apr. 18th Thur.<br/>A bunch of us visited the United Tobbacco Co's works in the morning &amp; had an interesting couple of hours.  Went to free entertainment in the Tivoli after dinner.</p>
<p>Friday, 19th Apr.<br/>Had a bust up in the mess.  A lot of men are drunk in Camp.<br/>Went to Pictures after tea.</p>
<p>Sat. 20th Apr. to 1st May<br/>Things going easy.  I go frequently to Alton's &amp; am having a real good time.</p>
