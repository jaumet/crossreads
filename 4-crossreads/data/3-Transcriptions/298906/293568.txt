<p>Have been drilling these Mortar chaps of ours all day, among them they have an ex 20th Battn Major, he was cashiered for cowardice last October &amp; sent back to Australia, he then re-enlisted &amp; is here as a private, seems funny don't it &amp; he don't seem a bad sort of fellow, I suppose he is out to wipe that stain off at any cost, for it must be a terrible thing to have hanging over one Ross is his name.<br/>Am Sergt of the Inlying piquet tonight &amp; it is raining as per usual<br/>Some Yanks were round here today with some of our heads the Yanks are taking all these Camps over I believe, &amp; we are (moving</p>
