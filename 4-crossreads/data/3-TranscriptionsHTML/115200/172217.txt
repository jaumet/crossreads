<p>4&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;Heliopolis</p>
<p>Cairo</p>
<p>23/9/15/</p>
<p>I&nbsp;am at present lying in No 2 auxiliary Convalescence Depot. Heliopolis. Suffering from wounds that I received from the turks while fighting them on gallipoli Peninsula. I was 4 months at the front and in that time I have been wounded twice. once on may the 3<sup>rd </sup>and again on august the 10<sup>th</sup>&nbsp; The former I received after our Brigade had made an attack up a very Steep gulley.
