  
 34 the day in the garden it was great. 
 Last night we sat out in front with the Adjutant, Doctor & Vet & had a real good yarn. They are 3 of the best especially the Adjutant (Capt Glasgow). The Dr shouted two bottles of beer. It is going to be another fine day to-day. 
 5/8/16. Still at Rubempre but expect to move in a day or so. Our Division is about to take a place in the front line of trenches, we've just received word from the General.
