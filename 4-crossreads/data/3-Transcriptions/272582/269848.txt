<p>(1st Australian Field Bakery) as Depot Reinforcements.</p>
<p>During the month of September there were (4) four crimes.  For the same period 37 N.C.O.s and men baked approximately 366,968 rations, the balance of personnel being at work with Imperial Sections.</p>
<p>On 31 October 1916, the equipment was received from Bakery South to Equipment Store, Details Issue Store, to 2 Base. Rouen.</p>
<p>On November 4th, 1916, the strength of the Unit was 1 Officer, 88 Other ranks.  There were no reinforcements for this month.  During the month of October 1916, there was only one crime. In the same period 34 N.C.O.'s and men baked 328,382 rations.</p>
<p>On December 2nd 1916, the strength of the Unit was 1 Officer, 75 O. Ranks. Reinforcements on December 7th were (9) nine O. Ranks "taken on strength" from Australian General Base Depot, through O.C. 13th A.A.S.C. (1st Australian Field Bakery).</p>
