<p>We have been having splendid tucker here the last few days, plenty of young potatoes &amp; peas which we pick from the deserted fields about, there are no end of them.  I had a letter from Ken about a week ago, he seems to be doing well &amp; enjoying himself although he says nothing of where he has been or what he has been doing.
