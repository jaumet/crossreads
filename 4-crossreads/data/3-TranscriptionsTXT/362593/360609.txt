
Thursday 3 October 1918Having a great spell in our lovely new camp at Haifa by the sea.First shower of rain about 20 points.McGuinness & Self have record hours flying for the stunt, 55 hours.
Friday 4 October 1918Tent fixed up, all camp in order.  Maughan, Tonkin, Ross Smith, Mustard & Ned Henry get D.F.C., very glad to hear it.  Smith & Mustard got it very backsheesh.
