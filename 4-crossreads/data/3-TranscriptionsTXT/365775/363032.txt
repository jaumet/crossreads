 finds us well on our way &amp; supposed to be sailing without an escort, not a very pleasant outlook in these times of Submarine warfare, but towards nightfall we pick up a gun boat which is sailing around us on the lookout for submarines. I assure we felt much more at ease having her with us, as we [are travelling] in rather dangerous parts.  21st finds us anchored just outside Malta, awaiting orders we presume; just ten months ago I was brought in here more dead than alive.
