<p>Feb. Monday 26 1917</p>
<p>Working in the dressing room all day. Wounded streaming along all day. Capt. Drummond carrying on day &amp; night. <br/>Troops on hill advancing . We are wondering what Fritz's move &amp; how far he is going back&hellip;.our possies are slight compared to the area gained.   <br/>Snipers &amp; guns get many.</p>
