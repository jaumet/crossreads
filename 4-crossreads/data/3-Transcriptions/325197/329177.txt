<p>After some fooling about I was shoved into No 13 Hut.</p>
<p>Sat. 23rd Nov.<br/>Up before Dentist &amp; Doctor for Classification</p>
<p>Mon 25th Nov.<br/>Result of classification B.1.A.2.</p>
<p>Mon. 2nd December<br/>Up before the Doctor again, still B.1.A.2.</p>
<p>Fri 6th December<br/>Put on the "Nestor" boat roll for Australia, and warned for draft.</p>
<p>Sat. 7th Dec<br/>Inoculated and medical inspection at No 5 Camp. Spent the rest of the afternoon in Warminster buying a few things for the voyage.</p>
<p>When I got back to camp, I was told not to leave the hut, may be marched off any moment.</p>
<p>Sun 8th Dec.<br/>Transferred to No 2 Camp.</p>
<p>Mon. 9th Dec.<br/>Handed in some of our kit to Q.M. and issued with extra uniform</p>
