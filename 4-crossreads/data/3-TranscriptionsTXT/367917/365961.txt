 Northern France Disgraceful with their Dirty work it made our Blood Boil when we passed Through the Villages, they left some Villages &amp; Towns in a shocking condition Some Towns had  Certain Desease  which I cannot tell you &amp; the military put them out of Bounds to all Troops 
 The Civilians were left Starving they stole all their Cattle &amp; when we passed Through their Cry was " Mungie " (Bread), we gave them our Rations Poor Devils
