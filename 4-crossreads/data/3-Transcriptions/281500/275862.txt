<p>29th.  On the same job again today only the parties are all split up.  We had a few anxious moments last night old Fritz dropped heavy shells all round us &amp; the clods fell like rain on our slender little humpy, we were expecting every shell to land right on us, but they knocked off in time.  A man would be dead stiff to stop a shell right back here, but you never know when one will blot you out for he drops them at random day &amp; night &amp; they are likely to catch some one.</p>
<p>It rained a fair bit last night &amp; melted all the frozen ground, the consequence is, that today everything is soft &amp; muck once more, &amp; King mud reigns supreme.</p>
