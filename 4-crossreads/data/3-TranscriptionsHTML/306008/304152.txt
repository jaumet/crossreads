<p>1914<br/>Nov 26 Th<br/>Fairly seasick</p>
<p>Nov 27 Fri<br/>Strill feeling a bit off<br/>Anchored inside Port Phillip for the night</p>
<p>Nov 28 Sat<br/>Berthed at Victoria Wharf at 9 a.m.<br/>Off on shore leave from 1.30 -  11.30 pm<br/>Visited Preston etc. &ndash; very enjoyable</p>
