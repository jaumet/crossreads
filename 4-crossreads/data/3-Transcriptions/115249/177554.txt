<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Brigadier Berrima</p>
<p>To - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 12 9 4</p>
<p>Time - 6 pm</p>
<p>Commander Beresford was directed when retiring from Wireless Station to remove Instruments - Motor Lorry evidently acquired to facilitate this duty - Do not apprehend any difficulty although I have had no report yet of his arrival at Herbertshohe Naval Guard at pier will be relieved immediately</p>
