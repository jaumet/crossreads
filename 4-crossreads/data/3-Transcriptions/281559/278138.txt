<p>Thursday July 4th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Friday July 5th &amp; 6th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Nothing much doing : air raid at night</p>
<p>Sunday July 7th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Worked through 6 a till 10 pm to change over shifts</p>
<p>Monday July 8th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Stroll to town &amp; seen the aeroplanes in the square : there are four &amp; one so fairly intact &amp; has a large dragon painted on each side of it with fire issuing from its mouth</p>
<p>Tuesday July 9th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Wednesday July 10th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Nothing much doing : on duty 2 &ndash; 10 pm :</p>
<p>Thursday July 11th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Friday July 12th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Nothing doing : on duty 2 &ndash; 10 pm</p>
<p>Saturday July 13th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Stroll in morn : working through from 2 pm till 6 am Sunday for the change over of shifts</p>
<p>Sunday July 14th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Air raid in early hours : Fritz volplaned down very low &amp; we could see him quite plain &amp; I thought it was a go for the station so hopped under a loaded truck : dropped a few bombs : one torpedo smashed through the signal box but fortunately it was a dud &amp; didn't go off so the damage was trifling : fair barrage put up :</p>
