
March, 1917. Monday 19Day cloudy with strong N.E wind & rain in evening. Cooked bacon for breakfast & ran her at 10.30. No inspection & only rations & fuel came. Another run in evening. Went over to 3rd coy in evening & got a letter. Home at 7 and wrote diary till bed at 9. News. Enemy 15 miles beyond Bapaume. [indecipherable] fallen. Success at Ypres. French success on 22 mile front to a depth 1-2 miles. Rumours of first german line captured from coast to here. Australians to spend another fortnight here & then rest for a good while & ten train journey.
