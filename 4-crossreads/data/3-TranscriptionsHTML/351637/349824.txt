<p>Saturday May 29th<br/>At sea in the "Ardroasan"<br/>    Sunday May 30th<br/>At sea in the "Ardrossan". The voyage was quite uneventful and we sighted no more of the enemy's boats. I passed the time writing accounts of recent events for the purpose of having them sent off from Malta, but I very much doubt if anything will be allowed to pass, about these disasters to the battleships.</p>
