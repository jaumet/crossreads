<p>19.3.15</p>
<p>Fri 19th<br/>Was hauled out at 5.30 this morning, &amp; was on parade early; had a lot of galloping, but no running on foot; had a rather tame time &amp; arrived back in camp soon after 3 oclock:</p>
<p>Idleboy was kicked coming in off parade last Monday &amp; has been "off duty" ever since;  Had an argument with Sergeant Simeon for not looking after him to-night &amp; had a good mind to report him,  Put in the evening writing to Jess &amp; Trix.  were issued with a new pair of boots each to-day &amp; are very well off for clothes of all descriptions now.</p>
