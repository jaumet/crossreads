<p>24 January 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out)<br/>X <u>Boils</u> under arms and groin  strange no sooner than I get out I suffer these things-X<br/>Divisional Sports are coming along soon &amp; football to keep the men fit-  Birds here are Crow, Blackbird, Ducks, plover, &amp; Grouse,  Gazelle in the Judean Hills, but I have no shotgun now-<br/>Every Evening the hawks chase the Starlings home to roost- the latter fly in large mobs-<br/>X Did I mention seeing General Allenby in Judea- he is not like some Gs who remained in Cairo X</p>
