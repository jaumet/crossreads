 Overseas Training Bde, Warminster 22.2.18 Dear Fergie Why the hell haven't you written you old malingerer &ndash; when I see you next, my boy, I am going to kick your posterior until your nasal organ becomes sanguinary. Well old thing and how do you like Sydney? &ndash; is it much better than Amiens? &ndash; not 'arf. I have heard lately from several people who have seen you lately, saying that you were looking well etc. They must have been lucky I suppose &amp; seen you during one of your few sober intervals. Still as long as you can keep up a good appearance do so by all means. Well my son I have clicked this time good &amp; hard.
