 Sunday 2/1/16 (11) Church Parade as usual &amp; then read all day. Sacred concert at night in F ward. No officers present. Men &amp; Nurses only. Very nice time. 
 Monday 3.1.16 (12) Nothing doing. Troop-Deck Sergeant in morning, looking for piano &amp; played cards.
