 May, 1915 Monday 3 Just received long letter from Daisy dated 28 March &amp; yours of April 4 with Grazier &amp; Australasian, so glad to hear all is well at home &ndash; I posted more snapshots &ndash; some of myself, hair cut a la jail bird &amp; moustache you can hardly recognize. Went Heliopolis where all our wounded are from &ndash; Sister Myra Nyse also there poor girl 13 weeks in bed from Pneumonia. You will have news in Australia of Casualties long before we see the correct list -
