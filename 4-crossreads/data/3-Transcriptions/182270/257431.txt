
<p>TUESDAY, APRIL 29, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Watering rams &amp; killers</p>
<p>in morning &amp; I shifted little</p>
<p>engine up to Woolshed</p>
<p>&amp; pumped there in afternoon.</p>
<p>Marj. &amp; Ham came over in</p>
<p>the afternoon, &amp; we went</p>
<p>out after bunnies again</p>
<p>at night. Let off a lot</p>
<p>of rockets ect at night</p>
