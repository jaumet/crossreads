<p>Tuesday 29 July 1919<br/>Got the 8.19 to town &amp; the 1.55 to Brighton. Janet met me at the station. We went to the hotel &amp; got a room &amp; then had tea. Went for a stroll along the cliffs came back &amp; had dinner &amp; then all over the race course for a walk. Won't see the dear kid again until she comes home to me.</p>
<p>Wednesday 30 July 1919<br/>Up at 20 to 8. Gee I had to go some to get my train to Vic. Went round to Belgrave Sq &amp; put in an app for Janet to come out at the end of April. Saw Trix &amp; had a yarn with her. Got the 12.15 to Warminster &amp; arrived here at 3 pm. Had tea with Bill. The Y.M.C.A. put up a good concert &amp; then an open air dance tonight. Wrote 10 letters &amp; went to be at 11 pm.</p>
