<p>January, 1917. Wednesday 17<br/>Usual work and loaded two pontoons to the trucks. Knocked off at 2 and when I got home cleaned up a bit and had a good <strike>watch</strike> wash. Spent evening reading and writing. Not very cold but very heavy snowfall which continued all day until about 5" everywhere.</p>
