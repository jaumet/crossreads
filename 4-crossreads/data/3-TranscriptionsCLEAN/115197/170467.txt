Feb. 6th
Return to town.  Call on Mr Fitzsimmons who unfortunately is away from office.
Matinee at the New Theatre.  A thrilling and capibally acted play.  "The Chinese Puzzle"- Ethel Irving, Lilian Braithwaite - Leon McLeon.  Athol Stewart.  Meet Rupert.
In the evening I see Charles Hawkey & Gladys Cooper at the "Playhouse"
