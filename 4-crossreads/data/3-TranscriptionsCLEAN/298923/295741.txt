  
 16 March 1918 Saturday Left Wadi  Auja  & marched in the rain to  Rummon  drew rations & on in the dark to Beitin 4 horses died of cold & the infantry plodded along in the mud. We could light no fires & had no tucker since yesterday mornings breakfast.  8 Nigger camel drivers died with the cold & wet.  They cannot stand the cold like our men.
