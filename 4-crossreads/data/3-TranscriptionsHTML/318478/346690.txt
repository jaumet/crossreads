<p>a perfect rabbit warren and too ghastly to people with the image of fighting.  We also rode down to the landing &amp; all that I had been told about the beauty of the formations &amp; colour was realised.<br/>[Drawing]<br/>It seems incredible that our fellows could land, climb &amp; establish themselves so quickly.</p>
<p>The gruesome is in fact scattered all over the battlefield.<br/>[Drawing]<br/>My friend the Jackal surrounded the camp &amp; nearly encouraged me to get out and have a shot, but it was raining &amp; I saved my fire for a future warm night when I hope to get revenge for Palestine.</p>
<p>Today it is raining &amp; this gives me an opportunity for writing you.</p>
