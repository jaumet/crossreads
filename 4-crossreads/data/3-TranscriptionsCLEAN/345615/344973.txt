 Jan 12/16 The Light Horse Regt have left this morning - they are returning to the railhead (el Dabaa) & thence by rail to Cairo to rejoin their various regiments from which they were drawn to make up this Comp. Regt. - As far as is known in the ranks their various regiments will be moving  shortly  down soon to the Canal where there seems to be big trouble coming.  All the Divis  A squadron of them are left behind & are to follow in a week 
 All the Divisional Train waggons have gone with them carrying forage - supplies & water & I expect will stop at el Dabaa & then return with the Complete
