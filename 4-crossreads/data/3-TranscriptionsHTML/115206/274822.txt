<p>September 1918.<br/>Monday 9</p>
<p>In Reserve position 4 miles from Peronne.<br/>A very heavy downfall of rain from 7am to 11am.<br/>Received a letter from home, also a visit from Fritz 7 planes &ndash; no damage.<br/>Had another nice little spell today. Salvaged a little wire, not very much.<br/>Major inspected guns.<br/>Wrote to Doug, Ken Gunn and Cousin Stell.<br/>Had a feed of good quaker oats.<br/>Rumors that the Corps is pulling out very shortly. Hope that is correct.</p>
