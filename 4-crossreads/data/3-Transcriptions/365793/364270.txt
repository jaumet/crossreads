<p>June 22 for brek. I know my head was dashed sore.<br/>Four Tommies on the Prom sat down in some deck chairs near a couple of girls. Presently the collector came along for the usual 2' each &amp; only one Tommy could pay up The others cleared out after vainly searching in every pocket for the necessary cash. Unable to get colors yesterday so took French leave today &amp; the picquets don't worry a P.U. man<br/>Almost certain we go tomorrow as there is a muster parade at 2 pm.<br/>Lovely on the Prom' this afternoon, &amp; got outside some icecream (the dinkum stuff) &amp; then</p>
