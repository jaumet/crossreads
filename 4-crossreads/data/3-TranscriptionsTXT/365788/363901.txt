 1st Field Amb. 1 Aus Brigade, 1 Aus Division Aus Intermediate Base Egypt. 10/3/16. 
 My dearest Irene, I was very pleased to receive a letter from you dated 7th Feby. My throat is well again I am glad to say &ndash; I write sometimes twice by each mail to you so you ought to get quite a lot of letters.
