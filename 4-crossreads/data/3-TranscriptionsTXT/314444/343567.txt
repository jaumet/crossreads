 Somewhere in France 13/12/17 
 Dear Mater Just a few lines to let you know that I am still in the land of the living I wrote to you last week also Miss Kelly thanking her for those socks she sent me we are having beastly cold and wet wether over here now. Tomorrow is my birthday I cant keep it up has we are out of civilization just now will keep it up at Xmas Day Ma I will soon be away on furlough again I cabled to you to send me some
