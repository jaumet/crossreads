  
 Saturday 19/5/17 Training, and Sports Football, tugs-0f war, and running. Officers had a jump. Sunday 20/5/17 Wrote to Doff, missed church parade. Robinson to Paris for sports material. Monday 21/5/17 Arrangements re dinner, played football against 7th Fld Coy & won. Tuesday 22/5/17 Rode to Senlis re staff with Bde, very stiff & sore. Wednesday 23/5/17 Sports meeting in afternoon, visited by General Smythe; letter from Doff.
