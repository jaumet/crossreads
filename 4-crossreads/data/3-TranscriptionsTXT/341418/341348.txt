 So tomorrow there will be alarms  &amp;  excursions I suppose  &amp;  poor old Jackie &ndash; our 8 hr &ndash; won't know whether he is on his head or his heels and the hard workers will do all the work while the pointers take a holiday  &amp;  do nothing; Oh! I could map out the programmes of the next few days as accurately as if they were already past! &ndash; But I had better not anticipate. Let it suffice to say that we are at least about to shake from off our perspirings feet the thrice accursed dust of Sohag.
