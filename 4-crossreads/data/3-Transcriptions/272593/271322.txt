<p>in the trenches now, the parapet is manned &amp; the scientific work of the sniper which has been going on all day gives way to more snap shooting than anything else.<br/>Working parties get busy this time in places where you can't get at in day light.<br/>Ration parties set off down the communication trenches, wounded are transferred under the friendly cover of darkness to the motor ambulances waiting in the roads at the rear<br/>Parties go over the parapet to repair the wire which had been damaged during the afternoon shelling, now comes the exciting part for occasionally a Boche patrol sneaks out &amp; gets busy, there will be no shots, only the cold steel, to fire a rifle in the middle of "No Mans Land"</p>
