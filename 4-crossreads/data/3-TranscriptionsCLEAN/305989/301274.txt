  
 Sunday 16 September 1917 On Day Stableman tied up 30 horses:  played bridge at night.  Wrote Nos. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 4e]. 
 Monday 17 September 1917 On fatigue on Officers Cookhouse.  Saw Guthrie.  Preparing to move to Kilo 9. 
 Tuesday 18 September 1917 Left at 6.30 for Kilo 9, arriving about 11.30 & struck camp, picquet at night.  Awfully dusty & windy. 
 Wednesday 19 September 1917 Making ourselves comfortable as possible.  Windy & Dusty. 
 Thursday 20 September 1917 On Q.M. fatigue, bought some canteen stuff at Tommies canteen, windy & dusty. 
 Friday 21 September 1917 Early morning parade & quiet day.  Wind lesser. 
 Saturday 22 September 1917 Quiet day.
