 June, 1915 Sunday 13 Returned from Alex:- It is full of French soldiers Blue shirts Red Pants &amp; like a Circus all colored horses. The streets were crowded with Lorrys made out of London Buses! to carry 30 &amp; every hotel full of officers &ndash; The bay full of ships &amp; every wharf crowded with Bully Beef &amp; Biscuits 320 casualties out of 1st L.H.B. to date. I expect letters today &amp; will then post.
