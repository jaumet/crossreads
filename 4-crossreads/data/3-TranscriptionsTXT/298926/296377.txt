 It was the surprise of my life. Never in my life have I seen such riotous fun  Everywhere we went some girl would  shove what they call a tickler into my face or throw a ball on a piece of elastic at you.     The girls are a fine type, some almost beautiful and it is said they save up all their  money till Bank Holiday and then blue it up in a day  Certainly the majority were  expensively dressed, some wearing 3 &amp; 4 guinea fur
