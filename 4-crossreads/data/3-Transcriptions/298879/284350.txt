<p>March 1915<br/>Thursday 25<br/>Only milk diet allowed me today. Much about the same Acute stomach pains at night time.</p>
<p>Friday 26<br/>Feeling tip-top except a little pain in the right leg which I judge to be<br/>Rheumatism. I expect to be kicked out of here next Monday or Tuesday. Have had a good holiday so had better get back to work now.</p>
