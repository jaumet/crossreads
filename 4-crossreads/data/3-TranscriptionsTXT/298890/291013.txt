 18. Thursday. Went to Minerva &amp; waited at table. Home about 4 &amp; did some washing &amp; typing. Fine day but showers in evening. 
 19. Friday. 
 Typed all day at home. Heavy showers all day. A.A,S. came home in evening. Depressed &amp; worried &ndash; money running out.
