<p>May 22nd.<br/>Having the chance to do our own cooking we live well at Bois-Grenier- I assist with a few wounded cases during the day.<br/>May 23rd.<br/>Busier day- Interesting if at times gory work- "Whist."  Lt. Porteus' Death.<br/>May. 24th.<br/>"Cribbage"- Few wounded cases- Pathetic incident of Two Brothers (2nd instance)<br/>May 25th.<br/>More cribbage- Many letters- Good news from Home- Still going strong- Plenty of shells from Fritz.</p>
