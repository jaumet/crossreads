<p>4-1-16<br/>Tue 4th<br/>Had a muster parade this morning , &amp; had Regimental drill on the desert till dinner time; This afternoon we had mounted work again but was not out long before we got wet. &amp; was brought back to camp again; We were issued with more equipment again today  &amp; I scaled away at stables again this afternoon &amp; went to the Hospital with Arthur &amp; Bottom to see Morgan: &amp; then went on into Cairo; Morgan looked quite well again &amp; was in the best of spirits; Last night we had a fair amount of rain &amp; all who slept outside had a wet time of it; The weather has been very cool the last few days</p>
