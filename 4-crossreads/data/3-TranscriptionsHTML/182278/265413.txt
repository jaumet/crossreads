<p>Thursday 2/5/18&nbsp; moved to CAMON&nbsp; I reconnoitred the front South of Somme arriving home tired after 20 mile ride.</p>
<p>Friday 3/5/18&nbsp; Men had a good rest&nbsp; Officers reconnoitring&nbsp; Huns bombed Amiens but did little damage</p>
<p>Saturday 4/5/18&nbsp; Reconnoitred area between Glisy &amp; Nicholas- moved to vicinity of Glisy.</p>
<p>Sunday 5/5/18&nbsp; Wisdom back- had dinner with 25<sup>th</sup>. who celebrated 1000 days service.</p>
