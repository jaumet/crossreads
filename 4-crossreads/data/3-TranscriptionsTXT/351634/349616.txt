
(Friday 24th continued)afternoon it is one of the large villages nothing of any importance there we got a lot of tomatoes on our return Had tea in Sarpi a village near camp had tinned lobster and tea flavoured with cloves got back tired out and went to bed early(Saturday 25th)Usual drill and card playing wrote letter to Kitty am going to bed early (Sunday 26th)Went to church and spent rest of day in playing cards and reading played casino and went to bed(Monday 27th)Fine weather very warm usual drill (Tuesday 28th)Usual drill and spent the afternoon as usual playing cards and reading (Wednesday 29th)Went for a route march of 5 miles to some village halted for dinner and returned to camp we are all tired out and are going to bed early(Thursday 30th)Drill as usual done some sewing this afternoon and went on picket to village opposite and came home at 9 pm(Friday 1st October)drill this morning and afternoon off am not feeling well turned in early (Saturday 2nd)Usual drill nothing of any importance happening (Sunday 3rd)Went to church wrote letters to Kitty and Mum and cards to Nellie Lex Bob and Shiela turned in early(Monday 4th)Have to go on guard at 9.20 (am) received two letters from Kate and one from Nellie Will be relieved at 9 (am) tomorrow(Thursday 5th)Relieved at 9 (am) and rested till noon went out to drill afternoon till 4 pm went over to Sarpi after tea
