<p>March<br/>&amp; 3 of us stayed untill 3 PM  putting in the iron rods to reinforce the concrete a <strike>n</strike> bit dificult in the dark. A cake &amp; sox from Aunt Mima. Much apreciated but am getting a bit overloaded with sox</p>
<p>Monday 11th<br/>Went out at midnight a very quiet night</p>
<p>Tuesday 12th<br/>Working from 2 AM untill daylight</p>
<p>Wednesday 13th<br/>Working midnight untill 5 AM Foggie &amp; dark There is a Comfort Fund depot close to us &amp; we can slip over at any time up to midnight &amp; get a hot drink. Some of the chaps went one night lately &amp; as there was no one in charge they pinched several tins of cocoa &amp; milk &amp; some chocolate. It was hardely a fair deal but as I have been drinking it am as much in it as anyone</p>
<p>Thursday 14th<br/>Steady rain all morning Night shift as usual</p>
