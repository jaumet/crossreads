<p>April, 1916<br/>Thursday 13<br/>The dreaded wind commenced to blow just after sunrise gaining strength all day until about 3 pm it was not possible to open ones eyes.<br/>The sand drifts into every crack &amp; hole making it impossible to keep it out.</p>
<p>Friday 14<br/>After blowing all night the <strike>Camseen</strike> x abates after sunrise   x Khamsin<br/>Altogether it has done a whole heap of damage  it will take us a week to get clear of the dust</p>
