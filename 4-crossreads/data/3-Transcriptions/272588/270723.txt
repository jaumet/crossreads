<p>Sunday 16 June<br/>Absolutely nothing happened last night, except a bomb was dropped about a hundred yards behind my tent, catching five men wounded.</p>
<p>Monday 17 June<br/>Still everything is very quiet.  The country behind here is now very heavily wired &amp; trenched in anticipation.</p>
