<p>After 30 here.<br/><strike>But on every hand we seemed to be face to face with war.</strike></p>
<p>There are always 2 army divisions 40,000 British troops here.  <strike>It is</strike> They are called the Army of Occupation in Egypt.  They are <strike>the police</strike> responsible for the safety of the route to India and <strike>now</strike> with all our men coming &amp; going &amp; with territorials &amp; Indian regiments with New Zealanders &amp; Hospital units the war seemed very close.  Every day we would see the N.Z. &amp; Australian Ambulances at work &ndash; for the only ambulances used in Egypt since the beginning of the war were those sent from Australasia &amp; N.Z.
