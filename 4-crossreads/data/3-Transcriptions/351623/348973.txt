<p>2 April 16<br/>At St Lucia. Some of the Canadian soldiers, stationed here, were on board this afternoon to hear our band. Each man of the ship's company received a box of chocolate to-day, which was a present to Australians at the front from the Australian people in England.</p>
<p>3 April 16<br/>Our football team played a match with the Canadian soldiers. Our team won. The band also went ashore &amp; played during the match. The British S.S. Midland left here today for Russia with munitions of war.</p>
