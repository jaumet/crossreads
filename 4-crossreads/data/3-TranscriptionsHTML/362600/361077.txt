<p>F 17 Sun<br/>&amp;hot. Shower at 6 pm. I go up to Hall &amp; sweep &amp; clean &amp; wash up. D &amp; I go for a walk round through milstone. We are hot &amp; tired  &amp; I am thirsty.<br/>Wrote P.C. to Dad. Mum. George. Marie &amp; Aunt Annie</p>
<p>F 18 Mon<br/>Lovely Day. I go up to Hall again for final clean up. Play Hockey against Waac's Have nice time. Do Night Part of orderly Corporal.<br/>Wrote Jennie. Capt. Robinson. Reg. Roy. Bess. Ada</p>
<p>F &amp; W 19 Tues.<br/>Catch first Train to London. Go H'ferry Rd Round Lamberth Bridge up through St.Pauls &amp; Holborn F.</p>
<p>London Bridge. Tea at Aldwych Theatre &amp; supper in Strand. Go to Drury Lane see Samson &amp; Dealilah [Delilah] &amp; sn tho. Buches orchestra play at 11 Aesb St.</p>
<p>F 20 Wed<br/>Glorious Hot Day. Go to London Bridge, Kingsway. Catch 11pm Train arr home 2pm. Recd. Letters from Home Marg &amp; Nell. Go to bed at 7.30 &amp; read.<br/>Go for walk round block with [indecipherable] meet Bainty [indecipherable].</p>
<p>F 21 Th<br/>Lovely hot Day. Recd letters from MRL. Jennie &amp; Doris. Arr Ord Corp. Go to merry-go-round with Dick. It is fine. The laughing Jackass is there again<br/>Wrote to MRL.</p>
<p>F &amp; Hot 22 Fri<br/>It is a real summers Day. I go for ride round B'ford after Tea. Then write all night.<br/>Wrote Marg. Dot (8) Laura. Norm Upton. Dulce. Go to bed at lights out &amp;  read for a little.</p>
<p>F 23 Sat<br/>Glorious Hot Day. Recd letter from Aldington Hunt. I play Hockey against N Z's over at Sling. We lose 6 goals to nil but give them a real rattling game. Col. comes over to see us &amp;  we all have one photos took. Dick &amp; I after Tea have bath &amp;  go walk round Block. I sit  &amp;  write all night.<br/>Nettie. Lottie. Muriel. Alice. Mrs White.<br/>Go to bed at lights out  &amp;  read.<br/>  <strike>Recd. Letter from DAD</strike></p>
