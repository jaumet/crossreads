
Nov. 16 Tuesday 1915Went to work at 6 AM. After breakfast went out with other Witnesses to Mustapha. The case started at 10 AM. The "papers"occupied most of the day. Stewart & Playdell & myself were called in & the case was adjourned till 10 AM tomorrow.
Nov. 17 Wednesday 1915Went to work 6 Am. Went out to Mustapha with other Witnesses  Case on all day. Adjourned till 11 AM tomorrow.Only two charges so far been brought against him 4 more to follow.Paid tonight 7/-Went to prayer meeting (spoke)
Nov. 18 Thursday 1915Went to Mustapha again with other witnesses - all day sitting returned 3. 30 PM. Dick & I worked till 8. 30 PM
Nov. 19 Friday 1915Out again to Mustapha all day.
