 Tuesday 18 April 1916 Father, Dick &amp; I branded Countess and the Colt. I walked up to the store and rode back on Dick's bike. Mother, Father &amp; I drove over to Warrobil;  Dick took sulkey home.  Saw Miss Smith &amp; Mrs. &amp; Dr. Nicholl. 
 Wednesday 19 April 1916 Very crowded train.  Found Alan at Lyttleton. Father, Alan &amp; I went up to Menangle Camp to try &amp; get my back pay, had to wait nearly all day &amp; then was told to go to Liverpool. In evening we all went over to Telarah.
