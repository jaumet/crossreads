<p>[Tuesday 27 June 1916]</p>
<p>Another Plane today but no Bombs, I posted you an Egyptian Paper showing how we treated them at ,<br/> [Following paragraphs are crossed out]<br/>AL ARISH<br/>Picking up daily both men &amp; Horses, getting plenty of sleep, but another Stunt is due about Friday<br/>Great rumours today that some wonderful news is about to be given out, so we all anxiously await it.<br/>We have no Papers so have to start a few rumours now &amp; again we hope this is not one.  Mulga wires are not in it compare with War yarns.</p>
