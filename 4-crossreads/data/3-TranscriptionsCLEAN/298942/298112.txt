  
 Tuesday 7 May 
 Last night it fell my lot to do gas guard.   Sat on a chair in a perfectly dark barn like central part of the hop place, listening to shells for two hours. 
 Then I went over to the living room & found Baird doing his vigil with a magazine & a solitary lamp.  We made some coffee & had a yarn then I returned.
