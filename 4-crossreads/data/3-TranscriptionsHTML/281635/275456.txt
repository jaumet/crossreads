<p>&amp; arrived at Rouen about 7am but did not get off until about 8.30am. Then went to a Rest Camp about 600 yds from station. At 10am went back to the station to the R.T.O &amp; received instructions to get in another train at 2 p.m. today. At Rouen station saw trainloads of wounded from the big stunt some coming down in trucks. Also saw some refugees from Amiens. Was talking to a wounded Tommy from 66th division &amp; he had been in a stunt from first day &amp; in it 7 days &ndash; retreating from front line to beyond Peronne.
