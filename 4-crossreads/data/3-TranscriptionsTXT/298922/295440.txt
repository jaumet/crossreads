 Tuesday 24 April 1917 
 ABUSITTA 
 Had a shave &amp; wash over in a quart of water hair cut close &amp; a good flea hunt so am fresher. Very hot &amp; crops ripening fast. 
 My Squadron doing pick &amp; shovel work making road over WADI. Enemy plane over again but we have shot down 3. 3rd Brigade captured a Troop of Lancers yesterday, Horses &amp; all.  don't think Turkish Cavalry have much GUTS.  
 [The following sentence crossed through.]  They serve their GUNS well &amp; are superior in the air coz of better machines (don't repeat this).
