<p>and travelling aver night arrived at Proven near Ypres at 6AM.</p>
<p>Sun Aug 27th &ndash; 16<br/>Had best meal today since leaving Home</p>
<p>Mon Aug 28th &ndash; 16<br/>Left Proven at 4PM and proceeded to wagon lines for a short rest before going into action at Ypres</p>
<p>On our way we passed through the town of Poperinghe the streets of which still showed signs of the close fighting which had taken place some months previously</p>
<p>Tues Aug 29th &ndash; 16<br/>Everything quiet owing to bad weather</p>
