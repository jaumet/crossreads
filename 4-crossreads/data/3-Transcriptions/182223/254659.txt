<p>Tuesday 4&nbsp;May 1915<br />9-15 a.m. Left No 2 G.H. at Mena House &amp; went to Abbassia to take charge of Abbassia Detention Barracks V. Hospital. A rotten game but General Ford asked me to do &amp; I said that I would if he so desired.<br />Arrived Abbassia D. H. at 10 a.m. Found [?]&nbsp;two medical officers there &amp; Some [?]&nbsp;details. Started at once to get matters ready.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5&nbsp;May 1915<br />Getting matters ready all day at the Abbassia D.B.H. Requisitions &amp;c.&nbsp;<br />Letters from Mrs Abbott &amp; Hugh&nbsp;Dr MacIntosh&nbsp;<br />By myself in a room,&nbsp;formerly a stable, writing this up.<br />Weather beautiful</p>
