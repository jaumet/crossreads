 THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 13, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; cold. Reported at 
 20 Belgrave Square in the 
 morning &amp; got my marching 
 orders to go to Sutton Veny 
 tomorrow. &nbsp;Went round &amp; 
 fixed things up afterwards 
 &amp; got a big mail at the bank 
 &amp; packed everything up. 
 Went round &amp; said good 
 bye to Dot Brunton 
 after tea.
