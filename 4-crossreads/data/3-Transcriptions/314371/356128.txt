<p>Disembarked &amp; marched to Rest Camp.<br/>Had a hell of a time with the men, getting blankets &amp; rations.</p>
<p>April 25, Wed.<br/>Marched, with full pack from Boulogne to Camiers.  Went into tents dead beat after the 14 mile march.</p>
<p>April 26, Thur.<br/>Marches to Etaples for rifles, Bayonets, steel hats, &amp; gas helmets.  Went through gas chamber.  One man got rather badly gassed.<br/>After dinner fired stoppages on 25 yd. range.</p>
<p>April 27, Fri.<br/>Parades all day &amp; kit inspection.  Another draft from Perham Downs marched in.  Put them into tents.</p>
<p>April 28, Sat.<br/>Marched Perham Downs draft to Etaples &amp; back.<br/>Had afternoon off.  Later second draft from Grantham came in.</p>
<p>April 29, Sun.<br/>Had a bath parade, then a walk to the beach.</p>
