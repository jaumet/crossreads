<p>June 22, Sunday</p>
<p>F.<br/>We sight land early but do not arrive in port until 5.30 pm. At 6.30 pm lots have already gone ashore the chaps off Rio Padro putting up a gangway. I go ashore with Win &amp; Nick. We dine at a lounge mego on Pier &amp; all around town I meet fitz Hill. We eat lots of fruit &amp; we have pains next day. Gell cab Erino Hagh.</p>
<p>Wrote M 7.</p>
<p>23 June, Monday</p>
<p>F.<br/>WROTE to Auntie &amp; M.</p>
<p>I go up town &amp; try to get Books etc, have tea at A Lounge. Ride up to town in motor. I jump into cab Bonnie Dundee &amp; arrive at boat just in time to catch motor party. We go to Cardonia Hotel for dinner &amp; then out to Hout Bay. I return &amp; go to Mrs Lidbury (Sister of Mrs Murray, Bondi Junct.) for Tea. There are five girls. We have music &amp; dancing. We all go down Town to see whether a peace is signed. H.A.D. back to supper other Town &amp; walk Home.</p>
