The barage was awful, but soon all fell asleep after one of the most eventful days in our lives and one that we shall always remember with horror & sorrow as long as any of us live. 
 6th Not called out yet, feeling much refreshed after a sleep & a meal.  The bombardment continues but on a small scale.  At 5.30 p.m. went back to sunken road & learned that we were held in reserve for the "hop over".  10.30 load of [indecipherable] blown up.
