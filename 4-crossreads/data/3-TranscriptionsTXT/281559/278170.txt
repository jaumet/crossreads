 Monday May 12th at Sea 
 up anchor &amp; away about noon to the strains of our band : foggy &amp; misty rain so couldn't get a photo of anything : struck a decent roll on : slept on deck &amp; it was nice : 
 Tuesday May 13th at Sea 
 decent roll on &amp; plenty of the lads were sick : I felt a bit squeamish but didn't miss any meals &amp; managed to keep them all down : seen a couple of small whales : 
 Wednesday May 14th at Sea 
 usual   routine : calmer sea so we settled ourselves down to things : good library opened
