I've had several narrow escapes from bullets, even when off duty.  One bullet struck a bank not an inch from my head whilst I was trying to have a sleep.  On another occasion whilst guarding our water tanks with six comrades a Turkish shell came over &amp; pieces of it went through a water tin a yard from me.  I picked it up and it was red hot.
