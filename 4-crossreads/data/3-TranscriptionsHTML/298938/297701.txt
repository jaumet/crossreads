<p>Church parade in Institute this morning.  Received 'Weekly Times' from Dad.<br/>Was sent up for medical board yesterday, on account of report re eyes, by Dr [indecipherable]<br/>Passed in A Class!</p>
<p>Sunday 10th Sept 1916<br/>Billy went to London with a programme of how to make the most of his four days, including two visits home.<br/>On the following morning W.O. Pollard late of the 3rd F.A., &amp; now in charge of A.M.C. details, asked on parade for a dispenser &amp; clerk &amp; also for 6 orderlies.</p>
