
Wed. 9th. May; -Left Noreuil & went via Vaulx onto Bancourt.
Sun. 13th May: -Left Bancourt & went through the heaps of bricks that mark the spots of Villers-au-Flos & Flers on to Bazentin-le-Petit & billeted in huts.
Mon. 21st. May.Left "Hot-Cross" Camp at Bazentin & marched through Contalmaison Meaulte, Villers-Sous-Corbie, to Buire & billeted.
Fri. May. 25th: -Left Bn. at Buire & went to Brigade Signal School at Lavieville.
Sat. May. 26thWent over to Contay & stayed with 19th Bn. for night.
Mon & Tues. 4th & 5th. June.I'm at Amiens on leave. Good times here & its hard to leave to go back to Buire.
Sun. June 17th: -Still billeted at Buire & had another day's leave into Amiens. Missed train home but hopped a motor lorry making for Albert to Lavieville corner.We are learning all the disc signals to tanks so must be in for a large "stunt"Certainly we will have to do something after this long spell.
