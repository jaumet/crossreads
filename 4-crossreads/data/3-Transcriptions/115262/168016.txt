<p>Thursday 6 September 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille 5 a.m.</p>
<p>At tent inspection at 9 a.m. in the &quot;dugout&quot; bivouac competition my &quot;Terra Domus&quot; got honorable mention.</p>
<p>Handed over patients from Sandcart to Hospital Train at Shiekh Nuran (dump) station. On the camels for watering parade &nbsp;to the &quot;Waddy&quot; at&nbsp;10 a.m. Back at 4.30 pm. Fooled about by a lecture &amp; tea at 5 pm. Next to no dinner &amp; no wash all day, no time since morning when breakfast &amp; wash had to be had in 5 minutes.</p>
