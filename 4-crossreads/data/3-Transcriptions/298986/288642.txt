<p>very soon the Coal barges came with there niggers &amp; the air was full of dust &amp; noise.  They sold exceptionaly nice oranges &#64; 2d. each but some of the digs who were swiming got into a boat &amp; threw all the fruit on board.  No more boats came about except selling lace silk &amp; other stuff.  All the pasangers got off &amp; some nurses &amp; officers came on.  The niggers struck on one coal barge.  It was funnie.  A few Digs got boats</p>
