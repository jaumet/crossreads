15th Friday
Turks very quiet indeed. Heavy Naval Bombardment South most of day. Small mail in.
16th Saturday
Demonstration given by us 4 a.m. with Artillery Navy. Poorest yet witnessed, turned in to sleep at 5-30 a.m. Much Artillery Naval fire later morning, answered weakly by Turks Guns. Very heavy bombmt. South.
17th Sunday
Line very quiet. Weather very dull cold.
