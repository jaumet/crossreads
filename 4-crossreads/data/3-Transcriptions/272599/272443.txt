<p>June Friday 22 1917</p>
<p>An easier night on the ward. Only traversed 15 miles last night. <br/>Jock Mac &amp; self went for trip to Le Portel this afternoon. Fishing village with plenty stink. Jock takes couple snaps. <br/>14 letters arrive from up the line also parcel socks etc from Wilsons.</p>
