<p>Tuesday 18 April 1916<br/>Father, Dick &amp; I branded Countess and the Colt.<br/>I walked up to the store and rode back on Dick's bike.<br/>Mother, Father &amp; I drove over to Warrobil;  Dick took sulkey home.  Saw Miss Smith &amp; Mrs. &amp; Dr. Nicholl.</p>
<p>Wednesday 19 April 1916<br/>Very crowded train.  Found Alan at Lyttleton.<br/>Father, Alan &amp; I went up to Menangle Camp to try &amp; get my back pay, had to wait nearly all day &amp; then was told to go to Liverpool.<br/>In evening we all went over to Telarah.</p>
