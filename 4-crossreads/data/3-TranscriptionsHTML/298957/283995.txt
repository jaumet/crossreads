<p>beautifully mild day. Engineers not coming today so I have charge of the job. We are now busy at the roof. News was today read out in orders about the engagement some 40 miles from us where the 8th &amp; 9th Light Horse captured 130 Turks  and 1 German Officer.</p>
<p>16th April (Sunday) Church Parade in morning. This was another bright day. Washing, general cleaning up and writing letters.</p>
<p>17th April (Monday)<br/>Beautiful Day. Swimming Parade to Ferry Post which we made  into a trip across</p>
