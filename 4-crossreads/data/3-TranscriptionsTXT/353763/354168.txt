 How Rawdon &ndash; holding me very firmly by the hand wd love all this pomp &amp; panoply &amp; what a supply of naval language he'd pick up  - The ladies on the Beach at Selsea (near Portsmouth) the daughters of naval men were seated near a lake &amp; a lot of swans came into view  -  Just then 2 Tars came into view also walking up to the edge of the lake  -  One lady said to the other &ndash; I can tell you what will be the first remark of those two men &amp; what the reply will be  -  They bet gloves on it &amp; wrote question &amp; answer on paper &amp; waited  -  Up came the tars 'What's them 'ruddy' birds Bill" said No 1 &ndash; 'Ruddy' Swans &ndash; Jack said the other  -  The elder lady's knowledge of the tar was correct  for that was her written question &amp; answer  -  I have been on the 'Protector' this morning  -  It is my 'pidgin' to examine any Diver before he goes diving  -  It was a rough sea &amp; I had but one sailor  -  I took the ropes  -  Just as we got alongside we snapped the starboard oar  -  They threw a rope &amp; we were all right  -  I took 2 coming back  -  From the Protector we saw the Admiral's
