Light shelling throughout the day.<br/>Enemy M.G's fired short bursts at irregular intervals.<br/>Enemy Aircraft.  A few planes up at dawn &amp; dusk, but slightly below normal.<br/>Weather.  Fine day &amp; night, visibility poor &ndash; hazy.</p>
