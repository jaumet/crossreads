<p>night &ndash; so I am continually imaging I can hear a 'plane.  I long for the time when I shall be no longer always subconsciously on the alert for unaccustomed sounds.  Immediately after the next pay I am going to importune the Colonel for leave to Cairo to get my teeth fixed up, they want it badly.<br/>There seems to be no chance of general leave for this bgde. though the 3rd are getting it.<br/>They have two wire roads down here now one on the beach &amp; the other on the south side of our Camp.<br/>They have this road &ndash; composed of four widths of wire netting &ndash; right from Bir el Abd &amp; I believe even further.  It makes the sand practicable for Infantry to march on &amp; the estimated cost of it is &pound;250,000!!</p>
