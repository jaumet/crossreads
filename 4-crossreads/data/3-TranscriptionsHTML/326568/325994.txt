<p>January 12th.  Visit Cemtery nearby where many Germans &amp; French are buried after battle here in September 1914.<br/>January 15th.  Rotten weather &amp; nothing to do here but read.  More demob. rumours.<br/>January 16th.  Fine weather like spring.<br/>January 17th.  Great dessatesfactions among the A.M.C. of this division consequent upon men from battalions &amp; from England of later dates of embarkation from Australia, going back Home.</p>
