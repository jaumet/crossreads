<p>1915<br/>Jan. 21 Thu.<br/>Early mornings are extremely cold.<br/>Camp still in a state of disorder.<br/>Reported again to Lieut. Col. Nash at Mena House.<br/>Working daily now at Mena House with Sgt. Clerk in Lieut. Col. Nash's office.<br/>Men working very hard in our camp which is somewhat apart from the big one, erecting tents &amp; field Hospital which our unit will also look after.<br/>Big bag of mail arrived &ndash; very busy sorting &ndash; received 10 letters, etc. - mighty glad to get them.</p>
