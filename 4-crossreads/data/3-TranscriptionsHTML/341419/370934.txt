<p>and waited for the force of Turks operating against the Sherif of Mecca to the South of Amman (from 6-10000 as rumours say) to give themselves up, as we held their base &amp; they had neither arms &amp; food.</p>
<p>Which they did this afternoon so now there is nothing here to keep us from moving north towards Damascus.</p>
<p>Our Forces are reported to be 9 miles from Damascus &amp; expected to be entering tonight.  So we have been in the curious position of carrying on fighting almost 100 miles behind the Front Line.</p>
<p>What will happen now I don't know.  Unless the turks can provide &amp; send a brand new Army down to oppose us there</p>
