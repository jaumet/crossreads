<p>Fred Reade, caterer &ndash; self secty &amp; treasurer &ndash; "Longfellows Poems." We again open at the Aerodrome (Bertangles) to nearly 700 Australians &ndash; about the same number is unfortunately turned away through want of room &ndash; Harold present at show &ndash; Having grown worse during the day it proves a hard task to get through at night &ndash; 101.5.<br/>June 5th<br/>A miserable day spent with one blanket separating myself from the ground &ndash; Are we downhearted? No!!</p>
