<p>Oct. 6th.<br/>"Talbot House" &ndash; Y.M.C.A. &amp; daily duties</p>
<p>Oct. 7th.<br/>News of Roull. Laren &ndash; Jimmie Garner &ndash; New Respiration drill &ndash; The Canteen.</p>
<p>Oct. 8th.<br/>George &amp; Stanley C. &ndash; Our third defeat of the Sixth F.A. at Rugby 3-0. &ndash; Our first experience of gas with box respirators.</p>
<p>Oct 9th.<br/>Transferred from Baths Staff to Unit &ndash; In a most memorable match we defeat the Wessex R.A.M.C. at Soccer by 6-3. The Tommies play a very dirty game but have to take more than they can give. &ndash; "The match and After"</p>
