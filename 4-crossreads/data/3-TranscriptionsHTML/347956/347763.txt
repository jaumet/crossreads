<p>Nov 1 &ndash; 2 &ndash; 3</p>
<p>Sun 1st<br/>Left King George's Sound at 6-30 A.M.  saw two sharks as we left the heads.  ships came out in single file, &amp; then steamed into position 2 abreast (3/4 of as mile separating them) &amp; a mile apart lengthways.  Major Vernon conducted the Church Service.  another horse died (Lieu. Wordsworth's)</p>
<p>Mon 2nd<br/>Idleboy has touch of influenza: attending to horses all the morning.  there are 35 Troop ships &amp; they make a very pretty picture steaming along all in line. can only see 3 cruisers.  another horse died</p>
<p>Tue 3rd<br/>The sea a bit on the rough side.  several lads seasick; &amp; a lot more home sick.  gambling still goes on to a large extent;  doing practically nothing morning.  giving semaphore lessons afternoon.  steering N.E. at 10 knots per hour,  two</p>
