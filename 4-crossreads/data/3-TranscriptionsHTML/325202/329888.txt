<p>The weather has broken and mud &amp; rain prevail &amp; we are back at last among winter conditions We have got the fire bucket going in the bivvy tonight for the first time since last winter</p>
<p>Oct 19<br/>Just three years since we sailed from Sydney Harbour</p>
<p>Yesterday afternoon I rode over to Vlamertinghe &amp; saw Tom the first time for 12 years &ndash;</p>
<p>am back at Scottish Line with D.H.Q the Company is still up at Dickebush and having a pretty hard time In addition to the ordinary work</p>
