 Tuesday 20th November 1917 Grooming and harness cleaning till11am. Saddled up a mule, and sent to a railway siding about six miles away for remounts. When we got to the siding, the train had not arrived so we had to wait about an hour. Brought two horses home. Arr. back in lines at 5.30 Mounted stable piquet 2nd shift at 7.20 to 9.40pm and 2 till 4am.
