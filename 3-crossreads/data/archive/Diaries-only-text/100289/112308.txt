Wednesday 10.10.17 to Sunday 14.10.17
Doing fine, all very quiet. Paraded before Major Colonel was marked for "Blighty" on 11th. Have been waiting to go each day since.
Monday 15.10.17
Received 3 parcels from home dated 20 June 4 14 July. Rigged out in an old khaki suit evacuated from here at about 8 pm. Driven by motor to Mers siding where we were put on hospital train which left about 11 pm
