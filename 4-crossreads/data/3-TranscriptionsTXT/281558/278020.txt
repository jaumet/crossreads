 as we had yesterday : the 18th took the part we did yesterday &amp; we had theirs : just waited in reserves till the Huns (18th) began to fall back &amp; then we followed them through the woods : nice stroll after tea &amp; listened to a "Tommie" band playing : 
 Tuesday Aug 7th 
 Westbecourt: warm day : revellie 6 am : packed up &amp; fell in 8 am : marched off with full pack till we reached the main road then we dropped our packs &amp; the lorries carried them : fair march to Arques &amp; went into the same tents : fine swim after tea : 
 Wednesday Aug 8th 
 Arques : clody : reveille 6.30 am : parade for "boys" : we S.B.s went for a stroll : helped to bring round a chap that was apparently drowned :
