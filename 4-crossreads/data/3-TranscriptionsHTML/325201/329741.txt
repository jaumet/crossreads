<p>19th Oct<br/>Yesterday was the third anniversary of our departure from Australia. And what remains of the first contingent here resolved to make it known <br/>Wherewith they fared forth &amp;   bought a case of whiskey &amp;   two of beer.<br/>I was in charge of the picket last night &amp;   so had to be careful not to drink too much. However I went along soon after the -what shall I call it - perhaps "bout" is the best, - after the bout started &amp;   thus from about 7 until 9.30 I drank neat whiskey</p>
