<p>Department of the Attorney General and of Justice<br/>Box 6A, G.P.O., Sydney.<br/>6th June, 1933.<br/>Ck. 33/4890 AGB.<br/>Dear Sir, I am directed by the Attorney General to inform you that His Excellency the Governor, with the advice of the Executive Council, has been pleased to appoint you to act as a District Court Judge and Chairman of Quarter Sessions for the State of New South Wales, from the 7th. June, 1933, to the 30th. June, 1933, both dates inclusive, and to authorise you to exercise jurisdiction in the Metropolitan District during the period mentioned.<br/>Your Commission and Railway and Tramway Book Pass No. 1341 are forwarded herewith.<br/>Yours faithfully, W.B. Geddes, Under Secretary of Justice<br/>K.A. Ferguson, Esquire, Barrister-at-Law, Chancery Chambers, Phillip Street, Sydney.</p>
