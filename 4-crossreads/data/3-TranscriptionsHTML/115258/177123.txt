Harold Thomas was in the camp whilst I was there, and we spent most of out time playing tennis in the morning; afternoon tea at the hospital and pictures or concert at YMCA in evening. The YM at the rest camp is about the best one in Egypt I think. Instead of picture decorations on its walls, the man in charge has got a lot of illuminated sentences framed and hung up. The same style as those &quot;What is home with out a Mother&quot; ones that you often see, but these ones are rather original I think</p>
