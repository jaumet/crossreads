<p>Were spent without tucker, having to rely on our twenty four hours reserve rations, which is four biscuits and a tin of bully. Things were in such a state that everything except 18 pounder shells had to be carried by fatique parties. This is very hard work indeed, and very little food reached us.<br/>18 pounder shells were carried up to them in reserve by Hundreds of pack mules each mule carrying six.
