  
 Yesterday evening we took the waterproof sheets off the roof, & partly dismantled our fine old bivouac, leaving a shell which, in its half trimmed condition, reminded us of the evacuated cottages. 
 Literary circle box of books, the "Chatte" box, & the Concert party box, were hurriedly packed & put on the waggons, & I proceed to sort out the books I really needed for a possible fortnight away from the waggons,
