After having Breakfast at 10 am we were paraded for a roll &amp; then dismissed until after Lunch when at 1-45 Pm we had a full dress parade &amp; were inspected by the Colonel of the Camp who spoke very feelingly in welcoming us to Bordon &amp; congratulated us on our successful although dangerous voyage from Australia. After giving us a few words of advice we were all Medically examined &amp; then given some drill to liven us up, as the Weather is very cold &amp; all of us feeling the change we welcomed the Marching Exercise. After witnessing a Fall in &amp; dismissal of a large number of Troops those movements were an eyeopener to us all. The Troops went</p>
