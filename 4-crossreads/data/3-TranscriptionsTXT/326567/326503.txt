Eggs can be bought retail at 2 &frac12; d each &amp; they buy them by the gross &amp; they have the cheek to charge us 10d for two also a small chop about 3rd of a lb also 10d anyhow after paying for them they gave us a fork, no knife &amp; we went into the next Room. There is no body there to clean up after one is finished so therefore the tables are very dirty.
