 July 27th Cannot sleep so well in this place too many in our room. 
 All officers who wished to give their Parole were allowed to for a swim this morning &amp; also for a walk round the hills afterwards. I did not go, though I would liked to have. If I could be taken in a cab, the outing would be very appreciable. 
 Recd 2 Pcls one of Bread biscuits from our Red Cross &amp; the other from Sir Henry Proctor Presd Y.M.C.A. 
 Played bridge tonight. Feeling very fed up with things in general, wish I was with Lanagan &amp; Gardiner &amp;c. 
 July 28th Good sleep. Played Bridge; Had my foot dressed by the Dr today, it is still discharging. Attended a Concert held tonight &ndash; it was an excellent show, violion solos  vocal  songs, stories &amp;c.
