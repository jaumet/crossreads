
Monday 10 February 1919Disinfecting saddlery & classing horses out to be shot & skinned tomorrow.Letter from Daisy, December 15 - sorry poor little Mother not been too well - of course I will telegraph from Western Australia my movements.Joe will be home by this.  I hope he escaped the flu.  Dear old Day has been very good in writing so often & so much.Jim Ayre may come.  Nowell has no intention but I have not seen or heard of him for a long time.We have no Padre only a drunken priest at present.
