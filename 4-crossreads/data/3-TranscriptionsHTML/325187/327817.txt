<p>12 March 1916 Sunday<br/>Caught the 8.30 A M back again- rode out 15 miles to Camp- all the better for my weekend.<br/><strike>Heard that the Infantry 1st &amp; 2nd Divisions are going to France <u>do not allow this to be circulated </u>  and I think we will be left in Egypt for the summer.</strike><br/>Saw Doly Wilshire in the Distance- but as I was in a train did not stop.<br/>If you do not hear from me for 6 weeks it is because our mail is stopped owing to leakage of information as per above  our outgoing English mail is to be stopped also.</p>
