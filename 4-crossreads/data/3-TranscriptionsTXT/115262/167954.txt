 Friday 6 July 1917. 
 Feeling off  cl &nbsp;color today. Riding test this morning. Passed alright and in the final allotment for our unit with is to be Imperial Camel Corps Brigade Field Ambulance. I am included in the Bearer Division. G.S.S.Cmn. also. 
 Paraded sick tonight and am at present in Hospital (Field Amb.) 
 A 4 funnelled Cruiser is this morning anchored in the Canal off Ferry&#39;s Post at entrance to Bitter Lakes. 
 Troops are concentrating at Kantara now, Thousands of Tommies have left here for there.
