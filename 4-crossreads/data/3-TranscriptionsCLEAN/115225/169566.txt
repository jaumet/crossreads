May 23rd  We went to Ailly-S.S. for petrol & oil then went out for a load of wood for the cook house (scrounging) & Incidently took a load of furniture to a place about eight Kilos from Picquigny, we had a lovely dinner with the Froggie at his country house - & on the way back to the park we picked up a load of light trees & were dry. We managed to drag them out of the wood & make a respectable load.  We got back to park at 5.P.M. & went down after tea for a swim -
 
May 24th  We went to Ailly.S.S. for Petrol & oil again & went out for another load of wood We took another load from Amiens to the same place as yesterday & again had dinner with the French family & picked up a load of wood on our way home. we got to park at 3.30 P.M. & after tea went down for a swim -
