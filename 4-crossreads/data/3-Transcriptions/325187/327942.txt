<p>[Tuesday 18 July 1916]</p>
<p><strike>Letters today from Mother June 4  England.<br/>Also English papers-</strike>   Mails are delayed a lot now-, but am glad all's well.<br/>Enemy plane came again but respects our gunfire &amp; did not drop any bombs.<br/>Altho the Weather is hot I have a rotten cold &amp; feel off color, but only a passing cloud no doubt &amp; have been fit for a long time.</p>
