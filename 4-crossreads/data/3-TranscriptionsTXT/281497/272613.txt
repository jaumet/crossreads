All day long the ground rocked &amp; swayed backwards &amp; forwards from the concussion of this frightful bombardment, it put me in mind of being on top of a well built haystack &amp; swaying it about, thats how the ground behaved, any amount of men were driven stark staring mad &amp; more than one of them rushed out of the trench, over towards the German's, any amount of them could be seen crying &amp; sobbing like children their nerves completely gone, how on earth we stood it God alone knows, we were nearly all in a state of silliness &amp; half dazed
