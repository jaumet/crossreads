<p>June, 1915<br/>Friday 25<br/>took Sister <strike>Nellie Hill</strike> late of Burrabogie for a run to Pyramids in Brigade car last night, she had news of Mrs Mills &amp; all Hayites &amp; is a most the most energetic of the nurses sent from Australia. All Cairo is closed now at 10 p.m. I could give you a lot of Military news about our men at Gallipoli &amp; what is doing here, but cannot <strike>break faith as an officer hence I fill up Diary with <u>Garden</u> stuff.</strike></p>
