
Tuesday March 13Another day on horses. Had some good gallops which finished up in a race. Charley[?] Woods winner. Jimmy Woodcock leading when his neddy misjudged jumping trench & landed him in it. The evenings drag hereWednesday March 14thDay out in sham fight with L H. Regiment. Officers simply do not know what to give the men to do to fill in time while we are waiting. Everybody fed up including myself.Thursday March 15Rain heavily all day did our best to keep dryFriday March 16Inspection by General Featherstone leave granted from tonight. Arrived
