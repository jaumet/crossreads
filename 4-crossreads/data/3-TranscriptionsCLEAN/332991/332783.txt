There are 30 iron beds in the ward, and 4 or 5 blankets to each ward.  There is a stove near the centre of the building and attached to one corner outside there is a small latrine for the use of patients at night & those who cannot walk very well. The ward is fairly comfortable, but in very cold weather the best way to keep warm is in bed.  The other wards are similar in construction.  There are two orderlies on duty in the day time and one at night.  The doctor visits the ward in the morning & evening.
