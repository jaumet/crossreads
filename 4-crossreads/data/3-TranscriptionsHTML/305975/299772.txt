<p>Saturday Feb 1.2.19<br/>turned out 6.35  went on Post.   Breakfast 7.30  was relieved from guard 9.   marched back to camp  dismissed.   rested all evening<br/>Sunday Feb 2.2.19<br/>turned out 6.30  game of footBall looked on  fed up Breakfast  cleaned up   rested all day.<br/>Monday Feb. 3.2.19.<br/>turned out 6.30 AM  fed up breakfast  mounted Parade 10.15. till 12.  rested all evening.</p>
