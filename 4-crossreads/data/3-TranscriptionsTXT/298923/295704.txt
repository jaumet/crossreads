 7 February 1918 Thursday Regimental Sports postponed. Sports postponed owing to rain    inches fell. Paid all hands, am president of a Court Martial tomorrow &amp; still doing Topographical work under Capt McLean the Chairman's son-  quite  a good chap- Sent a party to collect BOMBS left by the Turks as we don't want accidents on Sports Day- &amp; many varieties lie about with half buried Turks.  Our own men &amp; N. Zealanders are reverently buried a wooden cross at their head &amp; Records kept
