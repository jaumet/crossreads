<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Had tea.&nbsp; Got some physic from Sister.&nbsp; Felt better and spent evening by fire.&nbsp; Turned in early and slept well.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feby 8</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Not too good.&nbsp; Did not go out to breakfast.&nbsp; Had it in the ward.&nbsp; Still have the complaint.&nbsp; Day very wet and miserable.&nbsp; Dr. saw me and marked me for board.&nbsp; Saw eye specialist who advised me not to have any operation on my eye.&nbsp; Marked C3.&nbsp; Day turned out nice and bright.&nbsp; Spent most of it writing.&nbsp; Had afternoon tea in reading room with Lenehan.&nbsp; Turned in early.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feby 9</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Did not get up till 1.30 under Dr&#39;s orders.&nbsp; Went for board when previous marking was confirmed.&nbsp; Mrs and Mrs Linley came to see me.&nbsp; Very happy to see them.&nbsp; They brought all sorts of nice things.&nbsp; Sorry to have to say good bye to them again.&nbsp; Feel well to-day.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 10</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 2.&nbsp; Herb Larsen came down from London to see me.&nbsp; Spent a few very pleasant hours with him.&nbsp; He left at 4.&nbsp; Feel very well.&nbsp; Wrote after Larsen went until tea time.&nbsp; Turned in early.</td>
</tr>

<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 2.&nbsp; Wrote all afternoon till tea time.&nbsp; Arm hurts to write very much.&nbsp; Had visit from Lt. Wyndham, sho came from Mrs Shellshear.</td>
</tr>

<tr>

<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 7.&nbsp; Feel great.&nbsp; Spent morning writing and yarning by fire.&nbsp; Mr Brooke and Mrs Linley came down in afternoon.&nbsp; Spent most enjoyable time with them.&nbsp; Mrs Linley came loaded up with good things, as usual, as did Mr Brooke.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 14</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 7.&nbsp; Spent morning by fire reading.&nbsp; Wrote all afternoon by fire.&nbsp; Told I was to have 4 days leave.&nbsp; Leaving for London in a few days.&nbsp; Cannot get more leave as the Dr wants me to catch the Hospital ship home as soon as possible on account of my weak state.&nbsp; Feel fairly well.</td>
</tr>
