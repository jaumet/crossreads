<p>May 1/16<br/>We moved our camp yesterday to a spot near the "Supply Depot" I was only thinking that in happier times it would be the ideal spot for a week end camp. In a <strike>farm</strike> field adjoining the farm buildings we have pitched tents near the hawthorn hedge &amp; under some high trees. There is the farm pond close by &amp; at the farm we can buy milk</p>
