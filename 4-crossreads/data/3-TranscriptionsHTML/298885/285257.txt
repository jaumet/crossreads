<p>Oct 11th &ndash; 18<br/>Yesterday met Stan Rein and Bob Simpson whom I had not seen for 12 months</p>
<p>Oct 14th &ndash; 18<br/>Following a clue which came my way, I was soon with the knowledge of Norries whereabouts and lost no time in covering the 12 miles which separates us for the next 3 months</p>
<p>Anyhow after not seeing him for 8 or 9 months our pleasure in meeting can be imagined</p>
<p>Oct 19th &ndash; 18<br/>after riding to 5th Bty and asking for Charlie Burbidge</p>
