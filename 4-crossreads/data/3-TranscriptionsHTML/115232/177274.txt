<p>Wirelesss -<br />From Naval Board Melbourne<br />To Comg Off HMAS Berrima<br />Date 21st Aug 1914</p>
<p>Message from Secretary of State for Colonies -<br />In connection expedition against German possessions in Pacific - British flag should be hoisted in all Territory occupied with success by His Majestys Forces and suitable arrangements made for temporary administration.&nbsp; No formal proclamation of annexation should [indecipherable] be made without previous communication with His Majestys Government</p>
<p>Hoist Flag<br />proclaim to inhabitants that occupation has been established<br />Take charge of PO and offices of officials<br />Search for arms</p>
