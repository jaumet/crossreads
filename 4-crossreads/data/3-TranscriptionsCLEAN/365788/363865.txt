There is no where else to go as girls Cant very well come into the Camp. We had quite a nice time & on the last occasion Mrs Connor, (wife of Dr Connor) a very nice woman who used to live on the Richmond & a girl named Hariett from Parkes, and the brother of Miss Hindmarsh several other soldiers & I all sat down together & formed quite a nice luncheon party.
