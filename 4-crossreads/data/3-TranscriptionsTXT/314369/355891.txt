 18 September 1915 Saturday 
 Still going strong temperature normal   &amp;  eating like a horse. Put hot foment on foot to try  &amp;  soften it a bit Expect to be allowed up soon. 
 1915 September 19 Sunday 16th After Trinity 
 Feeling a heap better to day  &amp;  am allowed to get out of bed for which I am truly thankful. I was getting pretty sick of lying down after 20 days
