<p>No. Rifle  7628  8/3/15 camped at Broadmeadows<br/>Left Broadmeadows for Seymour 6/6/15. Sailed from Port Melbourne on S.S. Wandilla on Thursday 17/6/15 at 4.30 a.m. Arrived at Fremantle Wed. 23/6/15. Sailed again Fri. 25/6/15. Arrived at Aden midnight Sun. 11/7/15. Sailed again next morning 12/7/15. Arrived at Port Suez midnight Friday 16/7/15. Disembarked and boarded train for Cairo next day 1.30 arrived there 8 oclock same evening marched into camp</p>
