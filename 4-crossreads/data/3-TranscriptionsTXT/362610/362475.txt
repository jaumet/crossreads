Saturday 16 Sept. 1916A gloriously bright morning but overcast later, then bright again in the afternoon &amp; evening. At the Div. Gas School in the morning &amp; then walked to the Div. Baths at Hoppoutre where I enjoyed a good wash. In the afterward busy with the fitting of new respirators in the 1st Bn., &amp; in the evening writing home.
