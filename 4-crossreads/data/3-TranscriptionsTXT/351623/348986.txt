 11 June 16 At sea 
 12 June 16 Arrived at Grand Cayman &amp; anchored for a few hours &amp; left again for Jamaica. 
 13 June 16 Arrived at Kingston Jamaica &amp; tied up alongside pier in morning watch. Received a mail from Australia. HMAS Sydney is here. 
 14 June 16 At Kingston. Coaling ship all day. 
 15 June 16 ) 16 June 16 ) 17 June 16 )  Alongside pier at Kingston 18 June 16 ) 19 June 16 ) 
 20 June 16 Went down &amp; anchored at Port Royal. Sydney also anchored at Port Royal.
