 Sunday 6th Feb. 
 Paid on Friday last at Intermediate Base &amp; met Land in evening, &amp; spent happy time at pictures.  Saw others of 3rd Field Ambulance &amp; had good chat with Kennedy who is coming out to see me. Last  might  night Jack Buckle came to see me &amp; we had a cup of tea in the hospital.
