Then we started to return only got a little way, when the wind dropped &amp; we were becalmed &ndash; It was beautiful on the Lake, the moon rising was a grand sight &ndash; but we were all so worried that we could not enjoy it to its full. Anyhow we reached the pier at 9.15 p.m.  &amp; rushed to "The Belgian"  Had the Arabs hurrying as they had never done before. All had a ripping dinner &amp; returned to Home just as the clock
