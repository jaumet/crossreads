Our friend Barnes of the original 3rd Fld  Amb., in  a state of intoxication, was a frequent demander of "Spells"; but the boys themselves silenced him Clough got a ride in a waggon looking after officers kit, & Eva, Gray & I did the remainder with many a cheerful jest & without turning a hair. Packed into trucks at the Docks station & our truck, capable of holding 10 comfortably, received forty & so it was a night of misery.
