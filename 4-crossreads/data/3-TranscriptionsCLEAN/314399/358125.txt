
Thursday 8 May 1919Bonny day again. Saw Bill. He looks much better. Had another driving lesson. Went out to Brixton for tea & came home at 11 pm.
Friday 9 May 1919Lovely day again. Real decent weather at last. Went to the school. Had another driving lesson. Am doing well. After the lesson saw Bill. He looks a bit better. Then out to see Janet & Marie at their Jumble sale. Had tea there. Just as I left it rained very heavily.
