The whole Battn. including transports & horses went in the one train which was about as long as the average goods train in N.S.W.   We were in cattle trucks, of course, but made ourselves very comfortable, and slept all night like a litter of kittens, with legs & arms & heads & bodies  in an   tangled in every possible manner.   In this way we are able to keep warm, and also with a little difficulty, to find a soft, warm pillow.
