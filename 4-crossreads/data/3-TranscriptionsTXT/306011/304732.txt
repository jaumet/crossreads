 Thursday, 31st May 1917 First saw American Troops arriving in France last night. They arrived at Boulogne and probably belong to a Medical Unit &ndash; all were young men &amp; in brand new uniforms. 
 Had a yarn to a French-Canadian yesterday who didn't speak a word of English. He was born in Quebec and was now in France for the first time &ndash; he said he wasn't anxious to stay in France and would be quite satisfied to get back to Canada. Exped. Force entirely comprised of French-Canadians and all French speaking Canadians that now come over reinforce the Battalion.
