 25. Thursday Worked a lot in garden &amp;  had an attack of malaria  Went to bed in discomfort &amp; with a temp. of 103 &deg; 
 26. Friday Fine day.  Got up at 12. feeling very weak but improved &amp;  did a good deal of work in garden 
 27. Saturday Fine day at last.  Worked in garden some &amp; wrote some.  Guns waked us at 20 to 12 last night but no raid ensued.
