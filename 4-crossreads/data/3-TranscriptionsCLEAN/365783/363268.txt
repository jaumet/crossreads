
Before we were out of the harbour I dived down to my cabin & made it a bit ship shape in case things were not too good later on. It came on pretty rough during the night and all day yesterday & last night things were anything but comfortable however I managed to keep my end up all night. Today it is quite pleasant  Nearly all the nurses were very much off colour yesterday but this morning they are quite sprightly - there are no beauties among them but some of them seem very decent women. I was field officer of the day yesterday & my duties brought me all over the ship including the men's quarters on the troop decks.
