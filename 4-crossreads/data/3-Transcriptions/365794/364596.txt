<p>Saturday 14 [13] July 1917 [1918]<br/>Wrote Nell &amp; sent Muscio's letter.  Wrote Edith, Ida, Mrs. Luke.  All goes very nicely in the wards.  S. Nicol &amp; V.A.D. Dalzeel both good &amp; the boys all that one could wish for.  "Off"? well Miss Gemmell never loses an opportunity of being rude to me.  Miss Cheetham continues to post notices up from day to day, re our badness.  5-8 pass, went 27th G.H., everyone so kind there &amp; had long talk with S. Leed.  Arranged flowers in church 8.30 p.m.</p>
