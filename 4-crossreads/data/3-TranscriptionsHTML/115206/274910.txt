<p>December  1918.<br/>Friday 6</p>
<p>Wembley On furlough.<br/>Went up town to meet Smithie but arrived late and <br/>was of course disappointed. Ran home to see <br/>percy's friend and missed them there. Ran out to<br/>Mrs Rainers with Olive Very decent people they<br/>seem<br/>Arrived home and got in  A1<br/>Heard that D25 and D26 sail on 12th</p>
