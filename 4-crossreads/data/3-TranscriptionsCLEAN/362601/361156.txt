
8 February, Saturday
F & iceWe both go down to Dance & I have a good time. I scale Taney for the Maxima but fix things up. We make arrangements about a towel exchange. I am improving on my dancing & reverse rather better. I hop in to go Home with & M
9 February, Sunday
F & iceWork hard all day, go up for Tea to see Doris & other W.A.A.C.  for Tea. I go down to Y.M.C.A. a bit late & have to sit with Dick & others until 8 pm & then I go to sit with Q. M. We have a confidential chat & part good chums at the out of Bounds notice. Music good & enjoy myself.
