<p>Things are very dear about here. &nbsp;If you happen on an egg it costs 1 franc (9&frac14;d). &nbsp;Boose too is dear, and I could picture ourselves in the old days paying 1 franc or 1.50 for a bottle of crook beer. &nbsp;Things will gradually get better of course, it it will be some time (and perhaps we won&#39;t ve here) to get even fair value for our money.</p>
