19 January 1916
Wednesday
Up betimes homecoming. Meet 9 other at at Prince Alfred at 8.30 am. Dr. Willis takes us in hand. View 11 adenoid operations, 3 circ. in forenoon. Go to lunch at Brodies. Back in afternoon to Hospital. View 3 major operations viz Adhesion of stomach, hernia lump on neck. Tea with boys at Newtown back to hospital after; Venereal. Back to Macs for supper. Home at 10.30
Ashley Sid Woodward leave for front.
