<p>30th  Left Cape Town.  Outside the harbour started rolling.  Many interested <strike>in the</strike> hugging the railings in a loving embrace appear interested in the water. rolling increased  Learning  expression appears in many faces.  <strike>Speed of boat</strike> As one of our poets has expressed it 'the eternal main thing is to see the main spring"  The spring is, of course, upwards &amp; not forwards.  In fact at one time it appeared doubtful she was moving at all, for a lighthouse off our [indecipherable] she seemed to be holding its own.</p>
<p>31st  This being the day of rest the Main is to be commended in having made better headway.  She is now pitching as well as rolling.  General financial depression as a result of gay life in Cape Town. Unaccountable popularity of pay staff.  Many offers of refreshment in harbour.</p>
