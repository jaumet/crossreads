<p>Monday 10 May 1915<br />Sent 2 bottles of Champagne to Col Martin today in payment for bet made some time ago. saying that we should be away from Mena meaning Egypt by 9<sup>th</sup> May 1915<br />Ride between 7 &amp; 8 am.<br />About 12 patients came in up to 6 p.m. today. Some forty patients bewteen 6 &amp; 7 pm<br />In town for 1 hour at eight.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11&nbsp;May 1915<br />About the Hospital all day.<br />Patients coming &amp; going all day.<br />Everything all right&nbsp;<br />A good assistant in Captain Plant not lazy, or too Swell.</p>
<p>Wrote to F.&nbsp;Fletcher, Col Martin, &amp; Rene Silk</p>
<p>Wednesday 12&nbsp;May 1915</p>
<p>Ride before breakfast&nbsp;</p>
<p>Posted letters F. Fletcher, Col Martin , Rene Silk.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Not out during the day. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Reading &amp; writing, &amp; attending to patients all the day</p>
