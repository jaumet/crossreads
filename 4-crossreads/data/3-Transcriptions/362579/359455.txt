<p>No. 2. Aust. Aux. Hosp.<br/>Southall.<br/>Middlesex.<br/>England.<br/>16.12.17.</p>
<p>My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters.,</p>
<p>Saw Matron-in-Chief on Thursday morning   &amp;  she told me I was not leaving until January. So perhaps it will be February   &amp;  I shall get to Aust. about April. Anyhow 'm very comfortable at present   &amp;  like the work here but it is a rest after the work I've had.</p>
<p>No letters this week from home, hope to get some before Xmas. Yesterday I had a Day-off. Stayed in bed until 11. a.m. dressed   &amp;  had an early lunch, then caught the train for Paddington.
