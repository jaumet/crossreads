<p>We arrived at Leith (Scotland) at 11 p.m. on the 18th December and dropped anchor, and waited for the tide.  We did not go alongside the docks until 2 p.m. on the 19th December.  There were two Scotish band on the Docks to meet us and hundreds of school children and lots of other people.  We didn't get a chance to cheer, we had to be content to listen to them.</p>
