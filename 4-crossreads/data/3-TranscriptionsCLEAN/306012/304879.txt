An old chap told me that the evacuation took place about a fortnight ago - when the Germans were making good progress.  I have been in various unoccupied shops & houses and they are all in a state of wild confusion - clothes tumbled out of boxes and drawers, crockery smashed, cupboards ransacked etc.;  looting by the troops for the most part I am ashamed to have to say.  There are two or three estaminets - it is needless to describe their condition.
