 Sunday the 12th of May. Late reports say that the Germans have been pushed back in the region of Ypres by the French. They claim that the artillery fire was the worst experienced during the war. Sunday the 19th and Monday the 20th are public holidays. On Monday 5 Russians escaped, but were brought back a day or two after, and unmercifully beaten by the Germans with a whip.
