
1915Jan. 9 Sat.Attended physical drill at 7 a.m.Sea a trifle rough with the result that I "fed the fishes" twice and felt a bit off during the morning.During the day read over some shorthand and learnt a few points about the typewriter. Have resolved to study on board :-(1) Military books, regulations, etc.(2) The Remington typewriter and all about it.(3) Short-hand.(4) French(5) Books on other educational subjects.
Sea much calmer in the evening.
