<p>contain spy code information.<br/>Put in an application to O.C. asking that I may be transferred to a unit where I could be more suitably employed. My C.O.s gave me a good recommendation and officer promised to put it through to H.Q.<br/><u>Thu 14</u> More rain, plenty of mud. Camp again shelled.<br/><u>Fri 15th</u>. Our locality still being shelled. Gas somewhat troublesome.<br/><u>Saty 16</u> Everything fairly quiet. One shell fell within few feet of 40 men camped in surface structures. No damage done.<br/><u>Sun 17th</u>. All round us enemy artillery is searching for batteries with heavy shells. Our camp is in a bad position. A road and a light railway encircle it. Near us is situated a dump and our home is far more <strike>conspicious</strike> conspicuous than it should be. Complaints have already been made regarding this and we hear today that we are to be removed, shortly, to a spot not under direct observation. The heavy shelling continues yet our narrow strip of territory remains unmolested.</p>
