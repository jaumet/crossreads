 Friday (Oct. 30th) marks the first anniversary of Roy's Great Sacrifice &ndash; I write a simple but sincere tribute. 
 Oct. 31st Rehearsal &amp; change of programme &ndash; Missing" letters from Home come to hand &ndash; Another of Maeterlinck's books are read &ndash; "The Treasure of the Humble" and much enjoyed. The end of a great month and one which brings us nearer to Home.
