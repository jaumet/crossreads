
February, 1915Tuesday 2Arrived Alexandria 10 a.m. & waited all day for orders to go along side wharf -Night came & still here Very many captured Turkish German boats are here & the American Battleship Tennessee sailed out as we entered; our men fell foul of their Sailors had a fight.[indecipherable] O.B. Rylie met me on board, from the Reg gave all the news was glad to see him & went ashore.
