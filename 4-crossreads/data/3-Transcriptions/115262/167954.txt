<p>Friday 6 July 1917.</p>
<p>Feeling off <s>cl</s>&nbsp;color today. Riding test this morning. Passed alright and in the final allotment for our unit with is to be Imperial Camel Corps Brigade Field Ambulance. I am included in the Bearer Division. G.S.S.Cmn. also.</p>
<p>Paraded sick tonight and am at present in Hospital (Field Amb.)</p>
<p>A 4 funnelled Cruiser is this morning anchored in the Canal off Ferry&#39;s Post at entrance to Bitter Lakes.</p>
<p>Troops are concentrating at Kantara now, Thousands of Tommies have left here for there.</p>
