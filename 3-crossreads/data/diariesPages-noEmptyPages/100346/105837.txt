finished up through Calais to Audruicq : into huts cleaned ourselves up :
Saturday March 30th
Audruicq : cleaned up, washing etc during day : had a walk round the town after tea it is a decent joint :
Sunday March 31st
Audruicq : H.Comm in morn very nice : stroll round in afternoon after tea enjoyed it
Monday April 1st
Audruicq : nothing much doing : paid 20 francs in afternoon : went tom theatre with Gill after tea it was a good show too : lady impersonaters very good one had a splendid voice into the bargain : air raid at night :
