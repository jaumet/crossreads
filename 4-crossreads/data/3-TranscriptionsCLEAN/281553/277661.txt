
Nov. 18th ThursdayWeather again simply perfect with slightest breeze drying up things wonderfully.  Made some improvements to dug-out against rain.  6-30 p.m. to 10-30 p.m. on Ammunition Fatigue to Beach & back with bomb mule transport.  Shelled.
Nov. 19th FridayPerfect day.  Writing morning.  Gass apparatus inspected.  Writing evening.
Nov. 20th SaturdayMorning reading & writing, afternoon issuing underwear for Reserve.  Ammunition Fatigue 6-30 to 9-30 p.m.
Nov. 21 SundayEileen Pcl.Weather very dull and bitterly cold with wind.
