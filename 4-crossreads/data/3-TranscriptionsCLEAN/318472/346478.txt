
10 September 1918TuesdayLeft Port about 7.30 A.M. "Barambali D37" went out in Front of us carrying troops apparently for England. A beautiful day & nice calm sea. Everything appears to be going well on the Western Front the Allies advancing everywhere.
1918 September 11WednesdayMaking slow progress in beautiful weather & a nice sea. Passed Albany shortly after lunch time. We are running fairly close to the coast & can see it as far as the eye can reach. Nothing of importance happening on board.
