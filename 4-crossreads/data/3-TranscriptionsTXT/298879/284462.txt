 November 1915 Thursday 4 Windy    Our artillery fired a few shots this morning without getting a reply. Our H.Q. Sig. section are having an easy time. 
 Friday 5 More wind. We have a 'phone in the trenches and I went on this morning from 0800 to 1230 noon. Artillery duels are the order of the day. Our L. Horse gained a further 30 yds forward last night
