<p>August,1918<br/>Friday 16</p>
<p>Cayeau<br/>Still at this rather pretty place. But somehow after last night I can't claim to like it very much.<br/>Out salvaging wire all day. Ken Gunn rode across to new Bty position near Morecourt, and he arrived home looking fairly tired.<br/>Made up the old tarphouse again.<br/>Had a few Fritz's  across also a boots enclisers.<br/>Inspected a captured gun</p>
