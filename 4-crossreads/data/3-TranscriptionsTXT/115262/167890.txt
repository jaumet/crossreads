 Thursday 3 May 1917. 
 Reveille at 6.10 a.m. Breakfast at&nbsp; 8 p &nbsp;8 a.m. &nbsp;Left Yea 8.30 a.m. 
 Homewood 10.20&nbsp; p &nbsp; &nbsp;a.m. 
 Setting for final plunge to &quot;CAMP&quot;. 
 Dinner at Kerrisdale &amp; some more dropped out. Horses in G.S transport wagon knocking up. 
 Now gone 12 miles. Next stop at&nbsp;Trawool Hotel at 3 pm. Left Trawool Hotel at 3.15 pm. [After lunch we left Kerrisdale at 1.37 p.m.] 
 Arrived in camp at 5.15 p.m. 
 After&nbsp; tea  &nbsp;a&nbsp;shower then tea &amp; turned in at 8.30 p.m.
