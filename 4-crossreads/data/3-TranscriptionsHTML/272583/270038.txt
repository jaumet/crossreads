While they were in sight it seemed as though they were travelling through sand and not water on account of so many turns and twists.<br/>4.30.  The Sun is now sinking and the scenery about is something magnificent  In fact it is so great that it is impossible to describe.<br/>We are at present taking in fresh water.<br/>To-days order is that we will go on to Alexandra where we will disembark and proceed to Cairo by train.<br/>2nd <br/>Very dull day. No further move yet but will enter the canal some time to-day<br/>The Main Guard is now issued with ball cartridges the reason of which we are told is because it is expected we will be fired on any minute<br/>Moved off at 1 pm</p>
