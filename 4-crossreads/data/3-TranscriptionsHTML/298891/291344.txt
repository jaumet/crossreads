<p>1917 September</p>
<p>18 Tuesday</p>
<p>Feeling awful.  Brought down to the sick tent with temp. of 104.  Heart fluttering &amp; head aching.  Lay in the heat &amp; flies walked over me.  Oh the noise of heavy boots &amp; harsh voices in endless idle chatter.  No sleep during night.</p>
