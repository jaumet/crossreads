After this attack was over we formed up &amp; had a spell, of course the Gypsies were about with their baskets of chocolates oranges lollies apples &amp; etc. they are a dirty looking crowd &amp; resemble the Gyppos in their habits, to my mind they still show traces of Spanish blood for the girls are very dark, &amp; some of them not bad looking there are quite a number round these parts.<br/>Coming home I had to organise my Coy. for an attack on a small wood, had to arrange it so that after extending the whole Coy had to enter the wood together, miss no ground while going through, &amp; come out</p>
