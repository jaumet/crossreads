<p>Wednesday 8/3/16<br/>Caught 7.15.AM train from Tel-el-Kebir, on leave to Cairo.  Very pleasant trip through; arrived Cairo 9.30 AM.  Reg &amp; I bought a camera each &amp; then went direct to Roberts &amp; Hughes to see Mr Roberts, who unfortunately is at present in England.<br/>Left for Pyramids 10.30 AM with guide &amp; after spending a very interesting few hours arrived back to the city at 2 pm for dinner.
