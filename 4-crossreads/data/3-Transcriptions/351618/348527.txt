<p>Thursday 17th June<br/>I wake early to find a very cool and crisp and strong breeze blowing. We were now amongst the Islands of the Agean Sea. We passed several very high ones. The waves were lashing themselves into a white foam with the breeze but no sign was yet discovered of a submarine.<br/>Fare on board was not of the best. RMLI Scott- Bradshaw, Sergt Wallace, E.L Hughes-Darfield R.D.F Morris S.W.B.</p>
