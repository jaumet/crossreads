<p>Mines Dept. Sydney<br/>18 May 1915.</p>
<p>Dear Alan,<br/>We are wondering every day whether you are still in Egypt, &amp; hoping that if you have gone on to Gallipoli you are still safe. I know very well, if there is fighting to be done, that you will stand your risk with the others &amp; will be in the front rank, but I cannot shut my eyes to the risk being great. I hope for the best.</p>
<p>The casualty lists so far published here have been undeniably heavy, but the Australians have made a name that will last in their history, for gallantry &amp; success. All the papers &amp; all the people thrill with pride at the accounts of how they landed at Saba tepe (or whatever it is) &amp; held on in face of the Turks &amp; Huns.</p>
