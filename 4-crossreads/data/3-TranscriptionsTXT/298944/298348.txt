 So we move more quickly now, &amp; more comfortably: coffee or cocoa supplied by Y.M.C.A. or Aust Comforts Fund appears at stopping places. Last night at Wizerne we each had a cup of cocoa &amp; 10 White City cigarettes; which are above the ordinary run. At the same time, there should be quicker communication with the Somme from the Northern sector, than a night's railway journey via Abbeville.
