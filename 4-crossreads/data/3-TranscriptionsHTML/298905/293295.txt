<p>wasn't damaged much<br/>This evening we all packed up for we were being relieved by the 45th Batt. &amp; as I write this some of them are taking over the billets from us.<br/>Put most of the afternoon in playing Bridge", its a game I am very fond of.<br/>4th. At 8 oclock last night we held a full muster parade, &amp; were ready to move off, but we had to wait for the 45th. to come in, they kept us waiting there till 12.30. real old military style.<br/>We reached our old billets behind Sailly after a march of about 8 miles most of us were nearly knocked out when we got here which was about 4.a.m. some of us no sooner had our packs off when we made a break for some cherry trees that we knew were here, we had a good feed</p>
