 W3 arrive tea time. 25-2-17 Midnight to 2 am shift.  Printing photos all day.  Bedouins arrive with goats &amp; donks &amp; camels having been taken prisoners.   Mail &amp; surprise  26-2-17 Move over to the beach and bring the long wave gear to the site of the ancient Grecian  ornithological church. 27-28 and on to 2nd erecting &amp; preparing for the others to come. 3/3/17 Hooker arrives back from the hospital. 4-3/17 Down swimming &ndash; cold bleak day. 5/3/17 -6-7-8 down swimming and usual days. 9-10 Gales of wind blowing.  Two hostiles over towards evening. 11/3 Leave Clay Bluff at 9 am for Bir Shunnar arriving midday 12-/3 On watch 4 to 8 am &ndash; Two hostiles over 13/3/-  W3 leaves for Rafa.  Bill Cole leaves for A.M.C. 14/3/17 Squadron have horse races.  Testing gelignite on the beach. 15/3 Go to rafa with trench set &amp; new methods which turn out a failure. 16 out again on sets.  Taubes quite game hovering over. 18= Print all day &amp; surf. 19th Playing about with trench sets all day. 20/3/17 Go to Khan Yunus playing with trench sets. 21/3/17 [indecipherable] to 12 to 4 am watch.  Leave Bir Shunnar 9 am for NZ's &ndash; the great stunt.  Westwood from W3 remains as operator.  Three days rations issued and orders to "live on the country ahead if necessary.  Promises of Stoush.
