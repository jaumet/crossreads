 Oct 22nd My last night in pyjas &amp;   comfort, we move out at dusk the day after tomorrow I believe on a big stoush. All moving to be done at night henceforth which is particularly uncomfortable. I am getting too old for war. Oct 27th "Essani" We are at the second stage of this adventure now.
