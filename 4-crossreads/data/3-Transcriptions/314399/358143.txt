<p>Monday 26 May 1919<br/>Didn't know what to do with myself in the evening so went to the new theatre Winter Garden &amp; saw Kissing Time. Phyllis Dare &amp; Co. Was very good. Started at 8 pm &amp; finished at 11.10 pm. Was quite decent tho.<br/>Very dull day.</p>
<p>Tuesday 27 May 1919<br/>Fine again this morning. Went to bank &amp; drew out &pound;5 &amp; paid in 13/-. Actually did some work today at the school. My writing yesterday bore fruit. SWent out to Brixton for tea. Wrote a long letter to Janet.</p>
