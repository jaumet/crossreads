 13 April 1918 Saturday A very Cool Change after bright warm weather. In the evening R.S.M. Smith &amp; I went into Salisbury We went to see a couple of Shows which were fairly good &amp; altogether had a fairly good time. 
 1918 April 14 Sunday 2 after Easter 
 Same old Stuff In the afternoon Smith &amp; I went into Dinton a very small village on the South Western Line. Had tea there &amp; afterward went to Church (C.E) in a quaint old Church a mile or so out of the town. A number of the boys who have died at Fovant Hospital are buried in the Church yard.
