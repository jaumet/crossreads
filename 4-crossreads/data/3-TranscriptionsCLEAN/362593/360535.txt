  
 Tuesday 2 April 1918 News of Oliver on 30th March false - refers to officers captured in Mesopotamia. 
 Wednesday 3 April 1918 Escort to bomb raid.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 3 h. 5 m.  A good trip to Amman, fired 500 rounds at troop train and Amman station at height of 1000 to 2000 ft., train put on steam and went like the Devil.
