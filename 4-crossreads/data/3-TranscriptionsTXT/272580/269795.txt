Our journey along the Australian coast in the direction of Cape York was not one of any great moment. Thursday Island was our first port of call. The boys expected to be put ashore here but they were sadly disappointed. It is not usual to allow troops ashore from a troopship until their destination has been reached. The need to adhere to this rule was forcibly exemplified practically at Colombo our first port of call.
