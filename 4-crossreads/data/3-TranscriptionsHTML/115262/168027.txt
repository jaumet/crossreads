<p>Monday 17 September 1917.</p>
<p>Biplanes coming to new Aerodrome in large numbers <s>th</s> today. All new machines too.</p>
<p>Duties:- Despatch rider for week.</p>
<p>It is said that the Aerodrome is being opened today.</p>
<p>3<sup>rd</sup> &amp; 4<sup>th</sup> Brigade on the rising ground towards Marakeb start to move out to the other side of the &quot;Waddy&quot;.&nbsp;</p>
