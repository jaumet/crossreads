<p>September 1918.<br/>Wednesday 11</p>
<p>In Reserve and at Hebecourt<br/>Weather rotten. Heavy rains throughout day and during evening. Relieved by 42nd Battery  4th Div, battery proceeded via Cortigny, Le Mesnil, Barleaux and Flaucort  to a position near Hebecourt.<br/>Spent the night very comfortably. Fritz bombed as usual some fairly close.</p>
