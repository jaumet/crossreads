Suppose you will all think I'm foolish staying here for another Summer but really I think I'm doing the best. Though I do so long to get  <u>home</u> to my dearest mother &amp; fond sisters &amp;  darling nephew. I've sent my dress-basket by Cook's &amp; Sons, hope it arrives safely. It should not cost you anything because I've paid  &pound; 2.0.0 this end. I had too much luggage, so decided to send it to you &ndash; Hope you will be able to make use of the articles. If things go well &amp; I want civilian clothes before I return I can easily cable for money &amp; buy new ones. I have plenty of money to go on with. My uniforms are getting rather shabby.
