 Last two shows at the Olympia &ndash; Second House proves most enthusiastic of the week. April 7th Take 10.30 a.m. train for Manchester &ndash; Arrive there at noon &ndash; After two or three hours tramping in the rain (which is not devoid of humour) we at last find lodgings &ndash; Very comfortable we hope to be in this new place &ndash; A trip out towards Cheadle &ndash; Early to bed. April 8th Report at Theatre Royal 9 a.m. &ndash; Rehearsal &amp; Preparations for Evening &ndash; Meet new friends.
