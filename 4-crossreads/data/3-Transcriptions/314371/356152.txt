<p>Went to London &amp; Edinburgh, had a good time but was glad to get back.</p>
<p>March 13th<br/>Left Upwey Jnct. for Plymouth &amp; embarked on H.M.S. Dunvegan Castle same day.</p>
<p>March 14th<br/>Pulled out into stream to await completion of convoy.</p>
<p>March 15th<br/>High wind &amp; choppy sea, issued w/- Red X kit.</p>
<p>March 16th<br/>Sailed at 5 p.m.  Large convoy &amp; escort.<br/>Passed Eddystone Lighthouse at 6 p.m., ship rolling a bit.</p>
