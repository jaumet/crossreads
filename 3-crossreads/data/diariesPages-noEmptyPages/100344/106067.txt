Mr Anderson caught train for London. changed at Manchester on G.W.R. booked seats in a Dining Car where we did well getting into town about 10pm. from there went to Surbiton getting there midnight staying at Railway Hotel till morning.
22nd. Monday
Visited the Grandmothers stayed for bkfst going out after, taking photos along the Thames. after lunch went to Bushy Park Hampton Court both very pretty interesting. many snaps of same. evening at "Duneden" music returned to hotel to sleep.
23rd Tuesday
Left Surbiton by train 9am
