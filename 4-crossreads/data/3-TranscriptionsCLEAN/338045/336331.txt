 from Gympie.  Haven't had time to read paper, so don't know the news.  Plenty of mud here - main item.  Bed early. 
 Oct. 18th - Wednesday Reveille as usual at 6 a.m. & parade 6-30 a.m.  Raining hard all night but not so cold.  Sleep well.  Cold day, cloudy, but no rain.  I have charge of a fatigue party during the morning on lines & a picket for lines afternoon.  Most of the A.M.C. at the "Bull Ring".  Three trains pass the camp - the 5th Division Australians - going from Ypres to the Somme.  Scott goes to England.  News from the lines scarce.  Another "push" coming.  A walk to 4th Div. Sgts. Mess & bed 9 p.m.
