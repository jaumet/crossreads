<p>Letter to Mr. Fitzsimons &ndash;<br/>News regarding Paris Leave.</p>
<p>Dec. 13th<br/>Great unrest in Germany &ndash; Read and very much enjoy "Daddy &ndash; Long-Legs" (Jean Webster) Change of programme &ndash;<br/>"The Burglar &amp; the Girl" once again &ndash; Hughie leaves for Valenciennes.</p>
<p>Dec. 14th<br/>Assasination of Czar &amp; Family &ndash;<br/>Attempted suicide</p>
