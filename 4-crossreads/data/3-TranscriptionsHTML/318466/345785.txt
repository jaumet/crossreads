<p>Sunday 21 April 1918<br/>I would like to put on record that the nurses in No. 3 General Hosp. Le Treport <u>are</u> nurses and did their best to make my miserable life happy.</p>
<p>Monday 22 April 1918</p>
<p>Tuesday 23 April 1918<br/>Still being fed &amp; washed by the nurse!!!  Even gassing has its compensations.</p>
<p>Wednesday 24 April 1918<br/>The Doc. tells me he may send me to Blighty.  Cheers!!</p>
<p>Thursday 25 April 1918<br/>Anzac Day.<br/>Still in No. 3 Gen. Hosp. Le Treport</p>
<p>Friday 26 April 1918</p>
<p>Saturday 27 April 1918<br/>Left Le Treport for Boulogne &amp; went over to Dover in Hosp. ship.</p>
