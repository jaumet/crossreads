<p>Tuesday 21 August 1917</p>
<p>Went for a sail to "AGONY" island or those who are sea sick call it. One of the old Forts that were blown up by the bombardment.</p>
<p>I feel fitter but "short a gallop".</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>I only hope you are not worrying about me. Pick up &amp; leave for the Desert in the morning. Not much of interest in Diary to <strike>read out</strike> print them daily, but it is all of interest to you little Mother so I keep it going for your sake.</p>
