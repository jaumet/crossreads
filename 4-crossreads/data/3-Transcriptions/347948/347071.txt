<p>The log clock strikes every &frac14; mile.<br/>Have learnt Morse Code in my spare time.<br/>We have appointed 2 permanent mess orderlies for the trip for 1/- week  They are very enthusiastic M.O's and by the constant use of the holystone &amp; sand (without regard to the wear on the table) they have made our table the cleanest on the ship.<br/>Past St. Helena out of sight.</p>
<p>22nd  nothing to report.</p>
