They have done all that men could do against great odds &ndash; they have proved themselves heroes.  Contrary to my expectations they do not go on furlough but are being moved to another post in the field of operations &ndash; Table Top in left flank.</p>
<p>Russells Top at wh we are now stationed has been the scene of heavy &amp; severe fighting.  From here the 8th made their disastrous sorties.  We have visual evidence of the result of this great charge in the long rows of Turkish &amp; Australian dead that line the trenches not many yds. away.  Leiut. Col  White &amp; Major Redford fell in this memorable fight &amp; <strike>their</strike> the body of the latter has only just been recovered in a state of putrefaction for burial.  There has been no armistice &amp; the bodies of these brave leaders have lain rotting in the sun for days &amp; days</p>
