Old Fritz seems to be in the same fix as we are with his firing line no communication trenches &amp; all mud &amp; water &amp; he has more to put up with for our guns pelt his lines day &amp; night with high explosive &amp; shrapnel while he only drops a few now &amp; again on to ours.</p>
