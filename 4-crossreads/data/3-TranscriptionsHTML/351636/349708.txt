<p>Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers<br/>92B. Pitt St.<br/>13.4.16</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton,<br/>Mrs. Hablethwaite who brings this has got into debt while son in Camp &amp; wants a loan of &pound;20 which she will pay back at 5/- p wk at present may be able to pay back more per wk later on. Can you assist her in this?</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>B. H. Bennie</p>
