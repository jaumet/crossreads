 4.9.16 a cup of tea &amp; bread &amp; butter cost 1/3 &amp; every thing else equally as dear; I was surprised to hear that Les Smithers left for Australia on the Escanius, Col Meredith was in change of the wounded men returning &amp; Nelson is also with them.
