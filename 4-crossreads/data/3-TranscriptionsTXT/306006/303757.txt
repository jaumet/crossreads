 2-3-17 Fri 2nd Had some tests this morning in Semaphore &amp; Morse Flag reading, I topped the class with 100% Semaphore a 99&frac12;% with Morse. Lamp tests held tonight so could not get out &amp; wrote up a few notes instead; Robertson came out today he is down on leave &amp; said Lancy &amp; Small &amp; Kelly are also in Cairo. 
 3-3-17 Sat 3rd Flag wagging before breakfast then rifle examination followed by a Theory test, &amp; then we were finished for the day. Sergt Harris &amp; I did not apply for leave but just walked down to Heliopolis
