<p>11 March 1916</p>
<p>After 9 o'clock parade did fatigue work.  Hauled 3"Ethel' round &amp; picked up rubbish.  Nearly hooked for weekend duties. Sgt. Chard good sort.<br/>Dress for 11.30 parade. Dress inspection at 12 noon. Bob &amp; self go to station together. Mrs. Janie driving. I reach Hewletts for lunch. Ess. sick during week. Get out at Petersham to see dentist fills 4 more teeth. Call at Macs. Down to manly to tea. thence to "Martyrdom of Nurse Cavell" with girls.  Stay the night at Manly. Tony stays in camp sells his weekend for 7/6.</p>
