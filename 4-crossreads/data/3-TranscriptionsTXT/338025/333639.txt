 Tuesday 20th March 1917 Folkestone Arrived Folkestone 7am. Taken to the Victoria Hotel (which is used as Barracks) and given a good breakfast of porridge, ham, bread and tea. Embarked at 8.45am and sailed about 9.50am. Escort one destroyer. Arrived Boulogne about 11.45am. Disembarked about 12.30am and marched to a rest camp outside Boulogne about 1 mile from the city 3pm.
