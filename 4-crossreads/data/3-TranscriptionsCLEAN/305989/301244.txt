  
 Sunday 18 February 1917 Quiet day:  wrote Mum & Jess.  Clarrie got back from School. 
 Monday 19 February 1917 On Engineers fatigue in morning, mounted parade after dinner & 
 Tuesday 20 February 1917 On exercise during day:  A & B Sq.:  played footy:  A won 3-0.  On picquet at night.  Wrote Theo & "Purpley". 
 Wednesday 21 February 1917 Out on exercise in morning on "pumping party", did some washing:  got ready for leave. 
 Thursday 22 February 1917 Left camp at 5.30:  caught the 8.30 from Bir-el-Abd & the 1.10 from Kantarah, got to Cairo 5.3 - went round to Mona Piot's for evening, had a very enjoyable evening:  stayed at Anzac Hostel, got to bed about 1.30. 
 Friday 23 February 1917 Got a few things for the boys in the morning:  then got photo taken at Zola:  took taxi to Mrs. Brand's where had lunch, afternoon tea at Mrs. Walkers:  & dinner with Mrs. Piot & pictures afterwards. 
 Saturday 24 February 1917 Took Sister and Robbo & Jack Long for a spin to the Pyramids in evening & had tea at Anzac:  then to Zola for my proofs & then caught the 6.15 for Kantarah arriving at 10.30 stayed at Empire Club till daylight.  Wrote Mother & Jess.
