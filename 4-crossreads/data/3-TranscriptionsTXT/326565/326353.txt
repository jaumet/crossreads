 Monday 1/5/16 Water fatigue.  General Godley &amp; staff visited here this morning to inspect 59th &amp; 60th Btns. Grenadiers of Infantry Btns spent some time in bomb throwing practice, to the rear of our lines this morning; caused quite a sensation. Officially reported that General Townsend, with 2900 British Troops &amp; 6000 Indians has surrendered after holding out for over four months.  Caused quite a depressed feeling among the boys here  [British surrender at Kut in Iraq]
