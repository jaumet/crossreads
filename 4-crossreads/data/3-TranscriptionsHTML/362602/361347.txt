The parcel Ida Nelson gave me held a dozen packs of cards &amp; I do not know what the boys would have done without them.  Every pack is in use for at least 2 hours every day &amp; then some play at night till lights out at 9.30.  Well I will be able to tell you more tomorrow so will close for the night.</p>
