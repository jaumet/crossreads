The music was lovely so soft sweet I enjoyed this theatre the best of any I have ever seen in my life, after this we had supper by a streak of good luck we got a taxi for they are very scarce at night time so I run her home then back to my lodgings, she was a costly girl to me but the experience was invaluable who ever reads this at home will think I am a funny sort of fellow I suppose I guess they are right, there is a big difference in the Archie of old who in 1914 was almost too shy to kiss his sister, and the same ruffian of today, knocking about the world has worked wonders, whither for the best or not I should not like to say, that will be for other people to judge, but this is certain I look upon life in an entirely different manner to what I used to at one time long long ago.
