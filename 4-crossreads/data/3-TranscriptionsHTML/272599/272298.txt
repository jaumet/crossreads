<p>Jan.  Monday 29 1917</p>
<p>Nothing of moment happened today   We did not get breakfast till about 11 a.m. What's the use in getting up when we can't go outside dugout. A couple of walking cases only for the day.<br/>Letter from Mrs. Wheatley &amp; Cecil   Cold &amp; frosty on our noses</p>
