<p>Thursday 4 November 1915&nbsp;<br />Unable to be up for breakfast. Sent word to Col. Newmarch. Major&nbsp;(Dr.<sup> </sup>) Summons called to <strike>to</strike>&nbsp;see me about 11 am. Wretched in every way. Temp 100 to 101&ordm; during morning&nbsp; Pulse rapid.&nbsp; 4 p.m&nbsp; Still feeling wretched had bath &amp; came down to hospital. Temperature high<s>t</s> and pulse rapid.&nbsp;<br />Had to purchase Swiss Milk and biscuits for myself&nbsp;<br />No one has so far,&nbsp;12 midnight to examine me or take my temperature. Summons looked at me three times put his hand on my wrist &amp; said &quot;You&nbsp;will be all right.&quot;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Friday 5 November 1915&nbsp;<br />7.a.m. A more comfortable night, Some sleep. Wretched all over. Legs week &amp; aching below knee. Pain in head dull heavy. P. 97 to 100. Temp 100 to 101&ordm; to 9 am.<br />Breakfast. Porridge, Swiss Milk, biscuits.<br />Just like Anzac a thirty six hours fierce battle (Inside me between the lucocytes &amp; the organism), &amp; now 48 hours after settling down to a ding dong struggle. Which side will win<br />Two watery evacuations Copious &amp; yellow during night, two small during day, some gas and strain after the second.&nbsp;able to read &amp; write off &amp; on during day.<br />Posted letters home &amp; to M&nbsp;M&nbsp;Knowles.</p>
<p>Saturday 6 November 1915&nbsp;<br />4a.m,. Miserable off &amp; on during night. Tongue &amp; mouth becoming dry, the saliva not secreting. Slight continuous headache. Intermittent abd. pain of a mild character. 8p.m. A fairly comfortable day. slight continuous headache. Three attempts at bowel evacuation.&nbsp;One fair evacuation. Desire for food somewhat increased.&nbsp;<br />Letter from Car, Marion, Beechwood Cottage, Travers &amp; Dr Arthur.</p>
<p>Sunday 7 November 1915&nbsp;<br />Slept well all night. Temperature near normal 8 am. Pulse 85&nbsp;<br />Writing &amp; reading all day.<br />Sister Stone from Zetland hospital (Mrs O&#39;Conner Matron) brought me knitted sox from Joseph. Not at Mass. Read the Epistle &amp; Gospel for 24<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;Sunday a/[Trinity])</p>
