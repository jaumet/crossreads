  
 January, 1917. Saturday 20 - Your name must be registered before any claim can be recognised under the Insurance Coupon. Dowling now on job. Started at 9.30 after putting dubbin on book. Got two trains through and home about 3. Rested in evening. Still cold but invigorating. Rumours to move on 26th to Sausage Gully near Pozieres.
