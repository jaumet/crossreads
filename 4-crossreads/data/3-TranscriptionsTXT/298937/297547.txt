Unless promotion comes here, the job, although very easy &amp; delightful, is not worth keeping on with.  More hardships &amp; more to gain would be better. News arrives tonight of a visit tomorrow from Sir A. Murray the G.I.C. &amp; as H.R.H. the P. of W. is on his staff, he will probably come too.
