Eaucourt L'Abbaye2.2.17Gone is the rich luscious mud which we have grown to know but not to love &amp; in its place or rather over it has come a good frost making a hard but slippery footing.  For miles around snow frozen hard meets the eye broken here and there by the advent of a large shell which has torn up great masses of ice and laid bare Mother Earth again.
