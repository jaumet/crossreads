<p>12th. Have been doing a bit of printing today &amp; have got some very good results.<br/>There was a great stink kicked up in the Camp last night about 11 oclock, it appears that some women got in somehow, &amp; took up their quarters for the night in <strike>at</strike> a big shed at the back of the Guard room, it wasn't long before a crowd collected &amp; the Sergt of the Guard had to go round &amp; bunt the whole crowd, comes to something when they make a brothel of the Camp.<br/>Am Orderly Sergt. for this coming week started today.<br/>13th.
