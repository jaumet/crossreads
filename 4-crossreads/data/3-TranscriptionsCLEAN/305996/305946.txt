Sunday 2nd May 1915.During the last week events of world-wide importance have taken place. The Turk has been invaded in a manner that he would have thought would ever impossible. The Gallipoli Penin. has been the scene of terrific combats and one of the most difficult problems of modern warfare. viz the landing of troops on a fortified & well manned coast has been accomplished. Referring in particular to that portion of the work allotted to the Aus, Div. there can be no doubt that they achieved a wonderful success.
