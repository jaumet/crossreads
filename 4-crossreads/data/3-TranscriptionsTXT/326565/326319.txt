 Friday 10/3/16 Acting Piquet Corporal for the day.  Mail arrived after a fortnight's interval.  Capt North endeavouring to be transferred back to the 8th F. Amb.  Spent a few hours here to-day; already transferred from 7th to 13th  Fd.
