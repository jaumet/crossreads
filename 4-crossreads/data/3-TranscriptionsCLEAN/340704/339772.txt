
May 1917
Saturday 5Left Regt near Gamli for bomb school Biltoun. 5 hours ride before reaching train Khan ZunusTravelling all night in the train
Sunday 6Arrived Kantara 5.30 am. Had breakfast saw Olsson. Arrived Zeitoun 4 pm.
Mrs Chisholm and Miss Mc Phillamy are doing great work for the boys at Kantara in their refreshment room
