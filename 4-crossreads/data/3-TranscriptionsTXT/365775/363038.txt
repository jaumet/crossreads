 "Vaugres" and "Vienine Chasse", two of the places built along the banks of the river;  just before reaching the latter place, we saw a party fishing and what struck me as quaint was that they were all standing in the water up to their waist, of course it was no ways cold but still there was a keen wind blowing. one of the chaps said He can have that game on his own, for my part, I was of the same opinion myself.
