<p>12/12/17<br/>Raining again all day. no news</p>
<p>13/12/17<br/>The Boss gave us a nice speech through the interpreter this morning.  in future the man responsible for his bed (so that will be better). he will be straffed if <strike>this</strike> his bed is not up to the mark.</p>
<p>14/12/17<br/>News of Russia is still conflicting.  Jerusalem has fallen &amp; German east Africa is about mopped up.  Great talk about exchange again three boat loads of German exchange pris to leave [indecipherable] on 27/12/17  our boys are wondering when they will go to Holland</p>
<p>15/12/17<br/>News of Buchanans speech to Russian news reporters splendid</p>
<p>16/12/17<br/>another blanket parade today not long.  German Chief of Police shot from behind the Germans</p>
