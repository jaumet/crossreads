<p>Sun. 10th Sept.<br/>Still up to our necks in mud though we have been fortunate enough for the rain to cease.<br/>Collins was sniped &amp; killed today whilst on a message from Hqrs to 1st Bn.<br/>We have been out in No Man's Land each night on "Listening Posts" while fatigue parties are digging a new fire-trench more advanced .</p>
<p>Mon. Sept. 11th.<br/>A trench-mortar of ours behind 2nd Div (on left) put a few bombs over on "Fritz today in order to get their range &amp; that started him off.<br/>"Minnies" "Rum-jars" &amp; "pill boxes" came through the air from all directions &ndash; That's' the worst of being on a salient, they can get you from all sides.<br/>We had plenty of dodging around the buttresses to do as we did our best to judge the flight of the bombs &amp; we got off light with 2 killed &amp; 2 wounded.<br/>Corporal Cliff &amp; Corporal Flanagan being the unlucky two.</p>
