<p>[Colours of the 18th Battalion in the top left corner of the letter.]<br/>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>4.3.18.19</p>
<p>Dear hansom.</p>
<p>We are doeing some beads I have made two necklaces one wont fit mum only I think it will fit Ruth.  I have got the tooth ach.  Mum is <strike>makeing</strike> making a dress for Ruth.  Bing and Blucher are loose.  I expect you are out now it is a corter past 3.</p>
<p>Mum is going to write to you and send it by Mr Micklocklen like Mum did last time.  gust now Bing had a calfs leg and he left it out on the vrandah and <strike>whent</strike> went in side <strike>for</strike> to the basket and got a potato and took it out and put It down beside the leg</p>
<p>much love<br/>from Frank<br/>xoxo etc.</p>
