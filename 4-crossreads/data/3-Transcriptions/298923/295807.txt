<p>21 May 1918 Tuesday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Raided Beit Fejjar, but of course got nothing-   Women gave me a bad headache with their yells &amp; clamour-   Bought 8 eggs 4 Fowls, 2 Bundles onions some WOOD for 15/-<br/>This village is in Arub Wadi where Pontius Pilate, made an aqueduct from the spring to Jerusalem, &amp; got sent back to Rome for spending so much money about 18 centuries ago-  His water channel is now being unearthed &amp; <u>used</u> by British for a new supply to Jerusalem.<br/>X Inoculated again for Cholera tonight.X</p>
