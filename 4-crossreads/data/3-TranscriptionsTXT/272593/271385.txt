 This morning the 1st 3rd &amp; 4th Battn's were on parade together  General Birdwood was having a look at us &amp; was going to present the medals to all those who had won them in the Div, the Brigadiers of the 3 Bde's were present including our old Colonel Hean  the man who held Pozeries for the first 2 day's, he looked well.  After the Service General Birdwood gave us a short address &amp; told us about how our 5 th &amp; 2 nd Div took the great one time fortress of Baupaume, he said all the Australian Divs who have been on the Somme can write Baupaume on their colors  &amp; he said he guessed there
