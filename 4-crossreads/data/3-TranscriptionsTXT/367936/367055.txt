
Sunday 15 October 1916Swim in morning.Went into Alexandria in afternoon;  took tram down to Ras-el-tin (School now) then walked back through native quarter.Went to the Cursol [also spelt Kursaal], home about 12-30.
Monday 16 October 1916Wrote letters in morning.  Dick, Hunt, Hargrave and I went into Alex, saw Pompeys Pillar.Left shell bands at tin smith to be made into rings.Had dinner at Stella d'Italy.  Went to Cursol, also drove round Anfouchy in Garry.
