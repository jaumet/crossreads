
Sunday 13Move to England. Via Calais, Dover to Charing Cross. The railway run in France was beautiful, all the trees in blossom & leaf. Quiet crossing in H.S. Cambria. The strand had not a soul in it at 2.30 am this morning.
Monday 14At No. 3 London General Hospital Wandsworthy. Still in bed but to get up shortly.
