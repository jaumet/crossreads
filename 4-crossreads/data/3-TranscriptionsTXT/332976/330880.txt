 8th, 14th &amp; 15th arrived in the town to-day &amp; we are the first Australian troope ever billited in Corbie.   It is a large &amp; thickly populated place, &amp; from all appearances promices to afford us a very good time. 21st   This morning we paraded in "drill order" &amp; marched down the river (Somme river) to a spot about 3 Km. from our billets where we are to go through a course of Pontoon Bridging.
