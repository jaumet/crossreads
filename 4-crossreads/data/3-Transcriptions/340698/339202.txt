<p>6/7/17<br/>Marched from Mailley to Lavieville via Senlis and Henencourt. We expect to remain here about 4 days.</p>
<p>8/7/17<br/>Marched from Lavieville to Pozieres today for the purpose of attending the unveiling of a monument in memory of the chaps who fell in that stunt last July. It was rather a heavy march</p>
