 9-6-16 Fri 9th Received orders today to be ready to leave at 4 oclock tomorrow morning to raid another Turkish camp;  Were issued with iron rations this evening &amp; I went to the Y.M.C.A a couple of times to see if any eatables had arrived but there was nothing but smokes &amp; we were busy this evening getting things ready to move 
 10-6-16 Sat 10th We were called at 2 oclock this morning &amp; fed the horses. had breakfast &amp; had to bustle to get on parade;  The 1st Bde of L.H. &amp; a  Sqd  Regt of New Zealanders left Romani at 3.30 A.M. &amp; watered our horses at Qatia about 6.30, &amp; soon after leaving Qatia our plane flew over us &amp; dropped a message, &amp; the went on scouting;  We arrived at Hod.
