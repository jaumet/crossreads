  
 Thursday 1 June 1916 Very cold nights but terribly hot day.  Revallie goes at 5 a.m. do a little drill and have rest of day off. No leave allowed but was able to pinch out in evening, and get down to shops.  There were over 100 thousand men here but most of them have gone on to England now,. 
 Friday 2 June 1916 Went down to camp with Gant to draw stores. Bought three films and left two to be developed. Had fountain pen pinched. Wrote to Mother (1).  Sent cable.
