<p>[Rough translation &ndash; One distinguishes the Australian and New Zealand soldiers by their large hats &ndash; same as Buffalo Bill. The salute they do with the hand never touching the hat. The military salute of theirs is unknown. They allege their officers are similar to factory bosses. We are governed by the boss in the factories, and by officers in service. Out of service they are nothing more. This original idea is a first.]<br/>Leave to Orange (&amp; Paris and Wimereux)<br/>Receiving an invitation from a French family living in Orange in the Department of Vaucluse, France, to stay with them for a few days, I applied for six days leave &amp; was considerably astonished when I was granted 8 days commencing Monday, 13th Dec.</p>
