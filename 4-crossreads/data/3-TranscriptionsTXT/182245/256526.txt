 JANUARY 1917 
 11 THURSDAY 
 Cold &amp; dull with light snow falling 
 all day. Grooming all our new 
 grey horses all the morning 
 and we went to a gas 
 lecture in the afternoon, &amp; cleaning 
 harness when we came back. 
 Got a lot of parcels ect in 
 the evening, but I got none 
 except a letter from mother
