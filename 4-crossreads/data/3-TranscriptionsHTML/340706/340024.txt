<p>70<br/>was 10 am an hour ago as the daylight saving scheme is always in force here &amp; all clocks were put forward one hour last night. I then hurry out find Melhuish &amp; away we start for Dev. Wing which after walking every inch of the way we reach just after noon.</p>
<p>I dine here get papers but owing to delays cannot get away in time to catch 2 pm train so must rest to night in Abbeville though there is nothing more to see &amp; I have no friends. However I chum up with some English chap &amp; after tea go for a walk. He leaves me early to visit two friends Leoni et Marie 8 &amp; I return to sleep. To morrow I must rise at 5 as train leaves at 6.7 am.</p>
<p>3-3-19<br/>Roused myself at 5 am &ndash; dressed &amp; shaved &amp; as I was setting off found that I might have had breakfast had I desired &amp; also that train left 6.7 am &amp; not 6.20 as RTO had endorsed my movement order. However I had made my dispositions well ahead of time &amp; caught train together with orderly.</p>
