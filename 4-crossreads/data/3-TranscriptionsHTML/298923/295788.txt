<p>2 May 1918 Thursday<br/>Freezing at dawn but now at time of writing roasting hot as we came off the highlands back towards J. Valley-  have not had my boots off or a wash, much less a shave since stunt commenced-  <strike>Our H.D.C. had a bad time-</strike><br/>Germans Austrians among Turks taken prisoner.<br/>Sleep now if possible-<br/>Marched again all night had to carry <strike>a</strike> wounded men on foot 7 miles over mountains, as no wheels can come near us, &amp; we have no Camel Cacholets.</p>
