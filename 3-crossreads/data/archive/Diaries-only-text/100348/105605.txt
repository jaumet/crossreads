17/8/16.
It continues to be fairly quiet Fritz pays more attention to the communications in the rear, of the part we occupy. The trenches are of a rough and ready character here, they have been knocked about by the artillery of both sides; still they serve; although obliterated in places. We get much more duty now being so much under strength.
18/8/16.
Had rather a trying time last night. Was lying out on the top of the parapet observing, under heavy fire for four hours; it gave me the "Willies" properly.
