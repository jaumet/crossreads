S. S. "Rio Pardo"
May 23rd
Final preparations - at 3.30 p.m. the A.M.C. quotas for the "Bremen" & "Rio Pardo" march out of Fovant Camp.  Train to Salisbury then to Waterloo. 
Night at War Chest Club.
May 24th  (Empire Day)
Our quota for "Rio Pardo" leave town for the Surrey Docks.
