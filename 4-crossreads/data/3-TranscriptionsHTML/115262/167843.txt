<p>Saturday 17 March 1917.</p>
<p>In camp till 11.45.</p>
<p>2 carsloads of C.B.F.A. went for a day to Negambie [Nagambie].</p>
<p>Left camp at 11.45 a.m. Sat morng.&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">First &quot;blow-out&quot; at 12.45 p.m. at Mangalore. Photo taken.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Second blow out at Tahbilk 1.45 pm.</span></p>
<p>Arrived Negambie 2.5 pm.&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Had dinner at &quot;Valley Hotel&quot;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Left Negambie 3.10 p. for Weir.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Reached Lake Negambie 3.30 p.m.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Arrived Goulburn Weir 3.40 pm W.H 65&#39; 9 </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Took several snaps with V.P.R Auto. C.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Caretaker regulated flood gates to show us. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Channel runs&nbsp;75 miles further from West Channel.</span></p>
<p>Left weir at 4.30 pm arrived Negambie at 4.50 pm. Went rowing on lake&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Had tea 6.45 p.m at &quot;Valley Hotel&quot;. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Left Negambie 7.35 p.m. Puncture 8-10 </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Blow-out 8.40. Arrived 9.40 at camp.</span></p>
<p>Everybody pleased with the outing</p>
