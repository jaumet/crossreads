<p>Sunday 8 April 1917</p>
<p>Fixing up camp in morning quiet &amp; hot.  Two Taubes over but did not drop any bombs.</p>
<p>Monday 9 April 1917<br/>Quiet day, general routine.</p>
<p>Tuesday 10 April 1917<br/>On Q.M. fatigue, took a photo of old castle in the town.</p>
<p>Wednesday 11 April 1917<br/>Changed camp from first pozzy, took pack horses to Y.M.C.A. for stuff.  On picquet at night.</p>
<p>Thursday 12 April 1917<br/>Rifle Inspection in morning, usual routine.  Taube bombed Rafa &amp; hit canteen.</p>
<p>Friday 13 April 1917<br/>Brigade out on outpost left camp about 12 &amp; took over at 2:  very interesting country out ahead:  taubes in galore but no bombs.</p>
<p>Saturday 14 April 1917<br/>Stayed out on outpost till 2.30 in the afternoon &amp; then came back to Khan Yunus.  Big mail in, got several letters, parcels, including some feathers.</p>
