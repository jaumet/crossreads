
July 1918
July 1stPack up costumes, properties etc. & leave for Bertangles - We have had a very decent week at Vignacourt - Back to the bell tents in the woods - Bed made under the trees -July 2ndRehearsal & re-opening show in the "hangar" of 48th Squadron R.F.C. - At present very busy on long, new sketch - Heavy bombardments along the Front.
