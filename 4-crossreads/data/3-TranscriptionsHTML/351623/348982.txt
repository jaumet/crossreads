<p>11 May 16<br/>Arrived at Kingston Jamaica in morning watch &amp; tied up alongside the pier. Coaled off Collier Loriga, natives coaling in baskets. Ceased coaling at 1 pm on account of rain. Port watch ashore.</p>
<p>12 May 16<br/>Finished coaling ship at 1.30 pm went ashore at 5 pm for 36 hours leave.</p>
<p>13 May 16<br/>Ashore in Kingston Jamaica. Spent a pleasant day.</p>
<p>14 May 16<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;<u>Sunday</u> Came aboard at 7 am. Port watch went ashore at 1.30 pm for 36 hours leave</p>
<p>15 May 16<br/>Lying alongside pier at Kingston. Port watch ashore all day.</p>
<p>16 May 16 )<br/>17 May 16 )  Lying alongside at Kingston</p>
