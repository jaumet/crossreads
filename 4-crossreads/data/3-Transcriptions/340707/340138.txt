<p>6-5-19<br/>I rise at 5 am but do not start for 6.45 am which naturally I miss. Arriving in time for 8.30 am I am informed that I cannot travel by it so wait till 10.30 which does not go as far as wharf &amp; costs me the expense of transportation to wharf though assured by officialdom that I need not worry.</p>
<p>At Folkestone I met 2 Yankee medical officers with whom I dine &amp; visit the township of Folkestone.</p>
<p>Embark on Princess Victoria at 2.45 &amp; a sailor picks up a chair from a heap &amp; puts it down 2 ft away &amp; expects a tip for this &ndash; usually 6d or 1/-. This is the way you are robbed in England by English people One can forgive the continental people to some extent because we are foreigners &amp; so liable to be exploited</p>
<p>Arrive at Boulogne &amp; boat enters from sea stern first as there is no room to turn in harbour without inconveniencing the traffic &amp; perhaps the knife edged design is difficult to handle if she has not much way.</p>
