
[10 Sun]Granton
[11 Mon]Left Edinburgh (Wav.) 1-45pm & arrived London 11-30pm. Taken from Victoria by Y.M.C.A. taxi to rest hut.
London
[12 Tues]Left London 8am. Arr Dover 10am. taken to military barracks where we had dinner at 12-30pm. Embarked 2-30pm. Left Dover 4 pm  Arr. Calais 6 pm. & marched to rest camp
[13 Wed]Calais
Rev 6am  Left rest camp about 7-30am. & marched to station & issued with rations. Left Calais about 10-30am  Arr. Steenwerck 1-30 pm. Arr. Unit (Pont d' Archelles) about 2-30pm
