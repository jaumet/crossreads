 New Zealand mounted men holding portions of it, &amp; men are being raked up from practically everywhere, they had a lot of gas this time in the line &amp; nearly all the lads are talking a little hoarse &amp; husky &amp; quite a number have been gassed &amp; are now safely in Blighty On arriving back this afternoon I found quite a pile of letters waiting for me McManus went to Australia today how lucky is he, It has been very cold all day tonight light rain has set in  Major Jacobs came back from England with our draft today he has been away a long time now but he is a good fellow for all that &amp; very popular with the boys &amp; all the Battalion
