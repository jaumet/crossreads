<p>My feelings when he pulled out a 3 ft wooden drum of thick cable stuff, were indescribable. However it had to be done so I determined to do my best, and that must have been poor enough. I had not thought of the other chap's opinion &amp; was certainly past caring. We got there anyhow and I rejoined my section in their possie.</p>
