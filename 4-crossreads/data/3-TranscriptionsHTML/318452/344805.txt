The trees were white as if it had snowed heavily, even the mules with long coats were covered in frost I have never seen anything like it.<br/>On arrival at Poperhinge (Belgium) I found there was no train going till next morning, &amp; put up at the officers club for the night.</p>
<p>Friday<br/>Left Poperhinge by the leave train at 10.30, &amp; did not arrive at Boulogne till 7 oclock (about 4 hrs late on a usually slow train) I found the hotels nearly all full but managed to get a bed at the Paris hotel</p>
