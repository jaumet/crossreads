  
 October 1915 Wednesday 27 Windy Reveille at 5 AM, breakfast at 6 AM, and moved off at 6.30 AM. We marched to the  RE PIER  waited about two hours embarked on a large steamer and then transferred to the Osmanich. a fine passenger ship now used for transporting stores, troops and wounded to their destination. 
 Thursday 28 Dull. Still lying in harbour. Our Batt is situated in the 1st class dining saloon on the ship. The boys amuse themselves by playing card games such as; Nap banker, five hundred, euchure and whist.
