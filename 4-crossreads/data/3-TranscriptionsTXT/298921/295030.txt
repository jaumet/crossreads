February, 1915Friday 26Australian Papers mention I believe letter from Lieut Weir [indecipherable] is a military crime to publish war news, even if the Censor is dodged, so better not allow any names dates or places to appear in print from me. Had a Divisional Field day today about 20000 troops manouvering &amp; sham fighting in the Desert.I do not expect to be here very much longer in Egypt but where we go is a secret &amp; will till we land again somewhere!![indecipherable] Austin the Nuggety Cot turning out a tip top horse.
