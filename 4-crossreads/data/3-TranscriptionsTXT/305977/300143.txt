 Saturday 12th [Dec] Very hot &amp; dusty day with strong wind blowing.  Troop cleaned up gear in morning.  I was put on the making up of the nominal rolls.  Finished in the afternoon at 3 oclock.  Went to Burwood by the 5.30 train &amp; had tea with Mr &amp; Mrs Beaumont.  Saw Myer &amp; May &amp; Annie Cochrane.  Went on to the Fergusons.  Met young Edmundson of the 7th Regt there.  Seems to be a bit keen about Eva.  Caught the 11.33 train back to camp &amp; got in about 1.30.  Long went on leave.
