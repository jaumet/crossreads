He had it under observation and that meant when he saw a fatigue party going up for rations, they could expect a few of his lighter shells to hurry them up. Sometimes he was kinder and would let them pass without remark.
I was walking along one night and a shell flew by my throat and fell in the roadway. It did not explode or else I would probably not be writing now.
