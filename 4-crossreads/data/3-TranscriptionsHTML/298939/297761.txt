<p>26th April Thursday<br/>To day I have been exerting myself in the role of cart horse, on blanket fatigue with the Q.M.Sgt.<br/>Yesterday was Anzac Day, but being our duty week we worked (A Section), &amp; were promised a holiday next week.<br/>As a matter of fact I managed by luck to get a couple of hours of after dinner and in the evening I went to a show at the Empire with Alec Nunan, whom I have dug</p>
