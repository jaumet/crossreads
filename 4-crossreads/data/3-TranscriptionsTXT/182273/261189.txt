1918   December
12 THUR  Put in applications for leave.
Raining on & off all
day. Half passes finished by 8 PM
Rec. mine 10.15 caught
10.32 PM from St Budeaux & the 12.5
from Plymouth to London. went
with J.W.C. & H Cunningham
13 FRI  Arr Paddington 6.45. Left Euston
9.10 am home 3. PM. Had walk
round at night. Went to
Waterloo.
14 SAT  Fine day. Out with the
boys  Went to 'Metropole'
at night to see 'Tainted Goods'
very good show.
