There is a very fine fountain at the entrance surrounded by wonderfully green lawns. We then drove back to Alex. to Groppi's Cafe for tea  &  cakes. 6. p.m. Left there about 7. p.m. walked to Savoy had a wash &  tidy up then to dinner at 8. p.m. We then walked to tram for [Girginia?] and reached the Home at 10.15. p.m. after spending a most enjoyable day. Mr. C. is staying at the Savoy.,  &  he got back alright. Had not long to wait for tram.
