<p>May 9th<br/>Leave Bournemouth most reluctantly for Portsmouth &ndash; Give a matinee at Theatre Royal &ndash; Large audience, much appreciation &ndash; Manager, Mr. P. Davey &ndash; This Theatre (one of the most important outside of London contains a great deal of fine new scenery all of which is being prepared for Drury Lane etc)  The "Green Room" contains a fine large portrayal of late Sir Henry Irving.<br/>Leave for London.<br/>Back Home</p>
