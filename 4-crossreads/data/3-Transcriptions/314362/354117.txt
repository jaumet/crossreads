<p>16 Sunday  [Dec]<br/> Very Cold church parade Trist fall of snow (heavy) Parcels from Rup chockes</p>
<p>17 Monday<br/>Jolly cold route march  &amp; the roads were thick with snow. Feeling fed up.</p>
<p>18 Tuesday<br/>On Court of Inquiry over missing men all morng.  Went to Desvres in aftn &amp; dined with Browne &amp; Graham. Saw Col. [indecipherable]<br/>19 Wednesday<br/>Fixed up the line to the Adjt. C.O. Bre &amp; myself.  damned cold.</p>
<p>20 Thursday<br/>Got a brazier in my room at last !!!  Jolly warm too !  shall never go out now !!!</p>
<p>21 Friday<br/>Route march in morning.  Finished reading "Bindle" &ndash; topping book. must send it to Rup.</p>
<p>22 Saturday<br/>lres from Addie Warly &amp; Evans &amp; Rup.  answered them all.  Heath &amp; Sprattt joined up yesterday</p>
<p>23 Sunday<br/>Had a good sleep in as there was no church parade !!!</p>
<p>24 Monday<br/>Into Boulogne with Thompson &amp; saw the Smythes  Home in Davies car by nine o'clock</p>
<p>25 Tuesday<br/>Lunched &amp; dined at B &amp; G [?] mess.  Quite a good day considering.  Hear that Rup has had [indecipherable]<br/>26 Wednesday<br/>No parades thank goodness !!  Snow fell again.  Sent books to Rup.</p>
<p>27  Thursday<br/>Went over the Bde line with linesman.  Came back with Woolrych.  won another 8 frs at Bridge.</p>
<p>28 Friday<br/>Specialist parade in morng.  Went to B. &amp; bought new tunic &amp; was wanted while away ! just my luck</p>
<p>29 Saturday<br/>Read &amp; wrote a good deal.  Stunt in morng which was damned cold !!  No mail for day</p>
