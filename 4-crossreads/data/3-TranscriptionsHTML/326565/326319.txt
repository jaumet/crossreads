<p>Friday 10/3/16<br/>Acting Piquet Corporal for the day.  Mail arrived after a fortnight's interval.  Capt North endeavouring to be transferred back to the 8th F. Amb.  Spent a few hours here to-day; already transferred from 7th to 13th  Fd.</p>
<p>Saturday 11/3/16<br/>Field work with wagons during morning.  Afternoon half holiday; Selected to play cricket with first eleven against team of Field Engineers.<br/>Engineers won toss &amp; batted first, scoring 35.
