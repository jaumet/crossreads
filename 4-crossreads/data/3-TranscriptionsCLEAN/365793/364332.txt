  
 Aug 20 longingly at us. Record for last 24 hrs is only 254 miles. Usual Church Service at 10.30 & sing song at night. 
 21 Hunted in by a sharp shower at 7.15, but no damage done, altho' I only  just  got down in time for brek. Scran keeping good, but the head winds & heavy seas are bad for progress. Read a couple of good books, looking for another now (unearthed "Shirley") "Tableau' Starbrd Saloon deck - aft. Abramovitch & Cohenstein are on bad terms.
