<p>24.2.15</p>
<p>Wed 24th<br/>Was with C Sq to-day, we were out with the troops &amp; they were trench digging, while we laid down in the shade of our horses &amp; watched them.  it was very warm during the middle of the day &amp; it was rather late when we arrived back in camp in fact it was too late to get paid,  wrote home after tea &amp; told plenty of lies amongst ourselves</p>
<p>Thur 25th<br/>Had a very easy day, went out to the pumping station practising signalling with the HQ signallers &amp; returned to camp as soon as we</p>
