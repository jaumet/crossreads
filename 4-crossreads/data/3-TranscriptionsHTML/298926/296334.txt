<p>I would have been the Souvenir.</p>
<p>22nd Jan  Was taken by train to Boulogne on the French coast and by Motor Amb. to <br/>Wimeraux 2&frac12; miles out of Boulogne to <u>Wimeraux No 8 Hosp</u> the nurses are <br/>very good to the patients especially the Australian Nurses.  Two days after I learnt I was <br/>for blighty so at 8 o'clock in the morning I was put on board the Hospital Ship "Brighton" <br/>which sailed at 10.30 and at 1 o'clock</p>
