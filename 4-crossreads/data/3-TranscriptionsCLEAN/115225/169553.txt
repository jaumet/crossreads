 Apl 15  th    We left park at 7.30 A.M. & loaded ammo at Poulainville & took it to corps reserve dump on the  allon  Amiens Contay road  near S t  Gratien . got back to park at 4.30 P.M. & received some letters from home & a parcel. Cold day but no rain - 
   
 Apl 16  th    We went to Flesselles amunition dump at 8.15 A.M. & hauled to Corps reserve dump. heavy mist & cold wind all day. We heard that the Germans have captured Bailleul, & Meteren is  the front  no mans land 
   
 Apl 17  th    We stayed in to-day to work on the lorry - it was a dull rainy day - I lost 15 francs at a two up school in the evening there was a very big school & up for hundreds of francs. 
   
 Apl 18  th    Five of the lorries are out on supplies every day for the Brigade & when they have finished they return to Amiens & load up with furniture etc & haul for the civies - We got ready for the road. Cold bleak day & some rain.
