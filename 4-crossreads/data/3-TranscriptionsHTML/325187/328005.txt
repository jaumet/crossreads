<p>[Tuesday 19 September 1916]</p>
<p><u>St Annes</u> We have Hot OXO on the Pier every morning crowds of people there &amp; a <u>WOMAN</u> BAND.<br/>Dolly is a dainty little girl like Mother.<br/>Going to see old Aunt Anna &amp; Mr Ferrar at Ireland at the end of the week &amp; then coming back here &amp; on to London to an  Hotel next to where Laura lives, I don't think Bruce wanted me at Portman Square somehow, so Laura took a room at Somerset Hotel.</p>
