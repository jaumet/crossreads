<p>Over here, the war goes on just the same.  There is a general feeling of optimism.  Of course the Italian reverse is rather serious, and postpones the final issue for a bit but otherwise things seem to be going well.</p>
<p>We have been right in the thick of the show up here and have managed to keep the flag flying.  It has been very interesting.</p>
