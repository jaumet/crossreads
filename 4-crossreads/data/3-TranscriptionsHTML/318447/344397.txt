<p>I believe 1200 feet below sea level</p>
<p>From where we are camped, if one climbs the surrounding hills, <strike>one</strike> can be seen glimpses of the Dead Sea to the South East &amp; &amp; valley of the Jordan lying far below.</p>
<p>The mounted troops have done  nothing yet as far as I know. I believe that they are waiting for the infantry to take a place from which they can jump off to blow up the Hedjaz railway which is I am told, the main idea of the stunt. Shortly after leaving</p>
