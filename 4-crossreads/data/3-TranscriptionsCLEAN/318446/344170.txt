I have just dined inadequately & without enthusiasm on dry bread with a modicum of sand for spicing & a cup of alleged tea, which, were it not for the irrefutable evidence of tea leaves 'Thick as autumnal leaves that strew the Brooks in Vallombrosa' [Paradise Lost] in the bottom of the cup, I should be inclined to say was concocted by boiling down greasy tins & adding a generous handful of chloride of Lime.
