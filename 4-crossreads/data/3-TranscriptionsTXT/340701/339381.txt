16th  Tuesday 16We had a hot time today removing into new quarters thermometer about 118 red hot run off our legs.  Have just been notified that I must attend the Bombing School at 5.0 AM tomorrow.
Wednesday 17th -5-16Was advised that I had been accepted for the pioneers & to present myself at their orderly room.  I did so but not liking the look of things cleared out before I was interviewed.  I had to miss a lecture to do so   I seen Bernie this morning he has a parcel for me a small book I believe.
