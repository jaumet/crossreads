<p>12-3-17<br/>Mon 12th<br/>Usual routine of work today Harris left here at 4.30 &amp; took Hills in to Cairo. I went to the Hospital &amp; met Sergt Hayes &amp; Young there, Hayes should not be out yet, but got away to take some of the nurses for a spin; Young is the biggest rotter &amp; most unpopular man in Egypt &amp; has a bad attack of cold feet, he has been in Details for over 12 months now &amp; has great objections to rejoining the Regt. An Aust. Mail came out to the Hospital this evening &amp; Jessie &amp; I put in till 10 oclock reading her letters from home &amp; yarning. Hills &amp; Vern. Arrived back at 10 oclock &amp; Vern &amp; I came back to camp together. Wrote to Meredith today; Dev High went back to Kantara this morning</p>
