<p>10.  Books in to the Night Matron wardmaster.<br/>I have nothing more to write, Dad, Mum &amp; George.  I have a letter here from Cyril Whitehead who is Southall Hospital near London.  He must be alright again.<br/>I must go out for a run now.  It is only 7.30 pm but it is quite light till 9.30 pm.<br/>Give my Regards to all the Neighbours to Otto &amp; the Club Boys   We are doing well &amp; having</p>
