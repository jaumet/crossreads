<p>It is such a change to live in quietness, could not imagine such a war was going one, in the same country. The old Hun, did not get very near here, so no ruins -   &amp;  the crops are fine.</p>
<p>Shall try and get my Xmas Cards off this week, so hope I won't forget any of them.</p>
<p>Dorothy was well when she last wrote.</p>
<p>I should love to get home for Xmas or New Year to see you all.</p>
<p>Fondest love   &amp;  kisses to you all   &amp;  hope you are well.</p>
<p>Give my love to my friends.</p>
<p>From<br/>Yours lovingly<br/>Edith</p>
