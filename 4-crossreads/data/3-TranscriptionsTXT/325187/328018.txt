
[Monday 2 October 1916]
THE DAYWell at 10.45 AM I was married at St Thomas Church by The Rev Mr Ellison, to a dear little girl Dorothy Marguerite Isherwood - Laura travelled to London with us & we are now at Berners Hotel clearing out from Somerset after dinner as the little wife did not like it & our room had not been reserved."Jones" does not seem to understand the word STUNT & prints all sorts of variations-It is an Americanism used a great deal these days in speaking of military work.
