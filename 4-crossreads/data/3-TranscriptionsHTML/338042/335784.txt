<p>Jan.3rd&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; (14)<br/>News of Herb Marl Hammy &amp; Fred being in England &ndash; Engine again fails during the Panto. &ndash; Letter No.14 written &ndash; "A pin that was missing during a juggling act."</p>
<p>Jan 4th<br/>Letters to Home &amp; Scotland.</p>
<p>Jan.5th<br/>Last show in Sheenwerck -. Most successful night of Panto &ndash; English troops now in Sheenwerck &ndash;Anzacs depart.  Again meet Mr. Hewett.</p>
