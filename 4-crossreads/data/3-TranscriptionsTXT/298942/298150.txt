  Thursday  
 Saturday 18 May 
 The principal events of the past three days have been in the shape of extra work &amp; increased heat, &amp; these are bound up in each other. 
 The weather has suddenly become warmer then hot.  One wakes up after a heavy dead  sleep, (which the northern warmth always brings &amp; not the Australian heat) to promise of a hot day in the warm haze.
