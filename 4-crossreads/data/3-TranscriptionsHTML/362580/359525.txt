<p>The drive to the Canal is along the Fresh-Water Lake &amp; view over the water with the sun setting behind the hills was a grand sight.  I do wish I could paint but one could never get the colours.</p>
<p>Ismailia is a pretty spot &amp; every day I just think how lucky I am to have had this opportunity of coming here.</p>
<p>I wonder if any of my pals will be in the next batch, Sister Murray or Dorothy?  It would be lovely &ndash; but we are all very happy.  Miss McNulty is here but I do not see much of her, she is on &amp; off at different times to me &amp; works in a different part of the hospital.  Am nearly asleep &ndash; will add a few more lines later on.</p>
