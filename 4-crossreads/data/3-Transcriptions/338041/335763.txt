<p>Dec. 13th.<br/>Camp &ndash; guard duties. &ndash; Gas drill &ndash; A visit from General Legge.</p>
<p>Dec. 14th.<br/>Third trip to Amiens &ndash; pleasant day - weather cold but invigorating &ndash; a wait of 5 dreary hours for our train back to camp.</p>
<p>Dec. 15th.<br/>Gas drill &ndash; Snow fall &ndash; Mail from Home &ndash; camp fatigue duties &ndash; pick &amp; shovel work. "Germany tries hard for piece &ndash; Noteworthy change in British Cabinet &ndash; Lloyd George as Prime Minister.</p>
<p>Dec. 16th.<br/>Surprise victory of French at Verdun &ndash; physical exercises &amp; march &ndash; Mail to Home &ndash; (Cable)</p>
