
<p>May &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>5 SUN &nbsp;Dull day. thunderstorm&nbsp;</p>
<p>in aft. Lively</p>
<p>firing. Amm: going up. Rec:</p>
<p>letters from Celia &amp; photo &amp;</p>
<p>letter May Webb. Writing</p>
<p>at night.</p>
<p>6 MON &nbsp;Fine day. Advanced</p>
<p>position shelled out.</p>
<p>Went into Hondeghem in aft</p>
<p>Posted letter to Julie &amp; two photos.</p>
<p>Rec. letter from L. Berry &amp; Else P.</p>
<p>7 TUES &nbsp;Raining all morning.</p>
<p>Amm. going up. Quiet.</p>
<p>Rec. letters from Celie &amp; Tense.</p>
<p>Heavy firing evening&nbsp;</p>
<p>Billet shelled during night.</p>
<p>10 Horses Kld &amp; 9 Wdd.</p>
<p>8 WED &nbsp;Fine day. Posted letter &amp;</p>
<p>photo to M<sup><u>rs</u></sup> Mooney. Rec ... -</p>
<p>from Tense. Heavy firing all</p>
<p>day. Major Sexton took charge</p>
