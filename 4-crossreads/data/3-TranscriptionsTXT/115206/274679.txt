 April 1918 Friday 19 
 Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours.   D.R. Very quiet day. Groomed old Mick and had a go at harness &ndash; looks better. Went down to river and had a swim During afternoon had a swim. Sleet fell altho the day was a bright one. Scottie fell into the mud pond. Inspection by Lt Booth on the staff. Satisfactory.
