<p>September 1918.<br/>Sunday 22</p>
<p>Ahead of La Vigure, Bty Position.<br/>Weather wet, and fairly cool.<br/>Rained very heavily during the afternoon. Fritz very busy all day with shells.<br/>Smithy wrote to Marg [indecipherable] mother.<br/>At 9 pm Fritz put down a barrage on the gully. Scottie while sitting alongside me in dugout, stopped one up the forehead. Had a nightmare as the lines which were constantly broken by the barrage.</p>
