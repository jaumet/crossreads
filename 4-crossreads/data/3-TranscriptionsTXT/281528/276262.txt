 31/3/18 &ndash; La Basse Ville Was called at 1 a.m. and ascended the dugout stairs and mounted on the parapet alongside the gas sentry.  With dull explosions our gas bombardment went over with projectors and gas Stokes mortars.  They burst well into Warneton.  There was silence was a few minutes and then up went all kinds of flares &amp; lights, Serpents, clusters, twin lights red and green and other flares mostly white.
