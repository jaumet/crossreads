  
 Wonderful buildings and beautiful gardens.  Train and tram service very good. 
 Thur. Jan. 27th -16 Very heavy showers have fallen since 24th Inst the only rain for 3 years and heaviest since 1895.  Nights exceptionally cold. 
 Fri. Jan. 28th -16 
 Inspection by General Birdwood who was more than satisfied with everything. 
 Sat. Jan. 29th -16 Visited Pyramids and Sphinx and to see them one wonders how they were ever built.
