<p>April 1918<br/>Friday 26</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; dours.    D.R.<br/>Gallaghar returned to unit. For non delivery of despatches. <br/>Mounted instection at 10am.<br/>Weather wet. New runner for 113th arrived.<br/>Had a run down to Bonnay.<br/>Fred Aldred's brother died of wounds.<br/>Received a little mail one from Roy.<br/>Cant understand why folks don't get letters over in Blighty.</p>
