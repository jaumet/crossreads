<p>1917 September</p>
<p>29 Saturday</p>
<p>Lovely weather.  Dressed &amp; had a lesson with Milesov &amp; walked about with Scott Russell &amp; wrote some letters.  Nerves in leg distressing.  Dr. Muncaster, Mrs. Inglis et al came to the tent &amp; had a talk in the evening.</p>
