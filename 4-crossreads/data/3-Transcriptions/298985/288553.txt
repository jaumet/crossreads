But <strike>some</strike> 2 of the infantry who were on Patrol ran into a strong party of his &amp; were luckie to get away. We stood by for some time. Fritsy was only about 50 yds away as he shot 2 or 3 flames right over to us He did not attack so we went home caught 3 small shells on the way. No harm done except a scratch on Flemings hand. Got in at daylight. Wet muddie &amp; tired. Most of us had fallen down several</p>
