<p>16 November 1916 Thur<br/>Slept till 3 p.m. when I was awakened by Lou Hodge entering our dugout. He had with him a sac containing several bottles of Watson [indecipherable] Jack Peacock &ndash; Bell &ndash; Lou &amp; myself. Bell &amp; myself in bed &ndash; Had several &ndash; went right off. <p>17 November 1916 Fri<br/>&amp;[tomaine] poisoning</p>
<p>I knew nothing till breakfast when I was awakened by Mr. Long &amp; then I learnt all that had happened. Spent all day in bed as I was very sick. Couldnt eat. Griffin joins me in hospital he has influenza.</p>
