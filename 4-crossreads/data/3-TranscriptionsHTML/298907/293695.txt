<p>24th. Well I faced the fearful ordeal this morning of sitting in the dentists chair &amp; letting him bore my teeth with the drill, I had been dreading this all the week, but when it was finished I was surprised that I had got off so light, I quite expected to lose half my head, I had 3 drilled, &amp; he stopped one, I have go to back on Wednesday to get them completed.
