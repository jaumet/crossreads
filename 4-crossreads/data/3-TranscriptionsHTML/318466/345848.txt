<p>Sunday 1 December 1918<br/>Entered the Mountains &amp; are crossing them all day.  Some bon engineering.  Scenery very rugged &amp; grand.</p>
<p>Monday 2 December 1918<br/>Arrived in Vancouver in the rain at 9.45.  Went to the Lotus Hotel &amp; was not much struck with it.  Got passport vised &amp; tickets fixed.</p>
<p>Tuesday 3 December 1918<br/>Brekker in bed.  My cabin trunk came along but no other baggage.  Raing. like Hell.  Wrote to Dad J. in the evening.</p>
<p>Wednesday 4 December 1918<br/>Went on "Niagara" in morning.  Saw our cabin.  Lunched at the Good Eats!!! &amp; then took Rup to Stanley Park.  The zoo was a failure but the trees quite bon.</p>
<p>Thursday 5 December 1918<br/>Had lunch with Capt. Hamilton &amp; his wife at The Hud. Bay.  Went to the Pictures afterwards &amp; dinner at the Vancouver.</p>
<p>Friday 6 December 1918<br/>Raining.  Lunched at the Citizens Club with the Hamiltons.  Out to  Cairngorms in afternoon.  Dinner at the Castle Hotel &amp; dance after.</p>
<p>Saturday 7 December 1918<br/>Packed up.  Lunched at the Hudson Bay Co.  Got the baggage down to the "Niagara".  Saw the Hamiltons off to New York.  Slept the night aboard the "Niagara".<br/>Embarked on the "Niagara".  Had the week in Vancouver.</p>
