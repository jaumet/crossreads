<p>Beaumont Hamel was taken he told us all the tales we hear of how they feed their prisoners is quite true, he was very lucky getting through the German lines, the 48th Battn. collared him.<br/>In the morning we were carried out on stretchers, couldn't walk for they took <u>us</u> our boots away last night.  we were put into motors once more &amp; these ran us down to the Hospital train how splendidly they are fitted up bonza beds &amp; all</p>
