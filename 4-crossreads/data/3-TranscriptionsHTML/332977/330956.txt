<p>Saturday 12<br/>Carting stones for roads and boundaries visited Pyramids and Sphinx pay day arranged mess, visited Mena House baths</p>
<p>Dec 18<br/>Route Marches all the week. England annexes Egypt, our engineers leave for Suez to build Fortifications</p>
<p>19 and 20th<br/>had a swim a baths and visited Pyramids climbed &frac34; way up but retired.</p>
<p>Monday 21st<br/>Heavy tramp in the sand for 2 days</p>
<p>Dec 25<br/>Church Parade 10th Sports</p>
<p>26th<br/>visited Cairo had a good look around<br/>nothing but marches till January 8th</p>
<p>9th<br/>Heavy cold feeling rotten</p>
<p>28 January<br/>Recovered from cold received Xmas cards from Maud and Glady's</p>
<p>Jan  29th<br/>2nd  Australian contingent arrives at Alexander to camp at Heliopolis 17,000 Australians 3000 N Zealanders</p>
<p>Feb 1st<br/>Australians march to Jeda long line of troops, Artillery, AMC all out four miles long.</p>
<p>3rd 4th<br/>7 and 8th battalions goes to Suez Canal fight against Turks 400 captured</p>
