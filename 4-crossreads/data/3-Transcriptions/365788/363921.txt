<p>11<br/>deal of trouble &amp; next morning an order came for me to report at Head Quarters instanter which order I complied with &amp; they took all my particulars &amp; presented me to Brigadier-General Smythe who was really very nice &amp; told me he would try &amp; find me a place, but I am afraid moving off so soon will spoil my chances rather</p>
