  
 Fine day, half holiday for the men, plenty of mud.  Our stay is prolonged to three week.  Supposed to be mange amongst the horses, also Derminitis. 
 12-3-1917 Wet and dull, shoeing horses for Major Vaughn,  O.C. in charge of German prisoners.  Our men getting plenty of work.  Read of the fall of Baghdad tonight. 
 13-3-1917 Fine weather.  18 remounts recieved this evening.  Very sleepy hole of a place this. 
 14-3-1917 Reallotment of horses in Bty., teaming and etc. 
 15-3-1917 Same routine, a Bty. of the 4 Division 110th Bty. just in from the line, there 4 days, never fired a shot.  Huns still retreating. 
 16-3-1917 Frost this morning.  General Walker inspecting our lines yesterday.
