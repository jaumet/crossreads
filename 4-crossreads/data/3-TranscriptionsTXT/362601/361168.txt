 4 March, Tuesday 
 F &amp; W I go to Y.W.C.A. Dance &amp; play an extra which is well received. 
 I dance Canadian Barn Dance &amp; Brooklyn Schottische. I chat all about Plymouth to the Devon Lass &amp; have supper with the N.Z. Sergts. Kilties (N.Z.'s) are in abundance. 
 Wrote Mum. 
 5 March, Wednesday 
 W. I go to pictures which are rotten. The two Dicks are there &amp; I go to supper at Y.W.C.A.'s canteen apr&egrave;s. I am unable to get Boots or Breeches.
