<p>[4th Jan. contd]<br/>except Army biscuit &amp; cup of coffee which Sam brought.  Eating the biscuit I snapped off one of front teeth.  Consequently as I got well again about 12 I spent afternoon in Armentieres having the tooth repaired.  At night I saw the 3rd Div Follies or Pierrots in the Ecole Theatre Armentieres &amp; a very good show it was.  Back with Sergt Williams to Baths.</p>
<p>Friday 5th  Saturday 6th [Jan]<br/>Letters from Sth Africa &amp; Bro Ern.</p>
<p>Sunday 7th<br/>Moved our billet to front of baths as the old one was needed for other work.  Spent all morning transferring our goods &amp; chattels.<br/>FIRST VISIT TO FRONT LINE<br/>At 1.45 pm Joe Nankevil &amp; I obtained 'duty pass' to A.D.S. &amp; proceeded on our way thereto.  Passed Armentieres Railway Stn. where the sentry was very particular.  Then down roadway &amp; as we progressed we nearly walked on top of some of our batteries pounding away at Fritz.  On one occasion I was passing an opening in a wall when I saw a flight &amp; was nearly swept off my feet with the concussion of one of our big guns.  Couldn't hear for some seconds after.
