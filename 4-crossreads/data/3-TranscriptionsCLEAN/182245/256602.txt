MARCH 1917
28 WEDNESDAY
Fine but dull & cold. Cleaning
harness ect all day. I was
stableman for the day. Getting
ready for a turn out tomorrow
in full marching order & planting
all our surperfluous gear ect
so as the salvage people
wont get it. I put mine in
one of the barges up the river.
