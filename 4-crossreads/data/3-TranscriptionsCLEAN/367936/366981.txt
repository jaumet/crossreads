  
 Saturday 20 May 1916 Fairly cool. Fell out of my hammock last night but landed on my feet alright. Sergt. Smith in hospital. 
 Sunday 21 May 1916 Rained all day. Had afternoon off and wrote letters home. On picquet at night. Had to give horses lucerne feed at 8 p.m. My hammock broke down in the night.  I did  not  land on my feet!  Worse luck!
