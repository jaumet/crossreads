<p>Main product wine.  Made four stops for meals.  Passed Paris at 11.30 P.M.</p>
<p>Mon. Apr. 3rd -16<br/>Arrived Le Havre 10 A.M. and marched to No. 2 Camp at Sanvic (6 miles distant).</p>
<p>Tues. Apr. 4th -16<br/>Very comfortable in our new camp but weather extremely cold, heavy fogs.  After usual routine we visited the villiage.</p>
<p>Wed. Apr. 5th -16<br/>Equipped with new guns and limbers and warned to leave for front at any minute.</p>
