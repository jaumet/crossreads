 26 December, Friday 
 We work &amp; play &amp; gather X'mas bush some get stung by ants &amp; I get stung in a funny place &amp; I am told I am not nice to know. We try the motor bike. 
 27 December, Saturday 
 We send Mrs T Home early. 
 We go shopping &amp; up to the orchard. I get very dopey after sleeping &amp; it is very very hot.
