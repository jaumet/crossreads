  
 We left Colombo a little before midnight on 1st Feb, & woke up next morning with a strong head wind & a choppy sea.  We got some very changeable weather whilst crossing the Indian Ocean, also some beautiful sunsets.  We received our second pay on board on 5th February.  We passed Cocos Islands about 2 pm on Thursday 6th February.  The Islands are only small but the main one has a nice sandy beach.
