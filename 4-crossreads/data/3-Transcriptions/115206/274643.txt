<p>March 1918<br/>Thursday 14</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Went down forward, with Charlie Adey, and had a little while with Ken Gunn.<br/>By appearance of country around about the F.O.O. Fritz has been very busy with 4.2" and whizz bangs.<br/>Weather wet and indeed very sloppy.<br/>.60 hopped the bags at 3.20am. raid very successful. 4 prisoners and 2 casualties.<br/>Came home from L'aison with Kerr, at 9.30 am. after a very quite night.<br/>Things in general fairly quiet.</p>
