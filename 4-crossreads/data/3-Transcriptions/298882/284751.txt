<p>and we got slightly off the track but a W.A.A.C. lady <strike>gave</strike> told us the right direction.  She told us that her son was killed with the 2nd Aust. Bn on Gallipoli.  Strange that Mr Bates &amp; I should both have belonged to the 2nd Battalion.</p>
<p>Last night, the last of the old year was a quiet one for the men, though a very wild one for the officers.</p>
