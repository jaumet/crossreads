Had rather an expensive dinner, plenty of swank, but it was most enjoyable.  Visited the Cathedral which is one of the finest structures in the World. The carvings draping & paintings etc. were exquisite. 
 Monday 1st January 1916. New Years Day. We all sincerely hope that before this year is ended that this cursed war will  be over & that we will all be home once more with our loved ones in Australia.  A splendid Concert was given in the evening by members of the 8th, it was greatly enjoyed by all. Supper was served at the interval. You will see by the programme that it was some concert.
