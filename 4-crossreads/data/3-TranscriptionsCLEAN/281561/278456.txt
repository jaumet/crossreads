  
 March 3rd. 1918. As the roll of Carrier D.2. is over strength my name was deleted. Curses! 4/3/18. Another gleam of hope. My name was added to the roll of Carrier "D.1." Spent the evening with Arch Mc at the "Red House". 7/3/18. My name again deleted from the Boat Roll. Did anyone ever have such stiff luck. I'm anxious to get aboard that ship before I wake up and find it is a dream. Having a good time here though. 11/3/18 Put in for sick furlough this morning was full of waiting about here.
