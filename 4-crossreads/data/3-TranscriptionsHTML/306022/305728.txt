<p>Saturday 21st<br/>Rained heavy last night but hot again today. Sea a bit calmer than yesterday</p>
<p>Sunday 22nd<br/>Very hot all day and sea like glass, not a ripple to be seen anywhere. i didn't think it has ever been so calm in the Harbour, let alone where there is to be seen nothing but water. But there I am wrong for it is teeming with sharks, which all day long were swimming around us and chasing</p>
