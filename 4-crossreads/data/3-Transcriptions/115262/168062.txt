<p>Monday 22 October 1917.</p>
<p>Still on the move and riding the whole time with the exception of a few halts for 5 mins. or so, every 1 or 2 hrs.</p>
<p>Crossed <s>the</s> large Waddy at 3 pm and at 3.30 am halted &amp; lay on the ground and snatched a little sleep till dawn. When dawn broke, moved to a protected position <s>in a ho</s> surrounded by hills. Prepared a little bit of something to eat.</p>
<p>Stayed at this position till 11 p.m.</p>
<p>Saddled up and under cover of dark the whole Brigade moved on till midnight.</p>
<p>Abugahylum [Abu Ghalyun] was the name of this halting place.</p>
