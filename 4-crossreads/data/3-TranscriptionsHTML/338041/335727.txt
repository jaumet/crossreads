<p>Aug 23rd.<br/>We make an early start for Becourt-Wood where we relieve 1st Fld Amb and take up our new duties. (Day-duty dressing room. "Serious cases.) Plenty of solid work &ndash; Indescribable wounds &ndash; (*North, Fusiliers)</p>
<p>Aug. 24th<br/>(Elsie's Birthday) Many more serious cases<br/>Our Dressing station formerly a private chapel. Family vaults under the chapel.</p>
<p>Aug 25th.<br/>Another successful Anzac attack against the Huns. Our good work proceeds.</p>
<p>Aug 26th<br/>As a result of the previous nights work we are extra busy. Our room resembling a slaughter yard &ndash; During the day we have one man killed &amp; 12 wounded from our ambulance<br/>Inspected by two Generals</p>
