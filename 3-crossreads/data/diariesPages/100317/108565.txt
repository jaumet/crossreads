Retaliation was light and things quietened completely afterwards. Bazley from 21st was attached to me. An Engineer sergeant who spoke excellent German interrogated each of the prisoners. They were not very intelligent and said they were absolutely taken by surprise. They thought Australians were opposite and knew they were good troops but expected us to be black! Pinched the cap off one chap and gave him an old salvaged steel helmet without lining which came well down past his chin. Received congratulations fro the General and others on the raid.
