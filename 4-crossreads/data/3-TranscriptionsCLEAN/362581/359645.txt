
25.6.16France
My darling Eddy,
You must have a lovely party when Auntie Eadle come home. So glad you are growing stronger. You must get to know some nice little boys   &  girls at school, then you will have plenty to your party. What a beautiful party you had a Arthur Mitchell's Ask his father if he will come to yours. Fondest love darling   &  heaps of kisses   &  Good luck.FromAuntie Eadle.
