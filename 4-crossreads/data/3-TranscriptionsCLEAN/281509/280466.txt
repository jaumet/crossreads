
1916 August 31Thursday
Sanitary fatigue. Finish early. Cart, Fitz &Lamp; self go to Reminghelot to Y.M C.A in morning. A very big concern there. caters for Canadians & Tommies. Pay day. Go into Poperinge after tea. Cart, Mac. Jock & self. Rained very heavily all time. Are removing to Rest station tomorrow. A couple of miles away. C & self camp in barn on return from Pop. to escape the noise of the men who come home shickered after pay
