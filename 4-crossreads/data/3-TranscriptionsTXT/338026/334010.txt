  Boves.  
 [14 Sun] Grooming &amp; grazing "Donks" 
 [15 Mon] Grooming and cleaning harness. 
 [16 Tues] Left the lines with the Captain at 2-30 pm. Went to Paymaster to get my tele. remittance from Aust. put in pay book. Stayed over night with the section 
 [17 Wed] Left Contay at 10-30 am. Had dinner at Allanville. arr. back to lines at Boves about 4 pm. Went back as guide to limber with rations
