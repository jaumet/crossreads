APRIL 1917
1 SUNDAY
Fine till evening & then it
rained heavily for a bit & then
cleared off again. Cleaning
harness ect all day, & got all
mine up to the knocker, for
inspection at 4 pm, but the
inspection did not come off
We could see Old Fritz's
shrapnell bursting over
Armentieres way all day.
They started to shell our
battery out, they knocked the
houses that the right & centre
section were in down, but
did no other damage
