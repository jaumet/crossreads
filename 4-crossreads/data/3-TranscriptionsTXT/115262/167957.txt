Monday 9 July 1917.
In hospital.
A biplane was, owing to engine trouble, forced to decend near our lines. Another battleplane performed a very creditable movement - flew very low, almost pulling up, dropped passenger to give other pilot a hand and flew on. Soon a motor car with repair outfit was on the scene and in an hour the machine was in working order.&nbsp;
50 volunteers for A.M.C. out of A.L.H. Reinfts. Thousands of &quot;Tommies&quot; have now moved out on the way to the Gaza front from the main camp of Moascar.
