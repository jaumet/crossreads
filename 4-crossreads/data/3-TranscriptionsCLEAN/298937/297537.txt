Eggs & delicacies have been forced on them, to be received with surliness. Now Enver Pasha's brother Jaffa Pasha has been captured on the Western frontier & there was a tremendous excitement when he arrived. All the officers were dressed in their best & they waited at the entrance as if for a visitor of high rank.
