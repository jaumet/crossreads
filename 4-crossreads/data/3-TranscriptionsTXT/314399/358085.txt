 Saturday 29 March 1919 Very decent day. Went into town &amp; had lunch with the boys from the Nirvana. Went back to camp for tea &amp; then went in again &amp; did train picquet for Hopkins cos I want tomorrow off. 
 Sunday 30 March 1919 Had a look at a game of football &amp; then went out to St Addresse  [Adresse]. Had a sing &amp; a dance &amp; then came home. After getting into bed who should turn up but Bill Cowper. He's back for good. Do me.
