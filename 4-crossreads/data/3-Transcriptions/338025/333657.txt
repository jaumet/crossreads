<p>La Boiselle<br/>Harnessed up team and went into Albert but returned empty as we were not required. Arr. camp about 1pm.<br/>At 2pm we packed kits, as we were leaving La Boiselle that afternoon. Put our kits in the waggon and left 3.30pm. Arr. Bapeaume about 7 pm. After attending the horses etc. (in snow storm) we had to find a place to sleep (no tents or huts) Slept under an old motor cover. Tarp on top. As we were tired we slept A1</p>
