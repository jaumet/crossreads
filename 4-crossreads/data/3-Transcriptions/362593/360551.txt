<p>Wednesday 8 May 1918<br/>11 of us visited Jaffa, had a lovely swim and after a good afternoon tea at the Hotel Jerusalem.</p>
<p>Thursday 6 May 1918<br/>Escort to Jenin Hun Aerodrome.  In air 1 h. 50 m.  Pilot Lt. Paul.<br/>Bombing of 9 machines was good.<br/>Fin drove a Hun down that came up at us.</p>
