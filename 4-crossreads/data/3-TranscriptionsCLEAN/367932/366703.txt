  
 CR 44/27 
 Australian and New Zealand Forces. 
 Headquarters, Ismailia, 11th March, 1916. 
 Major H.V. Vernon, Reserve Regiment of Light Horse 
 With reference to your communication of the 2nd instant, I am to inform you that your claims to consideration for command in the new formations have been noted - but it is regretted that, at present, there is no suitable vacancy. 
 [indecipherable] Major A. & N.Z. Forces
