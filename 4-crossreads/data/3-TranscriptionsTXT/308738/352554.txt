 Nov. 20 Saturday 1915 Witnesses out at Mustapha all day Case concluded returned 3. 30 PM 
 Nov 21 Sunday 1915 Church morning &amp; night. went to Noosa Garden in afternoon 
 Nov. 22 Monday 1915 Worked till 12. 30 PM today Orders came to leave PO &amp; proceed with Pethard, Morgan, Rogers, Stewart to Over Seas Base Cairo. Reason unknown Left Alex &ndash; 4. 20 train. Arrived Base Cairo 8. 30 PM
