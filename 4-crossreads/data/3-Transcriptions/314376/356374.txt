<p>63<br/>through an inspection by the Divisional Commander Genl Walker. We fixed bayonets and stood at attention. When questioned by him as to our willingness to kill Germans, it took us all of a heap for the time and it was some little time before we were able to ejaculate yes.</p>
<p>The inspection having gone to his satisfaction we shouldered arms and set out for the Divisional Camp where the 3rd Battn which was out of the line was quartered. Arriving here we reported to the Orderly Room and had to undergo a further examination by the C.O. of the Battn Lieut Col Hasell-Price who made enquiries regarding our occupations in civil life.
