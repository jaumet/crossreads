<p>19<br/>Here is a high sided truck which appears empty</p>
<p>Let us climb and peep in.</p>
<p>It is full of gun barrels all damaged by bombardment of the enemy.</p>
<p>All this which we have seen is but part of the vast artillery wreckage suffered by us at the Front.</p>
<p>What of the enemy?</p>
<p>Theres is a hundred-fold more.</p>
<p>You cant look at these old warrier guns without thinking can you</p>
<p>Brave old guns</p>
<p>Yes, but nothing mortal</p>
