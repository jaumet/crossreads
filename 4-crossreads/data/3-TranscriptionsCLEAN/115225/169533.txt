Feb.19th  Fritz was over again last night dropping bombs. we went out on the Chinese stunt again. we stayed in Fletre for the day & took them home again in the evening. lovely day - we went up to Suzannes in the evening -
 
Feb. 20th  We stayed in to-day - Misty all day with showers. We went up to Suzannes for a while in the afternoon.
 
Feb.21st  On the Chino stunt again to-day - took them along the Hazebrouck road & we went to Meteren & parked with 3rd A.S.P. whilst waiting for them. (12th brigade lorries are attached to 3rd A.S.P.) In the afternoon we took the chows home & returned to Park. in the evening we went up to Suzanne's - lovely night -
 
Feb.23rd  We took the chows to Meteren. Whilst waiting for them we celebrated the fact of Bud's marriage & return to France with champagne & wine - We went to the pictures in the evening (at our park)
 
Memo.  The weather keeps warm, but is mostly dull with occasional showers.
