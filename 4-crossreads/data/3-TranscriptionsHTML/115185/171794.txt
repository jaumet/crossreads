<p>7 &ndash; frail &ndash; sympathising with everyone. Poor Turk's! Poor Kaiser! Poor Gibson etc. etc.<br/>I had not much news to tell her of you as I'd only seen you such a fleeting moment. I like the St Cloud house and have been there three times. They are all very kind to me and interested. I like the pantomime dog. Uncle Malcolm took lots of trouble to find out news about Gibson at the N.Z. offices. They are very badly informed from Dardanelles also in the Commonwealth offices &ndash; great delays in changes of wounded men's addresses etc. The girls will have told you</p>
