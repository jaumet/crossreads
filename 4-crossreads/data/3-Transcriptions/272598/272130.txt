<p>party to Meaulte siding to load trains.  Very cold; out practically the whole of the night.</p>
<p>Thursday 16.11.16<br/>Arrived back in Camp at 3 AM.  Turned in &amp; slept till fairly late.  Sent out after lunch to M.M. dump to draw heaters; again very cold.</p>
<p>Friday 17.11.16<br/>No duty.  Troubled with Neuralgia of late; not feeling too well.</p>
<p>Saturday 18.11.16<br/>Surprised to see a very heavy fall of snow this morning; weather exceedingly cold of late.  On duty in quarry loading limbers with stone.  Rained all morning.</p>
<p>Sunday 19.11.16<br/>General duties about camp.  Expect to be going out again in a few days to do a turn in the line.</p>
