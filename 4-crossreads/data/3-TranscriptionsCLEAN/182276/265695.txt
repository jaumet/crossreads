  
 9th M.G. Coy No 20 Intelligence Report from 6 am 19th to 6 am 20th:- Right Sector:- No I fired from I 10 d 75/20 sweeping enemy parapet and wire from 10 pm to 6 am. No 5 fired from I 10 d 42/75 on LEVEL CROSSING at I 12 d 04/64 from 6-9 pm  No 6 fired from I 11 c 05/90 sweeping enemy parapet and wire from 5 pm to 6 am. Quiet during morning and afternoon. M.G's active during night. Firing witheld from No 1 gun until 10 p.m. by order of Major Sneddon- wiring party out. Left Sector:- No 4 fired- indirect- I 12 b 9/8 Roads, Trenches, etc- Unobserved- from 6-11.30. At request of C.O. 36th Batt. no firing took place from front line. During enemy retaliation the trenches in vicinity of No's 4 & 7 gun positions were blown in.
