<p>Sunday 23rd<br/>Arrived back and joined Battalion &amp; Devsieys<br/>Monday 24th<br/>No drill had a look round<br/>Tuesday 25th Dec<br/>Nothing doing had a good Christmas dinner<br/>Wednesday 26th<br/>Still having a spell<br/>Thursday 27th<br/>Hut orderly<br/>Friday 28th<br/>Went up for D.Co. inspection<br/>Saturday 29th<br/>Mess and hut orderly</p>
