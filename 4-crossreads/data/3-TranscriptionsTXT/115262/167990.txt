 Saturday 11 August 1917. 
 On picquet 2 a.m. till 4 a.m,&nbsp;Sudanese on picquet also.  Told me that the  They were excited as raiding Bedouins had cut 1 camel loose &amp; taken it out into the desert. 
 Next post 8 am. till 10 am. 
 Printed &amp; fixed photos &amp; wrote letters. 
 Next post 2 p.m. till 4 p.m. 
 Whole picquet relieved at 6 p.m. 
 Had a shower &amp; turned in at 8 p.m. 
 &nbsp; 
 Sunday 12th (continued) 
 Mail delivered today. I received 8 letters. Only 1 of my Colombo letters received.
