<p>7/9/16  Thursday</p>
<p>Nice bright day. Heavy strafing off &amp; on all day &ndash; also at 1 &ndash; 3 &amp; 5 pm, it was just awful &ndash; our dug out was rocking like fun &ndash; Had no sleep &amp; I as most of the men were shifted back, while the strafe was on we had surprisingly few casualtys, We Heard old Fritzs horn blowing for his Ambulance men freely &ndash; Officers say our Fire last night played great havoc.</p>
<p>8/9/16  Friday</p>
<p>Dull day &ndash; Fairly quiet &ndash; day &amp; night. One of our Bearers stopped some shrapnel in the arm &ndash; One case of Typhoid from the front line.</p>
