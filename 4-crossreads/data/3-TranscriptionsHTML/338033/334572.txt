<p>Kept busy by the supply trains hours completing this journey, and mighty glad we were when we detrained at Tel-El-Kebir at thru in the morning Being dark, we saw very little on this train trip, but we went right back to Kantara before we branched off.<br/>After leaving this junction we passed through several small native villages where all you hear is the railway porters blowing kiddies paper trumpets. We marched  from station, taking with us our travelling homes and after a couple of miles tiring march, we were told to sit down where we were and remain there<br/>Until next morning, as you are</p>
