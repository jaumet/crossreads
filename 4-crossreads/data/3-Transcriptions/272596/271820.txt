<p>The Captain of the ship put his foot down properly tonight on the firemen he told them that if they caused any more trouble he would put them ashore &amp; in the clink in Colombo, &amp; he also cut all their extra rations which he had given them some little ago, down to their contract allowance</p>
<p>As I write this the mob are making a raid down here on the deck so I will have to finish "toot sweet."?</p>
<p>26th, This morning I had my payment of money fixed up, am going to get it sent to the Bank of N.S.Wales in Hobart, it will be handier there.</p>
<p>Heard a whisper today that they are going to tear our crime sheets up &amp; let the case slide</p>
