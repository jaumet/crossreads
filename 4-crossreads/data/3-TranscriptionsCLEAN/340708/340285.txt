  
 Sept 1917 Fri 28th My mate & I went to Billets today we struck good accomodation & very nice people Saturday 29th Went to Paington [Paignton] today it is another very fine place 3 miles from Torquay by tram Tuesday Oct 2nd I got my teeth fixed up so I will be going on leave soon Wednesday 3rd Put in for leave & got it will be going tomorrow Thursday 4th Started on our journey at 4.25 pm. Friday 5th Arrived at Edinburgh at 4.40 am my word they are some trains here & travel for some time.   Put up at the Soldiers Club.   Had a look round the town this morning it is a very fine town & puts me in mind of Melbourne    They have the old cable cars
