
<p>SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 14, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, with hot W gale blowing all</p>
<p>day. Al &amp; Barry &amp; Graham &amp; Mum went</p>
<p>over to Coomoo Coomoo for lunch</p>
<p>They brought Marj. back at night.</p>
<p>I was about the house all day. Went</p>
<p>for a walk round Hut &amp; Strand</p>
<p>scrubs&nbsp;in evening with Linda &amp;</p>
<p>shot a few bunnies</p>
