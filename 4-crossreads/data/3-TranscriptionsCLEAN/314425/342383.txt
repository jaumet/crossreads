Wednesday Jan. 8. 1919. 59.A fine day but cloudy.I took a 1/- ticket in a raffle for a French watch today.I am reading a magazine called "My Magazine" which is one of the best that I have seen. The articles are
mostly technical and scientific.I received a letter from my wife today dated Oct 27. No 163 written from Melbourne.
Thursday Jan. 9. 1919. 60.A very stormy day. I was issued with a new tunic today and a pair of second hand boots in place of the ones left behind at Little Moor.I am on a supplementary boat roll the boat is the ... this will put an end to my prospects of leave.
