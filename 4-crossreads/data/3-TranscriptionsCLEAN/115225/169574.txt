 June 6  th    We worked on the lorry (not too hard) all day - Went for our usual swim at noon - Jno was kept working late in the evening & I went to Zita's on my pat  We are looked upon as fixtures now, not visitors. We feel very much at home & enjoy the society of a family - 
   
 June 7  th    Worked on the lorry as usual - Jno was a bit crook & we did'nt go for our swim - We went to Church in the evening with Zita & Marie going home with them after & spent the evening there (it was a f&ecirc;te day at the Church) 
   
 June 8  th    Dull morning but it turned out beautiful at noon & Jno & I went for a swim. Frank was on guard last night so did'nt work this morning & I did'nt do too much either - Jno & I went as usual in the evening for our french lesson (so we called it)  We learned a useful phrase with proper prononciation every day & Zita learned the English of it. a very good system & it helps out, with what one picks up in conversation -
