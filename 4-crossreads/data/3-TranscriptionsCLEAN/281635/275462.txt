  
 all roads about here became extremely busy, there being hundreds of motor transport & Red Cross cars also refugees. At 9pm hauled out of bed & had to load up my wagons with stretchers. Then given 10 minutes to pack up our belongings & place them in the cars. Lined up about 10.30pm & Colonel told off certain of us for the cars & remainder had to march to the new C.C.S. I arrived 1.30am. 10/4/18 Slept well & rose about 8.30am. Given a job about 10am carrying material for huts. After dinner got job bearing wounded & at it till bedtime.  11/4/18 Wet in morning. Great number of wounded passing through. Heavy machine gun & artillery fire.
