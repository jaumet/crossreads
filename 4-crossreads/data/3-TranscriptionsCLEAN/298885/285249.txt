 Aug 28th - 18 Off again this morning for another promised visit and now am at Cardiff and made to feel at Home right away. 
 Sept 2nd - 18 Back again in London after a tip top time in Wales. Met Wal who informed me Geoff was in London and had made an appointment for me, so met Geoff at midday and had a real good fly around.
