<p>March 11</p>
<p>Church Parade on the open air this morning was disturbed by the rain &amp; the service had to be much curtailed as a consequence.</p>
<p>After dinner Ptes. Lloyd, Piggott &amp; McRae J.D. went to Shrewton for an early tea &amp; then walked to the Orcheston Church for evening service. The service was beautifully simple &amp; we thoroughly enjoyed it, as did we also the 3 miles walk back.</p>
<p>When we arrived back at our hut we were deeply grieved at finding four of our hutmates dead-drunk, and another one "on the road". Two of them were in an awful state, the other two were noisy &amp; merry.
