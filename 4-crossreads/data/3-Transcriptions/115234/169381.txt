
<p>A stranger in a foreign land</p>
<p>I boast no bright regalia</p>
<p>Bronzed of cheek, keen of eye</p>
<p>Chips of the old block all</p>
<p>We take our place by John</p>
<p>Bull&#39;s side, with him we rise</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; or Fall. &#39;Adieu&#39;</p>
