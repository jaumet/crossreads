Thanks so much for the parcels of stump socks, four have come to hand and have been such a real boon.  My Assistant - I hav'nt one now - & I used to knit at all odd moments but could not get ahead of demand and your parcels have saved the situation. It is wonderful to me now so many people seem to understand so little, how awful the war is even here, it is only now that food shortage is becoming rather serious, some seem to begin to understand a war is raging at our very doors.
