<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - R&nbsp;A</p>
<p>To - Brigadier</p>
<p>Date - 11 9 14</p>
<p>Time - 1.35</p>
<p>There appears to be a great deal of fighting going on in the bush on the road to W.T. Station The party that landed at Herbertshohe has not yet been heard of. &nbsp;I should suggest that as soon as the rest are landed they should try and get touch (1135)</p>
