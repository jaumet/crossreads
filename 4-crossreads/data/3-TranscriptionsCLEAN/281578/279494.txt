Lately it seems always a case of the Pommies losing a position, & our chaps holding Fritz, or having to win back what the Pommies lost. I had to laugh to see some more of our characters come back from the village, one chap had a tall black hat & white lace parasol, & on the post where our Lewis gun is hung to shoot up at any German aeroplanes that come over, is a pair of girl's white draws. Lor' isn't it funny, they take nothing, not even war, seriously, though in the trenches Fritz learns what they are made of.  Tuesday 7 May Up at 4 am. early breakfast, & we set off laying barbed wire. It came on to rain again at 9 am. & we came back. Had to carry a box of bombs each up to the new position we are making, & it was heavy work in the mud. Turned out fine afterwards, & we did some more wiring in afternoon.
