Aug. 28th A horribly persistent rumour is on foot that we are going out to relieve the 3rd Brigade at Bir el Abd where there will be taubes alarums  &amp;  excursions everyday. A taube was over the railhead &ndash; now about 4 miles out -  &amp; bagged a crowd of natives yesterday three killed  &amp;  seventeen knocked I believe.
