<p>Thurs. April 5th: -<br/>Left Fremicourt &amp; went further east to Velu &amp; bivouaced under tarpaulins in wood outside.<br/>There has been big German horse lines here.</p>
<p>Frs. April. 6th: -<br/>Shifted along road to midway between Yelu &amp; Bertincourt &amp; re-erected our shanties.</p>
<p>Mon. April 9th: -<br/>Shifted along cross railway line (absolutely stripped even of the sleepers by "Fritz") &amp; on to Hermies which village the 2nd &amp; 3rd Bns. took early this morning. We are awaiting a counter-attack.</p>
<p>Tues. April. 10th: -<br/>Situation absolutely quiet &amp; no artillery fire. Funny kind of weather &ndash; Sunshine one moment &amp; Snow the next.<br/>Good Country around here but not much material to fix up a decent camp.<br/>This is Easter time &amp; there is no chance of getting a letter away or buying a cigarette.</p>
<p>Wed. April. 11th: -<br/>Moved along to Coys headquarters on Sunken road at side of Demicourt relieving the 1st. Bn.<br/>Our line is comprised of outposts.<br/>Snowing &amp; things in general are miserable &ndash; not much shelter.</p>
