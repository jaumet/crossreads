<p>[August 1918]<br/>11 Sun<br/>Lead Kindly Light<br/>12 Mon<br/>Grouse shooting begins but we still are shooting &amp; caging huns in large number 30,000 prisoners<br/>13 Tues<br/>Had a fight today with a Tommy I won but the referee thought otherwise<br/>14 Wed<br/>The weather is ideal and the conditions almost perfect</p>
