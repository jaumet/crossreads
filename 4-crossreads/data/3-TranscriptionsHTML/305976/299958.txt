<p>Beilah for spell. Do not envy them trip through bad roads &amp; mud.<br/>Sunday Jan 6<br/>Drew for leave to day. Lucky to win. Expect to go to Cairo leave in about weeks time.<br/>Monday Jan 7<br/>Received Mail from Ausy. Usual camp routine<br/>Tuesday Jan 8<br/>Weather cleared up again. Beautifully fine. Went for ride to Yebnah.<br/>Wednesday Jan 9<br/>Went over to 1st LHFA. Farley on leave to Port Said. Some Turkish Planes over observing today. Flying very high.</p>
