 May 23rd  N.N.W.  M.314 Up usual time 6-30.  7-30 physical drill.  Dress for Church Parade.  Dress with Cap &amp; shoes.  Parade at 9-5 a.m., Service about 9-30 on Port side Boat Deck, Band in attendance.  Good service.  After mending till dinner.  Afternoon C &amp; S reading and resting about deck.  After tea quiet talk with Goode on N.Z. and present occupation, spent remainder of evening at Service, good singing, lecture on Drinking &amp; habits.  Weather morning changeable, fair after dinner.
