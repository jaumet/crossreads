<p>a fair sized town about 15 kilos from here.</p>
<p>We had no sooner arrived when we found ourselves being carried along in a human tide of rejoicing which marched behind and sang to one of the Aussie bands which played all National Anthems besides popular airs</p>
<p>Enthusiasm ran high despite a drenching rain which fell continuously from midday onwards.</p>
<p>The whole town was speedily covered with all national flags</p>
