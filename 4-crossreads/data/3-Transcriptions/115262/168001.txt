<p>Wednesday 22 August 1917.</p>
<p>No reveille here Turned out at 6 am. Fed camels. One of the party is doing the cooking. Breakfast 7 a.m. At 8.30 all went for an hr. in the surf. Watered camels at 10 a.m.&nbsp;Dinner 12 noon. Fed camels at 1 pm.&nbsp;Drew rations at 2 pm and then washed some clothes.</p>
<p>Went for a dip at 4.30. p.m. and another at 6.30 p.m.</p>
<p>(The amateur cook tried to make a custard but put salt in place of sugar.) (Result&nbsp;&ndash; a failure.)</p>
<p>Yesterday we passed Italian troops holding fortified sandhills &amp; redoubts, also passed several graveyards each holding 20 to 30 bodies of men killed in the Rafa stunts.</p>
