 23. Sunday In bed forenoon. F.P. rang up &ndash; I met him at Albert Hall the Felice Lyne - Sammons concert. Then we had tea at the Knightsbridge &amp; I brought him home for supper &amp; he stayed till after 11. He was cross &amp; tired &amp; instead of him  cheering me I had to turn around &amp; smooth his little feathers. 
 24 Monday Cloudy, cold. Maud came &amp; ate her lunch with me &amp; gave me some. Nancy ran in &amp; out. I stayed in bed till 5 when I arose &amp; wrote letters. 
 25 Tuesday Paul Walsoe called in morning saying we wd. Go to Bedales, tomorrow. Cold day showers &amp; shade &amp; occasional showers of rain. Pottered. Went to bed early. 
 26 Wednesday Met Maud at Waterloo at 9 &amp; left for Petersfield for the Whishaws  at the Village of Steep, Hants. Had a lovely day of rest and comfort among roses &amp; hot water. Lovely day.
