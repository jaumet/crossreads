 Feeling a bit off myself with Influenza. 5th Division went into the trenches this evening. 
 20th Monday &ndash; Tuesday 21st &ndash; Wednesday 22nd &ndash; Thursday 23rd &ndash; Friday 24th 
 In the Field Hospital pretty bad with Trench Fever. 
 Saturday  25-1916 
 Drizzling rain &ndash; Knocking about a bit but not feeling too good. Our Ambulance went into the trenches on Tuesday, so I have missed them. Dr. Beard &amp; his staff from the 60th Batt. Aid Post came in suffering from Bronchitis. 
 Sunday 26 &ndash; Monday 27 &ndash; Tuesday 28th &ndash; Wednesday 29th &ndash; Thursday 30th 
 Very sick &ndash; taking things easy. Very cold. 
 Dec 1st 1916  Friday 
 Feeling no good again.
