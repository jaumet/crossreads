 Sunday 1.9.18. Went all over Boulougne. Had lunch & tea at the Officers Club & then went out to No 2 A.G.H. with Garnsey & saw some Aussie Sisters. Had tea there & then went for a stroll & caught the train back home at 10.14 arriving at RUE  at 12.30 & back in camp 1.45am. Had a bonny time altogether. 
 Monday 2.9.18. Very tired this morning. Same old drill. Pictures in the evening. They were goodo. 
 Tuesday 3.9.18. Nothing unusual all day. ' cept that my mail at last arrived & got 10 letters. 
 Wednesday 4.9.18 Usual run in the afternoon. Came 92nd this time so improved a bit. Had a bonny swim after the run.
