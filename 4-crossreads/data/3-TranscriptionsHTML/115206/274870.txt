<p>October 1918.<br/>Sunday 27</p>
<p>1st Sthten. Stirchley Sec. Birmingham.<br/>After an long tedious trip awoke to find myself in the above hospital surrounded by Aus, Yanks and Kanucks.<br/>Feel a little better. Sent off four wires to different places. Wired Roy maybe he will come up here.</p>
