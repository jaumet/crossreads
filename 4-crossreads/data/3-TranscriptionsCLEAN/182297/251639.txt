  
 [Rear of postcard] 
 "Kinnoul" 
 Belmore 
 1.2.1915 
 Every thing getting on smothly in the Area been a felp. Changes in regards to Officers & Staff. Olds gone over to Milita, I hope to be their shortly. Draper is going. And a S.S.M. Coleman been attached not the first one we had. 
 Every body O.k. here at home, hope when you write again that you are "At Em! 
 "Seasons Greetings to you all 
 L.E.Y. 
 Major J.G. Tedder 
 Transport Division 
 Australian Expeditionary Force 
 Cairo 
 Egypt.
