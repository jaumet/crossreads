 Claude Leplastrier Esq &nbsp;[re?]  Sydney 
 Dear Sir 
 On arrival here I got a very pleasant surprise in receiving your letter forwarded on from Sydney &amp; a breath of the &quot;old times&quot; came along. 
 I shall be more than delighted to call &amp; see you on my arrival at Sydney to receive the copy of the &quot;Yandoo&quot; which you so kindly have secured for me. 
 During the whole of the [trooping?] during the war the only two contingents we carried which to my mind showed them selves as real Soldiers &amp; men were the 7th Light Horse Col. Arnott O.C. which went home in the Second Convoy &amp; the memorable voyage on which your Son went home.
