<p>1916 July 28<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Knock off work after cleaning up at 8 a.m. Seek my possie again  &amp; sleep all day. On duty again at 7 p.m. in operated patients ward. Missing arms &amp; legs. Young Roy amputated arm. mortification, wonderful heart. His gallant fight.</p>
