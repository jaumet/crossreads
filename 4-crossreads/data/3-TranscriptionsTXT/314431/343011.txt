 Original notes 
 Taking Mt St Quentin as centre, the bearings of the following places are:- 
 Peronne  - due south, distance 2000 yds 
 Doingt &ndash; South east of Mt St Quentin and south of east from Peronne 2,500 yds distance from Mt St Quentin 
 Allaines and Haut Allaines &ndash; North north east of Mt St Quentin 
 Feullaucourt &ndash; 1000 yds North of Mt St Quentin 
 Clery- Sur-Somme &ndash; 3000 yds north west of Mt St Quentin
