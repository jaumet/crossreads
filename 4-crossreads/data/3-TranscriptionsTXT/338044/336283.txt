 [The next paragraph is written in a different hand] 15th July  Brek 7 45  Sort of a greasy potato puff puff &ndash; slop tea &amp; bread. Overhauled &amp; passed two tramp steams bound west about 10 0. Sardinia in sight at 8-9 this morn.  Parade 10.0. Exs Jam  18 1/  chocolate 1/- 
 [The next sentences are in Hurley's handwriting] Alexandria &amp; we may go.  Home early &amp; learn French. Then to bed.
