 This afternoon walked over to Henencourt, a village 3 &frac12; klms away where the 1st Field Amb is at present. Met several old friends here, in fact am meeting chaps I know everywhere. The sector is full of Australians &ndash; the villages for miles around are full of our chaps and there are very few tommies about; quite a change after Wimereux. Heard that the 5th Inf. Battalion was in Henencourt Wood and went round to see a couple of cobbers, the battalion was away however on a bivouac. 
 The 3rd &amp; 4th Aust Divisions are at present up north in the Armentieres district. The 1st &amp; 5th Divn are resting in the district where we are now while the 2nd Div is up in reserve behind this (the Somme) front!
