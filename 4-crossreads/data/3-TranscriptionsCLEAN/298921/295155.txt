  
 July, 1915 Saturday 3  Blue Funnell Liner sunk in Canal yesterday, mined by Turks  we live on here hardly realising things, until the Hospital trains come in - We never forget reaching the Prussian Austrian Red Cross Train when wounded in S.A. it was heaven.  Letters from home as follows Mother two: 16th & 30th May Joe 22nd May & a Gumleaf May 23rd & Lorna 16th Aunt Louise May 20 Joe 23 May [indecipherable] 15th May & Paper  I note my news is published I suppose it can do no harm & may interest some Bushies, altho' they may think we behind with Horses have cold feet - but really are very tired of working along [indecipherable].
