
July 1 there are one or two aristo's & a few of the idle rich.Noticeable are several invalids some in bath chairs, others hobbling along on sticks or crutches. A rather ferocious bespectacled grey-haired lady keeps her eyes glued on the Prom' & doubtless sums everybody up! A young lady sews "industriously' & methinks smiles not a little - at yours truly I hope? And then there are the maids with their natty little caps & neat pinafores - they also love to see what is going on, & you bet they smile! Then we must not forget
