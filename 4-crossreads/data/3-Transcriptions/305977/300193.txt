<p>Tuesday 30th<br/>Did a hard mornings work.  Lt machine guns were planted in the hills about 4 miles from camp &amp; we had to locate &amp; attack them.  We were about done up when the "cease fire" went.  In the afternoon we got our kit fixed ready for service.</p>
<p>Wednesday 31st<br/>Got rid of all our surplus gear &amp; got ready for an evacuation of the camp.  Everything was packed up &amp; put on the transports.  The Brigadier inspected us at 11 oclock.  We then stripped our saddles &amp; unloaded the wagons.</p>
