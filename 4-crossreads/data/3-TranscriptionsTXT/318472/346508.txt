 10 October 1918 Thursday Left Sydney by the 4.45 P.M. &amp; got to Nowra about 9 P.M. where we were given a welcome by the Welcome Com: at the Station and on reaching Nowra in the Motor was given a Civic welcome by the Mayor. Got to Sydney from Queanbeyan about 6 A.M. &amp; went out to Waverly where we stayed till afternoon. Good News continues from all Fronts 
 1918 October 11 Friday Slept most of the day at least we didn't do anything of importance.
