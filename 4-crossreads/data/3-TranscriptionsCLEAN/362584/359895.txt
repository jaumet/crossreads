  
 Egypt 13 Feb 1916 
 My dear Mother 
 You might be interested to know what I am occupied with just now. Well I'll try to explain. I've just paid my Company & am trying to make the books balance. I claimed for 600 which I received in Egyptian pound notes. Now one English pound is equal to 971/2 piastres, & an Egyptian pound is worth 100 piastres, & consequently I received 58 500 pt. so far so good, but all payments had to be entered in English money & that's where the fun began.
