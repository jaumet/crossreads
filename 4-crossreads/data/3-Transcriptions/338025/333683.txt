<p>Thursday 10th May 1917<br/>Bapaume<br/>Harnessed up team and left park at 8am. Went to Dump at Vaux for a load of empty shell cases. While waiting there Fritz started shelling. Arrived back in camp about 11am.<br/>Went for another load in the afternoon and left them beside the railway line which runs alongside out camp.</p>
