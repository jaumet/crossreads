<p>JUNE&nbsp;&nbsp; WEDNESDAY 21&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>A cloudy day, but very warm &amp; muggy. The 27<sup>th</sup> Bat was on duty and I was detailed off in the &quot;ship&#39;s stores&quot; fatigue. Bill Hunter &amp; Self were down the hold getting things out, &amp; sending them up in the sling. Had to go into the cold storage room &amp; get meat out.</p>
<p>The A8 had a couple of shots with her gun at a cask at 1100 yards range &amp; got 2 hits&nbsp; HMS Kent had about 50 or more shots at targets astern of her &amp; did some fair shooting&nbsp; Was watching her through the glasses. We were all paid 15/- in the afternoon. The &quot;Marathon&quot; caught us up again in the afternoon after being some miles behind for a couple of days.</p>
<p>Days run to 12 pm</p>
<p>280 miles</p>
