<p>Jan</p>
<p><u><s>Dec</s>. 5th</u></p>
<p>Walk to ov 11 k.m to Avesnes to rejoin the Coves, most of whom have arrived back before me.&nbsp; Naturally things are rather unsettled, and news regarding the future refuses to be made known to us.</p>
<p><u>Jan. 6th</u></p>
<p>Cleaning up day.&nbsp; A number of cards &amp; photographs posted Home - Avesnes quite as uninteresting as ever.</p>
<p><u>Jan 7th</u></p>
<p>Paris Diary</p>
