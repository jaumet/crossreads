<p>June 1918<br/>Thursday 13</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather cool and very fine indeed, Smithy not too good<br/>at guns, with same complaint as myself.<br/>Still confined to bed, feel a little better, but very tender all<br/>over body.<br/>Peter promised medicine so far none to hand. Percy <br/>Grayling much better. I still feel very queer on the face of <br/>things.<br/>Had some horrible dreams. Costa arrive back at midday.<br/>He also is pretty crook. With this chronic complaint.</p>
