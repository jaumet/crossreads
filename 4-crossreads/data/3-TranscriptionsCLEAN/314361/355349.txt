I am told that it is nicer than Cairo. SS Caledonia 22. 6. 6 Got a move on at 0800 this morning. The sea is a deep violet, as the Indian Ocean. 1526. Have just had a salt water bath, in a large canvas affair.  We are making good headway now following on the track of another troopship a few miles ahead. June 25th 16 Lying in St. Pauls Bay Malta Sighted Malta at 0800 this morning. We are waiting in this bay which I am told is St. Paul's Bay, for an escort. The view we have of the island is not very inviting, forts showing their big guns, & a lot of buildings scattered over the place. It was a treat to hear the bells, which sounded well over the water.
