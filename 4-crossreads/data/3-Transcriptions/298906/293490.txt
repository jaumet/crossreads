<p>Had my letter to Harry Kelly returned today too much information of our unit or something like that was the cause of it.<br/>20th. Missed early morning parade &amp; chanced it.<br/>Had charge of a big party who were going over to the Gas chamber, made this job do for the mornings work.<br/>The late rains have done all the Camp vegetables the world of good, especially the potatoes &amp; turnips, wherever you go over here you will run across little plots of land between the huts &amp; such places all growing vegetables &amp; etc, it is a good idea &amp; will save an immense quantity of food, this is the result of the U. boat campaign</p>
