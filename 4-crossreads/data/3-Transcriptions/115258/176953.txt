<p>25th August 1918</p>
<p>My dear Father</p>
<p>There has been no more mail in since I last wrote home and your last letter I had received then was dated 19th June. I hope you will receive the &#39;Power o Attorney&#39; I signed and registered back to you some time ago - the unregistered copy has not arrived here so far. It seems much cooler down here in the valley than when we were down here before and we are on a much better job this time, so things are not too bad - we are camped well back from the front line, in a wadi, and right along side a beautiful spring of water, and &nbsp;we do mounted patrols from here.
