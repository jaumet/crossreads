<p>Tuesday 24 Dec.<br/>Arrived London 7 a.m. Out to Buxton. Had breakfast. Got some money. Visited town with Bert. Home &amp; cleaned up in afternoon.</p>
<p>Caught 4.53 pm to Sutton as guest of a Mrs. C.H. Benmore. Nice place &amp; turned in at Sutton after quiet evening.</p>
