<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;know definitely how he fared through it.<br/>Everywhere signs of harvest but labor seems very scarce in this country now.<br/>I am keeping in good health &amp; strength &amp; trust you are all well at home.<br/>Any amount of work in hand to keep us going, &amp; last few weeks writing of any sort has been impossible other than Field Service cards.<br/>Haven't seen anything of Rid for 4 or 5 weeks now but Wal said this afternoon he had seen him recently &amp; that he was keeping well.<br/>Must close now with love to all at home<br/>Your loving son,<br/>Leo.</p>
