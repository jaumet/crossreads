 I wish to make an application for a position on the nursing staff for active service abroad, with the Australian Expeditionary Force. I am 34 years of age &amp; am healthy &amp; strong. I have been ten years nursing   I trained in the Melb. Hospital &amp; obtained my General Certificate &amp; R.V.T.N.A. in Nov. 1907. I was on Dr Moore's nursing staff for nearly two years &amp;  for 12 month was theatre nurse. I have had varied experience in both hospital &amp; private nursing.
