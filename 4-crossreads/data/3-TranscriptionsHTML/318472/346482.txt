<p>14 September 1918<br/>Saturday<br/>A very quiet and uneventful day. A nice bright day &amp; a fairly heavy swell. No War news. Making very slow time &ndash; about 9 Knots per hour.</p>
<p>1918 September 15<br/>Sunday<br/>16 After Trinity</p>
<p>We are in sight of a good deal of Coast here again this evening and are amongst several Light houses &amp; we must be nearing Port Nothing particular happening.</p>
