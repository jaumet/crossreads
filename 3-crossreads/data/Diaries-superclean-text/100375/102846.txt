A parade was held in the evening, and much to our disappointment the A.M.C. A.F.C. members are to be left behind this trip, owing to there being lack of accommodation on the train ship.
A few of our number managed to get away and to pick them out we had to draw lots Learning that we will have to wait here a fortnight for the next train, we endeavoured to get leave either to Paris or London but all to no avail.
