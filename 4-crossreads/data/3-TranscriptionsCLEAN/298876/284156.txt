  
 Egypt, the original Gate or door to the Caves still exists in fairly good order, the cave & pillars are very substantial & still intact showing two crib affairs. Visited the Mosque of Amr - which is the Original Mosque, all others being built after this - also the oldest here can be seen the supposed pillars of Justice, where the Ruler used to dispense Justice if they pleaded guilty they were allowed to go free - if not guilty they were ordered to squeeze thro 2 Pillars about 8 or 10 inches apart, if they succeeded they were declared innocent & vice verso. 
 Visited the tombs of Klalips aso of the Mamelukes - 480 who were murdered & buried in 6 Tombs - The Khalifs Tombs are very gorgeous also all the royal Family - some costing up to 30,000.
