<p>1916 August 3<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Heavy bombardment recommenced last night. We have been warned that we may shift off today. So far nothing eventuated. Wrote more letters &amp; read. "Knight on Wheels". Drifting parachute</p>
<p>Order to stand by as we will probably get orders to move off. No order came through. Go up to see Bob before going away. Walk into Contag. Eggs &amp; coffee. See freshwater spring. nettles</p>
