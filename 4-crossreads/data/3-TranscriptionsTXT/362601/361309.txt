
8 December, Monday
Feel well & fit up after so many years. Mrs Doyle to visit in Home. Write after 9 pm to Auntie.
Wrote M & Auntie Annie.
9 December, Tuesday
Pick Gum leaves & post letter to Auntie. E on boat. We go to A[nthony]. H[orderns]. & Sons see Brultes, syce & beetles & butterflies.
Home & chatter.
Recd letter from MRL.
Wrote until bed time.
Wrote (M & Aunt Annie)
