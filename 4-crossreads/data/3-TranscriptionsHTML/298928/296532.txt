<p>The boys have often said to me when coming from the line<br/>He bargained for a minnie &amp; old Fritz's 5.9.<br/>But they never dropped a hint to us when asking us to come<br/>That the 3rd would be teetotal and we wouldn't get our rum</p>
<p>And we need it Yes we need it after lying in the cold<br/>And the other divvies get it at least that's what Ive been told<br/>So be a pal and scout around &amp; find out what you can,<br/>And all your cobbers in the lines will thank you to a man</p>
