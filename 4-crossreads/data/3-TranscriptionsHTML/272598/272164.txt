<p>Monday 26.3.17<br/>Terrific explosion at midnight last night;  Town Hall blown up by a mine laid by Fritz &amp; supposed to have been set by clockwork.  Many killed &amp; few taken from cellars alive.  Earth rocked like a boat at the time of the explosion.  Warned against sleeping in cellars.</p>
<p>Tuesday 27.3.17<br/>Fatigues for the day: walked around to the scene of the explosion; hundreds digging to try &amp; release any who may be alive in cellars.</p>
