 23rd February (Wednesday). Company drill in morning. Lecture on scalds and burns by Capt. North in afternoon. Big steamer "Northland" passed homewards (N.Z. Transport). There is great excitement in the Infantry lines tonight in view of their departure tomorrow morning for Tel-el-Kebir. We at last have our orders to move also. For three weeks we have been in suspense and are still living in the old buildings. 
 24th February (Thursday) All prepared for moving, presumably for Tel-el-Kebir,
