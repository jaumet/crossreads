
Sun.24th (cont.)a handsome but bantam-like "cocksure" person of 40 or so, leaned over the rail of the boat deck & absolutely screamed for quite 5 minutes at a "Matrose" who came up to trim the "Wairuna's" coal in a frock coat and "bowler" hat he had plundered from some ship.   Screaming is the only word which describes these outbreaks - Little Willie the Beautiful is addictedthem.-)
To resume, the band was earnestly discoursing Military marches & "Deutschland uber alles" when a big sea put the whole band out of action.   The wet musicians did not resume the programme.
Mon25 June.    A bad day for us.
On Tuesday evening, 5th June, Chf Officer Steers and 2nd Engr. Clelland  of the "Turritella" slipped over the side down a shark-line & struck for the shore.
