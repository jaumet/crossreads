 16 got up this morning just in time to miss all the pork (just our rotten luck) so had to open up a tin of Maconochies. Generals Birdwood (Com' Aust Army) &amp; Walker were present at this mornings Church Parade, but said nil. They are now inspecting the 3rd Batt.  Visited St Waasts Cathedral Bailleul. It's a beautiful building, the windows &amp; pipe organ being especially fine. The door leading to the belfry was smashed in by the huns &amp; one can see the broken woodwork which has been patched up. There are several large fine old pictures but owing the dim light, &amp; faded state of the canvas, I could only make out dim outlines of figures.
