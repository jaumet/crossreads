Oct. 16thDeparture of first section of our Second Division - now the time has arrived we are very loath to leave our home in Poperinghe - Nothing happened of importance.
Oct. 17thDay of Preparation - Abandonment of Shorts.
Oct. 18th.We take to the road once more and in departing Poperinghe we leave many kind friends & the nearest approach of Home or Home Living, since leaving Australia - Only pleasant memories shall remain - "Belgium we salute you." Arrival at Steenworde where we are billeted.
