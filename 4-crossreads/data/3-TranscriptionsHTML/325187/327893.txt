<p>[Tuesday 30 May 1916]</p>
<p>120&deg;  yesterday<br/>The men frequently find old Roman Coins along the Beach Track, evidently from the Monastery which is built close to Mohamediy shown on the map- on the Coast near the Cygoon I have not been to the sea yet-<br/>Rode all last night &amp; got into Camp at 5 AM New Zealanders are ahead of us.<br/>We are Camped at --------- (Ognatina) where some more Yeomanry were cut up- but they fought well judging by the heaps of empty Cartridge cases- &amp; many dead.  We found one poor horse still alive, doing a perish for Water</p>
