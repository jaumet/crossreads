<p>Friday 27 April 1917.</p>
<p>&quot;The&quot; day - a big one for Seyr &amp; district. &nbsp;</p>
<p>The Ceremonial Parade in the reserve near Seyr at 10.15 am. was a &quot;grand sight&quot; &amp; a &quot;success&quot; &nbsp;</p>
<p>Bivouac carried out for lunch after the review&nbsp;<s>order&nbsp;march.</s>&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>4000 troops took part. Colonel Semmens took&nbsp;<s>part&nbsp;</s>&nbsp;salute. &nbsp;<br />Aust. Camel,&nbsp;<s>Bgde&nbsp;</s>&nbsp;Fd.&nbsp;Amb. headed the march through Seymour at 1pm. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Sports program carried out in Kings Park in afternoon. A.C.<s>B</s>.F.A&nbsp;won relay race &amp;&nbsp;stretcher competition.. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Caught the 5 min&nbsp;to 5 pm&nbsp;train<sup>&nbsp;</sup>from Seyr. to Melbne on leave. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Stayed at Toorak&nbsp;<s>&amp; then into&nbsp;city in M morn</s></p>
