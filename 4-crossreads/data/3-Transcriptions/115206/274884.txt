<p>November 1918.<br/>Sunday 10</p>
<p>Hosptl. Birmingham<br/>Kaiser abdicated and Crown Prince relinquished all claims to the throne.<br/>Peace declaration is expected at any time now.<br/>Went to Birmingham with Johnson had an O.K. time yesterday and today.</p>
