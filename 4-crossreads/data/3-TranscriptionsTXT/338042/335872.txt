
May 14thNow that time is drawing near for return to France I am feeling very loathe to depart from all that is so dear to me in England. Marl returns from Manchester & makes the acquaintance of Mrs.Scales.  Marl & I enjoy a sumptuous lunch & then journey to Town where we spend afternoon at the "Palladium" which has on its Bill Harry Weldon. J.N. Rickaby. Ruth Vincent.
