It is just glorious weather now, & to walk anywhere the lovely scent from flowers & trees is just wonderful.  The scent is never out of one's     Nostrils, even if a tree or garden is not in sight the lovely sweet scent follows one.  It is very hot of course & on Sunday 6 of us went for a swim in the Avon at Bulford.  There were 100's in the water but by Jingo it was cold for the first 2 minutes.
