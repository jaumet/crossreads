<p>Monday 2<br/>I suppose my good letters went to the bottom of the sea with the S.S Mongolia<br/>Tuesday 3<br/>An impressive parade was held to day in memory of Major Gen Holmes who died from wounds received at Messines<br/>Wednesday 4<br/>Fighting again, I thrive on it I'm as lively as colt &amp; as wirey as whipcourd<br/>Thursday 5<br/>Had a yarn to a girl from N S Wales it reminded me of old times</p>
