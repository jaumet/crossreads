<p>Prisoners of war, but guests of Denmark and that after two days quarantine we would be allowed in the town.</p>
<p>15th December.  I went to a church service in camp.  The Chaplain was a Dane, but he spoke very good English.  After the service a Military Band from Copenhagen played all English patriotic pieces, Britania Rules the Waves, etc., and then in the middle of it all at Sergeant came in and announced officially that our transport was to leave for Scotland the following morning.  This speech was greeted with cheers, clapping</p>
