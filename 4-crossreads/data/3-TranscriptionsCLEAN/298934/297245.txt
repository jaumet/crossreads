  
 There are Punjaubis, Sihks & Gurkas here.  Several spies have been caught on the beach & there are many snipers behind our firing line.  At night the warships play their searchlights as far inland as possible & upon the fort.  All day they are firing.  Without them we could have done nothing.  Many brave deeds have been done.  The N.Z's captured 7 M.G's & a mountain gun.  Served out with a tot of rum each & cigs. last night.  Tip top.
