<p>sheets amunition &amp; all sorts of things all along the road (Some of the chaps making their load a bit lighter) We caught a train here (1st Class Cattle Trucks) for ASt Omer and marched from there to Arques. We were just about done when we arived. They billeted us in different French Farms we had a very good time. Practicing stunts for Ypres which is to come very shortly We were inspected</p>
