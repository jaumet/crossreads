<p>&amp; reached camp safely.<br/>Engaged in conversation with several French people since being in Marseilles.  One lady, the <strike>wife</strike> sister of two doctors, spent 2&frac12; months in the trenches with an ambulance, doing good work at the time of the stiff fighting.<br/>She gave me her address &amp; promised to <strike>help</strike> introduce me to her brothers if I wanted assistance in doing a medical course.<br/>The stamp of her experience in the trenches was on her.</p>
