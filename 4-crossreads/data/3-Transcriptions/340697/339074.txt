<p>We had a very severe storm here last night. Y.M.C.A. tent as well as a good many others were blown down 7.10.15</p>
<p>There was a general inspection here today. The inspecting general was the G.O.C. of L.O.C. 12.10.15</p>
<p>Packed up our kits today ready to leave camp for the firing line 26.10.15</p>
<p>Left Lemnos Ild. and embarked on Osmanich for Anzac 27.10.15.</p>
