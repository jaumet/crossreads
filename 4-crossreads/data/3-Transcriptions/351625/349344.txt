<p>27.7.18</p>
<p>hear on the Somme.  We came down from up north near Hazebrook [Hazebrouck] about three weeks ago.  So far our Batt. have not been in the front of the hop overs but have been following up as reserves &amp; have been rushing from one part to another taking up positions in the line &amp; have a pretty rough spin from Fritz's shells.  The German infantry hardly ever stand up against our pushes now they seem well fed up with the war &amp; the tanks seem to have been demobilised.  My word they are great, they save</p>
