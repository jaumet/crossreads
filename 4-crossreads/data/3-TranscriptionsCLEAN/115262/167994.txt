  
 Wednesday 15 August 1917. 
 Usual routine this morning. 
 Had a fall & have a bandaged arm & crook back as the result. 
 Received "The Bulletin" from Joy and 1st parcel from home. 
 Everybody in different spirits this afternoon. 
 On account of arm was not on night  wo   rk  march tonight.   
  Wrote letters instead.   
  Printed photos this morning.
