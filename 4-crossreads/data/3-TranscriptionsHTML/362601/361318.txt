<p>26 December, Friday</p>
<p>We work &amp; play &amp; gather X'mas bush some get stung by ants &amp; I get stung in a funny place &amp; I am told I am not nice to know. We try the motor bike.</p>
<p>27 December, Saturday</p>
<p>We send Mrs T Home early.</p>
<p>We go shopping &amp; up to the orchard. I get very dopey after sleeping &amp; it is very very hot.</p>
