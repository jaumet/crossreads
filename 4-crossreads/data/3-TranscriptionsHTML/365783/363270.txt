<p>take it in turns to go on sentry duty two hours on &amp; four off. Mick amongst others were sleeping in their hammocks when I made my round of inspection &ndash; they had to turn out &amp; fall in for inspection. I appreciated very much your little note in the Daily Light book. I will do my best, dearest old woman, to read a page every day and will always think of you doing the same at probably the same time. This should certainly prove a strong bond of union between us.<br/>I have never for a moment doubted that I loved you dearest old girl but since this our first real parting I have realized that I love you a good bit more than I thought I did.</p>
