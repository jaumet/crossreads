<p>1916 February 27<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Go down to Manly in morning.  Dinner with Mary.  Come back in aft. M coming as far as Quay with me.  Meet Cis at station we go out to Muir's for tea &amp; church.  Mrs Muir gives me 2 pr. socks. Home to camp at 10.30 pm.</p>
