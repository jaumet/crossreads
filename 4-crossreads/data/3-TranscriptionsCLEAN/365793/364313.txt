  
 Aug 2 The usual disturbance last night by the drunks brigade, but they quietened down about midnight. A couple of the boys were clinked for sundry offences. Tried out the boots, but don't go stacks on them. Wandered round the villages. 
 3 "Kut day", Rumored that several of us are to be struck off the Boat Roll. Sold boots for 15/-. Pass from 2 to 11, so "carred" to town, & bought another pair of boots & several other articles. Great crowds in the streets & along the sea front; & a fairly decent procession. Another
