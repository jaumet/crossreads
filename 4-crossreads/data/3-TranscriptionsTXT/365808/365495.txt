So here I am.It is a most delightful spot - fine house and magnificent grounds - and above all peace and quiet. One can only hear the guns as a muttering. My difficulty is sleep, or rather the want of it, and when I do go off I get horrid dreams.I'm afraid I am reluctantly compelled to admit that I am too old for a young man's job - and a Platoon Commander's job is a young man's show.Promotion is very slow nowadays - a certain number of officers do not go into a stunt.
