<p>likely we will be away again in 2 or 3 weeks.  The reinforcements were paraded &amp; our regiment were brought up to strength again.  Mr Dickson was made out troop officer &amp; I am troop sergeant of C Troop B Squadron  We got our rolls fixed up &amp; then were dismissed.</p>
<p>Thursday 30th<br/>We got our saddlery  out &amp; inspected the horses &amp; allotted one to each man the old hands being allowed their own horses back again if they wanted them.  I took mine.  The saddlery was not in the best of condition so we could not have a mounted parade.  In the afternoon we went out on troop drill &amp; were also allotted tents 3 to each troop.  Squadron Sergeants also had a tent.  A lot of parcels were distributed today.  We have plenty</p>
