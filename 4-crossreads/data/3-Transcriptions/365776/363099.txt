<p>Just as dawn is breaking a terrific bombardment opens up, heavys are booming all around us as we wait for the order to go forward, &amp; which we receive after about an hour of the heaviest bombardment I have witnessed. we now move up at the gallop to where our guns are, just as they fire the last shot prior to going forward, everything during this advance was done by clock work, shooting up to a certain time, having a specified time to go to advanced position. <strike>&amp; so long to get there.</strike> I think it was forty minutes we had to get into action, [Additional information transcribed from typescript at MLMSS 1025: having about two miles to]</p>
