<p>went on outpost on third Post 1 A.M. till 3 A.M. nothing about I turned in again.</p>
<p>Saturday Aug. 11.8.17<br/>turned out 5.30.  Packed up and returned to camp fed up had Breakfast cleaned up rested till 12.30 had dinner rested till 5.30 P.M.  fed up had tea watched the Boys Play cards till 10 P.M. and turned in.  I am getting a cold in the head.</p>
<p>Sunday Aug. 12.8.17<br/>turned out 5.30.  fed up had Breakfast.  Packed up and went over to Ballah arrived there 10 A.M.  off saddled and rested till 12.30.  had dinner very hot day.  rested till 5.30 P.M. had tea fed up saddled up went on outpost</p>
