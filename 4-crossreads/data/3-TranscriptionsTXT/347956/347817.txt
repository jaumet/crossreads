I do not know whether their prayers were for or against us;  We arrived at Cairo at little before sundown, we had a cup of cocoa &amp; a piece of bread &amp; cheese waiting for us at the station &amp; the cocoa was the best I have ever tasted; 
 by the time we unloaded the horses &amp; were ready to move off, it was beginning to get dark, we marched to the barracks &amp; had a few minutes talk with some Territorials &amp; learned that a Camel Corps of 125 men had gone out to meet some Turks 3 weeks previously &amp; only 5 men returned.  nothing has been seen
