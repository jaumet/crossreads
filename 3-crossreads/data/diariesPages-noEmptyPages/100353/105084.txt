Day after day we sailed on through smooth seas, under a burning sky with scarce a breeze. Several attempts were made to relieve the monotony of the trip. Boxing swimming were indulged in, concerts dances held at night so that with one thing another the time passed quick enough. On Saturday evening at 9.30 we crossed the Line at 80 E. The sea was like glass, with not even a ripple.
