  
 1914 Dec 24 Th Christmas Eve Received 14/- in pay Crossed the Line Father Neptunes Ceremony 
 Dec 25 Fri Communio Service at 7 a.m. Church Parade at 11 a.m. Good tucker - plum pudding & fowl Wrote letters First Christmas Day away from Home 
 Dec 26 Sat Sailed up coast of Ceylon in afternoon. Arrived in Colombo Harbour at 6 p.m. Ceylon coinage : Cents & Rupees x 100 cents = 1 rupee. Postage on letter to Aust. 6 cents
