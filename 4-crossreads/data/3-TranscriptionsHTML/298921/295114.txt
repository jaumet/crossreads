<p>May, 1915<br/>May 21-22 <u>missing</u><br/>Sunday 23<br/>Spent the day in orderly room busy with Pay Sheets etc:- Very hot &amp; our horses will be glad of the reed roof shelters being built &ndash; Rumour that 500 of our Brigade are back at Alexandria having had a tough time in the trenches but always successful.<br/>Here with the Horses we all endeavouring by Lectures in the heat of the day to instruct the Reinforcements as well as possible.</p>
