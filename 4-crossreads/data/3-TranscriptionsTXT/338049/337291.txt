 Friday 22 March Passed thro York &amp; Newcastle &amp; had a decent sleep as time went on. 
 Arrived Edinburgh about 8.30 am. Introduced to Cockburn Hotel by the cobber of [Korahs?]. Took up position at the Hotel. Very nice. At breakfast met Korah. Afterwards along Princes St, then to Holyrood (closed) so visited Castle. 
 Afternoon could not get accommodation to go to Forth Bdge so had photos taken. Visited Portobella. Not much impressed. Walked along beach. 
 Evening on walk &amp; to [Granton?]. Very enjoyable.
