 October 1916 
 billeted in a big Chataeu which is surrounded by fine hunting grounds &amp; a lake inside the grounds &amp; we remain there for a few days on battalion drill.  On the 19th I had to go with platoon bomb section to the Divisional Bomb School (on 19th had polling for Conscription) a distance of about three miles &amp; threw one bomb each &amp; returned to billet.  Our next move to   path   place the following night when the battalion left the chataue &amp; marched to St. Omer &amp; entrained.  The train moved off during the early morn of the 21st &amp; we passed through Etaples the main reinforcement training camp for Australians &amp; also through the seaport towns of Boulougne &amp; Calais &amp; it was the first sight of the English chanel since coming to France in March last.  We disentrained at Longpre &amp; marched a couple of miles to the village of L'Etoile where we billeted &amp; remained for a couple of days.  On the morning of the 23rd we moved off &amp; after marching about two miles we were put into French motors waggons at Mouflers.
