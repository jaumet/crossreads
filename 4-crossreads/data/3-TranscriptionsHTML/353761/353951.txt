<p>(20)<br/>The major is wandering along in the shadows to find out how the boys are standing the march<br/>"How are your feet Jones." We hear him ask<br/>"All right Sir" comes the answer<br/>And then again we hear him, nearer toward the rear<br/>"Well how are you standing it Bland"<br/>"Not too well Sir, My crook knee is going a bit<br/>"Can you manage to keep going" The majors electric torch flashes, and we know he is having a look at Bland, as he asks the question.<br/>"Yes Sir, I can stick it" replies Bland.</p>
