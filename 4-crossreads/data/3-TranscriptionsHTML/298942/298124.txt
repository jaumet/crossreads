<p>put over a fair quantity of shrapnel, evidently at a near balloon but was nearer to sprinkling our tents than hitting the balloon.  Baird got a hole through his hat &amp; a bump on the head from one.</p>
<p>About dusk a few long range shells fell between here &amp; Hazebrouk whose <strike> twin</strike> two church spires can be seen on the horizon.  A railway here seems to claim Fritzs attention a great deal for he is always shelling either [indecipherable] or shrapnel.</p>
