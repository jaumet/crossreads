 I'm afraid I shall not be able to send home many novelties now, we are not near the tempting shops.  This is a very quiet place &amp; nothing worth buying here.  Did I tell you Fred had an invitation to Eileen Grist's wedding. 
 Lucy wrote saying Thirza &amp; Ian were to be train-bearers.  Seemed to be great excitement.  He is a W.A. boy.  I daresay Al. will know him, Harold Hassell.  Lucy does not like Mrs Ma-in-law. 
 Sorry I cannot write much news but am on my honor. 
 I am very well &amp; happy.  So pleased to know Ethel &amp; Eddy are well again.
