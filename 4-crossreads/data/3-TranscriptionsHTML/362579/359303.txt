<p>Called several times   &amp;  I would not be surprised to see him to-morrow. I meet him also in England while on   <u>Leave</u></p>
<p>If you see Edmund tell him I'm very well  &amp;  shall never forget his goodness to me. Hope he enjoyed the voyage home   &amp;  had a good time in Paris. Awfully sad his life is, but her makes the best of it.</p>
<p>I think it best not to write to him. I'm sure</p>
