<p>this work was going on we were given our first Meal, which was Bread &amp; Butter &amp; Tea. As I was very hungry I enjoyed it as the Butter &amp; Bread was A.1. the Tea could have been better. After Tea we were issued our Hammocks shown now to sling them from the Roof above the Mess Tables on hooks. I did not fancy the idea of sleeping in Hammocks as I was more used to sleeping on the floor I decided to pick out a spot &amp; intend keeping it for the Voyage. Having left the Wharf at 4 Pm we did not leave the Anchorage until nearly 10 Pm.
