<p>July, 1915<br/>Friday 16<br/>Cairo for money, do you remember me telling you when I was captured by the Boers, taking 2000 sovereigns for the Munsters Territories so I take extra precautions here, but natives all over the world have a great respect for Whitemen.<br/>All danger of a native rising is now over but last February they were very restless.</p>
