<p>Friday Jan 4. 1918. 1250</p>
<p>Very cold &amp; frosty. The clouds are very low and there is a misty haze which obstructs observation.<br/>L/C J. Lawless &amp; Frank Hambrook are to go on Blighty leave tomorrow. I asked Mr Boyland to see the Adjutant if I can get Blighty leave next week to see my brother.</p>
