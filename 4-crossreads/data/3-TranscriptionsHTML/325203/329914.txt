<p>and a short though weary walk took us to our most welcome bivouacs. Here a drink of hot "Comforts Fund Cocoa" was awaiting us and each man with a blanket retired to his "deepie" and soon was in a sleep of exhaustion.</p>
<p>What happened to the gun after that I don't know, but we believe it is to be sent to Victoria, and so, if we see a field gun exhibited and placarded "Captured by the 21st Battalion at &hellip;&hellip;&hellip;on ? 4th July, 1918 well, we'll know how old Whizz-bang made part of its long trip to Aussie.</p>
