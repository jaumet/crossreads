<p>[Pages 54-57 postcards and envelope]<br/>I havnt written you since my visit to Paris - what is more I havnt heard from you lately I think there is only one mail a month from home now. A notification came round that a big mail from the troops went down on the "Mongolia" - I hope my "few-&amp;-far-betweens" werent on board.</p>
