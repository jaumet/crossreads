<p>Wednesday 13 February 1918<br/>Escort to bombing at El-Kataini [possibly Katana] on the Hejaz railway.  B.F. [Bristol Fighter]  'Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 2 hours 45 min., engine very bad, nearly cut out.<br/>Fired 150 rounds at troops in station.</p>
<p>Thursday 14 February 1918<br/>Cinametrograph man in attendance.  I appear in several films.  Played golf.  Did round in 65.  5, 8, 5, 8, 10, 7, 5, 10, 6.<br/>Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 30 min., cinema trip.</p>
