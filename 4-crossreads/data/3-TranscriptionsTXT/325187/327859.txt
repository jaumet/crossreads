 [Easter Tuesday 25 April 1916] 
 Court again today &amp; in the evening I dined with Sami Famous &amp; Zaki Bey, in one of the most gorgeous Homes I have seen.  A splendid dinner well served nice linen silver &amp; glass, the latter man was educated at Oxford &amp; is quite accomplished - &amp; very rich. [Following paragraph is crossed out] Received Mail- Mother  -Australian from Joe  - Daisy 12/3/  -Laura  -May 19/3/16 Please tell latter I got her Sox etc &amp; also Lornas I cannot write to everyone much as I should like to answer. Sorry some of my notes are again missing omit
