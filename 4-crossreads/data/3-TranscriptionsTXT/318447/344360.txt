 Today I made my last will &amp; testament owing to the fact that the paybook will has been proved valueless &amp; they require everyone to make a formal will which they are going to keep in Cairo. I must find out if it is possible to get a trip up there in order to change it. 
 January at The Full o' the Moon Great rumours going around this morn of a night raid such that one sleeps in suspense these nights.
