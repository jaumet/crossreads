 21/2/17  Wednesday 
 Warm &amp; Foggy &ndash; No aeroplanes have been able to fly for over a week &ndash; W.O. Geo Black left us to go up for a commission, also several others. Fairly busy. 
 22/2/17  Thursday 
 Warm &amp; Foggy &ndash; A lot of trench feet came through &ndash; Several raids took place last night. 
 Friday  23/2/17 
 Getting a bit colder &ndash; Fairly busy. 
 Wrote to Mrs O'Kelly about Hubert, after much enquiring. I can only get the information that he is posted as missing &ndash; after an attack before Bapaume on Nov 13th. 
 Saturday  24/2/17 
 Cold &amp; foggy. Starting to freeze again. Busy with trench feet &amp; wounded all night. All sorts of rumours about great gains on our front &ndash; but nothing official. 
 Sunday  25/2/17 
 Fairly Cold. Fairly busy night and day &ndash; Got orders at very short notice to be ready to march out ant time &ndash; Finally only B Section went out &amp; opened a new Aid Post.
