 Monday 4 
 Got to Salonique at 7 &amp; got out to camp very cold there. Tea at W. Tower with Reid &amp; Brown.

 Went down to Red Cross met Richardson &amp; Dick &amp; went on Board Lafayette 11.30. Sailed at 4 p.m.

 Thurs 7 
 Even keel. Visited Serbs. Talked with old col. Wrote some. Warm 
 Frid.8 
 Sea like glass. Ship absolutely steady. Read 3 acts of play to Sister McD &amp; finished the 4th.
