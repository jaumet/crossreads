 Australian Branch B.R.C.S. 
 Perham Downs 23.2.17 
 Dear Dad, Three letters from you dated 11th, 19th and 29th Nov. arrived today re-directed from France.  I note your remarks on modesty.  Personally I don't consider I am too modest at all.  There was a time early in the war when I would have been proud of the feat I described but now it is commonplace.
