<p>Miss Hodge &amp; I went to B. Museum for day.  Lunch &amp; tea at Minerva.<br/>7.Thursday.<br/>Air raid started about 11.30 p.m. "All clear" about 1.45 a.m. could hear every plane buzzing above us.</p>
<p>8.Friday<br/>B.M. in morning.   Miss H. took me to on "The Better 'Ole' at Oxford in the afternoon. A. Bourchier as 'Old Bill'.</p>
<p>9. Saturday.<br/>Stayed in all day &ndash; tried to do a little writing. Miss Hodge came home late with tales of bomb damage at Warwick Ave.</p>
