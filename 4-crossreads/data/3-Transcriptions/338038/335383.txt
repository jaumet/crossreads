<p>III<br/>forced to change.  Have nothing fresh from this side &ndash; Had letters this mail from Arny and Ath Cochrane.  The former has been further honoured with a D.S.O. &ndash; They are both out in the back country having a good rest and our Division is going back in a day or so to re-equip and do some training.
