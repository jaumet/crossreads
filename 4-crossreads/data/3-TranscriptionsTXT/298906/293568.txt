
Have been drilling these Mortar chaps of ours all day, among them they have an ex 20th Battn Major, he was cashiered for cowardice last October & sent back to Australia, he then re-enlisted & is here as a private, seems funny don't it & he don't seem a bad sort of fellow, I suppose he is out to wipe that stain off at any cost, for it must be a terrible thing to have hanging over one Ross is his name.Am Sergt of the Inlying piquet tonight & it is raining as per usualSome Yanks were round here today with some of our heads the Yanks are taking all these Camps over I believe, & we are (moving
