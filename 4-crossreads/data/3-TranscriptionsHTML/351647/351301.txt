<p>We had quite a thrilling time at Albert but I'll have to leave particulars till I see you.</p>
<p>Fancy I saw dear old Duncan up there.  I went to see a pal in the 9th &amp; the first one I saw was Duncan looking just the same only rather smart.  So I up &amp; saluted &amp; he didn't know me.  I felt quite crushed but we had a good yarn &amp; he was quite pleased to see me.  His overseer, Major Gee, is an old pal of mine &amp; calls me <u>Ern</u>.  Oh 'elp.  Alan Brown one of the Sergeants is a pal of mine, his people live next mine in Tassy so of course now I'm dying to get into the 9th but there's not much show.
