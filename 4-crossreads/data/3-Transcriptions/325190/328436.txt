<p>Saturday Oct 20th 1917<br/>Landed at Le Havre yesterday morning &amp; marched some six miles to Harfleur where we pulled up at Rouelles camp.<br/>Passed doctor; issued with tin helmet &amp; two varieties of gas helmets; also had vapour bath, all before tea.  Several of last draft still here.<br/>"Trish" of Tisbury fame, &amp; Naim &amp; Dunstan are here, but Harry Millard has gone up the line to a C.C.S.<br/>Missed first parade this morning &amp; hear there will be trouble over it, but<br/>---------  mabeesh.<br/>At 7.15 this morning</p>
