<p>Feuquieres. I wander about Abbeville &amp; then go to Club for dinner (4f) as the end of the trip is in sight &amp; I need not husband my cash at the expense of my stomach. Dine with Redfern Jock Stewart &amp; leave in middle of meal to catch train.</p>
<p>On arrival at station I find my luggage has disappeared &amp; miss train in order to look for it I then proceed to tell off the military police &amp; the french railway official in a way that must have been laughable to any onlooker. However this will not find the valise &amp; I decide to wait till morning to consider what is best to be done.</p>
<p>There is no room at the Club for a bed &amp; I try Tete de Boef then Hotel de la France &amp; several other places but unsuccessfully &amp; go to Club &amp; sleep in a chair in a warm room. For the last 3 nights I have been sleeping in the train &amp; am so tired that I go to sleep straight away &amp; do not wake up till 6 am the following morning.</p>
<p>12-1-1919<br/>Awoke much refreshed after my sleep &amp; breakfast (3f 50). I then travel out to wing to see if my kit has been taken there by mistake but all to no effect &ndash; it is not</p>
