<p>Thursday 6/12/17. Reconnoitred ground for tactical Scheme with Wisdom. Col. Hind to dinner.</p>
<p>Friday 7/12/17&nbsp; Left for Wisques for T.M. demonstration returned at 4.30 p.m.</p>
<p>Saturday 8/12/17. Saw Raiders at work- Quiet Day&nbsp; Wrote to Doff.</p>
<p>Sunday 9/12/17. Reconnoitred ground for scheme. Afternoon Address by Chaplain General. Evening Concert Brigade Very Good.</p>
