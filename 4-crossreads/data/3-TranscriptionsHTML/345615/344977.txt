<p>Contingent that went home to England. I suppose most of them will find their way out here, as France will be too cold for them</p>
<p>There is a rumour to the effect that we are to return to join up with the 1st Division to which we belong &amp; which is now down near the Canal</p>
<p>The enemy have started gathering again in force near the wells from which they were dispersed on Xmas Day &ndash; so I expect another column will be sent out in a day or two to deal with them.</p>
<p>Jan 21 (Sat)<br/>Orders came out this morning of a column leaving camp this afternoon to attack the enemy &amp; all our waggons available had to turn out. <strike>Just</strike> As I'm just</p>
