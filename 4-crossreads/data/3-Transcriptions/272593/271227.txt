<p>good to us I thought, so I said to the chaps we will have to sneak back one at a time &amp; I will start out first, so off I went  just before I reached our trench I watched for the others to come up, &amp; just as each man dropped into the trench a sniper had a shot at each of us, you would have laughed if you could have seen the dive that the last man made  he fairly flew into the ditch.<br/>"No mans land" is covered with German dead  they are laying all over the place &amp; I cut a fine pair of wire cutters off one chap &amp; also brought a rifle, bomb and gas helmet for curios. <br/>The next thing I had to do was to report to Mr Edgeley, &amp; then I had to write a "report" out on my night's work &amp; and it was sent down to Hqrs at once,  the Capt reckoned I had done</p>
