<p>1917 July</p>
<p>8 Sunday</p>
<p>Arrived in Taranto at 10 or so &amp; made for Y.M.C.A. town centre picking up a Mr Conyers Baker at M.C.A. in dismal cellar paid 6/8 for our three breakfasts.<br/>Got rooms in same flat as Miss Wells. Fleas attacked us immediately. Very hot glaring &amp; dusty &amp; oh, those shrieking Italians. Sat up late in Maggiore Napoleons Galassi's room. Reported to Base Commandant ciceroned by a Mr Butza from West Australia.</p>
