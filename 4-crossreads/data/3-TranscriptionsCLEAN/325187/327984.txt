  
 [Tuesday 29 August 1916] 
 Sister Calvert says I look 10 years older & she would not know- we made no such ungallant remark to her. "Gyppy Tummy" last night, I hope for the last time - sort of Colick caused by sand & Bully beef etc:- I suppose- but that is a month ago nearly- Lovely weather- all [indecipherable] the swell. Just heard by Wireless that ROUMANIA has joined us this will help immensely
