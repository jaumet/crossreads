<p>We strafed it soundly in our curses!</p>
<p>All manner of buildings were totally wrecked by Fritz's gun-fire. We had a grand wash with the aid of "nice clean shell hole water"!</p>
<p>We stayed there the day, cleaning our rifles and fixing our gear preparatory to moving towards the line.</p>
<p>Good news! A fine load of mail arrived from the transports! Letters were eagerly read everywhere, I received 9 from loved ones! Letters were the best tonic one could have, before going up to strafe Fritz or he doing it to us!</p>
<p>At 4 o'clock in the morning of Sept 23rd 1917, we had a very hurried breakfast and departed a few minutes after. It was pitch dark then!</p>
<p>Ypres was our objective, we reached this much shattered town, just as dawn was breaking.</p>
