<p>13-6-16<br/>Tue 13th<br/>This morning an aeroplane Sgd visited the Turks &amp; it is reported they bombed the Turkish aerodrome;  One of our planes also gave us a small scare as she was coming from towards Kantara flying exceptionally high.  when right above she commenced some fancy tumbling acts &amp; then drop straight down a few hundred feet regain its equilibrium &amp; start some fresh antics;  Everyone agreed that it must have been Samson in his war-plane, &amp; we received word later during the day saying it was him;  He has a steel rammer on the front of his machine &amp; boasts he will bring down the first enemy plane he sees;</p>
<p>The 3rd L.H.Regt came across a Turkish patrol of 7 men on camels last Sunday &amp; managed to wound two camels &amp; men &amp; brought them in;  One of the Turks had his arm amputated today as it was shattered.  The other was wounded rather badly through the leg</p>
