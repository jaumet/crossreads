 Monday 5 November 1917. 
 Brigade Orderly despatch till 10.30 am. 
 At 2.30 pm A.C.F.A moved to main road &amp; relieved N.Z&nbsp;Mtd Fd Amb at 4.30 pm when A Sec. moved out at dusk to the front line for active service. I was in the group as advanced dressing station for the 2 nd  Battn I.C.C. Bgde. 
 The whole Bgde was completely lost &amp; never got into communication before midnight tonight when N.Z.&#39;s were relieved. 
 El Jabri El Hawi Ras-el&nbsp;Nag Kuteelfah
