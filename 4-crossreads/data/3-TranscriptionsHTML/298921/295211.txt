<p>August, 1915<br/>Saturday 28<br/>Nile is now flooded, leaving its silt over the valley &amp; fertilizing it for successive crops &ndash;<br/>We get so little reliable news except from the wounded &ndash; Even the graphic [indecipherable] illustration of the Aust: L.H. charging <u>"on horseback</u> at Gallipoli &amp; with rifles slung &ndash; most absurd <u>no</u> horses being over there &ndash;<br/>I hope the seasons are better for Graziers all round the ram sales were not very brilliant this year.<br/>500 more men came in yesterday.</p>
