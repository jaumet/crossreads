  
 27th Thursday [January] Reported with 6 men to 5th Engineers for duty.  Marking out Fire Positions (Trenches).  Coy moved on Nth. to Canberra Post  fro  about 2 miles off and pitched tents.  Much rain in showers.  No. 8 on outpost duty from 6pm. till 6am. following day.  Wet & bitterly cold & tired. 
 28th Friday Continued with Engineering officer on Canberra Redoubt during afternoon.  Morn. at rest. 
 29th Sat. Measuring & shallow trenching redoubt trenches ready for workmen, finished about 4.30pm.  [See Page 36 for 30th January.]
