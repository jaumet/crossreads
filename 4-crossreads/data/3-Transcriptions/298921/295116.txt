<p>May, 1915<br/>Tuesday 25<br/>Horses all developed severe Catarrh 75% sick from sand &amp; this forms Bronchial trouble &ndash;<br/>The Shelter sheds are not finished &amp; the sun is very hot &ndash;<br/>I gave a Lecture today on Musketry &amp; notes from the front, in the heat of the day it is hard to keep awake especially as we rise at 4.30 A.m.<br/>Still in doubt as to whether our men have received their Baptisms of fire yet.</p>
