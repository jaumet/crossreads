  
 25 Aug 15. Cairo Dear Mrs. Read, I sent a parcel last Saturday containing 3 Camels for Nurse & two for Children. One high one with soldier on top for Miss Margaret & one for Miss Tingey, & the Key of toys box tied around neck of camel. Hoping you arrived quite safely & are all quite well & house is all right. Yours faithfully [indecipherable] from Mahomet
