May 30thOther reinforcements formed & I got into the 9lth of 6th Reg with some mates of mine.We had a very hard time at drill for some weeks under an officer named Prideaux Brune who was once an officer in a English Calvary Regt, he was not popular with the Boys  We are now ready for Egypt when boats are available .You will notice that I miss writing down notes for a few
