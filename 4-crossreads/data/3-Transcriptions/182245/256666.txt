
<p>385</p>
<p>MAY 1917</p>
<p>31 THURSDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Working round the</p>
<p>pit all day filling sandbags ect.</p>
<p>I was firing&nbsp;on some stunts</p>
<p>all afternoon. Did S.O.S. Guard</p>
<p>at night &amp; then pit guard after.</p>
<p>We had a fire in the pit</p>
<p>in afternoon &amp; it burnt a lot</p>
<p>of our camel fly &amp; sandbags</p>
<p>but did no further damage.</p>
<p>I fired my first shot out of</p>
<p>an 18 pounder. Fritz sending</p>
<p>over a few, but did not do</p>
<p>much damage. we&nbsp;were</p>
<p>sending them over all day</p>
<p>in one stream. Fritz got a dump</p>
<p>behind us at night</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
