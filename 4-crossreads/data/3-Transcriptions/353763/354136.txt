<p>The Grantala too was signalled  -  I hated this because I wanted the lot to myself  -  However I was very nice to the Protector crowd &amp; if they send a landing party you bet I'm there to help  -  Presently we went up on quarter deck &amp; yarned away  Spooner is English - 30 - out 2 years  -  Then our Motor boat hove alongside &amp; Elms &amp; Porter were asked aboard amp; we had Port wine &amp; yarned &amp; then left  -<br/>By the way a prize turned up &ndash; S.S. Sumatra in charge of a prize crew followed by the Parramatta - a German trader - but ours now  -  She anchored close to us &amp; we steamed round her &amp; had a look at the fat German skipper &amp; the armed blue jackets &amp; thanked goodness we were top dogs  -<br/>We then turned the nose of the motor in shore &amp; by Jove the dense jungle &amp; the coco nuts excited our great admiration  -  An intensely green dense jungle almost to the water edge forms the fore shore  -  That extends back &amp; then up go the hills &ndash; some low &amp; others cone shaped towering to heaven  -  A shrub some 8  - 10 feet was a blaze of scarlet but I could not determine its kind</p>
