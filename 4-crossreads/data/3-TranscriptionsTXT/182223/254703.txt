 Monday 4&nbsp;October 1915&nbsp; Left Cairo at noon today&nbsp; Arrived Alexandria at 4-30 pm.&nbsp; Reported to Act.G. 3 rd  Echelon at 6 p.m.&nbsp; At Hotel Majestic in Alexandria at 5 pm.&nbsp; 
 Tuesday 5&nbsp;October 1915&nbsp; In Alexandria. Arranged about departure for Mudros &amp; Anzac on Friday.&nbsp; Telephoned&nbsp;Mrs Rudd about letters &amp; papers.&nbsp; At Mustapha. Ride around in small trams with Jerom.&nbsp; Ramble round the city at night. Wrote many letters&nbsp; Bed about midnight&nbsp; 
 Wednesday 6&nbsp;October 1915&nbsp; In Alexandria&nbsp; Went as far as 17. Gen. Hospital, end of the Ramelah [Ramleh] tram line.&nbsp; Between 9 &amp; 11 p.m. rambled round the Native quarter of the city midst lanes, &amp; roads, narrow dirty, at Egyptian music halls playhouses Very interesting but horribly dirty. How is it that some epidemic does not come &amp; Sweep the whole people off the face of the Earth?
