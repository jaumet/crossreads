 July &ndash; 1918 
 July 22nd Reveille 6 a.m. &ndash; Load Motor Lorries and accompanied by the 27th Battalion Band we leave Bertangles for the Old French Town of Rouen &ndash; Having Australian Drivers on the Motors can you wonder that we travel at a reckless pace through country ad village on a wild endeavour to prove each Lorry the fastest &ndash; Beautiful Landscapes and Farms that are very rich in Crops &ndash; Rest at Neufchatel &ndash; Lunch &ndash; Journey resumed &ndash; German prisoners working on the roads &ndash;
