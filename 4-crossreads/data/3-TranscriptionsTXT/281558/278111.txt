 Wednesday May 15th 
 Dunkirk : on duty till 6 am : round to my friends place after tea another air raid : heavy couter barrage : book of views (Aust) &amp; letter to J B J : 
 Thursday May 16th 
 Dunkirk : on duty till 6 am : yarn to the little girl in the shop in afternoon : nice stroll to Rosandal after tea &amp; seen the largiest &amp; prettiest pansies I have ever come across : Madam Avery nice &amp; asked us to look all over the large gardens : very nice : three air raid alarms at night :
