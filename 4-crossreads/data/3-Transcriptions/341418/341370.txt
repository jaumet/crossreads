<p>But it was a terribly large net to catch such small fish.<br/>We were composed of: - one General with staff complete. Two Brigades of mounted men &ndash; roughly 1500 men &ndash; one big 18 pde gun. Two Ambulances and nearly a thousand camels bringing water, stores  &amp;  equipment.<br/>I think the trouble lay in the fact that since the aeroplane's last report &ndash; at sundown- the enemy had shifted about 4 miles further than Bir el Abd where we had proposed to surprise them, the result being that we came on them half an hour too</p>
