<p>Very dreary marching across sand.  Accompanied by camel train of 70 camels with provisions.  Had tea 5 pm, bully beef &amp; biscuits.</p>
<p>Monday 20/12/15<br/>In the desert, not a tree or growth of any description to be seen.  Infantry trench digging &amp; constructing wire entanglements.  Meals still of bully beef &amp; biscuits.  Easy day, lying about the most of the day.  Few dressings to do. caused while opening tins of bully beef.</p>
<p>Tuesday 21/12/15<br/>Arose 6.30 A.M. breakfast 8.30 AM.  Very busy day, making dug out &amp; communication</p>
