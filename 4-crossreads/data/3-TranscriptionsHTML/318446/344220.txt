<p>Of prisoners we took 1436 &amp; it is estimated that there were between two &amp; three hundred left dead upon the field &amp; well over 100 wounded.  In fact we accounted for about 2000.<br/>Also we got a battery of mountain guns &ndash; 10 pdrs. &amp; several Emma gees.  It transpires that the force of turks reinforcing though only three miles off at 4 p.m. never came much nearer &amp; a squadron of the 8th Reg. patrolling on the 10th met some Turkish Cavalry &amp; Camelry &amp; scrapped &amp; took 14 prisoners with no casualties.</p>
