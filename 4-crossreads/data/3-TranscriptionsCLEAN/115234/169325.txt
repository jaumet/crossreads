 In a flowerbordered six feet of earth, surmounted by a simple white wooden cross, the famous Irish Patriot, politician & soldier, sleeps his last long sleep.  His dramatic last appearance in the House of Commons & fervid plea for all parties in Ireland to sink their differences & work in Unity & Goodwill, lent, to his death, in actions, a few days afterwards, a glamour that has made a great impression  in  on the minds of all Classes in the British Isles.
