 20/7/16 (later) Thursday &amp; Friday 
 I do not know yet whether I am on my head or heels &ndash; but one thing seems to strike me most forcibly &ndash; some good friend of mine &ndash; must have prayed hard &amp; recently for me. For to have come through safely the last two days &amp; nights &ndash; is I take it a good answer to my friends. I really do not know what to say &ndash; but just Hell let loose in its worse form. Shrapnel &ndash; Shell &ndash; Bombs &ndash; Bullets. Deafening &amp; Terrifying &ndash; When ones nerves are good &amp; not carried away but resigned there is a sort of indescribable fascination, &amp; one thinks I've missed that &ndash; but the next may get me. At time of writing 8 pm, a lull. I've had 4 hours sleep out of the 48 hrs &ndash; hurried &amp; not too good snacks as opportunity occurred.
