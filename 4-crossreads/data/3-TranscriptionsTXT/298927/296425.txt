 23rd  Bull ring has begun for those who have not seen active Service. 
 25th  Went to Corfe Castle, a fair sized village about 4&frac12; miles from Wareham, the main features of which are the Castle and the church.  The Church has been rebuilt (1860) but the tower dates back to 1260.
