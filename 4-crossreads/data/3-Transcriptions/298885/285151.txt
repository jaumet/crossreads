<p>June 23rd &ndash; 17<br/>Left Bavilincourt at 7AM per bike proceeded to Beugny arriving here at 12.15PM. At this spot we are a couple of miles behind our old position [referring to V&eacute;lu] about one of the quietist places we have been in as regards shells the majority being aeroplane shells the result of a number of air-duels.</p>
<p>July 7th &ndash; 17<br/>Left Beugny at 8AM and proceeded to Meaulte per motor arriving at 11AM. Here we are staying</p>
