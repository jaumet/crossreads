<p>and then knocked off, which gave me to think furiously, for, with this gun shelling from Shunet Nimrin which is just under the hills on the opposite side of the valley, the turk could make us very uncomfortable indeed as we would be under direct fire from direct observation &amp; absolutely no cover.</p>
<p>And also with Jane shelling from the N. &amp; this gun from the E. our lot would be unenviable.  So every night I have retired to my downy couch with the possible contingency of an already repulsive breakfast of cold greasy bacon rendered even more so by the advent of intermittent shells in addition.</p>
