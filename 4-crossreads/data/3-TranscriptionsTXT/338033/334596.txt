 To Billingsgate fish markets. Worth several million, and are well guarded by veterans of olden wars. They wear the rather gay and queer uniforms of Henry VIII time, and every one of them have at least on row of ribbons on their left breast. From hear we entered the gunnery a very interesting place indeed. Guns, rifles trench mortars, bombs, lancers spikes stinkpots and many other kinds of weapons used in the wars, for centuries past.
