 Tuesday 25/4/16 Anniversary of landing at Anzac; recognised as a holiday by all Australian troops in Egypt.  Most enjoyable day.  Sports arranged for the afternoon &amp; with the assistance of the Officers, were carried out without a hitch, every item being keenly contested.
