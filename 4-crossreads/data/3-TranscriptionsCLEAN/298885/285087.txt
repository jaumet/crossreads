 Wed Aug 16th - 16 I am detailed as orderly for an indefinite time between Bde HQ Qtrs and Div Hd Qtrs. 
 Thurs  Aug 17th - 16 Great excitement this morning when a taube set on fire by one of our planes, came down about 500 yards away. 
 Fri Aug 18th - 16 Last night our boys made another raid and were very successful bringing back a good number of prisoners.
