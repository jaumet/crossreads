<p>26 June, Thursday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I begain work again in earnest &amp; the boat's rolling makes me &amp; my tried before I am finished my work. Work all afternoon. I go to Poetry lesson. Tell Sister to go to B &amp; get on B-------. I try to write to Auntie but I am none too bright so I write 3 pages &amp; get off to bed in Hospital on seat.</p>
<p>27 June, Friday</p>
<p>W.<br/>I watch a gale blowing up. We get it very windy but choppy &amp; swell very slight. I get very tired hopping about all the same. I go to Book Keeping but Branch accounts are tricky.</p>
<p>Gale blowing worse. I go to bed in top bunk in Hosp.</p>
