<p>Sunday 2 March 1919<br/>Cairo<br/>Actually travelling to Cairo in charge of Anzac leave Party &amp; to remain one week &amp; buy comforts for voyage &amp; write up War History &ndash; left kit at Kantara &ndash; could not sent a Civil telegram so sent an Army one as follows, "Stralis Cairo, arriving Sunday midnight advise other Unit," meaning Dolly.<br/>Found all well &amp; child grown.</p>
