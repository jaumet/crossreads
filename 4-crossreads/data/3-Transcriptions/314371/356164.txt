<p>Nov. 1917<br/>No. 24 Gen. Hosp.<br/>Britain<br/>From the woman who waits with a wistful smile to the man in the listening post.<br/>From the war worn workers at bench &amp; lathe, to the watch dogs round the coast.<br/>All are as one in the work in hand &ndash; Christian, Mohamedan, Jew.<br/>List to the message that fills the land &ndash; "Britain will see it through".</p>
