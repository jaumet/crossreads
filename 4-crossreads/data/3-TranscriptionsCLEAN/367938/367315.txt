 Send an envelope addressed clearly & fully to you each time you honour me or I shall be forced to use the post cards that give so much information. - - - - - 
 I shall never see my Burbery again - its on my saddle at Alexandria with the horses & its too useful for anyone to pass - also the jack shay - The heavy coat is useless here but will come into use next winter, if we can see through until then. Tobacco & matches are served out - A visitor in the shape of Padre Plane has just climbed up, so so long for the present -
