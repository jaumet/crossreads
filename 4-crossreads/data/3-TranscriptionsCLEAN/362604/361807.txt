  
 France 12/12/18 
 Dear Dad, Mum & George, 
 Just a few lines in answer to your letters of 29.9.18.  So, Tommy Adams got back to Aussie.  He was working for Ellis & I but was sent away to France when all the A. Class men were sent away.  I don't know how some manage to do it.  You mention 8 Hours Day.  I remember some of them as a Child & remember other striking events of later years.
