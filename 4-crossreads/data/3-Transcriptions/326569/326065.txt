<p>&amp; rifle exercises.  After tea went down town &amp; met Taylor with whom had a drink.  Went on Road picquet at 7.30 p.m. &amp; went off 9.45 p.m.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 5/6/17<br/>Threw more live bombs in morning &amp; drill with box respirators.  Received letters from Guild &amp; Uncle Fred.  Evening took stroll down the town.</p>
<p>Wednesday, 6/6/17<br/>Fine sunshine.  Wrote home.  After tea went down town.</p>
<p>Thursday, 7/6/17<br/>Rather warm today.  In morning went through real gas.  One chap outed.  After tea went down town.  Wrote letter to Guild.</p>
