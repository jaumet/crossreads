Went for drugs today to "Puchevillers" a new place to me being in the 5th Army area to which we now belong, distance about 20 kilos &amp; road not of the best. Heavy fog most of the day. At last the lack of supplies in drugs has been settled &amp; I now have a large stock of ointment of which we are using a large quantity.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20th.<br/>Nothing but rain all day so rested my foot which is not very bad altho' gave me a bad time during the night.</p>
