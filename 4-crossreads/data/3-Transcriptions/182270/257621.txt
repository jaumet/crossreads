
<p>WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 5, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; windy. Lopped in the Middle</p>
<p>paddock and then shifted the ewes</p>
<p>&amp; lambs to Top paddock and</p>
<p>lopped there for them. Lopped in</p>
<p>Boundary in afternoon, &amp; round</p>
<p>Cow paddock. Tickle at the fence</p>
<p>in afternoon, in Top paddock.</p>
<p>Dad &amp; Peter went to Quirindi.</p>
