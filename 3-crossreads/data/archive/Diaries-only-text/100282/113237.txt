Slipped down to Victoria by the tube then walked round to Peel House had tea, here I met a S.African Sgt born of Dutch parents but he could talk quite good English, soon we were yarning away like one thing. I wanted to know all about S.Africa he Australia, afterwards we went to the Hippodrome to see the popular revue Zig-Zag, George Robey Shirley Kellog were playing in the cast, but I did not care a great deal for it, but the scenes dresses were beautiful.
