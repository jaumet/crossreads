<p>Tuesday 1st January 1918<br/>New Years Day on Guard not thrilling Just enough snow for snowballing &amp; some of the lads had a great time potting every one who came along the road. Old ones got anoyed &amp; of course got a rough time</p>
<p>Wednesday 2nd<br/>Put on a marking party for the Butts a cold job stuck down in a trench all day pulling targets up &amp; down Harie Higgerly for a mate so am not dull</p>
<p>Thursday 3rd<br/>On the Butts again. Hall has the next target We backed our score against his for suppers &amp; won</p>
<p>Friday 4th<br/>Butts won another supper</p>
<p>Saturday<br/>Butts Supper again fell to us<br/>Went to Warminster Banked &pound; 5. Took Hall to the Pictures in return for all the suppers we have had out of him. We get the suppers at the Canteen quite a feed for about 10d</p>
