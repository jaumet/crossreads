Personal Diary from Reveille on date of Embarkation 12-5-1915.
May 12th.
Reveille 5 am. Bfast 6.30, Parade 7.15. Left Camp 9 am. Sydney 10.30 marched to Wharf via Park St. Wharf 11.30. On deck 12.10 left 12.20. Anchored between Cremorne Garden Island Good dinner then, and had afternoon to look over boat, weighed anchor 4.45 and passed out Heads 5 pm. Tea immediately after then issue of bedding. Lovely day night sorting things together and retired 9 p.m.
