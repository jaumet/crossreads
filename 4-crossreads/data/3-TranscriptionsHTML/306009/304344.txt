<p>1915<br/>June 7 Mon.<br/>Rather busy to-day but found time to write half a dozen letters after tea.<br/>Mena is closing down &amp; transferred 65 remaining patients to Gezira to-day.<br/>We also received 30 wounded from Ambulance Train &amp; are to <strike>receive</strike> get another 50 at midnight.</p>
