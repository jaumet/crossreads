<p>I was put in charge of evacuations at night and had a fairly busy time but had a man to show me the ropes. Had a good supper at midnight in the Cookhouse my friend being good with the Cook. It rained during our march down &amp; made things very uncomfortable &amp; cold &amp; may interfere with the stunt which is on. They hopped the bags this morning &amp; reports are very glowing.</p>
<p>Friday night &amp; Saturday morning 5th &amp; 6th  Slept all day Friday &amp; slept well &amp; started again at 8 P.M. but found practically nothing to do.
