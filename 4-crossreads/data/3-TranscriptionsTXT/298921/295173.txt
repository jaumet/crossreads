 July, 1915 Wednesday 21 More of my Reinforcements have drifted back shot through legs &amp; eyes etc. &amp; I am here fit and well poor chaps I do what I can in writing letters etc:- &amp; only regret I was not with them in the field &ndash; no doubt our time will come  Will post this tonight love to all, your son
