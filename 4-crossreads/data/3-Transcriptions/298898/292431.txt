<p>Ramsay off after Supper.</p>
<p>Friday 12th Jan<br/>Went down to Horseferry Road &amp; managed to get an extension of leave for 2 days which made a big difference &amp; feeling happy I had a long walk along Piccadilly. Went back to Hotel &amp; sent off photos &amp; postcards of France also a nose cap I brought from Delville Wood &amp; some books of views of London. At night we went to "Vanity Fair" a real good revue &amp; the best I had seen so far. Had photo taken at Histeds.</p>
<p>Saturday 13th Jan.<br/>Caught the 9.50 A.M. to Cambridge in order to see H.B. Carr a friend who was in a Cadet Battalion training for officers. Had lunch in first rate style at the University Hotel commencing with whisky &amp; soda &amp; finished with coffee &amp; liquers.
