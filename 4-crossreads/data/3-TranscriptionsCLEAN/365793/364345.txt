
Aug 31 along the wharf, fishing for their tea, & they seemed to be catching some too.Lost sight of Table Mtn 5 hrs out (60 miles). -Rec'd 2 to 4 oranges each which means finish buksheesh I suppose. Still in sight of land at 10 oclockSeveral Hospital cases came aboard this mn'g - chaps who, enroute to England were put off at the Cape with Meninghitis, brain-fever etc.
Sptr 1 Dull & cold. Going fairly well, but believe the coal isn't much chop. Did 302 yesterday & both sea & wind are with us today. Bough a 25
