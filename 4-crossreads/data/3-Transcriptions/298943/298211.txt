<p>The first clash I have had with Sam Rickson came yesterday afternoon, &amp; I spoke bluntly, to the point, &amp; a trifle sharply. Either from the mistaken notion that I was trying to snub him, or from disgust, he left before the chat I was having with Harry Melrose (in which he had joined) was finished -----</p>
