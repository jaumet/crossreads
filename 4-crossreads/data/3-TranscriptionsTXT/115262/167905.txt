 Friday 18 May 1917. 
 No sight of land yet. Right up close to &quot;Encounter&quot; (cruiser) this morning. Rough at first but not quite so later. 
 I finish up my mess orderly duties with G.S.S.Crmn&nbsp;today, for a month or so. 
 Expect to. sight land tomorrow&nbsp;evening.&nbsp; 
 Still in a northerlyly direction as on Thursday 17&nbsp; th&nbsp; inst 
 Saw with 2 of the boys a&nbsp; &quot;transparent&quot;&nbsp; &nbsp;&quot;flying fish&quot;. An uncommon sight so far south in a cold climate.
