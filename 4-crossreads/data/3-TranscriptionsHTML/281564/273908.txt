<p>In the afternoon we were issued with new uniforms</p>
<p>[Drawing of man in ill fitting suit]</p>
<p>They looked rather like this, but were most acceptable up here when comfits are few.<br/>During the night we gave a fine fireworks display.<br/>The "Lili" returned with a new boat. They had some excitement with the cannibals.</p>
<p>Sunday 8th Nov<br/>Caught the fever &amp; it lasted until Monday 16th Nov when I came out of hospital</p>
