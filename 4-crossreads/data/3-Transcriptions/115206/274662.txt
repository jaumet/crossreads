<p>April 1918<br/>Tuesday 2<br/>In Billet &ndash; Orville<br/>Bombed during early hours of morning. No casualties. <br/>A great number of R.A.M.C. arrived enroute to line, from Italy.<br/>During afternoon I performed the erroneous duty of Marker on Battery manoeuvres.<br/>Weather beautiful.<br/>Had a swim above the wier with Russell.<br/>Went along to Santos with Ken Gunn.<br/>Missed the boys so came home very early.<br/>Met Adranne &ndash; "a bon petite Cherie"</p>
