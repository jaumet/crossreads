<p>a few points about driving</p>
<p>Saturday 11.8.17<br/>Full day route march to Arques &amp; back then on through Racquinghem &amp; home about 4.30 pm.</p>
<p>Sunday 12.8.17<br/>Leave to St Omer, spent rather a good day, town crowded, band played in the park during afternoon; returned via Ronesque</p>
<p>Monday 13.8.17<br/>Second Anniversary of formation of unit.  Sports programme carried through &amp; concert at night;  Leave warrant for Blighty came through.  Rode to Blaringhem to endeavour to get a new rig out but failed, given authority to get same at Horseferry road London.<br/>Leave to-morrow.</p>
