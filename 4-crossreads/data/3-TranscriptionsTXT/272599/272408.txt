 1917 Friday 18 May 
 Jock, Mac &amp; I go for a walk in afternoon to Souverain Moulin a most enchanting time  The beautiful hills, fields &amp; all loveliness. Wimille. Snapshots. Cafe &amp; inquisitive Sister. Eggs &amp; chips.  Chateau. Mac's in rhapsodies. Jockeys broken wind. Our search for Happy Valley. Home at 9 p.m.
