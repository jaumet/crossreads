 May, 1915 Sunday 30 I will enclose a letter from one of our Sergeants &ndash; Poor old Man a D.C.M. man was one of my best reinforcements &amp; gave me his medals &amp; papers the night he left &ndash; I hope you are all well &amp; this goes along safely Love to all Frank As Sgt Lovetts letter has passed Censor it should go on to you it &amp; I cannot be blamed for writing War news "comprenez"
