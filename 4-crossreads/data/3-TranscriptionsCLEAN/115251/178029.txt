 At 8-10 p.m. on 9th October following message was received from Navy, Melbourne:- 
 "Thursday Island heard signals in German plain language, 4-45 am, 7th Octr., reporting that "Australia" and all large Men-o'-war had left Rabaul going Eastward.  Did you hear this signal and can you estimate where it was made from?. 
 Reply despatched at 9-30 p.m. 
 "Message not intercepted here;  Station believed to be working Tawanakus Bay.  Expedition has been despatched."
