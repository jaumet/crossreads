<p>Thursday 20 April 1916<br/>Hal &amp; I up early caught 8 o'clock train to Liverpool and received &pound;11 back pay.<br/>Had dinner at Sargents and then went out to show.  Met Alan &amp; Father &amp; Mother.  In evening we all went to pantomime "Mother Goose".  Bought camera.</p>
<p>Friday 21 April 1916<br/>All of us went to 9-30 a.m. service at St. Chads.<br/>Then took some photos.  Rained pretty hard in afternoon.<br/>Alan &amp; I went round to Heartley Vale and Mr. Sams came round in evening.</p>
