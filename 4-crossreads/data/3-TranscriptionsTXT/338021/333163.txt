 Situation Report 13-10-17 
 Everything nearly O.K. S.A.A expended Nil Change of Personnel Nil Casualties. Pte Ripps sick &amp; T. feet " Cox shell shock " Lewis "  " wounded " Watson wounded 
 Very heavily shelled all night all calibres still going yet. Am getting up all that bulk S.A.A. to dump near road. Men rather shaken &amp; would like rum ration. Lt Cowley reported I have sent for Mitchell. I have been in agony all night with feet, little better this morning after rubbing will try &amp; carry on here. Have plenty of Rations, not much water. Can I tell the men when they are being returned to buck them up 
 PHS Weston Lt 5.30am
