<p>9<br/>me to believe that we are close to the first of a series of peace offers which will become nearer &amp; nearer to our terms. Once it is clear to him that he cannot win &amp; that one day &ndash; no matter how far distant &ndash; he will surely lose, we should be able to look confidently for his overtures.</p>
<p>I fear that in my reference to our men here I do them far less than justice. Coming as I did from the shambles before Ypres &amp; finding the Light-Horse more or less in rest in the [prime] of the year I was unfairly biassed. Since then I have seen a lot of their life &amp; fighting &amp; am able to get a good sense of what they endured</p>
