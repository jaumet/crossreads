
Sunday 14 July 1918Had a lazy day & read & wrote.  Good Y.M.C.A. run by English ladies, this used to be the Vicarage.
Monday 15 July 1918Paraded sick about the teeth.  Wandsworth didn't finish.  They tried to put me on draft conductg. but I got out of it!!!
Tuesday 16 July 1918Plenty of rain.  Usual parades etc.  Fixed up with Dentist for next Monday.  Revolver shootg.
Wednesday 17 July 1918Think Sutton Veny is one of the best camps I have been in for accommodation.
Thursday 18 July 1918Went to Salisbury & Fovant.  Saw Matron Lamming.  Major Simpson was out so left a note.
Friday 19 July 1918Rained all day & we did live bombing in the middle of it!!  Got off early.
Saturday 20 July 1918Advance Guard stunt in morng.  Went shootg. in afternoon & got ptridge.  marker.Sister BirtEde
