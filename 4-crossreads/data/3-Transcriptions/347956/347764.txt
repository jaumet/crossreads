<p>Nov. 3 &ndash; 4 - 5</p>
<p>Trop ships from Freemantle joined us, <strike>also other boats. two of our escorts returned to Australia</strike> a mistake.  a concert after tea (37 troop ships now &amp; 4 cruisers <strike>&amp; a battleship</strike> are with us.</p>
<p>Wed 4th<br/>Had a very rough sea last night. the mugs &amp; dishes were racing each other up &amp; down the table all night; I was on duty from 4 AM. till 8 AM. on the bridge  Semaphore practice morning.  saw flying fish by the dozen yesterday another horse died.  teaching troops semaphore afternoon.  had a lecture on scouting after tea</p>
<p>Thurs 5th<br/>two more horses died  steering 30 deg west of north.  it is rather warm to be comfortable on duty on the bridge from 6 till 8 PM. &amp; from 12 till 4 AM.  The Osterley passed us (going along</p>
