<p>Friday 23 March 1917</p>
<p>Letter from ISHERWOOD my brother in law who is at SALONIKA. Good news on all fronts, I anticipate a resolution in Germany before it is all over.</p>
<p>ENEMY aeroplanes over again this morning, but they did not drop any bombs.</p>
<p>Yesterday the Races were held at Rafa.</p>
<p>Anzac mounted Division won 5 out of 7 Races. I did not go &ndash; the Races were sort of Public &amp; a Totalisator was worked.</p>
<p>Also heard our Division enters GAZA tomorrow. Just had a bathe &amp; am now Field Officer for the next 24 hours.</p>
