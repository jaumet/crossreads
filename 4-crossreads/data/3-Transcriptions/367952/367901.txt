<p>Transcribers note:  Written on Letterhead of Australian Imperial Force, Records Section. Westminster London</p>
<p>Quote: RED/10070/21/1.</p>
<p>29th November, 1918</p>
<p>No. 7040 Pte D.F. Campbell,<br/>18th Bn. Aust. Imp. Force.<br/>France</p>
<p>3770 Pte T. Bell 20th Bn, A.I.F.<br/>Deceased.</p>
<p>In reply to your letter of 7th instant which was forwarded to this office by Chaplain W.E. Burkett, 33rd Bn. Regarding your Uncle. The above-mentioned soldier you are advised that records show him to have been Killed in Action 8.4.18.</p>
<p>Burial particulars are not yet available, but enquries have been instituted and immediately additional information is forthcoming you will be again communicated with.</p>
<p>Signed:   [indecipherable]<br/>For Officer i/c Records.</p>
