  
 [Note paper with Union Line in Red lettering and a coloured photo of a passenger liner on the sea] T.S.S. Moeraki Wellington 19/2/18 
 My dear John, 
 My love to dear Anna  &  to  your children "large & small". This is a line to let you know that after a [indecipherable] joyable voyage, I am safely arrived in Port Nicholson. The "Moeraki" had a tempestuous passage of 10 1/2 hours from Newcastle on  Monday  Wednes. night 13th inst. She, therefore, was late in leaving Sydney.
