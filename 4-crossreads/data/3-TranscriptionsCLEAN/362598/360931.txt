  
 Sunday 25 June 1916 Wrote Lyd. Goddard & Doris Blackwell.  Matron, 2 men probable enteric.  Ellis & Earn go to Band.  Dave & Billy go to the Kirk.  Night Ward Master. 
 Monday 26 June 1916 Wrote to Mum & George 46-47, Dad 45.  Went to Heliopolis with Q.M.S. Williams.  Saw Pictures & ate Peanuts.  All the other Boys stayed at Home. 
 Tuesday 27 June 1916 Dreamt of Nell & Fanny.  Can't remember anything very startling. 
 Wednesday 28 June 1916 Dreamt again being at Church with Mum.  Played Cards a lot. 
 Thursday 29 June 1916 Wrote to Bess.  Met Dick Rowe.  Remount Unit give concert. 
 Friday 30 June 1916 All day leave.  Saw all the Boys off to Aust. & spent day at Giza & Zoo.  Dave, Bill & I played Billiards & I won one game.  Dreamt of Jess, mother again. 
 Saturday 1 July 1916 Wrote Home (48), Madge, Mrs. Whitehead, Nell, Cuneo.  In charge of Sydney Pavilion & put Patient Blackwell into Mel. 8.
