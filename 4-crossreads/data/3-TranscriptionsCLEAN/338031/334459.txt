  
 October 27 The men by now were settled down to the sea life. Physical drill & fatigues absorbed a certain amount of energy & there were great gatherings of a game of chance called "House", & the Liverpool seamen from the foc's'le who at first conducted the game must have made an enormous pile out of the profit per game, which is simple & lasts only a few minutes.
