
<p>What the music meant.</p>
<p>Hunger - and night - &amp; the stars:&nbsp; Then on a sudden the music changed, so soft that you scarce could hear, but you felt that your life had been looted clean of all that it once held dear&nbsp; That someone had stolen the woman you loved, that her love was a devil&#39;s lie&nbsp; That your guts were gone&nbsp; and the best for you was to crawl away a die.&nbsp; Twas the crowning</p>
