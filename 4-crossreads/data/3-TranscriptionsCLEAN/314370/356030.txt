
May, 1916Sunday 21Got up when the breakfast bugle went.After breakfast I went for a walk to the old Helmia camp. It is like a city of the dead.After tea I went for a long walk round Matariah in the cool.Write letters home
Monday 22We were granted leave again today & after dinner I went out to Patti's & made the acquaintance of their son who returned from the Italian front on Thursday last
