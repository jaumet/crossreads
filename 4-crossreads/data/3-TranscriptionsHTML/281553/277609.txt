<p><strike>Jan. 12th Wednesday</strike><br/>Jan. 9th Sunday<br/>Dissembarked Alexandria at 8-15 a.m.  Marched down line to Timber works where we waited train till 12-40 p.m.  Y.M.C.A. on Stn. &amp; plenty of fruit &amp; native dealers about.  Nile much flooded.  Reached Zagazig 6 p.m. and Tel el Kebir about 8-15 p.m., our camp site some &frac14; hour away where we rec'd tea all roung [round ?] &amp; then pitched tents getting to bed 10 p.m.</p>
<p>Monday, Jan. 10th<br/>Pitched tents properly in Regimental order</p>
