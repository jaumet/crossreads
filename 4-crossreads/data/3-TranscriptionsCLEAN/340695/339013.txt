Mademoiselle Germaine was very good to me she nursed a nasty boil on my neck and if I had been her brother she could not have done more. 20-11-17. We are well set here for a few days our Interpreter fixed Coog & I up for a bed at the village school. It is a lovely room with 2 single beds spotlessly clean put in a very good night's sleep. The apples here are bon am making a glutton of myself. It is very pretty country about here. Crecy is a quaint old village I thought of the old time wars as I passed through & thought how different things are now a days. 23-11-17 Moved from MACHY to GAMACHES
