<p>24-2-19 (Thur)<br/>Still engaged on the tent on the hill &amp; have not finished putting the floor down by midday. By which time I have handed over the job to the Engineers.</p>
<p>I am on the draught which goes into Camp this afternoon at 3 pm. On reporting I find Capt Seaton (43) is my new OC &amp; Tierney our adjutant. Col Farrell is in command. The draught comprises all states &amp; will most assuredly be split up on arrival in England.</p>
<p>On returning to the mess for dinner I find the place flooded with a party of French ladies from Fenquieres to whom I am presented but whose acquaintance I could have made in Fenquieres had I desired but it is too late now to do it</p>
<p>25-4-19 (Frid)<br/>Anzac Day &ndash; a commemoration service is held in hollow near the draught orderly room &amp; after that we have the sports.
