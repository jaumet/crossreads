 February 1918 Monday 11 
 Messines Raid last night on Fritz's strong post. Captured 25 Fritzies. Casual. 8 killed and 15 wounded. Some fellows of the 31st Batt took part. We supported with the Barrage. Walked across to Lamp Post Corner to see Gun, and brought back water. Da Costa arrived at S.O.S. gun. I am for O.P. tomorrow. Mills on laison. Alby on F.O.O.today.  Weather unsettled
