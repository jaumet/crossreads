<p>September 1915</p>
<p>Saturday 4</p>
<p>praises the education system here.  He advised me to get a billet teaching English after the war is over.</p>
<p>It is worth thinking over at any rate for the wages are good.</p>
<p>The educated class here is in great evidence.</p>
<p>In the native quarters one sees squalor in its worst form.</p>
<p>Houses built of yellow mud looking stuff, crumbling to pieces from each other across a street of a few feet wide.</p>
<p>Filthy with black piccaninies play about the middle of the alley way &amp; dirty natives in multi coloured dress lounge about or go about their occupations.</p>
