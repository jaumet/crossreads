<p>July 7th<br/>Last Day at Bailleul &ndash; preparation for departure.</p>
<p>July 8th<br/>We leave for Unknown destination &ndash; After 7 kilos we pass through Strazeelle &amp;amp; thence on to Borre where we camp for night in barn. Very fine country (Hops, wheat, Vineyards etc)<br/>Visit to church (1626) &ndash; Our billet originally in hands of British in a skirmish with Germans who occupied another farmhouse about 700 yards away &ndash; Germans driven out by Cavalry. Farmhouse shows signs of many bullet holes.</p>
<p>July 9th<br/>Glorious day for second stage of march &ndash; We leave Borre at Midday and after a march of 10 miles arrive at Ebblinghem &ndash; Pass the night in a huge paddock</p>
<p>July 10th<br/>Our billet extra good &ndash; Country farmyard. During the day "B" &amp;amp; "A" section play one another football.</p>
