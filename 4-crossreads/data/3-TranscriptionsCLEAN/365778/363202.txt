  
 Wednesday 20/6/17 Wrote to the General, raining Field day postponed. Thursday 21/6/17 Raining, went to see Corps Line, had dinner with 17th then visited the nurses at 3rd C.C.S. [Casualty Clearing Station] Friday 22/6/17 Field Day carried out, raining heavily. Saturday 23/6/17 Remained in bed all day with a touch of Malaria. Runner shot at night. Sunday 24/6/17 Church Parade, played Officers match against 28th cricket, we won, made a score.
