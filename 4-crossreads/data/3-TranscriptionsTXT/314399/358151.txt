 Tuesday 3 June 1919 Went to Garage &amp; did a bit of work. Bill &amp; I had dinner together &amp; then went to Hyde Park for a walk. Came home early &amp; read. 
 Wednesday 4 June 1919 Derby Day 
 Rain today. Went to the garage for an hour &amp; then to Reading. Had a good time. Went to Sonning. Walked of course. The Derby provided a huge surprise for everybody. Grand Parade won when everyone thought The Panther would.
