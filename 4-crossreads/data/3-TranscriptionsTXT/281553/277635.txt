
Diary of Camp Work.
[The following entries for 1st and 2nd August crossed through.]
Aug. 1stArrived home from all night manoeuvre about 2nd Tower.  Rested all day, letter writing evening.
Aug. 2ndLost a/c from 27/6/16 to Aug. 11th.
August 11thReveille 4 a.m.  Marched through to Citadel, stayed all day, left 5 p.m., arriving Heliopolis 8-45 p.m.
Aug. 12Bayonet sharpened.
Aug. 25Packing all day.  Left Camp (Heliopolis) 7 p.m.  Marched to Zeitoun thence by train to Alexandria, detrained
