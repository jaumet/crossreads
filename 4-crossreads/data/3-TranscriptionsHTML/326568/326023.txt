<p>June.<br/>7th.  After a very quick run arrive off Port Phillip heads &amp; anchor of Quarantine Station.<br/>8th.  Move up to port Melbourne &amp; after disembarking troops for that port, we had leave for a few hours.<br/>Also take on board troops for north, from Boonah.<br/>9th.  Leave Melbourne at 7 a.m.</p>
