  
 Friday 2/11/17  Left at 5.30 a.m for Camiers for musketry demonstration with Paton & Miles  returned at 11.30 p.m stayed night at Division 
 Saturday 3/11/17. Joined Battalion, just relieved from line, at Walker Camp, some of the men slightly gassed. Two letters from Doff. Wrote to Doff. sent Cable to Billie. 
 Sunday 4/11/17  Quiet day- saw Wisdom  had a game of cards. 
 Monday 5/11/17  Very quiet day  raining slightly- men resting. Night raid by Huns very close.
