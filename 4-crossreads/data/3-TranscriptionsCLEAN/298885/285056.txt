  
 Thur. Mar. 30th -16 Orders came through at 4 o'clock to be prepared for 3 days train trip to Le Havre and at 5 P.M. the dismounted party left camp and after 2 tram trips arrived at station only to find train left at 9 next morning.  We slept overnight in trucks. 
 Fri. Mar. 31st -16 9 A.M.  Horses and men entrained and ordered off on a run of 854 miles.  Dismounted party very comfortable.  The trip is all that could be wished for.  The scenery through Southern France is very pretty indeed.  Habitation all along the route.
