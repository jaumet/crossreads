<p>Afternoon spent with George Martin and evening (including supper) with Harry &ndash; I write a sketch (or is it an apology) entitled "Failed to Start"</p>
<p>Jan 20th<br/>Sabbath &ndash; Pay day &ndash; Reading &amp; Writing &ndash; We journey in the evening to Steenge (?) where we give a show to 27th Batt. (3rd Div. Artillery) arriving back at Ballieul late in the night.</p>
<p>Jan.21st.<br/>Lloyd George says "Fight On! &ndash; Change of Programme &ndash; Letter to M.B. "Les Miserables"- Fred Reade in Paris.</p>
