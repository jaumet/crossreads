<p>13th May (Saturday) Extremely hot. Marched from standing camp to standing camp, changing places with the 14th Field Amb. We were like drowned rats when we reached Ferry Post.</p>
<p>14th May (Sunday) Still hot. No Church Parade. Building stone incinerator.</p>
<p>15th May (Monday) Putting up flag pole in morning. Fearfully hot. Aeroplane flew over camp. Piquet at night. The hours we are now to have are as follows:- Breakfast 4.30  Parade 5.15 am to 8.45 am &ndash; Morning tea 10.30 am. Afternoon tea 3.30 pm</p>
