Jan 23 (Monday)Yesterday Sunday was on outpost duty all day in a Sangar (Block house) just a round wall of stone surmounted with sandbags & loopholes surrounded by barb wire. There were 3 of us & a Cap Corporal also two Signallers of the Royal Scots (who hailed from Manchester bye the bye) these two were in charge of the telegraph line & keyboard which connected us up with the Headquarters - we were on the left flank of the ring of hills which surround the camp
