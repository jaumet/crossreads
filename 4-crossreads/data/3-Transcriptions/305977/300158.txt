<p>The weather makes you very tired &amp; it is an effort to do anything.</p>
<p>Sunday 10th [Jan]<br/>Clear fine day strong wind blowing.  Getting pretty near the line.  One of the</p>
<p>German boats has dropped back out of sight &amp; the Persic is also hull down.  Had the usual sermon.  Cat Chaplain Miller preached one of his usual sermons. Afterwards we fell in for boat &amp; fire stations.  No parade between 12.30 &amp; 4pm.  Got in a good sleep.</p>
<p>Monday 11th<br/>Crossed the line this morning.  Had a pretty busy day on stables &amp; felt pretty tired at night.  Slept on deck but the rain drove me down.  Another horse was put overboard making a total of 8.  Loss was to A Troop B Squadron.</p>
<p>Tuesday 12th<br/>In the morning the "Ceramic"</p>
