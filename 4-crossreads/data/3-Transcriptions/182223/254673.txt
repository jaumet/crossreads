<p>Monday 21 June&nbsp;1915<br />Working in the office and about the place today.<br />Concert at 8 p.m.<br />Heliopolis at 11 p.m. some roast fowl, potatoes peas. Bananas.&nbsp;<br />Posted letters to The Girls, Mrs Knowles.</p>
<p>Tuesday 22&nbsp;June&nbsp;1915<br />Letters for the Girls (supplementry). Dr Paton, Mr&nbsp;Hennessey, Mollie, Dr Dunn, Clarie Bowen.&nbsp;<br />In Cairo for an hour during the morning.&nbsp;<br />Called see Colonel Barrett at night. Had Supper at Heliopolis hotel at 11-30 p.m.</p>
<p>Wednesday 23&nbsp;June&nbsp;1915<br />Ordinary work for a family of 350 people&nbsp;fairly standard. Six small operations.&nbsp;<br />Surgn Gen Williams called &amp; inspected the hospital, he appeared to be well pleased. Had a whiskey &amp; went away&nbsp;<br />Heliopolis hotel for Supper at 11 p.m.</p>
