September 1917Sunday 9thWent to St Homer [Saint Omer] this evening it is a fine town &amp; some nice people in itMonday 10thWe are having some fine weather &amp; the nobs are very pleased with the way the work is progressingTuesday 11thThe weather keeps fine but nothing much doingWednesday 12thStill carrying onThursday 13thHad another trip to St Homer [Saint Omer] todayFriday 14thWe have most of the stuff from Hazebrouck here now &amp; the buildings are going up wellSaturday 16th   [perhaps this should be 15th.]We shifted to Lumbres to day expect to be here a week &amp; then go to England
