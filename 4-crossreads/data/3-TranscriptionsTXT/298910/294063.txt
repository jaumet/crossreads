 27/9/16  Wednesday 
 Nice day &ndash; Witnessed an escaped observation Balloon from over Fritzs lines, the wind was blowing over our way &ndash; so it went as far as Estaire &amp; was then shot down by our anti aircraft guns. Very quiet in the trenches. 
 28/9/16  Thursday 
 Dull day &ndash; Very quiet &ndash; not disturbed at all last night. Got the news that Combles &amp; Thiepval had fallen &ndash; Tanks doing great work. 
 29/9/16  Friday 
 Raining all day. Very quiet in our line. Fritz very busy putting over Minniewurzen &amp; Shrapnel on Supports &amp; entrance to V.C.S ops. 
 30/9/16  Saturday 
 Raining &ndash; Heavy bombardment &ndash; Made raids on Fritz's trench at night time, very successful &ndash; very few calls &ndash; on N.Z. buried in his dugout. 
 1/10/16  Sunday 
 Nice day &ndash; very quiet. 
 2/10/16  Monday 
 Nice day. Got relieved about 10 am, returned to Nouvea Monde, had the day off.
