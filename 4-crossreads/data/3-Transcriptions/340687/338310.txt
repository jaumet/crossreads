<p>January, 1917. Epiphany. Saturday 6<br/>Slept deeply until 11 a.m. and got breakfast or rather dinner at 12. 2 parcels. Sarah &ndash; unknown. Stayed in bed until 3 when I visited Ludlow Day off to-night and am going out to survey our positions to-morrow morning at 8. Went early to bed and had a good sleep.</p>
