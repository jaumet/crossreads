 The 3rd Brigade is leaving for somewhere to morrow. We shant be long here now. We may go to Englang. France turkey or anywhere at all for all we know. This may be the last letter I shall write from Egypt. we wont be able to write very much after we move from here. 
 The 3rd Engineers came back from the canal yesterday they put in some good work there.
