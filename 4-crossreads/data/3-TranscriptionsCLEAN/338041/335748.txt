  
 -Old friends Cecil Moore & McDade - Letters despatched Home - Still on duty in Tent. 1. - "Aye indeed and 'tis a bloody business - (At least Shakespeare would say so. 
 Nov. 5th Transferred from No.1 to No. 3 operating Tent (Night Duties) - Under our own M.O's. Very much regret leaving Capt. Brown who on my leaving makes a most kind proposal on my behalf - Afternoon free 
 Nov. 6th. Night Duty under Major Brendan & Capt Hamilton - Zeppelin raid 3 miles from camp. Loss of Ammunition. rain and Death of over 200 men in C.C.S. Caused by Zeps bombs - Busy night. 
 Nov. 7th Alick Barr - Seventh Brigade stunt - Death of Ray Walters & other of Sixth F. Amb.
