<p>Situation Report<br/>14/10/17</p>
<p>Everything OK<br/>Casualties Nil<br/>Change of Personnel Nil<br/>S.A.A. expended 3,000</p>
<p>This position is becoming less tenable artillery is just in rear &amp; we are being heavily shelled by howitzers, 5.9 &amp; shrapnel. enemy aircraft all out on its own. Can you give me any idea what time tomorrow relief will take place. Plenty of water, rations, S.A.A.  Rum issue received.<br/>PHS Weston</p>
