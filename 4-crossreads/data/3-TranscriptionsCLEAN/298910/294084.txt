
Becordel where the balance of the Ambulance in conjunction with other ambulances are running a Clearing Station - all necessary & urgent operations are performed here previous to evacuating further on - Arrived here very tired about midday & had more & better tucker today than we have had for the past  6 days. Got a very nice letter from L. Nock which brightened me up a bit, also G. Hegerty.
5/11/16  Sunday
Raining - Breakfast at 7 am, which we all enjoyed - All hands mustered & the Bearers were told to report to Colonel Phipps at the 5th F. Amb who clear all walking cases - about 1/4 of a mile from us - only a rumour there was not a protest, we named every officer & paraded - our W.O. Geo. Black also worked hard to get us a spell - no luck, so we all paraded sick - it was the fun of the world - personally I was supposed to have a very bad cold & a strained tendon of the heel, some of them put up good cases - but it was very noticeable that we were all marked duty - The Officer saw the joke & laughed immensely - at any rate we got off till 2 pm & then had to report - they had the cheek to put us on trying to make the roads around the
