<p>and the Colonies are going to see a lot of faces that never thought of moving out of their native country and familiar grooves.</p>
<p>Auntie Maggie is bent on getting out but it will be hard to shift Uncle Tom. &nbsp;She is one of the Old School, well on in years, conservative and not far-travelled. &nbsp;But Auntie thinks that she would like to be out near Lockhart and would be a companion to Mother. &nbsp;And I agree.</p>
