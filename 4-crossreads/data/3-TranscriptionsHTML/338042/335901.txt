<p>Change of Programme &ndash; First Original Sketch (written by self) and acted by Fred Reade &amp; I, goes most successfully &ndash; Feeling rather pleased with nights performance.<br/>June 27th<br/>Italians still keeping the enemy moving &ndash; Trouble in the German Reichstag &ndash; Peace Talk &ndash; Commence reading "Hiawatha" &ndash; Although a fair way from the Line, we are not quite free from the Bombs of the Gotha &ndash;<br/>It is a nuisance being kept awake so late at night.- What!</p>
