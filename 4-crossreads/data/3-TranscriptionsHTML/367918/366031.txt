<p>[Ailly Le Haut Clocher]<br/>30/10/1919</p>
<p>To Sir Chief Justice Fergusion<br/>Sydney<br/>New South Wales<br/>Australia</p>
<p>I ask of you Sir for a form of Permission for my Marriage in France the reason I ask of you Sir are <u>these</u></p>
<p><u>No. 1</u> I stated my intentions to My Mother some 15 months ago of My Marriage in France Giving full Particulars of Every thing necessary also of My intended Wife and on 3 other occasions I have written but as yet I have received no answer to My letters My fianc&eacute; as also written to My Mother but as not received an answer.</p>
