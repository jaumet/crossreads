<p>June 1918<br/>Thursday 20</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather wet and miserable. orders for Rgt and Centre<br/>Section to pull out at 11.p.m. tonight . <br/>peculiar coincidence that rain always falls when we pull <br/>out.<br/>Gun Adam paid me a visit at W.L. we went for a long<br/>stroll to Bussy, Querrie and Pont Noyelles. Had a very<br/>nice food , and awbon parle.<br/>Grayling, D a Costa left, Charlie Adey has gone to a<br/>school as instructor to a  Tommy School, at Havre</p>
