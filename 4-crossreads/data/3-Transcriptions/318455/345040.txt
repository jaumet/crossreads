<p><strike>&amp; I</strike> This is I suppose the Riviera as we are en route for Nice &amp; Monaco I believe</p>
<p>5 p.m. are halted for an hour at Cannes right alongside the end of the promenade. Some of the men have been having a swim</p>
<p>Sept 30<br/>Passed through Nice, Monaco &amp; Monte Carlo in the evening too much except to see enough to wish it had been daylight. Woke up this morning to find ourselves halted in Savona which we are just leaving <strike>journeyed</strike> along the coast as far as Genoa then we have turned off <strike>in the</strike> at an angle &amp; are now in Novio.
