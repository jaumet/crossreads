 Our battery is situated about half a mile from the village of "Fleubaix" & when I tell you this village contains between four to five hundred civilian population, the greater part are women & children & most of them are Flemish, you will say we are in a very snug corner. Fritz occasionally shells the eastern side of the town, in the vicinity of the church, or what remains of it, all left standing is the great spire & tower, the rest of the building is a heap of ruins,
