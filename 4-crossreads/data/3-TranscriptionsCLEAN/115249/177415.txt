  
 Naval Signal 
 From - Berrima 
 To - Flag 
 Date - 13 9 4 
 Time - 10 30 am 
 From Brigadier - I was informed yesterday that a British General named Wylde was in Rabaul I understand he was travelling in these parts at outbreak of war and was taken prisoner s  by Germans and released on parole - I expect him to call at ship today and will advise you later
