<p>1915<br/>June 5 Sat.<br/>To-day got one of the Patrol Leaders doing scout duty at the Hospital to give me a few points on riding one of our motor bikes. In the space of a few minutes I could start &amp; stop the machine and was soon enjoying my first motor bike ride &ndash; just the thing.</p>
