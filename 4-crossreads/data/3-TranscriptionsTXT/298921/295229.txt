 September, 1915 Wednesday 15  See in Paper you send me of Diary &ndash; "Personal notes" &ndash; Would it not read better if "Censored" was published for all deleted stuff &ndash; sound more interesting anyway!!!!  Went for drive in [indecipherable] to a French Caf&eacute;; voluble, volatile, &amp; versatile. Lager beer (cold boiled egg, cucumber, Tomatoe &amp; bread all served at little table with drink &amp; much "Vive Allies" &ndash; Then threw &frac12; Piastres into Nile for the Arab waifs to fight for. Getting more able each day, but wound still discharging &ndash;  Love to all F.W.
