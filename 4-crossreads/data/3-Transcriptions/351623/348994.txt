<p>4 Oct 16<br/>Left Devonport, exact destination unknown, doing 23 knots</p>
<p>5 Oct 16<br/>Passed Isle of Man (where the cats have no tails) &amp; Ireland &amp; through Firth</p>
<p>6 Oct 16<br/>Arrived at Scapay flow, Scotland. We are now anchored with the First Battle Squadron of British Navy. Coaled ship from Collier Cedar Tree.</p>
<p>7 Oct 16<br/>With fleet. Done sub-calibre firing.</p>
<p>8 Oct 16 )<br/>9 Oct 16 )  With fleet. Weather rough &amp; cold<br/>10 Oct 16 )</p>
<p>11 Oct 16<br/>Done sub-calibre firing.</p>
<p>12 Oct 16<br/>Anchored with fleet</p>
<p>13 Oct 16<br/>Done 6 inch firing in afternoon &amp; sub-calibre firing at night.</p>
