 Sunday 13 July 1919 Dad's Birthday Many Happy Returns old man. Bonny day all day. Wrote a couple of letters. Met Garnsey. only came down last night. Had tea with him. Am set for the 59 quota. Should leave on the Ulysses next week sometime. Went into Warminster for a rise. 
 Monday 14 July 1919 Bit cloudy &amp; dull today. Wrote a few letters. Had tea at the Y.M.
