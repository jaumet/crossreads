 Monday 25/6/17 Night march and assault, returned at 6.30 am, went to Bde in afternoon, saw Bas. Tuesday 26/6/17 Wrote to Doff. Training as usual saw Bas again. Wednesday 27/6/17 Most lovely photo of Doff received, very good indeed. Thursday 28/6/17 Field Firing in morning, raining in afternoon. Friday 29/6/17 Battalion Scheme &amp; inspection in afternoon. Saturday 30/6/17 Raining heavily in afternoon, had a game of Cards.
