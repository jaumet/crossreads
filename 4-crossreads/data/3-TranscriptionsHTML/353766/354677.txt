<p>Captured small NDL steamer "Sumatra". Quite thrilling. Hodgkinson &ndash; boarding officer. Various other small craft taken<br/>Sept. 13 Coaled &amp; oil.<br/>Sunday. Landed 3 p.m. for annexation &amp; hoisting of colours. Never expected to see anything like this. Wonderful. Arranged as follows.<br/>[Transcribers note: a diagram of the public square is included in diary showing the location of various military units in Rabaul's public square during the ceremony for the annexation of German territories]</p>
