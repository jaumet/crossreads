It was done very quietly only a guard of honour from his own unit & the Anglican Chaplain attended to the last offices. The engines stopped for a minute; the little party carried the deceased to the stern; the Union Jack after the Chaplain had read a short service was withdrawn from the shot-weighted white shape which quietly slid along the plank into the depths below.
