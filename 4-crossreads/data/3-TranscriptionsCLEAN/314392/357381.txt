It was 104 degree so he forthwith bundled me into an ambulance which took me to the dressing station at Bailleul. 20th The Doctors here have diagnosed my case as Trench Fever and I am to be sent to the Base. Still feeling fairly rotten. 21st Arrived at No 2  Aust.Gen. Hospital Wimereux just outside Boulogne.
