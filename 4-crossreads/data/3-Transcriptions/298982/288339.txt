<p>Saty 21st Aug. Met Kim Went up to Cantly Gully. Heard Grace was dead. Hard luck. Milk 1/6 tin from Lyons &amp; glad to get it. Snipers had a couple at me in morning. Jack Clarke has got hit in the throat this happened several nights ago Serious but perhaps not dangerous.</p>
<p><u>Posted</u> this week to Mater Eustace &amp; Photo to Mrs Brooks. The nights are getting colder &amp; the days closing in. At midday (?) heard had to be examined re home commission. Dr Mainwaring examined me Lt I believe</p>
