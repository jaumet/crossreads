 Saturday 15 September 1917. 
 On QM&#39;s fatigue today &amp; missed the watering trip to the Waddy. Not sorry either.&nbsp;(One camel having died is struck off the strength.) &quot;Orders&quot;. (What next!!) 
 One taube over this morning. 
 Boys returned from  Marakeb  Malaleh Beach. Went short way &amp; had a good time. Had 24 hrs. on the beach. 
 No Australian mail yet. 
 Wrote 1 letter.
