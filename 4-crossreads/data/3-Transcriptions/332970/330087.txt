The first man in our company to go, passed out yesterday at dinnertime from pneumonia, a very decent chap too, and now everybody seem to have a little thought stowed away somewhere, as to the probability of a good many of us following him within a week or so.</p>
<p>9th Apr 1915 At last there is a date fixed &amp; early on Monday <strike>mornin</strike> Sunday morning, that is the day after to-morrow will see us en route for the war. It seems a time to sort of recapitulate impressions of Heliopolis &amp; Cairo.
