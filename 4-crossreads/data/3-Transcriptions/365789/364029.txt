<p>Commonwealth of Australia.</p>
<p>Governor-General's Office</p>
<p>Melbourne.<br/>28th. March, 1917.</p>
<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>I am desired by His Excellency the Governor-General to transmit to you the following message from Their Majesties The King and Queen:-</p>
<p>"The King and Queen deeply regret the loss you and the Army have sustained by the death of your brother in the service of his country.</p>
<p>Their Majesties truly sympathise with you in your sorrow."</p>
<p>In addition to the above message, I am desired by the Governor-General to express Their Excellencies' heartfelt sympathy with you on the death of this gallant officer, who fell fighting for his Country and Empire.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>George Steward</p>
<p>Official Secretary.</p>
<p>Mrs. W. H. Read,<br/>c/o Mr. A. D. Stobo<br/>Alliance Assurance Company,<br/>Pitt Street,<br/>Sydney. N.S.W.</p>
