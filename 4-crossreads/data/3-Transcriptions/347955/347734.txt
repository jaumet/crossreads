<p>wrote to Bell ang Josie.  Cleaned up rested till 11.30 groomed. and fed had dinner  we have a Canteen here wet and dry Plenty of grapes and wine very nice weather.<br/>Thursday Aug 1.8.18<br/>turned out 6.AM fed up watered horses 8  cleaned up dinner 12.  nothing much doing to day tea 5.  went for a swim<br/>friday Aug 2.8.18<br/>turned out 5.30 groomed and fed up.  things quiet to day nice weather.<br/>Saturday Aug 3.8.18<br/>turned out 6.AM fed up and cleaned lines Breakfast nothing doing<br/>Sunday Aug 4.8.18</p>
