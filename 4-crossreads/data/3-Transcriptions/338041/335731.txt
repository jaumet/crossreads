<p>Sept. 8th.<br/>Special leave to Poperinghe with Roy &amp; Harry. The day occupied in viewing Cathedrals etc &ndash; shopping &amp; visits to Y.M.C.A. &amp; Soldiers Club. Also in having photographs taken &ndash; We spend a most interesting day &ndash; A large mail makes a happy ending.</p>
<p>Sept. 9th, 10th, &amp; 11th.<br/>Route marches &ndash; Infantry drill &ndash; letter writing. (The two sisters)</p>
<p>Sept. 12th<br/>We defeat the 6th F.H. in a Rugby match by 5 to nil &ndash; a good game &ndash; Pay &amp; mail &ndash; Asher Chowne.</p>
<p>Sept. 13th.<br/>A number of us do some hop picking for some Belgian womenfolk -</p>
