
F 15 Sun1530 to 15th. Recd letters from Jennie. E & I go down Town. We meet someone & I come Home by [indecipherable] train early on long write.Write Hil Rayner. Jennie. MRL Aunt Annie. Dad. Gran. Nicholls. Mr F. Mrs Edmonds. Miss Dick
W 16 MonRecd letter from Aunt Annie. I am fed up  & have another [indecipherable].  I swear more than I've sworn for 10 years. Go down & send presents to Auntie. Meet [Sadie?]  & watch [Lena Ashwell's?] Party. See C. C as piano remover. Get [indecipherable] on Best
W 17 Tues.Recd letter from Peg. Doris. Norm. Bess. Nette. Geo. Home. Col. Raffran & Major Macky. Visit flat E & I go to Saadine's flat. Meet [mio?] Chapman off Kanawra. Run for train but have to walk Home. Glorious moonlight night.
W 18 WedChange old parts of tent & Major Cooks for me & finds me. Am Orderly Sergt. Major.Wrote Nette. Peg. Aunt Annie. Norm. Dick Toppin. Bess.
W 19 ThGo down to YMCA with S/Sgt. Lester to Aussie orchestra & then come Home about 8.30 pm. It is an awful wet windy night & we have trouble all the time climbing the Hill. Recd letter from Elsie F.
W 20 FriMy Day off & I go to the flat far afternoon Tea & then with Stubs to YMCA to meet Ellis & stay to Band concert. Meet Bert Brian at the Pig & Whistle. Meet 3 old patients & go to Tea with them. Recd letters from Mr F. Marion E.
W 21 SatRecd letter from Marion Clarke. Val. Billy. A & I go to Crystal Palace & then to the flat. I am Home sick (an excuse only). We have sing song etc. & coffee  & phonograph.  We are all a very merry Party except yours truly. We learn that Joy Chapman was on the Kanowra [indecipherable]
