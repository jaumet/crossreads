2/9/17 Slept soundly ? 3 of us in 1 bed rose 8am. Breakfast then shaved. Billet is wooden framework filled in with sand and straw plaster. Had good feed blackberries. Tasted green walnuts for first time ? rather pleasant to taste.
3/9/17 Fine day. Inspected by Colonel then summoned to new billet ? 8 of us in a stable. Squad drill etc in afternoon. Heard gun-fire all last night like muffled thunder all during the day as wind changed. Saw several aeroplanes an observation balloon.
