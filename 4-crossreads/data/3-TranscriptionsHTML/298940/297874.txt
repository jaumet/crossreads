<p>forms the train track where we should have put the stretcher  on a truck &amp; gone ahead pushing. The truck, however, has disappeared; stolen by some ration party, we presumed, despite the printed notice with a red cross is for wounded only.<br/>Hurried searching &amp; peering in the darkness, half helped &amp; half hindered by the frequent gun flashes &amp; star shells. Koll found a large truck but it proved to be too cumbersome so decided to carry him to the next relay. Stafford &amp; Koll carried on &amp; I ahead calling attention to missing boards</p>
