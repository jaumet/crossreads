<p>the firing line to the right of Lone pine  it was a very quite place and we had good trenches on high hills</p>
<p>29/11/15<br/>The turks bombard Lone pine cousing heavy casualties  they had good big shells and plenty</p>
<p>28/11/15<br/>we got our first shower of snow  it come heavy and we had to sleep out in it   it remained on the hill tops for a week  a lot got frost bittin</p>
