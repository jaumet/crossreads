1 oclock, got our billets men fixed up, drew our blankets rations, had a wash brush up then Bill Hunt, Price Bell I set off down to the big canteen which is at the railway station, it was still snowing heavily but that never stopped us, went down had some tea cake brought some things back with us.
23rd Last night we spent a couple of hours with the French people who own our billets, we (the Sergts) are going to dine in their dining room, that will be alright for us.
Lovely sunny this morning all traces of the snow has disappeared.
