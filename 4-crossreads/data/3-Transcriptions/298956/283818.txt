<p>We caught a train from Lubeck to Oldesloe at 4 p.m. and at Oldesloe the sentry told me we would have to walk the rest, a steady two hours walk.  I arrived at the barracks at 7 p.m. dead beat and as hungry as a hunter.  The other prisoners were out to meet me, and then the old Grandmother took me into her kitchen and gave me a plate of soup.
