
Noordhemhoek Broodsiende 18.10.17
Fine day.  Enemy planes very low and active during early hours of morning.  Our guns silenced an enemy gun and blew up an ammun. dump in direction of Moorselede.
19.10.17
Our guns fired heavily in direction of town of Passchendaele on left last night.  Enemy opened barrage on our lines shattering front line system for a short distance.
