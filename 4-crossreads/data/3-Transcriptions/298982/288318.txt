<p>later refer d to Bell per Rowell Eight of us on peq noon for late fatigue &ndash; Bell as nervous &amp; white as sheet. Griffin the swine lied as usual. Result an extra four hour fatigue. I spoke to Griffin re the business &amp; he lied again Bell refused to hear what I had to say. Convinced he shielded Griffin &amp; he is what I always believed an arbitary self aggrandised weak unjust prejudiced cad. Such a thing could not have happened with a fair man His phisiognamy is a give away. It's the worst of having cads as officers. In evening while we were on the fatigue he came &amp; worked for a while The usual crack hearty game of his kidney. I'll get even</p>
