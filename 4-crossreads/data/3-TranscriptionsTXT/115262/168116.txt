Saturday 15 December 1917.
More mail arrives. 6 letters I got for me. Messages of congratulation read out on parade at 7 am. today. Letters dated as lated as Nov 6th in today&#39;s mail.
Wrote letters.
Regimental paper the &quot;Cacolet&quot; at last comes to light.
Rumors of a move &amp; of Hollands entrance into war against Engd. News that Clarrie is wounded in France.
(NOTE) The mail has cheered everybody &amp; wh all seem pleased. I got some very nice letters especially from D&#39;nong.&nbsp;
