<p>One of our aeroplanes disappeared today &ndash; it is supposed to of had a mishap and planed down in the enemy lines. A taub came over today and dropped 3 bombs but luckily all 3 failed to explode 9.12.15.</p>
<p>There was a severe bombardment by our Navy today. It was one of the heaviest since the landing 10.12.15</p>
