<p>gas guard here at night so expect a lovely time of it in the wet at the camp I left there are 80 or more men some of them just arrived from Nucleus &amp; have not been near the line for months yet they must send me 46 years old up here &amp; leave those fit men back there &amp; I've not got over the gas yet. On duty from 9 to 12 deferred Pay. &pound;50-0-0 Owe me 32/6</p>
<p>Tuesday 14/5/18<br/>Rain stopped &amp; sun trying to get a look in hot when it does. Just been shelled out of our tin hut fritz put about a dz shells uncomfortably close One just near the top &amp; two just opposite guess Pat got out in time wonder if I am a Jonah every time I get in to the line or near it  fritz shells hell out of us. deferred pay. &pound;50-1-6 Owe me. 34/6 Boys Paid today did not draw anything fritz shelled all day. 7-30 p.m. Just got 7 of our men some of them very badly cut up this <u>is</u> a nice place wish I was out of it.</p>
<p>Wednesday 15/5/18<br/>on duty last night from 12 to 3. but did not go off till &frac14; to 4 fritzs planes up by the dz. &amp; shelling hell out of us. also bombing he got seven more of our chaps last night B. Coy the others were 4 D Coy 3 Yankey engineers &amp; 1 Tommy. one of the Yanks died in the ambulance his penis &amp; privates were blown off &amp; half his rear also got it in the stomache &amp; Back.  another of them had his hand blown off Jim Perkins was wounded in leg &amp; arm  Lcpl Miller hand blown off its unsafe to go out of your dug out, am on to night from 3 to 6 a.m. deferred Pay &pound;50.3-0 Owe me 36/6</p>
