 25.1.16 Today has been the coldest day I have ever felt.  A very keen wind blowing in from the Sea, &amp; rain off &amp; on all day. We marched out about 5 miles to Matarieh. Also visited the Aerodrome &amp; saw a few aeroplanes on the ground. Played Cricket &amp; Football in the afternoon to try &amp; get warm, but it was no good.  R Josephson visited me later on in the Evening.  Camel Corps leaving Race-Course Camp for destination unknown. 
 26.1.16 Another bleak &amp; cold day but came out sunny in the middle of the Day. We marched out to the First observation Post, &amp; saw for the first time, what trench life is like. Imagine acres &amp; acres of Desert all cut up into hundreds of Trenches &amp; all sandbagged in.  We went right through the trenches &amp; looked at all the Dug-outs &amp; Bombproof Shelters.
