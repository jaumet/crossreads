Several were up at the time and our ante-aircraft guns were popping away at them. but evidently a plane is a very hard target to hit. as the shooting all went wide.&nbsp; It was our first taste of gunfire. We left Estoures 10.a.m. July 9th and arrived at Erquinghem&nbsp;sur Lys the same day where we were billited about first farm house, the loft straw was a feathered bed to us &amp; we liked it very much we soon were having a feed of fried eggs &amp; hot coffee as only French people can make it, we also found out where the Hens laid their eggs &amp; often had some without buying for same.&nbsp;&nbsp; The French</p>
