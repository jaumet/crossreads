11/10/17 Have been busy drying blankets etc. Then we rigged up a tent cleaned rifles, bayonets, Lewis guns etc. Fell in 6pm for inspection then had stew at 6.45pm moved off to front line in single file. I started off with 4 panniers of ammunition for Lewis Gun but soon broke down with its weight so Henry (the officer) took 2 panniers Andy 2. About  hr after took 2 off Andy took them right into action. Had very rough march in dawn up
