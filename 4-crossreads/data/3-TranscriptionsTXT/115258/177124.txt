 A common one, that is in most Y Emmas is &quot;When did you write to Mother last?&quot; and in this YM there are &quot;Character is what a man is in the dark&quot; and &quot;Genius is one part inspiration and three parts perspiration - Slog in&quot; also &quot;If you sware at Home, sware here; we want you to feel at home&quot; etc. That jolly nice cake Mother and Miss Anderson made came while I was at Port Said so Harold and I took it over to the hospital and gathered three mates there;  and  got a cup of tea each from the Red Cross, and then attacked the cake. It was beautifully fresh and was not a bit broken and was voted A1 by all concerned. I went to see Capt&nbsp;Trelawny again  this time . He was at home this time, and no doubt! he is a jolly nice man. He is a commander now! I went and saw him in his office one morning and he asked me to come  up  to afternoon tea, so I did, and was introduced to some naval officers, and had a plesent afternoon generally.
