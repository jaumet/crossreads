After we pulled our truck out we came back to Camp &amp; after having lunch we took up a Gang of Men to unload the Mine Earth in H.1. &amp; then we came back &amp; finished. On Sunday morning at Daylight Fritz came over on a Bombing raid, &amp; as I was coming down from St Jean, he passed over my Eng, he dropped a Bomb but missed me &amp; it did no harm but just before I arrived at the Camp he dropped a Bomb a few feet from the Blacksmith Shop which made a big hole &amp; then he dropped an Aerial Torpedo which wrecked the Blacksmith Shop &amp; as there were 4 Men sleeping in the shop only one was slightly injured but he killed two horses near the shop &amp; blew one right into the hole the Bomb made.
