<p>10 July 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>Nothing in particular doing today. Expect a final Board on Friday Showery weather.</p>
<p>1918 July 11<br/>Thursday<br/>Put in a quiet day. We had exceptionally heavy rain last night and this morning &amp; all day it has been squally Went with Sgt Clark in the evening to the Pavillion Theatre &amp; saw Who's the woman. Only just fair Had some tea in town &amp; got back to camp 9 P.M.</p>
