<p>23-6-16<br/>a few hundred yards from Oghratina Camp.  I connected onto the wire &amp; reported to Debabis that we were at Oghratina.  Richardson &amp; I went on into Camp arriving there a few minutes before the Regt &amp; had our horses watered before the Regt arrived at 2.30 this morning.  all the horse feed &amp; rations had been sent on to Debabis but fortunately a few fantasies of water had been left at Oghratina &amp; we were all allowed as much water as we could drink, tied our horses up &amp; dropped off to sleep just as the sky began to get light in the east;</p>
<p>rose again at 6 oclock as rations fodder &amp; water had been hurried over to us &amp; after a drink of tea &amp; breakfast of bully &amp; biscuits we felt new men &amp; dropped off to sleep again;  The 2nd Regt &amp; Bde H.Q. arrived at Oghratina from Debabis at 2 oclock this afternoon;  &amp; rested till 6 oclock;  Major Smith &amp; his troop got to Debabis at 12 oclock last night &amp; went out to the plane again this morning <strike>bringing</strike> brought back the engine &amp; any parts of value &amp; fired the rest;  The aviator turned</p>
