<p>On the 14th The Regt was relieved by the 3rd L.H. Regt. The erection of barbed wire entanglements round the entrenched positions held by the Regt being completed &amp; on the following day they crossed the Jordan &amp; arrived at Bgde Camp at Tel Es Sultan. The weather being excessively hot &amp; flies very bad. For the next few days the Regt camped in the vicinity of Jericho. Patrols visiting the Jordan daily for the reconnaissance of fords between Ghoraniye &amp; Hajla.</p>
<p>On April 27th the Regt left the Wadi Kell &amp; crossed the Jordan at Ghoraniye &amp; marched down to Hajla ford &amp; held an outpost line until the Patiala Infantry arrived. They &amp;^ the 6th Regt then formed the outpost line the Regt going into Bivouac. On the 29th the Regt drew 3 days rations for the men &amp; food for the horses recd orders to march to Bgde concentration Point at Butmet Halal. Arrived at Concentration point on the 30th Inst &amp; took cover in gullies near Jordan before daylight. Meanwhile the 7th LH &amp; Patiala Infantry had marched to Kabr Mujahid &amp; Kabr Said which they occupied</p>
