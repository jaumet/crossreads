<p>18 Sun to 20 Tues November<br/>Watered our horses at a German Settlement Wilhelmia mostly women and children they do not seem too friendly but make the most of it  There is a fine avenue of Gum trees growing through the centre of village  The place boasts Hotel two storyes  The eatables are very dear  eggs p1 each  Butter 3/- lb and other articles at ruling prices wine is [indecipherable] cheap [indecipherable]</p>
<p>21 Wed<br/>We are doing Patrols and outposts around the Settlements  still in touch with Jacko  We do not know how we are going on in [indecipherable]</p>
