<p>3<br/>It I think it will someday be a great Curio.<br/> Ye thank you old girl I did get the money you sent me from Sue &amp; also &pound;3 from Mr Walton which Miss Norris sent.<br/>I am most grateful &amp; expect you have ere this had my letter telling you all about it,<br/>You ask me "did I miss you"! Well old girl, I Knew I should miss you horribly, but did not realize what an awful lonely place Cairo would be without you till you had gone &amp; felt</p>
