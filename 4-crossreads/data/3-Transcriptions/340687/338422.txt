<p>April, 1917. Saturday 28<br/>Little cloudy mostly pleasantly sunny. Up at 9 and washed some clothes. Tried to pump but engine refused duty. Cleaned her up and pumped again in evening. engine good but overheated probably scale in water system. Had a bath in evening. Davenport came up from W. Pin aftn. &amp; we acted the goat wrote letters in evening.</p>
