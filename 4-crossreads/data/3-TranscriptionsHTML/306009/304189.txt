<p>1915<br/>3 Jan.  Sun.<br/>Attended Communion Service conducted by Staff Sergt. Start in Music Room at 7 a.m. <br/>Fire alarm sounded at 4.50 p.m. &amp; Unit paraded with lifebelts on, on promenade deck starboard. ( Practice only )<br/>Wrote several more letters during afternoon.<br/>At 7.15 pm. attended a sing-song conducted by Chaplain &ndash; Col. Nye, followed by Service at 8 o'clock &amp; Communion at 8.30;<br/>Then to bed.</p>
