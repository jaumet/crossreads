  
 Speed increased as both boats with unpronounceable names seemed to be gaining on us.  We gave her a run for it then, & finally steamed in ahead of her at 14 knots. 
 Leave & pass  only by launches since anchored in mid stream.  No launches appeared before dinner.  Afterwards many noises because  frequent  husbands objected to [indecipherable], & were thus not allowed on leave  Speeches etc etc - women very noisy. 
 Thurs 4th Leave ashore with padre.  Lunch Anzac Rest - chased guide work After lunch - Library & art gallery.  - Tea at White City.    Tram ride round Marmott Road. [indecipherable] 
 5th Leave with Bill Adams & Pat.  Wesley Rooms - Book shops & Techn College Parliament   Boat moved up to wharf  on return & coaling.
