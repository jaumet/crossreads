
Oct. 12thA rainy day during which the "Church of Eu" is visited. A never to be forgotten evening - "The Buskers".
Oct. 13th (Sabbath)News of another move - But why so soon? - Probably to-morrow. That will make  "three in one week".Splendid news from all fronts - Cyril (one of our oldest members) leaves our ranks and takes up a position of importance and trust in Camp Commandant's Office.
