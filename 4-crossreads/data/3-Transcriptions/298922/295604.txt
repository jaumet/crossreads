<p>Thursday 1st November 1917</p>
<p><strike>91 Lord St. between 10 &amp; 12.30 a.m. ask for Lt. Findlay.</strike></p>
<p>Dolly of course did not get any rest as an anti aircraft gun was outside the Hotel. Had lunch at the Carlton with Mrs. Nevil of N. &amp; F. Mulberrygong then called on Col. Murdock Red +. To "Arlette" in the evening with Mrs. Burgess of SHAFTESBURY.</p>
<p>Raids do not affect the pleasure loving public &amp; every theatre was crowded. We were lucky in getting a TAXI home &ndash; Dolly smudged Mrs. &ndash;</p>
