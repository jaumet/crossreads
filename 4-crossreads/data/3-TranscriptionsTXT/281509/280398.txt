 1916 June 24 Saturday 
 Dispensary in morning. Tony out in charge of hospital tent. Heartburnings  of Box &amp; Payne: two other aspirants. Victory for Liverpool. Busy all afternoon fixing up hospital. 5 patients admitted.  This "soldiering game" is a queer thing. Everyone for himself. "Bunky pulls the strings, &amp; the puppets dance".
