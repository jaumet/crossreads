 H.M.A.T. Ulimaroa 18/4/19 
 The Secretary 1st Light Horse Comfort Fund Sydney 
 Dear Mrs. Vernon On behalf of members of 1st L.H. Regt. I wish to thank you for your hearty greetings, we all appreciated very highly your messages of welcome and for the many kindnesses extended to us whilst on service by your Committee. 
 Again thanking you all and hoping that we may have the opportunity within a few days of meeting you all again Kindest regards. Yours Sincerely TEW. Irwin Lt Col. 1st L.H.Regt.
