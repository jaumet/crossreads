
1917 Thursday 19 July
Medhurst & Hall marked for Blighty too. Good to see all these bad cases so much improved. but Sister White leaving ward for night duty. Sister Simpson succeeds. Army Pay Corp. "Frivolities" give a first class concert in Y.M.C.A.Afternoon "off".  Jock H & to W--- fool round back to Canteen & concert. No more convoys to come in for 10 days.  Clearing hosp. in anticipation of coming big stunt.
