<p>[Saturday 10 June 1916]</p>
<p>Camped at 10 AM (Saghia) &amp; tried to sleep till 9 at night when we saddled up for <strike>Bayud</strike>-<br/>WATER is the trouble always &amp; we never get enough for man or beast on these stunts.<br/>Marched all night &amp; a Rocket was seen to be fired out in the Desert to alarm the Turks.<br/>Our objective was to surround a Turks Patrol &amp; destroy 16 of their Wells.<br/>The nights are beautiful but the days hell &amp; before we got home "We did a PERISH" but not as bad as the 6 &amp; 7th L.H. had.  <strike>We had an aeroplane so bombed their Camels-</strike></p>
