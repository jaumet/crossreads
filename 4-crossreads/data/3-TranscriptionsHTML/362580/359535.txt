<p>This is my last night on duty, shall be on Day-duty on Sunday.  We go off-duty at 7 a.m. to-morrow &ndash; go to bed for a few hours then catch 2 p.m. train for Cairo &amp; arrive there about 5.30 p.m.  Staying the night at "The Empire Convalescent Nurses Home", not allowed to stay at hotels etc.  The Home</p>
