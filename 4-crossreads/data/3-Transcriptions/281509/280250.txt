<p>27 January 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Duty as usual.  Tony's foot better but still laid up.  New nurse on ward &ndash;<br/>Barry. Skip off parade in afternoon.  Get second issue of boots.<br/>Have group photo taken, comprising .  Two nurses --- Murell &amp; Cameron.  Sergeant Meadows, Tony, Bob, Happy Dick, Dix &amp; self. Self.  Then Bob &amp; self taken.</p>
<p>Concert at C of E Tent at night.  Where I wrote this. &ndash; Bob comes back into tent.</p>
