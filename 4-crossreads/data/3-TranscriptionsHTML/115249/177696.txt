<p>Sir;</p>
<p>I have the honour to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of 26th September forwarding me a copy of your despatch to the Minister of Defence, dated 23rd September, on the subject of the terms of the surrender of German New Guinea.</p>
<p>2. I wish to thank you for your courtesy in forwarding the same for my perusal.</p>
<p>3. I have no doubt that the future will prove the correctness of your opinion.</p>
<p>I have the honour to be</p>
<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Sir,</p>
<p>your obedient servant,</p>
<p>(Sgd.) George Patey,</p>
<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Vice Admiral</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The Administrator,</p>
