<p>May, 1915<br/>Monday 31<br/>Attended as Prosecutor all day at a Court Martial on one of our men, Hot, heavy weather. Posted Diary up to yesterday with an enclosure from a wounded Sergeant of the I.L.H. Our men have been having a bad time on the Peninsular but playing the game. Posted Diary Notes yesterday hope they arrive safely.</p>
