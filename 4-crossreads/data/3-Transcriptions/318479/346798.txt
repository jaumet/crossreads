<p>25 July Church parade with the 18th &amp; 19th.  Go on guard at 7 p.m.</p>
<p>26 July<br/>On Guard till 12 o'clock.  20 Batt arrive.</p>
<p>27 July<br/>17th on a route march.</p>
<p>28 July<br/>Pay day.  Receive mail from home.</p>
<p>29 July<br/>Field firing with blanks.  Pte. Harman out of hospital.</p>
<p>30 July<br/>No afternoon parade.  All night route march.</p>
<p>31 July<br/>Sham fight with blanks.  Receive letter from Aunt Fan.  Battle in the "Wassa".</p>
<p>1st August<br/>Church parade.  Go into Heliopolis.  Brawl in Pyrimid Hotel.  17th Batt sent into Cairo on picket duty.</p>
<p>2 August<br/>Receive letter from home.  17th on a night march from 7 till 1 AM.  Lose the bolt of my Rifle.  "Dad" Higgins throws his rifle to blazes and falls out.</p>
<p>3 August<br/>Kit inspection.  Hand in my rifle.  Route march through Zeatoun.  "17" Batt ordered to the Citadel in Cairo.</p>
<p>4 August<br/>"A" on Brigade duty.  I am on Quarter-masters fatigue.  Get a new bolt for my rifle.</p>
<p>5 August<br/>Write home.  "A" &amp; "B" Coys. digging trenches.  No. 4 Platoon dig themselves in with entrenching tools.  Men for Engineers taken out of the Batt.</p>
<p>6 August<br/>Colonel Holmes lectures 5th Brigade about the disgrace of the "Battle of the Wassa".  17th march out at 9PM for an all night march.  15th section remain behind for guard.</p>
<p>7 August<br/>No. 15 Section on perimeter guard.  Serg. Allan, Corp. Wolf over guard.</p>
<p>9 August<br/>Trench digging and Coy in attack.  Pay day.</p>
