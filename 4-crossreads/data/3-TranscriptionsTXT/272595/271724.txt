It has been a glorious day just warm enough to make the day pleasant, I have done nothing all the morning save sort some of my photos out I have hundreds & they take some looking after I also sell a good few especially groups of the Sgts  yesterday I got 48 of a group & next Saturday I am getting 50 of another they are pretty good too.
