<p>24th. Have been in charge of a working party all the morning, they are shifting all the potatoes from down below for they are going rotten   at present we are dumping them on the platform &amp; round the mast, &amp; a bright sample they are at that, we knocked off for dinner at 12 after having which I went to a quiet little</p>
