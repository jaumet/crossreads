  1917  
 51 
 
 
 Nov 22 
 Slept on side most of night.&nbsp; Back tired.&nbsp; Soon wore off.&nbsp; Wrote all afternoon. 
 
 
 Nov 23 
 Feel well.&nbsp; Slept all morning.&nbsp; Spent afternoon in xxxxx writing.&nbsp; A band supplied music to us from 3 to 4. 
 
 
 Nov 24 
 Had a rough night.&nbsp; Side sore.&nbsp; Got better after dressing.&nbsp; Spent day sleeping and writing. 
 
 
 Nov 25 
 Feel better than yesterday. 
 
 
 Nov 26 
 Spent day sleepnig. 
 
 
 Nov 27 
 Much better.&nbsp; Good night.&nbsp; Had side washed out and spent afternoon writing. 
 
 
 Nov 28 
 Feel great.&nbsp; Doctor said I could get up and sit by fire after tea.&nbsp; Terribly bucked at thought of it.&nbsp; Got up after tea and felt very weak and was rather glad to crawl back into my little cot.&nbsp; All hands pleased to see me up at last.&nbsp; Had first smoke since I was wounded.&nbsp; Did I like it? 
 
 
 Nov 29 
 Had first rate night.&nbsp; Feel great.&nbsp; Got lovely pair of soft slippers from O.G.B.&nbsp; Envy of everybody.&nbsp; Got up after tea and Sister Sanne put my slippers on my feet.&nbsp; Sat by fire for two hours.&nbsp; Felt very tired at end of time and had to ask to be put to bed. 
 
 
 Nov 30 
 Feel good.&nbsp; Read for an hour before breakfast the book of poems sent to me by O.G.B.&nbsp; Wrote letters all morning and most of afternoon.&nbsp; Got up after tea. 
 
 
 Dec 1 
 Feel good.&nbsp; Wrote in morning and read all afternoon. 
 
 
 Dec 2 
 Feel good.&nbsp; Rested all day it being quiet.&nbsp; More men go to Blighty.&nbsp; Envied them.&nbsp; Got up after tea and stayed for 2&frac14; hours.&nbsp; &quot;Darkie&quot; explains about a nurse to Sister with &quot;red&quot; on her stomach, much to the amusement of the ward. 
 
 
 Dec 3 
 Feel good.&nbsp; Wrote after breakfast.&nbsp; Had good dinner.&nbsp; Slept all afternoon like the blacks.&nbsp; Bluestone on wound gave me hell.&nbsp; Got up after tea for 2&frac12; hours and had music. 
 
 
 Dec 4 
 Feel good.&nbsp; Lovely day outside.&nbsp; Rested all morning.&nbsp; Wrote in afternoon.&nbsp; Up after tea by fire. 
 
 
 Dec 5 
 Fine to-day again.&nbsp; Very raw morning.&nbsp; Job to keep warm.&nbsp; Up after tea.&nbsp; Orchestral concert by Canadian boys this afternoon.&nbsp; Very fine turnout. 
 
 
 Dec 6 
 Slept most of morning.&nbsp; Concert by Lena Ashwell&#39;s party in afternoon.&nbsp; A fine show.&nbsp; Up after tea again
