
February, 1916Monday 21The whole school went out on an attack scheme in the early morningAfter dinner we put in a couple of hrs on stoppages.Draw P.T 200 pay & have a long chat w/- an old pal now a Lieut.Weather getting warm again
Tuesday 22Machine gunners go out on a Tactical scheme returning at 8.30Morning on stoppages & afternoon reviewing notes.At night I go to see a big old Roman picture "Cabiria" very good.
