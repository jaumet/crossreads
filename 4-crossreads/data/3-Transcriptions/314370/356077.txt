<p>August, 1916<br/>Tuesday 29<br/>Nothing doing</p>
<p>Wednesday 30<br/>Received 2 letters from Mum &amp; 1 from Rich. (the first for six months)<br/>Answered them immediately.<br/>After tea  we got our marching orders.  we are to leave for Grantham, Lincolnshire, it is the training centre of the M.G. C.</p>
