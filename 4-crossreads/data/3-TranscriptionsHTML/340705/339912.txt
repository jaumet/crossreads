<p>The Cairo Young Men's Christian Association<br/>with the<br/>British and Colonial Forces in Egypt.</p>
<p>Heliopolis<br/>near Cairo<br/>14/2/15</p>
<p>Dear old Mick,</p>
<p>Many thanks indeed for your very welcome letter of Jan. 7th.  You can't imagine the pleasure that it gave me to read all my letters from home.  Took me all the way back to Mosman for half an hour.</p>
