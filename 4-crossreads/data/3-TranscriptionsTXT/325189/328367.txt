When the building is ready for service again it will be washed down with rose-water.  In the first courtyard there is one school upon each of its four sides.  That is, one school for each of the 4 sects.  Each school contains 200 rooms.  Where the preaching is done the lamps are supported by 365 chains &amp; there are 12 windows (I think it was windows).
