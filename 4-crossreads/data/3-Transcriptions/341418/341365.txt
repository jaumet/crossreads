<p>That at any rate is the idea. How it will pan out we shall see. Promises to be a chilly night; there is a North wind off the meditearanean, will be really cold in the early hours. It will be somewhat of a strain keeping awake tonight as we were travelling all last night with the exception of an hour and a half  &amp;  then the sandflies had a huge meal.<br/>It will be hell for anyone who gets wounded badly as they have to travel by camel for over 30 miles and it is not very comfortable for an ordinary rider.</p>
