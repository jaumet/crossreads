<p>In September we were taken to work at Oldesloe.  This meant a ride one station in the train morning and evening.  Oldesloe was a railway junction and we often had to transfer vegetables from one truck to another and we used to steal everything we could get from the Germans.  My word there trucks used to be much lighter after twenty six men had had a share of them.  The penalty for this stealing if caught was 14 and 28 day solitary confinement but as luck would have it we were never caught.</p>
