<p>X<br/>Sunday 26th March<br/>Am writing this on the Mediterranean 3 hours out from Alexandria, making (it is almost certain for Marseilles.<br/>We remained under canvas at Heliopolis for 10 days &ndash; a pleasant &amp; healthy ten days too. The loading of our equipment &amp; stores commenced on Friday morning &amp; was completed next day. The Unit consisting of 25 Officers, 119 nurses,190 N.C.O's &amp; men left Cairo main Station by special train at 9.45 a.m. on the Saturday arriving at Alexandria 3 hours later. Everyone was embarked immediately on the British Hospital ship "Braemar Castle" &amp; getting on board of the stores commenced. Everything being O.K. at 11.30 this morning &amp; we are now off.<br/>Besides our personnel of 334 of all</p>
