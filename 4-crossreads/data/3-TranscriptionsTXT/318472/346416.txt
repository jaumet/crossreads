 10 July 1918 Wednesday Nothing in particular doing today. Expect a final Board on Friday Showery weather. 
 1918 July 11 Thursday Put in a quiet day. We had exceptionally heavy rain last night and this morning &amp; all day it has been squally Went with Sgt Clark in the evening to the Pavillion Theatre &amp; saw Who's the woman. Only just fair Had some tea in town &amp; got back to camp 9 P.M.
