  
 Monday 6th.12. 15. Still hot.  Passed "Line" 12.30 pm Sighted Ship but too far away to see who she was. We are now right in Eastern Waters & consequently our danger of Submarines commences. Name of Ship painted out & also Signs on Port & Starboard sides of Boat Bridge covered over (A34).  Raining hard off & on Tropical Showers. 
 Tuesday 7. 12. 15 Nothing much doing.  Weather very hot, & playing up with our fellows. 10 in Hospital with Sunstroke. Squall rising this afternoon accompanied by terrific rain & wind. 
 Wednesday 8.12.15 Still very hot.  Held Sports & Concert.  Two Reps.  A.S.C. again successful. Turner won on knock-out from Woods F.A. Charlsworth won on points from Phillpots B.A.C. Concert again held in the evening & proved very attractive.
