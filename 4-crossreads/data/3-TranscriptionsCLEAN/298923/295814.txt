  
 28 May 1918 Tuesday (X to X crossed out) Days pass quickly & the Gyppy Labour Corps sing "How many days & How many" "nights must pass ere" "I see my loved ones again" Am afraid you often have difficulty in reading my notes but pens & ink are all bad in this country. X I wrote Aunt Louie this morning X   The cake from Boomerang Stall arrived safely & in good condition thank you very much for it. X More letters Mother March 3, May 1st April-   Laura saying you will be a great grand Mother if all goes well ere long-X
