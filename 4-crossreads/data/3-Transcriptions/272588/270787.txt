<p>Friday 18 October<br/>In London in the thickest fog I have yet been in.  Shopping etc. all day.  Dinner at troc. &amp; to the "Maid of the Mountains" in the evening. Return to France to-morrow.</p>
<p>Saturday 19 October<br/>Leave Victoria St. at 9 a.m. &amp; cross to Calais from Dover.  Crossing fine.<br/>Now at rest camp at Calais.</p>
