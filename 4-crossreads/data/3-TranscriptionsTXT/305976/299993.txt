The Turk is trying to put the wind up us and we are at present having a very rough spin. Thursday April 4 1ST L. horse captured Turkish patrol last night 1ST Brigade digging trenches just over Jordan. Both sides shelling heavily. All hands ordered to dig in. Friday April 5 The weather is exceptionally hot here &amp; flies very bad. A number of the lads are getting sick. All Details I am told are coming down from Jerusalem. It will make things easier for we are very much under strength.
