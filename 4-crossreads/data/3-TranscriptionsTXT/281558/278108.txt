 Friday May 3rd 
 Dunkirk : on duty 12.30 &ndash; 8.30 pm at turntable : got a small parcel from J B J &amp; sent her some views of Sydney 
 Saturday March 4th 
 Dunkirk : took of the turntable job &amp; put in the office : poked about learning the job : stroll into town &amp; market day was on so I roamed through the market : air raid warning at night but none came : the precautionary &amp; preventative measures taken here are very good : the balloons &amp; smoke a great idea : 
 Sunday May 5th 
 Dunkirk : on duty till 8 am : stroll after tea but showery weather kept us from going to the seaside resort here : 
 Monday May 6th 
 Dunkirk : on duty till 8 am : stroll after tea three Fritz' planes over at dusk
