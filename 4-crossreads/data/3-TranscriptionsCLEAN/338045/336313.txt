  
 &spent some time looking round this really ancient & famous spot.  Coins, old china of the Romans, Roman walls, statues & baths.  Fronts of houses of the 14th Century carried wholly into the garden of this place, rare specimens of Henry VIII & other English Kings & I left well pleased.  Legge & I afterwards found the old tower in which Joan of Arc was imprisoned but, being closed we did not gain admittance.  However, we had a good view of it & the moat, & walked around many curious ancient streets
