<p>There was one funny incident while there, and that<strike>cons</strike> was connected with the trench mortar.  The chap's that fire these guns wear a different colour to the infantry and if any of them were noticed about the trench, there would be some funny remark's such as Here comes the apples on sticks, I bet our parapet gets knocket down tonight and a few other remark's.  Every time these chap's fired the missels we were bombarded by Fritz's artillery, so that was why so many remark's were passed.  I saw my first German prisoner at this place he was sniping when one of our boy's caught him.
