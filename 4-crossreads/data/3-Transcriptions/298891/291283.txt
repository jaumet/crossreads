<p>1917 July</p>
<p>19 Thursday</p>
<p>My day on with Zdravko &amp; Tretin. Got off in afternoon for a little swim with Miss Brown. Dr de Faris pricked our ears to test our blood for malarial germs. Had a beastly night disturbed by people snoring &amp; hunting for keys &amp; mosquitoes under netting.</p>
