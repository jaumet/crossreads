<p>Saturday 30 December 1916<br/>Left camp at 8 a.m. and whole Brigade rode out East on a recognoitering expidition.  Rained all morning.<br/>Went out about 15 miles.  3000 turks &amp; some cavalary reported at Rafa.</p>
<p>Sunday 31 December 1916<br/>Stood to arms at 5 a.m.  Rode back all morning.  Worked hard at making a bivey to sleep in in afternoon.</p>
