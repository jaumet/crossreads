 "C" Company, No. 4 Officer Cadet Battn., Keble College, Oxford] 14th May. 
 Dear Judge, 
 Have just received your letter of 13th. March and by the same mail one from Keith. It was stiff luck getting such a nasty crack but he seems O.K. now.
