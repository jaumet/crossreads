 Saturday 12th May 1917 
 Cleaning harness, and painting "A" Sub harness racks. 
 Sunday 13th Went to dentist. No parade at 2pm fell in at 4pm to feed mules. Went out as centre driver at 7pm. Loaded waggons with timber and sand bags (at Bapaume) for machine gun pits and dug outs for gunners. nr Lagnecourt
