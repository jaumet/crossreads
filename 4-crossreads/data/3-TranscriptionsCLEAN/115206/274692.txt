  
 May 1918 Thursday 2 
 Bussy la Daours Weather fine, Had a hurry up run to Bonnay and called at battery on way back. Received payment first for some considerable time, very welcome indeed. Col Caddy received  C.D.C. Slept in General's room and learnt quite a considerable amount of very important things while acting as bodyguard. Weather fine  plenty of flying. Bombs again around here.
