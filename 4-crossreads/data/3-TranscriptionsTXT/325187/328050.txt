
[Friday 3 November 1916]
On board A25 Troopship a Union Castle Liner taken originally from the Germans and named Huntsend   raining & cold Have written Dolly & telegraphed to Laura & herThe former is well & plucky   Very few Australians on board only about 5 Officers, & a few sick dismissed men for AustraliaPlymouth & Devonport are all about the same rather a pretty spot, if it were not so cold & wet-Goodbye old England I wonder when I'll see you again.We are to be vaccinated again against cholera I weigh 13-7lbs.
