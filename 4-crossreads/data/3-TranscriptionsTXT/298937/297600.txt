Friday 16th June
Yesterday I was appointed dispenser for A.M.C. details.  Took sick parade & fixed up medicines.Later in the day we moved back & pitched tents in Miscellaneous Details again.Took sick parade again this morning.Told about a dispenser wanted in the 6th Light Horse, & am debating on it now.Moved back to Miscell.
