<p>13.4.15<br/>Tue. 13th.  B Troop returned from Cairo &amp; arrived in Camp at 1 o'clock this morning, reville went at 5.30 this morning, as the days are getting longer.  Stan &amp; I went on parade with B Troop &amp; were getting squadron drill all the morning, arrived back in Camp for dinner at 2 o'clock;  had nothing to do between dinner &amp; stables;  boxing with Conway after tea, had a game of billiards, &amp; put the rest of the evening in writing.  Stan went into Heliopolis to-night.</p>
