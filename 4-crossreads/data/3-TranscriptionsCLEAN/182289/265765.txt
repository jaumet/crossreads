  
 Parkhouse Camp Draft of 24 men marched out as reinforcements to M.G. Cos. in France. 
 28.6.17 - No. 433 Sgt. McGregor and No. 365 Cpl. Francis, reverted to ranks at their own request and proceeded with the Draft to France. 29.6.17 - Five men marched in from 26th Batt. Rollestone. 2.7.17 - No. 420, L/Cpl. Kalucy appointed A/Transport Sergeant, No. 424, Dvr. Murdoch, R., promoted to L/Cpl. 7.7.17 - 3rd Div. M.G. Coy. allotted 16th Inf. Brigade, and renamed 16th Aust. M.G. Co. (Auth. D.A.G. A.I.F. 25/98, 13.6.17). 13.7.17 - 2/Lieut. Mathieson proceeded to Grantham for a course of Instruction at M.G.T.C. 14.7.17 - Lieut. Graham proceeded to Wareham to undergo a Revolver Course. 16.7.17 - Machine Guns of Company examined by Representatives from Vickers. 20.7.17 - The Company carried out practice on Field Firing Range at Beach's Barn for field firing. 26.7.18 to 28.7.17 - Route March and Field work.
