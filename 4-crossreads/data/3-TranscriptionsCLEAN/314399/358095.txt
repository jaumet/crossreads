  
 Tuesday 8 April 1919 Missed parade again this morning. Am on special patrol today & no job tomorrow. Met Garry & took him round to see different places. Bill came in & we had dinner at a little french cafe. Had lobster. Goodo. 
 Wednesday 9 April 1919 Bill & I visited St Addresse [Adresse]. Got the train home OK. Got my negs & prints. Quite good.
