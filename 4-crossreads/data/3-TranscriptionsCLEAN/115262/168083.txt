
Monday 12 November 1917.
In early hours of morning a terrific thunderstorm broke & continued till for an hr. Ground impassable for camels. Halted till dawn and moved off again till 7.30 a.m. Now camped in an orchard of olive trees etc, 4 miles from the beach and beside the village of Julis. Are now Nth of Gaza.
"Stand to" all night. Moved off at dawn in a Nth to NthWest direction
Turks counter attacked strongly at dusk, 14 planes go over on a bomb raiding expedition at 4 pm.
News that (Hebron) El Khalali has fallen.
