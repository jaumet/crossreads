  
 Sunday 18 February 1917 [17 February 1918] Long peaceful day "off", rose 9 a.m., printed photos (craze is bad just now, then Pyramids with S. Steele for the day & Church 6 p.m., All Saint's - Canon Garland the preacher & very good sermon for Lent - text, "Is it nothing to you who pass by?"  Church crowded as usual.
