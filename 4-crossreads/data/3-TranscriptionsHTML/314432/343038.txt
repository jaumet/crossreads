<p>However at *- PM orders were received stating that the Germans had blown up two of the Somme bridges, &amp; that the B'de sld retire from Ommecourt to Fenillieres, a distance of about 3,500 yds, &amp; there crossed the Somme, about 10 a.m. on 30th Aug, &amp; pushed forward with 'A Company 20th Bat in the lead, their right flank resting on the railway along the bank of the Somme, &amp; their left on the high ground occupied by the 3rd Division. After marching<br/>*some time in the night.</p>
