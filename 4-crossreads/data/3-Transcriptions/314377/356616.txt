<p>Eventually we arrived at Etaples and there I was put into the 56th General Hospital The train arrived about 2 o'clock in the morning, was left out in a tent previous to admission Sister Chadborn was the Sister in Charge of the hut. Everything possible was done for my comfort. Needless to say I was practically helpless</p>
