The darling little kiddies who come here to school gee I could dangle them all on my knee have already fallen in love with one little dear she has taken quite a fancy to me I think. 25-5-17. Still at the school, the teachers residing here could not be nicer & the kiddies are lovely. Went for a ride on the bike last night with Horace to La Motte a beautiful little village in the centre of a large forrest It was a glorious ride. 26-5-17.
