
12 February 1918 Tuesday  (X to X crossed out)Another fine day & busy on our survey work-  The fields are a mass of Red anemonies now-  One hold Hill we named "Anemone" Hill & one "Weir's" Hill so in future Maps of the Holy Land our these names go down to posterity-  Such is fame?"Snipe" & "Ducks" are plentiful but I have no good gun.X PEN bad, pen uselessAnother Officer has just left for Australia by getting his Family to apply to the Defence Department for him.  I long for the day when I see you all again with Dolly.   X
