<p>Wednesday 13 March 1918<br/>Recco done but very cloudy.</p>
<p>Thursday 14 March 1918<br/>Escort to bomb raid on pontoon bridge over Jordan, raid unsuccessful but we fired 500 rounds at camps and cavalry with good effect, height 2500.  Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 2 h. 15 m.</p>
