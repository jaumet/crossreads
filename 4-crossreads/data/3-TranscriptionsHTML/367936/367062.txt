<p>Sunday 29 October 1916<br/>Church parade in morning.  Got leave and went up to Port Said.  Mr. Trelawny not back yet.<br/>Went over French man-of-war.  Saw English &amp; French sub-marines.  Lot of English people in Port Said on account of P &amp; O boat being there on its way to Australia.</p>
<p>Monday 30 October 1916<br/>Day Stable man.<br/>Wrote some letters.</p>
