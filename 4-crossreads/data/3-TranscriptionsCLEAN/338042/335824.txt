  
 Scales- Call on Mr. Scales at his Office - Then we view St. Giles Church (Cripplegate) which is burial place of Milton etc.etc. - Also played prominent part in Great Plague & Fire of London - Luncheon - Take train at Cannon St for Blackheath and go to the Home of Mrs.Thomas (Mrs.Scales' Mother) - Pass most enjoyable evening with Mrs. Thomas - Mr. & Mrs Thomas (Jun) Miss Matthews, Mrs. Scales, Miss [indecipherable] - Stay at Blackheath for the night.
