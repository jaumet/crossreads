<p>Tuesday 20 March 1917 [19 March 1918]<br/>Fifteen of our pts. go out today &amp; wards at stagnation point.  One's patience &amp; hope are sorely tried with a sister in charge who is both rude &amp; incompetent though with one good trait, very good as to time of returning to ward, never late but the three of them so deadly uninteresting and cold.</p>
