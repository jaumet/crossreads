 December 1916 Saturday 30 Squally weather 
 Sunday 31 Terrific gale blowing no shelter. Everybody wet. Rained during night 
 New Years day . All 1917 Went down to beach bombarded by German Taubs. 3 shells fell down into the sea. Remainder went into palm groves. 13 casualties amongst British troops. Terrific wind blowing.  Had the coldest swim I ever had.
