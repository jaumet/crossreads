 May 7th Tues. Grand Concert by Aussies in City Hall. Have been going all over the country seeing everything worth looking at. Went one day w/- large party to town of Stellenboseh &amp; had a fine time. 
 Sun. 19th May Orders to pack up. 
 May 20th Monday Embarked on TSS Tofua. 
 May 21st Tue. Pulled out at dawn &amp; sailed about 11 a.m., very rough &amp; stormy. 
 May 22 &amp; 23, Wed., Thur. Still very rough &amp; rolling, tucker &amp; quarters pretty good.
