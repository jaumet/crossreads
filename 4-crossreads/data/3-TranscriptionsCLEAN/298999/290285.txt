  
 Monday May 28.5.17 Stand to 5 A.M. had Breakfast had shave done some washing wrote to maud Jessie aggie Johnnie tim dick auntie mae had dinner.  rested all afternoon very windy had tea 5 p.m.  turned in 6.30.  went up the canal on outpost on 2 Post 11.30 till 1 A.M. 
 Tuesday May 29.5.17 
 Stand to 5.30 had shower had Breakfast done some washing rested all day and read Papers things are very quiet.
