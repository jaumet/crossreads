 27 June 1, 2, &amp; 3 Platoons go to the range for rifle shooting.  4 Platoon granted leave for Cairo.  We go and see the Pyrimids and had a spin round Cairo in a motor with Sergeant Pulling &amp; corp. Bradford arriving back home at 9 p.m. 
 28 June No. 4 on duty. 
 29 June Issued with Kahki shorts &amp; shirts.  Go to the range for rifle shooting.  Fire 10 rounds in the "grouping". 
 30 June Shooting on the range. 
 1 July Finish all the ranges.  I score 105 points.  "A" Coy. marches home to Heliopolis. 
 2 July See "Tiny Ryan".  The 7 Brigade arrive at Abbasia Barracks. 
 3 July Am sick.  Go on guard at 7-12 PM.  Sergeant Robertson &amp; Corp. Perrol over guard. 
 4 July Guard dismissed at 8 AM.  Go into Cairo with 4 mates.  We see the zoo etc. &amp; arrive home at 9 PM.  Webb, O'Hara, Taylor. 
 5 July No. 2 Platoon sent on picket duty in Cairo.  27 Batt. arrive. 
 6 July "A" Coy. on duty.  Have a yarn with "Tiny" Ryan of the 13th Batt. 
 7 July Route march through Heliopolis &amp; out into the desert.  5th of the 13th Battalion leave for the front.  "A" Coy. digging trenches under Lieut. Pye. 
 8 July "A" Coy. on duty.  Address by Colonel Unsworth in the Y.M.C.A. 
 9 July "D" Coy. goes to the rifle range. 
 10 July Granted leave for Cairo. 
 11 July "A" Coy. on duty.  Am on guard.  Receive letter from home dated June 7th.
