  Feb.16  th    Last night was very cold. it froze for the first time since a long time - to-day was a lovely day - a Fritz Aeroplane was  brought  forced  down between Bailleul & Meteren without a shot being fired. by one of the new Dolphin machines. the Fritz tried later to make his escape but was caught before he got to the line - We walked into Bailleul for some photographique material in the afternoon 
   
 Feb.17  th    - Sunday - .We developed the photos of Suzanne's outfit last night in the lorry & printed them to-day - they were'nt too bad. it was cold last night but a lovely day to-day - We went up to Suzanne's in the afternoon for a coffee & to show them the photos. 
   
 Feb. 18  th     Fritz was over here last night dropping bombs, but did'nt drop any near here - We were called at 5.A.M. & left the park at 6.15 A.M. & carried the Chinese labour corps from their camp at Mont Noir to their work at La Flettre & back in the afternoon. they pinched our soap  we got home at 5.30 P.M.  lovely day  We had a look over the Bailleul Aerodrome during the day as we came back to Bailleul & parked there whilst the Chinese worked -
