Monday 6/5/18&nbsp; Had a few casualties by premature explosion of big French Gun.
Tuesday 7/5/18&nbsp; Letter from Doff- also Wrote Doff- Bn digging &amp; reconnoitring.
Wednesday 8/5/18&nbsp; More reconnoitring &amp; digging also Training&nbsp; wrote to Bas.
Thursday 9/5/18. Orders received to move again tomorrow
Friday 10/5/18. Moved to Fransvillers [Franvillers] arriving at 9 p.m. had a game of bridge
Saturday 11/5/18. Reconnoitring positions in Reserve &amp; forward
Sunday 12/5/18&nbsp; Enemy shelled village&nbsp; two civilians killed.
