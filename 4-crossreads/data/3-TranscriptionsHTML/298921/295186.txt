<p>August, 1915<br/>Tuesday 3<br/>No submarines &amp; in sight of all islands en route apparently inhabited by Greeks etc growing currants &ndash;<br/>A Period of dullness sleeplessness &amp; hunger is in front &ndash; but its the real thing now &ndash;<br/>Great secrecy was observed. We did not know the name of TRANSPORT till alongside &amp; nobody on board knew we were coming till 3 hours before &ndash; Out of the train into the ship &amp; away &ndash; all Chinese Crew &ndash; Rocky islands all around us now.</p>
