<p>July 1918<br/>Tuesday 23</p>
<p>Frechencourt.<br/>Weather very wet and miserable, no parades. During the day wrote three letters to folk at home, having had a quiet spell of work.<br/>Inoculation took place . I managed to dodge same.<br/>Walked up to see the concert at the A. Engineers camp but it did not come off unfortunately owing to the rain.<br/>Had a few hours conversation with an American corporal by name Sweeney. [indecipherable] th company</p>
