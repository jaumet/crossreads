<p>Egypt<br/>19/1/17</p>
<p>To 1st Light Horse Comfort funds depot</p>
<p>Dear Sir or Madam<br/>I here by beg to acknolage receipt of parcel safely to hand yesterday received in first rate condition. Thanking you sincerely for same</p>
