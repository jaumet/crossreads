Saturday July 27Grapes &amp; Melons are plentiful just at present. The fruit is very welcome after our long spell in the Jordan valley.Sunday July 28Rode to Jaffa today. Jaffa much altered since I was last there. The town has developed &amp; business is in full swing. We had a dip in the surf also giving our horses a swim.Monday July 29Took a trip to Richon to day. One of the prettiest little villages I have seen. The Jews seem well contented &amp; prosperous.
