The grasses growing round Rafa are chiefly rye grass &amp; Crow-foot,  two very excellent  both of which are well known in Australia &amp; excellent feeds for all classes of stock. After leaving Rafa we pushed on a further seven or eight miles to Khan Yunis, &amp; camped near the village for the night, &amp; we shall probably be a  week or so  few days here.
