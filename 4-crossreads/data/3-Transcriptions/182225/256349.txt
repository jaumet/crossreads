<p>AUGUST&nbsp;&nbsp; FRIDAY 18&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine, but cloudy all day. Out signalling all day with rest of headquarters. M<sup>r</sup> Rowe (Capt) took over the 27<sup>th</sup> Bty from Capt Playfair in morning. I went out with the 26<sup>th</sup> Bty headquarters in evening after tea &amp; got some practice with the director.</p>
