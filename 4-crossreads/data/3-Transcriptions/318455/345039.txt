<p>had a hot shower &amp; we are to fall in again at 11 a.m. The scenery is quite changed hilly &amp; timbered I believe we are in the Rhone Valley at St Germain au Mont D'Or. <strike>Left</strike></p>
<p>Left here at noon &amp; soon reached Lyon &amp; have been travelling down the Rhone Valley &ndash; a moving picture of Towns &amp; villages Farms, Orchards &amp; Gardens.</p>
<p>Sept 29<br/>Woke up this morning to find ourselves in Marseille Station &amp; have been travelling all day close to the coast towards Italy &ndash; getting some glorious views of the Mediteranean</p>
