It stopped raining at last so we made a big fire & the boys dried their cloths & we felt fit once again. It was now getting very cold at night & we felt the cold very much after the heat of Egypt. We left [?] on October 20 th.  . We marched to the Amines road where we got on to French motor buses and went many kilos the other side of Amines. Our feet were as cold as Ice & we were not sorry
