<p>15/10/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Nice day &ndash; but very cold also at night &ndash; went to Mass at 10.30 &ndash; by Father Gilbert. All confined to billet area &ndash; as I expected after last night &ndash; I managed to sneak out along the river as I have marked on a post card &ndash; Struck a lot of 59th Boys in an a Estaminet &ndash; also some French fellows who could play &amp; sing &ndash; had a good old sing song &ndash; Got a snap taken of myself all dirty &ndash; etc just as I came from Rifle Villa yesterday &ndash; they are breakups &ndash; owing to the Division moving all mail is hung up &ndash; just managed to get some of the snaps thro to Frank.</p>
<p>16/10/16  Monday</p>
<p>Very cold day &ndash; Our Section on Fatigues &amp; still confined to Billet area. Had read out on parade about voting on conscription &ndash; Amused ourselves most of the day by stump electioneering speeches for &amp; against, very funny. Most of the boys got out after tea &amp; got merry &amp; it is only a rumour things were not gay till the small hours &ndash; all determined to have a bit of fun if going to be confined.</p>
<p>17/10/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Nice day &ndash; All spare men went for a route march. Ordered to prepare pack etc to move out.</p>
