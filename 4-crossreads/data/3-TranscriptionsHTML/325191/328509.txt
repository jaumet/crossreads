Their wher several shools in languages &amp; proffessiones in this Camp, About 40 shops dealing with allsorts, but mostly foodstuffs, tobacco, Clothing &amp; fruit.  Now we comence 1918 &amp; no nearer to peace &amp; liberty than in 1915.  How long more will we hawe to remain in this living Dante Inferno or Black Hole of Australia.  Wher is Civilisation or humanity.  Are we forgotten.  It is impossible for us Internes to cry out to the world &amp; so contradict the glorious account printed in the daily Austr. Newspapers, as the Censorship of our Camp is very strict any mentioning of real facts in this Camp &amp; the letter to be shure to be returned to sender.  I personaly have had hunderts returned on account of containing as marked prohibited Matter"  <u>23 January</u> the first installation of Electric light on certain places in Camp, wich wher indeet much needet.  14/  1 Sh each Internee issued out of moneys recieved from the Germ Red Cross Society.
