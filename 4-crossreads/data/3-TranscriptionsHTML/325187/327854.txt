<p>[Wednesday 19 April 1916]</p>
<p>Went Assuit on Court Martial of a SIKH Gunner who shot his Sgt Major dead .  MURDER charge.<br/>Feel a but off color from inoculation.<br/>There's a large Barrage or Dam here across the Nile &amp; it is quite a nice place after Sohag.  Passed a lot of Poppy Fields these are sold straight from the fields to the Native Women, who use, steeped in Water &amp; as an anaesthetic   Not much "opium" is made locally- some Poppies exported.  One of the 5th Army Service Corps (Australian), gone to hospital today with Small Pox and another Queenslander with Plague.</p>
