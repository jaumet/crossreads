The French folks think a lot of their religion their churchs were always very nice indeed, & many of our men went to the services held in them.    We left Morbecque on July 8th at 9. a.m. before we left I met a chum from New Zealand who was in Hospital & we had a good yarn.  We marched to Etoures 16 kilers & we all felt it very much, during the March we passed through a small town of Merselle on the River Lys, Estaures seemed a compacted little place & some fine shops.  a good Y.M.C.A. in the Town Hall where we slept the sleep of the   Just next day the Battalions left in small parties.
