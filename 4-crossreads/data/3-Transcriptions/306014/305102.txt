<p>Feb 27 &ndash; Mch 1</p>
<p>Usual drill. We were also issued with razor, Jack-knife &amp; lanyard, flannel belts etc. which now makes our equipment complete.</p>
<p>Mch 2</p>
<p>I was detailed to-day with a party to do a few hours pick &amp; shovel work &amp; at this strenuous occupation filled in most of the day.</p>
<p>Mch 3</p>
<p>Usual Saturday's route march after which we underwent a feet inspection by our officers.</p>
<p>Mch 4</p>
<p>I was detailed as a member of a fatigue party this morning &amp; instead of attending Church Parade we were marched to head quarters to receive instructions &amp; to our gratification found that there was nothing to do. Alan &amp; I then went for a walk to the A.I.F.</p>
