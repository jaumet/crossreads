 As there is very little excitement to be got here in camp I have been spending my Saturday &amp; Sunday afternoons by taking long walks into the surrounding country.  Alan is not too fond of walking &amp; so he has not come with me of late on these excursions.  The country &amp; villages around here are just as Wordsworth pictured  the  English rural scenery &amp; I think I  will  can now better understand &amp; appreciate his feelings in this respect than ever I did before. 
 And so in the best of spirits &amp; with tons of love for you both &amp; for all at home. 
 I am as of yore Your affec. Son in khaki Duncan xxxxxx
