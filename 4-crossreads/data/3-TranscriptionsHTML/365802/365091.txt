<p>"Maiala"<br/>Whitton Rd<br/>Chatswood<br/>28.6.16</p>
<p>His Honour<br/>Mr. Justice Ferguson</p>
<p>Dear Sir,<br/>I beg to offer you my deep sympathies over the death of your gallant son, a personal acquaintance with him was not my pleasure; but I knew your son Bernard, (alas, like his brother, not among us!)<br/>And greatly admired both his character and his ability. I do not doubt that his brother was of the same fine stock. His loss is peculiarly painful to me because my brother is now in the thick of it, and I fear, while I ought to hope that great things are doing on the Western Frontier.</p>
