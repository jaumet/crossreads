<p>Early morning communion &amp; morning service at which I helped with distributing the books as usual I have been attending the 6 oclock services every evening down in the aft hold, but ommiting the Sunday evening services.</p>
<p>There is one on at present, a memorial service for Geddes who went overboard.</p>
<p>The weather today has been splendid.  This afternoon I lay down &amp; had a splendid sleep &amp; sun bath.</p>
<p><u>Thursday 14th Aug</u></p>
<p>Today like Sunday we travelled very slowly all morning on one engine &amp; only went 248 miles.  The mileage is going from bad to worse.</p>
