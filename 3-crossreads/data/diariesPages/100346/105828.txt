Tuesday March 5th
Misery : on till noon : worked on our shanty till tea then bed :
Wednesday March 6th
Misery : on till noon : usual routine
Thursday March 7th Friday March 8th
Misery : on till noon each day : nothing much doing : Fritz over on Friday night but didn't drop anything :
Saturday March 9th
Misery : frosty morn's but glorious days : usual routine : had a stroll over some old Fritz gun positions our guns have knocked things about some :
Sunday March 10th
Misery : had a stroll over the dump here : Fritz has lost at least one train (on fire) a fair bit of light railway material but the rest of the stuff looks as though he has set it on fire : on till noon
