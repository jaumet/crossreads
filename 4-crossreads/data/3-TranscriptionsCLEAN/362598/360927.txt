
Sunday 28 May 1916Wrote Jack Preddey, Mem & Esme.  Church by new Minister.  Jack Tredrea.  Nin & Bill with DOPE & Dev. go to Pt. Said.
Monday 29 May 1916All day leave.  A.M.C.'s not to be worn & belts to be worn.  Met McCoogi.  Dinner at 8 a maison & tea at Groppi's.  Kasr-el-Nil Jardin & went to his office near Esbekiah Gardens.
Tuesday 30 May 1916Eric & I go to French.  I am paraded for roll calling.  Mark a man present on Parade who is in Clink.  Gallagher & Dope Lord too.
Wednesday 31 May 1916Major Raffan receives orders to proceed to Alex. & London.  Ellis & meet McCoogi.  Pay day.
Thursday 1 June 1916Pasted Photos in Album.  Lovely Day.
Friday 2 June 1916Major Raffan goes to England.  Ellis stops in bed with screws.  Weather still Glorious, a bit windy this day but nothing much.
Saturday 3 June 1916Warm & windy.  Finish Roll Call.  I go to French myself & go very well.  Jump on donk. & gallop to meet Bill in Esbekiah Gardens.  Met Dave instead.  Saults for Soupper & Fast Train Home.  Stopped opp. gates.  Dope & Earn march through Jardin.  Wrote to Nettie.
