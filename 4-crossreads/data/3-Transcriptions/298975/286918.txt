<p>Sunday 8/7/17<br/>A good searmon song service in the afternoon Lollies supplied at tea time.</p>
<p>Monday 9th<br/>The Head has been on to our O C. Concequently he has he on to us The mail closed at 9 AM today. Must be getting close to Durban We hear that we will be there each day up to Friday. No doubt one of those days will be right<br/>Payed &pound; 1-0-0 today</p>
<p>Tuesday 10.<br/>Drill this morning Sports in afternoon Did some washing</p>
