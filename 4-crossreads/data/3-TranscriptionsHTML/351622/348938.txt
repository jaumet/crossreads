<p>16 December 1916 Sat<br/>Bad news again from Geoff saying he had had his arm taken off. 10 am. A battalion of highlands with pipes &amp; drums playing passing by on their way to the line</p>
<p>17 December 1916 Sun<br/>Wrote to McAlpine. Spent some time with Norm Burgess. He is going to England to night on furlough, whisky &amp; milk. Stapleton an old school chum comes through sick.</p>
