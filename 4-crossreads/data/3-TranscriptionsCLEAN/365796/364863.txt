
1913This is a retreat at the waterworks half way up Mt Wellington. We saw it on our way to Brown's River yesterday & will see it again when we are up at Fern Tree. The whole country is very pretty & the roads are splendid though very hilly. We were up 1600 ft yesterday.
Love from [indecipherable]
Alan Fry.Dene Gully.Northcote Rd.LindfieldSydney.N.S.W.
