<p>Monday 28<br/>Relieved last  night by the 7th Brigade. The field of action is now a huge puddle hole, mud and slime; Camped at Tara.[indecipherable]<br/>Tuesday 29<br/>Ready and strong marching along. But its damn hard last time dust this time mud. Warloy again<br/>Wednesday 30<br/>It is raining like blazes, we are camped in a rat infested dwelling, but grateful shelter<br/>Thursday 31<br/>Marching through beautiful country, as beautiful as the beauty of a woman thats done no wrong</p>
