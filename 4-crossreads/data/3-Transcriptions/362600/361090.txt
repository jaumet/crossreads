<p>W 18 Tues.<br/>Rained nearly all day. [indecipherable]  meeting.  Go to room &amp; write &amp; read at night. E &amp; I have a big talk &amp;  neither feel very happy after it is all over.   Je suis [indecipherable].<br/>Wrote Laura &amp;  Doris [indecipherable]</p>
<p>W 19 Wed<br/>&amp;fine all afternoon. E has cable-gram. Tam &amp;  I go to Romsey. Visit Abbey &amp; talking [indecipherable]. Eastleigh 4pm. Meet Col. Tea. Photo at Mill Stream. Leave at 6pm. Arr Romsey at 6.50. Salisbury 8.15 average 10 &frac12; miles. Visit 1 Close &amp;  Tea &amp; Avondale. 45 miles.</p>
<p>F 20 Th<br/>Yesterday I visited the Green Days, [indecipherable] at Alderburns &ndash;<br/>Have H- of a day. Tell a yid they are no good &amp; find it is his cousin I'm slandering. Co. goes into every Room in Division. Thomas comes to me to work. Top comes Home. Starts Training write Jennie &amp; M.R.L. Dream of Pats &amp;  Doris  &amp;  DJ's&amp; John Caswell.</p>
<p>F 21 Fri<br/>But windy. Another hellish day.<br/>Another H of a day. I come down to my Room &amp; read all night. Go to bed early. Major Bear the madman goes off Duty &amp; we all get happy.</p>
<p>F 22 Sat<br/>Bit windy. Tear up paper all night to Top's pleasure. Write letters all night.<br/>Roll up pictures from Sydney [Wakly's?] send X'mas Number Sunday Times to Aunt Annie.<br/>Wrote to Marion Clarke &amp; Maisie Carter.</p>
