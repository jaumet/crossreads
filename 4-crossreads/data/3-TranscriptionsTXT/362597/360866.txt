We had a paper issued the other day about all the doings on board. Some of the things were good &amp; interesting to us because we know the parties referred to.
I forgot to say that we had several cases to try for the mock trial. The canteen keeper was fined for selling 1d sardines at 4d &amp; matches that had been struck. The Bar-man was tried for refusing drink to a thirsty soldier &amp; another real fat old chap whom we call "John Bunny" was fined for throwing pillows
