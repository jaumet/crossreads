
Monday July 2Inspection by General Royston known as galloping Jack. N.C.Os arrive from field. In a new unit such as we are all NCOs lose their rank on arrival. NCOs drawn from other units who have seen service. Very hot day. & beautiful moonlight night. On duty all night, naturally wonder on such nights as these how all are getting on at home What a change a few months can make in a man's life.Tuesday July 3Our first Sand storm. Windy as            sand in everything including Food "What a lovely country" EGYPTWednesday July 4W.O. arrived today from 3rd Light Horse Field Amb Seems a good sort.
