<p>Sunday 11 November 1917.</p>
<p>On again till 9 am.&nbsp;Halted 3 to 4 hrs. &amp; then on again in direction of Gaza &amp; coast. An old mail somehow arrived. Letter from Arthur Thompson just in Egypt.</p>
<p>Travelled through till midnight with one stop for 20 mins. and numerous halts <s>for 5</s> when passing through villages when it was done in single file. <s>Line of</s> Country looking more settled &amp; civilized. Orchards here &amp; there, owing to war have been neglected.</p>
