<p>May, 1915<br/>Wednesday 19<br/>Prisoners got out of Guard room last night &amp; have gone up all night in Cairo doing the slums Very hot today &ndash; sheds are being built for the Horses with Reed Matting roofs, Australian Mail in but no letters for me Perhaps they have been sent to the Dardanelles.</p>
