<p>Sunday 17/10/15<br/>Remained in bed till 8.30 AM<br/>Left Camp about 12 am &amp; had dinner at Blue Bird, caught train in afternoon for Kew, visited cemetery, returned by tram &amp; went to Domain.  Had tea in town, Reg returned to camp ill.  While on leave Mr Sutton called.</p>
<p>Monday 18/10/15<br/>Ordinary Parades  At night visited Kew to see Mr Sutton. Met Mr &amp; Mrs Reid, arrived back at camp 9.30 pm</p>
<p>Tuesday 19/10/15<br/>Parades as usual.  Remained in Camp at night</p>
<p>Wednesday 20/10/15<br/>Usual parades, went in to City at night</p>
