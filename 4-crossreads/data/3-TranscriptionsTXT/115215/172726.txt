July 18 -1918
Things are going along Smoothly&nbsp; Just now at the present time we are out of the line with the 5th Brigade [indecipherable] While the main Body are in the front line&nbsp; We are Camped on the Banks of the [Seine?] just outside Amiens and [it?] is an Ideal Camping Ground under very tall Trees&nbsp; plenty of swimming &amp; Boating on this [Bonney like?] stream. Went to field ambulance this morning for Drugs.
