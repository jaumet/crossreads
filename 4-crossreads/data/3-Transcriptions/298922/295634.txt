<p>Saturday 1 December 1917</p>
<p>Saw 2 whales this morning &amp; these would make a good camouflage for subs. I got the wife up at 4.45 a.m. as we are now in the midst of the vermin, just outside <strike>GIBRALTAR</strike>. Other escorts have joined including a <strike>U.S.A. T.B.D.</strike>. It is not a good thing to stay long in the BATH <strike>&amp; other places of convenience with the</strike> door locked.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>I have just been yarning to an Italian Officer he blames his Government for sending the TURIN rioters to the one front, where they fraternized with the enemy &amp; sold ITALY a PUP.</p>
