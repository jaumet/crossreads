<p>6 November, Thursday</p>
<p>E &amp; E out to tea. We play Bridge. Meet Mr Smith on boat. Recd letter from M Smith.</p>
<p>Wrote M Smith.</p>
<p>7 November, Friday</p>
<p>I feel very tired after dinner we have a puffent[?] lady in the shop. I go down markets note the Circus.</p>
