Theres been too much stuff   "Pinched" off this line lately."(There has been so many petty thefts of saddlery off our line lately that I have been to Head Quarters and complained and now the sentries on the the Staff and Signallers have had drastic orders on that subject myself .   " I am in charge of this line horses saddles men and all" I had been standing close up to my horse during all this dialogue and then I came out the sentry evidently recognizing me. The sentry  " Oh!
