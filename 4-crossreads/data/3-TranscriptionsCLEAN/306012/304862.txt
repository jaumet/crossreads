I was surprised in Turin & Milan - we do not associate red hair & blue eyes with this country.  The Romans are dark and the Napolitans darker still. Food seems fairly plentiful in hotels & restaurants although I noticed people lining up in food queues in different places (there is not too much bread & sugar available).  The Italian meal consists of fewer courses than the French but each is more plentiful.
