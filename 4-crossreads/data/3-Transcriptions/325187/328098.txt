<p>[Friday 22 December 1916]</p>
<p>El Arish   Paid all Ranks-<br/>Trawlers &amp; Gunboats came in this morning &amp; landed after sweeping up the mines.  The local Egyptians pretend they are glad to see us but they sent up 2 pigeons as soon as we entered.<br/>Infantry &amp; Guns arrive today but the 1st L H had the honor of being the first to enter the town.<br/>Already ships are arriving with horsefeed &amp; Rations.  The railway will follow.  I am back in my old job as Adjutant.<br/>The Anzac Division attack MAGDHABA tomorrow a stronghold of the Turks.</p>
