<p>Hqrs. &amp; the Commandant of the Camp has very little power over us thank goodness.</p>
<p>A couple of big drafts went out of here today &amp; not before its time.</p>
<p>20th.   Len is now Regimental Q. Master, here, so if he can hang on to it he has a fine job one of the very best, but it all depends how the Dr's mark him next examination.</p>
<p>Sunday this morning &amp; they are holding a Field Service just outside the Y.M.C.A. where I am penning this scribble, there is a piano going &amp; they are running through some of the old old, hymn's</p>
