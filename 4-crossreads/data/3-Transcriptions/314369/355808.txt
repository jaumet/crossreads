<p>5 April 1915<br/>Monday<br/>Easter Monday, Bank Holiday</p>
<p>The storm continues all day. It is not possible to clean the tents at times without goggles which can be got here very cheap<br/>In spite of it the Battalion go out w/- full compliment of transport for a trial run.<br/>Storm abates   &amp;  finally run out about 7pm. for which we are thankful</p>
<p>1915 April 6<br/>Tuesday<br/>Easter Tuesday</p>
<p>Turn out all our belongings   &amp;   give them a thorough shaking to get rid of the dust.<br/>The work of preparation goes on but no definite day for departure.</p>
