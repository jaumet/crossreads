<p>Minister of Defence<br/>Commonwealth of Australia</p>
<p>Personal</p>
<p>Melbourne 15th January, 1917</p>
<p>Dear Judge Ferguson</p>
<p>I desire to thank you for returning me the correspondence I sent you in regard to your son. I should like to congratulate you on the feelings of pride you must have derived from this indication of your son's spirit.</p>
<p>With kind regards</p>
<p>Yours sincerely,</p>
<p>G.F. Pearce</p>
<p>The Hon D.G. Ferguson<br/>Supreme Court<br/>Sydney, N.S. Wales.</p>
