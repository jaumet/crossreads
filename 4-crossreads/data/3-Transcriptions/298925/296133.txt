<p>You see a clump of trees in a hollow, and, looking closer you see numerous buildings with thatched roofs and large overhanging eaves. coming closer still you see it is a little village with 3 or 4 main streets, &amp; a church, which is generally used  as a school, the rest of the week.  The streets are in some cases not <strike>known</strike> named except the ones which lead from one village to another.  These are called by the name of the village they lead to.
