
Friday 1st June 1917Nr BouzincourtReveille 5.30amPut waggons in line. Breakfast 7am.Cleaning lines and harness cleaning till 12 noon.2 till 5pm Harness cleaning.After tea went for a swim in lake about 1 1/2 mls from our camp. (The first swim I have had since leaving Australia) (but not the first wash).
