March 17.  Still at Brulooze (Mt. Kemmel) Fritz putting over some big shells, some landing near railhead.  Two shells landed 200 ydsf rom our Camp, setting fire to two barns & just missing farmhouse.
Mar 27. Loading at 5 a.m. Orders to pack & be ready for early shift next day.
Everything packed except blankets and stowed on lorries 6  p.m. Orders to dump all boxes and spare kit.  Blankets & kitbag & equipment only allowed.
Mar 28 Loaded at 5 a.m. Kits & bags spread over the various lorries carrying rations. Left Brulooze at 9.30 trekking down through Steenwercke, Merville, Lillers, St. Venant, St. Pol to 1 mile outside Doullens.  Very cold bleek day and dust from proceeding lorries stung our eyes.  Lorries proceed to various brigades I being on one that went to Vauchelles, 12 kilometres from Doullens.  Unloaded, parked and slept on lorry for the night.  One Brigade had to retire two villages to dump rations.
March 29. Column stationed at Pommera.  Arrived back at Column at 11 a.m. Loading at Warlincourt. Camped in tents outside an estaminet and at back of a church.
March 30. Loading Warlincourt at 10 am.
March 31 Went to Belle Eglise to load at 10 a.m.
