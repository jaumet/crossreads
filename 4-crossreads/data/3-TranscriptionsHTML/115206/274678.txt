<p>April 1918<br/>Thursday 18</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours     D.R.<br/>At 6.30 am I received a call went across to Bonnay, Battery moved.<br/>Came home and had a quiet day.<br/>Went out on a run to Blaney Tronville and La Motte, during night <br/>Fritz tried a stunt against 41st Batt, - he certainly came a cropper.<br/>Played cards until midnight &ndash; won.</p>
