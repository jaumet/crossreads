
Friday 4 May 1917.
Everyonebody tired of general breakups.  
Reveille 7 p.m. for our unit only.  1st parade at 9 p.m.  
Made out "Will" forms, & did nothing in  general.  
Official word that we are to sail next week, & everyone in A.C.B.F. Amb. granted leave for week-end.  
Bed 10 pm. after getting some washing done.
