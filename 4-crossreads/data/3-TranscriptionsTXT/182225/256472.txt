 1916&nbsp;&nbsp; TUESDAY 19&nbsp; DEC. 
 Snowing early in morning &amp; freezing rest of the day. Out exercising horses in the morning by Stonehenge, &amp; cleaning harness ect in afternoon. Bill Hunter &amp; Trev Cox &amp; Self went down to the Abbey Hotel Amesbury &amp; had dinner with mother &amp; Struan. We had to walk back afterwards.
