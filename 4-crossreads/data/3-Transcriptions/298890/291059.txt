<p>26. Thursday<br/>Frosty morning but grey afterwards.  Miss H. &amp; I went down to Piccadilly to see President Wilson.  Great crowd so I missed Ethel &amp; had tea with Mrs. French &amp; the Sawyers in Club</p>
<p>27  Friday<br/>Worked so late had no dinner.  Went to see Ethel at Dysart Hotel at 8 &amp;  then for night with Miss H. &amp; Miss Newcomb</p>
<p>28 Saturday<br/>Worked all day &amp; got to Dysart Hotel just in time to have dinner with Ethel and went to bed early &amp; talked till past midnight.  E. had a cold &amp; snored so I cd. not sleep</p>
