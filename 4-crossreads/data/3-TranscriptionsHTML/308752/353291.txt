<p>AUSTRALIAN IMPERIAL FORCE.</p>
<p>Base Records Office Victoria Barracks.<br/>Melbourne 13th June, 1918.</p>
<p>Dear Sir,<br/>I have much pleasure in forwarding hereunder copy of extract from Four Supplement, No. 30476, to the "London Gazette," dated 11th January 1918, relating to the conspicuous services rendered by the undermentioned member of the Australian Imperial Force.</p>
<p>AWARDED THE MILITARY MEDAL.<br/>"HIS MAJESTY THE KING has been graciously pleased to approve of the award of the Military Medal for bravery in the field to the undermentioned:-<br/>No. 6618 PRIVATE L.W. COLLEY-PRIEST<br/>The above has been promulgated in "Commonwealth of Australia Gazette" No. 76 dated 23rd May, 1918.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>W. Mackintosh.<br/>Capt. for Major,<br/>Officer 1/c Base Records</p>
<p>Mr G.W. Colley-Priest.<br/>Sutherland Street,<br/>Neutral Bay, N..S.W.]</p>
