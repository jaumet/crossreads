<p>suddenly by loud reports of Fireing in the Air. I could hear several Machines overhead &amp; I went outside &amp; what a Sight. The Guns were booming all around in the Bush &amp; lights of shells exploding up above also a number of Search Lights 12 in all were scanning the Heavens everywhere looking for Fritz. I could hear several Bombs dropped in the vicinity. This went on for about &frac12; an hour making a great Sight, what with the Search lights, shells exploding in the Air, flashes of light from our Big Guns &amp; the Liquid Fire lighting up the heavens it reminded me of a Gigantic Fireworks display, gradually Fritz dissapeared &amp; things quietened down somewhat, but only temporary, I turned in again &amp; tried to sleep but was continually being aroused by shells exploding close to our Camp or the report of our Big Guns near by sending shells over to Fritz.
