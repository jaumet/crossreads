  
 8/1/18 Spandish embassy here today in interest of the French not to see us Heavy snow  Packets  overcoats 
 9/1/18 Heavy snow again this morning.  shovelled up again  Letter & Xmas card from Coombs  card from Mrs [indecipherable] & letter from Mother Flo & [indecipherable] dated 21/9/17 
 10/1/18 Another heavy snow fall last night & continued all day. 
 11/1/18 Rain last night very strong wind with same  Hellish cold this morning on parade  we were kept for fully 1/2 hour  Heavy snow storm about dinner time it did fall some  had to wait quite a while for tin stuff  beautiful game.  sun came out for a few minutes this afternoon  snow  it was freezing at one time & then thawing
