  
 June 25th "We drank the Libyan Sun to sleep We lit lamps which outburned Canopus  Oh! My life in Egypt, Oh! The dalliance and the wit! The flattery and the strife!" "Oh!!!" indeed. Well, despite the monotony & the general unpleasantness of war & conditions I suppose some day I shall look back with pleasure on my life in Egypt.
