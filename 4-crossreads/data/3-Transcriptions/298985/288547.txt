<p>Thursday <strike>4</strike>  5th<br/>Went to Corbie as escort. Had a swim in afternoon</p>
<p>Friday 6th<br/>Drill in forenoon nothing in particular rest of the time. There are a lot of black &amp; yellow wasps here they are very fond of jam &amp; as soon as this tin is opened are in to it &amp; on your bread. There seem to be more every day I have been very gentle with them but got to stings to day. Of course I jambed them by acident</p>
