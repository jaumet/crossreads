<p>again am very tired and sleepy we are in reserve to day rested till 12 had some dinner battle is in full swing enemy shells are falling all around a piece of one fell in where we are slept all afternoon made some cocoa in a fruit tin got some tea D.P. went back for blankets and more rations 7.35. returned at 8.45. eat a tin of fruit and we turned in terrible cold night<br/>Saturday April 21.4.17. stand to 4.A.M.  waited till 5 got all gear on got picks and shovels and went about 200 yards and dug trenches had breakfast 6AM went on digging the artillery</p>
