  
 B Troop A Squadron 1st A.L.H. Regt. Romani 24th Sept.16 
 Mrs. Vernon Hon.Sec. 1st. Light Horse Comforts Fund Moore Street Sydney 
 Dear Mrs Vernon I am writing to acknowledge with thanks the receipt of a parcel of 21 shirts, addressed to me. I distributed them among the boys and I can assure you they were highly appreciated. most of the other troops received similar parcels a few days after mine. Thanking you and your fellow workers With kind regards Yours Truly NJMEdwards. Lt. O.C. "B" Troop
