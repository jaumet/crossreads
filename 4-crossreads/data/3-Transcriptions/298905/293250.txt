<p>26th. Last night we had a fatigue party up in the firing line earthing up Convent Avenue" it is nearly finished, &amp; a good job has been made of it<br/>Before we went we were warned to take cover in the firing line as soon as our guns opened out for we were going to give them a taste of our gun's.<br/>After about 2 hour's work with pick &amp; shovel, over come the first shot, almost instantly, the whole place was lit up by the flash of our gun's, &amp; the roar was like a terrific windstorm magnified 100 times the whole earth shook &amp; trembled from the concussion of our hundreds of guns, it was a magnificent but awful spectacle the whole horizion was lit with purple, red, yellow, &amp; dark green flashe's as our shells burst in the</p>
