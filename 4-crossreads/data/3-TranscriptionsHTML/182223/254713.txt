<p>Monday 8 November 1915&nbsp;<br />Air Temperature ideal this moment 7 a.m.<br />Left hospital ward at 4 pm. Nearly O.K.&nbsp;<br />At dinner<br />Mess meeting after dinner&nbsp;<br />Walk with Henry Dr after dinner.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9 November 1915&nbsp;<br />As I look out of my window at the central dome of this&nbsp;great building &ndash; Palace Hotel &ndash; &amp; note its surroundings of architecture &amp; atmosphere, my mind thinks:- How much a good Architect can do to fit puny art into great Nature?&nbsp;&ndash;</p>
<p>Wednesday 10 November 1915&nbsp;<br />Met [Lieut.]&nbsp;Col. Nicholson in Col. Newmarch&#39;s office this morning:&nbsp;He has just come from Sydney, in charge of troops.&nbsp;<br />In Cairo for a little during the afternoon</p>
