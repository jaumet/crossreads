<p>The Destroyers &amp; Una are out for a 10 day stunt &amp; we have to guard!! Rabaul &ndash; Lucky we've wireless &ndash; I did that job &ndash; If I hadn't been there I don't think those sick wd have ever left the pier &ndash; I sent men out to collar transport in the shape of trucks &ndash; They came back with a hand cart &amp; a truck without a deck &ndash; Sailors are fools &ndash; The wheels of the handcart dropped thro the planks of the pier &amp; what damned good the bottomless truck was God knows &ndash; I told a few men to come with me &ndash; In five minutes I had seen a truck &ndash; &frac14; mile off &ndash; I told the men to bring it along &ndash; This they did because I'd told them to &amp; it was a definite job &ndash; I got my sick inboard with their 'ammicks &amp; off we went &ndash; Got to hospital &amp; discharged cargo &ndash; piled their 'ammicks in a corner &ndash; got my shore crowd together &amp; off I went to the Fantome &ndash; Fitz had asked me to lunch on some chickens he'd got &ndash; First chicken I've had &ndash; Jackson, Bond, F &amp; I enjoyed 2 chickens &amp; cold beer &ndash; Then we went to the Una &amp; jumped from the ship's side &amp;</p>
