  
 26/7/19 
 To my Wife 
 My pocket book, left hand breast pocket of my tunic contains 50 English (Bradburys) also please find Draft &pound:50 Anglo Egyptian Bank.  This latter being deposit on commission Field Ambulance picture to be painted after Govt. contract leaves me free. 
 In the event of my not being able to complete this picture the 50 (fifty pounds) must be returned to the Field Ambulance Office, Ref. Col. Croal [Croll], Romani Sand Cast Incident. 
 G.W. Lambert
