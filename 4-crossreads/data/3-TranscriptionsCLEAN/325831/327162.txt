Has Ritchie left yet? He was thinking of it before I left, & his last letter suggested that he might be coming over. Mitch was a pretty considerable surprise to me, & I wrote him a letter to the effect that he was a dam fool. It was no trouble to compose it as I only had to remember the things people used to say to me.
