<p>Cranbrook Cottage,<br/>Rose Bay</p>
<p>May 4</p>
<p>I have been twice to see old Keith Ferguson at the Empire Hospital.  He was badly wounded first in the arm &amp; later in the head.  He is still in bed but is very cheery &amp; looks splendid.  He is suffering a little</p>
