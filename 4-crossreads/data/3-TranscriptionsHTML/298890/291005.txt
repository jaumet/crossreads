<p>20. Thursday<br/>Wet again.  Crawled out of bed in night clothes &amp; no wash &ndash; went with Mrs. Fisher, to look at premises.  Then home &amp; to bed.  Struggled downstairs after a little milk.  Feeling depressed &amp; in need of food.  Oh for hot water &amp; a cleansing bath after [indecipherable]<br/>Full of pain in joints but nothing to eat in hse.  So struggled down to Minerva.  F.P. called up there.  Home &amp; to bed. F. P. called again.  Wrote letters &amp; had slight temp.</p>
<p>22. Saturday<br/>Maud [Walsoe] came before lunch &amp; took me home with her for tea.  I got home very tired.  Pollard also called.  Sometimes warm sometimes cloudy. A Norwegian Capt. who had been in the Belgian Corp. was at Walsoe's</p>
