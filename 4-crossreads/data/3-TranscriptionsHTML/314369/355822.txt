<p>3 May 1915</p>
<p>Put ship in state of defence against the German submarine supposed to be in these waters.<br/>All the men are allotted alarm post and the passages are to be used   <strike>as</strike>  to take the ammunition forward.<br/>My job is to report on the bridge for orders when the alarm is sounded</p>
<p>1915 May 4<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Arrive at Lemnos Is. (which is about 4 hours run from the part of Gallipoli at which we are to land) at daybreak it proves to be a large hilly Is. Covered with short green grass but no scrub or trees.<br/>Our arrival makes a total of about twelve troopships  &amp;  Ten ships of war.</p>
