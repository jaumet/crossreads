<p>[Friday 18 February 1916]</p>
<p>Last night fed at Arab house- Fried Catfish, Camel Cheese Maize bread Gramophone played Arabic songs- Pretty girls peeped thro' <u>barred</u> doors at us-  Tea in glasses filled 3 times- this is the drink &amp; Coffee.<br/>Chance to post so send with love</p>
