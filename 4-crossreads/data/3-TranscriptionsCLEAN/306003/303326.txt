  
 22-4-16 Sat 22nd Lt. Snow went into Minia yesterday for duty;  Lt Garbett & Wright went to the market at Hassan Pasha this morning accompanied by the interpreter & bought 45 fowls for the Squadrons dinner tomorrow;  Abdul Moolah brought 1.0.0 worth of eggs along for the Sqd again today 
 23-4-16 Sun 23rd I gave Abdul Moolah 1.0.0 to enable him to buy turkeys, potatoes etc for todays dinner, Abdul wanted to bring a table & chairs but we objected & we have been rather doubtful as to the success of the scheme & received quite a pleasant surprise when we saw how our dinner was dished up for us, we had ordered it for 1 oclock & at 12 oclock he showed up with a very large metal tray & we
