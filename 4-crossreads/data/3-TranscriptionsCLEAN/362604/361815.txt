 Well, I must go for a tour round shortly so as there is no news to give you other than that which Ellis wrote this afternoon, I will say Bon Soir.  I must write to Billy H., our Prime Minister as soon as I return from the rounds. 
 Best of Love We'll soon be returning, Harry.
