<p>August, 1916<br/>Tuesday 1<br/>Muster Parade 5.30 am.  strike tents 9 am stack Baggage &amp; clear up the old ground.<br/>Parade &amp; march to train at 4.30 pm.  train consists of open trucks<br/>Reach Gabbari dock, Alex at 1 am<br/>Embark on R.M.S. "Franconia"  All Sgts allotted 4 berth cabins.</p>
<p>Wednesday 2<br/>are woke up by steward at 7 am<br/>Good breakfast<br/>inspect ship  play cards till dinner<br/>Still got my little red Box "Boite Rouge"</p>
