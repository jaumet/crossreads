1918   January
24 THUR  Fine day. Put in for
leave to Behencourt. Went
into Au Progres in aft. Had
a good time
25 FRI  Home 7.am. Fine day
Rec. letters from Tense & P.C.
& letter from Pollie. Quiet
Writing at night.
26 SAT  Posted letter to Tense
Rec. pay 40 Fcs. Rec. letter
from Else Parker. Writing
at night. Sgt. Dowe went
to Paris. Very quiet
