<p>Sunday 14 October 1917.</p>
<p>On water fatigue.</p>
<p>Monday (continued)<br />down the other day. Pilot &amp; machine smashed to atoms. Pilot being unrecognizable but identification disc showed him to be a N.C.O. &amp; not an officer. His machine fell just inside our lines at Gaza.</p>
