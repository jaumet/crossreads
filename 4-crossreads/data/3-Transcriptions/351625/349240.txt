<p>At present we are doing a lot of night work so that we sleep nearly all day, day &amp; nigh, [night] week days &amp; Sundays all are the same to us &amp; we rarely know the day.  I am writing this under difficulties, a mob of men shaving &amp; writing, yapping all round me.  I think I have just about run myself dry as we are not allowed to say too much</p>
