<p>24/9/17<br/>We shifted camp from Ceastre to billets about 5 kilo's north of Steenvoorde and expect to join up the battn. again tomorrow. There are a good number of german airoplanes flying round here. But they are flying too high for our Antiaircraft guns to get a decent shot at them.</p>
