<p>here &amp; the staff deserve a great deal of credit for their efforts on our behalf.  Won 1st prize at whist drive on boxing night with a score of 176, also won a prize for quoits on Xmas night.</p>
<p>Sunday 30th to Tuesday 1.1.18<br/>Sunday very quiet, papers came through to proceed to Headquarters<br/>Monday 1st Nursing staff entertained Patients &amp; Pantry staff on New Years Night, the night of the 1st Jan; fine concert given &amp; supper during interval.  Fine opportunity to say good-by to all staff as practically the whole of them were there.</p>
<p>2.1.18 (Wednesday)<br/>Feel very miserable having to leave hospital.  Caught 9.30 Am</p>
