<p>1917 Wednesday 22 August</p>
<p>A record number of chest &amp; penetrating lung wounds in the ward. 40 I'd say nothing of the doz. sick. Busy no name for it. Run off our feet --- Jock &amp; self hasty meet &amp; walk along beach. Dot &amp; Carry one. <br/>Poor old Mc. Carrol died this morning. It seemed a crying shame to have such old men in the firing line he must have been 50. <br/>Meet Billy Seeming in Wimereux. Looking well.</p>
