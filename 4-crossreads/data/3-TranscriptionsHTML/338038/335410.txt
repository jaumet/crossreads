<p>III<br/>castle &ndash; The tower in the centre &amp; the castle keep are about 700 years old but the remainder of the building was erected within the last 100 years &ndash; The inside is beautifully finished with modern appliances &ndash; electric light &amp; everything that goes to make life comfortable &ndash; I don't know whether I could describe the place fairly!  I only know that I have never lived in such a grandly appointed place &amp; at the same time the owners were the very acme of everything one that one expects from British gentle folk &ndash; The entrance Hall is entered from a big courtyard &amp; contains many trophies of the chase such as antlers &amp; stags heads, others, salmon, and also a fairly good armoury with all conceivable kinds of ancient weapons &ndash; The drawing room was large but not particularly interesting except</p>
