No fires are allowed to be lit in Camp lines.<br/><u>Punishement</u>  No punishement of any kind may be awardet except by the Visitor or Camp Comandant or Officer in Charge.<br/>No Pris. shall be punished untill he has had an opportunity of hearing the Charge &amp; evidence &amp; of evidence against him &amp; of making his deffence.<br/>The Camp Comandant is empowered to award close confinement up to 14 days &amp; an Officer in Charge up to 3 days, during wich time the pris. is to be placed on No. 2 scale of diet for longer than 24 hours without the sanction of the Medical Officer of the Camp.  Any Period during wich the Pris. is released will count as a portion of the term of his imprisonment.<br/>Close Confines will be carried out in the Camp Cells.
