They all barracked me so I must be more punctual in future. After Breakfast I visited two friends in Hospital both were down with Sea Sickness, they had been in bed since the day after our departure, still they are slowly improving. The Hospital is situated under the Poop Deck & has 40 Cots provided for patients & everything is painted White & spick & span, there are 8 A.M.C. Orderlies who came from South Australia & as we had 12 A.M.C. boys from Western Australia they all joined up together. Dinner time came round & we had Roast Mutton
