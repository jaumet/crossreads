<p>Saturday 14th July 1917<br/>Aire<br/>Left Nedon 6am and arrived Aire 1pm. Very heavy rain about 9am. About noon we arrived at a small village just outside Aire. Billeted in a shed at a large farm. Had a swim in the canal after tea.</p>
<p>Sunday 15th<br/>Left Aire 4.30am and arrived outside Cassel about 9am. Had an A1 feed of eggs at the village.</p>
