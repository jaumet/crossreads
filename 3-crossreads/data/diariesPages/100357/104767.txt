B.088 18/5/15 Bathing. It has been brought to notice that men in considerable Nos are in the habit of bathing S of No 1 Pier. Though there is no objection to few men bathing from time to time as opp offers the men should not collect in any numbers as this almost invariably results in casualties due to the enemy opening Art. Fire. All ranks are to be informed accordingly.
