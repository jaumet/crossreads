 Saturday 28 April 1917 
 HESIA 
 Left Camp at 0530 i.e., 5.30 A.M. &amp; patrolled NO-MANS-LAND got shelled but home without loss &ndash; by sundown &ndash; each man carrying a bit of Firewood from some HUTS &amp; no doubt many Fleas. 
 Too cloudy to Helio. 
 I just overheard the following "If you want good Bully &amp; milk look for an Australian brand it beats Argentine Yankee or any other, but the wrappers are not so gay.
