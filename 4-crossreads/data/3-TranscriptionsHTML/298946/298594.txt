<p>Afternoon the main Parliament held its second service when several lady members spoke on behalf of their husbands who were busily equipped in discovering  a certain principle. Mr. Paterson who replied for the Government, was often interrupted with irrelevant &amp; facetious remarks, but despite an urgent desire  on the part of one lady to do something more than talk ( at which she shone enough Heaven knows!), he held his own by the aid of his winning smile.</p>
<p>Later he drowned his sorrows in the comparative hilarity  of a launch ride to town</p>
<p>4th   48th  [indecipherable] formally takes possession of Durban.</p>
