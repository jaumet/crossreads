<p>a heap of ruins, almost <strike>a</strike> piles of dust.  The whole ground particularly that part where the shock of battle  was strongest, was covered with barbed wire entanglements, a maze of trench systems, &amp; broken with thousands of shell holes of all sizes.  The whole ground still clearly retained the scars of the great struggle between the French &amp; Germans, when in 1916 it took the French months to capture this strong position</p>
