<p>over the frontline. Fritz Put a heavy barage on our line on the 8th and we all thought our number was up. Sixteen were wounded in our part of the trench. were releived that night by the 23rd Bn and came back to Supports It had been raining all the time we were in and we were wet through and covered in mud. We looked just like wild men for we had no shave or wash for over a week Stunt next morning which carried us</p>
