 1 Feb 17 At Birkenhead. Went into Camel &amp; Lairds dockyard basin. It was in this yard the Melbourne was built. We are tied up to another Light cruiser, the Attentive. 
 2 Feb 17 In Dockyard Basin 
 3 Feb 17 In Basin. Went ashore in Birkenhead &amp; went to Argyle Theatre. 
 4 Feb 17 [Sun] In Basin at Birkenhead. Heavy fall of snow during the night.
