I'm not boasting when I say that the Colonial was very popular in England, the English people almost held them up as patterns their free &amp; easy ways quite caught the fancy of the Old Country &amp; the majority of the boys were on their best behaviour while there, though they used to let themselves go when on leave in Cairo.
