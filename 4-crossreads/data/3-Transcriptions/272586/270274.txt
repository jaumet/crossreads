<p>I am not sorry I was not there to help. yesterday was an awful day. &mp; we could not see more than a few yards for dust. the trench diggers must have had a bad time I went up this morning &amp; watched the the other chaps clean out the sand which had blown in. we came home early.</p>
<p>All the boys have gone out again for an all night game. there is going to be a mimic battle the 2nd Batt being the attacking force. everything is going to be very up to date. I should like to be there. but I won't risk it in fact I don't know whether I won't get into a row for not being there. was supposed to see the doctor to see what he said about</p>
