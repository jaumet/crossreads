<p>(18)<br/>The next event of importance was our arrival at Newcastle on Tyne, crossing the Tyne. Here we delayed less than ten minutes for refreshments, but little could be observed from the station. Our next town was Durham, and the railway being rather elevated a fairly good view of the town could be obtained from the window and the beautiful cathedral of Durham on the opposite slope.</p>
<p>We only made a stay of a few minutes here but at the next town, York we stayed a little longer for refreshments, which refreshments nearly cost me my small amount of luggage which I left in the train while visiting the buffet. I was last to be attended to and was only able to catch the trains by jumping in one of the last carriages already almost out of the station whereas my luggage was in the front portion of the train.
