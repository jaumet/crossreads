  
 23rd November (Thursday) Making incinerator at A.D.S.  Watching 6" Naval Guns at work.  Another busy day in the air.  Fritz ventured a little too far but our anti aircraft guns firing 11 pounder shrapnel soon drove him off. 24th November (Friday) Preparing to move out to the line up to 1 p.m. then we commenced our tramp through mud rain and water.
