  
 Monday 14 August 1916 Wrote to Father (11).  Was enoculated for Cholora in afternoon. Heard that there are a couple of cases of Cholora in Tommy camp at Katia. 
 Tuesday 15 August 1916 Got eighteen letters in one bunch.  Three from home 6th June, 8th June, Father 8th July (7) and 5th July No. 6. From Vida 26 June, Mrs. Nicholl. Issued with a lot of backsheesh tucker from War Chest Fund.
