 But they will not forget me though we are far apart, And memories so dear to me Are ever in my heart And with our Gods good grace Some day across the foaming main I'll come to thee "Australia" And nee'r leave thee again. 
 18th. Was out this afternoon with a nice little girl, we went to the "Scala" for she wanted to see the "Honor System" screened it was very good &amp; we thoroughly enjoyed ourselves, after this we went in to a first class restaurant &amp; had afternoon tea, &amp; then I took her home. 
 In the morning I was out on a hunting expedition looking for something suitable for a wedding
