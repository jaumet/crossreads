<p>July 1915<br/>Thursday July 1st<br/>B Squadron sent out a trench digging party at 7.45.  Cpl Cheater &amp; 4 of us formed a covering party in front.  The Turks put several shells in amongst us but they did no damage.  Waygood went away sick.</p>
<p>Friday 2nd<br/>Cool windy day.  Was sapping in a tunnel in the afternoon &amp; put in a good solid 4 hours at it.  Had a swim at night.</p>
<p>Saturday 3rd<br/>Had a spell in the morning &amp; four hours trench digging in the afternoon.  It was fairly warm.  The Turks gave us a bit of a shelling &amp; we had 2 men wounded.</p>
<p>Sunday 4th<br/>Was on water fatigue all day.  It was a fairly easy job.  Had only to go the long distance to Shrapnel Gully once.  Posted letters to Mrs Blomfield &amp; Dorothy Blair.</p>
