 Monday 16 April 1917. 
 Up at 5 p.m. Caught troop train at 7 pm from Spencer St. 
 Arrived in camp at 11.45 a.m. 
 Was marched over to races in afternoon and then back to camp at 6.15 pm. 
 Tea in the dark at 6.30 p.m. 
  Wandered round doing nothing at the racecourse . Wrote a letter after tea &amp; turned in at 9.15. 
 Heard Pat Lynch has been wounded.
