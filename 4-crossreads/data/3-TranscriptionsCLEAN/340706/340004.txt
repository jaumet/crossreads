 27-1-18 Mon Went to Chepy our CM asked to be instructed walked out & back. Met Hutchings. Heavy fall of snow, went out in it & reached Scotson [indecipherable] like Father Christmas covered in snow. The fall is very heavy. Snow forms an excellent method of catching a drink. Yarn with Scotson re his visit & he promises to give me some shells 
 23-1-19 Tues Snow ball fighting in mess & when on way to company men lining both sides of roads behind hedges. Then I march to Hocquelus which was a snowball fight en route & snowball battle during rest time. In afternoon chatted with Scotson & then dinner sit round fire which we have at last in our mess room & then bed 
 29-1-19 Wed Battalion parade & search for culprit who had under threat of revolver
