<p>&amp; a couple of them are station owners. I think I mentioned Grove. He's a fine looking huge kid of nineteen with an English accent. He's English. Well as I said he knows Krantz Kloof, &amp; the Field clan, &amp; we address &amp; swear at each other in Kaffir. I believe we are getting another hamper &ndash; so Sunny Robinson says. We've gone through one already We got a bag of onions sent us to-night. Living like Lords</p>
