 Sunday 12 Walk round a few villages about made up of thatched cottages etc. &amp; pretty garden 
 Tuesday 14 At dentists one tooth out &amp; three filled which finishes the contract. 
 Wednesday 15 Have come the proverbial "gutser" &amp; am now marked as A3 meaning medically &amp; dentally fit for France. Move to another camp 
 Thursday 16 Move from command depot to Overseas Training Brigade from where infantry go on draft to France. We still have to go to our depot at Park House before crossing over. 
 Friday 17 Rehearsal for big review on Monday next 
 Saturday 18 Mess Orderly for guard &amp; detention a rotten job
