<p>November 1918.<br/>Wednesday 20</p>
<p>Hosptl. Sthall<br/>Afta examination by Capt Pigott, I made a tour of workshops and hosptl surroundings with Roy.<br/>Ran out to see Eve and stayed all night with a Mr Harris &ndash; some friend of Roys.<br/>I am agreeably surprised to see agile manner in which Roy gets about despite his physical incapacity.</p>
