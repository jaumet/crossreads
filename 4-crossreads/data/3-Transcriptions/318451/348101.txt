<p>Monday Apl. 2nd<br/>Went to Baths this morning.  Had Church parade this afternoon.  There was a splendid crowd of men, "Who sh. separate us from Love of God wh. is in Xt Jesus".</p>
<p>Tuesday 3<br/>Snowing heavily all night &amp; this morning.  Strong wind.  Everything white.  I went to Houplines this morning to the cocoa stall.  2 letters from Home.</p>
<p>Wednesday 4th<br/>Houplines again today.  British &amp; French pushing in on the Somme &amp; lower down.<br/>Class this evening, 20 present.  We had the sacrament at the class.  A splendid meeting.</p>
<p>Thursday 5th<br/>Beautiful fine day.  Wrote letters Home in morning.  Went to Batt. H.Q. afternoon.<br/>Crackenthorp &amp; his friend came in &amp; we had some games of chess.</p>
