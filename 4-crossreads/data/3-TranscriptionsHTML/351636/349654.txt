<p>The Centre<br/>92 Pitt St. Sydney<br/>August 17 &ndash; 1915</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Erwin,<br/>I find the War Chest is not able to give anymore, so I am enclosing a form for you to fill in for the Lord Mayors Fund.</p>
<p>Please add also a list of you debts and we shall send a letter of recommendation for you from the Centre.</p>
<p>I have pencilled in a few remarks which express what you want &ndash; Fill up the form fully.</p>
<p>I am<br/>Yours very truly<br/>Mary Booth<br/>P.S. hope you are very well</p>
