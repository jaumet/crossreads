
Wednesday 12 December 1917Off duty 2-5, went town & met S. Cuthbert, shopped fitted at Cicurel's, met Cobs Groppi's but no time for tea.  Went Anglo Egyptian Bank & cashed ch. for 5-0-0, i.e., Pt. 500.On duty with S. Wormald 5-8.  We hear of fall of Jerusalem on 8th inst. and many prisoners taken, but we retreat in France & Roumania concludes an armistace so one ally has left us.
