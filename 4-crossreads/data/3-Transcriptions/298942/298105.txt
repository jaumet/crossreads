<p>a German shell case, an alabaster ornament, ivory bound prayer book, &amp; a plentiful stock of note paper &amp; exercise books (from school).</p>
<p>I noticed the Mayor of Bailleul had opened an office in a farmhouse, &amp; there we found stationary, ink, coal rations cards etc., all in one zig zag heap as a result of a shell in the roof.</p>
<p>Went via church &amp; <strike>passed</strike> past an estaminet with a French flag still flying from a lower window (evidently left by inhabitants). to another road out &amp; back</p>
