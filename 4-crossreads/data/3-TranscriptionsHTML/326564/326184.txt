<p>3rd.  Another wild night was spent in the boozer last night, the Jock stopped with us all day &amp; the whole bunch went out again. Price &amp; I went up &amp; saw the fun later on, on our arrival the room had been cleared &amp; dancing was in full swing, even the Madame was dancing &amp; stepping like a two year old oh she was some night &amp; it finished up by the crowd singing &amp; dancing on the corner by the Y.M.C.A. here a great crowd collected &amp; there was some more</p>
