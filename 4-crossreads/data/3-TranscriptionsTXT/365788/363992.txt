 16 Sept 16 My dearest Irene Just a hurried scrawl to say I am well &amp; we are out of the Trenches again. Thank you for two nice parcels you sent Chocolate, Books, Washer, Tooth Brush in one &amp; Choc Hankeys Books in other. You are very good to me old girl. I am kept pretty busy in my new role but find it interesting. How are you? Got my letter I hope. How well the Button Day did! Am sorry to hear Mrs Yead is so poorly.
