Dawn reconescense has it that Fritz has evacuated his trenches and retreated for a couple of miles. Still his shells continue to come over. About 8pm our artillery opened out a bombardment of Fritz's line which lasted long after midnight.27th Cool  & misty. Business is very quiet but during the night  our guns opened out.
