<p>15 &ndash; she is Mrs Criechtly &ndash; her husband's ship was shot at.<br/>Nearly all the nurses I know are over here &ndash; either married or at the war. Prince Alfred is well represented. Sisters Kirkaldie Welman and Childe &ndash; Freeman who you saw with bad leg at [indecipherable], an English girl &ndash; at Malta Sisters McGreggor, McKenzie &amp; Blackmore at [indecipherable] and ever so many in war office employed here besides about 250 or more in Egypt Australian nurses and that doesn't count us. I have seen cousin Florence Mrs Pollock. I went to see Humphreys Military Hospital at Hampstead and went to tea with him.</p>
