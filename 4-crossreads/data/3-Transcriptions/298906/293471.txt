<p>wherever these bombs dropped there was a heavy coating of yellowish powder.<br/>Met a lot of the boys "at Waterloo" &amp; we returned together reaching Amesbury at 11 oclock that night, &amp; by midnight I was in bed thoroughly tired out.<br/>10th. Medical examination this morning after returning from the School, have not been on parade yet am fixing up my cubicle.<br/>Yesterday I met quite a number of chaps whom I knew, a couple of them were out of my old platoon one of them had his leg off.<br/>Saw Stewart this morning he went back to Australia just after Lone Pine" &amp; has come back again he used to be in old H. Coy.</p>
