<p>Australian Branch<br/>Red Cross<br/>B.R.C.S.</p>
<p>Bulford<br/>10/3/18</p>
<p>Dear Dad,</p>
<p>I have received your letters of the 23.12.17, 30.12.17 &amp; 6.1.18.  I will answer those questions you have asked before I go on to my own experiences.  You speak of heat in Aussie.  It has been hot here to-day or hot for England.  It has made everyone very dopey &amp; lethargic &amp; we can hardly crawl about.  You know the feeling when a hot day comes along very early in the Spring.  George had a horrid experience with the pony.  Poor Lily had a terrible long wait.  Why did George not have another go at riding.  Poor Lily must have had about a three hour wait.  I am glad that you got the pony back O.K.  Someone got a bargain on the old</p>
