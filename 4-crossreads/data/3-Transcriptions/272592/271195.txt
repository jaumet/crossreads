<p>shot struck the detonator &amp; off she went, the concussion from her knocked the 6 men flat on their backs &amp; some of them had their clothing all torn by it, but the funny part of it was to come for our artillery hearing the bomb go off &amp; not knowing the strength of it, thought it was one of Fritzies, so they at once turned their guns loose &amp; gave him a bad 5 minutes &amp; all the while Fritz was innocent &amp; hadn't even fired a "pip squeak".</p>
