<p>Sunday 27th:  Left School. Rejoined Bn at Cantun Corner near Neuve Eglise<br/>Thursday 31st:  Entrained at Neuve Eglise for Lumbres. Marched from Lumbres to Coulomby.<br/>Saturday Feb 2nd:  School re-assembled at Bayenghem.<br/>Sat 16th:  Rejoined Bn.<br/>Thursday 28th:  Escort to Boulogne.<br/>Friday 1st March:  Returned.<br/>Monday 4th:  Entrained at Lumbres; detrained at Steenwerck. Marched to Kortepyp Camp near Neuve Eglise.<br/>Tuesday 5th:  Marched to le Rossignol camp.</p>
