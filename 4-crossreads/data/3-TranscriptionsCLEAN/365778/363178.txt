
Friday 2/3/17
Attack on MALT TRENCH at 3 am a great success by Bn, and Bde 2 M.G 4 T.M. and 75 prisoners out casualties 5 Officers 90 O.R.
Saturday 3/3/17
The Bn was relieved by Reid & we went out to Reserve in 26th Avenue & Crumpet Road.
Sunday 4/3/17
In accordance with the wishes of the G.O.C. went & took over again from Reid to readjust the line.
Monday 5/3/17
Took over all brigade, Front part from Norrie and made all dispositions. Had letter from Doff.
