<p>not feeling too well. Stomach trouble again.</p>
<p>Sunday 5th Sept<br/>A bit better to-day. Had to get a return of equipment for Battery &amp; went round to gun detachments. Fairly cool but a nice day. Saw Jack Fowles.</p>
<p>Monday 6th Sept<br/>A nice day &ndash; Fairly busy re equipment. It is lovely to [look] out over the ocean as the breeze comes wafting over its rippling water.</p>
<p>Tuesday 7th Sept<br/>A little shelling otherwise quiet. Preparing for winter etc</p>
