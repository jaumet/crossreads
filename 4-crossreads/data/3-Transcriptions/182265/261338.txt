<p>Entry Friday 7th July.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Entertained Col. P. McGlynn at Guest Night yesterday evening, heard of General Holmes&#39; death in France. This evening Mrs Padden&#39;s Bristol Concert Party gave a good Concert in Y.M.C.A Hall. Our Corporals also gave an Evening to which all officers were invited. I was sorry I was unable to attend but&nbsp;being Mess Secretary I had to arrange entertainment of Concert party at supper.</p>
<p>Wrote to Norman this evening.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Reg arrived here on Tuesday afternoon &amp; spent the night with me. We went to the Debate, of which I am President, in the Y.M.C.A. hut. The debate was &quot;A policy for Repatriation of Soldiers.&quot; It was of a fairly high standard, McQueen (13th Bn) from No4 Command Depot making the best speech. I gave Reg &pound;5 for his holiday which he intends to spend in Durham &amp; Scotland. He left by train about midday for London.</p>
