 It was raining hard here yesterday, so instead of going out on parade I kept them in the lines & discussed the advisability of getting a 12th Reins Sports Club under way. We elected a committee on the spot, & being the O.C. I started the ball rolling by giving a 1; then subscriptions of money, boxing gloves, bats, foot balls etc started pouring in from all directions, & at each successive donation there was a heartier cheer, till the Major sent down a message asking what the H-l I meant by allowing the men to cheer in the lines - so it had to be stopped.
