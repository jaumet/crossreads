  
 Monday 28 August 1916 Nothing much doing. Put up notice at Canteen offering 10/- reward for camera. Wrote to Mother (14). 
 Tuesday 29 August 1916 Taube round and was heavilly bombarded & reported to be brought down at Ogratina. All Section down Mediterranian for swim.  Got 10 letters and 7 papers.  Letter No. 8 July 17th from home. Put on horse picquet in evening.
