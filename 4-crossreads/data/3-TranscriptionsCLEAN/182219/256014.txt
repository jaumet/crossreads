The entire Bdge left El Burg at noon & moved of in section column the great serpent, over rocky defiles & plain made a very impressive spectacle. The distant travelled to our nearest camp was twelve miles - a long track of slipping, slimy mud, the Bdge drew into camp just before dark & are to move pff again tomorrow at 9 am. 
 Jan 3rd 
 We left the 3rd Bdge camp at 11 am. With a lumber for our baggage with horses for Capt H & myself & after a ten mile ride we arrived back at Dieran where we again took up our residence for a few days.
