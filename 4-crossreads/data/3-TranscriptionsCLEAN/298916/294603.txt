Numbers of military big guns admired & criticised the A13 & fussed around like a number of blowflies - all noise & no use. After a great deal of Persuasion I managed to get Teddy out in time for breakfast, & Teddy's troubles began then. Instead of finding his place at the table, he found it, & others filled with the military big guns who only wanted a "snatch" for breakfast. If that was only a "snatch", no wonder Teddy felt concerned about his breakfast, for a "grab" would have ended all hopes of a bit of breakfast. Of course, as one "head" finished his "snatch" he would "Excuse me Sir rather busy" etc & would pass the word to the next hungry head & a few moments later he would appear at the saloon door & begin his apologies thus:- "Er-er- Excuse me" - er-er - "Good morning major" er - "having a little breakfast"? Er - "I am going up town to get mine now" er-er. No! no! from the major in the act of placing himself outside some fish. Sit down. Captain doesn't mind er er "do you?"
