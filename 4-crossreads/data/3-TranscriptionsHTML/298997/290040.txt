<p>We are working, now, on Brandenmolen and towards Hill 60.  It is "a rough joint.  This morning, after filling my petrol tins at the water pipes near the advanced dressing station, I was having a rest when the shells started to come over.  Some piece from the first made holes in my petrol tins, which probably saved my legs.  I took cover at the "trot".</p>
<p>[The following passage addressed to the recipient of the diary]<br/>Tell me if you didnt receive all of these. I suspect interference with my mail and I can't always get a man going to Blighty to post it there.  My O.C. &amp; I never meet each other without having a thrust or so; and I know he hates the idea of my writing back to Australia &ndash; especially since I went over him to the Censor.</p>
