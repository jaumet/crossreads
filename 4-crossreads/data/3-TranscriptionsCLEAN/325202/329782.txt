 While in there waiting to be loaded saw an exciting aeroplane action nearly overhead. A German plane which our "anti-guns" were firing at for all they were worth & at the same time two of our planes pursuing it. 
 We heard a machine gun rattle overhead but couldn't see the finish as they got in between us & the sun. 
 A lovely day - one could almost see the crops & the hedges growing in the warm sunshine after all the rain we have had
