 POST CARD Dear Mrs. Vernon On behalf of the 20th Rfcts of the 1st A.L.H. and myself  in particular, I wish to thank you all for the comforts as supplied to us on the Malwa. I had the pleasure years ago of fighting alongside of Major Vernon with the NSW Lancers in S Africa. Kind regards to him and yourself and your willing workers. Yrs faithfully  S.R. Fallick  Sgt. 1st  ALH
