  
 Friday 15/2/18  Left by Train from Charing Cross, reached Boulogne at 5pm stayed night at Hotel De Paris. 
 Saturday 16/2/18. Reached Battalion at Henneveaux at noon by car- went to Brigade Dinner Party- Australian nurses present. 
 Sunday 17/2/18  Church parade- visited "D" Coy's billets- all Corp much dispersed in area. 
 Monday 18/2/18  Went to Bde HQrs to lecture by Wisdom on French Army- afternoon inspection by W very good- selected best Platoon. Wrote Doff
