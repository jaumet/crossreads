Friday 18 April 1919No sun at all today. Am on Harfleur picquet but am not going there as I have a meet on in town. Very trying day. Wrote a few letters &amp; went into town. Met the girl &amp; went for a walk to St Addresse [Adresse]Saturday 19 April 1919Very muggy again. A very trying day. Went to Montivilliers for a walk. Hotel de Ville again tonight. A few fights by the Canadians in the street. Otherwise everything very quiet.
