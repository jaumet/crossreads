<p>Friday Oct 5<br/>Usual daily visit by Jacko in planes to see what was doing. Was told today that all Turkish planes were manned by German Officers &amp; at present were superior to ours. Believe another Ausy Flying Squadron is being formed to fix Jacko.<br/>Saturday Oct 6<br/>Quite cool this morning 2ND Brigade sport in progress Tom came over from 1ST Brigade &amp; we had a good day at sports. What is wrong with the mail Sunday Oct 7<br/>Moving to Bulah on 17th All water sterilised on account of Bilharzia</p>
