<p>1915<br/>June 18 Fri.<br/>Attended weekly meeting of 1st Cairo Scouts &amp; met a couple of S.W. London Scoutmasters who are serving in the R.A.M.C. &ndash; Also saw S.M. Way of Melbourne who is a Y.M.C.A. representative with the L. Horse.<br/>Surg. Gen .Baptie inspected Hosp. to-day.</p>
