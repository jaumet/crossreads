<p>24th  Saw a Statue of <u>Boadicia</u> represented in a chariot driving 2 horses with the following inscription: &ndash; Boadicea, Queen of Icene Who died A.D. 61 after leading her people against the Roman Invaders. On the axles are knives about 15? long evidently for cutting people down.</p>
