<p>Friday 26th Oct. same weather, but turned out better at mid-day, we were warned this morning that we were to have a kit inspection prior to going to Salisbury, an hour later runners were sent out telling us that the Salisbury affair was cancelled rumours that we are all on Q boat.</p>
<p>Saturday 27 Lovely day on cooks fatigue, "Snowy" &amp; I went to Weymouth in afternoon a small whale was stranded in low water on the beach, was roped by fisherman &amp; was towed away, it was exhausted, had tea, waited nearly an hour for it then went to the pictures (Vaudeville) walked home lovely night. Heavy frost this morning &amp; yesterday.</p>
<p>Sunday lovely day, church parade, went for a walk to Upwey Wishing Well, after tea went for a long walk with "Snowy".</p>
<p>Monday very cold showery weather, O and P boat being fixed up, got some apples tonight</p>
<p>Tuesday lovely day, was paid, got some apples went to the pictures, rainy looking afternoon</p>
