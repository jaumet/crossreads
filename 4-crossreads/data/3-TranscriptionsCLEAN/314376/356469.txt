Fremicourt was just an ordinary French town. As usual the church had been destroyed by the Germans. An act of vandalism was the cutting down of the fruit trees by the Germans to prevent our troops from gaining anything from them.
Our next point was a railway cutting. We had a fairly long march to it on the way passing some artillery. Just close to the railway cutting was an old Chateau which had been partially demolished by the Germans.
