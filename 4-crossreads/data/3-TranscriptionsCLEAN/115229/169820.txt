This job lasted for a month, during which time I saw some most ghastly looking wounds being dressed. Christmas day was spent here and I tell you we enjoyed a fine dinner. Roast turkey and ham with boiled vegetables for first course, plum-pudding and custart second, and fruit, nuts and sweets to finish up with.
