
June 1918Tuesday 4
Battery W.Lines.  near Pont NoyellesWeather glorious. Mother's birthday today. I wonder  how my dearest friend is. Went along to see John during the day.About 9.30 am, we received a surprise, in the form of anan aeroplane of the fighting type armed with threemachine guns (named Sopworth Dolphin, flown by a young Indian Prince Lt Welinkar .Twas was a dandylittle machine. Our major entertained the pilot during the day and as the machine was not repaired by sunset thepilot does not leave until themorning.
