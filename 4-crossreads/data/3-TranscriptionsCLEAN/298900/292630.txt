  
 Saturday 16th Feb     Decided to have a look at our posts & Frontline today.   Set off about 11 oclock & first called at the A.D.S. at Wijtschaete.   Passing this point we had to give our names to a sentry as a check is kept of everyone past this point.   The first post we came to after passing through the remains of the village was Verne R.A.P.    There were a few of our bearers & we ran into our Major & a couple of Captains.  Then we crossed country, passing several fill boxes of Fritzs now used by our chaps & came to another post, Cabaret corner.   Picking up a Lance Jack as a guide here we then went on to the most advanced R.A.P. at Ravine wood.   We here visited the Frontline and were well repaid for our trouble.
