  
 24. - Thursday - Aug - Bkfst. & sandwitch and some tea. Continued saping in reliefs 2 men working forward on 3 parts of line. With 30 men, set out screens in pairs right across line each 2 in shellholes working outwards toward next pair only 3 to 5 yds apart. all did good work indeed & line was completely connected up by 5am.  though only.   Recvd issue & good rations later. 1/2 party rested morning & all hands pressed on from 2pm. and had trench deep enough to move along in safety.
