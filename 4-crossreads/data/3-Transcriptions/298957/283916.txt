<p>20th Jan (Thursday)<br/>Stretcher carrying over long distances across the sand. Trial trip over the new railway branch line to Serapeum. An engine pushing one carriage. O.C. &amp; others went to Ismailia to select our new camping ground there. 500 camels arrived with Indian transport from Ismailia.</p>
