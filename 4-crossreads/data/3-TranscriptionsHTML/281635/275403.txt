<p>&amp; biscuits in shop, drank beer each in an estaminet &amp; then steak &amp; eggs at restaurant. Left 7pm &amp; arrived back 8.10pm. Received 2 letters from Muriel &amp; 1 from Mrs Brown. Warned for trip to coast tomorrow for surf bathing. Turned out very fine in afternoon. Saw Chinese Coolies &amp; Portuguese troops in Lumbres<br/>17/9/17 Reveille 5am &amp; left 9am in 3 ton motor lorries for the coast. Travelled via Drionville, Senlecques, Desvres, Wirwignes, Cremarest, &amp; Marquise to Tardinghen near Cape Gris-Nez arriving 12.30pm. After lunch walked to Wissant &amp; saw remains of German submarine recently stranded there.</p>
