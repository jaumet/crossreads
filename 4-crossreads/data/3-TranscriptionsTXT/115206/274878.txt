 November 1918. Monday 4 
 Hosptl. Birmingham Good news again today. Austria has signed an armistice, the terms of which are not published. Had the pleasure of spending all day out of bed, but feel pretty seedy tonight. Grub improved just a little in quantity. Weather wet and miserable.  Received au petite correspondence pour la jeune fille, Simonn&egrave; au Busey-la-Dours &ndash; oui, bon neuvelle pour le guerre
