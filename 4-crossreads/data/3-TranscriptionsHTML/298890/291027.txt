<p>5 Thursday<br/>Fine day.  wrote &amp; studied shorthand.  Bessie Allin, sister of Norman of Beecham Co. sang Beautifully for us in drawing room &amp; a Miss Woledge accompanied.</p>
<p>6 Friday<br/>Fine day spent hours on beach listening to band.  Walked along sands before supper</p>
<p>7 Saturday<br/>Showry Day.  Stuck closely to writing &amp; shorthand went for walk with Nurse Grey &amp; Bessie Allin before supper</p>
