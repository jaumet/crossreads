Aug 1 (Tuesday) Bonnevillearrived here from Senlis yesterday afternoon about 15 miles back, a very hot day which the horses found trying, Passed through the villages of Contay & Herissart en route. Various units of the 1st Division are here waiting for reinforcements to make up their strength again. We have to go 11/2 miles to water our horses (that means 3 miles each journey 3 times a day)
