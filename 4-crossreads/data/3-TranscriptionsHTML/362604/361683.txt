<p>I have no idea what she is like.  According to Her letters I should think, she was a little slight smooth tongued Young Lady quiet like Elsie Miles, but in build like Chris Dobson.  The Picture of old "Freshy" is very good to have.  We will be pleased to have a few snaps of the old Place.  I am pleased to hear that Top is O.K. &amp; Milton still doing His bit.  He had rather a nice trip but was game to come at all.  I would not trouble drill if I were you.  Let the younger ones do it.
