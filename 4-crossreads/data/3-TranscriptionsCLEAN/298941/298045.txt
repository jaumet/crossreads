
Sat 27 ArilMisty, inclined to rain, on which account the kite balloon has again not been hoisted. In the morning we had a route march, & some practice in bandaging broken thighs.In the afternoon I intended to go to the church in the little village, to play the organ, as, before the shelling, Capt Griese had spoken to me about it.
