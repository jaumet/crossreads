The first of these army scourges is unfortunately fairly prevalent, but the latter more rare. being simply a plain stretcher bearer my work was usually on the customary fatigues. One might think this drearisome, but not so. The worker takes good care he is not fatigued, and also has his fun dodging the W.O. a chap named Mr Mobbs and any obnoxious NCO's .The NCO's are, however, on the whole a fine lot and always have been.</p>
