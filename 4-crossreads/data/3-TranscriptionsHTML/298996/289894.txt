<p>&amp; walked to Zeitoun from there to <strike>Heler</strike> Mathella it was a grand walk through the farms the native looked at us as though they had not seen a soldier before we saw the native making a fire of chalf this is how it was done they formed a ring with with the long hay anb then sat on the patent cutter this is the style of their cutter. It is a box afair with three rowlers in same each of same have about 9 Iron cutter adjust like a plate edgeway. it is drawn by a mule or Donkeys</p>
