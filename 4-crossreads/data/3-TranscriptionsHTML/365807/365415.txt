<p>[The page has the coat of arms of Keble College Oxford  and the letterhead:</p>
<p>"C" Company,<br/>No. 4 Officer Cadet Battn.,<br/>Keble College,<br/>Oxford]<br/>14th May.</p>
<p>Dear Judge,</p>
<p>Have just received your letter of 13th. March and by the same mail one from Keith. It was stiff luck getting such a nasty crack but he seems O.K. now. It has taken me a very long time to trace him and unfortunately I got the news that he was in hospital in London the day I got back from 4 days leave in the self same</p>
