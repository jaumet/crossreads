
<p>SATURDAY, OCTOBER 18, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; very hot. Tickle &amp; I mustered</p>
<p>Middle paddock wethers and</p>
<p>put the rams out of them in the</p>
<p>Cow paddock &amp; 452 wethers in the</p>
<p>Boundary and 378 in 4D. The</p>
<p>rest are in Mountain ewes.</p>
<p>Mustered Mountain ewes &amp; lambs</p>
<p>&amp; put them in bottom wheat</p>
<p>paddock.</p>
<p>Alan came home in morning.</p>
