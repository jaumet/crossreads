  
 W.T. Signal 
 Receiving Ship - Flag 
 Transmitting Ship - Protector 
 Date - 13.9.14 
 Time of Despatch or receipt - 4.11 pm 
 Pass to first ship arriving Herbertshohe  Tell O.C. troops Protector abreast Road between Kaba Kaul & Herbertshohe as directed just west. 
 Beresford only asked for maxim & field stand & for Protector to proceed to position overlooking Road between KabaKaul & Herbertshohe.  Shall I stay here heavy firing going on round Herbertshohe.  1605
