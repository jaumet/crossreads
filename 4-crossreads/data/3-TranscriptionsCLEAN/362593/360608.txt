
Tuesday 1 October 1918Recco, Rayak, Damascus.Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 5 h.Our troops in Damascus 3000.  Turks escaping to the North bombed and gunned, dropped 8 bombs, fired 500 rounds.For 13 days, 55 h. 25 m., bombs 111, rounds fired 10,500.[From opposite page] Have done record flying for Sqdn.
Wednesday 2 October 1918Escort to Recco Rayak.Pilot Ltd. McGuinness, in air 3 h. 15 m., fired 350 rounds.  Turks evacuated Rayak.Landed at Haifa at new aerodrome.  A lovely camp right by the sea.
