 January, 1916 Tuesday 4 No news yet as to our probable movements.  Put in the afternoon repairing a watch in dad's workshop.  It rains all afternoon &amp; night &amp; the water penetrates through our flimsey wooden houses &amp; makes things very unpleasant. 
 Wednesday 5 Write a letter home &amp; generally waste the morning.  Go on  a short route march with the Coy in the afternoon.  March through Matariah &amp; see one of the famous obelisks a small replica of Cleopatra's Needle.
