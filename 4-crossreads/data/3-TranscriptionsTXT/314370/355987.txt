
February, 1916Friday 25Early morning we do a little practice in Gun mounting & get our gun & Tripod ready for the standard testsAfter breakfast we examine all squads at DrillAfter dinner inspection & in the evening I went into Heliopolis for a walk.
Saturday 26First thing I heard this morning was "Cookhouse" at 8 am.After breakfast we had a muster parade & issue of certificates.After dinner all men for Ismalia & Tel-el-Kebir march outAt night I went to pictures w/- dad
