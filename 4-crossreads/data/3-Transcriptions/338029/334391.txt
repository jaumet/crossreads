<p>Sunday 12th<br/>Out to Hosp. again to complete arrangements, p.m. went to Shubra and called on Nuns, they took Gwennie, Bob &amp; Lewis to Luna Park, spent a very pleasant evening with them, the best holiday they ever had, sent Xmas letters to home at night.</p>
<p>Monday 13th<br/>Caught early train back and just as I left Cairo a Taube flew over for the first time and dropped bombs on Cairo, they killed fourteen people, 1 soldier and wounded a lot, it terrified the nigs, arrived El-Ferdan, no horse for me so had to walk, when Tommy officer asked me to ride his horse home I did so with celerity.  At camp had busy time making up for lost time both with my work &amp; mess.</p>
