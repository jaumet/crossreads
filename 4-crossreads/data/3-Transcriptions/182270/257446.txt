
<p>WEDNESDAY, MAY 14, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; cloudy, but S.E wind.</p>
<p>Eric came out with Considine</p>
<p>&amp; inspected the 4D cattle, he</p>
<p>bought 30 of them. Saw Campbell</p>
<p>the rabbit inspector round the</p>
<p>bottom end in the morning.</p>
<p>Went round Piccadilly &amp; the</p>
<p>Strand Scrub in the afternoon.</p>
