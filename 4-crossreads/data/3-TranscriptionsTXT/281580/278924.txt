18th Decr /17
At Headquarters morning, and arranged to leave Cairo by train tomorrow evening.  Afterwards visited that Treasury of the Ancient Egypt, the Museum of Egyptian Antiquities. This glorious treasure house is worth spending a month in. Here lie many of the secrets that have enabled us to peer into the lives and customs of the ancient Egyptians, and to unravel the mysteries of obscure antiquity. The jewelroom is especially fine.
