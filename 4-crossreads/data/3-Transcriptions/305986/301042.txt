<p>And then theres all that saddle soap<br/>And soap of common kind<br/>You know they both are waste of cash<br/>To any frugal mind<br/>The cheese that we are issued with<br/>When things are as I hope,<br/>Would do to wash our hands &amp; face<br/>And do away with soap.</p>
<p>Then why waste all that ration jam,<br/>With fancy names galore,<br/>When axle grease would do for both<br/>And save a little more,<br/>Or put some poison in it too<br/>And add a little fat &ndash;<br/>And we could use it very well<br/>In killing off the chat.</p>
