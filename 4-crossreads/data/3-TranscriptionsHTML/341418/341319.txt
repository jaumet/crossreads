<p>Assiyut<br/>Which I think is up to the time of writing the prettiest place at yet we have yet touched. Assiyut boasts of a barrage smaller than the world famous one of Assouan, of course, but by no means despicable..<br/>The European quarter of the town is quite distinct  &amp;   apart from that of the natives: some of the houses are very fine  &amp;  the gardens are a picture.<br/>The outstanding feature of the place is its atmosphere of American mission  &amp;  religion.<br/>The first night I was there I was asked to attend a meeting :  &amp;  a little further on a beetle browned square headed individual asked me whether I would "Keer to cum along to chourch".<br/>They have an excellent mission school</p>
