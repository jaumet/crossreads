 Thursday 22nd Was deepening trenches both shifts.  Things were very quiet today except for a bit of artillery fire. 
 Friday 23rd Very warm day.  Finished deepening our firing line.  The monitor put in a few heavy shells into the Turks in front of us & a cruiser further south got on to a party of Turks & gave them a rough time.  Got a letter from mother.
