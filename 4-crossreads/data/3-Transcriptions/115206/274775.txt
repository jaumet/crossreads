<p>July 1918<br/>Wednesday 24</p>
<p>Frechencourt.<br/>Weather showery, rode for a little exercise to Orville via Sarton, Rainches, Toutencourt, Contay, Bavelincourt Behencourt and Frenchencourt.<br/>Met Adrianne at Sarton and had a very exciting afternoon received the usual.<br/>Arrived home at 11pm after a very wet ride. Received a letter from Bully Clement, stating he was due to sail in a weeks time for Australia. answered same but doubt whether he will receive same.</p>
