 Oct 1917 Thursday 11. Went to the University this morning &amp; the art Gallery in the afternoon.   Went to a Revue called Goodbye this evening Friday 12th Visited a munitions works Saturday 13th Went to a Soccer match Sunday 14th Visited Lock Lomond   I would very much like to stop here.  The top of ald Ben Lomond as just got his winter garb on Monday 15th Went to Beardmores ship yards seen what will be the largest submarine afloat &amp; a lot of other interesting This this place covers a mile &amp; half on the bank of the Clyde.
