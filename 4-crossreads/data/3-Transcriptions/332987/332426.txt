<p>30-6-1918<br/>Fine day.  456 rounds of ammunition to Bty.  Half holiday, this evening, Sunday.  One Officer and 5 men to go to rest camp Boulogne.</p>
<p>1-7-1918<br/>Limbers up to gun position, ready for a move.  Six loads of ammunition there also.  Enemy planes over during the night wounded 2 Officers in 1st A.S.C.  The town of St. Omer bombed.  Our planes busy.  Saw 27 in one squadron, coming to aerodrome, and even then Fritz sneaked over our line.  The 6th Bty. moving their guns.  408, 8 inch shells just on right</p>
