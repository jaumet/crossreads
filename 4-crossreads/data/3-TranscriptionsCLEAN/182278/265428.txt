  
 Thursday 18.7.18  On invitation had supper with M rs  Lathbury  very interesting old lady. 
 Friday 19.7.18  Went to London for try on at tailors, had dinner at Club & then went to "Bing Boys on Broadway" 
 Saturday 20.7.18  Went to A.I.F  H.Qrs  no news of Ship yet, returned to Farm in afternoon 
 Sunday 21.7.18.  Exercised "Mayfair"  had a long talk with Daniel Edwards who is peculiarly English.
