Monday Sept 10th
Monasterie : reveille 6.30 am : we were on M.O.s parade in morn : went out to see the "stunt" ground after dinner received our instructions etc
Tuesday Sept 11th
Monasterie : reveille 3 am : fell in marched to the ground : went through the "stunt" all the Bgde were there : it ought to be a good one the boys seem in good heart so Fritz ought to get a towelling up : specialist parade after dinner
Wednesday Sept 12th
Monasterie : reveille 5 am : packed up moved off about 8 am : billeted at Winnezeele : good clean up then bed
Thursday Sept 13th
Winnezeele : reveille 4.30 am : handed in our packs with spare gear : moved off through Steenvoorde :
