<p>very windy all day and cold  went to Pictures  turned in 10.P.M.<br/>Tuesday Dec. 17.12.18.<br/>turned out. 6.30.   it is a holiday here to day   Break. 8. AM  fell in 8.30 for a Church Parade  nothing doing  wrote to Josie alma aggie walter maud and dorrie  went to ismalia to night  had a drink or so  got some Post cards  cought train and returned to camp   the town was quiet  turned in 9.P.M.  had Parcel from Dorrie</p>
