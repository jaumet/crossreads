<p>May 21st<br/>Physical drill, breakfast.  4 hrs. Boat positions &amp; roll call respective boats.  Musketry standing .trigger pressing till 11, 11-12. muscle ex. &amp; Fire disciplins.</p>
<p>Afternoon given make &amp; mend, washing, etc;  Washed shirt &amp; 2 of socks  sports held on boat deck, Musketry reading after tea.  Signaling.  Beautiful weather beginning to feel the heat even from sun rise.  Mileage 333.  Course N.N.W.</p>
