<p>On Saturday mornings we play for the troops so that they will learn to march.  The boys think the world of us &amp; recognise that the band is the soul of the battalion.</p>
<p>26th  Just played a draft away of 50 to Dinton for Rolleston &amp; Codford, there to have 3 months training as signallers before proceeding overseas</p>
<p>28th  Lt Mackenzie O.C. 'D' Co who has been on Gallipoli was applied to for 2 days leave by the greatest outlaw in the battalion</p>
