<p>King George and Queen Mary Victoria League Club.<br/>For men of the Oversea Forces.</p>
<p>Telephone:  Gerrard 6470</p>
<p>16, Regent Street, Waterloo Place.<br/>July 11th 1916</p>
<p>Dear old Smugly Ug,<br/>I hope this does not find you as this leave me &ndash; yawning my head off.<br/>Tis my night in at the Club and I'm that dead sleepy I don't know what to do with myself.<br/>I think the work harder than ever every day &ndash; it suits me except I never can get enough sleep.<br/>The getting home near</p>
