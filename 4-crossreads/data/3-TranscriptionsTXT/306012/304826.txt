
Etaples is not a very interesting place, especially in winter.  On the outskirts of the small town are British Hospitals, base camps, Y.M.C.A. huts & canteens;  also several American, Canadian & New Zealand hospitals & camps.  There are plenty of W.A.A.C. girls in their brown uniforms in the place as well as 120 navy-blue clad English women who are driving motor ambulances in the area.  (Note - Australians get on pretty well with the abovementioned.)Yesterday & to-day I have been running round the villages with a motor ambulance, collecting sick from the Units of our Division, and taking them to Etaples hospitals.
