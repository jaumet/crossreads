<p>Friday Sept. 7.9.17</p>
<p>turned out 5.30 A.M. fed up and went for a swim had Breakfast cleaned up.  saddled up went on outpost to sweet water Canal.  returned to Ballah 12 A.M. fed up had dinner rested all the afternoon fed up 5 P.M. had tea.  went on outpost 6.30 P.M. nothing doing turned in.</p>
