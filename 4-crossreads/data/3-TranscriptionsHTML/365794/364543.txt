<p>Tuesday 22 [21] May 1917 [1918]<br/>Rose 10 a.m. packed once again for Bulkeley, tipped Gyppos (Pt. 5 each), to report to M.O. Monday next.  Ambulance to No. 9 Conval. 2.30 p.m., waited &frac34; hr. for Miss Alderson to allow me go soom, tea 4 p.m., rules to be again read, town 4.30 p.m.  Glorious sunset.</p>
