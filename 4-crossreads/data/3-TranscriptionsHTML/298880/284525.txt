<p>3rd and 4th class. Most of us were put in 2nd class. We did a bit of flag wagging had a couple of lectures but learnt nothing So much for the first day. After school hours we went for a stroll (a couple of us) through the village of Zurftpeene [Oultersteene]<br/>8th <strike>July</strike> August</p>
<p>Did a bit of flag work again and <strike>a</strike> had a short simple lecture about electricity.</p>
<p>9th <strike>July</strike> August</p>
<p>Fine day. We were taken per motor lorry into Hazebrouck to one of the hospitals where change of clean clothing was given us and we had a hot bath. We got back to school about 2.30 PM. Did a bit of compass work till 5 P.M. I had to lead a few men to a certain spot per compass. I struck a hedge</p>
