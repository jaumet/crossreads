<p>inside secured our seats &amp; I engaged Ma with the latest news from N.S. While Charley was making the pace.<br/>The show was good, all was well &amp; we arrived back at Coffee Palace with 2d. Charley tells me her name is Dulcie &amp; he intends coming to Melbourne next week if we do not go away. He is sending home for money. He has gone stone mad on this girl.<br/>Tuesday  April 24<br/>Returned to camp by 7 AM train from Melbourne arriving about noon Wednesday April 25<br/>Semaphore signalling commonly known as scarecrow drill<br/>Thursday April 26<br/>Training for sports to be held at Seymour tomorrow Anzac Day Charlie tells me Dulcie &amp; Ma coming up tomorrow wants me to look after Ma</p>
