<p>Note. [indecipherable]<br/>[indecipherable] At Wesley Hut<br/>Collins:  Thomas<br/>A distressing affair is reported of two of our number.  One of our heavier operator friends blandly disappointed himself at an at home, was by Eva introduced as Mr[indecipherable].  Hearing one the first &amp; squabble, our friend understood that he was being introduced to something, <strike>and</strike> helped himself, and before any of the horrified family could intervene, had drunk off two pints of pawpaw water.</p>
