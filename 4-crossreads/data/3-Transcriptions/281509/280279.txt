<p>26 February 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>First aid bandaging over in paddock.  Dress for 11.30 parade. Reinforcements called out for picket-duty for weekend. Protest &amp; parade before O. C.  No go. Get down to Ashfield at 2 pm.  Call at Hewletts  aft. tea. then on to Summer Hill stay to tea. on to MacT's with Bill &amp; Tim who were on their way to see the latter's sister who is ill in asylum.  Stay all night at Petersham. Take Whitehouse.</p>
