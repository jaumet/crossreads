<p>Arrived at Albany at 2pm. left again at 5pm not allowed off the ship</p>
<p>10th/<br/>Raining. Passed Freemantle sea freshened.</p>
<p>11/<br/>Church Parade. Several good fights on board.</p>
<p>13/<br/>Thirteen days out at sea. Man died at sea he was out of the 4th of the 17 Batt.Beural Service at 5pm.</p>
<p>14/<br/>Twenty four of us on gaurd looking for submarines<br/>I lost my bayonet overboard came off gaurd at 8.30</p>
<p>15/<br/>Were all paid 14/-<br/>Saw a big shark. Sea very calm.</p>
<p>17/<br/>Went to church again.</p>
