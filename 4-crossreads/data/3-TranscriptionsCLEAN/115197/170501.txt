 April - 1919 
   
  April 1st  
 Light hearts & good fellowship.  Glorious weather which improves as we go South. 
 A long day in the train - Incident concerning a Chinamen - Arrive at Harfleur (Le Havre) at midnight.  Sleep in the train. 
  April 2nd  
 Disentrain at 6 a.m.  March to A.G.B.D.  Meet old friends - Norman Frettell.  Billy Dick.  Disappointment through not seeing Herb
