<p>The creature guns I have written of and the twelve inch monster are shredding the air to ribbands all the time; and the flight of their shells is like the rushing of overhead waters, without any intermission; when suddenly these new additions to the clamour assault the war with a splitting clearness.</p>
<p>We all know in a moment what is doing.</p>
<p>The anti-air-craft guns are busy.<br/>Everyone looks skyward<br/>"Where are they" this is a question someone asks in a general sort of way alluding to the enemy aeroplanes which must be somewhere near.<br/>"There, see, two of Fritz's taubes"<br/>"Where?"_ I can see the last shot anywhere near that?"<br/>"No" says Jim "Not there, but see away up higher to the right"<br/>"Ah yes, there one of them"</p>
