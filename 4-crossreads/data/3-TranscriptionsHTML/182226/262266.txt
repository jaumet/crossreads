<p>Wednesday 1 September, 1915</p>
<p>Nothing much doing. Camp routine.</p>
<p>While on watch from 4 to 6 p.m. I witnessed a lively little bomb throwing match between our chaps &amp; the Turks at Quinns Post. Our chaps won on points but I don&#39;t think much damage was done on either side.</p>
