<p>June 1918<br/>Sunday 23</p>
<p>On the road, Flexicourt.<br/>Weather fine, with no rain whatever. Plenty of dust though passed through several decent villages.<br/>Pulled in to a nice camp at 2 pm.<br/>Smithy on police duty .Walked with [indecipherable] Smithy and Gunn to [indecipherable] ,had some very decent eufs, viewed some bon mamselles .<br/>Fritz aeroplane bombed village near by,, rather large bombs.</p>
