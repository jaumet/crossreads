March 3rd
March rains & winds - Present months sailings to Australia clear the air a little.  Jeumont as uninteresting as ever.  The "Daily Mail" affords a little diversion.  More letters - and "Pilgrim's Progress concluded.
March 4th
The "Mardi-Gras" or "Shrove Tuesday" - Pre-war celebrations cancelled.  Prepare new items.  Write final letters to friends
