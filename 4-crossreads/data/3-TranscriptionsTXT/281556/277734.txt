 Mr Anderson caught train for London. changed at Manchester &amp; on G.W.R. booked seats in a Dining Car where we did well getting into town about 10pm. &amp; from there went to Surbiton getting there midnight staying at Railway Hotel till morning. 
 22nd. Monday Visited the Grandmothers &amp; stayed for bkfst going out after, taking photos along the Thames. after lunch went to Bushy Park &amp; Hampton Court both very pretty &amp; interesting. many snaps of same. evening at &quot;Duneden&quot; music &amp; returned to hotel to sleep. 
 23rd Tuesday Left Surbiton by train 9am
