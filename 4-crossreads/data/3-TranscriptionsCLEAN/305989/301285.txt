  
 Sunday 2 December 1917 Grazing in morning.  Nothing doing.  Picquet at night.  Wrote some letters. 
 Monday 3 December 1917 Grazing in morning, quiet & windy day. 
 Tuesday 4 December 1917 No grazing, dubbined up saddles instead.  Got news of move.  Left camp at 12 & proceeded to Jaffa. 
 Wednesday 5 December 1917 Arrived at Jaffa 2.30 a.m. & stood to arms 4 & changed position of camp at daylight, stayed the day under easy gaze of Jacko.  Good orchestral concert at night. 
 Thursday 6 December 1917 Still at Jaffa, usual routine, camped in orange orchard. 
 Friday 7 December 1917 Rained after "stand to" and kept going all day off & on;  left Jaffa at 5 proceeded back to Ayunkara, arriving about 9.30 raining. 
 Saturday 8 November 1917 Raining in early morning and continuing through the morning.  Jerusalem fell.
