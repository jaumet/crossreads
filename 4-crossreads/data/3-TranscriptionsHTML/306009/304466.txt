<p>1915<br/>Oct. 11 Mon.<br/>Walked into Cairo after tea to purchase a couple of sundries required.<br/>Went through Gezira passed the gardens, along the Nile bank, &amp; then crossed the River by Kasr-el-Nil bridge, passed the Barracks &amp; continued straight in to the city. Came out through Boulac by the tram.</p>
