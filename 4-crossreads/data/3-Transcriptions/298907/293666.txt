<p>It was very close &amp; hot yesterday afternoon &amp; <strike>close &amp;</strike> about 7 oclock it started to rain lightly, this had the effect of clearing the atmosphere, so this morning it is nice &amp; fresh though a little cloudy</p>
<p>13th.  Sunday &amp; we met the girls for the last time for a week at least for they won't be able to get out worse luck on account of their being so busy, we had a bonza time went boating again &amp; then from the reservoir down to the Botanical Gardens where we put the rest of the afternoon in among the lovely flowers</p>
