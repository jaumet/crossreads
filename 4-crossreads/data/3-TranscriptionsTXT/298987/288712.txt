Monday 5thSame old working party same old work
Tuesday 6thThe job is getting cut out Hard to find anything to keep going on now. Have a very good little mate does not lofe like most of them his brother has the VC
Wednesday 7thFound work today Had to cart muck about 150 yds in a wheel barrow most uninteresting or the in local terms a "Gutser"
Thursday 8thStill the Wheelbarrow Dinner was light so tried the local "Salvo" (Salvation Army Hut) No dout they are very good better in many ways than the YM The ones who run them are more suited to the work & take a greater interest in it But I am not running the YM down don't know what we would do without them
