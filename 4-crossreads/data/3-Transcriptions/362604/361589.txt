<p>Australian Red Cross</p>
<p>Bulford<br/>2/2/18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>Your letter of 16.12.17 has told me of your good health &amp; good spirits.  You enquire what kind of work I was doing in other camps.  I was only giving evidence on courts-martial on Patients who had crimes &amp; who had escaped from Hospital.  It is all finished now.  I have not been to one for some months now.</p>
<p>I think that you would know Minnie Burford.  I knew Her at once but of course I had seen her of late years.</p>
