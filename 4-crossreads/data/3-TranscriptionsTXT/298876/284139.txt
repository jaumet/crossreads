 Sunday 27/2/16 
 Very Cool &ndash; a Bit Rough. It was so quiet you would never dream that over 1500 men are on board. A passing Cruiser at night honoured us with her flashlights. Very pretty looking over the railing at phosphorous &ndash; in great quantitys tonight. I notice a lot of stars that I have never seen before &amp; much more brilliant than most of ours. The Southern Cross has disappeared to view. 
 Monday 28/2/16 
 Rather warm &ndash; a lot calmer &ndash; Passed several large Boats. Passed Socotra Islands in the forenoon &ndash; Very high &amp; barren. I don't think they are inhabited.
