 Tuesday 13th  Chief item of the day was a memorial service held late in the afternoon for Earl Kitchener.  To those who could hear the padre it was very impressive the point being made of this great success through concentration.  "Whatsoever a man putteth his hand to that let him do with all his might"  The secret of success of many. 
 Wednesday 14th The Divisional Sports were held at Moaska Camp a distance of 5 miles from Ferry Post.  The competitors did the journey the night before & we set off at 4.15 A.M. as they commenced at 6. A.M. Very few of the men attended from this camp owing to the early hour & the march but it was worth the trouble.  The performances were high class both man & horse & the [indecipherable] out of the various waggons etc first class.  Coming back we had a spell for a snack in Ismailia & reached camp again about midday.
