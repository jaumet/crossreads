AUGUST   WEDNESDAY 2    1916
Fine & hot. Out signalling in the morning & afternoon with helio & flags. Warren went up to the NCO school at Larkhill in evening. Bill & Jack & Self went into Salisbury in evening & had tea with Mother at the Country Hotel. She is very comfortable there  I got Linda's first letter today also the photos, they were sent to Albany. Also got one of the 1st June and one from Mother sent back from Australia
