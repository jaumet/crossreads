 Regis. Had some afternoon tea in there, then caught 4.30 pm train to Melcombe Regis Stn, Weymouth. Went to YMCA on the promenade &amp; sat in front of fire for time then walked back to camp, arriving about 6.30 pm. Very Cold. On way to Portland saw a torpedo factory of Whiteheads, &amp; on way back to Weymouth saw old ruins of a castle or residence of Henry 8th. At Portland also saw over a dozen large tanks, like gasometers, containing oil fuel for the Navy. 
 18/11/18 Recd letter from Uncle Charlie. 
 19/11/18 Recd letter from Dick Lusc, also 2 parcels from Muriel, 1 containing 1 pr Sox, Housewife [sewing kit], and 2 bxs Old Gold Chocolate, &amp; other contg Cake, Fruit, Sweets, Sugar, T.Paste, Soap, Coffee &amp; Chocolate. Not so cold. 
 20/11/18 After tea strolled into Weymouth. 
 21/11/18 Changed some of my French money at YMCA tonight. 
 22/11/18 After tea went to pictures in YMCA at Westham Camp. A Charlie Chaplin picture was shown. Beautiful sunshine day, not nearly so cold. 
 23/11/18 Wrote to Uncle Charlie. Put in application to be deleted from D 35 list. After tea went into Weymouth. 
 24/11/18 Sunday Was paraded to camp commandant re deletion from roll of D35 &ndash; he is recommending same. Put in another application to return via America at own expense, as last application was lost in Orderly Room. Raining this evening. Wrote home XX 
 25/11/18 Not nearly so cold. After tea went to Westham YMCA. 
 26/11/18 After tea saw a conjuring show at Westham YMCA. Raining today.
