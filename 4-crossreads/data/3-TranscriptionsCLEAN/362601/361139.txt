  
 5 January, Sunday 
 W Go down Town & to flat with Medicine. Go around to YMCA, see Chappie & Sallie but only stop a few minutes. Go down Rue Paris twice & then Home by long route. Try to write but too interested in Tales of Old Times at 39 Gen. 
 Wrote Lily. 
 6 January, Monday 
 F Recd letter from W.H. Hughes. We fill in all papers but pending move to Bulford they are not signed. Little "Bo Peep" at Cinema afterwards. I have trouble to get a dog for show. It is a great treat & I fall in Love with Bo-Peep who is glorious.
