31/7/19 Cape Town
spent the afternoon watching the niggers and gambling. The evening shades on Table Mountain were specially beautiful, a white cloud cascading down part of the slopes like a waterfall. Later only a small lot of cloud rested on the mountain and a good idea of its "Table" formation could be obtained. After dark the view of Capetown by night was memorable. The shore was a mass of bright twinkling lights spangling the rising slopes and much resembling the Bay of Naples by night. The concert party tonight gave an excellent program of music and songs. Just as I was sitting down to supper after it a deputation from all troop decks headed by one Finn put in an appearance
