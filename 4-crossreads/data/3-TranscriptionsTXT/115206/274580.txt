
January 10Thursday 10
At BeutinDick and Wattie to Montreuil, to repair magneto. Report having had a good time.Sir Douglas Haig's Hdqrs at above town, 6 kilos from here.Plenty of "Waccs" in village close by, numbers of fellows have made their acquaintances and pay frequent visits to their camp at La CotolierieWent for the Dr. at 9.30pm one of the pretty bad. Rode some 15 kilos before I found him.
