Saturday 7.10.16
Fritz has been rather quiet for the past two days. Twenty-ninth Btn, raided unsuccessfully last night, subsequently we had a number of casualties through us.
Sunday 8.10.16
Received more letters from home. Very quiet on the front line in this sector. Met Young Gascoygne from Hunter's Hill yesterday, had quite a long yarn to him. Very heavy Australian casualty lists published in the Home papers of late. Church
