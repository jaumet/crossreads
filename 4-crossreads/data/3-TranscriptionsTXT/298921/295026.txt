
February, 1915Monday 22Scouting & advanced workJust received Days & Mothers letter of the 10th January. Be sure & address me in future 1st Light Horse Regiment 1st Light Horse Brigade New Zealand & Australian Division Egypt.Was very glad to hear your [indecipherable] news & to know all well - I am A.1 but lighter I am glad to say camped for dinner along side of a Harem I tried to see the inmates - but they are very shy, Donkeys, [indecipherable], & a Camel appeared to be in the Hall.
