They are known locally as "Campsheen" which means 50., signifying a space of 50 days during which these winds are liable to occur.  Luckily the weather afterwards is invariably pleasant.
Friday 14th April.Working on the cookhouse range the campsheen still continuing  making things very unpleasant.  Quite a cool change but nevertheless I had a dip in the canal.  Not feeling too good inside.
Saturday 15thMore brickwork during morning still getting up early reveille 5 oclock but we have the afternoon free.  Feeling very off colour.
Sunday 16th April
