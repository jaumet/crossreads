 March,1915 Wednesday 10 Did musketry today at Abassia Range &amp; shot well, the Dust &amp; heat are getting worse &ndash; This country is very rough on your skin being so dry only 2&frac12;" per annum yet the natives celebrate the time when the winter is over &amp; the warm weather begins &ndash; We are still uncertain of our destination, but all will be glad to leave Egypt now that the Sarroceo winds blow daily &amp; the heat is like the Back country.
