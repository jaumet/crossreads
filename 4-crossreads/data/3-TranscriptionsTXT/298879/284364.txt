 April 1915 Thursday 23  Weather  still  cool  Several fresh transports and destroyers have arrived. One of the destroyers shows signs of a recent encounter. Many Australian troops  are  were on one destroyer. Evidently practicing getting on and off 
 Friday 23  No  change in  weather . Seaplane from one of the warships very busy. It has been flying about all day at a great pace. I with Nick Carter was mess orderly for the day.
