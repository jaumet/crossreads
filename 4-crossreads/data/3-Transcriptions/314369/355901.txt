<p>8 October 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Make myself at home in a new camp. am in a room with 4 other Sergts one of whom was on the old Seang Choon.</p>
<p>1915 October 9<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Nothing to do  &amp;  all day to do it in<br/>Pictures at night<br/>A Mr Bradley of the American mission gives a very good discourse on practical religion</p>
