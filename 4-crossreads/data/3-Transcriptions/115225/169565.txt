<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>May 20<u><sup>th</sup></u>&nbsp;&nbsp;We went to Ailly-S.S. for petrol &amp; oil for the park, Doc. came with us to get it &amp; coming&nbsp;back we had a few drinks of point blank. Doc &amp; Frank had a drop too much with the result that I took the load off whilst Frank propounded to Doc on the subject of a corporal lending a hand when there was no need. We went down &amp; had a swim in the Somme at noon, the water was cold &amp; the stream swift -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>May 21<u><sup>st</sup></u>&nbsp;&nbsp;We went out on Supplies to Ailly.S.S. &amp;&nbsp;Fr&eacute;chencourt. We generally stopped at Mme Bully Beef&#39;s Joint in the Boulevard in Amiens for a drink - lovely day, We had a swim down in a pool near the river it forms a backwater &amp; was a nice depth for diving &amp; quite big enough for a decent swim -&nbsp;several Fritz planes came over late in the evening with bombs &amp; dropped them about two kilometres&nbsp;<s>nea</s>&nbsp;away.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>May 22<u><sup>nd</sup></u>&nbsp;&nbsp;We went two trips to Ailly-S.S. for Petrol etc for the Park &amp; then went to Amiens &amp; took three loads of furniture</p>
