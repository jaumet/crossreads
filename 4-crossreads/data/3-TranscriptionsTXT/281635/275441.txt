
17/1/18 Recd letter from Dick Luscombe.18/1/18 Very mild temperature. Wrote to Uncle Fred. Went up to top of Napoleon's tower but could not see across to England on account of the haze. 19/1/18 Recd letters from Guild & Partridge. Still mild.21/1/18 Read letter from Uncle Will & answered same. Sent parcel to Australia last Thursday.23/1/18 Recd letter from home & answered same.24/1/18 Wrote to Bob Clarke. Marked No Four at the inspection. Sold revolver for 15 francs to the barman
