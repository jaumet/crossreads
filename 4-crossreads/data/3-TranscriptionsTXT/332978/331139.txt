 60 September 13th doing in a years time I don't know 
 Wrote P.C. to  Bert  Kate also enclosed 3 photos &ndash; 1 for Bert, K A &amp; B &amp; Home &ndash; 
 Boycott on wine raised today &amp; needless to say there was a colossal rush for wine with somewhat disastrous results later on &ndash; Played Bridge 
 September 14th Slept well &ndash; walk for officers today &ndash; 
 Recd letter from Aunt Sally &amp; PC from Van Der Zeyde advising that he is  forwarding a lot of books to me from camp [indecipherable] 
 Interviewed adjt today about our exchange &ndash; He is certain that it will be effected this month &amp; is writing today to [indecipherable] any information &ndash; 
 There is likely to be a civil war in Russia The Commdr in Chief has been  sacked  relieved of his command because he was asking too much of the Govt, &amp; the Govt attach a lot of blame on him for the great failures of the Russian Army. He has refused to hand over documents to his successor &amp; is now collecting an army to march on St Petersburg. The Govt is preparing for a planned evacuation of St Petersburg, as it is likely to be threatened by the Germans in the near future The situation is indeed critical, &amp; no further assistance can ever by expected from the Russians
