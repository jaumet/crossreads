 26 Dec 16 Anchored at Rosyth. We had a concert tonight among the ships company. 
 27 Dec 16 Got under weigh at 1.30 pm &amp; proceeded to sea. Turned round &amp; returned to Rosyth in First watch &amp; coaled ship. 
 28 Dec 16 Anchored at Rosyth 
 29 Dec 16 Got under weigh at 3.30 pm &amp; proceeded to sea in company with HMAS Sydney &amp; between 10 &amp; 20 Destroyers. 
 30 Dec 16 Patrolling in North Sea 
 31 Dec 16 [another year of war over] Returned with Sydney &amp; Destroyers &amp; anchored at Rosyth. Coaled &amp; oiled ship.
