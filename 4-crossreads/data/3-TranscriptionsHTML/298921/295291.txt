<p>November, 1915<br/>Sunday 28<br/>The Beach yielded a tin of Benzine last night, Tommy [indecipherable] my Batman is a Champion [indecipherable] I am wondering how it will <u>go</u> in a Primus Stove, it was really washed up.<br/>The trenches &amp; Saps are in a frightful state of mud &amp; the mules have difficulty in climbing the hill, with 10 gallons water on each side of Pack which Pack with Brass Plate is used as draft harness. <strike>Will post this [indecipherable] knowing the future. Love to all</strike><br/>I am pleased to say I wear 3 stars now for Captain.</p>
