<p>Edinburgh</p>
<p>[22 Sun]<br/>Met Fred [Meckif?] in Princess St, took him home for tea.</p>
<p>[23 Mon]<br/>Left Edinburgh (Waverley) 7 pm. for London.</p>
<p>[24 Tues]   <u>LONDON</u></p>
<p>Arr London 7 am  Spent the rest of the day in London.</p>
