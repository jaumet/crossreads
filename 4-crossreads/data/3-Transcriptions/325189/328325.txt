<p>mangosteens &amp; coconuts.  Before we sailed I sampled all of them.  I don' t think much of the quality of the fruit.  The bananas were sold first for 2/- &amp; then for 1/- a bundle &amp; a half a stalk full.  After tea there was practically a riot aboard because the Lt. Col. would not allow us to go ashore.  At length he said he would see the authorities ashore once more &amp; try to get us permission to land.  It appears that the 2nd Contingent went and painted the town red.  A week after the transports had sailed soldiers were being found &ndash; some in the native quarters</p>
