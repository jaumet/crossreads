<p>February<br/>5th.  Our route lay Charleroi- Mons- Vallenciennes- Douai- Arras- Albert- Amiens- Paris.  Y.M.C.A. to-night, Hotel Ostende near Opera.<br/>6th  Change quarters to Grand Hotel de la Malte, rus de Richeleu, which is very decent.  With Morris Briggs. <br/> This afternoon Louvre, Concorde &amp; Champs Elysees.  To-night at Opera Comique hearing "Tales from Hoffmann."<br/>7th   The day in Latin Quarter.
