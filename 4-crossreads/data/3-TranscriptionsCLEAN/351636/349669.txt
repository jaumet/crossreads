
The Centre for Soldiers Wives & Mothers92B Pitt St Sydney22.10.15
Dear Mr. LaytonI am sending Mrs. Sullivan to you to see if you can give her some help.
She is the wife of a Reservist of the R. Irish - & her allowance has been cut down 7/- per wk. & is short of any money to go on with till paid again. on Nov 1st.
Yours sincerelyE. H. Bennie -
