Sunday 2 April 1916Went to Kasr-el-Nil [also spelt Qasr al-Nil] Gardens &amp; Bridge.  Met Yankee girls.  Tea at Brit. Sold. Club at Esbekiah.  Met Said up the Waazaa.  Billy Bagnall &amp; all watched the Dance, Vin, Ellis, Bill &amp; I.
Monday 3 April 1916Nancy Cohen, H. Hey, Nettie, Home, Doris.Joined the Cricket Club &amp; started Hot Plasters.
Tuesday 4 April 1916L. Porman, Home.  2 m. issued 4 pkts. Cigarettes.  Dave Niness gave me 4 pkts.  Billy Bagnall &amp; I played Euchre, Crib &amp; Casino while Ellis, Billy &amp; Dave played Draughts.
Wednesday 5 April 1916Went for a tour round the Mouski.  Ellis buys Blouse, I buy Gold Ash Tray.  Eric &amp; I buy oranges &amp; dates.
Thursday 6 April 1916Ellis, Billy &amp; I played draughts.  Night Wardmaster.  Shifted man from Boob to 4 Sydney 12 midnuit, much language.
Friday 7 April 1916Morris' rabbit drive.  Tried to learn French verbs but am still trying.  Dreamt of J. Mather again, 3rd time.
Saturday 8 April 1916More Smallpox, more rabbit drives.  Bread throwing stops.  No leave.  All Patients vaccinated.
