<p>REPORT NIGHT 24/25 Aug<br/>D. Coy 12th BATT.</p>
<p>Events of Night<br/>Hostile party seen at 2215 working on ridge behind SNIPER RIDGE and KNIFEBACK. Machine gun was placed on parapet and opened fire at 2400. Sniping continued throughout remainder of night to harass enemy.</p>
<p>Work done<br/>Work continued throughout night on L4</p>
<p>Casualties NIL</p>
<p>Ammunition spent 220 rounds</p>
<p>Bombs Nil</p>
<p>Patrols<br/>Advised of one working party and one Patrol. Both of 9th Btn</p>
