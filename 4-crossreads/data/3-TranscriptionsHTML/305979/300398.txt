<p>whether going to church or etc.<br/>Our vessel is a fine one, 15.000 tons and doing about 12 knots, slow but sure.<br/>She was wrecked some years ago and lost half-of herself on the rocks and all of her stern had to be built again.<br/>From our position in the life-boat we can see her almost from head to tail and right proud we feel of her grand dimensions, more proud are we of all the brave boys aboard.</p>
