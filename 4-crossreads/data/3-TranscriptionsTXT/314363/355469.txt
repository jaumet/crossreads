
Wytchaete Mar. 17th 18Left this morning for leave to England, second leave. Was taken from Kemmel to Bailliue by motor lorry. had a very rough trip to Calais. Shall arrive in England tomorrow afternoon.Landed on Mar. 19th 18 Left Calais about 4 pm arriving in Dover about an hour & half later.We did not finish with Horseferry road untill about 9 pm.April 2nd.Had a very fine time in England, very good weather. Put the first two days in London, then left by the Midland for Bradford where I put in five very quiet days, did not go about very much. I then spent two days in Manchester, do not think a great deal of the place, saw the Art Gallery & went to Lohengrin (opera) in the night, the second day I went out to Droylesden, to see the Thomassons, they wanted me to stay longer but did not & came on to London where I put in the rest of my time. I went over St Pauls, House of Lords, Westminster Abbey & the Tower of London they are all very interesting.LowencourtI was held up for a couple
