
our bn marched to the south of Albert and bivouaced for the night 23.8.16
We marched to Warloy Baillon last night and were billeted in the town. Marched from Warloy to Beauval today, (about 14 miles,) and expect to move on to Doullens and entrain for the north tomorrow 24.8.16
Left Beauval at 9 p.m. and arrived at Doullens at 11 p.m. 25.8.16
Entrained at Doullens
