<p>Wednesday 19 December 1917<br/>Lovely day outside &ndash; in Connaught confusion reigns as usual.<br/>Town 2-5 &amp; bought bible &amp; French books.  Had farewell tea for E. Brown 9 p.m. (&amp; S. Leid of course).<br/>Now can find my way around in Cairo quite easily &amp; have one or two humble friends already.<br/>Yesterday bought bedside mat &pound;1-3-0, very pretty.<br/>M.O. sympathises with me over loss of "Elizabeth" from ward.<br/>Wrote Mother, Eva, P.C. to Edith.<br/>Kathleen born 22nd Sept. 1917.</p>
