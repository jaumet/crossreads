<p>[Saturday 29 July 1916]</p>
<p>While scribbling these notes little curls of yellow smoke from Bursting Turks shells are above the Palms-<br/>All day we have been at it- <strike>The 6th lost an officer &amp; 2 men &amp; we a few</strike> but  (and) we are keeping them back.<br/>I only hope they come on &amp; make a good fight- this endless watching gets on our nerves.<br/>Anniversary of outbreak of War, How long will it last?<br/>Had to dig fresh Wells today to water our Horses as Johnny Turk had our old ones.</p>
