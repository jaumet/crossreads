<p>Tuesday 8 August 1916<br/>In morning Dawson &amp; I rode to Rail Head &amp; bought some sardines.<br/>In afternoon fell in 2-30 3 brigades and rode to Katia, had tea &amp; rode on all night Birelabd [Bir El Abd].</p>
<p>Wednesday 9 August 1916<br/>Arrived near Birelabd dawn &amp; came under shrapnel.  Attacked Turks.  Ronald killed.  Machine gun fire.  Beaten back.  Awful shrapnel.  Fought rear guard action.  Very hard to get wounded away.  Retired about 5miles to Ogratina [also spelt Oghratina].</p>
