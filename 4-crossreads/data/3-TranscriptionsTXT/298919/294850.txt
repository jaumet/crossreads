We were then opposite the "Empire" so the Purser &amp; I went in to the first house show, the 4th Mate continuing on to the ship.  The performances we[?] very good the programme being made up of variety &amp; revue.  As is usual the house was crowded the people here seeming to have a mania for music halls.  The best item was that of two musicians, a pianist &amp; a violinist.  They rendered some beautiful music including "Barcarole" which the[y] played better than I have ever heard it played before.  The performance finished at 9pm
