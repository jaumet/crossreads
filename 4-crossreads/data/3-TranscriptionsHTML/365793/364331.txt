<p>Aug 19 midnight tonight. Only 288 during the last 24 hours. Buksheesh Concert ,&amp; as usual the Colonel caused much laughter, but the best singer was a deaf chap.</p>
<p>20 Heavy seas last night &amp; a strong head wind. Crossed the line about midnight. One of the lads (a dentist) extracted a <u>sailor</u> <u>boys</u> tooth, &amp; after the agony a wit advised <u>him</u> to fill the offending molar with sugar &amp; watch it ache. It was a <u>love</u> of a tooth. Just spotted a nice friendly shark which gazed<br/>[in margin] Sunday</p>
