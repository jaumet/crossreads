<p>411<br/>5<br/>Jack Club, where soldiers can put up 6d bed and breakfast, I think that will do me and I could see the youngsters every day.</p>
<p>I'll write to the Mater the first opportunity  I have after my operation &ndash; give her my fondest love and tell her<br/>Grandmere looks splendid &ndash; I haven't heard from you for months and months but know how hard it is for you to get a letter to find me<br/>Much love<br/>Your very affectionate son<br/>Gibson</p>
<p>Sir. W McMillan<br/>Sydney</p>
