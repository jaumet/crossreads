
Friday 5 August 1915 In Cairo during the morning Hospital all day Letters from Girls & others Cables to M.M. Knowles & Roger Arnott 
Friday 6 August 1915 Dr Maguire from Auckland lere to lunch with me. I called at Egyptian hospital to see the New Zealand Medicos who are installed there 
Saturday 7 August 1915 About hospital all day except for one hour in the morning when I cal was in Cairo. Called at the anti-rabic institute. 
Sunday 8 August 1915 Mass here at 7 a.m.- Father Richards.at Materieh & Zeitoun Dr Maguire here to tea.
