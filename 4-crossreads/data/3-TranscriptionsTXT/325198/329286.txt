 20 April, 1916 Salhia We went out &amp; bivouaced last night &amp; I struck horse picquet.  We did a bit better than the other two troops the last time B Sqd were out at Serapeum they got lost in the desert &amp; a lot did not get back until morning.  We had to go out as covering party &amp; came in to our lines at dusk.
