 country.  Erect 3 PM move off Camp at 7 PM with bde on outside HUJ. 10th Move off, 6 am arrive Huj 8 am, erect station and leave again 3 PM water midnight.  Johnston thrown &amp; kicked so placed in limber.  Stumbling over waddis and rough country. 11th Match famine. Arrive Hill 331 at 9 am.  120 enemy brought in.  Terrific thunderstorm at night &ndash; gloriously wet &amp; no shelter.  Sig struck dead by lightning. 12th Heavy shelling 16 allied planes bomb Jacko returning.   13th  Big attack at dusk. 13th Water Medjel.  6 am leave 331 11.30 am through Juli's and erect at Suffrie Gabri &ndash; Hill 154 at 2.30 PM stay night. 14th Leave 1 PM and gallop through Beit Duras, Esdud Burkah, Beshit and arrive Yebna 6 PM as fresh as a bad oyster.  After a hell of a rough ride. Engine peters out with the rough trip. 15th Camel W/T take over while we repair the engine. 16th Leave Yeknah 10.30 am and Reg &amp; I gallop to Devan and meet Freda.  Give kiddies rides up the street.  Change engines with W8.  Engine peters out. 17th  300 enemy arrive. 18th London W/T arrive terrific thunderstorm at night.  Done up and wet through.
