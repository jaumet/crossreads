Yet I was secretly glad that I had been enabled to find my way about. My one trouble is seeing at night, and in this game it is very vital, for one will not always have  the  such a track as we have now, which is reckoned good compared to some. It is hard to be a hindrance by reason of a thing beyond ones power, and Ted feels it and so does Hal I expect.
