<p>Sunday 10 February 1917 [1918]<br/>Quiet day very few dressings in Connaught.  &frac12; day &amp; went Pyramids for blow in the tram &amp; how pretty it is along the roadside, bright green fields, black soil where newly ploughed &amp; away in the distance yellow sand hills or palm trees rising out of the plain.<br/>Posted photos to Mother &amp; Ida.<br/>Printed various scenes &amp; took notes re photography from Cook (Orderly).<br/>S. Watson going back to Australia &amp; at her own wish, but I feel very sorry for her with all the bright hopes of 6 months ago dashed, &amp; a wreck as far as a bad cough can make her.  4.30 p.m. tea at Mrs. Horan's &amp; very nice people, then church 6 p.m. &amp; sermon on the Beatitudes.</p>
