<p>4 mins the Turk fled outmatched with ours rising &amp;  diving at him. Then he slowly keelled over &amp;  came straight down nose first slowly twisting for all the world like a piece of weighted paper. Halfway down several clouds of smoke burst from him, &amp;   then finally, nose down &amp;   tail up he disappeared below the farthest ridge.
