<p>I hope that you will let me come to see you towards the end of this week or early next. I will ring up to ask first. Please do not trouble to answer.</p>
<p>With the warmest hand clasps, &amp; with the boy to realise this. To keep us free you mothers &amp; fathers are paying the price &amp; that price is so frightful that we who have no share in it are stricken dumb. There is just one thing we catch at for relief, - the knowledge that courage is the supreme virtue in the world today. Your boy had it in high</p>
