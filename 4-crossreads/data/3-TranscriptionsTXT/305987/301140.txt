 Friday 16th:  Relieved Tuesday 20th:  Back again to Biefvillers Wednesday 21st:  Hun plane brought down containing Prince Fredrick of Bavaria who later Died of Wounds Friday 23rd:  In old Hun dug-outs in quarry. Tuesday 27th:  Relieved. Came back to Bazentin Wednesday 28th:  To Fricourt Saturday 14th:  In support trench Norreuil Sunday 15th:  Huns broke through 1st Div line &amp; came up sunken road behind us. We inflicted very heavy losses on him with rifle &amp; machine gun fire &amp; rounded up a few hundred prisoners
