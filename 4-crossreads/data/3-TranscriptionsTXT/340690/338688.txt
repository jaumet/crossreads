21.4.18Breakfast at 8 and got up and had a wash.  Stayed in bed rest of day and dinner at 12 and tea at 6.  To go out at 5 to front with shovels.  Sarre, Bailey & Anderson & Storr on camp jobs.  Rest going out, 20 in all.  Long walk out about 4 miles & very circuitous route.  Waited at Batt. H.Q. & again at C.H.Q. [Company Headquarters] & then went out with guide to No Man's Land to connect up trench on our left to French, 100 yds long, Arras.  Eleven dozen.  I worked all over place with Linton & Jacobs.  Fairly quiet and did work well getting down to 3 ft. by 1.30.  Then got a message so came home.  Kernot stopping at C.H.Q. 4 whizz bangs fairly close in hollow.  Back with Edmonds and started improving dugout, widened & lengthened it and we sodded and roffed [roofed ?] it and completed by daylight, very tired.  Finished bottle of champagne & turned in Sunday.  Fine but cloudy and chilly in evening.
22.4.18Stayed in bed all day.  Cold wind & cloudy.  Much more comfortable.
