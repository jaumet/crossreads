
<p>345</p>
<p>APRIL 1917</p>
<p>21 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; fairly warm. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness all day. I turned</p>
<p>my two horses out to grass</p>
<p>for a couple of hours. The Right</p>
<p>section &amp; Staff played the</p>
<p>centre &amp; left sections football</p>
<p>in evening &amp; the centre &amp; left</p>
<p>won by six to nil. Got</p>
<p>a couple of papers from home.</p>
