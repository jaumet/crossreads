<p>also Eddie. Well Dear Mother I will now have to end my letter till I have time to send you another later on. I ask God to Bless &amp; keep you Dear Mother &amp; also Rube in Good health &amp; happiness</p>
<p>Till we meet again<br/>Your loving Son<br/>Will<br/>xxxxxxx</p>
<p>[Note in red at the bottom of the page:]<br/>From Sgt. Major W. B. Schaeffer,<br/>Killed in the trenches at Anzac, 20th May, 1915</p>
