<p>Sunday 18th June 1916<br/>Sailed at 9.30 a.m. and my hat blew overboard very soon afterwards. Directly we left port the ship was set on a zig-zag course so: (zig-zag sketch). Meals on board very mean &ndash; all are complaining about the short rations. Notices such as "S.S. Starvania" etc prove that others before us thought likewise. Sleeping on deck again tonight</p>
<p>19th June (Monday) Mess orderly today. Powerful</p>
