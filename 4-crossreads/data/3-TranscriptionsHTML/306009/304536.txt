<p>1915<br/>Dec. 20 Mon.<br/>At Port Said there is now a camp of 4000 Armenian Refugees who have been able to escape from Turkey &amp; the Turks. The camp is under the supervision of the Military authorities.<br/>When at Alexandria last I noticed a large refugees camp on the way to Mex.</p>
