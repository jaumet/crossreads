
Thursday 23 December 1915 Run 305 miles. 23' - 40" - 56"' To Fremantle 506 milesAll going wellSouthern Cross high in the Southern sky at 11-30 pm. Atmosphere & sea propitious.At 5 p.m. Visited Engine room & boiler room with the Chief engineer.
