<p>January 1918<br/>Wednesday 30</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Weather fine, a little frost, roads fairly good "Pull out" tomorrow morning so rumor says, at 2.30 am .<br/>Billeting party to leave at dawn.<br/>Spent a few hours on my horse and harness. Cleaned all the old straw out of the billet and made the place a little cleaner.</p>
