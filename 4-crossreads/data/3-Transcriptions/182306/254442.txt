<p>Enlisted 30th October 1916</p>
<p>Went to Liverpool Camp on the 21st In camp five days doing a bit of drill &amp; being Issued with uniforms &amp; equipment Went on final leave 5th Nov. to the 9th Sailed on the 11th (eleven days after enlisting) from the. P. &amp; O. wharf. at. 5-30 am on S.S [Seuvic?] Cleared Sydney heads about 1-30 pm and had a good trip till we got to the Great Australian Bight where we ran into</p>
