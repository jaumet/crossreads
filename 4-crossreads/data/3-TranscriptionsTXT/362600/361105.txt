 W 29 Sun Ceaseless rain all day &amp; all night. I light fire in offices. Tomo &amp; I [indecipherable] get sopping wet. Come Home &amp; sit in office to read. After tea [Hunnie!?] Room come to play cards &amp; go to bed Very cold at night 
 F 30 Mon but windy. Wrote Hilda. [Rayner?]. Recd letter from Marion Edmonds &amp; Mr Havercraft. Go to D'ton with H.R. patients got another Blanket. Lovely night &amp; nice ride back Home. Wrote Hilda. Rayner &amp; Miss Peck. Very cold night. 
 F 1 Oct. Tues. Lovely day first white frost. All W.C.Os change jobs. Go for ride through Amesbury [indecipherable] with Tam. Come back &amp; play Billiards &amp; snooker. Rather play on [indecipherable] part. 
 F 2 Wed lightly Dull. T &amp; I practise Hockey on tap Ground. Isolation lifts. I write all night. Wrote Roy. Connie &amp; Marion Edmunds. Wrote M.R.L. Jennie. Mr Havercraft. 
 F 3 Th Go to Ball with Johnnie Walker. No 213 one both 1st prize winners. Dance with town Girl &amp; Miss Moore from Brisbane. [RNo?] Dances in 2nd half &amp; got disappointed in Waltz Cotillion with Miss M from Brisbane Recd letter from Auntie Annie. 
 F 4 Fri Recd letter from Maisie Edm  At Southport. Go for Hockey practise with Tom. Go to B'ford with Tap &amp; meet T &amp; E on Bridge. Come Home for Supper &amp; play cards 
 Dull 5 Sat Recd letter from Marion Edmonds. See Col. Raffon. Gen McKye &amp; GIB in Bath. Fix bike mat. D.O.R.A &amp; Go walk with H.R. Go to whist drive &amp; score 153 -190 178 wins. Meet Colleen again &amp; train Girl or Girl Guide. Home bonnie evening altogether.
