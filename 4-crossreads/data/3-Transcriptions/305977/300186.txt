<p>Thursday 11th [Mar]<br/>Same work as yesterday.  It was very windy which made it very uncomfortable.  The Chaplains handed round New Testaments to the boys, a present from a Sydney lady to all the members of the Aust. Expeditionary Force.</p>
<p>Friday 12th<br/>Went out in the morning.  "B" Squadron independently along the Nile for about 5 miles throwing out flanking &amp; advance scouts.  Had a good ride, passed through several villages.  We had to report on the nature of the country to the Major.  It was good practice.  All the sections had a go at it at different times.
