<p>18.11.6<br/>argument with the Orderly Officer (Otton) Lieut Guthrie (B Sqdn) &amp;Reid  told them I intended to have an understanding before afternoon stables, arrived back &amp; put in a complaint which meant another argument which resulted in Head Qrs being allowed to file off early &amp; water first always; Here we have a helio station which also acts as an observation post, &amp; all messages from Geisi are sent by helio for practice, we are also in Communication with every station for miles around with 'phone; Patrols are always out in front during the night. We are guarding the flanks here &amp; there is not much danger as a long dry stretch of Sand dunes are on our left, duty Sqdn stands to arms every morning</p>
