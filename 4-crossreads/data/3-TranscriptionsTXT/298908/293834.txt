I never expected Ethel to turn up but she was there at a quarter to 7, she came down in the tram &amp; hopped out at the Post Office where I met  here  her, we then caught the Plymouth tram which took us right to the theatre &amp; even though it rained cats &amp; dogs we never got wet, by the time the performance was over luckily the rain had stopped &amp; the stars were shining so we both got home dry.
