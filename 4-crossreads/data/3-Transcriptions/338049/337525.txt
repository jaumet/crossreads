<p>Monday 11 Nov.<br/><strike>Work as usual</strike> Word communicated that armistice signed. Therefore no work doing. Excitement great.</p>
<p>Afternoon to Abbeville to bury Shonan. Madame made boquet of flowers. After wait at hospital march behind Shonan through the happy streets to cemetry. Ted Briggs, Ted Bennar also dead.</p>
<p>Evening at farm.</p>
