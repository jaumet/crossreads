 11/10/18 Up 7.15 am.  Caught train leaving Kendal 9.5 am for Barrow. Travelled via Oxenholme, Heversham, Sandside, Arnside, Grange, Kentside, Cark &amp; Cartmel, Ulverstone, Lindal, Dalton, Furness Abbey, Roose &amp; arrived Barrow 11 am. Walked down street to Town Hall &amp; there got in a tram &amp; who should I meet in it but Auntie who had been down shopping. Then explained to her how I did not arrive last night. After dinner went for walk with her to Scale village, where there are several very ancient houses, &amp; then down to the beach on outside of Walney Island &ndash; then back home. Then went &amp; had a shave, bought p/cards of Barrow &amp; wired an appointment to Uncle Charlie. After tea went down into Barrow with Uncle Fred
