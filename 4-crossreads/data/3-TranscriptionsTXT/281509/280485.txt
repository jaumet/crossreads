 19 September 1916 Tuesday 
 Start work on shelter for cooks. Bush carpentering. Usual morning tea with Mitchell at Y.M. C. A. Leave to Pop. in afternoon with Cart. Mac. Anderson, Jockey Bob &amp; Potter round, dinner. Pictures. Letters from Glad R. &amp; BT. Put in leave for Blighty. Wrote to Reg.
