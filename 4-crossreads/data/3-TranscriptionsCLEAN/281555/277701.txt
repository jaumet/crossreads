  
 19th. Sunday. March Reading & Cards morning, ditto afternoon. Weather calm & clear. Writing evening. 
 20th. Monday. Weather continues good. parade for innoculations & reading. Afternoon Rifle cleaning & Seniority lists gone into. Recommended for 3rd Stripe. Writing eve. Passed Malta 5.30Pm. Looked fine Rugged & strong & large. 
 21st. Tuesday. Weather fair but cold. Good Hot Bath after lunch [Indecipherable] rooms.  Finished up letter writing for mail. Sea rising & heavy wind towards evening 
 22nd. Wednesday. Mail closed 9am. Weather fair again. S.A. Inspection. reading & washing. 
 23rd.  Orderly Cpl. Dissembarked about 10.30 Lunch on wharf dealing with French people
