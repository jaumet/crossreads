Two shows.<br/>May 4th<br/>Last day at Hammersmith &ndash; Most successful matinee &ndash; And a wonderfully enthusiastic night show &ndash; Great financial success &ndash; a heavy but a happy night.<br/>May 5th<br/>Sabbath &ndash; Breakfast 11 a.m. Reading.</p>
