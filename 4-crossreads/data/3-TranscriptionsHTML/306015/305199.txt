<p>In the afternoon we walked to Beaucourt, and there had some caf&eacute; &amp; biscuits.   On the return trip we visited a windmill near Contay &amp; saw it at work.</p>
<p>May 19.   Our Kits were inspected this morning and then Alan &amp; I had a splash in the Creek that runs alongside our village.  It is a beautiful little streamlet bordered with trees and with lilac bushes covered with sweet-smelling flowers, and the coolness from the sheltering boughs is altogether delightful, even on the warmest day.    After lunch we had a route march for about six miles, passing through the villages of Esbart &amp; Beaucourt.    After that I walked to Molliens-aux-bois and visited a caf&eacute; house for tea.
