  
 January 10 Thursday 10 
 At Beutin Dick and Wattie to Montreuil, to repair magneto. Report having had a good time. Sir Douglas Haig's Hdqrs at above town, 6 kilos from here. Plenty of "Waccs" in village close by, numbers of fellows have made their acquaintances and pay frequent visits to their camp at La Cotolierie Went for the Dr. at 9.30pm one of the pretty bad. Rode some 15 kilos before I found him.
