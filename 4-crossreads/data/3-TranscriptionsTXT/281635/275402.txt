
13/9/17 Route march with full packs thro' Fondebecques, Elnes, Ouve, & back to Wismes. Distance about 16 kilometres. Turned fairly cold. Wrote to Aunt Sara14/9/17 Very cloudy. On Lewis gun in morning. After dinner on stunt out past Drionville. After tea went blackberrying with Andy.15/9/17 Rained during night & came on to my bunk. Very muggy. Out with Lewis Guns. Received letter from Uncle Charlie. Bathed & washed clothes in afternoon as was fine. After tea went blackberrying with Andy. Mess Orderly today.16/9/17 Sunday. Cloudy. After dinner went to Lumbres with Andy. Tried every shop in town for book of Anglo-French phrases but unsuccessful. Had coffee
