<p>[indecipherable Thursday] 9 Bombardment of Sari Bair mountains by our land batteries<br/>Dec 18 13 Batt. Commenced by degrees to evacuated their position on Durrant's Post.</p>
<p>Dec 19 Embarked at Lemnos [indecipherable] liner Tunisian which just missed being torpedoed<br/>Dec 25. We spent our Xmas at Lemnos Island &amp; had an enjoyable time. A Turkish Aeroplane tried to bombard our camp but without success</p>
