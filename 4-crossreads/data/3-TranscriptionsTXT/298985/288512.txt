Had to walk about &frac12; a mile with the Masks on  When we thought everything was finished &amp; we had fallen in ready to move off there was a shout of Gass several smoke bombs were let off to windward &amp; before we could get our masks on we were covered with very smellie smoke.  Harmless of course.  Got back to camp by 1 oclock &amp; were warned that we were on to move up the line that night Started at 6 PM &amp; marched to Le Harve about 4 miles
