1916   TUESDAY 6   JUNE
A fine warm day & not much wind. It was great lying in sun out of the wind  Mr Neyland gave the headquarters staff a lecture in morning and Tom Reed & self did some buzzer work  We had a lecture from Capt Samson (Doctor) on venereal diseases gonorhea & syphlis. He gave us a very good lecture indeed. Passed a mob of gulls diving at a school of fish. They were in millions and lots of poiposes. It came up very windy again at night.
