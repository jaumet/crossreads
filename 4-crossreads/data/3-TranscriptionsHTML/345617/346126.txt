<p>Juillet 23<br/>Coy on usual Section Drill &amp; getting ready for a move somewhere in the near future. Painting transport etc.</p>
<p>Juillet 24<br/>8th 14th &amp; 15th F Coys journeyed to Bray &amp; played the 1st Div. F Coys football. 1st Div. won all games. I met a lot of the old boys of the 1st F Coy with whom I came away from Sydney 18/10/14. Had an enjoyable game.</p>
