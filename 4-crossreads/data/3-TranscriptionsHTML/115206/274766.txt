<p>July 1918<br/>Monday 15</p>
<p>Bresle.Bty Position<br/>Weather fine and clear about 7am very heavy clouds filled sky.<br/>At 1.30 pm a Bosche plane attacked an observation ballooon, and brought same down in flames &ndash; makes three in two days &ndash; not a bad average.<br/>At 5pm left with Mr Parsons and Percy Grayling for F.O.P arrived at 6pm at our destination. Returned to Bty for rations. Arrived at F.O.P. with rations at 10 pm.  Fritz shelled lightly till 2pm</p>
