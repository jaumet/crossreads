<p>14 October 1918 Monday<br/>Jerusalem   66&deg; Casualty Clearing Hqs arrived here midday-   "Tummy" still on strike but feel better goodness knows where we go next-<br/>Great rumours of peace but I believe nothing-   All Hospitals &amp; trains full so stop here -for present-<br/>Heard our Brigade is coming back to Richon way soon to reorganise so expect will join up there later on-<br/>This Hos. overlooks the Walls of old Jerusalem &amp; was a Monastery-   SISTERS here.</p>
