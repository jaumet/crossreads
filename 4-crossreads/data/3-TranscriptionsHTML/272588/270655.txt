<p>Monday 4 February</p>
<p>Tuesday 5 February<br/>Still fine weather.  A night at the Anzac Coves, Ballieul.  They are shortly to go to London where they are to show. Our bread ration up to to-day has been white but to-day saw the advent of brown bread which is not at all bad.</p>
