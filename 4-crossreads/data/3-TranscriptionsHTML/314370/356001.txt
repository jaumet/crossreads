<p>March, 1916<br/>Friday 24<br/>Exam stoppages this morning.<br/>I was lucky to draw blank &amp; have no class to examine.<br/>My boys do fairly good.  results of paper come out &amp; are a great shock  marks very low.<br/>I have 4 specials</p>
<p>Saturday 25<br/>Parade at 8.30 am to receive certificates.<br/>At 10 am the first parties to leave march out &amp; continue until only a few men are left who are to leave tomorrow</p>
