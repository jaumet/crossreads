 [Sunday 5 November] 
 Tremendous storm last night destroyed 5 of our lifeboats broke 6 mens legs, &amp; fractured several arms &amp;  legs  HEADS Our steering gear went wrong &amp; we sprang a leak necessitating gangs of volunteers to go into the engine room &amp; bale,  as the Bilge pumps were out of order-  In the dark we nearly ran down a Sailing vessels &amp;  1000/-/-s of &pound; worth of damage has been done.  I am not ill but feel anything but Cheerful, we are all battened down &amp; the majority of men &amp; officers are very sick.
