Clouds were fairly thick by this time &amp; the search lights were beautifull &amp; Only out a few minutes peace for rest of night<br/>Had a good breakfast  Marched back to Boulogne &amp; left there at 2.30 per train about 30 of us in a truck   Went through Etaples &amp; saw where the Hun dropped some of his eggs last night One hit on the line &amp; tiped an engine over another had killed a horse</p>
