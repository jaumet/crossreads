<p>8 Sunday<br/>Drizzle &amp; fog &amp; black twilight all day.  Claude called up at 5.30.  I met him down town &amp; went to pictures at Marble Arch.  Took quinine Sat &amp; Sun</p>
<p>9 Monday<br/>Fog.  Black &amp; wet  office [indecipherable]  went to bed very tired.  Finished re-reading "On a St" [?]</p>
<p>10 Tuesday<br/>Office routine home &amp; to bed very tired.</p>
<p>11 Wednesday<br/>Office routine.  In evening A A. Miss [indecipherable] &amp; Booth Scott Mrs Dalrymple &amp; S met at Old Vic  for 'Love's Labour Lost."  Dark, dark, days</p>
