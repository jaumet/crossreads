<p>We did not have a sermon, nor address. Always sing the National Anthem, standing to attention. After service I retired with the matron &amp; had morningtea &ndash; now shall try    &amp; write a few letters &ndash; have not written to Jackson yet. Eight of our Sisters had to stay at Malta were sent in two's, to different hospital to nurse until another</p>
