
Enemy plane over, Anti's shooting at him Weather fine
25-9-1917General Coxon and Staff also Col Watson, ADVS  inspecting our lines; The usual complaints, horses not sufficiently groomed, harness not clean
26-9-19172nd Brigade sports on this evening. Half holiday. Col Johnstone taking Coxon's place as.C.R.A  A few decorations awards out , Sgt Flett M.M. Sgt Todd, bar to M.M. Bdr Cullen, Gr Roper, Gr Anderson, and Gr Jennings M.M. No drivers mentioned, not because they did not deserve it. Some done excellent work. No one to recommend them.
27-9-1917Nothing doing. Btys of other Brigade on Bty manoeuvre
28-9-1917Marching orders recieved. Move off tomorrow morning, straight into action. All hurry and confusion. A party out after remounts
