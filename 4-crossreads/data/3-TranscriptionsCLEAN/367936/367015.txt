  
 Thursday 27 July 1916 Trench digging again in morning. Lecture & bomb throwing in evening. Had to parade in evening and go to station to unload horses and bring to camp. 
 Friday 28 July 1916 Bomb throwing.  Lecture & trench attacks.  On guard for 1 hour & a half in evening as Infantry were rioting prior to leaving for France.  Voulenteered for Machine Gun section.
