<p>began the trying time of winding in and out and ducking wherever you were a little exposed, or when machine gun bullets whistled over head. After being lost a couple of times we eventialy were suddenly told that we were about ten yards in rear of the front line and to make ourselves as comfortable as possible. We were lucky to have duck boards to sit on but there was no room to lay down so we simply sat on our packs.</p>
