It is about two miles up to our forward dump. Half the way is along Sausage Gully and is exposed to his observation and fire.
22/7/16
Yesterday and last night I did 7 trips to the forward dump. We have lost more than half our party so the loads are heavier, as the stuff must go up. I do not duck from the shells now.
