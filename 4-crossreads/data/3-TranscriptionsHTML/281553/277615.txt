<p>written at Lemnos, 'Simla', Alexandria &amp; Tel-el-Kebir up to date.  Weekend Cable fee 41 Millemes per word to Australia and 10 Millemes for England.  Dinner today as follows:-  1st Salmon, Tomatoes &amp; Sauce, 2nd Xmas pudding &amp; Tinned Apricots with Cond. Milk, 3rd Sweets &amp; Chocolates, 4th Tea.<br/>Tea 5 p.m.<br/>1st Curry &amp; rice, 2nd Cace [Glace?] Fruit &amp; Plain.</p>
<p>Jan. 17th Mon.<br/>B Coy for duty, off.</p>
