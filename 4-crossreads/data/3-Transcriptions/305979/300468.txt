<p>exceedingly commodious and to our surprise was quite a busy shipping centre with something like 2 miles more or less of wharfage.<br/>The only defect is with regard to the entrance which is very narrow, &amp; artificially deepened, &amp; protected by a stone breakwater.</p>
<p>We were somewhat chagrined when a yellow flag was hoisted to the mast-top, but our doubts were soon dispelled , for the medical authorities exempted us from quarantine &amp; down came the 'dirty yeller'.<br/>We went alongside the wharf &amp; coaling commenced immediately.<br/>We were granted leave from 3 to 10.30 P.M. and spent the time till then in watching the blacks on the wharf; they were an endless source of amusement to all the boys.</p>
