 in the afternoon went down on the pontoon to a place over the railway line beyond Longueval where we carted a load of rails (9) to dump at Longueval leaving the wagons there for the night arriving home about 5. After tea cleaned myself and had a shave and then turned in. 
 16-12-16 Light frost last night and fine day a little foggy. Paid in the morning. Last night 1 cpl &amp; 6 men went out to prepare dugouts at the line returning about midnight. We expect to go out to-day. Canteen doing well &amp; well stocked. 11 o'clock grew cloudy and started to snow pretty heavily.
