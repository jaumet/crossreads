Sunday 22
 Church parade
Monday 23
 Warned to move to another part of the camp tomorrow.
Tuesday 24
 Move to No 3 camp, 1st Command Depot, a hundred yards or so, where I believe we will go through preliminary training
Wednesday 25
 Training in earnest, physical jerks etc I think I will enjoy it, even though it is taking perspiration out of me.
 Loafing about makes one sluggish the jerks are just what I need.
Friday 27
 Still in No 3 camp, No 1 Command depot where we are doing physical jerks route marches daily. Saw Barnby Brown to-day
Saturday 28
 Concert at Y.M.C.A. by Lena Ashwell. Very decent, classical
