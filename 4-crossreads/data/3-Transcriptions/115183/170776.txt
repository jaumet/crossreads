<p>During the afternoon and evening we just sat and yarned about the sun in Australia etc etc. Outside the weather was just Scottish and we preferred the warm fire.  Oh I omitted to mention that the previous evening (Saturday) we had been for a stroll round town, so saw the sat'y night shopping crush which was rather interesting.</p>
<p>The next morning Mon 6/11/16 we set out together again crossing the bridge over Waverly Station took a few photos of Calton Hill and then mounted the cobbles to High St. down High St. We made our way seeing much of interest in the rather squalid old buildings many evidently on the tenement principle. At last at the foot of the street we came upon a flat expanse of open country in the dip between the hill, and there stood Holyrood Palace!</p>
