<p>Cologne 15/3/19<br/>Dear May<br/>Just a few P.C. to let you see what a fine city this is. I am on 4 days leave here. Tomorrow I am going up to Bon to see the Universities etc. Trusting you are all well as these leave me having a bon time.<br/>I remain your loving Uncle<br/>Clarence</p>
