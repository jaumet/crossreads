<p>failed to do, all men are supposed to roll up their own blankets into bundles of ten. was annoyed as we were in sight of land &amp; I wanted to see the boat entering the Heads. I set to hurrying on with the blankets &amp; finished just as we were entering the Heads, Coogee &amp; Bondi presented a fine spectacle from the boat, we were all more or less excited the air seemed electrified. We lay out at Watson's Bay at 9AM &amp; was inspected (medical) &amp; very ragtime one.</p>
