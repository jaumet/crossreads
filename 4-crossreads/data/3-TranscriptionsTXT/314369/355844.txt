 16 June 1915 Wednesday 
 Continued work in reliefs   &amp; make good progress. Shrapnell drive the men out of the water during the afternoon 
 1915 June 17 Thursday 
 Inlying piquet A lot of rifle fire through the night but nothing comes of it   &amp; we do not have to turn out until 3 am   &amp; stand to arms until 4.30
