<p>sometime tonight and entrain during the morning. 27th Reveille 3am Left for Poperinge at 10 am arriving about 2pm. Raining like hell. Entrained and left for Verchin 10am arriving at 5am   cold and miserable arrived at camp at 2pm.<br/>29th Raining. Shifted camp1/2 mile further into village. Here I obtained from an old lady who kept a farmhouse a bed with sheets and eiderdown quilt and some room.<br/>30th Raining. Foster left for Ypres with convoy Ordinary routine.</p>
