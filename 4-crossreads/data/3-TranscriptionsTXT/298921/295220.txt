 September, 1915 Monday 6 Hear there are Italians landing N. of Anzac this should help considerably &ndash; I have not come in from my first outing &ndash; the "car" came for me &amp; I lunched at Brigade HdQuarters &amp; saw my Horses Please tell they both look A.1. &amp; are ready if we are to have any mounted work &ndash; I've had a Bath &amp; clean Pyjamas &amp; am A.1. another week should surely see me out, I am quite strong. I hear the Pastoralist Review of July 24th honored me by reprinting photo I wonder who gave it to them.
