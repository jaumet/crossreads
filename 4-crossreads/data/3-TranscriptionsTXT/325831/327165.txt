 From Capt M M Sinclair c/o Bank of North Q'land 4 Queen Victoria St London EC 
 No 3 Australian Hospital Brighton 9/Jan/17 
 Dear Mr Ferguson 
 I was glad to get your letter last week &amp; to hear you were all well. You seem to have been having a lively time in Australia, with politics &amp; coal strikes &amp; what not &ndash; we were all sorry the vote was for "No" conscription but all the same fee the men will come along yet. Australia has done very well so far &amp; must keep it up.
