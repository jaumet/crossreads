<p>June. 1916</p>
<p>June 1st.<br/>Discharged from Hospital- Australians inspected at Croix-du-Bac by Messrs. A. Fisher &amp; Wm. Hughes.<br/>June 2nd.<br/>Trench &amp; dug-out digging in our paddock- safeguard against German shelling.  Parcel despatched to Home.  Our friends in P.C. shop &ndash; Correspondence.  Artillery active.<br/>June 3rd.<br/>Trench digging- detailed to bring stretcher case from Erquinghem.  Wet w.,</p>
