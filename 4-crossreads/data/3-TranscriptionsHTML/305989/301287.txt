<p>Sunday 16 December 1917<br/>Church parade in evening, got word of move into trenches (dismounted).</p>
<p>Monday 17 December 1917<br/>Preparing to move at 3.30.  Joined up with the 1st Regt. at dark &amp; proceeded to trenches &amp; took over from Scotties, horses went back to camp.  Did shift in open near Mulebis.</p>
<p>Tuesday 18 December 1917<br/>Quiet day, making bivvies etc. rained during night, got washed out.</p>
<p>Wednesday 19 December 1917<br/>On post all day, everything wet through:  rained incessantly &amp; hard too.  Went over to 2nd Reg. H.Q. with message.  Did shift on gun:  Whizz Bangs got into camp but did no damage, got 11 more letters.</p>
<p>Thursday 20 December 1917<br/>Showery &amp; rain alternately, fairly calm day.  Heavy Bombardment on Turco's line 2 hours on gun at night;  got 3 parcels in mail.</p>
<p>Friday 21 December 1917<br/>Infantry attacked on right, captured "Bald Nob" 3rd Regt. L.H. took over.</p>
<p>Saturday 22 December 1917<br/>Infantry attacked on our right &amp; left flank;  Turks in full retreat.  Infantry advanced about 3&frac12;  miles great sight, stayed where we were for the night.</p>
