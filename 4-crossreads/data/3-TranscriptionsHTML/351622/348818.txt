<p>[Newspaper clipping]</p>
<p>LESTER. &ndash; A tribute of love and affection to my dear father, who passed away January 9, 1929.<br/>I who loved you, sadly miss you,<br/>In my memory you are dear,<br/>Loved, remembered, longed for always,<br/>As it dawns the first sad year.<br/>Inserted by your loving daughter, Florrie.</p>
