
Australian BranchB.R.C.S.Bulford9/6/18
Dear Dad, Mother & George,
There is little worth writing about this week.  I rode to Salisbury on Tuesday.  It is such a long while since I did so.  We have had the bike tuned up & a new saddle & now she goes like a young horse.  I took me an Hour each way, but it was a very hot day & I walked most of the Hills to save myself knocking up.  I played Cricket on Wednesday against the pick of the Tommy Garrison.  I was only chosen because all the good players were away.  Anyway I fielded
