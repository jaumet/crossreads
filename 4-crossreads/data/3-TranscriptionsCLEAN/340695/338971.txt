  
 I went down to Oxford to see Eric, he looked splendid. My friend & I (Billy Cosgrove) also spent a day in Birmingham visited friends of his & they treated us like kings. The rest of the time we spent in good old London town. I stayed at 35 Inverness Tce Hyde Park with Mr Ferranti. The English girls are very nice we met some regular sports. Had two to see us off at Waterloo Station one was the Loddgate keepers daughter at Regents Park. Had a good trip across the Channel submarines were sighted but we slipped them.
