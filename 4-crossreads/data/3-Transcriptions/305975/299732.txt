<p>Sunday Nov. 10.11.18<br/>got up to day  saw Dr.  am feeling very much better to day  raining heavy and cold-  wrote to Josie Bell<br/>Monday Nov. 11.11.18<br/>am feeling much better to day  it is Raining cats and dogs this day  water running all through the tent.   am waiting to hear what germany will do now.<br/><strike>Tuesday Nov. 12.11.18</strike><br/>went to concert to night  word come through 8.P.M germany has signed the Armistice.  Chreeing any song god save the King not me</p>
