<p>Tuesday 9/4/18. More enemy shelling&nbsp; 2 Coys digging support Line- wrote Doff.</p>
<p>Wednesday 10/4/18&nbsp; Quiet Day&nbsp; had meeting of Coy [Cdrs?]&nbsp; more digging at night</p>
<p>Thursday 11/4/18. Taking over tonight from Currie- very cold day</p>
<p>Friday 12/4/18 Miserable day went up to posts at night&nbsp; Good deal of M.G. Fire.</p>
<p>Saturday 13/4/18 Men working well&nbsp; weather better- visited posts.</p>
<p>Sunday 14/4/18&nbsp; Attempted raid but failed&nbsp; one casualty&nbsp; enemy very alert.</p>
