  
 Oct. 22nd Rehearsal in the morning - Evening free. A chance of seeing & hearing "The Grousers" (English Show) is readily accepted - A great show. - Commence reading Eugene Grandel."  (Honore de Balzac) 
 Oct. 23rd Second night of "Saa-eeda Digger" - Audience lager and more enthusiastic. The show goes with a great swing - and I believe for the "Anzac Coves" a record number of laughs is scored - Complete reading Eugene Grandel.
