  
 I felt that disgusted & wild with myself that I never uttered a single word, never swore once, not that I don't swear oh no" but I felt that full up, to full in fact for words however the night was fine & by the time we reached our dugouts nearly 4 miles away I was beginning to get dry, & as soon as I got the men sorted out & fixed up I turned in & changed my clothes Dave Murray lent me a clean flannel & I had underpants & socks so I was pretty right for the night 4.30  this morning we were all roused out for fatigues & wasn't there some swearing & cursing we had an early breakfast & away we went up to the firing line to work for the Engineer's, arrived
