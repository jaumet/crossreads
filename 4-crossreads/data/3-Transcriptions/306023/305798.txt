<p>After passing a few yards down Victoria Street, where as I already knew were the Commonwealth Offices, and the Anzac Buffet, we turned into Horseferry Road, where are the A.I.F Headquarters at which we were <strike>requested</strike> required to report, before pursuing our own course.</p>
<p>Now that I was in London and not a penny in my pocket, and too late to go to the Bank and see if any had arrived from home I began seriously to think of the most advantageous stand for a pavement artist, or <strike>in other words, to think</strike> of going <strike>to</strike> direct back to camp.
