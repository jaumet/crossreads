<p>[Saturday 29 January 1916]<br/>A Welsh mounted Regiment is near us &amp; I expected to see some good Welsh Ponies, but the Horses are all a Suffolk Punch type Tails cut off &amp; the Tommies ride mostly at a jig jog or trot, they never seem to make a horse walk decently or Canter.</p>
