 [Thursday 30 March 1916] 
 Today I had some business with the Principal of Technical College- There are 14 of these &amp; include all Trades- &amp; Agriculture.  The Native students are making me a pair of boots for 80 P.T. &amp; presented me with a hand worked silk handkerchief. Slept last night in a double Bed - sheets- Cold shower-etc etc.  Recd letters from Laura Mch 1 [indecipherable] Joh:-  Heavy Khamsin or Dust Storm &amp;  very hot  On Court Martial of SIKH Indian Mountain Battery who shot his Sgt Major pure Murder.
