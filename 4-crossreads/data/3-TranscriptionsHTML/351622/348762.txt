<p>7 January 1916 Fri<br/>Had a fair number of dressings this morning afterwards breasting the officers bar &amp; securing light refreshments. Met Warrant officer of 20th Battalion who resides at Petersham. Never before have I witnessed such beautiful sunsets as these parts offer.</p>
<p>8 January 1916 Sat<br/>Sgt Bellemey before Colonel</p>
<p>Arrived Alexandria 4 p.m. Hover by mid stream. Played penny banker with the W.O also indulged in hot bath. First decent bath for six months Thousands of sea-gulls around the ships. Also H.M.S. Mars.</p>
