The Euripedes is a much faster boat than ours and many remarks of a good natured variety were passed between both ships. Such "We'll get the flags out for you". "Have you any letters for us to take home for you". Our band struck up "Goodbyee". 
 On our port we passed a huge native camp, the occupants of which rushed to the bank & cheered & yelled wildly. They always have done it and I suppose they always will. They are a queer crowd.
