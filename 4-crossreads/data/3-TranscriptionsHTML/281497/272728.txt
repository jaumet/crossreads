<p>Timber &amp; troops are passing in one constant stream past here train load after load of them.This morning, I saw a big train load of Jocks go past.</p>
<p>There is a military band in the camp here which plays fairly often it was playing at the Sports this afternoon.</p>
<p>They are feeding us pretty well here, we get far better food then the rest of the men, that is of course due to the position we are likely to hold later on. We live entirely on our own here so far, for our movements are directed from Divisional</p>
