<p>and our Infantry has been doing short spells spells in the Ypres trenches with longer spells in the rear billets &amp; huts Our spell is over &amp; we are under orders for a move in a few days</p>
<p>It is distinctly Autumn weather now. We have had some very wet &amp; cold spells &amp; during these days have been covered in mud &amp; wet through. This is what we must expect as a regular thing during the winter &amp; we are none of us looking forward to it to say the least. One &amp; all of us would not be sorry to be sent to Egypt for the winter again, although we cursed</p>
