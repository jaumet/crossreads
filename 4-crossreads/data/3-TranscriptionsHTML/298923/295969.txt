<p>30 October 1918 Wednesday<br/>Teeth- Major Douglas  Capt Donell<br/>Got parcel today from Hospital will keep till I go back again <u>if I do</u> feel sure of going home about Xmas<br/>Big mail from England Laura Joe, Day very glad Joe on way home he will meet Dolly perhaps.<br/>Took Dolly to dentist she has to go again next week-<br/>Had afternoon tea in Cairo-<br/>Wrote Nowell do not know where he is-<br/>Big mail from Australia.</p>
