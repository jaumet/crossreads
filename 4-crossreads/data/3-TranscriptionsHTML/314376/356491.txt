<p>180<br/>Going back a little way we found some dugouts already built. We passed the night very comfortably in one of these being issued with our ration of stew which was thoroughly enjoyed. From this position we marched back to the chateau spending the night in some woods adjoining. We had little "bivvys" erected for us and slept about 6 to a tent. We moved off next day to Bollencourt.</p>
