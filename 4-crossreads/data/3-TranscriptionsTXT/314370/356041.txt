 June, 1916 Sunday 18 wake up in our new home, completely disgusted No rations yet. At 4 pm tents arrive &amp; are erected 14 men per tent 
 Monday 19 Went to Tel-el-Kebir Rifle Range w/- a company of reinforcements. shooting was simply awful men have never had any proper instruction. splendid walk out &amp; on the way back I sniped a large bird in a tree at 60 yds w/- my pistol
