<p>Wednesday 8th May<br/>Fell in twice but did nothing</p>
<p>Thursday 9th to Sunday 12th May<br/>Hand dressed every day. Beautiful weather. Doing nothing but reading &amp; loafing about</p>
<p>Monday 13th May<br/>Nothing as usual Wet day again</p>
<p>Tuesday 14th May<br/>Was marked B.A. today as my hand is quite healed. Walked to Forant &amp; back this evening about 4 miles longest walk I have had for a long time</p>
