We have left our packs and over-coats stored in Warloy, and are in very light fighting order. Received a lot of mail you today. 20/7/16. Last night we releived the 13th Kings Royal Rifles, opposite the village of "Pozieres," which the Germans hold. There has been terrific fighting about here, and this battlefield is a dreadful spectacle, for the dead still lie unburied. The trenches here are very rough.
