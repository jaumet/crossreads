 1917 October 
 24 Wednesday 
 Awoke to a fine morning &amp; the place soon dried up.  Got grey &amp; cloudy again towards night fall. Stephie &amp; J. Lindsay taken to sick tent. Went for a little walk on the hill sides with Mr. G. Brown &amp; Smith &amp; saw old Kajmachalon crowned with snow.
