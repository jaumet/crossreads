<p>would call at Port Said tomorrow at Sunset he also informed us that <our>/strike>the 1st &amp; 2nd Divisions were in France &amp; that we would probably go there from Alexandria.  Port suez is connected to Suez Proper by a narrow Isthmus along which runs a Railway line.  There is a Hospital at the South end of the canal.  There is a Military camp on the opposite side to Port Suez.  Some of the Soldiers said they were the Essex.  They were camped on a most desolate spot sand everywhere it must be Hell when a wind blows.  The canal at its entrance is very narrow the channel proper is hardly wider than L. Hardt [Leichardt] canal.  I was told it was 88 miles</p>
