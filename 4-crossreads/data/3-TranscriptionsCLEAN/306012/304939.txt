 Sat. 10th Aug. The attack was on a 20 kilom. front from Albert to Montdidier & was carried out by 4th British & 1st French Armies under Gen. Sir Doug Haig.  The former Army includes the Australian & Canadian Corps as well as Scottish & English Troops.  Up to 3 p.m. on Thursday 7000 prisoners had been taken and a general advance made varying from 3 to 5 miles & in some places to a depth of 7 miles.  In our sector lines of communications are being hurried forward & the road we are on (Amiens-Villers Bretonneux) is alive with traffic.  We are no longer the M.D.S. which is now further forward &  few  very few casualties
