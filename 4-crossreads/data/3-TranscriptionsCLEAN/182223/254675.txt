  
 Monday 28 June 1915 Letters posted to Girls. Mollie. Strickland. Carruthers. Maxwell.   Working in office.  Reading & writing all day 
 Tuesday 29 June 1915 In Cairo this morning. Bank. Med. School. Dep. Public Health.  Posted letters to Dr Paton. To Girls  Cable from MacNamara at 6 p.m.  365 patients in hospital. 
 Wednesday 30 June 1915 Cabled Joseph at 6 p.m. for her birth anniversary tomorrow.  A hot day.  Busy.- 470 odd patients  Ten letters of Feby 1915 date to hand today.
