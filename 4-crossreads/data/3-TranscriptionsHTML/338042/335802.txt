<p>I see Gladys Cooper in the "Yellow Ticket" - Return to  &amp; sleep at War Chest.</p>
<p>Sunday. Feb. 11th<br/>Sabbath spent in long &amp; efficient rehearsal &ndash; I procure a private billet. Bus  &amp;  taxi drives.</p>
<p>Monday. Feb.12th.<br/>Amid great anxiety but "undaunted courage" the Anzac Coves appear for first time in London. &ndash; a small house does not promise much success but we do exceptionally well &ndash; "Sari Bair" brings only encore &ndash; Broken Bottles (?)  The evening</p>
