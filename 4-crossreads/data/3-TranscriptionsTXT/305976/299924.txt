 Friday Oct 12 Two new Batteries arrived to day moving into position midnight. Tom came up from Rafa. All dumps being moved up from Rafa &amp; El Arish to Shelal. Getting Horses fit for coming stunt. Beer a Sheba rumoured our objective. Saturday Oct 13 Sultan's funeral in Cairo. Soldiers armed in case of native rebellion. French, Indians, Jews, British West Indias, Tommys, Irish, Welsh New Zealanders Italians &amp; Ausys on this front. Sunday Oct 14 All ranks warned of prevalence of Spies. A snake came in Bivvy today &amp; curled up in Blankets. Finish Snake
