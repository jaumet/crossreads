<p>Turkish cigarettes &amp; writing notes.  Day &amp; night following quietest yet known.</p>
<p>19th Tuesday<br/>Another 'Parapet scene' &amp; exchange as on 18th, also seen on Courtneys Post till Germans opened fire with artillery &amp; Rocket Bombs of which they placed 17 along their lines with much apparent effect, remainder of day fairly quiet.  Evening at Cards.</p>
<p>20th Wednesday<br/>Business as usual, nothing out of ordinary.</p>
<p>21st Thursday<br/>Fair amt. of Artillery fire from both Navy &amp; Batteries.  Cards.</p>
