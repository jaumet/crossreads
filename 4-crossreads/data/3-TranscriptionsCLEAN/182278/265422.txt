
Thursday 20.6.18  Inspection by Wisdom  very good turn out reconnoitring  Wrote to Doff.
Friday 21.6.18  [Jumping?] contest against RHA  we won
Saturday 22.6.18  Went for a long ride. Had a game of Bridge.
Sunday 23.6.18  Letter from Doff  played cricket, made 18 against men v officers
Monday 24.6.18  Went to Bde during Wisdom's absence, to Concert Party at night after reconnoitring V.B. front.
