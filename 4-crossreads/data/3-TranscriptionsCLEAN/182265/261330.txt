 Captain Smith (15th Bn) with 4 platoons of 50 each attacked. A scout & myself led Lt Willis & 52 men to note a trap & captured them. Conducted debate among men in Y.M.C.A. Hall. 
 Thursday  14th  - Payday. I drew my Outfit Allowance of 5 and 1/10/- pay. 
 Paid Mess 3 and gave notice to Secretary that I did not desire hired furniture in my room. Played chess with Padre. 
  Friday  - Received letter from Mr Lord of Brmingham stating that my old C.S.M. Lord was a wounded prisoner in Germany. 
 Received list of casualties of men of my reinforcement from A.I.F. Headquarters. 
 Received letter from Reg in which he enclosed letter from Mother & Ina.
