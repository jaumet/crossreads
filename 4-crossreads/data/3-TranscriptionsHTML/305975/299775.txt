<p>Monday feb. 10.2.19.<br/>turned out 6.30.  went for run  fed up Bfast  on q.m fatigue  dipped saddles for handing in  Boxing on to night  wrote to Josie Bell stella Dorrie tessie.<br/>Tuesday feb.11.2.19<br/>turned out 6.30  went for a run  fed up  Breakfast  cleaned up all saddles and Packed em away  signed Demobilisation Papers a lot of messing abt  wrote to maud S Aggie and maud alie  turned in</p>
