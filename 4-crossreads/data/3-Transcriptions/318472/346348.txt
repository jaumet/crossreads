<p>3 May 1918<br/>Friday<br/>Things are very deadly in camp. <strike>My</strike> Nothing of much importance report from the Front It is thought generally that the Hun will again shortly launch another big attack.</p>
<p>1918 May 4<br/>Saturday<br/>Nothing special to record Beautiful sunny afternoon Things are very quiet in camp and I am just about fed up with things.</p>
