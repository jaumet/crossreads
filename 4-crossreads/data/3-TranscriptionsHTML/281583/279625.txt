<p>It gave me a pang when I saw the Canadian Scottish marching through Albert with shells tearing all round &amp; it seems such a shame that such fine men should be sacrificing their lives in thousands &amp; as the grave yards will tell you <br/>for the sins and errors of Kaiser Bill.</p>
<p>Great news is coming through from the Balkans but a reserve must be placed on a lot of it as some of it is of a startling rumour . None of us quite know how the Roumanian situation will affect this front but our conversations always get heated &amp; we knock off.</p>
<p>2.9.16  Saturday</p>
