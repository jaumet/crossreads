
Should there be any doubt of my word one has only to try all-night duty on A.I.F. Headqrs.
8th January:- SaturdayToday I spend a nice outing (after duty at 1pm) in the Gezinek Acquarium Gardens. This to my mind is the only nice little spot round Cairo.
9th Jan:- SundayRaining slightly for a few hours in the early morning. I am on duty until 1 pm. In the afternoon a few friends & I spend some time listening to the band in Esbekia gardens
