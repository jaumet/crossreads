  The  An interesting experience was narrated to me by a Queenslander Pte W.S. Lever who had been to Cardiff on his disembarkation leave. He was invited to the races, taken to the theatre, where, by the way he found the star artist was Miss Lulu  Birstead  Binstead, the Westralian singer.
