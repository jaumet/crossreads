<p>10 or 15 minutes I saw 4 return in irregular line &amp; make for cover above a bank of cloud on the Essex side on their way back to Germany. All returned safely unfortunately &amp; the London death toll in those few minutes was over 200, in addition to several hundred casualties &ndash; I should think America will be a great help to us in the aircraft fighting once she gets her sleeves rolled up properly.</p>
