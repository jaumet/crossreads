
Saturday 14/4/17Marched to Favrieul arrived by 3 pm commenced to rain - saw Vernon, also passed the General & had a long talk.Sunday 15/4/17Hun attacked Lagnicourt, Fifth Bde did the job splendidly & pushed him out. Moved at 7 pm to Corps Line.Monday 16/4/17Raining heavily all day, men all in bivouacs.Tuesday 17/4/17Still raining & snowing, bitterly cold - meeting of C.O.s at 7th Bde H.Q. Had dinner with Black 7th Fd Ambulance.
