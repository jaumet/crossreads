Then we began to traverse the German back &amp; forward area (of the Spring offensive), &amp; although the villages were in ruins, the country, being slightly hilly &amp; wooded in the middle distance where the Somme flowed, did not look so desolate. There were the same old gun positions, stacks of German ammunition &amp; trenches, which but these are to be seen everywhere on the Somme battlefields. There had been a great amount of clearing up &amp; salvaging in these regions further back, since we first advanced.
