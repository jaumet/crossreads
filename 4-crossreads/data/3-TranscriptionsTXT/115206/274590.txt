 January 1918 Sunday 20 
 At Beutin Macully left for Blighty on leave, Macaffery and Meredith went to report as instructors at R.B.A.A. Weather very fine. Big parade on tomorrow general inspection. Very busy polishing up limbers, harness and etc. Weather beautiful. Received a small parcel of socks from Mother and some papers from Blighty and magazines.
