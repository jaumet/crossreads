 August 1915 
 Tuesday 3 
 Two more admitted to hospital &amp; discharge of bayonet wounded men. 
 Trouble with Flint after I have reported him [indecipherable]. Apparently some of the men [indecipherable] for nice holiday &amp; get annoyed when expected to do a little work 
 What a terrible surprise is in store for them in the field and for me too I expect. 
 Often wonder whether I shall ever do this voyage on the return
