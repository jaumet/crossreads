<p>My travels with the 12th Rfets of the 3rd Batt.</p>
<p>Brown<br/>47 Albion St<br/>Annandale</p>
<p>Kept a diary until after the battle of Pozieres, when this was written up.  Whilst resting at Hermines the old diary was rewritten in this form &amp; then the narrative continued from time to time.</p>
