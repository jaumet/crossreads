<p>wish the cursed war would end so that I could get back to the sun again its worse than an existence here. 3 weeks here today another 3 weeks of it would drive me Balmy.</p>
<p>Friday 4/1/18<br/>big frost last night very cold this morning letter from Viv last night wrote to him Posted letter to Syd this morning American Genrl here to inspect this morning<br/>Posted letter to Viv. Ausy Mail in today at last may hear something of them in Sydney.  Pat gone to Boulogne today Bill Snoddy tomorrow. feeling a bit better today, Cognac no. Bon.</p>
<p>Saturday 5/1/18<br/>cloudy, dull &amp; raw no frost last night feeling much better to day.<br/>1 year ago today Uncle George died &amp; will not fixed up yet.<br/>deferred Pay &pound;40-8-6. Bill Snoddy gone to Boulogne to day. C.Os Orders at 3 p.m. Owe &pound;8-15-0 9. p.m. had great night plenty of Beer etc &amp; whiskey by the S.M. Must keep your spirits up. Mail in &amp; nothing for me so whats the use of worrying they dont worry about me will send for &pound;50 out of that Legacy May as well have some of it as leave it to those who don't care a dam whether I'm alive or dead, good mind to leave it to disabled Soldiers. They would appreciate it any way.</p>
<p>Sunday. 6/1/18<br/>Heavy frost last night beautiful designs on the windows. Owe &pound;8-13-0 deferred Pay. &pound;40-10-0. Mail to day came up cloudy after dinner Raining hard tonight in Canteen nearly all day serving</p>
