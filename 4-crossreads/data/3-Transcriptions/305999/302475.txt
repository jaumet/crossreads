<p>2/11/14<br/>In the morning we all left Albany we went under a good escort of war ships  we went in three rows of about 8 ships in a row  the Star of Victoria was in front of us and the Omera in the rear of us the Sydney was on our right flank</p>
<p>9/11/14<br/>early in November one after noon we were having a little lecture on the forcastle head when we saw the ship making to the left of us in a short time  We heard the bands playing god save the King and then</p>
