<p>Jly 22 outside  Went into Portishead for sundry articles (necessary and otherwise) &amp; returned at 1.30 to a splendid dinner &ndash; (duck, green peas, new spuds, gooseberry &amp; loganberry pudding, custard pie, &amp; fruit) Do me! Talk about being full; I'm well packed (A nice little kiddy named Audrey Weaver (7&frac12;) came up this afternoon to <u>see the</u> Australian. ?-)</p>
<p>23 Cut Church out &amp; went for a walk along the Coast. Am leaving for Weymouth this afternoon &ndash; via Waterbury. Theres a graceful sailing boat lying<br/>[in margin] Sunday</p>
