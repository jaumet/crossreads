<p>March, 1915<br/>Wednesday 31<br/>Squadrons go to the [indecipherable] Barrage to learn to swim horses. I hope to go later Luxor post-poned on eve may go any time took Papers &amp; Chocolates to Myra Nyse in Hospital she is getting on A.1. &amp; will soon be fit again.<br/>My knowledge of Perspective &amp; Drawing is coming in very handy in preparing outpost skeches Reconnaisssance, all boys might be taught general principals of sketching &ndash; I wish I had paid more attention sometimes to all you could have taught me.
