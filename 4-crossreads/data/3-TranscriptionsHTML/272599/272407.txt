<p>May Thursday 17 1917</p>
<p>Afternoon off. I go into Boulogne  Get Kipling's Barrack Room Ballads for Andy Carruthers. Leave coat at Merridews. Visit  At Boulogne "Haute Ville". An absorbingly interesting place. Ramparts, Belfrey, Cathedral etc. Home 7.30.</p>
