<p>One of our dolls was going over to the Una yesty. He was transferred &ndash; It was the aftn. He wasn't a very popular doll &ndash; He'd argue too much for his mates &ndash; As he &amp; his hammick went over the side &ndash; one wag of a doll got the bugle &amp; gave in full blast &ndash; 'The Admiral's Salute'. This is a well known call &ndash; Officers stood to attention &amp; the Commander rushed out wondering what was up &ndash; The bugler doll got 14 days  no 10 this morning for his infernal cheek &ndash; All his spare time for 2 weeks he drills &ndash; but really I think he ought to have got clean off &ndash; his sense of humor must be good =<br/>2 pm &ndash; Raining like only the tropic can rain &ndash; The Matunga has gone out - <u>At last</u> - Went out this morning after endless delay &amp; carries belated letters to Sydney &ndash; She's got a move on anyway &ndash; We're supposed to go to Bougainville to-morrow but I fancy we wont move just yet =</p>
