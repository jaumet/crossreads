 No further mention made about a transfer &amp; relations between Dr. Scrimgeour &amp; myself continue in the same state.  X Feel fit, but for the foot, which is extremely painful. We ordelies  see  rub along fairly well with not more than one brush a day, which is not bad considering. Letters slow in turning up again.  Am anxiously waiting for Rita's photograph. In regard to the war it seems from the news in the papers that we are in for a long stay before finally going home. The homers will be lucky.
