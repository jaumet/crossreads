<p><u>February</u></p>
<p>attached to the roll of the Steamship "Euripedes" which is to leave for Australia on or about the 20th of this month. If all goes well I should be home by Easter.</p>
<p>Here in Camp the rules and routine is not nearly as strict as it was when the war was on. I have no drill to do, I occupy myself by doing odd jobs around the camp.</p>
<p>Since my arrival at camp I have been in constant receipt of letters, both from here and Australia.</p>
<p>17-2-19 The weather for the past two weeks has been very cold with one or two very wet days.</p>
<p>I am at present at No 5 Camp at Sutton-Veny about three miles from the town of Warminster.</p>
