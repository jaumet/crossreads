27-4-1918At base detail camp, Dog tired, and hungry, dirty and lousy.  Weather fine.  Paraded to officer in charge to get put on draft, to rejoin my unit.
28-4-1918Warned for draft to rejoin Bty.  Weather wet, and cold.
29-4-1918Parade at 5-30 p.m. to entrain at Le Havre.  Wet weather.
30-4-1918, Le HavreThe 5-30 p.m. parade cancelled.  Move off now at 2-30 a.m.  On board train by 6 a.m.  I am writing this in the train.  I am in charge of 31 men including one A.M.C. prisoner.  Today so far is fine (10 a.m.) ordered back to camp.  Our train was to be attached to a French passenger train.
