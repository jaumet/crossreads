She was the first of my relations that I met in Ireland and she was also the last that I saw. At Dundalk I changed trains and caught the boat train for Dublin passing right through to Kingstown. At Aimens Station Dublin we were supplied with tea and sandwiches. The train ran on to the pier alongside the ship and we passed from the train to the SS Leicester leaving about 9.
