 Receive parcels from home with photo goods. 
 1/9/16 Cloudy morning &ndash; on watch WA5. Taubes bomb Pt Said &ndash; hear guns. Taubes fly over here. Try to warn  howitzer battery no good. 
 2/9/16 Leave Romani 7am for Hod Amara. Pass through Katia &amp; arrive Amara 3pm &amp; relieve WA2. Do 6 to 8 pm shift. 
 3/9/16 WA2 leave here 7am for Romani for a spell. Taube visits us &amp; passes to Romani. Guns heard evidently firing at the plane which returns and flies around us before leaving. 
 4/9/16 Go out for watermelon &amp; dates.Lovely cool day 
 5/9/16 Taube over again gets rather furious machine gun fire. Go to DeBabbio &amp; Freuva with Missen for curios &amp; photos. Many dead horses at Debabbio. Air raid on El Arish to take place tomorrow morning. Develop photos after tea. 
 6/9/16 El Maza &ndash; between Salmara &amp; El Arish bombed as a reprisal for bombing Port Said 
 7/9/16 Go out for water melons. Monitors lob a few shells on El Maza. 
 8/9/16 Visit Bir el Abd battlefield &amp; find scores of dead Turks &amp; many NZ's graves. The NZ's graves have a cross above each one &amp; the soldiers hat, rifle &amp; gear (what is left of it) is placed on top. A dreary resting place. Find a Turkish belt buckle. Have a tough go with my camel who clears out &amp; leaves me, after rolling me off. 
 9/9/16 Go Hassaniya on [indecipherable] to get mail &amp; have to belt him home. 
 10/9/16 Taube over during afternoon. NZ's say good bye. 
 11/9/16 3rd LH take place of N.Z.'rumours of an attack to be made on " Mazaro' at week end. 12/9/16 Turks reported out of Mazar so stunt will probably be postponed. Taube pays a visit as usual &amp; machine guns refuse to act as  usual.11pm three brigades 1-2 &amp; 3 leave Amara with 6 batteries &amp; 1000 mounted Tommy Camelry. Telegraph
