<p>4<br/>There will be much for all of us to do even if it is in the peaceful latter years instead of the impulsive firey days of youth.</p>
<p>Australians all make the discovery that their own dear land is best of all and they seem to have great love for their homes.</p>
<p>Measured against other troops I have seen the Australians are splendid men in rugged strength and vigor.</p>
<p>The free life they live has given them such a bold reckless spirit that I really doubt if there is anything else in the [man?] line which will make so fine a fighter</p>
<p>Best address<br/>1654 Sgt Hawkins<br/>C/o Major Wall<br/>DAQ.U.G<br/>Tel-el-Kebir<br/>Egypt</p>
<p>Much love to dear Mother and to you all<br/>Great love to you and the dear old children<br/>Geoff</p>
