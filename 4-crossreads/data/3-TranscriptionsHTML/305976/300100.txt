<p>Train to Ludd. after leaving Gaza it rained &amp; we arrived at Ludd wet through. We slept that night in the Canteen<br/>Thursday Dec 24<br/>We walked from Ludd to Richon to day &amp; borrowed a pair of Horses from Bridging train. We caught the 5pm train from Ludd arriving at Rafa about 6AM next morning.<br/>Wednesday Dec 25<br/>Christmas day. We spent a good Christmas. The weather was fine &amp; we had plenty of Food &amp; Drink. Our Boss told us today that we would soon be going home.</p>
