 This little book contains a diary of my travels since leaving Sydney on October 30th 1915 on the Argylshire. 
 Jan 14/1/15 Enlisted at Victoria Barracks for the Light Horse. 
 16th/ Went into camp at Liverpool. 28 miles from Sydney on main Southern line.
