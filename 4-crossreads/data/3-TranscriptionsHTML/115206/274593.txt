<p>January 1918<br/>Wednesday 23</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Cleaning and polishing up our phones for the road and line.<br/>Smith and I packed two panniers this morning. Thompson went into Etaples to purchase a few necessities.<br/>Bought some excellent bread at our canteen, 1&frac12; lb loaf.</p>
