<p>Any other day</p>
<p>Monday 4th February<br/>Very clear day plenty of our Planes up no Huns</p>
<p>Tuesday 5th February<br/>About 1 point of rain last night the first I have seen in France Fritz sent a fair lot of shells over this afternoon for a change the closest were 3 or 400 yds from our camp</p>
<p>Thursday 7th February<br/>At least I believe it is Thursday so have missed</p>
