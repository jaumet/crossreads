April
1st. Nurse this evening at Troe "Yes Uncle." To-morrow I go to Eastbourne. Discovered Old Curiosity Shop, whilst walking round the Inns of Court.
2nd. Arrived at Eastbourne this afternoon of a nice day. Have an idea I will like the place.
3rd. Glorious weather, fine sunny I have a nice companion we have been tramping along the coast across the downs this afternoon.
