May 14 W.N.W. 330 M.
Reveille 6 a.m., had about  hours physical drill on Boat deck. Breakfast 8 a.m., Porridge, Fish fried, Bread and tea. Parade 9.30 am Rifle ex. Firing position standing load. In charge of 12 men, draw meet potatoes for ship. 20 sheep, 16 of beef. 6 bales sausages. 12 sacks of potatoes Dinner usual time, good. Afternoon given for mending clothes, etc; 5 pm. tea. same as 13th. music dancing, singing and other high spirited amusement followed, played table games till 9 then to bed. Sea steady swell, weather
