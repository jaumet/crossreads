Reveille was at 3.30 Am. breakfast about 4. Am very pleased to be away from the post. On telephone duty to day. Monday June 19th 16 We are off to Alexandria at last. Just now we are all entrained in open trucks at Moascar June 20th 16 Alexandria Once again on a troopship. Embarked about 6.30 Am on the SS Caledonia a ship of 9000 odd tons. We are full up with troops & there are other troopships in the harbour. Very hot. As expected had very little sleep. though open trucks are more comfortable than third class carriages. Alexandra Bay on board SS Caledonia 21.6.16 Still waiting in bay, for some reason. We are making all preparations against submarines, every man has been issued with a life belt, & when we leave, he has to wear it
