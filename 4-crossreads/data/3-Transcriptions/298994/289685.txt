<p>training centre for Australians and other troops We camped here the night and at 7 in the morning, we left Larkhill to perform the remaining five miles to Bulford.</p>
<p>We arrived here in due course; after every minute detail that was possible to be done for the complete success of the function; we had a grand march past, the King taking the salute. Some 70,000 Australians took part in this affair. It must have been a grand sight to an onlooker</p>
<p>This function over, we had dinner and proceeded back to Larkhill again, We were thoroughly tired out after the day's operations, and needed no rocking to sleep!</p>
<p>We left for Codford early in the morning reaching that place again about 3 in the afternoon.</p>
