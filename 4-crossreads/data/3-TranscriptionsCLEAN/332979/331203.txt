As the Corps was formed on January 1st 1917 I was one of the first & had to assist the Officers in arrangeing with the others who were transferred into the Corps & get things into Ship Shape as the men were coming in Gradually until the 15th of the month when the Majority arrived. They formed up in Perth & after marching through the Streets they entrained for Greenmount, the Band came with them & brought them into Camp. It was a fairly hot day & when they arrived,  they  after their march the boys in Camp turned out to give them a cheer.
