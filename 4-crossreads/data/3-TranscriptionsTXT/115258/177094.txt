All their work is done to chanties. The chanty man sings one line of a song, generally in a high squeaky voice, then all the team, as they put their backs into the work, chant the chorus, in a slightly deeper tone. A chanty man seems to have a pretty free hand - he can sing about anything he thinks of, and if he knows his job well he, generally has the men in fits of laughter, and the harder the work, the more boisterous fun they seem to get out of it.
