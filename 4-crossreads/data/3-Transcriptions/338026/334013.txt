<p><u>Contay.</u></p>
<p>[25 Th]<br/>Washing and oiling Limbers</p>
<p>[26 Fri]<br/>[Diarist has indicated ditto marks:]<br/>Washing and oiling Limbers</p>
<p>[27 Sat]<br/>Harnessed mules into mess cart &amp; went to D.A.C. H.Q. at Beaucourt &amp; 2nd. Div. Salvage dump with U.S. wheels  Arr back to lines 12 noon, then sent to dump for a load of bombs. Dinner. 2pm. Harnessed up again &amp; took bombs &amp; rations to 6th Bdge. Got back to camp at 5 pm.</p>
