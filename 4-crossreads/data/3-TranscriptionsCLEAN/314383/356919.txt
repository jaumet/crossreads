 We had conferences with the chiefs until midnight but as nothing can be found out about the true state of affairs we decided to sleep on it.  Some Russians here. 
 August 4th Read & I rode out with Agha Petros this morning along the road that the people were streaming in on.  There are thousands here now, having come in during the night.
