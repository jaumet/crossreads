<p>5 September, Friday</p>
<p>Terrific rain storms. I buy fruit etc &amp; have a terrible job getting it Home.</p>
<p>6 September, Saturday</p>
<p>Dinner in Manly. No 13 ticket &amp; 1/3. Dis-app over marathon. Go Home, back to Dobboes to Ellis place &amp; to Britt Theatre for Fisk Jubilee Singers with Gen, Lil &amp; Mum. Great rain up till 11 pm &amp; then glorious day.</p>
