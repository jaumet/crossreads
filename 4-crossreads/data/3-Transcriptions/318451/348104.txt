<p>Tuesday<br/>We got away this morning at 7-30 &amp; marched to Bailleul over very muddy roads.  It was a bitterly cold day.  Snow fell heavily on the station &amp; it was a blizzard almost.  We had a trip of 2&frac12; hrs. to St. Omer by train in Horse boxes but it was not bad.  We passed through St. Omer &amp; on to Hallines by road about 5 miles where we billeted for the night.  I stayed at the priest's place, a quiet village.</p>
<p>Wednesday<br/>We got away early today, the wind was very cold.  We got snow &amp; rain.  At the lunch hour halt we practically took possession of a farm house where the womenfolk made us coffee &amp; were very kind.  Of course we paid them well.  Then we marched on through driving snow down the hills &amp; up again.  After a while the column looked like a great white</p>
