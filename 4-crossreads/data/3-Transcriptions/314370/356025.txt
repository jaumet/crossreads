<p>May, 1916<br/>Thursday 11<br/>Work as usual<br/>Very hot &amp; windy<br/>Go for a walk to Zeitoun in the evening</p>
<p>Friday 12<br/>Work as usual through the day.<br/>The summer heat struck us today in real earnest &amp; a hot wind was blowing enough to frizzle the hair off our bare knees    impossible to go into the sun without helmets on.</p>
