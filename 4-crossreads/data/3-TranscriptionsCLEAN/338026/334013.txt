  
  Contay.  
 [25 Th] Washing and oiling Limbers 
 [26 Fri] [Diarist has indicated ditto marks:] Washing and oiling Limbers 
 [27 Sat] Harnessed mules into mess cart & went to D.A.C. H.Q. at Beaucourt & 2nd. Div. Salvage dump with U.S. wheels  Arr back to lines 12 noon, then sent to dump for a load of bombs. Dinner. 2pm. Harnessed up again & took bombs & rations to 6th Bdge. Got back to camp at 5 pm.
