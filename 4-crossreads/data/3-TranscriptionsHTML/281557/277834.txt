<p>Sunday Jan 16th<br/>a fine day : not a ripple : very cold in early morning; the difference to our "tropical &amp; Red Sea night" was a startler especially as we all had to hand one blanket in yesterday leaving us with only one. Revellie 6 am: communion 6.30 am twenty one present : church 10 am : In daylight the scene was interesting the harbour is nothing for looks, but the boats including three warships &amp; the port &amp; town for a background was interesting to us . we seen</p>
