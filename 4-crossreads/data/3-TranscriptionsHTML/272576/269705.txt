<p>15th Sept 1916<br/>Signalling. Cold day but sunny. Walked down to Durrington to see Perce, but did not see him. Received photo from Millie.</p>
<p>16th Sept 1916<br/>Lectures Heliot Lamp. Received letters Rana &amp; Wyn. Wrote letters Aust. mail also to Millie &amp; Cousin Kate. Went down to see Perce. Met him &amp; went to Durrington. Had a beer at Plough Inn &amp; afterwards went to concert at YMCA. Came back &amp; had drink at canteen. Said Goodnight and came back with boys 9.45.</p>
<p>17th Sept 1916<br/>Sunday today. Cooks fatigue. Did not go out. Wrote letters for mail. Perce came up to the hut. Talking for a long while then we went over to the canteen with the boys and had a [indecipherable], afterwards we soon went away to huts.</p>
<p>Here endeth this epistle of joys &amp; sorrows of some months passed from 11th May 1916 to 17th Sept 1916<br/>Norman Edmondson<br/>serving with the A.I.E.F. Field Artillery 7th Brigade  18/9/16</p>
