<p>13 October 1916 Fri<br/>26th Battle are relieved by 27th. They are nothing like the old 5th Brigade. We are being relieved to-morrow by A Section consequently half of our section pushed back to the village of [indecipherable] this night. I am going to-morrow.</p>
<p>14 October 1916 Sat<br/>Releived by a party of A Section &amp; left the Bund at 10.30 pm. walking via the duckboards to Lille Gate start of town where we picked up a transport waggon returning from the line called in at Asylum midnight stopping a few minute before pushing off for the <strike>asylum</strike> Mill. Arrived Mill about an hour later &amp; turn in next to the Grave diggers.</p>
