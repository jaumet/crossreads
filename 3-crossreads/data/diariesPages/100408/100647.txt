8.15am. Bread jam for breakfast after having it went to a Froggies house had boiled eggs coffee costing 2 francs for the two of us. Out in morning cleaning rifles bayonet fitting equipment.
27/10/17 Have been in No10 Stationary hospital, St Omer, since last Tuesday with Myalgia have been very ill with it. Today am feeling slightly better more inclined to write. Food is very scanty not too good. Came in motor ambulance from Wismes. Second day in temp was 101 but is today just above normal. Have a boiled egg at tea time each day custard for supper.
28/10/17 Sunday Very cold all
