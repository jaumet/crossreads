<p>was my fourth time over we arrived at Suez on Dec 6th &amp; went alongside the wharf at 1.30 Dec 7th. I was one of the advance party to go ashore and we entrained for our new camp. left Suez 4 PM for Heliopolis where we arrived at 12.30 AM Dec 8th at a place called [Zeitoon?] during the journey up in the train we saw many interesting sights as we ran parallel to the canal where a P &amp; O&nbsp;Liner was going through, it looked as if a ship was passing through a desert, &amp; looked very strange indeed when our&nbsp;train journey was over we marched to our camp two miles away which turned out to be</p>
