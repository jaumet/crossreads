<p><u>9th A.M.G Coy.</u> No 46<br/>Intelligence Report from 6am 9/2/17 to 6am 10/2/17<br/>The following indirect fire was carried out:<br/>Gun SS 88 at C16d 15/40 on 1) Road from D 18a 56/25 to C12b 90/06 2) Fme du CHASTEL C18d 15/11 1000 rds between 6.30pm &amp; 9pm.<br/>Gun at C 28 c 60/45 on 1) CROSS ROADS S of L'AVENTURE C 30 c 90/90 2) on COMMUNICATION TRENCH C29b 90/35 to C 30 b 90/50. 3) X ROADS at C30a 15/75<br/>Gun at C 22 a 75/15 on 1) L'AVENTURE RD to [LES 4 HALLOTS?] C23d 80/10 to C30c 90/90 2) TROLLEY TRACK C24a 70/10. 1000 rds between 7 &amp; 9pm.<br/>Two guns fired throughout the night on gaps cut in enemy's wire.<br/>Quiet during day until 1pm when light bombardment occurred. Very little machine gun fire.<br/>Weather: Bright. Visibility poor in morning better in afternoon. Light wind<br/>Frost continues</p>
<p>D Whitehead Lieut<br/>9th. MG Coy</p>
