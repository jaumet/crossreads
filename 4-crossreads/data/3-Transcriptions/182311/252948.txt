<p><u>SYLLABUS OF TRAINING FOR A RAIDING PARTY</u>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>TO BE COMPLETED IN 16 DAYS</u>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>GENERAL</u>:&nbsp; Throughout the whole training, discipline should be rigid, a keen and careful daily inspection, and occas-ional periods of squad drill and rifle exercises are there-fore necessary.&nbsp; Gas Helmet Drill must bengiven amounting to at least one hour weekly, and the inspection of Helmets and Respirators be thorough and constant.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>X-DAY</u>:&nbsp; Party moves into Raider&#39;s Billets: very little work will be possible on that day.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>PERIOD X plus 1 DAY to X plus 4 DAY (inclusive</u>:)&nbsp; Physical Fitness Training as given by bayonet fighting - physical training - running - route marching - dummy grenade throw-ing - games.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>During this period a bath for all ranks is to be arranged.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>PERIOD X plus 5 DAY to X plus 9 DAY:</u>&nbsp; The Plan as concerns Infantry action should now be ready, and on the first day the following action should be made:</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Organising and Detailing of all Parties for the Operation of parties of men with similar tasks into specialist training squads.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The tracing of a replica of British and German trenches and No Man&#39;s Land.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Other days of this period must be spent in training as under:</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Mornings</u>;&nbsp; Specialist Squad Training.</p>
<p><u>Afternoons</u>:&nbsp; Concerted work.&nbsp; Details of this work.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>__________</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>SPECIALIST TRAINING SQUADS</u>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>All Rifle Grenadiers for R.G.Work.</p>
<p>All Bombers for Live and Dummy Throwing.</p>
<p>All pistol men for Revolver work.</p>
<p>All Demolition men for Demolitions.</p>
<p>All Bridge and Mat men and Scouts for Wire Crossings.</p>
<p>All Lewis Gunners to work under night condtions with clouded goggles or blindfolded assembling.</p>
<p>All Stretcher bearers at First Aid and carrying dead weight.</p>
<p>Trench Clearing.</p>
<p>Shooting from Hip.</p>
