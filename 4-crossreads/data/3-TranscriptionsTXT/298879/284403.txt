 July 1915 Friday 9 Fine Windy. Went aboard  HMS. Bacchante  with some companions and was shown round the Engine Room by one of the sailors. They gave us tea and made us feel at home. They invited us over on Sunday when I could look over the guns and torpedoe tubes. 
 Saturday 10 Windy. Horses, lorries and Ambulance wagons landed this morning.
