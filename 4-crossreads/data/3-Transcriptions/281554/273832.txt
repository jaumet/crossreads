<p>Jan. 5th Wednesday<br/>B. Coy. for Duty.  Self as Orderly Cpl.  Management very bad regarding food issues &amp; much duplication of orders.  Weather unsettled &amp; cold wind.  Ship still anchored in harbour.</p>
<p>Jan. 6th Thursday<br/>Ship weighed anchor 1 a.m. moved off 3 a.m. &amp; well out to sea at reveille with small war craft escorting us, later picked up large 4 masted transport &amp; was later escorted by 1 cruiser &amp; destroyer in addition.  During afternoon picked up two more destroyers.</p>
