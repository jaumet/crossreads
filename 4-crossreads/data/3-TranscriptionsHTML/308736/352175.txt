<p>Tuesday Nov. 19th<br/>Started boot repairing for Battery.  Inspection by General McClagin, went round all the billits.<br/>Saw some French Soldiers who had been prisoners in Germany.  They had a feed at our cookhouse.</p>
<p>Wednesday Nov. 20th<br/>Fog, sunshine and fog all day.  Put in the day repairing boots.  Got 37 remounts, drivers had a day out.</p>
<p>Thursday Nov. 21st<br/>Packed up for the road.  10 a.m. started our march to Germany.  Left Le Cateau went through Basuel, Catillon and La Groise.  Camped in a small house.</p>
<p>Friday Nov. 22nd<br/>A very cold frosty day.  Carted and cut wood to make myself warm.  Men went on strike because they weren't getting enough rations.</p>
<p>Saturday Nov. 23rd<br/>All hands taken for a march before breakfast.  Half holiday.  Made some "burgoo" for supper in petrol tin, highly flavoured.</p>
<p>Sunday Nov. 24th<br/>Church parade.  Got a new hat and pair Jack boots.</p>
<p>Monday Nov. 25th<br/>Stitched on my first pair of soles.  Sent away Xmas cards.</p>
<p>Tuesday Nov. 26th<br/>A dull cold day.  Closed up shop.</p>
<p>Wednesday Nov. 27th<br/>Up at 5-30 packed up.  Left La Groise at 8-30 a.m.  Went through Le Sart, Prischies, Petit Fayt and Cartignies and camped in a barn.  Passed through orchard country, some of the trees blown away.</p>
<p>Thursday Nov. 28th<br/>8-30 a.m. on the road again, plenty mud.  Left Cartignies, went through Boulogne, Semeries, Fayt, Sains, Ramousies and camped in Liessies, arrived 3 p.m., and camped in the clink.  Rained nearly all the time.  At tea time a big batch of "Frogs" lined up with their dishes for some curry.</p>
<p>Friday Nov. 29th<br/>Sewed another pair of boots.  Was asked to go into Cookhouse.</p>
<p>Saturday Nov. 30th<br/>Altered two pairs Jackboots.  Finished boot repairing job.</p>
<p>Sunday Dec. 1st<br/>Started work in cook house.  Get the life worried out of us by hungry french people.</p>
<p>Monday Dec. 2nd<br/>Got another new pair of pants.<br/>3 p.m. Major Stevenson gave a lecture on A.I.F. educational scheme.</p>
