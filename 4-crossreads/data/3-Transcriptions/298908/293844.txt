<p>9th.  Another lovely morning &amp; its quite a treat to be out.  There is not a cloud in the sky &amp; things look very beautiful.<br/>We handed in all our blankets this morning &amp; quite time too for they were absolutely filthy you could go to bed with clean pyjamas on &amp; get up in the morning real <strike>&amp;</strike> dirty, I don't think they could have been changed for ages.<br/>I got another nice batch of letters from Aussie &amp; letter &amp; a Xmas card from Dick Duke &amp; a parcel from New S.
