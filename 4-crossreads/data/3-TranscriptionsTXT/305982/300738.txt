14/10/16Good sleep last night raining like hell to day.  Went &amp; had a look at Edinburgh Castle caught train for Glasgow at 1 oclock to day arrived there at 2.30 raining here to so will not stop it is a fine city.  will leave by 5.55 train for Ulverston  women work here
