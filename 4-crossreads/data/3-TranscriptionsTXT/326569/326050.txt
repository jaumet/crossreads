 Tuesday, 15/5/17 Had to drill with company this morning in drill order.  After dinner had medical inspection, then detailed for inlying piquet.  At 5.15 p.m. when piquet fell in it was overstrong so I was one of the fortunate ones to be dismissed.  Then went to Perham Downs &amp; washed hdcfs. &amp; had hair cut.
