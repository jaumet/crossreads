<p>21 July 1916<br/>Friday<br/> <br/>Short route march before breakfast  Put on sanitary fatigue with Phillips same as at Doulieu fairly easy job. Beautiful warm morning, took opportunity of washing clothes &amp; airing same. Nothing doing much. A few casualties brought in victims of gas shells  Shortage of rations. Met Stewart Gibson, in Machine Gun Sec. of 5th Brigade.</p>
