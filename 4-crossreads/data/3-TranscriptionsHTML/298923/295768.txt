<p>12 April 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out)<br/>The Scotties who have been with us for so long have gone-   I cannot say where but the ANZACS have lost a lot of good pals. X I heard today a house suspected of German propaganda work in Jerusalem was searched 200 golden sovereigns, rifles, &amp; enemy documents, in one room were many beautiful frocks from Vienna &amp; Berlin in latest style, Ball shoes etc-  supposed to belong to German ladies-<br/>Another Canvass Trough stolen by Bedouins last night, notwithstanding a guard.X</p>
