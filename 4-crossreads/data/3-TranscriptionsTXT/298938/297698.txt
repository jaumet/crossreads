
In the evening Lily Hammond Frances Vi & Ernest, came & we spent a pleasant eveningHad to see Dad back to Caterham unfortunately at about 9, but hope to see him again soon.On Monday early I saw Mr Hammond Senr. & spent the rest of the day in the city with Lily, Ernest, Frances & Violet.Caught the 10.43 pm back, Lily & Mrs Elerage, seeing me off.  It was a very short time to have at home, & I was very miserable when I got back, but it was a very pleasant memory.
