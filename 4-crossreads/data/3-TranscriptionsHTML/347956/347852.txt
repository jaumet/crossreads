<p>9-1-15</p>
<p>to the highest, when we were put to a test by a stranger.</p>
<p>Sat 9th<br/>We had some tight squeezing to get in the tent last night, as it was raining &amp; all the open-air men made for cover, we did not more than a few points but it made things uncomfortable &amp; cold for us.</p>
<p>This morning we had to fall in at 9 AM in full marching order; Stan is on duty in the orderly room to-day, so Nelson &amp; I had to do all the work for C Sqd. we were working out on stations &amp; being tested by Maxwell.  our station was commended for the way our station was worked.  half holiday, I</p>
