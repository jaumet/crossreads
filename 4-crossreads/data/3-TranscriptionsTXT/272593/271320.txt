 Fritz, hammering sawing &amp; hacking   sometimes a shovel swings into view This happens too often sometimes &amp; our guns put a few rapid salvos into him bursting the shells all round the busy part, but again the situation is often reversed &amp; we get them at us, but one thing is absolutely certain which ever side starts it the other is bound to retaliate &amp; we knock off work for an hour or so &amp; let the guns fight it out.
