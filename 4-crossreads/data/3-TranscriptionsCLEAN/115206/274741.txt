  
 June 1918 Thursday 20 
 Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles Weather wet and miserable. orders for Rgt and Centre Section to pull out at 11.p.m. tonight .  peculiar coincidence that rain always falls when we pull  out. Gun Adam paid me a visit at W.L. we went for a long stroll to Bussy, Querrie and Pont Noyelles. Had a very nice food , and awbon parle. Grayling, D a Costa left, Charlie Adey has gone to a school as instructor to a  Tommy School, at Havre
