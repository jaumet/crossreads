 Keeping fairly fit. It will be a frightful strain when we reach land, and I think all round we will feel a lack of condition. The ships officers seem a very decent lot especially the troops officers a little Irishman who is awfully good company. All the time one id writing there is a sinister feeling that if all the recipients or our letters were to compare they would be shocked by the similarity' but it can't be helped. Arthur can tell you all the news this is just a friendly not to find out how you are and what is doing. Remember me to all the boys at College etc. take of yourself and hoping to see you soon. Yours ever [indecipherable]
