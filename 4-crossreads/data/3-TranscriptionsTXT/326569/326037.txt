 Assisted Pinchin adjust his equipment.  After review Wade &amp; party inspected around huts &amp; mess room also kitchen.  Had Miss Patman's brother pointed out to me today.  He is a sergeant. 
 Monday, 9/4/17 Strong wind blowing from West today which was exceptionally cold.  Route march in afternoon about 1 mile beyond Barford towards Wilton.  Storms of rain, sleet &amp; snow passed over. 
 Tuesday, 10/4/17 Last night coldest night experienced so far &amp; all the morning on the parade ground was very cold.  Few flakes snow fell.  Medical inspection in afternoon.  Had neck of tunic made smaller during evening &ndash; cost 1/6.  Bitterly cold all day.  Received letter
