Wednesday 2 May 1917.
Reveille 6.30. No physical jerks. &nbsp;
In full marching order left camp at 8.26 a.m. Spell 5 mins. Trawool Bdge at 9.25. Trawool Hotel 10. a.m. Left 10.15 a.m. Spell 5 mins. Kerrisdale 11.24 (station) 1.8 p.m. Homewood. Dinner here. &nbsp;
Now gone 19 miles. One 11 mile non stop walk.&nbsp;Nothing to eat since breakfast. &nbsp;&nbsp;
2.26 pm. onward to Yea. (Breaking up of the boys.) 4pm arrived in Yea.&nbsp;Decent reception. Supper &amp; bed 10.30&nbsp;
Too tired to sleep. Capt Heath &amp; 20 others (myself included) slept in Shire Hall. (Got cup of tea at Homewood at a farmhouse on the way up.)
