<p>Things have cleared up &amp; it is a glorious &amp; sunny morning like a Spring morning in Tasmania not a cloud in the sky &amp; birds singing everywhere, you would scarce know that a war was on save for the incessant &amp; never ending growl &amp; rumble of the guns in the distance, &amp; the stream of traffic on the roads &amp; the steady hum of the planes overhead, everything else seems so peaceful calm &amp; quiet. <br/>I got the full strength of the raid the Germans made on the 10th Battn. a few nights back, it appears that the 13th Battn. knew it was coming off but they did not know when, &amp; they had just been relieved when over Fritz came, 9 officers &amp; 200 strong</p>
