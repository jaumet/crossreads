<p>June 1918<br/>Saturday 8</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles.<br/>Weather very fine. Visited Jack Harvey, had some very <br/>refreshing drinks<br/>Rode across to Bonnay for a feed and certainly had one<br/>sang at Concert in <br/>Y.M.C.A. pretty successful turn out. Had a very enjoyable<br/>swim with Don<br/>Ack, P.E and Trail<br/>Red Palmer worries about something.<br/>Inspected a programme of events for tomorrow's sports.<br/>Met North and Robbie at 113th Lines.</p>
