 H.M.FORCES IN EGYPT 8 Dec 1915 1st Field Ambulance pans Division . 
 My dearest Irene, Here we are still at Lemnos Island & may be here some time. I think I told you we were actually on the transport to be taken to the Dardanelles when we got orders to disembark. We are  actu  officially taken on the strength of the 1st Field Ambulance  So if you address your letters here you will be quite in order & I will get them about two weeks earlier than when they are sent to indecipherable] first.
