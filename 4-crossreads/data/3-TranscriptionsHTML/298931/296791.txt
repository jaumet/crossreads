<p>Friday 8<br/>"Moving day" going back to the firing line after spending a good holiday still in good heart &amp; faith<br/>Saturday 9<br/>Ypres by moonlight was a weird sight passed the night in the ramparts of an old fort<br/>Sunday 10<br/>Last night we three (two Mack and Jackie) were lost on the battlefield Boy's of the Limber Lost Camped in a brick tunnel<br/>Chat's  are sweet little crawling animals, that make me, take my shirt off four times each day</p>
