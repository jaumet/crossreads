<p>SUNDAY, AUGUST 31, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Tickle got some of the horses</p>
<p>from Piccadilly into yards. Took out</p>
<p>two of mine, one of Tickles &amp; one</p>
<p>each of Alan&#39;s &amp; Joans &amp; put them</p>
<p>in 4D. Round the bottom end</p>
<p>and Kimber&#39;s Woolshed after foxes</p>
<p>but did not see any.</p>
<p>T. Kenning &amp; C. Gosden started</p>
<p>burning off in Wheelers.</p>
