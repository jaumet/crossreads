
Mon. 13 Nov. 1916Sighted lights of Cape Town at 7. 30pm.  Lay in Bay till next morning (arr in bay 9. 30pm)
Wed.  15 Nov. 1916Route march in the morning.  Sport in oval in the afternoon, concert and tea in the evening at Feather Market.
Thurs. 16 Nov. 1916Tram ride round lions head.Left Cape Town 6 am.
