 30 August, Saturday 
 I stay Home at night but Dad, Bess &amp; I drive to Dee Why in afternoon &amp; go walk on beach. We do not notice that the House has been moved. 
 I am very tired &amp; Bess is not well. Dad, George, Mum &amp; Lily go to theatre. 
 31 August, Sunday 
 Bess is down. I potter in garden all morning. We go gathering wild-flowers in the afternoon. E &amp; E come out for tea.
