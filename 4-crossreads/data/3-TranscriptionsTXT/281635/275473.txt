
Chocolate, Raisins, Fruit Cake & Paste; sent by Griffiths Bros.30/6/18 Sunday. Fritz aeroplanes over last night.2/7/18 Recd letter from Dick Luscombe. Three trainloads of Yanks passed through today going towards Lumbres. Fritz planes over last two nights.3/7/18 More Yanks passing through.4/7/18 About 11pm last night American troops marched past our camp towards line. Were going by pretty well all night & this morning.5/7/18 Feeling very tired & off colour altogether during afternoon.6/7/18 Had bad night. Reported such at 9am & sent into hospital with Spanish Influenza. Temp 100 in morning and 103.8 at 6pm.
