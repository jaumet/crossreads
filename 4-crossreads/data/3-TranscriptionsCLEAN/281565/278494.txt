Rounding the western point of the cliffs we beheld portion of Aden itself.  A few strange looking houses with red roofs beside a broad harbour sheltering several steamers & dhows.  Far away to the north was the low long coast with its trees & verdure in utter contrast to the volcanic outcrop we had just passed.
