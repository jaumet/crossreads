<p>lick of his life round to Peel House so as I could get my overcoat, got round there alright, &amp; picked a Tommy up who was going to Wale's, then commenced a mad race through the heavy traffic it was touch &amp; go but we got there by 2.30 after paying the taxi I had to race down to the R.T.O &amp; get him to alter my pass from Aberdeen to Llandudno, this took a minute or two, &amp; just left me enough time to hop into the train as she was moving out, I dare say some of the people thought me mad.
