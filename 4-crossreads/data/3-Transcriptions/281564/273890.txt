<p>were to be seen everwhere through the cane &amp; grass brakes &amp; under the matted scrubs of pawpaw trees.</p>
<p>The forest is, despite the fact that no rain has fallen for four months, on every side impregnable. The taller trees such as figs, &amp; banyans, readfruit, cocoanut besides hundreds of others without name, &amp; matted together by lawyer canes &amp; numerous other vines all joining to make an impenetrable labrynth. We saw one pig but did not get a shot at it &amp; eventually retuned at five to barracks. Of course we had shot a pig &ndash; a monster with wild eyes and curling tusks which charged us through the scrub &amp; fell dead a few feet off us with two bullets buried in his brain.</p>
