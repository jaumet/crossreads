 9 April, Wednesday 
 F with a shower or two. 
 Catch 8.45 train to B'ford. Recd wire from I.O.M. Recd letter from Mrs Grundy. Referee Hockey Match &amp; chat with Cor &amp; get Photos taken. Class &amp; write. Tea at a Med. Learn of probable march out to Park House. Lovely night &amp; Venus shines brightly. 
 Write Capt Collister, Mrs Grundy, M.R.L. 
 10 April, Thursday 
 W. Recd letter from M. I go for a walk &amp; then go down to my room to lie down. I am supposed to march out but I don't. I go to classes &amp; try to write at 9 pm but I make an awful mess of the letter. Get to bed very sick &amp; very weary at 10.30 pm. 
 Write Home, Marion Clarke, Marg, M.R.L.
