<p>On my way back I came through Renmelhurst, this is a great military town for there are several Hqrs in her, including some Belgian</p>
<p>I believe our aeroplane's are reporting that the Germans are withdrawing all their big guns from the Ypres salient, &amp; the night before last our guns waited on them &amp; rocked it in hot &amp; heavy where they knew they were, I hope they don't evacuate their position before we get our mines off, when they do go up they will account for thousands of the squarehead's.</p>
<p>They have got a very pretty little regulation in force now in the Batt it is this, whenever a man goes sick, &amp; gets marked medicine</p>
