<p>Wednesday July 4th</p>
<p>Bapaume : showery : reveille 4 am : usual parades during day : after tea went down to see the boxing &amp; it was pretty good</p>
<p>Thursday July 5th</p>
<p>Bapaume : reveille 4 am : usual parades : nothing much doing : walk after tea</p>
<p>Friday July 6th</p>
<p>Bapaume : thundery : reveille 4 am : usual parades : boxing finals after tea &amp; very good : "boys" are very sorry to hear of Gen Holmes's death : roam round village near by during afternoon &amp; had feed of berries, found a bees nest in a life size crucifix : the bees got into the hollow figure through a crack in the side :</p>
<p>Saturday July 7th</p>
<p>Bapaume : reveille 4 am : stretcher drill during morn : sleep in afternoon :</p>
