 Tuesday 3rd September 1917 [1918] Taken to Cairo by Lt. Morrice (1st A.L.H.) met Capt. Chitty at Groppis, Capt. John son (A.H.Q.) 5 p.m. tea with Lt. Wanden (goes up the line tomorrow) very nice boy &amp; enjoyable afternoon. Hair treated (4th occasion) then bought bread, Pt. 2&frac12; Groppi's &amp; home with Lt. Morrice &amp; S. Nichol.
