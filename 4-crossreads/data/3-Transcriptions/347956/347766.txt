<p>Nov 7 - 8</p>
<p>winning the first round. some of the lads had a great time splashing is a big canvas tarpauling  to-day has been decidedly hot.  I believe we cross the equator tomorrow<br/>All the ships close in very close together during the night &amp; only steerage lights allowed after 8 o'clock.  had a boat alarm at 8 PM. also had a concert</p>
<p>Sun 8th<br/>Another horse died. it is very hot;  Church Parade &amp; witnessed a burial on the Euripodies, my throat &amp; cold is worse &amp; had a bad nights rest;  on duty on the bridge from 12 till 4 PM. got a thunderstorm &amp; a few points of rain about 2 PM.  been showery ever since;  Still headed 35&deg; West of North going very slow;  The Minitor (warship) disappeared this morning under full steam</p>
