<p>The World War</p>
<p>Rough Diary of my Service in A.I.E.F.</p>
<p>Enlisted 26/4/15 at Victoria Barracks.  Entered camp at Liverpool 21/4/15.  Transferred from Inf. to Signalling Depot 27/4/15?.  Subsequently attached as Signaller, 20th Battalion, 5th B'de.</p>
<p>26th June 1915</p>
<p>Battalion left Liverpool Camp at 8.30 a.m. &amp; entrained for Woolloomooloo Bay where we boarded troopship "Berrima" for port "unknown" (rumours said Egypt).  Wife, Father, Mother &amp; Sister met procession from Central Railway Stn. at Goulburn &amp; Elizabeth Streets &amp; accompanied me to steamer, <strike>except Dad who having Mab in his arms had to break off at Oxford St.</strike>  Had a great send off.  A crowd of small motor boats loaded with friends of troops, accompanying Berrima to Heads.  A rough night out, many sick myself O.K.</p>
