 SUNDAY, JUNE 29, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; sunny. Peter &amp; I drove down 
 to the rocks after foxes but got none 
 Had the ferrets round the burrows 
 in Mountain &amp; Middle but only 
 got a few bunnies. 
 Jo went over to a fox drive 
 at Beauly they got 3 over there 
 &amp; saw a good few more.
