<p>This place is on the main road from Lark Hill. It is owned by some old Ladies   &amp;   they have allowed it to be used as a Convalescent Home. They live there   &amp; their own servants with the aid of the Soldiers &amp;  a couple of nurses do the work. The Boys say It's a Home from Home.<br/>Harry</p>
