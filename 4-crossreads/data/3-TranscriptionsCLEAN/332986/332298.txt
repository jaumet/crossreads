
Frosty, very cold. 9 mules recieved from D.A.C. real culls. The Major very annoyed. General Walker expected tomorrow, great preparation, A Sgt from D.A.C. attached, Sgt Bourne.
4-12-1917First snow this winter, very cold. Frosty morning. Hun aeroplane over this morning at 4-30. General Walker did not arrive.
5-12-1917Hard white frost, sunny day. Half holiday Wednesday, Great activity along the front, We can hear the guns firing. General Coxon and staff in our lines.
6-12-1917Frosty morning, glorious day, Concert to-night
7-12-19A bit of a thaw set in, Weather fine, Concert last night rotten. Nothing doing, very quiet
8-12-1917 SundayWet all day. Referendum voting tomorrow,
