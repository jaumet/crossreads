The steamer steered a zig-zag course as a guard against attack, and we arrived after a three day trip at Lemnos Aug. 19th.  On the way we passed Zyra, Thynos, Dhlos, and Mikonos islands belonging to Greece, very barren looking places, when compared to our own sunny shores.  The entrance to Lemnos is protected by rows of mines and the harbour is full of ships of every description.  The shores are hilly in most parts, but entirely void of vegetation.
