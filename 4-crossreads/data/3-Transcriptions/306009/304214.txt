<p>1915<br/>Jan. 28 Thu.<br/>Still very busy. <br/>Knocked off at 8.30 p.m.<br/>Number of patients in hospital yesterday was 476.<br/>After knocking off went round lines &amp; visited Scoutmaster Joyner, 1st Woollahra &ndash; Paddington Troop (Pte. 1st <strike>Brigade</strike> Div. Signal Co.) &amp; S.M. Marshal 2 Waverley Troop  (2nd Battalion, Infantry Scout).<br/>Very pleasant evening with them.</p>
