<p>Longmoor : fatigue in morn &amp; issued with leggings &amp; other changes in afternoon :</p>
<p>Wednesday Jan 30th</p>
<p>Longmoor : fatigue in morn &amp; nice march in afternoon : we details get a very square deal here &amp; don't do much work :</p>
<p>Thursday Jan 31st</p>
<p>Longmoor : fatigue in morn : very cold &amp; damp mist which froze on everything &amp; presented a pretty appearance : V>D> inspection in afternoon</p>
<p>Friday Feb 1st</p>
<p>Longmoor : fatigue in morn &amp; afternoon : got 17 letters so it will be something to keep me going again : paid 30/-</p>
<p>Saturday Feb 2nd</p>
<p>Longmoor : on our ration job again :</p>
