<p>Yesterday afternoon I believe there was a tremendous reception given to the boy's they were some of a most successful raiding party which entered the German lines done a lot of damage &amp; brought back some prisoners. There is also in London here a big motor firm called the "Anzac" &amp; a new seaside resort Anzac on the sea. The train I came on this morning came right through without a stop, she pulled up at London Bridge, I then had to catch a train to Victoria &amp; from there I walked round to Peel House where I am now scribbling these lines, there was some rare fun last night packing up the little pictures I bought from Portsmouth Mr Duke would insist on packing them he is a</p>
