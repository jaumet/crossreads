<p>&amp;I swam right across &amp; back. Very fine eh? Ask the feeble one if he could do it?<br/>No more news dear old girl<br/>very much love to you all,<br/>always your loving brother<br/>Fred.</p>
<p>8/3/16<br/>Very sore throat today<br/>Hope Fieysie's did not turn out to be mumps<br/>It is rumoured that we may not be allowed to write for a few weeks if we move off from here.<br/>Fred</p>
