<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Brigadier</p>
<p>To - RAC</p>
<p>Date - 12 9 14</p>
<p>Time - 7.5 am</p>
<p>Have already recommended withdrawal of Troops from Wireless to KabaKaul thence to Herbertshohe as garrison - Infantry now at Herbertshohe to come on board Berrima which should then proceed to Rabaul to carry out original intention - If approved please instruct Beresford</p>
