 But we&#39;ve been very busy since we&#39;ve been on this stunt, due to the exigencies of the advance. &nbsp;It took just on 12 hours to reach our own lorries in a lorry, where we put in the night in a cellar. &nbsp;Next day we pushed on to here and have been our own bosses since. &nbsp;We have seen some of the liberated French peasants coming down from their villages to places of greater safety. 
 German signs and German work are evident everywhere about here.
