 No. 2 A.G.H. on the island of Gezireh in the Nile &ndash; another suburb, connected by 2 fine bridges &amp; with a good electric tram service. Island has sporting Club, Palace Hotel, Public Gardens, Aquarium &amp; homes of wealthy native &amp; Europeans. Accommodation for 1000 patients. Will Senior Major, 60 men in 6 wards. People in Cairo provided concerts &amp; comforts, papers etc. When men are wounded they are first attended to in the field by a Dr. if one is handy or by A.M.C. orderlies &amp; hurried back to a Clearing Station or Field Ambulance.  These are like small base hospitals.  They are staffed by Drs. &amp; orderlies
