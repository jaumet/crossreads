<p>Bailliue<br/>Friday 28<br/>Tom Livermore and I are living like we never lived, since leaving home, dainty meals &amp; good beds "Ville De Grand"<br/>Saturday 29<br/>A ride to Bailleu took "Sky Pilot" to the Veterinary Hospital Roads like glass<br/>Sunday 30<br/>The last Sunday of a year that was good, of a year that was hard, Delval Wood Malt Trench Loupart Wd Le Sars Lagnicourt Noreuil, Bullecourt Clairmarias Glencorse Passchendaele, all hard but still we live.</p>
