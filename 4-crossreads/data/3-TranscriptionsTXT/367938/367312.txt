 anything &amp; the hours seem like days: that is myself &amp; some of the higher officers &ndash; the others are fairly busy with the digging parties up on the posts but are not overworked. 
 Food is plentiful &amp; good &ndash; Breakfast was biscuit, cheese, jam &amp; tea: lunch was "steak a-de-onion" as good as any greek fish shop in Sydney: tea will be bacon, biscuits, jam &amp; tea &ndash; the bacon &amp; bully beef in the day time, I find makes me too thirsty &ndash; so cutting out meat 3 times a day &ndash; others similar &ndash; Rum ration frequently at night &ndash; 
 Clark is fatter than ever &amp; sleeps 20 hrs out of the 24. Gwilliam I have lent or given to Fitz P. as his man has influenza &ndash; We lose about 2 men a day from sickness or unfitness &amp; the large majority of these, like the wounded, disappear on  hospital  ships. About 4 of our casualty list of 90 or so have returned &amp; I hope they will come back in larger batches in a week or two &ndash; 
 In case the Turks get this &amp; it is "Information of use" to them I might say our force is being augmented daily &amp; that they will know it to their extreme discomfort when we want them to.
