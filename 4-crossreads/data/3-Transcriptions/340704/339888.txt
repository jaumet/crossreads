<p>December 1917<br/>Sunday 23<br/>Suffra.   Cold and showery all day<br/>Went outpost. Rained all night. Got soaked. 12 MN sat up.under tree until daybreak.<br/>Letter 47 JAR received.</p>
<p>Monday 24<br/>Suffra.  Xmas eve. Showery and cold. No chance of drying clothes<br/>Terrific gale and rainstorm during night. Got wetter.<br/>Xmas parcels arrived from [indecipherable]</p>
