 After I had  kept  got a few of the men away an English Staff Sergt. came along to tell me that he had got some trucks &amp; an engine on the light railway, &amp;amp that he could take the men right through. I told him to ask for instructions on the way, &amp; if he met nobody to take them right through which he accordingly did with the result that the whole of the cases were evacuated in 1&frac12; hours.
