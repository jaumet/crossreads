 My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters, 
 Am delighted to say a nice mail arrived from Australia about 2 days after I last wrote. One from Alice dated 11th Feb   &amp;  Ethel's with dear old Mothers dated Feb 25th. 
 The socks arrived to-day   &amp;  yesterday a nice little New Year Gift from Annie Roddick. A Diary   &amp;  Calander  so you see my old friends are still thinking of me. &nbsp;I was so pleased to know that you are all well   &amp;  seems to  be getting along all right. 
 Very pleased to hear that Elsie Gourlie is so happy, hope she has got the right man this time.
