<p>[Page 151]<br/>Passed a very cold night through having to sleep without blankets; which was quite unnecessary as the other sections had the use of theirs. <br/>Turned out at 3.45 AM &amp; moved off at 4.45 AM with our packs up for the first time &amp; after a fairly sharp march of about 4 &frac12; miles arrived at Moascoar at 6.40 AM.  At once set to to pitch camp which took up the greater part of the day.  Eventually we were allotted to our respective tents &amp; again settled down to await our departure from this country.</p>
