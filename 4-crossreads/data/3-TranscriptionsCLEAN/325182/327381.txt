  
 The enemy objective is Amiens.  He is held fast in front of Arras.  He is held all along the front according to latest reports. 
 5th We left Acheux by lorries after a long wait and reached Aubigny about 1 p.m. passing through  An  Amiens. 
 6 We managed to get a sleep in after all last night!  I went out to front line Bn. Hqrs. this morning with two of my sigs. and a runner to have a look round.  On returning I was informed that we were to move up into supports at once behind right Bn.
