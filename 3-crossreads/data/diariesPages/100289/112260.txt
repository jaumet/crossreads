Monday 4.6.17
Reveille 7 Am. Breakfast 8, Parade 9 Am Lecture "Gas" 9-12 noon. Dinner 12 to 2 pm. to Lecture Helmet practice 2 pm to 4 pm. Walked to Albert went to 5th Army Cinema show.
Tuesday 5.6.17
Lectures Gas Helmet drill as yesterday; fairly interesting but very hot oppressive in hut where lectures are given.
Wednesday 6.6.17
Last day of the School. Put through Smoke clouds, Tear Gas Cloud Gas. Have benefited by the 3 days instruction. Ford car called to take us back to Bapaume at 3.45 pm; had 3 blow outs 1 puncture on way back. 
Sent home saw edged bayonet to Father.
