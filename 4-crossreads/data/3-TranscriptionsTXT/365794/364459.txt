 Tuesday 27 February 1917 [26 February 1918] &frac12; day &amp; called to see Col. Dawson re red capes &amp; seniority here.  He &amp; his assistant tell me am 10 years the senior of these Aust. but no hope of taking my proper position for ages as there are crowds ahead of me &amp; as I did not come when should have done can only agree to this.
