<p>Sunday 21 January 1917<br/>Church parade in morning, outpost at night.  Scotties took over the redouts in front of us.</p>
<p>Monday 22 January 1917<br/>Cruel day, rained &amp; blew periodically, rained at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 23 January 1917<br/>Making targets all day for M.G. practice.</p>
<p>Wednesday 24 January 1917<br/>General fatigue.</p>
<p>Thursday 25 January 1917<br/>Nothing in particular doing.</p>
<p>Friday 26 January 1917<br/>Clarrie went to M.G. School Zeitoun, took horses to 152 &amp; led them back.</p>
<p>Saturday 27 January 1917<br/>Took horses for exercise.  Mail in.</p>
