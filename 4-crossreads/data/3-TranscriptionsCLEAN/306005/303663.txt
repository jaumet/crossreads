  
 23-12-16 totalling 1300 were with us they were allowed to camp here & make themselves comfortable for the night & a guard was posted around them, the first lot that came in were marched off to El Arish as soon as they arrived, there were a good many slightly wounded amongst them & the Medical men were kept busy tonight,  the mud huts (one of which was a Field Hospital) were all turned into hospitals.
