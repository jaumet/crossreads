<p>Continued from old diary.</p>
<p>28/9/16<br/>A good deal of artillery activity round here lately</p>
<p>30/9/16<br/>Seventh and Eighth bns. are having a raid tonight in front of our lines.</p>
<p>1/10/16<br/>The raid was successfully carried out last night. We hear the raiders killed about 40 germans. Our casualties were 2 killed.</p>
