
March, 1916Thursday 16After the parade this morning I got two bundles of letters, one from Mum dated 4-1-16 which gave me some pleasant reading.After tea went & played cards w/- dad & had a long chat about the various things the letters brought up.
Friday 17Sent off a cable to be despatched to mum.  Every spare minute I get during the day I spend answering my letters.In the evening I went into Heliopolis to get my watch from the jeweller8 am to 1 pm on Abbassia Range
