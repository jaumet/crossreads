  
 [Wednesday 1 November 1916 ] 
  Received your Parcel from Egypt with Goggles Glasses Razor Chocs Handkerchiefs & Bowel stuff, thank you all so much for sending same,   they will go back to Egypt with me tomorrow.  I only got word this morning that I had to catch a boat at  Devonport  tomorrow, one must be always ready, when a soldier. (Following paragraph is crossed out)   Had dinner with Laura at Holborn Restaurant, afternoon tea at the Trocadero, & lunch with Rupert Mathews at the Automobile Club.  Dolly of course was with me she is a real soldier's wife & does not make it worse for me.
