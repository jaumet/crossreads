<p>Sunday 20 May 1917<br/>"Bible Bangers Parade" did not go, nothing doing except grazing in evening, saw some troops going through gas.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic number for 6 and 6] (P.C.).</p>
<p>Monday 21 May 1917<br/>Preparing for "stunt" for blowing up Turkish railway bridge, big things expected.</p>
<p>Tuesday 22 May 1917<br/>Left camp at 3.30 for a big stunt (60 miles) proceeded to Tel-el-Fara &amp; formed up &amp; proceeded S.E. watered up in Wadi 10 miles S.E. of here &amp; proceeded to Khalasa all night march covering 30 miles arriving at 4 a.m.</p>
<p>Wednesday 23 May 1917<br/>Arrived at Khalasa at 4 a.m. &amp; waited for demolition party to commence, did a lot of work, we were reserves so saw nothing, left at 12.30 &amp; proceeded back to form up, waited up at a big well of water, arrived back at Shelal about 11-5.</p>
<p>Thursday 24 May 1917<br/>Mother's Birthday, sent cable through Mrs. Rickman.  "Stand to" at 3, fairly warm day.</p>
<p>Friday 25 May 1917<br/>Windy but quiet day.  Billy Campbell came over to see me, issued with new Day Books.  Picquet at night.</p>
<p>Saturday 26 May 1917<br/>On Q.M. fatigue, the rest of squadron went through a gas demonstration with their helmets.</p>
