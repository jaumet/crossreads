<p>day<br/>Monday Dec 24<br/>Preparing for Christmas dinner. Wet to day<br/>Tuesday Dec 25<br/>Christmas day Wet &amp; Cold. In morning we went to church service in a huge wine cellar. The service was conducted by Padre Waddy. After the service all hands could get as much wine as they wished. As usual several abused the privilege &amp; made fools of themselves. Our Christmas dinner was a success. What odds if we did get wet eating it. The toast of the day Absent Friends &amp; may we all be home Christmas 1918. Word came through the division would</p>
