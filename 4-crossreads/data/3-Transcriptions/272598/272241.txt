<p>though still a bit weak, have not been out of doors yet.  Very rough outside, rough sea running, have a great view of the sea from my window.</p>
<p>Wednesday 10.10.17 to Sunday 14.10.17<br/>Doing fine, all very quiet.  Paraded before Major &amp; Colonel &amp; was marked for "Blighty" on 11th.  Have been waiting to go each day since.</p>
<p>Monday 15.10.17<br/>Received 3 parcels from home dated 20 June 4 &amp; 14 July.  Rigged out in an old khaki suit &amp; evacuated from here at about 8 pm.  Driven by motor to Mers siding where we were put on hospital train which left about 11 pm</p>
