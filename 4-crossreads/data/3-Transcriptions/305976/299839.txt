<p>as bearer. Poor old Jack one of the best.<br/>Evening Glad &amp; I visited Theatre.<br/>Sunday Feb 25<br/>Last day at home, spent quiet day and caught Melbourne Express back to Camp<br/>Monday Feb26<br/>Back to the old home again with its stew, bread &amp; jam &amp; plenty of work.<br/>Tuesday Feb 27<br/>Usual routine<br/>Wednesday Feb 28<br/>Riding morning, afternoon resting night, sham fight collecting wounded not permitted return to camp until supposed patient brought in.<br/>Thursday March 1<br/>Photo of unit taken. Final equipment served out. Expect to embark Monday. All week end leave cancelled. Pretty rough</p>
