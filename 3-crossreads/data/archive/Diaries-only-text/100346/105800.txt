Saturday Nov 24th
Sutton Veny : still bad so went on sick parade got two days T D : went to Warminster in afternoon with Morrie went to pictures after tea : very fair
Sunday Nov 25th
Sutton Veny : church parade in morn nice : Fred self to Warminster in afternoon to Church at night : tres bien : met Bluey King : cold weather now
Monday Nov 26
Sutton Veny : route march games in morn : classification in afternoon also V.D. scabie inspection : sent the fags to Ern Beale : very cold weather set in lately : classification in afternoon I was left as I am for a wonder : Fred came up we wrote letters
