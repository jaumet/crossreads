
Sunday 8 October 1916Had fairly quiet day.  Usual work strengthening position.
Monday 9 October 1916Wet weather retarding work, things going well.
Tuesday 10 October 1916A good deal Artillery activity.  Nothing fresh of importance.
Wednesday 11 October 1916Expect to move very soon.  Finished up work in Trenches today.
Thursday 12 October 1916Finished up in Trenches today.  Expect to move tomorrow for "Somme".
Friday 13 October 1916Nos. 1 & 4 Sec. left as advance Guard to go as far as "Bac St. Maur".  Nos. 2 & 3 to leave tomorrow.  "Somme"!
Saturday 14 October 1916Coy. arrived at Vieux Berquinat about 8 p.m.  Marched about 2 miles to Billet.
Sunday 15 October 1916Drilling & other kinds of training all day.  Went to Vieux Berquinat - a small village.
Monday 16 October 1916Coy. training during morning.  Played football afternoon.  Vieux Berquinat at night.
Tuesday 17 October 1916Left Vieux Berquinat 6.15 p.m.  Marched to Bailieul west & entrained.  Left here 11 p.m.  Wet weather.
Wednesday 18 October 1916Arrived "Pont Remy" about 11 a.m.  Unloaded transport & marched about 4 miles to Billet at Francieres.
Thursday 19 October 1916Transport left at 6 a.m.  Had look at old Church built 1713.  Still in state good preservation.  Heavy rain.
Friday 20 October 1916Travelled all day per foot and motor & reached small village near Albert and camped for night.
Saturday 21 October 1916Left billet early & marched through Albert - much knocked about & reached bivouac on high ridges near High Wood.
