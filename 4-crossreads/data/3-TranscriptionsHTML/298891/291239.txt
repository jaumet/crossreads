<p>1917 June</p>
<p>5 Tuesday</p>
<p>Lovely warm clear day. At 10 went to see Miss Willis at 66 Victoria St. about going to Greece with women's S. Hospitals. Cooked at Minerva. Went to Sir John Tweedy about [indecipherable] at 3 &amp; returned to Caf&eacute; &amp; left at 6.</p>
