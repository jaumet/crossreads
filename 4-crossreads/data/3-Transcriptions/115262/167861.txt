<p>Wednesday 4 April 1917.</p>
<p>Field day till 12.30 pm.</p>
<p>Afternoon easy.</p>
<p>Big riot of 1500 men in evening, on account of general camp conditions &amp; Easter leave. Not much outcome of it. Men, especially interstate, riotous.</p>
<p>Wrote 2 letters &amp; turned in feeling not too good.</p>
<p>P. J. Fogarty transferred for duty to No 11 A.G.H. (Downhearted.)</p>
<p>Allo AM.C Reinfts&nbsp;<u>unallotted</u> now.</p>
