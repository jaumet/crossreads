  
 Octobre 7 
 Weather continues to be very cold & wet. We went for a short route march this morning full marching order - otherwise resting. Expect to move up toward the Lines tomorrow. 
 Octobre 8 Left camp at 10 a.m. & marched to a camp near Onderdom where we are staying for the night. Expect to leave here in the morning. It is raining very heavily tonight.
