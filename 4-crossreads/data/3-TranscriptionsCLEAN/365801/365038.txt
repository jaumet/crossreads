
Victoria BarracksSydney8.6.1916
Dear Mr Justice Ferguson,I thank you for returning Captn. Ross's letter.The arrangements in regard to the 'Cyclists' Unit are now made much cleared by a Table just received from Head Quarters.There is now embodied a 1st Anzac Army Corps. & a 2nd. [ditto] and N.S.W. (or 2nd M. Dist.) finds the reinforcements for the 1st Army Corps.  The Cyclist Unit is to be known as 1st Anzac Army Corps Cyclist Battalion & is to find 60 "other ranks" a month as reinforcements.Similarly, Victoria (3rd  M.D.) supplies the 2nd Army Corps, with 60 a month.  No other State or District will be called upon
