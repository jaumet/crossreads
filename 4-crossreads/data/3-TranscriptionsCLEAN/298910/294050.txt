
Not much doing all day. Had a letter from Mrs O'Kelly - wrote to May.
17/8/16  Thursday
Dull & raining - not many casualtys. French mortar Batterys very busy stirring up Fritz.
18/8/16  Friday
Dull & raining - Rather lively trench warfare - had a few calls during the night, the Snipers & Machine guns are cruel the way they play around our dug outs & thro the Cemetery - luckily for us we know the danger spots & try to duck when duty calls us.
19/8/16  Saturday
Dull & raining - motor Battery fellows got hell today - as soon as they start Fritz trains his artillery on them & before they get 6 shots over. We seem to think Fritz has some mechanical contrivance for detecting sound & is ready for them when they start. Poor Capt Jack & Corporal Fisher got badly knocked about.
