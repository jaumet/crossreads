  
 They used to hire donkeys & organize races thro the streets.  They visited the Citadel & exclaimed over the view. Wonderful things to see.  One place always intrigued our men on the way to Mena. 4 wives & 4 houses.  Many of the women still veiled - girls marry from 12 to 15, flies beggars, dirt, disease & smell. But soon the delight in something strange dies out & they realize that they are fighting to preserve their liberty & our happiness & well being - officers & men soon spent a good deal of time studying books & military methods.
