<p>2 February 1918 Saturday (X to X crossed out)<br/>X In a Tobacco tin sewn up in a part of my sleeve of undershirt I have posted Day 2 Bead necklaces 1 from Jerusalem, 1 from Bethlehem of little commercial value post also to Joe an Olive wood cigarette Case &amp; leather Match book from Jerusalem hope they arrive.X<br/>Am field officer today did a sham fight preparing for our next advance which will not be for some 5 or 6 weeks yet.</p>
