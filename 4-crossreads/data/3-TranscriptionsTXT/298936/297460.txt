 October 1915 
 Sunday 31 
 Artillery Rd. 
 Went into the trenches today &amp; had a good yarn with the fellows 
 All seem to think  of  a change is a likely thing in the near future. 
 The  [indecipherable]  trenches are being strengthened  &amp; prepared for such a contingency. 
 Parts of the units which that to Mudros for a rest have returned &amp; the rumour of our immanent departure is once more on the rounds.  The position is that the 3rd Brig. who landed first are still at it.
