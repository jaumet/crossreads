<p>Tuesday Feb.25.1919.   (107)<br/>7th day of leave. There was another severe frost last  night. I wrote to Cis this morning and also to Lizzie No 159. 3x My cousin Tom drove me into Pettigo this evening,&amp; my cousin Barbara also drove in with us. We travelled in an Irish Jaunting car.<br/>I stayed a liitle while in Pettigo, and saw Bella &amp; Kitty there, also their cousins the Shaws who have the saw &amp; corn Mills there which I was shown over yesterday. They accompanied me to the station &amp; caught the 10 past 6 train for Kesh, arriving there about 6.</p>
