
April, 1917. Friday 20Finer day & pleasant. Up at 9 & got rations. 1 letter. Read in afternoon and got new stove & then tried to pump. No good engine choked with oil. Spent till 6 cleaning her & must continue to-morrow. Man coming round to-morrow for pay & may mean a move. Read till 11. Hodgson came on his way to England for observatn. A.F.C. Very heavy bombardment on.
