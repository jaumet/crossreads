
25 April 1915Sunday3rd After Easter
No church parade because we have no parson.Clean up the lines   &  have a good rest.6 pm Get orders to prepair to shift but do not know where toAllied Troops make first successful landing on Gallipoli peninsula
1915 April 26Monday
Everything packed   &  on the New Zealand Transport waggons when we find it is only to Zeitoun a large reinforcement camp about two hours march from aerodrome.Arrive in Zeitoun very much disgusted with the new state of affairs.Pitch our tents on a rather low   &  loose sand patch in the burning sun
