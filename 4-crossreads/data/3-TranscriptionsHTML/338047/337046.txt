<p>1919<br/>May 28<br/>the party started out before us.  Sister Haggard &amp; I decided to study the map &amp; work it out on paper first. We got the solution &amp; walked straight out &ndash; no sign of the others. We chuckled &amp; waited  - still no sign. At last we asked the man in the lookout box if he could see them.  He said, Oh yes they came out about 5 minutes before you did. We crawled away crestfallen &amp; caught up with the others<br/>[Three photographs of, the party, the Dutch Garden &amp; the Avenues, Hampton Court]</p>
