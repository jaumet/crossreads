<p>There ain't no whores &amp; you can't get on the piss<br/>He's gone to a better land.</p>
<p>Also &ndash; (The game of Nap)<br/>I'll go one said Austria<br/>I'll go two said France<br/>I'll go three said Russia<br/>But she never got a chance<br/>I'll go four said Germany<br/>And wipe them off the map<br/>But they all dropped dead when John Bull said<br/>I'll go Nap!</p>
