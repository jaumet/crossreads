 December 1917 Wednesday 5 Enab. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quiet day Rained at night, Kept fairly dry We get plenty of wine from monastery.  Septics healing rapidly 
 Thursday 6 Enab. Nice morning. Hope to be in Jerusalem within 7 days. Night turned very cold and rainy. The coldest night I have ever felt. Just like Katoomba.
