<p>This is my last straw. Having done my best to get away I am refused. My friend Sgt A.H. Legge, who accompanied me all along to back me up will certify to all &amp; sundry  who wish to doubt  that I have not done my duty at least in trying. (He has had fourteen weeks on the Peninsula) I may again make an attempt at some future date, but now oblivious to anybody's remarks I shall consider I am doing my share in Cairo as my officers think &amp;  be   d &ndash; d to all in Australia.</p>
<p>Whilst in Ismalia I had a good look all around the town, which is a rather pretty little place, something remarkable for Egypt</p>
