
A.1. Our comforts are on the way   &   we will soon have them too.  I see nothing, my dear ones   &   cannot give you any news.  I spend my time writing   &   with an occasional visit to the Theatre I have no idle moments.  We are both well & very soon may get some more leave.  I will spend a few days with Jennie   &   on the next leave I'll go to the Island again.  I have just received 10   &   Ellis has 10 also. We still have a 5 each so we will be able to go on for quite a long time.  Now My Dears, I have a little job to do   &   having no news I reluctantly say au RevoirHeaps of Love FromYour own Boy Harry
