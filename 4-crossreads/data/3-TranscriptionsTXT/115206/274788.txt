 August,1918 Saturday 24 
 In Rear of Proyart. Bty Position Weather dull, but nice and cool. Fritz planes flew fairly low all day. News from all fronts seems pretty good. Built a nice  dugout under the road. Received a letter from Uncle Will &ndash; a nice interesting one. Ken Gun and Red Palmer left for Waggon Lines early in  the evening. Had trouble with Bdge line and walked it . Bty pulled out on the main road. Fritz bombing all roads fairly heavily. Slept in a dug out near the road.
