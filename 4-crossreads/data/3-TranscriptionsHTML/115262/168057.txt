<p>Wednesday 17 October 1917.</p>
<p>25 Reinforcements arrived today from Moascar where they left a couple of days ago.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Some of them who came to Egypt on the &quot;Wiltshire&quot; have not been off the boat a fortnight yet, and are up here at the front already.</p>
<p>Gas parade at 3 p.m.</p>
