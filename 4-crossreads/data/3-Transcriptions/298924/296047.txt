<p>Thursday 6 March 1919<br/>Latest is Dolly goes on the 14th Mch. for certain on the Euripides or Derbyshire, both fast good boats so I will cable when she sails name of Boat &amp; probable date of arrival Melbourne.  It will be a load off my shoulders when she is safe on board with her Kiddy.<br/>Love to all,<br/>All's well.</p>
