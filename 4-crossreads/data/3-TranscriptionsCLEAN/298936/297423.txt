  
 September 1915 
 Friday 24 
 Still on fatigue work but did not overwork. 
 There are not a great amount of invalids here for the hospital is expected to move to Anzac any day. 
 Got notice to parade at 6 p.m. 
 Paraded & got kits ready to move away. Taken in ambulance to camp commandants camp over on an island which is reached by a causeway of stones. 
 Miserable  [indecipherable]  camp; had to sleep out to avoid lice.
