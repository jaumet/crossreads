<p>Nov. 15th (Wednesday)<br/>Route march in the morning, and back in time for dinner.<br/>After dinner we were marched to the oval where we had sports, Tea and cakes provided by the Ladies of Capetown.<br/>About 5 O'clock we marched to the Feather Market where the Y.M.C.A. provided us with Tea, scones, cakes and fruit, then a concert after, but I don't think many of the Boys remained</p>
