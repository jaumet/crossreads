 18th August Tuesday 10.30 Marched down to the McQuarie Wharf. Taken by ferry to Cockatoo Is. where embarked on the Berrema &ndash; Mess C10 Dinner very decent Sling hammocks for beds &ndash; also extremely decent. Sweet girl gives our hero a handkerchief as a mascot to carry him through the campaign.
