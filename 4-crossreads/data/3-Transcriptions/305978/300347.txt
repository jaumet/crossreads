<p>It appears that a Tommy had taken a prisoner &amp; only took his rifle from him &amp; never troubled to search him for arms. Col Snow approached the him with some question relating to the enemy, where upon the prisoner drew a flint lock pistol &amp; shot Snow in the stomach, the poor fellow suffered great pain before he died. Of course the prisoner did not stand up long after that affair.<br/>It just shows that you cannot be too careful.<br/>All the nigger population has left this town, they were frightened</p>
