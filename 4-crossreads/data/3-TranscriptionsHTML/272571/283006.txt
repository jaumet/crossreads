<p>Jan 20th<br/>Finished reading "A Son of Hagar" by Hall Caine.</p>
<p>Jan 21st<br/>Tugged up to the wharf about two thirty. An exciting thing happened as we were being tugged up the harbour. A few of the native boatmen who pester our lives out in these harbours were sailing near the ship. Our propellor suddenly began to work causing a kind of suction and it was with great difficulty that these boats got free of it. One boat with a man and a boy in it upset but were saved.</p>
