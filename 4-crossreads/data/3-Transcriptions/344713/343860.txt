<p>Thursday 4th November.<br/>Joined our respective companies in the battalion.<br/>Captain Stacey in charge of Company "D"<br/>We are in reserves &amp; have our quarters dug into the sides of the communication saps.<br/>"Jacko" had a bit of a demonstration affair our first night here.<br/>Bullets like bees.</p>
<p>Tues. 9th Nov.<br/>Our first payday on ANZAC today. Paid the large sum of 10/- by Capt. B.V. Stacey.</p>
<p>Sat 13th Nov: -  The first casualty the 8th regts of the 4th occurred today pte A J Stanhope got a bit of a shell up against his ankle as he was lying in a dugout.</p>
<p>Thurs 20th Nov.<br/>Paid &pound; 2.10.0 today by Cpt. Stacey.<br/>Received letter from Mother  &amp; the first letters I've had since I left Aust were only received just a week ago.<br/>Written from Anzac to everyone but the outgoing mails are reported to be capsized  a Lemnos.<br/>I wonder if we'll receive a Christmas mail.<br/>Still in reserves.</p>
