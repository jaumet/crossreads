<p>&nbsp;Gardens (Sick Sisters Hospital)   &amp;  had dinner with them, saw all my late patients   &amp;  we had a merry time together.</p>
<p>Sister Pilkington   &amp;  Jones are going back to Aust. so may be on the same  boat as I'm on.</p>
<p>Yesterday (Saturday) Sister O'Dwyer   &amp;  I went to Myra's from Dinner   &amp;  we had a jolly time. Her husband has been mentioned again   &amp;  he is a D.S.A., so she is very delighted, my she is a lucky woman.</p>
<p>Received a nice photo from Dorothy for Xmas   &amp;    a little letter.</p>
