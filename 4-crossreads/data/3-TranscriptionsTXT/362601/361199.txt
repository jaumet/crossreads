5 May, Monday
F &amp; WI go down to Station to see auntie off to Douglas. More Brekker. I do all the scullery work &amp; get nicely blacked up. Dinner &amp; I am cook's offsider. We sit by fire &amp; I wash although I have to stop 2 yards away &amp; I don't stop. We go inside for music but read &amp; talk a little instead. I slip onto the floor. M. is very tired but we go to meet auntie in the rain. Vivian's horse has a foal &amp; he does not go to town Elddoc.
6 May, Tuesday
F.Marie &amp; I go to Douglas by 9 train, morning tea, shopping, dinner &amp; I walk to Granma's. we get costume, hat, stockings, catch electic train back &amp; I see Ballandane &amp; [?] Mona Lasdy &amp; Snaefell. Auntie Jennie tells fortune. We are very confidential in Laxly train &amp; I hear of years of awful trials of a very lovely girl.
