4.1.1917
My dear JudgeI am back again in London after spending a very enjoyable five days in Scotland. We went to Inverness for two days & spent the remainder on the time in Edinburgh.This morning I was summonsed before a Medical Board & passed fir for general service. I report to Base camp on Saturday 6th & shall probably got to France again a few days later.I have had a grand rest & a splendid time over here & am quite ready to go back to the Battalion now, even if I had any choice.
