  
 Thurs. 23rd:  Crossed Equator. Sat 1st Feb:  Reached Fremantle 9 a.m:  Went ashore after dinner. Sailed again at 7 p.m. Thurs 6th:  Arrived Adelaide. Went ashore after dinner Frid 7th:  Reception in the town Sat 8th:  Picnic at National Park. Sun 9th:  Sailed 6 a.m. Mon 10th:  Arrived at Port Melbourne Tues 11th:  Pulled in to pier 9 a.m. Went ashore until Wed 12th:  Sailed 4 p.m.
