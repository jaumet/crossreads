<p>from the desert tenfold. But I would much rather have it like this than cold like at Zeitoun a month or so ago. The nights turn pretty cool, and we have to be very careful not to catch cold. Already several of the boys are sniffing &amp; have watery eyes, and something worse sets in so quickly unless you are very careful.</p>
<p>So long for the present Mother dear and I expect to receive a big mail in a day or two now. I am, honestly, quite well so you need have no fear in that direction.</p>
<p>Heaps of Love to all the family and yourself as well.</p>
<p>Alan</p>
<p>I don't know how much of this will be censored or if I have put any thing I should not have, but I am afraid I will no be as lucky as I have been in respect to censoring.</p>
