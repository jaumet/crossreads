<p>1916 September 28<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Usual duties. &ndash; erecting cupboards in rear of wards.  Mitchell &amp; self. Usual morning refresher at Y.M.C.A. Cart &amp; self into Pop at 4 p.m. fail to meet Bob &amp; Tony at Talbot House at 6 p.m. Go to Cinema at Railway. Good programme of singers.  Met Bob coming out.  He was late &amp; came on after.  Home to Y.M.C.A. refreshments. To bed &amp; "chats." Further great victories on the Somme.  Combles &amp; Thiepval fall.</p>
