<p>one is apt to be downcast when looking backwards &amp; recollecting the mates he has lost, one dismisses the past &amp; looks forward to a brighter future.</p>
<p>We are at present camped in big marquees, fifty men to each with plenty of squeezing. It's some job writing under the circs, two parties of Nap &amp; Bridge players in full swing, "Snowy" Chudleigh (Barney knows him) banging discord? from his commotions &amp; ex Woolloomooloo policeman Flynn in opposition with his mouth organ. To make matters worse a dispute <strike>of</strike> on War is heard waging hot &amp; strong when the others harmony lulls somewhat.</p>
<p>Notwithstanding the foregoing it's a real treat to be spelling away from</p>
