<p>16 Th August<br/>Taking line 600 yds  Jacko very warm laying out no cover  Lieut Tim Black our troop leader seriously wounded [indecipherable]</p>
<p>17 Fri<br/>Having a quiet day  Arrived back from Post 8 am  Tim Black died wounds at Rafa</p>
<p>18 Sat<br/>Leaving for Beach for a good spell  relieved by Tommies</p>
