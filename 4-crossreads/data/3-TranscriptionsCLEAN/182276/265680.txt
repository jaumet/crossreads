  
 9th. M.G. Coy No VI Intelligence Report from 6 am 5th to 6 am 6th Right Sector:- No 5 gun fired from I 17 a 8/4 to I 18 c 3/0 road from La [Bleau?] a Bas Trou- between 6 pm & 8 pm. No 6 gun fired I 17 b 5/8 to I 11 a 9/3 communication trenches- 750 rnds- results not observed. There were two alarms of gas at 6.45 pm and 11.30 pm. Left Sector:- No 7 gun fired 1000 rnds on I 6 b 8/9 roads and trenches round Le Temple. No 8 fired on I 6 a 5/0 Road and Dump at Intact Farm. between 7 & 11 pm. Aeroplane heard overhead about 12.15 mid. Bright moonlight night. Sleeting & cold at Stand to. 
 B.J Carey Capt 9th M.G. Coy 6.12.16
