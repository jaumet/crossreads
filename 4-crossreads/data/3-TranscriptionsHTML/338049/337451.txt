<p>Thursday 29 August<br/>Had tea on arrival home. Slept in until about 10.30 am breakfast being brought into the tent. Had an easy morning.</p>
<p>Move ordered immediately after dinner for 2 pm. Move via Bray, Cappy to Suzanne &amp; camped near Chateau. Made ourselves comfortable.</p>
<p>Then I went in box car to Curlu to take cable forward to Page. Shelling heavy. Great ride.</p>
