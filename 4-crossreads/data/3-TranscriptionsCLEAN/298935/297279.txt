Full baskets are brought up one plank and empties  can  carried down another.  Natives do not appear to work happily unless making a terrible din and so the whole bunch, a hundred or more, sang es  and bawled continuously.  The row, or I should say the strains of melody, nearly tickled our fellows to death.  Any slackers in the gang were brisked up by the ganger who carried a little cane for the purpose.
