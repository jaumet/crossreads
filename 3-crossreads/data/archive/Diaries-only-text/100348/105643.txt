There was some snow last night. I thought it was frost at first. Paraded early to hear that the fiat has gone forth; saying that we are very lax, and need to be straightened up; so the next week or so will be spent, doing infantry training, and "physical jerks", to make us fit to go "once more to the breach";. A nice district this; I am looking forward to a pleasant holiday.
24/11/16.
