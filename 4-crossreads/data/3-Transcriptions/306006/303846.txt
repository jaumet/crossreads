<p>Hissieh . B midway between, &amp; A out in front, I was going till after dark; Our Headqrs is situated on a hill in the Wadi &amp; quite close to the water which is very good &amp; is a spring from a rock, supposed to be the rock struck by Moses the air around here is rather putrid from 18 dead horses on the side of the hill &amp; a little higher up the Wadi, the result of a bomb; The banks of the Wadi are very steep &amp; high &amp; only crossable at certain points Sutherland left us this morning.</p>
<p>27-4-17<br/>Fri 27th<br/>The last 3 days have been very hot &amp; no blankets required during the night, but there is a change</p>
