
May, 1915Sunday 16Col Meredith & I went up to the Nile in a [indecipherable] or Felucca with some English people we have met. I feel it is no time for pleasure jaunts with our mates fighting across the sea, but it was very enjoyable -I did not post as stated on the 10th as I expected to have news to tell you but post tonight & trust it May reach you safely.Posted you Egyptian Paper yesterday.F.W.
