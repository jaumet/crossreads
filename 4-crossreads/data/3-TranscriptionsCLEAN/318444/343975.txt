  
 Tues Jan 1st 1918 New Year's Day spent on the 'phone lines from Derry House to front positions. Plenty of snow. 
 Tues Jan 15th 5th BN. Relieved us in line & we are still kept around Wytschaete in open positions. 
 Tues Jan 22ND:- Relieved 2nd BN in line near Ousterverne Wood. 
 Wed Jan 30th Left line tonight being relieved by the 54th BN. & went back to dug outs at Wolverghem & stopped the night.
