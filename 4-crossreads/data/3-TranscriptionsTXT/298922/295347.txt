 Sunday 21 January 1917 
 Regiment staying out at the Bivy &amp; the rest may go out tonight. Just got letters from Mother l5/11/16 &amp; Joe enclosing Diary 12/11/16 Diary &ndash; been to England &amp; back, I don't think we missed many. 
 Very sorry poor little Blackie is dead &ndash; I know what a dog's affection is worth. Re Bulletin Pars re self &ndash; there  won't be  any PRAM coming home with Dolly &amp; I!  Will post this as I never like risking losing or anyone finding my Diary on me. 
 28 Turksjust be sent in by Regiment. The Bedouins kill them if they get stragglers or deserters. I suppose they would us too.
