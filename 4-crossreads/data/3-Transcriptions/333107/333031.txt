<p>Thur. Ap. 25  -  Anzac Day</p>
<p>Glorious sunshining day.</p>
<p>Had good dinner and some were supplied with Beer. I had none, but as it was heavily watered, did not miss much.</p>
<p>George Lawson, Tich and I attended the Sports in the afternoon. Two Bands were there and a good number of Ladies.</p>
<p>After Tea went into Warminster to the Pictures.</p>
