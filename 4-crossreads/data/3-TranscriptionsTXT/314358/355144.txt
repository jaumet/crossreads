 5 This fall of snow is fine There is as much as four inches of the magic whiteness everywhere over the ground, and of course deep drifts of it in places. The khaki boys are making great ugly snow men. Oh dear, Oh dear, how ugly and vile is everything which the masses do. The horrible and base the ugly, the crude and the sordid. All this is theirs to be thoroughly enjoyed. The man how drew our bones in the old stone age was an artist nobler and greater and cleaner than any one of the masses of today.
