
solid Dickson, & very strong. His face is reddish with broad effects, & shows more signs of obstinacy & strength than of intelligence. Dickson described his mental attitude well yesterday when he said, "He's got a little box of opinions & when he starts to argue the point he brings it out, opens it, & dishes them out one by one & then ---" I finished the comparison for him. "He shuts the box up, puts it back & goes home, leaving you gasping," said I.
