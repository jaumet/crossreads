<p>another site in Jerusalem. Arrived there about 8 pm and slept in open. It rained during night and in morning all hands were drenched.<br/>Friday March 15th<br/>Foggy morning. Seems like home to hear the clocks striking the hour church bells ringing. Left for sightseeing 9 AM. Riding along road we passed English Cathedral a massive stone building, the German[?] Hospital Damascus &amp; Jaffa Gate. These gates with others are the entrance into old Jerusalem. They are a tremendous height about 90ft. foundation<strike>to</strike> of Jerusalem wall supposed to be 40 to 50 ft depth &amp; walls 40 ft high. We rode round the outside</p>
