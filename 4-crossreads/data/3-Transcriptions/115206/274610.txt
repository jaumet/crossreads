<p>February 1918<br/>Saturday 9</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Weather very windy and dull. Took a turn at O.P.<br/>Kelly and Jones observing officers<br/>No. 3 gun did a good piece of shooting scoring four consecutive hits on a tree.<br/>Was on 2 to 4 last night.<br/>Dugout leaking somewhere.</p>
