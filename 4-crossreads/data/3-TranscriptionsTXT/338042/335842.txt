
April 3rdA trip out to Fred's lodging - A rehearsal of a "Breezy Morning - A new acquaintance - two shows - Bed after midnight - Previous to retiring we meet & converse with American sailors off the Leviathan (65.000 strong).April 4thM.M. - Voluntary entertaining to Wounded Pommies at a Liverpool Hospital - On our Bill at the Olympia are King & Benson, Elliot Savonas, Tom Clare etc.
