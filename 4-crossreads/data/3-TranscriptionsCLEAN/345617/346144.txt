  
 Aout 10 Not much doing today. A little Sec. drill & overhaul of equipment & a little marching. Nothing of importance reported from the Fronts. 
 11th Had Coy route march today, distance about 12 miles. The roads are very hard & are very tiring for the feet. 
 12 Aout Had easy time today. Cpl. Morrison & I went to St Omer & had an enjoyable day.  Had a look at 3 old cathedrals. One is a mere ruin only the tower & one wall standing.
