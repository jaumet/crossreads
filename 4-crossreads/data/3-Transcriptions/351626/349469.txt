<p>21.5.1916 Tel El Kebir Light Horse Details<br/>Dear Mother,<br/>Just writing a few lines to let you know everything is alright, although there is no news to tell you even if the letters were not censored everything seems the same here from week to week.  I have so far been unable to get a move since returning from England.  It is not much use writing from here as there is nothing to write about the</p>
