<p>August, 1916<br/>Saturday 19<br/>The last stage of the journey completed<br/>Arrive at Havre in afternoon &amp; after long march made camp again<br/>obtained some rations &amp; TEA<br/>spent very cold night</p>
<p>Sunday 20<br/>mooched about all morning<br/>After dinner were moved back to the docks &amp; embarked on the "Queen" for Blighty.<br/>spent an awful night<br/>men were sick all round me but I escaped</p>
