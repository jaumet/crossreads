 July 1917 The wharf is very nearly finished now.   The 8th &amp; 14th Field Coys &amp; Nos 2 &amp; 3 sections of our company have been working on it of late &amp; have made a considerable progress. 24th   The section were out drilling again this morning &amp; had the afternoon off.   The Major left for Blighty this morning on leave, &amp; Capt Caddy in acting O.C. 25th   This morning the whole Company went for a route march for about 10 miles &amp; returned at dinnertime.   This afternoon we all went for a swim.   I made out my will to-night making everything over to Mother.
