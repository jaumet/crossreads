<p>Thursday 16 August 1917.</p>
<p>Heard that mail for Australia closes today. Posted letter<s>s</s>.</p>
<p>Paraded with arm which has turned septic. Put on no duty &amp; treatment. Had arm dressed at 5 pm at Convalescent Depot and went on leave to Cairo to get more camera supplies for taking up the line.</p>
<p>Wrote home this afternoon.</p>
