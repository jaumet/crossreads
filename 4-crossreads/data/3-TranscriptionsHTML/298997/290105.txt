When I saw my bearers I wanted to get out &amp; walk worse than ever; but they did not let me drop.  Flowers were thrown in to us in the Ambulance &ndash; a nice idea.</p>
<p>Fritz was over the <strike>day night</strike> week before we arrived here, on a bombing excursion, and did a lot of damage near this hospital.  A big barrage of searchlights is put on nightly, searching the heavens for marauding Germans.  I lie awake at night and watch them.</p>
<p>When I asked the doctor when he thought I'd be fit to return to France he seemed to think the question a joke; so I dn't think an early return likely.  The doctor here has assured me that he does not believe in the consumption idea, however.  That worried me a lot, because consumption it seems to me, necessitates a semi-isolation from ones family, if it is severe.</p>
