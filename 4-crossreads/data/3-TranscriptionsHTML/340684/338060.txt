<p>&amp; the post is closing-  If we do go to Aldershot by any chance the first thing I shall do will be to go out to Hampstead-  I was so hoping we would go to Sydney first I might have had a chance of seeing you-  It would have made things easier-  However come what may you can rest assured I'll do my duty-<br/>Please give my love to all the family-  All my belongings such as they are are stowed at Archibald Clarke &amp; Sons.<br/>Good-bye Father I'll write every chance I get-<br/>Fondest love to you<br/>Your affectionate son<br/>Gilbert McMillan<br/>P.S.  I have given your name &amp; address as my next of kin-<br/>My number is 13/604<br/>11th regiment &ndash;New Zealand Mounted Rifles</p>
