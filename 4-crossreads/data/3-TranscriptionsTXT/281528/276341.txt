
22/4/18 - Lavievilleall on the run.  Sgt. Hewitson was talking to me and got a smack on the finger that drew blood.  Fatty Sutherland a subaltern who only joined up yesterday after a long time on soft jobs was wounded in two places. Luck!.  After dinner Bowes Kelly blew in - 6 feet odd of misery.  Put him to bed and gave him some tea.  He has had a rough time today having a number of men killed and others wounded.
