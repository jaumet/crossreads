<p>June 17th<br/>Reveille 6 a.m. &ndash; Pack up &amp; leave for Montreuil (G.H.Q's) &ndash; An omnibus ride of 65 kilos &ndash; We pass through some very fine country &amp; some equally wretched villages &ndash; A spell at Abbeville &ndash; Arrive Montreuil 1 p.m. General Haig's H.Q's &ndash; This small town was originally fortified by Romans &amp; for hundreds of years has been a garrisoned town &ndash; It stands on a great hill from which surrounding villages &amp; landscapes may be well observed.</p>
