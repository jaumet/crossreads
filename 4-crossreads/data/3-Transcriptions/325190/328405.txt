<p>Apparently they had rioted some days ago, &amp; consequently their ranks had been depleted by the exit to Australia of numerous undesirables.  It appeared to me a great pity that numbers of very young &amp; manly boys should have so deteriorated in serving their country.<br/>Their appearance, language, morals &amp; general behaviour are bad on the whole.  This seems to be the result of mixing first with the elder men, &amp; their being grouped by themselves to foster the growth of the bad influence.  In addition, things were not improved by the parade ground harsh discipline</p>
