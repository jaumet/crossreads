About 5 p.m. it commenced to rain and I took a hurried walk down to the Galleries Royale and round the secondhand bookshops. Dined at the Palace Hotel with the General and James and then went down to the Opera or Theatre Royale as it is called. We found here that we were rather early so we walked up to the Boulevard Anspach. This is a remarkably fine street and straight as a die. Handsome shops line each side like Boul Mich at Paris and it is most brilliantly lit by the street lamps close together which stretch in two long lines for what seems to be miles.
