 Friday. 6/4/17 cloudy &amp; Raining slightly. Plenty of Planes about. also some of fritzs. was told we were here for a month &amp; that leave would start Pay 16/7 &pound;23.9.0 Bought some Butter F.4. 30 s. cigs. 1.30 Biscuits 3. frs. milk 1.20 Syrup. 2. frs. spend all your money in eatables. our ceiling leaking, no roof, all gone 
 Saturday 7/4/17. Rained all last night &amp; the water dripped on us no way of stopping it. so put Blanket over head &amp; snore &amp; bore it. Snowing this morning a glorious climate no wonder we are cheerful. 2 days ago hot enough for summer now winter again. Not feeling too well today Pay. 17/7. &pound;23.10.0. clear again this Afternoon. 
 Sunday. 8/4/17 A Beautiful night last night, full moon. Cloudy this morning but fine &amp; mild, not feeling well yet. Pay. 18/7. &pound;23.11.0 mail in got letter from Trustee Coy. telling me of &pound;450 left me. &pound;200 now &amp; &pound;250 when Uncle Jack &amp; Miss Wyndham dies answered it letter from Viv also still in Hospital. Newton a Major Genr now Thank goodness theres something waiting for me if I get home Paid 14/4 Bal owing to me 4/3 
 Monday. 9/4/17 Rain, sleet &amp; snow this morning no two days fine can bet on it Pay 5/3 &pound; 23.12-0 Wrote to Syd &amp; Viv will have to wait till I hear from Nell. Terrific Bombardment left of Bapaume this morning 3rd Army going them hell taking Prisoners by the thousands Livingston the waster gone to B. Coy as Platoon Sergeant
