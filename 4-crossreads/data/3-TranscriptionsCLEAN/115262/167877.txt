  
 Friday 20 April 1917. 
 The march. Early breakfast. 
 Left camp 8.25. Trawool bridge 9.30 1st Hill reached summit & was 4th on the highest peak at 10.15   Some climb too.   
 Trawool weir at 11.15   
 Trawool falls 11.25. Dinner at T'wool Hotel at 2.15   Rested 3/4 hr on the road. Arrived camp 4.30. Shower & tea. Early to bed. 
 We carried water bottle & haversack each. The scenery was  beautifu  l   in the gully below the Trawool falls. Best part near Seymour for scenery.    
 Volunteered for week-end duty.
