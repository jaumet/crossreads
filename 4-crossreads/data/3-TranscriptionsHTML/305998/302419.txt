<p>some have gone to Bombay India. There is talk of us being sent to Aldershot to be trained, but I don't think somehow; of course it depends where we will be sent to after trained.</p>
<p>21/ 14th Rfts of 6th Rgt. arrived here 100 strong, a reinforcemt used to be only 50 men. I was short of a fur band for my hat till they came; in fact there is not many of the Rft left with furs. We were issued with helmets for hot weather they are very heavy things after the old felt hats.</p>
