<p>Tuesday 24 July 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille as usual. No riding on mules this morning as they are being inoculated.</p>
<p>Received local papers from home and &quot;Bulletin&quot; from Joy.</p>
<p><s>Kantara was bombed by a Turkish taube last night so report goes. Hardly creditable</s>.</p>
<p>Rumors as to marching out.</p>
<p>Went to main Moascar Camp to see 99 Cpl. H. J. Brennan 4 A.L.H. who called about G. Kelley last Friday afternoon. Never saw him.</p>
