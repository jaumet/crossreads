<p>17/1/18 Recd letter from Dick Luscombe.<br/>18/1/18 Very mild temperature. Wrote to Uncle Fred. Went up to top of Napoleon's tower but could not see across to England on account of the haze. <br/>19/1/18 Recd letters from Guild &amp; Partridge. Still mild.<br/>21/1/18 Read letter from Uncle Will &amp; answered same. Sent parcel to Australia last Thursday.<br/>23/1/18 Recd letter from home &amp; answered same.<br/>24/1/18 Wrote to Bob Clarke. Marked No Four at the inspection. Sold revolver for 15 francs to the barman</p>
