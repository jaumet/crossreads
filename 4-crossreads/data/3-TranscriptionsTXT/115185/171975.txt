 Much much love my exquisite Mother Devotedly your daughter Betha 
 5th March 1916 3. A.G.H. Abbassia Barracks Cairo 
 Sunday 10.15 pm 
 My own beloved Mother I thought of sending you a cable to wish you many happy returns of today. But I could not get into town to do so until too late. I have thought of you all day. I have had to write the date so often on requisitions etc and each time, the thought, "Mother's birthday", has come to me. I wonder what you did and if Father remembered in time to have a gift ready? I do wish you were here. You would be so interested in everything. But it's awfully sad. Of course &ndash; I know it's sad in Australia &ndash; but here you'r up against it all the time.
