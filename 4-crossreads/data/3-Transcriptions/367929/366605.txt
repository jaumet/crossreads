<p>March 1915<br/>30 Tuesday<br/>A Sqn go by road to Barrage to practise crossing rivers by means of ropes endless.</p>
<p>31 Wed<br/>Went by train to Barrage with Dr Fullerton. Had tea &ndash; and dinner at Mr Smith the chief engineer of the Barrage System<br/>A Sqn swam horses and B arrived per road.</p>
