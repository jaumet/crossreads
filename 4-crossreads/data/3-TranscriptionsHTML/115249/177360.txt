<p>W.T. Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship - Berrima</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 11 Sep</p>
<p>Time of Despatch - 11.20 am</p>
<p>For Brigadier and Commander Beresford From information from a prisoner all Germans have orders to wear a green band on each arm and natives have orders not to shoot at any one with this badge.&nbsp;</p>
