<p>I was at the Base at Havre for about five days &amp; was in the Town for one night for which I was up for orderly room but I got dismissed with a caution. I left the base on Friday 24th Aug in the usual troop-train cattle &ndash;trucks &amp; no room to move in them  After two days travelling I got back to the Battn after being nearly six months away.  I found a lot of new faces there &amp; a good many old ones missing<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Battn were in the rest area at a small village named <u>Vandringheim</u>, about 6 kilos from <u>Lumbres</u>)  We got plenty of drill &amp; had to polish everything up to the knocker  On the whole we had a good time whilst</p>
