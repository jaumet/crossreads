<p>Situation Report<br/>13-10-17</p>
<p>Everything nearly O.K.<br/>S.A.A expended Nil<br/>Change of Personnel Nil<br/>Casualties.<br/>Pte Ripps sick &amp; T. feet<br/>" Cox shell shock<br/>" Lewis "  " wounded<br/>" Watson wounded</p>
<p>Very heavily shelled all night all calibres still going yet. Am getting up all that bulk S.A.A. to dump near road. Men rather shaken &amp; would like rum ration. Lt Cowley reported I have sent for Mitchell. I have been in agony all night with feet, little better this morning after rubbing will try &amp; carry on here. Have plenty of Rations, not much water. Can I tell the men when they are being returned to buck them up</p>
<p>PHS Weston Lt 5.30am</p>
