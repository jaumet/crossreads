12.6.16 Monday
I am still feeling a little squarmish on it as the sea has not abated last night was a pearl I can assure you. The boat seemed to stand on her beam ends.We left Corsica behind last night are now steaming up the Gulf of Lyons.Our Captain is trying to get into Marseilles before nightfall, if he doesn't we will have to fool about outside until morning. The realization of my hopes is is now pretty well fulfilled tomorrow I will stand upon French Soil.A good way of expressing
