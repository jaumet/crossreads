<p>12 June 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Go on a sapping job at 8pm   &amp; get the order to dig a hole for  [indecipherable]   <strike>for</strike> cover then to continue to connect them into a new line of trenches. Under fire the whole time during the night one man killed   &amp; 2 wounded one of whom dies next day</p>
<p>1915 June 13<br/>Sunday<br/>2nd After Trinity</p>
<p>No 12 platoon detailed for permanent Water party which makes my job easier having no fatigue parties to tell off.<br/>The two men killed last night are buried</p>
