<p>Birdcages are circular sandbagged dugouts about 30ft in diameter and may contain 1 officer 4 N.C.O's and 28 men. Tonight we were listening to the experiences of another Indian friend Tikakhan, whose full name and address is</p>
<p>Acting Naick Tikakhan 1390<br/>No 1 Mountain Battery<br/>H.K, S.B. R.G.A.<br/>Indian Exp Force, Egypt.</p>
<p>He was in the South African War under Col. Kitson (now in some high command in France) when he (the Indian) was shot through the head. The mark is still there. Today two companies march a few miles further</p>
