 15 May 1915 Saturday 
 All parties doing well. the A L H lose a lot of men, in their eagerness to outdo the infantry they put their heads above the parapet   &amp;  get   hit  This is a place where you learn most by the mistakes of others, because the one who makes the mistake does not learn any more, he gets planted. 
 1915 May 16 Sunday Sunday After Ascension 
 Get a new visitation in the shape of a few 8 &frac12; inch high power shells which burst w/- a tremendous explosion but do comparatively no damage except break down a few barricades   &amp;  kill one or two men
