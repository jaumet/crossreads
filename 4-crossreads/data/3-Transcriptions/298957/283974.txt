<p>tonight a considerable number dropped out exhausted &ndash; In fact the lot of us were very footsore &amp; tired after the day's 16 mile tramp, with full equipment on of course.</p>
<p>(Tuesday) 28th March<br/>Up at 4 a.m. and resumed the march at 6 am bound for Moascar 22 miles off. Gradually the heat worked up to a fearful temperature and before long we were into soft white sand &ndash; From then on the whole journey was nothing but torture. The sand slipped from under us and made conditions almost unbearable,</p>
