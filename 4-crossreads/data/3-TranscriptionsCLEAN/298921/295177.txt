
July, 1915Sunday 25Rode over to 12th L.H. expected to see Cuth Fetherstonehaugh but he's returned with [indecipherable] at "Suey" as latter has measles. This Regiment left all their horses at Melbourne. Am posting you Snap Shots of Gaba Tepie - & some of other spots imagine my delight at getting my other Medal the Kings from the Intermediate Box from the War Office England after all these years NOW I will wear the ribbon.
