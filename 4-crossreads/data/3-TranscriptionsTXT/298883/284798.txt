 19th I went forward again this morning and after climbing the hill opposite Jerry's position pulled out my glasses and had a good look round the country.  Nothing was discernible over Jerry's way however except a few of our shells bursting in a wood.  Instead, a grand panorama of majestic hills behind rewarded me.
