<p>Saturday 1 June<br/>Up about 5.30 and moved about about 8.30 from Hangest sur Somme.</p>
<p>Arrived Longpres &ndash; or Amiens about 1.30 pm. Did not ride Snowy. Camped there for the night in the old position.</p>
<p>Inspection Respirators. Field Dressings &amp; Steel Helmets 7 pm.</p>
