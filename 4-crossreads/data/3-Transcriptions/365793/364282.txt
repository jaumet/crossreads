<p>July 1 there are one or two aristo's &amp; a few of the <u>idle</u> rich.<br/>Noticeable are several invalids some in bath chairs, others hobbling along on sticks or crutches. A rather ferocious bespectacled grey-haired lady keeps her eyes glued on the Prom' &amp; doubtless sums everybody up! A young lady sews "industriously' &amp; <u>methinks</u> smiles not a little - at yours truly I hope? And then there are the maids with their natty little caps &amp; neat pinafores &ndash; they also love to see what is going on, &amp; you bet <u>they</u> smile! Then we must not forget</p>
