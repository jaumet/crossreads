
SeptTuesday 18Have been B.O.M today not a bad job but you are kept moveing
Wednesday 19Did 15 minutes PT for the days work Rained most of the day. Concert in evening quite a good one. Payed 10/-
Thursday  20thAs usual. The hut was very quiet last night considering it was pay day.
Thursday  21stAs usual. Good concert in evening. Had 2 Aussie letters today
Saturday 22ndWent over the hill in front of the Camp as a start for the march did about 10
