 August 1915 
  Sunday 1  
 found men lined up on wharf &amp; none allowed on board. 
 Ring leaders picked out &amp; returned. Men who did not want to go on ship expected to leave ranks one man left ranks. 
  Sunday  
 After pulling into mid stream left at midnight Saturday. 
 Total casualties:- 
 3 men severe bayonet wounds. 2 men cut eyes and few minor injuries.
