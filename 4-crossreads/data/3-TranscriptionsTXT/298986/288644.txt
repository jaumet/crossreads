 It was more interesting for a time traveling through a canal instead of just open sea.  But soon lost interest.  It is mostly open sand instead of sea.  A "Teare" every few miles.  A lot of wireing &amp; trenches on the Asiatic side.  Reached port Suez about 3 &amp; left at 6.  Anchored well out in the Bay so could not see much of it.  Agricultur &amp; Accountancy classes as usual. 
 Thursday 3rd 
 Comfort fund gave Tob. &amp; Cig. &amp; Biscuits out also tined fruit last night.
