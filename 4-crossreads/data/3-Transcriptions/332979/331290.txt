<p>gradually getting colder &amp; colder. I was on again at 5.45 Pm for Submarine Guard on the Bridge Deck it was a very cold position &amp; I was glad to be relieved after 24 hours. Things generally were quiet all day After finishing guard I got my Hammock down &amp; turned in for the night.</p>
<p>23/3/17. Friday. On Guard</p>
<p>24/3/17. Saturday<br/>The Weather still being cold &amp; Wet I remained down below all the morning. About 1.30 I was laying down having a sleep when I was awakened by the Engines working faster &amp; on going on Deck there was great Excitement as a Steamer was bearing down on us so our Armed Cruiser went</p>
