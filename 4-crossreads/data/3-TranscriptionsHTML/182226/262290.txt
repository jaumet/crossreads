<p>Saturday 25 September, 1915</p>
<p>On guard.</p>
<p>Had a talk to Lieut Wright. He is acting B.M.G.O. [Battn Machine Gun Officer] He is looking well.</p>
<p>Had a shave &amp; a wash. Feel a new man.</p>
<p>Bread &amp; cigarettes issued today. It is nice &amp; warm in the sun today.</p>
