<p>26 November, Wednesday</p>
<p>Jack fails to ring Nette but I go out. We go on beach for a walk &amp; eat peaches &amp; cherries.</p>
<p>27 November, Thursday</p>
<p>Come Home &amp; play Cards. Dad &amp; I badly defeat George &amp; Ellis.</p>
<p>Wrote to Auntie Annie.</p>
