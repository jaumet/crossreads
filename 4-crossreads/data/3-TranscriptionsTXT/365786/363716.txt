The fine mosque of Mohammed Ali lined with yellow alabaster is enclosed in the Citadel.The huge mosque of Ibn Talun [Ibn Tulun] is surrounded by a wall along the top of which is a frieze of sycamore wood - said to have belonged to Noah's Ark which was found by Ibn Talun on Mt. Ararat.In the 11th Century there were 60 gates to the city, now there are only 2.  There is a fine zoo of 52 acres rich in rare animals & birds & with beautiful ponds of lotus lilies, papyrus reeds & royal palms.
