<p><strike>Feb</strike>Mar 12.  Issued with swords today.  Each of us know about as much about our sword as it knows about us.  Russian classes still continue.</p>
<p>The advance party, including Hitchcock and Turner push on upstream today.  Rotten luck I couldn't go with them, still why growl.</p>
<p><strike>Feb</strike>Mar 15  Rained all night, tent flooded, but all had too good a night at the officers club to care much.</p>
<p>Packing up ready to go.</p>
<p><strike>Feb</strike>Mar 16.  We leave Ashar, by boarding a paddle steamer, our gear is on a raft attached</p>
