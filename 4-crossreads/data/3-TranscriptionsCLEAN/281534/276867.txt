
9/3/19 MarcinelleAt 11 a.m. conferred at D.H.Q with Generals Robertson & Martin and Colonel Davis about running a fair at Charleroi a month hence and also decided on the method of reorganizing the Brigade and Divisional Staff as demobilization progresses. Gave Bazeley an order on Cooks for plain clothes so I will be equipped for Switzerland should occasion later offer. After lunch visited BnH.Q and found that our 1915 men have evidently had to leave England without any of their promised comforts as I sent over 1000 francs by an officer Bailey and he cannot now account for same.
