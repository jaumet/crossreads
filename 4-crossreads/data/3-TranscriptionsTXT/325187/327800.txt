 [Thursday 24 February 1916] 
 Well we fed with Pasha taking our revolvers in one Pocket   This man is a Copt &amp; on the Walls in Picture frames were Texts in Arabic- "Do unto others as tho' you "were going to live 1000 years" another "The head of Wisdom is to fear "God"    etc &amp; etc:- Arabic looks like a glorified shorthand commencing on the wrong side of the page ie from right to left- The War office gives a 50/- BONUS for any soldier who learns to read &amp; write Arabic- I'm not a starter- These old Pashas &amp; Boys have a lot of native courting /COURTESY &amp; do us well in Coffee &amp; Cigars
