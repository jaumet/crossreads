 Dec 17  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;The weather was wild &amp; snowing in the morning - I had to get a tow to start my engine again, but carried on with the Supplies - Fritz is bombing near here very heavily - our guns are bombarding continuously - 
 &nbsp; 
 Dec 18  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;Fine day but a bit of wind made it very cold - I got a tow to start my engine again, after the engine is warm, I can start it by hand. Our guns are still hard at it - To-morrow we draw at Steenwerck railhead as the Brigade is moving towards De Suel - The 1  st  &nbsp;D.S.C. took the place of the 5  th  &nbsp;D.S.C. &amp; we remained attached to them. 
 &nbsp; 
 Dec 19  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;Beautiful day but cold, freezing all day - We drew to-day at Steenwerck . the brigade moved to-day - 106  th  &nbsp;&amp; B.A.C. up at Neuve Eglise &amp; 16  th  &nbsp;17  th  &nbsp;&amp; 18  th  &nbsp;Batteries at De Seul &amp; on Bailleul.Armenti&egrave;res road near La Cr&egrave;che - We did&#39;nt get back to park till 5.P.M. The 12  th  &nbsp;Brigade moved into the waggon lines which the 6  th  &nbsp;vacated -
