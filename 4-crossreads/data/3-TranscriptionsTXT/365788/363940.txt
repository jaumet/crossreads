 12th May 16   France 
 My dearest Irene, It was very nice to get a letter from you yesterday &amp; also one from dear little Tinys What a little pet she is Irene! &ndash; Also thank you very much for    a  nice parcel containing:- a very nice silk hankey, a novel. some cigarette papers, two cakes Cadbury's Chocolate &amp; a nice tin of sweets (from Farmers Very kind of you old girl I am most grateful.
