 September   1918 
 1 SUN  Fine day. fixing up camp. 
 Rec. pay 40 Francs. paid 
 J.W.C. 25. Rec. letter 
 from Zelia. Things going well 
 Inf still advancing. also 
 up North. Quiet. writing at 
 night. 
 2 MON  Fine day. Fixing up camp 
 Posted letter to Amy  Swimming 
 sports in aft. Happy time 
 at night. 
 Fresh successes all along line. 
 3 TUES  Fine day. Concert in 
 aft. Writing at night 
 Fresh advance near Arras. 
 Quiet. Writing at night 
 W. Craig went on leave  send German  towel to Amy. 
 4 WED  Fine day. very warm 
 Posted letters to Gladys & Else P. 
 Rode over to Behencourt in aft 
 saw Tense. had a bonne time 
 Stayed overnight. Started 
 leave to Amiens.                &middot;X&middot;
