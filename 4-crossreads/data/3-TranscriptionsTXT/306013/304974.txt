 Vol. II 1916 Dec.11 &ndash; 1917 Feb 9 
 Diary of experiences etc. on Military Service 1916-17. 
 Pte. J.D.McRae 18th Rein 19th Batt. 5 Bge. A.I.F. 
 To father and mother, without whose love and care my life would be a fading dream &amp; not a casket of rich, inspiring experiences, I send these outlines of my work beyond the seas, hoping that they will help to keep us closer together in spirit, if not in the flesh. From your loving Son John.
