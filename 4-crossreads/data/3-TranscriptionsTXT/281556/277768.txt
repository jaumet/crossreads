 for La. Vicogne about 12 miles off. Had 3 course dinner at Talma &amp; got tinned fruits, fish &amp; bread for the boys &amp; self. Bivouaced the night in big orchard alongside large white Chat&acirc;e. The rest of Bgde also rested here for night. 
 9th. Wednesday. With cycles left at 6am for Halloy about 8 miles off. Grand scenery here passed aerodome early in journey. Reached Halloy about 8am &amp; got billets alloted early. 17th. in last about 3pm. very quiet little village. Slept out under hedge in orchard close
