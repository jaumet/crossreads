
28 April 1916Friday
Picked for guard duty for next 24 hours. Howells, Parsons, Tony & Jock too. 2 hrs on 6 off. sleep down in guard tent.Bob goes over for my watch at Bazaar. found shop wrecked & owner gone.Dave Watts comes down from Light Horse lines & finds me. Glad to see him, have a good yarn. D Waddell & Ern Noble in same regiment.
