
1915 Thursday December 23Got a bad cold no matter soon OK again as we sail South to a warmer clime -Left outer Harbour of Lemnos at midnight, convoyed by Battleships & Torpedoe boat destroyers - 11 Hospital Boats including the Acquittania were in the Harbour.This boat is a big Atlantic Liner belonging to Allen Line & had taken troops to Salonika -By wireless we hear that the Constantinople report is that we are driven into the Sea of Anzac. We are to hold on to Helles & winter there. Am detailed as Capt. of the day -
