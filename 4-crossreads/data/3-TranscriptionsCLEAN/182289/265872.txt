  
 about D.23.c.40.40.  Our guns completely relieved by 11th Company by midnight 22/23rd.  Five guns of 7th M.G. Co. relieved by 3 a.m.  Relief reported, complete by 3.30 a.m. Three guns not yet placed held in reserve in J.5.a. & c. 
 22.4.18 The following targets were engaged at irregular intervals throughout the night & morning, 1500 rounds fired, (1)  Hollow in K.7.c.   (2)  Trenches K.7.c.10.75.   (3)  Sunken Road K.1.b. & d.   (4)  Streets, Ville-Sur-Ancre E.25.d.30.45.   (5)  Road &map; Trench E.26.c.00.25.   (6)  Trenches K.2.c.1.7.   (7)  Cemetery, Ville-Sur-Ancre K.1.a.85.75. Enemy artillery quiet except for short burst of fire at about 4.30 a.m. Enemy Machine Gun fire, Normal. Enemy aircraft patrolled his own lines but owing to the large concentration of our machines did not attempt to cross.
