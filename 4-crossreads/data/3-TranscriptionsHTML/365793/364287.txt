<p>July 3 &amp; the City of Weymouth. The background of cliffs stands out clear &amp; distinct against the deep blue of the Harbour. "And it is a <u>perfect</u> day"  From the trees &amp; the hedgerows come the songs of the birds, &amp; some happy youngsters are playing among the heather. Thank God that the children at least no little of the horrors of war!</p>
<p>4 Parade at 6.30, half an hours physical drills &amp; then finish. Listened to the Band outside the Officers mess &amp; then went for a walk with Fred out past the Coast-guard Station &amp; along</p>
