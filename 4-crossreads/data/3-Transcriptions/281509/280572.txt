<p>1916 December 15<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Feeling off colour this morning with a cold coming on. After 24 hours duty we have 24 off.  Stay in hut all day. Tony comes in with a cake from home &amp; we partake.  Bob receives parcel from Mary  Also both of us receive a good mail up to Oct. 29th .</p>
