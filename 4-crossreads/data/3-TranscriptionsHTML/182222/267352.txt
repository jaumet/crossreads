<p>21<u><sup>st</sup></u>&nbsp;&nbsp; Saw Halborn again and reminded him of crazy promises re a fly. To my horror he said &quot;Right now!&quot; Will write my impressions later when I have time to do it Justice. Saw about umteen Cities towns etc, all the paysage as far as <u>H</u>as<u>t</u>in<u>g</u>s Port &amp; the N. Sea in the distance. Lovely day &amp; clear views &amp; high wind up landing but otherwisw most scrumptious Experience. Worth 14 Days pay noisy. lovely sensation. flying along like a cloud hope to go up again if the chance offers</p>
