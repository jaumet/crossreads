<p>Thursday 10 August 1916<br/>Retired a little further back.  Resting all day.  Saw Colin.<br/>Bit short drinking water.  Not allowed to take off Boots or any gear of course.</p>
<p>Friday 11 August 1916<br/>Still in reserve.<br/>On outpost at night.  Horses getting very tucked up owing to only getting handfuls of oats sometimes 3 times a day.<br/>Men not much better.</p>
