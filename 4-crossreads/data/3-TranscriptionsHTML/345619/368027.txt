<p>[Reverse of the above postcard]<br/>No. 7008<br/>Sgt. Burrowes A.J.<br/>21st Rft<br/>18th Batt.<br/>Australian Imperial Forces</p>
<p>26th June 1918<br/>Dear Man<br/>We are hoping you will get these for your B.Day. There is a mail going out today which they will catch. D.V.W.P. I would have liked to have written a long letter but came home late from Sydney last night &amp; Have to go to St Mary's tonight so will write tomorrow. Much love &amp; many kind thoughts. Annie</p>
