<p>1 October, Wednesday</p>
<p>Jack &amp; Rory come home to Tea. Singing &amp; music. Jack stays all night &amp; I sleep indoors.</p>
<p>2 October, Thursday</p>
<p>Catch 7.45 boat. Feel funny all day. Mum lets me lie in 'cos I look so comfy &amp; I have 30 minutes to dress &amp; all. Ring Mrs Harper &amp; Doris. Catch 7 pm boat. Miss Jack &amp; Roy. Meet Ted Mel. Cold &amp; wet. I walk up &amp; down outside Home &amp; go in at 8.15. Jack comes at 9. I undertable[?]. Hear Girl &amp; Jack say funny things.</p>
