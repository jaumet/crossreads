<p>[Corporal Gilbert Goldie Anschau, No 244, enlisted at age 29 on 4 September 1914. He served at Gallipoli in the 1st Field Ambulance, where he was wounded and invalided back to Australia. He re-enlisted in 1916 and embarked on HMAT A40 Ceramic on 7 October 1916 with the 3rd Infantry Battalion, 21st Reinforcements and served in France where he was killed in action on 5 May 1917. Includes an envelope and covering letter from the Dunlop family of Hamilton, NSW.]<br/>[Envelope]<br/>Mrs Dunlop (Dr.)<br/>c/- Miss Short.<br/>The Commercial Bank of Sydney Ltd<br/>Newcastle</p>
