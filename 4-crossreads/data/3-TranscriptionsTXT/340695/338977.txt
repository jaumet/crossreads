It is the only damage done though so we are fortunate believe there are no buildings at all in Baupaume. 28-3-17 Arrived safely n BAPAUME after a trip I shall never forget. We came by motor lorry &amp; the sight was past all imagination. The village of Le Gars was completely demolished not the smallest portion of a wall standing it was enough to break ones heart. I thought of the folk that once lived there in peace &amp; happiness. The fighting was very severe at this particular point the ground as far as you could see was pitted with shell holes a wonderful sight.
