<p>Isn't this simply absurd paper &amp; I have simply nothing to write about.</p>
<p>Still plenty of noise about &amp; still plenty of good news trickling in.  Hope things continue to go well.</p>
<p>Suppose you are back home again.  I suppose the weather is just glorious again.  How is Grahame &amp; the Boss &amp; old Mac.  Most insulting to call him old.</p>
<p>Did I tell you I missed seeing Stuart in England.  I had lunch with his Father &amp; Mother.  Stuart wrote me a most violently insulting letter giving me hell.  I deserved it, I did rather mull things.</p>
