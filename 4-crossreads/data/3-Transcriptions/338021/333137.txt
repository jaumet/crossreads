<p>Lt Weston<br/>Re- inspection of gun positions &ndash; you will carry this out tonight. Personally inspect the three positions &amp; send in report on personal &amp; general condition of same. Have remaining belt boxes etc collected from Right position &amp; distributed giving detailed account of what you have at each position, this report to reach me by 6am.</p>
<p>We will not be relieved tonight.</p>
<p>WM Cory Lt  13th A.M.G.Coy  26.9.17  11.20am</p>
