 Sunday 28 January 1917 Took horses down to "Kilo 155" for reinforcements but missed them. 
 Monday 29 January 1917 No. 1 Sectn. on shooting practice firing a belt of ammunition. 
 Tuesday 30 January 1917 On a muster parade to 155 for "Delousing", pretty chilly while clothes were being fumigated. 
 Wednesday 31 January 1917 Foggy in morning.  Two Taubes over:  dropped several bombs. 
 Thursday 1 February 1917 Quiet day:  saw Guth &amp; got my disk made.  Outpost at night. 
 Friday 2 February 1917 Windy, dusty day. 
 Saturday 3 February 1917 Found a Turko mine on the coast, pulled it in &amp; put a guard round it.
