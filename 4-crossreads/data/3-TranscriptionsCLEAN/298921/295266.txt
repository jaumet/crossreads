  
 October, 1915 Friday 22 We are now zig-zagging among the islands, & all hands  wear  their life belts, boats swing out, etc: etc:  One becomes like a Mohammedan & says to himself if Allah wills it, so be it, still just as well I am a good swimmer. A new name for submarines is  Tin Fish:- distinct from Porpoise etc:- which nervous people often mistake for the real thing.
