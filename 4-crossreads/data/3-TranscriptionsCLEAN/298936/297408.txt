
September 1915
Thursday 9
Good bye to the doctors & and such nurses as I know & away by car to Cairo station catching the midday train for Alexandria. The line runs through the very fertile land in the basin of the Nile delta.
Corn & other crops as far as the eye can see.
Arrived at Alexandria & drafted to Mustapha barracks. Leave in Alex. for evening.
Went in with Kennet & Hunter
