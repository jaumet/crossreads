<p>British front.</p>
<p>14/10/16<br/>Marched to Steenevorde. Crossed border at about 2 p.m. 15 miles march.</p>
<p>15/10/16<br/>Another march to [Buisscheure?] about 13 miles.</p>
<p>16/10/16<br/>Still on the move. Crawled into Nortleulinghem (after marching about 15 miles) at about 3 p.m.</p>
