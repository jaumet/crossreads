  
  Cardonette. Bussy  &  Blangy Tronville  
 [4 Th] Did not get out of bed till 11 am. Harnessed up & moved off at 3 pm. Arr. Bussy 5 pm. 10 pm. ordered to harness up again, moved out  strike>in  45min. later. Arr Blangy Tronville about 3am. 
 [5 Fri] Rev. 6-30am fell in at 7 am. Fixed up lines & waggons. Went for ammunition in the afternoon, got back to lines about 7 pm 
 [6 Sat] Grooming & cleaning harness. Harness & kit always to be together so as to be able to move off at an hours notice
