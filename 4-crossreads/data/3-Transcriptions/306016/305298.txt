<p>As there is very little excitement to be got here in camp I have been spending my Saturday &amp; Sunday afternoons by taking long walks into the surrounding country.  Alan is not too fond of walking &amp; so he has not come with me of late on these excursions.  The country &amp; villages around here are just as Wordsworth pictured <strike>the</strike> English rural scenery &amp; I think I <strike>will</strike> can now better understand &amp; appreciate his feelings in this respect than ever I did before.</p>
<p>And so in the best of spirits &amp; with tons of love for you both &amp; for all at home.</p>
<p>I am as of yore<br/>Your affec. Son in khaki<br/>Duncan xxxxxx</p>
