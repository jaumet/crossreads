
Saturday 16th MarchDid a day shift again
Sunday 17th MarchAll hands went out in evening & finished job by about 10 oclock. Had to stop work twice on account of gas shells. Fritz started to put over a lot of mixed stuff when I was about 1/2 way home was just nicely in front of it - only had to duck once. Things livened up at the Camp soon after we got there - Mostely Gas
