  Mar  April. 2nd 18 Near Fouilloy. We moved into this front relieving a cavalry bde. it was a very dark night when we took over &amp; very muddy. These Tommies appear to be a shiftless lot, there only a couple of posies they seem to have just hung about with their horses. We did four days in, then were relieved by the 56th &amp; came back to the road, there we were much better off &amp; made ourselves very comfortable.
