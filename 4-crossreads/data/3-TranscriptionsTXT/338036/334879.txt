 OUR RAIDING PARTIES [27th May contd] also shelled out. 
 Monday 28th [May] At about 1.30 am we sent a raiding party of about 200 over in Prowse Point Sector but success was limited and only secured 1 prisoner &amp; lost about 80 wounded &amp; 10 killed.  Our bearers &amp; effectives had to go at top [?]  At Dead Horse we were more fortunate and except for the barrage were not disturbed.  I received instructions at noon to report to Charing Cross for another stunt to night.
