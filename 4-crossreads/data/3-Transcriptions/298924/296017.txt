<p>Tuesday 4 February 1919<br/>Usual Parades &amp; Football in afternoon.  Drafts left for Kantara getting very drunk before leaving which is considered to be the correct thing &ndash; by all ranks.  We all find something to growl about &amp; hardly appreciate the war being over!! till we thinks.</p>
