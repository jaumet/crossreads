<p>1916 November 29<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Day shift begins at 8. 30 a.m. About 30 cases  through. majority  trench feet. Very painful &amp; somewhat serious. caused by standing in cold mud &amp; water for days on end. Hundreds on hundreds are affected.  Got another good mail last night -21 letters, up to October 16th telling about floods &amp; receipt of my Pozieres letter.</p>
