<p>seated &ndash; but found a very swank place in the end also met a Red Cross Commissioner, who we knew in Egypt, so he joined our party, making us six. Afterwards we went to Regent Street to Cinema Show Picture "Battle Somme" &amp; a very good show it is but very heart rending. Then had more tea &amp; nourishment &ndash; Sister Watt had to catch 9. p.m. train &amp; I also got one about the same time, reaching home about 10. p.m. tired out but found my letters waiting for me. Sorry I cannot tell you more but shall try to send a few more before the mail closes.<br/>I'm very well &amp; as happy as can be under the circumstances. Suppose you have received my cable by now. Fondest love &amp; kisses to you all &amp; hope you are well.<br/>From,                                                                                                                                                             Yours lovingly.                                                                                                                                             Edith.</p>
