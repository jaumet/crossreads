
3/9/15  LemnosMen in swimming and look splendid naked giants.  Reported the Brigadier (Linton) has died in the Southland.  There is a furphy today that 5000 Aust. are being sent back from Anzac on account of water shortage.  Very peaceful in this calm harbour - yet fighting tops of warships meet the eye and the gun mutter tells of trenches only 4 hours away.  We sail tomorrow for Anzac at 11 am 2 days war ration one water bottle full has to do us for two days.
