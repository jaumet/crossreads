<p>&amp; cream &amp; as much cider as I wanted &amp; lots of other little trifles.  We then had a musical evening which lasted till 8 oclock then the pony was put in the trap &amp; we were driven back to the Station &amp; so on to Plymouth.<br/>It is Sunday &amp; very cold, Munch McDougall &amp; I went &amp; had a bath a hot one as we thought we were going to, but unfortunately it turned out to be stony cold &amp; nearly froze us to death.</p>
<p>11th.  Went to the Concert last night at the Theatre Royal &amp; it was simply splendid it was principally a patriotic one in honor of Raleigh, &amp; the singers especially one soprano was beautiful.<br/>At last the great day has come &amp; the whole air as I write now is filled with screaming &amp; howling whistles &amp; sirens.  The time we first got the news here was 9.20 &amp; it is now 11.45 &amp; the noise is increasing if anything all the Church bells are ringing &amp; clamouring &amp; the little church hard by is floating a big old Union Jack &amp; ringing her bells</p>
