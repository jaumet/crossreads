<p>It was the loudest that I have heard since we came here, &amp; some shells fell pretty near. It lasted all through the night &amp; slackened off at about 7 a.m. Our heavies seemed to have affair amount to say in affairs. Luckily I did not have much trouble in getting to sleep. To try to sleep when bombs &amp; shells are falling any where near is awful.<br/>We had a service in the evacuation hut</p>
