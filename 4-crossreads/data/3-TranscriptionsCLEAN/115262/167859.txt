
Monday 2 April 1917.
In camp & on no duty special duty.
Signalling in morning.
Field stretcher work in afternoon till 3.15 p.m. Individual drill after smoke oh!
Lecture by Capt C J Oliver on Haemmorhage from 4.15 to 4.30 pm
(For first time in C.B.F.A. had 1/2 hr. till 5 pm to ourselves.)
General Relief that we are not going this week.
