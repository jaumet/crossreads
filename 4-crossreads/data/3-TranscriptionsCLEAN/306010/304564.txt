Patients are being rapidly evacuated from the palace & there now only remain about 250; several of the wards having been closed down. The question on everyone's lips is now "where are we going?" Feb 26" - Satur. A Hospital train runs daily from Cairo to Ismailia picking up on the return journey patients from ismailia, Moascar & Tel-el-Kolin. The train is staffed by No.2 G.H. personnel & to-day I went down with them (as a passenger).
