<p>Tuesday 26th Oct.<br/>Left Ghezireh at 11 by motor lorry. Station Dept 12 o'c. Reached Montazah about 4 pm. Not a pleasant journey. Hungry to bed. First impressions not very good.</p>
<p>Wed 27 Posted to [indecipherable] Sister Lehman, &amp; Card to Base office.</p>
<p>Thur 4th Nov Posted to Mr &amp; Mrs Cripps also Massingham</p>
