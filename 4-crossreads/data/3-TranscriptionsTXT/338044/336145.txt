We have no time even for writing letters, &amp; how must friends at home know this  Impossible to write, so they must rest content with a few letters now &amp;  again. 
 23rd Jan. Sunday Afternoon off  &amp; I spend it in sleep &amp; writing a few letters. Feel properly knocked up &amp; am sure I shall be pleased when these long hours are over. The weather now is not quite so cold &amp; we soon expect it to rather pleasant. Still cold at night
