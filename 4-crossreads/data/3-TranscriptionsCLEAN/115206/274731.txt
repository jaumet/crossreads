  
 June 1918 Monday 10 
 Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles Weather dull, heavy rains. No swim today. Charlie Adey a little better.  Received a long letter from Len Clements, - quite a decent one. Practiced for concert, postponed until Thursday owing t heavy rains. At 10pm Fritz dropped his barrage, lasted  until 11.10 pm. A beautiful display of fireworks. Regst. Another 40 francs to O.J. Expect to go out for a spell very shortly. Had a decent yap with Percy. Posted art studies from La Vie Parision to M.J.Wh. also wrote Ead.
