<p>Lieut Weston<br/>Re- forming up. you will form up in rear of D Coy 49th Battn (Support Coy) Lt RICHARDS in charge.<br/>B Coy 49th will be attacking Coy Capt Tambling O.C.</p>
<p>A.Coy 51st Battn (Support Coy) Capt Wm Beanie O.C.<br/>B.Coy 51st Battn (attacking Coy) Capt Smith.<br/>The Batts say they will be formed up on the tape at 1.30a.m. You will be ready to form then.</p>
<p>The 49th left flank will be about 100 yds to the Right of Garter Point.</p>
<p>Six belt boxes will go up with party tonight</p>
<p>WM Cory  25.9.17  8.50pm</p>
