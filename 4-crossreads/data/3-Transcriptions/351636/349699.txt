<p>Dear Mr. Layton<br/>We are sending Mrs. Cato up to you. Her son has been missing since last April, and his pay, on which she was quite dependent has been stopped.</p>
<p>I do hope you will be able to help her.</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>[indecipherable] A Bolton</p>
