<p>[Wednesday 29 November 1916]</p>
<p>Arrived safely presented Skipper &amp; Chief Officers with silver smoking outfits- in recognition of their skills.<br/><strike>Ordered report Ismalia Regiment near Bir el Abb</strike><br/>Stayed the night at Windsor Hotel <strike>Alexandria</strike> &amp; with a party from Ship heard old Ada Raeve sing- The Long Long Trail a very pretty popular sad song<br/>I shall leave a lot of Kit with the Base Stores- Gezirah <strike>Cabled the wee wife &amp; also Mother.</strike></p>
