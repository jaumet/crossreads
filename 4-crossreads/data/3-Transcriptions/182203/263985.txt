<p>P. cards, photos of this Palace, but really one cannot see the immense &amp;  beautiful place it is by picture.<br/>Well, I'm having a real good time while off duty. I try to rest as much as I can but always something comes along to make me go out. I received Ethel's charming letter with Eddy's also Alice's of May 10th.  I have received a number of letters &amp; papers which are greatly <strike>f</strike> valued. I pass the papers on to Charlie &amp; then to Australian Officers. We have a number of English Officers &amp; one Canadian, but they all praise <strike>d</strike> our Boys for their bravery.<br/>Charlie is improving slowly. I have just returned from visiting him. He may have a chance of going to England  I am taking down the addresses of his relations (Mr Copp gave me) in England, so that he can copy. I got a great surprise last night.
