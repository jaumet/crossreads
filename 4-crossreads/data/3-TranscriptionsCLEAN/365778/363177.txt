  
 Monday 26/2/17 
 Moved to Acid Drop Camp. Bde taking over the line, wrote to Doff. 
 Tuesday 27/2/17 
 Received orders to proceed to line and attack, Battn moved up to get into position. 
 Wednesday 28/2/17 
 Attack timed for 3 am with artillery postponed owing to guides losing themselves - Battn returned to Acid Drop Camp, men very tired. 
 Thursday 1/3/17 
 Orders received to have another attempt with artillery preparation, all arrangements made and Bn got up well early.
