22.6.1847 C.C.S.B.E.F.
Dear Mr. ArmstrongI am very sorry to inform you that your dear boy's condition is still very bad. I can't say there is any improvement at all. I'm afraid there is very little hope of recovery. He is an excellent patient, is so good quiet, and gives himself every chance, but his wounds are so bad, almost more than he can battle with.
