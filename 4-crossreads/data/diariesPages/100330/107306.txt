at 2 Pm, after a stay there for 30" we were off again arrived at Centreau at 4 Pm where a large Iron Works is in full swing. A large number of French Women could be seen working about, some with Shovels others with Wheelbarrows. They were all dressed in Trousers looked comical to us, they all seemed to be of a hard working class of women. On proceeding further on for some little distance we crossed the River La one could see several Bridges built of stone fairy low only allowing Barges to go up down the River. At 4.40 Pm we arrived at Boulogne had a 10" spell. This is another fine Town contains another large Loco Depot, Leaving there at 4.50 Pm saw a large Air Ship Hangar but could not see the Air Ship but noticed several Anti Air Crafts Guns about for the Ships protection.
