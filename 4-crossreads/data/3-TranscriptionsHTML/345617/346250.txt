<p>Novembre 24<br/>Part of the Section working in Camp &amp; part in the Reserve Line. I had a good look round our defence work to pick locality. Good news still coming in from our advance near Cambrai.</p>
<p>Novembre 25<br/>Out with Mr Crawford taking map locations of dugouts &amp; pill boxes. Most of Section working on strong points in Reserve &amp; Support lines. Still getting good news from the Cambrai Front. Over 100 guns have been captured &amp; a large number prisoners beside a lot of civilians released.</p>
