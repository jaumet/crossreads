<p>8 Th February<br/>Visited Auckland Regt at 7 wells. Also Headqrs Camel Corps. Flies very bad</p>
<p>9 Fri February<br/>Very fine day.  2nd Bgde moved from Mazar to El Arish   saw Ted Grace from Kantarah. Rum Issue</p>
<p>10 Sat February<br/>Drops of rain  very cold  1st Brigade moved back to Bayond.[?]  Auckland &amp; Canterbury football  A scoreless match  Anxiously awaiting Aus. Mail<br/>Jacko's airmen have forgotten to visit us</p>
