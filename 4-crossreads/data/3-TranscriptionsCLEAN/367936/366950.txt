  
 Sunday 19 March 1916 Fixed up show sheep.  Heard that one of the Cassilas horses had fallen on a stump and killed itself.  Father & I rode up and look at it. Mr. & Mrs. Dick Blackman came over in afternoon. 
 Monday 20 March 1916 Helped Father make frame for lorrie, got show sheep in and then started Dick off for Mudgee with sheep. Father & I went up and got 11 horses and took them down to Wonga.
