<p>May 1915<br/>27 Thursday same as yesterday.<br/>Working parties. Swim<br/>2 wounded today. Stood to arms from 4.30 pm to 2.30 am as Turks massing at left head of Monash Gully.</p>
<p>28 Fri<br/>Same. Road to beach very dangerous from machine gun fire and bullets.<br/>About 40 men hit by Turks marksmen while drawing stores from supply depot, before 10am.Weather warm<br/>I man of ours wounded.</p>
