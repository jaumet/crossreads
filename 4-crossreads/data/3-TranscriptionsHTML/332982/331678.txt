<p>The Admiralty, so runs the tale, asked skipper if he could get 850 tons on board I 48 hours.<br/>He replied it could be done in 36 hours a statement which was received with polite incredulity.<br/>Well; it was done in 28 &frac12; hours, and everybody concerned can be very proud of the performance.<br/>John and I were in the second shift of the F.A.3 till 6am.Trousers, white hat and boots</p>
