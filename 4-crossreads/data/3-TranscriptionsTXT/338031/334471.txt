
neighbours quarrel - two stout negro women slang whanging each other & with the gestures of any back street denizens of Redfern, the Glebe or the 'Loo, the husbands meanwhile languidly smoking & casually eyeing the disputants with a philosophically bored expression, quite satisfied to let them gas each other out. The fat babies were very noticeable in this strange flicker of humanity. They were like round eyed rabbits peering about from the shawl in which they were strapped to the mothers' back & pot bellied little rascals were scampering about as full of life
