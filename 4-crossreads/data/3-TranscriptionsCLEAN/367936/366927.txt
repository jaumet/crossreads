
Wednesday 2 February 1916N.C.Os class in morning.  Lecture on outposts.  A very easy day, lectures on musketry etc.Uncle George & big party came up and brought tea for all hands in tent, had a great feed.  Went back to Sydney with them and saw Alan off to Nyngan.
Thursday 3 February 1916Church parade before breakfast.Rifle exercises & bayonet fighting then route march this afternoon with about a mile's run to finish coming home.  Slight thunder storm but not much rain.Hot day but cold night.
