<p>British Red Cross<br/>Convalescent Hospital No. 7<br/>Montaza<br/>Alexandria</p>
<p>14.8.16</p>
<p>Dear Mrs John,</p>
<p>Just a few lines to let you know, that I am still in the land of the living.  Was pushed in here for a fortnight owing to an attack of Nile fever.  I expect to leave here for the Details about the end of the week.  They are at Ismalia [also spelt Ismailia] but owing to the casualties last Saturday week I expect I will go on to the front.  Frank Weir is there so I may get with him.  He writes me that the wound he received sometime back is quite OK again &amp; that it does not worry him.  We have</p>
