<p>Friday 31 August 1917</p>
<p>Bomb practice &amp; Hotchkiss gun Class &amp; GAS DRILL, pity something could not eventuate out of GAS to Kill SCRUB, Prickly Pears &amp; RABBITS.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>I read the new AWU arrived with interest. I &amp; the wife can get a job as married Boundary Riders somewhere I hope "Apres la guerre finis". Tonight BOXING. Brigadier General RYRIE acts as Referee &amp; gives the boys a fair go.</p>
<p>Feel a bit DOPEY from inoculation but a great deal fitter all round for my spell in Hospital.</p>
