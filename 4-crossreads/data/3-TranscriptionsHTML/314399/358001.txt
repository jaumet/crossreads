<p>Saturday 4 January 1919<br/>Started putting in the sets today. Also a little filling in. Saw a game of Rugby. Very poor.</p>
<p>Sunday 5 January 1919<br/>No work today cos we ran out of nails. Major rang up for "rugby players but the boys refused to go in.</p>
