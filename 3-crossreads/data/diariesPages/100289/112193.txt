Monday 6.11.16
Kept going continuously the whole of the night; rained about midnight made work much harder. Practically the whole of us dead beat, but had to continue carrying till 12 noon when we were relieved by 6 F Amb, 5th F Amb took over advanced posts. Feel the strain of short rations.
Tuesday 7.11.16
