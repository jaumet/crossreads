
In PalestineSept.2nd.1917
Dear Mrs VernonBy the parcels mail received today a consignment of Khaki shirt and socks despatched by the Comforts Fund reached us. Each man in "B" Troop  received one. They were very much appreciated by us all. We are now resting on the beach somewhere along the wash and find the life quite a treat after our recent operations which have occupied the greater part of the last two months.
