<p>February 1918<br/>Monday 18</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Well our friend the Bosche was giving a fine exhibition of daring flying to-day. Went down to canteen and "Pip" having some lunch in Gunn's company.<br/>Weather frosty and clear, very piercing wind from S.E. Garfoot out on O.P. all day. Chas Adey, on a visit to W.Lines.<br/>Had a shot at old Fritz with the Lewis gun. <br/>Went on duty at 12 to 2am during the night.</p>
