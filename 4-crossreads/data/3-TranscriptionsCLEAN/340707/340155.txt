We buy liqueur 50 centimes (anywhere else 2 or 3 fr) & part saying good night to everyone & hearing a Thank you from proprietor, apparently for our patronage for we have only spent 1 fr.
We then visit Source & then Soufflet up stairs where dancing is in progress to violins & guitar music. Next we visit Pantheon down stairs where dancing is in full swing, though floor which is carpeted will only allow of about 5 couples & place is crowded & stifling. The girls here have come mainly for the pleasure of dancing & have forgotten their profession for the time being, & are thoroughly enjoying themselves.
At length we moved homewards but not home for we yarn till nearly 2 am outside the Ecole des Droit.
