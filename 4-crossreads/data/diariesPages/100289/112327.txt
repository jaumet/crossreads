Tuesday 5.3.18 Classified again marked B2 A which means 6 months service in England.
Thursday 28.2.18
Pay parade in connection with leave. Purchased railway ticket. Went to Warminster at night. Transferred to different section of camp.
Friday 1.3.18
Paraded at 2 pm for pass sugar card. Caught 5.40 pm train at Warminster after a very amusing trip arrived at Bristol about 10 pm where we remained for the night.
Saturday 2.3.18
Caught 9.10 Am train Bristol to Gloucester, arrived at Gloucester 10.5 Am. Found accommodation
