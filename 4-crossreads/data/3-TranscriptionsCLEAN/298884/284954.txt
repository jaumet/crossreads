
on our way home to Aussie.  Gramaphones peg quoits deck quoits reading & various other forms of amusement in which the very fine piano plays a prominent part.
All the boys are as happy as larry.  The food on board is excellent and is easily the best we have ever experienced since in troopships.  Nurses & officers fraternise a little more oftener than when they first came on board and it is highly amusing to watch a couple of lovebirds billing & cooing.
12th Our passage continues to be very smooth.
