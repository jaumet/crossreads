 Sunday 17 March 1918 
 Monday 18 March 1918 Recco Tul Keram the 19th.  Pilot Lt. Taylor.  In air 2 h.  Dropped message from Hun observer on Nablus. Archies severe and accurate, propeller and top plane shot through. Capt. Austin &amp; Oliver Lee in B.F.  Capt. Evans in a Martinside missing.  A chance that they may turn up later on.
