<p>We are seeing a   <u>bit</u>  of France, came quite a different road to the last trip; it was a most interesting   &amp;  pleasant run   &amp;   we had the front seat. This C.C.S. is very quiet but expect to get busy, so that is why they have a large staff.<br/>Six of us came from 42 C.C.S.</p>
<p>Had a letter from Dorothy written Thursday   &amp;  she said they were leaving next a.m. at 10 o'clock.</p>
