
started & had to march 4 miles before we reached her, & it just started to rain as we got there.   We reported & after a little while we were taken to some quarters where we are to put the night in.   After we had got our gear off we went into the little Belgian cafe where we had a good tea on bread butter & coffee & eggs the Belgian girls are very funny with their broken English.   They are pretty smart at their work.
30th.   It rained like cats & dogs all night fully 2 inches must have fallen, we got up at 8.30 shaved cleaned up & had our breakfast in the cafe.
