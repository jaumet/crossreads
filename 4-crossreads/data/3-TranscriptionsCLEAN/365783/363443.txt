  
 No. 2. General Hospital 2nd Australian Imperial Expeditionary Force. 29th May 1915 
 Dearest Mrs, Col. Martin & I will accept your kind invitation for dinner in grill room at 7 to-night.   We will afterwards take you & Dr Bennett out to see the Spinx by Spoon-light.   Hope you had a good night.   Please give bearer a note to say how Tingsey & Peter are 
 Yr. loving husband
