<p>Fauquembergues<br/>at 9.30 a.m.  This party also acted as loading party for Supply Coleman &ndash; detrained at Wizernes, marched to Fauquembergues.</p>
<p>22.10.17 to 31.10.17<br/>Reorganisation of Company &ndash; men being rested as much as possible &ndash; short route marches each day.</p>
<p>Fauquembergues &ndash; 1-4.11.17<br/>Reorganisation of Company still being proceeded with, the men being rested as much as possible, short route marches each day.<br/>One lot 25 and another lot 19 Reinforcements reported from Base and taken on strength.<br/>5 &amp; 6th.11.17<br/>Syllabus for week ending 10-11-17 attached.</p>
<p>8.11.17<br/>Sergeant J.O. Henrick promoted 2/Lieut.</p>
<p>9.11.17<br/>Preliminary orders to move to Forward Area received.<br/>10th &amp; 11th.11.17<br/>Spent in packing Limbers, and all preparations to move.  Final Move Orders received on the night of the 11th.</p>
<p>12.11.17<br/>The Company moved off from Fauquembergues at 8 a.m., passing through Dohem, halting for lunch just outside Therouanne.  Starting point 400 yds. S.W. from X roads was passed at 1-11 p.m., passing through Clarques and Aire, arriving in billets at Widdebroucq at about 5 p.m.</p>
