<p>19th Batt<br/>Popes Post<br/>2-12-15</p>
<p>To Lieut Boyden<br/>The Adjutant sends his compliments &amp; asks you to please let me have 3 men to go to Anzac tonight for Bombs.  Men to report to Pte Ross at Regmt QM Stores at 6.30 tonight.<br/>A Gray<br/>RSM</p>
