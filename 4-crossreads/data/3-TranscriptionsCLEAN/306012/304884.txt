 Mond. 29 April Fritz is pushing hard up north and has taken Kemmel Hill and Dranoutre from the British & French allied forces.  The French have rushed up large numbers of troops & plenty of guns to aid the British in Flanders. We, of course, are still down on the Somme.  As far as I can make out the 13th Aust. Brigade is on the right of  the  our Division and on the right of the 13th is the world famous French Foreign Legion.  Australians & Foreign Legion evidently are the connecting link between British & French, a link that the enemy is trying hard to break.
