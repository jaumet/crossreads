<p>Monday 18<br/>Afternoon at Richmond through most terrific thunder storm.</p>
<p>Tuesday 19<br/>Secretary for Colonies Mr Walter Long spoke this evening to the men.</p>
<p>Friday 22<br/>Not to go to Dartford, but to come here, Holborn Military Hospital Mitcham has been my convalescent. The only decent thing about the place is a permanent daily pass. Otherwise it is rotten.</p>
