<p>15-4-16<br/>pointed piece of iron riveted to the end of it, &amp; the pipe has 3 rows of holes for 2 feet up, covered with fine gauze with another covering of coarser gauze on the outside, this is forced straight down &amp; an ordinary handle  pump to pump the water with;</p>
<p>We have been in the habit of having cocoa every night before retiring but tonight we found that our cocoa supply had run out, so we had poached eggs instead, &amp; were just about to make our bed down when Abdul Moolah appeared on the scene with a kettle, teapot, glasses &amp; Egyptian tea<br/>I then remembered asking him to come &amp; have tea with us, so we soon had the kettle boiling &amp; by 10.30 was filled up with sweet tea, &amp; enjoying a very amusing evening with Les the interpreter &amp; Abdul Moolah;<br/>D Troop supplied a very fair programme tonight;  Was issued with a watch today</p>
