<p>Wednesday 31st<br/>The Clinck was not just a success as the Birds pulled some of the fence down last night &amp; got away We are putting up a Bayonet course in the same camp</p>
<p>Thursday 1st November<br/>Bayonet Course quite a job. Ground to be levelled Trenches dug Sand bag brestworks put up &amp; bags of straw hung at intervills to run ones bayonet into</p>
<p>Friday 2<br/>Had my first go at Sand bagging They make a real good job when properly done</p>
<p>Saturday 3<br/>Working Party Warminster in afternoon It is a dull dreary hole Weight 11 st 3 lbs according to a penny in the slot machine</p>
<p>Sunday 4<br/>As usual. Attended Evening Service at the Home Hut Miss Asbournne gave a splendid little Sermon. This Home Hut is the only one I have come across Same style as a YM only run entirely by ladies It is well</p>
