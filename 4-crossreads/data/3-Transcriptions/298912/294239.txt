<p>Frewin. A quiet "domesticated "fellow who prefers a quiet life of playing cards to anything lively. Our best church goer.</p>
<p>Nicknamed  "Gus" or "Gussy"</p>
<p>See A.C. A rather selfish fellow but perhaps unintentionally, that may make him more self centred. An enormous eater. Bad sailor &amp; gets seasick easily</p>
