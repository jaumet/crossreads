  
 December 1916. 12th   This morning we had a light fall of snow.  Later in the day it began to rain heavily & the ground has been made terribly slushy   We went out again to-night & continued with the duck-walk track. 13th   To-day I started with the party on the revetment & drainage of Needle Trench at "Windmill."
