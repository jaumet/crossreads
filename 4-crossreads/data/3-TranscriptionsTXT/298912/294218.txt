At 2pm the drums & musical instruments played Tiddley Winks & other high class music, whilst the whole camp - bar us - left for the route march that returned at almost midnight. All the tents of the Signal School were old condemned ones &were marked for repairs so we chose the best whilst they were absent &                    replaced ours with it, the new one being absolutely perfect. We then "shook"  a table & made ourselves comfortable thus in the tent
