  
 December 1916 Thursday 14 Left Gara per camel for oasis junction. 5 miles entrained camels and baggage and left at 4 oclock for Cairo 
 Friday 15 Arrived Cairo station 1030 am had lunch and left for Kantara at 11.30. Passed thro' Tel el Kebir Moascar Ismaelia and arrived Kantara about 5pm. Unloaded camels and baggage and camped for the night.
