<p>1917 MARCH</p>
<p>25 SUNDAY</p>
<p>Raining &amp; cold most of the day</p>
<p>but cleared up towards</p>
<p>evening. Cleaning harness</p>
<p>ect all morning, &amp; took</p>
<p>ammunition up to the</p>
<p>battery in the evening.</p>
<p>Left here at 5.45 about &amp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>got back about 9 pm.</p>
