<p>1915<br/>Jan. 5 Tue.<br/>In the Gulf of Aden &ndash; African coast well in sight. (Somaliland) before breakfast.<br/>Very busy in office to-day<br/>No.2 Gen. Hospital held a Sports Meeting during the afternoon.<br/>Pte. Bay defeated Sgt. Baker in a smart bout with the gloves after tea.<br/>Have gone 274 miles in 24 hrs. 18 mins. At rate of 11.4 miles per hour. Still 293 miles to go which should bring us to Aden at 2 p.m. to-morrow.</p>
