
16 October 1915Saturday
Get leave  &  knock round Helouan for a couple of hours but soon get full of it.The usual pest of locusts have invaded Egypt again just as it did when Moses was here.
1915 October 17Sunday20th After Trinity
Church at 10 amDad  &  I go for a drive to the Nile   &  see Sakkara Pyramids in the distanceWe catch 5.30 pm to Cairo for a ride   &  I get back home about 9 pm
