 Saturday July 6 Usual routine Sunday July 7 Very windy clouds of dust in valley. Turks shelling today.  Monday July 8  Sunday July 14 During the last week there has been nothing out of the ordinary. Jacko has been shelling at various times and the weather has been exceptionally hot. The boys are just about fed up with this place &amp; would welcome anything for a change.
