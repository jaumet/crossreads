
France8.9.17 - Arrived at Havre at daylight.  Disembarked at 7 p.m.  Marched to No. 1 Rest camp, Havre.
Havre9.9.17 to 11.9.17 - Company at Rest Camp.  Inspection by Camp Commandant on 9th.  Equipment of Company completed.  3 O/Ranks admitted to Hospital.12.9.17 - Company marched out of Rest Camp and entrained.  Train left at 2.17 p.m.  Strength of Company, 10 Officers, 173 O/Ranks.13.9.17 - Detrained at St. Omer 9.15 a.m.  Company proceeded to Billets at Fauquembergues.
Fauquembergues15.9.17 to 25.9.17 - Advanced training carried out preparatory to going into Action.  All preparations made for Action.20.9.17 - 2/Lieut. F. Thompson marched in from M.G. Base.22.9.17 - Inspection of 3rd Aust. Division by Field Marshal Sir Douglas Haig.24.9.17 - 5 Officers & 20 O/Ranks proceeded to M.G. Base to witness a demonstration Machine Gun Barrage.26.9.17 - Company marched out of Billets towards Forward Area.  Billeted night 26-27/9/17 at La Belle Croix.
