  
 Wednesday 25 June 1919 Down at garage about 11. Had a bonny sleep. Wandered about till 8 pm & then went to Bills dugout. We went to dinner & then out Marie's flat for mail. None there tho. Came home about 11 pm. A letter from Janet awaiting me. Answered it & went to bed. Rained for 1/2 an hour or so in the afternoon. 
 Thursday 26 June 1919 Cold today. Had a drive & did OK. After lunch I went home & wrote a couple of letters. Went round to garage & worked. Came home & read & then went to Bill's place. We went out to dinner & then walked over to Battersea Park & to Victoria Palace. Couldn't get in so walked back to his flat. Came home at 10.30.
