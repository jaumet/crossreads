I remember last year when we were living up Chalaik dere on the peninsular &ndash; I think it was on the 13th August, - we were standing about outside our dug outs after sheltering from half a dozen shrapnel that had just come over without having done any damage, when we for our first experience of high Explosive. There was a noise like a train coming straight above our heads  &amp;  everyone made a dive for their dugouts, little fat Major Halsham threw himself into his  &amp;  three fellows standing by piled up on top of him.
