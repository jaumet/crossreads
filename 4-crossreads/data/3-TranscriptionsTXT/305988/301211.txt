 On the night of the 10th the 7th Brigade &amp; a battalion of the 6th Brigade hopped over &amp; took every objective. They took about 800 prisoners &amp; suffered very light casualties. Well that is the lot now Mick. We are still at Franvillers, but will more than likely be relieved to-morrow. 
 I don't know when I will be able to get this posted a s I will have to wait on somebody going to Blighty. 
 Blue 
 29th August 1918 I have been carrying this about with me for a long time &amp; have not had a chance to get it away, so I am going to complete it up to date. 
 If you still have the first one that I sent you hang onto it; also this one. 
 B.
