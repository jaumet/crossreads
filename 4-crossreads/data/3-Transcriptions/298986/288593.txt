<p>Wed. 27th to Sunday 31st</p>
<p>Things have moved as usual.  Rain nearly every day.  A parcel from Aunt Mima.</p>
<p>Monday 28th to Sunday 8th Dec.</p>
<p>So little to put in lately that I seem to leave it for a week at a time.  Was in Havre on Tuesday not raining but not a fine day.  Poked down some of there dirty little side streets.  Had 2 f. worth of Photos taken 20 of them.  Had 2 meals &amp; came home about 8 p.m.</p>
