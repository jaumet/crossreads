
<p>THURSDAY, JUNE 5, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Linda &amp; Jo &amp; Peter &amp;</p>
<p>self went into Quirindi in</p>
<p>little car &amp; Peter &amp; Linda got</p>
<p>the day train to Sydney.</p>
<p>Jo &amp; I did a few things in</p>
<p>town &amp; had lunch &amp; came</p>
<p>out afterwards.</p>
<p>Skinned a cow in the</p>
<p>Strand in afternoon</p>
