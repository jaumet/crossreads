 [Thursday 15 June 1916]   Ognatina  "In the shade of the Sheltering Palm" Sounds all right but when shared with Camels- &amp; only a few leaves on the top, the sun travels very quickly around, &amp; every 2 hours a man must shift, or his Water bottle boils!
