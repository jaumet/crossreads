<p>expecting a counter attack at any minute, the cry for stretcher bearers was very insistent &amp; these brave chaps were kept very busy bandaging &amp; getting the wounded under cover, of all the things I hate to hear in a trench is "stretcher bearers" for it always means that some poor devil got a knock perhaps killed &amp; we are never satisfied until we know who it is.  Jock &amp; I had a lucky escape while this Hell' was raging we were standing trying</p>
