 Australian Red Cross Bulford 20/10/18 
 Dear Dad, 
 Your letters of 21/7, 28/7,4/8, 25/8 have arrived &amp; I'll answer them before I knock off for the evening to enjoy myself.  I went to Salisbury on Wednesday &amp; visited the Close first.  I helped Mr. Freemantle tidy up the lawn &amp; paths &amp; trim the creepers around the House.  Autumn is well on now &amp; the leaves are falling very fast.
