<p>Everything is very expensive. Send to England for a new cape (red)   &amp;  it costs &amp;pound 1.2.0 (one pound two shillings) so shall have to be very careful.</p>
<p>Consie Murray is in England at Sick-Sisters Hosp. so she has offered to do any shopping for me.</p>
<p>Her sister got married to Captain McLaren, relation of Alice Bowers &ndash; she is living in England.</p>
<p>Our Tennis-Court is very popular but I'm very lazy on night-duty   &amp;  don't play much.</p>
