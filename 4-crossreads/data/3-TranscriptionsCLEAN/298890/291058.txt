  
 22. Sunday Miss Smith & I at home.  Many Indians.  Ethel & K. Adams. F. Post. Mr. & Mrs. Carpenter & others.  Wet dull day. 
 23. Monday Worked in office & went home to Miss Hodge at night.  Miss Wren had nice dinner waiting. 
 24. Tuesday Worked in office & went home to Miss Hodge. 
 25. Wednesday Lovely frosty morning but grey later.  Dinner with Miss H & Miss W.  Supper alone & went to bed.
