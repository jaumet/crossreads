<p>[Letterhead: New South Wales  Coat of Arms.]<br/>Premier's Department<br/>Sydney, 3rd May,1917</p>
<p>A.17/2707</p>
<p>Dear Judge Ferguson,</p>
<p>I have the honor to inform you that a further message has been received from the Agent-General for New South Wales in London regarding the health of your son, Lt. Ferguson, viz:-</p>
<p>"Referring to your telegram of 24th April. Advised to-day Ferguson progressing very well."</p>
<p>The cost of the above cablegram was 6/6d.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,</p>
<p>F.C.G. Tremlett<br/>Acting Secretary<br/>per W.H.T.</p>
<p>The Honorable Mr. Justice Ferguson,<br/>Supreme Court,<br/>Sydney.</p>
