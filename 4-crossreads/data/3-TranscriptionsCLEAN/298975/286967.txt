  
 2 miles to another camp & the Officer evidently could find nothing to work at for we loafed about & then marched home were told not to fall out on Paraide as we were wanted again. Did as we were told & just stayed in the Hut all the afternoon 
 Tuesday 16 One hours Musketry this morning loafed rest of day Showery all day Have to wear overcoats with equipments over them on all paraides now. But tags on my coat to day to keep it out of mud 
 Wednesday 17 Drill very good lecture afternoon by Archdeacon
