
16/6/17Handed over Camp today to 5th F. Coy. Engrs. & marched into Baupaume arriving there about 7 p.m.  Took train to Warloy & marched from there to Milancourt.
17thNot too well today.  Nothing much happened.
18thDoing Squad drill & general training.
19thMarched 15 --- today & had swim.  Having easy time.
20thLeft Milancourt at 8 a.m. & marched to Corbie arriving about 11.30 a.m.  Nothing doing during evening.
21stCompany training on Pontoon Rafting on River Somme on outskirts Corbie.  Everywhere roundabout Corbie is very beautiful & it is an important manufacturing center.
22/6/17Coy. doing Pontooning on Somme.  Nothing fresh to report.
23rdCoy. still on same work.  Off duty today.  Not well.
24thWas innoculated today.  Coy. Pontooning.
25thCoy Pontooning.  Nothing fresh going.
26thShifted Pontoons further down River where we have G.O.C. Inspection tomorrow.
27thThree Coys. put bridges across water today & were Inspected by G.O.C.  Our Coy. built bridge 170' in 15 min. & were fastest in time.  Everything very satisfactory.
28thNo bridging today.  Went to Amiens with Sgt. Barnes & Cpl. Morrison.  Amiens very busy.  Good time.
29thCoy. training for Div. sports a "Aquatic".
