
Saturday May 18th
Dunkirk : off duty 6 am : stroll with Gill after tea & watched the boats in the dock :
Sunday May 19th
Dunkirk : on duty 2 - 10 pm : plenty of air raids lately :
Monday May 20th
Dunkirk : went to Malo in morn & had a stroll round : hot day :
Tuesday May 21st
Dunkirk : yarn with my French friend & stroll through the town : air raid alarm :
Wednesday May 22nd
Dunkirk : stroll round the docks in morn : another hot day : raid alarm at night :
Thursday May 23rd
Dunkirk : wrote in morn : weather inclined to be stormy : on duty 2 pm :
Friday May 24th
Dunkirk : stroll round the docks in morn & worked 2 - 10 pm :
