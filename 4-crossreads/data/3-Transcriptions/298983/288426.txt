<p>Went to a Fair at Bainbridge one day motored about 50 miles &amp; saw a lot of pretty places about Guilford being particularly fine. Some large estates with fine trees, Also about Kilmarnock. Sheep &amp; catle are all sold privately in the main street. Was in Newing quite a large place, &amp; Visited a lot of the neighbours, All very nice people.
