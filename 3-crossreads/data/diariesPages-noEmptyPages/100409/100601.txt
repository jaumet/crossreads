6.4.19 Sunday
Up 9.45 ? breakfast 10.30. Went in car to Mill Valley and got train there and went to top of Mt Tamalpais, about 3000 ft above sea level. Stopped there couple hours, having several photos taken ? also had light lunch. I then walked back to Mrs Stephensons with Rolfe and Kyle ? taking 1 hr 20 min to go down. Somehow or other we got off the track and came straight down through the bush and hills, landing out on road about  mile from house. 
On arriving back had hot bath then played pianola for almost an hour. Had dinner 7.45 pm. Crabs, Soup, Artichokes, Turkey, Asparagus, Ice Cream, Cake and Coffee ? was very nice. Guthrie of Balfour, Guthrie and Coy was also in to tea. Had music after dinner and went to bed midnight.
7.4.19
Up 7.30. After a hurried breakfast went to Frisco by motor and ferry. I went to hotel and packed up, then had my boots cleaned. Then went with Hawkins to the photographers and got our photos after waiting about an hour. On way back to hotel called at Canadian Bank of Commerce and got 12 in Australian notes for 56.40 dollars.
Went to canteen and posted away 3 of the photos and gave one to Mrs Stephenson. The 6 of us then went for a drive to the Twin Peaks, Ocean Beach and Golden Gate Park, finishing up at her fathers house in Jackson St. Waited there while she changed her dress then went to Mrs Hale's about 2 blocks away, and had music and dancing for 2 hours, also nice afternoon tea.
