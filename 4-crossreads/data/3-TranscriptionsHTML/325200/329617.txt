<p>was J H  Kemp  who strikes me in some ways as being somewhat callow  &amp;  crude,  &amp;  is apt when enjoying himself to become a trifle boisterous  &amp;  uncouth. Many was the time I wished for Yeats or Tommy Rutterford [transcribers note: T Rutherford , not  Rutterford, is listed in Embarkation Role, #34, 1st LH Brigade] to be down there.<br/>However on the whole I enjoyed myself very much. I would get up late  &amp; at about 10 go into the sea remain there till 12.30 then to lunch at the Caf&eacute; du Nil &ndash; I would like to know how many Caf&eacute;'s du Nil there are</p>
