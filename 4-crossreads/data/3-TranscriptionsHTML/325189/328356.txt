<p>When we said we should have to go Mohamed came along with us &amp; bought us some stuff called sahleb (that' s what it sounds like).  It is made from a tree.  The vendor first filled a cup with a hot paste-and-water looking fluid &amp; then sprinkled it with two or three powders.  The tout ensemble was very good.  We then had a tea (M. could not understand tea as a meal) of caf&eacute;, olives, cheese &amp; a strange substance resembling yeast made from <strike>yeast</strike> nuts but tasting nice &amp; sweet, &amp; a roll of bread.
