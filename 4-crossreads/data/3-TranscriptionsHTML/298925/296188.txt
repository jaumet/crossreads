<p>The Recruits 10 commandments</p>
<p>I.  When on guard thou wilt challenge all parties approaching thee.<br/>II.  Thou shall not sent any engraving of any airship in the heavens above or any postcard of the earth below or any drawing of any submarine under the sea For I the censor am a jealous censor visiting the iniquities of the offenders by giving 3 months C.B. but showing mercy unto thousands by letting their letters go free<br/>III.  Thou shall not use profane language unless under extroardinary circs such as getting your comrade shot or getting petrol in your tea.<br/>IV.  Remember the soldier's week consists of 7 days 6 shalt thou labour and do all thy work &amp; on the 7th do Odd jobs</p>
