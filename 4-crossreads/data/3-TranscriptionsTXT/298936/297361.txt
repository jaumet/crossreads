 July 1915 
 Thursday 1 
 Reached Brisbane &amp; had tea at [indecipherable] with Fothergill &amp; Johnson [late of R.N.] whom we picked up at Murwillumbah. 
 Met De Lacey &amp; Saunders &amp; had a glass of port wine with them. 
 De Lacey informed me that promotions are out. Thinks I am good for a corporal &amp; raises my spirits, only for a time however. 
 Arrived at camp I find Clarke will probable be last Corporal &amp; I have been passed over 
 Depressed &amp; dreary
