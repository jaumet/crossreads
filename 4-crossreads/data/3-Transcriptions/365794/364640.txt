<p>Monday 26th August 1917 [1918]<br/>Col. Onslow &amp; Marge &pound;1 Holland motor for me 4 p.m., lovely drive to Mena House, tea &amp; home 7 p.m., fine people &amp; very nice to hear all Aust. news again.<br/>Reported pts. food to Col. Onslow, watch result.<br/>Officers' ward &ndash; convoy 7 pts., mostly convalescent.<br/>3 malaria, 2 dysentery, 1 diarr., 1 sand fly.<br/>Names &ndash; Lt. Kildea, Lt. Lane, Lt. Bancroft, Lt. Robinson, Capt. Cramb, Capt. Drury &amp; Capt. Chitty.</p>
