<p>427<br/>New Zealand Hospital<br/>Walton on Thames<br/>11.11.15<br/>Dear Father<br/>Thanks very much for your kind letter &ndash; I can't make out how you hadn't received one from me written from Epsom about August 6th. Still it may have turned up later &ndash;<br/>From this on I am going to make an effort to write to you once a week &ndash; I don't know why I can't write a decent letter &ndash; I read it over after I have written it and it doesn't seem to be worth posting.  I have had a pretty rough time lately &ndash; just as my eye  was healing up  I caught a cold in my head and the eye got very bad  I thought it would burst &ndash; the pain was rather horrible but the doctor got some stuff to put in it which</p>
