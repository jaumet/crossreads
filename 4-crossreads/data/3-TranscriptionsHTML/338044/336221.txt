<p>1st August:- Tuesday<br/>Having to report at Headquarters at 9 am for instructions we are about early    &amp; off by 8.30 am train to Waterloo Station after a good breakfast. Transpires that the usual hanging round for a few days is to take place    &amp; we are to await further instructions from the War Office for travelling facility. To report again at 5 pm. Straight to Peel House &amp; remove all my kit. Take a taxi to Grosvenor Gardens &amp; leave all my kit there   Now have it together ready to depart at any notice. Take the tube to Mansion House    &amp; Lake visits his old employers in this district, East C. I think   We meet here a fellow worker of his, who has been badly knocked about, his hand still paralysed. We lunch well</p>
