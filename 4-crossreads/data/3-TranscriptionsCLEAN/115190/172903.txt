I have now  ^  only  a water bag slung round the old mares neck but alas it contains hardly a teacupful of water.   I  God only knows when I am to get more.  It is very hot & the dust helps to parch my mouth.  We ride on to another ridge when there comes the rattle of machine guns  not far away   in front &  then ping-ping-ping as the bullets fly past & we are off at another gallop,  truly things are getting very interesting.  A little further on we see another batch of prisoners and these are ours, taken by the 15 th  Regt which has just been in action.  We cross the last ridge & descend on to a plain.
