
By the time these two trains were loaded up & finished with, it was dinner time & for the afternoon work another rake was loaded up & brought out on to our main line & was emptied out by Portugese assisted by a batch of German prisoners.
It was close on 4 pm their work was finished, when these [men?] knocked off & we took the trucks back & left them in the ballast pit siding & returned to the depot & signed off at 5 pm and that finished another good days work.
Tuesday Nov 5th 1918
