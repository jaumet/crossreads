
June 1918Saturday 15
Battery W.Lines.  near Pont NoyellesWeather wet, during morning fine and clear, in afternoon. Had a very enjoyable concert at W.L. a very good tenor,and ebcontionist.  The Star items. O'Kelly the tenor,sang several very sweet songs. Polished up old"Acks" bit and irons "Smithy  still feeling bad.Had a run to Bussy; and saw folks there.Second divvie pioneers and 5th Bdge came out. MetHarry Rich now Lt  Rich, - congratulated him on hisprogress. Received 3 letters from Australia  20/4/18
