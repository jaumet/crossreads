
Covered in dust &  feeling tired & sore after such a long journey and we arrive in Rouen three hours before the expected time - Unload Lorries & camp in 1st A.G.H. -  Meet old acquaintances including Jack Moran - Australian Sisters - Our camp just out from the Town on a pre-war Race Course - A run into the City & Return.
