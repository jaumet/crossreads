 September 1916 Sunday 19 Fine lovely morning. Had church parade every man shaved and with his best gear on and belt and bayonet. felt quite brand new again Had another issue of clothing 
 Monday 20 Fine Cool breeze. We did  some station[?] work with flags from 9 AM. A team of sailors played a team of Australians soccer but I do not know who won. The sailors were very fast.
