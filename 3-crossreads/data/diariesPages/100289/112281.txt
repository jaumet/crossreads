Friday 3.8.17
Losing our new Sgt who is going to Officers school, am again only N.C.O. left on bearer section, any amount of work Walked to Recquotoile at night where a number of the Portuguese are billeted, met one who could speak English well, was very interesting to talk to.
Saturday 4.8.17
Sent home full volume (8 copies) of "Australia in the great war"
Sunday 5.8.17
Went to Church service in a little barn at night. Very little doing during the day.
Monday 6.8.17
Granted a days leave to St Omer had a good day, rode to fro on bike. Had a yarn to a couple of Scotch lassies from
