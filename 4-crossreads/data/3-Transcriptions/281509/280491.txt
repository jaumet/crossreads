<p>25 September 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Cart, Bob, self, Jockey, Mac, Tony &amp; Bill Parsons get our phizogs taken in group in Pop. Tea after, &amp; get it cooked by waitress. Cart, Bob &amp; self back early carrying sponge cake for trifle for banquet tomorrow night.<br/>Glee Club practice. Soldiers' Chorus, Little Tommy went a-fish &amp; Dear little Shamrock.<br/>Views of 2 Zepps destroyed in England.</p>
