<p>Gallipoli Peninsula<br/>17th October 1915</p>
<p>My dear Father &amp; Mother<br/>I am "up to my neck" in work this week hence this short note. A new class of gun has been landed here and I have been placed in charge of it so I have had to go for all I am worth this last week &ndash; day and night &ndash; I am still living at 8th Battery.<br/>Your delightful letters of Sept 6th to hand &ndash; one from Mum: 2 from Father also 4 Heralds &ndash; will acknowledge next mail but am just sending this along to say I am still going strong"<br/>Fondest love<br/>Your loving Son<br/>Terence</p>
