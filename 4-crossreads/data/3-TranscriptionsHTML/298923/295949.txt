<p>10 October 1918 Thursday<br/>Medical Officer enoculate me to-day- 1st to Field Ambulance then Amman   Rough ride over the stones &amp; the men suffered-<br/>The Hospital is an ex-Turkish one at the foot of the old Amphitheatre to seat 4000 with carved granite Pillars along a drive once 400 yds long-<br/>The Amphitheatre is now occupied by Bedouin dogs-<br/>As is usual I was robbed of my big oil sheet &amp; Motor goggles off my Hat on arrival!!</p>
