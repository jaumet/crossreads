
<p>347</p>
<p>APRIL 1917</p>
<p>23 MONDAY</p>
<p>A beautiful warm sunny day.</p>
<p>I was at a gas lecture in</p>
<p>morning &amp; we had a half</p>
<p>holiday in afternoon. Bill</p>
<p>Hunter &amp; self went up a hill</p>
<p>near by &amp; lay in the sun</p>
<p>all afternoon yarning &amp;</p>
<p>writing letters. We had a</p>
<p>beautiful view of the country</p>
<p>&amp; it looks gloriously pretty.</p>
<p>Campbell recomended me</p>
<p>for S<sup>t</sup>&nbsp;John&#39;s Wood Officers</p>
<p>school &amp; filled in particulars</p>
<p>for same Had no more</p>
<p>word about Alan.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
