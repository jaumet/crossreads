<p>Thursday 25 January 1917 [1918]<br/>Went town 5.30 p.m. &amp; sent cable to Mother for birthday.<br/>Bought "guide to Cairo", Pt. 45.  Had rubbed heel on tan shoes (Pt. 10).<br/>Went 14 A.G.H. 8 p.m.  English sisters changing from 31st G.H.<br/>Am going to ask for trip to Luxor.</p>
