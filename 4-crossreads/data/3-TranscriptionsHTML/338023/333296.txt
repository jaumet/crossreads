<p>The Photo of the group was taken by one of the instructors in the school. Other photos I have, I am sending to Mrs Horton to look after and you will get them later on. They represent a few of the characters one meets on the business of Boche hunting and some day may be useful.</p>
<p>Since writing last I have nothing to report about my job. I still have it, and am likely to do so, having passed all tests. At present I'm putting in a few hours daily at map work &amp; its just now that my old efforts at this are proving invaluable&hellip;.for the simple reason they make it possible for me to study the surroundings [indecipherable] than the job. In fact the 15/- I once spent on a compass was an A1 investment.</p>
<p>Well, now, having no more to write for the present, I will say good night &amp; turn in, with love to all from Harry.</p>
