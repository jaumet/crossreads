<p>Saturday 24th Oct<br/>Spent morning at usual pastime &amp; the afternoon went with Hynes &amp; Drummond for coral. We found a patch which looked beautiful from the boat but on diving for it it was not very good so we returned. Received first war news.</p>
<p>Sunday 25 October<br/>In the afternoon we secured a native canoe, and after much manoeuvring (greatly to the merriment of the niggers) we succeeded in taking it up the harbour.. We were about to land when we were overhauled by three Chinese in an outrigger who hailing us told us in quite unconcerned tones that there were two "pook-pooks" on the shore. We could not realise</p>
