<p>Sunday 26th Sept<br/>It was again a lovely day. Ordnance visit again.</p>
<p>Monday 27th Sept<br/>A lovely day again &ndash; just mild. On the hills of the islands as the sun is setting there is a wonderful glow tinted with every shade reflecting on the white clouds that seem to be resting on the ridges. It has been quiet all day excepting a few fusilades from several Batteries on our left flank.</p>
<p>Tuesday 28th<br/>I had a quiet day. In the evening there was a very heavy bombardment taking place both on our right &amp; on our left.
