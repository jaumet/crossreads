  
 I could stay quiet & comfortable in our dugout & felt more or less safe but still they were very unpleasant.  None dropped very close to us, though I was surprised that they didn't have a go at the camels. 
 10 p.m., 5th May Tonight of course our 'planes have been on the rampage.  We suspected that they were our 'planes but still we felt impelled to blow the light out & give little apprehensive shivers as they droned their way overhead.  Queer how nervous this sort of thing makes one. 
 10 p.m., 6th May For the second time in three days our nightly game of crib has been interrupted. Those Taubes have been at it again! Dropped about 20 bombs & set fire to our supply dump at railhead but fortunately none came near us.  Our A.A. guns replied
