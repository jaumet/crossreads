  
 [December 1918] 5 Th Nutty the old b-is having a royal time good grass & stable, he reckons it is just like being at home. 6 Fri Letters from home ar pleadings of "amour", but that sort of stuff with me is dead a mans mind as been altered with the war 7 Sat It matters not what men assume to be, good or bad, they are but what they are a statement with a lot of meaning some men think or assume to be wise and clever, and others big things with talented ideas but I ( Ca ne fait sien )
