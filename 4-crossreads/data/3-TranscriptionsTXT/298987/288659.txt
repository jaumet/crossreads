
Thursday 21stFinished guard at 9 aM Just the same old thing as at Liverpool only less style & no arms to carry. There has been some mixup with the blankets & hammocks. Two many are supposed to have been issued & the Pursar was collecting spare one this morning & got mine & most of the guards while we were being relieved am without a bunk tonight
Friday 22ndWeather has improved greatly. Shoe Deck shoes were issued today. We do a little drill on deck now about 1/2 an hour morning & afternoon After a good deal of trouble got a hammock & blankets for my self & any others of the Guard who were short.Reported we are to call at Fremantle
Saturday & Sunday 23 & 4Two nice days. Nothing out of the way has happened. Much letter
