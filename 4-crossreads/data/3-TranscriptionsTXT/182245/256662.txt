 381 
 MAY 1917 
 27 SUNDAY 
 Fine &amp; warm. I was stableman 
 all day. Went down to the 3 rd  
 DAC in evening &amp; saw Poss 
 Nivison &amp; he is in great form. 
 Went up to the pits as a 
 gunner during the night &amp; 
 we had a raid &amp; fired 134 
 shells, raid was not all the 
 success we hoped for.
