  
 Oct. 16th Rehearsal & Show. Good house. Splendid reception. Commence the preparation of the "Monkey's Paw". 
 Oct. 17th Excellent War News. - Important towns recaptured. Brilliant achievements of General Foch - Rehearsal Shows and study. 
 Oct 18th Change of programme. - Many Australians in town - In taking Lille & Douai General Birdwood has his wish of "1916" gratified.
