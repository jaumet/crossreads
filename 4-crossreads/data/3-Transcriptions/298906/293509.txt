<p>All those who were on the route march this morning had lectures all the afternoon, this was very handy &amp; far better than drilling.<br/>The farmers are all busy spraying their potatoes around here, they are frightened of the Irish blight.<br/>26th. No P.T. Instructors available this morning, so the Sergts had to give the physical jerks, old Tubby was buzzing round, but he never had much to say for a wonder<br/>Had a light afternoons work had X &amp; No 1 Coy. wiring &amp; digging trenches.<br/>27th. Full muster parade this morning at 9.45 we had our Coy out on the first parade, Lord how old Tubby</p>
