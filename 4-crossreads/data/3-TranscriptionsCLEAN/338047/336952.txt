We were billeted in some big stables & as there was a little spare floor space, we all started dancing.  Everyone was dance mad, & danced whether they were able to or not.  Each pair supplied their own music by humming or whistling as they danced.  After tea I went for a walk, & had a look at the churchyard & view.  It was pretty & no mistake.  It reminded me of the scenic pictures one sees at the picture shows at times.
