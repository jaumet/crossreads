<p>L5 and upwards according to the credits in their books. I drew &pound;5, which was paid in English notes. These notes are guaranteed by the English Government &amp; there is no exchange on them in Australia or South Africa.</p>
<p>The delay in sailing this afternoon was caused by the delay in repairing the engines.</p>
<p>About 8.30 we had passed through Back Stairs Straits a narrow passage between the mainland &amp; Kangaroo Island and was soon out into the ocean when she commenced to roll and she met the heavy swells of the Southern Ocean.</p>
<p>This evening I repacked my things in the two kit-bags and also crammed in the red cross goods which were issued to us in a large pillow slip. A haversack which I used to contain some souvenirs, papers, etc. I gave away to a coal trimmer to get rid of it, as in all probability our things will be inspected by the customs &amp; military authorities in Melbourne. The customs will look for dutiable goods and the military for army goods that are not an issue.</p>
