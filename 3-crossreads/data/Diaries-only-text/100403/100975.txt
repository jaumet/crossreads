March
10th. Entrain this morning at 10 a.m. move out 15 minutes later. Adieu Charleroi Belgium. Our route lays through Mons Douai etc. Comfortable truck.
11th. After a remarkably quick comfortable journey of 30 hours we arrived at Harfleur to-night at about 5 p.m. marched to the Camp where we spend the night. Everything on the journey was the best possible.
12th. Generally fixed up with bathing, clean clothes (new) sea kit bags, after which we move under canvas for the night. Equipment handed in.
