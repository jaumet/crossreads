 Good Friday 6-4-17 We made a fresh start today erecting Huts at Grevillers.  The 21st Battalion was there on 13-3-17.  I strolled across to Bihucourt.  The country is covered with barbed wire & honeycombed with deep dug-outs.  Plenty of Cavalry about.  Railways are laid through both the above mentioned villages.  There is a report the an N.C.O. & 14 men of the 56th Battalion walked over on this front lately.  Machine gunners have been advised to act in future cases.
