
No 3 Canadian C.C.S.B.E.F. FranceSep 25th/17
Mrs C. HillEuterpeKurraba RoadNeutral BayNorth SydneyN. S. Wales
Dear Mrs HillIt is my sad duty to inform you of the death of your Son Sgt Hill 874; 19th B'n, 2nd Aus Div: on Saturday Sep 22 at 2.10 p.m.: I had worship with him a short time before he died & he asked me to write to you giving his love: He was seriously wounded but everything possible was done for his comfort & recovery: His End was peaceful: He will be buried in the Lejssenhoek Cemetery, Belgium:You have my deep sympathy & I pray that God may Comfort you in your hour of sorrow:
Yours sincerelyA. H. Devon
