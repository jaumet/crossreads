<p>Tuesday 20&nbsp;November 1917.</p>
<p>&quot;Stand to&quot; ar 4 am (a kind of punishment for not turning out yesterday morning.)</p>
<p>Picquet 6 to 8 am.</p>
<p>Breakfast and started to write letters.</p>
<p>French Jews bring bread, eggs, cake &amp; wine &amp; brandy round on sale to the troops.</p>
<p>Mail to hand. Letters as late as 23rd Sept dated in Australia.</p>
<p>Cold &amp; wet day. Very bleak.</p>
<p>Wrote a letter tonight.</p>
