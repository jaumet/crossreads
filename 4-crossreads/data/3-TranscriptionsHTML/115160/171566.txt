<p>Paris on the 25th <s>October</s> Nov 1918</p>
<p>My dear Sir;</p>
<p>There is already a long time I have received no news of you. &nbsp;I hope that you are always in good health.</p>
<p>I suppose that you have not received my two last letters that I have sent to you before the armistice.</p>
<p>At last! the war is over, and it shall be possible for you to go back at home. You and your fellow soldiers have deserved well of the country.</p>
<p>When the signature of the armistice</p>
