 May 1915 9 Sun O/b the P and O SS Devanha are Col Chauvel and staff 1st and 2nd regts, several infantry details, wounded officers returning and various officers to replace etc. Also the carts and blankets of bns that had come in her originally.  heard many details of the fight. 10 Mon At sea &ndash; no other ships in sight. Passed some beautiful islands like Saros and Lemnos.
