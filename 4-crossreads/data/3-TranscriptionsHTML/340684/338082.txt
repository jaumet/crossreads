<p>355<br/>Manchester Eye Hospital<br/>Oxford Rd<br/>Manchester<br/>8th July 1915</p>
<p>Dearest Joker and Bobbie<br/>Very many thanks for your letters &ndash; I got quite a bundle this morning &ndash; Ann and Maria and Harry</p>
<p>Mrs Evans- wrote also a Mrs Evans &ndash; mother of Bella's friend &ndash; I went out to lunch yesterday with a Prof.Dickson &ndash; He met you in Sydney last</p>
