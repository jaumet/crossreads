<p>24 July 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>Proceeding well &ndash; so far no further mishap. This afternoon after wishing us luck our Escort left us. Sea rough all day with heavy wind &amp; rain squals  A good many of the boys sea. sick. We appear to be off the usual track as we have only sighted one boat since leaving &amp; that a Destroyer.</p>
<p>1918 July 25<br/>Thursday<br/>Nothing unusual happening. The weather has cleared up considerably the sea is much more smooth. Reported by Wireless that Submarines have been sighted about a days sail ahead. A Large ships passed close about 10.30 P.M.</p>
