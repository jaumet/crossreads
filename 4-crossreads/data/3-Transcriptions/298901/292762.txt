<p>number down from 70 to 20 &amp; we will carry on as before.  Busy all the morning getting them away also extra gear otherwise nothing doing.  Rain nearly all day.  Had a swim in the baths, which are first rate, after tea &amp; enjoyed it thoroughly, the water being quite warm.  Started on French again.  Fourth anniversary of France declaring war today.</p>
<p>Saturday 3rd August<br/>Things very quiet &amp; I appreciate it.  Made up a small requisition for the brigade &amp; sent it along to the A.D.M.S. for signature.  Rumours are about of something doing and I have had several requests for shell dressings which points to us soon being in action again.  Had a swim after tea again and enjoyed it immensely.  The baths are about 100 yds long &amp; 14 ft deep with spring boards, etc &amp; dressing cubicles</p>
