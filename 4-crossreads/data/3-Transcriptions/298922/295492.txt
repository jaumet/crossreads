<p>Monday 25 June 1917</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]  The Bathing has done the men a lot of good. Septic Sores are healing fast. Managed to get 26 pairs of Boots but they want HATS &amp; Riding Pants badly. Only a <u>single</u> railway line makes it difficult to deliver any stuff beyond bare necessities.</p>
<p>Beer at the rate of 2 per man for 5 P.T. the medical folk think this is good for the blood. Very easy to take anyway. I hope to go up in a plane tomorrow from our local Aerodrome.</p>
