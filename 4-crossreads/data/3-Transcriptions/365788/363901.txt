<p>1st Field Amb.<br/>1 Aus Brigade,<br/>1 Aus Division<br/>Aus Intermediate Base<br/>Egypt. 10/3/16.</p>
<p>My dearest Irene,<br/>I was very pleased to receive a letter from you dated 7th Feby.<br/>My throat is well again I am glad to say &ndash; I write sometimes twice by each mail to you so you ought to get quite a lot of letters.<br/>Was very sorry to hear that Will has been sick with that beastly Lumbago, tell him he has my sympathy for I am told it is an agonizing Complaint. I do hope</p>
