  
 Saturday 27/4/18  put in a good night wiring along front 
 Sunday 28/4/18  Finished second belt of wire  orders for relief by 28 th   
 Monday 29/4/18  Relief cancelled 25 th  took over part of line- being relieved by Tommies  Wrote Doff 
 Tuesday 30/4/18  Relieved by Buffs, at midnight marched back to Behencourt arriving at 3am 
 Wednesday 1/5/18  Left Behencourt at 3pm & moved to Pont Noyelle [Pont-Noyelles] road over to Bussy about 6 to see Madam Domart
