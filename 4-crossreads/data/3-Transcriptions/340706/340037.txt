<p>22-3-19 Sat<br/>As Mansell has received orders to leave the school decide to go to town, lunch together &amp; go to theatre. As I am orderly officer I cannot go &amp; spent the day in solitude with a fire &amp; a book which I am too sleepy to read.</p>
<p>At dinner I disclose my real identity to Major McColl &amp; we chat on for some time &amp; write letters. The major is sorry that I am in &amp; wishes he had known &amp; I could have gone &ndash; However it suits my purpose to stay in as I have no money &amp; must economise &amp; I get his commiseration</p>
<p>23-3-19 (Sun)<br/>Lectures cease at 12 noon to day &amp; Mansell Richard &amp; I sail into down into the Museum of antiquities most clerical from old churches with some old armour. One rifle about 10 ft long &amp; a good collection of spurs.</p>
<p>Thence we visited museum with anotomical castes of neck face hand feet &amp; development of foetus. Then to an Exhibition of painting in the Mus&eacute;e in <strike>Luxembourg</strike> Solferino Garden.
