<p>Dysentry very bad in camp. Concert in our tent at night.</p>
<p>11th April (Tuesday)<br/>Warmer still. Carpentering most of the day and carting stones from Road Head North on Camels. This meant a few rides for us. We gave the "Gypo" &frac12; piastre to let us have a gallop, so, using the whip, we got such a rough trip that we don't look forward to another like it.</p>
