  
 Monday 28th About 1 PM the W.A. went off on an old punt. It was showery & looked a miserable turn out for men returning to there homes. We put on some meat & a lot of water & moved out about 10 PM. The Red X & YM sent some cases of very good apples on for us  We hear the WA had a great reception ready for us. But I doubt it as the 7 days quarantine seems to be a fixed regulation.
