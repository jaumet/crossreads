<p>1915<br/>Sep. 24 Fri.<br/>Inoculated against cholera.<br/>An N.C.O. &amp; 22 men sent from No. 2. G.H. to reinforce at Gallipoli.<br/>Feels strange being back at Cairo again &ndash; tucker pretty rotten &ndash; Montazah every time.<br/>Weather much cooler now &amp; shower cold first thing in the morning. Days shorter &ndash; Winter coming.</p>
