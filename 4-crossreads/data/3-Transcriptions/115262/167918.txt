<p>Thursday 31 May 1917.</p>
<p>Up at 5 a.m. Mounted guard on No 7 post from 7 am till 9 a.m. (No 6 &amp; 7 decks aft)</p>
<p>Air very bad on No 3 troop deck.</p>
<p>Dismissed at 9.15 am. Did washing</p>
<p>Marching order parade at 1.30 pm I was minus 1 cap &amp; 1 boot, found cap later but not other boot.</p>
<p>Awning taken down altogether as a monsoonal disturbance is expected tonight. Hatchways closed over.</p>
<p>G. S. S. Crmn &amp; I went down to Engine Room, propeller shaft tube, &amp; stokehold at 6 pm.</p>
<p>Sleeping on deck &amp; chancing rough weather.</p>
<p>3 wks now since leaving Port Melbne.</p>
