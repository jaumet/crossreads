<p>Friday 22/6/17<br/>The wether has improved greately quite nice &amp; fine today. It is reported we are to call at Freemantle Much letter writing is the order of the day. Shoses issued today</p>
<p>Sat 23 Sunday 24/6/17<br/>Two nice days fairly calm nothing out of the way happened<br/><strike>Monday 25</strike> There is a report that there is melingitis on board &amp; that we are to call at Freemantle</p>
