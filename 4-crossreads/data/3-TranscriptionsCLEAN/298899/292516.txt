  
 I was put in charge of evacuations at night and had a fairly busy time but had a man to show me the ropes. Had a good supper at midnight in the Cookhouse my friend being good with the Cook. It rained during our march down & made things very uncomfortable & cold & may interfere with the stunt which is on. They hopped the bags this morning & reports are very glowing. 
 Friday night & Saturday morning 5th & 6th  Slept all day Friday & slept well & started again at 8 P.M. but found practically nothing to do.
