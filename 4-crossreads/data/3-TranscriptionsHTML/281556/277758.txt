<p>28th. Friday.<br/>Turned in 4am for sleep till about 10 then continued sap, making dugouts. Were fed very well all day Stew Ham sandwiches, strawberry jam, bread, butter, tea &amp; rum issued freely. Coy. strength in Sap. 90. Instructions issued for Start tonight. Attack by 5th. 6th. &amp; 7th. Bgd's. I was with O.C &amp; Chambers in 1st. Line. 50 strong. Chambers was killed about 50 yds out while returning to our shellhole.
