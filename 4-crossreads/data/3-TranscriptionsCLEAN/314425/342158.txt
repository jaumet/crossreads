Thursday Aug 15. 1918. 1472.Another hot day Foggy early this morning. We did not get orders to shift last night but may do so today. In the recent stunt our Division took 4000 prisoners. A good record.This morning three of us were late on parade myself amongst the number and we have to do an extra picquet tonight.
Friday Aug 16. 1918.1473.Another hot day. I drew first picquet from 9 to 11.30. About 10pm enemy bombing planes were over in large numbers boming woods roads & gullies in search of troops, Transports and artillery.I had a walk around Susan wood yesterday and saw a number of German 9 in guns and two of their 3 in guns which latter they had rendered useless by exploding charges in the barrels thereby bursting them.This evening with Mathews I had another tour around
