A very bad accident was narrowly averted this morning about 2am,  We  passing about 20 yards off a partly submerged derelict.  The O o W immediately worked out the position, and from then till daylight &#189; hourly warnings were sent out by wireless to all vessels in range.  Nothing of interest during day.
