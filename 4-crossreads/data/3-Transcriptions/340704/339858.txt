<p>October 1917<br/>Thursday 25<br/>Went out on a stunt abt midday towards 630. At night I was one of a party on out post while others were digging trenches. Saw few Turks at daybreak our patrol fired on. Got shelled after daylight. Jacks occupied 630 soon after we left. Got back to camp 5pm.</p>
