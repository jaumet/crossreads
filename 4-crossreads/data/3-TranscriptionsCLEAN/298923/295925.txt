  
 16 September 1918 Monday Received a Parcel from Packing Store Sydney with no name of sender contained loaf sugar Chewing gum etc:-  I wonder who the donor is?  How about asking May what clothes Jack wants & give him some of mine-  I think the farther a man gets away from Poverty- the greater the fear of it- if once known.
