
13th.  Well this has been a red letter day without mistake, the trouble all started over this early morning roll call, for none of the huts would get up for it this morning, no not even for the officers & they tried their hardest, the consequence was that 6 chaps out of the Artillery & 3rd Div were shoved in the clink for a crime that every man in the Camp was guilty of, this so enraged the mob that they swore they would not go on parade until they released the men from the clink, & when the bugle sounded the "fall in" not a single man budged you should have seen the officers running about trying to get them on parade but no good, the 2nd  Div fell in & I just got round to the Coy. parade ground to see a pretty
