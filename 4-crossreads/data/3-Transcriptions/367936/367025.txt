<p>Wednesday 16 August 1916<br/>Nothing doing.<br/>Wrote to Mother (12).  Wrote to Mrs. McDonald.</p>
<p>Thursday 17 August 1916<br/>On fatigue all day putting up tents for Divisional Head Quarters as they are shifting up to here.<br/>Got 4 more letters.  None from Home.<br/>Good concert in Y.M.C.A. in evening.</p>
