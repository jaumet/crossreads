<p>Saturday 30/10/15<br/>Signalling Competition during morning, did fairly well.<br/>Went to Flemington in afternoon to see Derby run, had a very pleasant afternoon.  Flemington course very fine, railway accommodation very poor.  Returned to city for tea at Boulevard Caf&eacute;.<br/>Went to Tivoli fairly good programme.  Had supper at Boulevard Caf&eacute; returned to camp 12 pm.</p>
