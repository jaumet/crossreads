<p>27-1-17<br/>get sorted, &amp; then went to the Field Ambulance &amp; made myself conmfortable; One of the Sig Troop told me he saw a letter saying Sutherland had applied &amp; received special permission to rejoin the Regt &amp; would be back on Monday so that knocks any chance I had for a commission on the head</p>
<p>28-1-17<br/>Sun 28th<br/>Two enemy planes came over this morning flying high they kept well out to sea but were within range of the guns which did remarkably fine shooting but failed to bring either down.</p>
<p>Put in a very quiet day lying down reading &amp; writing</p>
