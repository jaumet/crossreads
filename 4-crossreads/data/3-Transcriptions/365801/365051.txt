<p>a very severe cost.  I only now have one of those officers left (wounded), &amp; can recognise very few of my men.  It will take a long time to train these reinforcements to the same state of efficiency as the old company, but still it will be done.</p>
<p>You were asking me to send along snapshots, but we are not allowed to carry cameras.  I got yours &amp; gathered from [indecipherable] that the German prisoners were having too much of a good thing.  We have heard stories here about the treatment they deal out to our men, which will not improve matters for themselves if we get into their trenches, which I hope we will do.<br/>I will write again in a day or two.<br/>Your affectionate son<br/>Arthur</p>
