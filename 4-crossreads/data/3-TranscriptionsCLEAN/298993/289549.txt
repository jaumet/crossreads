  
 Monday Oct 9. Revellie 4 A.M. trecked all day over desert and camped at wells in oasis at Barda. Accompoamed by section of indian mountain Battery and section of 12th I.C.C.company.  Enemy country . Tuesday 10.10.16 trecked to magiebia camped for night route very hilly watered camels. Wednesday. 11.10.16 trecked to Bayud. first line of defence joined up with 4th company oasis and watering Place Depot formed last few days camped in hollow of hills, Thursday 12.10.16 Fatigue A.S.C. stand to arms 4.A.M. Friday 13.10.16 watered camels and filled fantasies ready for stunt. issued with extra ammunition
