<p>January, 1915<br/>Wednesday 20<br/>This Convoy apart from the troops &amp; horses is loaded with valuable Cargo for London &ndash; thousands of carcases of sheep, wool, Ores, etc. so Germany would have secured a good haul had she been able to catch us. Have been reading about German atrocities which for a civilized nation are appalling.<br/>The White Star boats - [indecipherable] &amp; Versic which left us to Coal at Aden should be waiting for us there.</p>
