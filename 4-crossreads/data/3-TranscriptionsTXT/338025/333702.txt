 Friday 1st June 1917 Nr Bouzincourt Reveille 5.30am Put waggons in line. Breakfast 7am.Cleaning lines and harness cleaning till 12 noon. 2 till 5pm Harness cleaning. After tea went for a swim in lake about 1 &frac12; mls from our camp. (The first swim I have had since leaving Australia) (but not the first wash).
