 Dec 27/15 One of the prisoners brought in on Xmas Day was one of the deserters - a corporal - of the the party of 12 men & officers  of the Camel   who went over to the enemy  (all Egyptian) of the Camel Corps who went over to the enemy with their camels He was tried yesterday & shot today - The only possible sentence for his crime. -
