Of what I have seen up to date it is a credit to the Drill Instructors. After a splendid Hot Tea provided I took a stroll up to the Village of Borden & was struck with the quaint Homes of the People. The Houses are built of Stone & Slate & Tile Roofs. After a pleasant walk up & down I adjourned to the Picture Hall to see a programe which was crowded with Troops. Afterwards I being tired & sleepy I made my way back to my quarters, & made up my bed for the Night. After enjoying a quiet Chat of our day experiences when lights out was sounded at 10-15 Pm all hands were soon fast asleep.
