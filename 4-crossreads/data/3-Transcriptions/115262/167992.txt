<p>Monday 13 August 1917.</p>
<p>Inspection in drill order &amp; camels in marching order by Colonel Buxton at 6.30 a.m. Result not too good.</p>
<p>Finished grooming after breakfast at 10.15 a.m.</p>
<p>Mess orderly today.</p>
<p>Went on leave to Cairo at 5 pm. &amp; back at 10 p.m.</p>
