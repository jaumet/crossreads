<p>May 25th  N.N.W. M.322<br/>Coy for Duty myself Cpl. of Guard over stern Gun 9-30 &ndash; 24 hrs. 4 men 2 hrs. on six off, no physical drill for Guards.  Ship's officers rifle shooting floating boxes etc., range by sextant 5 to 1400 good shots.  2-30 p.m. dropped Life belt practice man over.  Lowered boat, ship turned right round &amp; reached belt same time as boat.  Gun practice 6 a piece at 6 ft. sq. target drifting astern, engines stopped, good aiming, all over 4 p.m. &amp; reviewed course.  Card writing on after deck from sunset, study after from 7-30.  Weather perfect nearly.  [See Page 27 for May 26th.]</p>
