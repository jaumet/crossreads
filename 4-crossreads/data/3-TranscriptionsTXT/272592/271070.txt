 As I write this the Germans are shelling 2 of our aeroplanes for all they are worth but they don't appear to take much notice of the shells that are bursting like white wooly fleeces all round them. 
 All vegetation especially trees &amp; hedges show the effects of the gas upon them, a lot are dead &amp; the majority are very seedy looking on it.
