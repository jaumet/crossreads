<p>12 December, 1916 Tuesday</p>
<p>Examined candidates for school. Received 8 parcels &amp; 7 letters, met Cyril Downes. Went to the movies with the Doctor.</p>
<p>13 December, 1916 Wednesday</p>
<p>Left Moascar for Alex at 2 arrived Sidi Gaba 7.30 was met at station &amp; am now quartered at Cleopatra</p>
