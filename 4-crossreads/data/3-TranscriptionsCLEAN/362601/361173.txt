  
 14 March, Friday 
 F. We go to Beach for a walk. Meet Uncle & Pig Trough on way home. I learn more about my Fate & I tin coiffeur. Play Rummy & other games in Kitchen. Supper at a late Home. 
 15 March, Saturday 
 F. We go to Ramsey & Pictures & see the Deemster. I get puzzled in the Darkness. Tea at Cingalee & Home via Lezayre road. We are both very tired. I turn hair-dresser & I am made very happy. We top up til near 12 & are so sleeply.
