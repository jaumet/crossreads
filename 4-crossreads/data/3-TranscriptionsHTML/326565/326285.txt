<p>Small Christmas gifts from Australia given out during afternoon.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12/1/16<br/>Received further news re evacuation of Dardanelles; very successfully carried out with only slight losses.  Practically all Australian troops withdrawn from Gallipoli camping in vicinity of Tel-el-Kabir; all looking much the worse for wear.</p>
<p>Thursday 13/1/16<br/>Rained slightly early morning.  Very quiet day.</p>
<p>Friday 14/1/16<br/>Cold morning.  Received mail from home; first for nearly 3 weeks</p>
