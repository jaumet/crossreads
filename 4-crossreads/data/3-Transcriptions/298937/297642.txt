<p>Tuesday 8th August</p>
<p>Touched the islands off the French Coast at breakfast time &amp; soon the South of France came in view.<br/>After dinner we picked up our pilot &amp; entered a strait at the outer part of the harbour.<br/>In the centre of this strait is an island fortified strongly, &amp; on either side are forts &amp; barracks.<br/>Rounding a bend on the right</p>
