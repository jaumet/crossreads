
you know it is summer time here & the middle of the day is very warm especially when you are marching & carrying everything you possess, & the dust kicked up by the troops nearly chokes you, Saw some beautiful crops of wheat round this part, they were a picture Halloy where we are billeted is the same as most of the little French towns, very pretty but I fancy this a prettier one than most of them, there is a nice little stream running through her, & a very fine chateau & beautiful green trees line the roads & stream, & flowers, flowers, every where especially rose's
