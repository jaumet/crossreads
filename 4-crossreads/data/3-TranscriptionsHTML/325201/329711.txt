<p>refused  a corporals job back in Xmas 1915.<br/>31st August<br/>Well, today I had intended to write long  &amp;  pithily   <strike>to night</strike> , my theme being the  innocuous  passage of the Month of August  of which I believe was rally superstitious   <strike>over</strike>  .
