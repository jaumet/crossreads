<p>October, 1915<br/>Saturday 9<br/>Put on my heavy service boots for the first time, they are stiff from the blood that ran down my leg when wounded &amp; I find I am not as strong as I thought. Walked through the village of Helonan &amp; watched the milk being delivered by driving the cows or water buffaloes to the doors. The bedouin drives his goats &amp; sheep to the Butchers shop &amp; the butcher picks out a lamb or a ram &amp; pays &amp; kills it right away meat is dear in Egypt &amp; the troops are fed on frozen meat.</p>
