Monday 24.7.16
Walked to Armentieres, a distance of about 4 miles fairly large town, but has been somewhat battered about since the beginning of the war. Met Capt Chaplain Cranston had a very pleasant yarn with him.
Tuesday 25.7.16
Still very quiet on our part of the line, very few casualties coming through. Official report re casualty list from 19th inst states that there were 9000 Casualties among in our division
