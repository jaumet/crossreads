  
 Tuesday 7 August 1917. 
 Instruction in full marching order for the field where it appears we will move out to shortly. 
 To Cairo in evening to have envelopes containing silk handkerchiefs censored at Anzac Hostel. Had to leave them there till tomorrow.  
 Supper at Y.M.C.A. & back to camp. 
 During afternoon we were issued with the rest of our equipment for the field & had to fill water tanks & dhurra bags for tomorrow.
