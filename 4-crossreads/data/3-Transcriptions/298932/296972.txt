<p>Bushmills<br/>Monday 26<br/>Coleraine, it is hard to leave you treated me like a toff<br/>Tuesday 27<br/>Now in the city of Belfast staying with the Miss Millers of Unnersely Avenue, nice girls<br/>Wednesday 28<br/>Met a little Aust nurse, a dear little girl but she is married. Port closed Sister Caddoux<br/>Thursday 29<br/>Arrived at Dublin for tea fine city but not as good as Belfast<br/>Ports still closed</p>
