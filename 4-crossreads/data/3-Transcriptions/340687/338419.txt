<p>April, 1917. Wednesday 25<br/>Day dull and windy some sun. Up at 10 and cleaned up. Did some more writing. Pumped in afternoon and went to same concern in evening. Bed at 10.30. Australians supposed to be off this front by May 8th and we are to go too.</p>
