Good crowd of people were out to view the proceeding.  The procession was nothing extra ordinary being composed mainly of volunteer Corps.  After tea aboard we walked down town & through the gardens.  All business places were closed today being bank holiday here.Tuesday 3rd August 1915.  Aboard all day & went ashore after tea.  Witt & I attended Empire where a revue was staged.  Thing very poor, left early.
