<p>Friday 15th.<br/>My chums &amp; I slept on deck and intend to do so all along as it is not too pleasant inside on account of the stuffiness. The hammocks are very comfortable as they give to the motion of the boat, though the weather is very calm and there is very little seasickness.</p>
