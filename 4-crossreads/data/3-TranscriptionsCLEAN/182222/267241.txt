One man died of exhaustion as result of march, one of snakebite; Bill Hillyer in Hosp. not expected to live. Out of 1200 men only 40 of us were fools enough to march right up without a sleep on the track but am not sorry as most of the others are suffering from sort of low fever from the exposure to the jungle mist. This place a nest of deadly snakes & brilliantly colored lizards.  Spent part of day hunting fruit, snakes, monkeys etc. Monk is a simple proposition.
