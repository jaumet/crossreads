 TUESDAY, AUGUST 5, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; warm, but heavy frost in 
 morning. I rode round bottom 
 end &amp; then started stacking 
 up wood in corner of Middle 
 paddock. Tickle drawing water 
 and mending the fence between 
 4D and Wheelers. 
 The Bore down 137 ft &amp; no water 
 yet.
