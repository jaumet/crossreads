10th January 1916 El Arish WednesdayNo authentic news has percolated through yet from the front line at Rafa! Only rumours I quote several(1) The Australians have been successful in capturing a redoubt(2) The Australians and Yeomanry has had many casualties(3) 3,000 Turks have been surrounded(4) A Turkish Division has been surrounded.The moon was obscured last night by clouds and it looked like rain in fact a few drops did fall but not much. I was glad it did not rain for the sake of our boys out on the open country.This morning the sun is shining beautifully and there is a cold wind blowing a  Our aeroplanes are flying to & fro the whole morning just before dark last night there was 7 of them came back together like   birds coming home to roost.3 Field Guns [18 pounders] arrived back here and a heavier gun has gone outNot feeling much better am run down and want a spell.12.30 pm. Same date The troops have just returned from Rafa
