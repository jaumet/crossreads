<p>This town is situated on a hill from which a splendid view of the surrounding country can be had. This town is of a very historical nature. One comes across its name frequently in history</p>
<p>April 8th.<br/>Painting the O.T.</p>
<p>April 9th.10th.<br/>Fritz broke through near Laventre and got into Estaires  Hand to hand fighting took place in the streets there</p>
<p>A terrific bombardment commenced on the morning of the 9th.<br/>In the evening wounded began pouring in, working the whole night long</p>
<p>Rumour of terrific fighting in and around Estaires.</p>
<p>Bailleul heavily bombarded also Estaires, La Gorgue, Hazebroucke, Outersteen, Meteren.</p>
<p>The roads are said to be blocked with people flying for their lives, from the towns and villages  Everything seems black against us at present, although I must say that I feel confident that we will win through.</p>
<p>The men whom we left at our old station had to have a rapid exit from that place. They were bombed. The Sgt who was left in charge of these men has been recommended for a M.M, for some work that he had done there during the retreat.</p>
<p>April 12th.<br/>After having operated all the time since the wounded started</p>
