<p>400 today.  Our unit is busy either carrying these men on their backs or taking them to Quarry Dump on the trolly.<br/>29th November (Wednesday)<br/>Chopping timber for the A.D.S. fires from Bernafay Wood.  The French join the British lines here &amp; we were up alongside their trolly line in the wood.<br/>30th November (Thursday)<br/>Left again for duty in the line <strike>again</strike> at 1 p.m.  This time we are out at No. 4 post in a sunken road some distance past</p>
