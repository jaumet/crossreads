<p>These are the men who shortly after midnight of Sunday July 23. 1916, took Pozieres by  splendidly dashing advance through shrapnel, shell and machine gun fire</p>
<p>[Following is handwritten on the post card and addressed to Mr.Justice Ferguson]<br/>Thank you for the post cards of Bowen. Made me think of Queensland. As you know Keith is in the 20th. I'm in the 1st but won't be getting over there for a couple of months. These chaps look like the 20th.See their colours. Best wishes to Mrs. Ferguson and Dorothy.<br/>Yours John  R  Mont.</p>
