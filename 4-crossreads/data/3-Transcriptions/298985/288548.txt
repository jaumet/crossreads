<p>Sunday 8th<br/>Fell in &amp; messed about for a considerable time then moved up to the road &amp; got on Motors. Started about 12 traveled through Merecourt Bray &amp; a lot of other places demotored about 2 30 near Mt St Quintine about 2 K, N of Pevone. Were given an old trench to make our dugouts in  Plenty of Hun timber on a railway  Dump near by also Ruberoid so had no trouble</p>
