<p>Continuation of A.A . Barwicks diary of the 1st battalion, A.I.F.<br/>5th March. 1918<br/>Yesterday a 6 inch gun and its carriage capsized into a big drain running alongside the road, she looked a hopeless case but last night a traction engine appeared on the scene &amp; hauled her out.<br/>Gill &amp; I went &amp; heard the "Boomerangs" last night in a Y.M.C.A. a few hundred yards from our Camp, they were very good indeed.<br/>I was to have gone into Bailleul this morning &amp; bring another prisoner out, but Golder wanted to go into the town so I handed the job</p>
