<p>July 1915<br/>Monday 19<br/>Dusty. Received a welcome letter from home. I have to feed the mules today as our driver has gone as brakesman.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20<br/>Dusty. Fed the mules again today. They are stupid animals and require a good deal of patience while being handled. A number of fresh mules arrived tonight to allow the others to have a spell</p>
