
anyway after got on trams and went for rides to the terminus's and saw as much of Aberdeen as possible. and it was a great place. in the afternoon I wired to the commonwealth Bank in London asking if any money had reached there for me from Australia and shortly got a reply back No. So that evening visited the palace theatre and Tivoli afterward stayed at a Sailors home that night a Rough place and go as you please next morning after breakfast went out it was pouring rain and bitter cold and windy had dinner and left Aberdeen for perth and passed through some more very pretty country though this time it was dull and miserable
