<p>aid.  Marched <strike>out</strike> to an old camp about two miles out, for the purpose of bringing in some sandbags to build a dug out.</p>
<p>Wednesday 3/5/16<br/>Practically the whole of the day building a dug out, - hard graft.  Official word received through Intelligence Department stating that a large body of Turks, chiefly Camel Corps, are now about 40 miles to the East &amp; advancing toward where we are camping.  Stretcher squads formed &amp; instructed to be prepared for duty at any time.  Appointed No 4 in charge of a squad.</p>
