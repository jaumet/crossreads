  
 1916 at a standstill owing to the weather.  Home early & learning French.  Bed late.  No expenses to-day 
 5th  Sept.  October - Thursday Drizzling rain & high winds.  Unpleasant day.  Received a letter from our friend Belge.  Legge & I take a walk after tea & return home.  The watch has not returned so to-morrow I shall require payment.  Still awaiting news of departure to Etaples Camp or London.  First news the cold-footed crowd being called up in Australia & aren't we pleased.  Second French war loan advertised.
