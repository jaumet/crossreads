<p>4 December 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Get parcels censored &amp; posted  Prepare for moving off.  Major Wilson leads us. off at 10 a.m.  our old packs never seem to get lighter our blankets etc. are weighty thing &amp; indispensible.  Back to our former possie at Longueval Dressing Station  That's located  now much more comfortable. Coffee  Tea Room at Ledagan. Settle for night.</p>
