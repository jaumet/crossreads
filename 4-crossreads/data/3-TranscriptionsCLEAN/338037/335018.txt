  
 Dear Mother We are working hard loading transports There are lots of French and English soldiers here. I am prettty well. This is a P.C. issued to the French troops for use at the Front. love to Jim and Paddy and yourself. Best love to all, your loving son Arthur  XXXXXXXXXXXXXX There are Zouaves and Turkos here and all sorts of French uniforms. The town is alive with soldiers. My letters may be irregular after this as we do not know when we may move. a lot have gone
