<p>It is rumoured that a large amount of damage was done. Most of the civilians shifted into the surrounding villages.</p>
<p>9/8/17<br/>We marched from Wallon Cappelle to billets near Sec&ndash;Bois and expect to remain here for a few days.</p>
<p>Bombed Hazebrouck last night and are shelling it all the morning.</p>
<p>10/8/17<br/>Awakened last night by our antiaircraft guns firing at "Fritz" planes.</p>
