<p>Friday 16/3/16</p>
<p>More training, inspection by G.O.C. Bde tomorrow.</p>
<p>Saturday 17/3/17</p>
<p>Inspection of Battalion by G.O.C. Very Goof turn out &ndash; played football against 25th &ndash; Meeting of C.O's.</p>
<p>Sunday 18/3/17</p>
<p>Quiet Day generally &ndash; very stiff after Football &ndash; experiments with Bombs. Wrote to Doff &ndash; orders to march forward tomorrow night.</p>
<p>Monday 19/3/17</p>
<p>Moved at 9 am, had mid-day meal at The BUTTE moved to BAPAUME and billeted for night, reconnoitre towards VAULX.</p>
