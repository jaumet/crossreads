Saturday March 10. 1917It is not so cold today and the weather is much more pleasant.  The ground is getting soft again.  There are no newspapers today.  An English doctor visited this ward this afternoon on a tour of inspection.  This ward is built of wood with an iron roof and the ceiling and sides are lined with soft wood.
