<p>Aug 16th &ndash; 18<br/>Left Villers Brettoneaux at 10 this morning and moved to next villiage Corbie. Here we are out for 3 days spell before returning for another big stunt</p>
<p>Here we are billeted in an old castle which is evidently associated with history dated as far back as 1659. The building contains a great number of rooms and passages and it quite an easy matter to get lost</p>
