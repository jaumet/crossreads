Some of the officers were round tonight seeing how many volunteers they could get for a raiding party
4th. This morning the Coy. was "fell in" volunteers called for this raiding party they got 28 from the Coy. including my humble self. but they turned nearly everyone down only took 2 from us they had such a crowd to select from they generally take those men who have not seen much service.
A furious bombardment broke out early last night lasted fully a couple of hours I cannot describe the terrific thunderous roar of our terrible artillery the night seemed full of roaring rushing shells as they sped overhead on their destructive career, just close to our huts there is a battery of 9.2 everytime these
