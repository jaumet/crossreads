<p>Wed Sep 6th &ndash; 16<br/>All last night and today Fritz has been sending over a new kind of trench mortar, the Minnewerfers.  They resemble an oil drum only a shade smaller.</p>
<p>Fri Sep 8th &ndash; 16<br/>last night proved very exciting for us as an oversupply of "Wafers" fell too close. One just missing our dug out but close enough to shake everybody &amp; everything even to delaying our cup of tea as the dixie was knocked off the primus just as the water was boiling.</p>
