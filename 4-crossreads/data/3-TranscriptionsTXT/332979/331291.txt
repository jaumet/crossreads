The Slow Boats of our Convoy made away in another direction &amp; the Walmer Castle slowed down &amp; put her nose to the oncoming Steamer, &amp; we did the same. The Cruiser ordered the stranger to stop &amp; show her Signals at the same time turned her stern Gun on to her. After Signals were exchanged she was allowed to proceed on her journey. It was very amusing to see the movement of the Convoy in a scare. The Cruiser acts like a Hen with her Chickens, rushing away when there is any likely danger. After a while the cruiser Signalled to all the boats &amp; we soon were into line again. After the usual Cards &amp; Draughts all hands turned in to bed in good time.
