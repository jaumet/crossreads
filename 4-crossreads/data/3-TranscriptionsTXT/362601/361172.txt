 12 March, Wednesday 
 F. We sit up until 1 am talking about Everything &amp; all. We go to Ramsey &amp; buy some things &amp; I hurry M. Home. It is just lovely this day to be HOME. 
 13 March, Thursday 
 F. Ellis hears of his acceptance of Early Repatriation form. Auntie, M &amp; I go for ride to old Ballaugh Church &amp; the Sun Dial. We come Home for Music &amp; we stay in front room all night.
