 1915 Sep. 24 Fri. Inoculated against cholera. An N.C.O. &amp; 22 men sent from No. 2. G.H. to reinforce at Gallipoli. Feels strange being back at Cairo again &ndash; tucker pretty rotten &ndash; Montazah every time. Weather much cooler now &amp; shower cold first thing in the morning. Days shorter &ndash; Winter coming.
