<p>Saturday 12 May 1917.</p>
<p>2 more transports came up with troops during day and anchored close by. A rough cold day.</p>
<p>Mess orderly duty as usual today Getting into the way of things now. Fellows taking more to the life of the ship. <s>No onee seasick yet.</s> off 5.15 pm. A destoyer <s>&quot;a real beauty too&quot;</s> The &quot;Warrego&quot;. came in the bay, &amp; cut the pace out &amp; lying low in the water. Signalling going on everywhere between boats. 5.20 pm <s>6.0 pm.</s> weighed anchor &amp; 6.0 pm all the transports started to move slowly towards &quot;heads&quot;. All going on deck to watch us going through the heads shortly. 6 35 pm through the heads. Boat rolling now in open sea. A beautiful sight with searchlights playing between the heads.</p>
