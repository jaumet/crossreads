<p>Thursday 30 September 1915&nbsp;<br />About Hospital all day&nbsp;<br />Captain Bourke left hospital, as he was ordered for Alexandria.&nbsp;<br />He was insulting when going away.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Friday 1 October 1915&nbsp;<br />Notification received that I was to hand over hospital to Captain Bourke &amp; that I was to go to Alexandria on Monday the 4<sup>th</sup> inst.&nbsp;<br />General Ford called with Major Bagshaw. Had a talk with the General&nbsp;</p>
<p>Saturday 2&nbsp;October 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo during morning. Called on General Ford.&nbsp;He took me to General Maxwell.Had&nbsp;brief chat with the latter.&nbsp;<br />Settled up accounts.&nbsp;<br />Handed over to Captain Bourke at 1 pm.&nbsp;<br />At Confession in Heliopolis at 8 p.m.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sunday 3&nbsp;October 1915&nbsp;<br />Rosary Sunday. Mollie&nbsp;<br />Communion 7 am at Bassilica Heliopolis.&nbsp;<br />Packing all day.&nbsp;<br />At Zeitoun Convt between 6-30 &amp; 8-30 pm.&nbsp;<br />Clearing up everything.&nbsp;</p>
