<p>This has been an exciting week again. Poor old Fred came in Monday to say he was leaving on Wednesday. So I had dinner at The Club with him that night. We did it in great style. He got a car from somewhere, so we had a little spin before dinner. It was my long day too. So I hurried up &amp;  got dressed  &amp; we left the Home at 8. p.m.  Then had dinner at 8.30 p.m. finished about 9.15. p.m.  &amp; then for another</p>
