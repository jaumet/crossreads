 THURSDAY, OCTOBER 23, 1919. 
 Fine. Tickle &amp; I put the Mountain 
 mill together again. We got her 
 all ready to connect up. Pryor came 
 out &amp; got a start on building the 
 other tower in Middle paddock. 
 Paid Darby off in the morning &amp; 
 he has gone. 
 Alan went down to Sydney by 
 the night train.
