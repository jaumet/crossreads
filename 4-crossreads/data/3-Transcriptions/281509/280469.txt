<p><br/>3 September 1916<br/>Sunday <br/>Quiet day. Reading "Barlech of Guard". Mess orderly for day. After tea C &amp; self &amp; Mac go across to Canadian Hosp. to English service. Two parsons, 3 nurses. High Church. Swift exit of parsons after conclusion of service Y M. C.A. tent. Speaker &amp; good teeth. Good singing</p>
