 Monday 4th June 191 6  7 
 A lovely day warm with a breeze - I am feeling very tired somehow or another. 
 Conference (at 5.30pm) of Group &amp; Bn. Bombing Officers at Group Hqrs which I attended. Played cards after attending a good concert given by R.G.A club. 
 Tuesday 5th June - Conducted bombing all day. Attended Lewis Machine Lecture in Evening after which Smith &amp; myself went to Y.M.C.A. to Conjuring performance. 
 Entry  Thursday 7th  - Wrote to George &amp; Reg last night &amp; also wrote several postcards to Jess &amp; the &quot;littlies&quot;.
