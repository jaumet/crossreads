<p>But enough of disagreeable things.  I would just Love Auntie &amp; Marie &amp; Uncle Tom to come out to Australia.  I can laugh &amp; joke about the fun we've had together.  Auntie is a bit like yourself &amp; I say the same things to Her as I would say to you.  I feel as if I have always known Her.  I have been over 4 times now having spent altogether about 16 days with Her.  I know all the ins &amp; outs of the place &amp; I really believe I know more about some things in the House than Auntie.  I have had one game of Hockey since I returned &amp; have been writing for dear life ever since.  I only have one letter to write after this &amp; my mail will be complete.  Up to date I have a record of 1030 letters that I have written.  Many more I have perhaps forgotten to put in my little diary.  Roy &amp; Jack, George Millar &amp; Billy also Reg. Keirle I often Hear from &amp; they are all doing well.  I was sorry to hear</p>
