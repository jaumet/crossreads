<p>[Friday  January 7 1916]</p>
<p>Captain of the day today so I have little time to self day or night.<br/>Cold nights beautiful sharp days &ndash; plenty of good tucker &amp; no SHELLS.<br/>Drilling each day on the Desert like long ago but many familiar faces are missing-</p>
