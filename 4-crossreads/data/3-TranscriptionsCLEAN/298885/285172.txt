  
 Oct 14th - 17 Dad's Birthday. Met my brother Stan for the first time in France. Also met Harold & Norrie 
 Oct 27th - 17 After a month of uncertainty as to whether one of us would get a "Blighty" as a result of bombs we came into action at Ypres and are once more doing the "rabbit-act" living in the bowels of terra-firma.
