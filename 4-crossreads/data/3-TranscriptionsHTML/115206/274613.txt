<p>February 1918<br/>Tuesday 12</p>
<p>Messines<br/>On F.O.P, today with Spence and Jones. Spence remained with me till 4.30 pm. Fritz shell 54 Battalion's line and Rifle Farm to the extent of 180, 5.9. <br/>A good chap Spence who is being spoiled by "booze".<br/>Weather unsettled and visibility at times &ndash; good. <br/>I have found a dog, a "foxic" who is a terror on rats. He has been all day with me at F.O.P.<br/>Johns has taken up his abode of love in our domicile.</p>
