
Left Albert for Doignes on the right of Baupaume, with all the horses, the bty, had moved from this Urvillers to this position. The same old work went on here when one gun was knocked out of action another was pulled in.  We were shelled out of the horse lines one day with long range shells. We hear we are leaving here to go out for a spell & become mobile artillery. Left Doignes & went through Baupaume to Albert. camped for night next day went to Baveincourt & stayed a fort-night, after cleaning up all harnes, horses
