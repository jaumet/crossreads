<p>I.B. Res. Brig.<br/>Forest Row</p>
<p>1 Oct. 1917</p>
<p>Dear Mrs Jack,</p>
<p>I am afraid I haven't written since Aug. the 6th.  I have been saving all letter writing for when I get on the ship.  As I expect you have heard before this that I was one of the lucky ones detailed for India.</p>
<p>I have now been waiting 6 weeks &amp; have heard nothing so far, been at this spot two weeks as I have 140 men to pick up here to take out with me.  There is some talk of a P &amp; O going this week taking passengers &amp; troops so may be on her.</p>
