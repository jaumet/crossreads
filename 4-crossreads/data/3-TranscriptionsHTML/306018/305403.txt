<p>February 1917<br/>were our Back line.  Got a letter from Arthur Blomfield &amp; one from Bob Hill.</p>
<p>Sunday 18th<br/>Church parade in the forenoon.  Played football in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Monday 19th<br/>Mounted parade in the morning.  Got some training in judging distances, fire direction, control etc.  In the afternoon was sent over to the Camel Transport for Instruction.  Found things fairly easy with plenty of food &amp; fodder for the horse</p>
<p>Tuesday 20th<br/>Went with the camel train to El Arish for the Brigade supplies.  Got back about 3 in the afternoon.  Met Lieut Lockingtons  He is in the N Z Engineers</p>
<p>Wednesday 21st<br/>Took the camels to El Arish &amp; had a fairly busy day as we had to take loading in.  Was busy packing up at night ready for a move in the morning.</p>
