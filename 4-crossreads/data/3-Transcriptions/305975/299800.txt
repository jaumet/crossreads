<p>went to wins Place for dinner a long way to go too  went to victoria barraks 2.P.M. all rush there  they are shoving one another like wild animals.  got away a 5.15.P.M  went to station and caught 10 to 6 train for home  arrived ingleburn 7.P.M a few People there but they were done in Because I never sent them word I was coming</p>
