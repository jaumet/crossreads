  
 Sutton Veny (WILTS) (Near)-  WARMINSTER.  
 [21 Th] Discharged from hospital. In charge of two other patients. When we got to Paddington stn (London) we desided to have another day in London, before going into camp (Eight week in Southall) 
 [22 Fri] Met the other boys at Paddington stn at 11-45am  Left there by the 12-15pm train & arr. Warminster 3 pm. Took motor to No.8. Camp No.1. Command depot Sutton Very Wilts (13 Hut) 
 [23 Sat] Up before Dentist & Doctor for Classification
