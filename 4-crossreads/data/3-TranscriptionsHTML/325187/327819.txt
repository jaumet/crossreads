<p>[Tuesday 14 March 1916]</p>
<p>On Patrol I took my gun &amp; shot<br/>3 Widgeon 3 Jack Snipe 4 Quail 3 Plover and 1 snake-  With a game of poker or Bridge &amp; a whisky before turning in life is very good at present, &amp; not half as strenuous as it should be of course there are always possibilities of a scrap.<br/><strike>Great gambling going on among the men as there is no way for them to spend their money.</strike><br/>Hear that the German fleet is out<br/>No word of any Xmas Billies for the 1st L H who were at ANZAC the men feel rather disappointed.</p>
