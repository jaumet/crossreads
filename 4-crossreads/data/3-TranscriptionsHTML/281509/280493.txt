<p>27 September 1916<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Usual duties, leisurely done with frequent spells. Cart, Bob &amp; self go for walk on country road. rather pretty &amp; picturesque. Call in at a couple of estaminets. Hot milk &amp; biscuits &amp; tinned prunes (home parcel.)  over to Canadian Hosp to weeknight service. Lecture by Capt. &ndash; from Winnipeg. not much chop. Coffee refreshments &amp; song. Bill P. perturbed on acc. of being away from billet after 9 p.m.</p>
