22/8/15
Stayed in camp all day
23/8/15
Mitchell returned by Sig. Dec. DHQ as unsuitable most pleased. Busy - hear we are off on Friday. To HQ to see Field Cashier with other Adjutants. They are very comfortable there all white building. From window some idea obtainable of native life, women washing and general backdoor scenery of the better class civilian native.
To 24th After very hot walk. Two iced lagers!! Evening Platoon work
In Camp
24/8/15 Battalion drill. Came into office. Busy preparing for departure all sorts of furpheys going round but apparently Friday the most popular day. Krozier finished painting forwarded it on to Melbne. A beastly dusty day with hot wind ? the worst since leaving Broadmeadows. Everything filthy
