 20th Dec. (Monday) Making an underground sandbag dugout. Tomorrow I believe we move further out to occupy a more advanced ridge. The trees lining the Canal are visible in the background but the Bitter Lakes further down may be clearly seen. On our way out we passed camel bones &amp; Turkish graves, indicating the scene of battle in Feb 1915. Thousands of Turks we killed here.
