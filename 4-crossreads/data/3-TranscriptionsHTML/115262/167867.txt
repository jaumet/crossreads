<p>Tuesday 10 April 1917.</p>
<p>Up at 5 a.m. 6.14 train from Burnley to City. 7 am troop train to Seyr. from Spencer St.</p>
<p>Arrived camp at 12 noon.</p>
<p>Signalling in afternoon.</p>
<p>Detailed for Camp Hd Qrs fatigue tomorrow. Wrote letters &amp; back to lines. Bed at 9 pm.</p>
