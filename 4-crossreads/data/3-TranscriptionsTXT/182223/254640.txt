 Thursday 25 February 1915 Wrote Delargie &amp; Buckley &amp; Hopenden. Mat. Harris.  T  he Girls  General Williams. Mrs&nbsp;&nbsp;M. M. Knowles.&nbsp; General work. Emptying the hospital, preparatory to shifting Camp to some other part of the world.&nbsp; Rode on a Camel to the Sphynx &amp; back&nbsp;8. P.T. [8 Piastres] 
 Friday 26&nbsp;February 1915 Posted letters to the Girls. 1&frac12; P.T. stamps. Day as the other days. All my patients can leave the hospital at any moment except one Empyema.&nbsp; Letter from Dr Harris, &amp; New Year Card from A.F.N.A people of Syd.&nbsp; Dinner at 8 p. m. with Dr Adam Scheuber, a native of Riga, Russia - sharia-el-Genaineh. An educated gentleman. Enjoyed the dinner immensley. the fowl&nbsp;&amp; parsley sauce, &amp; the peche Melba [indecipherable]. home at 1 a.m. 27-2-15. 
 Saturday 27&nbsp;February 1915 Wrote to Dr H. L.Harris. Wimereux&nbsp;France.&nbsp; Wrote Mrs Fraser.&nbsp;Thorn Bank, Cedar Hill, Newport, Isle of Wight.&nbsp; Dined at The Chalet on top of Sand hill above Mena House with Dr Scheuber 
 Sunday 28&nbsp;February 1915 Mass at 9 a.m. At Museum of Egyptian Antiquities during the afternoon. Beastly sick. Recovered about midnight.&nbsp; Wrote Geo Reid, Dr Arthur, Dr Paton, Mrs Huxley, Roger Arnott, Clarrie Bridge, Dr Fox,&nbsp;J. Roach, and The girls.
