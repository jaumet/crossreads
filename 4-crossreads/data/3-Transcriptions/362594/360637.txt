<p>[Janvier 19 Dimanche]<br/>Went to Orchestra Symphony Concert at the Alhambra Theatre.  Heard a most beautifull piece played.  Dance des Heures by Ponchieli.</p>
<p>[20 Lundi]<br/>Getting on well.  It is touch &amp; go whether this place closes down before I finish, but I hope to just do it.  Flying all I can.</p>
