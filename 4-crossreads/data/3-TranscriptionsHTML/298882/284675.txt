<p>Chateau Wood.  Sun 23rd 4pm</p>
<p>Wonderful sights seen in the air.  Fleets of aeroplanes, banking climbing, doing spinning nose dives and various other antics.</p>
<p>Flies are very plentiful.  Big sleek blue chaps.  So pretty, frightfully pretty.</p>
