
Thursday 25/1/17have put in an application for leave to Paris - work finished.
Friday 26/1/17
Leave for Paris for 7 days approved - General report on Training.
Saturday 27/1/17
Men marched out at 10 am. I remain & had Luncheon with M Domart who drove me almost to Amiens. Arrived Paris 8.30 pm had dinner & went to Follies Bergere.
