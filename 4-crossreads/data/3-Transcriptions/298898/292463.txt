<p>Tuesday 6th <strike>Feb.</strike><br/>Great activity in the air today several encounters taking place. In one which I watched the Hun was forced to descend in his own lines. Went for a ride during the afternoon about 15 miles. Received by this mail Mums photo which gave me a great shock. It looks like a person years older &amp; much saddened by great worry. 'Tis really a good photo but I cannot get the sad look out of my mind, I only hope the war will end soon that I can get home &amp; try &amp; alter it. Dads photo was just what I expected, practically no difference at all.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7th <strike>Feb.</strike><br/>Had a few cases through otherwise very quiet. Strong wind &amp; bitterly cold all day, managed to write a few letters.</p>
