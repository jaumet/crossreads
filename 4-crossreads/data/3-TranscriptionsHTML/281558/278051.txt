<p>Wednesday Oct 17th</p>
<p>Westham : wet : fell in for roll call before &amp; after dinner : walked to Weymouth after tea &amp; bought Xmas cards &amp; also got my films &amp; prints : not too bad</p>
<p>Thursday Oct 18th</p>
<p>Westham : better day : fell in for roll call before &amp; after dinner : sent a Xmas card to all the family, all at Lithgow, Muir, Moyle, &amp; Aunt Myra &amp; Grandma : Ern Beale is over on leave &amp; looked me up : the "boys" have had a bad time &amp; the names of those who are dead shocked me : went to his peoples place &amp; had a pleasant talk</p>
<p>Friday Oct 19th</p>
<p>Westham : I am M.O. today : Ern came along after tea so we went for a stroll &amp; got my films &amp;</p>
