<p>Somewhere in France<br/>1st July 1917<br/>Dear Bee<br/>&amp;Our children</p>
<p>This daddy is off to the front any day now, but dont worry for all will be well</p>
<p>We hope for a fine victory.</p>
<p>And wish for Peace</p>
<p>Your loving Geoff and Dad</p>
