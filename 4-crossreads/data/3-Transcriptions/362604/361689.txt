<p>Poor Mrs. Ali is going mad I think.  She cannot have all Her senses about Her.  I don't suppose she can help it, so you had better regard Her as a dangerous lunatic &amp; leave Her well alone.  I am glad that you are so friendly with Dave's people.  He is a second Brother of ours.  We will always be the same &amp; with Otto, Dave &amp; our present chum Dick Toppin we have three true &amp; trusty life long Brothers.  Did you ever meet Mrs. Harper at your comforts show.
