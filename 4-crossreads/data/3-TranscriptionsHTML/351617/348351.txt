<p>It was while here that we were issued with our winter Issue from the Comforts Fund 1 sheep skin Vest; 1 pr. Gloves, 1 pr. Sheep skin Mittons, 1 Cap Comforter &amp; Muffler, 2 prs. of Socks. It was now fearfully cold and the slightest breeze cut right through one to the bone. While here I took all the spare clothes they could give us and although they were extra weight, I could see what the winter was going to be and I was not going to get frozen if I could help it.</p>
