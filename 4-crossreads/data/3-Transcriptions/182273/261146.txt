
<p>July &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>14 SUN &nbsp;Cool. raining on &amp;</p>
<p>off all day</p>
<p>Amm. going up. very quiet.</p>
<p>Posted letters to Peggy Gladys</p>
<p>&amp; Celie. Writing at night</p>
<p>Raining hard.</p>
<p>15 MON &nbsp;Fine day. Cool, very</p>
<p>quiet. Sgt Clarke went to O.T.C.</p>
<p>Rec. letter from Julie for Martin</p>
<p>Posted S. Card to Lily. E Wilson</p>
<p>Swim at night. Heavy storm.</p>
<p>16 TUES &nbsp;Fine day. Very warm</p>
<p>Rec: letter from Amy. Posted</p>
<p>letters to W.T.B. &amp; Miss G. Taylor.</p>
<p>Very quiet day &amp; night.</p>
<p>Swim. Sgt Magee went to Rest</p>
<p>Camp</p>
<p>17 WED &nbsp;Fine day. Warm.</p>
<p>Helped officers with dinner.</p>
<p>Swim at night.</p>
<p>Amm. going up. Sgt Palmes</p>
<p>went for gun. Heard of new</p>
<p>&#39;Hun&#39; offensive.</p>
