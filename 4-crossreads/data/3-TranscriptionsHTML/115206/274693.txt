<p>May 1918<br/>Friday 3</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daours<br/>Had a run to F.H.B. at Bonnay. Saw three Fritz's dropped near P.O.W. camp, from La Neuvelle.<br/>Received several letters,two from cousin Mary and one from Jack Harvey, also several others including one from Geo Drury.<br/>Received also, a letter from Adrienne.<br/>Paid Ken Gunn 15.  Rect   Alex Mouab, at battery baggage lines had an agreeable talk. He is in in 24th A.S.C. 3rd divvy.<br/>Weather glorious.</p>
