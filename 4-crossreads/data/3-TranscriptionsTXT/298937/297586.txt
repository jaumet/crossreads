Went to Bases Stores at Heliopolis in response to a telephone message from Taff Jones &amp; there found them preparing to leave for England. Taff tried to see Major Buchanan so that Molloy &amp; I could be attached to them &amp; then get to England. Taff missed the Major so matter is in abeyance.
