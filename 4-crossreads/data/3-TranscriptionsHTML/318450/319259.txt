<p>There is nothing to see around Wareham, so we had to go out a few miles to amuse ourselves.  About 3&frac12; miles from Wareham there is a very ancient castle which we read about in the history book's at school, and that was Corfe Castle, the castle the Roman soldiers could never take.</p>
<p>This castle dates back many and many years, but to day it is laying in ruins, being destroyed by act of parliment somewhere in the 17th century, for what reason I do not know.</p>
