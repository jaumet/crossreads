 Diary of Camp Work. 
 [The following entries for 1st and 2nd August crossed through.] 
 Aug. 1st Arrived home from all night manoeuvre about 2nd Tower.  Rested all day, letter writing evening. 
 Aug. 2nd Lost a/c from 27/6/16 to Aug. 11th. 
 August 11th Reveille 4 a.m.  Marched through to Citadel, stayed all day, left 5 p.m., arriving Heliopolis 8-45 p.m. 
 Aug. 12 Bayonet sharpened.
