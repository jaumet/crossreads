He like Galleo cares for none of these things & thus continues to make his pitiful little clamour & cannot realise how diminutive & of little account he appears. I have just caught a flea which has mercifully stopped me wasting valuable paper on subjects which a common trooper is not supposed to realise much less to appreciate.  His place is with the fleas in the dust and - under the dust. 
 Monday 11th 9 p.m. A tremendous bombardment & fusillade of rifle fire has just broken out.  It sounds as if we or the Turks have attacked or raided or something.  I suppose we shall hear what it was about tomorrow.
