<p>Wednesday 3 April<br/>Up at 6 am with nothing to do.</p>
<p>Met Korah who has been hung up here. Had bath, &amp; strolled about camp. Changed to tents. Afternoon parade &amp; on fatigue to docks. Had good look around.</p>
<p>Evening walk about Calais shopping.</p>
