  
 Wednesday 24/1/17 same to day, frost on the snow five minutes in the air outside & you dont know if you have any hands or feet yesterday & to day sun shining but not enough power to melt the snow, wake up in the night shivering two Blankets & overcoat on all the same Pay 2.3-9. 19.17-0. Getting near to my trip now. The Australian Army has at last started to economise Boots, Tunics, sox, shirts, Boots etc are to be gathered up in future & will be allowed for to the Platoon that brings them in 2/6 for Tunic, & so on down to sox 6d Terrific Bombardment been going on these last 3 or 4 days also Air fights 2 huns brought down yesterday. Having fairly good time here this time, duty 2 hours a day. Canteen would be alright if not so bitterly cold. 
 Thursday 25/1/17 Fine & cold snow still on the ground frozen 3 more days, then for Blighty. Cant sleep for chats & the cold. have to get ice & melt in over the fire, no water, all the mains been frozen for four days. Pay 2.4.9. 19-18.8. huns very busy with their Taubes these last 2 days. Consequence they are shelling us only a little way from here. 11" at that. Got about 12 Tommies yesterday working near our fellows. No word from Viv he may have gone to Blighty. 
 Friday 26/1/17 same as yesterday frost more severe if any thing two more days, how they creep on. Pay 2-5-9. 19-19-0. 3 P.M. wind blowing hard & snow flying off the ground everything freezing have to cover up your ears or get them bitten off. On duty tonight 2 hours 6 to 8 it will be lovely. How I wish Tomorrow was Sunday.
