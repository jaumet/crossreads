<p>Sunday 18 June 1916<br/>Arrived Alexandria about 6 a.m. &amp; went aboard the Kinfauns" [King Janus?] &amp; remained in Harbor all day.</p>
<p>Monday 19 June 1916<br/>Lying in Harbor all day.  The "Georgina" with 14th &amp; 15th F. Coy. Drivers &amp; Horses aboard got away about 6.30 p.m.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20 June 1916<br/>Lying in Harbor all day.  Weather very hot.  Do not expect to leave for several days for some reason or other.</p>
<p>Wednesday 21 June 1916<br/>Lying in Harbor all day.  A few Transports left.  Yachting in Harbor in afternoon, very pretty.</p>
<p>Thursday 22 June 1916<br/>Left Alexandria at about 10.30 a.m.  Had very good trip during day.  Sea nice &amp; smooth.</p>
<p>Friday 23 June 1916<br/>Still making very good progress.  Weather calm &amp; cooler than in Egypt.</p>
<p>Saturday 24 June 1916<br/>Said to have sighted submarine about noon.  Still making good trip.  Weather rather good.</p>
<p>Sunday 25 June 1916<br/>Altered course for some reason about daybreak &amp; ran into Malta, ship here all day.</p>
<p>Monday 26 June 1916<br/>Left Malta about 6.30 p.m. with "Caladonia" &amp; 2 other Transports.  Escorted by destroyers &amp; cruiser.</p>
<p>Tuesday 27 June 1916<br/>Making good progress.  Sighted African coast in morning.  Travelling along Sardinian Coast in evening.</p>
<p>Wednesday 28 June 1916<br/>Made good passage.  Arrived Marseilles about 8 p.m.  Anchored in outer for the night.</p>
<p>Thursday 29 June 1916<br/>Left Marseille port about 9 p.m.  Left Kingfauns [Kinfauns?] Castle about 3 p.m.  Every thing going well.</p>
<p>Friday 30 June 1916<br/>Making good journey.  Country extremely beautiful along the Valley on the Rhone from Mornas to Lyon, also right along.</p>
<p>Saturday 1 July 1916<br/>Making good trip.  Nice weather &amp; everything looking beautiful.</p>
