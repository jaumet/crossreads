  
 Sept. 4th Last day in Havre. - Meet Bert Fergusson & Charlie Bourne - Entrain at 4.30 p.m. at Gare De la Merchandese - A long journey in cattle trucks. Disappearance of our Comedian (H.Shaw.) 
 Sept. 5th Arrive and disentrain at Corbie (8 a.m.) - In the afternoon we are billeted in the ruins of a "school" (L'Ecole Communale) and its attached dwelling place - Our Hall (?) well ventila-
