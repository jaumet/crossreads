<p>Thursday 25 October 1917.</p>
<p>Awaiting in case of an enemy attack at &quot;Stand to&quot;</p>
<p><s>On watering</s> Watered camels.</p>
<p>Saw families of Bedouins with their flocks and herds watering at the Waddy. A picturesque sight undoubtedly.</p>
<p>A mail as late as Sept 3<sup>rd</sup> to hand. 8 letters I received.</p>
<p><s>Malgo</s> Maalaga was the watering place.</p>
<p>Wrote Home to Joy.</p>
