<p>Thursday 27 July 1916<br/>Trench digging again in morning.<br/>Lecture &amp; bomb throwing in evening.<br/>Had to parade in evening and go to station to unload horses and bring to camp.</p>
<p>Friday 28 July 1916<br/>Bomb throwing.  Lecture &amp; trench attacks.  On guard for 1 hour &amp; a half in evening as Infantry were rioting prior to leaving for France.  Voulenteered for Machine Gun section.</p>
