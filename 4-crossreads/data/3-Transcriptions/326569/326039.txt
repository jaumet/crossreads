<p>Doctor gave me some tablets but not much good.  Have to see him again in morning.</p>
<p>Friday, 13/4/17<br/>Saw doctor in morning &amp; sent to Fovant Hospital in ward 5.  Feeling very off &ndash; case diagnosed as influenza.  Temperature in morning 101 &amp; in afternoon 100.  Receiving good food.</p>
<p>Saturday, 14/4/17<br/>Did not sleep too well, feeling feverish &amp; very restless.  Temp. morning 100 &amp; afternoon 101.2.</p>
<p>Sunday, 15/4/17<br/>Had another fairly bad night, but got in good sleep after 5 a.m.  Temperature normal.</p>
<p>Monday, 16/4/17<br/>Slept better last night &amp; throat feeling much better.  Case marked Tonsilitis.</p>
