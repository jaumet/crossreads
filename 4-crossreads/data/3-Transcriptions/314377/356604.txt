<p>Now I come to the last day of my leave. Mr. Philpott with a friend of his a Mr. Skeet came and said Good-bye to me Mrs. Blechingden had been knitting a scarf. Mrs. Philpott also did not forget the "miner man" but provided me with a packet of sandwiches.</p>
<p>I spent that night at the War Chest Club after getting all my pack fixed up. Planes were over that night Cries of "take cover." Gases were fired</p>
<p>Left Victoria Station early in the morning eventually reaching Dover after midday. The climate of England is mostly grey This day was not exceptional Dover is a very old town surrounded by cliffs.</p>
