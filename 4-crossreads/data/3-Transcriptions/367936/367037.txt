<p>Saturday 9 September 1916<br/>Had rifle exercises all morning and afternoon.<br/>Got six letters, one from home August 3rd.<br/>Other chaps went to Port Said on leave.</p>
<p>Sunday 10 September 1916<br/>Up early and on Camel Escort from Railway up to here.<br/>Got some papers from home.<br/>Lot of sand blowing about in afternoon.<br/>On picquet at night and could not go to Church Parade.<br/>Wrote to Father (16).</p>
