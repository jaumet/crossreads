<p>Salonica, the Imperial Yeomanry Division.&nbsp; There has been a considerable amount of sickness amongst our men lately &amp; a great number of our horses are weak &amp; in very poor condition &amp; badly need a spell.</p>
<p><u>Sept 18th 17</u>:&nbsp; The Anzac Division moced out to the Second Line of defence today our month&#39;s rest on the beach having expired.&nbsp; We relieved the Australian Division, which moved out to the Front Line to relieve the Yeomanry Division which moves into the beach</p>
