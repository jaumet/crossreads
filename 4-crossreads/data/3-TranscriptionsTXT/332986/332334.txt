 8-2-1918 Wet and miserable. As to be worn, Bty order. One N.C.O. and one man to go to Australia on 6 months leave. Driver Dawes nominated from this Bty. Later, Dawes gone to Australia. 
 9-2-1918 Cold and windy. Football match. Half holiday Our Major and officers playing. General Rosenthal inspecting 
 10-2-1918 Sunday, nothing doing. Lecture for junior N.C.O. tonight. Weather windy and cold 
 11-2-1918 Our Bty S.M. (Crittenden) assaulted last night. Three men under arrest in guard tent. Real wasters. All this disturbance was caused by drink, In almost every farm house , beer is sold. Named bock, 11/2 Francs per bottle. Col Watson inspecting our horses
