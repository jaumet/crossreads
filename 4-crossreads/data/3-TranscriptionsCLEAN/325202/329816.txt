 Aug 10 After 2 days at Bonneville we moved back 4 miles to a fresh 'Bivvy' handy to water at Pernois & have been here since. 
 The Division is on its way in again. The Infantry Brigade passed out yesterday & the Divisional Troops expect to move out today.
