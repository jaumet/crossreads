<p>Pentaloupe a French penal settlement.  650 patients on board, 7 doctors &amp; 80 on the staff.  French battleship passed us.  It was very hot during the evening &amp; the ship was put about to try &amp; get a breeze.</p>
<p>Friday 25th June</p>
<p>Up to now sea has been dead calm but this morning a breeze has sprung up &amp; it is nice &amp; fresh.  At breakfast time 2 submarines &amp; a destroyer passed us.</p>
<p>Saturday 26th June</p>
<p>Land visible on European side.  Sea dead calm.  Very pleasant.  We hugged the coast of Spain.  Saw the snow capped Sierra</p>
