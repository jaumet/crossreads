
393New Zealand HospitalWalton on Thames5.9.15
Dear Aunt IsabelVery many  thanks for asking me to stay at your house last weekend - I enjoyed  my stay immensely, but sorry you weren't there - I have been looking forward so much to meeting you and hope I shall have the opportunitybefore long - The specialist came up and examined my eye. He is going to  operate and says when he has finished with me I will be able to pass the army test so am very cheered up - This is a glorious place - I go on out on the riverevery day and New Zealand Sisters and nurses wait on us hand and foot - I was awfully pleased to see Grandmere and got  a great surprise to see her look so wonderfully well - but then she is a wonderful person and looksmuch the same as she did years ago when she came to Sydney and used
