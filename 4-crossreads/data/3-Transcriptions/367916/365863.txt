<p>The men were not up to the same standard but still were very good. There is a concert here every Tuesday night but compared to this party all those I have seem so far have been very "dud".</p>
<p>I am keeping well and fit in spite of the heat, but am getting rather fed up with foot slogging. I want to get back onto a "bus" again and I'm afraid I'll have forgotten how to waggle a joystick if they don't let me get on to it again soon.</p>
