<p>[Thursday 24 August 1916]</p>
<p><strike>Parcels from Dotty &amp; Dungalear</strike><br/>Used a blanket for the 1st time last night-<br/>Just heard I am to leave here on Friday 25th catch Hospital Boat for England will take about a fortnight as boat very slow.  I go into a Hospital in England &amp; get leave from there.<br/>Feeling very "bucked" about it.<br/>Send you this now as may not get chance later.<br/><strike>Love to all</strike> &ndash; Snap enclosed.</p>
