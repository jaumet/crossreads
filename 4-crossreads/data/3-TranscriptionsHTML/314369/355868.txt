<p>3 August 1915<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>We settle down to live peacefully in our new home<br/>No fatigues to speak of   &amp;  we wonder what it means<br/>The Turkish Artillery has been very busy again consistently shelling Anzac beach   &amp;  the Gullies</p>
<p>1915 August 4<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>get orders to see every man has his equipment complete  &amp; Iron rations.<br/>so expect the move to be made soon<br/>Several officers from the native Indian Regts have been inspecting our gully so we expect the Indian Troops will take part in the new move</p>
