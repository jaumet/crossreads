<p>Sunday<br/>July 23 about half a mile out, at first (being painted white) I almost mistook her for a Hospital Boat. Weather close &amp; muggy. Not feeling too good. Left for Weymouth at 3.35, reached Bristol at 4.15; left again at 6.15 &amp; arrived home at 10.10. Came by car from the Station.</p>
<p>24 3 letters waiting for me, &amp; perhaps a Board. A lot more chaps here now. Back on the old job Rec'd 44 letters &ndash; nearly all from Australia. Had a nice hot &amp; then cold shower, &amp; am feeling A1. Just been to the Salvo's &amp; had [in margin] M</p>
