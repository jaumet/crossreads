<p>Friday 7<br/>The best day of the trip a real Aus spring day I felt kind of homesick when I thought of a spring day at Carnsdale<br/>Saturday 8<br/>The journey almost over we are now in the Gulf of Suez. Good Luck I am not sorry<br/>The end of a perfect trip<br/>Sunday 9<br/>Disembarking today after a very pleasent voyage, farewell Star of England A15<br/>Sat 8th When this far from Suez on the voyage home, I will get down on my knees &amp; PRAY</p>
