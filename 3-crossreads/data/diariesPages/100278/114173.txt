29th Everything half frozen this morning but so far I have not noticed the cold so much for it has been fairly dry.
Usual morning parade the worst trouble drilling this weather is the cold on your hands, they turn almost blue.
A lot British artillery passed through here this afternoon and nearly all new stuff too.
Route march again this afternoon and it resulted in a great argument of a challenge which led to a drilling competition, it happened this way, Capt Jacobs was taking us out and he marches too fast altogether about 140 to the minute, after we had gone a mile or so the boys began to flag and drop behind and soon the whole Coy was strung out in a most disgraceful way but still
