Windmills are still largely used for power one not far from here is over 200 years old and is still working.  In many respects, the people are miles &amp; miles behind the times.  The girls still use the wooden yolk across their shoulders for carrying water and use the flail for threshing.  One seldom sees a two-storied building, and in all cases the colour scheme is white-washed
