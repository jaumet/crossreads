Saturday 7th OctoberHave had another week-end in London since I wrote last in this book.Saw Rushy at the School on the Monday morning and chatted with several masters, including Nixon, McNair, Hope &amp; Kingdom.Paid 7/-, the subscription for 2 years Elizabethan membership.Was not exceedingly pleased with reception which was hardly enthusiastic.
