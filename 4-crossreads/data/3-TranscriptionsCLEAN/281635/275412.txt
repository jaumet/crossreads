  
 Bought Anglo-French dictionary 3 francs. 2/10/17 Night not quite so cold. Had little drill both morning & afternoon. Day fairly warm. Pay books collected preparatory to pay. After tea went into Morbecque & bought loaf of bread 1 franc & 3. Had scare this afternoon an aeroplane passed over & they thought was a Fritz so all had to take cover under hedges & trees. Turned out to be an English plane & the bombs were at our own bombing school. 3/10/17 Not nearly as cold in night - rained a good deal too. Muster parade in morning & little drill during day. After tea in Morbecque picquet with 11 others & a 36th Sergt. He shouted all hands beers, biscuits & pears. Got back about 10.15pm. 4/10/17 Thursday. Short route
