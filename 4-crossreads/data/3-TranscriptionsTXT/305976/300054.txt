Beyond this we are told is a 50 mile desert. Practically the whole Turkish Army has been captured &amp; Jacko is completely smashed. The area round here is a sight to see. Amman station is blocked with Jacko transport, They have practically left every thing behind. There are dumps with any amount of equipment. Dead Horses &amp; Camels are all over the place &amp; the smell &amp; Flies  [indecipherable]  are very bad.
