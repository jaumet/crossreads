<p>Especially to night the 25th our Secret service is supposed to have announced that a hostile raid will be carried out about 11.30pm it is now 8.30 I am prepared in every possible way &ndash; a water bottle full of rum a hole outside my home to get into if things become too bad</p>
<p>But it is beastly this suspense, last night I thought that I could hear one &amp; lay in frozen stillness sweating gently for about five mins until I discovered that it was a motor in the distance. Tonight</p>
