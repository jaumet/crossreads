 Friday 13 October 1916 Left Kantara early, came through Ishmalia [Ismailia], Tel-el-Kebir, Zagazig, Benha [also known as Banha] and on to Alexandria and then to Sidi Bishr. Wonderful crops of corn all the way.  Cotton being pulled.  Nile in flood. 
 Saturday 14 October 1916 Lecture and inspection by Dr. in morning. Leave from 2 till 11 every night.  Dick West, Aire and I went in for afternoon and had a look round.  Had two good feeds and went to Alhambra vaudeville show.
