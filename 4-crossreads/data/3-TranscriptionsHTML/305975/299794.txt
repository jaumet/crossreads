<p>to day.<br/>Monday April 7.4.19.<br/>am well  nice day  I feel the Cold<br/>4.P.M. sports to day  am in sight of freemantle  arrived at freemantle 7.P.M  anchored out.<br/>Tuesday April 8.4.19<br/>still at freemantle  watered and coaled all day.  very nice day.  health Dr on board all day.<br/>Wednesday April 9.4.19<br/>left freemantle 9.AM  nice morning  feeling well</p>
