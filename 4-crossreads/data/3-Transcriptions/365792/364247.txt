<p>June 17 Transport next Tuesday although I believe one eyed men are now put on Transport duties. Am back in the old ward &amp; feeling good.</p>
<p>18 Jolly good long sleep last night thank you. Had the clothes hoisted off me this mng &amp; a couple of the boys had a pillow fite Going on Tuesday alright &amp; the Doc says I may not get my eye until I reach Australia. Sort of impromptu Concert &amp; dance in the Hall this afternoon &amp; we had rather a good audience too including several girls &amp; a couple of Frenchmen who appeared to be enjoying themselves no end. There was a good pianist on the job too. Am writing this by the side of a shady hedge far from the madding crowd &ndash; So far.   An Aust from the "Warspite" gave an interesting a/c of the Jutland fight. [in margin] <u>Sunday</u></p>
<p>19 Drawing kit at 1.30 today &amp; leaving here tomorrow. The long wavy grass is like a carpet underfoot &amp; a myriad</p>
