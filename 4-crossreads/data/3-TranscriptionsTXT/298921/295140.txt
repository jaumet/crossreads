 June, 1915 Friday 18 Young Cutler got a Deniliquin Paper I read news with interest. Have not seen Lorimer since &amp; do not know where to look for Harry Phillips. Wounded still arrive daily. Out of the new Infantry arrivals about 12 go to Hospital daily with sunstroke they cannot stand the Heat first off the boat it agrees with me, you note my weight. There goes my Fast it is now a month since the Regiment Marched out &amp; left us behind.
