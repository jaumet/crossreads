<p>Aug 22 they don't like the restriction either. But Mc makes himself perfectly at home.</p>
<p>23 Still cloudy &amp; unsettled  Understand the figures on the Chart are faked by the 2nd Officer, &amp; that we will reach Capetown on Sunday or Monday instead of Wed! Beginning to get a jolly good appetite, &amp; the weather is perceptibly cooler. After sundown it is interesting to lean over the rail &amp; watch for the flash of fins as the fish (&amp; sometimes sharks) come to the surface. Evidently they are attracted by the light.</p>
