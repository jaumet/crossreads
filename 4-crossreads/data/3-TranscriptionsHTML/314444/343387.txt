<p>Sept 29th 1915.</p>
<p>Rubery Asylum<br/>2nd Birmingham War Hospital.<br/>Northfield Birmingham<br/>England</p>
<p>A Merry Xmas and happy New Year to all</p>
<p>Dear Mother</p>
<p>Just a few lines to let you know that I very near well again. I am allowed up and am walking about in the Hospital now. <strike>Now</strike> Every where I have been, and I have been in four Hospitals and 2 hospital ships, I have been treated so kindly and so well looked after</p>
