<p>it was to be among English people once more, so far this trip had all seemed a dream, but once we arrived in Blighty we woke up, had a bonzer run to London &amp; then we all marched around to <strike>Victor</strike> Horsferry Road &amp; reported got fixed up &amp; Jock &amp; I went up to Wellington Barracks where the Guards are Jock had a brother in law there so we left our packs &amp; etc. here</p>
