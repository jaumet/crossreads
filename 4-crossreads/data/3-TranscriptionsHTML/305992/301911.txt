<p>The Diary of the C 810</p>
<p>H.M.A. Transport Indarra<br/>From Oct. 13. 1917 to Oct. 29 1918</p>
<p>P.S. In writing these notes I am perfectly aware of the fact that if it should fall into the enemy's hands it would be usefull. Well should anything happen it will always be kept in such a position that in the event of capture it will be destroyed. JEM</p>
