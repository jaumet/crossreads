 My Battalion was in Poppy Valley which is open to view & fire from Gabe Tepe for its whole length. On the afternoon of the day we left, (luckily they were not shelling us at the time - thought they used to strafe us with shrapnel at odd frequent & uncertain intervals) turned left up a communication 
 3. trench - on entering which they were lost to view - into the Fire Trench - back down another communication Trench which when crossing Poppy Valley was sunk & screened by bushes so that we could not be seen - out & back into the Valley again at the point of commencement (as they say in a Conveyance). Each time we dropped or added 2 or 3 men, so that all friend Abdul saw was 3 or 4 squads of men about 25 strong each, going up to reinforce the Firing Line. 
 We left that night. We also left some farewell notes "Good-bye Abdul - meet you again some day". "You fought fair sorry we have to go" etc. We also left some gifts in the shape of booby-trap-bombs.
