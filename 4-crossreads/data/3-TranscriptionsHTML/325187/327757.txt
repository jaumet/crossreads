<p>[Wednesday January 12 1916]</p>
<p>[indecipherable] today Letter from Laura &amp; Charlie all seemed well-<br/>Posted you some verses written by a Bushie now in the Pay office, being wounded too badly for further Trench work- It is the high qualities of the Rank &amp; file which keep our Regiments going &amp; I always feel how extra good the officers <u>should be</u> to lead such men-<br/>Anticipated being away ere this but here we are still as no water available at our outpost at present.<br/>Climate is lovely here, now warm days &amp; cold nights.</p>
