 Saturday 26th [May] Fritz made it exceptionally warm all the morning and on two occasions I had narrow squeaks.  First one &ndash; I had just finished shaving &ndash; at door of dugout &ndash; when whizz-bang and had I not unconsciously ducked, my head would have stopped a number of flying pieces.  As it was I got a shower of leaves &amp; sprigs from the tree I was under.  A few minutes later I was standing in the trench cooking some rice when bang &ndash; and I thought my last hour had come.
