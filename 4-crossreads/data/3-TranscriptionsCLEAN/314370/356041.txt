
June, 1916Sunday 18wake up in our new home, completely disgustedNo rations yet.At 4 pm tents arrive & are erected 14 men per tent
Monday 19Went to Tel-el-Kebir Rifle Range w/- a company of reinforcements.shooting was simply awful men have never had any proper instruction.splendid walk out & on the way back I sniped a large bird in a tree at 60 yds w/- my pistol
