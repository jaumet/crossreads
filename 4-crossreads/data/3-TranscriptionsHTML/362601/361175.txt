<p>18 March, Tuesday</p>
<p>W.<br/>We have music &amp; cards in the front Room. We are all very happy &amp; don't want to go to bed. Ian H. Dring &amp; make Supper. Another night until 'heart am.</p>
<p>19 March, Wednesday</p>
<p>W.<br/>We read about the Soldier Pacts. We have dinner very late &amp; M &amp; I play cards until tea. More cards after tea but I only score. A &amp; M do not K---- &amp; poor M is very lachrymose at night.</p>
