<p>Jan 22nd 1918<br/>Left Sutton Viny on Draft. Arrived Southampton 1PM.<br/>Embarked about 6 &amp; got of again &amp; marched about 2 miles to Rest Camp. Issued with 3 Blankes &amp; put in a fair night. Has been allmost like Summer the last few days. There is a lot of fun in going on Draft &ndash;<br/>What, without asking, hither hurried Whence? And, without asking, Whither hurried hence?<br/>Of course there is a good deal of Drinking &amp; any sort of travel</p>
