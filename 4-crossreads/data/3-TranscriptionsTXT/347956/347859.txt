 signalling as usual in the same old spot near the camp.  lamp practice after tea &amp; it was rather cold to be signalling at present &amp; did not stay out overtime on that account 
 Sat 16th Signalling until dinner time &amp; then received a pass out from 1.30 till 11.30 PM.
