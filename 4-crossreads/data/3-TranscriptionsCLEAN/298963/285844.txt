
1st MayNothing happened worth recording.
2ndAlso uneventful, "C" section proceeded to Beecourt Chateau.
3rdPlayed cricket against 1st Amb. Supply Train, won by 100 runs at Aliert. Our opponents treated us royally, supper supplied after the match & a lorry brought us home to Pozieres.
4thGot orders at 4 a.m. to prepare to go into the line. to Left at 7 a.m. for main dressing station, loafed about all day, met a lot of old boys at night went to into line, encountered two or three duds of tear gass, & sundry other unpleasant experiences but reached the Regimental aid post without casualties,
