<p>come in Blighty amongst the labouring classes may cause a lot to let their present castles in the air totter when it comes to the actual decision. &nbsp;But if you make as many enquiries as will set them thinking without prejudicing or offering anything, well, that&#39;s as much and as far as you can do. &nbsp;You can put the particulars before them as you glean them. &nbsp;It is not necessary to continue the matter with me but write direct to them.</p>
<p>Well, Dad, there is not much else to mention, as if I have to finish my letter to Mither. &nbsp;By the time you get this you will just have about 6 months to go, and I hope nothing happens to keep you from consumating your long-looked for and hard-earned rest.</p>
<p>Fondest love,</p>
<p>Your sincere Son,</p>
<p>James x</p>
