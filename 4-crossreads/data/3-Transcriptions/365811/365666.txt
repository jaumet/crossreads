<p>16 Spring St.<br/>Sydney, 2-Janry 1918</p>
<p>My dear Judge,<br/>I received your letter and thank you very much for copy cable. We have not had one &amp; Mrs. Lucas was much disappointed but we are quite satisfied now. We may of course receive one yet.</p>
<p>With best wishes from Mrs. Lucas &amp; self to you Mrs. Ferguson &amp; family for the new year.</p>
<p>I am<br/>Yours very sincerely,<br/>E. Lucas</p>
