
England. He reached Plymouth about Aug. 26th. Ran is in England too.
Nurse Woods has returned to Sydney - I think I told you - & is in charge of a hospital for wounded here. Am going to write & see if we may call.
Hazel Father & I went to Ingalara last night for tea & had a nice time. Dorrie Ward the singer was there - a girl with a lovely voice, & very like Hilda Trugeon only much fatter & not such a pretty complexion.
No word this week from Harold. Reg has changed into Motor transport but I dont know his exact address. Ernie was in [indecipherable] Battalion but has been attached to some special company over there.
With much love from us all hoping you are still unhurt.
Mother.
