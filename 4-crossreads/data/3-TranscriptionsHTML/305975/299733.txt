<p>Tuesday Nov. 12.11.18<br/>great Excitement this morning  the war is over  Bells ringing and ships whistling flags flying  all the Place is excitment    <strike>t</strike> here in Port said Ballons up in the air  very cold morning   am feeling very much better.  2 P.M.   Raining and thunder  great doings here at the hospital  they are giving an afternoon tea to all the Patients  flags up Band Playing everyone is eating</p>
