<p>Friday 21 March 1919<br/>Was orderly officer all day. Did very little indeed. Got out of everything. Voice still gone.</p>
<p>Saturday 22 March 1919<br/>Have some cold can't speak. Went to Bills cousins place. Took George. Played cards all the time. Managed to get our train home OK.</p>
