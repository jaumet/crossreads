<p>Thursday 21 March<br/>Morning at Grafton Galleries War Pictures. Extremely good &amp; interesting. After Hyde Pk. Met another chap &amp; spend afternoon interestingly. Instead of meeting <strike>Bert</strike> Will only met Bert also on leave. All off to Scotland.</p>
<p>Got new tunic &ndash; fixed colours had tea, visited Ciro's. Then off to Kings Cross being warned of an air raid on the way.</p>
<p>Got good seats, left for Edinburgh about 10.30. Pretty crook with biliousness. Met mate of [Korah?] Les &amp; Will in carriage.</p>
