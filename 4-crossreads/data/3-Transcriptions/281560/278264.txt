<p>26/6/16.<br/>There was a big "strafe" last night with artillery. The din was terrific. I was nearly skittled by a shell that burst directly in front of me; within three feet of my face as I stood observing over the parapet. Was stunned and blinded for a while, but soon recovered. Received some newspapers.<br/>27/6/16.<br/>A fine day with a overcast sky. Our planes again attacked the german balloons and destroyed three of them. Our boys cheered when they fell. Fritz's evening "hate" was rather prolonged today.<br/>28/6/16<br/>Our trench mortars sent over some favours to the huns this morning but he seem peeved about the frightful</p>
