<p>Cinquains</p>
<p>Bulletin, R.P.<br/>Sept. 13, 1917</p>
<p>Beware!<br/>The still night air<br/>Echoes the littlest sound:<br/>And softly 'long the line is breathed<br/>"Don't sneeze!"<br/>C.V. Jervis</p>
<p>"Somewhere"<br/>In that long lane<br/>Of crosses crude he lies:<br/>And sleeping here at my full breast<br/>His son.<br/>Frida E. Hamilton</p>
<p>It comes.<br/>High in the air<br/>We hear the warning note;<br/>Then bolt like hell and wait the crash<br/>A dud!<br/>Block 10.</p>
<p>Caprice!<br/>How durst thou come<br/>In loves disguise and touch<br/>a heart that waits for love - &amp; then<br/>Depart?<br/>E.H. Wills</p>
<p>Sweet word,<br/>Softly spoken, sad.<br/>How oft has love grown cold,<br/>Rekindled at the whisper of<br/>"Good-bye!"<br/>Ray R. Rede</p>
<p>When I<br/>Was young I held<br/>The blue Bird in my hands.<br/>As I grew old he crept into<br/>My Heart.<br/>Allan Denny</p>
