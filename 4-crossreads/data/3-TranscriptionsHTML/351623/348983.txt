<p>18 May 16<br/>At Kingston. Our concert party &amp; band gave an entertainment ashore in aid of Red Cross.  They had a large audience &amp; it was a great success.</p>
<p>19 May 16<br/>At Kingston</p>
<p>20 May 16<br/>Left pier in forenoon watch &amp; proceeded to Port Royal where we anchored. 22nd May anchored at Port Royal.</p>
<p>22 May 16<br/>Proceeded to sea a short distance from Port Royal &amp; done sub-calibre firing. Came back and anchored at Port Royal.</p>
<p>23 May 16<br/>Anchored at Port Royal.</p>
<p>24 May 16<br/>Done 6 in firing &amp; came back &amp; anchored off Kingston.</p>
<p>25 May 16<br/>Anchored off Kingston.</p>
<p>26 May 16 )<br/>27 May 16 )  Anchored off Kingston.<br/>28 May 16 )</p>
