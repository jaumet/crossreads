Friday 22 February
March winds are commencing. The "Sentimental Blokes" in Ballieul are still very good, the lady being more charming than ever. A violent bombardment on our left to-night.
Papers profesy a big German attack on this front but I don't know whether it will eventuate.
Saturday 23 February
Walk over to 4th D.S.C. find George Petersen in have a good evening with him.
