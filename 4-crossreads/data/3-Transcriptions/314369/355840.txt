<p>8 June 1915<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Start out at 7 am this morning as we have further to go on the same job.<br/>One of A co's men wounded not far from us.<br/>Get good news of one of our submarines having sunk several warships &amp; transports belonging to Turkey.<br/>Write letters home.</p>
<p>1915 June 9<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Inlying piquet again<br/>Have a turn out   &amp; inspection at 9 am.</p>
