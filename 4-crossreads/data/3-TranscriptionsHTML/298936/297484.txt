<p>November, 1915</p>
<p>Monday 29</p>
<p>After a bitterly cold wind last night, woke up to find snow this morning.</p>
<p>Bitterly cold wind blowing all day: simply impossible to keep feet &amp; hands warm.</p>
<p>It is miserable working <strike>all</strike> Several water tanks frozen &amp; pipes burst so <strike>that</strike> [indecipherable] us [indecipherable] to quarter rations of water or just enough for a couple of small drinks a day as tea.</p>
