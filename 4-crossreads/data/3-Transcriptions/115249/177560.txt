<p>Transmitting Ship: Berrima</p>
<p>Date: 12 9 4</p>
<p>Brigadier to R A C</p>
<p>OC garrison troops reports Mayor has formally handed over town of Rabaul, guards have been posted on principal buildings &amp; advance guards [thereon] out main body of troops remaining in town&nbsp;<s>Letter received from</s>&nbsp;A promise has been made that a reply will be given to my letter to Governor at 4.30 pm tomorrow. Propose to hoist flag at 3 pm tomorrow.</p>
