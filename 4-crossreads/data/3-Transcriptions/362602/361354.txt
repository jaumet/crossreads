Fancy a very cold wind.  It is colder to-day than it has been since we left Sydney.  That is in the shade of course.  On the sunny side of the deck it is hot.</p>
<p>Ellis &amp; I still have a bit of the cake that we bought at the F. Food &amp; Ice Co's place in King Street &amp; a few pea-nuts and sultanas.  We have had a few wireless messages through about the war in France but nothing about the Dardanelles or Salonica.</p>
