<p>May, 1915<br/>Thursday 6<br/>A thunderbolt fell today when I heard that Col. Meredith remains here o/c Camp &amp; I am detailed to remain with him &ndash; The Reg. leave their horses &amp; go as Infantry &ndash; Both the c/o &amp; I feel it very much &amp; he is selfish in keeping me, when he was going I was one of his Officers that would go at any price, now that he stops he keeps me as his aide -<br/>Letters from Laura   (23/4/15<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(10/5/15<br/>  "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   12/5/15<br/>Also from Lorna &amp; Ralph I shall probably see him now, my job is to come on with the Brigade Horses &amp; 600 men.<br/>Laura sent scarves &amp; Tobacco</p>
