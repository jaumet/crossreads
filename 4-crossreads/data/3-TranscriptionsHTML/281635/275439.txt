<p>within about 200 yds of destination when coming down from line. Carriages telescoped &amp; one this chap was in was smashed to pieces injuring him, also all his platoon except 7 or 8. Temperature much milder &amp; all snow thawed away making place very sloppy. Met Bob Clarke who came down from No 59 2 days ago. Had supper with him.<br/>11/1/18 Saw French airship. Milder temperature. Had couple route marches. Had supper with Bob Clarke.<br/>12/1/18 Read letter from Mr Randall. Raining all morning. After dinner went down to Boulogne for 4 or 5 hours &amp; had tea down there &ndash; steak, chips &amp; coffee costing 2fr 2&frac12;.</p>
