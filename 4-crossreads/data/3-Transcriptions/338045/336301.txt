<p>1916<br/>Sept. 28th - Thursday<br/>Drizzling rain, but not cold.  Very heavy thunderstorm, hailstones &amp; lighting at 6 p.m.  More casualties coming in &amp; as a consequence, plenty of work.  Meet the Belgian &amp; have a cup of coffee &amp; cakes with him &amp; learn some French.  Still raining, I see him off in the tram, which is late &amp; return to the office to secure my greatcoat, which left there.  Legge still working &amp; I wait for him until 9-30 p.m., meanwhile doing some French.  We walk home together &amp; go straight to</p>
