
7th Today we had a day out doing tactical work & planning how to attack & defend positions.8th. This morning all lectures & in the afternoon we each fired 12 rounds per man from Lewis guns out on the range.Tonight we are going out on a bombing stunt, which will Keep us out till about 2 oclock. So you see they have a very practical way of teaching.9th. Well I never laughed so much as I did last night for a long long time.We moved off from our Barracks about 10 oclock carrying rifles 10 rounds blank, 2 small bombs each, the majority of the men
