<p>That is the way of it. We pay no heed to any old thing but Umpty Iddy. If asked our religion we are bound to reply: Umpty &hellip;iddy&hellip;iddy&hellip;umpty etc. Oh &ndash; it's a cow to be a lone Australian in the heart of the British Army &ndash; I don't think.</p>
<p>Had a look at Grantham yesterday. Dirty slushy squdgy mean looking place full of poor people &amp; pitiful kids&hellip;don't like it any.</p>
<p>By the way now that we're settled in the 3rd Division our uniform is altered<br/>[Transcribers note: letter continues in a different ink] 26.2.17 so don't bother to write for more photos &ndash; we're saving friends &amp; relations the trouble by getting the job through right here.Will send them along one time. Note how I broke off this last night &ndash; that's the sort of thing</p>
