 May 1815 Wednesday 12  Dull  showery Went into fire trench last night at 6 P.M. Navy bombarding a troublesome gun of enemies. Weather brightening up again. Practically no rifle fire from the trenches of a day time Hauled out at 6 AM from the dug-out to do [indecipherable] with two others. Went into the fire trench again at 6 P.M &amp; watched all night  wrong date here 
 Thursday 13  Fine  and hot Hauled out of dugout to go sapping with three others for three hours from 6 AM to 9 AM. And went into fire trench at 6 P M. In our front the Turks are entrenched on a large ridge. beyond this is a beautiful stretch of country. Had a fair nights rest.
