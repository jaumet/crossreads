<p>Saturday 22 September 1917</p>
<p>Alex:<br/>Very disappointed probably will not get away till next month so post this today.</p>
<p>[The remainder of the page crossed through.]<br/>All my letters have gone on to England but I should not growl the expense is what annoys me so unwarranted still I do not lose my seniority with the Regiment &amp; many would give much to be in my shoes. Love to all &ndash; Frank.</p>
