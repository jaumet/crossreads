 four & half hours journey from Boulogne was my father & home. For over 3 1/2 years I had not seen them & to think so near yet so far. Scotty was in the same frame of mind. We saw the channel for a bit & then lost it only to pick it up again near Callais. Here we saw plenty of Sommies who seemed to be quite contented with their lives & duties. Several Troop trains passed us here & we cheered. We also saw several French troop trains on our way up & we cheered them, at times we cheered almost anything, more so if we saw a crowd of girls.
