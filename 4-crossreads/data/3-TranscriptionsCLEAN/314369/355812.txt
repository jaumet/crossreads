  
 13 April 1915 Tuesday 
 Turn the whole lot into one unit   &  call ourselves the 4th Inf Bgde Details A B C   &  D companies. Pitch camp all in one spot near the old Brigade Headquarters which is used as orderly Room   &  Officers Mess 
 1915 April 14 Wednesday 
 Go on a route march to the first Tower a distance of about 3 miles from camp along one of the roads built by Napoleon during his occupation of Egypt.
