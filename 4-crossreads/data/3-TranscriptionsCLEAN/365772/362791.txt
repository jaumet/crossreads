  
 & we cut about together. Ralph no doubt knows all the places of interest & can describe them much better than me. My next stage in convalescance, then furlough. Winter is starting now, never mind sending my anything dear as I can buy all I want through the ordinance on my deferred pay. Well Lizzie you will have to excuse this scribble as I am going out for the day. So love to self & family. From yours as ever Ken.
