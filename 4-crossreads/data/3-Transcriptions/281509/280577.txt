<p>20 December 1916<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Off evacuation duties, put on road making &amp; stone quarrying. Take things very easy &amp; only work 3 hours a day. Fritz evidently has a new gun to work. he shelled h&mdash;out of the surroundings with high explosive. Several huts blown up, and some casualties occurred.  Frost thawing.</p>
