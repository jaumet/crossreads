<p>Tuesday, 17/4/17<br/>Had good sleep last night.  Notice temperature slightly up &amp; down all time.  Throat feels much better in day time but becomes very sore when darkness comes.</p>
<p>Wednesday, 18/4/17<br/>Still on the mend.  Have not been ordered out of bed yet.</p>
<p>Thursday, 19/4/17<br/>Getting better &amp; better every day.  Weather outside does not seem nearly so cold.</p>
<p>Friday, 20/4/17<br/>Got up today &amp; enjoyed sitting in sun.  Has been fine &amp; sunshiney all day.</p>
<p>Saturday, 21/4/17<br/>Having meals in dining hall today &ndash; get plenty to eat.  Saw football match in afternoon.</p>
