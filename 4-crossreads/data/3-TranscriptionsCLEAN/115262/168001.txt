  
 Wednesday 22 August 1917. 
 No reveille here Turned out at 6 am. Fed camels. One of the party is doing the cooking. Breakfast 7 a.m. At 8.30 all went for an hr. in the surf. Watered camels at 10 a.m. Dinner 12 noon. Fed camels at 1 pm. Drew rations at 2 pm and then washed some clothes. 
 Went for a dip at 4.30. p.m. and another at 6.30 p.m. 
 (The amateur cook tried to make a custard but put salt in place of sugar.) (Result - a failure.) 
 Yesterday we passed Italian troops holding fortified sandhills & redoubts, also passed several graveyards each holding 20 to 30 bodies of men killed in the Rafa stunts.
