<p>March 1919.<br/>22nd.  Another injection of germs, innoculation against influenza.<br/>24th.  Very cold indeed.  Twice to Church yesterday.  We go on leave on 27th.<br/>27th.  The much feared workmens strike has not come off &amp; we are now in London on 14 days leave at <strike>Shelb</strike> Lincoln Hall Hotel after much trouble.<br/>28th.  At National Hotel, Russell Square, Westminster Cathedral &amp; National Galleries.  "As you Were"<br/>29th.  Croydon.</p>
