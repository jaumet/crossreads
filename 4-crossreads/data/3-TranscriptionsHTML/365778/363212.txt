<p>Monday 13/8/17<br/>Married 3 years today. Wrote a long letter to Doff. Visited Billets at tattoo.<br/>Tuesday 14/8/17<br/>General Training, paid visit to Billets. Band played well.<br/>Wednesday 15/8/17<br/>Raining again, gave a lecture to N.C.O.'s &amp; men.<br/>Thursday 16/8/17<br/>Took battalion for 16 miles Route march, no one fell out, had a swim at Arques on the way.<br/>Friday 17/8/17<br/>Battalion Drill on the Distillery Grounds &ndash; Good &ndash; Had dinner with Slane at Brigade H.Q. Wisdom returns tomorrow &ndash; wrote to Doff.</p>
