<p>Crushed in the grass on which we sit and recline</p>
<p>Immediately at rear of the hedge is a small wood of oaks and ash cool with green shade</p>
<p>Above is the sky of a pure and perfect blue</p>
<p>White summer clouds are like snowy continents<br/>[following text crossed out] in the blue<br/>And island in the blue</p>
<p>Doubtful isthmuses of cloudy rust join many of these</p>
<p>The sun is like a golden ship sending forth rich warmth of light to all the floating lands</p>
<p>The kahki of the soldiers uniforms has just that tinge of green in it to make a pleasing harmony with surrounding shades  A small watch glistens here and there on a brown wrist</p>
<p>Red brown faces in shade of slough hats give the strongest note of color</p>
<p>Grey hair shows up on the head of one man who has thrown of his hat</p>
<p>Little skirls of smoke rise and pass from the pipes and cigarettes of the men</p>
<p>One man has gone to sleep</p>
<p>The officer has been lecturing on trench warefare in France  While taking note of the scene before me I am listening</p>
<p>One part of the lecture is distinct in my ears.  Advice to the men in taking prisoners, bring back only the number</p>
