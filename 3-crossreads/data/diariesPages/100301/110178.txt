Oct 26
arrival of Mail steamer return of men who went as guard to Sydney with some more german prisoners sent from here
Oct 27
arrival of Masina with stores
Oct 28
drill marches are the order, getting stale
Oct 29
nothing happens now only the usual Parades until about Nov 10th
Nov 10th
when we hear that an English missionary has been flogged by Germans in New Ireland G Coy are to provide 50 men to go arrest the 6 Germans who took part in the flogging we hear that they have 200 Native Police are ready for us
Nov 11th
getting ready for the Trip which is about 100 miles away the Germans call it New Mecklenberg we call it New Ireland
Nov 12th
We embark on a small wooden steamer called the Medang bound for a place called Namatinia all well until 9.30 pm when they discover that she is leaking badly, there is a rough sea on, we fire rockets burn Blue lights Turn back she is making water fast, we have 50 native Police on board 7 maries besides 52 whites, the niggers are calling out are very frightened as the water is still coming in, the Pumps are working hard some of the boys are sleeping through it all, some men are sick few realise the danger we are in, our signals
