<p>I was there I saw a kid hook a big fellow twice but he broke away both times the water of this river is very clear &amp; pure.</p>
<p>We made our way along towards the Cathedral we walked through some little narrow streets then all of a sudden a most beautiful view met our gaze, &amp; it looks all the more beautiful on account of its severe simplicity this cathedral is built on a piece of land about 7 acres in extent I should think</p>
