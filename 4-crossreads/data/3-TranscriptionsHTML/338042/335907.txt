<p>Successful attack by Australian Fourth Division. A good substantial advance &amp; many prisoners (2.200) &ndash; a Good many of these (900) we pass on the way to the show &ndash; Very sad news from Mrs Scales &ndash; her Dear Mother, Mrs. Thomas, has departed from this Life. "Requescat in Pace!"<br/>July 5th<br/>A happy dream in the early morning, of one who is far away &ndash; Rehearsal &ndash; Continuation of "Eastern Sketch" &ndash; Correspondence &ndash;</p>
