<p>1915<br/>May. 29 Sat.<br/>Another 60 wounded admitted about 11 p.m. last night from the Dardanelles.<br/>Spent the afternoon with 1st Cairo Scouts at Wilcocks' (Y.M.C.A.) Grounds.<br/>Good Concert held in No. 1 ward by party from Westminster Dragoons &ndash; Some class, rather!</p>
