<p>The amount of money collected for Ellis the fireman who hurt his wrist while boxing some time back was published yesterday &amp; amounted to &pound;27-10, &amp;pound20.0 of which they cabled to his wife in England so he won't be very much out of pocket after all.</p>
<p>14th. Not quite so rough thank goodness today but still bad enough for me.</p>
<p>The day dragged its dreary length through, the afternoon was made a little shorter by some "lemon slicing" which caused a fair amount of fun from the antics &amp; caperings of the contestants</p>
<p>The crew have been busy holystoning the decks, getting her clean &amp; shipshape for our run into port,  they are also battening down the hatches so they must be expecting</p>
