 March 1917 16th continued   &amp; all the infantry here in reserves are "Standing to" 17th.   St Patricks Day to-day.   Fritz has cleared out!   There has not been a shell fired over here all day, &amp; rumours have been running wild.   During the afternoon we got the news that the Australians have occupied Bappaume, Boulencourt &amp; a few other small villages &amp; that the 8th &amp; some of the 14th Brigades shared the honours of being first in the villages.
