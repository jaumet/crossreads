<p>1915<br/>Apr. 17 Sat.<br/>Our Q.M. has been placed under open arrest as the result of the inquiry into tucker question &amp; other matters.<br/>Food is now pretty good as the men are managing their own ration allowance &ndash; it having been taking out of the hands of the Q.M.</p>
