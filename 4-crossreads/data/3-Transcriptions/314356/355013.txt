<p>Her tired eyes didn't even see me.</p>
<p>Luncheon was a funeral affair.  The cat, a lovely Persian tabby in a highly interesting condition, clawed for food!  Said the 'girl' &ndash; "He's <u>wounderfully</u> well fed, isn't he?", and the seeded satyr "Naughty!" - wether to the girl or cat is not certain.</p>
<p>Then she whispered a tender [indecipherable] word to the waitress and a moment later she reverently carried in a damned ball-eyed spaniel who "spoke" for his dinner &ndash; in automaton phrases.</p>
<p>February 23<br/>Commences today the fourth Phase of the homing, and the well-circulated rumour is of all boats cancelled for February, and only two sailing during March!</p>
<p>Leave enjoyed, tho the weather was uniformly dull and rain-hazy, and bitterly cold.</p>
