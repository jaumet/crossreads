 Monday 
 Attended a sergeant from 51st D'A'C with both hands nearly blown off, just like pieces of raw meat &ndash; also had abdomenal wounds &amp; face wounds &ndash; sent him on to the big Hospitals. 
 11/7/16  Tuesday 
 Very cold last night &ndash; 15th F. Ambulance &amp; Dr Coppleson done good work last night. Most of the bearing has to be done under darkness &amp; difficultys, as long as we get them into the dressing station, we can evacuate them during the day, we only hold men here who will be fit for service again in five days &ndash; 128 shells were dropped into Lavinte yesterday &amp; the only damage was Batterys nil &ndash; 1 cow &amp; calf injured, two Farm houses blown down &amp; burnt &ndash; I could have got plenty of souvenirs &ndash; but a man would soon want a horse to carry them.
