<p>21-12-16<br/>Thurs 21st<br/>We left Camp at 6 clock yesterday evening formed up with the 1st Regt leading, 2nd next &amp; 3rd last C Sqdn of 1st Regt was the advance guard as usual. We had a very trying nights  ride as we had all been on the go <strike>from</strike> since "stand to Arms" at 5 oclock yesterday morning, just at daybreak we struck the Wadi one mile south of El Arish crossing the wadi we spread out &amp; soon had a large circle around the town, three Turks on camels trying to get away found themselves inside the screen &amp; made back to the town where they surrendered, the Regiments took up positions on the hills a mile or two to the East of El Arish</p>
