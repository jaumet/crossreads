<p>Frid 14th Feb:  Reached Sydney 7 p.m:  Anchored in Watson's Bay.<br/>Sun 16th:  Disembarked at Woolloomooloo Bay 4 p.m.<br/>Frid 21st:  Reported at No. 4 H.G.H Randwick. To report on 27th .<br/>Frid 28th:  Admitted as an out-door patient.<br/>May 9th:  Discharged. Furlough commenced<br/>June 22nd:  Completion of Furlough.<br/>June 12th:  Commenced work at Cockatoo.</p>
