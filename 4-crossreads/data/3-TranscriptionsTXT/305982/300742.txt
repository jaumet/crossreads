
29/10/16Sunday to day did not go to church  Notice to be prepared to leave Weymouth for Wool Tuesday next.  on our way back to France.  Letter from Mother.
30/10/16Girl in taxi  Pavillion [indecipherable]Nothing of interest today  Hellish windy & needless to say cold.  rain also
31/10/16We left Weymouth for Wool 2 PM, arrived in to new camp 4 oclock rather a well laid out camp  Raining like Billy OH  Marched through Weymouth with band
1/11/16Rained all night rather cold into the bargain  2 blankets each  before medical officer passed OK etc  usual parade during the day morning nooncabled for 15/10/0
2/11/16Rain again today  placed in charge of a draft for the 4th Div a hopeless mix up it is.  plenty of work & no instruction.
3/11/16No leave granted here so broke camp went to Weymouth in motor car a fine beano.  we arrived back in camp about 12.30 it was glorious trip 6 of ussame old messing about today
