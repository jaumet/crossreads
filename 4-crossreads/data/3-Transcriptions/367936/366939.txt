<p>Saturday 26 February 1916<br/>Dick plowing in Front pdk. till dinner time.  I went over to Barham and got "Tiger" and 2 sets of harness.<br/>Father &amp; Geoff fixed hoist for wheat bag.  In afternoon we worked at seed drill putting axel in.  Cool day.</p>
<p>Sunday 27 February 1916<br/>Ten horses to feed.  Fixed seed drill and took 2 furrow plow to Front pdk.  Got cart and took bags down and covered up wheat from rain.<br/>Had rest after dinner then fixed plow and had a hit at pots.<br/>Easterly wind with clouds.</p>
