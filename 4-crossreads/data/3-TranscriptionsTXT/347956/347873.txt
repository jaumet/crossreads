 of Cairo, but went through only the outskirts.  we arrived at Heliopolis &amp; found the lines down, mess rooms up &amp; everything ready for us, even had all our tents up;  I believe there are about 10,000 Terriers here &amp; about 10,000 colonials at present;  Heliopolis appears to me to be a much better place than Maadi &amp; a large skating rink is very handy &amp; Heliopolis seems more of a town than Maadi.
