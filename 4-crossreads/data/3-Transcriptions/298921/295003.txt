<p>January, 1915<br/>Saturday 30<br/>We anchored all night in the Bitter Lake, protected by a French Cruiser the old Himilaya converted into a Cruiser &amp; the "Minerva", having no Searchlights on board, 6 of the others went on to 'Ismalia' as they had lights.<br/>Aeroplanes scouted around this morning ere we moved off. If the Turks come they will get a hot reception as the Canal is protected by trenches along each bank. Cavalry &amp; Camel Corps Field artillery etc: from whom we get Who are you? Got any papers? Are we downhearted etc:-</p>
