<p>MONTABAUN<br/>[JANUARY 1917]<br/>Monday 1st<br/>"Cheerio" this year will see it out &amp; we wont be sorry<br/>Tuesday 2<br/>Rain, Rain, mud and more mud what a dirty world<br/>Wednesday 3<br/>Lieut Haigh was wounded this morn bullet pierced his chest<br/>Thursday 4<br/>I am out of a job, spare man</p>
