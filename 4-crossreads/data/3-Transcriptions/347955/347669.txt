<p>left the waddy 11 AM came to a nice green hill and camped 12.  unpacked watered our camels had dinner there is lots of water in the waddy had a good wash.  Camels out to graze we are stopping here for a while  went out grazing was relieved 3.PM  had tea 5.P.M. it looks like rain very windy turned in 7.30.<br/>Sunday feb.17.2.18.<br/>turned out 6.AM fed up had Breakfast  Packed up moved off 8.A.M.  trecked ten miles halted 11.AM had dinner let camels graze  moved off again 1.30  reached our camp again raffa 4.30 P.M had tea went to canteen had some Beer rum issue</p>
