However I wish you the merriest of all Xmases &amp; the most prosperous of all New Years.  I only wish to God I was at your table to enjoy it with you, but still I'm fighting, that yours may not be molested, and that's sufficient compensation. 
 In future you may have the pleasure of addressing me as Sgt. as I have gained another promotion. 
 Your affect. Son George
