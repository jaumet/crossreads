<p>September 1918.<br/>Friday 20</p>
<p>Bty Pos.Rgt of Bernes and ahead of Villeret.<br/>Weather wet right through the day. Ordered to new Bty position beneath the wrecked village of Villeret, ahead of the old Green Line.<br/>Made a scratch dugout but during a Fritzer duty shoot, we had a few whiz bangs near the phone.<br/>Fritzer night bomber came down in flames whilst we were on the line.<br/>Spent a very unpleasant night in our makeshift dugout.</p>
