  
 Tuesday 6/1/17. Rode to Reninghelst and had tea with Lloyd. 
 Wednesday 7/11/17. Raining heavily all day- O'Hara Wood came along for game of cards. 
 Thursday 8/11/17  Orders received to move  saw Bas had letters from Hetty & Pattie. 
 Friday 9/11/17. Battalion moved by bus to Steenvoorde  I rode with Little. Had dinner to celebrate Billie's Birthday with Robinson. 
 Saturday 10/11/17  Quiet day- a bit off color- presumably Billie is the cause.
