<p>Jan 13.  Went to Church with Gracie, then Seven Kings at night.  All very much surprised to see me.  Stayed Seven Kings.</p>
<p>Jan 14.<br/>Reported A.I.F. H.Q.s.  Jogged about town all day.  Had lunch with Betty, caused quite a commotion on walking in on her and her pals at Wilkinsons for lunch.</p>
<p>Jan 15<br/>Reported Tower of London (what a place).  There medically examined with remainder of force.  Canadian, S. African, New Zealander, Tommie and Aussies.  Very gratifying</p>
