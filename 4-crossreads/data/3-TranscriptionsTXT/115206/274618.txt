
February 1918Sunday 17MessinesFritz's planes have been very active - throughout the day. One was brought down on our right. One of ours was was brought down in flames, the wing following the body in descent.Crossed one of our howitzers fire.Went on L'aison with Mr Kerr, to left Batt. Les paid me a visit, and remained here for tea.Weather glorious but a little frosty. Smithy getting quite well again, and looks much brighter.
