<p>Monday 29 July<br/>Fair. Obtained piano from mess in morning installed in our tent. Horses out later. Singsong at dinner time.</p>
<p>To Cooees at night but hall full by the crowd rushing it. Played cricket &amp; then another sing song under the cover stretched out.</p>
