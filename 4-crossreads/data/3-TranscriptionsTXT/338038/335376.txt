 France 17.8.17 [Transcriber's note: There is a notation in red pencil at the beginning of this letter: Coln Kings leaving Battery through nervous strain &ndash; ] 
 Dear Father &amp; Mother, 
 I am sending this off as theres a mail leaving, but a usual I've nothing to say. The "war is still on" That's about all &ndash; we are in a quiet part now, &amp; will be I think for the next week or so, which is something these days as all idea of rest while the country is dry seems to be out of the question these days. 
 Several changes have taken place in our Brigade
