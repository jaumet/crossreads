<p>October 1918.<br/>Thursday 24</p>
<p>12th St Louis, Hos. Rouen<br/>Well I feel a little better this morning but gee whiz I had a pretty rough spin these last few days.<br/>I can remember fleeting glimpses of scenery and then folk but nothing else. Fine doc here only a youngster but for all that he is very good.</p>
