  
 Monday 30 April 1917. 
 On leave.   
 Caught 10.45 am train and went as far as Dandenong.      
 In afternoon went along Melbne road for walk for a mile or so. Came back at 5 pm.    
 An enjoyable walk too and a fine day.   
 7.46 pm train to City.   
 Stayed at Federal Palace Hotel    
 Crook during night.
