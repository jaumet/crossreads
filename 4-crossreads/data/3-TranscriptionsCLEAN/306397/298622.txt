 Monday 17/8/14 Bad weather. 
 Tuesday 18/8/14 Do. 
 Wednesday 19/8/14 Arrived this morning at Angruer (Pagan Island) To take coal from "Markomania" on Anganer is a german Phosphat company. Here I meet a Mr.Boekhoff from west E-----. In the evening we go to sea again after the Lloyd steamer "Princess Alice" has arrived with 2nd officer of the Reserve, Capt. Leut. Klopper and Vice Steersman Mayer, some men of the reserve and volunteers.
