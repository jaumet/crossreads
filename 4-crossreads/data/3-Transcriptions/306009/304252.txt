<p>1915<br/>Mar. 7 Sun.<br/>Attended Holy Communion at 6.45 a.m. in the fine little Chapel at Mena House &ndash; First Service I have been able to attend since leaving the Kyarra.<br/>Parade of the Unit in camp to award punishment to two of the men concerned in the thieving of &pound;6 from the Pack Store &ndash; Pte. Linquist got 90 days detention &amp; Pte. Gow got 6 months imprisonment with hard labour.</p>
