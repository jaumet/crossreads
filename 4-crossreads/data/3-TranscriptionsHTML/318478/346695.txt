<p>thankful that I have been trained by circumstance <strike>to</strike> of the past to stop my emotions at the border line.</p>
<p>From the point of view of the Artist Historian The Neck is a wonderful setting for the tragedy and I could not wait for the proper time which was just before sunrise but abandoned the "Mission" &amp; did a sketch which is Buckshee Souvenir, about 3 o'clock to 5 o'clock.  The Sun smiled nicely but a howling wind made it difficult for detail and many miss fires struck my new trench coat and other parts of me.</p>
<p>The fleas have found us out and are co-operating with the Jackals in night disturbing.</p>
<p>I wonder in my resting moments how you are managing with yourself the offspring &amp; the house, but can only at this distance rely on your never failing good management &amp; fairly reliable instinct.</p>
<p>The horses are a Godsend &amp; this evening I am a bit sore in the seat but fresh in the brain.</p>
<p>A demain.</p>
