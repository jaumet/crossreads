<p>28 February 1915<br/>Sunday<br/>2nd in Lent</p>
<p>Work on spuds suspended for the day<br/>Church parade at 10 am   &amp;  8 pm. Nobody attends.</p>
<p>1915 March 1<br/>Monday</p>
<p>The Spud Job commences again on new line. We rig up a set of pulleys   &amp;  set to w/- the steam winch result &ndash; about 10 times as quick   &amp;  hardly any labor.<br/>Weather continues fine   &amp;  cool.<br/>The old man is made Armourer Sergeant</p>
