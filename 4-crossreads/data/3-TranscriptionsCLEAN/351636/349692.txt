
The Centre for Soldiers' Wives & Mothers,92B. Pitt Street, Sydney.7-3-16
Dear Mr. LaytonCould you assist Mrs. Saunders in the payment of debts. She is owing rent & grocer's bill. She was left in debt when her husband sailed & has been paying off gradually - Having an invalid son & delicate daughter she finds it difficult to pay up in full
Yours faithfullyB. Booth
