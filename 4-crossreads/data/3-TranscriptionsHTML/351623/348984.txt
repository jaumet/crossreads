<p>29th May 16<br/>Heaved anchor in morning watch &amp; came alongside pier &amp; filled up with coal.</p>
<p>30th May 16<br/>Left Jamaica in morning watch proceeding to Grand Cayman Island.</p>
<p>31th May 16<br/>&nbsp;Arrived at G. Cayman Island in forenoon watch. No leaf given. The people seem whiter here than most of the other Islands.</p>
<p>1 June 16<br/>Anchored at G. Cayman. Left at 9 am in First watch</p>
<p>2 June 16<br/>At sea proceeding to Belize. Capital of British Honduras.</p>
<p>3 June 16<br/>Arrived at Belize in forenoon watch. British Honduras is in Central America. Went ashore at 1.30 pm. Belize did not</p>
