<p>Tuesday May 1st 1917</p>
<p>Tidworth : good day : mounted guard 9 am : No 2 Post</p>
<p>Wednesday May 2nd</p>
<p>Tidworth : good day : mounted 9 am : our passes did not come through so we went for a stroll after dinner : pictures after tea</p>
<p>Thursday May 3rd</p>
<p>Tidworth : good day : mounted guard 9 am : No 3 post : photos</p>
<p>Friday May 4th</p>
<p>Tidworth glorious day : relieved 9 am : caught noon train to Swindon : put in a great time &amp; caught 9.40 pm home</p>
<p>Saturday May 5th</p>
<p>Tidworth : windy &amp; dusty : mounted guard 9 am : M.O.</p>
<p>Sunday May 6th</p>
<p>Tidworth : good day : relieved 9 am " stroll after dinner : stroll</p>
