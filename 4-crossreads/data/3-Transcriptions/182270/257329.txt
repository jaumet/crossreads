<p>FRIDAY, JANUARY 17, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Knocking round London</p>
<p>all the morning. I went out to</p>
<p>see Aunt Olive in the afternoon</p>
<p>but she was out. We all went to</p>
<p>&quot;As You were&quot; at the London</p>
<p>Pavilion at night &amp; it was not</p>
<p>much of a Show.</p>
