23th July:- Saturday SundayWake at 8 am &  have breakfast. This is a fine club & does great credit to the ladies who  so voluntarily assist in it's upkeep. The meals are good & half the price of restaurants. A good warm bath to follow, then a shave &  boots cleaned & I feel A1, although troubled slightly with a cold. Walking along Victoria St, Westminster greatly to my surprise, I see, above all persons, Justin Cunningham from the Town Hall, Sydney &  in uniform. Catch the next bus and chase him but after a few streets he descends &  our pleasure at meeting is mutual - after 12 months. His brother is introduced & we all three set forth to church, the R.C. Cathedral at Westminster just in time for 11 am Mass. A beautiful Cathedral, larger than St Mary's Sydney, but apart from it's historic attachedment, no finer
