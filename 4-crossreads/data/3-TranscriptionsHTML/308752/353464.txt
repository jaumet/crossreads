<p>July 19th. 1917.<br/>What awful memories does this day bring back to us.  That terrible battle on the 19th July last one of the never forgotten days in my experience.</p>
<p>July 29th 1917.<br/>Preparation all the week for a big move somewhere. I believe we have a long trip before us. It will be a change to move to distant scenes.</p>
<p>July 30th 1917.<br/>Ambulance move off today &amp; board the train at A ---.  Train left at midnight.   At 9 a.m. we passed through a large Port of France, at 11 a.m. arrive at St. Omah or Omer, a pretty French town.  Probably Leave will be granted to this city. We then marched</p>
