
Friday 12 October 1917Once more we may go down the Hold but not for me today.We carry 2 prisoners Servian - rate of pay in own country 1/6 pr. day, in Aust. 6/- therefore they prefer ours.  S. Watson has pleurisy (?) & temp. 103, etc., cough troublesome.Life of Buddha heard in temple 8 a.m., 10.10.'17.
