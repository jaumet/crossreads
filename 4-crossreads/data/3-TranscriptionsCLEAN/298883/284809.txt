24thThe band of this Battalion arrived this evening and gave a few selections which were greatly appreciated by the boys.
25thIt has been cooler and inclined to be showery today.  A voluntary church parade was held today for all denominations.  A parade was also held for inspection by the Brigadier Gen. Elliot.
