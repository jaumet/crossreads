<p>18.1.17 Nothing much has happened the last few days, it snowed heavy night before last, and we had a march through the snow, it wasnt very cold while we were moving but when we stopped it was some. Have written a few letters home and have got myself fairly clean on it now. Saw Claude Wilkins tonight, at least I think it was Claude</p>
