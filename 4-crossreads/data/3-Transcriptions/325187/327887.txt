<p>[Wednesday 24 May 1916]<br/>Empire Day<br/>Our Patrol were in touch with the Tank Patrols today &amp; exchanged a few shots<br/>The N.Zealanders lost a Pack Horse.<br/>All hands hope for a good scrap soon to balance a/c for the Yeomanry losses.  <strike>The more one sees of the latter smash, the less palatable it appears.<br/>Boils again on the arms.</strike><br/>You might post me a Packet of Mighty Coal Tar soap, it lathers in the Brackish Water.  We sleep in our Boots &amp; Clothes every night but nobody minds as we are on the "job" again now.</p>
