<p>How pleased we shall when we get up out of the valley. It has been an eye opener for the Turks who it is said confidently expected us to retire on Jerusalem. It is the thoughts of getting out of here which makes one able to stand it.. Scorpions are numerous &amp; a bite from these pests is no joke. Flies are bad &amp; with other pests do not make it by any means a desirable camp. One on seeing Jericho as it is now, with the turret of the Mosque &amp; the hovels which constitute this place can hardly realise that in the old days it was a big city.
