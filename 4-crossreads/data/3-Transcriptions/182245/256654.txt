<p>MAY 1917</p>
<p>19 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Slept all the</p>
<p>morning &amp; cleaning harness</p>
<p>in afternoon. I was on</p>
<p>stable picket at night. It</p>
<p>was very quiet all day.</p>
<p>There was a battery turn</p>
<p>out in evening but being</p>
<p>on picket I did not go out.</p>
