
July 2ndBeautiful surroundings - Aircraft very plentiful. Bad state of Hospital etcBig British offensive begins with great success. I journey to see Ed Cornish but without success. I view a "Taube chase".-
July 3rdI see most of the town which is an important military base - Owing to C.C.S. being numerous the town has a great number of K.C. Cars
July 4thReceipt of home letters - The Big Offensive continues. Mother and Fathers "Silver Wedding").
July 5thY.M.C.A. visited also R.C. Church near our billet - Work as usual
July 6th"Soccer" again - Y.M. & Canteen - British still doing well.
