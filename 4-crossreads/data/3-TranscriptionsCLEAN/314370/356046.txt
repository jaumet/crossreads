  
 June, 1916 Wednesday 28 12 instructors were sent on the H.Q. staff of the CTB & BD,  we have here a splendid mess which sells splendid cheeroots. 
 Thursday 29 This morning there was a huge split up of the camp.  All details were cut up into Bdes. ready to go to their units. about 4 of us are now without a job for which we are truly  thankful.
