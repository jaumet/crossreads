<p>13<br/>And swaying above us oscillating slings of ammunition, and cases of bully beef and biscuits etc.</p>
<p>Here we are in the second shed</p>
<p>Horses!</p>
<p>Yes horses for France</p>
<p>All kinds of horses, they have just come in from the country by rail, and will soon be shipped for duty at the Front</p>
<p>They are wanted for the guns, for general service for the Red Cross, for munitions.  In a word for the numberless lumber</p>
