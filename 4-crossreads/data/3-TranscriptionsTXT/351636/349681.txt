 The Centre 4-1-16 4-1-16 
 Dear Mr. Layton &ndash; Mrs. Main the bearer is seeking assistance for the payment of debts. She owes &pound;7 rent Grocer &pound;2 Milk 10/- She can manage on her pay if these are paid. 
 Perhaps you can help She will explain her case to you. 
 Yours faithfully B. Booth.
