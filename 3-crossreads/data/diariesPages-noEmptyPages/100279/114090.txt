There has been a lot of rivalry lately between A. C Coy. over their platoon drilling etc so this morning each platoon selected their 6 best men they were put in a "special squad" they shaped real well, so there is a promise of a competition coming off.
A few of us went over to the Y.M.C.A. hut tonight to see Charlie Chaplin a concert which was being run by Old Mac. What a man he is a man can't give him too much praise absolutely the finest man in the Division
