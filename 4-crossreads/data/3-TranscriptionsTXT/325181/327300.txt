Inspected Bazaar at Cairo paid guide 10 piastres & driver 4 piastres. Little or no news from The Dardanelles. Drill about 51/2 hours & one hours lecture.20th Parade 6 am & 5pm Lecture. Plenty of ices & drinks. Tucker plain but fairly good. Had a look at Heliopolis (or new Cairo) Some splendid buildings but roads & streets as yet only half formed.
