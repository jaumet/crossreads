<p>Tuesday. 3rd Nov<br/>Most of the men left the barracks to bring in a wireless pole. At two o'clock two parties left here, -  one (so I believe) to raise the British flag at Petershafen &amp; the other as a punitive expedition to Alexishafen. Only two men were taken out of "H" &amp; of course I was not one of these.
