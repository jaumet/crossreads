<p>WIZERNES<br/>Friday 27<br/>Sunrise on the western front this morning is beautiful, the sky is one blaze of color's. "Glorious Gully adeiu"<br/>Saturday 28<br/>Now camped between between Thiepval &amp; Beaumont-Hamel. Had a swim in the river Ancre at dawn the coldest thing I've ever struck<br/>Sunday 29<br/>A.D.C Co's entrained for the north yesterday, B Co staying behind on railway work weather very hot and the boys dead beat<br/>Now in the train bound for goodness knows where passed through St Pol Doullens eventually arriving at St Omer and marched to Arques</p>
