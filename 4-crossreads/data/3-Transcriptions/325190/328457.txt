<p>Clough &amp; Eva went to Hazebrouck in the afternoon &amp; thereby <strike>had</strike> nearly had a little bombing experience.<br/>Soon after we got to bed in our crowded tent we heard the distant sound of bombs bursting, then the whine of aeroplanes &amp; finally the noise of anti aircraft guns.<br/>The Boche was on his nightly raid over the <strike></strike>district (he missed our first night here as it was stormy).  The bombs were dropped round <strike></strike> &amp; seemed to get nearer, but after a time the Boche seemed to be driven off.  It was amusing to hear all the boys on the country side barking loudly: they had evidently experienced the sound of bombs before</p>
