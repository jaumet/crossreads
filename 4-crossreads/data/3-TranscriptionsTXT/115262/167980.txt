 Wednesday 1 August 1917. 
 The camels again.  After breakfast equipment fixed up for 4 hrs. march into desert. &nbsp; 
 In Cairo again last night. Went to Anzac Hostel, Y.M.C.A. &amp; Br. Soldiers Home. It was a pleasure to be served with supper in the Y.M.C.A. in Esbekia Gardens, by the British ladies who were very polite. 
 A biplane came to grief within 30 yds of our section this morning&nbsp;Pilot escaped with his life. Machine smashed to pieces. 
  Pilot died later from shock . (1/5/18) CCF&nbsp;
