Unless promotion comes here, the job, although very easy & delightful, is not worth keeping on with.  More hardships & more to gain would be better. News arrives tonight of a visit tomorrow from Sir A. Murray the G.I.C. & as H.R.H. the P. of W. is on his staff, he will probably come too.
