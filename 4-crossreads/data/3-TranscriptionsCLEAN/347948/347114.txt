  
 The next thing we saw was an old disused well, covered over by thatched roof with a wheel by which a bucket was drawn up. We then crossed over some private ground and entered a wood. These woods are only a small portion of the original forest, and seem like islands on the grassy plains.  They are very thick and present a very picturesque appearance.
