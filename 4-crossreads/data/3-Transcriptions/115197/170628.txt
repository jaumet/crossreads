<p><u>May 28th</u></p>
<p>Reveille 6.30.</p>
<p>Account of Hawker&#39;&#39;s &amp; Grieve&#39;s rescue &amp; return.</p>
<p>Australians to the front!</p>
<p>Stirring scenes.</p>
<p>At 11 a.m. we draw out from the wharf at Devonport and sail slowly down the harbour of Plymouth.&nbsp; While our band plays patriotic airs - &quot;Auld Lang Syne&quot; .&nbsp; &quot;Good-byee&quot; and lumps rise in our throats (for after all we are human &amp; have human sympathies).</p>
