 2 October 1918 Wednesday 16 October 1918 Wednesday Started the day by missing the morning train. Caught the 4.45 P.M. &amp; went to Nowra &amp; out to Selwood reaching there about 11 P.M. feeling rather tired. Saw reinforcement march through City Good news from all Fronts. 
 1918 October 17 Thursday A very warm windy day. Got up late and the principal event I think was that Arch and I went fishing and caught two fish about this length [drawing] Good news continues to come from all Fronts.
