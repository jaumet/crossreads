 Wednesday June 12 Heard rumour to day that our division may be going to France. Causing mild excitement. Thursday June 13 Saw Monastry built on old site of Solomons gardens. Parts of the old garden still intact. Moved out from Solomon's Pools to Telat El Doon [Talat ed Doon] at 5pm. Sorry to leave this camp. Passed through Jerusalem about 10pm arrived Telat el Doon[?] 7 AM.
