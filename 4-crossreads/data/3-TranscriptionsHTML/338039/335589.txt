<p>Mar. 26th<br/>Sabbath- Mass in village church- service in French- underclothing &amp; blanket issue (most acceptable) - rain- "caf&eacute;" again.<br/>Mar. 27th.<br/>Inspection of our 7th. Brigade by General Sir Douglas Haig. C.I.C- village street- Sir Douglas said: - I am very pleased with 7th. Bde and I am sure they will acquit themselves excellently.  We move from school to Chateau.<br/>Mar 28th.<br/>Billeted in barn over stable</p>
