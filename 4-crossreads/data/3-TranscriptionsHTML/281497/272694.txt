<p>That done we went &amp; had a wash &amp; got sopping wet through on the job, we had to haul our water about 150 ft up a well on a windlass &amp; when we had just got it started on the way up, I'm hanged if a heavy shower didn't fall on us we could not let the bucket go so we wound it up &amp; got wet through.</p>
<p>That done we come back &amp; had some tea, which consisted of bread &amp; water for that was all the rations we had brought with us, some tea wasn't it, but one a soldier</p>
