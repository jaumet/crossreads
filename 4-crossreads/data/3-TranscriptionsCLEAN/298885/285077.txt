  
 Sun July 2nd - 16 Had rather an exciting time down at OP with snipers. Raining very heavy. 
 Tues July 4th - 16 At shortest notice we were ordered back to Doulieul to be called on for mobile action 
 Still raining 
 Wed July 5th - 16 Ordered into action twice today, but at each time when everything was prepared the order was cancelled. 
 Still raining 
 Thur July 6th - 16 Inspection in full order by Colonel Burns. 
 Still raining
