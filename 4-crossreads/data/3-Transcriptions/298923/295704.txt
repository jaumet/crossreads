<p>7 February 1918 Thursday<br/>Regimental Sports postponed.<br/>Sports postponed owing to rain    inches fell.<br/>Paid all hands, am president of a Court Martial tomorrow &amp; still doing Topographical work under Capt McLean the Chairman's son- <strike>quite</strike> a good chap-<br/>Sent a party to collect BOMBS left by the Turks as we don't want accidents on Sports Day- &amp; many varieties lie about with half buried Turks.  Our own men &amp; N. Zealanders are reverently buried a wooden cross at their head &amp; Records kept</p>
