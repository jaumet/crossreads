<p>Sunday 13 May 1917<br/>Had a bath down at the Wadi, usual routine, &amp; grazing horses, still on outpost.  A couple of Abduls came in.</p>
<p>Monday 14 May 1917<br/>Still on outpost, wrote several letters, had a shower of rain at night, got parcels from Mother &amp; Enid.</p>
<p>Tuesday 15 May 1917<br/>Still on outpost, showery in morning.</p>
<p>Wednesday 16 May 1917<br/>On outpost all day, relieved about 4.30 by B.103, came into our old camp.</p>
<p>Thursday 17 May 1917<br/>Hot day, horses out grazing, they stampeded, had a fine gallop after them, got letter from Jess.</p>
<p>Friday 18 May 1917<br/>Very hot &amp; windy, took horses out on grazing.  On Picquet at night.</p>
<p>Saturday 19 May 1917<br/>Awfully windy &amp; dusty &amp; hot day, went over to the F.A. to get my hand dressed.</p>
