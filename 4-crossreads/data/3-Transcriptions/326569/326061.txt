<p>Saturday 26/5/17<br/>Beautiful day &ndash; warm yet mild.  Only &frac12; days work.  After dinner took washing to laundry &amp; then went down town with Dawson.  Posted Wareham History &amp; London maps to Bourke, N.S.W. &amp; sent some money to C/wealth Bank.  After tea went down town again &amp; had good look around.</p>
<p>Sunday, 27/5/17<br/>Reveille at 6.15 a.m. but stopped in bed till 7 a.m. as it was Sunday.  No parades at all today.  Rained heavily all day long &amp; could not go out anywhere.  Tea time it cleared up so walked to Stoborough.</p>
