 24-1-17 Wed 24th I have a badly swollen throat &amp; the Dr was thinking seriously of sending me off to Field Ambulance this morning but decided to give me till tomorrow to show an improvement; Two English Officers were out looking over the country today, a Taube flew over us today very high &amp; received a warm welcome at El Arish from the Anti Air Craft guns. 
 A few more Turkish deserters came in today
