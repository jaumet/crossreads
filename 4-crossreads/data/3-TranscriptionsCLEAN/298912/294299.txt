 23/1/16 Just before daybreak the sky around the east was of a beautiful pink & golden colour that showed off the land well. At 7 am we drew into Colombo Harbour which is recognised by its lighthouse that shows three successive beams at about three or four seconds interval. A pilot came out from the pilot station on the breakwater & at 8 am we entered the Inner harbour & anchored to two buoys 
                "Colombo" ([Ceylon pop. 7 million) 
 The water & coal barges soon came alongside & began filling the tanks with water & the bunkers with coal. Bumboat men too, like a lot of crows gathered (Took 2, 000 tons coal 900 tons water)
