<p>OUDERDOM.<br/>Friday 5<br/>Winter's first appearence cold winds heavy rain and of course mud. Tonight the 5th Brig. releive the 6th Brigade<br/>Saturday 6<br/>Sleeping in a pool of water for the rain as submerged my dwelling, "ah" it's awful but it's got to be done<br/>Sunday 7<br/>Sunday 7th, and so far the worst day I have experienced in my life. For we packed the rations on horses &amp; led them to Anzac House through mud waist high and blinding rain and shell fire</p>
