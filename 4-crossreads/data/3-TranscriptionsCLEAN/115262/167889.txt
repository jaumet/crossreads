  
 Wednesday 2 May 1917. 
 Reveille 6.30. No physical jerks.   
 In full marching order left camp at 8.26 a.m. Spell 5 mins. Trawool Bdge at 9.25. Trawool Hotel 10. a.m. Left 10.15 a.m. Spell 5 mins. Kerrisdale 11.24 (station) 1.8 p.m. Homewood. Dinner here.   
 Now gone 19 miles. One 11 mile non stop walk.  Nothing to eat since breakfast .    
 2.26 pm. onward to Yea. (Breaking up of the boys.) 4pm arrived in Yea. Decent reception. Supper & bed 10.30  
 Too tired to sleep. Capt Heath & 20 others (myself included) slept in Shire Hall. (Got cup of tea at Homewood at a farmhouse on the way up.)
