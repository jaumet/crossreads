<p>18/2/17</p>
<p>This is quite a nice place to visit. I have walked here twice. I will take Ellis over one day. It is about 50 minutes walk over the Fields. Note the thatched Cottage. The Smithy Shop is very very old &amp; draught &amp; dilapidated.<br/>Harry</p>
