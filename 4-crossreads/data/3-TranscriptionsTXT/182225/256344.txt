 1916 &nbsp; SUNDAY 13 &nbsp; AUGUST 
 Very windy &amp; fairly warm. Went with the Presbeterians to Church at No 3 YMCA hut in morning &amp; then wrote some letters. Wrote some letters in afternoon and had a sleep till tea. Went up to the Artillary School of Instruction to see Warren with Bill &amp; Jack Hunter. We went for a walk round Durrington Camp ect after &amp; Warren came back with us. 
 Wrote Linda, Jo, Jessie, Nan (Coomoo Coomoo) M rs &nbsp;Mac Father and Alan
