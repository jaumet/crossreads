<p>Miss Lena Ashwell's concert parties of which there are a considerable number, often play at the Y.M.C.A's and we spend many enjoyable evenings there.</p>
<p>6th  Playing Batt. out to all day stunts.  While the battn work we go out getting filberts &amp; raspberries.<br/>Cec. said "If only our people could see us now they would think we were all right".</p>
<p>9th  Went to Hastings for 2 days and had a ripping time with Kit   Went all over Hastings</p>
