 April 3rd A trip out to Fred's lodging &ndash; A rehearsal of a "Breezy Morning &ndash; A new acquaintance &ndash; two shows &ndash; Bed after midnight &ndash; Previous to retiring we meet &amp; converse with American sailors off the Leviathan (65.000 strong). April 4th M.M. &ndash; Voluntary entertaining to Wounded Pommies at a Liverpool Hospital &ndash; On our Bill at the Olympia are King &amp; Benson, Elliot Savonas, Tom Clare etc.
