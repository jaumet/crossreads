<p>The band was playing "the Girl in the Taxi". Women &amp; girls standing about in their best dresses &amp; every time the music softened one could hear the rat tat tat of the M.G. in the cloud above &amp; now &amp; again a sombre WE would burst miles above our heads  It made a curious scene.</p>
