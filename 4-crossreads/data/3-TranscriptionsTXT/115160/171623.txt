 Paris on the 12th October 
 My dear Sir: 
 I have received your kind letter there is a few days, and I am glad to know you in good health. 
 At the beginning of October, I have gone in at school again. &nbsp;I study chemistry, physics, natural history and principaly philosophy. &nbsp;Ths last matter, at the beginning, is very difficult to understand and&nbsp;to read.
