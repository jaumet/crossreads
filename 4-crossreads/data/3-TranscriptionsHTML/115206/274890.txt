<p>November 1918.<br/>Saturday 16</p>
<p>Hospital. Birmingham<br/>Weather fine and frosty<br/>Wired Roy for further particulars of transfer.<br/>Heard a wild rumor to the effect that 3rd Divvie was being demobilised.<br/>Went to Kings Heath village with N.<br/>Spent a good time up town with Sgt Gott.</p>
