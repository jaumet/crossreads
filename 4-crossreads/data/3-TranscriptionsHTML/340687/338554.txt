<p>Fine day sultry and warm. [indecipherable] on parade till 10 when Sinclair came with orders to be ready within 20 min in drill order with 1 ration Marched at 11 towards La Motte and after lunch worked hard digging dummy trenches 3' wide &amp; 9" deep until 7.30 when we arrived home very tired considerable excitement about moving but may be here for another week yet.</p>
