<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; My meals I took out always trying a new place, Dinner at Romanos, at the Maison Lyons, a Dinner &amp; a Lunch at Simpsons the modern survival of an old time coffee house; &amp; teas at fashionable little tea shops.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; Fairweather took me visiting a friend at Lancaster Gate Miss Portia Wheeler a Queensland heiress, she &amp; her<s>e</s> mother are <s>a</s> real friends to Anzacs in London &amp; particularly the Queensland Lads, who are her special care.</p>
