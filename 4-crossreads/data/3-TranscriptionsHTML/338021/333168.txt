<p>Lt Weston<br/>You will be relieved at about 7a.m. tomorrow morning (15th inst.).  Two sections will go up at about 1/2 hour intervals &ndash; Hand over everything except gun, Spare part wallet and Clinometers. On relief you will report here and your section will move to CANAL AREA.<br/>Cheers</p>
<p>CF Duchatel Capt<br/>15/10/17</p>
