<p>2 miles from there and later was transferred to a Red Cross Motor Car.</p>
<p>My first stop was to arrange a splint on my leg. From here put into the car and taken I think to the Clearing Station at Poperinghe Here my clothes were taken off me. The Orderly giving me a shoulder strap as a souvenir Later on in the day I was moved in to be operated on.</p>
<p>The operating theatre was full of cases being repaired. I had a look round before I was made unconscious The orderly I think said kindly not to look and put a handkerchief over my eyes. I was not long left to myself</p>
