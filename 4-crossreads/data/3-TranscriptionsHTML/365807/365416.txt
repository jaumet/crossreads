<p>city. My first thought when I got the news from A.I.F. H.Q. that Keith was doing well was to cable to you but then it struck me that you would already know everything. Keith seems dead cheerful as usual and I'd give the dickens of a lot to see him. But the Army's the Army &amp; leave is cut down to a minimum so as to dodge overcrowding the trains.</p>
