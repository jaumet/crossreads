  
 [Wednesday 23 August 1916] 
  Days are   Days are  still  still warm even hot here,  I don't know how I'll get on with the cold of England but I haven't gone yet. My Chart is normal now  not so much sore back  , Pulse good every thing good- my wound was nothing to what some chaps got.
