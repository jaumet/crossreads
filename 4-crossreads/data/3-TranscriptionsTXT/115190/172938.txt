 3rd October 
 &nbsp;&nbsp; I crawl into the saddle &amp; we are off again just after daybreak.&nbsp; Through the choking dust again until at midday we reach Khan [Me?????] were we unsaddle on a stony ridge.&nbsp; I see the M O who marks me no duty &amp; I lay on my blanket &amp; suffer with the Turkish fever Spanish Flu or some outlandish disease  till next morning .
