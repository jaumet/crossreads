  
 up any old Weymouth boys meet youn. Roberts &  Escott. Packed saddles to leave on the 12th. 
 12th/Left Mera Matruh at 9 am., arrived at first well at 2pm. put down horse lines & camped for night, looking like rain plenty of good tucker some the S.M. has stored up & now wants to be rid of. not much spare rum come to light yet. I gues he knows where that went. 
 13th/ Travelled 17 miles to next well & camped for night got in at six pm 
 14th Up at 4.30 Issued
