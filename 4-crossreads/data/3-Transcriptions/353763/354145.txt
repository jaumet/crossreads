<p>Part I<br/>I hope you'll get this all right  -  You've a narrative outline of events &amp; what I've seen &amp; done  -<br/>Kiss the children  -  I'm always thinking about you &amp; the peaceful Glen Innes home  -  The contrast to all this grim armament is striking but this armament is the sole precursor &amp; the bedrock mainstay of peaceful homes thro our empire  -  There is but one feeling here &amp; that is that Germany has got to be thoroughly thrashed &ndash; at any cost &ndash; as one old sailor said when we hoisted the white ensign on our little launch "That'll take a lot of hauling down Sir  - "</p>
<p>We've no news of Belgium  -  We did here the Germans were near &ndash; Paris &ndash; 12 miles off  -  We're going to stop here I fancy &amp; our AE1 &amp; 2 mount guard  -  This harbor is I am convinced the crater of an enormous volcano &ndash; not extinct for a fire can be seen &amp; smoke on a hill top  -  Its huge &amp; its lips are like a bowl  -</p>
<p>Much <u>Love</u>  -  Fred  -</p>
