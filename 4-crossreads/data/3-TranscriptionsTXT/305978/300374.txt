
we mounted & started off for the hill in extended order, after going about a hundred yds bullets began to rain around & upon us. I thought it foolish to continue, but the officer did not till one of the boys were wounded, then he gave the order to retire, & about we went. I was soon out of that warm spot, if the enemy had held there fire till we reached the foot of the hill, they would have had some good shooting. Then we retired to the gully &
