10th. AugustShower early but cleared off later. Bath parade & clean clothes later. Mail rec'd.
11th. FridayWarned off at Bkfast time to report to Bgde Hqrs. for Course of bayonet instructions for one week.
Tommy Sgt Major of the Coldstream Guards very good indeed, got very stiff with exercises & play he gave us.
12th. SaturdayContinued instruction knocked off about 3pm till Monday. Concert evening.
