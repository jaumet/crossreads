<p>on our way home to Aussie.  Gramaphones peg quoits deck quoits reading &amp; various other forms of amusement in which the very fine piano plays a prominent part.</p>
<p>All the boys are as happy as larry.  The food on board is excellent and is easily the best we have ever experienced <strike>since</strike> in troopships.  Nurses &amp; officers fraternise a little more oftener than when they first came on board and it is highly amusing to watch a couple of lovebirds billing &amp; cooing.</p>
<p><u>12th</u> Our passage continues to be very smooth.</p>
