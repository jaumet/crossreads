 October-November 1915 Sunday 31 Windy. Quiet day. A church service was conducted on the side of the ridge about 6 PM 
 Monday 1 Our chaps settled in the firing line. We had to shift out of a dug &ndash; out to the let the 3rd Bn Machine Gunners in. Arthur Valentine &amp; I secured a good dugout on the opposite ridge. I am mess orderly for our section today
