
May. 19th.Self in Party to Bois Grenier.  Four miles from Ft. Rompu- British Guns very active near latter part of our march- "Oh! that screeching!"May 20th.Change of duties from S.B to Dressing Room- New Friends- "R.B."May. 21st.Sabbath- Roy develops & prints photo of self at Virgins Tree- "The Village of Bois Grenier"- Activity of Aircraft & Artillery- German bombardment as usual on Sunday.
