<p>have been a good few thousands sitting round about, there was also a fair sprinkling of French officers &amp; plenty of French women &amp; girls. I wonder what they think of the English style of amusement they were very interested &amp; highly delighted judging by their antics. Some of the fights were good, but they were a bit slow in getting the men into the ring.<br/>9th. Still hanging round &amp; no orders yet about going back, if we had any definite orders we could get a "pass" &amp; go into Le Havre, but as things stand at present, we have to stop in camp. What a difference over here in the bread, the military in France have no such thing as war bread they believe in giving the soldiers</p>
