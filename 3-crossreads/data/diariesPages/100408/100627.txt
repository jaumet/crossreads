After tea went into Winnezeele with Andy had drink each. Streets packed with men of 1st 3rd divisions. Sent Ppcards of Wissant to Australia.
29/9/17 Heavy gunfire audible all night, especially at about 5am. Fairly cold night. Cloudy. Up 7.30am breakfast 8am. Boot parade 11am had couple nails in my boots knocked out. No other parades but had band playing. Sharpened my bayonet. Some of the Lewis gunners Snipers left for trenches after dinner,
