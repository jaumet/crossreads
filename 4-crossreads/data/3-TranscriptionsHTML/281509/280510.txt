<p>1916 October 14<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Leave Remi Sidong Rest Station where we had 5 pleasant weeks. at 12.5 &amp; march to Steenvoored 1st stage to St Omer. arrive there at 3 p.m &amp; camp in a pretty open spot. Green hill &amp; running stream. thatched huts. Incident of Moylas &amp; Dickson fight. Bob. Cart. Mac. Jock &amp; self hunt for tea. C &amp; self lace shop. damoiselle miss the others. poor tea. back to lace shop. madeselle's bro.  Home to bed thatch waterproof.  Receive acknowledgement from Bank of our draft &amp; enclosing cheque book.</p>
