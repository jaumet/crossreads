<p>Red Cross letterhead as before.<br/>9.  in charge of the Medical Ward &amp; is equally good as a Nurse.  I hope that you told Billy Gosper our address.  Not one of those whom we know that You have told us were coming away have ever written to us.<br/>Fancy Eric Merrett getting away again.  I hope that He will look me up when He gets over here.<br/>Give Our regards to Nurse Lascelles.  She was a very good Friend to both of us when we at Miss Dunn's Dancing classes &amp; afterwards.  Give our Congrats to Annie &amp; our well wishes to poor Auntie France.  We think of them all but we have so</p>
