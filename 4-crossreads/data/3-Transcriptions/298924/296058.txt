<p>Monday 17 March 1919<br/>Red Sea<br/>Boat drill &amp; education classes but as the latter are voluntary, the average soldier thinks he has learnt enough.<br/>Fresh Breeze &amp; cooler.  We get up at 0530 &amp; have half an hour physical jerks.  It is a treat to eat good meat again -all N.Z. frozen mutton &amp; Beef as the Ulimaroa was last there.</p>
