<p>Viv Nell Jack Tony &amp; Carol Maude &amp; Mr McLoughlin.<br/>quite warm like a spring day</p>
<p>Monday 14/1/18<br/>last night was the first I have slept with my head out of the Blankets too warm.<br/>this morning there is 4 inches of snow on the ground &amp; the trees are smothered again What price such a climate. got all letters censored by Tub Mathews now Lieutenant &amp; just back yesterday from Blighty<br/>Posted them to day. also Parcel to Nell with Australias etc in. quite warm again &amp; sun shining at intervals. Cold as it is this is a grand spell we are having &amp; the Boys are looking fine &amp; fit expect we shall be going to Belgium again in about 2 weeks. I am having good time doing nothing and all day to do it in.<br/>deferred Pay &pound;41-0-6. Owe &pound;7-19-0</p>
<p>Tuesday. 15/1/18<br/>Pay day to day but I draw nothing. Owe &pound;7-17-0 deferred Pay. &pound;41-2-0<br/>was out at 2 a.m. Snow everywhere when I got up this morning not a sign of it slight rain during the night melted it all. Windy but not Cold. Pat going to Blighty to day luck beggar. The day turned out awful a Hurrican Blowing &amp; Raining like hell also Thunder &amp; lightening which means more snow what a country getting much colder.</p>
<p>Wednesday 16/1/18<br/>An awful night Rain &amp; Gale, still going strong but not cold did not go to Canteen for first time last night &amp; they had stout in too, d.-------- talk about a Brothel this morning though.  Boys getting paid today. deferred &pound;41-3-6 Owe &pound;7-15-0.</p>
