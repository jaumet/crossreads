<p>Tuesday 16/10/17&nbsp; Leave to England turned down&nbsp; went &amp; saw Collett re. same.</p>
<p>Wednesday 17/10/17. On Court of Inquiry re. train stopping at Hell Fire Corner instead of proceeding to Birr X Roads [Birr Cross Roads]&nbsp; Dinner with [Fernie?]</p>
<p>Thursday 18/10/17. Left by train to Hazebrouck&nbsp; there caught leave train arrived at Boulogne at 2 am Friday.</p>
<p>Friday 19/10/17. at Morleys for lunch after good trip across&nbsp; met Hardie &amp; had dinner with him.</p>
