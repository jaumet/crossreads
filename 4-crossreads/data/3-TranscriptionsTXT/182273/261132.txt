 May &nbsp; 1918 
 26 SUN &nbsp;Fine day. Went into 
 Cassell in aft. Had 
 a very fair time 
 Home 9.PM French troops 
 in galore. Rec. letter from 
 Amy. Quiet night. Heavy 
 firing at daybreak. 
 27 MON &nbsp;Fine day. Windy. 
 Heavy firing all day. Rec: 
 letters from Else P. Gladys C.&nbsp; 
 Sgt: Clarke arr: back. Writing 
 at night. Few bombs 
 28 TUES &nbsp;Fine day shelled out of 
 Wagon lines 
 by quick Dick. Camped about 
 a mile away for the night. 
 Went into Hondeghem at 
 night. Quiet. 
 29 WED &nbsp;Fine day. Moved to new 
 Wagon lines near Hondeghem in 
 aft. Quiet. fixing up camp 
 Few bombs. heavy firing 
 at night.
