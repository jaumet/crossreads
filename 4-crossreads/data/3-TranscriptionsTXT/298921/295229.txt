
September, 1915Wednesday 15See in Paper you send me of Diary - "Personal notes" - Would it not read better if "Censored" was published for all deleted stuff - sound more interesting anyway!!!!Went for drive in [indecipherable] to a French Cafe; voluble, volatile, & versatile. Lager beer (cold boiled egg, cucumber, Tomatoe & bread all served at little table with drink & much "Vive Allies" - Then threw 1/2 Piastres into Nile for the Arab waifs to fight for.Getting more able each day, but wound still discharging -Love to all F.W.
