 JANUARY 1917 
 25 THURSDAY 
 Fine &amp; sunny, but cold. I was 
 on stableman all day. The 
 right section got some 
 ammunition ready to take up. 
 It was the first we were 
 able to draw for three days. 
 There was a very &quot;big bombard&quot; 
 last night but have not 
 heard any result yet.
