<p>Nov 17</p>
<p>Tues 17th<br/>Had 8 natives alongside diving for silver, &amp; they got plenty of it, a lad of about 17 or 18 years of age dived twice off the bridge for 5/- each time.  even half crowns were thrown overboard for them to dive for &amp; needless to say they missed nothing, when they had a pounds worth of silver they would come aboard &amp; change it for a soverign,  they had quite a mouthful of sovereigns when they left here.  we were about the last boat they visited.   apparently they made a little fortune,<br/>their boats did not appear to be very expensive as the consisted of 3 logs roughly cut out, &amp; bound together at each end by a piece of rope; their paddles are flat pieces of board shaped something like a boomerang; but it is wonder how they can row them along as they</p>
