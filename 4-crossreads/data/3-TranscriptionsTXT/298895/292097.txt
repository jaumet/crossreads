We marched a short distance and then took motors in which we journeyed to "Lumbres" about 8 miles from St. Omer.  The trip was very interesting it all being new country to us.  Fixed up billets etc. by dinner time & had the remainder of day free. Our mess very good, a room in our billet.Tuesday 7th.  Orderly Sgt for day.  Bearers marched out to attend a sham fight in the training operations.  Village of "Lumbres" about the best we have been in.  People of good class & estaminets etc high class Liquors Benedictine whisky etc sold freely in marked contrast to other places, the regulations not being enforced to any
