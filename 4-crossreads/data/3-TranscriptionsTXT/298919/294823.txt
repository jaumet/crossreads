 We walked down two especially beautiful lanes Frognal Avenue &amp; Burnt Oak Lane.  Along each side of the road were hedges &amp; trees the tops of which overhung the road many feet in the air.  We walked through the golf course where many people were enjoying the delights of that very fascinating game.  We walked leisurly about enjoying the delights of the countryside immensely.  As Mr Newton had not arrived when we reached home we walked down to the Golf Club.  He had not returned, but Witt had met several of the members so he introduced me to them.
