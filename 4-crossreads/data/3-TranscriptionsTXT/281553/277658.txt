 Mrs. Sanders, Eileen, very good gifts including sweets, books, underwear, soap &amp; hdkfs.  Action quiet all day &amp; night. 
 Nov. 8th Monday Very much colder &amp; duller.  Front quiet. 
 Nov. 9th Tuesday Weather fair.  Front quiet.  Naval small craft active especially about midday.  Cruisers firing heavy broadsides during afternoon onto Gun ridge. 
 Nov. 10th Wednesday Rain threatening.  Turks busy with hand bombs about 10 a.m. on Posts 3 &amp; 6, quietened by Garland &amp; Catapult.
