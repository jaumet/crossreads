  
 we arose at 6. am..passed an Island at 9am supposed to be Point Hells; a few houses on it & wireless station, light house. we saw a shark that looked quite 20 ft long. The sea is lovely & calm here. We had to fall in at 10am. to be inspected by our OC passed a chalk Island with a lovely looking lighthouse upon it. Two small steamers passed us at this point . A Boxing contest passed the evening away & we gave a few good coo-ees to a passing ship. 
 27/ Nice day. Three boats passed.
