 16 August 1916 Wed Geraghty from Enmore comes into hospital. Sgt Casson very bad. Sgt Bates also comes in later in day. Suffering with bad headache nearly all day.  17 August 1916 Thur Steve Jeffs &amp; Arthur Preston leave the old B Section being made sergeants in C Section. Good luck to them they deserve it. Major Aidy puts me in charge of the medical ward of fifty patients. Keith Harris comes to work with the letters received from Joe &amp; Fred Strong.
