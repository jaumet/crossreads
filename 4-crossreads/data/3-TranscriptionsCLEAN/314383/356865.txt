  
 May 6th Today we had a short march & three regular meals for the first time since we took the track, usually we wait until we reach camp before having a meal & as water regulates the camping grounds.  We often do 16 - 20 miles before or between meals.  We were also able to purchase a quantity of eggs. Dick Hooper assisted by a Sgt. does the Q.M. job & generally rides ahead & purchases extra food for the party on arrival at camp
