
Dec. 30thAs a preliminary to moving into the line we handed in our felt hats & all blankets but one, to the QM store.  To me, naturally, the move was full of interest: my curiosity about the line will soon be satisfied.  The boys, who have noted my anxiety to wander forward, tell me I will soon know all I want to know.  To tell the truth I don't  think I would be sorry if the trip would in were put off a day or so; yet in spite of a kind of apprehensive feeling, I know I would also feel disappointed.
