<p>Tuesday 22 August 1916<br/>All hands enoculated against Cholera again.  Got three letters.  One from Mrs. Nicholl &amp; one from Aunt Katie 15th May.</p>
<p>Wednesday 23 August 1916<br/>Day stable-man for D Troop.  Wet canteen opened also A.I.F. canteen.<br/>An awful lot of men got tight but were not very unrully.</p>
