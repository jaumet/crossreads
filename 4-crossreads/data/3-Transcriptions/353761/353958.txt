<p>(27)<br/>Of some buildings there is nothing left but a slender towering corner of brickwork.<br/>Of others the end walls and a broken backed roof alone remains.<br/>The most common feature of all this fearful destruction is the tearing away of a complete side or front of a building; something of which I have already mentioned. Look where you will and you are staring into the poor broken rooms of once sacred homes.<br/>You can see the thickness of broken walls and depths of floor joists and you are strangely reminded of the sections of architectural designs.</p>
