We had to move off for the Sinaii Peninsular Side, &amp; had to cross the Suez Canal Bridge, which is closed for a certain number of hours each day, this made it very hard. On arriving at our Camp which is situated in the middle of the Desert we had to unload, erect Tents, &amp; get things in readiness for further work.  I will close now &amp; continue tomorrow, as I have been 48 hours up with no sleep &amp; very little food. 
 11 Thursday Description of Serapeum This place is situated on the Sinaii Peninsular, or one might say Arabia.  It is of course very hot, being situated in the Desert, in fact it is almost unbearable. 5 miles from here is the Railhead, &amp; out from that is where our army is entrenched. Very heavy fogs fall during the early hours of the morning &amp; it causes much anxiety
