<p>[Tuesday 5 December 1916]</p>
<p>Inspecting our Mess supply this morning I noticed the Beef came from the ARGENTINE<br/>Word has just come from Regiment for me to go very close to Al Arish you will see it on Map.  I am writing &amp; posting this from Moascar near Ismalia-<br/>I am claiming 25/-/- from Egyptian Railways for lost Kit<br/>Am letting [indecipherable] tonight.</p>
