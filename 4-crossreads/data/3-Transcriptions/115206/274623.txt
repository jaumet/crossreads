<p>February 1918<br/>Friday 22</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Walked down to F.O.O. and "took" Jones dinner. Jones is going to another Battery.<br/>Had a great feed last night and tonight. I was chef and dished up, Soup-a la Oxe-des Ra, Turnips, Mashed &ndash;a la Bully Beef, and Xmas Pudding and choice almond flavoured custard. Had the 10-12 and 6-8, so had my share of "Yakker".<br/>Fritz very quiet.<br/>Wrote to London for Blue,bhewars</p>
