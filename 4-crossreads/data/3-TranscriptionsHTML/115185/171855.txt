<p>Goodbye my blessed parents. I think of you all the time. I am very well - &amp; not tired by my two hard days work in the heat &amp; sand. Others as I have told you suffer from their voddies.<br/>Enteric is rife. Almost 300 cases at the hospital where I was.<br/>Devotedly your daughter.<br/>Betha McMillan.</p>
