<p>time I do not think anyone had much water left owing to the great thirst produced by the heat and excitement.<br/>At 4 p.m. I should say we were about three miles inland but finding that the Turks were being strongly reinforced we commenced to retire.  My company captain was killed in this movement and also many of the wounded had to be left behind.  Four of us carried one badly wounded man right back to the beach.  He died just before we laid him on the sand.  The situation now looked very black and we had visions of being swept into the sea.
