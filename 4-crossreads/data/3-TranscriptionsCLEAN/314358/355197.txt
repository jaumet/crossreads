Assistants round them are patiently carrying our orders with the same care and attention that nurses show to the sick
Shattered bones of steel are removed and replaced by new. Crumpled wounded parts of iron and brass are taken away and new parts put in place
Twisted joints are righted.  New wheels are put in place of the old and splintered
Adjustments are finally made during convalescence, and the brave old guns go back to France once more with the brilliance and paint and burnish of youth.
