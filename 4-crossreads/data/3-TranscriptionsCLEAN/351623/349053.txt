 4 January 1819 Went back to Devonport Barracks. Arrived at 10 oclock in forenoon & went out again at 12.30 in afternoon for weekend leave till 7 oclock on Monday morning 
 5 January 19 Ashore in Devonport. 
 6 Jan 19 Came into barracks again at 7 oclock & went ashore again for afternoon & all night leave.
