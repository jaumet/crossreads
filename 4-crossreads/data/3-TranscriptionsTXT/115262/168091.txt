 Tuesday 20&nbsp;November 1917. 
 &quot;Stand to&quot; ar 4 am (a kind of punishment for not turning out yesterday morning.) 
 Picquet 6 to 8 am. 
 Breakfast and started to write letters. 
 French Jews bring bread, eggs, cake &amp; wine &amp; brandy round on sale to the troops. 
 Mail to hand. Letters as late as 23rd Sept dated in Australia. 
 Cold &amp; wet day. Very bleak. 
 Wrote a letter tonight.
