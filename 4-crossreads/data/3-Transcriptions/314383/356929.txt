<p>We then rode on to the others &amp; found Nicol doing the same on his flank.  The Armenians with us with the exception of 2 &amp; the Russian officer bolted.  I sent Murphy back to hurry up the loading while the rest of us formed a line on a ridge a little to the rear where we could command the flank &amp; protect the boys as they came out of the village.  We got into action twice on the left &amp; drove them off with M. G fire then we heard the gun in action in the village, shortly after they came into view leading the mules.  Murphy had the gun &amp; took up a position between us &amp; the village he was no sooner there when a crowd of horsemen galloped out.  Murphy knocked them back &amp; again they tried with the same result.  Then they got back &amp; shot down all the mules but fortunately the lads got clear.  Nicol  went to their assistance &amp; and was heard</p>
