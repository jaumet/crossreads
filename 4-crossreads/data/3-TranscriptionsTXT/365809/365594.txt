 Dear Judge Just a very hurried line with reference to my application to be enrolled as a Solicitor here, and my last letter to you thereon. 
 Apparently your certificate has to be in a set form, which I now enclose, together with certain regulations, which I have just received  - In haste Charles M Macnaghton
