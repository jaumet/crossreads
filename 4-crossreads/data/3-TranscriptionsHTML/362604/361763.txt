<p>Australian Red Cross<br/>Bulford<br/>12/10/18</p>
<p>Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George,</p>
<p>I received one stray letter from you to-day but the mail is very straggling this time.  We have had a long spell of showery weather but it is the usual weather here &amp; not remarkable &amp; we are used to it.
