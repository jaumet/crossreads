We got out at Neitoun at 5.30 &amp; I was by no means sorry as it was the worse trip I have had &amp; ever want, we saddled up &amp; was soon writ riding down to to the racecoarse that we left some weeks previous. We received a cheer from all the reinforcements in camp, we must have looked breakups, as most of our clothes were torn or worn out. We soon water &amp; feed up the horses.
