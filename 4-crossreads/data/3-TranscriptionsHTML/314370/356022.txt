<p>May, 1916<br/>Friday 5<br/>Judging distance test this afternoon.<br/>Heat is increasing every day.<br/>My class is getting on well with the work</p>
<p>Saturday 6<br/>Anniversary of MY landing on Gallipoli.<br/>Exam in description took up all morning.<br/>After dinner I went to the Pictures</p>
