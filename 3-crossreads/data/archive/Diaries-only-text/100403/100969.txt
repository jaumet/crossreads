February
5th. Our route lay Charleroi- Mons- Vallenciennes- Douai- Arras- Albert- Amiens- Paris. Y.M.C.A. to-night, Hotel Ostende near Opera.
6th Change quarters to Grand Hotel de la Malte, rus de Richeleu, which is very decent. With Morris Briggs. 
 This afternoon Louvre, Concorde Champs Elysees. To-night at Opera Comique hearing "Tales from Hoffmann."
7th The day in Latin Quarter. Notre Dame, Hotel de Ville, Palais de Justice St Chapelle, Gervais St Pres (struck by German shell) Pantheon, Luxembourg Gardens St Sulpice St Ettienne. Dinner at "Bisson" the
