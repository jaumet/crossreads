<p>(You might note that I'm getting distinctly uppish, once more?  The reason is simple that our horses have got 'pink eye' or influenza, and we've been obliged to retreat from the big battle &ndash; I mean our Brigade of batteries &ndash; and are going into winter quarters for a rest.)</p>
<p>I'm afraid the fighting spirit has died out of me a lot and have still got a considerable desire to see the Murrumbidgee plains once more:  so my motto now is to minimize risks.</p>
