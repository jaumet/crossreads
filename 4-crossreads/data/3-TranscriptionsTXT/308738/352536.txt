 Oct. 21 Thursday 1915 P. M. came &amp; interviewed Grice &amp; O. C. after which Grice was arrested &amp; taken to Mustapha. He was let out tonight on Bail. Went to hear a lecture on the Bible in many lands it was very interesting. 
 Oct. 22 Friday 1915 Working as usual P. M. came &amp; interviewed the O. C. again re Grice nothing definite done yet with him. Lieut Cunningham went to Cairo today.
