<p>Get your guns firing during the day by using the same direction but add 1 &deg; to your elevation &ndash; Fire two guns at a time using 1 belt per gun.<br/>Mount one gun during day for Anti-aircraft work and keep off any low planes.<br/>Rum sent by Carroll</p>
<p>CF Duchatel Capt<br/>OC 13th A.M.G. Coy.  14/10/17  7am.</p>
