 Nov 18/17 Samer &ndash; arrived here today after about 18 mile trek and are billetted on a farm about 1 mile out of the village. It is we are only 15 kilo from Boulogne. It is typical November weather &ndash; mild grey days 
 Dec 14/17 For the past month six of us have been billetted on the farm of Lance-Lelin, Breuil W. Samer &amp; it has been quite a home &ndash; The most kindly people we have met in France
