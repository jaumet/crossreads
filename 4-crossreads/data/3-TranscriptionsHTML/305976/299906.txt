<p>Saturday Aug 11th<br/>On duty<br/>Sunday Aug 12th<br/>On duty<br/>Monday Aug 13th<br/>Last night in Cairo before leaving for the line. Sent home parcels &amp; had a good time<br/>Thursday Aug 14th<br/>Inspection of unit by Major Buxton<br/>Wednesday Aug 15th<br/>Night ride on Camels. Saddling in dark. Rode 5 mile unsaddled &amp; saddled 10 minutes allowed. Capt. S. informed us if anybody had a spill they would hear from him. He was the only one who fell due to his Camel putting his foot in a hole. What Joy among the boys.</p>
