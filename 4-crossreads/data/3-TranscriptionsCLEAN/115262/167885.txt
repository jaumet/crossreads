  
 Saturday 28 April 1917. 
 Up at 8.30 am. & went into city.   F.C M never turned u p. With Frank Pratt  introduced me to    and L A. Bizley on the block. Had dinner at Y.M.C.A. Soldiers Club at 12.45 pm.   
 To Dandenong on 4.35 pm train from City.   
 Tea in Dandenong (Thomas St) & caught 7.30 train to Lyndhurst.   
 Bed at 12 midnight.
