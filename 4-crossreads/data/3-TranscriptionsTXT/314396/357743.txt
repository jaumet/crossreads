31st Oct 1914.Very funny thing happened today. No parade in the afternoon &amp;  4 of us were playing cards when one of the boys came in &amp;  through his white hat at one of us that went clean through the port hole without touching. Lost hat.
Sunday 1st Nov 1914.Left Albany at 6 o'clock for England   on the Sydney Menetor  Menator in the lead &amp;  Orvieto following.Church parade in the morning by Meth. Minister. Slept all afternoon after having a good read.Caught Orderly Corporal at 6 o'clock for next day until 6 o'clockTravelled 44 miles
Monday. 2nd. Nov 1914.Orderly Corporal until 6 o'clock.Sick in morning &amp;  again just after dinner. Had no dinner, but ate apples instead &amp;  of course got sick.Travelled 241 miles
Tuesday 3rd. Nov. 1914Didn't go on parade but stayed in bunk. Went up about 11 &amp; got inoculated again on right arm. Arm sore at night.Travelled 216 Knots. very  Rough.
Wed. 4th. Nov 1914.Attended not any parades again.Inoc. Went to neck &amp;  made it very sore.Not a bad day. Big roll. Travelled 219 Knots. Bit tired of things in general to-day. Will attend parades tomorrow &amp;  break the monotony of things.Had a very good concert in the Sergeants Mess in the evening. It was really A1. Went to bed at &frac14; to 11 the latest since being on boat.
