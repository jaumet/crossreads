 There is also a village adjoining this one called ["Ampeliers"?] &amp; we are five kilos from a large town called Doullins. 
 Mon Sep 4th To day we are resting but a few break into parties &amp; do a bit of exploring in the village &amp; find something good to eat &amp; drink. 
 We are standing by to continue our march. 
 Tues Sept 5th We still camp here &amp; fill in the time visiting the people &amp; making things agreeable. 
 We very likely move off at midnight. 
 Fri Sep 5th Still waiting orders to move. 
 I went with others to get a bath but we were unfortunate as they were not ready. 
 The guns are very active here &amp; can also be heard at Yepres. 
 I in Waratah camp I omitted to write that on Wednesday.
