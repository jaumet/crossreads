<p>At 7, Herbert Crescent<br/>London S.W.<br/>1.7.16</p>
<p>Dear Judge Ferguson,</p>
<p>Nothing I can  say will be of any comfort to you but I must write to tell you what we have lost in Arthur &ndash; if we had only had more officers like him things would be much brighter and better than they are now.</p>
<p>The Regiment has lost its best Company Officer &ndash; the ideal one &ndash; the company has last a parent &ndash; a fairly stern one says a</p>
