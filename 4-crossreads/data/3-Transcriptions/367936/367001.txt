<p>Thursday 29 June 1916<br/>Went into Alexandria.  Sent parcel home registered containing table clothe, sleeve links &amp; studs.<br/>Got films of Nouzha gardens, etc.</p>
<p>Friday 30 June 1916<br/>Went into Alex on my own.  Got receipt for registered parcel.<br/>Met Nelson came home in Garry.</p>
