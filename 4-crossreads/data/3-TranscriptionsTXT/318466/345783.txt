 Sunday 14 April 1918 Feelg. very dirty &amp; unshaven.  Reminds me of Gallipoli.  D. Corp captured 2 prisoners last night.  One died on the way down. 
 Monday 15 April 1918 Came down from the Bn. to the horse lines in Aubigny &amp; had a good bath &amp; the first shave for nearly two weeks!! 
 Tuesday 16 April 1918 We move up &amp; relieve the 53rd Battalion at Villers Bret.  The village reeks of gas &amp; we wear our masks all the time.  Some of my sigs. go out to it. 
 Wednesday 17 April 1918 Gassed &ndash; well &amp; truly!!! at Villers Bretonneux!!  Don't know much what happened after 8 a.m. 
 Thursday 18 April 1918 I was unconscious most of this day but remember being bandaged by a nurse in the C.C. Station. 
 Friday 19 April 1918 Admitted into No. 3 General &ndash; Le Treport. 
 Saturday 20 April 1918 For the next few days I lost all interest in life being blind &amp; a lot more hopeless things.
