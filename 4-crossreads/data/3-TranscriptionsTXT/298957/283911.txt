 9th Jan (Sunday) On guard all day. Big steamer Marwarri sailed down to Suez. 
 10th Jan (Monday) Issued with Jack Knives. Otherwise nothing of importance. 
 11th Jan (Tuesday) Digging trenches and dug-out over on the soft sand hills all day 
 12th Jan (Wednesday) Light Duties again. Preparations are now advancing rapidly on both sides of the Canal. Natives are busy cutting a roadway to the water edge, removing the sand in small baskets. Railway to Serapeum also advancing.
