
Tuesday 9th January 1917Arrived in Plymouth at 4am this morning. Left the Borda at 2pm by the steamer (Sir Walter Raleigh)   Left Plymouth 4-15pm by G.W.Rly. Stopped at Exeter for about 20 minutes (arr. there about 6pm) and were given tea and buns by the ladies of that city. Arr.Tidworth at 10pm then had a four mile march to Parkhouse Camp.
