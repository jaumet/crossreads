
Sunday 17/10/15Remained in bed till 8.30 AMLeft Camp about 12 am & had dinner at Blue Bird, caught train in afternoon for Kew, visited cemetery, returned by tram & went to Domain.  Had tea in town, Reg returned to camp ill.  While on leave Mr Sutton called.
Monday 18/10/15Ordinary Parades  At night visited Kew to see Mr Sutton. Met Mr & Mrs Reid, arrived back at camp 9.30 pm
Tuesday 19/10/15Parades as usual.  Remained in Camp at night
Wednesday 20/10/15Usual parades, went in to City at night
