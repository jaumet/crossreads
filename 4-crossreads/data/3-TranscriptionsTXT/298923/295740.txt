15 March 1918 FridayStill at the WadiRemounts came from Col : Mc Leans  today mostly Americans, we did hope to be relieved but no word yet, still cold &amp; wet.On Outpost again tonight- the Wadi opposite is lined with Jacko's but they leave us alone for the present, it is too cold &amp; wet for any fighting &amp; both sides are so tired out.We have been [indecipherable] but still have no Bivies, it may seem strange but 20 miles changes the climate here from the warm Dead Sea to the snow clad mountains.
