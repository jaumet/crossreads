  Feb. 10th  
 Miss Doris Scales (Niece of Mr & Mrs) arrives from Cambridge to spend week at "Elm Bank". 
 Morning and afternoon in London.  Booking for Theatres etc. 
 Visi residence of Miss Fitzsimmons but have no luck as she is not at home. 
 Reading & writing. 
  Feb. 11th  
 Another walk to "Parliament in the Fields" which was the original meeting place of the Wilena(?).
