  
 2 Army divisions 40,000 troops. British Army of occupation in Egypt. Territorials & Indian Regiments. N.Z.s & Hospital Uniting N.Z. & Aus. Australian Being summer while I was in Egypt. No. 1 A.G.H. at Heliopolis. The Ancient city of On now a suburb of Cairo 7 miles out. Huge palaces, one was built for a hotel but not a success - no licence with annexes. Luna Park a wonderland city. Men lying on matresses in the chutes & in the lower track of the scenic ry. Train right to Heliopolis. Also big Camp where Reinforcements & new Regiments were in the desert with glare heat dust flies - send light jam - [indecipherable] can see the flies better.
