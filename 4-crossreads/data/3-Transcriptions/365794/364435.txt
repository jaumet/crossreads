<p>Sunday 4 February 1917 [3 February 1918]<br/>Breakfast in bed, rose 9 a.m., interviewed C. Collins re arm, very pleasant, then Church All Saints, 11 a.m.<br/>Letters from Mother &amp; Nell, very full of our trouble, life is indeed sad for us with this coming in &amp; how difficult it all might have been.  Spence is back again after 3 years of A. [active ?] service, very sad home coming in many ways.<br/>Letters from dear Dr. F. &amp; T.A.P., how I should love to see the "Lord" again.</p>
