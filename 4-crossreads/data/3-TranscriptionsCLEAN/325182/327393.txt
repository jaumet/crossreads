
We have not had any official news of events from up there for a couple of days.
We were relieved from our position up last night and came back to a reserve.  Our Hqrs. now are in a house fine Chateau.  We were called out last night and all hands were ready for the Hun to come across.
He didn't come over though and we all returned and had a sleep.  It has been misty all day driving rain and wind and bitterly cold.  Sleep is a grand thing.  Pure unadulterated sleep is better than
