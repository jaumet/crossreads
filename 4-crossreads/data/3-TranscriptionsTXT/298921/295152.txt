 June, 1915 Wednesday 30 Last night I visited our wounded in No 1 A.G. Hospital Hospital which was the PALACE Hotel a great Marble Palace I have mentioned before &ndash; Now filled to overflowing with maimed humanity &ndash; Poor fellows some will never walk or see again.  Letters from Fennor Dalgety &amp; Com good sale of Dungalear [indecipherable]  We are all working hard Reveille 5. Squadron drill till 9 &amp; then the sun is too hot. Lectures etc till Midday &amp; then out again in the evenings &ndash; Not been going to Cairo lately.
