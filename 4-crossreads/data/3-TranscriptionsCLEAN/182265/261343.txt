  
 July 
  Aug  22nd Sunday --- 
 In camp at Codford. A Beautiful day. The 25th reinf. of 13th. Bn. (142 men & officers) arrived here on Friday night. Yesterday afternoon we had Group Bomb & Bayonet Fighting Sports. The 4th T.B won the Bayonet Fighting which went to 16th. Bn. & also the Bombing. 4th. T.B. won 1st. 2nd. & 3rd prizes for Bomb throwing & tied for 1st. in Rifle Grenade work - the 1st & 2nd. throwers being from 13th. Bn. & the 3rd. from the 15th.  
 Beautiful weather all week.
