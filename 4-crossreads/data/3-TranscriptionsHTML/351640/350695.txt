<p>Availing themselves of the brightness and the glory of the evening Colonel Braund and a large party from here have gone for a night picnic to the pyramid.  Good luck to them, may they enjoy themselves!</p>
<p>Rumour is rife again that the Australians will soon be on the move from Egypt.  Wonder is there any truth midst the sentences produced by the lieing jade?  My anxiety to get away is not so great just now as it was three weeks ago,  because I am eating, and enjoying, plenty of food for me, the world was not much worth living in then, it is right enough today, yesterday &amp; let me hope for tomorrow.</p>
