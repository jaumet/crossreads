<p>9 April 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Three men still away.<br/>We go for a short forced march of about 4 &frac12; miles which we do in about 45 mins out   &amp;  40 m. back which is very fast over the sand<br/>Have a good shower as soon as we get in.</p>
<p>1915 April 10<br/>Saturday</p>
<p><u>Richies Birthday</u><br/>Quiet morning in camp for us but a bustle for the Bat.<br/>All their Kit bags are packed in the mess huts   &amp;  boarded up.<br/>I come back to camp from Cairo at 11 pm   &amp;  am warned for Guard to mount at 6.45 am on Sunday</p>
