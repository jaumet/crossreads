
<p>FEB. &nbsp; SUNDAY 20 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; cool. Helped, or tried to do a bit in the house in the morning. Linda &amp; Self lay under the trees at back of house all afternoon and read &amp; yarned &nbsp;We went for a walk together in evening round over the hill &amp; back by M<sup>rs</sup>&nbsp;[Rutgers&#39;?]</p>
