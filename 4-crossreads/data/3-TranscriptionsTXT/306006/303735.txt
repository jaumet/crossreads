13-2-17Tue 13thTold the Dr. this morning that I was fit for duty & he said he would get me away on the first train tomorrow & advise the Regt. Got dressed this afternoon & went to the E.F.Canteen & bought a supply of cigarettes & sauce for the lads here. Aeroplanes have been going out today loaded with bombs
