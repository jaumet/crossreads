<p>Thursday 20/12/17&nbsp; Still very cold &amp; frosty&nbsp; visited 10<sup>th</sup> Battalion.</p>
<p>Friday 21/12/17&nbsp; Park of USA left to rejoin his Regt. W came around the trenches.</p>
<p>Saturday 22/12/17. Visted by Gen&#39;l Birdwood, men working well</p>
<p>Sunday 23/12/17. Relieved by 28<sup>th</sup> at 8.20 p.m. three casualties coming out.</p>
<p>Monday 24/12/17. In catacombs, had a good sleep &amp; a bath.</p>
