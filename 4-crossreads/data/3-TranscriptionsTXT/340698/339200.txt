 29/5/17 Bde sigs. School started today 10 days course. 
 24/6/17 Bde church parade today. Gen. Birdwood and Walker were present issuing out decorations. I received my M.M. 
 29/6/17 Marched to Englebermer from Lavieville via Hellencourt and Senlis. We expect to remain here about 10 days for a course of field training.
