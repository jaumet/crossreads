 November 1918. Sunday 17 
 Hospital. Birmingham Weather wet but not too bad. Accompanied by Sgt we strolled to a recital by an orchestral society. The turn out was excellent. Had afternoon tea at the [indecipherable], only a small affair . Scaled wall and found a nice French Bed and pajamas all ready for me. Sister P. helped me to straighten out the muddle like a thorough sport.
