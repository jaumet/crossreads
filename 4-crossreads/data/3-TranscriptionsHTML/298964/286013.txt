<p>20 Sun May<br/>Awful night got smothered in dust could not see when I got up don't want to experience another like it</p>
<p>21 Mon May<br/>still blowing hard all day with dust the same&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;give me the desert  Very hot and dust bad  our New Planes very busy.  Good news from France</p>
<p>22 Tue May<br/>Getting warmer as days go by.  and flies they are thriving  simply eat a chap<br/>Very good shooting at Jacko with Air craft  French Gunners</p>
