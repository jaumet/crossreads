8 August 1918 Thursday  CairoThe "Package" is doing well I go out each morning &amp; afternoon &amp; do small commissions between whiles.  It is cooler down here than last month.  I told you am keeping Flat till Oct 15th  1st.   An Australian Mail is in but will be out at the Camp.Had dinner at the "Continental" with a Major man I know as I leave the Hospital at 6 at night.Enclose 2 bad snaps- Shots of Dolly &amp; the Bairn &amp; the Nurse taken inside to please the Mother, good ones will be sent later.
