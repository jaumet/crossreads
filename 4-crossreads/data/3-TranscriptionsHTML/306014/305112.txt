<p>Mch 17</p>
<p>Usual Saturday's route march in morning. Walked with Les Dimond &amp; his friend to Orcheston in afternoon &amp; there we bought a couple of bags of fancy biscuits &amp; sat down on the roadside &amp; shared them with three wee boys whom we met there. We had tea in Shrewton &amp; strolled back early in the evening.</p>
<p>Mch 18</p>
<p>Church Parade in the morning. After dinner I went alone for a stroll across the hills. The afternoon was glorious. The sun forgot his bashfulness, and ventured once more to brighten the hills with his brightness.
