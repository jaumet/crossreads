<p>was on board a train at Dover.  From Dover we left for Brighton Passing through <br/>Canterbury, Gillingham &amp; alongside the Medway till at 2 o'clock in the morning I <br/>found myself in a comfortable bed at <u>"Kitchener's No 3 Aus Hosp</u> Brighton</p>
<p>26th Jan I cannot sit up or lie on my back but am feeling O.K.  Have had another A.T.S. <br/>inoc which makes 3.<br/>Fed very well but will be glad when I can get up.</p>
