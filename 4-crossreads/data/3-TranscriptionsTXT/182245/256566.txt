FEBRUARY 1917
20 TUESDAY
Raining all day & its very
muddy & sloppy everywhere.
Cleaning harness ect all
day. Out exercising horses
before breakfast. Capt Campbell
inspected the stables in
evening & everything passed
satisfactorily. We had
to turn out in evening & take
ammunition up, being section
on duty. Got parcels from
C.S. Stores & Mother & Aunty Florence
