<p>Here all the feed you get<br/>Is Biscuit and some Bully wet<br/>They say the cooks have got us set<br/>Because we cannot pay</p>
<p>There is a happy land far far away<br/>Where all is peace &amp; quiet<br/>All the livelong day.<br/>Were you've got to duck like hell<br/>Bowing to the Turkish shell<br/>Busting with a rotten smell<br/>In a rotten Turkish Way</p>
<p>There is a happy land far far away<br/>Where you can get youre beer<br/>By handing out a tray<br/>And well get back there<br/>some day<br/>And we'll pay our little tray<br/>Well join for war again<br/>next day<br/>Oh yes I don't think</p>
