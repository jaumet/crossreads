
July 191711th   The baths were open for the company this morning & those who were not on duty went down, but unfortunately there was no change of underclothing to be had.   This afternoon I had to go & see the mayor of the town, for purpose to getting permission to fell about a dozen trees by the canal for piles.Later I went around with the "Forrest Guardian" & marked the timber I wanted.   A concert was held by the Div. Engrs. in the "Tivoli" to-night with great success.
