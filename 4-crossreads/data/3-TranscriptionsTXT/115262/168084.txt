 Tuesday 13 November 1917. 
 Moved off at dawn in a Nth-Nth Westerly direction till 10.30 am. Halted for a rest for camels &amp; a snack for ourselves. 
 3 miles now from line and our guns &amp; boys are making it hot for &quot;Jacko&quot; on this new front. 
 Camels watered &amp; fantasies filled. Turks held this position Esdud yesterday morning.  Left  
 We left at 3 p.m. and travelled till  dawn  dusk almost in sight of the village of Yebna. 
 &quot;Stand to&quot; all night.
