<p>W.T.Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship - Warrego</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 12 Sept 14</p>
<p>Time of Despatch or Receipt - 9-15</p>
<p>Commander Beresford per Warrego. &nbsp;The Brigadier requests that your party be withdrawn from WT Station to Kabakaul and then to Herbertshohe to form part of garrison there. &nbsp;Make certain that wireless is absolutely out of action before leaving station. &nbsp;0915</p>
