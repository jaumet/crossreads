<p>July 1918<br/>Wednesday 3</p>
<p>Vaux sur Somme.1st Bty Position.<br/>Weather glorious , fine and clear: Received a long letter from Mother. Wrote one or two to different folk.<br/>Final preparations made for tonight's stunt . Tom Code<br/>and Da Costa are to hop the bags tonight. I wish them<br/>both the very best of luck.  <br/>Georgee Baker and Fitters North to hospital with Trench<br/>fever.I <br/>am Assistant linesman in the company of Ken Gunn for the stunt.</p>
