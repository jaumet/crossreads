 Feb. 10 Wed. Worked till  11 p.m.  11 a.m. in office &ndash; then went off to Cairo on leave &ndash; about an hour's run in tram &amp; only costs 1 pastre. Walked through Ezbekieh Gardens &amp; visited British Soldiers Caf&eacute;, Roller skating rink, Head fire station &amp; Greek Boy Scout Headquarters, very smart lads &ndash; saw &amp; spoke to a B-P Scout &ndash; Had haircut &amp; visited Dentist&ndash; teeth alright &ndash; Arrived back at Mena at 7.15 p.m.
