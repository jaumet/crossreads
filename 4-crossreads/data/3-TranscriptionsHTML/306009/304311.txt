<p>1915<br/>May. 5 Wed.<br/>In afternoon went out to Lunar Park Hospital at Heliopolis to see L. Cpl. Val Marshall who after <strike>getting</strike> being in the trenches 3 days at Dardanelles got a bullet in the shoulder &ndash; Discovered him after a great deal of difficulty &amp; had an interesting yarn with him &ndash; as far as I yet know he is the only members of our Scouts Assn. who has been wounded although the casualty lists are not yet out.</p>
