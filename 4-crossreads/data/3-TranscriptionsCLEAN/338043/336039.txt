
The first stage of the journey from Avesnes to Le Cateau is made in a motor lorry - We rest for the night in this latter town & enjoy the hospitality of the owner of the billet which we occupied a few weeks previous -
Dec. 21stRise 6 a.m. and walk 8 kilo's to Bertry.- At 10 a.m. entrain for Busigny - Then on to Cambrai - Change trains at Cambrai -
