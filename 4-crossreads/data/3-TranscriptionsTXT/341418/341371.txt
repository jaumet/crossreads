We left Bit el Abd at about 8 oclock got back to our temporary camp &ndash; the place where I commenced this lengthy despatch, I don't know its name &ndash; about 3 oclock; drew fodder  &amp;  rations; stopped at "Oghratina" for tea; then proceeded back arriving here at about 1.30 on the morning of June 1st Thursday (today), having sustained one casualty &ndash; a horse, which was shot as its leg was broken by an exceedingly well-directed kick  on the   as we  coming   came home &ndash; It was exceedingly fortunate for us that we came straight home
