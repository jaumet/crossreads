<p>1916 August 21<br/>Monday</p>
<p>No duties last night. slept fairly soundly in dugout at Casualty Corner. Up with squad to <strike> Casualty</strike> Lees's Post. Write note to Ethel MacT. Go thro' sap to wait patients 6th in Pozieres. Heavy shell in while up there. Carry from there to Lee's Post. Are to be relieved tomorrow. Stay at dugout all night.</p>
