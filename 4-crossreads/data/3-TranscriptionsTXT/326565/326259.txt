 Saturday 4/12/15 Most interesting day to date.  Sighted Island of Perim &amp; Arabian mainland early this morning on the Starboard side also a number of small barren islands on Port side.  They form entrance to Red Sea into which we passed about 7.30 A.M.  Perim Is a fairly large barren Island, with a fine lighthouse situated on either end &amp; is to a certain extent populated.
