<p>16/2/17<br/>Marched from Fricourt Camp to Bresle about nine miles we expect to remain here six days and then return to Fricourt.</p>
<p>We are now attached to the Fifth Army the 4th Army having taken over more French territory south of Perrone</p>
<p>22/7/17<br/>Marched from Bresle</p>
