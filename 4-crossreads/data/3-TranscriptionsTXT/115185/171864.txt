 3 &ndash; too badly to live &ndash; but on the whole against fearful odds our skilled surgeons &amp; sisters have been able to bring the wounded into the happy state of convalescence. But the discomforts for the Pts in other ways, have been terrible to witness &amp; still work cheerfully amongst them. Very bad food &ndash; bad cooking I hope only due to the want of the sergeant cook's equipment &amp; not his methods &ndash; no clean clothes, no sheets pillows, mosquito nets, no beds &ndash; no spoons knives or forks or cups &ndash; but what the orderlies &amp; surgeons gave from their own personal kits, a drop in an ocean for feeding 800. I cannot think how we have managed but here we are since the 9th August.
