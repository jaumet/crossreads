 1915 Dec 28 nible only by its shadow on the clouds beneath. of course we are supposed to be drilling. Kidded to shovel sand this afternoon. Offered position in PO during Xmas &amp; N Year rush 
 29 Dental parade to Palace to have tooth filled. Rec'd cable from home &ndash; Xmas Greetings. Interesting Lantern Lecture last night. Have to revisit Dentist at 2 pm Jan 25th. 
 30 Put in rifle squad but nicked out of it again No good to Ben.  Paid 100 pT. Short arm parade for Details &ndash; 1 Casualty &ndash; 
  31 N Yrs Eve  Bit of a riot in the hut last night &amp; the provost Sgt nearly stopped one. Details erected a few tents for tomorrows Sports. Good Concert in C of E room. A couple of drunks woke us up after midnight.
