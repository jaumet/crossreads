<p>a dozen times.  Were warned of the escape of german prisoners, we encountered what we believe were same disguised, later we armed ourselves with a rifle &amp; bombs (mills) to lessen any risk that there might be.</p>
<p>28th<br/>Got leave of absence &amp; proceeded to Becordel with Bill Roach, passed thro death valley, sausage gully, casualty corner &amp; the other old place where hell reigned on earth.</p>
<p>29th<br/>Polling day on active service, great interest shown by most of the boys.</p>
<p>30th<br/>Sunday afternoon off uneventful till its close.</p>
