They were leaning very much, and rather dangerous looking I thought. Bow church was pointed out to me and I was informed that the origin of the name cockney, was here. Anyone being born within sound of the chimes of this church, being called a cockney. I enjoyed the theatre very well, but didn't think it any superior to Sydney theatres.
