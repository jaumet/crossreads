<p>February 1918<br/>Saturday 2</p>
<p>On the road<br/>Reveille at 5.30 am very cold but fine<br/>Passed through St Omer and Wizernes at a fairly early hour<br/>Arrived at our billets a big shed, at 4 pm.<br/>Watered and fed. Short speech by Major congratulating Bty on their smart turn out.<br/>Went into Staples with Jamie, Smith or orderly Sgt. Had a feed of eggs and chips, also some fun in an Estaminet.<br/>Lost ourselves coming home.</p>
