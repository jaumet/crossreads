This morning Burling Pickering McManus turned their "Blighty" leave down, they are expecting to get their Australia trip very shortly so they are saving up for that I don't blame them, I would do the same thing myself.
This afternoon Mr. Sampson took all the School mob down to Div. to draw their pay, from here I had to go on to Bailleul to get 3 prisoners who had finished their time in the clink, but only 1 of them was ready the other 2 being up round Ploegstreet Farm would not be back till late, so I fetched 1 only back we were lucky for we got a ride in a car right up to our Camp
