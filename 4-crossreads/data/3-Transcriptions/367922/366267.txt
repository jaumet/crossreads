<p>4.<br/>fond.</p>
<p>Went to London on 6 days leave from 18th to 23d Oct; was very disappointed in it all, due I think to my utter loneliness &amp; longing for my Dearest One. Knowing how she would be fretting for me I felt absolutely disloyal to be there seeing it all without her.</p>
<p>While in London I spent many hours choosing little things to send Mother, &amp; one afternoon I managed to get out to my cousin's house so that she could write Mum &amp; cheer her by telling <strike>&amp;</strike> how well I was looking.</p>
