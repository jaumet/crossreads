<p>passed the convoy &amp; told us there was a fight last night near Matrugh &amp; we had 16 killed &amp; 30 wounded So far we have seen no sign of the enemy we travel with a small escort of yeomanry scouts out all round</p>
<p><strike>Told by my</strike> We are camped with waggons drawn up in a square &amp; all horse inside &amp; have to sleep with rifle &amp; bandoliers alongside us I was told by our O.C. that there is a force of about 500 Bedouin somewhere between us &amp; Matrugh whether they will attempt to attack us or not is for the future</p>
