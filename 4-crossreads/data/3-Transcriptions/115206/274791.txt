<p>August,1918<br/>Friday 9</p>
<p>Bainvillers.  Bty Position<br/>Weather fine and clear. Plenty of air fighting. General advance during the afternoon pretty solid work hit easy going as far as fighting was concerned.<br/>15th Bdge did good work also 2nd Bdge 1st Divvie. <br/>1st Div Art pulled in to a possie alongside of us.<br/>Harbonnier in our hands &ndash; at 5 pm.<br/>Spent night in old German dugout evidently a Hdqrs, electric light laid on and plenty of foodstuffs.</p>
