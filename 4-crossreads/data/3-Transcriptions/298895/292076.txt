I spent the night there &amp; set off home about 9 A.M. walking through Millencourt near where I picked up a lorry which took me to Albert from there I managed to get to Le Sars where I located Will Harris's grave, a great friend of Morris's   Near here I picked up a lorry to Bapaume &amp; later an ambulance in which I finished my journey having covered in all about 36 miles.  Now I have just finished a letter to Will which I will send over to him tomorrow<br/>Saturday 19th May<br/>    Just an ordinary day with Fritz sending a few over near by.  Watched a Howitzer battery firing in the afternoon &amp; could plainly see the shell by standing behind the</p>
