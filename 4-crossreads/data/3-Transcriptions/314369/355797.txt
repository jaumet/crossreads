<p>14 March 1915<br/>Sunday 4th in Lent</p>
<p>More rumors, the chaplain says it is the last Sunday we will be on the ship   &amp;  he ought to know.<br/>A steamer shows up astern of us this afternoon but is too far off yet for us to make her out.<br/>Weather cool   &amp;  pleasant steaming W.N.W. ever since leaving Colombo</p>
<p>1915 March 15<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Wake up to find the Seang Bee   &amp;  the S.S City of London quite close to us but travelling much faster<br/>City of London nearly out of sight ahead of us by 10 am   &amp;  Bee slightly in the lead.<br/>At Sunset the C of L out of sight   &amp;  the Bee on the horizon</p>
