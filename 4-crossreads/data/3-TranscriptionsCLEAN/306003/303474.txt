  
 13-8-16 Sun 13th Rose again at 5.30 this morning  watered & fed our horses & at 9 oclock had orders to be ready to form up at 9.30 to proceed to Romani.  we gat away to time & escorted the Art. home arriving here at 1 oclock this afternoon;  & we are to go to Ballah for a spell; 
 The New Zealanders & 2nd Brigade came back to camp last night;  several case of cholera was reported yesterday one fatal & all drinking from the wells is prohibited in consequence, but the men will drink anything when thirsty enough cholera or not. put in tonight washing dirty clothes & a bath once more is a trea. a service is being held in the YMCA & the singing sounds good from here
