<p>Wednesday 20th  [Jan]<br/>Another fine clear day.  P &amp; O Steamer "Morea" passed us in the morning &amp; was in Aden a couple of hours before us.  The armed auxiliary cruiser Empress of Russia, the C.P.R. boat was in port.  We were surrounded with Indians in small boats wanting to trade.  They will work points too if they get a chance.  Got several things off them but nothing they sell seems to be of any quality.  Got our first war news since leaving Albany 3 weeks ago.
