<p>Wednesday Dec 4<br/>Auckland &amp; Wellington both held races today. We saw the Philistines Concert party in the evening who gave a good show.<br/>Monday Dec 9<br/>The last few days have been wet &amp; cold so  there has been nothing doing. I have spent the time Reading, Writing mail &amp; playing cards<br/>Tuesday Dec 10<br/>Weather has cleared up again &amp; today we had our Photos taken by the official Photographer</p>
