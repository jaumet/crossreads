Here we received very kind attention in a Red Cross tent.  We slept here and then embarked on the "Braemar Castle".<br/>With 843 patients on board we sailed for Alexandria.  Our ship had never been intended for wounded yet we had many serious cases on board and only two doctors to attend us all.  The worst cases were put ashore three days later and the rest of us taken to Malta.
