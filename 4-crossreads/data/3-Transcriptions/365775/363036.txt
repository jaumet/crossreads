<p>&amp; the country as we leave Marseilles is the most picturesque I have ever seen, everything looks simply lovely. We travel some four or five miles along the coast and here &amp; there the little red bricked cottages with their little garden plots of flowers in bloom, surrounded by hedges &amp; crops waving in the wind, with here &amp; there many vineyards making rather a striking foreground &amp; with the blue ocean as a background, tis a fine picture, one I am afraid beyond my powers to describe the grandeur of coming from the sands of Egypt, it was a pleasant sight for us.</p>
