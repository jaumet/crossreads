
6 July 1918SaturdayThings as usual in the Depot. In the afternoon Sgt Connell & I rode bikes into Colchester & had a nice afternoon. A fete in aid of the Lord Roberts Memorial Fund was the chief attraction & it was well patronized Arrived back in Depot about midnight
1918 July 7Sunday6 After Trinity
The usual quiet day in camp. The camp is open on Sunday afternoons & a good many townspeople pay us a visit & listen to the Band. Expect to go to Weymouth tomorrow.
