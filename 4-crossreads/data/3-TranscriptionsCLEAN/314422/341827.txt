
Sunday Jan 6. 1918. 1252
The first Sunday in the New Year. The King has issued a proclamation that this Sunday be observed as a day of Prayer by all classes & creeds. The London papers give special prominence to it & print various articles &notes by leading ministers.Let us hope that the days of trial will be shortened and that peace will be hastened for a troubled world.There is the usual half holiday today. The weather is bitterly cold all day. Last night there was an exceptionally heavy frost and it is freezing all day. Water thrown out soon becomes ice.
