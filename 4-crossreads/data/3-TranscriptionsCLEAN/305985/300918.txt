 Sixth day of my defaulters just put me on fatigue but was not in the humour to do any digging so I pulled out suppose I will be up again tomorrow Saw Walter Christian last night he looks alright. They say we are going to move out on Wednesday and speculation is rife as to our destination personally I don't care where to . After all I have posted that letter to Miss R.
