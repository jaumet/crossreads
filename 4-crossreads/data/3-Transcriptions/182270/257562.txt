
<p>SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 7, 1919.</p>
<p>Cold &amp; squally with few showers. Had</p>
<p>&nbsp; &nbsp;points&nbsp;here. Al drove Jo &amp; Myee</p>
<p>Barry &amp; Self over to Coomoo Coomoo</p>
<p>for the day. Had some tennis in</p>
<p>afternoon. They were all over at</p>
<p>the unveiling of Wallaces Memorial.</p>
