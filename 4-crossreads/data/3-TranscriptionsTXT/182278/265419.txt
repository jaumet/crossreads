
Tuesday 4.6.18  Meeting of C.O's at Bde re stunt, taking over from Martin's tomorrow. Wrote to Doff
Wednesday 5.6.18. Took over from Basil's old Bn. no casualties- now in Reserve.
Thursday 6.6.18. Reconnoitring & digging at night- cables
Friday 7.6.18. Gas shelling at night, digging again, otherwise quiet
Saturday 8/6/18. More digging  orders received for Stunt- Bn in Reserve.
Sunday 9.6.18  moved into position before daylight & remained quiet
