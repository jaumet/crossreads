<p>Sunday 14th July<br/>a fine morning &amp; Fritz sent several of his balloons up, but each had a plane patrolling in front of it.  None of ours went up.  Hun planes also over in the morning  Rain during latter part of day.  Had a game of cricket.  Wrote home.</p>
<p>Monday 15th July<br/>Sent off for drugs.  Did a little French during day &amp; minor jobs.  About 2.30 P.M. Fritz again managed to get a balloon, one which had replaced the one he destroyed on Saturday.  It was all over in 5 minutes but the barrage put up by our anti aircraft guns saved the next balloon in line.  Its observers however jumped out &amp; all landed safely.  'Tis wonderfully exciting while it lasts.</p>
