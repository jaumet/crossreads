<p>13 March 1918 Wednesday<br/>Still drizzling, we have not had time to bury the dead Turks yet, this hill is littered with Rifles grenades etc etc:- the debris of a fight-<br/>I hope you can read this scribble- expect to go back to <strike>Bethlehem</strike> tomorrow for a days spell very cold &amp; rough on horses up in these hills.<br/>In your last letter you mentioned cuttings re Diary only Water Carnival etc came, it is of interest to the wife to read, as I only write each day to you &amp; twice a week to her so like to read back what is printed.</p>
