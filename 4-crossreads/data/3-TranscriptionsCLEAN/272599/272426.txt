  
 1917 Tuesday 5 June 
 Sleepin till 7 a.m. After breakfast go out all morning  Seek a shady green dell away from everyone & enjoy a lovely old laze. Read & write. A beautiful quiet & peaceful morning. Flowers, butterflies, birds, frisky little foals. War seemed an unheard thing.  Back to dinner. On duty at 2.30 till 10 p.m.
