April
14th. Move to-day en route to A.M.C. Depot at Fovant via Salisbury, but on arriving find that camp is full of 1916 men we had to go on to Hurdcott.
15th. Demob. of A.M.C. men is not satisfactory since we 1915 men are not being fairly treated here, not being on any precedence roll. Existence is awfully monotonous here weather unpleasant.
17th. Walk to Wilton this evening after tea, which is a town
