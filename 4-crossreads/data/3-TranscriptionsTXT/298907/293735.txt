 After this was over I went down to the "Bull ring" to listen to a few of the stump orators who hold forth there every Sunday &amp; they are dead funny, its surprising the crowd they collect, but I expect the majority of them were there like myself out of mere curiosity 
 I am at my window again scribbling this &amp; the country is looking lovely for the rain has ceased &amp; the sun is struggling to show himself through the fleecy clouds that are drifting lazily across the blue sky, all the foliage &amp; trees are looking bright &amp; clean &amp; you can almost see the crops springing to it while the perfume is glorious that is floating about, the atmosphere is very clear &amp; I can see quite distinctly the beautiful Lickky Hills many
