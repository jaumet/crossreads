
Friday 22ndVery little done today and a general feeling of gloom on all. Most of the talk was about home and Australia.
Saturday 23rdWeather still good, getting a bit warmer. Nigger barber starts work, and most of us get our hair clipped, including officers
Sunday 24thHad a concert last night; it was pretty good for an impromptu affair. Church parade again otherwise, only rest, after strenuous week we have(n't)
