Good luck now favours him, those eyes do glisten, vigorously does he cash in those winning cards out. Finishes the game a victor. Everything is comfortable here. Each hut contains twenty beds, are light and bright and though built within a few chains of the sea are moderately warm.<br/><u>Saty 23rd.</u> Not as fit as I would like to be, but considering complaints perhaps am fortunate to be so well. Headache remains about the same. Fritz paid us a visit about 9 p.m. The anti-air craft guns and machine guns got busy. Several bombs dropped. No damage done in our locality, returned again about midnight and carried out a similar performance. That Joe got into trouble this morning. The head sister (in a somewhat spiteful mood) robbed him of his stick.<br/>Immediately he returned to his bed and could not be persuaded out.
