<p>We had a glorious time yesterday afternoon   &amp;  shall send you a few p. cards later. Hope to be able to tell you all about it very soon.</p>
<p>We are very busy again but have a good staff   &amp;  the weather is prefect &ndash; To-day is quite warm, A cold misty morning but a beautiful sin-shiny day. We are able to get a few hours off-duty each day, so that is not like to C.C. Station, where we worked over 12 hours each day, most of the time. I'm glad I've done it   &amp;  should very much like</p>
