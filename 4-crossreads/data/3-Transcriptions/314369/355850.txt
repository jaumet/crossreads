<p>28 June 1915<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Allied troops make several very successful attacks on Achi Baba. Advance about 1000 yds   &amp;  hold the ground won.</p>
<p>1915 June 29<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>The Turks furiously attack our positions at Quinns Hill  &amp;  Walkers Ridge but only succeed in losing heavily themselves our loss only seven killed   &amp;  a good many wounded while the estimated Turkish loss is about 1500 to 2000, 250 dead were buried by our men they having got through our lines   &amp;  promptly cut up.</p>
