<p>26th Paper boys come yelling round the huts singing out "All About it" at 6.30 am.  They <br/>don't give any more information.<br/>There is a picture &amp; Vaudeville show here where women about 50 dress up as <br/>flappers and if they don't sing a "rough" song they are counted out. I was to have gone <br/>on leave this morning but owing to a clerical error my name has been cut out till <br/>Monday. Hard Luck.</p>
