<p>arrived Sueze Friday morning<br/>Friday March 14.3.19<br/>left Suez 11.A.M. very hot day.   am Mess orderley.<br/>Saturday March. 15.3.19<br/>am still MESS orderley  calm sea  feeling well.<br/>Sunday March 16.3.19<br/>still Mess orderley  calm sea  am well <strike>sa</strike><br/>Monday March 17.3.19<br/>am on guard to day in stern of boat  Passed troop ship going towards Suez  very rough to day  good few sick.</p>
