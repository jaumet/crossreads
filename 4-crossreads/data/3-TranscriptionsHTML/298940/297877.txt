<p>I was glad I got on well, and hope it will always be the same, for otherwise, that is where my conduct rests with myself,<strike> I have no fear of</strike> &amp; on my own power, I have no fear of myself. Much as I hate &amp; fear the dangers, as indeed all do for that matter, <strike>I</strike> my sense of honour will be stronger than my fear &amp; keep me up to the sratch. As far as the physical endurance goes I find I am far beyond my Gallopili standard now, and can keep going with the strong ones - perhaps nearly as long in a stunt, when I am used to it.</p>
