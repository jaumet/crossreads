<p>9am set off on foot and reached Billets near Renescure, distance of about 12 miles. about 5pm. Biked into Ebblingham for eatables with Ern &amp; Robo. Had to hand in our blankets to night.</p>
<p>11th. Tuesday.<br/>Up 6a.m. Fell in 10am full marching order. Cleaned up billets and surroundings and moved off 12. noon. Steady march of about 7 miles through "Renescure" via "Arquis" to "St Omer" where we entrained about 3.30pm. in stock trucks &amp; steamed out of stn about 5pm passing Etaples, Calis &amp; Bologne and detrained about 2am.</p>
