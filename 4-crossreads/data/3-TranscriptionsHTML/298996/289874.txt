<p>Sunday 4.4.15<br/>Daybreak brought forth a beautiful sunrise it was <strike>glorious</strike> glorious Hardly a ripple on the water to day. Hot a the So &amp; So. <strike>Had rather harsh words with the [indecipherable]</strike><br/>Church Service this afternoon We met another rainstorm to day which cooled the air for about 5 minutes.</p>
<p>Monday 5-4-15 E. [indecipherable]<br/>Sighted <strike>Clumbo Colombo</strike> Ceylon about 5 Oclock a was a beautiful sight to see the sunrise. Especially when the sun began to rise behind the mountains. We entered the harbour of Clombo 8 AM today which is a made harbour built on one side with breakwaters. No sooner we anchored than the boat was swaramed</p>
