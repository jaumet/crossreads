 Nov. 25th. Shelling of Albert - very bad weather - "Our Officers" - what we think of them! 
 Nov. 26th. A visit to Amiens - The first trip to & from - Trams. Buildings etc - First tram ride since Cairo - Many cards sent home - "The Cathedral & its magnificence". 
 Nov. 27th. Parcels from Home - Squad drill - Fritz on the Roads - also the Anzacs. Treatment of prisoners - miserable weather. 
 Nov. 28th Long route march & squad drill - The irony of it all - Mail of 16 letters from A.S.W. - Xmas Cards - Bad news from Roumania - Sad news of Sid Goode.
