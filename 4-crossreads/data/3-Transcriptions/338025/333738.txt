<p>Thursday 12th July 1917<br/>Fouffin-Ricametz<br/>Reveille 6am. Had breakfast, then started to fill nose bags hay nets as I am stable-man for the day. Dinner 12 noon.<br/>"Donks" went to water at 11.30am did not return till about 2.30pm.<br/>Had one da spell here. Plenty of current bushes in the grounds, but not very many left when we'd finished.</p>
