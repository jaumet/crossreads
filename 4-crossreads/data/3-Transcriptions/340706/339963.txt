<p>centres in one street the Rue de Paris Tortonis the popular caf&eacute; is a great hotel like building with a little square in front of it. The park in front of the imposing Hotel de Ville is simple but forms a pleasant oasis among the buildings &amp; a happy ending to the many streets that converge here.</p>
<p>I visited the old church &amp; thence on to Quay. Plans of the city are in Badeker so I will not repeat. Took tram for Harfleur &amp; then had &frac12; hour walk to reach No 12 camp only to find this was not our real home but that the AIBD is some miles further on &amp; through mud we flounder til we reach our destination.</p>
<p>We have an officer returning to duty with us &amp; the camp has contracted since his departure. There are now only two camps one for convalescent &amp; the other is the base. Here we are well received &amp; alloted to little canvas cubicules strange that after 4 yrs the Base should not have some more permanent form of abode.</p>
<p>After a short address at 2 pm we were free to go on leave &amp; not knowing how long I shall be here I take full advantage of the leave &amp; with Davies proceed to Havre</p>
