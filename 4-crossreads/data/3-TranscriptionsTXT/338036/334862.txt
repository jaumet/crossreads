 [10th Jan. contd] It may be interesting to describe an Estaminet scene (I have often been delighted to visit to see the fun)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They are not like our Hotels and the villages simply abound with them.  There is a counter in some case where the beer etc is handed over.  You walk in get your drink &amp; sit down at a table of which there are usual a dozen in each outfit.  Then the piano starts &amp; fun commences &amp; more you drink the better you sing.  Style Compre.  At 8 o'clock (weet heure) kick out time comes. 
 Thursday 11th Box [?] bombardment 
 Friday 12th Did not get to sleep until midnight as Phelan &amp; Wilson created a disturbance &ndash; after being paid - by coming home shiggered &amp; ended up by tossing things about a little. 
 Saturday 13th Mrs Bs birthday.  Sent a F.S.C. tonight  Also recd a letter from Ernie &amp; answered it.  Today has been coldest for some time &amp; I spent all the afternoon working in snow fall accompanied by little rain.  Glad of a bath &amp; change.  Sam at a Gas School at Casserou (?) 
 Sunday 14th River again in flood and came up very fast during the night &ndash; so fast that I spent the afternoon building a path to the cookhouse.
