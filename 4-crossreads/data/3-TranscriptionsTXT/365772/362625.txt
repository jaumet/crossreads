 Mena Camp 11.3.15 My dear sister Your letter to hand yesterday also a Morning Herald with the swimming news in it, the Duke is a fine swimmer and it too hot for our champions his companion is a warm swimmer too. We have a chap called Murray from Manly with us he is a crack swimmer does 100 yards in 59 secs.
