  
 June 18th Continued The horses should improve here, they were falling  away for the last month - No grazing & too many flies & Dust & not enough water - My two are fit & well, not a blemish on either of them - June 19th "Ramadan" The month of fasting for the Turks - they do not eat even drink water but have a great spread at Sundown & feast all night, if we would only let them would sleep all day.
