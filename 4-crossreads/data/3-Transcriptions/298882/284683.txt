<p>and has done valuable work and set a splendid example also.  All the men have been working bravely through heavy barrages to try and maintain telephone communication.  Our runners have been having a very hard time also and have gone on many long errands through terrific fire and delivered their despatches safely.  Fortunately no casualties amongst the runners up to date.</p>
<p>27/9/17  Signaller Wheen</p>
