 Tuesday 1 Jan. New Years Day once more. This time in Steenwerck on the stables. Up at 6.30 morning stables at 7 finish about 7-45. Parade 9-15 - Grooming. Afternoon on at 2. Harness cleaning & preparing for move. Evening writing & study. Weather exceptional cold. Billet over Estaminet near YMCA. & mess at Sergts mess.
