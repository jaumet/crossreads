 June 8th Just now I am engaged in guarding our water supply &ndash; a futile business, largely the outcome of the stealing episode. However I am on first shift and there is a good moon so it is not so bad. In fact it is mainly on these occasions alone that one has time for ant protracted meditation in this chequered existence.
