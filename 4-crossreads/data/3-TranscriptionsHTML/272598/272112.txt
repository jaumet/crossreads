<p>Saturday 7.10.16<br/>Fritz has been rather quiet for the past two days.  Twenty-ninth Btn, raided unsuccessfully last night, subsequently we had a number of casualties through us.</p>
<p>Sunday 8.10.16<br/>Received more letters from home.  Very quiet on the front line in this sector.  Met Young Gascoygne from Hunter's Hill yesterday, had quite a long yarn to him.  Very heavy Australian casualty lists published in the Home papers of late.  Church</p>
