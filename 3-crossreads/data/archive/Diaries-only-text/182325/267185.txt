Battalion orders No 165 by Lt. Col. HG Bennett CM.G. Commanding 6th Battn A.I F In the Field10-10-16 Part 1 Para 1 Precautions against Enemy Observations All Ranks are warned that it is forbidden to stand or move on the skyline on the Railway Embankment or anywhere near the embankment in view of enemy lines during the hours of daylight. Para 2 Sanitation Company Commanders will be held responsible for the Sanitation of the ground in their area. Especial attention must be paid to the stream in the rear of B, C and D Coys dugouts. On no account must tins or rubbish be thrown into it.
Signed T H Boyd Lieut A Adj
