 Bulford 13/3/19 
 My dear People I have some good news to tell you, do you remember Harry and I saying that we had applied to get home while we were in France, well my application had been approved of but poor Harry's not. We were only saying I suppose only one of us shall be able to get away, and I said to Harry you go and he said he would rather me go. Better anyhow the A.I.F. headquarters has picked one of us, and I am the lucky one.
