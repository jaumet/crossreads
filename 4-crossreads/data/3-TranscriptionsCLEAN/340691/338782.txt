  
 31.12.18. Usual. Read in leisure. In aftn. walk towards Le Roux  Read in evening till 11. 
 1.1.19. Usual nothing extra. Fine  Walked to Chatelet & back by 4. With 1st Coy man back from leave. Rest out. Spent evening reading. 
 2.1.19. On leave to Namur. Caught lorry at 10 right in although cramped  Fine look round. Picture show at 6. Train late. Got away at 10.10. Home at 1.15. Read till 3. Bed tired 
 3.1.19. Cloudy. Read in leisure. Tied & bed at 7. 
 4.1.19. Sun in morning rather cloudy in afternoon. Visit from Birdwood at 11.30 and got some photos also of staff. Full turn out of officers and C.R.E. In aftn. walk to Devant les Bois with Edmonds. Some more photos, and enjoyed it. Nice people there. Home for tea at 4. In evening wrote diary and read wireless till 10. 
 5.1.19. Sunday. Dull, windy, and some showers all day. Parade at 9.15 cancelled. Soaked equipment and spent rest of spare time reading French, Pelman & Wireless. Bed at 9.30. 
 6.1.19. Rather good day and only sometimes cloudy, couple of light showers. Drill order parade for all hands at 9.30. Set out for Charleroi about 10 and got ride in lorry from Presles. Usual dinner. Got 6 photos. Walked round a good deal and gave in films. Very enjoyable. Walked back at 3.30. Saw Edmonds at Chatelet. Home at 7. Bed at 10 after some reading. 
 7.1.19. Usual. Fine day. Spent morning and part of afternoon fixing equipment. Rest of time read. 
 8.1.19. Fine day & sun in morning
