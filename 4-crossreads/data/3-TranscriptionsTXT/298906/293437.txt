Half holiay this afternoon &amp; I went strolling round as usual got a few nice snaps &amp; just scraped out of a row with the "red caps" nice amiable chaps these "red caps". Saw Les Clark this afternoon he had been down this way on a funeral by Jove he looks a hard case does old Les.
