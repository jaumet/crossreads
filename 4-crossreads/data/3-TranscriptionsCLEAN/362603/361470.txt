  
 3.  responsible will be fined a  5 at least.  I have been nowhere & done nothing since I wrote last so I must not expect to be able to write any news.  We have had very few letters for over a month & have been so busy that we could not write to anyone.  So I sat down on Sunday & wrote 15 Post Cards to different ones that I usually correspond with.  I had a letter from a Pal who said that Mrs A. was telling her, she had a letter from Billy in France.  Mrs A said I am so surprised because I did not know that He had left England.  Neither He has.  Someone is telling Stories.  Well, Dad, Mum & George,
