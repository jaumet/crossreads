<p>3.  for a little while this morning, am not quite sure yet.<br/>Oh I should have said that Rid had not had my reply cable when he wrote, he had only just sent his, &amp; so did not like to commit himself too much but he wrote very hopefully of my coming &amp; almost excitedly; he had some plans in prospect for a boarding place for me &amp; was very cheerful about it all.  It was all quite encouraging to me.<br/>My little table steward is as attentive as ever &amp; do you know I am disgraceful enough to have had two ices every meal, I mean every night.  Last night I tried the experiment of asking him for "only one to-night please steward."  But alas, he could not have understood for it</p>
