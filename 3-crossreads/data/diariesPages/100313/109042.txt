10/1/18 Oxford
The Ashmolean Museum is just opposite the hotel and has many old Greek Roman marbles all mutilated of everything phallic like those of the Roman museums. Only in Naples do you meet Nature unadorned. In a cabinet upstairs is King Alfred's jewel with a circular inscription. There was a good deal of interesting pottery of Roman days also some Egyptian mummies c. To the Bodleian Library which is entered through a quiet and beautiful old courtyard. On view were many old manuscripts and books, missals c and old heathen screeds including one of the Iliad. Upstairs is a picture gallery and more manuscripts also a number of models of churches c. After lunch set out to find New College and it is not an easy matter to find any particular place here so crowded together are all these places. Through
