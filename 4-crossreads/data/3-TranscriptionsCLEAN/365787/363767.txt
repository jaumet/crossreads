
[Card]DYMOCK'S BUILDING68 ELIZABETH STREETSYDNEY
4th March, 1919
Dear Madam,
I have to acknowledge with many thanks receipt of your parcel, containing a donation from the Wahroonga-Warrawee War Chest Branch of 117 pairs of socks and 1 knitted muffler.
Yours faithfully,"WAR CHEST" SUPPLY DEPOT,[Signed] J. M. AntillHon. Superintendentper Cu.E.R.
