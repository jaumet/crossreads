 1916 February 9 Wednesday 
 First day of Nowra Show.  Get up &amp; help milk.  Harry goes up early. Mary not much better.  Bob &amp; self drive up in fore-noon &amp; go to ground after lunch. Called at Borrowdale's &amp; heard of Bob's illness.  Visit him at Private Hospital also see Lil Brown &amp; Mrs Graham.  Call then at Edwards &amp; Annie Gardners. After lunch at Stan's go to show for afternoon. After tea Bob &amp; call round at Baillies &amp; take Miss L &amp; B to pictures.  Ice cream &amp; home.
