  
 1919 May 27 Garden (an old theatre re-conditioned) to see "Kissing Time" & just arrived as it commenced. 
 May 28 Mrs Mackenzie had arranged for me top go on a trip up the river to Hampton Court by the "Sunbury Belle", an ancient river steamer.  I met Miss Tweedie & Sister Haggard (niece of Rider Haggard) & Sister Galvin & a New Zealander, & embarked with them at 10 am at Westminster Pier.  It was a beautifully fine day, & the scenery was beautiful once we got clear of London.  We passed Richmond & went through several locks.  I had often read about these in the BOP & "Three men in a boat" but had never seen them work before.  A clergyman, his wife, & child (on holidays) did the catering on board.
