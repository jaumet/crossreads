<p>My Diary<br/>22.12.15<br/>We embarked at 11 am at No 2 W"loo wharf &amp; put out into the stream at 3 pm &amp; set sail at 5.50 pm. Sea very calm &amp; we slept well.</p>
<p>Thursday 23.12.15 (1)<br/>Sea still calm &amp; lovely breeze blowing. Had my hair lopped. Full Dress parade &amp; boat drill.</p>
<p>Dinner &amp; Tea was a huge scramble. Plum pudding for dinner very good. Passed Gabo [Island] &amp; can see the shore all the while. Very rugged &amp; uninteresting. Boat very slow but very steady. Plenty of fellows sick but</p>
<p>the jig-jig of the boat does that. Saw a sea-lion while on parade.</p>
<p>Friday X'mas Eve (2)<br/>Sea still smooth just like a harbour trip. Washed some togs &amp; took them out on to the f'k'slehead [forecastle head] &amp; sat alongside till they dried.</p>
<p>Saturday X"mas Day (3)<br/>Sea still very calm we are between Melbourne &amp; Adelaide &amp; have not sighted land since yesterday at noon. Spent the morning in the fore-hold &amp; looking for our piano but was unable to find out.</p>
<p>Broached a tin of preserved fruit &amp; one of prunes. Had a bonza dinner &amp; read magazines all the afternoon. We held a concert in F</p>
