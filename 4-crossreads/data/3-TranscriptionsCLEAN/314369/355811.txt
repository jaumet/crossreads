  
 11 April 1915 Sunday Low Sunday - 1st After Easter 
 Sunday morning 5.30 arise   &  get my gear ready mount Guard at 6.45 a.m. take over 16 prisoners most of which are released to join their Battalion. Bat moves of at 6.30 pm followed at half hour intervals by the 14th 15th   &  16th with all baggage   &  Kitchens. 
 1915 April 12 Monday 
 The work of reorganising the remains begins All told, there are in camp about 400 of all ranks. The 1st ALH lend us three officers to help us along as we have only 3 of our own left.
