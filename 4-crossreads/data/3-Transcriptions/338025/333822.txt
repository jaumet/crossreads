<p>Thursday 18th October 1917</p>
<p>Left lines at 7am with wagon.<br/>Went to 104th Batt. Wagon lines (nr Ypres). Carting bricks from Ypres to 104th    horse lines. Got stuck one or twice in shell holes, finished about 4pm and arrived back in our lines about 5.30pm</p>
