<p>Saturday 10/3/17</p>
<p>Training and re-organising &ndash; another letter from Doff.</p>
<p>Sunday 11/3/17</p>
<p>Church Parade. Training move to line cancelled now going to make an attack in a few days.</p>
<p>Monday 12/3/17</p>
<p>Training and re-organising moved from Acid Drop to Becourt Camp, wrote letters to Doff.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13/3/17</p>
<p>Grevellers Line evacuated &ndash; meeting of C.O's at Bde H.Q/</p>
<p>Wednesday 14/3/17</p>
<p>Battalion Training Platoon &amp; Coy Schemes.</p>
<p>Thursday 15/3/17</p>
<p>More Training saw The General who is looking well.</p>
