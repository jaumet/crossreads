<p>1 Field Ambce<br/>5 March 16.</p>
<p>My dearest Irene,<br/>I was very delighted to receive a letter from you dated 23 Jany. I think the authorities must hold on to our letter for 3 or 4 weeks before they let them go on. I saw Dr Harris just before we left Lemnos &amp; he seemed in the best of health so perhaps the paper which reported him as having been sent to London ill made a mistake.
