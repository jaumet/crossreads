 12th I have got a dose of bronchitis but not [indecipherable] back &ndash; I am wondering how I would stand a  winter in France if I have to go there 13th Censored 14th Not much [indecipherable] &amp; the days are slow I am not able  to go out at night at all just going along quietly &ndash; What a bevy of letters must be following me up, When they do come what a day I will have reading them all. 15th I went to Head Quarters AIF &amp; got sick leave, one month, hope to get advance.
