<p>Thursday 31st Jan 18<br/>Went to our job a concrete Observation Post &amp; did a shift this afternoon about 6 hours Foggie for a change</p>
<p>Friday 1st Feb<br/>Same as yesterday</p>
<p>Saturday 2nd<br/>Shift changed now go out at 6 AM &amp; return at noon. The old hands say this is a very good job all day work. Mixing concrete is hard work &amp; when mixed it has to be shovelled up about 6 ft into a truck &amp; then pushed up a fairly steep grade for about 20 yds as the O Pip (observation post) is on top of a rige</p>
<p>Sunday 3rd<br/>Same as any other old day</p>
<p>Monday 4th<br/>Very clear day plenty of our Planes up. No Huns</p>
<p>Tuesday 5th<br/>About 1 ft rain last night the first since landing in France. Fritzie sprinkled quite a lot of shell about this afternoon The</p>
