Wednesday 12/7/16
Quiet morning but rater a hard afternoon; number of casualties result of bombing in the 55 Btn. Very nasty carrying along saps, duck boards narrow, likelihood of slipping over side. Relieved at 10 pm.
Thursday 13/7/16
Btn refused to issue rations through some misunderstanding. Went to Hqrs to report. Passed through Fleurbaix under Artillery fire. A couple of high explosive shells landed close by; caused quite a stir in town
