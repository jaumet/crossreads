 Each gun fired 4 charges &ndash; It's a deafening roar &amp; your ears get touched up &ndash; You cd. see the men putting their fingers into them &ndash; Cotton wool stops the sound waves &ndash; Then we tried the Maxims &ndash; quick firers &ndash; They are like revolvers fired very rapidly &ndash; We had to go back to harbor for our speed tests for it was too rough outside &ndash; We go up to Garden Island &amp; may not go out tonight &ndash; I got your nice letter written on Thursday &amp; one from Turner &ndash; The few moths I got in New Britain he is sending to the British Museum he tells me &ndash; This day week is Mab's birthday &ndash; I remember as well as yesterday her natal day &ndash; I had but very little scope then for my energy &amp; vitality &amp; I fear I was rather a nuisance &ndash; Men with energy &ndash; unless use is made of it &ndash; are apt to become a nuisance I think &ndash; Remember that for Rawdon &ndash; He is chock full of joy &amp; ginger &amp; will bubble over &amp; do queer things unless vents are found for it &ndash; Well &ndash; Mab is quieter &ndash; I hope she likes her hat box &amp; that its all right &ndash; Mind you get your things safe &amp; sound &ndash; Give Mab my warmest love &ndash; I am sure she will be a fine woman &ndash; Tell her from me not to worry over the future &ndash; its not in our hands &ndash; but to be quite true to the present &amp; to enjoy life &amp; all that comes with it &ndash; Tell R to wash his mouth out with warm water night &amp; morng &amp; to see Sinfield &ndash; Be obedient &ndash; clean &amp; play games.
