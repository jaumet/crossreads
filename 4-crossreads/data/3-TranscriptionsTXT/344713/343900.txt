Mon. 21st Aug. 1916.Marched from Albert through Senlis to Warloy & billeted.
Tues. 22nd Aug,
Marched from Warloy through Contay, Herrisart & Valdemaison to the old apple orchard at La Vicogne & bivouaced.
Thurs. 24th Aug.Left La Vicogne & marched through Candas to Fienvillers where we billeted.
Sat  Aug. 26thMarched from Fienvillers to Doullens & entrained to Poperinghe & camped in huts at the Scottish Lines about 4 miles the other side.Our turn in Belgium now.
Thurs. 31st  Aug.Left Scottish lines which are between Busse Boom & Ouderdom on our advance guard &  marched through Vlamertinghe to Ypres & took over underground billets in the cellars of remains of houses which the Bn. will come & occupy tomorrow as reserves for front line.The town is absolutely a heap of ruins - Cathedral & Cloth Hall have only parts of wall left.
