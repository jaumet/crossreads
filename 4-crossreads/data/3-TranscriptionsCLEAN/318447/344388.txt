The road is really an engineering triumph, beautifully evenly instaled & extremely well graded. It winds along like a huge white snake up around the hills & the majority of the way it is walled up on the outside & cut into the hill on the inside; thus enabling the traveller frequently to look down some 5 or 600 feet almost sheer onto the roofs of & orchards of some villages
