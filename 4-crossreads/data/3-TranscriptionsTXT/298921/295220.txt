
September, 1915Monday 6Hear there are Italians landing N. of Anzac this should help considerably - I have not come in from my first outing - the "car" came for me & I lunched at Brigade HdQuarters & saw my Horses Please tell they both look A.1. & are ready if we are to have any mounted work -I've had a Bath & clean Pyjamas & am A.1. another week should surely see me out, I am quite strong.I hear the Pastoralist Review of July 24th honored me by reprinting photo I wonder who gave it to them.
