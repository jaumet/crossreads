<p>Wednesday 7 June 1916<br/>Came up to Cairo by Red Crescent Ambulance train.  Beautifully <strike>Lovely</strike> fitted out train.<br/>Country very pretty, as train follows canal, all small patches like Chinamen's gardens.  Natives plowing with bullocks &amp; donkeys just like Bible pictures.  Brought out to Abbassia.</p>
<p>Thursday 8 June 1916<br/>In hospital Abbassia about three miles from Cairo.  These were the Egyptian Artillary Barracks;  most beautiful big stone buildings very thick walls.<br/>Wrote to Mother (3).</p>
