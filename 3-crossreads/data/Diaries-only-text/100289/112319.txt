Sunday 6.1.18
Walked to Newlands Mousehole, raining all day, returned very early.
Monday 7.1.18
Went to St Ives, snowed rained, very miserable. Returning had to wait 1 hours at St Erth junction so went on to Redruth instead of waiting caught train back from there to Penzance.
