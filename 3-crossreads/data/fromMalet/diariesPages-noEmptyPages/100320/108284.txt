Went to Arrigonis for dinner and then to the Opera to see "Thais". Had a few words with a very insolent elderly doorkeeper and came near to knocking him right down the marble staircase. Had a fairly good seat next to some Yank VDVADs and we were soon friends. The house was crowded and there were any number of English speaking people there. A very fine dancer in the "divertissement".
