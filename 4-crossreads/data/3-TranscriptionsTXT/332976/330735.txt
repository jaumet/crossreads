 October 1916. 30th.   I have been on water picquet all day to-day.  Water is very scarce here as it has to be carted for miles &amp; consequently is guarded.  It has been raining very hard all day &amp; a strong wind has been blowing. 31st   Pritchard &amp; myself went out to High Wood this morning.  High Wood a few weeks ago was the scene of a very severe battle &amp; many hundreds of lives were lost in its capture.
