 Wednesday 24th October 1917 Therouanne Rev. 6am moved off at 9am our next stop to be Hazebrock 18 miles from Therouanne. Passed through Rebecq, Roquetorie, Blaringhum,Sercus, arriving Hazebrock about 4pm fed up at 5.30pm.Detailed for piquet which I mounted at 6pm filled hay nets and as my shift was from 12 till 1am I went into Hazebrock for the evening which was about 2 kilos away. Very heavy rain all night.
