<p>1916 &nbsp; MONDAY 7 &nbsp; AUGUST</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. They started to train us in earnest this morning &amp; about time two. We were doing standing gun drill in the morning and they got a signalling school going in the afternoon &amp; I joined up with that. We have the English lieut and sergeant over us.</p>
