<p>night,  &amp;  in the morning were lucky enough to secure a Y.M.C.A. lorry which took us to the Aust. Rest Camp at Catillon. Here we found that we <strike>she</strike> were not required  &amp;  so leaving our valises at the house of a French family we took a lorry to Le Cateau. Here a French repatriated prisoner attached himself to us &amp; we secured a Red+ car to the Busigny X road on the main Cambrai route. Another lorry took us a few miles along the road  &amp;  at dark put us down at a village, After a good deal of trouble we secured another lorry &amp;  hanging to its splashboard with one hand  &amp;  to our friend the French  prisoner with the other we arrived</p>
