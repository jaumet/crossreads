After this, our first meal in the old country, we continued on our way through the same class of country, but all the houses had thatched roofs. Several very old disused castles we passed and I was thinking over the description of these places given in books of various kinds. The opinion and picture I had got in my memory was very like the real thing, but naturally some details were quite different.
