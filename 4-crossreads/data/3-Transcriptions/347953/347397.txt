<p>&amp; men packing  &amp;  unpacking stores reminded one of an Egyptian business quarter.  The field ambulance in well sheltered dug-out at the foot of the hills did great work, and were subject to much shell fire.  At the northern &amp; southern ends of the beach were situated the most pathetic of all the sites &ndash; graveyards where the heroes lay at rest with crosses well moulded &amp; painted  &amp; neatly arranged with reverence so well deserved  &amp; perhaps in years to come the wattle trees planted there will pay a better &amp; more homely tribute to heroes almost forgotten.</p>
