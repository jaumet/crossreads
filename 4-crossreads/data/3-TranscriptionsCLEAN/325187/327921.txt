
[Tuesday 27 June 1916]
Another Plane today but no Bombs, I posted you an Egyptian Paper showing how we treated them at , [Following paragraphs are crossed out]AL ARISHPicking up daily both men & Horses, getting plenty of sleep, but another Stunt is due about FridayGreat rumours today that some wonderful news is about to be given out, so we all anxiously await it.We have no Papers so have to start a few rumours now & again we hope this is not one.  Mulga wires are not in it compare with War yarns.
