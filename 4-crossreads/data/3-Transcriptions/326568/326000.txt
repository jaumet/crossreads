<p>February 1919.<br/>21st.  Close hospital &amp; wind up 1st Aust. Field Ambulance as a medical unit.  Draft will now not leave before 5/3/19.<br/>26th.  Hand over all equipment of Ambulance, except things which are absolutely necessary to carrying on of unit.  Doing nothing but wait orders for Australia.  Weather unsettled.<br/>28th.  Dirty day and miserable but nevertheless went for a walk to Devant-les-Bois.</p>
