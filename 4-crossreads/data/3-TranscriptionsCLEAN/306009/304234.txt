  
 1915 Feb. 17 Wed.  Worked until 4.30 p.m. and then went off to Cairo with S.M. Joyner  - had  Work brisk again. I have the following regular jobs to do - (1) Report deaths (2) Make out Morning State of Sick (3) Make out Admission & Discharge sheets (4) Write up in triplicate all admissions & discharges.
