Owing to looking after the Hot Baths for nearly a fortnight I was granted a spell from Saturday until Monday, so I decided to take a run up to London. After Breakfast  I cleaned my Leggings &amp; Boots  I made my way to the Station &amp; booked my Ticket to Bentley  wit  The Train took 20" to arrive at Bentley where I had to detrain &amp; wait for a connecting one leaving Bentley at 12.32 Pm, so  to  having about 1 hour to wait I adjourned to the Hotel &amp; had some lunch.
