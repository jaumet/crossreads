 Oct. 19th Motor trip to Poperinge (Car orderly). Wet &amp; cold weather. 
 Oct 20th Australian Mail &ndash; Route march to village of Eecke &ndash; outgoing letters still prohibited. 
 Oct 21st Our Ambulance leaves "Steenworde" &amp; after passing through Cassel &amp; other villages we finish our days march at Noordpeene still in doubt as to our final destination &ndash; Our billets the worst we have had in France &ndash; Weather very cold &amp; unsettled &ndash; Farmhouse &ndash; Concert 
 Oct. 22nd Resume march from Noordpeene via the large town of St Omer and we are billeted at Pilques &ndash; Australian Mail arrives. 
 Oct 23rd Football etc &ndash; further mail
