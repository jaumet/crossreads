<p>Saturday 3rd November 1917</p>
<p><strike>Troc. 4 p.m.</strike><br/>Club 1 p.m. lunch, Troc. 4 p.m. Cottons ask for John Egan's address &amp; buy whisky. Met Cottons, all wished to be remembered to you. Letters from Mother, Day, Joe, Australasian Hay Grazier. Two each dated Aug. 19th, Aug. 26th, glad to hear alls well. Took baggage to Waterloo this morning raining as usual.</p>
<p>Went to a pretty Japanese play called WILLOW REEL. Dolly enjoys Theatres. No word from Laura yet, she is at WALSALL &amp; I am afraid we will miss her.</p>
