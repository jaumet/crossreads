<p>4 May 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Native wedding over at quarters.  Bride enveloped in sheet &amp; hidden from view  Camels, family party. Music(?).  Single sticks<br/>Hospital messenger. <br/>Concert in Hospital mess-tent. Sang "My Old Shako".<br/>Dave Watts down again. Ashley appears from Convalescent Home.</p>
