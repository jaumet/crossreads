 Thursday Sept 5th Mardyk 
 had the day off &amp; cleaned up my letters a bit : air raid at night : 
 Friday Sept 6th Mardyk 
 Went to the old job : sent a map of Paris &amp; programme of that opera affair to Mother : 
 Sat Aug[Sept] 7th &amp; Sunday Aug[Sept] 8th Mardyk 
 dull &amp; showery : nothing doing : 
 Monday Sept 9th Mardyk 
 our signal box stunt has finished so we are on camp fatigue : not too bad : 
 Tuesday Sept 10th Wed Sept 11th Mardyk 
 cold &amp; showery : camp fatigues 
 Sept 12th Thursday 
 went to pictures at night &amp; they were very good too : camp fatigue 
 Friday Sept 13th Mardyk 
 got a parcel from Alf Stewart's sister also a letter from his sister at Kogarah &amp; one from Connie : 
 Saturday Sept 14th Mardyk 
 fatigue during day : pair of socks from Beattie Collins but no letters 
 Sunday Sept 15th Mardyk 
 went to Belgian sports at Malo in afternoon &amp; were very good : good crowd there &amp; the weather fined up nicely : had a run in 100 metres but was too stiff &amp; done no good : Belgians are very poor sports &amp; do not like anyone but their own lot winning a prize : air raid at night : heavy barrage put up : a few bombs dropped round about :
