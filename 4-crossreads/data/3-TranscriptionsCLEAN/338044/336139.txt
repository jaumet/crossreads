
Dec 31st New Years eve, 1915A repetition of Xmas in almost every respect. Raining heavily & after work I go down town looking for a few friends whom I eventually find in the P.O. mess. We keep up the old traditions until 12 midnight, again worry the Pay Corps & eventually get into bed at 3 am.
1st Jan. 1916 New Years Day.Am very tired this morning but awakened early by the inevitable nuisance of a noisy band. W.O. Walker goes down to them in pyjamas &  parades the band, himself leading the way with the drum right around our quarters  creating a most unpleasant din.
