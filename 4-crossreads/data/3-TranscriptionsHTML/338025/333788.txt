<p>Saturday 8th September 1917</p>
<p>Grooming and harness cleaning, five or six Taubes over dropping bombs, but all fell well away from our camp.</p>
<p>Sunday 9th<br/>Church Parade 10am. 12 noon watered and fed mules. N.F. band played selection in our lines from 2pm till 3.30pm</p>
