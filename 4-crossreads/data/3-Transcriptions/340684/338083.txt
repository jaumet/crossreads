<p>356<br/>2<br/>year so did Mrs Dickson. They were going to ask you to stay with them if I had been laid up here for anytime. They had me on the operating table last night but after a careful examination they decided it was too risky to operate &ndash; I am rather depressed about it as it is rather a horrible sight. &ndash; I shall probably pull this rotten thing out one of these days, it is jolly painful at times &ndash; Mrs Evans called this morning and brought a large basket of strawberries</p>
