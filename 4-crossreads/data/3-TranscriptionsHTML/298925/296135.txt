<p>dropped into a fold of the ground and returned the fire (of course all with blank) cartridges) the A.M.C. &amp; Transport waggons making a detour and halting about half a mile ahead.</p>
<p>After a reconstruction of our troops we continued our march through <u>Winterbourne Stoke</u> and back to camp via the Stonehenge arriving at 5.30 after marching 19 miles for the day.</p>
<p>We are feeling tired, especially in the shoulders where the equipment strap cut us.  Our feet are allright as we well soaped our socks</p>
