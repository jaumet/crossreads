 1917 August 
 8 Wednesday 
 My day off &amp; Matron sent me up to the nurses' holiday camp at Carikovo Bacilo with sister angel where Cpt. Phil took me up to near Kajmackalon &amp; I had a nice day with strawberries &amp; raspberries. Regaled with scandals of Miss Dick's indiscretions with various men.
