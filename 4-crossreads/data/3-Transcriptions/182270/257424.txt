
<p>TUESDAY, APRIL 22, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Went into town early</p>
<p>&amp; met Barry &amp; Mary &amp; Lorna &amp;</p>
<p>then met Mum &amp; had a feed</p>
<p>of fruit salad. Met Linda &amp;</p>
<p>put in the afternoon reviewing</p>
<p>relations. Went out to Barracks</p>
<p>apr&egrave;s &amp; fixed up about my pay.</p>
