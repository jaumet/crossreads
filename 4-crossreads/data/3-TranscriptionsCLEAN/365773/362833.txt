  
  
 Hospital 29.1.17 My dear Sister, Just a short note to inform you that I am O.K. again & will be leaving here for the line tomorrow, am quite strong again & feeling very fit. The cold here is now intense we have had over a weeks frost & everything with a touch of moisture is frozen stiff. I feel it very much I didn't think previously that it could get so cold & am now praying for spring & a little heat. Tell Mother
