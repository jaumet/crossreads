<p>June 19th<br/>Now we are down by the sea, an excellent camp, not 60 yds from the water's edge on clean white sand.<br/>Thank God we shall be able to keep clean while we are here at anyrate.<br/>June 23rd<br/>Just at present war seems very far away indeed. It is now half past six of a beautiful evening. A gentle breeze, gradually dying away, comes in from the sea. In a few minutes the sun, in a sky devoid of clouds will cut the clear cut, blue horizon of the sea. Meanwhile a path of golden glory stretches</p>
