However going across country by the stars we got about half way when by great luck we fell in with a few Yeomanry going there &amp; went with them.<br/>Arrived there about 8 p.m., we waited until 1 a.m. for the camels who had missed the way, got them loaded &amp; started back by the motor load at 2.30 arriving at railhead shortly after dawn, a tiring &amp; annoying trip.  Today was profitably put in sleeping.<br/>I hear that the infantry have done all right, but the mounted troops have got cut up a bit &amp; the position at Tel el Jemmi seems to be stalemate.<br/>Going out to Tel el Jemmi I saw some fireflies for the first time in my life, very curious that they should be here as it is not really in the tropics.
