Nov. 18th Thursday
Weather again simply perfect with slightest breeze drying up things wonderfully. Made some improvements to dug-out against rain. 6-30 p.m. to 10-30 p.m. on Ammunition Fatigue to Beach back with bomb mule transport. Shelled.
Nov. 19th Friday
Perfect day. Writing morning. Gass apparatus inspected. Writing evening.
Nov. 20th Saturday
Morning reading writing, afternoon issuing underwear for Reserve. Ammunition Fatigue 6-30 to 9-30 p.m.
Nov. 21 Sunday
Eileen Pcl.
Weather very dull and bitterly cold with wind.
