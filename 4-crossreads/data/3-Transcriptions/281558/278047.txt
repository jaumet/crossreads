<p>Friday Oct 5th</p>
<p>Dartford : cold day : up at 8 am : helped to clean up : went to Dartford with Monty in afternoon for a stroll : thigh still stiff after walking any distance at all : cold night</p>
<p>Saturday Oct 6th</p>
<p>Dartford : cold day : changed the wards linen in morn : wrote in afternoon : splendid "comedy play" in theatre after tea entitled "Jane". I have seldom enjoyed such a laugh before &amp; the acting was splendid</p>
<p>Sunday Oct 7th</p>
<p>Dartford : wet cold day : read most of the time</p>
<p>Monday Oct 8th</p>
<p>Dartford : inspected by doctor : my scabies are pretty right so I got marked out to B 29 ward so as to be able to stroll round &amp; get used</p>
