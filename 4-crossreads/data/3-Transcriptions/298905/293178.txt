<p>he would be home if I came down I got an answer 2 hours after, saying; yes come at once.<br/>Saw Tom Kearney this afternoon he has not changed much.<br/>Have been all around Trafalgar Square, saw Nelsons monument (whe the Kaiser say's Peace will be Signed) Downing Street where all the Heads live, the War Office &amp; the Cavalry mounted as sentries dressed in all colour's, have been down the Strand &amp; Fleet Street. I saw General Joffre just out side the War Office he was over on a big war conference, no doubt the amazing success of the Russians &amp; the naval battle had a lot to do with it, there was a great crowd waiting outside to see</p>
