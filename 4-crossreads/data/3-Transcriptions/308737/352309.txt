<p>I am now at Aboukir, Alexandria, graduating. I go through advanced flying here &amp; all sorts of other things before I am ready for dinkum work.</p>
<p>We have been going through all kinds of schools for the last 3 months &amp; hav'nt been near the front where there is much fighting now. Do you know I hav'nt had a word from you for 6 weeks, I cant make out whats up. I've written to the Aus. Field Post office &amp; perhaps they can throw some light on the affair. I'll send you a snap of me in flying rig-out &amp; my pet aeroplane</p>
