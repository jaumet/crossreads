<p>You would have smiled had you seen the numerous changes of clothing that I made yesterday. I started off at 5 in the morning in the kahki drill uniform with cotton underclothes. The great coat was next requisitioned &amp; later the new heavy kahki uniform. Later still heavy underclothes appeared &amp; in the late afternoon I fished out one of the Balaclavas. In the evening you would have found me shivering in heavy underclothes, heavy uniform great coat and Balaclava. I forgot about the silk muffler. We had a game of bridge last night but it was too rough to be interesting. I will keep off cards on Sundays, there are plenty of other days and one must</p>
