
21 Dec 17In sight of land on both sides.
22 Dec 17Arrived Scapay Flow & coaled & oiled ship.
25 Dec 17Piped down all day.
28 Dec 17Left to convoy ships to Norway in company with 2nd Battle Cruiser Squadron.
29 Dec 17Escorted one convoy to Bergen, as another one was leaving. A 25 knotter tried to break away & had to be fired on to make her stop.
30 Dec 17With 130 of a convoy. Second Light Cruiser Squadron ran into a flotilla of U. Boats & Melbourne fired 3 depth charges. Arrived safely at Scapay.
31 Dec 17Coaled & oiled ship. 2nd advance party came on board. 3 days adrift
