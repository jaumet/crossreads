<p>darkness of an eastern night descends on land, sky &amp; sea.<br/>That is all very well in its way but now by the light of a prosaic candle (tallow one) let me detail the happenings of the last day or two.<br/>To begin with the day before yesterday, a rapid course of mental arithmetic denotes the 21st as being the most probable date, there were alarums &amp; excursions &amp; much cleaning of horses, saddlery &amp; stall, for Major General Chauvel ex brigadier 1st LH Bgde. was C in C of Desert Column</p>
