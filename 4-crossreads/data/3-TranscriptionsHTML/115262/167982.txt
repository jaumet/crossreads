<p>Friday 3 August 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille 4 a.m. Saddle up camels &amp; set&nbsp;off. A round-about kind of a march it was. Country in places limestone hills. These hills were very steep &amp; camels advanced in single file till we reached the top.</p>
<p>(Breakfast 9 am.)</p>
<p>We passed beside the old city of Cairo &amp; near &quot;The Citadel&quot;.</p>
<p>Leave from 4 pm till 9.30 p.m.</p>
<p>Got wristlet watch from jeweller 1st repair.</p>
<p>Saw a stylish French funeral in Cairo and snapped it.</p>
