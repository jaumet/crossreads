  
 I rise at 6.30 Groom & feed my pony breakfast at 8 at 10 the horses have to be watered & after that besides drawing the horsefeed nothing is done till Lunchtime at 12 when we feed the horses & ourselves again 
 After lunch I have a pipe & then usually go out for a ride - there are one or two quite pretty corners around here - at 4 oclock we water feed etc again tea at 5. After tea we usually make a fire & sit around it until about 8 or 9 & Then I retire to my home &
