<p>March 13th<br/>For the last three days we have heard nothing but the continual rustle of sand blown against the tent by the [see image for Arabic writing] (Khamseen).<br/>Today thank goodness it has stopped.<br/>Another move in the air.  To come off in a few days to El Arish.</p>
