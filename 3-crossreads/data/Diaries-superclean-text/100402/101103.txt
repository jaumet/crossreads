Saturday 13 July
The country is looking well after the rain I have just had a walk round Sercus Wallon Cappel. Fritz was over bombing last night. A rest camp is opened for Australians at Bolougne one is to go from the section, possibly myself.
Sunday 14 July
Wet all day. Fritzs ?planes ?were active last night bombing his our guns were busy to-day.
