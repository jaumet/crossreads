<p>[postcard - apparently censored]</p>
<p>A Fry Esq.</p>
<p>Northcote Road.<br/>Lindfield.<br/>N.S. Wales<br/>Australia</p>
<p>Sund 19th Nov.</p>
<p>Dear Father</p>
<p>My word its cold here. The water is frozen all day sometimes. There is hail &amp; sleet. It snowed on the hills nearby but not here yet. This is [censored]</p>
<p>We wont be here many days &amp; will then go into the line. Tommy came &amp; spent the evening with me before leaving England. Am very well. Love. Dene.</p>
