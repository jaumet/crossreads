<p>Sunday 11 August<br/>Fine day. Down to Vare for water in morning. Had a swim at the same time.</p>
<p>Then <strike>to</strike> 10th Bde line out 3.30. Walked &amp; walked for miles past 10th Bde old position. Line under traffic everywhere. Finally got line thro. returned home dead beat 10.30 p.m. Must have walked about 13 miles.</p>
