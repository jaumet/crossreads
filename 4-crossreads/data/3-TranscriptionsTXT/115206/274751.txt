 June 1918 Sunday 30 
 Poulonville. &ndash; On road and at Bty Posit. Pulled into School yard at 3.50 am. After a farily solid trip all night. The road Palmer, Evayling and Garfott to Bty position 1.25 C. We proceeded at 8 pm for Battery position with the guns. Arrived at position at 2.30am 1/6/18 Just arrived when we received two salvoes of whizz bangs. 1 horse killed and Cpl Kelly slightly wounded &ndash; Mr Koens horse also slightly wounded.
