<p>22.4.15<br/>Thurs. 22nd.  Awoke this morning at 5.30 feeling fit for anything, had a good breakfast &amp; were on the road to Helouan at 9 oclock, had a very good trip &amp; a nice breeze was blowing, the boats on the Canal were busy &amp; were sailing along the Nile in great style.  We arrived at our destination at 2.30 &amp; were soon settled, with the horses lined up facing the Tewfik Palace Hotel, we were off duty by 3.30 &amp; then strolled to the Canteen &amp; tested the Ice-creams &amp; cakes, after tea we went to the Winter Palace Hotel &amp; had supper for 10 pt. each &amp; then played billiards till it was time to return to Camp.</p>
