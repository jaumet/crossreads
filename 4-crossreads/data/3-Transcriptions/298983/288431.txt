<p>Left London at 6 &amp; got to Forant about 9 Reported drew blankets etc got some letters &amp; just had time to read one before lights out</p>
<p>Friday 7th June<br/>The usual routeen, Medical inspection Dentist Etc Got 12 letters &amp; two parcels one from<br/>Ann one Aunt Nima Am Marked B.A4 so will not be here long The Dentist has my teeth it is uncomfortable without them.</p>
