Erected over the largest boiling spring is a stone building inside which is excavated a bath.  We all went down &amp; revelled in the waters, a real treat. 
 June 1st A march of 12 miles fairly good going. 
 June 2nd 19 Miles today in the broiling sun, had good feed &amp; am turning in.  Its now a case of bed before dark &amp; out again before daylight &amp; plodding along all day.
