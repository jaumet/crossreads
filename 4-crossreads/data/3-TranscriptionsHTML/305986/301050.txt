We were relieved on the night of the 7th by the 48th Bin, and just before they took over, I'm hanged of the line didnt break, Mac &amp; I were ready to get out of the line, but we had to drop our packs and go out on the wire, we mended it got back safe, and Im hanged if it didnt</p>
