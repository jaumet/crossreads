 16th December (Saturday) Woke up at 8 o'clock, not having been disturbed once.  It was a treat to get our wet boots &amp; socks off and have a sleep as we needed one badly.  This morning the air is very clear and our Capt. Parkinson is fussy about keeping under cover.  German &amp; British planes are busy overhead.  Fritz continues his heavy shelling.
