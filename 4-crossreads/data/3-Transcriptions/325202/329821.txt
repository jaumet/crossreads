<p>Aug 16 Rubempre<br/>have been here 3 days on detachment duty with D.E.</p>
<p>The 1st Div. is 'going in' again &amp; we leave this afternoon for Albert</p>
<p>Aug 17 Albert<br/>arrived last night and are camped near a long 12" Naval Gun on a railway mounting which was firing as we passed &amp; nearly sent the horses silly</p>
<p>Have been out this morning distributing rations to several working parties Guns of all sizes firing but saw very little reply from the Huns.</p>
<p>Passed through what was La Boiselle but should not have known it had been a village unless told &ndash; Not a wall</p>
