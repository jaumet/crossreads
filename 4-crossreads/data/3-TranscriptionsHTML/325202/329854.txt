<p>notice things &amp; give us credit for any criticism <strike>of their doings</strike> &amp; memory</p>
<p>one day a Major came into the mess hut &amp; warned us "not to waste <u>biscuits</u> as economy was of great importance &amp; every <u>individual</u> effort was necessary" That day I was up at the Quartermasters Hut &amp; saw in his waste box two slices of <u>buttered toast thrown away</u> that I would have willingly paid a franc for, for we are on short bread rations in the mess hut &amp; have to make up on biscuit. Also <strike>the following</strike> the following day the officers gave a dance &amp; we had to clean out the mess hut &amp; I was one that was set to shred out about 7 lbs of <strike>Gover</strike></p>
