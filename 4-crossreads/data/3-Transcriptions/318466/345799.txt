<p>Sunday 9 June 1918<br/>Had tea &amp; comp. Tennis at the Hotel.</p>
<p>Monday 10 June 1918<br/>Arrived in Town in the evening from the River &amp; heard that I was for a board on the 11th.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11 June 1918<br/>Went up to the Board with Bull &amp; got two weeks convalescence.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12 May 1918<br/>Toddled down to St. Neots with Rup &amp; stayed at the X Keys Hotel.</p>
<p>Thursday 13 June 1918<br/>Market Day &amp; looked out for the Magers but did not see them.  On the river most of the day.</p>
<p>Friday 14 June 1918<br/>Drove thro' Hunts &amp; called on the Magers later.  Had our lunch on the roadside near Offord.</p>
<p>Saturday 15 June 1918<br/>St. Neots is very sleepy and I don't think they know there is a war on at all.</p>
