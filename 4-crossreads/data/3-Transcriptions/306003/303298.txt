<p>24.3.16<br/>Fri 24th<br/>Put in a quiet day under the palm trees teaching the new hands how to wag a flag;  Sergeant Warden went to Tukh-El-Khail this morning &amp; I asked him to bring out form for me from the Orderly.  I filled it in this evening &amp; took it to Major Smith &amp; asked him to recommend me for a commission as a Signalling Officer &amp; he promised he would forward the form with his recommendation tomorrow;</p>
<p>The Troop had musketry again this morning &amp; a route march again this afternoon;  The weather is getting very warm;  An order came today to say applications for transfers to the Artillery Column would be considered;  Received a parcel of socks, balaclava &amp; mittens from home today.</p>
