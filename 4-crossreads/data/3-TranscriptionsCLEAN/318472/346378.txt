
1 May 1918Wednesday1 After Trinity
Got up late and after breakfast went to Rouken Glen which is a very beautiful place - quite the most beautiful park or public grounds I have seen about Glasgow. In the afternoon I went out to Govan & met with Mary, Georgie & Peggy to some friends place out near the University.
Altogether spent a nice day.
1918 June 3MondayTravelled per train to Balloch -thence by boat up Loch Lomond as far as Inversnaid. Took rowing boat across Loch - about two miles & walked down to Tarbet. Took train from there to Glasgow Via the Gare Loch & Loch Long arriving back about 5.30 P.M. The trip was altogether was a very beautiful & enjoyable one. In the evening I visited my friends the Boyds at Govan - altogether had a very enjoyable day.
