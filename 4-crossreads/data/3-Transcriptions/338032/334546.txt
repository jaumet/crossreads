<p>all shore leave, &amp; at Melbourne we were passed from the Ship into the train waiting for us in the pier on Sunday Dec. 30th.   At Albury, after a good Supper, we were placed in sleeping cars for a comfortable rest, arriving in Sydney on the last day of the old year.  There is a mixture of joy &amp; sadness about the homecomings.   Many of us feeling fit &amp; in fairly good health were scruffy looking forward to a joy expedition combined with a determination not to leave Sydney, again a hurry.
