  
 Thursday Aug 16th Camels & equipment handed in today. Moving on Saturday Friday Aug 17 Packing up leaving early tomorrow. Saturday Aug 18 Reveille 3.30 AM. Told off for baggage Guard stations. Main body left at 7pm. I. J.B. & Cpl M leaving by luggage train at midnight. We placed all gear in truck sealed it & spent a wild day in Cairo. Who knows we may not see it again. A small half starved little dog followed us about all day so we took him on the strength & took him with us.
