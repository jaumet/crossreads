<p>the noses of the horses formed by their breath condensing &ndash; pulled the blankets over my head at night &amp; woke up to find where my mouth was &ndash; it was frozen hard &amp; covered with a white hoar frost on the outside of the blanket &ndash; it was my breath condensing</p>
<p>Great difficulty in watering horses as the troughs &amp; shell holes were frozen solid &ndash; While washing was just a neglected detail.</p>
<p>Seven days of it did for me &amp; I had to report sick &amp; was at once ordered away to hospital with Bronchitis after passing through various Clearing</p>
