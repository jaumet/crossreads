 usual there was more cheering. Altogether we were 3 nights &amp; two days in the train &amp; finally we were landed at our destination at about 5 am in the morning at a place called "Eklingham" &amp; marched to a place called "Renescure" 
  In Hospital  After we had stayed in "Renescure" for 3 weeks we made a move up towards the line &amp; went into the line at a place called Paten -Hall. the following day I became very sick &amp; was sent to No.7 General Hospital. with mumps. I was very Crook for a few days.  I came down here on the 21 April. 
 On the 12th May I was discharged from Hospital &amp; set out to join up my unit which I did not reach until 4 days afterwards &amp; finally found them at a place call "Fleuabere" I can tell you I was very pleased to see all the boys again they were out of the line &amp; in supports. 
 30.5.16 on the evening of the 18.5.16 I got a terrible shock when they came &amp; told me to get ready to go on 7 day leave to London.
