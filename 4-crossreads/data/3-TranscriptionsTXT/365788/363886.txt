Fred 1st Field Amb1 Aus Brigade1 Aus DivisionAus Intermediate BaseEgypt 24/2/16My own dear Sister Irene,I am today acting as guard over a water cart - & am taking advantage of the spell to write to you. I wrote to Mrs Carter & thanked her for the sox she was so good as to send me. I enclose herein a map from a Railway Time Table & have marked on it where we are camped - so if you call to mind w your last journey to Australia you will very likely be able to picture me as I sit on the sand of the Desert & write this. The water cart guard is a priviledged person, is not made stand up all the time, so I am not neglecting my duty by writing to you.There are some miserable little gnats here like flying
