<p>1915<br/>Aug. 7 Sat.<br/>Kept pretty busy to-day as we admitted about 90 wounded &ndash; all stretcher cases &ndash; last night.<br/>One man died to-night, diagnosis: Enteric.<br/>In the evening with a couple of others, visited 2 of Cairo's largest &amp; finest mosques &ndash; One of them in particular looked splendid: beautiful carpets &amp; rich carvings &amp; ornamentation in a blaze of illumination &amp; the Mahommedans chanting their prayers.</p>
