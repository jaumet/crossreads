<p>Ministry of Munitions of War,<br/>Whitehall Place,<br/>London, S.W. 1.</p>
<p>17.xii.17</p>
<p>Dear Judge,</p>
<p>Just a line to wish you all the best possible in this so called festive season of Yuletide.</p>
<p>As you will see from the above, I am still engaged on War work, though not of a desperately dangerous character.  I like the work, it is infinitely preferable to the Law &ndash; but the hours are rather long, and I come here on Sundays.</p>
