
Fri July 14th - 16Left Flexincourt at 7PM and proceeded to Flesseles where we bivouacked for  the night but I had to go on stable picquet so was done out of my night's sleep.
Sat July 15th - 16With 1/2 hour's notice we left Flesselles at 8.30AM and proceeded to our next camp at Puchevillers a distance of about 20 miles arriving at 1,20AM.  Here we are about 25 S.W of Albert [actually about 15 miles WNW of Albert].
