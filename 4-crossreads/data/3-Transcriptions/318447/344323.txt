<p>&amp; slipped through a little neck between the Turks &amp; the sea, caused by the Turkish Right flank surrendering &amp; came on up the coast about 10 miles or perhaps more. Now we are into it again close to a Jewish settlement which is here, it is a pretty little town quite clean &amp; English in appearance full of Russian French &amp; English Jews, who were delighted to see us &amp; gave us oranges &amp; bread etc etc, very acceptable.</p>
<p>We had our battery on a hill just above the town &amp; it was very funny to see the womenfolk &ndash; which are very pretty</p>
