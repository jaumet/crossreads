 Just now I am living in a beautiful chateau overlooking lawns gardens and woods. The trees are just beginning to burst into blossom and in a week or so the place ought to look very pretty. 
 But I'm afraid the scene is very different to what it would be in peace time. The place is alive with troops, horses, tents, motor lorries wagons and motor cars spread out all over the place and the chateau is devoid of furniture so that things are quite different to the days when the Comte proudly overlooked his property from his bedroom window &ndash; c'est la guerre.
