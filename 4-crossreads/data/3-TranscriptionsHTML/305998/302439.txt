<p>Nov. 3/11/16. The New Zealand have taken our infantry's places in the trenches, they have just come up from the Somme where the took the village of Flers &amp; other small villages they were cut up badly. We expect to be relieved by N.Z. Artillery on Tuesday which will be the 6th Nov.  I expect we shall find it</p>
