<p>We passed through Abbeville Rouen Durnatel and many other towns of importance finally reaching Harfleur about 12 noon yesterday 29th.  I marched the party of about 200 men up to the office O.C. Harfleur Base Camps handed in my papers and the men were then taken in hand by adjutant.  I came on myself to my own Divisional Base and reported to the adjutant here and am now</p>
