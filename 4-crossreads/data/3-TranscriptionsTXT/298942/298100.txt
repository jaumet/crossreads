
Sunday 5 May
This morning afternoon Cecil Rogers (who has acquired a reputation for "saloaping") & I went into Fletre to see what there was to get.  Fritz shells the village with heavy stuff every day, but he had a good smack at it in the morning so we decided to chance it.
