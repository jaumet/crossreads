<p>just detrained &amp; are in a rest camp overlooking the harbour The town in the distance looks <strike>an insignificant</strike> a very ordinary little place &ndash; before the war just a port of call for coastal trade but now it is an important base <strike>for</strike> both naval &amp; military for the Macedonian <strike>Front</strike> &amp; Turkish Campaign &amp; I don't suppose there was ever so much work &amp; money in the place before.</p>
<p>The country has been very monotonous these last two days &amp; we are all glad to have got this stage of the journey over &ndash; 8 days in the train</p>
<p>Oct 3. <strike>S.S.</strike> H.M.T. Ormonde Glasgow<br/>was on</p>
