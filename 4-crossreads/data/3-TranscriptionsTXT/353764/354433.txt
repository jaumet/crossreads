One of them would sing just in French &amp; then English.  She received a great ovation. I received the parcel of pipes &amp; tobacco safely &amp; have written by this mail.  I have got a pretty bad cold in the throat &amp; it troubles me a good deal during the night. 
 29.3.16 Wednesday Weather very hot &amp; health far from good.  I think I have got the Influenza. I went for rather a weird ride last night with a couple of Sergeants. The night was black as pitch &amp; our road ran through some thickly timbered ground &amp; squalid Mud built Villages.  On one side was a deep gully &amp; the other was bordered by filthy smelling Native Villages - at 8.30 the weather suddenly changed &amp; it blew a hurricane the Horses were very spirited &amp; as we could not see where we were going, it was very exciting.
