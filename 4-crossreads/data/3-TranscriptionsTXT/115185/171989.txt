
3 - the time our poor Gibson was drilling in the desert here all those dreary months. The men were glad to have their own country women to take out and converse with and so these women of the dull life - met with unexpected sprees. Then the troops went to Gallipoli - then they had the excitement of nursing their revelators on their return from battle. Then the hot Summer came and no more troops or excitement. Then less troops or excitement. Then less troops, now practically no troops of the sort - who have time and money to trip about  - so when we come to Egypt we met a lot of discontented women all wanting to go to France - dreading another Summer & hating tombs.
