 [Thursday January 6 1916] 
 Cold here now &amp; it would be Blizzard at Anzac.  Please thank Day for Bed soks &amp; May for her Parcel; all received safely. Had 2 teeth filled today. Busy getting Squadron equipped &amp; ready to move any moment [Following paragraph is crossed out]  Now that we are reforming men &amp; officers are turning up from all points of the Compass  They kept quiet while a Winter at Gallipoli was expected &amp; enjoyed themselves in England &amp; Scotland-  All ranks say they have been treated like princes in the old country
