<p>27th<br/>The boys have had a win though we are assured that is only a temporary <strike>ly</strike> arrangement.  However that may be we are going into action as the 54th Bn.  The good old 54th.  Every body is delighted.  The men are taking it <strike>more</strike> very soberly though.</p>
