All the mounted troops are to be sent indecipherable off the desert & all the Camelry will be kept here to do the Desert patrolling etc. We are all looking forward to the campaign in Palestine & shall be glad to once again get into fertile & interesting country. Some of our mounted men have already penetrated to within 15 miles of Jerusalem. The Turks have lately been getting a bad time on all fronts & during last week the British had a great victory in the capture of Kut-el-Amara & Bagdad.
