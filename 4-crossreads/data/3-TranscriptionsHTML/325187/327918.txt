<p>[Saturday 24 June 1916]</p>
<p>You might order from Chorley Military Tailor Sydney a Light Horse Puggaree for Felt Hat they know size of Hat tell them to post to my address Egypt<br/>Australasian from Joe 2/5/16<br/>Letter from Mother 7/5/16 &amp; Joe 7/5/16<br/>  "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strike>Da</strike> May 7/5/16<br/>Also D'quin Papers-<br/>Please thank all<br/>Sleeping mostly today 25% of Horses on the sick list.<br/>Our Interpreter says "We shoot men like birds" (ie [indecipherable])<br/>We travel in the night like Robbers.  We stand to arms the best part of the night &amp; live on Bully &amp; Weevil Biscuits both sides of my stomach &amp; marmalade I'm sick with Brackish Water &ndash; I want to go home I &amp; my donkey are tired.</p>
