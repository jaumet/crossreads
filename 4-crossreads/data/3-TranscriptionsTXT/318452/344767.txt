Sunday 7thThe Batt. Came back to supports today.
Monday 8thWe moved back to Dickebush huts, & the Batt. came out to here, tonight in the driving wind & rain.
Tuesday 9We left again this morning, by Motor busses, for Connaught, lines near Abeele, where we are in tents We are back from the line about 8 miles.
