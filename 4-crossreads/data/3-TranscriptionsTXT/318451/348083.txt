 Friday Mch. 9th Nothing much doing today. Weather still cold.  Snow falling slightly. 
 Saturday Mch. 10th Went to Trenches this afternoon &amp; made arrangements to go down tomorrow to line.  It has not been quite so cold today, although in sheltered places the ice was unbroken all day.
