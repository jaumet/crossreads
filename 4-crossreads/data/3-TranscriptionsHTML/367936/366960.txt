<p>Saturday 8 April 1916<br/>Swim as usual.<br/>My arm very stiff from the enoculation.  Squadron drill all the morning.<br/>Caught the 12.20 train, had dinner in town, came over to Mosman.  Boys at Springwood.  Vida, Aunt K and I went to "So Long Letty".  Great fun.</p>
<p>Sunday 9 April 1916<br/>Rained all day.<br/>Went over to Telarah also round to Hartly Vale where I met Dora.<br/>Mr. Sams gave me a pipe.<br/>Came back to camp by 5 to 11 train arriving at a quarter to one.</p>
