<p>to the Ploegstert sector. And took over the reserve trench on the 19th Dec. On fatigues at night carrying duckboard &amp; retions up to the Front line. We had a lovely time oing over the frozen tracks. I dont think there was a man who did not have a fall (Duckboard used for covering the bottom of the trenches) On Xmas Eve 24th Dec moved up and took over the Front line. On a listening post <s>which</s> I once a shell hole We had no room to move</p>
