<p>On Xmas eve we left Boulogne   by train. The day was cold so  <strike>we</strike> as there were only three of us we constructed a stove in the carriage. We arrived at Cambrai early on Xmas morning  &amp;  breakfasted at the club. After this we set out for Catellon,  &amp;  were lucky enough to secure a French lorry to Caudry. It was a beautiful morning, bright  &amp;  frosty. Our next left was to Le Cateau  &amp;  here we had our Xmas dinner of bread  &amp;  butter at the corner of the street on the road to Cantillon. A large number of French refugees</p>
