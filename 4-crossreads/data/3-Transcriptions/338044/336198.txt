<p>for a wash. Afterwards I enjoyed the luxury of a shave &amp; long wash in the cool water &ndash; a treat denied to us during the three days in the train. Feeling now refreshed, we started to look about &amp; had a walk until 11 pm, when we paraded for instructions. Issued with bread &amp; hot tea &amp; we made a good meal of boiled bacon, bully beef, bread &amp; tea &ndash; about the best we had for some days. At 2.15 pm another parade was called &amp;  we marched out on a route march to the wharves, arriving there at 3.30 pm, unloading all our cases from the waggons (hard work for two hours) put on our coats &amp; marched back to camp. We are now issued with blankets &ndash; one each for this nice cold weather - &amp; after having tea I had a little    <strike>lit</strike> walk &amp; it now being very cold, I am feeling rather tired    &amp; shall not be long in getting to sleep, although the the bed is not of the best</p>
