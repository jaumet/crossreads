<p>Diary</p>
<p>From Jan 1st 1919</p>
<p>to</p>
<p>Jan 1st (Paris. cont.)</p>
<p>New Year&#39;s Day -</p>
<p>Army &amp; Navy Leave Club</p>
<p>Windsor Hotel</p>
<p>With my friend I visit Bois-de-Boulonge, a reserve of 2.000 odd acres once used for hunting but now an immense park greatly frequented in summer.&nbsp; It has artificial lakes &amp; cascades and is most popular with horse riding parties.</p>
<p>Return to town and thence to Wagram.</p>
<p>A cold &amp; rainy night.</p>
