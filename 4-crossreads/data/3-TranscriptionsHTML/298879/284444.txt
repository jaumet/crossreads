<p>September 1915<br/>Wednesday 29<br/>Fine nothing of note to record. More talk of a canteen being erected. If they would pay us a little more oftener, there would be some sense in it    Splendid British success in France. Many prisoners including 200 officers and a large number of big guns and machine guns captured.</p>
<p>Thursday 30<br/>Bgde. orderly again. Cricket matches and two or three football games in progress.</p>
