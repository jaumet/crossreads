<p>but by the time this letter arrives with you, I shall be back again in La Belle France.</p>
<p>I had a letter from John last week:  full of news about affairs in general:  the taxes look like being strenuous in New South Wales, and money cannot be as plentiful as usual.</p>
<p>Personally I am spending more than at any other period of my life, as I intend to get all I can out of this next six weeks.</p>
<p>But we work long hours, 8.30 till 6 p.m. every day except two per week, when we sleep here in Barracks and start at 6 a.m. instead of 8.30 a.m.</p>
<p>We have Sundays off, except Church Parades.</p>
