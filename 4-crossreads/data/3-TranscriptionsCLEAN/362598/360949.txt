
Sunday 29 October 1916B. meets Alick Bax.  Receive Parcel from Bess.  Very wet & rainy.  I didn't go to church.  Wrote to Dad (3 cards), Mum (Xmas card), Mum.  Sent view to Marie.
Monday 30 October 1916Recd. letter from Marie.  Wrote Dad, Geo., Jennie & P.C. to Bess.  I am Canteen Corp. & don't go on Duty till 8.40 p.m.  Marg. & Dulce & Marie 2 P.C's.
Tuesday 31 October 1916Wrote to Marie.  Major Raffan visits us.  Ellis mags all night so that I can't write.  I finish up about 10.30 p.m.
Wednesday 1 November 1916We fix the Electric Light on properly.  Wrote to Maynie & Queenie.  Dave comes over for supper.
Thursday 2 November 1916Receive letters from Roy & I go out for my washing & send films to be developed.  Wrote to Frances, Nettie, Jack Preddey & Ada Cohen.  Ellis & I go to the Pictures.
Friday 3 November 1916Recd. letter from Marie.  Wrote to H. Hey, Dad, Roy & Marie.
Saturday 4 November 1916Ellis goes to Amesbury with W. Ham.  Nin & I go to Tuti-Fruiti.  We take Photos round the Ground.  Gen. Moore comes around.  I get a new pair of Boots.  Staff issued with Cigarettes.
Ellis meets Leo. Baker in Amesbury.  I dream about M. & apricots.
