<p>Dec 13th<br/>Spent most of the remaining part of rest in arranging concert.  Stage managed the whole &amp; produced burlesque after writing same, leaving the musical directing to Tom Smith.<br/>Gave three shows &ndash; Thursday last to 12th Batt &ndash; Sat to 3rd - &amp; Monday to civilians.  Voted success on last occasion, but the 3rd show marred by undeserved allusions made by Burnett to Q.M.S &amp; cooks.
