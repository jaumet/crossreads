<p>30 June 1915<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Put in part of the day in going along our firing line   &amp;  having a look at the dead Turks who are lying in heaps in front of our trenches</p>
<p>Heavy thunder storm overtakes us while returning from a sapping job at 8.30 pm   &amp;  wets us   &amp;  our camp while we are away</p>
<p>1915 July 1<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Sun shines brightly again this morning   &amp;  the wet is soon forgotten</p>
<p>Six men wounded while getting water this morning by shrapnell</p>
<p>Sapping again 4 to 8 pm</p>
