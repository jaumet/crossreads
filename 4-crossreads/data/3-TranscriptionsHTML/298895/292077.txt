Some of the tommies arrived today.  Weather fine again.  Artillery and aircraft active during the day.<br/>Monday 21st May.<br/> O.S. again so had a bit to do getting the camp fixed up.  Handed over to the new crowd, the 61st. Amb. at midday &amp; marched to "Avesnes les Bapaume."  Here we took over a Gas Case hospital from the 3rd. &amp; I met some of the old 1st. stationary chaps who had been sent out for duty in the field.  Had a look at the cemetary where two of our bearers were buried &amp; later inspected a Wilds Cooker at the C.C.S. which is a wonderful affair &amp; does an extraordinary amount</p>
