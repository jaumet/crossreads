<p>Well, Mum, it is just on tea-time &amp; I must go &amp; have a snack.  We are to have a little card party in our Flat to-night just Dick, Tommy W., Ellis &amp; I with supper to follow of course.  Kind Regards to all my Friends, to Otto, Amy &amp; His 10 children.</p>
