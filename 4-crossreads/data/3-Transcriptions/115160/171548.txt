<p>another draft for which I am very grateful. &nbsp;I wrote you some time ago on the matter and will be pleased if you will discontinue until such times as I again write you. &nbsp;I am still holding well over here. &nbsp; What you have sent will not come amiss and will be handy when I want it.</p>
<p>I enclose a bill for some purchases I made when finishing with my leave. &nbsp;It was my biggest expense.</p>
<p>Well, Mither, things are very cheerful looking, aren&#39;t they. &nbsp;Germany has come a gutser at last and left to battle away on her own. &nbsp;On paper it looks as if the end will come any minute, but I some-how have the idea she will stick it to the last fence hereself. &nbsp;Once&nbsp;</p>
