  
 25/9/17 We are billeted at a farm about two kilo's from Wateau. 
 There was a good deal of aerial activity last night and Fritz dropped a number of bombs on different villages round here. The battn. marched into billets here this afternoon. 
 I believe our casualties for the whole affair was about 200 and about 20 of these were killed.
