 5. place for surf bathing. 
 At night it is all lit up with various coloured lights - very pretty. Mixed bathing of course. 
 It is a very expensive town. Lately they have had thousands of troops passing through - New Zealanders & Australians, all of which must be good for trade. 
 There is a great shortage of Literature on board - so I laid in a supply. How long it will last I don't know, for the borrowing on board of every article is  very bad . 
 We have of course had the usual amount of sickness - colds, measles & influenza, & two or three cases of Meningitis.  I have not yet visited the doctor, & hope I never shall. You never know what the Medical gents mat discover; and
