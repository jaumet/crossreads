<p>passing islands small &amp; large &amp; they presented a fine sight A few of them had light houses on the summit. They seemed of volcanic origin : just poked up out of the water : one especially seemed like a volcano &amp; had valleys running to the water's edge which put one in remind of lava that had broken away. They all seemed very barren ; passed  a lot of large steamers (eight) Had a very interesting bible class : no physical class after tea : Lecture on first aid &amp; arabic language during</p>
