<p>had a sort of pudding but it was that sweet that we could hardly eat it so our Xmas was not to flash was it.<br/>We had a very nice run coming across we passed some islands here &amp; there &amp; very pretty some of them look in the setting sun the water round this part of the world is a very pretty blue as we sailed south we could notice the weather getting warmer &amp; warmer,  our only danger now was the submarines &amp; we kept a pretty strict look out for them, we had to carry our lifebelts wherever we went &amp; that is an infernal nuisance hanging round you.</p>
