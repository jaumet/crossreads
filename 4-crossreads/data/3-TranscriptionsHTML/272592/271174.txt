<p>however I am not banking on it as yet after my last experience of going away to a School of Instruction</p>
<p>To-day Len has been gazetted full Corporal &amp; he should have had it long ago but for pure accident</p>
<p>I have withdrawn my Platoon from the firing line &amp; am now in "supports  We are still dropping bombs &amp; shells on old Fritz &amp; rolling his barb wire up lovely, they are making a good job of it.</p>
<p>I done a bit of firing last night with a Lewis gun they are little bonzer's &amp; very light &amp; deadly.</p>
<p>Got a quantity of stuff up from the Bde canteen this morning for we run one almost in the firing line &amp; it is a great success</p>
