<p>April 1918<br/>Thursday 4</p>
<p>In Billets &ndash; 7pm .12 mid on Road.<br/>Spent morning cleaning up bits, and irons.<br/>Left for exercise arrived back after dinner.<br/>Great number of Tommies miving around village.<br/>Went along to Sarton and had a snack; just finished when Percy G. arrived in breathless haste and announced the news that Battery was moving out.<br/>Caught up Batt as they were moving out of village and at mid shell on road.</p>
