<p>Monday Oct 29.10.16<br/>roll call 5.30 fed up Breakfast watered camels dinner 12 rested till 2 P.M.  fell in 3.P.M. semaphore instruction wrote to alma Josie<br/>Tuesday Oct. 30.10.17<br/>roll call 5.30 fed cleaned up Breakfast  camels out to graze dinner 12.  rested till 2.P.M. fell in semaphore instruction tea 5.P.M. wrote to Maud veronica<br/>Wednesday Oct 31.10.17<br/>roll call 5.30 fed up Breakfast went for water got rations and left Serapeum 10.A.M. for a 4 days Patrol trecked 8 mile had dinner moved off again 1.30 went 10 miles cameped at some huts near Bailwill windy night nothing doing turned in.<br/>Thursday Nov.1.11.17<br/> turned out 5.30 A.M. fed up had Breakfast</p>
