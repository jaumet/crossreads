  
 1919 June 1 the battery in his car was worn out & he could not get a new one.  So we sang at the piano instead.  In the evening we went for a walk & saw the Church in the Woods, build by the devil according to popular tradition.  I wont vouch for the statement, but it has undoubtedly been there a long time.
