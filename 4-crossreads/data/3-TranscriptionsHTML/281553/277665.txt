<p>Nov. 30 Tuesday<br/>Bright &amp; clear but very cold, doing little pick &amp; shovel work to get warm.  Walked to Beach Sap for rain water lying in hollows covered with thick ice &amp; dirty but lovely after long fast, got back 3-30 p.m., made up diary.  Collected Mess tin full of snow to melt down for water.  Lone Pine bombarded terribly by Turks gun fire  reported 150 Casualties on Tuesday.  Today's rations bacon &amp; tea for breakfast.  Bully &amp; Bisc. for dinner.  Tea same as dinner.</p>
<p>Dec. 1st Wednesday<br/>Day off duty, collected wood &amp; rain water for trench use.  Cooking &amp; eating most of afternoon.  Evening very keen, day glorious sun.  Fired [rocket ?[ opp No. 2 Bomb Pit.</p>
