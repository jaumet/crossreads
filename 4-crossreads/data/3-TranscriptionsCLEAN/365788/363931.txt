
North of France3 May 16My dearest Irene,I was very pleased to get another nice little letter from Margaret the other day. -How well she is getting on old Girl.We are at present living in a village about 4 miles from the trenches & our other section are acting as stretcher bearers out at the trenches & also runs a huge hot bath & clean clothes concern in a village near us.
