  
 Tuesday 1 July 1919 Had drive & then went down to Brighton. Left from London Bridge. Had lunch at Brighton. It rained a bit. Janet & I went for a walk & then had tea in Kemp Town & afterwards she had to go back for duty. Got the 6.42 to London Bridge. Had something to eat & then came home to bed. Cos it was cold. 
 Wednesday 2 July 1919 Rained some this morning. Had my lesson, lunch at Arding & Hobbs. Went in to town in [indecipherable] met Bill & then we met Trix & she took me into the city & we did some shopping. Tried to get a coat for Mother but couldn't find one till we got to Oxford St. Bought scarves for the girls, Mother & Janet. Went round to Harry Brevitt & had tea. Didn't leave him till 8.30. Bill & he were almost blethered. Both very merry. Then we met George & had dinner. Went to Bills place & read & then came home to bed.
