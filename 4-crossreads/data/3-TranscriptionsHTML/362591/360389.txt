<p>I suppose you've heard that Caesar has gone to the 54th or some such outlandish battalion, &amp; has consequently been left behind.  I've heard nothing of Evan Richards, &amp; Walter is still the Brigade's Orderly Officer.</p>
<p>Having just been handed orders which require immediate attention I'll have to close.  From them I gather that by the time you get this we will have been two or three weeks in the trenches.</p>
<p>With love to all<br/>Your affectionate son<br/>Arthur</p>
