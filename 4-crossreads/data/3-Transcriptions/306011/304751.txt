<p>I used to go to school with him once. And by a curious coincidence I met a chap from Lindfield (Wynne, Gladstone Parade) who knew Allen Fry and I showed him the grave.</p>
<p>Wed 4th July 1917</p>
<p>Went for a walk yesterday evening over to Varennes, 4 &frac12; kiloms. along a white road bordered by well cultivated fields in which old men &amp; women were working industriously while all the able-bodied men are away fighting in the French sectors. The wheat &amp; oats are ripening</p>
