<p>10.am Fritz is trying to locate one of our new batteries on the hill.</p>
<p>26 December 1916 Tues<br/>Letter from Horace saying loss of arm. Received letter from Mrs. Peter McLeod. Canada aeroplanes came out this morning 25 in a bunch. Fred Searle evacuated.</p>
<p>27 December 1916 Wed<br/>Up to eyes in work. Notified by [Cop] to get ready for England. Left Becordel 9.30 pm. with Harry Benson. Car pickup up [Mac] going to Albert Station Station in ruin</p>
