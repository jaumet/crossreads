<p>Saturday Nov 10th<br/>Had a days leave went to Bailliue rainy day<br/>Sunday 11th Nov<br/>Marched out of camp for line arrived and camped in huts &amp; Locre for night<br/>Monday 12th<br/>Detailed for a job of guarding enemy prisoners &amp; No 1 Cage &amp; Bailleue arrived here about ten got leave to Bailleue evening<br/>Tuesday 13th<br/>Fell in for parade morning also medical inspection]</p>
