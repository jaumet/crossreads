<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - &nbsp;R.A.</p>
<p>To - Brigadier</p>
<p>Date - 14/9/14</p>
<p>Time - 6.20</p>
<p>Following received from Comdr. Beresford. Have had no news whatever from Colonel Watson nor has firing been heard. German residents&nbsp;<s>here</s>&nbsp;are of opinion that no resistance will be offered but enemy will keep retiring for many miles (1745)</p>
