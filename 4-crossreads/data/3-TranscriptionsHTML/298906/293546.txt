<p>fairest way, one chap punched the other till he could scarcely stand up, then he had to give in knocked himself right out, but he made a fight of it, the other fellow smothered all the time &amp; scarce made a punch, the little chap was by far the cleverest boxer &amp; the crowd gave him a great reception at the finish &amp; they hooted the big chap, although he won, but he never made a fight of it when they started hooting the Colonel jumps up on the stage &amp; tells them to play the game or he will stop the fight, this quietened the mob, there were 2 more fights after this 1 being a knockout, &amp; the other threw the towel in.<br/>Heavy rain fell again yesterday.</p>
