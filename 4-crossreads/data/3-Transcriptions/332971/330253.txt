<p>The last night, Sunday at about 10 o'clock we stood on a bridge &amp; watched them all pass to the station &ndash; some were singing &ndash; others whistling, a great many waved to us and called out "Good night.  Good-bye"  <u>"And so they gave their lives"</u></p>
<p>Its after <u>lights out</u>.  Sweet dreams.</p>
<p>21st  Twas the procession of the Sacred Carpet.  This morning I saw it again &ndash; Lucky aren't I?  The Hospital has been given over to No 14 &amp; we are practically read to march on.  I can't say more &ndash; because that little word <u>Censor</u> comes before me.  So I must come to a full stop &amp; with very much love to you all.</p>
