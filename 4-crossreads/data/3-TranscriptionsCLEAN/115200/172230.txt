  
 17) No 2. Auxiliary Hospital Cairo 
 Egypt 
 26/9/15 
 today has been very quiet  There has been Church Service twice. I am still in Hospital at Heliopoli. to day there is a rumour going round that Bulgaria has [got?] in with the Central powers. This is rotten news as I thought She would come in on our Side and now that She has gone against us it remains to Be Seen if we can [Lose?] the Dardanelles or not.
