<p>you can see from this, we don't have much time for ourselves, then of course we are always supposed to be ready to move off at a minutes notice in case of an attack<br/>Last night we were on the Dead dog" dump again, but as all the men were not wanted there I had to take 6 down to the "Tin Barn" &amp; take a truck of timber &amp; iron from there, to the reserve trenches of the 4th Battalion, we had no trouble at all on this line, &amp; were finished about 1 oclock.<br/>Yesterday evening there was a magnificent sight, for the sky was just black with aeroplane's, I counted no less 47 of them as they flew past, I expect they were bound for a raid somewhere how old Fritz fired at them but he never done any good for they just kept on going as if nothing out of</p>
