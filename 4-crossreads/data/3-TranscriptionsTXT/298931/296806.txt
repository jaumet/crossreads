 [Oct.-Nov., 1916.] Monday 30 Midnight in an officers mess they are all drunk and yet we privates are crimed for being so Tuesday 31 A drop of spirits is all we have to make our future cheerful but we never get any, at least very seldom Wednesday Nov 1 Drunk as a fool Thursday 2 Sleep and forget the day will come P.R.I 2 ARMY
