<p>Definitions</p>
<p>No. 6 Detonator enclosed in a copper tube.  Closed at one end.  About 1 3/4 inches in length &amp; containing about 35 grains of fulminate of mercury.  Bursting power =  about 20 lbs per sq. inch.</p>
<p>No. 8 Detonator generally painted red and contains about 60 grains of mercury.  Bursting power = 120 lbs. per sq. inch.</p>
