Fri. Aug. 18th .A Coy made a small "stunt" tonight & made new position.
Sat. Aug. 19th.Relieved tonight by the 12th Bn. Some tangle getting out owing to the Blue in the 12th Bn's. Colours looking like Green, at night & causing our men to follow the wrong [indecipherable]. Got lost along the line but eventually we found another way out & reached Albert & billeted in the deserted houses.We suffered from the artillery fire while in line & we had some more losses.Sergeants Ryder & Taylor killed & Lt. Hart & Sergeants Hill & Gove wounded.About half of what our Company went in with are left.Our big guns from right behind us were hitting our own front line & helping towards the muck-up in general.Had about 5 minutes sleep in four days & nights as everyone had to be on the alert.
