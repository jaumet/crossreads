<p>I remember exactly at 12 noon on Christmas day I was with Bill Rigby and Sam Charles looking at one of our Aeroplanes that had been brought down just before the armistice, The two Airman were killed and had been given a decent buriel by Fritz in Solre-Le-Chateau Cemetry. There seemed to be a lot of respect held by the Airmen on both sides for one another and on more than one occasion little things happened that showed this.</p>
