Friday August 30th : Paris
Up at 8 am : left the Club at 10 am in a motor for a trip round the city : drove through the principle Boulevardes etc over the glorious Bridge of Alexander (the finest in Paris) thence to St Cloud : returned via the Bois de Boulogne which is an immense well wooded park with lovely roads through it lakes etc here there : flower beds lovely : passed the arc de triomph thence to the steps in front of the Grand Palace had our photos taken : home at noon : hasty dinner then off to Malmaison the beautiful home of Napoleon the empress Josaphine : the gardens outside were nice plenty of flowers : carriage still in the stable in which they entered the grounds : the interior fittings are genuine save for a little renewing here there everything is still as it was at time of their occupation : very interesting pretty : sent home 17 views of it to Mother : home about 5.30 pm tea : strolled round after tea had some fun in the Boulevarde :
