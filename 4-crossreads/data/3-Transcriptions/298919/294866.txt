<p>we ran into a light fog.  When we had passed the town we could see an aeroplane circling round it.  One of our own watchers evidently, because if it were hostile the couple of cruisers in the harbour would be blazing away at it with their anti aircraft guns.  A few miles South of Dover we passed a cross channel Hospital Ship returning from France with a crowd of wounded soldiers.  Since dinner time the sky has been overcast and we have had a couple of showers of rain and a thunder storm making working rather unpleasant.
