 21st. July, 
 Dear Captain Kilby, 
 I beg to acknowledge receipt of your letter of 10th. July, and also the framed photograph of Their Excellencies The Governor-General and Lady Helen Munro Ferguson. 
 I shall be glad if you will convey to Their Excellencies my sincere thanks for the gift, and also for their kind wishes, which I deeply appreciate. 
 Your very truly, 
 &nbsp; 
 Captain H.H. Kilby, 
 A.D.C. to 
 His Excellency The Governor-General, 
 Melbourne, VIC.
