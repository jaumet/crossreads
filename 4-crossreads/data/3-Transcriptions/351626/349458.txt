<p>Westham Camp Weymouth 4.2.1916.<br/>Dear Mother,<br/>I have not heard from Australia for about 6 weeks now I expect some letters may be side &ndash; tracked somewhere.  The last two days it has been raining cats   &amp; dogs as the saying goes.  We had a parade in the rain to-day which was not too pleasant.  It is likely I shall in due course be going back to Egypt or elsewhere as I have got alright again.  Aunt Bell says Norman's people have not heard from him for some time.  I only had one letter from him it was a long time ago though.  He does not write much at all.<br/>How are things progressing with you all?  There is nothing much to tell you of here it is not much of a place to be in Winter.  We often go to the theatre such as it is.  Syd Stout was</p>
