 32 Warwick Square S.W. 24-x-17 
 Dear Judge. I have two of your letters to answer - and feel ashamed at my negligence in not replying to thank you for writing so regularly, and so interestingly &ndash; I am afraid you have had a bad time lately with these infernal strikes &ndash; but I hope they are now a thing of the past, and that the government have learnt 
 (2) the lesson of taking a firm stand &ndash; Nell Knox wrote me that the girls have to volunteer as cooks etc. on the interstate boats. A pretty state of affairs. 
 You must be glad he has got over it all entirely.
