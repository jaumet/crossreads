 December  1918. Monday 30 
 At Sea. Red Sea. Indian Ocean. Weather warmer by a great deal, and the climate is beginning to swelter along a fair amount. Ran into a heavy ground swell. Passed the twelve apostles early in the afternoon. Played in the Bridge Tournament and lost in the first round by 28 points. Played cribbage for about three hours and lost 2/6 on the deal. Days run very poor. 254 miles only.
