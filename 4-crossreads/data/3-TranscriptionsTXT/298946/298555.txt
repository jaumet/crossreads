Still, we have been able to repay in some small way their kindness in making us free in the bed room without taking any money for it, &amp; doing such friendly service as cleaning muddy boots against our will &amp; giving us unlimited coffee as well as cooking little dainties which we have been able to buy. 
 Dick Barlow has visited us many evenings, &amp; has delighted all of them by sketching them each &amp; individually, which he is able to do in a not inexpert manner.
