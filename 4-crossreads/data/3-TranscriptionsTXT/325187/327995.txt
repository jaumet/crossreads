 [Saturday 9 September 1916] 
 No word from Laura since. 1 Sister in charge assisted by 4 probationer nurses &amp; they work 10 hours a day ring for orderlies. These girls are run on a very strict discipline- Open the door for the Medical Officers etc etc. Cold better but I'm not at all fit some how.  Photo of X Ray Chest I hope to be able to send you, but nothing is  given  away here. Soldier patients too common and numerous  By  With the Experts son from Dungalear who is a  Captain !! now, we went to see the Battle of the Somme. (Pictures)  I just wanted to compare Desert with France.
