<p>February 1918<br/>Tuesday 19</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Left "Pill box" at 6 am, with Mr Kerr, for Forward Observation Post, via Bob trench.<br/>There was a cold hard surface on the ground, and  a knife edged wind.<br/>I had to fall in a shell hole, and make myself a iceberg until the sun thawed me out.<br/>At midday we did our shoot,Baab Farm and Pill ditto.<br/>At 9 pm, we had a stunt and supported our infantry in a raid.<br/>Fritz had his S.O.S. up very quickly<br/>Approached re leave to Australia.</p>
