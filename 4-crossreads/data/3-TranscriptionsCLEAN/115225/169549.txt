Apl 6th I had to act as escort for Sid on his charge, he asked for a court martial & was remanded (asking for a court martial was only bluff & remanding was also bluff)  Frank went out on Bud's lorry - we got the gear box finished & are mobile if wanted  it was fine in the morning but rained all the evening -
Memo.  With the arrival of our 4th Division on the Somme the Germans seem to have brought to a halt at Albert, it seems that we & the French will make a combined counter offensive all along the line -
Apl 7th  We drew went out on supplies for the brigade as they are moving down South. We drew at Steenwerck & hauled to Strazeele & delivered to the individual batteriies - We parked in Strazeele for the night - fine day -
Apl 8th  We started up at 10.30 A.M. & drew at Steenwerck  We made two trips as the brigade are entraining to-night.
