  
 16 December 1916 Sat Bad news again from Geoff saying he had had his arm taken off. 10 am. A battalion of highlands with pipes & drums playing passing by on their way to the line 
 17 December 1916 Sun Wrote to McAlpine. Spent some time with Norm Burgess. He is going to England to night on furlough, whisky & milk. Stapleton an old school chum comes through sick.
