
<p>The villages of Ligny. Ligny Thilloy &amp; La Barque were right close to Bapaume:&nbsp; Each smiling little Hamlet was just a mangled heap of Debris when I last saw &#39;em.&nbsp; Factory Corner was the busiest spot I ever saw behind the Lines.&nbsp; A small heap of bricks represented the spot where the factory had once stood.</p>
