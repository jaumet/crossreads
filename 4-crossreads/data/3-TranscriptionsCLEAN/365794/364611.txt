  
 Sunday 28 July 1917 [1918] Pts. 29.  All convalescent except one, Matheson with fracture. Treatment of stomach cases - Tannic washout, stomach, 1ce daily, on Iod. mist - 4 hrly. [indecipherable]. Malaria.  If B.T. quin. is given as soon as blood film indicates B.T., Quin. grs. x 4 hrly. for 24 hrs., then 6 hrly. for 24 hours, then 8 hrly. for 24 hrs., then 1 d.s. for 3 months (3 weeks in ward).  Apl. daily to remove accumulated quin. & so prevent headaches. One pt. asks for phenacetin to cure headache, told to wait for M.O.'s permission, replies, "Oh but I might be asleep then". Church 6 p.m.  Met C. Garland.  Posted 3 pkts. photos (regt. Pt. 6).  No silver in Egypt now Gyppos having buried it all thinking Turks coming to take possession.  Pt. 5 notes issued.
