<p>Saturday 14th Sept</p>
<p>At midday yesterday it was definitely decided that the ADS should be shifted. Bearers moved off with transport first; then we packed up &amp; waited the usual hour or two. Shelling round about, but not near.</p>
<p>[Line of shorthand follows; not transcribed.]</p>
<p>We were to keep the building as a reserve A.D.S. <strike>but</strike> when we could get Fritz out of his position.</p>
<p>[Lines of shorthand follow; not transcribed.]</p>
<p>[Pointing to shorthand entry:] Refers to big guns all round the A.D.S.</p>
