<p>19 Th July<br/>Arrived at Rafa  went into Detail camp for 24 hours</p>
<p>20 Fri<br/>Left Rafa for Regt at Tel-el-Fara  arrived at 2300</p>
<p>21 Sat<br/>Left Fara for outpost line 5 miles north W. for one weeks duty in trenches and patrols 5 miles to water horses</p>
