 Mar 3rd We change over from the "Nile" to the Erin Pura which draws less water in order to cross the bar before the river.  On guard. 
 Mar 4th We move up the Shal-el-Arab as the river is called, after the Euphrates junctions the Tigris. It is about &frac12; a mile or more wide.  The banks are lined with beautiful green date palms, the stream swarms with craft of all descriptions. Hugh transports, Hospital ships, motor boats, and native boats of many designs.
