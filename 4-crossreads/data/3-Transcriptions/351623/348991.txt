<p>18 Aug 16 )<br/>19 Aug 16 )  Alongside pier<br/>20 Aug 16 )</p>
<p>21 Aug 16<br/>H.M.S. Berwick came in &amp; anchored in Grassy Bay.</p>
<p>22 Aug 16<br/>Collier Zillah came alongside us</p>
<p>23 Aug 16<br/>Coaled from Collier Zillah</p>

<p>25 Aug 16 )<br/>26 Aug 16 )  Alongside pier<br/>27 Aug 16 )</p>
<p>28 Aug 16<br/>Oiled ship from H.M.S. Ceaser &amp; left Bermuda bound for England. We have been 1 year &amp; 8 months in the West Indies &amp; North American Station.</p>
