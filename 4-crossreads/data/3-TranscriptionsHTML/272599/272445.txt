<p>June Sunday 24 1917</p>
<p>A beautiful brilliant day. Jock &amp; self go to Hardleot in aft. A lovely tram ride of 1 hr &amp; quart. thro' woods over hills, change at Pont a Briques. Hardelot Plage fine beach. Convalescent Hosp. No 25. Kaffir sports endless stream hospital blue. Frenchy &amp; the girl. <br/>The pantomime on tram coming home. Quite casual trippers. Voluable Frenchy Make up lost time. Tea at. S &amp; S Club  R.C. Held up again on tram half hour, overcrowded. Driver refuse to start, at last gives up in desperation. Late for parade at 8 p.m.</p>
