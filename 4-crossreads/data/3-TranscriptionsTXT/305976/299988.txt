
We crossed a Wadi I believe it was near Ain Sin[?]. About every 3 miles we passed a patrol Only for these patrols I think we would have been hopelessly lost. Just on daybreak we located advanced dressing station of 2ND LHFA unit. The Casualties were heavy owing to no Artillery.Friday Good 29th MarchWe were exceptionally busy all day. Stretcher bearing, bringing in wounded & despatching them back to next station. There was heavy fighting all day the New Zealand Brigade particularly Auckland Reg suffering severely Our position I believe is known as Birket
