 F 13 Sun 
 We mark out 83 men. Write all afternoon &amp; read at night 
 Wrote to mum 10 Jack. Preddey. Home 6 
 14 Mon 
 Snow (5") Very busy day. Visit Nin who is sick. Jim Robby turns up. I go down through snow but miss him. 
 We talk all night &amp; have big supper 
 W 15 Tues. 
 Very rainy. Go to Sarum. See   [indecipherable]   Girls in fancy dress. Go to Broadway Jones. Awful night. Rain like Torrents all the time. 
 Recd. Letters from Cis. Con &amp; Selfridges 
 16 Wed 
 Snow &amp; Rain. Nasty day. E catches late train to Sarum. Am ord. Corp. Visit Nin. Practise piano. Visit Collins. Do my washing at 9.15 pm &amp;  just finish at lights out. Very lonely nothing to do at night. 
 Wrote to Con. 
 17 Thur. 
 Snow (4") Rain &amp; muck &amp; rubbish. 
 Recd. Greeting Card from Flo &amp; Chas. E. goes to Glasgow. Am Contun corp. Read all night &amp; tidy up room generally 
 Wrote Jinnie 
 18 Fri Dull. Gasticho pretty Bad. Recd. Letters from Marg. Nette  &amp; Dad Cold windy wet day. Stan marries the WAAC at B'ford. The Gen Band plays "Hold your hand out naughty Boy" Wrote. W.H.A. MRL.. 
 19 Sat Dull. Plenty of Rain &amp; slush I have frightful busy day. [indecipherable]  Better. Go to Theatre with being Beashy &amp; Robby. See Camonflage Very good. Dr of MRL.  &amp; church. She is awfully loving. 
 Wrote Nette &amp; Horchas.
