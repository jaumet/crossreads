<p>Wednesday 5 December 1917<br/>Off duty 2-5 with E. Brown so went Cairo &amp; enjoyed life.  Tea Groppi's, bought spirit lamp Pt. 35, 2 Australias Pt. 8.<br/>Busy evening in Connaught.  Capt. Grant aspirated Adams chest, drew off fluid, blood stained N.Z. pt. much relieved.<br/>Ward full, pts. 44.</p>
