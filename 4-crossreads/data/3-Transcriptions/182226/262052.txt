<p>Sunday 30 January, 1916</p>
<p>Church parade in the morning.</p>
<p>Played the Signallers football &amp; beat them 9 to nil. Too much of a crowd in the tent so I slept outside.</p>
<p>Finished all our extra tucker, are waiting for the next man to go into civilisation. Feeling O.K. again.</p>
