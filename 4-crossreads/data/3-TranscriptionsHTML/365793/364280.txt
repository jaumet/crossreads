<p>June 30 Half way up the hill I sat down on a heap of stones by the wayside &amp; watched 2 or 3 Companies of Tommies March past.<br/>Believe they are trying to crime me again &ndash; for something I don't know anything about &ndash; to wit &ndash; the non return of a tea bucket to the cook house.<br/>Girls, did you say? Not for mine &ndash; yet! &ndash;</p>
<p>July 1 Like rain again. Saw the S.M.O. about my leave which he says will be granted, but first I must have my eye attended to here. If Board Papers turn</p>
