  
 10 October 1918 Thursday Medical Officer enoculate me to-day- 1st to Field Ambulance then Amman   Rough ride over the stones & the men suffered- The Hospital is an ex-Turkish one at the foot of the old Amphitheatre to seat 4000 with carved granite Pillars along a drive once 400 yds long- The Amphitheatre is now occupied by Bedouin dogs- As is usual I was robbed of my big oil sheet & Motor goggles off my Hat on arrival!!
