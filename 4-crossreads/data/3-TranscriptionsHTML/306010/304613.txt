The French are jolly decent people, there is no doubt about that, although they think a good deal of themselves. They are intensely patriotic. Care<strike>nothing</strike> very little for their southern neighbours &ndash; the Italians. (82)<br/>Sunday 25th June:-<br/>We have been pretty busy for the past few  days &ndash; no leave being granted at all until after tea. Yesterday afternoon however, general leave was granted &amp; I managed to get away. First paid a visit to the fine big Cathedrale of Marseille which overlooks the Port and will hold 12,000 people. Then walked back to the Fort St John, a fortress of the old Knights of Malta but now used as military</p>
