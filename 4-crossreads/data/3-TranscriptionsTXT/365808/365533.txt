 A.507/4/34. N.F. COMMONWEALTH OF AUSTRALIA. 
 DEPARTMENT OF DEFENCE MELBOURNE AUG 31 1917 
  No.63433  
 Dear Sir, I have to acknowledge receipt of your telegram of even date relative to your son, Lieut. K.A. Ferguson, 4 th Divisional Headquarters, and to state that it is officially reported that he is returning to Australia. A communication containing this information was sent to you on 6 th idem addressed "Supreme Court, Sydney." Information is now to hand stating he is on the Hospital Ship "Kanowna" which is due at Fremantle today. A   letter  lettergram or telegram addressed care of the ship should be delivered without difficulty. 
 Yours faithfully T.Trumble [indecipherable] A/SECRETARY. 
 Mr.Justice Ferguson, Menzies' Hotel,  MELBOURNE
