<p>to you separately but I really cannot find enough news, so my letters home are for you all Lilly included and I so hope you will forgive me.<br/>Now as for coming home well we are just as far off as ever. I mean, to know when but surely our turn will soon come, as we are fed up and want so much to come home to you all.<br/>Harry has gone to Salisbury to a Dance and will not be home tonight. He is just the same as ever always away somewhere as for me I am a stay at home especially as it is so cold. Well I shall have to beg off to bed and will close with fondest love to all at home and kisses for all<br/>Believe me<br/> Your loving Son and Brother<br/>Ellis</p>
