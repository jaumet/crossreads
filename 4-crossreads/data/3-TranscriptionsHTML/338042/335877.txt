<p>May 20th<br/>Preparing new numbers &amp; rehearsing sketches &ndash; Re-writing of diary &ndash; Contentment is returning &ndash; "How to dine with everything, excepting plate, knife. fork &amp; spoon."<br/>May 21st<br/>Daily reading of "New Testament" given me by my "English Mother". A long run of excellent weather continues &ndash; So does the brilliant work of the "Australian Infantry" some hard study &ndash; The growing superiority of our Air Service over that of the enemy.</p>
