<p>Weymouth<br/>Australian Imperial Force<br/>22/11/1918<br/>My Dear Sis<br/>At last I have had a letter from you. I thought you had died or something like that. I heard all about your illness dear but I have written several times since then. However I have a letter now so never mind about the past. I am so glad you are feeling a bit like yourself again  &amp; as for being fat well I think we are a good pair. I was indeed surprised to hear about Vol enlisting but hope the armistice was signed before he sailed. I think he was very foolish to enlist however it is all over now so it does</p>
