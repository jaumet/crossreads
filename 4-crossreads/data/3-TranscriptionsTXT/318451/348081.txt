 Tuesday Mch. 6th Cold again this morning but clear.  Spent the morning writing letters of sympathy to relatives of deceased soldiers, wrote 6.  Have been to Cemetry this afternoon. 
 Wednesday Mch. 7th The weather is bitterly cold with a bleak wind, the glass is below freezing &amp; has been all day.  The ground is frozen hard again.  I have been to Billets &amp; B H.qtrs. this morning.  Visited the Hospital this afternoon.  We had a surprise this morning.
