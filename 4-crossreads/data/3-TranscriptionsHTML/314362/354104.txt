<p>1 Sunday  [July]<br/>Had a good sleep in!  Rained all day! Guns making hell of row. Wrote  much mail.</p>
<p>2 Monday<br/>Weather much warmer- nothing much doing the weekenders had rather a strenuous time!!</p>
<p>3 Tuesday<br/>Beastly hot  &amp; the 'round me'[indecipherable]  man was very energetic!!  Feeling fed up with things somewhat.</p>
<p>4 Wednesday<br/>Quite an interestg morning on trench to trench attack.<br/> no mail from Rup.</p>
<p>5 Thursday<br/>Paid 270 Frs. &amp; sent &pound;5 to Rup.  56th came up to do a stunt on O.Villers. Put in some good revolver shootg.</p>
<p>6 Friday<br/>Concert in evening by the Div. Concert party. Rather putrid   &amp; not to be compared with the "Coves."  Put in for leave</p>
<p>7 Saturday<br/>demonstration to all Corps Platoon Cmdrs.  Into O Villers &ndash; met Major Sumpter [?] &amp; on to Amiens with him.</p>
<p>8 Sunday<br/>up late so went for stroll with Major Sumpter.  Lunched with him  &amp; his  mates also dined with her and her Mater. Also dined with Mrs Trip and Mrs Anderson<br/>9 Monday<br/>Home to Albert at 12 M.D.  up at 6 AM &amp; got back to Aveluy for brekker. Picked for revolver team.  No lres from Rup.</p>
<p>10 Tuesday<br/>Big stunt in morng on attack. Rather bored  &amp; tired. Parcel came from Rup. Heard that Graham [?] was back with the 8th T.B.</p>
<p>11 Wednesday<br/>I took the 5th Div. Ppat. in attack &amp; did quite well. Had the windup Rather!!  saw Major Knight in evening. Parcel from Rup.</p>
<p>12 Thursday<br/>Went to the 32nd for dinner  &amp; saw Col Davies re transfer.  Called on Perkins in hospital</p>
<p>13 Friday<br/>out all day stunt. Shot on the revolver competition &amp; the 5th came second.</p>
<p>14 Saturday<br/>Another demonstration stunt to Platoon Comdrs. Into Amiens with Lamerton.  Tead at Temporaine 1.2. Met Dundas [?] stayed at the du Rhein.</p>
