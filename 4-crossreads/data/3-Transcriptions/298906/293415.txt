<p>"doing their bit."<br/>25th. We were a bit more successful last night with our photography got some new paper &amp; it was good stuff &amp; while we were deeply interested in it, we heard an awful howl &amp; a sudden rush &amp; then a sound of many voices, I slipped outside to see what was going on, &amp; what do you think happened, well it was this, by some means or other, the electricity which lights our Camp, got connected on to the barb wire fence which runs along the huts, &amp; it appears the chaps had been amusing themselves by touching it quickly, they liked the sudden sting &amp; the novelty of it I suppose but one more bolder than the rest, got a grip of it, then there was a stink for try as he would he could not let go &amp; the electricity coursed</p>
