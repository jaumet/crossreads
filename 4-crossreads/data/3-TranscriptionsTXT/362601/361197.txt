 1 May, Thursday 
 F. I am up [?]times &amp; very hungry. I make a hard confession quite easily. M &amp; I go for a walk. We clamber over the hedge &amp; have to get over the water ditch. We sit by the fire &amp; talk all evening. I do ironing. 
 2 May Friday 
 F. Hot shower in afternoon. I get up early &amp; light fire etc. I print P.Cto M. do ironing &amp; Auntie &amp; I go for a walk all around by back road. We get caught in the rains. I sit by the fire &amp; write. I have my nails manicured &amp; thank manicurist. 
 Wrote Dick &amp; D. Op
