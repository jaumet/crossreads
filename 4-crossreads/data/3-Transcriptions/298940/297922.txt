<p>Cross hut offers shelter only at midday &amp; after ten, so the only alternative for spare time is to shiver in the open,. Then (&amp; worse still) the food is bad and just sufficient to keep one alive, while there is nothing to be bought in the Expeditionary Force Canteen but tea, bread &amp; salmon. Lastly there was not prospect of pay for over a week.<br/><strike>He</strike> The doctor (a fat &amp; jovial South African, in great contrast to the thin "aristocratic" type at No 1) told me I should have to be looking better, before he marked me A. Then I worked the broken spectacle</p>
