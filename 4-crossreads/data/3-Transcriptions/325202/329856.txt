<p>Monday (April 23 [about])<br/>We left Etaples on <strike>Wednesday</strike> <strike>Thursday</strike> Wednesday morning &amp; arrived at Albert during the afternoon &amp; camped there the night. Next morning marched down to the gas school &amp; were issued with box Helmets &amp; put through the gas test left there about midday &amp; marched through Contalmaison to Bazentin where we camped the night in some huts.</p>
<p>The country looks more devastated than ever for now there is no traffic on the roads</p>
