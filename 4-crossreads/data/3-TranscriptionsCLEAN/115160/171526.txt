 France 
 Sat. 28 Sept 1918. 
 Darling Mither, 
 Another week nearly gone and I have not yet got those photos away yet.  I have been waiting to get some stout cardboard.  Nor have I sent the registered letter.  But will do so at first opportunity. 
 We had rather a hurried shift and are up a bit further.  We are in a wee village which in common with all the rest about here is in ruins and fouled with rubbish and filth of all sorts. Our hands are getting quite used to the pick and shovel for we dig in nearly everywhere we go now.
