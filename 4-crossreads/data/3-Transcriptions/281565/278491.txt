<p>Day after day we sailed on through smooth seas, under a burning sky with scarce a breeze.  Several attempts were made to relieve the monotony of the trip.  Boxing &amp; swimming were indulged in, concerts &amp; dances held at night so that with one thing &amp; another the time passed quick enough.  On Saturday evening at 9.30 we crossed the Line at 80&deg; E.  The sea was like glass, with not even a ripple.</p>
