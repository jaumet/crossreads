<p>50 Pennsylvania Rd<br/>Exeter 26.11.16<br/>My dear Father &amp; Mother,</p>
<p>Have delayed writing you for few days as have been rather unsettled as to my movements but I had better tell you the whole history. I was to have been discharged from Hospital last Tuesday but on Monday developed a distinct yellow tinge and the Doctor thought I was in for jaundice and postponed my discharge  However, on the Wednesday</p>
