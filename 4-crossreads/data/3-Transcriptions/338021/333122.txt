<p>To O.C. 13 M.G. Coy<br/>Gun at J3d 7.8 blown out. Gun damaged also several belts &amp; boxes (2)</p>
<p>Cpl Pike wounded rather serious. Pte Neal badly shaken.</p>
<p>Send new gun. also N.C.O. &amp; man to replace casualties as soon as possible.</p>
<p>Will return damaged material by same party</p>
<p>Pte Neal will probably be O.K. tomorrow. Cpl Pike evacuated by Stretcher Bearers. Will have to get new position dug during night.</p>
