The CentreSoldiers Wives &amp; Mothers92B Pitt St.August 23rd 1915
Dear Mr. LaytonWe are sending to you an applicant in need of help until she receives her Military Pay &ndash;
Do you think she will be able to obtain assistance this week? as I am afraid she may be placed in awkard circumstances unless she has some help.
Yours sincerelyEthel M. Wallack
