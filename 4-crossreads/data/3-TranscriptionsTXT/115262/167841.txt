 Thursday 15 March 1917. 
 1st  Sham  fight that we (A.C.F.A) took part in.&nbsp; 
  The &quot;white hats&quot; were forced to retreat over the hills &amp; across the &quot;Trawool&quot; bridge. Our No1 dressing station had to be disbanded in the retreat.  
  After dinner on the banks of Goulburn River we were marched in a round-a-bout way to the camp. Dist. 20 miles.  
  No lecture. &quot;Furphies&quot; still going strg.
