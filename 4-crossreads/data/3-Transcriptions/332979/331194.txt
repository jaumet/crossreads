<p>outside into the Street behind the Blackboy Hill Band. After a moment or two the Band Struck up a Patriotic Air. (We must all fall in) &amp; We all marched off on a route March through the Principal Streets. On the March we were Cheered by the Crowds who turned out to give us a hearty send off. I can hardly describe my feelings when marching to the Tune I felt that the time of my life had arrived &amp; I was more determined to do my duty. We duly arrived at the Station were our Kits were sent direct from the Drill Hall. After sorting out each Kit I found myne &amp; when the Train arrived we were told to entrain &amp; off we went to the Camp.
