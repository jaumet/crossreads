<p>near by. As there were a fair Moon &amp; partially clear Fritz was out on a Bombing raid, in fact there were several about &amp; we could hear the Bombs dropping in all directions. After leaving L8 we eventually arrived at St Loop we we were held up there for a long time as the Telephone wires had been broken near Peselhoek &amp; we had to wait until Pilot Working was fixed up &amp; several trains had to come from Peselhoek, When our time came to depart off we went &amp; soon saw what caused the damage, Fritz had dropped two Bombs along of the line &amp; made very large holes blowing the Telephone Wires in all directions, he dropped another Bomb in a A.S.C. Camp &amp; killed &amp; wounded 39 horses &amp; killed the Corporal of the Picket who was off duty &amp; laying down in a Shed, the pieces of the Bomb penetrated the Iron &amp; practically riddled the poor chap &amp; wounded one of the picket. Another Aerial Torpedo was dropped along</p>
