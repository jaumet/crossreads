<p>Sunday 17th February<br/>Same as any other day Fritzy droped a couple of H [indecipherable] near our job just before we knocked off. A lot of Arial activity today, Fritzy got well over our lines several times &amp; got away allright one of our machines came down. Hit with one of our own shells I believe.</p>
<p>Monday 18th February<br/>Still working Fritzy came over the same as he has</p>
