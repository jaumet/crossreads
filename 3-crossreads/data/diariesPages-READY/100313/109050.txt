12/1/18 Oxford London
Rose and breakfasted by 8.30 and then took a fourwheeler for the station catching a train leaving at 9.12. A pleasant swift nonstop run of an hour into London. Taxied from Paddington to hotel and then at once caught a ?bus to the Bank. A fine day with a cold wind. The streets of London are weirdly twisty and irregular. A big proportion of the people are in uniform and many wear the blue armbands or carry some other evidence of being in hospital. The traffic is congested and some streets seem almost wedged with vehicles but it all flows smoothly on like a great river. About the Bank there is a great concentration of streets and the busses don't linger long to let you off. Went into Bank and got a fair number of letters. Returned to hotel and
