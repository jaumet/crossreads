At midnight we commenced to coal ship.<br/>Sep 13th  At 8 am we finished coaling.<br/>Sep 14th  5th Advance leave party proceeded on leave.<br/>Sep 16th  at 8.30 P.M. we proceeded outside harbour to carry out heavy night firing in the Firth returning to anchorage at 11.45 P.M.<br/>Sep 17th  During the morning we carried our sub-calibre firing in the Flow<br/>Sep 18th  At 7 am. we coaled ship and during the morning the 5th Battle Fleet arrived.<br/>Sep 19th  At 8 am. The "Melbourne" "Yarmouth" and two destroyers again proceeded on Convoy duties - all the remainder of squadron together with the Fleet had been on short notice for several days,  We joined our convoy off Kirkwall at noon and proceeded towards Bergen.</p>
