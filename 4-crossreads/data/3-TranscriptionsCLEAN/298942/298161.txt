  
 Thursday 24 May 
 Am on guard to night, & although I cleaned up with great display of energy, failed to get off. 
 Kneeling in the grass at the cross roads writing this up by the light of the lantern I use to signal traffic along the by- road leading past our farm. 
 Very cool wind blowing in great contrast to the heat of late. 
 All quiet & calm.
