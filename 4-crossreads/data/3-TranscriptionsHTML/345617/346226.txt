<p>Octobre 31<br/>No. 2 Section in camp having a rest after their bad day yesterday. Cpl Gough went to Hanebeke this morning &amp; buried the men who were killed yesterday. Sap Busby had been blown right away &amp; could not be found. Enemy aeroplanes are very busy bombing &amp; doing a lot of damage.</p>
<p>Novembre 1<br/>Working at Belgian Battery Corner putting up shelters for H.A.C. Heavy bombing by enemy aeroplanes last night &amp; a lot of damage was done. Two bombs were dropped in our camp &ndash; one Sap was killed &amp; two were slightly wounded. Sap McInnis was the man killed.</p>
