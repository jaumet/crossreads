<p>21st. July,</p>
<p>Dear Captain Kilby,</p>
<p>I beg to acknowledge receipt of your letter of 10th. July, and also the framed photograph of Their Excellencies The Governor-General and Lady Helen Munro Ferguson.</p>
<p>I shall be glad if you will convey to Their Excellencies my sincere thanks for the gift, and also for their kind wishes, which I deeply appreciate.</p>
<p>Your very truly,</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Captain H.H. Kilby,</p>
<p>A.D.C. to</p>
<p>His Excellency The Governor-General,</p>
<p>Melbourne, VIC.</p>
