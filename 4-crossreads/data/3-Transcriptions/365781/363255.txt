<p>Now for my dear Girls and boy I do hope they are strong and well again Dot told me how good Roy was &amp; looked after his Mother well, my Chicken I know is a good girl &amp; will be glad when her Dad comes home, I am answering Dot's letter if I can by the same mail Roy and Linda will be the next Tell Ivy Malone I got her lovely letter. Will also write Mrs Roche(?). If we could only write what we think we could make a decent letter For this time darling I must close<br/>With Tons of Love &amp; Kisses US elf Dot Roy Linda &amp; Amy Your loving Husband Wall</p>
