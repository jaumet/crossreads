9  Sunday  [Sept.]Took church parade. Heard  &amp; was going on leave next Thursday wrote &amp; told Rup.
10 Mondaysent 4 lres over to Blighty by a chap on leave. My leave looks quite promising
11 TuesdayRumours of  an early move. Leave still on. Great excitement.
12 Wednesdaypreparing for Thursday sport. Was paid at Blaringhem. Bought  [indecipherable]and beer at  [indecipherable]13 ThursdayOff to dear old Blighty!!!  Train took 4&frac12; hours to get to Boulogne. Stayed at Paris Hotel.
14 FridayLeft Boulogne at 8 pm &amp; had pretty rough crossing. Reached Town at noon &amp; reported. Dined with Dad at N.10
15 SaturdayMet Rup &amp; Muriel at Victoria in morngshopped in afternoon.  Stayed in all evening
16 SundayFixed things up with the Vicar at St Augustines &ndash; called on Dorothy [indecipherable] in afternoon
17 MondayBusy day with Rup in Town- bought her present. Ring Licence etc. Stayed the night at [indecipherable]18 TuesdayGot married today at St Augustine's Highgate.  Rex Turner was my best man. Went to Wargrave.
19 WednesdayRained all day so we stayed in, played bridge. Wire from HQ for 4 days extension.
20 ThursdayRowed up to Henley with Rup. Hard pullg coming back. Havg quite a happy time!
21 FridayWent to Maidenhead by train  &amp; called on the Webbs &ndash; up to Cookham lunch by electric boat.
22 SaturdayDrove into Reading  &amp; lunched there. Tead at Sonning I took photos Played billiards with [indecipherable].
