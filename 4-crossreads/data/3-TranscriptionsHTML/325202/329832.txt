<p>Brewery to make us a dinner who should I meet that afternoon but Scottie just come into the village with an advance Billetting Party. So he made a fourth.</p>
<p>Madame had 'spread herself' &amp; we had the happiest little dinner imaginable Chicken Broth, Boiled Fowl, White Sauce, potatoes, cabbage, carrots &amp; turnips, a bottle of Vin Rouge &amp; coffee all for 10 francs.</p>
<p>Left Tilques Saturday morning at 3-a.m &ndash; freezing hard - &amp; got to Ardruick Railhead at 7 a.m. Ardruick was a big ammunition depot &amp; had been bombed in July by the German Planes &amp; blown</p>
