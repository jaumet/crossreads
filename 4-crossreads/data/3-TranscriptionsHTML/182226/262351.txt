<p>Thursday 25 November, 1915</p>
<p>Sid had to meet Carrie &amp; take her out rowing. I went out to see Mac at Cattigera [?]. He &amp; Bernie both expect to be sent to convelescent camp shortly.</p>
<p>I played billiards after tea &amp; met Sid after he had left his club.</p>
<p>We slept at the Hotel as usual.</p>
