<p>From - Commander Beresford. &nbsp;To - Flagship &amp; &quot;Berrima&quot;</p>
<p><u>12th Septr. &#39;14</u></p>
<p>(9-30am)</p>
<p>Engineer who reported on Wireless is a man who was leading Telegraphist on board H.M.A.S. &quot;Yarra&quot; three years ago, named Rutzon. Submit wireless expert be landed to report. Distance inland about six miles.</p>
