 Thursday 22&nbsp;April 1915 Working all the morning. Letter to Mrs Hughes (schoolteacher), &quot;Airlie&quot;, Chatswood, N. S. Wales. Writing &amp; reading all day&nbsp; Acting for the O.C. during his absence. B.W.Hosp Salvarsan named Kharsivan &amp; Neokharsivan 
 Friday 23&nbsp;April 1915 Operation for Varicocele this morning. Ride on the plateau between 7 &amp; 8 a.m. Collected some alabaster. Air of the best.&nbsp; Wrote to Dr D. Dunlop, Ed. dptmnt. in Cairo.&nbsp; Ride across the dessert during the morning. 
 Saturday 24&nbsp;April 1915 Saw General Ford at 10 a.m. He was corteous and civil, &amp; I have hopes that he will bring to me relief. Good. Back at Mena at 2-15 p.m. At The Medical school till 1-30 pm. Saw some specimens &amp; looked through some journals. Very good. 
 Sunday 25&nbsp;April 1915 Mass at 7-30 a.m. in Light horse lines. Ride between 5 &amp; 6-45 p.m. Reading &amp; writing rest of day. Day of the gallant charge of the Australians at Anzac Cove, Galipoli. A great feat of arms
