<p>5 March 1916<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Go up to Pyrmont to Communion at Muirs church.  Stay to dinner.  Meet Ella McDonald. Go up to see Mrs Coleman &amp; say goodbye. Thence on to Gardens &amp; meet Tony MacT &amp; J &amp; M. take tea in Hyde Park Kiosk go to St Stephens Church [indecipherable]. Walk up to station &amp; home to camp at 10.30</p>
