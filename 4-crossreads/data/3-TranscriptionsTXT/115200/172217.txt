
4     Heliopolis
Cairo
23/9/15/
I am at present lying in No 2 auxiliary Convalescence Depot. Heliopolis. Suffering from wounds that I received from the turks while fighting them on gallipoli Peninsula. I was 4 months at the front and in that time I have been wounded twice. once on may the 3rd and again on august the 10th  The former I received after our Brigade had made an attack up a very Steep gulley.
