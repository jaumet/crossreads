 15th. Physical jerks again this morning, &amp; I purposely scaled them for I was pretty tired after the picqueting stunt last night. I had a row this morning with one of the most unpopular &amp; rotten good for nothing men as ever breathed Sergt Major Barber of B. Coy. he got the worst of the argument with a vengeance I can tell you.
