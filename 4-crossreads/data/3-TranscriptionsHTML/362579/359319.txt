<p>C/O Major Cuningham<br/>Aust Base P. Office<br/>Horseferry Rd.<br/>London<br/>25.6.17</p>
<p>My dearest Moth   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>I'm on day-duty once more, came of Saturday-morn, so please forgive me if I have missed the mail this week, quite forgot yesterday was Sunday    &amp;  my day for home letter.</p>
<p>Well, Saturday went to bed until 2 p.m. then go[t] up, had some food, dressed   &amp;  left at 2.30 p.m. in motor car for Sports. It was a most perfect day   &amp;  we had a</p>
