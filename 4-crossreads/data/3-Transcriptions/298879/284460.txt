<p>October-November 1915<br/>Sunday 31<br/>Windy. Quiet day. A church service was conducted on the side of the ridge about 6 PM</p>
<p>Monday 1<br/>Our chaps settled in the firing line. We had to shift out of a dug &ndash; out to the let the 3rd Bn Machine Gunners in. Arthur Valentine &amp; I secured a good dugout on the opposite ridge. I am mess orderly for our section today</p>
