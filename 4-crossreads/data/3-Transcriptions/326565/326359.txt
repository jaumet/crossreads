<p>&amp; papers</p>
<p>Monday 8.5.16<br/>Continued fatigues which are anything but pleasant in the heat of the day.  Engineers commenced to build another mess shed in connection with this camp.<br/>New officer not very popular among the boys.  Paid 150 piastres &pound; 1-10-9;                                   do not expect many more pays in Egyptian money.<br/>Six months to-day since we left home.  Piquet to night to celebrate the occasion.  Leave for Cairo starts again to morrow</p>
<p>Wednesday 10/5/16<br/>Relieved from Piquet duty at 6 pm,</p>
