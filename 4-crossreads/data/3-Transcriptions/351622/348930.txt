<p>30 November 1916 Thur<br/>Xmas parcels from Winnie &amp; Mother. Toby Wheeler evacuated. Still C.C.S. Tent. Sid Coleman comes to see me. Big feed in tent at night cream peaches cake prunes &amp; chocolates.</p>
<p>1 December 1916 Fri<br/>Lux Manfred evacuated another old bund gone. The cold weather is fixing the boys up.</p>
<p>20 more come into dressing station on stretchers [lad] with [drum] &ndash; Madman in tent</p>
