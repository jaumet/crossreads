<p>Saturday Nov 17th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : fatigue in morn but didn't feel too good : Fred Morrie &amp; self went to see Eric Bracy but he wasn't in : talk in Army hut after tea : sand bag scare acute &amp; I pity the curs should the boys lay hands on them</p>
<p>Sunday Nov 18th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : went on sick parade &amp; got L. Duty : Fred Morrie George Frank &amp; self went to meet Eric &amp; Gordon &amp; we had a great time talking over old days : went to the village church together</p>
<p>Monday Nov 19th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : fatigue in morn &amp; pay parade in afternoon but no pay : Fred Morrie &amp; self went down to see Beatty &amp; he is still the same old stick</p>
<p>Tuesday Nov 20th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : schemed out of parade in morn &amp; wrote a few letters :</p>
