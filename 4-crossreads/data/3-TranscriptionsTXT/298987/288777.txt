 Saturday 29th June Was put on as Hut Orderly. Have missed the Signaling School as the others went to it whilst I was in Hospital. It would have been interesting Walked to Sutton Veny &amp; found a lot of my old mates out of the 11th Reinforcement still there. Drew &pound; 2 from Savings Bank Got my Photos they were rotten 
 Sunday 30 Went to Sutton V again 
 Monday 1st July Still on as Hut Guard 
 Tuesday 2nd Being Hut Orderly or Guard I do not go on Parade.
