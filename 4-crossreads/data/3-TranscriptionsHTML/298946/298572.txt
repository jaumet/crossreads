The unsatisfactory nature of the difficulties to be met, &amp; the increased work, added to the cold rooms, made that work unpleasant.  Through moving desks about too strenuously I made myself a little sick, &amp; the result was that I  spent a miserable day in bed <strike>on</strike> at Christmas.  I had, however, enlisted the services of Stuart Kidner as librarian &amp; help &amp; that made matters as satisfactory for me as possible.</p>
