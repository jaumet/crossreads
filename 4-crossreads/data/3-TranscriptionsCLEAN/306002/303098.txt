  
 4.4.15 Sun. 4th.  Were called  called  out of bed at 5 oclock this morning & were sorry to say good-bye to 'Barrage".  We had a 6 oclock breakfast  this  & were shortly afterwards on the road;  & making for Heliopolis.  At 9 oclock we halted, & gave our horses water & feed;  also had a meal ourselves.  It was a lovely morning & the ride delightful;  we arrived back at Heliopolis shortly after 12 oclock & found Crook is in Cairo giving evidence on the riot, as it is asserted he was standing beside one of the men that was shot.
