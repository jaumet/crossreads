<p>March<br/>10th.  Entrain this morning at 10 a.m. &amp; move out 15 minutes later.  Adieu Charleroi &amp; Belgium.  Our route lays through Mons Douai etc.  Comfortable truck.<br/>11th.  After a remarkably quick &amp; comfortable journey of 30 hours we arrived at Harfleur to-night at about 5 p.m. &amp; marched to the Camp where we spend the night.  Everything on the journey was the best possible.<br/>12th.  Generally fixed up with bathing, clean clothes (new) &amp; sea kit bags, after which we move under canvas for the night.  Equipment handed in.</p>
