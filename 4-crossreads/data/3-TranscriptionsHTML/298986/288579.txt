<p>so Collier &amp; I went in per crowded tram.  It is a fine City wide streets &amp; clean looking buildings, but we saw very little of it as it started to rain soon after we got there.  Had 2 meals 2 eggs each, each time.</p>
<p>Saturday 12th</p>
<p>Nothing in particular except the usual C.Os inspection in the morning.</p>
<p>Sunday 13th</p>
<p>Went to church in evening.  It was really a lecture on Repration, not very briliant.</p>
