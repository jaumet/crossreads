 20th Dec. Another move now &amp; we are gathering in or trying to gather in a Turkish Outpost on the way.  It was to have been tomorrow night but they have suddenly changed it in the last half hour to today so I must stop and pack up immediately.  More anon. 
 El Arish, 21st Dec. &ndash; 7 a.m. Just arrived after 14 hours in the saddle in El Arish or rather on the farther side of it.
