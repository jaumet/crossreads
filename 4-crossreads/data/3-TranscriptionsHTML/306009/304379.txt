<p>1915<br/>July 12 Mon.<br/>Went into Cairo in the afternoon &amp; made some purchases &ndash; walked back to Gezira through Kasr-el-Nil.<br/>Mena House is to be opened up as a Convalescent Depot to-morrow &amp; to-day 3 Medical Officers, 5 Nurses, a dozen orderlies &amp; a clerk were detailed from our Unit to staff the place &ndash; Major Grey in charge (N.B. Not one seems to be terribly anxious to get back to Mena).</p>
