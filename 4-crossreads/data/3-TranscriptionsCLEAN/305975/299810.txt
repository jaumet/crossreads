  
 Saturday May- 24.5.19. went to sydney to day  was out at wins place  raining heavy Sunday May.25.5.19 am having a day with tom and annie very wet  stayed in bed till 12.30 A.M. Monday May.26.5.19 am still with tom and annie  raining again  went into town  left my razors to be done up   measured for a suit.
