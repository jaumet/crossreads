<p>I went out on the Lumbres shunter working 13 hours signing off at 3 Pm, &amp; today Friday Oct 4th I am standing by in the Yard, in case of a breakdown or a special being required, as my Engine is still laid up, the broken castings have been sent away for new ones, I will not have her again for a week or two yet.</p>
<p>Saturday 5th.<br/>I was called for duty at 9.30 to proceed to St Omer to stand by as Pilot &amp; at Midnight was told an Engine had left Ligny at 11 Pm &amp; required a Pilot between St Omer &amp; Zenningham, so I proceeded up to the other end of the station to wait. I laid down in a Carriage for a sleep until the Train arrived, which was not until 6 am, so I had a fair sleep.
