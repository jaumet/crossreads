
It was great. The prettiest Bon.-Bons with little Robin Red Breast Birds, which were given to each of us. Sad to say there were only four (4) sisters, the fifth was on night-duty so could not be there. About ten men, so we were a very happy party. About 9. p.m. we went to the Orderlies Concert & stayed about 1 hour - then left them to their fun. We returned to the Mess-rom & had some music    & fun - a little dance on the quiet - Mums the word.
