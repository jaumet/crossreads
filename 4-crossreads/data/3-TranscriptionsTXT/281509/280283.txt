 1 March 1916 Wednesday 
 Sleep all forenoon after guard duty.  Draw fortnightly pay  at 11.30 am.  Dull showery afternoon, clears later.  Party including, Mary, Janet &amp; Essie, Jean Aberdeen, Nurse S[indecipherable] &amp; Miss Cameron come up. Have  boska tea in tent. T. Temperly &amp; friend come too. Show ladies round &amp;; hear concert at night. Farewell to Reinforcements &amp; 10th Field Amb. Good. Scurry in getting guests off in motor.
