<p>10 December, 1916 Sunday</p>
<p>Left Ibrahamnich for Moascar arriving at 9.30 that night feeling none too good.</p>
<p>11 December, 1916 Monday</p>
<p>Made the acquaintance of the Commandant, not a bad old cove &amp; he intends sending me back to a school at Alec. Good oh! nothing like it</p>
