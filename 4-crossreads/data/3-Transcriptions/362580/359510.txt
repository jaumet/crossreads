<p>Rested in the a.m..  Then Consie Murray &amp; I had our lunch in my room &amp; we left here early for the Pyramids &ndash; We had a grand Donkey-ride around the Sphinx &amp; Pyramids at Mena &ndash; Returned to Cairo about 6.30 p.m. did a little shopping &amp; then went to Shepheards for dinner, had a grand feed &amp; thoroughly enjoyed ourselves, got back to hospital about 9.30 p.m., had a hot bath &amp; to bed, read for a bit &amp; then slept soundly all night.</p>
<p>Worked well this morning, then had this afternoon off.  Got paid, then Dorothy &amp; I went into Cairo &amp; did some shopping &ndash; got back in time for 7 p.m. dinner, so am writing now.  I had a wire from Norman asking me to postpone my visit to Maadi, so suppose he was on duty.</p>
