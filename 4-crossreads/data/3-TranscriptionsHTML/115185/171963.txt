Of course "Shepheard's" is the world famed Tourist hotel and very gay I'm sure, but I have not ever been inside yet.<br/>After lunch we went by train to the Barrage &ndash; the part of the Nile where the splendid engineering feats have been performed to control its waters. It is all fascinating &ndash; when we got out of the train we were met by swarms of arrabs with Donkeys. I would have been knocked over in the medley if I'd been alone &ndash; but Alma's big cousins steered us through &ndash; shouting at the clamouring natives who swept aside for the broad chested big fisted young Australian &ndash; and we got on a four wheeled trolly &ndash; with one seat on which two sat one way &amp; two the other &ndash; supported by each others backs. The trolly ran on rails and was pushed by two Arabs most amusing. There were about 6 trolly with other sisters &amp; soldiers coming after us. If we wanted to stop and take a photograph or anything like that &ndash; we'd get off and our Arabs would lift the trolly</p>
