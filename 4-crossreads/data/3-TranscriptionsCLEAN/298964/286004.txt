
19 Th AprilTravelled all night in Position at dawn terrible bombardment with B. Guns  tanks & Gas used  Fokkers doing damage with bombs  no advance made
20 Fri Apriltravelled until 1 am to 3 miles W of [indecipherable]  dog tired and very sleepy
21 Sat Aprilmoved to new camp about 3 miles W of J.  water very scarce  General Chauvel left  Anzac Div for Desert  Column Headqtrs  also sent with him
