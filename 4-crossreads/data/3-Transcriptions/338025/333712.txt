<p>Wednesday 13th June 1917</p>
<p>Amiens  leave. Left camp about 7.15 am and walked to Albert station&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;where I got the train. Left Albert 8.30 arr. Ameins 9.30am. After having a good look round and visited the Cathedral we left at 8 pm. Arr. back at camp at 10pm</p>
