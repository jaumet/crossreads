Every few hundred yards along the road from Ammon up to here there were motor cars and big &quot;Praga&quot; three-ton lorries; also in one place a couple of lorries with flying corps repair shops on them. The road was rather rough along there and I suppose the turks thought that they were not getting away quick enough; anyhow they have turned all these cars over there and have set fire to a few of them and have evidently escaped, or tried to, by the train. We camped alongside the stream</p>
