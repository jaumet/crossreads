29/7/16Big bombardment last night. Same old work as yesterday  Sent to parcels home  Pay to day.   General Birdwood gave the boys some papers.  Fine fellow.  Our packs handed in  on taking marching order with our packs with waterproofs & overcoats.  ready to move at any time for some stoush up.
