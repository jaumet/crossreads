 11. Thursday Drizzle in morning &amp;  Dense fog in afternoon.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10.  Got home at six. 
 12. Friday Partly grey but warm &amp; sunny in afternoon.  Went to Miss Newcomb in morning &amp; to see Maud in afternoon.  Came home tired &amp; went to bed. 
 13. Saturday Dull &amp;  damp.  Was at Mrs. Newcomb's all day.
