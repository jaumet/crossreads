<p>Capt Ronald Wounded<br/>[October 1918]<br/> 7.30 Am<br/>3 Th<br/>The big battle is now raging barrage opened at 6.AM a good morning, we now await the result.(10 AM) Many prisoners &amp; Still advancing<br/>4 Fri<br/>Fritz your beaten although our list of casualtys were 281 but you bombed us hard last night<br/>5 Sat<br/>6th Brigade went over this morning on ferry prisoners Tonight the 2nd Div are being relieved I am on pick BEAUREVIOR</p>
