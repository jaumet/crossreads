<p>9th M.G. Coy No VII<br/>Intelligence Report from 6 am 6th to 6 am 7th<br/>Left Sector:- At about 1.7 pm a [Minnewerfer?] destroyed gun position No III Killing outright two of the gun team, &amp; damaging the gun.<br/>No night firing owing to Lewis guns not being available to relieve the M.G's<br/>Battle emplacements of concrete with overhead [cover?] are urgently required.<br/>Medium T.M. firing from within 10 yards immediately in rear of No 8 position draws fire, and its removal is recommended.<br/>This report reached M.G. Hd Qrs at 8.30 the runner stating he had lost his way</p>
<p>B.J. Carey Capt<br/>9th M.G. Coy<br/>7.12.16</p>
