<p>6th /<br/>Sea calmed down most good spirited again</p>
<p>7/<br/>The sea is lovely .<br/>Rifle inspection &amp; fixed life belts on. I was made hammock stacker, my duties are to issue hammocks at 6 pm.&amp; stack them at 6am<br/>Wrote a few p-c to Sydney &amp; home</p>
<p>8th/<br/>Plenty of Physical stunts my birthday just 20 yrs.<br/>We have change our course for Albany to drop some very bad cases of sickness.</p>
<p>9th/<br/>Cleaned up equipment &amp; dressed for parades.</p>
