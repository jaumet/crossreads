He says that later on if things are going along all right he will get them to go home. I think I will be dining with him tonight at Broadmeadows. He says that he will not be returning to Newcastle &amp; in this I think he is quite right.<br/>I hope to write again before leaving Melbourne. Our next call will be Fremantle. Goodbye dearest Mrs &amp; God bless you all. Let Father &amp; Mother see this letter.<br/>Your loving husband<br/>Will.<br/>Haven't seen Fred since Sunday.<br/><u>W.H.R.</U></p>
