<p>whether we had to stop in the train or change, at last they found out &amp; we changed &amp; 4 other chaps &amp; I were a little lucky hopped in a 1st Class carriage &amp; got a ride to Arnecre where we got off. the rest rode in trucks our luck still stuck to us for after having a bit to eat we got our packs on &amp; made off up the road, the rest of them stopped &amp; waited for the officer to turn up but hang that game he was too late as it was so off we set we had not gone more than a couple of hundred yards when we struck a lorry &amp; we got a lift for just on 9 kilos saved our life for we should never have been able to walk that far with our packs up</p>
