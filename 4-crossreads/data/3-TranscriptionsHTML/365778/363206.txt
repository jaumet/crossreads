<p>Monday 9/7/17<br/>Umpiring for Div Scheme, going all day, very tired.<br/>Tuesday 10/7/17<br/>Field Firing, Pow Wow of Umpires during the afternoon.<br/>Wednesday 11/7/17<br/>Training continued, saw Bas, went to 28th Bn concert.<br/>Thursday 12/7/17<br/>Reconnoitred ground for scheme tomorrow with all officers.<br/>Friday 13/7/17<br/>Brigade Scheme, General sent us to repeat where ignorance is bliss etc &ndash;<br/>Saturday 14/7/17<br/>Corps School near Albert for demonstration very good &ndash; won a rifle match.</p>
