<p>the sky and later flame leaping in the air in towards the town.  We walked along the road for a couple of miles to where 3 steam fire engines were pumping sea water in hoses.  Firemen Police and crowds of others were shouting and gesticulating wildly because the hose were leaking, had burst or something. We walked up a small street to where a store was burning away with great vigour.  There was a big crowd of people around looking on but the police kept anything that was wearing a sirong or that looked like an Egyptian at a great distance from the place.  People in European dress were not even spoken to.  By the time I got back to the ship I was quite fit for sleep.</p>
