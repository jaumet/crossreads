<p>1916 March 10<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Stayed on early morning parade Brown &amp; physical drill. Help Turner Moir arrange tables in mess tent.<br/>Put on tattoo this morning for being absent on parade on Wednesday. Get off with a little dissimulation. See about allotment money  No duties in afternoon. Bob self Heylin go home with MacBurney to tea. Meet his family father mother brother &amp; sisters. Nice old home at Kirribilli Point Australian flora. Sing &amp; Miss Williams recites Reggie on beach at Manly. Home at 12 pm</p>
