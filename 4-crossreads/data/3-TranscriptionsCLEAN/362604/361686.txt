  
 Y.M.C.A. with the Australian Imperial Force Bulford 30/6/18 
 Dear Mum, Dad & George 
 Before I go on to answer Mum's letter of 21.4.18 which we have just received, I'll relate just such few experiences that we have had during the week.  I have been trainin' lately & were you about you would hear the slapping of legs as of yore.  I have not been able to procure a pair of spikes & it is hard work with only rubber shoes.
