<p>385<br/>3<br/>"Leg of mutton and Cauliflower Inn" where we often go for a meal &ndash; Everythng is scrupulously clean and food excellent &ndash; today there is a sports carnival and crowds of people are expected &ndash; Aunt Mabel came to see me yesterday, but unfortunately  I had gone in to Epsom to get a feed so missed her &ndash; she left a note saying Miss Lea Watson could come and see me this week so although I shall be horribley shy I shall be delighted to see her &ndash; Nothing further has been done to my eye so I am as far off as ever of getting</p>
