<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>To - Flag&nbsp;</p>
<p>From - Captain Travers</p>
<p>Date - 24 9 14</p>
<p>Capt Travers has returned with the Chief Official of the place with his staff. &nbsp;The governor is inland&nbsp;&amp; cannot be communicated with at present. &nbsp;This chief official assures the Staff Officer that the place is undefended&nbsp;&amp; there are&nbsp;no mines. &nbsp;He wishes to see the Administrator and original of terms of capitulation. &nbsp;I am sending him to Berrima now.</p>
