
<p>MONDAY, APRIL 21, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Met Barry at the</p>
<p>Metropole in the morning &amp;</p>
<p>was round with him. We</p>
<p>had lunch at Carnarvon &amp;</p>
<p>met Linda there. Got a</p>
<p>car &amp; went down to Manly</p>
<p>for afternoon tea, picking</p>
<p>Eileen up on the way.</p>
