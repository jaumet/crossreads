<p>16 February 1915<br/>Tuesday<br/>Shrove Tuesday</p>
<p>Our company on duty Have a very busy time<br/>No sight of the Bee all day</p>
<p>pass light ship in the afternoon</p>
<p>Get a good view of the Barrier Reef. Some of the islands look very pretty</p>
<p>1915 February 17<br/>Wednesday<br/>Ash Wednesday</p>
<p>Continue Northly course all day   &amp;  do nothing<br/>The Heat is terrific with not a breath of wind to relieve it.<br/>Not a ripple on the water. watch flying fish during the afternoon<br/>Write letters home We should make Thursday Is. In the morning<br/>Wonder what has become of the Bee</p>
