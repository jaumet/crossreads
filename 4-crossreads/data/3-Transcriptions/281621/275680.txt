<p>Monday 8<br/>A big bombardment is current to the left towards Thiepval way and rumours are rife as to an advance and prisoners.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9<br/>At the Merricourt show again but the performers are not as good as last time.</p>
<p>Wednesday 10<br/>Still at Buire but the fine weather has gone and given way to windy and cold days and nights</p>
<p>Friday 12<br/>Weather still windy and cold</p>
