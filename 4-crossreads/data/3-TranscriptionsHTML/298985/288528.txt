<p>Sunday 11th<br/>Some bombs &amp; shells were dropped about last night. None close to us Had dinner &amp; moved of at 1.20. It was very hot &amp; the road thick with dust &amp; there was a good deal of traffic to stir it up. Flat uninteresting country. The fighting was here about 4 days ago Passed a few dead horses only noticed one dead man. The losses are said to have been light about here. Marched</p>
