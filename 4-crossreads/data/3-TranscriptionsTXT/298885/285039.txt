
1916Sat. Jan. 1st -16
Train from Black Boy Hill Camp to Fremantle, boarded A35 again at 2.30 p.m.  Sailed 5 p.m. for unknown destination.
Sun. Jan. 2nd -16Received first mail.Church Service
Mon. Jan. 3rd -16Innoculated for second time.
Tue. Jan. 4th -16Fell out of my hammock in the middle of night owing to heavy roll of boat.
Wed. Jan. 5th -16We are just approaching the tropics.  The weather is getting warmer each day.
Thur. Jan. 6th -16Start of heavyweight Boxing Tournament.  Pay Day.
