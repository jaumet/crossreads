<p>My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>Have not received any home letters for weeks, the last dated May 27th so y ou can imagine I'm wondering how you all are.</p>
<p>Received a nice letter from Lucy yesterday dated June 27th   &amp;  no other from Aust., so you will know how I feel.</p>
<p>Well, this life is full of comings   &amp;  goings. Kelly   &amp;  I are still here   &amp;  I'm very contended   &amp;  comfortable, this place will do me for some time.</p>
