Thursday24-2-16
A nice day & very calm.Saw a light about daylight, it must have been some part of Indian Coast - Saw a lot of sea snakes - very quiet on board.
Friday25th
Fine day & very calm.It is a treat to watch the sun set - more especially when the sea is calm. Most of the Boys have converted their trousers into shorteys. Kept very busy of an night - Having the 2 Hospitals - Mumps - Measles and Isolation to attend to.Boxing Tournament has started quite a number of willing bouts already - AMC are being represented.
