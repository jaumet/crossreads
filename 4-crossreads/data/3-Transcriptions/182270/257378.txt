
<p>FRIDAY, MARCH, 1919.</p>
<p>7 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;1017</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. With a following breeze</p>
<p>Played games &amp; read all day. The</p>
<p>hold was open&nbsp;<s>mos</s> in the morning</p>
<p>&amp; I got some cool clothes out.</p>
<p>Got didinfected in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Won the first round of deck quoits</p>
<p>&amp; tennis on a forfeit. L<sup>t</sup>&nbsp;Binnington</p>
<p>is my partner.</p>
<p>Days run 345 miles</p>
