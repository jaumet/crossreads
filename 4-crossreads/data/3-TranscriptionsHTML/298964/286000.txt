<p>5 Th April<br/>Very quiet usual artillery duels and Taubes very busy  cleaning all steel gear I cleaned mine with a rasp  lost a lot of sweat</p>
<p>6 Fri April<br/>Talk of the fight coming off on Sunday hope so  Taubes keeping very busy also very hot  Aeroplane dropped a bag of mail from prisoners</p>
<p>7 Sat April<br/>Enemy very active also Fokkers flying overhead throughout the day  An old sailing vessel arrived with a cargo of shells  beautiful weather</p>
