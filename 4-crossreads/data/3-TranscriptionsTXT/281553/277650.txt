15th FridayTurks very quiet indeed. Heavy Naval Bombardment South most of day. Small mail in.
16th SaturdayDemonstration given by us 4 a.m. with Artillery & Navy. Poorest yet witnessed, turned in to sleep at 5-30 a.m. Much Artillery & Naval fire later morning, answered weakly by Turks Guns. Very heavy bombmt. South.
17th SundayLine very quiet. Weather very dull & cold.
