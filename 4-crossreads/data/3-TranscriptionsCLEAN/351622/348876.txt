 18 August 1916 Fri Did a lot of writing during the day. Jim Murphy one of the old originals evacuated sick. One of the best. Went to the Rest camp after tea & met Len Pearce one of the boys from the old school. He looks well & is in the artillery. Asked Mr. Minter to get me in the [text continued at top of page] 
 19 August 1916 Sat Saturday kept going all day. Spent several minutes in Madams estaminet & after I had finished work I went there again & enjoyed a few with Bell & Sharpley. Went for a walk with Keith it was beautiful walking along there just before dark with all the harvest fields in full swing
