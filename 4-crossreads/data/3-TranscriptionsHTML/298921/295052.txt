<p>March, 1915<br/>Saturday 20<br/>Lecture from General [indecipherable] &ndash; Owen who has just come from the front, My conclusion he said, in a fortnight we would be side by side with English &amp; French<br/>Troops, so we now expect it to be the Dardanelles &ndash; news just arrived re loss of the 3 Battleships which is unfortunate.<br/>Papers arrived today from Mrs Collin Bystander &amp; the Sphere &ndash; Did not go to the Military Ball at Shepherds, felt off color &amp; so saved money.</p>
