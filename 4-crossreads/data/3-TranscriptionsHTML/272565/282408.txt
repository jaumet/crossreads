<p>the right &amp; left of Delville Wood.  Our artillery were busy bombarding Bapaume.  We were watching our shells bursting over that town.  The tower was knocked a few weeks back.  Returned to Bernafay Wood by the new duckboard track passing under one side of Trones Wood.  Camped in Bell tent.  Drizzling rain.<br/>11th December (Monday)<br/>No duty today.  Reinforcements arrived for the 14th F. Amb.  Raining and cold.  Good coke fire going in our tent.</p>
