                                  1919 
                                  June - 
  June 1st  
 Sunday.  Church Service on the "Rio Pardo".  Our Padre a very popular man. 
 The boat shows a daily increase in speed.  We are now approaching the Canary Islands.  The first instalment of my long "English" letter is written and it will be continued on subsequent Sundays.
