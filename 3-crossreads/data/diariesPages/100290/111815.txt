24 February 1916
Thursday
Church parade as usual this morning. Capt Bayer gave the address. Reinf drill in forenoon. First Aid to wounded. Squads had to go out in bush find patient. Render first aid according to specification. . Improvise stretcher carry him in. interesting good work done. 10th field Ambulance picked out finally. 
Three McTavish girls Miss Wilson up for afternoon tea. Trio Willy Maxwell. Broncho Pearson Frank Heylin. Go over to new trenches back to concert. Down to station in crazy buggy. 11 all told in 4 seater Bobby for driving overloaded. 
Back to camp supper.
