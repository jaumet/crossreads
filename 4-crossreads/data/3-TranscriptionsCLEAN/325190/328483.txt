  
 Marched with 2nd from Halte D'ouve to Senlecques (about 13 km) where we halted for  two days  36 hours & then continued march through Desvres to Wirwignes (about 15 km altogether). This place was the official resting village of the 2nd Fld Amb.  Billeted in hay loft at side of estaminet. Bread to be bought in village but little else, which did not affect us as we were without money. Stayed in Wirwignes for a week altogether leaving on Tuesday 20th Nov. for the 3rd.
