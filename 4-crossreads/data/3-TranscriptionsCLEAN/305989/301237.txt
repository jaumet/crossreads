
Monday 1 January 1917Cruel day.  Taubes over early & dust storm the rest of the day, wounded "2 bints".
Tuesday 2 January 1917Wretched day sand & rained all day one of the most cruel nights I ever remember our "possy" was near the sea, was wet through by 8 o'clock.
Wednesday 3 January 1917Cold all day:  got good mail.  Got cable from home, price 30/-.  Mother Lucie (3), Theo, Lorna, Rex, Elsie.
Thursday 4 January 1917Cold & windy:  also several heavy showers.
Friday 5 January 1917Fairly clear.  Section down for swim clear night up till 9.30.  Greased saddles made dugout.
Saturday 6 January 1917Got up early & went to "Kilo 139" for reinforcements.  Taube flew over but did not drop any bombs.
