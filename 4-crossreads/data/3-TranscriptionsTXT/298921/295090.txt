
April, 1915Tuesday 27My man Our got thrown off the Buck jumper making the 28th success for the Horse.Came on to Heliopolis today & found mothers letter of 21/3/15 & one from Tommy Redford who comes near us now to Zeitoun.Glad you have at last got a letter - Re Bucket - all I got was an Air Pillow - Sorry no rain - I note Ralph Arnolds name, & have written Charlie.Did you hear ought of J.A.C. in Melbourne, [indecipherable] was [indecipherable] by 5" of rain at Xmas & there is grass to burn.
