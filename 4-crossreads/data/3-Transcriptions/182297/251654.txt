<p>[Messages and Signals Form]</p>
<p>Recd at 8 am</p>
<p>To Mashobra</p>
<p>Date 26/4/15</p>
<p>Up to last night estimated 10,000 landed. Australians have been suffering badly from heavy shrapnel. Last night heavy rifle and Maxim fire on ridge to south of hill 971. We have captured three Krupp guns and reported nine silenced - many Australians killed and wounded especially on left flank. Enemies guns being shelled by men of war in Nullah to south of hill 971. Gala Tepe is not absolutely silenced yet.</p>
<p>Place Pera</p>
