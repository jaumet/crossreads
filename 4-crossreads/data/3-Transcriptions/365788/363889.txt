<p>4<br/>the Freehold will become the property of their wives &amp; children.<br/>A very generous gift is it not?<br/>Have heard several times of our Aunt Mary &amp; the other day was very glad to hear from Fred.<br/>I do hope Will has not been called on to go to Newcastle again to medically examine Recruits. A nice grey car has just passed containing two Officers.<br/>Very nice to be an Officer, eh?<br/>Did you hear that Betty Waldron has a little daughter - &amp; has named it "COO-EE WINSOME" ?<br/>Is not that Ghastly?<br/>Have not heard from Nell Sherwin for some time &ndash; but got some Sydney Mails she was so good as to send.<br/>No more news. Very much love from<br/>Ever your loving Brother<br/>Fred</p>
<p>P.S. Please post enclosed letters<br/>F.M.R.</p>
