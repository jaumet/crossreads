<p>1917 June</p>
<p>30 Saturday</p>
<p>Arose &amp; took my luggage to Wilton hotel. Then reported at 66 Victoria St. &amp; Lindsay, Russell &amp; I went to B. Red.C. to find that we cd. not get passport until Monday. Back to hotel. Had dinner &amp; rested &amp; at night Jean Lindsay &amp; I went to "A Little Bit of Fluff" at Criterion &amp; oh my, the crush coming home on the Underground took us an hour from Piccadilly to Victoria. Cold &amp; grey.</p>
