<p>No 3 London General Hospital Wandsworth London SW<br/>8th Aug 1916<br/>My dear Sister,<br/>As you will see by the above, I am now again in England, only this time under different circumstances, as you know the last time I was on leave.<br/>I was sent over a couple of days ago, they dont keep you in France unless you are bad, as cases come in continually from the trenches &amp; room must be kept for them. I liked the Hospital there too, the nurses were OK anyway it is close to our Base so will call on my</p>
