<p>There was an accident happened one afternoon on the bombing field Some of No 6 platoon were practising bomb throwing One of the bombs was detonated A chap threw along his bomb and called (private) "pin out" It exploded and wounded about six soldiers who were taken away in the motor ambulance</p>
<p>We had a mile and a half race too. The event attracted a lot of attention I was among them we started out gaily I didn't go the full distance.</p>
<p>One of our chief pleasures was to receive letters from home and to answer same. Although I received such a lot yet I can recall the fact that I never missed answering any.</p>
