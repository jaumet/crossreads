<p>El Arish &amp; also having a pretty easy time, all hands are very pleased at the prospect ahead &amp; are anxious to get into Palestine &amp;  on good country. We camped the night at Sheiks Zowaiid about 16 miles East of Kilo 152 our starting point, (about 1 &frac12; miles West of El Arish).<br/>Apl 7th The Brigade left Sheiks Zowaiid at 8.30 A.M. &amp; camped at Rafa on the Sinai &ndash; Palestine <strike>for lunch</strike> frontier for lunch, &amp; near where the Rafa engagement was fought on Jan. 9th last.
