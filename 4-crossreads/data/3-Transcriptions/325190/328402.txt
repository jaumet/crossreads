<p>Wednesday 26th Sept. 17<br/>Parkhouse again!!</p>
<p>I shall have to review the last six weeks rather briefly for the detailed diary of that period has been unfortunately lost.<br/>Left the 16th Field Amb on the morning after the successful concert about the beginning of Aug. &ndash; a Saturday - &amp; arrived at Perham Down.<br/>There I discovered that my antics  as Sir Frances Levison had caused a broken rib, on the strength of which, for a week I was hut corporal.<br/>Followed blissfully easy</p>
