<p>It is so strange a change that were one to stop &amp; think he would become melancholy to the extreme, but we do not think, to do so would make us cowards.</p>
<p>Overhead the skies a bright &amp; blue, everyone save a few grumblers are bright &amp; happy.  Underfoot the green grass reminds us of home &ndash; way up country where life was gay &amp; free of care.</p>
<p><u>Later</u> Our 1st 11 won by 10 runs, our second lost, due mainly to my mistake in changing the bowling.</p>
