<p>Harefield.</p>
<p>1st A.A.H. Hospital</p>
<p>[25 Sun]<br/>Leave into Brighton.</p>
<p>[26 Mon]<br/>Left Brighton in the morning.  Arr London about 11-30 am. Had an hour in London. Arr Harefield about 2pm. Put into bed Trench fever</p>
<p>[27 Tues]<br/>In Ward 2<br/>1st A.A. Hospital<br/>Transferred to Ward 3.</p>
<p>[28 Wed]<br/>Feel better so got up. X-Ray on arm  Dr. Broadhead in next bed.</p>
