  
 Time's pendulum is on the swing And Autumn dulls the Summer sky Like Summer birds our thoughts take wing And joyfully, they homeward fly. 
 These birds of thoughts and mem'ries dear, Our greetings take o'er leagues of foam: A merry Xmas, bright New Year, To all our folks and friends at home. 
 S.W.H.
