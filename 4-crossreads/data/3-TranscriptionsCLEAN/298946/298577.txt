 Then came another four days in London after the 9th was arranged.  Spent with Kit again & Hills. 
 Arrived back & found again put back to 16th.  Strike of men against continual postponements.  One day's fatigues not done.  Was chief delegate & did not enjoy it. 
 Final date given as 23rd - more leave granted.  Did not take leave.  Instead I went to Stockton got a room, and started studying & working on the Short story course which I had paid 10 for. Worked until 20th. 
 Left Watford for Plymouth on 23rd morning & embarked by tea time
