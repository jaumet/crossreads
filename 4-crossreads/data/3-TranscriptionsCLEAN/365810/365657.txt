  
 2.11.17 
 Dear Judge Ferguson, 
 Very many thanks for your letter of congratulations.  It was most kind of you to write to me.  Father & Mother were awfully bucked by your writing [?] them up & wrote specially about it. 
 I hope you are all keeping very fit.  Mother mentioned that you had a car which Mrs. Ferguson & Dorothy were learning to drive.  That must have been great fun.  When I was up at Division, it was always a great grief to me that G.R.Os [General Routine Order] forbad officers to drive cars, otherwise there would have been ample opportunity to learn.
