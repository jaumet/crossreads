I was dead lucky this afternoon, I just had enough money to land me in London & they were paying a lot of new men so a few of us went over & platted our frames in they never even looked at our books to see when we were paid last, had they done so we would have got no money & I should have landed in London penniless.  We got a 1 each & this was worth 3 at any other time coming as it did in the nick of time. I am writing this in the Refreshment room on Plymouth railway station while waiting for my train which does not leave here till 7.30 p.m. & lands me in London at 3.30 a nice time, too dead of night & a Sunday morning but that won't trouble me much.
