<p>6-2-16<br/>Sun 6th<br/>Church parade this morning &amp; this afternoon B Sqd beat C. football by 3 pts to Nil, Percy Treasure came out today &amp; I sent a note in to Stan by him; A fine rumor sprang up today that we will be away from here before next Sunday, destination unknown, but probably to <u> Salonika; I fancy it is only a rumor.</u></p>
<p>7-2-16<br/>Mon 7th<br/>This morning Nelson was busy getting Sig. gear from the 2nd Regt. &amp; the Signal Troop, to make us up to strength .A Troop of the 2nd Regt left this morning for Terieh to relieve Mack, &amp; the Sig. gear Traill took with him is to be left there for the 2nd Regt. Sigs; It is now a certainty that we are leaving here to relieve the composite Regt. near Alexandria; I accomodated a Tommy  who came along this evening looking for boxing, but he was no champion</p>
