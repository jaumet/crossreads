  
 Thursday Oct 18 Troops moving up towards Gaza. Every appearance of something doing shortly. Tom came over today attached to Battery's as Inspector Friday Oct 19 We heard to day that Turkish were suffering heavily from sickness. Looking like rain Saturday  Oct 20 Division moving up to Shelal to night Battery moved up to Shelal returned to unit. Struck decent patch of grass to day. Neddy had a good time Sunday Oct 21 Visited old pals at Ambulance. They are attached 20th Army Corp. 2nd Brigade moved up. We are moving tomorrow. Tom rejoined unit from 1st Brigade
