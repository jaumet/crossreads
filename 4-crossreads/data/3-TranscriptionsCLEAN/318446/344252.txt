
Bir El Abd, Friday, March 15thTomorrow we start out to trek to El Arish by easy stages, taking, I believe 4 days in which to cover the distance.  As usual yesterday was a regular Saturnalia - drunks all over the place.  Even today whiskey has been the chief article of diet.  Staff Serg. Earle filled me with whiskey almost by force during evening stables & then commiserated with me on still being a Tpr.Bernard is very poor indeed just now, has, in fact been ailing for about 10 days & is now as poor as a rat;  & will not, I am afraid, carry me to El Arish.The trains have been very irregular lately owing to sand being blown over the line by these pestiferous winds lately.
