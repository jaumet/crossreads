<p>March 1, 1916 Wednesday</p>
<p>81 wks of soldiering</p>
<p>Hopped out as usual</p>
<p>Things have been humming some at Verdun &amp; the enemy seems to have got it pretty thick.</p>
<p>Spent a quiet day &amp; this afternoon started a water flume to enable us to draw water to fill our trough which now is a beastly job.</p>
<p>Aeroplanes busy with their bomb dropping &amp; several give us a good display of fancy flying.</p>
<p>Our arabic is still progressing favorably &amp; we can now read the papers easily.</p>
<p>Bed at 9</p>
<p>Parcel &amp; papers arrived from Nell</p>
<p>March 2, 1916 Thursday</p>
<p>Armored cars arrive</p>
<p>Revielle as usual</p>
<p>Early this morning Capt Wollesly one of the aviators came a cropper this morning while out on reconnaissance his engine failed &amp; he had to effect a bad landing in very rough country &amp; damaged the under carriage of his plane.<br/>Great excitement amongst the niggers tonight when 6 Rolls Royce armoured cars joined us they are from Mersa-Matruh where they have made a name for themselves, they have 2 Talbots &amp; a Ford attached to them also 8 Douglas Bikes.</p>
<p>Had a late supper &amp; still there's no money, turned in at 10.30</p>
