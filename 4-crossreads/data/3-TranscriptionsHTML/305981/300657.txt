<p>a batch of about 12 Aussies and 8 Frenchmen were sent away to another camp named Konigsmoor.<br/>On the 10/7/17 We were sent to a small village to work called Karlshofen. Here we were engaged cutting peat.<br/>On the 28th October we returned to Konigsmoor<br/>on 29/10/17 returned to Soltau here we remained working for about 10 days</p>
