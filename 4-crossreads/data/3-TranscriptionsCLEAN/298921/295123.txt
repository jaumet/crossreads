  
 June, 1915 Tuesday 1 Joes' letter of 3/5/15 Days    "     25/4/15 Jack Harrison 2/5/15 MOTHER 2/5/15 All of which I will answer by letter as soon as possible. Very busy with much [indecipherable] work. Ran over a Nigger today in Brigade Car & killed him they are like ants on the roads & life not of much consequence apparently - still I was sorry, but they do not like the English.
