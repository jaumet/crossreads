 Monday 29&nbsp;November 1915&nbsp; In Cairo last night with Dr Henry &amp; others at a picture show, too sleep to enjoy it, home about 1 A.M.&nbsp; In Cairo at 10 am. saw Col. Featherstone.&nbsp; Writing &amp; reading during the rest of the day 
 Tuesday 30 November 1915&nbsp; Reading &amp; writing all day.&nbsp; The coldest day I have felt in Egypt. Wind from the North&nbsp; Report says that snow is falling at Galipoli. 
 Wednesday 1 December 1915 No wind this morning Put on winter clothing today. Too heavy.&nbsp; In Cairo during the afternoon. Had a chat with Knox who is managing the Red Cross work. He told me 3000 to 4000 Australians in hospital here today as against 7 or 8 thousand some time ago.
