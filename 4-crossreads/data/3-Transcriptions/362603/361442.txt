<p>4.  Auntie Helena took a stroke when Jennie was only 13.  Auntie was paralysed down one side &amp; lay an invalid 4 years &amp; Jennie had even to dress Her.  John is 4 years Her Junior &amp; she had to Mother Him.  In spite of it all She is a bright little soul.  She has a girl Pal &amp; one only.  She is now engaged practically &amp; soon poor Jennie will be all alone.  My, it hurts to see one's own flesh &amp; blood with such a cold, dreary life in front of them.  She is 26 now &amp; does not think of marrying at all.  On my return I found 14 letters for Myself.  I am now making a start to answer them all.  The House must look great now With glass all around it.  It is much better I think.  It was a nuisance when we were out &amp; had forgotten to let the blinds down &amp; when the wind blew as on that memorable</p>
