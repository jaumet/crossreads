
Thursday [2] 3 November 1916Rode on all day.Camped just past Romani.Slight storm with a little rain in evening.
Friday [3] 4 November 1916On again.Camped in hod North of Ogratina, [Oghratina] near where we camped after the Bir-el-abd scrap.Storm at night with heavy shower which wet all blankets.Had to carry overcoats on horses as rain made blankets too heavy for camels.
