Fortune was favouring us, for the weather was holding fine, a most essential thing in this great undertaking.We had aeroplanes in the air all day long sometimes as many as 5 at once up scouting the object was of course to prevent the Turks from getting over our lines & seeing to much, as soon as ever they sighted a Taube they would be after it like bull-dogs.
