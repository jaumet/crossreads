  
 Tuesday 11 July 1916 Discovered Museam on my own.  Saw a lot of old Egyptian stuff & mummies but as none of them were branded, I did not think much of them. Met Nelson in town, came home in Garry. Sent book of Alex home. 
 Wednesday 12 July 1916 Walked round sea wall talking to kids and taking photographs.  Had a look round old Egyptian fort at Anfouchy.
