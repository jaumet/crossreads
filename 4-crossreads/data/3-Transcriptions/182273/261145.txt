
<p>1918 &nbsp; July</p>
<p>11 THUR &nbsp;Dull day. raining &amp; blowing</p>
<p>hard. Amm. going up</p>
<p>Posted letter to May Webb<sup>&radic;</sup>.</p>
<p>Major inspected battery.</p>
<p>Sgt Savage acquitted. Very</p>
<p>quiet day &amp; night.</p>
<p>12 FRI &nbsp;Cool, raining &amp; blowing.</p>
<p>Rec: pay 40 Francs. Writing</p>
<p>at night. Very quiet</p>
<p>few bombs.</p>
<p>13 SAT &nbsp;Fine day. Posted letter</p>
<p>to Julie<sup>&radic;</sup>. Rec letters from</p>
<p>Amy Mc. &amp; Peggy. writing</p>
<p>at night. Very quiet.</p>
<p>Walked to A.S.C. in</p>
<p>aft. Writing at night.</p>
