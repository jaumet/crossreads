<p>December  1918.<br/>Sunday 15</p>
<p>At Sea. H.S."Karoola."<br/>Weather unsettled, wet and fine &ndash; fairly heavy seas. Shipped a few .<br/>Plenty of chaps very sick.- so far no sickness.<br/>Beautiful food aboard as the example menus show.<br/>Breakfast ,porridge, sugar and milk, steak bacon and potatoes. Dinner. Boiled beef turnips potatoes and plum duff, soup. Tea. Bread and butter, jam, tomato sauce and cold beef etc. with cold potatoes.<br/>Had made a run of 299 miles in the 24 hours from <br/>noon yesterday.<br/>George still seasick.</p>
