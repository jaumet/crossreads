  
 Thursday 13th June Marched about 4 miles to Wilton train to Warminster about 4 mile march to Camp arrived about 4 oclock drew 3 blankets & had a good tea. The marching made me a bit tired today as I have done so little of any thing for so long. 
 Friday 14th June Swimming paraide Marched about 4 miles to Sheerwater a very pretty little Lake on Lord Baths estate, A bit cool for most of us to swim so only a few went in, Afternoon Medical & dental inspection put in
