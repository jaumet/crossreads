<p>Nov. 1st Monday<br/>Very strong wind during Sunday night to midday with clouds.  Beautiful afternoon following.  Mail in mainly papers.  Front very restless till Garlands were fired over from 8-15 till about 9-30 p.m.</p>
<p>Nov. 2nd Tuesday<br/>Weather Bright but sharper.  Took 4 Photoes with No. 1 Sigs. borrowed Camera, viz., 1. Dugouts of 18 Batt. Gulley, 2. Courtney's Post from 18 Gulley, 3. Quins Post new Cook houses, 4. My own Dugout.  Much Gun fire about 4 to 6 p.m., otherwise front quiet.</p>
<p>Nov. 3rd Wednesday<br/>Still beautiful weather, front quiet.  Beachy busy.</p>
