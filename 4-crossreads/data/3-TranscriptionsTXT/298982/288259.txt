 14th Filled bottles &amp; back Yarned &amp; tea til 8 pm when turned in Up at 10.15 to work on a barricade which kept us going until about 2 am. 
 About 1.15. The enemy sent up a sort of star shell which was evidently the signal for a hellish fullisade of rifle fire &amp; apparently bombs &amp; was kept up for about 15 minutes.
