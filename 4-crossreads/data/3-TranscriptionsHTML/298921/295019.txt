<p>February, 1915<br/>Monday 15<br/>Moved out at 7 am for Heliopolis to join the regiment, leading out Horses from Remount Depot &ndash; Said Goodbye to Abassia still no Mail &ndash;<br/>The men gave me a good Welcome &amp; I am once more beded down in a fresh Camp,<br/>Horse feed is bad. Rice Straw a sort of Alfafa &amp; Maize &amp; the horses are getting badly sanded.<br/>Hope you can read this scribble.</p>
