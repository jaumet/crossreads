 11.4.15   Today is a beautiful day nothing at all doing most of us laying around on the decks. 
 12.4.15  Nothing doing 
 13.4.15  It is raining very heavy. 
 14.4.15  About 10 am we were all put into rowing boats &amp; tugged ashore to have a dumy landing it was great excitement when we landed we marched up on one of the hills close by which was covered with beautiful grass &amp; wild Poppies which were all out. 
 15  Nothing doing 
 16.4.15  Its a beautiful morning most of the troops went ashore but our Coy. did not go.  During the afternoon a lot of the boys went in for a swim. 
 17th &amp; 18th  There was nothing doing yesterday &amp; today to write about 
 19.4.15  I got up this morning not feeling to well had a very sore throat so went to the Dr. &amp; he gave me some tablets &amp; the day off duty. 
 20.4.15  Today There was nothing doing 
 21.4.15  When we go up it was very rough looking &amp; started to rain it has continued all day its terrible miserable everything being so wet. 
 Thursday 22  Nothing. 
 23.4.15 Its a beautiful day during the after noon
