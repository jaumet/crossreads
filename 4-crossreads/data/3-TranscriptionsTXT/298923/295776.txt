 20 April 1918 Saturday Still Bethlehem. Just got a Debit note for 52/-/- value of Troughs stolen by Bedouins also a memo:- Saying Divisional Commander General Chaytor considers I did not take necessary precautions!!! Posting you a General Allenby's Proclamation on entry into Jerusalem- Am always afraid I'll get into trouble with the Censor, so you fill up the gaps in writing in the Book you are keeping for me Mother dear.  Will fight Debit Note, expect will pay about 5/-/- though.
