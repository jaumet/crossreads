
January 1918Tuesday 1
At BeutinWell I have had a very good time today under the prevailing circumstances.The weather has been very cold's late, but today the Clerk seems to have made some resolutions, for the New Year, and has certainly given us a glorious day.I went for a long walk this morning to Beutin, a la Coloterie, a village ten kilos off.My billet pals two, being Scotchmen have celebrated the festive day rather well.Our lunch today was cooked by the Madame who owns our billet, and consisted of fowls and potatoes roasted, washed down by coffee
