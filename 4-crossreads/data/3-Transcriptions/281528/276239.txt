<p>24/3/18 &ndash; La Basse Ville<br/>An officer of a special Gas company called in early.  An enthusiastic youth whose preparation caused recipients to vomit and so pull off their masks thus getting their lungs full of poison.  A fine day but a thick haze is hanging about.  Went up to the O.P. <strike>of</strike> &amp; from there got a good view of the country as far as the haze permitted.  The village of Warneton looks like Warlencourt used to when viewed from Gallwitz Tr. [Trench].  A number of 5.9's were being chucked into La Basse Ville and sending up big columns of dirt and debris.  Had a talk to a Tommy sound ranging</p>
