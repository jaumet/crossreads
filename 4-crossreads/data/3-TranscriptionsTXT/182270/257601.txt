 THURSDAY, OCTOBER 16, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; S. Wind. Lopping in Mountain 
 in morning and ringing in the 
 Middle in afternoon. 
 Darby down 140 ft in the new 
 bore and has got rotten rock &amp; clay 
 all the way. Tickle drawing 
 water to him. The 200 ft of casing 
 came along &amp; was dropped at 
 Tickle&#39;s.&nbsp; 
 I got my new sheep dog. &quot;Wallace&quot; 
 from Tickle&#39;s &amp; brought him home 
 Dad came back in the mail car.
