 Sunday 15/7/17 Church Parade &ndash; Bas had cable from Doff &ndash; went for ride with Bas &amp; Martin. Monday 16/7/17 Field Firing, new horse arrived named him "Banjo" &ndash; meeting of C.O.'s. Tuesday 17/7/17 Wrote to Doff. Company Training. Wednesday 18/7/17 Route March &ndash; Full order 11&frac12; miles &ndash; very good work Via Transloy &ndash; Hapincourt. Thursday 19/7/17 Attack practice &ndash; Trench to Trench conducted by Currie. Friday 20/7/17 Field Firing morning, night stunt meeting of C.O.s.
