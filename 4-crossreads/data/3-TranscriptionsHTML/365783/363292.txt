<p>but hadn't a hope. They have a lot of motor buses here the same type of vehicle as the London bus. We went for a ride to St Kilda on one yesterday afternoon and on the way passed some nice suburban homes. I did not admire the style of architecture very much but the gardens are beautifully laid out.<br/>Major Gray my cabin mate &amp; I had a great round of sightseeing yesterday &amp; ended up with the theatre Muriel Starr in the "Yellow Ticket" We also saw the German flag that was captured in New Guinea. Hope the wedding went off satisfactorily I was thinking of you all at the time. The moustache is doing real well.<br/>Best love dearest old girl &ndash; will write from Fremantle<br/>Yr loving husband<br/>Will.</p>
