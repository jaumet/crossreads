It was a clear moonlight night, we were "all lights out", & we sped along the surface of the moonlit sea like a black phantom of the night. Quite distinct to our silent secrecy were the hospital ships flamboyant with their rows of green lights & huge red crosses brilliantly alight & their reflected beauty in the unruffled sea enhanced the charm & serenity of the night.
