<p>26/12/17<br/>until 12 oclock plenty of fun amongst ourselves</p>
<p>27/12/17<br/>Snow again today  not nice  it was very cold all day</p>
<p>28/12/17<br/>It is hard to walk around the compound because of the frozen snow.  The boys sliding on the ice</p>
<p>29/12/17<br/>Nothing to note  the ice still in all water holes</p>
<p>30/12/17<br/>The old Wabbler &amp; young on leave the Q Master is in charge. a beauty he is watchmaker would stop until one out paraded three times today hellish cold</p>
<p>31/12/17<br/>More left for holland exchange today.</p>
<p>1/1/18<br/>Midnight service last night  still cold  Boys throw bucket of water on the ground &amp; have made a fine slide  they were at it all day  80 more men for holland</p>
