  
 July 1918 Thursday 18 
 Bresle.  Weather showery, with a fair amount of sun. Tom and myself salvaging about three miles of good metallic. As expected we did not go out of action today. The French made a splendid attack on the Soissons and at 10 pm tonight had captured 20,000 Fritzs.  I believe they have advanced 10 kilos on a 45 K front.  Third day made a capture of 400 yds of ground around [indecipherable]
