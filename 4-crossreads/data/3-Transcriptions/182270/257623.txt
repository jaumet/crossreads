
<p>FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 7, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Lopped in Top paddock</p>
<p>in morning &amp; the Boundary in</p>
<p>evening. The casing in the</p>
<p>Middle bore slipped about a</p>
<p>foot &amp; George &amp; I raised it with</p>
<p>a lever. We got one strain of fence</p>
<p>wired and strained.</p>
<p>Mum &amp; Al &amp; Ray Battye arrived</p>
<p>in the car about 10.30 pm.</p>
