<p>September 1918.<br/>Saturday 21</p>
<p>Ahead of Villeret.Bty Post.<br/>Weather fine for a change. Jim Colbie went on leave to U.K. on a telegram from U.K.<br/>Built a nice little possie splinter proof only.<br/>Whilst on line I picked up a Fritzer revolver.<br/>Sheedy, Waters, Thomson, Steibel and Armstrong left for Aussie leave &ndash; very lucky, Believe I will get mine.<br/>Had a lovely time on the lines; Smithy feeling very happy.<br/>Had a heap of bombs in gully.</p>
