
August 191711th   We had more rain to-day & at times was very heavy.   During the morning we had some Physical drill but practically nothing else was done during the day.13th   Rain has cleared & it is now quite fine.   Yesterday & to-day we have been doing squad drill & bayonet fighting.   Harvesting has been in progress for the past week or so & the lads have been giving the farmer a hand in the field after parades.   It is quite a change & we all appreciate it very much.14th   We had more squad drill, gas drill etc etc this morning & a route march in the afternoon.China officially declared war on Germany & Austria to-day.
