
On Sat. I went to see Frances she was the only one at home, except Mrs A but was expecting Violet at any moment, in response to my card    Arranged for following night.Dad & I went to [indecipherable] & found Bowles manager.  Thence to Lily at Prudential & lunch at Slater's.We saw Mr Manhattan at the Prince of Wales' Theatre & in the evening brought a very pleasant day to a close,  Am seeing "The Battle of the Somme" on the Pictures. On Sunday I saw Mr & Mrs Upjohn & Hilda & the youngsters
