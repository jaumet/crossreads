  
 June 1918 Sunday 9 
 Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles Weather dull, and fairly close. Attended Church Parade in the morning, had a nice swim after lunch. Sports very successful l"F" Sub second 15 pts. "C" first 20 pts. Bob and Mick Fargo rode very well in Y.C. and Bending  races. "F" Sub won wrestling on horseback. Walked over to Bussey with "Pip" C, and "Don Ack". Had some eufs. Met John on way home testing? Mills Bombs.
