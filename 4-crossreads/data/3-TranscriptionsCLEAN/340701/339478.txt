Friday 13-4Majah Churchill seems to be in charge of this job now.  He told us off the other day but done no good.  He is about 6 feet high, blue eyes & a typical english toff.  Blizzards & snow every day now.
Saturday 14-4Fritz is shelling vigorously this morning.  He must know we are leaving.  One of his shells hit a Railway Engine about 200 yds from
