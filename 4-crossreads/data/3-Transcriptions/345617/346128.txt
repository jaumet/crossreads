<p>Juillet 25<br/>Squad drill in morning. In the afternoon we went for a short route march up the Somme Canal &amp; the Coy were inspected by the O.C.</p>
<p>Juillet 26<br/>Squad Drill &amp; general over haul of equipment &amp; clothing. Getting ready for a shift north.</p>
