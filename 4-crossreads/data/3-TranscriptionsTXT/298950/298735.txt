 Hague Convention dated Hague Conference May 18th 1907. 
  II Capt. 4 Article .  Prisoner of War will be under the Rules to Comands of the Governement.  But not to be the property of the Persones or divission who has effected their capture. The treatement of the Pris. shall be in a "human" manner. All personal effects to remain their property with the exception of Wappones, Horses or any Written documents containing Military deposition.
