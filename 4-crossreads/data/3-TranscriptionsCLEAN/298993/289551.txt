
Sunday 15.10.16
Moved camels took up a position on opposite stone hills looked after camels all day could see shrapnel bursting from indian battery in Turkish trenches held position  till evening Bedowins arrived with long rifles turks drew off.Monday 16.10.16 Returned to Zagdon 2 PM.stunt successful our casualties 1 dead 1 wounded.Tuesday 17.10.16 Revallie 3 A.M. trecked to Bayud 14 turkish Prisoners 4 Bedowins 1 Bedowin turned traitor. Wednesday 18.10.16 watered camels and filled fontesies had good wash.Thursday 19.10.16 voted on conscription I voted no. German taube over camp to day fired on but not hit
