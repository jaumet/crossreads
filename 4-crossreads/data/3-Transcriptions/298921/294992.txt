<p>January 1915<br/>Tuesday 19<br/>The days are becoming shorter &amp; the nights colder as we move away from the Equator it is winter in Egypt &amp; I expect cold enough at night.<br/>To date 7 horses have died, mostly from Pneumonia &ndash;<br/>Day follows day, stables routine broken by Rifle inspection, we are able to walk the horses all the way around the ship.<br/>Orderly officer today.</p>
