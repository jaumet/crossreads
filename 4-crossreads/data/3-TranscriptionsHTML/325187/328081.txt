<p>[Monday 4 December 1916]</p>
<p>Col <strike>L Meredith</strike> has been in Australia a month <strike>&amp; may not return</strike>-<br/>Major <strike>Granville</strike> now O.C. &amp; his fianc&eacute;e arrived from Australia &amp; married yesterday.  We go <strike>Ismalia </strike> &amp; give him a dinner tonight .<br/>I speak of WE I am at present in Detail Camp near <strike>Ismalia</strike> awaiting instructions.  Still Kit missing.<br/>Am a bit stiff after riding been so long away-<br/>Hear young Charlie is a Captain now.<br/>Found a Mongoose in my Tent a pet one of the Bengal Lancers   I have seen them before [indecipherable]</p>
