Dec 26th Thurs:-My yearly furlough to the United Kingdom has again meandered along so left Bn this afternoon &amp; got leave train from Charleroi for Boulogne at midnight.
Fri Dec 27th:-Travelling slow &amp; it is raining heavily.Passed through Ghislain, Douai, Arras &amp; St Pol
Sat Dec 28th:-Reached Boulogne at 11 am &amp; got the leave boat " SS Victoria" to Folkestone. By train to London &amp; arrived at Victoria Station about 8 am. Stopped night at Maida Vale hostel.
Sun Dec 29th:-Down to friends at East Dulwich.
Mon Dec 30th:-Visited the Alhambra Theatre tonight. Bing Boys on Broadway. George Robey &amp; Vi Lorraine.
Tues Dec 31st:-Coliseum Theatre Variety show
Wed Jan 1st 1919:-A wet day so off to Madame Tussauds' Waxworks at Baker St.
