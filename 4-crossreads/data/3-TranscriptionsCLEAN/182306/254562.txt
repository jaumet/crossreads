 Feb 11th Tuesday. 
 nice and warm to day still in sight of the Arabian Coast which in on our left side. The Sisters gave the boys a dance to night which  were  was very good. Saw a big school of Porposises there must have been a couple of thousand of them Another one of the boys died to day and we have to go to the service to niht at 8-30.
