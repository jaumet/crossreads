Not a tree was unmarked or its proper height. Bare trunks torn &amp; scarred &amp; cut off short was all which remained of the wood the sight was truly terrible &amp; one never to be forgotten. "Longueval" the same as other villages in this area is but a name. Nothing now remains except the remains of farm implements, broken bedsteads etc. All bricks &amp; stones have been used on the roads &amp; now one wanders at will over cellars, seeing here &amp; there relics of Estaminets, farmhouses etc. Not a foot of ground remains unshelled, torn by one side or the other &amp; as we walked along the enemy was shelling different areas with both shrapnel &amp; H.E.
