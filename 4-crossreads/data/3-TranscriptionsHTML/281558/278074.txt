<p>but I missed it : Tom &amp; self went for a stroll in afternoon &amp; again at night : wrote a few letters :</p>
<p>Sunday Dec 30th</p>
<p>Fovant : shifted huts in morn : Tom &amp; self strolled round in the afternoon : service in YMCA after tea :</p>
<p>Monday Dec 31st</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades in day : wrote in YMCA at night : very bitter weather &amp; fall of snow at night :</p>
<p>Tuesday Jan 1st New Year's day</p>
<p>Fovant : rotten cold day so didn't go out : wrote letters in the hut :</p>
<p>Wednesday Jan 2nd</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parade : YMCA at night &amp; wrote letters :</p>
<p>Thursday Jan 3rd</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades &amp; lectures : YMCA at night : my leave turned down again so this rotten army system is putrid</p>
