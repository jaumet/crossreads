<p>Hospital (No 2) is said to be going to France. Needless to say, we have been for some time expecting a removal of all our troops to Salonika or France after such a long spell since the evacuation of the Peninsula  My boil has departed</p>
<p>20th February:- Sunday<br/>I arrived here No 3 Aust Gen Hosp Abbassia yesterday.  It was the opening day of the hosp, which has just retuned from Lemnos Island &amp;  of course everything was in a state of unreadiness  To-day is much better, but as I was put straight to bed and am still here have not seen much of it.</p>
