<p>2 &ndash; if he is at Blackheath Convalescent Depot. I went to N.Z. office and enquired what would be done with him. They await the medical board's decision but they think he'll be given a job here in the army services corps. They don't want to lose him to the N.S.W. troops that was very evident. They are all sticking to any men they can get. All the boys who came over on the Mooltan to volunteer for Commission have got them easily &ndash; had their choice about regiments. I hope Sir George will get him a commission in the army service corps.</p>
