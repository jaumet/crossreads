<p>3rd A. Gen H.<br/>B.E.F.<br/>France<br/>16.9.17.</p>
<p>My dearest Mother.</p>
<p>It does not seem a week since I last wrote to you but it is so   &amp;  no letters from Australia for me, hope to get some this week.</p>
<p>Dorothy has been on Leave   &amp;  did the 'Trossacks" (Locks of Scotland), I did want to do them but it is too late now, even if I do get my leave but no word of it yet.</p>
