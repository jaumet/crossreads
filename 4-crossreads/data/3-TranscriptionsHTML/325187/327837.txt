<p>[Saturday 1 April 1916]</p>
<p>Sports on at Highland Mounted Brigade &amp; I got some snapshots of the Scotchmen wrestling on Donkeys &amp; a Camel Race.<br/> Letter from Nowell Forth today, from Sudan.<br/> We sent out Aeroplanes reconnaissance &amp; dropped Bombs on some 200<br/>Senussi at <strike>Kharga Oasis</strike> but the weather has been against Flying.<br/> If you look at map of Nile you will see <strike>Sohag</strike> is a long way South   All sorts of rumours about returning North again soon but nothing definite.</p>
