 16.3.15 we bought 4 new mats this morning &amp; have a new floor to the tent. the mats are made of reeds plaited &amp; cost (5 piastres) 1/- each Went nowhere after tea but stayed in the tent &amp; spun yarns;  We have given up all hopes of leaving Egypt again for some time. went nowhere after tea &amp; turned into bed early were issued with a couple of pair of socks &amp; they were very acceptable as we have not been issued with any since leaving Australia &amp; we are getting rather short &amp; cannot buy thick woollen ones in Cairo
