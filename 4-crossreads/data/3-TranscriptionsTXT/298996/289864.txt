I went down to the Refigerating room where all meat is kept for the troops and what a sight it was. I had to lend a hand to lift a bag of, well I did not know what it was but I found out afterwards they were sheeps kidneys they were as hard as boards. Plenty of fun today chasing the Lads about the boat they are as slippery as Eals. Somebody nailed my hammock &amp; blankets but I soon found another set. We are just starting to go through the great bight. Sea moderate. 
 23-3-15 Bitterly cold this morning we are will in the great A. Bight
