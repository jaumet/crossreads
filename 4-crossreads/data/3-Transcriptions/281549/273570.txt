We are now getting into what is termed the infected area and every precaution is taken against any surprise enemy submarine attacks. At night all lamps are covered between decks &amp; on deck no light whatever is allowed on any pretext. Smoking after dusk is not allowed. Durban &amp; Cape Town is I think about 8 to 10 hours behind Sydney time.</p>
<p>On Thursday November 2nd We left the land behind on the Starboard side, the weather is nice &amp; sunny &amp; the sea is very smooth. We are now in the South atlantic Ocean &amp; are steaming in a North-westerly direction with Africa on the Starboard.</p>
<p>To-day November the 3rd fresh instructions as regards letter writing have been issued <strike>&amp; rules are these &amp;</strike> the following matter is not allowed to be inserted. &ndash; Names &amp; Places of despatch.
