<p>the boys reckon it is the change of food &amp; etc that was the cause of it for they said they had the same attacks themselves, when first they came over.<br/>Packed up this morning &amp; reported at B.O.R. at 1300 &amp; walked down to the station at Amesbury where we caught the train, the country looks magnificent after yesterday's heavy rainstorms, all the hedges are snowy white &amp; the fields present a great contrast of colour with the daisies &amp; buttercups in full bloom &amp; horses &amp; cattle feeding contentedly.<br/>We had to change at Salisbury &amp; then again at Andover for they are all branch lines down this part, the weather has cleared &amp; the sun is trying to shine, but somehow or other it has not got that same clear bright</p>
