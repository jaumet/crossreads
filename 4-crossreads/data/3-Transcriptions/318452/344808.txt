<p>A thick fog hung over the city &amp; the congregation was not very large. I enjoyed the service. Rev Dinsdale Young preached from 5.40 to 7.8.Then said he Lo I am come to the volume of the book it is written of me I delight to do thy will<br/>It was a good solid sermon  4 points were taken (1) The reminiscent sermon<br/>2. The Annunciatory sermon<br/>(3) The prophetic sermon<br/>4 The obedient sermon<br/>I thought the first point a little strained but with the 10th Chapter of Hebrews as a commentary on it. It was a fine exposition.<br/>The prayers were helpful &amp; the theme was the advent.<br/>No one spoke to me either going in</p>
