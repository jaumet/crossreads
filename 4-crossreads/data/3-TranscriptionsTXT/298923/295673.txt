 7 January 1918 Monday Field Officer today. Everything wet- Jim Ayre came into "bivy" &amp; had a whiskey managed to bring a bottle with me- Roads awful but WOOD plentiful &amp; no bombing, we are top dog at present in the air here.  Am glad to be back &amp; working altho' a long way from Dolly now- letter from Laura.
