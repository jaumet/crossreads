 13th May Thursday. 
 Arr. Alexandria.  Very hot.  Put worst cases off.  Hospitals in Egypt full up.  One large + ship arrv. just before us & another just after. 
 15th May Saturday 1915 
 Sailed for Malta yesterday at 2 p.m.  Weather cold & bright.  At night all lights out fear a torpedo.  Very little done to our wounds. 
 17th May Monday 1915 
 2 p.m. arr'd Malta.  Big island.  Very pretty.  Solid rock.  All soil been imported.  Plenty of guns.  Brilliant sunshine.  Landed at Valletta.  Ladies gave us chocolate, lemonade, cigs. matches.  Everybody very kind.  Driven in motors
