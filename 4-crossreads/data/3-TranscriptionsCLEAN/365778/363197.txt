  
 Thursday 24/5/17 Robinson returned. Regimental dinner a huge success. Friday 25/5/17 Had a game of cricket. Boxing competitions at night. Had a spar. Saturday 26/5/17 Visited Wisdom at Senlis, played football 1st round of Competition. Sunday 27/5/17 Brigade Church parade at Senlis - Genl Birdwood presented medals to Officers, N.C.O.s & men. Monday 28/5/17 Refereed football match for 7th Engineers, Lloyd to Rouen & Robinson to England.
