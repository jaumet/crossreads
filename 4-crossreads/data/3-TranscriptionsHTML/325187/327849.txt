<p>[Thursday 13 April 1916]</p>
<p>Today a long string of Camels &amp; the Highland Mt. Brigade with Hong Kong Mt. Battery go to Kharga Oasis, we are in support.<br/>(By tonight's Cables I see Capt C.D. Tyson Royal Artillery is wounded in France &amp; I'm wondering if it is young Charlie.  He may have been tempy Capt: but I hope it's not true) omit<br/>Severe "Khamsin" Dust Storm today, dined with Fife, [indecipherable] Yeomanry &amp; had a HAGGIS, a real one &amp; I'm not keen on 'em- in the heat.<br/>Back to Brigade Hd Qrts today as one Brigade has gone &amp; I'm sent to Minia to enquire into misdeeds of 2 N.C.O's up there.<br/><strike>I Received letter from May 5/3/16 SOKS &amp; stamps (we don't use) &amp; Mits from Mother &amp; Boracic Acid &amp; CHOCOLATE " May</strike></p>
