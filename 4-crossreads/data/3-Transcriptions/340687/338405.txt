<p>April, 1917. Wednesday 11<br/>Up at 7. Day windy some sun and rain in afternoon. Waited for rations came about 10.30 with Farr Saffin near here. 3 letters &amp; 56 big parcels all food stuffs. Read and sorted parcels all day. Also asbestos and wrench came. Engine wouldn't go at night. Tank empty. Cleaned cylinder and put in packing. Bed at 10 very tired after filling tanks a little.</p>
