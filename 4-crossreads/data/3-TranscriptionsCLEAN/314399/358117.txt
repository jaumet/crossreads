
Wednesday 30 April 1919Up at 8.30. Gee Bill took some waking. Brekker very nice. Met Bill again after lunch. Went to Bank. Drew 40 for paying for the flat for 1 month. Went to the Popular for tea. Clicked. Took up to dinner at Molanaris. Had a little Champagne as Molanari shouted. Harry & Trix came in. Went for a walk & came home early.
Thursday 1 May 1919[SS. Phillip & James]Went to see about transfer of work but papers not back yet. Have to go again tomorrow. Reported sick & got some medicine for my stomach. Went to Hyde Park & listened to a band concert in the evening.
