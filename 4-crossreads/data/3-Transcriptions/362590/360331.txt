<p>I saw Caesar yesterday &amp; also Walter MacCallum. They are both well. Caesar is sitting on a board of officers held in our lines, &amp; from the look of things it is likely to last a week or two, so I ought to see a good deal of him in the next few days. He said he had heard from you last mail, but had no other interesting news.</p>
<p>I was sorry to hear that everything wasn't going as it should at the camps at home. What a pity we can't get a few of those troublesome men over here.
