<p>[Wednesday 25 October 1916]<br/>A.I.F. Headquarters<br/>Still counting Ballot Papers 10 hours a day-   The Soldiers fight a lot better than they fill in their voting papers but so far only 1% are informal &amp; the majority is in favour of NO conscription.<br/>Laura called today at Berners Hotel but only saw Dolly-   She is coming to dinner tomorrow night I hope.  I may be given some leave after this job I hope so.<br/>Had lunch at Army &amp; Navy Club.<br/>Bad news from Roumanians &amp; the war is a long way from over yet<br/>The Votes here goes against conscription.</p>
