
May, 1915Sunday 2Went to church under the Bough Shed of VMCA & could not but think of our dead mates I sent you a letter saying flourishing yesterday as you May not realize that the 1st L.H. were still here & had not crossed the water. Donald Campbell I hear is mortally wounded -Daisy letter 28th MarchMothers  "     4th April  & papers arrived safely tonight so will post these notes now -Love to you all. I see Joe has gone to Melbourne I trust nothing seriously wrong with eyes -
