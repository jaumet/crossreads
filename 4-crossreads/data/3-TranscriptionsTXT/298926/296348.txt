Arrived at Denham 12.6 and as usual in the Australian Military, no Transports were there to meet us so we had a 21/2 miles cross country walk Through lanes line with hedges we went and reached the hospital where we had a medical examination, changed back into hospital blues, had a feed and were placed into Military Huts.
