<p>Saturday 2 February<br/>In pack store. The thaw has set in &amp; the ice on the trees is falling down very heavily.  Aerial activity.</p>
<p>Sunday 3 February<br/>Weather fine<br/>From the Airdrome nearby a new bi plane has been on trial.  Rumour has it that it is 90 feet across the wings &amp; carries 1 ton of bombs.  It is a tremendous size &amp; has two sets of engines.  Looks very stable but slow.</p>
