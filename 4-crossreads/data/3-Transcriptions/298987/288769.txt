<p>Sunday June 9th<br/>Nothing doing</p>
<p>Monday 10th<br/>Did nothing. Have finished Massage &amp; R.C. Quite cold &amp; some rain.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11th<br/>Got my teeth back am glad to say as my mouth was getting sore. There is usually one bird in a [indecipherable] who stands out more than any other. He may be funnie dopie or just noisye. In my present hut one chap "Blue" OBrien is very amuseing especially when he gets in about 11 PM He has been in some of the concert parties &amp; <strike>cam</strike> knows a lot of patter can also imitate a Phonugraph exactly</p>
<p>Wednesday 12th<br/>On Draft for the Overseas Training Brigade Sutton Veny. Medical inspection Etc.</p>
<p>Thursday 13th<br/>Marched about 4 miles to Wilton train to Warminster &amp; another 4 miles to camp. Drew 3</p>
