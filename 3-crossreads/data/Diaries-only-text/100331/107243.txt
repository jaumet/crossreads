The Observer he jumped out as the Machine was near the tops of the trees, hoping to get caught in the Branches, but did not he crashed down was killed instantly, he was badly smashed up but was breathing but died a little later. The Machine fell on a square piece of cleared ground alongside of a Dump. Crowds soon gathered around to gather parts for Soveniers.
