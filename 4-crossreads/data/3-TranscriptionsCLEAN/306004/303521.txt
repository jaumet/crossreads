  
 12.9.16 Tue 12th The 2nd Brigade pulled their tents down today as they are to remain at Bir-El-Abd for some time; The Colonel called me up today & told me he was very sorry to have to say that Sergt Sutherland was getting the Commission in this Regt as Sig Officer he said the Brig promised him to give me the first vacancy in the 2nd or 3rd Regt; the Regt had a Mounted Parade today but the Sig did not have to go but carried on as usual. I received a letter from Ted Edwards yesterday wanting to be claimed back to the Regt. he is OK again & in Details
