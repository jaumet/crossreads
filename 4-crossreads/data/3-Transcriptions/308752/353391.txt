<p>Sunday 10th December.<br/>Relieved at 2 p.m. the walk back to camp was frightful, mud feet deep. The camp was like a lake, just a mass of water &amp; mud. The <u>stew</u> was delicious, our first hot meal for 72 hours.</p>
<p>Tuesday 12 December.<br/>On looking out of my shanty this morning a wonderful spectacle I saw.  It was snowing heavily &amp; as far as the eye could see everything was white. The weather was perishing also. As soon as the snow melted the mud &amp; slush was terrible, the track to the cook house was awful, it was almost up to our knees in mud.
