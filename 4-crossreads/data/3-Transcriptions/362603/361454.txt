<p>10.  Me quite casually coming out of Westminster Abbey &amp; has promised to shew Me all London if ever I have the time.  Don't be afraid that this Lady is one of the Many Bad Ones.  I am no fool after 18 months in this Business.  We have all been inoculated again for Para-Typhoid.  Our arms were sore for a couple of days but are pretty right now.  I had a Parcel of Comforts from Mrs Stephen by the Comfort Clubs Case.<br/>We are all to have pyjamas Shirt socks also.  Thank Mrs Stephen for me if you receive this letter before she receives Hers.<br/>Now Dad I must go</p>
