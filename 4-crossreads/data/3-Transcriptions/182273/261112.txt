
<p>March &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>17 SUN &nbsp;Fine day warm.</p>
<p>Rec. letter from</p>
<p>Tense. Work very hard.</p>
<p>Writing at night.</p>
<p>18 MON &nbsp;Fine day. Heavy</p>
<p>guns active. Ordered to</p>
<p>report to Bty position. Went</p>
<p>up in cooks cart. Position</p>
<p>at Spoil bank. Quiet night</p>
<p>19 TUES Raining. Very quiet</p>
<p>Posted letter to Amy &amp; Lizzie</p>
<p>&amp; May. enc. &amp; 1. to &nbsp;Tense.</p>
<p>Writing. Quiet night</p>
<p>20 WED Rain in morning. Cleared</p>
<p>up in aft.&nbsp;<u>Fritz</u>&nbsp;very active</p>
<p>day &amp; night with H.E. &amp; gas</p>
<p>early morning gas shell barrage</p>
<p>for 3 hours. necessary sleep</p>
<p>in gas helmets.</p>
