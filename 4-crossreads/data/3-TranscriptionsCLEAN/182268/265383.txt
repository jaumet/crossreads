  
 Tuesday 22/1/18  Wrote to Doff and then went to Malden 
 Wednesday 23/1/18. Spent the day at Malden and went for a long walk 
 Thursday 24/1/18  Returned to Town  saw Howse, had lunch with Gray  afterwards met Hardie- had dinner at Club 
 Friday 25/1/18  Got some new clothes finished, sent wire to Doff asking for 50 which I will repay. Went to "Romance" very good
