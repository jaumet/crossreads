  
 [Printed letterhead - Military Forces of the Commonwealth District Head Quarters, Sydney] 5th June 1916 
 Dear Mr Justice Ferguson, Your letter came duly to hand.  Mrs Blanchard's letter was posted on, immediately to "Ericville"  Oakville Road, Willoughby which is her new address.  I hear on pretty good authority that the 3rd  Division has been 'pulled up' by wireless and sent via the Cape.  The Field Artillery are already at Durban and all are, I understand, to proceed to Liverpool, England, for training, replacing a Division of Englishmen ready for transfer to France.
