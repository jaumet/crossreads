
<p>1916&nbsp;&nbsp; MONDAY 24&nbsp;&nbsp; JAN.</p>
<p>Very hot again &amp; dry.&nbsp; Father &amp; I rode round bottom end in morning.&nbsp; Saw the foals down there &amp; they have grown splendidly.&nbsp; Linda &amp; Self went for a drive round the Hill paddock in the spider when it got cool in evening &nbsp;Shot a few rabbitts ect.</p>
