<p>I forgot to comment on the manner of conducting Pats exam. It is very disappointing for him of course, but he musn't lose heart but just keep pegging away. The longer he is in the Army the more he will realise that    <strike>a</strike>  things of that sort, and changes of orders at last minute will be frequently met with.
