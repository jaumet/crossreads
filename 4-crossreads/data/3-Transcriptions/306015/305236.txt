<p>June 25.   Drill in the morning, 4.30-11 A.M.   After dinner three of us went shooting.   We picked up a hundred or so rounds of ammunition between us &amp; erected a few bottles etc., as targets behind a bank, and let fly to our heart's content.</p>
<p>June 26.   Drill and recapitulatory bombing work.   At 8 P.M. our section mounted guard.</p>
