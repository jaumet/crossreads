News coming from the Russian front very discouraging. Heavy bombardment going on out on the Ypres sector.
Saturday 8.9.17
Number of N.C.Os men crimed for having overstayed their leave in "Blighty".
Cpl Herford L/Cpl Hand both reduced to the ranks, few other crimed. Walked to 2nd Div Cinema show at Arques
Sunday 9.9.17
Took a run to St Omer for a few hours. Met Roy McPhee spent some time in conversation about old times.
Monday 10.9.17
Usual morning parade, spent evening with the transport boys. Getting more sick of
