The other day I went into a ward where there were eighteen men, all with only one leg each & one poor chap with two off.  What do you think they were doing.  The matron & I could hear peals of laughter before we got to the doors she said they are up to some mischief while the sister is away.  Well - they were playing  football  without even sticks. they are simply wonderful the dear things.  We heard some very sad news about poor Harold Jaques this week
