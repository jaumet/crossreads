<p>here are all getting 12 days. Mr. &amp; Mrs. Corne are away at London for theirs I had a letter from Lochhart a week ago they were all well excepting &nbsp;your Dad he was up the country for a holiday. &nbsp;I hope he would would come home quite recovered he has not much longer to work now till he will be retiring. &nbsp;We are very quiet just now the election caused a little stir but it is quitened down again. &nbsp;Uncle James is at a volunteer&#39;s supper in the Kings Arms hotel tonight, he is working with Uncle Tom at&nbsp;the waterworks at present. Auntie Mary &amp; the family are all well &amp; wishes to be remembered to you. &nbsp;I will now close with kindest love from Uncle Tom &amp; I &amp; wishing you all the complements of the season.</p>
<p>Your loving Auntie M. Kelly.</p>
