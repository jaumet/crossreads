 1916&nbsp;&nbsp; FRIDAY 25&nbsp;&nbsp; AUGUST 
 Showery all day. Out signalling all day. We were in No 19 Camp huts in afternoon doing &quot;buzzer&quot; work. Bill Hunter &amp; self went for a walk up to Larkhill in evening &amp; we went to the pictures&nbsp; &quot;Military cinema&quot; after. They were not much good but help to pass the time. I got another letter from Linda dated 26 th  June. Making all received up till July 2 nd .
