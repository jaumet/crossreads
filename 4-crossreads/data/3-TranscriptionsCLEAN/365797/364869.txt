
"Eumungerie"Newport.18.1.15.
Dear Alan,Just a few lines to say good-bye as I will not be able to see you or ring up before you leave. I was so sorry I could not join you all at Liverpool yesterday, but hope you all had a jolly time. I thought of you all having tea together.
