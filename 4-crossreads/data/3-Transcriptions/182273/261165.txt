
<p>1918&nbsp;&nbsp; September</p>
<p>19 THUR&nbsp; Fine day. blowing hard. Moved</p>
<p>to new possy close by. Very</p>
<p>quiet. Consolidating. &quot;Hun&#39;</p>
<p>counter attacked in evening.</p>
<p>Amm. going up. Rec: letter from</p>
<p>May. Cloudy night. quiet. all</p>
<p>1914 men to go away very</p>
<p>shortly. Rumour re pulling out</p>
<p>for six months</p>
<p>20 FRI&nbsp; Posted letters to Else P. &amp; Zelia</p>
<p>Fine day. Amm:</p>
<p>going up. Guns moved forward</p>
<p>Fairly quiet. Bombs at night.</p>
<p>1 Plane brought down. Billy</p>
<p>Williams paid us a visit</p>
<p>21 SAT&nbsp; Fine day. cool. Amm:</p>
<p>going up. Gunners &amp; teams</p>
<p>changed over. &#39;<u>Fritz</u>&#39; active</p>
<p>with H.V. Guns in back areas</p>
<p>about 12 passes through for</p>
<p>&quot;Aussie&quot; for tomorrow. Bombs</p>
<p>at night. Sgts. Gilligan, Fitz &amp; Dowe.</p>
<p>Cpl Parker. Murray. Lang. Garniss. Perkins</p>
<p>Donoghue. etc. to go &amp; Wallace.</p>
<p>Fairly quiet.</p>
