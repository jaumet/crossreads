 [Sunday 15 October 1916] 
 Delaney comes to town. Dined with Mrs Cotton No 17 Bus get out at Windsor Road &amp; Parkers to Tea 11  Cof  Corfton Road only 10 minutes walk Met Laura &amp; Connie at the Parkers In the morning went to Regents Palace Hotel to meet Doctor Eastwood &amp; his wife who go out to Australia this week &amp; may see some of my friends out there.  I am introducing Dolly to all I can.
