<p>J'son thought he might catch cold with the sweaty walk up &amp; then resting. Up at 4 &amp; stood to arms.<br/>Fine dawn Still firing One fellow carried down head tied up quiet.</p>
<p>Heard there had been terrible losses &amp; the chief hill the objective was a mass of killed &amp; wounded so much so as to hamper further action</p>
