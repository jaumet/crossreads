  
 February, 1915 Friday 5 Yesterday went to Pompie Pillar & the Sphinx & then on to Catatombs passing Natives quarters where incerinator buried up the dead on one side of street & Baker cooks on the other - Afterwards to the museum to see Mummies from the different Tombs - 5000 years or more!!! old 3 Trains today for Cairo. Off in March we hear for France - only hope it is true. At 1 A.M. more tired & colder than ever.
