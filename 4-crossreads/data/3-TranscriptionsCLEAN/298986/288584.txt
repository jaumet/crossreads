  
 Havre with Morison.  Tram to the Ocean Front, Quite a nice place.  Several very nice hotels now used as hospitals or for other military purposes.  The architecture quite reminded me of Mosman.  It is not quite so prudish.  We walked back to town & polished off 2 meals.
