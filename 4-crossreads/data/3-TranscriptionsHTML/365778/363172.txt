<p>Thursday 1/2/17</p>
<p>Visited the General who is at present in the line, he is very fit, had 3 lovely letters from Doff.</p>
<p>Friday 2/2/17</p>
<p>Moved to Acid Drop Camp. Very cold indeed, wrote to Doff.</p>
<p>Saturday 3/2/17</p>
<p>Went up the line &amp; had a good look around, saw General Wisdom, returned about midnight.</p>
<p>Sunday 4/2/17</p>
<p>Not feeling too good, remained in bed all day, bitterly cold.</p>
<p>Monday 5/2/17</p>
<p>Visited General Wisdom, still very cold, going into the line tomorrow night.</p>
