 5/4/19 Marcinelle A delightful day. Completion of arrangements for our departing draft filled in the morning &amp; I also paid a visit to General Martin regarding Brevet rank in the C.M.F. A short walk up to Charleroi after lunch. In the square there were many market booths erected and the people were crying their wares &amp; evidently doing good business.  At 5 pm went to the 23rd dinner and arrangements were by no means as good as they might have been and the sting and zest was absent from proceedings. Met Jack afterwards and we promenaded up to the University de Travail. Streets full of people and shops are getting bright &amp; well stocked. After some cakes and chocolate we walked home.
