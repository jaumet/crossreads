<p>Had lunch at the Cheer Up then went &amp; saw more of the city, then back again to the Cheer Up for tea.<br/>This Cheer Up Society is very good, provided us with meals, drinks &amp; as much fruit as we wanted.<br/>On Saturday we were taken by the Red Cross for a  picnic on the mountains, by train. During the morning it rained a little but cleared off later on in the day.<br/>We had a grand time, had everything we wanted, ate fruit all day &amp; drank soft drinks. We had two very fine meals, we left the mountains 6.20.<br/>The Ship sailed at 5 AM &amp; expect to arrive in Melbourne in a couple of days.</p>
