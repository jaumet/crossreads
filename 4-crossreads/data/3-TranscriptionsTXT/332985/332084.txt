
Squadron.  Rumoured that we move wagon line.  600 prisoners in the hop over the other night.  Took the Germans 7 minutes to establish their barage.  We maintained a steady rate of fire all night, expecting a counterattack.  It did not come off.
8-2-1917Cold and windy.  Hun aeroplane over, one bomb dropped about 200 yards from horse lines.  Terrific bombardment on from 5-30 till 6 p.m.  Our Bty. had a stunt for 15 minutes.
9-2-1917Fine day, the wind has dropped, not so cold, but still frosty in the morning.  Nothing doing, very quiet.
10-2-1917Bombardment just opened up, 8 p.m., supposed to last 6 hours.  Hun aeroplanes up again last night, beautiful and moon light opened fire with machine gun.  Lieut. Morris back from leave.  Leave again cut out, this has happened several times lately
