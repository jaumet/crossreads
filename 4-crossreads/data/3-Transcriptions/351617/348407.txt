It may be impossible to imagine boys going to sleep wrapped up in a wet overcoat and wet through to the skin, while the noise of the guns and shells bursting shook the heavens themselves, and sleeping in places where at any moment a shell might blow them to pieces, yet that is exactly what happened all the wet season and winter.</p>
<p>The next morning owing to 2 more Casualties on our stations up the line H.O. Thomas and myself were sent up once more to CHQ and MAB. respectively and once more I landed up on the same station as I had left the day before. Things had now quietened down a good deal but rations were still scarce, but we managed to keep going.</p>
