<p>Dec 5<br/>Arrrive in Durban at about 9.30 this morning after a fine approach.</p>
<p>To summarise re the trip from Sydney, we had a smooth passage to Bass Strait but met heavy seas &amp; stormy weather through the Bight. The Indian Ocean was exceedingly calm except for two days which were a bit blowy &amp; rough.</p>
