<p>IV<br/>Gardens &amp; is going to send you some &ndash; Her husband could have got us  trip out to the fleet but time was too short -  After lunch we went over the City visiting Sir Walter Scotts monument in Princes St and Edinburgh Castle, the Barracks of the Royal Scots &ndash; we then walked along the High St past John Knox's house to Holly Rood Castle, the Royal Seat in Edinburgh &ndash; By this time it was dark &amp; wended our way home feeling that our trip to Edinburgh had been well</p>
