<p>2/ I was on duty &ndash; we leave here today. I don't suppose it matters in telling that for when you get this all importance of the news will be over - I don't suppose that letters to England will be censured.<br/>I have seen quite a lot of people &ndash; they come on board from time to time. I have not seen Julian as he is at Williams Haven. He was at Herbertshoe when I arrived &ndash; I sent him his mothers parcel but he was rushed off as soon as he got back here &ndash; I think he got the parcel.<br/>Captain Jackson who</p>
