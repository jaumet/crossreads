 &nbsp;&nbsp; Then nearer home some prehistoric ramparts known as Vespasians Camp, three distinct tiers of earth evidently forming a fortress in olden days, the name is Roman, but they are much earlier. 
 &nbsp;&nbsp; We are real soldiers now, and don&#39;t stop work for any trifles, Friday we were six hours at the range, firing, although it rained for nearly three hours and nobody is the worse for it.&nbsp; Tomorrow I start a school in range-finding at Hayling Island, it is a good school I hear, plenty of time to do the work and really good information to be derived therefrom.&nbsp; I meet so many chums and acquaintenances here on Lark Hill that almost feel it to be an Australian Camp, well, so it is, but I mean a camp in Australia. 
 &nbsp;&nbsp; I think I&#39;ll close now, with love to all as my head aches very much from the effects of innoculation. against typhoid
