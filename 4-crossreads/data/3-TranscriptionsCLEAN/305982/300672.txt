The 4th brigade & my Batt. are the 4th Pioneer Batt is to move of early to morrow.sent bomb home also diarys
26-3-16Revellie 3.30 this morning.  Packing blankets in fact everything in general.  Big camal camel corp going with use  Beautiful sight: started off 7 oclock & marched until 12 with breaks of 10 minutes each hour. we are marching to Syrapia [Sarapeum] on the canal Suez.  Boys knocking up wholesale. Bands with Battalions grand sight to see men marching in route we started again about 3 oclock & marched upt  until 5 oclock with the usual rests men very tired  excellent preparations made.
