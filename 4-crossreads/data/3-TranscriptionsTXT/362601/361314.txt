 18 December, Thursday 
 Go to Inksdale. We laugh &amp; talk. I try to put the acid on Bill but fail. 
 19 December, Friday 
 Fairly quiet. I go to Ot's Solicitor &amp; get Riding Licence. Meet Bob Ward. Try to fix make but fail. 
 Recd letters from Connie &amp; Mr F. 
 Buy P.C's of Birds &amp; animals but do not buy dinner.
