<p>the trench we were building came in very handy for that practically saved us &amp; I can tell you we hugged it closely.   I don't know what their object could have been for there was no method in their shelling they shot their bombs anywhere &amp; most of them fell, where from our point of view, where they all ought to go, out in an open grass paddock that's the place for them.</p>
