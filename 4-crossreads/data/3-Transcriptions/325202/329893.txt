<p>one of the family was home 'en permission' &amp; they opened a large bottle of Champagne in his honour so what with the violin &amp; choruses we had a merry evening</p>
<p>Nov 15/17 The Chateau, Fauconbergue<br/>arrived here last night &amp; are stopping here a few days. The Chateau is a very fine place but the owners are away in Paris &amp; it is given over to a H.Q. Billets when troops are moving through. We have got our horses tied up practically on the front lawn</p>
<p>The Country is now quite different to flat Flanders it is hilly with running streams.</p>
