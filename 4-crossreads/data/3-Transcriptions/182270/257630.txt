
<p>FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 14, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Lopping in Top</p>
<p>in morning. I put salt in</p>
<p>Boundary &amp; Mountain paddocks</p>
<p>and started to paint the Middle</p>
<p>paddock mill stand.</p>
<p>Tickle at the fence &amp; we lopped</p>
<p>in Boundary in evening.</p>
<p>Mum went to Sydney by the</p>
<p>morning train.</p>
