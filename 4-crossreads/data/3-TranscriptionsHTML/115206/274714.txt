<p>May 1918<br/>Friday 24</p>
<p>Battery W.L.<br/>Weather rotten, rain and a very cold wind. Fritz bumped one or two around the district. Smithy and Adey visited the home.<br/>Purchased several items of groceries for guns and gave them to Smithy. Malcom to L'aison for three days, Knipe very ill? Lofty Hallwood received shell shock from a local shell. <br/>Had the 2 to 4 this morning.</p>
