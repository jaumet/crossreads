<p>Ballock Pier where we arrived about midday. It was from here we caught the boat to view Lock Lomond.  Scenery surpassed all my expectations; landed for 1&frac34; hours at Inversnaid at the head of the Lock; returned as we came via Ballock Pier, through Partick which I took particular notice of &amp; on to Queen St Station, where we arrived just on 9 pm.  Strolled around the remainder of the night, life too short to return early.</p>
