<p>Turkish than Persian.  Not much foodstuffs in the bazaar &amp; the people disinclined to sell anything to us.  After tea Scotty &amp; I went for a stroll &amp; had the fright of our lives. We left our tunics &amp; revolvers in camp &amp; when wondering across the open two Turks rose from a Hullah.  We rushed them &amp; brought our first prisoners back in triumph.  The Interpreter got to work &amp; they stated that they are deserters who heard the British were about so came along to surrender.</p>
<p>June 13th<br/>We heard that there were more Turks about in Zenjan, so I obtained permission to take Sgts. Carson &amp; Brophy along to see if we could collar any.  We hunted about for a couple of hours without success</p>
