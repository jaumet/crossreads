  
 Mar.29th (Good Friday). A day off from duty -. The solemnity of London - A day of serious devotion & sincere worship - Denomination quite rightly thrown to the winds - Visit friends at Wood Green - Return "Elm Bank" 8 p.m. and meet & enjoy company of Mr. & Mrs and Misses Sargent - English beauty & refinement. Mar.30th (Easter Saturday) Renewal at Ambassadors - Most successful shows of the week - Many friends present at matinee - Meet & have dinner in town
