 6.12.15 Mon 6th Good news came again today of the Russians coming down on to Bulgaria relieving the situation of the Serbians; Nothing unusual here; Weather nice &amp; warm 
 7.12.15 Tue 7th A Cruiser &amp; Monitor standing out off Anzac gave the Turks a very heavy bombardment this afternoon which lasted until sundown otherwise everything same as usual 
 8.12.15 Wed 8th Last night there was a rattle of rifle &amp; machine gun fire apparently near Achi-Baba; The warboats have been shelling the Narrows constantly all day but we get no news but the warboats are speaking fotr themselves, all quiet here
