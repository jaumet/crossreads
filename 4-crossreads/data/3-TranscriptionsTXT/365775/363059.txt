
of course, no doubt we helped our comrades lower down the line, for there is no doubt we gave him such a bad scare that he dare not take any men away, for fear we would push right through his lines here. the art of warfare is much the same as the art of boxing, feint with the left & hit with the right. of course the artillery did their share but we had nothing like the percent of causalities the infantry had. we had our guns up to within 1,400 yards of the front line & for days before the stunt were carting ammunition day & night.
