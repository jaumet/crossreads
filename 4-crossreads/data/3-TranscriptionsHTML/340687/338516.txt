<p>July, 1917. Tuesday 31<br/>Dull all day. No parade. Fell in at 8.15. marching order and marched to Blaringham arriving after 9 kilo march at 10.30 near church. Had dinner &amp; moved up to 5 D.H.Q. and pitched our 8 tents. 4 in ours. played bridge after tea as it started to rain again.</p>
