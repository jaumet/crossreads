Friday &amp; Saturday finished my run to Bolougne &amp; on Sunday 7th I stayed home &amp; had my Hot Bath as usual &amp; after Dinner up to the Park, where I again met my Lady friends &amp; Promenaded with them, &amp; returned to the Estaminet &amp; had Tea &amp; spent the Evening until 9 Pm with them &amp; when the Estam closed I went with Madam out to a village called Clairmair where they seek refuge from Bombs &amp; Shells, with her two children &amp; was met by two other Girls &amp; we all walked together to the house where I found two other Soldiers waiting.
