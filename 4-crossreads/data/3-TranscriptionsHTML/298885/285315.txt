<p>Apr 8th &ndash; 19<br/>Today we all proceeded on our pre-embarkation leave, and once more we are, in a sense, are our own masters for 14 days and one &amp; all intend to make the best of it. I spent my first 5 days in London saying Good Bye to friends whom I had met on my previous leaves.</p>
<p>The following week I spent in Cardiff where I enjoyed to the full a real royal good time with walks, motor trips theatres etc</p>
