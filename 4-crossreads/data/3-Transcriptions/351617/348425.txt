<p>That night we stopped at "One Blanket Hill" Boulogne named so because Summer or Winter they only issued one Blanket and I dont think I ever put in a colder night in my life than I did that night. It may be hard to believe but one couldn't possibly sleep for the Cold, Chattering teeth, &amp; language, not a bad mixture to keep one awake.</p>
<p>Next morning we boarded the same old cattle-trucks and off we went with our destination Bailleul as our boys were then in the line at Messines just south of Yares where the 3rd Div made a name in their first Battle at Hill 60.</p>
