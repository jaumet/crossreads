  April 14th  
 Parcels made up &amp; posted.&nbsp; More letters written.&nbsp; Poems copied.&nbsp; A busy day.&nbsp; Spend evening at Wimbledon where the great Tennis Courts are - Wimbledon Common. 
  April 15th  
 Morning in town.&nbsp; Book buying.&nbsp; Parcel posting.&nbsp; Visit the Canadian Beaver Hut.
