<p>22 October 1918 Tuesday. (X to X crossed out)<br/>31 General<br/>"Tucker" good here &amp; altho' short of Orderlies everything O.K.  I hope to be discharged soon as Temp. normal now.<br/>X In my ward are 60 Officers &ndash; Poor little daughter was vaccinated for the 5th time but she will "not take" neither do I for Small Pox is it because you once had it?<br/>Spent afternoon with Dolly &amp; wife &amp; get 14 days leave tomorrow  love-  I'll cable if any chance of coming home-</p>
