<p>IV<br/>amongst them.  Mum will remember him as a friend of Reg Wise's &ndash; Aspinall is doing well, I saw him flying past my billet yesterday on a fresh horse &ndash; and he didn't look too happy.  Knees up near his chin and feet forming a right angle with the horse's body.  I think Gavin is much more at home on the end of a main sheet than a pair of reins.  However he is keeping his end up allright</p>
