  
 Monday 13.5.18  Reconnoitring- had a game of cards with the Yanks. Wrote Doff. 
 Tuesday 14.5.18  "D" Coy detached working "C" Coy  concert & Supper very pleasant Evening. 
 Wednesday 15.5.18  Saw Martin & Paton at Heilly- Had game of bridge. 
 Thursday 16.5 18  Went to Bde HQrs  letter from Bas, reconnoitring- D. Coy returned 
 Friday 17.5.18. Off color with touch of malaria- Officers reconnoitring 
 Saturday 18.5.18  To Bde- Paton doing stunt tomorrow at Ville Sur Ancre [Ville-sur-Ancre]
