<p>1917 June</p>
<p>7 Thursday</p>
<p>Gloriously warm soft day.Cooked &amp; prepared at Minerva &amp; went down to Mrs Black. Paul brought me to [indecipherable] from stn. &amp; I went to Ladies Gallery in H. of P. Dull as ever but saw a division. Home at 20 to 12 very tired.</p>
