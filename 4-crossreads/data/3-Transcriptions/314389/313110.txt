<p>Tuesday, June 27th:<br/>Showery.  Rise 5.30 a.m.  Ride out on limbers to Sailley.  Work there all day, and walk home to billet at 5.30 p.m.  Retire 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Wednesday, June 28th:<br/>Showery.  Rise 5.30 a.m.  Ride out to job.  Work and finish harness room.  Return home 5 p.m.  Decide to learn French if possible.  Retire 9 p.m. and bombardment through night.</p>
<p>Thursday, June 19th:<br/>Dull, but does not rain.  Later in day, fine.  Rise 5.30 a.m.  Ride out on waggons to 1st A.S.C.  Commence another harness room.  Return home to billet 5 p.m.  Retire 9 p.m.  Heavy bombardment by our artillery.</p>
<p>Friday, June 30th:<br/>Slight showers.  Rise 5.30 a.m.  Ride out on limber to work.  Return to billet 5 p.m.  Retire 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Saturday, July 1st:<br/>Rise 5.30 a.m.  Fine ride out on waggon to work.  Return home 5 p.m.  Finish another harness shed.  Retire 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Sunday, July 2nd:<br/>Fine.  We expect to move at any time.  Rise 6.30  a.m.  Rifle inspection and rifle and platoon drill.</p>
