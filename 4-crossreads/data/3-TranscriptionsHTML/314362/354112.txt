<p>7 Sunday  [Oct]<br/>Cold  &amp; Wet &amp; fed up!!! Take me back to dear old Blighty.</p>
<p>8 Monday<br/>shifted camp from Reninghelst to Ondernoom [?].<br/>Rained of course &amp; we got washed out.  What a life!!</p>
<p>9 Tuesday<br/>In Evan's court martial as Prisoner's Friend. [Three lines deleted]</p>
<p>10 Wednesday<br/>Evan court  M finished &amp; he got off. Don't know whether it was due to me tho !!!</p>
<p>11 Thursday<br/>Had quite a good feed in Reninghelst with Pennie. Raining muchly.</p>
<p>12 Friday<br/>Hear that we are move tomorrow upwards!!   Wrote lng  lre to Rup.</p>
<p>13 Saturday<br/>Packed &amp; moved up to Westhoek Ridge at night. up to our necks in mud but still happy !!!!</p>
<p>14 Sunday<br/>Am on Sigs job &amp; with HQ. in a Boche pill box. Gas shells at night &amp; lost my sleep !!</p>
<p>15 Monday<br/>Wrote a short note to Rup. Received quite a number of lres from everyone. Plenty of gas shells at night.</p>
<p>16 Tuesday<br/>Heavy shells all day &amp; we had some narrow escapes. More Gas damn it !!</p>
<p>17 Wednesday<br/>Moved up to the line at 4 oc &amp; had rough time!  Boche plane chased us on the duckboards.</p>
<p>18 Thursday<br/>Our phones lines are dis. on &amp; off but we get along somehow.   Buzza [indecipherable] &amp; D M blown out.</p>
<p>19 Friday<br/>Lines going well especially the one to Bde. Heavy shelling all day</p>
<p>20 Saturday<br/>Saw  Woolrych in morning.  Getting fed up &amp; was gassed by the beastly tear stuff.</p>
