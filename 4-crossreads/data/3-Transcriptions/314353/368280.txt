<p>December 1<br/>a fine study of Borcas, by H. Speed.</p>
<p>Blue Duncan, seeing sketch of Roy F.- "Gee Frank, if Johnny Norton saw that, 'e'd put it in Truth!".. so quite obviously the pencilling of Roy is false.</p>
<p>Mac shews me tonight a fine 37th Batt., 3rd Div. Christmas Card..severely plain.. a small folding of stiff hand made paper, a gold outline of the Rising Sun, over the "eggs a'cook" colour patch. Black and Red, and in Black plain lettering the Christmas greeting&hellip;  We should have had them:  the reason ?...the influence of one man who has taken the place from the Ambulance it held in Egypt, in the Peninsula, in France&hellip;.</p>
