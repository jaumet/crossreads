<p>Monday Nov 8th 1915<br/>Light horse take Turks trenches, they meet with no opposition, Howitzers blown the trenches up first, Turks shelling our lines to make up for us taking their lines</p>
<p>Tuesday 9th<br/>Beachy Bill fires 16 shots into Chathams Post, trenches knocked about badly, we cannot man one trench on account of it being blown to pieces, things are in a bad way down there, bags smashed all over</p>
<p>If we had of countered attack on May 19, we could have wiped the Turks off the Peninsula war will finish next Easter</p>
<p>Nov 12th<br/>Large bomb explodes in 23 Battalion AMC dugout 3 killed 5 wounded 20 casualties caused by another bomb</p>
<p>Nov 13<br/>Lord Kitchener, arrives put in for Japanese Mortar 75 knocks officers WC all roads bloke cooking in dugout never heard it</p>
<p>Monday 15th<br/>Heavy bombardment Achi Baba cannot leave for Lemnos sea too rough</p>
<p>Nov 16<br/>Rough sea delaying our tugs little water for 2 days captured Hill 60,Mortars heavily bombard Gallipoli<br/>Flour Mills destroyed, shelled city</p>
<p>17 =<br/>Left lines for bivouac heavy gale blowing, high sea, all our dugouts drenched we have a lovely night, drying blankets all next day we sneak back to old lines for shelter</p>
