<p>Friday, May 12th:<br/>Same as above.  Usual routine.  Letter to Jack.</p>
<p>Saturday, May 13th:<br/>Wet day.  Rise 5 a.m.  Work on huts till 3.30 p.m.  Hot bath at Divisional Baths.  Retire about 8 p.m.  Letter to Mother.</p>
<p>Sunday, May 14th:<br/>Rise 6 a.m.  Off to work as usual.  Return to Camp 4.30.</p>
<p>Monday, May 15th:<br/>Rise 5.30.  Fine.  I ask our Lieutenant to allow me back to trenches as I am not contented at this work.  No definite reply to my query.  Work all day building huts.  Return to Camp 5.30 p.m.  Retire 8.30 p.m.</p>
<p>Tuesday, May 16th:<br/>Rise 5.30.  Work at Huts all day.  These huts are really hospital wards for the A.M.C.  Back to Camp 5.30 p.m.  Retire 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Wednesday, May 17th:<br/>Rise 5.30 a.m.  Ride out on transports to Bombing School (Sailley)  erect hut, return home 5 p.m.  Gas alarm at 8 p.m.  Retire 9 p.m.  Fine day.</p>
