<p>Sunday 15/4/17.<br/>After Breakfast the usual Church Parade was held &amp; was largely attended by the Troop. During the Afternoon I went for my usual walk till Tea Time. As it came up wet after Tea all hands remained in their Huts.</p>
<p>Monday 16/4/17.<br/> Nothing unusual happened during the day but having had my Invitation to a Send off in the Sergeants Mess I &amp; my chum attended, we were introduced to several of the Sergeants &amp; made welcome straight away. The Spread was held in the Billiard Room, small tables were placed around the Billiard Table &amp; Sandwiches were supplied in plenty</p>
