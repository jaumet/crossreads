<p>the first &amp; third I just spoke &amp; in the second, I read a paper on "Love &amp; Mr Lewisham".<br/>Outstanding men in the circle are Hall, Barlow, Rickson, Dickson, Staff, Parlay, Betram, &amp; Reynolds. Barlow has given us some good stuff both in papers &amp; speeches. The circle has grown considerably &amp; is officially recognised, with a couple of representatives on the central committee. What I have had particularly in mind <strike>in all</strike> both these affairs is now assured. No harm will be done either the Circle or the Concert Party by the secession of one man, for <strike>it &amp;</strike> each has a strong constitution.</p>
