<p>Centre for soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers &ndash;<br/>9.3.16</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton<br/>Could you assist Mrs. Duck until her military payments will be settled her husband discharged &amp; now recruiting &ndash; She has struggled along always until now &amp; will be alright when payments are sent from Melbourne, but in difficulties until then</p>
<p>Yours respectfully<br/>E. H. Bennie</p>
