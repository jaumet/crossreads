<p>1 April 1915<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Batt off on route march   &amp;  Bivouac until tomorrow<br/>Go for a stroll into Heliopolis   &amp;  watch the pictures   &amp;  have several cold drinks   &amp;  Ice-creams as night is very warm.</p>
<p>1915 April 2<br/>Good Friday</p>
<p>Early morning parade only. Holiday for the rest<br/>Battalion returns to camp about 11 am<br/>The Australian and New Zealanders take charge of the rough part of Cairo   &amp;  wreck   &amp;  Burn the contents of about 6 houses   &amp;  cut the hoses of the fire brigade. In the riot which ensued several of our men were shot   &amp;  a couple were shot dead by the M.P.   &amp;  Red Caps.</p>
