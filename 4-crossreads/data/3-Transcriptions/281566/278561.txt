<p>The 10th saw further activity in the artillery  line. Our guns  &amp;  mortars began to spread German sand bags over the landscape, continues for a few minutes quite unopposed. Then the answer came by way of a rifle grenade, whizz-bangs  &amp;  and heavier shells. This was kept up for the best part of the afternoon but very little damage resulted.<br/>[Sketch of soldier wearing a gas mask, captioned: New Gas Helmet (Box)]<br/>The Glorious month of June &ndash; Summer 1916<br/>June 11th 16</p>
