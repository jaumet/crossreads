<p>Sunday 3 September 1916<br/>Col. Fuller &amp;amp; I drove to Shepards in Garry this afternoon from Hospital.  Went to Y.M.C.A. to hear Lecture on "Scars of Battle".<br/>News has been received that S.M. Paulin late of 6th A.L.H. Reg. has been killed in France.  He lately was made a Lieu. &amp;amp; succeeded S.M. McPheeley who was also our old Instructor, &amp;amp; was killed in France.</p>
<p>Monday 4 September 1916<br/>Col. Fuller left Hospital &amp;amp; took Rooms at National Hotel, No. 20.  Lieu. Chisholm to see Col. Fuller at Hospital.  Cost 45 L.E. in Train.  7 men &amp;amp; Lieu. Hardy up to take lessons in Machine Gun School.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5 September 1916<br/>Lieu. Chisholm called this morning to see Col. Fuller at Hotel.<br/>I went out in afternoon to Re-Mounts Depot Racecourse to see Tom Saunders.  He has returned to Aus.</p>
<p>Wednesday 6 September 1916<br/>Col. Fuller &amp;amp. I out buying shirts etc. at Steins, &amp;amp; High Life also job Boots.<br/>Went to Maadi this afternoon to see our old Camping ground.<br/>Col. Fuller to Hospital.<br/>Serg. Hartley in Hospital with Septic foot.</p>
