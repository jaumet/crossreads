  
 Thursday 3 May 1917. 
 Reveille at 6.10 a.m. Breakfast at  8 p  8 a.m.  Left Yea 8.30 a.m. 
 Homewood 10.20  p    a.m. 
 Setting for final plunge to "CAMP". 
 Dinner at Kerrisdale & some more dropped out. Horses in G.S transport wagon knocking up. 
 Now gone 12 miles. Next stop at Trawool Hotel at 3 pm. Left Trawool Hotel at 3.15 pm. [After lunch we left Kerrisdale at 1.37 p.m.] 
 Arrived in camp at 5.15 p.m. 
 After  tea   a shower then tea & turned in at 8.30 p.m.
