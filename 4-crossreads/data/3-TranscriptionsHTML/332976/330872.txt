<p>June 1917<br/>10th   This morning the company went out to Biefuillers to complete the wire entanglements around the trenches &amp; returned at midday.   In the afternoon we played the R.E's of the 20th Divn cricket &amp; beat them.<br/>11th   We had an exceedingly heavy thunder storm last night &amp; it has been <strike>rail</strike> raining all the morning, but during the afternoon it cleared up.<br/>12th   This morning I went out to make a survey of some new trenches which the 59th Battn. have been digging &amp; finished about 2 o'clock this afternoon   I rode out to near Velu on the motor bike &amp; returned through Boulencourt.</p>
