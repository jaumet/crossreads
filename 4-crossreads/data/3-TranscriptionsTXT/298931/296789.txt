 [SEPTEMBER, 1916.] Friday 1 "Beauval", at present our home nice place, beautiful church good beer, lovely champagne and the women are pleasing to the eye Saturday 2 Letters from Mary Elsie Nell Hilda &amp; Muriel, a happy day Their health in champagne Sunday 3 So long they power hast blest me sure it still will lead me on back to those I love A chap is playing "Rememberance" it takes me far across the sea, to  Botany
