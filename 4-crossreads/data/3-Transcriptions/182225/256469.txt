<p>DEC.&nbsp;&nbsp; SATURDAY 16&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; frosty. Cleaning harness all the morning &amp; we had the usual inspection. Saw Hughie McMaster, Reg Watt &amp; Ophir Taylor in the afternoon. Ophir is in the officers school at Larkhill &amp; doing well. Frank Payne from Wambramurra came over in afternoon &amp; he looks very well. Charlie Scott &amp; self went &amp; had tea with Major Lloyd &amp; his wife - Carline Rodd that was from Parramatta at Amesbury. We had a real good little time. I saw mother at the Abbey also.</p>
