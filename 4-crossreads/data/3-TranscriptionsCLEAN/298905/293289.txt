Battalion, they killed 40 odd Germans took prisoners 26 men & 2 officers & brought back 2 machine guns & a trench mortar, that will take some beating Our Battalion received to-day a congratulatory letter from the 2nd Army Corp on the successful raid we made though we did not get so many prisoners as some of the others, we got a lot of valuable information. A few days ago a large fleet of our aeroplanes crossed well over the German lines, they carried liquid fire with them & when they came to any crops they shot this stuff over them
