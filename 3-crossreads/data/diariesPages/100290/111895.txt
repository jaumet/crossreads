14 May 1916
Sunday 
Sunday in mid-Mediterranean Think of it!! 
Another night passed without incident.
Food pretty rotten. Indigestion rife among the troops ? Bread poison.
Church parade on promenade deck at 9. a.m. Pres. Chap. Hutchison No singing. Bob, Tony self take communion in company of about 30 others in Sergeants Mess Room at 10 a.m. Rev. Hutchison officiating
Cold wind rising. sea with it. Sailing ship at pitch toss.
