<p>D.I. and myself are getting away to-gether</p>
<p>Nov 23rd.</p>
<p>A long drawn out journey to Boulogne. The usual large crowds on leave</p>
<p>Nov 24th.<br/>Arrived at London. Got my affairs fixed up at Horseferry Road.</p>
<p>Had some time to wait in London. Went to the theatre to the "Maid of the Mountains"  Had a few trips in the London Tube.</p>
<p>Left London by the 10.30 p.m. Reached Glasgow at 10.10 a.m. Got the 12 mid day to Stirling. Very wet</p>
<p>Nov 25th.<br/>Walked over Sheriffmuir with Jeanie and D.  Bridge of Allan in the afternoon. In the evening church.</p>
<p>Nov 29th. Thurs.<br/>In the newspapers we read of a fierce battle raging at Cambrai. Visited Edinburgh and Prestonpans  Had some difficulty with my pass</p>
<p>Very wet in the morning but good during the day</p>
<p>Dec 1st. Sat.</p>
<p>News of numerous casualties amongst local men in the Cambrai affair</p>
<p>Heavy snow fall</p>
<p>Dec 4th. D.I.</p>
<p>D.I. Left Stirling</p>
<p>Dec 5th.<br/>Visited the factory. Left home 9.52</p>
