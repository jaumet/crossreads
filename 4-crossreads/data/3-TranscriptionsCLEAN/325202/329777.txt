
this is only the second day since reaching here that it has been fine & only at night when between our blankets at the billet have we had dry feet & we are just slathered in mud & it is impossible to keep clean when you are working amongst horses this weather. But everyone is fit & there is hardly a case for the sick parade except sores and bruises.
