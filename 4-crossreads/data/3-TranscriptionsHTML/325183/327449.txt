<p>Tours En Vimeu<br/> <br/>Somme, 17th Nov. 1918 A meeting of all officers of the Bn. was held at Bn Hqrs. this morning after Church Parade <strike>5E</strike> 4D<br/>Major Cameron who is temp. commanding the Bn. told us that we were to be prepared to move into Germany within a few days. Four Divisions of the A.I.F are to form part of one of the armies of occupation in German Territory. These four are the 1st. 2nd 4th and 5th Divisions. The 3rd Div. it is rumoured is to take part in all the reviews etc in London and Paris as the present G.O.C. A.I.F. in France was the Div Commander of the 3rd Div until July of this year.</p>
