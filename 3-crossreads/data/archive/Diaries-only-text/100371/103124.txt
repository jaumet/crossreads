A.E.Grinyer 
Diary
1916


Continuation of Diary 
Monday 29th January 1917 fine day Tuesday same. Wednesday it snowed. Thursday fine, Friday Taubes come over nearly every night, it being moonlight bomb shell us. Have been doing sanitary fatigue for the last week. Had a bath today. Saturday fine, went to a concert tonight. Sunday Feb 4th Gas parade and pay. Monday Fine though dull, met Arthur Elliott Saturday. Tuesday sick parade, still fine. Wednesday 7th fine Taubes came over every night Thursday same, met Carl Hopkins tonight. Friday we marched out of Albert through Contalmaison to Martinpuich. Where we billeted in German Dugouts, the one where we are in is a tremendous size.
