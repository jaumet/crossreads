<p>Monday 18/12/16<br/>Got here Montauban about 2 oclock &amp; the mud here is worse than Thronnes Wood, unsafe to get off the duck boards In huts so are dry.<br/>Got papers from Kate &amp; Mrs Parsons.<br/>very cold today<br/>Pay &pound;2-10.9 &pound;18-0-0<br/>Heavy gun fire last night must have been an attack<br/>Leave here in 3 days time for [indecipherable] 3 weeks.<br/>A month on the Somme to day &amp; our second visit hope its the last.<br/>Viv very Poorly can't eat &amp; got diarrhea &amp; generally broken up. Not much better, myself but must not let him see that.</p>
<p>Tuesday 19/12/6<br/>left Montauban about 16.30 arrived here at Dernancourt about 2 Pm Pretty done up<br/>snowing &amp; cold<br/>Viv anyhow, must see Dr.<br/>stay here two days then on again<br/>on duty till 9 P.M.</p>
<p>Wednesday. 20/12/16<br/>snow frozen on the ground &amp; a lovely morning<br/>had a bath first for 2&frac12; months feel grand.<br/>Viv went to Dr exempt from duty hope he perks up now looks a bit better this morning.<br/>Moving off again tomorrow by Train I think<br/>Pay now &pound;2-12-9 deferred &pound;18.2-0</p>
<p>Thursday 21/12/16<br/>Raining &amp; as usual slushy mud &amp; cold looks like snow, clouds very low &amp; blowing hard.<br/>move off tomorrow about 3 Kilos away, trip to england seems a long way off.<br/>Viv still crook.<br/>Pay &pound;2-13-9. &pound;18.3-0.<br/>read to day of the Caledonia being sunk &amp; the Capt taken Prisoner this is the boat we came from Egypt in. in June last.</p>
<p>Friday 22/12/16<br/>Up at 6.30 Bks 7. off at 8.  Buirre 9.30.<br/>Raining all the time wet through. Got Rheumatic feet bad expect it will mean hospital if not better soon.<br/>Pay &pound;2.14.9. 18.4.0 &pound;.<br/>Rain stopped &amp; a fine afternoon, much better than last posi cleaner etc.<br/>Have to shave every day now.<br/>will spend Xmas here &amp; New Year at Rainneville.</p>
<p>Funny thing about the Boats I have been in Oratava went down tilbury docks London, went to Westralia in her that trip. Elingamite went down 3 Kings NZ. came from W.A. in her New Caledonia from Egypt Here</p>
