<p>He's likes to meet the ladies, he's out on pleasure bent<br/>And when he puts his bed down he takes up half the tent<br/>He's next to me as bedmate &amp; in his sleep he'll talk<br/>On everybody's elses's feet &amp; clothes he seems to walk<br/>And now I've told you something of each the seven<br/>chums<br/>And if you do not know me say I am one of the bums.<br/>Wednesday 23rd. Dec. On parade 6.15 A.M. at stretcher drill. Spent morning doing fatigue work picking up papers, cleaning latrines etc. afternoon demonstration on wagons. Had leave at night &amp; went to Tivoli, amongst various acts saw one I had last seen at Liverpool, England<br/>Thursday 24th Xmas Eve. Physical drill 6.15 till 7.45. Saluting drill &amp; bandaging till midday, Stretcher drill during afternoon. Went out at night &amp; had a good supper at the expense of the Padre<br/>Friday 25th. Xmas day Church Parade. Had leave from 5 o'clock<br/>Saturday. Semaphore drill, Company Drill in the afternoon then general leave.<br/>Sunday 26th. Having my brother's phonograph had some music during the day &amp; church at night.<br/>27th. Drill in morning. Football match in afternoon against Engineers. They won after a good game. Went home at night.<br/>Tuesday 28th. Heavy rain storm monday night. I stripped off &amp; went out into rain to loosen guy ropes but the rain continuing we were flooded out. Water rushed through our tents 8 inches deep and mixed up our blankets, boots &amp; general</p>
