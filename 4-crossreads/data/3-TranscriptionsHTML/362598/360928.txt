<p>Sunday 4 June 1916<br/>Church Parade.  Hot &amp; windy.  Ellis &amp; I go to Helio.</p>
<p>Monday 5 June 1916<br/>Wrote to Marg., Home (38).  Nice Day again.  We have only had 3 hot days so far.</p>
<p>Tuesday 6 June 1916<br/>Billy, Dave, Ellis &amp; I go sailing on the Nile.  Ice Cream Ghezireh Gardens.  Tea at Anzac &amp; Billiards at Y.M.C.A.  Billy beat Dave 50, 48.  I beat Ellis 50, 48.  I beat Billy 50, 34.  Dave beat Ellis.  Had French Lecon &amp; drinks at Saults.  Letters from H. Hey, Home, Mrs. Stephen, Roy, Wal Fisher.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7 June 1916<br/>Wrote Roy.  Finish No. 2 Hut.  Received Photos of Marjorie Melville &amp; letter from Nettie.  Last night we met Ken Prior, Capt. of the 51st Artill. Battery at Groppi's.</p>
<p>Thursday 8 June 1916<br/>Start No. 2 Ward, Sydney.  Went &amp; met McCoogi.  Bought 2 bottles Scent 3 P.T.  Met Billy Reid &amp; saw Jack Mitchell in a Gharry.</p>
<p>Friday 9 June 1916<br/>Kit inspection by Major Morris &amp; Lieut. Teece.  We pack parcel to send Home.  Wrote Marjorie Melville &amp; George (41).</p>
<p>Saturday 10 June 1916<br/>George's Birthday.  Went to Cairo for French Lesson.</p>
