<p>November, 1915</p>
<p>Friday 5</p>
<p>Light horse captured a trench from the Turks &amp; made another by sapping cut.</p>
<p>Turks tried to bomb them out but were unsuccessful.</p>
<p>New squad from [indecipherable]/[indecipherable] station, carrying along the beach.</p>
