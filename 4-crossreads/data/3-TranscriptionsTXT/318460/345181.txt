 Wednesday 10/1/17 More Rain &amp; snow bitterly cold, getting another man for Police D. &amp; C. Coys moved off this morning the rest of us go on Friday. The Trenches are awful so they tell us. Pneumonia, fever &amp; Trench feet have pulled out the 2nd division Sergt on duty last night from 8 oclock &amp; drill all day to day. had best Tea since I joined the Army Roast Beef Bld &amp; Bkd Potatoes &amp; Turnips dish full Pay 2-5-1 &pound;19-3-0 Paid to day 14/4 (20 francs) 
 Thursday 11/1/17 heavy frost, commenced to snow at 11.30 still at it 1-30 but very light, cold. Pay to date due &pound;1-10.9 &ndash; &pound;19-4-0 snowing hard when we went to bed. Viv returned last night Two letters &amp; a Parcel for him still no letters for me. 
 Friday 12/1/17 Rained during the night so the foot of snow I expected was melted. Pay 1-11-9 &pound;19-5-0 looks like a day of rain, much milder not going to the front to day. Lobster, coffee Beer &amp; Whiskey for supper tonight living high for once. Snowing. 
 Saturday 13/1/17 Rain, Sleet &amp; Snow bitterly cold, going back tomorrow by Train. Got 2 letters from Nell &amp; Jack. They are thinking of going to hobart, let em. I wont. Viv still in rest Hospital. Pay &pound;1-12-9. &pound;19-6-0 wrote to Maude &amp; Nell not Posted, last night in the Barn with the cows.
