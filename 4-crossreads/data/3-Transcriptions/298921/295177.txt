<p>July, 1915<br/>Sunday 25<br/>Rode over to 12th L.H. expected to see Cuth Fetherstonehaugh but he's returned with [indecipherable] at "Suey" as latter has measles. This Regiment left all their horses at Melbourne. Am posting you Snap Shots of Gaba Tepie - &amp; some of other spots imagine my delight at getting my other Medal the Kings from the Intermediate Box from the War Office England after all these years NOW I will wear the ribbon.</p>
