  
 BUZINCOURT Friday 15 Awakend from a sound sleep by a gas alarm early this morn Camped between two hills "Dolce Far Niente" Saturday 16 We are the reserve division. Summer Sun good rations, & beautiful nights. Lifes worth living. Plenty of "baloons about Sunday 17 Not a single shot fired on this front for 48 hours there must be something in the air for tis very unusual, either a calm before the next storm or peace terms in view
