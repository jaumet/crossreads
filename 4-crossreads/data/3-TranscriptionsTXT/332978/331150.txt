 71 October 6th Went for a walk this mng &ndash; very cold out &ndash; Concert held this evening when a French sketch was produced with great result &ndash; visited every room with Lt Montague who was my wife for the occasion &ndash; 
 October 7th Sunday Church service conducted by Comm Lewis this mng &ndash; Did not go &ndash; stayed in bed till 11 am &ndash; 
 Played Bridge &ndash; Raining all day &amp; very cold. 
 We have pushed at Ypres advancing 5 Kilos on a 20 mile front &ndash; 5 German Div. were concentrating preparing for an offensive &amp; were mostly taken prisoners. Our losses were slight &ndash; The Australians took part &ndash; we also surprised a Turkish force taking several thousand prisoners, including a Divisional Hqrs &ndash; General Holmes our Div Commr has been killed &ndash; The Commdt has returned &ndash; 
 October 8th Walk held this mng, but very few attended as it was so cold &amp; miserable I did not go &ndash;
