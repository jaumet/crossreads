 19 October 1916 Thursday 
 Heavy rain last night &amp;amp this morning.  Parade dismissed at 9.30. on  acc. of inclemency of weather.  Smart route march in afternoon led by Major Wilson along Calais road.  Meet Tony in afternoon &amp; take 3 cafes with him. Jock Heylin &amp; MacB. call for me &amp; we go 2 k.m. to Tournhiem. A quaint old fashioned village. Stone gate 600 yrs old. Water wheel &amp; electric light. So in cafe. Tea &amp; walk home &ndash; pitch dark. Mirage of lake &amp; trees.
