<p>to see us so had afternoon tea   &amp;  then a game of Tennis before dinner 7 p.m. They (two Officers) stayed for dinner so afterwards went to court again   &amp;  played until nearly 9 p.m. (got too dark to play longer). The days are very long now. One of our Medical Officers went to Amiens   &amp;  bought we a basket of strawberries, so I had to sneak away from the crowd   &amp;  stawk them. We (three sisters   &amp;  3 officers) went to my old ward (Officers)   &amp;  had Supper in Pantry &ndash; Strawberries   &amp;  cream   &amp;  coffee. Got to bed</p>
