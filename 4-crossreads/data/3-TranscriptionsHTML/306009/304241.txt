<p>1915<br/>Feb. 24 Wed.<br/>"Everything in the garden is lovely" &amp; I am quite content with my present lot but it looks as if we were going to get an early move on &ndash; Rumour is very busy.<br/>Capt. Jacobs who used to be my cadet area officer is in the 4th Infantry &amp; was admitted to Hospital to-day for measles.  Capt. H.E.U. Burke is also in Hospital.</p>
