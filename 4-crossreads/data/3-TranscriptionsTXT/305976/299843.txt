 Melbourne midnight. Had trouble in getting a bed. At last Tom &amp; I managed to get in Coffee Palace. During the night there was a fight in the lane below. We told them to clear out &amp; Tom emptied a bowl of water over them. They abused us the boys hopped down but they cleared out. Saturday March 17th Spent weekend at Dr Moores[?] at Canterbury. Had a fine time. Tom &amp; I spent time playing tennis. In evening music. Fine family. Sunday March 18th Had a walk round district. Monday March 19th Caught 5.57 train from Canterbury to Spencer St. then back to Camp. Tuesday March 20 Spent day Semaphore. Good fight at night at stadium
