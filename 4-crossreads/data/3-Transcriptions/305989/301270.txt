<p>Sunday 19 August 1917<br/>Still at Khan Yunus dump, relief came about 9.30:  came onto the beach after dinner:  had some fun with niggers.</p>
<p>Monday 20 August 1917<br/>On pumping party several swimming, very nice to be back at the beach.</p>
<p>Tuesday 21 August 1917<br/>Nothing much doing, ordinary routine.  Picquet at night.</p>
<p>Wednesday 22 August 1917<br/>Bought a lot of stuff at A.I.F. Canteen, got poisoned hand dressed. Concert at Y.M.C.A</p>
<p>Thursday 23 August 1917<br/>Day stableman, quiet day, had a couple of swims.  Went over to Regt. at night.</p>
<p>Friday 24 August 1917<br/>On beach Guard all day, bought some things at Canteen Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Saturday 25 August 1917<br/>Inoculated for <u>T.A.B.</u> feeling very "onkers" at night, arm very sore.</p>
