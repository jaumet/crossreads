
22 April 16Received mail this morning.Left Trinidad & proceeded to Scarborough, on the island of Tobagos.
23 April 16Easter SundayAnchored at Scarborough Tobago
24 April 16East MonHeaved anchor & left for British Guiana
25 April 16At sea
26 April 16Arrived off Georgetown on the Demerara River in British Guiana.There is only leaf to Petty Officers & Chief Petty Officers, so us common sailors could not go ashore.
27 April 16Anchored off Georgetown.
28 April 16Left British Guiana, bound for St Lucia.
29 April 16At sea
