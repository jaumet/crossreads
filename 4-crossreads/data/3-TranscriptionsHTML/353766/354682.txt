<p>Solomon Isdrs. On altar under glass is a tiny piece of the mat in wh. The body of Coleridge Patterson was wrapped after his murder. Everything in perfect taste. Visited Sol. Isdr chief, Fipe. House like inside of a great barn. Women's qrs on right, men on left. Grass mats on floor. All natives fine physical specimens, women so plump &amp;  cared for. Introduced as "missionary big battle ship". They grinned a huge welcome. Chief gave me wooden pillow, very quaint &amp;   a fly whisk of cocoanut fibre. I paid him. We talked in pidgin English &amp;  when at leaving I said some native wds, niggers rocked with laughter &amp;  shook hands, the women also who were as shy as school girls. Awfully funny.<br/>Landed 3 or 4 times. Visited Club, Grand Pacific Hotel, &amp;   two or three long walks in the country. Lovely place. For nine mos of the yr good place to live in. Natives made ship huge gift of bananas &amp;   sang old battle songs for our benefit.</p>
