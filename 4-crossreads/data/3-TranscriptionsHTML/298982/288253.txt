<p>13th May. At 11.30 about 16 of us from "C" Squdn went trench digging on left near flank (Communication trench) Maxwell &amp; self worked together. A pretty lively fire overhead whole time. We had to run for it going &amp; coming back as a sniper was at work on about 150 yds of main track &amp; one man "Irish" was killed &amp; another badly wounded.</p>
