 13 January, Monday 
 F Go down town with Jeff Claite. H'cut &amp; shave with a Lady in other chair. Change 2F 1C. Go to YMCA &amp; see Chappie. Only smile at Chappie. Meet James who has just escorted Chappie to dinner. Good Yankee Concert by 2 men &amp; ladies from "Now there Revue". Man [indecipherable] over the biggest wish. Girls are good impressionists. I see a Froggie &amp; Girl leaning against a wall near St Advense[?]  Tram turning. 
 14 January, Tuesday 
 F Work all morning returning Boards etc. Am called a Burglar by Quarter Bloke. Bath in afternoon &amp; doing nothing else. Recd parcel from Nette with socks, sugar &amp; fruit. Wear socks over to Blighty. I have to warn draft for 8.30 in morning after returning from Town. I do a bit of shopping after YMCA band has finished &amp; chat with Yankee Stelple Jack.
