<p>Things are very easy with us, and we are still detached. &nbsp;Our mail comes in dribs and drabs and altho&#39; Headquarters of the Column are about 10 kilos away, we hear little indeed of t&#39;other part of the Section. &nbsp;I expect tho&#39; they will be back up here in a week or so.</p>
<p>The detached part of us with the lorries went to the Column for Xmas Dinner, where there was plenty of beer (crook), turkey and so-called cake. &nbsp;It wasn&#39;t so bad, but not as good as our little turnout last time. &nbsp;This time last year, you will remember I was in Paris.</p>
