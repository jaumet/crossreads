<p>the water was drawn &amp; the old style threshing. On arrival at Cairo I went round to Anzac Hostel &amp; to bed. The first sleep in a decent bed for some months<br/>Saturday Jan 12<br/>Morning Took Tram to Abbasia where General Hospital is situated. Afternoon visited Gezeria[?] Kit stores situated some two miles from Town across River Nile. Bought some Bead Snakes  &amp; other work from Turkish Prisoners. Tea at Grappis (some meal) &amp; evening Concert Party.<br/>Sunday Jan 13<br/>Spent morning at Bazaars buy few things. Met Cpl Vine at hostel. Evening met some of the boys &amp; had a good time round City</p>
