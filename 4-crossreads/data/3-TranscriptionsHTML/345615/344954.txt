<p>We are camped on the flat alongside the town &amp; about a mile away there is a ring of <strike>hit</strike> low hills &ndash; along the top of which about every &frac14; mile <strike>are a</strike> apart are a series of block houses with a small outpost in each. I should judge there are about 4000 of us here all told &amp; if rumour is correct about 10,000 Bedouins some 5 miles to the west of the Camp.</p>
