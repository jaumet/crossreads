12th Tuesday
New Spring Bomb thrower to hand tried with much success. Mail in. Weather Bright but cold.
13th Wednesday
Weather very cold breezy. Artillery duels during afternoon. Land Batteries only. Big Mail in.
14th Thursday
All very quiet all day. Turks using explosive shells attached to rod for firing during morning against our Post from top of No Man's Ridge, 2 passed right over our front Reserves only 1 exploding without
