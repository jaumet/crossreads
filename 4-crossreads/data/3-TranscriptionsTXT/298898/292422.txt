 Tuesday 2nd Jan. 
 Had leave granted to England today &amp; have just completed packing. My first leave for over two years so am looking forward to it greatly. Went by car today to various battalions to evacuate sick &amp; had a good run round. 
 Have just returned from leave &amp; will try &amp; write up my diary for that period. I found it utterly impossible to do so when away, my time was too much occupied, spare time all too little was spent in sleep.
