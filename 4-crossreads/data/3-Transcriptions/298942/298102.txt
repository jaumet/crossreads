<p>Having the wheels with us, &amp; finding the piano fairly light we decided to take it back right away, there being four of us (James. Rogers, Walton &amp; I).  It took manoeuvring to get it through the garden gate, not to mention the front room window, but we managed it &amp; then did not waste a second in hurrying back</p>
