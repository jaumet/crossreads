It is interesting to note the small recess which appears in a certain wall of all mosques, known as the mihrab.  This is so placed that when the congregation face it they look towards Mecca.   A recess is chosen so that a blind person, of whom there appears to be a large number in the East, can find it by feeling.  The priest ascends 30 steps into his pulpit and preaches with his back towards the listeners. Quite close to the Citadel are two other fine mosques.
