Considering everything they had very few casualties. Ewing MacKay were blown to pieces with shells. After tea went into Winnezeele with Andy Burrell met some of the old 62nd chaps who have joined 13 Btn
7/10/17 Sunday ? Very cold all night but slept well. Plenty of mud about the camp. Issued with woollen undershirt. After dinner walked to Le Rivelt with Andy K Fourell had meal of eggs, also bought bread. Rained heavily all afternoon.
