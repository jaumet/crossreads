<p>15/4/18 &ndash; Lavieville<br/>A cold dull day but fortunately no rain.  The night was quiet and uneventful.  At half an hour before dawn I was awakened but dropped off again for a sleep when it became apparent that no attack was coming.  Our guns registered on an S.O.S. line and I recommended a drop of 50-75 y. in order to catch any advancing troops very soon.  Plenty of engineers material is now being dumped round this part &ndash; indeed too much.  The day was very quiet and we had sundry visitors &ndash; Engineers &amp;c.  Walked up to Pioneer Trench and saw Rodda &amp; Dooley.  The latter I am sending out</p>
