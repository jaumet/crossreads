
Friday 27.4.17Was induced to play with the Aust Rules team against Reinforcement Camp, won by 10 points but feel the effects of it to-day.  Big mail came on to-day.  Received watch.
Saturday 28.4.17Watch keeps jibbing.  Appointed one of the Scrutineers for the polling to-morrow.  4th F. Amb defeated our Rugby team 7 to nil.
