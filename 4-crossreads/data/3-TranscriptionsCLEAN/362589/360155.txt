I came in here two days ago & am Medical Registrar to this Hospital which is only in process of formation; we have about 200 beds occupied but will have 400 to 500 beds in the course of another 2 weeks; it is pioneer work getting things in order & organizing the place & very interesting work. I am a kind of medical superintendent with administration work which is right into my hand, & I just carry on, subject of course to the C.O. in charge.
