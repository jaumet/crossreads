<p>[Thursday 15 June 1916]<br/> <strike>Ognatina</strike><br/>"In the shade of the Sheltering Palm" Sounds all right but when shared with Camels- &amp; only a few leaves on the top, the sun travels very quickly around, &amp; every 2 hours a man must shift, or his Water bottle boils!<br/>Moved at 10 p.m. or as we have it 2200, &amp; marched all night &amp; tried to surround a Turkish Outpost- I rode into one of their Telegraph Wires &amp; my horse bolted &amp; threw me I hung on as I did not want to be left &amp; only got a few bruises my horse is cut pretty badly, I pulled down 2 Poles no wonder the Turks heard us &amp; got</p>
