<p>Also C.D. Bell &amp; Lionel Sheldon, Doherty &amp; Dave.  My they are all getting Home.  But, they have done their bit so it's very good to hear of their return.  I have just returned from 7&frac12; days leave in the Isle of Man.  I'll describe it all in Dad's &amp; Mum's letters as I've to write to them yet.  The weather here is abominably hot &amp; it is awful to get about during day-time.  This may seem incredible but it is nevertheless true.  But in 2 months time, Gee! we'll have snow &amp; ice &amp; wind &amp; hail, ugh!!  You don't know what cold is like.</p>
