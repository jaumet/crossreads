<p>Sunday 9 March 1919<br/>No parades still on account of the rain. Wrote 4 letters. Went out to Bill's cousins &amp; had a great old time. Kept them full of laughter. We told their fortunes by cards etc. Sang quite a lot. Had a very pleasant day. Just managed to get our train.</p>
<p>Monday 10 March 1919<br/>Wrote letters all day &amp; went in late to bring out a team. Everything was goodo. Very quiet. Had parade today.</p>
