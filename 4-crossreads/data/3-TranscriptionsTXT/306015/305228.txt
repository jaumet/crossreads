 At the Y.M.C.A. we then had tea, which consisted of biscuits &amp; preserved fruit, eaten by  mea  aid of the fingers, &amp; tea, drunk from the fruit tin when it was empty. 
 June 20.    On account of the mid-day heat, we rise at 4:30 here &amp; cease drill for the day at 11.   Being on 'light duty' I did nothing during these hours. 
 In the afternoon Alan and I walked about 2 kilos to the Railway town of Achiet-le-grand and commenced our business there by visiting the Expeditionary Force Canteen.
