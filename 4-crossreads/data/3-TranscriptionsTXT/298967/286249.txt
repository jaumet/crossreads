 DIARY OF 10th BATT 
 AUG 4/1914 'TILL DEC. 30/1918 
 DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS 
 1914 Aug. 4 &ndash; Adelaide &ndash; War Declared 16 &ndash; 09.00 &ndash; Adelaide &ndash; Lt. Col. Weis informed to take command 10th Battalion. 
 17 &ndash; 10.00 &ndash; Adelaide &ndash; Lt. Col. Weis proceeded to Keswick to select Officers (Maj. Hurcombe, S.M. Capt. Lorenzo Adgt. Capt. Minigal Q.M.) 
 19 &ndash; 10.00 &ndash; Morpethville &ndash; Mjr. Hawkes Istate commenced enrolling and attesting. 
 Oct. 20 &ndash; 4.30 P.M. &ndash; Outer Harbour S.S. Ascanius &ndash; Sailed.  Total embarked 31 officers 969 other ranks 1 Y.M.C.A. man 2 sister nursed and one man excess.  Transport under command Lt. F. Owen Smyth &amp; 22 other ranks embarked on Saldanha 108 horses. 
 25 &ndash; 8 A.M. &ndash; Freemntle &ndash; Arrived Freemantle Lt. Col. Johnston, 26 Officers and 759 men of 11th Batt. 2 sister nurses and 2 Chaplains. 
 Nov. 2 &ndash; 8 a.m. &ndash; Freemantle &ndash; Left Freemantle and joined main convoy on 4th. 
 9 &ndash; 7.15 &ndash; At Sea &ndash; H.M.A.S. Sydney steamed West
