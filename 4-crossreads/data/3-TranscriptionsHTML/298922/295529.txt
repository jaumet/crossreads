<p>Tuesday 7 August 1917</p>
<p>Go Alexandria <strike>today</strike> tomorrow &ndash; better but sore.</p>
<p>I'd like to warn the new Sisters arriving not to use Arabic words till they know their exact meaning. One in this ward made a tremendous "faux pas". To mislead patients from thinking she was one of the WHEEL BARROWS as the last reinforcements are called &ndash; i.e., "Pushed all the way". This girl used an expression which in English is unprintable. JESSIE McDONALD is in France it is a cousin here named Flora.</p>
