 Things are very dear about here.  If you happen on an egg it costs 1 franc (91/4d).  Boose too is dear, and I could picture ourselves in the old days paying 1 franc or 1.50 for a bottle of crook beer.  Things will gradually get better of course, it it will be some time (and perhaps we won't ve here) to get even fair value for our money.
