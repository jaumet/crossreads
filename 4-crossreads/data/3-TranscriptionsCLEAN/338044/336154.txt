  
 11th March:- To-day was at the camp of Tel-el-Kebir, that historic spot where Kitchener fought his battle. Visit the graveyard of the English soldiers fallen in that battle    &  afterwards, My friend (Sgt Legge) & I are both on business   After trying various visits (5th Fed Amb)  (4th Div Hdqus & 15th Fld Amb) where I have little success  &  return disappointed. 
 13th March. Again at Tel-el Kebir & after tramping all around the desert for hours, to nearly every unit in the place, I & Legge, eventually arrive at 4th Division, right out on a sand-hill at the back of the camp & here I meet with some success. I am taken for trial
