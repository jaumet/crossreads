<p>June, 1917. Friday 15<br/>Very hot all day. 7 Parade and 7.45 went down to river. Colonel rather touchy and kept us working till 1.30. Fell in again at 3.30 for bathing parade and after read paper. Paid in evening at 7 and got some wine. Bed at 10.</p>
