  
 Sunday [it seems this should be September] 1917 Sunday 16th This is a very quiet little village but there is plenty of rum & fun about Monday 17th Had a motor trip to the country today Tuesday 18th Put on a job painting the new bricks so that is another new trade on to the very long list Wednesday 19th Still slapping paint about Thursday 20 Nothing doing Friday 21st. A draft of P.B's arrived today so we expect to be leaving tomorrow or the next day Sunday 23rd Left here at 2 pm arrived in Boulounge at 6.30 pm doing the 25 miles in that time so it was [indecipherable] stopped in Boulounge for the night
