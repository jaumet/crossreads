  
 Anecdote 
 An officer lay between the Australian and Turkish lines wounded. 
 Two Australians saw him from a trench and discussed which of them should go into the fire swept zone im endeavour to bring the wounded one to safety. 
 The toss of a coin determined the choice. 
 He who called head crawled stealthily to the prone officer. Pulling him by the coat sleve he said. "Get on my back Sir & I shall endeavour to carry you to the trench & safety". 
 "My good man six bullets have passed into me or through me all-ready.
