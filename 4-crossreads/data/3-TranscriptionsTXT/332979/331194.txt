(We must all fall in) & We all marched off on a route March through the Principal Streets. On the March we were Cheered by the Crowds who turned out to give us a hearty send off. I can hardly describe my feelings when marching to the Tune I felt that the time of my life had arrived & I was more determined to do my duty. We duly arrived at the Station were our Kits were sent direct from the Drill Hall. After sorting out each Kit I found myne & when the Train arrived we were told to entrain & off we went to the Camp.
