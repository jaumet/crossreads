<p>On June 6th a party from the 7th Brigade successfully raided the Hun trenches doing considerable damage and taking some prisoners.</p>
<p>Two days later we heard of Lord Kitchener's death. This threw a gloom over everything for a while but we realised that it was no use worrying but just to carry on as usual.</p>
<p>On the 21st I went to Fort Rompie to see Jack. I found him in good health and as full of life as ever.</p>
