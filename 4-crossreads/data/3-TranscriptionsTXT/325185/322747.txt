 [Letter from AIF to Mr Giles] H.W. 
 AUSTRALIAN IMPERIAL FORCE 
 Base Records Office, A.I.E.F. Victoria barracks. Melbourne. 18th September 1916. 
 Dear Sir, 
 I now beg to advise you that information has been received to the effect that No. 2837, Private A=Giles, was admitted to the Ist.. Eastern General Hospital 25/8/16 suffering from Gun Shot Wound (mild). 
 His postal address will therefore be:- 
 No. 2837 Corporal A.C. Giles, "Wounded" Ist. Australian Infantry, C/o Australian Imperial Force Headquarters, 130 Horseferry Road, Westminster, LONDON. S.W. 
 Any further information coming to hand will be promptly transmitted. 
 Yours faithfully, J.M.LEAN. Major. Officer i/c Base Records. 
 [Addressed to] Mr. J.C.Giles, 20 Marshall St., STANMORE. N.S.W.
