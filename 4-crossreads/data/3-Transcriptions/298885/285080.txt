<p>Tues July 18th &ndash; 16<br/>At 12.30 we left Puchevillers and in heavy rain found our way to Bouzincourt. Here the whole of the 1st Aust Div have assembled before going into action.</p>
<p>Met Len and Eric and had a great old yarn</p>
