
Thursday July 5Mail arrived. Our first mail since leaving Ausy. All the boys excited. Pleased to hear all are well at home & with Glady. Charlie tells me Dulcie is sending him a parcelFriday July 6Riding test. Practically all passed. Saw first dummy gas attack to day & Scotchmen manoeuvering.Rumoured that big attack coming off.Saturday July 7Usual drill. Looking forward to getting out of isolation Started Bridge tournament to day to pass away long evenings.Sunday July 8Inspection by A.D.M.S.
