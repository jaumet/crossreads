 Thursday 15&nbsp;April 1915 Attended to JBN. 10 a.m.&nbsp; Report to O.C. re officers&nbsp; Riding on plateau between 7 &amp; 8 a.m.&nbsp; Also between 5 &amp; 6-30 p.m.&nbsp; Reading, writing, &amp; sleeping the remainder of the day.&nbsp; Several copies of the D. Telegraph recd from Doffie Williams 
 Friday 16&nbsp;April 1915 Riding morning &amp; afternoon&nbsp; Not out during the day otherwise. 
 Saturday 17&nbsp;April 1915 Hospital. Reading and writing all day.&nbsp; Went to the pyramid of Aborawash [Pyramid of Abu Rowash]&nbsp;this evening, about 7 miles to the Westward along the dessert. 
 Sunday 18&nbsp;April 1915 Mass at 7-30 a.m.&nbsp; Reading &amp; writing all day&nbsp; Ride between 5 &amp; 6-30 p.m.&nbsp; Concert in hall between 7-30 &amp; 9 p.m.
