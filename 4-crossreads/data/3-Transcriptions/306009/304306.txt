<p>1915<br/>Apr. 30 Fri.<br/><strike>Patients com</strike> Wounded commenced to arrive at 9.30 a.m. &amp; kept coming in all day by motor ambulances until 5 p.m. when 250 had been admitted &ndash; nearly all minor wounds. Everone excited &ndash; wounded have plenty to say about the engagement on the previous Sunday &ndash; Large numbers of extra beds are ready &ndash; We are also to mann a hospital at Ghezireh Palace Hotel which is to be converted into a Hospital. We are to have 300 beds in it by Sunday ready for more wounded.<br/>Number of sick patients admitted since No. 2 G.H. took over Hospital is 3,750.</p>
