<p>The name of this boat is the Kalagan of Liverpool a P &amp; O boat run by dirty smelly Lascars, the food very poor;</p>
<p>18th/ Passed land also island of Crete this morning, Two French mine layer escorting us-</p>
<p>21/ Ran into Malta for three hours, not allowed ashore. The island look very barren but thickly populated</p>
<p>22/ Full marching parade, a torpedo our stern, thank god it missed us</p>
<p>23/ Arrived Marsellies, Pulled guns to station</p>
