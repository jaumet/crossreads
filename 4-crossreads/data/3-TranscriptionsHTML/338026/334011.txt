<p>[18 Th]<br/>Grooming and Cleaning harness</p>
<p>[19 Fri]<br/>12 noon ordered to harness up. Left Boves at 1pm. and arr. Pont Noyelles. about 4 pm. Had billets to sleep in  Tied mules on to waggons</p>
<p><u>Pont Noyelles</u></p>
<p>[20 Sat]<br/>Left Pont Noyelles 1pm. and arr. At our next stopping place Beaucourt about 4 pm. Made a dug out as the billets were very poor.</p>
