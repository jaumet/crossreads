20-12-16Very cold all and snow lay unmelted on the thick ice. However, the day was quite fine and sunny Aeroplanes active &amp; Fritzs artillery very active bombarding all day long billets adjacent to here Appeared to be a stunt on our right (French) and we heard of French success at Verdun and British at Arras. Roads were extremely hard and [motor carrier] had difficult in moving owing to their slippery nature.
