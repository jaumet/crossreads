<p>6.11.1913  Many kisses dear Eddy for Yr very welcome letter &ndash; with the new Stamp! Tell Aunt Ethel I think her design is wonderful! I Hope you will like this pretty church &ndash; I am so pleased you like Yr P.Cards. Fond Love from Yr dear friend  CB</p>
