<p>are all the tombs of all the nations great people.  Lots of the tombs were protected by sand-bag work as protection against air-raids.  Two things I took more interest in than the rest they were:  The small casket  with the remains of the two murdered Princes &amp; the place where King Charles had the bones of Oliver Cromwell dug up &amp; hung on a pole as a warning to anyone else who tried to overthrow the Monarchy.  The leave ended all too quickly &amp; I went back to Camp in a sort of dream.  With all the roar of traffic &amp; a longing to get another leave to London</p>
