
<p>FRIDAY, OCTOBER 17, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Lopping in Mountain in</p>
<p>the morning &amp; measured up the</p>
<p>Boundary scrub for Radcliff in afternoon.</p>
<p>Darby down 175 ft &amp; no water yet.</p>
<p>Tickle drawing water to Darby</p>
<p>all day.</p>
<p>Peter &amp; I shifted my office desk into</p>
<p>my room at night.</p>
