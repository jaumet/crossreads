  
 [Reverse of the above postcard] On Active Service 
 Mrs A. J. Burrowes Franurra Rooty Hill N.S.W. Australia. I have just been given this by a nice girl who has offered to post it for me. We are still on the train, and nearly into France. Hope you are well dear. I am still quite well & happy as can be without you Love to the kiddies & your dear self from your Husband.
