<p>of the ignorance &amp; folly of the Fijian Mother  -  You'd think that any mother even half savage would prefer a clean skin to the ulcerated one of Yaws  -</p>
<p>I had tea with Dr &amp; Mrs Burge ? in their vy nice bungalow &ndash; they are young Scotch people  -  She looks vy  vy delicate  -  Two nice children were playing about  -  We ate &amp; talked &amp; laughed till 5.30 when all of us went down to the pier head &amp; picked up our respective launches &amp; so off to the parent ship  -</p>
<p>I mentioned a stone obelisk in a small triangular compound opposite the P  -  Office  -  What I thought registered a ship's name turns out on closer examination to be 'Cross &amp; Cargill' First missionaries &ndash; October 1835 &ndash; Pretty early days  -  Fiji became a B. colony 1874  -  1st land sales 1880  -  Suva made the capital 1882 - All this the carving on this obelisk perpetuates  -  It's been a glorious day climatically &amp; I've enjoyed it thoroughly  -</p>
