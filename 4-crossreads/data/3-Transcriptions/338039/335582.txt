<p>Continuation of Diary.<br/>The voyage from Egypt to France.<br/>Mar. 13th.<br/>Inspection by O.C- Break Camp at Moascar- March with full kit to station.  Entrain &amp; leave Moascar.<br/>Mar. 14th.<br/>Monotonous train journey to Alexandria- Arrive at Port at 7 am.  Embark on A. 41- S.S. Minneapolis- Destination unknown- ship packed with men &amp; horses- Food poor- hammock issued.</p>
