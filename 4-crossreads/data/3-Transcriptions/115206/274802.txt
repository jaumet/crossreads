<p>August,1918<br/>Sunday 18</p>
<p>In front of Morcourt, Bty Position<br/>Weather fine with cool breezes. O.P. line relaid.<br/>Bosche shelled O.P. and Bty the former with 5.9 How and the latter with whizz bangs. &ndash; no damage done.<br/>Salvaged a considerable amount of red and material  - gave same to Doc Knipe . Returned Line to Bdge<br/>Went at 11.30 p.m. Ken and I fixed her up again.<br/>Fritz phone in  action. A very heavy concentration of artillery in this district,<br/>60's, 9.2?, 8?, 6?, 4.5 and 18s all inside an acre of ground well forward.</p>
