A squadron of mounted police, at one part of the proceedings, charged clean through the mob, & by a freak nobody was killed. It was not till dark however & after a Colonel had come up to address them, that things quietened.  Now things are fairly smooth but guards are everywhere & sentries are posted at intervals along the line.
