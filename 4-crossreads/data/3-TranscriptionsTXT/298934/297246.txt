2nd May Sunday 1915
Relieved the Marines in the firing line.  Last night/This morn the Turks at 1.30 a.m. blew bugles & advanced with cries of 'Allah'.  As a result there are a lot more of dead 'uns in front of the trench.  We have very good trenches now.  A few snipers about troubling us.  Kitchener gave us 3 days with 30% losses to effect a landing.  We did it in as many hrs.
3rd May Monday 1915
