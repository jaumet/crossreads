It took us about thirty-eight hours to get to our destination  We arrived at night time & then got out of the trucks all of us as stiff as crutches & marched about seven miles to Billets in a village named  Outsetan   We had a couple of days spell & on 27th Nov we moved on in Motor Lorries right through Armentiers to the mouth of the communication trenches  Taking with us full pack & blankets & we just got settled in our fire-bays
