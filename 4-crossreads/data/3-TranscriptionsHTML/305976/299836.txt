<p>Tuesday Feb13<br/>Usual routine<br/>Wednesday Feb 14<br/>Stretcher drill today<br/>Remarkable different methods of NCOs giving command. Our S.M. glares &amp; yells. At &ndash; ten &ndash; shun. The pretty English Sgt (full of style) gives us a twang about it so At &ndash; ten &ndash; sheon  While the lusty youth also a Sgt.  shuts his eyes makes faces &amp; yells shun.<br/>Fine concert at stadium in evening by Melbourne Concert party.<br/>Thursday Feb 15th<br/>Riding exercise again all day<br/>Friday Feb 16th<br/>Our weekly Route march again to Trawool very enjoyable</p>
