<p>January 22 Tuesday<br/>During Last week Col Holden held a conference with the Chaplains at Salisbury (Thursday). It had snowed very heavily during the night before &amp; it was very heavy walking to the station.<br/>We had lunch at the Nelson Temperance Hotel &amp; engaged a room for the conference We discussed a scheme for meeting the needs of the Aust. at Horseferry Road by taking over the Wesleyan Church near by &amp; fitting it out as a social room during the week &amp; having evangelistic services on</p>
