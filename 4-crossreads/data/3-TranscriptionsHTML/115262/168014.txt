<p>Tuesday 4 September 1917.</p>
<p>Nothing much doing this morning till 10 a.m. Left shortly after for watering parade&nbsp;<s>at</s>&nbsp;to the Waddy 7 miles distant. Passed over ground wntrenched the by [by the]&nbsp;Turks and underground stables etc. of theirs. The Waddy is a creek that flows in a valley it has made for itself through countless years. The erosion each rainy season can be seen on the &quot;banks&quot;.</p>
<p>On the way we passed the old fort used by the Crusaders in their wars in Palestine. 12-15 miles lower down the Waddy Ghuzzi, the Turks hold it.</p>
<p>Detailed &amp; fell in for picquet at 6.30 pm. for the <s>1<sup>st</sup></s>&nbsp;next 24 hrs<br /><s>Wrot</s>&nbsp;Struck 1<sup>st</sup>&nbsp;post&nbsp;<s>on picquet</s>.</p>
