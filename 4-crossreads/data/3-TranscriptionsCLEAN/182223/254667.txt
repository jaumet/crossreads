  
 Monday 31 May 1915 Posted letters to Girls, Mollie, M. M. Bertram, M.M.Thomas, Senator Pearce.  Busy at office all day.  Old Watson at evening meal.  Captains Williams & plant back from Alexandria. 
 Tuesday 1 June 1915 In Cairo between 9-30 &11 am  Office work all day  Letters dated January 1915 from 219 Macquarie St & Blackheath. Not out during Evng  Letters from Arthur, D Wakins & Sen Pearce 
 Wednesday 2 June 1915 Weather as usual. Just [indecipherable] first thing in mng  Not out all day  Typewriting &c.  Many patients
