 25-4-17 probably be alright in a few days time, later he went to the Field Ambulance &amp; saw "whisky" Dawson (Col) who told him he would have to be operated on again straight away; At 9 oclock this morning all the horses were taking off the lines &amp; grazed on their way to Hissieh (6 miles away) here they were watered &amp; then brought back; we have been watering them with water brought in fantasees on camels since we came here.
