Le BizetWeather cloudy with bright intervals with temperature falling.
1st.3.18The following targets were engaged during the night, viz.,(1)  Laundry, C.5.c.70.5(2)  Soap House, C.5.c.70.28(3)  Suspected Coy. Hqrs., C.6.c.18.20No. of rounds fired 1500.  Time occupied 4 hours.  Enemy Artillery and Machine gun fire at normal activity.Weather gusty wind from north.In connection with a minor operation, the 43rd Battalion against an enemy Listening post at C.5.a.40.50, the Daylight Battery was instructed to "Stand To" hostile action was expected from enemy Machine guns situated in the Laundry, C.5.c.70.55.  A machine gun was specially emplaced at C.15.d.52.90. to fire intermittently on the Laundry.  Signal for "Action" of the daylight battery was a Red Very Light, for "All Clear" a Green Very Light.
