Walked along the river front ? the opposite bank of the Rhine here is precipitous and crowned by a large fort similarly situated to Edinburgh castle. At the junction of the Rhine and the Moselle there is a gigantic equestrian statue of Kaiser Wilhelm I. We climbed up on the pedestal of this and were rewarded by one of the most magnificent views imaginable of the junction of the two rivers and the surrounding country.
