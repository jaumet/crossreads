<p>Monday May 7.1917<br/>A nice fine warm day.  There was very little bombardment last night.  I had a walk around the town last night with C. Handcock.<br/>I got a copy of the "Argus" from Merrigum today, dated March 20.  <strike>Last</strike> To night there was a gas attack in the Armentieres sector and we all had to get out our gas respirators &amp; wear them at the alert, &amp; go to bed with them by our side.  From 8 O'clock until near mid  night our artillery in this sector kept up a tremendous straff.  Fortunately the gas did not reach here.  The local people were greatly excited &amp; made for their houses.</p>
