 Sunday 2 September 1917 On Q.M. Fatigue (water) and saw Keith Slade &amp; Russel Gilbraith.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 2]. 
 Monday 3 September 1917 Firing in morning &amp; exercise in evening. 
 Tuesday 4 September 1917 Exercises &amp; shooting, on Mess duty. 
 Wednesday 5 September 1917 Nothing doing:  on picquet at night. 
 Thursday 6 September 1917 Quiet day.  Good concert at Y.M.C.A., best ever held this side of the Canal. 
 Friday 7 September 1917 On beach guard, got books from Mrs. Walker &amp; Reg letters from Jess. 
 Saturday 8 September 1917 Nothing much doing, good concert by the Welsh Choir at Y.M.C.A. at night.
