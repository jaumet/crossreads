 46 Private John D. Wilson No. 46. Head Quarters (Pioneers.) 37. Battalion 10 Inf Brigade Australian Imperial Force On Active Service 
 June 2, 1916 Friday evening June 2/1916 we packed our kit bags in readiness to march into Seymour in the morning to catch a special train to Port Melbourne and thence march on board the "Persic" enroute to England. J.D. Wilson. Seymour Camp.
