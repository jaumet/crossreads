<p>April, 1917. Tuesday 17<br/>Day very windy only a little sunshine. Pumped at 12 after getting rations and cleaned up everything in afternoon until evening. Savage came over and stayed till 9.30 taking back 2 chairs from 12th Bde huts. Brigade moved out this morning. Bed very late after reading.</p>
