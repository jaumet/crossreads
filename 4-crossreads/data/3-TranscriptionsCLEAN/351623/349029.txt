  
 13th March 18 Arrived Rosyth & coaled & oiled. 
 14 March 18 Anchored at Rosyth. 
 15 March 18 Went into 14 days cells on HMS Crescent. Had a good time on a few biscuits a day & picking oakum. I also had 45 days pay stopped, dipped a badge & 6 months leave stopped, for forgetting there was a Navy for 6 days. 
 29th Mar 18 Released from cells. Had to wait on board Crescent till 1st April till ship returned. 
 1st April 18 Went on board. 
 2nd April 18 ) 3rd April 18 ) Anchored at Rosyth 
 4th April 18 Went out & done firing 
 5th April 18 to 10th April 18 Anchored at Rosyth
