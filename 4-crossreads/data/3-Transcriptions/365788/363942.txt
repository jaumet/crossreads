<p>3<br/>12 midnight &amp; then walk back. It was such a funny experience working in the dark &amp; we used to praise Fritz (as they call the Germans) when he sent up his star shells every 3 or 4 minutes. The dug-out is to be for the reception of wounded in the event of a rush of Casualties. Its walls are nearly six feet thick &amp; composed of sand bags (about 2 feet thick) &amp; about 4 feet of earth &amp; the roof will be about 4 feet thick (great beams sandbags earth stones &amp; bricks.)<br/>A shell fell within about 12 feet of our sergeant's mess the other day &amp; bored a hole some 12 feet deep into the soil, but did not explode.<br/>A Taube flew over today</p>
