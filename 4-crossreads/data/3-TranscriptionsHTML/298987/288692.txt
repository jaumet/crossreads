<p>After a bit of messing around to Headquarters Etc we got Tea consecting of 2 slices of bread each margarine &amp; Blancmange &amp; Tea. We only had Mess tins or clash brunes so Blancmange was rather slipery stuff to handle but it did not go to waste<br/>Were issued with 3 blankets &amp; allotted to tents with board floors Kitbags were left at the railway</p>
<p>Sunday 26th<br/>Not the bright England of yesterday heavy clouds &amp; showers all day. This camp is on a gentle slope &ndash; one side of a valey the opposite side was abruptly for fully 300 ft. On the side of this hill there are quite a collection of Regimental Badges &amp; designs made by cutting away the turf &amp; exposeing the clean white chalk that is only covered by a few inches of soil. An immense "Rising Sun" is cut out opposite here &ndash; very well done. A outline of Australia &amp; a Kangaroo further up</p>
