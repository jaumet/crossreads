<p>May 1918<br/>Monday 6</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daours<br/>Had a run out to 19th Bdges Hdqrs. also to 150th Bdges Hdqrs.<br/>Had a rough spin owing to condition of ground and horse.<br/>Rained very heavily all day<br/>Had Ginger dressed by V.serg. and he looks a little better<br/>Ken Gunn came across to Btry Hdqrs. He is looking very well.<br/>Looked them up whilst on a run to Villers Bretnoo.<br/>.</p>
