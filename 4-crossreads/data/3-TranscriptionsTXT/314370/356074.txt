 August, 1916 Wednesday 23 doing nothing &amp; getting a bit "fed up" rains nearly all the time &amp; everything is cold &amp; dismal If this is the summer, God help the winter 
 Thursday 24 wrote letter in the morning played cards until tea time &amp; walked to Tidworth to the theatre to see the "Play" Tidworth about 3 miles from our camp. Located old Tom Kennedy &amp; his crew
