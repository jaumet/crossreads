<p>July 1918<br/>Friday 12</p>
<p>Vaux- sur- Somme.2nd Bty.Position<br/>4 am. Weather fair to good. Fritz doing a big duty shoot on right.<br/>At 9 am the weather became very much cooler.<br/>Salvaged one mile of wire, and had a fair amount of graft.<br/>Finished plenty of  graft on dugout, <br/>Played cards and finished a fair amount of cash.<br/>Wrote letters to Marguette and [indecipherable] for Smithy.</p>
