<p>Breakfast cleaned up camp helped scrub 2 tables rested till 12.30 had dinner.  the heat is frightful rested till 5 P.M. fed up had tea 6 P.M.  Saddled up and went on outpost to the canal was woke up 12 midnight two gippos are sweeping the road.  turned in again 1 oclock.</p>
<p>Sunday July 22.7.17<br/>turned out 4 A.M.  there is a fog thick enough to lose a man.  followed Suept road Back to camp had Breakfast 6.30 cleaned up camp rested till 12.30 had dinner.  had three letters one from maud Bell and Joe Cohalan very hot day.  rested till 5 P.M. fed up had tea 5.30 read till 10 P.M. was</p>
