<p>Monday 10.6.18&nbsp; All ready for stunt&nbsp; 4 officers wounded&nbsp; fight commenced at 9.45 p.m.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11.6.18&nbsp; All objectives taken&nbsp; 300 prisoners, M.G. &amp; mortars- own casualties very small&nbsp; Hun shelling all day.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12.6.18.&nbsp; feeling off color&nbsp; sent for Robinson&nbsp; Wrote to Doff, went to Transport lines</p>
<p>Thursday 13.6.18&nbsp; Shelled all night, went by car to Div with Paton, then on to Beateaucourt [Berteaucourt] saw Wynter at [Coy?]</p>
