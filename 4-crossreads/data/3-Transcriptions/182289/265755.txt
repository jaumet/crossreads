<p>4.12.16<br/>No. 6 position searched roads and Communication trenches in rear of enemy's line 3&deg; elevation.  Notice of discharge of Gas by 10th Brigade received, necessary precautions taken.  Heavy bombardment by our Artillery and T.M. Battery from 2 to 3.30 p.m.  Lightly replied to by enemy.</p>
<p>5.12.16 &ndash; Chapelle Armentieres<br/>Right Sector:- No. 5 Gun fired from I.17a/08.04 to I.18c/0300 road from La Bleon A Bas Trou between 6 p.m. and 8 p.m.  No. 6 Gun fired I.17b/05.08 to 11a/09.03.  Communication trenches 750 rounds result unknown.  There were two alarms of Gas at 6.45 p.m. and 11.30 p.m.<br/>Left Sector:- No. 7 Gun fired 1000 rounds on I.6b/08.09 roads and trenches roun [round] Le Temple.  No. 8 Gun fired on I.6a/05.00 Road and Dump at Intact Farm between 7 and 11 p.m.  Aeroplanes heard overhead about 12.15 midnight.  Very quiet day.  Heavy bombardment by our Artillery from 10 p.m. to 12.45 a.m.  Gun crews stood to from 8.30 to 10.30 p.m. with masks.</p>
