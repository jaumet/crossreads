
3.  I stayed all night & slept well being used to a soft bed again now.  I was up early & out in the back garden at 7 am.  It was frosty but not cold.  I amused myself again till down rushed the youngest girl in a flutter to get to Her Duties in some War Club I think.  She & I sat down to-gether but She had to rush off in the same style as we had to do, & by that time another of the Girls came down.  Then the rest of the family one by one.  I sat at Breakfast for over an Hour.  After Breakfast
