It has not suffered as badly as I had thought or indeed as badly as it might have done.  Workmen have repaired a good deal of the damage of course.  Many of the beautiful windows have been completely shattered.  Amiens will leave Abbeville in the shade as a commercial city soon.  All the people have a business like appearance and are very business like in reality as I found to my cost.
