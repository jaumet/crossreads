<p>June 1918<br/>Sunday 30</p>
<p>Poulonville. &ndash; On road and at Bty Posit.<br/>Pulled into School yard at 3.50 am. After a farily solid trip all night. The road Palmer, Evayling and Garfott to Bty position 1.25 C.<br/>We proceeded at 8 pm for Battery position with the guns.<br/>Arrived at position at 2.30am 1/6/18<br/>Just arrived when we received two salvoes of whizz bangs. 1 horse killed and Cpl Kelly slightly wounded &ndash; Mr Koens horse also slightly wounded.</p>
