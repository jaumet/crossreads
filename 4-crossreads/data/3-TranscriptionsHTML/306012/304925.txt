<p>Tues. 9th July<br/>Yesterday I managed to get away from the A.D.S. for a day collecting drugs &amp; medical stores from the Base Depot.  It was a pretty warm day although we did not notice it so much on the Ambulance, on which we ran through village after village, getting further &amp; further away from the line.  The country looked very fine &ndash; numerous fields covered with ripe wheat and others one mass of flowers of different colours blue, red, white, pink &amp; yellow - the pretty wild poppy being much in evidence.  The villages further back were full of civilians, many of them refugees from evacuated</p>
