<p>10-7-16<br/>Mon 10th<br/>Mills went back to Tel-El-Kebir this morning;  It is rumored the 6th L.H. got 3 prisoners yesterday two of them wounded, the 6th came on to the enemy &amp; should have drove them on to the 7th Regt. but the 7th were not in position &amp; blame the 6th for starting too early;  Nothing out of the ordinary took place today.</p>
<p>11-7-16<br/>Tue 11th<br/>A Taube came over this morning at 10.30 &amp; had us leading our horses on to the parade ground again but she did not visit us for long &amp; did not interfere with us  a couple of shots were fired at her from a mountain battery &amp; a few of the machine guns also gave her a few shots but she did not come close enough to be in much danger;  Our</p>
