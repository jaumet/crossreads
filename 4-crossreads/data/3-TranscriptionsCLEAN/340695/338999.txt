  
 24-7-17. Mr Bosche Has been keeping us busy with air raids of late he was over in  great force last night & it was pretty to watch our search lights looking for them. They dropped a good many bombs nearby, one did some damage to a field Hospital. 27-7-17. Yesterday was St Anna's day & the Mademoiselle where we mess (Butts Coog Mac & I) is named Anna & it is customary to wish girls so named "bon fete" & she in reply kisses you, What! Last night I wandered round as is our want (was on my own last night) at 9 pm for a cup of coffee, before turning in.
