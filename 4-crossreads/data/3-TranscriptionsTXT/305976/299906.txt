 Saturday Aug 11th On duty Sunday Aug 12th On duty Monday Aug 13th Last night in Cairo before leaving for the line. Sent home parcels &amp; had a good time Thursday Aug 14th Inspection of unit by Major Buxton Wednesday Aug 15th Night ride on Camels. Saddling in dark. Rode 5 mile unsaddled &amp; saddled 10 minutes allowed. Capt. S. informed us if anybody had a spill they would hear from him. He was the only one who fell due to his Camel putting his foot in a hole. What Joy among the boys.
