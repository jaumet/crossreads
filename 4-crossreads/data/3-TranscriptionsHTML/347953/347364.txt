<p>16</p>
<p>Sights</p>
<p>The famous pyramids at Mena &amp; elsewhere are very wonderful built during the 3rd &amp; 4th dynasty or 5000 years  B.C. Cheops and the Step pyramid  at Sakkara are the largest &amp; most conspicuous reaching to a height of about 400 feet</p>
<p>The Citadel built in 1174 on an elevated position overlooking the city of Cairo &amp; from which the whole town could be bombarded &amp; destroyed unmercifully. It contains a Mosque &ndash; beautifully decorated especially the chandeliers &ndash; a hospital &amp; a prison containing many Captured Turks</p>
