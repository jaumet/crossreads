
<p>MONDAY, OCTOBER 13, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Round town in morning.</p>
<p>Had lunch at Usher&#39;s with</p>
<p>M<sup>rs</sup>&nbsp;Allison, Mum &amp; Linda &amp; went</p>
<p>to the pictures after. Went home</p>
<p>for dinner &amp; went to Officers</p>
<p>Mess again with Mum &amp; the</p>
<p>Allisons.</p>
