<p>got some <strike>coffee</strike> soup on the way home arriving at breakfast time. I went to sleep after, had dinner &amp; then slept again until 7. Wrote diary and slept all night.</p>
<p>19-20-16<br/>Breakfast at 7 and got orders to go on job at 8 at 7-45. So had to hurriedly get ready and moved out at about 9. Got another set in and had dinner there. Flers is quite close and I had a short look over it. The cemetery has numerous german graves in it. Fine in the morning although a sharp nip in the air and when we knocked off at 3 it snowed and continued until about 6 o'clock. On the way home passed [Close] who afterwards returned</p>
