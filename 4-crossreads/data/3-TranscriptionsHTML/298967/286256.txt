<p>DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS</p>
<p>18 &ndash; Petillon Sector &ndash; 10th &amp; 12th releive 1st and 2nd Bns. in reserve billets.</p>
<p>19 &ndash; Billets shelled 2 officers &amp; 1 other ranks killed 7 other ranks wounded.</p>
<p>30 &ndash; German Raid on 11th Batt.</p>
<p>June 6 &ndash; Fleurbeaux line Fleurbeaux &ndash; Batt. went into line.  Gas alarm, no gas.</p>
<p>28/29 &ndash; Sailly &ndash; Relieved by 51st Batt. A.I.F. proceeded to billets west of Saily.</p>
<p>July 1 &ndash; Bn. marched from Sailly to Moolenacker.</p>
<p>1/22 &ndash; Organising and training raiding party.</p>
<p>9 &ndash; Marched to Mt. Des. Catto billets for night.</p>
<p>10/11 &ndash; Godewaersvelde &ndash; Marched to Godewaersvelde and entrained for Doullens.</p>
<p>11 &ndash; Marched to Halloy.</p>
<p>12 &ndash; Naours &ndash; Marched from Halloy to Naours.</p>
<p>16 &ndash; Herissart &ndash; Marched to Herissart.</p>
<p>18 &ndash; Forceville &ndash; Marched from Herissart to Forceville.</p>
<p>19 &ndash; Marched from Forceville to Albert and Billited.</p>
<p>20 &ndash; Marched from Albert to Sausage Valley.</p>
