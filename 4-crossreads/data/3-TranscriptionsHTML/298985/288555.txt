<p>very far from Fritz but were behind the crest of a ridge so bullets kept high Got amongst a fair patch of small shells on the way but no damage done except that they had some gass in them &amp; we all got a bit of it Harry Collier had to taken back with it Had to ware masks 2 or 3 times. A few shells near the job one about 19 yds from me before I had had time to dig a big enough hole to get into. Saw a great display of Searchlights after Taubis Counted 15 lights All centered on a Plane. The bright little Shrap bursts</p>
