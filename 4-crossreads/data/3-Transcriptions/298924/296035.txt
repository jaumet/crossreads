<p>Saturday 22 February 1919<br/>Rafa<br/>Daisy's letter Dec. 29.  Sorry Egyptian officials mutilated Cable &amp; I printed it, remaining &ndash; note re Katandra &ndash; St. Ives do well, but I hope to meet myself now if only 1 night will go Scotts.  Mother's letter Dec. 22, also letter from Pearce of Pastoralists Review, he said old R.B. Wilkinson still going strong.  Very glad Joe is safely home &amp; well &ndash; we will have a lot to talk about.</p>
