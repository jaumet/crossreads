 Sunday 28 October 1917. 
 On again at dawn travelled all day till 2 pm when we reached a point between Shiekh Nuran Stn &amp; Shellal. Camel Brigade  Brigade  bivouacked.&nbsp; and &nbsp; 
 Another batch of 18 letters I received by 2 lots of mail that must have gone to Engd &nbsp; before coming to Egypt. This is good, oh! 
 On picquet tonight.
