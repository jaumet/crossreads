<p>"Francois Josef est mort." It is plain enough written but when yelled at me in his accent he completely bamboozled me. Seeing that I was dumfounded he seized a paper and pointing to a picture of the late emperor of Austria he stabbed it through with a penknife. There was no mistaking his meaning then. I bought a couple of french papers and am forwarding them to you. They will probably be of interest to Miss Magner too.<br/>Another peculiar feature here are the drays.
