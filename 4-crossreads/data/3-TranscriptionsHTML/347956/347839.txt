<p>Mon 28th<br/>Were signalling all day with Head Quarters;  The B. Sqd signallers were paraded before the Colonel &amp; Howarth asked for a district court martial but at dinner time the Colonel had an enquiry &amp; found he could not get a case against the signallers &amp; they were dismissed.</p>
<p>we fell in at 6 PM &amp; were marched off for an hours lamp practice &amp; were dismissed in time for the commencement of the concert which was a great success.  the Chairman read a telegram for George Reid saying he was sorry he could not attend as he had to appear at the Palace.  I suppose we shall see him along any day now.</p>
