
19-12-15Turks attention off us a bit as we are bidding the Peninsula Goodbye some time tonight or early tomorrow morning; Everything has gone along smoothly so far & only the 1st. L.H. remain ashore belonging to the 1st Aus. & New Zealand Division.Les has been put on the FH phone in place of Tomlinson who is being sent away as he has a sore foot & he loses his head too easy at anyrate to be on duty.
20-12-155Mon 20thHad a very exciting & interesting time last night; We fell in & filed into the trench at 5 o'clock shortly after the 4.30 party had left for their boat; About 3 mile of trenches from Destroyer Hill to No.2 sub-section of this Regt. was manned by 11 men only including the Adj & 2 signallers Traill on Destroyer Hill & Leask on Camel
