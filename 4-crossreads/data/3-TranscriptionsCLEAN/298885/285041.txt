  
 Thu. Jan. 13th -16 Mess Orderly.  Fell downstairs with can of coffee, dish of porridge and dish of stew. 
 Fri. Jan. 14th -16 Innoculation for third time.  First rifle drill and also inspection of kit. 
 Sat. Jan. 15th -16 Fire and boat drill in case of fire or emergency.  Concert at night. 
 Sun. Jan. 16th -16 Church Service 10.30 A.M. 
 Mon. Jan. 17th -16 Passed another ship.  Also passed Cape Guardefui on east coast of Africa. 
 Tue. Jan. 18th -16 Passed Aden at present entering Red Sea.
