
March 1918Tuesday 26
Hollebeke. Zero Road3.30am Fritz shelling at intervals 10 seconds, - road.for the past few days. I have made no mention of a terrific battle in progress down south. The latest news today regarding the crisis is that Fritz is in Albert. Our Divvie is going down at any time now.Read a circular issued by Sir Doug Haig, in which he appeals to every man to do his utmost in the critical battle of the war.Weather fairly cool; Charlie  Smith died of wounds at 3pm.
