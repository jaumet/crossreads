The sex God bless them, did their share amply &amp; well, to win the war.<br/>At Waterloo Station most of the company were assembled well ahead of time &amp; after punishing the refreshment room, entrained, went to sleep &amp; it was like the shattering of a beautiful dream to be kicked &amp; cuffed into wakefulness to commence the dreary tramp from Wilton to Hurdcott.
