I came in here two days ago &amp; am Medical Registrar to this Hospital which is only in process of formation; we have about 200 beds occupied but will have 400 to 500 beds in the course of another 2 weeks; it is pioneer work getting things in order &amp; organizing the place &amp; very interesting work. I am a kind of medical superintendent with administration work which is right into my hand, &amp; I just carry on, subject of course to the C.O. in charge. Later on General Howse talked of attaching me to a big new Hospital of 2000 Beds, but that may or may not eventuate: I have learned now to take each day's work as it comes along; &amp; to be surprised at</p>
