  
 Aug 15. 16. 17th. Nothing of much interest except news from Russian & Italian fronts - Receipt of large mail from "Aussy". 
 Aug 18th. Fourth Division relieved by First. Parcel despatched to home. 
 Aug. 19th. Our spare time occupied by "Soccer" - More letters to & from home. 
 Aug 20th. Harry parsons - Keith Harris etc - Death of Jim Cunningham & 3 others 1st Fld. Amb. 
 Aug 21st Sabbath - Church "Confession & Communion" - Fifth Fld Amb relieved us at "Anzac Rest Camp 
 Aug 22nd. Our Ambulance moves off from Vadencourt to Albert where we bivouac for the night.
