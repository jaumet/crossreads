I hope some final victory comes before next Christmas.
I have heard nothing of Keith but am hoping very much I may see him; I had a line from Jim Lee who is at the front & he is going to try & look me up next short furlough. Syd Lee was killed not long ago: so was Young Woodhill of Wentworth Falls; the toll is very heavy.
