<p>5-5-16<br/>Fri 5th<br/>Received an order today to send to Minia any blankets, tents, the water troughs &amp; anything we could do without so as to make things light for the transports  when we move<br/>The party for Tel-El-Kebir left this morning;  A fire broke out in a village a couple of miles from here this evening &amp; burned very willing for a few minutes, a swim &amp; cards as usual tonight;<br/>Sgt Pannier went today</p>
<p>6-5-16<br/>Sat 6th<br/>Received news from Minia this morning saying the Allied Forces made a big advance in France but the news has not been confirmed;  Nothing out of the ordinary happens here &amp; are expecting marching orders at any time;  Had a concert tonight with some very good items.</p>
