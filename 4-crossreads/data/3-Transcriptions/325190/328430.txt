<p>(100 years or so one said), I pointed out that one cant judge a country after a week's sojourn.<br/>This morning we marched to the boat &amp; embarked before dinner time, but being on a ration fatigue, I in common with the other two, were able to remain on shore longer &amp; indulge in sundry cups of tea at the refreshment stall in the sheds.<br/>The boat is of a fair size &amp; contains both English &amp; Australians, tho' the latter predominate.<br/>Just before tea time we left the wharf &amp; slowly pulled out of the harbour, then hugged the coast of the Isle of Wight</p>
