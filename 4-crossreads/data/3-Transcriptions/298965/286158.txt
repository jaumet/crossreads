<p>From 12th April to 17th didn't do anything only stopped on the ship.</p>
<p>April 17th<br/>Went by means of two or three boats drawn by a pinnace to H.M.S. Queen to practice embarking &amp; disembarking on the battleship.</p>
<p>April 24th<br/>On April 24th all the troops got a bit excited as this was the day we got orders to have everything ready to leave the Malda. During our stay on the Malda we went through a great deal of practice in embarking &amp; disembarking &amp; night attacks. We left the Malda &amp; embarked on a torpedo destroyer &amp; subsequently boarded the H.M.S. Queen at about noon we left the Harbour at Mudros on the battleship</p>
<p>There is a lot of things of interest to be seen on a battleship &amp; we kept ourselves busy looking round.</p>
