 Perhaps I may go again but once home my memory will keep its own secrets &ndash; so I bid you farewell. 
 A.P.K. Morris "Tottington" Armadale 
 18/10/1914 to 22/2/1917 
 Finis 
 Sequel After being in Australia one month and losing my father I became so disgusted with the apathy of the eligible men who remained here, I determined to again enlist.  I spent 5 weeks in camp, was promoted, but illness overcame me and I spent six weeks in hospital and was discharged from the army as permanently medically unfit on 12th July 1917.
