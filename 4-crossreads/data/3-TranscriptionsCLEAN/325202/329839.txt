Nov 20/16The Division 'pulled out' about a week ago & we put in 4 days at Ribemont & came on yesterday to Vignacourt (10 miles from Amiens) where we are to put in the rest of our spell.
The weather has been very cold & several frosts & our first fall of snow It was only a taste of what we must expect but I am not looking forward
