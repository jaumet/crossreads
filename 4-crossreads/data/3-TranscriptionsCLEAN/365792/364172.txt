 3  Turned out about 7.15. parade 9 to12, plenty of bayonet fighting, & had to muck about with my puttees & put them on Regimentally. Getting warmer every day. Looks as if we are going to stay here for a while as the Batt is connected by phone.  Route march for 11/2 hrs in the afternoon; jolly fine going too, didn't change step all the way, but worked up a sweat. Went for a stroll after tea; Harry played a game of Soccer on the green & we finished up at the Village. The Port Wine isnt half bad. On our way home we were stopped by 'Sticky" The Major - who wanted to know what we were doing out after 7 pm. Night march to the square in absolute quiet just to test us, & the boys behaved well.
