  
 September 1915 Wednesday 15 Rained heavily through the night. Our flags were wet through and we had to take the panniers[?] and put them in a tent. We are having an easy time up to now. 
 Thursday 16 Fine Beautiful morning. Lovely fresh sweet air. Was up before reveille and went about half a mile to a spring and had a good wash in fresh water. A terrific downpour of rain with thunder and lightning completely flooded all our tents. We were just about to shift camp to higher ground.
