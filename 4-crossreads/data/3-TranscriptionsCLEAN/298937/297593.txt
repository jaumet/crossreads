  
 Saturday 10th June 
 I am writing this on my kit bag in a tent at the Miscellaneous Details Camp, Tel-El Kebir.  We left Maadi yesterday morning, had breakfast at Mrs. Nyland's, catching the 11 oclock train for here, which arrived at 1.15 p.m.  They took our medical history & also informed me that I stood a good chance of getting dispensery job, for there were plenty wanted.
