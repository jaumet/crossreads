We had several very fine concerts here one of them was a beauty & there were some very fine artists there I was down in Helouan several times there are one or two very fine hotels here, mostly tourists patronize them, but theres very little doing while the war is on. On the 20th Len came out & fetched  me a bunch of letters & papers he was down on leave from Tel-el-Kebir for 24 hours so he did not have much time to spare A few days after this I was one of a party of 7 [?] patients & about 10 nurses who set out for a picnic
