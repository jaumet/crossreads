<p>29/1/17<br/>Plenty of shelling round Hd. Qrs. last night and we had several casualties.</p>
<p>30/1/17<br/>A raid by the Fifth Yorks last night and we hear it was successful.</p>
<p>We showed the advance party of the releiving bn round our lines last night.</p>
