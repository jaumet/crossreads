just a bare existence, it takes a lot of hardships to kill a man since we have been in here we have not averaged 2 hours sleep per night only 2 meals a day, the men have worked fairly hard
This afternoon the whole Coy. is on fatigue cleaning out an old communication trench that the Canadians let got to ruin, we are doing it so as we can have a safe passage down to the dumps a 4th Bn. runner was shot dead yesterday an Engineer wounded through not having this sap. 
Our Bombers killed 3 Germans last night they attempted a small raid on one of our Sections fell in the muck.
