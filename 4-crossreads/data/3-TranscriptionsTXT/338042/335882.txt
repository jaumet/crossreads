
May 27thEarly rise - As in the old days we pack up our goods - and after being issued with new gas masks we set out from Bertangles for Flixecourt - passing through an old residential town of ours - to wit: "La Chaussee. - Pirancourt - Arriving at our destination, we encamp in Huts - and at 6 p.m. give our "first show" to Officers amp; Men of Fourth Army H.Q's.May 28th:- The "Coves" are being very decently treated but do not altogether care for increased army discipline.
