<p>October 1918.<br/>Saturday 12</p>
<p>In reserve, Ramicourt<br/>Weather wet. Tommy burial party very busy getting the dead buried. Trumpeters played the Last Post over 200 buried. Received a few letters, very welcome too. Had a shot with Jim's revolver. Had a good rest all day.</p>
