<p>May, 1917. Monday 21<br/>Day cloudy &amp; very sultry. Reveille at 5 &amp; breakfast at 5.30. Fell in at 6 and after cleaning billets moved out at 7.15. Arrived at Ovillers at 12.45. Very tired but visited Becourt saw-mills and got my pack and to chateau for canteen stores. Had a good bath in evening &amp; wrote this up &amp; read papers until dark. Moving Henincourt in morning.</p>
