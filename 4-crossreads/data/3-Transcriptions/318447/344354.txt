<p>22nd January<br/>Near Richon-Le-Zion<br/>We had not been more than five days in that camp at Deiran when we got news that the camp had been condemned &amp; we had to move.  Accordingly we came about 4 miles further on to the outskirts of Richon.</p>
<p>I had just got my home put up &amp; drains dug around it when I got orders to take six time expired natives down to Rafa &amp; bring their relief up, So I rode to the station with six natives on donkeys streaming</p>
