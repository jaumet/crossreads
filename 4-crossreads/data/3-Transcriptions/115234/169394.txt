
<p>Came to &#39;Blighty&#39; on March 22<sup><u>nd</u></sup> 1917.&nbsp; Taken to 3<sup>rd</sup> London general Hospital. Wandsorth.&nbsp; Had six months in England enjoyed every hour of it.&nbsp; Made many friends.&nbsp; On Sept 28<sup><u>th</u></sup> marked fir again for active Service.</p>
