<p>came our turn to go into the firing line, which we did some time in the middle of April.</p>
<p>If I am not mistaken we went in on a Friday night, and after the first night we were getting used to it, it was very funny the first night, I will bet the Germans knew there was some new crowd in, as the boy's were firing as fast as they could load, and firing at nothing at all.</p>
<p>The first Sunday we were in the German's bombarded our front line, of course as we had not had a bombardment before we naturally thought the world was coming to an end, I think they sent over about 74 shells, but after we got hardened to it that bombardment was only a flea bite.
