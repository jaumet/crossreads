  
 30 November 1916 Thur Xmas parcels from Winnie & Mother. Toby Wheeler evacuated. Still C.C.S. Tent. Sid Coleman comes to see me. Big feed in tent at night cream peaches cake prunes & chocolates. 
 1 December 1916 Fri Lux Manfred evacuated another old bund gone. The cold weather is fixing the boys up. 
 20 more come into dressing station on stretchers [lad] with [drum] - Madman in tent
