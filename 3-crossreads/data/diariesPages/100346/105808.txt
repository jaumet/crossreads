Thursday Dec 20th
O.T.B. : warned to go to signalling school tomorrow : V.D. dental inspection in morn : issued with our U.K. kit too : went with Tom Clunie up to see Reg after tea :
Friday Dec 21st
O.T.B. : Reveille 5 am : fell in 6 am marched to Station : train to Dinton marched to Fovant camp : camped at 5th T.B. met many of the Batt N.C.O.'s etc : stroll with Jock Craig after tea :
Saturday Dec 22nd
Fovant : medical dental exam in morn : concert at night :
Sunday Dec 23rd
Fovant : church parade in morn : stroll through village in the afternoon : Xmas carols hymns etc in YMCA at night were very nice :
