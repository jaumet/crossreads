<p>Jan. 2 Saturday 1915<br/>Troopship Suevic changed position falling in rear of Thermosticles</p>
<p>Submarine cast off from Berrima accompanying the Squadron as protector. Orders issued that all lights out as far as possible.</p>
<p>Jan. 3 Sunday 1915<br/>2 Horses died. Weather getting warmer smooth sea. Averaging over 11 knots per hour. "Ayrshire"falling back.<br/>Examination of rifles<br/>Fire drill &amp; boat parade.<br/>Berrima Hauled to for the purpose of replenishing submarine with oil.</p>
<p>Jan. 4 Monday 1915<br/>Weather calm &amp; fine<br/>9th day vaccanated, feverish, off tucker, went to bed early. Temperature 101<br/>Washing day</p>
