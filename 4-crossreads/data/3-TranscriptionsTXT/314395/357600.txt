The Military people are organising a march through London on April 25.  All Peninsular men or men with Decorations are to take part, so I suppose I will be there in both capacities.
The food problem is becoming very serious these days.  Almost everything is subject to government restrictions as to the amount purchasable etc.  The more wealthy people are hoarding the scarce commodities, thereby making things far worse.  Sugar and Spuds are almost an unknown quantity.
