 Tuesday 15th Jan     Weather mild with some heavy rain.   Felt a bit off colour during the day but managed to get some writing done during the evening.   Received more mail during the day. 
 Wednesday 16th     Fairly cold and felt very off colour a reaction from my trip.   Did some writing and made another start on learning French.   Drugs very hard to get all the battalions etc crying out for stuff. 
 Thursday 17th      Much milder and feeling better.   Spent some time reading my papers.   Will return them to Oldnance as waste paper is now being collected, a step in the right direction in economy.   Had a sponge bath before turning in.   Rain during the day.
