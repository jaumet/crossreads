One chap had his place lined with white lace curtains and furnished with elaborate bedclothes. The walls had photographs on them and just under the first blanket screen from the trench was an inner curtain of black lace material. Milord took his ease here naked except for a pair of women's drawers. It is a queer war! Just 300"in fron of us are trenches that were our front line prior to launching the attack of 19 May. Ville-sur-Ancre sadly bombarded and with its slate rooves partly demolished lies in the valley close to the river and in temps de paix was no doubt a nice little village. The Caterpillar could be located by the fringe of hedge on top of its banks. The ridge of Morlancourt with Boche posts on it looks down on us here. All the positions are open cultivated
