<p><span style="line-height: 1.6;">Saturday 21 April 1917.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>In camp and on fatigue duty. On ASC. fatigue lumping stores &amp; loading &amp; unloading wagons during morning and in&nbsp;afternoon on AM.C. QM&#39;s fatigue. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Rather easy in afternoon<sup>&nbsp;</sup>and was able to&nbsp;get washing done. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Camp seems deserted. All on extended leave. 2.400 troops marched out on leave today. &nbsp;<br /><s>Did some w</s>a<s>shing</s></p>
