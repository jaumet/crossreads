
Monday 10 April 1916Raining hard all day.  Stables in morning then guard instruction in Y.M.C.A. tent with Stables again in evening.
Tuesday 11 April 1916On Guard all day.  Five prisoners in guard tent.  Got a letter from Father saying they had had an inch of rain and got 24/3 for Store merino ewes.Was transfered to B Squadron (17th Reinforcements).
