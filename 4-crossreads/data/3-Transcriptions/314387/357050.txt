<p>Monday Oct 1st<br/>Left Dickebush one o'clock marched to Wippenhook arrived about five same afternoon<br/>Tuesday 2nd<br/>Addressed and congratulated by Colonel on good work the Battalion did in the stunt<br/>Wednesday 3rd<br/>Had a spell all day.<br/>Thursday 4th<br/>Same as previous day<br/>Friday 5th<br/>Nothing in the morning afternoon route march.</p>
