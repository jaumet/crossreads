 1915 June 11 Fri. Secured the two tunics alright &ndash; they are better than the originals but can hardly be said to look respectable. Have now over 700 in hospitals. No Australians coming in &ndash; only Imperials &ndash; it appears  only  all former are being sent to Malta hospitals &amp; then sent back to Egypt when alright to join new units at the Base.
