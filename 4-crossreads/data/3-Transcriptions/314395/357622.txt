<p>Belgium<br/>3.8.17</p>
<p>Dear Dad,<br/>The attack eventuated on July 31st and so far I have survived with a whole pelt.  Things have been fast and furious since the kick-off and are likely to be so for some time, but I fancy that the venture will be a success.  The attack began at 3.50 am on the 31st and the Field Artillery limbered up and advanced at 5.45 am.
