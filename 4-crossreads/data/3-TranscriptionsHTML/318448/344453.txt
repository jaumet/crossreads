<p>11/7/18<br/>I have just been able to have a smoke again now,  I have been unable to touch tobacco for over a week to the great detriment of my temper.<br/>There was a concert last night down at the main Supply Dump Jericho,  given by the "Bats" They were extremely good indeed. One of the cleverest &amp; funniest  things being one fellow who came in dressed as rather a moth eaten Prima Donna &amp; proceeded to sing "Il Miserere" in falsetto They had a wonderful stock of clothes &amp; were all good dancers.
