<p>then and it came to mother earth again, the Natives soon shifted and made a way for us it was like a pantomime while it lasted. we arrived back in camp after a little excitment. I was looking at their railway Engines &amp; cars, the are quite up to date, as regards the carriages, such as dining car ect. The engines are very big &amp; powerful, they are altogether different from ours. I think they are just as fast as ours. I could not make out why the people in Cairo were laughing when we went past them. The boy say they were laughing at me or rather my hair I had my hat off.</p>
