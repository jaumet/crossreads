The address I had was Albert House &amp; after walking along for a good way I at last arrived at a large Building with the name outside, I went upstairs to No 3 &amp; knocked at the Door. A young lady answered the Door &amp; after introducing myself I was invited in. The Lady of the House had received word from Australia so was expecting me. The name of the Family is W.A. Smith. After a few words I was told to make myself at home &amp; then I entertained them with some of my experiences until Dinner Time.
