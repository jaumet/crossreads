<p>August,1918<br/>Sunday 4</p>
<p>Lamotte and Villers Brettoneau.<br/>Arrived at Bty position at 5 am. this morning, wet and fairly tired. Young Ken and Mc settled in a good sort of possie. <br/>Spent a quiet day, built a little home our original one having been "snowdropped."<br/>Fritz commenced a "mad minute" shoot but did no damage to speak of. Weather wet during the evening Fritz visited district with bombs.</p>
