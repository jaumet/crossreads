<p>On the night of 15th of October about 11 pm we were awakened by the gas alarm going, and on looking out, found Fritz was bombarding our ridge along both sides of us with gas shells and high Explosive. We got into our gas masks with all haste, and sat there waiting for him to stop until we were nearly suffocated and then took a risk and took them off for a few seconds then on again.</p>
<p>All that night he kept on with a short break now and again and it was about 9 am when he finished up. All that day we could smell the gas about and that night when I took over the Instrument I did not feel at all clever.</p>
