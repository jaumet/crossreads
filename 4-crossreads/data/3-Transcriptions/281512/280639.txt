The fine tower is a pitiable apology of a brick dump, scarred &amp; riddled with shell holes. Its beautifully carved facades are "small-poxed" with shell splinters, not a vestige of the carving having escaped.  The figures are headless &amp;amp: the wonderful columns &amp;amp: carved pillars lay like fallen giants across the mangled remnants of roofs &amp;amp: other superstructures.
