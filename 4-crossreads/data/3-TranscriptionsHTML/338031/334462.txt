A good few whales were sighted &amp; some of them presented the appearance of a submarine emerging from the vasty deep.<br/>November 17th<br/>At daybreak the ship was stationary in Table Bay. The previous day we had sighted the brown &amp; somewhat dismal coast of South Africa. In many respects it is like the Australian coast as a monotonously sad and desolate landscape.
