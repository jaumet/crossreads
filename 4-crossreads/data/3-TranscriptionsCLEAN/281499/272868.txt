Still alive & kicking had a little better rest last night for it ceased raining about 6, our chaps bombarded something terrific all night it was one constant roar & rattle, old Fritz poked a few over among us, & one 9.2 lobbed about 15 yards to the left of our little shanty throwing dirt & mud all over us, it was wonderful no one was hurt for it did not lob more than 3 yards from where the 2nd Batt. Bombers were sleeping with only waterproofs over their heads, talk about narrow shaves.
