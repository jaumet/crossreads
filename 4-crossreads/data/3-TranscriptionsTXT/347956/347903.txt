 28.2.15 
 Sun 28th A record attendance at Church this morning &amp; Col Plaine preached a rattling good sermon;  Stan has had a sore throat &amp; was not on parade yesterday or Friday but is OK again thanks to my Lungkura it cured both him &amp; I 
 Stan received a pass this afternoon &amp; has gone to Cairo &amp; I am putting the afternoon in scribbling;  I lost the top of my pen a few weeks ago &amp; the nib has been twisted about every way &amp; is not much good now   Went to Luna park after tea with Butter &amp; Cameron
