<p>August,1918<br/>Friday 23</p>
<p>1&frac12;kilos from Morcourt. Bty. Pos.<br/>The stunt opened up at 4.20 pm. And we were supposed to move forward, but did not do so until early in the evening when we took up a position in the right rear of Proyart.<br/>The weather was very good, nice and warm with a fair breeze.<br/>Up till 5 pm we had captured our objectives and 1900 prisoners.<br/>Laid a line to our new possie from Bdge. Slept under the stars last night.<br/>A few bombs</p>
