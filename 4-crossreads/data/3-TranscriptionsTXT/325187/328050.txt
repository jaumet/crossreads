 [Friday 3 November 1916] 
  On board A25 Troopship a Union Castle Liner taken originally from the Germans and named Huntsend     raining &amp; cold   Have written Dolly &amp; telegraphed to Laura &amp; her The former is well &amp; plucky    Very few Australians on board only about  5  Officers, &amp; a few sick dismissed men for Australia Plymouth &amp; Devonport are all about the same rather a pretty spot, if it were not so cold &amp; wet- Goodbye old England I wonder when I'll see you again. We are to be vaccinated again against cholera I weigh 13-7lbs.
