
69 Gallipoli
26/11/15
I have been back here a fortnight now and in that time things are pretty quiet. We are holding Durants post and Enemy trenches are up 75 yards from ours  So not many get hit  the shells will come and explode on the trench  But then they Do not hit many. This last few Days a sniper has Been giving Some trouble  one has Killed two [men?] one the day before yesterday and another today
