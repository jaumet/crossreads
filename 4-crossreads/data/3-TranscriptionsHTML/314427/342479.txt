<p>Kesh. I had to buy a ticket on my way from Enniskillen to Bundorn junction, when I changed to the Kesh line arriving at Kesh about 8 and walked down the line to my Uncles house.</p>
<p>Sunday March2.1919  (112)<br/>12 day of leave.<br/>It is a showery and cold day and not very inviting to go out of doors. A young man named Bob Brandon was here this morning. He is a descended of George Brandon who was an employe of my Grandfather Gilmore.<br/>I went for a short walk this evening to the top of a high ridge from which a good view of Loch Ern could be obtained.</p>
<p>Monday March 3. 1919  (113)<br/>13th day of leave.<br/>I was up very early this morning and caught the 7.30 train.  My uncle accompanied me to the station to see me off &amp; wished me a safe journey to Australia.
