 April 1915 Tuesday 20 Physical drill. Fine day. Orders to go ashore in haversack and water bottle. Cool wind blowing 
 Wednesday 21 Very  cold weather . Nothing exciting to recite We are still lying idly here with many transports &amp; cruisers destroyers mine sweepers &amp; ships of every description. I managed to change the rest of my Egyptian money to one of the firemen aboard
