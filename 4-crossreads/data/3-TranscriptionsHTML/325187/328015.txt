<p>[Friday 29 September 1916]</p>
<p>Went to Lunch at Brother of Mrs Ferrars a lovely place on Belfast lock then afternoon tea in Belfast &amp; on to a Sir- Brett an <strike>cousin</strike> uncle Lawyer &amp; home to a Champagne Dinner.<br/>Mr Ferrar is alive &amp; well &amp; 80 years of age.<br/>Recd. Australian Mail from Laura. up to July 27<br/>2 from Mother 1 from Day 1 from Joe but NO parcel or fountain pen these may come from Egypt.<br/>Letter from Kathleen &amp; May &amp; many others.  Glad all is well.  11.15 on Monday will see me a married man-<br/>Love to you all.</p>
