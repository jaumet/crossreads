
June, 1915Sunday 27Went to Church this morning in Y.M.C.A. recd covered Armour No Galvanized iron is used I am glad to say in Egypt - No rain means flat roofs & a handy place to keep the wood  & the fowls etc:-I hear our 5th Reinforcements are here so something may be doing shortly to give us a more I hear that Typhoid & measles are prevalent at Gallipoli & hope it is not true for our mens sakeI keep fit by exercise, & a bucket of water Bath morning & evening - flies not so bad now.
