 four &amp; half hours journey from Boulogne was my father &amp; home. For over 3 1/2 years I had not seen them &amp; to think so near yet so far. Scotty was in the same frame of mind. We saw the channel for a bit &amp; then lost it only to pick it up again near Callais. Here we saw plenty of Sommies who seemed to be quite contented with their lives &amp; duties. Several Troop trains passed us here &amp; we cheered. We also saw several French troop trains on our way up &amp; we cheered them, at times we cheered almost anything, more so if we saw a crowd of girls.
