A little while ago, before our last push, there were some great old arguments carried on in our sig troop. I suppose it was another form of the mid summer madness. One very fierce argument was on Darwin&#39;s theories on Origin of Species. I sat back and listened and at the finish I asked each one if he had read Darwin&#39;s book and not one of them had! Of course every one believes in Darwin theory! Yes! that is right in our world; but when you meet a big mass of men drawn from all classes of society it surprizes you the number fo men who dont believe in it.
