  
 Novembre 4 Left "Martins Camp" at about 8 a.m. & marched to wharf but did not get away on the first boat. Left about 4 p.m. & reached England about 6 p.m. Didn't finish at Horseferry Rd till about 10 p.m. 
 Novembre 5 Poking about London most of the day doing nothing in particular. Went down to Stratford where I am staying with Mr & Mrs Smith or rather I am staying at Leytonstone near Stratford.
