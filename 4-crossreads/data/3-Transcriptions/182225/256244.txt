
<p>APRIL &nbsp; TUESDAY 4 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Who pants &amp; struggles to be free,</p>
<p>Who strives for others liberty,</p>
<p>Who, failing, still works patiently,</p>
<p>He truly prays.</p>
<p>Who, loving all, dare none dispise,</p>
<p>But with the worst can sympathise,</p>
<p>Who for a truth a myrtyr dies,</p>
<p>He truly prays.</p>
<p>. . . . . . . . . . . . . .</p>
<p>Petitions are but empty air</p>
<p>Brave action is the only prayer.</p>
<p>Thus learn to pray.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Annie Besant.</p>
