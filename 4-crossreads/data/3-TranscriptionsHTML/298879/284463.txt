<p>November 1915<br/>Saturday 6<br/>A strong N. Westerly off the sea. Turks have been very restless firing great quantities of <u>Ammo</u> at night time. They also opened up with some howitzers during the night. Our guns replied.</p>
<p>Sunday 7<br/>Beautiful day again. The sea is calm and beautiful. Fairly quiet day.</p>
