The Train arrived at Victoria Station 30 minutes late &amp; I soon made my way to a Bus going to Waterloo Station I sat on the Top so I could get a good view of the Sights enroute the Bus wound around &amp; around through the busy Streets &amp; at last I arrived at Waterloo Station. I walked across the road &amp; into the Union Jack Club where I intended Staying, during my rest of my leave.
