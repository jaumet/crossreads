<p>Three Deep we told Bill to try and regain the Support trench&nbsp; all right he replied and with that he jumped up and no sooner had he than he cried out oh my God I am Badly wounded &amp; he was Shot through the groin and the Bullet Struck a pouch of his Bandolier and set 5 cartridges off. On wensday april the 28<sup>th</sup> we started to Dig our trenches Deeper and connect Them this was awful work as I was in a little Bit of [hole?] with two other [men?] on top of me nearly all night and my legs were cramped up</p>
