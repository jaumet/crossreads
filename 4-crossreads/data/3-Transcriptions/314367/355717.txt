<p>What makes Rabaul bad &ndash; is &ndash; that it is the bottom of a crater &ndash; we are at the bottom of a huge funnel &ndash; It's probably the only crater harbor &ndash; in this part &ndash; anyway &ndash; The weather is fine but hot &amp; the air full of moisture &ndash; Our fans act or we could not stay in our rooms &ndash; Fitz is particular about officers wearing their coats &amp; socks &ndash; This I think rot &amp; as soon as F is well out of sight off comes <u>my</u> coat &ndash; Fitz has a little book with orders in it &amp; he sends this round by a quarter master &amp; calls attention to his ruling &ndash; So far I've not been honored &ndash; One of my socks has 12 holes in it I know &ndash; Holes don't count &ndash; Socks do &ndash;<br/>Its 11 am &ndash; the hour of Defaulters parade &ndash; The Corporal looks sadder than ever &ndash; I hear that the assizes are over &amp; everybody honorably acquitted &ndash; hence &ndash; I say &ndash; the C's sadness &ndash; He's like Dennis in Barnaby Rudge &ndash; While I think of it I want to put on record a physical feat &ndash; a diving feat &ndash; performed by a native</p>
