 Beassart 10/7/17 Todays paper states that a big air raid over London by german aeroplanes has taken place. The number of enemy planes was greater than any previous raid but the casualties were fewer. 11 of the enemy planes were brought down by our R.N.A.S. Many aerial fight occurred which were witnessed by thousands of Londoners. The weather lately has been a trifle wintry. Heavy storms followed by rather cool winds occurring almost every other day. 
 Beassart 12/7/17 At 8 A.M. this morning the whole of the 14th Inf. Bde marched into Henencourt (in drill order) and on to Henencourt Wood.
