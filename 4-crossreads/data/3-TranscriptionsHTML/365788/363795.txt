<p>[YMCA Letterhead]<br/>No 2 Gen Hospital<br/>Cairo<br/>7 Sept 15<br/>My darling Sister,<br/>I leave with 9 others this morning for destination &amp; duty unknown, but we are told on good authority we are going to the Dardanelles as Stretcher bearers. I am afraid my chance of a Commission ends here. I got the &pound; 5 from the Anglo Egyptian Bank &amp; thank you very much I am very grateful. I was given one stripe the other day but as 1 stripe carries 7/6 extra pay &amp; one of the powers that he told me that it was only in order to have plenty of orderly Corporals  acting &amp; several others are</p>
