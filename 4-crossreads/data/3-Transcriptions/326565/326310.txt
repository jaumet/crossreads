<p>of canal.  P &amp; O, Australian mail boat Khiva passed down canal bound for home, fine crowd on board.  Lecture by Capt Donald, dealing with correct methods of writing a military letter &amp; other points dealing with military discipline.</p>

Sgt Matheson &amp; Pte Wickers transferred to No 1 Casualty Clearing Hospital, ill.</p>
