<p>We've had very hot weather here lately &amp; the shower baths have been very well patronised There is a a big brick well built or sunk into the sand to receive the waste water from the showers &amp; washing taps. This is about 5 ft deep &amp; about 7 ft square. It often overflows &amp; makes a small pond. The other night I was having a shower &amp; I heard a loud splash &amp; a shout. Looking over the mat-work partition I saw ripples &amp; bubbles on the water, &amp; presently a head in hat, with drooping &amp; dripping plume, rose above the surface &amp; with a couple of bold strokes the chap struck out for shore. It was one of reinforcements not long from Aus. &amp; taking the pool for a patch of dry</p>
