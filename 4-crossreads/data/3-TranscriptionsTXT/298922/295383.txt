 ZEITOUN Cavalry School on Wesminster Dragoon Horses great collar  maketed  marks &frac12; draft animal, very suitable for Gunners or Poison Cart but awful brutes to ride. Only  3  2 paces are known WALK TROT anything over Trot is called galloping!!  However these are side lines &amp; a man can learn a lot.
