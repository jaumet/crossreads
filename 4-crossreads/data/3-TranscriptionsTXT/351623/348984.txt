 29th May 16 Heaved anchor in morning watch &amp; came alongside pier &amp; filled up with coal. 
 30th May 16 Left Jamaica in morning watch proceeding to Grand Cayman Island. 
 31th May 16 &nbsp;Arrived at G. Cayman Island in forenoon watch. No leaf given. The people seem whiter here than most of the other Islands. 
 1 June 16 Anchored at G. Cayman. Left at 9 am in First watch 
 2 June 16 At sea proceeding to Belize. Capital of British Honduras.
