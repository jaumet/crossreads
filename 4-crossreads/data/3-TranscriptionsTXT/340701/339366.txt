19-4-16 WednesdayA fair crowd left her today for an unknown destination.Received a letter from MacAll the men excepting the gaurd were paid today.  All the returned men here can be picked out they are generally hard cases.  I am on quarter gaurd today again and at present very hungry I seem unable to get enough
20-4-16This morning for the first time I seen a man overhauling his shirt for live stock.
