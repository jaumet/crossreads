<p>Sun 10th<br/>On duty all day in the orderly room, a soft snap had to check seven messages for the day, the rest of the day I put in writing.  Stan received a pass out all the afternoon &amp; Nelson went out from 4.30 PM.   after tea I went to Recreation tent for Church Service.</p>
