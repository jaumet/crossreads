<p>Sing and wake now<br/>Thy sweet lute's soft numbers<br/>Lull me to rest<br/>Charm my sorrows away<br/>Oh charm &ndash; sorrows away<br/>O sing and charm &ndash; sorrows away<br/>Lull me to rest<br/>Give me your smile'<br/>The lovelight in your eyes<br/>Life could not hold a fairer paradise<br/>Give me the right to love you all the while<br/>My world for ever,<br/>The sunshine of your smile.</p>
