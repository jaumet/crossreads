<p>11 May, Sunday</p>
<p>F.<br/>We sit about all day &amp; look at Books all afternoon. I play Hymns all morning &amp; Auntie sees Septers tail go up. M &amp; I have to rush to stop her talking because the V. has come in quietly.</p>
<p>12 May, Monday</p>
<p>F.<br/>We all walk around lane by rush cutter's place &amp; round one of the hedge again. No good night &amp; I sleep ever sowell &amp; wake up very late, &amp; then, addition to the family &ndash; Septer's foal.</p>
<p>Auntie, [indecipherable]</p>
