<p>[On letterhead of the Military Forces of the Commonwealth District Head Quarters]<br/>[Transcriber's note:  Major Ivan Brunker Sherbon died in France on 14th November 1916]<br/>Sydney 12th Dec. 1916<br/>My dear comrade in sorrow,</p>
<p>Please accept heartfelt thanks for your kind letter of sympathy on the loss of my dear son.</p>
<p>It has been a severe shock to my family and myself.  Soon, however, we hope to conquer the bitterness of our grief by the pride we feel in our boy's splendid life and his gallant death.  He fell while leading a charge on the 14th November, presumably in the attack on Bapaume, a glorious ending to a career full of promise.  The</p>
