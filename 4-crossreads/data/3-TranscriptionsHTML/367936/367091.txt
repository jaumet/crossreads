<p>Tuesday 26 December 1916<br/>Bit of telephone work in morning.<br/>Inspected by G.O.C. Desert Colum in afternoon, very funny turn out;  our general got nervous, gave wrong orders.  Heavy storm.</p>
<p>Wednesday 27 December 1916<br/>Nothing much doing in morning.<br/>Harry Jacob, Cpl. Leaske &amp; I on out post at night.<br/>Rained a good bit which short circuted telephone.</p>
