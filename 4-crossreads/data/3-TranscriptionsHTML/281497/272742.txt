<p>would care much about it for it must be deadly quiet &amp; slow we walked home along the railway line 2 more miles. This evening I saw a small French airship. She looked very nice &amp; graceful as she flew slowly along the sea, they patrol the Channel &amp; Sea on the look out for submarines &amp; Zeppelins. Saw Brummie Hall &amp; Dick McKenzie tonight, they are here in the Convalescent Camp.</p>
<p>24th.   To day is the anniversary of my second year in the Army. I never thought it would last this long when I enlisted, but you can never tell how long</p>
