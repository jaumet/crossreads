19 Oct 16Lit up all boilers & all ships of fleet heaved anchor. We done 6 inch firing. Fleet all anchored again after few hours.We got under weigh again in first watch & in company with cruiser Weymouth & some destroyers went to sea.
20 Oct 16Cruising with destroyers & Cruiser Weymouth in North Sea. In sight of coast of Norway.
21 Oct 16Arrived at Rosyth, Scotland. Passed under Firth of Forth bridge
