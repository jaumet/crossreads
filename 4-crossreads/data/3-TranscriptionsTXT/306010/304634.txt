Sund 16" July (8 p.m):- Friday 14" inst was a public Holiday throughout France &ndash; it is so annually in commemoration of the taking of the Bastille. At Paris there was a combined route march of French British, Belgian &amp; Russian Troops following which President Poincare precented diplomas to the families of those who have fallen for their country.
