
15 April 1918 MondayTurks attacked Bridge head over Jordan- we buried 300-   I was not there but safe in a Monastery-Big mail arrived.Mother & Day 9/2/18   16/2/18   27/2/18Joe 9/2/18   18/2/18   3/2/18Mrs Cotton, May 25/2/18 Ferrar Laura & am relieved to think Joe cannot come-   Still no word of my leave but I'm getting rid by degrees of different details, & we hope to get relieved soon from front line-   Am fit & well & post this with love to all-   We all hope the big Push in France may be beginning of end-  Would like to read my printed notes please
