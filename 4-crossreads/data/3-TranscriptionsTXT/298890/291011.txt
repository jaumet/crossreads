 11. Thursday. Rained heavily nearly all day. Typed "On a Street" [?]. Fred P. rang up at 6 &amp; came out to supper with me &amp; stayed till nearly 12. Had a fire &ndash; such a luxury. xxxxx 
 12. Friday. Heavy showers again. Typed again. Depressed &amp; miserable. 
 13. Saturday. Fine in morning but heavy showers again.  Miss Ince took me to a dancing exhibition at Albert Hall in afternoon. Typed again &ndash; making my shoulder very tired.
