<p>10th  In the morning we left at 8 a.m. for Ypres which is in <br/>Belgium about 10 kilos from Poperinghe.<br/>The aeroplanes are far bigger and faster than any I have <br/>previously seen, the main feature being the new British Triplane.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    <br/>Ypres is simply a mass of devasted Brick-work, ruins, ruins as <br/>far as the eye can see.</p>
