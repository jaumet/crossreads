<p>Judge took it very calmly in court but it must have upset him terribly all the same &ndash;</p>
<p>Mr Holman arrived in London about a week ago: he is sure to be working at full pressure; what a wonderful worker &amp; organizer he is. The nationalist Victories in Australia were splendid &amp; bucked us all up.</p>
<p>I hope this finds yourself, Mrs Ferguson &amp; Dorothy well.<br/>My very kindest remembrances to you</p>
