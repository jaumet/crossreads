<p>but still a little of that goes a long way so a lot of the Boys counted them out, that is they count, 1. 2. 3. 4. 5. 6. 7. 8. 9. &amp; then Out which means (stop it). Gradually they all settled down &amp; went to sleep, during the Night I <strike>was</strike> awoke up by one of the Boys shouting in his sleep as if he was having a bad time or a Sub-Marine had got him, he must have had a bad Night Mare anyhow some sang out to him to drop it &amp; again all was silent.</p>
<p>6/2/17. Tuesday<br/>All hands up again at Bugle Call &amp; found the Weather Cloudy Windy. Sausages was served up for Breakfast &amp; relished by all. Sports started at 10 am &amp; was <strike>h</strike> held on the Bridge Deck. I was unable to see them as it was</p>
