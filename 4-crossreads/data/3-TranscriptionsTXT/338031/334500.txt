The writing tables were provided with notepaper & envelopes timetables &c. Away from the writing table & the hard backed chairs a person could slip into the welcome arms of the stuffed lounge chairs & if he felt in a smoking humor he need not grope for matches, for on a little table at his elbow there was an ample supply of wooden matches free.
