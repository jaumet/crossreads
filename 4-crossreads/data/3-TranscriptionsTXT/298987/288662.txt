Sunday July 1stChurch parade in Morning &amp; Song Service afternoon &amp; evening. What an easy time Chaplins have About &frac12; an hours service on Sunday &amp; nothing every other day. YM gave Monday out lollies at tea time
Monday 2The finest day since we left home &amp; the sea is a most beautifull shade of blue different to any thing I have ever seen. Washed my clothes
Thursday 3A wireless that a raider is out no lights tonight as a precaution We are not takeing this seriously as it is probably only to get us used to things when we reach the danger zoneA Court Martial today. 96 days for disobaying  an order
