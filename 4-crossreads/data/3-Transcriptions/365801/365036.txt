<p>[Printed letterhead &ndash; Military Forces of the Commonwealth<br/>District Head Quarters, Sydney]<br/>5th June 1916</p>
<p>Dear Mr Justice Ferguson,<br/>Your letter came duly to hand.  Mrs Blanchard's letter was posted on, immediately to "Ericville"  Oakville Road, Willoughby<br/>which is her new address.  I hear on pretty good authority that the 3rd  Division has been 'pulled up' by wireless and sent via the Cape.  The Field Artillery are already at Durban and all are, I understand, to proceed to Liverpool, England, for training, replacing a Division of Englishmen ready for transfer to France.  So I think the boys will have a more comfortable time than if they had gone to Egypt at this season.  I heard from the "Cyclists"</p>
