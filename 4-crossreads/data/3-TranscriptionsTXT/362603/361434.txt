
8.  She must know my Taste cos ginger & sultanas are just lovely to me.  I have only been to Richmond on the Thames.  I have yet to visit Windsor.Bess is having a pretty rotten time with Her poor Dad.  I do not think that the Poor old chap will last much longerWell Mum, I am pretty tired & I have to write to George yet so I will wind up for this time  We are having glorious weather now & all my leave the weather was glorious.  I am in good health & so is Ellis & Dave.  Ellis has gone to Salisbury for the afternoon.  Heaps of Love to You all & Regards to Jess Sheldon & the Neighbours.  X  Harry.
