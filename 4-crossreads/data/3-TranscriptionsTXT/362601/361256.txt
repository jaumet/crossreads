 24 August, Sunday 
 O go out to Cock's for dinner &amp; tea. We go looking for land at North Harbour &amp; rate several new houses. Cocky that sneezes. I go on South Steyne beach for first time music &amp; chatter all the time. 
 25 August, Monday 
 Painter at shop &amp; is a nuisance. Meet Les Merrett &amp; go Home.
