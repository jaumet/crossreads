
Monday 29" May 1916:-Yesterday afternoon, I went for a run in the tram out to the old Provincial town of Aix - 29 km (18 miles). The journey took 2 hours & was very interesting - through fertile undulating country with everything so green & fresh and the fields covered with wild poppies & marguerites. Had a look at the Cathedral, public dispensary & museum - all of which arefearfully ancient. Arrived back at Marseille at 8 p.m. and then went to the Reiric[?] "Zone Novo" at the Casino de la plague - getting back to camp about midnight.Thursday 1" JuneOur camp is only about 1/2 mile from seafront and yesterday morning I commenced a series of early morning swims,
