<p>May, 1917. Wednesday 23<br/>Fine day. Spent intervals in making things more comfortable. Up at 7 and drill order at 8.30. Went for route march to Lavieville &amp; returned at 10.30. Drill order again at 2 &amp; did squad drill. Some drunks &amp; rather amusing. Some reinforcements in. Slept out again.</p>
