  
 Mar.22nd During the day I visit, & enjoy the kindness & hospitality of (1) Miss Green (2) Dr. & Mrs. Bunting - (3) Dr & Mrs Bunting - (3) Dr. & Mrs. Holdsworth  & (4) Mr. & Miss Barnsley - All are friends of Mrs. Scales - hence my good fortune in meeting them - Cordial invitation for future holidays from Mr.Bunting (Guy. John Elizabeth & Moseley) - Dr. & Mrs Holdsworth take me for a great run in their motor-car - Tomb & statue of James Watt & Matthew Boulston - (Miss Margaret) Birmingham becomes much dearer.
