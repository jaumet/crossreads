  
 September 1918. Monday 23 
 Ahead of La Vigure. Bty Position Weather wet, but getting a little better. Our twin on Bdge O.P. found the crossing to "Line" through barrages, our line became a casualty in many places. Georgie Baker knocked in temple, just a Blighty only. Have been refused the promised leave to Aussie don't feel very happy about the matter. Evidently the bosses consider that sole support is not a good enough reason.
