<p>April, 1915<br/>Saturday 10<br/>Along the Nile, it is all Agriculture just one long strip of fertile valley about 8 miles wide &amp; the same implements are used as in 3000 years ago &ndash;<br/>Luxor Hotel lives on the Tourists in fact, Agriculture &amp; Tourists are the sole product or money making part of Egypt.<br/>Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Donkeys has done a lot for these beasts of Burden. I rode one 10 miles &amp; he carried me well. Shelters and drinking troughs have been erected by rich American Heiresses etc.</p>
