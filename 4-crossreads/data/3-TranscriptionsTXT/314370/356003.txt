 March, 1916 Tuesday 28 Got up fresh as daiseys, had a wash &amp; breakfast then went up to see dad. After dinner went out w/- an old Pal from Liverpool Harry Grove sent &pound;10 home &amp; had a real good time turned in 11.30 pm. 
 Wednesday 29 Very quiet all day reading &amp; poking about Very hot &amp; sultry after sundown cools off Play cards w/- dad &amp; the boys Write letter &amp; up my Diary Turn in at 11 pm.
