  
 Dec 13th Spent most of the remaining part of rest in arranging concert.  Stage managed the whole & produced burlesque after writing same, leaving the musical directing to Tom Smith. Gave three shows - Thursday last to 12th Batt - Sat to 3rd - & Monday to civilians.  Voted success on last occasion, but the 3rd show marred by undeserved allusions made by Burnett to Q.M.S & cooks.
