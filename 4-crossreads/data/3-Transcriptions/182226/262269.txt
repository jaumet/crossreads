<p>Saturday 2 September, 1915</p>
<p>Still off duty, for talk of a Turkish attack. 18<sup>th</sup> Batt came up as reserves, nothing eventuated.</p>
<p>Have heard that Capt Fitzpatrick is not dead but is suffering from nervous breakdown. Bob Dryden is safe, have been having a yarn to him, he has had a pretty exciting time.</p>
