 OCTOBER &nbsp; THURSDAY 5 &nbsp; 1916 
 Fine but came on to rain again in evening. Out with the signallers all day; and Capt Campbell gave the headquarters a parade at night &amp; a bit of practice&nbsp; at night . Poss, Bill Hunter &amp; I went up to the pictures at night &amp; they were pretty rotten.
