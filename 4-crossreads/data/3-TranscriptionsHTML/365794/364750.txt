<p>Wednesday 12 December 1917<br/>Off duty 2-5, went town &amp; met S. Cuthbert, <strike>shopped</strike> fitted at Cicurel's, met Cobs Groppi's but no time for tea.  Went Anglo Egyptian Bank &amp; cashed ch. for &pound;5-0-0, i.e., Pt. 500.<br/>On duty with S. Wormald 5-8.  We hear of fall of Jerusalem on 8th inst. and many prisoners taken, but we retreat in France &amp; Roumania concludes an armistace so one ally has left us.</p>
