 Tuesday 20th June 
 After having thought over the Light Horse job I don't feel like accepting it, involving as it does an indefinite stay in this murderous climate. We are now confined to camp.  That is to say, if we wish to leave it we must report to an N.C.O.  As I am writing this in the New Zealand canteen, I have accomplished this, it follows.  The reason is supposed to be that we are under orders to be prepared to leave at short notice.
