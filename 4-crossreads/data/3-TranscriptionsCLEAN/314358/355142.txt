
3March 9th
The country out yonder is converted into a series of black and white drawingsSo intense is the white and so vast its broad masses that no color is left in any unsnowed object.No color except black.Nature can only oppose her simple wealth of white with that other extreme pole, black.All the jewellery of color between those absorbing extremes has been banishedAll objects with vertical or hoodied parts, and those with warmth enough to smelt the silent flakes, are as it were, drawn in inky blackness.For all the world it looks as though a master hand to give variet to a great white waste, had
