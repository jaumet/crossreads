<p>11) &nbsp; Dead mans ridge</p>
<p>four hours and then&nbsp;I was transferred to the Glouster Castle were I agreed to go Back on Shore again&nbsp; I was put on another Ship This time the Serang Bee wich to us from from Egypt to the Dardanelles Early in april. from this ship I went Back to Shore on Saturday may 8<sup>th</sup>&nbsp; But I did not want any more like Sunday may 3<sup>rd</sup> it was just Hell Pure and Simple with the gates open wide at that</p>
