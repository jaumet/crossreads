<p>8 August 1918 Thursday  Cairo<br/>The "Package" is doing well I go out each morning &amp; afternoon &amp; do small commissions between whiles.  It is cooler down here than last month.  I told you am keeping Flat till Oct <strike>15th </strike> 1st.   An Australian Mail is in but will be out at the Camp.<br/>Had dinner at the "Continental" with a Major man I know as I leave the Hospital at 6 at night.<br/>Enclose 2 bad snaps- Shots of Dolly &amp; the Bairn &amp; the Nurse taken inside to please the Mother, good ones will be sent later.</p>
