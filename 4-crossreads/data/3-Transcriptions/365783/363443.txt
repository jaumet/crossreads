<p>No. 2. General Hospital<br/>2nd Australian Imperial Expeditionary Force.<br/>29th May 1915</p>
<p>Dearest Mrs,<br/>Col. Martin &amp; I will accept your kind invitation for dinner in grill room at 7 to-night.   We will afterwards take you &amp; Dr Bennett out to see the Spinx by Spoon-light.   Hope you had a good night.   Please give bearer a note to say how Tingsey &amp; Peter are</p>
<p>Yr. loving husband</p>
