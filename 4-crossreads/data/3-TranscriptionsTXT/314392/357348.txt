Fritz has left quite a lot of useful things in our hands, such as coal, metal ammunition etc.Fritz what I can learn he is still retiring.During the night it blew a hurricane  & rained like hell. I was indeed glad when I saw the lights of the Camp show up in the distance.28th Still blowing some. Fritz has retired a few more miles. Hardly a shot is heard now21st Moving day.
