<p>To my dearest sister,<br/>"Rouen is a large city on the 'Seine' full of hospitals being handy to the coast. I spent 9 days there in 1917 - it is famous, for Jeanne 'de Arc' came from there - there is a large statue of her there - she was burnt there.<br/>Ken</p>
