
There was a bombardment however, & a pretty heavy one too I heard from Caesar Hood of the transport (motor) who, by the bye, was once the hero in the Cormont burlesque.The first thing I knew of the results of the raid, was when I rose at about 5 Am when there were many cars coming & going, bringing cases from the line, & taking cases on to C.C.S. The lorries batch, attached to us, did good work in the latter sphere with walkers
