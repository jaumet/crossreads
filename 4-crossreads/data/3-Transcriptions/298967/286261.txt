<p>DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS</p>
<p>10 &ndash; Bapaume &ndash; Moved to camp near Bapaume.</p>
<p>19 &ndash; Battalion sports.</p>
<p>22 &ndash; 09.00 &ndash; Bazentine &ndash; Left Bapaume marched to Bazentine.</p>
<p>23 &ndash; 09.00 &ndash; Ribemont &ndash; Left Bazentine marched to Ribemont.</p>
<p>May 28 &ndash; Ribemont &ndash; Battalion Championship in Brigade.  10th 17 points first, 12th eleven pnts. second.  Casualties for operation at Bullecourt 7 officers 174 other ranks.</p>
<p>June 1/6 &ndash; Ribemont &ndash; Training and company competitions results D.C.A.B.  Batt. comp. result <strike>10th (pnt)</strike> 9th 1st, 10th 2nd.</p>
<p>12 &ndash; Henencourt &ndash; Marched Henencourt bivouacked.</p>
<p>13 &ndash; Henencourt &ndash; Divisional sports ("OCHRE" 1st).</p>
<p>14 &ndash; Ribemont &ndash; Return to Ribemont.</p>
<p>20 &ndash; Ribemont &ndash; Final of Div. coy comp. "D" Coy. second in Div.</p>
<p>23 &ndash; Mailly Maillet &ndash; Col. Jacobs to O.T.B. (England).  Mjr. Nelligan in command.</p>
<p>24 &ndash; Mailly Maillet &ndash; Marched to Mailly Maillet.</p>
<p>24/31 &ndash; Mailly Maillet &ndash; Training.</p>
<p>July 1/5 &ndash; Mailly Maillet &ndash; Training.</p>
<p>6 &ndash; Ribemont &ndash; Marched to Ribemont.  Mjr. Giles returned.</p>
<p>8 &ndash; Ribemont &ndash; Pozieres memorial unveiled.</p>
<p>12 &ndash; Ribemont &ndash; Inspection by H.M. the King.</p>
<p>13 &ndash; Bray &ndash; Marched to Bray.</p>
<p>15 &ndash; Bray &ndash; About this time Col. Neligan assumed command.</p>
<p>26 &ndash; Albert &ndash; Marched to <strike>Abl</strike> Abert to entrain.</p>
