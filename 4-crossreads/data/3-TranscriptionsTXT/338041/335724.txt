 Aug 5th Good news of previous nights attack all along the line. Continual stream of patients pass through our hands. 
 Aug 6th Two more of our boys (making 4 in all) killed &ndash; Tragic but courageous end of Harry Smith &ndash; He, together with Pte Griffiths are recommended for D.C.M's Success of 2nd Division. 
 Aug 7th. Return of our bearers from Albert &ndash; The boys have done excellent work. Losses killed 4 wounded 8 Praiseworthy speech by our Colonel &amp; General Legge. 
 Aug 8th Heavy losses in Fourth Division &ndash; 1st Div reorganised &ndash; Patients galore 
 Aug 9th. Pay-day &ndash; Departure of 20 men from our section for Puechvillers (?) Arrival of Reinforcements to supplement our Amb.
