<p>18/4/17<br/>Section pegging out position for Defences round Bievilliers.  Very cold windy wet weather.</p>
<p>19th<br/>Sec. standing by today.  Expect to leave early in morning for unknown destination.  Reported French have captured 19,000 prisoners and 90 guns.</p>
<p>20th<br/>No. 2 Section marched out of camp &amp; proceeded to "Bazentin" followed later by the whole company and camped for the night.  Nice fine weather.</p>
<p>21st<br/>Marched to Becourt &amp; took up camp there.  Expect to leave here soon.</p>
<p>22nd<br/>Nice fine day.  Went into Albert with Sgts. Banks &amp; Barnes.  A few civilians coming back.  Saw Reg. Monaghan.  Still good news coming in from all allied Fronts.</p>
<p>23rd<br/>A little training &amp; camp Fatigue.</p>
<p>24th<br/>Nothing much doing.  Training &amp; Fatigues.</p>
<p>25th<br/>Carrying on as yesterday.</p>
<p>26th<br/>Nothing fresh doing.</p>
<p>27th<br/>Just a little Coy. Fatigues &amp; Drilling.</p>
<p>28th<br/>Went to Albert &amp; saw a Picture Show, wasn't too bad.  Nothing fresh going to report.</p>
<p>29th<br/>Still out resting.  Nothing fresh of importance to note.</p>
<p>30th<br/>Doing a little fatigues &amp; Drill.</p>
<p>1-5-17<br/>Same routine as yesterday.</p>
<p>2nd<br/>Nothing fresh going.  Having beautiful weather.</p>
<p>3rd<br/>Still having lovely weather.  Sections doing Pontooning &amp; fatigues.</p>
