<p>June 10th<br/>German guns silent. We witness eight of our planes chasing 2 Taubes one of which was brought down. Truth of Naval Engagement.<br/>June 11th<br/>Whitsun day. Verdan fiercer than ever. Burial of a 20th lad. Miserable weather (N.D.)<br/>June 12th<br/>Enemy guns very silent in our quarters. Ypres battle going strong. Artillery heard by us. Our party returns from Bois Grenier to Ft. Rompu. Quick march under wretched conditions.</p>
