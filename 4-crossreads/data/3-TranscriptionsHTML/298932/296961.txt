<p>STEENVOORDE<br/>Friday 19<br/>I am lousey these billets are alive with vermin, just had a chat, caught fifty five<br/>Saturday 20<br/>Good things come to those who wait a good job offered me on the Divisional staff but the O.C (Mj.H) wouldent hear of it<br/>Sunday 21<br/>Heavy Artillery fire on the front evidently a barage being sent over prior to a big attack, it makes me sigh<br/>A bright blue Sunday "Ah" I wish it would end for I am sick homesick. AUSTRALIA</p>
