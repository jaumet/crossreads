
<p>and came right up to Williamstown to get Coal &amp; water. It is very shallow here and the boat stirred up the mud like a volcano. We Cant see Melbourne from where we are but just the suburbs. A big paddlewheel Pleasure Steamer passed us crowded with people Coaled up about 5pm The Red Cross supplied us with the best meal while on the boat we had 3 apples &amp; a pound of Cake to each man</p>
