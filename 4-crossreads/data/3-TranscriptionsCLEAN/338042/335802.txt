  
 I see Gladys Cooper in the "Yellow Ticket" - Return to  & sleep at War Chest. 
 Sunday. Feb. 11th Sabbath spent in long & efficient rehearsal - I procure a private billet. Bus  &  taxi drives. 
 Monday. Feb.12th. Amid great anxiety but "undaunted courage" the Anzac Coves appear for first time in London. - a small house does not promise much success but we do exceptionally well - "Sari Bair" brings only encore - Broken Bottles (?)  The evening
