 POST CARD [Postage stamp is 1d stamp for UNION OF SOUTH AFRICA] The Hon Mr Justice Ferguson Judges Chambers Supreme Court Sydney Australia 
  28.9.15  Have spent two very pleasant days at this hotel in Durban. It makes a welcome break after the weary run across from Fremantle. I am posting you a local newspaper to give you some idea of both journalism and politics in this part of the Empire. With kindest regards to all at 'Wimbledon" Yrs very sincerely  Lt. S. Whitfeld.
