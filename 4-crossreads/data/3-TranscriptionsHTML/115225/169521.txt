<p>Jan 21<sup><u>st</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;I did&#39;nt like too well the place I was staying &amp; I met a couple of fellows out of 31<sup><u>st</u></sup>&nbsp;Canadian Battalion in the same way so I went with them to Hotel D&#39;Egypte, they took a double room between them &amp; I took a single room. they were nice rooms &amp; nicely furnished in the most central position two hundred yards from the opera. We went &amp; saw the Eifle Tower &amp; went up in the great wheel in the afternoon &amp; went to the Olimpic theatre in the evening</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Jan 22<sup><u>nd</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;We got up at 9.A.M. &amp; had breakfast at our hotel. We went &amp; saw Napoleons tomb &amp; Hotel d&#39;Invalides in the morning &amp; in the afternoon to &quot;Mus&eacute;e Gravin&quot; which consists of waxwork tableaux of historical interest. cabinette fantastique. conjouring etc &amp; Palace des Mirages - We had tea at the Soldiers &amp; Sailors club &amp; stayed to the concert in the evening. it was splendid &amp; consisted of 1<sup><u>st</u></sup>&nbsp;class talent only -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Jan 23<sup><u>rd</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;We went on Y.M.C.A excursion to Versailles at 10.A.M. went by train as all lorry seats were booked up. had a very good guide to take us. the trip cost 10 francs only which included a very good meal at a hotel in Versailles &nbsp;After dinner we were taken all over the Grand Palace &amp;</p>
