<p>At about 9pm all the head men came along with Agha Petros &amp; expressed great disappointment at our small numbers.  We went into matters, such as future operations, provisions, payment of troops, who were without food etc until 2 am.  Agha Petros told us how he defeated the Turks.</p>
<p>He left Urmiah &amp; brushed aside the first Turkish defences.  Then he advanced in three columns some miles apart towards Suldaz.  On coming into contact with the Turks he established a line &amp; sent a strong mounted force to capture &amp; hold Suldaz the Turks H.Qrs.  Before dawn</p>
