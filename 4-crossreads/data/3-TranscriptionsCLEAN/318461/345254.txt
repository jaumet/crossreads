
French money has gone up. Stand now deferred Pay 24.17.0 owes me 3/1 this is correct & in hand 104 francs & a half 3.14.10 2/5
Thursday 3/5/17forgot pm yesterday am writing this on Friday.Great scare, got orders to stand by to go to the front again, at 4 P.M. but it was a false alarm the 2nd div. held what they had taken so we wern't wantedWeather keeps fine & if the wind dyed down would be hot.Pay 4/1 def 24.18.0Gee whiz food does run away with your money.
Friday 4/5/17A lovely morning same old wind with us yet.Boys very lively owing to not having to go in again div sports tomorrowPay 5/11 24.19.0.letter from Viv. wrote Jack Carol Tony & Nell.
Saturday 5/5/17cloudy but fine hot night but cool in the early morning took off underpants.wrote Viv.Pay 6/1 25.0.05. P.M. been very hot like summer in Sydney these last 2 days & only about 10 days ago were shivering round fires. Truly a remarkable climate.We had 2 firsts & 1 second at the sports. not so bad when considered that competitions were open to 4th & 5th Army.Am living very high 1 tin of Salmon 11/2 francs 1 tin of Peaches 11/2 francs for Tea tonight wont have much left for Blighty at this rate.Seems funny our having sports & a few miles away others are being blown to atomsin hand 91 francs about 3.6.9.looks like storm wind getting up & turning cold.
