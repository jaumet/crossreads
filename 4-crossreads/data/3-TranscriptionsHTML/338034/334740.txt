I think we are waiting for fresh Infantry Divisions to arrive. The Turkish fortifications around the hills near Gaza are apparently of great strength &amp; will take a great deal of winning. We certainly have great superiority in artillery here, &amp; warships lying off the coast assist in the bombardments.
