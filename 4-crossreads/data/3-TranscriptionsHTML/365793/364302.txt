<p>Jly 20 a score of houseboats lined the side of the River. Jolly fine 'boats too some of them. At first I caught several crabs, but was going good at the finish. The Park is a real beaut', &amp; old Father Thames runs smooth &amp; clear between banks of emerald green. Had a good dinner at the Hotel overlooking the water &amp; then made for home. Met Phyl' coming home from work, &amp; the three of us walked home. Tip top supper at 10.30.</p>
