<p>Sunday 23 March 1919<br/>Cold on the improve. Had brekker in bed. Have a poisoned leg now. It is some sore too. Went in &amp; did Rue de Paris picquet tho. It was icy cold &amp; rained some.</p>
<p>Monday 24 March 1919<br/>In bed all day with my leg. Read a couple of books. Very cold. Rained all day &amp;   night &amp; also snowed to some order.</p>
