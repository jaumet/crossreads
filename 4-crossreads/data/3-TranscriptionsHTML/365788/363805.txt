<p>comes I shall look him up &amp; introduce myself. There is with the No 3 Gen Hospital one Lieut. Cambage a Contemporary of mine at Kirys &amp; a dentist withal. This afternoon I go &amp; pay him a visit to get 2 teeth stopped with Amalgam They have broken away a lot, &amp; are not front teeth. Did you know that Major Scobie had been killed?
