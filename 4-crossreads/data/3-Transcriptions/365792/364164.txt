<p>Mch 29 scattered hamlets, roads, rivers etc etc passed before the eye in one lovely panarama, &amp; then the train would dive into a long tunnel &amp; all would be dark &amp; dismal. After reaching the highest point the speed swiftly increased &amp; we swept downhill &amp; round corners at a great rate, doing at times as much as 50 miles an hour.</p>
<p>Gradually the clouds gathered, old Sol disappeared, &amp; a misty rain swept over us, but it was only temporary, although the air was chilly no end &amp; we covered up &amp; snuggled into our coats. The people are invariably polite &amp; most of them are kindness personified. At Lyons they gave us fruits &amp; bread gratis, &amp; the boys threw down badges etc which were eagerly picked up.
