<p>Theatre at night.</p>
<p>June 16th &ndash; 17<br/>Last 3 days have been spent in visiting friends and having a real royal time.</p>
<p>This morning Wally Platt and I came to Maidenhead for a stay of several days</p>
<p>June 19th &ndash; 17<br/>Left Maidenhead by small pleasure steamer at 10AM and after a 4 hours run of glorious scenery we arrived at Henley well known for its regattas</p>
<p>An hour's stay here we returned arriving back at 7PM</p>
