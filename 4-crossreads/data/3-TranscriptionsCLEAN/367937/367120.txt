My dear Mother and FatherI wrote to Mother last night but could not get much in on that paper so will write to you both now.  I can only tell you all the minor details but suppose you will be interrested in them.Here is a list of some of the things we are not to put in letters.Date and place of dispatchNames and numbers of transports & escortsNumbers & descriptions of troopsDisposition of forceRoute & ports of callCriticism judged harmfullReferences to larger units & superior commanders.My word I am thankful that I am such a good sailor
