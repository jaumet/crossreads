Some of the vendors arrange their carts as stalls, while others simply spread out some gaudy coloured material on the cobbles, and display the articles for sale.  The demoiselle squatting behind the pile, ogling all &amp; sundry to purchase.  Females of all classes and dimensions carry on the various businesses while the menfolk suppress the invader.  Girls work in the fields, and in fact at anything at all.  Judging by the appearance of the middle-aged ones, I think this was customary before the war.  Mostly the young girls</p>
