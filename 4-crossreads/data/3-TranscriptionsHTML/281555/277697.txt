<p>March 5th. Sunday.<br/>Musketry Grouping morning afternoon off. Warned to shift Camp</p>
<p>March 6th. Monday<br/>Bkfast 7am. Messed clear of camp about 8am. Cleaned up lines and struck camp about 10am. N.Z.M.R. took over about same time. Our band played them in: looked well. Marched off about 11.30am. Picked up A.Coy &amp; with 30 minute marches reached road 1pm. &amp; Ferry Post 5pm. Pitched tents &amp; settled down with O.K. about 7.30.</p>
<p>7th. Tuesday.<br/>Without previous warning struck tents about 8am and prepared to move out. Camp cleaned &amp; OK ready for marching 10.15am. Marched Pontoon Bridge &amp; halted at Ordinance Stores where we changed rifles</p>
