<p>Saturday 2 December 1916<br/>Went out as signaller to patrol in morning.<br/>Wrote to Mother No. (27).<br/>Big mail in but did not get any letters, wrote to base.  Have not received letters since 7th Nov.</p>
<p>Sunday 3 December 1916<br/>Church parade in morning.  Stayed back for communion.<br/>On telephone from 10 till 2 p.m.</p>
