 15/6/18 Slept in until 10 a.m. and then got up and took a walk around. It was a very quiet day and there was not much eventful doing. A number of 59th Bn. People round reconnoitring. Yarned with our Amurkan Major and heard all about Hickey Dick's saloon bar and Bathhouse Joe's resorts in Chicago. An American medical Colonel came around after lunch and little Cadman guided them to the front line. It was remarkably quiet all the afternoon and well into the evening. Just before dark we were treated to an aerial display of no ordinary kind.
