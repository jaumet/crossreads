We were taken into the Censors room via the Operations room.  The latter was a very large room with hundreds of men &amp; instruments working.  In the Censors room presided over by a Staff Major were several men busily engaged perusing &amp; censoring cablegrams.  No difficulty was raised by the President who took the telegram and handed it direct to a censor.
