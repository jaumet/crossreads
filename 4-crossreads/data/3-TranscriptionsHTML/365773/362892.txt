<p>My dearest Sister,<br/>Since the 17th a batch of home mail came in &amp; of course I received two from you &amp; others. Am sorry your health doesn't improve, look after yourself dear.<br/>A lot of Australian mail has been lost lately.<br/>We are still spelling behind the line &amp; having plenty</p>
