 May 5th We are supposed to be moving shortly &ndash; I hope to goodness that it comes off &ndash; and mean while I am    supposed   somewhat exercised as to how I am going to carry all my stuff. We are supposed to be travelling light i.e. one is permitted to carry what one can get on one's horse  &amp;  a few things rolled up in ones blankets.
