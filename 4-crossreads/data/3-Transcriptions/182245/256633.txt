
<p>352</p>
<p>1917 APRIL</p>
<p>28 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; beautifully warm &amp; sunny</p>
<p>Cleaning harness, horses,&nbsp;</p>
<p>stables ect ready for Capt</p>
<p>Campbell to inspect. He</p>
<p>said all were good though</p>
<p>not up to inspection standard</p>
<p>Mine was very good. Had a&nbsp;</p>
<p>half holiday in afternoon &amp; I</p>
<p>lay in sun &amp; wrote letters</p>
<p>The 26<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;Bat played 107<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;Bat</p>
<p>football &amp; beat them 6 - 0.</p>
