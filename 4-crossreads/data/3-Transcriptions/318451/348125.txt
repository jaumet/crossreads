I reported to Bde. &amp; got to my batt. which is the 4th.<br/>On Friday I went out with them to sports &amp; had a good time but it was very hot.  I think I shall be very happy here.  It is a fine Regiment well disciplined &amp; very keen.</p>
<p>Saturday 2<br/>We had sports all the afternoon.</p>
<p>Sunday 3<br/>Muriel's Birthday.  We had a Brigade parade at 10 oclock.  I suppose there were about 3,000 men, the Brigadier &amp; Staff Cols. &amp; officers of all Batts. &amp; it was all carried out in splendid order.
