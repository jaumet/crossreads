<p>The troops broke into canteen &amp; took &pound;20 worth of stock &amp; rolled two barrels of beer out side. The canteen was run by Gypos who had been overcharging the troops hence the bust up.</p>
<p>18/<br/>Shifted camp, now quartered in large E.P. tents .Paid 68 pisastres.</p>
<p>20/<br/>Received orders to get ready mounted, only half the troop available others being away sick.</p>
