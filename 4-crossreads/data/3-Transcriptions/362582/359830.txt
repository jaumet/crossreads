<p>5.<br/>I have not heard from poor old Fred, suppose they are having a busy time too. I've written to him but evidently they do not get their mails very regularly. We may not leave for England for a couple of weeks yet but never can tell in the Military. I must write to some of Mr Capp's relations &amp; let them know where I am when I arrive there.</p>
