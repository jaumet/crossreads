 25 December 1916 Monday  XMAS DAY   El Arish Got back midnight with 4 Guns 1000 Rifles 100000 rounds ammunition, Telephone instruments Oil engine, Machine Gun parts. Prisoners sent away by Boat. We buried 96 Turks yesterday   Our casualties were alright. Burnt Wagons Huts &amp; every thing else burnt &amp; after smashing Pumps evacuated with Loot- Such is War including 2 little Bedouin children of about 4 &amp; 5 deserted- these we fed &amp; put with our sick &amp; wounded. Turks had plenty Tucker &amp; a good stronghold- they have a great respect for our felt hatted Bushies- I collected Papers, Photos, stamps &amp; such like stuff &amp; have sent all to Hd Qts to be interpreted- We have so little it was hard lines to have to burn Rations Horsefeed etc but can't carry much on Camels
