 Wed. 9th. May; - Left Noreuil &amp; went via Vaulx onto Bancourt. 
 Sun. 13th May: - Left Bancourt &amp; went through the heaps of bricks that mark the spots of Villers-au-Flos &amp; Flers on to Bazentin-le-Petit &amp; billeted in huts. 
 Mon. 21st. May. Left "Hot-Cross" Camp at Bazentin &amp; marched through Contalmaison Meaulte, Villers-Sous-Corbie, to Buire &amp; billeted. 
 Fri. May. 25th: - Left Bn. at Buire &amp; went to Brigade Signal School at Lavieville. 
 Sat. May. 26th Went over to Contay &amp; stayed with 19th Bn. for night. 
 Mon &amp; Tues. 4th &amp; 5th. June. I'm at Amiens on leave. Good times here &amp; its hard to leave to go back to Buire. 
 Sun. June 17th: - Still billeted at Buire &amp; had another day's leave into Amiens. Missed train home but hopped a motor lorry making for Albert to Lavieville corner. We are learning all the disc signals to tanks so must be in for a large "stunt" Certainly we will have to do something after this long spell.
