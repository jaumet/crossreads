  
 9 September 1916 Sat Slept the best part of last night. After Dinner I went into Poperinghe with Cliff Searl & Frank [indecipherable]. Motoring both ways. Eggs & chips & wanted to know if Bob was my father. 8.30 pm. B section joins us up. Hot baths change of clothes. 
 10 September 1916 Sun Slept the best part of the night. Sgt Beatties wants to know how everything is at 6.30. The Answer is all had a good night. Wingrove from the Anti-craft came to see me & Sid & I went with him to his quarters. Big pig sitting in old trenches all the afternoon.
