<p>Saturday 11 May<br/>Up as usual packed up and luggage dumped.</p>
<p>Out at 8-30 to Lahoussye with wagon to reel up two spare lines to Behrencourt &ndash; Pont Noyelles Road. Had dinner. Picked up drum cable at 2nd Div.</p>
<p>Arrived home 3 p.m. at Allonville. Evening to Pierrots with Hoppy. Really enjoyable. Salved good book of Aussie.</p>
<p>Mod off evening hay.</p>
