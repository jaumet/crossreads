
Saturday 16th Feb. 1918.Tent pitching this morning.Parade dismissed in afternoon when I prepared some notes on Omar Khayyam for the literary evening. Good meeting - about 20 out of 24 members turning up. I was elected to chair & we got to work immediately (held in barn near the stables . Forms & chairs by provided by farmhouse people).Sam Rickson read a passage from "The Sky Pilot".Pte Barlow read a passage from "Young Lord Stranleigh" by Robert Barr. H.A.T. Reynolds read told the story of "Mick Arden" illustrated by quotations
