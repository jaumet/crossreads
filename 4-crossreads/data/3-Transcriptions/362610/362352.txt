<p>Tuesday 16 May 1916<br/>Very tired during the day which was a perfect spring day, clear &amp; still. Had breakfast in bed &amp; then got up spending the day in attending to company affairs. A mail of 8 letters in from Australia, together with papers &amp; a pair of socks from Father.</p>
