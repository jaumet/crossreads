"Argoeuves" about 9 miles off and were billeted about 8am. Very poorly stocked shops.
13th. Thursday.
Town picquet all day till lights out. 10pm. Very cold at nights with greatcoat only.
14th. Friday.
Drill all morning nice Rout march afternoon, very pretty surroundings, hilly country all in crops beautifull to see the wild poppies thick among the corn. gold red green a charming scene on such slopes with little woods here and there scattered windmills. Out one road returned by different course.
