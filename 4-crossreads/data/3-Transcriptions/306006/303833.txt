It is rumoured this evening that Gaza fell into out hands this morning but everything is very vague &amp; doubtful, the Turks attacked all along the line this afternoon; At dark we left an outpost to hold the positions &amp; commenced to go back to Bir-El-Jimonil, here we watered our horses  <strike>&amp; came on another two miles to camp</strike> about midnight after a very tedious journey halting &amp; dismounting every few hundred yards; The Regt only received two casualties today both C Sqdn</p>
