<p>November 1917<br/>Sunday 25<br/>Still camped on side of hill near Saris.<br/>All very tired after yesterdays stunt.<br/>Col Todd had us road making and shifting stones. His pet mania is road making.</p>
<p>Monday 26<br/>Still camped on side of hill in Enab and Saris.<br/>Got ready to move  and when we came back from water orders were cancelled.<br/>Very cold.</p>
