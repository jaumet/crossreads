April.
4th. This afternoon, we walked to Pevensey, seeing the "Old Mint House" the ruins of the castle. "Daddy Long Legs." Beachy head in the morning 
5th. This afternoon we walked across the downs to Sevington an ordinary kind of English village Fine weather still holds is very enjoyable. Madame Kirby Leewithers this evening at Devonshire Park was very fine indeed.
