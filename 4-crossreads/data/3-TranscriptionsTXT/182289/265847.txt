 26.3.18 The day was spent in final preparations for moving out.  Stores were again carefully overhauled, guns inspected by Sections and as much stores as were not of vital importance were dumped. Men were paraded at 2.30 p.m. and a thorough inspection of kit made.  It was found that a good number of men did not realise the importance of safeguarding their Iron ration.  Revolver and rifle ammunition was made up in every case.  By 5.30 p.m. everything was complete for moving out. Marching out state was as follows, 8 Officers, 152 Other Ranks. Company moved out at 5.50 p.m. passed starting point Blaringhem Station 6 p.m., marched by easy stages to Steenbecque Station - arrived there 8.45 p.m.  Trained arrived 11 p.m.
