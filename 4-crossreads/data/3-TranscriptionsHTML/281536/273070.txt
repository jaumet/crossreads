<p>15/6/19 Sutton-Veny<br/>Spent this quiet Sunday idling and walked with Pike in the evening.</p>
<p>16/6/19<br/>Caught a train before 10 at Warminster and in an hour had reached Salisbury &ndash; not a particularly large town. In shopping found the prices much below London ones. Many old buildings here. Turning down a side street went under an old gateway and stepped into quite another world. This was the Close of the Cathedral. Green lawns, high shady trees &ndash; a large expanse of trim open ground bordered with creeper covered houses. In the centre rises the Cathedral &ndash; not enormous not over ornamented but with a fine high plain spire. When you look close the pointed elaborate stone carving becomes evident. The big front doors were wide open and the whole interior flooded with air</p>
