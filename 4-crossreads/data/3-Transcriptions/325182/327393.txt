<p>We have not had any official news of events from up there for a couple of days.</p>
<p>We were relieved from our position <strike>up</strike> last night and came back to a reserve.  Our Hqrs. now are in a <strike>house</strike> fine Chateau.  We were called out last night and all hands were ready for the Hun to come across.</p>
<p>He didn't come over though and we all returned and had a sleep.  It has been misty all day driving rain and wind and bitterly cold.  Sleep is a grand thing.  Pure unadulterated sleep is better than</p>
