<p>11th Dec. Saturday:-<br/>During the week I have had the usual routine of daily work &amp; to bed early. Also made the acquaintance of some friends at the house of Madame Alice, a French mistress, where I determine to learn &amp;  revise my French. Two Armenian girls, the Misses Bequian are here  &amp;  speak English fluently. They entertain us with some music (De Mestre &amp; I)</p>
<p>12th Sunday<br/>Today met Darce Murphy in Cairo. Has just arrived with 30th Batt. After showing him about Cairo, we visit the Citadel  &amp; mosques again &amp;  he leaves for camp about 6 pm, quite satisfied with his visit. Are to meet next Sunday, &amp; visit the Pyramids</p>
