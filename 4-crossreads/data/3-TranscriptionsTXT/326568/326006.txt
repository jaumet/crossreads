 March 1919. 22nd.  Another injection of germs, innoculation against influenza. 24th.  Very cold indeed.  Twice to Church yesterday.  We go on leave on 27th. 27th.  The much feared workmens strike has not come off &amp; we are now in London on 14 days leave at  Shelb  Lincoln Hall Hotel after much trouble. 28th.  At National Hotel, Russell Square, Westminster Cathedral &amp; National Galleries.  "As you Were" 29th.  Croydon.
