<p>We have football &amp; cricket going again, you can't beat a bit of sport to liven things up a bit</p>
<p>Len &amp; a few more of the boys have gone into Poperinge today</p>
<p>I heard this evening that the 3rd Division had been sighted at last &amp; were about Hazebrouck its time they done something for they have not struck a blow as yet, they have had a good time in England.</p>
<p>Leave for England started again the other day its about time I should think.</p>
<p>Received a big parcel of sweets from home today they were in splendid condition &amp; had kept well.</p>
