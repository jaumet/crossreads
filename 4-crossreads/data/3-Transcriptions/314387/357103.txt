<p>Tuesday 11<br/>Shooting and instruction in Lewis gun again came home and shifted from Rivery to next village in shelters.<br/>Wednesday 12th<br/>Regimental guard all day<br/>Thursday 13th<br/>Came off guard in morning paid forty francs rested all day<br/>Friday 14th<br/>Left camp two o'clock in lorries for trenches left Cowis &amp; Pont-Noyelles then marched to trenches relieved 26th A.I.F. midnight in reserve trench</p>
