  
 [Picture of Nouzha Garden, Alexandria. Annotated with:-] Tell Janie and Mss Rogers I am quite well and write often 
  Dardanelles 5 June 1915 from Gunner Pyke Am quite well and happy 7 L  Aus and N.Z. men made a brilliant job of the landing . I arrived here on 17 May. We get plenty of shrapnel on the beach. The ammunition section is dong the ammtn work on the beach (advanced base) I am very pleased to know Jim has joined Your loving son Arthur XX 
 Addressed to Mrs. James Lennie 32 Thorne St. Paddington Sydney N.S.Wales Australia 
 Passed by censor 2199
