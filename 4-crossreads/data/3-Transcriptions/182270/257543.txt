
<p>TUESDAY, AUGUST 19, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Started into Quirindi with</p>
<p>Dad &amp; Jo in little car but the</p>
<p>oil gave out at Boundary gate</p>
<p>&amp; we had to come back. Linda &amp; I</p>
<p>took our dinner out &amp; went round</p>
<p>bottom end.</p>
<p>Tickle finishing off Cow paddock</p>
<p>troughs.</p>
