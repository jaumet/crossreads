<p>After getting off the boat we did not have to go far for the train, which was waiting for us a few hundred yard's away.</p>
<p>When we marched onto the platform, we were told off to our carrage's and luck was with me as I had a first class seat all the way, and as I had the corner seat I was pretty comfortable as we had arm rests, of course every one did not get these.  We had a long wait before getting a move on, we started about 1 o'clock in the morning.</p>
<p>When we did move off I besides a lot more was fast asleep, but as soon as day light started to break I think everybody's head was out of the window, and as the train was a fairly big one there were a few Australian faces looking</p>
