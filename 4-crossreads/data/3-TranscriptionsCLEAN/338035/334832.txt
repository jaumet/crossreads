Monday 23rdAt 7 a.m. left Camp 3 to relieve "B" Section at the 9th Brigade Hosp. No. 2 Camp 19 (Artillery).I was clerk there and was kept going.
Tuesday 24thKeep Kept going all day.  Received letters & parcel of socks.
Wednesday 25thWas relieved at 10 a.m. by "A" Section and we proceeded to Camp 3 again.Cleaning harness afternoon.  Mail from home.
Thursday 26thClean up in anticipation of leave tomorrow.
Friday 27thAlthough wet & rain was about we left Camp at 9 a.m. and arrived in London about 1 p.m.  Thence round to Aldwych Hut with Corp. Macaulay and booked our beds etc. then dinner.  Walk with Mac down Strand and I had a photo taken at Chesneys Ltd. and we purchased several necessities also Christmas Cards.  Back to Aldwych for tea.Leaving Mac I went to Charing Cross Stn. but rain prevented me from walking about much.Had supper in the "Corner House" about 9 and then went for a run by tube to Upton Park & back arriving Aldwych at midnight.
Saturday 28thReferendum Polling Day in Australia.Morning.  Stroll along Embankment with Mac, Harold Lees & Ron Ponton and inspecting Cleopatra's Needle we pushed on to Parliament House.
