
Tuesday 4 December
Frosty and bitterly cold.Assisting in the cleaning of a motor ambulance during the morning.After dinner went in a car to the 5th Div. Workshops near Bailleul, calling at the 2nd Aust. Casualty Clearing Station near Steenwerck on the way.  Rode the motor bike back to camp.After tea took the usual Casualty wire to 15th Brigade Sigs.Fritz was over early this morning dropping bombs
