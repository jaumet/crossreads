<p>2 November, Sunday</p>
<p>I go to N Steyne in morning. Surf rotten. Go to Dobboe's Baths after dinner &amp; hand. Meet Bert C &amp; Lady from W.A. E &amp; E for tea. Home &amp; write at 8 Mr Tran. Meet Ted Collis &amp; wife &amp; family. Plant Dahlias &amp; do weeding.</p>
<p>Wrote M 29</p>
<p>3 November, Monday</p>
<p>Go to B Cuneos. Mrs C away. We have music &amp; chatter until bed time.</p>
<p>Sent mail &amp; P.C. of wild flowers &amp; ocean beach pamphlet.</p>
