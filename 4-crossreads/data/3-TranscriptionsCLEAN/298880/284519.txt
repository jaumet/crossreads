
July 21st Friday.Went out last night with R.S.M. Bramhall to bring in wounded. We got several poor fellows in. I got back to billet about 6.30 AM this morning.July 22ndSlept well last night. All feeling stiff sore and tired. Colonel spoke very feelingly to us all yesterday afternoon. He thanked the Bn for their splendid behaviour first trial under fireJuly 23rdWe left Bac St Maur about 10 PM and marched in single file to the town of Fleur Baix. Despite the fact that the Germans shell this town at occassionally civilians mostly women and old men and girls  still live here.
