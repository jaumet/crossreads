 Friday 25 May 1917. 
 Further&nbsp;still&nbsp;into the &quot;danger zone&quot;&nbsp; still . 
 A slight swell this morning. Marching order parade at 10 a.m. (All kit correct.). Physical training from 2 pm. till 4 pm. Games on deck later till 5 p.m. Did some sewing from 6 to 8 p.m. 
 Tonight swell about the same although the surface of the water is unbroken by any ripple. Perhaps &amp; indication of stormy weather coming? 
 1 st&nbsp; case of C.S M. on board.
