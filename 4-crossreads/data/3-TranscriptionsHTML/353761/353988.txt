<p>3<br/>space. Officers notice this: They hasten to other officers of higher rank, they salute, they seek information, they return to their places Something has been learned that confounds the little already known. In sheer desperation of ever discovering what to do, a decision, calculated to be on the safe side, is made and acted upon. The troops are moved somewhat further forward and an order is given, an order that every man has been wishing for.<br/>"Mark time in front!"<br/>"Halt!"<br/>"Into line! - Right Turn!"<br/>"Right Dress!"<br/>&nbsp;"Stand at Ease!"<br/>"Take off Packs!"<br/>"Dress packs in line!"<br/>"Troops may be easy and sit down!"<br/>The final command is obeyed with a willingness that proves how wonderful is the discipline</p>
