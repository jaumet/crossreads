  
 Jly 22 outside  Went into Portishead for sundry articles (necessary and otherwise) & returned at 1.30 to a splendid dinner - (duck, green peas, new spuds, gooseberry & loganberry pudding, custard pie, & fruit) Do me! Talk about being full; I'm well packed (A nice little kiddy named Audrey Weaver (71/2) came up this afternoon to  see the  Australian. ?-) 
 23 Cut Church out & went for a walk along the Coast. Am leaving for Weymouth this afternoon - via Waterbury. Theres a graceful sailing boat lying [in margin] Sunday
