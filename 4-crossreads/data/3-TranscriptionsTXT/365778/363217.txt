 Tuesday 4/9/17 left at 9 am arrived Calais at 6 pm missed Train, Calais air raid 100 casualties, to Hotel Sauvage for night. Wednesday 5/9/17 Returned by car arriving at 10 am. Battalion at Bombing. Thursday 6/9/17 Battalion practised in Divisional Stunt clearing Clairmarias Wood. Saw Genl Plumer. Friday 7/9/17 Inspection of Battalion. Night stunt. 28th Sports. Saturday 8/9/17 Quiet Day &ndash; half holiday. Bean came along for account of Malt Trench.
