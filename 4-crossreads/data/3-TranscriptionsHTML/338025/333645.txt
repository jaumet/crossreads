<p>Tuesday 27th March 1917<br/>Etaples<br/>Issued with underclothing, cardigan, sheepskin mittens. After that had a good hot bath. Goodness knows when will get another.</p>
<p>28 Wed<br/>The rumble of gun fire can be heard from this camp (Albert). The Rly station and nearly every building I passed was damaged by shell fire.</p>
