<p>April, 1917. Palm Sunday. Sunday 1<br/>Day windy &amp; rainy. Ration came in morning. Up at 9. Cleaned up and got more fire wood. Pumped 1 &frac12; hrs in aftn. &amp; after cleaning myself went to Albert for provisions. Exchequer 2 1/2f. Paid end of week perhaps. Service at Y.M.C.A. very sloppy. Home at 9 and read paper before bed.</p>
