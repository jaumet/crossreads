3rd Aust C.C.S.C/o 29th C.C.S.B.E.F.France14.1.17
My dear Mother   &amp;  Sisters,
Am expecting an Aust. mail this week. Last night I received about 6 Bulletins from Mr Osbornw an Australasian   &amp;  two Argus' from Home. It was lovely to see a good old Aust. paper. Hope now the others will come through. I have not even opened them yet. Did not get off-duty until very late last night   &amp;  have been very busy all day &ndash; have about an hour nor, so shall
