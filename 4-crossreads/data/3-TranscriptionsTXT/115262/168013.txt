
Monday 3 September 1917.
Reveille 5 a.m. Turned out and ready to groom by 5.15 am. Breakfast 6.30 a.m. (Smaller ration than at Malaleh Beach) Turned camels out to graze at 9.30 a.m. Feed up & stables at 3.30 pm.
Issued with & instructions in use of P.D Gas helmet respirator 4 till 5 pm.Tea, a wash & turned in.
During today I dug in with L/Cpl Watson. Made a plain & comfortable dugout too.
It is now a Military Standing Order in this country behind the firing line that every man must be quartered in a dugout.
