<p>1915<br/>July 4 Sun.<br/>Went to church to-night at All Saints, C. of E. Sharia Boulac &ndash; A1. Service<br/>Service commenced at 6 p.m. &amp; concluded at 7.30.<br/>Heard English Military Band playing in Esbekieh gardens.<br/>All the Australian Troops in Egypt are now being issued with khaki shorts &amp; they look very serviceable too.  Previously anyone could wear them but now they are recognized as <u>the correct uniform.</u>  The latest battalions that have arrived are wearing plumed hats with brim down &amp; 4 dents.</p>
