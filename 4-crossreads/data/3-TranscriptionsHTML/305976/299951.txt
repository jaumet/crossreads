<p>Saturday Dec 15<br/>Voted on Referendum question<br/>Rain coming up again. However we are more prepared for it this time<br/>Sunday Dec 16<br/>Bitterly cold &amp; windy to day Wrote mail<br/>Monday Dec 17<br/>First Brigade moved up into Trenches to relieve New Zealanders Fine again today.<br/>Thursday Dec 18<br/>Rode into Esdud to day to bring some of our boys back. Esdud now a big dump &amp; railway station. All speed being made to bring up Railway as quickly as possible</p>
