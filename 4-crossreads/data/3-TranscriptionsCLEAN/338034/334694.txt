
very dull Xmas & Boxing Day here, it has been raining intermittently during the past couple of days making things generally uncomfortableDec 30th '16 :- The whole of our Brigade moved out of camp at 8 a.m. on reconnaissance work & marched out practically due East for about 15 miles where we camped near a Hod for the night. (Sheiks Zowaiid)Dec 31st '16 Part of the Brigade moved again last night a further six miles & entered Rafa unopposed early this morning. Rafa is a small port on the frontier between the Sinai Peninsula and Syria Palestine. The force returned early this[Sheiks Zowaiid is Sheikh Zowayed]
