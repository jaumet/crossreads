<p>I am afraid I cannot help you at all to get him to Millbank; the CCS's have nothing to do with a patient's future hospital; in fact we never know the base hospital in France &amp; often not even the town to which he is transferred. Your  <strike>les</strike> best way I think would be to write to him at the base Hospital in France, but I am rather doubtful whether you can secure his transfer to Millbank.</p>
<p>The Military authorities do not allow anyone to visit patients in CCS's, but if a patient is in a base Hospital &amp; is "dangerously ill", permission can be obtained. As Fergusson <strike>is</strike> will not probably be put on&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<strike>wha</strike>  the "D.I." List at the base, I am afraid you will not have much chance of obtaining permission.</p>
<p>Yrs v. truly,<br/>B.C. Maybury<br/>9th C.C.S.</p>
