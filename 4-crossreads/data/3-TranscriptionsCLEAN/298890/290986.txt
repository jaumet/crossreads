
14. SundayVery cold &  grey.  Went to hear Rev. Campbell Morgan at 11.a.m and Usshers  at 4.  Leaving them at 9 p.m.
15. MondayWet dull day.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10 & home at 6.
16. TuesdayAgnes Nestor rang up at 9.30.  Went to Miss Newcomb & left late at 5.45.  Snow on housetops & slushy in streets.
17. Wednesday.Cold showery day.  Went to Miss N. at 10. Down to Bond St. for her after lunch &  home - tired & depressed.
