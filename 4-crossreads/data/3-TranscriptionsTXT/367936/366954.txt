 Monday 27 March 1916 Mother &amp; I up early and drove into Mudgee.  Saw Dr. Nicholl;  he thinks I am about fit to return.  Roads in fearful state on account of dry weather.  Helped Dick with fence round stack.  Found young lamb bust on lucerne. 
 Tuesday 28 March 1916 Dick and I finished putting up fence round Straw stack. After dinner helped Father &amp; J. Oakley fix sheep then took 2 [indecipherable] up to Cliff pdk. with Father.  Mother, Father &amp; I went burning off up the Gully in the evening.
