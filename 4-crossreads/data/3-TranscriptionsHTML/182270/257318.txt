<p>MONDAY, JANUARY 6, 1919.</p>
<p>Raining off &amp; on all day. Went out</p>
<p>to the Bank &amp; sent a cable in</p>
<p>the morning, and knocking</p>
<p>about yarning all the afternoon.</p>
<p>Went for a drive round the</p>
<p>Park with Al &amp; a few more</p>
<p>late, &amp; had some fun</p>
