1/9/16Cloudy morning - on watch WA5. Taubes bomb Pt Said - hear guns.Taubes fly over here. Try to warn  howitzer battery no good.
2/9/16Leave Romani 7am for Hod Amara. Pass through Katia & arrive Amara 3pm & relieve WA2. Do 6 to 8 pm shift.
3/9/16WA2 leave here 7am for Romani for a spell. Taube visits us & passes to Romani. Guns heard evidently firing at the plane which returns and flies around us before leaving.
4/9/16Go out for watermelon & dates.Lovely cool day
5/9/16Taube over again gets rather furious machine gun fire. Go toDeBabbio & Freuva with Missen for curios & photos. Many dead horses at Debabbio. Air raid on El Arish to take place tomorrow morning. Develop photos after tea.
6/9/16El Maza - between Salmara & El Arish bombed as a reprisal for bombing Port Said
7/9/16Go out for water melons. Monitors lob a few shells on El Maza.
8/9/16Visit Bir el Abd battlefield & find scores of dead Turks & many NZ's graves. The NZ's graves have a cross above each one & the soldiers hat, rifle & gear (what is left of it) is placed on top. A dreary resting place. Find a Turkish belt buckle. Have a tough go with my camel who clears out & leaves me, after rolling me off.
9/9/16Go Hassaniya on [indecipherable] to get mail & have to belt him home.
10/9/16Taube over during afternoon. NZ's say good bye.
11/9/163rd LH take place of N.Z.'rumours of an attack to be made on " Mazaro' at week end.12/9/16Turks reported out of Mazar so stunt will probably be postponed. Taube pays a visit as usual & machine guns refuse to act as  usual.11pm three brigades 1-2 & 3 leave Amara with 6 batteries & 1000 mounted Tommy Camelry. Telegraph
