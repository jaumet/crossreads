<p>Sunday 14th April<br/>Rather a quiet day but had a couple of bad cases in from the neighbourhood.  Felt off colour in the morning.  Just about 10 PM a fellow in the D.T.'s was brought in &amp; we had a lot of trouble giving him the Stomach tube.  From then on I had a bad time, diarrhoea started so badly that I was unable to go to bed, so sat up in the Dressing Room the whole night.  All the morning I was bad &amp; it was not until evening that I began to improve</p>
