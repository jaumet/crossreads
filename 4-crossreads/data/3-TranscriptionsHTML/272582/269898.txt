<p>24/3/18.<br/>43 N.C.O's and men baked 37,120 lb rations.</p>
<p>25/3/18.<br/>43 N.C.O's and men baked 23,980 lb rations. Temp. B. 52. F. 49. W. 106 to 102. M. 46. 1 man rejoined unit from hospital.</p>
<p>26/3/18.<br/>No baking done by Unit. Route march and foot drill and remainder detailed for general Camp fatigues.</p>
<p>27/3/18.<br/>1 man rejoined unit from hospital. Same as 26th. Nothing further to report.</p>
<p>28/3/18.<br/>22 N.C.O's and men baked 15,360 lb rations, the remainder of personnel were taken for a route march, and some detailed for general Camp fatigues.</p>
<p>29/3/18<br/>43 N.C.O's and men baked 30,720 lb rations. Temp. B. 53. Fl. 48. W. 106 to 102. M. 48.</p>
<p>30/3/18<br/>22 N.C.O's and men baked 19,200 lb rations. 1 man evacuated to hospital (sick).</p>
<p>31/3/18.<br/>22 N.C.O's and men baked 19,680 lb rations. 1 man rejoined unit from leave to U.K. Strength. 90 Other ranks, not including 2 in hospital, 1 P.B. personnel (attachee) in hospital.</p>
