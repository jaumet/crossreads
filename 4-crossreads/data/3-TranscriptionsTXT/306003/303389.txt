 We came back to Oghratina with horses &amp; men very tired &amp; thirsty as the day was very hot, &amp; although everyone made the bottle of water go as far as they could they were dry long before we reached Oghratina;  we arrived here about sundown &amp; received plenty of fresh water for ourselves, &amp; after watering &amp; feeding the horses had only half an hour to make a drink of tea &amp; saddle up, &amp; feeling much refreshed started for Romani arriving in camp at 12.30 tonight with knocked up remounts as they were too soft to stand the
