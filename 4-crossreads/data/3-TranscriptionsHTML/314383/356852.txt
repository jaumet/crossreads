<p>Mar 29<br/>Today we devoted more time to Persian &amp; are all hard at work at the various classes, such as Vickers Machine Gun, Lewis machine gun, sword exercises, &amp; native transport loading, (donkey &amp; camels).  We went into Baghdad in the evening &amp; explored the residential quarter.  The streets are extremely narrow, &amp; wind in all directions.  The houses are built like those one sees in the plates of the time of Queen Elizabeth, the top storey jutting out above the lower, in many places it would be an easy matter to shake hands with the person in the top room in the opposite house.  The smells &amp; flies are terrible, sanitation being unknown.</p>
