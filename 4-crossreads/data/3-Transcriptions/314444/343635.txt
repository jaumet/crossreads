<p>Thomas Hedley Hill enlisted end of Jan. 1915 at Victoria Barracks, Sydney &ndash; Trained at Liverpool &ndash; at landing &amp; evacuation of Gallipoli one of the last to leave the Peninsula &ndash; Wounded there &ndash; Went to France &amp; died of wounds at Paaschendaele</p>
<p>Presented by<br/>Mr. A. H. W. Hill<br/>Euterpe,<br/>Kurraba Rd<br/>Neutral Bay.</p>
