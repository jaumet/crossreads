
"A" Squadron1st. Light Horse Training Regt.Egypt26th Oct.1916
Mrs. Vernon Secretary1st A.L.H. Comforts fundSydney
Dear Madam,By latest mail two parcels of Champion tobacco came to hand.On behalf of the men of our Regiment I desire to thank you for the above, which are still another proof of the kind thought of the people at home and of the benefit of your fund.thanking you and wishing you every successYours faithfullyJ Norman MacMillan
