<p>W.T.Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship: Berrima</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship: Sydney</p>
<p>Date: 14 Sep.</p>
<p>Time of Despatch or Receipt: 7.55am</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Captain to Brigadier</p>
<p>A large number of native canoes were crossing from Matupi island to main land this morning as I went out. They may be removing stores.</p>
