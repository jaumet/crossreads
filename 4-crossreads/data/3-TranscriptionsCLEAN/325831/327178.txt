 Telegram 14 July 11916 
 Hon Judge Ferguson SS Wodonga Brisbane 
 If your son is consenting party I could induce Federal Government withdraw him from firing line & give him duties elsewhere. Would he consent? 
 Hall 
 Attorney General 
 15 July 1916 
 Hall, Attorney General Sydney 
 Would be much relieved if son employed for present on staff or similar duties, but he must decide. Would not feel justified using pressure. 
 Ferguson
