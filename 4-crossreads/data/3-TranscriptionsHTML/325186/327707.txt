<p>to-date  We stayed a Meteren until 26th January and whilst we were there I went to Q.M. Stores for awhile to take one of the chaps place that went on leave. From there we moved on to Bulford camp. Stayed there about one day &amp; the Battn moved up on 27th &amp; occupied a reserve line at Red Lodge for one night they camped out in the open &amp; next day moved back to Romarin Camp. We were there about three weeks in Romarin doing working parties mostly building concrete pill-boxes.
