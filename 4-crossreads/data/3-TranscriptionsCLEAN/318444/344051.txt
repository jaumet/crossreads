
Thurs Apr 24th:-Got train to Warminster & back to Sutton Veny Camp.
Fri Apr 25th:-Anzac Day commemoration service on the grounds of Green Hill House Sutton Veny. Addressed by Lt-Col James & Fred Norwood.
Fri May 2nd 1919:-My n.m.e. orders have come along at last. Left No 7 Camp Sutton Veny & went by the 1.36 pm train from Warminster via Reading to Paddington Station London.Starting my Motor Course.
Sat May 3rd:-March of the overseas troops through London.Fine weather & I made one - of the spectators - up in the Strand near Australia House.Coliseum theatre at night.
Tues May 6th:-Started my motoring course at 10 Heddon St off Regent St Piccadilly. Know now why the boys call this stunt bon - Military Enjoyment.
Wed May 7th:-Fixed up lodgings at 73 Belgrave Road near Victoria Stn.
