<p>Wednesday Jan 2.1.18<br/>turned out 5.30 Breakfast 6.A.M.  Preparing to leave Ballah Post  fell in 7 A.M. Pulled camels across canal till 12.  dinner carried my own gear over in Boat  Pulled more camels across till 3.30 P.M. carried own gear down to the camp Ballah east had tea 5 went on guard 5.30 on [indecipherable] 5 Post 2 till 4 turned in nothing doing<br/>Thursday Jan 3.1.18.<br/>turned out 6.A.M. dissmissed from guard Breakfast fell in on fatigue till 12 fell in again 2.P.M. went across Canal loaded rations and ammunition on Boat.  Brought it across tea 5  turned in.  Papers from home.3.<br/>Friday Jan 4.1.18<br/>turned out 5.45 fed up Breakfast had a clean up rested till 12.  fell in 2 P.M. read a few orders dissmissed tea 5.P.M. turned in cold night.</p>
