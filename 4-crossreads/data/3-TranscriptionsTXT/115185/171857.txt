The officers and sisters mess together on this ship not as on the Grantala &ndash; and their quarters are very comfortable of course it is a beautiful ship. We anchored out in the outer harbour this morning and &ndash; what I saw was just a repetition of Simpsons Haven on a huge scale &ndash; and of course different back ground.
