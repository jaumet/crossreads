  
 [8 March] Landed Battalion shore for route march.  very wet    &  muddy. 
 [9 March] Landed Battalion ashore for manoeuvres with Brigade.  route march - Bivouac    &  attack. Return to Ship. very stiff.seas Two French Transports arrived. 
 [10 March] Commenced landing Battalion for route march had to discontinue leaving 60    &  1 boy ashore.  Weather very rough. another   [indecipherable]   Transport arrived.
