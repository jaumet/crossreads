
<p>1916 &nbsp; TUESDAY 1 &nbsp; FEB.</p>
<p>Very hot again &amp; we had a drunk Sargent Major to drill us in &quot;marching past&quot; in morning in the Park &amp; he did race us round. Got our second issue in afternoon so had no drill &nbsp;Got the 4.32 train in &amp; 5.20 boat to&nbsp;the bay. Linda &amp; Jo in town all day &amp; they were dead beat in evening when I got there. Lay on the tennis court for a bit after tea &amp; then got the 9.45 train back to camp</p>
