<p>Sunday Feb 3rd</p>
<p>Longmoor : usual fatigue : went to Liss in afternoon for a stroll : nice parcel from West Hatch :</p>
<p>Monday Feb 4th</p>
<p>Longmoor : usual fatigue job : drizzly rain all day : went to pictures after tea,</p>
<p>Tuesday Feb 5th</p>
<p>Longmoor : usual fatigues : wrote at night : drizzly weather though very mild :</p>
<p>Wednesday Feb 6th</p>
<p>Longmoor : usual fatigues : pictures after tea :</p>
<p>Thursday Feb 7th</p>
<p>Longmoor : warned for draft, so had to hand in spare gear, &amp; draw shortages :</p>
<p>Friday Feb 8th</p>
<p>Longmoor : supposed to go tonight</p>
