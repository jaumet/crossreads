<p>Saturday 16 December 1916<br/>On engineer escort with troop.  Captured turk walking about near camp.<br/>Saw tracks where turks had come in at night and looked at our camp.<br/>Saw 3rd Regt. Patrol, thought they were turks so went out to capture them;  but did not.</p>
<p>Sunday 17 December 1916<br/>Out on patrol as signaller.  Saw Turkish patrol of about 300 men &amp; camels.  Found some good Hods.  Saw dead Turkish horse shod with steal plates right over hooves.</p>
