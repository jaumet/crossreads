
Wednesday 5 March 1919There was a race between 2 Yank boats. Brekker was cruel. 1 cup coffee, Scrambled egg, some potatoes & a bit of bacon. Cost us 4/-. Our boat lost the race. Got up at 2.30. Had afternoon tea at the club & then back here to camp. Met Bill Cowper. Got about 20 letters. Had dinner & went to a concert.
Thursday 6 March 1919Raining today but cleared up about 11 am.  The sun then coming out. Bill & I went out. Had lunch at the club & then went on to the Nirvana & had tea with a couple of boys I met. Then went out to Bill's cousins & had dinner. After dinner had a bit of a sing & rushed down for our train but missed & had to get a taxi for the camp
