<p>detailed to report with my limber waggon to the Sanitary Section in Sailly-sur Lys about 4 miles away a small town just within range of the German Guns but which doesn't receive much attention from them at present. Most of the windows are broken in the houses &amp; now &amp; then one passes a building which has "stopped one"</p>
<p>The principal Church has been destroyed by fire &amp; now is nothing but 4 walls &amp; a tower. It is now the wood &amp; coal supply depot &amp; proved to be where I had to go to pick up a load for the San. Section</p>
<p>I drove straight through the Tower where the main entrance</p>
