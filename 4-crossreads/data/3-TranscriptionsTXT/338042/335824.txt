 Scales- Call on Mr. Scales at his Office &ndash; Then we view St. Giles Church (Cripplegate) which is burial place of Milton etc.etc. &ndash; Also played prominent part in Great Plague &amp; Fire of London &ndash; Luncheon &ndash; Take train at Cannon St for Blackheath and go to the Home of Mrs.Thomas (Mrs.Scales' Mother) &ndash; Pass most enjoyable evening with Mrs. Thomas &ndash; Mr. &amp; Mrs Thomas (Jun) Miss Matthews, Mrs. Scales, Miss [indecipherable] &ndash; Stay at Blackheath for the night.
