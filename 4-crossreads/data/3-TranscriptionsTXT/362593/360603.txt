 Saturday 21 September 1918 Fired 2000, 24 bombs.  3 trips, 2 h. 0, 1 h. 35, 1 h. 15. Johnnie Webster missing.  Saw him go down in a spin and burst into flames.  He was evidently shot from the ground, very sorry about it. 
 Sunday 22 September 1918 Fired 400.  Dropped 8 bombs.  1 trip, 2 h. 45 m.
