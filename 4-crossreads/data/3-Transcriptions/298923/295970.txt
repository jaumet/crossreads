<p>31 October 1918 Thursday<br/>Stillman<br/>Mother's letter with enclosure from HLA dated Aug 18th<br/>I hope you will let Bronte for the summer too hot to take Babe &amp; Dolly there for a start<br/>Very glad to think Joe will be home when I come- can't say anything definite yet<br/>Snakebite Antidote came along safely but no one lives in the Jordan Valley now<br/>I enclose cutting from Times almost verbatim what I wrote you in my Diary re J.V.</p>
