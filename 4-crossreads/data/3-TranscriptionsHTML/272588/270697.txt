<p>Saturday 27 April<br/>Quiet to-day, no casualties all day.<br/>News to hand that Kemmel Hill has been taken by the Boche after a big fight with the French defending troops.</p>
<p>Sunday 28 April<br/>Last night the Boche shook things up round here &amp; caused us much uneasiness with his shells.  One direct hit on our cow shed. This afternoon too, he straafed here &amp; dropped some 5.9's on the road. Heavy bombardment on our left again to-night.<br/>No casualties except two.</p>
