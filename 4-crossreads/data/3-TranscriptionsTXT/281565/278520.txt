Old hands who had toiled &amp; bled in the gullies over which they had watched so long, who had seen comrades &amp; brothers killed, felt the matter keenly &amp; later reinforcements who had had their mates shot down in cold blood at their posts, murmured for a chance to avenge them.  It felt like running away as one glanced down seaward &amp; saw the little white crosses dotting the hillsides a feeling of resentment for the move &amp; a sudden anger against those who had bungled the Gallipoli campaign right from the start spread over one.  Officers Kits were sent to the beach &amp; on Tuesday 13th I received orders with two others to pack up for embarkation &amp; proceed with Chaplain McAuliffe's effects to the Beach.  Sneaking down to the sap, dodging by graveyards, silent but accusing, we reached the beach.  Piles of broken stores, ammunition limbers &amp; surplus supplies lay scattered everywhere.
