
[Wednesday 2 February 1916]
I don't know whether you realise the difficulty of writing up each day.  Some days Fountain Pen won't work then I use Pencil & hope you can read & after all it is a pleasure to let you know my daily life & its little enough . [Following paragraph is crossed out]Thank Joe for sending [indecipherable] I am nearly alright again.  The Plaster I must have mislaid never mindI don't think you need worry about the Turks beating us on the Canal it is nearly as tough a proposition as our try at the Dardanelles.Joe mentions Cornolla sold but he does not say who to?
