<p>Sunday 29 April 1917<br/>Stand to at 4.30.  Nice cool day out on outpost in Wadi, relieved the 2nd at 5, on Picquet at night.  Got some more mail.</p>
<p>Monday 30 April 1917<br/>In camp till 3.30, wrote Home [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 6 and 3] No.  Out on outpost again.</p>
<p>Tuesday 1 May 1917<br/>On outpost all day, polling day.  Usual shift at night.</p>
<p>Wednesday 2 May 1917<br/>Still on Outpost.</p>
<p>Thursday 3 May 1917<br/>Still on Outpost.</p>
<p>Friday 4 May 1917<br/>Still on Outpost, sent in for Canteen stuff.</p>
<p>Saturday 5 May 1917<br/>Medical Inspection, relieved off outpost by 314.</p>
