<p>[Saturday August 5 1916]<br/>WOUNDED again<br/>Usual dead &amp; dying all around among the Ambulance tents, iron sledges for the single casualties &amp; Sand carts for groups of 4 &amp; 5.<br/>Plain covered with Dead Horses mine among them- I still have one that HLA gave me.<br/>Wound is painful but not serious I shall cable to you "Nothing serious, flourishing"<br/>Have got to ride a Camel to Railhead that will crucify the flesh.<br/>Their planes dropped <strike>again</strike> dozens of Bombs- &amp; smashed up the Railway- From where [indecipherable] a wounded man is cleaning his pipe sitting on a dead horse giving us information as to number of Turk prisoners 700 just passed &amp; a German section of Machine Gunners</p>
