<p>Dates worth noting</p>
<p>2nd May - Landed at Galipoli</p>
<p>19th May - Big Turk attack</p>
<p>20th May  -  Armistice</p>
<p>Aug 1st  - First issue of rum prior to attack</p>
<p>Aug  6 - Charge of Lone Pine, rum finished</p>
<p>Aug 10 -  Came out of Lone Pine 122 out of 800</p>
<p>Sep  -  Leave for Lemnos</p>
<p>Oct 30 - return from Lemnos</p>
<p>Nov 2  -  Lost my teeth</p>
<p>9/11/15  -  Paid 10/-</p>
<p>10/11/15 &ndash; Paid 2/-/-</p>
<p>17/11/15  -  Rum issue</p>
<p>25/11/15 &ndash; Paid &pound;1<br/>25/11/15  -  First fall snow</p>
<p>9/12/15 -  had a feed</p>
<p>15/12/15 - heard officially that we are going to evacuate</p>
<p>19/12/15 -  Evacuated the Anzac Position</p>
<p>24/12/15 -  On  board SS Simla for unknown destination</p>
<p>25/12/15 -  Second I was in the army on board "SS Simla"</p>
<p>25/12/15  -  In harbour at Alexandria</p>
<p>30/12/15  -  Arrived at Tel-el-Kebir</p>
<p>31/12/15  -  Orderly room big lecture</p>
<p>13/1/16  -  Went on leave and played up, went gay, and came home<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   under escort</p>
<p>17/1/16  -  awarded 10fPH2 for same</p>
<p>1/2/16  -  Got my teeth in very rough</p>
<p>1/3/16   -  Arrived at Serapheum</p>
<p>4/3/16   -  Arrived Zeutoun</p>
