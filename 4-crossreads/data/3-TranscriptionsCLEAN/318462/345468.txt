 Sunday 27/1/18 A lovely morning, no frost, sun shining quite hot. church Parade. deferred Pay 42- Owe 6-13-0 getting close to Trenches now Advanced guard gone to day, soon be amongst the Bombs etc. This is a glorious day & the Bees are out so spring is not far off Feeling happy to night got letter from Nell she got the 10- so got a letter accordingly. If only peace would come 
 Monday 28/1/18 off tonight about 2. a.m. lovely morning slight frost, Posted letter to Nell. deferred Pay 42-1-6. Owe 6-11-0. Its a glorious day & I think the winter is over off Tomorrow morning at 4 a.m. Train at Samer at 7. Back to Kemmel. or Lindenhoek.  8. p.m. Plenty of excitement thank goodness all the boys are broke or ----- The Band wakes us up at 2 a.m. gee whiz to catch a Train at 7-15 8 kilos away thats the stuff to win the war. will have to stand about in the frost till 4.30 before moving off. of then.
