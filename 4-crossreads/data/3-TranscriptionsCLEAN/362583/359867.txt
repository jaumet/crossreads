  
 Gallipoli 25-10-15 
 My dear Mrs Ferguson 
 I was very pleased to receive your letter of 6th September. 
 It is very slow here at present and it looks as though we would be here all the winter which is just about to start. I am afraid it will be very severe but the men are fairly well equipped with warm clothing and we should be fairly comfortable
