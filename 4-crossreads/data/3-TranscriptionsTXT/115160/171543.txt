 Paris on the 7th November 1918 
 My dear Sir, 
 Not be surprised, if you receive my letter before having your answer. &nbsp;I write to you this letter, for I have much time: I am in vacation for a week. 
 How the life in Paris has come again thanks to the very good communiques. 
 Sunday on the 27th October here was a review in the honour of the class 1920 which has passed the revision in August and September. &nbsp;I was summoned for the filing off.
