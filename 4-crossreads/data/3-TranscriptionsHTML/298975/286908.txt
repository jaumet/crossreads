<p>At 10 oclock we pulled out from the Wharf &amp; it was imiadetially filled with wating friends who cried &amp; chered as we went out in the stream At 12.30 PM we left the bouy on the start proper for the great adventure &ndash; What will it bring forth Why think about it the future will tell  No arrangements had been made to feed us so that when dinner came on about 2 PM the men mostly had a full meal &amp; as there was a fair see running sickness held full <strike>swing</strike> sway and many a good soldier cared not</p>
