<p>[30 Sun]<br/>Church Parade</p>
<p>[1 July Mon]<br/>Cleaning waggons.</p>
<p>[2 Tues]<br/>Loading waggons ready to shift camp.</p>
<p>[3 Wed]<br/>"B &amp; D" Subs: packed up and left Rivery at 10 am. &amp; arr. Lamotte Brebiere about 11-30am. Dug in and had dinner. Ordered to move out again about 1 pm. Arr. Glisy 3 pm.<br/>Pitched our camp near river.<br/>(Following up the 5th Bdge [inf?])</p>
