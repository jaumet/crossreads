Hqrs. &amp; the Y.M.C.A. playing all the time &amp; so round to our billets once inside they started again &amp; the dancing was kept going to a late hour, old Jock &amp; McPherson were in their element for they are both born Scotchmen &amp; dancers of these Scotch hops &amp; jigs,, the Jock belonged to Argyll &amp; Sutherland Highlanders &amp; was in his kilts, &amp; that night he slept with his declaring that he was not going back</p>
