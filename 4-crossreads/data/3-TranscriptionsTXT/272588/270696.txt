
Thursday 25 AprilAnzac Day, & the troops were liberally supplied with1 pair socks1 pkt. chewing gum1 pkt. cigarettesGenerous, eh.Very quiet on the front all day but we hear rumours that the Boche has taken Kemmel Hill.  Ye Gods it is marvellous.  No doubt, the bombardments on the left for the last two nights were preliminary to the attack.
Friday 26 AprilQuiet to-day.
