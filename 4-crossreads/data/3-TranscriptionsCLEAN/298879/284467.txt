  
 November 1915 Sunday 14 Cool breeze blowing. We relieved the 9th Bn out of their old position. We got a good pozzy for the 'phone. Violent artillery duel about 1600 to 1700. 
 Monday 15 Showery settled down in our dug - out for another artillery duel one man killed too. A fierce Naval bombardment at Achi Baba this afternoon which lasted about two hours.
