Friday Dec 7th
Sutton Veny : mild & inclined to rain : before the S.M.O. in morn & marked for another weeks m'ssge : concert at night in Y.M.C.A. & was very nice indeed
Saturday Dec 8th
Sutton Veny : fatigue at Y.M.C.A. : Reg & self went for a stroll after dinner : concert at Y.M.C.A. after tea & very good indeed
Sunday Dec 9th
Sutton Veny : stroll in afternoon : service at Y.M.C.A. after tea & it was very good indeed
Monday Dec 10th
Sutton Veny : kit inspection in morn : classification & V.D. inspection in afternoon
