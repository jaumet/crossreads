<p>Sunday 25 August<br/>Mr Cook did not turn up so as place shelled began line without proper direction. Nearly finished when I met Mr Cook. Finished all right &ndash; began to return home. Met Chanc. wagon. Returned together as relieving the congestion of 3 chaps to the cart. Home about 1.30 am.</p>
<p>Up at 6 am advised Sergt of wagon abandoned. Away about 8.15 a.m. with limber, 4 horses. Found screw washer had loosened and had tightened on wheel jamming it. Returned after repairing.</p>
<p>Afternoon spent completing repairs wagon. Running through cable at night very busy &amp; tired.</p>
