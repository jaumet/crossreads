<p>Thursday 27th Dec<br/>   Plenty of snow and fairly cold.    Still awaiting my pass now due so finished packing up and otherwise fixed things up so that I could leave all straight .   Am taking across a French bayonet I brought from Cape Helles also a German bayonet, a couple of bombs etc etc.   The usual game of Crib. at night, a rubber each night now being quite a habit with me.</p>
<p>Friday 28th Dec<br/>   Cold with plenty of snow.   Walked to 'Frenck' in order to work off some of my stiffness after the football match.    arrived back at 1 P.M. &amp; found that my pass for "blighty" had arrived with that of C.
