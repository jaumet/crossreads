<p>July 1st<br/>stormy night, kite sent over Turk lines to drop messages, our dead still in front of Turk trenches, make envelopes out of paper</p>
<p>Thursday July 2nd<br/>going out for spell, dirty night Thunder lighting and rain, Turks bring up two new guns Pte Handcock supposed to be shot as spy</p>
<p>Monday 5th<br/>got 50 shells a 4. AM. after arriving from digging, Jack Johnstone fires on our Beach, swimmer hit by schrapnel swears he is Torpedoed, French Transport sunk 30,000 Tommies arrive Tucker very poor two wet nights</p>
<p>Tuesday<br/>Marching Order inspection<br/>till Sunday 11th July<br/>Left for Imbros on large tranship on to Al Kahira at 4 oclock in morning frightened of Beachy Billie two regiment of Gurhkas and one of Tommies arrive ships sank for breakwater</p>
<p>Monday<br/>Had a good rest left for Anzac Cove in evening reach beach at 12 oclock to tired go and I sleep there till morn</p>
<p>Tuesday<br/>Rested Tuesday took over WA trenches go into firing line</p>
<p>Wednesday 14th<br/>Working hard digging Artillery active</p>
