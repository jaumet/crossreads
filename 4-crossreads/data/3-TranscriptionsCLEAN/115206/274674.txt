
April 1918Sunday 14
Bussy - la - Daours      D.R.Weather dull, - no rain in day  - rained all night.Witnessed a good horse race for five pounds over 5 furlongs - easy win for Hdqrs . 4 lengths.  Bob Butcher came around had the usual yap.Tom Gallaghers horse wounded in off hind with shrapnel.Fritz put down a fairly heavy barrage on Abinqy Rd [Aubigny ?], and lengthened to Daours - Rumours of pay tomorrow; Had a win at midnight.
