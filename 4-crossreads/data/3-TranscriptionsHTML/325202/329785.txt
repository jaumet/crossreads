<p>This morning their guns started shelling Sailly which is about a mile away from the camp we could hear the shells coming through the air. We learned they were shelling the Railway Station &amp; also the big building where the Engineers have their quarters. Had to go into Sailly this morning &amp; as we crossed the river we could see the smoke from the explosion of the shells &amp; we met the Drivers</p>
