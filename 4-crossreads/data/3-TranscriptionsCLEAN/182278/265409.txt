  
 Tuesday 9/4/18. More enemy shelling  2 Coys digging support Line- wrote Doff. 
 Wednesday 10/4/18  Quiet Day  had meeting of Coy [Cdrs?]  more digging at night 
 Thursday 11/4/18. Taking over tonight from Currie- very cold day 
 Friday 12/4/18 Miserable day went up to posts at night  Good deal of M.G. Fire. 
 Saturday 13/4/18 Men working well  weather better- visited posts. 
 Sunday 14/4/18  Attempted raid but failed  one casualty  enemy very alert.
