
20th March 1917. Woke at 8 for breakfast cleaned up & fooled around during the morning  Went on duty from 12 till 4. Went on again at 8 till 12. Another very quiet day. Wrote to Millie
21st march 1917. Woke at 8 for duty till 12. Came off & fooled around during afternoon. Came on again at 6 till 8. Very quiet day. Went to bed at 9. Going on duty at 4 till 8.
22nd March 1917. Came off at 8 & fooled around during the morning. Left at 12 for baths. Had bath & had dinner in Armentieres. Came back at 4.30 & went on duty at 6 till 8. Went to bed ready for duty at 12 till 4.
23rd March 1917. Woke at 8 for breakfast. Went on duty at 10 till 4 oclock. Came off & had tea. Afterwards went to estaminet & had a few "wads". Came back & went on duty 8 till 12.
24th March 1917. Went on duty at 8 till 12. Fooled around during the afternoon. Went on again at 6 till 8. Very quiet day.
25th March 1917. Got up at 4 for duty till 8. Fooled around all day till 4 when I went on duty till 6. Had some tea at estaminet. Went to bed early. On duty at 12 till 4
