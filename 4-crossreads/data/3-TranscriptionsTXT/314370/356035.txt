
May, 1916Wednesday 31Half holiday todayToo hot & lazy to do anything except try to sleep which is almost impossible.After tea I went to Cairo for a change of air but came home early.
June, 1916Thursday 1Weather continues stifling & oppressive & would bring a thunder storm every day under the same conditions in Aust.  But it never thunders here.Rumours are rife about all Australians leaving at the end of the course
