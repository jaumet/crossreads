
Monday 29/10/17  Dressed in kilts, went shooting grouse, not easy to hit only got one
Tuesday 30/10/17. More shooting, left by car for Dunkeld then to Perth and got sleeper for London
Wednesday 31/10/17  Arrived at Euston at 8.45 a.m  caught train for Folkestone from Charing Cross at 11 am & boat to [Bolongne?] [Boulogne] arriving at 4 p.m.  met Gale. stayed night at Folkestone Hotel.
Thursday 1/11/17. Left Bologne [Boulogne] at 9.30 arrived at Ypres at 11 p.m. tired stayed night with Div.
