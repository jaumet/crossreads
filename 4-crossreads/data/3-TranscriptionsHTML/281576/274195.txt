<p>Mon. March 12  Went into Albert to see Bobby Roberts first appearance with the Anzac Coves".  His turn took very well.  Then we returned to our Rec. Hut &amp; had a rehearsal  Got Letter from M.S.</p>
<p>Tues. Mar. 13.  Snowy" Hargraves went away today to Bernafay &amp; Jack Edgar is taking his place as "Props".  He is busy making an aeroplane &amp; tools for our Barber's Shop Scene.</p>
<p>Wed. 14 March  Small mail in.  Got note from Bank today.  M.S. had wired me &pound;5.  Good old S.H.</p>
