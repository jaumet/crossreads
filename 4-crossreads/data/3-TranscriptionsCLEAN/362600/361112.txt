  
 F 17 Sun Ice.  We take over & it is us now. Generally [alone]. Tidy up. Go down in Cliff slide with R.A.W.E. Sergent. Feet very sore. All shops open.  Return early. Wrote M.R.L. Dad.  Dream of Flint with [in?] F 18 Mon F. Warmer. Go down Town alone & try to [indecipherable] for Maisie but nothing doing. Buy P.C's etc. Note all cafe's on Quay. Pay day. Wrote Auntie Annie 
 F 19 Tues. F. I do dressing blister my fingers. Play piano & yawn all night. Hard work all day - [indecipherable]. Wrote Roy. Jennie. Laura. - 
 F 20 Wed Ice & Fog. I fail to [indecipherable] in D.T.S. Go to Y.M.C.A. concert reserve seat but Val &  I have to get out 'cos WO. Sees us. We have grand inspection of Tents. Wrote M.R.L. - 
 F21 Th Go down Town & all about docks etc. Trying to buy present. Meet [Livo?] & go to Y.M.C.A. hear band concert. Dr - M. falls into my arms & he caresses my much. I gather lots of passion-fruit which no one knew about. Home 8.15 pm - 
 F 22 Fri Things awful humbug. I am properly fed up especially at being told not to mention Bulford. Write & sit by fire all night. Ken Pine. Bess Wrote Dad. Mum. Geo. Julia. Norm. M.R.L. A Annie 
 F 23 Sat Lovely day. We shift all timber from Trenches.  I am Ord. Sergt. Major. Stay in mess all night. Play gramophone & write all night. Dream I am married to Ollie Condell - in Burwood. Peg. Marion Clarke. Nette. MRL. Edie Wrote Nancy Dewas. Ethel. Bert. Jess.
