  
 26 March 1915 Friday 
 Begin to learn experience by finding trouble. Our rifles are chock a block with dust   & sand. So we buy rifle covers   & avoid any more hours work cleaning. Big detachment of Army service men from Kitchners New army arrive w/- waggons   & mules 
 1915 March 27 Saturday 
 Things progress in a quiet manner. Receive     you  first letter from home Very glad to hear about the people there
