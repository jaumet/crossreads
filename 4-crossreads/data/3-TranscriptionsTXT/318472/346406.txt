
30 June 1918Sunday5 After Trinity
Put a few men through the equipment store in the morning & in the afternoon rode a bike to Clacton - on Sea. It is a very nice little place & I believe very fashionable & gay in times of peace.
1918 July 1MondayVery quiet. Got about 40 men away in the afternoon. Italians are doing well according to reports & British & French have had successful minor affairs on the Western Front. Nice fine weather.
