<p>Sunday 10 September 1916<br/>Work going on as usually.  Generally strengthening position, revetting &amp; so on.</p>
<p>Monday 11 September 1916<br/>Things going well.  Good work being done by Artillery &amp; stokes Guns.</p>
<p>Tuesday 12 September 1916<br/>No alterations in position.  Usual Artillery preparation.</p>
<p>Wednesday 13 September 1916<br/>Still working on revetting &amp; other strengthening work including Concrete D.outs.</p>
<p>Thursday 14 September 1916<br/>Work going on as usual.  Still work on concrete Dugouts, etc.</p>
<p>Friday 15 September 1916<br/>Went to Gas School today &amp; had very instructive lecture &amp; course.</p>
<p>Saturday 16 September 1916<br/>Working as usual revetting Avenues, etc.</p>
<p>Sunday 17 September 1916<br/>Had quiet day.  Fair amount of Artillery bombardment work same as usual.</p>
<p>Monday 18 September 1916<br/>Had very wet day causing most work to be abandoned.  Still raining.</p>
<p>Tuesday 19 September 1916<br/>Still very wet making work progress very slow.</p>
<p>Wednesday 20 September 1916<br/>Weather taking up but still very sloppy.  Working on revetting drainage, etc.</p>
<p>Thursday 21 September 1916<br/>Working on revetting concrete Dugouts, drainage &amp; other work improving position generally.</p>
<p>Friday 22 September 1916<br/>Beautiful fine day.  Usual work strengthening position.  Much aerial activity.</p>
<p>Saturday 23 September 1916<br/>A good deal of Artillery activity.  Very little response from Fritz.</p>
