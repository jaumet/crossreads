  
 Saturday Dec 1 Returned to Jaffa Found the boys standing to owing to Jacko shelling Some spies were caught signalling & directing artillery fire from housetops. Taubes came over at night Sunday Dec 2 Visited Jaffa & visited ruins near the shore. Saw remains of House where Simon Peter supposed to have stayed Monday Dec 3 Looking like rain today Received some parcels. Anzac Divisional  Post Cards  Christmas Cards came along today. Posted some to friends. In afternoon visited foundry
