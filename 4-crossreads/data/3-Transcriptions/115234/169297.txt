
<p>31st</p>
<p>We were in the front line trench.&nbsp; We are in on the left of Warneton:&nbsp; I am attached to no 10 platoons Lewis gun.&nbsp; There are six of us &amp; we are holding an isolated post on the left flank&nbsp; Fritz is 130 yards across from us.&nbsp; I am writing this seated on a sandbag with a row of bombs on the parapet in front of me&nbsp; I am day sentry &amp;</p>
