<p>30th June (Friday)<br/>Raining. Lecture on the gas now being used in warfare, "Chlorine", when it was explained to us that our helmets are superior to those used by Fritz. Route march all round the district in afternoon. Towards dusk German Anti-aircraft guns were again busy at our planes. Whilst lying in bed tonight we could hear quite distinctly the awful rumble of guns. It was something terrific and kept us awake &ndash; The sound</p>
