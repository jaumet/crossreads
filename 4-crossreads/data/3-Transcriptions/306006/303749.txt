<p>25-2-17<br/>Sun 25<br/>The sun was well up when I awoke this morning had breakfast &amp; went up the street &amp; bought a couple of singlets &amp; a pair of tennis shoes, as I am wearing new boots &amp; they are far from comfortable, then had a hot bath &amp; caught a tram out to Heliopolis &amp; walked  to Zeitoun &amp; reported at the school half an hour later the lads from Kantara arrived &amp; I reported to Lieut Heegan that all the lads who came down yesterday had reported here this morning, I applied for a pass from 1 oclock this afternoon but was told I would probably</p>
