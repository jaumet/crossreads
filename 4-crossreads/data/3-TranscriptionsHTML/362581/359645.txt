<p>25.6.16<br/>France</p>
<p>My darling Eddy,</p>
<p>You must have a lovely party when Auntie Eadle come home. So glad you are growing stronger. You must get to know some nice little boys   &amp;  girls at school, then you will have plenty to your party. What a beautiful party you had a Arthur Mitchell's Ask his father if he will come to yours. Fondest love darling   &amp;  heaps of kisses   &amp;  Good luck.<br/>From<br/>Auntie Eadle.</p>
