<p>Your mind before you strike the first one, as a brigade of men in single file stretch out a great distance. The great trouble with these trucks is, that you are halting every minute for a second or two. It takes about seven hours to go six miles and all this time you do not sit down for a spell. Coming out it is much better as you know where you are to camp so come out independently as you get relieved</p>
