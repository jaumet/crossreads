Their drivers dressed in light blue dungarees, along they move stately & dignified out of the Vil along the green prickly pear hedged road & away into the yellow sands of the desert, there must be at least 500 of them. They are all heavily loaded & are moving forward to the front line dump. Hail to the gipo without them progress against the turks would have been slow indeed.
