 Friday 19 November, 1915 
 Went in for our pay &amp; came out to Valletta again in the afternoon. 
 We met a corporal out of our hut &amp; took him round with us. We went to the Olympia Club &amp; played billiards. 
 Sid &amp; the corporal went back to Camp, I stayed at the Hotel De France.
