<p>June 4th<br/>Sabbath Mass 7.30am (Bac-St- Maur)<br/>News of Naval Engagement. Reported German victory. Easy day.<br/>June 5th<br/>British Victory apparent- weather still wet and miserable. Take part in Concert at Y.M.C.A.- "Australian Corroboree".<br/>June 6th<br/>Pay day. We leave Ft. Rompu for Advanced Dressing Station at Bois Grenier- safe arrival- detailed to Dressing Rooms. (Fierce prolonged bombardment of our guns)</p>
