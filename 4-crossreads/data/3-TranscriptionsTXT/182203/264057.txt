 At Sea. Aug. 20th 1916. My dearest Mother &amp; Sisters, I last wrote to you I think the day before we left Cairo. Well on Saturday Aug 12th we left Cairo &amp; arrived in Alexandria that day about 2. p.m. &ndash; went on board the hospital ship &quot;Kanowna&quot;.
