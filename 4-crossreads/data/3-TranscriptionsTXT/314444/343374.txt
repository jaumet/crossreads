 [Corporal, later Sergeant, Thomas Arthur Henry Breaden MSM (Meritorious Service Medal), No 2471, clerk of Dulwich Hill, NSW, joined the Army on 22 July 1915 at age 29. He embarked from Sydney on HMAT A32 Themistocles on 5 October 1915 and served with the 17th Battalion, 5th Reinforcement in France. He returned to Australia on 20 May 1919.] [Envelope postmarked 27 July 1916 with censor's mark] Breaden 
 On Active Service
