<p>August 1917<br/>Tuesday 14th<br/>This is a crook place to be sick &amp; I will have about a week of it.  The weather is still bad but they are advancing on parts of the front<br/>Wednesday 15th.<br/>Fritz made a big raid early this morning we brought two down<br/>Thursday 16th.<br/>A few shells dropping in the town &amp; I believe he gave Hazebrouck another sally up   they have decided to shift the hospital   I don't think any of us care how soon we get out of this dirty hungry gutted show   The Tommies are a filthy crowd<br/>Friday 17th<br/>We had a great sight this evening.  Fritz came over in force for an air raid he dropped a few bombs &amp; our search light picked him up &amp; brought one down &amp; of air brought another down</p>
