<p>Mr Weir<br/> Heliopolos Camp<br/>Camp<br/>[Postmarked] 1 &ndash; VI &ndash; 15</p>
<p>Dear sir. -  T. Orford<br/>Just to let you know that I am not dead yet but to ask you to send me a few Post Cards &amp; envelopes &amp; I'll send you the price of them. remember me to the boys there. Tell them moye Halestones clarke . gilroy Harold Baker are killed. . and you will oblige<br/>PASSED<br/>BY<br/>CENSOR</p>
