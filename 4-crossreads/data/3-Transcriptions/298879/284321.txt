<p>January 1915<br/>Tuesday 26<br/>Mounted Canal Guard 8.45 A.M. Inspected by W.O. Lowe. Marched to our post. Natives to cook our meals, Engineering party practising bridge building.</p>
<p>Wednesday 27<br/>Dismounted from Canal Guard. Had a holiday for the rest of the day. Very windy. Clouds of sand blowing everywhere.</p>
