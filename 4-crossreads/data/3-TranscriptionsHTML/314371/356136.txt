<p>Mon. &amp; Tues. 4 &amp; 5 June<br/>Same job.</p>
<p>Wednesday 6th June<br/>Warned for Base.</p>
<p>Thurs. 7th June<br/>Marched out &amp; went by train to Dannes, Camiers.</p>
<p>Friday 8th June<br/>Examined by M.O. (3 days L.D.).</p>
<p>Mon. 11th June<br/>Met two Sgts. from our Coy. down on course of Inst.  Had quite a celebration.  (The Coy. suffered terribly at Bullecourt.)</p>
<p>Thurs. 14th June<br/>Warned again for line draft.</p>
<p>From June 15th<br/>Returned to unit by way of Achiet-le-Grand, Albert &amp; Becourt, found 'em at Warloy-Baillon.<br/>Had a good time there practising for a competition (which, by the way, never came off).  Aug. 17, J.C.L.<br/>Went from Warloy to Mailley [Mailly]</p>
