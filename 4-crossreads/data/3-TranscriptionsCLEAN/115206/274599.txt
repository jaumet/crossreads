  
 January 1918 Tuesday 29 
 At Beutin Paid forty francs at 9.30 am. Inspection of gas masks at 11 am. Cleaned the leatherwork of my saddle before lunch, and the steel portion after I had finished lunch. Thompson rode to Etaples on business for Smithy.  Cinema left Attin. For Dranoutie, I suppose Dick has gone with it.
