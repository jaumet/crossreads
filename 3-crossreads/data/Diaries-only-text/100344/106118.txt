Left of Hill 60 last night about 10pm. Conditions much above expectations. All very quiet not a shot fired nor gun heard till afternoon about a dozen Wizbangs came over but not on our trenches. Rations very good Meat potatoes tea (cold) with Bread Tinned stew Jam butter. Night just too beautiful to be stuck in trenches at war. Myself did many odd jobs carpentering to fill in the night. Sentrys only real need for Gas Guard. distance between trenches in front line being 700 yds. On big salient our right flank showing direct in our rear and left flank straight as
