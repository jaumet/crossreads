
1-2-16Tue 1stMounted parade today till 2 oclock then had dinner & had nothing to do this afternoon;We played the 2nd Regt a football match this afternoon & it was a draw after a good game the scores being (3 points each, put this evening in throwing a medicine ball about till it was too dark to see; A metal road has been made from from Wardan station down to the camp, it is marvellous the pace these niggers can put down a road.
