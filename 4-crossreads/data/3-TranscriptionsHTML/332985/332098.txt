<p>1-3-1917<br/>The best and finest day for months, still moving forward the Artillery, our Bty. out of range, never fired a shot.  Sending up hundreds of rounds of ammunition.  Lieut. Archer in charge of wagon line.  Rations and ammunition going to Bty. on light railway working day and night.  One of our baloons brought down in flames, by a Hun plane.</p>
<p>2-3-1917<br/>Service stripes issued, one for each years service.  We are still advancing.  A few shells flying about light velocity.  Carting ammunition all day.  Our first line wagons in a new position.  Likely to move our present line.  Rations and 2 men lost during the night.  Bty. without</p>
