 Octobre 7 
 Weather continues to be very cold &amp; wet. We went for a short route march this morning full marching order &ndash; otherwise resting. Expect to move up toward the Lines tomorrow. 
 Octobre 8 Left camp at 10 a.m. &amp; marched to a camp near Onderdom where we are staying for the night. Expect to leave here in the morning. It is raining very heavily tonight.
