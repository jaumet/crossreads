<p>14th<br/>Filled bottles &amp; back Yarned &amp; tea til 8 pm when turned in Up at 10.15 to work on a barricade which kept us going until about 2 am.</p>
<p>About 1.15. The enemy sent up a sort of star shell which was evidently the signal for a hellish fullisade of rifle fire &amp; apparently bombs &amp; was kept up for about 15 minutes.</p>
<p>15th Sunday.<br/>The firing was slacker today then since we landed &amp; we all put in</p>
