 19 Thursday Fine windy day.  Mrs. Fairweather &amp; Mrs. French came over from Hove in afternoon &amp; I had tea out with them &amp; sat on the Front. 
 20  Friday Showery day.  Listened to band in morning.  Little Grant girl &amp; I walked to Felpham before dinner 
 21 Saturday  Left Bognor in morning  Had lunch with N.N. at 22 Harley Rd.  Then went on to stay with Miss Hodge.  Lovely day.  Good many leaves coming down but trees still well dressed.
