The big motors are eyeopeners I never thought they could stand the rough wear roads like they do ,but they seem to revel in them plough their way through like steam engines
Well after we had walked 3 miles or so nearly into Fricourt we pulled up, there we waited in the pouring rain for 3 solid hours before they could find the officer in charge of the job,at last they produced him and the work he had for us was carrying timber and old sleepers from the railway to where they were making a corsway road about  of a mile away, the sleepers were not very heavy for they were old and well dried
