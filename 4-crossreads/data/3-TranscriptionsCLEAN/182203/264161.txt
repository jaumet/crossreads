  
 [Letterhead Pilrig, Burwood Road, Hawthorn. (Auburn Station).] November 2. 1914. I have much pleasure in stating that Miss Thistlethwaite has nursed many cases for me. She has always given the greatest satisfaction to me & to her patients, & I consider her well qualified for any nursing position she may seek. Lewis J. Balfour M.B.B.S.B.A.
