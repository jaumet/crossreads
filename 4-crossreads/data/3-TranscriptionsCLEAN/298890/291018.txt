  
 4. Sunday Cloudy day.  Got up at 1 pm  Went to Mrs French for tea Came back feeling very ill & ghastly depression.  Reeled to bed somehow. 
 5. Monday Ghastly depression.  Cold & dull.  Lady Byles took me to dinner & brought me back. 
 6. Tuesday Went to Minerva to cook.  Got thru day less painfully.  Called at Mrs. Aldridge's on way home.  F.P. called twice but did not get me.  Showers. 
 7. Wednesday 
 Worked all day at Minerva.  Showers & cold & grey.  In evening Phill Meggitt & Fred Post came
