 1st Australian General Hospital Egypt 8/11/15 
 My dear Mrs. Thistlethwaite, 
 Every good wish to you all for Xmas &amp; the best of luck for the New Year.  Edith &amp; I are still together, both very well, it is so nice still being together like this &amp; only hope we will remain together.  The weather now is just beautiful quite like an Australian Spring 
 Lots of love to you all 
 Yours very sincerely Dorothy Newton
