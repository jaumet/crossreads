<p>Express bound for Scotland.  Edie does not allow the grass to grow under her feet.  She is the picture of health and looks fresh and rosy.  In fact I have never seen her look better.  You cannot see her shoulders for stars.  She seems to have covered herself with a glory which tine will never dim.</p>
<p>Edie is a great girl &ndash; as lively as a kitten.</p>
