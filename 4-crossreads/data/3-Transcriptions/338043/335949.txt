<p>Summer's Day &ndash; The sea side resort is neither "Manly" or "Bournemouth" &amp; yet in many respects resembles both to a great degree &ndash; While lacking the size &amp; importance of Bournemouth and the sands &amp; surf of Manly, it is more picturesque than either &ndash; A resting &amp; pleasant resort for Parisians &amp; Tourists &ndash;<br/>The Coves indulge in a morning swim which is greatly enjoyed by all &ndash;<br/>In the afternoon we give our "second entertainment"</p>
