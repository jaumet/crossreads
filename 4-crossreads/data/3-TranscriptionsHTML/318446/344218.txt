The wretched turks were crowded 40 in a big marquee &amp; the scent of suffering humanity was awful inside.<br/>Finally at midday on the 11th we started to El Arish with this remaining 140 on board &amp; finally got rid of them to the clearing station &amp; reached camp here at about 11 p.m. fairly done up having had about 7 hours real sleep since the morning of the 8th.  It was a great success as a whole though we paid a large price.<br/>I believe the casualties for the whole of our force are about 600.  This unit lost two men, one shot through the chest &amp; the other Robertson who, poor chap, died late the same night &amp; was buried out there.<br/>The Anzac Division got a great reception on its return here.  The pipers of the whole 52nd Division turned out &amp; played them into &amp; across the wadi.  A very nice compliment.</p>
