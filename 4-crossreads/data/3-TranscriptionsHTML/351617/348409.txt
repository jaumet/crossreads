<p>Next morning Sam Charles and myself set off to Wippenhock to join up our Brigade again. On the way out we struck the 30th Battalion Q.Ms. store and managed to get a couple of tins of Machonochies, or Tinned Meat and vegetables, and some bread and Cheese as we had nothing to eat since the night before. About 1 pm we reached our destination, just in time for a hot dinner of stew and tea and it never hit the dixie before it was gone.</p>
