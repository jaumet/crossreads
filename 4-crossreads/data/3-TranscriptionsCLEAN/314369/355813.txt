  
 15 April 1915 Thursday 
 Repeat the dose but go on to the second tower 6 1/2 miles from camp. Return very tired   &  footsore. 
 1915 April 16 Friday 
 We receive reinforcements in the shape of about 15 officers from the CPRC that is The Ceylon Planters Rifle Corp. They are very nice fellows but not very good instructors.
