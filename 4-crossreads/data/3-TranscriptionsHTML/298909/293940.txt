<p>4/4/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Cold again last night &ndash; very pleasant during the day. Forenoon took a party through Matarich for a route march. Went for a route march across the desert in afternoon.</p>
<p>All leave stopped.</p>
<p>5th  Wednesday</p>
<p>Very nice day. Had charge of fatigue partys all day. Went to Aerodrome Camp after tea, to look for Lt Trickett, to sign some papers for me, but he had gone to Alexandria or some where. Still under orders to move out at a moments notice. Had a check roll 9-30 pm.
