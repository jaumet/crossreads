
24.8.16Thurs 24thOn account of our horses being innoculated against glanders we were not allowed to ride them yesterday, today a party from B & C Sqdn went swimming; Got my teeth back from the dentists this afternoon; Last night Ted Oates & Maybury came over & paid me a visit, it is rumoured we are to go to Bir-El-Abd at the end of this month to relieve the 3rd Brigade,a concert was held in the YMCA tonight & was one of the best I have heard;Renaldi was sent into Port Said a few days ago to get goods for the Officers mess & was held there on account of Cholera, but managed to get back today.
