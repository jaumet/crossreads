<p>this ancient hostelry beer cost 5d to 6d a pint and fairly good whisky &amp; rum were obtainable at reasonable rates. Some of the old rustics were taciturn &amp; hard to draw out. I think the outrageous rise in the price of beer &amp; the inferior quality of what they used to get at less than half the price had gone into their hearts like cold steel.<br/>"It's snowing hard tonight, sir," said I to an old codger who was sadly gazing at the great fire.<br/>"Yes."<br/>"Will you allow me to replenish your pot, my friend."<br/>"Thank you."</p>
