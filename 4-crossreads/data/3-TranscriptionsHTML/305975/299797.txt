<p>Friday April 18.4.19<br/>am well  Passed woolongong this morning 7.AM  arrived in Sydney harbour 12. oclock<br/>Saturday April 19.4.19<br/>am well  still in Sydney harbour in quarantine  nice warm day<br/>Sunday April. 20.4.19<br/>am well  still on Boat  Plenty of people about in Boats<br/>Monday April 21.4.19.<br/>am well  all excitement  this morning getting ready to go ashore  went ashore 2.PM and of to quarantine  long walk.  had Parcel and letter from Josie</p>
