<p>Argyle-Cut to the wharf.</p>
<p>I embarked at about 6 am on the morning of the 7th of October, Besides my Reinforcement there were Reinforcements for the following Battallions &amp; Corps &ndash; 1st.  2nd. 3rd. 4th. 17th. 18th. 19th. 20th. 30th. 45th. 46th. 52nd. 53rd. 55th. &ndash; and  56th. Battallions One Reinforcement of the Cycling Corps, A full Corps of Engineers and Army Medical Corps.</p>
<p>Altogether there was about 3,500 men on board. The Ceramic is the largest boat that has entered Sydney harbour up to the present date, She has about 16,000 tons displacement.</p>
<p>At 8 am everybody was on board &amp; everything was ready so we pulled out from the wharf &amp; anchored out in the stream till 4.40 pm, when we weighed anchor and passed out through the heads at 5pm.
