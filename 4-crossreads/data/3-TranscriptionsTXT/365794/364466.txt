
Tuesday 6 March 1917 [5 March 1918]Further news of S. Lee is no better.  We have been an unfortunate little party from our arrival.  S. Watson chest, I T. 104.4 etc. & very suggestive of pneumonia or malaria, McDonald Diphtheria & sent Shubrah, [also spelt Shubra] Lamb always in trouble & now this to crown all.A Reception given by Lady Wright was not for us yesterday as we are in isolation.  I am the senior A.A.N.S. here so was to go to represent us.Photography & a grey silk frock - Pt. 400 - have disposed of all my cash but one lives once only & it is worthwhile as my photos show.Off 2-5 & went town, played bridge with my dear pts. 7-8 p.m.  6 go to "Blighty" tomorrow.
