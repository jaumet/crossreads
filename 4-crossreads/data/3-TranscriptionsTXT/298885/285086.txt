
Fri Aug 11th - 16King George and Prince of Wales who were visiting troops along the Somme, passed through this village at 3PM
Sat Aug 12th - 16A quiet day for a change. Received a letter from Len telling me he is in hospital in England suffering from shell shock.
Mon Aug 14th - 16Left Bouzincourt at 9AM & at 1.30 arrived at our temporary wagon lines in Albert.  We were off again at 6PM. and in heavy rain and ankle deep in mud we landed at our destination . The spooks and gunners came straight on to battery
