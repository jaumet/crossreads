 9th. M.G. Coy. No. I Intelligence Report from 6am 30/11/16 to 1/12/16 at 6.am. Right Sector:- 1.30-1.40 pm. enemy trench mortars fired on 102nd Brigade lines. 2.30 -3.10 pm Heavy bombardment of enemy lines by trench mortars and Artillery in Left. Sector. During night enemy snipers &amp; M.G's active. No 5 Gun fired 750 on I 12 Central - I 12 d 0/7 X Roads, Trenches, Tracks, etc. No 6 gun fired 750 rounds I 17 d 8.4 - I 18 c 30 Road from [La Bleue-Bas Tron?] (Indirect) Left Sector:- Indirect fire:- I 6 b 8-9 roads and trenches round Le Temple. I 12 B  9-8 Trenches &amp; road. Light bombardment during afternoon- Intermittant- No observation of working parties. 
 [signed] [B.J Carey?] Capt. 9th M.G. Coy [Diarist has written in left hand margin: 10.2.20 Whitehead Part [ea/-/-?]
