<p>Sunday 29 April</p>
<p>Glorious Day.<br/>At 9 a.m. I went to assist Major Wooster in the Polling Booth.  Voting in connection with the Aust. Commonwealth Federal Elections takes place today.<br/>Got another chap to take my place after midday because of the Aust. Rules Football Match V Div. Supply Column.  Well, this time we got beaten.</p>
