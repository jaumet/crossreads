
IVOld Ross is away in England at present doing a school of gunnery.  I miss him a lot as he is great company  -I have not been able to hear anything about Terence Wise - He would probably be over here now so I shall write to Horseferry Road & try & ascertain his address.  You ask me for a few suggestions as to Pat's kit - There is really nothing he could buy to advantage in Australia except a bed valise.  These used to be made very well by S. Walder, the Tent maker.
