<p>21 October 1918 Monday (X to X crossed out)<br/>No.31 General Hospital Cairo<br/>At last we crossed the Canal &amp; in a Red x<br/> Train arrived at what was the old Australian 14 Gen Hos X now the native.<br/>This afternoon the wife came along &amp; told me all about the Bairn   I go out to Heliopolis tomorrow to see them.  She is fit &amp; well- I hope the Photos arrive of the KIDDY.  I am here for a few days &amp; will then get 14 days leave with Dolly instead of going to a Convalescent Home.  Cabled you tonight FLOURISHING CAIRO   I hope you understand X</p>
