<p>24 December, Wednesday</p>
<p>Not very busy. I buy wines &amp; send Home. We catch 10.10 tram &amp; after good journey although that &amp; heavy job parcel carrying we reach Forest Home. Bed at 2 am &amp; up at 5 am. We have nice merry day.</p>
<p>25 December, Thursday</p>
<p>Up at 5 am &amp; spend a Merry X'mas.</p>
<p>Plenty of music &amp; merry arie.</p>
