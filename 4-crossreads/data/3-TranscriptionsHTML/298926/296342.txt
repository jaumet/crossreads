<p>March 1st  Our leave has been extended to 5.30 and we go out every day now we have <br/>our clothes.  Today I went down to the theatre on palace Pier and saw a very interesting <br/>play called "Sappho" from Daudets book.  The acting was not too good but it was an <br/>agreeable change to us.</p>
