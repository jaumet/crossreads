 oppressor.  We had a very fine church parade on Sunday the whole Brigade was drawn up as usual but General Birdwood and his staff came along and the sermon was delivered by the Bishop of Jerusalem. News has just come through that their is fighting going on with the Turks on the Canal.  I wonder if we shall have the luck to get down there for the fun. Hal of course is full of ideas about our movements but George refuses to listen to them.  They seem to be getting very nasty in the hot weather. There are some queer little creatures here who turn eggs into rabbits and rabbits into pieces of colored paper and all that sort of thing and they murmur "Golla Golla Golla" all the time.  What language is that?
