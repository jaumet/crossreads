<p>March 7, 1916 Tuesday</p>
<p>Revielle at 6.30 as usual for me</p>
<p>Spent a decent night at Natsi &amp; up at 6 had breakfast then went &amp; got my machine &amp; set her going, everything went well for the 20 miles this side of Samalut then I broke my control &amp; had to scratch home on full air where I arrived a 8.30, messed about all day &amp; cleaned my machine.</p>
<p>Two aeroplanes left us today one while making a trial flight came a cropper she made a nose dive &amp; lobbed heavily.</p>
<p>Wrote a few letters then went to bed tired out.</p>
<p>March 8, 1916 Wednesday</p>
<p>82 wks of soldiering</p>
<p>Revielle as usual</p>
<p>Got orders to accompany a motor ambulance to Egbit Sisloun, where we had to get the body of the man that was drowned, calling first at the Miniah Hospital for the coffin, left at 9.30 &amp; had an uneventful trip to Etsa where we turned off as the railway road was closed had to make our tracks 3 times, we got there at 12 &amp; then had to box the body, he was a frightful size &amp; weight &amp; was very decomposed, got him on OK &amp; left at 1.30 for home, got there at 3.30 &amp; glad to get rid of the burden. He is to get a military funeral at 4 today.</p>
<p>Bed at 9.10</p>
