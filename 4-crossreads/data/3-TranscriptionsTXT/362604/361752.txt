Con & I went over to Mr. Fremantle in the Cathedral at 9 p.m.  It was very dark & we had to go through the Cloister Gate & through the Cloisters to get into the Cathedral.  When we got in there it was pitch dark too, so asthe moon was shining through the panes, myriads of Ghosts seemed to be hovering around.  It was very weird but a sensational experience.  We helped Mr. F. put some white chrysanthemums on the Altar in the morning chapel for a wedding.  Back at the old game, eh!  But in a different way for there are about 6 men
