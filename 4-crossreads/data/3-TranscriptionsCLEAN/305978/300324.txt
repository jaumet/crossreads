
Sunday 29th/Friends took us to Wigham by car, went up the manning river by boat.Had an invitation to tea from Mr. George Langly. Had a good evening, found the old fellow a real sport, he has a son away with the 6th L.Horse.
30th/ Left Taree for West Maitland  Had breakfast with the Gilberts. Said good-bye to friends & left for Sydney.
31st/ Spent day in Sydney & arrived back to camp for bed.
Sept 1stReceived Regimental No/1227.  Full dress parade, Payday, had a letter from the mater.
Sept 2nd.Riding all day
