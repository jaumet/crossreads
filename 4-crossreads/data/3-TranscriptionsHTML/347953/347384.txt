<p>on our way to Mecca. This gully was obviously well known to the Turks who continually &amp; unmercifully shelled it so long as their shells were available.<br/>Fatigue parties were soon at work &amp; in wonderfully short time had an adequate &amp; suitable number of roads, paths, &amp; saps for safe transportation of troops and supplies. Drainage, sandbag barracades, water-pipes &amp; tanks, field dressing stations, mortuaries, depot for food supply &amp; ammunition, were speedily &amp; temporarily placed in position giving it the appearance more of a great gold mining centre than a field of battle.</p>
