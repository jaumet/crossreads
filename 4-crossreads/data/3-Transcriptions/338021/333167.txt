<p>Lt Weston<br/>You can tell your men they will be relieved tomorrow.<br/>Get everything in order &amp; check all stores preparatory to handing over.<br/>The people who relieve you will bring only guns &amp; spare parts wallet, you will take the same out.<br/>Careful receipts for everything must be kept.<br/>Rum will be sent up later on to-day when it arrives.</p>
