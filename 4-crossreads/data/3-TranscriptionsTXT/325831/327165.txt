From Capt M M Sinclairc/o Bank of North Q'land4 Queen Victoria StLondon EC
No 3 Australian HospitalBrighton 9/Jan/17
Dear Mr Ferguson
I was glad to get your letter last week & to hear you were all well. You seem to have been having a lively time in Australia, with politics & coal strikes & what not - we were all sorry the vote was for "No" conscription but all the same fee the men will come along yet. Australia has done very well so far & must keep it up.
