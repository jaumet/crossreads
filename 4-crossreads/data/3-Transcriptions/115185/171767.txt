<p>Cape Town. I shall post this to Grandmother because if you have not left Sydney by any chance &ndash; you will read my scribble to Father. I can't write interesting letters because I'm not allowed to tell anything about what I see or what we do. This is really only to say I am well &amp; as happy as anyone could be during this terrible war - &amp; being away from all I love especially Lawrence &ndash; though I suppose considering the uncertainty of his future it is just as well that I am occupied - &amp; have</p>
