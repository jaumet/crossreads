<p>April 1917<br/>25th continued   The weather was fine although a little cloudy &amp; everybody seemed to be enjoying themselves.   A "wet" &amp; "dry" canteen had been erected &amp; was well patronised.   At night English beer was given out to the troops in the camp.<br/>26th   The usual "routine of the day" was carried out as usual &amp; this morning the company went for a route march to Freicourt where we had a bit of "company" drill, &amp; returned at noon.<br/>27th   We were duty section to-day &amp; "mounted a guard"   We are in an infantry camp &amp; are not liable for guard, but for purpose of training, this one is to be mounted daily.</p>
