I am keeping in splendid health & it is really surprising to note how little sickness there is considering the contrast of climates from Egypt to France.  Formerly we only saw rain once a month, & here we have been mucking round with wet feet for a fortnight at a time.Haven't heard since your letter of 6th Feby but suppose our mails will turn up in time.  Very strict Censorship here.  Must close,Love to all at home,from Leo.
