Men & women wear ornament around the waist, ears, arms, & head, & ankles.
They are very fond of beads & brass ornaments.
Never eat Chicken and eggs. They say a chicken eats dirt and worms. Grow no fowls. Eat fish catching them with a spear.
Buy wives with Cows or iron spears.
A man may have as many wives as he likes but mostly he has but one.
No slaves now, since the British came to the Soudan.
