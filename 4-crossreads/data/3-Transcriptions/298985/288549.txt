<p>Very dusty at the start but a few light showers made the traveling pleasant. One heavy shower in the evening nearly wet us through. A good many planes over at night. Can just hear the guns <strike>from</strike> again. Quite a cold night.</p>
<p>Monday 9th<br/>Improved the home by raising the roof otherwise dug the floor deeper. 50 men transfeered to 2nd Brigade from each Co</p>
<p>Tuesday 10th<br/>A showery day Walked around Mt St Quintin only a bit of a rise but slightly</p>
