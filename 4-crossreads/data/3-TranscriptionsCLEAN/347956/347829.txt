Fri 18thOn doing stables this morning   we were told we would have to fall in at 9.30 AM to be marched off for a bath;  We had a couple of miles to march & ended up at a prison where we received the best shower bath I have ever enjoyed in my life.  first cold & then hot & finished up cold;  I suppose colds will be prevalent here now;  after we had finished washing we had to wait till the rest of the Light Squadron finished & it was very interesting watching the native prisoners (in chains) being exercised & worked,  by the time we marched back to camp, it was
