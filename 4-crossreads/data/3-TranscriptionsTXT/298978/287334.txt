 Feb: 14 &ndash; 16 All drill. 
 Feb: 16 Visit Mosque Amur, Coptic Church and Greek Church &ndash; Bell and myself meet a Copt student, who shows us these different places of interest. 
 Feb: 17 Night alarm; the whole Battalion turns out, all tents packed up, everything prepared as though we were off to the Front &ndash; the whole taking something under an hour. 
 Feb: 18 Manoevres. 18 miles across the desert to the third tower on the Suez road. 
 Feb: 19 See Egyptian burial close to where we are. Signalling. Visit Boulac Museum with Bell. 
 Feb: 20 Visit Cairo &ndash; call upon Professor Adolfe Scarselli, a very clever artist, and his wife &ndash; it was a treat to be once more in a studio. 
 Feb: 21 Military duties. 
 Feb: 22 Visit the mosques of the Caliphs, and the Bazars &ndash; I had a splendid day with Bell  B.A. &ndash; also we visit the Palace Hotel Heliopolis &ndash; it is like Fairyland to be in this lovely building and see these quiet sisters at their duties.
