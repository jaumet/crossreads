<p>Louve 5-1-18<br/>I am now fully settled down with the 3rd again. I have no doubt that they are a good collection of men, &amp; so far I have been far happier with them than I was in England<br/>Paddy Waly the Irishman who was in the 10th with me is carrying on with general [indecipherable] in his usual humorous way<br/>We are running a [indecipherable] signal rest station and leave behind Kemmal on the marshes just west of Amenteres.
