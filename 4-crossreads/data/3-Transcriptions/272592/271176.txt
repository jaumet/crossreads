<p>30th.    Had my platoon out digging &amp; repairing trenches which had been damaged by the bombardment of yesterday, we were supposed to work from 8 to 11. but about 10.30 we ran out of sand bags &amp; so had to chuck it so we had a fair nights sleep for once in a while.</p>
<p>5 more details came in last night with Len when he brought his ration fatigue back to the trenches</p>
<p>After breakfast this morning I took my men up to the firing line to work on a job of trench building &amp; I'll bet we done more work in this one morning than has been done on this same job for the last 3 days the Capt reckoned</p>
