<p>Saturday 14th April 1917<br/>Grooming in the morning. Fritz shelling township no damage done except piece of shell struck railway engine boiler. Carting ammunition to forward dump.</p>
<p>Sunday 15th April<br/>Loading wagons wit ammunition and ready for taking away but orders cancelled, cleaning harness in the afternoon.</p>
