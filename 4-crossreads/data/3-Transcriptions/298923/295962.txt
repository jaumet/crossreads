<p>23 October 1918 Wednesday<br/>Villa Montrose Heliopolis &amp; your letter 25th Aug just received-   Baby is like me  Blue eyes &amp; a little pug nose.<br/>Tommy Orford was a Boundary Rider on Big Yanko Jerilderie <u>not</u> Hay.<br/>I hope you will let Bronte &amp; be in Melbourne see my letter in answer to Days long letter of Aug 28th.</p>
