<p>I have seen one solitary Taube today &amp; he was mighty soon chased back</p>
<p>29th.   Nothing much doing last night except now &amp; again our artillery would cut loose &amp; give old Fritz a couple of bad minutes, the shells would come like a thunderclap, one minute everything would be nice &amp; quiet the next nothing but a roaring crashing sound of bursting shells, this sort of thing naturally keeps old Fritz on the jump &amp; gives him what is known among us as nerves for he never knows when it is coming or where it will fall.</p>
