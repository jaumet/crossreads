  
 had several Tram rides free. They have quite a fine Puraide & baths on the ocean front  Some of the people throw us fruit cigarettes  There are 2 or 3 blacks to one white. Found quite a fine Mosque at night & saw them worship 
 Monday 16 The Coaling finished this morning. All the work is done by natives with much noise. Postcards fruit Etc was sold all the morning & thrown on to the boat, and a lot of fruit was given away especially by a Miss Campble of the YMCA. Left about 12.30 PM with much cheering & cooeing.
