<p>1915<br/>Sep.16 Thu.<br/>Up at 6 a.m. &amp; had a swim before breakfast.<br/>The rest of the day was devoted to 2 walks round the coast, reading, another swim, smoking &amp; a yarn to 2 Scouts of 1st Alexandria B-P Scouts who are acting as orderlies.<br/>My uniform consists of rag hat, light blue coat with sleeves rolled, short pants tucked up as far as they will go &amp; a pr. of white shoes.<br/>Weather &ndash; Snifter.</p>
