 1-2-17 trying to trace 8 miles of wire we left at Gererat &amp; which has gone astray, had no success bought some goods from the E.F.Canteen &amp; returned ; Lieut Sutherland was to have arrived today but did not turn up 
 2-2-17 Frid 2nd My throat is still a little sore, &amp; half the Regt are suffering the same complaint. 
 Lieut Sutherland came back this afternoon looking very thin, &amp; half dead; Browne went to the Camel Corps today &amp; saw Jock Davidson &amp; Moylan; A day or two ago the 5th Mtd. Bde sent in half a mile of wire in exchange for what we left out
