<p>Southall.</p>
<p>[17 Sun]<br/>London Hyde Park.</p>
<p>[18 Mon]<br/>Remained in hospital for a change.</p>
<p>[19 Tues]<br/>London<br/>Had a look at King George, Queen Mary, Prince of Wales &amp; Princess Mary driving along the Mall. Met W. Beaton in YMCA</p>
<p>[20 Wed]<br/>Spent the afternoon in London but went home early as it was very foggy.<br/>Got a lift from E. Broadway to Hospital by one of our cars.<br/>Would never have got home otherwise at <strike>cars</strike> traffic at stand still</p>
