<p>Diary of Camp Work.</p>
<p>[The following entries for 1st and 2nd August crossed through.]</p>
<p>Aug. 1st<br/>Arrived home from all night manoeuvre about 2nd Tower.  Rested all day, letter writing evening.</p>
<p>Aug. 2nd<br/>Lost a/c from 27/6/16 to Aug. 11th.</p>
<p>August 11th<br/>Reveille 4 a.m.  Marched through to Citadel, stayed all day, left 5 p.m., arriving Heliopolis 8-45 p.m.</p>
<p>Aug. 12<br/>Bayonet sharpened.</p>
<p>Aug. 25<br/>Packing all day.  Left Camp (Heliopolis) 7 p.m.  Marched to Zeitoun thence by train to Alexandria, detrained</p>
