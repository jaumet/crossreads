<p>Anzac Cove</p>
<p>The word "Anzac" was formed from the first letters of the words "Australian and New Zealand Army Corps" who were responsible for most of the fighting that took place at Gallipoli.</p>
<p>Anzac Cove was the name of the beach which runs in a semi-circular wave, and which lays about half a mile walk from Gape Tepe.</p>
