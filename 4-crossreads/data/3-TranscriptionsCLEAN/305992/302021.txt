We are delayed from docking as 4 cargo transports are here after having been torpedoed & awaiting their turn for repairs. No steel plates available in the country. Several consignments sent out from England & the vessels sunk with their cargo.All the streets decorated with arches & flags. The Egyptian natives dressed neat in their native dress. The streets crowded & all in very good order. All leave stopped for troops at the camps for fear of riots. So that the City of Alexandria is minus soldiers on leave. A very exceptional occurance & by what the shop people say very little business done.The average damage done to these ships (which are being repaired here) by torpedoes. Their side have holes about 30 ft X 20 ft. Their divisional bulksheads saved them from sinking.
