<p>In the evening we set out through the snow to St. Mary's Anglican Church, at Shrewton &amp; after a smart half-hours walk arrived there warm &amp; happy &amp; feeling extra well. Our heart melted within us when the pipe organ commenced on the celebrated 'Andante in G' by Batiste &amp; at once we were quite at home. After Service we had a fine walk home across the moonlit snow, the moon being well-nigh full, and with feet as warm as toast we tucked ourselves between the blankets as cosy as well could be.</p>
