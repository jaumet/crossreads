 Wednesday June 13.6.17 turned out 5 A.M. fed all company camels had shower and Breakfast done some washing rested till 11 A.M. went to canteen had three mugs of Beer it was very good to day had dinner 12.30 done some washing watered our camels very hot day rested till 5 P.M. had tea and turned in. 
 Thursday June 14.6.17 turned out 5.30 A.M.
