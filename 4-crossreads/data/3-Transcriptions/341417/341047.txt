I met a great lot of men in hospital who was in the thick of the push &amp; they say it was terrible the way Fritz was cut up and the dead were there in hundreds.<br/>Some of our divisions are cut up a good deal and they are breaking up some of the Battalions to make the others up to strength but I see by the papers that they are enlisting fairly well in Ausie but they will be a long time before they are in the line.<br/>There seem to be plenty of Yanks about London &amp; I have me very few in France but they have come over a good bit of late.<br/>Well the flowers were just lovely and the quantity of daffadills &amp; narcisis &amp; Tulips and the size are great. the flower sellers in</p>
