 19 Thursday Dark foggy day.  Eye tiresome bathed it in 
 20. Friday Met Ethel &amp; Claude at 7.30 &amp; saw 'Man from Toronto' at Duke of York's  Tully &amp; Iris Hoey, principals.  Dull dark day.  Fred called up about 6 &amp; had a yarn.  Mrs. Aldridge &amp; I had lunch at Minerva 
 21. Saturday Worked in office till 3.  &amp; got home &amp; wrote Xmas notes &amp; got my few parcels ready.  Dull day.
