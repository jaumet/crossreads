<p>17th October (Tuesday)</p>
<p>Coffee as usual from the girls as soon as we got out of bed.  Infantry are now moving out.  Yesterday afternoon we marched to Caestre and Flerte for exercise.  About 2 pm started on march to Bailleul</p>
