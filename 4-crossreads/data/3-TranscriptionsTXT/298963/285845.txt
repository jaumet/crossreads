5thWounded still arrive in large numbers, the bearers can scarcely keep up all fagged out.  Leaske killed, Ridgeway, Gibson, Haymen, Madocks, Marlow, Sgt. Clifton wounded in our lot, many bearers killed & wounded at all the posts.  Fritz barage lashed all night & nearly all day, it was awful, everyone was hit but not all wounded.  It seems miraculous that anyone came out alive.  In afternoon went for water & rations into village.  Fritz shelled it with 9.5 while we were there, I never saw such a scene in my life.
