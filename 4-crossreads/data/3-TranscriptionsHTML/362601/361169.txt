<p>6 March Thursday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Esme P. (20 yrs)</p>
<p>E. comes home &amp; Dick packs up for going away. I learn of New W.O.'s but I am not eligible. We talk about Trade &amp; other things. I do not send Photo away. I do some codging. We find a Batman. We are both up to see J.M.Y.'s.</p>
<p>7 March, Friday</p>
<p>W.<br/>I write several letters to M &amp; Tweam en[d] up eventually get one away &amp; go to [indecipherable] for dance &amp; get scaled them go to Y.W.C.A. until 10.45. Ellis thinks I have gone astray when I don't come home. We are all dopey &amp; sick &amp; I am very very much longing for Isle of Man &amp; can't sleep.</p>
<p>Dick Top goes away.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L.</p>
