July 5th. Wed.
A Big number of serious cases came in to-day. Numerous deaths

The people who have Cherry trees are all busy picking the cherries of the trees. Tall ladders are used to reach them.

The Australian troops are on the march : and are passing this way to-day. These chaps have just come over from Egypt. They have done a good bit of marching since landing in this country and many of them were foot sore and limped along as best they could, some even had their boots off

On the whole however they seem a fine body of chaps.

The streets are now crowded with them, they are billeted in town in all kind of places any place that a man can stretch himself in the village is used up.
