<p>February, 1917. Wednesday 7<br/>Day fine but same wind &amp; aerial activity &ndash; Felt very sick all day and had to carry wood for cooks in morning. Slept nearly all day and feeling better to-night &ndash; Filled [pallios] with straw and am very comfortable and warm.</p>
