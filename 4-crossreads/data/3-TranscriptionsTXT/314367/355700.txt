 I soon picked up all the points &amp; I can swear as well as any N.O &ndash; Don't tell R. this &ndash; It's only a naval detail &ndash; By the way Fitz told me Johnson had been up before him &amp; showed him my 'moderate' in his character sheet &ndash; Johnson said he'd like a committee!! to inquire into his ability &ndash; Fitz said he was satisfied as to my knowledge of J's abilities &amp; dismissed him &ndash; 'Right about turn &ndash; quick march' shouts the Corporal &amp; off poor &ndash; J &ndash; goes -  He is a fool  - Talk &ndash; Dinner &ndash; Bridge &amp; bed but coupled to this I had a lot to do looking after the men &amp; getting them comfortable &amp; fixing them up &ndash;  Eventually I got things square &ndash; Johnson here is the difficulty &ndash; They all hate him &amp; wont do what he says &ndash; They say they'll chuck him over the side one dark night &ndash; However I got thro &ndash; 
 Wednesday Jan 20  Fitz &amp; I swimming &ndash; Then a sick parade at 7.30 &ndash; Another at 8.30 &ndash; Got things squared up &ndash; Sent to G Hosp for a kit &amp; 'ammick left behind &ndash; Got a reply that the man was to return to Hosp  at once  &amp; they wouldn't return clothes &ndash; Right said No 1 (Nelson)
