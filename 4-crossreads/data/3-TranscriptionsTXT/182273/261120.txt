April   1918
14 SUN  Fine day left 5.45 am
in train all day. Rec. two
letters from Amy.
Secured plenty of salvage
on the trip & had a good
time.
15 MON  Arr. [Hongendem?] 4.am
unloaded & went via Haze-
brook to Morbecque. Bty went
straight into action about
10 min away. Fairly quiet.
Thousands of cavalry about.
16 TUES  Reveille 3. am standing
by for move - Fine day. Plenty
of Artillery & cavalry going
up. heavy firing. Rec. letters
from May W (Else P. 2) & Gladys.
17 WED  Refugees everywhere.
Dull day. heavy firing all
day & during last night
Heavies moving forward
Bty. firing best part of
the day. Posted F.S PC's
