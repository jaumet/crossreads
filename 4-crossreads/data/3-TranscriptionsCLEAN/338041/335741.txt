  
 Oct. 19th Motor trip to Poperinge (Car orderly). Wet & cold weather. 
 Oct 20th Australian Mail - Route march to village of Eecke - outgoing letters still prohibited. 
 Oct 21st Our Ambulance leaves "Steenworde" & after passing through Cassel & other villages we finish our days march at Noordpeene still in doubt as to our final destination - Our billets the worst we have had in France - Weather very cold & unsettled - Farmhouse - Concert 
 Oct. 22nd Resume march from Noordpeene via the large town of St Omer and we are billeted at Pilques - Australian Mail arrives. 
 Oct 23rd Football etc - further mail
