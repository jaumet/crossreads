Every now &amp; again I got up &amp; went outside to see what was going on; the searchlights <strike>every</strike> were <u>very</u> busy but strange to say the guns were silent.  After listening  intently I failed to hear engines of any sort either Zepp or 'planes.  After one of the jaunts out into the street outside I came in feeling convinced that the raid was over &amp; that the raiders had been driven off.  I had hardly expressed these sentiments before there was a terrific explosion, (altho' a mile away it seemed much closer).  Everything rattled on mantelpiece &amp; cupboards &amp; reports through this morning show that it was an aerial torpedo.
