  
 Tuesday 25/12/17  Wrote to Doff wishing her a Merry Xmas  Snowing- men on fatigues. 
 Wednesday 26/12/17. Quiet Day  wrote to Mother & Bas  Men on fatigue 
 Thursday 27/12/17  Still snowing & cold  saw [Bundock?] of [R.A.A.?]  men fatigues. 
 Friday 28/12/17. Quiet Day but very cold, reconnoitred front near La Basse Ville 
 Saturday 29/12/17  Received cable from Doff saying all well & wishing me Xmas Greetings.
