Anecdote
Derby's translation of Homer's Odyssey Book Vlll line 325 et Seq to 450
Vulcan Mars & Cytherea
I forged a snare of bands indisoluble, by no art to be untied, durance for ever firm.
Which not the gods themselves could see Journey thence to Lemnos.
Mars flew to the house of Vulcan.
Dawn streamed the net around them, labour exquisite of hands by ingenuity divine informed. Neptune promised to pay the money to Mars.
