<p>4.<br/>Blighty. Haven't heard anything more of Brissenden since he told me he was a sergt in 3rd Div H.Q. Flannery is with the 1st &amp; is very fit. The men like him immensely.</p>
<p>There are a lot of the old 3rd D.C.C.[Division Cyclist Corp]  knocking round &amp; they are all doing well.</p>
<p>Well goodbye for the present, Judge.</p>
<p>Remember me to Mrs Ferguson &amp; Dorothy</p>
<p>Yours very sincerely</p>
<p>John F Mant</p>
