<p>"Wimbledon"<br/>Greenknowe Avenue<br/>Potts Point<br/>4th Feb. 1915</p>
<p>My dear father,</p>
<p>I have been putting off this letter ever since I reached Sydney, but after seeing a march through the streets this morning, I am unable to put it off any longer.  Nearly all my friends who have not already gone to the front were in that march, &amp; of the very few that were not the following have volunteered or are going to as soon as they reach the February school of instruction:-  Walter MacCallum, Alan O'Connor, Caesar Lucas, Harper &amp; many others <strike>who</strike> you don't know.  I am</p>
