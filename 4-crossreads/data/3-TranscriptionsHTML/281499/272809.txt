<p>when we captured "Pozeries" the whole French line cheered right from the "Somme to Switzerland, &amp; Australia's name was made that day with both French &amp; English soldiers for they had proved themselves even better than their Gallipoli reputation led them to believe.<br/>11th.  This morning rose at the usual unearthly hour &amp; had breakfast, &amp; was then informed that my platoon would not be for duty today, but that I would be taking a party from another platoon of course I pricked up my ears at this &amp; over to the Sergt. Major I went &amp; if I did not tell him off" well may I be shot" he was trying to work a point on me but he found I was not so green as I looked &amp; the result was that he had to find another Corporal in my place, he got one from the bombers</p>
