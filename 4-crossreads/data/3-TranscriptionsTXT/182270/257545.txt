THURSDAY, AUGUST 21, 1919.
Fine with cold SW wind blowing.
I lopped in 4D then went round
all the paddocks. Met Linda at 4D
mill and we started burning off
in Middle paddock
Tickle finished poisoning in Top
paddock and started Middle.
Alan came out in big car & brought
Myee, Maisie and Jack Charley
with him.
