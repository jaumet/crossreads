I hope to get there soon. 11th  (Wed) Firing incessantly with rifle last night expected Turkish bomb attack. &ndash; One enemys guns smashed Rather cool during night &amp; feel really hungry &ndash; Dug out a new latrine during afternoon of 10th  Our Sergt Major shot couple Turk snipers. French aeroplane chased Taube but German too fast &ndash; Cleared out. Left at 3 pm for resting base after 48 hours in the trenches . 12 Few shells &ndash; went for dip in briny Jolly nice after 6 days trenches &amp; no wash  Hospital ships 4 cruisers in distance mules &amp; men busy drawing water etc.
