
2 November 1916 ThurVery busy all day attending to the sick & dressings of the wounded. Walked over to the 17th Field to see some of the boys.
3 November 1916 FriCounted as many as 47 aeroplanes up at once. Evidently they were trying to locate the guns that have been shelling Fricourt wood all day. Captain Alsopp temporarly attached in the dressing station. Powell of Enmore passes through sick.
