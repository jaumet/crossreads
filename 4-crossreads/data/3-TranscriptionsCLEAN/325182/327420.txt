24thWent before my Medical board this morning and was awarded a fortnights holiday and general service thereafter.  So I returned to the club packed my bag and caught the 1.15 p.m. train at Euston Station for Glasgow.  I changed at Carstairs and reached Edinburgh at 11.15 p.m. and came straight on here to Mrs. Evans place.
