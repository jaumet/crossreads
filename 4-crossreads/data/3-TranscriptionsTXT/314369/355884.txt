 4 September 1915 Saturday 
 Now that the excitement is over   &amp;  I have nothing to worry about I begin to realise how completely run down I am When I wake in the morning all my joints ache   &amp;  I feel terribly tired but the feeling wears off through the day I can see now it was only will power that was keeping us going   &amp;  as soon as you release that you simply go to pieces 
 1915 September 5 Sunday 14th After Trinity 
 Things go on as usual am feeling better generally Dad brings in some reading matter in the afternoon   &amp; has tea w/- us. One of our chaps comes in w/- the news that my officer Lt Ford has been shot dead [Previous paragraph shows question mark dated 10/9/15 and then annotation Incorrect  15/9/15 and initialled JCL] 
 700 wounded arrive Cairo today
