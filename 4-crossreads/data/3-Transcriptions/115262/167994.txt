<p>Wednesday 15 August 1917.</p>
<p>Usual routine this morning.</p>
<p>Had a fall &amp; have a bandaged arm &amp; crook back as the result.</p>
<p>Received &quot;The Bulletin&quot; from Joy and 1st parcel from home.</p>
<p>Everybody in different spirits this afternoon.</p>
<p>On account of arm was not on night <s>wo</s><span style="line-height: 1.6;"><s>rk</s> march tonight. </span></p>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.6;">Wrote letters instead. </span></p>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.6;">Printed photos this morning.</span></p>
