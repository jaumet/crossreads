 Friday 30th 1917. Up again at 7 am & paraded at 9 am &  was  all was marched up to the Parade Ground & had Company Drill until 12.15 Pm when we were dismissed for Dinner at 2 Pm we were again paraded for Roll Call & then dismissed for the Day.
