We returned to harbour at 2.30 PM.<br/>Aug 14th  We again carried out torpedo firing and various evolutions in the Flow during the day - the whole squadron taking part.<br/>Aug 16th  During the forenoon we coaled ship and during the afternoon Captain Dumaresq of the "Sydney" decided to carry into effect a Glee Party contested by the ships of the squadron, held on shore at the Y.M.C.A. Hut and adjudged by  a person from shore,- naturally "Birmingham" again won 1st beating us by 3 points - nevertheless the show was a complete success, and a credit to the Captain who was himself present and keenly interested &ndash; At 10 PM the squadron proceeded to carry out night firing.</p>
