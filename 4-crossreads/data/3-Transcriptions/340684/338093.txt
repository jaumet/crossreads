<p>367<br/>2<br/>Went away on their holidays.  I have written to them telling them not to alter any of their plans on my account as I know they need a change- Mr Malcolm Watson has asked me to go to Eastbourne at the end of August &ndash; he is going to write his family and J and R are going with them &ndash; Of course it is hard to tell where I shall be at the end of August but if I am able to go I shall only be too delighted &ndash; my eye is getting no attention but neither are the terrible</p>
