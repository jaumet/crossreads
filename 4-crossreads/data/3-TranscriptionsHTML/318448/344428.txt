<p>Things can never be the same again<br/>'History repeats itself'  you will say.<br/>Yes the same scene is laid &amp; the play is played over &amp; over again, but with the same cast, never. But the world advances steadily civilization grows, man improves his status . True, but the individual regrets always his youth, the golden years when he was so happy &amp; - did not know it<br/>The Thread is spun, woven &amp; cut, cut, woven, &amp; spun &amp; still Time remains inexorable, unrelenting, transient.</p>
