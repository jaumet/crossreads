<p>They took a blanket off each man today, leaving us with two.</p>
<p>It has been decided to have a book printed of our trip when we get to Melbourne, the Y.M.C.A are undertaking the job,  5/- is being asked for each copy, I put my name down for one &amp; chance what it turns out like</p>
<p>Have been passing through lots of flying fish today, I like watching them dodging the boat</p>
<p>Covered 266 miles yesterday a vast improvement on the last week</p>
<p>We are getting wireless news from Perth now, it has been decided that the W.A get off at Fremantle instead of going on to Melbourne.</p>
