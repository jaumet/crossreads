 21/9/16  Thursday 
 Nice day. Got a parcel from Mrs O'Kelly. Got letters from Ammi, Eileen, Frank, Aunty Lyons, Mrs O'Kelly &amp; J. Hagan. On Gas Picquet night &amp; day. 
 22/9/16  Friday 
 Nice day. Went down for some ashes for filling up around the Camp. Very Cold. Went to evening devotions at the Church. 
 23/9/16  Saturday 
 Nice day. Got orders to pack up &amp; be ready &ndash; Finished up by taking over Aid Post at "Rifle Villa" &ndash; at last some of us are with our own brigade &ndash; few calls during the night. Most comfortable post we have been in yet. 
 Sunday  24/9/16 
 Nice day. Lively trench warfare &ndash; Fritz putting them in freely to V.V. Sap. Very few casualtys. 
 Monday  25/9/16 
 Nice day &ndash; Fairly quiet &ndash; Very little sleep last night. Aeroplanes busy. 
 26/9/16  Tuesday 
 Nice day. Have a nasty cut in my hand, two stiches. Very quiet.
