 11th.  Another rotten day &amp; to make it worse it is snowing outside like the middle of winter I pity the poor devils in the trenches one cannot help thinking of them wounded are coming in whole sale but they all seem cheery 
 12th. Fed right up to the neck &amp; had a small row with the Dr. &amp; nurses.  I wanted to get out of it &amp; after a lot of argument the Dr. consented, how glad I was that evening
