<p>1917 MAY</p>
<p>16 WEDNESDAY</p>
<p>Raining all day &amp; miserable</p>
<p>Cleaning harness ect all day</p>
<p>&amp; as we have no conveniences</p>
<p>its not up to much. The harness</p>
<p>rusts if you look at it now</p>
<p>too, &amp; is very hard to keep clean.</p>
