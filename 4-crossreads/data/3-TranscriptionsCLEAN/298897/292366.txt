  
 It was my first entry into Belgium & I soon came on Belgian soldiers who were very English looking their Khaki uniforms helping the deception.  The various names of villages etc were of great length & most difficult to pronounce.  Altho' I failed to see Morris still I had an excellent day visiting old friends pedalling a distance of close on 50 miles. 
 Thursday 21 Sept Nothing of note the last few days except rain & mud.  Days are noticeably shorter & weather getting very chilly.  At night went into "Estairs" to see a concert at the 1st Cas. clearing station which was first rate.  All the fellows were dressed in Peirrot Costume & the various gags etc hitting off various members of the unit were much appreciated.  8th brigade moving into armenturing during the day which I fancy foreshadows a move for us all. 
 Tuesday 26th Sept. Things very quiet everywhere bar the Somme. Yesterday I attended the Gas school & spent a very interesting day.  During the morning we received a lecture which described the gas, how used & its results etc.  Before we managed to get anything like an efficient helmet we lost 15000 men.
