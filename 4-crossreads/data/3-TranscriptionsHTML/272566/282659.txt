<p>Notes</p>
<p>These notes are those with which I armed myself for the Court of Enquiry re <u>accident</u><br/>Routine Orders 396 of 28th July 1917 for 3rd Army Area<br/>10 miles per Hr through towns or villages and 25 m.p.h. elsewhere.<br/>When passing lorry parks or stationary transport reduce speed to 10 miles per hour.<br/>Routine Order 410 of 12th August.<br/>1.066 Headlights &ndash; Lights to be extinguished if enemy aircraft are in vicinity or when held up at level crossings.  These lights may be relit about &frac12; mile from the crossing</p>
