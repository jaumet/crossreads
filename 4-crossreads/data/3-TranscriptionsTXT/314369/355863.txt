 24 July 1915 Saturday 
 At work this morning from 5 am to 9 am. Carrying pipes   &amp;  laying them On again from midnight to 2.15 am then "Stand to" until 4 am until the Moon sets This job is permanent until further notice Rum issue at 6 pm 
 1915 July 25 Sunday 8th After Trinity 
 Great doings in camp our Brigadier has been promoted to Brigadier General John Monash Usual midnight job but not hard work this time
