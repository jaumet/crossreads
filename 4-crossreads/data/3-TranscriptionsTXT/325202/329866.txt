 June 23/17 Peronne Rd near Bapaume pulled in here again with the Company  travelling  from Behencourt in one long days trek travelling through Albert Pozieres, Le Sars. all last years battlefield is now a blaze of colour &ndash; covered with wildflowers  where  last winter one would have thought nothing would ever grow again &ndash; 
 Albert itself is being cleaned up, the least damaged houses being repaired and the inhabitants are beginning slowly to come back &ndash; a dreary homecoming it must be.
