<p>14 February 1915<br/>Sunday<br/>Quinquagesima Sunday</p>
<p>Wake up   &amp;  find Seang Bee in line with us but much closer to shore which is just in sight</p>
<p>9.30 Church parade</p>
<p>Sit on rail all afternoon and look at the sea which is dead calm</p>
<p>Course NW BY N all day play hide   &amp;  seek w/- Seang Bee.</p>
<p>1915 February 15<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Keep Seang Bee in sight all day<br/>Bee Keeping very close to reef our officers think it very dangerous. At about 12 noon we Pass Collier "Titanic" bound South give her a good old cheer.<br/>4 pm bee Turns inwards   &amp;  cuts into the inner passage to try   &amp; pick up some of the lost distance</p>
