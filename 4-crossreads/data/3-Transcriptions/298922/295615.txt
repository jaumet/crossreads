<p>Monday 12th November 1917</p>
<p>See Mrs. Wilkins after reporting A.I.F. 10 a.m. Another week to wait &amp; we sail <u>together</u> on a boat straight from London Docks on or about --- so I shall be with the little wife.</p>
<p>Posting this to let you know my movements. Love to all, F.</p>
