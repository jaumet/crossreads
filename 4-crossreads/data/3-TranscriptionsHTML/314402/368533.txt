<p>Night Report Sept 1/2 1915<br/>D. Coy 12th Batt</p>
<p>Events of Night Very quiet on our front 9th Btn patrol reported sniper close to centre of our line. Bomb thrown to shift him 2030.</p>
<p>Work done<br/>Work continued throughout on L4</p>
<p>Ammunition used 230</p>
<p>Patrols Advised of departure &amp; return of 9th Btn. patrol</p>
<p>Casualties NIL</p>
<p>Bombs 1</p>
