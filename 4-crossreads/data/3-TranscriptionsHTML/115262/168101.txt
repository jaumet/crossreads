<p>Friday 30&nbsp;November 1917.</p>
<p>Everything quiet until &quot;Jacko&quot; started with <s>6 inch</s> 5.9 inch Howitzer shells to shell the watering place of the British Yoemanry at 4 pm.</p>
<p>Shells went wide at first and landed short near Hospital tent. Cpl. &amp; W.O. knocked over by concussion of shell.</p>
<p>Village stopped several, killing &amp; wounding <s>natives</s> bedouin inhabitants.</p>
<p>Shelling ceased at dark.</p>
<p>On picquet at 6 pm.</p>
