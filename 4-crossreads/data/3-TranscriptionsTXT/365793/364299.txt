 July 18 about 3 hrs sleep, &amp; then we were roared on this m'ng for not turning out at 6 oclock. Left for London at 2.20 &amp; arrived at Wimbledon about 7 pm. Managed to find Uncles place after sundry inquiries, Phyl met me at the door. 
 19 Went to the City with Uncle (who took a day off) &amp; saw St Pauls, Houses of pl't, Westminster Abbey etc. &amp; in fact had a good tour round. There is the quietude of the old world cloisters where ones footsteps echo &amp; reecho along the dim, arched &ndash; [in margin] Wimbledon Park
