<p>1916 April 29<br/>Satsurday</p>
<p>Nothing of import. Make enquiries re the chances of getting my watch back. To go back tomorrow.<br/>Start meals in mess room. Great babel &amp; confusion.<br/>Dave Watts, D. Waddell &amp; Ern Noble come down to see me after tea. We all go up to Y.M.C.A. &amp; yarn over cocoa &amp; biscuits.  All look well &amp; fit.</p>
