<p>The landing</p>
<p>29)</p>
<p>our Bayonets fixed ready to charge. When the order is given Pte Thompson is near me and we deside to have Something to eat wich was Bulley Beef and Biscuits&nbsp; I had just pulled these out of my Havesack and put it on the ground when the order came to stand fast&nbsp; So we Did not get any lunch that time again&nbsp; The order came are you ready and we said yes&nbsp; Then follow me said Major Brown and with that we leapt out of the trench Singing out Hurrah and making all sorts of [row?]. we never got in</p>
