 24.2.15 
 Wed 24th Was with C Sq to-day, we were out with the troops &amp; they were trench digging, while we laid down in the shade of our horses &amp; watched them.  it was very warm during the middle of the day &amp; it was rather late when we arrived back in camp in fact it was too late to get paid,  wrote home after tea &amp; told plenty of lies amongst ourselves
