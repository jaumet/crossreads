 Tuesday July 31st We returned to Buire last Tuesday, on Monday I went to Amiens It is a very fine town the principal sight is the Cathedral. It presents a bold fa&ccedil;ade to the square & is very picturesque with carvings & statues around the entrance it is sandbagged to preserve it against Ariel Bombardment.
