  
 [Typed letter] "Dene Gully" Sept. 12. 1916. 
 Dear Alan Just a line or to. "To wish you a merry Christmas".?? - Oh Da - "I always forget to go on to a fresh line. It must be getting very cold over that way now? Just getting Hot out here. 
 Hazel, Mother, have gone into town so I have to get the meals. Bill's deserted me, gone over to see his friends. "OH" "I forgot to tell you that I am at home for a holiday "Sports Day" you see. The last we heard of Dene was from Fremantle (w.a.) He then thought He was going round the Cape. 
 At Cooking  Class . we are all going in for a compition of "scone" making the one that makes the best scones gets a watch?? - :://// and we are going to send the scones to the "Roseville" Camp 
 The best going to the "Colonial" of cause. "Strike me pink" I pity the poor chap that ever gets mine. He won't live long to tell the story. 
 I am sending some mittens along soon. I think they are pretty big. so they might come in handy for "BED-SOCKS.??? - ::: you never know your luck. Tommy wrote to Hazel the other day and said that I owed Him a letter or two, He owes me a " dozen ". "OH" - Arthur Wynn is "MARRIED" "???????? :::: - - - -?? I dont know who to though. 
 Well, here s Bill He wants his dinner I suppose, so I will have to say good-bye, 
 xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx 
 With heaps of love from "Wilga" -
