<p>Our crowd left in various conveyances such as Wagons, motor lorries and bikes.</p>
<p>As usual it was my job to push one of the latter which met with my approval, especially on such a trip as this one, as having new country to travel over and being on our own we can have a good look round</p>
<p>On 21st we covered our first day's run of 35 kilos to Boutillerie</p>
<p>Nov 22nd Reveille at 5AM left 6-15 and after 40 kilos arrived at Cappy.</p>
<p>Nov 23rd Reveille 5AM left 6AM.</p>
