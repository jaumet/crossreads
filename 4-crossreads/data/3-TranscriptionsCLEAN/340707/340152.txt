 The hall is partly underground - with a vaulted roof in imitation masonry & at far end a little stage on left a box for director of performance overhead a window whence drinks are drawn & on the other side a staircase used for performers. [Small sketch in right-hand margin]. 
 The walls are adorned with black cats in all position & by drawings by renowned artists probably the proprietors. A representation of jazz band by all nationalities is excellently arranged & the badinage is fare I really amused myself very much & so did my American friend though he could not follow many items. 
 On our return we are stopped many times but are [indecipherable] to the alluring beauty that frequents Paris by night & near home meet 2 student & then I accompany them to "Rue Mezet & have a drink & then making arrangements to meet them at La Source go home having experienced an strike stricken person on my way home.
