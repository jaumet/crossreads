<p>Tuesday 19th Nov.</p>
<p>Cooking all week.  Nothing startling happened.  Went to R.G.A. Concert one night good.  Had a parcel from Aunt Mima.</p>
<p>Monday 25th</p>
<p>Whalley has the cooking again, I am cleaning up the lecture hall.</p>
<p>Tuesday 26th</p>
<p>Went into Havre with Newton.  A wet afternoon.  Had tea at Y.M. &amp; came home early.  The A.I.F. educational</p>
