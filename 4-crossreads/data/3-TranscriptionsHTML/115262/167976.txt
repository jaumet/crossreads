<p>Saturday 28 July 1917.</p>
<p>Feeling crook this morning. Reveille 4 a.m. Breakfast 6 a.m. Baggage guard on wagon &amp; left at 7.30 a.m. to Moascar Station. The rest of C.B.F.A. came along later. Entrained at 10.20 a.m. Arrived at Cairo and disentrained at &frac14; to 2 p.m.</p>
<p>Baggage guard again out to Main Abbassia Camp. Nigger driver tried to take me down. Feeling all <s>all</s> out to it.&nbsp;</p>
<p>All the way along tlhe railway line the fields are highly cultivated &amp; as the result beautiful crops are on the land. Saw guns used by Napoleon and in the distance saw Pyramids.</p>
<p>2 of the boys L.H.F.A. who left a week or so ago have come to grief &ndash; 1 killed &amp; 1 wounded so&nbsp;we hear.</p>
