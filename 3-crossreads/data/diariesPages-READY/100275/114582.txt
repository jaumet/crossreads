31st. Last night a most wonderful thing happened to me a messenger came down said that Capt Mackenzie wanted to see me I thought at the time that it was something about my second stripe he wanted, but when I got to him he told me the Colonel wanted to see me at Headquarters at once, what on earth is the matter now I thought; as I went round, arrived there I reported at once the Colonel told me to wait a little he would see me, there was another chap waiting there also he had been sent round for the same as I had after a little time the Adjutant came outside called me in, I entered
