
Tuesday 3rd September 1917 [1918]Taken to Cairo by Lt. Morrice (1st A.L.H.) met Capt. Chitty at Groppis, Capt. John son (A.H.Q.) 5 p.m. tea with Lt. Wanden (goes up the line tomorrow) very nice boy & enjoyable afternoon.Hair treated (4th occasion) then bought bread, Pt. 21/2 Groppi's & home with Lt. Morrice & S. Nichol.
