<p>7/10/16  Saturday</p>
<p>Dull day &ndash; On Picquet. Fritz hopped the parapet at Levantie &amp; Armentiers &ndash; he got particular hell in both places &ndash; Very few casualtys came in.</p>
<p>8/10/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Dull day &ndash; Went out to Rifle Villa about 9 a.m. Lively trench warfare &ndash; Had a few calls.</p>
<p>9/10/16  Monday</p>
<p>Drizzling rain. Heavy bombardment from both sides &ndash; Got a letter from M. Single. Fritz very busy with deadly minniewurzen, he got some M.G. fellows &ndash; one fellow named Jim Harsthorn, although badly injured injured, was a model patient, he was actually singing &amp; joking &ndash; as he said to keep himself awake. Les Bowden took on Groom to A.D.M.S.</p>
<p>10/10/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Nice day &ndash; A few calls during the night &ndash; Fritz putting over more minnies than shells on supports &ndash; Wrote to Frank, Anne &amp; M.
