<p>1915<br/>May. 12 Wed.<br/>Last night under the auspices of the Y.M.C.A. a fine concert was held in the main hall. Mr Virgo, Gen. Sec. of London Y.M.C.A., conducted proceedings &ndash; he delivered a fine address &amp; also played piano &amp; sang &ndash; there is no doubt that he is the man for his job.<br/>Busy to-day getting ready 70 patients for transfer to-morrow to the Sultan's Palace, Helouan which has been converted into a Convalescent Depot.<br/>A fine photo. was taken this afternoon of Patients, Nursing Orderlies, <u>OFFICE STAFF,</u> Nursing Sisters, Officers etc.</p>
