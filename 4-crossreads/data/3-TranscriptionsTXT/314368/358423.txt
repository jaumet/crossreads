I have not heard from her for some time, but am expecting a letter daily.  We have received very few letters within the last weeks but here are rumours of a big Australian mail shortly.  I was very sorry to see that conscription had been turned down again. I had a vote on the question this time, but it turned out to be a lost cause. I think most of the soldiers voted yes, although no doubt there were many who did not.
