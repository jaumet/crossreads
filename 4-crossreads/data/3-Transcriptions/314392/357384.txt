<p>France and joined the Battalion at Bussy les Daours.<br/>Everything is quiet, but from what I can hear I guess there is to be something doing soon-Just my luck I always find trouble.<br/>July 31 I am more than convinced now that a big stunt is coming soon. Tanks are rolling in galore. Big guns come up from the base by every train and ammunition columns are going up to the line all day and night.<br/>Aug1st  Shifted camp to the village of Boves. Here we camped in an old chateau and slept</p>
