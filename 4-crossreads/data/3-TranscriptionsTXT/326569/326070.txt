Thursday, 21/6/17Reveille 4.30 a.m., breakfast 5.15 & moved off 6 a.m.  Marched 12 miles to near Shaftesbury via Iwerne Minister, Sturminister Newton.  Rained heavily in morning & got wet through.  In afternoon went with Taylor on the big hill & then down to the bridge.  Had tea at a farm house which we enjoyed immensely, cost 1/6 each.  Turned out fine in afternoon.  Not allowed into Shaftesbury although several tried to get in.
