 We know we are close to land we can't see it. 8.30am Have just sighted a collier who told us we are at Broken Bay so we are turning about now. 10am Manly is sighted on our right and Sydney Heads are not far distant. 10.15 Have just entered the heads and are heading for Coasters Bay  11am The health officer has been aboard and we are now on our way to the wharf.
