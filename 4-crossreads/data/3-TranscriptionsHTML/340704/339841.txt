<p>September 1917<br/>Thursday 20<br/>Went to Marakeb for two chaps from Rest Camp<br/>15 miles each way</p>
<p>Friday 21<br/>Out on outpost (day)<br/>Fired upon<br/>These day outpost stunts are funny &ndash; like draughts &ndash; our move today to hill 630 &ndash; turkos move next day on same hill</p>
