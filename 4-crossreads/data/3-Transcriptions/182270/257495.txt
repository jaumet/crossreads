
<p>WEDNESDAY, JULY 2, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm with very heavy frost</p>
<p>in morning. Burning off in the</p>
<p>Middle in morning and round</p>
<p>stock in afternoon.</p>
<p>Tickle collected sheep skins &amp; brought</p>
<p>them up in the dray. Put new cut</p>
<p>off wires in 4D &amp; Mountain mills</p>
