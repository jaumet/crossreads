
Went to London & Edinburgh, had a good time but was glad to get back.
March 13thLeft Upwey Jnct. for Plymouth & embarked on H.M.S. Dunvegan Castle same day.
March 14thPulled out into stream to await completion of convoy.
March 15thHigh wind & choppy sea, issued w/- Red X kit.
March 16thSailed at 5 p.m.  Large convoy & escort.Passed Eddystone Lighthouse at 6 p.m., ship rolling a bit.
