<p>1916 November 17<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Visit from New Lieut Col. Col Shaw has been transferred to C. C. S. at Estairs &amp; is succeeded by L. Col. Williams. He has a good man to replace. Short squad drill. Ice &amp; frost continues. The coldest day ever I experienced, a piercing wind blowing all day.  Cuts Australians up this weather. To bed early &amp; read local papers 2nd batch arrives Sent by Cis . Sept 20. also 4 letters.</p>
