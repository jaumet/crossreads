<p>Tuesday 20/11/17&nbsp; Complained to [Jess?] about Waters- posted picquets, Morning Walk</p>
<p>Wednesday 21/11/17. Gen&#39;l White visited the camp. Rode to Steenvoorde &amp; had dinner there. Walk</p>
<p>Thursday 22/11/17&nbsp; Orders received to close camp, general clean up.</p>
<p>Friday 23/11/17. Left for Division reported through Bde &amp; joined up Battalion. Walk</p>
<p>Saturday 24/11/17&nbsp; Had a good look around- Letter from Doff- Had game of H ball against 25<sup>th</sup>.</p>
