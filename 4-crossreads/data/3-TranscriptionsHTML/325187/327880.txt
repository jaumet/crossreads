<p>[Wednesday 17 May 1916]</p>
<p>Signaller shot today   Scattered Turks all around- 120&deg;  in the shade 1 gallon Water per man-<br/>160 Camels arrived to take us on<br/>No more roads<br/>No more Railway<br/>No more Pipe Line<br/>Only wells &amp; brackish water<br/>60 men Sunstroke<br/>20 more sick<br/>survival of the fittest now, I prefer the heat but I like water.  The unfit will go to the Wall out here- 2 Camels &amp; 3 horses died -coming out</p>
