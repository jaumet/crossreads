Mrs. Sanders, Eileen, very good gifts including sweets, books, underwear, soap hdkfs. Action quiet all day night.
Nov. 8th Monday
Very much colder duller. Front quiet.
Nov. 9th Tuesday
Weather fair. Front quiet. Naval small craft active especially about midday. Cruisers firing heavy broadsides during afternoon onto Gun ridge.
Nov. 10th Wednesday
Rain threatening. Turks busy with hand bombs about 10 a.m. on Posts 3 6, quietened by Garland Catapult.
Nov. 11th Thursday
Much like rain, but
