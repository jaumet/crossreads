<p>A medical officer came on board &amp; inspected the hands &amp; arms of everybody on board to satisfy himself that no small pox existed on board. Despite the fact that we have so far a very clean health bill it was reported in the W.A <strike>newspaper</strike> Times that a case of small pox had broken out on our boat soon after leaving Colombo.</p>
