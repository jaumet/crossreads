 Tuesday 14 August 1917. 
 Drill order only with camels before breakfast. Posted Registered Parcel to Mother. 
 Received &quot;Table Talk&quot; from Joy this afternoon. 
 Had snap taken on camel this afternoon. 
 After feeding parade went to Cairo &amp; got photographic material for taking &quot;up the line&quot;. 
 Tonight the light saving action came into force &amp; it makes a difference to Cairo city.
