<p>I'll get a chance to post this  -<br/>All has gone very well so far  -  No hitch  -  Our boat is very slow &amp; that's a draw back  -<br/>Keep this epistle of mine &amp; number it for I may want to refer to it  -  I want to get a knowledge of &amp; be able to express that knowledge about the finest navy amp; the biggest navy of all time. We are a humble unit but our chaps of course know the ropes &amp; I'm here to learn all I can of men &amp; ships &amp; war  -  They say our two submarines are the finest in the navy  -  &amp; that the Commanders Besant &amp; Stoker are two very fine sailors &amp; fighters  -<br/>I think of you all &amp; what's going on at my little home  -  Kiss the children &amp; tell Rawdon I'm always thinking of him &amp; trust he's a fine boy<br/>&amp; learning to be punctual &amp; generous &amp; to help you &amp; Maidie - don't forget a quiet pony - Maidie is a fine girl &amp; will come on all right  -  We are all cheerful optimists on the Upolu &amp; the world goes all the better with us because of the cheeriness of everyone  -  Goodbye  -  Don't talk <u>about our equipment</u> &amp; be vy  -  careful not to tell the <u>press anything</u>  -<br/>No leave allowed  -  (2 pm)  -<br/>Your lvg husband<br/>Fred</p>
