  
 1915 May. 8 Sat. In afternoon went out with motor ambulance load of patients to Light Horse Camp, Heliopolis Race-course & base Details Camp, Zeitoun - Called in at No. 1 General Hospital, Heliopolis & left a few useful odds & ends with Val. Marshall (to-days good turn ! What!).  Very fine run through Gizeh, Gezireh, Old Cairo, Cairo, Abbassia, Heliopolis, Zeitoun, Abbassia, Cairo, to Palace Hotel, Gezireh. Left car here & caught a tram back to Pyramids. In evening Y.M.C.A. organised a fine concert for the patients in smoking lounge at Mena House.
