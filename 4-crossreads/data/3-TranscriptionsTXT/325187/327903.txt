
[Friday 9 June 1916[
Our 2nd in Command just got a D.S.O., so the Regiment is pleased with itself-Entrenching in this drift sand is heart breaking work, each day the Trench fills up with the wind-Just heard of KITCHENERS death, is it true? Or like Enver Pasha??Moved out to Reconnoitre in force C. 3.45Camped at 10 AM Sahia  Hear Kitcheners death is true & we are wearing Black Bands-Left againRecd letters from England 1 from Laura April 28 Mother Easter Sunday 23rd one from Day
