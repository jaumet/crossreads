<p>He then becomes askance &amp; inquisitive preparatory to the attack which on its actual commencement he becomes callous, indifferent  &amp; determined being very cautious knowing full well that every moment  [indecipherable] means the sacrifice of his life.  This is followed by hysterical cries or movements subsequent to the final dash when pure madness, excitement, &amp; carelessness control his being.  This follows the consolidating &amp;  duty to the fallen, the dying  &amp; the wounded.</p>
