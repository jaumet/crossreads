<p>Nov. 21st.<br/>Return of Bearer to the Quarry &ndash; Preparations for departure &ndash; A miserable train trip followed by a more miserable march &ndash; "Where are we? &ndash; Somewhere in France". The thoroughness of Fritz's positions "avant l'offensive"! (Dugouts etc) &ndash; Back to the good old Huts.</p>
<p>Nov. 22nd.<br/>We lose our O.C. much to our regret. We are all decidedly pleased to be away from the war &amp; its Horrors &ndash; A trip to a neighbouring village - "Vaudeville".</p>
<p>Nov. 23rd.<br/>General duties &ndash; News of "Britannic" &ndash; An expensive bath in Albert. &ndash; Promotion of Capt. Donald &ndash; Our air-service.</p>
<p>Nov. 24th.<br/>Mail from London &amp; Home &ndash; Our recent camp &amp; its surroundings</p>
