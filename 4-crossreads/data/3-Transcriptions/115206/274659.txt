<p>March 1918<br/>Saturday 30</p>
<p>On the road.<br/>Reveille at 2.15am. journeyed to Poperinge and entrained at 7.30am . Passed through Hazebroack, Ca&mdash;tie, Melin, St.Pol, and Buiserbrouge, arrived at Doulens at 5pm.<br/>Owing to heavy traffic train held up until 12 midnight. Feeling fairly tired.<br/>Boys fairly happy under existing circumstances.<br/>Raining very hard.<br/>Ground fairly sodden.<br/>Truck with old "Morse Code" aboard had a slight mishap &ndash; ran off line "Morse Code" fairly excited as a result of it.</p>
