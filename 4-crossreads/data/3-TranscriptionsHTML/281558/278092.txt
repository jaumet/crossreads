<p>Tuesday March 5th</p>
<p>Misery : on till noon : worked on our shanty till tea then bed :</p>
<p>Wednesday March 6th</p>
<p>Misery : on till noon : usual routine</p>
<p>Thursday March 7th &amp; Friday March 8th</p>
<p>Misery : on till noon each day : nothing much doing : Fritz over on Friday night but didn't drop anything :</p>
<p>Saturday March 9th</p>
<p>Misery : frosty morn's but glorious days : usual routine : had a stroll over some old Fritz gun positions &amp; our guns have knocked things about some :</p>
<p>Sunday March 10th</p>
<p>Misery : had a stroll over the dump here : Fritz has lost at least one train (on fire) &amp; a fair bit of light railway material but the rest of the stuff looks as though he has set it on fire : on till noon</p>
