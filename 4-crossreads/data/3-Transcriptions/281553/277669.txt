<p>18th Saturday, December<br/>Day very dull, heavy cloud, fire still fierce.  Water bottles filled midday.  Iron rations afternoon.  Paraded 4 p.m. ready for Beach.  Moved off at dusk.</p>
<p>Continued on loose leaves (18/12 to 6/1/17) thence to Back of Page with 22nd May's records.</p>
<p>By 618 B Coy, 17th Bn.<br/>Donald MacDonald</p>
<p>If found return to above or 'Avondale', Victoria Parade, Manly, N.S.W., Australia.</p>
