<p>rifle &amp; gas helmet inspection in the billets : paid 20 Francs in afternoon : Gib Alf &amp; self walked to the 18th after tea : good feed of greens before bed :</p>
<p>Friday Aug 3rd</p>
<p>Westbecourt : raining : reveille 6.30 am : inspection in the billets : news of the "push" but can't get a paper anywhere : went to our house &amp; Madam supplied us with coffee &amp; bread : Mddle tres bien : very nice hour : feed of fruit after tea :</p>
<p>Saturday Aug 4th</p>
<p>Westbecourt : showery : reveille 6.30 am : fell in after dinner for a route march : we S.B. did not go : Gib &amp; I had a fine stroll &amp; met an old woman with a flock of sheep : got a "P" : interesting talk : dropped in at her daughter's cottage on our way back</p>
