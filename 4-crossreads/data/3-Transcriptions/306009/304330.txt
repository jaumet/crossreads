<p>1915<br/>May. 24 Mon.<br/>Kept pretty busy with work to-day which was a public holiday in Cairo. - Was unable to attend Scout display in the afternoon.<br/>Great rejoicing by the Italian community on the declaration of war by Italy against Austria.<br/>Fine Empire Day concert held at the Hospital in the evening &ndash; many distinguished people present &ndash; songs in English, French &amp; Welsh and many other items &ndash; Flags of the Allies conspicuous.</p>
