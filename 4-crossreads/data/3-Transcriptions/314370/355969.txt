<p>January, 1916<br/>Thursday 20<br/>Work as usual<br/>Semaphore Exam after dinner<br/>I made a little table &amp; book shelf this evening &amp; it looks O.K. with a white cover on top &amp; some red stuff on the sides &amp; front.</p>
<p>Friday 21<br/>Work as usual in the morning &amp; after dinner march to Abbassia Range to fire Grouping &amp; Traversing practice<br/>return in time for tea</p>
