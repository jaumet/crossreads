No 142 Cont. 3-9-16
Para 4 Smoking, striking matches, use of unmasked lights throughout whole brigade area has already caused hostile machine gun fire, direct and indirect - this must cease absolutely except where shielded by Buildings or Dugouts from any possibility of enemy observation. All ranksmust be immediately acquainted with this and offenders punished.
5 All fortifications of pay are to be entered in paybook at full daily rate of pay minusdeferred pay - The fortification of deferred pay will be adjusted at the final settlement of soldiers account.

Signed
C Guilfoyle
