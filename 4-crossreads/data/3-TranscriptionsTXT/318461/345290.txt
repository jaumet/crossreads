 Wednesday 6/6/17 felt a bit stiff this morning, same kind of weather as yesterday, tried to rain early but cleared off. Terrific Bombardment going on last night &amp; still going on; its on all along the line from Cambrai to Ypres. Saw that Brazil is now in it surely it can't last much longer. Wrote to Jack Tony &amp; Carol this morning. Got new Pants &amp; Boots half soled so am looking decent again. Pay 22/5 &pound;26.12.0. Thunderstorm to night so no Taubes. 
 Thursday 7/6/17 lovely Morning, did not rain much but cooled the air, going to be hot today. 7 of our Blighters to be Court Martialed today, means 3 &amp; 5 years for some of them The cat wouldn't do two of them any harm, foul mouthed brutes. Am going to play cricket this afternoon will suffer for it tomorrow. Pay. 23/5 &pound;26.13.0 sent letters to Mrs Parsons &amp; Jack Tony &amp; Carol. have had one letter since coming out, that is one other letter &ndash; from Nell since May 1st. the last  May 27th &amp; yet they tell me they write once a week. Had a game of cricket this Afternoon 2nd for 15 yrs made Top score &amp; got 5 wickets the old un isn't a back no. yet Nice &amp; cool this evening. Div. Concert on to night. 
 Friday 8/6/17 Route March again today 10 miles full Pack up, that's it, come out for spell &amp; get your inside walked off, ass is no name for it, great fighting going on big Push at Ypres. Beautiful morning got back about 12.15 started 8.15. done up. Heat awful also dust, nearly fell out. Beer in to day, some of the boys will be tight tonight. Got fearfully stiff must have been the cricket the day before. looks like heavy rain, &amp; the guns going it, big barrage about 5 P.M. No word of leave yet. Pay 24/5 &pound;26.14.0
