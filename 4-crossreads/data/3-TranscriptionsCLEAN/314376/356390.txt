If one looked up in the air he could see them coming over our trenches in a long curve and as they could be seen so easily it was also possible to judge where they would land and run as hard as possible from that place.
I was not at all sorry to see the advance guard of another battn come up to "take over" from us. Six days continuous strain in the eyes had played up with me and I wanted a rest.
