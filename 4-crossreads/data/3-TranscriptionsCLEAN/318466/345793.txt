
Sunday 19 May 1918Major Lucas & Anslow, Auntie Lu, Aunty Ema & Rose, Ede & Dorothy came to aid.Air Raid 11 p.m.
Monday 20 May 1918Reached Hospital at 3 p.m.  The Air Raid last night caused quite a stir here.  No damage done though.
