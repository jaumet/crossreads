<p>somewhere in France.  I must leave off soon &amp; write to his sister.  He or I had an agreement once, that if anything happened either of us the other would write home.  I wish I could see him but alas &ndash; I cannot yet I feel him near and Harry too, has saved his brother at the cost of all that made life worth living.  Poor Harry I feel sorry <strike>from</strike> for him.  He has an old mother to keep &amp; someone else is waiting for Harry, far away over the water.</p>
