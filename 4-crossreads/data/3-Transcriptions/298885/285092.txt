<p>bed at 10.30 I was told to report to the Battery at once and after an eventful ride along the dark roads and through scrub arrived there at 4AM. It was "NO, Bon"</p>
<p>Mon Sep 4th &ndash; 16<br/>Today there has been a continual fall of 5.9 all around us, the roof of our telephone dugout is covered with clods of earth showered on as each shell hits the ground.</p>
<p>Tues Sep 5th &ndash; 16<br/>3 spooks and myself came forward to OP in front line we are here for a week. and do not feel too comfortable as the front line is mined for several hundred</p>
