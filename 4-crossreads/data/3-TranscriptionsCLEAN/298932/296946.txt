  
 Monday 27 A parcel from Dad which I shared with the folk of the good farm 'Clarmary'. Very wet day. Tuesday 28 A letter from Emma The mail of late as been coming very slowly but I suppose the hun as sank some mail boats Wednesday 29 The Division reviewed to day by the Com. In Chief of the British Army Sir Douglas Haig. The 17th Batt won his confidence Thursday 30 A great boy for boils twelve week of them & still got two to keep me company
