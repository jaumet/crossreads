<p>3-8-16<br/>Thurs 3rd<br/>3 enemy 'planes came over this morning, &amp; our Bristol plane was well over them &amp; opened fire on them but his machine gun jammed on him &amp; he got away &amp; came down &amp; was not hurt although he got a couple of bullets through his plane.<br/>A Turkish officer captured yesterday says 27 enemy planes are coming over to pay us a visit tomorrow;  I received a birthday parcel from home this morning;  We are expecting the Turks to attack here at any time now probably tomorrow morning (the anniversary of the war)</p>
