<p>April 1917<br/>Monday 9<br/>Moved out to Tel el Jimmi as screen for 9th LH who were digging reservoirs by night; in preparation for the next big offensive on Gaza</p>
<p>Tuesday 10<br/>Movved out to Tel el Jimmi we dug the reservoirs tonight<br/>to hold water for the big push.</p>
