 7th day out Have been orderly corporal to-day & I have been buzzing round everywhere.  I have been writing to Bess & Ida Nelson & I will write a few lines to you before I go to bed.  We sighted land again to-day, the first time for 3 days & we are now steaming along the coast.  We expect to be in port tomorrow so will drop you another line while I am ashore.
