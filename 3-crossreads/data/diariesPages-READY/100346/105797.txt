Wednesday Nov 14th
London : "the end of a perfect holiday" : caught 12 noon to Warminster on to camp : V.D. scabie inspection then drew blankets went to hut : wrote after tea : have had a decent time on furlough it is a strain to come back to camp life : must just bog into work now till next holiday comes
Thursday Nov 15th
Sutton Veny : reclassified B1A2 in morn : shifted to our right camp in afternoon : wrote at night
Friday Nov 16th
Sutton Veny : didn't feel too good so went on sick parade got L.D. met Fred Poole Morris so had a night together : met F Costello George Hanmer too
