<p>Entry</p>
<p><u>11th. Sept. 1917</u> A beautiful calm day, sun shining brightly. <s>calm.</s>&nbsp;Have just come in from giving instructions in firing Hales No 20 Rifle Grenades &amp; Mills No. 23 with a 10 inch rod.</p>
<p>Last night we marched a mile across country in our box respirators.&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Entry 19/9/17</u> In tent at Staples. We arrived by motor bus yesterday. I am Battalion Orderley Officer today.</p>
<p>The people at L- treated us very well &amp; were really sorry when we were leaving.</p>
<p>We had a Bn. dinner on 13/9/17 at L-&nbsp;a very decent affair.</p>
<p>Wrote home to J today.&nbsp;Received letter from George.</p>
<p><u>20/9/17</u> In tent shelter at Steenvoorde, West of Ypres. A terrific cannonading is going on this evening west, north west &amp; South of us.</p>
