<p>&amp; both sides have patrols out constantly, already our boys have some scraps with them &amp; rushed them out of some shell holes &amp; craters, it seems impossible to hold Australians back they will have a smack at anything.</p>
<p>About 11' oclock we got a shock for the Germans opened a terrific mortar bombardment on our trenches &amp; they gave us particular Hell you could count as many as 10 in the air at once &amp; you can see them quite plainly for they leave a trail of sparks behind them &amp; when they turn in the air they glow like red hot bulbs, the pace they travel at generates the heat, you can hear them coming</p>
