<p>1917 Wednesday 7 March</p>
<p>On guard from 2.a.m. to 8. The old dugout makes one sick &amp; dopey. <br/>Beaucoup shelling &amp; bombard over on our right last night. 3 Walking cases all that came through tonight. The front is fairly quiet on both sides.</p>
