 Fifth Division Quotas arrive from Weymouth.  
 Alexander Watson in camp. 
 More verses and also letters written. 
  May 20th  
 "Rio Padro" - a vessel of 8,000 tons, which will carry 750 men.  Farewell letters.  Glorious weather.  Washing day. 
  May 21st  
 No news of Harry Hawker.  The worst is feared.  Australia predominates in Sport.
