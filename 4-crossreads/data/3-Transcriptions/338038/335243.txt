<p>After the 3 days at Havre we started out again for the front and after about a week in billets at the rear of the line, we were pushed up here on the 24th to take up our positions. Everything is very different  here to the previous service we had in the Peninsula. I don't know whether to call it better or worse. We either get it very quiet here or as hot as it can be &ndash; There is no half &amp; half. The artillery is much more active here &amp; some of the bombardments</p>
