<p>Sat 17 Same as Fri</p>
<p>Sun 18 On Morgue all day one man in. Shot through head</p>
<p>Mon 19 Inspected Steeles &amp; saps work. Cleaned up road noon with Kiwi Jenkins &amp; Murray Very hot day. Talk of early advance or move Reptd. &ndash; Andrianople in hands of Bulgars Also 100000 turks reinforcements. The balloon up today. Much reconnaissance going on it appears.</p>
<p>Tues 20 Much same as day before. Missed bathe as all bathing stopped after 7.30 PM. So had soap wash Second since we arrived</p>
