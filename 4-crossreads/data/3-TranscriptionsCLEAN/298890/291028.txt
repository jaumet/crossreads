
8 SundayShowers & wind.  Went to Vile Church in morning.  Beastly old men sat in front in armchairs.  Tired.  Wrote & studied and walked on beach before supper.  Leaves coming down
9 MondayWorked on shorthand & story all day.  Wild & showry
10 TuesdayFine day excepting short thunder shower.  Similar routine to above.  Walked on London road for an hour
11 WednesdayWrote & studied shorthand.  Tired & not very well.  Food not sufficiently nourishing.  Walked to Felpham with Miss Woledge
