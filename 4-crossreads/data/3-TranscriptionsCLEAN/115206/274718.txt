
May 1918Tuesday 28
Battery W.Lines.Weather fine but fairly cool. Received a letter from Clar, enclosing some splendid snaps of the family. Also received a letter from "Toots" a beauty. Fairly heavy bombardment during morning.Wrote several letters.Help to complete construction of Y.M.Cleaned steelwork of harness.One or two fine air fights resulting in odds even over Corbie. 1 of ours 1 of theirs. Heard rumors of Fritz's attack up North near Lochre. Good news.
