 Monday 8 November, 1915 
 Went into Valletta &amp; made arrangements to have our teeth done. 
 Had a good time. 
 Put R.M.L.I. [Royal Marine Light Infantry] badges in our caps so we could get beer served to us but it wouldn&#39;t work much. 
 Visited the Admiralty arrived home early 8.20 p.m.
