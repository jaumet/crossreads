
April 1918Friday 26
Bussy - la - dours.    D.R.Gallaghar returned to unit. For non delivery of despatches. Mounted instection at 10am.Weather wet. New runner for 113th arrived.Had a run down to Bonnay.Fred Aldred's brother died of wounds.Received a little mail one from Roy.Cant understand why folks don't get letters over in Blighty.
