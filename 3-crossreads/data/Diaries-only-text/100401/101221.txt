 Letter from Dock that everything has gone wrong that I cannot get into the offices.
Friday 31
 Up till late the weather has been very unsettled raining off on every day but now we are having beautifully warm days.
Sept., 1917
 Saturday 1
 Walk all over the country to Wallop on the Southampton road. The country villages especially the trees about here are beautiful this is Salisbury Plain
