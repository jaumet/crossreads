<p>[Saturday 23 September 1916]</p>
<p>Went to Ireland from Fleetwood by night boat-<br/>Packed up &amp; got room for Laura for Oct 2 our wedding day Ill send you a Cable when the deed is done- with the one word SPLICED &amp; date-<br/>Arrived 6.30 AM at Belfast calm voyage.  These are good fast boats &amp; have only been running a short time as the service was curtailed owing to submarines etc:-</p>
