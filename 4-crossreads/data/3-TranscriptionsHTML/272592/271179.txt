<p>the enemie's trenches, some dull red, yellow, dark green &amp; different sorts of colour as they flashed for an instant against the dark sky. there must have been a demonstration for heavy rifle &amp; machine gun fire broke out &amp; continued for best part of an hour &amp; then gradually died down.</p>
<p>About half past nine they sent down for me &amp; I had to bring 4 men with me for "prisoners guard" so up I went &amp; they gave me some more from D. Coy. the raiding party looked very funny they all had their faces blackened, &amp; no hats on, nearly all wore steel waistcoats strong enough to stop a bayonet &amp; they carried short "waddies" to break their heads in with, they are a nasty looking thing, there is a handle</p>
