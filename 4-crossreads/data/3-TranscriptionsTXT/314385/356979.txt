 Sun Sep 10th We are still living at our leisure doing as we like. 
 There is also part of the 7th FA with us. 
 I tried to see the Popge church but they were all locked up owing to the constant shelling but there are many nice shops here &amp; everything is cheaper and better than in France, beer is far superior also cigars &amp; other things. There is also some beautiful lace things to be had. 
 Mon Sep 11th To day we take over the rest Station &amp; start duties. I am detailed to one of the wards. The 7th are carrying on in conjunction with us. 
 In the afternoon a football match was played between the 6th &amp; 7th 7 AS but we beat them out of sight. 
 Weather fine but inclined to be foggy &amp; chilly, the ground here is very low lying all roads ligned with deep ditches. 
 Tues Sep 12th We are getting well into our duties.
