<p>Sunday 30 December 1917<br/>Church parade (not compulsory) wrote 96.  Sent parcel of badges.</p>
<p>Monday 31 December 1917<br/>Usual routine, drawing rations etc., raining.  Cuth came over from 6th to see us.</p>
<p>Tuesday 1 January 1918<br/>Delousing parade pretty cold had a couple of showers of rain.</p>
<p>Wednesday 2 January 1918<br/>Moved camp about 200 yards to new site pretty busy day.  Got some Baksheesh from Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Thursday 3 January 1918<br/>Very warm day, rumours of move to Yebna.</p>
<p>Friday 4 January 1918<br/>Left Esdud with 2nd Regt. &amp; 1-4-5 Sections &amp; proceeded to Ras Deiran camped for night, had a stay behind &amp; came up late at Nalan.</p>
<p>Saturday 5 January 1918<br/>Left Ras Deiran early &amp; travelled to relieve 4th Bgde., very hilly site &ndash; followed up the Jordan Valley for several miles, sent horses back, rained all night, very miserable.</p>
