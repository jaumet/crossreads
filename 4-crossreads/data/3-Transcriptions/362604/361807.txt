<p>France<br/>12/12/18</p>
<p>Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George,</p>
<p>Just a few lines in answer to your letters of 29.9.18.  So, Tommy Adams got back to Aussie.  He was working for Ellis &amp; I but was sent away to France when all the A. Class men were sent away.  I don't know how some manage to do it.  You mention 8 Hours Day.  I remember some of them as a Child &amp; remember other striking events of later years.  Once nearly drowned &amp; once nearly eaten alive by ants.  It has rained all night so that the Hut where I put in my spare time was on an Island &amp; I had to wade</p>
