<p>Sunday 23 June 1918<br/>H.A.P.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 25 m., dud engine, set out again, in air 2 h. 25 m.<br/>Saw 4 Hun scouts, our escorts gun jambed so we took them on single handed.  Addie gets one with front gun, I get one good burst in, rest driven down under low clouds.</p>
<p>Monday 24 June 1918<br/>Escort Bomb raid on Amman.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 2 h. 10 m.<br/>R.E.8 forced landing at Jericho, bombing good.</p>
