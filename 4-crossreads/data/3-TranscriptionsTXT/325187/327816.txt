 [Saturday 11 March 1916] 
 Exchanged  warm  Warm clothing for summer things at J Cooks &amp; Son &amp; bought 500 Cartridges for my gun at  75  100 P.T. per 100- Went Heliopolis saw old Camp met Miss Myra Wyse &amp; took her to Tea.   Mardi Ritchie was out.   The old Saturday night Dances are on again, at the Principal Hotels but I did not join in.  Got some boots &amp; Trousers for the men.  Had several Hot baths &amp; took back Mosquitoe Nets for all Officers-
