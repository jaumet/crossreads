<p>[Tuesday January 18 1916]</p>
<p>The Water here is obtained from 4 sources-<br/>1. One is a Cistern built by the Romans &amp; dependent on the rainfall, cannot be relied on &amp; is very hard to find<br/>2. Underground springs deep &amp; requiring proper pumping appliances.<br/>3. Sand Wells like Riverina   (4) unreliable small supply.<br/>Went Cairo per horse &amp; Train a long wearisome journey on duty for missing Equipment &amp; paid 10 PT = 2/s for a Bath on arrival- Sore lips &amp; sore eyes from the drifting sand</p>
