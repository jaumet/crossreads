<p>En route to Egypt <br/>= On Active Service =<br/>On the Eve of the 15th April 1915 we were notified of our intended embarkation on the morrow. We were also given instructions to get out of bed at 4 am that morning but long before that hour the camp was in readiness for instant removal. Our run through to Pin Renba was a memorable  event and was witnessed by many</p>
