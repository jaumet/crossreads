<p>Was very delighted "comme toujours" to hear from you two days ago and I often send brain waves of thanks to you on receipt of papers, magazines &amp;c. with the magic Tog in my corner, do you ever feel these Psychic messages?  But as pleased as I was to get your letter, there was that in it to wound my "amour propre" &amp; to <strike>wither</strike> clamp my conceit &amp; self complacency in it you remark that you have lost your heart to a Colonel &amp; gloated on your rise up the military ladder having started with &amp; had only Privates "not even a Lance Corporal".  Really Mrs. J.! didn't you know that my rank was Captain's?  I had hoped at least that with Dr. Barrington &amp; others I --- But no I didn't, I didn't dare!!  (Run away you boys as Alison would say).  Ah well! tis kind to be cruel sometimes.</p>
