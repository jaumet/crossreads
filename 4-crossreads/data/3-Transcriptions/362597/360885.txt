<p>Monday 24.1.16 (32)<br/>We arrived at Suez to-day at Dinner-time &amp; we are now anchored out in the stream. There are plenty of ships, including another Indian Hospital Ship "The Assaye".  It is funny to see the town all white &amp; yellow square looking buildings.</p>
<p>It is blowing a stiff breeze &amp; this morning &amp; when on Parade it was perishing cold, the coldest I have ever felt I think.
