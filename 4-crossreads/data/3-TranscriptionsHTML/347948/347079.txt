<p>All round us are porpoise and dolphins in thousands.  Outside the port on the right side is a big rugged island on which is a big lighthouse and probably a fort.  On the mainland we can make out numerous forts with the glasses.<br/>At 3 p.m. we went into the port.<br/>Round us flocked niggers in canoe shaped boats 3 or 4 in a boat.  Some of these boats had a sail about 12? square and presented this appearance [sketch of boat]</p>
