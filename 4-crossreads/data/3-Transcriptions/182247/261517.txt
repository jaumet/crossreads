<td style="text-align: right; vertical-align: top;">36</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 21</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Arrived at billet in Favrieul at 10.45 am.&nbsp; Glad to be back there.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 22</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 7.30.&nbsp; Breakfast 8.&nbsp; Inspection and parade 9.&nbsp; Wet.&nbsp; Nothing doing pending break in the weather.&nbsp; Slept on wire bed.&nbsp; Good-o.&nbsp; All sorts of &quot;furfees&quot; about moving to goodness knows where.&nbsp; Smith rejoins the company.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 23</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 7.30.&nbsp; Still here.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Did distance judging till 11.30.&nbsp; Dismissed.&nbsp; Spent afternoon washing and watching cricket match on grounds.&nbsp; A beautiful spot.&nbsp; Lovely day.&nbsp; First five out for 3.&nbsp; No mail.&nbsp; Turned in soon after tea.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 24</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Empire Day.&nbsp; Rose 7.30.&nbsp; Nice morning.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Route march to and through Bapaume.&nbsp; Broke off 9.40.&nbsp; Went for ramble with Larsen among the ruins of what was once Bapaume, the capital of Picardy.&nbsp; Saw the beautiful cemetery which was intact.&nbsp; Returned to billet 11.30.&nbsp; Spent rest of day playing cricket on cricket ground, playing No. 1 section.&nbsp; Got beaten sadly.&nbsp; After tea saw football match and turned in early after having had some supper.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 25</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 7.30.&nbsp; Glorious day.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Did apron wire drill in the fields.&nbsp; Very warm.&nbsp; Put some work in on stage for to-night&#39;s concert,in afternoon.&nbsp; Concert at 6.&nbsp; Good band music from a Tommy band.&nbsp; Open air affair.&nbsp; Did Gunga Din.&nbsp; Did not expect having to do it.&nbsp; Had made up my mind not to but Larsen gave me away.&nbsp; Fairly well received.&nbsp; Found it hard to work doing items in the open air.&nbsp; Turned in at 10.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 26</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 7.30.&nbsp; Nice morning.&nbsp; Fritz aeroplane brought down by gunfire at 5.&nbsp; So it is reported.&nbsp; Did not see it but heard the firing.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Did wire work in fields.&nbsp; I was among those exempted being among yesterday&#39;s class.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 27</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 7.20.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Route march of 6 miles through Achiet le Grand and neighbouring country.&nbsp; Though tiring I rather enjoyed marching through such a pretty countryside.&nbsp; Equipment inspection in afternoon.&nbsp; Herb and I fixed up our wire beds so as to be comfortable.&nbsp; After tea went to hear the Tommy 20th Divi. Concert party.&nbsp; Great show.&nbsp; Thoroughly enjoyed it.&nbsp; Posted mail.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 28</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.&nbsp; Nice day.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Spent morning at section and rifle drill.&nbsp; Spent afternoon in the cool shade of trees yarning.&nbsp; After tea went to hear same concert party as last night.&nbsp; Another good show.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">May 29</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.&nbsp; Fine day again.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Rifle drill and route march.&nbsp; Cricket in afternoon.&nbsp; Went to concert at 5.&nbsp; Same place, same people.&nbsp; Good show.</td>
</tr>
