<p>a great deal of trouble.<br/>Ali Ibrahim, the consumptive, has been court martialled for plotting, &amp; the result is not yet out, but both he &amp; the other two have been treated exceedingly leniently.<br/>Eggs &amp; delicacies have been forced on them, to be received with surliness.<br/>Now Enver Pasha's brother Jaffa Pasha has been captured on the Western frontier &amp; there was a tremendous excitement when he arrived.<br/>All the officers were dressed in their best &amp; they waited at the entrance as if for a visitor of high rank.</p>
