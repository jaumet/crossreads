
Tuesday 19/6/17Wether still bad. This Boat is very fond of shipping water in a small way & many have got good wettings to day Canteen opened
Wed 20The rougest night we have had so far waves comeing right over the bows. I am on guard today
Thursday  21Came of guard this morning. Just the same old thing as Liverpool. Only not quite as much style. No arms are carried & the relief cannot march very well up & down gang ways
