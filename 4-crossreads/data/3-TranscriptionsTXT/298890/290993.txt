 9.  Thursday Went to Miss N. at 10 &amp; stayed till 5.  Mrs. N. Smith took girlie &amp; me &amp;  A.A. Smith to Pageant of Freedom in Queen's Hall.  Cara Butt divine as ever.  Lovely day all day &ndash; Warm &amp; sunny 
 10. Friday Started fine but clouded after lunch 
 11. Saturday Went to see 3000 U.S. troops march up  Piccadilly.  In evening F. Post came to dinner &amp; stayed v. late.  Cold in morning.  Grey but occasional patches of sun
