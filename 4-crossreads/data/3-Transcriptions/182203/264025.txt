<p>Had a lovely luncheon &ndash; then took a Gharry to the Post Office (Mr. C's office) and saw the great amount of work that is done there. Then we drove to the wharf and saw two huge ships,  <strike>g</strike> loading up &amp; troops going aboard to leave for the Dardanelles that night. It is awfully sad to see such numbers of fine, big brave fellows going to fight in that awful place.
