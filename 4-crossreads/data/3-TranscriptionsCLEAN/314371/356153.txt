March 18thNaval escort & several ships left us during the night, we are now only 4 Ships & an Aux. Cruiser.
March 19thHeavey swell on & ship is rolling awful making reading, writing & eating a misery to say nothing of mal-de-mer.
March 20th to 26thSplendid weather.  Have been receiving very bad news per wireless re enemy advance.  Not much doing on board.  Convoy keeping well together & improving speed, now about 12 Knots.
