<p>Thursday June 21st<br/>We started yesterday morning at 5.30 from Wareham.  I had goodbye to Mrs Brooks [?] at the School overnight, &amp; on our way through town most of the population waited to see the last of us.<br/>We marched 10 miles before dinner &amp; finished at Blandford (17 miles) at 3 p.m.  Here we camp in a big private park on the outskirts of the town</p>
