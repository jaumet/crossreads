<p>Allemand" alright.</p>
<p>Arrived at Abbeville about 8.30.   We got a pass here for the lot of us to go into the town, so off we went she is a very nice little town &amp; very clean, the shop windows too are very well dressed, the best I have seen in France so far, a big Cathedral is in the town &amp; the Somme" runs right through the town they have so turned the river that she forms a complete circle on the edge of the place &amp; a fine park &amp; promenade of trees are planted hereabouts, that's the one outstanding feature of all the French towns &amp; villages I have</p>
