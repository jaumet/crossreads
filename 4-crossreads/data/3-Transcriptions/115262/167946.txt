<p>Thursday 28 June 1917.</p>
<p>Orderly Sergeant today and a fair amount ot duty too. Responsible for all A.M.C. &amp; A.C.B.F.A. lines in West end of Maascar [Moascar] Isolation camp.</p>
<p>Finished letter started yesteday. &quot;Old&quot; mail from Australia given out. Mostly interstate men got letters.</p>
