<p>Close by in one of the houses of the villagers it was possible for the troops to obtain coffee and cakes at a very reasonable figure. Here we would meet troops from England and Scotland who were camped in the vicinity. We would enter into conversation with them. Whilst here we witnessed an interesting game of "Soccer" between picked teams of Imperial and A.I.F. men. Needless to say who won.</p>
<p>We were greatly surprised one day to hear our names called out to report to the Orderly Room to see Capt [Arthur Leslie] Hewish our new O.C. there.</p>
