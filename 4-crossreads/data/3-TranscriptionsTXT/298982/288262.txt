At night I was on fatigue at 8 pm again, digging a way &amp; bank which went to fill sandbags for a new barricade near B Viney's hole Copley in charge. Our party came on three corpses buried where we were digging &amp; the smell hung heavy. A lovely night with the cresent of the three day old moon &amp; never a cloud.
