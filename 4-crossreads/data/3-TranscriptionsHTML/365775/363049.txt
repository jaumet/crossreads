<p>got hold of a very decent pair of browns each, in all a decent team, well matched &amp; teamed four of them.  we have with us still, my two leaders, one of the centres &amp; one of the wheelers, they are just getting their old dash again after the severe winter on the Somme, "Billy" &amp; Dick.</p>
<p>Well, so much for our equipment, we are now quite ready for the field, nor are we to be kept long before we are on the move again.</p>
