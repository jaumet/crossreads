16th. Lectures again this morning up till 11 oclock then we had physical jerks till 12.Most of the afternoon was taken up in writing up our lectures, its astonishing the amount of writing one has to do at these schools, after tea I had a walk down to Merckenghem to see the watchmaker, it was a lovely evening nice & sunny & all the country looked nice & green, young crops coming on all
