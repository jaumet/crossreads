<p>They are a noisy &amp; dirty lot although very industrious They go to work very early in the mornings and return always after sundown.</p>
<p>18th Feb Sat 1916.</p>
<p>Camp. Tukh-El-Kheil.</p>
<p>Went out to A Squadron at BENI.SAMRAG.about 8 miles up the canal rather an awkward place to find.</p>
<p>They have an ideal Camp there right in the centre of a clump of date-palm trees The whole Squadron are completely undercover impossible for an aeroplane to locate.
