  
 6 February 1918 Wednesday (X to X crossed out)  Regimental Sports  Big football match on this afternoon, it keeps the men fit & well   X Had letter from Lorna Hanson & Laura & papers which speak of the riots in Berlin can we believe it all. X I get a lot of Riverina news from Jim Ayre he's a great letter writer. This Camp & ground is the sight of a big fight & live bombs & dead Turks plentiful- the jackals dig the latter up & we are constantly burying
