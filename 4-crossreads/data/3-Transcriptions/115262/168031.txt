<p>Friday 21&nbsp;September 1917.</p>
<p>4 Taubes over this morning and things livening up on the front by the sound of the guns. His Taube monoplanes can travel some &ndash; too fast to be shelled with any effect by our guns.</p>
<p>One of his machines had some narrow escapes.</p>
