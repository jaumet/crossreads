<p>We learned the second objective was also gained &amp; more prisoners &amp; wounded followed. The Artillery duel raged fiercer than ever but as the daylight began to improve I decided to push on &amp; reach the infantry in the advanced positions. Whilst leaving the crater a 5.9 shell landed in <strike>the crater</strike> six or seven paces away from us: fortunately it failed to explode.
