<p>at Caestre, had some eggs &amp; then made for the camp.<br/>Meanwhile, tired &amp; thirsty Eva visited every estament he came across on the way.  In a semi alcoholic condition he seems to have gone across country by dirty lanes &amp; miraculously done what he failed to do the previous evening when sober; found a short cut.  It brought him out at Strazeele, &amp; if he had not been unfortunate enough to get shut up in a pigsty by a garrulous &amp; angry <strike>Frenchwoman</strike> Belgian lady of ripe years, he would have forestalled us by arriving some time before.  As it was our return was almost simultaneous</p>
