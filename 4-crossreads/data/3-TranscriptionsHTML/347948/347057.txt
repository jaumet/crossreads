<p>One of the most interesting features of the town is the monument and pier.  The monument is one of Rubeck, some notable of South Africa and just in front is a model of a ship in stone, a memorial of Capt Scott's Expedition to the South Pole.  Leading from this is the pier running out into the Bay a distance of 600 yds and on the end is a lighthouse and band rotunda.  The Capetown Prof. Orch. Plays here every Sunday.  Admision to pier 3d.
