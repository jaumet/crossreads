<p>1 April, Tuesday</p>
<p>F. Frosty Lovely Day</p>
<p>Recd P.C. from Doris. I go to Sarum &amp; meet Ellis. Go to No 13 others back to Town. I dine at Osmonds &amp; Matt's Girl tells me that she looked after me not for Matt's sake. We shop &amp; go back for Tea &amp; Music. E. &amp; I go to see Winnie &amp; she does not mind a bit. We talk business etc. I see Ellis off &amp; Marion &amp; I go round Mill Walk also see Floss Home. They all tease me about I DM. I have nice bed &amp; dream of back at D.J.'s &amp; also M. sawing wood all over Summer Hill yard. She is Tres affect.</p>
<p>Sent coins to M.R.L.</p>
<p>2 April, Wednesday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I play Hockey. The civs &amp; we 5 play the Tommies &amp; W.A.A.C.'s. It is a game of rough play &amp; squeaking all the time by the Tommies. Score 3 all. I come Home write &amp; go to mess to read until 10 pm. Read in class room for an hour. The moon has a black appearance as if it were an eclipse.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L.</p>
