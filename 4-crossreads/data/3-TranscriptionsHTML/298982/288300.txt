<p>up till about 5 pm Slowly dying down Turks repulsed &amp; it is said one or two machine guns out of action &amp; two trenches taken. Alf Cook hit in arm while standing outside posse. Quiet night a few of us quite F.U. with the Rag time Farce from which the RT is never absent.</p>
<p>Mon 31st. Quiet morn lay in &amp; slept all morn Aeroplane &amp; the job below spotted submarine perhaps. She seems to do some great work Webster shot at noon while carrying timber up road. Dead.</p>
