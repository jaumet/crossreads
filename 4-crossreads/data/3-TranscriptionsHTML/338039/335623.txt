<p>July. 12th.<br/>Another march (4 miles) - Billeted at Breilly till 5 p. m. - Deoart for Vaux (5 miles) - march of 50 to 60 miles since leaving Ft. Rompu- Billet very poor- Village worse- Four of us spend night on cart.<br/>July. 13th.<br/>Anniversary of Enlistment- Good weather.<br/>July. 14th.<br/>Large mail from Home.  Route march (6 miles)   During march we encounter cavalry &amp; M.M. Gun Corps going through training.</p>
