<p>Oct. 1 &ndash; 8</p>
<p>Thur 1st<br/>Station work all day had trouble to get pass.  Jessie, Nowland &amp; Hayley came out.  went to Sargeant's for tea, &amp; then to pictures saw Jessie &amp; Nowland to the Sydney &amp; Hayley to Moorcliff  hospitals.</p>
<p>Fri 2nd<br/>Raining a little &amp; very little work done stayed in camp evening for a change</p>
<p>Sat 3rd<br/>Went to Randwick for the day signalling, went to Coogee &amp; Felicia evening</p>
<p>Sun 4th<br/>Signalling morning got passes from 4 PM till 9 PM.  went to Felicia &amp; to Church got back at midnight.  met Jess at Felicia</p>
<p>Mon 5th<br/>Sig. at Maroubra had dinner on the beach;  Jessie &amp; Pens came out to camp; Stan Jess &amp; I went to Griffiths &amp; saw Aunt Agnes there  was paraded before Colonel for coming home late the night before, &amp; had our passes stopped</p>
<p>Tue 6th<br/>Paraded the city with all the N.S.W. troops camp fire evening</p>
<p>Wed 7th<br/>Went to Maroubra signalling.  went to Edwards for the evening</p>
