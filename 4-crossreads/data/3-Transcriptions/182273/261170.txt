<p>October&nbsp;&nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>6 SUN&nbsp; Dull day. cool. fixing up wagons</p>
<p>&amp; harness cleaning.</p>
<p>Guns went away for calibrating</p>
<p>in morning. Drew</p>
<p>48 hours rations. Packing up</p>
<p>Writing at night. Quiet.</p>
<p>7 MON&nbsp; Fine day. Moved off at 9 am. via</p>
<p>Bray etc to Vaire sous Corbie</p>
<p>Saw all the old boys at Bray</p>
<p>awaiting departure. Good camp etc</p>
<p>Rain at night. Rode over to Beh-</p>
<p>encourt at night. Heavy riding.</p>
<p>Had a bonne time. hard Good-bye</p>
<p>to Tense. Left 2.30 am.</p>
<p>Fine day. arr home 4.15. Moved</p>
<p>8 TUES&nbsp; off 7 am via Amiens</p>
<p>etc. had a good time in the</p>
<p>old City. Camped out at</p>
<p>St. [Savereuse?]. very cold.</p>
<p>Turned in early</p>
<p>Fine day. Moved off 9. am.</p>
<p>9 WED&nbsp; via Piquany. Sous etc.</p>
<p>to Airainnes. Very fine village</p>
<p>clean etc. fixed up billets.</p>
<p>Had a good day. all hands</p>
<p>very merry at night.</p>
