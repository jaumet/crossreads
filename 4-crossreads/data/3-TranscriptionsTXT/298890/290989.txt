
25. ThursdayWorked a lot in garden &  had an attack of malaria  Went to bed in discomfort & with a temp. of 103 
26. FridayFine day.  Got up at 12. feeling very weak but improved &  did a good deal of work in garden
27. SaturdayFine day at last.  Worked in garden some & wrote some.  Guns waked us at 20 to 12 last night but no raid ensued.
