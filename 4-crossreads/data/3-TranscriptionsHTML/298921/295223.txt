<p>September, 1915<br/>Thursday 9<br/>Went Intermediate Base &amp; [indecipherable] BK today , find &frac12; a day is as much as I can stand just yet. Am trying to get sent to El Hoyat Convalescent Hospital at Helonan. A big Hotel built by Belgian Syndicate for "Tourists", about 20 miles from Cairo.<br/>Already about 700 men &amp; 30 officers are there <u>mending</u> It is at Heloman the Sulphur Baths are, you may remember me mentioning them when Bivouaced at Helonan months ago.<br/>What a lot of good men have been killed since then.</p>
