 1/4/16  Saturday 
 Very hot all day &ndash; Cleaned up the lines till 11 am. No passes granted till 3 pm. Went into Cairo &amp; was going out to  Gizeh  &ndash; none of the party had passes, so the picket, would not let us over the bridge, in fact we were lucky not to be detained. Went to a variety show at night &ndash; after making a good inspection of the fashionable parts of Cairo &ndash; round about "Shephard's Continental etc. 
 2/4/16  Sunday
