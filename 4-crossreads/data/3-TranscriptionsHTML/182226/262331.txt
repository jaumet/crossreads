<p>Friday, 5 November, 1915</p>
<p>Pay day: Drew&nbsp;&pound;1, first pay of over 2/- since Aug 9<sup>th</sup>.</p>
<p>Went into Vallentta &quot;Did ourselves well&quot;.</p>
<p>Went to chapple of bones, took a couple of snaps, arrived home at 9.45.</p>
<p>Had the first game of billiards I have had since Egypt.</p>
