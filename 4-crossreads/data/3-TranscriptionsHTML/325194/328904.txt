<p>had a fine time got back at 2.30 AM and justed missed getting court.M.</p>
<p>18.7.16<br/>Marched 11 miles to Warloy pretty hard work after last nights dissipation</p>
<p>19.7.17<br/>Just finished on two court Marchals of Stoushing up NCOs<br/>Both wash outs.</p>
<p>20.7.16<br/>Left Warloy for the Front<br/>Hit Albert at about 8 PM<br/>Met Tommies coming out carrying German helmets look all right, must get one or two if I can</p>
