<p>1915<br/>Nov. 17 Wed.<br/>Managed to get into town yesterday for a couple of hours. Went into the Staff Pay Office &amp; had my Pay Book fixed up. My 10/6 per day will (as from 1st.Oct. last) be distributed as follows :-<br/>Drawn by myself  2/-<br/>Allotted in Father's name 6/6<br/>Deferred until end of war 2/-.</p>
