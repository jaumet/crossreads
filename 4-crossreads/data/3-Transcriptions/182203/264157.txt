<p>Matron                                                                                                                                                                     Queen Victoria H.                                                                                                                                                                           Adelaide.<br/>Dear Madam.<br/>As I am desirous of obtaining my midwifery certificate, &amp; have been advised to train in the above-named hospital, I should be obliged if you would forward me full particulars, re- applying as a trainee in your hospital.<br/>I trained &amp; received my certificate at the Melb. Hospital about 6 years ago.<br/>I shall be pleased to forward copies of testimonials &amp; certificates if necessary.<br/>Thanking you in anticipation,                                                                                                                                         I am, Madam,                                                                                                                                                                       Yours sincerely</p>
