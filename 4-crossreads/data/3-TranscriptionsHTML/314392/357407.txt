<p>I have seen better stuff thrown into a piggery. Still we can do nothing, for complaints avail to nothing so we must abide by the decisions of the 'powers that be'.<br/>This ship boasts a Canteen which makes about 800% profit on each article. Muk may be obtained for 3shillings per tin and small tins of salmon (very inferior quality) cost us 2shillings and 6pence.These prices are exorbitant and I will starve rather than pay the profiteering crew such amounts. I think it is disgraceful that troops should be submitted to living (or rather should I say existing) conditions like these.<br/>Gee! How glad I will be when I have my freedom if it is only</p>
