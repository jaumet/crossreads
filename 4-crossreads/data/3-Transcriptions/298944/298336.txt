<p>Monday morning<br/>We were supposed to be moving out at dinner time, but that is evidently knocked on the head, for the 9 oclock parade was dismissed on account of rain &amp; nothing more has happened.</p>
<p>Since then, taking advantage of an attempt by which Garner was trying to show Vic Hall the mistake of applying himself too much to the acquiring of knowledge, in which I detected the self satisfaction &amp; egotism referred to, to show him the fundamental mistake he was making. Result: absolutely nothing</p>
