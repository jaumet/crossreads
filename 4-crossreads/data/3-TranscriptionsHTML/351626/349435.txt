<p>2<br/>the furlough.  As I have had no letters for about three months I will not give any more addresses while I am in England for the present as it will give them a chance of catching me up.  If I leave here I will call or send for them here later on.  The Army postal service is apparently rotten.  The last letters I got were dated about 9thJune   &amp; addressed to Egypt except your cable   &amp; a letter from Alf</p>
