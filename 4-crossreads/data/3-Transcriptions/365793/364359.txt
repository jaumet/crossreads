<p>Spt 11 following us. &ndash;<br/>Some of the Canteen prices go up this week when we reach Australian waters. Have got 3 notes 2/8&frac12; left; Query? Will I have to break a quidlet before we get paid again?<br/>Back to the deck again &ndash; "finish hammock"!</p>
<p>12 Blowing half a gale from the N.E. &amp; we're shipping water a treat. One wave doushed me while I was hanging a shirt on the f'cstle, but luckily it was an infant. Today is washing day, but most of us have turned it into a holiday owing</p>
