
22/8/16  Tuesday
Dull & raining - Lively trench warfare - slight casualtys - Snipers very busy with enfilade fire on our roads.
23/8/16  Wednesday
Dull & raining - Fairly quiet in the Saps. Fritz very quiet - Got a letter from Jen Hagen & Mrs O'Kelly.
24/8/16  Thursday
Mysely & 5 others were ordered in to "Nouvea Monde" for a blow as we had the longest spell in the trenches - Will be pleased to toget away from the shells for a while.
25/8/16  Friday
Dull day - Did a few odd jobs about the Dressing Station - Very few cases from the trenches - Have a lot of Tommys & Scottys with us in the Billets.
26/8/16  Saturday
Very heavy wind all day - Doing some odd jobs all day - Went up to Bac St Maur in the afternoon.
