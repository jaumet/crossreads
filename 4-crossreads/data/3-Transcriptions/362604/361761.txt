<p>They are both well &amp; Jack has not even been wounded yet.  We are getting cold weather now.  Ern has 5 blankets &amp; sheets &amp; Ellis &amp; Dick 6 &amp; 7.  I only have 3 so far &amp; I am lovely &amp; warm.  Now, my Dear ones, I've no more news.  The mail has not come for 3 weeks now, but I had a stray one from my Friend at Inverell so I guess there will be heaps along To-morrow</p>
