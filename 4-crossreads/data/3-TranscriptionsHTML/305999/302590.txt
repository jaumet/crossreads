<p>30/1/18<br/>at 4 AM we had revaley and at half past we had breakfast<br/>at a quarter to six we fell in on the perade ground  we had a lot of shifting about finelly we marched to the station with two bands<br/>at half past seven we got a cup of coffee each man from the Australian red Cross   at 8.40 we left Weymouth Station<br/>at Exeter station at 1 PM</p>
