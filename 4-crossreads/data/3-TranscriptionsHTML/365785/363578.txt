<p>Australian Commonwealth Military Forces</p>
<p>Ras el Tin<br/>Alexandria<br/>7.VI.16</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Read,<br/>Just a short note to let you know that I have survived the operation of appendectomy &amp; in spite of the fact that that organ was extensively diseased I have made a remarkably quick recovery.<br/>At present I am at the Convalescent Depot here and am having a good time &amp; trying to pick up a little more French &amp; will probably take lessons for a week or two.  I am also reading Anatole France in the original so as to acquire</p>
