
& du vin rouge (red wine) played a good part & the Gendarmes were already talkative & in good voice. A couple of them had very good voices & joined in heartily singing their National Anthem jove how they did they rap it out it takes a Frenchman to sing it alright. Mademoiselle Anna was systematically hugged & kissed & seemed rather to enjoy it. I was the only outsider there the party broke up about midnight. It was a new experience for me & I thoroughly enjoyed myself.They are very nice people & cannot do enough for us.29-7-17. A mail to hand to-day 5 from home the first for over 6 months weeks.10-8-17. Moving to-morrow to DRANOUTRE Belgium all is bustle. Fritz is shelling one of our balloons
