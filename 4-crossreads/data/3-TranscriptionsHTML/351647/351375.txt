<p>more settled.  All the officers are suffering bad colds &amp; I don't wonder the weather today is vile, wet &amp; sloppy &amp; the mud is awful.  There are not many comforts.  You would laugh at me &amp; my four orderlies, I am their Boss &amp; I hate bossing but it has to be done &amp; I do a great rouse now &amp; again.  But they are good lads &amp; give very little trouble.  We, the two Warrant Officers &amp; another Sergeant have a little Mess to ourselves.  They are not bad chaps at all.</p>
