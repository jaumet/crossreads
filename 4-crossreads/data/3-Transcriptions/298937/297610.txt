<p>of getting to England.<br/>It is rather miserable waiting here in the terrific heat &amp; doing practically nothing.  We get drill &amp; route marches early morning &amp; late evening, but during the heat of the day one just lays down &amp; tries to sleep.<br/>2 days ago we rose at 4 &amp; went on a five mile route march, were inspected by a General &amp; then came back.  It was ten miles of hard work for the heat was very great even at that early hour.</p>
