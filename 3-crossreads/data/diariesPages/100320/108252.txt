The woman question is rather acute here and a great deal of VD is about. The Huns left all diseased women behind and our preventitives will be sure of a good sale. Round the fire at Bde H.Q. we talked about old battles all the evening. As a mob near the picture show seemed rather unruly I ordered an officers piquet to be on duty there tomorrow night.
