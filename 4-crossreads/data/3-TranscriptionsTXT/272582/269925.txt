At about 10.50pm air-raid signals were sounded, &amp; machines were heard distinctly at 11.55 pm. They were heard again, &amp; after a strong barrage was put up which lasted till 12.30 A.M. the machines made off. They returned at 3.A.M. &amp; circled around until driven off at 3.20 A.M. Several bombs were dropped in the city &amp; suburbs. Considerable damage was done to more than 18 buildings, resulting in the death of 6 &amp; many injured according to local reports. 14.8.18. 78 men &amp; 24 women baked 65,660 lb rations Temp.
