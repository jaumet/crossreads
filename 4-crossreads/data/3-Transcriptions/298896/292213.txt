The Aussies, being tired of being taken for Yanks, lined up with an Australian flag &amp; paraded the streets. They gave us a wonderful reception, Vive l'Australienne coming from all sides &amp; we were pelted with confetti whilst some French buglers in front &amp; half the population tailing up behind, we did the main thoroughfare singing Australia will be there. Dancing &amp; kissing was the order of the night &amp; I sought my couch at 1.30 A.M.<br/>12th Nov. Tuesday.<br/> Had breakfast in bed &amp; got up at 8.30.
