
(No.8.)Troopship "Kyarra"Nearing Fremantle11th December 1914Dearest old Mrs,Well here we are nearing old Mother earth once more and it is pleasant to see the hills & dales after five monotonous days of winds & waves. But we have not had too bad a time, it was a bit unpleasant for the first day out from Melbourne but since then it has been all right. We expect to reach Fremantle late this afternoon & will probably be there till Monday. We have not yet heard what our destination is to be but will probably know after we get away from Fremantle.
