 28. Thursday Got little work done. In and out of bed, very weak &amp; shivery. Cold &amp; grey &ndash; showers. Miss H. insisted upon reading to me. 
 29. Friday. Cold, grey day. Miss Hodge read to me interrupting any attempt to work. 
 30 Saturday. Went back to Miss Smith's. Had to go up on top in rain on tram. Found Miss Smith gone to Keighly with Nina Bogle to ask nom. in bye election.
