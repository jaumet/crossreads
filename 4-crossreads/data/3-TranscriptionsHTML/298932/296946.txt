<p>Monday 27<br/>A parcel from Dad which I shared with the folk of the good farm 'Clarmary'. Very wet day.<br/>Tuesday 28<br/>A letter from Emma The mail of late as been coming very slowly but I suppose the hun as sank some mail boats<br/>Wednesday 29<br/>The Division reviewed to day by the Com. In Chief of the British Army Sir Douglas Haig. The 17th Batt won his confidence<br/>Thursday 30<br/>A great boy for boils twelve week of them &amp; still got two to keep me company</p>
