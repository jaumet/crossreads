<p>May 1918<br/>Friday 10</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daours<br/>Glorious weather.<br/>Had the usual journey at 4 pm, to Lailly Le Sec, via Vaux sur Somme and Corbie.<br/>During afternoon drifted out avec Muller, to Querrie Pont Noyelle, and La Neuvelle, as Mounted Orderley.<br/>Wrote a French epistle to Adrienne, aided by Lemon, at Madame Isobel.<br/>Harry came in from Aubigny.</p>
