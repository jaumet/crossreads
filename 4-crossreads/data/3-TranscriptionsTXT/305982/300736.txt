 6/10/16 X Ray taken of shoulder to day 
 7/10/16 Raining again all day 
 8/10/16 Church service to day beautiful day 
 9/10/16 I go on furlough within the next couple of days  nothing doing other than watch the rain falling. 
 10/10/16 Am on furlough starting from 12 0clock today.  10.50 train from Weymouth 
 11/10/16  arrived at London Waterloo 4 30 AM this morning motor cars waiting for us.  driven to where we want to go.  Kings X station no train to Dundee so waited &amp; got one to Manor Park saw Mrs Coombs &amp; Master Lennie. fine little home etc.  Lennie &amp; his train etc  Left about 6 oclock arrived at Manor Park 7.30 had hot bath at public baths it was hot into the bargain.
