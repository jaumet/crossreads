  
 we had good orchestra stall seats & enjoyed the turnout. From what I can see of Cardiff she is famous for 2 things one is the enomorous amount of coal that is lying about on the wharves trucks & in fact everywhere, & the other is the number of colored people that there are knocking about the Streets just stink of them, the most I have yet seen in any English town. This is a bonza hotel we are stopping at they are so obliging & kind, quite unlike any other place I have ever stopped at, when I went to pay my bill this morning they refused to take the money, its alright she said you can pay us when leaving, I should imagine they are taking a bit of a risk for one could easily slip off without paying them, & lots
