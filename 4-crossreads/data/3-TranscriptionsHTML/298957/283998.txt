<p>20th April (Thursday)<br/>Mail Day. Carpentering at Mess Shed. Peaches &amp; cream before going to bed.</p>
<p>21st April (Friday) Good Friday. Glorious day. Parades cancelled except the Church Parade. Reading &amp; writing letters.</p>
<p>22nd April (Easter Saturday) Warm. On Cooks Fatigue &amp; writing. Route march for the remainder. In evening playing draughts.</p>
