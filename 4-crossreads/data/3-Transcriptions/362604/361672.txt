<p>wanting to borrow a strop, or a bit of Kiwi or a bit of soap &amp; always touch for money, cigarettes &amp; matches.  I've given up smoking.  It is too much of nuisance in 50 different ways.  I hope that you completed the verandah o.k.  It will be a great boon to anyone renting the place &amp; will make it ever so much more valuable.  We are highly pleased to think that you snapped the chance at Dee Why while you had the opportunity.  Well, Dad, it is now "First Post" half-past nine.</p>
<p>We are all making beds &amp; getting ready for our little Couch.  We have to rise at 7 a.m., first parade at 8 a.m. &amp; breakfast immediately afterwards.</p>
