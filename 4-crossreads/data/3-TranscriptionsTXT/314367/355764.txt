 I'm debating as to what I shall do next &ndash; Unless offered something decent I shall wash out &ndash; I've shown capacity &amp; worked for 6 months &amp; seen the N.G. trouble through &ndash; I have decided not to strive or cry &ndash; just to see if I'm offered anything that will pay &amp; if not to go 'on the land' once &amp; again &ndash; That's definite &amp; best =  W-day Feb 17-15 &ndash;  The weather is cooler &ndash; A level sea &ndash; A fair passage to date &ndash; We are off Townsville &ndash; to the East of that place some 300 miles  - What is doing?
