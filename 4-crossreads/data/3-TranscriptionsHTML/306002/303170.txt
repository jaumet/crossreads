<p>June 1st 1915<br/>Tue. 1st.  Had an excellent nights sleep last night &amp; lay about in our dug-out till dinner time.  "Jock" Davidson (Quartermaster Sergeant) was going down the gully this morning &amp; was wounded through the head <strike>with</strike> by a sniper.
