<p>31 July 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Rest all day &amp; write some letters.<br/>Receive old letters in bunch dated Feb &amp; March.<br/>Post my home letter dealing with our late fight also note to A.G.<br/>Bob down to see us.   paid today.   Lash out with tinned fruit, chocs. &amp; biscuits.   He is sending home joint cable.</p>
<p>Hostile Tauabes pass overhead at lunch time. Nose caps from anti-aircraft guns drop perilously close.</p>
