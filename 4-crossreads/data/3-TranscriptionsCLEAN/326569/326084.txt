  
 Friday, 27/7/17 Fairly warm all day.  Went out from 9 p.m. to 12 midnight on night stunt.  Coffee & biscuits supplied for supper. 
 Saturday, 28/7/17 On Lewis Gun stoppages all morning.  In afternoon went to Tisbury with Smith, Meldrum & Dawson.  Had tea there & arrived back 9.15 p.m. 
 Sunday, 29/7/17 Reveille 6 a.m., up 7 a.m.  Went with Palmer to farmhouse & had 2 each of pint mugs of milk, cost 2d. per mug.  Rained in afternoon.  Took laundry to Fovant.  Posted photos to Australia etc. 
 Monday, 30/7/17 Went skirmishing over the hill all day.  Afternoon & evening rained continuously.  Paid after tea. 
 Tuesday, 31/7/17 Rained all morning so did not go out on parade.
