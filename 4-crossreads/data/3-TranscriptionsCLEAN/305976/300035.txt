  
 Friday Aug 23 The weather is exceptionally hot & one feels fit for nothing here. Things are quiet but it is rumoured an attack is expected at any time. Saturday Aug 24 Jacko shelling to day Everything ready for expected attack. Sunday Aug 25 Went out in Sand Cart to 2ND Reg. to day. We had great difficulty finding them owing to number of attacks. Monday Aug 26 Usual routine to day. Weather still very hot
