Several "Bullies" have arrived since my last letter also a few mags. which are all very welcome and much looked forward to.  The Wide World & Munseys are great favourites with the boys and myself.  We appreciate them greatly.  None of the parcels you advised have ever arrived, which looks as if they have fallen by the wayside and the thistles etc.  Met Dan Cullen a few nights ago.  He is very well.  Do you remember him? 
 After many enquiries, I have received information that Kittie's brother was wounded on May 3rd and is now in hospital in England.
