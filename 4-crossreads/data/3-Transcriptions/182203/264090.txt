<p>London.                                                                                                                                                           Sept 7th 1916.<br/>My dearest Mother &amp; Sisters.<br/>I have now definite news to tell you of my position. I'm leaving here the end of this week for France <strike>and</strike> rejoining my own Unit. No 1. A. Gen. Hosp. Rouen. So I'm very lucky in one way but sad of heart am I, have not heard a word about or from my boy. May perhaps hear something when I get to France.<br/>I had a letter from Dorothy &amp; she is delighted I'm in England, she will be surprised when she knows I'm going to No 1. A. Gen. H.<br/>I have sent a number of P. Cards to Belle but to keep for me &ndash; They are worth keeping, so I hope you receive them safely.
