<p>4.  Georgie, old man, I have give Dad &amp; Mum pretty well all the news so I must dry up now.  Have a good time while You can &amp; don't join up till You absolutely have to whatever You do stick to the A.M.C.  You will have to work with M D's who are much more sensible &amp; reasonable than the ordinary Military officer.  Remember Me to Os Merrett &amp; the Club Boys &amp; Mr Love.  I have been going to write to Mr Love 100 times &amp; yet have delayed.<br/>Tell Him that I do not forget Him though I do not write<br/>Heaps of Love to LIL &amp; yourself<br/>Harry</p>
