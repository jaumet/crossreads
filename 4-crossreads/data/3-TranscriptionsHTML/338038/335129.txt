<p>I was quite well, but the way the postal matter is being handled it is, I should say a very open question whether it reaches them.<br/>While on the subject &ndash; After the big scrap here on 6 &amp; 7th May I sent you a cable saying I was OK. My friend in Alexandria to whom I sent the text &amp; money for transmission, Capt Webb, has never acknowledged my letter, so Lord only knows if this will reach you.<br/>There has been very little fighting on our immediate front since last week. Only the usual artillery duels in the evening which usually results in a great expenditure of ammunition by both sides and very little damage done.<br/>Out on the left the British force assisted by the 10th A.L.H. went in and took a line of trenches after fierce fighting.
