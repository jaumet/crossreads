<p>1917 October</p>
<p>24 Wednesday</p>
<p>Awoke to a fine morning &amp; the place soon dried up.  Got grey &amp; cloudy again towards night fall.<br/>Stephie &amp; J. Lindsay taken to sick tent.<br/>Went for a little walk on the hill sides with Mr. G. Brown &amp; Smith &amp; saw old Kajmachalon crowned with snow.</p>
