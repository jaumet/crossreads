 16/10/16 Nothing much doing to day other than went for a walk with a pal of Freds named Herbert from works.  then along the canal near by & home again talk about rain it just did. 
 17/10/16 Rain again this morning Fred took me out with him on his motor cycle.  went to Windemere Lake beautiful sights enroute great ride  Belt broke she would not go again for some time, however we got home again without a stop Bowness.  some 40 miles we went all told. 
 18/10/16 Raining again to day left Ulverston for London 1 oclock went to Wigan.  Dirty place arrived at London 9.30 went to U Jack Club Waterloo Rd London 
 19/10/16 London a fine place had a good look around here or rather [indecipherable]  Parliament houses
