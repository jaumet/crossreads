Capt Luce who was in command of Glasgow during each action was still in command of her at this timeWe were close to Pernambuco by now, and "Glasgow", who took charge of our collier, proceeded with her into Southern waters, whilst we proceeded back to our starting place Trinidad - Accordingly captains exchanged visits, and it was then learned that the German raider was believed to have been successful in returning to Germany - At 10.15 am we parted company each proceeding on their course.
