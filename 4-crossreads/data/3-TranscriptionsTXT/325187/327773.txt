 [Friday 28 January 1916] 
 [Following paragraph is crossed out] I hope ere this Friday Mother has gone South &amp; Day too if it can be arranged; in easy stages our Mother must be much stronger now, thank goodness  Omit Tins of Kearnigs Powder from  arrived tonight, here we are able to keep comparatively clean- On the salt Lakes &amp; away on the Nile Valley, there are Plover Duck &amp; Snipe, but the Sheiks or Chiefs of friendly natives say if we fire one shot the natives will all clear  ed  out, they are so shy &amp; frightened, that something dreadful is going to happen 12 Bore Cartridges at  20 shilling a 100 can be bought, but guns are scarce.
