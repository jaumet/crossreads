<p>Saturday Feb 19th<br/>windy dusty day : on guard Gypo barracks : done one shift 6 am to 8 am : did not sleep too good : concrete too hard &amp; also felt "chatty" : relieved by Helmia Camp boys &amp; got home about 9.30. It was not a bad job as they gave us plenty of tucker this time : Handed in rifles &amp; ammunition &amp; had a shower : all the boys had a hunt &amp; a lot of "chats" were found Tom &amp; I got a beauty each : dinner : Dan came up from bomb school &amp; told us about the accident : premature explosion : two killed &amp; one badly injured : Well's had  a very lucky escape also our boys : Tom, Bill Fahey &amp;amp myself went into Cairo &amp; had our photos taken : Tom paid for them as we were both mafishfaloosh till Tuesday : Had a walk through the Wazza &amp; seen some fine wood turning : done with a native lathe the chisel is guided by their big toe &amp; the work is very fine met Williams (two) &amp; Whichels at the Y.M.C.A: it was their first trip to Cairo : came home &amp; had tea : read &amp; wrote till bedtime : we have had butter  each meal for the last few days. Seen a monkey Can tan</p>
<p>Sunday Feb 20th<br/>Dull cold day : Revellie 6 am : on duty piquet to report at Headquarters 6.45 am : a sweet job one hour on &amp; three off : I finished at 4.30 pm &amp; after tea Alf, Lock, Bill Fahey, Ed Peatfield &amp; myself went to Cairo (Alf shouted) to go the St Andrews Presbyterian church but could not find it &amp; finished up at All saints C of E : We were &frac14; of an hour late</p>
