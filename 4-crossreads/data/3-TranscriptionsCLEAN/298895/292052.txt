
Sunday 22nd. AprilNice bright weather.  Played the 8th Australian rules & won 67 to 23.  Some win.  Four English letters in mail & wrote four at night Australian mail still slow.Monday 23rd. AprilBeautiful day.  Sound of guns still going another hopover in the offensive being about due, walked into Albert, where taubes dropped bombs last week, & went to a [indecipherable] Show, the company being called "The Anzac Coves," not of much class & nothing to write home about, still it's a good work & does the fellows good.   Wonderful how the chaps hoy with delight when a fellow dressed as a girl comes on 'tis almost the most popular itemTuesday 24th. AprilSailed out of Lemnos Bay for the landing this day two years ago, it seems but yesterday.  Beautiful weather today & every promise of spring being now with us.  News came this morning of another hopover in the offensive, 2000 prisoners
