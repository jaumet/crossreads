<p>11-15AM.  11-11-18<br/>Our suspense has come to an end and the reader will easily guess the answer when I say that everywhere, from one side of France to the other, is just one seething mass of applause, appreciation and happiness, more especially among the civilians, as it is well known what true patriots they are.</p>
