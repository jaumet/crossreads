<p>August 1917</p>
<p>at last. Would have been very useful when the Hatch &amp; ports were closed. Every thing has been opened since Capetown</p>
<p>Friday 3rd<br/>The Afric took a trip on her own this morning went almost out of sight &amp; came back to her old place. This evening she sent up 3 small ballones &amp; practiced on them with <strike>her</strike> her anti-aircraft guns no hits.<br/>The awning has been put over the forward well deck. Boxing contest on this afternoon also a lecture on the aft well deck by one of the Paders. YMCA gave us chocolates for tea Quite warm tonight</p>
