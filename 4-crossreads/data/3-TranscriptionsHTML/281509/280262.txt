<p>1916 February 9<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>First day of Nowra Show.  Get up &amp; help milk.  Harry goes up early.<br/>Mary not much better.  Bob &amp; self drive up in fore-noon &amp; go to ground after lunch.<br/>Called at Borrowdale's &amp; heard of Bob's illness.  Visit him at Private Hospital also see Lil Brown &amp; Mrs Graham.  Call then at Edwards &amp; Annie Gardners. After lunch at Stan's go to show for afternoon.<br/>After tea Bob &amp; call round at Baillies &amp; take Miss L &amp; B to pictures.  Ice cream &amp; home.</p>
