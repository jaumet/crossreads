
March 1917Monday 26Very foggy morning did not lift until 9amFirst operations at Gaza 10th LH in reserve - going in-Standing to arms all night moved at 2 am .10th as rearguard to the brigade. Major Dangar does some good work
Tuesday 27Retired from Gaza shelled by the Turks also fired upon with rifles. One of our planes came down in flames. Almost surrounded and captured.Got back safely thanks to Genl Roystons leadership. Total British losses - 13000 and Gaza not taken.
