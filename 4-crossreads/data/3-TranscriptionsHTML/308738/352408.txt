<p>March 13 Saturday 1915<br/>Mounted Parade in morning<br/>Went into Cairo for Major Fuller conveying an important letter to the Australian &amp; N Zealand HeadQ Shepherds Hotel.<br/>Colonel Cox &amp; party of  Qld Lancers left this evening to take Dinner at the Continental Hotel 7. 45</p>
<p>March 14 Sunday 1915<br/>Colonel Cox major Fuller &amp; party went out for the day returned 6. 30<br/>Church parade this morning<br/>I rode into Cairo with Haigh today<br/>Measles broken out in camp.</p>
<p>March 15 Monday 1915<br/>Mounted Parade in morning<br/>Major Fuller went into Cairo in connection with Court Martial Case.<br/>Received 2 letters from M. one dated 24th Jan &amp; other dated 15th Feb.<br/>Posted letters P. 6 to M &amp; mother.</p>
