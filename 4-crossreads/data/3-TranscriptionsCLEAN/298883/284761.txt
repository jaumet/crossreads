
ToLieut. Hector BrewerHead Quarters Signaller54th Battalion14th BrigadeAustralian Imperial Forces
Sent to you with best Love & Wishes
FromYour Loving Sister & NephewFlo & Roy KnightApril 25th 1918This is "Anzac Day" Dear Hec, the Day that will be well remembered in the History of this War.The Landing of our dear Lads in Galipoli & my Brother.This Day should be kept as a Sunday - to my mind.
