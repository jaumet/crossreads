
Monday 26 November 1917Connaught again & in charge 2-5.  All peace during that time then over to 14 A.G.H. for a bit of "home" & life.  Saw Dick & had a laugh again.Matron on duty after 4 days honeymoon but going away for longer leave.  Happy & gay as possible.6 empty beds but convoys always come daily & hosp. must be up to its full capacity.  1800 pts. in one night lately & most of the new arrivals lying about for several days before sent in here.
