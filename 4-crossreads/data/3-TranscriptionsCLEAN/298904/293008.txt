  
 one night about the 15th a chap came round to say goodbye" to his Brother as his Battalion (5th) was leaving the penisular that night that set the tongues going I can assure, & we had to admit to ourselves at last that Anzac" was to be abandoned, for a long time we kidded ourselves that Canadian troops were going to relieve us for the winter, & that we were going to either Mesoptamia or the Suez Canal. Another rumour got about that they were going to withdraw all troops & put in some big siege guns & they with the help of the Navy were going to hold the position
