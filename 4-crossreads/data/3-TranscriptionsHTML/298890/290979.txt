<p>21. Thursday.<br/>Glorious spring weather. Feeling weak in morning. Better during day and did good work. Went for a walk &amp; called on Mrs Oliver. Sun all day.</p>
<p>22. Friday<br/>Lovely weather. Offensive of Germans in France.</p>
<p>23. Saturday<br/>Went down town to bank etc. Lovely day came home on top of bus. Quinine [indecipherable]</p>
<p>Weekend<br/>Terribly depressed by quinine.</p>
