For the first couple of thousand yards we didn't see a Fritz, then all of a sudden we discovered about 20 behind us so we bailed them up without any trouble they came out with their hands up squealing like a lot of pigs for mercy.  Kamarad &amp; Mercy Kamarad is all they can say.  I just had time to pinch a couple of watches from them &amp; I had to chase on after the other chaps who had gone on.
