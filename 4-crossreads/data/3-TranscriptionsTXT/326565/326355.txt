 No 4s medical panniers, chequed &amp; handed out in readiness for immediate use.  Infantry  camping near by, all out in the trenches ready for an attack.  Received parcel from home. 
 Thursday 4/5/16 All rush &amp; bustle.  Bearers rushed out towards the trenches, about 1&frac12; miles out from here, to construct a dug-out, to act as a dressing station, if the expected attack eventuates.
