<p>June, 1915<br/>Saturday 19<br/>Winter with you, mid summer &amp; on the Desert not a sign of Animal life except a few hares. Tis wonderful how one gets accustomed to sand, we eat it, sleep in it &amp; never notice it now. But it is the very devil on leather goods such as Boots &amp; Leggings.</p>
