<p>5 Nov 16<br/>Anchored at Rosyth. H.M.S. Southhampton our flagship has gone into dry dock. While she is in dock Admiral Goodenough has come aboard the Melbourne. So the Melbourne is flagship till Southampton is out of dock.</p>
