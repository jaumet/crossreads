 Wednesday 13 June 1917 
 Last night a terrific bombardment went on on our left. The SKY was bright with Flares &amp; SHELLS bursting &ndash; result an important forward position of Johnnie Turkstaken. 
 "Camels" had a day's sports &amp; Carnival &amp; Concert in the evening.  I went &amp; saw Nowell, he's a great man among the English Aristocracy now &ndash; LORDS &amp; ragged EARLS, etc:   One item  a  Musical Chairs by mounted Camels was screamingly funny &ndash; Camels are riden in a circle, bags on ground at intervals, on the Band stopping, Camels are made to sit on nearest bag. 
 [The following paragraph crossed through.]  BEER night again &amp; many have yet to learn "How much they can carry". Broken noses &amp; more SKIN off the result.
