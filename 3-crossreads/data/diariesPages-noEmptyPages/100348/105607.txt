20/8/16.
We are in billets in Albert; we got here last night; it saved my life. I had no sleep for 3 nights, but I had a splendid sleep today; and received a big mail, so everything is bright and beautiful again.
21/8/16.
Broke into the cellar of a ruined house yesterday and found a lot of wine. I lead my braves to it and we finished the lot before we left for Warloy
