 29 October 1918 Tuesday Villa Montrose    Graham Went Hospital, 1000's of Turks are in the Hospital here, &amp; many die from Plague Typhus etc- about 10 000 have gone to Salonika in labor corps-  To show how well they have been treated about &pound;700 000 has been collected from prisoners this in gold &amp; silver.  German paper money is of no value at all here. Rumours tonight about Austria Am getting stronger each day &amp; the bairn is growing.
