 Monday 21 June&nbsp;1915 Working in the office and about the place today. Concert at 8 p.m. Heliopolis at 11 p.m. some roast fowl, potatoes peas. Bananas.&nbsp; Posted letters to The Girls, Mrs Knowles. 
 Tuesday 22&nbsp;June&nbsp;1915 Letters for the Girls (supplementry). Dr Paton, Mr&nbsp;Hennessey, Mollie, Dr Dunn, Clarie Bowen.&nbsp; In Cairo for an hour during the morning.&nbsp; Called see Colonel Barrett at night. Had Supper at Heliopolis hotel at 11-30 p.m. 
 Wednesday 23&nbsp;June&nbsp;1915 Ordinary work for a family of 350 people&nbsp;fairly standard. Six small operations.&nbsp; Surgn Gen Williams called &amp; inspected the hospital, he appeared to be well pleased. Had a whiskey &amp; went away&nbsp; Heliopolis hotel for Supper at 11 p.m.
