<p>Well we got to Kawieng about 3 pm &ndash; Thorold &ndash; the administrator came off &ndash; At 4 pm we put out to sea without having landed &ndash; The weather was perfect &ndash; Once &amp; again I saw  those wonderful seascapes that attracted me so much the first time &ndash; To day &ndash; they were clear cut as cameos &amp; as beautiful as ever &ndash; Seen thro driving mist with the roar &amp; lash of the combers I think the effect is even more magical &ndash;  We are chasing the Destroyers with a message for them &amp; wireless went &ndash; crack &ndash; crack &ndash; every 15 minutes all night until we got them at dawn &ndash; We have changed our course &amp; are said to be going back to ?Rabaul &ndash; War without wireless seems impossible &ndash;</p>
<p>Monday Jan 25-15  Up at 6 am Cocoa &ndash; Then I read &ndash; I always read if its cool enough &ndash; Reading the words of great writers</p>
