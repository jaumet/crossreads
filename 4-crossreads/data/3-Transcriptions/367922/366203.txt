<p>last year, which was only three weeks, I had no opportunity of visiting any of the museums or other places I had hoped to. In a combatant force leave is very scarce, and I dont suppose I will be due for any for another 3 or 4 months. But then I hope to make use of yours &amp; Dr. Johnstons letters.</p>
<p>It is very cold here. The snow has been on the ground now for 5 weeks and the shell holes are covered by more than a foot of ice. The water freezes as soon as poured out. Our breath freezes on the mufflers. So we have learnt of another foe besides the Hun.</p>
<p>But for all that, I prefer this weather to that of 5 weeks ago, when the mud was just inconceivable. We literally sink above the knees every steep. The Trenches, which now have duckboards in them, were then a stream of slowly flowing</p>
