 Saturday Dec 9th The past 2 days have been very cold but clear &amp; light. Sharp white frosts which lie unmelted all day Today is warm &amp; beautiful. Last night it rained again The news yesterday was not too good We evacuated Bouston Wood &amp; have to withdraw &amp; straighten our line in the Cambrai Salient
