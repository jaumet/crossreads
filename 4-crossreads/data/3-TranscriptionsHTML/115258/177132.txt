<p>Did not finish this before Father and now I&#39;m off to night down to Luxor. Three other, very decent chaps are coming with me and the military have given us tickets to Assuan &amp; return; so the trip is costing us &pound;5-10- each for 7 day, with Hotels &amp; all exes paid by Cook&#39;s. Seems reasonable. I met went out <s>t</s> to the 31st and saw Sister Davidson</p>
