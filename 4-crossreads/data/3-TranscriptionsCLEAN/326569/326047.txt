
east wind sprang up & developed into a real Darling River dust storm, also making temperature very cold.  Just before bed walked down to Ludgershall for cup of coffee.
Saturday, May 6thVery cold this morning but nice fine sunshine although windy.  After dinner went to Perham for hot shower & after tea to Perham to Y.M.C.A.  Still cold.
Monday, 7/5/17Frosty early in morning. Practising bombing morning parade & afternoon ditto.  After tea went to Ludgershall Y.M.C.A. for some stamps.
Tuesday, 8/5/17Very cold.  Making new bombing course during day.  After tea an aeroplane fell 11/2 miles from camp & was smashed.
