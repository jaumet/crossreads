 F 18 Sun 
 We hear on Fortunes. I will have to cross to lots of water for my Happiness. I look over Books. I am tied. I am not very Brotherly. Drive down an at Stanley Home 7.30. Walk on Prom. Hear band play abide with me. Am very dopey Wrote Auntie Annie. 
 F 19 Mon Wake at 5.25. On Boat at 5.50. Leave 6.40 arr L'pool 11.20. Dinner at Caf&eacute; Ratten Concert by Sweets or Music. Catch 2 pm arr. Euston 6.50. Meet Neville Seatree. Catch 8.55 at W'loo change [Porton?]. Am Home 12.0/e. Very tired &amp; weary 
 Write to Marie &amp; Dad 
 F 20 Tues. [indecipherable] Day. Recd letters from Doris. Mem. Muriel Davies. Sadie McNiven. Edie. T. Laura. Geo. 2 Dad. 2 Mum. Also papers. Read &amp; wrote all night. I am very lonely &amp; in love with all the world. Recd Field and Norm. 
 F 21 Wed Recd letter from Maisie Halm. Last of War Babies march out. Have a general tidy up all round. Wrote the bottom letters on 20/8/18. 6 F. Cap to in. Wrote Norm. Jennie. Roy. Very Hot Day. Wrote to LN.W.Rly &amp; MRL &amp; Auntie Annie. 
 F 22 Th Very Hot Day. Generally tidy up &amp; read for the remainder of the evening. 
 Wrote to MRL. Dad &amp; George 
 F 23 Fri &amp;Showery. Darn socks &amp; play Billards &amp; Snooker. Ellis goes to Leeds only with a head cold. 
 Print two negatives. 
 F 24 Sat Glorious Day &amp; Glorious Moonlight Night. Ride to Sarum. Go to C2013 &amp; then to CC &amp; close. Nat Australian Ententainer (Pte Taylor alias Turrell) &amp; Jack Kershaw. Go on River &amp; have just over the Hour. Go back to C2013 for supper. Recd letter from Norm. Send Parcel to Marie. 
 1317 letters to Here.
