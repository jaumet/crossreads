We are still amongst these ruins and draw 20 kilos away at 9 a.m.  The last four mornings have been very frosty and the country has been white with it, but no snow yet.  Today has been one of those bitterly cold & frosty days, you know, that's too cold to snow.  Needless to say, the fires are well stoked.
You might wonder when I tell you that I haven't worn drawers for over 6 months now, but I'm afraid I'll shove 'em on pretty soon now.
