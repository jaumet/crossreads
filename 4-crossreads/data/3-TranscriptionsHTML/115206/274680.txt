<p>April 1918<br/>Saturday 20</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash;Daours    D.R.<br/>French made a small advance down in Montideder region.<br/>Had a despatch for Bdge Hdqs, rode over accompanied by Jackson 13th Bdge.<br/>Seven hundred cases of gassed men recently .<br/>Fritz having a whack at Bethune.<br/>Weather fairly cool but very nice.<br/>Planes over all night, and they dropped a few of the best in village.<br/>Relieved sick on Bty [indecipherable] <br/>Bloomsbury parte pour Batteri&eacute; &ndash;</p>
