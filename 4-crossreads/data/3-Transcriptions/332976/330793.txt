<p>Febuary 1917.<br/>8th   The news has been confirmed that China has sent Germany a "Note" but the papers give no details   No further news is to hand with regard to America's attitude.<br/>George Hart passed through here to-day on his way to the Casualty Clearing Station with Pneumonia.<br/>14th   The weather has started to "break" today &amp; by the looks of things we are in for some better weather.  The sun has been warmer &amp; the snow has practically all melted &amp; the majority of ice is that way inclined.</p>
