<p>It is just eight weeks since we set out from Devonport.&nbsp; What promised to be a slow &amp; monotonous voyage, has certainly been slow but (thanks to the Padre) far from monotonous.</p>
<p>It is now over, save for the all-important arrival, and we are all grateful for the protection, guidance &amp; salvation which He has given us.</p>
