<p>Wednesday 1st Nov<br/>Sea a bit calmer and during the morning we picked our escort up and passed dozens of destroyers on patrol duty. They told us there had been a submarine in the vicinity which was the reason for the anxiety yesterday.</p>
<p>On guard again, for the last time as we reach England tomorrow morning.</p>
<p>We were disappointed that land was not seen</p>
