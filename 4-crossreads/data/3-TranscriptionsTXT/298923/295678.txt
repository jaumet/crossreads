 12 January 1918 Saturday Shifted Camp to  KHIRBET DEIRAN , on the foothills of the Range that runs to Jerusalem- Colder but dryer.  Snow fell at Bethlehem Allanson is here still doing despatch carrying he is a good soldier. We do not anticipate any fighting for a couple of months, too wet for transport, by that time the war may be over.  Absolutely soaked to skin, no bivouacs &amp; no blankets or food tonight as all transport is hung up in the Black Soil-
