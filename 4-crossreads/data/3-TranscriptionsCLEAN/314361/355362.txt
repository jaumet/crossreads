This morning so far has been very quiet.July 31st 1916 Foray FarmReceived No 15 letter from Lou this morning.Last night we thought that things were going to liven up, so we all got into a dugout near the Sig. office, but it was not so bad & it soon passed over, one shell however lobbed about three yards in front of the dug out door.
