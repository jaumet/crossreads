Wednesday 28th 1917.After having 3 hours rest we were all up again being anxious to have a look around the Camp. In describing the Camp, I am afraid I am unable to do it justice, but I will do my best. Of what I have seen this Camp which is named Bordon is built on both sides of a long road enclosed with a hedge on both sides as far as the eye can see & the Huts are built of Iron
