Packed up and returned to camp which Place we reached 5 P.M. all clear had a letter from maud Jessie aggie Bell.  had tea and wrote to Bell Croft turned in 10.30 very windy. 
 Saturday May 26.5.17 Stand to 5 A.M. had shower cooked Breakfast and eat it.  6.30 saddled up in full marching order and watered camel left camp 7.30 am out looking for foot marks.
