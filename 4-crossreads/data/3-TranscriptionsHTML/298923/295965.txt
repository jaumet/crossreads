<p>26 October 1918 Saturday<br/>Not available<br/>Took Dolly to dinner at Heliopolis House-   Champagne Band etc   she loves this &amp; has not been out since months before Baby was born-<br/>She does look so pretty in her nice frocks of course I like to take her out but the pennies !!!<br/>oh dear,<br/>In the afternoon we went for a drive with the Babe-<br/>3 of us in one room in this Pension &amp; no nurse is good training for the little Lady- She is never bad tempered.  I mean Dolly.</p>
