She was surprised, at first, at the question &amp; asked the boy his reason for making this inquiry.  He replied that none of the angels which he had seen in pictures had moustaches. <p>After tea we had a bright sing-song in the large Y.M.C.A. Hut.   One of the hymns was "When the roll is called up yonder, I'll be there".   It was sung well by the boys;  the chaplain pertinently remarking that there will be no A.W.L. up there.</p>
<p>We were then addressed by Chaplain - Captain on "The Sentimental-Bloke's religious Views".   The evening was completed by a "Social Hour" &amp; a free bun &amp; tea from the Institution.</p>
