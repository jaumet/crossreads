
Naval Signal
From - Administrator F/Wilhelmshafen
To - VAC
Date - 24 9 4
Time - 3.30 pm
Garrisons posted.  All in suitable quarters.  Flag hoisted proclamation read - Seventeen Germans surrendered - Four of these are officials who will probably be employed.  The others are Planters Missionaries and business men all of whom have taken oath of neutrality - Chief official not yet in but has been sent for
