 To be forwarded to Mrs. E.  Murray Berry St Nowra N.S.W. Australia 
 8.4.15 First Field Coy boarded Ionian 
 9th Nothing important  [indecipherable]. 
 10 Preparation for landing Dardanelles &ndash; forming barrel Piers etc. 
 11th Little work - barrel Piers etc. 
 12th Nothing interest &ndash; Piers &ndash; All going well. 
 13th Same as yesterday 
 14th Did a little barrel Piering. Things quiet. 
 15th Still on Ionian.  Nothing fresh. 
 16th Still doing a little work. Nothing of interest. 
 17th Awaiting orders, nothing fresh. 
 18 Usual routine.  Tired waiting 
 19th Transferred to "Malda".  Got Webb equipment.  Heard that a Torpedo boat "Turkish" attempted to torpedo a transport coming in a few nights ago but missed &amp; was in turn beached. Panic on transport resulted in a good many being drowned 
 20th Nothing of much consequence today. Was told that we are to form part of first landing party.
