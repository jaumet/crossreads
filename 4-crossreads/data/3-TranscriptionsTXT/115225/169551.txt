 Amiens arriving home 11 P.M. we heard during the day that Fritz had broken through at La Bass&eacute; yesterday morning &amp; came right through all along the line up north 
 &nbsp; 
 Apl 11  th  &nbsp; We went at noon to Amiens (S  t   Roche) railhead for supplies &amp; found the 1  st   Div. on their way back up north having been down here about three days - We loaded up &amp; hauled to [Frankvilliers?] to the brigade &amp; got back to Park at 11.15 P.M.&nbsp; fine day but it rained in the evening. 
 &nbsp;
