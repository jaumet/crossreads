<p>69 Gallipoli</p>
<p>26/11/15</p>
<p>I have been back here a fortnight now and in that time things are pretty quiet. We are holding Durants post and Enemy trenches are up 75 yards from ours&nbsp; So not many get hit&nbsp; the shells will come and explode on the trench&nbsp; But then they Do not hit many. This last few Days a sniper has Been giving Some trouble&nbsp; one has Killed two [men?] one the day before yesterday and another today</p>
