<p>about &frac12; mile from Amman Village. The Railway is the Mecca to Damascus line. Amman proved a prize to the Boys.<br/>It is here that our campaign is ended. Beyond this we are told is a 50 mile desert. Practically the whole Turkish Army has been captured &amp; Jacko is completely smashed. The area round here is a sight to see. Amman station is blocked with Jacko transport, They have practically left every thing behind. There are dumps with any amount of equipment. Dead Horses &amp; Camels are all over the place &amp; the smell &amp; Flies <strike>[indecipherable]</strike> are very bad.</p>
