<p>1915<br/>Oct. 13 Wed.<br/>Went over to Heliopolis (No. 1 Gen. Hosp.) to look up a chap who was supposed to be there but could not find him.<br/>Went out to Mena in motor ambulance &ndash; had tea there &ndash; stayed to a concert &ndash; and then back to Gezira at 10.15 p.m.<br/>Water still rising on the Mena Flats.</p>
