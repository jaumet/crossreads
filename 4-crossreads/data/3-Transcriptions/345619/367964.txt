<p>[Written on YMCA paper]<br/>In Hospital<br/>Boulogne<br/>11-8-18</p>
<p>My dearest Wife,<br/>I wish and wish that you did not have to wait so long between the Cable I sent yesterday, and which I am told by a Chaplain should reach you in a few days, and this bit of a letter which you will not get until I am real well again, I know you and Mother will be awfully worried when you get the Cable and will think it is worse than it really is, but I thought it would</p>
