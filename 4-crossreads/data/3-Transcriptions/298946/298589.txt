<p>23rd Aug Arrived Cape Town</p>
<p>Ashore this evening with Padre</p>
<p>23rd -29th  Ashore on leave every day</p>
<p>30th Left at 2.30 pm for Durban. Boat rolled badly.  Many sick.  Almost stopped opposite lighthouse</p>
<p>31st 218 miles (Sunday)</p>
<p>Going better but still rolling heavily.</p>
<p>Services as usual</p>
<p>Meeting in afternoon. " Reconstruction from Christian Standpoint"</p>
<p>1st  248 miles. Classes recommenced. Rumoured pay tomorrow. Badly needed on all hands.</p>
<p>2nd 263</p>
<p>3rd Arrived Durban last night.  Anchored all night off harbour, noticing the twinkling lights of town &amp; wharves.  Ship rolled pretty badly.  6 am started to move towards land from which we had drifted a considerable distance, and went at very slow speed.</p>
