<p>light cruiser we passed this morning was the "Pioneer" of the Australian Squadron and which has Austln reservists as the crew; she has been anchored here for the past couple of weeks.<br/><u>Monday 13th Septr.</u>  This morning Natal Mercury had a big article in the shipping column concerning our brave craft.  At 11am a representative of one of the other papers came aboard and received particulars of our voyage from the Purser and had a big article in the news page of tonights paper.  After tea had music in the 2nd Mates room till bed.<br/><u>Tuesday 14th Sept.</u>  At 6am we were shifted to a berth</p>
