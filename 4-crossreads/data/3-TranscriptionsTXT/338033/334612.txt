 Arriving there late in the afternoon. Immediately we removed our gear we were warned to mount guard within half an hour. In order to do this we had a hurried tea and readjusted our gear. We had a tiring tour of duty for 24 hours and were then relieved. No sooner had we been dismissed than wen turned in, and enjoyed a much earned pest.
