Went back to La Barasse &ndash; walked down to St. Marcel and caught a tram near St Valentine (where there is another British camp) back to the city. The country round Marseille is wonderfully pretty &ndash; everything so fresh &amp; green, little narrow lanes bordered by low hedges  gras  grass covered meadows besprinkled with poppies, tall mountains in the background, etc. etc. The people are very countrified &amp; "aimable". Speaking with a strong southern accent.  Thurs  Frid 16th June:- Our Unit here in Marseille has commenced to disappear. On Tuesday 10 Nurses were sent to Boulonge, 10 to Etaples &amp; 5 to Abbeville.
