<p>Thursday 21 Feb.<br/>Pay at 10 am G. W. Stanbury.</p>
<p>Visited joint GR &amp; bury. Returned via BN &amp; fixed up re gas doors being done by Spr Worthington.</p>
<p>Afternoon out to joint in paddock. Obtained pump but finally fished out cable and jointed.</p>
<p>Fine day. Evening bit crook &amp; had Alex Lilburn down for an occasion.</p>
