  
 Friday 9 November 1917. 
 Thursday 8th (Contin'd) message for 2 stretcher parties. I felt dead beat & winded. Rested 1/4 hr and had to lead a cacolet camel with 2 wounded to  Clearing  Collecting Station. Relieved by B Section Handed over patients; packed up with full gear and moved off with A.C.F.A. 2 miles closer up than Collecting Station & established Collecting Station there at 5 p.m. Prepared tea of bully & biscuits & turned in after having my septic hands dressed, 1 st  time for  almost  60 hrs.  Cpl May killed this morng.  
 Friday 9th 
 Did an hrs picquet from 3.30 to 4.30 am. 
 Awakened at dawn by a Taube that dropped 4 bombs on a ridge right close. 
 Had a shave. 8 days since last shave. Appreciated it too. Saddle up at 8.30 with full gear. This time in the group to relieve B Sec. group at 2 nd  Battn. I.C.C. advanced dressing station. Things quiet here as " Jacko has now got the wind up him ". Picquet from 10 pm.
