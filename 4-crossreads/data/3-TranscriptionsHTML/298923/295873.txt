<p>26 July 1918 Friday   (Whole page crossed out)<br/>Dear old Day I know she is busyier than ever now so I must try &amp; write a little plainer-  I have bought to sell to the men 7 hundred weight of grapes &amp; 3 Casks of WINE-  better to be supplied with good stuff at a decent price than be robbed &amp; get into trouble.<br/>I wonder what you have done re the old home-   Who will clean the Spouts &amp; mend the fence!  etc etc:-   I only hope Joe turns up in 6 months time again-<br/>Love to you all.<br/><u>Censored </u></p>
