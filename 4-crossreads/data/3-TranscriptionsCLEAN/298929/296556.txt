
7th  Preparations for the biggest stunt of all so far is marvellous  12 inch Hows have been put in & the miles of transport.The attack has started on a 12 mile front.  Led by 3000 tanks and covered by the biggest barrage of the war.  We were up very close to the hopping off point & last night was shelled & gassed
