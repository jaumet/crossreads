  14th   We steamed up Doncaster Channel in Tasmania about 3 P.M. &amp; anchored in Barnes' Bay. 
 We passed a queer little lighthouse on our starboard side called Ironpot Lighthouse. The quarantine station could be seen tucked away in a little nook near the mouth of the Derwent River. We discharged the Tasmanians about 45 in all pushed off again about 4 P.M. The little we have seen of Tasmania &amp; the waterway has impressed me very much.
