<p>Although the bullets were strays they fell like rain upon the ground the S.M. lead us over.  We returned down a gully safely.  Goldsmith was struck upwards through the right eye &amp; in the arm whilst we were lying flat after a rush.  (Got very tired rushing with equipment &amp; rifle to carry.)  Took G. to the Red + base.  On the way the poor fellow managed to sing us a verse of a song.  Helped the beach party to carry ammunition.</p>
