<p>Thursday 19 July 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille 7 a.m. No parade until 9.15 after breakfast. Appreciated late rising.</p>
<p>Inoculated against Cholera &amp; Para-typhoid at 11 a.m.</p>
<p>Wrote letters during afternoon to Home &amp; D&#39;nong.</p>
<p>24 hrs light duty for those inoculated.</p>
