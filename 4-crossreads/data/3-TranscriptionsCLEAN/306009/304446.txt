
1915Sep. 21 Tue.Got leave to go into Alexandria to-day. Left Montaza by 2 p.m. train to Victoria (4 miles). Caught a tram there for Mustapha Camp - Then went on to the city - Met several No. 2 G.H. chaps who had been detailed for overseas duty - Visited Mr. D. Allen. S.M. of 1st Alex. Scouts & also Rev. Fry, Local Sec.Struck an old Scout serving in the 20th. A.A.S.C. - we had tea at the Soldiers & Sailors' Club - then went to the Pictures.Caught a tram back to Victoria & train to Montazah - Back at 10 p.m., Alex full of Troops Aust, N.Z. French, Naval, Eng, etc.
