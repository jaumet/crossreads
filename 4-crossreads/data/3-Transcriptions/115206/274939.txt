<p>Memoranda<br/>Breadth of 8 feet, and a height of 12 feet. The air is polluted with the stench of decomposition of human bodies. On the left hand side of the chamber there are two vats situated about halfway down the length of the room. At the far end there is an ordinary chopping block with a knife. <br/>In the first vat is a little water and portion of man's body. Lying on the floor in grotesque attitudes are twelve bodies of soldiers wearing the uniform of our enemy. Directly above the vats a hole is noticeable</p>
