<p>Septembre 3<br/>Working on refugee huts. Dirty weather with heavy squally rain. Nothing of importance to note.</p>
<p>Septembre 4<br/>Went to Aire to get instruction in heavy bridging-steel. Very vivid aerial activity last night. Enemy planes bombed a number of villages including St Omer, Hazebrouke &amp; Le Nieppe &amp; it is thought a lot of damage was done. A good many casualties are reported from St Omer.<br/>Section working on refugee hutting.</p>
