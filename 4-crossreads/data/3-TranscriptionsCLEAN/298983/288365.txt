23rd January 1918Have fared well for tucker 5 of us got 4 tins of Bully between us & were able to buy good bread on the Boat
Thursday 23rd WednesdayHad a very light breackfast Bacon & Bread at 6 & started right away for the Boat Southampton seems a fine place Went straight on to the Boat & anchored in the Harbour until 7.15 P.M. There are a lot of horses on board
