In front of Morcourt, Bty PositionWeather fine and clear.At 4 am this morning we had a fairly heavy dose of gas, sneezing stuff sent everyone seedy. Fritz cleaned up all our lines again and shelled Bty fairlyheavily during morning. Had a day's solid going on Bdge and Bty lines.During lunch hour Fritz opened up on 60 pdr Batteryand knocked them about badly. He killed a sgt there, and jiggered the lines about until they  looked like concertinas. Knipe to L'aison for stunt tomorrow. Good luck
