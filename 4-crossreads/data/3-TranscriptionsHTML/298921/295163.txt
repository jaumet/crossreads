<p>July, 1915<br/>Sunday 11<br/>Have not gone to Church too busy in O.Room.<br/>Brigadier Chauvel here from Dardanelles yesterday he promised me I should go with next draft also Col <strike>Meredith</strike> - This War will last for 18 months yet is the current opinion &ndash; Brig: says a Gooska when a shell comes says<br/>Englishmen get into "Dug-outs" 2nd Shell Gooska get into 3rd Shell Australians say you B-B-<br/>This sounds tripe but our men have been very game thro' it all; naturally blow a bit.</p>
