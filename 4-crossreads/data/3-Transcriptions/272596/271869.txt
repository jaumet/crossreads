<p>17 th. Sat up late last night discussing farming &amp; the land &amp; etc, there are a few city chaps on our table who are going to give it a fly &amp; they are very anxious to hear our versions of it, drawbacks, advantages &amp; etc</p>
<p>Ran into King Georges Sound at 6.15 this morning, had an early "reveille" &amp; breakfast, it was mighty cold on top a heavy wind tearing &amp; beating the open waters of the Sound to fine spray which occasionally dashed over the ship</p>
<p>Albany looked very pretty when the sun came out from the clouds put me in mind of Gibraltar with its red roofs &amp; white walls with 2 big hills flanking the town only &amp;</p>
