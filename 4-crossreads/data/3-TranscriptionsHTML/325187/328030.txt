<p>[Saturday 14 October 1916 ]</p>
<p>Connie &amp; Laura Theatre<br/><strike>Misleading Lady</strike> &ndash; Globe- around the clock.<br/>Went &amp; saw Mardi Ritchie at Swedish Hospital, she looks so sweet in Uniform  I introduced my wife so that she could tell you what she is like.<br/>Took Laura &amp; Connie to Globe Theatre to see "When the Clock goes around" a pretty little show.  Tea at Trocadero Dinner at Frascati I'm getting a bit tired of spending money on all this frivol.</p>
