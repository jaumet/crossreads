<p>7<br/>a very good light.<br/>We hope that the dugout will be gas &amp; shell proof, save for the large shells.<br/>Had quite a nice letter from Miss Elanie asking me to come &amp; see them if I get to England. Also letters from Nancy Morris &amp; Edie Budden.<br/>Au-revoir dear, with very much love to you all.<br/>Always your loving brother<br/>Fred.<br/>I practice French with a French girl near here but it is tedious /FEP</p>
