
The many mosques are well worth a visit - that of El Azhar [also spelt Al-Azhar] being a University founded in 988 - in 1912 there were about 15000 students & 500 odd teachers.  The complete curriculum lasts 17 years & seems to be a sort of high school & continuation school.Instruction is free & bread & spending money are supplied to the students.  Near one of the this mosque is the town gate called Bab Zuweileh [also spelt Bab Zuweila].  On it are hung shreds of clothing, teeth etc. - votive offerings placed there by sufferers hoping for a cure.  The museums are noted.  The Citadel
