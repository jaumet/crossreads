  
 Oct 1917 Tuesday 16th Had a general look round went to a coal pit caught the 10 pm express for London arriving at 7 am Wednesday 17 Had a look at London Bridge The Tower Madam Taussards the strand Marble Arch & various other places of interest.   Went to the Club & met an old mate.   Our boy have been knocked about in this last stunt Thursday 18th Left for camp at 10.15 am arrived at 2.30 pm.  It is the finest day for a fortnight Friday 19th Pension Clarification  another fine day Sunday 21st Visited Paington & Brixton Monday 22nd A wet miserable day
