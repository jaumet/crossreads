 Friday 18 Dr De Garis returned from Salonique bringing Mrs McHardy White. Lovely mild bright day. 
 Sat.19th 
 Mrs Green went to Salonique &amp; took Mrs McH.W. Home. 
 Sunday 20 
 Lovely day &ndash; perfect frosty mornig no wind. Walked with Tubby &amp; saw bulbs opening all over hillsides Radomir's slava. 
 Monday 21 
 Deep fog made day cold. No incident. 
 Tues 22 
 Fog in morning &ndash; glorious afternoon. 
 E. C. Brown &amp; I went to Serb mess for music Rugitich a nuisance.
