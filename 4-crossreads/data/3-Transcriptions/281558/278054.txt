<p>Friday Oct 26th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : showery : classification in morn &amp; furlough parade in afternoon : sent a dozen views of Weymouth to Mum &amp; Aunt Myra :</p>
<p>Saturday Oct 27th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : showery : cookhouse fatigue in morn : stroll to Warminster in afternoon &amp; met Amos Miller &amp; Vic Berghoffer : wrote in army hut after tea</p>
<p>Sunday Oct 28th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : frost &amp; cold day : fatigue in morn : wrote in afternoon : song service in Army hut after tea</p>
<p>Monday Oct 29th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : frost : showery day : fatigue in morn : parade for furlough particulars in afternoon : read in the army hut after tea</p>
