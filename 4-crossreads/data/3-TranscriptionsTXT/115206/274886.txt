
November 1918.Tuesday 12
Hosptl. BirminghamWeather fine and clear. Spent quite a happy time up town, amongst the crowds. Could not get anything stronger than sugarless tea to drinkArrived home a little late. Brad went out to the tents, and Les pretty disappointed judging by his outward appearance.Spent a nice quiet afternoon and evening at Sutton Coldfields with - Managed to get some Barribals and Kirchners.
[William Barribal was a London artist, famed for his images of women and also for ww1 recruitment posters.Ernst Kirchner was a German artist influential in the Expressionist movement]
