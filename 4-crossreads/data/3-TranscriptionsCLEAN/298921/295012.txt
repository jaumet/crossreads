  
 February, 1915 Monday 8 Go to Pay Office for Pay & left old Canvas Bag for safekeeping also New Big Kit Bag with mufti and Trouser Stretches covered in oilsheet containing Rug, Mufti Overcoat, & Trousers - opening a/c with Anglo & Egyptian Bank for Pay & deposit Gold. Cairo is a beautiful city & Shepherds Hotel is better & larger than the Australia or Menzies. Last night went to the Casino dancing girls, Bar, etc. etc:-
