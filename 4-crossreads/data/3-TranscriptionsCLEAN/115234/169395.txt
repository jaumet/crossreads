 was one of the first ten men into Bapaume.  Had a stouch with about fifty Fritzies.  Had to hop it lively.  I went out to bring in a wounded mate was shot in the leg & shoulder by snipers.  Eventually got my mate out with assistance of others.  Went to 15th field Ambulance then to Casualty Clearing Station. Edgehill thence to Rouen.
