<p>started &amp; had to march 4 miles before we reached her, &amp; it just started to rain as we got there.   We reported &amp; after a little while we were taken to some quarters where we are to put the night in.   After we had got our gear off we went into the little Belgian caf&eacute; where we had a good tea on bread butter &amp; coffee &amp; eggs the Belgian girls are very funny with their broken English.   They are pretty smart at their work.</p>
<p>30th.   It rained like cats &amp; dogs all night fully 2 inches must have fallen, we got up at 8.30 shaved cleaned up &amp; had our breakfast in the caf&eacute;.</p>
