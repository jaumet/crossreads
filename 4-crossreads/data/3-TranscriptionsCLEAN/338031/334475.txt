  
 December 15 to 25 The quality & quantity of food on board began to slacken. This was due to the long stay at Sierra Leone & the fact that we had to carry our share of the men on the transport Port Lincoln which had grounded on the sand bank at the entrance to the harbour at Freetown, so that Christmas Dinner was a comparatively poor turn out.
