 92 Nov 22nd Cabbage &amp;c same as our tommies &ndash; 
 Cinema tonight when a very startling Detective yarn was filmed &ndash; war films of the Kaiser with Turkish forces &ndash; German lesson from 5 &ndash; 6 no private lesson tonight as arranged 
 November 23rd Up early &ndash; Recd letter from Father dated 22-7-17 &amp; &amp; 19-7-17 also from Doris 10/8/17 &ndash; Recd 4 parcels 
 Football held today &ndash; Did not go. Lecture tonight on Rubber Plan 
 Played Bridge &ndash; To German class again at 6 pm &ndash; 7 pm &ndash; 
 We are still pushing in the vicinity of Bullecourt &amp; have to date captured 8000 including 180 officers &ndash; also numerous guns The Germans here were completely surprised &amp; the success of our manouvers was greatly due to the tanks, which have been improved wonderfully since our failure. We are also continuing our offensive at Egypt &ndash; 
 November 24th Recd parcel 2 pcls &ndash; walk this mng &ndash; did not go &ndash; German Class from 12 &ndash; 1 &amp; 6 &ndash; 7 &ndash; 
 Concert this evening when Col. Williamson presided &ndash; It was rather a good show. I was called upon to sing, but did not oblige on this occasion. 
 The Concert Comm are organising a pierrot show &amp; hope to give us a very good performance next Saturday &ndash; thence once fortnightly The adjt has arranged for a stage to be built, &amp; authority for them to purchase special dresses &amp;c. 
 The Commdt has at last approved of the Concert Comm purchasing costumes &amp; promises them his assistance always &ndash; 
 adjourned to Room 67 where we partook of some excellent beer &ndash; 
 Antill has received some very bad news that his wife has died whilst giving birth to a child (the first) the child is also dead &ndash; 'tis an awful pity &ndash; poor man is prostrate with grief &ndash; they have only been married 12 months &ndash; The Commdt adjt &amp; Censor have been exceedingly good to him, to say nothing of officers here &ndash; 
 November 25th Sunday Holy Communion 8 am &amp; Morning Service 10.00 
 Feeling off colour today, especially early this mng &ndash; 
 General meeting at 1 pm when the new Committee was elected &ndash; Olphert as President &ndash; Nervis as usual Hot aired about nothing &ndash; &amp; accepted all suggestions as personalities &ndash; he reserved his right to appoint the Presd. &amp; O.C. orderlies &amp; O.C. parcels &ndash; ordinary [indecipherable] Committee meeting was entirely ignored. The barbed wire itis element are not represented at all on the Committee much to their annoyance &ndash; 
 Played Bridge &ndash; Had dinner with McAnally &amp; Co &ndash; 
 November 26th Snowing this mng &ndash; first fall this winter &ndash; it is very cold also &ndash; Rearranged my bed, double banking with the padre, much to the annoyance of a few officers &ndash;
