<p>through their work like a clock to be exact, it was impossible to find a fault &amp; shewed us what could be done by Systematic Training &amp; Discipline. Of what I have seen up to date it is a credit to the Drill Instructors. After a splendid Hot Tea provided I took a stroll up to the Village of Borden &amp; was struck with the quaint Homes of the People. The Houses are built of Stone &amp; Slate &amp; Tile Roofs. After a pleasant walk up &amp; down I adjourned to the Picture Hall to see a programe which was crowded with Troops. Afterwards I being tired &amp; sleepy I made my way back to my quarters, &amp; made up my bed for the Night. After enjoying a quiet Chat of our day experiences when lights out was sounded at 10-15 Pm all hands were soon fast asleep.</p>
