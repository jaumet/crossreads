<p>16<br/>2/6/16<br/>Very quiet day to-day but to-morrow will see things busy again as the 13th Bde leave. We entrain on Sunday so our time in Egypt is limited now. I haven't received word from home for some time now I suppose it will pick us up in France.</p>
<p>13-6-16<br/>Arrived Marseilles.</p>
<p>23/6/16.<br/>Quite a lot has happened between the 2nd &amp; present date but it is all so impressed on my memory that the need of a diary is out of the question.</p>
