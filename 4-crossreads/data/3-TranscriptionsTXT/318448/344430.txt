 17:5:18 On the last day on which I wrote we reached Talut ed Dune, a spot -  &amp; a very dirty one at that &ndash; in the hills about a mile or two in the hills towards Jerusalem. Here we languished &amp; Hope nearly died but finally on the 15th we left for Jerusalem which we reached just as it was breaking dawn after a very tedious &amp; slow trek.
