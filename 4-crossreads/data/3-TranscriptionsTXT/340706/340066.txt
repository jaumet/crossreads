We go to the upper promenoir which is not frequented by the demo monde & around the exterior edge of this runs a glass promenade scintillating with light on which tight clad girls (50) gambol & on which a danseuse gives a magnificent exhibition. The costumes & staging are far more magnificent than Australia has ever scene seen
I miss nearly an hour of the performance by going to [Gare du Nord (3 frs) auto) to change my instructions to my orderly whom I do not see. Finally I return to theatre & find the cricket or rather she finds me & at end of performance we see girls to their home 134 Rue d'Henri Monnier & slip off to our hotel
11-4-19 (Fri)McColl does not rise till nearly noon & as my cash has long ago vanished With difficulty we dressed & without any coffee or midday meal unshaven just catch 1.15 train for Abbeville hoping for the best.
The train passes out from Nous (du Nord) through pleasant villages amongst forest & undulating hills covered with verdant green, Surrey beautiful as it was on my first
