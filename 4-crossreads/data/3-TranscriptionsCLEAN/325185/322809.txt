 26/10/17 Dear Clyde - I have forwarded Dick's Wallet to you by registered post. Kiwi is back now with Weatherheath. Les has not yet returned. I had a letter from Tom - he was then still in France and doing well. I may be there soon. So long. I am etc.  Jack  [To the right of this is written;] No 2857 L/Cpl A. C. Giles 1st Batt A.I.F.
