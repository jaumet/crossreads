 We are camped on the flat alongside the town &amp; about a mile away there is a ring of  hit  low hills &ndash; along the top of which about every &frac14; mile  are a  apart are a series of block houses with a small outpost in each. I should judge there are about 4000 of us here all told &amp; if rumour is correct about 10,000 Bedouins some 5 miles to the west of the Camp.
