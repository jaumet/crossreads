FEBRUARY 1917
28 WEDNESDAY
Fine but dull. Cleaning
harness all morning & we
went out exercising horses
in afternoon. We have to
go out with bandoliers ect
now & the O C waggon lines
has to be with all exercising
parties. No letters from home
yet & all the others have got 
late letters. Wish they would
turn up.
