<p>24. Sunday.<br/>Beautiful warm sun all day. Miss Hodge had tea at  Lyceum for Helena Normanton then Miss H &amp; I went home to supper with Mrs Baft.  Dr. Schutlebury from Endell St. there. Fog down at Piccadilly. Clear moonlight at Golder's Green.</p>
<p>25. Monday<br/>On Monday night shivered with Malaria again. dull and cold. Struggled out of bed &amp; spent a miserable day on sofa. Dull &amp; cold.</p>
<p>27. Wednesday<br/> Feeling very depressed and weak. Cold &amp; grey</p>
