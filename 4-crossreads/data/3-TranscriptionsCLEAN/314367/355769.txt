 Sunday 7.30 am. 21st-7-1915-  We are up - going at this moment to No 9 Buoy for Anchorage - 7 days & 16 hours out from Rabaul - Saty we steamed magnificently & all went very well indeed - We were all up - dressed in clean white & on the quarterdeck soon after 6 am - We passed the Heads at 7 - I've been 6 months in the Navy to-morrow =  11 am  Mails have arrived - I have one from Rawdon & three from you - Papers galore - Give Rawdon my love & say his shooting was all right - I have no more cartridges but they can be got 6 - a packet - anywhere - Sing or Utz -
