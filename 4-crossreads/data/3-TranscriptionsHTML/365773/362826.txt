<p>after landing before I fired a shot, &amp; the others two days later after I had fired a h-1 of a lot of shots. Our boys struck a pretty tough job and were knocked about a great deal.<br/>I am in Egyptian Hospital was too late to get into our own as they were full up, we chaps who are here are real downhearted we expected to be nursed by our own nurses &amp; doctors.<br/>We were brought to Alexandria by boats from Dardanelles &amp; here by special trains. Well Mother dear I will close now with fondest love to all don't forget Lizzie, I am,<br/>your loving son<br/>Ken</p>
