<p>CASSELL<br/>Monday 22<br/>An Australian mail in, I got 2 one from Muriel a good letter &amp; one from the bank good also<br/>Tuesday 23<br/>The weather is getting colder every day, but never the less it as commenced more favourably than last year<br/>Wednesday 24<br/>I think we are winning good news in today<br/>Down on the Somme we knocked Fritz pretty hard<br/>Thursday 25<br/>Birdwood, General of the A.I.F was here to day and gave the boys their medal's</p>
