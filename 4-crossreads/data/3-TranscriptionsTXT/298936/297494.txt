 December, 1915 
 Friday 10 
 Anglo Indian doctors, R.A.M.C. orderlies &amp; nurses of Queen Alexander's nursing reserve on board. 
 Treated splendidly. 
 Leg quite straightforward &amp; progressing wonderfully. 
 Wrote field service cards for about 8 people. 
 Getting fit again onboard with the good food.
