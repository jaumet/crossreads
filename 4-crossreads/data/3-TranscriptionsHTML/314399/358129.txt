<p>Monday 12 May 1919<br/>More driving today. Did fairly well. did some work on the bench too. Went out to Brixton for tea. Saw Bill. He is ever so much better. Looks well today. Bonny day again.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13 May 1919<br/>Met Dad at 4.45 pm. Went round to Harry Brevitt then to the Trocadero for tea. From there to Molinari's for dinner. After dinner we went to see Marie Lohr in Victory. Enjoyed it immensely. [indecipherable] but acting good. Came home after the show.<br/>Janet, Marie &amp; Co went to Eastbourne.</p>
