<p>the retirement our Brigade did excellently, an officer friend of mine an awfully good man got an M.C., it was the finest small affair we have been in, the 2nd were in reserve, another excellent chap a pal of mine was killed in it, we put in our sick leave in England together, I forget whether I told you or not, it made me think hard when I saw his grave on a green slope behind the Hun line, all the good old hands are going</p>
