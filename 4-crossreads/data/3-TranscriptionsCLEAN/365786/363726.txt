Then there is the personal side & I must say that I think this is the side that touches us women most.  We often hear it asked "what right have I to vote away the life of another's woman's son?"   Now now  why didn't we, in 1914 & 1915 object to our men leaving here to risk their lives for us & our country?  What did we do then?
