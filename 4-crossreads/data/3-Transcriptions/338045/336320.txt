<p>make my final departure.  Parade at 8-30 a.m. on station.  Plenty of German prisoners with their French guards.  Obtain a few badges as souvenirs.  Several aeroplanes going up the line.  An ambulance train of wounded arrived &amp; another to Havre for England.  Hundreds of English Reinforcements from England.  Waiting round until 12 noon talking to some Tommies &amp; having tea in Y.M.C.A. Hut, &amp; leave the station for lunch &amp; last walk around that fine old town Rouen.  Good-bye to Legge, Palliser, Scotty &amp; all the boys, &amp; go up town to buy a knife, soap box &amp; some food &amp; cigarettes.  Parade again at 3 p.m. &amp; equipped</p>
