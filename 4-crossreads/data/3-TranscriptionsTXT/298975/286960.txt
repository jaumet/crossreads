 Sept Thursday  27th Early breckfast Busseled about without any reason caught train at Dinton 9 AM Arrived Waterloo about 12 Marched to Horsefury Rd &amp; were turned loose. Had dinner at War Chest an exelent instituon Wandered around (4 of us) &amp; got a room at 150A Southamton Row Near Russel Square. A Salvation Army place &amp; very good. Wandered about some more &amp; went to a rotton play A Little bit of Fluff 
 Friday 28th Went to The Bank &amp; Wax Works Etc Lindfield Gardens in the evening 
 Saturday 29th Met Miss Murray had a look at Picture gallery &amp; spent most of the day at Kew Gardens
