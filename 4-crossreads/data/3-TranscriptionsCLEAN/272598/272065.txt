 Sunday 4/6/16 Our new skipper making matters rather warm; this morning half a dozen of us were given pack drill after the section had been dismissed, for very trivial matters.  Unfortunately, I appeared on parade with a button undone, subsequently I was given pack drill; he is a bit over the odds.  Very warm again to-day.
