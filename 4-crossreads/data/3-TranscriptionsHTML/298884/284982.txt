<p>He then ordered the natives to pull their rickshaws off the stone barricade &amp; on to a path which was studded at intervals with blocks of stone in the centre.</p>
<p>Again the natives demurred &amp; again Brolga burst forth into a torrent of abuse which had its effect. We were now quite near a bridge or a main road on which we could see a group of Aust soldiers &amp; a number of natives gathered greatly interested in this sudden appearance of a string of rickshaws being pulled over this forbidden ground.</p>
