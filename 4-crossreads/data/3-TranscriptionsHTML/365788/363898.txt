<p>1st Field Amb<br/>7 Mar 16.</p>
<p>My dearest Irene,<br/>Since writing the enclosed letter I <strike>enclosed</strike> received a parcel containing 1 very nice pair of sox, 1 tooth brush 1 very nice cake of Cashmere Boquet Soap  I cake Pear's [indecipherable] &amp; 1 block Camphor for all of which I am very grateful.<br/>We have not left yet, but I expect very soon a new part of the sky will cover us.<br/>We had a lovely swim the other day Carpawarpal</p>
