<p>had to wait for some time, a good many of the lads went in for a swim, it was 12 oclock before we got to Mahommedah &amp; we rode the horses back to Camp that the men going on leave rode over;  we arrived back in Camp after having a very enjoyable holiday;  <strike>Major</strike> Col. Granville received leave to visit Cairo yesterday &amp; just managed to get through in time as no one is allowed to go any further than Port Said from here now on account of a case or two of Cholera breaking out in the Hospital at Port Said, Ireland has overstayed his leave &amp; is to be put under arrest when he returns</p>
