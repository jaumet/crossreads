 May, 1916 Sunday 21 Got up when the breakfast bugle went. After breakfast I went for a walk to the old Helmia camp. It is like a city of the dead. After tea I went for a long walk round Matariah in the cool. Write letters home 
 Monday 22 We were granted leave again today &amp; after dinner I went out to Patti's &amp; made the acquaintance of their son who returned from the Italian front on Thursday last
