<p>Wednesday 1 March 1916<br/>Loaded up carrier with wheat.<br/>Dick &amp; Geoff plowing all day.  Father &amp; I fixed up sheep.  Put 5 horses in Castle Rock.</p>
<p>Thursday 2 March 1916<br/>Dick &amp; I plowing till dinner time.<br/>Then carrier came for wheat.  Father &amp; Geoff mustered Waterfall.<br/>F. Warner came down.<br/>Put 5 horses over in Lease.</p>
