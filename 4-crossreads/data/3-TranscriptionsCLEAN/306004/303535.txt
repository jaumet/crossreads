  
 24.9.16 Sun 24th Had a very quiet day there was a Church parade this afternoon but did not go. leave has been stopped to Port Said with the exception of the men allowed to go in for a weeks spell at the Rest Camp & these have to be recommended by the Dr. 
 25.9.16 Mon 25th Was told confidentially this morning by Sutherland that the 1st L H Bde leave here Thursday morning for Hill 70 & are going to Maadi; The Sergts mess is now going strong & it is good to have nothing to do but stroll up to the Mess for meals
