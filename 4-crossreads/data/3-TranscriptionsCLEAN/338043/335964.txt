  
 Aug. 28th Visit the Dentist at A.G.B.D. - In the evening I see first Picture Show (French) since coming to this country - Strange to say the girl playing "lead" in Star Picture is Enid Bennett  from our own Australia. 
 Aug. 29th Show at 40th General Hospital is cancelled - Study of French & English - Wonderful battle successes - Australians always prominent.
