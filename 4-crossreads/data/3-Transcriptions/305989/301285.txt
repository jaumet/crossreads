<p>Sunday 2 December 1917<br/>Grazing in morning.  Nothing doing.  Picquet at night.  Wrote some letters.</p>
<p>Monday 3 December 1917<br/>Grazing in morning, quiet &amp; windy day.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4 December 1917<br/>No grazing, dubbined up saddles instead.  Got news of move.  Left camp at 12 &amp; proceeded to Jaffa.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5 December 1917<br/>Arrived at Jaffa 2.30 a.m. &amp; stood to arms 4 &amp; changed position of camp at daylight, stayed the day under easy gaze of Jacko.  Good orchestral concert at night.</p>
<p>Thursday 6 December 1917<br/>Still at Jaffa, usual routine, camped in orange orchard.</p>
<p>Friday 7 December 1917<br/>Rained after "stand to" and kept going all day off &amp; on;  left Jaffa at 5 proceeded back to Ayunkara, arriving about 9.30 raining.</p>
<p>Saturday 8 November 1917<br/>Raining in early morning and continuing through the morning.  Jerusalem fell.</p>
