<p>Saturday 26 August 1916<br/>Wrote to Father (13).<br/>West &amp; I were put on fatigue at Canteen picking up empty beer bottles and packing them in cases.  Thorpe &amp; Dawson out on a stunt over the old battle field.  On Canteen guard all the evening.</p>
<p>Sunday 27 August 1916<br/>Canteen guard all day.  Some gentleman thought he had a better right to my camera &amp; compas than I did so took it out of my haversack.<br/>Taube flew round.</p>
