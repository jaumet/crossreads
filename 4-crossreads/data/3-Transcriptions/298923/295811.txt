<p>25 May 1918 Saturday<br/>2 <strike>letters from Laura I am afraid Charlie [indecipherable] gets on her nerves</strike><br/>Paid all ranks today some spend it on "Two-up" other's on "Souvenirs" of all sorts at robber prices &amp; again most goes on Tucker, very peaceful here, no Aeroplanes, only distant gun fire.<br/>Enclose some photos of Jordan etc from Jim Ayre- Flies bad big &amp; little, same as Riverina, but I've never seen a Blown Sheep, perhaps it is because of the big fat tail they all carry- certainly not more than 7 lbs wool per sheep.</p>
