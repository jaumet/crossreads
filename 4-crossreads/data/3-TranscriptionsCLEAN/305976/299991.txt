We rode all night through mud & water & at day break Monday came across a patrol.Monday April 1The Turks at this time were practically on us in large numbers & it was only the darkness which pulled us through. 3 of our boys were caught through going back for some gear.We rode on till we reach Esault situated half way in the hills of Moab & had breakfast Bully & Biscuit & pushed on for Shunet Nimron arriving about 2 pm. We were so worn out that we slept all afternoon & night without waking
