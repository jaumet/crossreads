<p>1915<br/>June 16 Wed.<br/>To-day at 1 p.m. it was 115 in the shade &ndash; about our warmest day so far.<br/>Much too hot for patients as their wounds will not heal.<br/>Should not be at all surprised if we moved on shortly to either Malta or England.</p>
