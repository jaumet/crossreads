<p>November 1918.<br/>Tuesday 26</p>
<p>Hospital and Wembley<br/>Left hospital at 10 am and spent a very quiet time at Hsferry Road.<br/>Arrived at Wembley in due course. Met O on the platform<br/>Had a very pleasant evening &ndash; a musical one with Vic and a few others.<br/>Had lunch with Bert in town. Received a letter of some character from D.B.F.</p>
