<p>I caught the 9 pm train to Perth, 12 miles away. The trains are very slow &amp; dirty. We travelled 1st class &amp; the compartments were no cleaner than a horse box on our own railways. The railway gauge is only about 3' 6" &amp; of course teams the same.</p>
<p>I helped the Nurses over with their luggage &amp; helped them into a cab. The horse was very frightened &amp; nearly pawed me 5 or 6 times but eventually they (4 of them) got aboard. Ellis &amp; Dev High, our new mate, had walked on so Dave &amp; I hurried &amp; caught them up. We stayed at the same</p>
