<p>with Jerry's shells it got worse and worse until you could only see a few feet in front of you it was most uncanny as the shells were bursting all around one.<br/>The progress was slow, the ground very hard to walk over then after we had gone 5 miles or so the fogg lifted &amp; the shells were not so bad as Jerry was so taken by surprise that he had started to retreat with great haste. <br/>It was about now that we saw the first prisoners brought down, both they and our men being wounded &amp; the look on the mens faces was something to stay in ones memory for ever.
