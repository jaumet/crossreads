Work during the day has been very slack & I hope to get away tomorrow to go up & see a special dugout near the line. I can combine business with pleasure as I really want to know what stocks they hold.
Thursday 1st March 1917
Things very quiet the last two days very few cases coming through. No one yet knows the extent of Fritz's retirement, our chaps moving forward cautiously & consolidating as they go. Went out to day to the advanced possies one of which is a most elaborate affair. 'Tis a series of tunnels extending for miles, very deep down & provides accomodation for ambulances, signallers
