  
 31 January, Friday 
 F & ice The Stunt comes off & the supper, dancing & games are tres bon. Recd running shoes from George. I am very quite at show & cannot get to sleep at night for thinking of I.O.M. 
 1 February, Saturday 
 F & ice Go down to YWCA to dance but do very little. Come Home take 2 WAAC's Home. Dance mostly with Alice who is very nice dancer. Came Home & read until Ellis comes Home. Dicky & Dicky go Home for first time.
