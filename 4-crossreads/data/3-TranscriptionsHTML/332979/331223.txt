<p>&amp; around &amp; if one man allows him to fall down he has to take his place this game is a favorite pastime. After Tea our Corp gave a Concert &amp; enjoyed by all, we have some good talent although only about 25 &percnt; gave an item, our next concert others will assist. Light's now go out at 8.30 Pm &amp; all the Port holes ar closed up tight as I believe we are now in the Danger Zone, as <strike>we</strike> to where we are, nobody nows for sure. The Weather has been Cloudy &amp; Drizzly days &amp; we will all be glad when we have some Sunny Weather. Lights out at 8.30 Pm was to early for us all so to amuse ourselves in the Dark, some of the boys started imatating the Fowls, Ducks &amp; Cattle it was very amusing</p>
