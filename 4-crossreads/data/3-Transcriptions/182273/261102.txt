
<p>February &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>10 SUN &nbsp;Fine day.</p>
<p>Football in aft. received</p>
<p>letter from Amy. Writing</p>
<p>at night.</p>
<p>11 MON &nbsp;Posted letter to Amy &amp; enc</p>
<p>for Myers L<sup>td</sup>. Fine day.</p>
<p>Touch of trench fever.</p>
<p>not feeling too good.</p>
<p>Bed early.</p>
<p>12 SHROVE TUES &nbsp;Went into Ballieul</p>
<p>for stores. Rec. 2 letters &amp; photos from&nbsp;</p>
<p>Gladys. card E.P. letter Maggie.</p>
<p>Had a merry evening at night.</p>
<p>13 ASH WED &nbsp;Raining on &amp; off. Horses</p>
<p>dipped. Rec. reg. letter from Amy &amp; &pound;5.</p>
<p>Football in aft. Fixing up&nbsp;</p>
<p>phone at night.</p>
