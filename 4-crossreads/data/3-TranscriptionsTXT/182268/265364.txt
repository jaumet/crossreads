 Thursday 25/10/17. Met Mr Gray &amp; had lunch with him at Cafe Royal&nbsp; saw &quot;Romance&quot;. 
 Friday 26/10/17&nbsp; Went to &quot;Fovent&quot; to the Training Battalion&nbsp; came back same night 
 Saturday 27/10/17&nbsp; Went out to Malden to say Goodbye to the Bamfords&nbsp; had lunch with them. Had dinner with Gray &amp; left for Scotland. 
 Sunday 28/10/17. Arrived at Dunkeld had breakfast and then went to Amulree Hotel, went for a walk. Good dinner&nbsp; Wrote to Doff.
