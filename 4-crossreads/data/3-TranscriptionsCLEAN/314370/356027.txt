  
 May, 1916 Monday 15 5 am to 9.30 am Tactical scheme. When they return a lot of the men fell out sick on account of the heat. After breakfast Lecture. After dinner we had to demonstrate use of Graticules for overhead & indirect fire Temp 112 
 Tuesday 16 Early morning. Tactical handling & gun mounting. After breakfast Stoppages & S.T. After dinner we had a lecture & stoppages. Temp 113 at 2.30 pm
