<p>to the King &ndash; Wives &amp; Sweethearts &ndash; Absent ones &ndash; The Navy &ndash; The Allied forces &ndash; I cleared out then &amp; turned in &amp; woke up nice &amp; fresh at 6 am &ndash; Jan 1-1915 &amp; had my cocoa &amp; a small bit of biscuit &ndash;<br/>Friday Jan 1-1915 -  Had a hose bath &ndash; Read the 'Memorabilia'. Breakfasted on a bit of bacon &amp; some rice &amp; coffee &ndash; Sick parade &ndash; My SBA knows his <u>exact</u> position now &ndash; Give him an inch &amp; he'd go to Hell &ndash; I make him do physical drill with the men &amp; told him I want no complaints &amp; also told him to cultivate by every means his physical powers &amp; I've locked up harmful drugs &amp; taken the keys from him &ndash; I'm making a man of him &ndash; He's bad material &ndash; 'Au Ullage' says Mr Bumpus every now &amp; again &ndash;<br/>We had prayers &amp; then Fitz &amp; I walked &amp; talked. He's going to hang on so long as his coal lasts &ndash; We cant get Rabaul or the Eastern by wireless &ndash; We've stuck here 3 days but it must be Rabaul before long for coal is going out &ndash;</p>
