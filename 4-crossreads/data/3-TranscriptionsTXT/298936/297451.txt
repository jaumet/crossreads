 October 1915 
 Friday 22 
 My turn to wait at table &amp; managed to get through with nothing worse than spilling rice all over the table. 
 Very easy job, for tomorrow I simply clean a dug &ndash; out &amp; get some water &amp; then "Finish". 
 Started raining in afternoon. 
 Very miserable day.  Feeling rather sick.
