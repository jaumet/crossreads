<p>Saturday 23 March<br/>To Holyrood. Good look round &amp; interesting yarns.</p>
<p>Afternoon no bus available for Forth Bridge so took taxi. Then to HMAS Australia by launch. Met Frank Rogerson's brother who showed us about. Home by about 6.30 by bus.</p>
<p>Evening visited [Carlton?] Gardens &amp; strolled about.</p>
