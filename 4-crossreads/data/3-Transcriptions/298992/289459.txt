<p>Papers are still arriving very regularly from Father, and the one I look for as much as any, is the "Gossip" column from the Stock &amp; Station" Journal. Some of them are delightful to read, and it certainly is a style all of its won. Ask Father to cut it out, and sent it regularly, I look forward to them very much now.</p>
<p>How is Bill getting on with the dancing? Is he still as fond as ever? And Hazel has found a voice! Golly! I'll hardly know home when I get back again.
