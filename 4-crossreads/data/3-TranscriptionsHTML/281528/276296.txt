<p>8/4/18 &ndash; Albert-Amiens Road<br/>During the night we had a few men hit &ndash; some by a mills bomb exploded by a sniper's bullet.  The Hun was firing a good deal of tracer ammunition mixed in with his ordinary m.g. stuff.  Otherwise the night was comparatively quiet.  Day broke dull and turned out wet and miserable.  The men are standing in mud and the only cover they have is waterproof sheets.  Many cannot lie down at all.  Hot food is hard to get forward and then is most likely cold by the time it is issued.
