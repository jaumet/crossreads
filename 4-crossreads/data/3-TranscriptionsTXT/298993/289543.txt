 Monday September 4.9.16 Still at moaskar Tuesday Sep 5.9.16 Still at moakcar I am sick of the place Wednesday Sep 6.9.16 Still at moaskar. Fixing bayonets by numbers Thursday Sept 7.9.16 Still at moascar Friday Sept 8.9.16 Still at moascar Saturday Sept 9.9.16 left moascar to day and glad to get away nothing but work. Arrived Poly-gon Camp Abbassia midday met Jim Bradbury. Sunday Sept 10.9.16 Sleeping all day wrote home. A very nice camp.
