<p>Thursday, Nov 15th<br/>On Tuesday morning 13th we left Halifax Camp and came into Berthew. The day was fine &amp; the roads fair The men were tired but in good heart<br/>Valises were left behind, slept in clothes, no towels soap or toilet requisites next morning<br/>Wednesday 14th we came in to Le Neippe</p>
