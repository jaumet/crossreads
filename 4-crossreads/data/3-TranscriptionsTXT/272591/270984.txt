Then went on to Westminster Bge &amp; saw the 2 German submarines in the distance. After that went to Anzac Buffet, had tea, &amp; stopped there couple hours. Then drew my hut, got tube at St James Park stn &amp; arrived at Clapham Common about 8.15 pm. Went to bed at 43 Klea Avenue about 10 pm. 
 10/12/18 Up 8.30 am, slept like a top. Was on a moving stair-case last night at Charing Cross Tube Stn on way to Clapham. Left Klea Avenue at 9.30 am &amp; took dirty linen to a laundry. Then took tram to Victoria &amp; went to Pay Office re pay book but same had not arrived from Weymouth. Had dinner at Anzac Buffet &amp; then went to Passport Office. After waiting 1&frac12; hours was told passport was under consideration for a few days, so I kicked up a row &amp; asked to see the boss of the show. This had the effect of adjusting matters &amp; they said they would do it up straight away. I gave one of them 2/- &amp; he got it fixed up for me in no time. Then went to War Chest Club &amp; wrote Base Post Office asking them to send my mail to Clapham.
