<p>1915<br/>Jan. 30 Sat.<br/>Number of patients in Hospital up to this morning was 630. The number of patients to be taken in as laid down in Military regulations is 520.<br/>Paid 12/- in piastres.<br/>Worked until 10.45 p.m.<br/>Deputation of 3 men paraded before the O.C. in connection with the supply of tucker to the camp &ndash; The food is poor &amp; scanty &amp; it is quite likely that the Quartermaster (Capt. Gray) &amp; his storemen are rooking us &ndash; Wait for developments &ndash; an inquiry will be held.</p>
