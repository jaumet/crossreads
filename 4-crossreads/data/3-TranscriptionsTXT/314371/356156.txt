 Apr. 17th Wed. I went on my own to Tea w/- Alton's &amp; spent a very happy evening with much talk &amp; music. 
 Apr. 18th Thur. A bunch of us visited the United Tobbacco Co's works in the morning &amp; had an interesting couple of hours.  Went to free entertainment in the Tivoli after dinner. 
 Friday, 19th Apr. Had a bust up in the mess.  A lot of men are drunk in Camp. Went to Pictures after tea. 
 Sat. 20th Apr. to 1st May Things going easy.  I go frequently to Alton's &amp; am having a real good time.
