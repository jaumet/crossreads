<p>19-3-17<br/>Mon 19th<br/>Started today on the Telephony Course; Ben Woodhouse came over this afternoon. I could not go out as I am Orderly Sergt today, so Ben went in to meet Jessie, he arrived at the 14th at twenty to seven &amp; was told that Jessie was on duty till 8  oclock, so he did not think to wait but came back &amp; was back in camp here before 8 oclock; Les Regan was over for an hour or two tonight; Ben left at 9.30 for his camp they expect to move away on Wednesday 140 lads were caught coming in late last night &amp; a good many are for Orderly Room tomorrow</p>
