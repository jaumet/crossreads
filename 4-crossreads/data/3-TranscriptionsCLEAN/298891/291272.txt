
1917 July
8 Sunday
Arrived in Taranto at 10 or so & made for Y.M.C.A. town centre picking up a Mr Conyers Baker at M.C.A. in dismal cellar paid 6/8 for our three breakfasts.Got rooms in same flat as Miss Wells. Fleas attacked us immediately. Very hot glaring & dusty & oh, those shrieking Italians. Sat up late in Maggiore Napoleons Galassi's room. Reported to Base Commandant ciceroned by a Mr Butza from West Australia.
