All War industries will receive 1st consideration as in  all the allied  countries.  Has Australia done enough?  Gt. Britain has sent more than 10&amp;percnt; of her eligible men to the war &amp; has 9&amp;percnt; more engaged in munition making.  Australia has only sent 5&amp;percnt; of her eligible men.  Does not the difference leave Australia plenty of room for greater efforts still?  100,000 for reinforcements would still leave Australia's efforts much short of Gt. Britain's.  Surely we cannot say that Australia has done enough, so long as she has only provided half the percentage that Britain has given!
