<p>20th  It is unofficially (very) stated that Gen'l Foch asked Haig for 40 divisions of British troops for a certain front.  Haig said it was impossible as we had not the men.  Foch said Never mind let me have the 3rd &amp; 4th Australian Divs.</p>
<p>23rd  Shifted at 11 at night to a new position near Sailly le sec and fritz welcomed us with a barrage.  One man was killed.</p>
<p>25th  barraged beaucoup. No more cas. heard of up to now.</p>
