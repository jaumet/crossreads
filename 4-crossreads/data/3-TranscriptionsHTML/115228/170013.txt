<p><u>Nov 18th</u>:- Moved on to within 2 1/2 miles of Jaffa, the New Zealand Brigade having already entered the town &amp; taken up positions beyond. Jaffa out of bounds to us unfortunately.</p>
<p><u>Nov 19th</u>:- Raining all day.</p>
<p><u>Nov 20th</u>:- Outpost duty last night, terribly wet &amp; cold &amp; miserable.</p>
<p><u>Nov 21st</u>:- Our Brigade was relieved by the 2nd Brigade &amp; we moved back a few miles (about 12 miles today).</p>
<p><u>Nov 22nd</u>:- Moved back a further six to eight miles</p>
