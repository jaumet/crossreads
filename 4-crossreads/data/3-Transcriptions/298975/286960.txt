<p>Sept<br/>Thursday  27th<br/>Early breckfast Busseled about without any reason caught train at Dinton 9 AM Arrived Waterloo about 12 Marched to Horsefury Rd &amp; were turned loose. Had dinner at War Chest an exelent instituon Wandered around (4 of us) &amp; got a room at 150A Southamton Row Near Russel Square. A Salvation Army place &amp; very good. Wandered about some more &amp; went to a rotton play A Little bit of Fluff</p>
<p>Friday 28th<br/>Went to The Bank &amp; Wax Works Etc Lindfield Gardens in the evening</p>
<p>Saturday 29th<br/>Met Miss Murray had a look at Picture gallery &amp; spent most of the day at Kew Gardens</p>
