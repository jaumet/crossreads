Luckily the men are a good lot, but judging by the remarks being now passed around me, their colonel &amp; major are absolutely despised. Well may we be called Bill Burrages circus. Everyday here our operations remind me of :&ndash;<br/>"The king of France went up the hill<br/>With twenty thousand men<br/>An when they reached the top,<br/>He marched them down again.</p>
<p>12th Apr 1915. 1 a m in the morning. At last we are in the train waiting patiently for it to move. The day has been one of bustle, the packing &amp; stowing of bags, overhauling equipment, rolling blankets &amp; downing tents, and we are off.
