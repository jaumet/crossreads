
<p>The following Parody was written at 4 Command Depot Codford. Oct 1<sup>st</sup> by P<sup>te</sup> Jim ODonnell 30<sup>th</sup> Battalion.&nbsp; The beer in the Canteen had been unduly watery &amp; the bold Anzacs more than usually overbearing &amp; officious.&nbsp; Hence these verses.</p>
