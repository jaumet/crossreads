<p>1917 September</p>
<p>7 Friday</p>
<p>Beautiful weather as usual &ndash; chilly in morning &ndash; glorious heat gone. Still warm in middle of day in my tent but not comfortable lying without something on. Very tired being driven all day by Matron. No incident of any importance or interest.</p>
