 Mrs A. Fry Lindfield Sydney Australia 
 Dear Mother 
 Arrived 31st Oct and have had the day ashore in Suez. Karoola is here but could not get out to see if Dene was aboard. Hard luck. Love to all. Alan 
 [Card ] 
 Christmas 1915
