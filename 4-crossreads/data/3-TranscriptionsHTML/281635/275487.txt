<p>They included a couple of"Springboks"<br/>28/8/18 Recd p/card from Cohen at home.<br/>30/8/18 Recd letter from Mother written on June 16th.<br/>31/8/18 Recd letter from Dick Luscombe. Just told today we are to go up near Ebblinghem. Marching up I think. Wrote home.<br/>1/9/18 recd letters from Mother dated 5/11/17, 25/11/17, 7/12/17, 23/12/17, &amp; a Xmas Card for 1917 from Mrs Doyle. Taking down the tents today. During afternoon went on top of hill &amp; as atmosphere was very clear had a magnificent view. Could see Cassel, Mont des Cats, Mont Rouge &amp; Kemmel in the distance; &amp; to the Sth East could see a long ridge right out past the three slag heaps. After tea went to aerodrome &amp; saw an aeroplane that had smashed up when landing. Great numbers</p>
