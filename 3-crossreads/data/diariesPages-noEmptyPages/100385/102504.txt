On the night of 19.6.18 we were in the front line a mile south west of Merris and received orders that Nos 19 2 platoon of my Coy had to make a day light stunt 9 am of the 20.6.18. Of course this meant a lot of bustling about detailing parties for carrying ammunities fatiqure party and all these being found from my je Palour N841 as we were holding the line on the left of where the stunt was to take place.
