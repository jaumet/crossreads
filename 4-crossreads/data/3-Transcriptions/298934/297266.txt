<p>Passed the Ushant light at 2 a.m. this morn &amp; enterred Channel.  Met Dale's son aboard &ndash; steward (pork pie shop)</p>
<p>1st July Thursday 1915</p>
<p>Passed Isle of Wight last night &amp; entered <strike>alon</strike> Sthmpton Water about 8 p.m.  Berthed this morning &amp; all Colonials entrained at 11 a.m.</p>
<p>Friday 2nd July 1915</p>
<p>Last night arrd. at Ducie Avenue Hospital, Manchester.  Told to stop in bed to day &ndash; a bit off color.  In bed for 3 days.</p>
