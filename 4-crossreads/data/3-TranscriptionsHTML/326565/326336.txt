<p>Wednesday 29/3/16<br/>Arose about 5.30 AM &amp; made steady preparations to finish the march.  Morning again very warm. Moved off from Moascoar about 9.30 AM &amp; marched through Ismailia to Ferry post, where we arrived at 11.30 &amp; soon took shelter in the tents which our advance guard had pitched.  End of a very trying march of 40 odd miles.  Rested till 3 pm, when we marched</p>
