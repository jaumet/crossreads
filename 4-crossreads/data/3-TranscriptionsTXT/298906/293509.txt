 All those who were on the route march this morning had lectures all the afternoon, this was very handy &amp; far better than drilling. The farmers are all busy spraying their potatoes around here, they are frightened of the Irish blight. 26th. No P.T. Instructors available this morning, so the Sergts had to give the physical jerks, old Tubby was buzzing round, but he never had much to say for a wonder Had a light afternoons work had X &amp; No 1 Coy. wiring &amp; digging trenches. 27th.
