<p>because I go at it for a learner with such pace &amp; rashness, but I am getting on splendid &amp; soon will be a right as rain.</p>
<p>I had to get the Dr to have a look at my arms this morning great big lumps came out on them in a few hours &amp; they were very sore, the Dr ordered them to be well rubbed with some ointment, they seem a good deal easier tonight.</p>
<p>There is great fun in our Ward every evening for as sure as you go out some of the nurses are bound to short sheet your bed, &amp; there is the devil to play.</p>
<p>It has been a glorious day quite hot &amp; I thoroughly enjoyed my evening stroll round the city, I think half the girls in Birmingham know us now for we get the glad eye all along the streets.</p>
