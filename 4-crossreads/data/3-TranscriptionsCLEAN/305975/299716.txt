Wednesday Oct. 2.10.18 feeling a little better to day had some dinner   was packed  slept all afternoon Thursday Oct. 3.10.18 feeling much the same to day  saw a good few germans about  saw one chap carried in here and he only lived half an hour.   Had dinner  was Packed   Friday out 4.10  in motor lorries and sent into Jerasulem to hospital.   we got in there after dark
