<p>six in the afternoon. You could<br/>Not work during the day on accounts of the heat, so remained in your tents. Although the days were so hot the nights were beautiful and cool. In fact you needed both your blankets to keep you warm. Sometimes, If there was a slight breeze during the night, you would wake up smothered in sand and feeling quite grimy.<br/>There was an ample supply of water and we were dressed in kaki with helmuts, so life was not so dreadful. On the 19th we paid a visit to the old battlefield at Tel El Kebis. How different to the present day ones.</p>
