<p>[18 Th]<br/>Reported to D.A.H.Q. for position of Draughtsman  Drawing office &amp; sleeping quarters about 50 feet under the surface.</p>
<p>[19 Fri]<br/>Out of bed 7-30am breakfast 8am. office 9am, Dinner 12-30 pm. Tea 4-30pm. making out charts of Hostile shelling &amp; <strike>Harrass</strike> harassing fire till 11-30 pm.</p>
