 1915 Jan. 27 Wed. Commenced work this morning in general admission &amp; administration office at Mena House. Five clerks kept busy all day.  Knocked off at 9.30 p.m. Had a hot bath at Mena House &ndash; the first since home (first hot bath, not first bath) just the thing. Our complete unit is made up as follows :- Officers - 22 N.C.O.s &amp; Men - 148 Nurses - 93 
 Total - 263
