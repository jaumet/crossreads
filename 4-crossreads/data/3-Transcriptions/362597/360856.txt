<p>Saturday 8.1.16 (17)<br/>It is a lovely day &amp; we are looking forward to the final of the  sports. The sports were held &amp; I lost my heat being beaten by Roy Farnsworth, the League Footballer. He eventually won the race. Ellis beat the gong &amp; was disqualified in the sack race which was won by Capt Rickson, a Salvation Army Captain but a private with our unit. The Sergeants won the Tug-o-war.</p>
<p>We had a concert on the Promenade Deck at night &amp; I can tell you these concerts are alright. Matron who is grand woman, gave the prizes out to the successful</p>
