<p>I have had a party of men building a big dugout all the morning &amp; we have made a good job of it.</p>
<p>3rd.     Big top boots are all the rage to-day for the trenches are swimning in water &amp; there is a foot of sloppy mud everywhere especially in our "communication trench" &amp; "Hedgerow" it has been drizzling all the morning &amp; things as you may guess are in an awful state, but the big rubber boots which reach to our thighs are waterproof so we can paddle away to our hearts</p>
