 21st Left the hospital at 1 A.M. this morning and came on to Havre by Amb. Train  to  where I was carried aboard the hospital ship Granturny Castle. 
 22nd We left Havre last night and arrived at Portsmouth this morning about 6 a.m. 
 Since coming to hospital I have had umpteen tickets attached to and detached from my valuable person and nearly everybody  conc  concerned has found a new way to spell my name.
