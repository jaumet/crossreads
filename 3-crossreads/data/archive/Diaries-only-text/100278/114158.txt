Out at drill this morning and I had a go at Coy drill the Major was there sizing everything up, but I only made about 2 mistakes which was not so bad, considering it was the first time I had ever had a go at so large a body of men.
It is very dull and raining slightly this afternoon.
Our billet is starting to fall down and the boys upstairs have to move this afternoon or the old house will collapse and then every beam will be a "Blighty".
