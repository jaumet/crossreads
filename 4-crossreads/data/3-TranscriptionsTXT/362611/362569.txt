[Note paper with Union Line in Red lettering and a coloured photo of a passenger liner on the sea]T.S.S. MoerakiWellington 19/2/18
My dear John,
My love to dear Anna  &amp;  to  your children "large &amp; small". This is a line to let you know that after a [indecipherable] joyable voyage, I am safely arrived in Port Nicholson. The "Moeraki" had a tempestuous passage of 10 &frac12; hours from Newcastle on Monday Wednes. night 13th inst. She, therefore, was late in leaving Sydney.
