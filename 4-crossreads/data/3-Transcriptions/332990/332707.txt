<p>Romsey Abbey<br/>1516. Anne Westbrook<br/>1523 Elizabeth Ryperose</p>
<p>This Abbey was seized by the King, Henry VII. 1539.  its property valued at &amp;pound 528.10.10 &frac12; per annum, and was granted to Lord High Admiral Seymour.</p>
<p>The Abbey House being granted to the town in 1543.</p>
<p>The church was purchased from the King by the Parishoners to use as their Parish Church for &amp;pound 100 in the year 1543.</p>
<p>Another notice gives the names of the Abbesses of the Conventual  Church of S.S. Mary &amp; Ethelflaeda Romsey.</p>
<p>The first was in a.d. 907 Ethelflaeda another 996. Elfleda the last 1503. Gundeld  or Joyce Rons.<br/>Romsey church Aug 26. 1916.</p>
<p>My Trust Will   [image]<br/>To -  Morrow<br/>Answer. My trust barrell will be tapped tomorrow.<br/>Romsey August 23. 1916</p>
