<p>January  1918</p>
<p>Sunday 6<br/>Went to Church &amp; a walk in afternoon came on to rain the first for some time Have had 18 Australian letters through the week</p>
<p>Monday 7<br/>Marking on Range Hall won the Suppers today</p>
<p>Tuesday 8<br/>Range again also another win for Hall &ndash; Rought march at night</p>
<p>Wednesday 9<br/>Same as yesterday finished today Drill in afternoon. A cold day</p>
