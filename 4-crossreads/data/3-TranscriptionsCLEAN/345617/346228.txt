  
 Novembre 2 Working on H.A.C. shelters. Heard today that Cpl Arnold has died from the effect of wounds received on 30/10/17.That makes 8 deaths from the one shell. Weather damp & miserable. Bad news continues to come from the Italian Front. Enemy planes bombed Ypres very heavily last night. 
 Novembre 3 Section working on shelters for H.A.C. at Belgian Battery Corner. Very little bombing by enemy planes last night, but heavy shelling. Started for England today on leave. Reached Boulogne at about 8 p.m. after a long tedious journey & went to Martins Camp for the night.
