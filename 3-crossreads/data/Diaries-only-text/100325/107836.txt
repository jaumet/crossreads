The buildings in this European quarter (round Bank c) very fine and in many cases much more imposing than Melbourne. No trams makes the locality much more quiet. Passed the house Napoleon made his headquarters while in Cairo apparently used as a moslem's residence now as their windows are concealed by the mouribisha woodwork now so rare. The woodwork a close screen the only opening being that in which the goolah is placed to be in the draught .
