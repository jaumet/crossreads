<p>22 June 1918<br/>Saturday<br/>Nothing of much importance to write about Just the same old camp Life.</p>
<p>1918 June 23<br/>Sunday<br/>4 After Trinity</p>
<p>A rather quiet day in Camp on Sunday Church parade in the morning &amp; not much doing in the afternoon except that a very indifferent Band plays &amp; a lot of the Townspeople come to hear it.</p>
