
23/10/16Went per motor lorry to a position about 8 miles from Fricourt and marched to that place.
24/10/16Camped in tents and [bivouacs?] about a mile from Fricourt last night and moved on to Hametz Woods today.
We expect to go into the line in a few days.
25/10/16We are camped in all kinds of rough biveys here.
