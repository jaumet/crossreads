<p>Young Hunter came along only the day before your letter reached me &amp; spoke of you. I had met him many times. A fine fellow. I am sorry to say he is now in hospital with dysentery.</p>
<p>Desert Mtd Corps<br/>Hqrs<br/>Palestine<br/>May 21/18</p>
<p>My dear Judge Ferguson<br/>I was delighted to get your letter &amp; the news of you all, &amp; have sent it on to Penelope who will be as pleased as I was.</p>
<p>It was good to learn that your son is mending hopefully. How strange to think of him going back to the University again. What a background to his thoughts as he works at his peaceful Arts course. Happily our minds are complex &amp; fickle enough to allow us at times to escape from grieving about the days in which we live.</p>
