<p>Pte J F Mant<br/>3rd Div Cyclist Coy</p>
<p>26 July 1916</p>
<p>My dear Mrs Ferguson</p>
<p>Will you please accept my very deepest sympathy in your great loss. Arthur was one of the finest fellows I have had the honour of knowing &amp; it has left a blank in my life.</p>
<p>Believe me, Mrs Ferguson,</p>
<p>Yours very sincerely</p>
<p>John F Mant</p>
