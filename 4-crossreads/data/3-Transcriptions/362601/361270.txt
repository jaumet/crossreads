<p>21 September, Sunday</p>
<p>Very dopey. Think I have got Jo. Pick peas. Go to Manly to Ellis' place. Meet Dave &amp; Doris also Jeffes from 39 Gen. Catch 8 pm tram Home. Take Argyrol Treatment.</p>
<p>22 September, Monday</p>
<p>Go for treatment twice. Go to Inksdale via P'sham. No Tea. Go for stroll. Laura &amp; I sit &amp; talk also Bess.</p>
<p>Write Queenie.</p>
