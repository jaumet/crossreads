  
 Church parade in Institute this morning.  Received 'Weekly Times' from Dad. Was sent up for medical board yesterday, on account of report re eyes, by Dr [indecipherable] Passed in A Class! 
 Sunday 10th Sept 1916 Billy went to London with a programme of how to make the most of his four days, including two visits home. On the following morning W.O. Pollard late of the 3rd F.A., & now in charge of A.M.C. details, asked on parade for a dispenser & clerk & also for 6 orderlies.
