 Thursday 2 December 1915&nbsp; Orders at 10 am. to take Medical Charge of the S.S. Themistocles at Suez for Australia Preparing all day to set out.&nbsp;&nbsp; Wrote letters to Hyman, Mrs Reardon, Peck, Sister Sutcliffe, Sister Johnson, Mdm Andebon, Dr Schuber.  Sent cables to Girls &amp; Mrs  &nbsp;M. M.Knowles. Everything ready just at midnight.  
 Friday 3 December 1915&nbsp; Left Cairo 11-30 a.m. for Suez. Arrived Suez 4-30 am&nbsp; Went on board S.S. Themistocles. Good ship. Not too many to look after.&nbsp; 30 orderlies about 150 patients. 
 Saturday 4 December 1915&nbsp; Left Suez wharf about 8. am. A comfortable &amp; agreeable day.&nbsp; Getting all into working order&nbsp; Bed 11 p.m. 
 Sunday 5 December 1915&nbsp; Mass 7-30 a.m. Steaming comfortably down the Red Sea&nbsp; Ordinary routine work during day
