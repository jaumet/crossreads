<p>I have not received any reply from Major Lawes. I sent on the letter addressed to Liverpool Camp - which should find him provided the the title is right and has not been magnified by his sister. Most doctors are captains in the A.A.M.C.<br/>I was very pleased to receive Douglas's long and amusing letter.
