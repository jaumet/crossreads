 Tuesday 25th Came out of supports at 5 am &amp; turned in at 5 30 &amp; slept till 7 &amp; bed again in dug out Had a good sleep till 12 30. During the morn a battle ship was sunk (torpedoed?) just in the bay. I did not see it. Noon the torpedo destroyers &amp; the airship were making great search while the battle ships went out to sea. HMS Triumph. 
 In the trenches again at 4 pm. After a wet after noon. Things damned cloggy. 
 Quiet night. On alert but little doing. [indecipherable] hardly awake all night.
