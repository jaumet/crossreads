 19.9.'18 D. i &ndash; 7.30 a.m. Began diet sheet &ndash; a difficult task.  9-30 came the O.C. Colonel ?, did extensive rounds.  What is the matter with you? "toe nail off Sir", "Sister, why do they all have toe nails off just as a stunt is coming off!"  Off duty 2-5.  Laurie &amp; L. Lawton visit me 5 p.m. &amp; arrange an outing for next day. A great success of the Palestine front &amp; some of our boys recalled.  Pt. Woodhouse very ill meningitis symptoms showing tonight, eye L has slight squint.  Pain very severe.
