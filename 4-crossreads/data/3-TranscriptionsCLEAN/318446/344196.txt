Sheik ZowaiidDec. 30th 1916Out on a stunt again to Sheik Zowaiid about 12-18 miles beyond El Arish along the coast.This is fairly good country out here, there must be well over 1000 acres under crops of corn & several rough sort of orchards of fig trees.
