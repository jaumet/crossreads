 Sunday 1 July 1917 On "Sky Pilot" parade in evening.  Instruction on stoppages. 
 Monday 2 July 1917 Quiet day, packing up for move, usual routine. 
 Tuesday 3 July 1917 Brigade left Abassan el Kebir for El Gamli at 7.30:  was left in charge of dump. 
 Wednesday 4 July 1917 On dump at Abassan-el-Kebir.  Taube came over, and two shells fired by taube fell near our camp &amp; exploded. 
 Thursday 5 July 1917 Limbers came in from Gamli &amp; took remainder of stuff from Dump, went out to new camp on "Ahmed" at El Gamli. 
 Friday 6 July 1917 General Routine, took horses down to water (3 miles).  Beer issue;  on picquet at night. 
 Saturday 7 July 1917 Went to Dentist at F.A. had a tooth bored out &amp; nerve killed pretty crook rest of day.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 7 and 4].
