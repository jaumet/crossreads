
February 1918Thursday 14
MessinesMade two trips to F.O.O. today, gee but it's a long step.Made enquiries about E.G.F. and find he is at Spy FarmWeather has been very unsettled.Received a wire that Fritz was massing at Lake Farm . Our Bty put 20 rounds in. Had the 2 to 4 this morning.Feeling fairly tired after so much travelling today, so I must drink my run and pop into bed.
