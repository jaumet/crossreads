 Same place Same day 7.15pm. I am now sleeping at the Telephone, or, should I say, about to sleep at the telephone. The firing has died down with the advent of night, and everything is quite still now, but I expect that we shall be woken up by shells in the morning as we are very much within range. However I believe we are going directly after stand to, which is at 4 ac emma.
