 20 June 1918 Thursday Doing very little work. Things are going along in just the same old way in the Depot. Nothing exciting. 
 1918 June 21 Friday Took over Company orderly Sgt job in the morning &amp; hand it over to Sgt. Connell of the 7th F Coy. before night. I am now to be Draft Sgt. Nothing fresh to report Italians reported still doing well against the Austrians.
