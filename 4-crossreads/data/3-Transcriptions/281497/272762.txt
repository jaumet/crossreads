<p>I then reported to the Company &amp; was put on the strength, was handed out a fine batch of letters which I was very pleased to get.</p>
<p>31st.  This morning is a beauty after the rain &amp; the sun is shining brightly &amp; mud is drying fast as ever it can.   I saw the officer &amp; got off parade this morning, &amp; am now writing this in a fine Y.M.C.A. within a hundred yards of our camp.   You can buy apple tarts &amp; etc in here not bad is it.
