<p>August 3 Tuesday 1915<br/>Left Cairo by 7. 30 AM train with the party under Col. Grice for Alexandra. Arrived here 12 AM staying at Hotel-de-Rome near the Square. The Post Office is only 5 minutes walk &amp; the ocean the same distance. Went to Thos Cook's re kit bag, also to Mustapha Barracks. Had tea 6 PM worked from 7 PM to 8. 30 PM<br/>Feeling very tired</p>
<p>August 4 Wednesday 1915<br/>Working today feeling much better<br/>Hours of work 8. 30 AM till 12. 30 PM, 2. 30 PM till 5. 30 PM, 7 PM to 8. 30 PM.</p>
<p>August 5 Thursday 1915<br/>Worked all day feeling well<br/>After work tonight I walked around the principal streets of Alexandra. Weather is beautifully cool.</p>
<p>August 6 Friday 1915<br/>Walked round at 7 PM to Eastern Telegraph Comp, &amp; sent cablegram to Maude. "Birthday greetings love"worked all day feeling well<br/>General &ndash; came &amp; inspected post office today</p>
