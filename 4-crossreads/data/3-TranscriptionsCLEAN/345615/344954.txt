We are camped on the flat alongside the town & about a mile away there is a ring of hit low hills - along the top of which about every 1/4 mile are a apart are a series of block houses with a small outpost in each. I should judge there are about 4000 of us here all told & if rumour is correct about 10,000 Bedouins some 5 miles to the west of the Camp.
