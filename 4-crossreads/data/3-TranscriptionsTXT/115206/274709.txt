 May 1918 Sunday 19 
 Bussy &ndash;la-Daours 3 am Was aroused by the detonations of bombs and gas shells, now damage whatever. Had a midnight toot to Villers Bretnoeaux. Weather beautiful. Swam three horses during day. had old Ginger dressed. Ken Gunn paid me a little visit and had the usual eufs. Won a few francs at the old game.
