<p>Continuation of Diary<br/>Rouen 14/4/17</p>
<p>I am now going to try to pick up the ground I have lost between the night I was wounded or to be exact the night we left Ville de Flosse for I left my diary behind there &amp; had to take notes.</p>
<p>6th Last night we lobbed in the trenches such as they are, we crossed the open country in artillery formation, no one was more</p>
