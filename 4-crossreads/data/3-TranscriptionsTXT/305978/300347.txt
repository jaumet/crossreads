 It appears that a Tommy had taken a prisoner &amp; only took his rifle from him &amp; never troubled to search him for arms. Col Snow approached the him with some question relating to the enemy, where upon the prisoner drew a flint lock pistol &amp; shot Snow in the stomach, the poor fellow suffered great pain before he died.
