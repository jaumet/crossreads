 Novembre 14 Went up to town in afternoon. Went to Australia House to draw money &amp; met Cpl Gough there. Had a knock round with him &ndash; made some purchases &amp; got back to Leytonstone at about 7 p.m. 
 Novembre 15 Went to town about noon &amp; in the afternoon Mrs Smith &amp; I went to the Lyceum &amp; saw "Seven Days Leave". It was very good though perhaps not quite up to the usual London standard. Met Mr Smith on the way home &amp; got back about 6.30 p.m.
