<p>5.<br/>from AIF.</p>
<p>The Director of Education is Lieut Colonel Long &ndash; otherwise Bishop Long of Goulburn, assisted by one Lieut Allen, who is I understand a professor of English at Melbourne. Between them they display a shocking ignorance of military matters, and only follow the best traditions of the British Army in always being late.</p>
