<p>Monday June. 2.6.19<br/>went to sydney to day  was up at Barracks  went and saw win  she was in bed sick.   came home by 10.6.6.<br/>tuesday June. 2.6.19<br/>up at home to day  looks like more rain  up at mrs ushers to night Playing Cards<br/>Wednesday June. 3.6.19<br/>at home to day  nice day  went down to kemps place to night  Played cards  stayed till 11.30.</p>
