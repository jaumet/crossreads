 20th February:- Sunday I arrived here No 3 Aust Gen Hosp Abbassia yesterday.  It was the opening day of the hosp, which has just retuned from Lemnos Island &  of course everything was in a state of unreadiness  To-day is much better, but as I was put straight to bed and am still here have not seen much of it.
