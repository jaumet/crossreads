 I had no one there to see me off but some young ladies made themselves known &amp; helped carry a few of the many things a soldier has. We embarked on the good ship Argylshire at 8pm. Great crowds came to say their last good-bye &amp; many thousands of flags &amp; banners waved &amp; the boat being tied to shore by streamers the sight was a grand one to be sure.
