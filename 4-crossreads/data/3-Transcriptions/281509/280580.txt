<p>1916 December 23<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Fatigue as usual.  Get up at 8 a.m. breakfast of fat pork &amp; tea.  Big wind storm &amp; rain. tents blown down. Get Xmas box each from "War Chest Fund" comprising tin tobacco &amp; cigarettes, tin lollies, handkerchief, handkerchief, throat losenges, mint, chewing gum, ginger, etc.</p>
