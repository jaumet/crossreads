Friday 29th
Things all day were quiet except that the continuous Road of the Big Guns, a Big Gun close by seem to be playing Lawn Tennis with Fritz as the Gun would send over a shell to Fritz in a few minutes Fritz would send another back that's how they amuse themselves. After Tea I went along to our O.C. quarters for my Pass to go see my brother over at Steinvercke after obtaining the Pass I met a Company Sergeant Major who was after a Pass also waited for him when he received his pass we both came back to our Camp I drew two days rations consisting of 1 Tin of Meat vegetables 1 of Bully Beef, 1 Loaf of Bread, Tea Sugar, Cheese, some Butter a Tin of Jam.
