<p>Monday Sept 16th Mardyk</p>
<p>camp fatigues &amp; drizzly weather,</p>
<p>Tuesday Sept 17th Mardyk</p>
<p>Camp fatigues : went to pictures at night &amp; they were very good.</p>
<p>Wednesday Sept 18 &amp; Thursday Sept 19th</p>
<p>Camp fatigues : cool &amp; drizzly :</p>
<p>Friday Sept 20th Mardyk</p>
<p>camp fatigues &amp; dental : twelve months ago today since I was hit so I am now a dinkum neutral : am in far more pleasing position now than I was then &amp; this will do me for the duration :</p>
<p>Saturday Sept 21st Mardyk</p>
<p>went to work at the Dunes at night</p>
<p>Sunday Sept 22nd Monday 23rd Tuesday 24th Wednesday 25th Thursday 26th &amp; Friday 27th Sept : Mardyk :</p>
<p>nothing much doing : am working down at the Dunes 10 pm : 4 am : not too bad : Fritz was over a couple of nights this week but didn't drop much :</p>
<p>Saturday Sept 28th : Mardyk :</p>
<p>nothing doing :</p>
<p>Sunday Sept 29th to Friday Oct 4th :</p>
<p>on 2 &ndash; 10 pm shift at the Dunes so couldn't go out at all.</p>
<p>Saturday Oct 5th Mardyk :</p>
<p>our Anniversary sports on today : was a great success also the concert at night : a few light</p>
