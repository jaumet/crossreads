 ? Guns     12/ Officially informed of the why & wherefore of things We're here because its better to be hot & hungry than cold & stiff. We're to go on 1/2 ration forthwith. We're to wash no more in fresh water nor are we to shave until further notice on pain of instant death & dismissal from the A. I. F. (I don't think) we've got to help unload cargo & coal ship (we smile) We're to stoke fires, launch boats, learn navigation & be prepared to duck overside & swim ashore & take to the tall timbers tother side the hills --- if Fremantle says so. Tonight there are 20 ships & Hear that many thousand men bottled in this jar of pickles
