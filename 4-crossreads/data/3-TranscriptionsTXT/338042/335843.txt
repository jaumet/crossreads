
April 5thMeet Mr Davison friend of Mr & Mrs Scales with whom I converse, have luncheon & proceed to his Home at Oxton (across the River) - The Suburbs of Birkenhead & Port Sunlight - Liverpool fine buildings - Exterior & interiors of St Georges Hall Walker Art Gallery. Liner Building - Cunard Liner Offices.April 6thWriter new Original Monologue "The Opium Den" - See & hear "Madame Butterfly" at the Shakespeare Theatre - Meet Beatrice Waycott (member of Opera Co).
