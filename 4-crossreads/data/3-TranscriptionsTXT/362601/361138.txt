 3 January, Friday 
 F &amp; W My day off. I go down to flat via long route. Have afternoon tea. Go to Nice Harrais &amp; back to YMCA for Tea. Am bossed about &amp; made to sit where I don't want to sit. Meet all the Girls. Ellis &amp; S/S Lilley come down &amp; we watch rotten concert. 
 The "Ceramic" is in the Harbour. Receive letters from Lottie, Home &amp; P.C's from Os Merritt &amp; Lily. Monkey has not left yet. Supper &amp; bed early. 
 4 January, Saturday 
 W Recd letter from M.R.L. Go to YMCA alone &amp; see good concert by Crocks. Kissing, Les Holmes old song is a reminder of old days. Come Home tout[?] Suite &amp; write. Monkey goes away. 
 Wrote Dad &amp; M.R.L.
