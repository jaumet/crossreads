 Sunday, 22/4/17 Sunshine in morning but cloudy afternoon.  During morning an aeroplane alighted in paddock close to hospital, but rose again before we had time to go &amp; see it. 
 Monday, 23/4/17 Nice fine but cloudy day.  Expect to go out in day or two.  Concert was given in dining hall during afternoon by visiting professionals &amp; was very good. 
 Tuesday, 24/4/17 Nice fine day.  Went for walk in scrub adjoining hospital with "Gunner".  In afternoon went over railway line &amp; watched some "Tommies" playing Soccer.
