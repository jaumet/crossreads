<p>7 &ndash; to the time in the country. They have plenty of friends in fact are over embarrassed by invitations etc.<br/>Stephanie is such a darling and Mrs Scot Skirving is very fond of her, finds comfort in having her with her. She is looking much thinner poor child &ndash; but her face is more like when she was a little girl.<br/>We passed a scarlet floating bouy today, and stopped while the officers on the bridge inspected it and had some shots of it with a rifle. Of course when we stopped we all thought it was a submarine that was sighted.</p>
