<p>FEBRUARY 1917</p>
<p>18 SUNDAY</p>
<p>Fine but dull &amp; cloudy. I was</p>
<p>stableman for the day, &amp; got</p>
<p>all my harness clean in the</p>
<p>spare time. We were paid in</p>
<p>afternoon. They issued us</p>
<p>with new pay books, when the</p>
<p>pa<s>y</s>ied us, &amp; they are a big</p>
<p>improvement on the old omes</p>
