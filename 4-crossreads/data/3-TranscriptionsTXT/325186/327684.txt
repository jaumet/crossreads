 Alexandria  We had to take a zig-zag course all the way on account of the danger of submarines  It took us twenty-one hours to reach Alexandria which should only take eight hours in ordinary sailing.  We pulled in to Alexandria about 7am on Monday 12th June.  We changed boats here leaving the Hororata &amp; going on board the R. M. S  Aragon.  We packed in like sardines with scarcely any room to turn round.
