
Saturday 26 May 1917.
A little warmer still today & sea not quite so rough smooth. 
Swell as usual and surface a little disturbed.
Physical jerks for 11/2 hrs. 10 to 11.30 a.m.
Travelling 10 1/2 knots an hr.today.
Flying fish (1 shoal) seen this afternoon at the bows of boat.
this morning "Port Sydney" practised with her maxim gun across the water.  In afternoon from the stern from boat (A42) the C.O troops on board &another officer practise with revolvers.
Cool on upper deck but on troop deck very close tonight.
