<p>I don't know whether I have told you what a comfort your letters are, they are great &amp; you're a brick.  I roar over them at times.  Graham must be a great kid everyone speaks so well of him.  I laughed over your party but I wouldn't have any more such unless the Boss is home.</p>
<p>Thanks for telling me of your romance.  I didn't feel like laughing at you.  I'm not surprised, I'm jolly glad &amp; I'm sure it will be OK.  You have it in you, you know only you must keep it up &amp; not get tired or bored.</p>
