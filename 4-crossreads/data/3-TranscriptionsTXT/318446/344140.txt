
on Bir el Abd - about 21/2 miles off killing 4-5.  Nothing doing today.  I have built me an house of palm sticks & sand bags & am now very comfortable, have a fire outside the door every night and a beautiful washing basin hollowed out of solid Limestone - as much as I can lift and Lord knows how old - I picked it up close here - one finds all sorts of queer remnants of pottery & such like on the desert.
