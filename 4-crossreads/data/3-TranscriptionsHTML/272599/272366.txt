<p>April Thursday 5 1917</p>
<p>We are to move out of here tomorrow. After getting things fixed up for the Scabies hospital. <br/>Made a tour of the underground passages &amp;amp cellars this evening &ndash; a veritable  underground village  This place must have been very strongly held &amp; would have been a hard nut for Fritz to crack if he'd attempted to take it from the French. <br/>Carruthers Hayman Johno &amp; self go into A &ndash; to Y.M.C.A. joint. 8th Field Amb. "Nightbirds" gave a very enjoyable concert. Madame Melba.</p>
