firing occasional salvoes so I arranged for our guns to give Herbecourt another caning up with shell fire. This took place although owing to all the batteries moving forward there were not many guns sufficiently far forward to be in range of the place. This is the first stunt I recollect where we have been able to carry out lamp signalling and in which the Colonel has been able to direct the show with field glasses and map standing out in the open and watching the men getting into position. Getting up ammunition is one of the many problems here as we are going ahead so fast. On our right the 18th is going through the 21st I hope this hurries the Boche on.
