My party has made a forward dump of 200,000 rounds of ammunition, 5000 boxes of bombs, 2000 gallons of water, and rations for the brigade, and various other stores. And there are those who lie beside the truck with their loads who will never carry again. The Brigade attacks in the morning, and I shall go over the top with them. If this book is returned to you; Remember that I would not be elsewhere, than with these men you will be so proud of. 23/7/16. The Brigade advanced about a 1000 yards before daylight this morning.
