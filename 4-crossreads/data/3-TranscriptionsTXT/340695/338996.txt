The window was smashed & the ceiling pitted with holes & we were covered in bits of glass, mortar & dust. I've never been awakened so suddenly before the report was terrific. The roads are very narrow so the explosion was delightfully close. The residents got a scare they lined up out in front yapping like fury we yelled out through the window "Alle tout de suite". They mobbed our room afterwards nice girls & all while we were still in bed we must have cut a funny picture.Our Anti Aircraft guns brought down a plane to-day from almost above our heads it was a grand sight.
