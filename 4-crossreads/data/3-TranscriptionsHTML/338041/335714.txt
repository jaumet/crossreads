<p>July 2nd<br/>Beautiful surroundings &ndash; Aircraft very plentiful. Bad state of Hospital etc<br/>Big British offensive begins with great success. I journey to see Ed Cornish but without success. I view a "Taube chase".-</p>
<p>July 3rd<br/>I see most of the town which is an important military base &ndash; Owing to C.C.S. being numerous the town has a great number of K.C. Cars</p>
<p>July 4th<br/>Receipt of home letters &ndash; The Big Offensive continues. Mother and Fathers "Silver Wedding").</p>
<p>July 5th<br/>Y.M.C.A. visited also R.C. Church near our billet &ndash; Work as usual</p>
<p>July 6th<br/>"Soccer" again &ndash; Y.M. &amp;amp; Canteen &ndash; British still doing well.</p>
