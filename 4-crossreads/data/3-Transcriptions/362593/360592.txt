<p>Thursday 22 August 1918<br/>Stand by for H.A. alarm.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness.<br/>In air 2 h. 50 m.  H.A. of 2 B.F. over our drome, meet a Hun.  Walker &amp; Letch brought down in flames.<br/>Capt. Brown &amp; Fin bring Hun down in our lines, both Huns O.K. but machine shot about and crashes.</p>
<p>Friday 23 August 1918<br/>In bed with a temperature, a touch of fever.</p>
