<p>The chaps in surrounding beds would get "chummy" and talk Those who were any length of time in the ward would get acquainted Lights out was at 9. In an English Hospital I noticed rules were always carried out</p>
<p>Visitors were frequent and regular Thursdays and Sundays were the days, 2 till 4 after that the Sister would come in and with a "4 o'clock visitors please" notify them of the fact that it was time to go</p>
<p>The food question was a serious problem in England at this time When I first got to England the usual formula was to get into "queues" and wait this</p>
