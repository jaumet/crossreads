Jan 6th.  We pulled into Durban first thing in the morning & were welcomed by a lady whom we saw signalling to us with flags - We had a route march through Durban in the morning & at were then given leave from 2 till 10.30 P.M. The trams were free to troops & we had a ride round. The day was a little bit showery but the sun was very hot. Had a hot bath & went to the Y.M.C.A. concert in the evening. Like Durban very much. Had Had a ride in a ricksha
 
Jan 7th  Sunday & we had a church parade ashore. it was raining all day, & we were given leave 11.30 A.M. till 10 P.M.
 
Jan 8th  Went for a five mile route march in the morning. lovely day - given leave from 2 P.M. till 10.P.M. Went to museum & art gallery. didn't think much of them - Had a swim at the Marine Parade baths, & had tea on the Parade - Went to Y.M.C.A. concert in the evening.
 
Jan 9th  We put out into the bay & left Durban at 12.30 mid-day three hours behind the "Berimar". we were in sight of land during the rest of the day - lovely day & smooth sea.
