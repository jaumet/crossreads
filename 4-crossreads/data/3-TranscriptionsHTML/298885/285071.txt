<p>10 yards away and only for it landing in ploughed ground I might not have written this</p>
<p>Fri June 2nd - 16<br/>Fritz continued today with "coal-boxes" (15 inch shells) and gave us a lively time.</p>
<p>Sun June 4th &ndash; 16<br/>Last night about 11.30PM a heavy bombardment began, all variations of guns taking part. we lay in our bunks awake for a while and hearing a shell coming close we all began running to our dugouts in our little short shirts.</p>
