<p>side of the Jordan.<br/>April 7th<br/>Nothing much doing the last few days. I heard tonight that we had let the Turks [indecipherable] bridge over the Jordan &amp; send two aeroplanes over &amp; then we landed a big shell fair in the middle of the bridge &amp; smashed it &amp; took the two companies prisoner<br/>[The above paragraph has been over written in large letters with the word "ROT"]<br/>Some of these lads here get hold of some weirde ideas to amuse themselves now &amp; again; the other day two "dags" were on leave in Cairo</p>
