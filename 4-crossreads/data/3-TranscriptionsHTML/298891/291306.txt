<p>1917 August</p>
<p>11 Saturday</p>
<p>Busier day because Brown was off after lunch. In evening patients were entertained at simple games. How very simple the male of the species is. With him running the planet little wonder it is being run off its axis. Went swimming with Read &amp; Freece.</p>
