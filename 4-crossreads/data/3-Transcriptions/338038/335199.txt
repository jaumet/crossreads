Only thing I hope is that I stay in the old Third Brigade but one cannot be sure of what changes will take place, with new establishments coming into force etc etc. The second division Artillery are in camp now at Maadi, under Colonel G.Jamieson Johnstone, who formerly commanded the Second Brigade of our Division. I saw several of their officers when in Cairo but not Maurice Lenehan, as he was still on the water having come along in the horse-boat.
