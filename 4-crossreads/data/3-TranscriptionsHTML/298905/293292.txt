<p>They burst the shrapnel all over us but luckily no one was hit<br/>The shelling lasted for about 2 hours &amp; then our batteries slackened off. After it was over a patrol of ours went across to the German trenches &amp; there was not a living soul left nothing but a mass of dead &amp; mutilated men, I believe it was something awful in the trenches, the patrol penetrated to their second line &amp; it was nearly the same there, they brought one prisoner <strike>went</strike> back with them &amp; strange to say he had a pass on him made out for Berlin, how stiff was he, though I suppose he can count himself one of the lucky one's, the power &amp; fierceness of our bombardment was indescribable, Lone Pine," was only a flea bite</p>
