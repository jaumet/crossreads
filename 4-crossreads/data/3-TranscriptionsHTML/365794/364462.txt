<p>Friday 2 March 1917 [1 March 1918]<br/>&frac12; day &amp; went Pyramids with S. Saunderson &amp; "Boy", tea provided for us by Miss Farly [Farley] Matron of N.Z. Home "Aotea", then saw Sphinx &amp; rode back to Mena on a large &amp; unwieldy camel.  Sphinx gazing with that inscrutable face far away over the desert.</p>
