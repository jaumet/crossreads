<p>We have let this giddy old house &amp; are following the head of the family to ugly horrible nasty objectionable Melbourne. I don't want to go <strike>far</strike> exactly till Dene departs, &amp; the poor chap is having such trouble over his commission, that he hasn't the faintest notion of when he will be leaving. Therefore we are in a decidly unsettled condition &ndash; however such is life &ndash; especially in war time.</p>
<p>Marjorie Burnett &amp; Una were at the wedding. Very naughty young damsels they were too, what do you think they did? Literally stuffed every article belonging to the bride with confetti, I should have liked to have seen her face when she unpacked her luggage. Oh Alan! It's a terrible thing to be frivolous minded.</p>
