<p>Today there is a terrific bombardment all round.  Dunkirk is being bombarded again also Furnes [Veurne] from early morning.   Taubs [Taubes] over nearly all night and day.  A few of the Furnes shells have fallen short and dangerously close to our hospital.</p>
<p>For several nights there has been big cannonading something big must surely happen soon.   Oh! it is a sight to sit at ones window and see these great flashes.   It all seems to facinate one so</p>
