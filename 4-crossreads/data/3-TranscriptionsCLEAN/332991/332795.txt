 Friday March 16.1971 The sun shone out bright and early this morning but later it again became cloudy. The British are now 30 miles beyond Bagdad, and it is reported though not yet in the papers, that Bapaume is fallen.  I hope it is true. I am always getting boxed up with the points of the compass here.  It is not an easy matter to tell where you are by the sun in the North Hemisphere  by the sun.   In Australia the sun travels across the sky overhead, & a glance at the sun will always tell the aproxaminate position.
