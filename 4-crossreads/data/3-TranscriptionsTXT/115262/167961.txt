 Friday 13&nbsp;July 1917. 
 Usual parades and drill etc. 
 Isolation believed to be lifted tomorrow at midday. 
 At 4 p.m. we handed in, to Camp Q.M. the following articles :- 1 pr. (mounted service) breeches, 1 tunic (service dress), 2 complete suits dungarees, 1 pr. boots. 
 Awaiting orders  of  for further movements from this camp.
