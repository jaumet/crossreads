29/3/16Young Gow lays bet of 10 disasters with Eric, Fred &amp; I that the Australians will not be in Egypt in 4 days. Take him on. Freddy's transfer to Postal Corps turned down &amp; he ramps &amp; raves like a lion over it.
30/3/16Hot as hades all day.
31/3/16Close of another month. Put in private letter to Naval Commander of Transports Alexandria for transfer.
1/4/16April Fool's day. Freddy falls headlong into a trap &amp; goes to see about his transfer but only gets laughed at.At night he give us his opinion
