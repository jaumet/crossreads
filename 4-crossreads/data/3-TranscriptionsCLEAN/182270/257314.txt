 THURSDAY, JANUARY 2, 1919. 
 Fine but showery towards the 
 evening. Went out to the bank 
 with Alan; & round to see Sir 
 Charles Wade re my getting 
 back, but could not see him. 
 Went out & had tea with 
 Cam Williams. Went to 
 dinner at the Pic. Grill room 
 afterwards with the mob.
