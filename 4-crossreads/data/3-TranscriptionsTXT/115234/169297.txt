31st
We were in the front line trench.  We are in on the left of Warneton:  I am attached to no 10 platoons Lewis gun.  There are six of us & we are holding an isolated post on the left flank  Fritz is 130 yards across from us.  I am writing this seated on a sandbag with a row of bombs on the parapet in front of me  I am day sentry &
