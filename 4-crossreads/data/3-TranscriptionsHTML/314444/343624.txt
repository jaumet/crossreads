<p>to France he has been admitted to Hospital twice &amp; at present he is down at one of the convalescent camps about one Hundred miles from here. Poor old Chas is getting out on Sept 1st so Lizzie wrote &amp; told me. I expect he will be in camp in England a week or Two before coming out here again I will be looking forward to seeing him again. He is one of the best Teen  &amp; I am proud of him for carrying that sleeping <strike>draft</strike> draught of his that he administers to the big cold footed M.P.'s</p>
<p>Well Cheero Harold &amp; look pleasant. I will soon be home &amp; bringing Jack &amp; Chas back with me. Don't forget Mother &amp; Irene. Please help Irene with her correspondence. Au Revoir mon cher frere, Bonne Chance et bonne Sonte. Votre affectueux Frere Alfred xxx</p>
