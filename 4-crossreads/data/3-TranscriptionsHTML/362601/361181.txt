<p>30 March, Sunday</p>
<p>F. Some snow storms.</p>
<p>Recd letter from M.R.L. (L/L). Over 130 admiss, &amp; I work until 4 pm. Wrote until 6.15 &amp; D &amp; Lord &amp; I walk all around &amp; to Bulford. Meet Cpl Niuttall &amp; we have supper at Garmain Canteen &amp; listen to Mechanical Music. Walk around to Sling Y.M.C.A. Home &amp; sit by fire until 10.30. Have long talks about the good old times.</p>
<p>Sent Dane's letter &amp; Blighty to Auntie.</p>
<p>31 March, Monday</p>
<p>F. but tries to snow.</p>
<p>I go to English Class &amp; am at Dance 7 pm. I play until 9. I get astray in Tango. Come Home &amp; write 4 pages &amp; post letters at 10 pm 30. An awful L &ndash; letter. Bill Trouter comes down to sleep &amp; snores. D. &amp; D. go back to London. Matt comes over.</p>
<p>Recd letter from Miss Scroggins &amp; Ellis also wire. Lights out starts at 10.30 pm.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L.</p>
