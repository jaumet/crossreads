 last Sunday from Col Owen who commanded the 3d Battalion &ndash; he was critically in the spot where Frank met his death. I do hope you will have good news of  your  dear ones. &ndash; That 'Enola' business was a tragedy &ndash; almost nine 10 times the landing on the 25th April! My daughter in law &amp; her mum are still in London with her [indecipherable]. I hope they will be arriving here mid year.
