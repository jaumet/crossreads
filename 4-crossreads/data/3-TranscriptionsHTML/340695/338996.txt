The window was smashed &amp; the ceiling pitted with holes &amp; we were covered in bits of glass, mortar &amp; dust. I've never been awakened so suddenly before the report was terrific. The roads are very narrow so the explosion was delightfully close. The residents got a scare they lined up out in front yapping like fury we yelled out through the window "Alle tout de suite". They mobbed our room afterwards nice girls &amp; all while we were still in bed we must have cut a funny picture.<br/>Our Anti Aircraft guns brought down a plane to-day from almost above our heads it was a grand sight.</p>
