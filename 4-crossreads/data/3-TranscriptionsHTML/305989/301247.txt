<p>Sunday 11 March 1917<br/>Packing up for move to Bir-el-Abd, wrote No. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 5 and 6].</p>
<p>Monday 12 March 1917<br/>Left Bayud for Bir-el-Abd at 8.30, arriving about 1.30.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13 March 1917<br/>Quiet day:  holding horses for farrier.</p>
<p>Wednesday 14 March 1917<br/>Crook day, windy &amp; sandy exercise in morning.</p>
<p>Thursday 15 March 1917<br/>Cruel day:  windy:  fairly warm, packing up.  Mail Mother (2), Theo (2), Elsie, Jean, Nedda, Norma, Lucie, Mollie (2), Jess, Gussie.</p>
<p>Friday 16 March 1917<br/>Got everything fixed up ready for treck to El Arish.</p>
<p>Saturday 17 March 1917<br/>Left Bir-el-Abd early &amp; proceeded to Tilul arriving early:  tired.</p>
