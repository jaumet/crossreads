<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>I have to acknowledge with many thanks receipt of your parcel, containing 37 pairs of socks &amp; 4 knitted Balaclavas.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>"WAR CHEST" SUPPLY DEPOT,<br/>[Signed] J. M. Antill<br/>Hon. Superintendent<br/>per M.A.</p>
