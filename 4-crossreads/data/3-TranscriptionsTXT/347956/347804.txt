 Dec 3rd 
 Russia captured 70,000 Germans the Crown Prince included. 
 Thur 3rd Slept on deck last night but it was a bit on the cold side &amp; woke up to find ourselves anchored at Port Said, nothing very exciting happened last night &amp; we were passing troops all night stationed along the canal. this morning we are taking fresh water on board;  during the night we passed  a ship with the Royal Engineers on board &amp; they said they expected to be fighting any day now.
