<p>From an old Clerk <strike>in the</strike> who had been in the service of the original partnership of Want and Johnson I was given an interesting insight into business methods in the earlier part of the century. He told me that there was then a dining room <strike>in the orig</strike> annexed to the office, where a formal midday dinner was regularly served every day. Mr. Want <strike>sat</strike> took his seat at one end of the table, Mr. Johnson at the other, with a butler in attendance, and generally one or more favoured Clients would be invited to join in the meal.</p>
