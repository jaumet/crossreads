 July Saturday 14 1917 
 As expected poor Taylor died last night at 1 a.m. Thus the toll mounts.   Jock, Carty &amp; self go into Boulogne Mac's on night duty &amp; disinclined for jaunt. We get our phizes taken. Visit old Boulogne. French National Fete Day. Everyone in holiday attire  Buy favours. Listen to band on Esplanade.  Wimereux to . Tea at C.W.L. &amp; then to Kursaal. Cinema  Home walk. Shikkered Aussies. Tony supposed to go on leave to Blighty today.
