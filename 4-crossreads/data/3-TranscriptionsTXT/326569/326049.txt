Friday, 11/5/17Much warmer today.  Afternoon went for short route march in morning saw two aeroplanes manoeuvring at battle practice.  After tea went to Perham with Meldrum for bath.
Saturday 12/5/17Nice fine day, threatening rain in afternoon.  After dinner went to Tidworth &amp; after tea to Salv. Army Institute &amp; Regt. Inst. at Perham Downs with Andy Ken.
Sunday, 13/5/17Reveille at 7 a.m.  After dinner washed my laundry &amp; after tea went to Y.M.C.A. to post letters to Aust.
