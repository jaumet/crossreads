<p>26 March 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Begin to learn experience by finding trouble. Our rifles are chock a block with dust   &amp; sand. So we buy rifle covers   &amp; avoid any more hours work cleaning.<br/>Big detachment of Army service men from Kitchners New army arrive w/- waggons   &amp; mules</p>
<p>1915 March 27<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Things progress in a quiet manner.<br/>Receive    <strike>you</strike> first letter from home Very glad to hear about the people there</p>
