<p>TUESDAY, JANUARY 7, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Knocking about town</p>
<p>in the morning. Went down &amp;</p>
<p>put in an application for an</p>
<p>early return to Australia at</p>
<p>Horseferry Road in the&nbsp;<s>evening</s>&nbsp;afternoon.</p>
<p>A big mob of us went down to</p>
<p>the Pic for&nbsp;<s>lunch</s>&nbsp;dinner &amp; got</p>
<p>a couple of boxes for the &quot;Boy&quot; after</p>
<p>&amp; had a good evening.</p>
