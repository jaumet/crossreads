  
 Thursday 15/2/17 
 Left at 7 am & went by car to FIESSELLES giving a lecture Div School, returned about 10 pm, very tired. 
 Friday 16/2/17 
 Visited front line Battn & made preparations for taking over. 
 Saturday 17/2/17 
 Took over front line from 28th - one casualty - applied for leave of absence raining slightly. 
 Sunday 18/2/17 
 Wrote to Doff - Thaw set in - very muddy and wet. 
 Monday 19/2/17 
 Visited front line posts. Enemy machine guns very active.
