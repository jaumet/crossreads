  4  decidedly bad for the nerves. My nerve has gone rather badly &ndash; and though I at first refused &ndash; the Doctor finally insisted on my going to a Rest Camp for a spell. So here I am. It is a most delightful spot &ndash; fine house and magnificent grounds &ndash; and above all peace and quiet. One can only hear the guns as a muttering. My difficulty is sleep, or rather the want of it, and when I do go off I get horrid dreams. I'm afraid I am reluctantly compelled to admit that I am too old for a young man's job &ndash; and a Platoon Commander's job is a young man's show. Promotion is very slow nowadays &ndash; a certain number of officers do not go into a stunt.
