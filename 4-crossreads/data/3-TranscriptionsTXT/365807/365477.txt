 183 Belvedere St. San Francisco 6. July 1916 
 My dear Dave &amp; Alice, 
 I cannot comfort you, I can only send you our love &amp; deepest sympathy. We still feel very sad about Arthur, &amp; wish we could help you to bear this trial. I did hope you would be spared this blow &ndash; Strange that his call should come in France!
