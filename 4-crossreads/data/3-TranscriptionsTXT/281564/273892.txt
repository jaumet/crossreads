 The excitement was not all over yet. One of the Noncoms reported a couple of men for have &pound;500 in hiding. Fred &amp; Ron subsequently fell in for having loot in their possession. The barracks wen wild with excitement &amp; during the night Scotty nearly murdered the non-com, was removed to "calabash" 
 Tuesday 13th Oct Scotty got 7 days hard for the assault &amp; the guards got 7 days on outpost. Concert at night. 
 Wednesday 14th Oct Marched out early in the morning &amp; had a sham fight (on active service remember) "H" fairly cut "E" to pieces. After partaking of a hasty lunch we again set out over a new route in search (so I believe) of a pole for a new wireless station. I hope they found a suitable one as we went far enough.
