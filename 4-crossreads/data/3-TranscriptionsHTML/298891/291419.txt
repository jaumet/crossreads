<p>2 Sunday</p>
<p>Sister Davis &amp; I went to Vodena to lunch with Lt. Borzidar Djordjivic's train sat 2 hrs. at Vladevon &amp; we got there at 3.30. He gave us lunch at Serbian Caf&eacute; &amp; tea at Tel.office. Walked to waterfall &amp; to train. Met Sheila, A. &amp; Olding in town Raining when we got out of train. Merednik came to meet us with lantern. Tubby had lovely meal hot for us. Lovely plane tree in grave yard still in leaf &ndash; autumn tinted.</p>
