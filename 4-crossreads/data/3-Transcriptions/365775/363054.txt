<p>front, for us at least, all our divisions come in here first. it is rather a quiet part of the front being on the right of "Armenti&egrave;res" &amp; almost opposite that great town of "Lille".</p>
<p>Our battery is situated about half a mile from the village of "Fleubaix" &amp; when I tell you this village contains between four to five hundred civilian population, the greater part are women &amp; children &amp; most of them are Flemish, you will say we are in a very snug corner. Fritz occasionally shells the eastern side of the town, in the vicinity of the church, or what remains of it, all left standing is the great spire &amp; tower, the rest of the building is a heap of ruins,</p>
