 Tuesday, 5/6/17 Threw more live bombs in morning & drill with box respirators.  Received letters from Guild & Uncle Fred.  Evening took stroll down the town. 
 Wednesday, 6/6/17 Fine sunshine.  Wrote home.  After tea went down town. 
 Thursday, 7/6/17 Rather warm today.  In morning went through real gas.  One chap outed.  After tea went down town.  Wrote letter to Guild.
