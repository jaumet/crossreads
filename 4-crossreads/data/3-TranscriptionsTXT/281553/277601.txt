 May 15th Woke up to find it very dark and rainy &amp; windy sea about same as 14th.  Running on deck &amp; other physical drill till breakfast.  Sausages &amp; Porridge etc. passed sailingship on starboard side about mile off same time going opposite direction. 
 9 a.m. Parade Musketry between showers. sea rising.  Dinner stewed rabit &amp; vegetables.  To wet &amp; rough for drill afternoon. wind &amp; sea high by tea.-time. all temporary structure on fore deck lashed down for weather. music after tea.  Bed 7.30 pm. [indecipherable]
