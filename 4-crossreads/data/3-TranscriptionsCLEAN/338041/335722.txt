
July 29thReturn of Pte Stock (Wounded) & news of Bert Mallet's death etc.
July 30thWeather hot & depressing - The Anzacs continue their excellent work round Pozieres - I pay a visit to Eddie Cornish 45th Batt and have a pleasant evening - More casualties among our Ambulance.
July 31stAustralia Day - First hand news from the trenches - News of Harry & others - A Hell on Earth - "Gallipoli insignificant" - Another visit to E.C. [Eddie Cornish] wherein I meet Capt. Perry & Lieuts Murray. Fergusson. McIntyre Storer.
Aug. 1stMail from home - Presence of numerous bomb-dropping Taubes. Quiet night on Duty.
