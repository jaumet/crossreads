<p>October 1916<br/>France<br/>the boys were seasick.  We had about 900 troops aboard including a lot of Highlanders &amp; officers returning from leave in "Blighty."<br/>At Boulougne we had to march about two miles to the rest camp which was near a very big wireless station, the biggest I have ever seen, &amp; remained there for the night.<br/>9th   We left Boulougne by train for Etaples where the Australian General Base Depot is situated, &amp;  arrived in camp about 1 PM after</p>
