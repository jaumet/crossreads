 1917 MARCH 
 13 TUESDAY 
 Fine, but cloudy most of 
 the day. I went for a 
 bath parade in the morning 
 &amp; saw old Warren up 
 there. Cleaning my saddle 
 all the afternoon. There 
 was a big stunt on about 
 midnight but Fritz got 
 wind of it &amp; our infantry 
 could not get over.
