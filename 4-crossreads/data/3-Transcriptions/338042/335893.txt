<p>June 13th<br/>Daily Routine &ndash; Letters from Hornsey &amp; Wim-don. A praiseworth feat by American Pilot who on losing propeller travels 9 miles over enemy country into our own line &amp; makes safe landing &ndash; Show as usual &ndash; Debut with "The Whitest Man I Know" &ndash; <u>My</u> Testament.<br/>June 4th.<br/>A morning spent in rehearsing &ndash; an afternoon spent autographing "cards books etc. &ndash; and studying new sketches &amp; numbers &ndash; Signatures of Gen Birdwood Andrew Fisher atc atc side by side with those of the "Coves""</p>
