 1915 Mar. 15 Mon. Wrote up a lot of Scout work during the day. Cloudy all the morning &amp; in the afternoon rain fell &ndash; first since arriving in Egypt. Attended a meeting of our Scouts Association in the evening, which was adjourned until to-morrow night. Capt. G.L.B. Concanon came into Hospital with Enteritis.
