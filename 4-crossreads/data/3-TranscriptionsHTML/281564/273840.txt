<p>20th August Thursday<br/>Continue with the same aforesaid exquisite scenery. Receive instructions as to treatment of Papuan princesses, &amp; incidently as to treatment of selves. Informed that letters will be taken at Moreton Bay but after writing one &ndash; long &amp; loving &ndash; mails cancelled. Said letters heaved over board in bottle.<br/>Pass evening very enjoyably with singing &amp; cockfighting</p>
