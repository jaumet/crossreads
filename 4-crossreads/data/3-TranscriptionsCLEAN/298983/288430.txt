  
 Wensday 5th June Cycled about 14 to Hatford up the River that runs between Harwich & Shotley Took lunch with us & got a boat Rowed a couple of miles this very pretty river Came back to Allens for tea & stoped for Diner 
 Thursday 6th June Left Harwich about 10, Bussed to Bayswater quite a long trip from Liverpool St Station & spent a few hours with Aunt S
