<p>23<br/>night, and there's little yet builded for the day.<br/>Outer edges of such broad features as woods and even the skyline are uncertain and remote.<br/>But there is a growing purity in the higher sky.<br/>Shrouds of grey are taking the form of clouds with clear touches of light on their lofty edges.<br/>The silver of night has failed and vanished of its dull gilt and now the sky is bleached blue, brighten-ing each minute.<br/>Soon there is light enough to see the scarlet of poppies by the wayside<br/>And soon, and rapidly, nights shadows have disappeared while yet the holy grey of dawn</p>
