<p>10-3-17<br/>spin to Helouin after doing some shopping, passed Brains &amp; Hicks &amp; felt inclined to get out &amp; have an argument but they gave us a smile of recognition &amp; did not pass any remarks on us wearing caps; Had tea at the Tewfik Palace Hotel at Helouin arriving back at the Nurses Club at 6 oclock Cobb met us here at dinner, we then went to the concert at the Anzac Hostel &amp; found it very good, the items were contributed by the Welch singers; We then engaged gharries &amp; went for a drive across the Nile &amp; back to Zeitoun; Harris &amp; I left the party here &amp; the girls &amp; High went back to Abassia; High is only a kid &amp; a fine little fellow, midnight before we got to bed tonight</p>
