
1915Feb. 21 Sun.Patients in Hospital  RECORD 906.Not much doing in the work line.  In the afternoon went into Cairo in one of our Motor Ambulances & returned about 7 p.m.We have attached to No.2 G.H. 3 Motor Ambulances & 2 Motor bicycles & 1 ordinary bike.During the afternoon discovered a fine room upstairs for sleeping purposes & Cudmore & I Intend to sleep up here. Room gets morning sun. - has balcony - two beds with soft mattresses - 2 chests of drawers - chairs - bathroom & lavatory attached. - some class. Took C. Wade for a tour of inspection over the Hospital.
