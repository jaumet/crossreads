
<p>1052</p>
<p>FRIDAY, APRIL 11, 1919.</p>
<p>42</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm, but cooled down &amp;</p>
<p>came up windy at night. We dropped</p>
<p>anchor in the bay about 12.15 AM</p>
<p>The boats took the troops off</p>
<p>during the morning &amp; we</p>
<p>left again at 3.30 pm for Melbourne</p>
<p>They did not even take off the</p>
<p>cargo they had. The troops</p>
<p>went into quarintine.</p>
<p>I got a wire from mother.</p>
<p>Days run 149 miles</p>
