<p>July 1918<br/>Monday 29</p>
<p>Ribemont. Bty Position<br/>At 12.30 am the stunt opened and the barrage went over for 20 minutes then lulled for ten. After 10 minutes it opened again and continued until 20 minutes had passed again. <br/>At 4.30 am another heavy barrage was laid down for 10 min.<br/>During the stunt Bdge went twice and guns 5 times.<br/>Ran a new piece of line out with Smithy to the O.P. line. Fritz shelling fairly consistently all day. scored a direct hit on the Canal Bank and nearly did some seroous damage.</p>
