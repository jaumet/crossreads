<p>February 1918<br/>Friday 1</p>
<p>On the road<br/>Our reveille was at 4.30am today and we pulled into our billets at 4 pm.<br/>The 53rd, 54th, and 55th Batteries are all billeted in the village <br/>Walked to Lumbres with Jamie and Smithy and had a decent feed of eggs and chips.<br/>Weather fairly good  spent an hour on my harness and horse.</p>
