<p>July 1918<br/>Friday 5</p>
<p>Vaux sur Somme.1st Battery Position<br/>At 1am this morning Villery Brettoneaux and Hamel were burning. Captures in this Sector now mount to 100 M.E. and 42 Officers, as well as the prisoners and material captured yesterday.<br/>Very quiet throughout the day, had a few minutes fishing, received a letter from Toots.<br/>Weather glorious.at 10.40 pm Fritz counter attacked and we received the S.O.S. the attack failed. At 2.35 am Fritz again counter attacked, we received another S.O.S.  Attack failed</p>
