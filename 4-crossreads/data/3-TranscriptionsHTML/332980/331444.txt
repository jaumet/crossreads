<p>was more so as there were Signals of all colours ahead, after I left the Main my Guard who was on the Engine who knew the roads &amp; whistles directed me &amp; I just crawled along not knowing in the Dark where I was, all I could see was stop Signals here &amp; there &amp; Trains &amp; Engines moving about, after a few moments I found myself on the Main line <strike>lane</strike> to Bolougne, &amp; passing another Signal I was turned onto the Loco Triangle so as to be able to turn my Engine, &amp; soon was turned &amp; arrived at the Column for Water, where I received instructions to leave at 2. am it being then 11 Pm so I had 3 hours stand by &amp; after obtaining a Dixie of Tea I cooked some Bacon &amp; had some supper &amp; waited for 1.30 am where my Guard piloted me out of the Depot &amp; into the Marshalling Yard on to my Train of 750 Tons &amp; at 2.5 I was ready to start, but could not make out where &amp; what direction I was going anyhow after giving several whistles</p>
