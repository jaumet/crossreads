This evening all C coy Sergts were invited over to the Officers Mess for an evenings fun, so over we went, the evening started off with the usual toasts & then followed "dinner" which was very good, after dinner more toasts & so they went round, at first some of them were cunning & only sipped their whisky but soon all was changed & they were tipping it down wholesale I think the Capt set himself out to get the R.S.M. (George Gould) drunk & he succed-
