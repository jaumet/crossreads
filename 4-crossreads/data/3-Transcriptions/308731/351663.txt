<p>Franurra<br/>Rotty Hill<br/>5.2.18</p>
<p>Dear Dad.</p>
<p>I do think that. Mum is going to write.  And it has been hot too day. and Mum put 5.217 untell. I told her and then she put it down. and. Ruth is writing to day the three of us are writing to day to and I can not <strike>writing</strike> write very much and. Anna is up hear now and. Miss Heffernan is up here.  On Friday we went too. Mrs Tanners.</p>
<p>Much love<br/>from frank.  xxx etc.</p>
