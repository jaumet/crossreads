<p>A gallop to Nauroy and then<br/>[October 1918]<br/>6 Sun<br/>Going out of action to Hervilly<br/>7 Mon<br/>Entraining at Tincourt for civilisation.The batt numbers about 190<br/>8 Tues<br/>After an all night ride we arrive at Vignacourt, it is raining &amp; so cold<br/>9 Wed<br/>The Angelus is ringing &amp; pretty girls friting about, life in billets is good, after up the line</p>
