<p>Monday 4.9.16<br/>Sent parcel home (registered) containing paper knife &amp; brooches,  Expect to have all patients evacuated &amp; be away from here by end of week.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5.9.16<br/>Bleak &amp; Cold; rained practically the whole of the day.</p>
