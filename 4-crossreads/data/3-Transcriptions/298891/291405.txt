<p>18 Sunday</p>
<p>Cold grey day.  Sister Davis &amp; I went to Vodena by train &amp; to Convalescent camp &amp; poked around &amp; met Borzidar who took us to tea &amp; then we had the luck to catch 2 lovely cars &amp; be brought right home in time for dinner.  Matron came back.</p>
