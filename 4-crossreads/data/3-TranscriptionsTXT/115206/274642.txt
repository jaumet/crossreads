 March 1918 Wednesday 13 
 Messines Another glorious day, beautiful sunny and warm day. After hurrying home last night we did not have to go forward. A 15 pounder was taken up as advanced A.T. gun. Procceded to L'aison with Mr Kerr at 6.30 pm. Raid timed to come off at 3.20.am tomorrow morning. Weather at 6.30 pm, considerably colder and Meteor figures show rain is about. No.6 came over to Bty. Positon at 6 am. also the spooks with exception of Ken Gunn who has gone forward with A.T. gun
