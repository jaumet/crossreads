The transport fleet known as The Flying Fleet consisted of the same ships. Kaisar I Hind. Indarra. Canberra. Malwa. Caladonia. Fine & splendid weather prevailed throughout.9.30 pm British submarine passed between the lines of transports with signal lights flying. It being a clear moonlight night & passed within a few yards of one of the destroyers with signals displayed & 400 yards of the Indarra & passed astern out of sight.
