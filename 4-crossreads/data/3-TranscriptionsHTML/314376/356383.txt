<p>72<br/>my heart in my mouth I picked up my rifle and prepared to resist. However nothing came of it, we heard a few bombs thrown and exploded in "NO MAN'S LAND" so off we set and equipped with rifle and ammunition set to work.</p>
<p>After this we were put on Gas Guard for the rest of my term in the front line.</p>
<p>So anxious was I to do my duty and not let Fritz come across [indecipherable] that I got quite a strain in the eyes from looking through the periscope.</p>
