
24/7/18 After tea went to top of big hill at back of camp. Had a grand view of the English Channel & surrounding country. Can see Le Toquet quite distinctly. 25/7/18 After tea went over hill for a walk.26/7/18 Raining heavily during morning. Marked for Con Camp & Board.28/7/18 Sunday Drew kit from pack store at 12pm & had to walk to Etaples to the medical Board Base Depot - arrived there about 4pm.
