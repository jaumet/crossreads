The falling masonry fell on an improvised cookhouse, inside, burying five men, but only one was hurt.<br/>With three others I have a dugout, that, after the exposure of the tents gives a great sense of rest &amp; security .  It is a cellar, about eight by ten with two bunks on each side, an arched brick roof, &amp; all the ruins of a big house on top as a pad between us &amp; danger.  We have a nice stove in it; &amp; can have a light all night without fear of Fritz 'planes seeing it.</p>
<p>Eight men were killed &amp; wounded by a shell which landed in an engineering dump at the back of us.  The Division is evidently being withdrawn &amp; sent somewhere else.</p>
