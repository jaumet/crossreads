 Wednesday 10 April 1918 Hun Aircraft Patrol.  Pilot Eustace Headlam, in air 2 h. 55 m. We met no Huns. An R.E. 8 was shot down by a Hun today. Our first success for many weeks. 
 Thursday 11 April 1918 Hun Aircraft Patrol.  Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 2 h. 50 m. No excitement, reported new camp of 225 T.S. &amp; 3 H.L.
