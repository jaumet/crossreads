<p>6 October 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Better this afternoon Swallowed a lot of "dope" my attacks never last long-  Some talk of 1914 men going back again but as I have been to England &amp; now fairly well expect to have to wait a bit longer still it sounds hopeful.<br/>X Post this with love to all Parcel mail due tomorrow so hope get yours- Cheer up Mother dear X<br/>War news is good &amp; we will all be home again ere long  <strike>Your fond son</strike></p>
