<p>From - Flagship. &nbsp;To - &quot;Warrego&quot;</p>
<p><u>12th Septr. &#39;14</u></p>
<p>(9-15am)</p>
<p>Commander Beresford per &quot;Warrego&quot;:- Brigadier requests that your party be withdrawn from W/T Station to Kaba Kaul and then to Herbertshohe to form part of garrison there. Make certain that wireless is absolutely out of action before leaving station.</p>
<p>0915</p>
