<p>Mademoiselle Irma-Marie received my next visit, and we enjoyed one another's chatter for a couple of hours.   While there I was one more reminded that there is a war on.    A neighbour came running through Irma's house &amp; across the street in a great flurry.   The two letters in her hand suggested the cause of her haste &amp; Irma showed me the photos of her two sons, both of whom were at the Front.</p>
<p>In the evening I went on to Rainneville, and returned home through St. Gratien.</p>
