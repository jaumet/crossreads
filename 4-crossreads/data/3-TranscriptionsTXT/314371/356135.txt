
Fri. 25th MaySpent day in camp.
Sat. 26thWent into Boulogne at 2.30 p.m.Fine town but not as good as Marseille.
Sun. 27In camp.
Mon. 28In Camp.
Tues. 29Leave to Boulogne.
Wed. 30Leave to Boulogne, saw the American medical unit arrive.  Climbed to top of Grand Army Column.Marked out.
Fri. 1st JuneMarched out of No. 7 Con. Camp to Base Details & went to town.
Sat. 2nd JuneWent out i/c of big fatigue party to Rue Constantine Bks., Boulogne.
Sun. 3rd JuneSame as yesterday.
