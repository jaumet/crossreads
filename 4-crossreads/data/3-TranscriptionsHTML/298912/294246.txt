<p>Dinner consisted of pea soup, that without a word of lie set like candle grease as soon as it got cold. This is an absolute fact, so one may guess the <u>soup</u> it was .I had a biscuit &amp; a cup of water, the biscuits being hard enough to stand a brace &amp; bit.<br/>After dinner the warship began firing away, as we afterwards found to find the range of several points where an attack is expected  at any moment. HMS JUPITER she now [night] stands off the shore with her guns trained, a dark smudge of black, grim death ready in an instant to send an iron foundry of shells into the enemy. Two other cruisers &amp; a big battle cruiser stand right in the mouth of the canal, also ready to reply to the attack. Two</p>
