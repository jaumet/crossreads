
29/3/17All quiet - go for swims all the afternoon - mail arrives30/3/17Move overMove over to HQ position and do not erect.  At tea we shell a Turkish working party at Gaza.1/4/17Jacko shells us during morning.  Lovely surf in morning2/4/17Our bombardment of Gaza starts.3/4/17Still shelling. Chandler & I leave 9 am for Rafa for goods.  Pass through KHAN-YUNUS.  Busy little market place here.  Take photos and have look round.  Arrive Rafa 4 PM and bivouac with some 52nd Division  A.S.C. men.  Go to Flying Corps & see a number of photos of Turkish positions.4-4-17Leave 9 am for Bela arriving 4 PM.  Seven of our planes up for a Taube - no good.5-4-17Go down to Bela & see the tanks.  Tiger-Kia-Ora.  Pincher Sir Archibald, Knutly and three others - 8 in all.  Male tanks carry two 6lb guns & female 4 maxim and smaller arms.6 to 10thNothing doing.14thJacko shells railhead & busts up things.  Blows away half the Clearing Station.*See the tanks go out 10 PM.*15thShells camel corps & wings our 60 pdr of 90th batter.  Stunt on
