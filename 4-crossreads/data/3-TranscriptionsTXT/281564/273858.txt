
Monday 7th September7 AM Landing party No 5 company Marines left for  Fly River to guard wireless station7.45 We left port & by 8.30 were well outside the reef. During the morning we received a lecture on "what to do in the tropics" We are not to wash in the morning  but at night, & then in hot water. Mosquito nets are to be supplied & camps burnt off to drive off the insects.
12.30 do - more oblivion.During the morning the Queensland troopship "Kanowna" returned for some unaccountable reason to Townsville. Some say - probably the bloodthirsty element that her firemen struck.  Li'ghts out.
Tuesday 8th SeptemberEarly this morning promoted for bad drill & insubordination to the kitchen & received the first meal since my departure from home. This is so auspicious event that the courses of the dinner might be worth noting.1 Meat Pie & vegetables.2 Rice pudding & prunes3 Tomatoes, carrots & turnips for dessertMay I remain there until the end of the voyage.
