<p>1916 August 9<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Slackness continues thank God. It indicates that not so many of our boys are bowled over.  I go to Senlis on a service waggon for rations. Good view of observation balloon we pass right under it. French artillery camped nearby.<br/>Bob down for a while returns letters I gave him to read.</p>
