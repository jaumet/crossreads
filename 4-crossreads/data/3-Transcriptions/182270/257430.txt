
<p>MONDAY, APRIL 28, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Went round the</p>
<p>bottom end in morning, and</p>
<p>round the Hill &amp; Ben Ledi with</p>
<p>Jo in the afternoon. Jo &amp; Peter &amp; I</p>
<p>went out in the car after dinner</p>
<p>shooting rabbits ect in the</p>
<p>head lights. We got a few hare&nbsp;</p>
<p>&amp; 4 rabbits.</p>
