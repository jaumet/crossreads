  
 2.  Well Dad, I think I must have given Mum all the news.  It is 11 pm now & I've finished Duty so as I have had a long day, I think I'll undress & roll into Bed.  Ellis will not be Home for an Hour or so.  The Bulford Express is a wearisome journey at Times.  It never takes less than 1 hour & 1/4 to do the 12 miles. Give My regards to Otto & to those who remember Me. Best Love From Harry X
