<p>Friday 10 August 1917.</p>
<p>Usual routine before breakfast &amp; after breakfast sadde cleaning &amp; watering camels.</p>
<p>Detailed for picquet for 24 hrs from 6 p.m. tonight.</p>
<p>Printed photos.</p>
<p>Posted as 2nd relief on guard from 8 till 10 pm. but managed to work it so that I could see the concert, which was a success too.</p>
