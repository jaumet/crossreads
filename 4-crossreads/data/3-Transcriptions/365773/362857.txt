<p>as was anticipated struck his strong posts with machine guns etc &amp; we fought him in the dark for VA hours &amp; then the ones that didnt run surrendered, collected 51 unwounded Huns in one batch, the Coy gathering in over 70 sound &amp; 20 unsound prisoners. I had 1 officer &amp; 26 men killed &amp; over 40 wounded out of 150 who went over. I am glad to say the other Coy's didnt suffer nearly as much.<br/>I believe we are going out for a spell shortly &amp; by gum we need it.<br/>During this</p>
