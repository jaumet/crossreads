
Friday, Feb.14The weather continues fine & is getting warmer every day. Flu' is still in the air & a number of patients in Hosp. have high Temps.Sat. Feb.15.A week of our Homeward Trip has gone & we are not doing bad at all.The ship is quite steady now, & we can walk the decks in comfort. I wrote a couple of letters, one to Sam Ibbs & one to France, but its not much use writing Home as I should be there as soon as the letters.
