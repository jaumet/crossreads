&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Saturday 20 April 9 A.M.From later information it appears we are a fair distance due south of Hazebrouck  &amp; nearer Merville which is a few kilos distance. This confirms the idea that we were on the southern side of the [indecipherable]: I wonder if the powers that be are preparing to strike hard towards Merville, &amp; so cut off or chase the retirement of the Bailleul &amp; Keterin forces.We are now preparing to move up the line.
