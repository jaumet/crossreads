We get on to the Jaffa Road &amp; there discover British Cavalry going the same way.&nbsp; They are the 4 th  &amp; 5 th  Cav Divisions.&nbsp; We leave them &amp; go through a ploughed field, down an avenue of good old Australian Gum trees &amp; halt in a field by a tall hedge.&nbsp; We unsaddle,  &amp;  feed  up  and tie up our horses &amp; I spread my horse  rug   blanket  on some hard clods &amp; seek repose.&nbsp; It is my first night on trek.
