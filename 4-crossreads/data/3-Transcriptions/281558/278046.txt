<p>came home : people seem to have the "wind up" with the constant air raids : pictures in hosp theatre after tea &amp; very good : another air raid so Fritz is going strong</p>
<p>Tuesday Oct 2nd</p>
<p>Dartford Hosp : got a few things from the red cross in morn met Tom Clunie so we went into Dartford for a stroll : paid 3-6 : Tom is marked for Weymouth tomorrow</p>
<p>Wednesday Oct 3rd</p>
<p>Dartford Hosp : nothing much doing : rash came out on my arm so the Dr sent me to isolation for early scabie treatment : good place &amp; no restraint at all</p>
<p>Thursday Oct 4th</p>
<p>Dartford Hosp : helped to clean up the ward : bath after dinner &amp; treated</p>
