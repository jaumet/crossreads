  
 I remained sick for a full week and then began to eat like a wolf. We passed Gabo Island late on the night of the 10th and Melbourne during the afternoon of the 11th. Towards night we lost sight of land for the first time.  We soon caught sight of land next day and all the way until we left Western Australia we lost sight of land for a short periods.
