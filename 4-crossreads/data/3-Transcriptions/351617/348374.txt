<p>west side of the town which ended up with a full victory to the Ausies &amp; Fritz going for his life with our fellows in hot pursuit. The total Casualties for this stunt was 5 killed &amp; 9 wounded which was not too bad considering the shooting done by Fritz.</p>
<p>Assisted by the 13th Light Horse, we pushed on after Fritz and a couple of days after entering Bapaume we found a solitary Hun hiding in the sugar Factory about 1 mile from the town. He told us that two others and himself had been left in Fremicourt to explode a mine on the X Roads, and they had decided to give themselves up.</p>
<p>He had set out alone to find us and having told the Intelligence officer, myself &amp; 2 others were sent up with an officer and German Interpreter, to get them out. Armed with 2 Mills Grenades &amp; our Rifle and Bayonet</p>
