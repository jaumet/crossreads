<p>Jan 9th<br />Went across to the beach @ the Wady Nahr Sukereir where a vessal is being discharged the beach was teeming with natives engaged in the work &amp; was one of the most animated &amp; noisy secens I have witnessed here,</p>
<p>The vessal lay out about 1/2 mile&nbsp;&amp; a large number of small craft were ferrying the cargo ashore,</p>
<p>When the bows grounded a line was hooked on &amp; about 100 natives hauled the craft inshore Imense dumps were formed which were being transported by thousands of camels,&nbsp;</p>
