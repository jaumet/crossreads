 May 3rd Hughie Gannon, Ill  &ndash; A criticism &amp; kind words from Mr. Hennessy are much appreciated by myself. Two shows. May 4th Last day at Hammersmith &ndash; Most successful matinee &ndash; And a wonderfully enthusiastic night show &ndash; Great financial success &ndash; a heavy but a happy night.
