
27 ThursdaySlept & rested & read & in the afternoon Maud & I went for a nice walk. Nice day but cold.
28. FridaySame routine. Went to Bedales after tea to see Girlie Smith.
29. Saturday.Lovely day. Miss Whishaw Maud & I walked  to Frox  [Froxfield?] in afternoon Girlie Smith & a small companion came.
