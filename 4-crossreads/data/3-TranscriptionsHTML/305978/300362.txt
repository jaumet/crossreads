<p>Dec 24th/<br/>Nothing much to write of since the 17th<br/>Xmas Eve. On gaurd at well for 24 hours.<br/>The Scoties are camped here near the well &amp; we hear some broad Scotch they seem a happy lot of laddies if they do only get a small sum to bust up.</p>
<p><u>Dec 25th Xmas Day  1915</u><br/>Reville at 3.30 am.<br/>Saddled up at 4.30 am. left all spare kit etc rolled up &amp; put on waggons, so it seems as if we intend</p>
