<p>17 June 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Another march right out to beach.  Large hotel undermined &amp; fallen.  Cartwright shows us how rings are made. Cuttlefish used for moulds.<br/>Miss Ada Wright entertains us in Y.M.C.A. Tent. Very clever. Men &amp; Women. Sketches.</p>
