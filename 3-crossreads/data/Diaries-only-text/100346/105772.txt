for stretchers : at dinner time about 14 planes of Fritz's sneaked over above the clods suddenly appeared over head : great barrage of machine gun bullets a few shells made them go back to clouds again : did not bring any down : dropped a few bombs over a bit from our camp : some of the boys on fatigue to the front line
Sunday Sept 16th
Ypres : reveille 7 am : answered my letters also sent a few photos for Mum to keep for me : Holy Com very nice : about 160 present : Fritz squadron overhead some noise when our barrage got onto them : boys on fatigue again : Gib's squad went
Monday Sept 17th
Ypres : reveille 7 am : rifle gas helmet parade : fell in for fatigue : 7 8 platoons my squad went : had a lot of fooling around finished up in the supports digging a cable trench : plenty of shell
