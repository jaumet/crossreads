Our first introduction to the Egyptian who put the hard word on us for Cigarettes & Backsheesh  as he called it. Boarded some cattle trucks & away we went. Imagine our condition, a boiling hot day, full dress, an open truck and travelling up till 1 O,clock practically through sand. The native Town of Suez is a very ramshackle affair.
