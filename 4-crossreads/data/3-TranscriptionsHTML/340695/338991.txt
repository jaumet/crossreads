<p>and arranged the sheets to their liking laughing all the time they are great but elderly they were once sisters in a convent our Interpreter tells us. The darling little kiddies who come here to school gee I could dangle them all on my knee have already fallen in love with one little dear she has taken quite a fancy to me I think.<br/>25-5-17. Still at the school, the teachers residing here could not be nicer &amp; the kiddies are lovely. Went for a ride on the bike last night with Horace to La Motte a beautiful little village in the centre of a large forrest It was a glorious ride.<br/>26-5-17. Moved from Vieux Berguin to-day to Bailleul &amp; are billeted in a very fine house. The folk</p>
