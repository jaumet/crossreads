  
 Thursday 25 November 1915  Some arrivals from Australia today, parsons and doctors. 
 Friday 26 November 1915  In p.m. room all the morning. In Cairo about 4 pm.  At 4-30 pm. met Miss Delohery on the balcony at Shepheards Hotel. Had a talk with her.  A train load of sick & wounded in at 4.30 pm today. 
 Saturday 27 November 1915  Another Morn Aurora rosy daughter of the Dawn has risen with splendour in Egypt   Reading & writing all day. 
 Sunday 28 November 1915  At Basilica at 10 a.m. Mass. Afternoon tea at No 4 Auxillary hospital  Big batch of delayed letters from home, they went after me to Lemnos.
