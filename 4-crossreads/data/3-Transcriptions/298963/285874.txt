<p>29th<br/>After 10 hours &ndash; long weary hours too, I am snugly tucked away in 24 General.  Thank God the people are different here.  All the "show" and red tape are gone and I am in a hospital again.</p>
<p>Was Xrayed this forenoon, the operation is tomorrow.  Then I hope a few weeks in Blighty.  My companion here on one side is a Gordon Highlander, the other is a Derby man.  The Scottie &amp; I are good chums, the other chap I feel</p>
