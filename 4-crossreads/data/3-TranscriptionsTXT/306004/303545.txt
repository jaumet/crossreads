 2.10,16 Mon 2nd Put in this morning cleaning up &amp; getting ready to move on, had lunch at 11 oclock &amp; at 11.30 filed off the lines &amp; took our horses down to the troughs watered up &amp; formed up to the West of the troughs put our nose bags on our horses &amp; waited for the 2nd Brigade to arrive, they turned up at 1.30 &amp; we commenced our journey to Kantara, had a very decent trip in &amp; had to wait for some time before we could cross the Canal
