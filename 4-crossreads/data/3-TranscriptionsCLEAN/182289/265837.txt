  
 Le Bizet Weather hazy and dull all day with light drizzle about 7 p.m. 
 5.3.18 Advance parties of 22nd M.G. Coy reported and were guided to positions.  C.O. being taken round the line.  Main body arrived about 6 p.m. - relief completed by 8.30 p.m. when command of the barrage position passed to the 22nd Company.  Transport was provided for taking the men to the rear lines, and a hot meal served on arrival. 
 6.3.18 - Moquet Lines The day was spent in packing limbers, cleaning camp and disposing of surplus. During the night several amendments to the original Move Order were received from the 9th Brigade.  See Appendices. 
 7.3.18 Reveille at 6.30 a.m. - men on parade at 7 a.m.  Final packing of stores completed and all transport "hooked in" at 8.45 a.m.
