<p>Then visited the Cathedral before reporting back at 9.45 to R.T.O. (Railway Transport Officer). Informed we were to report again at 3.15pm. Therefore we trammed out to St Etienne - a suburb about 4-5 miles out. &nbsp;Very poor quarter of Rouen. Returning to Hotel de Ville we saw monument to Napoleon 1, &amp; visited L&#39;Eglise Saint Ouen, a magnificent but dirty &amp; neglected structure. We walked around the interior balcony &amp; exterior walk on roof of this church &amp; had more interesting views - several churches, the tower where Joan of Arc was imprisoned, the Seine &amp; its shipping, several bridges, gardens &amp; busy streets.</p>
<p>We lunched at Hotel Bordeaux - a poor lunch with sour cider which we unfortunately changed for sour wine and paid 3 francs extra. Again walked the streets, bought a few little trinkets - some swimming animals for Billie &amp; Alan, a salt spoon &amp; paper knife for Jay. I sent Merle some cards yesterday from Havre.
