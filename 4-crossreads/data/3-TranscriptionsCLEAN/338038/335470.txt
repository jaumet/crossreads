  
 Brig. Genl Burgess In the Field . France 14th April 1918 
 Dear Sir, I am writing these few lines, inadequate at best, to express to you, for myself & all the officers of the 4th Aust. Divisional Artillery, our muted [?] & sincerest sympathy on the loss you have sustained on the death of your Son, Majr T.W. Garling, 37th Battery, 10th A.F.A. Brigade.
