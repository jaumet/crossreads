 8.  a fellow gets Homesickness very bad.  Auntie &amp; Marie are absolutely lovely in everything they do &amp; say.  Uncle tells Her that    she    anyone would think it was her own son she was pining for &amp; Auntie could not love a son very much more than her goodness to me indicate. Now Dad, Mum, &amp; George, I must toddle off to bed.  Best of Love &amp; Good wishes to you all.  Keep well &amp; cheery &amp; don't think because You get no letters that we have grown callous &amp; do not write every week but lots of mail gets lost. Regards to our enquiring Friends too. Lots of Love &amp; Kisses From Your own Boy Harry X X Regards to "Good Old Bess"   I will write to Her soon.
