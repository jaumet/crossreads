<p>1917 June</p>
<p>15 Friday</p>
<p>Stayed in bed till 4 p.m. got up &amp; pottered. Had a bath &amp; went to bed at 9. Shoulder very stiff. Feeling weak &amp; snivelly. Another gorgeous day. Mist on garden like a [indecipherable] at dawn. Had the flat to myself.</p>
