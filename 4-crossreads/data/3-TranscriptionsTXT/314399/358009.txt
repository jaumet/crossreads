
Sunday 12 January 1919Left Beaurieux [indecipherable]Got 2 pm train to Brussels & arrived there at 5.30 pm. Got a hotel &then had dinner. Went to a picture show & [indecipherable] night dancing places.
Monday 13 January 1919Left Brussels at 5.20 am & arrived Paris 9 pm. Went thro Tournai, [indecipherable], La Bassee, Loos, Dernancourt, Heilly, Corbie, Amiens all along the old battlefront & gee it is some knocked about. Taxi couldn't find the place we wanted for a long time but finally got settled.
