<p>2 October 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Get leave   &amp;  go out to Zeitoun to see the old man.<br/>Clean   &amp;  oil my pistol which is getting quite lonely it having never been   <strike>being</strike>  off my belt for 4 mths<br/>Have tea w/- him  &amp; return via Heliopolis</p>
<p>1915 October 3<br/>Sunday<br/>18th After Trinity</p>
<p>I have a restful morning  &amp; go to church<br/>Doctor thinks me fit to go to Alexandria so expect to leave here shortly<br/>Very good concert this afternoon</p>
