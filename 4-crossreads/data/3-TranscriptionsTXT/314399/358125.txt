 Thursday 8 May 1919 Bonny day again. Saw Bill. He looks much better. Had another driving lesson. Went out to Brixton for tea &amp; came home at 11 pm. 
 Friday 9 May 1919 Lovely day again. Real decent weather at last. Went to the school. Had another driving lesson. Am doing well. After the lesson saw Bill. He looks a bit better. Then out to see Janet &amp; Marie at their Jumble sale. Had tea there. Just as I left it rained very heavily.
