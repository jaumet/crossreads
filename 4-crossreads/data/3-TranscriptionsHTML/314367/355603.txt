<p>Bompas has 2 medals &ndash; One for the Gambia &amp; the other he hasn't said what for &ndash; Mr Bompas uses a slight deflection of either eye to convey his appreciation or otherwise &ndash; He's been 25 years in the R.N. &amp; you bet he knows Routine &ndash; He can tell you anything but I don't know enough to probe very deeply &ndash; 'A lovely little ship' 'First class tucker' Never had such a good turn since I was afloat says Mr B when the Paymaster curses the ship, the grub &amp; everything in general &amp; then Mr B looks at me &amp; winks &ndash; He'll tell you 'Tug Wilson' made the navy &ndash; That the Australia has 12 inch guns &amp; throws an 850lb bomb &amp; 2 of them &ndash; that the new 13.5 guns will knock spots out of the Australia &amp; throw a 1400lb bomb &ndash; Mr B generally has a pipe in his mouth &ndash; addresses the men as 'my sons' &ndash; is always clean shaved &amp; very neat looking &ndash; punctual to the minute &amp; when he gets into position, turns &amp; looks at you &amp; winks &ndash;<br/>Our crowd runs thus &ndash; Commander F-gerald RN &ndash; Navig lieut &ndash;Walker R.A.N.R. - Executive lieut Nelson &ndash; R.N.R. (Reserve) &ndash; Bompas WO-RN &ndash; Chief Bosun. Gauntlet WO-RN &ndash; Gunner &ndash; Lane RN-Pay &ndash; Self &ndash; Volunteer &ndash; Davies &ndash; WO &ndash; Chief ERA &ndash; We all mess together save F-gerald. We have also Gordon &ndash; R.N &ndash; WO &ndash; Gunner &ndash; He joins up the Warrego at Rabaul &ndash; He's intelligent &ndash;</p>
