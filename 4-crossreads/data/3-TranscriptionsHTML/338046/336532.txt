<p>1917<br/>June 10<br/>ourselves on the shore of the lake with Telfont Manor on the farther side.   We walked by the side of the lake till we were attacked by a swan.   I dived behind a tree.   Cameron had ideas of his own about taming swans but soon followed suit.   After several runs around the tree in which we managed to get a few photos of the attack we made a strategic move to the fence and hopped out on the main drive.   The swan waddled off victorious and joined his mate, and we walked up the driver for the remainder of the distance as if we had been ordinary people instead of being adventurers.
