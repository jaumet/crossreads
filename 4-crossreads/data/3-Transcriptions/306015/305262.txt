<p>The Provost authorities allowed us to remain in Amiens until 11 next day and so we had ample opportunity for having a good rumble round the city.   The only hindrance was the fact that we were soon penniless &amp; none of the banks would assist us in our dire need.</p>
<p>Nevertheless we saw everything that was to be seen, and smiled at all the bright "froggy" girls, and looked at the shops, and parleyed with the French "soldats".   We had decent meals at the barracks, &amp; also slept there at night.</p>
