 10-3-16 two houses but managed to get through O.K.;  about 12 oclock, B Sqd &amp; Head Quarters continued on their journey &amp; C Sqd turned off to the right &amp; going through another native village we came to a clump of palm trees in a mud wall, where a part of the 2nd Regt have been stationed. here we put down the breast lines as this is our destination for the present; Major Smith said he expected a message from the 2nd L.H. from off the desert by helio during the afternoon &amp; I put in the afternoon looking for a flash which never came; Reid &amp; I have to attend to a telephone that is set on a post here, but as it rings when called up, we are not tied to it, &amp; sleep under it during the night; A canal is running within a few yards of the camp &amp; it is about half a mile to the edge of desert with cultivated fields between us
