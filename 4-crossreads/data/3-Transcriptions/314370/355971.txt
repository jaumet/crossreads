<p>January, 1916<br/>Monday 24<br/>Work as usual<br/>after dinner get guns ready for the range<br/>in the evening make a lot of cartridges ready for the stoppages tomorrow</p>
<p>Tuesday 25<br/>Range practices stoppages<br/>My foot is very sore &amp; troublesome<br/>Weather cold &amp; we are very glad to get back home to dinner</p>
