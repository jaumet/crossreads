<p>Rollestone<br/>27.11.16<br/>Dear father,</p>
<p>It is just 8 p.m., &amp; yet all the mud outside is frozen stiff.  Instead of going in up to your knees, you slip on your head which is infinitely preferable.</p>
<p>Since writing last I have seen all sorts of people that you either know or have heard of.</p>
<p>Broadbent is here.  Of course you know he was in the dug-out when Arthur was killed.  He wishes to be remembered to you.  Barlow of the original Battalion is also here.  I think his picture is in that photo of the 20th Bn. Officer &amp; over 6 feet.  He has had all sorts of narrow escapes but so far has not been touched.</p>
