<p>Sunday 20 October<br/>Entrain at Calais &amp; travell to Etaples by an awful train arriving after six hours journey &amp; put up for the night at rest camp.</p>
<p>Monday 21 October<br/>Arrive back at the unit at Pont Remy after travelling from Etaples to Longpre &amp; having to walk back to Pont Remy at night.</p>
