 Sunday January 26. 1919. (77) A fine day. The ward next us (40) is isolated today for mumps has broken out there. I went for an hours walk this afternoon around Chicherell. Monday January 27. 1919. (78) A fine day but cloudy. I finished reading "Private Spud Tamson" a humorous  tale of the [indecipherable] in the early days of the war. This is the ex Kaiser's birthday. This afternoon is showery so I did not go out. Tuesday Jan 28. 1919. (72) A fine morning. A workshop has been established here in connection with the A.I.F. educational scheme. The garage has been cleaned up and fitted with a few tools such as vices, benches, a small forge and lathe. [Shorthand not transcribed] Wednesday Jan 29. 1919. 80. A fine day but cloudy. I received a letter from Kitty Martin today dated Jan 26, 1919 which is a record for speed. I drew &pound; 0. 15/- today. My credit balance
