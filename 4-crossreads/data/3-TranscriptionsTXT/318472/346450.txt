 13 August 1918 Tuesday The same old Boat routine &ndash; 3 meals per day &ndash; ships Inspection and put in the remainder of the day as best one can. 
 1918 August 14 Wednesday "Concert at night" 
 A very considerable swell developing &amp; a rough choppy sea running. The weather is now very much cooler than in the early part of the voyage &amp; one can do comfortably with a blanket at night.
