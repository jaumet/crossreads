<p>Sunday 31<sup>st</sup> Dec.</p>
<p>Reviellie at 3.30 AM and after</p>
<p>a hurried breakfast we set off</p>
<p>again for [Harvre?] &amp; entrained</p>
<p>there, for as we all thought</p>
<p>Armenti&egrave;res. Bill Hunter &amp; I</p>
<p>had a compartment to</p>
<p>ourselves &amp; were very comfortable</p>
<p>Country much the same as</p>
<p>England only warmer.</p>
