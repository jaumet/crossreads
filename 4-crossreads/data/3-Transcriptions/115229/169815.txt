<p>I was lucky enough to get a lift on a car for about three and half miles.&nbsp;At lengh we reached Ribemont again a sore and sorry looking lot and greatly depleted in numbers owing to heavy casualties.&nbsp;This was the end of our first trips in on the Somme.&nbsp;While at Montaband huts the first lot of snow fell on the 18th. We reached Ribemont on the second occasion 22nd and received our first big pay in France the same</p>
