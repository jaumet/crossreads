 Rain seems to have set in this evening &amp; it is blowing &amp; howling outside a treat, but I am quite comfortable sitting in our well appointed billiard room in a nice easy chair beside a good coal fire, I am just counting the days now till we get our trip home to "Aussie."  I see the 1st &amp; 4th Divisions have just put up another splendid stunt &amp; the papers are giving our splendid troops great praise, I wonder how Len is getting on he will be in all this heavy fighting. 21st.
