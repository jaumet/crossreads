<p>[Monday 30 October 1916]</p>
<p>At office all day counting votes no- Egyptian Ballot Papers have NOT arrived from Egypt yet-<br/>Fruit, Flowers are very dear in London &amp; it costs me 10/- for a meal for 2 at a decent Restaurant.<br/>Laura seems to spend her time in an endless succession of dinners &amp; afternoon teas   She goes out a great deal &amp; seems to have many friends, going out keeps her from thinking a good deal about the mess she made of her life &amp; the War &amp; her sons marriage so young, but don't say I said so, she has many friends but likes to be "a Triton among minnows"  few are Well Known</p>
