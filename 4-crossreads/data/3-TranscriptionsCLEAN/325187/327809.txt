  
 [Saturday 4 March 1916] 
 Arranged some sports today to celebrate opening of Pontoon Bridge break the monotony for the men   Some Copts are building a Church near here altho' Mohommadens & Copts live together in same villages they would cut each others throat- The Copts show their wrists on which is tattoed a +  & reckon we are same sort of Christians as I have some + on my arm where BOILS lanced by Doctors.  Letter from Joe & Day 23/1/16 & Australasian. Mits & Soks from Kathleen please thank her- Also letter from Laura  Papers from Mrs Cotton - We are glad to get news of the world -You will find me little altered except an extra line or two has been etched on my hard face.
