<p>1917 Tuesday 28 August</p>
<p>Afternoon off. Take it easy read write &amp; sleep a little   Terrific gale of wind &amp; rain raged all day.<br/>Great excitement about tea time. A sailing vessel blown ashore on rocks below cliff. Thousands watch her being pounded to pieces. 4 of the crew rescued after great difficulty. Rockets &amp; life line. Wind blowing hurricane force. <br/>Chat at YM.C.A. Win to Miss P--</p>
