<p>April 1918<br/>Monday 8</p>
<p>Blaney Tronville  - D.A.H.Q.<br/>Had a fairly busy day <br/>No lines.<br/>Met Bob Bulcher, and a few other old companions including young Craven from the Oaks.<br/>Rained hard throughout the day.<br/>"Morse Code" looks fairly tired on the strength of his heavy work.<br/>Fritz bombed the vicinity with some fairly nice bombs. He is also shelling the roads between Corbie and Fuillory</p>
