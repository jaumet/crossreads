Today nearly all our company were sent to Bacstinaur [?] on fatigue the work in question was loading barges with coal, after they finished they had a hot bath. I hear Capt McKenzie is going to make enquiries over my leave, &amp; find out why certain persons have gone before me, who were not entitled to it.
