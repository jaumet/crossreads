<p>We also voted this afternoon held it in the trench, I'll bet 90% voted for Hughes's party, they will have a big majority from the soldiers this time.</p>
<p>I believe old Jock is going the England on the Instructional Staff I will be very very sorry to lose Jock for he &amp; I have been great pals &amp; I have always found him a chap worth trusting &amp; a right good fellow few better &amp; honest as the sun,  I will be</p>
