20thI visited the 54th Bn. today proceeding across country on a bike.  I passed by a huge dump and cluster of hutments captured by our chaps.  It was blazing merrily, the enemy having fired a number of incendiary shells into it for this purpose.  I also passed a group of our boys examining an enemy aeroplane.  Such common sights still seem to attract a large number of the troops.
