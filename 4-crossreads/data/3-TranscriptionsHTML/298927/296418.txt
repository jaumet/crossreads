<p>We have a chap here so cross eyed that when he crys the tears roll down his back. He was put in charge of a section one day and he said "Fall out the man I am looking at".  They all fell out.<br/>The camp and its environments (woods etc)  is a harbour for women of loose morals and much harm is being done among our boys, the Bulford total being steadily swelling.</p>
