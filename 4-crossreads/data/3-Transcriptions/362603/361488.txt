<p>Red Cross letterhead<br/>Bulford<br/>15.7.17<br/>Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George,<br/>I have had no exciting experiences since writing last Monday but I will just write a few lines to let you know that we are both quite well.  Last Tuesday I rode to Salisbury on the Bike through a round circuitous route.  It was a lovely route that I chose.  The Road in places was an avenue of great tall Beeches which met overhead &amp; formed a great Arch.  I only passed one village in 3 pieces.
