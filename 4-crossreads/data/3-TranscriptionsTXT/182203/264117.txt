Had a nice letter from Mrs Copp, Mollie, Miss Eyres, 2 Charity, one before &amp; the other after baby arrived. Doris Bower &amp; her dear Mother, Mrs McQuie, &amp; hold your breath one from   Elsie Cock. Quite a number from other friends in the firing-line &amp; nurse friends. It is delightful to hear from one's friends &amp; relations.Darling little Eddy's drawing &amp; paper cutting arrived safely &amp;  I shall decorate my room with the later. He must be getting a big boy now, what a difference I'll see in him when I return to dear old home.
