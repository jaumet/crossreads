<p>The hopes with which our eyelids close each night.</p>
<p>By way of anti-climax, we had a novelty in the way of jam for tea to-night. It is called rheubarb jam and judging from taste &amp; appearance I  should say that it consisted of rheubarb &amp; plums in about equal proportions &amp; is really excellent as is also the marmalade which we occasionally get.<br/>In fact, although I do not generally eat marmalade at all, I liked it so well the other night that I ate so much that I do not think I will ever be able to look at it again - till next time.</p>
