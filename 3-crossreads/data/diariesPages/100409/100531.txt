After that went to Anzac Buffet, had tea, stopped there couple hours. Then drew my hut, got tube at St James Park stn arrived at Clapham Common about 8.15 pm. Went to bed at 43 Klea Avenue about 10 pm.
10/12/18
Up 8.30 am, slept like a top. Was on a moving stair-case last night at Charing Cross Tube Stn on way to Clapham. Left Klea Avenue at 9.30 am took dirty linen to a laundry. Then took tram to Victoria went to Pay Office re pay book but same had not arrived from Weymouth. Had dinner at Anzac Buffet then went to Passport Office. After waiting 1 hours was told passport was under consideration for a few days, so I kicked up a row asked to see the boss of the show. This had the effect of adjusting matters they said they would do it up straight away. I gave one of them 2/- he got it fixed up for me in no time. Then went to War Chest Club wrote Base Post Office asking them to send my mail to Clapham.
