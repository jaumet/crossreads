 The women of England and Scotland are simply splendid &amp; one must take off their hats to them &amp; no mistake. Women train conductors, train &amp; motor drivers, munition workers; women farm labourers; women collecting tickets in the trains &amp; railway stations &ndash; I saw one girl last night at midnight at Paddington; she collected my ticket &ndash; tired &amp; heavy lidded but quite cheerful. They are even in dungarees cleaning out the railway engines in the workshops. We fight to win with a backing like that.
