 Did a turn as mess orderly this morning, &amp; yesterday was on Y.M.C.A. fatigue.  Both afternoons read &amp; slept with a liverish result.  Had very little exercise lately.  Don't care a brass fender. Wrote Rita a doubting letter a week ago, which may have the effect of causing unhappiness &amp; even a rupture.
