<p>1915<br/>Jan. 8 Fri.<br/>Parade of Unit for allotting duties to each man in hospital on landing &ndash; Attached to office as Clerk.<br/>Weather still continuing fine and sea calm.<br/>Mess Orderly to-day.<br/>Only ship seen to-day was a cruiser in the distance.<br/>Weather pretty warm to-day especially in the evening.</p>
