<p>Friday Feb 2.2.17. stand to 5.30AM had breakfast and cleaned up went to wells to do washing had a bath done neither no water the wind would blow one away everything covered in sand received 5 letters from maud .win josie walter aggie.<br/>Saturday feb 3.2.17  stand to 5.30AM had breakfast went to wells for water taube over to day.<br/>Sunday 4.2.17. stand to 5.30AM had breakfast and moved camp made a dug out on &frac14; guard to night in the desert a cold night<br/>Monday 5.2.17. stand to 7.AM still on &frac14; guard fine morning wrote to Josie Walter aggie mick relieved from &frac14; guard 5.PM had tea</p>
