<p>I had to get ready &amp; take charge of the week end picquet so I did not get off <strike>for</strike> at all last week end.  We are expecting a mail in tomorrow &amp; I am hoping to get some <strike>thing</strike> letters &amp; a parcel too that Aunt Eva has sent me.  We are still in the tents &amp; have no idea when we will get out of them but as the weather is keeping pretty good we don't mind.
