
Sept. 8th.Special leave to Poperinghe with Roy & Harry. The day occupied in viewing Cathedrals etc - shopping & visits to Y.M.C.A. & Soldiers Club. Also in having photographs taken - We spend a most interesting day - A large mail makes a happy ending.
Sept. 9th, 10th, & 11th.Route marches - Infantry drill - letter writing. (The two sisters)
Sept. 12thWe defeat the 6th F.H. in a Rugby match by 5 to nil - a good game - Pay & mail - Asher Chowne.
Sept. 13th.A number of us do some hop picking for some Belgian womenfolk -
