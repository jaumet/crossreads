<p>Saturday 21.4.17<br/>Suffering a good deal with my teeth.  Bearers beat Transport at football 5 to nil.  Colonel went on leave to Paris.</p>
<p>Sunday 22.4.17<br/>Things a bit lax while the Colonel is away.  14 F Amb beat our boys at Aust Rules  Went to church in Albert at night, 5th Div Band played Hymns &amp; a few selections after the service.  Appendicitis very prevalent of late.</p>
<p>Monday 23.4.17<br/>Bearers again played &amp; defeated the transport.  Albert at night</p>
<p>Tuesday 24.4.17<br/>Walked to Poziers during afternoon</p>
