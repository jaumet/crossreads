 1st May 1915 Saturday 
 On the 21st April we were all served out with 2 bags of 'iron rations'  &ndash; (1 bag) biscuits, beef &amp; a tin containing tea, sugar &amp; oxo tablets.  Transports moving off all day.  We started at 3 o' c &amp; anchored with a few others on a bay of an island.  Moved off again at 8 o' c p.m. &amp; we had a little sleep.  Up again at 3 a.m. Sunday &amp; had breakfast.  Land visible each side. 
 24th April 
 At daybreak saw heaps of transports &amp; men of war near us.  The 'Galeka'  was nearest to the shore.  On one side was Gallipoli peninsula &amp; 2 large islands on the other (in the distance).
