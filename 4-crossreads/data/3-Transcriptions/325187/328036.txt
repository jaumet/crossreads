<p>[Friday 20 October 1916]</p>
<p>Went to Brooklands Hotel in Manchester as Dolly's home not yet out of the hands of the Decorators &amp; Painters.<br/>North Western Express a splendid Train<br/>Received Australasian from Joe, readdressed from Egypt &amp; [indecipherable] Hay Grazier &amp; local papers- please thank him for all his trouble I hope to get a billet in Riverina when I come back as Dungalear no good to me or Dolly   Stead is a great friend of J A Campbells so be careful what Joe says about his treatment of me.</p>
