<p>(I believe) attack Birsaba  from the East in the morning. Having to circle the place to do it. An Army Corps is attacking I believe and we have only two days rations + iron ones &amp;   are relying almost entirely on Turkish dumps being captured, so if the matter proceeds badly we shall starve as far as I can see. Kismet.<br/>31st Oct. Beersheeba Road opp. Tel es Saba<br/>9.30 pm.<br/>We attacked this morning about 10 oclock &amp;   got into Beersheba late in the afternoon attacking from SE direction &amp;   Infantry making Frontal attack from W, As soon</p>
