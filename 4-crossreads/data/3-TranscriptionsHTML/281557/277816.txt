<p>Sunday Jan 2nd<br/>A very calm day hot &amp; sultry Revellie 6 am : church at 10 am &amp; very nice : bible class after dinner; the two birds seen yesterday have departed. After tea we had a sacred sing song &amp; it was very good A lot of us attended in "evening dress"; stripped to the waist on account of the heat. Slept on deck ; no land or ships sighted. Had our physical drill class on boat deck.</p>
