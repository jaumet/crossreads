<p>3 July 18 ) Anchored at Rosyth.<br/>4 July 18 ) A great number of ships company have been bad with influenza.<br/>5 July 18 ) People are hailing with it ashore throughout the country.</p>
<p>6 July 18<br/>Admiral of squadron came aboard off HMS Birmingham. Went to sea 7 pm in dogwatches and are flagship of Light cruiser squadrons &amp; destroyers.</p>
<p>7 July 18<br/>At sea doing manoeuvres with Admiral aboard. Seas calm.</p>
<p>8 July 18<br/>Came in &amp; anchored outside of bridge at Rosyth &amp; coaled. Admiral went aboard his own ship Birmingham as soon as we arrived in.</p>
<p>9 July 18<br/>Anchored outside bridge Rosyth. King &amp; Queen of Belgiam visited Fleet today</p>
