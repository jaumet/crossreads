
Tuesday 6/2/17
Took over line from 28th Battalion everything quite.
Wednesday 7/2/17
Usual shelling visited Companies in front line sent out patrols at night one casuality.
Thursday 8/2/17
Enemy shelling heavier 4 casualties - good work done at night - went out to Bde for about 2 hrs.
Friday 9/2/17
Quiet Day one casualty relieved by 18th and returned to Cinque Ports Camp.
Saturday 10/2/17
Slept well mid-day went & saw the General who leaves for England for 10 days tomorrow.
