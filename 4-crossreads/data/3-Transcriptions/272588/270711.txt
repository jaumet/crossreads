<p>Friday 24 May<br/>Fine again &amp; morning runs &amp; showers &amp; again in vogue.</p>
<p>Saturday 25 May<br/>Big Aust. mail to-day, always the most welcome. Fine &amp; playing cricket to-day.<br/>An attack is rumoured to be made on Mont de Merris to-night. The Bosch has been shelling heavily all round the line with gas, perhaps preparatory to the second phase of the offensive.</p>
