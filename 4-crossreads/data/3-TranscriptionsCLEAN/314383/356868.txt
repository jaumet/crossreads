 May 10th Had a look over Kermanshah today & at present feel sick at the sights one saw & weary after tramping through the Bazaars.  We left camp (Capt Scott Olsen & I) at 10 am & on the way down saw the first sights of the poverty of the people, for batches of them were out in the fields actually eating grass.  Their bodies were nothing more than skin & bone, yet their stomachs were swollen to an awful extent.  On reaching the Bazaars, the worst cases were either laying in the streets dead or dying.
