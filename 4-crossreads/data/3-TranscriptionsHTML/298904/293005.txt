<p>There was another terrific bombardment of Achi Baba, Kilid Bahr" &amp; the Olive Grove" they fairly pasted them with high explosive shells  About this time most mysterious things began to happen, first of all they stopped all mail from either coming or going, &amp; cleared the Post Office off the penisular, next thing to go was all the hospital patients everyone was mystified, some of us even thought another great offensive was about to take place, &amp; they were getting everything in readiness for it, but it began to gradually dawn on us that we were about to leave Anzac" I need scarcely say how sorry  downhearted everyone</p>
