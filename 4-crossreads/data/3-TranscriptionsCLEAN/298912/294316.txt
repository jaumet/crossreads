Land fades away at 7pm with a steamer's smoke showing away in the distance. This being the last of Australia. Eric & I have a confab on the poop until almost 9 oclock. Hop joins us towards the end & as usual argues over some things through pure habit. Eric in disgust says he would argue the point with a snail for 2d & then tread on it & kill it. 12/1/16 Lovely morning, with fair wind & sea. Banker is played for large  sums on the poop & seemingly the man running the bank makes a fair sum in a short time. About a score of little parties of a dozen or so can be seen close up together heads down laying 5/- on this & 10/- on that, as Eric says "making the money laugh".
