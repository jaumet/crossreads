<p>the sniper got me, hit me a clean knock out in the chest, a nice clean little hole in front &amp; a great big one where it came out behind, bleeding like a pump at every breath.  I had to lay still for a couple of mins., then they got my equipment &amp; clothing off &amp; found the bullet had entered my left breast &amp; come out at the back, the exit hole was bleeding profusely but they did their best to staunch it, gave me a drink &amp; one of them went for the Stretcher Bearers.  After some trouble he found a party &amp; brought them along.  They bandaged me more carefully &amp; put me on the stretcher.  From then on for about an hour &amp; a half</p>
