
13th  Feb  Wound healing very well.  Am up and about now.  Yesterday I had leave to go down to Brighton from 2 to 4 pm.  Brighton is a big place, and along the beach there are scores of big residences lining the promenade.  There is an electric railway along the beach the only one of its kind in England.  The main feature is the pier which is 1/4 mile long and on the end of the pier is a beautiful theatre of the best class I have seen, better in fact than any I have seen in
