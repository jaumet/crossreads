 Thursday 7 June 1917. 
 Fire not yet located but smoke d &nbsp;has ceased to issue from air vents. Watch &amp; precautions still being taken. One boat in sight at 7 a.m. but now as far as we know we are &quot;unescorted and alone in open ocean&quot;. 
 Weather moderate. Did washing during morning &amp; more in afternoon. Wrote home in aftern.&nbsp; 
 Cpl in charge of Isolation &amp; no fault found in any way at the inspection.
