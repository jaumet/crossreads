When we arrived in Egypt at the beginning of May the first of the wounded &ndash; our wounded had arrived.  When a man is wounded he is first attended to on the spot by a doctor if one is available or the A.M.C. orderlies or his own mates &amp; hurried back as soon as possible to the nearest clearing station or Field Ambulance.  These are like small base hospitals &amp; there urgent work is done.  Limbs needing immediate amputation are removed etc. &amp; from there the men  are  were transferred to the Hospital ships &amp; removed to the  Base  General Hospitals at the Base
