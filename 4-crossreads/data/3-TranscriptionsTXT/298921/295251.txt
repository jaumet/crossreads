 October, 1915 Thursday 7  People will want to be careful of Imposters, posing as  Heroes  a hateful word when bandied about &ndash; As in Africa a lot of wasters are making back to Australia when they should be returning to the trenches &ndash; One man with an ingrowing  Toenail  has never done a days work since he embarked another gone to England, one day shot himself thro' the foot.  Tis true tis Pity &amp; pity this be true.  A lot of the best men are also going back unfortunately &ndash; minus limbs &amp; broken. They know the wasters &amp; do not like associating with them.
