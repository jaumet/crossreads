 22nd Miserable &amp; wet. Heavy mist encircles the Camp. Business is very quiet except for an occasional shell. 23rd Misty &amp; wet. The ground is fast becoming a sea of mud. Every day we sink deeper as we walk. A peculiar fit of depression &amp; [indecipherable] came over me tonight and I would have given anything to have been in London or some big city. There is talk of a move soon. Gee! How glad I shall be to get away from this confounded hole. 24th Heavy mist. Sent to Meaulte with convoy. Here I met Geo. Tobson with whom I had a talk.
