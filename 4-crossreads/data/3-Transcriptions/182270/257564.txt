
<p>TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 9, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp;&#39;very cold. Lopped in the</p>
<p>4D in morning &amp; the Mountain</p>
<p>in afternoon. Tickle lopped</p>
<p>in Top paddock &amp; then he &amp; I</p>
<p>pulled up a couple of chain of</p>
<p>fence in Top paddock.</p>
<p>Al. sent Quainton down</p>
<p>for the dray &amp; jack.</p>
