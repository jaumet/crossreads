<p>Tuesday 28 January 1919<br/>Taken off ord. officer but got another job for the day. Had to do some reconaissance work &amp; only just got back when a message came for me that I was to go to Havre on the Demobilisation staff.</p>
<p>Wednesday 29 January 1919<br/>Met Johnny Walker at 9.30 &amp; left Sobie at 11 am for Charleroi arriving there at 1. Went to the Club for lunch &amp; then out to Chatelet to see Dad Hunter Had to wait about 3 hours for him. Had dinner with him &amp; then slept the night at his billet. He looks as well as ever. Light fall of snow.</p>
