<p>Mch 17th The 1st and 3rd Regts moved out of Gila and Bir-el-abd respectively today for Tilul where we bivouacked. On the night of the 18th we camped at Mazar &amp; on the following night at Kilo 139. We arrived at El Arish on 20-3-17 <strike>at</strike> shortly before mid-day. One Squadron of the 2nd Regt remained at Mazar &amp; the other two Squadrons of that Regt are camped separately from the brigade. great advance just taken place in France on a 45 mile front by 10 miles deep. Bapaume &amp; Peronne captured. Revolution in Russia.</p>
