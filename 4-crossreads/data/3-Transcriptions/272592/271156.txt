<p>we struck one snappy &amp; knowall Sergt. but I promptly shut him up &amp; in front of his officer too</p>
<p>As soon as we got our "posts" told off the men got their packs off, &amp; what a relief it was for the straps had cut into our shoulders &amp; stopped the circulation of the blood, &amp; they were quite numbed &amp; had no feeling in them however it soon came back, but it left our shoulders very sore, we took 4 posts over of 6 men &amp; an N.C.O. to each.</p>
<p>Bright &amp; early this morning our aeroplanes were up &amp; over old Fritz's lines, you could not see them for quite a long time for it was almost dark so they were losing no time.</p>
