<p>May 1918<br/>Tuesday 7</p>
<p>Bussy-la&mdash;Daours<br/>Weather wet, heavy rain all day. Had a run to Bonnay via Corbie and Daours.<br/>Chestnut gassed in both legs, shot as a result of injuries.<br/>Ken Gunn and Pea paid me a visit during evening.<br/>Borrowed a large reel of D.3, and sent same to Batterie.<br/>Had a couple of eufs avc du pang, du beure et coffee, at Le Maison des Madame Isobel.</p>
