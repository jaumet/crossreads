<p>Sanvic<br/>Le Havre<br/>France<br/>29.11.18</p>
<p>Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George,</p>
<p>Ellis has all the letters that we have just received so I cannot answer them but I'll tell you such of the events of the week that I think will interest you.  Firstly, we received your X'mas Parcels, 1 each, &amp; they have just come in nice &amp; handy.  The cakes are lovely &amp; have kept beautifully.  The other niceties are lovely also.  Ellis &amp; Len Valentine have gone to the Theatre but I have been out in the wet all day &amp; prefer to sit by the Fire.  I went out by Car yesterday to see Roy, i.e., Jack's Brother, the nice big country Boy that I have often told you about.  We got bogged on the road but a Tommy</p>
