<p>nearly all night.  In thick of it, we moved forward in rear of 16th &amp; 17th line about 7 p.m., returning about hour later.  Roused 10 p.m. &amp; again moved to the Line where we commenced digging the communication trenches on extreme left near N.Z'rs.;  being relieved 5 a.m., returned to our reserve gulley as earlier for rest &amp; safety till further orders.  (Midday writing up.)</p>
<p>18th Saturday continued.<br/>Rested all day.  8 p.m. Manned Trenches 16th Line (Left flank) 24 hrs., withdrawing 9 p.m. Sunday, sleeping close by.</p>
<p>30th Monday<br/>Returned to reserve gullie 5 a.m. to rest till 7 p.m. when we set out for sapping to connect with N.Zrs. Line as before digging all night.</p>
