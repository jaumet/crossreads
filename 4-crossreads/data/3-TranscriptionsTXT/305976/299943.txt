 Sunday Nov 25 Our way to water lays about 3 miles down a red road with Wattle &amp; Gum Trees plentiful. I never expected to see this out of Australia. The Turks are shelling particularly the water so there is still plenty of excitement. Monday Nov 26 Town is well occupied with Hebrews &amp; Bedouin &amp; swarms of Hebrews are returning with their Donkeys laden with all manner of goods. Some of the shops are reopening &amp; the Jew is out to do business in Souvenirs &amp; take you down if you stand  it. Jacko shelling. Auckland Reg suffered heavily taking this place
