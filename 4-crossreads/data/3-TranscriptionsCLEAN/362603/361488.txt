
Red Cross letterheadBulford15.7.17Dear Dad, Mum & George,I have had no exciting experiences since writing last Monday but I will just write a few lines to let you know that we are both quite well.  Last Tuesday I rode to Salisbury on the Bike through a round circuitous route.  It was a lovely route that I chose.  The Road in places was an avenue of great tall Beeches which met overhead & formed a great Arch.  I only passed one village in 3 pieces.  First I came to Lower Woodford & then middle & upper.
