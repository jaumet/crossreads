 Sunday 10th February As usual Pross &amp; I went salvaging this afternoon &amp; got a full tin on Biscuites 8 tins Butter 3 of Jam besides other odds &amp; ends useful about the humpy. One can get almost anything about here &ndash; The waste is terrible.
