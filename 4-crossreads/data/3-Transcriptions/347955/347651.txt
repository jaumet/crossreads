<p>Saturday Jan 5.1.18<br/>turned out 5.45. fed cleaned up fell in 8 A.M. for fatigue went to railway load a lot of gear on train dinner 12 very windy had a letter from Alma.  rested till 4.30 fed up wrote to Alma the wind would Blow you away I am covered in dirt and dust.  had tea turned in<br/>Sunday Jan 6.1.18<br/>turned out 6.30 A.M. fed cleaned up Breakfast rested till 12 dinner had letter from maud rested till 2 P.M. wrote to maud got gear ready had tea 5.P.M. left Ballah<br/>10 P.M. train went to kantara got on a motor lorry went to Camel remount depot slept on the wet mud 12 midnight<br/>Monday Jan 7.1.18<br/>turned out 5.A.M. fell in marched to Camel depot messed about one hour was marched Back mile had Breakfast 8.45 fell in again</p>
