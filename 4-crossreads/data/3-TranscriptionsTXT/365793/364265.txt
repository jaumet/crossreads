 June 20 in this hut &amp; we sleep on straw mattresses on the floor. Just been down town &amp; made a few purchases for the voyage Feeling goodo. Was paid &pound; 1 at Harefield so am fairly financial yet. 
 21 Rotten night last night , fellows coming in all night variously shickered &amp; confoundedly talkative and I was scratching some too &ndash; must have been the rough blankets.
