 17-3-17 Bapaume fell into our hands this morning, our lads took it (5th Division). Good news from all fronts. 22-3-17. Our lads are still advancing great excitement when the papers arrive. Heavy guns are being moved up quite a number went past today. Weather is unsettled, sunshining one minute &amp; snowing the next. Its fairly cold but nothing like it has been. 26-3-17 Moved from our billet in Rue Peronne Albert to this place 114 Rue Bapaume Albert expect to go to BAPAUME on the 28th.
