<p>4 June 1918 Tuesday<br/>Yes I remember Harry Watton carrying me on his horse to Correagh &amp; you greeting me there.  A.W. Pearce letter speaks for itself-   Thank Day &amp; K for Parcel not yet received- but coming-<br/>Note all your news-   Nothing doing here yet so no need to be anxious-   Papers coming later on-<br/>Dolly well &amp; happy.  Tell me what <u>name</u> you would like for the Trumpeter-<br/>All my love<br/>Your son F W</p>
