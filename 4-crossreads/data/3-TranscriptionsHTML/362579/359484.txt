<p>it. The Hun has no fear. Some of the Sisters left this week   &amp;  cannot say if they have sailed yet. Daresay they are waiting somewhere for us. Who knows? The Victorians are to be on our boat, so we are the last to load up.</p>
<p>Received a nice letter from Lucy this morning dated 23.11.17   &amp;  she told me all about Isabel Thompson's illness, how dreadful. Fancy such an awful thing happening to her. I hope she will not suffer much   &amp;  that it will not be a long illness.</p>
