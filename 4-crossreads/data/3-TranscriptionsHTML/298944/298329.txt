<p>That was the first time it had been bombed since we first formed it.</p>
<p>Landed at Hoer[indecipherable] at 1 A.M, waited interminably in a share for "comforts fund" cocoa &amp; Australian biscuits, then started on a 4 kilo march. I was so tired &amp; sleepy that I almost went to sleep <strike>standing</strike> as I was walking along.</p>
<p>Reached Questede (where we formed up the 2nd Field last Nov.)</p>
