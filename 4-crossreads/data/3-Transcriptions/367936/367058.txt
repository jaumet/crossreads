<p>Saturday 21 October 1916<br/>Cleaning saddles etc. all the morning.<br/>Rode up to Kantara to bring down remounts.<br/>Beverage came down to regt.</p>
<p>Sunday 22 October 1916<br/>In beer fatigue picking up empty bottles.<br/>Then draughting the remounts.  In afternoon we fell in and marched to Head Qrs. and were issued with some fruit salad each, given by Port Said ladies.<br/>On picquet at night.</p>
