<p>9/5/17<br/>Our bn was releived again today and we are back in the old position we occupied at Sugar Factory before going into stunt.</p>
<p>13/5/17<br/>Up at 2.30 a.m. and marched to Bapaume to catch train for school. Arrived at Albert in train at midday. At 3 p.m. we marched to a School</p>
