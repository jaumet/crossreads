 4 July 1918 Thursday Depot sports held on the flat below the village. Every one appeared to be very well satisfied so they must have been a success 
 1918 July 5 Friday Kept fairly busy today getting a Casual Draft ready Nothing of much importance to note. The Aussies have had a successful Stunt on the Somme capturing about 1500 prisoners Italians reported doing well!
