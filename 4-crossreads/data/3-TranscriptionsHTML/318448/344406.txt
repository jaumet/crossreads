<p>April 4th<br/>Still in this damnable Jordan Valley. Yesterday morning some &frac12; doz. turkish planes came over &amp; bombed us &amp; this morning they were here before I was out of bed; the result being that I had to run out in my pyjamas &amp; dive into an old well about 6 feet deep.<br/>It was just as well that I did so too, for I had no sooner done so than 4 bombs exploded about 10 yds away. We had two white men wounded &amp; eight natives &amp; one</p>
