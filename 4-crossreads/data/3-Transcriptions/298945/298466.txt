Two alternative theories are either that they were after jewels which might be found in the vault of such a great family,&ndash; or that the object was to gain the lead lining from the coffins. Considering that the lead was taken from the coffin at Roisel, while the zinc <strike>lining</strike> lined coffin here was untouched makes the latter seem reasonable.</p>
<p>The vault had been used as a dug-out by the Germans (probably in the last retreat), who had even slept in the apertures, &amp; there were signs that the ghoulish work had been done very recently probably when they were quartered there.</p>
