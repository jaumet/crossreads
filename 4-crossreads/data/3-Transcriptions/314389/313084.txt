<p>January 15th:<br/>Saturday &ndash; fine day &ndash; quiet.</p>
<p>January 16th:<br/>Sunday.  Visit to Heliopolis.  Bert Argall shows me round the town.  Have tea together.  Am stoney broke, so Bert pays the piper.  We attend American Soldiers Y.M.C.A. service.</p>
<p>January 19th:<br/>Meet Fizzr Flin and hear about Hay.</p>
<p>January 20th:<br/>First Route March with full pack, around Heliopolis.  Visit Obelisk (erected three hundred years before birth of Moses).  Visit Holy Tree and Mary's Well.  March about seven miles &ndash; not yet able to do my fifteen miles.</p>
<p>January 23rd:<br/>Church Parade.  Shift camp, from tents into huts.</p>
<p>January 24th:<br/>Route march, with full pack.  Rain in afternoon.  Rain at night time came through roof of huts.</p>
<p>January 25th:<br/>Route march to dry our clothes.</p>
