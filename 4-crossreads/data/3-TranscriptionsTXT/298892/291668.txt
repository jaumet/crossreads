
2nd. February '17.
Dear Mother/.We are having a spell in billets again now so I have at length an opportunity of writing you a long letter in answer to your last two.I sent a couple of active service Post cards as I was unable to write the last two weeks to let you know I was well. I was more than pleased to receive a photo of you although as you say you don't look quite yourself. I am looking forward to receiving more of the family and a more faithful likeness of yourself.
