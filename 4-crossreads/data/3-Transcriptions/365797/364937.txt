<p>From.<br/>Mrs. A. Fry.<br/>Dene Gully.<br/>Lindfield.<br/>Sydney.<br/>Australia.</p>
<p>Home.<br/>15.8.16</p>
<p>My dear old boy,</p>
<p>Dear old Billie has just been struggling with the intricacies of "pit pat pot" &amp; "as &amp; has" &amp; has gone off exhausted to refresh himself with two juvenile Billinghams who are staying with Mr. Coates.</p>
