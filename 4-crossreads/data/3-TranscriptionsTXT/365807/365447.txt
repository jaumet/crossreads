 Royal Herbert Hospital Woolwich  19 June 1917 
 Dear Mr Ferguson 
 Your two letters duly arrived &amp; I am glad to hear you are all well at your end. Am sorry to say my work in France came to rather a premature end &amp; I am now at above military hospital.
