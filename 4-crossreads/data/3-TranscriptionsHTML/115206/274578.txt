<p>January 1918<br/>Tuesday 8</p>
<p>At Beutin &ndash; France<br/>A heavy blizzard commencing at 6.30 am and ceasing at 7 am placed 10" of snow over everything. Drifts in places were 5 to 6 feet deep.<br/>Smith and Thompson's leave passes &ndash; for Paris via Etaples, in high glee.<br/>Went in evening to rendezvous and played cards. Wrote to Roy and Eve.</p>
