<p>Not much doing all day. Had a letter from Mrs O'Kelly &ndash; wrote to May.</p>
<p>17/8/16  Thursday</p>
<p>Dull &amp; raining &ndash; not many casualtys. French mortar Batterys very busy stirring up Fritz.</p>
<p>18/8/16  Friday</p>
<p>Dull &amp; raining &ndash; Rather lively trench warfare &ndash; had a few calls during the night, the Snipers &amp; Machine guns are cruel the way they play around our dug outs &amp; thro the Cemetery &ndash; luckily for us we know the danger spots &amp; try to duck when duty calls us.</p>
<p>19/8/16  Saturday</p>
<p>Dull &amp; raining &ndash; motor Battery fellows got hell today &ndash; as soon as they start Fritz trains his artillery on them &amp; before they get 6 shots over. We seem to think Fritz has some mechanical contrivance for detecting sound &amp; is ready for them when they start. Poor Capt Jack &amp; Corporal Fisher got badly knocked about.
