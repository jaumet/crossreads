<p>Saturday 17th<br/>Struck heavy seas, boat rolled terribly, bottles &amp; patients flying always.  At five o'clock a wireless was received saying boat was not to call at Melbourne but go right on to Sydney &ndash; why we know not &ndash; but all S. Austs. and Melb. lads are furious.</p>
<p>Sunday 18th<br/>Boat still rolling &ndash; a lot are seasick.  Everyone except Sydney people are wearing a very glum expression, however we expected the staff to go on at any rate I had to put the dispenser on night duty so when I can find a spare moment I have to do the ship's dispensing I won't be sorry to get off.</p>
<p>Monday 19th<br/>Passed Melbourne to our regret and through the night Cape Howe.  All our kits are being fumigated, but I have packed two bags.  I am dreading disembarkation, so far everything has been messed up.</p>
