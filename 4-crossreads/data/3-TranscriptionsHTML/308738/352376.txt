<p>Jan. 16 Saturday 1915<br/>Sea smooth. Cool Wind changed Course, making up coast of India towards Bombay.<br/>Have been inoculated against typhoid final dose.<br/>All lights out early in dangerous water.</p>
<p>Jan. 17 Sunday 1915<br/>Changed course due West &ndash; sea Choppy, stiff Breeze blowing<br/>Fortnightly pay today<br/>2 Horse died.  making 16 to date -  changed course to North West-</p>
<p>Jan. 18 Monday 1915<br/>sea smooth, wind dropped,(Man died &amp; buried off Themosticles)<br/>Rifle inspection.</p>
