Tuesday 23.10.17
Morning strolled out of town into suburbs. Afternoon being cold went to pictures which are all free to "boys in blue", Allowed out from 10 Am to 12 noon from 2 pm to 6 pm, have to answer roll call at 6 pm. Being the Bishops Palace, all join in prayers after roll call for a few minutes.
Wednesday 24.10.17
Very cold wet, spent hour or so in town, but very little to do.
