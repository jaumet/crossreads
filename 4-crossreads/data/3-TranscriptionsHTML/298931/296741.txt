<p>Friday 17<br/>Pay day. Put up a good go last night, cheers &amp; laughter at my antics in the ring<br/>Saturday 18<br/>Sports to day tug of war was beaten on points in the final of championship<br/>Sunday 19<br/>Lovely day we are now about 3,400 from Sydney<br/>A fortnight today I was at Manly with Elsie &amp; Muriel</p>
