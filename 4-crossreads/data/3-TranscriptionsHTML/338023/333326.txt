<p>14th July</p>
<p>[Out of sequence?]<br/>France's Day was quite a popular day in the Smoke. Plenty much talk, plenty much toast, plenty much butter &ndash; to the nation that, as they truly said, saved them, us &amp; the whole bang shooting match &amp; is still doing the Full back share &amp; then some. But we're all glad to see that, once again, Aussi, partic. Sydney, was not beaten in practical celebration. After having a No 1 Class  <u>loaf</u>  I rejoined my crowd (the Skeeter &amp; Turner) at W'loo &amp; got back to quarters about 3.30 am 16th inst. &ndash; in time to take part in our first big stunt.
