Sunday 30th
Final alterations to Canberra Redoubt thence to Lithgow Post. Coy shifted camp N.W. some 300 yds. into deep hollow nearer Canal.
Monday 31st
Siting different position for Lithgow Redoubt all day. Very cold wind.
February commenced in check covered Book.
