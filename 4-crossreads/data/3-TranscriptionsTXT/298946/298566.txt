 We arrived at Chatelet at about 3 p.m. after passing through about two miles  of  town with tram lines complete, not to mention numerous coal mines.  The hospital which I found some of the tent subs had commenced to run, was a large building built three sides round a small quadrangle reached by a small gate. I slept the night on the third floor after a trip to the "Sentimental Bloke" in company with Scotty McNeil, Monaghan &amp; Walter Connor.
