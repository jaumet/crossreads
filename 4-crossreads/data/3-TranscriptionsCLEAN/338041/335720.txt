  
 July 23rd Sabbath - We are now very handy to the firing line and throughout the day we have seen reinforcements & vast amounts of munitions going up to the Line, whilst car loads of our own wounded boys are proceeding from the Line. 
 During the previous night & to-day the 1st & 3rd Brigade A.I.F. captured in first attempt a village & wood which the Pommies failed to capture on six occasions - Visit to 2nd F. Amb to see our wounded & German Prisoners. Presence of German Souvenirs 
 July 24th Despatch of Parcels to home. Our camp in the woods is proving a good one - German counter attack repelled by "our boys". Heavy bombardment heard all day & night. 
 July 25th Practice with the stretchers. Harry & I pay a visit to Contay and then to 2nd Fld. Amb - 600 passed through during the day.
