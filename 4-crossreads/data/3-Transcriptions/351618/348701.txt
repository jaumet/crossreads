<p>ashore this time safely. Wagon was loaded up &amp; the broken Pole had been mended during the day. I let Eltham go to his Battery &amp; get a change &amp; wash &amp; I stayed on &amp; had tea with the Lowland Battery Q.M.S. McLaren. When the Horses arrived after dark to take the stores we started on our round journey to Headquarters. We had to unload a number of cases at Victoria Gully &amp; then took the others up to the top of ridge &amp; unloaded there. With a big fatigue Party the cases were carried up to Headquarters. Unfortunately one of the Ammunition Column got away with a case of milk &amp; we had other losses in items getting broke etc</p>
