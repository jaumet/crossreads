 25th August In the morning I did the rounds of the "red Tabs" (Staff officers) endeavoring to make arrangements for the transference of the men's rations &amp; for sundry affairs to Steenvorde. What absurd &amp; unnecessary formalities I had to go through &amp; now I am further off than ever. My Contempt &amp; disgust for army administration increases with every trifling matter I have to see headquarters about. Things that could be settled in 5 minutes take usually 10 days! And why?  Because it has to pass through a score of unnecessary channels that seem to have been created to find soft jobs for "string-pullers".
