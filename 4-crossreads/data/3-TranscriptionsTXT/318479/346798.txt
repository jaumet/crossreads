
25 July Church parade with the 18th & 19th.  Go on guard at 7 p.m.
26 JulyOn Guard till 12 o'clock.  20 Batt arrive.
27 July17th on a route march.
28 JulyPay day.  Receive mail from home.
29 JulyField firing with blanks.  Pte. Harman out of hospital.
30 JulyNo afternoon parade.  All night route march.
31 JulySham fight with blanks.  Receive letter from Aunt Fan.  Battle in the "Wassa".
1st AugustChurch parade.  Go into Heliopolis.  Brawl in Pyrimid Hotel.  17th Batt sent into Cairo on picket duty.
2 AugustReceive letter from home.  17th on a night march from 7 till 1 AM.  Lose the bolt of my Rifle.  "Dad" Higgins throws his rifle to blazes and falls out.
3 AugustKit inspection.  Hand in my rifle.  Route march through Zeatoun.  "17" Batt ordered to the Citadel in Cairo.
4 August"A" on Brigade duty.  I am on Quarter-masters fatigue.  Get a new bolt for my rifle.
5 AugustWrite home.  "A" & "B" Coys. digging trenches.  No. 4 Platoon dig themselves in with entrenching tools.  Men for Engineers taken out of the Batt.
6 AugustColonel Holmes lectures 5th Brigade about the disgrace of the "Battle of the Wassa".  17th march out at 9PM for an all night march.  15th section remain behind for guard.
7 AugustNo. 15 Section on perimeter guard.  Serg. Allan, Corp. Wolf over guard.
9 AugustTrench digging and Coy in attack.  Pay day.
