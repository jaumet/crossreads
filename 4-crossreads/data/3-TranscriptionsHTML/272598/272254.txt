<p>&amp; I. Noakes came by 2.1 pm train from London.  Went to Hospital at night &amp; saw them all again.</p>
<p>Saturday 12.1.18<br/>Spent afternoon in Cheltenham &amp; the night at the Hippodrome</p>
<p>Sunday 13.1.18<br/>Very cold.  Went for a long walk in the morning.</p>
<p>Monday 14.1.18<br/>Played Billiards at Y.M.C.A. in afternoon.  Had a fine night at hospital fancy dress whist drive at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 15.1.18<br/>Caught 9.30 train Gloucester arrived London 1 pm.  Went to "A Little bit of Fluff at criterion at night.</p>
