
Sunday 26th SeptIt was again a lovely day. Ordnance visit again.
Monday 27th SeptA lovely day again - just mild. On the hills of the islands as the sun is setting there is a wonderful glow tinted with every shade reflecting on the white clouds that seem to be resting on the ridges. It has been quiet all day excepting a few fusilades from several Batteries on our left flank.
Tuesday 28thI had a quiet day. In the evening there was a very heavy bombardment taking place both on our right & on our left.
