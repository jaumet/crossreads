 Spt 9 Much laughter &amp; cries of "give it buksheesh lemonade" &amp; "let me like a soldier fall". The usual Saty night concert, but the audience was small owing to the Polar weather. Several chaps have put up colors &amp; gold bars when they're not entitled to them, so theres to be an investigation by one of the ossifers.
