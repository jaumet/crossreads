  Raily  Railway (Inner Circle) at the Back of the House is a large Shaft for Ventilation purposes &amp; one could hear the Rumbling Sound as each Electric Train passed along. When we went inside there were two more ladies who had arrived during the Afternoon so I was in Good Company 5 Ladies &amp; Mr Smith &amp; myself, we were soon seated around the Tea Table &amp; all enjoyed the Compliments from one to the other. After Tea Mr &amp; Mrs Smith took me around to see her Sister so after bidding the Young Ladies Good Bye we took a Low Train Underground to Waterloo. This Train goes down under &amp; along a long Street, it has 3 Stopping places &amp; there is 2 Lines, it goes underground for about 1 mile &amp; then up again
