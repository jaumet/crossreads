<p>1915<br/>Jan. 25 Mon.<br/>Hospital inspected to-day by Surgeon-General Ford.<br/>Cold in the morning &ndash; hot during the day &ndash; mild in the evening.<br/>Worked at Mena House until 11 p.m.<br/>Hospital now in full swing &amp; plenty of work to do. &ndash; Going until 11 p.m.<br/>Tucker no better.</p>
