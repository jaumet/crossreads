<p>16 Mon. Soldier's fined for abusing Steward's Cook's Etc.  They still sell Cooper Etc at 6 a mug. 1 Bun</p>
<p>17 Tues.  Competitions starts.  I win Both. Heat's Chess and Draughts also am Training 3 Pug's, Fletcher Eather &amp; Stanley.  Fletcher is good one of skuthorpe's old Buck Jumper men.  Enters for Heavy &amp; Middle weight.</p>
<p>18 Wed.  Harvey.  Church England Padre organizer Boxing   Catholic Preist Time keeper  Lt Pettit Official Referee  Fletcher meets Cooks, Heavy 1 punch with Eather Bantam Beat Feild on Points  Feild licks Stanley K.O.  not too bad two wins to start</p>
<p>19 Thurs  I win 2 round chess.  Raining   Indoor Games.  Concert in Saloon we nearly Smothered in Hold's  Win again Draught's tough game  M.P. Sgt ?lassoed &amp; Hung up was nearly choked before release  He is a character  cannot read or write, yet is made sergeant of the Police.  Fun over lights out  Spoils nap and Poker School's</p>
<p>20th Friday  Doing splendid with Barber's shop.  Cut C.Os.Hair  Dr Stewart and Shaved a nurse  Comfort fund's Pay me to shave all Hospital Patient's.</p>
<p>21st  No Parades, Saturday, sport's.  I win rope quoits out right  Fletcher fight's Conquest.  He knocked the Preist out with a Back Hander meant for Conquest  Preist very fat.  S.A.Army captain recovered, sea sickness good sport   Fletcher wins Middle Weight also a Fiver.  I get 30/-  Officers win a lot give us nothing only a whiskey each.  Eather wins 2nd Heat</p>
