<p>On Mar 25/16 we went on board the S.S. Manitou; It used to be a heavy task embarking &amp; entraining the horses but now they are all veterans and walk into railway trucks or up ship's gangways and along the horse decks as part of a regular routine.</p>
<p>We pulled out from the wharf at about 5 p.m. and anchored out stream for the night. Our ship load consisted of the 1st Aust. Div. Train about 500 men &amp; 700 horses and a Brigade of Howitzer Artillery another 500 men.</p>
