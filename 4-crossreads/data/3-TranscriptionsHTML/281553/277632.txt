<p>13th<br/>Issued with Blkts. &amp; [indecipherable] Sheet 7 p.m.  Finished Kittie's letters off &amp; posted at G.P.O. 8 p.m.  Bought Camera midday near Y.M.C.A. Dust &amp; heat awfull.</p>
<p>Coy. for duty fatigues taken but remainder doing nothing, lounging in shade till 9 a.m.  Bkft. 9-15.</p>
<p>[Comment in margin.]<br/>May 12th on opp. Page.</p>
<p>Entrained 1-40 p.m. Suez, detrained 8-30 Heliopolis, arrived Camp about 10 p.m., retired about 10-30 to 11 p.m. in Mess Sheds as we were where we lived for several days.  Put tents up on Thursday &amp; occupied them same night, 24 men to each.  Camera June 14th.</p>
<p>June 19<br/>Spent afternoon in Heliopolis [indecipherable]</p>
