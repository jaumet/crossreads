<p>close by stands the native village commanding a glorious expansive view over the desert &amp; the lowlands away to the Judean Hills. The colors are wonderful, the whole country is a blaze of beautiful tints. The distant delicate blue mists that veils the hills the brilliant blue of the sky. The emerald of the prickly pear hedges the brown sands, &amp; the gorgeous colors in which the people are dressed.</p>
