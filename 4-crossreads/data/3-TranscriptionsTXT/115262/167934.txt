 Saturday 16 June 1917. 
 Still in Red Sea and a very hot day but breeze is fairly strong making all the difference. 
 A number of boats seen today&nbsp;Most of them going in direction of Aden. 
 No land yet today at 3.30 pm. Several shoals of &quot;dolfins&quot; seen right against boat. Mail steamer passed at 3 p.m. 
 Sickness on boat decreasing 
 Wireless news of last air raid on London published today. 397 killed &amp; 439 injured. (mostly women &amp; children.).
