January 3rd For the morning physical exercises, bayonet fighting, bombing and drill generally we were detached from our own officers &amp; placed under special instructors &amp; from then on till January 15 when we had four days leave in London we had a steady consistent training &amp; the majority of us were well fitted to spread our legs over London.
