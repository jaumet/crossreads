<p>I reckon it served him right, he ought to have known better, &amp; to make it worse just as the Colonel was passing he "opened a bottle of champagne &amp; it fizzed &amp; bubbled all over the place, &amp; I suppose the Colonel was thirsty &amp; this narked him.<br/>Shortly after we lobbed in Camp old Fritz brought down 2 of our balloons we could see them come down in a big flaming mass miles away.<br/>Dead funny today with our blankets, it is a rule in the Battn when we roll our blankets to put 10 in each bundle &amp; no more, but a lot of boy's roll their overcoats sheepskins, sheets &amp; etc with the blankets to save carrying them, but tonight instead of issuing the bundles to the platoons, the Q.M. had them all taken into his tent &amp;</p>
