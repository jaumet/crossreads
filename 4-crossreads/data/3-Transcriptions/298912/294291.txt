<p>Hop &amp; I then did some pricing &amp; we all then adjourned to the British India for more refreshments. Hop &amp; I then left the others &amp; went down town to buy some curios. We visited several ships where curios are stocked in endless variety but at amazing prices, that will soon sink to a much lower level. Some shops had some good stuff &amp; others very bad .</p>
<p>At last we came to a decent place .Immediately we slowed down in front, two natives of somewhat higher education besieged us with "Come inside" Come inside.
