
Septembre 15Left Ebblinghem at 1 p.m. & marched a little beyond Steenvoorde a distance of about 14 1/2 miles - reaching billets about 6.30. The roads are formed of smooth cobbles the whole of the way & the marching was very trying - everybody suffering with their feet.
Septembre 16Left billet at about 1 p.m. and marched to a point about 4 K. from Popperighe marched about 6 miles. A good many mens' feet are breaking down and mine are badly blistered - the hard roads accounting for the damage.
