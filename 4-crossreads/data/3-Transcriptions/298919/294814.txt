<p>After coming out we lost our bearings, so had to take a taxi.  We motored to the Imperial where we tidied up and had dinner.  We then went to the Globe theatre where "Peg o my heart" was played with Miss Laurette Taylor in the principal role.  The play was splendid the most delightful humour emanating from the lips of this unsophisticated Irish-American maiden.  Tubed to Mark Lane which is the Fenchurch st stopping place and entrained for the Ship.  The streets of London do not present a very animated scene by nightime, a lot of lights being doused and all lights shaded.  Motors show no flashing headlights and all shops are enveloped in darkness owing to Zeppelin scares.</p>
