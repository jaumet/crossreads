<p>23/4/18 &ndash; Lavieville<br/>It was the early hours of the morning before we could consider ourselves settled down and as it was getting on towards "stand to" I did not go to bed at all.  Dawn broke late and slowly low dark clouds and a misty rain.  On our left the English put down a heavy barrage early to counter any attempt at counterattack.  As soon as it became apparent that the Hun was not going to attack I went to sleep and continued so until awakened by General Paton.
