The days are frosty and misty with fitful bursts of sunshine and the familiar colourless skies of 1916 and 17 Now the war is about quite finished and the fighting stopped, the papers have come down to stale old political news. All the politicians are 'pushing their frames in' for a good job. It is generally believed that Lloyd George is just about 'set' for another long run  run  in his position.
