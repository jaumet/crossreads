
June 1918Sunday 16
Battery W.Lines.  Pont NoyellesVery peculiar weather prevails at present. Our experience here today was quite a typical tropical one.Did usual shift on phone. 6-8 - and thennoon, thoroughly cleaned my saddle etc.During afternoon Battery sports was held, my sub carriedthe highest points for the day, winning the tug-o-war onfoot and horseback. I was in both teams. They also won the Rescue race, high jump, and Bendingrace. Received a few letters one from Home.
