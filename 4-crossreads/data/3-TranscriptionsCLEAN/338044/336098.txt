  
 Islands are frequent  &  lighthouses plentiful. Pass a large boat about 8 pm. Very hot night 
 Oct 27th :- Frightfully hot to-day   Don't like too much of the Red Sea. Am still on duty, dressing in Dispensary. Worst day of trip  No land in sight. Passed sloop early in morning 
 Oct 28th Not so hot to-day. Still in Red Sea. We passed several vessels to-day, but still out of sight of land. 
 Oct 29th Great excitement on board, as we are nearing the end of our voyage. Sighted land about 3 pm. Much cooler &  we are running close to shore all night. a very brilliant spectacle as the moon rises, showing land on both sides
