 There will not be any mail for me for a considerable time I imagine for when I do move off, it will probably be in a new unit. "Sniper" Welchman of the 3rd is here &amp; also Brokhoff of the Queensland mob.  He transferred to Base detail from No. 2 Stationary. Today we have had a bad sandstorm (or "bad" to us).  The hot wind blows the sand all over one &amp; the flies are simply cruel. At night one is plagued by mosquitoes. Its all in the game however.
