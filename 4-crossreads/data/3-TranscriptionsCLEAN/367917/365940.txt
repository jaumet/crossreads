
Belgium. 26.12.18.
Dear Judge Ferguson -Thank you very much indeed for the parcel you were so good as to send me for Xmas - the cigarettes and sweets were most acceptable.
We are at present in a small village called Chastres about 14 Kilos South of Charleroi - that is B Company are here - the remaining three companies are in Pry about two Kilos from here - we are very comfortable and had a very good Xmas - much different to the past three - the first of which was on Lemnos Island - the next at Fleurs and the last one in the line near Armentieres.
