 Friday 19th February 1917 LONDON Disembarkation leave started at 6am. Left camp about 7.30 am marched to Amesbury arr. there 8.30am train to London&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;changed trains at Salisbury. Arr London (Waterloo) 12 noon. Marched to Horseferry Rd. and paid up to the end of our leave then dismissed. Spent the day in London and caught the Flying Scotsman at 10pm from Kings Cross to Edinburgh. Rec.3 pounds 4 shillings at Horseferry Rd.
