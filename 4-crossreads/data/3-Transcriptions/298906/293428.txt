<p>[Note : 1st June 1917]<br/>30th. Lectures up till 4 oclock &amp; then we had the day off, which never meant much for it took us a good while to write our lectures up.<br/>31st This morning I am platoon commander for 4 days have to be pretty slick for we do things in style down here.<br/>Lectures all day today will take us till late tonight to catch up, we have fine times here treated like gentlemen &amp; have girls to wait at our tables &amp; 'orderlies" to make our beds for us we do nothing in that line, officers &amp; men are practically on a level footing &amp; they treat us as such.<br/>1st June. Lectures &amp; live bombing today each man threw 2 live bombs &amp; fired 1 rifle grenade</p>
