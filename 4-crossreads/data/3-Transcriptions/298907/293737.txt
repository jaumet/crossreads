<p>10th.  Very misty looking this morning but shows signs of clearing up.</p>
<p>There is a great argument going on in the next room between an Aussie &amp; some Tommies over the Irish question that everlasting topic that never seems to get any nearer being settled downstairs someone is playing My home in Tennesee", &amp; among all this you can hear the smack of the billiard balls, such is the average morning in our little quarter of the Hall.</p>
