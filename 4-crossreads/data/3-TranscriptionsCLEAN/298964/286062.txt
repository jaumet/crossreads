  
 8 Th Prisoners by the hundreds also Battery of Guns  millions of rounds of ammunition  Shells by the stacks   Advanced about 4 miles during the day Jacko giving us plenty of shells 
 9 Fri Still in pursuit of Jacko who is burning as he goes  Water is scarce at present  C. Troop position with plenty of water  Casualties not too heavy 
 10 Sat Still after the Brown chap who is going for his life  we are holding about 17 miles of Beersheba Jerusalem Railway  Very big post in Turks lines  more stores being burnt  Lack of men [indecipherable] more prisoners
