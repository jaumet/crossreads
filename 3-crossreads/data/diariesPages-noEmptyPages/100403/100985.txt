April.
27th. March out this evening to report to London. Sleep at War Chest Club. Wonderful weather as late as to-day, London had six inches of snow.
28th. Join "China" at Albert Docks, this afternoon went to London. We will be very comfortable on this boat are due to arrive in Sydney on 12th June.
29th. Leave docks wend our way down the Thames. We are now in Channel opp Dover, en route for Plymouth.
