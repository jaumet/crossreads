<p>Men are complaining about the food as indeed it is far from satisfactory.</p>
<p>Arrived at Colombo 13th Jan 1915. The coolies met us in their dingies, sold us fruit and cigarettes, and dived after silver coins.</p>
<p>Colombo harbour rather pretty but we are not permitted to land &amp; so cannot judge the quality of the city. Some of our men climbed down the ship's side after dark &amp; escaped to town. They were arrested &amp; received heavy punishment.</p>
