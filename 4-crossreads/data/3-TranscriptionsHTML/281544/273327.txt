<p>foggy he came down pretty low &amp; then cleared out. After handing in my teeth for repairs I then returned to Camp. I had to go on duty at Midnight &amp; had a fairly quite night, although Fritz was putting shells over now &amp; again, I don't think he did much harm, one blew up a lot of timber in the R. E Yard &amp; one passed over very close to us &amp; exploded in the Wood, it gave the shunters &amp; ourselves a bit of a Start but we soon got over it. After having a sleep all day Wednesday 3rd I &amp; my chum had Tea early &amp; left for Town at 5 Pm to go to the Variety Show in the Tivoli Theatre, on the way in we passed a very long string of Artillery &amp; their Wagons they were over a Mile long all going up towards our Front. We arrived at the Theatre just in time to get in as it was about full up &amp; we managed to squeeze in &amp; saw a splendid troupe of Artists, in fact the best I have seen in Poperinghe up to date, it is grand for one</p>
