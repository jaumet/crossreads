<p>Tuesday &amp; Wednesday<br/>Captured two submarines in nets</p>
<p>Thursday<br/>25,000 Turks arrive more sport<br/>Friday June 4th<br/>severe bombardment by Warships we fire on Voltures kill three, fresh meat for tea</p>
<p>Sunday<br/>Goliath sunk using Mauser all night<br/>Church service<br/>Riot amongst Australian and Tennese crew Australia wins</p>
<p>Wednesday<br/>E11 sinks 10 Turk boats to date maggots in N.Z. trenches, sig arrested Monitor arrives</p>
<p>Friday<br/>Heavy machine gun bombardment star shells used</p>
<p>Saturday 12th<br/>Turks give us heavy bombardment. two men killed, layed up with fumes, throw bomb to wake men up</p>
<p>Sunday<br/>Our warships bombard gun displacement and incoming Turkish troops for one hour</p>
<p>Tuesday 14<br/>Drilling reinforcements under heavy schrapnel fire, men had no training, Monitor fires on shore Aeroplanes about<br/>Wednesday<br/>Turks shell our trenches, sawtor shot throug head, 35,000 troops land Cape Helles</p>
