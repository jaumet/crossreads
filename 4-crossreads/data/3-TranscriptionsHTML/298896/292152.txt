Very interesting with a happy view right through &amp; strongly for Australia. Dad's letters more serious, gives political news with criticisms, religiously answers everything mentioned in my letters &amp; instructive on matters in Australia. Mother's letters between herself &amp; son, my absence &amp; danger absorbing her thoughts with occasional mention of local people &amp; doings. Fan's letters specialised on her family. Between the four I get an excellent mail &amp; very regular, never having any complaints in that direction. Just had word that 2 or our bearers have been killed, a shell getting them in an R.A.P.</p>
<p>Wednesday 28th Aug.<br/>Very quiet. Rain at intervals. Advance still continues &amp; the hun is rapidly falling back. Since middle of July we are reported to have captured over 100000 prisoners &amp; 1800 guns which must go a long way towards wiping out our earlier defeats.
