<p>9.10.16  Monday</p>
<p>The rain has given over &amp; dry weather once again reign supreme, although one always know that it is not for long. In fact the seasons are known here as being 3 months winter &amp; 9 months wet weather.</p>
<p>I went into Balleiul in France today on a bicycle. The distance was about 24 kilometres &amp; am feeling fairly stiff on it. This is a fair sized city &amp; at one time has been a pretty lively place. Its business side is now all military on account of it being a railway centre.  It is where we entrained when going down to the Somme from Armentieres. Its chief points de advantage are now mainly "high prices &amp; fever". Some malady has broken out here with the result that all Estammets &amp; public places are closed &amp; out of bounds to troops whilst the only available shops have seized the opportunity to raise their prices.
