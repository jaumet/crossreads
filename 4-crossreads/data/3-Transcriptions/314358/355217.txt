<p>18<br/>cookers and 15 wounded.</p>
<p>As the party of sappers work the "Knoor-up" of shrapnel and burst of high explosive tare the sky to ribbands and pound and smash anew the soil already in blackened ragged holes and ridges.</p>
<p>The party is under a charm this day, which is unbroken by disaster, but almost there is a stream of wounded passing down the sap from the front where shells and shrapnel and machine guns make Hell on earth.  Streatcher bearers tend them.</p>
<p>Detail of this poor blood stained procession would serve no end, nor would detail of what is seen in the trenches and over</p>
