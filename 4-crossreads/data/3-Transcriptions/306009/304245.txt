<p>1915<br/>Feb. 28 Sun.<br/>We are not to take any more patients in &ndash; getting them out in large numbers.<br/>In the afternoon 11 S.Ms, Scouts, &amp; ex-Scouts from various Units met on the sandhills with the Great Pyramid as a background &amp; had photos taken.  Spent the rest of the afternoon with V. Marshal &amp; R.A. Doyle.<br/>Was over at the Melbourne Y.M.C.A. after tea.<br/>3rd Brigade of 1st Division struck camp &amp; marched out from Mena about 5.30 p.m. destination unknown. The 1st &amp; 2nd Brigades are very busy getting ready for a move &ndash; likewise ourselves.</p>
