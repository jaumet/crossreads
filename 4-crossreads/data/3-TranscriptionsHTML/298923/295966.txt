<p>27 October 1918 Sunday<br/>Loveridge not available  [indecipherable]<br/>No church but busy packing up to help Dolly when I'm not here.  Getting stronger every day but had a bad night with Neuritis in my leg, had to get up &amp; have a hot bath.  I enclose Photo of Tom Kidney at the Pyramids.</p>
