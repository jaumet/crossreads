<p><br/>1916 January 20<br/>Thursday</p>
<p><u>Red letter day</u>  Picked in the 15th of 1st F.A. reinforcements.  After being in camp since Aug 4  Rob in it too  Tony sees his wife &amp; baernies off to England.<br/>Willy White receives news of his brother's death at the Front Aug. 8th.</p>
<p>Mrs Wheatley &amp; MacKenzie up  show trenches  see them to train.</p>
<p>Go to Hewletts &amp; MacTavishes &amp; tell news re reinforcements</p>
