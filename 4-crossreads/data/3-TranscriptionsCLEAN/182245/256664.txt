MAY 1917
29 TUESDAY
Fine & cool. I went before
Gen Grimwade in morning
with six others re St John's Wood
but he did not give us any
information. Went round by
Bailleul after & saw some
old TKS boys - Lionel Bigg
& one of the Moor's. Poss Nivison
was attached to the 26th
Battery during the day. I came
up to the pits after dinner again
Building all day.
