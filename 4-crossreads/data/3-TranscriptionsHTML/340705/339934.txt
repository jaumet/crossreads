<p>Sarpi Rest Camp,<br/>West Mudros,<br/>Lemnos.</p>
<p>Sept. 28th '15</p>
<p>Dear old Mick,</p>
<p>Very pleased to get yours of Aug. 4th &amp; Aug. 9th.  Many thanks for all the news &amp; the mementos of Australia Day.  It appears that everyone lost their heads, more or less, that day and the succeeding days, &amp; from the speeches of many bum politicians, I judge that Australia is perhaps unintentionally so, getting swelled head over the military achievements of her boys &ndash; in other words, the first signs of jingoism are beginning to show themselves.  Don't become a military maniac, Mick.  War is not all champagne and fifteen course dinners &ndash; there's to much Army tea and bully beef attached to it.</p>
<p>Sept. 30th<br/>(Got a little ink to-day.)  The mail has much improved, and I now get my letters &amp; papers quite regularly, thus managing to keep well in touch with Sydney and its doings.  Thanks for the little Australia Day book.  Ashemead [Ashmead] Bartlett is some correspondent isn't he?  Bean, in my opinion is not a war correspondent's bum.</p>
