<p>with 200 rounds of ammo. Rode about forty miles looking for enemy supposed to be in vicinety, found their camp &amp; burnt most of their tents &amp; chattels. took several prisoners. most of the fighting force had Imshied as fast as their camels &amp; donks could carry them, They light fires to warn each other when we are coming, We arrived back in camp at 7 pm.</p>
<p>15th/ Up at 5 am. on the road for next well, took many poor harmless prisoners en route, of course they may have been spies,</p>
