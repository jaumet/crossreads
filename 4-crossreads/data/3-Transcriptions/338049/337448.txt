<p>Monday 26 August<br/>Up as usual but suddenly advised 8 am. that we were moving immediately.</p>
<p>Packed &amp; moved out in no time via Sailly-le-Sec &ndash; Laurete, Chipilly to Etinehem. One Detachment left <strike>at</strike> behind.</p>
<p>Pulled into Div H.Q. three wagons ordered immediately. Was able to have dinner first. Ran line from 11 Bde to 10 Bde roads harassingly shelled so had to try a different route. Got to 10th alright at Rly Stn Bray. Line not O.K. Returned to 11th repaired incorrect joint.</p>
<p>Home to horselines on Chipilly Rd. Well tired. Got knocked on back of knucles only scratched.</p>
