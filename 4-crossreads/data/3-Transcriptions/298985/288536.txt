<p>Sunday 18th<br/>Had a very quiet night. A little rain this morning. The dugout is very comfortable &amp; warm. Some Indian troops passed yesterday they look well on horseback.</p>
<p>Monday 19th<br/>Did a little Drill this morning. Swiming Carnival in afternoon. Too cold for me.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20th<br/>Dull in morning. At least we are supposed to drill. We do a few movements &amp; then stand about for some time. The Sgts don't want to do anything</p>
