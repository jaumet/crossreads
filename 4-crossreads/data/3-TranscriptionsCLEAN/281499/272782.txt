It was the newer reinforcements first baptism of fire & it frightened the life out of some of them while others seemed to enjoy it like a huge joke, however we got it finished about 11oclock & just as we were knocking off the Major & Capt came around to have a look at how things were getting on, & they reckoned I had made a good job of it & were well pleased, the Capt said we need not "stand to" this morning so we had an extra hour in bed.
