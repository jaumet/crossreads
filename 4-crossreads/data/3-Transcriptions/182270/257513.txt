
<p>SUNDAY, JULY 20, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Peter &amp; I &amp; Ham went over to</p>
<p>Coomoo Coomoo in big car. Called</p>
<p>in at Merrilong on the way &amp; picked</p>
<p>up old Billy Dick. Played tennis</p>
<p>all the afternoon. The Trails from</p>
<p>Colly Blue were there too. We</p>
<p>stayed at Coomoo for dinner &amp;</p>
<p>came back after.</p>
