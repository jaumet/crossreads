
<p>11</p>
<p>1916&nbsp;&nbsp; SUNDAY 21&nbsp;&nbsp; MAY</p>
<p>A decent calm fine day the first since we left. Still in hospital &amp; got a fresh cold on me. There was a church parade in the morning for all denominations and there was a kind of a hymn service in evening. Billy Hunter Warren Hogarth &amp; Sarg. Macdougal were in a lot of the day yarning.</p>
