<p>September 1918.<br/>Monday 23</p>
<p>Ahead of La Vigure. Bty Position<br/>Weather wet, but getting a little better. Our twin on Bdge O.P. found the crossing to "Line" through barrages, our line became a casualty in many places. Georgie Baker knocked in temple, just a Blighty only.<br/>Have been refused the promised leave to Aussie don't feel very happy about the matter. Evidently the bosses consider that sole support is not a good enough reason.</p>
