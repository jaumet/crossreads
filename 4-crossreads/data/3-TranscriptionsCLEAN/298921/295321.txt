
28 December Tuesday 1916working hard reorganizing a lot to be done - Recd cards from Mrs Cotton & othersPlease Mother write to D. Ish.  I send you some of her letters she is dear kid & thinks or may I say loves me I know. I told her to send you her Photo. Her address Brooklands  Hotel Brooklands [indecipherable] but they are talking a house again.More letters delivered of Mothers & Day Oct 10th - Aunt Louise Oct 16 everything is turning up from the 4 corners of the MediterraneanTop hole waistcoat but lovely weather here after Anzac
