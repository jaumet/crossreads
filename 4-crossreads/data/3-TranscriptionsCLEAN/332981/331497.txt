 And with a characteristic tenderness for the conquered he engaged in conversation on other topics with Lee. 
 At Pardeberg in 1900, General Cronje made his surrender in person to Lord Roberts, after his stubborn and gallant resistance. In honour to the defeated Boer leader, Lord Roberts wore for the first time since he opened his campaign his ceremonial sword, "a heavy sword with a jewelled hilt" and received his opponent with the words, "I am glad to meet so brave a Man".
