<p>on our right flank. after about four or five days here we again advance to our left flank just outside the village of Morchise &amp; on the right of "Lagnicourt" with "Bullecourt" on our left front. here our battery remains till we pull out of this front. practically all of this advance has been left to the Aus troops. the remaining days of March &amp; beginning of April has consisted mainly of minor events Fritz being afraid to put a decent stand against these big Australians, but towards the end of April, we having forced him back to the great "Hindenburg" line.</p>
