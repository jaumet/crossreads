<p>9.  <u> Further Change of Camp.</u>  (Mar. 6th)<br/>Ferry Post to Moascar:-<br/>A strenuous march owing to hot weather &amp; amount of luggage- Camp near Railway Line- All hope of joining 1st. Fld. Amb has vanished-<br/>We become attached to 7th. F. A. &ndash; Queenslanders &amp; S. Australians-  Good food- O.C. Fair- Plenty of toil- Boys downhearted- Big movement apparent, judging by number of troops in the vicinity of Moascar-</p>
