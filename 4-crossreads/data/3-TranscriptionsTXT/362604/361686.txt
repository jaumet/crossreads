 Y.M.C.A. with the Australian Imperial Force Bulford 30/6/18 
 Dear Mum, Dad &amp; George 
 Before I go on to answer Mum's letter of 21.4.18 which we have just received, I'll relate just such few experiences that we have had during the week.  I have been trainin' lately &amp; were you about you would hear the slapping of legs as of yore.  I have not been able to procure a pair of spikes &amp; it is hard work with only rubber shoes.  On Wednesday I put on a long tailed flannel shirt under my other togs to sweat.  I went out &amp; ran a lap round the oval &amp; forgot that I had not tucked my shirt in.
