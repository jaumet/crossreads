 31 May 1918 Friday Left Dinton about 9.30 &amp; got to London about midday. Did some business went to see Mr Smith at the office and later went to Leytonstone to see Mrs Smith &amp; Daisy. Stayed with the Smiths for the evening &amp; left for Glasgow at 11.45 P.M. 
 1918 June 1 Saturday Arrived Glasgow about 9.45 a.m. Had a clean up &amp; did a bit of business &amp; then went to the office to see my friend Mary Boyd When I got there found that she and her two sisters a friend had a trip arranged to Gourock and expected me to go too so away I went nothing loth. Got back to Glasgow about 9.45 P.M. after an enjoyable day. The girls too enjoyed themselves thoroughly. Very warm weather. 
 2 June 1918 Sunday
