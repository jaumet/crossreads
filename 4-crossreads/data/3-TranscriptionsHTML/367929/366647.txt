<p>July 1915<br/>2 Fri<br/>On Sicilia. The wind made the beach landing too dangerous and the bringing  of wounded on to Sicilia too difficult. She was to go to Lemnos, but they could not land me nor transfer me to the Neuralia.</p>
<p>3 Sat<br/>Arrival Lemnos 40 miles away. Huge Brit and Fr navies in [indecipherable]. After midday moved into the port and was landed at 5.30 pm.to No 1 Hosp, No food till 10 to 10 pm. Lt Green of Inniskilling Fusiliers ,Ealing London</p>
