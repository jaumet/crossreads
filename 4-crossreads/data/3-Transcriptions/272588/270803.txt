<p>Monday 18 November<br/>Fearfully cold to-day and our first glimpse of snow.  Very little however.<br/>Another evening at the Pedlars who are very good indeed. Great preparations are being made for this march to Germany.</p>
<p>Tuesday 19 November<br/>Nothing doing, still at Busigny, where it is very cold.</p>
