Just as we were falling in this morning down dashes the Orderly Sgt; &amp; wants to know where I am he had an urgent message in his hand &amp; I wondered what was up now, it turned out to be from the Battalion wanting to know if I had warned McCarthy the day when I got him from Bailleul Compound, that he was going to the line the following morning, they wanted an answer at once so I had to make a statement &amp; get it signed by an officer looks as if he has done a bunk again this will be the 3rd time.
