<p>near done &amp; there were scores more the same way, but the worst part was yet to come, &amp; that was when we were going up the "communication trench" to the firing line, for the trench was that narrow &amp; at intervals of a yard there were wooden uprights to hold the sides in place &amp; on these uprights our packs used to catch, talk about pull, struggle, &amp; swear well you should have heard us I think this sort of thing is about the most aggravating thing we have to put up with &amp; no one likes the idea of shifting about with packs up in the trenches, well at last we arrived at our posts, &amp; proceeded to take over from the 6th.</p>
