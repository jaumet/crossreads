<p>March 1917<br/>12th   Fritz has bombarding this vicinity very heavily all day to-day &amp; yesterday, &amp; has accounted for a large casualty list.<br/>At 4.20 P.M. our artillery opened up a heavy barrage on Fritz's front line &amp; supports, lasting about an hour, but he retaliated soon after we had started &amp; kept a rain of shells constantly between "Needle" &amp; "Blighty" trenches.<br/>We have a large amount of material here &amp; about half a dozen batteries are immediately behind it<br/>He kept up the shelling nearly all night &amp; we were handicapped considerably <strike>with</strike> in carrying material &amp; stores forward.<br/>At about 6 P.M. two of the carrying party were killed &amp; 6 wounded.</p>
