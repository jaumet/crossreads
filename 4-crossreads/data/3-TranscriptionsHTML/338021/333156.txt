<p>To Capt Duchatel<br/>Herewith correspondence that arrived here during the night for you and as some is marked urgent I am sending it out with one of my men to ensure safe delivery.</p>
<p>Everything is alright here this morning no casualties. There are some cigs &amp; chocolate here for you which will be returned later.</p>
<p>CHP Weston Lt  10A.M. 30/9/17</p>
