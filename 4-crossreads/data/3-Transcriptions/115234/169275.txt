<p>Arrived in Havre on Dec 6<sup><u>th</u></sup> - Humped our packs to Base, five miles out.&nbsp; That night went into City &amp; dipped our nose in foaming beakers &amp; gazed on the wine while it was red:&nbsp; Everything gay &amp; bustling&nbsp; The French, unlike the English, do no take their pleasures sadly.&nbsp; We had a bon time among the sprightly Mame Belles:&nbsp; on Dec 8<sup>th</sup> we started</p>
