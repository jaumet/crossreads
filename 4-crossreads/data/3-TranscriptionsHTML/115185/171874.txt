<p>13 &ndash; that he gave me &ndash; has been a constant comfort. I forgot that in my summary. I wrap it round me to sleep in and it keeps the prickly grass seeds etc from my skin.<br/>Mrs Dick who is going out to Australia will take this letter to you &amp; post the copy to Jocelyn &amp; Rosamond in England who will show it the Grandmother &amp; then post it to Aunt Maria &ndash; who will if possible post it to Gibson. I may write other letters but not another about these first days. I shall try to forget them.<br/>Much love to you all. I think of you all &amp; long to be home X Devotedly your daughter Betha McMillan<br/>X But I want to stick to it till the war is over.</p>
