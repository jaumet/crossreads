<p>last five days and nights it has been one continual frost and is still going strong.</p>
<p>One would think the ground telephone wires &amp; trees had undergone a heavy fall of snow, but such is not the case, it is frost.</p>
<p>On looking around on either side it presents quite a pretty picture, BUT also a COLD one.</p>
<p>Poor feet and hands especially sitting up all night on duty as I am at present, from 8PM to 9AM. Oh MY! I don't want to die. I want to go Home. And yet they say "cold feet" can be had in Australia</p>
