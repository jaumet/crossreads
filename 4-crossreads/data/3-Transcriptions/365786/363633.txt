Ancient writers mention of mouths to the Nile, but now practically only two channels discharge water into the sea &ndash; namely the Damietta &amp; Rosetta branches.  The huge barrage erected <strike>at the commencement of these two</strike> where the Nile divides into these two arms is a wonderful work &amp; the beautiful botanical gardens at the parting of the ways are well worth a visit.
