 August,1918 Sunday 11 
 In front of Harboniers, Bty. Pos. [indecipherable] Weather warm and bright. Battery had a duty shoot at 9 am this morning  - preparation for 1st Bdgs attack. Left with Smithy for the Waggon Lines and picked up a transportat Bayonville [indecipherable] as far as Villers Brettoneaux and thence lorry to Daours eventually arriving at Bussy when we had an excellent lunch consisting of skats and eggs. Returning to our W.L.  which were fairly close to Home we had a sumptuous  supper of bread and jam. Had so few visits from the [indecipherable] little Fritz.
