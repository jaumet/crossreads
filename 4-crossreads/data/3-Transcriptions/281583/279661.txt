<p>More promotions have come to light, but as usual they all belong to the family that runs them. &amp; then they ask one to vote for compulsory enlistment, I know what my vote will be like.</p>
<p>2.19.16  Monday</p>
<p>Eight hour day in Sydney &amp; a holliday . But it is no leisure day here. It has again started to rain &amp; looks like setting in windy the mud is fairly deep.</p>
<p>I went to Boschip this morning &amp; it was a very cold side as well as being on the miserable side. The guns are fairly active just now but I think it is only a reciprocal bombardment.</p>
<p>I was thinking of home today &amp; the longing to be back is great, especially as the Head of our Unit is making himself felt in every way possible.</p>
<p>3.10.16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Another rainy day with nothing to do &amp; plenty of time to do it in.</p>
