<p>we have a great big shower rigged up with cold spring water running through it and it is great after you come it from a long ride through the dust here. I have not been issued with a new horse yet. For<s>t</s> the treck down here we were lent horses by another regt, and of course they were not their best horses! The best description I can give you of the one I got is a description I heard an interpriter once give of a horse. He was not much of a rider so he asked for a quiet horse, and he got one. At the other end of the first day&#39;s treck when the interpriter was just about knocked up with having to work his passage all the way along, they asked him if his horse was quiet enough</p>
