
Thursday 29 AugustHad tea on arrival home. Slept in until about 10.30 am breakfast being brought into the tent. Had an easy morning.
Move ordered immediately after dinner for 2 pm. Move via Bray, Cappy to Suzanne & camped near Chateau. Made ourselves comfortable.
Then I went in box car to Curlu to take cable forward to Page. Shelling heavy. Great ride.
