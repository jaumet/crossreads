I was there 4 weeks & during that time got steadily worse - so now they are sending me to England - the voyage alone ought to put me right - & I hope to be back again by the end of this month. If they had only sent me to England straight away, instead of keeping me in Alexandria, I should have been fit my now, & back again with my Regiment, or what is left of it. First of all to answer the questions in your letter, & then to send you such news as I can.
