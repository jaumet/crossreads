  
 March, 1915 Wednesday 31 Squadrons go to the [indecipherable] Barrage to learn to swim horses. I hope to go later Luxor post-poned on eve may go any time took Papers & Chocolates to Myra Nyse in Hospital she is getting on A.1. & will soon be fit again. My knowledge of Perspective & Drawing is coming in very handy in preparing outpost skeches Reconnaisssance, all boys might be taught general principals of sketching - I wish I had paid more attention sometimes to all you could have taught me.
