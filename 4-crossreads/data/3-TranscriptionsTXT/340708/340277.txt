August 1917Tuesday 14thThis is a crook place to be sick &amp; I will have about a week of it.  The weather is still bad but they are advancing on parts of the frontWednesday 15th.Fritz made a big raid early this morning we brought two downThursday 16th.A few shells dropping in the town &amp; I believe he gave Hazebrouck another sally up   they have decided to shift the hospital   I don't think any of us care how soon we get out of this dirty hungry gutted show   The Tommies are a filthy crowdFriday 17thWe had a great sight this evening.  Fritz came over in force for an air raid he dropped a few bombs &amp; our search light picked him up &amp; brought one down &amp; of air brought another down
