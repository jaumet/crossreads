Wednesday 8th MayFell in twice but did nothing
Thursday 9th to Sunday 12th MayHand dressed every day. Beautiful weather. Doing nothing but reading &amp; loafing about
Monday 13th MayNothing as usual Wet day again
Tuesday 14th MayWas marked B.A. today as my hand is quite healed. Walked to Forant &amp; back this evening about 4 miles longest walk I have had for a long time
