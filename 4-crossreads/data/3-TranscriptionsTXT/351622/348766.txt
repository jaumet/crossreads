 15 January 1916 Sat Physical-drill before breakfast. Morning off Scotty Thompson cuts my hair. Big review in the afternoon inspected by General Murray. A great sight bands playing. Vere came to see me in the night &amp; we went out to-gether. Won &amp;pound 4. C&amp;A. 
 16 January 1916 Sun Compulsory church-parade. Ken sings very well. Met Horace Birchers cousin. Cockie came from Cairo to see me. A great meeting. Spent the day with him talking about old times &amp; had our dinner composing half a dozen oranges. Saw Jimmie Frank &amp; Ronnie.
