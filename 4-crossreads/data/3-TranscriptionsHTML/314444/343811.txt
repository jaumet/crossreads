<p>[If] that should be I do hope my little chaps will grow up with a strong sense of patriotism and that they may shew it to the full if ever need be.</p>
<p>Mrs Larkin, in her last letter to hand said she might come to Egypt. How delighted I should be to see her, but for her own comfort I hope she will not make the trip. I might be in France or any where when she arrived here and possibly not have an opportunity of seeing her at all.</p>
<p>Ta ta for the present,<br/>Yours very sincerely,<br/>E. R. Larkin</p>
