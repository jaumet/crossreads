  
 Brunswick St. - Sad partings - a weary train journey to London. Mar. 5th Arrive "Kings' Cross" 7.30 a.m. Train to Crouch End - Return to "Elm Bank" -Home Again. Meet Mr. & Mrs Matthews - Afternoon trip to London on business - a heart to heart talk with Mrs Scales - Correspondence. Mar.6th Feeling ill - Breakfast 11 a.m. - A run into Town - Spend afternoon at Coliseum.
