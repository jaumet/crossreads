After I take an Arabier to find my nephew Ross Donnell who came by the Morea last week, making numerous enquiries &amp; an hours driving in the hot sun &amp; dusty sands  I find him  through different camps- lucky hit- I find him, but as he is on duty can only spare a &frac14; of an hour, never mind I go away happy, to seek Miss Graham &amp; many old nurse friends at Luna Park, arriving in time for afternoon tea &amp; wasn't it lovely to be in the midst of Australians many I knew &amp; some I loved.  Miss Graham invites me to stay to dinner c them &amp; in the meantime wander off to find Leslie Holden, he was out, but he will be able to meet me later, so my luck seems really in.
