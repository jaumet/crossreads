THURSDAY, JANUARY 9, 1919.
Fine. I was knocking round
with Alan all day. Al & I took
two of the nurses from Lady
Carnarvon's hospital to dinner
& then on to "Soldier Boy" after.
It was a jolly good show and
Dot Brunton was very good
indeed. We all went round
to Rector's after to a dance.
