<p>27 August 1918<br/>Tuesday<br/>Just the same old ship routine. Reported things going well on all Fronts "Albert" re-captured &amp; British troops advanced well well forward of that position &ndash; taking fair number prisoners.</p>
<p>1918 August 28<br/>Wednesday<br/>Making good progress in fine weather &amp; favourable Sea Good news still coming through from all Fronts Good advances and many prisoners. Concert at night.</p>
