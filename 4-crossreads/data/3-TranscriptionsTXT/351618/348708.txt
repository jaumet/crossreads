For some reason we did not go &amp; I had to sleep on the Beach. Some of our men &amp; another gun went by way of a Transport. My instructions were to stick to the luggage no matter where it went. 
 Wednesday 15th December It seems a coincidence. I was guard on the baggage leaving Enoggera &amp; Pinkerba in Sept last year &amp; now I am the same on leaving the Peninsula. I went to the Engineers Store for a little Breakfast with Olly Gunderson &amp; had a wash &amp; also had dinner there later on.
