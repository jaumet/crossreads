<p>9 Thursday<br/>Got up 9.45 am worked on plant all day didn't go out at night time.  A fine day</p>
<p>10 Friday<br/>Worked until 4 pm nothing happened in morning went round for Breeches but couldn't get them.  Got leave &amp; went to Sydney with G Handley.  Caught 5.30 pm train nothing doing except walking about the streets. Caught 11.33 pm train home which took 1 hr 40 min.  Slept all the way, sprinkling rain about 12 pm. came up to camp in Bus.  Very tired. Portugal joined allies.</p>
<p>11 Saturday<br/>Bill Garwood came down on holidays.  Worked until 12 noon caught 12.15 pm train to Sydney &amp; 1.20 pm train home went for a ride on Bike in afternoon met Elsie Long &amp; gave her her gloves, also went down to Wirrells with her met Ethel Spowart &amp; Jane, &amp; went to Pictures with them, went round to station &amp; was talking to Jeff Turner, got home at 12 midnight.</p>
<p>12 Sunday<br/>Went for a surf very rough. Went for a walk to Austinmer in afternoon.  Saw Ethel Spowart, but did not talk to her. Caught 7.10 pm train to Sydney &amp; 9.40 to Liverpool<br/>[continued on bottom of previous page]</p>
