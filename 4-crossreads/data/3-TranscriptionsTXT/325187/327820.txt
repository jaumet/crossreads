
[Wednesday 15 March 1916]
I find the cartridges are all rejects of  Clay Bros, all Brands & Powders.  Egyptian Storekeepers are Robbers- I had to get a Permit from the Consul to buy them-Today I shot10 Jack Snipe1 Duck3 Plover3 Big Brown Doves5 Quail& nearly drownded a young native getting the Duck.  Wish I had my good gun this "made in Germany" is only Goopipe & very old & very cheap.Just heard the news re ROUMANIA joining in - Good luck to her   We will all be home by Xmas- you see
