<p>4-4-17<br/>Wed 4th<br/>All men going to the races had to march down under an officer I decided to stay at home as I have a sore foot, the result of playing football in Tennis shoes &amp; getting skin taken off the left ankle. I came on the phone at 12 oclock, &amp; worked on till 6 being the only Sig in camp, a couple of enemy planes were reported from Bir El Abd &amp; Nazar today, but they appear rather afraid of our guns to come too close over El Arish; Some Welsh lads coming through wounded counted the 3rd Regt out a few days  ago, they are a fine lot to count anyone out, as this is</p>
