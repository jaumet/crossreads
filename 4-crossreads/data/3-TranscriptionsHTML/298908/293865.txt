You should see the look of disgust on each mans face as our sailing is put off from week to week.  The latest is that we might get away by the end of next week, that means pretty well a fortnight &amp; this will seem a year to be at least.  I have pretty well given up hope of ever getting away &amp; feel more restless &amp; discontented than ever I can remember before in my life &amp; as each day goes by it only increases.
