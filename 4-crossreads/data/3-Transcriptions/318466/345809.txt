<p>Sunday 14 July 1918<br/>Had a lazy day &amp; read &amp; wrote.  Good Y.M.C.A. run by English ladies, this used to be the Vicarage.</p>
<p>Monday 15 July 1918<br/>Paraded sick about the teeth.  Wandsworth didn't finish.  They tried to put me on draft conductg. but I got out of it!!!</p>
<p>Tuesday 16 July 1918<br/>Plenty of rain.  Usual parades etc.  Fixed up with Dentist for next Monday.  Revolver shootg.</p>
<p>Wednesday 17 July 1918<br/>Think Sutton Veny is one of the best camps I have been in for accommodation.</p>
<p>Thursday 18 July 1918<br/>Went to Salisbury &amp; Fovant.  Saw Matron Lamming.  Major Simpson was out so left a note.</p>
<p>Friday 19 July 1918<br/>Rained all day &amp; we did live bombing in the middle of it!!  Got off early.</p>
<p>Saturday 20 July 1918<br/>Advance Guard stunt in morng.  Went shootg. in afternoon &amp; got ptridge.  marker.<br/>Sister Birt<br/>Ede</p>
