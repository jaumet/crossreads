No146 Cont 10-9-16
Part 1
Para iv
Lights on waggons
Side lights or Tail lights are not permitted east of VLAMERTINCE. Units are responsible for screening the roads in their area. Canvas for the purpose may be obtained on indent.
Para 2
Bounds
The town of POPERINGHE is placed out of bounds for all troops except those billeted there or on duties and provided with a pass.
Part 2
