<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>Ship of Origin - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 14/9/14</p>
<p>Time received or Sent - 7.55</p>
<p>To General</p>
<p>The following message was received from the Governor General of Australia. Begins. The Governor General congratulates the Admiral in Command on the success at Simpsons Haven &amp; desires that his high appreciation of the achievements shall be conveyed to the forces engaged . R.A.M. Furguson. Ends</p>
<p>The following reply to the above has been sent. Begins. Please thank the Governor General of Australia in the name of myself &amp; the force engaged for his kind message which is much appreciated (0747.)</p>
