This evening Jack, Lindsay I went down to the O.T.B. had dinner with Len, there was a great gathering of C. Coys Sgts, out of the 12 1st Battn. Sgts there 8 of them belong to C. Coy. some going isn't it, I should think so. Got a batch of photos this evening from Warminster they were very good.
2nd. Bit of excitement in the Camp today, a certain Sgt. named Willis was put under arrest for supposed stealing a sum of money totalling 16. I was acting Sgt Major at the trial heard all the evidence, I got my own back on our Sgt Major in a quiet way he was a witness in the case, the day before I was one also when he marched me in, he used to do it regimentally, so my chance come this afternoon I took full advantage of it, when he was wanted
