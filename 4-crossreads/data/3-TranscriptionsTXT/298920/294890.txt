 He sometimes makes jokes which afford him great amusement but which fail to kindle even a smile in anyone else.  Pimples and sores are great sources of annoyance to him his chin rarely being free from one or the other.  His forehead and cheeks are deeply lined probably because he is possessed of a worrying nature.  He is engaged to be married to a young lady who lives in Gloucester, &amp; would have married her this time only he did not wish to leave her a widow this early in life, and things looked so unpromising for us on account of the submarine blockade.
