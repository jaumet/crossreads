 Sutton Veny 22 Nov. 1917 
 Dear Mother, 
 Thanks very much for the tin with a cake &amp; some sweets in, I received it today &amp; found it in excellent order, this is the seventh parcel I have received altogether, not too bad is it.  You just should have seen us opening it, there was Ken &amp; a couple of others round me at the time all waiting expectantly to see what was inside &amp; I can assure you we enjoyed it properly.
