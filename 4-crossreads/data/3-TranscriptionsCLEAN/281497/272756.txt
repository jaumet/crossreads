One of the 4th Div Captains took me into their Hqrs to see if he could find where they were but they knew nothing about them, so I left the party there & set out myself to see what information I could get.    I was directed to the other end of the town to the Engineers, but nothing doing there, next I tried the 12th Battalion, they were in Ontario Camp but they knew nothing of our Hqrs.
