<p>23 May 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Hebron Road<br/>Queenslanders wear EMU feathers so do the Anzac Band in London-  A South Australian Colonel of L.H. recently went on leave- to England &ndash; also has [feathers]  An old lady near Regents Hotel asked him- Was he a Q'lander on receiving reply "No" She said "Oh! you are a reinforcement for the BAND."<br/>X Received 2 Australasians March 9th &amp; 23rd &ndash; thank you for posting.<br/>Am Field Officer again tonight, so post this with love to all at home-  Hoping it won't be long ere I see you all again. X</p>
