  
 Belgium 4.8.17 
 Hope you are all well. In Haste 
 My dearest Moth   &  Sisters, 
 I've done some travelling this week, came by car all the way from last C.C.S. on Thursday - Jolly glad to be with our own Unit again. We are twice as many sisters   &  ever so many more Medical Officers.
