<p>September, 1915<br/>Monday 20<br/>In these grounds a very fine leafy flowering tree Golden Mohur Flamboyant, I believe<br/>ComMon in N Q'land will not stand frost well but is most picturesque &amp; should suit the back country &ndash;<br/>Col <strike>Manifold</strike> P.M.O. inspected Hospital &amp; says a fortnight will see me right. I hope so: <strike>Heard today that the L.H. Brigade is knocked on the head, &amp; all available men go back to Gallipoli &ndash;</strike><br/>I will not be fit for some 10 days yet at least.</p>
