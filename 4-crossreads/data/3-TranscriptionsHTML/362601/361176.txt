<p>20 March, Thursday</p>
<p>W. we stop in &amp; have music, singing &amp; play cards all night. In afternoon we go for walk to P.O. &amp; both are very silent. We have further chat on the things &amp; on Botany. In evening M sings &amp; plays quite a lot. I try to turn over music &amp; sing but am not required so I sit down &amp; do nothing. We play Rummy late &amp; I easily run both the V &amp; M out &amp; we go upstairs together.</p>
<p>24 March, Friday</p>
<p>Snow &amp; F.</p>
<p>We go to Bridge &amp; to Douglas. We ride through the snow storm &amp; have 40 minutes wait. Tea at Calm Caf&eacute; &amp; we are taken for honeymooners everywhere, we go to Gran's by Broadway &amp; have tea. I go to Mrs Grundys. We have nice chat. We buy cakes etc. nice ride home in Train warm &amp; comfy although we are tired. We walk home from Bridge in the Dark.</p>
