<p>Thursday 4th April<br/>Hand dressed again first time since Sat</p>
<p>Friday 5th April<br/>Fresh paking put in wond Inoculated again Very comfy here</p>
<p>Saturday 6th April<br/>Packing taken out of wond. Heard I was for Blighty</p>
<p>Sunday 7th April<br/>A very fine day. This ward is filling up again mostly Aussies coming in</p>
<p>Monday 8th April<br/>Up at 5 a cup</p>
