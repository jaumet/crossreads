<p>Tuesday 17 October 1916<br/>Went to Museum then round to tin smiths and got rings.<br/>Kursol in evening and home early.</p>
<p>Wednesday 18 October 1916<br/>Swim first thing.  Dick &amp; I went into town then out to Nouzha Gardens.  Picture show in evening then dinner and Concert at Alhambra.<br/>Did not get back to camp till after one a.m.</p>
