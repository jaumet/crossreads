Preparations for furlough.  For first time we have to pay for Railway Tickets.  "An injustice or not"- Army masqueraders - and a "camp robbery".  Meet Mrs Wighram of Manor House and give a few items.  The village of Sutton and the Cemetery.
April 11th
Reveille at 5 a.m.  Leave camp at 7 a.m. for Warminster
