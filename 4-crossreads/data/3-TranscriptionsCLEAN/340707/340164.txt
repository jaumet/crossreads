  
 Hotel De L'Yvonne near the rue a [Brossac] where Mathilde the friend of an American officer lives. 
 22-6-19 (Sun) This is the day that I have received the invite to M. Edouard Dolleans Rue Marbeau. He is a professor of the Law School & has some political pull He is the centre of a very nice social circle & I enjoy myself very much. Remaining dancing till nearly 8 pm I meet a M. Truechi a Professor of Law school & he thinks it is hopeless for me to get a law degree or official recognition but will make enquiries from the Dean. 
 I dine with Duke at his rooms tonight & we yarn so late into the night re poetry that I stay the night at his place in Rue de Petite Ecuries 
 23-6-19 (Mon) Spend day with Loepp - dine at YMCA & then to Mont [patnass]. We return to Rue de Montaigne to meet Duke & then decide to go to a concert. While at dinner siren sounds & we know that Germans have signified their intention of signing the peace We decide to visit the town & if
