 5-8-16 Sat 5th Last night we gave the bridle reins a twist around one foot &amp; lay down anywhere &amp; dropped off to sleep &amp; our horses were about as tired as we were &amp; did not disturb us; at 2 oclock this morning we were mounted again after getting out water bottles filled from fantasies brought up by the Indian Camel Transports, &amp; also got two more horse feeds to take with us, we went due south for 3 miles &amp; saw the results of our artillery fire;  Camels were lying dead everywhere some loaded with artillery shells &amp; others with machine guns
