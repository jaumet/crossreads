
Aug. 18Passed British war craft, cruiser & submarine about 6-30 a.m.  Steadied engines 2-30 p.m., anchored at Lemnos about 3 p.m.  The ships in port simply amazing.  War crafts of all descriptions as well as Red Cross & Transports.  Harbour well mined all over especially across channel.  Drew anchor & steamed further up harbour in among the numerous ships & again dropped anchor about for night about 6 p.m.  Several photos taken.  Many of the boys arrested for diving overboard.  Submarine Guard dismissed at 2-30 p.m.
