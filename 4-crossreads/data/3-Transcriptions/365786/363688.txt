<p>The men in the hospitals were delighted to receive visitors &amp; I used to go almost every day &amp; see the 50 odd men in my husband's 6 wards.  I hired a machine &amp; made them all fruit bags to keep their few treasures in &amp; as we found it impossible to get tooth-brushes, razors, handkerchiefs, etc. from the Red + we were doing our best to procure these ourselves (1 hank. in ward).
