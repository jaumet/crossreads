<p>was 2 between 3 men which took cutting up, the lads drew &amp; the lucky two had one each.<br/>Water is our cheifest difficulty the allowance per man is 4 pints per day, when you get 3 drinks of tea every day &amp; a stew "made of bully-beef &amp; dried vegetable", there isnt much for personal use, one has a shave occassionally &amp; makes a wash out of that, the beach is too far down the hill to go often &amp; is highly dangerous as "Johnny" has a gun called "Beachy Bill" which gives the beach hell hence his name. It is estimated that he has 1000 casualties to his credit.<br/>I am writing this note right in the firing line with only a parapet of sandbags between me &amp; the Turk, who is about 200 yards in front &amp; has a trench running round us horseshoe shape about 20 yards away &amp; to the right the chief trouble here is shells &amp; bombs both of which we are very nervous of. Yesterday "Johnny" put 6 75 shells into our trench about 10 yards from where I was, he bagged 6 men wounded &amp; dirtyd several others our subaltern had his face blackened &amp; got a shaking up. They have 2 French 75 guns which we suppose they took from the Servians in the Balkan war &amp; by gum they are dangerous. Well Lizzie dear I cannot say any more about here or I will be infringing the aforementioned</p>
