<p>[This page crossed through.]<br/>The hot wind, the Khamasin which blows from the south often causes a yellow fog to prevail owing to the sand &amp; dust it brings from the desert.  It may blow for three days at a time.<br/>Cairo is the largest city in Africa contains the residence of the Khedive, it is a mixture of old &amp; new.  The Romans under Agustus fortified the citadel &amp; it became the headquarters of one of the 3 legions stationed in Egypt.  (Saladin built parts of it.)  Napoleon Bonaparte occupied it for several months &amp; wherever one turns something historical can be seen.</p>
