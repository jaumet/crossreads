<p>July 1915<br/>16 Fri<br/>Popes Post shelled<br/>3 wounded on Post<br/>3 wounded on Beach.<br/>ANZ MO attached vice Wells</p>
<p>17 Sat<br/>Capt Fraselin arrived and NZ dr went<br/>Application for temporary Lt Col returned not granted. Col Meredith still in command and can be brought here at a moments notice.</p>
