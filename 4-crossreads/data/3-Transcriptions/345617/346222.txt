<p>Octobre 27<br/>No. 2 Sec: working in camp today. Sap's McIntosh &amp; Linaere killed by shellfire today near Birr X Rds while returning to billet. All six bodies of last two days casualties buried in cemetery near Ypres. Shell got 19 8th F. Coy. in billet last night. Eleven have died &amp; others are not expected to live. There were very few light wounds.</p>
<p>Octobre 28<br/>Section working in camp. I went out early to have a look at &amp; take over some work from 7th F. Coy. Usual artillery dual going on. Enemy aeroplanes very busy bombing day &amp; night.</p>
