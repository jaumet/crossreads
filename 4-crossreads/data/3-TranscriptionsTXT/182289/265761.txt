
23rd Aust. Machine Gun Coy.
Perham Down Camp - 13-2-17Acting under Authority C.R., A.I.F. 5103 (A) dated 29-1-17 the 3rd Divisional Machine Gun Coy. was formed, strength 3 Officers and 150 Other ranks.  The following Officers and other ranks had joined from France.
Lieut. R.B. Coleman, No. 10, Sgt. E.D. BennettLieut. C.P. Bradburn, No. 35, Sgt. W.J. Corby2/Lieut. R.L. Walker, No. 79, Sgt. T.R. IrwinNo. 40, Pte. T. CoplandNo. 125, Pte. C.W. Taylor.
15.2.17Lieut. Bradburn & 2/Lieut. Walker proceeded to Grantham to undergo a Machine Gun Course.
17.2.1734 specialists including 25 Drivers, 5 Signallers, 2 Saddlers, 2 Artificers, proceeded to Grantham to undergo Specialist courses.
20.2.17The following Officers & N.C.O. reported from France,
Capt. Algie, W.M., 10th M.G. Coy.Lieut. Chambers, A.L., 10th M.G. Coy.Lieut. Wedd, C.C., 9th M.G. Coy.
