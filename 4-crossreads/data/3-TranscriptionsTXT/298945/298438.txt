 I had a chat with Freddy Grey yesterday about a feeling I have had lately that Ob (his brother) seems to have left his influence behind him, or perhaps still is with us in spirit. I have felt greatly encouraged &amp; comforted lately by the feeling that he is still with us in spirit &ndash; encouraging, helping &amp; soothing. Freddy has felt the same, he told me. I have still to write to his father.
