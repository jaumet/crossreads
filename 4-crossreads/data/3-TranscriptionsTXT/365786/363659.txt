 There are numerous house boats on the Nile &amp; a constant succession of sailing boats called feluccas pass up &amp; down the Nile laden with grain &amp; produce, stones &amp; stores of all sorts.  I visited some of the old Coptic Churches but the ones I saw were very dirty &amp; uninviting.  During my stay in Egypt the fasting month of Ramadam, 9th month, was observed &ndash; the Moslems fast strictly during the day but eat &amp; drink &amp; smoke a great deal during the night.  The minarets of the mosques were illuminated &amp; various ceremonies take place.  They are a most devout people &amp; one frequently saw them praying even in the main streets.
