
417New Zealand HospitalWalton on Thames6.11.15My dear AuntMany thanks for your kind note - I have had a bad time since you saw me - I caught a cold in the head and it affected the eye - it swelled up and gave me a great deal of pain. When the specialist saw it he said it would have to come out in case it affected the other eye, but as there was a chance it might get better decided to leave It a few days - I am glad to say that when he came again he found the improvement so great that he says there is a chance of saving it, but I have to keep in bed which I have been doing for the last 10 days and hate it  - however its worth it if I save the eye - I don't
