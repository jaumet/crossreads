  
 June, 1915 Saturday 12 Came up to Alexandria on the Coast to visit our wounded. Many of the men I enrolled have lost limbs and eyes but are quite cheerful. Cooler here by 10 degrees than Cairo & the Transport A42 is in the Bay some go there tonight to see Daisy & Joe's letter of May 9th just recd have not seen Toby Phillips yet but mentioned Jack Lorimer Glad to hear all is well & so am I. Am posting this to night  morrow Love to all F.W.
