 August 1917 17th   We went for a route march to Blaringhem where we had a little practice on "bridge reconoisence" and returned after lunch. 18th   I have been granted leave to-day &amp; a couple of us went into Aire, a large village on the Neuff-Fosse Canall.   We rode on bikes &amp; spent an enjoyable day there. There are a great number of Portuguese soldiers in the town &amp; I  understand it is their Head-quarters. A few Fritz  aeroplanes came over during the night but did practically no damage. 19th   Sunday   After a little squad drill and a lecture, given by the doctor, we had the rest of the day off.
