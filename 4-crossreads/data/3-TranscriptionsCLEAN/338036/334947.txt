  
 [12th Sept. contd] of our section second.  Joe Nankivell of our sect pulled off the high jump (about 5 ft) & long jump (about 18ft 3)  During the sack race I injured two of my toes & they were very sore for rest of day. Thursday 13th  Friday 14th No duty.  The toes turned out to be more injured than I imagined. 
 Saturday 15th This morning we were reviewed by General Porter, D.M.S. 2nd Army who presented a few medals for latest stunts.  He also ripped in the bulsh a lot - much to the delightment of the boys. 
 Sunday 16th Water party & swim. 
 Monday 16th Cricket match between B Sect & our Sect resulted in a win for us by 15 runs.  We scored 77 (Wagstaffe 16 Sundries 15) & B scored 62.  I only scored 2 but shaped very well behind the sticks. 
 Tuesday 18th Meeting of all 'C' Section sports to elect a committee to carry on the Sports Program including Cricket Football Baseball etc.  Pte Geo Pickering was elected Secy, Major Williams Pres.  Sectional Committee :- Sgt Crook Cpl Macaulay  L/C Nankivill, Pte Pearson  Wagstaff & Duggan  Football Committee Wagstaff
