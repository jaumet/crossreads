  
 1914 Dec 10 Th Still unwell Reported sick to Medical Officer for day Fine day 
 Dec 11 Fri Feeling fairly good. Wrote several letters Arrived at Fremantle at 9 p.m. Very pretty by night Rained during day - Read Magazines 
 Fine sunset Sea smooth 
 Dec 12 Sat Lovely day Worked in office - Place very pretty Did some signalling to H.M.A.S. Pioneer. Read first newspaper for a week. Went ashore at  3 pm. Saw Fremantle & Perth - In touch with scouts - Good look round. 2 letters
