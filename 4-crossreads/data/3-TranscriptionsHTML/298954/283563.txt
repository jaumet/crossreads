<p>Some of us (including, of course, myself) had life-belts.</p>
<p>The hold was the German crews quarters;   it was the first time I had been down here.   Everything was very comfortable and very clean &ndash; in marked contrast to the prisoner's quarters.</p>
<p>Many of the neutrals were down here with us.</p>
<p>The hoses were out &ndash; ready for action.   We spent a scarcely-enjoyable 10 minutes waiting for the vessel to open fire but she turned out to be merely a Skandanavien-Amerika passenger line, glittering in ice from bow to stern.</p>
<p>Wed. 6th Feb.<br/>On Monday, the 4th, we again encountered the "Ingotz Mendi".</p>
<p>Last night the "Tuscania" was torpedoed South of us on the Irish Coast, so we are informed.</p>
