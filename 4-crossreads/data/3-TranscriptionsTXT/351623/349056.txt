21 Jan 19Anchored at Port Suez. Still taking in coal from lighter. It is a pretty slow process of coaling here, the natives carrying it up in baskets. Hospital Ship Kanowna came in about 12 noon from Port Said. She brought our chaps along from Port Said, that we left behind there. Heaved anchor at 2 pm &amp; proceeded to sea. Weather getting warm now.
