<p>3am got 4 letters 1 from Kitty &amp; full of cheer &amp; cheek. Parcelled up Souvenirs etc for K. sending by Courtney Sgt for posting in London.</p>
<p>[A space has been left in the page indicating a gap in the diary, then towards the bottom of the page is written:]<br/>Much wet weather followed. nightly trench fatigues, on mining &amp; trench reconstructing. Left Ypres first week October.</p>
