<p>[Monday 31 January 1916]</p>
<p>[Following paragraph is crossed out]<br/><strike>Letter from Mother &amp; Joe written on Xmas day so that is not bad</strike>  I should have called but guessed the Papers would let you know we had left ANZAC.<br/>We are expecting Gen Birdwood here this afternoon he is a popular Officer with the Australians &amp; N. Zealanders &amp; we all recognise he got us away from ANZAC very cleverly.<br/>Will post these notes with love to all it is great luck to think all my notes have reached you &amp; of late, your letters have also been delivered most regularly.</p>
