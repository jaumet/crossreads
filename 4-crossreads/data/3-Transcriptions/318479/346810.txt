<p>June 4<br/>Artillary activity.  Enemy bombard two salients heavily.</p>
<p>June 5<br/>Wet day.  Very quiet.  We are on fatigue in firing line.  "Stand-to" all night as we are expecting the 27th Batt. to raid the German trenches on the left.</p>
<p>June 6<br/>Wet day.  Very quiet.</p>
<p>[Private Middleton was killed in action this day]</p>
