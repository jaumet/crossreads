<p>MARCH 1917</p>
<p>12 MONDAY</p>
<p>Raining off &amp; on all day.</p>
<p>I rode into Stenwerck with</p>
<p>Cyril Ruhu in morning. The</p>
<p>C.R.A (Gen. Grimwade)</p>
<p>inspected the billets ect</p>
<p>in morning &amp; he was so</p>
<p>pleased with them that he</p>
<p>granted us a half holiday</p>
<p>so we did not have to</p>
<p>fall in till 3.30 pm stables.</p>
<p>We heard that Bagdad</p>
<p>had fallen.</p>
