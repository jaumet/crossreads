
3rd A.C.C.S.                                                                                                                                                   29th C.C.S.                                                                                                                                                B.E.F.                                                                                                                                                  France.                                                                                                                                           12. 11. 16.My Dearest Moth. & Sisters,No Aust. mail last week, hope I get one at end of this week, no doubt some of mine have gone down in the ship that was submarined the other day.Our enemy has been very busy about here lately but we are quite safe & I dont feel at all nervous. Only worried at the thought of our brave boys having to go through such dreadful work. How I wish this wicked war was ended, especially this cold wet miserable weather.
