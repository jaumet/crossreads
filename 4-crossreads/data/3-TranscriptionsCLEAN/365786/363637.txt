There are the bazaars with their street called the Mouski [also spelt Muski] where each trade has its own quarter.  I preferred the spice quarter for here the scent of the spices overcame the smells of the people, not all are interesting, the beads & precious stone shops merchants being most attractive.  We used to watch the Tailors too sewing & then pressing their goods with huge flat irons worked with their feet.
