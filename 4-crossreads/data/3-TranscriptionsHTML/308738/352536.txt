<p>Oct. 21 Thursday 1915<br/>P. M. came &amp; interviewed Grice &amp; O. C. after which Grice was arrested &amp; taken to Mustapha. He was let out tonight on Bail.<br/>Went to hear a lecture on the Bible in many lands it was very interesting.</p>
<p>Oct. 22 Friday 1915<br/>Working as usual P. M. came &amp; interviewed the O. C. again re Grice nothing definite done yet with him.<br/>Lieut Cunningham went to Cairo today.</p>
