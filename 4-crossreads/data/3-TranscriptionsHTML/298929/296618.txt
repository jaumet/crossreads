<p>He was no gold braided colonel stuck away behind the line,<br/>With an easy chair and heater prettied up to make a shine.<br/>He was always where the men were even though 'twas hot as<br/>hell,<br/>And he set them the example, can you wonder they fought well.</p>
<p>In the struggle for a village German hordes came rushing down<br/>The English broke and fled and Billjim saw it with a frown,<br/>He stepped into the breach, and gave the order to attack.<br/>As the boys all fixed their bayonets and soon Fritz was<br/>scampering back</p>
