<p>flat. Horsemen. Down Cantby Gully. Leinsters gone. No more in Mortuary. Had good tea &amp; then home (?) to bed.</p>
<p>Mon 9th Things still lively on the left it appears &amp; large numbers of wounded being sent in also plenty of rumours Commenced collecting rifles &amp; equipment</p>
<p>Tue 10th Nothing very fresh Hill taken &ndash; Hill retaken Hill not taken at all etc etc Corp Villis not yet found or body in. Turks gave the valley the dence of a shrapneling in the morning &amp; got some Terriers.</p>
<p>Wed 11th Heard from Eustace also paper from Watson Butterworth &amp; Jacobs injured by own bomb. Former serious Seymour had arm smashed</p>
