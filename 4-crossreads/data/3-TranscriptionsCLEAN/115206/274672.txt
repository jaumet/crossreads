  
 April 1918 Friday 12 Bussey - La - Dours.  D.R. Weather glorious. During morning rode across to Pont - Noyelles, a Querrie had a look at bridges there incomplete. In the afternoon saw a very spectacular feat in air - one Fritzer dropped. Heavy bomb raid by Gothas at 5am. Rode across country to Bonnay and reported at Bty for mail. - received 15 letters and a parcel of socks. Another bomb raid per Fritz at 7.30pm.
