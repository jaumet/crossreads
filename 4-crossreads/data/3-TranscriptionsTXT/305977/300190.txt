
March 1915Friday 19thB & C Squadrons marched out to the quarries 4 miles towards the citadel.  The 5th Regt was placed between us and the camp & we had to get through them.  However there was some misunderstanding & the scheme did not work properly.  Was feeling a bit off, have caught a cold somehow so didn't shun in the afternoon.
Saturday 20thThe whole brigade turned out in full marching order & were inspected by the Brigadier.
Sunday 21stSpent a quiet day in camp.  Couldn't go anywhere, as I was broke.  Edmundson came round & we yarned most of the afternoon.
Monday 22ndUsual mounted drill in the morning.  Scouting & advance guard work.  Didn't go out in the afternoon.
