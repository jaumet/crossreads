<p>August 1915</p>
<p>flare went up at Chatham's posts &amp; a green one at the Lone Pine.  Immediately there was heavy firing &amp; the artillery joined in also a destroyer on the right flank.  I think it was only a demonstration they were making.  The firing lasted about half an hour.</p>
<p>Monday 23rd<br/>A beautiful clear cool  morning.  We got orders to go out with the squadron when they filed out of the trenches.  At 1.30 pm C Squadron relieved us &amp; we occupied the back fire trench.  I was posted on the observation post with 2 others for 24 hours.  You can get a great view of the Turkish trenches &amp; can see practically all the right &amp; cemtre of our position.  Things were very quiet.
