  
 467 New Zealand Military Hospital Walton on Thames 16th December 1915 
 My dear Father Many thanks for your kind letter which Jocelyn forwarded on to me. I was awfully pleased to hear such good reports about the Farm and that Billy had quite recovered from  his illness - Mr Brewerton came on Wednesday and was very pleased with my eye - He has finished giving me treatment and I will go before a medical board next week when they will decide what they are going to do with me - the New Zealand people absolutely refuse to part with me and as they are likely to put me down for home service, guarding a railway line or bridge or some similar work  - I am asking
