Sunday Nov 11th
Leicester : met my friends seen most things worth seeing in the town : went to church at St Marys which is a fine old church : old Roman wall other sights very interesting : walk to Western Park after dinner talk after tea : slept at Y.M.C.A.
Monday Nov 12th
Leicester : caught 1 pm train to London : strolled about went to the Coliseum at night : excellent programme Ellen Terry came in for a fine applause : marvellous actress in spite of her age
