 18 October 1918 Friday Today the same as yesterday &ndash; lazing about and having a good time generally. Reported Germans evacuating the whole of the Belgian Coastline Good news coming in from all Fronts. 
 1918 October 19 Saturday Put in the day pottering about the farm. Hilled a few rows of potatoes in the morning and in the afternoon Arch &amp; I did a little fishing in the Currumbene &amp; I caught one fish as the result of our efforts. Good news continues from all Fronts. Lille has been evacuated by the enemy - occupied 5th Army
