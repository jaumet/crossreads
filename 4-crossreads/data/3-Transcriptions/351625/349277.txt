<p>I suppose you have heard a good deal of the doings of the Australians troops in France lately &amp; the honour they have been winning.  Arthur's Batt. has been in the thick of it but I had a card from him today saying he is quite well.  A short time ago we met a Capt. Plunkett of 3rd Batt.</p>
