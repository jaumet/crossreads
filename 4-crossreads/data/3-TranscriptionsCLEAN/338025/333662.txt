
Monday 16th April 1917BapaumeGrooming in the morning and had just started cleaning harness about 2.30pm when we had to leave the lines as a few of Fritz shells landed near the mules, so we ran back and took them about a mile to a flank, as our camp was in the line of fire. Another shell landed about 50 feet away from the wagons loaded with ammunition, but did not damage it.
