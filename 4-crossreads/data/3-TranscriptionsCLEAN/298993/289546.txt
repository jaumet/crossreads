  
 Saturday Sept 23.9.16 Ticking and exercising camels cleaning up for inspection was inspected had dinner, rested in afternoon Sunday Sept 24.9.16 Went to helipolis to church a long walk. Warm day returned to camp for dinner. Monday Sept. 25.9.16 On camel picquet to night , nothing doing Tuesday Sept 26.9.16 Dipping camels today. Had two letters from Jose arrived then wrote to mother Wednesday Sept 27.9.16 Drilling to day am not too well went into cairo got a bottle of Physic.
