<p>Home to our Mountains<br/>Il Trovatore</p>
<p>Rest thee O mother<br/>I will watch o'er thee<br/>Sleep may restore<br/>Sweet peace to my heart<br/>Home to our Mountains<br/>Let us return love<br/>There in thy young days<br/>Peace had its reign<br/>There shall thy sweet lay<br/>Fall on my slumbers<br/>There shell thy lute<br/>Make me joyous again<br/>Rest thee my mother<br/>Kneeling beside me<br/>I will pour forth<br/>My troubadour lay</p>
