14th to 19thThis week in hospital is not at all to my liking the continued inactivity after so much work I find to be very boring. The weather is still fairly cold &  during the last few days I manage to develop an attack of Influenza (slight)  The hospital is being cleared out  &  I am to leave with the first batch to-day for the No 3 General Hospital Abbassia.
