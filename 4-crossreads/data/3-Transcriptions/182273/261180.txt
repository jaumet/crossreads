
<p>November&nbsp;&nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>10 SUN&nbsp; Fine day out for</p>
<p>walk in aft. also at</p>
<p>night. Had a good time</p>
<p>called at Mme Marie&#39;s</p>
<p>Great expectations of</p>
<p>peace tomorrow. Evening after.</p>
<p>11 MON&nbsp; Fine day. Armistice</p>
<p>signed 11 am.</p>
<p>News through</p>
<p>1.40.P.M. Great excitement.</p>
<p>Town en fete. Took Madeline to</p>
<p>Cinema. Had a bonne evening</p>
<p>12 TUES&nbsp; Fine day. Holiday to</p>
<p>celebrate peace. out in aft.</p>
<p>had a &#39;bonne time&#39; also</p>
<p>at night. Rec. letters from</p>
<p>Amy &amp; Jack Sidlow. Posted letter</p>
<p>to Amy.</p>
<p>13 WED&nbsp; Fine day. Received</p>
<p>letters from Reg C. Gladys &amp;</p>
<p>Else P. Bty manouvres.</p>
<p>Writing at night.</p>
