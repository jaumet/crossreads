I passed through the devastated town and made for the village of Aveluy, two and a half kilometres away where the 14th Field was running a scabies hospital. About half a mile out of town I met a section of a Field Ambulance on the march. It was 'C' section of my unit leaving Aveluy for another village. I arrived at Aveluy in due course and found the Unit or at least one Section of it, quartered in a fine old ch&acirc;teau with the river Ancre running far below it and a fine big lake in front.
Here I found a score of great hustle and activity &ndash; the Unit was moving out that afternoon to Warloy, a village twelve kilometres away.
