
1st South African Gen. Hosp.                                                                                                 France.                                                                                                                                  September 24th 1916.My dearest Mother,My last letter was not very cheerful I'm afraid but the weather & conditions were unusual for us - after the heat of Egypt. The last few days have been perfect & the country is very beautiful when seen in the sun-shine, - We are about one mile from a little country village. The district is under cultivation & the crops are ripe & gathered in some fields & quite green in the next.
