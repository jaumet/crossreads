Friday 10thWet &amp; miserable all morning but not very hot. Takeing in water &amp; Niggers coaling today more noise than work. Four other boats came in from England Officers went on shore A good many have been sleeping on deck for some time Have not tried it a there is rain nearly every night &amp; it is hard to get a dry spot A supply of Smoke Boxes were put on board today.
