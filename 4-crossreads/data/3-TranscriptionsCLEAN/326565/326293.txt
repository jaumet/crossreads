 Friday 28/1/16 Rainy & cold weather still continues.  Piquet duty; first for some considerable time; drew 12-30 AM - 4-30 AM shift. 
 Saturday 29/1/16 Received letters & cards from home  Celebrated the anniversary of my birthday on piquet duty. Relieved from piquet 4.30 pm; Walked to Serapeum Station about 11/2 miles across desert & caught 7.30 pm train for Ismailia, arrived back in camp 12.30 AM.
