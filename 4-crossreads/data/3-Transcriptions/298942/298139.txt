<p>Every one of this crowd is strong &amp; burly, &amp; the right class of stretcher bearer.  Many have been through nearly all the big stunts in France, including the heaviest of barrages &amp; are undoubtable game.  For me <strike>who</strike> not having done what they have done, &amp; being above all of a very mild disposition compared with them, they have <strike>the</strike> a certain contempt with a little admiration for my brain.</p>
