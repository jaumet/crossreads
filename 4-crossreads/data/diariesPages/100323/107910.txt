21/6/19 London
To Old and New Bond streets and went to Chappell Co's famous music shop and then down Bond St where abound. Several sharp showers. Round the Strand did some more shopping and then went back by tube to GW2 Hotel Paddington for lunch. At 2 pm went up to the British Museum where, unfortunately, the Egyptian rooms were not yet open but I saw the Rosetta Stone, the Elgin marbles and other Praxitiles work and the famous Portland Vase. Particularly interesting were many old Roman paintings much like those in the Naples Museum and a lot of Roman and Greek jewellery ? some well made.
