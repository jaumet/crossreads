
Sunday Feb 10Reported German Division reinforcing Turks Washaway on Railway again railway up as far as Ramli. Divisional sports postponed indefinitely as we may be moving out at any time. Monday Feb 11The villages round here look lovely. All the fruit trees are in blossom and it is the commencement of spring.Tuesday Feb 12Football Match Auckland v 6th light Horse. Good match won by AucklandWednesday  Feb 13Trains running again. Jews & Natives very cruel to domestic animals. Reinforcements 6 arrived for unit today.
