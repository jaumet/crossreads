 Friday July 13 Handed in all surplus kit to day. Saw Camelian to day. Funny little animals something like a lizard. Peculiar point is that they can change colour. Useful as fly catcher. Egypt commonly called land of Flies, Sand &amp; Dirt. All well Saturday July 14 Usual routine. Hot windy day Sunday July 15th Isolation lifted today. Now we shan't be long.
