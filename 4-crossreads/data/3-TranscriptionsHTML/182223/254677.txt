<p>Monday 5&nbsp;July 1915<br />35 patients from Alexandria&nbsp;&nbsp;<br />Busy about hospital all day&nbsp;<br />Posted letters to The Girls Mrs Knowles, &amp; C P Hyman of London&nbsp;<br />Letters to 31<sup>st</sup> May from Girls Buddie [pet name for Mollie] &amp; others</p>
<p>Tuesday 6&nbsp;July 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo during morning Letter<s>s</s> posted to Mollie.&nbsp;<br />Major Bagshaw, Col. Manifold, &amp; Lieut. Col. Barrett here between 5-30 &amp; 7 p.m.&nbsp;<br />Writing letters to Joseph after evg meal</p>
<p>Wednesday 7&nbsp;July 1915<br />In Cairo twice today&nbsp;<br />Cable from and to Mr (The Hon) Meeks of Sydney, about his son Victor.&nbsp;<br />Close. But all going well.&nbsp;<br />Wrote to Glen Sparks Goomerri Queensland.</p>
