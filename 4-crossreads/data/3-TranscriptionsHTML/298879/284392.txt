<p>June 1915<br/>Thursday 17<br/>Fine Beautiful day. Still on the mend. We have plenty to eat. Am on Milk Diet but am expected to be off it soon. Breakfast about 7.30 AM dinner at 12 PM. And tea at 5 PM. Lights out at 9.30 PM. Rise about 7.15 AM. All those who are able to do so</p>
<p>Friday 18<br/><u>Fine</u> On Light Duty. Nothing of interest to record</p>
