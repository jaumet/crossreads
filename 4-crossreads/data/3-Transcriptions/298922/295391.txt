<p>Tuesday 6 March 1917</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>Young ROSS son of KIM ROSS people who used to have Horse Teams in the old days is here as a Lieut. He goes into Cairo with a car every day, when I go I go by TRAM or TRAIN, plenty of L.S.D. Do not mention Revolver in notes as we are not allowed to keep any arms taken in war but I have saved so few Trophies though would like to keep this one. NECK alright now &amp; am feeling A1.</p>
<p>Thank you for Australasian. I see a lot about DELATITE in the last.</p>
<p>[The following sentence crossed through.]  Love to you from your son Frank.</p>
