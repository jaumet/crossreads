  
 17 April 1915 Saturday 
 Make a short excursion to Cairo through the native Quarter   &  Moslem bazaars. Our men build a barbed wire enclosure round the Quarter Guard to give the sentries a better chance of keeping the prisoners in. 
 1915 April 18 Sunday 2nd After Easter 
 I miss Church parade having to make out a whole lot of lists of names   &  numbers.
