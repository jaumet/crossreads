<p>again had breakfast 7.30 carried on with the trenching taube over again they are firing all around him they made him wobble once fancy he got hit a cool morning breeze blowing can hear the big guns firing they are making an attack on our left about a mile away. Wrote to win and Josie<br/>Wednesday 4.4.17. stand to 4AM had breakfast and saddled up took two pack camels and went to the dump two miles away to fetch machine gun drums watered camels saw the village a dirty on to that returned to camp 3P.M had dinner rested till 5 had tea got all gear on walked about</p>
