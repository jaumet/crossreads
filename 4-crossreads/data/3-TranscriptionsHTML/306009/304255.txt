<p>1915<br/>Mar. 10 Wed.<br/>Pretty warm to-day - looks as if the hot weather was commencing in earnest.<br/>No definite news yet of our departure although we are ready to move at short notice.<br/>Decided to get a pair of khaki shorts which are being largely worn by the troops.<br/>Mail in to-day. Received 3 letters.</p>
