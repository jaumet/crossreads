 XXXX France 19.5.17 
 My darling Eddy, 
 So pleased to hear you are getting such a strong boy. Hope you will be able to go to school   &amp;  like it. How are the little chicks? Suppose you will have names for the, all. Hope they all grow up healthy. Tell Mummie to have your photo taken   &amp;  send me one in a frame.  Fondest love   &amp;  a bog hug with kisses From  XOXOXOXO  auntie Eadle.
