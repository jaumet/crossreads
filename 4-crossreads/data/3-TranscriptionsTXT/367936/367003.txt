 Monday 3 July 1916 Dose of caster oil! 
 Tuesday 4 July 1916 Rostron, Nelson, Vint &amp; I went for a swim in afternoon then Rostron &amp; I went to Alex.  I got my films &ndash; picture show not bad. Lecture on Ancient Egypt by Dr. Arnold very interresting.  Had a talk to Lin Alworth.
