
THE ABERDEEN LINE          T.S.S.MARATHON     15-IX-15,
Dear Judge,
So very many thanks for your two letters - all the more welcome as they arrived when I was in Hospital.As you know by now we had a very big fight, & the first Brigade had to attack some Turkish trenches known as Lonesome Pine, took it all right, but our casualties of necessity were heavy. I got a H crack on the knee - very slight - it was more the concussion of the shell bursting that bust my knee up. I also got dysentery rather badly - this was just before the attack, & after the show
