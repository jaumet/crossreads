
April 1917Sunday 15Moved out to night from our old camp. camped for night
Monday 16at 2 pm 3rd Bgde moved out to take up outpost line prior to the big push which we are to hop into tomorrow at daybreak.Saw cousin Bob Fell out here  He is with 4th Signal troop 4th Brigade - (in our division)Moved out for the big fight at 7pm. Reinforcements arrived in darkness.
