<p>(31)<br/>shreds of loose timbers and torn iron and spouting hang, and sway, and rattle in the wind.<br/>Loosened masses of plaster flop, and torn edges of wall paper flap.<br/>And they flop and flap and clap together, making weird and hollow sounds.<br/>No matter where you may wander in this wilderness of shell wrought destruction you notice again and again the ruin and the similar character of ruin.<br/>Where I almost repeat myself in this plain description, I do so intentionally.
