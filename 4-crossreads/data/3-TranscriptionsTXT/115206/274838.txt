
September 1918.Wednesday 25
La Vigure, Bty Pos and W.Line and PeoullyWeather fine and clear. 6 inch How. Registering under Fritz balloon - drew crabs.Relieved by 231st Bdge, Artillery.Left old possie and arrived at W.L. about 7.30 pm[indecipherable] almost caught "F" subs team and crew, also "D" subs.Spent night with Doc with the old Fritzer bombing about. News from Palestine is wonderful. Two Turkish armies cut off. Yanks relieved fourth Bdge. Big stuff coming off very shortly perhaps 29th
