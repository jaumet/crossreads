<p>7th. Been a very quiet &amp; tiresome day  how the time drags &amp; I suppose the nearer we approach "Aussie" the slower the time will go, I am just beginning to realize that at last I am going home, &amp; I wonder &amp; ponder how everyone will seem to me] after being away so long  they are certain to see a vast change one way or the other for it will be just on 7 years since I left Tassy with the intention of stopping 18 months in N.S.W. but as the old saying goes "man proposes but God disposes", &amp; this is what my few months have led too, &amp; during that period a chap must alter considerably</p>
<p>theres one thing this knocking about has done for me &amp; that is killed all desires I might have had for rambling</p>
