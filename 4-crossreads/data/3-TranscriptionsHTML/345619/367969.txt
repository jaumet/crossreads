<p>Hospital, if he is wounded you may know before I do. I think this writing must be a bit worse than usual, but you will know by getting this, written three days after being shot, that I am not too bad, Of course I immediately ran up a Temperature varying between 101 &amp; 104. the last one being 102, and that is in spite of a lot of blood lost, my pulse was up to 132 so my love organ must have been working overtime</p>
