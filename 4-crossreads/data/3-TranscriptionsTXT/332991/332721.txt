Thursday Feb. 15. 1917There was a heavy frost last night.  The street was all white this morning until near dinner time.  It is very cold today.  Part of our battalion is going into the trenches to night.  Some of the Pioneers are also going , but a few are wanted in the town.  I have not heard whether I am to go or not.Sgt.
