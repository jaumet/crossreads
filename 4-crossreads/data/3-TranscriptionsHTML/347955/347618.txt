<p>dinner 12 rested all afternoon had a swim fed up 5 tea 5.30 &amp; turned in.<br/>Saturday Oct.6.10.17<br/>Turned out 5.30 fell in fed up had breakfast Bayonet fighting cleaned out huts went to ismalia on leave a rotten Place to go too  returned to camp 12 midnight was halted all the way By indians turned in 12.30.<br/>Sunday Oct 7.10.17<br/>turned out 5.30 roll call fed up Breakfast rested till 9 went for a swim fed up 11.  dinner 12.  got water tanks repaired  1. till 5.P.M. had tea wrote to win and Bell had a letter from win one from Bell.<br/>Monday Oct 8.10.17<br/>turned out 5.A.M. fed up cleaned up had Breakfast got gear all ready for a stunt watered all</p>
