<p>A Colonel marched in, followed by a Major, done up in red braids.  Then came another Major, then the ward doctor, next came the <strike>order</strike> matron, then the orderly officer, W.O., Company S.M., Orderly Sgt., orderly Corp. &amp; vice Corp. &amp; two off siders.  The Col. made a close scrutiny under the beds &amp; over the beds, found something amiss, &amp; passed on to the next ward.  What a sight, what polish &amp; what a waste of money</p>
