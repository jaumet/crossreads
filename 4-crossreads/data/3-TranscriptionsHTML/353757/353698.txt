<p>23/7/15</p>
<p>Dr. Vol</p>
<p>Received "Cumberland Times" of June 5 &amp; read same from front to back.  Thanks.  Hope you're keeping better now.  Will write soon as there is any news.</p>
<p>Yours<br/>A. Hine</p>
<p>Printed text: Nothing is to be written on this side except the date and signature of the sender.  Sentences not required may be erased.  If anything else is added the post card will be destroyed.</p>
<p>I am quite well.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>I have been admitted into hospital sick and am going on well.<br/>I have been admitted into hospital and hope to be discharged soon.<br/>I am being sent down to the base<br/>I have received your letter dated<br/>I have received your telegram dated<br/>I have received your parcel dated</p>
<p>Letter follows at first opportunity.<br/>I have received no letter from you lately.  <strike>for a long time</strike></p>
<p>Signature only<br/>A. Hine<br/>Date &ndash; 1/8/15<br/>(Postage must be prepaid on any letter or post card addressed to the sender of this card.</p>
