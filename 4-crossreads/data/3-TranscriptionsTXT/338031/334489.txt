
January 5th & 6thSnowing steadily, the countryside looks grand in its white covering & the boys have taken to snowballing & guy making as if they had been brought up to it from childhood of course a percentage of Australian troops, about 20% I think, were reared in some part of Great Britain, so what the Australian natives don't know they can rapidly pick up and I must say that snowballing is an exhilarating, healthy pastime.
