  
 November 1917 Wednesday 21 Very cold. Started for Jerusalem road up through steep hills Halted on side of one of the stoney hills. Olive trees everywhere. Great number of wells at Saris where we watered. Stayed all night. Village half mile farther on called Enab Converted into hospital. 
 Thursday 22 Still camped on hill below Saris. "B"squdn started on outpost or reserve and infantry. Camped in rocky slope of hill near Indian cavalry. Very cold at night.
