
Monday 4 December 1916Cleaned up camp and moved off to new wells about 3 miles North.  Regt. now detached from brigade.
Tuesday 5 December 1916Dick & I rigged up "bivey" with piece of canvas.  Taube over, dropped bombs on 2nd Regt. looked at us but we scattered with horses & it imskied.On telephone from 6 till 10 p.m.
