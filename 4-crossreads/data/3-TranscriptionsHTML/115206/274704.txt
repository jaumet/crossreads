<p>May 1918</p>
<p>Tuesday 14</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Dours d-y<br/>At 4 am Fritz arrived at our little district &ndash; well loaded . No military damage.<br/>Weather today &ndash; glorious. Rode across to Bonnay, and had a swim in the big pool at Querrie. Temperature reached to 95   at midday.<br/>At 7 pm Fritz's waves commenced , and numbering twelve visited Amiens . Artillery very active at 10 pm.</p>
