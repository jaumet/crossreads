<p>16 August, Saturday</p>
<p>Ellis is married. Breakfast at Paddington Town Hall. 9 pm boat Home. Meet Mrs Royle. A grand day.</p>
<p>17 August, Sunday</p>
<p>Stop Home in garden all day. I go to Dobboe's to Tea at night &amp; Chris &amp; I again win at 500.</p>
