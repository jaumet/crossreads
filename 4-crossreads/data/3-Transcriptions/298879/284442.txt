<p>September 1915<br/>Saturday 25<br/>Fine   More Helio    We all had tunics issued to us. A person would not know whether we were English Tommies or Australians.</p>
<p>Sunday 26<br/>Beautiful day. A lovely little breeze blowing to prevent the sun's rays being felt. All looking spick and span for Church Parade.</p>
