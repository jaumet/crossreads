
15-6-1917Hot day.  General Coxon stirring things up in 3rd Bty. lines.  He was there from 9 a.m. till 12 noon.
16-6-1917Sgt. Major Fitzsimmons recommended for a commission.  Muster Parade at 9 a.m.  O.C. inspection.  New officer attached to Bty.
17-6-1916  SundayMuster Church Parade, weather hot.  Lieut. Heppingstone, new officer.  Capt. Hogan back from Paris.  Lieut. Morris on Blighty leave.
18-6-1917Thunderstorm, very little rain.  Rumoured that we move into action this week.
19-6-1917Thunderstorms prevails.  Move into action tomorrow.  Reveille 3 a.m.
20-6-1917Left Behencourt at 5.20 a.m.  Arrived spring garden camp 11 a.m.  Raining at 3 p.m.  Off again tomorrow.  Two shoes off on march.  Shod 4 horses this evening.
