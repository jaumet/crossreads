<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>Ship of Origin - OC Infantry</p>
<p>Date - 12/9/14</p>
<p>Time Received or Sent 12.2 AM</p>
<p>To Brigadier</p>
<p>I propose to move at&nbsp;5am tomorrow morning&nbsp;coast road until contact with Beresfords party. &nbsp;I then intend to move south with my left flank on road from Kapa Kaul to supposed position of Wireless Station after shelling ceases. &nbsp;Half Coy will move along road which branches from coast road 1,100 yds East of Wunapope. &nbsp;Half Coy to occupy position at cross roads due south of Herbertshohe one mile from Grunapur&nbsp;&amp; to patrol to Grunapur. &nbsp;Near here today 6 men were taken on suspicion. &nbsp;The 12 pdr&nbsp;to move to KabaKaul cross roads &amp; thence along road on my left flank. reference map issued by OC intelligence this morning.</p>
<p>From - Watson</p>
<p>I concur in your proposals&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
