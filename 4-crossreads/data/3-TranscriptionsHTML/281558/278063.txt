<p>Wednesday Nov 21st</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : Q.M. fatigue in morning : pay parade in afternoon but did not get any : met Toss Donelly : concert in Y.M.C.A. after tea</p>
<p>Thursday Nov 22nd</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : usual parades : on fire picket at night</p>
<p>Friday Nov 23rd</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : sick parade in morn garden fatigue in afternoon : Morrie, Fred &amp; self to Y.M.C.A. concert after tea &amp; it was a splendid affair : have seen far worse talent in many high class theatres</p>
