<p>Sunday 2 April 1916<br/>Went to church at St. Chads, Cremorne with Aunt Katie and <strike>the</strike> Hal &amp; Fred.<br/>Spent the evening at the Sams.</p>
<p>Monday 3 April 1916<br/>Came up to L.H. camp at Menangle.  Had great job to get my blankets etc. and was told I would get no pay for time I was away, however I went and saw our officer &amp; he is going to try and get it for me.<br/>Have got in with same chaps I was with before;  they have all had their final leave.</p>
