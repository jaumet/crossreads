<p>too for such a long time.<br/>At 2 pm I journeyed to the Barracks received my suit of Civvys &amp; discharge. Once more a Civvy and in the best country in the world.<br/>I hope never again to go through the same experiences but must confess that now it is all over I would not have missed it under any circumstances. There is no need to write how pleased those at home were on my return. The house was gaily bedecked with flags &amp; everybody was happy. I now close my Diary feeling a better man for my experiences in the Army &amp; am fully convinced had</p>
