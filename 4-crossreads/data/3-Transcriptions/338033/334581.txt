<p>caught sight of a French submarine. The same night we<br/>passed Algiers, which looked very pretty with the setting sun shining on it. The following day a wireless message was received informing us of the naval battle in the North Sea. The same afternoon we entered the Straits of Gibraltar, passing Gibraltar late at night, much to our disappointment, as we had hoped to view this famous place in daylight. We entered the Great Atlantic Ocean on the 4th and crossed the Bay of Biscay during the 6th.  The same night we picked up a torpedo boat for escourt, which circled around us until we reached Plymouth.</p>
