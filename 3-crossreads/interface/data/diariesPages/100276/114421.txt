We had to carry a lot of duck boards hurdles up with us for we were going to put down a floor in the portion of trenches we had completed a nice job it was for there was easily a foot of water slush in the bottom the men had to work in this, we finished at 2 oclock come home then.
The 2nd Battalion got a nasty dishing up last night had 18 men Killed including 2 Capt. 4 Lieut's "Aerial torpedoes" got them I believe.
Saw another Taube brought down this morning by one of our planes he come a beautiful buster.
