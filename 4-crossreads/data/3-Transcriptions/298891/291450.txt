<p>Wonderfully warm. Wed 9th</p>
<p>Met C. Reid for lunch at W. Tower &amp; went out to Major Frogley &amp; on to Serb. Performance at Matron sent car for me &amp; in evening I went with her to party at 42nd Gen. Hosp. [indecipherable] night.</p>
<p>Thurs 10th</p>
<p>Milan called for me at 11 &amp; thence to S.W.H. at 2 we left Salonique with Miss Barker. Bitterly Bitterly cold.</p>
<p>Frid 11th</p>
<p>Suffering for want of sleep busy camp day. Still cold.</p>
<p>Sat. 12</p>
<p>Milder. Terrible day in dump shifting. Sheila had birthday party at night.</p>
