  
 Friday 7 June 1918 Pilot Capt. A. Brown, in air 35 m.  Flew back to Ramleh [also spelt Ramla] very releaved to be back, just had a good bath. Played 14th Sqdn. cricket and beat them. Shepheard 39 Self 23, pulled our side out of the fire. 
 Saturday 8 June 1918 Photos & H.A.P. with Addie.  In air 3 h. 10 m., uneventful trip. First trip with Addie since I came back. Burton crashes in 2E, badly hurt, wonder I was not with him, would have been killed.  Should take a Ticket in Tatts after that.
