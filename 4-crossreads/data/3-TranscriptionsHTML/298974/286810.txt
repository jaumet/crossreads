A large Fritz grave behind it shows how successful it was. There is a slightly smaller one each side of it, &amp; then Fritz got one under some 10th W. Shells shell cases, grenades, bombs, old bits of rifles &amp; ammunition abound, &amp; last &amp; certainly most annoying to us, endless field of chopped up barbed wire entanglement.
