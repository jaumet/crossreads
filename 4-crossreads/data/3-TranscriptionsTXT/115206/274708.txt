 May 1918 Saturday 18 
 Bussy &ndash;la-Daours Weather very warm, received a little present of eufs from a pal. A heavy thunder storm broke about 3 pm which greatly freshened things. At 6.30 pm went for a swim and swam Gen's horse. At 7 pm Fritz shelled village fairly severely with HV, HE and gas . 9.30 pm Run to Bonnay a little rough. 4.30 pm returned from run. Fritz still shelling . Plenty of bombs during night.
