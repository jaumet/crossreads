Thursday 20/2/17The guns just out in the paddock keep me awake by heavily shaking the place (orderly room) where I sleep on my own.   Sam heard from Harold Williams whilst in the office at night a dispatch runner arrived inquiring for an officer and it turned out to be Donaldson from Five Dock.  He informed me that Chas Bignall had been wounded in stomach.
Friday 23/2/17Recd letters from bro Ernie & Miss Warren.
RAID AT HOUPLINES
Saturday 24/2/17Sunday 25/2/17Got word of In afternoon Sam some of the boys & self went across to Pont de Nieppe to visit our old friends inc Madame Wable & Nile.  Also with Sergt Taylor called in to see the Estaminet people & had coffee there.  Purchased some eggs returned to Erquinghem & had the eggs for tea.  Pont de Niepper looked very gay this afternoon with all the new Zealanders in town.
Monday 26/2/17Got word of another stunt tonight & accordingly had to report to Billets - with all the other boys at 6.30 pm.  We were not clearing in the Tenth Ambulance area but were assisting.
