Returned to car & handed over my stores, then went to Red + depot & obtained some supplies for patients, picking up the Mj & civvies at 4.30 when we returned home.  A fine day but very cold in front of car.  Had more orders in referring to equipment.  We have been given up to the 10th to make a thorough check of equipment & to indent for requirements, after which all shortages will be at the cost of the C.O.  Fortunately I have been checking B & C Sections so to do this will be easy.
