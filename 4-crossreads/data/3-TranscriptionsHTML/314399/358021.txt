<p>Friday 24 January 1919<br/>Started  off  by nosing round for sports teams very slow tho. Too many away &amp;  being demobilised. Can't keep a team at all.</p>
<p>Saturday 25 January 1919<br/>Put on as duty officer as well as Sports Officer. Kicked up a row of course. On board of enquiry into loss of two horses. They were pinched.</p>
