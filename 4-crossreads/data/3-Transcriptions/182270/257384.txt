
<p>THURSDAY, MARCH 13, 1919.</p>
<p>13 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;1023</p>
<p>Fine &amp; cool. Was inoculated</p>
<p>for influenza in morning, &amp; it</p>
<p>has made my arm very sore</p>
<p>Reading on the top deck all</p>
<p>day. Had a band concert</p>
<p>there at night &amp; it was very</p>
<p>good.</p>
<p>Days run 325 miles</p>
