This week is the anniversary week of my trip from Alexandria to the Peninsula. Shall I be here or in France, or back in Australia this time next year? Either of the last two I imagine. Have heard from Dad &amp; Lily Vi &amp; Ernest &amp; Frances, also from Rushbrooke, who simply sent the card with "absolved" written on it. A short way of forgiving me for not calling on him.
