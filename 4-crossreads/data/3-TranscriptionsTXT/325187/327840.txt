 [Tuesday 4 April 1916] 
 Sandflies have bitten one Scotch Officer so badly he has gone to Hospital. Camels even are feeling the heat now poor brutes get hell- if they lag behind, the natives pull out a knife &amp; cut a hole in  their nostril  Camels Nose &amp; tie a piece of leather or hide on &amp; drag 'em along.
