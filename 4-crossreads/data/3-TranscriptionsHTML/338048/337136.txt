<p>&amp; went home to dinner.</p>
<p>After dinner went in a carriage sort of affair around hop fields saw a baloon land got some hops &amp; plenty of plums &amp; came home. Visited Uncles Club &amp; the drill room (Market) &amp; went to Pictures with Him.</p>
<p>In bed again 11.30 pm.</p>
<p>Wed 13th<br/>Same pleasant pre-breakfast routine as yesterday. Afterwards visited Mrs Sparrow whom must visit again.</p>
<p>Caught 11.11 am train to London, visited Harold Porsford strayed about until 7.30 when I met Harold at the Abbey Caught 8.40 pm train at St Pancras Stn. just in time Harold seeing me off.</p>
<p>Arrived Bedford 9-50 pm &amp; set out for camp on foot &amp; got a slight lift on a bike. But mainly walking I got home 11-35 p.m. doing nearly 7 miles in 1 hr 45 mins</p>
<p>Very enjoyably surprised to find a bed already made for me so turned in straight away.</p>
<p>Thursday 14th<br/>Paraded sick &amp; awarded 3 days light duty Doctor very pleased with the way my toe is doing. Writing in afternoon <strike>We held a concert in the Y.M in the evening</strike></p>
