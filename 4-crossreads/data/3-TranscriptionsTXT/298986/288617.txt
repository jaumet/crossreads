 Friday 28 
 Back to the old camp life.  Parade at 9 A.M. &amp; 2 P.M.  Some fatagues folded a few blankets this morn. 
 Sat.  28th  1 March 
 Paid this morning &pound;12.0.0 to go on leave on Monday morn.  Met my old mate N. Bell went to Warminster for a walk.  Had the studs taken out of my boots &amp; rubber heels put on a great improvement.
