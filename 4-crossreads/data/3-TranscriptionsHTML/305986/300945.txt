<p>whistling in the grass a few inches above our heads, we had to hug the wind for 20 minutes, then we crawled the rest of the way and got the line through, It has not broken again as yet. We are not being relieved today as I hoped, but its coming off tomorrow. A few more trench mortars came over last night and got a few of us.</p>
<p>13 Sept being relieved today. Just been showing the other crowd's [indecipherable] his lines he doesn't look overjoyed at the prospect. Its still raining nice drizzly dope you know. War news this morning is good and they reckon</p>
