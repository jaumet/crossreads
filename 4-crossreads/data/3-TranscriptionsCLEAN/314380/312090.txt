Case for EnquiryAt the Pyramids Hotel on the night in question a raid was made by a number of Police four arrest where made the culprits where Pte Green Pte Scott, Pte Hodges and Pte Brown on a charge for gambling. where driven in a Motor to the Luna Park Detention Barracks.
