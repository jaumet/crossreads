<p>September 1918.<br/>Wednesday 18</p>
<p>Ahead of Bernes. Bty Position<br/>The stunt is timed of 5.20am. good luck to all the lads.<br/>Weather very wet all day. Barrage opened at 5.25am and there was plenty "hanging" for old Fritz.<br/>At 10am all but one objective was taken.<br/>Grabbed 2635 prisoners and 114 prisoners and advanced in places to a depth of 4000 yds, penetrating the Hindenburg system of outposts.<br/>Moved to a new position near a village, no dugout available.</p>
