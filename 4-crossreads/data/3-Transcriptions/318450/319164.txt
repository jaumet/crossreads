<p>that afternoon.  We left Sydney at 5 pm on the 7th Jan 1916 on H.M.A.T. Transport A7 otherwise the Medic.  After clearing Sydney Heads we headed down the coast, and after four days sail we put into Port Adelaide where we remained only half a day as there was only a few troops to be taken aboard.  Although we only remained here half a day, it did us a lot of good, as we had not seen any thing for a few day, and besides the tucker was not much good, and when in port we could buy some, and we took advantage. <strike>as</strike> Although we did not get ashore, we sent one of the ship boy's out.  After leaving Adelaide we headed across the Australia Bight</p>
