<p>Wednesday 7th June</p>
<p>Left for Maadi on the Monday following &amp; found a man from Citadel had arrived to learn dispensing so that he could take my place.  Has rather a good general knowledge of drugs, but not so well versed in practical work.<br/>Went to Base &amp; put in a second &amp; more emphatic application so that Molloy &amp; I should soon leave.<br/>Yesterday I went with Molloy to Base again &amp; was told that application</p>
