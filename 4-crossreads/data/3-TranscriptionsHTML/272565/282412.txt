<p>were blown to pieces and others were wounded.  Only one man was there to be carried out so two of us jumped into the trench &amp; got the man out from his difficult position and put him on the stretcher.  No sooner was this done than Fritz rained the shells all round us.  They hit the sides of the trench and splashed into the muddy ground only a couple of yards off.  It was a case of getting this man away and nothing else</p>
