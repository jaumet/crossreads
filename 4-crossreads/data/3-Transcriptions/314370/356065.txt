<p>August, 1916<br/>Saturday 5<br/>same as yesterday<br/>read a good deal.  have got hold of a book worth reading "Valley of the Moon" Jack London</p>
<p>Sunday 6<br/>Reached Malta about 6 am &amp; spent about 7 hours in Masa Scirocco harbour<br/>8 pm just come off signal bridge 16 knots.<br/>Anniversary of our attack on Suvla Bay &amp; the Lone Pine charge</p>
