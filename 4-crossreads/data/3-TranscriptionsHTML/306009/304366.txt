<p>1915<br/>June 29 Tue.<br/>Went into Cairo in the afternoon &ndash; had hair cut &ndash; visited the Y.M.C.A. where arrangements have been made to have a meeting of the Aust. Scouts Assn. every Wednesday at 7.30 p.m.<br/>Went &amp; had a look at the Greek Scouts &amp; also at the Explorateurs (Italian Scouts). The latter are really cadets &amp; wear green uniforms with puttees, black hats with big drooping plumes.</p>
