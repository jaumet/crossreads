Sunday 19
 Met Bob Goldrick now Capt in this camp. Leaves for France on Tuesday.
Monday 20
 The troops about here, including O.T.B. have been inspected by H.R.H. Duke of Connaught. 
 The review was a success
 On account of my 6?2" of length I was given a rifle put in front line of Battalion.

 Letter from Dock that he can get me in the AQMGs office. H.Q.
