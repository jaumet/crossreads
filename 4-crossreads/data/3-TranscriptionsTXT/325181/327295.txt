 high points to waters edge at entrance. Barracks bungalows etc on right. Rather picturesque but very barren &amp; darned hot. Highest summit crowned with wireless station. Number of transports &amp; other boats in Harbor including a cruiser which convoyed us. Full kit inspection. Reported 15,00 Turks within 25 miles of us &ndash; 4.15 pm Orders to steam in an hour Warship getting steam up Talk about Turks all Kid-stakes after all. The natives rather interesting as they hang about the ships sides &amp; try to sell cigars cigarettes, cordials silks, dates, feathers, etc &ndash; which they send up the side in baskets. They ask exorbitant prices but one can easily beat them down.
