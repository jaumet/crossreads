<p>on the wharf and a dirty-looking lot too. The native police have their work cut-out keeping them back. Alexandria is a big shipping port and is a fair-sized town. One wants to come out here in the East to see the fashions. Why, you see women and men wearing harem skirts. One thing to me seems a very peculiar custom, the women wear veils, or they are very like masks, over the face. If they are married they wear a bridge from the forehead to the nose, if they are engaged they just wear a veil from the nose -- hiding all the lower part of the face.
