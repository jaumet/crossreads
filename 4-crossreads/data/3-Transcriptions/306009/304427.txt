<p>1915<br/>Aug. 31 Tue.<br/>Capt. Mackenzie, Secretary &amp; Registrar of No. 2 G.H. is now attached to the Staff of the D.M.S. Egypt as Staff Officer for Australian Medical Services &amp; has an office in the Australian Intermediate Base. I was loaned to him this afternoon &amp; worked in the Inter. Base from 2.30 till 7 p.m.</p>
