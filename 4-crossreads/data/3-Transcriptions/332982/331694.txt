<p>To the Doctor this morning.<br/>He was sent into the Hospital with measles, and all the Boys in the Hut (No24) were isolated.<br/>We were taken off the Parade ground and sent into the hut, and not allowed to mix with the other men.<br/>I don't think the Boys mind, as they will get out of the long route marches.</p>
