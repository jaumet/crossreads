
[Wednesday  29 March 1916]
Luxurious Bathroom & cool Bedroom.My duty work seems to try & settle claims for damage to crops, by Camps etc:-Using the water pumped at the Railways & Town Council & all the questions of a Town Council, Sanitation, Bounds Town Police etc:-How long will all this last!I forcaste a change back up North again very soon.Senussi are funished I think.  Gyppies are rogues if the British paid nothing, it would serve them right, they claim for all sorts of imaginary things & lie thro' a 10" board.
