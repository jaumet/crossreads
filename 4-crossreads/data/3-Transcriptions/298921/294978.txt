<p>Dec 21<br/>Revielle 4 &amp; Marched to ships after handing in all &ndash; Saddlery to ordinance &ndash; in addition to own Horses had to take 281 from [indecipherable] Depot unbroken a lot of them some strangles and ringworm. Raining all hands wet I went ashore to Wilkinson &amp;  Lavenders for &pound;S.D. returned per Motor Launch &ndash; sailed at 4 p.m. a tough night &amp; raining</p>
