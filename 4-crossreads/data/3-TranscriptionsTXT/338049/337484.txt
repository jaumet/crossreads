
Monday 30thCold day - Up & walked horse Had horses fed & watered as possible during the day. No trips. Living in a tent 7 of us abreast in a 10 yds space.
Tuesday 1st OctoberWalked horses about - fed & watered.
After dinner ordered out with full gear. Packed up & with Mr Fountain moved out to 10th Bde again. Still pretty rough, took shelter behind a tank. After waiting about 1 hour, further work found impracticible. Returned home none too sorry.
