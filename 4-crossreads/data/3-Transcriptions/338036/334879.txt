<p>OUR RAIDING PARTIES<br/>[27th May contd]<br/>also shelled out.</p>
<p>Monday 28th [May]<br/>At about 1.30 am we sent a raiding party of about 200 over in Prowse Point Sector but success was limited and only secured 1 prisoner &amp; lost about 80 wounded &amp; 10 killed.  Our bearers &amp; effectives had to go at top [?]  At Dead Horse we were more fortunate and except for the barrage were not disturbed.  I received instructions at noon to report to Charing Cross for another stunt to night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 29th  [May]<br/>At 1.45 one of the heaviest barrages I have witnessed was put up by us and the concussion was tremendous.  Soon after the wounded began</p>
