Thursday 23.8.17Left our "digs", a private boarding house about 9 Am, had breakfast at the Royal in Princes St.  While waiting for bus for Forth Bridge, met a tourist from Mosman who promised to call at the school on his return.  Returned from Forth Bridge in time for lunch.  Afternoon paid a visit to Edinburgh Castle & down High St to Holyrood Palace, both places being highly interesting.  Strolled around after tea.
Friday 24.8.17Down around Arthur's Seat in a Taxi before lunch; afternoon had a look over Art Gallery.  Filled in time till 12 midnight,
