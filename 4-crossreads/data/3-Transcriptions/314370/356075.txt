<p>August, 1916<br/>Friday 25<br/>walked out to Bulford camp to see the New Trench system<br/>Not bad but containing many glaring faults<br/>played cards all afternoon</p>
<p>Saturday 26<br/>played cards all morning &amp; after dinner walked into Tidworth<br/>After Tea for which we paid 1/10 each went to pictures, very poor class.<br/>returned about 10.30</p>
