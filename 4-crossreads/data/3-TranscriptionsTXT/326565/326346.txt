Very severe sandstorm unexpectedly arose about 8 pm &amp; lasted for a couple of hours; during the night it came on again &amp; we had a fine job hanging on to the tent from the inside, trying to prevent it from blowing down. 
 Thursday 13/4/16 A fearful day.  Unable to leave tent without covering your face.  Cooks unable to carry out their work. During the day a number of the tents were blown down
