16/1/19 Brussells
Rested at the hotel for an hour and then bought some tickets for Faust at the Theatre Royal tomorrow night. The old hall porter with whom we booked was detected flagrantly attempting to swindle us out of 10 francs in the change. While waiting for dinner everyone rested in the lounge and fine stylish girls (not quite equal to the petite Parisienne) walked in and out with all the airs and graces so peculiar to their class. We dined in the restaurant quite well for 10 francs being shrewd enough to avoid the dishes pushed by the headwaiter. Walked down the brilliant lighted streets where flashing electric signs pointed out caf and picture show. The latter all showing fine Allied war films ? I see the hand of the propagandist.
