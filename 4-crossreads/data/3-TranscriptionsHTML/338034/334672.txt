We left Bir-el-ab at 10 a.m. on the 18th arriving back at Romani just at dusk.<br/>Sept 21st :- Hind, Martin &amp; myself left Romani 7 a.m. for 48 hours leave to Port Said. Had a good trip but was violently sick on the last night there. Was over the Mooltan on 22nd. <strike>Co</strike> O'Connor &amp; Andy Kerr arrived in P. Said on 22nd. Had some good sailing on the Canal. Arrived back Romani on 23-9-'16.</p>
