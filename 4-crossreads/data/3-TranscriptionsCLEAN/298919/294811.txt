Strolled along Piccadilly (where a lot of the stylish Clubs are) across the Circus (so called because it is Circular) to a restaurant where I had tea.  After satisfying my hunger, strolled down Regent St & went to the West End Kinema.  This though a Picture Palace is much prettier and better decorated than some of our playhouses.  After that returned Ship[?].  Though by myself I enjoyed the afternoon better than most days I have had company, & certainly it was more inexpensive.
