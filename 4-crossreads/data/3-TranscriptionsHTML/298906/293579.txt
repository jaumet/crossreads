<p>won the big fight of the night.<br/>8th. Went for a long march this morning started out at 7.45. &amp; finished 1.15. during this time we had 4 halts only, none being 10 minutes we covered about 16 miles All the hedges round here now are covered with elderberries in places they area just black with the berries.<br/>9th. Woke up this morning to find drizzling rain falling, &amp; it is very little better this afternoon &amp; being Sunday makes it seem all the worse.<br/>10th. A bit of a stink on the early morning parade, my platoon &amp; one of D. Coy's had been playing football &amp; were putting plenty of ginger in to it, but one of the P.T.</p>
