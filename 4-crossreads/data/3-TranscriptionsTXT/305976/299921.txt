Rode over to Bedouin camp with Tom to give our neddys a feed of grass. Would not change my horse now we know one another.Wednesday Oct 3Rumoured big air raid coming to morrow night. Two planes over lines observing to day. Rumoured big stunt on 10th. Hope there is anything is better than this dull monotony.Thursday Oct 4New Zealand mounted band gave us a tune today, usual routine.
