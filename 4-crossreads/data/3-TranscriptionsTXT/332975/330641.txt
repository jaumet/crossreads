 8th &ndash; A splendid Concert &ndash; Row in Boat. 10th Finished Dental treatment one tooth out &ndash; another  tooth  swim. 11, 12 &amp; 13 &ndash; Wandering about. 13 - Put in for 14/-.  R. Ackett Discharged . 14 - Received pay &ndash; discharge B Duties. On 16th arrived at Cairo 4 o'clock.  Taken to Overseas Base Ghezia.  Went to Aerodrome Camp to see about mail. 17th &ndash; Got up 6-30 fell in names taken had breakfast, fell in names taken about twenty different times then dismissed.  Marched to A details camp. 18th Taken to Aerodrome Camp back with same reinforcements met Alick &amp; Murphy. 19th &ndash; Rained all night wet to skin. 20th &ndash; Paid 10/3.  Sent home silks.  Given Sergents Orderly Job, had Photo taken with Alick &amp; Murphy 25 piasters.  Wrote Dolly Ralton. 23rd &ndash; Very warm. 25th Native funeral &ndash; Washing day.  Rumors we are going to be transported on Monday morning.  Experience in Cairo.
