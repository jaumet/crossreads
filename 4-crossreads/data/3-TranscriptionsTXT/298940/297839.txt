  12th Feb. 1918 Tuesday  Vic Hall &amp; I are going to start a literary society amongst just a few of us with the idea of getting a library of good books together at a small cost to each Discussions etc. will follow later on. It is a first rate idea, &amp; I shall follow it up with zeal. We went for a short walk before tea today, &amp; discussed Wells, Ibsen &amp; Shakespeare's Macbeth.
