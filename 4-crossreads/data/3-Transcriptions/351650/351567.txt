<p>South Australian Hotel<br/>North Terrace,<br/>Adelaide</p>
<p>1 October 1915</p>
<p>Dear Mrs John</p>
<p>Thankyou for the pen and clinical thermometer you sent me &ndash; they will see pretty continuous use after they arrive in England with me.</p>
<p>I should be honored to write you a letter occasionally, and will do so if you do not object.</p>
