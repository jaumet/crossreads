 July 1 there are one or two aristo's &amp; a few of the  idle  rich. Noticeable are several invalids some in bath chairs, others hobbling along on sticks or crutches. A rather ferocious bespectacled grey-haired lady keeps her eyes glued on the Prom' &amp; doubtless sums everybody up! A young lady sews "industriously' &amp;  methinks  smiles not a little - at yours truly I hope?
