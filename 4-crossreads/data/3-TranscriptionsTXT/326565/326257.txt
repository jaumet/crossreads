All received medical attention during afternoon.  Getting fairly well known on board through being continually on duty at the dispensary.  Very heavy wind &amp; rain storm at night. 
 Thursday 2/12/15 Fairly strong wind blowing though sea fairly calm.  Getting very tired of ship's food, cannot enjoy meals at all lately, will be glad to get on shore to have a good meal.  Canteen stores getting very low, unable to buy any tinned fruits or biscuits at all.
