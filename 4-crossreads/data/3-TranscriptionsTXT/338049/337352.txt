 Wednesday 22  Things as usual but a  Up on racecourse to try Grey with Sapper Dodd up. I was on Hopkins Darky which cast a shoe. Milly too strong for Dodd. Had Darky reshod. 
 After to Races (8th Bde) &amp; watched couple of races &amp; met Jimmy Force. O.W.C. Then 4 p.m. to Abbeville &amp; knocked about enjoyably. 
 Hot but thunderstorm about 6 p.m. Very heavy rain for &frac12; hr.
