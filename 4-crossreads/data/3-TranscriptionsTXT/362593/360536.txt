
Saturday 6 April 1918Trip to Keikit [?] Deran in our 2E Pilot to drop a message, pilot Lt. Murphy, in air 40 m.
Sunday 7 April 1918Letters from Home.On photos, with Addie.  In air 2 h. 5 m., did not make a very good job of them, my first trip with large camera, am going out again tomorrow and will make a better job of it.
