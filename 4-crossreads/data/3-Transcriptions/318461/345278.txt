<p>swooping up again firing all the time there must have been about 10 or 12 of them but how many of each could not tell, it was too quick &amp; exciting, There seems little danger of these new monsters falling they fly anyhow &amp; at any angle. come to think of it, its a wonder the two of us weren't hit as we were just under them, but you don't think of these things till after.<br/>Am hoping to be named to go to blighty today some went last Sunday<br/>suppose tonight will be a repetition of last sundays straffing. Must have been a hop over on our left last night a barrage was put up about 8 P.M.<br/>Pay. 20/1 &pound;25.15.0</p>
<p>Monday 21/5/17<br/>dull &amp; like rain<br/>fairly quiet last night, can't make it out something is in the wind, must be a big attack our Planes very busy.<br/>Some of our chaps coming out tonight.<br/>Pay. 21/1 &pound;25.16.0.<br/>Been quiet all day except for some Artillery exchanges now 6.30 pm.<br/>Brigade goes out tomorrow, thank heavens.<br/>Been playing Patience these last two days nothing to do &amp; all day to do it in bit different last week. Expect we have a nice old tramp in front of us.<br/>Tuesday 22/5/17<br/>Rained all night, heavy firing on Both sides think it another hop over. Go out to night.<br/>Pay. 22/1. &pound;25.17.0.<br/>only a rumour about going out, coming out of Trenches but only into reserves, more rain coming ,dull.</p>
