<p>30:  Spent afternoon with friends at Heliopolis &ndash; Diner at Zietoun.  Had a fine time, my last night in town.<br/>31:  After a fine holiday, left Cairo at 11 a.m., transferring to trucks east of Canal at Kantara 6 p.m., spent a miserable night travelling &ndash; Bed very hard after Cairo hotels.</p>
<p>February</p>
<p>1:  Reached Richon 5 p.m., Brigade camping area, having returned from the line yesterday, after a month's hard graft.</p>
<p>5:  Evening at the Mayor's home &ndash; Messr. Bendsiff &ndash; had a fine evening.</p>
<p>9:  Birthday party at Richon, celebrating S.M. Tancred"s (1st rgt.) 27 birthday &ndash; also one of the Mayor's daughters natal day.  Had good food dance &amp; music, returning after midnight.</p>
<p>12:  Measles in unit, one of our latest men taken ill, camp moved from Brigade area &amp; everyone quarantined on the sand.  During the afternoon one of our boys went for water to the canteen &amp; was fined &#163;2 by C.O., but our officers left the quarantine camp riding the N.Z. sports &amp; nothing was said regarding their actions &ndash; Boys fed up with rank treatment.</p>
