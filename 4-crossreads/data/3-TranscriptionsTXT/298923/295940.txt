
1 October 1918 Tuesday (X to X crossed out)Big Australian Mail in &X Mother & Day July 21, 28, 31                                Aug 6 & 11Also from May, H.L.A. & many others such a treat.  All of love Aug 4   please thank everybody.  Very busy today- read between halts- Also one from Miss Mack & Lorna-  Am sorry poor old Day is again without a maid too much altogether- hope you let the house. X Events happening very fast more prisoners X than we can handle, X Aust Div. is around Damascus & we expect it to surrender hourly -  Laura writes Joe is on the land!!
