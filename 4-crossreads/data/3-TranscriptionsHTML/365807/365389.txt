<p>April 24th 1917</p>
<p>Dear Sir,</p>
<p>I was very glad to receive your letter today, as I promised Lt Fergusson that I would write to you &amp; give technical details of his wound. Unfortunately, your telegram containing your address was lost, &amp; I have therefore not been able to write till now.</p>
<p>The military authorities will not allow any medical details to be sent by telegram to relatives at home. This will explain why my wire to you was so short.</p>
<p>Fergusson was wounded by a bullet passing through his R. arm; I think on Mar 31st; while waiting to be brought down he was struck by a small piece of shell on the left side of the head.
