<p>1915<br/>Apr. 5 Mon.<br/>Dust storm blowing pretty hard.<br/>Troops still moving off &ndash; Attended a meeting of the A.S.A. in the Adelaide Y.M.C.A. but only two turned up; there are not a dozen of our members left at Mena.<br/>Plenty of flies in the daytime &amp; stacks of mosquitos at night.</p>
