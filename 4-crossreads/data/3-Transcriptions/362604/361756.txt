I have not been to Salisbury lately for one of my Patients died of meningitis &amp; I was loth to visit my Friends until I was sure that I was not a carrier.  It is the first case I've ever had.  I met an English officer last week while out walking.  I am going over to see Him to-night at His Home in the Village.</p>
