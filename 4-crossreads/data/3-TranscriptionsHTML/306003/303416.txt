<p>3-7-16<br/>would compete for the middleweight &amp; also found that a featherweight, a lightweight &amp; a heavy weight would also box in their class;  James then went to the Brigadier who promised him he would call a meeting of the officers &amp; see if they would subscribe to trophies for the winners in each class &amp; it now looks as though the tournaments are likely to come off shortly as the Brig. said he would do all in his power towards it;  Several lads of the 3rd Regt have also promised to have a go for the Championship.</p>
<p>James came over to this Regt tonight to see the Adjutant in reference to the boxing &amp; it was decided that I am to receive nominations &amp; look after anything in reference to it in this Regt.  he was delighted at the willing manner in which Major Granville &amp; Capt Weir agreed to all of his suggestions &amp; I have had orders to supply him with  a horse anytime he requires one</p>
