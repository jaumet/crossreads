<p>standing 5 feet high. Tremendous shell &amp; mine craters alround &amp; the line of the German Trenches almost indistinguishable. It seems impossible to have lived through such an upheaval. Although all the German Dead had been put into shell holes &amp; covered over &ndash; yet the smell was very bad.</p>
<p>Watched two anti aircraft guns firing at a Taube &amp; they got it. Must have hit the petrol tank for it caught fire &amp; came down looking somewhat like a falling rocket or shot pigeon. Fell I believe just outside our present</p>
