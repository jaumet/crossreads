
BASE DEPOT LE HAVRE[31st Oct. contd]Marched to camp feeling very tired & hungry & on arrival at Reception Room we obtained slice of bread & dripping & cup of tea.  Cardinal [Francis] Bourne arrived in Camp to the tune of an Anzac Band soon afterwards.  After an hour or so we arrived at our AMC Details Camp & was allotted our quarters.  It is the worst camp I have seen for a long time & the surrounding grounds are an ocean of mud.  Met Plumb of Leichhardt suffering from heart & eye trouble (from 11th Fld Amb) & chatted over local affairs.  On settling down was surprised to meet Gordon Dawkins of Water Board & also above all chaps, Dick Lamars [?] my old cobber attached to Engineers Reinfs.  Slept well.
NOVEMBERThursday 1stIn morning was surprised to meet 'Nipper Swinnerton Bert Crook & Joe Cooper (from Accounts Branch).  Capt Birkett also popped in to see me this night.  In addition  Dvr [?] Davidson & Stet Jones are here in Camp.  Was medically examined this morning & marked A.
