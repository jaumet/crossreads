Warloy 29/6/17Plenty of amusement for the boys lately.  The Aust. Comforts Fund has provided a cinematography show and it he shows every evening in the town for the Australian Troops billeted here.  The 5th Div. Concert Party called "The Anzac Coves" also gave a bonser show last night in the Y.M.C.A.
