<p>Saturday 22nd<br/>Medical Inspection as we go on leave tomorrow. Thawed last night after being frozen all week</p>
<p>Sunday 23rd<br/>Roused out at 4 a.m. Quite a mild morning Arrived Padington about  10 &amp; had an excelent  lunch at a YM for 1/- After considerable hunting found my Aunt Susies place</p>
<p>Monday 24<br/>Saw a good deal of the Town Lunched at New Palace Hotel Could not get into a Theater so spent some time in Picture Show</p>
<p>Tuesday 25th<br/>Went to St Pauls &amp; several other places Lunched at the "Trocadro" some place many courses but not much to eat</p>
<p>Wednesday 26th<br/>Wandered around considerably &amp; saw many &amp; various places Aunt stayed at home</p>
<p>Thursday 27th<br/>Westminster Abby a musty old place but interesting. The Curate who acted as our guide</p>
