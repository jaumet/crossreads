<p>30 h. Tuesday May<br/>Kit inspection. Letter writing etc. Truck fatigue at night</p>
<p>31st Wednesday<br/>Dull weather. Mail in &amp; letter writing. Much airial activity.</p>
<p>June 1916<br/>1st. Thursday.<br/>Lovely day. Fritz shelling our batteries nearby also Armontieres. Also got onto our fatigue party &amp; caught several.</p>
<p>2nd. Friday.<br/>Nice day. letterwriting etc. Trenches on fatigue at night.</p>
<p>3rd. Saturday.<br/>Nice quiet day. Packed up belongings &amp; relieved 19th. Battn in Support Trenches. Plenty of fatigue here, Engineering by day &amp; ration</p>
