While on this classification I went up with O.T.C. forms & he refused (Capt Myers) to sign them till the rib was healed. Two days afterwards I tore off the strapping & again paraded with the forms.  This time he discovered lung trouble (imaginary) at the posterior right base.  A sputum test was taken & the glorious old suspense business began in real earnest.
