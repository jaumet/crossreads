<p>Jly 19 to mention the changing of the guard at the Palace, &amp; the Scots Guards Band, &amp; the goosestep. Some soldiers &ndash; What?<br/>Guess Phyl &amp; Mary seem nicer every time I see them. Came home at 5, &amp; after tea went for a nice long walk round the Common.</p>
