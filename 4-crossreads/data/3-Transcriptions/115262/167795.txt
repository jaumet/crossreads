<p>Colombo. has a fine rich appearance as we saw it. Cocoanut &amp; other tropical fruit palms give the place a fresh rich dark green appearance. Coaling is the chief thing here and coolied work in swarms at it. No wharf of any description here.</p>
<p>A fine breakwater harbor. On hill near entrance there is a fort with long distance guns and several others screened by palms.</p>
