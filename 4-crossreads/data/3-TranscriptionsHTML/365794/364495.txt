<p>Wednesday 4 [3] April 1917 [1918]<br/>Kind letters from S's Leed &amp; Peile &amp; flowers from Miss Devonshire.  Lovely red roses that last for days &amp; give one hope of some day seeing one's own garden again.<br/>Visited by our C of E Padre from the 27th &amp; very kind he is.</p>
