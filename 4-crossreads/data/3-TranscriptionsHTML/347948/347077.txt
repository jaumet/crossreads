<p>28th  To night I have seen the most glorious sunset of my life.  Picture the grim lines of a cruiser silhouetted against the grey murky background of the sea &amp; above a dark bank of cloud tinged with heliotrope, dying into what looked like a lake of the most glorious azure such as was never given to man</p>
