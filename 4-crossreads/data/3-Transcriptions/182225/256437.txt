<p>NOV. &nbsp; TUESDAY 14 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine but very dull &amp; foggy. We took the horses out for exercise in the morning &amp; gave them an hours grazing. Cleaning harness all the afternoon &amp; Poss &amp; I &amp; George Holloway went to the pictures at night, but they were not up to much.</p>
