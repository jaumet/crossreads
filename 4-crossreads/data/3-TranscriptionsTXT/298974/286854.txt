The following day we spent keeping our heads well down, one man observing, &amp; widening the trench, as much as we could, without making a commotion. Next night it snowed like mad; a tearing wind turned it into an icy blizzard, but we had to take our turn on watch and dig like mad just the same. Some of us were sent out a bit to put up some barbed wire. I can tell you it was just about as much as we could stand by 4 in the morning. I could hardly feel any part of my body, &amp; digging was a farce. Then the rum issue came, &amp; made us all as warm as if by a fire, &amp; put new life into us. But some just threw themselves on the floor of the shallow trench, &amp; took no notice of the snow, just fell asleep out of sheer exhaustion.
