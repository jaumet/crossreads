<p>Well dear old friend I only intended to write a few lines but here I am on the 4th page &ndash;</p>
<p>Give our kind regards to Mr. &amp; Mrs. Fred also to Your husband Is Mrs. Weaber with you? if so please convey our deep sympathy to her. Goodbye &amp; I hope soon to hear from you &amp; be of good cheer Mrs. Bilney its such a hard blow but you can console yourself with the thought that he gave his life in a great cause.</p>
<p>I remain<br/>Your loving friend<br/>Rhoda V. Blood.</p>
