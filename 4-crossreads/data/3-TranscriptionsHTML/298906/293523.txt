<p>their money, they make straight for the beer, &amp; here they are tonight arguing &amp; sprawling all over the lines, I am either missing a glorious time as they call it or a damn fool, &amp; of the two I am quite satisfied to remain the latter, for the more I see of drink the more I am predjuiced against the cursed stuff, it has ruined many of our lads that I know of to my own knowledge. As I was walking across the parade ground this evening I picked up 7 plugs of gelignite nice stuff to have lying about.<br/>5th. Jock &amp; I went to Church this morning by way of a change. Hanged if I can get any mail to speak of since leaving the Battn. I have wrote to Horsferry</p>
