<p>&amp; Officers in military life just at present.</p>
<p>I'm afraid I shall not be able to send home many novelties now, we are not near the tempting shops.  This is a very quiet place &amp; nothing worth buying here.  Did I tell you Fred had an invitation to Eileen Grist's wedding.</p>
<p>Lucy wrote saying Thirza &amp; Ian were to be train-bearers.  Seemed to be great excitement.  He is a W.A. boy.  I daresay Al. will know him, Harold Hassell.  Lucy does not like Mrs Ma-in-law.</p>
<p>Sorry I cannot write much news but am on my honor.</p>
<p>I am very well &amp; happy.  So pleased to know Ethel &amp; Eddy are well again.</p>
