<p>July 4th<br/>Diddler [nickname for Captain Wilson] is out holding on a village with a N.Z. Sgt.  Got wind today that the Kurds are going to raid the place.  Diddler's people (12 Kiwis) reckon they will fight so Starnes sent out another Sgt.</p>
<p>Mail today, 39 letters &amp; 1 parcel.  Good O.  This is the first mail yet received which Fisher brought back from Hamadan.</p>
<p>July 5th<br/>Dead  tired.  The Gov. Sirdar, Com. of Police, Com of Postal Services, Com of Telegraph Service &amp; others came along to dinner.  The Sidah provided the goods, for which we paid.  The meal commenced at 9 pm. &amp; went on with intervals until 3 am</p>
