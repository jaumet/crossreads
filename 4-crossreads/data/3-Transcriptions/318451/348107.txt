<p>As I have not been able to keep a daily record of events while at Alquines, I will set down a general account of our stay.  The past ten days has been a time of very solid training with a march of about 3 miles every day to the parade ground.  Men have been instructed in Bomb throwing.  Every man threw live bombs, fired Rifle grenades &amp; most of them Lewis guns also, besides going through a course of musketry.  Then followed Battalion drill &amp; Brigade drill in preparation for "going over the top".  Each Batt. &amp; each Coy. in the Battn. had its place allotted &amp; the practice was very satisfactory.<br/>A holiday was given the men on Tuesday last.
