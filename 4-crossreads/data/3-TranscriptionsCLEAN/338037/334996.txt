
3.6.16Postmark dated 5 JU 16Addressed toMr. Frank Ryan77 Clarenden StNorthcoteMelbourne
Dear FrankA view of the beach from the [indecipherable] is not visible form here. Could see shells bursting day and night on Cape Tekke . note the dugouts, and stacks of stores. This southern part of Anzac is called Hells Spit and it is a very appropriate name.Drop a line sometimes please.Yours sincerelyA Pyke
