  
 [Easter Monday 24 April 1916] 
 Court all day today as  all evidence   Evidence is given through an Interpreter HINDUSTANI & again sometimes in ARABIC with the Native witnesses it is a long tedious case. Did I tell you it was a case of a Sikh soldier murdering his Native Officer shot him in the night while asleep but evidence is difficult to get, & the Witnesses swear Black is White & vice versa.
