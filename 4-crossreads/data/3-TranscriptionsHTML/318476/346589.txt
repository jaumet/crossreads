<p>May 8  1st Light Horse Brigade leaves Cairo.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    9  Arrive Alexandria.<br/>Exchange leggings for puttees on the warf, and thus miraculously transformed into infantry embark on SS Nevahna &amp; sail at 6.30 pm<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  11 Arrive Dardanelles.   Light rain.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  12 Disembark.  Spend night in reserve trenches.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  13 At 12 noon we take over Quinns Post from 15th Bat.   Knowing that we are new&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  arrivals Johnny Turk kids to be busy and shows a lot of fixed bayonets above his parapet.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;We fix ours so he takes his off again and cools down.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  14 Enemy bombs very persistent.   Stretcher bearers busy all day.   15th Bat. returns in evening &amp; 2nd Regt, except our sqdn goes down the gully.</p>
