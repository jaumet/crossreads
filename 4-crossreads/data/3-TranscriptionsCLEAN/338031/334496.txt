Bareheaded there in the presence of the battalion he had disgraced the prisoner stood to attention, while the officer read out the proceedings & decision of the Court Martial, confirmed by General Sir Newton Moore. Sentences from six months to five years hard labour were awarded for desertion, being absent without leave when warned for draft, assaulting an officer or N.C.O. and other serious crimes.
