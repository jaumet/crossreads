
This we are told is the last time General Allenby will inspect our Division. We have had the Odds On & Brigade Concert party of an evening and on the whole having a good time. The 1914 men have left for home & it is expected we will get away in the New Year.Sunday Dec 1We were inspected by Gen Chaytor the Div General to day.
