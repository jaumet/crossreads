
9th M.G. Coy No 15Intelligence Report from 6 am 14th to 6 am 15th:-Right Sector:- No 5 gun fired - indirect - from I 12 Central to I 12 d 0/7 X Roads, Trenches, etc. at irregular intervals from 9.45 to 12.15. Result unknown.No 6 fired from I 10 b 2/1 to I 17 a 8/4 to I 18 c 3/0 Road from LA BLEUE to BASTROU, irregularly from midnight to 2.30 am.No damage done to M.G. Positions during enemy bombardment. Enemy M.G's active during night on front line.Left sector:- No 7 (S.P.X.) fired indirect on I 6 a 5/0 Road and dump at INTACT FARM. (from 5 to 11 pm) Result unobserved. Day was dull & hazy, early evening and night drizzly & dark, morning, moonlight  Wind light.
B. J Carey Capt9th M.G. Coy15.12.16
