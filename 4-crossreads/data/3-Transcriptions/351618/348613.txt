<p>All day it blew a strong Nor' Westerly and cold with it &amp; plenty of dust. I had several trips to for timber etc to Stores</p>
<p>Thursday 2nd September<br/>I was bad during the night and had to go to the rear on several occasions. Although it was midnight &amp; later had company on nearly every occasion. Things were still a bit quiet during the day.</p>
<p>Friday 3rd September<br/>The weather nice &amp; crisp with a cool breeze. Getting the store rebuilt a little in case of Winter protection &ndash; a quiet day. Went to Ordnance for clothing etc.</p>
