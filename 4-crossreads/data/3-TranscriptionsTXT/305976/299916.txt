Arrived section 7 pm reported & Joe Fraser returned back to unit with Camels.Saturday Sept 15Our new unit resting right on beach. Met Percy Knight. Were allotted our horses & gave them a swim.Sunday Sept 16Rumoured two more divisions arrived from Salonica.Monday Sept 17New Camp situated on beach some 2 miles below Marakat with the Blue Mediterranian to bathe in. Swim Horses every morning while Anzac Division resting.
