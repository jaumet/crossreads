<p>History of &quot;Boorara&quot;</p>
<p>The &quot;Boorara&quot; H.M.A.T. (A42.) is a captured German boat &amp; was used at Dardanelles as supply ship.</p>
<p>Had a shot fired across her bows at Port Phillip heads. Her German name was &quot;Pflaz&quot; [S.S. Pfalz] &amp; was rammed at Dardanelles by French cruiser Bov&ecirc;t&nbsp;in a fog&nbsp;(since sunk). In recognition of good work in reserving she was presented&nbsp;<strike>with</strike> by H.M.S. Triumph with their flag the Union Jack.</p>
<p>Pfalz was safely towed with rent 27&#39; x 28&#39;. Transport work since &amp; once</p>
