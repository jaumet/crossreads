 But Look! a wisp of smoke goes curling high, Towards the glorious sun which gave it life, And bears aloft the spirit ne'er to die, And triumphs still o'er season's bitter strife. 
 O noble life, so handsome and so brave, That cheered less happy fellows with a smile, Till duty's finger pointed o'er the wave, And pangs of parting gripped his heart the while
