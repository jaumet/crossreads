We have received no newspapers since Saturday.  There is all sorts of rumours about, one of them being that Germany is asking for an armistice. 
 Wednesday Feb. 21. 1917 A dull misty day but much finer than yesterday especially in the afternoon. To day I was doing odd jobs and helping to repair a bridge and make approaches to it.  I believe the whole Battalion is to be removed from this front very shortly, in fact the Transports have received orders to be in readiness to move at any time.  We are also to get leave very shortly.
