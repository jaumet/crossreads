<p>in B Tent Sub) &amp; I went for a walk along the canal. I was struck by the terrific desolation of the city. It is not shelled nor bombed now; but all that can be seen is the preoccupied military life. The civilian life is dead except for the very infrequent old men &amp; old women one sees outside old houses doing nothing. <strike>Others</strike> The desolation of the town, which is of a great age, is impossible to be described.
