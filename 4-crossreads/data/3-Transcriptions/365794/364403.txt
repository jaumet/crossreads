<p>Wednesday 3 January 1917 [1918]<br/>&frac12; day &amp; went town &amp; to the Museum &amp; saw Queen's jewels then shopped &amp; home 7 p.m.  Wards very quiet &ndash; only 34 pts. in and all very light duty in the Hosp.<br/>Left silver watch for repairs (Pt. 30).</p>
