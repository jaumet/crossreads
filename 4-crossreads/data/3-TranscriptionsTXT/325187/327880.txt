
[Wednesday 17 May 1916]
Signaller shot today   Scattered Turks all around- 120  in the shade 1 gallon Water per man-160 Camels arrived to take us onNo more roadsNo more RailwayNo more Pipe LineOnly wells & brackish water60 men Sunstroke20 more sicksurvival of the fittest now, I prefer the heat but I like water.  The unfit will go to the Wall out here- 2 Camels & 3 horses died -coming out
