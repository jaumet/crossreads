<p>Friday 9th<br/>The relief of the Division commenced today. An english division is coming in today Our Brigade will be relieved by them on the 10/11th &amp; after a day or so here we are going back for a rest.<br/>Last night was very cold &amp; wet. Today is fine, &amp; not so cold</p>
