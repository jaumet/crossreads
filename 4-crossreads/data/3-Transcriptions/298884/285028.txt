<p>This immediately put everybody in a fearfully bad humour &amp; I received some of the backwash of the flood of indignation from the men when I went below to examine their food. They were nearly as savage as I was. From our anchorage here we can see the Manly ferries dashing to &amp; fro with their loads of summer<strike>clad</strike> dress clad trippers waving &amp; yelling.  At various periods all day until late at night boats containing relatives of chaps on board came along &amp; created a bit of a diversion for the fed up troops.</p>
<p>Manly. Rose Bay. Mossman</p>
