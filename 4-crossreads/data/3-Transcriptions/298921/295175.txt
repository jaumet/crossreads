<p>July, 1915<br/>Friday 23<br/>Again the rumour that I am to go to the front &ndash; so banked <strike>15/-/-</strike> Anglo Egyptian BK [indecipherable] money &amp; packed up Kit again &ndash; nails in my boots &amp; hung round like Father Xmas &ndash; with equipment I believe 40/-/- has been spent by Red X on mosquitoe Nets for Gaba as the <u>flies</u> are very bad indeed &amp; after feeding on dead Turks not nice to think of but WAR is not over nice at any time but has a great fascination.</p>
