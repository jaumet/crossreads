<p>A long &amp; miserable march under wretched conditions &ndash; We pass through Meaulte, Becordel. Fricourt. Mametz, and pass the night in the last named.</p>
<p>Dec. 23rd.<br/>Reveille 3.45a.m &ndash; We march off in the dark through rain, mud, slush on a road half under water &amp; stray mettle &ndash; After a solid march we arrive at a miserable spot in Bannefay Wood &ndash; Take up duties in A.Div. Much improved service directly behind the Line light railways.</p>
<p>Dec. 24th.<br/>Xmas Eve &ndash; A fine day but cold &ndash; distribution of Gifts - Taubes once more &ndash; Application for new position &ndash; News of Eric Gordon. A midnight meal.</p>
