
24 December, Wednesday
Not very busy. I buy wines & send Home. We catch 10.10 tram & after good journey although that & heavy job parcel carrying we reach Forest Home. Bed at 2 am & up at 5 am. We have nice merry day.
25 December, Thursday
Up at 5 am & spend a Merry X'mas.
Plenty of music & merry arie.
