 of the doctor. xxxxxx When the doctor came to me, he inspected the foot &amp; pronounced it not yet healthy;  then by way of talking of Australia (he is an Australian) he asked me if I knew Southport. That is a town I have never visited.  I have passed through it by train, nay more, for the train changes its direction there &amp; there is a halt for refreshments. A nice clean bushy seaside town, with the beautiful Pacific washing its shores &amp; health giving tonic in its air. Of course I knew. Then Toowoomba, did I know that town.
