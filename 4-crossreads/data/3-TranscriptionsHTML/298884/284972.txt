<p>At 11A.M. the rain fell in sheets.</p>
<p><u>25th</u>  We reached Colombo &amp; dropped anchor in the harbour about 3.30p.m. Much speculation as to whether we will be allowed ashore or not.</p>
<p>6 P.M. Instructions received from the shore authorities to the effect that all troops may go off our boat but that the town must be strongly picketed.</p>
<p><u>26th</u>  All Companies fell in on their Parade decks at 7.30 A.M. Picket parties were told off &amp; we <strike>went</strike> filed on to barges.  At 9.15 A.M. a small tug pulled the whole bulk of the 6 huge barges</p>
