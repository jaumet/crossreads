<p>At Port Said we expect to get mail which has been posted in England for us up to 17th Inst</p>
<p>May 25th &ndash; 19<br/>At 5-45 yesterday morning we were awakened by the old familiar lingo of Gippo land and found we had arrived at Port Said.</p>
<p>We dropped anchor for the day while fresh stores were taken aboard but we failed to get coal as the niggers were on strike.</p>
