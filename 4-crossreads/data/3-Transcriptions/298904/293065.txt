<p>While we were down on the range one of our chaps killed a small death adder, you could hardly notice the adder even when he was dead he was the exact colour of the sand.<br/>The Colonel gave us a lecture here one day as to how we should behave when we got to France, &amp; everyone was as pleased as Punch for this was a pretty good hint as to where we were bound for, we were about fed up with Egypt &amp; everything connected with it &amp; all were longing to get to France &amp; have a go at the sausage eaters our real enemy we done our work with a much lighter heart after this, for all were looking forward to getting away</p>
