 Sig Troop.  The heavy rain has swollen the river & it is overflowing its banks.  There is a rush & tear for the next 1/2 hour saddling up in the dark up to our knees in water.  We stand by for an hour whilst another position is found for us.  This is in a lane further up the road.  I push open a gate in the lane & discover a garden with trees, at the back of a house.  Whose it is I neither know nor care.  I lead the old nag through the gate, tether her & crawl under  the  my blanket, just about knocked out. 
 5 th  October to  18 th   24 th
