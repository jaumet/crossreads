 8 February, Saturday 
 F &amp; ice We both go down to Dance &amp; I have a good time. I scale Taney for the Maxima but fix things up. We make arrangements about a towel exchange. I am improving on my dancing &amp; reverse rather better. I hop in to go Home with &amp; M 
 9 February, Sunday 
 F &amp; ice Work hard all day, go up for Tea to see Doris &amp; other W.A.A.C.  for Tea. I go down to Y.M.C.A. a bit late &amp; have to sit with Dick &amp; others until 8 pm &amp; then I go to sit with Q. M. We have a confidential chat &amp; part good chums at the out of Bounds notice. Music good &amp; enjoy myself.
