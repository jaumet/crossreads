<p>The Australian Museum,<br/>Sydney, 1st. May, 1914.</p>
<p>This is to certify that Mr. Dene B. Fry has been in the service of the Trustees of the Australian Museum for 6 and &frac12; years as a Cadet &amp; Junior Assistant in the sections of Invertebrate Zoology and of Reptiles, Snakes and Fishes, during which period he has had opportunities of making himself proficient in the subjects.</p>
<p>He has passed his professional examinations in Zoology at the Technical College &amp; the University with honours and performed his Museum duties to the entire satisfaction of his Superior Officers.</p>
<p>H. H. B. Bradley<br/>President</p>
<p>R. Etheridge<br/>Curator</p>
<p>S. Sinclair<br/>Secretary.</p>
