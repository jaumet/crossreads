<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Flag</p>
<p>To - Berrima&nbsp;</p>
<p>Date - 12-9-14</p>
<p>Time - 4.25</p>
<p>Signalling was observed last night from mount Towanunbati the high peak to North of Rabaul. &nbsp;Arms and ammunition found in the lighters in port have been removed. &nbsp;The lighters containing coal are moored between the piers. &nbsp;A kite apparently fitted for wireless purposes was sent up this morning close to sheds flying Japanese flags bearing North. &nbsp;The kite in question appeared to be 50 feet long and 8 men were employed in handling it (1618)&nbsp;</p>
