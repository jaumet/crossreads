<p>plenty of fruit from the native "Bum boats", very hot &amp; sultry.  The town is small but prettily situated at the foot of some fine hills.</p>
<p>March 19th<br/>Pulled out at 5 p.m. &amp; continued our so far very pleasant trip.</p>
<p>April 1st<br/>Ships fired big guns.  One S.A. boy effected by firing.</p>
<p>April 3rd<br/>South African died.</p>
<p>April 4th<br/>Dead man buried at 9.30 a.m.</p>
<p>April 5th<br/>Am Orderly Sgt.</p>
<p>April 11th<br/>Saw the Southern X for the first time.  Sighted 4 ships to N. at 6 p.m.  Lost them at dusk.</p>
