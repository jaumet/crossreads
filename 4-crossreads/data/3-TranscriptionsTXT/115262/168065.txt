 Thursday 25 October 1917. 
 Awaiting in case of an enemy attack at &quot;Stand to&quot; 
  On watering  Watered camels. 
 Saw families of Bedouins with their flocks and herds watering at the Waddy. A picturesque sight undoubtedly. 
 A mail as late as Sept 3 rd  to hand. 8 letters I received. 
  Malgo  Maalaga was the watering place. 
 Wrote Home to Joy.
