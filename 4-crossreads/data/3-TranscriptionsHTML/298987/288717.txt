<p>Wednesday 5th<br/>Nothing of importance to report</p>
<p>Thursday 6th<br/>A fresh party arrived yesterday to take our job as most of us have not completed our training. Our Officer was late starting so we just missed the 9 oclock train &amp; had to wait untill 12 arrived Sutton Verry about 3 and got settled in our own quarters Heavy frost this morning</p>
<p>Friday 7<br/>Parade morning M.O. &amp; Dental inspection afternoon</p>
<p>Saturday 8<br/>Scrubbing Mess Huts</p>
<p>Sunday 9th<br/>Church Parade Hall &amp; I went for a walk around Warminster</p>
<p>Monday 10th<br/>Miniture Range shooting. Had a Night Stunt out about 2 hours Spent most of the time waiting for something or someone fortunately it was not cold</p>
