<p>29-1-16<br/>Sat 29th<br/>Had a kit inspection this morning &amp; had to put in for any shortages, as my black kit bag is still missing I had to put in for a new rig-out;This afternoon the 1st Regt. played the 2nd Regt. Football, &amp; we won by 11 points to nil. After tea we commenced boxing &amp; some good bouts were put up till it got too dark to see.</p>
<p>30-1-16<br/>Sun 30th<br/>I cleaned the Sig. gear this morning instead of going to Church.
