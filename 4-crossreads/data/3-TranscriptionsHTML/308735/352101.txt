<p>dropped on one of our ammo dumps (made a great amount of flame &amp; Smoke.</p>
<p>Thursday May 23rd<br/>Had a shoot during morning.  After tea had to go and cut logs in the woods, then carried them back to guns.  9 p.m. had a shoot.</p>
<p>Friday May 24th<br/>Rained all day.  Amused myself making a few things from shell casings &amp; bullets.</p>
<p>Saturday May 25th<br/>On S.O.S. 4.30 to 6 a.m.  Things very quiet along the line.  Had new glasses put in my gas mask.  After dinner had to dig a trench behind our gun's pit (for more protection).</p>
<p>Sunday May 26th<br/>Was awakened early with gas in our dugout.  9.30 parade.  Finished the trench behind the gun.  9.30 to 11 p.m. on S.O.S.  While on duty saw a German dump blown up by one of our planes.</p>
<p>Monday May 27th<br/>Roused out of bed at 2.30 a.m. to do a shoot, lasted until &frac12; past 4.  fired 130 rounds.  After we had cleaned the gun we had breakfast then went to bed until 12 mid-day.  Had to get out of bed 10-30 and go to the rear position for the clerk (gave him my blessing?).</p>
<p>Tuesday May 28th<br/>Out of bed again 2.30 a.m. to do a shoot, had another shoot 2.30 p.m. and 4 p.m.  Saw a good box-on between our planes &amp; Fritz's (about 16 of them, very high up) don't know who won.</p>
<p>Wednesday May 29th<br/>Fritz still keeps up his shelling on our right.</p>
<p>Thursday May 30th<br/>Had a good unbroken nights sleep.  Old Fritz</p>
