<p>[4th Sept 1916]<br/>I fulfilled the first requirements &amp; six others were selected including Jimmy Hyde, both of whom were in the dispensary at Marseilles.<br/>Given charge of party by seniority under Capt Rhodes also travelling with us to No 2 Command Depot, Weymouth.<br/>Went by 12-10 pm train via Andover &amp; Southampton West, at both of which places we changed.<br/>Arrived at Weymouth at 4 pm &amp; after 2&frac12; miles march, reached the camp which is at Monte Video out near the sea west of Portland Bill, at tea time.</p>
