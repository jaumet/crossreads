 Well &ndash; its war time &ndash; Something may have been done &ndash; They've had from Sept to now to get beds up &ndash; Why the devil they haven't done it nicks me &ndash; My men want to sling their 'ammicks there &amp; asked me but I've no authority  off  a ship &ndash; I told Commander D. &amp; he said lying on the floor alone wd make him ill &ndash; His costume is a singlet &amp; a Rami &ndash; His navg lieut &ndash; wears a Rami only &ndash; I believe Rami is feminine &amp; Lava Lava is the male garment but it matters not. Sports this aft &ndash; I'm going so I will spell on - I went &ndash; The sports were held at No 2 pier &ndash; What spoilt the effect was that at least &frac14; of the men were nude &ndash; now 'lava lava' costume was the one thing needed &amp; it should not have been omitted &ndash; The Administrator (Pethbridge) was present &ndash; I stayed some time &ndash; Swimming &ndash; Catamaran racing &ndash; Our men in our whaler won the oar contest by 4 lengths &ndash; This is due as I tell Fitz to physical drill &amp; constant exercise &ndash; Keep it going I say &ndash;
