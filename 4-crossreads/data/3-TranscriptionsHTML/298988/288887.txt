<p>8 p.m.  Got wireless news that the ship sank by the submarine this morning was the Armenian &amp; that some of the crew were drowned.</p>
<p>We get press news every night from Eiffel Tower.</p>
<p>July 2nd  4.30 p.m.  The "City of Edinburgh" just wirelessed "S.O.S. &amp; gave the position she was in &amp; said we are being chased by submarine. On working the position out we find that she is about 40 miles away from us &amp; on the course we should have gone only for getting word about the sinking of the Armenian from the Japanese boat.  A Warship replied to the City of Edinburgh &amp; said "good luck" &amp; has gone to their assistance.</p>
<p>4.40 p.m.  The City of Edinburgh just signalled "Submarine now shelling us four men killed."</p>
<p>4.45 p.m.  The Zealandic just wirelessed "S.O.S." &amp; said, "being chased by submarine but our wireless man could not get her position owing to interruption.  We passed Ushant at midday today &amp; a code message received says submarines are operating from there right up the English Channel.</p>
<p>5 p.m.  Got Zealandic's position &amp; it is 13 miles from the "City of Edinburgh".</p>
<p>5.25 p.m.  Zealandic signalled that she was firing at the submarine, but had now lost sight of it &amp; could not say whether they had struck it or not.</p>
<p>5.27 p.m.  City of Edinburgh wirelessed to Lands End to say that she was safe.</p>
<p>5.40 p.m. Bollington Grange steamer just wirelessed S.O.S. &amp; said being chased by submarine.</p>
