<p>From there we sauntered round viewing the latest modes. Gee. Beautiful women &amp; girls one has to see before believing. After being in these deserted villages for so long these fairly took my breath away.<br/>Had my hair cut alongside a French General wonderful!<br/>On passing a photographers I suddenly decided to try my luck. Was met at the top of the stairs by a lovely girl, thought my heart would bruise my ribs. She spoke fairly good English so my bad French wasn't necessary. She beckoned me to sit on her chair at the desk <strike>while</strike> to write my name etc while she directed operations at my elbow.</p>
