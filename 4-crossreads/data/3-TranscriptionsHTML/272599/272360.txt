<p>March Friday 30 1917</p>
<p>7th Field bearers arrive back to their unit yesterday. <br/>We get orders to rejoin our unit after lunch.<br/>Move off from Becordel at 2.30 p.m. march by way of Albert to Buire 6 miles with only one stop, full pack &amp; blankets up.<br/>Doss in Recreation Hut for night. <br/>Two big suprises &ndash;Les Underwood &amp; young Patterson from Nowra joined up unit while we were away.</p>
