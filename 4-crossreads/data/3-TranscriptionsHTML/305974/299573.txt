<p>1 January Saturday [1916]<br/>Out at Yowie Bay. Very lovely place.  Slept at [Yowie Bay] that night</p>
<p>2 Sunday<br/>Still out at Yowie Bay.  Caught afternoon train for Sydney with Mrs Nelson.  Rang up Mollie McBeth but could not get her to come out, went back to camp in afternoon.</p>
