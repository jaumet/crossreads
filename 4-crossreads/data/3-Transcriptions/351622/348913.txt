<p>Reveille 6 am passed Amiens<br/>6th Brigade attacked</p>
<p>27 October 1916 Fri<br/>Left Bouchon 9.30 march by to Mouflers where we picked up the motor conveyances 21 22 25 Battalion of Field Amb. Engineers &amp; ourselves. Hundreds of ears. Dinner bread &amp; bully beef in motor. 35 miles travelled raining. Arrived Buire Sur Ancre. 5 pm. Starving. fruit 3 francs tin Carry 2 blankets</p>
<p>[Note in margin]<br/>Rotten billet mud over ankles</p>
<p>28 October 1916 Sat<br/>Jeff Wood &amp; myself shared our blankets overnight as usual. Raining all day long. After dinner Jenns &amp; I went over to the next village to see some of our friends in the 5th Brigade. I called on Jim who had a lot to tell me after just returning from his furlough to England. [Marie] shots a cake &amp; a kiss when in bed. Met Cec Alworth also. German Prisoners all along the road working.</p>
