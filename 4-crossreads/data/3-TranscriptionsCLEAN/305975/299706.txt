  
 Sunday Sept.8.9.18 turned out 6. AM fell in  nothing doing Breakfast  rested all day. Monday Sept. 9.9.18 turned out 4.30 musketry instruction to day hot day Tuesday Sept. 10.9.18 Turned out 4.30 musketry instruction  had a letter from maud and maud S. Wednesday Sept. 11.9.18 turned out 3.30 going to rifle range  there seems to be a mess up about the Breakfast  fell in   had to go back for Breakfast
