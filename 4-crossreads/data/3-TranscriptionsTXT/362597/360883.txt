 We had the afternoon off but the Colonel has cut out all sports &amp; concerts so I finished off nearly all my letters. The Boys held a bit of a concert on the fore-deck &amp; two of our Captains &amp; the Major sang a song each. A dirty rub to the Colonel that was. 
 I have to use my balaclava cap to sleep on the deck now it is so cold. We passed the "Karoola" this night bound for Australia again.
