<p>went &amp; thoroughly enjoyed the evening's entertainment. All the heads were there &amp; our seats were just behind them. It was an excellent show the singing &amp; acting fine. Kerry &amp; Dawes were the actors the former having played with Julius Knight so the talent was there alright. Foolish Ford of Wirths Circus was the comediam &amp; a hard case he was too.<br/>The show was held at BOMY, next village, so Mac &amp; I walked, it only took us 20 minutes.<br/>Mitchell, aforementioned in this rough diary, presided at the piano he is a brilliant pianist &amp; plays accompaniments lovely.<br/>Had a yarn to the lads at D.H.Q. afterwards in their office (At [indecipherable]}<br/>11-11-17. Another invitation to the show above mentioned last night.</p>
