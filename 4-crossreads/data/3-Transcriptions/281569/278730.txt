<p>15 September, 1916 Friday</p>
<p>Joined 1st Regt today &amp; left at 1800 for the attack on Mazar, arrived Bir-el-Abd 0230 &amp; bivouacked all day.</p>
<p>16 September, 1916 Saturday</p>
<p>Left Bir-el-Abd at 0530 for Salmana arrived there at 0630 then marched a further 12 mile. Hostile planes very annoying.</p>
