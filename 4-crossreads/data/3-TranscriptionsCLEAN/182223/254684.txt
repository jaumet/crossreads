  
 Thursday 29 July 1915  Letters to Pearl Drury, & some others.  In Cairo for an hour this morning at chemical laboratory & other places.  
 Friday 30 July 1915  About hospital all day.  The cry is still they come.  
 Saturday 31 July 1915  Pres 658 patients today.  General Legge called this afternoon, about 6 p.m.  
 Sunday 1 August 1915  Heard Mass at the Basilica 9.30 am.  Father Vrigille S.J. lunched with me.  Went with the padre to visit a French family at Heliopolis stayed for two hours
