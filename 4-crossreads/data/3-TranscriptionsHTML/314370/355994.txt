<p>March, 1916<br/>Friday 10<br/>Work as usual during the day<br/>After tea several of us went for a walk to Zeitoun &amp; had coffee &amp; cakes<br/>Wrote a letter &amp; sent some stamps to Amy Bryant &amp; finished up by giving one of my pupils an extra hours work.</p>
<p>Saturday 11<br/>Exam on Description takes up all morning.<br/>After dinner went along &amp; had tea w/- Dad &amp; go to pictures<br/>Make arrangements about going out tomorrow</p>
