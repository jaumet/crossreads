<p>Wednesday 6 December 1916<br/>Jacob and I put in the morning on helio work.<br/>I got paper from home with wool sales in it.  24 bales Cor. E.F. 1st 22&frac12; 10 bales Cor. 1st Pro. 14d.  Not too bad!</p>
<p>Thursday 7 December 1916<br/>More signalling as per usual.<br/>On out post at night.<br/>Jolly cold.</p>
