For the last 8 weeks we have been working in it, our clothes are thick with it. We are miles away from any village or canteen practically cut off from civilisation. I can feel myself getting weaker every day, how I will enjoy a rest. Luckily our squad had only one call during the night, it was very dark &amp; it took us a good two hours to reach the next Post. The duck, boards were blown to pieces in places where Fritz's shells had lobbed during the day.  The weather is <u>charming</u>, cold, snow, ice, frost &amp; plenty of rain.</p>
