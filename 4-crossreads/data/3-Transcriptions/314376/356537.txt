<p>The place we fired off was near Albert. Many of these days were like picnic parties. We would sit down and eat our rations.</p>
<p>Usually the cookers would accompany us and we would have an issued of hot tea. We received orders one day to go to a place called Bray.</p>
<p>It was a beautiful spot. I had many happy times there. I found too an old mate, Harold Davis who had come into the Battalion after an absence of over 12 months. I was having a discussion about drink with about 20 men outside our hut.</p>
