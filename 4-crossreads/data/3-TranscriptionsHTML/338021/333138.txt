<p>To O.C. 13th M.G.Coy</p>
<p>Have personally visited guns &amp; crews. Everything alright. Cannot find any good material at Right position.</p>
<p>Can manage with present s.a.a. getting supplies from Infantry Casualties, but shall need more sent up tonight.</p>
<p>Casualties up to present<br/>Hinton, Hunter wounded<br/>Maples killed<br/>Cpl Hamper missing<br/>Some of Battalion men are missing but expect they have reported their [Indecipherable]<br/>Condition of crews: in excellent state especially men who were sent up to line first &amp; are asking for spell.<br/>CHP Weston Lt</p>
