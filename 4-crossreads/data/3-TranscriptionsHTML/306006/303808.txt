<p>7-4-17<br/>Sat 7th<br/>Reveille at 5 oclock this morning &amp; left Sheikh Towaid at 8.30 arriving at Rafa at 11.30 here we had dinner; This is fine country we are now in &amp; the horses have no trouble getting a belly full of either grass or barley.  We arrived at Khan Yunus at 4 PM &amp; found it a much bigger &amp; prettier place than we expected; We rode through the narrow dusty streets &amp; found a fine water supply in the centre of the village watered our horses &amp; came on 1&frac12; miles where we camped a big area of the country here is taken up with orchards &amp; gardens &amp; it is a fine sight, in the centre are a large number of Hospital</p>
