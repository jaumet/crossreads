
Spt 2 than usual. A couple of the stewards took part & livened things up a bit. Tom Tit sang 3 times, & caused more laughter than all the rest. "Wanted" one Ragtime Band, Tin whistles, mouth-organs or any other old thing.
3 Fairly rough during the night, one or two waves coming well into the forrard well deck. Misty, cold & windy No Church on a/c of the weather. A few waves came over the Saloon deck & made us duck for cover. Raining now like a son of a gun, & cold as blazes.[in margin] Sunday
