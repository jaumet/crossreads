
11 August 1918 SundaySame routine, to Hospital, all's wellI take Mangoes, Flowers, etc etc:-  & today the little lady is sewing!! & sitting up, while Dorothy Kathleen snores.I wish you could see them little Mother-   I sleep at the Flat & generally dine out- no word of any Base job so must carry on, War news is good-   Dolly got a cable from her Mother I hope mine reached you.  Had a letter from Nowell he is now C.O. 1/7 ESSEX
