<p>in front of the canvas bath &amp; then the fun started close on two hundred going through the agony of being shaved &amp; dosed with Soap Pills &amp; Oil Tonic.<br/>To day has been very busy cleaning up ship &amp; getting things ship shape for our arrival in port to morrow. I have had the job of Troop Deck Sergeant since leaving Albany and I can tell you it is not the best job on the ocean.<br/>I say Mum how is Ruby &amp; all the others. I do hope they are all well &amp; in the best of health. Say Mum dear how has my intended getting on tell her I intend to come back one of these fine days &amp; then we can have a good chat altogether about what has happened during the voyage. Remember me to all down at Nobles &amp; say that I am AI at Lloyds.<br/>Well Mother I ask God to bless you &amp; keep you safe &amp; in good health until I return to my Native land<br/>Your Loving Son<br/>Will xxxxxx</p>
<p>P.S. Will write again very soon</p>
<p>[Note in red at the bottom of the page:]<br/>Sgt Major W. B. Schaeffer killed in the trenches at Anzac, 20th May, 1915</p>
