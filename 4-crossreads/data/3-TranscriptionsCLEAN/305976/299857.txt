  
 Tuesday May 1 Two day Route march to Yay starting tomorrow. Distance there & back is approx 50 miles full pack & marching order. Out for record, resting today Wednesday May 2 Started for Yay 8 Am. Arrived at 4pm record for distance. 7 failed to see it out and were brought on by waggon. Yay is a pretty little country town principally devoted mixed farming. Inhabitants very hospitable and most of us spent evening at various private houses,  sleep  slept at Shire Hall. Thursday May 3 Left Yay 8.30 People lined streets to give us send off. Had dinner at Sherbrooke bridge. Arrived back at Camp at 5.30 pm 6 failed to finish.
