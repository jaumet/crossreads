  
 27/4/19 Wiesbaden Had a good bath in the waters of the place which are saline and refreshing. The bath of marble into which one goes by steps and towels almost red hot. Had breakfast and then tried to get a place for "Lohengrin" tonight and am afraid I made a mistake in not booking up yesterday. Sunday morning. The Opera colonnade looks out on to another the same over fine gardens with splashing fountains. Read until lunch and afterwards got my place alright by paying double price to the porter at the Hotel. All the various ticket offices reported having sold out but I had to tell the porter pretty straight that he would benefit financially and then the tickets were forthcoming.
