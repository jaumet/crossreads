<p>Lindsay Graham has just turned up from his leave 2 days overdue.<br/>24th.  Nothing much doing today save that I had an hour of the dentists joyful company I'm afraid to think of the boring hooking, scraping, spitting &amp; etc. that I went through, in the evening Lindsay &amp; I went down to Warminster to do a little business, on the way back we met a couple of our officers &amp; had a yarn.<br/>25th.  Raining today quite a change to the hot weather we have been having of late, have put most of the afternoon in writing letters. <br/> This evening 2,4th Battn.
