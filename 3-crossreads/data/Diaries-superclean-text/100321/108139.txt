We went inside and were shown the fine Council room and then a museum from which the Huns had taken all the best. Some addressed and sealed packing cases were still in one room. We walked round the old Cathedral opposite and judging from pictures it had been very fine but the whole interior was burnt. All round here there is nothing but wreckage. We now took the Brussells road and traversed low country. Just before coming into the city we saw the musee de Congo Belge which is delightfully situated overlooking terraced grounds and woods.
