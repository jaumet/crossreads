<p>Thur. JAN. 10  Train for London.  Ruth came with me to Station.  Arrived London about 8.30.  Slept at War Chest Club.  Good clean Bed for 1/-.</p>
<p>FRI. 11.  Furlough ended.  Boarded Train at Paddington at 12.20 &amp; came away to Sutton Veny on the famous Salisbury Plain.</p>
