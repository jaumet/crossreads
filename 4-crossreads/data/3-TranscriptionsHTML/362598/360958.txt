<p>Sunday 31 December 1916<br/>Ellis stays in bed.  I receive Parcels from Marion Clarke &amp; H. Hey.  Cards recd. from Laura &amp; Clare, Letter from Mem &amp; H. Hey.  Wrote to M.R.L.  514 letters to Here.  Night Duty.</p>
