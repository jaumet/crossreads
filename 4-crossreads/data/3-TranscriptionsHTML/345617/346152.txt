<p>Aout 18<br/>Had big route march about 17 miles with pack up. Every body felt very tired afterward as the roads are very hard.</p>
<p>Aout 19<br/>Coy not doing much. Cpl Morrison &amp; I went to "Aire" which is a rather large town &amp; has a very fine &amp; large cathedral. Aire is about 9 miles from Ebblingham.</p>
