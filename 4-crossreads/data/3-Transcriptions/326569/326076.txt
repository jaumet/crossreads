<p>Sunday, July 1st 1917<br/>Up 7 a.m. &ndash; Church Parade 8.30 a.m.  Bought pr. breeches from Pratley for 7/6 &ndash; nice fit.  Afternoon went for stroll down Fovant.  Fine sunshine all day.</p>
<p>Monday, 2/7/17<br/>On Lewis Guns all day.  Left them 3 p.m. &amp; came to hut &amp; got cleaned up &amp; received leave pass for Barrow.  Caught 4.50 p.m. train from Dinton &amp; 6.23 p.m. from Salisbury &amp; ran to W'loo [Waterloo] without a stop.  Went per tube to Euston &amp; booked bed at Y.M.C.A. &amp; looked up trains at station.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 3/7/17<br/>Up 3.30 a.m. &amp; caught 4.55 a.m. train ex Euston &amp; travelled via Willesden, Bletchley, Northampton, Rugby, Stafford,</p>
