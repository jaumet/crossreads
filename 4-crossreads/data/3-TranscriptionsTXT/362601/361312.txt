
14 December, Sunday
Up at 4.30. Lovely morn fishing we go 15 off rocks other all round lake but to rocks & I get bites even. Swim. Girls run in bush. Struggle round to swim. Dress on boat. Good train Home 9.30. Supper of Toma & apricots & go bed at Beulah.
15 December, Monday
Come Home & Dad & Mum go to Progress Meeting & I go to bed.
