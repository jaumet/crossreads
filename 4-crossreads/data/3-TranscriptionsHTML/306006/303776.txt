<p>night I left a note for Ben Woodhouse asking him to get Saturday afternoon &amp; evening off as we were arranging a party.</p>
<p>16-3-17<br/>Fri 16th<br/>Had a theory test this afternoon having from 2 to 5 to finish but as I was in a hurry to go out I finished at 4  went to Abbassia &amp; took Cobb to St James for dinner &amp; then decided to see French war scenes at the Cosmograph we were late for the first session but went in &amp; saw a few films &amp; they are the best I have ever seen we then went for a gharry ride to Gezerieh but were not away long as Cobb had to</p>
