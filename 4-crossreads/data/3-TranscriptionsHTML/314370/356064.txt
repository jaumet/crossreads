<p>August, 1916<br/>Thursday 3<br/>We sailed shortly after breakfast.  Accompanied by a destroyer.<br/>Franconia armed with 6" gum mounted right aft.<br/>Told off to life boat stations &amp; have to carry life belts wherever we go.</p>
<p>Friday 4<br/>continue to move on a Zig Zag course to Malta which we expect to reach Saturday morning.<br/>Boat stations at 10.30 am.<br/>spend a couple of hours on the signal bridge.</p>
