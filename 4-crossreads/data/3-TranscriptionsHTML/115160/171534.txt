<p>Articles of no present use are everywhere. &nbsp;How would you like to see your machine or your bed lying out amongst rubbish in the street, or your clothes-boxes ransacked and left like rubbish; or your cupboards turned over the people looking for anything they might fancy. &nbsp;What is not fancied is left perhaps for someone else to continue the ransacking. &nbsp;Stoves, chairs, tables, clothes, pieces of furniture and articles perhaps held dear by the one-time occupants of the houses are lying everywhere. &nbsp;This territory has been in German hands for over 4 years and what the Huns have not made use of, has been left to the four winds of heaven. &nbsp;Of course, the houses are in a more or less ruined state, with great rents in the walls, no roofs, no windows and littered with rubbish. &nbsp;Such may convey to you a rough&nbsp;</p>
