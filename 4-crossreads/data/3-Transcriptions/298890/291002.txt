<p>9. Sunday<br/>Went to Miss N. at 10.30.  Left at 4.30 &amp;  went to Mrs Usher &amp; Keith. F. Malone also there after being gassed.  Stopped in to see Mr &amp;  Mrs de Valder.  Home at 10.30 quite exhausted.</p>
<p>10. Monday<br/>Went to Minerva at 10.  Oh so exhausted.  Home &amp;  rested an hour.  Then Mme. came &amp; gave F.P. a lesson in French.</p>
<p>11. Tuesday<br/>Stayed home  Wrote article. &amp;  sent it to Paget.  F.P. rang up at lunch &amp; I met him at Piccadilly [indecipherable] at 7. Dinner at 'Popular' then sat in Rgts Palace talking &amp; he saw me home by 11.30 lovely day</p>
<p>12. Wednesday<br/>Horribly fatigued.  Went to Minerva at 10 &amp; home at 5.30.  Rested a little.  Then dressed up in S.W.H. overall &amp; cap.  Nelson &amp; Miss Barfield came.  Lovely day.</p>
