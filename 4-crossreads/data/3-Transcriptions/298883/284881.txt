<p>3rd</p>
<p>This morning <strike>about</strike> at 06.05 A.M. <strike>the</strike> our artillery put over a heavy strafe on the hun positions and the 2nd Aust Division passed through our front line and attacked in the same manner that we attacked on the 1st.  They were equally as successful but suffered slightly heavier casualties.</p>
