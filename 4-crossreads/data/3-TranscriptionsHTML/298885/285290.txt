<p>Dec 31st &ndash; 18<br/>EXIT 1918</p>
<p>1919<br/>The year has begun which is destined to bring peace to a world which has been struggling against strife and crime for 4 long years</p>
<p>It will also see the Aussies well home in their native land once again enjoying the ways of civilisation and Freedom</p>
<p>This fact was born in mind by all the boys when they set out last</p>
