<p>17.3.18<br/>Balance of Transport that had moved by road marched into billiting area about 4 p.m.  As storage was limited only guns, tripods, and ammunition were stored at Q.M. Store the remainder being left in Limbers which were parked along the road.<br/>First Battalion Church Parade held at Battalion Headquarters at 11 a.m.by Divisional Padre, Major Pitt-Owen.<br/>Captain A.L. Chambers and Sgt. McCarthy reported from Machine Gun School at Camieres.</p>
