<p>15.3.17<br/>This letter has been delayed some &amp; it saves me writing a new one. I am enclosing a piece of M.C. ribbon which I want you to keep for me - we had a presentation of Medals, ribbons etc. this morning by General Birdwood &amp; he congratulated me etc. &amp; handed out the said piece of ribbon.<br/>Well my dearest sister I will close off now with fondest love to self &amp; family Ken<br/>Your cardigan jacket to hand yesterday &amp; I thank you dear very much for it, the fit is O.K. &amp; as the winter isn't over yet it will be very useful indeed, thanking you again sister mine.<br/>Ken</p>
