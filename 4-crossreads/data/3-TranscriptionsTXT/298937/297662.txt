 landed &amp; waited in the Shed for some time. Got a carpenter to send a note to Dad for me. 3 Trains for our crowd &amp; I was in the 2nd train. Felt delighted to once again behold the green fields &amp; lovely landscapes of England. After a splendid journey arrived at Amesbury where we disembarked &amp; marched 6 miles to Rollestone Camp, passing near Stonehenge.
