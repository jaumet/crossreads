June 12th
Up 5 a.m. Orderly Cpl., time dragged terribly. 1st party left ship about 10-30, 2nd 1-40 p.m., 3rd about 5 p.m. 1-40 p.m. reached Heliopolis, at 8-30 gave kits to baggage waggons marched to camp. Waited about there for quarters but finally turned in to rough it in Eating sheds, sleeping in clothing. Up 6 a.m., paraded for our kits 7 a.m. Lecture at 11 a.m. by Colonel not 25 of. present .
