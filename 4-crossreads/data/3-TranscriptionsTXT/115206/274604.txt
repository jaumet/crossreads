
February 1918Sunday 3
On the roadReveille at 6.30 am no parade until Boot and Saddle.Left at 11am. passed through Hazebrouck at 12.30Arrived at billets, three kilos from Bailleul, at 5.30pmAm feeling in very high spirits, received letter from Mrs. Adam.Warned off as horseholder for party going to the guns tomorrow morningHad a feed of eggs and chips at  a little Estaminet facing the billet.
