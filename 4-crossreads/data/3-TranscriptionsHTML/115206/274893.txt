<p>November 1918.<br/>Tuesday 19</p>
<p>Hospital -  Birmingham and Southall<br/>Left hospital at 11.30am and caught the 12.10 back to London arriving at S.thall eventually at 7.30<br/>Met Roy and was placed into the same ward as he, much to my pleasure. As I did not feel too well, I went to bed.</p>
