 2/7/16. Took the bikes out for exercise last night &amp; thoroughly enjoyed the ride along the country roads through the beautiful crops. Soon after we arrived back a heavy bombardment commenced not far from here. It shook the windows &amp; doors &amp; kept going until about 4 o'c this morning. It was believed to be in the vicinity of La Bassee. An occasional flash could be seen from the back door.
