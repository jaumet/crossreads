
We went in per Motor Lorry & back by train part of the way the rest per buz wagon. It was in the train I met "her" - Usual thing I always meet "her" in train tram or boat but this one was indeed a peach growing at Corbie. There were a couple of French Soldiers in the carriage & one was a singer & sang a couple of very pretty songs & taught me the chorus of one. His fiancee wrote out the words & sent them to him. He was a jolly spark was sorry when they got out.We detrained at Mericourt & there I met a driver of a motor lorry I knew so we were in lucks way. Arrived back in good order to find
