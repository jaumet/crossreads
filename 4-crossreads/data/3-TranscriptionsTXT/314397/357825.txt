 Monday. 23.9.18. Still in bed. Temp won't go right down. Gen. Birdwood came in this afternoon &amp; had a talk with all of us in the ward. 
 Tuesday 24.9.18 Still in bed. Nice day outside. For a wonder it didn't rain all day. 
 Wednesday 25.9.18. Got up for a while in the afternoon Strolled all round the hospital. Slight air raid tonight. 
 Thursday 26.9.18 Went to the town &amp; bought a little clothing. We brought back flowers etc for the ward. Feel good. Had afternoon tea at the club. 
 Friday 27. 9.18 Am marked for 3 weeks sick leave. Do me every time. Had afternoon tea with Miss Murdoch (Red Cross) &amp; Sister McKay. Was goodo. 
 Saturday. 28.9.18 More rain today. Saw Sister Vicars Foote last night. She came over on the boat with me. Went to concert at 3rd A.G.H &amp; it was very good. Enjoyed it immensely.
