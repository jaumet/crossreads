  
 April 1915 Wednesday 28  Fine  One man shot dead & a N.Z. Officer badly wounded alongside me during the night. Heavy fighting or firing all night. The Turks waste much ammunition & use explosive bullets. We were relieved this morning by fresh N.Z's about 6 AM. & and came down to the beach where we stayed all day & moved along further in the night. Here we were reorganised 
 Thursday 29 Roll call  Fine . Naval Bgde. Russian Jews N.Z's & more  Australians landing to our aid. We are to have a good spell before going back to the firing line. Bert Sanders & myself have a comfortable dugout to sleep in. Arthur wounded Tuesday. I hear many Royal Marine Light Infantry here
