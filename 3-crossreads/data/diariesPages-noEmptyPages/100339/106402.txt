Saturday June 7th
The position of the ship at 12 noon was Latitude 22Â°.04 degrees South by Longtitude 104.51 degrees East and the distance travelled for the 24 hours was 236 miles. The wind and sea are still very heavy as a result of last nights severe storm.

Sunday morning when I came on deck I was surprised to find a clear sky and all the rain gone. The wind slackened down a lot. It had changed round from the South to a South Westerly quarter, and it is now blowing head on to the ship.
