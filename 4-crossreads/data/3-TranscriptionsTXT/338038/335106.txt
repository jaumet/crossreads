He has just arrived. Told me that he thought his brother Joe would be coming over.26th June   Mail closes tonight but I have nothing much more to add. It is rather funny, the Turk's attempt at "bluff". They are in the habit of sending messages warning us to surrender. Yesterday they dropped a message for us from an aeroplane, but it drifted back into their own trenches.
