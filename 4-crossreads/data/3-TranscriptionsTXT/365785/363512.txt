 Dardanelles, Sep. 9/1915 
 Dear Mrs. Reid, Just a line to let you know I am doing well, having a fairly good time, plenty of work.  I hear we are likely to be sent away for a spell shortly, it will do us good.  This trench life gets very tiring, the Turks are getting a very rough time this last few weeks.  It is a beautiful sight to watch our Navy bombard the Turks trenches of a night, the flashes from the guns light up everything.
