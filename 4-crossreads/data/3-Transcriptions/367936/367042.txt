<p>Tuesday 19 September 1916<br/>Nothing doing all day;  on picquet at night.<br/>Dick got two parcels from home.</p>
<p>Wednesday 20 September 1916<br/>John Bay and I got 48 hours leave to Port Said.<br/>Went to see Mr. Trelawny but found he was in England.<br/>Tried to get rooms in Continental Hotel but it was full so went to Great Brittian Hotel.  Rotten place!  Went to Empire Club and picture show in evening.</p>
