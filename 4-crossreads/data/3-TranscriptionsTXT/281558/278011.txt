 Friday July 13th 
 Bapaume : good day : reveille 4.30 am : fell in for "stunt" : march to Warlencourt then the "boys" attached &amp; "took" some trenches then Loupart Wood : seen a taube come down : home about 3 pm : 
 Saturday July 14th 
 Bapaume : good day : reveille 6 am : sent map to Aunt Myra &amp; Hun paper to Mum : stroll round after tea :
