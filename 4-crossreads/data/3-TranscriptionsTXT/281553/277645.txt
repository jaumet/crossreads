17th2nd Reinforcements arrived at Post & begin trench Duty.
18th SaturdayAnother small mail in early, 2 Parcels from Lindfield.  U/load Duties very easy.  Very sharp attack with Artillery & Rifle fire from Turks about sunset 1/2 hour.
19th SundayThings very quiet on Post all day.  Wrote letters.
20th MondayQuiet.
