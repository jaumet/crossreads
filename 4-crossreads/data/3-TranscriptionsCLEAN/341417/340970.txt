
15/9/18
Dear Miss King
Your welcome letter safely to hand and I was very pleased to hear from you again, and that you received my two letters alright. I have just come out of the front line when I received your letter and it made me feel quite satisfied getting a letter from you.We hopped over the bags twice in 24 hours and we done real well.
