 May 1918 Monday 20 
 Bussy &ndash;la-Daours Weather beautiful. Had an inspection at 9am by Bdge Major, Received orders to promenade to my unit and stand by there as an emergency. Should a hostile bombardment take place to report immediately. At 3 am this morning an aerial torpedo fell on a B,A.C's Lines &ndash; made a mess. Arrived at W.L. at 3.30 pm and reported to Capt Redmond. Ran to Fuilloy during the morning.
