<p>Thursday 3.8.16<br/>Spent whole of the morning getting place in order; left very untidy by the Ambulance who we replaced.  Have to make out menu for each day &amp; draw necessary foodstuffs.</p>
<p>Friday 4.8.16<br/>Getting more used to new work, rather intricate at first but now becoming much easier.  About 180 patients on rolls at present .  A &amp; B Sec. arrived here to-day but are not to take part in the <strike>D.R.</strike> running of the D.R.S.</p>
<p>Saturday 5.8.16<br/>Large mail from home, first for over 3 weeks.</p>
