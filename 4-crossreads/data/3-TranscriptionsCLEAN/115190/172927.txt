 27 th  Sept.  Away again early in the morning.  The road winds along the sea shore for some distance & we fill our bottles & water our horses along the shore.  The water tastes good but by now I could drink  drain   sewer  water.  We pass a grim object  - very dead -  lying across the seat of a buggy.  The two horses are also lying dead.  A little further on an old Bedouin with a long grey beard lies dead  in the gutter   by the roadside .
