
Tuesday 1st May 1917Bapaume
Watered mules then gave them a run on the grabs till 11.30 amCleaning up the mule lines in the afternoon.Paid 40 francs on e shilling and four pence left.Line Piquet at 5.15pm while taking of nose bags one of the mules took a liking to me and let fly his two hind legs. I got them all right so the Piquet had to get another man.
