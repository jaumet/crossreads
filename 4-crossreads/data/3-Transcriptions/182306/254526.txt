
<p>44</p>
<p>light of day for three weeks. and was in bed two month before I was allowed up to get about the ward I never saw much of the town while I was there. Saw two of the German Submarines come in to surrender themselves. Left Portsmouth on the 18th Dec and Came to Dartford And went on my sick leave ont he 20th Dec. Had Xmas &amp; New Year in London &amp; had the time of my life while there</p>
