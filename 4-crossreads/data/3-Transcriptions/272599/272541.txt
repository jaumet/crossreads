<p>October Saturday 20 1917</p>
<p>Catch train for Newcastle at 9.50.a.m. Change there into "Flying Scotsman" in half hour. slipped out &amp; saw a bit of Newcastle. Stephensons statue. Rocket. Tyne Bridge. <br/>Via Berwick. (border) Tweed River. Dunbar. Bass Rock mouth of Firth of Forth. <br/>Arrive Edinburgh 2 p.m. Fine day. Scotch laddie takes me in tow &amp; conducts to Overseas Club. Warm welcome by ladies Mrs Jackson. Dinner &amp; yarn. Tour round the City. Edinburgh Castle. Holyrood. Scott Memorial etc. Tea. Amusement. Bed noise.</p>
