<p>Dairy February 1916</p>
<p>Feb 1st Still on Engineering but given orders to rejoin unit tomorrow. Bright sun cool breeze, exempt from Outpost duty.</p>
<p>Feb 2nd Wednesday<br/>Lecture on Outposts &amp; [Indecipherable] positions etc. Afternoon 1 hours Signaling. Letterwriting afternoon &amp; evening. Short Rations. 96 Biscuits 4 Breads 8 tins (1lb) Jam &amp; light cheese between 34 men for 24 hours.</p>
<p>Feb 3rd Thursday.<br/>Bkfast &frac12; hr. earlier marched with Packs, East forward past "Camels Humps" returned to camp 1.30pm. 3Pm. pitching tents for D.Coy &amp; C.Coy</p>
<p>On Outpost 7 &amp; 8 Platoons.</p>
