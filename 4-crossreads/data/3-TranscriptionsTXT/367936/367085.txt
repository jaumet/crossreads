
Thursday 14 December 1916On telephone from 6 till 10 a.m.  Watching Tommies sham fight.  Our areoplanes dropping smoke bombs close to our camp.  Afterwards Taube over dropped three realuns on us but did no damage.  Patrol from W. El Arish got back safely, with a few prisoners.  On out post at night.
Friday 15 December 1916All got ready early and shifted camp;  came to Wells about 31/2 miles on east.  Had to ride about 3 miles to Kilo 128 to water horses, got bucked for a bit coming back in dark.
