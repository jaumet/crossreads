<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Sydney</p>
<p>To - Berrima</p>
<p>Date - <strike>6</strike> 7-9-14</p>
<p>Time - 5-28</p>
<p>Your (1710)</p>
<p>Reply:- &nbsp;It was only the firemen who mutinied there were volunteers from the troops to do the stoking. &nbsp;I suggest that trainees be disbanded and if more troops required seasoned men passed medical test&nbsp;be employed 1720</p>
