<p>Thurs. 1st Feb (9.15 am)</p>
<p>The weather is still jolly cold but nothing like as bad as a week ago. We haven't had a drop of rain since I came back from leave, but plenty of snow has fallen, and everything has been covered with a white mantle for the past couple of days.</p>
<p>Last night went into Boulogne &amp; saw "Carmen" (Opera) at the Th&eacute;&acirc;tre Municipal. All the artistes were from the Op&eacute;ra Comique of Paris.</p>
<p>Thurs. 8th Feb (11 am)</p>
<p>About four days ago had a very heavy fall of snow and it is still lying on the ground. The winter sun shines brightly every day but there is no heat in it, while a cold wind often blows across the snow from the interior.</p>
<p>Have had two letters lately from Lieut Col Upjohn. One on "trench foot &amp; its treatment" and the other on "fractures caused by shell &amp; bullet &amp; their treatment".</p>
