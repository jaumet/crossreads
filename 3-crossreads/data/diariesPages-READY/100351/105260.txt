
Sat. 22-7-’16

& other evidences of comfortable living & also provision for a long stay. Part of 1st B’gade moved out as we moved in. We started a big fire & I searched & found a German Dixie & filled same at W’cart on road & we had Cocoa & Biscuits & cheese supper.

Sun. 23-7-’16.
1 a.m. still up at fire. Charlie self slept at intervals on M. G. emplacement. Very cold with only O’coats & W’proof sheets. Up at 4.30 a.m. Started fire again & was looking around for water when orders came to dump O’coats & move on to Support the men who had taken Part of Pozieres during the night
