The ground is cut about by shell holes to a tremendous & unimaginable extent, and old rifles and gear, together with all kinds of smashed up military equipment of  of  all kinds, lay everywhere. We arrived at the bearer post & found Vic Hall, Harry Reynolds, Mc ilroy & Strange installed therein. It was a German pill box & evidently the scene of lot of fighting, and the centre of a hot barrage, for confusion reigned all around.
