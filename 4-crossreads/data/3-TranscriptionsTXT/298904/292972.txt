The chap he hit was one of our own & one of the quietest & most harmless chaps you could well imagine, when he was hit he was to surprised to speak, it nearly knocked the wind out of him, By Jove that caused a laugh for many a day".Pretty close to our last position was Victoria Gully" & on the first day I saw something in this gully that I had often read about, but always refused to believe, it was this a running stream of water that choked with dead & dying men, running red with blood, its seems hard to believe
