 17/3/16 Dust storm &amp; heat, followed by heavy rain that soaks through the tent. At midnight have such a happy family in the tent that I cannot move. 
 18/3/16 Saturday. Have a glorious sleep all the afternoon &amp; night. 
 19/3/16 Sunday. Another loaf all day &amp; sleep. Write few letters &amp; take few snaps. 
 20/3/16 Cold night that makes us shiver all night, followed by beautiful morning. Early morning (2/30 am) moon shining
