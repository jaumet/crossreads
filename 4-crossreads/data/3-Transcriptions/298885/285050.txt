<p>Thur. Feb. 24th -16<br/>Very happy to day with a young "post office" (9 letters).</p>
<p>Fri. Feb. 25th -16<br/>Australian and New Zealand soldiers have small fight with a few Turks on other side of Canal.</p>
<p>Sat. Feb. 16th -16<br/>Nearly 1000 Light Horse sent from Heliopolis to Canal.</p>
<p>Mon. Feb. 18th -16<br/>Light Horse have an encounter with Arabs while on their daily patrol.  Several Light Horse wounded.</p>
<p>Tues. Feb. 29th -16<br/>Received more letters (3)</p>
