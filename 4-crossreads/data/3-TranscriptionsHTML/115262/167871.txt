<p>Saturday 14 April 1917.</p>
<p>Stayed Federal Coffee Palace on Fri eveng</p>
<p>Up at 7.30 &amp; after a time out to Burnley.</p>
<p>In city on Sat morning.</p>
<p>Out to Toorak in afternoon</p>
<p>Met Joy at 5.50 in city &amp; after tea went to &quot;Canary Cottage&quot; at &quot;Her Majesty&#39;s theatre&quot;. (Joy lost her purse.)</p>
<p>Bed at 12.30 am. at Burnley</p>
<p>Most enjoyable evening.</p>
