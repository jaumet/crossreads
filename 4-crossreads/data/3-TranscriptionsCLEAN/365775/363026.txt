 Just before going further I will endeavour to give you a brief outline of our journey across Egypt. packed in trucks like so many sheep, the shades of night are falling as the last of us are hustled aboard, & consequently getting cooler, tis the one thing that makes life worth living in Egypt, is the cool nights, one is always able to get a good nights rest.
