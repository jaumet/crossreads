Sunday 7.4.18 to Sunday 5.5.18.Went on leave from 8th.4.18 to 11.4.18 which time I spent in London.  Very quiet in London compared with ordinary times owing to no men being on leave from France.Visited a few of the theatre's "Box of Tricks" at Hippodrome, "Bing Boys on Broadway" at Adelphi & Colliseum.
