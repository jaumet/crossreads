 of the Wadi Kelt Sunday June 16 Jacko over at daybreak this morning Very nearly lost my neddy to day during shelling. Auckland Reg went into Trenches last night. Moving into position to night Very Hot day Monday June 17 Sent out on message to Auckland Reg. Had great difficulty finding them. Very nearly rode into Turkish Lines. On arrival back at Camp Found the boys moving up to their old position. Tuesday June 18 Wrote mail, getting very hot here Several of the boys evacuated with Malaria. Jacko
