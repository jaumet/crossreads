<p>9th. M.G. Coy No VI<br/>Intelligence Report from 6 am 5th to 6 am 6th<br/>Right Sector:- No 5 gun fired from I 17 a 8/4 to I 18 c 3/0 road from La [Bleau?] a Bas Trou- between 6 pm &amp; 8 pm. No 6 gun fired I 17 b 5/8 to I 11 a 9/3 communication trenches- 750 rnds- results not observed.<br/>There were two alarms of gas at 6.45 pm and 11.30 pm.<br/>Left Sector:- No 7 gun fired 1000 rnds on I 6 b 8/9 roads and trenches round Le Temple. No 8 fired on I 6 a 5/0 Road and Dump at Intact Farm. between 7 &amp; 11 pm.<br/>Aeroplane heard overhead about 12.15 mid. Bright moonlight night. Sleeting &amp; cold at Stand to.</p>
<p>B.J Carey Capt<br/>9th M.G. Coy<br/>6.12.16</p>
