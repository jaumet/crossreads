<p>To make this Kennel more like home I have placed a photo of the family on the dug-out ceiling &amp; Alan has put some fine big poppies in a jar which he picked up and which adors</strike> now adorns our main &ndash; and only &ndash; entrance.</p>
<p>We purchased a few things at the canteen this afternoon &amp; so we able to enjoy <u>'some</u> supper'.    Alan made <u>some</u> caf&eacute;-au-lait &amp; this washed down very comfortably with a few biscuits.</p>
<p>July 6.   We all slept in till "Cookhouse" went at 8 A.M.   At 9 we were divided into two parties, to work in shifts from 9-3 &amp; 3-7 respectively.   We were detailed to salvage certain articles from the ruins for use at the Front.  These things we pulled out, loaded</p>
