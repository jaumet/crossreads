  
 Cairo & the train was rushed by a crowd of niggers selling all kinds of fruit & cigarettes. The noise of them shouting out oringars & other pecular names. I shall never forget. There was no tucker for us when we arrived at the camp so we had to go into Heliopolis for a feed & the niggers did try & do us on the prices as we had English money.
