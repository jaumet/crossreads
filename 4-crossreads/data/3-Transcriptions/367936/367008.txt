<p>Thursday 13 July 1916<br/>Rostron and I went to Comforts fund but did not get anything.  Then took photo to police of man whom I had photographed.  Policeman took us over barracks &amp; showed us the court house.  Left films to be developed.</p>
<p>Friday 14 July 1916<br/>In town with Nelson.  Went to Comforts fund depot but they had gone to France.  Sent registered packet to Stella.</p>
