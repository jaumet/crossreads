 Aug 15. 16. 17th. Nothing of much interest except news from Russian &amp; Italian fronts &ndash; Receipt of large mail from "Aussy". 
 Aug 18th. Fourth Division relieved by First. Parcel despatched to home. 
 Aug. 19th. Our spare time occupied by "Soccer" &ndash; More letters to &amp; from home. 
 Aug 20th. Harry parsons &ndash; Keith Harris etc &ndash; Death of Jim Cunningham &amp; 3 others 1st Fld. Amb. 
 Aug 21st Sabbath &ndash; Church "Confession &amp; Communion" &ndash; Fifth Fld Amb relieved us at "Anzac Rest Camp 
 Aug 22nd. Our Ambulance moves off from Vadencourt to Albert where we bivouac for the night.
