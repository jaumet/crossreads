<p>17 March 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Left <strike>Beitin</strike> in the rain I have never been so cold for a long time 4 Niggers died, however we moved on to a Russian Monastery which the Germans had used for their head quarters-   we were thankful to get under a roof &amp; bought Charcoal from the natives &amp; got fires going - X Laura writes my August Notes you sent had been heavily censored so in future please if sending to England run a pen thro' any incriminating X</p>
