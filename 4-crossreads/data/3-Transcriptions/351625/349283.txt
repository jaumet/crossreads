<p>I hope you get this letter as you will think I never write, if you miss a few Ken is cabling to say we are both well as soon as he gets to Blighty.  Oh well I will have to say au revoir.  Give my love to all &amp; whips to yourself.</p>
<p>From<br/>Douglas</p>
