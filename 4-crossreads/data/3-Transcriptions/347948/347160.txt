<p>Was imprisoned  Further particulars see guide.<br/>Went Down Bond Street Regent St &amp; Piccadilly and am thoroughly disappointed.  Am satisfied that Sydney Streets are far better shops bigger &amp; more splendid. Why these shops have no verandahs how do people get on in Wet Weather.<br/>Went over Zoo  Very interesting.  Saw Alligators &amp; Crocodiles, and seals in their natural state with Their dog like bark.</p>
