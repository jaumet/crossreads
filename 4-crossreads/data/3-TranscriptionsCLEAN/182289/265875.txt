 26.4.18 Arrangements made for establishing S.A.A. [Small Arms Ammunition] dumps for working of guns on northern bank of the Ancre.  Site of dump selected D.30.a.90.10.  Owing to hitch in Transport ammunition ordered did not arrive. The following targets were engaged at irregular intervals throughout night & morning, 3000 rounds expended:- (1)  Streets, Ville-Sur-Ancre.   (2)  Sunken Road K.1.b. & d.   (3)  Hollow K.7.c.4.   (4)  Cross Roads K.1.b.48.55.   (5)  Cemetery, Ville-Sur-Ancre.   (6)  Battery Position K.7.b.7.7. Enemy Artillery carried out light harassing fire throughout the night and on roads leading to Buire, Treux & towards Ville-Sur-Ancre.  Use of Verey Lights not so marked as previously.  Enemy Machine Guns fired short bursts on roads and tracks leading to Treux & Buire throughout the night.  Enemy Aircraft nil.
