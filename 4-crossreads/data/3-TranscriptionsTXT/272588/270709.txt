
Monday 20 MaySummer weather still continues & is simply glorious. We were relieved this evening & while we were coming out the Bosch gave us a hail of gas & H.E. shells.Now back at H.Q. near Hazebrouck.
Tuesday 21 MayDown here, at Hazebrouck we lead the simple life and get round all day in shorts playing cricket & croquet.  Bathing every morning after a mile run. Still glorious weather.
