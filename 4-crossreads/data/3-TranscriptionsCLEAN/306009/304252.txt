  
 1915 Mar. 7 Sun. Attended Holy Communion at 6.45 a.m. in the fine little Chapel at Mena House - First Service I have been able to attend since leaving the Kyarra. Parade of the Unit in camp to award punishment to two of the men concerned in the thieving of 6 from the Pack Store - Pte. Linquist got 90 days detention & Pte. Gow got 6 months imprisonment with hard labour.
