<p>Sunday 25 June 1916<br/>In afternoon Rostron, Nelson &amp; I went up to Alex and <strike>caught</strike> took a Garry out to Nougha Gardens.  Most beautiful gardens &amp; pretty drive out.  Most of shops open in Alex.  No Sunday kept.  Service in Y.M.C.A. in evening.</p>
<p>Monday 26 June 1916<br/>Wrote to Father No. (6).  Went into Alexandria on my own.  Got two developed films &amp; left four more.  Photos of Pyramids came out very well.<br/>In morning I took a film of Rostron &amp; Nelson acting the goat.<br/>(No. 6)</p>
