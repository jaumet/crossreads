<p><u>February </u></p>
<p><u>1st</u> -</p>
<p>Awake with nothing more than a cold to my credit or should I say discredit.&nbsp; Late breakfast.&nbsp; Return of Mr Scales with whom I journey to Wanstead for purpose of meeting my Dear English Mother - Happy meeting - A long conversation and much laughter.&nbsp; Return to Highgate.</p>
