<p>Wednesday 2 May 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille 6.30. No physical jerks. &nbsp;</p>
<p>In full marching order left camp at 8.26 a.m. Spell 5 mins. Trawool Bdge at 9.25. Trawool Hotel 10. a.m. Left 10.15 a.m. Spell 5 mins. Kerrisdale 11.24 (station) 1.8 p.m. Homewood. Dinner here. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Now gone 19 miles. One 11 mile non stop walk.&nbsp;<s>Nothing to eat since breakfast</s>. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>2.26 pm. onward to Yea. (Breaking up of the boys.) 4pm arrived in Yea.&nbsp;Decent reception. Supper &amp; bed 10.30&nbsp;</p>
<p>Too tired to sleep. Capt Heath &amp; 20 others (myself included) slept in Shire Hall. (Got cup of tea at Homewood at a farmhouse on the way up.)</p>
