<p>Diary while in France<br/>March 19th Arrival at Marseilles in A.41 (Minnapoles). Impression of Marseilles Harbour and township.<br/>March 20th<br/>Left vessel and marched to special encampment site of Indian G.H. originally German residence- fine grounds with abundance of fruit trees and luxuriant vegetation- great contrast with Egypt.<br/>March 21st<br/>Bully beef and biscuits. Inspection by a French General. Break camp at Marseilles &ndash; Baggage party leave by train for unknown</p>
