 Thursday 23rd May Roused out early & had to put our Blankets &  cooking  eating utensils away before we had breakfast. Got Passes sugar & meat tickets Etc marched to Forant & got the train there. Arrived London about 12 Got a Railway Warrant for Goralwood Had Dinner at War Chest.Bussed to Bayswater & spent afternoon with Aunt Susie Caught train at 8.20 from Euston for Hollyhead
