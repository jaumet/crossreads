  
 [Sunday 15 October 1916] 
 Delaney comes to town. Dined with Mrs Cotton No 17 Bus get out at Windsor Road & Parkers to Tea 11  Cof  Corfton Road only 10 minutes walk Met Laura & Connie at the Parkers In the morning went to Regents Palace Hotel to meet Doctor Eastwood & his wife who go out to Australia this week & may see some of my friends out there.  I am introducing Dolly to all I can.
