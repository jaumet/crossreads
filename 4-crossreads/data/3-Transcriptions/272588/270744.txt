<p>Saturday 27 July<br/>Last night the battalion here was relieved &amp; changed over without a casualty.  These men seem a better kind of men. North Staffs. relieve South Lancs. The doctor here R.A.M.C. is from Chatswood, Giblin. Very quiet on the front.</p>
<p>Sunday 28 July<br/>Rotten weather raining &amp; stormy, but very quiet on the front. A game of bridge with the English padre to-night was very enjoyable. The ambulance is to be relieved in a day or so.</p>
