<p>22 August, Friday</p>
<p>Catch 10 pm boat &amp; do not feel as tired as expected.</p>
<p>23 August, Saturday</p>
<p>Elizabeth Dee Why at 7.30 pm.</p>
<p>Work in garden all afternoon. Go to Manly with M Paul &amp; ring Mrs Preddy. Meet two Miss Thomas'. Go to evening. Meet nice people. Play. Meet George ventriloquist. Come home with Mrs Moran, only two in tram. Meet Mr Wiltshire.</p>
