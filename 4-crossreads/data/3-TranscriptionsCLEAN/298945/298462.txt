  
 As soon as we arrived we were sent to a good dug-out a hundred yards along a by road overlooking a large valley, & it is there that I am writing this on Friday afternoon. 
 Just down in the valley we can see a battery of howitzers; on the opposite hill in patches of green other batteries of howitzers, & at least one big gun; & on e  our left on another hill some 60 pounders. These are firing all day & part of the night, & I suspect there are many other heavy guns here that are lying doggo.
