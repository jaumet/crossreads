<p>Set. 14th<br/>"Beau-coup" rain prevents anything unusual from happening</p>
<p>Sept. 15th<br/>Third journey to town at Poperinghe, where we are sent to purchase footballs &ndash; in the afternoon we again defeat the 6th Fld. Amb at Rugby 2-0.</p>
<p>Sept. 16th<br/>Close of mail. We won our first soccer match against Sixth by 5-1 &ndash; a good game &ndash; Harry &amp; self represented our Unit &ndash; Issue &amp; lecture on new type of Gas helmet.</p>
<p>Sept. 17th<br/>"Sports day" is abandoned owing to another move of camp &ndash; We leave Waratah for Reninghelst (which is on the road to Ypres). Visits to Y.M.C.A &amp; village.</p>
<p>Sept 18th<br/>Guard duties &ndash; Signs of the approaching winter</p>
