<p>London.  The pier must look beautiful in peace time for it is covered with electric lights.  <br/>Slots machines and games abound in profusion all over the pier.  There is also another <br/>Theatre &amp; tea rooms where a first class programme is rendered by the Pier <br/>Orchestra.  These are gratis to wounded soldiers.  Opposite the pier is a large <br/>underground aquarium where the piscatorial displays are very interesting and <br/>educational.  There is a concert in the hospital every other night</p>
