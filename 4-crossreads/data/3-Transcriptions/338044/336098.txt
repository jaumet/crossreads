<p>Islands are frequent  &amp;  lighthouses plentiful. Pass a large boat about 8 pm. Very hot night</p>
<p>Oct 27th :-<br/>Frightfully hot to-day   Don't like too much of the Red Sea. Am still on duty, dressing in Dispensary. Worst day of trip  No land in sight. Passed sloop early in morning</p>
<p>Oct 28th<br/>Not so hot to-day. Still in Red Sea. We passed several vessels to-day, but still out of sight of land.</p>
<p>Oct 29th<br/>Great excitement on board, as we are nearing the end of our voyage. Sighted land about 3 pm. Much cooler &amp;  we are running close to shore all night. a very brilliant spectacle as the moon rises, showing land on both sides</p>
