
March 1918Thursday 21
Messines4.35 am Fritz is having a great time. For the last quarter of an hour he has pummeled the line and BH. Hdq both, right and left. 2 am. a shell probably an 8" fell on "6" subs hut 9 casualties. Young McCarron died of wounds at 14th C.C.S.At 7.7 a.m. S.O.S. through Bdge,and cease fire at 7.35 am.Fritz shelled corduroy, and around 53rd and our positions, fairly constantly throughout the day. Went down to O.P. with Kerr and laid a new line to Spy Farm. Had a few around our residence - small ones
