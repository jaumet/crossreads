<p>(4/3)</p>
<p>5-65 for a pot of black tea &amp; a handfull of biscuits later we had a decent meal for 8-1 at a less stylish place.</p>
<p>Everyone behaved well as far as I saw.  Expected to hear more music.  Got to the train early to get a seat but it was about 1&frac12; hours late starting &amp; very slow when it did.  Whally is doing the cooking this week.</p>
<p>Tuesday 12</p>
<p>No thrills.</p>
<p>Wed. 13th</p>
