I am writing Mother by this mail also, I have instructed her to draw any amount of my cash &amp; buy me some winter underwear "Dr Jaegers" heavyweight 2 singlets &amp; 2 underdrawers a heavy cap comforter mittens &amp; cardigan vest &amp; gloves, give Mother a hand Lizzie dear. Go to Peapes Ltd &amp; dont spare expense for by gum we are in for a rough time when the winter starts properly. I am afraid dear that your sheepskin vest has been lost, I have another one but would have liked to have got yours.
