<p>April 1916<br/>2nd April-   We are traveling South &amp; have passed Wilson's Prom.  So far the weather has been splendid &amp; we have had a very plesant time.<br/>3rd   We have passed Melbourne &amp; the weather is beginning to get bad.<br/>4th   We are now in the Australian Bight &amp; the weather is very rough &amp; a heavy sea is running.  Many of the chaps are sick &amp; the parades have been cancelled.<br/>5th   The weather is getting worse &amp; heavy showers of rain are falling at intervals</p>
