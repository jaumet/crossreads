<p>go for his life as I thought I was done, but no he came back and was pulling me down the trench to the dressing station, anyway that happened about half past ten, I layed in the trench till the early hours of the morning and then off I went to the dressing station and from there on to the ambulance waggon which was waiting just behind the line.</p>
<p>I soon got a move on through a few dressing stations and on to what is know as No 3 clearing station where you get the train for Etaples, which I did after about a day's rest at this station.</p>
<p>I went to the Canadian hospital at Etaples which is mostly Marques, I was in x ward and the second afternoon we were there</p>
