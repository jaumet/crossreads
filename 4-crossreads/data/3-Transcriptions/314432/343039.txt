<p>about 7 kilos we struck the main road to Peronne, &amp; on the right side Clery-Sur-Somme where the ground is high, of chalky nature, &amp; a network of German trenches had been dug. Our position now was almost opposite Bazincourt Wood, the bridges which we had intended to cross on the previous day.<br/>We then switched off the road, into a trench named "Glen Alley" we had to crawl through this. It was only 3 feet deep. Battn Hqrs was established at the entrance to it</p>
