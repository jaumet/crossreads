 Warloy-Baillon 27/6/17 Two days ago I had to shift my quarters from a comfortable billet to my present very unpleasant abode.  Unpleasant, because the people are too confounded mingy to waste breath passing the time of day &ndash; one has to be in early else one finds oneself locked out.  For instance, last night I had pressing business &ndash; ahem &ndash; which prevented my arrival  "home"  ?? until eleven oclock and I found the door locked.
