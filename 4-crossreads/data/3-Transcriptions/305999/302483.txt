<p>11 PM a little destroy came along side and gave us orders to fetch up the anchor and get under way in which we did with not a sound</p>
<p>25/4/15<br/>We came in sight of Anzac at about 4 oclock in the morning a lot of troops where on destroyers and war-ships but we where on the troop ship until about 5.30 AM when a lot of small boats and naval steem pinices came to take us ashore</p>
