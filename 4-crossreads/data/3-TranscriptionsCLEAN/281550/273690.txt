  
  July  When I first heard them talking to one another I could hardly believe my ears. The poor kids speak to the German kids in English & the Germans answer them in German, neither can understand the other, Its very funny to watch them. The Eldest girl does not like Germany & wants to go back to England, The second eldest says that she does not like the English, because they  take all the food from the poor Germans. 
 I must mention this little incident as follows, It occurred ond day whilst I was working on the Salt making job. 
 One day some little while back I was working at No 2 salt room in the factory, And was using a barrow to wheel the salt about from one place to another, When a German who that morning had stated work for the first time for some months, came and claimed the barrow as his property.
