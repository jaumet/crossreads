<p>1915<br/>Feb. 5 Fri.<br/>Fighting has taken place on the Canal &ndash; nearly 400 Turks captured &amp; sent to Cairo - few casualties on our side.<br/>No. 1 General Hospital is established at Heliopolis; No.1 Stationary is at Mahdi [Maadi]; No. 2 Stationary is at Mena Camp here. <br/>Knocked off at 10 p.m.</p>
