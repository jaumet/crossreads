<p>Tuesday<br/>5 Oct. 1915</p>
<p>R.M.S. "Osterley"</p>
<p>Dear Katherine</p>
<p>Just a line to let you all know at Burrabogie that all's well so far:  I hope the season continues good at home and that the great sale on Oct. 20 turns out a success.</p>
<p>Love to my little God Son.  I hope he lives long &amp; flourishes, and that his legs grow fatter than his Uncle's.</p>
<p>Bad wether till today has made the fishes fat &ndash; 70&percnt; casualties among the passengers.  I have been</p>
