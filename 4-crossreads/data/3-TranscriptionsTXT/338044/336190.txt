Some of the buildings are fine from an exterior view  conspicuous is the Cathedral, a remarkable edifice, the old castle, the big convent on the height &amp; the marvellous high bridge. Our little escort, the small cruiser dropped back &amp; we gave her three rousing cheers as we passed &amp; she earned them  Always on the alert, darting from side to side of our boat she was a good companion &amp; earned the admiration of our boys. When we parted it seemed like losing a friend. The sailors returned our cheers &amp;  my opinion of the British Navy has gone up.
