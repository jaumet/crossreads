<p>At Sea<br/>17.10.16<br/>My Dear Sis,<br/>I suppose by the time you receive this letter you will be a mother. I sincerely hope you will have an easy time  &amp; free from all danger. I did not go to see you again as I thought it would be better not to as it would only have upset you. You will readily understand that that was my motive for not going to see you again before I left. <br/>Well dear I'm well on my way now  &amp; have not been a bit sick. We had it very rough</p>
