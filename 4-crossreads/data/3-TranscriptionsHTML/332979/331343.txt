<p>were marched away to the Drill Ground for Practice. After being Shewn how to put the Masks on we were given the Signal to put then over our heads. This had to be done in less than 6 seconds. The sensation was strange at first. The Masks are placed in a Water proof bag inside of a satchel which is hung over the left shoulder. After taking the Mask out it had to be opened &amp; put over the head &amp; the Valve inside is put in the Mouth &amp; you can breath through your nose or mouth but you must exhale through the valve, after we had done this a few times to the satisfaction of the Instructor we were then dismissed for the Day.
