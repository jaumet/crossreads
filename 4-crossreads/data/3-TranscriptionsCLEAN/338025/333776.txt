  
 Saturday 25th August 1917 
 Took two pack mules to nr. Shrapnel corner taking away 18lb ammunition from old pits and dumping it at the side of the road. Seven Taubes flew overhead just as we were knocking off. Dropped bombs very close. Arr. camp about 6 pm. 
 Sunday 26th 
 Grooming and cleaning lines. Half holiday 12 till 4pm. Very heavy rain at 10pm.
