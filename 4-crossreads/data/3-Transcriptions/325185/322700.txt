<p>rise I expect Bert will be here shortly now I would like to meet him. There is a chance we shant get together you see now the Batt is reorganised it is hard to say what they will do with the Reins. They have used a good few of them to build the new Batts up. Well father I don't know if you will receive this o not don't hope you do wont worry at no letters for a while. It is hard to say when we shall be abel to write again. I am not writing any more letters just now. You can tell all the reason why<br/>Have to close. Love to all<br/>From your<br/>Sincere son<br/>Clyde.</p>
