<p>Yesterday a chap fell from the deck right through the hatch &amp; down to the steps of the lower deck it's a wonder how he never broke his neck or some bones for it is a very nasty tumble he was knocked unconscious &amp; they carried him away to hospital.</p>
<p>While we were having tea this evening a Sgt who is attached to the Steward of this boat to help with the rations came down &amp; wanted to know which sort of fruit we preferred, for he said that a lot of fruit was going to be bought at Port Said for the troops &amp; the steward wished to know what we would like, the steward suggested dates &amp; figs but this did in no way agree with our tastes &amp; ideas we promptly turned this down &amp; voted</p>
