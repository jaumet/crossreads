About 5 oclock the Town Major came down to our camp gave me some more orders as regards the tents. 
There are 30 of us altogether from the 1st Division, I believe we are on our way to Bristol or Scotland, where we will go to our Training School, how lucky were we to get away for a big scap is coming off.
16th. This morning General Walker inspected us gave us a lot of advice told us where we were going to, Etaps the great Australian training Camp is to be our first stop there we are to have platoons Company's to drill before
