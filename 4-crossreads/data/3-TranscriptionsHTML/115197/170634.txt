<p><u>June 1st</u></p>
<p>Sunday.&nbsp; Church Service on the &quot;Rio Pardo&quot;.&nbsp; Our Padre a very popular man.</p>
<p>The boat shows a daily increase in speed.&nbsp; We are now approaching the Canary Islands.&nbsp; The first instalment of my long &quot;English&quot; letter is written and it will be continued on subsequent Sundays.</p>
<p>During the evening</p>
