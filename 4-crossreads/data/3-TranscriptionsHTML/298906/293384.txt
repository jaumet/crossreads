<p>In the afternoon we went for a route march &amp; hit the Main road &amp; all along there were gangs of German prisoners working repairing the road &amp; etc, one of the men in my platoon had a couple of ribbons of the Germans, a first class &amp; a second of the Iron Cross &amp; these he showed to Germans here &amp; there, they recognized them at once you would have laughed if you could have seen the grabs they made at them.<br/>27th My luck was out last night just as we were leaving our billets I got grabbed for a picquet, we were all going down to Mericourt to see the fight between Mellish of our Transport &amp; Norton of A.
