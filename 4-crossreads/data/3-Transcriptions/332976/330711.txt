<p>9th to. 2nd Aug.  Acting Company S.M. (in place of Pasfield) of 1st Divnl. Training Coy.<br/>Two new drafts from Australia came into camp during that time, one being the 1st Reinf. of the 11th Field Coy (3rd. Divn)  which are at present on Salisbury Plain.<br/>[indecipherable]<br/>July 12th<br/>Schools were formed &amp; the Engrs. were divided into four School parties for instruction in Field Engineering &amp; Musketry under the supervision of the R.Es.</p>
