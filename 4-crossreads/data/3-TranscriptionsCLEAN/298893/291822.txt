
19th BattPopes Post4-12-15
Capt Hale,Last night my fatigue party went down to Anzac for bombs with the other Batt fatigues, They assisted to load the mules & came back without any bombs for this Battalion.Re the Garland bombs, Corp Conley who has charge of the Garland mortar reported to me this morning that 6 bombs did not explode last night he says that the Geletine in the bombs is very poor.A GrayRSM
