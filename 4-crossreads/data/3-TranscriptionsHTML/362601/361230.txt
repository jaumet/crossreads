<p>1 July, Tuesday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Major has liver &amp; makes all the others buzz around. Go to Boo-Keeping. Sleep downstairs on the floor. I go in Crib Tourney &amp; miss first round we fasten Ports as Gale is expected. We are warned to parade come on deferred pay.</p>
<p>Sit in Oper Theatre &amp; read M's letters. I am very much overcome.</p>
<p>2 July, Wednesday</p>
<p>W.<br/>Gale raging. I mess around all morning. Go to Book-Keeping in afternoon. Play draughts before going on Duty. We develop films in pantry after [?] pm.</p>
<p>Go to bed on seat &amp; pack myself in like a parcel about midnight. Arise at 6 am &amp; have K bustle. Music makes me very thoughtful about my chere solin. Lecture on deciduous fruits.</p>
