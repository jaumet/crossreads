<p>16 September 1918 Monday<br/>Received a Parcel from Packing Store Sydney with no name of sender contained loaf sugar Chewing gum etc:-  I wonder who the donor is?  How about asking May what clothes Jack wants &amp; give him some of mine-  I think the farther a man gets away from Poverty- the greater the fear of it- if once known.<br/>Letter from Joe Laura 12/8/18 the former will go back soon   I think He will be disappointed but I think it is all for the best <strike>and that a</strike><br/>(Rest of page blacked out)</p>
