<p>1 Th<br/>Moved about 3 miles further W  Turks returning towards Gaza   having a bad time for rations</p>
<p>2 Fri<br/>Outpost night  moved on to a Turk position about 9 am  met opposition  Cpl Thompson killed  Snipers very active and good shots  occupied hills day</p>
<p>3 Sat<br/>Outpost night still occupied with enemy   9,000 prisoners 80 guns  heavy fighting on our left  dog tired and sleepy</p>
