<p>6th September 1915</p>
<p>Martin called today</p>
<p>Oh for the six&nbsp;months that I lived as his second, &amp;&nbsp;as a loyal officer to him</p>
<p>Green food</p>
<p>Filthy Conversation</p>
<p>Want of discipline</p>
<p>Crime after Crime</p>
<p>Starvation of Men</p>
<p>Not able to manage even the parsons</p>
