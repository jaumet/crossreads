 23 May. Friday 
 F. QM inspection. Pack bag. Read all afternoon. Concert by "Waratahs" &amp; lecture on "League of Nations" great crush to get in Hall so I get bowled over Kelsey lectures. Concert not extra good. 
 Wrote M.R.L. Auntie Annie. 
 24 May, Saturday 
 F. Hop on motor to Sarum. Buy present for MRL. See Nelson Watch, Hope diamond imitation. Go to close on River. Say Good Bye. Return of 1st Wilts. Crowds in Salsibury. Very sad &amp; Concert at night. Can't sleep in feather bed. Lachry Mose. Rec'd letter M.R.L. [indecipherable]. Stay with Mr Edwards. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie
