My Diary from 11th May 1916 Tel-el-Kebir
Orders came for us to be prepared to move out to the Canal where a big fight is in progress. General Muster Parade for inspection by the Colonel. I had my squad out. Col complimented us on our work.
Friday 12th MayAnniversary of my landing at Gallipoli celebrated quietly. We had our first meeting of our Sergeants Debating Society. The subject Town versus Country life. I took TownLife and Country
