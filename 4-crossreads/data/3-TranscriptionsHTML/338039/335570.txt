<p>Feb.8th.<br/>"One Day" (Glyn) - good but hot weather- Rifle &amp; Physical Drill competitions among Infantry- Tug-o'-War won by Artillery- Correspondence<br/>Feb.9th.<br/>Gulf of Aden- Arabian Coast &amp; Islands- Lantern lecture by Capt Jenks &ndash; Practice with ships gun- Belt Parade.<br/>Feb.10th.<br/>Pass "Twelve Apostles" Islands Also many vessels.  The Red Sea- First &amp; only death on Board- L. H'man- Burial at sea- impressive ceremony.</p>
