<p>Saturday 1917 4th August<br/>Yesterday the Corned Beef was very hard to take at dinner time so we got extra chees for tea. It was mostely uneatable today the Pudding was condemed by Dr &amp; was not replaced &ndash; This shows true economy. Fire &amp; Boat Drill also yesterday.</p>
<p>Noticed many flying fish today. Boxing this afternoon The Ballmoral Castle dropped out for gun practice this evening &ndash; could hear the shell bursts distinctly. This is the first day in the 4th year of the War</p>
