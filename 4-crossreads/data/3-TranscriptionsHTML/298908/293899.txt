She looked as rusty &amp; as dirty as could be as did a couple of squarheads who were standing on her deck she was flying the White Ensign &amp; all eyes were turned upon her &amp; many were the curses chucked at her &amp; her likes.  We dropped anchor right close to a Japanese cruiser but did not remain long about 5 oclock a chap who had got on the boat somehow was caught &amp; sent off, he nearly got away.
