
Sunday 21/1/17
Depot closes on 28th went to Corps and also to Ribermont re same. First Draft leaves tomorrow. Wrote to Doff.
Monday 22/1/17
First draft left & were very good. Had a visit from Somerville.
Tuesday 23/1/17
Visited by Birdwood & White - gave lecture on Map Reading & Field Sketching - bitterly cold return home from Divisional School.
Wednesday 24/1/17
Second Draft away. School closes on Saturday - am removing all equipment etc.
