
23rdFatigue in morning, & had a swim.  Trench digging night.
24thRested all day.
25thRested in morning.  Digging in afternoon.  Lord Nelson came up and bombarded Kilid Bahr.
26thTrench digging in afternoon.
27thQuarter-master's fatigue.
28thWent into reserve trenches in support of the 5th, who made an attack on the right.
29thTrench digging at night, but the Turks opened out on us with machine-guns, & we retired.
30thShifted into reserve trenches on the right.  Colonel Cox returned.  Big rainstorm at night.
July1stRested all day.
2ndWater fatigue.
3rdFiring line.
