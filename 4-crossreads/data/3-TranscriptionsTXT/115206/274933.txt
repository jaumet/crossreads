 December  1918. Tuesday 31 
 At Sea. Indian Ocean. "Karoola." Weather fair not quite so warm today. Passed [indecipherable] at 8 am. Commenced the second round of the Tournament. One of our members died a chap named Private [indecipherable] from W.A. he was buried at "Lights out." There was no actual attendance of the patients. Hard luck for his people. Had a nice iced glass of beer to wish the New Year in. Awoke at midnight to hear a Scotchman playing the pipes. Passed Aden at 10.30 pm. Travelled 251 miles only
