<p>No (3</p>
<p>Franurra<br/>Rooty<br/>Hill</p>
<p>Wensday</p>
<p>Dear Dad.</p>
<p>I am going to give puppy a bath to day and muct not for get and he is such a funny little puppy he is a real silly little puppy and we call him bing after the bing boys. and he is licking my legs and arms wasent he bad to do that to me. and he is only six weeks old and he is very big I must go and lift him up on the vrander he is crying and growling and when I am writing he is a necunce to me and every time I dip my pen in the ink I hafto put the cork on a gain isent he a noucence I will hafto go and write on the table now.  All of us are going over to grand mas at mail time and we have sent a a pacle to you we sent it yesterday and on the other letters I could not write much.<br/>xxx etc.</p>
<p>[Transcriber's note: "The Bing Boys" was the name of a series of revues playing in London during the last two years of WW1.]</p>
