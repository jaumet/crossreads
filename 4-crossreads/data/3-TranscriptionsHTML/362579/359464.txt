<p>back to Southall. The train took twice as long on account of the fog, got back safely about 10.30. p.m. The London fog is   <u>some</u>  fog. Worked all next day. Friday afternoon did shopping for Xmas. Met a friend at 6. p.m. in London. Had Dinner with him, then saw a good show   &amp;  got back about usual time 10.30. p.m. Had a most enjoyable time. He is such a nice boy. An old patient I nursed at Jerries (Moore's) Hosp. Only a private but a great boy (very young)   &amp;  he was delighted at me going with him, poor boy is on crutches.</p>
<p>Worked Saturday   &amp;  Sunday-morn. Sunday-afternoon went to Southwell Gardens, had tea there, left my Xmas gifts then rushed out to Myra's got there about 6.p.m.   &amp;  left my Xmas Gifts &ndash; Got back safely at usual time. Very tired next morn but worked hard all day   &amp;  fairly late (decorating).</p>
