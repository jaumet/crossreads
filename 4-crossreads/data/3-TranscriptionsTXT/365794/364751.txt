
Thursday 13 December 1917Wrote Rob & I. Hunt & from the latter recd. a long & "homey" letter which was very enjoyable.  All goes on well, dated 10/10/17 (day we were in Colombo).Off duty 10-1 so went town with S. Allen.  Kursaal first for tickets then coffee & cakes at Groppi's, home 12 noon & duty 8.30 p.m. as on alone & pt. adm. with badly injured leg, compound fracture of tibia (R), very offensive quantity of bone removed & pus pouring away.S. Brown leaving us & going to Cantara!! [also spelt Kantara]
