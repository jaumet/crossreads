<p>June 21 clean hut, but we wont be here long.<br/>Rather nice here by the quiet backwater, with the green low lying hills in the foreground &amp; the intermittent chirping of the birds. And what a change after the everlasting desert, the intolerable heat &amp; the dirty niggers of Egypt. In this secluded spot there is a peace &amp; quietness not found in the vastness of the desert.
