<p>Saturday 29 July 1916<br/>Stables in morning.<br/>No Grenade school owing to sports being held.<br/>Got letter No. 5 from Father &amp; Mother dated 22 June.  They seem to be having good time with car.<br/>On Canteen guard in evening.</p>
<p>Sunday 30 July 1916<br/>On guard all day.</p>
