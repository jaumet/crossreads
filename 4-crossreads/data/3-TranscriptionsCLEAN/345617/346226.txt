
Octobre 31No. 2 Section in camp having a rest after their bad day yesterday. Cpl Gough went to Hanebeke this morning & buried the men who were killed yesterday. Sap Busby had been blown right away & could not be found. Enemy aeroplanes are very busy bombing & doing a lot of damage.
Novembre 1Working at Belgian Battery Corner putting up shelters for H.A.C. Heavy bombing by enemy aeroplanes last night & a lot of damage was done. Two bombs were dropped in our camp - one Sap was killed & two were slightly wounded. Sap McInnis was the man killed.
