<p>10 Th May<br/>As usual on front with Aeroplanes very busy with bombs  Aus mail in<br/>letter from D. N.G. also writing all lights out at night</p>
<p>11 Fri May<br/>not feeling too well had a terrible job to finish letters  Usual on front<br/>large quantities of stores being removed back.  Big attack expected</p>
<p>12 Sat May<br/>Usual on front  Artillery very active  two Divisions arrived and camped at Khan Yunus  Keeping very warm and dusty</p>
