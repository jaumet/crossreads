
Thursday 2 September 1915 Not out all day. Major Purdy took tea with me at 8 p.m. Letters from home to 25th July  Car, Joseph, Buddie, Kitty & a lot of friends. Reports good in all ways. 
Friday 3 September 1915 In Cairo for one hour during the morning. About hospital rest of the day. 
Saturday 4 September 1915 Not out all day Father King from Melbourne partook of tea with me a 7-30 pm  He looked after the Roman Catholics during the afternoon. 
Sunday 5 September 1915 Mass here during the morning, I served. 23. Communicants. At Convent Zeitoun, between 6 & 8 pm.
