
Sunday 27 January 1918See last date.Called on Musgrove but he was away.  Lunched with Tibbitts.  Tead at Regents with Dad & Chicken.  Saw Musgrove there.
Monday 28 January 1918Down to Fovant by 6.35 train.  Rup got a month's leave.  Cheers!  Properly fed up with that awful place!!  On to B'mouth & stayed at the Highcliffe.
Tuesday 29 January 1918Did a good deal of shopping in B'mouth in morning.  Had boots & watch fixed up.  Rup & I went to the Hippodrome in evening & saw "Flying Colors".  Awful show.
Wednesday 30 January 1918Turned out very late & went for stroll along the cliffs.  Splendid day out.  To Boscombe in the afternoon & back for tea.
Thursday 31 January 1918Rode out to Poole in a s'nice little dogcart & pony!!  Tead at Bobby's.
Friday 1 February 1918Left for town at 11.0 but could not get a lunch car.  Saw Mrs. Mehan re Ede's job.  Stayed at No. 10.  Very foggy.  No air Raid!!
Saturday 2 February 1918Jolly cold compared to B'mouth!  Had a topping lunch of Lobster salad at Scotts.  Matinee at "Pamela".  Not too bad.
