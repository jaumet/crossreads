 "Campbellfield" St Albans 17.4.1918 
 My dear Friends 
 Just to let you see that I had at least made the attempt to send you a long letter I am sending the following which was written during the first few days at Cannes but has never been re-written for you.  I think you will all know why &ndash; so I need not apologise &ndash; and I am hoping to make amends by sending another within a month which will include the G.G.S. for on second thoughts I am sure that any little [indecipherable] will be as nothing to what you are reading to-day or since the great offensive started. 
 Go Lady Gifford Hotel de le Esterel Cannes Feb 1st 1918
