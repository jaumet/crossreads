<p>Aunt Mirna more tucker &amp; a pair of sox</p>
<p>Saturday 16th February<br/>Work as usual. A very clear day plenty of Planes about Fritz seems to be kept well back Bell &amp; I walked to Ypres this afternoon about 2 miles A few houses are left standing except for a few shell holes through them. The Cloth Hall &amp; Cathedral are terribly battered even today 3 shells</p>
