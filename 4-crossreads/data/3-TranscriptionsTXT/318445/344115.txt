The excited men rested on the intermediate objective and there were calmed by their officers; they were shaking hands violently and smoking cigarettes; they greeted each other, "to such an extent that you would have thought they were having a brothers' meeting" .( Private P.Morris. See page 7 , appendix 4) Our troops resumed the advance at 6 am. There were 1500 yards of open country yet to be crossed, much of it being covered with a webwork of wire and a network of trenches.
