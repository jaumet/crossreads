<p>quiet and once more we received orders to move down south.</p>
<p>Apr 8th &ndash; 18<br/>At 4.30PM all packed up and nothing to do but loll about till 1-30AM at which time we commenced our journey to the Somme<br/>Several proceeded per horses while others used the boot leaving the remainder including myself to get a wriggle on per bike.</p>
<p>Our first stage was soon over as it only needed an hour to reach Hopoutre Siding where we had a cold wait for 5 hours before we began loading our gear, wagons etc</p>
