
Friday 15 September 1916On Q.M. fatigue in morning carrying cases amunition.  Each man to have 200 rounds.  Spent day getting things ready.Leave at 6.15 tonight to go out after Jhonnie.
Saturday 16 September 1916Rode all night and camped all day at Bir-el-abd.Good aeroplane dual in the morning but our plane had to imsky.
