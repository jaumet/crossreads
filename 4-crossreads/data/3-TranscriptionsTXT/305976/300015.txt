Saturday June 22 Went over to 1ST FA today Just as Tom &amp; I landed there Jericho Jane dropped one in the camp &amp; we had to get down for our lives. We were in a funk hole for nearly two hours while Jacko heaved them all round the camp. Killed several horses. Sunday June 23 Jacko shelled us again to day.
