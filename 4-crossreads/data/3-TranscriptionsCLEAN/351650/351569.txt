
Tuesday5 Oct. 1915
R.M.S. "Osterley"
Dear Katherine
Just a line to let you all know at Burrabogie that all's well so far:  I hope the season continues good at home and that the great sale on Oct. 20 turns out a success.
Love to my little God Son.  I hope he lives long & flourishes, and that his legs grow fatter than his Uncle's.
