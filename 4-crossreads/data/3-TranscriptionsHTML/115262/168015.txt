<p>Wednesday 5 September 1917.</p>
<p>Sports today at Shiekh&nbsp;<s>Narang</s> Nuran&nbsp;and to be concluded&nbsp;<s>tomorrow</s>&nbsp;Friday. A Taube came over about 11 am. &amp; although shelled from all directions escaped unharmed to his own lines. Wrote letters in time between reliefs on the picquet. Did first lot of cooking&nbsp;&ndash; a success too.</p>
<p>Boys returned from Marakeb Beach, &amp; another detachment went to take their place (Tent Division.)</p>
