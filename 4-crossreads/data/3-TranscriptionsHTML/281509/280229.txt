<p>1916 6 January 6<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Sleep in [indecipherable] till 10.15am.  Roused for genrl parade &amp; medical inspection----once a month. Sergeant leaving ward.  Tony to be given charge.</p>
<p>Lecture in Rec. Tent by Capt. Willis on Personnel &amp; Equipment of Field Ambulances at Front.<br/>Inoculated 2nd time.</p>
<p>Mrs Gelling &amp; party bring flowers to ward.  Invite me to tea. Go &amp; enjoy it.</p>
<p>Back to "C" Ward at 7 pm to lecture by Capt. Willis&mdash;special class.  Gotain's Aspirator, Wright,s Capsule, Southey's tube: Haematemeters etc.</p>
<p>Still very hot &amp; sultry</p>
