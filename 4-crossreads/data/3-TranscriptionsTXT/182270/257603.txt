 SATURDAY, OCTOBER 18, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; very hot. Tickle &amp; I mustered 
 Middle paddock wethers and 
 put the rams out of them in the 
 Cow paddock &amp; 452 wethers in the 
 Boundary and 378 in 4D. The 
 rest are in Mountain ewes. 
 Mustered Mountain ewes &amp; lambs 
 &amp; put them in bottom wheat 
 paddock. 
 Alan came home in morning.
