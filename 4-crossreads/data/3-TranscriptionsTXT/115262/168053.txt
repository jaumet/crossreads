 Saturday 13 October 1917. 
 Full dress parade with camels &amp; ourselves in full marching order. Inspection by O.C. 
 &quot;Jacko&quot; has not been over for a day or so. 
 Had a narrow escape from being killed tonight at the aerodrome after having seen through the R.F.C. section of it. 
 A.C.F.A&nbsp;Football team played A.F.C&nbsp;on Aerodrome football ground &amp; were beaten in the last quarter.
