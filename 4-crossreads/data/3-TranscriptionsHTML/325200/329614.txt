<p>civilian can come to Port Said unless he has some very excellent reason.  Hence there are comparatively few civilians there.<br/>As I predicted I did not have any delicious amusement there though I enjoyed my stay very much.<br/>But the place is chock full of soldiers  &amp; so there are many restrictions  &amp;  thus one has to make ones own amusement; and to do so a companion who has much the same tastes  &amp;  temperament is essential.</p>
