<p>26th Aug.<br/>1000 Matunga proceeded to sea followed by "Wolf". Both ships stopped about 10' off the land &amp; preparations were made for sinking Matunga<br/>130 First bomb exploded port side engine [indecipherable] &amp; ship settled quickly by stern with heavy list to port.<br/>132 Second bomb exploded &amp; ship straightened up with after deck awash.</p>
