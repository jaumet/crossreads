
Sunday 6 August 1916Played Piano for Church.  Played too loud, gave no breathing time & no expression.  Malish.
Monday 7 August 1916Sick Parade.  I get a cold from Playing Football.
Tuesday 8 August 1916One letter from Bess.  Went next door & watched arrival of 130 Turkish Prisoners.
Wednesday 9 August 1916Leave all day.  Played Billiards, saw Australian wounded come in to Cairo.  Dinner Y.M.C.A., Tea at Groppi's.
Thursday 10 August 1916I go on S.P. 2 days, no drill.  Cois Cateer.  Play Cards with neighbours W.D. & Tommy W.
Friday 11 August 1916Ward Master.  Finish top floor of Mel. Pav.  Work finishes.
Saturday 12 August 1916Route March.  I am well away.  I am O. Corp. & Postman.  I help sort letters.  I get 2 from Home, one Hilda & one May Coppin.  Parade at 4 p.m. & I don't hear the Bugle.  Shepherd's Band plays Y.M.C.A.  Dick Rowe comes to Tea.
