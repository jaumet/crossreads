
Isn't this simply absurd paper & I have simply nothing to write about.
Still plenty of noise about & still plenty of good news trickling in.  Hope things continue to go well.
Suppose you are back home again.  I suppose the weather is just glorious again.  How is Grahame & the Boss & old Mac.  Most insulting to call him old.
Did I tell you I missed seeing Stuart in England.  I had lunch with his Father & Mother.  Stuart wrote me a most violently insulting letter giving me hell.  I deserved it, I did rather mull things.
