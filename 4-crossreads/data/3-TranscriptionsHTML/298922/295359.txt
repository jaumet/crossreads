<p>Friday 2 February 1917</p>
<p>One gets very tired of the eternal sand, in face, ears tucker Bed etc. One continual dust storm for a week &ndash; We must eat lbs of it cooking in the open &ndash; but again MUD is worse.</p>
<p>Terrific sand storm. Think the worst we have had.</p>
<p>Enemy airships over again last night. <strike>Got pen going again.</strike></p>
<p>Allanson is despatch rider now.</p>
<p>Did I explain a new nice sort of sandal the Infantry wear in about 4 sizes bigger than boots &amp; strapped on like snow shoes the idea is to make marching easier.</p>
