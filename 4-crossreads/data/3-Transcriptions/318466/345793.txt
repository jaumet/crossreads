<p>Sunday 19 May 1918<br/>Major Lucas &amp; Anslow, Auntie Lu, Aunty Ema &amp; Rose, Ede &amp; Dorothy came to aid.<br/>Air Raid 11 p.m.</p>
<p>Monday 20 May 1918<br/>Reached Hospital at 3 p.m.  The Air Raid last night caused quite a stir here.  No damage done though.</p>
<p>Tuesday 21 May to Saturday 25 May 1918<br/>[No comments recorded on these days.]</p>
