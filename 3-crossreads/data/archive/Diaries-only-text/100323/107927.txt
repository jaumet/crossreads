6/7/19 "Chemnitz"
Morning found us off Dover with its white chalk cliffs. A steady sea running and a dull sky. We voyaged along steadily all day passing other craft at intervals. McLean was busy drawing up orders for the ship and Miles and Galway each as energetic in their own department. A confined cramped space on a ship and one very soon tires of it.
