<p>68 Gallipoli Peninsula</p>
<p>30/11/15</p>
<p>today on the peninsula is not too bad. The sun has come for the first time this week and in that time it has been snowing here, and it knows how to snow here when it starts and it is playing up with the Boys. here the Doctors parade in the morning is a very big one. The men are suffering from frost Bite and it is not the best of complaints from what I have Seen of it. The toes Drop off the feet</p>
