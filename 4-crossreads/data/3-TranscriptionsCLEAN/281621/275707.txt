  
 Sunday 15 The country about here is made up of undulating downs dotted here & there with woods & villages & on the 
 Monday 16 whole just now is very pretty, especially in the woods. I am debating within myself as to whether I should apply for a com & if augmenting my chances of being killed or wounded, at least being able to live decently & get out of this rotten life. 
 Thursday 19 Still here doing nothing & enjoying books and fine weather. One book Ernest Maltravers by Lytton has impressed me very much. I have continued my walkings about the country & have found some 
 Friday 20 very pleasant spots. This Commission business has a good hold of my mind 
 Sunday 22 Visit the neighbouring town of Andover which is very slow & quiet
