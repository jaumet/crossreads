<p>for the welfare of the soldiers. Passing along we saw the big motor transport section of the Army Service Corps. This is a sight of a lifetime to see, acres and acres of big transport wagons, all spotlessly clean, lined up in rows upon rows and as far as you can see. They must have cost millions of money and the petrol stores are immense. Close by are the big stables for horses and mules. The latter are used to a great extent here for all kinds of transport and there are simply hundreds of them but the sight of all is the horses and their stables. Quite a small army of horses are here and like grooms to attend them. They are well fed and in the pink of condition and the piles of hay etc make quite a paddock full of stacks.
