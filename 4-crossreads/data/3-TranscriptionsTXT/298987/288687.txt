Saturday 18thWere to be payed 8/- today. Had to sign for 10/- 2/- being held to cover shortages in ships stuff such as knives forkes hammocks etc. These cannot be made up untill we land & should not come to more than 6d each. There was so much dissatisfaction over signeing for more than was received that it has ended by not getting any pay at all Identification Discs issued today.
