<p>May 30th<br/>Still at Flixecourt &ndash; bad news from French &amp; British Fronts &ndash; Fall of Soissons &ndash; Australians upholding their great rep.,  Show as usual to a packed house.<br/>May 31st<br/>Continuation of fine "Home" weather -  Write poem "The Woodland" Long Rehearsal &ndash; Bob Bower takes a run to Amiens &amp; returns with some necessaries &ndash; I make debut with my own monologue, entitled "The Opium Den" &ndash; Most Successful.</p>
