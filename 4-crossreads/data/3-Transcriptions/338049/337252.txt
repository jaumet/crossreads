<p>Monday 11 Feb.<br/>With 4 cyclists went out to RD2 to locate fault. Found it early and going to Y for test. Go sleeves from H.P. &amp; jointed cables identifying them later.</p>
<p>Home very tired indeed receiving kind treatment from chaps here. Arranging at night to meet M. wagon at 7-15 a.m.</p>
