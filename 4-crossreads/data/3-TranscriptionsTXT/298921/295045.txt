 March, 1915 Saturday 13 my a/c No 10850 with the Commonwealth BK of Australia London making 200/-/- there to my Credit. We spend a lot here, an officer is expected to go to all the most expensive parts of every place &amp; every one is out to rob &amp; get Backsheesh. We only take 35 lbs luggage from here &amp; our Blankets, &amp; expect to leave on Tuesday the 16th Mar probably Alexandretta, but will let you know later meantime I post you this with love to you all.
