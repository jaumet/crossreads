  
 Saturday 21/7/17 Brigade surprise night attack returned to Camp at 8 am, resting. Sunday 22/7/17 Church parade - meeting of C.O.s - Night stunt. Monday 23.7.17 Div. attack with barrage, returned to Camp at 9 am. Muster parade at 3.30 pm. Tuesday 24/7/17 Battalion Route march, moving on Saturday next. Wednesday 25/7/17 Battalion Bombing Scheme carried out well. Thursday 26/7/17 Bn employed on fatigues generally for Corps (IV) orders received for move.
