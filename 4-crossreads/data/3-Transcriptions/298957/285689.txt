<p>8th Field Ambulance marched well on the 28th inst. Graves of victims in the "Great March" close by.</p>
<p>31st May (Wednesday)<br/>Route march through Nefische with packs up followed by tribes of arabs. They speak English fairly well, especially the youngsters. Travellers to Egypt after the war will be shocked at the fluent way these natives speak the <u>"Australian"</u> language.<br/>Swimming Parade in afternoon to the Lake at Ismailia. 15th Brigade came across the Canal to Moascar today. Letter</p>
