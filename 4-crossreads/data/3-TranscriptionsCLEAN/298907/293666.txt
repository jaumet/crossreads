  
 It was very close & hot yesterday afternoon &  close &  about 7 oclock it started to rain lightly, this had the effect of clearing the atmosphere, so this morning it is nice & fresh though a little cloudy 
 13th.  Sunday & we met the girls for the last time for a week at least for they won't be able to get out worse luck on account of their being so busy, we had a bonza time went boating again & then from the reservoir down to the Botanical Gardens where we put the rest of the afternoon in among the lovely flowers
