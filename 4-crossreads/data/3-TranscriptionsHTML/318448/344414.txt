<p>I was much amused today by one fellow -  rough as guts- as the saying is who declared the subject under discussion being the Jordan Valley in Full Summer, that the man who would live in Jericho in the summer would "Box on in Hell"<br/>14th April<br/>Yesterday we were ordered to move our site so in the afternoon we moved about half a mile into a patch of scrub close to 32 Coy the company to which we are attached for rations etc&hellip;&hellip;</p>
