<p>Wednesday 12/12/17.<br/>Posted letters 2 to Viv one from Grace Bros 1 Manager Com Bank London Wrote to Mr Norrie. Much warmer this morning great artillery battle all night &amp; still going on. I pity those poor chaps in the front line. off tomorrow about 10 for Samer<br/>deferred Pay. &pound;38-12-6. &ndash; 112/11</p>
<p>Thursday. 13/12/17<br/>off this morning to catch Train left at 9 in Train at 1 off through Bailleul, Borre, Lumbre to desvines got here at 10-30 men drunk all over the Train speechless some of them got at some rum on the station at Lumbre. The swine got to bed about 11-30 out of danger zone at last. def &pound;38-14-0 117/5</p>
<p>Friday 14/12/17.<br/>up at 7. BK fell in at rear of Bat &amp; marched through Samer to here Hallingen 14 miles &amp; what a march to finish up had to climb a mountain fully 2 miles long &amp; steep all the way Just like Waterloo Range. Got here about 2 oclock done up, cold &amp; miserable as it is an awful village the worst we have been in &amp; they brought us about 100 miles to this, would sooner have stopped where we were. One Estaminet in the place &amp; no store of any kind deferred &pound;38-15-6 124/11</p>
<p>Saturday 15/12/17<br/>up at 8 no sleep last night too cold &amp; windy awful billets, plenty for us to do here Picqueting the roads deferred Pay &pound;38-17-0 &ndash; 128/5 ended up fine night but frosty &amp; cold.</p>
<p>Sunday 16/12/17<br/>cloudy again &amp; like snow this is an awful place could not find worse if they went all over France &amp; this is our Xmas home Boys sooner be in the trenches. We are on top of</p>
