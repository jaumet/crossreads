<p>Sunday 23 December 1917<br/>Stayed at Redout till about 3.30 when our horses came up for us &amp; went back to Ayun Kara arriving about 6.30.</p>
<p>Monday 24 December 1917<br/>Stayed at Ayun Kara, very wet &amp; windy, got some War Chest fund stuff &amp; a few parcels.</p>
<p>Tuesday 25 December 1917<br/>Quiet day, rained off &amp; on all day.  Put on Q.M. for No. 1.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26 December 1917<br/>Got word of move early very hurried move.  Left about 8.30 &amp; proceeded to Esdud, very muddy trip, rained during day, arrived at dark.</p>
<p>Thursday 27 December 1917<br/>Drawing stuff for Section, quiet clear day.  N.C.O. of picquet at night.</p>
<p>Friday 28 December 1917<br/>Usual routine, got some Canteen stuff.</p>
<p>Saturday 29 December 1917<br/>Parcel mail in.</p>
