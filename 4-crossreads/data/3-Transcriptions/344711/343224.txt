<p>24th<br/>Passed Gabo Island at 6 a.m.  A Queenslander died today, and was buried at sea.  We all came up on deck for the burial service.  Passed Kiama at 9 p.m.  Bush fires all along the coast.</p>
<p>25th<br/>Arrived Sydney Heads at 3 a.m. and anchored in Watson's Bay.  At 9 a.m. we went up the Harbour to No. 1 Wharf, Woolloomooloo Bay.  Were taken in motors to Randwick Hospital &amp; had a good reception all the way.  Had dinner and got leave for 24 hours.  Went up to Riverstone.</p>
<p>30th<br/>Received my pass just in time to catch the Moree mail.</p>
<p>January 11th 1916<br/>Received my discharge as medically unfit.</p>
