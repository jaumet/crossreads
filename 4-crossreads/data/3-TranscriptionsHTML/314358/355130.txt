<p>9<br/>As usual the rear portion of the column slowly understands</p>
<p>The whole line is crushed up and close packed.</p>
<p>Mules raise their heads to avoid baggage in front of them.</p>
<p>Lumbers and houses become a jumble.</p>
<p>Then cramp up and wedge together to give the transports room and to avoid danger from restless animals</p>
<p>The officer leading is doubtful of the way</p>
<p>Who knows?</p>
<p>"Where do you want to get to"<br/>This query comes from us<br/>"Road leading to the Lylle gate, comes the answer through darkness<br/>"You are almost on it. Turn to the left and you're in it"<br/>"Good &ndash; Thanks lad" comes answer through the darkness again</p>
<p>The column moves once more, turns and slowly assumes its easy links of space ridding itself of cramped wrinkles</p>
