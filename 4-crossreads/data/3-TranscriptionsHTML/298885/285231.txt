<p>Pont Asquin is a small but pretty villiage situated on the banks of the Canal Du Nord which has a great length and each bank has its drive for its vehicles from end to end.</p>
<p>At this particular time of the year it presents a very pretty sight and incidentally great advantage is taken of the cool water by the troops who are camped on either bank.</p>
