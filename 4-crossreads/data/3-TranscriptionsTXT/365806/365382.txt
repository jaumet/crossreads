 3 Lucy is seldom out of bed &ndash; and always an invalid &ndash; Robert is still on sick leave with a dilated heart &ndash; his legs are no longer swollen &ndash; but  I  can walk him [indecipherable]!! &ndash; I don't know what the medical board will decide to do with him next week.  I was ultimately given charge of all the people of Nullbank Hospitals &ndash; and I worked myself out &ndash; got an infection in my nose &ndash; boils &ndash; and pus in my antium and ear catarrh &ndash; I went into Hospl for treatment and am now out on sick leave.  I think my autumn is going to get well &ndash; but I've got colds and colds &ndash; and my leg is bad again &ndash; Curiously I feel this winter much more than I did last winter.
