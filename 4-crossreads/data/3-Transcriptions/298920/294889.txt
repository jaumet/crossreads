<p>According to his humor he can tell the most or the least becoming yarn of the whole company.  He is very hospitable and open hearted, and is a very considerate master.  The 1st Mate is a slightly built man of about of about 30 years of age at times he looks 20 &amp; at others 40.  He appears to have had rather a good education but he does not always use the beneficial effects afforded thereby, and his conversation at times develops into a strain that a 15 year old schoolboy would be ashamed to use.  He has rather a bad habit of criticizing things about which he cannot fully understand.</p>
