<p>about once a fortnight and we could go wherever we wanted to&nbsp; Were put on the Armagh boat roll and shifted to another camp. We had nothing to do only eat and were all beginning to get lazy. S.S Armagh cancelled and put on the Delta instead Had inspection &amp; received our boat tickets. Four of us went for a motor ride to Frome &amp; missed the last train So had to settle down</p>
