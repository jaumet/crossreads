
Revue, done by Amateuers on Alexandria  &  things Alexandrian which was quite clever in parts. I also had a much-needed Turkish Bath.Now no one knows where we are going though I fear it will be Bir el Abd.23rdWhile at Alexandria I contracted with one Khalil Ibrahim Saffi for him to send me some cigarettes fortnightly, the first insallment to be sent "le dimanche' it is now Monday  &  elles ne sont pas arrive. The transaction was carried on in bad French which probably explains.
