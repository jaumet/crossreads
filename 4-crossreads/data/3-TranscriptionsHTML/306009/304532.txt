<p>1915<br/>Dec. 16 Thu.<br/>Fighting continued on Egypt's western frontier. On 13th inst. British Troops came into contact with force of 1200 Arabs with guns &amp; machine guns, 24 miles west of Marsa Matrouh. The enemy were driven off with considerable losses.<br/>No.1 Gen. Hosp.,  A.A.M.C. played No. 2 G.H. rugby football on Gezira Sporting Club grounds in the afternoon &amp; we were beaten 9 to 3.<br/>In the evening attended a Y.M.C.A. Bazaar at Connaught House. Very decent.</p>
