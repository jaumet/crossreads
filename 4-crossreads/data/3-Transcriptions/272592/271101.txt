<p>11th.    Went into the firing line last night &amp; relieved D. Coy. things are very, very quiet about here so far, such a difference to the "Somme" the only thing we have to contend with seem to be bombs &amp; a few shells &amp; sniper's.</p>
<p>I have charge of a post named "Tara's Hall" she is a bombing possy &amp; one in the event of an attack I have orders to hold at all cost</p>
<p>The Germans are about 75 yards away &amp; we can hear them working away of a night, driving stakes mixing mortar, &amp; etc. we are on the defensive here for the present, so as to allow us to get on with our work &amp; get things ship shape for the winter, &amp; the Huns seem quite</p>
