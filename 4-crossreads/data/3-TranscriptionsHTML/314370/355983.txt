<p>February, 1916<br/>Thursday 17<br/>Instruction as usual, Semaphore exam &amp; get guns ready for range practice tomorrow.<br/>Go &amp; have a chat w/- Dad &amp; play a couple of games of Bridge.</p>
<p>Friday 18<br/>A most distressing accident occurred this morning.  An Instructor was demonstrating w/- a Garland Grenade Mortar &amp; live grenade, when by some means the grenade exploded in the gun C.S.M Kirton was killed &amp; 5 others more or less severly wounded.</p>
