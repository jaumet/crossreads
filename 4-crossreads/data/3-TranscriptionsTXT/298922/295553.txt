 Friday 31 August 1917 
 Bomb practice &amp; Hotchkiss gun Class &amp; GAS DRILL, pity something could not eventuate out of GAS to Kill SCRUB, Prickly Pears &amp; RABBITS. 
 [The following paragraph crossed through.] I read the new AWU arrived with interest. I &amp; the wife can get a job as married Boundary Riders somewhere I hope "Apres la guerre finis". Tonight BOXING. Brigadier General RYRIE acts as Referee &amp; gives the boys a fair go. 
 Feel a bit DOPEY from inoculation but a great deal fitter all round for my spell in Hospital.
