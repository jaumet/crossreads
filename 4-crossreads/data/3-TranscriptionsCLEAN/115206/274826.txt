
September 1918.Friday 13
In Reserve. HebecourtWeather showery, inoculated against typhoid fever. Worked during afternoon, Cox leave to Blighty. Paid some money  - no good - no canteens.Played cards and lost a little. Weather wet. My spectacular sight during evening about 9 pm. When a Fritz bombing plane was brought down in flames by one of our planes, near our camp. Five Fritzs in searchlights.
