<p>At 4 oclock we caught our car <strike>again</strike> &amp; soon we were home again. Saw 2 Australian Bands playing in Salisbury &amp; they were very good.</p>
<p>13th Got up late this morning for it was Sunday after breakfast Jock &amp; I went to our little den &amp; wrote the rest of the morning</p>
