<p>Saturday 24 February</p>
<p>Reveille at 3 a.m.  Straggled in a mob to the railway and scrambled on to the first train to be seen.  This was very amusing.  Luckily the train happened to be the right one.<br/>In the train all day making back for Albert.</p>
