 Sunday 6 August 1916 Played Piano for Church.  Played too loud, gave no breathing time &amp; no expression.  Malish. 
 Monday 7 August 1916 Sick Parade.  I get a cold from Playing Football. 
 Tuesday 8 August 1916 One letter from Bess.  Went next door &amp; watched arrival of 130 Turkish Prisoners. 
 Wednesday 9 August 1916 Leave all day.  Played Billiards, saw Australian wounded come in to Cairo.  Dinner Y.M.C.A., Tea at Groppi's. 
 Thursday 10 August 1916 I go on S.P. 2 days, no drill.  Cois Cateer.  Play Cards with neighbours W.D. &amp; Tommy W. 
 Friday 11 August 1916 Ward Master.  Finish top floor of Mel. Pav.  Work finishes. 
 Saturday 12 August 1916 Route March.  I am well away.  I am O. Corp. &amp; Postman.  I help sort letters.  I get 2 from Home, one Hilda &amp; one May Coppin.  Parade at 4 p.m. &amp; I don't hear the Bugle.  Shepherd's Band plays Y.M.C.A.  Dick Rowe comes to Tea.
