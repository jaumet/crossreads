<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 24</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Had tea and supper in billet.&nbsp; Turned in 10.30, tired.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 25</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 9.30.&nbsp; Church till 10.45.&nbsp; Had dinner with Lady Bateman.&nbsp; Jim Johnson came.&nbsp; Enjoyed it thoroughly.&nbsp; Struck us as being almost ethereal to sit down and eat our food - and such food - like civilised beings again.&nbsp; Returned town and had kit inspection at Rec. Grounds.&nbsp; Returned billet and went to Rec. Hall for tea.&nbsp; Returned billet and spent evening by fire reading.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 26</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 8.&nbsp; Minefields 9.30.&nbsp; All day on pick and shovel and breastworks.&nbsp; Hard time.&nbsp; Feet much better after treatment.&nbsp; Left 3.30.&nbsp; Town 4.15.&nbsp; No mail.&nbsp; Went to billet and got tidy.&nbsp; Tea.&nbsp; Saw Mr Dumas and handed him &pound;4/18/4 on account of souvenirs.&nbsp; Returned billet to soak feet.&nbsp; Spent all evening in billet.&nbsp; Had practice for to-morrow&#39;s concert at Methodist hall.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 27</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 8.&nbsp; Minefields all day on breastworks.&nbsp; Day extended till 4.30, and no more jerks, as time available for training has to be curtailed.&nbsp; Left for town 4.30.&nbsp; Town 5.30.&nbsp; Tea.&nbsp; Stayed at billet till 7.&nbsp; Went to Concert.&nbsp; Did two items.&nbsp; Encored each time.&nbsp; Crowded house.&nbsp; Lt. Gray in the chair.&nbsp; Concert a great success.&nbsp; Returned billet at 10.&nbsp; Had supper and went to bed.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 28</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 8.&nbsp; Minefields all day.&nbsp; Left 2.&nbsp; Town 3.25.&nbsp; Tea.&nbsp; Task work again on trenches at night.&nbsp; Good light.&nbsp; Did my task in allotted time and was among first to leave.&nbsp; Home 10.15.&nbsp; Supper.&nbsp; Bathed my poor feet and turned in.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">March 1</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 8.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Church till 11 revetting trenches.&nbsp; Took it easy.&nbsp; 11 am went to Minefields and saw 12 lbs of Gun-cotton exploded on Machine Gun emplacement with little or no effect.&nbsp; Dinner.&nbsp; Church grounds again and resumed this morning&#39;s job.&nbsp; Left 4.30 after an easy day&nbsp; Good mail.&nbsp; Tea.&nbsp; Spent evening reading.&nbsp; Turned in early.&nbsp; Feet very bad.&nbsp; First intimation of final leave to-day.&nbsp; Granted 5 days, from 13th to 18th instant.&nbsp; Great rejoic-ing.&nbsp; No extra money to be granted for leave.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">March 2</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 8.&nbsp; Church all day revetting.&nbsp; Easy day.&nbsp; Nice balmy warm weather.&nbsp; Left 4.30.&nbsp; Town 4.50.&nbsp; Got souvenirs and handed same out after tea.&nbsp; Spent evening in billet reading and writing.&nbsp; No mail.&nbsp; Sent concert program home.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">March 3</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 8.&nbsp; Minefields all day on demolitions of breast-works, etc.&nbsp; No mail.&nbsp; Dismissed 11.15.&nbsp; Bath.&nbsp; Dinner.&nbsp; Cleaned up and went for tea to Mrs Bateman.&nbsp; Had gorgeous time.&nbsp; Returned to billet 8.30.&nbsp; Supper.&nbsp; Bed.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">March 4</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 9.30.&nbsp; Church.&nbsp; No mail.&nbsp; Dinner.&nbsp; Stayed home all afternoon.&nbsp; Very cold and bitter day.&nbsp; Had tea in billet.&nbsp; Spent evening writing home mail and London letters advising of leave.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">March 5</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Parade 8.&nbsp; Snowing and bitingly cold.&nbsp; Church grounds</td>
