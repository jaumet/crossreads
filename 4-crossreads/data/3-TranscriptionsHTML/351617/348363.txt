<p>Some of our time was spent up around Delville Wood where one of the hottest fights on that sector took place, the wood being taken &amp; lost six times until finally the South Africans at great loss took it &amp; hung on. They say Delville Wood is one great grave-yard although they were all out of sight when we arrived there.</p>
