<p>1919<br/>May 28<br/>skedaddled while we were a long distance away from it.  We caught a train back part of the way &amp; then a "tube".  I alighted at South Kensington to see Miss Violette.  She was out, but the superior lady help gave me the papers &amp; diary that I had left with her.</p>
<p>May 29<br/>About 29 of us went out in a party from the War Chest Club by train to Windsor.  At Windsor station we were met by Miss Nicholson &amp; taken to the Castle.  We were shown over the state rooms by an old guide with a very sing song voice.  He was very bored with the whole thing.  We then saw St George's Hall.
