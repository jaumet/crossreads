<p>Saturday 9th March 1917 [1918]<br/>Saw Matron re photo of grave she asked for &amp; very delighted she is with it.  They recused late pass 2nd this week.  Went whist drive at Railway Institute &amp; very weird was the drive down only with Mr. Shaw I felt safe, played 20 games with "all sorts &amp; conditions of men" and found them well behaved &amp; quiet &ndash; do not understand me I think playing with them as one sister, Hughes, refused to play cards in the ward, infradig.<br/>Home through curious back lanes &amp; streets, places not to be through if alone but came with 2 nice people &ndash; 27th G.H. 11.45 p.m.</p>
