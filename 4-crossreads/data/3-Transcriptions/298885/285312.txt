<p>Mar 27th - 19<br/>Once again another step nearer Aussie. This morning we set out for Marceinnes a distance of 7 miles</p>
<p>Apr 1st &ndash; 19<br/>Two mornings ago we were packed 36 in a truck and arrived at Le Havre a tired and sorry lot</p>
<p>It was a 36 hour trip and we considered ourselves lucky as previous drafts have taken 53 to 60 hours. Arriving at Le Havre at 11PM we remained in train</p>
