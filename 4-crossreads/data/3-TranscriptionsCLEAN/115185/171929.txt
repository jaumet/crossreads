She looks a very sensible sort of person - and all the matrons of the various units are under her. She has had several interviews with our Matron - Miss Wilson - she has made all the timetables for our meals and on duty & off duty times - and rules about our dress etc. The whole way 3 hours from Alexandria to Cairo - I hung out of the window - gazing at a flat country covered with luxuriant emerald green crops - small fields with drains round them, I suppose irrigated from the Nile. Men, women & children were working, loading donkeys with green lucerne looking hay - every now & then little mud flat roofed houses were grouped together in villages - the hay drying on the roofs - looked like dung - reminded me of some scripture which I couldn't remember - that sounds absurd doesn't it - I think it goes dunghills & cesspots of Egypt?!!
