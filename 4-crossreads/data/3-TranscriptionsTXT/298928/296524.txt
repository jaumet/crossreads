 31st Jan.  Left Meteren for canteen Corner, a muddy camp about  2 kilos from Steenwerck. 
 3 rd Feb.  Played at church parade and presentation of prizes by  General Birdwood, over 20 men were decorated with D.C.M's  &amp; M M's  one with both. 
 5th  Left Canteen Corner for Blighty Leave.
