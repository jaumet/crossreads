
F & w 21 SunTommy Sports. T & I go look at 'em at 6 pm. Team up [indecipherable] all night straighten out things a bit.Go to bed early
W 22 MonHave a long day & say Au Revoir to Mark Marsh. Get a horrible Electric shock & wet everything in the jointE. reads a book.Wrote Mum (Photos) Dad (Photos) M.RL.
W & F 23 Tues.Wet all morn. I go to. Warminster. Get sopping wet. Meet Sten. Ronie & Jane from Collingwood. St. Chapman bolts & I have to come back [Tranch?] with Amesbury girl to Sarum & Bristol Girl to W'minster. Salis. [indecipherable] back to Salisbury & then Amesbury Girl.
F 24 WedLovely morning & rain. 3 pm. Arr in bad [skin?] with everyone. Bath. Supper. & write. Recd. Letter from M.R.L. Feeling pretty well again Wrote MRL
W 25 ThRecd letter from Jinnie. Rotten Day. Go up to mess & place Billiards all night
W & F 26 FriTom comes down & we play cards all night in our Room
F 27 SatWith one huge storm. T & I go to Rotten W.C. Sports. Go up again after Tea & just get wet a little. Play cards & go to Dunnington Cross Stones afterwards. I come Home Knocked out & T & D come down afterwards & we talk about various subjects for an Hour or more.
