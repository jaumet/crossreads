<p>last dated 21st Dec. Con had just left for Tasmania. It was splendid hearing all your news darling mum. I drank in every work. You cant realise how much it means to me getting your letters &ndash; you are the only one who writes to me from Australia. Precious Father has not written lately &ndash; but I know he is very busy. I cant bear to think of the work you are doing in the house and hope you let Jocelyn go home to you as she suggests. She can</p>
