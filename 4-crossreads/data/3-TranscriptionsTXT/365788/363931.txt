 North of France 3 May 16 My dearest Irene, I was very pleased to get another nice little letter from Margaret the other day. &ndash;How well she is getting on old Girl. We are at present living in a village about 4 miles from the trenches &amp; our other section are acting as stretcher bearers out at the trenches &amp; also runs a huge hot bath &amp; clean clothes concern in a village near us.
