<p>weather of late, its raining again today, hanged if I know how the farmers get their crops cut &amp; in Johnnie Nicholson went to London on escort &amp; brought a slippery customer back Mayne's his name, used to be a Sergt in our Battalion, but got reduced in Egypt, since then he has been in every gaol in England I think &amp; has caused a lot of trouble Jock has gone on his 10 days leave, &amp; I am following him in about a fortnights time Judging by our last lot of reinforcements Australia must be full of weeds, our draft are a very poor lot, &amp; the majority are no ways keen on getting to France</p>
