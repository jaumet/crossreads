 Ballak 6 P.M. went for a swim in canal fed up had tea got two Beers and turned in.  the ground here is very damp. 
 Sunday July 29.7.17 turned out 6 A.M. fed up went to canal had a swim had Breakfast cleaned up rested till 12.30 had dinner.  rested till 5.30 fed up not feeling well could not eat my tea.  saddled up went up canal on outpost turned in 11 P.M. nothing doing. 
 Monday July 30.7.17 turned out 5.30 saddled up and returned to camp fed up had Breakfast cleaned up camp cleaned rifle had shave watered camels hot day rested till 5 P.M. fed up had tea turned in am not feeling well at all.
