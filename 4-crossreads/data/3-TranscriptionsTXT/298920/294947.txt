Saturday 11th Septr   Passed the F.H.S. liner "Durham" at 2.30 this morning, with lights blazing from all her ports she presented a grand sight as we passed her.  African coast in sight all day, in parts hills thickly covered with vegetation, in other parts sand hills, &amp; yet again low hills covered with green grass.  Very squally throughout the day, heavy showers of rain passing over every &#189; hour.
