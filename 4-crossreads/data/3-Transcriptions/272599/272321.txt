<p>1917 Wednesday 21 Feb.</p>
<p>Not much improvement last night &amp; today. Its a crook place to be crook in. This is when one misses home  The boys attention is rough but kindly. My bingy rejects all food. <br/>The Doc. has a look at me on the afternoon orders me back to Edward's Post. Ticketed Gastric Enteritis.<br/>Arrive back at Edward's Post about 4 p m found Ridgway &amp; Sergt Clifton had been sent down to Bazentin.  Hunt keeps me here &amp; doses me up. <br/>Turn in to bunk in dugout &amp; put in most joy.</p>
