
Thursday 16 September 1915 About Hospital all day 
Friday 17 September 1915 About hospital al day, Nothing particular doing 600 patients today 
Saturday 18 September 1915 Went with Father Vrigille to Heliopolis to see some of his friends. Had afternoon tea with them, then went for a motor drive. In Cairo during the evening 
Sunday 19 September 1915 At Basilica 9-30 am. Not out otherwise.
