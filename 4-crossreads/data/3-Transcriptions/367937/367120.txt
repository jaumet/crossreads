<p>No 2</p>
<p>My dear Mother and Father<br/>I wrote to Mother last night but could not get much in on that paper so will write to you both now.  I can only tell you all the minor details but suppose you will be interrested in them.<br/>Here is a list of some of the things we are not to put in letters.<br/>Date and place of dispatch<br/>Names and numbers of transports &amp; escorts<br/>Numbers &amp; descriptions of troops<br/>Disposition of force<br/>Route &amp; ports of call<br/>Criticism judged harmfull<br/>References to larger units &amp; superior commanders.<br/>My word I am thankful that I am such a good sailor</p>
