<p>4<br/>else would go near her save one Spanish Jewess who could scarcely keep herself let alone caring for others.</p>
<p>She was lying in a sort of stable place crawling with flies, with one 'boarding house blanket' to cover her &amp; nearly starving. The people owning the building, for whom she had worked, intended throwing her out &amp; as she is an  orphan it would have been onto a vacant piece of ground alongside.</p>
<p>All the concert money &ndash; taken from us of course &ndash; "was not for these cases" &amp; no one could help her I waxed eloquent &ndash; I was quite pleased with my effort and managed to scare them into getting a carriage &amp; moving her to Jaffa. But its disgusting to have to do with such people. I put them in the category Z&mdash;</p>
<p>Excuse my bad mood tonight &ndash; these people may be alright according to their lights but as David Harum says "the burn a mighty poor brand of oil"</p>
<p>Remember me kindly to Major &amp; Mrs Curtis</p>
<p>Kindest regards to yourself &amp; all at Wimbledon<br/>Yours very sincerely<br/>R. J. Hunter</p>
