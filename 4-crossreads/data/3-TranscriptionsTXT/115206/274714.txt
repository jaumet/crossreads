
May 1918Friday 24
Battery W.L.Weather rotten, rain and a very cold wind. Fritz bumped one or two around the district. Smithy and Adey visited the home.Purchased several items of groceries for guns and gave them to Smithy. Malcom to L'aison for three days, Knipe very ill? Lofty Hallwood received shell shock from a local shell. Had the 2 to 4 this morning.
