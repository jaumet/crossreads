<p>told us tales of their previous wars.<br/>I rolled into bed thinking of what I should be doing on the morrow. I woke up to the sound of bugles blowing all over the camp , "Get out of bed you sleeply heads"' so the next thing was to up &amp; dress &amp; have a cup of coffee with epsom salts added; The bugle sounded  "fall in" &amp; away we went for physical excersises till 8,am. which was breakfast time.<br/> I had a small breakfast as I did not understand the idea of getting in for your cut, We again fell in at 9 am &amp; were sworn in as soldiers of the King, blue suits &amp; white caps</p>
