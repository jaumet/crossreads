 30 October 1918 Wednesday Teeth- Major Douglas  Capt Donell Got parcel today from Hospital will keep till I go back again  if I do  feel sure of going home about Xmas Big mail from England Laura Joe, Day very glad Joe on way home he will meet Dolly perhaps. Took Dolly to dentist she has to go again next week- Had afternoon tea in Cairo- Wrote Nowell do not know where he is- Big mail from Australia.
