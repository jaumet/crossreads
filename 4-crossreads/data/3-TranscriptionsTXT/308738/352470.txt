 June 29 Tuesday 1915 Feeling little better. Met Young &amp; Walters of B Squadron both doing well Dr. gave me some medicine &amp; to drink plenty of water. 
 June 30 Wednesday 1915 Feeling worse again very little to see or hear. Men are continually coming in &amp; out. Lieu Drummond came in to see me gave me 20 piastres. 
 July 1 Thursday 1915 Feeling very little better &ndash; got up after tea &amp; got dressed cleared out of the Hospital. Took tram to Cairo bought one or two dainties to eat- returned 8. 30 PM 
 July 2 Friday 1915 Feeling bit better again V. Convas came in to see me this afternoon
