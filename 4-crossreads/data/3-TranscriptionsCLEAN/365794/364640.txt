  
 Monday 26th August 1917 [1918] Col. Onslow & Marge 1 Holland motor for me 4 p.m., lovely drive to Mena House, tea & home 7 p.m., fine people & very nice to hear all Aust. news again. Reported pts. food to Col. Onslow, watch result. Officers' ward - convoy 7 pts., mostly convalescent. 3 malaria, 2 dysentery, 1 diarr., 1 sand fly. Names - Lt. Kildea, Lt. Lane, Lt. Bancroft, Lt. Robinson, Capt. Cramb, Capt. Drury & Capt. Chitty.
