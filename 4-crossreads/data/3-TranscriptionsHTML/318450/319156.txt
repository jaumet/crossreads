<p>they though we would all be suffering from thick head's and a few other things, but we were all there when the wistle blew.  We moved off from the ground at about 5 am, and from the gate down to the wharfe there were friends waiting to see their beloved ones.  As I have said we left the show ground, and we were all in good spirits, and with our friends besides us,<strike> we</strike> you would never have thought we were going to war.  We marched down Flinders St. through Oxford Square and then down Burke St which led us down to the wharfe, I can tell you there was some good sights to be seen, if you had have had time, as it was</p>
