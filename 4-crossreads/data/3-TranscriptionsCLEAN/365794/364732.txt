  
 Saturday 24 November 1917 In Connaught & very busy day.  Off 2-5.  One pt. died after op. 24 hrs. previously, bullet through abd. & deep seated foul pus coming away before op., some haem. after op.  Saline P.P. given 6 a.m. not retained.  11.30 a.m. sub cut. saline attempted, H.I. strych. with digatilis [digitalis ?] gr. 1/100 given., oxygen, hot foments over cardiac region.  Many secondary haem. occur within last few days.
