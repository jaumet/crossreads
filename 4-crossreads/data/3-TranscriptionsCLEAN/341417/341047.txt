I met a great lot of men in hospital who was in the thick of the push & they say it was terrible the way Fritz was cut up and the dead were there in hundreds. Some of our divisions are cut up a good deal and they are breaking up some of the Battalions to make the others up to strength but I see by the papers that they are enlisting fairly well in Ausie but they will be a long time before they are in the line. There seem to be plenty of Yanks about London & I have me very few in France but they have come over a good bit of late.
