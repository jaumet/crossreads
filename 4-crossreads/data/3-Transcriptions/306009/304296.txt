<p>1915<br/>Apr. 20 Tue.<br/>Still plenty of work &ndash; The enquiry investigating charges against Quartermaster's Department under Capt. Gray &amp; Cooking Dept. under Sergt. Smith.  Am typing all the evidence which shows pretty serious irregularities.<br/>The Q.M. (Capt. Gray) has been relieved of his duties &amp; placed under open arrest while the Sergeant Cook (Sgt. Smith) has been transferred to general duties.</p>
