  
 5 - destroyers etc. Hospital ships come & go - store ships, sailing boat & motor launches day & night for the first 6 days we could hear our guns booming continuously at the Dardanelles! The sea is gloriously blue. The earth brown & dry - not a blade of green grass, all dry grass lying or blowing with the earth in the wind, on the hills one can see in spots cultivated patches - a few trees round the village I can see 5 villages from my tent, rows of round tower wind mills decorate the hills - I think are used for grinding. The sun rise & set are wonderful, also the hilly horizon. The time, I can always tell by the numerous ship's half hourly bells. I have two pieces of mattress now - nearly long enough for me. There should be a third bit - on the floor
