<p>put in charge of the baths where all patients are washed as part of their treatment for scabies. Had a very busy day as we were late in getting a start on but tomorrow will be better. Weather on the mend. Had a congratulatory message from The Army Commander read out to us for our divisions fine work in the advance. The divisions mentioned were the 7th &amp; the 4th &amp; 5th Australian divisions, certainly they did do good work &amp; at the end of a gruelling time in the line too.</p>
<p>Sunday 8th April Easter Sunday<br/>Had a very busy day &amp; could not get away to church altho' we had a service in the camp. Managed to get the day through with great success making my own system which will be further enlarged tomorrow. A beautiful day it being too warm to work in a tunic a vast change &amp; almost unbelieveable.</p>
