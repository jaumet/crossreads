<p>2.<br/>longer necessary to keep moving or shiver over a fire. Golf and Badminton &amp; Riding fill in the spare time very comfortably. I have even tried golf before breakfast but found it rather a failure.</p>
<p>The school moves from here in the first week of May. We should be in England by the 8th and should embark about the end of the first week in June.</p>
