<p>Short diary of the second spasm of the war in Galipoli</p>
<p>27/10/15 -  Got on board 'Oxuauich' for the front a practical illustration of packing sardines or boxing dates</p>
<p>28/10/15 - Still on board too rough to land betting 10 to 1 on Anzac</p>
<p>29/10/15 - Still on board betting 100 to 1 Anzac</p>
<p>30/10/15 - Landed at Anzac started hill climbing the Major leading with a walking stick in one hand and fist full of air in the other we had packs so lost the Major.</p>
<p>31/10/15  to  3/11/15 - Nothing doing, digging dug outs all day for some one else all spare time devoted to swearing and commenting on the wonderful organisation of the army especially the Aus Quota</p>
<p>4/11/15 - Sick as a dog went to Europe and done my ivories</p>
<p>5/11/15 - Installed in my new dug out 2nd  I wonder for how long</p>
<p>6/11/15  - Sitting on a phone in the trenches lisping messages and cursing everything &amp; everybody. Had three spoonfuls of cornflower three times a day, and Im hungry</p>
