 Dec.25th (Xmas Day 1918) (Noel) - Spend a few minutes at Notre-Dame (Xmas Mass) - A very pleasant promenade along the Champs Elysees, the entire length of which lies between the Arc de Triomphe & Place de la Concorde - Laid out in the 17th century - Resort of pleasure seek[er]s -Scenes of animation - Very fashionable quarter.
