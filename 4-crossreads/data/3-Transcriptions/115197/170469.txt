<p><u>Feb. 9th </u>(Sunday)</p>
<p>Letters Home.&nbsp; London in Winter.&nbsp; An afternoon of exercise and amusement.&nbsp; Walk to Parliament in the Fields, which lies adjacent to Hampstead Heath - and there I witness<s>ing</s> the delightful recreation of tobogganing for the first time.</p>
<p>Great sport.&nbsp; Many laughs!&nbsp; Return to Elm Bank and spend the evening reading aloud to Mr &amp; Mrs Scales.</p>
