<p>December 1916<br/>19th (Continued)  We heard to-day that the French a few days ago advanced two miles on a six mile front &amp; captured <strike>of</strike> over 8000 prisoners.<br/>To-day we had to made 50 "Hop over" ladders to be taken out to the trenches this afternoon for a raid which anticipated for to-night. We have been taking a few prisoners here lately, two &amp; three a day.  They have evidently given themselves up &amp; seem to be very pleased about it.<br/>20th   The weather has been extremely cold all day &amp; the ground has been covered with snow &amp; frozen hard.
