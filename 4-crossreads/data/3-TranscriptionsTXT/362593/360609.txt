 Thursday 3 October 1918 Having a great spell in our lovely new camp at Haifa by the sea. First shower of rain about 20 points. McGuinness &amp; Self have record hours flying for the stunt, 55 hours. 
 Friday 4 October 1918 Tent fixed up, all camp in order.  Maughan, Tonkin, Ross Smith, Mustard &amp; Ned Henry get D.F.C., very glad to hear it.  Smith &amp; Mustard got it very backsheesh.
