 Dysentry very bad in camp. Concert in our tent at night. 
 11th April (Tuesday) Warmer still. Carpentering most of the day and carting stones from Road Head North on Camels. This meant a few rides for us. We gave the "Gypo" &frac12; piastre to let us have a gallop, so, using the whip, we got such a rough trip that we don't look forward to another like it.
