<p>at all cost (Hill 9 of 1( and he also told us that the British were going to make another landing to the North of the peninsula this way Suvla Bay. The whole Brigade was on parade at about 9.pm. ready to march out to the asault. We left reserve [Gulley?] at 9-30 pm.. the 13<sup>th </sup>Battalion in the lead and then came the 14<sup>th</sup>. 15<sup>th.</sup> 16<sup>th</sup>. Behind us. We marched out along the Beach for 1&frac12;miles and then we came to our No 3 out post wich had just attacked the turkish outpost and captured their trench</p>
