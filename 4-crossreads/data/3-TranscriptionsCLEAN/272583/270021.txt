The fellows are continually going under the shower on account of the heat. 3 pm. Three deaths on the Ascanices. It is rumoured they died from entenic [typhoid] fever. There is now a nice breeze blowing which is quite a change after the heat of the day.    Write to indecipherable   12th Sea like a millpond  Expect to be on the equator Some time to-morrow  The men are now rigging up the bath. A Ship passed outward bound at about 1.45. She was painted grey but her red water mark was easily discerned. Later.  I have just found out that she is the Empress of Asia. taken over by the Naval Authorities and sent to fix cables at Cocos Island which were destroyed by Emden.
