<p>Wednesday 2nd May<br/>Had half holiday in return for working (?) last Wednesday.  Hired cycle for afternoon &amp; starting at 12.30 p.m. for Bournemouth, reached there, after a splendid ride, at 2 pm<br/>Found Aunt had moved from Lincoln Avenue to Orcheston Rd, near Em's.<br/>Aunt was astonished to see me, &amp; so was Bert, who by good luck, I found at home.<br/>Em amp; [indecipherable] her husband (who is the Sec of the Chamber of Trade) were both in &amp; so was Rachel.</p>
