Wednesday 1st August. 1917Still raining curse it nice holiday got no coat so get wet every day. & have bonzer cold.
Take good care of these Books for me & I hope they will give you some sort of entertainment. I generally put down in it what is happening & how it strikes me. BertLove to all.
