
26 March, Wednesday
F. Lovely Day
I referee Hockey Match. Ellis goes away by 2 pm Train. I go to English Class & then to office to write & study, get to bed at lights out.
Wrote Home to dad.
27 March, Thursday
F. but very windy. End of Period.
I go to B-Keeping Class & English Literature Class.
Wrote Ida Dean, Dulce, Bert, Esme, Lottie & Bert Cocks.
Wrote M.R.L. & sent Books.
