<p>January 1918<br/>Tuesday 22</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Grand parade and march past at Attin. Inspection by Generals, Brown and Hobbs.<br/>I was mounted today on a very famous charger, named by some "wag" "Semaphore".<br/>After the inspection Hobbs made a little speech. But we heard very little indeed. I believe we are moving shortly.</p>
