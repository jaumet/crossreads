<p>the Aden at the harbour. The road lies over high hills and very winding &amp; are quite good going.<br/>The natives here speak both Hindustani &amp; Arabic They are a distinct Indian type.<br/>- Later &ndash;<br/>A steamer going out from the Harbour this morning about 10 miles away ran into a mine and came back in a sinking condition and as near as I can ascertain she has been beached.</p>
