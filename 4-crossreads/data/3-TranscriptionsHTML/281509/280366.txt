<p>1916 May 23<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Another inspection parade at 9.30 a.m. address by camp commandant on "Playing the Game" Washing parade in <br/>fore noon. <br/>Route march to Etaples &amp; back 6 miles. Etaples a dirty hole, fishing village, inhabitants poor, common &amp; ugly. Squalid &amp; filthy streets stink. <br/>Stroll round after tea. meet &amp; yarn[indecipherable] to Gog Aldous Alexander, Piper &amp; Elliot. The latter to be boarded for medically unfit.</p>
