
Perpetual Trustee Coy Ltd of SydneyI stipulate the amount coming to each of my three sons viz 100 one hundred Pounds in all, with interest if any, shall be paid to each of them on his attaining his 21st twenty first year & in the event of the death of one or more of them before reaching this twenty first year the survivor or survivors shall share his or their share & in the event of all three dying before reaching their 21st year the whole to go to my Wife Ellen Harris & in the event of my wife also dying before my Sons reach their 21st year the 300 & interest to go to my two Brothers Sydney George & Vivian Glen Harris in equal shares.
Signed Herbert Henry Harrison active Service France25/4/17
