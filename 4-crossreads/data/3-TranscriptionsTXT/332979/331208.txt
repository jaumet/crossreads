
Gang Way we proceeded & into the on to the Deck & down a flight of Stairs into our Mess Room. In describing our Mess Room it is on the lower Deck & it has two Rooms of Tables & Forms, 10 tables to each row & Each Table was called a Mess & Our Mess numbered 13 & it accomadated 16 men 8 each Side. After all the Troops were on board we were allowed to come up & say Good bye to our friends from the Deck. I took up a position right at the Stern of the Boat right on top of a large Boat which gave me a Splendid View. The people ashore started throwing fruit up to us & it was great fun catching the Oranges Etc. I caught 2 Oranges & 1 Peach & 1plum.
