 I have no time now for the Egyptain &amp; they will always keep their distance from me. The Bedouin &amp; Senussi tribe numbers about 2,500 strong &amp; they are a cruel race of people; they kill all the wounded they come across. So God help a man if he fall into their hands. 
 We have our saddles packed with coat &amp; blanket ready to move out at a few minutes notice if wanted I was put on horse picquet .We had a little better tea than we have had on the track from the Railhead
