
<p>275</p>
<p>FEBRUARY 1917</p>
<p>10 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; Sunny, &amp; fairly warm, in</p>
<p>comparison to the weather we</p>
<p>have been having. We had</p>
<p>the usual Saturday full dress</p>
<p>inspection &amp; everything was A1.</p>
<p>Cleaning harness ect in morning</p>
<p>&amp; I drove the officers cart up to</p>
<p>Armenti&egrave;res in afternoon with</p>
<p>Cyril [Rohu?] for stores ect.</p>
<p>The battery went up with amunition</p>
<p>at night, but I did not go.</p>
<p>There was a fair bombardment</p>
<p>going on all day &amp; they livened</p>
<p>up at night.</p>
