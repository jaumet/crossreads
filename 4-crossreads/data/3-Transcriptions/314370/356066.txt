<p>August, 1916<br/>Monday 7<br/>Made good time all day<br/>Making in direction of Marseilles, so our destination is now certain</p>
<p>Tuesday 8<br/>In sight of the harbour about 2.30 pm.<br/>Pulled into wharf by tug at 5 pm.<br/>First to disembark when all were off we marched behind the band to rest camp about 2 miles from the town.<br/>got into tents about 10 pm damned tired</p>
