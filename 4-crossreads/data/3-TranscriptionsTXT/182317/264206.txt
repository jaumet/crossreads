 R.A.S. Grounds 26/1/16 Dear Capt Bilton, re Triangle of Error proposition this causes me much interest, &amp; I would be glad if you would explain to me how you would find your true position from the following data :- You have correctly plotted on your map Points A, B &amp; C. Scale 2" = 1 mile From A :- B is 45 &amp;deg &amp; 600 yds C is 69 &amp;deg &amp; 1150 yds You are somewhere to the South of these points &amp;  their magnetic bearings are A 340 &amp;deg B 10 &amp;deg C 40 &amp;deg  1. How would you find your correct position , &amp; 2. What is the variation of your compass? K. Ferguson. Lieut.
