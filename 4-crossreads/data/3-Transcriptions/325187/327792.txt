<p>[Wednesday 16 February 1916]</p>
<p>Had a swim in Canals   I am getting a bushel of Visiting Cards from the Sheiks &amp; Pashas &amp; enclose some samples-<br/>Just a small pkt of Chewing gum would be acceptable in the heat if we have long days in the Desert in April-  On my road today I saw a Traction Engine &amp; 11 furrows Plow on one side &amp; on the other side wooden plow &amp; 2 oxen, the old &amp; the new.  I have had the Marmoor or Superintendent &amp; Secretary with me for 2 days, they say they are both sore from riding as our horses are fresh &amp; they are unaccustomed to ride much &amp; generally on Donkeys &ndash; [&amp; we ate it [indecipherable] Bedouin killed a Cock for [indecipherable]</p>
