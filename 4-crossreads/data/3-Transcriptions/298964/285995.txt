<p>18 Sun March<br/>Baldy out  hasy day.  Very quiet &amp; hot  Taubes giving us a spell</p>
<p>19 Mon March<br/>Taubes over but dropping no bombs  A big move expected</p>
<p>20 Tues March<br/>Still working Baldie  gave him a good gallop this morning at 4 a.m  expect him to win Race Thursday</p>
<p>21 Wed March<br/>Race coming off to day.  Fine day and big crowd present  Baldie ran third in Anzac Steeple.  Taubes over not dropping bombs<br/>[See also p191]</p>
