<p>13.3.15</p>
<p>Terriers at all, as the infantry fixed them up on their own &amp; the Light Horse was not called on.  went nowhere after tea &amp; retired to bed early</p>
<p>Sat 13th<br/>Had to parade with our blankets this morning &amp; had to hand over any blankets we had over two each, every one is confident that we shall be leaving here in a few days.  The only parade we had to-day was  to clean our equipment;  Stan went to Heliopolis tonight &amp; I had a game of billiards.  am on the feeding party at 9 oclock;  Yule has caught a sore throat</p>
