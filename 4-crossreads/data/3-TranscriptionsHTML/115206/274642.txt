<p>March 1918<br/>Wednesday 13</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Another glorious day, beautiful sunny and warm day.<br/>After hurrying home last night we did not have to go forward. A 15 pounder was taken up as advanced A.T. gun.<br/>Procceded to L'aison with Mr Kerr at 6.30 pm.<br/>Raid timed to come off at 3.20.am tomorrow morning.<br/>Weather at 6.30 pm, considerably colder and Meteor figures show rain is about.<br/>No.6 came over to Bty. Positon at 6 am. also the spooks with exception of Ken Gunn who has gone forward with A.T. gun</p>
