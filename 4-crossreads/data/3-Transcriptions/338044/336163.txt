<p>30th March to 9th May<br/>There is so little to write of in this miserable, monotonous never-to-be-forgotten place, Cairo, that I have entirely neglected writing my diary during this time. Much as one can do to find time for writing letters.</p>
<p>During my time off duty (which is very little) I have spent learning Arabic    &amp;  French, which I now speak well. (French more so) Prying into the customs &amp; habits of the dirty Arabic classes, who, although a contemptible lot are rather interesting &amp; going to different places sightseeing.</p>
