  
 Wednesday July 4th Went to Abbeville for a day had a good time. Thursday 5 July Fell in warned for guard following day.  On guard all day Saturday 7th Came off guard went to Abbeville had a good day Sunday 8th Moved out of Chest Camp morning left Faint Valery station half past two arrived Albert six o'clock marched out and joined Battalion again & Lenlist.
