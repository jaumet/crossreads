 Sunday 4 August 1918 4th Anniversary of the war.  Played billiards with Williams in the morn. 
 Monday 5 August 1918 
 Tuesday 6 August 1918 Warned for draft conductg. &amp; could not get out of it tho' I did my best!! 
 Wednesday 7 August 1918 Had an easy day, spent most of time at home.  Off to France with the draft at 10.30 p.m.  Most of the Draft were well oiled!! 
 Thursday 8 August 1918 Arrived Folkestone early &ndash; brekker at rest camp &ndash; then to boat and good crossing.  Lunch at "Ostrohove" Camp.  The diggers took French leave &amp; toddled into Boulogne!! 
 Friday 9 August 1918 Entrained at 12 HO &amp; made a good trip to Rouen.  Kept thr. 8 hrs. during air raid.  Played bridge &amp; read a good deal during the trip.  5 officers left behind at B. 
 Saturday 10 August 1918 Arrived Havre at 12 noon &amp; handed over all without a man missing.  Saw Mrs. Hendry at 40th Stat.  Into Havre later &amp; bought cigarettes. "Love is not to be reasoned down or lost in high ambition &amp; a thirst of greatness. "Tis second Life, it grows into the soul, warms every vein &amp; beats in every pulse."  Addison.
