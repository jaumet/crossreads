  
 49 16/9/16. The weather has been very cold lately, have all available clothes on every night. By jove it hurts getting out of a morning. More letters to hand to-day my mail is coming along splendidly now. 
 18/9/16. Last night Hack called & we went for a ride into Poperinge about 3 miles away. It is a fine large place & shows  plenty  signs of street fighting & bombardment. It was alive with soldiers but very few civilians. We were on bikes so had to watch them very carefully this is a great place for bike thieves. Bought a Belgian Hand made lace H'chief & had a long yarn to the shop keepers, two charming Belgian lassies.
