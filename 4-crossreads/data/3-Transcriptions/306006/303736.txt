<p>14-2-17<br/>Wed 14th<br/>Harris is OK again &amp; the Dr. says both he &amp; I can go on the 9 oclock train tomorrow morning owing to a breakdown on the line no trains are running today. A lad named Martin (2nd Regt.) is also going back with us Our planes went out again this afternoon loaded with bombs</p>
<p>15-2-17<br/>Thurs 15th<br/>Left the Field Ambulance at 9 oclock this morning &amp; at 10 was on our way to Bir-El-Abd where we left the train at 1 oclock (Martin (2nd Regt) left us at Nazar) Olson met me at Abd with my horse, collected some letters from Rich (the Postman) who</p>
