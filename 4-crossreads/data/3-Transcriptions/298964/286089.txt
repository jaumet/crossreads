<p>Thursday 22nd February 17.<br/>Was a great day for all  we moved out of the heavy sand into the good country where there are green fields galore and we are as happy as sand boys.  Looking forward to the time when we will be in the Promised Land  We have had a glimpse of it and are longing for more</p>
