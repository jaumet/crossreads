
Tuesday 20 November 1917.
"Stand to" ar 4 am (a kind of punishment for not turning out yesterday morning.)
Picquet 6 to 8 am.
Breakfast and started to write letters.
French Jews bring bread, eggs, cake & wine & brandy round on sale to the troops.
Mail to hand. Letters as late as 23rd Sept dated in Australia.
Cold & wet day. Very bleak.
Wrote a letter tonight.
