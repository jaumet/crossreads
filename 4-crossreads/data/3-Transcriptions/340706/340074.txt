<p>120<br/>Still on the stunt of billeting &amp; this morning have to revisit a Dr Courmontagne re soldiers billets Madame implores me to leave her home alone if I can &amp; she is ably supported by her father a Maire of a neighbouring municipality I promise to do my best &amp; on learning that I am an avoca] invites me to her home any time I am free to make the acquaintance of her husband.</p>
<p>This afternoon I am deputed to take a statement from a French girl Germaine Bandelin who witnessed the death of one of our men who fell from a horse &amp; a pleasant interview it was &amp; laughable when a baby on the floor gave a representation of the Mannicken Pis.</p>
<p>This afternoon I am on a Court Martial a pleasant way of spending Saturday afternoon so we sentenced him to 12 mos hard labour which is subsequently commuted to 90 day F.P. Then I review the French girl &amp; obtain her signature to the typewritten copy.</p>
<p>20-4-19 (Sun)<br/>This being Easter Sunday we decide to dine in style &amp; really do ourselves well. Dinner consists of Soup Devil kidney &amp; Macaroon, Roast Duck, Roast Pork Roast Beef Potatoes Green Peas</p>
