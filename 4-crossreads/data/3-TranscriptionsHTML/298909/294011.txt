<p>Wednesday</p>
<p>Got paid 86 Francs &pound;3-0-11 &amp; the boys are spending money freely in the Village &ndash; I think the business people never experienced such good business &ndash; the children nearly drive us mad for pennys &ndash; we have taught them to say Saida &ndash; &amp; gib it Backsheesh &ndash;  B-Section changed to C. Section on account of our new Major being a Junior.</p>
