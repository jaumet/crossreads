<p>26/1/17<br/>Went up to line today. I was in the advance party. We are releiving the Fifth Yorks tomorrow night. Bn Hd. Qrs. is in [Heyam?] Road.</p>
<p>28/1/17<br/>Went into line today. The enemy are only about forty yards away and everyone seems neutral. This sector lies between Flers and Warlincourt Butt.</p>
