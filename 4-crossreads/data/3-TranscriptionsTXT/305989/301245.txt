 Sunday 25 February 1917 Left Kantarah by the 5.45 train &amp; came back to Bir-el-Abd &amp; then into Bayud. 
 Monday 26 February 1917 On day stableman. 
 Tuesday 27 February 1917 Played N.Z. football &amp;  won  played a draw, hard match. 
 Wednesday 28 February 1917 Nothing doing in particular, had to get my flipper dressed. 
 Thursday 1 March 1917 Quiet day:  Billy Ahearn got his com. 
 Friday 2 March 1917 Billy A'hearn left for Bir-el-Abd.  Very windy day.  M.G. &amp; H.Q. v B. Sq. &ndash;  won 9-3. 
 Saturday 3 March 1917 Got my hand bandaged in morning.  A Sq. v M.G. &amp; Q.V.
