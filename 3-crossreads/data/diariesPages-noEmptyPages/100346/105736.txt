Friday June 15th
Bapaume : hot : reveille 6 am : early parade 6.30 : got wood for cooks during the day : pass to town after tea : been knocked about some : cemetery very nice plenty of soldiers buried there of both sides also Huns from 1870 war : got some flowers to send home :
Saturday June 16th
Bapaume : hot : reveille 6 am : early parade 6.30 : "wood" racket again : walked over seen Tim's grave it is to the Hun's credit that he has had a decent burial : the country here looks splendid just an occasional shell hole having
