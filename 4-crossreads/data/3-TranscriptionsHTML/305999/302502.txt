<p>3/1/16<br/>We embarked on the SS Empress of Brittan  she was a verry big ship  we here carring 800 troops  we where not propelly full up  then we did not lose any time in gitting away</p>
<p>4/1/16<br/>We left lemnos for Egypt leaving at 8 AM  we where glad to git to old Egypt again</p>
<p>5/1/16<br/>We disembarked at Alexanderia and entrained for Tel-El-Kebir the</p>
