<p>Pry, Belgium<br/>2/2/1919<br/>Dear Aunt Evie<br/>Today is the 2nd Feb 1919 &amp; still I am here in Belgium kicking my heels &amp; waiting for the day when I get my sailing orders for home.  If things go on as they are I ought to be going within six weeks or two months.
