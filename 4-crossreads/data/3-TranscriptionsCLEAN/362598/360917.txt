
Sunday 19 March 1916Bill, Ellis & I went to Mary's well & Virgin's Tree & to Kursaal after tea.
Monday 20 March 1916Dave Niness took Photo E. R. I.  Roy & I went to Kursaal together.  Roy came out to dinner but got very little.  Came down to my ward to talk to Corbett.
Tuesday 21 March 1916Ward-master.Home, Nettie, Bess, Laura, Nell & Pa, Ma, Cock & Ida.Went to Pyramids & round the Houses of Cairo.  Bill, Dave, Charlie & E. & I.  Rode from Mena in motor car.
Wednesday 22 March 1916Met Johhno & went to Pictures.  Special leave from 5 to 10.
Thursday 23 March 1916Wrote to Mem & Cohens.
Friday 24 March 1916Went to Blue Mosque.  Met Roy in Gardens, Tea at Cafe de la Paix.  Tea Misrquash.  Roy, Dave Niness & I went to Pictures.  Pictures Misrquash.  Pictures Colosse.
Saturday 25 March 1916Our Theatre Experts gave concert in Esbekiah Gardens.
