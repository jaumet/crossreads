<p>February 1915<br/>Saturday 27<br/>Taken over to isolation Tent with measles. Feeling pretty <u>crook</u></p>
<p>Sunday 28<br/>Measles out pretty thick. First time I have ever had them. Feeling pretty crook. Lots of chaps have been sent to other hospitals as they are clearing out this one.</p>
