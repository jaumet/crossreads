30-4-19 (Wed)This morning is cold & wind cuts through through while rain clouds burst about every half hour.
This morning receive an additional 50 men (22.35 [indecipherable]) & in evening [indecipherable] - skipper delivers an address on future routine in Eng & repatriation schemes
Dr Courmontagne is away all this afternoon & has not returned by 8 pm so after 2 calls I retire to my [domicilum] & drink coffee with the ladies of the house.
1-5-19 (Thur)Usual routine in morning in rain mud & loafing around awaiting dinner time, so I leave camp about 10 am & come to my billet. Gamaches is out of bounds to our men as it is supposed that there is measles in town & 2 cases have been found in camp one of them of my lot. L'Cpl Cowley.
