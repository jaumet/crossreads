  
 Thursday 6 September 1917. 
 Reveille 5 a.m. 
 At tent inspection at 9 a.m. in the "dugout" bivouac competition my "Terra Domus" got honorable mention. 
 Handed over patients from Sandcart to Hospital Train at Shiekh Nuran (dump) station. On the camels for watering parade  to the "Waddy" at 10 a.m. Back at 4.30 pm. Fooled about by a lecture & tea at 5 pm. Next to no dinner & no wash all day, no time since morning when breakfast & wash had to be had in 5 minutes.
