<p>17th August Monday<br/>3.30 Troops addressed &amp; reviewed by Sir Gerald Strickland. Terribly exciting like Sams lectures</p>
<p>5.5  Monotony relieved somewhat by the first pay 35/- Kicked up a row because I got 5/- too much. Scaled out by the transport gate &amp; duly delivered up my spare cash by sundry means</p>
