<p>Wednesday 24/11/15<br/>Very warm.  Large sick parade, very late breakfast.  Chloroform administered to lad who fell down hatchway to ascertain nature of injuries, fortunately no bones broken, although apparent internal injuries.<br/>Innoculation during afternoon for our own boys, some felt effects badly.
