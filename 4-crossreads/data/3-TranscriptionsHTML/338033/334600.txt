<p>Colleseum and booked seats for the night, afterwards going for tea.<br/>Going out we passed through Holiven and saw the few very old buildings left in London. They were leaning very much, and rather dangerous looking I thought. Bow church was pointed out to me and I was informed that the origin of the name cockney, was here. Anyone being born within sound of the chimes of this church, being called a cockney. I enjoyed the theatre very well, but didn't think it any superior to Sydney theatres. I lodged at the same place again, and next morning</p>
