<p>As we went out on the case last night there were flares &amp; gun-flashes everywhere, &amp; all noise, so that it was one continuous noisy firework display.  Many flares &amp; gun reports over the direction of Kemmel, told of heavy fighting there.  It is impossible to say who holds Kemmel.  The latest paper reports say Fritz does, but countless rumours &amp; definite statements by French &amp; English soldiers say we have counter attacked &amp; driven him out.  I hope so</p>
