<p>25 August 1915<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>After breakfast this morning I went for a bit of a stroll   &amp;  heard from one of the medical officers that they were going to clear all the patients out to day.</p>
<p>About 4 pm we were all marched down to the Peir   &amp;  put onto a trawler   &amp;  she took us to the Hosp ship<br/>About 8 pm we had some beautiful Bread, Butter, jam   &amp;  tea which was most welcome because we had nothing since 12 o'clock</p>
<p>1915 August 26<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>The ship moved out at daybreak this morning and at 8 am we had breakfast of Porridge Bread   &amp;  Butter   &amp;  Tea<br/>We passed through a heavy downpour of rain.<br/>Arrived at Mudros about 11 am.<br/>Move in   &amp;  tie up alongside of Hospital Ship "Assaye"<br/>During the night we are told that some of us are to go to Alexandria   &amp;  the rest to Gibraltar<br/>  <strike>This</strike><br/>Rains most of the night</p>
