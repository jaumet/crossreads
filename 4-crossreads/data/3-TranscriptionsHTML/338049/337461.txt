<p>Sunday 8 Sept.<br/>Went out to the scene of yesterdays operations &amp; found Milligans tunic. Picked up Hendersons plyers &amp; knife. Henderson not too good according to reports. <strike>Returned</strike> Took private gear to Major.</p>
<p>Ordered back to Clery scrounging cable. Returned via Peronne. Made camp at old lines. Pleasant supper &amp; to peace peacefully.</p>
