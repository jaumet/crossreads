<p>Tuesday 27th<br/>Hospital</p>
<p>Wednesday 28th<br/>Hospital</p>
<p>Thursday 29th Decr<br/>Hospital in Etaples, still going strong here, slept fairly well, Doctor round.</p>
<p>Friday 30th Decr<br/>Hospital</p>
<p>Still in Etaples, up all day feeling none too good, old American Doctor, ordering fellow with influenza glass of water every half hour.</p>
