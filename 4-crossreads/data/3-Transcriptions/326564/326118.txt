<p>7th My birthday once again &amp; the 4th in the Army 2 in Egypt &amp; 2 in France, surely this will be my last in the Military, at any rate I am hoping so.<br/>Went down to the A.S.C. last night to spend the evening, played "crib" till about 11 with varying success we then had some  supper, &amp; finished up yarning at 1.30.A.M. its wonderful how the time flies when you start talking about old</p>
