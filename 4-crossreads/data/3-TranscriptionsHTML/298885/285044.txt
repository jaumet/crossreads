<p>Wonderful buildings and beautiful gardens.  Train and tram service very good.</p>
<p>Thur. Jan. 27th -16<br/>Very heavy showers have fallen since 24th Inst the only rain for 3 years and heaviest since 1895.  Nights exceptionally cold.</p>
<p>Fri. Jan. 28th -16</p>
<p>Inspection by General Birdwood who was more than satisfied with everything.</p>
<p>Sat. Jan. 29th -16<br/>Visited Pyramids and Sphinx and to see them one wonders how they were ever built.</p>
