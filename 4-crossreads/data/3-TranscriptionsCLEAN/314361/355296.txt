
This Diary begins in Aug 1915SundayAug. 8th   15th Liverpool CampHave volunteered, joined camp last Thursday week. Just now am in Q 3 Coy, but expect to get transfer to Signallers tomorrow. Camp life not too pleasant, food bad & far too roughAug 25th Contact CampAm now in Contact Camp. There was a case of measles in my tent, so we had to come to this place.Will be here for ten days. Then we have to have four days leave.There are only two in my tent in this camp. We each drew 24 hours rations.Sept 3rd 15Managed to get out of contact Camp on the 9th day & am
