
1-6-15We arrived at Peninsular of Gillopi - save and sound at 4 A.M this morning & we were not fired upon. Where we are at present is in a valley, with the sun at our rear, with a big wall of Cliff, the boys are like Angora Goats in their dugout or possys, the Shrapnel is flying in all directions which make the boys duck we rook a risk and went in for a swim this afternoon we have to cook our own food and when the fire go out the language Jus rather rive ripe How on the il earth the boys landed & took the hills around about is marvellous. things are very tame today
