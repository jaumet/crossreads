<p>October 1915</p>
<p>Monday 25</p>
<p>Feeling better on rising but during the day got worse.</p>
<p>Scrambled through fatigue somehow, &amp; on someones advice took aspirin which fixed me up helped by a nip of whiskey from Roberts who with Kause ( 7th Reinforcements) .</p>
<p>Another narrow escape.  A shell landed a foot from head while I was in Robert's dugout</p>
