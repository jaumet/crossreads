<p>Dec.25th (Xmas Day 1918)<br/>(Noel) &ndash; Spend a few minutes at Notre-Dame (Xmas Mass) &ndash;<br/>A very pleasant promenade along the Champs Elysees, the entire length of which lies between the Arc de Triomphe &amp; Place de la Concorde &ndash; Laid out in the 17th century &ndash; Resort of pleasure seek[er]s &ndash;Scenes of animation &ndash; Very fashionable quarter.</p>
