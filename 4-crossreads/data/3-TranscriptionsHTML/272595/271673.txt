<p>Got a few snaps today from Miss Jervis which she took while I was in Birmingham.<br/>Passed the Dr. again this afternoon   Lindsay came a gutser, got marked A.3 which means Active Service, but he has got his teeth to be finished yet.  Great rumour flying round that the 1914 men are all going home shortly.<br/>6th. Rained like blazes out on parade this morning, consequently no roll was called till we marched our various parties into the Assembly sheds, I had to go through the torture of sitting in the dentists chair &amp; let him bore 4 of them out, no one knows how I dread such a thing as this the dentists say that I will have to have a back tooth pulled &amp; a small plate fitted before I am fixed up something to look for-</p>
