 April, 1915 Sunday 4 Went to the oldest obelisk in the world at old Heliopolis created by rameses II &amp; brought from Luxor, one solid piece of Granite 3000 B.C. Near here is the Virgins Tree where she rested with Joseph &amp; also the old Church with Dado showing Josephs flight x Egypt- Terrific Dust storm.
