 Monday 15 February 1915 Two appendix operations &amp; one hernia this morning.&nbsp; About hospital all day. Posted letters to the Girls, Matron Sherwood, and Doffie Williams. Walk about 6 pm. 
 Tuesday 16&nbsp;February&nbsp;1915 One appendix operation Busy all day.&nbsp; Atmospheric conditions ideal. Walk around Native village during afternoon. Dirt in excelsis. 
 Wednesday 17&nbsp;February&nbsp;1915 3 operations - 2 Haemorrhoids &amp; 1 fistula in&nbsp;ano.&nbsp; In Cairo and at Heliopolis with Colonel Martin. At a conference re Neisserian affection at The Palace, Heliopolis;&nbsp;General Williams presided.&nbsp; Col. Braund, Major Scobie, Father McAuliffe, dined with us at Mena House.&nbsp; Ordinary hospital work.
