  
 29/3/16 Young Gow lays bet of 10 disasters with Eric, Fred & I that the Australians will not be in Egypt in 4 days. Take him on. Freddy's transfer to Postal Corps turned down & he ramps & raves like a lion over it. 
 30/3/16 Hot as hades all day. 
 31/3/16 Close of another month. Put in private letter to Naval Commander of Transports Alexandria for transfer.
