  
 Sat. 8-7-'16 Slept till 9 a.m. Bath afternoon. Full Mching Order 9.30 P.M. Marched to Aldershot Camp Neuve Eglise Slept between Bivouacs from 2 a.m. Sunday 9-7-'16 Sports during Afternoon. Packed up & marched to Bailleul 8.30 pm. - 11.30 P.M. Billetted in large School 12 Midnight Sunday 9-7-'16 Above Mon. 10-7-'16 Awake 7-30 am. Bk'fast. Wained for Guard 9-35 a.m. Mounted same 10 a.m. Little girls of School Sang danced & played for us in exchange for pennies. Nearly all Orphans here. Y.M.C.A tent here very good & cheap.
