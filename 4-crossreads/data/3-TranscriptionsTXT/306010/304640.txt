Wed. 26" July 1916 (4.30 pm) :- Am whacking into the French as hard as I can go   - there always seems to be something fresh to learn. At Mussot we had an Interpreter attached from the French Army but here there is no one so I am filling the position of "Acting Interpreter &ndash; in Chief" to the Unit as well as translating correspondence for chaps individually &ndash; mostly from amorous young ladies in the south of France who profess undying, unquenchable, etc. etc. love for the gallant of their choice.
