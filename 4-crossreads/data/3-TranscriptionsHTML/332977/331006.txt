<p>Gaiting  gun on Boulder dumps</p>
<p>Oct 27<br/>Our fleet bombard Dedargatch, knock the Pier to pieces, blew up Torpedo Base, knocked railway to pieces, cuts off communication with Turks etc, French link up with Serbians, everything is lovely</p>
<p>Oct 28<br/>German Aeroplane brought down at Suvla Bay, another Fritz comes over but keeps up very high, 6 Beaship open out on our stores we have half rations for a week, &frac12;  rations water, our names come out in orders for doing 6 months continuous service,<br/>Our troops are giving Bulgaria a hard time</p>
<p>Nov 1st<br/>Our lads blow up Johnsons Jolly they rush the trench to soon two officers and 5 Pte die from fumes a mug pulls the tags on a bomb<br/>bomb goes off finish men, 17" gun supposed to be on Imbros, spies expited in they will have a job on 200 men left in 3rd Battalion</p>
<p>Nov 5 Guy Fawkes<br/>Turks try to charge light horses lines, they sneak up with sand bags for protection<br/>our lads bomb them off, we are preparing for another fresh attack tonight machine guns and Mountain Battery in trenches</p>
