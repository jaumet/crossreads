1917 MAY
14 MONDAY
Raining lightly most of the day.
took horses to the dip between
Steenwerck & Bailleul in
morning. I saw young [Seray?]
Kinross there & he is in good
form. Got word we were leaving
the waggon lines for open
lines in the morning & got
everything ready to get away
early in morning.
