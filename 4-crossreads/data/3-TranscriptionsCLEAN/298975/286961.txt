Sunday 30thCaught 9:15 to Harwich & met Mab on the Station Cycled out a few miles & had tea in evening.
Monday 1st OctWent shopping in Harwich in morning Cycled out for tea again to a very pretty wood Have learnt quite a lot about Submarines Etc Air raid in evening.
Tuesday 2ndCaught 10 AM train to Victoria Liverpool St Messed around a bit & caught 6 PM for Dinton in camp by 9-30 after a very pleasant 6 days. Fine wether every day we were on leave
