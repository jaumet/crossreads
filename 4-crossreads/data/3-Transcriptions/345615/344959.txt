<p>it seems we have again been true to our British Tradition &amp; underestimated the enemy Whether they will have the numbers &amp; confidence to attack <strike>up</strike> us remains to be seen, but I think we would easily beat them off &ndash; But it looks as if it will be some time before we can take the offensive against them</p>
