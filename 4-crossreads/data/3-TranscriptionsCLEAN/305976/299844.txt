  
 Wednesday March 21 Usual routine. Plum Jam still hot favourite for tea. Thursday March 22 Riding exercise, Squad drill & Lecture in evening Friday March 23 Route march to Reservoir & surrounding hills, about 16 miles Saturday March 24 On Duty S. Majors mess            the Sergeant Major's Sunday March 25th Same as yesterday Monday March 26th N.S.W boys very discontented with Seymour Camp & no wonder. It is the worst camp I was ever in. I predict trouble if system not changed.
