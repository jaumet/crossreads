<p>Sunday 21 July 1918<br/>Went to Salisbury &amp; saw Major &amp; Mrs Simpson.  The old C.O. is not looking too good.</p>
<p>Monday 22 July 1918<br/>Plenty of rain these days.  Doing most of parades under cover.</p>
<p>Tuesday 23 July 1918<br/>Expect I shall get my marchg. orders soon.</p>
<p>Wednesday 24 July 1918<br/>Usual parades.</p>
<p>Thursday 25 July 1918<br/>Revolver shootg. in afternoon.</p>
<p>Friday 26 July 1918<br/>Saw the Eye Specialist at Sutton Veny Hosp.  Raining all day.  Did no work.</p>
<p>Saturday 27 July 1918<br/>Major Lucas left the OTB for overseas.</p>
