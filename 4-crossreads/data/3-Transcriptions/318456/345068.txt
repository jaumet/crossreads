<p>Mena, Anzac, Mex, Matruh;<br/>Also France &ndash; and Me and You<br/>Horrible thirsts &ndash; Mafeish faloosh<br/>Pay-day again and on the loose.<br/>Sometimes hot, sometimes cold<br/>Sometimes regretting the clobber we've sold<br/>Up the Somme and down again<br/>On to Belgium &ndash; Oh! curse the rain!<br/>Cursing reveilles and cursing the horses<br/>Oh! for a dinner of nineteen courses.<br/>Lousy barns and ploughed up fields<br/>Damn long treks with Faith for meals<br/>Talking of girls we used to know<br/>Dressing our nose-bags in a neat long row<br/>Wishing the Kaiser was far from well<br/>Wishing the war was raging in Hell<br/>Wanting a "Blighty" small or large<br/>And praying to God for the treasured<br/><u>Discharge</u></p>
<p>Robin Duncan<br/>The Somme<br/>27-10-16</p>
