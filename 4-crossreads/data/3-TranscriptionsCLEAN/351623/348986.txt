
11 June 16At sea
12 June 16Arrived at Grand Cayman & anchored for a few hours & left again for Jamaica.
13 June 16Arrived at Kingston Jamaica & tied up alongside pier in morning watch. Received a mail from Australia. HMAS Sydney is here.
14 June 16At Kingston. Coaling ship all day.
15 June 16 )16 June 16 )17 June 16 )  Alongside pier at Kingston18 June 16 )19 June 16 )
20 June 16Went down & anchored at Port Royal. Sydney also anchored at Port Royal.
21 June 16Sydney done firing, while we towed target for her. We came back & anchored off Kingston pier. Sydney
