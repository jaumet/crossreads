  
 March  1.3.15 
 & had a good time there, we had a cup of tea at the New Zealand Cafe & arrived back in camp at 8.30; 
 Mon 1st Dismounted parade to-day & were signalling amongst ourselves close to camp.  this morning we had a test in reading; it is said to be a test to see who gets made Corporal of A Squadron.  Lamp practice after tea & it is time we started again as we are not to good with the lamps;  We were reading groups & when we were about to come in some of the lads would not march properly & we were marched around the flat for ten minutes before coming in. was writing until bed time
