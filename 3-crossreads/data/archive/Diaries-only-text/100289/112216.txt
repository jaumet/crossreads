Thursday 1.2.17
Leaving to-morrow; wore boots putties for the first time for weeks, not as well as I would like to be
Friday 2.2.17
After straightening up surroundings of "Chateau" left in a "Char-a-banc" for somewhere in vicinity of Albert. Exceptionally cold trip, had to stop a few times en?route get out to warm ourselves. Passed through Amiens on way; not sorry to get to our destination.
