<p>Sighted small school of whales in the distance, during morning and have been accompanied by a great number of porpoises who are always playing in front of the ship.</p>
<p>Several cases of mumps reported.</p>
<p>Monday 18th<br/>Still good weather. Only a bit of life boat drill done this morning. Haven't seen any passing ships as yet and are 400 mls. from land.</p>
