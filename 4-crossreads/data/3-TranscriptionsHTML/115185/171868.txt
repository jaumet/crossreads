<p>7 &ndash; that I have not been able to sleep with the ache at nights &ndash; in my thighs as well now they are alright. I had diarrhoea for two days &ndash; but starved &amp; got better I am grateful to say had not to stop work. I feel very well today &ndash; but hungry. The food is still very bad. But I find I am better not eating than eating rotten food.<br/>However by the time you get this I expect I shall be getting better food &ndash; all efforts are being made by the heads of all departments on the island to improve the food etc.</p>
