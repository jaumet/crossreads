<p>August,1918<br/>Thursday 22</p>
<p>In front of Morcourt, Bty Position<br/>Weather fine and clear.<br/>At 4 am this morning we had a fairly heavy dose of gas, sneezing stuff sent everyone seedy. Fritz cleaned up all our lines again and shelled bty fairly heavily<br/>Weather fine and clear.<br/>At 4 am this morning we had a fairly heavy dose of gas,<br/>sneezing stuff sent everyone seedy. Fritz cleaned up all <br/>our lines again and shelled Bty fairlyheavily during<br/>&nbsp;morning. Had a day's solid going on Bdge and Bty lines.<br/>During lunch hour Fritz opened up on 60 pdr Battery<br/>and knocked them about badly. He killed a sgt there, and<br/>&nbsp;jiggered the lines about until they  looked like<br/>&nbsp;concertinas. Knipe to L'aison for stunt <br/>tomorrow. Good luck</p>
