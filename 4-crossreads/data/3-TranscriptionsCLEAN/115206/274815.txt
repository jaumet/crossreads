
September 1918.Monday 2Bty Position close to the SommeArrived after an uneventful journey at an old Bty position very close to the river, at 2.15am. Slept under the stars until 4.30am when Smithy and I laid a line to 14 A and B HQ. and O.P., a tramp of about 41/2 miles in all.Weather showery .5th Pioneer moved out and we occupied dugouts vacated by them. Established a pit and worked on wire and etc till lunch. Had a shift on the phone  until [indecipherable], slept til 5.30pm.Smithy's feet pretty bad, bandaged same for him."A" battery 298 pulled in close to us.
