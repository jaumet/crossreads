<p>Aug 15. 16. 17th.<br/>Nothing of much interest except news from Russian &amp; Italian fronts &ndash; Receipt of large mail from "Aussy".</p>
<p>Aug 18th.<br/>Fourth Division relieved by First. Parcel despatched to home.</p>
<p>Aug. 19th.<br/>Our spare time occupied by "Soccer" &ndash; More letters to &amp; from home.</p>
<p>Aug 20th.<br/>Harry parsons &ndash; Keith Harris etc &ndash; Death of Jim Cunningham &amp; 3 others 1st Fld. Amb.</p>
<p>Aug 21st<br/>Sabbath &ndash; Church "Confession &amp; Communion" &ndash; Fifth Fld Amb relieved us at "Anzac Rest Camp</p>
<p>Aug 22nd.<br/>Our Ambulance moves off from Vadencourt to Albert where we bivouac for the night.</p>
