<p>Lemnos,<br/>October 17th 1915</p>
<p>Dear old Mick,</p>
<p>Was pleased to get yours of Aug. 23rd &amp; 30th especially as I was down with a slight attack of the flu.  And letters from home are doubly welcome at such times.  I am all right now, however;  the Canadian medical officer informed me of the good conditions of my throat and lungs etc., which were probably instrumental in my throwing off a few unwelcome visitors in the shape of a few thousand typhoid germs.  So my run of luck still continues good.  I only hope that the "Coppabella" luck is still holding good &ndash; that the Exams will come off all right &amp; that Dad will be his old self ere you receive this.
