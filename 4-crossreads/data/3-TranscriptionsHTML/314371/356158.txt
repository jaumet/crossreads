<p>Friday, May 24th</p>
<p>June 7th<br/>Sighted land 11 a.m.  Picked up Fremantle Pilot at 1 p.m., berthed about 2 p.m.<br/>After tea Perth Ladies came on board w/- fruit &amp; cake for us &amp; we had a great feed.<br/>Pulled out about 9 p.m., sea very rough.</p>
<p>June 13th<br/>Arrived Port Melbourne about midnight.  Disembarked from "Tofua" &amp; reembarked on "Suevic" same morning, 14th June.  Sailed at noon.  Beautiful weather.</p>
<p>June 15th<br/>Weather continues "Bon".<br/>We are getting quite excited.</p>
