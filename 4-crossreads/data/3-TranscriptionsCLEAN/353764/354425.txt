  
 Friday 17.3.16 It has been very hot here to-day & also  a  very windy & unpleasant.  Another one of our Officers is leaving us, having been appointed to a position down the Canal, you will know him when I mention that it is Mr Wells. I have not been out to-day, having had a very busy time on the lines. We take in 195 more horses in the morning which I understand will make our full complement. The 3rd Divisional Train will soon be here & I understand they are a certain number of  horses  officers short, so my chance may come along after all, as it is it looks pretty dark on it.
