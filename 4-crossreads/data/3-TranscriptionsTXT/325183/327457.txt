
About 5.30 p.m. whilst going up to the Mess to hear the latest 'oil' I met our little Padre dashing down the street with a purposeful look on his face. He was full and brimming over with information chief of which being the fact that all orders kits had to be at the Company Quarter Master Store by 6 p.m. I retraced my steps to my billet therefore where I found my batman Peter Alexander who was of course delighted to hear the news.
