<p>Sunday 28 July 1916<br/>Inspection by outsider at 5.45 p.m.  I meet Mr. Nicholas, go to the Band.  Came Home with Annie's Room.</p>
<p>Monday 24 July 1916<br/>Mac goes to Ras el Tin.  33 men come from Sydney Pav.  I have jelly &amp; cocoa in No. 8 &amp; play Bridge.  B. &amp; D. go on leaf.  Get letters from Home, George, Bess, Marg., Jess, Julie &amp; Mem.</p>
<p>Tuesday 25 July 1916<br/>Full Dress Inspection.  Molly picks her Boy.  I ride to Abbassia &amp; call at McCougi's.  Dinner &amp; go to Cairo.  Meet Roley Hale &amp; play Billiards.  Met McCougi &amp; go to Pictures with Maltese Dames.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26 July 1916<br/>Went to see Dick Rowe at Pyramids Bar.  Ellis &amp; I, we talk with two Pommys &amp; listen to Gippo songs on Phonograph.</p>
<p>Thursday 27 July 1916<br/>Wrote to Jess, Julie &amp; Mem.  Ellis &amp; I go to Cairo, meet Cougi &amp; ride back to Heliopolis Hotel with Maltese Lady.  It is very nice.</p>
<p>Friday 28 July 1916<br/>I go to Cairo for my Photos.  Meet Kinna &amp; Ice Cream, Lemonade &amp; Peanuts.  Wrote to Bess, Marg., Lily, Mum (58).</p>
<p>Saturday 29 July 1916<br/>I play Cricket at 6 p.m. &amp; nothing else is worthy of note.  Send Photo to Jennie.</p>
