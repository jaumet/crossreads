At Asluj I believe there are Turkish Barracks which have been evacuated.Asluj Oct 30th '17This is a queer little place ringed in with hills it has walls, barracks  & a small mosque. The Railway (Turkish) from El Wadia to Beersheba passes through here, or I should say, used to pass here, for last March the Div. stunted out here &  blew up the bridges  &   also the viaduct at this place.We move out of here tonight and
