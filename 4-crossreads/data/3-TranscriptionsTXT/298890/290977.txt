 14. Thursday Worked at removing things from drawing room a.m. at 3 met Nell at Kew &amp; had tea down there. It rained I got wet &amp; sat 20 min on open station. Result:- rigor, temp, malaria. 
 15. Friday In bed with malaria all day. Temp. Not able to see Nell off. 
 16. Saturday Up again but feeling desperately depressed &amp; weak. Fogs for days
