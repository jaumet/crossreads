<p>Administrative Headquarters A.I.F.<br/>130 Horesferry Road<br/>London  S.W.</p>
<p>30th October 1916</p>
<p>Senator the Hon. George F. Pearce<br/>Department of Defence<br/>Melbourne, Australia.</p>
<p>Dear Mr Minister</p>
<p>Re Lieutenant K. A. Ferguson</p>
<p>Following up my letter of the 27th instant, I enclose you a letter I have received from young Ferguson, together with a copy of my reply, which speak for themselves.  You will see what a jolly good chap he is.</p>
<p>Kindly thoughts</p>
<p>Truly yours</p>
<p>Robt. McC. Anderson</p>
