<p>Tuesday 30 October 1917.</p>
<p>Posted letter.</p>
<p>Watered camels.</p>
<p>Moved out at 5 pm sharp &amp; commenced a trek till midnight.</p>
<p>(Wed 31<sup>st</sup> Oct continued.)<br />Beersheebah by our guns.</p>
<p>2 redoubts have fallen and at 2 pm B&ndash;h may fall as our artillery fire is very effective.</p>
<p>Taweil-el-Habair is the hill from which we watched the bombardment and behind which the collecting station was.</p>
<p>H Qrs also dug in there.</p>
