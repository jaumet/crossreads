<p>March 1918<br/>Tuesday 12</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Weather beautiful, warm fine and clear.<br/>Spent forenoon at pit, and proceeded with Russell and Costa down to Kemmel.<br/>Met Dick Pennell at 5.10 pm. and had the privilege of seeing a very good show. Kookaburras. Hobbs and his staff were present.<br/>During the afternoon a German Observation balloon was brought down at Kemmel.<br/>I received a souvenier portion from Watson.<br/>Anti-tank spooks went forward to O.P. an we had to rush up home again to go forward.</p>
