
Friday, May 12th:Same as above.  Usual routine.  Letter to Jack.
Saturday, May 13th:Wet day.  Rise 5 a.m.  Work on huts till 3.30 p.m.  Hot bath at Divisional Baths.  Retire about 8 p.m.  Letter to Mother.
Sunday, May 14th:Rise 6 a.m.  Off to work as usual.  Return to Camp 4.30.
Monday, May 15th:Rise 5.30.  Fine.  I ask our Lieutenant to allow me back to trenches as I am not contented at this work.  No definite reply to my query.  Work all day building huts.  Return to Camp 5.30 p.m.  Retire 8.30 p.m.
Tuesday, May 16th:Rise 5.30.  Work at Huts all day.  These huts are really hospital wards for the A.M.C.  Back to Camp 5.30 p.m.  Retire 9 p.m.
Wednesday, May 17th:Rise 5.30 a.m.  Ride out on transports to Bombing School (Sailley)  erect hut, return home 5 p.m.  Gas alarm at 8 p.m.  Retire 9 p.m.  Fine day.
