 Monday 3 June. Last night there was a stunt on up our portion of the line, &amp; successfully it was accomplished too. The squads referred to were not even warned, but we knew there was to be a stunt, nevertheless, &amp; were prepared for a call up, which, however, never came. The bombardment was supposed to begin about one or two, but I never heard a sound, although I dreamed of being wounded in a barrage
