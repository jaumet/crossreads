<p>Thursday 29th/3/17.<br/>As there were no early morning parade at 6-30 am all hands did not rise until 7 am when breakfast was over we were paraded at 9 am &amp; had some drill &amp; marching until dinner time &amp; at 2 Pm we were again paraded &amp; marched on to a parade ground to await an Inspection by Sir Newton Moon Bridager General of the A.I.F who duly arrived at 3.30 Pm &amp; after being inspected <strike>was</strike> we were welcomed &amp; congratulated on our appearance, we were dismissed &amp; after Tea I went for a long walk about 5 Miles along the Road with two chum.
