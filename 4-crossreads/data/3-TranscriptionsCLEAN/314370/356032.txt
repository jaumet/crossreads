
May, 1916Thursday 25Mopped about camp all morning & after dinner went out to Pattis'Guiseppe had gone to Port Said for a few days to visit some relatives.After supper several more people came in & we had a glorious time till 11.30 pm
Friday 26very warm againvery little to do, so do nothing but read & smoke & smoke & read.After tea I went to a picture show in Cairo.
