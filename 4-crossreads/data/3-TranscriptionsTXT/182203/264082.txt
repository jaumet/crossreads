Have not heard from Fred & no news of him, so suppose he is still going strong.George Bower is much better & went out of London yesterday to a convalescing Home. He rang-up & asked me to have luncheon with him yesterday but I had a previous engagement. Norman, I cannot hear anything of nor Rhon. so no news, good news. I received a nice lot of letters last night, some from France & Aust. Two from home July 2nd & 10th , so I felt happy to know you were all well then, also one from Ella, Harry & Flo.
