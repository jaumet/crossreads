<p>25 February 1918 Monday<br/>Started back at 5A.M. bitterly cold &amp; halted at 4 pm not far from Junction Station  See February 16th   <strike>Many of the men with sore heads the result of Jerusalem cogniac</strike><br/>Our Field Post Office sent us letters. <strike> I got 3 from wife 2 from Laura.</strike><br/>Horses very tucked up from cold &amp; shin sore from stones-   I stand these perishes well, fresh air &amp; exercise-<br/>I enclose a pressed Anemone with this- Cyclamen to bulky-<br/>Just heard VERDUN had fallen I mention this to show how rumour floats about.</p>
