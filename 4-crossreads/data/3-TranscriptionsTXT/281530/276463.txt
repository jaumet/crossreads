
8/6/18Firing Line. Ville-sur-AncreQuiet and dull in the morning. After lunch I went off with a Map of Artillery, Thewlis and Smith and we walked over grass paddocks and along C.T.s until we struck the front line. We passed several old German posts now well behind our own line and well bombarded. There were many old Hun helmets and pieces of equipment lying about here. Pushed on to the front line and observed our 18 pounders firing. We look well into Morlancourt in different places and have the Boche's head well down. Out behind one of his posts is an old abandoned 77 gun pretty well knocked to pieces by our fire.
