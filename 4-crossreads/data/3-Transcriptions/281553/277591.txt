<p>France 9/5/16</p>
<p>To Kitty</p>
<p>This is a rough diary kept from May 12th 1915 to <strike>Jany</strike> Dec 18.  Evacuation on loose pages pinned to back cover dating from Dec. 18 to Jan. 6. then continued in this book 9th Jan. opp Record for May 23rd.</p>
<p>Many dates left out as not kept regularly, but nothing of importance during those periods.</p>
<p>From Don</p>
<p>This was not intended for others eyes (by untidyness)</p>
<p>out of paper was cause for such changing of pages</p>
<p>Don</p>
