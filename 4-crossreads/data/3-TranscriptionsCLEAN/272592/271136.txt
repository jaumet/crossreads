 They are now issuing daily rum to the troops & how the boys like it. 
 I saw to-day in a French paper that when we took Pozeries the French cheered along the whole of their lines from one end to the other thats something for Australia. 
 22nd.   We are holding our Sports this afternoon, & for a wonder the sun is shining brightly & things are drying fast, all those with the exception of the rifle teams marched down with their belts on 
 I am now penning this laying on the green grass, & at the present time I am watching the rifle squads in the competition drill A. & B. Coy have just gone off
