 Page 31 
 France 
 2/11/18 
 Darling Mither, 
 Have just received the end of August eltters and very pleased indeed to note you are all in your usual.  I see poor old Dad had a touch of the cold but note he is about again. 
 I note that the shop is keeping its end up altho' I see things are becoming more stringent to do business.  Still, I suppose Lock will be using his head and take no risks.  And what sort of stuff is butter? I have forgotten the taste of it, and quite used to margarine altho I don't go nap on it.
