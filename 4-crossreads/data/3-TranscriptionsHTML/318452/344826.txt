<p>Yesterday Friday. I went ashore had lunch at the Club, made a few purchases, &amp; had tea at Mr Hodges at the parsonage Went to see Mt Timberlake in the afternoon<br/>We have been Coaling here for two days &amp; nights &amp; the dust. Noise &amp; general confusion has been most uncomfortable I shall go ashore this afternoon<br/>Left Capetown 3rd Feb<br/>Arrived Melbourne 22 Feb<br/>Left by train to Sydney 22<br/>Arrived 23<br/>14 days leave went to Uralla<br/>Receive Discharge 4th Apl</p>
