<p>1916 November 23<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Another fairly easy night. Sleep all morning as usual. Have a shave &amp; general clean up. Nothing of moment occurs during day.  Frost &amp; ice this morning. Fog lifted in early morning.<br/>Nothing doing all day. Sleep most of the day light hours.</p>
