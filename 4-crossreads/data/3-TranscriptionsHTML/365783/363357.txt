<p>After dinner the reading of stories, the issue of chocolates, the goodnights &amp; saying of prayers, a possible picky back entreaties for a jiggety jig &amp; then peace perfect peace.   I also thought of dear old Father &amp; Mother &ndash; Farther in his arm chair reading a book, Mother seated in the most uncomfortable chair in the room and sewing. My thoughts also turned to all the boys &amp; their wives &amp; to Aunt Sara Cassie &amp; Frank.</p>
