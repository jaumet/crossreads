<p>Now that I have had to commence a new book while on the homeward trip I am going to endeavour very hard to fill it up, before our two months journey draws to a close, and we once more land on Australian soil.  The feeling that we are homeward bound is a very gratifying one, for notwithstanding the fact that I have had a most enjoyable holiday, and have seen things that I have longed to see since childhood I am very anxious to rejoin my family circle.
