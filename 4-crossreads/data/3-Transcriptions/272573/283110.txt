<p>June 27th. Tues.<br/>Owing to the large number of cases that have come in, nearly all our store of sterilized dressings have been used up</p>
<p>I don't think our French conversation lessons are going to be much of a success The interpreter who is teaching us does not seem to have the happy knack of imparting knowledge of that kind.</p>
<p>June 28th. Wed.<br/>In order to give other men experience of work in the D.R. I am relieving the orderly in the Officer's Ward.</p>
<p>There is a very pleasant outlook from the top windows of this building. We are   (<strike>well</strike> ) well out into the country and one can look into the pretty gardens of the villagers gardens which are well kept and containing beautiful fruit trees. Further over we look into beautiful green fields with cattle quietly grazing in them and bordered with tall trees and with hedges. Only the grazing fields seem to have hedges in this country.</p>
