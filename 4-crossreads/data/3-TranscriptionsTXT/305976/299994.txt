 Saturday Ap 6 Visited Greek Monastry near camp today. It is built on Mt Forty[?] also Mt Temptation next to it. This monastry is built in the face of the cliff of stone &amp; inhabited by a few monks. Practically a impregnable position Sunday Ap 7 An absolute roaster to day Temp 120 degrees had to go across River on message While over, Jacko started leaving some coal boxes. Was night before I could get back.
