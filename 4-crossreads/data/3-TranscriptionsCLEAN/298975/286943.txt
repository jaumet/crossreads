Monday 20thIn spite of all the talk we got 8/- today I payed the Mess Orderlies in full 7/- & 1/- for 2 copies of the Beltana Bugle just cut it out. They say a Cruser passed early this morning & a steam boat passed a long way off this evening. Sports on deck. The sea is calmer than today than I thought possible
