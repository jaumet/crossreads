
Friday 6/7/17lovely morning, nippy almost a frost last night.off this morning 7 miles to Mailley Maillet. Hut camp good bye estaminets.Pay 15/9 28.2.0.left Mellingcourt 10 to 11 stopped at Senlis 12 for cocoa & Biscuits hot marching but not too bad, Pack dam heavy.Through Hedoncourt then on to Mailly Millet got here Mailly 1.30 P.M.Beautiful camp among the Trees & shrubswent into the Town about 1 mile away, been a fine place but the huns as usual have knocked it about, but not as bad as some,The Town Hall is intact, also church.
Saturday 7/7/17.Beautiful morning but cold last nightcan hear the guns here, going night & day 20 miles from Arras.Pay. 16/9 28.3.0Strong wind blowing & getting cloudy expect it means rainleave here in a few days & off to [blank] 10 Kilos from Amiens.wish they would hurry that other stripe of mine alongAmiens no good without money & mine all gone buying food, cigs, Battery etcPosted Post Cards to Nell, Tony, Jack & Carol.
Sunday 8/7/17.Terrible storm last night thunder & lightening awful never saw anything worse in Ausy. started 2. a.m. & to day another came on been so bad that for the 3rd time since I joined the army no Parade was blownwe are camped in a wood on a rise & the huts down the slope were swamped fortunately we are under a Tarpauline on the Hill & even so got wet throughPay. 17/9. 28.4.0.slept all the morning & nothing  much else to do this Afternoon turned out fine about 4 PM. cool wind blowingD. Coy. going guard of Honour to the King.
