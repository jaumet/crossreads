<p>left Larkhill Camp and marched to Amesbury in the dark, through mud up to our ankles <br/>with full pack, blankets, 150 rounds of ammunition and rifle, the whole weighing nearly <br/>120 lbs.  We only had 2 spells of 2 or 3 minutes each so were pretty well done up when <br/>we got to the station. The train left at 10 to 8 and arrived at Southampton at 10.20, the <br/>latter part of the journey being by the sea.  We rested in the station shed for the <br/>remainder of the day  At the wharf beside the sheds lay the African Princess</p>
