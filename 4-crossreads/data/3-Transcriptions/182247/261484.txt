<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 8</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Eighth day out from Sydney.&nbsp; Arrived Fremantle and anchored in the stream at 6 a.m.&nbsp; Good weather, but hot and sultry.&nbsp; Steamer was granted pratique.&nbsp; General shore leave granted from 8 am till 6 pm. Left steamer with chum Larsen and Norman Walker ar 10.15 and arrived Fremantle station at 10.30, having come ashore in a motor launch.&nbsp; Paid 1/- launch and train fares both ways.&nbsp; Entrained for Perth in special, arriving there at 11 am.&nbsp; Sent telegrams home.&nbsp; Went into Ice Cream shop for cool drinks and wrote cards and letters home.&nbsp; Had Dinner at Moana Cafe.&nbsp; Had a good grill and enjoyed the diversion from transport food.&nbsp; After the meal, had a stroll round Perth then went for motor ride through King&#39;s Park which took but half an hour.&nbsp; Raining nearly all the time. Enjoyed and appreciated the magnificent views of Perth and its environs from the park.&nbsp; Had Tea.&nbsp; Bought provisions and comforts for Mess and caught special train for Fremantle on the return.&nbsp; Train was jammed.&nbsp; Lots of men &quot;tanked&quot;.&nbsp; Douglas landed back injured and went into hospital.&nbsp; Arrived back on ship again at 6.45 feeling tired, but having enjoyed the day immensely. Wrote diary and turned in.&nbsp; While in Perth I called on Harry Crust at the Pavilion Theatre.&nbsp; Was shown over his theatre.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 9</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Sailed from Fremantle at 4pm.&nbsp; People gave us a great send off.&nbsp; Crowds lined the shore.&nbsp; Boats whistled, trains whistled, crowds cheered as did the soldiers on our boat, and the &quot;Argyllshire&quot;alongside.&nbsp; After tea, had some harmony for an hour or two.&nbsp; Sea a little rough.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 10</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Fine day.&nbsp; Sea smooth again.&nbsp; Did nothing in morning.&nbsp; Comforts from Comforts Committee were opened.&nbsp; Got my share of cigarettes, fruit, biscuits, Milk, Jam, etc in plenty.&nbsp; We all gave cheers for the good old Comforts people.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 14</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Sea somewhat moderated on yesterday&#39;s showing.&nbsp; Spent most of day at Morse signalling and watching teams train for coming sports competitions on the morrow.&nbsp; Had a box with Norman Walker.&nbsp; Have seen nothing at sea since we left Fremantle.&nbsp; Held third concert at night.&nbsp; Great success.&nbsp; Did &quot;Dandy Fifth&quot; and &quot;Ode to Kitchen-er.&quot;&nbsp; Got 2 printed souvenir programs to send home.</td>
