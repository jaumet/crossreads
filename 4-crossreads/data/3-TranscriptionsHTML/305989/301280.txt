<p>Sunday 28 October 1917<br/>Left Kossarne about 5 p.m. &amp; proceeded onto Khalassa arriving at 10 p.m. stayed the night (on picquet) beautiful clear night full moon, chilly.</p>
<p>Monday 29 October 1917<br/>Usual routine;  Taube flew over.  Left Khalassa about 2 p.m. &amp; proceeded to Asluj arriving about 8.45 beautifully clear night but dusty.  Had a good night sleep.  Stand to 4 a.m. foot very sore.</p>
<p>Tuesday 30 October 1917<br/>Usual routine, drew 3 days rations.  Taube over very high, two of our planes hovering about  early warm, got my foot dressed.  Saw some Arabs from Mecca, left Asluj at 6 travelled all night.</p>
<p>Wednesday 31 October 1917<br/>Arrived in view of Beersheba about daylight, heavy bombardment from the other side, engaged the redouts S.E. of Beersheba at dinnertime, Beersheba taken at 10 to 6 &amp; all other [indecipherable].</p>
<p>Thursday 1 November 1917<br/>Camped on Wadi near Beersheba for day, got a few curios.  Had good nights sleep.</p>
<p>Friday 2 November 1917<br/>Still in Wadi.  Watered up at Wadi Viaduct, good view of Beersheba.  Stunt at 11.35 travelled all night.</p>
<p>Saturday 3 November 1917<br/>Attacked redouts at Khuweilfeh at daylight.  No. 1 &amp; 2 in action with A of 1st Regt., very heavy casualties:  very busy day;  continued all day, withdrew at dark &amp; came back to Beersheba &amp; watered up at 12.30 &amp; got to bed about 1.30.  Poor old Alec killed.  10 killed &amp; 60 wounded in 1st Regt., got home 12.30.</p>
