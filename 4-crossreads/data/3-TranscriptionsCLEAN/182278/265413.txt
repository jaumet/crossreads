 Thursday 2/5/18  moved to CAMON  I reconnoitred the front South of Somme arriving home tired after 20 mile ride. 
 Friday 3/5/18  Men had a good rest  Officers reconnoitring  Huns bombed Amiens but did little damage 
 Saturday 4/5/18  Reconnoitred area between Glisy & Nicholas- moved to vicinity of Glisy. 
 Sunday 5/5/18  Wisdom back- had dinner with 25 th . who celebrated 1000 days service.
