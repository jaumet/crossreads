 A New Zealand picket was shot last night by a Bedouin. Excitement is running high among the Boys as what Headquarters will do in the matter. Wednesday Dec 11 The crowd went mad today. Unless something is done they threaten to burn down the Bedouin camp. Things are looking ugly. I do not think it will come to this but I think it is time Headquarters moved on the matter.
