<p>21 April 1918<br/>Sunday<br/>3 after Easter<br/>Beastly cold weather. Nothing of importance going. Have not heard anything of my discharge so far. Things appear to be at a dead lock in France.</p>
<p>1918 April 22<br/>Monday<br/>Nothing of importance going. Just the same old camp routine. The weather has at last turned out nice and fine and warm.</p>
