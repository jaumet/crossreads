 Australian Red Cross Bulford 12/10/18 
 Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George, 
 I received one stray letter from you to-day but the mail is very straggling this time.  We have had a long spell of showery weather but it is the usual weather here &amp; not remarkable &amp; we are used to it.
