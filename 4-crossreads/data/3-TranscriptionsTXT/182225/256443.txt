 NOV. &nbsp; MONDAY 20 &nbsp; 1916 
 Fine but bleak Came on to rain a little during the night. Took horses out to graze ect in the morning &amp; cleaning harness ect in afternoon. &nbsp;We had an impromptu concert at night &amp; the prizes won&nbsp;at the Brigade sports were given out by General Grimwade. The prizes consisted of silver 1 st &nbsp;&amp; copper 2 nd &nbsp;medallions. I got a copper one for the wrestling on horseback which 27 th &nbsp;team won.
