<p>About this time a message came along from 15th Brigade Hdqrs. to move up to the assembly position for the attack at 12 midnight.  It was now nearly 11 p.m. so I decided to turn in for some sleep toute suite.  About 11.30 p.m. a notice came from Bde cancelling our move for the night much to the intense satisfaction of all concerned.</p>
