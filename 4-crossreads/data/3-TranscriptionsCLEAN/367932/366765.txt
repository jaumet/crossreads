
Extract from letter from Trooper Basil Thorpe, C Troop B Squadron,1st. A.L.H. to his father Mr A.J. Thorpe, Coonamble.
[Basil Thorpe 2652, 20 year old Agriculture Officer from Park Rd, Camden NSW.  He enlisted on 17 January 1916 and at the conclusion of the war returned to Australia on 22 Jan. 1920.  (See 1st Light Horse Embarkation Roll)]
