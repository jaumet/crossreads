<p>&amp; I'm not worrying.  I don't care which way the wind blows as long as it will one day blow me back to Australia &amp; that the date will not be far distant.  We are likely to be moved down to the Seaside to Weymouth or thereabouts but I do not suppose that Dave Ellis &amp; I will go to France for quite a long time.  I suppose You still have Your Electric Torch.  Does Sandy ever come down to meet you now?  No, Dad the Pipe, pouch &amp; gloves are for you if you want them.  I do not smoke &amp; I don't care for gloves.  Woollen Mits with one's hands in the Pockets are the best.  We get the morning Herald now &amp; again so some do get here alright.
