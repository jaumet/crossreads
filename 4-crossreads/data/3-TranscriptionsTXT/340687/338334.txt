 January, 1917. Tuesday 30 Early up and fell in at 8 then cleaned camp and started at nine via Montauban and down to quarry and over line past Bazentin to a camp near a big wood at 12. Railway close and appears to be a large place. Canvas covered square huts which appear very leaky and a bad stove. Mad a bed and did fatigue of water which has to be brought on railway about &frac12; mile (12 in hut). Very cold. Two big german howitzers close by evidently abandoned &amp; blown up and a big dump of old shells just behind us. Heavy howitzers quite close. Shell landed just in front coming up. Very cold and sunny in morning and snowed heavily in afternoon.
