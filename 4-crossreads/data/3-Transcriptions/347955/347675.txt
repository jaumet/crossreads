<p>left Raffa 9 A.M. we had to walk and lead our camels to Shelall twelve miles we got to there 3.30 PM a lot more messing about had dinner.  I am so tired as blases had to lay down tea 6.30 turned in.<br/>Sunday March 3.3.18.<br/>turned out 7 AM.  Camels out to graze Breakfast fell in 9A.M shifted the camel lines they were down the wrong way dug latrines had dinner went to the waddy done my washing had a good wash had tea turned in.<br/>Monday March 4.3.18<br/>turned out 4.30 A.M fed and saddled up went on march was advance guard went over near Gaza halted 10 AM grazed camels had</p>
