<p>For not "standing to" of a morning quick enough, we have to get up &frac12; hour earlier in future, at least they are going to try &amp;  enforce it.</p>
<p>Plenty of shooting for the anti-aircraft &amp; machine guns for the German planes have been very busy of late.  All this afternoon great strings of men have been passing along the road bound for the firing line I fancy they are 2nd Div.</p>
