<p>Monday 8 April 1918<br/>Cloudy, no photos.  Letters from Home.  Wrote Mother, Mrs. Howey, Mrs. G. Lee.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9 April 1918<br/>Photos.  Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 3 h. 35 m.  A good got a good lot of photos, very successfull.  They were very important as they were wanted to complete map.<br/>News from Hun prisoner that Oliver Lee is a prisoner and safe.</p>
