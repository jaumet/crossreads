 Outside Jerusalem 14th March '18 Today starting out along the Jaffa-Jerusalem road from Latron we advanced into a tortured looking country composed of huge hills &amp; deep gullys. On the hills line on line of twisted strata of white Limestone &ndash; or what looks like limestone &ndash; rock heaved up by some prehistoric earthquake.
