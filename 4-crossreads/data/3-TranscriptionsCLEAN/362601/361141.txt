  
 9 January, Thursday 
 F Go down town but get the cold shoulder from all so I return Home straight away & get by mess time to talk until bed-time. Have long argument about Burford system as compared with this system of running a Hospital. 
 10 January, Friday 
 F S/S Lilley & I go down town to YMCA. Band concert. We wait an Hour for the Pictures & then return Home without seeing any. It is very "cold & windy".
