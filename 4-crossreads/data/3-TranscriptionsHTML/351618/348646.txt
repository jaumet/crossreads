<p>Life under active service does not tend to improve ones morals as far as cribbing is concerned. To use an Australianism one is rather forced &ndash; I should say tempted but the word is hardly strong enough &ndash; to pinch anything in the form of eatables that might be handy. Those working on the Beach have ample opportunity to make use of their "pinching" propensities.</p>
<p>During the last few days a good deal of shelling has been taking place on the other side of the Peninsula. The rolling boom of heavy guns can be heard daily.</p>
