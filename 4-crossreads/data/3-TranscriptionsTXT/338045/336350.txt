 Nov 10th &ndash; Friday 
 Feeling very tired &amp; not fit for much exertion. Very monotonous &amp; I am already sick &amp; tired of hospital. No treatment here &amp; very little notice taken of any of our movements. What is ultimately our fate is hard to say. Probably back to France. 
 Nov 11th &ndash; Saturday 
 Beautiful day, for a change. Very little to do. Write some letters, walk round the grounds. Bed early.
