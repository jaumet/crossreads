<p>Tuesday Jan 15th</p>
<p>Bristol : wet day : caught the 4 pm back &amp; got to camp about 8.30 pm : very heavy rain : got a parcel from Aunt Myra also socks from J B J &amp; mittens from C H.</p>
<p>Wednesday Jan 16th</p>
<p>Fovant : cold day : usual parades during the day &amp; had a couple of reading tests : wrote at night :</p>
<p>Thursday Jan 17th</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades : all inside work on account of the sloppy ground &amp; rain</p>
