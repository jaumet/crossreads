<p><u>Dec 4th</u>:- Moved out to Jaffa, passed through the town at night &amp; camped about 1/2 a mile outside.</p>
<p><u>Dec 5th</u>:- Obtained leave &amp; spent part of the day exploring the town of Jaffa. The population of the place in normal times amounts to 45,000, there are now however only 12,000 people there which gives the town a rather deserted appearance. There is a&nbsp; great dearth of &quot;change&quot; in this country for the present &amp; one is almost forced to spend either a 10/- or &pound;1 note when purchasing</p>
