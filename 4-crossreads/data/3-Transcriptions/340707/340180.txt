<p>I visit Mdlle Hensson &amp; find friends from Tahiti there. Mde Julien who knows Mde Tomel very well indeed &amp; once made some trip with her. These people are very good &amp; offer to get their son to buy my law books for me &amp; the son makes an appointment the following day.</p>
<p>To day I visited M Chapries at Law School &amp; find that I can pass my exams on producing certain certificates which I have left in England</p>
<p>3-7-19<br/>Visited bank this morning &amp; find that after repaying money expended I have 246 fr (&pound;8) to carry on with This places me on firm footing again for the other day all the money I possessed was 1 &frac12; francs &amp; no opportunity of getting more even by borrowing although 2 ladies had offered to lend it to me</p>
<p>Believe the knowledge that you are without money in strange country &amp; with no friends from whom you would care to borrow or ask for a meal is not a pleasant sensation</p>
