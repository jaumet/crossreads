<p>Government House,<br />Rabaul.<br />4th Oct. 1914.</p>
<p>Sir,<br />I have the honour to forward herewith list of German Government Officials and their Wives, who are being deported this day by H.M.A.S. &quot;Berrima&quot;.<br />The Officials have not taken the Oath of Neutrality/</p>
<p>I have the honor to be,<br />Sir,<br />Your obedient servant,</p>
<p>Colonel</p>
<p>Administrator.</p>
<p>To:- The Military Commandant,<br />Sydney.</p>
