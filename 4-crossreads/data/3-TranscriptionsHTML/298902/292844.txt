<p>to rest about 11 oclock, the people having supper ready for me.  Was vastly amused at their comparison of English &amp; Australians, they saying that the English were too stand offish whilst the diggers had quite won their hearts.</p>
<p>Tuesday 14th Jan.  Called at 7A.M. as I had decided to get back by any means possible.  Said goodbye to our friends after breakfast again no payment, &amp; tramed it to the city eventually travelling by lorry to Beaumont, where we met a car which took us right to Sautain, our village.  A very cold day and I was glad to be back, having been away six days for a two days leave.  Received a small mail on arrival, telling of the Armistice celebrations in Sydney.</p>
<p>Forgot to mention a wonderful Church of St Paul's in Antwerp, wonderful</p>
