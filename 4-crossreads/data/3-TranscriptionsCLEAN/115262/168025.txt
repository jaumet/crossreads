  
 Saturday 15 September 1917. 
 On QM's fatigue today & missed the watering trip to the Waddy. Not sorry either. (One camel having died is struck off the strength.) "Orders". (What next!!) 
 One taube over this morning. 
 Boys returned from  Marakeb  Malaleh Beach. Went short way & had a good time. Had 24 hrs. on the beach. 
 No Australian mail yet. 
 Wrote 1 letter.
