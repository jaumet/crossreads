<p>Lunatic forced with Bayonet to work (Beyer)<br/>Provision stealing by Soldiers.<br/>Luggage robbed, luggage not delivered.<br/>No Straw delivered for Bedding<br/>Pris being arrested without course &amp; kept under arrest without trial.<br/>Letter containing complaints made by Pris of War addresses to U.S.A Consul confiscated &amp; not delivered.<br/>Complaint to Hawkes &amp; authority impossible.<br/>Treatment of sick Pris while in hospital<br/>And what is our main and mos prominent complaint, and wich we look upon as a disgrace on our persones and an insult to our Nation is the Flogging Incident of one of our fellow Pris, wich we demand to be investigatet</p>
