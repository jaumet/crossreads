<p>December 1917<br/>Friday 21<br/>Suffra.  Went out at 8 am as covering party to infantry reconnaissance. Showering all the time. Returned to camp 1 pm. Showering all afternoon. Went on outpost. Wet cold blankets. Fine night.</p>
<p>Saturday 22<br/>Suffra.  Fine day at last. Dying blankets. Cabled home for Xmas  more mail I got 13 letters and a parcel from home.</p>
