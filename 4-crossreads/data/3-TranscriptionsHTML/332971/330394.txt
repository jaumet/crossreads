<p>On Monday evening I found myself in a small Inn at bideford, bound for Clovelly in the morning 12 Miles away and going by the Mail Motor &ndash; Ordered an early breakfast and as I was having it in walked an Aussie for an early breakfast and to my delight bound for Clovelly also &ndash; so we spend the day together &ndash; trust the Aussies &ndash; one meets their turned up hats everywhere.  I just loved Clovelly &ndash; sweet Clovelly full of charm and sweetness &ndash; I will not attempt to describe it myself but instead give it you in Charles Kinsley's words in case you have not read his "Westward Ho"  "Take the steepest hillside with which you are acquainted.
