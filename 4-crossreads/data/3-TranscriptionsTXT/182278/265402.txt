
Wednesday 6/3/18. Went by car to Boulogne had dinner & returned about 11 p.m.
Thursday 7/3/18  Orders to move forward received, Bn cleaning up.
Friday 8/3/18  Left Henneveaux at 5Am and entrained at Desvres arrived at Steenwerck at mid-day, took over from Goddard at Canteen Corner  very Dirty camp.
Saturday 9/3/18  Cleaning up- men resting officers reconnoitred for forward areas
Sunday 10/3/18. Formed Bn Mess again Reconnoitred area around Nieppe.
