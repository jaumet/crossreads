 1st March Did nothing all day. 3rd. March Did a guard last night &amp; had a bath &amp; change of clothes in the morning. Moved back to C Camp Bernafay Wood in the afternoon. 4th March Did a fatigue carrying barbed wire. 5th Fatigue in the morning &amp; came up to Switch trench in the afternoon* Snowed all the morning. 6th Stayed in the Switch trench all day &amp; moved up to Rose trench tonight. 7th &amp; 8th Stayed here both days &amp; to go up to the front line tonight. 9 th I acted as stretcher bearer &amp; sat in a dug out all day. Fritz made it very warm all day but we had only one walking case. 10th, March It was pretty hot again today &amp; we had one stretcher case tonight- It took us three hours to get him out. Were relieved at about 12.30 &amp; took four hours to get back to C Camp. 11th March Got to bed about 4.30 this morning &amp; had a spell all day. 12th March Did a small fatigue. 13th March Had a bath &amp; went out to Needle trench for Soup Container. 14th March Fatigue in the morning at Waterlot Farm &amp; came to Millers Son for ration fatigue at night 15th March Spell all day carried rations at night &amp; had to stand by all night. 16th March Spell all day &amp; carried rations again at night
