After lunch at the Hotel we got the felucca again, stocked it with provisions & started down the River. Everything was comfortably arranged.  Played bridge till dark & then turned in.  We reached Kom-ombo at about 11 oclock that night.  It was bright moonlight so we inspected the big temple by moonlight.  We had a good sleep on the felucca.  The guide & the natives cooked us a good breakfast.  There was a good wind on the River so we made good sailing to Edfu where we went ashore & walked about a mile to the temple of Edfu.
