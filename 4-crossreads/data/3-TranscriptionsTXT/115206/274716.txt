 May 1918 Sunday 26 
 Battery W.Lines Weather glorious, nice and fair, no cold wind.  Albert Garfott still down with Trench Fever. Fritz came across early with his bombs and shells and illuminated the countryside with their splashes. Finished the Y.M.C.A. dugout at 9.30 pm Went for a swim with Albert and Ernie to the pool. Fritz put wind up avec bombard avec aeroplane. C'est la Guerre.
