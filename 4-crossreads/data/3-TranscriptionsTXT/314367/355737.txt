 Monday Feb 1-15 &ndash;  No mail so far &ndash; Swim at 6.30 with Fitz &ndash; I am medical guard &amp; Commander D is under my care &ndash; Chief act this morning was my having Johnson up before the Captain &amp; the end of J's career - he is to be recommended for "Dismissal from H.M.Service" &ndash; I gave him his character in full on the quarter deck &amp; said I regarded him as distinctly harmful to the quiet &amp; efficiency of the medical service of the ship &ndash; I also remarked that if a direct lie was of no avail half a dozen statements &ndash; mostly false &ndash; wd act in the same way &ndash; In a way I felt sorry to do this but he's no use to any doctor on a ship &amp; the sooner he was ended the better &ndash; By all accounts he was sent to the Fantome so as to be got rid of &ndash; you cant get away from your character however  &ndash; either on sea or land &ndash; in fact &ndash; the sea &amp; your mess will write your character quicker than anyone else &ndash; "Cap on &ndash; Right about &ndash; "Dismissal from Service" Quick march &ndash; Exit Johnson &ndash; SBA &ndash; Dismiss.
