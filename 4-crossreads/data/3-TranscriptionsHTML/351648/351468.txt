<p>are closed at 9 p.m. &amp; when we got there all Australians had been moved out of the environs of the city further into the desert &amp; there was hardly a trooper to be seen anywhere, there were a considerable number of officers both Australian &amp; English up on leave &amp; the Hotels were full of them.  Going into the Continental I met more men I knew than one would see in Sydney at present in a month, it was delightful meeting them all again &amp; hearing of the others.  I have been talking today to a chap from the 6th L.H. who is now in the artillery, he told me all about everyone, <strike>they are</strike> it was indeed good to hear of them.</p>
