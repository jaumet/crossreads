<p>severely.  Your son had a marvellous escape.  He was with Connor when the latter was hit &amp; has holes in his uniform &amp; deep cuts in his boots from the shell fragments.  He kept his head splendidly in the most trying of circumstances &amp; without waiting for the reinforcements which were being rushed up to him, he retook his lost trenches with the few men he had available.  Unfortunately the Boche took 2 valuable trench mortars, which a fool who was in charge of a trench battery left in the front line contrary to orders.  This has been seriously taken up by the Divisional General &amp; the loss of these guns has rather detracted from the good work of the battalion, &amp; of B.
