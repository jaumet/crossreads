Quite a treat to get somewhere where it is a bit quiet
Wednesday 20.6.17
Slight disturbance between a couple of our Sergeants. Parade preparatory to inspection by G.O.C. to-morrow. Two N.C.Os 22 men went on leave to Amiens. One of our men placed under mental observation; general opinion is that he is working his ticket.
Thursday 21.6.17
Inspection postponed owing to rain till to-morrow. Acting postman for the day. Played Baseball in the evening.
Friday 22.6.17
Camp all in a state owing to the inspection by General Hobbs which took place at 11.15 Am. Again played the role of postman. Received
