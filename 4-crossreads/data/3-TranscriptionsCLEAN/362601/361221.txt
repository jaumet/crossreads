  
 18 June, Wednesday 
 F. We go round in circles morning & evening. We get paid & I have plenty of feloos. I read & study all morning. Read sleep & fritter around all afternoon. We have Tea from cherries etc. comforts fund gift. I dream of sunshine mound 1 pram. M.I & B. Brian M. married waiting at Power House on the little boarded grating also home pony & trap & she comes to L meforit[?] likener the hat. 
 19 June, Thursday 
 F. Go to English lesson in morning, read & write all afternoon. We have a lecture on Morocco which is tres bob. Incident of Native Harem. Read all night. Go to bed early. 
 Dream of all washing on line in this boat. I help Auntie [indecipherable] M to make tea. Dream of Burwood also & T Clarke. 
 Wrote M. 5
