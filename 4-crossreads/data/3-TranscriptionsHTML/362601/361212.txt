<p>31 May, Saturday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Up at 7 am. Write letter go out to town to Post. If a woman says "Good job you've got no bloomy hair on your knees or they'd get burnt off". I am on guard over gangway. We sail 12.25 pass Goodwin Sands 8 Bells &amp; Dover at 9.30 pm. I play the piano in V.P. Deck &amp; on Upper deck. Eat large meal very hungry. See a scooter &amp; a muzzled dog.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L., Auntie Annie &amp; D.J's.</p>
<p>1 June, Sunday.</p>
<p>F.<br/>I work in Hospital all day. Go to bed early very tired. Read a little &amp; thought a good deal about my own. Wrote a few lines to Auntie &amp; Marie. We get caught in awful fog but the Pansy puts the Pilot aboard &amp; before tea we we lay off Plymouth Hoe.</p>
