<p>Friday 15 June 1917.</p>
<p>Went up on deck at&nbsp;<s>5 p</s>&nbsp;5 a.m. and on starboard side a lighthouse showing a powerful light was to be&nbsp;<s>bee</s>&nbsp;seen.</p>
<p>Guard on upper deck reported having seen during night 1 liner lit up and 2 gun boats cruising round Perrim. 4 lighthouses also seen. At 9 a.m. a tramp steamer crossed our bows and we then passed her on her port side.</p>
<p>Land sighted quite close. We could plainly see the &quot;12 apostles&quot;. on&nbsp;<s>starboard side. portside.</s>&nbsp;Looked at them through field glasses.</p>
<p>3 projections now on port side Passed very close. 2 boats on starboard side (horizon) seen at 6 pm. Land on same side seen at 7 pm &amp; 1 lighthouse. (7 sisters.)</p>
<p>Full dress parade at 2.30 pm today.</p>
<p>Another lighthouse seen at 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Peculiar red fish seen&nbsp;<s>today</s>&nbsp;also.</p>
