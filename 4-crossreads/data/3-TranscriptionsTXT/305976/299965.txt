 heavy storm Monday Jan 28 Still raining. Flat under water. Heavy going on horseback Have usual cold Tuesday Jan 29 Weather cleared up. Saw beautiful Air duel over camp to day. 2 Turkish planes attacked one of ours &amp; after a very exciting duel, one of the Turkish planes crashed &amp; the other had to fly for home. Wednesday Jan 30 Major Leggitt from corps inspected today. All satisfactory.
