  
 7/8/19 Durban long overcoat over their native robes. As far as the local native are concerned - the average wharf labouring man is a weed but the Zulu type (the rickshaw men and others) are fine well built chaps about 5'10" or 11" on the average lithe and agile. No wonder they are good fighters. Rarely one sees the wax head ring. The heads of hair of some Kaffirs are done with oil I think and made into many little curls or twists. Some carry big things in their ears. The women are fine hefty specimens. Stayed aboard until late afternoon & then went to afternoon tea at the Royal Hotel.
