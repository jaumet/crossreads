<p>(2)  On no account must the frog or sole be paired or interfeared with in any way.<br/>(3)  No rasping of walls is permitted, except a small nick to let in clenches.<br/>(4)  No more than 7 nails should be used, as too many nails weaken the wall;  one shoe seen contained eleven nails.<br/>(5)  Nails must not be driven too high, and should shew in a level line on the outside of the wall.<br/>(7)  Walls should be lowered sufficiently to allow frog to toutch the ground, except in deformed feet.<br/>(8)  Heels should not be left too long, as this causes contracted heels.  This is the kind of rot a man has to put up with, too many so called Farriers in the Army.  I may say this order is by Col. Matson, A.D.V.S.</p>
