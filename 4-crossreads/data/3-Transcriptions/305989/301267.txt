<p>Sunday 29 July 1917<br/>Stunt across Wadi got home about 3.30, watered up 6.30.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic number for 7].</p>
<p>Monday 30 July 1917<br/>Quiet day:  played 2nd Reg. got beaten:  very hot &amp; dusty.</p>
<p>Tuesday 31 July 1917<br/>Very hot;  on mess orderly.  Out grazing all day.</p>
<p>Wednesday 1 August 1917<br/>Out grazing all day.  On Fatigue.</p>
<p>Thursday 2 August 1917<br/>Out Grazing all day, pretty hot.</p>
<p>Friday 3 August 1917<br/>On Pump fatigue, washing parade in Wadi:  (picquet at night).  Reached the dignified age of 24 precisely, Clarrie home for R.G.</p>
<p>Saturday 4 August 1917<br/>Nothing doing, on Q.M. fatigue.</p>
