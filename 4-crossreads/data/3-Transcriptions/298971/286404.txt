<p>Fritz a lively time tonight.  A few more shells dropped in town principally at 11.30 square &amp; Houplines.  Each evening we visit some French friends and sit talking to them till 8.30.</p>
<p>27th Sept. Wednesday</p>
<p>Incinerator.  More shells in the evening.  Numbers killed &amp; wounded though none are hurt in the trenches.  1000 British drove 40,000 Germans out of Armentiers two years back.  Spies are pretty numerous.  German balloon broke loose &amp; 5 of our planes</p>
