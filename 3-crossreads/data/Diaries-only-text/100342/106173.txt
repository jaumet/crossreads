Jan. 5th Wednesday
B. Coy. for Duty. Self as Orderly Cpl. Management very bad regarding food issues much duplication of orders. Weather unsettled cold wind. Ship still anchored in harbour.
Jan. 6th Thursday
Ship weighed anchor 1 a.m. moved off 3 a.m. well out to sea at reveille with small war craft escorting us, later picked up large 4 masted transport was later escorted by 1 cruiser destroyer in addition. During afternoon picked up two more destroyers.
