Sunday 25
 Beautiful day. Like last Sunday only spent under different circumstances.
Monday 26
 Raining, snowing windy. In village of Buire met a Parisian girl who knew Brighton Hove well. There on holidays.
Wednesday 28
 Anniversary of our arrival in France. Celebrate it with a bottle of Haumont drink to "next year at home". Unsettled weather.
Friday 30
 Very unsettled weather. A strong rumour hath that "B" Section is to go to form part of a new ambulance in the new 6th Division but no confirmation.
Saturday 31
 The advance generally continues now the line must be 20 miles away.
