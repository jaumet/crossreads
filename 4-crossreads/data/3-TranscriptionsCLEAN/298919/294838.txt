After dinner again went  ashore, & had a tram ride to Norton Green a few miles out of the city, passing on the way Thornaby & Stockton the latter being rather a large sized town.  As rain was falling I did not alight from the tram but came back again.  After tea Watt & self went for a walk through the City & to the gardens.  Returned to ship about 8.30pm.  Saturday 30th[31st] July 1915.   Assisted Purser during morning  & afternoon went ashore after tea & to Confession at St Marys Cathedral  a very fine church &#188; of an hours walk from the Docks.
