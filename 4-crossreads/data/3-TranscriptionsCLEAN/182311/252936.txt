AUSTRALIAN  IMPERIAL  FORCE.
 
RECORD OF NON-MILITARY EMPLOYMENT.
 
 
Nature of Employment arranged -   Experience in English Bookselling and Publishing Methods.
 
Duration.   1 month from 17/3/19 to 17/4/19.
 
Pay.   Full Military Pay.
 
Subsistence allowance.   6/- per diem.
 
Extensions.   14 days from 18/4/1919 to 2/5/1919.
 
Whether course completed and date of completion.   Yes, on 2/5/19.
 
Report of College or Firm.  
Mr.John Clark,
12, Ludgate Square.
London.  E.C.4.
 
Extract from letter from above named firm dated 10/4/1919.
 
"Captain Peters came to my business to gain touch with
"the London end of the business.  (I am an Agent and
"Buyer for Melville & Mullens, his principals in Australia)
"to visit the trade houses and investigate the latest
"English methods in the trade and future tendencies and
"intentions.
 
"All this he has done and I am quite satisfied that his
"time has been fully and advantageously used."
 
(SIGNED)   W.H.SANDY,
Lieut-Col.
Department of Repatriation and Demobilisation, A.I.F.,
London.
 
4th September 1919.
