<p>1915<br/>Mar. 25 Thu.<br/>Rather busy to-day &ndash; Patients coming in in fair numbers &ndash; have received orders to take another 100 patients into Mena House which will bring our total number up to about 280 &ndash; The excitement &amp; bustle of getting ready to move has disappeared &amp; it looks as if we were to stay at Mena until Doomsday.<br/>Attended A.S.A. French class in Adelaide Y.M.C.A.</p>
