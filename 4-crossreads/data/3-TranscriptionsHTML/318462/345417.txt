<p>Wednesday 7/11/17<br/>Cold &amp; horrible snow not far away trying to rain now so snow not far away.<br/>Taking our steel Helmets &amp; stacking them, must be going to get our winter clothing &amp; time to. deferred Pay &pound;36- &ndash; 33/3 Wrote to Viv &amp; Nell, Jack &amp; Carol Posted them</p>
<p>Thursday 8/11/17<br/>frost last night, lovely morning, but not for long cloudy &amp; cold 11 a.m. like snow<br/>nothing much to write about Got my Boots soled for the winter, deferred Pay. &pound;36-1-6 &ndash; 37/9. Ought to get our sheepskin vests to day. On duty from 6 to 8.30 or 9 in the rain &amp; cold wind &amp; mud.</p>
<p>Friday 9/11/17<br/>off tomorrow about 5 miles getting nearer the line again lovely morning, sun shining, but clouds blowing up. deferred Pay &pound;36-3-0 &ndash; 42/3. our last night in Ooterstein fine but cold, on from 6 to 9. leaving at 9 for a 12 kilo Tramp.</p>
<p>Saturday 10/11/17<br/>Off this morning to Locre about 12 kilos away. through Bailleul Wet, &amp; heavy clouds looks like rain all day we are in for a nice march. Reville at 5.20. BKs 6.30 Blankets carried thank goodness. deferred Pay &pound;36-4-6 &ndash; 46/9<br/>got here Locre at 12. marched all the way in the Rain &amp; wind very cold at first but soon got warm splendid camp best I've been in yet Huts with fire place in it &amp; them &amp; fires.<br/>Going in tomorrow night. Raining like hell, Tea for dinner with what you brought with you Bread &amp; cheese, BKs consisted of 1 small Piece of Bacon &amp; Tea this is usual when you have a long march on. We are in Belgium again. letter from Viv. The guns are slamming away near here Ypres sector we are quite close to Dickey Bush.</p>
