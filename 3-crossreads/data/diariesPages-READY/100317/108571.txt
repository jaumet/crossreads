12/6/18
Morning sleep as usual was broken by the signing of intelligence summaries. At 9pm. Went down to BnHQ. It was a lovely fresh morning. At 10 p.m. the General B.M and the other C.O.s met at my mess and conferred. He was not long in broaching the subject. We are to do another operation and the necessary parties are to be found from the 21st. hurriedly drew up plans and let them have them. Got into touch with the Artillery and T.M.s and gave Reed as much help as possible with the preparations. Busy on this and interviewing people until late in the afternoon. In the evening Major Sheeney USA Army from Illinois reported to be attached to us and see the line. To sleep at .
