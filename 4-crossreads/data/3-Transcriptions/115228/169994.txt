<p>Front &amp; we are well equipped with artillery, &amp; the air service has wonderfully improved recently, we having a number of the latest fighting planes added to a large number of the older machines which are chiefly used for reconnaissance &amp; bombing.</p>
<p><u>Oct 26th:</u>&nbsp; Brigade moved out of camp at 4..30 p.m. and travelled a further 13 miles to Esani where we arrived in camp at 11 p.m.</p>
