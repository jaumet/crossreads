Wednesday 19 June
To-night a German aviator attempted to bring two of our balloons down but in so trying he met three of our ?planes and was brought down in flames. Still fine weather.
Thursday 20 June
A long walk to-day through Hazebrouck to Sercus, the first walk into civilization I have been for months. It is quite good to be able to talk to several very nice girls again.
Friday 21 June
Another walk to Sercus through Wallon Cappel, to see dentist who did a filling for me.
