<p>28 June 1918 Friday<br/>Sending this along, don't worry about us we are all O.K. only very hot.<br/>We are relieved from Trenches tomorrow night-   I am applying for leave 7 days in August <strike>to be with Dolly.</strike>  Am not building much on getting leave to Australia; but influence does a lot in promotions, decoration &amp; leave altho' Kings Reg's to the contrary.<br/>Love to all Your son  FM</p>
