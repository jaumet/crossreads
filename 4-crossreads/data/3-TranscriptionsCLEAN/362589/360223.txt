
Jack Massie is to be here man - The Bartons are returning to NSW in a few days. Robert will get [indecipherable] leave and sick leave - Then he exchanges into an English Corps - gets a commission, and returns to the front in about 3 months I suppose - Arthur was next on the list for a commission in Gallipoli but of course Archies death and his marriage made us wish to get him here for 3 months breathing space. Robert says little but he has us having had some extraordinary escapes, notably a piece of shrapnel striking his chest and flattening out all the bullets of his bandolier - and so merely knocking him down. M White troops - of those who started out as troopers only he and two others remain the rest are dead or invalided! -My plans are still uncertain - The First have really gone out of their way to
