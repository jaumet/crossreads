23 Still cloudy & unsettled  Understand the figures on the Chart are faked by the 2nd Officer, & that we will reach Capetown on Sunday or Monday instead of Wed! Beginning to get a jolly good appetite, & the weather is perceptibly cooler. After sundown it is interesting to lean over the rail & watch for the flash of fins as the fish (& sometimes sharks) come to the surface. Evidently they are attracted by the light.
