<p>Steenvorde<br/>Monday 5<br/>Canadians went over this morning and met with success casualtys very light<br/>Tuesday 6<br/>A ride for life through a wall of fire on Westhoek Ridge, thank the Lord we came through unscathed<br/>Wednesday 7<br/>Batt releived today resting until morn at the "Half Way House" on the Menin Road<br/>Thursday 8<br/>On the move, but it is out of the fray so we are happy. Arrived at a farm near Steenvoorde at dusk</p>
