So far the weather is nice &amp; mild bar for the rain, but we have struck no cold as yet &amp; I don't think we will before November after that I'll bet she's a freezer.<br/>Since we have been in here we have received a fair lot of reinforcements, so that now the Coy is fairly strong again which is much better, for it lightens the work all round.
