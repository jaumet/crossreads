<p>Mesopotamia</p>
<p>Operations against the Turks in Mesopotamia began on Nov 1914 when Basra was occupied by a force under General Sir A.A. Barrett. On September 28th 1915, a force under the command of General Townshend, which had been pushing forward up to the Tigris, defeated the Turks before Kut-el-Amara. Two months later General Townshend won the battle of Ctesiphon, but was compelled to retire.</p>
