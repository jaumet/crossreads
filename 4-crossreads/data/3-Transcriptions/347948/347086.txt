<p>To-night a concert was held in the after well deck. After a song was sung and a request made to the men to let the Frenchies yonder hear them in the chorus. One of the men rejoined "We can't do it on the tucker, Sir"</p>
<p>30th  The French pilot says that the rumor of the sinking of the Australian transport is untrue  But states that the French have captured from the Germans:-<br/>192,000 men<br/>4,000 officers<br/>284 Heavy guns</p>
