 20th Jan (Thursday) Stretcher carrying over long distances across the sand. Trial trip over the new railway branch line to Serapeum. An engine pushing one carriage. O.C. & others went to Ismailia to select our new camping ground there. 500 camels arrived with Indian transport from Ismailia. 
 21st Jan (Friday) On guard. Camels crossed Canal.
