
3 - France where she has been since October - just as sweet as ever.I'll write more later.Nurse Cumming came to see me with one of Lady [indecipherable] children yesterday - wonderful - I thought she was at Arras - bombardment. She & Phyllis & Nurse Tully go to Alexandria 30th August - I aim to write to them if we want them Lemnos. Two men a surgeon & an engineer from the Grantala, called one just going to France in firing line. The other into the navy - Dr Thomas of Manly - nice lad - resident at London Hospital - military 1st Lieut - came - I introduced to J & R - saw Grandmother last night. So sweet & hopeful - will write much later. Clapin Greatrix was with me yesterday also Mrs S. Skirving & Stephanie.Devotedly your daughter Betha
