<p>15/10/17<br/>We were pulled out of bed at 1.30 this morning and paraded down to the reception tent for England. Here driven in motor lorries to station at Camiers and then put into hos. train.</p>
<p>Left Camiers at 3.30 this morning and arrived at Calias at 0745 there we embarked on hospital boat.</p>
