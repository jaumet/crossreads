<p>to last for 50 days with perhaps a break of a few days during the period. <br/>Cooks fatigue for the day.  Engagement reported between Turks &amp; Squadron of 9th Aust Light Horse a few miles out from outpost at 'Habuta', where I was camping at Christmas.  Ten Turkish casualties &amp; 30 prisoners taken, our casualties very slight.</p>
<p>Monday 17/4/16<br/>Caught train to Ferry post where I spent most of the morning in the Canal; although very warm, had rather a good day.  Five of the eight ambulance waggons returned to Ordnance this morning.  Expect to be equipped</p>
