  
 1916 September 6 Wednesday 
 Have job of help put up meat room in cook house - Mitchell & self drift over to Y M for cup tea & biscuit at 11 a.m. strenuous work, needs nourishment. Great & pleasant surprise. Bob arrives having had his transfer fixed up at last - Johnston leaves for England to see wife. Fysh sick. Bronchitis. Bob, Cart & go for walk. visit cemetery. then hop pickers. Talk to Belgian girl. Daughter of farm.  help pick hops . Y M for supper.
