<p>Thursday 15&nbsp;April 1915<br />Attended to JBN. 10 a.m.&nbsp;<br />Report to O.C. re officers&nbsp;<br />Riding on plateau between 7 &amp; 8 a.m.&nbsp;<br />Also between 5 &amp; 6-30 p.m.&nbsp;<br />Reading, writing, &amp; sleeping the remainder of the day.&nbsp;<br />Several copies of the D. Telegraph recd from Doffie Williams</p>
<p>Friday 16&nbsp;April 1915<br />Riding morning &amp; afternoon&nbsp;<br />Not out during the day otherwise.</p>
<p>Saturday 17&nbsp;April 1915<br />Hospital. Reading and writing all day.&nbsp;<br />Went to the pyramid of Aborawash [Pyramid of Abu Rowash]&nbsp;this evening, about 7 miles to the Westward along the dessert.</p>
<p>Sunday 18&nbsp;April 1915<br />Mass at 7-30 a.m.&nbsp;<br />Reading &amp; writing all day&nbsp;<br />Ride between 5 &amp; 6-30 p.m.&nbsp;<br />Concert in hall between 7-30 &amp; 9 p.m.</p>
