 23 Nov 16 Returned to Rosyth &amp; coaled ship. 
 24 Nov 16 ) 25 Nov 16 )  Anchored at Rosyth 26 Nov 16 ) 
 27 Nov 16 Our Rugby football team played the H.M.S. Yarmouth. Our team won. 
 28 Nov 16 )  Anchored at Rosyth. 29 Nov 16 )  Admiral Beatty has taken over the sole command of Fleet &amp; Admiral Jellicoe has gone to the Admiralty. 
 30 Nov 16 Saw two British Airships. Proceeded to sea at 1 pm. At 7 pm we were recalled &amp; anchored at Rosyth again at midnight. 
 1 Dec 16 Coaled ship at 2 am in morning watch.
