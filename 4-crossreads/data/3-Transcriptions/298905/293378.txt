<p>think of that, at last we reached the railway line, &amp; laid down while our boys pasted the second line with shells, we had not long to wait, but it seemed hour's to us laying out in the open waiting for the fire to lift, at last the fuses were lengthened, &amp; like a pack of hounds the boys rushed the trench our objective, but there was very little in it &amp; it was ours in a few minutes, at this period over came the 3rd Batt. closely followed by the 9th 11th 12th. their objective was in front of us again, &amp; scores of our Battalion went on with them to the wood which was just in front, we started straight away to consolidate our position so as to be ready for the counterattack which usually follows the charge, by daylight, we had a fairly good line dug out &amp; organised &amp; were ready for them to try their luck against the scum as they had sneeringly referred to us in their papers.</p>
