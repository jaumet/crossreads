<p>1916 May 21<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Up betimes after good nights rest.  Scratch breakfast. fatigue work. Go across flats with Bob &amp; Tony to bathe. Rapid tide.<br/>Open air church parade. Scotch minister glengarry cap. fine sermon straight from shoulder.  <br/>Wrote my 6th home letter. also several postcards to friends.  Twilight till 9 p.m.</p>
