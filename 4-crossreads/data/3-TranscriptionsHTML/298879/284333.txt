<p>February 1915<br/>Friday 19<br/>Pen very bad. Damned nuisance. Bayonet drill. Straightening up lines. No leave granted today. Mounted Guard at 4.30 P.M.</p>
<p>Saturday 20<br/>Hot day<br/>Dismounted guard at 5P.M. Very glad to get off. Nearly all our chaps got to the camp picture shows.</p>
