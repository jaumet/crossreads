Before he left the trench however our sentries had caught sight of him with the result that under the Machine Gun & rifle fire concentrated on him that the attack broke  &  the remnants dodged from crater to crater to safety. At about 10 P.M. we were relieved by the 12th Bn &  and went out for a nights sleep to the sunken road near Porciul. There we remained until the night of Sunday 7th when we relieved the 3rd Bn on the outskirts of Bullecourt.
