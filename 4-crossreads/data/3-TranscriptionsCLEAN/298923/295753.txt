  
 28 March 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out) Visited a Monastery called Kremezan away on the Hills for Austrian orphans, winery attached (there always is plenty of grog in these Monasteries)   Took Capt. McLean's gun & had a shot at some partridge but only got 1 they are shy & fast flyers. X In the evening paid a visit to another monastery supported by the Argentine people  & called  & close to King Solomons Pools- These places are mostly deserted now, but the pools are a wonderful bit of engineering, the Pipe line being made of stones to Jerusalem. X
