
<p>134</p>
<p>SEPT. &nbsp; THURSDAY 21 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; fairly warm for this place. Out with the signallers all day, but did not do much. We had a very hard football match in afternoon. Probables V Possibles for the brigade team and the Possibles one 8-4 after a hard game. I am that stiff tonight that I creak every time I move.</p>
