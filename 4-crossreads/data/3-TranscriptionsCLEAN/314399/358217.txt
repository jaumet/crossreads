
Friday 8 August 1919Chry's & Madge's birthday
Been thinking a lot about the girls today. Hope they had a pleasant time. Very dull today. Felt like sleeping all day. Played quoits & cards. The diggers had a concert in the evening. It was very poor tho'.
Saturday 9 August 1919Very warm indeed today. Coy. Orderly Officer today. Had to fill in a lot of forms about deferred pay. Got inoculated against flue. Saw quite a lot of dolphins & showers today. Also on ship.  Another cinema show tonight it was quite good. Am one of three officers on guard tomorrow.
