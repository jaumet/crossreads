 8th May They did bomb us last night &amp; made a very protracted affair of it too, dividing their attention between us &amp; Khan Yunis &amp; coming over first at about 9 p.m. &amp; then about 2 or 3 a.m.  Now we lie in bed in the spirit of Kismet &amp; hope that they won't lob one too near. 
 9th May Mist last night &amp; the taubes did the same.  Oooooh. 
 Deir el Bellah 15th May Nothing at all has been doing of late.  Guns are heard of course at all hours of the day &amp; night but lately they have slackened off a great deal. The Taubes have not been over again. We call our home here "Avilion".  Certainly it lacks the three Queens with crowns of gold &amp; also fortunately we are not here for the specific purpose of healing a grievous wound.  The "Bowery hollows crowned with summer sea" [Morte d'Arthur]
