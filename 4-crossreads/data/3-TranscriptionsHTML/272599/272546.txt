<p>1917 Thursday 25 October</p>
<p>Tea in bed. Breakfast. Hyde Park.. pick up Guide. Rotton Row, Serpentine Pall Mall, Piccadilly, Colonial Exhibition, Indian Royal presents &ndash; superb. Hampden Court, Putney Fulham. Wimbleton Common Ascot. Kingston, Coronation Stone. Richmond. Kew Gardens, Shaftsburry tea. <br/>Opera Kovantchina at Drury Lane in evening. Russian Opera. Heavy. Get lost going home no unusual occurrence.</p>
