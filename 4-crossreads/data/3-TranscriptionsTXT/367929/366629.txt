May 191527 Thursday same as yesterday.Working parties. Swim2 wounded today. Stood to arms from 4.30 pm to 2.30 am as Turks massing at left head of Monash Gully.
28 FriSame. Road to beach very dangerous from machine gun fire and bullets.About 40 men hit by Turks marksmen while drawing stores from supply depot, before 10am.Weather warmI man of ours wounded.
