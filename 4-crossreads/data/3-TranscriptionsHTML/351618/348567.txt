<p>Saturday 10th July<br/>My knee was right again to-day &amp; at about 5PM I went down to the beach and had a dip. It was lovely I gathered a few pods of yellow Brown flowers.</p>
<p>If any one ever suggests Bully Beef &amp; Biscuits to me or stew when I get back it will be as much as their life is worth. I have tummy aches every day etc. Of course it might be the water that was responsible for it.</p>
<p>We fired a few rounds in the afternoon to register the distance of certain targets. One of our Cruisers sent in a fusilade of shells on to where the Turks are putting guns. The snipers got a few</p>
