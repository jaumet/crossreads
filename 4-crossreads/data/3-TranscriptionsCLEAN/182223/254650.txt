  
 Thursday 1 April 1915 Ride before breakfast.  About the hospital all day. Writing, reading, & sleeping.  Riding between 6 & 7 p.m.  Sent a cable to the girls at 8 p.m. Nash, Sydney. Happy Easter. Well. Love  Cost 28.5 Milliemes 
 Friday 2 April 1915 [Good Friday] Not riding this morning.  O.C. away during most of the day.   1  st  Brigade & other troops under orders to move out tomorrow.  Reading and writing all day 
 Saturday 3 April 1915 About hospital most of the day  After dinner went with Dr Purdy to Cairo. At Continental & Sheppheards Hotels. Left Cairo by 11-45 pm tram.  1 st  Brigade Inf left camp & Cairo. Col MacLaurin, Col. Braund, Captain Maguire & others. 
 Sunday 4 April 1915 [Easter Day] Wrote Holman, Flowers, Paton, Mrs Thompson (Cooma), Fallon, Father Perrin, Pat. Watt., The Girls.  2 nd  Brigade of Infty left Camp & Cairo Ride during morning & afternoon
