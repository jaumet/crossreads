  
 Sunday 29 October 1916 Church parade in morning.  Got leave and went up to Port Said.  Mr. Trelawny not back yet. Went over French man-of-war.  Saw English & French sub-marines.  Lot of English people in Port Said on account of P & O boat being there on its way to Australia. 
 Monday 30 October 1916 Day Stable man. Wrote some letters.
