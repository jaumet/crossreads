 Friday 3 August 1917. 
 Reveille 4 a.m. Saddle up camels &amp; set&nbsp;off. A round-about kind of a march it was. Country in places limestone hills. These hills were very steep &amp; camels advanced in single file till we reached the top. 
 (Breakfast 9 am.) 
 We passed beside the old city of Cairo &amp; near &quot;The Citadel&quot;. 
 Leave from 4 pm till 9.30 p.m. 
 Got wristlet watch from jeweller 1st repair. 
 Saw a stylish French funeral in Cairo and snapped it.
