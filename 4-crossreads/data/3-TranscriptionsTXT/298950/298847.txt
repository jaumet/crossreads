
to Commandant R.S. Sands previous to the structures wher sold build by the Military Authoritys.  This sume covers the material & expencess to build same.  They all wher build by Internees labor consisting of 3 structures (Wood & Iron) 2 Structures for four Restaurants & the remaing one for the other Shops.  These shops each wher 20 feet by 20 feet.  They all wher private & well paying concerns.  The Camp Musseum (Shop) bought & sold every thing.  Yes it also wher the Camp Pawnshop, wher a Inmates could recieve ready Cashe on articles.  The charge of interest being 3 pence per week on each Sovereign lend.  However when the new Comandant toke charge, same permitted more shops to be build entirely by Internees themselves.  So their got build another 1/2 dozend Restaurants & Caffees 2 Fruishops, another Sausage shop.  One Ham & Beef shop 1 Cigarette & tobacco shop, 1 Milk shop, 1 Clothing Shop & 2 fried fishe shops, also 2 Launderys.  This proved a boon in Camp as it proved opposition & reduced former prices.  All goods for these shops had to be ordered through the Canteen & a 5 per cent increase on City prices wher charged.
