<p>Tues May 7th:-<br/>Got some shelling today &amp; "Jerry" managed to lobbed a howitzer shell through the roof of our farmhouse &amp; blew the big kitchen right out. Everyone just out in time.</p>
<p>Wed 8th May:-<br/>Were to be relieved tonight but it is put off for 24 hours. Passfield &amp; Fitzgerald left for Corp Signal school.</p>
<p>th May 9th:-<br/>Relieved tonight by the West Yorks ( Leeds Rgt.)<br/>Got away the last of the Bn. With Lt. Leitch.</p>
<p>Fri May 10th:-<br/>Walked through Fletre at 3 A.M. &amp; on to Caestre where we picked up the Bn. in motor buses &amp; rode to a camp near Sercus; not far from Wallon-Cappell.</p>
<p>Sun May 12th:-<br/>Camp bombed tonight by 'planes. We were out in shelters.</p>
<p>Tues May 14th:-<br/>Thorpe our Hq detail cook was killed tonight by a bomb.</p>
<p>Wed May 15th:-<br/>Left BN. &amp; reported to Bde &amp; went with other N.C.O.'s by motor to 4th Squadron R.A.F.</p>
