<p>April 1917<br/>Sunday 15<br/>Moved out to night from our old camp. camped for night</p>
<p>Monday 16<br/>at 2 pm 3rd Bgde moved out to take up outpost line prior to the big push which we are to hop into tomorrow at daybreak.<br/>Saw cousin Bob Fell out here  He is with 4th Signal troop 4th Brigade &ndash; (in our division)<br/>Moved out for the big fight at 7pm. Reinforcements arrived in darkness.</p>
