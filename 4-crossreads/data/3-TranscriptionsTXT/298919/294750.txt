With the sinking of the Sun a cool Southerly breeze arose.  Thursday 20th May 1915.   This morning the Sea is even calmer than yesterday but the scene presented is not so beautiful, there being no islands to improve the bareness of the limitless ocean.  Colonel Armstrong our passenger has been travelling about the Cape Hellis position for a couple of days.
