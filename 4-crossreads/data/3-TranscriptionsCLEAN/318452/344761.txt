 Thursday 27 We are still here. This morning the Col & I rode into Steinvoorde we passed the 3rd Division & the N. Zealander going up. Tonight, the batt. went to Steinvoorde to a Concert. We took the hall & the Kookaburras put on a very fine show. The weather continues fine with clear moonlight nights. 
 Friday 28 A beautiful day again with a sharp tinge in the atmosphere
