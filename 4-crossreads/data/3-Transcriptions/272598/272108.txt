<p>Thursday 28.9.16<br/>Fritz commenced to bombard about 4pm &amp; landed a 15 centimetre high explosive shell in a workshop occupied ay the time by the Fifth Divisional Pioneers, about 70 or 80 yards from us.  Six were killed &amp; 8 or 9 others wounded.  Total casualties in the town for the afternoon were 10 killed &amp; 25 wounded.
