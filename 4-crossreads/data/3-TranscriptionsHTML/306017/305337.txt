<p>May 1916<br/>we sent a few parties out, have not been able to locate it.  Heard later that the Turks had captured the aeroplane.</p>
<p>Sunday 7th<br/>We left Romani at 1.30 pm &amp; marched to Qatia both the 6th &amp; 7th  Regts camel train ambulance.  We are going to the Turkish camp at Bir al abd to make a reconnaissance in force. After watering our horses &amp; attending to our minor wants we left Qatia at 8 pm &amp; arrived at Oghratina at 11 pm.  The moon was only a few days old.  We fed up again &amp; snatched 2 or 3 hrs sleep, got our water bottles full &amp; at 4 oclock our advanced guard got away &amp; we followed at 4.30 am.  The riding was slow as the country was mostly drift sand.  We reached Bir-al-abd to find the last of the Turks clearing off in the distance.  They left nothing &amp; we didn't even get a shot at them.  Our aeroplane followed but I didn't hear how far they went.
