<p>continued his shelling of last week : Aust'n mail still absent so Fritz must have got it</p>
<p>Saturday August 10th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Nothing doing : went to pictures with Gil &amp; they were pretty good : couple of letters</p>
<p>Sunday Aug 11th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Knocked off at 11.30 &amp; laid down in afternoon : went to service in the Church Army rooms after tea</p>
<p>Monday Aug 12th to Thursday 15th</p>
<p>Nothing much doing : air raids at night &amp; very heavy barrage put up also down Calais way :</p>
<p>Friday August 16th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Still at Poste H &amp; it is an easy cop</p>
<p>Saturday Aug 17th to Tuesday Aug 20th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Nothing much doing : a couple of air raids</p>
<p>Wednesday Aug 21st : Mardyk</p>
<p>Pictures at night &amp; they were very good indeed :</p>
<p>Thursday Aug 22nd Friday Aug 23rd</p>
<p>Pictures on Friday night &amp; good : French orchestra in attendance :</p>
<p>Saturday Aug 24th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Bergues sports on so we went up : had a good day : good programme &amp; well organised : all hands got a sandwich cake &amp; cup of tea at 4 pm : enjoyable day &amp; home about 8 pm : raid at night : old Fritz has been shelling a bit during the week :</p>
<p>Sunday Aug 25th : Mardyk</p>
<p>Told that I am to go on Paris leave tomorrow :</p>
