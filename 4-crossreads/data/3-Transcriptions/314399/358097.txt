<p>Thursday 10 April 1919<br/>Orderly officer today. Did very little. Rained all day. Bill got word of going to Blighty for non military employment.</p>
<p>Friday 11 April 1919<br/>Pass came thro &amp; Bill managed to get a duty pass for Paris. Rushed around a bit &amp; we managed to catch the 5 pm train. Got to Paris &amp; we went to the Aussie Y.M. &amp; got a room (Hotel [indecipherable]). Took Bill &amp; Sgt. Kerr to the Boulevard for a walk &amp; showed them a few things.</p>
