<p>here now the the Australians are going back to Egypt or Palestine.  I wish it would come true, we would see a bit more of the world then &amp; the war is much quieter over in that part.  When you send me parcels &amp; want to know what to put in, a little pepper, sugar &amp; small tin of cocoa would always come in handy.</p>
