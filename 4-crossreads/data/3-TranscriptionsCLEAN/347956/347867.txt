 drove us up the street again free of charge & we came home on the 10.30 train 
 Mon 25th Stan was paraded before the O.C. this morning & has to help feed up the horses seven nights running at 9 o'clock, for being in the canteen on Saturday night & not getting out lively enough when the officer came along & said it was time the canteen was closed;  The Squadron are out for the day; we were out with the lamps after tea group work. went to pictures afterwards 
 Tue 26th Same as usual morning, but the pump at the Nile got out of order yesterday & had to take the horses to a canal
