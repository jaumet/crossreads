Sunday 7th [Feb]Cool cloudy day.  Finished stables early in the morning.  Church parade at 8.40am.  Presbyterians had service in recreation tent.  Got leave from 2 oclock till 11.15pm & went with McCarthy to Mena where the main Australian camp is situated.  It is 10 miles from Cairo by train & takes about an hour to get there.  We took a carriage & pair from Bab-el-Louk Ry Station to the Opera Square.  Cost us 5 piastres (1/-1/2).  Got the tram from the Square (21/2 d) to Pyramids.
