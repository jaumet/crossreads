<p>Friday July 27th</p>
<p>Bapaume : hot day : reveille 5 am : boys went to Ligny on a bombing "stunt" : home at dinner time : packed up &amp; moved off at 4 pm : solid march &amp; very hot to Beaucourt : off packs &amp; went into the Ancre for a swim : tres bien : this place has seen plenty of "stoush" by the look of it : got one of a ruined chateau : bivouaked but the "skeetas" were very vicious :</p>
<p>Saturday July 28th</p>
<p>Beaucourt : hot : the boys on loading work : we S.B.s are on piquet work : the rest of the Bgde came in during the day &amp; had had a rough time especially</p>
