 2 p.m. News (authentic ?) has just come that the Turks are evacuating along the road to Jaffa.  The guns certainly sound a good deal farther off.  And the monitors have gone up the coast. We shall probably be moving up tonight. 
 7 p.m., 20th April We were called out last evening about 5 p.m. and it was a chapter of petty but annoying accidents.
