<p>21 October 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Cold &amp; stiff we disentrain at Longpre in  Somme area. Scratch  breakfast at cafe coffee &amp; biscuits. While frost &amp; icicles on railway carriage  Take up our march at 10 a.m. &amp; walk 4 miles thro' villages &ndash; Tolim L'etoile &amp; Bouchon at which latter we billet. &ndash; a dirty poor hole it is. nothing to buy &amp; little grub. Wander round to get warm. &amp; pinch wood. buy straw &amp; eat green apples.  Make fire in old fire-place &amp; warm ourselves as best we can.</p>
