<p>Sunday 29 July 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille 7 a.m. Planes kept us awake from 4 a.m. Still as we were tired we appreciated the extra rest.</p>
<p>Egyptian troops drilling in our <s>troops</s>&nbsp;camp. Muster parade &amp; lecture by Lt Colonel. 25% got leave.</p>
<p>Harry Flavell &amp; I went to 14 AGH. Saw Ted Holden (Sgt), Tom de Gruchy, Chas Hirsefield, Jim Tatterson. Heard that [blank] McKinnon has been mentioned in despatches.</p>
