There saw a memorial stone in honour of a benefactor of the town who had lived about 1628-1692.16/5/18 Started in Officers Mess again. After tea walked to Aviation Camp near St Omer & saw several aeroplanes starting their flight. Very warm &^ fine. Fritz planes over last 4 or 5 nights. 19/5/18 Blendecques & surroundings very heavily bombed by Fritz planes last night. He caught an ammunition dump near Arques & cause big blaze.
