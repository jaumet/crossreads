  
 Octobre 19 Working on drainage again between West Hoek & Anzac Ridge. Shelling very heavy. Reported some of Infty party killed & wounded. Lft Fullerton 54th Batt attached to our Coy killed. Sap's Ross & Bernard killed. Cpl Furnell wounded. Reported heavy enemy shelling on three Comps Front. 
 Octobre 20 Working on Rd. ditching again today. Fairly heavy shelling again. Some of our Infty party wounded. Nasty weather.
