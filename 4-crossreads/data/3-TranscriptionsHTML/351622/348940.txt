<p>Saw the famous Mametz Wood</p>
<p>20 December 1916 Wed<br/>Went to Fricourt. Saw and Got inside the famous tanks H.M.S.S. Duchess &amp; Dinghy. Fritz all over the place buried</p>
<p>21 December 1916 Thur<br/>Stayed in bed all day &amp; slept till 6 pm. Expecting to go on leave any day.</p>
