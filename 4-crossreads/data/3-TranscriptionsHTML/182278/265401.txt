<p>Friday 1/3/18. Snowing &amp; raining. Bn marched to Rifle Range but unable to shoot (10 miles).</p>
<p>Saturday 2/3/18&nbsp; Snowing &amp; raining&nbsp; had a game of bridge&nbsp; wrote to Doff.</p>
<p>Sunday 3/3/18. Church parade, went for a long ride, very cold weather.</p>
<p>Monday 4/3/18. Battalion to Rifle Range at Escoulles [Escalles?] - very good shooting under bad weather conditions</p>
<p>Tuesday 5/3/18. More Shooting- Bn very good indeed, had a game of bridge.</p>
