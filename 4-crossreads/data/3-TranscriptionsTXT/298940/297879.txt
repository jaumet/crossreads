  Tuesday 12th March  Over a week has passed since I wrote in this book, &amp; a great deal has happened. I am writing this on the grass outside my [indecipherable] ward at No 83 General Hospital Wimmmeriux, (Bologne), on a beautiful warm afternoon. The night after the last entry  was  (Tuesday) was very quiet, but the following day was busy from an artillery standpoint. No cases occurred during the day.
