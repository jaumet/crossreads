 Monday 21st. Dec 1914. NCOs marched up &  down the hill all the morning. A route march in the afternoon & I got a blister on my heel. Wrote 3 letters & went to bed early. 
 Tuesday 22nd. Dec. Cold this morning. Went for marching both in the morning & afternoon went to city in the evening. 
 Wednesday 23rd Dec 1914 Cold again. Big marching order parade all day. Went all over the desert left at 9.30 & got back at 4.15. Went out to Sheeshara tonite. Some real lovely granite there.
