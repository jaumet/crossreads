<p>for we tackled all sorts, it did not matter about them being green, I guess we will have a funny feeling in our tucker bag shortly.<br/>I dished our platoons rations out this morning instead of the sergeant.<br/>As we were coming down last night a great fire started in the German lines &amp; lit the whole sky up for miles around, it was either a big billet or an ammunition store that was alight I suppose our gun's got on to it<br/>A big mail came in this morning but I never got a thing out of it.<br/>5th. Nothing worth writing about has taken place since my last entry except a heavy fall of rain yesterday evening &amp; the sky is still dark &amp; gloomy looking the old farmer here has lots of green hay out &amp; he is having some</p>
