<p>18<br/>Ah, there are many like this, perhaps a dozen, all hurt in the same way with variations</p>
<p>Then there are lumbering carriages of great guns, battered and bent and perforated, with every part of their wonderful construction thrown out of the true and hopeless of adjustment</p>
<p>Overmastering force of terrific explosions and lightening swift shards of powerful sells have cut inches deep into solid metal, and broken asunder great plates and heavy assembled parts; as though all this high wrought wonderful work of mans ingenious mind and cunning hand, had no more strength than the fragile toys of children.</p>
