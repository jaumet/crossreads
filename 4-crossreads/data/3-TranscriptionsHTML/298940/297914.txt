<p>2p.m.<br/>More Achie firing, so Fritz having another spy out over the landscape.<br/>The papers last night told  us that what had been rumoured for a day &amp; that was that the much talked about German offensive had at last started, and on a grand scale about a 50 mile front. The "Mail" goes so far as to say that the deciding battle of the war has been started, &amp; to judge by the German papers it seems probable.<br/>What horrors will <strike>not</strike> have been experienced before</p>
