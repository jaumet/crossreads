<p>1-1-17<br/>Mon 1st<br/>Small Pox has broken out in El Arish &amp; the villages are all "out of bounds" to all ranks; The sun came out bright this morning for a change but it did not last long as the clouds banked up after dinner &amp; a very cold wind blowing &amp; rain tonight;  A couple of "Scotty" Officers paid the Regt a visit today to see some of our Officers &amp; they say the 42nd Division is leaving El Arish tonight for Rafa, we hope they have a pleasant trip but are not anxious to accompany them;  A rum issue was given out today the first since we left Gallipoli; Put in today writing letters; A/Sgt Hill who has relieved me of a good deal of work, is being sent to A Sqdn tomorrow as L/Cpl</p>
