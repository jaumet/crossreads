<p>It is about half a mile from our quarters. I paid the wreck a visit a few days ago and am enclosing portion of the colours which I cut off the wing. All the covering of the 'plane is made of the same material.<br/>I received a letter from Stirling yesterday in reply to one of mine. He is well and wishes to be remembered to all ast home.<br/>Some of the Portuguese, who are working somewhere at the back of the front wondered round the trenches looking for souvenirs yesterday. I was talking to one of them and secured his everlasting gratitude by the gift of a Fritz bullet. There are hundreds of them lying about the ridge but it was a real novelty to him.
