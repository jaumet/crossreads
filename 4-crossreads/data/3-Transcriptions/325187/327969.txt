<p>[Monday 14 August 1916]</p>
<p>Had a Tube stuck in my side today like [indecipherable] a cow I know why they bellow now-painful but its now over<br/>The Ward is full of flowers today, brought by the Women of Cairo yesterday- to complete the Midsummer's Night Dream "Blossom" runs about in the shape of a wee pet Donkey, the orderlies are rearing on Milk.<br/>Had the stitches taken out of my Chest today high up under the right arm, &amp; Im feeling A1, being a healthy beast in good condition can recuperate easily.</p>
