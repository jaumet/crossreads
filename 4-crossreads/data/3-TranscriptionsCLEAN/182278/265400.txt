  
 Saturday 23/2/18  Meeting of CO's at Bde.HQrs. Robinson went on leave- went for a ride 
 Sunday 24/2/18. Church parade, went for a long ride with Lloyd 
 Monday 25/2/18. Went to Boulogne to play football- match off- returned at 9.45 p.m. 
 Tuesday 26/2/18. Route March 15 miles walked all the way. wrote to Doff. 
 Wednesday 27/2/18  Div Boxing Competitions  stadium in this area  I judged. 
 Thursday 28/2/18  Final of boxing- 26 th  won by two firsts & one second  rained in afternoon.
