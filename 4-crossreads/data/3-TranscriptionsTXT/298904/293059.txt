After we had a swim we were marched off up the canal a bit to where our engineers had constructed a pontoon bridge across the water it used to be open for a certain number of hours daily to allow the steamers through, the bridge was worked by ropes from either side &amp; it would carry a good big load, it had to be strong for practically all our food &amp; stores had to come across this slender thread &amp; it was the only way of getting troops backwards &amp; forward's
