
4th August
Pte. Guilliaume Casimir W. has come to me as my batman.
Bill, as he is called, is a Frenchman with a varied and extremely interesting career.  Born at ... in 1862 he was educated at school and given every opportunity to study by a very wealthy and indulgent parent of whom he still cherishes affectionate memories.  But Bill early showed a roving disposition.  When quite a young man he entered the French Army where he soon rose to Commissioned Rank.  He was of course too young to take part in the war of
