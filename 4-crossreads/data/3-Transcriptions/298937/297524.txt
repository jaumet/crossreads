<p>In the evening Jack came to see me in the Dispensary &amp; he &amp; his friend with Graysmarsh &amp; I, adjourned to the canteen, where to my amazement I disposed of three shandies.</p>
<p>Monday 31st January</p>
<p>Weather very much better to-day.  Cold nights still.<br/>Prisoner brought in this afternoon from the Citadel, who had been a jeweller in town.<br/>Arrested &amp; sent to Citadel where he fell sick.  Now in our hospital.<br/>Given Turkish rations today of olives &amp; meat cake which he would not eat (of course).<br/>Poor fellow is very thin &amp; looks ill &amp; being hours without food</p>
