  
 Wrote to Auntie Effie & Minnie Luscombe. 24/6/18 Recd 2 parcels - one containing 1 pr Brown Sox, & 1 ctn Griffiths Chocolate, & other Cake, Biscuits, Sardines, Strawberry Conserve, Pineapple, Tomato Soup, Chocolate & Milk & Butterscotch. 25/6/18 Receiving wounded today who were in a stunt near Meteren during which our people advanced. Wrote to Muriel. Raining today. Wrote to Dick Luscombe. 27/6/18 Recd parcel from Mother contng [Indecipherable] Caps (From Minnie Lusc[ombe]) Insecticide Butterscotch, Soap, Hdfs, Boot Laces, Writing Pad & Tooth Brush. It was posted 9/4/18. 28/6/18 Recd 2 parcels 1 contg big tin of Griffiths Chocolate & the other Lollies, Ginger, Preserved Peaches
