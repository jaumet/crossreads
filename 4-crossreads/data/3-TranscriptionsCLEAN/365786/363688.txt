  
 The men in the hospitals were delighted to receive visitors & I used to go almost every day & see the 50 odd men in my husband's 6 wards.  I hired a machine & made them all fruit bags to keep their few treasures in & as we found it impossible to get tooth-brushes, razors, handkerchiefs, etc. from the Red + we were doing our best to procure these ourselves (1 hank. in ward).
