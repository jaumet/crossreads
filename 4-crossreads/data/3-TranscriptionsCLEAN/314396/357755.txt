  
 Changed  1 &  got 97 1/2 piastres Went up street for 2 hours &  drove around Alexandria Had a real good look at the town. 500000 people live here &  800000 at Cairo. When came aboard was orderly corporal so went on duty. Very funny. Plenty of drunks & we sent out a patrol to bring in the drunks. 
 Friday 4th. Nov 1914. Orderly Corporal. Plenty of work today. Had to parade a few of the drunks for duty in hold. Entrained at 1/4 to 2 &  left at 3.30 pm. Arrived at Cairo at 8.30 pm. The country is very well irrigated all the way & very green at present. Saw 3 or 4 cemetries. plenty of date palms. Got out at a siding 1/4 mile after Cairo. Picked up Stylo pen when getting out of train. Went to Mena in electric cars &  camped at foot of pyramids in sand. Eat sand & drink it. 
 Saturday 5th Dec. Went to have a look at pyramids from hill. Large one is 457 ft high. Altogether 9 of them & sphinz. Camped on edge of Saraha Desert. Windy & of course plenty of sand to eat. Put up tents all the morning. All meals on tin meat & dog biscuits so far. Plenty of  donkeys here.
