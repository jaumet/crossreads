 February, 1915 Friday 5 Yesterday went to Pompie Pillar &amp; the Sphinx &amp; then on to Catatombs passing Natives quarters where incerinator buried up the dead on one side of street &amp; Baker cooks on the other &ndash; Afterwards to the museum to see Mummies from the different Tombs &ndash; 5000 years or more!!! old 3 Trains today for Cairo. Off in March we hear for France &ndash; only hope it is true. At 1 A.M. more tired &amp; colder than ever.
