  
 June 1918 Tuesday 11 
 Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles Weather fairly cool, not feeling exactly brilliant.  Went for a swim with P.L.C. Charlie Adey got up, and seems to be a little  Better. Went down with trench fever during the  afternoon, - feel pretty rotten,  Percy Gray being also effected, he has the same complaint. Saw John Harvey on the road to his home. Expect to go out for a spell on the 18th. only a rumor so far
