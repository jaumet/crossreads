Our Machines also very active, these being heavily engaged by enemy Machine Gun and Anti-aircraft fire.  Artillery generally active to the rear.  Enemy flash observed about 4.30 p.m. from C.9.b.72.30, 9&frac12;" left Fraulinghien [possibly Frelinghien] Church.<br/>D.M.G.O. visited line in Company with C.O.<br/>During the 2nd and 3rd, a test was made with one of the guns less the Check Lever, as per request of D.M.G.O.
