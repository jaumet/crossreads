<p>Thursday 19 September<br/>Many men leave this week for Australia on a six months furlough stunt.  Lucky beggars.<br/>The 1st Aust. Division now holds two records, 1. holding the line continuously for about 130 days, (2) the record for the greatest numbers of guns &amp; prisoners captured in one day by one division.</p>
<p>Friday 20 September<br/>A walk this afternoon to Tincourt over the hills. A small stunt is coming off in the morning &amp; then the Division comes out for a good long spell.</p>
