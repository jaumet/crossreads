  
 F [1/4/17 Sun] Reg. Keirle over again Snow at midnight. Play for Kirk & Jail. Go out for a walk by myself to Durrington Lark Hill meet Sgt Creft, Roley Hole's pal - lovely moonlight night. 
 W & Snow [2/4/17 Mon] Capt Gibbs comes up for an Hour's chat. Long day Hard work Jim. Robertson visits us. Snowing hard at night 
 Snow [3/4/17 Tues] heavy falls of snow heaviest for the season. Glorious sight. I am Policeman till 9.30 & go over to Mr Hunt for Cocoa & Cards. 
 F [4/4/17 Wed] I again play for Lantern Lecture & go to Bulford afterward Wrote to Cyril & recd letter from Jennie. 
 F & W [5/4/17 Thurs] Write to Mum. Nette. Bess. Otto. & George. Recd. letters from Mum & Bess & P.C. from Nettie. Otto 
 F [6/4/17 Good Fri] I play for Kirk. We have a very bad Friday. Ellis & I walk to Durrington after tea & have no money so walk back again Hungry. Recd. letters Grannie. Mem. Parcel from Nette 
 F [7/4/17 Sat] I receive Marie's letter. D E & I walk to Amesbury to the Pictures. We are all dead weary on arrival. We see the Picture "For Australia's Honour & it shows views of Sydney. We wearily set out to walk back but do not notice the distance coming back Receive letter & Photo from Roy.
