<p>Dear Father<br/>Will try &amp;amp: get down during the week don't come up were out for a meal last night have to go again soon don't know when we shall be in camp sail on 15th for certain. Heard nothing about the ships yet. We have been too busy. They are appointing more N.C.O.s tomorrow I think. Love Clyde</p>
