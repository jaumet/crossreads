<p>To-day is nasty &amp; wet &amp; we are going out again tonight Len is on Battalion Hqrs tonight he has the guard &amp; a good job it is especially these wet nights.</p>
<p>5th.    The whole of C. &amp; B. Coy's were out last night &amp; didn't we have a time of it, it rained more or less heavily the whole time, &amp; it was the fun of the world to see us all slipping all over the place into the shell-holes nearly full of water, &amp; sometimes on to the broad of your back in the slippy sloppy clay talk about a time we could not stop ourselves</p>
