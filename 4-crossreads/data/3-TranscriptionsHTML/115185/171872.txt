<p>11 &ndash; and, he had his greatest desire fulfilled &ndash; active service for the empire &ndash; and now one has to do without his gracious presence, that's all.<br/>Mrs Ashburton Thompson has lost her son Brian. The awful discomfort that Gibson must have been through that 8 months in Egypt is past anyones imagination. I saw the troops there just as he was, and I marvel at his lack of complaint - &amp; of course of other men too who have &amp; are facing the same thing.<br/>We have many eye injuries from flying stones here &ndash; I long to hear from him. I have had two letters</p>
