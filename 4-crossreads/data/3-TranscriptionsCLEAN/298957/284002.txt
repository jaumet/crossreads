  
 30th April (Sunday) Lovely morning. Church Parade 
 1st May (Monday) Fine Day. Rail Head on Dental Leave & finishing off the Mess Shed 
 2nd May (Tuesday) Fine. Orders read out about Gen. Townshend surrendering with 2900 British & 6000 Indians. Squad & stretcher Drill and practise at carrying patients by the "Fireman's Lift". March over desert for sandbags. Infantry having a bombing attack on trenches close by
