<p>1917 Saturday 12 May</p>
<p>MacB &amp; self beach.  French kiddies stroll round &amp; tea same place as yesterday. <br/>Letter from Ridgway. Big scrap up line. He wounded in abdomen, also Sgt. Clifton, Hayman, Gibson, Maddox, Leask killed &amp; others wounded</p>
