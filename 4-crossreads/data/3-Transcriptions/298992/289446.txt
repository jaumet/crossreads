<p>Serapeum<br/>Suez Canal<br/>9-4-16</p>
<p>Dear Mother</p>
<p>I am afraid this will not be much of a letter, but I think I have a chance of getting it home per special messenger, and as the lad who is returning is leaving Camp in an hour or so, I will not be able to write much.</p>
<p>Well, here we are, a couple of miles on the Asiatic side of the Canal, not very far from Ismalia. A very interesting place &ndash; no picture shows or anything like that, and fearfully dusty.
