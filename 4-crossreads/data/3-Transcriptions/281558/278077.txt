<p>passes &amp; went to the station : got to Bristol about noon : had my "P" taken &amp; then took Bill's bag to Fishponds : J B J came up after tea so we had a good time : stayed at the Cathedral hotel :</p>
<p>Saturday Jan 12th</p>
<p>Bristol : out to Eastville in morn &amp; pictures after dinner : went to Clifton after tea &amp; had a good time</p>
<p>Sunday Jan 13th</p>
<p>Bristol : out to Hotwells in morn &amp; afternoon : Durdham after tea but the snow came along so we went home</p>
<p>Monday Jan 14th</p>
<p>Bristol : after seeing J B J off had a roam round : The food shortage is rather acute &amp; meat butterine etc is</p>
