5/9/16.Left Senlis at 9 am this morning & travelled per Motor Lorry to Rubempre & are camped in an old barn. We are only here for a day or so. Am in hopes of joining up with the other portion of the Coy. We are on our way to Ypres or somewhere near it.
10/9/16. Sunday.Quite a lot has happened since the 5th. I am writing this in our suite of rooms at Reninghelst (Flanders). Well to get back a few days
