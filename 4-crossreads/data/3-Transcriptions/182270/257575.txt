
<p>SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 20, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot westerly gale blowing.</p>
<p>Lopped in Top paddock in</p>
<p>morning. Set a few fires in</p>
<p>the Middle scrub. Tickle</p>
<p>lopped in Mountain and then</p>
<p>drew water to Darby.</p>
<p>Went into Quirindi in evening</p>
<p>to see Laurie Cadell about</p>
<p>grass down the coast.</p>
