<p>Wednesday 25 July 1917.</p>
<p>No riding this morning but swimming parade instead, and on return to camp were marched back through Nafisha and Nefiche.</p>
<p>The &quot;Tommy&quot; Howitzer Battery is preparing to move out and our unit has now been issued with 24 hrs. rations (iron) as the A.S.C. Depot has moved this morning.</p>
<p>Inoculated again against Para-typhoid (a double dose.)</p>
