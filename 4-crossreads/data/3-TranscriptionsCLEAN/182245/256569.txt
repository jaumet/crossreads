 288 
 1917 FEBRUARY 
 23 FRIDAY 
 Fine but dull & misty. We 
 were called out at 1 AM 
 to take ammunition up to 
 the left section down in 
 Belgium, got back & loaded 
 at the Croix du Bac dump 
 & home about 8 AM. Cleaning 
 harness & horses all day. Was 
 dead tired at night & turened 
 straight in. I got my other 
 boot from Poss & a parcel 
 of Champion from the Civil 
 Service.
