
21stRaided Village - Women gave me a bad time with their yells & clamour - Bought 8 eggs & Four [indecipherable]  2 Bundles Onions some wood for 15/-  - This village is in Arub Wadi where Pontius Pilate made an aqueduct from the spring to Jerusalem & was sent back to Rome for spending too much money about 18 Centuries ago - This water channel is now being unearthed by British for a new supply to Jerusalem
