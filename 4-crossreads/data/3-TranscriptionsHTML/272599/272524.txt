<p>1917 Friday 21 Sept.</p>
<p>7th Field chap in ward sick.   Tells me of a calamity happening to 1st Field bearers.  20 knocked out by one shell 5 being killed.   First thought is whether Bob was in it. &amp; will be anxious till I get news from him.   Wounded beginning to come in.  operations successful but it all costs many casualties.</p>
