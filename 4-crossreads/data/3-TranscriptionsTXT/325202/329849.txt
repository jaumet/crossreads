 Stations landed down by hospital train at the Base &amp; was sent to Casino Hospital &ndash; a very pleasant change &amp; one I was not too sick to appreciate but my chief grievance was  too  being  too much   "tucked in bed" that is 'niceness' seem to count more than 'comfort' &amp; the nurses turn the sheets down so as just to leave your shoulders out &amp; then tuck the close in to the sides so tight that it leaves a fearful draught down your back!
