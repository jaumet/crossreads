 13/5/16  Saturday 
 It is a wonder there is anything of us left after the awful heat &ndash; just perspiration from daylight till sunset &amp; the flies did not help things at all. General cleaning up of the lines. At 3.30 pm - a General parade was called for inspection by General McKay &amp; Godley &ndash; They seemed quite satisfied &amp; said that we were doing splendid work that is running a Field Ambulance &amp; yet they make use of us a clearing Hospital also, also have in our charge 40 rejects for Australia &ndash; We all seem to think we will not be here long now after this inspection. 
 I bought a pocket Kodak for 75 Piastres off a fellow in the lines, hope to get a lot of pleasure out of it, if they will allow me to keep it.  Have since heard that in the coolest E.P. Tent the Thermometer registered 117.
