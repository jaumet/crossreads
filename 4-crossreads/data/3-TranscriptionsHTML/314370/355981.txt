<p>February, 1916<br/>Sunday 13<br/>Write letter home in the forenoon.  It is so long since I have heard from mum that I find it hard to put anything into letters.<br/>After dinner take dad to the Zoo, &amp; see the new wonders of a hippo &amp; several splendid Giraffes.<br/>Have dinner in Cairo &amp; return at 8.30 pm</p>
<p>Monday 14<br/>Peg away at the care &amp; cleaning subject; the men a very keen to learn.<br/>Have great hopes for them.</p>
