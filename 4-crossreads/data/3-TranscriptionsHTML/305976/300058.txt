<p>Saturday Oct 5<br/>The Turks arrived last night about 8pm. They were in a pitiable condition. They were starved &amp; the wounded had not had their wounds attended too. A number died coming in &amp; others after they arrived. We worked all night making them as comfortable as possible. Today a convoy of Motor Lorries came and took them further down the line.
