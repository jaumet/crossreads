 Tuesday 26 December 1916 Bit of telephone work in morning. Inspected by G.O.C. Desert Colum in afternoon, very funny turn out;  our general got nervous, gave wrong orders.  Heavy storm. 
 Wednesday 27 December 1916 Nothing much doing in morning. Harry Jacob, Cpl. Leaske &amp; I on out post at night. Rained a good bit which short circuted telephone.
