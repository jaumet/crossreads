<p>R.A.S.G.</p>
<p>6.2.16<br/>My dear Father</p>
<p>I am glad you are enjoying your trip so well; &amp; when you come back I expect to hear great tales of men falling overboard, millions of cheering troops, exciting adventures in gold mines etc &ndash; especially from Dorothy. As I have nothing to the contrary, I conclude that you are all well, &amp; have no idea of returning yet a while.</p>
