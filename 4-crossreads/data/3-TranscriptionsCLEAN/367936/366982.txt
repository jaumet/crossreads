
Monday 22 May 1916Have two machines going clipping the horses.  Lucerne feed in evening.  Heavy swell & head wind.
Tuesday 23 May 1916Hot misty day, sea as calm as glass.  Sighted Cape Guadafui [Guardafui] and came past pretty close.  High cliffs & sandy beaches & a sandy headland.  Passed two villages which we could see with glasses.  Sea teeming with fish, saw 2 sharks, turtles, sword fish pourpoise etc.  Passed 2 dohws.  Turned round headland "The Elephant" in evening.
