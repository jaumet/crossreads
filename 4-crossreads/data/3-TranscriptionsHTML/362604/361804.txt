<p><u>the</u> Lady, as Aunt Pollie was <u>the</u> Lady of Dad's Family.  She is or was the one to entertain while any of the other Cinderellas made the Tea.  But don't let on about my opinion.  I would not offend good old Marion.  So D. Drayton is getting into the Limelight on the big Theatres at last.  He was always clever enough but too bombastic.  Dave is a terrible skite so I am not surprised if the rest of the Family are, but that is the only failing &amp; one can easily get over that.  Poor Mrs. Niness, she dotes on Dave but then He is a lovely Boy, you know.</p>
