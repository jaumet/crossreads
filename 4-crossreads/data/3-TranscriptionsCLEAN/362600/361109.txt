
F 27 Sun1430 lettersRay Mitchell comes down to D'ton with medicine. Go walk with Tam. See Bernard Moore on road. Very stiff & sore. Don't sight T. at all.Recd letters from  Lottie & DulcePlayed cards all night
D 28 MonBut no rain & misty. Toms goes to Blackpool. Go to Pictures as I'm all alone. Come Home to play Billiards
Wrote Dad. Mum & George. 1430 letters to here.
F & W 29 Tues.Go to Pictures after Packing Bag for Island. See [aerodrome?]Submarine Picture. Come Home & go to bed early
F 30 WedBustle around. Recd. Parcel of Socks from Miss [Niness?]. Send Parcel to Auntie & [Uncle?] G. Catch 12.27 with Jack. Dinner in Salisbury. Trend with Tommies & ladies from Southport - stay at c/o #8
F & W 31 ThCross with Young Downward & I get very sick twice awfully rough day. Go from Coburg Docks. Go through Liverpool Museum Tynwald - arr. 5.40 pm I sight all my Island Folk
W 1 Nov. FriAwful Rain Thunders. Go to Sullyhill in [Cart?] & come Home for music & millinery. We all sit & tell stories around the fireside.
W 2 SatTerrific Gale. Go to [Sulby?] to send Telegram. Awful gale & make no headway.Go around by [Gladdaugh?]. Get sopping wet. Home & we play Patience all night. Clean camping sacks [indecipherable] I dream of Auntie & Uncle [indecipherable] chasing me down the drive in Night Clothes
