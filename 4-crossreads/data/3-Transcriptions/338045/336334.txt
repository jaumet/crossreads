<p>&amp;parade ground &amp; the work is complimented by Orderly Officer.  At this until 12 noon.  Afternoon off (Sat.) &amp; having a leave pass (my first in camp) visit Etaples.  It is a dirty little town, all side streets &amp; small.  Do some shopping with Sgt. David &amp; return to camp.  Cold night.  Expenses:-  P/cs 2fr.60, Supper 3fr.40 = 6Frs.  Very little occurred at the front.</p>
<p>Oct. 22nd &ndash; Sunday<br/>Another bitterly cold night.  Heavy frost.  Parade 6-30 a.m. &amp; after breakfast, fall in for church parade at 8-30 a.m.  I have charge of R.C. parade of 28 men &amp; march in (with other units) to the Church in Etaples.  Mass at 9-30 a.m. &amp; a fine sermon</p>
