
Sept 15thRumour is very strong now everywhere  [indecipherable] leaving Egypt shortly for Salonica or England. It is feasible, as a Camel Corps has come out here  &  may relieve this division here.Bir el Abd. Sept 16Here I am at Bir el Abd, again stuntingWe left Romani last night at 6 oclock, or rather 1800, travelled all night  &  got here at 5 or 0500, very irritable, very tired  &  with a very unwashed feeling.
