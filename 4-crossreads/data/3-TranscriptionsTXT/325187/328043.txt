 [Friday 27 October 1916] 
 Polling Booth. London is not a place of pleasure in the dark we come back here each night till 10.30. Don't mention how the votes go as strict secrecy must be observed. Laura dined with me last night she is very thin but glad to get away from Walsall. Colonel Ryan Colonel Manderby were on the Medical Board- English Hospital vote YES Trench Field Force NO   Egyptian not yet here but think they will vote YES- it will be a close go
