<p>Mentioned in despatches  December 6th, 16</p>
<p>Extract from General Staff orders by General McKay, G.O.C.  6.12.16</p>
<p>L/Cpl Berry H.V.<br/>On three separate occasions L/Cpl Berry, who was in charge of a party of bearers for more than three weeks, displayed conspicuous bravery, handling his men with ability &amp; inspiring them throughout.</p>
