<p>Fri Dec 26th:-<br/>Arrived at Port Melbourne &amp; pulled into Central pier at Victoria Docks about 1 pm. As we are here for a few days allowed on shore. Telegrams are flowing to Sydney &amp; no doubt some will "cut the painter" &amp; do the overland trip.</p>
<p>Sat Dec 27th:-<br/>The rain today spoils St Kilda beach.<br/>Staying at Glenferrie House, Glenferrie</p>
<p>Sun Dec 28th:<br/>Out to see our relations at the zoo at Royal Park &amp; picking up friends at Brunswick.</p>
<p>Mon Dec 29th:-<br/>Out to the Melbourne Cricket Ground for the NSW v Victoria match.<br/>Stopped on boat tonight as we are getting ready for our final crawl to Sydney.</p>
<p>Tues Dec 30th:-<br/>Left for Sydney at 8 am this morning. Bad weather again.</p>
<p>Wed Dec 31st:-<br/>Nearing home &amp; the ship is " topsy-turvey" with the preparations of getting all the "glad rags" out.<br/>Suppose it will be our luck for someone to have pinched the 'arbour.</p>
