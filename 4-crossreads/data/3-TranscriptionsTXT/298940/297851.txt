  Saturday 16th Feb. 1918 . Tent pitching this morning. Parade dismissed in afternoon when I prepared some notes on Omar Khayyam for the literary evening. Good meeting &ndash; about 20 out of 24 members turning up. I was elected to chair &amp; we got to work immediately (held in barn near the stables . Forms &amp; chairs  by  provided by farmhouse people). Sam Rickson read a passage from "The Sky Pilot". Pte Barlow read a passage from "Young Lord Stranleigh" by Robert Barr. H.A.T.
