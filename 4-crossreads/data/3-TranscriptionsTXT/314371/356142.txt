 We have a couple of small primus stoves to each section which means we can always have a warm drink.  After breakfast we changed barrels &amp; stood by for S.O.S. signs.  Altogether we answered 4 S.O.S. calls during the next 24 hours. We were withdrawn about 4 p.m. on the 21st, got back to camp dead tired &amp; hungry.  After a good feed of hot stew &amp; a cup of tea we were ready for bed. Next day was spent in cleaning the guns &amp; putting things in order.
