<p>August,1918<br/>Friday 2</p>
<p>Ribemont. Bty Position<br/>At 1 am Fritz again put a heavy general barrage down. I think we shall have a stunt from him shortly. <br/>No stunt but a heavy front line strafe with Minnies.<br/>Weather fairly moist all day. Right section took over at 9 pm.<br/>Up till midnight nothing had happened at all, save a slight bombardment .<br/>Clive and Johnnie Walker went down to W.L.</p>
