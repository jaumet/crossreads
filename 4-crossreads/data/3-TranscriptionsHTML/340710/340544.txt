I was directed to the next floor and there- but no let us pass up still another flight where I brightened considerably on observing bedsteads.  I felt I was getting to the end of the chase and indeed on enquiry I was conducted to a counter and then followed a harangue between shop-walker and the assistant, I suspected them of conspiring to assassinate me but gripped my nail-file firmly.
