 14-2-17 Wed 14th Harris is OK again &amp; the Dr. says both he &amp; I can go on the 9 oclock train tomorrow morning owing to a breakdown on the line no trains are running today. A lad named Martin (2nd Regt.) is also going back with us Our planes went out again this afternoon loaded with bombs
