<p>17th Sept Tuesday<br/>A cool breeze blowing all day. Like rain again. Every one wants blankets etc. Wrote letters to-day.</p>
<p>Saturday 18th Sept<br/>Quiet</p>
<p>Sunday 19th<br/>Went to Ordnance again. My gang fitted themselves out - ! &ndash; a good deal of artillery firing to-day. last night our infantry attacked the Turks &amp; captured several trenches near lone Pine</p>
<p>Monday 20th Sept<br/>A Blowy day &amp; keen</p>
<p>Tuesday 21st Sept<br/>The same. We had a lot of shelling to-day.</p>
<p>Wednesday 22nd<br/>Very keen again. Winds just like our Westerly. Busy on fresh indents etc. Ordnance &amp; Ground</p>
