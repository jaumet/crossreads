April, 1915Friday 16Brigade day today fighting the New Zealanders, near Mataria &amp; the old Suez Road.Personally I am better, but ever since the bullet went thru' my nose, the Doctors say I'll sniffle with my throat as I breath per mouth. Am feeling A.1. again. Myra Nyse very ill &amp; I am told at Hospital she has only a fighting chance.
