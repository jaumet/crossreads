<p>Friday April 27<br/>Review of Troops in morning afternoon sports. Our unit successful in relay race. Good days sport<br/>Saturday April 28<br/>Charley sneaked to Melbourne persuaded me to take his place wish him luck. She seems a nice Girl &amp; Charley is a straight go'er. Wish my little girl in Sydney was closer<br/>Sunday April 29<br/>Seymour absolutely packed with interstate Troops. There has been no sailings since January.<br/>Monday April 30<br/>Usual routine. All hands seem to be losing interest owing to constant delay in sailing</p>
