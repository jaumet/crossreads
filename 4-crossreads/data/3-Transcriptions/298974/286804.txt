<p>3<br/>my feelings of regret at the news. I have written, but that's such a poor way of doing a thing like that. Nim was a chap I have the profoundest respect for, and was one of my best chums. Just before leaving Blighty I saw a letter of Marg Burnetts to Tommy, telling me about things at Denegully &amp; about how sad Una was. I do hope you have all got a peace of mind now, &amp; can review old times in only a happy retrospect.</p>
<p>We get the sun here about once a fortnight, but to day is just glorious. Theres always an ice &amp; frost coating to the earth each night tho', &amp; the proverbial mud of this district is generally frozen.</p>
