We have not had mail for nearly 6 weeks and no-one can tell how much longer we shall have to wait . It does seem rotten having to wait so long on account of people striking like this. Oh well we shall have to wait but I hope not for long.Harry and I are quite well only for a cold which is quite the fashion now, and poor old Saddie likes the cold weather as you know only too well.
