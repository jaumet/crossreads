  
 Wednesday 24 October 
 Cold winds - dull and showery To 1st Anzac Corps. Hd. Q. Sigs To A.D.M.S. 5th Aust. Div. Hd. Qd. at Ypres via Poperinghe and Vlamertinghe Playing cards after tea Over Ypres the aeroplanes were very busy about midday.  The air was thick with them.  A few German new planes of the huge variety were over and ours tried unsuccessfully to attack them.  Men came down in paraschutes from their balloons.
