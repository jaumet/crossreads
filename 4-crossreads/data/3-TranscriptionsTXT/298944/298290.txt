 Wed 10 July Yesterday, for the first time in my week off nursing duties &amp; on parades, I did some army work. I was put on wad making, and put in the morning, from 7am till dinner time, on carting bricks, and placing them in position on the wad which we are making for the motor transport on account of the skidding which the earth causes in wet weather.
