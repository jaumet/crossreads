Feeling a bit off myself with Influenza. 5th Division went into the trenches this evening.
20th Monday - Tuesday 21st - Wednesday 22nd - Thursday 23rd - Friday 24th
In the Field Hospital pretty bad with Trench Fever.
Saturday  25-1916
Drizzling rain - Knocking about a bit but not feeling too good. Our Ambulance went into the trenches on Tuesday, so I have missed them. Dr. Beard & his staff from the 60th Batt. Aid Post came in suffering from Bronchitis.
Sunday 26 - Monday 27 - Tuesday 28th - Wednesday 29th - Thursday 30th
Very sick - taking things easy. Very cold.
Dec 1st 1916  Friday
Feeling no good again.
