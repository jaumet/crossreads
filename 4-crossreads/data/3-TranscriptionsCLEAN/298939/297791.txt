Monday 25th June FovantWe arrived in Fovant Camp at midday on Friday & found everything ready for us - even beds madeThe position is not nearly so bad as we had been led to expect, & our actual camp is rather good.Lord Werley [?] reviewed the 16th Brigade on Saturday & the Ambulance was adjudged the smartest on parade
