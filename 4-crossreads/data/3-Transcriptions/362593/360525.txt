<p>Saturday 9 March 1916<br/>Recco.  Es Salt-Nablus, in air 2 h. 50 m.<br/>Pilot Lt. Headlam.  B.F.  A good recco but very cloudy.<br/>Got an anti-aircraft shell extremely close but no damage done.</p>
<p>Sunday 10 March 1918<br/>Recco Es Salt, Nablus.<br/>Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 2 h. 45 m.  A good trip.<br/>Reported cavalry in Jordan valley.<br/>It is a pleasure to be flying with Addie again.</p>
