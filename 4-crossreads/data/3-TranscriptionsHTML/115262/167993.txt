<p>Tuesday 14 August 1917.</p>
<p>Drill order only with camels before breakfast. Posted Registered Parcel to Mother.</p>
<p>Received &quot;Table Talk&quot; from Joy this afternoon.</p>
<p>Had snap taken on camel this afternoon.</p>
<p>After feeding parade went to Cairo &amp; got photographic material for taking &quot;up the line&quot;.</p>
<p>Tonight the light saving action came into force &amp; it makes a difference to Cairo city.</p>
