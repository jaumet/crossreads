<p>Saturday 1st September 1917<br/>Went out in W. Flinns team as lead driver carting 4.5s from Spoil Bank to Dump near camp. One load in the morning and one in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Sunday 2nd<br/>Fed mules in the morning 2nd parade 10 am. Church service 10.30am. 3rd parade 4pm.<br/>10pm Tubes dropping bombs. They kept coming over till about 3.30am.</p>
