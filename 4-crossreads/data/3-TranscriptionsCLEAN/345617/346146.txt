  
 French cathedrals the internal fittings are very elaborate & beautiful. Had lunch at the E. Canteen & dinner about 5p.m. at a French cafe. The Royal Fusiliers were playing good music in the park during the afternoon. The appear to be a good many Australians in the Military Hospital at St Omer. 
 Aout 13 Had an easy day today. General clean up & overhaul of equipment. Inspection of Coy by O.C. in the afternoon.
