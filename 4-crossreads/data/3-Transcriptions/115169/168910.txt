<p>July 25th During forenoon torpedo firing exercise was carried out, also 1"electric aiming</p>
<p>July 26th the same exercises at yesterday</p>
<p>July 27th During forenoon, we again proceeded outside to Pentland Firth to carry out 2/3 calibre firing long range after which we returned to harbour, and the afternoon was spent coaling ship</p>
<p>July 30th This was regatta day for our squadron (each squadron had its own day for regattas) &ndash; the usual races were gone thru and sorry to say "Melbourne" was just no good at all Birmingham gaining 1st place, but still it was a very exciting day and the weather very nice indeed.</p>
<p>July 31st Day and night firing and torpedo running was carried out.</p>
<p>Aug 1st Same as yesterday</p>
<p>Aug 2nd During the morning we proceeded out to Pentland Firth to carry out <br/>torpedo firing at full speed, after which we returned to our anchorage<br/>Aug 3rd We coaled ship</p>
