
Aug 30 The mountains are rocky rugged & steep, in fact in many places perpendicular The tram winds quickly down to the Bay - on our right the "Lions Head" pierces the upper air, while the far famed Table Mtn (wilder & more rugged than our dear old Blue Mtn's) guards our left.The tram line from Capetown to the bay is cut into the side of the mountain & one wonders what would happen if she skidded or back-kicked.
