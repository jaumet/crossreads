<p>Aug 8th 1917<br/>41,068 rations</p>
<p>Aug 9th 1917<br/>2 men proceeded on leave to the United Kingdom.<br/>69 N.C.O.'s and men baked 41,452 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 10th 1917<br/>69 N.C.O.'s and men baked 40,320 rations</p>
<p>Aug 11th 1917<br/>2 proceeded on leave<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 47,030 rations</p>
<p>Aug 12th</p>
<p>Strength: 1 Officer 73 O.ranks not including 1 in detention 1 P.B. man attached, 2 in "Hpl, 20 on leave to U. Kingdom.<br/>1 Rejoined from leave. 70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 46,560 rations</p>
<p>Aug 13th <br/>47 N.C.O.'s and men baked 29,560 rations</p>
<p>Aug 14th<br/>3 N.C.O.'s and men reported from leave.  2 proceed on leave, 1 to hospital, 1 rejoined from detention.<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 39,180 rations</p>
<p>Aug 15th<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 39,326 rations</p>
