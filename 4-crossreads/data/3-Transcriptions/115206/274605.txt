<p>February 1918<br/>Monday 4</p>
<p>On the road<br/>Left billet at 9.30 for the Guns. Smithy, Adey, Cody and Garford. Jamie and I as horseholders. Arrived after a tour through Bailleul and Neuve Eglise, at old Battery position, near Messines. Returned to Battery's billets and had lunch en route.  Felt pretty tired so retired at an early hour.<br/>Weather very cold and damp</p>
