She is wrinkled, bent & shapeless.  Later you learn that these hard-working girls crumple up & lose their appearance much quicker than our own or English girls.  During the week they are mostly untidily dressed, with great heavy boots or wooden clogs and ragged clothes.  On Sunday they are a revelation.  Nearly all devout Catholics they don their glad rags and neat shoes, and will walk miles to mass.
