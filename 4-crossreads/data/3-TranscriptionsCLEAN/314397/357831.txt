 Friday 11.10.18 Dr Speirs says my complaint now is scabies. Gee they itch some. Can hardly get a sleep now at all during the night. Took Marie & her pal to dinner at Simpsons & then to see Shanghai. Dot Brenton & Ivy Shilling were playing in it. It is very decent but the music is poor. After the show we went back to the flat & had supper. Had a big pillow fight. Gee it was some fight.
