Wednesday 18.7.17
Left Corbie at 8 Am marched back to Senlis where we arrived at 12.30 in time for dinner. Fairly tired, slept all afternoon.
Thursday 19.7.17
Played 15 F Amb Cricket won by 21 runs. Concert party making very elaborate preparations for their show on Saturday night.
Friday 20.7.17
Picket placed on the camp on account of money being stolen civilians suspected. Went to Albert at night to while away a few hours, place improving every day.
Saturday 21.7.17
Nine months since we first went into action on the Somme.
