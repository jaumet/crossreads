<p>3 October, Friday</p>
<p>Go down markets shopping.</p>
<p>Recd letter from Auntie &amp; Marie last night. I read them before going to bed.</p>
<p>4 October, Saturday</p>
<p>Meet Ray Clarke. Trams crowded E &amp; I try to catch horse but fail. Mum &amp; I go to Taubs. Meet Nellie.</p>
