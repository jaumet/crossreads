<p>knew not where.  At the cross roads where we should get off for Mont de Cats, (Gray had stayed to see his brother) Pinky got off the lorry &amp; later we did.  Then we decided after reassembling, that we could get back by lorries somehow.  The first we caught took us on the road to Sixibusch [?]&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &amp; then we got another back to Berthen.  From Berthen we <strike>got a</strike> walked to the main road, got a drink &amp; then dashed after some lorries.  Pinky &amp; I got on but Snowy Eva did not get on, &amp; for some reason (fatigue as I after found out), could not catch the following ones either.<br/>Pinky &amp; I arrived back</p>
