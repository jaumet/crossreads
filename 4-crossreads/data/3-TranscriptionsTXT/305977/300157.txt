 Friday 8th Usual work on stables all day.  Hot muggy weather with showers.  More boxing at night.  Some of the finals were put through.  Pickering &amp; myself mess orderlies.  Mail day.  Flagship took the letters off.  I wrote one letter home.  Was vaccinated today. 
 Saturday 9th Strong head wind blowing.  After inspection several of us were late on the 11.30am parade &amp; got 4 days stables at reveille.  Had to put in stable piquet at 6pm.
