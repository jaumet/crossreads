<p>Tuesday 17 April 1917</p>
<p>Very Hot. On OUTPOST tonight in the front line.</p>
<p>Enemy bombed us while watering &amp; killed 5 of another Regiment.</p>
<p>Rode about most of the night keeping up inter-communication. Picquet fired on Turkish Patrol. INFANTRY took first position of GAZA. <strike>many prisoners</strike> We hope to finish the job tomorrow, the Cables will tell you all details, long ere this reaches you &amp; besides I cannot break faith &ndash; but we are hopeful of doing great things with our TANKS etc.</p>
