
25 SundayGrey day & showers in the afternoon.  Raining as we went to bed.  Did not go out at all.  Very debilitated.
26 MondayWrote & pottered about Fairfield & walked into Hitchin.  Nice day.
27 TuesdayMatron Nye & I came up to London in the morning.  I went to Threadneedle St. to be passed by Merchant Taylors for Convalescent Home then to 22 Harley [Road, South Hampstead]. Dull day
28 WednesdayDull dreary day.  Busied myself writing letters etc.  Struggled to put down depression.
