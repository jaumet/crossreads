<p>1916 June 30<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Slept in stable last night billet. Pigs fowls, cows &amp; horses. Did not prevent sound sleep tho', Tired after long walk. Heavy bombardment continued all night. Tony to see me after tea, -- re transfer.  Cart. Net &amp; self put on gardening all day. Receive 5 more letters. To bed early. Great difference in tucker here than anywhere else. Cocoa, ham, puddings. "tres bon". Receive pay. 20 francs. also note from Bob. per ambulance waggon  Watched a thrilling air fight this evening. 32 aeroplanes up at one time. Plenty of excitement in that game</p>
