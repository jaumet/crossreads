  
 August 1917 Wednesday 22nd Bad weather as set in again so things are very quiet Thursday 23rd Weather is still bad Fritz paid us a visit this evening Friday 24th Put in the Hospital today   [indecipherable] a least the place they call a hospital it is not fit to house a good dog much less men who are crook   [indecipherable]   have been risking there lives for months the British army is the rottenest organization in the world Saturday 25th Still having a crook time will most likely have to be operated on Sunday 26th It as been raining & blowing a gale today there was to be a big offensive tomorra  this will just about knocked in the head
