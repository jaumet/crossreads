Monday 12.2.17 to Wednesday 14.2.17
Weather getting warmer, snow ice beginning to thaw. Several heavy bombardments have taken place. Received parcel from home containing putties. Rations very poor scanty. Reg evacuated to C.C.S. Lads again went into line for a week. Walked to Albert to try find 3rd Div Artillery, but was misinformed for they are still up North.
Thursday 15.2.17
Hospital full. Ground getting very muddy owing to thaw setting in.
Friday 16.2.17 Saturday 17.2.17
Rained first time for a couple
