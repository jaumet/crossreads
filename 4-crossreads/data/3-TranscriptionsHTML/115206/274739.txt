<p>June 1918<br/>Tuesday 18</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines. nr. Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather unsettled. Paid 100 Francs to-day. Played two<br/>up and dropped the whole issue, as well as 50 others.<br/>Fritz plane fired one of our balloons, one observer<br/>parachute failed to open, but he fell on a tree and came <br/>out with a few scratches. The other chap <br/>landed in Bty W.Lines and had an explosive bullet drilled<br/>through his side. He seemed just about done when he<br/>landed. <br/>Met Gun Adam outside Querrie.</p>
