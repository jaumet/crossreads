 22 October 1918 Tuesday. (X to X crossed out) 31 General "Tucker" good here &amp; altho' short of Orderlies everything O.K.  I hope to be discharged soon as Temp. normal now. X In my ward are 60 Officers &ndash; Poor little daughter was vaccinated for the 5th time but she will "not take" neither do I for Small Pox is it because you once had it? Spent afternoon with Dolly &amp; wife &amp; get 14 days leave tomorrow  love-  I'll cable if any chance of coming home-
