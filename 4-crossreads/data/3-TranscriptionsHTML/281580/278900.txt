<p>1st December 1917 Sunday</p>
<p>Divine Service In the morning by Rev. McNeil. A dogmatic harangue which provided much discussion. Evening, Rev. Curtis held service which was more to the general taste. His broad minded views were ably expressed and contrasted strongly with the narrow outlook of McNeil. X'lent weather. Steam along the Southern Coast of Spain.</p>
