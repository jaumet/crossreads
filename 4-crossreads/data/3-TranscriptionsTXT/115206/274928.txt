 December  1918. Thursday 26 
 Suez Canal, Suez, Red Sea."Karoola" Passed all the old spots, Gereffe, Shallufa, Kubri, and Gurkha and Baluchistan Posts. Weather ideal a nice cool breeze. Passed the old "Daphne". Had a yarn with Harry Rich who is on board. Left Suez at 1.35pm and commenced our run down to Colombo where we expect to arrive on or about the 10th.
