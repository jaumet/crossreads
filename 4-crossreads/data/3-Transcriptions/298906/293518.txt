<p>2nd. Heavy rain last night &amp; still very cloudy this morning. Old Tubby was very busy again this morning.<br/>Saw Lindsay Grahams brother tonight, he has just come across from Australia.<br/>3rd. This afternoon we done a nice little stunt "tactical schemes,' I had charge of No. 1 Coy. this consisted of 70 N.C.Os &amp; men, had to tell them off &amp; fix up the various sections as are used in an attack, this was just into my hands for I have a good idea of all the new formations, we learnt all that in France, &amp; in actual fighting, shortly after we moved out, we got into artillery formation, &amp; advanced</p>
