<p>Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers<br/>17.3.16</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Layton<br/>Could you assist Mrs. McPherson to get a prambulator for her very heavy baby. She has got along very well on her pay with 3 children &amp; so far asked for no help &ndash; It would be a great help to her to have some means of taking the child about</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>E. H. Bennie.</p>
