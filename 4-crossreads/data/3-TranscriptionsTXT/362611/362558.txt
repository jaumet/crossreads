I am very doubtful whether he will be able to leave for Australia with the next few days, as he was hoping he might. His name is down for the hospital ship due to leave within a week, but he says he heard the Sister-in-charge telling the doctor that she thought he ought not to go out of hospital just yet, and he thinks "she will have her way".
