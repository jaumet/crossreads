<p>24 November 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Another easy night. A chap of 56th brought in about 3 a.m. smothered in mud, hair, eyes &amp; mouth &ndash; over supply of rum, his own  complaint.  Lost his teeth, such work to get . . hsis clothes off. no more disturb'. thro' morning. Sleep during forenoon.  B &amp; go to canteen in aft. Plenty Fritz's on roads. Nothing doing.  Wrote letters &amp; New Year Cards.</p>
