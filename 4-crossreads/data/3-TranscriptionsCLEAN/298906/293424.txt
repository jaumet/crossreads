After reaching Camp I went down & had a good shower, wrote my diary up & then went to bed.27th Have been real crook all day long, I don't know what is the cause of it perhaps the exertion of yesterday was too much, & that combined with the thundery weather may have upset me, I felt weak all over had a rotten headache & could not eat a single thing.
