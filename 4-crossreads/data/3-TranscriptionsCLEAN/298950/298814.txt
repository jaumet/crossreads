Laundry Ruhleben:   An efficient laundry is permitted to enter Camp, charging moderate prices.   Liverpool .  No laundry.  No hot water may be had for washing purposses.   Churches Ruhleben   New Church for Church of England in course of construction in Dec. 1915.  Jewish Synagogue.  Catholic Chapel.  Shool Rooms.  Club Rooms.  No English Clergy interned in Ruhleben.   Liverpool .  Services of all denominationes are held in the Theater on Sundays.  1 Shool tent supplied.  Clergymen includet in general internement.   General Treatement Ruhleben .
