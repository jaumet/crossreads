<p>KIT AND KAT<br/>Friday 12<br/>Shifting out of Ypres left by train got off at Abelle &amp; marched to the frontier of France &amp; Belgium, resting at a farm<br/>Saturday 13<br/>Drinking the blue's away "Vin Rouge Vin Blange Beer ect. Had a wash &amp; shave the first for ten day's<br/>Sunday 14<br/>Still drinking. Old Nutty is a complete wreck. All the boys are "fou the nou but it is a case of drink and forget The sun is shining and life is a good deal better.</p>
