<p>Tues 9th Another field day out early until about 3 30 pm.<br/>Happily we were allowed to feed up early &amp; go to bed.</p>
<p>Thurs 10th<br/>On Regimental guard at 8.30 am until next day Peaceful rest.</p>
<p>Sat 13th Went into Cairo with Wright &amp; to Zoo.</p>
