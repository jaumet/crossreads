<p>February 1918<br/>Tuesday 5</p>
<p>On the road &ndash; and Messines. Left our billets at 8.30am and arrived at Daylight Corner via Dranoutie and Bailleul at 12.30pm.<br/>Warned to leave for guns immediately on arrival. Packed my kit and went up with gun and Kempe .<br/>Arrived at S.O.S. gun at 2 pm and at pill box at 5 pm.<br/>Took up the 10 till 12 watch.<br/>Weather wet<br/>Have a fine dug-out almost 5.9 proof.</p>
