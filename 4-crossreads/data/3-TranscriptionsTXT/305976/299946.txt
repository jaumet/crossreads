
Saturday Dec 1Returned to Jaffa Found the boys standing to owing to Jacko shelling Some spies were caught signalling & directing artillery fire from housetops. Taubes came over at nightSunday Dec 2Visited Jaffa & visited ruins near the shore. Saw remains of House where Simon Peter supposed to have stayedMonday Dec 3Looking like rain todayReceived some parcels. Anzac Divisional Post Cards Christmas Cards came along today. Posted some to friends. In afternoon visited foundry
