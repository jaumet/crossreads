<p>18-19.1.15</p>
<p>Mon 18th<br/>Signalling as usual Chasper Moore, Plushy &amp; Gwilliam overstayed leave last night &amp; were arrested in town;  to-day they were before the Colonel &amp; were sentenced to 7 days cells &amp; 10 days C.B.<br/>Lamp practice after tea, I bought some fruit from the canteen &amp; lost my purse shortly after but did not miss it till going to bed</p>
<p>Tue 19th<br/>Found my purse a few yards from the canteen door &amp; was lucky to get it as it was trampled into the sand<br/>were signalling in same old spot &amp; doing the same work.  had lamp practice again to-night.  Stan &amp; I stayed out after the others came in and were practicing on our own  Sergeant Mack sent to hospital at the Citadel</p>
