 Friday 16/11/17&nbsp; Marched to Berthen via Mont Des Cats [Mont des Cats]&nbsp; I walked- orders received to report to Reinf Camp. 
 Saturday 17/11/17&nbsp; Bn moved to Neuve Eglise- I to Fletre. after a walk of 5 miles&nbsp; Saw Div at Eche 
 Sunday 18/11/17&nbsp; walked to Neuve Eglise &amp; had breakfast with Battalion- walked home in afternoon 19 miles 
 Monday 19/11/17&nbsp; Walked to Mont Des Cats 5 miles&nbsp; Inspected Camp which is in a fearful condition.
