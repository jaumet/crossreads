
Wednesday 15 March 1916Dick finished 40 acres, Geoff still sowing there.  Father & I mustered Lease and weaned Lambs.  Thunder storm but no rain.
Thursday 16 March 1916Dick drove up to the Ryans and cut 49 gate rails in Castle Rock.  Father & I rode up and looked round horses in Long pdk;  they are getting short of water, will have to shift them.  Geoff finished 40 acres.
