  
 Thursday 15/3/17 Rain & mud, Cold wind from the North this morning, but it's drying up the mud, Fritz's artillery very active this morning. Pay 8/9 22.7.0 letter from Viv answered it &  owe 20 francs . Says Nell has had her hair-dyed & & that Grace Box sent her 2.0 0 for Xmas Box very secretive about it never mentioned it herself. Paid 20 francs leaves me 5/9 owining them. 
 Friday 16/3/17 Frost & fog, lovely clear night last night. Posted letter to Nell, Viv & Maude owe 4/9. deferred 22.8.0 no more news about going but leave is starting again I hear 
 Saturday 17/3/17 Beautiful Morning stiff breeze blowing & drying up the ground, Artillery very active the Big Push cant be far off now Owe 3/9 deferred 22.9.0
