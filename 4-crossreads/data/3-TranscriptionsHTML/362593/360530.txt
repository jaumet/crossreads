<p>Thursday 21 March 1918</p>
<p>Friday 22 March 1918<br/>Recco, Damie [possibly Damieh] bridge, Es Salt.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 1 hr. 30 m., returned owing to clouds.  Started again, in air 2 h. 40 m., clouds very bad but did part of recco.<br/>Total hours,<br/>Reccos &ndash; 193 h. 57 m.<br/>Odd trips &ndash; 7 h. 17 m.<br/>Total &ndash; 201 h. 14 m.</p>
