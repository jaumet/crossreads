<p>Monday 26 July, 1915</p>
<p>Work commences today, most of it is done in the cool &amp; we are allowed to lie down &amp; rest in the hot hours.</p>
<p>The drill hours are from 6 a.m. to 9, lecture in tent from 11 a.m. to 12. Dinner at 1 p.m. drill from 5 p.m. to 7.</p>
<p>We are allowed 8d per day by Egyptian Govt &amp; it is given to us in the shape of tinned fish &amp; fruit etc which we have at midday.</p>
