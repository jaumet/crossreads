<p>[Monday 24 July 1916]</p>
<p>Night marching again  We manage to keep our friends engaged-  Not much time for letters or notes, no sleep- as we draw their fire each morning &amp; keep them in check till night, still very hot.<br/>Clive Weston asked for Eleanor Austins address he had seen Stanley or Rupert in Cairo.<br/>Two Taubes over today <strike>they are top dog till we get faster machines.</strike><br/>Shelled Ognatina last night again, but the Light Horse are doing all the work here.<br/>Infantry cannot travel over the sand.<br/>The Horses eat the green dates well.</p>
