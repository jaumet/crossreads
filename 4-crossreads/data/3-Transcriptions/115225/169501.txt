
<p>Nov. 18<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Sunday - I stayed at home all day &amp; had a good rest - Went to Church in the evening with my Aunt.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Nov. 19<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;I went up to town in the morning &amp; saw The Tower, &amp; S<sup><u>t</u></sup>&nbsp;Pauls Cathedral. went up into the whispering gallery &amp; heard the history of same whispered on one side of the dome to be heard distinctly on the other side. I Bought a book I had heard a lot about - &quot;Raymond&quot; by Sir Oliver Lodge. &amp; returned to Kingston - The weather miserably dull but fine.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Nov. 20<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;I took my cousin to M<sup><u>me</u></sup>&nbsp;Taussauds in Baker Street in the morning. we had dinner at Slaters restaurant in the Strand &amp; afterwards met M<sup><u>r</u></sup>&nbsp;M. to go to Bairnsfathers &quot;Better ole&quot; but we couldnt get seats, so we Went to the Palladium &amp; saw Geo Robey - We got back home 6.30.P.M.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Nov 21<sup><u>st</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;I went to Euston to enquire about Glasgow trains etc. I had dinner at Victoria S<sup><u>t</u></sup>&nbsp;Y.M.C.A. &amp; wrote some letters. Went to the Bank &amp; back home. Left Kingston at 9.30 P.M. &amp; caught 11.30 P.M. train to Glasgow to see a friend.</p>
