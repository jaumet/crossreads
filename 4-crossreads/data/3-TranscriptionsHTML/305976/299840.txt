<p>on Victorians whose homes are in Melbourne<br/>Friday March 2<br/>All hands excited at prospect of sailing. Leave granted. Left for Melbourne.<br/>Saturday March 3<br/>Spent week end at Oakleigh. Tom's uncle &amp; friends gave us a fine time.<br/>Sunday March 4<br/> Caught special Train back to camp in preparation for leaving in morning. Wrote letter in Evening<br/>Monday March 5<br/>Medical inspection. Bruckwood, Squires, Fletcher, McKinnon turned down medically unfit. Word came through sailing postponed until Thursday.<br/>(Bad Luck)<br/><strike>Friday</strike> Tuesday March 6th<br/>Nothing doing.</p>
