  
 11 Sept 18 At sea with convoy. 
 12 Sept Arrived at Scapay Flow 8.30 & anchored off Kirkwall with Yarmouth. Left again at 12.30 & picked up convoy again & dropped it at Aberdeen. We then proceeded back to Scapay Flow.
