<p>and marked A 4  that means that I am to go to my training unit until I come of age  Same day I was tried and given 2 days pay 10 shillings  I was also transfered to No 1 Coy to be drafted away</p>
<p>16/6/17<br/> I was drafted to Lark hill to 2nd T Bt  we intraned at Tidworth changed at andover and at Sailsbury  I had to walk 2 &frac12;  miles from amberry station with my kit  hot</p>
