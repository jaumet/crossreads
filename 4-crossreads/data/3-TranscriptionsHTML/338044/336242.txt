<p>15th August:- Tuesday<br/>Arose late as usual &amp; hurried to work. Looked clear &amp; bright weather until 9 am but during the morning the rain again came down in torrents &amp; this is a change after our Egyptian life of nine months. Plenty of work to-day &amp; we simply just went at it until 5.30 pm. Casualties in plenty for 19th Battalion. We received the full list to-day  of casualties sustained at Pozi&egrave;res by the Australians on July 25th &amp; 26th (two days)    &amp; until August 6th amounting to 6,600. What a record! Killed, wounded    &amp; missing. Again spoke to Capt Parker in regard to my application of transfer to the front &amp; it seemed as though I were to get small satisfaction.
