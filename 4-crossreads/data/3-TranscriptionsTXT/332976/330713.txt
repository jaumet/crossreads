
Aug. 1916.Four other Australians from the 3rd Divn Engrs, who arrived in England a few weeks ago, were in the school & two N.Z. Engrs. from Christchurch.16th September. 1916.Sat for final Exam of the school & finished up about 12 noon.  We stayed at Esher that night & left for Christchurch about 10 AM on the 17th.The school was a very interesting one & there was plenty of work to be done   The last week I was there Capt Courtman, the chief instructor, offered me a commission in the R.Es.
