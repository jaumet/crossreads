
May 1918May 1st
Matinee & Night show - Heavy work - Bouquets, or as one might better say congratulations from the Conductor of Orchestra - Comparison of "Coves" with Pellisiers "famous" Follies - letters received & post -Hammersmith Bridge.May 2ndTwo shows again today - I read "Ghosts" (a much discussed play by Ibsen) - Also selections from Byron - Much time spent in travelling from Highgate to Hammersmith.
