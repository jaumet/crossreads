  
 Heliopolis 3 Nov 1915 Darling Teddie, 
 I have only just received my first letters from you and mother One from you dates 24th August One from Mother same date One from Mother 7th Sept One from you no date in one of mothers date 14 Sept One from Mother 14 Sept One from Dear Edina September one from the [Moyens?] Aug 30th 
 It has been such a very long wait my darling, but it is good to get the letters at last dear one. I do hope you have got some of my letters by now I will write offtener dear girl but you cannot know what a little time I get sometimes not enough time to undress for bed not to get a bath for several days As Regimental Quartermaster Sergeant for this Battalion I am almost the busiest man here Our strength now is nearly 1400 men all of whom have to look after generally.
