<p>11 &ndash; two men had deserted the day before, so no leave was given. I sat beside him &amp; talked for two hours &ndash; finally Dr Stirling, the house surgeon came in and gave me permission to take him out for 4 hrs. I took him to lunch. He eat enormous meal, poor chap he's very thin. He said he hadn't had a feed for a year. Then we went over to see Fred at the Millitary Hospital from where Gibson had been transfered for eye treatment. Fred looks splendid but cannot get heel to ground &ndash; I doubt if he ever will &ndash; but didn't</p>
