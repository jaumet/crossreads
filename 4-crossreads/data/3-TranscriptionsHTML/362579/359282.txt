<p>Firing line.  Fred is out, just at present but not near us now. Have just has a present sent to me from one of his officers in another squadron not far from here. What do you think the dear boy sent me?</p>
<p>Some oranges, tin of fruit (pears)   &amp;  a cake &ndash; sent by his bat-man; said he got them at a M.C.A. so you see they get near the boys   &amp;  it is a great help to them.</p>
<p>We are not allowed to go beyond our tents, in fact, have not even thought</p>
