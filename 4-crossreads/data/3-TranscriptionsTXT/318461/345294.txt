 Wednesday 13/6/17 Route March, up at &frac14; to 5 shave, BK, &amp; off at 7.30. 10 miles, New Boots, home again at 11 blistered heels &amp; half crippled. curse these Route marches. Rumour that Ausy's don't go into Trenches any more &amp; that Peace is very near. Next. Pay. 14/9. &pound;26.19.0. Been a lovely day not as hot as the other march, Cool tonight. 
 Thursday 14/6/17. Cool morning but getting warm, off Tomorrow by Train, heel not so painful, &amp; not stiff this time. Pay. 15/9. &pound;27.0.0. going to Millincourt 9. a.m. Friday. Big battle last night heavy firing all along line from Arras to Peronne. 
 Friday 15/6/17 Gas alarm at 1 a.m. up at 5 off to Millincourt go to Bapaume then Train. about 20 miles then march about 6 miles back to billets again. Hot this morning. Pay 16/9 &pound;27.1.0. Marched through smashed up Bapaume. After being messed about for 1/2  hour got into train &amp; am still on it not moved off yet about 2 hours. Taube Bombed the line half an hour before we embarked still here 2.30 P.M. since 11 am off at last 5.30. 7 Bombs were dropped on line. out of train 6.15. marched 7 miles, feet blistered, Toes &amp; heels skinned curse the marches. Got here Millincourt 9 P.M. done up. 
 Saturday 16/6/17. On duty early, got another man 5 of us now so ought to be able to manage. Plenty of beer &amp; wine here &amp; drunks saw the first women &amp; kids yesterday for 6 months. This village hardly knocked about at all though there are trenches all round it. Got the boys to bed all right not much trouble. Pay 17/9. &pound;27.2.0.
