<p>On Tuesday November 20th A general and his staff from Soltau visited the Factory, In consequence the food that day was good.</p>
<p>On the night of Sunday the 20th the snow fell very heavy, and remained on the ground all day Monday.  Monday was very wet, cold, frosty &amp; foggy all day. Sunday snow was the first of the season.</p>
