<p>Same Day 7 p.m.<br/>El Arish is a mere village that is to say a fairly considerable cluster of mud huts with a large graveyard decorated after the native style with rags, bones, etc.;  and also a very respectable mosque with as good a minaret as is to be seen in many a larger town in Upper Egypt.<br/>The existence of El Arish is justified by the fact that it is the seaport for all the dates, melons, etc. produced in the upper half of the Sinai Peninsular:  no small item either.<br/>It was believed that the Turks were holding the place &amp; intended at any rate to make a show of</p>
