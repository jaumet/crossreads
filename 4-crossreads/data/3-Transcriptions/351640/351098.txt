<p>[On letterhead of the Majestic Hotel, Alexandria.]</p>
<p>27 October 1915<br/>2-10 p.m.</p>
<p>Dear Girls:/</p>
<p>Arrived &amp; landed here safely.</p>
<p>At luncheon learned of the torpedoeing of a ship in the Gulf of Salonika or thereabouts, and the loss of a number of Australian Nurses. You no doubt have had information by cable of the event.</p>
<p>Jerrom has gone off for a polish up. No room is available for me until 4 p.m. I shall fill up the interval finishing</p>
