<p>April, 1915<br/>Sunday 11<br/>Returned to Camp travelled all night in wretched train but I am glad to have had the opportunity to see so much of Egypt &ndash;<br/><strike>Hear today Myra Nyse has had a relapse I am sorry for her.<br/>We are all grieved to think the Infantry have gone &amp; we are left behind no doubt our time will come tho; it is hard on the men to damp their ardour;</strike></p>
