The troops then rested for some hours, and at daylight were refreshed with a good hot breakfast.<br/>The variation of the original plans evidently was contained in orders received early in the morning of the 30th August- the 5th Brigade to deploy across the Somme bridge at Feuillieres, to occupy the "Ellen"  system, and then to storm the ultimate objective. At 10.30 am the 5th Brigade moved out of billets, and <strike>after</strike> crossing the bridge at Feuilliers, resumed the action on the other side of the Somme, marching up hill and down dale, through marshy and boggy ground, until a main road was struck, along which progress was made until a linking up with the Third Division troops was accomplished.
