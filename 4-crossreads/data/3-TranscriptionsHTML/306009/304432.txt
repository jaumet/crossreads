<p>1915<br/>Sep. 7 Tue.<br/>For the past week have not been feeling too good. Paraded sick about 6 p..m. &amp; admitted to Ward 6 with tonsilitis.<br/>My case sheet in the ward says for the last 4 or 5 days has had a sore throat &ndash; to-day feels feverish &ndash; no headache &ndash; felt inclined to be sick from the stomach &ndash; no general pains &ndash; constipated.<br/>On admission:- T 99.6, P120, R24.</p>
