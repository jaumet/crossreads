<p>Friday 17 August 1917</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>Went Alexandria &amp; drew some money an expensive place this in one way as Officers are expected to be extravagant in many ways.</p>
<p>Feeling a bit better but will be here a few days yet.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through]<br/>Post this to let you know, there is nothing the matter with me &amp; a boat sails on Sunday, love to all. I sent another little Water Color &amp; some snapshots.</p>
