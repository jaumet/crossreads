<p>5 &ndash; all the boats are swung out -) and read <strike>y</strike> for a [indecipherable] &ndash; presently a steward came with some chairs &ndash; and I spent a very comfortable day reading in the sun. The sea is gloriously smooth.<br/>I slept from two to four after lunch. I feel it almost a duty to get the benefit of these last days before our work begins.<br/>The officers and the sisters have not spoken to each other. I do not know if they should or shouldn't. Our Colonel looks after us well and the Captain of the ship &amp; the Commanding officer have discussed our comfort with Mrs Wilson, the Matron &ndash; and are very considerate for us.</p>
