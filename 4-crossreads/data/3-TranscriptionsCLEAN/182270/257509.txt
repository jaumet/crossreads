 WEDNESDAY, JULY 16, 1919. 
 Fine. Drove over to Coomoo Coomoo 
 with Jo in little car for lunch then 
 on to Oakey Creek for afternoon 
 tea. Got changed at Oakey & got 
 to Binnia about 7 pm. Wedding 
 started at 7.45 and everything 
 went off O.K. Bride & 'groom 
 went to Colin Cameron's place 
 We danced till 4AM, then got 
 home to Oakey. It was a great 
 show, just like pre war days.
