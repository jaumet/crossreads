<p>went back to the billets, just like Jock, argue with anyone, so this morning I took his place &amp; put the day in at it.<br/>Got our new Colonel today  he comes from the 4th Bn. &amp; does not bring too good a name   Stacey's his name,   when Major Jacobs went away our band played him out to the tune of Auld Lang Syne. <br/>26th. Last night all the clocks were put on an hour, so we lose an hours sleep by it.<br/>Sunday again &amp; we had a Brigade Church Parade, but could not hear a word, where we were, right at the rear of the Battn.<br/>After the parade was dismissed there was a football match between 1st &amp; 2 nd Bns</p>
