<p><br/>1916 January 18<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Cooler today.<br/>Patient brought in last night suffering from concussion.  Removed to Garrison Hospital this morning.  Sergeant received letter from Jack Norton.</p>
<p>Lecture in aft on "Water.  Picked to go to Prince Alfred Hospital tomorrow.<br/>Go to tea with Rob. Welby. [indecipherable] to Tony's. Farewell to [indecipherable] "Tony" We all come down to Sydney after. I going to McT's to stay the night, boys into town.</p>
