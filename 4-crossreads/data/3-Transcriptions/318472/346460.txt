<p>23 August 1918<br/>Friday<br/>A fairly heavy breeze &amp; a rising sea getting up to quite a considerable roll by bed time. Nothing extraordinary though &ndash; just a few things being dislodged (seasick ch &ndash; Serg?) R.M.</p>
<p>1918 August 24<br/>Saturday<br/>A nice fine day &amp; the sea has calmed down considerably only a small roll now running. Nothing of importance to note.</p>
