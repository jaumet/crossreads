 11 September 1916 Monday 
 Carpentering work, palmstand. Frank C. gets transfer to nursing staff. Bob takes his job as mess orderly to patients. Our tent taken down &amp; shift into large oblong one. Fresh reinforcements blow in &amp; fill tent. Glee Club formed at meeting this evening  Cricket match between A &amp; B sec. resulted in win by batter.
