  April 24th  
 Early breakfast. 
 Make preparations and set out for the village.&nbsp; My brave &quot;English Mother&quot; - Our sad &amp; painful farewell. 
 Bid &quot;good bye&quot; to Mr &amp; Mrs Matthews, Honor &amp; Joyce whose great kindness is beyond my poor expression. 
 Motor bus to Lymington - Train to Paddington (changing at Brockenhurst)
