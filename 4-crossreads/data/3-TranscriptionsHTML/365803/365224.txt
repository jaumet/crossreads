<p>Not transcribed &ndash; Lettrhead YMCA</p>
<p>26th July 1916</p>
<p>Mr J.F. Mant No. 96<br/>2nd Dv Cylist C</p>
<p>My dear Judge,</p>
<p>We have just learned of Arthur's death and it has   <strike>filled</strike>  hurt us badly. Will you accept    my deepest sympathy? I was looking forward to a chance of seeing him as he was such an awfully fine chap. We can only carry on and hope that we can face what he faced in the same brave way. I can't express my feelings, Judge   <strike>and</strike>   but you know how I feel about it.</p>
