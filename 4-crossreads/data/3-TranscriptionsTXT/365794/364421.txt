 Sunday 21 January 1917 [1918] &frac12; day and went town with S. Richardson &amp; bought cakes etc. for Sakhara side &ndash; then 4 p.m. tea with the Maddens very nice home &amp; 4 white children present. Church 6 p.m., All Saints &amp; filled as usual with soldiers.  Mr. Holan did not preach but good sermon from someone else on dangers to these boys in Cairo. At 9 a.m. had camera in ward &amp; took snaps of our departing boys for Blighty &amp; C. Grant as well.
