Lindsay Graham has just turned up from his leave 2 days overdue.
24th. Nothing much doing today save that I had an hour of the dentists joyful company I'm afraid to think of the boring hooking, scraping, spitting etc. that I went through, in the evening Lindsay I went down to Warminster to do a little business, on the way back we met a couple of our officers had a yarn.
25th. Raining today quite a change to the hot weather we have been having of late, have put most of the afternoon in writing letters.
