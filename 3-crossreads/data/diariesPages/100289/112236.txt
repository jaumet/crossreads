Saturday 7.4.17
Kit inspection followed by issue of shortages. Paid 40 Francs. Afternoon at our disposal. Dinner given to all returned bearers at night also concert which was none too bad.
Sunday 8.4.17
Orderly Corp for the day. Issued with a new Aust uniform. Good number of sick coming through. Leave granted to 1 NCO 10 men per day to Amiens
