
Sunday 29 July 1917Stunt across Wadi got home about 3.30, watered up 6.30.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic number for 7].
Monday 30 July 1917Quiet day:  played 2nd Reg. got beaten:  very hot & dusty.
Tuesday 31 July 1917Very hot;  on mess orderly.  Out grazing all day.
Wednesday 1 August 1917Out grazing all day.  On Fatigue.
Thursday 2 August 1917Out Grazing all day, pretty hot.
Friday 3 August 1917On Pump fatigue, washing parade in Wadi:  (picquet at night).  Reached the dignified age of 24 precisely, Clarrie home for R.G.
Saturday 4 August 1917Nothing doing, on Q.M. fatigue.
