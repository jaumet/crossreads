 December  1918. Saturday 14 
 At Sea. H.S."Karoola." 
 Weather very moist and a slight sell running. A great number of the lads aboard feeling very seasick and looking it. The food is excellent in quantity and quality. Had a game of Loto. Purchased some stores for the trip. Left Sthampton roads at 11.30pm. last night and proceeded down stream We are fast entering the Bay of Biscay. &ndash; 7.50 pm. Wrote to D.B.F.
