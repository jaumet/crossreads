<p>8/3/18  La Basse Ville</p>
<p>About midnight commenced the rounds of the frontline posts and found them to be fairly good positions but thinly held. The men all fairly happy &ndash; sentries on the firesteps &ndash; machine guns flicking bullets across every now and then. Some German flares were being fired so close that they dropped right into the trench On the right the Lys bends right round our position and the enemy are on the opposite bank. A waning moon was reflected in the water of the river. The whole scene was desolate in the extreme It took us 5 hours to go right round the line and it beats me how runners and others find their way.
