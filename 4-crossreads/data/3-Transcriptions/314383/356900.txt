<p>during which time Chaildecott made a speech outling our policy of famine relief work, road construction &amp; levy raising.  The Gov &amp; Sirdah responded.  McLean foolishly brought his cigarettes which came in the mail to light &amp; the visitors blew them away in smoke in quick time.  Mac is now swearing beautifully in his room.  This afternoon he prided himself on being the only man with a supply of smokes (the rest of us are reduced to smoking dried Pomegranite skins).  The dinner was a scream.  Forks etc. were dispensed with &amp; we eat with our hands native fashion; anyhow we hope for something as an outcome from the dinner as Starnes is finding it mighty difficult</p>
