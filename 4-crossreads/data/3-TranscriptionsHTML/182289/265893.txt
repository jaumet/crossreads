<p>12.5.18<br/>Men paraded at 9 a.m. sorting stores.<br/>Church Parade at 10.30 a.m.<br/>Orders were received that O.C. Companies were to meet C.O. 3rd Aust. M.G. Batt. at Cross Roads N.26.b.10.90.<br/>O.C. left Camp at 8.45 a.m., remainder of day spent in making reconnaisance of ground in vicinity of Villers Bretonneux.</p>
<p>13.5.18<br/>Bath parade from 8 to 10 a.m.<br/>Inspection of arms in the afternoon.</p>
<p>14.5.18<br/>Company carried on work as per attached Syllabus.<br/>O.C. (Capt. D.A. Whitehead) visited Somme Defences in company with C.O. 3rd Aust. M.G. Batt.</p>
<p>15.5.18<br/>Inspection of Camp by C.O. and Second-in-Command, 3rd Aust. M.G. Batt.</p>
