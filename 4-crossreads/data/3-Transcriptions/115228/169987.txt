<p><u>Jly 28th &#39;17 </u>:- During the past</p>
<p>week our Brigade has</p>
<p>been acting as Duty</p>
<p>Brigade of the Division,</p>
<p>consequently we have</p>
<p>had a good deal</p>
<p>of reconnaissance work</p>
<p>to do capturing a</p>
<p>few prisoners during the</p>
<p>week. Today our Regt.</p>
<p>moved off from the</p>
<p>Brigade camp, situated</p>
<p>about 11/2 miles S.E of</p>
<p>Tel-el-Fara, &amp; took over</p>
<p>three redoubts further</p>
<p>round on the flank.</p>
<p>These are man every night.</p>
