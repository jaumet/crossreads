 11 May 1915 Tuesday 
 Several of our men sniped while getting water   &amp;  one killed by a shell They are working on a 24 hour system A   &amp;  B coys do one shift   &amp;  they come down   &amp;  C   &amp;  D coy take their place. We get the first real rain since leaving Sydney everybody gets sopping wet as we have only one W.P. sheet   &amp;  1 blanket each 
 1915 May 12 Wednesday 
 Get up damp   &amp;  cold   &amp;  try to make a billy of tea   &amp;  luckily having a supply of dry wood have a good hot drink w/- a ration of rum in it by six o'clock Our turn in the trench to night
