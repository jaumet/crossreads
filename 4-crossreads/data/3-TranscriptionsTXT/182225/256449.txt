 NOV. &nbsp; SUNDAY 26 &nbsp; 1916 
 Fine &amp; warm. We went for a ride in the Rotten Row in the morning &amp; it was rotten row with mud up to the horses fetlocks. M rs &nbsp;Angus Poss, Eric Murray, [Terry? Ferg?] Fullerton &amp; Self went out &amp; we had a good time. Saw Hugh M c Neil,&nbsp; Jack &nbsp; Warren , Gregory, &amp; Jack Ryrie in the row. I had dinner &amp; spent the afternoon at Aunties flat. Untie Mabel came in &amp; Auntie F &amp; Self went rouond to her flat after &amp; met the Countess of Carnarvon. Went &amp; said good bye to Kieth later &amp; Poss &amp; Self got the 7.30 train to Salisbury &amp; got a car home &amp; were here by 11 pm. Had a very enjoyable week end
