
[Saturday 11 March 1916]
Exchanged warm Warm clothing for summer things at J Cooks & Son & bought 500 Cartridges for my gun at 75 100 P.T. per 100-Went Heliopolis saw old Camp met Miss Myra Wyse & took her to Tea.  Mardi Ritchie was out.  The old Saturday night Dances are on again, at the Principal Hotels but I did not join in.  Got some boots & Trousers for the men.  Had several Hot baths & took back Mosquitoe Nets for all Officers-
