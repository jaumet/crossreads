 W 22 Sun I work all afternoon. Go for walk with T.W. &amp; E after Tea wind through D ' Con meet Ethel on Road &ndash; we nearly get caught in Rain Wrote Mum 
 W &amp; F 23 Mon Lovely day only showery. Go to see C. Chaplin a Dog's life. With B.B. V.W. TW &amp; E. Come home &amp; go to bed to read. Recd letter from Roy 
 W &amp; F 24 Tues. Tap comes home. Trains are on strike &amp; Patient are sent talk from Salisbury. Play at the mess have Tea once again &amp; go to bed to read 
 F 25 Wed Recd letter from M.R.L. Fox dies with meningitis &amp; I write all night until Last Post. Come home very tired Wrote Mr Havercraft. 
 F but dull 26 Th Recd Parcel from Bassie X. Have a long busy day. Come home &amp;  read all  Write to M all night [indecipherable] up about calcium. Go to bed &amp; Read Recd letters from Jennie. Wrote M.R.L but did not post until 3.10.18 
 F 27 Fri I move to [indecipherable] Hut &amp; go to Dunnington for walk. Tea at Mrs Seawoods Meet Tommy officer &amp; Prains &amp; Mr Church of Plough Inn. Tea after Piano &amp; walk nearly to Bulford with H.  Come home &amp; play Billiard &amp; go to bed early 
 F 28 Sat Lovely Day but I am too busy to go out. I have to write 2 letters by morning.  Meet at 7.45 &amp; walk home to Dunnington &ndash; Lovely clear night &amp; I thoroughly enjoy walk. 
 Wrote Mrs Edmonds &amp; Hilda.
