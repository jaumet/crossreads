<p>1917 Monday 3 Sept.</p>
<p>Lights switched out 10.30 p.m. &ndash; Enemy aeroplane raid.   Bombs dropped on Boulogne but more near here.<br/>Took the lassie to Wimille tea at Madame's thence walked to Happy Valley back to Madame's for omelette.   arrive back Y.M.C.A. 7 p.m.<br/>Duty at 8.</p>
