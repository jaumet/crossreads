
11 May 16Arrived at Kingston Jamaica in morning watch & tied up alongside the pier. Coaled off Collier Loriga, natives coaling in baskets. Ceased coaling at 1 pm on account of rain. Port watch ashore.
12 May 16Finished coaling ship at 1.30 pm went ashore at 5 pm for 36 hours leave.
13 May 16Ashore in Kingston Jamaica. Spent a pleasant day.
14 May 16  Sunday Came aboard at 7 am. Port watch went ashore at 1.30 pm for 36 hours leave
15 May 16Lying alongside pier at Kingston. Port watch ashore all day.
16 May 16 )17 May 16 )  Lying alongside at Kingston
