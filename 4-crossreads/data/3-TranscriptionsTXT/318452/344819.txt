 Sunday Feb 3rd On Tuesday Jan 29th I received instructions to proceed to Weymouth for Embarkation and caught the 11.55 from Dinton arriving at Weymouth about 4 oclock Next Morning I left by troop train for Plymouth &amp; we travelled through Exeter The day was fine &amp; bright &amp; mild.
