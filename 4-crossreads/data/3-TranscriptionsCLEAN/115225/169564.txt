 May 16  th    We went to Ailly-Sur-Somme for supplies, but had to go on to Vignacourt for oats, we got to the brigade at 1.P.M. & rested there till 5.P.M & returned to Park. 
   
 May 17  th    We left park at 5.A.M. & took supplies from Ailly.S.Somme to Frechencourt returned to Amiens & took a load of gluecose to Saleux for a froggie, got back to Park at 7.P.M. Fritz comes over every night with bombs 
   
 May 18  th    We left park at 7.A.M. for Flesselles railhead on amunition & took one load to Corps reserve dump - got back to park at 6.P.M. & moved the park to Belloy-Sur-Somme three kilos past Picquigny. arriving 10.P.M. we unloaded our echelon of amunition & parked down the road behind the Church. 
   
 May 19  th    We went out on Supplies to Ailly-S.S. railhead & Frechencourt. but they had no hay at Ailly-S.S - so we had to go to La Folie past Flex-icourt, we went to Frechencourt Via Conde la Folie, I met Eric at Frechencourt
