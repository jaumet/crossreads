
Wednesday 14/3/17.Our Musical Competitions were continued & the Events decided were Impromptu Speech. There were 5 entries & the Winner turned up in Private Peter Hodd of the Trades Hall, Perth. The competitors had to draw their Subject from which was on a piece of paper from a Hat, the Winner drew for his, Is life worth living, & Peter made a splendid speech & was given a great reception when he had finished the other Speeches were, The Girl I left at home, Is married life happy & Curiosity. The Afternoon was put in well also the evening Card playing.
Thursday 15/3/17.The Weather now is glorious cool & refreshing. I spent the morning reading & after Dinner
