<p>21 Friday</p>
<p>Came on to drizzle in afternoon. Sister Saunders &amp; S had tea in Serbian camp with Prof. &amp; he gave me a Serbian Badge. Dancing in evening in mess tent. During forenoon an A.J.C. boy was brought in with both hips dislocated. Pollard &amp; I had Matron's tent till 3 p.m. she copied music for me.</p>
