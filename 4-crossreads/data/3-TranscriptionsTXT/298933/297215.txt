 [December 1918] 22 Sun At Hammersmith 23 Mon Wandering about London 24 Tues Xmas eve I visited the Princes Theatre "Jolly Jack Tar" good show, and then I journeyed to Hampstead and spent the evening with Mr &amp; Mrs Pugh 25 Wed I attended Westminister Abbey and when the royal party were leaving the church they stopped right by me, "Mary P of Wales looked nicely at me &amp; I looked nicely at her and smiled Xmas dinner at Wimbledon
