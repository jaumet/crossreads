 4. Thursday F. P. called up. Met him at Oxford Circus at 1.30. Went to see " Old Glory" &ndash; the U Jack was floating over H. of P. &amp; other public buildings &amp; then spent the afternoon at Battersea Pk. Brought him home to supper. He has had Spanish flu &amp; was most astonished by sickness. Feeling dead exhausted. Lovely day. 
 5. Friday. Grey day. Sun in afternoon. Went to Peter Robinson &amp; bought pink suit then to Miss Fairburn's dancing with Maud but had to leave &amp; crawled to bed very ill. Vomited and head bad. 
 6. Saturday. In bed all day. Unable to go out for food or cook anything. Weak &amp; terrible depression.
