<p>Spt 1 box of cigars for 10/-, &amp; they are a tip top smoke. Patiently waiting for pay, expect to get 1 quidlet.<br/>Still plenty of birds keeping us Company, some of 'em only little uns too.<br/>Boat parade at 2 pm &amp; of course I fell in on the wrong boat. Recd the large sum of ten bob.</p>
<p>2 Head wind &amp; sea, &amp; rather chilly. Paraded to the Doc &amp; he gave me some pills of aloes. Played several games of deck quoits just to fill in the time like. Good cigars which I got in Capetown. Concert tonight, &amp; better</p>
