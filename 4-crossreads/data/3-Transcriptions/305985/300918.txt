<p>were clean and free from vermin guess the reaction would be more than I could stand.<br/>I think I ll turn in only one more defaulter call to answer thank God .<br/>I hope he chokes when blows it.</p>
<p>Sixth day of my defaulters just put me on fatigue but was not in the humour to do any digging so I pulled out suppose I will be up again tomorrow<br/>Saw Walter Christian last night he looks alright. They say we are going to move out on Wednesday and speculation is rife as to our destination personally I don't care where to . After all I have posted that letter to Miss R. I hope it does not reach her .<br/>I put it in Lills letter and forget to take it out.<br/>I don't think that things can be too good at home so have written to Griff</p>
