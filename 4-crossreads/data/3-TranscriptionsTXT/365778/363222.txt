 Monday 1/10/17 Moved forward &amp; took over front line from 33th Battn. Enemy very active. Tuesday 2/10/17 Relieved by Norrie at midnight 34 casualties stunt on morning of 4th. Wednesday 3/10/17 Final preparations at Bellewarde Rdge for stunt. Thursday 4/10/17 Moved to Zero (6 pm) to Westhoek &amp; then to St Josephs eventually forward to reinf. Norrie &amp; 26th, a bit of a misc. Friday 5/10/17 I personally reorganised the whole Brigade &amp; got things straightened out had some nasty close ones relieved by Basil (17th)
