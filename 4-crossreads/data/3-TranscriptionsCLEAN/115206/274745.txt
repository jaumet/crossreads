
June 1918Minday 24
On the road, Ebevnville.Weather very dusty, cold winds. Left billets at 7.30 am. And pulled into final camp at 12.50 pm.Walked into Pont Remf with Smithy, Gunn and [indecipherable]. Had a very decent snack in a farm close to railway station. Walked home through Friedcourt.Reg Harris and a few others returned to Battery. Teddie Dunn also.
