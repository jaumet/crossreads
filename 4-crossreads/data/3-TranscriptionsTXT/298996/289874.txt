 Sunday 4.4.15 Daybreak brought forth a beautiful sunrise it was  glorious  glorious Hardly a ripple on the water to day. Hot a the So &amp; So.  Had rather harsh words with the [indecipherable]  Church Service this afternoon We met another rainstorm to day which cooled the air for about 5 minutes.
