 C/o Miss Conyers Matron-in-Chief A.A.N.S. Horseferry Rd Lon don 28.1.17 
 My dear Moth. &amp;  Sisters 
 Received Ethel's also Nurse A's letters with Eddy's usual love letter, which I was more than delighted to receive. Am so pleased the darling got on so well   &amp;  trust he has improved in every way since. Hope you were all able to get a holiday this Xmas.
