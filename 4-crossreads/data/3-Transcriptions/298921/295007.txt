<p>February, 1915<br/>Wednesday 3<br/>Got home to A42 at 3.30 A.M. we had a rough passage back to ships in a "Felucca" manned by Arabs Head wind &amp; rough sea we tacked backwards &amp; forwards for 2 &frac12;  hours &amp; got wet through &amp; chilled to the bones.<br/>Alexandria a great city of learning in the past is now very squalid in places &amp; cosmopolitan Many Refugees from Jerusalem are here &amp; wounded soldiers are brought from the Continent.</p>
