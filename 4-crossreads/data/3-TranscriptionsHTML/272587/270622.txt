<p>1917</p>
<p>Jany 1st  Dull &amp; raining and in no wise different from other days.<br/>Jany 3rd  Move back to Buire.  Saw George yesterday &amp; he is OK.  Warned to-day for leave on 27th inst.<br/>Jany 5th  Still at Buire.  Lately it has been nice and fine to-day Fritz has been shelling the ridge above the village, further back than I have ever seen him before.  Last night at the Dandies at Merricourt<br/>Jan 10th  At Buire weather cold and windy with rain.  Visit Merricourt again last night   Lately the bombardments north of Thiepval have been very severe &amp; rumours of an advance &amp; prisoners are many.  Rumour has it that Baupume has fallen but it lacks confirmation.</p>
