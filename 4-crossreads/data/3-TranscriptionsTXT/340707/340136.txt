 I then have dinner at the aerodrome &amp; return to London now the worse for the adventure. I now want to get into a submarine &amp; then I shall have completed in water &ndash; under water &amp; in air. 
 4-6-19 Visit Harrowby although this is Derby Day but I do not know the horses &amp; do not see the use of wasting all days in queues &amp; being crushed especially as I have little money to spare. 
 I visit Mrs Talbot of 32 St George Road Eccleston Square with Mrs Gore for luncheon. She is one of the Buxtons &amp; a Miss Buxton is present the exact replica of Mrs Rutson.
