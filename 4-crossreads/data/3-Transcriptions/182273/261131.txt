
<p>1918 &nbsp; May</p>
<p>23 THURS &nbsp;Change. Weather cool</p>
<p>&amp; heavy wind. Posted</p>
<p>letters to Amy &amp; (Maggie)</p>
<p>Julie &amp; Tense. Very quiet.</p>
<p>Cricket match. &amp; two</p>
<p>bands playing. Writing.</p>
<p>Sent letter to J.W.C.</p>
<p>24 FRI &nbsp;Raining &amp; very cold.</p>
<p>Rec. letter from Celie.</p>
<p>Very quiet day &amp; night</p>
<p>Writing.</p>
<p>25 SAT &nbsp;Fine day but cold.</p>
<p>Posted letters to Thelma &amp;</p>
<p>M<sup><u>rs</u></sup> Mooney. Amm. going</p>
<p>up. Capt. Malley paid</p>
<p>us a visit. Had a good evening.</p>
