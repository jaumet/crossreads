<p>while every dark object or dead man was regarded with suspicion, as Fritz was up to all sorts of ruses to take prisoners. Here &amp; there we gave them a dig in the ribs with the muzzle of the Rifle but they were beyond recall.</p>
<p>The next day we were asleep in our dugouts when one of our 18 pounders started to drop short and dropped 3 shells right in our trench. The first did no damage the second blew in Corpl Wells dugout wounding him in the lower part of the leg. The third landed right on the Vickers machine Gun position, but except for blowing a few sand bags away no damage was done.</p>
