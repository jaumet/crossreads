 2 Th August Very easy Guard at Water.  relieved at 6.30.  Regiment marched out laying cable to Khan  back at 12 night 
 3 Fri Very lazy all day.  Leaving for [indecipherable] at 6 pm  about 16 miles 5th Reg carrying out stunt  we took out post on W. Imlay  cold as charity  left W. at 4 am  for Fara 
 4 Sat Very sleepy and tired  having a good old sleep
