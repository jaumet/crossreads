<p>Leaving the Canal we passed thro a constant succession of fertile fields &amp; picturesque villages, Arab Cemetaries, innumerable clumps of date palms, large herds of sheep &amp; goats feeding together &amp; minded by women &amp; children.  We also saw water buffalo &amp; camels, donkeys &amp; mules, cows &amp; horses all at work, carrying produce to market, <strike>or</strike> turning water wheels or pulling the little carts they use to thresh the wheat.<br/>The whole place looked like a constant succession of Chinamen's gardens &amp; this in a country whose average annual rainfall is less than <strike>an</strike> one inch!
