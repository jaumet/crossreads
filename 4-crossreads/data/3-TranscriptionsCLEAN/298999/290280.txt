  
 Saw gippo hare.  followed the track 15 miles due east and they lost them said they got covered over by sand from the storm we had dinner 11.30 and returned to camp which we reached 3 P.M.  unloaded Put camels away had a wash I was dirty too covered in sand.  had tea 5 P.M. Biscuits and Jam a man is getting very little rations here.  on line guard to night.  on 3rd shift 1 till 3.
