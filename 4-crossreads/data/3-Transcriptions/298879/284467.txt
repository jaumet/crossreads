<p>November 1915<br/>Sunday 14<br/>Cool breeze blowing. We relieved the 9th Bn out of their old position. We got a good pozzy for the 'phone. Violent artillery duel about 1600 to 1700.</p>
<p>Monday 15<br/>Showery settled down in our dug &ndash; out for another artillery duel one man killed too. A fierce Naval bombardment at Achi Baba this afternoon which lasted about two hours.</p>
