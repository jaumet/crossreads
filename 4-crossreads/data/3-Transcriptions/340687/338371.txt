<p>March, 1917. Thursday 8<br/>Reveille 6 &amp; parade 6.30 when we marched round for "physical drill". Rifle parade 8.30 &amp; dumped bottles till 12. At 2 fell in &amp; cleared old barb wire. Dave threatened to make some of us &ndash; laugh one of these days when half didn't come out for work. Bridge in evening &ndash; Extremely cold wind all day &amp; everything froze. Slept better</p>
