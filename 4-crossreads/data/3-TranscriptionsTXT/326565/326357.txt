 Saturday 6/5/16 Heavy day.  Fatigues the whole of the day &amp; till rather late in the evening.  Col Shepherd very strict regarding discipline &amp; dress.  Will not allow shorts to be worn, much against the wish of the boys, for the weather is now so warm.
