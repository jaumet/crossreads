  
 Wednesday 26/9/17 Spent day with 26th Bn who are very pleased with stunt 2 Off Killed 3 W. Thursday 27/9/17 Rode over to see Bas also had a talk with Hotchkiss the General's old groom. Friday 28/9/17 Had letter from Doff & also wrote to her. Dinner with 2nd Div farewell to A.D.Ms 28th Bn bombed - took over command. Saturday 29/9/17 Pow-wow about new attack made all arrangements for move. Sunday 30/9/17 Left by Car to reconnoitre front line near Zonnebeke [Belgium] returned & rejoined 28th at Canal Area.
