 Monday 13/8/17 Married 3 years today. Wrote a long letter to Doff. Visited Billets at tattoo. Tuesday 14/8/17 General Training, paid visit to Billets. Band played well. Wednesday 15/8/17 Raining again, gave a lecture to N.C.O.'s &amp; men. Thursday 16/8/17 Took battalion for 16 miles Route march, no one fell out, had a swim at Arques on the way. Friday 17/8/17 Battalion Drill on the Distillery Grounds &ndash; Good &ndash; Had dinner with Slane at Brigade H.Q. Wisdom returns tomorrow &ndash; wrote to Doff.
