<p>plenty of shipping. several Troop ships in Harbour Natives in sailing boats selling sweets, papers &amp; pcs. A few melons also available. Prices fair. Am on guard today. Just our luck. Fairly warm<br/>18th Entrained at Suez for Cairo at 11.15 am Fairly hot. Few Imperial Officers, Natives &amp; Indian Police to twig us &ndash; the former to judge, Ay what? Train travelling fairly fast but rocking - I am perched on back of seat trying to write x (suburban seats) x  Canal (lined with banana groves grapes corn etc) on right for first few miles. Mail boat just passed bound for Suez</p>
