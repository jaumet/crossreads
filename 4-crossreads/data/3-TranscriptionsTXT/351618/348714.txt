 I do not know where our Guns have gone but assume they have gone on to Egypt.  We are now living well &ndash; cooking good &amp; plenty of extras we can purchase. 
 Monday 20th Dec I forgot to mention that in getting the Beer the other day I forgot to mention I asked the officer to have a drink &amp; they would not serve me as it was for Officers only. I had to see my officer friend drink his first while I looked on &amp; paid for it. 
 Where our Baggage Guard is camped is on the side of the road &amp; we have officers enquiring every day who we are etc.
