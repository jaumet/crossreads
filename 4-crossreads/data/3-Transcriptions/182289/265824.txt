<p>Le Bizet<br/>(6) Twin Fm. C.12.a.<br/>At 7.10 p.m. one gun was emplaced at C.25.b.18.43 for counter machine gun fire coming from C.16.b.2.8.  This gun was afterwards moved to C.15.d.5.8 for the same purpose.  No. of rounds fired 1250.<br/>Enemy artillery inactive.  Machine guns showed more activity than usual during the earlier part of the night.  Aircraft Nil.  Weather squally, strong wind, visability fair to indifferent.</p>
<p>23.2.18<br/>Lieut. F. Thompson reported back from leave.<br/>At 4 p.m. 300 rounds were directed on Working Party at C.5.d.30.05.  The results of the shoot were not seen however, owing to poor visability.<br/>Between 7 p.m. and 3 a.m. 900 rounds were expended on the following targets, by one gun,<br/>Track, C.11.d.18.15 to C.11.d.02.20.  Coy. H.Q. (inspected) C.6.c.20.45.  Twin Farms, C.12.a.65.48.  Enemy M.G. position, C.17.a.72.60.  Cemetery Drive, C.11.d.52.52.  Circle Point, C.12.c.25.20.  Converging Tracks at C.5.d.30.05.  (Working party seen during day.)</p>
