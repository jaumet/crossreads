<p>March 1917<br/>15 continued   counterattacked &amp; some of our advance machine gun positions evacuated to the front line.   He was driven of &amp; we got a few prisoners.<br/>The casualties in the front line have been very small lately, but around here, within the last four days, we have had over 80 killed &amp; wounded.<br/>16th   Fritz's shelling has been a bit quieter to-day, but still, he didn't forget to let us know he was still alive.   A great number of our "plumb puddings" went down to the line from here this afternoon &amp; it is rumoured there is to be an attack tonight.</p>
