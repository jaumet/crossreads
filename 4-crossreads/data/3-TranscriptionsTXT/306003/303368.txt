
27-5-16word came along that A Troop were sighted about 6 miles out, so we came back to camp everyone calling Lieut Mills choice names.  we lost no time on the way back arriving here at 8.30 watered & fed our horses & finished tea by 9.45;  For the 16 hours we were away we only had a little bully beef & a piece of bread.  The sand flies are getting very troublesome
28-5-16Sun 28thSlept this morning & had nothing to do but read this afternoon but were told at stables that C Sqdn had to leave here at 6 oclock & go out on outpost duty;  We have to run a telephone out from the outpost & T into the wire running from Bde Headquarters to the Pinnacles;  B Sqd also goes out but in a different direction
