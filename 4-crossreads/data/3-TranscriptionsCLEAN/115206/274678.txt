
April 1918Thursday 18
Bussy - la - Daours     D.R.At 6.30 am I received a call went across to Bonnay, Battery moved.Came home and had a quiet day.Went out on a run to Blaney Tronville and La Motte, during night Fritz tried a stunt against 41st Batt, - he certainly came a cropper.Played cards until midnight - won.
