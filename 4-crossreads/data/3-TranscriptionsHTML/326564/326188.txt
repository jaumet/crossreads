<p>5th.Rained fairly heavy again last night &amp; when we moved off this afternoon it was very wet &amp; sloppy, one of my birds done a get, he was missing when I called my roll previous to moving off, so I went &amp; had a look in his hut but the bird had flown sure enough</p>
