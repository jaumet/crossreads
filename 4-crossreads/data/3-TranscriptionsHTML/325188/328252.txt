<p>August 6th (Continued) duly inoculated with anti Tetanus Serum, one more puncture.<br/>August 7th No3 A.G.H . Sisters to wash one, clean sheets, same old charts &ndash; I know the routine well &ndash; this my 3rd time wounded in a month I hope to go to England &ndash; Very stiff &amp; sore but am really thankful to be alive x see notes x <u>IN DIARY</u><br/>August 9th I am much better &amp; everything going well &ndash; the officers got it pretty hot &ndash; Here the nurses are mostly new ones but all are very kind but one has to do what one is told promptly &ndash; Several officers are insensible &amp; all night you can hear them rambling on about machine guns bombs etc<br/>August 10th (See Diary)<br/>August 11th a number of men have been in to see me "bushies" mostly &ndash; I have got very fat here. Same routine &amp; tucker as in No1. This Hospital was at [indecipherable] &amp; the Sisters all experienced they cursed the L.H. keeping them in Egypt till the [indecipherable] came, now they are happy &ndash; got something to do. Some more LH officers came in last night knocked out by camel rides after being wounded &ndash; its hell in those "Cacolets" &ndash;<br/>August 12 Getting on well &ndash; hope to get up today.</p>
