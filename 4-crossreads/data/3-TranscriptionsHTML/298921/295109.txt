<p>May, 1915<br/>Sunday 16<br/>Col Meredith &amp; I went up to the Nile in a [indecipherable] or Felucca with some English people we have met. I feel it is no time for pleasure jaunts with our mates fighting across the sea, but it was very enjoyable &ndash;<br/>I did not post as stated on the 10th as I expected to have news to tell you but post tonight &amp; trust it May reach you safely.<br/>Posted you Egyptian Paper yesterday.<br/>F.W.</p>
