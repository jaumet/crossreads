<p>Monday 24<br/>SYDNEY CUP. DAY Usually (with me) a day of gambling so not to be out done I will x50Pt a chance at 2 up<br/>Tuesday 25<br/>Anzac Day, very warm, was put by in good fashion, washing wishing whistling &amp; walking cheers Jack<br/>Wednesday 26<br/>Warmer still, but it agree's with me, like Johnny Walker I'm still going strong<br/>Thursday 27<br/>I am getting well fed and I eat a hell of a lot getting stout and feel very fit</p>
