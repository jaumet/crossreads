 WEDNESDAY, JUNE 18, 1919. 
 Fine & cold. Father & Mother & Peter & I 
 went into Quirindi in the big car. 
 Father & I attended the court & were 
 fined 5 & costs for not killing rabbits 
 They went home for lunch & I stayed 
 in. Went up to "Woodlawn" with 
 Owie & Laurie & played tennis all 
 the afternoon. Caught the 9.30 train 
 to Sydney at night.
