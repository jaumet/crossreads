<p>Ships Officers whom was on watch answered (Balls) instantly the Cruiser came along side of us <strike>a</strike> asked again a he got quick &amp; brisk then they let us go without any further ado.</p>
<p>15.4.15<br/>Wednesday<br/>The ship has Slackened it pace some what to day because we have passed all the ships going to the <strike>Sewy</strike> Suez that is that left Aden a day or so before us. The weather is a little cooler, I went down the stoke hole this <strike>evening</strike> afternoon to get a sweat up before I was down amongst the boilers, as so <strike>once hit</strike> started to shovel</p>
