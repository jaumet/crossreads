 Chap lll   Egypt. Mena Camp 22 December 1914. 
 We are settling down on the sands of the Libyan Desert where it is hot in the day time and cold at night.  The sands of the desert do really get cold so the song is right after all.  Ive been into Cairo a couple of times and I was on a train between there and Alexandria for the first 10 days after we landed.  It is a strange city and I think all the flotsam and jetsam of this earth must have gathered together at one time and sent here.
