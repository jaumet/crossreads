<p>23-3-17<br/>Officer whos name I do not know.  During the attack Fritz kept heavy shelling with H.E. explosives both shrapnel &amp; percussion.  We held the position with very few men.  29th Batt. held Bometz &amp; 30th in support.  Good support is coming in today.  Major McArthur has given our Secn. high praise for the support we gave the Inf. this morning.</p>
<p>24th<br/>Further fighting has been carried on amongst the outposts.  A good deal of shelling going on on both side.</p>
<p>25/3/17<br/>Fritz made another attack on the left flank of Beaumetz this morning.  We repulsed him but had fairly heavy losses.  This afternoon the 59th Batt. made an attack on our <strike>our</strike> Right flank doing fine work running Fritz out of his position &amp; occupying a sunken Rd. near Beaumetz.  We suffered fairly heavy losses from shell, shrapnel and M.G. bullets.</p>
<p>26th<br/>Did some reconnaisance work for water at Velu where some beautiful old Chateaux have been mined &amp; layed in ruins.  This morning 2nd Div. attacked &amp; captured Lagnicourt on our left.  Fritz attack four times during the day to try &amp; regain it</p>
