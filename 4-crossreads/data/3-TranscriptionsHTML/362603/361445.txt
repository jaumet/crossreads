<p>Australian Red Cross letterhead.  [This letter predates the one above by 2 days.]<br/>Bulford<br/>22.4.17<br/>Dear Dad,<br/>I wrote to Mum last Sunday &amp; have been going to write to You all the week.  It is now My dinner Hour &amp; I will try to answer Your letters.  I am going to the Village Church about 2 &frac12; miles away to-night &amp; I will not have the chance to answer to night.<br/>On Easter Tuesday the King reviewed all the Australians camped Hereabouts on the Bulford Field about a mile from Our Hospital.  We could view the whole of the operations   Ellis dressed &amp; went over &amp; got into a good position right at the Saluting Base.  He went as a spectator not as a Soldier in the March Past.  He was able</p>
