<p>17. Sunday<br/>Stayed in house feeling a bit better but very depressed. Foggy.</p>
<p>18. Monday<br/>Feeling a bit better &ndash; weak now &amp; again. Fog</p>
<p>19. Tuesday<br/>Fog. Feeling better. Put in good industrious day.</p>
<p>20. Wednesday<br/>Sun Bedad! Unbelievable! After tea came to Miss Hodge. Feeling very weak. Lugging bag too much.</p>
