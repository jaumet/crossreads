<p>Later in the day, the <strike>1st</strike> 3rd L.H. Brigade, a Yeomanry Brigade, a Brigade of New Zealanders, &amp; the Australian Camel Corps arrived here. The beach has been found to be infested with mines &amp;  today our Squadron Serg. Major (Geo. Shepheard) &amp; Corp Struthers were blown to pieces by one which had been blown up on the beach.<br/>Dec 22nd A Cruiser , a couple of Monitors &amp; about 5 mine sweepers have been traveling all day &amp; fished up a number of mines.</p>
