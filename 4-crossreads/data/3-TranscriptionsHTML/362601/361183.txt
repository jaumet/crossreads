<p>3 April, Thursday</p>
<p>F.<br/>French draft in again. C.O. blows me up about Extension Report. Go to English &amp; Book-Keeping Class &amp; come down to write letters.</p>
<p>Afterwards, I go to bed at 10 pm &amp; do all night about W. at S. Hill. I am then fatigue.</p>
<p>Wrote Miss Scroggins, Dad &amp; Auntie Annie. Sent Brooches &amp; Coins to Auntie.</p>
<p>4 April, Friday</p>
<p>F. &amp; Hot</p>
<p>Go shopping &amp; to take my washing. Go to Dickens Lecture &amp; is very good. Meet Cal Slater, come back to office &amp; write &amp; study. Do some English later on.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L. &amp; Mais. Carter.</p>
