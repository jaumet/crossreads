<p>Postage will be delivered at your house Friday night. 22/5/90. A.F.</p>
<p>26th May, 1890.</p>
<p>Dear Arthur,<br/>On the occasion of your marriage we desire to accompany our united &amp; very cordial congratulations to Carrie and yourself with a memento of our warm interest in the event, and trust that a dinner service will be both acceptable &amp; useful, &ndash; ever reminding of the good wishes for a truly happy union which are herein sent by</p>
<p>Yours affectionately<br/>Jessie Herbert.</p>
