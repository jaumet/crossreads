<p>108 Ord Services Small staff has been attached to No 3 &amp; 4 Supply depots to deal with Ord questions all indents from with in these Sections will in future be handed to ofr [officer] i/c who will in turn indent in bulk on the DADOS in exactly the same manner as the indents on the Beach Supply tfr for Ration.</p>
<p>109 Courts Marshall<br/>When a term of imprisonment or detention has been awarded  by a Court Marshall proceedings should be at once forwarded to DHQ who will send it to the II JAG &amp; the prisoner should not be evacuated to the Base until DHQ's sanction has been obtained.</p>
