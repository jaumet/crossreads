<p>19/10/16 Crossed the line</p>
<p>24/10/16 Arrived at Dakka had feed of fish</p>
<p>2/11/16 Arrived at Pymouth &amp; went to Wareham</p>
<p>3/11/16 Went to Hurdcott via Southampton</p>
<p>24/11/16 London four days leave. Met Jack Wirth &amp; went to Butchers Ernie dead.</p>
<p>11/12/16 London again to bank. Good trip home.</p>
<p>14/12/16 France at Boulogne (Blanket Hill)</p>
<p>15/12/16 Etaples</p>
<p>23/12/16 Buire to battalion Good Xmas dinner. Spent New Years Day with (Archie) Griff, Ben &amp; Strange.</p>
