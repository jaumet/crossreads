
Friday 16th:  RelievedTuesday 20th:  Back again to BiefvillersWednesday 21st:  Hun plane brought down containing Prince Fredrick of Bavaria who later Died of WoundsFriday 23rd:  In old Hun dug-outs in quarry.Tuesday 27th:  Relieved. Came back to BazentinWednesday 28th:  To FricourtSaturday 14th:  In support trench NorreuilSunday 15th:  Huns broke through 1st Div line & came up sunken road behind us. We inflicted very heavy losses on him with rifle & machine gun fire & rounded up a few hundred prisoners
