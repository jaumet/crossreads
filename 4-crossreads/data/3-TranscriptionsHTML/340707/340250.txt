<p>At Maison Lafitte we had an example of the triomph of commercialism over [aestheticism] The Chateau de Maison constructed in 1717 in the midst of a beautiful park was acquired in 1818 by Lafitte a banker who lacked sentiment &amp; cut up parts into small building lots which he sold at high prices in order to keep it select &amp; incidentally realise more. The chateau itself is isolated without even a tree to relieve it &amp; fenced in by an iron picket fence it seems a prisoner or a criminal guilty of some crime.</p>
<p>Disgusted we turn homeward &amp; I have just finished selecting a few pc when before I can pay the town supply of electricity fails or is probably cut one is never certain here where respect for law is always lacking.</p>
