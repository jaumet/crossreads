 Saturday March 22nd Codford 
 caught the 7.30 am &amp; arrived in London at 10.30 : went to the Aldwych &amp; cleaned up : joined a party to see the guards procession : the[y] got a good welcome especially the Prince of Wales : concert at night at the Aldwych theatre &amp; very enjoyable 
 Sunday March 23rd London 
 went to Petticoat Lane in morn &amp; had some fun down there : stroll through the Horse Guards parade in afternoon seen the Hun tanks &amp; then strolled down Rotten Row &amp; around the Serpentine : pictures at night 
 Monday March 24th London 
 stroll round during day : out to Harringay at night &amp; had a good talk : am going there to stay from tomorrow 
 Tuesday March 25th London 
 went for a stroll in morn : to Windsor Castle by invitation in afternoon &amp; were supplied with refreshments by Princess Alice &amp; other ladies : nice : took photo of her daughter, Lady May Cambridge : Castle &amp; grounds interesting especially the old belfry : went to Eton College &amp; seen all through it : out to Harringay &amp; had an "evening" of music etc : pleasant
