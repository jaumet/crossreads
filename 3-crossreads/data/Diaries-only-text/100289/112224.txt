Tuesday 6.3.17
Met Colin Mitchell the two Hawkless's Fritz landed few shells in Albert during the morning
Wednesday 7.3.17
Cold windy. Bob Roberts transferred to Anzac show. Endeavoured to find No 2 Coy A.S.C. but failed. Scotch band played in Albert at night.
Thursday 8.3.17
Snowing. Boys still in the line likely to remain there for some time.
Friday 9.3.17
Rather quiet on line. Concert party showed at Meaulte. Still snowing.
