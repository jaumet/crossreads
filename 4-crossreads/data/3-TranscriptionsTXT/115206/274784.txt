 August,1918 Friday 2 
 Ribemont. Bty Position At 1 am Fritz again put a heavy general barrage down. I think we shall have a stunt from him shortly.  No stunt but a heavy front line strafe with Minnies. Weather fairly moist all day. Right section took over at 9 pm. Up till midnight nothing had happened at all, save a slight bombardment . Clive and Johnnie Walker went down to W.L.
