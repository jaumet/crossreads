<p>FRIDAY, JULY 11, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Drove down to Boundary</p>
<p>&amp; carried on with the fence. Got</p>
<p>the netting up to 4D almost now.</p>
<p>Saw Kimber &amp; he took my offer</p>
<p>of 20/- for 250 pick of 350 of</p>
<p>his sheep.</p>
<p>Tickle helping me at the fence.</p>
