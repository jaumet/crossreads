  
 Friday 4 February 1916 N.C.O's class;  had a go at drilling the troup and got on pretty well.  After breakfast we were drilled by a Sergt. who did not know drill but was most careful we knew it, gave me the blues.  Extended order drill with Squadron. 
 Saturday 5 February 1916 N.C.O's class then ordinary drill till dinner time.  Came to Sydney had dinner at Sargents then over to Mosman. Found Hal & Freddie & Edward here and decided to go to White City. Party of ten of us went and had great time, all went twice on Scenic Railway, got home about 12 o'clock.
