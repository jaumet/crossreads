<p>1917 Sunday 17 June</p>
<p>Trotting round as usual all last night. Some more evacuated &amp; some fresh admissions. Get up at mid-day &amp; in afternoon.  Mac. Jock &amp; I go into Boulogne. Tea at English shop. thence board tram to Le Portel fishing village. Stink never was there anything like it. Portuguese officers &amp; --- Old fish wives &amp; men. Jock takes couple snaps of beach &amp; plage. Back to B &ndash; tea at Soldiers &amp; Sailors Home. Wire taken a "jerry" to French Cafes &amp; shops with their exorbitant prices. Buy a 10 fr. watch Invicta. Home in time for duty at 8 p.m.</p>
