<p>Taxi, so caught milk card to station.  Train moved out at 6.30 Am, sad sight to see all the "Tommies" once again leaving their people, raised a lump in my throat for a time.  Arrived Folkestone at about 8.30 Am &amp; went into billets to await the departure of the boat.  Marched to Pier &amp; went on board about 2.30pm, left under escort at 3.30pm arriving Boulogne in the heavy rain at about 5 pm.
