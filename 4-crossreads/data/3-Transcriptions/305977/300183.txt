<p>Tuesday March 2nd<br/>Warm day, very dusty on parade.  Regimental mounted drill in the morning.  In the afternoon instructions in fire control &amp; concentration on given points.  Our troop was inlying picket.  We turned out very well &amp; were complimented.  We were turned out at 4.30am &amp; stood to arms until 5.30.</p>
<p>Wednesday March 3rd<br/>Was mess orderly.  Got in some time in the morning to write letters.  There are a lot of rumours about leaving here next week for Palestine or Smyrna to operate against the Turks.  Had a spill in the afternoon.</p>
