<p>October 1918.<br/>Tuesday 15</p>
<p>Ahead of Busigny Bty Position<br/>Weather wet and showery all day. Bty did a few small duty shoots. Tom and myself went forward to dig a position for our advanced pit. Had a fairly easy day considering the circumstances.<br/>Had a nice long shift 12 to 4 this morning . expect to be moving up very shortly to a position 800 yds behind the line. One section is to, I believe go forward.</p>
