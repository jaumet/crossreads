<p>Novr. 20th 1906</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Fry,<br/>Next month's issue of the Journal will be a Christmas number. I shall be very thankful if you will give me a contribution for that number. I am desirous of making it worthy of the Service and attractive to the readers of the paper.</p>
<p>Very truly Yrs<br/>John Osborne</p>
<p>A. Fry Esq<br/>Mines Dept<br/>Sydney</p>
