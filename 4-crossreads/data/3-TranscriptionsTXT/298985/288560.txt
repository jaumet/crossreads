 down an Infantry chap came along &amp; sayed we might as well come down  to there Dugouts as it was safer. Just as we got there the barage started &amp; seemed to allmost take the paint of our Tin Hats. The Dugout was about 20 ft deep &amp; dry so we were dry &amp; comfortable for the time. As there was too much smoke &amp; fog to see any distance did not move the Tank again in fact doubt if we could have done so as the ground was soft &amp; stickie. About 7 our Officer sent us home.
