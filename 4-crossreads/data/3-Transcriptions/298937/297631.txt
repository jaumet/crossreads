<p>Tuesday 1st August</p>
<p>Packed up remainder of stores in the morning &amp; held an embarkation parade of whole of 2nd Division, before dinner.<br/>By 2.20 our dispensary marquee was struck &amp; all in readiness.<br/>Marched to Station at 6 &amp; left at 10.<br/>Rotten night in train packed like sardines.</p>
