<p>parted &amp; Jock &amp; I went down the town &amp; had a look at the pictures "for an hour" or so.</p>
<p>The day was very hot &amp; muggy &amp; I think that we must have had about 4 washes during the afternoon &amp; 2 or 3 lots of tea it seems funny over here you are only allowed one helping of food, this is a law that has just been passed in England.</p>
