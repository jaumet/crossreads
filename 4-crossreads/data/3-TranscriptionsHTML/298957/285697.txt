<p>6 p.m. all troops attended a Memorial Service held in connection with Lord Kitchener's Death. It was a most impressive scene. At the end of the Service the Dead March was played, followed by the buglers blowing the Last Post.</p>
<p>14th June (Wednesday) On Piquet but managed to get over to see the Divisional Sports meeting. It was a fine display resulting in our winning the "Limber" entry.<br/>Met Hughie Steanes, Cornish &amp; Reg Cannon. H.S. was telling me that at Ferry Post the other day the temperature was 127&#176; in the shade. "Tommy" regiments</p>
