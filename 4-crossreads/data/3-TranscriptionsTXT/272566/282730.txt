 Thursday 13 December 
 Misty &ndash; Greasy Roads Dark at 4.30 p.m. Working at the bike all morning. After dinner I took the A.D.M.S. Run and continued on to Bailleul via Ravetsburg Took the 6 a.m. casualty wire and another this afternoon to 15th Bgde. Sigs. 1st Div. Troops are now arriving to relieve our 5th Division &ndash; 14th Brigade &amp; 14th Field Ambulance moved today for a destination near Etaples.  The 8th Brigade &amp; our Ambulance move on the 17th inst. (Monday)
