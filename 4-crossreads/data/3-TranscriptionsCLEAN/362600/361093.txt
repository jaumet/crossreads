  
 F 7 Sun Hot. Dopey day. Play with the cricket Ball all night & enjoy myself immensely. Send photo of Southampton & of Room & [indecipherable]  & of  myself at Eastleigh. Wrote Mum 
 F 8 Mon Very hot but lovely at night. Poor May goes away & Siddie  &  I play cricket  &  have supper with Top. Wrote Dad. Mum 6  & George. 
 W 9 Tues. Heavy Rain Storms etc. Read all night & go to bed at 9.15 am in daylight. First time all Summer with one exception. Draw up new standing orders. 
 W 10 Wed Heavy Rain Storms & Hail. Get organised & dressed to go to Picton Go with T. Park. Paint Hall E & I all around Beacon Hill in bush walk. Wrote Roy. Norm. Bess & Aunt Annie 
 W 11 Th Torrential Rains. Joe is [indecipherable] & sleeps in our Room. Write all night. Col. Goes on leave.  Ellis goes playing [whist?] its keen. Wrote M.R.L Wrote Doris. Maisie. Carter. Peg. Cyril. Bert 
 W 12 Fri Recd Socks, Chocs  &  Cigs from Maisie Carter. Rains like Babylon &  I stay in room & read all night. T.B. Wright   & I play with cricket ball & we get sore hands T &  I go for our  & [rall?] wicket 
 W 13 Sat &Fine. Go to Sarum at 2 pm. Go to 13 & close [ice?]. Have nice time &  dinner at YM & Osmonds. Ride home in 45 minutes. Go all over old Sarum it is a wonderful place.
