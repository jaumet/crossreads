 Go to Ottos's for plants. Ida &amp; Nat come down &amp; kids. I take 'em all paddling in creek. Gee they get in a mess. I dig &amp; plant all day. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie. 
 6 October, Monday 
 Dig &amp; plant all morning. We go to Dee Why in afternoon. Meet Geo Lil &amp; Myrtle. I am very quiet. I am better after [indecipherable] &amp; watering my garden. Amuse Dad &amp; Mum until nearly 9 pm.
