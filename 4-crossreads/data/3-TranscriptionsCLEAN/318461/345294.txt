
Wednesday 13/6/17Route March, up at 1/4 to 5 shave, BK, & off at 7.30. 10 miles, New Boots, home again at 11 blistered heels & half crippled. curse these Route marches.Rumour that Ausy's don't go into Trenches any more & that Peace is very near. Next.Pay. 14/9. 26.19.0.Been a lovely day not as hot as the other march, Cool tonight.
Thursday 14/6/17.Cool morning but getting warm, off Tomorrow by Train, heel not so painful, & not stiff this time.Pay. 15/9. 27.0.0.going to Millincourt 9. a.m. Friday.Big battle last night heavy firing all along line from Arras to Peronne.
Friday 15/6/17Gas alarm at 1 a.m. up at 5 off to Millincourt go to Bapaume then Train. about 20 miles then march about 6 miles back to billets again.Hot this morning.Pay 16/9 27.1.0.Marched through smashed up Bapaume. After being messed about for 1/2  hour got into train & am still on it not moved off yet about 2 hours.Taube Bombed the line half an hour before we embarked still here 2.30 P.M. since 11 am off at last 5.30. 7 Bombs were dropped on line.out of train 6.15. marched 7 miles, feet blistered, Toes & heels skinned curse the marches.Got here Millincourt 9 P.M. done up.
Saturday 16/6/17.On duty early, got another man 5 of us now so ought to be able to manage.Plenty of beer & wine here & drunkssaw the first women & kids yesterday for 6 months.This village hardly knocked about at all though there are trenches all round it.Got the boys to bed all right not much trouble.Pay 17/9. 27.2.0.
