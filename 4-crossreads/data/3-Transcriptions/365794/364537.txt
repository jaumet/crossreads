<p>Wednesday 16 [15] May 1917 [1918]<br/>Town with Sisters Soar &amp; Furnifull.  Tea at Club then shopping.  Drew pay Pt. 300.<br/>Wrote Nell long letter.<br/>Wrote Mrs. Simpson.<br/>Wrote S. Leed.<br/>Questioned M.O. re this last attack.  No dysentery germ but a lot of colitis going around, probably that, no fear of recurrence.  Going out to Bulkeley in a day or two.</p>
