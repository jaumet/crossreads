<p>July  1917  August</p>
<p>Only one small light shows on the stern of each boat.</p>
<p>Monday 30th<br/>Put Wet equipment in forward hold today, Well out of the way. Did some washing Lecture on Ropes &amp; Splices Borderer had gun practice</p>
<p>Tuesday 31st<br/>Sports on deck. Crew have started putting awnings up Lecture on Knots</p>
<p>Wednesday 1<br/>The Hororaia pulled out of line today &amp; fired 3 shots for practice. Roumer of course says there was a buryial Concert this afternoon. Lecture to N.C.Os on Map Reading</p>
<p>Treevers YMCA man had tea with the 1st &amp; spoke later He seems a good sort &amp; there is plenty of room for him to improve most of us. The</p>
