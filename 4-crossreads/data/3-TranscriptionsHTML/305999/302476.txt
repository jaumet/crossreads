<p>We got told that the H.M.A.S. Sydney had sunk the Emdon at Cocasiselan we where under full steam and between Calambo and Albany when it happened it was lucky for us</p>
<p>16/11/14<br/>We arrived at Calimbia it is a nice little port and the niggers can run some with the rickshaws they are very nice to ride in there is plenty of cocunats and oranges there they come out to the ships with them</p>
