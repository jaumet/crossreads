<p>General Concentration Camp<br/>Holdworthy 28th June 1916</p>
<p>To,<br/>Messrs. Bungardy, Thomzick, Korth &amp; Villiers<br/>Prisoners of War. Holdsworthy</p>
<p>Sirs,<br/>Your letter 28th Instant is received, the contents of which are noted.</p>
<p>Your letter, with the two previously received will be forwarded to the American Consul General in Melbourne.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>W S. Stuart Major<br/>President. Court of Enquiry.</p>
