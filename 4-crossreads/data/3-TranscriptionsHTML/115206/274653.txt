<p>March 1918<br/>Sunday 24</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Arose at 6.30 and prepared breakfast, worked on souveniers until 10 pm.<br/>Another glorious day, Charlie Adey and Gunn went to W.L.<br/>At 12.15 pm orders came across wire that M.G.s had to report home again<br/>At 1.30pm we packed up all our kits in readiness to "Emshee" to another Bty possie near Oostenhaven Wood.<br/>11.45 pm arrived to our destination and took over from some fellows of the 15th Bty .Damn rotten possie with no dugouts.</p>
