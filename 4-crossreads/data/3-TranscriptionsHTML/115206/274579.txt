<p>January 1918<br/>Wednesday 9</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Heavy fall of snow, weather very cold and bleak.<br/>Carried out usual routine during morning<br/>Our battery played 55th and won easily &ndash; Australia rules<br/>Relieved Gun [Gunn?] on Runner work and did a few trips to Bdge Headqrs, at Attin</p>
