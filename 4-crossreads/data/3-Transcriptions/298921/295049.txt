<p>March, 1915<br/>Wednesday 17<br/>Projected move is knocked on the head we hear owing to Constantinople being evacuated, however we go on a 4 days Bivouac soon &amp; all are ready to go Marseilles England Syria or the Canal &ndash; All horses now clipped tell H.L.A. the Cob (young one) has learnt a lot &amp; is a good horse. I have been lucky to keep my 3 alive &amp; well.<br/>Letter card from May re Ralph Arnold I will look him up also one from Joe &amp; enclosure from Mother of one from Laura addressed to Deniliquin.</p>
