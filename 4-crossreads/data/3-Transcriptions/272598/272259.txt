<p>Sunday 17th to Tuesday 26.2.18<br/>Depot life not too bad, far better than I expected rather monotonous though.  Go to Warminster rather frequently to visit YMCA.  Applied for four days leave which I expect to get on Friday next.  Do practically no drill, whole of time taken up with fatigues &amp; piquets.  Good number of our boys are here in the depot, saw Sg. Matheson this morning.  Big drafts going to France from Training Btns &amp; O.T.B.</p>
