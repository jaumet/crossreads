I.B. Res. Brig.Forest Row
1 Oct. 1917
Dear Mrs Jack,
I am afraid I haven't written since Aug. the 6th.  I have been saving all letter writing for when I get on the ship.  As I expect you have heard before this that I was one of the lucky ones detailed for India.
I have now been waiting 6 weeks &amp; have heard nothing so far, been at this spot two weeks as I have 140 men to pick up here to take out with me.  There is some talk of a P &amp; O going this week taking passengers &amp; troops so may be on her.
