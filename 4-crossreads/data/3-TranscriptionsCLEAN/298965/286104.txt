 Diary 1914 We left Warwick for Ennogera on Sept 24th for Pinkenba & at noon on same day  left Pinkenba by TS Omrah for Melbourne where we arrived on 27th at midnight & anchored out in the bay four days & then proceeding to the wharf after stopping in Port Melbourne for 3 weeks we again shifted out to to resume the voyage to Albany. During our stay in Melbourne we had a fairly good time of it doing a good bit of drill in the way of skirmishing. After leaving Melbourne we struck very fine weather the sea
