<p>Monday Oct 21st I arose at Reville at 6.30 am &amp; after breakfast 4 of us Australians &amp; myself paraded &amp; left by the Staff Special for St Omer at 8 am &amp; then as the personnal did not leave until 10.35 am we all went up the street for a stroll where I bought several cards to send out to Aussie for Christmas, we returned at 9.45 to the Lord Roberts Church Army Hut for a cup of tea some biscuits, when I left my mates &amp; went along for a cup of coffee at the Estaminet for the last time, as it was before hours I had to keep in the back ground until 10.30 when I left the family &amp; went to the station &amp; waited for the train to arrive, as it was running late due to heavy traffic on the line, it came in at 11.45 am &amp; soon we were aboard &amp; left at 12 noon, after a good run only stopping at Audruincq &amp; arrived at Calais at 1 pm &amp; after a wait of 15 minutes, off again, to Marquise having to climb a long &amp; heavy bank arriving at Marquise at 2.30 &amp; off again after 10" delay passing through Boulogne to Pont de Brisquet arriving at 3.45 &amp; after detraining</p>
