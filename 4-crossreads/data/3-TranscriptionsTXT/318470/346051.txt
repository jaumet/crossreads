7th January 1917Still on Road Supervising.  Cold &amp; wet.  Things going well.  Nothing fresh going.
8thThings going well on Road work.  A good deal of shelling going on.
9thTransferred from 14th F.C. Engrs. to 1st Anzac Light Railway Operation Corps.
10thTook up duty with C.L.R.  Things very much mixed up at present.  Expect to go better before long.
11thBeen having a very strenuous time.  Things are very much mixed up owing to overlapping of the old &amp; new formations.
12thThings are not working at all smoothly. Having a rough time trying to cope with work.
13thStill doing plenty of work trying to get things going on the Railway work.
