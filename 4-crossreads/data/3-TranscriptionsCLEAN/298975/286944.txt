  
 Aug 1917 passed today. Got our water bottles out today have to carry them full all the time, but not of Rum 
 Wednesday 22nd Had a full Marching Order Parade today Got my fiver out of Orderly room today. Small steamer passed this evening Fair sea running strong W wind. They are making out a  long  list of all our trades or kinds of work we have done. Mine is a fairly long list 
 Thursday  23rd Fair sea and much cooler. But am still wearing shorts
