A sympathetic Constable asked if I could identify anybody.   I answered in the negative &amp; said I had determined it was a Case of "Good bye, wallet."  Fortunately I had a few pounds in my inside field dressing pocket.   So I had to put in a modest holiday   I was relieved from any actual shortage of necessities by an arrangement with Mr A.C.
