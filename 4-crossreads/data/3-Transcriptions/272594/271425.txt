Hqrs. &amp; there the Dr. dressed the wound &amp; an A.M..C. man took me down the road a little way &amp; showed me the way to the next dressing station reaching this they directed me to a place where the wounded caught ambulances.  had to wait here a fair while &amp; saw some horrible wounds, got away at last in a horse ambulance &amp; so ended Easter Sunday for me.</p>
