
Saturday Jan 15thCold & windy up till 6 am then a fine calm day. On guard till 8 am : passed a light house on the African side at 12.30 am & another at 6 am proving to be on a large island passed a hospital ship From thence on we kept in sight of land : sometimes on both sides : packed our kits & pack : also passed some islands : still volcanic looking; now in the Gulf of Suez : land on both sides up the gulf. passed red channel lights & arrived at Suez 7.30 pm : A fine sight: two hospital ships with their row of lights all round "Karoola" & "Marama". One had green &' the other white lights all round & a red cross
