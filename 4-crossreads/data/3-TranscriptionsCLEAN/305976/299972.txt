
Sunday March 3New Zealand band at Richon. Only band in Richon division which sticks to DivisionMonday March 4Rode over 1ST Brigade to see Sgt Beny[?]. found he had gone to hospital with bad knee. Received paper & mail from HomeTuesday March 51ST Brigade moved out to day. Supposed to be returning in few days. Will be glad when we move for a change.Wednesday March 6Very windy to day. Rumours of moving in few days.
