<p>31st March (Friday) Fearfully Hot. Digging rubbish pit. Swim in Canal. A rather good road and a light railway known as the Anzac Desert Railway run out some 9 or 10 miles from here to Rail Head.  The train engine is run by petrol. The 5th Division is now being concentrated at Ferry Post. Met Day &amp; Sherring from Mosman.</p>
