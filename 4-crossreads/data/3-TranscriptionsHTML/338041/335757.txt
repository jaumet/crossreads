<p>Nov. 25th.<br/>Shelling of Albert &ndash; very bad weather &ndash; "Our Officers" &ndash; what we think of them!</p>
<p>Nov. 26th.<br/>A visit to Amiens &ndash; The first trip to &amp; from &ndash; Trams. Buildings etc &ndash; First tram ride since Cairo &ndash; Many cards sent home &ndash; "The Cathedral &amp; its magnificence".</p>
<p>Nov. 27th.<br/>Parcels from Home &ndash; Squad drill &ndash; Fritz on the Roads &ndash; also the Anzacs. Treatment of prisoners &ndash; miserable weather.</p>
<p>Nov. 28th<br/>Long route march &amp; squad drill &ndash; The irony of it all &ndash; Mail of 16 letters from A.S.W. &ndash; Xmas Cards &ndash; Bad news from Roumania &ndash; Sad news of Sid Goode.</p>
<p>Nov. 29th.</p>
