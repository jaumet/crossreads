<p>12 February 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>6am Rise   &amp; stow Hammock<br/>Beautiful weather in sight of coast all day<br/>1pm Ship begins to Roll a bit<br/>Plenty of wind all night<br/>2a.m. Deep Sea pilot comes aboard</p>
<p>1915 February 13<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>8 am Arrive at Pinkenba sea port of Brisbane</p>
<p>Find sister Troopship Seang Bee already alongside wharf<br/>11 am Brisbane Troops come aboard<br/>Take in provisions   &amp; cargo all day<br/>5.30pm Seang Bee pulls out<br/>6 pm Seang Choon follows<br/>Many cheers   &amp; Auld Lang Syne<br/>(Play Banker   &amp;  win 6/-.)</p>
