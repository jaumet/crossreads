<p>Friday 17 March 1916<br/>Barked the rails in morning.  They were very tough on account of drought.  In afternoon we all cut chaff.</p>
<p>Saturday 18 March 1916<br/>Finished cutting chaff.  In afternoon Dick drove over to Warrobil with Geoff who is returning to Sydney.</p>
