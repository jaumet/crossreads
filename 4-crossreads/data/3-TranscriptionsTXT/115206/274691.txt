
May 1918Wednesday 1Bussy La Daours.Bill Aldred died of wounds at 11th C.C.S.Sympathised with Fred.Weather fine.A great amount of air fighting one of us was dropped handily, and another of Fritz came a wallop.Two more Fritzs dropped.Received 13 letters from home and elsewhere in Aust very welcome they were . one from Roy very cheerful . Going to Southall Hospital Rained at 10 pm.
