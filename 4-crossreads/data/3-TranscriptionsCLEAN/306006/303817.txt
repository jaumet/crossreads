  
 13-4-17 Fri 13th Last night the troops were digging trenches all night; I had to send out sigs to two observation posts early this morning, all the horses were led into the barley for grazing this morning; We were in touch with all squadrons . B.H.Q. & the 2nd regt per Helio & had some good practice; At 9.30 Sutherland & I decided to visit the Obervation posts as we could get a view of Gaza from one of them, both Posts were on top of deserted mud buildings; lizards of every description can be seen on any of the buildings & there is nothing inside any of them but remnants of broken earthenware
