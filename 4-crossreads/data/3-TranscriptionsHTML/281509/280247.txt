<p>1916 January 24 <br/>Monday</p>
<p>Come down to Sydney again this morning.  Knocked around all day, bought 2 prs. pyjamas &amp; bathing suit.<br/>Went to Annies &amp; Idas to tea.  Thence called at  Robbins about 9 pm.  Found them out but they came along in a few minutes. Had a yarn. &amp; some passion fruit<br/>Home to camp at 11.30 pm. <br/>Two peculiar dogs on street<br/>Go to Swiss Studios to get photo taken in overcoat</p>
