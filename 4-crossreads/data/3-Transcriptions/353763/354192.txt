<p>draw their pay regularly &ndash; from our government  -  Seems queer don't it? They got 2 month's advance report says  -  4 Companies &ndash; 120 to a C - of soldiers &ndash; our men &ndash; &amp; some 500 men &ndash; remain in Raboul  -  They picket the place &amp; look after it generally  -  Colonel Paton is in charge  -  Brigadier Holmes has gone to Wilhelms Haven with the Berrima  -  Next we saw the native police  -  Some 20 in number paraded &ndash; rifles &amp; a costume of Kharki with red braid for a loose tunic  -  Some had pants &ndash; others only loin cloths  -  However they did their drill smartly  -  Their Sergeant was a fine buck Papuan who yelled at his small squad <strike>in the</strike> most efficiently <strike>manner</strike> &amp; was obeyed in the promptest manner  -  Off they marched  -  We then boarded the prize   -  the Madang  -  I went thro their medicine chest &amp; got some Quinine  -  She was captured on the high seas &amp; will be sold  -  <u>They say</u> all prizes will be sold &amp; the prize money allotted to the Fleet  -  [indecipherable] shall have mine if I get any at the end of the war  -</p>
