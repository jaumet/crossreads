<p>July 26.   Drill in morning, bath-parade after dinner.</p>
<p>July 27.   We went through a stunt today in trench work, especially with regard to the relief of bombing parties against counter-attacks.</p>
<p>We marched three miles to the scene of operations, (which, by the way, was one of the trenches actually occupied a few months ago,) and returned after dinner feeling very hot &amp; tired.</p>
<p>A cool bath proved to be an excellent antidote.</p>
<p>July 28   We packed up camp this morning and by 3 P.M. not a stick or a stone was to be seen on the ground which we had occupied.   At this hour we set out on an 8 mile march for the railway line.   As the day was extremely hot we felt the march</p>
