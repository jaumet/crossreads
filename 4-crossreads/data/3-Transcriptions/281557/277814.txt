<p>New Years Day Saturday Jan 1st<br/>A very calm day : Sunny &amp; hot : Revellie 6 am : Two birds were following the ship; the first since our Australian feathered friends left us. Had my wrist dressed with iodine Vic came out of the hospital : Sports on board &amp; went off a great success. They consisted of :- tug-of-war, scratch pulling, pillow fight, cock fight, treacle bun eating, floury mop etc. The pillow fight had over ninety entries &amp; as the pole was over a large canvas tank with about four feet of water the contests were very amusing : especially when</p>
