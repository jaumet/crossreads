30/9/17 Very cold night. Up 6.30am then all busy getting ready packing up for the line. Received 2 letters from home dated 11/3/17 17/6/17 also 2 parcels 1 letter from Uncle Will. All men not going to line marched out, full packs, about 12 noon marching around the outskirts of Steenvoorde St Sylvestre-Cappel arrived 5.15pm at Morbeck just outside Hazebrouck through which we marched. Hazebrouck is a very fine town with good buildings ? has a big square right in centre of city.
1/10/17 Cold all night. Heavy fog early in morning. Nice fine sunshine. Paraded 9.30am after roll called
