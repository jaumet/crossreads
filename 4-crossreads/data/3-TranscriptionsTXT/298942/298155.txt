 indications I should judge that we will be in the line till Fritz has finished his push.  Any day he may commence, &amp; that he will push us is as certain as can be. He will not cease trying til all means have been exhausted, &amp; though he may not commence here, it is certain that he will try here sooner or later for Hazebrouk &amp; the coast.
