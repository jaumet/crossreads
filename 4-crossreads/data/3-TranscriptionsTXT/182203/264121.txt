France.                                                                                                                                                                              (Nearly mid-night) 2. 12. 16. My dearest Mother &amp; Sisters., No Australian mail for me this week again, no doubt shall get one beginning of next week, but it seems a long time between drinks. Well, I'm on night-duty still &amp; likely to be for a few weeks longer. Oh, it is cold, you would be surprised at the amount of clothing I'm wearing. Bought a pair of Kharki knitted leggings, could not get gaiters nor black ones but these are great &amp; go alright with my tan boots.
