<p>After the attack was finished we were all "fallen in" &amp; marched back to out tents all thoroughly tired out<br/>Had another Mess "meeting last night to settle a few questions &amp; to draw up rules for the "Mess"<br/>Starkey &amp; Whittaker came back with a draft last night, &amp; Whittaker was shoved into the "clink" straight off   he has been over 2 months overdue with his "leave" so he's looking for trouble I reckon.<br/>20th. Roads are very slippery &amp; hard owing to the severe frost which settled on the land &amp; the whole countryside is covered with a white mantle but the snow is no longer fit for snowballing for it is frozen hard, it looks comical for all over the joint can be seen huge</p>
