
May 1918Monday 27
Battery W.Lines.Weather pretty decent.Wrote Stell.Went during afternoon for the usual swim. Albie a little better.Knipe professes to be ill.Percy Grayling visited the lines at 4.30 pm. Heard today that Archie Innes was wounded recently."Gibbie" is also in hospital The Y.M.C.A. hut or rather dugout opened with much entertaining speechmaking and singing. Fritz planes avc bombs as usual;.Fritz shelling Pont Nooyelle and Querrie fairly regularly.
