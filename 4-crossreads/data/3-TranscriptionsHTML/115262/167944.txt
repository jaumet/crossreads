<p>Tuesday 26 June 1917.</p>
<p>The parades this morning were under Lt Col. Croll. our O.C. (orig 2nd L.H.F.A.) &amp; he complimented us on our marching etc. He said it was best of any Fd Amb. that had come to Egypt. Allotted to diffeent <s>divizions</s> sections&nbsp;for active service in the field. I was allotted as Stretcher Bearer. George Cameron &amp; I are now separated in this respect.</p>
<p>Issued with plate &amp; pannikin &amp; khaki shorts during afternoon. Up to this date since disembarkation we were compelled to use our mess tin for all meals.</p>
<p>Mess orderly for B mess.</p>
