<p>Knights &amp; Knighthood</p>
<p>In days of old when knights were bold,<br/>They wore tin suits that tinkled;<br/>And it could seem rain with might and main,<br/>But these suits never wrinkled</p>
<p>In days of old when knights were bold,<br/>Whene'er their tin suits rusted<br/>They used a cake of Monkey Brand<br/>Except when they were busted</p>
<p>In days of old when knights were bold<br/>And tin mitts needed pressing<br/>A Tin can opener was required</p>
