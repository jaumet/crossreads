 April, 1915 Thursday 15 Posted a lot of snapshots of Luxor &amp; myself &amp; Horses hope you may receive safely &ndash; Recd letter from May 22/2/15 from Beerwah, I have written but she may not receive it so please if you get this do so the pet Rabbits belonging to the Infantry who have left are now camped in our Lines a white one in my Tent &amp; I give an allowance of Horse feed daily &ndash; trek postponed till next week. Better today, weather getting hot. I think it is the Desert sand getting to ones innards.
