On Monday Wal called up for me where I was working & as I happened to have the afternoon to spare we set off to hunt Rid up.  We found him eventually, after a mistake as he has shifted to a new position on the line, & by the time we got home we had walked close on 10 miles & my word I  was  wasn't sorry to crawl into bed that night.
