<p>September 1918.<br/>Saturday 7</p>
<p>Bronne. And a position in Reserve line.<br/>Weather fine and clear during morning, heavy rain in afternoon. Walked across to B.H.Q. and received a nice surprise on having my pay book readjusted.<br/>At 1 pm we took to the road again passing through Cortigny and Le Mesnil towards Beaumetz. Rested and had tea in a gully beneath a very old chateau. Proceeded to the Bty Position  and secured a good dug out. In communication at 8.50 pm. With 14th Bdge.</p>
