  Friday 4th May  17 miles, but I was in no way tired by it.  In fact I went to a concert with Aleck Nunan in the evening. 
 Friday 4th May Today was spent in lectures to give us an easy time after yesterday. The morning lecture by Cpl Merrilees dealt with the circulation, &amp; the afternoon by the same, dealt with the digestion.
