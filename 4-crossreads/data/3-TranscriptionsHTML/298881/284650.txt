<p>past quaint <strike>ly</strike> thatched-roofed cottages with ivy or the beautiful Virginia vine clambering all over them.  Clematis and fuschias grew in profusion in all the little patches of gardens.  Here and there a velvety green paddock with sheep nibbling the short grass.  Anon we pass a field in which bewhiskered old sports are tossing hay at each other and building very neat hay stacks.  After a large supper we went for a motor drive out to Corfe Castle a distance of about 7 miles.  The evening air was delicious.  We drove along the high road all the way</p>
