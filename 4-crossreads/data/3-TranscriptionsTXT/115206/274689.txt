 April 1918 Monday 29 
 Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours   D.R. Weather wet 4am. Fritz made a very heavy attack at Villers Bretnovino. Gained 100 yds. 5th Mobile Vet section moved out. At 5 pm an enemy aeroplane was brought down intact and the pilot and observer captured. The machine was intact and I believe belonged to the circus of the German Flying Corps. Heavy stunt commenced but ended early.
