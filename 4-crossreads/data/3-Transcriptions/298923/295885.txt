<p>7 August 1918 Wednesday<br/>Out at the Hospital the wee wife &amp; Bairn both so well-   I am very thankful all has turned out so well.  I hope you got Cable.  Dolly is so good &amp; helping to nurse another Lady's child here.<br/>I enclose you the Military message I received "<u>in code</u>" !!!<br/>Love to all.</p>
