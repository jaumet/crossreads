<p>26 Aug, 1916<br/>And then he reached Et Maler<br/>And started his advance<br/>But found against Australia<br/>He had no ------ chance.</p>
<p>He thought that with his rifles<br/>He would easily get through<br/>But found that these were trifles<br/>To old Man Kangaroo.</p>
<p>He brought up reinforcements<br/>And snipers by the score<br/>But found this to his loss meant<br/>Our rifles spoke the more.</p>
<p>He tried to get around us,<br/>But here he tried in vain<br/>And very soon he found us<br/>In front of him again.</p>
<p>He cried out allah! allah!<br/>As through him cold steeled seared<br/>But discretion more than valor<br/>So Allah disappeared.</p>
<p>And Abdul taking Allah<br/>As leader good &amp; true<br/>He also did a 'yallah'<br/>From the wily Kangaroo.</p>
