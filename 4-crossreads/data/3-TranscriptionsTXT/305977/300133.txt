 Wednesday 18th  [Nov] Nothing about McDermott.  Looks as if he isnt coming back.  Bayonet drill all the morning.  Captain McLaghlen Instructor.  In the afternoon independent troop drill mounted.  Fairly cool day rather cloudy. 
 Thursday 19th Full regiment parade in uniforms with rifles.  Cinematographer operator took pictures of the regiment marching past.  In the afternoon went out to Holsworthy with an advance party of 9 from B Squadron to help get things fixed up.  All leave stopped at night.
