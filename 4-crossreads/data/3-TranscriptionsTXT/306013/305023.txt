 To the Ocean. 
 Roll on, thou deep &amp; dark blue Ocean &ndash; roll. &hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip; Thou glorious mirror where the Almighty's form Glasses itself in tempests;  in all time, - Calm or convulsed, in breeze or gale or storm, Icing the pole, or in the torrid clime Dark heaving &ndash; boundless, endless &amp; sublime. The image of Eternity; the throne Of the Invisible&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;. 
 Thou goest forth, dread, fathomless alone. 
 Lord Byron.
