 Thursday 12&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; Bairam festival in Cairo today The Mohammadams in their best clothes in the streets.&nbsp; In Cairo for one hour between 5-30 &amp; 6-30 p.m.&nbsp; Sent a cable message to the girls, A telegram to Weston, Orsova, Port Said&nbsp; 
 Friday 13&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; About hospital all day.&nbsp; Letter from Carrie &amp; Mrs Knowles.&nbsp; 
 Saturday 14&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; Everything quiet.&nbsp; Hospital work going smoothly.&nbsp; 
 Sunday 15&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; At the Basilica for Mass at 9-30 a.m.&nbsp;At the African Mission Convent, Zeitoun, at 7 p.m. for Benediction.&nbsp; Wrote Knowles, Girls, Mollie.
