<p><u>May 27th</u></p>
<p>Plymouth Harbour.&nbsp; Natural beauty.&nbsp; Not unlike Farm Cove in parts.&nbsp; The Rio Padro passes up stream and is moored to a wharf in the naval docks<s>yard</s>.&nbsp; The troops for the Pardo arrive per train.&nbsp; They consist<s>s</s> chiefly of 3rd Div. Infantry.</p>
<p>Band selections by 39th Battalion.&nbsp; A last letter!</p>
