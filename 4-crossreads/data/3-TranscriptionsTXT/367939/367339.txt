 Thursday still on water 
 Dear Ted 
 Just a line to let you know I am alright hoping this will find you Grace   &amp;  Mavis still the same we stayed in the harbour until sunday evening I have had a fair trip I have a bad cold so there is nothing to tell I will say good bye for the present with best wishes from your loving brother Charlie 
 Address: C Bathersby A. Squadron B. 12 A.4.House C. no 94 D. C/O Victoria Barracks 
 [Written on side of letter at bottom 'they will send it along']
