<p>Monday 27 March 1916<br/>Mother &amp; I up early and drove into Mudgee.  Saw Dr. Nicholl;  he thinks I am about fit to return.  Roads in fearful state on account of dry weather.  Helped Dick with fence round stack.  Found young lamb bust on lucerne.</p>
<p>Tuesday 28 March 1916<br/>Dick and I finished putting up fence round Straw stack.<br/>After dinner helped Father &amp; J. Oakley fix sheep then took 2 [indecipherable] up to Cliff pdk. with Father.  Mother, Father &amp; I went burning off up the Gully in the evening.</p>
