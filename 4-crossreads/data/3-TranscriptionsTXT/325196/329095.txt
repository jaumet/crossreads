
23-1-16Sun 23rdChurch parade this morning conducted by Sol Green & I attended  I think the Reverend gentleman must have been sent to war by the Church of England on account of him not being a "wowser" as he is rather partial to a wee drop occasionally & suits a certain part of the men.This afternoon we had nothing to do but kill time till stables; the Corporals of C.Sqd have a tent to ourselves; After tea most of the lads put the remainder of the evening in while the daylight lasts boxing & wrestling received a parcel from home today in excellent order; We have been getting much better food the last couple of day so our complaints have done some good
