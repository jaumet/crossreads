<p>May 19th<br/>Physical drill before breakfast.  9-30 Drill order with rifles, Bayonet fighting, lecture on Correspondence, lecture on aiming &amp; parts of rifle, saw whale blowing about 9 a.m., came in sight of land on starboard side, low scattered hills first then higher chain later, ran parallel with them all day.  Full marching order &amp; inspection after dinner, later lecture on Landscape targets with map.  After tea, haircut &amp; writing &amp; reading, sentinel.  Concert after deck.  Weather beautiful, sun &amp; sea, fair wind W.N.W. direction, 313 M.</p>
