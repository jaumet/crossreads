<p>ASC dump &amp; after a couple of hours fixed up fairly comfortably especially after collaring some fuel from the dump with which we lit a fire and cooked a good feed.  In my section are Capt Kay MC. (British OC Cap Fisher MC (Canadian Capt. Cockrain (British Capt Hooper MC &amp; Scott-Olsen (Aust).  We have one batman to every 3 officers poor devils they dont look too strong so we all grubtogether, one officer helping the boys each day.  We are indeed a happy &amp; contented lot.</p>
<p>April 20th<br/>Rained all day, confined to camp, cooking in the open simply heartbreaking.</p>
