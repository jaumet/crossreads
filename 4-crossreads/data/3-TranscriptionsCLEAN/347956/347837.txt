  
 27-12-14 
 5 % of the lads were allowed out till 12.30  Stan & I put the afternoon in writing   rather a dull time all day. 
 Sun 27th Yesterday morning the B squadron signallers went out on horseback for the day & did not turn up at night fall.  I heard this morning that they went to Mena Camp & were arrested there for being so far from camp without having a pass & put the night  out  in the guard room;  This morning a Corporal & two men were sent out to bring them back.
