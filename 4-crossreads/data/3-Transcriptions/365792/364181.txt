<p>Apl 10  last one either. Just about 10 miles from the firing line. Good pond of water just behind us, plenty of straw in the barn, also numerous rats &amp; mice, a pub 50 odd yards away, &amp; several graves (soldiers) scattered here &amp; there. Must pay a visit to the township about a mile distant.<br/>Washed my feet etc took off my sweat soaked shirt &amp; sox, in fact had a good clean up, &amp; am feeling good. Can see two observation balloons, &amp; 1st Army H.Q. is about 2 miles away. Had a bonsa hot shower &amp; Harry changed his clothes &amp; got clean ones; great idea it is &ndash; there are a number of Belgian refugees doing nothing else but wash &amp; iron clothes.</p>
