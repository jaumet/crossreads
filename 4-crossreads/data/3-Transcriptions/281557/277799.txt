<p>1915<br/>Monday 20th Dec<br/>Embarked at 7am:<br/>Pulled out &amp; anchored in the stream. Sailed about 4.30.P.M. &amp; cleared the Heads at 5. P.M. The weather was fair though a bit rougher as darkness came. Slung my hammock &amp; slept below. Passed a sailing ship under sail &amp; was a fine sight.</p>
