<p>Quite intended to but slept in. Left instructions to be called but was forgotten. Had a very busy time since on Day-duty but am on a very <u>soft thing</u> at present. Decorating &amp; preparing for Xmas made us really busy but it was worth it.<br/>We had breakfast half an hour later, so that all of us (day &amp; night staff) could have it together. It was just like home, little presents with cards of good wishes on our plates.<br/>We Australian Sisters got a box of &frac12; doz Linen-handkerchiefs &ndash; beauties &amp;  on the box a picture of an Australian Mounted Rifleman with the Rising Sun. It was a lovely idea &amp; a great surprise to us. Had a box of lovely soap &amp; everyso</p>
