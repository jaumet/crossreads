<p>Saturday 15 December 1917.</p>
<p>More mail arrives. 6 letters <s>I got</s> for me. Messages of congratulation read out on parade at 7 am. today. Letters dated as late<s>d</s> as Nov 6th in today&#39;s mail.</p>
<p>Wrote letters.</p>
<p>Regimental paper the &quot;Cacolet&quot; at last comes to light.</p>
<p>Rumors of a move &amp; of Hollands entrance into war against Eng<sup>d</sup>. News that Clarrie is wounded in France.</p>
<p>(NOTE) The mail has cheered everybody &amp; <s>wh</s> all seem pleased. I got some very nice letters especially from D&#39;nong.&nbsp;</p>
