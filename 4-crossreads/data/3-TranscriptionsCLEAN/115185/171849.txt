
4 - If the relations ask you what they can send Betha say - papers - Daily papers magazines etc. We will want them for the men too.Give my love to Aunt [indecipherable] & Uncle Charles if you see them & to Aunt Isabel & Helen. I wish I had seen Gibson & Cicily again. Don't forget to post this on to Aunt Maria. It carries my love to her. I'm very well & the weather fine & sunshiny.I'll write again at next port. I've written to Gibson.Your loving sisterBetha McMillan
