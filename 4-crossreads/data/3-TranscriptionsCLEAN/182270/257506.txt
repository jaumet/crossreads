 SUNDAY, JULY 13, 1919. 
 Fine & warm. Went down to the Rocks 
 with Tickle at day light. He got two 
 foxes in Mountain & one in the Rocks 
 & I got one in the Rocks. 
 Took delivery of 269 X bred ewes 
 and 160 X bred wethers from A Jones 
 on a/c Barney Perkins. Put ewes 
 in Cow paddock & wethers in 4D.
