31st December 1914
This day we left Madang it was a very enjoyable sight for many to see the old barracks, the store with its bad debt incidentally the Kalabush fade from view as we turned south to Erima. Personally I did not take pleasure in it for having suffered with intermittent fever since Christmas eve, camped as we were on the hatches, it was not too pleasant.
