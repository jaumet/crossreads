<p>Well I think I will now end this letter as I am shure you are tired of wading through my bad spelling &amp; writing.  Give my love to Hilver &amp; I hope she is well &amp; as nice as ever.  Remember me kindly to your Jack.  Well I will say good bye till Germany is under.</p>
<p>I am<br/>Yours sincerly<br/>Dave</p>
<p>Trooper D. Peter (22)<br/>A Sqd., 8th L. Horse, A.I.F.<br/>3 Brigade<br/>Egypt</p>
