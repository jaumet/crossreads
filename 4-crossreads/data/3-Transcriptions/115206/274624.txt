<p>February 1918<br/>Saturday 23</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Spent the day in usual duties, and had some visual work in morning.<br/>Weather dull.<br/>On L'aison with Kerr and listened in at 59th Batt. wireless receiver, to Kiel Wireless Station and also a couple of Dutch Stations at Zeebrugge.<br/>Camped with Bombers and found them good chaps.<br/>Day a very quiet one had a couple of shots with 3 star rifles at an old turnip.</p>
