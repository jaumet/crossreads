<p>Saturday 23 June 1917.</p>
<p>Usual routine &amp; extra work this morning with extra work again during afternoon out on the desert in the scorching sun.<br />2 more aeronauts killed (accidents to aeroplanes.) Aeroplanes still continue to fly above us all the day &amp; some practice at shooting from them.<br />More wounded&nbsp;<s>arrive</s>&nbsp;pass through here from Gaza. (Minor cases.)</p>
<p>A hospital train goes to Suez, and a train load of Camels and another of supplies seen passing our camp.&nbsp;Cases of mumps &amp; measles break out.</p>
<p>We are beginning to see that our soldiering in Vic. <s>is</s>&nbsp;was play to what we get here.</p>
<p>I am out of tobacco &amp; cigarettes.</p>
<p>Working late till 6.30 pm.</p>
