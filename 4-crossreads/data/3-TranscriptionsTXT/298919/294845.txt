No rails to load.Tuesday 10th August 1915.  As per usual rain falling on & during day.  Just sufficient rails alongside to allow two hatches being worked all day.  P&O boat "Nellore" which was brought alongside yesterday morning still here loading iron & steel from barges.  About 3pm a big Jap boat Fushuni Maru, which has been here for a fortnight sailed.  At 6p the Nellore was moved to a new berth.Wednesday 11th August 1915.  This mngs papers report an air raid on the East Coast, but the places raided were not named.
