 We achieved something, but the cost was great On our left the new landing party made 4 miles inland &amp; when this letter reaches Australia &amp; reports from the Dardanelles are open &ndash; I lost [indecipherable] Kit, but that is a detail, when so many good fellows lost their lives &ndash; Now don't worry about me I'm writing in bed hence bad writing &ndash; I'll soon be about again &amp; am in good hands Love to all &ndash;Your fond son Frank Keep the same address 1st L.H.
