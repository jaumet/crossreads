<p>P.S.  How are the Owens nowadays.  Give Mrs. Owen &amp; Mifanwy my kind regards please.  A.M.W.</p>
<p>1st Dismounted Regiment<br/>Aust. Light Horse,<br/>Egypt<br/>6.X.16</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Read,<br/>Please excuse pencil but ink is just at present only sufficient to write addresses.<br/>I was very pleased indeed to receive your nice letter today, and hope you will favour me similarly again soon.  I shall appreciate it very much indeed if you do.<br/>Thanks so much for the news about Margaret, give her my love and the enclosed photo will you please.
