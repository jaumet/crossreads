<p>9<br/>I get no time to go anywhere since my last trip to Cairo of which I wrote last time I have been nowhere out of camp.</p>
<p>Of the shops in Cairo I could write much. They are splendid especially the jewellers and the fabric sellers</p>
<p>So many splendid trinkets of Egyptian character. Such fine silks and cloths</p>
<p>I will send some to you soon as possible.</p>
<p>Although I have written these few pages it has only been done between duties for we are very busy just now. Important events may happen here at any moment. Am hitherto unexpected new front may bring changes which fighting natures will welcome with a soldiers glee</p>
<p>My duties are important and I accept the position as one which may lead to much</p>
