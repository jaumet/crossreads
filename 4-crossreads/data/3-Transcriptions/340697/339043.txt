<p>Left Sydney 19.10.14 arrived at Albany 25.10.14. Stayed at Albany harbour a week. Went ashore and had a route march through the town. Fire on Afric [Africa?] morning before our departure from here. About &pound;300 worth of damage done.</p>
<p>Sydney sighted Emden 9.11.14. E under crossed our course about 20 minutes after we had passed along. Sydney chased the Emden ashore about 20 miles from our squadron.</p>
<p>Arrived at Colombo 15.11.14</p>
