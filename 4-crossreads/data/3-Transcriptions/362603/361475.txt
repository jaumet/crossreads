<p>4.  the pleasant way in which I spent yesterday afternoon.  It is now Tea-Time &amp; I am quite hungry again so as I've written 4 long letters this afternoon, I'll go to tea now &amp; finish Dad's letter afterwards.<br/>Well, Mum, I must go now or the mob will have eaten all the butter &amp; Jam.<br/>Best of wishes to all the neighbours &amp; to Mrs Dobbo, Bunting &amp; Stevens &amp; Ali.<br/>From Your Own Boy<br/>Harry   XXX</p>
