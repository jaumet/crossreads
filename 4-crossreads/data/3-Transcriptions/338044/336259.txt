Go it Boches, you'll get no further this way. No work after 12-30 pm    &amp; Legge &amp; I, take take a great tram ride right out of the town for about <strike>three</strike> seven miles, right along the Seine, viewing Bonsecours en route, to St Etienne  a nice walk to the river &amp; take a boat across to a pretty little village resting on the banks of the old river Seine    &amp; the foot of some hills, St Adrien. Here we have some tea in a garden caf&eacute; on the river bank &amp; inspect a very ancient little church, built in 13th Century &amp; cut right into the rock. Most interesting. Return across the river    &amp; come back by the same route to Rouen very satisfied with having spent</p>
