 [Wednesday 10 May 1916] 
 Still Cairo &amp; enjoying every hour of it, Plenty to do fixing up affairs for the Regiment  Very Hot. Met an old Bushman Sgt who has come over to open a Branch of the Commonwealth Bank- [Following paragraph are crossed out] but too late as I hope in 2 months we will be in France or England (Private) Horses, Kit, &amp; men arrive 9 pm &amp; I leave at 7 AM in the morning DIVISIONAL C.O. Gen CHAUVEL BRIGADE C.O. Gen COX Reg C.O. Col MEREDITH
