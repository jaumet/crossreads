<p>April 12th Friday<br/>Sighted Table Mt. very early &ndash; made the bay about 10 a.m., berthed &amp; disembarked the Springboks.<br/>After dinner we disembarked &amp; went by train to a Rest Camp at Sea Point.  Went into Cape Town in the evening.</p>
<p>April 13th Sat.<br/>Went into Town &amp; had a good look round Cape Town.  Find it very pleasant.</p>
<p>April 14th Sunday<br/>Went to dinner w/- Mr. &amp; Mrs. Alton &amp; had a fine afternoon &amp; evening.  The people cannot do enough for us.</p>
<p>Ap. 15 &amp; 16<br/>Spent in Camp fixing up tents, Sgts. Mess etc.  Weather pleasant but threatening rain.<br/>All men from Qld., S.A. &amp; W.A. went off on Monday to Durban.</p>
