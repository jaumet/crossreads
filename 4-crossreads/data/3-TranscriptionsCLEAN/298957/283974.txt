 (Tuesday) 28th March Up at 4 a.m. and resumed the march at 6 am bound for Moascar 22 miles off. Gradually the heat worked up to a fearful temperature and before long we were into soft white sand - From then on the whole journey was nothing but torture. The sand slipped from under us and made conditions almost unbearable,
