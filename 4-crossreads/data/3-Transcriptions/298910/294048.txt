<p>11/8/16  Friday</p>
<p>The usual trench warfare going on, &amp; only a few casualys night &amp; day.</p>
<p>12/8/16  Saturday</p>
<p>Trying hard to rain &ndash; Fritz shelling villages of Laventie - Fleurbaix freely &ndash; of no military importance. Altho still running Eton Hall Dressing Station &ndash; we were ordered to also relieve the 8th Field Ambulance &amp; take over the First Aid Post below Fleurbaix &ndash; no rest for the wicked or willing &ndash; Luckily we can handle all at present very conveniently.</p>
<p>13/8/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Went to Mass &amp; Communion at Nouveaux-Monde   6.30 am. Fritz shelling around "La Bassee". Having a blow from the trenches (Late Parade. Crimed but appealed &amp; got off.)</p>
<p>14/8/16  Monday</p>
<p>Rain off &amp; on all day &ndash; Got orders to get back to my dug out at Eaton Hall &ndash; Very lively trench warfare, also lively sniping all night.</p>
