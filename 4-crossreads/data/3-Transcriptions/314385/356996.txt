<p>I turn at 6 p.m. it is too cold to stay up.</p>
<p>Mon 23rd Oct.<br/>We rested all day as we still seem to have some heavy marching ahead of us.</p>
<p>In afternoon walk over to a village a real ancient old place there we had some refreshment &amp; a game of Billiards (French).</p>
<p>We are warned to be up at six in morning marching order ready to move off.</p>
<p>Tues 24 Oct<br/>Morning broke with rain but we nevertheless move off about midday doing about 10 miles to a rather important railway Station called Ardrick.
