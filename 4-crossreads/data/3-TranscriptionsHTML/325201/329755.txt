<p>over me must have been a bad one for the pellet hit my hat &amp;   never went through it or Bernard.<br/>The turks were deliberately sniping at us all day &amp;   we spent a very unpleasant day one man being wounded five horses killed &amp;  two injured  &amp;  two sandcarts smashed  &amp; about four or five put out of use.<br/>We finished up at about 11 pm &amp;   got back here &amp;   watered the horses for the first time since 2 pm on the 2nd November &amp;   turned in about 3 am this morning.
