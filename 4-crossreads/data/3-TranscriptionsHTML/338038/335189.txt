<p>Majestic Hotel<br/>Alexandrie-Egypte<br/>Later<br/>Monday 27/12/1915</p>
<p>Dear Father &amp; Mother<br/>Just a hasty addition or rather conclusion to my letter of 21/12/15.<br/>Arrived here yesterday midday having escaped the submarines etc and start tonight inland to Tel-el-Kirbar I think for reorganisation</p>
