<p>24.8.15<br/>Tue. 24th.  Last night an order came through from Brigade H.Q. to the 1st L.H. to send 100 men of all ranks to Popes Hill at 3 oclock to-day to relieve 100 men of the 2nd L.H., a reply was sent back a few minutes later saying all told the 1st L.H. could only raise 96 men;  we have about 45 snipers in various positions &amp; that would bring our total up to 140 men.  When we landed we had 442 <strike>officer</strike> men besides officers, &amp; since then we had 120 reinforcements in one lot &amp; on several occasion we have had two &amp; 3 men at at time coming into the Regt. so counting killed, wounded, missing &amp; sick we have had a rough time.  The attack still continues on the left &amp; the Turks who have been fighting very fair up to <u>date have at last resorted to foul tactics.
