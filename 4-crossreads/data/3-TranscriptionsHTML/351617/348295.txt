<p>The next pest were the "Gippos" themselves who were there rending Doughnuts and oranges &amp; a few other things in the eating line. I dont know what the Doughnuts were made of but Judging from the way these fellows were always around the cookhouse refuse tins, I should say our scraps had something to do with them. They were there last thing at night &amp; at daylight in the morning crying "Doughnuts 2 for 1 Piastre" &amp; "Oranges 2 for &frac12;"</p>
