We had dinner with them and in the afternoon went out to Hyde Park where the Irish Guard was playing.  After this we took the tube back to the City, had tea & strolled around the City passing through the Streets of which one reads of in books.  Piccadilly Circus, Leicester Square, Regent St, Strand, Oxford St, Trafalgar Sq, etc, etc.  We visited several places of International fame such as the Leicester Lounge & Hotel d'Europe.
