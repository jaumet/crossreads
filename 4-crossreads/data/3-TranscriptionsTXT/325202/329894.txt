
Nov 18/17 Samer -arrived here today after about 18 mile trek and are billetted on a farm about 1 mile out of the village. It is we are only 15 kilo from Boulogne. It is typical November weather - mild grey days
Dec 14/17For the past month six of us have been billetted on the farm of Lance-Lelin, Breuil W. Samer & it has been quite a home - The most kindly people we have met in France
We have had a table put on one side for us in the big farm kitchen & had the use of their fireplace in the kitchen while we have been able to buy milk from them & they have given us
