 Monday 19 March 1917 Went through the gas test this morning. Helmets on and put into the gas chamber for two or three minutes. Medical inspection in the afternoon, then issued with 24 hours rations. (6 biscuits, 1tin sardines and 1 pound tin of meat). Left Larkhill camp at 11pm. Arr. Amesbury station about 1am. Left Amesbury 1.15am and arr.Folkestone 7am the next morning. Before leaving camp issued with 1lb. tin meat 1tin sardines and 6 biscuits.
