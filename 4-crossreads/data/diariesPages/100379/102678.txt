20th June 1917. Went on at 10 till 2. Fritz gave us some strafe today. Came on again at 8 till 10. Received letters from Rana, Connie Charles.
21st June 1917. Came on duty at 6 till 10 oclock this morning. Came on again at 4 till 6. Fritz sent us another "home run" again today. Slept till 12 came on till 4 in the morning.
22nd June 1917. Woke up at 9 with the welcome sight of letters from home being handed me. Went on at 12 till 4. Built a new telephone dugout in the trenches. Fritz rather quiet as far as we are concerned today. Went on duty again at 8 till 12 midnight. Wrote letters home.
23rd June 1917. Went on duty at 8 till 12. Very quiet day. Fritz brought down 3 of our balloons this morning. On duty again at 6 ? 8. Received papers from Charles.
24th June 1917. Went on duty 4 ? 8. Fritz shelling at intervals all day. Received papers books also some more letters from home. Fritz shelled heavily tonight with gas shells HE for 5 hours. Went on duty 12 midnight till 4
25th June 1917. Did not get up till 12 Went on duty till 4 oclock this afternoon. Feel pretty seedy through the gas last night. Went on at 8 oclock. We left this position at 10 30 tonight.
