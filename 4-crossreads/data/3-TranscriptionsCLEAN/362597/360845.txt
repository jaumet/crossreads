  
 and saw the last of Australia at 7.30 pm. 
 Saturday New Year's Day (10) No parade in the morning. Sea a bit choppy & one wave came into the Port Hole & wet some of the chaps beds. 
 Held a boxing match at 2.30 pm. Rigged up a bonza ring on No 1 Hatch. Ropes & corner posts & all things stadium like. Spent a nice afternoon. Nearly all the boxers have bruized arms or fingers or cut lips & black eyes. 
 Ship's staff gave another concert in F ward. Our nurses seated near us provided chocolates. We had sent them New Year Greetings early in the day.
