<p>[Decoration on card.]<br/>Xmas, 1917</p>
<p>Loved ones, now so far away<br/>Battling 'mid the winter's gloom,<br/>Thus we greet your Christmas-day<br/>With a sprig of wattle-bloom!<br/>Here at home fond hearts beseech<br/>God to bless you, all and each.</p>
<p>20th Battalion Comforts Fund.</p>
