<p>reached the heart of the town &amp; then walked back to the southern outskirts, we went to about a dozen estaments in search of one Rachel, whom he had known when in that part of the country before.  We did not see her, but met Madeleine at a private house.  She was a delightful little girl who spoke good English in a fascinating way.  In one of the estaments we ran across Andy Chalmers.<br/>Poperinge is a big town, &amp; has suffered pretty heavily from bombardments.  The square &amp; main street run due north &amp; south &amp; thus is right in line with the salient north of Ypres.<br/>It is in that part of the town</p>
