This Village like all others is a mass of ruins. Laid on the floor in the dust of a ruined house right under shell fire but did not get any sleep. Our Canteen is here so got some cake 2 francs Biscuits 1 F. cigs 6d leaves me 76 francs. 2.15.8.Rained on & off to day & this afternoon had some shells very close to us, never seems safe even when out of Trenches.Much cooler to-night.
Tuesday 15/5/17I can hardly believe I am alive this morning after the awful time last night commencing at 9 P.M & finishing about 6 a.m today. All ideas of Fritz being short of amunition was rudely dispelled for the Rain & hail of shells during that time beggars description & of course they took their Toll. Among the first being our corporal (Birch of Goulbourne) & Percy Peacie my mate Birch killed outright & Peacie badly wounded a third of the shells were gas shells & I had my Helmet on & off hundreds of times.
