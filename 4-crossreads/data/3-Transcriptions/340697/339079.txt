<p>A severe bombardment all day today. The turks got information regarding the changing of shifts at the Pine. The 6th Bde had about 157 casualties 29.11.15.</p>
<p>The turks threw some lachoramatory bombs over in the 3rd. Bn lines last night. They also used the same class of bomb at Chatham Post a few nights ago. There were no serious consequences with the exception of a severe smarting of the eyes to all those in the vicinity of the explosions 8.12.15</p>
