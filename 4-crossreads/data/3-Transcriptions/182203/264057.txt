<p>At Sea. Aug. 20th 1916.<br />My dearest Mother &amp; Sisters,<br />I last wrote to you I think the day before we left Cairo. Well on Saturday Aug 12th we left Cairo &amp; arrived in Alexandria that day about 2. p.m. &ndash; went on board the hospital ship &quot;Kanowna&quot;. Were allowed shore leave until 10. p.m. &ndash; so we all went off &amp; saw the sights of the town. We went in parties &amp;</p>
