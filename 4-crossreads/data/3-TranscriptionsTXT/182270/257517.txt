 THURSDAY, JULY 24, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; hot. Went into Quirindi 
 with Dad &amp; Mum. They got the 
 10.17 AM train to Sydney. Marj. &amp; Olga 
 went with them in an engaged 
 carriage. Got home for lunch. Peter 
 shifted horses from Seven Star 
 &amp; I picked out 5 &amp; put them in 
 Middle Paddock. 
 Darby down 42 ft in hard 
 sandstone. Tickle drawing 
 water to him all day.
