<p>4344 Pte. Bowden L. T.<br/>Telegherry<br/>Via Stroud<br/>N.S.W.</p>
<p>8852 Pte. J. L. Penfold<br/>Bridge St<br/>Kauge<br/>Toowoomba<br/>Queensland</p>
<p>17/6/16  Saturday</p>
<p>we were working <strike>etc</strike> all night getting Ambulance waggons &ndash; Panniers etc away. Saw our Aeroplanes coming home last evening after successfully attacking enemy Camp &amp; Aeroplanes. Capt Coppleson took us for a route march &amp; swim &ndash; Hope to leave during tomorrow for Alexandria.</p>
<p>18/6/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Odd jobs about the Camp &ndash; not quite so hot, <strike> as The Camp are getting ready to mov</strike></p>
