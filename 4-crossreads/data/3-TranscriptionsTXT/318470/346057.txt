
14-2-17Still working on Deep dugouts.  Brisk shelling both sides.
15thNothing fresh going.  Still on C.R.E. Defence work.
16thLarge amunition Dump blown up this morning by "Taub" continued to burn & shells to explode all day.
17thBig Dump still burning & shells exploding.  Said to have been 2,000,000 lost.
18thWeather getting warmer & wet weather set in.  Still on C.R.E. Defences.  British Success on the Ancre.
19thVery wet & ground getting very muddy.  Heavy artillery fire.
20thStill on C.R.E. D.  Very foggy weather and wet.
21-2-17
Finer weather.  Fair amount of shelling from Jerry.  Still on C.R.E. Defences at Ginchy and 2nd Line.
22ndNothing fresh to report.  Things going well.
23rdHand over C.R.E. Defences to 8th & 15th Field Coys.  Look over work from other Sections.
24thWorking on various jobs Delville Wood, Montauban, etc.
25th3 Sections has gone to camp at Fleurs.  No. 2 Section is doing back work.
26thStill on back works.  Germans are said to be retiring to new Lines.
27thStill on back works.  Nothing fresh going.
28thStill working on hutting & other back line work.
29th
1st [March 1917]Good advances are being made along our front in small Sectors.
