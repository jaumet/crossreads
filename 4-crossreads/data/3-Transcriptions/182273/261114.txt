
<p>March &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>24 SUN&nbsp; Fine warm day.</p>
<p>Very quiet. Rec.</p>
<p>letter from Amy &amp; Julie (enc),</p>
<p>Tom U.S.A. Else P. &amp; Tense.</p>
<p>Some Aust. Div: moved to</p>
<p>the Somme. Quiet night</p>
<p>25 MON &nbsp;Fine day. cool.</p>
<p>Writing.</p>
<p>Posted letter to Amy &amp; enc for</p>
<p>Tom. Else P. &amp; Gladys Cavanagh</p>
<p>Rec. letter from&nbsp;&mdash;&ndash; &amp; photo.</p>
<p>26 TUES &nbsp;Fine day. Fritz shelled</p>
<p>us out in aft with 11<sup>.</sup>3 .A.P.</p>
<p>naval gun. 1 gun blown out</p>
<p>our dugout blown in. also RGA</p>
<p>dugouts with many men.&nbsp;<sup>lost all</sup>&nbsp;our kit</p>
<p>27 WED &nbsp;Fine day. Digging hard</p>
<p>all day, but</p>
<p>no luck. Still digging out</p>
<p>R.G.A. men. very quiet.</p>
