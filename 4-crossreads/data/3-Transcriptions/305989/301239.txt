<p>Sunday 14 January 1917<br/>Went to "Kilo 139" with limbers (about 28 miles) boys expecting a stunt when we got back 23 &amp; 5 went out but came back about 9.30 false alarm.</p>
<p>Monday 15 January 1917<br/>Spent all day making a cross for Rupert Ackland, long &amp; tedious job.</p>
<p>Tuesday 16 January 1917<br/>Erected the cross over Ackers grave.  Took several photos of it:  a mine was exploded at sea &amp; a scotty drowned.  Sent "Purp" a cable throu Misk.</p>
<p>Wednesday 17 January 1917<br/>Went down for swim, our planes were hovering about &amp; dropping smoke bombs.</p>
<p>Thursday 18 January 1917<br/>Clear day.  Taube over very high:  got Billgun clipped.</p>
<p>Friday 19 January 1917<br/>Went with limbers to "Kilo 152" for stuff from Port Said.  Got fountain pen.  On picquet at night.</p>
<p>Saturday 20 January 1917<br/>Nothing doing, same routine.</p>
