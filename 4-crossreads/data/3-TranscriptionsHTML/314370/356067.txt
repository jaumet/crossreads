<p>August, 1916<br/>Wednesday 9<br/>This is the prettiest little camp I have ever been in.  A small hollow, sloping from all sides to the centre surrounded by trees right against the train line.<br/>After dinner went out to the public bathing place "Bains de Mer" Chatalory  beautiful.<br/>Fine Town.</p>
<p>Thursday 10<br/>Played banker &amp; won enough for tram fares.</p>
