<p>Lemnos<br/>12.10.15<br/>My dear Sister,<br/>Your letter of 31.8.15 &amp; Beres also Mrs Pollock to hand today. I get one &amp; sometimes two from you every mail now, all mail is sent to the unit to be marked first &amp; if you are with your unit you get your mail straightaway, all mail is marked by Coy Quartermaster Sergeants from the record books &amp; sent to base &amp; it is at the said base that a man loses run of his mail when he is unfortunate enough to be wounded</p>
