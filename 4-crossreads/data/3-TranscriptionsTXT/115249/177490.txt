 Naval Signal 
 From - Administrator F/Wilhelmshafen 
 To - VAC 
 Date&nbsp;- 24 9 4 
 Time&nbsp;- 3.30 pm 
 Garrisons posted. &nbsp;All in suitable quarters. &nbsp;Flag hoisted proclamation read - Seventeen Germans surrendered - Four of&nbsp;these are officials who will probably be employed. &nbsp;The others are Planters Missionaries and business men all of whom have taken oath of neutrality - Chief official not yet in but has been sent for
