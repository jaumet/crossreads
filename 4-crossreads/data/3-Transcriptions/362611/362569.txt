<p>[Note paper with Union Line in Red lettering and a coloured photo of a passenger liner on the sea]<br/>T.S.S. Moeraki<br/>Wellington 19/2/18</p>
<p>My dear John,</p>
<p>My love to dear Anna  &amp;  to  your children "large &amp; small". This is a line to let you know that after a [indecipherable] joyable voyage, I am safely arrived in Port Nicholson. The "Moeraki" had a tempestuous passage of 10 &frac12; hours from Newcastle on <strike>Monday</strike> Wednes. night 13th inst. She, therefore, was late in leaving Sydney.
