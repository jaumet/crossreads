22/5/19
Dear Dad, Mum, George &amp; Ellis, I was No. 13 in the ranks this morning &amp; felt sure that something would happen.  Sure enough, I was called out for a boat roll &amp; I am due to sail on the "Beltana" about the first week in June.  I shall go via the Cape amp; aught to be Home about the middle of August.  Billy Allison &amp; Ern Thomas are not on my boat but they will not be very long after me.  I am enclosing a few snaps that I took while on embarkation leave.  Ellis will explain details to you.
