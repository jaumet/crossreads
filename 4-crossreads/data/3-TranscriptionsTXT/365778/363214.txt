 Thursday 23/8/17 Whole Division marched past General Birdwood along Canal De Neuf-Fosse. Raining but a splendid turn out. Friday 24/8/17 Battalion Training in wood, fighting cleared forest of Clairmarais, met Bas who has applied for commission in Indian Army. Saturday 25/8/17 Went with Bas to see Birdwood at Hazebrouck afterwards went and had dinner with Victoire at Steinbacque, arrived home at 10.30 pm. Sunday 26/8 17 Church parade afterwards went with Wisdom to see ground for Sir Douglas Haig's inspection on Wednesday next &ndash; Letter from Doff.
