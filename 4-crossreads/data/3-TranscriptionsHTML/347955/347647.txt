<p>had a Parcel from maud one letter from alma maud Josie.  Beer issue 10 A.M. had two Pints not much good   the Beer kings are drinking up a treat.  had dinner 12 had a duff that cost three Bob wrote to Josie and alma.  rested till 5 .tea had a stew that I would not offer to a respectable Pig turned in 6.30.  all the Boys are Pretty near drunk.<br/>Wednesday Dec.26.12.17<br/>turned out 6.30 A.M. fed and cleaned up Breakfast  saddled up and went out grasing camels returned to camp again 11.30 dinner 12- wrote to maud rested till 5.P.M. tea turned in<br/>Thursday Dec 27.12.17.<br/>turned out 5.45 fed cleaned up watered camels dinner 12 fell in 2.P.M cleaned saddles tea 5  one</p>
