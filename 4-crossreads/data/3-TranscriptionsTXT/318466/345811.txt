Sunday 21 July 1918Went to Salisbury &amp; saw Major &amp; Mrs Simpson.  The old C.O. is not looking too good.
Monday 22 July 1918Plenty of rain these days.  Doing most of parades under cover.
Tuesday 23 July 1918Expect I shall get my marchg. orders soon.
Wednesday 24 July 1918Usual parades.
Thursday 25 July 1918Revolver shootg. in afternoon.
Friday 26 July 1918Saw the Eye Specialist at Sutton Veny Hosp.  Raining all day.  Did no work.
Saturday 27 July 1918Major Lucas left the OTB for overseas.
