<p>Now I must tell of other things  -  I mentioned heavy firing on Monday 14th about 6 am  -  Word had come that a German force was attacking Herbertshoe  -  Well <u>this</u> time there was no half hearted killing business &ndash; war isn't kid gloves  -  You must <u>stike</u> &amp; kill &amp; kill until every atom of resistance is over  -  Its quickest &amp; best  -  Our <u>Tommies</u> were there  -  Down went the Encounter &amp; shelled the bush properly &ndash; Lyddite shells &ndash; first exploded low on the hills &ndash; then gradually sweeping up  -  Everything went down before this hurricane of death &ndash; shrubs &ndash; trees &ndash; men  -  The effect was A1  -  By noon all was over  -  The white flag up  -  The Governor surrendered &amp; I yarned to a man this morning who had commandeered &amp; was wearing a pair of <u>his</u> the Governor's white boots  -  Brigadier Holmes was in charge of the Military operations  -  Not a single fatality  -</p>
