 Gibralta 
 This rock together with its neighbouring harbour depicted 
 an excellent view as we moved slowly to our moorings. The houses &amp; general appearance of the township looked remarkably clean &amp; neat, situated on the slopes of this magnificent &amp; solid rock which is literally covered in guns commanding a dominating position over the entrance to the Mediterranean Sea.  Their warships and merchantmen lay in the harbour. 
 May 18.  A terrific &amp; determined onslaught by the Turks to drive us into the sea.  They were repulsed everywhere and their dead numbered approximately 7,000.
