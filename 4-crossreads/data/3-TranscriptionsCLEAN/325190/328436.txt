  
 Saturday Oct 20th 1917 Landed at Le Havre yesterday morning & marched some six miles to Harfleur where we pulled up at Rouelles camp. Passed doctor; issued with tin helmet & two varieties of gas helmets; also had vapour bath, all before tea.  Several of last draft still here. "Trish" of Tisbury fame, & Naim & Dunstan are here, but Harry Millard has gone up the line to a C.C.S. Missed first parade this morning & hear there will be trouble over it, but ---------  mabeesh. At 7.15 this morning
