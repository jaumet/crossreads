
Saturday 31/3/17nothing doing much today, same old frost & fog & same old canons bellowing all daythink the 14th Brigade go over in 4 days time, Bapaume I fancy, we are about a mile from it now 3 more villages taken during last few days.No word of leave yet.Pay. 3.7.5 21.15.6.Gas Alarms twice, had us up with helmets on, creepy feeling waiting for gas, all ended alright.
Sunday 4/3/17letter from Nell & Jack first since Jany 13/17. 50 days. they left for Hobart on the 27 Jany, good luck to em,very heavy fighting on the Right this morning & the Barrage terrific still going strong 11. a.m.Pay 3.8.5 21.16-0. hurrah Paid 100 francs 3-11-8 leaving me in debt 2/3. what next.
one of our Planes came down on fire Just near us but only engine trouble too much petrol no one hurt.fighting on the Right still going on8. P.M. No rum issue sundays. "ah. me"Bitterly cold wind blowing though fine
Monday 5/3/17Snow again about 1 ft deep winter all over again just as things were drying up.think we go out in 11 days timeowe 1/3 deferred Pay 21.17.0.wrote to Nell at Hobart.fine again in the afternoon & snow melting. fine moonlight night. Fritz sending shells over us again.
Tuesday 6/3/17heavy shelling by fritz again this morning, bitterly cold wind blowing, 20 odd planes up, more snow comingowe 3d deferred 21-18.0.
