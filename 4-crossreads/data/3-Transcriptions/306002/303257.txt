<p>23.8.15<br/>Walkers Ridge their officers refused to take over the trenches during the night time for want of confidence in their men &amp; the Aus. hold the trenches during the night time &amp; the Lensters during the day.  On all the other Posts they are put in day &amp; night with an equal number of Australians mixed through them &amp; they appear to be ideal soldiers but do nothing without orders from an officer, without officers they are lost &amp; some of their officers treat them like dogs.
