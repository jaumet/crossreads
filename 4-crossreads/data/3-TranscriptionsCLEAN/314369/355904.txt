
14 October 1915Thursday
Things as usual am going along steadily but getting as lazy as a Turk.Very good Pictures at night.Nights very cool. making use of blankets again.
1915 October 15Friday
First interview with dentist. He filled one tooth very well   &  I have to go again in a couple of days.A big draft of Reinforcements   &  men returning is being got ready to leave for the front next week.
