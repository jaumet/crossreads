<p>The sector is called La Toquet, and within 3 hours of taking over <br/>our scouts discovered a sunken bridge over the canal 3 feet <br/>under the water by which means the germans were able to <br/>capture N.Z. patrols, Tommy mail sergeants with mail etc for the <br/>last 3 months</p>
<p>19th  Two of our birds had their sentence read out to-day, both <br/>were sentenced to be shot</p>
