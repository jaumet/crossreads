<p>To the Soldiers of the Australian Army Corps.</p>
<p>For the first time in the History of this Corps all five Australian Divisions will tomorrow engage in the largest and most important battle operation ever undertaken by the Corps.<br/>They will be supported by our exceptionally powerful Artillery and by tanks &amp; Aeroplanes on a scale never previously attempted.
