<p>25. Thursday<br/>Worked a lot in garden &amp;  had an attack of malaria  Went to bed in discomfort &amp; with a temp. of 103 &deg;</p>
<p>26. Friday<br/>Fine day.  Got up at 12. feeling very weak but improved &amp;  did a good deal of work in garden</p>
<p>27. Saturday<br/>Fine day at last.  Worked in garden some &amp; wrote some.  Guns waked us at 20 to 12 last night but no raid ensued.</p>
