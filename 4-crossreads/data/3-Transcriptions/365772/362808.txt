<p>What Price Conscription<br/>Hospital<br/>3&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Nov 16<br/>My dearest Sister,<br/>Just a note to wish you Ralph &amp; Beres a Merry Xmas &amp; a Happy New Year. I hope we are all together for the next one - did I thank you &amp; the Mater for cabling on my birthday.<br/>I am getting a board tomorrow so will start on my furlough next week - so by the time you get this I will be back with the Bn. &amp; hope my past good luck continues &amp; I pull through.<br/>Owen called here to see me, he looks very fit &amp; gave me all the Sydney news.<br/>Well my dear Sister wishing you the best of health etc. for the coming year, your loving frere<br/>Ken</p>
