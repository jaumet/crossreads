<p>They simply make you take all they offer. Altogether I had a perfect time and am looking forward to going ashore again tomorrow.</p>
<p>Saturday 7th<br/>Awakened at about 5 am by the noise of the niggers on the wharf singing for pennies. They make a great thing of hissing and stamping their feet too. We threw some water over to shut them up but they became more noisy still.</p>
