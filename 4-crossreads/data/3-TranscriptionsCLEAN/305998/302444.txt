 We have to pull the guns out of Flers & go to Ginchy a better position with a little less mud. The sickness has started amongst the boy's many have gone away sick, it makes more work for those left. I have felt out of sorts, temp 102 & the Dr gave me medicine & duty so I will not go to him again till I die. We shifted our wagon line a little nearer up, so had to leave our dear home with laboar
