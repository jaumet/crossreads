<p>6/ On regimental 2 hr fatigue loading forage &amp; water from ship. We seem to be getting plenty of rain, it must be the wet season for this part.</p>
<p>9/Went out along the coast about 10 miles in direction of Solloum &amp; found coast clear. burnt  ten houses &amp; destroyed some old wells. While halted a nigger or senussi, rode up on a fine arab horse, to speak to the O.C., he soon delivered his message &amp; went back towards Solloum; we hear that he came from the Grand shiek of the Senussi tribe to say that they were neutral</p>
