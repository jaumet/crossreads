<p>1918 &nbsp;&nbsp;Feb &amp; March</p>
<p>28 THUR &nbsp;Arr Lime St 6.20 am &amp; home</p>
<p>at 7.30 am Raining. Writing</p>
<p>letters. posted same to Julie</p>
<p>Hortense. Else P.&nbsp; &amp; P.C. to L Berry.</p>
<p>Visiting friends at night</p>
<p>1 MARCH FRI &nbsp;Fine day. blowing</p>
<p>hard. Went to Shakespeare to see</p>
<p>Marriage of Figaro.</p>
<p>2 SAT &nbsp;Rec. P.C from L Berry. Went</p>
<p>to see match Lpool v. Stoke. crowded</p>
<p>gate. good game. Went to</p>
<p>Olympia to see G Formby in 40</p>
<p>Thieves. very good. Maggie</p>
<p>came home. Mild night</p>
