  
 6th. Reveille at 2. AM. this morning, so you can see we have had a lot of sleep, we moved off at 3 to catch the train at Goddersville  arriving there at 6. here we had breakfast, & then entrained in horse trucks &. they were in a most abominable state not being cleaned out for some time how the troops roared when they had to set to work shovel the mess out, the train pulled out about 7 & we had a very cold ride for the next 10 hours when we arrived at the storm centre Amiens, all along the line from Ailly sur Somme to Amiens the people are packed up & prepared to move at a minutes notice while the road from Amiens is crowded with refugees of all sorts carrying or pushing their few belongings all hastening to get out of
