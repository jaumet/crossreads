
Sept. 19thNeuralgia - visit to dentist. Continuation of good news & bad weather.
Sept. 20th."Our boys" beaten at Australian Rules. Mail from Home.
Sept 21stWe win our second game of Soccer, beating 4th Field Amb by 4 goals to nil - I spent the night at Y.M.C.A with Asher Chowne - Glorious weather.
Sept 22ndWet & stormy weather prevents work or pleasure.
Sept. 23rdA route march gives us our first distant view of the town of Ypres - In the afternoon we move camp from Reninghelst to Poperinghe.
