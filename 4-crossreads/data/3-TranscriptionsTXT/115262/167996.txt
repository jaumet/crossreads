 Friday 17 August 1917. 
 On no duty &amp; treatment again today. 
 Received marching orders for the firing line. All kits for base packed &amp; on the wagon by 10 a.m. &amp; taken away to Base Depot. 
 Went to 14 A.G.H. &amp; said goodbye to Ted Holden &amp; Tom de Gruchy. Met Ern Harris in Hosp. again 2nd time down from 3rd L.H.F.A. (In going through kit thought I had lost  your  some photos. Found them later). Went to bed at 9 am. &amp; ready for TOMORROW.
