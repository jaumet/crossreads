<p>Dec. 30th.<br/>Artillery more lively &ndash; Anti-tetanus &ndash; Many of our troops go down to trench feet &ndash; wretched infantry conditions &ndash; Letter Home &ndash; Peace negotiations German bluff.</p>
<p>Dec. 31st.<br/>New Years Eve<br/>Artillery active at midnight &ndash; a couple of serious wounds &amp; many trench feet &ndash; Otherwise quiet &ndash;<br/>The Last Day of the Darkest Year in History</p>
