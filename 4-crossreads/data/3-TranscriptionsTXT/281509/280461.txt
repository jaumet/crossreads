 26 August 1916 Saturday 
 Relieved of guard at 8 a.m. Cold wind with indications of rain.  We move off from Beauval at 2 p.m. carrying pack arrive Doullens about 4.  Take couple of hours to entrain. Run down town for a few minutes hurried look at church etc. back just in time to catch train. Bump along in cattle trucks to Hazelbrook. Coffee given out by Y.M.C.A. on again into Belgium &amp; disentrain at Hopunge about 2 a.m. Dark. March 3 miles to camp.
