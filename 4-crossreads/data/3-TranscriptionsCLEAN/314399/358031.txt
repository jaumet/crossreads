
Monday 3 February 1919Nothing to do at all today. Very cold all night. Still on the floor. Moved to a new hut. Tons of mud everywhere. Into Havre today. List went up showing our positions etc on the staff. In an HQ & [indecipherable] allotted.
Tuesday 4 February 1919Still cold. Moved again to another hut. this time to a bed & the hut partitioned. Much better. Into Havre again.
