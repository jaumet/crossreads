
W 22 SunI work all afternoon. Go for walk with T.W. & E after Tea wind through D ' Con meet Ethel on Road - we nearly get caught in RainWrote Mum
W & F 23 MonLovely day only showery. Go to see C. Chaplin a Dog's life. With B.B. V.W. TW & E. Come home & go to bed to read.Recd letter from Roy
W & F 24 Tues.Tap comes home. Trains are on strike & Patient are sent talk from Salisbury. Play at the mess have Tea once again & go to bed to read
F 25 WedRecd letter from M.R.L. Fox dies with meningitis & I write all night until Last Post.Come home very tiredWrote Mr Havercraft.
F but dull 26 ThRecd Parcel from Bassie X. Have a long busy day. Come home & read all Write to M all night [indecipherable] up about calcium. Go to bed & ReadRecd letters from Jennie.Wrote M.R.L but did not post until 3.10.18
F 27 FriI move to [indecipherable] Hut & go to Dunnington for walk. Tea at Mrs SeawoodsMeet Tommy officer & Prains & Mr Church of Plough Inn. Tea after Piano & walk nearly to Bulford with H.  Come home & play Billiard & go to bed early
F 28 SatLovely Day but I am too busy to go out. I have to write 2 letters by morning.  Meet at 7.45 & walk home to Dunnington - Lovely clear night & I thoroughly enjoy walk.
Wrote Mrs Edmonds & Hilda.
