
July, 1915Monday 5Have read printed Diary Notes, they sound interesting to me a few typo errors - I'll be glad if you'd keep a Copy of them all for me.Mother 2. 9 Letters today Aunt Louie (2), Days & others from England - Joe.Went to Bakeries Napoleon used them & before that they were a slave market, there's 5 of those ovens like a row of Shearers Brick ones.40,000 Loaves daily are turned out we eat Australian frozen mutton & Beef - The flour comes from America Butter from Australia. Like everywhere, the Lord sends the Tucker & the Devil sends the Cooks.
