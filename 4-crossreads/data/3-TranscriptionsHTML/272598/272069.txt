<p>interesting &amp; exciting events contested.  Our transport entered for the best turned out general service limber, &amp; much to the delight of all the unit; were successful in pulling off 1st prize;  All looking forward now to the Divisional sports laid down for Tuesday next.  Pitched tents again which were struck about 8 or 9 days ago, prior to our anticipated departure.</p>
<p>Sunday 11.6.16<br/>Church parade; reference made to the death of Lord Kitchener</p>
<p>Monday 12/6/16<br/>The whole of the unit marched to the palms on the opposite bank of the lake at Ismailia, where we spent a very pleasant morning at our leisure, returning to camp at 12 noon.  Have been given very little work since the postponement of our departure.</p>
