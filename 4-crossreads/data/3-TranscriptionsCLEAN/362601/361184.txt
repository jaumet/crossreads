  
 5 April, Saturday 
 F. Go & play Hockey. Bonnie game I score 2 & miss 6 goals. Bath & go to Dance. Play & dance with some girls whom I have never danced with. I am very dopey especially between 8.45 pm & 10 pm. 
 6 April, Sunday 
 F. Recd P.C. from Ellis. I go to Hop over & get annoyed over Snooker. Tea with Doris & Dick's (2). Go for promenade after & meet Dick 3 times. Painful walk to B'ford Bridge & return to Supper & snooker. 
 Very tame affair all through & see D.O.R.A. again.
