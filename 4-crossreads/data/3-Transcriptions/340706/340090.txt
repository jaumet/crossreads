<p>136<br/>to Tortoni where I see Egypt &amp; thence on to Gabriels whence we go to stations &amp; catch train home as the leave regulations seem to have put the wind up my friends</p>
<p>5-5-19 (Mon)<br/>I perform all my duties for to day that is I report to the Cpl in the company orderly room &amp; am free for the day except that I must not leave camp till 2 pm which I obey by catching 1.25 train from a station nearly a mile away &ndash; Rouelles.</p>
<p>I forgot to mention that yesterday we moved into a new camp &amp; in consequence there is a lecture to day after which I walk in to Havre through the village of Rouelles I might mention here that these stations of Rouelles &amp; Harfleur are quite a long distance from the village of that name.</p>
