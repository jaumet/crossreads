This connection gives it a dingy appearance &amp; distracts from its dignity.  At <strike>Harfleur</strike> Rouelles one side of the valley ends in a bluff, &amp; the other side turns a right angles north towards Le Havre.  The road at the foot of this ridge, along which we marched from Havre, is dirty &amp; bad, &amp; goes through dirty half town &amp; half country quarters.  Also it runs past a very large collection of big huts forming a munition factory.</p>
