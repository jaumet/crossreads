They must, I think, have gone to reinforce their friends in Messypot, which is very excellent for they had strong positions which would have probably cost much blood &amp; money to take.  March 13th For the last three days we have heard nothing but the continual rustle of sand blown against the tent by the [see image for Arabic writing] (Khamseen). Today thank goodness it has stopped. Another move in the air.  To come off in a few days to El Arish.
