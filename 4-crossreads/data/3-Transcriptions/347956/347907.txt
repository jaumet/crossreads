<p>4.3.15</p>
<p>Thur 4th<br/>Dismounted parade for all of us to-day &amp; we have been signalling close to camp all day;  We were told at dinner time to-day that we were to hand in a pair of boots to get hobnails in them, as we will probably have all foot work when we shift from here;  All the boy are cribbing at the idea of tramping;<br/>I handed in a pair of boots to get half soled a couple of days ago;  None of the infantry camped here are on parade to-day, on account of the strenuous day they had yesterday;  Went nowhere after tea; some of the boys received passes to-night to</p>
