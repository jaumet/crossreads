<p>10th  Went over to the German Prisoner's camp.  The German's are camped in tents surrounded by a fence of barbed wire 8 ft high and numerous sentries marching round with fixed bayonets.  They are a big stamp of men and have been sent from Dorchester Prisoner's camp where they have been since Oct 1915.  There are several of the Prussian Guard among them.
