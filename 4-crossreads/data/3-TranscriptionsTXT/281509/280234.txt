 11 January 1916 Tuesday 
 Tony bad with sore leg.  Most horrible hot dusty day. 
 Lecture in Rec. Tent by Capt. Willis --- Fractures. 
 Bob &amp; self go down to Petersham to tea.  Trilby Doyle met on train.  After tea go with Macs to Summer Hill  Dr &amp; Mrs Grant etc.  Evening &amp; supper.  Hamper heavy &ndash; into town to catch train for camp 11.15 pm.
