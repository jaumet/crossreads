<p>20 August 1916 Sun<br/>The 12th Field Ambulance relieve us &amp; by ten oclock we were on the road bound for Contay. On arriving at Contay sometime after dinner I was laying down on a paddock feeling pretty tired when someone said Well Sting how are you doing &amp; there in front [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>21 August 1916 Mon<br/>Reveille 6 am. Most of the boys leave for the trenches. I am not going out this time but doing night duty with McGee. Said farewell to Ack, Geoff, Toby &amp; Cliff as they left. Did some washing &amp; indulged in the old game of pontoon before going on duty. First Anzac Rest Station</p>
<p>[Note in margin]<br/>All in the tent I slept in tent bag. Pyjamas.</p>
