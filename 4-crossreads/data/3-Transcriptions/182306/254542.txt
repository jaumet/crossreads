
<p>52</p>
<p>Sunday 2nd Feb.</p>
<p>Calm with Clear sky. All the boys on deck having a sun bath Passed anothe boat tea time which signalled to <s>b</s> us. We have a piano out on the deck tonight and had some fun dancing &amp; singing which helped to liven thing&#39;s up a bit.</p>
<p>Feb. 3rd</p>
<p>Arived at Port Said about 11 oclock The European <s>w</s> quarters look fairly decent. But the nigger quarters take the bun for durtiness Some</p>
