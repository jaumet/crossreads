<p><u>Glisy</u></p>
<p>[4 Th]<br/>Grooming and fixing up lines</p>
<p>[5 Fri]<br/>Standing to<br/>Our Artillery put up a very heavy bombardment. Fritz dropping bombs near camp</p>
<p>[6 Sat]<br/>Left Glisy at 10am. Arr. Rivery about 11am. Building dugout the rest of the afternoon.</p>
