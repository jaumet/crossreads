
It look rainy in the morning but cleared up later.  We could get practically no news of the fighting but heard that the Australians had done well & that casualties on both sides were heavy.
Tuesday 10th [Aug]Had an easy day.  Went round the harbour in the motor launch & saw several transports loaded with freshly arrived soldiers & saw the hospital ships transferring wounded to others.
Wednesday 11thDid absolutely nothing but rest.  We enjoyed a splendid swim in the bay in the afternoon.  There was a big gun bombardment at night & early in the morning.  We heard the French had captured 2 trenches.
