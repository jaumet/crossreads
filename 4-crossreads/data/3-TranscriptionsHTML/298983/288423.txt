<p>Thursday 23rd May<br/>Roused out early &amp; had to put our Blankets &amp; <strike>cooking</strike> eating utensils away before we had breakfast. Got Passes sugar &amp; meat tickets Etc marched to Forant &amp; got the train there. Arrived London about 12 Got a Railway Warrant for Goralwood Had Dinner at War Chest.Bussed to Bayswater &amp; spent afternoon with Aunt Susie Caught train at 8.20 from Euston for Hollyhead</p>
