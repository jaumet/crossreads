<p>The first I saw of him was near our hospital &amp; he was leaning on Major Simpson's (O.C.) arm.  Soon after he was established in the room next to the hospital, containing a bed, <strike>&amp;</strike> table &amp; chair, with mats on the floor, I was sent in with a bottle of Vichy water, so I was able to speak French.<br/>He (the General) is an exceedingly polite fellow.<br/>Dressed in khaki from head to foot excepting the fanciful tarbush with embroidery which gave a touch of distinction to him.<br/>His leggings were worn &amp; clothes slightly war-stained, &amp; his right arm was in a sling.</p>
