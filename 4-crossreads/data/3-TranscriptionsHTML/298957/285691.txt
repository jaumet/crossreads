<p>2nd June (Friday)<br/>On Piquet. Hand &amp; foot inspection. Sing-song in our tent tonight. Circulars from Gen Birdwood &amp; Kitchener handed round relative to behaviour in France.</p>
<p>3rd June (Saturday)<br/>Windy. Squad drill in morning. Tents have been packed up ready for moving on Wednesday. Now sleeping in the Mess Hut. A party of us went to a concert in the Y.M.C.A. pavilion tonight.</p>
