  
 Wednesday 8 August 1917. 
 Saddled up straight away in marching order and went for an hours jaunt.  
 Washing & grooming parade then to water & back to feed. 
 Arranged to have the name of Regtl paper changed from Stretcher to "Cacelot" ["The Cacolet" was the journal of the Camel Field Ambulance],  and a concert with outside & 14 A.G.H. artists is being arranged in our huts for Friday evening. 
 Another attempt to finish a letter to D'nong and finished it too. 
 Went on leave to Cairo & regd three envelopes with handkerchiefs enclosed. 
 Met Mal Jamieson 3rd L.H.F.A. who is down from  Gaza  the line for 7 days leave.
