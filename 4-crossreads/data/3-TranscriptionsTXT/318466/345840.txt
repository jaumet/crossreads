
Sunday 3 November 1918Did some painting in the morning.  Aunty Lu & some more people came to tea in aftern.  Packed my Kit in evening.
Monday 4 November 1918Caught the 11.45 from Kings X for St. Neots.  Mr. Mager met me.  Beaucoup rain & no shooting today.  Cartridges did not turn up from Tibbitts.
Tuesday 5 November 1918Lovely day.  Not cloud in the sky.  Got out early with Tibbitts amn. which came this morn.  Bag:-  2 hares & 1 Rabbit in morng. & 2 rabbits in aftern.  Missed 2 bon cock pheasants.
Wednesday 6 November 1918Again out early.  Got 2 rabbits & a pheasants.  Caught the 5.17 for home, arrd. in time for tea.  Ida was at the Webbs & Mrs. Hoare at home.  Glad to be home.
Thursday, 7 November 1918Dinner - Tibbitts Senior.Went to G. Tibbitts with hares & stayed to lunch.  Back to tea at No. 10 & then on to Tufnell Park for dinner.
Friday 8 November 1918Dinner - Hoare.  Into town in aftern. & got my P/B & drawn up to date.  Back to tea & on to Sam Hoares in eveng. with Madge.
Saturday 9 November 1918Matinee - Aunty Lu.  Dinner - Tibbitts & George.  Saw the Lord Mayors Show.  No lunch.  "Sails Up" with Aunty Lu & dinner at George Tibbitts later.Kaiser abdicates on the 9th.Saw the Lord Mayor Show on the 9th.  Aussies looked well in it.
