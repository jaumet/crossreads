 did not buy anything. 20th. Fine bright day. Course NW by N, N, NE &amp; E at 5.30. Am told on good authority that our detour has taken us well over 1000 M west of the trade route &amp; consequently we wont get to old Blitie before 27 or 28 Inst. 21st  ENE, heavy swell running. This is our 63rd day out from Melbourne. We are all reckoning on a fine pay after landing. &pound;4/6/- owing me to date. Medical experiment in progress &ndash; Several down up to date. Our turn to-morrow. have a cold but don't expect any trouble with the M.O. as regards fitness. Got my notes jotted down on old Lincoln &amp; at Mt Aureol &amp; am entering them here in case the other papers go astray. We have difficulty this way as one cant always carry ones things about in onship rig, &amp; the spirit of comradeship &ndash; the Esprit de corps &ndash; of our army is so fine that nothing is safe from the sneak thief and the casual pilferer.
