<p>GANDER'S GAP</p>
<p>Thursday 28th<br/>Mortar operations wrecking part of his first line caused activity among his working party. Stand down. Fired one magazine at party, dispersing it. Following this fired 3 &frac12; others during various periods of the night.<br/>4 &frac12; magazines &ndash; 200 rounds.</p>
