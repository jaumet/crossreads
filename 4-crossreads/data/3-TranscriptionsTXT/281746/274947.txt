 Sept 21st 1918 to Dec 5th 1918 
 Sept 21st&nbsp; 1918  
 Walked on the hills. A lovely view of the valley of the Somme is got from these hills. 
 Had to do fatigue work fixing up a tumble down cottage as a [__llet] for the Major, this afternoon. 
 Walked along the banks of the River Somme. A number of Aussies were out on the river in a flat-bottommed boat fishing.
