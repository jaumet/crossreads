<p>52<br/>9 men 1 cpl which constitute the guard &amp; for whom all this paraphernalia takes place. These four men are inspected by Ord. officer &amp; the band lead them down the street accompanied on each side pass near by the awe strucken populace who do not seem to know what to make of these English</p>
<p>1-2-19 Sat<br/>Murder of woman in estaminet &ndash; Carr parade on a no parade day &ndash; light fall of snow &ndash; reply to my application for Paris &ndash; fill in 540 Form &ndash; row with Chinese at [indecipherable] before dinner &ndash; snow falling all the afternoon &ndash; fine sleet &ndash; like drizzling rain only it falls much slower &ndash;</p>
<p>Most of the trees &amp; walls have icicles on them as the cold is intense &ndash; nevertheless I have not felt the need of our overcoat so far though chilblains are showing on my hands &amp; I have some severe ones on the little toe of left foot.</p>
<p>2-2-19 (Sun)<br/>Nothing important</p>
<p>3-2-19 (Mon)<br/>Snow still on ground, chilblains have taken possession of my hand &amp; I have lost use of little</p>
