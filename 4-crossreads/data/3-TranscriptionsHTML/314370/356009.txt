<p>April, 1916<br/>Sunday 9<br/>After Church Parade walk over to Dad.  have lunch there &amp; stay till about 3 pm.<br/>came home &amp; wrote two letters home.</p>
<p>Monday 10<br/>Richie's Birthday<br/>After dinner I went over to see dad as usual, - found that they had no word of going yet.</p>
