<p>Jly 30 rather inquisitive kiddies sort of paraded round me &amp; argued with one another as to whether my eye was dinkum or merely artificial.</p>
<p>31  Rumors still floating round (re the Seramic in which we are supposed to leave shortly). Priv Bendrey of the 2nd Batt <u>is</u> on the Boat Roll for Australia &amp; tomorrow we draw our extra kit from the QM.</p>
<p>Aug 1 Pay day today &amp; I drew 28/-. So am only 26/- overdrawn.<br/>Spent sundry quids on brooches, belts knives, etc for home so hope I've made a good selection? Nothing doing down town.</p>
