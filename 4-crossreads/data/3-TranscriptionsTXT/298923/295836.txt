 19 June 1918 Wednesday We hear now that we remain here for 10 days so will be "livery" by then as we get no exercise at all-   A signaller was killed by a shell yesterday &amp; a few men wounded.  Very hot, survival of the fittest in this spot.  Ordnance have issued mosquitoe nets 1 to 2 men so that is a comfort for them-  Shirts &amp; Tobacco arrived from our Comforts Fund, Mrs Lennon is Secretary in Sydney &amp; has done a lot of work for the 1st L.H.
