<p>Sunday 1 Sept.<br/>Wet in morning. Nothing much doing so watched prisoners &amp; wounded coming in at Ambulance &ndash; very busy, &amp; interesting. Saw several burials.</p>
<p>Afternoon finer. Writing in afternoon.</p>
<p>Prussian Guards (volunteered to stop us) amongst prisoners.</p>
