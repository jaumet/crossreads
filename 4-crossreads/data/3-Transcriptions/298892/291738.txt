<p>interesting. As we are not very busy these times I was able to settle down and read right through it in a couple of days. I would be glad if you could send me a "Life" magazine now and again. It generally contains some instructive reading which is very necessary in the trenches.<br/>Our Commanding Officer, Lieut. Col. Morshead received the D.S.O. recently in recognition of the good work done by the 33rd. Btn. under his leadership.<br/>The photo which I enclose with this letter was overlooked and should have accompanied one written some months ago. The scrap of paper is one of Fritz's Field Post cards, (one of the relics I acquired lately) which may prove of interest to you. Unfortunately the enclosed slip is not written on.</p>
