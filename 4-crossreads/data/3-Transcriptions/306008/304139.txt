<p>1914<br/>Oct 11 Sun<br/>Went for walk</p>
<p>Oct 12 Mon<br/>Duty in Garrison Hospital</p>
<p>Oct 13 Tuesday<br/>Completed duty in Hospital<br/>Worked in garden<br/>Helped at Killara Class<br/>Decided to join Expeditionary Forces</p>
