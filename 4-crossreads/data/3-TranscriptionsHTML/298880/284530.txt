<p>Friday 25th August</p>
<p>Showery weather. We <strike>are</strike> commenced our (AI) assistant instructor exam.</p>
<p>Sunday 27th August</p>
<p>We finished our reading and <strike>seld</strike> sending test and heliograph setting yesterday.</p>
<p>Sat. 26TH. We had lamp reading last night. I am confident of passing.</p>
<p>28th August</p>
