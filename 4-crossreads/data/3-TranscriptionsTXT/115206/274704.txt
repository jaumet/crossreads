 May 1918 
 Tuesday 14 
 Bussy-la-Dours d-y At 4 am Fritz arrived at our little district &ndash; well loaded . No military damage. Weather today &ndash; glorious. Rode across to Bonnay, and had a swim in the big pool at Querrie. Temperature reached to 95   at midday. At 7 pm Fritz's waves commenced , and numbering twelve visited Amiens . Artillery very active at 10 pm.
