
Saturday 21st July 1917
Took wagon to Busseboom for a load of timber forth 13th battery. Left camp at 12 noon and arrived back at the 13th battery about 4pm where we left wagon.
Sunday 22ndGreat number of our searchlights sweeping the sky looking for Fritz planes. Lovely sight. Half holiday from12noon to 3pm. About 10.30pm a few of Fritz planes dropped bombs around our camp for nearly 1/2 hour. No damage done.
