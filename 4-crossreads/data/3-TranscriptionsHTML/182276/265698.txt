<p>9th. M.G. Coy No 23<br/>Intelligence Report from 6 am 22nd to 6 am 23rd:-<br/>Right Sector:- No 1 gun fired 2000 rounds from a position to right of No 1 Locality in "A" Gap at I 16 b 45/86 from 7 pm to 6 am. No 6 fired 2250 rounds from I 11 c 05/95 from 7.30 pm to 6 am.<br/>Enemy M.G. not as active as usual. Three searchlights engaged and forced to change position- Quiet during day except a few light shells near No 1 position.<br/>Left Sector:- No 7 fired- indirect- on I 6 a 5/0 INTACT FARM.- unobserved- <br/>During the night enemy M.G.- three- fired at intervals of about &frac12; hour.<br/>The enemy M.G.'s have been much quieter during this last period than heretofore.</p>
<p>B J. Carey Capt<br/>23.12.16</p>
