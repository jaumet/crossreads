<p>June 25<br/>Hot as blazes in Red Sea</p>
<p>28<br/>Passed Perium Hells Gates at 10-30 Aden 8,PM</p>
<p>29th<br/>Pay day out in mid ocean, very hot  &amp; developed Prickly Heat</p>
<p>30th<br/>very rough sick as a dog had no lunch all the lads are knocked out 14 days to WA cross the line on the second</p>
<p>2nd<br/>Cross the line at 4.30 very close still</p>
<p>July 4 th<br/>buried Pte Tucker 27 Batt, buglers play last post, firing party fire volley of three rounds, Dysentry</p>
<p>5 July<br/>Pte McDonald lost supposed to have gone overboard, man throws hot water over Sgt Gibbs</p>
<p>July 9th<br/>going good want men to handle coal</p>
<p>Saturday July 16th<br/>Arrived Fremantle Friday night anchored outside in roadstend pulled in to Wharf on Saturday morning troopships there bound for France, left WA at 12-30 for Melbourne.</p>
<p>Monday<br/>Good trip but very cold</p>
