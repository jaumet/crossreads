
May 1918Wednesday 8
Dussy-la-DaoursStill raining, had a run at midnight to Villers Brettoneaux, plenty of the Bosche dead still unburied.Paid a  visit  to Jame's grave and took a snap of same.Wrote to Uncle Will, Mum, Toots and Adrienne also Ray.Slight gas bombardment at Corbie and FuilloyMet an American from Florida in Engineers.
