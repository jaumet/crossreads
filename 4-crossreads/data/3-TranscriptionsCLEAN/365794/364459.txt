  
 Tuesday 27 February 1917 [26 February 1918] 1/2 day & called to see Col. Dawson re red capes & seniority here.  He & his assistant tell me am 10 years the senior of these Aust. but no hope of taking my proper position for ages as there are crowds ahead of me & as I did not come when should have done can only agree to this.
