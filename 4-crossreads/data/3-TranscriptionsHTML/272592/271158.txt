<p>than anything else for they go off with an ear splitting crash which makes the earth tremble for a long way round &amp; will put the fear of God in a nervous man, they are just an iron case &amp; the end which the detonator fits in is made of wood, they are very roughly made but of course, they are good enough for their work.</p>
<p>While they pasted us with their "oil drums our Stokes" were very busy &amp; shot bombs like lightning into them, they are far smaller than Fritz's oil drums but far superior, they are about the finest bomb going &amp; when once they get the range are very deadly.</p>
<p>The 2nd Bde seem to have done a lot of work in these trenches, &amp; they wanted it badly too.</p>
