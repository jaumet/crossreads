15/5/19
Havre
Overcast and cold in the morning it came on to rain in afternoon. Absolutely nothing to do but loaf. Sat long over lunch talking with Colonels O"Donnell Heron. In the afternoon more idleness. In the evening went out for a walk with Elwood round pretty winding roads twisting English like lanes and quaint little villages. 
16/5/19
All our baggage went in at 9 a.m. A rather overcast day. In the camp many Hun prisoners hard at work tarring the huts. My batman now is Private Scotty Watson M.M. 15 Star a wild dag. Left camp by motor lorries at 2 p.m. and made for Havre. At the quai we found the embarkation staff and I went ahead first and inspected the ship SS Londonderry. Cabins were soon allotted to some
