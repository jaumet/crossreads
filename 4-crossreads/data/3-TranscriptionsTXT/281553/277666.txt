
Dec. 2nd ThursdayGlorious day.  Tinned meat & vegetable ration issued just lovely cold.  Rice & tea breakfast, Tea evening.  Letter writing & improving bunk afternoon.  Bomb burst within 5 ft. of 3 of us morning fortunately no injuries.  No. 2 Northern.
Dec. 3rd FridayLovely day, mild night, rations still very low, 1/4 water.
Dec. 4th Sat.Day fine, evening not too cold.  Line quiet.
Dec. 5th SundayWeather continues mild.
Dec. 6th MondayWeather very nice.  Mail in.  Parcel from Eileen.  Day off duty
