 15-8-16 Tue 15th At Bir-El-Abd the troops found a notice in English left by the German Field Ambulance saying cholera patients who had died were buried at the spot;  This morning I was at the Orderly Room to give particulars as Major Granville is recommending me for a Commission in this Regt as Sig Officer. 
 This afternoon at 4.30 the 1st Light Horse Brigade was formed up by the YMCA &amp; General Chauval thanked us for the way we fought during the last couple of weeks, &amp; said he is trying to get leave for a percentage of us to visit Port Said.  we are to remain here but the 1st &amp; 2nd Bde of light Horse are to be spelled as much as possible
