 February, 1916 Tuesday 15 First thing exam in care &amp; cleaning &amp; I have pleasure in stating my class had highest average. Have tea w/- Dad, evening cold &amp; inclined to rain. 
 Wednesday 16 Work as usual &amp; at night I visit the Casino Kursaal.  The "Revue" is absolutely the prettiest thing I have yet seen staged, the music &amp; costumes &amp; really fine &amp; altogether a show worth seeing. Turn in at 1 am
