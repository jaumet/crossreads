<p>Dec 26<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;We went out on supplies as usual the snow from last night stayed on the ground &amp; it was squally again to-day, with sunshine in between whiles. Jim stayed the night with his brother in the amunition section at La Cr&egrave;che</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dec 27<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;I picked Jim up on the way to Steenwerck for supplies - We went back for coal for the brigade &amp; delivered same &nbsp;We had some whisky at the 18<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;canteen &amp; went back to Park feeling very pleased with ourselves &amp; looking&nbsp;<s>thro</s>&nbsp;at the world through rose-coloured glasses - We stayed in the lorry all the evening &amp; with the primus kept good &amp; warm. We have an issue of rum every night which we use as a night cap&nbsp;&mdash;</p>
