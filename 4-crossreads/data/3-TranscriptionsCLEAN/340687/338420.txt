
April, 1917. Thursday 26Up at 9.30 and procured rations which are not good cloudy all day. Decided to try for pay in aftn. so walked over to Bapaume road about 2 and after waiting a while received 60f. In meantime inspected an old tank which soon started up and we rode for a while in her. Visited Y.M.C.A. and then caught a lorry to Albert. Accosted by M.P. not being properly dressed. Mat Savage & Johnson got in first to second show of which was very fair although [indecipherable] old items. Home via Mametz at 9.30 & after reading paper to bed.
