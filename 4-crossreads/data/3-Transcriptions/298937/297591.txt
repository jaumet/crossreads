<p>He was trembling violently in every limb &amp; his face was awful.<br/>At a signal the chair was kicked away &amp; -- the rope broke.<br/>By all human laws &amp; by the Turkish criminal law he should have been released then, but no.<br/>The next attempt was successful &amp; in a short time the others shared his ghastly fate.</p>
