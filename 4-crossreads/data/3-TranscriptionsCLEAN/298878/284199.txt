
a portion of the deck having been cleared, awnings erected, flags hung in profusion and colored electric lights strung up, a concert was commenced.  Items were rendered by soldiers & military & Ships officers; a very enjoyable two hours had been spent, when the blaring of trumpets announced the arrival of Neptune and his court:  His and Her Majesty and suite were immediately conducted to a dais on the hatch, which was being used as the stage, and the invitation into the brotherhood of travellers of those who had not previously crossed the line began.  A list of those whom it was proposed to initiate had been prepared during the day, and was handed to Father Neptune; after perusing it he handed it to the President, Dr Brown who called out the names.
