<p>The whole Coy went for a bath this morning &amp; we had the band with us It was a lovely morning as <strike>wh</strike> we marched out, reached the baths &amp; had a good wash the second in 3 days not bad going, just as we finished it started to snow lightly, it came up very quickly but did not last long.<br/>The last couple of days we had a list posted up in our mess room &amp; for every time one of us swears in the mess room it is pencilled up &amp; every swear costs a penny, I have two to my name already, but I am not the only one who is going strong, it is a good idea for it keeps bad language down.<br/>This afternoon we were out manouvering &amp; attacking certain positions which were in the plan, we walked &amp; ran</p>
