<p>and after a bit of fooling about we were taken to our wards.</p>
<p>That night was about the best I had had since I had left home as I had a nice bed to sleep on and no shell's buzzing about<strike>me</strike> to wake me.</p>
<p>Next morning I was not allowed to get out of bed, in fact for 3 days after we were not out of bed, but it got very tiresome especially seeing all the people coming in to visit their friends and girls coming to see their boys, I can tell you it made one feel off side, although I had a mate and he was the same way.</p>
