
<p>May &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>26 SUN &nbsp;Fine day. Went into</p>
<p>Cassell in aft. Had</p>
<p>a very fair time</p>
<p>Home 9.PM French troops</p>
<p>in galore. Rec. letter from</p>
<p>Amy. Quiet night. Heavy</p>
<p>firing at daybreak.</p>
<p>27 MON &nbsp;Fine day. Windy.</p>
<p>Heavy firing all day. Rec:</p>
<p>letters from Else P. Gladys C.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sgt: Clarke arr: back. Writing</p>
<p>at night. Few bombs</p>
<p>28 TUES &nbsp;Fine day shelled out of</p>
<p>Wagon lines</p>
<p>by quick Dick. Camped about</p>
<p>a mile away for the night.</p>
<p>Went into Hondeghem at</p>
<p>night. Quiet.</p>
<p>29 WED &nbsp;Fine day. Moved to new</p>
<p>Wagon lines near Hondeghem in</p>
<p>aft. Quiet. fixing up camp</p>
<p>Few bombs. heavy firing</p>
<p>at night.</p>
