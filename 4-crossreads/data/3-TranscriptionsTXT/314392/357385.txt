But the 4th Pioneer Btn. is not fated to have easy times and on 6th August we shifted Camp and returned nearby our last camping ground at Daours. 7th Big arrangements are still being made and I guess Fritz is going to get the biggest hiding he ever had in his life. Charlie Dingle of Sydney and Bill Townsend of the Yarra City have been picked to go over the bogs tomorrow morning with half timbers and to carry pickets to mark the advance. Gee they certainly expect to move at some pace.
