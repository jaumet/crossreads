 16 October 1915 Saturday 
 Get leave  &amp;  knock round Helouan for a couple of hours but soon get full of it. The usual pest of locusts have invaded Egypt again just as it did when Moses was here. 
 1915 October 17 Sunday 20th After Trinity 
 Church at 10 am Dad  &amp;  I go for a drive to the Nile   &amp;  see Sakkara Pyramids in the distance We catch 5.30 pm to Cairo for a ride   &amp;  I get back home about 9 pm
