  
 Perham Down Camp Lieut. T. Graham, 9th M.G. Coy. 162 Q.M.S. W.H. Mathieson, 10th M.G. Coy. Capt. W.M. Algie to command temporarily. 
 23.2.17 - Lieut. J. Kissane reported for duty from 11th M.G. Coy, France. 24.2.17 - 17 Men were taken on strength from Aust. Details Camp. 7.3.17 - Company fired portion of Pt. 1, General Machine Gun Course. 10.3.17 - 5 Men taken on strength from Australian Details Camp. 13.3.17 - 24 Drivers having completed their course at Grantham returned to Perham Down. 14.3.17 - Company completed Pt. 1, General M.G. Course. 17.3.17 - No. 162 Q.M.S. Mathieson, to be 2/Lieut., A.I.F. List 149. 18.3.17 - Lieut. Bradburn returned having completed Course at Grantham. 25.3.17 - On account of General Mobilisation, 2/Lieut. Walker returned from Grantham. Orders were received to hold in readiness 5 Sections of strength, 1 Officer, 1 Sergeant, 1 Corporal, 12 Gun numbers, 3 Drivers and 1 Batman to act as emergency Machine Gun Sections for Home Defence.
