  
 Jly 20 a score of houseboats lined the side of the River. Jolly fine 'boats too some of them. At first I caught several crabs, but was going good at the finish. The Park is a real beaut', & old Father Thames runs smooth & clear between banks of emerald green. Had a good dinner at the Hotel overlooking the water & then made for home. Met Phyl' coming home from work, & the three of us walked home. Tip top supper at 10.30.
