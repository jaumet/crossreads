 Saturday 18 January 1919 Did Versailles with the YM &amp; gee it was great. also went to the opera with them &amp; saw A Ida. Was exceptionally good specially the orchestra. 
 Sunday 19 January 1919 Went to see the Australians play rugby against the french. Very poor game. Diggers won by 1 try to nil.
