<p>Dick Barlow on the Balkan Question. After the meeting was over Dick, Vic Hall &amp; I returned to my chamber, discussed a Christmas pudding that Dick had received, as well as many other interesting phases of our literary activities, &amp; our respective pasts &amp; futures, before they went off back to their respective homes. Incidentally I touched Vic on the raw rather, by dwelling on his part withdrawal from the good work, but the unpleasantness was only evident long enough to recognised as such before all was happy again.</p>
<p>Dick contemplated transferring to the infantry, but his transfer was refused much to my pleasure, so he will have to remain with us. As a man of tact Dick shines in a very definite matter. He is fat faced</p>
