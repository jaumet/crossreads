<p>they have alot of things in connection with their Business to do before that could be possible &amp; anyway they will not be allowed to leave the Country for some time yet.  I want to get Home &amp; well set up in business before that occurs also because I want to give them as good a time as they have given me.  I can give you no news about our return for we know nothing &amp; cannot gain any information as to when we are likely</p>
