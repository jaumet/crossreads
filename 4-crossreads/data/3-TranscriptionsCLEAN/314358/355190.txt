  
 17 Here they are, just landed from France. 
 Put out of commission by the enemy 
 Brave old guns which have done their duty and are back as hospital cases 
 Look at them! See this one has a breech which cannot open on account of a lucky hit to the credit of the Hun. 
 The solid brassworks of the block is kneeded into shapliness as though it were dough 
 Next is a smaller field gun with a wrecked carriage and its bullet proof sheild crumpled up and torne like brown paper.
