<p>July 1915<br/>4 Sun<br/>Walked with Capt Williams and Lane out to a Greek village 2&frac12; m. out. Villages and houses poor. Lunched on hopbeer sardines and bread. At 3.30 went on b Arragon and later on to Prince Abbas with 4 others for Anzac</p>
<p>5 Mon<br/>At 11am getting in to Lock Esk from Pr. Abbas an enemy submarine appeared about 5o yds off and the two boats parted hurriedly &ndash; Major Rutledge having Knox's box &ndash; went to Imbros and lost it there &ndash; I went to [indecipherable] till 4.30 when got on Lock Esk again &ndash; ashore 11.30 pm and thence to bivouac.</p>
