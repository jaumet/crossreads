  
 12 May 1918 Sunday Travelled all night - Nowell Forth met me on the Platform at Ishmalia & told me of Mardi Ritchie's engagement   He was well-  Breakfast at Rest Camp Gaza & change trains at Ludd for Jerusalem- expect it will be 3 months ere I go Cairo again- Got letters Mother 13/3/18  Mrs Strange   Day      " &Mr Robertson who manages Cocketchedone now, also Papers & Diary thank you for sending all-   My fountain pen bad
