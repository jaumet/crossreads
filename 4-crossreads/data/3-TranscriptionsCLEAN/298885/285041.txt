
Thu. Jan. 13th -16Mess Orderly.  Fell downstairs with can of coffee, dish of porridge and dish of stew.
Fri. Jan. 14th -16Innoculation for third time.  First rifle drill and also inspection of kit.
Sat. Jan. 15th -16Fire and boat drill in case of fire or emergency.  Concert at night.
Sun. Jan. 16th -16Church Service 10.30 A.M.
Mon. Jan. 17th -16Passed another ship.  Also passed Cape Guardefui on east coast of Africa.
Tue. Jan. 18th -16Passed Aden at present entering Red Sea.
