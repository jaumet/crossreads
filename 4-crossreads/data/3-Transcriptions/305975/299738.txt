<p>Saturday Nov. 23.11.18<br/>am feeling quite well to day  seem to have a swelling in the left side still  went up before the Doctor  showed it to him  he had a look and felt it  I felt no Pain.<br/>Sunday Nov. 24.11.18<br/>am feeling well  Paraded before Colonel Summers and was discharged from 14.A.g.Hospital to day  went to Porrt said rest Camp  arrived there 11.AM  was issued with belt</p>
