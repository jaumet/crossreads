In evening we went to Chu Chin Chow now in 4th yr. & were disappointed. It contains all the [indecipherable] of barbaric display & has natives from different parts of world.
13-5-19 (Tues)Get passport issued but not vise Thompson having wired to Codford decides it will be best & quickest to ride 300 miles in the train & bring the reply himself which turns out best as telegram does not arrive till 3 day after he has returned with the permission.
I visit Museum, Art Gallery & generally wander over London into places that I have not seen, & dine at night at Gustaves who chat with me & drink cognac till 11.40 pm
14-5-19 (Wed)Continue my wanderings of London but forget exactly where. Get passport vise for nothing this usually costs 8/- Thompson leaves for France. I dine [Au Petit Riche & say farewell to Elise. Get NME approval & am told to report back
15-5-19 (Thurs)Bought shirt etc at Ordinance for 1 - no uniforms Saw funeral of [indecipherable] Cavell - Band playing Chopin - visited army & Navy stores - Alcocks Kennedy - on way home met some French ladys on way to Elysee dance near Queens Rd. Lond
