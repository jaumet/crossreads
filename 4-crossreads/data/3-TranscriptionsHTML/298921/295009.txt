<p>February, 1915<br/>Friday 5<br/>Yesterday went to Pompie Pillar &amp; the Sphinx &amp; then on to Catatombs passing Natives quarters where incerinator buried up the dead on one side of street &amp; Baker cooks on the other &ndash; Afterwards to the museum to see Mummies from the different Tombs &ndash; 5000 years or more!!! old 3 Trains today for Cairo.<br/>Off in March we hear for France &ndash; only hope it is true.<br/>At 1 A.M. more tired &amp; colder than ever.</p>
