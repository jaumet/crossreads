 7 November 1915 Sunday 23rd After Trinity 
 Dad   &amp;  I go into Cairo after dinner   &amp;  have a very pleasant afternoon in the Sgts mess belonging to the Ordnance Dept of Kasr-el-nil Barracks   &amp;    Also   go through the Cairo Museum. 
 1915 November 8 Monday 
 Spend most of the day pottering about in Dad's work-shop. having nothing to do in my own Coy. I have applied to attend a School of instruction in Zeitoun.
