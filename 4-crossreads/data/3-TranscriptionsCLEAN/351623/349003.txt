
11 Dec 16Left Rosyth with Second Light Cruiser Squadron, HMS Southampton, HMS Dublin HMAS Sydney & Destroyers.Proceeding to Scapay Flow.
12 Dec 16Arrived at Scapay Flow at 9 am. Coaled ship. Left again at 3.30 pm with 2nd Light Cruiser Squadron in search of German armed Merchantman.
13 Dec 16Cruising in vicinity of Iceland searching for German Raider.
14 Dec 16Searching for Raider. Heavy seas running
15 Dec 16Arrived at Scapay Flow at 5 pm & coaled ship
16 Dec 16 )17 Dec 16 )  Anchored at Scapay Flow
