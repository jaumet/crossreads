<p>May 29 togged out as a Kitcheners Army man. Oh Lord! These women! &amp; their letters. Have just received three long effusive epistles from Leeds. Ewart my son, I'm afraid you're in for it; &amp; it's a pity too, because you hate visiting &amp; kidstakes. And again you'll have to behave real well &ndash; perhaps "Gods demesne, Australia" is for "mine" always.  Just licked a couple of other patients at Croquet; rather a nice, quiet game it is &ndash; for one eyed men. Left for Harefield per motor bus at 2 pm. My mate &amp; I were on top &amp; had a glorious view. We passed through some beautiful Country, made a fellow feel happy to be alive &amp; well. Arrived here about 4.30 changed into Hosp suits, saw the Doc, &amp; then had tea. It's a ripping place &amp; our own nurses too! The village is just close by &amp; the grounds &amp; country round about are beautiful. A fellow can go for a nice<br/>[in margin] Harefield</p>
