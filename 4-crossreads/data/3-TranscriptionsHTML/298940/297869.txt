<p>German machine guns were being busy too, for a hail of bullets came over our dug out.<br/>,strike>[indecipherable]</strike> Although we were ready for a trip at any moment, strange to say, no ,strike>cassulties</strike> casualties came through our way, &amp; after about an hour we were able to go to sleep.<br/>This morning we found out that Fritz had raided one of the battalions of our brigade &amp; taken six prisoners. Three or four wounded passed through the other post, so we did very little out of it.</p>
