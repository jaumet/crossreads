<p>Thursday 11 January 1917 [1918]<br/>&frac12; day &amp; a glorious day to have for it &ndash; went to town &ndash; in Ambulance, then on the old Nile for 2 hours, pay Pt. 4 any town trip for 20 minutes, pay Pt. 5 per hour for boat &amp; like "Solomon with the one eye" (reliable) give backsheesh Pt. 1 or 2.</p>
