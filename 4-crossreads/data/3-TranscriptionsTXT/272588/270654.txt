
Saturday 2 FebruaryIn pack store. The thaw has set in & the ice on the trees is falling down very heavily.  Aerial activity.
Sunday 3 FebruaryWeather fineFrom the Airdrome nearby a new bi plane has been on trial.  Rumour has it that it is 90 feet across the wings & carries 1 ton of bombs.  It is a tremendous size & has two sets of engines.  Looks very stable but slow.
