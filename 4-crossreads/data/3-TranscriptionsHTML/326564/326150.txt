In their Battalion, after this was over the whole lot of us were marched down to our parade ground &amp; we done an hours physical training but it was nearly all games &amp; we had a rare bit of amusement.<br/>The afternoon being a half holiday 3 others &amp; I took the opportunity to slip up to the baths at the Infantry School to see if we could get a wash, but our luck was out for the baths had stopped for an hour or two, so we spun the bloke in charge a yarn, &amp; he gave us each a clean change of new clothes this done us down to the ground so we came back stripped off &amp; had a cold water bath took our breaths away for a start but it was   bonza after the first shock of electricity was over.</p>
