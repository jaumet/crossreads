
W 1 Dec SunJoe & another come over to Tea & we go down Town. Go to Church at R.C. &  then Y.M.C.A. We take 2 WAACs for walk & wWe misses the tram.Recd letters from Mum. Maisie Carter.  & from M.R.L. on 2/12/18. Rotten Day
W 2 MonE. goes to Hospital & Thomas also. I go down at Night to Casino. Go to YMCA hit concert gives me ding-bats- hat so feeling very rotten. I come Home & write & then go to bed. Wrote Con. MRL. Marion Clarke & F.D & Hunter
W 3 Tues.I catch [indecipherable]. Recd letter from Dad & parcel from Whites. I am still my crook. I do Carter for Val. Recd parcel from White. Posted parcel Town Havercrafts. Wrote [Haveray/]. Wrote MRL & Aunt Annie. Elsie. Freemantle
F 4 Wed& Wet. Go down to Casino at night. Go concert & supper stay until nearly 9 pm. Meet WAACs & give message N.Z. Sister gives me wine. Wrote Aunt Annie
5 ThHarvest of go in with Monkey. Go down Town on Yankie motor. Go to Museum & along Buckwater to see Sea planes. I go shopping & to YMCA at night to American Concert. Ellis at Home from Hospital. Wrote M.R.L & Aunt Annie & Mrs Clarke.
F 6 FriI am bad tempered at night for Carter Job. Recd letter from Mrs Edmonds & [indecipherable] from May Clarke. Go to bed at 7 pm very tired. He is very  [smoogy?]. I dream of M.R.L. Millie Porter. Con. Elsie Freemantle. M is lovely & so kind to me, so gentle & loving
F 7 SatGlorious Day. Write all afternoon & go shopping & to watch [indecipherable] display in YMCA. Meet Joe at YM. Recd parcel of Tinned fruit Coco etc. from Home. I am lonely but so Happy realise that the comfort & Happiness is thoughts of loved absent ones is quite true. I am getting better & happier every day. E is Ord. Sergt Major to note. Wrote Dad. Mum. George. Aunt Annie.
