<p>19 April 1918 Friday<br/>Bought 5 Rabbits for 2/- each for Pasteur Institute, Jerusalem wanted for Bacteriological purposes, &amp; the M.G. was sending to Cairo for them<br/>Took them in;  many natives being inoculated a green parrot was imitating their yells when the needle went in, awful curs, <strike> &amp; no one sort</strike> the cleverness of the Parrot.  I see a poor old Galah occassionally, natives will spend hours in front of a talking parrot.</p>
