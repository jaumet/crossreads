<p>Southern Pacific Stn and got a seat in Pullman Car to Frisco &ndash; 8 am Monday morning &ndash; costing 1.65 dollar. Also met a nice Canadian lady for asked if I were fixed up alright, also if I wanted any money. Walked about till 2.30 pm, having lunch at the Boston &ndash; then went to office and met Mr Mathews. He then came around with me and we went thro' some of the department stores.</p>
<p>After tea played draughts all evening. Bought some chocolates for the two girls.</p>
<p>16.3.19 Sunday<br/>Stopped home all day reading and talking. Strolled into town before lunch and again in afternoon went in by tram. Nice fine day.</p>
<p>17.3.19<br/>Up 6.15 am &ndash; breakfast 6.45 Mr Van Norman called with car at 7.30 am and drove Mt Mathews and Self to Southern Pacific Stn. Train timed to leave 8 am but left 8.30 on a/c of New Orleans train being late. My seat was in last car just near the observation platform.</p>
<p>Travelled via Montalvo, Ventura (where there are numbers of oil wells), Santa Barbara &ndash; which is rather a pretty little town -, San Luis Obispo &ndash; where 2 extra engines were put on to pull up into the mountains. Just near this place is a large horseshoe curve on a very heavy grade, and near Santa Barbara saw oil wells right out over the sea.</p>
<p>Leaving Los Angeles we ran down centre of Alameda St for about 2 miles to Continue travelling via Del Monte and San Jose, arriving San Francisco 10.45 pm.</p>
