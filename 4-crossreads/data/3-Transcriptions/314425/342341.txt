It is stoked with English fish of various kinds also shells &amp; [indecipherable].<br/>The Acquarium is alongside the citadel and is built out of stone that once formed the outer ramparts of the citadel.<br/>We had tea at the Royal Sailors Rest Devonport.</p>
<p>Tuesday December 3. 1918. 23.<br/>We are to be issued with new hats and as they have not arrived yet, we are detained here until they come. That may be any day or it may not be for a week.<br/>We were issued with tobacco &amp; cigarettes today.<br/>About 1000 Australians</p>
