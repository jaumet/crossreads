
In the evening Jack came to see me in the Dispensary & he & his friend with Graysmarsh & I, adjourned to the canteen, where to my amazement I disposed of three shandies.
Monday 31st January
Weather very much better to-day.  Cold nights still.Prisoner brought in this afternoon from the Citadel, who had been a jeweller in town.Arrested & sent to Citadel where he fell sick.  Now in our hospital.Given Turkish rations today of olives & meat cake which he would not eat (of course).Poor fellow is very thin & looks ill & being hours without food
