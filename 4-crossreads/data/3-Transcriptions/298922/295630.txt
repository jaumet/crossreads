<p>Tuesday 27th November 1917</p>
<p>Skipper says good news came through on the Marconi last night, he will not say "what" so must wait patiently till Malta is reached.</p>
<p>Clear fine day &amp; a calm sea once more. Do you remember me telling you of my voyage this time last year when we were nearly wrecked off the USHANT ROCKS.</p>
<p>[The remainder of the page crossed through.]<br/>Our Convoy now numbers 14, &amp; more boats will leave us tomorrow &ndash; I write more than is good for others than you Mother to read.</p>
