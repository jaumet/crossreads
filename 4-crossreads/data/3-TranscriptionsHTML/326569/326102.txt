<p>Forward Movement Mechanism</p>
<p>Action of Return Spring &ndash; When gasses have expended then forces the return spring, unwinds, in doing so it rotates pinion &amp; teeth of pinion being engaged in teeth of rack forces piston rod forward.</p>
<p>Action of Bolt &amp; Ejector &ndash; As bolt travels fwd. the top face of bolt striking base of bullet &amp; forces it into chamber &amp; Extractors are sprung over rim of bullets &ndash; bolt is fully home.  Recess on left of bolt face kicks head of ejector into its housing &amp; ejector being pivoted the tail goes into bolt way ready for next backward movement.</p>
