<p>June Monday 4 1917</p>
<p>Duty again at 6 a. m to 2.30 p.m. Off now till 2.30 tomorrow. Jock &amp; I go down W. rouge vin &amp;amp sweets. Plage. Visit old fort, tide rises rapidly nearly catches us. Y.M.C.A for drink &amp; sup. Past few days quite hot.</p>
