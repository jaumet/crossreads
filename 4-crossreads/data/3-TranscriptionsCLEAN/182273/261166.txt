September   1918
22 SUN  Dull. windy day. raining at
night. Boys left
for Australia 8 am. Good
send off. No amm.
Guns active on both sides. Yanks
started to arrive. Rec. letters
from Maggie & Amy Mc. Writing
at night. Quiet.
23 MON  Fine day. 1st Div. Inf went
out of action. Yanks in galore
coming in & taking over. Our
artillery to cover them. Guns
active on both sides. Rec. letter
from Amy. Writing.
24 TUES  Fine day. Amm. going
up for stunt. Americans & flying
columns & B.A.C's arriving in galore.
Tanks going up. Quiet. Few
bombs at night. very handy
Little rain. fine after. Posted letters
25 WED  to Amy. Maggie & May. Amm
going up in galore also guns
etc. Good news from the east.
'Hun' counter attack in morning
repulsed. Quiet. Few bombs
at night.
