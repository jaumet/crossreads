<p>Sept. 4th.<br/>Spent quiet day in Longuevillette prior to entraining for (?) &ndash;<br/>(Apple fights)</p>
<p>Sept 5th &amp; 6th.<br/>Night march commenced 1am &ndash; arrive at Doullens station 3a.m. entrain 5am and after uneventful journey arrive at Proven at 2p.m. (First glimpse of Belgium March to Poperinghe &ndash; We encamp at Canadian Camp on outskirts of town in the vicinity if Ypres.</p>
<p>Sept. 7th.<br/>Very pleased with new Camp &ndash; As we are in a very prominent position as regards German gas attacks, a most vigilant watch is maintained.<br/>"Impressions of Belgium"</p>
