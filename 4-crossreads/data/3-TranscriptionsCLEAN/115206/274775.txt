  
 July 1918 Wednesday 24 
 Frechencourt. Weather showery, rode for a little exercise to Orville via Sarton, Rainches, Toutencourt, Contay, Bavelincourt Behencourt and Frenchencourt. Met Adrianne at Sarton and had a very exciting afternoon received the usual. Arrived home at 11pm after a very wet ride. Received a letter from Bully Clement, stating he was due to sail in a weeks time for Australia. answered same but doubt whether he will receive same.
