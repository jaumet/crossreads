<p>the 22nd December we got the train to Hamburg. At Hamburg an English Transport the "City of Poona" was waiting for us.<br/>On the 26th December we arrived in Hull England From here we took the train to a camp at Ripon in Yorkshire. Here I was fitted out with a uniform. on the 31st December we proceded to London on a months leave.</p>
