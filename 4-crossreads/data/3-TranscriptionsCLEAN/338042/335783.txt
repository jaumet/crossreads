  
 January 1918 
 1st: New Year's Day - A Happy, Bright & Peaceful New Year  to friends & enemies - Our Hall graced by the Gen. Sir Wm Birdwood who shakes with one & all after the show and is mighty pleased with his Concert Party - General Coxon also present. 2nd Two Australian Letters received. A strange coincidence concerning one - Our Panto has a most disastrous ending this evening - A fire takes place in the engine room & two engineers are badly burned
