  
 11 April 1918 Thursday An uneventful day as most are in camp. At night RSM. Smith and I went to a concert given by the Kangaroos - the No. 3 Command Concert party. It was very  good  but not so good as that given by the Owls. 
 1918 April 12 Friday Nothing of any importance going at all. This is a very dull & uneventful place after being used to so much Military work & bustle in the Field At night Smith & I went to Albany Wards Show. A very medium affair of pictures & Vaudeville
