<p>Saturday 30th March<br/>Went out to the job until about 11 till in about 2 PM with full packs for inspection. Were just dismissed &amp; had got into our Humpie when he started drooping 5.9 the last one fell just out side our door &amp; caught the 3 of us Pross &amp; Thompson very badly. Damaged my R hand a bit. Bell was out side near the back he only got one in the leg. Walked to the Dressing Station at Vormassle. Fresh</p>
