Kemp &amp; I went down to Elbow Farm for tomorrow's rations.  This was at about 9 p.m., and on the way back, just in rear of the farm on the road nine bullets flew past us.<br/>Yesterday's paper "The Daily Mail" refers to our recent fight as a raid.</p>
