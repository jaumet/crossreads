1916 September 10
Sunday 
Scotch ? Canadian conducts service in mess Room at 10.30 a. m. very good. Fine singing. In aft. we wait for Tony who does not arrive. Cart, Bob self go for walk to next village. Fascinating waitress in tea room. Buy two brass serviette rings. Back to service at Canadian hospital. Another good time. Y.M.C.A after, then tea biscuit.
