<p>30 Oct 18<br/>Out with 2nd L.C.S. running torpedoes back to anchorage off munition factory.</p>
<p>31st Oct 18 )<br/>1 Nov 18&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;) Anchored off Charlestown<br/>2 Nov 18&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;)<br/>3 Nov 18&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;)</p>
<p>4 Nov to 10 Nov<br/>Anchored off Charlestown</p>
<p>11 Nov 14<br/>Hostilities ceased today on all fronts, result being that everyone is very happy. Served a tot of rum to all hands &amp; we had some singing &amp; band. Ships were blowing syrens &amp; sending up rockets, all round the harbour.</p>
<p>12 Nov 14<br/>Went ashore in afternoon to Dumfermline to celebrate.</p>
