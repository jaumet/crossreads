  
 1916 Jan 21st Further organizing of personnel. Major P. Vaughan, LH, to rejoin his unit while awaiting his new position, came on to the strength for rations & tentage, but not duty. Major Bisley V.C., with Lts Beck, Bare, & Dobson arrived with 414 of other ranks: of these the 4th & 5th reinforcements of the 12th LH were immediately attached to 'A' Sqn. 14 men two up school after several warnings & note in orders: 96 hrs C.B. ea.. 22nd  Saturday Completed personnel recommendations list. As the 12th LH reinfents of the 6th & 7th portions of 12th LH were sent under my charge I went to Maadi for information as to all the details of the 2nd LH Bde: but could get none. 1st LH Bde were for 3 officers & 40 others for 3rd LH instructed to prepare draft for "C" Sqn  23rd Notice that Brdr Col Cox CB. to arrive Monday Lt Whitfield reports: allotted to 'B' (11th LH) All papers, & recommendations completed. 6
