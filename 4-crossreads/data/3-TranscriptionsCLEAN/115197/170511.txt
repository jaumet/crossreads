  June 16th  
 Read "The Pocket Thackeray", which consists of popular excerpts from the works of the novelists - Note book entries - The "Rio Negro" is now reported to be 350 miles behind our ship.  A nasty cold causes discomfort.  A "home remedy" is brought to light. 
  June 17th  
 Nearing the "Cape" - four days sail to Cape Town.  Will we be allowed ashore? 
 "Quiet Talks about the Tempter".  (S.D. Gordon.)
