<p>22-2-17<br/>Thurs 22nd<br/>Jack Wright came over today &amp; by all accounts he had a great time while in at the School . Heard today that we go in to the school next Saturday morning.</p>
<p>23-2-17<br/>Fri 23rd<br/>Word came through this afternoon that men going to the school must catch the 8.20 train at Abd tomorrow morning, busy tonight handing over to Elliott &amp; straightening things up as we have to get away from here at 5.30: There are a good many going in from this Regt for different courses.</p>
