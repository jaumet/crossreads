<p>Sunday [it seems this should be September] 1917<br/>Sunday 16th<br/>This is a very quiet little village but there is plenty of rum &amp; fun about<br/>Monday 17th<br/>Had a motor trip to the country today<br/>Tuesday 18th<br/>Put on a job painting the new bricks so that is another new trade on to the very long list<br/>Wednesday 19th<br/>Still slapping paint about<br/>Thursday 20<br/>Nothing doing<br/>Friday 21st.<br/>A draft of P.B's arrived today so we expect to be leaving tomorrow or the next day<br/>Sunday 23rd<br/>Left here at 2 pm arrived in Boulounge at 6.30 pm doing the 25 miles in that time so it was [indecipherable] stopped in Boulounge for the night</p>
