
Mr Southwell. Very cold all day & showery rain. 27/3/18 Paid today. Wrote home. Fairly cold & fine. Met a chap named Boldra of 4th btn who was in camp with me at Dubbo.28/3/18 Warned at 9am to go on PB draft at 5pm. Full marching order less rifle, bayonet, & ammunition. Started raining after dinner. Left Havre Camp at 5.40pm, left camp adjutants 6.30pm & caught a tram through Havre arriving Gare maritime about 7.30pm. Am placed in charge of 2 other chaps & going to No1 C.C.S. Estaires. Raining heavily again.29/3/18 Got into trucks of a leave train plying between Arras & Havre at about 11pm. last night. Train left 1.45am.
