<p><u>Friday 27th March</u><br/>Moved into convalescent depot No 1 on Monday by char a banc, &amp; found it none too cheerful because injuries were few, money non existent as far as we were concerned, &amp; food only bare.<br/> The impressions of the camp (which was situated half an hour's walk from the hospital almost in Boulogne, &amp; near the Napoleon statue) were of countless particulars being taken on countless parades &amp; inspections, most of them run by Warrant Officers <strike>of</strike> both of first &amp; second class.
