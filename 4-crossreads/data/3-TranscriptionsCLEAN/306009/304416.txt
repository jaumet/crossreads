  
 1915 Aug. 20 Fri. Our former Quartermaster - Capt. C. Gray got the sack when we were out at Mena, and was sent back to Australia for certain irregularities in connection with his department. To-day a new Q.M. arrived for us from Alexandria - Lieut. Beers - he was Q.M. of 1st Field Ambulance. W.O. Emmett had been acting Q.M.
