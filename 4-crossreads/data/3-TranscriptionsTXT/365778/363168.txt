 Monday 15/1/17 Training progressing slowly &ndash; interviewed N.C.O's. 
 Tuesday 16/1/17 
 Rifle Range completed commenced firing. Concert at night very Good &ndash; letter from Bas. 
 Wednesday 17/1/17 
 Inspection of 7th Coy quite good &ndash; Rugby and Victoria Rules football matches. 
 Thursday 18/1/17 
 Murphy arrived for C.M. Cases went into Amiens for dinner with him. 
 Friday 19/1/17 
 Murphy left &ndash; snowing heavily, sports tomorrow. 
 Saturday 20/1/17 
 Excellent Sports Div Cmdr present marched past in forms very good day but bitterly cold.
