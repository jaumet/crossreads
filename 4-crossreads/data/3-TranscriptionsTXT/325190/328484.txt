 Nov 24th Since I have been with the boys I have got to know some of the new ones, &amp; had good yarns with the old ones. We are billeted in an old farm building in a hay loft over the main denuded rooms, &amp; are fairly comfortable.  We rise at 6.45 for parade at 7, march a short distance &amp; then have breakfast.
