<p>25 July, Friday</p>
<p>Go to Town, meet Os Young, Dave Vernon, Frank Falls. Buy boots. First time in Civvies. Go to Pictures with Dave. Buy fruit &amp; peanuts. Go out to Glebee. Come Home with Bert Cocks. Go to Manly with Mum about Brook. See Ray o'Grady's people.</p>
<p>Received several letters that I wrote in England.</p>
<p>26 July, Saturday</p>
<p>Recd Discharge &amp; medal. I do gardening &amp; clean up yard &amp; act as executioner of the Ducks. Look out old books &amp; photos.</p>
<p>Ring Mary &amp; Mrs Hoper 529K or to apply my job. I go to Dobbers, forget shirt, get dressed in dark mum's overcoat &amp; forget mungaree. Play cards &amp; win easily.</p>
