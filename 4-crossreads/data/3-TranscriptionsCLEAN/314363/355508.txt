  
 Sept 26th 18. Raining today. Yesterday afternoon I went as far as the old norman Castle, it appears to be ages old, standing on a  hill from which there is a fine view all round. Received some more mail from home. Friday Sept 27th Clevedon Red Cross Hospital Today has been very fine This afternoon I went for a walk in the village, & bought a few things, set of chess etc. This afternoon I had a new glass put into my watch, which had started going again. Received 2. & 5 francs yesterday, which was the 60 francs which I gave to Jack Coyle to mind, before going forward the last time. Saturday 28th. Clevedon This morning as usual after being dressed, which is usually after breakfast, I went out, bought the Times & sat in the sun reading. In the afternoon I went to the pictures, after tea went out for a short while, then came in & played a couple of games of chess with a bed patient. Sunday Sept 29th Clevedon Raining all day.
