<p>1915<br/>Jan. 9 Sat.<br/>Attended physical drill at 7 a.m.<br/>Sea a trifle rough with the result that I "fed the fishes" twice and felt a bit off during the morning.<br/>During the day read over some shorthand and learnt a few points about the typewriter. Have resolved to study on board :-<br/>(1) Military books, regulations, etc.<br/>(2) The Remington typewriter and all about it.<br/>(3) Short-hand.<br/>(4) French<br/>(5) Books on other educational subjects.</p>
<p>Sea much calmer in the evening.</p>
