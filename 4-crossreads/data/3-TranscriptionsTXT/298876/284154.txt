Krephren is 445 ft high &amp; MyKerinos 225 ft high. The Sphinx is also quite near here. It takes about an hour to run out in the tram &amp; is a grand run. I had my fortune told right in the Kings Chamber something like this &ndash; I am going to be married, will receive 16 days (800 Pts) will be back in Australia 2 months &amp; 13 days from date. Will get some shot or shrapnel in the back, but will get over it quickly, Going to attain a much higher rank &ndash; Wouldn't advise any nervous person to visit the Pyramids &ndash; as I suppose only some 75 percent of Soldiers complete the Tour. Had a run around Cairo at night &amp; back in camp at 10 pm.
