<p>April 1917<br/>with a party of about a dozen, near Fremecourt all day &amp; got a good supply.   It is officially announced that yesterday there was a severe ariel battle on this front in which 28 of our planes &amp; 46 of Fritz's planes were brought down, &amp; that 8000 prisoners were captured on this front since yesterday midday, &amp; also that the 1st Divn captured 3 villages Quient, Boursiers, &amp; Hermies.    to-day.<br/>10th   Rotten weather again to-day.   It has been snowing all night &amp; this morning we had a blizzard which ceased during the afternoon.<br/>It is officially announced at D.H.Q. to-day that the 3rd Army have captured between 40 &amp; 50 guns including a 9.5 battery</p>
