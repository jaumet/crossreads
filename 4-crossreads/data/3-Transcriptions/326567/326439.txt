<p>we had our Tents alloted to us we soon made ourselves comfortable &amp; turned in for a few hours sleep. At 10 am we had some more Tea &amp; Iron Rations &amp; then at 10.30 am were marched over the line to the Depot Head Quarters to give in our names &amp; what Railway Experience we had. This was necessary so as we could be placed in our positions. After Dinner all the boys rested, &amp; after Tea I had my first stroll in the Village near by called Audruick, On leaving our Camp we take a narrow Road bounded on one side with a Crop of Wheat &amp; on the other a Fruit Garden enclosed by a Hedge. The Trees are mostly cherries &amp; grow very large &amp; are now loaded with cherries.
