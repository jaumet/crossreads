<p>[Sketch map of Turk positions around Canterbury Slope by J.M. Antill dated 24.9.1915]</p>
<p>1915  24th Sept.<br/>Inspected Trenches with C.O.R.E.  again shelled by our own 18 prs. &ndash; third time in last 4 days &ndash; also by enemy at 1730, both Bivouacs and Trenches &ndash; One man killed and 3 wounded &ndash; former by our own guns &ndash; Working incessantly to improve our trenches and saps but are very short-handed and men dropping out each day.
