  
 Thursday 30 January 1919 Came in from Chatelet & had brekker at the club. Then going to the train. It consisted of converted trucks. Some have five places & some haven't so of course got in one with the [indecipherable] crowd of fellows were good. Had about 12 in our truck. About 1800 on the train. Had tea at Mong. Very cold out. 
 Friday 31 January 1919 Still on the train. Had tons of stops. The boys ratted a couple of supply trains & got wine etc. Quite a lot finished up being drunck. The driver took his engine off at Arras because he couldn't get a relief We waited there 10 hours till the frogs put an engine on. Got plenty of strain
