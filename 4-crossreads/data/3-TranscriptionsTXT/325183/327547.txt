I also made a noise.  7  Mrs. Saunders came along this morning and asked me to extract a bullet from cartridge case take out the powder and blow out the cap which I gamely consented to do. Mrs. Saunders is a fine little lady but suffers badly from deafness.  8th  I went up to see the Gypsum Works this morning and was shown all around the top by the top foreman Mr. Goodsall.
