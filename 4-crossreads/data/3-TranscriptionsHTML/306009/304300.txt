<p>1915<br/>Apr. 24 Sat.<br/>Tumbled out of bed, dressed &amp; went down to the office &ndash; experienced terrific shock when found it was 8 a.m. &ndash; flew down to camp to get a few scraps of breakfast &ndash; then back to work &ndash; appropriated couple of hours in the afternoon to write a few letters.</p>
