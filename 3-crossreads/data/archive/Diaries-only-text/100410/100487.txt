Did Harry Stead go to England with the Expedition? I may go there yet. The old Berrima ought to be back in Sydney again by now. She left last Sunday week. What did the people say when the prisoners arrived in Sydney. I'll bet the boys who took them back got a roaring reception or was it all done on the quiet. Well there is not much to talk about, all the days are alike nothing exciting happens. We had an explosion the other day. One man had his chest blown in, another I think will loose one of his eyes one or two other minor injuries. Five were injured
