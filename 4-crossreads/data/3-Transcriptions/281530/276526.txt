<p>7/7/18<br/>Still very heady and unwell especially late in the afternoon when a bit tired with letter writing and with reading. The band came down here and played me a rather good programme. The day fine and sultry like all days lately.</p>
<p>8/7/18<br/>Got up and sat in a chair for a while in the morning and also in the afternoon. A hot and clammy day. The Battalion moved back last night upon relief but are still well ahead and near Villers Brettoneux</p>
<p>9/7/18<br/>Got up, dressed  and wandered round. Eventually reached Bde HQ saw the General and returned with a leave pass for the 14th inst. Feeling very weak but otherwise very much better. Little news except they are going forward in easy [indecipherable]</p>
