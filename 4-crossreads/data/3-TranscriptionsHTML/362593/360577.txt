<p>Saturday 13 July 1918<br/>Recco Amman.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 3 h. 5 m.<br/>Took important aspect photos of viaduct &amp; tunnel, saw 2000 camels, machine-gunned them from 2000 ft. and killed a good many, did good recco, fired 400 rounds.</p>
<p>Sunday 14 July 1918</p>
