  
 27 October, Monday 
 Not too bright. Meet Rup Beale wife & Les Merrett. Motor Home. Walk from Depot 20 minutes. 
 Write Cards to M. Mr F & Mr E. recd letter & film from Dave Lord. 
 D.I.C.M. [In rectangular box] & S.L.I. 
 Letter to A. Annie, D Webster & H Rayner & Jennie. 
 28 October, Tuesday 
 Play whist & Bridge & write. 
 Wrote Marie 28.
