<p>all have been wiped out for a certainty, however we did not have a bad time, there was a lot of gypsies around selling orange lemonade &amp; etc. &amp; we enjoyed ourselves, though one of the officers got very cranky coming home, made the men march to attention &amp; threatened to put any one who spoke in the clink.<br/>28th. Usual early morning parade old Frith tried to catch me this morning, gave me a pretty difficult move to execute with the platoon, but I managed to do it correctly, though really I don't know how I came to do it, must have got an inspiration, I don't think any one was more surprised than I.</p>
