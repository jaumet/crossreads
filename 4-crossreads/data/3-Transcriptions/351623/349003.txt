<p>11 Dec 16<br/>Left Rosyth with Second Light Cruiser Squadron, HMS Southampton, HMS Dublin HMAS Sydney &amp; Destroyers.<br/>Proceeding to Scapay Flow.</p>
<p>12 Dec 16<br/>Arrived at Scapay Flow at 9 am. Coaled ship. Left again at 3.30 pm with 2nd Light Cruiser Squadron in search of German armed Merchantman.</p>
<p>13 Dec 16<br/>Cruising in vicinity of Iceland searching for German Raider.</p>
<p>14 Dec 16<br/>Searching for Raider. Heavy seas running</p>
<p>15 Dec 16<br/>Arrived at Scapay Flow at 5 pm &amp; coaled ship</p>
<p>16 Dec 16 )<br/>17 Dec 16 )  Anchored at Scapay Flow</p>
