<p>1916 June 4<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Our squad to Bull Ring in morning for instruction in Goat First Aid. <br/>Went for walk up to Headquarters P.O.  Nearly mulcted of 10 francs only for Bob's intervention .<br/>Meet Cartwright &amp; Nettleton. &amp; we stroll thro' Tommies' Camp  Well appointed. Anzacs barred from all refreshment &amp; recreation rooms. <br/>Back, to tea at our own Y.M.C.A  Church after. <br/>Hospital train &amp; wounded</p>
