<p>Monday 2&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />About Hospital all day.&nbsp;<br />Getting ready to send away a lot of men to Australia.&nbsp;<br />Letters posted to Girls &amp; Mollie&nbsp;<br />689 patients here tonight&nbsp;</p>
<p>Tuesday 3&nbsp;August 1915<br />Sent 369 patients to Australia this morning, they were amenable to discipline and gave no trouble.&nbsp;<br />Was in Cairo at 12 noon, interview with D.M.S. &amp; General Maxwell. Martin (L.Col.) had sent on my personal letter to Gen. Maxwell.&nbsp;<br />Mainfold here during the afternoon.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Wednesday 4 August 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo during the morning&nbsp;<br />116 patients admitted, from Alexandria and locally&nbsp;</p>
