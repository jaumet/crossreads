<p>are refugees from Belgium &amp; speak English very well. The little kiddie is a darling &amp; loves dangling on one's knees a very pretty kiddie with beautiful hair.<br/>Experienced a very touching good-bye at Vieux Berguin they were very sorry we had to leave. I was sorry too it was a very pleasant village all hands enjoyed their stay there.<br/>29-5-17. Had the afternoon off &amp; sauntered forth on the bike to try &amp; find Charlie Griffith &amp; Acko &amp; met with success. Found Acko on the corner near their camp &amp; was told Griff would be already with his team any minute so we sat down on the edge of the ditch &amp; waited.
