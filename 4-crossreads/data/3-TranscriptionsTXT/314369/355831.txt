  21 May   1915 Thursday Contd    Friday  the firing is resumed about 2 hours later   &amp;  kept up nearly all night together w/- heavy artillery fire. 
 Friday 21 may 
 Firing eases off during the morning   &amp;  we are relieved about midday Return to camp   &amp;  find dad very bad w/- piles. Go down to field Hosp. with him   &amp;  see him off to Hosp Ship about 8 pm. 
 1915 May 22 Saturday 
 Have a welcome rest for a few hrs. Nothing doing except a little fatigue duty
