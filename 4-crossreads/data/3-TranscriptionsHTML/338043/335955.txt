<p>J. Morris, T.J. Peaston (late of 7th Field Amb.</p>
<p>Aug. 14th<br/>Twenty first anniversary of Maude's birthday &ndash; Best wishes &ndash; A new book "Little White Bird" (J.M. Barrie) &ndash; An afternoon walk with Fred &amp; Bobby to Harfleur, which is the "great scene" of Shakespeare's Henry V. Being actually in this famous district I have no difficulty in creating visions of "Harry the King" &ndash; "His fighting Englishmen"</p>
