<p>not many persons would attend all these times to religious duty I enquired and found that Mahomet allowed a busy man to do all his praying at one time.  Before entering the sacred building we should have removed our boots but instead we were allowed to place slippers over them.  The interior is magnificent.  The walls are of polished alabaster and a lovely red carpet covers the floor.  The roof, worked in beautiful colours, is formed by a large dome (220 ft high, 120 ft in diameter) resting upon four smaller domes. It is said to be the only roof of this shape in the world and the designer was blinded by the ruler, Mahomet Ali, so that the work should not be duplicated.
