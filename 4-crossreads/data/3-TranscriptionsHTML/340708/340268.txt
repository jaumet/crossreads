<p>July 1917<br/>Sunday 8th<br/>We shifted our quarters today to the attic over the Hospital.<br/>Monday 9th<br/>Fritz flew over our way again tonight the anti aircraft guns were going to some [indecipherable]   He dropped a lot of bombs around a munitions works but did no damage.<br/>Tuesday 10th<br/>Very cold &amp; rough today so we were free from aeroplanes to night.<br/>Wednesday 11<br/>A big lot of patients evacuated today so we ought to have a quiet time for a day or two<br/>Thursday 12th<br/>The Hun has been putting it into Baillieul again so they have sent all there patients in here so our good time was short lived</p>
