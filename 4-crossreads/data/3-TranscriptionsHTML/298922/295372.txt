<p>Thursday 15 February 1917</p>
<p>On Duty last night but I don't think there is a TURK within 100 miles of us. Raining slightly.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through]<br/>Many were drunk from the Canteen on the Railway &ndash; Like South Africa greed is an awful curse to the young soldiers altho' I am no wowser. (Clive Norton in trouble.)</p>
<p>Had a bath &amp; put on a pair of "judpores", i.e. (Indian riding Pants), old Tommy Orford my Batman was greatly taken by the cut [The following crossed through]<br/>&amp; called a Tommy Officer &ndash; this is not a compliment out here. Hear a mail is coming along &amp; our pay &ndash; neck nearly allright.</p>
