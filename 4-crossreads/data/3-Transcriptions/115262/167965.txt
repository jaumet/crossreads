<p>Tuesday 17 July 1917.</p>
<p>Riding again this morning on mules. A little amusement also. A heavy fog hung over the desert for 3 hrs this morning.</p>
<p>Nearly all the A.M.C. Details &amp; L.H.F.A. Reinfts in Isolation area are leaving <s>for Gaza</s> tomorrow morning at 1.30 from this camp. Several of them put their names etc in my autograph album.</p>
<p>They moved off at 8 pm from our lines and are to leave Moascar station at 1.30 am tomorrow morning for &quot;up the line&quot;.</p>
