<p>Wednesday 18th<br/>Very hot today. Another shooting competition held.</p>
<p>Couple of chaps knocked out by the heat. Thousands of flying fish about, and several swallows seen.</p>
<p>Thursday 19th<br/>Getting hotter. nearly all going out with trousers only Worst of all I have to stay inside, on picquet. Flying fish still about. Last night chap had nightmare and gave submarine alarm</p>
