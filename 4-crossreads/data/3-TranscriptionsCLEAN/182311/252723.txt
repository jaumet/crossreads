 PETERS, CHARLES HAROLD             ML MSS. 1887 
 'SOME WAR RECORDS.  From June 1916 to Aug. 1919'.  1930.  MS., typescript and carbon  typescript 
 1v. 
 Captain Charles Harold Peters of Melbourne enlisted 21 July 1915, served with the 38th Battalion, A.I.F. in France where he was awarded the Military Cross and Bar.  He also served as an instructor.  He was discharged in Australia 19 Aug. 1919

 Include letters describing life in the trenches and in billets, raiding parties in which he was a successful leader, also essays (2) contributed to the Australian Imperial Forces War Records Competition,  notes for lectures and letters ffrom the Australian War Memorial
