<p>Of war so far. This is up to Sunday night</p>
<p>Monday 28th January<br/>Had a very short march this morning &ndash; without Packs Nothing in afternoon<br/>Can hear the guns from here &amp; saw some shell bursts around a plane this morning &amp; some to night behind us. They were a good distance away.</p>
<p>Tuesday 29th January<br/>Left Castre this morning at 9.30 &amp; marched to the Battalion about 12 to 14 miles. Got here about</p>
