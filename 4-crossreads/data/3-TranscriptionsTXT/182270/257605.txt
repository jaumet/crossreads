 MONDAY, OCTOBER 20, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; very hot &amp; windy. Darby got 
 a real good supply at Middle paddock 
 at least 12000 gal per day at 186 ft. 
 I brought Cy back at lunch time 
 and took the block &amp; tackle ect to 
 Mountain bore. Tickle &amp; I got all 
 the pipes ect up &amp; the sails of the 
 mill down.
