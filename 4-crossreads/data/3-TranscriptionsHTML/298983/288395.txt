<p>Saturday 16th March<br/>Did a day shift again</p>
<p>Sunday 17th March<br/>All hands went out in evening &amp; finished job by about 10 oclock. Had to stop work twice on account of gas shells. Fritz started to put over a lot of mixed stuff when I was about &frac12; way home was just nicely in front of it &ndash; only had to duck once. Things livened up at the Camp soon after we got there &ndash; Mostely Gas</p>
