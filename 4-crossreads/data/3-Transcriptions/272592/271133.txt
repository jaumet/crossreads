<p>The Hqrs are in a big tunnel &amp; here we got some "runners" &amp; they took us to the respective portions of trenches which each Coy. N.C.O. had to look over &amp; a nice state they are in without a word of a lie you have got to plough through sticky clay a foot deep &amp; in lots of places the duckboards are floating on top &amp; when you tread on them down you go talk about a time, when we finished inspecting, we were wet to the skin &amp; quite satisfied to make back to our bikes &amp;</p>
