  
 Monday 5 April 1915 A severe dust storm today. The wind began about 2 a.m and continued till about 6 p.m.  In Cairo for an hour. Spoke with General Williams at Shepheards hotel  Writing & reading all day. 
 Tuesday 6 April 1915 Acting O.C. today Colonel Martin away.  Letters from Car, Joe & Kitty. 
 Wednesday 7 April 1915 Acting O.C. today Colonel Martin at Alexandria  Letters from Mrs Fraser of New Port. Isle of Wight.
