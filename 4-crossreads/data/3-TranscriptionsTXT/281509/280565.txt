 8 December 1916 Friday 
 Lay abed till 10 a.m. scratch breakfast, wet wood &amp; sulky fire. No 3 squad on cooking.  Nothing doing all day.  Fritz shells consistently &amp; makes us remain under ground. Write a couple of letters to Em. Gulliford &amp; Mrs. Gelling. also card to Rev Inglis, Ain MacL &amp; Bob. Case at 11 p.m. dies at station
