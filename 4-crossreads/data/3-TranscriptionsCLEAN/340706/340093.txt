 7-8-19 I decide to pay a last visit to quant little Montivilliers & visited all places shown on p.c. The Bois a thickly wooded sunken road with a sheen on their newly shed leaves which embrace all shades of green is glorious. All the way on the right land dips into a gully cultivated on both sides & cut by a railway line on which few trains ever run perhaps for fear of destroying the rustic beauty of the place. 
 8-5-19 (Thur) Orders for embarkation remain unchanged. Baggage is collected at 9 am. - I ask for a coat from Scanlon but he is unable to issue it though he he QM so I ask a cpl Q.M. Coy & he at once gets me one
