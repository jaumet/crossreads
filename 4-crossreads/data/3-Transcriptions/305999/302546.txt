<p>26/10/16<br/>At 8.30 we fell in with every thing and marched 4 &frac12;  miles back to Albert because our larst quarters where condemmed not fit for us</p>
<p>28/10/16<br/>We where isued with a yellow patch to so on our packs   it is for the aeroplanes and artillery to reckernise us.  The first time we where down here we had pink ones    on Anzac we had white ones 6/2/15<br/>We have been isued with our steel helmets and box resperators again</p>
