  
 F [2/12/17 Sun] Perfect Day. read all afternoon. Officers play Hockey. I have big supper. Wrote Mum & sent snaps. Sent Photo to Maisie P.C. to dad & George. 
 F [3/12/17 Mon] Go to Albany Wards. E. D & I. Have Hockey meeting. Rotten show & E goes crook at Concertina 
 F [4/12/17 Tues] Tries to snow & I go to S. by Train. Go for walk & after Tea up to Edm. for a walk Temp way below Zero. Recd. letter from A. Annie. 
 F [5/12/17 Wed] Am Ord. Corp. E. goes to S. Recd. letter from Norm. Upton. Wrote to Mum & Dad. Dream that [Indecipherable] washed overboard in Indian Ocean 
 F [6/12/17 Thurs] but very heavy Frost. Dream that Maisie has pony in our Hand cart We play Hockey against the Colonel etc. & play a draw. We have a supper in Honour of the occasion Reg. Top. E & I. I am elected Secretary of mess etc. Write to George. 
 W [7/12/17 Fri] Misty Rain. Nothing doing I read my Book "Our Mutual Friend. Wrote Billy. Norm. Con. M.R.L. Supper 
 F but Dull [8/12/17 Sat] Stay in all afternoon & read Book. E & Top go out for a walk. Nothing doing after Tea except Read. Supper [Indecipherable] in a Paisley shawl SV raiment R. E. booked.
