
June 25thNothing unusual. Mass 7.30a.m.

June 27thLetter to E.C.
June 28th"The White Company"
June 29thI again meet Roger Morgan - Capt. Strachan annoyed by the Boys.
June 30thPreparations for evacuation of Ft Rompu.
July 1stWe leave our "Good old home" at 10.30am for Bailleul - March not enjoyable owing to rapid pace heat of day etc - We arrive at our new quarters - very tired.
