 14 December, Sunday 
 Up at 4.30. Lovely morn fishing we go 15 off rocks other all round lake but to rocks &amp; I get bites even. Swim. Girls run in bush. Struggle round to swim. Dress on boat. Good train Home 9.30. Supper of Toma &amp; apricots &amp; go bed at Beulah. 
 15 December, Monday 
 Come Home &amp; Dad &amp; Mum go to Progress Meeting &amp; I go to bed.
