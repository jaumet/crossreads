<p>17.11.16<br/>Fri 17th<br/>Had to bustle around today to get the lines cleaned up &amp; ready to move off at 9 oclock arrived at Willegha at 9.30 &amp; found A squadron has a very comfortable Camp; the Hod is only big enough to hold one Sqdn &amp; C Sqdn struck Camp to the East of the Hod &amp; B to the West, The only drawback is, we have to go about half a mile to water our horses; Put this afternoon in erecting shelters &amp; making ourselves comfortable.  A Troop of Anzac Mounted Police came along this evening for experience in patrol &amp; Outpost work &amp; they are likely to get it; <strike>Harold</strike> Leo Peake is with them, &amp; had a long yarn with him tonight.</p>
