  
 Tuesday. 3/4/17 The Boys held the Village alright & the worst part of all begins viz collecting the dead. The snipers are still busy & have got some of our chaps already Passed 5 dead huns on the road we don't give them time enough to bury  them. No chance to write letters or get any. Pay 13/7. 23.6.0 up to present have had 1 Piece of Toast. 1 Piece of damper & 2 Pieces of Bread & margarine in 3 days & 1 drink of Tea. 4. P.M. Just got word that we go out 53rd relieving us go to Velue again. Met young Stagg of Tamworth. Knew Mother & Loo. Dvr in 12th field Ambulance. C. Sect. off through Bemetz at 8 PM. got to Velue about 10.30. Bread & milk A Tommy gave us Tea on the road here. Rained all the way back. 
 Wednesday. 4/4/17 been snowing for hours. about 6 or 7 inches deep. & this is spring Anyway the starlings & sparrows are nesting. It was a beautiful walk last night our sweet Sgt. half drunk & quarrelsome all the way. He took 3 Pulls at Mr Gardeners Bott without leave, hide enough for anything lost 7 officers in the stunt B. Coy took in 160 men came out with 72. rain & snow all day. Pay. 14/7. 23.7.0. 
 Thursday. 5/ 4/17 off to Le Barque up at 7. dread the walk. 11 miles or more. fritz slinging shells over us early this morning woke me up. Pay 15/7. 23.8.0. Got here about 4. done up as usual wont be able to stand much more of it.
