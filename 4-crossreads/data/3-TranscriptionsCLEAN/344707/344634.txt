
Dec 2ndto get home news especially at presentDec 3rd1916Going Port Said to look for Kit as do not want to go- without certain Articles - Expect our Regiment to go --  --  & have a fight there, Spend Xmas day there!!Sorry my greetings to all at Home will be late - Published soldiers letter in Walgett Paper, from our local Parson re Red X  works, "I notice lot of pyjamas made by the dear Ladies of W,algett which made me feel in touch with you all !!!Dec 4thMajor -- & now OC. And his fiancee arrived from Australia & were married yesterday - We give him a dinner tonight. I speak of we, I am in Detail camp awaiting instructions - Am a bit stiff after ridin, being so long awayFound mongoose in  my tent, a pet one , of one of the Bengal Lancers, I saw one, once before "out back".
Decbr 5thInspecting our meat supply this morning I saw it came from the Argentine - Word has just come for me to join my regimentDecbr 6Off to - may be something soon - Xmas greetings to all
