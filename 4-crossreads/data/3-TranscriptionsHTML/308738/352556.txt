<p>Nov. 23 Tuesday 1915<br/>Reported sick at Base Camp &amp; was sent to Ghizera Hospital No. 2 General put to bed &amp; dieted.<br/>The hospital is manned by Australian Dr &amp; Sisters</p>
<p>Nov. 24 Wednesday 1915<br/>In bed all day.<br/>The Dr attending me is a fine chap &amp; appears anxious to put the men on their feet again.<br/>The food is excellent but I cannot eat.<br/>The Dr name is "Mayo"</p>
<p>Nov. 25 Thursday 1915<br/>In bed all day.<br/>The sisters are exceeding kind &amp; attentive. They are very strict with what a man should eat.</p>
<p>Nov. 26 Friday 1915<br/>In bed all day.<br/>The ward No 8 is a fine place on 2nd floor, &amp; is well ventilated. The sun shines in &amp; makes the place bright &amp; cheerful.</p>
