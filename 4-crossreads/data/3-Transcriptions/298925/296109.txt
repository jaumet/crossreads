<p>so the servant took us in.  We were amazed with the thick carpets, beautiful furniture, Silver plate etc. but what interested us most were the paintings of all the dead &amp; gone Carys looking down at us <strike>for</strike> from the walls just as you read about in books.  On one of the pictures is the inscription, Presented to Lord Cary by King Charles II.</p>
