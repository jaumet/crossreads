<p>after some opposition. Two Brigades of the 60th Division supported by N.Z. Bgde were attacking Shunet Nimrin &amp; El Haud. At 1530 the Bgde less the 7th Regt was ordered to proceed to the left flank via Umm es Shirt road &amp; join the Australian Mounted Division. The Regt forming the Advance Guard, marched via Ghoraniye &amp; watered at Wadi Nimrin. A guide from the Australian Division joined us at Umm Es Shirt &amp; we cut across to the foothills &amp; marched up No 7 road. It was very precipitous &amp; rough and out of the question for any wheels which had all been left behind. We all had to dismount &amp; lead our horses to the top of the plateau arriving at Divisional Hdqrs on the morning of the 1st May about 2 miles from Es Salt which had been taken by the 3rd Bgde the night before. We halted here &amp; had breakfast &amp; fed up &amp; watered until Divisional Hqtrs &amp; the 1st Bgde had got clear, as the road was so rough &amp; narrow we could only march in single file.
