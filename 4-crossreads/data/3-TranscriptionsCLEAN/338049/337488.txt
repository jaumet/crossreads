  
 Saturday 5 October Up & preparing for move off. Packed up & ready to move early but  delay  to wait. Finally had dinner & moved off 12.30. 
 Moved to near Halle & had to wait for 9th Bde transport. Detailed for billeting party so rode forward with officer 6 others to Marcourt. Division of transport on rode. 
 Very longer. Fixed up gully at Marcourt. Colum arrived about 9-30.
