 8 Thursday Cold &amp; grey.  Cooked all day at Minerva.  Ankles bad by 5.30 but still got thru  day and feeling much bucked thereby. 
 9 Friday Fine day.  Waited &amp; cooked at Minerva &amp; got thru all right  considerably bucked thereby.  Heels &amp; feet hurting 
 10 Saturday Pottered &amp; slept.  In afternoon Mrs. Holmes came &amp; after supper we all went to call on Evelyn Isilt in Moscow Court.  No rain.
