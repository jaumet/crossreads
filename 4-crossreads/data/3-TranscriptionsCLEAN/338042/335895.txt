  
 June 17th Reveille 6 a.m. - Pack up & leave for Montreuil (G.H.Q's) - An omnibus ride of 65 kilos - We pass through some very fine country & some equally wretched villages - A spell at Abbeville - Arrive Montreuil 1 p.m. General Haig's H.Q's - This small town was originally fortified by Romans & for hundreds of years has been a garrisoned town - It stands on a great hill from which surrounding villages & landscapes may be well observed.
