I am writing this from Villers B. as I came up here last night - The Division is I believe pulling out for a few days rest - Our Infantry could do with a few months after what they have gone through.
Hot weather & the dust in the roads terrific - the traffic on the roads has been enormous but this will be relieved in a few days if not already the railway will be connected through with the advance
