<p>24 September 1914<br/>Nothing unusual. In the evening watch from 8 to 12.</p>
<p>25th Sept 1914<br/>Cruise to Colombo. Probably we are to clear another trade route. At dusk the gleam of a far off searchlight appears in the sky . At first we do not know whether it is from a warship or from the coast of Ceylon. As we approach Colombo the gleams of 4 searchlights on the coast of Ceylon are seen on the horizon.</p>
