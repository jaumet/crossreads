About 11 oclock Bellchambers blew in, in a terrible state, had been waiting for hours to guide the 11 th Batt. party in they never turned up, however we set to cooked him a good meal persuaded him to stay the night Next thing 3 oclock in the morning the long expected party turns up wants me to hand over the orders at once. I told him not very politely either Im afraid to go to a very hot place be damned for my past, so they camped for the night in some old dugouts, we rose about 8 oclock cooked our breakfast which consisted of steak
