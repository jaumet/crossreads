 19/3/16 Sunday 
 Had the most wretched night I've had for a good while &ndash; often got slightly wet &amp; had rig tents &ndash; tighten pegs of an night at Enoggera &ndash; but last night was exceptional for Cairo &ndash; It had been blowing dust &amp; Sand all day  yesterday  last night about 1-30 am the rain came down &ndash; had about half an inch, it came thro our grass hut &ndash; my blankets kept it out for a while, then I tried my Coat but had to get up &amp; walk about, when I found I was laying in water &ndash; never said anything but booked it up against the Kaiser. 
 I went down to Mass at the Zeitouin Convent.
