<p>August, 1916<br/>Monday 21<br/>Disembarked on southampton docks &amp; waited round all day<br/>At about 5 pm we entrained for Salisbury Plain.<br/>Arrived at Tidworth stn at 7 pm.  Men marched to Parkhouse camp.  I went on baggage motor<br/>Got into huts &amp; turned in about 11 pm.<br/>1 year since I was wounded</p>
<p>Tuesday 22<br/>Our quarters rather good.  Large wooden rooms containing 30 men &amp; mess tables for same, electric light.<br/>Walked into Shipton Bellinger a village about a mile from Parkhouse.</p>
