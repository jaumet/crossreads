
3/7/16
Poor food  -tea & tea. Try to align key contacts & break same.
4/7/16Same old food. Buy salmon - bad. Heated argument over Melb & Sydney
5/7/16Terrible stew that makes us crook. Tea that is vile. Practice with calico aeroplanes ships. George Hamilton gets a cake from Australia.
6/7/16More bad food. Aeroplane warning from Romani
7/7/16Officer tearing about on horse 2am looking for patrol.
8/7/16Artillery and machine gun guns etc out on an imagined attack firing live, shell overhead. Stoke guns also making big noise. TAT TAT TAT TAT  All over the place.
9/7/16Rice for tea hailed with expectation - uneatable - bitter & mushy & as usual no milk or sugar. Catch a young snake.
10/7/16George finds a chameleon that causes great amusement. No letters again. Plum duff for tea that is as heavy as lead.
11/7/16Aeroplane message from Romani. Catch another chameleon. No letters again, terrible food.
12/7/16Catch another chameleon which is called Hector. Cuthbert is others's name.(note. No 13/7/16)
14/7/16Still bad food. Arthur's birthday.
15/7/116Payday. Come to rescue of the fiend who missed a message from Ismailia & generally does his block
16/7/16have a great blow out & get sick through eating. have some of Davies' bully beef with "hooks" in it or rather suspected of having such.
17/7/16Bunny Brett leaves for Pt Said prepared for a big parcel of goods
18/7/16War news begins from Ismailia. Message missed owing to jamming & is not received till 9am
19/7/16Bunny returns from Pt Said with photos goods etc. Receive three letters at last.
21/7/16Taube up again flying over Kantara. Overhaul generator to get 16 strength from Ismailia.
22/7/16Taube up over Kantara.
23/7/16Aeroplanes up over Tantara
24/7/16Rig up buzzer for the fiend & send him war news. Have to get others
