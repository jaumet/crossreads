<p>Saturday 10th<br/>Was mess orderly.  No parade today.  Lines were thoroughly cleaned &amp; disinfected.</p>
<p>Sunday 11th<br/>Got away fairly early in the morning on 3 days leave, &amp; caught the 11 oclock train for Port Said arriving there at 2.30pm.  Went to the Continental Hotel, had dinner, went out for a good walk around the town.  All the inhabitants seemed to be in the streets, <strike>&amp; they</strike> it all seemed quite gay.  Bands &amp; orchestras played outside caf&#233;s &amp; the streets were full of soldiers.  Didn't get to bed until quite late.</p>
<p>Monday 12th<br/>Went on board the "Orsova" early in the morning &amp; saw mother &amp; father.  Spent a busy time answering inquiries &amp; remained on board till the</p>
<p>[The troopship HMAT A67 "Orsova" had left Sydney on 11 March for England]</p>
