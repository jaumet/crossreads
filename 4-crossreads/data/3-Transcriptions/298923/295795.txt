<p>9 May 1918 Thursday<br/>Leave.  Dined last night at Shepheards with Dolly as that is the place I met the little girl.<br/>Hot in Cairo now but they have cool rooms-   The little wife is sitting on my knee now &amp; joins in love to you all, someday we will walk into the old garden gate<br/>Love from F M<br/><u>Address</u> Dolly to Anglo Egyptian Bank.</p>
