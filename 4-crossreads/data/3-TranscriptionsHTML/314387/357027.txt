<p>Friday 29th<br/>Muster parade had a look round village - Saturday 30th<br/>Went to city of Abbeville on leave had a bonzer time returning to camp about ten &amp; night<br/>Sunday July 1at<br/>Afternoon went to Leehiers Coyeux and New Brighton had a good time.<br/>Monday July 2nd<br/>Fell in as usual muster parade - Tuesday 3rd<br/>Fell in as usual afterwards had a swim</p>
