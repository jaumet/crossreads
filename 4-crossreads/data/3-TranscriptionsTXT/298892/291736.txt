He is well and wishes to be remembered to all ast home.Some of the Portuguese, who are working somewhere at the back of the front wondered round the trenches looking for souvenirs yesterday. I was talking to one of them and secured his everlasting gratitude by the gift of a Fritz bullet. There are hundreds of them lying about the ridge but it was a real novelty to him.
