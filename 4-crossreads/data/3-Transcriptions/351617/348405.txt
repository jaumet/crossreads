<p>The next morning we received word to move back to Brigade HQrs. about 400 yds away as things had settled down a bit and we were to work a station from there &amp; carry on work with Battalions by runner. Here we found H.C. Tollis (Tock) and W Lickiss, and we settled down to keep things going.</p>
<p>It was impossible to sleep as one half the pillbox was a dressing station and in the other half was 5 of us, our instrument, Sig Sergt, 4 runners, and 4 Sigs, and comfortably it would have held 2. So from the morning at Half Way house till now we had had no sleep.</p>
<p>For 2 days we stopped here sometimes the shelling was terrific sometimes it was quiet but whichever way it went it was no picnic. For rations we had to go out</p>
