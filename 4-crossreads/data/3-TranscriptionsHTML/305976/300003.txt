We saw the Natives irrigating the crops &amp; chopping the Tibbin[?]. Arrived Alexandria 3 pm. Put up Windsor Hotel. Visited Noriza Gardens &amp; had a run around Town. Alexandria seems a nice city much nicer than Cairo. The streets however are so narrow. Returned back to hotel &amp; once again slept on a soft bed.<br/>Tuesday May 28<br/>Alexandria strikes me as being more of a Cosmopolitan City than Cairo. There are all Nationalities here &amp; most seem prosperous</p>
