
8 July 1918 MondayWe had been warned to expect an attack today but it did not come off only an enemy plane overBlood test very simply taken- prick tip of finger & smear a slideHave not heard of Nowell Forth for some time   His crowd are getting Horses in lieu of Camels.There are still a good many Partridge around here notwithstanding the shelling & they do drink whatever the "Field" may say about the home birds.
