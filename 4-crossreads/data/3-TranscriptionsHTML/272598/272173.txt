<p>still for the word to go, praying all the time that it will never come.  All sorts of rumors coming through with men that come from the line, hardly know what to believe, still there is no doubt they are having a rough time.  Good news comes through each day from further North round Arras.  Bert Hunt's brother wounded in the recent engagement &amp; since has had his left arm taken off.  Great French Victory 10,000 prisoners taken</p>
