<p>2 July 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>The heat is very unpleasant after the rain but we are getting used to anything</p>
<p>Working party again to night with better luck no more wet</p>
<p>1915 July 3<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>The enemy put several shells into our water tanks this morning so it is decided to remove them  &amp;  the supply depot to another spot better protected as we lose men here nearly every day.</p>
