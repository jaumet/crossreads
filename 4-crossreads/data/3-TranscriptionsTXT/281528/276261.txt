 30/3/18 &ndash; La Basse Ville before dusk.  At 7.30 went up the line and walked round to La Basse Ville where I saw my two firing line O.Cs and Major Dooley having a yarn.  The place was all dripping and wet.  The Billjims standing to arms were all encased in ground sheets and all fairly cheerful.  Some of them were not wearing their water-proofs because out on patrol or on wiring party they glisten.  A shrewd old bird is our Billjim.  Back to Bn. H.Q. calling first at the R.A.P.  The night was most devilishly dark and we kept tumbling all over the shop.
