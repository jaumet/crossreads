<p>Saturday 18th August 1917<br/>Grooming and harness cleaning.<br/>Taubes over about 9.30pm<br/>Sky lit up by our search lights, got on to a Taube. Guns gave him a hot time but he managed to get away.</p>
<p>Sunday 19th<br/>9.30 pm Taubes over dropping bombs.<br/>Church service 10am.<br/>Half holiday. Harnessed up wheelers about 3pm went to H.Q. for comforts. One of Fritz planes brought down near camp by anti-aircraft guns.</p>
