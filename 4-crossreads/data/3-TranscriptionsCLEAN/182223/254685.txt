  
 Monday 2 August 1915  About Hospital all day.  Getting ready to send away a lot of men to Australia.  Letters posted to Girls & Mollie  689 patients here tonight  
 Tuesday 3 August 1915 Sent 369 patients to Australia this morning, they were amenable to discipline and gave no trouble.  Was in Cairo at 12 noon, interview with D.M.S. & General Maxwell. Martin (L.Col.) had sent on my personal letter to Gen. Maxwell.  Mainfold here during the afternoon.  
 Wednesday 4 August 1915  In Cairo during the morning  116 patients admitted, from Alexandria and locally
