<p>1915<br/>July 6 Tue.<br/>Hospitals continued.<br/>Egyptian Govt. Hospital &ndash; 50; Ras-el-tin Convalescent Depot; Mustapha Conval. Depot . Total Aust. Wounded at Alexandria 35 officers &amp; 783 men.<br/>Number in Hosp. here yesterday was 340 of which 273 <strike>were</strike> are Australians.<br/>150 more patients arrived about mid-night . Very animated scene with numberless motor ambulances snorting up &ndash; depositing their human freight - &amp; off again for more.<br/>100 patients sent out at 7.30 a.m. to Ras-el-tin Conval. Depot, Alexandria.</p>
