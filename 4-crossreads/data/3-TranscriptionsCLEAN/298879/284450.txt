  
 October 1915 Monday 11 Fine Our old transport officer Capt. Rowlands has joined us now as a Coy. Commander. Major Stevens and several new men also arrived. Lieut Tedder has taken Capt. Rowland's place in Egypt 
 Tuesday 12 Fine   On Bgde. Orderly again. The Bgde was inspected by the Gen O.C. the First Line of Communication. A team out of our Batt Details played the 7th Bn. Details at cricket yesterday. They made 163 runs and we made 101 thus being badly beaten. I made 4 & was then badly stumped.
