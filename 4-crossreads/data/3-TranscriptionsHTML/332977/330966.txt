<p>Oiling ammunition 220 rounds each<br/>Inflexible leaves for Malta</p>
<p>7 Wednesday<br/>loading horses, record job, working winch on Malta</p>
<p>8 April<br/>Route march first and second Brigade arriving captured 2 German cargo boats land Dardanelles Sunday</p>
<p>Friday<br/>Took rashions ashore, boat got astray<br/>Suffold giving us stores. <indecipherable> swim with boat, fire in Bull ring Cairo<br/>Saturday 10th<br/>Seaplane above more British troops arrive 5 letters arrive for me, first and second Reinforcements arrive</p>
<p>April 12<br/>Arrested four spies on Lemnos Island, priest</p>
<p>13<br/>All high Officers leave for the front to spy out position, nothing doing for us</p>
<p>14th<br/>On Guard receive 8 tins tobacco per man</p>
<p>15th<br/>Received iron rations two days, 50 extra rounds</p>
<p>16 Friday<br/>Spell all day boarded Prince of Wales to practice landing, shown over Warships. Turks get to our transport 100 drowned in panic.</p>
<p>17<br/>shifted out to mouth of Harbour</p>
<p>18th<br/>Practised landing off warship, Nurses</p>
