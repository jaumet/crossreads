  
 here in C Squadron 
 Dec 3/ Route march through Alexandria. The whole Brigade turned out . We rode in half sections & made it look as if there were double the number. It was a ruse to let the niggers see that their was still plenty of troops left in Egypt. The niggers have been playing up with Turks urging them on 
 Dec 7/ Left Mex camp at 11.30 pm My old horse Kaisa Bill gave me a good fly for freedom but I managed to sit him. We entrained at Gabarry Station for some unknown
