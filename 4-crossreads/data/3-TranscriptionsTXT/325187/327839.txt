 [Monday 3 April 1916] 
 Sgt WEIR a namesake died today of TYPHUS &amp; PNEUMONIA a good fellow who did good work on the Peninsular. Photos prints are an exorbitant price in this part of EGYPT 5 PT = 1/- for each print so I send you Films only. One aeroplane was fired at this morning &amp; two bullets put through the Wings but the Senussi are not in any strength &amp; have mostly gone  Tripoli  way. [Following paragraph is crossed out] Met Captain Arnold a Doctor brother to Ralph Arnold the latter also has a Commission now in an Infantry Battalion. I suppose Lorna knows this.  When is Charlie's 21st Birthday sometime in April?
