<p>22 November, Saturday</p>
<p>Meet Roy. I look for stakes but heat &amp; flies worry me. Dig my own old garden specially sort out bulbs. Roy for Tea &amp; Mr &amp; Mrs Johnson. Roy &amp; I go to N. S. but do not swim. Play snooker. Go to Smiths down road. Confetti fights all night meet lots of old pals.</p>
<p>23 November, Sunday</p>
<p>Look for stakes again. Go to Manly after dinner. Mooch around band. Meet N. Dempsey &amp; the violinist. Go for swim &amp; up to E &amp; E. Meet Dave Williams. E &amp; E out go to Muriel's place meet Edie &amp; uncle[?]. play billiards &amp; buy hat. Dream that M is going to Australia &amp; I say Good Bye in England.</p>
