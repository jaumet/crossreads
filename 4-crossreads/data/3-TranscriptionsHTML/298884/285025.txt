<p>having been gone through<strike>the</strike> with the Health Officer the Victorians to the tune of about 400 were discharged about 10 A.M. Very few officers &amp; nurses are left on board now. A tremendous lot of handshaking had to be gone through before they all got away.</p>
<p>At 11.30 A.M. we were on the move once more &amp; passed through the heads amongst the myriad of whirlpools &amp; jelly fish at 12 noon.</p>
<p><u>18th</u>  Had a good game of bridge with the Ship's Captain (Captain Adamson) Brolga Brett &amp; Capt Rossiter. We played for 6d 100 points</p>
