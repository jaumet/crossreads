<p>Sunday 3<br/>Visitors Miss Collings<br/>Ravenslea Road<br/>Wardsworth Common</p>
<p>Monday 4<br/>Afternoon in London at Bank &amp; walk round. Lyons Picadilly Corner House. Meet Trotter now a Lieut in R.A.N. Not allowed to draw money at Bank without permission of Military Auth.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5<br/>Outing arranged by some lady to Hanky Panky at Empire Theatre &amp; tea.</p>
<p>Wednesday 6<br/>Visiting day. Capt Hunt &amp; Mr Neave.</p>
<p>Thursday 7<br/>Whole day off from 10 am. Spent on the river on an excursion steamer &amp; travelled up as far as Richmond via Barnes Putney Kew etc. The most beautiful of places now is Richmond, far more so than in March.</p>
<p>Friday 8<br/>News of fresh advance on Messines Ridge where we were last June.<br/>Hospital Sports this afternoon were a great success.</p>
<p>Saturday 9<br/>Afternoon at Kew Garden &amp; Richmond.</p>
