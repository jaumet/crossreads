<p>Saturday July 6<br/>Usual routine<br/>Sunday July 7<br/>Very windy clouds of dust in valley. Turks shelling today.<br/><strike>Monday July 8</strike><br/>Sunday July 14<br/>During the last week there has been nothing out of the ordinary.<br/>Jacko has been shelling at various times and the weather has been exceptionally hot. The boys are just about fed up with this place &amp; would welcome anything for a change. Last night we had a bomb raid just about sunset &amp;</p>
