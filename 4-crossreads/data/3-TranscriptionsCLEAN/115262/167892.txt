  
 Saturday 5 May 1917. 
 In camp in the morning as not having a vote was on "fatigue" cleaning up the lines. 
 2 nd  troop train to Melbourne from Seymour at 1.45 pm. 
 A very dirty & dusty march from camp to station. 
 1/3 of the boys had to ride in cars as result of march on Wed. & Thursday. 
 Arrived City. 4.10 pm. Caught 4.35 pm. train from Flinders St. 
 Had tea in D'nong. & caught train later (7.30) to Lyndhurst. 
 Home at 8.15 pm. Spent evening at home & bed at 11.30 pm. 
 Present from "Gannons" Deniliquin.
