<p>as well as from Salonica, Murocco and Tunis. I was well received wherever I went. The Australians have made a name for themselves in France ( as have the N.Zs. &amp; Canadians) and another point is that practically all our troops over here are in the field.</p>
<p>France is greatly concerned over the backing down of the Russians, as she is on the discovery of two of her members of parliament implicated in a big plot against the country which is now being unravelled by the Suret&eacute;. The tenacles of this plot, known as the "affaire Bole" and organised by German brains and money, extend into Italy &amp; Britain.</p>
