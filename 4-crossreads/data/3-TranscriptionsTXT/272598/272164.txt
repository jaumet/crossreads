Monday 26.3.17Terrific explosion at midnight last night;  Town Hall blown up by a mine laid by Fritz & supposed to have been set by clockwork.  Many killed & few taken from cellars alive.  Earth rocked like a boat at the time of the explosion.  Warned against sleeping in cellars.
Tuesday 27.3.17Fatigues for the day: walked around to the scene of the explosion; hundreds digging to try & release any who may be alive in cellars.
