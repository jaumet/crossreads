 May, 1916 Saturday 13 Exam Mechanism, my class very anxious  one Sgt got no marks at all. After dinner went into town &amp; out to Ghezireh garden   later to Kursaal Temp 119&deg; 
 Sunday 14 After lunch I went out to Patti's &amp; later to another friend with Miss Patti Met several nice young ladies &amp; 3 more British N.C.Os. All to leave Cairo &amp; Heliopolis stopped to A.I.F &amp; N.Z.E.F. Temp 115&deg;
