
<p>THURSDAY, OCTOBER 23, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Tickle &amp; I put the Mountain</p>
<p>mill together again. We got her</p>
<p>all ready to connect up. Pryor came</p>
<p>out &amp; got a start on building the</p>
<p>other tower in Middle paddock.</p>
<p>Paid Darby off in the morning &amp;</p>
<p>he has gone.</p>
<p>Alan went down to Sydney by</p>
<p>the night train.</p>
