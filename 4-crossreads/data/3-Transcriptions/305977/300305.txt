<p>Monday 3rd [Jan]<br/>Had a busy day.  We mounted at 9.15 &amp; the horses went very well.  They seemed very pleased to get out for a run.  In the afternoon it rained fairly hard, so we cleaned up our rifles.  Wrote some letters at night &amp; did not get to bed till late.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4th<br/>Another mounted parade in the morning.  We had our horses going at the trot &amp; everything is going on well.  The reinforcements drill very well.  There was more rain in the afternoon.  We got paid I drew &pound;15 &amp; also got an outfit of clothes.  Wrote more letters at night &amp; sent some photos away</p>
