<p><u>Saturday 16th Feb. 1918</u>.<br/>Tent pitching this morning.<br/>Parade dismissed in afternoon when I prepared some notes on Omar Khayyam for the literary evening. Good meeting &ndash; about 20 out of 24 members turning up. I was elected to chair &amp; we got to work immediately (held in barn near the stables . Forms &amp; chairs <strike>by</strike> provided by farmhouse people).<br/>Sam Rickson read a passage from "The Sky Pilot".<br/>Pte Barlow read a passage from "Young Lord Stranleigh" by Robert Barr. H.A.T. Reynolds <strike>read</strike> told the story of "Mick Arden" illustrated by quotations</p>
