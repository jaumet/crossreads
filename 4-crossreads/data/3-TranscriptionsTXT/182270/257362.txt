 WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 19, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; cold. Knocking round 
 in the morning, and went 
 out to the rugby match 
 Australia V South Africa in 
 the afternoon. It was a fair 
 match &amp; we won by 9 - 8 &amp; were 
 very lucky to do so. Stayed 
 at the flat all the evening.
