In 1807 the Church of England Missioners arrived & established a Mission & later on the Wesleyan Methodist people did the same so today there stands two Churches in Free Town which belongs to these Denominations. The White Population numbers about 400 & the Native some where near a Million in the District generally. Their Commerce is made up of Rubber, Copra & Tropical Fruits. Vegetables have to be imported & Cabbages cost 2p each, Bananas are sold by the Natives who have small Canoes & come alongside at 1/- dozen & are a very intelligent class of Black.
