<p>Squeezed my finger a day or two ago &amp; doctor took off the nail;  it is now doing well.<br/>Met Harold Clarence in the N.Z. Canteen two nights ago.<br/>He has not been away from here since landing 3 months ago.<br/>His brother is a Serakim [?] a few miles desert wards from here.<br/>It was very pleasant to spend a few hours with the man who was a good friend to me in the stormy days of Whiporie.<br/>We had an evening at a scratch Cinema show that has been erected here.</p>
