<p>One of my rather special camarades got killed the other day but I simply can't write about it yet.</p>
<p>I forgot to thank you for the socks, I created quite a sensation in them the other morning.  I had to fly across to the telephone early (its across the road &amp; I hadn't my leggings or puttees on, just the purpuel socks.  Coming back I struck a whole company, couldn't turn &amp; flee so there stood your James, in shirt, pants &amp; purple socks &amp; slippers half way down the roadside bank while the company, officers &amp; all, went marching by.  You should have <u>heard</u> the remarks, they would have pleased your ear.</p>
