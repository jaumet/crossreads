 E" Company No. 4 Officer Cadet Battn., Keble College, Oxford. 
 10.4.18 
 Dear Vol 
 Just a short note to let you know I'm cooling off at above address, for a 6 months course.  Sympathise with me, please.  Your letters lately have been going astray somewhere, because I know you write when you can.  I haven't heard from George lately but have written him today.  His Btn. has, I think, been in action on the Somme, not certain.
