 France 14.9.17 
 Dear Dad, Your letter of July 9th arrived tonight and I am very pleased to see by it that you have again had some tangible recognition of your services.  I often wonder whether your dreams are troubled with bags of wheat and bales of wool, during the strenuous times you are contending with, and how you manage to conjure up enough trucks to shift it all.  According to papers, there has been a great shortage of rolling-stock and many stacks have suffered in consequence.  However, I offer my sincere congratulations. 
 Many thanks for your efforts re furlough, I hope they are successful.  It seems as if the government has turned us down.  They are very tardy anyhow.
