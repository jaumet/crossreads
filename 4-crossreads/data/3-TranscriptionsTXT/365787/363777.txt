 [Card] Dymock's Building 68 Elizabeth Street SYDNEY 
 11.12.17 
 Dear Madam, 
 I have to acknowledge with many thanks receipt of your parcel, containing 42 pairs of socks and 7 Balaclavas from the Wahroonga-Warrawee War Chest Branch. 
 This Depot will be closed from Dec. 22nd to Jan 14th 1918. 
 Yours faithfully, "WAR CHEST" SUPPLY DEPOT, [Signed] J. M. Antill Hon. Superintendent Per N.H.M.
