<p>&amp; I'll bet he was no more pleased than we were, it was just another case of illustrating how secure we hold the mastery of the air.</p>
<p>This bomb of ours is a beauty, when they drop it, it bursts into 4 pieces &amp; a little lower down these 4 pieces burst into 8 &amp; lower down still they become sixteen &amp; any one of these portions is sufficient to bring any balloon down, they are what they call incendinary bombs &amp; are used sometimes to destroy ripening crops.</p>
<p>Today all our watches &amp; clocks go back an hour &amp; many were the jokes caused by it.</p>
