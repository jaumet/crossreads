<p>Lemnos Island</p>
<p>5/11/15</p>
<p>We arrived here at 2.=30 today after 56 hours Sail from Alexandria&nbsp; on our way accross we were met with a Destroyer and it was zig zaging about looking for Submarines</p>
<p>Luckily we Did not meet any. I am at present on Board Ship &amp; looking from here you can See Hospitals &amp; on Shore Canadains English and Australians</p>
