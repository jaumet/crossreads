<p>21st<br/>The long looked for Hun offensive has begun.  It broke out on the point where the British and French Armies meet like a fearful storm.  Masses of huns have been flung into the fray so far they have recaptured a lot of ground in the Somme area.</p>
<p>22nd<br/>The Huns have taken Bapaume and have pushed our line back on a front of about 60 miles to an average depth of about 5 miles.</p>
<p>1st April<br/>The Huns have now reached Albert and have taken Montedidier from the French.  This latter is a serious loss.</p>
