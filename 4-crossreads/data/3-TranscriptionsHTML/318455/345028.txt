<p>Aug 24.<br/>The Div. has gone in again three days ago &amp; I have been <strike>busy</strike> taking rations up to advanced H.Q. some 9 kilos up from here The Tanks went through here for two successive nights just before the Div. went up this was a sign of Dirty work at the Cross Roads &amp; this last two days there has been a steady stream of prisoners coming back &ndash; I met one batch of 200</p>
