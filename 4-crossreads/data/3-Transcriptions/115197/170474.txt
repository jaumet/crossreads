<p><u>Feb. 15th</u></p>
<p>Reading &amp; Writing.</p>
<p>Matinee at the Strand which features Arthur Bourchier.&nbsp; Noel Coward.&nbsp; Alex Scott Gatty &amp; Kyrle Bellew - A poor play with no decent situation or dialogue and certainly no <s>oc-cup</s> opportunities for good acting.</p>
<p>Return to Elm Bank and have very pleasant evening.&nbsp; Read &quot;Nonsense Novels&quot; &amp; &quot;De Omnibus&quot;.</p>

<p>Morning of writing and preparation</p>
