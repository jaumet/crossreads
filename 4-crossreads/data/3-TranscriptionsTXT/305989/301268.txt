Sunday 5 August 1917Usual routine.  Church parade in evening didn't go.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 7 and 8].
Monday 6 August 1917Quiet day, got news of stunt, left camp at 3.30 &amp; travelled all night towards Erk:  got to wadi about daylight.
Tuesday 7 August 1917Arrived in Wadi &amp; stayed out in outpost all day:  cruelly hot day:  up about 6.30 &amp; arrived back at 11.30.
Wednesday 8 August 1917Hot day:  De Lousing parade to Wadi Shiek Narran, went to Capt. Pascoe to get tooth fixed pretty crook time.
Thursday 9 August 1917Hot and windy day.  "Bluey" &amp; Dal went to Rest Camp.
Friday, 10 August 1917Usual routine.  Mail in.
Saturday 11 August 1917Hot day, getting ready for stunt.
