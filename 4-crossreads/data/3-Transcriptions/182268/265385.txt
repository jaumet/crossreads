<p>Wednesday 30/1/18&nbsp; Had lunch with Gray, Reiss &amp; Johnson (same party as at Amulree)&nbsp; went to a picture show- Dance at Lady Mond Lowndes Square.</p>
<p>Thursday 31/1/18. Letters from Doff&nbsp; Had dinner at Ritz and then went to see &quot;Love in a Cottage&quot; with the Grays.</p>
<p>Friday 1/2/18. Again dined at the &quot;Ritz&quot; and then went to &quot;Pamella&quot; with the Grays.</p>
