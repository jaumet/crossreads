<p>Left Raboul on the 18th of October with H.M.A.S. Encounter, Destroyers, Warrego, Parramatta, Yarra, with Storeships and colliers, the Main Fleet going on ahead with French Cruiser Montcalm, arrived at Suva on the 28th<br/>Carried on Patrol Duties on the 29th<br/>Fleet Dispersed about the end of October Melbourne and Sydney left to escort troops, Australia left for South America, following up the German Fleet leaving Encounter and destroyers at Suva with Convoy, German Gunboat was captured at Raboul during this time<br/>Left Suva on the 8th of November with H.M.A.S Destroyers Parramatta and Warrego called at Noumea on the 11th<br/>Left again on the 12th and arrived in Sydney on the 16th<br/>Went into Dry Dock at Cockatoo on the 21st for slight overall, came out on the 28th and went alongside Garden Island for a quick refit<br/>Left Sydney on the 19th with H.M. Transport Berrima and others arrived at Melbourne on the 21st picked up more Transports and left on the 23rd<br/>Arrived at Albany on the 28th</p>
