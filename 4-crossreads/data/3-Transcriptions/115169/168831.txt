<p>January 1st [1916] at 9.30 am. we proceeded inside basin and secured to dockyard wall,</p>
<p>Jan 3rd at 9 am we once again entered the floating dock, to undergo short refit</p>
<p>Jan 4th at 10 am "Carnarvon" anchored in Bay</p>
<p>Jan 6th at 9 am "Cumberland" departed to sea</p>
<p>Jan 8th at 10 am "Isis" departed to sea</p>
<p>Jan 10th 8 am "Suffolk" anchored in Bay,</p>
<p>Jan 12th 7.30 am "Carnarvon" departed to sea</p>
<p>Jan 13th 9 am "Isis" arrived and anchored in Grassy Bay, whilst "Caesar" came alongside dockyard wall.</p>
<p>Jan 15th 9 am "Isis" again went to sea</p>
<p>Jan 18th 2 PM "Suffolk" departed to sea</p>
<p>Jan 22nd  7 am "Isis" again returned and anchored in Grassy Bay &ndash; 1 PM "Carnarvon" did likewise.</p>
<p>Jan 30th at 7 am. "Carnarvon" departed to sea and at 9.30, our dock commenced to flood preparatory to our undocking</p>
<p>Jan 31st 3 am we left dock and secured to dockyard wall and at 7 am we went and anchored in Grassy Bay; at 8 am</p>
