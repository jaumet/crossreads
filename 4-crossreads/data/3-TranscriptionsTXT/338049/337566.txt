 Sunday 22 Dec. Breakfast in bed but up 9.30 a.m. Met Miss Cumming at church &amp; slightly late owing to Irish time being 20 min slow. Snowing. 
 To Cummings for dinner. Then to Wesleyan Bible Class Musical afternoon. Tea at Fergusons. Home in hurry. Goodbye to Cummings very hurriedly. Caught special tram home.
