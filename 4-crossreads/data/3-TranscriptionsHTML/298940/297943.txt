<p>From No 10 Con Camp we went by char a bancs to St Martin Base Detail Camp, &amp; found it as all the other camps are, on the top of a hill. It was windy, rainy &amp; miserable, also the camp was overstocked with men. This was the result of the rush of Somme men to No 10 which accounted for our sudden departure.<br/>The arrangements made for food were miserable, so that we had to stand in long queues for every meal.
