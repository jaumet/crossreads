 Friday 20th Oct. In the saddle again our last stage, left camp at 8 a.m. in the van rode right on to Jeffin  but did not water, finished 20 mile ride in the beautiful date oasis called Baada, plenty of water for horse & men, good camp all night, to-morrow we go right in to El-Ferdan. 
 Saturday 21st Oct. Off again at 7 a.m. we leading rode direct to Mahadan where the regt. halted for lunch.  I rode on alone to El-Ferdan.  Old Zigi walked at a great pace knowing she was nearing civiz  I arrived there and was rushed for news of the scrap after a great meal at the canteen my A.M.C. men took me to the goods, it was all there by some miraculous fluke, nothing lost at all, I have to stay here with A Squad as R.M.O. but the regt.
