<p>25<br/>The heavenly chorus of girls gives a noisy display with tambourines &amp; the audience is informed that search is finished, &amp; they must return to earth receiving a blessing at foot of stairs I forgot to mention that St Peter with his crook welcomed us at door on entering &amp; conducted us into heaven.</p>
<p>After this we adjourned to Le Chat Noir a much more interesting show. Price of admission is 1.85 &amp; beer costs 75 centimes &amp; was flat. On entering we were greeting with a remark Here are live real Parisians All girls are addressed as Princess, soldier as generals &amp; civilians as monseigneur &amp; some remark passed about each. A lady accompanied by 2 men enter, &amp; are introduced as the man his wife &amp; the baby. A front man entering introduces himself as the l'amant.</p>
<p>The songs are rather good &amp; at conclusion singers try to sell them. There seem to be 4 male &amp; 1 finale singer &amp; a pianist. How it pays I dont know. The method of acclamation is directed by one of show &amp; many be a succession of 3, 4 or 5 claps repeated 3, 4 or 5 times. The shallow shows are over the odds of the soldier who sees a girl &amp; [indecipherable] much movement after which a duel &amp; then his funeral.</p>
<p>Some of the songs are very hot &amp; much talk is of Americans</p>
