<p>what is known as Chateau Segard area. The camp consisted of a few dug outs in very poor condition &amp; small canvas shelters for the men I was in a dug out with six other officers We stayed here the night.</p>
<p>Monday 1st Oct<br/>Remained in camp all day.</p>
<p>Tuesday 2nd<br/>The Battalion moved up to the line this evening. I had a service this afternoon There was a very good crowd of fellows present. At nine oclock they left camp.<br/>They were all in very fine spirits.</p>
<p>Wednesday<br/>It is very miserable here</p>
