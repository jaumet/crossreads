<p>About 5pm we observed a big 4 funnelled cruiser away, hull down on the Western horizon, gradually she rose higher and higher till all the hull could be seen, and she was on the same level as ourselves.  As she steamed towards us with smoke streaming thinly from her four funnels and flag signal flying from her signal yard, rising and falling gently on the long rollers, she presented a beautiful picture.
