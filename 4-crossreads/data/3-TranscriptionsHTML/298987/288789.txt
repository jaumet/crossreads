<p>July<br/>by this time &amp; the Searchlights playing on &amp; through them were beautifull only out for a few minutes &amp; peace for remainder of night<br/>Had a good breckfast &amp; Marched back to Boulonge leaving there at 2.30 PM by train About 30 of us in each Horse Truck. Went through Estaples &ndash; only a few miles from Boulonge &amp; saw where the Hun dropped some of his eggs last night. One had hit the railway line &amp; tipped an engine over another had killed a horse &amp; there were several holes near the line. There is a big camp there &amp; we hear they had a lot of casulties. Had about 2 ft 6 in by 6 in of floor space to sleep on for a start last night &amp; it gradually grew smaller as the others sleeping around stretched out all the same slept some of the time &amp; as a lot of Tommies got out at Rouen at 5 a m some of us moved into an empty &amp; had plenty of room. Reached Harflluer at 10 30 a m (Friday morning) &amp; marched about 1 mile to Camp Have been issued with rifle cover &amp;</p>
