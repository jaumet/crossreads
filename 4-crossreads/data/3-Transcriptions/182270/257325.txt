
<p>964</p>
<p>MONDAY, JANUARY 13, 1919.</p>
<p>Very heavy fog all day.</p>
<p>The first I have seen in London</p>
<p>&amp; I hope it is the last. Went to</p>
<p>Horseferry road in the</p>
<p>morning but got no</p>
<p>Satisfaction at all.</p>
<p><s>Poss went up to Hurdcott</s></p>
<p><s>Eric &amp; I &amp; Capt. Ferrier went</s></p>
<p><s>to the Hippodrome in the</s></p>
<p><s>afternoon</s></p>
<p>Had lunch at the flat</p>
<p>&amp; stayed there all the</p>
<p>afternoon. Went to the</p>
<p>Soldier Boy at night</p>
<p>with Poss and the mob.</p>
