<p>[Friday 16 June 1916]<br/> We went over our Map boundary <strike>yesterd</strike> this morning but very few tracts, [Following paragraphs are crossed out]<br/>we were in the advance Guard for about 15000 men but the Turks retired .  Our own aeroplane reported us as being Turks, we were lucky not to be Bombed by our own machine.<br/>Got back to our Camp in the middle of the night having done the round of the clock again-<br/>We have to keep going because of the Water difficulties-</p>
<p>Got letters just before leaving from Charlie Tyson &amp; Laura the latter never says anything about her own affairs or intentions.</p>
