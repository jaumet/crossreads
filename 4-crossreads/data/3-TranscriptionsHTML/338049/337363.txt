<p>Sunday 2 June<br/>Up about 6.30 a.m. Watered up etc. &amp; moved off from Longpre about 10.30 passing thro. Amiens arrived Glisy. [Rode?] Returned to Coy. Horse lines behind hill in wood. Had dinner then fixed things up.</p>
<p>Fixed tent in evening. Swim in lagoon.</p>
