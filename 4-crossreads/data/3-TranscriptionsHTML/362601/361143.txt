<p>13 January, Monday</p>
<p>F<br/>Go down town with Jeff Claite. H'cut &amp; shave with a Lady in other chair. Change 2F 1C. Go to YMCA &amp; see Chappie. Only smile at Chappie. Meet James who has just escorted Chappie to dinner. Good Yankee Concert by 2 men &amp; ladies from "Now there Revue". Man [indecipherable] over the biggest wish. Girls are good impressionists. I see a Froggie &amp; Girl leaning against a wall near St Advense[?]  Tram turning.</p>
<p>14 January, Tuesday</p>
<p>F<br/>Work all morning returning Boards etc. Am called a Burglar by Quarter Bloke. Bath in afternoon &amp; doing nothing else. Recd parcel from Nette with socks, sugar &amp; fruit. Wear socks over to Blighty. I have to warn draft for 8.30 in morning after returning from Town. I do a bit of shopping after YMCA band has finished &amp; chat with Yankee Stelple Jack.</p>
