Put on job hunting away French schmiddies on account of them stealing.
4/4/18 Dull overcast. After tea walked to Meteren., about 2 kilos. Bought ppcards of the place. On the way in had haircut shave at a French barbers. Charged 1 franc 3. A woman helps him with the shaving.
5/4/18 Drizzling rain all day. Put on a job cleaning up for Capt. Barton. After tea went into Outtersteene, almost 1 kilos.
6/4/18 Saturday. Nice fine morning but at 5pm it started raining again. After tea went into Meteren with Degrandy. Had nice hot bath after tea.
7/4/18 Fine day. After dinner washed my underclothing also pair of slacks I salvaged. After tea walked to Le Steetze. Great aerial artillery activity.
8/4/18 Cloudy morning wet afternoon also
