<p>at Helioplis a distance of five miles arrived  9.30 same evening camped there a fortnight. Whist there visited Cairo and Helioplis which are great places . Left Helioplis camp on Sun. morning 8 oclock 1/8/15 boarded the train there for Alexandria, a distance of some 140 miles  Arrived there at 3 p.m. same day boarded a large tramp boat named Georgian and sailed at 6 p.m. same evening Sun 1/8/15. on the evening of Tues 3/8/15  all hands ordered below except one company, who was on guard on top deck with Rifles and ammunition every light out at dusk the boat in darkness throughout the night in fear of submarines we are just through the Mediderrian Sea.
