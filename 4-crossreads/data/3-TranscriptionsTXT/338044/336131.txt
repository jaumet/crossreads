 13th Dec., Monday Start on night duty 6 pm to 12 pm. Very much better than day work in my opinion, but prevents me from visiting our friends. 
 18th Dec. Saturday:- During the week nothing of much consequence has happened outside the regular nightly routine of work  Sleep during the morning &amp; walk after dinner &amp; go on duty at 6pm. Our friendship with Madame Alice &amp;  the girls has been proceeding favourably &amp; we are invited to tea on Xmas Day, next Saturday. 
 19th Dec: Sunday
