 Leaving Colombo we hugged the Indian coast for a couple of days &amp; cut across the Arabian sea. The heat was terriffic at Aden. We were promised leave ashore here but when arrived we were told of a Smallpox outbreak so after staying half a day we left. The natives here again are great dealers curios &amp; eatables &amp; Tobacco. Saw a red headed nigger.  Arrived Suez  There is a big army garrison at Aden. It is said that rain falls about once in seven years. Arrived Suez.  April 1 We the red sea is so called
