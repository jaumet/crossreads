<p>Wednesday 14 March 1917.</p>
<p>In camp.</p>
<p>At Lt. Horse Fd Amb. bivouac in the morning and in afternoon practised for sham battle with the 23/7th Lt Horse to take place tomorrow all day.</p>
<p>Latest furphy &quot;Sailing&nbsp;<strike>before</strike> about Monday 19th inst&quot;.</p>
<p>&quot;Euripides&quot; Artillery move out for, nobody knows where.</p>
<p>No lecture tonight.</p>
