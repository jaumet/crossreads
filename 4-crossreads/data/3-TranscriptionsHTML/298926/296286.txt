<p>23rd  We played a few marches which were appreciated by the inmates of the hospital.  <br/>At 7 o'clock we took off our packs which were placed on the transport and we played the <br/>troops to a rest camp 7 miles from the wharf.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    <br/>Havre is a large seaport where there is a tremendous amount of shipping.<br/>We did not get a very emphatic reception but rather a respectful and quiet welcome that <br/>was more heartfelt than expressed.</p>
