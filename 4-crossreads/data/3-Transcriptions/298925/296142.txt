<p>They were Australians.</p>
<p>They say the rates of pay are Privates 3&frac12;d  Corps 5d  serg. 7 per day  They wear their stripes on their collars [sketch of collar]. They look perfectly happy and well fed.</p>
<p>10th  Marched 7 miles to trenches near Shrewton with stretchers, where we had our first taste of stretcher work under service conditions at the front, returned at 3 o'clock.</p>
