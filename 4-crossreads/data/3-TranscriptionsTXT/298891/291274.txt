 1917 July 
 10 Tuesday 
 Rode at anchor till six o'clock p.m. then pulled out escorted by 2 Jap destroyers &amp; accompanied by an Italian troop ship. Great exchange of cheers between people of Taranto &amp; H.M. Saxon. Passed three Italian cruisers &amp; crews loudly cheered. Parade at 10.30 Life boat alarm drill a little later. Devilish hot. Breeze after we started.
