<p>Wednesday 6 October, 1915</p>
<p>Arrived at Lemnos last night &amp; find that our destination is Malta. I would rather go to Alexandria.</p>
<p>Saw a submarine in the harbour also the Mauritania, the second largest ship afloat.</p>
<p>Left Lemnos for Malta at 6 p.m. after putting the Indians ashore.</p>
