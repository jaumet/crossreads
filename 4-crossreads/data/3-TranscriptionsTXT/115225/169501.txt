 Nov. 18  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;Sunday - I stayed at home all day &amp; had a good rest - Went to Church in the evening with my Aunt. 
 &nbsp; 
 Nov. 19  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;I went up to town in the morning &amp; saw The Tower, &amp; S  t  &nbsp;Pauls Cathedral. went up into the whispering gallery &amp; heard the history of same whispered on one side of the dome to be heard distinctly on the other side. I Bought a book I had heard a lot about - &quot;Raymond&quot; by Sir Oliver Lodge. &amp; returned to Kingston - The weather miserably dull but fine. 
 &nbsp; 
 Nov. 20  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;I took my cousin to M  me  &nbsp;Taussauds in Baker Street in the morning. we had dinner at Slaters restaurant in the Strand &amp; afterwards met M  r  &nbsp;M. to go to Bairnsfathers &quot;Better ole&quot; but we couldnt get seats, so we Went to the Palladium &amp; saw Geo Robey - We got back home 6.30.P.M. 
 &nbsp; 
 Nov 21  st  &nbsp;&nbsp;I went to Euston to enquire about Glasgow trains etc. I had dinner at Victoria S  t  &nbsp;Y.M.C.A. &amp; wrote some letters. Went to the Bank &amp; back home. Left Kingston at 9.30 P.M. &amp; caught 11.30 P.M. train to Glasgow to see a friend.
