<p>Saturday 17 February 1917 [16 February 1918]<br/>&frac12; day &amp; very instructive one seeing Coptic churches.  Left Y.W.C.A. 2.30 p.m., took tram to Old Cairo &amp; met Canon Garland with 4 Australians in tram &amp; oh what a hard clasp he gave to an old T'mba [Toowoomba ?] woman.  We last saw him in "Wilga" when Mrs. Fairfax was there with measles.<br/>Note for the Copts &ndash;<br/>Saw oldest Copt Church &amp; supposed place where Mary hid the infant Christ.</p>
