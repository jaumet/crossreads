 Sunday, July 1st 1917 Up 7 a.m. &ndash; Church Parade 8.30 a.m.  Bought pr. breeches from Pratley for 7/6 &ndash; nice fit.  Afternoon went for stroll down Fovant.  Fine sunshine all day. 
 Monday, 2/7/17 On Lewis Guns all day.  Left them 3 p.m. &amp; came to hut &amp; got cleaned up &amp; received leave pass for Barrow.  Caught 4.50 p.m. train from Dinton &amp; 6.23 p.m. from Salisbury &amp; ran to W'loo [Waterloo] without a stop.  Went per tube to Euston &amp; booked bed at Y.M.C.A. &amp; looked up trains at station.
