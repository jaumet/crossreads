In quarter of an hour I had changed and packed and was seated on a taxi on my way to Bde. Hqrs.  I like these short notices.  Much better than hanging about.  Four of us are going back to France together.  Arrived in London about 8.15 and I put up at this little joint here near Victoria Station.
