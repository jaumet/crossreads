
Mar. 26thSabbath- Mass in village church- service in French- underclothing & blanket issue (most acceptable) - rain- "cafe" again.Mar. 27th.Inspection of our 7th. Brigade by General Sir Douglas Haig. C.I.C- village street- Sir Douglas said: - I am very pleased with 7th. Bde and I am sure they will acquit themselves excellently.  We move from school to Chateau.Mar 28th.Billeted in barn over stable
