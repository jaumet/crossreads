<p>Sunday 13</p>
<p>Camp routine. Tried to get a sleep but Jean had no mercy.</p>
<p>Mon 14</p>
<p>Serb N.Y. day party at night for Bolnicar's. Very mild.</p>
<p>Tues 15</p>
<p>Sister Davis &amp; I went to Vodene &amp; saw Dr Petrovic &amp; Major Popovitch &amp; Bozidan &amp; his commandant. Went on engine returned in 1st class carriage. Warm day.</p>
<p>Wed 16</p>
<p>Sister Davis left. Warm bright day. Tried to sleep in Pollard's tent. Thurs.17 No. 9. Matron Miss Munn &amp; I went to Vodena.<br/>Camp routine</p>
