<p>Octobre 25<br/>Working today on sleeper Rd at Birr XRds. Heavy scattered shelling on gun positions. Sapper Miller was killed today by shellfire near Chateau Wood.</p>
<p>Octobre 26<br/>Working on sleeper road at Birr X Roads. Cpl Scotland, Sap's Cook &amp; Hunt were killed today &amp; Sap Hudson was wounded while attempting to bring out the body of the late Sap Miller from Chateau Wood.</p>
