<p>Thursday 24 May</p>
<p>Lovely Day.<br/>The boys are busy digging and decorating gardens round the premises.<br/>A new Seaside Rest Camp has been opened at the north of the River Somme.  72 men from the 8th Brigade are to go fortnightly, our allottment being three.<br/>Walked round the Bapaume fortifications after tea.</p>
