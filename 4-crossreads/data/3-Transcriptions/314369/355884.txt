<p>4 September 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Now that the excitement is over   &amp;  I have nothing to worry about I begin to realise how completely run down I am<br/>When I wake in the morning all my joints ache   &amp;  I feel terribly tired but the feeling wears off through the day<br/>I can see now it was only will power that was keeping us going   &amp;  as soon as you release that you simply go to pieces</p>
<p>1915 September 5<br/>Sunday<br/>14th After Trinity</p>
<p>Things go on as usual am feeling better generally<br/>Dad brings in some reading matter in the afternoon   &amp; has tea w/- us.<br/>One of our chaps comes in w/- the news that my officer Lt Ford has been shot dead<br/>[Previous paragraph shows question mark dated 10/9/15 and then annotation Incorrect  15/9/15 and initialled JCL]</p>
<p>700 wounded arrive Cairo today</p>
