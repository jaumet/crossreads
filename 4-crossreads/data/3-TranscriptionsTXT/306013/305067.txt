At the village itself the characteristic church spires attracted us &amp; we visited the two local Anglican Churches &amp; had a good look, inside &amp; outside of both of them &amp; were much struck by their age &amp; by the many fond associations which they brought into our minds.
Altogether, we spent quite an enjoyable afternoon at Shrewton, wandering through its chief streets &amp; peering at anything &amp; everything that attracted our curiosity.
