 24 November 1916 Fri Slept all day. A very quiet night on duty played cards till daylight winning about 50 francs. Issued with a tin of fruit &amp; guermsey Clarry Power Cpt &amp; [indecipherable] our new Sgt. 
 25 November 1916 Sat On being relieved from duty at 7 am I turned in &amp; slept till 5 p.m. Raining very hard. No aeroplanes about lately owing to the fogs.
