<p>1915<br/>July 31 Sat.<br/>Another big row in Cairo in which the Troops took a prominent part. Houses set on fire, bonfires of furniture etc. were the order of the day &ndash; About 10.30 p.m. the Military Police restored order. We admitted one man to hospital suffering from concussion.<br/>The previous row was on Good Friday.</p>
