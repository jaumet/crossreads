
18-2-17Sun 18thI went to the Dr. this morning & got an order to go to the Field Ambulance at Abd to have my teeth attended to, went in at 11 oclock & arrived back in camp at 4.30 I also brought out a 'phone as the C.O. wants one in his tent. The lads going on leave got away to to time this morning.
19-2-17Mon 19thSent Cpl Leask & two men away with a pack mule (which we commandeered at El Arish) this morning to get posts to make a breast line for the Sig Troop & put in this morning putting it up Jack Wright returned from the School
