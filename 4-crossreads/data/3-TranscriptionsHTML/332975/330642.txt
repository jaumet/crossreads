<p>Monday 27th &ndash; Revale 2-30 &ndash; left Heliopolis 5-00 entrained at Zee Toun 700.  Arrived at Alexandria 2-00, marched aboard Saxonia &ndash; 3500 troops on anchored in harbour all night.<br/>28th &ndash; Sailed from Alexandria 8-00 o'clock morning steering zig zag course, alarms for submarines very often &ndash; Going about with Life belts on.<br/>29th &ndash; Sergents mess &ndash; a submarine alarm 9-30 p.m.<br/>30th, Escorted by a destroyer.<br/>31st, Steamed very close to Malta at six o'clock morning.</p>
<p>1st April, sea with a swell on.<br/>2nd, Sailed into Marsailles 10 a.m. &ndash; very beautiful scenery &ndash; Disembarked 6-00.  Camped at Marsailles for the night.<br/>3rd Revalle 5-30 moved off 7-00 entrained at Marsaille nine o'clock &ndash; dinner at Orange 1-00 beef &amp; Biscuits &amp; cigarettes &ndash; again at 1 o'clock in morning &ndash; beautiful scenery all the way.</p>
