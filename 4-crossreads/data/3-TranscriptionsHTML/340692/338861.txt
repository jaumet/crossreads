<p>lazy rise &amp; fall of the star shells that light up this strange land &amp; then die out making the darkness fall on you more closely than before, makes a walk like this anything but a pleasant one, &amp; causes you to doubt if you are realy living on this beautiful old world of ours.</p>
<p>In daylight the landscape is a never varing &amp; desolate one, &amp; the overlaping shellholes give the impression of a rolling ocean which reaches the horizon on all sides, &amp; it does not take a great deal of imagination to think of the remaining stumps of the woods, that were, as storm battered</p>
