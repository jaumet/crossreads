 THURSDAY, JULY 3, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; warm in the sun. We all went 
 into Quirindi in the big car in 
 the morning Father &amp; Mother &amp; Peter 
 went on out to Wallabadah &amp; Jo 
 &amp; I drove her car home. 
 I fixed up with Bank N.S.W for a 
 loan up to &pound;6000 for building 
 stocking ect, Drove old Cy down 
 to Middle in afternoon. 
 Tickle round ewes in morning 
 &amp; cutting logs for mill stand.
