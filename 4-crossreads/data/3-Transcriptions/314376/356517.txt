<p>206<br/>possessor of a Military Medal.</p>
<p>For his conduct in this instance he was again recommended.</p>
<p>Eventually we were relieved about 2 o'clock in the afternoon &ndash; not at all sorry either. We had to run the gauntlet of the German Artillery who knew we were "changing over". Speaking of the German artillery I was under the impression that it was worked by fiends and not men.</p>
<p>The continuous sound of discharge &ndash; whoof-hoo is the nearest approach I can get to it which also seemed like a cough.</p>
<p>Well, we got back as far as Vaux and thought that we had definitely finished</p>
