 Thursday 16 September 1915&nbsp; About Hospital all day&nbsp; 
 Friday 17&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; About hospital al day,&nbsp; Nothing particular doing&nbsp; 600 patients today&nbsp; 
 Saturday 18&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; Went with Father Vrigille to Heliopolis to see some of his friends. Had afternoon tea with them, then went for a motor drive.&nbsp; In Cairo during the evening&nbsp; 
 Sunday 19&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; At Basilica 9-30 am.&nbsp; Not out otherwise.&nbsp;
