Hard cheese! Yesterday was my first free day. Went into Cairo in the morning & saw Rev. Horan Rector of All Saints to whom I had just received an introduction. In the afternoon went to my French lesson & after tea to the CinemaMarch 10 FridayI have been pretty active during the last few days although there is not much work attached to it. While the O.C. is away I am attending the N.C.Os instructional squad which drills from 6.30 to 7.30 a.m. daily. All our nice comfortable beds have been packed
