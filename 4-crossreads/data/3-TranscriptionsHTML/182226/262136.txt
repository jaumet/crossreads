<p>Saturday 24 April, 1915</p>
<p><s>The 5th Brigade went on route march 18 miles.</s></p>
<p><s>The Brigade consisted of 17 - 18 - 19 Battalions of Infantry.</s></p>
<p>The 5<sup>th</sup> Brigade with Light Horse, Artillery, A.M.C. and French Sailors march through Sydney on a fare well send off.</p>
<p>Total number of Troops present 5,000.</p>
<p>25,000 people to view us, we marched part of the way, From Victoria Markets to&nbsp;Station through the rain.</p>
