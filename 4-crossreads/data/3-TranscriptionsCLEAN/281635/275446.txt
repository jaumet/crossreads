
5/2/18 Sent into hospital again today. The Dr here is the one I saw when first reported sick at Ypres & then again at the Field Ambulance when first went into hospital6/2/18 Food excellent at this hospital. Best have had in France. An Aust. lady in this afternoon issuing toilet necessaries to all men.7/2/18 Sent picture post cards of Boulogne to Australia.11/2/18 Marked out of hospital & recommended for Medical Board. After tea went into hospital & played 3 or 4 games of chess.12/2/18 Went before Board and marked"Temporary Base 3 weeks". Recd letter from Uncle Fred.13/2/18 Wrote to Uncle Fred.
