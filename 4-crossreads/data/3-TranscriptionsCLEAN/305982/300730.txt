  
 Lady Birdwood visited the hospital today - Slim woman.  nothing doing afterwards 
 8/9/16 Half a sleep last night, before doctor again to day he cannot make out what is wrong a bit of a  mists  mystery about it  Beautiful day again just nice for a good swim.  food OK in this hospital, fellow has sunstroke here in my ward a pitiful sight in fact one sees same wherever he looks now. 
 9/9/16 22 years old today.  beautiful day. 
 10/9/16 Nothing of any interest Sunday to day.  Still in hospital.  Weather beautiful went for a walk this evening towards Weymouth (town) on bridges  Beautiful girls going to church quite a difference to what I had seen previously about wrote letter to adjutant 13th Batt 
 11/9/16 Nothing doing other than to eat & lay about the joint etc 
 12/9/16 Letter from young Brown Hubert in answer to mine.  out of hospital
