She has been converted into an aeroplane ship and is to sail for the coast of Syria in a few weeks time.  The day continued hot, but a cool breeze sprang up at sunset.  Tuesday 1st June 1915.   Very warm again, nil doing everything quiet.  Wednesday 2nd June 1915.   Bruce from 42 paid a visit this afternoon.  They expect to go to the Dardanelles as a Storeship in a week or two.  Yachting regatta took place after dinner.  Ten fine yachts participated.  Received a letter from KA, tonight.  Thursday 3rd June 1915.   Kings Birthday.  Many ships in port decorated with flags to commemorate occasion though the day is not a public holiday.
