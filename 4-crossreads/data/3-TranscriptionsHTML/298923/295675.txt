<p>9 January 1918 Wednesday<br/>Cleared off &amp; a Parcel mail arrived- Skin trouble still worrying me but better.  The whole landscape is mud &amp; Water but Johnny Turk quiet &amp; no enemy Aeroplanes-  Gen. Cox has left- I think for good- Col Onslow is expected to get the Brigade-   Day's Bed Soks most useful- have 2 pairs now-  Warm weather comes in March<br/>Dont know how mails are going but post this with love to all   FW</p>
