May 19th
I write this in Nasrich Hosp.  I kept going with the bad foot, &amp; apparently it was healing nicely when, this week, without apparent reason it grew inflamed again &amp; more painful.  The following morning I thought it was better, so I kept going resting, as I had always done, in the afternoon (when possible).
