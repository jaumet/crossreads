 Suzannes in the evening - Sunny day with Snow squalls. 
   
 Mar 2  nd    I went out at 8.A.M. & took a fatigue party of 45  th   Battalion up to Vierstradt. it was very cold & windy & snowed a bit in the afternoon - We slept well on our new beds last night. the night was cold. 
   
  Memo .  Weather mostly cold & windy. with spells of bright sun, this week - Fritz raided our lines on Friday night whilst our 13  th   battalion was changing over but he came a thud. 
   
 Mar. 3  rd    Still cold but the wind dropped towards afternoon - We cleaned the lorry in the morning & did nothing in the afternoon. In the evening we went up to Suzanne's for a while then came home & did some Pelman 
   
 Mar. 4  th    We did'nt go out to day. we are said to have finished with the chows now. we went up to Suzanne's in the afternoon & took their photos to them. fairly cold all day but no wind, it rained in the evening. It looks like Japan coming into the war -
