
Thursday 20/4/16Fatigue work practically all day.  All camps about the desert given Australian names such as Duntroon, Narrabri, Brighton Beach.
Friday 21.4.16Good Friday; water fatigue.  Blankets fumigated by a sanitary squad, who travel from camp to camp for such duty.  Church parades during day, but was unable to attend owing to fatigue duty.  Paid 150 piastres =  1-10-9
Saturday 22/4/16Mail day.  Route march afternoon.  Scratch football match at night.
Sunday 23/4/16Very warm.  No duty to day  Meeting at night of Officers & men
