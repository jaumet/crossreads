<p>DEC.&nbsp;&nbsp; MONDAY 18&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; misty with heavy frost. Making &amp; leveling stables in the morning and I took a horse to the A.V.C yard in the afternoon. Bill Hunter &amp; I went to the pictures in the evening &amp; they were not up to much.</p>
