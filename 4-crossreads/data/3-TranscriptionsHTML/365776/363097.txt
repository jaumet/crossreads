<p>name of Mustard Gas.<br/>Today the 30th July or <strike>Y</strike> day is drawing very close to the great offensive &amp; attack about to be launched at the Huns. this will be the third great battle of Ypres &amp; by the preparations, the greatest, for tomorrow the 31st is the beginning of the greatest battle in the history of this war. everything is in readiness for the move forward &amp; as perfect as is possible for such a huge offensive, for weeks these preparations have been going on, with all the secrecy possible, massing artillery, &amp; infantry up close to the line, getting ammunition dumps up close of all sizes from 18 pr. &amp; six</p>
