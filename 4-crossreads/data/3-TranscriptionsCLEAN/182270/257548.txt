 SUNDAY, AUGUST 24, 1919. 
 Fine but cloudy & dull. Drove down 
 to Abbottslea in the morning with 
 some woolpacks ect. Linda came 
 with me. Tickle & I put the 4D 
 sheep in the Abbottslea shed in the 
 afternoon ready for shearing 
 tomorrow. Al. drove Maisie, Myee 
 Jo, Jack Charley over to Coomoo Coomoo 
 in big car for lunch.
