  
 Friday June 27th Adelaide 
 Moved off about 1 am : quiet time : ran into bad weather & got a good throw about 
 Saturday June 28th at Sea 
 Sighted land all the time : the "Orontes" behind us & we will just about beat her in : moored up about 6 pm : after the Vic's got away we were given leave : Snow Gordon & self went to "Going up" & it wasn't too bad : plenty of soldiers about : three transports in port : 
 Sunday June 29th at Sea 
 Church Parade in morn : comforts issue after dinner :
