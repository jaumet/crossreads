<p>1918</p>
<p>Jan 2</p>
<p>In for a mysterious show. Have been asked by Gen Smythe V C, Dir. Com if I would undertake a desperate venture, which would probably cost me my life, but had a sporting chance.</p>
<p>Nature of job and place of operations not stated.</p>
<p>Jan 4th.  Got word today to report at Corps H.Q. tomorrow.</p>
