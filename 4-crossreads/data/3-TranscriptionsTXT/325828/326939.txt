Well all went well &amp; we were going along when Wal turned round &amp; gave me his cigarette saying "Here Le, finish this one for me:  I've only had about 2 or 3 draws at it, &amp; now I'm going to "puke." Talk about laugh. Rid &amp; I nearly choked laughing at poor old Wal. Must close now, Daddy dear Trusting you are all keeping well at "Cairnleath" With much love to you &amp; Mother dear, not forgetting Ol &amp; Anna, Your loving son, Leo.
