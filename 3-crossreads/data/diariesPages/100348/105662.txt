31/12/16.
Duty in the outpost last night was a terrible ordeal. There was pitiless rain and the water rose and rose; it was bitterly cold too. One poor fellow collapsed, and although we tried to restitute him, he died. I survived the ordeal somehow and got back to the dugout at daylight. We were releived from the outpost at 10 P.M. I helped my mate, who was very sick; to Company Headquarters; where I managed to get a double issue of rum.
