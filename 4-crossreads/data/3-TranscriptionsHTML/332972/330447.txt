<p>[Newspaper clipping with handwritten date &ndash; 22/4]<br/>Der Holdsvorthich Himmel<br/>"The German internees in Australia have been reposing in the lap of luxury, . . . There were butchers' shops, fruit shops nine cafes and restaurants. There were numerous athletic and social clubs, dramatic chess clubs, two choral societies, several theatres, and an open-air picture show. "<br/>Und how you vos put in der day,<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And how you vos amuse,<br/>Vhen out you vos bei Bot'ny Bay<br/>In yoal, pecause you disobey,<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und spracht dose Deutschtum views?<br/>Ach, not so bad it vos, by Gee,<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und not so bad at all,<br/>Far besser als in Germany You been in, Berlin bei der Spree<br/>Vhen Kaiser Bill vos call.</p>
<p>Weies brot, und blenty fleish zu eat,<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und no hard vorks zu do!<br/>Ach, ja! It vos ein lofely dreat<br/>Dot surelich neffer can be beat,<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I bet zwel mark mit you.<br/>But efenin's, dey vos pooty dull?<br/>Ach, nein, Fritz, you vos wrong,<br/>Die nachts mit harmony vos full,<br/>Und barties meet to hate Yohn Bull,<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Mit speech, und yoke, und song.</p>
<p>Und did die guards der fist und boot<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und rifle-butt display,<br/>Like in our brison-kampfs so goot?<br/>Of course, dey somedimes shtab und shoot<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In Deutschland's downright vay?<br/>Nein, neffer vonce, dey lofed uns wohl<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On far Owsdralia's shore<br/>Dey dreated uns,lieb' Fritz, top-hole;<br/>Now Germany ist oop der pole,<br/>Weh! Ve must vork some more. .</p>
<p>Gott! Hans, you don't not mean to say<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Dot you brefers to be<br/>Shut in dot Holdsvorthick avay,<br/>Vhere all you do ist eat and blay<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und schlep? Py Chiminy!<br/>Dot's vhere you're allzugedder wrong<br/>Dot's yoost der bloke I am,<br/>Fur Berlin, where die shtarves belong<br/>Vhile Holdsvorthick ist going strong<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I do not care ein dam.</p>
