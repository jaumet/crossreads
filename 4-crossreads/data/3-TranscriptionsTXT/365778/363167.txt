 Thursday 11/1/17 
 Visited Rifle Range now almost complete. Cable from Doff, also letters. 
 Friday 12/1/17 
 Visited by Colonel Smith 5th Bde, also Babe, they both were delighted &ndash; went for a spin to Amien returning at 6.30 pm. 
 Saturday 13/1/17 
 Usual Training &ndash; men shaping well &ndash; lecture at night to Officers &amp; N.C.O's on Map Reading. 
 Sunday 14/1/17 
 Inspection of 6th Bde. No bad &ndash; visited by Col Reynolds &ndash; went to see Rifle Range. Men played football in afternoon &ndash; Wrote to Doff.
