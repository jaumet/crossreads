<p>Saturday 3rd August 1917 [1918]<br/>Went 27th 4.40 p.m., tea with Mrs. Belcher.  Very enjoyable &amp; what a grasp she has of hosp. management &amp; a good nurse or a good idea of one under all the military stuff.<br/>Some nice pts. out today.<br/>Major Adderley<br/>Lt. MacIntyre<br/>Lt. Horan<br/>Lt. Reid, N.Z.</p>
