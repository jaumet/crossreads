<p>Monday 21 February, 1916</p>
<p>We have been living on the fat of the land for the last six days, what with our canteen stuff &amp; the comfort fund we have had a high old time but all our good things have gone now I finished my last tin of fish today.</p>
<p>Lieut Hamilton came back to the section today, he is fatter than ever.</p>
<p>Was mess orderly today.</p>
