<p>1916 2 January 2<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Jack rouses me at 7. 30 am for surf.  Enjoy it immensely.  Breakfast &amp; church in forenoon.  Go to Swansai for dinner.  Leave at 4 pm.  Go out to Robbins to tea at Waverly.  Methodist  Church at Junction with Bob in evening &amp; see Charles Lette there.  At Stburys &amp; Jack Netria's Lee  Miss Noble  Home with Rob.  Back to camp with Astbury &amp; Rob 11 pm.</p>
<p>Captn. F. G. Ward<br/>Chaplin<br/>3rd Battn.</p>
