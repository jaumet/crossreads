I met her mother & quite a number of others who seemed to regard me as quite a curio.   I believe that no Australians have ever been billeted here before & so the appearance of one is quite a novelty.   I like M'Selle partly on account of the fact that she supplies me with cider and cafe gratis.
May 23   Usual drill in morning and half-holiday in afternoon, I walked to Molliens-aux-bois and visited a quiet home in a side street for a cup of cafe.   I love visiting these places, for by so doing, one is enabled to see into the inner side of a French home & to hear, from the lips of those most deeply concerned, opinions upon the war.
