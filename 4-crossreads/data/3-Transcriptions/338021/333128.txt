<p>To OC 13th M.G. Coy<br/>Situation report</p>
<p>New gun received &amp; mounted last night.  a.a.a. Am returning Pte Neal as his leg is too stiff to be of any use in action. O.C. took away spare parts last night. My co-ordination was wrong apologise a.a.a.</p>
<p>Thanks for letter &amp; cigarettes.</p>
<p>Casualties Nil<br/>S.A.A. expended Nil<br/>Heavy firing occasionally during night by enemy artillery. No damage</p>
<p>CHP Weston Lieut<br/>8.15am 25/9/17</p>
