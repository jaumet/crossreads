 11 April, Friday 
 Dull 
 Recd P.C. from Ellis. Left Glasgow on 10.4.19. I pack up my bags I am supposed to be on Orderly Room &amp; I go for a walk up Tin-Town. Ask F to Dance. Send Parcel &amp; paper to Auntie &amp; Marie. I go down to room &amp; write, read &amp; talk with Mac &amp; Joe. 
 I d. that I find all the jewels &amp; the Diamond on the railway line at bottom of Platform at Strathfield. 
 Sent Parcels &amp; Paper to Aunt Annie. 
 12 April, Saturday 
 F. I scrounge around wards but get nothing. 
 I go to Dance at night &amp; I am real lively. Play &amp; dance &amp; say Goodbye to several old Friends. I go down &amp; talk about Coast Hospital for an hour with Mac.
