  
 Friday Oct 5 Usual daily visit by Jacko in planes to see what was doing. Was told today that all Turkish planes were manned by German Officers & at present were superior to ours. Believe another Ausy Flying Squadron is being formed to fix Jacko. Saturday Oct 6 Quite cool this morning 2ND Brigade sport in progress Tom came over from 1ST Brigade & we had a good day at sports. What is wrong with the mail Sunday Oct 7 Moving to Bulah on 17th All water sterilised on account of Bilharzia
