Was nice fine sunshine day, but had shower rain after tea. Was marked C2 on Board on Friday.
10/11/18 SundayWrote Home XX & to Watty Last. Sent before Dr. this morning & he marked me to work in a ward in the hospital here. Saw the Sergt in medical office after, so he is putting me on clerical work in the office. Fairly cold & windy.
11/11/18Started in medical office this morning on "records" work. After tea went into Weymouth. The town is all decorated with flags on account of the "Armistice" but not much excitement. A few kids were making bit of a row. Showery all day.
12/11/18Handed in application for leave to return to Australia via America.
13/11/18After tea walked into Weymouth. Free beer was issued to the troops at lunch time. Fine sunshine.
14/11/18List came out for transport D 35 today & see that I am on it on "Ships staff". Recd letters from Muriel dated 1/9/18 & 15/9/18 & one from Mrs Brown dated 15/9/18, also p/card from Minnie Lusc. Still nice &  fine but chilly breeze blowing. After tea went into Weymouth & cabled to Australia thus "Mail nothing. Returning. Well".
15/11/18Wrote to Muriel XX and to Mrs Brown. Handed in pay book to be exchanged for a pay card.
16/11/18After tea went into Weymouth.
