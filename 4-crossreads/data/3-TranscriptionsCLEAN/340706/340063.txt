But there is no annoyance in a refusal & no complaint for wasting time. 
 There is nothing repellant like the same class of English girl. It is their business & while one pities their choice one does not feel disgusted with these votaries of Love or Lust. 
 While waiting for Doc I met Jessie Alosi an acquaintance of my friend & she took me in behind the now closing scenes of the Casino, & a rare sight it was to see these girl - women in age & intellect but romping & skylarking like a lot of schoolgirl. Truly the temperament of the French is hard to grasp. 
 I wait until midnight for the Doc & many a [Montmatroise] endeavours to entice me from my tryst. However as Doc does not come I go home to the hotel where I am staying tonight Hotel Mondain 44 Rue Pigalle & am so tired that I do not get up till 11 am though I have to meet Dr at 11 am
