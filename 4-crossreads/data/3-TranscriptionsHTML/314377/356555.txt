<p>There had been a roof in it some years ago but a great fire had partially demolished the structure The streets were cobbled. There was a stationery shop in the town We purchased several items therein.</p>
<p>Our first billet in the Cassell area was a small outhouse adjoining a farm building. My fiends wouldn't stop in the shed but went out under the trees. I decided for a particular reason that I would stop in the outhouse. My argument was that I should never be afraid to display my allegiance to the Saviour of men. (It had been my practice to pray nightly in front of them.) I went there and said my prayers there.</p>
