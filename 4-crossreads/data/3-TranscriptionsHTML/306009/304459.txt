<p>1915<br/>Oct. 4 Mon.<br/>Was very glad to get a birthday parcel containing something from each one at home  &ndash; 12 papers came through the post all of which, except 1, were from Eric Burke &ndash; 100 three Castles Cigarettes also arrived, from Wal. Crocker.<br/>Had a very interesting letter from Ray Smith relating his experiences since leaving Gezira. He is now with No. 2 Stat. Hosp at Mudros.</p>
