<p>[May-June, 1916.]<br/>Monday 29<br/>France a perfect fairy land, still in the train and not far from the sound of the big guns feeling OK. N E M<br/>Tuesday 30<br/>Visited the Bull-ring full marching order and the Bull turned out to be a bloody cow<br/>Wednesday 31<br/>Eyes right eyes left, thirty miles from the firing line bully beef bed and biscuit long marches and short sleeps, 3 hours from London<br/>Thursday June 1<br/>Arms Armys Ammunition Aeroplanes Airships<br/>Bombs Bully Biscuits &amp; Beer parts of France</p>
