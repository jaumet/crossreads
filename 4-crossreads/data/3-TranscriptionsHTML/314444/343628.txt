<p>[Private Claude Hansen, No 1020, a storeman of Dawes Point, NSW, enlisted on 10 September 1914 at age 20, and embarked from Sydney on HMAT A23 Suffolk on 18 October 1914. He served with 2nd Battalion, H Company at Gallipoli where he was killed in action on 25 April 1915.]<br/>[Envelope]<br/>1020 Private C. Hansen<br/>H. Company<br/>2nd Battalion<br/>Australian Expeditionary Force<br/>Egypt</p>
