Secret wires were found in the cellar of this "mansion". Experts were called in to examine &amp; pronounced it safe still we are living in a delightful uncertainty. It was funny putting our beds down last night, gee it was breezy. Our Pay Sgt, McLaughlan &amp; myself slept side by side on a once tiled floor.
