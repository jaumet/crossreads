<p>25 April 1918<br/>Thursday<br/>Anzac Day was kept up in the Command by sports. Beautiful fine day and the sports are said to have been success-ful.</p>
<p>1918 April 26<br/>Friday<br/>Just the same old camp routine. Things on the French Front still going against us. Nice warm weather.</p>
