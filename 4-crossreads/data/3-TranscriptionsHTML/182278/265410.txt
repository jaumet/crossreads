<p>Monday 15/4/18. Intercompany relief&nbsp; caught prisoner, visited posts</p>
<p>Tuesday 16/4/18&nbsp; wired half front &amp; commenced to connect posts. Wrote to Doff.</p>
<p>Wednesday 17/4/18. Finished wiring &amp; connecting posts&nbsp; dug a C.T. Herbert Killed.</p>
<p>Thursday 18/4/18 Relieved by Currie- moved back to Support</p>
<p>Friday 19/4/18 Remained in support&nbsp; 2 Coys digging in front area</p>
<p>Saturday 20/4/18. Relieved by Dawson&nbsp; moved back to Reserve&nbsp; one Coy bathed- Two letters from Doff.</p>
