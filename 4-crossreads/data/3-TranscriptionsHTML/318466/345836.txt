<p>Sunday 20 October 1918<br/>Still in bed &amp; crook.</p>
<p>Monday 21 October 1918<br/>My flu changes to pneumonia, at least I <u>think</u> it was today.</p>
<p>Tuesday 22 October 1918<br/>Matey came up from Worthing.</p>
<p>Wednesday 23 October 1918<br/>Gettg. rid of the pneumonia.</p>
<p>Thursday 24 October 1918<br/>Getting better &amp; sitting up &amp; taking notice.  The Doc. looks quite happy!!</p>
<p>Friday 25 October 1918<br/>Still in bed.  Rup called at C.P.R. but we could not get double berth yet.  Doc. says illness due to gas but don't believe him.</p>
<p>Saturday 26 October 1918<br/>Got up today, feeling somewhat groggy.</p>
