 December, 1915 
 Saturday 11 
 Arrived in Alexandria at wharf during the morning. 
 Disembarked to hospital train in afternoon where we were accommodated in bunks. 
 During the ride to Cairo we were served with tea, chocolate &amp; cigarettes by the nurse &amp; red cross lady. 
 Arrived at back of 1st A.G.H., Derbyshire was the first man aboard.  He brought Kennett &amp; Lightbody &amp; in a few minutes I had met a lot of old friends.  Woodberry an old room mate is orderly in No.10 ward  to which I was sent.
