
Great  excitement over the loaded biscuit tin & a Bosch auto-Starter.3rd Sunday.  More fun with the biscuit tin. Arabs willingly give bread for this tin but can't carry it away. Cold and cloudy.4th Cloudy & cold5th Change sets with Singo. Move off at 7pm for Tripoli. Camp at midnight and5/11/18 move off at 6am crossing the crater of an extinct volcano. Wish the big German car would smash & it did Camp in a hell of a place away from water and among boulders.6/11/18 move again 6 am and camp 2pm on beach near Tripoli.7/11/18 Move 8am through Tripoli camping 5 miles S & up on the hills
