<p>Several more horses got bullet wounds  &amp;  another man belonging to this outfit got hit in the foot.<br/>Then until 5 oclock pm. we stood by back in camp while the infantry held the turks. All this while the Turks could shrapnell all the camp,  &amp;   - which was a great surprise &ndash; had a couple of 6" g howitzers in Katia with which they bombarded the redoubts  &amp;  railway unmercifully but fortunately a great part of their effect is lost in the soft sand.</p>
<p>At 5 pm we started off  &amp; made a big detour to the N  &amp;</p>
