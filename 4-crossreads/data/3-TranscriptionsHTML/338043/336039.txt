<p>The first stage of the journey from Avesnes to Le Cateau is made in a motor lorry &ndash; We rest for the night in this latter town &amp; enjoy the hospitality of the owner of the billet which we occupied a few weeks previous &ndash;</p>
<p>Dec. 21st<br/>Rise 6 a.m. and walk 8 kilo's to Bertry.<br/>- At 10 a.m. entrain for Busigny &ndash; Then on to Cambrai &ndash; Change trains at Cambrai &ndash;</p>
