 Sunday 20 August 1916 Play for Church.  No sleep.  D., E. &amp; I go to old Cairo to see cutting of Khalig.  We meet two Gentlemen in the Royal Salon.  I come home for Duty &amp; E. &amp; D. go Feasting. 
 Monday 21 August 1916 I play Tennis &amp; play the Piano.  Ellis goes to Cairo for Films &amp; comes Home in a side car.  Wrote to Marg. &amp; Maisie Carter. 
 Tuesday 22 August 1916 Mail Day.  E.T. gets 4 letters.  Letters from D. Vernon, Dad, Bess, Otto, H. Hey, Mum &amp; George. 
 Wednesday 23 August 1916 Wrote to H. Hey &amp; D.C. Vernon, Otto &amp; Annie (5) &amp; Jess Mather, Bess.  Ellis plays tennis &amp; hurts his foot.  The mob have Physical Jerks. 
 Thursday 24 August 1916 Wrote Home (65).  Very windy, dusty day.  E.T. Condon &amp; I dine in Chanchie [?] Alley on Tomatoes, apricots, Bread, butter &amp; coffee. 
 Friday 25 August 1916 Ellis goes to No. 3.  Dave &amp; I go to Cairo &amp; Old Cairo Cemetery.  I take Photo &amp; Beat D. at Billiards.  I still go on Night Duty yet no sleep.  Hot Fom. from 3 to 6.30 on one chap. 
 Saturday 26 August 1916 Leave all day starts from 9 a.m. to 9.30 p.m.  I go to Cairo for Film.  I go off night Duty &amp; have no Duty at all.  I receive letters from Dad, Mum, Maisie Carter, Julie, Nettie.
