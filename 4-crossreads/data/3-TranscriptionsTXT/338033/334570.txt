 Kept busy by the supply trains to the many large camps situate on this God forsaken country. Great systems of trenches could be seen everywhere with light railways running right to convey the supplies to the troops in the lines of defence. One could easily understand the difficulty in obtaining sufficient water to the troops, and our comrades in arms who were suffering so much.
