 Friday 31st Air raid last night with plenty of  dogin  dodging about overhead, but no local bombs. Yesterday afternoon I commenced helping Vic paint physical jerks diagrams show correct &amp; incorrect methods. A couple of days respite from chills etc. Glorious weather, &amp; feelings of good health &amp; confidence in the future. Fritz is at it again, &amp; now it is south of the Somme
