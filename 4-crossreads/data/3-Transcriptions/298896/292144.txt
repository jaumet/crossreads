<p>Saturday 17th Aug.<br/>Up feeling fit &amp; after breakfast carried on &amp; completed my tent. Then decided to go easy which I did in the morning but set to in the afternoon &amp; squared up my stocks, sorting &amp; arranging etc. also wrote a letter home, the first for many many days. Weather still warm. Latest news gives total captures for one month 75000 prisoners &amp; 1700 guns so his earlier efforts are to a certain extent nullified by our two offensives.</p>
<p>Sunday 18th Aug.<br/>Very quiet so wrote a letter home, also prepared an indent to stock this place up with drugs we having only dressings at present. Interviewed the Colonel about my taking on a job in England for a few months.
