<p>Wednesday 5.7.16<br/>Loaded wagons in readiness for moving off.  Issued with 2 gas helmets which we are compelled to carry always, also glasses to counteract the effect of the tear bombs.  <br/>Small tube of Iodine given to each man for use in the field.  Paid 25 Francs 17/11</p>
<p>Thursday 6.7.16<br/>Barn piquet.  Results of pay day; disturbance in camp at night among some of the men who imbibed too freely</p>
<p>Friday 7.7.16<br/>Mail from home, first for over 6 weeks.  Capt Donald visited camp.  Received instruction to move off in morning.</p>
<p>Saturday 8.7.16<br/>Moved off from Morbecque in rain about 9 AM.  Roads very muddy in parts.  Passed through Merville at midday.  Numbers of Infantrymen</p>
