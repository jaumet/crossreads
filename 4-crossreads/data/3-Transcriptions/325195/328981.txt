<p>Sun 24th<br/>Today has been very cold, Church Service was held his afternoon in a large Hospital tent that was erected this morning at the Field Ambulance as it was drizzling rain, I was too comfortable lying down in my dugout with a blanket over me to attend &amp; I think nearly every one else felt the same .Reid took last shift last night &amp; Les &amp; I slept in till 9-oclock this morning; The warboats have been very busy this afternoon to the south of us but everything is very quiet here; the poor beggars in the trenches &amp; on patrol get a rough time of it these nights; 3 Officers &amp; 85 men arrived at 10 oclock tonight, Harris, Weir &amp; Snodgrass &amp; the 8th Reinf.</p>
