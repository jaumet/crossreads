<p>Thursday 10 May 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille 2.30 am. Breakfast 3 am.&nbsp;Sea kits on baggage wagon 3.15 am. &nbsp;</p>
<p>&quot;Fall in&quot; on parade ground in full marching order 3.45 a.m. Left camp 4.15 am. Entrained at Seymour Station 5.25 am. Arrived at Port Melbourne 8 p.m. Disentrained &amp; marched to boat&#39;s side. Marched on board 11.30 a.m.&nbsp;<s>Embarked</s><sub>&nbsp;</sub>Sailed 12.15 pm.</p>
<p>(Visitors not allowed on pier at all.)</p>
<p>Anchored just inside heads 4.30 pm. Tea at 4.35 pm. Clan McGill<s>ll</s>iveray anchored close by at 4.45 pm.</p>
<p>Slung hammocks at 7 p.m. for 1<sup>st</sup> time. Went on deck again &amp; turned in.</p>
<p>Transport (A42) &quot;Boorara&quot;.</p>
