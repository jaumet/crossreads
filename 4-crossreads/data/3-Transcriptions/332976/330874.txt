<p>June 1917<br/>15th   The company were on pontooning again this morning &amp; had the afternoon off as usual.<br/>Hard luck came my way to-day-  I had left some photos at Contay when I was there to be developed by a photographer <strike>there</strike> but most unfortunately the M.P.s  got to know of it, with the result that they were sent on to H.Qs who now demand an inquiry into the matter.   This look a serious affair, seeing that cameras are forbidden in France, &amp; may probably mean a court martial for me.   <strike>The</strike> I am now awaiting developments, &amp; a report has been sent to C.R.E.</p>
