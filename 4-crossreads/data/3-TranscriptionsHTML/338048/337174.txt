<p>manage a little walk in the evening to Durrington which is about 1 &frac12; miles away. We went to church in the Y.M.C.A. first &amp; then walked. It is not dark here until about 10 p.m. owing to the long twilight and it being midsummer.</p>
<p>I have been very agreeably surprised with England which appears to be one great garden with little old rustic villages scattered about. We have not yet had leave but expect 4 days soon. If we are lucky we will get <strike>the</strike> a Sunday &amp; Saturday thrown in.</p>
<p>I have not seen any of the boys yet but have Harold Ponsford's location. I saw Vic French of Wesley this morning.
