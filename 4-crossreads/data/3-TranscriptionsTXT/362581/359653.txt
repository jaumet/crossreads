 XXOXOX 3rd A.C.C.S. 11.3.17 My darling Eddy  XOX 
 Received your lovely letter to day   &amp;  I think  you are a very clever little boy. Hope you are growing strong   &amp;  able to have a good time   &amp;  run about. You will be a very big boy by the time I return &ndash; soon be as big as auntie Eadle .  Fondest love   &amp;  kisses   &amp;  hugs. From OXOX Your auntie Eadle  XXXXXX
