
Monday 25/6/17Night march and assault, returned at 6.30 am, went to Bde in afternoon, saw Bas.Tuesday 26/6/17Wrote to Doff. Training as usual saw Bas again.Wednesday 27/6/17Most lovely photo of Doff received, very good indeed.Thursday 28/6/17Field Firing in morning, raining in afternoon.Friday 29/6/17Battalion Scheme & inspection in afternoon.Saturday 30/6/17Raining heavily in afternoon, had a game of Cards.
