Big push up North with good results.
Saturday 9.6.17
Played 5th Div. Hqrs in afternoon defeated them; unable to strike form. Two more went on leave.
Sunday 10.6.17
Team played 4 D.A.P won by 7 wickets. Church parade for all hands.
