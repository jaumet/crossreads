  
 4 - that all our troops have done when they left for the front to go to the Mediteranean seat of war. Archie S. Skirving came just this way a week ago. He, his mother told me had a cabin to himself. There are wardrobes and chest of drawers in the cabins - after 9 pm every one officers & sisters are not allowed to go on the decks. I suppose in case of accident all lights are shaded. I should imagine we are a misty shade stealing over the sea - if we can be seen at all. Of course we wear uniform all the time now.
