<p>for Boulogne. We arrived at Boulogne about 6 Am &amp; embarked on fast paddle steamer about 11 Am.<br/>The journey to Folkestone took nearly two hours.<br/>We arrived London about 4 pm. marched to Horseferry Road where we drew our money &pound; 15 in my case.<br/>After booking a bed in this place another 55th &amp; I took a motor bus ride. We went through the strand passed St Pauls through Fleet St &amp; pulled at the Tube Stn. On the way back we got out, had a look at Nelson's Monument, &amp; walked through St James Park<br/>London June 22nd 17<br/>Am writing in train for Bradford Starts midnight. This morning went out to Gillingham, find that it is about 30 miles from London. Found Alice &amp; Syd. well. This evening I came from Victoria to King's Cross by underground, to put in time I went to a Vaudaville show opposite stn. which turned out to be a rotten show. I then went to a picture show,</p>
