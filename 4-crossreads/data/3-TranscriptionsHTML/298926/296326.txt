<p>27.  We managed to get 8 germans that is known of. ('We' does not in this instance <br/>mean our battalion but another battalion on what will be our front next spell in)</p>
<p>Jan 3rd  The Germans planted a German flag out in No Man's Land.  The next day our <br/>troops were continually firing at it but failed to bring it down  That night a patrol went out <br/>to bring it in but not one of them came back, the flag being surrounded by wire bomb <br/>traps.  What eventually came to the flag our C.S.M.</p>
