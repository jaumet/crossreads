<p>sifter thus providing for fuel economy.<br/><u>19th</u> We started off at 10A.M. this morning for Sains and after passing through the towns of Boulogne and Croix Rouge marched into billets at our destination about 2 p.m. Sains is a compact little town of about 2000 inhabitants. It rained and haled nearly the whole way. The roads were in a very bad condition.
