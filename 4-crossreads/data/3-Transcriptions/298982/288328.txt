<p>We talked of the so called Advance. The opinions of L &amp; I are not optimistic They are giving out rum again now. Courage. A la demi John.</p>
<p>Tues 3rd. All talk is of the move.</p>
<p>Wed 4th. Tite &amp; Cooper came to tea &amp; voted it a blow out. False attack by our front at 9 pm. The Aust &amp; Col generally seems to lack an eye for method &amp; thoroughness</p>
<p>Thur 5th. Called on Tite in morn. They cleared over to left in evening &amp; same with NZs. Bill Jenkins left &amp; Fred Price went away sick The move begins soon I hear Tommies landing every day or night now</p>
