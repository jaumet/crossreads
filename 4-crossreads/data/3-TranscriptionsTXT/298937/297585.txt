 Wednesday 7th June 
 Left for Maadi on the Monday following &amp; found a man from Citadel had arrived to learn dispensing so that he could take my place.  Has rather a good general knowledge of drugs, but not so well versed in practical work. Went to Base &amp; put in a second &amp; more emphatic application so that Molloy &amp; I should soon leave.
