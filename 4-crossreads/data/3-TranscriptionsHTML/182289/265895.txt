<p>18.5.18<br/>Company complete with Transport moved from camp along Albert-Amiens road to parade ground in H.20.b.  Formed up as Company in line with Transport in rear on the left of Battalion.<br/>Inspected by G.O.C. 3rd Aust. Division who remarked on the steadiness of the Company.  After the march past the G.O.C. spoke a few words to the Battalion as a whole and after being photographed the Coy. returned to camp.</p>
