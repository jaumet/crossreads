 26 December 1916 Tuesday Bank Holiday 
 Rest &amp; sleep during day. On duty again at 7 p.m.  Some of the boys shikked up today on Rum.  Sully, Paddy, Sol, Mac &amp; Bib. Paddy gets D.T's. struggle to keep him down.  Lob G. kicks over stove &amp; nearly sets hut alight &ndash; a lot of logs.
