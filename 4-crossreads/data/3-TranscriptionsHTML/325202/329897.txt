<p>This has been the mildest February for Europe that I have ever remembered.</p>
<p>Horwood has left us for a furlough to Australia The first man to go from D.H.Q. We are all hoping that it is the commencement of the promised furlough to Australia for the original men.</p>
<p>March 6 La Clytte<br/>we pulled in here on March 2 &ndash; (it is a sector of the Ypres front) it was one of the bitterest days I have spent on the road &amp; glad it was only some 16 kilos journey.</p>
