<p>The convoy consisted of<br/>[sketch of convoy formation]</p>
<p>&amp; 3 hydroplanes which swept down &amp; did some splendid work dropping depth bombs on the submarines.<br/>Having lost the Leasowe Castle 2800 troops &amp; 280 crew &amp; Omrah 1800 troops &amp; 180 crew during the last 14 days &amp; the total missing of both ships were crew 6 troops 89</p>
<p>June 15<br/>All troops disembarked &amp; com. discharging field stores &amp; ambulance waggons.</p>
