<p>Wednesday Nov 14th</p>
<p>London : "the end of a perfect holiday" : caught 12 noon to Warminster &amp; on to camp : V.D. &amp; scabie inspection &amp; then drew blankets &amp; went to hut : wrote after tea : have had a decent time on furlough &amp; it is a strain to come back to camp life : must just bog into work now till next holiday comes</p>
<p>Thursday Nov 15th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : reclassified B1A2 in morn : shifted to our right camp in afternoon : wrote at night</p>
<p>Friday Nov 16th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : didn't feel too good so went on sick parade &amp; got L.D. met Fred Poole &amp; Morris so had a night together : met F Costello &amp; George Hanmer too</p>
