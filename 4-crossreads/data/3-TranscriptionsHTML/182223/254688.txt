<p>Thursday 12&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />Bairam festival in Cairo today The Mohammadams in their best clothes in the streets.&nbsp;<br />In Cairo for one hour between 5-30 &amp; 6-30 p.m.&nbsp;<br />Sent a cable message to the girls, A telegram to Weston, Orsova, Port Said&nbsp;</p>
<p>Friday 13&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />About hospital all day.&nbsp;<br />Letter from Carrie &amp; Mrs Knowles.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Saturday 14&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />Everything quiet.&nbsp;<br />Hospital work going smoothly.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sunday 15&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />At the Basilica for Mass at 9-30 a.m.&nbsp;At the African Mission Convent, Zeitoun, at 7 p.m. for Benediction.&nbsp;<br />Wrote Knowles, Girls, Mollie.</p>
