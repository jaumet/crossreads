
Sunday 12 March 1916Went to Barage.  Lovely gardens & good & bad donkey rides.  Staff Moore, McIvor, Vin Devaney, Billie A., Ellis & I, Billie B.
Monday 13 March 1916
Tuesday 14 March 1916Dad & Mother, Mary Westy.  Reinforcement from No. 2 Gen. arrived, 60 & 4 Doctors.  Cois Cateer [?].  Milford Brothers work with Ellis.
Wednesday 15 March 1916Route March to Gizeh [also spelt Giza].  Went to Kursaal & saw Picture & People acting together.  Photo of P.C.
Thursday 16 March 1916Spring Beds issued or rather taken.
Friday 17 March 1916More Wireless going.  Boulogne our Destination?
Saturday 18 March 1916Nothing I can make a note of.  Just work but having easy time with 3 orderlies.
