We arrived at Enab about 2 pm &amp; camped here. Sunday Aug 18 Staying here until tomorrow. Horses &amp; men in good condition. Nothing doing to day Monday Aug 19 Moved from here 2 pm for Jerusalem. We arrived on outskirts of Jerusalem about 5 pm &amp; halted 1 hr for Tea. It was cold on the hills. After Tea we moved out passing through Jerusalem
