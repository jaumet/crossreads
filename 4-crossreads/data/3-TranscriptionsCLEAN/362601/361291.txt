  
 2 November, Sunday 
 I go to N Steyne in morning. Surf rotten. Go to Dobboe's Baths after dinner & hand. Meet Bert C & Lady from W.A. E & E for tea. Home & write at 8 Mr Tran. Meet Ted Collis & wife & family. Plant Dahlias & do weeding. 
 Wrote M 29 
 3 November, Monday 
 Go to B Cuneos. Mrs C away. We have music & chatter until bed time. 
 Sent mail & P.C. of wild flowers & ocean beach pamphlet.
