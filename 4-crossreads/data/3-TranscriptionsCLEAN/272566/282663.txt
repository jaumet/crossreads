  
 Tuesday 2 October 
 At 7.45 a.m. set off for trip to A.D.M.S. on the Menin Road about a mile further out than Ypres.  Returned by 9 a.m., travelling via Poperinghe both ways. Todays runs also include 14th Fld. Ambce. Busseboom - Poperinghe and the 6th Brigade Artillery at Ouderdom. To "K" Supply Column at Abeele. The weather has been hot and tiring denoting rain although there are no signs of clouds about. The moon being still bright "Fritz" came over again on his bombing expedition (or egg-laying competition, as the boys term it)
