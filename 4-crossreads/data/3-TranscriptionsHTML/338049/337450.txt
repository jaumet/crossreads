<p>Wednesday 28 August<br/>[King?] back to finish Div Train line. Fixed up many matters and stood by.</p>
<p>Ordered out immediately about 5 pm just before tea. Out to 251 Bde R.F.A then to 10th Bde then to 7th A.F.A. &amp; finally to Bray. Last section in pitch dark. Very hard.</p>
<p>Home about 12.30 a.m. Lines working well. Shelling heavy.</p>
