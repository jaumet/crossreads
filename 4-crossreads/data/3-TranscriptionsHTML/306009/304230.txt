<p>1915<br/>Feb.13 Sat.<br/>Am very lucky in being able to get hot water baths, washes, etc. as required.<br/>Mena House is a very fine place with every convenience.<br/>Saw Mr. Stevenson Jr. of Walther &amp; Stevenson Ltd. to-day. He is a Staff Sgt. In some mounted Unit</p>
