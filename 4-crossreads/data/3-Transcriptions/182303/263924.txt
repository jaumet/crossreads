
<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;TO OUR CRITIC</p>
<p>FROM AN AUSTRALIAN SOLDIER IN EGYPT</p>
<p><em><span style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">Aint yer got no blanky savvy,</span></em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Have yer got no better use,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Than to fling back home yer inky</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Products of your pens abuse.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Do yer think we&#39;ve all gone dippy,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Since we&#39;ve landed over here,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Is a a soldier less a soldier!</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Cause he socks a pint of beer.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Do yer take yer whisk&#39; and soda,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>In a cool and shady spot,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Waited on by little Hebes</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Who the boys don&#39;t know as &#39;ot</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Have yer got no loving Mother!</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Waiting for yer over &#39;Ome,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Do you own no smilin sister!</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Over there acrost the foam.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Do you thinks they likes you better,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Fer yer tales of drink and shame,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Do yer think they&#39;ll praise yer action,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>In defamin our fair name.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>One swallow makes no summer,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Three schickers not a force,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Where the few makes it a welter</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>You condems, the lot o&#39;course.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Do yer think yer Gawd Almighty,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Cos yer wears a captain&#39;s Stars.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Thinks us blokes is dirt beneath yer,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Men of low degrees &amp; bars.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Say yer cannot be AUstralian,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Let us say in yer defence</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Yer can read it on your coinage</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Honny soit qui mal y pense.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Cease yer woweristic whining.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Tell the truth &amp; play the game!</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>and we only ask fair dinkum!</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>How we keep Australia&#39;s name,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>We&#39;re not out to fight the Devil,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>On a new Salvarmy stunt,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>To reform the Arabs morals,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>While we&#39;re waitin for the front.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Let me ask you Mr Critic</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Try and face things with a smile,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Don&#39;t be finding all the crook-uns,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Studying them blokes all the while.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>Then write home nice and Proper,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>&quot;Bout the boys thats all true blue,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>And they&#39;ll love yer better mister,</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;"><em>This is my advice to you.</em></p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Gnr. F.E.WESTBROOK</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;4th Battery, A.F.A.</p>
<p style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;1st A.I.F. MENA.</p>
