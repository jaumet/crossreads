  
 the terrible procession from six o'clock onwards was something to marvel at. when I crossed Westminster Bridge on the way to the station on the last day of leave just after six I took it in as a perilous enterprise although I am a city bred man. The streets were slippery as I crossed over from the House of Commons side, there was a haze over the Thames and the searchlights playing up for a possible enemy aircraft made up a weird scene that will last in the memory for life.
