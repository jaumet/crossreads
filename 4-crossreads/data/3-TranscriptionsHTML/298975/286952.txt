<p>Sept 1917<br/>more seriously, &amp; do there best at what ever they have to do Polers will in future get a rough time</p>
<p>Sunday 2nd<br/>Church Paraide as usual a poor specimen of Padre. Went for a Walk in afternoon. One sees little else but soldiers about here. There is no where to go</p>
<p>Monday 3<br/>Nothing unusual. On Picquet in evening went to a little Pub "The Popplers", to keep order just the sort of Pub you read about even to the oldest inhabitant but it was a bit overrun with soldiers</p>
<p>Tuesday 4<br/>Second inoculation &amp; issued with new pay books today.</p>
