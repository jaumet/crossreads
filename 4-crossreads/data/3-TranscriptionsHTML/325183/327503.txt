<p><strike>in</strike> at Marseilles today or tomorrow Paris has arranged a grand reception for him.<br/>Mr. McKenzie B.A. is the Educational Officer attached to our Battalion and a very able and energetic officer his is too. He commenced his duties exactly nine days ago. He has been busy interviewing men in the Bn. ever since, finding out what study they wish to take up and organising classes for them. Bishop Long is supervising the Educational Scheme and Gen. Monash has the Demobilisation Scheme in hand.<br/>The Educational Scheme forms the basis of the Demobilisation Scheme.</p>
