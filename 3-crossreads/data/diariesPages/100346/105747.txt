Friday July 13th
Bapaume : good day : reveille 4.30 am : fell in for "stunt" : march to Warlencourt then the "boys" attached "took" some trenches then Loupart Wood : seen a taube come down : home about 3 pm :
Saturday July 14th
Bapaume : good day : reveille 6 am : sent map to Aunt Myra Hun paper to Mum : stroll round after tea :
Sunday July 15th
Bapaume : reveille 6.30 am : Holy Comm 9.30 am : stroll after tea : interesting debate between "Griffin"
