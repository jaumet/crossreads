<p>Saturday 8 September 1917.</p>
<p>Out at 5 a.m. as usual. Grooming and feeding as usual.</p>
<p>Gas parade at 7.30 am. in full kit for&nbsp;&frac12; hr.</p>
<p>Generally fooled around till midday. A little time to myself later in day.</p>
<p>My dugout companion has gone to Malaleh Beach on duty. Have &quot;Terra Domus&quot; to myself now.</p>
