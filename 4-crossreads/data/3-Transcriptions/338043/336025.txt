<p>- Diary. Rehearsal of Sinbad.</p>
<p>Nov. 23rd<br/>New members join our Party &ndash; Continuation of Rehearsals but no shows &ndash;<br/>New verses written</p>
<p>Nov. 24th   (Sabbath)<br/>Further additions to the strength of our Company &ndash; Le Cateau holds but little interest for us, and the days are now most uneventful &ndash;<br/>"Sinbad" &ndash; Letters.</p>
<p>Nov. 25th<br/>Letter from Herb</p>
