
To O.C. 13 M.G. CoyGun at J3d 7.8 blown out. Gun damaged also several belts & boxes (2)
Cpl Pike wounded rather serious. Pte Neal badly shaken.
Send new gun. also N.C.O. & man to replace casualties as soon as possible.
Will return damaged material by same party
Pte Neal will probably be O.K. tomorrow. Cpl Pike evacuated by Stretcher Bearers. Will have to get new position dug during night.
