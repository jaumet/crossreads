 Wednesday 1 March 1916 Loaded up carrier with wheat. Dick &amp; Geoff plowing all day.  Father &amp; I fixed up sheep.  Put 5 horses in Castle Rock. 
 Thursday 2 March 1916 Dick &amp; I plowing till dinner time. Then carrier came for wheat.  Father &amp; Geoff mustered Waterfall. F. Warner came down. Put 5 horses over in Lease.
