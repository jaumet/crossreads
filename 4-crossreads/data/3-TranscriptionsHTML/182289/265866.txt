<p>15.4.18<br/>Dispositions of guns sent to 37th &amp; 38th Batt.  Rear Hdqrs. moved to Franvillers.<br/>The following targets were engaged throughout the night &amp; morning, 1200 rounds being expended:-<br/>(1)  Battery Positions in K.7.b.  <br/>(2)  Street, Ville-Sur-Ancre E.25.d.30.45.  <br/>(3)  Sunken Road K.1.b.05.40 &amp; d.  <br/>(4)  Cemetery, Ville-Sur-Ancre K.1.a.90.70.  <br/>(5)  Trenches K.7.b.10.75.  <br/>(6)  Cross Road at J.1.b.05.40.<br/>Enemy Artillery at about 7.45 p.m. shelled the village of Treux and Marrett Wood for about 6 minutes, dropping about 30, 77 mm. &amp; 10.5.  These salvos were repeated again at 8.30, 9.30 &amp; 10.15.  Cutting in front of Marrett Wood was also shelled.  Enemy again opened at 3.30 a.m. and 4.15 a.m.  Enemy M.G. below normal.  Aircraft, Nil.</p>
