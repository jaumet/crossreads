<p>26 Dec 16<br/>Anchored at Rosyth. We had a concert tonight among the ships company.</p>
<p>27 Dec 16<br/>Got under weigh at 1.30 pm &amp; proceeded to sea. Turned round &amp; returned to Rosyth in First watch &amp; coaled ship.</p>
<p>28 Dec 16<br/>Anchored at Rosyth</p>
<p>29 Dec 16<br/>Got under weigh at 3.30 pm &amp; proceeded to sea in company with HMAS Sydney &amp; between 10 &amp; 20 Destroyers.</p>
<p>30 Dec 16<br/>Patrolling in North Sea</p>
<p>31 Dec 16<br/>[another year of war over]<br/>Returned with Sydney &amp; Destroyers &amp; anchored at Rosyth. Coaled &amp; oiled ship.</p>
