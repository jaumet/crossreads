Tuesday 9/4/18. More enemy shelling&nbsp; 2 Coys digging support Line- wrote Doff.
Wednesday 10/4/18&nbsp; Quiet Day&nbsp; had meeting of Coy [Cdrs?]&nbsp; more digging at night
Thursday 11/4/18. Taking over tonight from Currie- very cold day
Friday 12/4/18 Miserable day went up to posts at night&nbsp; Good deal of M.G. Fire.
Saturday 13/4/18 Men working well&nbsp; weather better- visited posts.
Sunday 14/4/18&nbsp; Attempted raid but failed&nbsp; one casualty&nbsp; enemy very alert.
