
<p>357</p>
<p>MAY 1917</p>
<p>3 THURSDAY</p>
<p>Beautiful &amp; sunny. Left Hazebruck</p>
<p>about 7 AM &amp; came through</p>
<p>Baileull &amp; Steenwerck to our</p>
<p>waggon lines near Steenwerck</p>
<p>They are good stables but rotten</p>
<p>billets. I took the gun up to</p>
<p>our new position halfway</p>
<p>between Fleurbaix &amp; Chappelle</p>
<p>Armenti&egrave;res. Its not a bad</p>
<p>posie but might be better.</p>
