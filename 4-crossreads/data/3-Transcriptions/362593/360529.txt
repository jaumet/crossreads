<p>Tuesday 19 March 1918<br/>Events for this date written on last page.</p>
<p>Wednesday 20 March 1918<br/>Oliver still missing.<br/>Recco &ndash; Photos, took 44 plates, took photo of gun positions reported by me a week ago.<br/>Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 2 h. 40 m.<br/>Photos were successful.</p>
