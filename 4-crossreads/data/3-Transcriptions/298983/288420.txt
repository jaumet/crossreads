<p>Wensday 15th May<br/>Saw dentist &amp; had a tooth stopped</p>
<p>Thursday 16th May<br/>Moved from Hut 34 No 1 Co Camp 5 to No 5 Co, Hut 28 Camp, only a few 100 yds</p>
<p>Friday 17th may<br/>Am down for Massage &amp; R C treatment (exercising the affected tissues) But was sent to the dentist waited there until he was knocking off &amp; as he had not got around to me got nothing done. It is an easy</p>
