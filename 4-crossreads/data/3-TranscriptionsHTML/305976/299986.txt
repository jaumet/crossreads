<p>busy. Good grazing for horses &amp; just in this spot Partridges, quails &amp; wild flowers plentiful. Tribesmen here strip wounded &amp; dead. Saw cases my own eyes Jacko retiring.<br/>Wednesday March 27<br/>Fine warm day. It is said White man cannot live in this valley in summer time owing to heat &amp; valley being 1200 ft below Sea level. Jordan River flows from Sea of Gallilee to Dead Sea. Have seen Dead Sea. Water so salt that one cant sink in it. Bringing in Wounded. Moved to another position on Main road at dusk and camped at place called Shunet Nimron</p>
