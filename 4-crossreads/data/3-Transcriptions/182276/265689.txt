<p>9th M.G. Coy No 14<br/>Right Sector:- No 6 fired from I 10 b 26/11 on defensive works at I 18 b 3/3 from 10 &ndash; 11.45 pm.<br/>Hostile bombardment during afternoon near No 5 night firing position, and No 1 gun.<br/>Left Sector: No 7 fired from I 4 d 9/9 to I 12 a 4/9. trenches &amp; buildings round RETALIATION FARM. Unobserved. fired from 7 &ndash; 10 pm.<br/>Light good- Wind favourable to enemy</p>
