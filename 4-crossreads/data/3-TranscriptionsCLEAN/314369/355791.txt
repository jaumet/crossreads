
2 March 1915Tuesday
Finish the work at 11 a.mThe mate personally thanked us for our trouble. We get 2 or 3 bottles of whiskey for the men from the officers.We get a sight of the south coast of Java about 2 pm
1915 March 3Wednesday
Continue along the Java coast all day. Surprised at the height of some mountains which seem to rise sheer up from the waterMuch hotter the wind having dropped to a light breeze.
