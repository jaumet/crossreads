A.I.F. Desert Mounted Corps HeadquartersPalestineJanuary 29/18
My dear Judge FergusonI am ashamed not to have written: we had so much kindness from you & Mrs. Ferguson & have lasting memories of the hours we spent with you.
I was not, when the test came, strong enough in my desire to be a combatent to resist the argument put to me as soon as I reached England that I would be better employed at my own work. And so altho' at the war I never have the satisfaction of feeling in it.
Before coming here a few months ago to act as official historian & precisely perhaps as official correspondent & also to organise records so that Australia will get the best of the lessons of the campaign, I spent some time in France.
[The following text is written along the left-hand margin]I saw a lot of Brissenden in France: usually on a light racing bike with very low handles & a very high seat. Hair clipped to the bone, big hat, shorts & [indecipherable]. Head down, tail up, plugging along like a pugilistic criminal on a racing track.
