<p>This afternoon all the Coy. N.C.O's were called up to enquire into the conduct of a certain working party who were detailed off to earth up a communication trench, the engineers complained about the men not working on the job, my diary came in handy for we could easily cast back &amp; see who it was that was in charge, it turned out to be Corp. Suiter &amp; Andersen, &amp; they have to make out a report.<br/>Our band was out this afternoon with B. Coy.<br/>There was a full muster parade of the Coy. this morning, the bombers &amp; stretchers were out with them<br/>A good few howitzers &amp; field guns are passing here today they have been relieved &amp; like us are out for a bit of  spell, &amp; like us, the spell</p>
