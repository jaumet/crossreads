<p>Lying around. Right opposite is the church where the Smith daughter used to sing. He and his family are supposed to be buried in the grave yard here.<br/>We went in and had a good look at the church coming out to have afternoon tea in a small building adjoining the church.<br/>Here we were served by kind ladies, with tea, cakes and scones and on asking the price were told it was a free buffet. We came back by Durington attending service at the Methodist church there.</p>
