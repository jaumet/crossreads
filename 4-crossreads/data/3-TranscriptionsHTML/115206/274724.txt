<p>June 1918<br/>Monday 3</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather delighful. I notice one R.H.A. Battery has pulled out on to the Footer Ground. Perhaps the gas last night was too much for them. Fritz is well down the Marne once again , - pas bien neuvill, Streaked into Bussy three times.<br/>Met Jack Harvey and spent a very salubrious evening. Had a glorious old gossip about things in general. he looks ever so fit. Spent a very quiet anniversary of departure from Australia for second time. Three years today, gee whiz.</p>
