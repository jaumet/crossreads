<p>1917 MAY</p>
<p>14 MONDAY</p>
<p>Raining lightly most of the day.</p>
<p>took horses to the dip between</p>
<p>Steenwerck &amp; Bailleul in</p>
<p>morning. I saw young [Seray?]</p>
<p>Kinross there &amp; he is in good</p>
<p>form. Got word we were leaving</p>
<p>the waggon lines for open</p>
<p>lines in the morning &amp; got</p>
<p>everything ready to get away</p>
<p>early in morning.</p>
