<p>10/9/17<br/>We travelled on the Great Western to Fishguard from Paddington via Newport and Cardiff.</p>
<p>Went by boat to [Rosslare?] about 3 &frac12; hrs. and took the train again to Mallow changed trains at Mallow and arrived in Killarney about noon of the 11th.</p>
<p>Done a trip across the lake to Innisfallen Island and viewed some very romantic places.</p>
<p>12/9/17<br/>Done a big motor trip of about 120 miles around</p>
