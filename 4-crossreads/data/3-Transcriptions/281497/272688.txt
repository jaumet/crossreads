<p>he also said that our artillery knock out on an average 10 guns a day, &amp; he remarked how absolute was our mastery of the air &amp; the ease with which our boys beat back a heavy German counter attack on Pozeries &amp; he said he was fully convinced now that we have tried ourselves against the German that one Australian was worth 12 Germans once we get to close quarters, &amp; I think he is right, for our scraps have proved that fully, all this of course coming from a General puts the boy's in great heart &amp; even if he is kidding it has a good effect.</p>
