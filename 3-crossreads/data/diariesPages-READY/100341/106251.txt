Dec. 2nd Thursday
Glorious day. Tinned meat vegetable ration issued just lovely cold. Rice tea breakfast, Tea evening. Letter writing improving bunk afternoon. Bomb burst within 5 ft. of 3 of us morning fortunately no injuries. No. 2 Northern.
Dec. 3rd Friday
Lovely day, mild night, rations still very low,  water.
Dec. 4th Sat.
Day fine, evening not too cold. Line quiet.
Dec. 5th Sunday
Weather continues mild.
Dec. 6th Monday
Weather very nice. Mail in. Parcel from Eileen. Day off duty
