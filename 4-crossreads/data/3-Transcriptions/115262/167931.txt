<p>Wednesday 13 June 1917.</p>
<p>Sea a lot calmer this morning and we will soon be in the Gulf of Aden.</p>
<p>Two boats&nbsp;&ndash; 1 tramp &amp; 1 liner passed us going in direction of Colombo at 6.30 a.m. 2 sharks 12 feet long following boat with a dozen or so smaller ones. Several whales seen some distance away at 9 a.m. One came up on surface &amp; was plainly seen. All were spouting. Flying fish were being chased by (&quot;Beeneta&quot;)&nbsp;<s>(Dolfins)</s>&nbsp;fish [Bonita]. These (Beeneta)&nbsp;<s>(Dolfins)</s>&nbsp;are as long as 5 feet and of a bluish green color. They can swim very fast.</p>
<p>At 6.30 pm a large shoal of porpoises was seen close to boat.&nbsp;<s>In early hours of tomorrow morning we pass Aden.</s>&nbsp;2 boxing contests this afternoon. Heat very great &amp; atmosphere moist &amp; very oppressive.</p>
