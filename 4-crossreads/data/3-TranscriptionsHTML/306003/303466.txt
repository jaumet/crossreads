<p>7-8-16<br/>Mon 7th<br/>Rose at 6.30 this morning &amp; had a very quiet day;  The Turks are at Oghratina &amp; our infantry are between Qatia &amp; Oghratina.  a Bde of Mounted Yeomanry went out this morning &amp; we had orders to be ready to go out at seven oclock tonight but it was cancelled at 4 oclock this afternoon;</p>
<p>Last night I was busy finding men to make up strength of Signallers &amp; in ordwers this morning Cabban came out as Sig Cpl of C Sqd, &amp; Bassan as Temp Cpl of A Sqd, &amp; Corliss taken on A Sqd &amp; Wride, Locke &amp; Olson on Head Quarters  these men are only learners &amp; I can see a busy time ahead;  Sgt Major Mills came down today with a Lewis gun &amp; is to instruct men how to work it.</p>
