<p>Stations landed down by hospital train at the Base &amp; was sent to Casino Hospital &ndash; a very pleasant change &amp; one I was not too sick to appreciate but my chief grievance was <strike>too</strike> being <u>too much </u> "tucked in bed" that is 'niceness' seem to count more than 'comfort' &amp; the nurses turn the sheets down so as just to leave your shoulders out &amp; then tuck the close in to the sides so tight that it leaves a fearful draught down your back!</p>
<p>From there to a Convalescent Depot &ndash; nine days there &amp; then marked A. &amp;</p>
