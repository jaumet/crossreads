  
 February, 1916 Tuesday 15 First thing exam in care & cleaning & I have pleasure in stating my class had highest average. Have tea w/- Dad, evening cold & inclined to rain. 
 Wednesday 16 Work as usual & at night I visit the Casino Kursaal.  The "Revue" is absolutely the prettiest thing I have yet seen staged, the music & costumes & really fine & altogether a show worth seeing. Turn in at 1 am
