<p><u>Cardonette. Bussy</u> &amp; <u>Blangy Tronville</u></p>
<p>[4 Th]<br/>Did not get out of bed till 11 am. Harnessed up &amp; moved off at 3 pm. Arr. Bussy 5 pm. 10 pm. ordered to harness up again, moved out  strike>in</strike> 45min. later. Arr Blangy Tronville about 3am.</p>
<p>[5 Fri]<br/>Rev. 6-30am fell in at 7 am. Fixed up lines &amp; waggons. Went for ammunition in the afternoon, got back to lines about 7 pm</p>
<p>[6 Sat]<br/>Grooming &amp; cleaning harness.<br/>Harness &amp; kit always to be together so as to be able to move off at an hours notice</p>
