<p>1915<br/>Sep. 17 Fri.<br/>Swim again this morning before breakfast &ndash; only one in the water.<br/>During the morning went for a row in one of the 2 boats here.<br/>In the afternoon further explored the locality. The old Khedive did things in style alright.<br/>4 new Scouts came to the Hospital to-day &ndash; such fine little chaps &ndash; especially a Second -This chap remembered me as having visited the Troop clubroom on January 16th (see diary for that date).</p>
