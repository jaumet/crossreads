
feet are sore mud to my knees  the camels are knocked up too  crossed a waddy day break 5.AMTuesday March 26.3.18there is camels down all along the road gear lying everywhere  reached a villigge called ------9.AM had Breakfast  thousands of Syrians here all armed to the teeth  moved on again  am still walking through water [indecipherable] I am nearly settled  caught up to the Colum 2.P.M  they are camped  had some dinner and went to sleep woke 6.30 fed up had tea and turned in my feet are nearly cut off  I am covered in mud.Wednesday March 27.3.18turned out 6.A.M fed up Breakfast feel much better now  saddled up and moved nearer to the  (we are short of rations)
