<p>Decembre 14<br/>Coy resting today after tiring day yesterday coming from Hanebeek. Had 10 hours travelling in train to do a distance of about 60 miles &amp; then marched about 7K from Devres.</p>
<p>Decembre 15<br/>Coy engaged on fatigues making themselves comfortable. Went into Samer in the afternoon. A very poor class town. Coy transport arrived after four days travelling.</p>
