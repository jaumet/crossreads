<p>Well, to get on, it was the morning of the 17th March St Patrick's Day, well I shall remember that day, that we received our orders to commence our advance.</p>
<p>We were told what we had to do &amp; where the battery was to cross no mans land, an almost impossible task, to get guns through, but they had to go through somehow.
