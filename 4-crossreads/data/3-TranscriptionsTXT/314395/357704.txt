It is my desire to go through a munition factory while here, and next time on leave, I will endeavour to achieve it.
I am also enclosing a dance programme which I would like you to keep for me.  You know that the light fantastic never held much attraction for me but this time I bent a little, and had a real good night.
The Hun is making good progress in the West, and I am anxiously waiting to see the Allies cut in on his flank to restore our line.  If they don't move quickly, it will throw us back about three more years.
Best wishes fromYour affect. SonGeorge.
