<p>24<br/>for some time sapping ahead of their trenches towards ours forming a new firing line. This was dangerous to us. &amp; we made saps towards theirs &amp; blew up three. We then charged [11 Batt. Supported by fire from Second Light H. &amp; rest of third Brigade. We were successful and killed  60 or more Turks. Our casualties were 11 killed and 74 wounded. Men behaved gallantly &amp; deserve high praise</p>
<p>Aug 6. A great flanking move by us &amp; we left Rest Gully at 7.P.M. on our adventurous exploit</p>
