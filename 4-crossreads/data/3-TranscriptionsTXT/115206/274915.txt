 December  1918. Friday 13 
 Southall and H.S.S. "Karoola" Weather as usual &ndash; dull and miserable. Handed in kit and wrote a few letters. Received an answer pre paid cable from Elsie. I was certainly very pleased to receive same.  Entrained and left S'thall at 1.30pm.Went aboard and was allotted a cot up at the fos'cle head of the old "Karoola". Quite a large number of patients aboard. Left the berth and anchored in mastream off battery.
