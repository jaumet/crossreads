Thursday 8/11/17.
All day long the aerial work of our planes was very exciting, just before lunch a Fritz Machine came over St Jean for Observation purposes, he was sailing around having things all his own way when out from a Cloud came one of ours made straight for Fritz. as our Machine was up above him.
