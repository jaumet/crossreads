<p>6 June 1915<br/>Sunday<br/>1st After Trinity</p>
<p>Church parade Roman Catholics at 7 am   &amp; C of E at 9.30<br/>No work today</p>
<p>1915 June7<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Go to work on a long sap leading to one of our outposts at 8 am   &amp; work till noon<br/>Clean rifles   &amp; equipment in afternoon<br/>Receive a letter from Miss Coulson</p>
