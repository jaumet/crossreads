  
 March, 1916 Tuesday 28 Got up fresh as daiseys, had a wash & breakfast then went up to see dad. After dinner went out w/- an old Pal from Liverpool Harry Grove sent 10 home & had a real good time turned in 11.30 pm. 
 Wednesday 29 Very quiet all day reading & poking about Very hot & sultry after sundown cools off Play cards w/- dad & the boys Write letter & up my Diary Turn in at 11 pm.
