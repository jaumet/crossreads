 August -21 &ndash; 25 
 Aug 21st Left N'mine for Sydney  Exped. Force, had a good reception all along the line in troop train 
 Sat 22nd Arrived  Redfern  Darling Harbor at eleven oclock was met by Mrs Pen, Jessie &amp; Mylie Edwards &amp; family, arrived at Rosebery about sundown 
 Sun 23 Found out at 8 o'clock we had to supply our own cooks, one cook put a knife in his leg, &amp; I volunteered &amp; had breakfast ready by dinner time, it saved cooking dinner 
 Mon 24th Resigned the cooking in favour of better man, &amp; started drill.  Uncle Jack came in &amp; saw me for half an hour &amp; had a job to get out again  no passes were given &amp; some of the lads scaled the fence &amp; went to the city 
 Tue 25th Passed in all accoutrements belonging to the 9th L.H. our horses examined &amp; Idleboy bought &pound; 16-0-0, we received a lecture for [indecipherable], &amp; trampling the caretakers garden
