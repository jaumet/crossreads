(No 3" Divers.)Ministry of Finance,Cairo
Australian Comforts Fund.
17th. July 1915.
Dear Mrs. Read,
At a meeting of the above Committee held on Thursday July 15th. it was proposed and carried that you should be asked to join our Committee and I was delegated to write to you on the subject.
I should be glad to know if you would &ndash; kindly consent to the proposal of the Committee.
Yours sincerely,
James F. S. HopkinsHon: Secretary.
Mrs. Read,c/o Dr. Madden,St. Davids Buildings,Cairo.
