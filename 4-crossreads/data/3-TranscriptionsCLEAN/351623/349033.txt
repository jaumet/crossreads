  
 13 June 18 Anchored at S. Flow. Blowing a gale all day, at its worst about 10 pm. Two observation baloons broke away & have not been seen or heard of since. 
 14 June 18 Still at anchor 
 15 June 18 Went to sea & done 6 inch firing 
 16 June 18 At anchor 
 17 June 18 Done firing in the Flow. 
 18 June 18 Left Scapay Flow to pick up convoy. 
 19 June 18 At sea. Convoy arrived in Bergen Norway safely & we then picked up another to bring back to England. 
 20 June 18 Returning with convoy 
 21 June 18 Dropped convoy at Lerwick Scotland
