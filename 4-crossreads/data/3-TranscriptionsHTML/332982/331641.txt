<p>Pier till 12.50pm and as we sailed at 1pm we had not much time to say good-bye.<br/>After leaving Melb. We got paid for the few days that was due for the time in camp, as we start afresh on board.<br/> Arrived off Queenscliff about 6.30 pm and lay there for about an hour. Paymasters' disembarked and mails sent ashore.<br/>We then sailed through the Heads and about 10pm the lights of Australia disappeared.</p>
