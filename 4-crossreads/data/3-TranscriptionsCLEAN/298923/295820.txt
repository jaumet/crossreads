  
 3 June 1918 Monday (X to X crossed out) Kings Birthday X Dolly sent me your letter Mother dear it is so good the way you never forgot any of us-   I know poor old Laura often wishes she was nearer to you.X Procession in Bethlehem & all the Notables around turn out to hear Band & God save the "King"   I did not go as am on duty.
