<p>July 1917<br/>Thursday 5th<br/>Pay day today so thing were pretty lively this evening    There are no restrictions as to leave here but we are supposed to be in Billets at 9 pm but needless to say very few were in at that time.   I have had more liberty here than at any other time in the army.<br/>Friday 6th<br/>I have satisfied the Sister that I am no nurse so she has put me in the kitchen it is a better job that in the ward.<br/>Saturday 7th.<br/>Fritz paid us another visit this evening &amp; dropped a few bombs in some works outside the town but did very little damage</p>
