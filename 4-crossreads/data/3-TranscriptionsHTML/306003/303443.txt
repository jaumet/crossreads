<p>24-7-16<br/>English Infantry has sprung up near Katib Gannit (Pinnacle) within the last couple of days;  All enemy 'planes are referred to here as Taubes but it is seldom we see a Taube now, most of the 'planes we see now are Albatross 'planes, &amp; all the enemy planes look faster &amp; neater than ours, &amp; their engines have a much nicer hum</p>
<p>25-7-16<br/>4 Turks were captured today &amp; one of the 3rd L.H.R. wounded through the leg;  Left Qatia at 8.30 tonight &amp; arrived back in camp at 11.30</p>
<p>25-7-16<br/>Tue 25th<br/>Frank Hobson turned up here on the 20th with a few other Reinforcements;  Today 23 more reinforcements came on the scene &amp; 17 horses.  Had nothing to do today, but had to stand to our horses once as an enemy 'plane, (Avatic) paid us a visit, a</p>
