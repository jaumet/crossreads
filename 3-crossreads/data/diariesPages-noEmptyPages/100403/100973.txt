March 1919
1st. Beautiful spring day and walks over the fields have been very enjoyable. 
This "Grippe Espagnol" is still rife in Belgium, and is the cause of a heavy mortality in the A.I.F. also.
Sales of Army Horses are going on daily prices realised are enormous, 2600 francs for one old crock, and 1000 francs for a mule.
It is now definite that our draft will not leave until 15th of the month. Traffic has been dislocated by strikes in England. There is also unrest here.
