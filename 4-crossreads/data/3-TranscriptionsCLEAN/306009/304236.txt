  
 1915 Feb. 19 Fri. 850 in hospital. Worked until 4.30 p.m. & then went off to Cairo with S.M. Joyner - had tea & then went to 1st Cairo B-P Scout's clubroom where met several Territorials & a New Zealander (mostly S.M.s} - about 22 boys present - some of their work was very good - must attend some more of their meetings on a Monday, Wednesday or a Friday night - Returned to the camp in a taxi which cost me 10 piastres. Note - My total wealth now amounts to 1 piastre (21/2d).
