<p>April, 1915<br/>Friday 2<br/>Left my horse &amp; returned by Boat with the 4 Infantry Battalion, their Colonel met them on the wharf &amp; told them to pack up as they were leaving tomorrow.<br/>X Good Friday today &ndash; Unfortunately last night there was a riot in Cairo, some troopers tried to burn a house down &amp; would not let fire Brigade work, it ended in the [indecipherable] firing on them &amp; killing 1 &amp; wounding 4 others. X<br/>X not to be printed</p>
