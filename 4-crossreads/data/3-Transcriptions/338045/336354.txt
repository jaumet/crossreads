<p>first view of snow falling as it continues until about 11 am. Some fun in the ward with snowballs. A drizzling cold rain &amp; sleet follows the snow, but having my leave pass determine to go out &amp; set forth at 2 pm well wrapped up. Bus to St Mary Cray, meet my friend Miss Schwerer &amp; go home. Formal introductions &amp; a warm fire &amp; have tea. Some music &amp; yarns in the warm room until 8.30 pm &amp; set out home, arriving late. Very tired &amp; soon in bed.</p>
