<p>[Thursday 14 December 1916]</p>
<p><strike>Today the Infantry are practising their attack on El Arish</strike> Aeroplanes dropping Smoke balls &amp; spotting for the guns   Enemy bombed 2 L H   I rode over &amp; had Tea there they showed me a 20 lb bomb that had not exploded<br/>[illustration ] Vane, Powder Cap &ndash; (Smaller than the Romani ones)<br/>than most  <u>Later</u> 4 of these were dropped on our Brig but did no damage-<br/>I watched with my glasses &amp; altho' the sound is very clear the bomb is not seen till it bursts- before this happens the horses &amp; men have scattered.</p>
