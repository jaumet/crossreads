 From Capt. Jack  Frost  Adams 
 1/2/19&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Military [indecipherable] &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Shepard's Bush  
 Dear Brew I was very pleased to get a few words from you &amp; to know there are still a few bricks of the old monument left. Thanks very much old boy for your kind offer &amp; I certainly will give you a look up when I get out there. Well things are going fairly well with me the last few days I have been getting along on crutches &amp; I'm feeling much fitter. I have to get another operation later on, one thing its only a minor one. The damned thing that did give me trouble was three abcesses
