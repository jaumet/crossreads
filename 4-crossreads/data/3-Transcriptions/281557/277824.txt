<p>parade for physical drill 10am boxing finals : Neil won the 8 st 6 lb for our Coy &amp; Madgwick was runner up in another division so our Coy done well : Land sighted at noon &amp;  proved to be Cape Guardafui the most easterly point of Africa. We drew close in &amp; passed into the Gulf of Aden not a mile from shore. It was a splendid sight : Very high mountains running right to the water's edge &amp; sheer cliffs of rather a white looking stone but the country seems rather on the barren</p>
