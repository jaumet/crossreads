 Fifth Division Quotas arrive from Weymouth.&nbsp; 
 Alexander Watson in camp. 
 More verses and also letters written. 
  May 20th  
 &quot;Rio Padro&quot; - a vessel of 8,000 tons, which will carry 750 men.&nbsp; Farewell letters.&nbsp; Glorious weather.&nbsp; Washing day. 
  May 21st  
 No news of Harry Hawker.&nbsp; The worst is feared.&nbsp; Australia predominates in Sport.
