<p>to join new unit. Sorry to leave boys &amp; if we strike as good crowd in new unit as those we have left we will be lucky. Arrived section 7 pm reported &amp; Joe Fraser returned back to unit with Camels.<br/>Saturday Sept 15<br/>Our new unit resting right on beach. Met Percy Knight. Were allotted our horses &amp; gave them a swim.<br/>Sunday Sept 16<br/>Rumoured two more divisions arrived from Salonica.<br/>Monday Sept 17<br/>New Camp situated on beach some 2 miles below Marakat with the Blue Mediterranian to bathe in. Swim Horses every morning while Anzac Division resting.</p>
