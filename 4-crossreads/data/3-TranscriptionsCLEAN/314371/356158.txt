  
 Friday, May 24th 
 June 7th Sighted land 11 a.m.  Picked up Fremantle Pilot at 1 p.m., berthed about 2 p.m. After tea Perth Ladies came on board w/- fruit & cake for us & we had a great feed. Pulled out about 9 p.m., sea very rough. 
 June 13th Arrived Port Melbourne about midnight.  Disembarked from "Tofua" & reembarked on "Suevic" same morning, 14th June.  Sailed at noon.  Beautiful weather. 
 June 15th Weather continues "Bon". We are getting quite excited.
