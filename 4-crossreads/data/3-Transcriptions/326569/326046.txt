<p>Friday, May 4th<br/>General clean out in tents this morning &ndash; no bombing class on account of medical inspection.  Afternoon went route march about one mile.  After tea went into Ludgershall for laundry.</p>
<p>Saturday, 5/5/17<br/>Reveille 6 a.m. &amp; &frac34; hour physical exercise.  Medical inspection during morning.  After dinner walked to Tidworth &amp; met Cale &amp; Turner &amp; several of the 9/53 who have been transferred to the Veterinary Corps &amp; are camped at Park House.  Had tea with Cale.
