 June 1918 Wednesday 19 
 Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles Weather wet, heavy rain throughout day. Gun came across in evening, we went for a long stroll and managed to crib some eggs, quite a very pleasant supper. Percy Grayling also visited canteen and purchased syrup Quaker oats, milk and one or two other necessities. Weather still raining . Expect to leave at any time now. Officers of the 42nd Battery were across here this morning.
