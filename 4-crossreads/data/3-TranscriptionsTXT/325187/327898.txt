 [Sunday after Ascension 4 June 1916] 
 Posted Notes today.  As Adjutant gone away sick have taken on Adjutant's job for the Reg. again What I would like now is 3 months leave &amp; go home but the War does not seem to change things much . [The rest of the page is crossed out but is totally illegible]
