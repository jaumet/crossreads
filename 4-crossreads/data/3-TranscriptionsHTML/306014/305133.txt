Our walk home was made enjoyable by the fact that the sky was clear &amp; the moon was full &amp; shed a clear silver light, &amp; the stars twinkled down upon us [indecipherable]in their wonted way. We love the stars because we know that all our loved ones can see them as well as we can &amp; we often whisper quiet secrets to them to carry across the seas for us.</p>
<p>Of course our absence from the isolation hut had been noticed by "the heads" but nothing came of it except a threat for the future.</p>
<p>April 8</p>
<p>Still "isolated". Exercise etc. I read. Emerson's essays on "History" &amp; on "Love"; the latter is particularly inspiring and the former is a fine appeal for a practical, personal interpretation of historic events &amp; characters.</p>
