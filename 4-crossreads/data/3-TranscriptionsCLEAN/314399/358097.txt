
Thursday 10 April 1919Orderly officer today. Did very little. Rained all day. Bill got word of going to Blighty for non military employment.
Friday 11 April 1919Pass came thro & Bill managed to get a duty pass for Paris. Rushed around a bit & we managed to catch the 5 pm train. Got to Paris & we went to the Aussie Y.M. & got a room (Hotel [indecipherable]). Took Bill & Sgt. Kerr to the Boulevard for a walk & showed them a few things.
