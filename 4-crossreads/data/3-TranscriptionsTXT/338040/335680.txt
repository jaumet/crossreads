
June 4thSabbath Mass 7.30am (Bac-St- Maur)News of Naval Engagement. Reported German victory. Easy day.June 5thBritish Victory apparent- weather still wet and miserable. Take part in Concert at Y.M.C.A.- "Australian Corroboree".June 6thPay day. We leave Ft. Rompu for Advanced Dressing Station at Bois Grenier- safe arrival- detailed to Dressing Rooms. (Fierce prolonged bombardment of our guns)
