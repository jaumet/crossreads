<p>May 1918<br/>Wednesday 15</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daours<br/>Weather &ndash; very hot, and muggy, evening fairly mild. Wrote one or two letters. Acted as guide to several Tommies. <br/>2 98 DAC changed places at 6 pm with the 13th FAB.<br/>Paid 60 francs and had a run across to Villers Bretonneaux.<br/>At 9pm Fritz visited Amiens, Querrie and Daours. Had a swim during afternoon. A 3rd Division chap was drowned in the big pool.</p>
