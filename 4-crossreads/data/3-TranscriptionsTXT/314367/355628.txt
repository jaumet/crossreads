 The seining &amp; outing has done me a lot of good &amp; Richard is his self once &amp; again &amp; played a fair game of bridge last eveng &amp; smoked cigarettes &amp; drank &ndash; Lemon squash &amp; soda &ndash; By the way Lemon squash is in bottles 1/8 each (about). We pour a lot &amp; fill up with soda &amp; its good, cheap &amp; quite the sound drink &ndash; never exceed in alcohol &ndash; if you feel anyway heady &ndash; the signal is &ndash; Stand by &ndash; The censoring of letters is going on &ndash; I've written a nice little account of "An outbreak of Ptomaine poisoning on a man of war" but its publication will be delayed because I read it over to Fgd &amp; he said &ndash; Better wait &ndash; 'There is a little criticism in it &amp; the naval board might have a pang' &ndash; Its all right but it must wait &ndash; We go out at 6 pm &amp; all is in readiness &ndash; I'll wind up with a Bluejacket's wit when the storm opened up yesterday &ndash; "Quartermaster &ndash; send a couple of hands aloft &amp; have that noise stopped"
