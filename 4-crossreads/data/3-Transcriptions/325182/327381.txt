<p>The enemy objective is Amiens.  He is held fast in front of Arras.  He is held all along the front according to latest reports.</p>
<p>5th<br/>We left Acheux by lorries after a long wait and reached Aubigny about 1 p.m. passing through <strike>An</strike> Amiens.</p>
<p>6<br/>We managed to get a sleep in after all last night!  I went out to front line Bn. Hqrs. this morning with two of my sigs. and a runner to have a look round.  On returning I was informed that we were to move up into supports at once behind right Bn.</p>
