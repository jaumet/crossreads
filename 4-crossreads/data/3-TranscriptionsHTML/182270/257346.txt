<p>MONDAY, FEBRUARY 3, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Buzzed round Town in</p>
<p>the morning. Got Alan a passage</p>
<p>home ect. Met Major Glover in</p>
<p>the afternoon &amp; was round</p>
<p>with him. Went to the pantomime</p>

<p>much in it.</p>
