<p>5 P.P. turned in 8 P.M.</p>
<p>Thursday Aug. 2.8.17<br/>turned out 6 A.M. am feeling all right had Bath and got Breakfast.  rested till 12.30 had dinner rested till 5 P.M. had tea turned in 8 P.M.</p>
<p>Friday Aug. 3.8.17<br/>turned out 5.30 had a Bath and Breakfast am feeling much Better now.  rested all day very hot.  there is sports here to day had a look at the swimming and camel racing not much to look at had tea turned in 9 P.M.</p>
<p>Saturday Aug. 4.8.17<br/>turned out 5.30 had a Bath and had Breakfast rested all day everyone gone to the sports to ismalia very hot day am feeling much</p>
