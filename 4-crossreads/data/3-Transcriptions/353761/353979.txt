<p>(48)<br/>Can you march away and leave the place where the Jesus and the Mary are without a thought?<br/>Nay, can you do so, without a host of thoughts?<br/>Thoughts which come to question, and disturb, and haunt.<br/>That place you have just now seen was once a home<br/>Home, Ah what does that word mean.<br/>It means all that which men may write of it, and much more which none may write nor tell.<br/>It means the love and joy, laughter and music, fear and care, faith and duty, ambition, regret, gain loss and grief: and sorrow and despair, and the loyalty and honour of a little world.</p>
