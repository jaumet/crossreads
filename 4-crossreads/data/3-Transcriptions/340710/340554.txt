<p>Somme<br/>Aug 1916.</p>
<p>Mein Leibe Frau,<br/>At last the English we on the run have but they this way   <strike>are</strike>  running are but our Hindenburg a great general is and makes us faster run and so we soon shall tire the enemy out with exercise much and "Der Tag" will come be.  You say a lot of prisoners we have lost but I can that explain.  The great Hindenburg lets the English many prisoners take,   <strike>and</strike> so that they will soon them out of house and house eat and then the Great War shall we win.  The Australians very keen on marriage are and wish to be prisoner taken so that</p>
