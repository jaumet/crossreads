<p>the shattered village of Fremincourt, some three miles from Bapaume on the "Cambria" road.</p>
<p>Next day we pull our guns into action, about two miles further along this road, but we get heavily shelled &amp; have to retire in double quick time, getting out of it with no causalities &amp; get into action some thousand yards further back. as soon as the guns are unhooked we get back with the horses under cover &amp; to a flank &amp; await orders. the guns are soon in action, firing for their lifes. after about an hours solid bombardment</p>
