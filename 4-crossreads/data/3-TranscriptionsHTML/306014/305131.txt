<p>Apl 6</p>
<p>"Good Friday". Still "isolated". We marched to Shrewton for exercise. After tea the boys got the guard to act as messengers to the canteen &amp; between themselves they got fairly well "boozed" &amp; by bed-time the hut was rather rowdy, but most of the boys only get merry &amp; joyful when drunk &amp; there is really nothing disagreeable about such festivities. It is just as well that such is the case for we have a couple or more such jollities every week.</p>
<p>April 7</p>
<p>Alan &amp; I "scaled" out this afternoon to the town of Devizes which is about 13 miles from Camp. We walked both ways and <strike>took the</strike>passed over part of the most desolate of the all times notorious Salisbury Plains.</p>
