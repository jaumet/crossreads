
<p>372</p>
<p>1917 MAY</p>
<p>18 FRIDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; fairly warm. Out exercising</p>
<p>horses in morning &amp; cleaning</p>
<p>harness in afternoon Fritz</p>
<p>sent a few big shells (</p>
<p>probably 10 inch) very near</p>
<p>our waggon lines &amp; in</p>
<p>evening he came over &amp;</p>
<p>tried to get the ballon</p>
<p>practically over our heads</p>
<p>but he missed it first</p>
<p>go &amp; did not try again. I got</p>
<p>a letter from mother.</p>
<p>We had a turn out about</p>
<p>1 AM &amp; did not get back till</p>
<p>about six oclock.</p>
