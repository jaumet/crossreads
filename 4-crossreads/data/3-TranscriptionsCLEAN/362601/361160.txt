  
 16 February, Sunday 
 W. Very Rainy 
 Get my work absolutely square. Go to Y.M.C.A. & go straight up to D.M. I witness Pictures of D.A.M. on the Beach in Costume & Pictures of Doris. I am very dull & dopey. I speak behind fellows Backs. Dicky T's is very exuberant & plays up generally. 
 Siddie comes Home at 11 pm. 
 17 February, Monday 
 W. Sgts mess meeting which is attended by C.O. Write by Mess Fire & go down to Dick's flat to Play Cards. 
 Got some at 9 pm 
 Wrote M.R.L., Mrs Clarke
