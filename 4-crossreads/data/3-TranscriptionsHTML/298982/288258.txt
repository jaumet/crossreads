<p>squeak. Then 10 of us under Sgt Burbury went down to beach for bathe &amp; to fill water bottles. Shrapnel came along close when half way &amp; we ran for it. Two exploded close to us, one drove the sand into my right hand the next sent a piece across the sole of my boot below the instep when on the trot. The sea was sprayed in front of us while undressing but we had our dip &amp; it was refreshing The first wash I've had since landing.</p>
