  
 Dad moves His possie. He sleeps so well that He does not hear S & I come in (because we don't come Home at all). Dick & I go to Zoo & Bot. Gardens. Meet Harry & Mrs Hey. I go to P's with two Nurses after meeting Mr Lord in Macquarie St. I play piano until Girls go to sleep. 
 26 October, Sunday 
 Have stayed at P's. Get up awaked by Jackass & Kid. Go walk Brekkie etc. 45 minutes along the Forest Rd, sweet smelling rose to Beulah. Dinner & catch 2.30. I come Home to bed. Mum & I go to Ellis' place after tea. Go down to Band. Last tram Home.
