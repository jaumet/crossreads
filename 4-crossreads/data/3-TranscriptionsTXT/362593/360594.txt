 Friday 30 August 1918 
 Saturday 31 August 1918 Feel better. H.A.P. over drome.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 3 h. 5 m. Got 2 Hun two-seaters personally. Both fell to pieces in the air.  Very pleased, 1 in our lines, 1 in Enemy lines. McGuinness did some great piloting enabling me to shoot down the 2 Huns.
