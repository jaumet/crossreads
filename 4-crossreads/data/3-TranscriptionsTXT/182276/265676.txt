 9th M.G. Coy 5-12.16 Brigade Major 9th Inf Bdge 
 I Re form 42/2 dated 4-12.16. The instructions contained in Brigade memo of 30th Nov were read and initialled by the Section Officers of this Company. Further instruction have been forwarded to the Officer concerned. II The officer in charge Left Sector.- Lieut Graham- explained to me last night when I visited him that the reason his report was late in reaching me was that his runner lost his way. This report reached me at 3.pm yesterday. 
 B.J Carey Capt 9th M.G. Coy.
