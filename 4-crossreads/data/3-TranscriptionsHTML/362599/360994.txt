<p>F &amp; muddy [25/2/17 Sun] E. D &amp; I go to L. Hill to Billy. Meet Martin Wright I cook on Tea in Durrington. We have a bonzer walk through the mud. Ellis rouses at the mud.</p>
<p>F [26/2/17 Mon] Wrote P.C. to Alice Meth.<br/>Dave &amp; I go to Albany Wand's Show</p>
<p>F [27/2/17 Tues] Recd. letter from Bess. I go to Fighledean with Kingy. Round through Durrington Home. Meet Marjory, We also fall in the ditches</p>
<p>F [28/2/17 Wed] Recd. letter from Bess &amp; Nette<br/>Dream that Q.O.M. marries Mr Reid.<br/>Very Busy but no news.</p>
<p>F [1/3/17 Thu] I close canteen Two<br/>Recd. letters from Auntie. M.R.L.  Rained during the night &amp; foggy till 10 am. Recd. letter from Bess. Mem. H.Hey &amp; Cyril. I go &amp; watch our Footy Team &amp; spend the evening at Durrington with Martin Wellington &amp; Marjory</p>
<p>F [2/3/17 Fri] Recd. letters from Home (7) Bess. Hilda. Dot. Queen. Ellis goes to Grimsby. Gen. Howse visits Hosp &amp; combing out Starts. Receive news that I have been writing to an unknown Girl.</p>
<p>F [3/3/17 Sat] Very Busy. I go to see "Lion &amp; the Mouse" with Dave &amp; the Boys. Welshy from Milson's Is. came from Brighton on Escort Duty.</p>
<p>Wrote to Jennie. Roy. White &amp; M.R.L.</p>
