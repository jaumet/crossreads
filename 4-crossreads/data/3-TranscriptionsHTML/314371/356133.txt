<p>About 7 o'clock I started for the clearing Stn.<br/>Went from there by motor to Bapaume where we spent the night.</p>
<p>Wed. 16th<br/>At 9 a.m. we were put aboard the hosp. train, made a very slow passage to Boulogne where we were carried by motor to No. 3 Canadian Gen. Hosp.</p>
<p>Thur. 17th<br/>After a good sleep woke up feeling real good &amp; Hungry.<br/>After breakfast the doctor had a look at us &amp; we had our hurts dressed.<br/>The weather is perishingly cold again.</p>
<p>Fri. 18th<br/>Still resting.  Cut now almost O.K. again.</p>
<p>Sat. 19th<br/>Same again, getting restless &amp; want to get out.</p>
