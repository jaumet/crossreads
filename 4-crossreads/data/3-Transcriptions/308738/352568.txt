<p>Dec. 14 Tuesday 1915<br/>In bed all day<br/>A few of our room mates left today. We get used to one another, &amp; then the parting comes. Oh well it is all meeting &amp; parting in this World.</p>
<p>Dec. 15 Wednesday 1915<br/>In bed all day<br/>Feeling a bit better &ndash; Dr still thinks I will not be able to do any more active work, but it is wonderful what a man can do, if he tries in this World</p>
<p>Dec. 16 Thursday 1915<br/>In bed all day<br/>A big theatre party today went to see a beautiful play in Cairo of course I missed it as the Sisters refused to let me go. Well they know best.</p>
<p>Dec. 17 Friday 1915<br/>In bed all day<br/>Anniversary of my departure from Sydney. The year has been the most eventful of my life. I look back with gratitude for much that has come my way.</p>
