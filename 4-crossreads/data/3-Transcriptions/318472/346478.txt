<p>10 September 1918<br/>Tuesday<br/>Left Port about 7.30 A.M. "Barambali D37" went out in Front of us carrying troops apparently for England. A beautiful day &amp; nice calm sea. Everything appears to be going well on the Western Front the Allies advancing everywhere.</p>
<p>1918 September 11<br/>Wednesday<br/>Making slow progress in beautiful weather &amp; a nice sea. Passed Albany shortly after lunch time. We are running fairly close to the coast &amp; can see it as far as the eye can reach. Nothing of importance happening on board.</p>
