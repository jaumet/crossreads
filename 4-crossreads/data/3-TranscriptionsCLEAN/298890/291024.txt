  
 25 Sunday Grey day & showers in the afternoon.  Raining as we went to bed.  Did not go out at all.  Very debilitated. 
 26 Monday Wrote & pottered about Fairfield & walked into Hitchin.  Nice day. 
 27 Tuesday Matron Nye & I came up to London in the morning.  I went to Threadneedle St. to be passed by Merchant Taylors for Convalescent Home then to 22 Harley [Road, South Hampstead]. Dull day 
 28 Wednesday Dull dreary day.  Busied myself writing letters etc.  Struggled to put down depression.
