We came across a damaged well here, amid a mass of debris, down which a plough was suspended by its handle;  apt symbol of the invaders 'Kultur'.   (at Contalmaison)</p>
<p>We also came across a small Church-yard with headstones etc shattered and in ruins.   At the cross-roads about four shells had hit a large cross there and the figure of Christ had been knocked off &amp; was half-buried in the debris.</p>
