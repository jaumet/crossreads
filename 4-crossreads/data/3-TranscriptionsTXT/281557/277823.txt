 Sunday Jan 9th A very calm day : fine : Revellie 6 am : dressed my wrist : church 10 am :&amp; very nice. The music was a piano, violin, hobo, cornet &amp; bassoon &amp; Lieut Boyd sang a solo in his usual fine style. Bible class 1.15 pm &amp; just on forty present. No physical as the officers wanted the boat deck after tea. Sacred sing song after tea slept on deck : no land or ships sighted.
