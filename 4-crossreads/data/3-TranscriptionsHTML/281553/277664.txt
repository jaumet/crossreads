<p>rain up to 5 p.m.  Turks machine guns traversing our parapets for &frac12; hour on &amp; off, otherwise quiet.  Conditions to return to normal at midnight.  Big demonstration expected by us to follow with mines etc.  Bread, Cheese, Jam, Rice &amp; Meat issued.</p>
<p>Nov. 28 Sunday<br/>Snow followed Saturday afternoon storm from about 10-30 p.m., still snowing this morning, everything covered thickly, bitterly cold, slight fall even in evening.</p>
<p>Nov. 29 Monday<br/>Snowfall ceased but threatening to return, everything frozen still &amp; too cold to thaw.  Registered 22&deg; below freezing point.  Night clear &amp; crisp and <strike>biting</strike> bighting.</p>
