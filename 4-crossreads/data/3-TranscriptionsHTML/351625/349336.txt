<p>Not far away is a bosca deep canal where we have a good swim whenever we feel inclined.  You were asking how I got on for socks, well I always seem to have enough, sometimes more than I want, then I give some away, there is always someone who is glad of them.  I always wash every chance I get thus I always have a clean pair to put on every few days.  One day we took up a possition in a German dugout which had been a Q.M.
