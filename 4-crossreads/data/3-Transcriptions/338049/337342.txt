<p>Sunday 12 May<br/>Up &amp; cleaned for presentation decoration parade. 8.30 fell in &amp; finally in order on Aerodrome ground.</p>
<p>General Birdwood <strike>arranged</strike> arrived &amp; church parade started. Medals presented &amp; then ribbons. March past &amp; rain hurried end. Afternoon at ease.</p>
<p>Evening Mod bad. Had to keep her on move as she could not keep still. Vets paid 2 visits. Getting worse. Finally shot about 10 p.m. after tiring evening.</p>
