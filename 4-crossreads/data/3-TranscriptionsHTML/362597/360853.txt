<p>We were going to throw "Nikko Maru" &ndash; you know our captain that is no good &ndash; into the bath but he hid himself. We could not find Dave Williams for a long time. At last Horrie Norton found him in a dark corner downstairs &amp; when Horrie spoke to him Dave said "Are you looking for me". They gave him a minute to get undressed &amp; carried him right along the deck. I sneaked away &amp; got my swimming costume on. So ended "a perfect day".</p>
<p>We are getting into warm weather now but we still get a cool breeze.</p>
