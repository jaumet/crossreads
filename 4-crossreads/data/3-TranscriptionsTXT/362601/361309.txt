 8 December, Monday 
 Feel well &amp; fit up after so many years. Mrs Doyle to visit in Home. Write after 9 pm to Auntie. 
 Wrote M &amp; Auntie Annie. 
 9 December, Tuesday 
 Pick Gum leaves &amp; post letter to Auntie. E on boat. We go to A[nthony]. H[orderns]. &amp; Sons see Brultes, syce &amp; beetles &amp; butterflies. 
 Home &amp; chatter. 
 Recd letter from MRL. 
 Wrote until bed time. 
 Wrote (M &amp; Aunt Annie)
