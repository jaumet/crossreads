<p>POSTCARD  18/1/17</p>
<p>Am writing to acknowledge receipt of parcel sent through your Depot to me containing food stuffs, milk, cheese,  asparagies etc, which reached me safely and in good condition &ndash; I would also like to take the opportunity ---<br/>You all that is good in this New Year which I hope will see us home again</p>
<p>Yours sincerely<br/>Cpl A Metcalfe  1030<br/>B Squadron<br/>1st Regiment 1st A.L.H.Brigade<br/>Egypt</p>
