<p>December  1918.<br/>Monday 23</p>
<p>At Sea. H.S."Karoola."<br/>Weather wet and drizzly with very heavy rain at intervals. At lunch time we had a little matter of 104 miles to do before the port closed for the night. Our trip today was 327 miles.<br/>At 8.30 pm we were still outside Port Said perhaps tomorrow we shall be inside when we awake.</p>
