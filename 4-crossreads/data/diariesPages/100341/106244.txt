Nov. 12 Friday
Cloudy all morning, heavy showers toward midday, afternoon clear sky with bright sun moonlight night. Camping in Gullie alongside 5th F. Ambulance for week's rest.
Nov. 13th Saturday
Sky dull keen air early. Resting taking things easy. Went down to Y.M.C.A. Canteen roung beach during afternoon.
