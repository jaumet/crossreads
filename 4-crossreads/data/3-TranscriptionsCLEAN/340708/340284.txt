
September 1917Monday 24thLeft Boulounge at 6.30 pm had good trip across arriving at Folkstone at 8 pm   an air raid on so all lights were out.   Went to London & stopped at Peel House for the nightTuesday 25Had a tour around London in a Taxi this morning & called at the Bank for my money   Left Paddington Station at 1 pm & arrived at Torquay at 6 pmWednesday 25thThis is a very decent camp & I think we will have a good time   I wont get my leave for a day or so as I have to get my teeth fixed upThursday 26thHad look round the town it is a folly fine place & an ideal health resort & there are some fine girls here
