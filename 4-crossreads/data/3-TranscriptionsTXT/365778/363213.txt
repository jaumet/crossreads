 Saturday 18/8/17 Saw men working, during afternoon holiday went for a ride. Sunday 19/8/17 Church Parade &ndash; rode to 7th Canadian Stationery Hospital (re man killed) near St Omer &ndash; letter from Doff. Monday 20/8/17 Training as usual &ndash; saw Wisdom &ndash; not feeling too good. Tuesday 21/8/17 Training &ndash; lecture to whole Battalion on Gas &ndash; had great joke let down Doctors tent. Wednesday 22/8/17 Battalion went to Badringhem for bombing and organised Trench Clearing. Lee, new B.M. broke his collarbone &ndash; meeting of C.O.s.
