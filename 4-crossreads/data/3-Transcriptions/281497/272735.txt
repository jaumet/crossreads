<p>with the railway running at the foot, &amp; the great military camps &amp; Hospitals of Etaps, also the town of the same name, it is principally a fishing place &amp; many curious sights are to be seen when walking through it, on the flats below me a lot of our chaps &amp; some "Scotties" are playing football, &amp; about 100 yards from where I am sitting an elderly Y.M.C.A. man is giving they boys his weekly lecture on the French language.</p>
<p>It has been a lovely day today something like an Australian day in early spring just cold enough for us to keep our coats on &amp; plenty of sunshine.</p>
