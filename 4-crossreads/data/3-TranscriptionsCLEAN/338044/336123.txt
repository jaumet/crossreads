 25th Nov:- Cold day & very windy. Becoming rather monotonous in hospital & I shall be glad to get out. 
 26th Nov:- Expect to leave hospital to-day as nothing can be done for shoulder. Capt Knight is to speak to the O.C. &  I am to go on light duty
