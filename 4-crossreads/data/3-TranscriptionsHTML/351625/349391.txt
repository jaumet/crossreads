The night before Bert got married I went out with him on a farewell batchelors binge or bust up &amp; had a very merry time with a couple of other officers &amp; had a very merry time &amp; came home pretty well sober.  Bert had a beautiful &amp; expensive lot of presents.  He has got a real good job over here with some big aeroplane people &amp; is not likely to go back to Australia for some years yet.  Mrs. Germaine, his mother in law, is a very nice lady.  I must say I was always at home there,</p>
