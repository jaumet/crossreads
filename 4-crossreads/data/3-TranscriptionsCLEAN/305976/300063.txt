There is some kind of Plague in Amman. It is estimated 40 are dying daily. It is a big percentage for a small village. Saturday Oct 12 We are losing men every day going to Hospital with Malaria. We opened this station with 12 of us & O.C. Today there is only 3 of us & O.C. Our duties are heavy. Every day we are up at 4 AM & generally finish about 11pm We are getting more patients
