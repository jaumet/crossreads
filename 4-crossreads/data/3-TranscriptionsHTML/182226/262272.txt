<p>Tuesday 7 September, 1915</p>
<p>Received letters from home &amp; papers.</p>
<p>Letters from Gibby, Mrs Parish (2), Jessie Chilsholm, Ollie Turner, Phyllis Floden, &amp; Mater &amp; Ida.</p>
<p>Went down &amp; had a yarn to Bob Dryden. Cheered his heart by giving Tobacco, Matches &amp; Cigarette Papers. His battalion are having a rest down Monash Gully.</p>
<p>The 24<sup>th</sup> Batt arrived last night as support to the 8<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;who are so weak in numbers that no man has a chance of being out of the firing line day or night</p>
