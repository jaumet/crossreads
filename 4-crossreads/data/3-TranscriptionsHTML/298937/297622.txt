<p>Embarkation is now supposed to commence on Wednesday, &amp; our day is to be Friday.  I think this is nearer truth this time.<br/>I am doing more packing on the strength of it anyhow.<br/>Have discovered Hanna (George) of Whiporie in the bell tent nearest the Hospital.<br/>One day he came up sick &amp; with a temperature.<br/>I was called to have a look at him &amp; a mutual recognition took place.<br/>He has been here some 5 weeks.<br/>Harold Clarence dropped in yesterday afternoon &amp; I am to meet him at the Pictures</p>
