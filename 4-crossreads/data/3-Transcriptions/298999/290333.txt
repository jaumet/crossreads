<p>rested all afternoon.  for a while wrote to maud Bell and Joe C.  fed up 5-5.30 had tea 6 P.M. on line guard to night.</p>
<p>Tuesday July 24.7.17<br/>Turned out 5.30 A.M. fed up and had breakfast cleaned up camp rested till 12.30 had dinner very hot day.  there was a heavy fog this morning rested till 3.30 P.M. went on Patrol took a camel to water saw a fox had tea 6 P.M. went up to camel hospital no Beer there.  Posted letters returned to camp turned in 10 P.M. mosquitoes are bad.</p>
<p>Wednesday July 25.7.17<br/>turned out 5.30 A.M. fed up had Breakfast cleaned up the Camp we have a native here now Played euchre till 12.30.</p>
