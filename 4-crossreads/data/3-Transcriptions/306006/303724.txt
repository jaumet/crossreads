<p>31-1-17<br/>Wed 31st<br/>The foundation for the Railway is being laid down rapidly &amp; is 3 miles East of El Arish, also a wirenetting road for the Infantry to march on; last night there was a false alarm aeroplane scare at 8 oclock. an enemy 'plane was over early this morning &amp; dropped 10 bombs, killing several Artillery horses &amp; a couple of Tommies.</p>
<p>1-2-17<br/>Thurs 1st<br/>The Arillery has been firing today, getting the range of certain points; 2 Taubes paid us a visit today but dropped no bombs, six of our planes went out early this morning I went to Kilo 254 to the Ordnance</p>
