<p>Friday 12 April 1918<br/>Hun Aircraft Patrol.  Pilot Lt. Lucis.  In air 3 h. 0 m.  A quiete trip, can't see any Huns at all, they seem to avoid me.<br/>Total hours,<br/>Reccos &ndash; 223 h. 47 m.<br/>Odd trips &ndash; 7 h. 57 m.<br/>Total &ndash; 231 h. 44 m.</p>
<p>Saturday 13 April 1918</p>
