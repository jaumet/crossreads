
Friday, 8/6/17Went route march for the day to Corfe Castle & back by different road, total 10 miles.  Had dinner near the Castle & had 1 hour off to see it & the town.  Extra good meals today.  After tea went to town & read papers.
Saturday, 9/6/17Fairly warm.  Feet slightly sore after march.  When came back from bull-ring at 12.30, Reg was waiting for me, had been waiting 3/4 hour.  Stopped about 1/4 hr. then he motored to Bournemouth.
Sunday, 10/6/17Warm day - got up 7 a.m.  In morning took stroll up valley & gathered nice wild flowers.
