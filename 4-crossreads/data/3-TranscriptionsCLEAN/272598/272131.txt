
Monday 20.11.1614 F Amb went out to take over A.D.S.  Not quite as cold as usual.  Turned out to unload train about 8pm.  Issued with 48 hours rations.
Tuesday 21.11.16Turned out at 6 Am & moved off in motors for about 2 miles.  Walked remained of distance to Bernafay Wood near Longueval.
Wednesday 22.11.16Made ourselves comfortable & had a fairly good night.  Morning very foggy.  Working two days in line & 4 out.  14 F.Amb relieved by 15th F Amb today, our turn will fall due in a couple of days time.  Fatigue work at A.D.S.
