 notice things &amp; give us credit for any criticism  of their doings  &amp; memory 
 one day a Major came into the mess hut &amp; warned us "not to waste  biscuits  as economy was of great importance &amp; every  individual  effort was necessary" That day I was up at the Quartermasters Hut &amp; saw in his waste box two slices of  buttered toast thrown away  that I would have willingly paid a franc for, for we are on short bread rations in the mess hut &amp; have to make up on biscuit. Also  the following  the following day the officers gave a dance &amp; we had to clean out the mess hut &amp; I was one that was set to shred out about 7 lbs of  Gover
