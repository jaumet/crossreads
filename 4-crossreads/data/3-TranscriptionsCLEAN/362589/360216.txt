  
 [Printed on card] "A hearty Signal." - From across the foam  Flashes this message to our loved at home - "May God protect you bless you on your way, And health and happiness be yours for aye". 
 With Sincere Good Wishes For A Merry Christmas And A Prosperous New Year From Walter 
 2nd Australian Division, Gallipoli, 1915-16.
