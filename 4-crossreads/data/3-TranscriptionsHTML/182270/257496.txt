<p>THURSDAY, JULY 3, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm in the sun. We all went</p>
<p>into Quirindi in the big car in</p>
<p>the morning Father &amp; Mother &amp; Peter</p>
<p>went on out to Wallabadah &amp; Jo</p>
<p>&amp; I drove her car home.</p>
<p>I fixed up with Bank N.S.W for a</p>
<p>loan up to &pound;6000 for building</p>
<p>stocking ect, Drove old Cy down</p>
<p>to Middle in afternoon.</p>
<p>Tickle round ewes in morning</p>
<p>&amp; cutting logs for mill stand.</p>
