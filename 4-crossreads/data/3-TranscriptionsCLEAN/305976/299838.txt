
Wednesday Feb 21Raining like            Woke up in water.  Sham fight on today. Plenty of funThursday Feb 22Final leave through. Left for Sydney 6pm. Had good trip overFriday Feb 23Arrived Sydney 12 noonSaw Mother, Fred & Glad again. How pleased they were to see me. I really think those who love us feel it more than we do on parting. Spent  quite evening at Home & enjoyed it.Saturday Feb 24Visited Garrison Hospital in morning surprised to hear poor old Jack Smith dead. Buried today, went to funo funeral
