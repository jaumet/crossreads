
I shall return to Australia when the War ends - No word of going back yet.Collected 214 Bedouins today. They live in caves, Donkeys & all together, & are very poor -See DiaryMarch 19thI do love to get home news & to hear All's well - Can't understand my cable not reaching you I sent it Dec 15th -20thMove postponed owing to Jordan being in flood anyway I am staying here - with details - the regiment will be away some days - Still wet - Rain saved the men a lot during the last fortnight.
