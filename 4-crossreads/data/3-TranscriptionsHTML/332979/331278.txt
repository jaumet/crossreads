<p>Wednesday 14/3/17.<br/>Our Musical Competitions were continued &amp; the Events decided were Impromptu Speech. There were 5 entries &amp; the Winner turned up in Private Peter Hodd of the Trades Hall, Perth. The competitors had to draw their Subject <strike>from</strike> which was on a piece of paper from a Hat, the Winner drew for his, Is life worth living, &amp; Peter made a splendid speech &amp; was given a great reception when he had finished the other Speeches were, The Girl I left at home, Is married life happy &amp; Curiosity. The Afternoon was put in well also the evening Card playing.</p>
<p>Thursday 15/3/17.<br/>The Weather now is glorious cool &amp; refreshing. I spent the morning reading &amp; after Dinner</p>
