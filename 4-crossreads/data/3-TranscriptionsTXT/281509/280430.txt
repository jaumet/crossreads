 1916 July 26 Wednesday 
 Slept all day till 6 p.m. Feel in very good nick. Go up to Vadencourt mile up road to see Bob.  2nd Field running clearing station. Found him in dressing room.  Glad to see him, had a good yarn about my experiences. Back to 1st Field &amp;and carry from ward to dressing room from midnight till breakfast. Bill Coulthart  passes thro' with small wound in neck.  Mar  had &amp; arterial bleeding at ankle. Shrapnel pellets out of wounds  Make up "possey" in garden, quiet &amp; pleasant. Sleep &amp; read till 4 p.m.  Go on duty in charge of ward.
