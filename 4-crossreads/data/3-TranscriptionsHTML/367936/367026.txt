<p>Friday 18 August 1916<br/>We all rode over to the Meditteranean [Mediterranean] for a swim and had a great day.<br/>Bought some soda water &amp; condensed milk &amp; mixed em and did not feel well after it.  Had good time.</p>
<p>Saturday 19 August 1916<br/>Stables only as per usual.  Went to lecture in evening in Y.M.C.A. by Chaplain on the Brittish advance in France.  Very interresting indeed.</p>
