<p>Aug 19th &ndash; 18<br/>All on board at 1.45PM we sailed for Folkestone and an hour and a half later we felt a thrill when we were able to plant our beetle-crushers on Blighty soil.</p>
<p>Another 4 hours later and we were free to do as we pleased for 14 whole days</p>
<p>Aug 20th &ndash; 18<br/>I have spent the last 3 days in London looking up old pals and visiting a few theatres etc</p>
