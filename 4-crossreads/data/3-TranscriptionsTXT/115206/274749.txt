
June 1918Friday 28
Erronville. - On the roadWeather glorious , fine and clear. All kinds of rumours prevalent as to moving up again.Orders to prepare for evacuation in a half hours time have arrived.Packed Smithy's kit and my own and set off to bid my friend au Revoir.Left Battery W.L. at 8.45 pm and arrived at [indecipherable] just before midnight. At 12 midnight  we were still on the road near Longpre.
