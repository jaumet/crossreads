  
 F 17 Sun Recd.  Letter from Geo. Millar 
 Glorious Day. D. Tommy W & I go to see N.Z. Hockey match. Norm  Keirle comes over. We go round block to B'ford Tea & supper together &I walk halfway Home. He's very altered in appearance. 
 F 18 Mon but icy. I have very busy day. E. plays Hockey but hurts his leg. Recd. Letter from Reg. I try to write letters but only get time for PCS Wrote Dad. Mum & MRL. 
 F 19 Tues. Perfect Day. Ride to Sarum Visit both homes. C. not well. Leave at 7 pm from close &7.45 pm after cup of Tea to B'ford. Arr. 8.40 pm. Have supper shave& get ready for Parade. Read till 11pm when E. comes home. Have nice time. 
 W 20 Wed Recd. Letters from M.R.L. Eileen Stirling & Esme P. Go to WAACs dance. Meet May & [indecipherable] Davies. Have lovely time. Huge orchestra & topping supper. My partner is not boney 
 W 21 Th. I am ord. Corp. I have a real long day. Nothing doing all day. 
 F 22 Fri. Go to see "Caste" with Jim Robby. We walk all around camp & Tea at Salvation Army. E. goes to RHA. Dance. Recd. letter from Bess 
 23 Sat Go to Sarum by 2 trains  &  go to Mrs E  &  to cc  &  home with Con. Meet Geo. Green in Fisherton. Travel home with Ma'am 1st Class. Esq. Scotch  &  Welsh Girls are have great argument. Arr. Home 8.30pm for supper. I fetch the clock from Sarum which is very busy as to people & Soldiers.
