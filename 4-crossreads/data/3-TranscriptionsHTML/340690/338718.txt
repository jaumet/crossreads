<p>Fritz attacked but got badly pushed back and we used a smoke screen.</p>
<p>5.7.18<br/>Fine but cloudy.  Up for breakfast &amp; spent morning cleaning up.  Rumours of moving right back in 4 days.  Edmonds very dopey.  Got all ready after dinner and were to move at 1 but played bridge until pontoons arrived at about 3.30 with 1st Coy.  Rode all way and followed railway line then to right, left &amp; right camped between Hondeghem &amp; Wallon Cappel in tents and fairly clean &amp; comfortable.  Mail in and Pelman &amp; read in evening, played tennis &amp; read magazine before bed.  Couldn't sleep till late although fairly soft.  Linton's Guard.</p>
<p>6.7.18<br/>Fine &amp; cloudy.  Up at 7 and prepared for parade at 9.30.  Dismissed and spent morning digging cess hole for cooks.  In aftn. watched 1st A.A.S.C. [Australian Army Service Corps] sports, read &amp; wrote.  Our scout flight exhibition flying over us and tricks very fine.  One came down with engine trouble &amp; broke wrist.  Read in evening.  Slept badly.</p>
<p>7.7.18<br/>Out to work at 7.45 &amp; had to wait for wagons.  Mess up.  Worked till one on formation for light railway at Dump &amp; railway crossings.  Very hot.  In aftn. cricket v Artillery.  Won by about 30 runs, 19 &amp; 110.  19 &amp; 110.  [indecipherable]  In evening wrote some &amp; played bridge.  Warm.</p>
<p>8.7.18<br/>Very warm.  Out on job at 7.45 &amp; worked till 1.  Linton, Edmonds &amp; Reid gong to Field Ambulance to superintend road.  In aftn. cleaned up,</p>
