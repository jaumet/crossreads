<p>28/9/16<br/>Capt Downing adjusts set. Have a talk to him and put in for a transfer to transports. Visit Eric after tea &amp; start ear biting with young Gow &amp; Hoppy.</p>
<p>29/9/16<br/>Leave Romani at 7am  for Amara. Have dreary trip out &amp; arrive at 2pm</p>
<p>30/9/16<br/>WA2 leave 6 am for Romani. Find camp in filthy state so have to clean up.</p>
<p>2/10/16<br/>Go out to Bir el Abd to try &amp; bum some coal from the engine driver of the railway  train but has now reached Bir-el-Abd. Have poor success owing to just arriving too late. 3rd Brigade quarantined for cholera which has shown itself again.</p>
<p>4/10/16<br/>Find lovely green centipede in my bed on turning in  Stunt leaves for Mazar.</p>
<p>5/10/16<br/>Aeroplane on the way out to Mazar.</p>
<p>7/10/16<br/>Have some fun trying to get war news from the Tommies on the buzzer.</p>
