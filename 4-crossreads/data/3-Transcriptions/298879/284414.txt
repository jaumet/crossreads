<p>July &ndash; August 1915<br/>Saturday 31<br/>Warm. Cool breeze blowing from the South. Water fatigues for Hd. Qrs. In fire trench tonight. Heavy bom. by our howitzer guns. Two Turkish trenches taken on our right by 11th Batt. with a number of prisoners. My best friend, Joe Winterbottom gone to hospital sick</p>
<p>Sunday August 1<br/>Hot   Quiet day, so far 9 A.M. Artillery duel raging 12 p.m. Turkish gun on our right flank bombarding the beach. Both sides aeroplanes busy.</p>
