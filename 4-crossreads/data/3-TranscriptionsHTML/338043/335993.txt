<p>Oct. 16th<br/>Rehearsal &amp; Show. Good house. Splendid reception. Commence the preparation of the "Monkey's Paw".</p>
<p>Oct. 17th<br/>Excellent War News. &ndash; Important towns recaptured. Brilliant achievements of General Foch &ndash; Rehearsal Shows and study.</p>
<p>Oct 18th<br/>Change of programme. &ndash; Many Australians in town &ndash; In taking Lille &amp; Douai General Birdwood has his wish of "1916" gratified.</p>
