 just on the hill a matter of about a mile from our camp stands the Stone Henge, a well know curio of England, &amp; many a time we passed it on our way for a Stroll. 
 On the 26th of June word came out that 4 days leave would be granted to 50&percnt; of the camp for the purpose of visiting London, and I was one of the lucky ones that got in early &amp; got a pass from the 28th June to midnight on the 1st July &amp; away I went to London free for 4 whole days.
