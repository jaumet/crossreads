
<p>Feb.19<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Fritz was over again last night dropping bombs. we went out on the Chinese stunt again. we stayed in Fletre for the day &amp; took them home again in the evening. lovely day - we went up to Suzannes in the evening -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Feb. 20<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;We stayed in to-day - Misty all day with showers. We went up to Suzannes for a while in the afternoon.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Feb.21<sup><u>st</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;On the Chino stunt again to-day - took them along the Hazebrouck road &amp; we went to Meteren &amp; parked with 3<sup><u>rd</u></sup>&nbsp;A.S.P. whilst waiting for them. (12<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;brigade lorries are attached to 3<sup><u>rd</u></sup>&nbsp;A.S.P.) In the afternoon we took the chows home &amp; returned to Park. in the evening we went up to Suzanne&#39;s - lovely night -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Feb.23<sup><u>rd</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;We took the chows to Meteren. Whilst waiting for them we celebrated the fact of Bud&#39;s marriage &amp; return to France with champagne &amp; wine - We went to the pictures in the evening (at our park)</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Memo</u>. &nbsp;The weather keeps warm, but is mostly dull with occasional showers.</p>
