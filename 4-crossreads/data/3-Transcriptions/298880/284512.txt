<p>finally arrived at Estavies where our Bn. Was billeted in a large flour factory.<br/>July 10th<br/>Left Estavies about 8 PM marched about two miles and again put up in a barn.<br/>July 11th<br/><strike>W</strike> This morning I was warned by the  C.O. to get my gear ready and go with four others of our Bn to Paris.
