
July Saturday 14 1917
As expected poor Taylor died last night at 1 a.m. Thus the toll mounts.  Jock, Carty & self go into Boulogne Mac's on night duty & disinclined for jaunt. We get our phizes taken. Visit old Boulogne. French National Fete Day. Everyone in holiday attire  Buy favours. Listen to band on Esplanade. Wimereux to. Tea at C.W.L. & then to Kursaal. Cinema  Home walk. Shikkered Aussies. Tony supposed to go on leave to Blighty today.
