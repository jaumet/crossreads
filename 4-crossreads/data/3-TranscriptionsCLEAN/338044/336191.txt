 17th July:- Monday Awake at 5.30 am to find the weather very cold. Breakfast 6 am (Bread    & jam & tea) & parade 7 am, when we disembarked. A muddle of useless waiting on the wharf until 10 am follows & at last we march off along the docks through part of the town, passing a large munition factory en route & arrived at Fournier camp about 11 am. I am on cook's duty    & after making a fire, prepare dinner (tea & bully beef for the boys). We parade at 2-15 pm when parties are told off for fatigue duty. Take a walk
