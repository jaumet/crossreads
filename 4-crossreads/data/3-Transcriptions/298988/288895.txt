<p>&amp; the roof was lifted almost off the butchers cabin &amp; lot of glass broken in other parts.  We get a sea aboard now every few minutes &amp; consequently it is very uncomfortable, however one will go to sea so its no use growling.  "The Ships that pass in the night are a grand &amp; wonderful sight, but the nights we pass in the ships &ndash; well &ndash; rotten."</p>
