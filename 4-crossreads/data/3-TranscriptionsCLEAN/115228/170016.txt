Dec7th:- Moved back again in the early hours of the night to our old camp at Rechon-le-Zian. Our duty at Jaffa was merely to protect some battries of guns until Infantry arrived and relieved us. Been raining off and on for two days & nights & still unsettled.
Dec 8th:- News was received today that Jerusalem fell early this morning to our Infantry, we have thus now occupied practically the whole of Palestine, after a most successful campaign.
