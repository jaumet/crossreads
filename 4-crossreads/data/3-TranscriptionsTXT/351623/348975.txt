 6 April 16 Still standing by the Taff. French Cruiser Conde came close up this morning, she brought another small mail. Heaved anchor in forenoon &amp; went up to Castries &amp; alongside pier in order to procure a stronger wire hawser, to tow the Taff off the beach. Came down the coast again in afternoon &amp; anchored off her again, to have another try to tow her off at high tide. 
 7 April 16 Succeeded in towing the Taff off the beach last night. She was not damaged much. Heaved anchor at 5 p.m. &amp; came up to Castries &amp; tied up alongside coal wharf. 
 8 Apr 16 Coaled ship in forenoon. American yacht Speedwell of Chicago, both sail &amp; steam driven, is anchored near us.
