<p>29th  Started practicing the Mass bands of the Brigade.</p>
<p>30th  Divisional Church parade on Common near Camp 9th Brigade Band Played past.  Presided over by the Bishop of Salisbury from one of the Roman Tuberlix or burial grounds.</p>
<p>After church parade was a march past and general salute to General Monash.</p>
<p>About 80 German Prisoners arrived here to-day, nearly all of which had lost limbs, under a strong guard.</p>
