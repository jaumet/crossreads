 20-9-1917 Raining last night. Shoeing smith Forsythe returned from leave. Some of our men over-staying leave, one in particular, fined 28 days pay. Had a field day amongst the mules, some quiet, some not. 
 21-9-1917 Fine weather continues. Saddler Sgt. and Sgt Flett gone on leave. Our Infantry done well on the Ypres sector yesterday. 
 22-9-1917 A little driving drill for the new hands 
 23-9-1917 Sunday This evening half holiday. The morning was taken up with Church parade. We could hear a big bombardment all night. The nights and mornings are getting chilly. This is the quietest rest we ever had. Major Byrne on leave 
 24-9-1917 Our officers turned out of their billet. The C.R.A. and staff coming there. I suppose we will see a lot of them now.
