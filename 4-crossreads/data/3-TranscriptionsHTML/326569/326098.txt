<p>Position &ndash; Probable Cause &ndash; Immediate action<br/>1.  Right Home or Forward &ndash; (1) Empty Mgzine. &ndash; (1) Rotate Magazine &ndash; may free.  No. 2 New Magazine.</p>
<p>C.H. Home or forward &ndash; (2) Space in Magazine &ndash; (2) Rotate mgzine. &ndash; magazine fixed.  Pull back C.H. &ndash; continue fire.</p>
<p>C.H. Home or forward &ndash; (3) Miss fire &ndash; (3) Rotate mag. &ndash; fixed.  Back C.H. gun fires &ndash; live round ejected.</p>
<p>C.H. Home or forward &ndash; (4) Broken pawls or damaged magzne. &ndash; (4) Rotate mag. &ndash; fixed.  Back C.H. &ndash; no ejection.  Gun stops again &ndash; remove mag. &amp; examine pawls &amp; mag.</p>
<p>C.H. Home or forward &ndash; (5) Broken Striker &ndash; (5) Rotate mag. &ndash; fixed.  Back C.H. &ndash; live round ejected.  Fire &ndash; dud.  Back C.H. &ndash; live round ejected again.  No. 2 new piston &amp; assemble gun.</p>
<p>C.H. Home or forward &ndash; (6) Hard Extraction &ndash; (6) Rotate mag. &ndash; fixed.  C.H. will not move.  No. 2 Double pull through &amp; pull C.H. back.</p>
