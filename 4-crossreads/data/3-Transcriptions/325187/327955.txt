<p>[Monday 31 July 1916]</p>
<p><strike>I was glad to hear all's well in the old home &amp; in c/- anything goes wrong I'll post this today- With love to you all-</strike><br/>I had a note from Jim Ayre he is with our 18th Reinforcement &amp; should join up soon.  I will be glad to have a yarn.</p>
