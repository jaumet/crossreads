<p>Friday 14 April 1916<br/>Was granted final leave from 4-30 today till midnight Thursday.<br/>Was enoculated for 2nd time.<br/>Went down to Sydney, met Aunt Katie and boys and had dinner on Station.<br/>Came up to Mudgee by night's train.</p>
<p>Saturday 15 April 1916<br/>Had a good trip up, only three in carriage.  Father met me at Warrobil.<br/>The place looks beautifully green after the rain.<br/>Father and I took salt trough up to Castle Rock and then fed horses.</p>
