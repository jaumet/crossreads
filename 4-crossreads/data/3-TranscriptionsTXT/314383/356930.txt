We then  They then came back to us along a &amp; we had a pretty stiff go before falling back to the next ridge.  Cameron made another attempt afterwards, but the enemy was between him &amp; Nicols in such force that I called him back. 
 We then fought from ridge to ridge 8 against at least 700 until they almost surrounded us &amp; then would pull back to another position.  Not once would the Christians come to our assistance even tho all the men were armed &amp; when it got particularly hot.
