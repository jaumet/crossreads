  
 March, 1915 Saturday 20 Lecture from General [indecipherable] - Owen who has just come from the front, My conclusion he said, in a fortnight we would be side by side with English & French Troops, so we now expect it to be the Dardanelles - news just arrived re loss of the 3 Battleships which is unfortunate. Papers arrived today from Mrs Collin Bystander & the Sphere - Did not go to the Military Ball at Shepherds, felt off color & so saved money.
