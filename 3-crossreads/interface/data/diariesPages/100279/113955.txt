It is snowing lightly this afternoon but not enough to make it lay.
12th. Was up last night till after 11 playing "Bridge" we put most of our spare time in playing this game it is very popular in the Army.
Nearly all the Coy. went out this morning to do some shooting on the range they had to take their watersheets with them for it was raining lightly, but they had to turn back before all the shooting was done.
I also had a nice little job 4 Sergeants were sent from each Battalion down to the aerodrome to learn how to signal to aeroplanes when an attack is on, we left
