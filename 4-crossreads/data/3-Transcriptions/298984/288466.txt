<p>Monday 14th<br/>8 Barges were brought alongside about 8 to take us ashore. It was a slow job getting on board &amp; very hot trip to the landing about &frac12; a mile First thing was to look for a cool drink. A native took us in hand, but did not produce the drinks as cureo selling was his game. By being sterne we got the drinks &amp; left the cureos. Then went to</p>
