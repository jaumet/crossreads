Monday 24th JulyThey have brought some big guns around to play on us & are giving us a lively time with H.E.All we can do is to keep well down & build up the trench as it gets skittled.2nd Bn. Have gone out & made a new front line in front of us. Yesterday when Fritz made a new advanced firing line our bombers whe went out in broad daylight & bombed him out of it.Got buried & had a few abrasions today but still O.K.Relieved tonight by A &B Coys & are now just behind in support trench."Fritz" bombarded us all last night & kept it up pretty well today. Trenches are not much protection against well-directed H.E. but it is some satisfaction to know that "Fritz" is getting his issue too.All going well this afternoon as "Fritz" was in a little too far with his range on us & the shells were bursting about 100 yds. behind when unluckily one of the shells in flight hit a tree of the grove in front of line.We got the whole explosion & tree on top of our bay & the result
