
Wednesday 9/5/17Moved by decauville [narrow gauge light railway] from Bapaume to Shelter Wood, arriving at 8.30 pm.Thursday 10/5/17Very hot day visited Heritage, went to bed at 8 pm tired.Friday 11/5/17Wrote to Doff, saw The General who is going north on Wednesday to 2nd Anzac.Saturday 12/5/17Went to Amiens and then to Bazuux and saw frinals of 4th Div Boxing, stayed the night with the General.Sunday 13/5/17Returned on horseback, had luncheon at Officers Club, arrived home about 6 pm.
