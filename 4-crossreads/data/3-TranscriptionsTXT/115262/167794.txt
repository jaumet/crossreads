History of "Boorara"
The "Boorara" H.M.A.T. (A42.) is a captured German boat & was used at Dardanelles as supply ship.
Had a shot fired across her bows at Port Phillip heads. Her German name was "Pflaz" [S.S. Pfalz] & was rammed at Dardanelles by French cruiser Bov&ecirc;t in a fog (since sunk). In recognition of good work in reserving she was presented with by H.M.S. Triumph with their flag the Union Jack.
Pfalz was safely towed with rent 27' x 28'. Transport work since & once
