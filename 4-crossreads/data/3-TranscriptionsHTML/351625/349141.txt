<p>Post Card</p>
<p>Mrs. R.H. Caldwell<br/>"Ingledene"<br/>York St.<br/>Epping<br/>Sydney<br/>N.S.W.<br/>Australia</p>
<p>I have got a little heavier since I left home &amp; hope soon to go 11 stone.  I sent you a booklet of cards.  I hope you get them alright.  Best love to all &amp; very much to yourself.</p>
<p>from<br/>Douglas</p>
