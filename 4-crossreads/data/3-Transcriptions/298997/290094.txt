We got away about 8.30 and reached the Battalion after midnight.<br/>'June 4th<br/>Sick as a dog after the march, Doctor offered to send me to hospital, but I am trying to hang on.  Doctor suspects influenza.  My bones are aching.  Today (5th) doctor wanted to send me to hospital; and when I objected gave me "medicine &amp; duty"!  But Wilkinson told me to stay off parade &amp; rest.</p>
