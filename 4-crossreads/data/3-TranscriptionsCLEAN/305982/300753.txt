 27/12/16 Freezing cold last night needless to say  like wise to day  ground quite hard 
 28/12/16 Raining today visited 8th G.H.Q. mess Sgt  amm park. 
 29/12/16 Raining hard today mud & slush all over the place.  Went to Amiens on special duty bought prisoners etc muf & fur for Mother  also handkerchief  left same behind at 8th G.H.Q. on way home 
 30/12/16 Light rain today mud etc  Parcel from Mrs Coombs very nice   nothing interesting doing otherwise 
 31/12/16 Nothing doing today until later in the evening  New Years Eve tonight
