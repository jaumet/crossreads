<p>Monday 9/7/17<br/>Cold, windy &amp; cloudy, like rain<br/>having great spell here "Mailly Mailet" nothing to do all day.<br/>Wrote to Mrs Parsons, Loo, &amp; Nell got letter from Viv.<br/>D. Coy. goes Tomorrow on Guard of Honour to the King.<br/>Pay 18/9 &pound;28.5.0</p>
<p>Tuesday 10.7.17.<br/>still cold &amp; windy, scotch mist last night<br/>Big guns booming all along the line must be big battle going on.<br/>Pay 19/9. &pound;28.6.0.<br/>wrote to Percy Peacy.<br/>King going to interview us shortly. Hoorah do do ditto<br/>called to orderly room about my leave expect to go next week.</p>
<p>Wednesday 11/7/17.<br/>D,Coy gone as Body guard to King Brigade Band with them.<br/>Lovely morning, fine &amp; clear been very cold these last 3 days getting like winter again,<br/>Terrible air Raid on London on Saturday &amp; Premier Holman nearly blown out the other day Major Gen Holmes who was with him killed<br/>May go to Blighty Tomorrow<br/>Pay. 20/9 &pound;28.7.0.<br/>Brigade out on stunt to day<br/>Got new shirt &amp; chat shirt to day first for about 2 months. also Pr Braces first since leaving Ausy.</p>
<p>Thursday 12/7/17/<br/>All the Boys gone to see the King the whole Division &amp; others besides to be there I &amp; Pat Shelly left behind on guard<br/>Pay. 21/9. &pound;28.8.0.<br/>A Beautiful day Hot &amp; sunny<br/>Expect to go to Blighty Tomorrow.</p>
<p>Friday 13/7/17<br/>Boys disgusted with long march to see the King so were not in too <u>good</u> a humour when he arrived &amp; the cheers <u>not</u> what one would read in the Papers they were.<br/>Another lovely day but it's getting colder of a night &amp; one Blanket is not enough.<br/>Pay 22/9. &pound;28.9.0<br/>Brigade stunt tonight taking Beamont Hamel.<br/>all leave stopped till the 19th could have bet on it.</p>
