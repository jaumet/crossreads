<p>26th May (Friday)<br/>Kit inspection. Only allowed to have what will go into the pack. All surplus gear is to be burnt. More Gas Helmet practise.<br/>Bandages and iodine issued to be put into our leather pouches. Inspection of Division by General Godley.</p>
<p>27th May (Saturday)<br/>On Q.M. Fatigue. Met Hughie Cameron again. He expects to be getting a star shortly. All blankets have been taken from us &amp; packed with the other</p>
