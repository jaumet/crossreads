<p>Tuesday 15th Jan<br/>    Weather mild with some heavy rain.   Felt a bit off colour during the day but managed to get some writing done during the evening.   Received more mail during the day.</p>
<p>Wednesday 16th<br/>    Fairly cold and felt very off colour a reaction from my trip.   Did some writing and made another start on learning French.   Drugs very hard to get all the battalions etc crying out for stuff.</p>
<p>Thursday 17th<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Much milder and feeling better.   Spent some time reading my papers.   Will return them to Oldnance as waste paper is now being collected, a step in the right direction in economy.   Had a sponge bath before turning in.   Rain during the day.</p>
