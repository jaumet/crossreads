<p>Sanitation 113. Officers commanding unit will arrange that all manure of animals in their charge is disposed of by incineration.</p>
<p>With Ref to Routine order 159 dated 4-5-14 Receipt for refuse have been placed at 3 4 &amp; 6 piers in which all refuse must be deposited. Dead animals when brought to beach must be placed in water close to a pier.</p>
<p>114 Order  Burial Places Identification. Officer commanding sect of defence will make arrang for ident of each spot where off or men have been buried &ndash; places to be surrounded by wire &amp; wooden + erected. Notes will be made of the bearings of each burial place to ensure easy Ident of spot &amp; list of offcrs &amp; men buried will be added. a copy of these notes will be for[warded]</p>
