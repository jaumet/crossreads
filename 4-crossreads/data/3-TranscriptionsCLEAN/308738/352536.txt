  
 Oct. 21 Thursday 1915 P. M. came & interviewed Grice & O. C. after which Grice was arrested & taken to Mustapha. He was let out tonight on Bail. Went to hear a lecture on the Bible in many lands it was very interesting. 
 Oct. 22 Friday 1915 Working as usual P. M. came & interviewed the O. C. again re Grice nothing definite done yet with him. Lieut Cunningham went to Cairo today.
