Formed & took charge of a relay post on the outskirts of the town & chose a cellar as a place of abode.  Called out a few times during the night in the rain. 
 Tuesday 20.3.17 Snowing a little, passed some of the time souvenir hunting in & around the old buildings.  All dug-outs & anything of military value destroyed.
