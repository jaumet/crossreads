<p>Tues Jan 1st 1918<br/>New Year's Day spent on the 'phone lines from Derry House to front positions. Plenty of snow.</p>
<p>Tues Jan 15th<br/>5th BN. Relieved us in line &amp; we are still kept around Wytschaete in open positions.</p>
<p>Tues Jan 22ND:-<br/>Relieved 2nd BN in line near Ousterverne Wood.</p>
<p>Wed Jan 30th<br/>Left line tonight being relieved by the 54th BN. &amp; went back to dug outs at Wolverghem &amp; stopped the night.</p>
