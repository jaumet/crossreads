 next day left at 8 a.m. passed through Amiens & after pushing on reached this Billet at Belle Vue farm at 9 p.m. dead beat men & horses the latter especially as the column had got no oats with them & our horses had nothing since 6 a.m. 
 Oct 26 Went this morning with the limber to draw Rations - The dump is at Fricourt then one of the villages captured in the first push of July 1 Now about 3 or 4 miles behind the line Shows how we have advanced since then - Everything a sea
