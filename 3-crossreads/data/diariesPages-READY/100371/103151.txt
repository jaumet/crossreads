C2 roll put on C1., which means home service, either in England or Australia. On guard today 4.30PM till 4.30PM. It is "Keystone" handling a rifle when I have never done so before. Lovely day today.
Tuesday September 4th lovely day guard duty is no bon. I put in for furlough Wednesday unsettled weather, done up last night after guard duty, doing casual fatigues today. It rained tonight ten collasped collapsed. 
Thursday Sept.6th on cooks fatigue solid work 6.15 AM till 3PM dreary weather, Good lecture at the Y.M.C.A. entitled "The Tabby on the rocks". 
Friday fine, I volunteered as Mess orderly it may effect me going Home. Saturday Sept 8th fine, put in for furlough.
