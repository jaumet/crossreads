  
 [Postcard - photograph of North Tidworth. Very pretty.} 
 [Reverse of the above postcard] Mrs A. J. Burrowes Franurra Rooty Hill N.S.W. Australia. 
 30-4-18 This is a very pretty little old village on the outskirts of the Barracks. Our written Exam happened yesterday and we will know at the end of the week. We are almost certain to get 4 days leave from here, & I will go to Cornwall. Goodnight Mum dear, love from your Husband.
