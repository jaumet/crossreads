  
 February, 1917. Tuesday 27 Breakfast at 7.30 and after a general clean up fell in at 8.50 and humped all the heavy gear on to job about a mile away. General interesting lecture on appliance till 12.30 when we had dinner with tea. Passed time in afternoon till 3.25 & then came home and after tea went for a stroll towards Albert and then to bed.
