<p>February, 1915<br/>Friday 5<br/>Rifle Range. More shooting. Our company (C) won the shooting for today. Brigadier very pleased with the shooting generally received telegram from Arthur. 2nd contingent at Heliopolis. Received two photos from the Egyptian Inspector of Schools in Cairo.</p>
<p>Saturday 6<br/>All day holiday. Went to see Arthur in Heliopolis Camp found him in the Palace Hotel now a hospital. Beautiful place. Built to beat Monte Carlo. Has over 1500 grand rooms in it. W. Law one of our comrades died at Mena Hospital. First death in our regiment</p>
