<p>[Post card &ndash; cartoon of a German soldier asleep in very basic circumstances with mice crawling over him and dreaming of a woman coming to him carrying a tray laden with food.  The following words are printed on the card:]<br/>Ich Traumpt von Kuchen Bieru Schinken;<br/>ein Holdes Madchen tatmir winken.<br/>Doch ach ashatten unterdessen<br/>Die Ratten mir das Brot gefressen.</p>
<p>[Roughly translated I think:<br/>I dreamed of cake beer ham;<br/>A fair maiden waving at me.<br/>But alas meanwhile<br/>The rats have eaten my bread.]</p>
