  
 31-7-16 Mon 31st The Sigs who went to Mt Royston & Meredith yesterday did not have to come out today but worked the phone in camp, we left at one oclock this morning & found the Turks entrenched within 2 miles of Qatia.  The 1st Regt was in reserve today, & we were not allowed to camp in the palms as it was feared the Turks would shell Qatia today, but we watered there & the 1st Regt camped about  a mile to the west of Qatia.  we had 5 helio stations out & was also in communication with Bde per phone.
