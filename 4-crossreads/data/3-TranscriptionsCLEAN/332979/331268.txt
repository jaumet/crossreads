
nap. Sergeant Burden & myself starts a Class for First Aid tomorrow amongst the Railway Boys we have about 40 names on our list so hope it will be a Success. We have a large number of Certificated Men amongst us so we will not be Short of instructors.
Wednesday 6/3/17.As I anticipated there were a fair number of Lads turned up for the First Aid & having a fair number of Bandages & Splints we had a very interesting two hours, as we are close on our next port the class will be postponed until we get to sea again.
Thursday 7/3/17.Passed off very quietly nothing happened worthy of note.
