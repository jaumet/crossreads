  
 June 1918 Friday 21 
 Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles Weather still raining and a very driving mist with a cold breeze. Went up to Bty with a horse for Smithy. Left section pulled out at 9 pm and we eventually arrived at a small village on the outskirts of Amiens, where we billeted for the night. Smithy and Gun slightly inebriated under the flue of Vin rouge et Vin Blanc.    Still enroute at midnight.
