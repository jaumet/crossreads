  
 Saturday 4 May 
 The central relay is a cottage whose back door is away from Fritz's lines, but whose surroundings are under direct observation.  Capt Young & Sgt [indecipherable] live here, & we all mess together at a hard double table in the living room.  There was a food stove in originally, but a better has been salvaged (with an oven) & put in today.
