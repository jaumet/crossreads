Last night we were out steering our way by compass &amp; it was a pretty mix up, we had 4 different "bearings" to steer by &amp; all got hopelessly tangled up our little party finished up in a potato field easily 200 yards from where we should have been but we were not the only ones it took a good couple of hours to collect the different parties &amp; it was easily 12 by the time we got back.</p>
