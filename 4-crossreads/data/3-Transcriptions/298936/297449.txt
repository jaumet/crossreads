<p>October 1915</p>
<p>Wednesday 20</p>
<p>Artillery Lane.</p>
<p>No patients in morning.</p>
<p>Heard that Sir Ian H. has been recalled &amp; Gen Munro sent in his place.</p>
<p>Fellows very pessimistic so tried a little optimism on them, chiefly of the "Watch Russia" description.</p>
