<p>Saturday 6th<br/>Rain has been falling all day &amp; the battle rages up in the trenches. Our Transport had a terrible time going up last night. There was a block on the road &amp; the Huns were shelling. Dead &amp; dying horses and men were mixed up with splintered wagons in the dark &amp; the mud. We lost four horses. One was killed under the rider but he escaped with a severe shaking. The timber waggons were blown to matchwood. The road was blown up for a hundred yards &amp; it was impossible to travel for the deep slime &amp; mud.<br/>The weather has been bitterly cold &amp; our men have neither coats nor blankets &amp; they have been in it for</p>
