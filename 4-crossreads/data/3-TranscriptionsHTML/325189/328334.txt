<p>7th March 1915 Sunday.</p>
<p>Passed Mt. Sinai at 4 a.m.  The land all the way up the Gulf is barren, sandy hills.  Reached Suez at 12 o' c noon and dropped anchor.  The 'Chilka'  &amp; "Mashubra'  came to anchor directly afterwards.  A (British the 'Kent' ) &amp; French man-of-war were also anchored here.  After dinner we were surrounded by boats</p>
