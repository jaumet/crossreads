EgyeptMarch3/1917
My Dear Mother
Just a line to let you know that I ham well and I recived your kind and welkom letter the other day and was glad to heir from I never got your Sydney mail yet that you were send me with our Poto inI not seen Steave for about 6 weeks but he will be up to us in a few days now as they have been back for a spell we are expecting a big fight in a few days
