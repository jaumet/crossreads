<p>Authoritys on shore &ndash; both military &amp; Medical, the first batch of about 600 were allowed ashore, in a body in barges &amp; had to do a Route March thro the City to the Military Barrack where we halted &amp; all sorts of things are to be purchased. One very soon realizes the Cingalese as a race are born money grabbers &amp; keen bargainers &ndash; they generally start off at a price 5 or 6 times its real value &amp; one soon realizes he is foolish to give the price asked.<br/>We were allowed (my 20 men) an hours liberty to do the City &amp; of course indulged in the oft heard of Rickshaw, some of these nigs would keep up if not exceed the average horse for a couple of miles, we were backing our nigs to race others &amp; so on, some of them would <u>bite</u> &amp; go like fury.
