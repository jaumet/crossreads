<p>Jany 1st 1918<br/>1 man rejoined unit from hospital. 70 N.C.O's and men baked 24960 lb rations.</p>
<p>2/1/18.<br/>70 N.C.O's and men baked 28700 lb rations.</p>
<p>3/1/18.<br/>70 N.C.O's and men baked 31840 lb rations.</p>
<p>4/1/18.<br/>1 N.C.O rejoined unit from leave to U.K. 1 man evacuated to hospital (sick). 70 N.C.O's and men baked 38820 lb rations.<br/> <u>Meteorological Report</u>  Temperature 36 Deg. Fahr. in bakehouse. Flour 45 Deg at 6.15 a.m. Water for mixing dough 110 Deg. The first round (i.e. the Capacity of 4" Double Deck Collins ovens": approximately 2560 rations) mixed and placed in troughs for "prooving" at 6.45a.m. The 2nd, 3rd, and 4th rounds were finished at 8.15a.m, 9.15a.m and 10.15a.m respectively, with the same temperatures.</p>
