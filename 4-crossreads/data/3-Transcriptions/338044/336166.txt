<p>10th May Wednesday<br/>This morning all our boys fell in at 8 pm &amp; marched off. My old friends going affected me just as much as when I left Australia  We may leave soon, but the idea of staying in this heat &amp; plague stricken fly pested country much longer is a thought one cant bear.</p>
<p>11th May<br/>The overseas contingent of our boys (A.I.F. Hdqrs) depart from Alexandria to-day. We who are left behind needless to say, do not feel inclined for work    &amp;  it will take a few days to get into stride.</p>
<p>12th May:-<br/>We have almost got going again, having now  contented</p>
