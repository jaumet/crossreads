
Frid. 23-6-’16.
open formation to the Trenches. We were under fire all the time & observation too. We cut thru a wood & found the going very heavy on account wet & mud. "A" Sector Subsidary line reached just about dark & a dugout allotted to C.R.A., Frank Sullivan, Jack Toogood & self. My 1st exp. Of duck-boards & dug-outs. Our Dug out faced West (S.S.W) & was built of Sand bags, 3" timber, Corrug. iron, V iron & steel sections [sketch of dug-out with note – Toogood Ander. Self – Sullivan] Length = 4 sleepers. Width = 3 sleepers. Height = sitting up – just Steel sec. on roof with bags of mud on top supposed to be shrapnel proof. 10 p.m. we "Stood To"
