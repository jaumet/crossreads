Bean left us at and went on to the Brigade Headquarters at Hooge. Wilkins I secured lodging for the night in the advanced Red Cross Dressing Station. As there are a large number of batteries in the surrounding ruins, we hardly expect to obtain any sleep. We are apprehensive for the weather as a light shower has set in, the whole success of the attack is dependent on suitable "ground" conditions. We all anxiously await the morrow.

 20th September Thursday.
It has been a glorious frightful day. The Battle is over we have achieved our objectives. Fortune has favored us with weather which in a very great measure attributed to our success.
