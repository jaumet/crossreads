<p>Friday 26 April 1918<br/>Flew over to Junction Station with E. Headlam in our 2E to see John Webster who is with No. 14 Sqdn. A.F.C.  In air 18 m.<br/>John looks well but much knocked about by bomb.</p>
<p>Saturday 27 April 1918<br/>H.A. [Hun Aircraft] patroll over the Jordan.  Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 3 h. 10 m., no excitement.</p>
