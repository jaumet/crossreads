We marched along a beach parade lined with beautiful villas & reached a parched looking park enclosed with high iron railings. We had our own guards within & outside military police were on the watch so that a stampede into town to see the sights was almost impossible. And for the rest of our stay in Capetown we had one more trip to the same park
