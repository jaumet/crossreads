<p>we are decided that it would be impossible for us to compete against big firms until things have settled down a bit.  We are trying our D____ to get Home.  We have 4 strings going &amp; if Bunty can't pull one of them He will be very stiff.</p>
<p>We celebrated New Year vey happily &amp; merrily at our Friends Flat.  All except Ellis &amp; I were in Fancy Dress having returned from a Ball.  We had music &amp; singing from 9 till nearly midnight.  We sat down to a nice supper at 12.55 am</p>
