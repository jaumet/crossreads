  
 Wednesday 20 March Visited hairdressers, visited bank & looked around in morning. Visited Opera Marriage of Figaro. Very good. Had company of elderly Capt. Very good. 
 Met Will had tea & then to the Better 'Ole. Met Roy Ewart & Nick there. Show not too bad. Slept at Horseferry Rd. 
 Arranged to go to Scotland with Will tomorrow night.
