  
 The Company is still  tak  working at night on the advanced road work 
 Bert Ridewood was killed up there - Devine & Bill Baird have been wounded while we have had over 20 horses killed, a number wounded & lost some 30 waggons - 
 Quiney & Macrow have been given the M.C. while Humphreys, Col Hall, L. Collen S. Dunstan & Percy Dixon have got the Military Medal. 
 They dropped a bomb in the Company lines a few days ago & killed the Majors Hack & wounded 15 other horses luckily no men touched - It was the worst
