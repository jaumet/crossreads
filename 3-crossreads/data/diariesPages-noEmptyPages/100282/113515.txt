Then again after our match was over the final for the Australian Rules was played between the 8th Pioneers it was a very fast interesting game the first one of Australian Rules that I have seen since leaving Tassy. The 8th Battalion won by 4 points scores 20 to 16, there was a great crowd to see these matches we had a very good time saw some good fun
Lindsay Graham our C.S.M. came back from leave this morning he in common with many others has fallen a victim to the Scotch girls charms, got engaged while away, I had an idea he would for he met his girl last leave has since been corresponding with her. You can't blame our chaps in one way for its a long long time since we have seen any of our own girls
