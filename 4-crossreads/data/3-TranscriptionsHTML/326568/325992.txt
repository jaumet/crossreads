<p>January 2nd.  Raining again to-day.  Rumours that some men are going Home on transport duty soon.<br/>January 3rd.  Ambulance reduced to two sections, C Section being merged into "A" &amp; "B"<br/>January 5th.  A Tommy on sick parade amused me this morning, it being at least five months since his last bath.<br/>January 8th  Leave to Charleroi from to-morrow but am going on to Bruxelles to-morrow morning all being well.</p>
