<p>October 1916.<br/>The roads are covered with mud &amp; in places over six inches deep.   At Fricourt the 5th Division headquarters are stationed &amp; we stayed there for the night   Light rain was falling &amp; we slept in an old German dugout.<br/>This place a little while ago was the scene of a large battle &amp; everything is now in ruins.<br/>25th   After receiving orders from Headquarters we proceeded to the Company which is about 4 Km further at Montauban &amp; arrived there about 1 P.M.  I, with two others were taken on</p>
