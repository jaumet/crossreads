<p>4 February 1918 Monday (Whole page crossed out)<br/>Censored<br/>Letters from Mother Dec 23rd no mention of receiving my cable on arrival Egypt.<br/>Hope ere this you are in Melbourne.<br/>Just had my PAY Book debited <u>half pay</u> during time I was away- did not anticipate this- [indecipherable] thank you &amp; acknowledged muffler long since &amp; am wearing Days soks now- but cold weather nearly over now-  I hope Joe got to Warrnambooll  Some day, we may go again to the Hopkins.  Will post this with love   Frank</p>
