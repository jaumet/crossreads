<p>Sydney, but even now in spite of the long time we have been away &amp; the trials &amp; hardships I have had to put up with I'm glad I was one of the first away, &amp; never regret joining up.<br/>Well it has turned out a wretched day it has rained steadily all the afternoon &amp; is still doing so.<br/>This morning we had Battalion drill I had charge of a platoon &amp; got on alright, in the afternoon we were in a hut the whole time for the men were receiving instruction on the Lewis Gun.<br/>Some rotter broke into my cubicle while I was away &amp; stole some of my clothes several other articles &amp; a</p>
