
April 1918altogetherI went to Alex on Saturday afternoon.  Stayed at the Bonnard Hotel & next morning went up to [indecipherable].  It was a beautiful day but I wasnt feeling too good having caught a cold so wasnt able to enjoy my trip a bit.  Had a sleep on the lawn till dinner time.  After dinner went & picked up my VAD & went out for a drive and afternoon tea.  She had to go back to the hospital on duty at 5 oclock so after that I went & had a sleep & caught the 11.30 pm train back to Cairo.  It was a rotten trip.  Wasn't feeling like work on the Monday.
