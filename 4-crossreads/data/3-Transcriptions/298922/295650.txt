<p>Monday 17 December 1917</p>
<p>[This page crossed through.]<br/>Arranged Dolly's affairs &amp; she remains for present at Heliopolis House, Heliopolis, may come to Ismalia later on. My Diary now will only be of interest to the family &ndash; if I am not in the front line. The Moral of all ranks has improved tremendously with the success of the advance on Jerusalem. I have not got any mail yet, but it will be with the Regt. address as before &amp; I will get it stopped if remaining here.</p>
