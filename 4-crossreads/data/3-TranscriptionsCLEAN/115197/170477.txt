 Charleroi - a journey which knows no joys.  Night on the train. 
  Feb. 19th  
 Pass through Bailleul.  Steenwench.  Armentieres.  Lille.  Tournai & Charleroi.  Walk with pack from Charleroi to Beaumont - a distance of 23 kilo-metres (15 miles). 
 Billet for night at Beaumont. 
  Feb. 20th  
 Set out from Beaumont and arrive at Jeumont.
