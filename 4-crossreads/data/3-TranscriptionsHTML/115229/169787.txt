<p>in cattle trucks very early in the morning, 30 men to a truck and travelled back to Boulogne where we branched of for Ypres. The journey was slow and monotonous taking about eighteen hours to complete. Every time the train stopped, which was very often, the troops jumped out and picked blackberries. On the way we passed St Omer where the German&nbsp;gunfire has reached to do its destruction to the cathedral. We detrained at Popperinghe a few miles</p>
