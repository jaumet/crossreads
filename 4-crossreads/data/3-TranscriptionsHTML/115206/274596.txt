<p>January 1918<br/>Saturday 26</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Today has brought a stranger to our village, so the gossips tongues report. The stranger is a fair Mmslle, and has come from Samor, to reside temporarily in this quiet village.<br/>I can see the old villagers tongues wagging in the "tap room" of the Estaminet tonight.<br/>Thompson reports fair progress, also Jamies.<br/>Weather beautiful</p>
