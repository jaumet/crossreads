 early. We marched down to the pier before daylight and got on board the "SS Scotia one of the London &amp; N. Western Railways Co. steamboats on the channel. There was a stiff breeze and a choppy sea but no one was sea sick. We done the trip in about 1 &frac12; hours, disembarked at Dover and took trains to London arriving about 2 o'clock at the Victoria Railway station.
