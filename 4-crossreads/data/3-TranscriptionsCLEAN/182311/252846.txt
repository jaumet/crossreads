   One day last week the wind being very favourable Fritz had a balloon which looked right into our trenches & every movement drew artillery fire but an aeroplane of our flew in that direction & the German Sausage was hauled down without any delay.
 
   There is no doubt of the Air Supremacy being British daily we witness encounters between planes & tis always the German who is broken & falls & where the observation balloon is used always you see British, once nineteen were counted, the Germans perhaps a few.
