<p>was a very dark night &amp; almost impossible to do anything in the way of getting a horse out of a shell hole, &amp; so a kindly bullet finished their career. however we got them all out after being nearly smothered in mud ourselves. we were pretty objects next morning, I tell you.</p>
<p>The position of our guns now is behind the ridge in front of Ginchy a decent sized village before the war, but now all that remains is the sign board &amp; a few bricks &amp; debris. not a sign of a wall standing, absolutely wiped off the map. Our waggon lines also are alongside the town of "Mametz", like "Ginchy", it also is wiped</p>
