<p>July 26/17<br/>Cycled over yesterday afternoon to Neuve Eglise &amp; saw both Colling &amp; Jimmy Shaw. Coming back at dusk I saw a procession of <strike>many</strike> tanks moving up nearer the line, today there has been heavy artillery all day &amp; the air alive with lanes The offensive is evidently not far off.</p>
