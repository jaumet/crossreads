<p>Sunday 19/3/16<br/>Rained heavily during night; five of the tents swamped out; quite an unusual occurence in this part. Church parade 9.30 AM 8th  Brigade parade ground; service conducted by Capt Chaplain Beverage.<br/>Played team from 30th Btn &amp; won by six wickets &amp; 6 runs &ndash; Scores 30th Btn, 52 &amp; 67.  We scored 66 &amp; 4 for 59.  Scored 14 first innings 32 not out second innings &amp; took 3 for 9.  Went on piquet duty as acting piquet corporal at 6 pm.</p>
