<p>Thursaday Jan 4.1.17. still mounted orderly very cold and wet went to the beach to day rough sea very windy.<br/>Friday 5.1.17.bed clothes all wet very cold and windy. 10AM sun shining drying to day clothes still mounted orderly a rotten job To post  weather fine moonlight<br/>Saturday Jan 6.1.17. turned out 7AM fine day despatch rider to day 2AM sun shining very warm had my hair cut and lime too   taube over camp<br/>Sunday 7.1.17 fine morning turned out 6.30. had breakfast still mounted orderly washed camel met some of the 4 company at the wells11AM two taubes over</p>
