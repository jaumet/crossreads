<p>Thursday 30 Decr 1915</p>
<p><strike>Sent Mab letter.</strike></p>
<p>Friday 31st Decr 1915</p>
<p>Quiet New Years Eve.  Capital impromptu concert on piazza.</p>
<p>Saturday 1st Jan 1916</p>
<p>New Year's Day.  Very quiet.  <strike>Sent Mab "billet doux" &amp; letter.</strike></p>
<p>Sunday 2nd Jan 1916</p>
<p>Attended Church Parade.  Some "straight" talk from Chaplain.</p>
<p>Monday 3rd Jan. 1916</p>
<p>To be discharged tomorrow under "A" class to Gizeh.  <strike>Sent Mab &amp; Mum letter each notifying discharge.</strike>  Raining heavily &amp; frequently.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4th Jan 1916</p>
<p>Sent with 150 others to Gizeh, via Bab-el-louk.  Gizeh, on the bank of the Nile, composed of 2 camps, 1st &amp; 2nd Aus. Divs. respectively.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5th Jan 1916</p>
<p><strike>Have attended about 12 parades.</strike></p>
<p>Thursday 6th Jan 1916</p>
<p>Friday 7th Jan 1916</p>
<p>Saturday 8th Jan 1916</p>
<p>Sunday 9th Jan 1916</p>
