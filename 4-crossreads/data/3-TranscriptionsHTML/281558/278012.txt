<p>the padre from the 20th &amp; a few others :</p>
<p>Monday July 16th</p>
<p>Bapaume : reveille 4 am : showery : nothing much doing : artillery &amp; 'planes have been more active lately :</p>
<p>Tuesday July 17th</p>
<p>Bapaume : reveille 6 am : Alf &amp; self went for a long walk in morn : slept in afternoon : bath after tea :</p>
<p>Wednesday July 18th</p>
<p>Bapaume : reveille 6 am : carried Bob Bradford to the hospital : showery day :</p>
<p>Thursday July 19th</p>
<p>Bapaume : reveille 6 am : usual parades, etc :</p>
<p>Friday July 20th</p>
<p>Bapaume : fair day : reveille 6 am : nothing much doing : wrote in afternoon : stand to about 10.30 pm for a "stunt" : we marched over the ridge to take up positions for a counter</p>
