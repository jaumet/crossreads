They handed in last night from B.O.R. a "state" which was the most complicated affair as ever I had time to look at, &amp; there being no S.M. I had to try &amp; puzzle it out, I got quite a sweat up &amp; then never made head nor tail of it &amp; was in consequence  was  20 min late with my "parade state", I didn't give a hang, but they never said nothing, it was supposed to be in by 9 oclock  wa  but it was nearer 10 by the time they got it. All our 24th Reinforcements were medically examined this morning &amp; 1 chap was absent.
