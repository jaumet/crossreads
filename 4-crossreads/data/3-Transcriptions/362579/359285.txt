<p>Long for letters, they would  write a few lines.</p>
<p>My papers are not sent on now, so think you had better stop sending the "Argus" until I get to Base.</p>
<p>It is not quite so cold   &amp;  our tents are fairly comfy.</p>
<p>The horrid noise of the firing worries us sometimes but never feel afraid.</p>
<p>Sister Annie Bell is back. Poor old Sister Pilkington has appendicitis   &amp;  may have to be operated upon. So she won 't be back again.</p>
