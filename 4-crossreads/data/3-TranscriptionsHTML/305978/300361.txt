<p>mail just arrived, on from May saying that Frank had died from wounds in France on Oct 4th.<br/>Had a nice letter from a Lady in Australia saying she was a member of a Lonely Soldiers League  &amp; would like to send me letters &amp; parcels being a lonely soldier. Well I am not exactly a lonely soldier but I wrote &amp; assurred her that I would be pleased to hear from her in the future.<br/>We have 12 boys in the tent so a parcel will not go far amongst the mob, but</p>
