<p>15th<br/>Orders for our "hop over" here have been definitely cancelled.  We are to go into a reserve position instead, behind the village of Morcourt on our immediate left about 8 kilometres distant.</p>
<p>8.30 p.m.  We left our position below Guillaucourt and marched to up here to Morcourt.  The enemy bombed the road in our vicinity</p>
