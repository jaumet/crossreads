<p>12<br/>as I washed in. There's not too much fresh water about the place.</p>
<p>A lovely cool night, will use the blankets to-night.</p>
<p>Received 5 letters after tea Ruth Eva Hobden, Dorie, Charlie McNamara Trish. Ruths contained the news of Doss's wedding, it was dated 5/4/16.</p>
<p>21/5/16.<br/>A quiet day yesterday, Rodger &amp; Bill called about 3-30 &amp; I went down for a swim with them it was great. After tea they came across &amp; we went down to the canteen &amp; had some peaches &amp; milk, quite a feed. Received a long letter from Doss telling me of the wedding.</p>
<p>It is pretty warm to-day &amp; the flies are busy.</p>
<p>Sunday to-day it's like all other days here.</p>
