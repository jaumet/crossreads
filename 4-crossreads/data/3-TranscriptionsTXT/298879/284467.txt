November 1915Sunday 14Cool breeze blowing. We relieved the 9th Bn out of their old position. We got a good pozzy for the 'phone. Violent artillery duel about 1600 to 1700.
Monday 15Showery settled down in our dug &ndash; out for another artillery duel one man killed too. A fierce Naval bombardment at Achi Baba this afternoon which lasted about two hours.
