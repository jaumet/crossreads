I discovered that it was only about 200 yds of a ride &amp; that the car was pulled up by stout wire cable. On going out found that I was near one end of bridge, I did not go on it, there was a small toll. While there I discovered that the Zoo was only about ten minutes walk so decided to go to it. It was a very pleasant walk along the top, with a grand view. The Zoo I found to be first class, plenty to see &amp; very compact. Had some refreshments in the Zoo. I came back the same way, down the rock railway. Had tea in the city &amp; arrived back before dark. Sept 17 &ndash; 18. Beaufort Hospital This afternoon I visited the museum &amp; Art Gallery they are very fine, especially the Museum, which has a great number of very interesting things.
