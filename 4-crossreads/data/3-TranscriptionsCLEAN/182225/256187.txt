 1916   TUESDAY 1   FEB. 
 Very hot again & we had a drunk Sargent Major to drill us in "marching past" in morning in the Park & he did race us round. Got our second issue in afternoon so had no drill  Got the 4.32 train in & 5.20 boat to the bay. Linda & Jo in town all day & they were dead beat in evening when I got there. Lay on the tennis court for a bit after tea & then got the 9.45 train back to camp
