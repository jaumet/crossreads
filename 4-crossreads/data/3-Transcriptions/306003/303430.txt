<p>16-7-16<br/>Sun 16th<br/>Last night Mr James forgetting today was Sunday said the boxing would be continued tonight &amp; did not notice the mistake before the crowd dispersed &amp; Col Meredith advised him to let it continue as one day was the same as another out here;<br/>Church Service was well attended this morning. I had a trip to the 2nd Regt just before dinner to fix up the contests for tonight;</p>
