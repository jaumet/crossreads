<p>we (the new draft) marched about 3 miles to gas school.  We are at present fitting on box respirators.  They seem to pay particular attention to fitting of helmets on account of the new mustard tear gas .<br/>4.30 Returned from gas school with a headache from the helmet; had the first shave since leaving Parkhouse &amp; feel all the better for it.<br/>This camp is in a very pretty valley at the opening to which is Rouelles, facing a kind of backwater of the harbour.
