
23-2-16Wed 23rdSome English troops have taken over from our machine gun men at Sequela & are guarding the bridges. B troop do nothing but mounted patrol work; The Tommies are making their post very strong for defensive purposes & barbed wire has been laid down through the crops; Nothing happened today beyond the usual routine of stables Etc.
24-2-16 Thurs 24thWas on mounted patrol duty last night; & this morning I was kept busy getting things out of the black kits for B Troop; This evening 8 men under Capt Higginson went out to try & surprise some Senoussis who come in close every night & there tracks are very plain
