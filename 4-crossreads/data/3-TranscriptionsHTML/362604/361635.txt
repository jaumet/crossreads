<p>Next day I travelled around London Bridge, St. Paul's, Lambeth Bridge &amp; Westminster, Whitehall &amp; St. James Park.  I saw a procession of women farm hands going through the Park up to be viewed by the Queen.  They all dress like men &amp; look so funny "all a-- &amp; pockets" like this [see image for drawing].</p>
<p>Well, Mum, it is getting on to supper time &amp; I'll have to get it ready.  I'll write a few lines to Dad &amp; George after supper.  Best wishes to all the Neighbours, Mrs. Stephen, Bunting &amp; Annie Dobson.</p>
<p>Your Fond Son<br/>Harry</p>
