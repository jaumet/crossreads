<p>Sun Dec 17th &ndash; 16<br/>Anniversary of leaving Sydney</p>
<p>Mon Dec 18th &ndash; 16<br/>There is a rumour that we go out for a spell but I very much doubt it as by what I heard we are here for 2 months</p>
<p>Tues Dec 19th &ndash; 16<br/>Although the batteries have been going out in turn for a spell, we poor unfortunate blighters can't get out.</p>
