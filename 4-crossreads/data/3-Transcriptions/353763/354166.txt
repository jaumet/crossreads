<p>Oct 4 &ndash; 1914<br/>All is well  -  I'm sending 3 serial letters by a friend who goes on the Berrima to Sydney  -  I trust you'll get them all right  -  They are fairly accurate of course they are for you &ndash; not for general circulation  -  Weather &ndash; fine &amp; dry  -  We are not going to be Stationary &amp; you may not hear again for a bit  -  Its Sunday &amp; we've had no leave this week at all  -</p>
