<p>[SEPTEMBER, 1916.]<br/>Friday 1<br/>"Beauval", at present our home nice place, beautiful church good beer, lovely champagne and the women are pleasing to the eye<br/>Saturday 2<br/>Letters from Mary Elsie Nell Hilda &amp; Muriel, a happy day Their health in champagne<br/>Sunday 3<br/>So long they power hast blest me sure it still will lead me on back to those I love<br/>A chap is playing "Rememberance" it takes me far across the sea, to  Botany</p>
