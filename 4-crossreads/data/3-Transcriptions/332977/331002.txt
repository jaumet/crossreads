<p>Thursday  Sept 30th<br/>Taylor A Cm Killed bullet through head<br/>75, lands 5 shells in our Circular trench Tommy Kelly blows half his foot off with bomb<br/>Dr Pott leaves for Hospital Enteric</p>
<p>Saturday Oct 2nd<br/>20,000 Gurkhas &amp; New Founlanders arrive on the left, Essie's cake arrive heavy Howitzers play havoc with Turks trench. Monitor fires on Gaba Tepe lay barb wire in front heavy firing at Ache Baba, seven days rations come up<br/>King of Greece betrays us</p>
<p>Monday Oct 3rd &amp; 4th<br/>Turks shell us in there trenches with Bayonets fixed, we all stand to, but they don't come, Gaba Tepe again bombarded,</p>
<p>Thursday Oct 7th<br/>Attacked lone Pine, one of our lads kill 5 Turks and wound two with bombs, three lads shot while saving a Turk</p>
<p>Saturday Oct 9th<br/>Turks use gas on the left, Bulgaria enters the war arena, Jim Merry killed by a bomb falling in his dug-out in old firing line,</p>
<p>Sunday 10th<br/>Turk surrenders is shot in leg on Beach Turks fire on him. our stretcher bearers go for him at night our covering party fires on theirs we win</p>
