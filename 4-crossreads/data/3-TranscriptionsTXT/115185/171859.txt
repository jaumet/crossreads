 off in a sailing boat, which they have bought for our hospital. Col. Fiaschi spoke to us all and told us that they were only just landed from their ship &amp; only this morning had been able to secure tents &ndash; which are going to be put up for the sisters at once. They are all sleeping just on the rocks with a shelter to keep the sun off them. We are expecting a great lot of wounded and every effort is to be made to have something ready to receive them in the meanwhile we sisters are to stay on this ship &ndash; in our dormitories. The embarkation officer came off this afternoon and I heard that Archie is in the firing line only 45 miles from me.
