<p>Memoranda</p>
<p>Jan 5th 1917<br/>Rained again in showers. Wind very cold. Met a lot of old friends. Lt Lamb, Lt Dawson 5th LH Lt Bell 2 LH all in 11th Coy ICC</p>
<p>Jan 6<br/>Nothing of note today.</p>
<p>Jan 7th<br/>Work very hard today all bustle whilst issuing rations at 8  pm moonlight our camp was bombed by Taubs 2 bombs dropped 30 yards away. No damage. Moving out to Rafa for battle tomorrow.</p>
