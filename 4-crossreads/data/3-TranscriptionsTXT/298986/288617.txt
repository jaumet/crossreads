Friday 28
Back to the old camp life.  Parade at 9 A.M. & 2 P.M.  Some fatagues folded a few blankets this morn.
Sat. 28th 1 March
Paid this morning 12.0.0 to go on leave on Monday morn.  Met my old mate N. Bell went to Warminster for a walk.  Had the studs taken out of my boots & rubber heels put on a great improvement.
