<p>which was previously a large lace factory. This room is 300 x 150 ft &amp; filled with looms and other machinery.</p>
<p>During the day I had to present my eyes for examination which will not finish to day.</p>
<p>Fri Sept 15<br/>I again go up on the eye parade &amp; in afternoon went through the city.</p>
<p>There are many handsome buildings &amp; a large open square in the centre as is usual in all French &amp; Flemish towns. About 10 acres in extent where the [indecipherable] &amp; councils are established &amp; all the other main buildings are.</p>
<p>I was supplied with glasses and left in the evening for home. The weather is showery. Recd letters from Paris.</p>
<p>Sat Sep 16<br/>The 7th F.A. left us to day to take over another station.</p>
<p>We are losing our OC Leiut Col [Fergasse?] Stewart who is being transferred to another unit and our new OC</p>
