<p>IX<br/>but as you know all cameras except official are "tabooed".  The same pony is at present suffering from a kick on the knee, but will soon be going strong again.  Horses are a problem in this country and with the light ration it has one thinking how to keep them in condition.  We are allowed 12 lbs of Oats (whole) &amp; 10 lbs of Hay per diem for our horses which is allright for these</p>
