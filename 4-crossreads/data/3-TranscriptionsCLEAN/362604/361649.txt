The butter you sent was eatable but was not so very nice so I gave it to Marion Edmonds & she made me a big cake.  Civilians here only get 1/4 of fat a week.  If they have butter well they can't have suet, or lard, or dripping.  Sugar 1/2 a lb, meat about 1/2 lb a week, meat meaning bone, fat, gristle its all the same.
