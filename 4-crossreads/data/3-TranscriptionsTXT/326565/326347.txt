 Tuesday 18/4/16 Attached to Fifth Division which consists of 8th, 14 &amp; 15 Brigades &amp; is one of the two Divisions which form the 2nd A &amp; NZ, Army Corps.  The Artillery attached is equipped with 60 guns, 48 of which are 18 pounders &amp; 12 are 4.2 Howitzers.  Struck tents for the purpose of airing the ground on which they are pitched.  Received parcel from home containing Diary &amp; Ink Pellets. 
 Wednesday 19/4/16 Route march morning &amp; afternoon.  Rather a very quiet &amp; uneventful day.
