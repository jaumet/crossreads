<p>August 1915<br/>Thursday 26<br/>Cool. A heavy bombard preceded on attack by our chaps on the left. Results of the attack have not come through officially. The capture of a couple of howitzers by our lads is reported. The enemy shelled our trenches considerably about 5 PM</p>
<p>Friday 27<br/>Cool. Cloudy. A couple of Taube aeroplanes were seen above our trenches yesterday evening. They did not stay long though.</p>
