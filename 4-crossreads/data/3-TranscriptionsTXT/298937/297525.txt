 Tuesday 1st February 
 Starting on Turkish &amp; improving French by conversation with Dr. Cockinos. Busy dispensing special prescriptions during morning. Aft. off.  Went to Maadi Camp &amp; found Sellars who accompanied me back to the Camp (or Prison rather) where I was just showing him round when a messenger arrived telling of accident nearly by.
