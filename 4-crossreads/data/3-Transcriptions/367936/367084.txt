<p>Tuesday 12 December 1916<br/>Nothing much doing.  Carried on as per usual.</p>
<p>Wednesday 13 December 1916<br/>Put on patrol with troop of C Squadron.<br/>Harry Jacob &amp; I as signallers.  Mended telephone line on way home.<br/>Patrol left in evening to look for wells in Wadi El Arish 25 miles away.</p>
