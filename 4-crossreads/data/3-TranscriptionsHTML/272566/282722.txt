<p>Wednesday 5 December</p>
<p>Heavy frost through the night and bitterly cold today.  Cleaning the bike all the morning.  At midday dropped returns at A.D.M.S. Office Dranoutre then continued on through Bailleul to 1st Anzac Hd. Q. at Flete where I dropped the 5th Div. Returns.  Came back by the same route.<br/>German Balloon drifted over our lines today dropping circulars written in French &amp; German detailing the great German success in Italy</p>
