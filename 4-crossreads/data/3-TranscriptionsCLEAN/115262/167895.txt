
Tuesday 8 May 1917.
Very little drill doing but things busy about embarkation.
Returned stores & struck some tents during morning.
Afternoon ― drill ― packed kit bag (universal) and placed in "store" for A.S.C.  At 8pm. placed on G. S. Wagon for removal tomorrow morning at 4 a.m.
Advance guard for A.C.B.Fd.Amb. detailed.
Wrote 10 notes tonight & posted same.
A cold as result of Monday. 1st cold since last October.
A.M.C. (2 lots) leave for various transports.
