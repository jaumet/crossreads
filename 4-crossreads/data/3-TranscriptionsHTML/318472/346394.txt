<p>18 June 1918<br/>Tuesday<br/>Haven't been given a job yet. Nothing of importance to note.</p>
<p>1918 June 19<br/>Wednesday<br/>Squally wet weather &ndash; sometimes fine &ndash; sometimes raining My feet are treating me fairly badly again &amp; I am having them massaged again. The Disease that has been raging in the Depot appears to have almost died out.</p>
