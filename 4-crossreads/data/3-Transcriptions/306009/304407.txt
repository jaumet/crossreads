<p>1915<br/>Aug. 11 Wed.<br/>This afternoon had the best feed of strawberries I think I have ever had. They were bosker!<br/>The office staff get all sorts of tit bits in the tucker line.<br/>To-morrow is the Anniversary of the Birthday of Mahomet &ndash; The Egyptian Christmas Day on which the great Bairam Feast will be held. The festivities will continue for two days after Bairam Day &amp; all leave for Troops has been stopped for that period.</p>
