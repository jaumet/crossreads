21/2/18 Went to pictures with Murray then to Canteen had supper of Tinned rabbit. Temperature much milder.
22/2/18 Issued with new boots tunic. Wrote home.
23/2/18 Met palmer of 6th Btn. He was wounded on Oct 31st at Ypres arrived yesterday from England on his way back to the line. Told me Burrell Snowy James both going to Australia.
24/2/18 Sunday Palmer went up to line this morning, also Murray.
25/2/18 Recd letters from Uncle Fred Mr Southwell. Latter says he will probably be made a chaplain. 26/2/18 Met 2 Railway unit chaps who left Melbourne on Nov 7th.
