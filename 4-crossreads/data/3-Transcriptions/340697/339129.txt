<p>"Later". We hear that the 10th gained their objective last night but had to retire owing to our own shell fire. We expect to be releived tonight. Taube dropped a bomb on the artillery horse lines yesterday and done a good deal of damage. 9 men killed 21 wounded and 18 horses killed 22.8.16</p>
