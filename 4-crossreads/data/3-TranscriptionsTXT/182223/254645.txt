 Monday 15&nbsp;March 1915 Ride between 7 &amp; 8 a.m. Reading &amp; writing all day Wrote to P.J. MacNamara, Jack MacNamara &amp; Mrs&nbsp;&nbsp;Potts&nbsp; A shower of rain this afternoon&nbsp; Ride during the afternoon 
 Tuesday 16 March 1915 Letter from Joseph of date&nbsp;8.2.15 this morning. Answered it. Posted letters to Mollie, the Minister for Public Works Egypt, Clarence Bridge, The Admiralty London, Ivy Macdonald.&nbsp; Wrote to M. M. Knowles, David Watkins.&nbsp; Showers of rain during early morning &amp; before mid-day.&nbsp; Riding during Morning &amp; afternoon.&nbsp; Father McAuliffe gave me a shamrock plant from Limerick. 
 Wednesday 17&nbsp;March 1915 Gave Col. Martin&nbsp;most of the Shamrock plant which was given to me by Padre McAuliffe.&nbsp; Received orders today that I was to be left in charge of Mena House when No 2 G.H. has departed.&nbsp; Ride during the afternoon.&nbsp; Quiet day in every respect.&nbsp; Death of Major Parker from Diplococcal meningitis  of Major Par &nbsp;Came from W, Australia
