<p>he went travelling, stayed in West Aust., worked in the bush for experience, got some land &amp; made a farm of it, then got married.<br/>He speaks good English with an accent &amp; argues well.  We discuss all subjects in which we are interested &amp; thus pass many a pleasant hour.<br/>The whole tent is a goodly company, &amp; I feel lucky to have struck such good fellows.<br/>I hope we stick together, for if we do we shall be able to have a good time, wherever we may</p>
