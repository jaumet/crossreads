
Frid 14th Feb:  Reached Sydney 7 p.m:  Anchored in Watson's Bay.Sun 16th:  Disembarked at Woolloomooloo Bay 4 p.m.Frid 21st:  Reported at No. 4 H.G.H Randwick. To report on 27th .Frid 28th:  Admitted as an out-door patient.May 9th:  Discharged. Furlough commencedJune 22nd:  Completion of Furlough.June 12th:  Commenced work at Cockatoo.
