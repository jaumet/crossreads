<p>Monday 18 February<br/>Still weather gloriously fine &amp; moonlit nights favourable for night flying.  Fritz comes over nightly &amp; drops a few bombs in the vicinity of camp.</p>
<p>Tuesday February<br/>Still beautiful weather.<br/>Second match of Competition against Engineers ended in a win, 6-nil for the Ambulance.  To-morrow's match against 1st Battn. will be fierce.</p>
