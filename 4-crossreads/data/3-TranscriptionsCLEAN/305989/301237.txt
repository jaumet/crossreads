  
 Monday 1 January 1917 Cruel day.  Taubes over early & dust storm the rest of the day, wounded "2 bints". 
 Tuesday 2 January 1917 Wretched day sand & rained all day one of the most cruel nights I ever remember our "possy" was near the sea, was wet through by 8 o'clock. 
 Wednesday 3 January 1917 Cold all day:  got good mail.  Got cable from home, price 30/-.  Mother Lucie (3), Theo, Lorna, Rex, Elsie. 
 Thursday 4 January 1917 Cold & windy:  also several heavy showers. 
 Friday 5 January 1917 Fairly clear.  Section down for swim clear night up till 9.30.  Greased saddles made dugout. 
 Saturday 6 January 1917 Got up early & went to "Kilo 139" for reinforcements.  Taube flew over but did not drop any bombs.
