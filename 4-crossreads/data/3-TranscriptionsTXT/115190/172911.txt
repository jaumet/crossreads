No one speaks, in fact my comrades must be as fatigued as myself.  At last just as day is breaking we come into a wadi & we strike a level track.  With the new day I feel a little fresher.  About 8 am we pull up & have bully & biscuits.  We commence to climb again & this time the track is so bad that we have to lead our horses.  Several times my horse slides & falls on the slippery rocks but the only damage is the loss of a few strips of skin & hair.  Eventually we reach a small village at the top of the hill.
