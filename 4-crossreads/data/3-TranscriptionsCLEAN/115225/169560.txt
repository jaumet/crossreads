 May 4  th    Fritz came over bombing during the night - We got up at 6.30 A.M. & loaded up with Fodder rations & cooks furniture etc & took it to  Ravelcourt   Ravelincourt  . the Q.M.S came on my lorry & brought to light some champagne & 1908 Sheblis which we all enjoyed, as was evident by the way the telephone poles went by - we got home at 6.P.M. 
   
 May 5  th    We left the park at 5.A.M. for Querrieu where we picked up men of a labour battalion & took them to Bertangles - It rained most of the day - when we got back to the park   We had to shift our amunition echelon back to Allonville & Park with the 3  rd   M.T. Coy. again   x see  memo . 
   
 May 6  th    We left the park at 9.A.M. & went to Ferreres & picked up a load of ammo & took it to Querrieu - lovely day - 
   
 May 7  th    We left park at 6.A.M. to work for 1  st    Aust. corps troops (old Anzac.C.T.S.C.) took two loads from Vignacourt railhead one to Poulainville & another to Rain ^  n eville & got back to park at 6.P.M.
