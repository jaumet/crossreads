 July 1918 Wednesday 3 
 Vaux sur Somme.1st Bty Position. Weather glorious , fine and clear: Received a long letter from Mother. Wrote one or two to different folk. Final preparations made for tonight's stunt . Tom Code and Da Costa are to hop the bags tonight. I wish them both the very best of luck.   Georgee Baker and Fitters North to hospital with Trench fever.I  am Assistant linesman in the company of Ken Gunn for the stunt.
