We arrived at our destination Seraphium about dinner time, &amp; it was a scorching hot day, we were not long in getting out of the trucks &amp; getting into the shade of a belt of trees that have been planted along the bank of the canal to act as a break for the wind I suppose there is also a freshwater pipe line running alongside &amp; we were not long in quenching our thirst
