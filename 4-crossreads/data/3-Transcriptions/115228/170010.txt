<p><u>Nov 13th</u> :- The Brigade moved back in to the beach about four miles to the rear, evidently with the idea of giving us a short rest. It actually proved very short, for hardly had we our lines (down?) &amp; stripped off for a swim when we were ordered to saddle up at once, &amp; we again moved out.</p>
<p><u>Nov 14th</u> :- The Turks having retired during the night, we advanced another ten miles today through Askalan &amp; on to (Duran?), a pretty little Jewish village. Only</p>
