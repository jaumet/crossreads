 Thursday 4 February 1915 Hospital in the morning. Writing &amp; reading most of the day&nbsp; Walked to top of sand hills just before dinner.&nbsp; Air very cold.&nbsp; Expected letters but none came. 
 Friday 5 February 1915 One operation this morning Haemorrhoids&nbsp; A beautiful morning 12 noon.&nbsp;No letters In Cairo during the afternoon At Heliopolis for half an hour. Shepheards to dine with Major Barrett. Left for home at 10.30 pm. 
 Saturday 6 February 1915 At Mena House all day. Working, writing, and reading&nbsp; 10.45 p.m. Must go to bed early. 
 Sunday 7 February 1915 Mass. 9 a.m. Padre McAuliffe &nbsp;preached against use of&nbsp;immodest&nbsp;profane &amp; vulgar words in use about Camp. Called 3 rd  &amp; 4 th  Infty&nbsp;Battalion L. Col Owen L. Col. Onslow Thompson also 1 st  F &nbsp;Ambulance L Col. Newmarch.&nbsp; Col Beeston &amp; other officers, from the 3 rd &nbsp;Exped. force visited Mena House. They are camped at Heliopolis
