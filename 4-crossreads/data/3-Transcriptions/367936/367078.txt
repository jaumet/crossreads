<p>Thursday 30 November 1916<br/>Washed clothes and self in morning.<br/>On telephone at out post at night.<br/>Exciting aeroplane chase but Taube got away.</p>
<p>Friday 1 December 1916<br/>H. Jacob &amp; I practiced signalling in morning.<br/>Went out with M.G's range finding with mychrometer also box &amp; shroud which Germans left behind at Romani &amp; is worth [indecipherable].</p>
