<p>December 1917<br/>Wednesday 19<br/>Suffra.  Showery day and cold.<br/>Went on night outpost. Rained during night and got wet thro<br/>Blankets all wet. No shelter</p>
<p>Thursday 20<br/>Suffra. Cold day with occasional showers. Tried  to dry blankets by fire. Cold damp blankets at night.<br/>Still well. No colds. Big mail I got 34 letters.</p>
