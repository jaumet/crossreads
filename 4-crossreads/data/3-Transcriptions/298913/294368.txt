<p>While on the subject of drink, I may say that those who indulge in the whisky end up in a pitiable state. The quality is such that it turns one into a raving lunatic for the time &amp; I have seen men in the camp "boob" under the influence of it, that are enough to cause any sane person to swear off it. The soldiers have been warned times without number about this, but they still persist.<br/>The native quarters have a fascination owing to their eastern custom &amp; also because there are no other places to go to. The town is usually crowded at night time &amp; everyone goes to Cairo to have a look around. Excitement is always high &amp; shops are open every day &amp; night without regard to what day.
