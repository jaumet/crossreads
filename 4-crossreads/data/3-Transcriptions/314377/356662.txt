<p>The bathing accomodation was very limited and it was doubly difficult for me to secure a decent bath on a/c of my disability. We had a library and by depositing 1/- we were able to secure a book</p>
<p>Discussions on various subjects were the rule &ndash; one of them being the work of the Y.M.C.A. They did not seem to like the Y.M.C.A. In an argument I tried to point out to them that a balance sheet was provided to show what was done with the collected money. The Salvation Army was more highly spoken of.</p>
