<p>Caves"- danger of shells at all times "Maggie's socks"- Big British Guns Active round about us. Leave trench at 6pm for Dressing Station-Great destruction of village - no inhabitants - our billet carries no Red Cross and our chaps no Brassards - owing to handiness of British Batteries to our Station  we are liable to German shells at all times.<br/>April 21st<br/>Good Friday no Buns, plenty of rain</p>
