
July 1917Boxing Tournament.  There were some very good fights
Thursday 19thTook over SQM Sergt from Halloran who is going Sergeant Major.  The job is fairly easy & I for one am not going to make it hard.
Friday 20thThis Quarter Master's job is too easy.  There is absolutely nothing to do for most of the day & one doesn't know what to do with oneself.  Besides the weather is too  hot to sleep or read.  In the cool of the evening went for a walk along the canal & on our return went to the pictures.
Saturday 21stGot some more men into camp, some from hospital & others from the School of Instruction.  It will make the duties much lighter.  Today was very hot fully 108 in the shade.  Applications were called for the Flying Corps.  I put in one, but don't expect anything will come of it.
