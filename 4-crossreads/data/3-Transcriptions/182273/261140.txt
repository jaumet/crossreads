
<p>June &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>23 SUN&nbsp; Fine day. Blowing hard</p>
<p>&amp; cool. Amm: going up.</p>
<p>Fairly quiet.</p>
<p>Preparing for stunt tonight for</p>
<p>Meteren. Writing.</p>
<p>24 MON &nbsp;Fine day. rain in aft &amp;</p>
<p>night. amm. going up.</p>
<p>Heard</p>
<p>boys had</p>
<p>took Meteren) Posted letters to Celie<sup>&radic;</sup></p>
<p>Else P. Gladys. C. Writing.</p>
<p>Heavy rain at night.</p>
<p>25 TUES &nbsp;Fine day. Amm: going</p>
<p>up. Posted letter to Julie<sup>&radic;</sup>.</p>
<p>Inspection by C.O. Preparing for</p>
<p>stunt. Italians doing well.</p>
<p>Quiet day &amp; night.</p>
<p>26 WED &nbsp;Fine day. cool. Rec.</p>
<p>letter from Amy<sup>&radic;</sup>, posted ans:</p>
<p>at once. Further success by</p>
<p>Italians. Quiet. Went to Renescure</p>
<p>at night. met Savage &amp; boys had</p>
<p>a good time. Home 11.PM</p>
