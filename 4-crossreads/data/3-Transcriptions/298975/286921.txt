<p>Finished at 9 AM today About midday we pulled in to the Wharf &amp; started coaling. At 2 PM we got of &amp; marched to the Town Hall about 2 miles. It simply poured &amp; we were mostely wet through when we got there. Went for a Tram ride to Toll Gate quite a pretty suburb. Had several Ricksha rides, &amp; several meals dear &amp; not very good Could not get into the only picture show we found. Ricksha back to the boat about 10 PM</p>
<p>Sunday 15th<br/>Marched to Town Hall &amp; back to boat in morning To Town Hall again at 3 PM dismissed for afternoon. Fine day</p>
