  
 Monday 8 March 1915 Letters posted to The Girls, Mollie, Dr Arnott, Miss Garran, & one written to Maggie. Busy about the hospital all day. Saw the horses in the stable.  Dardanells being bombarded by the British & French fleets, have been for several days. How history is being made. 
 Tuesday 9 March 1915 At No I Hospital, The Palace, Heliopolis for dinner 6.30 pm. Antimoly[?] at the Christian Brothers school at Fagabah. Went on from No I Hospital to the Camp at Heliopolis (Col Smith gave me a car to go in), called upon Captn Fiaschi, Col. Beeston, Col. Monash, Col. McGlynn [McGlinn]. Saw Colonel Burnage. Home 11-45 pm 
 Wednesday 10 March 1915 Wrote Roach, Fred Flowers, the girls, Fox Royal Artillery. About hospital all day. An Australian mail in, a letter from Senator Pearce to me, none from home.  Rode for one hour during the afternoon across the dessert
