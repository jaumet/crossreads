<p>August 1917</p>
<p>Thursday 2nd<br/>The heads still have the wind up over the shelling so we are receiving no patients<br/>Friday 3rd<br/>Another board today I got [indecipherable] again some talk of shifting the Hospital<br/>Saturday 4th<br/>Still hanging on here waiting for Fritz to shell again<br/>Sunday 5th<br/>Pay day &amp; the usual birthdays<br/>Monday 6th<br/>Some talk of stopping here as Fritz as not shelled again<br/>Tuesday 7th<br/>More rain got a good dosing.  Met some Aus. this evening<br/>Wednesday 8th<br/>Fritz gave us a good sally up with bombs all night</p>
