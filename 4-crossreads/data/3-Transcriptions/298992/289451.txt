<p>The Canal<br/>24.4.16<br/>My Dear Mother,<br/>Your letters are coming more regularly now, and I received another one that was written early in March. In none of the letters from home have you mentioned if you got any of the films that I have sent at various times, and they are more important than the photos really. I knew one lad that went home some months ago now, and I gave him a little box of negatives and several Egyptian coins, which he said he would post when he arrived at Sydney. Did you ever get any of those? There must have been close on 100 altogether. I have also posted them in letters several times, and as my letters are very seldom censored</p>
