<p>I rejoined my section.</p>
<p>That afternoon &ndash; the 6/9th &ndash; we all moved on to follow the 8th Bde who were keeping in contact with Fritz, but only went as far as a big copse near the village of Bouvincourt, where we settled down for an indefinite period.</p>
<p>Just after we got there a stray Hun arrived and I was told to question him. As the crowd were all there gaping at him, he got the wind up and I got stage fright so things did not go too well. However I got what news he had, and for my trouble was prac[tically]</p>
