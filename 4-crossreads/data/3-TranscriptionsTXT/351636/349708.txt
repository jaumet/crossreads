 Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers 92B. Pitt St. 13.4.16 
 Dear Mr. Layton, Mrs. Hablethwaite who brings this has got into debt while son in Camp &amp; wants a loan of &pound;20 which she will pay back at 5/- p wk at present may be able to pay back more per wk later on. Can you assist her in this? 
 Yours truly B. H. Bennie
