<p>6th day out<br/>We have had concerts &amp; sports since I wrote the foregoing.  We have very easy times on board ship.  We have divided the whole unit up into groups of 20 for feeding purposes &amp; each "mess" has its 2 orderlies permanent so we are very well fed &amp; the orderlies fetch our tucker, serve it out &amp; wash up &amp; we just walk up when the bugle goes, sit down &amp; order what we want &amp; then go back &amp; lie down &amp; read.  We held the sports on the upper promenade deck yesterday afternoon.  I got 2nd in the potato race &amp; Ellis &amp; I 2nd in the carry-your-chum race &amp; Ellis &amp; another chap 2nd in the wheel-barrow race.  Just as the sports ended, we had a bit</p>
