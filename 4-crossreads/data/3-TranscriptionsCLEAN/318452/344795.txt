  
 The trees have all lost their leaves & it does not look its best. I have a very nice room & the owner is very hospitable It is a great change after the noise of war no sound of it ever reaches us. The position is South of Boulogne & between it & Etaples about 43 miles from Etaples off the railway & about 6 miles from Samer. 
 Tuesday 20th Went round the grounds for a walk this morning & then wrote letters 
 Wed 21st Have not been out. British made a push near St Quentin  Successful
