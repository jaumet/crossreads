<p>19.11.15<br/>Fri 19th<br/>Put in this morning making improvements &amp; giving our home a general cleaning up; Went on duty from 1 oclock till 6 tonight: Riddett who returned from Lemnos a few days ago, &amp; Watson have been put on duty here, &amp; we are now working 4 hours on &amp; 12 off so have a much easier time; We have a good deal of running about working here running messages, there is one thing about it we know where anyone &amp; everyone of any importance is camped   A warboat &amp; a Monitor have been giving us a treat in rapid firing today; Our patrols  still go out every night &amp; the bombing on Walkers just as willing as ever during the night time, &amp; everything as quiet as ever here; One of our reinforcements was sentenced for a Field Court Martial today for malingering</p>
