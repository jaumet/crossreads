Monday April 1 The Turks at this time were practically on us in large numbers &amp; it was only the darkness which pulled us through. 3 of our boys were caught through going back for some gear. We rode on till we reach Esault situated half way in the hills of Moab &amp; had breakfast Bully &amp; Biscuit &amp; pushed on for Shunet Nimron arriving about 2 pm.
