
26 February 1915Friday
More rain last nightEverybody sleeping on deck come down about midnightWeather continues cool with light wind
1915 February 27Saturday
A lot of potatoes   &  onions stowed in the Aft hold right under our quarters begin to rot  &  stink (horrid.)A gang of men are put on hauling them out   &  dumping them overboard.The work is very slow   &  dirty.
