<p>The show finished at 9 pm and we all returned to our Van for supper and then turned in.</p>
<p>Sunday 26th</p>
<p>When I awoke at 8 am it had been snowing all night and every thing was covered. This being the first fall of snow for the winter.</p>
<p>It was now necessary for us to draw fresh rations, so two of us went over to the R.O.D. Control for a docket to be handed in at the Storeroom and soon we were issued rations for a further 24 hours, consisting bread, meat, butter, jam, rice, tea &amp; sugar and bacon, so we were O.K. for the day.</p>
