 1915 Feb. 22 Mon. Paid &pound;1-0-6 today. Up at 6.20 a.m. &ndash; bed fine and soft after sleeping in the sand.  Morning very cold &amp; heavy fog - mist away &amp; sun out about 9 a.m. During the morning had a ride round the camp lines on our bike. Rumours are about  there  that there will be an early move of Troops from Egypt &ndash; I hope we get a move on too. Had a hot bath &amp; shave in "our suite of rooms" upstairs before turning in.
