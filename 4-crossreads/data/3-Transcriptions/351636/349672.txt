<p>The Centre Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers<br/>4/11/15</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton,<br/>Could you give Mrs Taylor some help? She seems much in need of it.</p>
<p>She will explain her case.</p>
<p>I will make some inquiries at Barracks about her husband &ndash; He probably has gone back to Rabaul &amp; she has not <u>heard</u> had time to hear from him.</p>
<p>Believe me<br/>Yours sincerely<br/>E. M. Wallack</p>
