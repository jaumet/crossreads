
<p>Strayable the same day &amp; got a good billet in a farm. We had great feeds of Blackberries which were very plentiful, everyday found Scotty &amp; I having our usual tuck&nbsp;of them we even made puddings we also found out where spuds were plentiful the result was that we had potatoes for breakfast and dinner we did not stay here long &amp; we left Strayable&nbsp;for Bailleul Oct&nbsp;17<sup>th</sup>. Where we entrained&nbsp;for the Somme. it was raining hard all the time &amp; we got wet through [?] about we travelled in the usual cattle trucks &amp; I slept well in spite of the crowded way we were packed it rained all night next day found us at long[?] the rain had stopped we got tea at the Y.M.C.A. then&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
