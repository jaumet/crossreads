  
 Monday 6/5/18  Had a few casualties by premature explosion of big French Gun. 
 Tuesday 7/5/18  Letter from Doff- also Wrote Doff- Bn digging & reconnoitring. 
 Wednesday 8/5/18  More reconnoitring & digging also Training  wrote to Bas. 
 Thursday 9/5/18. Orders received to move again tomorrow 
 Friday 10/5/18. Moved to Fransvillers [Franvillers] arriving at 9 p.m. had a game of bridge 
 Saturday 11/5/18. Reconnoitring positions in Reserve & forward 
 Sunday 12/5/18  Enemy shelled village  two civilians killed.
