<p>February 1918<br/>Thursday 7</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Weather drizzly with a little wind<br/>Dugout making plenty of water<br/>Had another look around the "possie"<br/>Mills on O.P. Walker on liason<br/>Boys feeling pretty happy good issue of rum tonight <br/>Smithy still a teetotaller</p>
