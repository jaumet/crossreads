<p>not the best, but what there was of it was good, nice &amp; warm &amp; best of all we got a bonzer change of clean clothes, &amp; got rid of our chats, "hurrah".<br/>The other night the officers had a great spread, to celebrate Captain Jacobs "crown" he is a Major now, his promotion was very popular in the Battalion for he is well liked.<br/>Had to give evidence this evening on the Stokes case at the "Orderly room". McKillop went on leave today &amp; I sent one of my completed diaries with him.<br/>23rd  Our "Mess" closed last night &amp; we had a most enjoyable evening Young Hodges' was there from the 1st Pioneer Batt. he was one of the old original Battalion, &amp; the President made a pretty little speech in which</p>
