<p>[Printed letterhead]<br />H.M.A.S Australia.</p>
<p>To the Admiral of the Australian Fleet</p>
<p>In the name of the officials taken prisoner I have honour to protest against our being kept prisoner, in opposition to the common law of nations.</p>
<p>1&nbsp; We do not belong to the armed forces&nbsp; We were forbidden by the Governor to <s>employ</s> offer any armed resistance.</p>
<p>2&nbsp; We offered no resistance of any sort</p>
<p>3&nbsp; We were ordered to go to the Government, went there and were immediately seized and taken prisoners</p>
<p>4&nbsp; There is every chance of losses of private property.&nbsp; Yesterday as it is several things were stolen</p>
