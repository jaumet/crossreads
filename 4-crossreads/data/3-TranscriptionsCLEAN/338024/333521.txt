  
 Thurs. 9 Nov.  1916 Sighted land at 10-15 am.  Arrived Durban about 12 noon.  Allowed on shore 3. 45 pm and marched into Durban (1. 75 miles) and granted leave till 11pm 
 Frid. 10 Nov. 1916 Not finished coaling.  Granted leave from 10 am till 2 pm.  Sailed at 5-15 pm. 
 Sat 11 Nov. 1916 Very rough and keeping in sight of land.
