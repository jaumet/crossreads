<p>Following written in "Daily Light" book:-</p>
<p>"This little book to be sent to my Mother if I should be called before my Redeemer.</p>
<p>"My dear Mother,<br/>"Weep not for me.  I fell doing my Duty.  My trust has always been in God.  Kind love to all.  May God guide you and give you a calm and serene peace.</p>
<p>"No. 4019,<br/>"Jim Bell,<br/>"Your ever loving son."</p>
