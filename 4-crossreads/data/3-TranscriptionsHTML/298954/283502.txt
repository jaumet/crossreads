<p>Sun.<br/>5th Aug (cont.)<br/>We are in the locality where the Australian submarine AE 2 disappeared in 1914.   At abt 11 am this morning we were cruising slowly along when what looked very much like a periscope &amp; conning tower appeared just ahead.</p>
<p>The "Wolf" swerved quickly &amp; manoevred round &ndash; an old banana palm!</p>
<p>The plane rose again at about 4 pm;  at about 6 p.m. we were busy at tea when a rush took place on deck and the 'plane was hastily launched.   A wireless message had been intercepted from our intended prize.</p>
