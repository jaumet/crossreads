<p>April 15th<br/>3 months (13 weeks) since departing Sydney. March in afternoon and visit Erquinghem in evening.<br/>April 16th<br/>Sabbath- attended Mass at village of "Croix du Bac". Later written home Y.M.C.A. in afternoon. Gas Picket at night (6 hour shift).<br/>April 19th<br/>Happy day * letters from home Y.M.C.A. Gas Picket.</p>
