We had to cease &amp; put our gas masks on, &amp; as it continued to come over, the digging was stopped. We stayed in a trench till 2 a.m., &amp; then returned "home" to our dugouts. <br/>Sunday 9 June Usual thing again today. I wrote to Mother &amp; Elsie this afternoon. The weather turned cold, &amp; we had some rain this evening. Lumped a lot of shell cases from an old gun pozzy today, they will be sent back as salvage. Went out trench digging as usual at 10.30 p.m., &amp; came back at 2.30 a.m. without mishap. It is a queer game, there we were digging this new reserve line, &amp; shells soaring well over our heads both to &amp; from Fritz. Monday 10 June Slept till noon as per usual. At 2 p.m. our company went down to Corbie, &amp; had hot showers &amp; change of clothes, very nice &amp; refreshing the bath was too. We went to a postcard shop (the town is deserted &amp; shell torn) &amp; there were thousands &amp; thousands of very nice postcards &amp; c.
