
Sunday 15The country about here is made up of undulating downs dotted here & there with woods & villages & on the
Monday 16whole just now is very pretty, especially in the woods. I am debating within myself as to whether I should apply for a com & if augmenting my chances of being killed or wounded, at least being able to live decently & get out of this rotten life.
Thursday 19Still here doing nothing & enjoying books and fine weather. One book Ernest Maltravers by Lytton has impressed me very much.I have continued my walkings about the country & have found some
Friday 20very pleasant spots.This Commission business has a good hold of my mind
Sunday 22Visit the neighbouring town of Andover which is very slow & quiet
