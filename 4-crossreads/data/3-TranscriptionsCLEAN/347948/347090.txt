
5th  We were issued with our webbing equipment to-day.Medicine Ball Sports on Saloon deck at 10 a.m.At 11 a.m. we sighted on the horizon what looked to be a waterlogged sailing ship with one very long mast. There seemed to be the stump of another mast left standing and she was drifting about in a manner which showed her helplessness.Our escort raced over to see what was up and lay about 500 yds off it.
