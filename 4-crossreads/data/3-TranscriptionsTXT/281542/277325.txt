 Monday 12th July 1915 
 Burning hot day &ndash; calm sea &ndash; little breeze.  Stifling conditions aboard &ndash; nothing of moment, mess orderly. 
 Tuesday 13th July 1915 
 First sign of flying fish &ndash; huge schools all day.  Finals of "B" Co.'s boxing contests. 
 [Paragraph crossed out] 
 Wednesday 14th July 1915 
 Anniversary of establishment of French Republic &amp; Fall of the Bastile.  Half holiday on board.  Officers' dining saloon gaily bedecked with flags at night, &amp; champagne, beer &amp; cigars enjoyed with riot enthusiasm.  Troops also celebrated the event with &ndash; sour bread, marmalade, &amp; shadow tea.   Crossed the Equator about 7 or 8 p.m.   Nearing the equator, officers enjoyed a Father Neptune pageant
