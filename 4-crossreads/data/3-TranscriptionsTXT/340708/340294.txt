 Dec 1917 Thurs 6th Left this morning at 11 am got clear of the Harbour at 1 pm getting very warm but it is pleasant after the cold. Sun 9th Very warm we are in the West Indies   The sea is dotted with islands Thursday 13th Reached the Panama Canal at 5 am pulled up outside by the destroyers   The harbour is closed at night so we couldn't got in till daylight  The town of Colon is at this [end]    The Tahiti Manyanui Willockra came through with the 31 Reinfts.   The canal is a marvellous bit of work the boat is lifted 4 ft above sea level &amp; by [lock] &amp; the lowered down the other side
