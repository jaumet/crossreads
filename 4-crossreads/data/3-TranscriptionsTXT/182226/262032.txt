 Monday 10 January, 1916 
 We were cruising around all night &amp; ran into Alexandria at about 9.30 a.m. 
 Lay in the harbour all day, we are very anxious to get ashore which we won&#39;t do till tomorrow, we are lying near the entrance to the harbour &amp; have some fine scenery to look at.
