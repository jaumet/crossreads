<p>28<br/>Mon 3rd of Jan, Arrived at Ismailia &amp; pitched our own camp. The conditions here were very bad for we were cramped together in marquees the accommodation being quite inadequate for the demand.<br/>Our next camp was situated further west along the railway &amp; the conveniences here were much more satisfactory. But our training was much too severe after the hardships endured on the Peninsula. We got together a football team and played several matches</p>
