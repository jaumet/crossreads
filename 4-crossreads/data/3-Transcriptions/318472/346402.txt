<p>26 June 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>Made a start with another Draft of about 60 today &amp; got them through. The M.O. &amp; R.M.O. kept very busy all day.</p>
<p>1918 June 27<br/>Thursday<br/>Busy all day getting Draft through the Kit store &amp; now have them all complete for France Got through about 170 this week &amp; they are ready to go at any time.</p>
