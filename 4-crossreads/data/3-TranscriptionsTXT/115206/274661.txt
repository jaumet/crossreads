 April 1918 Monday 1 
 In Billets- Orville Went down to Santos last night , with boys, had a great time, had quiet a good series of manoeuvres during morning and afternoon.  Weather glorious At 2 am. this morning Fritz paid us a visit and dropped quite a considerable number of bombs around the district. After tea walked to Santos and had a feed with Ken Gunn.
