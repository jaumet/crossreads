 friday August 4.8.16 calmer to day Saturday. Aug 5.8.16 calm to day Sunday. Aug 6.8.16 Arrived in aden 6 A.M very hot morning went ashore in afternoon saw the camels very bad Beer had one drink. Monday Aug 7.8.16 Still at Aden went on shore in evening got 2 Pipes very hot had a motor ride out to the town and back, all natives very cute lot white Peigon on boat. Wrote home.
