<p>223<br/>here as well. The 3rd Brigade band gave us some music in the town square. I put a flower in my coat here but was ordered to take it out by Lieut Watson (who was killed a little later on).</p>
<p>At the time the band was playing in the square some Artillery recently returned were sitting in the gutter. They each had a bottle of beer and had "dressed them by the right". All bottles were in line. They would have a drink and a talk saluting others by the way.</p>
<p>After going through the preliminary practice we were marched back to Buire for a time. One day in June 1917 we were told to clean up our equipment and to brighten up the brass</p>
