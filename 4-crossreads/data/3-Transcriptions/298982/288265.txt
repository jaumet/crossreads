<p>17th heavy guns which made our fellows laugh at first. But one landing within 50 yds of their holes made the tune alter a little &amp; there were one or two pasty faces. I was asleep at the first &amp; thought it was our naval guns &amp; came out to see what was doing. Was not pleasantly surprised at the real facts. To be blown to cats-meat at short notice without firing a shot would be a little off.</p>
