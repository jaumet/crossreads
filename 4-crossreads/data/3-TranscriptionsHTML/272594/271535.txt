<p>ight &amp; I jolly soon got out of it, for I felt quite sick.  The bombardment continued all night long &amp; the air was full of screaming whistling roaring shells for our guns were tearing it in properly &amp; when morning broke we could see great spouts of dark coloured earth shooting into the air &amp; shrapnel lashing all over the ground in front of us, it was fairly hailing down, &amp; so it was on us it seemed as if both sides were revenging themselves</p>
