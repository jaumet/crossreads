<p>[[March-April, 1916.]<br/>Friday 31<br/>This trip is good, seems as if the weather was made to order, we are fast approaching Aden, mumps fashionable I am in the fashion, thank you<br/>Saturday April 1<br/>Sitting here on the hatchway smoking my G. B. D<br/>Thinking of friends in the southland far across the sea<br/>Sunday 2<br/>Holy Communion aboard ship, sighted a speck of land after dinner<br/>Fought a big chap from the 18th on Saturday never got hurt</p>
