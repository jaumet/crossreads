 Ammon is a dirty little place but I got the surprise of my life on going through it. 
 Just on the left as one goes through there is an almost perfect old Roman Ampitheatre.  The Columns &amp; peristyles of the entrance are still standing &amp; most of the Auditorium is complete.  Unfortunately a road now runs through what was the arena though some of that is also left.  In fact it is probably as good a representation as you would see anywhere &amp; probably the only one extant outside Italy. 
 I think we move tomorrow sometime.
