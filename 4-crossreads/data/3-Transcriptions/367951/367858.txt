<p>Beat i do not know what sort of weather you are having over their but here it would roast you in the day and in the night you cannot keep warm. Send me over the Truth and Saturdays paper now and then after you send one lot wait till i tell you to send more as i might not get them Beat their is no more news dont worry about me as i am in the pink of condition it is me that ought to worry about you but never mind i will soon be back.<br/>From your ever Loving True Husband Tom</p>
<p>Give my best Love to All Beat</p>
