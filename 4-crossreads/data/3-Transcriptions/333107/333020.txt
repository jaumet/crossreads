<p>Mon. Apr. 1st<br/>No mail in for me yet, I wonder where it has all gone to. Suppose mine went over to France, so Goodness knows when I'll get it, as things are.</p>
<p>Tues. 2<br/>Bill Beards came back from Leave at mid-day so now things are moving smoothly again.</p>
<p>The Germans are making a bold bid for Amiens, but our chaps are managing to hold him back.</p>
<p>Went up for classification. "Vow" gave me a thoro' overhauling and marked my card "Heart, hyper-tropical".</p>
