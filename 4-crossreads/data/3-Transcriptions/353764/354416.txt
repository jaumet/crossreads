<p>I could find &amp; finally landed them in the Ancient Egyptian Bazaar, which is one of the worst places in Cairo.  They all reckoned the S.M. had gone mad.<br/>However we got out O.K. &amp; after have some Refreshments at Saults, got a Taxi &amp; drove home, at the rate of close on 80 miles an hour.<br/> I often wondered how we would get on if a wheel came off.<br/>Our 4th Divisional Train is now forming &amp; in a week or so will be complete.</p>
<p>2nd 3.16 Thursday<br/>We were inspected by Major Holdsworth to-day who is the Divisional Train Commander &amp; he told us that we would shortly be on active Service in a strange Country, so he has left us guessing.  He expressed himself as being satisfied at our turnout.<br/>Moving to-morrow so am very busy.  Expecting draft of 105 men to-night</p>
<p>3.3.16. Friday<br/>105 fresh men came in last night &amp; I had a very busy time fixing them up.<br/> We shifted Camp to-day to a place called Luna Park, &amp; after a rather hard day have now settled down again</p>
