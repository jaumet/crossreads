<p>Sunday 8<br/>Return to London &amp; visit Mrs Caper <br/>Clapham Common</p>
<p>Monday 9<br/>Off to Birmingham tonight &amp; stay for the night at the Grand Hotel. Birmingham is a very busy place, but is made up of nearly all industrial houses. Some of the suburbs are very nice</p>
<p>Tuesday 10<br/>Walk round the Black Country which in the vastness of its works is a revelation to me. See Lea-Brook Wednesbury Bilston Smithwick &amp; have indeed an interesting time. <br/>"Catch of the Season" at the theatre.</p>
<p>Wednesday 11<br/>Back in London. Stay at Strand Palace Hotel.</p>
<p>Thursday 12<br/>At the Opera this evening. Tristram &amp; Islode at Drury Lane which is gorgeous. Go to camp at Perham Downs tomorrow.<br/>Tea with Mrs Capes, Clapham Com.</p>
<p>Friday 13<br/>In camp at Perham Downs. It is pretty rotten being back in camp after having the last two months in luxury comparatively.<br/>However things are not too bad &amp; the food is eatable.</p>
