  
 Thursday Nov 2nd:  Marched to Flers 15 miles. On Light Railway Fatigue Wednesday 8th:  Attached to 9.2 Battery. Running Ammunition Sunday 12th:  Relieved. Came back to Mametz Wednesday 15th:  Shifted to Montauban Saturday 18th:  In front line - Flers. Tuesday 21st:  Relieved by "Tommies" 9 p.m.  Came back to Carlton Camp. Wednesday 22nd:  Back to Ribemont Thursday 30th:  Marched 10 miles to Cardonette
