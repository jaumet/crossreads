<p>attack (dummy) : Bill Wad Bryant &amp; self caught by New &amp; had our names taken for insolence to them : The cold footed coot wants to go to the trenches &amp; do a bit of scrapping then he may learn how to speak to men.</p>
<p>Saturday July 21st</p>
<p>Bapaume : good day : home about 1.30 am &amp; up at 7 am : parade to M.O. at 10 am : stroll after tea through Sapignies &amp; round the block home</p>
<p>Sunday July 22nd</p>
<p>Bapaume : good day : reveille 7 am : Holy Comm. 9.30 am &amp; was well attended : fell in 7 pm in battle order for a practice "stunt" : marched to near Bancourt &amp; bivouaced : tea at midnight :</p>
<p>Monday July 23rd</p>
<p>Bapaume : good day : started the "stunt" 2.30 am : a "dinkum" stunt was on up at the line : the guns</p>
