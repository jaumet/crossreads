<p>Monday 1/1/17<br/>Major Read 28th Bn reported in lieu of Bridges sick. Work progressing well.<br/>Tuesday 2/1/17</p>
<p>Bridges evacuated &ndash; things going well.</p>
<p>Wednesday 3/1/17</p>
<p>Route march in Marching Order through Daours. Afternoon (holidays) went over to site for Rifle Range.</p>
<p>Thursday 4/1/17</p>
<p>Wisdom arrived with Murphy. I left for front line to see 26th Bn.</p>
<p>Friday 5/1/17</p>
<p>Spent day with Battn at Pommieres stayed night at Bernafy Wood.</p>
<p>Saturday 6/1/17</p>
<p>Returned to Bussy at midday &ndash; Wisdom &amp; Murphy left.</p>
