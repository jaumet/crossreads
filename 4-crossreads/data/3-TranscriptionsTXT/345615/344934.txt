 All our kit bags went into store 3 days ago &amp; that same night our No 4 Coy. left by Rail to the  nearest  Railway Station nearest the scene of trouble. There is only a single line narrow guage railway so troops cannot be moved quickly or in quantity 
 Today No. 3 Coy struck tents &amp; took them in to Ordnance Stores &amp; are bivouacing tonight &ndash; ready to move off tomorrow &ndash; so our turn will evidently come in a few days. 
 So at present we are 'standing by' with full marching order kit &ndash; that is our equipment &amp; uniform &amp; clothes
