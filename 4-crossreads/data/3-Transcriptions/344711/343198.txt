<p>4th<br/>Mess orderly.  24 hours spell from the firing line.</p>
<p>5th<br/>Back in firing line.  Heavy bombardment this morning.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Fatigue, digging trench deeper.  In support trench at night.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Observation day and night.</p>
<p>8th<br/>Water fatigue.</p>
<p>9th<br/>A bit off-colour.  Observation night.</p>
<p>10th<br/>In reserve trenches.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Firing line.  Very dusty in trench.</p>
<p>12th<br/>On water guard.  Good easy job.  Poor Bonner, of our troop, fatally wounded.  Otherwise a day of rejoicing, for our mail turned up.</p>
<p>13th<br/>Firing line, &amp; observation night.  Issued with bread for the first time for over a month, and we enjoyed it immensely.</p>
<p>14th<br/>Observation.  Sgts. Collins and Lamborn both wounded at once.  JAPANESE [indecipherable].</p>
