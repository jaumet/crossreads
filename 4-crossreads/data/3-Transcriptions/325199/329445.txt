<p>46 Private John D. Wilson<br/>No. 46.<br/>Head Quarters (Pioneers.)<br/>37. Battalion<br/>10 Inf Brigade<br/>Australian Imperial Force<br/>On Active Service</p>
<p>June 2, 1916<br/>Friday evening June 2/1916 we packed our kit bags in readiness to march into Seymour in the morning to catch a special train to Port Melbourne and thence march on board the "Persic" enroute to England. J.D. Wilson. Seymour Camp.</p>
