<p>August 1915<br/>[Note in margin:] Reported Capt Luther killed Thursday<br/>26th &ndash; Duty 8AM. Had a visit from Dave. He had been off colour &amp; in rest camp. Brought <u>Register</u> with him. Went back as far as our camp. Gave him razor, cholera belt etc. &amp; couple papers. Also scored bottle bovril &amp; two tins meat extract from panniers. Commenced writing long letter to Molly. Intended give same to Hammond to post in Egypt. Turks attack reported to be at Lone Pine &amp; MGs from Walker's playing on them. Destroyer also busy firing on Walker's. Suspect something doing at Walkers Red &amp; Green lights visible.<br/>27th &ndash; Got our canteen stuff. Not a bad lot. Managed to score a pkt of Candles 6d a lb, tin tooth powder 6d &amp; tin Cafe au Lait 1/- 4 tins chicken &amp; ham pate 1 &frac14;  each. Went up to see Dave for few minutes. No further word of them being relieved, so far. Beachy Bill firing at a transport which came close in, as usual rotten shooting. Imshi T'port. Destroyers (2) &amp; a cruiser bombarding positions on left &amp; about<br/>27th &ndash; 4pm heavy rifle &amp; MG. fire started &amp; continued during night. Dave came over. Gave him some Anchovy paste, Cafe Au lait etc. Duty at 8pm. A.D.M.S. enquiring if we have 2 Bearers &amp; tent sectns with us Got a bulletin &amp; Mail yesty.<br/>[Note in margin:] Saturday<br/>28th &ndash; Got tin of salmon, potted meat, &amp; few cigars this morning. The W.O &amp; Bob T went out on left wing. Reported that Conn. Rangers assisted by Austns. Etc took 200 yds of new territory previous day. Conn Rangers too a trench, lost &amp; recaptured it. Did not attain their objective. Too many guns <strike>etc</strike> Turks etc in front of them 15 Btn again in it. Not feeling too good. Anzac complaint. <strike>29th</strike> Horsley &amp; Power sent to base. Power suffering from ulcer on liver. Piece of 75 shell hit plank sitting on.<br/>29th &ndash; On Dressing stn 8AM. Very quiet except for 75's which seemed more sudden than ever. Caught a chap near 2nd Bde H.Q. blew him to pieces. Peninsula press said Germany asking Bulgaria what her attitude would be in event of her wanting send army</p>
