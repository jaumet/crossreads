 25 September 1916 Monday 
 Cart, Bob, self, Jockey, Mac, Tony &amp; Bill Parsons get our phizogs taken in group in Pop. Tea after, &amp; get it cooked by waitress. Cart, Bob &amp; self back early carrying sponge cake for trifle for banquet tomorrow night. Glee Club practice. Soldiers' Chorus, Little Tommy went a-fish &amp; Dear little Shamrock. Views of 2 Zepps destroyed in England.
