
<p>on 10th Dec</p>
<p>___________</p>
<p>We left rest camp in Motor Vans for front Line.&nbsp; Got off at [?] Hill a dreary spot 10 kilometre from that most pathetic heap of ruins that represents the once flourishing town of Epres.&nbsp; All night long the angry mutter of the guns on this the most dangerous salient on the Western front kept up a hideous <s>Lul</s> melody surely the most grim</p>
