<p>Sept 4th &ndash; 17<br/>Today we returned to our unit at Reninghelst prior to going back to the rest area for a few days. I would much rather have remained where we were as here in Reninghelst every night is one long weary wait of suspense on account of Fritz's bombs.</p>
<p>I must say that shells are not so bad but bombs, Gee! they claim a lot more respect on account of their sudden descent</p>
