<p>Monday 15 November 1915&nbsp;<br />Wrote to Gen. Ford telling him that if Col. Martin is agreeable, I am willing to take up my old position at No. 2 A. G. Hospital. Letting byegones be byegones&nbsp;<br />In Cairo during the morning.<br />A&nbsp;walk round Heliopolis between 5 &amp; 6-30 pm.<br />Writing &amp; reading during rest of the day.<br />Met Father McAuliffe from Anzac, down stairs at 6.30 p.m.</p>
<p>Tuesday 16 November 1915&nbsp;<br />In operating room during the morning&nbsp;<br />Writing &amp; reading&nbsp;<br />Cairo during the afternoon&nbsp;Tram ride to Old Cairo&nbsp;4 Millimes for one hours ride.<br />Looking at Xray pictures after dinner with two medicoes in my room. Wrote Girls.<br />Received cable from girls dated 5-11-15</p>
<p>Wednesday 17 November 1915&nbsp;<br />Reading &amp; writing all day.&nbsp;<br />In Cairo during the afternoon&nbsp;<br />Had afternoon tea with Mrs Bacon.<br />Posted letters to Girls and Hyman.</p>
