  
 June 16th 22 miles, a very hard day on hardly any food & brakish water no villages & road frightfully rough.  We got down into a valley & followed a river which we had to ford by jumping on to the loaded mules.  Many came off when in midstream.  We crossed further on over a fine stone bridge apparently very ancient.  Food again is very scarce & we are forced to make one meal spread over three. 
 June 17th 24 miles.  Water very scarce & it's frightfully hot reduced to sucking a stone to keep my mouth dry.  Arrived at the first inhabited village & were able to get some grub including a sheep so had a good feed at night.
