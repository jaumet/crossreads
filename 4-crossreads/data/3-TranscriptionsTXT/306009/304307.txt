1915May. 1 Sat.This morning had my first swim in the Baths at Mena House followed by a sun bake &ndash; splendiferous!In the afternoon was lucky enough to get a few hours off for a run into Cairo &ndash; had haircut, watch mended etc.The rush of wounded patients was continued to-day &amp; motor ambulances brought in another 230.
