 18 January 1918 Friday Speaking about Venus I was watching our Shells bursting away two Taubes when the star caught my eye- at midday!!!   I hope you are all in Melbourne now at Mrs Jack Austins  Moved on to   RICHON-LE-ZION   which means the new Zion- not far from  Jaffa.  This town the Jews are shorter for food- NO SUGAR, TEA or COFFEE- Tea is made out of dried figs &amp; sweetened with JAM-   Coffee-burnt MAIZE.
