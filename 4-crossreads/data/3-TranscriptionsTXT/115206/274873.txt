 October 1918. Wednesday 30 
 Hosptl. Birmingham Last night the Yank and Pedderson were taken to the tents. Inspection by some long streak of a Capt. Played  Brad draughts and cards all day. Have come to the final conclusion that the biggest wag and best sport on night duty is Nurse Macdonald &ndash; an Irish nurse.
