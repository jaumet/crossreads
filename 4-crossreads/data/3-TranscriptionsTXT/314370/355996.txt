 March, 1916 Tuesday 14 Care &amp; Cleaning exam took up to dinner time After dinner we carried on as per syllabus Weather very hot during daytime 
 Wednesday 15 Work as usual nothing to disturb the peace After tea I went to Heliopolis to put my watch in for repairs Met an old chum &amp; had a long talk over old times (Fred Williams)
