
Thursday 12 August 1915 Bairam festival in Cairo today The Mohammadams in their best clothes in the streets. In Cairo for one hour between 5-30 & 6-30 p.m. Sent a cable message to the girls, A telegram to Weston, Orsova, Port Said 
Friday 13 August 1915 About hospital all day. Letter from Carrie & Mrs Knowles. 
Saturday 14 August 1915 Everything quiet. Hospital work going smoothly. 
Sunday 15 August 1915 At the Basilica for Mass at 9-30 a.m. At the African Mission Convent, Zeitoun, at 7 p.m. for Benediction. Wrote Knowles, Girls, Mollie.
