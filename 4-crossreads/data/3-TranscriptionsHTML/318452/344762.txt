<p>Have been writing letters of sympathy all day.</p>
<p>Saturday 29.<br/>Nothing of importance.</p>
<p>Sunday 30th September We are under orders to leave this afternoon We had a service this morning I spoke on "A Man shall be as the Shadow of a Great rock in a weary land".<br/>At I oclock we moved off to catch busses on the main road.<br/>After a wait of 2 hours the busses came. Owing to the dry weather the roads were terribly dusty &amp; we were smothered in dust. It took about 30 big busses to carry us We left the busses at Dikebush &amp; marched about 32 miles to</p>
