<p>February 1918<br/>Sunday 10</p>
<p>Messines <br/>Spent a very quiet day mostly on the phone. Fritz put a few cinq.neuf over.<br/>Weather very unsettled. Hear there is a raid coming off tonight. Our guns were cutting wire this afternoon. <br/>Was on 4 to 6 this morning. Went down to canteen and heard the 15th were at Spy Farm, and the 8th were at Daylight Corner.</p>
