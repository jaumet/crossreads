<p>Friday 14th Feb  Rumours of our departure today so packed my gear ready arranging my sea kit bag with straps as a pack.  Immediately after tea we fell in ready to march off and were checked over a couple of times &amp; then given a ticket for the boat.  This took about 1&frac12; hours after which we marched to the boat about 5 miles through mud and slush, the thaw having set in.  The boat, a paddlewheel, left about 8.30 making a remarkable easy trip &amp; anchored off Weymouth about 5 a.m. &amp; we</p>
