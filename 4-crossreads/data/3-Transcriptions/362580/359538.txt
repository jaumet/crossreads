<p>the Lake to the Canal Sports.  Saw some great diving &amp; obstacle races in the water.  The men are like a lot of fish in the water.  We got on to a big ship from our launch &amp; I know ever so many of the officers &ndash; at last we left the crowd &amp; returned to our own launch, arriving at the pier about 5.45 p.m.  Walked to The Club with my boy &amp; a major &ndash; had afternoon tea, then left</p>
