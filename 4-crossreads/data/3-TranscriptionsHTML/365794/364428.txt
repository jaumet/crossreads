<p>Sunday, 28 January 1917 [1918]<br/>Arrived Luxor about 8.20 a.m. (I fancy), tea &amp; B. &amp; butter for breakfast then on again to Assouan [Aswan] where we arrive 4.30 p.m. &amp; stay at Grand Hotel situated on bank of the Nile &amp; Oh, such a glorious view &amp; sunset.<br/>Tea at once then S. Hills &amp; I hire a boat &amp; out on the river for a row &amp; sail.  Dinner &amp; the room is pretty &amp; beautiful roses out.  Mess dress makes a bright spot &amp; with the khaki of the men of the party (about 5 or6) we are a big crowd of warriers.  After dinner out in the boat &amp; so peaceful &amp; lovely away behind Elephantine Island &amp; so home to my room 3 floors up &amp; such a view &amp; fresh breeze blowing all night.</p>
