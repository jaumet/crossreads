  
 Le Havre [September 1918] 8 Sun At church 9 Mon With my rifle on my shoulder faith theres no man could be bolder 10 Tues Left Roulles yesterday midday still in the train, arrived at Bray for dinner then Marched 11 Wed 15 kilos barefooted along the river Somme to the battalion at the Village of "Frize"
