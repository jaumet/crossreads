
January, 1916Wednesday 12Mum's BirthdayAfter the mornings work was over I went onto Cairo to try to get some Bostock Books the afternoon being a holidayBut owing to the wreck of the "Persia" these books are not available.Everything they cannot find any other excuse for they blame on to the "persia"
Thursday 13Very dull & signs of rainWork as usualGo with dad over to L.H. lines & score a pair of leggings.The 1st L.H. Regt are to ride to Wardan starting at daybreak tomorrow
