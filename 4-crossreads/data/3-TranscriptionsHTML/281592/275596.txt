<p>270 miles to-day.</p>
<p>Church Parade, a Bright Service.  Had a ration of "lime juice".</p>
<p>Jan 4th  Phy Ex.  F.</p>
<p>F. drawing up boxes of rotten potatoes.  The smell from them was something awful and were tipped over the side of the ship.</p>
<p>283 miles today, 572 miles from Aden.</p>
<p>Passed two islands about six o'clock this evening.  They rose like sheer masses of rock out of the sea to a great height and looked as level as a table top.  Got our photo taken by one of the officers this evening Col. Sp..</p>
<p>Jan 6th Tue.  Phy Ex</p>
<p>The coast of Africa was in sight when we rose this moring.  The dawn breaks about 6 am.  Read the "Kyarra Truth" this morning.  It is becoming too personel and the editors may get into trouble if they are not careful.  Met in with a chap from W.A. who previously belonged to Ayrshire.  There are quite a number of Scotties on the ship.</p>
<p>Jan 6th Wed.  M.O. F.</p>
