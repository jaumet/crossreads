 A family of Coli Snakes infest its roots. We got one of them 18" long with fangs like a cat. Saw the Boa -- 34 feet long but they say he's only a small one. They are considered common. Went out to the observatory & saw sev. snakes in boxes. Also chased a monk. Saw big hairy face peering from Gloom of bush. Joe flung a stick at him & then went away, also me. A Baboon, Coast Ape(?), big as they grow & the terror of the nigs! Up the track two Queensland lads had roused one up in the crown of a palm & were shying at him. We went along to see what was up. The old monk got annoyed showed signs of coming down so we left there also nig guide
