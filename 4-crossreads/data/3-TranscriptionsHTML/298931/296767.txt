<p>Friday 16<br/>3 weeks to day we landed in France so far so good<br/>Saturday 17<br/>Nothing to do only read and write slow very slow, but better than being fast and furious<br/>Sunday 18<br/>Good old Sunday tis the same as Monday or Tuesday to us but they dont work on Sundays in Stralia<br/>MEMORIES<br/>of many things to numerous to mention<br/>Feeling very lonely with a greasy smile</p>
