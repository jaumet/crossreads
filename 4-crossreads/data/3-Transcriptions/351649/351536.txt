<p>it will be then before I get get away from here goodness only knows but it will take months probably to disperse our armies.</p>
<p>What a rotten season this apparently continues to be in Australia:  according to what I know, there should be little if any green feed at Hay yet &amp; past middle of June.</p>
<p>I sent one of my photographs out for you:  posted them all to Brent who was to deal them out.  I own up to being flattered.</p>
<p>By the way I meant to say that none of the rustic French people who live near the lines carry helmets against gas:  they must get settled alright if there is a gas attack, though against our soldiery now gas is a failure, though always to be very much feared.</p>
