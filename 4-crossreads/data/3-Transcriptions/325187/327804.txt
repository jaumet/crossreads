<p>[28 February 1916 Monday]</p>
<p>Todays Patrol was along miles of Cotton crop just irrigated &amp; Beans (Broad beans) quite 2 miles of them- used as cattle feed later on.<br/>We fill our Nosebags after the horses have had their feed<br/>Tried to get a c/- of Johnnie Walker from Cairo, but with so many soldiers about failed again- Stolen-  When the war is over some Hero's !!! will find it hard to be honest again.<br/>Bridge of Pontoons nearly finished across the Canal here so we need no longer share the Punt with Camels Donks, Water Buffaloe &amp; Sore eyed kids.</p>
