<p>Aug 1st &ndash; 18<br/>Since last date of entry there has been very little of interest to relate until this morning when we were ordered to prepare for another journey down South on the morrow.</p>
<p>With this news I could see faint visions of my impatiently waited Blighty leave which should have come any day. But now good only knows when it will come I don't.</p>
