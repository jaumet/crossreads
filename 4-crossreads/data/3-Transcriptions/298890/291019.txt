<p>8 Thursday<br/>Cold &amp; grey.  Cooked all day at Minerva.  Ankles bad by 5.30 but still got thru  day and feeling much bucked thereby.</p>
<p>9 Friday<br/>Fine day.  Waited &amp; cooked at Minerva &amp; got thru all right  considerably bucked thereby.  Heels &amp; feet hurting</p>
<p>10 Saturday<br/>Pottered &amp; slept.  In afternoon Mrs. Holmes came &amp; after supper we all went to call on Evelyn Isilt in Moscow Court.  No rain.</p>
