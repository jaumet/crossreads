 We had a fine concert in the Main Hall here last night. There were only four performers and the pianist but they gave us a splendid two hours performance. There were two ladies &amp; two men. Both the ladies, a soprano and a contralto had beautiful voices and were encored again &amp; again. Among the songs they gave us were "My Ain Folk" "A Perfect Day" "Tosti's Good Bye" "I know a Lovely Garden" "A May Morning" and a selection from "La Boheme" Also a duet "A Bad Boy &amp; a Good Girl"
