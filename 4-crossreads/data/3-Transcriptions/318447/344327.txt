<p>&amp; came straight on to Lud, chiefly interesting for a fine old Greek Church containing the tomb of St George. Here two troops of the 1st Reg charged into the place &amp; <strike>found</strike>took 309 prisoners in a bunch &amp; 4 MGs, with only three casualties.</p>
<p>I suppose tomorrow we shall investigate Jaffa. Noone seems to know yet whether the Turk intends to hold it or not.</p>
