<p>11 June 1916<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Fourth Sunday in Etaples Camp. Church parade as usual at 9. 30 a.m. Chaplain's address on Lord Kitchener. Two chops play cards during service, sacrilege. Write letters to Bess Haas &amp; post cards to Nell, Jessie Mon. Jack &amp;amp Arch.<br/>Bob Tony &amp;amp self go for walk to Camiers, blocked by military police. Same old story. Anzacs barred. French peasant, chicory. Rain, shelter in dug out. Tony receives parcel from England. Cake &amp;amp apples. Former crushed but nice.</p>
