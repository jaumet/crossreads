<p>2-4-15,  Good Friday<br/>Beautiful morning<br/>We ran into three rain storms today &amp; what a grand sight when we were going towards them. very close to night.</p>
<p>3-4-15<br/>There was not a ripple on the <strike>water</strike> sea this morning <strike>the sea</strike> is it like a mill pond which stopped like it all day. What a beautiful sunset this evening.<br/>Crossed the Equator this day, <strike>mid</strike> about 9 Oclock</p>
