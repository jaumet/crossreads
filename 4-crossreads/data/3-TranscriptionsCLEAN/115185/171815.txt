  
 28 - Jeanne - Neville Rich sent them a German shell picked up on battlefield. They are devoted to him. He's signalling now they say. I also saw Aunt Jessie & Uncle Charles en route to Harrowgate from Portishead & Gibson McMillan & Cecily. Denis is a dear & brought Kitty Maxwell to see me at the [indecipherable] flat. I've promised to go to Mrs Maxwell's to see the baby if I have time. But the getting about is the trouble. B.
