<p>November 1st 1917.<br/>1 man proceeded on leave to the United Kingdom.  Period 14 days leave.  64 N.C.O.'s and men baked 29080 lb rations.</p>
<p>2/11/17.<br/>1 man proceeded on leave to U.K. 64 N.C.O.'s and men baked 31,996 lb rations.</p>
<p>3/11/17.<br/>2 men proceeded on leave to U.K.  64 N.C.O.'s and men baked 32,300 lb rations.</p>
<p>3/11/17<br/>Discipline 2 convictions of offences.  1 case of drunkenness, and the other A.W.L. from duty and insolence to his superior officer.  Awards.  Deprived of (14) fourteen days' pay and 21 days No 1 F.P. respectively.</p>
<p>4/11/17.<br/>1 man evacuated to hospital. 1 to 21 days. F.P. No.I.  43 N.C.O.'s and men baked 23,340 lb rations.</p>
<p>5/11/17.<br/>64  N.C.O.'s and men baked 36,150 lb rations</p>
