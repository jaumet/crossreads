<p>F [14/1/17 Sun] Wrote to Jennie &amp; read Book. Lone Marie<br/>Slept in bed all day</p>
<p>F [15/1/17 Mon] Recd letter from H. Hay. 2 Bea Mum, France, Chris, Cards from Queen &amp; Mrs Mcl.<br/>Walked to Village of Millstone. Very small Kirk &amp; mill few stones &amp; bricks all that was left of Poet Addison's Home in Vicar's Garden.<br/>F [16/1/17 Tues] Recd letter from George &amp; lovely Card from Lily<br/>Walked to Fighledean. Saw many aeroplanes. Village Black Smiths Tree &amp; grave. Old stone images in church garden Good tea &amp; walked the plank. Had to see Colonel about absence from Pay.</p>
<p>F [17/1/17 Wed] Wrote to Mum, George, Lil Bess. Queen. D. Nin dreams He marries L.R.M. [The original writing is M.R.L who is Nicholls girlfriend and it seems he changed the letters around]<br/>Nothing Doing.</p>
<p>F [18/1/17 Thurs] Wrote to M.R.L (5) Recd. letter from M.R.L Walked to Newton Toney with Dave, Percy &amp; Drake. Very tired.</p>
<p>ICE [19/1/17 Fri] Recd. letter from Jennie &amp; H Hey. Card. Stayed in bed all day.</p>
<p>ICE [20/1/17 Sat] Recd. letter from Roy &amp; Bess. Photo of Dad &amp; Mum &amp; Self on Steps. Sent Book of Aust. Birds &amp; flowers &amp; 2 page letter to M.R.L. Go for a walk with Nin round the Camps.</p>
