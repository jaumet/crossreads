<p>May, 1915<br/>Saturday 15<br/>Letter from Joe 13/4/15 &ndash; W'bool<br/>   "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Kathleen 28/3/15 so you see how erratic the Mails are probably some of yours have gone on to the Regiment.<br/>Myra Nyse very little better has been transferred to Ghezirah Palace Hospital on the Nile &amp; is to be sent to Australia poor girl she has had a very bad time. Sincerely hope Joes' eye gets no worse, note he has sold [indecipherable] when rain does come all classes of stock will be very dear. Thank Kathleen for her letter &amp; give Aunt Louie my love.</p>
