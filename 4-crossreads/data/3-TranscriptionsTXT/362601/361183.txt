
3 April, Thursday
F.French draft in again. C.O. blows me up about Extension Report. Go to English & Book-Keeping Class & come down to write letters.
Afterwards, I go to bed at 10 pm & do all night about W. at S. Hill. I am then fatigue.
Wrote Miss Scroggins, Dad & Auntie Annie. Sent Brooches & Coins to Auntie.
4 April, Friday
F. & Hot
Go shopping & to take my washing. Go to Dickens Lecture & is very good. Meet Cal Slater, come back to office & write & study. Do some English later on.
Wrote M.R.L. & Mais. Carter.
