I stopped before we got that far forward but the acrid smell of explosive smoke was pungent. Relieved Colonel Forbes with just on 300 men - the weakest I have ever yet gone into the line. A fine  dugout lined with white sheeting and plenty of good furniture from Villers Brettoneux. The walls are covered with erotic pictures from La Vie Parisienne. My headquarters this time in consists of Stewart Harricks, McCartin, Hughes, & Harricks all first class chaps. It was well after midnight when I was able to sit down and to write this record of events of what has been an intensely busy day
