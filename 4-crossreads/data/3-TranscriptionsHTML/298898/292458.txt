<p>on with sick &amp; wounded as well as unpack &amp; arrange our gear etc. By the end of the day we had everything in apple pie order altho' I found many very necessary things very short. We have several huts to work in with a few dugouts inhabited by a few of the personnel. Found the roads very fair altho' very soupy, still the 3 days rest from traffic insisted upon from commencement of the thaw saves them from being cut up.</p>
<p>Sunday 25th Feb.<br/>Anniversary of this units formation at Tel-el-Kebir and we had arranged festivities to commemorate the event at "Buire" which however was knocked on the head by this move Menus &amp; programmes had been printed Guard's band engaged to play during the afternoon &amp; a good dinner prepared all of which had to be given up. Had a fairly busy day with dressings</p>
