
We roamed round town most of the day enjoying ourselves by looking at the shops & buying some Souvenirs. In the evening visited Theatre.Wednesday May 29Went out to City Bish & St Stephens in double decker tram. The suburbs of Alexandria are very pretty & we saw some fine gardens. Would not mind spending a few months here. We passed the Racecourse & Casino. Evening we all had a night out. The trams of Alexandria are similar to those in Cairo. Small & uncomfortable.
