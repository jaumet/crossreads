 May 1st (5) Arrived KARLSRUHE 2 am &amp; after an hours wait in a Red Cross Room where we had a cup of tea, we were put on an ambulance &amp; arrived our Hospital 3.30 am. Had a few hours sleep. Received great attention all day today. There are 6 of us in a room, more particulars taken, splendid dinner &amp; tea. 
 Leg dressed after tea, foot washed for first time. Leg received great attention. Pieces of Bone or Shrapnel extracted. Leg put in an iron splint &amp; it has to be kept in one position all the time. It is very tiring &amp; limbs get sore. Had injection Morphia. 
 May 2nd Had little sleep last night, leg painful. I am not used to lying in the one position, but will soon settle down.  Beef  Cup cocoa Breakfast. Beef tea 11 am &ndash; Issue of Honey daily. R.C. Priest in to see us &amp; had a yarn. 
 Russian Orderly attends us. We have a Sister also, she is very good &amp; a hard case &ndash; nothing a trouble. We can write as many letters as we like, &amp; they are posted of a Monday. Splendid meals here. Doctors say I will be on my back for 3 months. Very quiet here &amp; a glorious climate. Wrote letter to Kate Had morphia tablet in my soup &ndash; awful taste &ndash; slept a little.
