  
 F 1 September Sun Go to Sarum. Go to [indecipherable]  E and meet [Mrs Gon?]. Go to Close. Meet Cpl. Weymouth at C.C. I have night chat with C. Lancashire lass & Cpl W. Go back to Mr E. Meet Tom at 6 mile post. Supper at Amesbury YMCA. Hang on back of Amesbury. Bus on way in. 
 F 2 Mon Long hard day. Go to To-nights the Night. Front Row. It is lovely. I get very [indecipherable] over it. Go & play Billiards afterwards & get badly beaten. We T & I talk confidentially. Ellis  goes B [indecipherable]. 
 F 3 Tues. Send Parcel to MRL & to M [Geyser?]. Recd. Parcel from Mrs Clarke. Letter from Roy. Take half-holiday & fix up my kits Wash all my clothes & write PC's. D. E. & I play Cards Wrote Dad. Mum. George & Marie 
 F 4 Wed Recd letters from Maisie Carter. Esme & Alice. Meth. Darn socks at night & waste rest of time at Billiards. Sibby is happy. Dream of [indecipherable] party. Sit on Knee. Dream of Pond for Coffins & [indecipherable] to go in airman in Trinity church yard of [indecipherable] again playing Piano. 
 W 5 Th Very dopey day.&I'm L-sick or H-sick. Recd 2 letters from Home & photo of Georgie's skiff. Ernie. [indecipherable]  send off. Recd letter from Mr & Mrs Gregson. Bluey Joy comes over - Wrote to M.R.L. (6 PPs) 
 F&W 6 Fri Met Bob Simpson & Harry Johns. Also Eugene / [Boxer?]. We all play cards at night. I am very dopey & tired Go up to impromptu concert at the mess. Write to Peg R 
 F 7 Sat Sports We go at 10 am. I run 3 & Parky 2 in 440. In 60 seconds. Home for Dinner. Run in Final. No good. We run 2nd in [Rily?] & get a prize but do not know about it. Come Home feeling O.K. I lie down & we play cards afterwards. We have Tea on the Ground but the Sports are [indecipherable] seen. We have Aussie Girl from A.M.T.S for lunch & Tea It is quick & leave her again. I hurt my toe in the [sprint?] shoes.
