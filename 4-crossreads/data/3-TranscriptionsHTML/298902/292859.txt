It was an awful trip, taking 5 hours to do 15 miles &amp; most of the time we were choking with smoke endured for the sake of the warmth.  Eventually we reached Charleroi, detraining outside at Marcinelle  which left a march of 5 kilos to the camp.  Had struggled along with heavy gear through snow for part of the distance when our Ford passed &amp; he gave 3 of us a lift which made things more pleasant.  We spent the night in a huge shed &amp; after gathering some boards to sleep on spent a very fair night.</p>
