
<p>M<u>em</u>o - The weather has been grand all this week past. Warm sunny days &amp; cool nights - Fritz is pushing on the French front -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>June 2<u><sup>nd</sup></u>&nbsp;&nbsp;Sunday - We worked on the lorry till 12 noon - had dinner &amp; went down for a swim with Jno. stayed in the water two hours swimming &amp; diving. We returned &amp; had tea &amp; in the evening&nbsp;Jno introduced me to some French people he had got to know &amp; he, Bluey &amp; I took (or went with) three madamoiselles to Church &amp; afterwards went home with them &amp; talked till midnight</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Jue 3<u><sup>rd</sup></u>&nbsp;&nbsp;We worked on the bus all day. Jno &amp; I went down for a swim at noon. I had a letter from Marion in which she mentioned the Etaples bomming incident when Fritz bombed the hospitals there heavily, killing nurses &amp; patients etc. Marion was sent to another unit &nbsp;in England a few days before the incident. No 1 Canadian Gen (her late unit) was one which suffered a number of casualties.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>June 4<u><sup>th</sup></u>&nbsp;&nbsp;We worked on the lorry all day breaking off at noon for a swim with Jno they are very long winded with my engine</p>
