<p>3.<br/>get home quickly and the school work continues satisfactorily, things ought to pan out alright.</p>
<p>Am at present entering upon the stage of demobilising stores and up to date things have happened more smoothly than I had hoped.</p>
<p>I have got a camera over from England &amp; have taken dozens of photos but the place does not lend itself much to photography.</p>
<p>Some rather unpleasant excitement this morning. One of the French boys at the farm was bitten by a dog a few months ago &amp; developed rabies Today he had a recurrence &amp; [indecipherable] wants me to send him to Paris in my ambulance car. The poor old car simply couldn't look at a trip to Paris without breaking down</p>
