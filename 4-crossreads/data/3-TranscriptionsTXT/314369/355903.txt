 12 October 1915 Tuesday 
 Very quiet  &amp;  restful receive two parcels of chocolate through the post Interview Dental Surgeon to have my teeth repaired  &amp;  put in order while I have so good a chance. 
 1915 October 13 Wednesday 
 Not much doing lie  &amp;  sit about all day   &amp;  read Write a couple of letters in evening. I am now practically right again my foot is completely healed  &amp;  I have no inconvenience from it at all 
 expect go back to duty shortly
