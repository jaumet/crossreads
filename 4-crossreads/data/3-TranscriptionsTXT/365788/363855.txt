 23 Jan 16 3 planted round the paddock next the house.  Young P.H.  There is a rumour current here to the effect that all troops who have been on Active Service Some Aus. 12 mos. are  going back on 3 mos furlough, but I don't believe it. Harry Budden may be here today, so I hope to see him. He works awful hard , old girl for the things he representing. No news old girl. Very much love to you all. Your loving brother Fred
