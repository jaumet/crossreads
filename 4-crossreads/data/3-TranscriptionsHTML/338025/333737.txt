<p>Wednesday 11th July 1917</p>
<p>Foufflin-Ricametz<br/>Reveille 4.30, Breakfast 5,, stables 5.30am and harnesses up and ready to move off at 6.30am. Arr. Foufflin-Ricametz about 1pm. Watered and fed mules.<br/>The country we came through was all cultivated and with the trees all scatted about it looked A1.<br/>Arr. at our billet about noon, which was a large house of about 12 rooms, lovely avenue leading up to the house used avenue for mule lines.</p>
