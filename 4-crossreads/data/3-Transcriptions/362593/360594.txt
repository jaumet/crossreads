<p>Friday 30 August 1918</p>
<p>Saturday 31 August 1918<br/>Feel better.<br/>H.A.P. over drome.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 3 h. 5 m.<br/>Got 2 Hun two-seaters personally.<br/>Both fell to pieces in the air.  Very pleased, 1 in our lines, 1 in Enemy lines.<br/>McGuinness did some great piloting enabling me to shoot down the 2 Huns.</p>
