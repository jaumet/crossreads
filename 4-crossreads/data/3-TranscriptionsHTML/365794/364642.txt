<p>Wednesday 29 [28] August 1917 [1918]<br/>Town 4 p.m. with S. Andrews.  Hair treated then tea Groppi's, drove home, bought hat &ndash; Davis Brian, [Davies Bryan] Pt. 100 (hat Pt. 90, band Pt. 10).<br/>There are 10 to 14 sisters off duty daily T.103 etc.  Report says 42 male staff off.</p>
