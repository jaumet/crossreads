 AUGUST   MONDAY 28   1916 
 Raining & fine all day again. Did a bit of fatigue work shovelling dirt from in front of huts before breakfast. Out signalling the rest of the day & had a lamp parade at night. Warren Hogarth came over in evening  I got a letter & parcel from Linda, the letter in answer to mine from Durban. She was very glad to get it & I think it cheered her up a lot I think
