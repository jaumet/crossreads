
7 October 1918 Monday (X to X crossed out)Well the Parcel arrived & I was so thankful Coal Tar Soap especially good Paper  mail also on the way X I would not bother Mother about trying to get my Diary printed too many already & its for you-  mostly I do it- XFever continues men 50% lying around our Medical Cart like wounded after a scrap-  Its one long line of Ambulances to the rear & another line of reinforcements coming up but the latter is never enough   The men have only overcoat & Mosquitoe nets (no blanket) so as to travel light & the nights are cold now.
