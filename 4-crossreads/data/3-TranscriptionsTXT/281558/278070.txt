 Friday Dec 14th 
 O.T.B. : storming &amp; raiding in morn : gas helmets &amp; trials in afternoon : piquet in night to Warminster : leg very sore after the long walk : home about 10.30 pm : 
 Saturday Dec 15th 
 O.T.B. : sick parade but didn't see the quack : went to No 1 in afternoon &amp; met Reg : also run against Ken Wilson &amp; A Sharrock : home early as I am not feeling too good
