 Sari Buir this time last year.  In future I shall spend the 1st week of every August safe in bed I think. However, we clapped our spurs in  &amp;  away we went hell for leather over the ridge into the next little dip  &amp;  after us came the sand carts rolling  &amp;  ploughing up the sand like ships in a heavy sea. After them came some ammunition limbers. The enemy evidently spotted these  &amp;  began to put shrapnel systematically into this hollow.
