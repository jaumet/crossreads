<p>August, 1917. Saturday 4<br/>Showers of heavy rain during day. 6.30 parade. 8.30 drill order &amp; gas helmets and did some drill. 12 check parade 2.30 drill order went for some route march and got caught in severe shower. 5 check parade. 9 check parade. 9.30 lights out. Visit from Mitchell.</p>
