<p>May 1918<br/>Wednesday 8</p>
<p>Dussy-la-Daours<br/>Still raining, had a run at midnight to Villers Brettoneaux, plenty of the Bosche dead still unburied.<br/>Paid a  visit  to Jame's grave and took a snap of same.<br/>Wrote to Uncle Will, Mum, Toots and Adrienne also Ray.<br/>Slight gas bombardment at Corbie and Fuilloy<br/>Met an American from Florida in Engineers.</p>
