One big shell fell 20 yds from our tent & splashed it with mud etc. Got up 8.30am & had good breakfast & have since been trying to dry some of my clothes but almost impossible on account of the rain. A heavy thunderstorm splashed over & heavy hail fell. Very cold all day.
