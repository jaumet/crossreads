
December 1916Saturday 16Started on our long trek 7am.Passed several graves of L.H. on the journey saw lots of dead horses.Arrived at Duedar and camped for the night. There was a battle here last Easter Sunday between LH and the Turk.  The LH held out ; numerous graves.
Sunday 17Left very early. Passed thro Katia and then 5 miles south of Romani.Still seeing graves.
