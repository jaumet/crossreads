<p>Wednesday 25 October 1916<br/>Rode up to Kantara and sent telegram to Port Said asking had Mr. Trelawny returned.  Reply "Expected daily".<br/>Lecture on scouting.<br/>Parcel from Stella</p>
<p>Thursday 26 October 1916<br/>Getting ready to move in morning.<br/>Rode over to Kantara East to Rifle Range.  Shot fairly well.<br/>Got 7 letters but none from home.</p>
