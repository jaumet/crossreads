<p>making a list of shortages for the new 2.M. Had a bath during afternoon &amp; read some papers a good afternoons rest. Rumours of us going in again. Latest official figures of yanks stunt is 20,000 prisoners &amp; the British since Sept 1st have taken another 20000 odd, good going. Billy Hughes drove past yesterday &amp; meeting some of our chaps shook hands all round, he is very enthusiastically received. During the day we had some balloons brought down, I saw two but rumour hath it that the number was 3. Splendid mail in from Aussie, 11 letters &amp; a parcel from home, a real treat, a bright moonlight night so expect the loches.<br/>Monday16th. Sept.<br/>  Practically no huns over strange to say. last night Had a quiet day and did some washing and writing. Rumours of a big stunt coming off in a few days and we are getting ready by dumping stuff so as to travel light. The 8th &amp; the 15th. have also dumped one sections gear in our camp. I believe the main roads are closed each night now for tanks coming up &amp; probably cavalry.</p>
