<p>Saturday 11 August 1917.</p>
<p>On picquet 2 a.m. till 4 a.m,&nbsp;Sudanese on picquet also. <s>Told me that the</s> They were excited as raiding Bedouins had cut 1 camel loose &amp; taken it out into the desert.</p>
<p>Next post 8 am. till 10 am.</p>
<p>Printed &amp; fixed photos &amp; wrote letters.</p>
<p>Next post 2 p.m. till 4 p.m.</p>
<p>Whole picquet relieved at 6 p.m.</p>
<p>Had a shower &amp; turned in at 8 p.m.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sunday 12th (continued)</p>
<p>Mail delivered today. I received 8 letters. Only 1 of my Colombo letters received.</p>
<p>Sunday 12th (continued)</p>
<p>shot as a spy 2 months ago. Bloodstains still on tree &amp; ashes of burnt clothing nearby. Grave not far away.</p>
<p>Saw Marmaluke&#39;s leap and chipped pieces of Alabasta off back of Mohammed Ali Mosque.</p>
