<p>[Tuesday 12 December 1916]</p>
<p>The Desert is at <strike>this</strike> its best now &amp; the nights perfect, it seems wrong to be killing men all these years, how long is it all going to last.<br/>Aeroplanes bombed <strike>3rd</strike> Reg <strike>last</strike> yesterday &amp; they sail over us each day.<br/>We can see the domes &amp; minarets of <strike>Al Arish</strike> from our lines.<br/>I can't give much information as to what we are doing but it is much the same as of old.<br/>Clothing always sticky from the Brackish water &amp; our skin gets cracked &amp; sore.<br/>I told you I've got the Criminals hair cut again.<br/><u>All</u> the ANZAC Mounted Division are now to wear Emu feathers in their Hats certainly adds to picturesque of Millinery &amp; breaks</p>
