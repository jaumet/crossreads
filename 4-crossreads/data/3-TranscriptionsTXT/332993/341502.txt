1053. Thursday June 21, 1917.
There were occasional storms last night, today is dull & cloudy.A new baloon is up today in place of the one that was burned down alongside this camp last night. So far none has yet been up in place of the other burned one.The enemy is sending over occasional shells this morningWe expect to be leaving this camp for a new one probably at Bailliul any day now.
