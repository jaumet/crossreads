<p>1915<br/>Oct. 24 Sun.<br/>With the Armenian Scouts again this afternoon &amp; acted as Scoutmaster for the afternoon &ndash; all orders in English of course &ndash; Troop &amp; stave drill, bugle calls, signalling &amp; first aid. Pretty smart chaps.</p>
