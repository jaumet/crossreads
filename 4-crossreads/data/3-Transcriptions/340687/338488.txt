<p>July, 1917. Tuesday 3<br/>Fine and warm. No 1st parade. Fell in at 8.30 for drill order &amp; gas bags and went for route march near Cappy where we spent &frac34; of an hour in swimming home at 10.30. Read. Parcel from A.S. Drill order for squad drill at 2.30 and spent an hour pleasantly in the shade. Broke off wrote and read. Much transport moving through here.</p>
