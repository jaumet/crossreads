<p>Afterward I got a chair near the stove next to "Gran'pere" <strike>who is I guess</strike> He had the biggest pair of feet I ever saw on a man &amp; seemed proud of them. He was a garruluous old fellow &amp; when he found I had a smattering of French there was no holding him I don't think he'd had such a hearing in his own home for years.</p>
<p>He told me he was a soldier in the war of '70 and as he only had one eye I though I was on the track of a tale but he missed a great chance &amp; told me the truth &ndash; it was the result of a hayfork accident 30 years ago.</p>
