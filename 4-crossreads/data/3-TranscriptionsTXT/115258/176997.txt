 Early on the morning of the 25th we pushed on again and came up with the NZ&#39;s in truck with the Turks, a few miles this side of Ammon so we turned off to the left and got down onto the railway line a bit north of the station. The country out there is rolling stony hills of red soil with pretty steep wadis in places running down to the Wadi Ammon.
