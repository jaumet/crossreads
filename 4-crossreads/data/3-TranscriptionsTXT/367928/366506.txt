
6th Nov 1916
To Lieut Col VernonCommanding4th DAC
Dear SirI wish to thank you for the parcel which you sent me. I was getting short of sox as where some of my mates they came at a very good time. The socks and mittens I shared out among them but the balance I kept for myselfAgain thanking you sir on behalf of my unit and myself.
I am yours obedientlyDrv Oswald Allsopp
[added in different handwriting]1st L.H.C.D.Mostly socks from the girls of An Electric Light [indecipherable]
