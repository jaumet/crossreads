<p>many other handkerchiefs also the daintest little slip-bodice. <br/>Well, we had a jolly breakfast, then to our Wards &amp; a Merry time there with our patients &amp; Medical Officers. Another Sister &amp; I filled 4 long white bed-stockings with different things &amp; they cannot make out who did it &ndash; had an idea I had something to do with it but now think they are on the wrong track. Onion, lemon, chocolate, turnip, Toy (teddy-bear etc). packet of the cheapest cigarettes. Sorry we did not manage to get one for them all but were unable to procure the articles to fill them. Well, we got up all the patients that could possibly manage to sit, some walk &amp; others were</p>
