<p>6 p.m.</p>
<p>Stopped at Montelimar &amp; had a yarn with 2 French soldiers, wounded at Verdun.<br/>One was at the Peninsula &amp; helped at the Australian evacuation.<br/>He thinks highly of the Australians.</p>
<p>Friday 18th Aug.</p>
<p>Passed Lyons in the night &amp; breakfasted at Ma&ccedil;on.  Very cool in morning but as we neared Dijon the sun came out &amp; it became warmer.  Rain finished.</p>
