<p>Y.M.C.A. tents here, which are being managed by Harry Peake B.A.</p>
<p>Aug 17   Bull-ring again!   The platoon made an attack on a supposed "strong-point".   Physical exercises, organised games on the grass &amp; bayonet-work occupied the rest of the day.</p>
<p>I finished "Farewell, Nikola! by Guy Boothby to-night.   It is a weird story, and its weirdness is hardly justifiable, in my estimation.   I am reading novels at present, because I can't get anything better.</p>
<p>Aug 18.    We had our blankets etc. fumigated in the portable disinfect machine this morning.   After dinner we were inoculated.   Sore arms are the fashion now!   Beastly fashion, isn't it?</p>
