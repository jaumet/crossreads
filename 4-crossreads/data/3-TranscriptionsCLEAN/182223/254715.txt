  
 Monday 15 November 1915  Wrote to Gen. Ford telling him that if Col. Martin is agreeable, I am willing to take up my old position at No. 2 A. G. Hospital. Letting byegones be byegones  In Cairo during the morning. A walk round Heliopolis between 5 & 6-30 pm. Writing & reading during rest of the day. Met Father McAuliffe from Anzac, down stairs at 6.30 p.m. 
 Tuesday 16 November 1915  In operating room during the morning  Writing & reading  Cairo during the afternoon Tram ride to Old Cairo 4 Millimes for one hours ride. Looking at Xray pictures after dinner with two medicoes in my room. Wrote Girls. Received cable from girls dated 5-11-15 
 Wednesday 17 November 1915  Reading & writing all day.  In Cairo during the afternoon  Had afternoon tea with Mrs Bacon. Posted letters to Girls and Hyman.
