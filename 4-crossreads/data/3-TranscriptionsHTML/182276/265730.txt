<p>9th. Aust Machine Gun Coy [No 51]<br/>Intelligence report from 6am 14/2/17 to 6am [15/2/17]<br/>Indirect fire was carried out during night:-<br/>Gun at C28c 60/45 on 1) ROAD from C30c 91/90 to C30a 20/60. 2) ROAD from C30a 20/60 to C30a 18/75 3) ROAD from C23d 45/45. 1000 rds between 7 &amp; 9.30pm<br/>Gun at C16d 15/40 on 1) X ROADS LA HOULETTE D13a 56/25 2) X ROAD Fm du Gd VERQUIN C12b 90/06. 3) Fme du CHASTEL C18d 15/11. 1500 rds.<br/>Gun at C22a 75/15 on 1) ROAD C29c 85/35 to I 5b 45/15 2) CROSS ROADS C30a 20/22 to C24c 01/50 3) ROAD C24c 85/15 to C24b 85/40 1000 rds.<br/>One gun fired from C23a 27/43 on enemy's wire at about C23b [0/35?] to C23 central throughout the night.<br/>One gun fired from "J" Gap from 7.30pm to midnight at enemy's wire between N of C29c 90/08 to S of C29c 40/30.<br/>Owing to poor visibility no daylight firing could be carried out.<br/>Gun at "J Gap successfully an enemy machine gun &amp; again silenced it.<br/>Quiet during day, except for slight bombardment on left Sector at 4.30pm. Very slight retaliation by enemy to our MTM fire at 12.30pm. Machine guns of enemy only fairly active.<br/>Weather: Bright: Slight ESE wind. Visibility indifferent</p>
<p>D. Whitehead<br/>Lieut<br/>9th. AMG Co.</p>
