 18 July 1915 Sunday 7th After Trinity 
 On account of a late issue of rations I miss church parade. A huge pile of mail arrives but proves to be all old letters returned to us after being re-sorted. Working party from 4 pm to 8 pm making ledges or terraces on the hill side for the accommodation of a lot of troops who are to arrive here shortly one man wounded 
 1915 July 19 Monday 
 No rations at all this morning A.S.C. are taking stock. take on the same job as yesterday
