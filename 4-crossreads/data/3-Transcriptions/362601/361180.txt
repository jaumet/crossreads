<p>28 March, Friday</p>
<p>F. but snow at intervals</p>
<p>Recd letter from Ellis.</p>
<p>I go to W.A.A.C.'s Dance in Sgts Mess. I play &amp; dance &amp; we have chocs, wine trifle &amp; fruit salad. At midnight it is snowing hard EW &amp; I are left to carry on alone.</p>
<p>Have bath &amp; clean up after dinner.</p>
<p>29 March, Saturday</p>
<p>F. 6" covering of snow &amp; much snow-balling in consequence.</p>
<p>Recd letter from Ellis &amp; Miss Scroggins. Promotions come out as 14.2.19.</p>
<p>Sit &amp; write until 8.30 pm &amp; then go to bed. D. &amp; D. come down at 9.30 while I am in bed &amp; jam until lights out.</p>
<p>Wrote M/R.L.</p>
<p>Sent Mag to M.R.L. &amp; S. Mail &amp; A.B. to Auntie. Wrote Ellis &amp; Miss Scroggins.</p>
