<p>hospitals there was the same tale.  Someone had blundered for as early as Sepr. 1914 Red + Goods had begun to leave Australia &amp; there should have been plenty for all.  The men came back in most cases absolutely minus everything &ndash; landing in bandages &amp; blankets.  One boy I saw was quite proud because he had managed to retain his cap &amp; one puttee!  And if they did bring their kit back it went straight to the sterilizer, for vermin, the bane &amp; scourge of armies, were present in force.  Then to the pack store &amp; here it stayed till the patient left</p>
