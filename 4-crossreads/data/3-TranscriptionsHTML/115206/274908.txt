<p>December  1918.<br/>Wednesday 4</p>
<p>Wembley On furlough.<br/>Received a letter from Smithy, so he did not go to Ireland.Gives Leeds a good name.<br/>Weather wet. Went to Palladium with Charlie, Vic and Olive. Splendid show too. Visited Cooks and made inquiries for sailing.<br/>Weather still wet at midnight, I wonder does it ever cease to rain.</p>
