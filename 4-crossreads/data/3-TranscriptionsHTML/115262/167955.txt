<p>Saturday 7 July 1917.</p>
<p>In hospital. Feeling not too good, but expect to be out in a couple of days.</p>
<p>Another mail in. Got 4 letters. Wrote 2 this afternoon.</p>
<p>A very heavy fog hung to the ground till 7 am. when it lifted. 1st dampness of any description that we have seen in atmosphere since arriving in Egypt.</p>
<p>Feeling worse this afternoon if anything &nbsp;running a temp.&nbsp;</p>
