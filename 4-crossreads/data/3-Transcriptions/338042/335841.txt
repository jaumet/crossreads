<p>April 1918<br/>April 1st<br/>Rehearsal at the Olympia Theatre, where we are to show for a week &ndash; We are wonderfully well advertised &ndash; (The long poster) &ndash; American Y.M.C.A. and its system &ndash; Both houses receive the Anzac Coves with the utmost enthusiasm &amp; appreciation &ndash; During the evening we play (2 shows) to 9.000 people<br/>April 2nd<br/>Reading &ndash; Writing and a walk around town &ndash; Two shows.</p>
