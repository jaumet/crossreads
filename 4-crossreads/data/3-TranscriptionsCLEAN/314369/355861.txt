
20 July 1915Tuesday
At work today on the water works splendid advancements being madeWeather very hot   &  SultryWe "Stand to arms" at 3 a.m.The period of the Mohamedan Ramadan is at present in force   &  our people are expecting trouble.
1915 July 21Wednesday
Terracing againThe valley is nearly finished now   & we expect to start in another part next shiftTurks fire twenty shells at our aeroplane without getting near it
