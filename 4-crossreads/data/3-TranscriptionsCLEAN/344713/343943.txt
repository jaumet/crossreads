
Thurs. April 5th: -Left Fremicourt & went further east to Velu & bivouaced under tarpaulins in wood outside.There has been big German horse lines here.
Frs. April. 6th: -Shifted along road to midway between Yelu & Bertincourt & re-erected our shanties.
Mon. April 9th: -Shifted along cross railway line (absolutely stripped even of the sleepers by "Fritz") & on to Hermies which village the 2nd & 3rd Bns. took early this morning. We are awaiting a counter-attack.
Tues. April. 10th: -Situation absolutely quiet & no artillery fire. Funny kind of weather - Sunshine one moment & Snow the next.Good Country around here but not much material to fix up a decent camp.This is Easter time & there is no chance of getting a letter away or buying a cigarette.
Wed. April. 11th: -Moved along to Coys headquarters on Sunken road at side of Demicourt relieving the 1st. Bn.Our line is comprised of outposts.Snowing & things in general are miserable - not much shelter.
