 Sunday 15 July 1917 Revaille 2.30, parade moved off at 4.20 &amp; proceeded to Shelal following the wadi along to Iran.  Stayed the day arrived home 11.30. 
 Monday 16 July 1917 Very warm day.  Nothing much doing.  M.G. played C. Sq. draw 0-0. 
 Tuesday 17 July 1917 Usual routine, on pumping fatigue. 
 Wednesday 18 July 1917 Very warm day:  wrote two letters;  usual routine.  Picquet at night. 
 Thursday 19 July 1917 On pumping party in morning and had "iggary" stunt about 10 o'clock.  Turks came in:  saw 15 Prisoners (Cavalry), got back at 2.20. 
 Friday 20 July 1917 Saddled up at 2.45, and went on a long stunt to El Bugga, &amp; got bombed but no damage done to us.  N.A. got it pretty bad, arrived home about 4.30. 
 Saturday 21 July 1917 Usual routine:  quiet day, went to water twice during day, filled water bottles in afternoon.
