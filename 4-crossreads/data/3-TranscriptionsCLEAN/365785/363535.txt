Some of the nurses went to Lemnos quite a lot are on Transport duty and twenty six went to England;  just after you left for a few weeks, after the by engagement 7, 8, & 9 of August.  They were all very busy but for many weeks things have been slack & wearying with - almost - more Drs. & Nurses than patients.  However there are now three of us sewing & mending and there is no slackness in our Department, pyjamas are wearing out & we are everlastingly patching;  in addition the Red Cross has established Aux. Stores at all the Hospitals & I have charge at Gezirah & there is a fair amount of work attached
