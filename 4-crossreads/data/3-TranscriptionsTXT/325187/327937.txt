 [Thursday 13 July 1916] 
 News has just come from Kharel Oasis of an aeroplane coming down, another plane took a mechanic &amp; water &amp; left  again  The Pilot worked in the sun till he got sunstroke &amp; mounting the machine with mechanic started in WRONG direction, then went mad, &amp; the mechanic drank spirit out of Level both found dead, this happened out from Sohag, which we left ere coming here, so you see the possibilities for Flying men- 7 of our Officers have volunteered to join an Aviation School to learn    Now office is short  Left at 10 pm for another Desert Raid rode all night- Same old moon that shines over you &amp; Verdun   The dawn is very beautiful on the Desert.
