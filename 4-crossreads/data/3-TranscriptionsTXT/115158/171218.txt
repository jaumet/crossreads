 Engineer Lieutenant H. Bleackley, R.N. served on H.M.A.S.&nbsp;Australia in the Pacific and in the North Sea until transferred&nbsp;back to the Royal Navy in Dec. 1916

Includes list of&nbsp;officers and programme of movements of H.M.A.S. Australia, list of&nbsp;ships in Pacific Ocean Aug. 1914, printed map with Australia&#39;s&nbsp;movements marked in ink and copy of a letter from one of the crew&nbsp;of the Scharnhorst published in Kieler Neuste Nachrichten, 10 Jan.1915, describing German naval movements in the Pacific Aug.- Oct.1915
