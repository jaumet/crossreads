<p>Sunday 2 July 1916<br/>Wrote Home (49).  Spent a quiet evening outside our tent listening to Phonograph.  Night Matron again falls to my lot.  Hooted out of Bed at 10.45 p.m. to go to Billy's Ward.</p>
<p>Monday 3 July 1916<br/>Ward Master all day.  Wrote Cards to Mem, Edie Tory, Ada Cohen, I. Westaway, Jess Mather.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4 July 1916<br/>Bill, Dave &amp; I go to Cairo for a run.  Visit all the Popular places.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5 July 1916<br/>Ellis, Bill &amp; I go to Heliopolis to meet Dick Rowe.  Also meet McKenzie, Dinkum Soldier McKenzie.  All Patients come inside the Ward.  A kid of 5 talks French &amp; English with equal ease.</p>
<p>Thursday 6 July 1916<br/>Everything as usual, had a very lazy day.  Lay in bed at evening &amp; thought of Home &amp; Friends.</p>
<p>Friday 7 July 1916<br/>Late dinner day.  Wrote Harry Taubman &amp; Edie Taubman.  Tried hard to rain.  Staff gives a concert in Y.M.C.A. Hut.  Very hot day &amp; Earn malingers.</p>
<p>Saturday 8 July 1916<br/>The 4 Jews go out to Y.M.C.A.  I met Roy Ingram.  Ellis wins 3 games Billiards.  Dave &amp; I beat Billy &amp; Ellis 105 to 50.  We dine at Y.M.C.A. &amp; have a sing-song with McKenzie from Manly.  Went to Anzac Hotel for Tea &amp; concert afterwards.</p>
