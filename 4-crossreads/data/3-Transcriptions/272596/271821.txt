<p>Passed the coast of Somaliland yesterday could not see it very plain for a mist was hanging over it.</p>
<p>27th. Very quiet today nothing much doing with the exception of some impromptu speeches which caused no end of fun for one did not know what his subject would be, &amp; when he did he had 1 minute to think about it.</p>
<p>Have been hard at it washing clothes I tackled my tunic, I don't know what kind of a job I made of it but I know I got an amazing lot of dirty water from her.</p>
