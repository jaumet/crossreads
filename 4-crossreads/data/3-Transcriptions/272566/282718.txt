<p>Saturday 1 December</p>
<p>Very cold indeed today.<br/>Took the Triumph Cycle to the workshops at Bailleul and returned with the New "Douglas" machine.  This was the only job I undertook throughout the day.  With a good fire going in the hut I wrote a number of letters on the Paris trip </p>
