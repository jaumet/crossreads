<p>Friday 7 September 1917<br/>Reported Miss Innes 2 p.m. to go to No. 4 A.G.H., Randwick 6 p.m.  Had a quiet 2 hours in gardens and Art Gallery.</p>
<p>Friday 6th [September] 1918<br/>How much has happened since this day last year.<br/>Go Cairo with Nichol &amp; Walker (Sisters), tea Groppis then 5th hair massage at Weinrecks [?] (7 more hair massages), drove back to 31st.<br/>Interviewed M. Leach re his friend coming tomorrow to tea.<br/>English sister Rowe sent to ward for sake of British Officers!  Very thin that.<br/>R.A.F. Taylor takes late pass, kisses sister &amp; murmurs about a gift.</p>
