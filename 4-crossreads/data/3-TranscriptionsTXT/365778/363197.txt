
Thursday 24/5/17Robinson returned. Regimental dinner a huge success.Friday 25/5/17Had a game of cricket. Boxing competitions at night. Had a spar.Saturday 26/5/17Visited Wisdom at Senlis, played football 1st round of Competition.Sunday 27/5/17Brigade Church parade at Senlis - Genl Birdwood presented medals to Officers, N.C.O.s & men.Monday 28/5/17Refereed football match for 7th Engineers, Lloyd to Rouen & Robinson to England.
