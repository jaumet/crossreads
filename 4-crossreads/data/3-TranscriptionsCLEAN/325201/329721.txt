
Palais de danse. Admission 1 franc. Quite a sprinkling of females were there, not evening dress of course they being daughters of the French  &  Italian Shopkeepers I should imagine. I had my eyes on a little Flapper who danced well &   asked her in my best French but got turned down, "mademoiselle" apparently being frightened of soldiers.However the third shot I picked a winner, who gave me two dances
