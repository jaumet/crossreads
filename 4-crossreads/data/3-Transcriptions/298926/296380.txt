<p>Happy bank Holiday on the Heath.<br/>Now when I was at Harefield I met a chap, he was 57, Held highest educational honours <br/>obtainable in Australia.  I saw him in London and took him out to the heath with me.  He <br/>got a few whiskies in and as the policemen were real good sorts we were chatting about <br/>the fair when my mate started quoting Greek Latin &amp; Shakespeare to them and <br/>talking over their heads in a manner that made them open</p>
