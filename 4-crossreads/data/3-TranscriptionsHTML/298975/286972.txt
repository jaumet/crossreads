<p>Wednesday 7th Nov<br/>Came rather a gutser today. Had to get on to a Wheelbarrow &amp; cart much about 150 yds What one could call uninteresting work.</p>
<p>Thursday  8<br/>Same as yesterday The midday meal was a bit light today so tried the Salvation A hut. Had a very decent meat pie &amp; good cup of tea for 3d</p>
<p>Friday 9<br/>Same old job</p>
<p>Saturday 10th<br/>Same. C. Watson shouted for us Take your choice Beer or cigerates, took the later</p>
<p>Sunday 11th<br/>As usual.</p>
