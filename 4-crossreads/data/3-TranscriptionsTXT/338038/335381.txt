
[Letter from Garling to his Father, 3rd Aug. 1917.Re allotment & deferred pay 72France3rd August 1917My dear Father,I have been treated very well the last two nights as the mail brought me your letters (3 of 22nd 25th and 26th June) also a number of Heralds & Bulletins -Apart from the usual pleasure your letters bring, there was the great news of Pats admission to officers school - Its simply grand, and I know now he's got that far he will come through allright.  Your letters also contain the information that news of poor old [indecipherable] disaster had been received
