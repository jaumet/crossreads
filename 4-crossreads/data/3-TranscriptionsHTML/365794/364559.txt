<p>Thursday 7 [6] June 1917 [1918]<br/>&frac12; day &amp; went Heliopolis 5 p.m.  Ch. Stockings for tapestry (Pt. 30) work.  Bought for Miss Hunt 1 piece tapestry Pt. 45, got it for 8/-.<br/>Flame trees (?) are glorious, long rows of them lining the streets.</p>
