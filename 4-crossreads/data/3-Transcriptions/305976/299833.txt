<p>Thursday Feb 1<br/>sailing postponed on account of Raider out. Troops from all states banking up in Seymour.<br/>Friday Feb 2<br/>Weekly route march, across fields to Goulbourn River, across Railway Line &amp;  back through old Seymour Town. Route marches very enjoyable.<br/>Saturday Feb 3<br/>On duty, off in afternoon. Went fishing to Goulbourn River with some of the Boys. Change from Camp<br/>Sunday Feb 4<br/>Washing day (nuff said)<br/>Monday Feb 5<br/>Waggon Drill &amp; Lecture<br/>Tuesday Feb 6<br/>Signalling, Evening wrote letters</p>
