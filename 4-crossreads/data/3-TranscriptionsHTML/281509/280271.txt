<p>18 February 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Up at 6.15 after a fair sleep. Tony did not get home till 3 am. Pretty knocked out. Bob sleeps in too. Reinforcement drill in morning &amp; lecture under tree on Nervous System.<br/>Afternoon company drill beastly monotonous. Bob &amp; self go to tea at [indecipherable] Tony too tired to come. After tea journey to Hurlstone  Park to Patons. (Wilson Grants etc.) Broncho Bill, Willy Maxwell. Good evening  tram to Summer Hill &ndash; train Strathfield.  Home to camp at 1.10 am.</p>
