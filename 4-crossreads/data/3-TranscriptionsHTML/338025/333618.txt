<p>Wednesday 21st February 1917<br/>Caught the 8am tram from Waverley station for Glasgow. Met at Queen St station by Bella, Jeanie and two boys. Had a good time, and as I had to return to Edinburgh that night I had to leave Glasgow at 5 pm. Arr Edinburgh 7pm. Supper at Aunt Jess's. Caught the London Express from Waverley at 10pm</p>
