  
 July 10 been picked for guard  Damn disgrace I reckon. Unable get leave for over a week. 
 11 Two of the cripples are doing a hop on the hut floor; in fact the one limbed, & the lame are the jolliest boys in the crowd. Wandered away over the hills & discovered the picturesque Village of Wyke with its fine old Church, neat little gardens, & shady avenues. In the distance was Portland with its famous prison.
