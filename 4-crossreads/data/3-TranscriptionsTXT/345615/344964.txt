At daybreak our ALH & a Regt of Yeomanry moved out with some of our AASC wagons they went west along the coast road. About 8 o'clock I heard big gun fire for the first time since Gallipoli in June - It was our cruiser along the coast giving the Bedouin some "hurry up"
