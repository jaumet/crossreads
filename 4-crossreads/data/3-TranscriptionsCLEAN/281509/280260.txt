
1916 February 7Monday
Janet, Bob & self take trip up river. in milk launch.  Beautiful calm morning & a pleasant trip. Side show companions.  Land at Wogamia. have a snack & re-embark for homeward trip take pot - shots at rabbits etc. Expect Tony by mid-day train does not arrive. Bob & self drive out home to Mayfield.New family arrive for. the farm  Franklins.Take Bob to Morisons at night.
