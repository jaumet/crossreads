 Nov. 16 Tuesday 1915 Went to work at 6 AM. After breakfast went out with other Witnesses to Mustapha. The case started at 10 AM. The "papers"occupied most of the day. Stewart &amp; Playdell &amp; myself were called in &amp; the case was adjourned till 10 AM tomorrow. 
 Nov. 17 Wednesday 1915 Went to work 6 Am. Went out to Mustapha with other Witnesses  Case on all day. Adjourned till 11 AM tomorrow. Only two charges so far been brought against him 4 more to follow. Paid tonight 7/- Went to prayer meeting (spoke) 
 Nov. 18 Thursday 1915 Went to Mustapha again with other witnesses &ndash; all day sitting returned 3. 30 PM. Dick &amp; I worked till 8. 30 PM 
 Nov. 19 Friday 1915 Out again to Mustapha all day.
