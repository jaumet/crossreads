<p>started away early in the morning &amp; marched to Cairo where he was laid to rest. first came the firing party then the gun carriage with the coffin on it &amp; the pall bearers each side of the carriage the band also was there. we are having a bit of bad luck as all the good chaps are being taken &amp; the rough ones left.</p>
<p>I say mum it is a bit rough on the part of the correspondent who is with us. condemning the lot of us through the weakness of a few bad characters who happen to be causing trouble &amp; I can say the boys here are very indignant. I can tell you the first time they lay hands on the correspondent he will get a very rough handling. I may say he is not game to come near them for fear his pretty face may get hurt.</p>
<p>Mum Dear you would laugh if you could see me now sitting here writing this letter to-day it is too hot to do any drilling so we are having a holiday &amp; trying to keep cool I am wearing a summer costume Short pants &amp; Singlet no socks only wearing sandals &amp; also killing flies by the hundreds. I say they are the greatest pest one can have</p>
