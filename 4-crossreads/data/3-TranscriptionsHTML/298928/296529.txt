<p>Then we went back to our billets to think the matter out<br/>While one went round to find out if the colonel was about<br/>But he had gone on leave so our enquiries had to stop<br/>But don't get thinking cobbers that were going to let it drop</p>
<p>We'll find the missing issue if we have to go to Haig<br/>&amp; if his answers seem confusing and his answers rather<br/>vague<br/>We'll ring up Mr Bottomley the soldiers strongest chum<br/>And ask him can he find out why the 3rd don't get their rum</p>
