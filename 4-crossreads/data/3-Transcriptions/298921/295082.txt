<p>April, 1915<br/>Monday 19<br/>Field day on the old Suez Road after went Pay Office Cairo &ndash; Tomorrow we go on the promised 8 days trek to Saccora &amp; Memphis &ndash; the old Capital Weather warmer &amp; all Egypt goes to sleep midday, except we soldiers who train all day in the European fashion or should I say English.<br/>Myra Nyse better, she had a close shave, &amp; is still very weak.<br/>My "Mufti" is still at the Pay Office &amp; I will not send it till we move.</p>
