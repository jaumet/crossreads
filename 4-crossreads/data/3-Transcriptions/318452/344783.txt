<p>At 6 oclock our barrage opened along the whole <u>front</u> of the operation which was about 12 miles long. The barrage was excellent and moved with clocklike precision. Immediately it opened  battalion in conjunction with other front objective troops moved up &amp; advanced with it. The enemy was surprised not expecting an attack that morning. The opposition was very slight except in the centre of the sector where he was occupying a line of pillboxes from which he brought mortars bombs machine guns &amp; rifles to bear upon us.
