<p>15-3-16<br/>Wed 15th<br/>Davies who went ot Heliopolis with sick horses from Bahnasa arrived back here yesterday &amp; says that nearly all our reinforcements have been transferred to the Ammunition Column &amp; are to go to England. he said Stan is also trying to transfer, but was told that no Signallers would be taken.</p>
<p>Received orders at dinner time to go onto a hill in the desert about 3 miles from here &amp; try to pick up the signalling station at Minia with the helio. The trumpeters took over the phone work. We had an enjoyable ride through the cultivated fields (Reid &amp; I) &amp; had no trouble in getting in touch with Minia about 10 miles away &amp; arrived back in camp to find a mail had arrived for us, also a parcel of Cocoa &amp; milk from Stan;</p>
