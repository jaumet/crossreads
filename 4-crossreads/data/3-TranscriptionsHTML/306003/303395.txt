<p>16-6-16<br/>of our camp &amp; that is the reason the 2nd Regt went out as we came in as they were fresh &amp; was in hopes of meeting the Turks following us in again;  Our aeroplane flew over us this morning while we were at Bir-El-Abd &amp; she reported to Head Quarters that about 200 Turks mounted on horses &amp; camels were there,  she had mistaken us for Turks, but fortunately she did not drop any bombs on us</p>
<p>Heard tonight that both Shepheards &amp; the Continental Hotels (Cairo) have been caught red handed as German spies both places had wireless plants installed;  I heard while in Cairo that the National was being closely watched but Shepheards &amp; the Continental would be the two last place the ordinary individual would think to <strike>too</strike> suspect especially Shepheard who could never do enough for the Aust. soldiers, &amp; was a great favorite</p>
