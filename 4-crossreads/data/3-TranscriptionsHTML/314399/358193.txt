<p>Tuesday 15 July 1919<br/>Got taken off the quota 'cos the strength was reduced. Was playing bridge in the evening when the adjt. came along &amp; said he thought I'd checked for it again but to go to the office in the morning &amp; make sure. Won 4/- at bridge. A huge amount for me.</p>
