<p>Wednesday 22 August 1917</p>
<p>Left Alexandria 8 a.m. arrived KANTARA 6 p.m. &amp; travelled all night in company with Details from my Regiment in Egypt, SCOTCH, WELSH, IRISH, FRENCH, British WEST IndiaN etc:  New Zealanders &amp; Australians more comfort on the train now that it is fitted with Westinghouse BRAKES etc.</p>
<p>Mrs. Chisholm at Kantara with Miss McPhellamy are still running the Soldiers Club, which is so much appreciated that the G.O.C. has given it official recognition.</p>
