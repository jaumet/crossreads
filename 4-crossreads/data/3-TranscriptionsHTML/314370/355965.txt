<p>January, 1916<br/>Wednesday 12<br/><u>Mum's Birthday</u><br/>After the mornings work was over I went onto Cairo to try to get some Bostock Books the afternoon being a holiday<br/>But owing to the wreck of the "Persia" these books are not available.<br/>Everything they cannot find any other excuse for they blame on to the "persia"</p>
<p>Thursday 13<br/>Very dull &amp; signs of rain<br/>Work as usual<br/>Go with dad over to L.H. lines &amp; score a pair of leggings.<br/>The 1st L.H. Regt are to ride to Wardan starting at daybreak tomorrow</p>
