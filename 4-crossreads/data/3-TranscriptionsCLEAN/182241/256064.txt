
several weeks are all in fine fettle The riding was admirable & all seemed to enjoy the exercise & turnout The immediate neighbourhood is the centre of a number of camps, so that the sand hills are always a bustle with horses & men & the unending streams of accompanying camel tports our Hdqs have their tents pitched in what was once an old figgery & isolated from the other camping what astonishes one is the great size of the fig trees & the density of their growth more so as their owners apparently pay little or not attention to pruning or firtilising.
