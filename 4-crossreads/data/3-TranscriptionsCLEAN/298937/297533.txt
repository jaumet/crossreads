 Tuesday 7th Mar. 1916 
 Received a second bundle of letters, making about 17 in all.  Work in dispensary is going along fairly well. Capt. Scrimgeour found fault (quite rightly) with some ointment yesterday & gave some praise for the new labels I have put on the bottles.
