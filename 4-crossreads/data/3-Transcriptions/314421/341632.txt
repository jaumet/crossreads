<p>Blequin<br/>Sept. 19. 1917</p>
<p>From September 19th 1917</p>
<p>To December 28. 1917.</p>
<p>7th Diary, continued from Book VI.</p>
<p>Wednesday September 19, 1917.<br/>This is the 1143 day of the war.<br/>Continued from 19 September in Book VI</p>
<p>We left Tardinghem at 2.15 by motor lorry. There were some 25 lorries to convey the whole Battalion. We marched from the beach to the lorries at Tardinghem about 15 minutes march.</p>
