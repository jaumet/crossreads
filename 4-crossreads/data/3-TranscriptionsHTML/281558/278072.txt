<p>Thursday Dec 20th</p>
<p>O.T.B. : warned to go to signalling school tomorrow : V.D. &amp; dental inspection in morn : issued with our U.K. kit too : went with Tom Clunie up to see Reg after tea :</p>
<p>Friday Dec 21st</p>
<p>O.T.B. : Reveille 5 am : fell in 6 am &amp; marched to Station : train to Dinton &amp; marched to Fovant camp : camped at 5th T.B. &amp; met many of the Batt N.C.O.'s etc : stroll with Jock Craig after tea :</p>
<p>Saturday Dec 22nd</p>
<p>Fovant : medical &amp; dental exam in morn : concert at night :</p>
<p>Sunday Dec 23rd</p>
<p>Fovant : church parade in morn : stroll through village in the afternoon : Xmas carols hymns etc in YMCA at night &amp; were very nice :</p>
