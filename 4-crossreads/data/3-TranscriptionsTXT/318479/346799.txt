 10 August Company drill.  Bayonet fighting and Batt. drill. 
 11 August 17 Batt. march to the Citadel &amp; back to Heliopolis. 
 12 August Kit inspection and muster parade.  Foot inspection. 
 13 August 1st of the 17th come from Zeatoun and are drafted into the 17th Batt.  The officers, machine gunners and signalers return from the school at Zeatoun.  15th Section go on guard at 7-10 PM.  Receive letter from mother (dated July 9th) and Lou.  Also 1 pair of sox and Coota papers.  "B", "C" &amp; "D" Coys go on night march. 
 14 August 5 Brigade receive pay.  15 Section dismount guard at 12.30 P.M.  Write my last letter from Heliopolis.  Hand in pith helmet.  Issued with colours. 
 15 August Pack kit &amp; prepare to leave camp at 7.30 PM.  Have our last church parade in Egypt.  17th Fall in at 7.30 PM.  March to Zeatoun and entrain for Alexandria. 
 16 August Arrive at Alexandria at 2.30 AM.  No. 4 platoon picked out for loading baggage.  Sail at 6 p.m.  Muster parade in full pack. 
 17 August Life belt parade.  Pass a British submarine. 
 18 August Cranstone &amp; I on as Mess Orderlies.  At Lemnos Is.  Strike Clarry Le Suer of Cootamundra. 
 19 August Tranship from the Alaunia to two small steamers and leave Lemnos for Galipoli.  Issued with ammunition 200 Round. 
 20 August Land at Anzac &amp; waiting in Rest Gully to be sent into the firing line.  17th &amp; 18th leave rest gully at dark.  March up in rear of firing line on the left.  I sleep in a dugout with a New Zealander.  Marten of 3 Pla. wounded.
