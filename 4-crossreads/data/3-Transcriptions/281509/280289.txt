<p>7 March 1916<br/>Shrove Tuesday</p>
<p>Heavy continuous rain up till eleven o'clock. Bob &amp; self detailed for stretcher party for day. Tony for washing party. Send Mary &amp; Ess wires cancelling parade. 10th of 6th &amp; 4th of 8th Reinforce. leave for parade &amp; embarkation next day. <br/>Down town again in aft. Tony &amp; self have tea at Majestic &amp; see the waitress again. Buy present for H's. I go to Hewletts to tea. Ess sick. After call at Palser's get this pen presented to me. Home at 11.30. Meet Alexander at Strathfield station home with him</p>
