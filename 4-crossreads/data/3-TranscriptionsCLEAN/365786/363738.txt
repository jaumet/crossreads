  
 The war.  By reducing our forces at the Front we reduce both our own power in the peace negotiations & Britain's strength in any future conflict.  To stay at home & allow our foreign enemy to grow in striking power is to commit national suicide.  In modern war we can only beat the enemy by seeking him out & destroying him. Therefore send Reinforcements to France. The majority has no right to compel the minority to fight.  But is not the principle of compulsion applied to all citizens in every direction?
