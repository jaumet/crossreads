<p>Monday 10th September1917</p>
<p>Dickebrusch<br/>Reveille 5am<br/>First parade 5.15am. Fed mules then loaded wagons with ammunition as we moved to another position today. Harnessed up and moved out at 11.45am. Officers hid in our waggon. After a few stops on the road we arrived at our new camp about two Kilos from Reninghelst about 2pm, had dinner then back to old camp for a load of timber. Arr. home about 10pm</p>
