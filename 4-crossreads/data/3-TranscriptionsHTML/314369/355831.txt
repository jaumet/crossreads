<p><strike>21 May</strike>  1915<br/>Thursday Contd<br/>  <strike>Friday</strike><br/>the firing is resumed about 2 hours later   &amp;  kept up nearly all night together w/- heavy artillery fire.</p>
<p>Friday 21 may</p>
<p>Firing eases off during the morning   &amp;  we are relieved about midday<br/>Return to camp   &amp;  find dad very bad w/- piles. Go down to field Hosp. with him   &amp;  see him off to Hosp Ship about 8 pm.</p>
<p>1915 May 22<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Have a welcome rest for a few hrs.<br/>Nothing doing except a little fatigue duty</p>
