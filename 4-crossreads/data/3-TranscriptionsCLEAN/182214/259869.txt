Several were up at the time and our ante-aircraft guns were popping away at them. but evidently a plane is a very hard target to hit. as the shooting all went wide.  It was our first taste of gunfire. We left Estoures 10.a.m. July 9th and arrived at Erquinghem sur Lys the same day where we were billited about first farm house, the loft straw was a feathered bed to us & we liked it very much we soon were having a feed of fried eggs & hot coffee as only French people can make it, we also found out where the Hens laid their eggs & often had some without buying for same.
