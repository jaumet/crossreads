 2.  It has not yet ceased raining here To-day.  It is the rainiest day I have ever spent in Blighty.  I have to go out in it but I've got a cape &amp; good boots.  We are still anxious to know the whereabouts of Vic Davies &amp; also Willie Davis.  Cyril Whitehead is on His way back to Australia I think.  Perhaps He will have seen You before this letter reaches You.  I will try &amp; see Bob Lawrence or whatever His name is.  I expect He will go under the name of Waine.
