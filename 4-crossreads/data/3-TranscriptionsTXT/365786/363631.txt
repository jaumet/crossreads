
Leaving the Canal we passed thro a constant succession of fertile fields & picturesque villages, Arab Cemetaries, innumerable clumps of date palms, large herds of sheep & goats feeding together & minded by women & children.  We also saw water buffalo & camels, donkeys & mules, cows & horses all at work, carrying produce to market, or turning water wheels or pulling the little carts they use to thresh the wheat.The whole place looked like a constant succession of Chinamen's gardens & this in a country whose average annual rainfall is less than an one inch!
