Generally the queues resolved into a mad rush, &amp; squeezing, jamming &amp; pushing at the door.<br/>The expeditionary Force Canteen sold tea, chocolate cigarettes &amp; other foods, but here again one had to wait in queues for the best part of an hour generally.<br/>I went into Bologne twice, got some money, [which was good, considering I had lent out some of the other &amp; generally made it do for more than me] &amp; also had one or two food feeds</p>
