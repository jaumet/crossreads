
June, 1916Thursday 8Work as usual all morning & after dinner get the guns & Amtn ready for the range tomorrow.Rigged an old belt filling machine & it did very good service in filling 20 belts in an hour
Friday 9Reveille  4 amBreakfast  4.15 "Target Party  4.30 "Waggons  4.45 "Range  5.30 "first shot fired  6.15 "Each man fired a group of 20 rds & a traverse of 60 rds at Khaki targets at 400Gun works perfectly fired 1300 Rds without a stoppage.
