 Head Quarters 4th Aus Div 5th May 17 
 1st Anzac Corps Death sentence in case of No 3301 private H R Edwards 50th Battalion. I have given this case very careful consideration and although it would appear from the evidence that there was very little doubt  that  the desertion was deliberate with a view of avoiding a specific duty 
 [Harold Roy Edwards death sentence was commuted to 5 years in prison. He returned to Australia in 1919]
