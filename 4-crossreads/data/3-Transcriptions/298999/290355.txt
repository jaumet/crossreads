<p>(wrote to tim and maud.)</p>
<p>as far as I can see.  rested till 2 P.M. cleaned saddle done some washing had tea 5 P.M. fed up and turned in 8 P.M.  was wake up 10.30.  some of them have returned from leave.  they talked half the night never got much sleep.</p>
<p>Wednesday Sept. 5.9.17<br/>turned out 5.30 fed up had Breakfast cleaned up fixed saddle watered Camels.  went for a swim and a good wash.  had dinner 12.30 very hot day rested till 4.30 P.M. fed up had a good swim and wash saddled up and went on outpost nothing doing turned in.</p>
<p>Thursday Sept. 6.9.17<br/>turned out 5.30 saddled up and returned to camp fed up went</p>
