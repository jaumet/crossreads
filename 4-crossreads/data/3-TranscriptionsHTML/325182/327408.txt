<p>They did a good deal of damage and killed or wounded many people.</p>
<p>Amidst all the trouble in France Gen. French was selected to go across and take up duties of Commander in Chief and he began by gaoling every Sinn Fein leader he could lay his hands on.</p>
<p>We have had the most wonderful weather up till the 22nd when dark clouds began to gather.  On <strike>23rd</strike> night of 22nd it rained heavily.  The morning of 23rd opened dull but fresh.  The air was cleared nicely.</p>
