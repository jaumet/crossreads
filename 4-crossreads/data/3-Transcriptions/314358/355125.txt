<p>4<br/>Again the ringing as of waters through the air.</p>
<p>Then</p>
<p>A whine, like swift anger and agony mingles, passed high over us from the enemy, and changed slightly as it passes, till it is almost like a whistle from the lips of [indecipherable]</p>
<p>It is the sound of a shell sent into the city, to further wreck and destroy and to slay.</p>
<p>We listen</p>
<p>Hark!</p>
<p>We wait and watch</p>
<p>For we know there will soon be a merciless explosion, and a flash to tell us in what part of the city this messenger of evil falls.</p>
<p>Bang-r-r-r-rack_Ping-g-g- Wizz-z-z-z Flash<br/>We hear, and behold and from the knowledge we have already obtained of the avenues of wreck, we judge the heavy curse has fallen and expended its terrific fury</p>
