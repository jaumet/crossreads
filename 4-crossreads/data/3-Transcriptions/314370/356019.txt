<p>April, 1916<br/>Saturday 29<br/>Went for a run round Cairo this afternoon &amp; after tea to the empire Cinema<br/>My chum Wrigley has received his commission in the 5th Div.</p>
<p>Sunday 30<br/>Went out to Patti's for dinner &amp; had a real good afternoon<br/>in the evening we went to the American Cosmogaph</p>
