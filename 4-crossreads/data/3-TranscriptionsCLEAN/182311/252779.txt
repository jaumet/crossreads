 of course we did so, breaking our journey for four hours in Portsmouth, seeing (only at a distance) the Brittanic, the largest vessel in the world, now employed as a hospital ship; the City Walls, Tudor House, Bargate, Norman House, all in the few hours, and as well a call of about half an hour's duration on Miss Bound, a friend of Miss Hayes.  Then back to camp and to hard work again.  Ah well - - 
    With love. 
   
          Harold.
