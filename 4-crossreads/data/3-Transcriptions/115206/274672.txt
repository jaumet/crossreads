<p>April 1918<br/>Friday 12<br/>Bussey &ndash; La &ndash; Dours.  D.R.<br/>Weather glorious. During morning rode across to Pont &ndash; Noyelles, a Querrie had a look at bridges there incomplete.<br/>In the afternoon saw a very spectacular feat in air &ndash; one Fritzer dropped.<br/>Heavy bomb raid by Gothas at 5am.<br/>Rode across country to Bonnay and reported at Bty for mail. &ndash; received 15 letters and a parcel of socks.<br/>Another bomb raid per Fritz at 7.30pm.</p>
