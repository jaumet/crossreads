As it is necessary for all ships travelling through at night to have searchlights, quite a large amount of gear for them was taken on board, as well as half a dozen men to operate it.  Immediately inside the Canaal a big French warship was anchored; as we passed her our ensign was dipped, and a crowd of soldiers drawn up on the main deck; in reply her ensign was dipped, and officers mustered on the quarter deck saluted.
