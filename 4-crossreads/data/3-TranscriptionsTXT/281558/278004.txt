 wrote after tea 
 Monday June 25th 
 Bapaume : cloudy : reveille at 4 am : parades 5 &ndash; 6.30 am, 8 &ndash; 11 am, &amp; 9 &ndash; 9.15 pm : we S.B.s went for wood : 
 Tuesday June 26th 
 Bapaume : still thundery : reveille 4 am usual parades : went &amp; looked over a cemetery &amp; seen some of our lads (450) Harry Bolt is buried there : got a "P" of Tim Lawler's &amp; Harry Bolt's : 
 Wednesday June 27th 
 Bapaume : thunder showers : revei 4 am : usual parades : after tea went into the town with Alf for a stroll round : 
 Thursday June 28th
