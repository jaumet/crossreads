<p>confusion in narrow saps, being crowded so. had few casualties returned about 1am OK. &amp; turned in for sleep.</p>
<p>3rd. Thursday.<br/>Stayed with Kitchens all day, boys sent on several fatigues, rations &amp; munitions during day.</p>
<p>4th. Friday. &amp; 5th sat<br/>Bombardment by our artillery commences at 9.15pm. and 15 minutes later our boys were in Fritz's 2nd line. We in gully stood too till 10.30 then turned in but called out at midnight &amp; with Picks &amp; Shovels all</p>
