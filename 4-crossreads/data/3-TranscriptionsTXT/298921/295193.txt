 August, 1915 Tuesday 10 Not far from Alexandria beautiful weather to be killing men &ndash; The Turks fought fair but shelled us going out to Hospital ships, probably could not tell we were only for the meat ships, they hit 2 with Shrapnel. Out of the Boat into Hospital Train. No.1 Australian General Hospital Heliopolis the same place I used to visit the sick ere I was one &ndash; SIKHS attended us on the Train &amp; nurses were very good &ndash; A good many died coming over.
