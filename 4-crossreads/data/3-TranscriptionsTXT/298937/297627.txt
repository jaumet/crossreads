 Friday 28th July 
 Capt. Earle Page of Grafton came here &amp; took charge this morning. He is a good sort &amp; had quite a long chat with us. He has gone to Alexandria on a few hours leave so Dr. Van Epen has not left us altogether yet. Today we had a big riot in the Camp.
