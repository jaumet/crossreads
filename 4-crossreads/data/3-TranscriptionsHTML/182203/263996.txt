<p>get his things together &amp; fix up. I'm seeing him later on  &amp;  shall give him this letter to post as soon as he gets to Melb. Suppose you will hear all about me from the dear boy. <br/>Am going to a grand dinner to-night at the Camp of 21st Batt. Shall write &amp; tell you all about it in a day or so.<br/>I really have very little time for writing &amp; it is too hot to sit for long in one place. I must have a bath now    &amp; get dress.<br/>Sister Bolton has been sent off to find her brother, he has been ordered to Australia too by the same boat as Charlie. He is <strike>Lt.</strike> a Lieutenant &amp; they did not tell him until just now. <br/>A train is just arriving with some more poor wounded men. Hope they are not badly wounded. Have been expecting a number for days now &ndash; this hosp. has been cleared out &amp; sent to Atelier or Luna Park to make room for others. Oh, this is a dreadful war</p>
