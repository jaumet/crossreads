I had no less than 32 trucks to fill with various explosives &amp; I was responsible for the whole concern, the gang started about 7.30 &amp; did not finish till 12 oclock &amp; by the time I had finished balancing my books &amp; getting the requisition for tomorrow it was 2 oclock, to give you an idea of the stuff we sent out, 52,000 rounds of S.A.A.
