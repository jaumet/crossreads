 28 December, Sunday 
 I try to K another fellow &amp; fail. I do all jobs even to nursery maid. We try to ride the bike but the swim beats us. We go up for eggs but get no fruit. We chop a lot of wood. Am tired. George sick. We all go to bed about 8.30. plenty music to make me remember. 
 29 December, Monday 
 We (O &amp; I) come down alone. A lovely run on a cool morning. I work well all day. Rec'd X'mas Card from I of M. Nette &amp; Johnsons are at Home &amp; we have Music etc.
