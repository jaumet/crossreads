<p>so we marched down to the tram terminus in a 2 parties the R.S.M. in charge of one &amp; Sgt Stott &amp; myself in charge of the other, 130 men in all &amp; we had a sprinkling of N.Z. S. Africans &amp; Canadians with us, so we were all Colonials reaching the terminus we caught the trams which ran us across the busy Seine up through Rouen &amp; opposite the Park or Gardens we all got out for our Pay Office was quite close here.  we were soon paid &amp; then</p>
