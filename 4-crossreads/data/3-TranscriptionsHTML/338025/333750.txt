<p>Thursday 26th July 1917</p>
<p>Came of guard at 6pm.<br/>At 9.30pm I was warned to go out with team at midnight but the guard came round at midnight and said that we would not be wanted till 5am.<br/>About 11.30pm 2 of Fritz planes dropped bombs near our camp.</p>
