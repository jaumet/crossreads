<p>Tuesday 28/12/15<br/>All morning trench digging; afternoon half holiday.  First aid Lecture at night by Capt North, subject &ndash; Arresting Hemorrhage &ndash; Exceptionally cold night.</p>
<p>Wednesday  29/12/15<br/>False alarm at 10 AM; quite a stir in camp; all tents dropped &amp; men rushed to their respective trenches.  Col Tibbey visited camp 12 noon.  Enemy scouts again seen in close proximity to camp.</p>
<p>Thursday 30/12/15<br/>Route march during morning, otherwise</p>
