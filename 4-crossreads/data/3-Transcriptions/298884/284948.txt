<p>It has been my misfortune hitherto as a rule to strike a girl who became emotional as the hour of parting drew nigh but these two were made of much sterner stuff and were very sensible so that our parting was a cheerful and not tearful one.</p>
<p>The girl workers at the Y.M.C.A. canteen and Salvation Army huts have had a strenuous and often a very rough time in the camps in Bath England and France but they have <strike>had a</strike> stuck to their good work gamely. The diggers are not exactly a complimentary crowd but a more appreciative lot of fellows do not exist.</p>
