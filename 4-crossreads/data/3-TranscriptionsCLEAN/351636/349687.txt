  
 The Centre for Soldiers' Wives & Mothers, 92B. Pitt Street, Sydney. 31-1-16 
 Dear Mr. Layton - Mrs. Machron who is now receiving a small weekly allowance from your Fund is in difficulties about meeting payment on a [indecipherable] on furniture. Could you assist in this case? Mrs. Machron is under heavy expenses in trying to pay off her small home as well & could manage tho' with difficulty to get along if this debt were paid as they are pressing her 
 Yours faithfully B. Booth
