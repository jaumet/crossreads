 Sunday 19 August 1917. 
 Gave up trying to sleep at 4 a.m. Had a little hard rations &amp; water. Are now passing over captured ground &amp; through stations built by Allies on occupatn. 
 Every mile or so there is a redoubt on a sandridge and surrounded by barb &amp; springwire entanglements. Soldiers graves often seen here &amp;&nbsp;there near Stations. 
 Arrived 7.40 a.m. at No 4 Staty Hospl &nbsp; on shores of the Mediterranean Sea. Hosp. not yet staffed as it is new. First sighed Medit. Sea at 7 a.m. Rly line follows shore now. 
 Arrived El Arish 8.45 am. Left 9.30 a.m. &amp; crushed still more in trucks round meat boxes.&nbsp;H ead for open desert . Passed through fortified &amp; entrenched country. Taube flew over at 10 a.m. 
 Arrived Raffa 11.15. Shiekh&nbsp;Nuran at 12.20 (due at 12.17 pm) Transferred our kit on camels to Scottish Horse Amb. bivouac at 2.30 pm. Light ration at 3 p.m. &amp; at 3.30 started to build our own bivouacs. Bully &amp; bread ration for tea. At dusk watched from a distance an aerial&nbsp;engagement. Shells seen&nbsp;bursting round the 6 planes up. 
 Guns on Gaza front heard plainly through the night.
