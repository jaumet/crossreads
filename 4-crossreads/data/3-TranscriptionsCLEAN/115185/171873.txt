  
 12 - from Archie S. Skirving since I arrived here - one from Rosamond - one from Father one from Constance one from Mrs Man & one from Major Man who is invalided from the firing line to Alexandria - one from Aunt Maria. These letters have been with the view & a few books - chiefly the Psalms in Modern Readers edition. My only comfort & help. I must go back on duty now. We are expecting another convoy of wounded. The camel hair shawl belonging to Denis Allen
