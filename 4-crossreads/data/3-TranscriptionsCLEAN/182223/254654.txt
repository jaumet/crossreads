
Thursday 15 April 1915Attended to JBN. 10 a.m. Report to O.C. re officers Riding on plateau between 7 & 8 a.m. Also between 5 & 6-30 p.m. Reading, writing, & sleeping the remainder of the day. Several copies of the D. Telegraph recd from Doffie Williams
Friday 16 April 1915Riding morning & afternoon Not out during the day otherwise.
Saturday 17 April 1915Hospital. Reading and writing all day. Went to the pyramid of Aborawash [Pyramid of Abu Rowash] this evening, about 7 miles to the Westward along the dessert.
Sunday 18 April 1915Mass at 7-30 a.m. Reading & writing all day Ride between 5 & 6-30 p.m. Concert in hall between 7-30 & 9 p.m.
