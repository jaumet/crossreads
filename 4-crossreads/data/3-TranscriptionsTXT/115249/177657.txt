 From - Brigadier. 
 To - Rear Admiral C. 
  12th Septr.14:  
 (6-5 a.m.) 
 My only object suggesting retention wireless station was to effect repairs and turn to our own use. &nbsp;If, however, not so required, recommend abandonment of same and withdrawal of all troops. from there to Herbertshohe, and carry out original programme of Naval Reserve garrisoning Herbertshohe , balance of force direct to Rabaul.
