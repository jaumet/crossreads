<p>1915<br/>May. 4 Tue.<br/>The little Chapel at the House has been converted into a ward with 25 beds in it &ndash; men are also on mattresses in all passages &amp; on stair-landings throughout the house &ndash; Our daily returns to Headquarters are shewing "No empty beds"<br/>At 8 p.m. sent into Ghezireh Palace to assist with rush of wounded expected.<br/>They commenced to arrive about 10.30 p.m. &amp; as there was no clerical work doing <strike>leant</strike> lent a hand to get them into wards &ndash; about 300 altogether- returned in Motor Ambulance to Mena arriving at 1.15 a.m. About 230 admitted.</p>
