<p>Docks Rest Camp till 3.30 p.m. then boarded the "Viper" &amp; crossed the Channel to Southampton.</p>
<p>Mon. Nov. 27th: -<br/>Arrived at Southampton at 8 o'clock this morning (The "Viper" did not leave Havre till midnight). Entrained for London &amp; arrived at Waterloo Station at 10.30 A.M. put up at the Union Jack Club opposite station.</p>
<p>Thurs. Dec 7th: -<br/>My furlough &amp; good time is finished &amp;  left Waterloo Station at 4 P.M. this afternoon &amp;  aboard the "Viper" &amp;  away from Southampton tonight.</p>
<p>Fri. Dec. 8th: -<br/>Arrived at Le Havre early this morning &amp; put up again at the Docks rest Camp.</p>
<p>Sun. Dec 10th : -<br/>Left Le Havre by train at 6.30 this morning &ndash; on our way to Albert.<br/>Hear that my battalion has moved from Flesselles up to Mametz<br/> Just passed through Rouen.</p>
