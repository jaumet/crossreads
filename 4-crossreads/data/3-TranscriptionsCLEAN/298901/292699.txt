Passed through Amiens on my return and lately it has been heavily bombarded.  Not a street seems to have escaped & all is gloom, desolation & destruction where once was fashion & amusement.  Started getting busy after I got back, both wounded & gas & apparently a stunt is in close proximity. 
 Wednesday 24th April Went up to the Dressing room before breakfast as cases had started coming in freely by daylight.  All night long a heavy bombardment was going on & the enemy shelled our villages at intervals of about 15 minutes putting over about 10 shots, a second between each.
