<p>18 Sunday<br/>Same routine.  Miss F &amp; I went for a walk after tea.  Fine day  Started a story about Iain Price</p>
<p>19 Monday<br/>Fine day.  Wrote all day.  Walked with Miss F. after supper.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20</p>
<p>Went with Miss Lay to Hitchin market.  Then met Matron Nye at Stn.</p>
<p>21 Wednesday<br/>Had various little walks &amp; much talk with Mrs Nye.</p>
