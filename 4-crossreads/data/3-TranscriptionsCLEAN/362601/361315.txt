  
 20 December, Saturday 
 Otto does not come in. but Esme does. Pa comes up & we have lot of work. I try to get Bike Home but come a thud at North Sydney. Tram to Mosman ans Motor to Manly. Dick & Ellis at Home. We catch Dolly. Tea & Etta & E goes to pictures. D & I to band, & D & Mum up & down Corso. 
 21 December, Sunday 
 Dick goes to Town. D G & I do garden. I go to Manly. Ring up Dick. E & I surf & there are many rescues. I go shopping in my deshabille & Home & go to meet Reg & Dick in deshabille, we go to Baths to swim. It is very cold. tea at E's & go to bend. Catch 9 boat & tram.
