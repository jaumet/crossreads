<p>Fri. 25th May<br/>Spent day in camp.</p>
<p>Sat. 26th<br/>Went into Boulogne at 2.30 p.m.<br/>Fine town but not as good as Marseille.</p>
<p>Sun. 27<br/>In camp.</p>
<p>Mon. 28<br/>In Camp.</p>
<p>Tues. 29<br/>Leave to Boulogne.</p>
<p>Wed. 30<br/>Leave to Boulogne, saw the American medical unit arrive.  Climbed to top of Grand Army Column.<br/>Marked out.</p>
<p>Fri. 1st June<br/>Marched out of No. 7 Con. Camp to Base Details &amp; went to town.</p>
<p>Sat. 2nd June<br/>Went out i/c of big fatigue party to Rue Constantine Bks., Boulogne.</p>
<p>Sun. 3rd June<br/>Same as yesterday.</p>
