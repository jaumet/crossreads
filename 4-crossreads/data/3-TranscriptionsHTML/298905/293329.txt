<p>I have been trying all over the place in each place we come to, to find a book suitable to keep my diary in but have had no luck so far, they all have the French note books which have very fine lines ruled both way's, &amp; they are no good to me I shall have to one of some sort shortly for this wont last much longer<br/>Saw General Birdwood &amp; Walker here this morning they watched us pass through the town, we done the march quite easily &amp; the boys all came swinging down the street singing for all they were worth I expect the French people thought we were a happy go lucky lot.<br/>There are 10 of us where I am billeted &amp; the old couple are fine typical French people as soon as we had got our packs off they pumped some water up for us to have a wash with, &amp; they were as</p>
