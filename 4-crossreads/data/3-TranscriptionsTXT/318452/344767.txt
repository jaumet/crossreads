 Sunday 7th The Batt. Came back to supports today. 
 Monday 8th We moved back to Dickebush huts, &amp; the Batt. came out to here, tonight in the driving wind &amp; rain. 
 Tuesday 9 We left again this morning, by Motor busses, for Connaught, lines near Abeele, where we are in tents We are back from the line about 8 miles. The wind is terrible &amp; living in tents with no floors is anything but comfortable.
