
13.3.15
Terriers at all, as the infantry fixed them up on their own & the Light Horse was not called on.  went nowhere after tea & retired to bed early
Sat 13thHad to parade with our blankets this morning & had to hand over any blankets we had over two each, every one is confident that we shall be leaving here in a few days.  The only parade we had to-day was  to clean our equipment;  Stan went to Heliopolis tonight & I had a game of billiards.  am on the feeding party at 9 oclock;  Yule has caught a sore throat
