<p>Saturday 19 February, 1916</p>
<p>Am not sure whether I am sickening for the mumps or not.</p>
<p>This afternoon we were inspected by Genl. Legge who said there&nbsp;was a chance of us going to France. He read us a lecture on discipline which I hope does some good as we are very lax in that respect.</p>
