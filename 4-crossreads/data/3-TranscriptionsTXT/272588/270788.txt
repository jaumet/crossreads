 Sunday 20 October Entrain at Calais &amp; travell to Etaples by an awful train arriving after six hours journey &amp; put up for the night at rest camp. 
 Monday 21 October Arrive back at the unit at Pont Remy after travelling from Etaples to Longpre &amp; having to walk back to Pont Remy at night.
