<p>Raining heavily again. Signallers below us flooded out. The Arabic name for Richon is Ayn[?] Khua[?]<br/>Thursday Dec 20<br/>Still raining had to move horses Rode to Canteen for Christmas supplies<br/>Friday Dec 21<br/>Saw Squadron of our planes flie over today laden with pills for the Turks.<br/>Saturday Dec 22<br/>On half rations &amp; roads impassable on account of heavy rains which is also destroying Railway<br/>Sunday Dec 23<br/>Prospect for fine Christmas very poor. Cold, wet, miserable</p>
