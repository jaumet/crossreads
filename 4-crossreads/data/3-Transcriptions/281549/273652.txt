<p>at Buckholz Junction and thence on to Soltau, arriving there at 4 pm.  On arrival in Soltau we were all given a hot water bath and we all had our hair cropped very short, We also had our clothes fumigated to get rid of the chats, if any.</p>
<p>In the camp I was doing odd jobs. On Wednesday I went into Soltau to have a tooth drawn. The Hun nearly drew my head off with the tooth.</p>
<p>In the same afternoon I was given orders together with 5 other Australians 3 Canadians and 3 Englishmen to prepare to move out to L&uuml;neburg by the 6.30 am train the following morning.</p>
<p>We were all put into Clink for the night, so that we would not be missing when the time came for us to move off in the morning</p>
