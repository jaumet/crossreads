<p>The hot wind, the Khamasin causes a yellow fog to prevail owing to the sand &amp; dust it brings from the desert.  It may blow for three days at a time.  In spite of this all plants flourish.  The land is truly a land flowing with milk &amp; greenstuff.  The only honey we saw was imported &amp; very poor.<br/>Cairo is like a dirty mixture of Naples &amp; Colombo.  Cairo is the largest city in Africa &amp; contains the residence of the Khedive.  It is a mixture of old &amp; new.  The Romans under Augustus fortified the Citadel &amp; it became the headquarters of one of the 3 legions stationed</p>
