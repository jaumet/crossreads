
Tuesday 21st OctApptd Temporary Corp vice 20th BlissIt was a crisp nice day. In the morning I was along to Ordnance.
Friday 22ndWet and windy. Very miserable in the morning but in the afternoon it cleared up a little & I went to Ordnance but got wet coming back as it poured til about ted [?]. Our trip to supply that night was not too pleasant.
Saturday 23rdIt fined up to-day a little but still it was overcast & windy. Had a trip to Ordnance & saw Owens of YMCA who run a Canteen Store with goods from Embross Isld. He makes trip to & fro every week. A big crowd was there but I managed to get inside knowing Owens & secured some cakes & tin fish
