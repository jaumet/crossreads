<p>26 August 1918 Monday (X to X crossed out)<br/>X Have just read in the Town &amp; Country journal of May 1st two columns of "piffle" headed Value of Cavalry.  It is a pity the editor allows such tripe to be copied from the New York Herald.X<br/>Thank God Recruiting has improved, it would shame many men to see some of the old chaps hanging on half dead with fever out here.<br/>Tried the stream with a Mess Tin for any "Color" but not much alluvial plenty big "crabs."</p>
