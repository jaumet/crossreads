  
 13.5.15 Thurs. 13th.  Had a very good nights rest & were up at 4 oclock ready to go into the trenches if required, we had to stand ready in case of emergency.  It looks a grand sight this morning from here;  the hills are covered with green bushes & the hill opposite us is so steep steps have had to be cut in it to allow the lads to go up & down.  With the glasses we can see a few dead Turks among the bushes also one or two Australians.  I believe there are dozens of our lads dead & they cannot be got at on account of the fire from the Turks.
