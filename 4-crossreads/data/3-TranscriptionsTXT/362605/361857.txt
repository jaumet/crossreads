No 2 CampPARK HouseSalisbury,13.4.19
Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George,
Here once again on the 13th I have been sent from Bulford to this Camp, this one where I landed from Egypt &amp; later from France.  I come over here on a better job as W/O in charge of Nursing &amp; Treatment.  It is a very clean &amp; compact Camp &amp; is about 1/3 of the work at Bulford.
