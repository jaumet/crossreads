<p>one minute the sun will be shining and the next it will be snowing.  The food is very <br/>good, eggs for tea at 4d each seems an extravagance but we have them.  There is a <br/>band there but according to the advertised programme it is only a 4th rate band.<br/>It is said that after the war, it will only take 1 transport to take the Australians back, 2 to <br/>take back the identification discs and 6 to take back the crime sheets.<br/>The orderlies here are being transferred to form</p>
