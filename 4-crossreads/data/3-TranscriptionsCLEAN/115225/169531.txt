 Feb. 11  th    In the morning we cleaned up the buss for another inspection. In the afternoon we walked up to Neuve Eglise & back - 
   
 Feb. 12  th    Joe Grim is due back from Blighty leave today - We went up to Suzanne's for our usual coffee in the morning & afternoon  Frank was on guard in the evening so I went along to 5  th   A.S.P & spent the evening with Len - 
   
 Feb. 13  th    . We went out to-day for 1  st   Anzac C.T.S.C. & did a lot of running about. Raining mostly all day - I was on  guard in the evening, they do a dinkum guard here & sleep in the guard room. I had reliefs 5.30 P.M. till 7.30 & 11.30 P M till 1.30 A.M. 
   
 Feb. 14  th    Weather still dull but no rain to-day. We walked to Nieppe in the afternoon (next village to Armentieres) & had a look round the church, which was much battered. our troops occupied any shelter in the way of houses that remained fit for shelter. I wrote to Jim in Hospital in Blighty -  
   
 Feb. 15  th    The sun came out to-day for a change. Frank & I went  up to Steenwerck Station & had a hair cut. took some photos of Suzanne's family -
