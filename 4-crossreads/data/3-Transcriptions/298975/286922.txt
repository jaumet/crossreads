<p>had several Tram rides free. They have quite a fine Puraide &amp; baths on the ocean front <strike>Some of the people throw us fruit cigarettes</strike><br/>There are 2 or 3 blacks to one white. Found quite a fine Mosque at night &amp; saw them worship</p>
<p>Monday 16<br/>The Coaling finished this morning. All the work is done by natives with much noise. Postcards fruit Etc was sold all the morning &amp; thrown on to the boat, and a lot of fruit was given away especially by a Miss Campble of the YMCA. Left about 12.30 PM with much cheering &amp; cooeing.</p>
