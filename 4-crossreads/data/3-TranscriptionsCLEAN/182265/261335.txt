  
 All the huts have been overrun with Earwigs for about a week. They crawl up ones legs & sleeves, fall from the roof on one's face as he sleeps, get in socks & clothes & generally make pests of themselves. 
  Tuesday  - Visited Salisbury this afternoon with Mess Sergeant Crofts to arrange purchase of spirits, also tobacco, groceries etc for our Officers' Mess, of which I am Secretary. Left my watch to be cleaned & inscribed with my name & Battalion. Bought a few little souvenirs for Jess. Returned to Wylye Station & walked from there to Camp. 
  Wedneday  - Bombing all day. Special demonstration by Group N.C.O's of Rifle Grenade firing at Shell holes, communication trench, and also from a Rifle Battery.
