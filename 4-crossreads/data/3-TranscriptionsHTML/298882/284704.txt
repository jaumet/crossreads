<p>High Explosive.</p>
<p>Hostile shelling fairly heavy each day about 12 p.m to 4 p.m. otherwise quiet during daylight.  Night of 15th many gas shells and H.E. fired causing a number of casualties amongst our men on working parties.</p>
<p>Weather has been fine and ground drying up rapidly.</p>
