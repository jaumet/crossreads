<p>12th July '16<br/>Aeroplane scare yesterday morning again. The machine came right over the camp  &amp;  we had two shots at it with a 3" gun which is mounted close to the camp here, the second was a good shot  &amp;  must have been unpleasantly near him; he cleared off anyhow. It is very curious how one instinctively ducks now when hearing a gun. I, for one, simply can't restrain myself from so doing even though I know it is our own gun. It is instinctive &amp; natural &amp; everyone does it, in fact some people do the most</p>
