<p>Thursday 7 June 1917.</p>
<p>Fire not yet located but smoke<s>d</s>&nbsp;has ceased to issue from air vents. Watch &amp; precautions still being taken. One boat in sight at 7 a.m. but now as far as we know we are &quot;unescorted and alone in open ocean&quot;.</p>
<p>Weather moderate. Did washing during morning &amp; more in afternoon. Wrote home in aftern.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Cpl in charge of Isolation &amp; no fault found in any way at the inspection.</p>
