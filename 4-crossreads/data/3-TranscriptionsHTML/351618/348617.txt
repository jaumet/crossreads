<p>Tuesday 14th<br/>I went to Ordnance to get officers clothing etc also to arrange about mules for drawing indent goods on the following day. Received note from Jack Fitzgerald our Field Cashier</p>
<p>15th Sept Wednesday<br/>It rained. Had to get up to catch the drops through my roof etc. Mud everywhere &ndash; sticks like glue much worse than our black earth etc. Fairly cold etc. Fine big mail arrived.</p>
