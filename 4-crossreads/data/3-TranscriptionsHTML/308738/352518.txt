<p>Sept. 21 Tuesday 1915<br/>Working all day. Had some washing done.</p>
<p>Sept. 22 Wednesday 1915<br/>Working all day.<br/>Thorpe and one of the clerks left tonight to join a regiment for the front. Also Little who is suffering from a veneral Desease discharged from Post Office, &amp; sent to Base Mustapha.<br/>Pay day I drew 7/- or 34&frac12; piastres for the 1st time since leaving Australia.</p>
<p>Sept. 23 Thursday 1915<br/>More new hands put on at Post Office this morning.<br/>Went out this afternoon to see Watkins at Con Home Mustapha took his pay out 43 piastres. Very windy. Saw a number of prisoners who are sentenced to 1-2 &amp; 3 years imprisonment for refusing to go back to the firing line &amp; insulting officers.</p>
<p>Sept. 24 Friday 1915<br/>Working all day</p>
