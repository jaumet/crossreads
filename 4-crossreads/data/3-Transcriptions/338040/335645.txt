<p>Mar. 28th<br/>Quartered in barn over stables-Route march- sick parade. Constant firing of British Artillery plainly heard. Coffee au Lait.<br/>Mar.29th<br/>Sick Parade- Fatigue-Practical Experience in combating gas by means of helmet "Weeping gas" Accident to Major Dolman &ndash;myself Mess Orderly.<br/>Mar.30th<br/>"Pay Day" (45fr) Busy estaminets and Canteens. Big Battle evident judging by continuous firing.</p>
