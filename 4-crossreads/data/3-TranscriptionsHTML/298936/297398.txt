<p>August 1915</p>
<p>Sunday 29</p>
<p>Stocktaking continued &amp; finished upstairs.</p>
<p>Getting near to Suez now.</p>
<p>The intense heat which has oppressed us for the last day &amp; now has disappeared at the bidding of a cool breeze.</p>
<p>Everybody getting ready to disembark.</p>
