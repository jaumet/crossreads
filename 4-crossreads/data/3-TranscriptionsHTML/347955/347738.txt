<p>[wrote to Alma ang aggie]<br/>sent to 31st G.H. Cairo which Place we reached 2.P.M. after a lot of messing about  had dinner and rested  had tea 4.30 Bread a dripping<br/>Tuesday Aug 13.8.18<br/>feeling a little better  getting a good dose of quinine<br/>Wednesday Aug.14.8.18<br/>feeling better to day it is very hot here.<br/>Thursday Aug 15.8.18<br/>feeling better to day.<br/>Friday Aug 16.8.18<br/>felt a little dizzy in the head to day have been lying down all day.  was examined By a lady Doctor I don't know what the next generation will be like  she is a nice Dr dressed in military uniform all Pockets</p>
