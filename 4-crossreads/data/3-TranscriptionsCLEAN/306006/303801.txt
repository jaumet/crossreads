 1-4-17 Sun 1st Church parade this morning but I did not attend; & an inspection of the lines by the C.O.; This afternoon we played B Sqds (No.2 team) football & scored a win 5nil; No 1 team of A Sqdn defeated No 2 of C Sqdn
