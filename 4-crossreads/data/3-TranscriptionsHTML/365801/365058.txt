<p>2nd D.H.Q.<br/>June 14th 1916</p>
<p>My Dear Judge Ferguson,<br/>A few hours ago the sad news of poor Arthur's death reached us here at Divisional Head  Quarters.  I hasten to express to you all my deepest sympathy in this your hour of grief.  The receipt of the news shocked us all.  No officer of this Division was more universally respected!  The Officers and men under his command, his senior officers, all had the fullest confidence in his military ability, -  All believed he had a brilliant future before him as  a soldier.  I need scarcely say, I more than shared this opinion.  At present I am attached to General Legge's General Staff, and when the news came through all were unanimous in their expressions of regret at his untimely</p>
