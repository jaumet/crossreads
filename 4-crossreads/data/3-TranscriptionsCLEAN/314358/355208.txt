
9grave where no cross has been erected
Vile gilded flies leads ones sight to where the shoulder bones of another hastily buried brave man lies.  A single blood red poppy droops close by a little cross on which is crudly written in indelible pencil "Here lies a soldier unknown"
A white butterfly like the ghost of a holy thought flits by to rest awhile on a struging nettle, and flit again and he gone
A silent brown sparrow is in search of vanished eaves which never shall be built again.
A party of soldiers are mending a road through this festering scar of war to lead to the front that the enemy may be reached
