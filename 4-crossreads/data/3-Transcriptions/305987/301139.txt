<p>Wednesday 28th:  In supports<br/>Thursday March 1st:  In front line 5 a.m. at 10 p.m we "went over &amp; bombed Huns out &amp; occupied trench<br/>Friday 2nd:  Barrage of intense Artillery fire on the trench.  Huns counter attacked. Shortage of bombs we had to retire:  7 am bombed him out again. 9 a.m retired again having run out of bombs. 12 noon advanced again. Huns retired, leaving trench in our hands.  Relieved at mid-night. Came back to Shelter Wood.<br/>Wednesday 7th:  Moved up to Bazentin-le-Petit.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13th:  Huns retired. We advanced &amp; occupied the villages Biefvillers &amp; Grevillers</p>
