<p>21 May 1918<br/>Whitsun Tuesday<br/>Nothing to report still acting as Canteen Sergt. Nice warm weather.</p>
<p>1918 May 22<br/>Wednesday<br/>Just living in our own little narrow Camp world and hearing nothing much of the doings of the Outside world. Nothing to report.</p>
