<p>Don't think that I want to take you away from your present drugdery &amp; promote you to a more strenuous job of waiting hand &amp; foot on an invalide.  I want you about the place &amp; I fear I shall want a bit of coddling but I am perfectly sane &amp; muscularly fit &amp; no venereal disease meeself not having taken no risks.  Just this finally we must have a place of sorts no matter how makeshift out of London.</p>
