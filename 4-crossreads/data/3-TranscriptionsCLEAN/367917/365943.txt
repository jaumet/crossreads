
Pry.BelgiumDecember 26th 1918
Mr. Justice FergusonSupreme Court ChambersSydney N.S.W.
Dear Sir,I yesterday (Christmas day) received your very valuable Xmas parcel containing cigarettes, tobacco, lollies & etc, together with sprig of wattle, for which please accept my very best thanks & I must say it is very kind & thoughtful of you in thinking of us out here -
On Xmas eve I received notice to be in readiness to leave here on 28th inst I proceed to England thence on furlough to Australia, this notice together with Your parcel, I look upon as receiving two valuable gift, & possibly
