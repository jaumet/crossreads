<p>We stood by all day Saturday as supports &amp; it rained in the morning</p>
<p>Butcher &amp; self remained in firing trench until 9 30 am &amp; the rain turned everything into mud &amp; we were in the hell of a mess Snatched about 2 hours sleep at our spell time in a small hole</p>
