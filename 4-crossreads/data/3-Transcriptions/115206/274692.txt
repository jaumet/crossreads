<p>May 1918<br/>Thursday 2</p>
<p>Bussy la Daours<br/>Weather fine, Had a hurry up run to Bonnay and called at battery on way back.<br/>Received payment first for some considerable time, very welcome indeed. Col Caddy received  C.D.C.<br/>Slept in General's room and learnt quite a considerable amount of very important things while acting as bodyguard.<br/>Weather fine  plenty of flying. Bombs again around<br/>here.</p>
