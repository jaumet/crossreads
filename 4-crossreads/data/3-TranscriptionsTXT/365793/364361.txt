 Spt 13. Cold, wet &amp; windy today as yesterday, but we still hope to see "old Sol' score a victory Several fairly decent seas have been shipped on the Port Saloon deck &amp; the well decks. Q'ld pineapple dished out again last night; a tin to 3 or 4 men. No 7 table &ndash; noted for hums &amp; appetites. When we eat all our own tucker we hum some more from the other tables. The mail for Melb' closes at 8 pm on the 13th ins't. Expect to reach Melb' in time for the show.
