<p>Tuesday 10th June<br/>The letters to Rita of the last fortnight will, in them selves, serve as a diary, so there is no need to keep so closely to this<br/>We are still drilling hard &amp; the general effect of this camp &amp; surroundings on the men is depressing.<br/>It is not that there is not good scenery near, but the actual camp commands only a bare hill, &amp; that naturally has a depressing effect.</p>
