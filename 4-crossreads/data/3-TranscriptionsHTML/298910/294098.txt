<p>26/1/17  Friday</p>
<p>Heavy frost &ndash; absolutely the coldest night I have ever experienced &ndash; should say the Thermometre must have been much under zero &ndash; probably 30 degrees &ndash; Hardly any cases in last night.</p>
<p>Saturday  27/1/17</p>
<p>Heavy Frost &amp; cold wind blowing &ndash; The snow is all contracting with the frost. At daylight this morning the 29th Division on out right opened up a severe bombardment &amp; followed it up, taking two lines of Fritzs trenches, they advanced 800 yards on a three mile front, captured a very important redoubt near Les Bouefs &amp; brought in about 400 prisoners</p>
