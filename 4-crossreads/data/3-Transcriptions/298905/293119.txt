<p>we will be moving in a day or so at most<br/>The morning is nice &amp; warm &amp; I hope it continues like it. The other day not far from here there were a few Maoris &amp; some Frenchmen doing a job, when one of the Frenchmen asked a Maori how long it would take him to chop a certain tree down &amp; he answered 1 min, the Frenchman laughed at him, &amp; challenged him to do it, so the Maori hopped into it, &amp; fell it 14 sec. under the min. the Frenchmen were astounded.<br/>18th.
