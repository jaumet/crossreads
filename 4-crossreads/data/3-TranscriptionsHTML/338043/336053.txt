<p>(according to Cook's, the finest Place in Europe)<br/> Situated between Garden of Tuileries and the Champs Elysees &ndash; It has a tragic history. During a fireworks display over 3,000 were killed &amp; injured &ndash; Later it was the site of the Guillotine when so many were beheaded during the Reign of Terror &ndash;<br/>- In the centre of the Place stands the Obelisk of Luxor, a sister stone (weighing 240 tons) to Cleopatra's Needle,</p>
