<p>Riddles  1.<br/>What is it that God never sees, Kings never see, and we see every day?  Ansr. Our equals.</p>
<p>What is it that can be seen in mountains but not in hills, once in a minute, twice in a moment, in the moon but not in stars? Ansr. The letter U.</p>
<p>When and where did March fall into May on the 27th of July?<br/>Ansr. At the Battle of Hastings the Earl of March was killed &amp; fell into the river May on the 27 of July 1066.</p>
