
1915Nov. 18 Thu.Another big batch of 93 reinforcements arrived from N.S.W. & Queensland for us last night. Goodness only knows what we are going to do with them all, there being fully 150 occupying their time with drill & marching only. There is nothing doing on the Peninsula & A.M.C. work in Egypt is pretty slack.Our staff at Gezira to-day is :-Officers - 34Men - 385Nurses - 75Natives - 75 = 569There are only 400 patients in Hospital.
