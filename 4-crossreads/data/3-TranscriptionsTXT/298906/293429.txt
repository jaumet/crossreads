 we were to have gone out on a night stunt but it was put off at the last moment. 2nd. Full marching order parade this morning &amp; I was platoon Commander, but I managed things alright &amp; it went off smoothly the Colonel inspected us &amp; we marched past him afterwards on the Barrack Square. 3rd Went to Church this morning a military Church. In the afternoon I went for a stroll took my Kodak &amp; got a few snaps round about  Has been a rather nice day. I hear that Colonel Abbott. is camped at Wareham, so I am going to look him up shortly. Have had a very sore mouth for the last week got a cold
