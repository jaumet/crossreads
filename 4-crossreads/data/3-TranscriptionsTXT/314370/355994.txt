
March, 1916Friday 10Work as usual during the dayAfter tea several of us went for a walk to Zeitoun & had coffee & cakesWrote a letter & sent some stamps to Amy Bryant & finished up by giving one of my pupils an extra hours work.
Saturday 11Exam on Description takes up all morning.After dinner went along & had tea w/- Dad & go to picturesMake arrangements about going out tomorrow
