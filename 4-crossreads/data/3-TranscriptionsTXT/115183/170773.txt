At the top of the ascent , for High St runs along a ridge gradually rising till ending in the bluff Castle Hill, one comes to the Castle parade ground, crossing that one may enter the portal of that famous old monument of mediaeval times - Edinburgh Castle.
To return, however to my doings in this pleasant city Mr Mann showed me, with much pride I fancy over the house which was indeed - very nicely furnished and appointed. A very cosy room on the second floor was placed at my disposal and owing to the beautiful situation of the house, my window opened on a glorious view across the valley over Holyrood Palace
