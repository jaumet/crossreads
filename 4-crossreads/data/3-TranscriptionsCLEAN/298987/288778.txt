Wednesday 3Went to Sutton V
Thursday 4Sport at S.V. Had afternoon off to attend them. Hall & I went to a concert afterwards. While the Sports were on the Officers Quarters caught fire & mostly got burnt It is generally reported they were fired with the idea of looting.
Friday 5Still the Guard
Saturday 6Went sick this morning as I have a slight cough got some stuff for it Went to S-V- & Hall & I went to Warminster & saw a picture show Stayed in S-V- camp all night as I have a week end pass
