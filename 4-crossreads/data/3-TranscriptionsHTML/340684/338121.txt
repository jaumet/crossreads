<p>423<br/>2<br/>deadened the pain a good deal &ndash; I have been kept in bed for the last 8 weeks and as I eat like a horse and get no exercise you can imagine the state I am getting into  -<br/>The specialist saw me yesterday and said I would have to keep in bed and keep on two hourly fermentations for another week, and the week after that if the eye continues to improve he will leave off the fermentations and I will just have a dry pad on the eye &ndash; the week after that he says I may be able to expose the eye for half an hour an day and so on till the eye is exposed altogether. By the look of things I will be here for some time yet &ndash; I am getting jolly sick of hospital life.</p>
