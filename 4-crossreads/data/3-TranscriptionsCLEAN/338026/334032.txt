
[30 Sun]Church Parade
[1 July Mon]Cleaning waggons.
[2 Tues]Loading waggons ready to shift camp.
[3 Wed]"B & D" Subs: packed up and left Rivery at 10 am. & arr. Lamotte Brebiere about 11-30am. Dug in and had dinner. Ordered to move out again about 1 pm. Arr. Glisy 3 pm.Pitched our camp near river.(Following up the 5th Bdge [inf?])
