<p>to Fovant 3.30 p.m. with laundry then went to Tipperary Hut &amp; had some pastry.  At 8.30 went to C.E. Hut with Taylor &amp; had some supper.</p>
<p>Monday, 23/7/17<br/>On Lewis Gun all day out on range.  After tea went with Palmer &amp; James to Tipperary Hut &amp; had fruit salad, etc.  Received parcel pastry from Uncle Fred.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 24/7/17<br/>Lewis Gun all day &ndash; watching stoppages with live cartridges.  After tea went to Tipperary Hut with Palmer &amp; James for supper, then had game chess.  <strike>Wednesday</strike> Put up battalion colours.</p>
<p>Wednesday, 25/7/17<br/>Lewis Gun in morning &amp; gun drill afternoon.  Rained before tea.  After tea went to Y.M.C.A.  Wrote to Uncle Fred &amp; Reg. Nancarrow.</p>
<p>Thursday, 26/7/17<br/>Firing Lewis Gun all day.  After tea had dental inspection.</p>
