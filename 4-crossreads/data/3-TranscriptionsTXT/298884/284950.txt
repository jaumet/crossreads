Soon after leaving Exeter we came to the sea front and travelled along it for miles in fact nearly all the way to Devonport. It was a pretty journey and I enjoyed it immensely whilst I was able to keep awake. We arrived at Devonport about 16.30 and went straight on board the Derbyshire where a steward showed us to our cabins. We were then informed that tea was on in the saloon and I bolted down.
