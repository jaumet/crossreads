Coy. had got out we set to work repaired the broken trench we worked on it till nearly 1 oclock the work proved a blessing in disguise for it was a freezing night snowing lightly so the work kept us fairly warm During the night the men oiled their feet 3 times, they reckon it keeps them warm.
