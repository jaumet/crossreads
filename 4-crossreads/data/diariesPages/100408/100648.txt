29/10/17 Still improving. Food is too scarce though. Cold
30/10/17 Got up for a while after dinner. Very cold.
7/11/17 Wet cold day. Expect to leave any day now.
9/11/17 Aches pains got worse today so transferred to No59 General Hospital, 3rd Section at Moulle almost 7 kilos from St Omer.
12/11/17 Heard tremendous gunfire all last night all today. Told by chap from 1st British Division that they have been in a secret camp at Dunkirk for 4 months practising landing from monitors. No one was allowed in the camps and they were not allowed out
