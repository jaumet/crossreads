The turks were deliberately sniping at us all day &   we spent a very unpleasant day one man being wounded five horses killed &  two injured  &  two sandcarts smashed  & about four or five put out of use. We finished up at about 11 pm &   got back here &   watered the horses for the first time since 2 pm on the 2nd November &   turned in about 3 am this morning.
