<p>I fear he'd waxed excessively convivial.<br/>With the fine fermented liquor he'd had strife<br/>He had gazed upon the vintage when twas crimson<br/>When bacchanalian revelry was rife<br/>He'd partaken of therassic lubrication<br/>And with ambrosial nectar he was lined<br/>I regret to say he was non compos mentis<br/>In fact "he was blind"</p>
