17.5.15Mon. 17th.  I heard last night on the telephone that Italy had joined the Allies.  Last night was the quietest we have had &amp; the firing was not near as heavy as usual.  This morning the Turks got to work early with artillery onto the beach, &amp; spent a good deal of ammunition before our guns silenced them.  Yesterday afternoon our Artillery blew up some trenches on Quinns Hill but the Turks were busy during the night &amp; had the trenches reformed this morning, but our artillery made a mess of them again.  This hill we are holding is named Pope's Hill, &amp; Fishermans Hut is the place we landed at.  Our Artillery have been very busy all day &amp; the Turks have been having a very rough time of it.
