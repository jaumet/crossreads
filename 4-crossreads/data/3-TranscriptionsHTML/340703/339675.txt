<p>December 1916<br/>Saturday 16<br/>Started on our long trek 7am.Passed several graves of L.H. on the journey saw lots of dead horses.<br/>Arrived at Duedar and camped for the night. There was a battle here last Easter Sunday between LH and the Turk.  The LH held out ; numerous graves.</p>
<p>Sunday 17<br/>Left very early. Passed thro Katia and then 5 miles south of Romani.<br/>Still seeing graves.</p>
