We were among all the heads it was no trouble to see that she was well born she carried herself so well, I felt quite proud of myself all this was a new sensation to me for I had always been shy of girls but things seemed so different now I seemed lifted out of myself as the saying is, I am sure I am a different man today, I can quite understand how it is now that a woman can have such an influence for good or evil over a man. I took her home again that night a fine place she lived in, as the next day was a general holiday I asked her if she would come out again, she said yes, so we decided to meet again next morning at 10.30. I was there to time she woman like was about 10 minutes late, what a day we had I shall never forget it, first of all we went out by train to the Red Braid
