<p>Duty in trenches at night (5pm till 9am next day) sleep in dug-out.<br/>April 22nd<br/>Easter Saturday quiet day in trenches, night duty 'College Green'.<br/>April 23 rd<br/>Easter Sunday proves a bad day for our boys. Germans fire 250 shells in 40 minutes on Australian lines- 4 killed and many wounded in our Dressing Station, indescribable sight of wounds only equalled by remarkable fortitude of our boys.</p>
