 26th Sept; Tuesday. Raining again this morning & afterwards a beautiful day & very warm. Our boys doing great work & cut off Combles. No more re the transfer. After tea return home & study French until 10-30 pm. Expenses 5c (3c for papers 2c for Tram fares) 
 27th Sept; Wednesday A fine day, & pleasantly cool. Great news from the front. Combles taken    & great advance by French & British. 5000 prisoners in two days. Thiepval taken at last.
