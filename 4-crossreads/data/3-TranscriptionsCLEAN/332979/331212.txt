Awoke next morning at 6 am 31/1/17 & had a good Breakfast, all my Sick friends were fairly well & as the Weather was still fine it was very pleasant on board. In the afternoon there was a parade & each man had to come on deck with his life Belt on & instructed what to do in a case of an alarm. Another Concert in the Evening & then to byebye. 1/2/17 Thursday All hands up at 6 am. The Weather being still fine & the Sea Smooth all hands enjoyed their Meals & the Sick were their old selves again.
