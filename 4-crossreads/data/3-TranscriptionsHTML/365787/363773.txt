<p>26.10.17</p>
<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>I have to acknowledge with many thanks receipt of your parcel, containing 48 pairs of socks &amp; 2 Balaclavas, knitted from Wah: Warrawee Wool.  Also sent in 5 prs war chest wool per Mrs. A. Reid.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>"WAR CHEST" SUPPLY DEPOT,<br/>[Signed] J. M. Antill<br/>Hon. Superintendent<br/>A.D.M.</p>
