<p>30/9/17<br/>about the fighting</p>
<p>1/10/17<br/>Hellish cold today.  after the heavy mist or fog.  <strike>it was</strike>  after dinner the sun appeared.  Dinner mangroves.  football match today.  Beautiful sunset.  Ball of fire with two streaks of cloud across same at equal intervals of course that was only momentary</p>
<p>2/10/17<br/>Big letter mail yesterday I did not receive one single line.  The trouble about N.C.O working  they did one day at the job but were not sent there again<br/>their parcels are stopped &amp; they are on fatigue every day indefinite period.  No news war but strong rumours about Reichtag meeting which has broken into three distinct parties. also about peace demonstration at Frankfurt on Maine &amp; another big town.</p>
<p>3/10/17<br/>Nothing of interest other than thunder storm    a few chaps sent for from Soltau for Swiss Board [indecipherable] for exchanges  Big mail today not a card even.</p>
<p>4/10/17<br/>The storm of yesterday turned into a dandy storm with a few showers.  it did blow a real bonzer.  Very very cold today a driving wind with same.  news about</p>
