<p>25.1.15</p>
<p>drove us up the street again free of charge &amp; we came home on the 10.30 train</p>
<p>Mon 25th<br/>Stan was paraded before the O.C. this morning &amp; has to help feed up the horses seven nights running at 9 o'clock, for being in the canteen on Saturday night &amp; not getting out lively enough when the officer came along &amp; said it was time the canteen was closed;  The Squadron are out for the day; we were out with the lamps after tea group work. went to pictures afterwards</p>
<p>Tue 26th<br/>Same as usual morning, but the pump at the Nile got out of order yesterday &amp; had to take the horses to a canal</p>
