  
 Friday 31st May 1918 In trenches all day nightime patrol /  Sunday June 1st Same old thing relieved & night by 50th Battalion Split marched all night Sunday 2nd Marched till dinner time then billeted in big empty Hospital outside Amiens suburb Privery Monday 3 Parade in morning afternoon off -   Tuesday May 4th Parade and two hours fatigue in morning paid thirty Francs afternoon
