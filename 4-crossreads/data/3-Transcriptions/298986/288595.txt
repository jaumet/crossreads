<p>Tuesday 11 Dec.</p>
<p>Went to a Lecture by Rev. Norwood good.  Last night the Tommie in a camp about &frac12; a mile away kicked up.  From what I hear they are not getting a fair deal.  Some Canteens Officers &amp; Sap Mess were burnt &amp; looted.  There was some fireing at least am not sure &amp; have not heard</p>
