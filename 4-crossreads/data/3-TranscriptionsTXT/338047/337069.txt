 1919 July 27 went by train to Mowbray past Salt River junction in first class carriages, equal to our 3rd class (if we had any).  On alighting we walked up the hill passing gum trees (they smell good too) &amp; wattle trees till we reached the Rhodes Memorial. There is a very fine view to be obtained from the promenade in front of the Memorial &amp; a still finer one from the top of the same.
