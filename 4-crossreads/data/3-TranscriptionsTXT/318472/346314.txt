 30 March 1918 Saturday Two American soldiers were walking up the Main Road toward Albany Wards Picture Show Said one American. Wal, I guess you could drop England in one corner of America and [indecipherable] not notice it [indecipherable] Garn, said one of the Aussies &ndash; there is plenty of spare land here pointing to the high hill near by with the map of Australia dug deeply into the chalk on its side. There is that much &ndash; land about here that they have to pile it up in heaps! to get rid of it. The Yank appeared to be thinking for awhile!
