<p>20-2-17<br/>Wright failed to put in an appearance today as his presence was required at Mageibra; The Sergts mess in going strong &amp; we are living exceptionally well; 5 Sigs are to go to the next School of Instruction from here &amp; our names went in today, Hillier, Mann, Olson, Pearson ( a supernumary) &amp; myself so I am in</p>
