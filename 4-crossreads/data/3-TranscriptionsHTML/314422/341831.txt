<p>Friday Jan 11. 1918. 1257</p>
<p>On parade this morning Mr Hunter told me that Bil Kiley and I were going on Blighty leave tomorrow. This afternoon we got our pass, warrant &amp; my our ticket directions and an order for an advance of &pound;10 from Pay Master Horseferry road.<br/>Tonight I packed my things, polished my boots &amp; had a shave ready to go in the morning at 9.a.m.<br/>Saturday jan 12, 1918<br/>Left the camp at 9 a.m and motored to Baileul<br/>1258</p>
