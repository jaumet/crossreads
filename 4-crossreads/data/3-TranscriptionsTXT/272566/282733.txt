 Wednesday 19 December 
 Snowy &ndash; ground Frozen.  Beautiful sunny day followed by bright starry night. A busy day on the bike.  Visited La Capelle, Bellebrune &amp; Cremarest and ran along practically all the roads within a radius of 10 kilos. Trip to A.D.M.S. Sigs. etc. at Samer. Then after tea I had to run back to Desvres to look for our horse transport which is coming here by road
