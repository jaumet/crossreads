  
 393 New Zealand Hospital Walton on Thames 5.9.15 
 Dear Aunt Isabel Very many  thanks for asking me to stay at your house last weekend - I  enjoyed  my stay immensely, but sorry you weren't there - I have been  looking forward so much to meeting you and hope I shall have the opportunity before long - The specialist came up and examined my eye. He is going to   operate and says when he has finished with me I will be able to pass the army  test so am very cheered up - This is a glorious place - I go on out on the river every day and New Zealand Sisters and nurses wait on us hand and foot - I  was awfully pleased to see Grandmere and got  a great surprise to see her  look so wonderfully well - but then she is a wonderful person and looks much the same as she did years ago when she came to Sydney and used
