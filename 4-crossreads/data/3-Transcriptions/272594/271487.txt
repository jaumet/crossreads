<p>was spread over a big area of ground before we finally marched into our camp.  I was very crook for I had caught another cold &amp; felt anyhow.  I laid down all that day &amp; had a wash just before tea &amp; then went to bed, we had a concert in our little tent to celebrate "Anzac Day" some of the boys got a little merry for rum was fairly plentiful.  Freddie Turners brother was killed the other day at Arras.  He was in the R.M.L.I.s &amp; and was attached to the Tanks</p>
