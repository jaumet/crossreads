
Australian Div & 5th Mounted Brigade are practically at the back of the Enemy.Sunday Sept 22Haifa was captured today also complete Aerodrome & thousands of provisions.Brigade preparing to move out. We are moving at day breakMonday Sept 23Moved out at 5 AM towards River crossing about 10 AM.We moved on to Shunet Nimrin about 7 miles across flat from Jordan River. Once again we are here but I think this time there will be no turning back. The 1ST Brigade are ahead somewhere and the Turks are retiring. We moved on again
