Since I have been in camp this last six months I have learnt two things :- firstly that it seems to be the duty of all military authorities to put a damper on keenness & enthusiasm, & secondly that if you have the misfortune to fall in love with a company, the eagle eyes of the authorities will spy you out, & refuse to allow you have anything more to do with it.
The fact that I have already passed two schools of instruction seems something more to my detriment than in my favour.
