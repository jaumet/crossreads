<p>received a couple of letters from Australia this week 2 from Ian &amp; one from Muriel.</p>
<p>Sunday Feb 3rd<br/>On Tuesday Jan 29th I received instructions to proceed to Weymouth for Embarkation and caught the 11.55 from Dinton arriving at Weymouth about 4 oclock<br/>Next Morning I left by troop train for Plymouth &amp; we travelled through Exeter The day was fine &amp; bright &amp; mild.
