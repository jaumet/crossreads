8th July 1915To Mrs E. Doolan. Jnr"Grocery Store"41 Pitt. StWaterlooSydneyN. S. WalesAustralia
A.I.F.
Dear Mother.Just a line to let you know that I am still well hoping all at home are the same. Things are quite the same here now only duck your head to a few Jack Johnsons which whizz about now and again but they are quite harmless here. Well good bye & loveFrom your ever loving SonWill. Reece.
