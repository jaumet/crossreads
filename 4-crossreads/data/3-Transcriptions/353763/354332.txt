<p>Upolu again I can tell you  -</p>
<p>Harrison &ndash; our wireless man &ndash; gives us news whenever he can  -  A ship is much safer at sea with wireless  -  Knock your propeller off &ndash; catch fire &ndash; out go distress signals &amp; people know where you are &amp; off they go  -  We learn that a German cruiser has been hobbled up at Mafia island &amp; that island is German E. Africa  -  The usual ebb &amp; flow of babble in N France has ceased to interest  -  We want a definite issue badly  -</p>
<p>Nat history stirred my sluggish brain one day when I saw a tropic or 'bosun' bird &ndash; Phaethon tropicus? He or she is about the size &ndash; perhaps larger &ndash; of a silver gull  -  They have a raucous gull like note  -  They are white &amp; you spot them by the long tail streamer &ndash; 12 -18 inches of feather  -</p>
