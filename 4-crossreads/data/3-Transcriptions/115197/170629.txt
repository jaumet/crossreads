<p>The Commanders, officers and crews of Britains Iron-clads stand-to and give us rousing cheers.&nbsp; By submarine destroyer.&nbsp; cruiser &amp; battleship bearing the &quot;Jack&quot;&nbsp; and in some cases the &quot;Stars &amp; Stripes&quot; we are truly honoured in a British manner and given a farewell so sincere &amp; spirited that the passing years cannot possibly efface its stirring memories.</p>
