<p>1917<br/>Diary<br/>August 20th<br/>Hospital &ndash; Have applied for leave but do not think I will get it<br/>21st<br/>Went for a [indecipherable] to "Agony Island" as those who are on deck call it &ndash; it is one of the old Forts blown up by bombardment<br/>I feel fitter but "short of a gallop" Still packing up &amp; leave for the Desert in the morning</p>
