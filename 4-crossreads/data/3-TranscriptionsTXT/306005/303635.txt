7-12-16Thurs 7thAthorn & I went to Mazar today accompanied by Cpl Harris who has a couple of inventions to improve the Lewis gun, & apparently Division thinks it will be a success as they asked for him to be sent in. We hit the Railway line at Kilo 127 & from there had a good road in to Mazar. I took 1450 piastres in with me to buy goods but could only get 430 piastres worth, coming home we to struck right in a direct line for our camp cutting
