<p>Sunday 22 April 1917<br/>General routine, taube dropped 3 bombs but did little damage pack up &amp; proceeded to Abu Sitta.</p>
<p>Monday 23 April 1917<br/>Quiet day.  On Q.M. fatigue, went over to Nakhara Wells for water.  Very hot day.  P.C. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 7 and 2].</p>
<p>Tuesday 24 April 1917<br/>Weather still hot nothing much doing.</p>
<p>Wednesday 25 April 1917<br/>Another very hot day but did not do much as it was too hot.</p>
<p>Thursday 26 April 1917<br/>Revaille at 2.  Saddled up &amp; left for Tel-el-Fara at 3.30, passed 2nd Brigade at Guz-el-Selist, watered up at Wadi &amp; went back to Abu Sitta, left again at 4.30 for Shelal.  On outpost at night.</p>
<p>Friday 27 April 1917<br/>On outpost all day.  Very cloudy &amp; had a couple of showers, very acceptable change.  Relieved off outpost at 4.30.</p>
<p>Saturday 28 April 1917<br/>Stand to at 4.30, had some grilled steak for breakfast issued with grit stuff.  Jimmy Gibbs went away crook.  Very cloudy:  like rain got Levison shod.</p>
