<p>Wednesday May 21st at Sea</p>
<p>Very hot &amp; calm : lantern lecture at night</p>
<p>Thursday May 22nd at Sea</p>
<p>Usual routine nothing much doing</p>
<p>Friday May 23rd at Sea</p>
<p>Usual thing : band on deck after tea :</p>
<p>Saturday May 24th at Sea</p>
<p>Nothing much doing : concert on deck crossed the equator :</p>
<p>Sunday May 25th at Sea</p>
<p>Church on deck in morn : interesting lantern lecture at night on deck</p>
<p>Monday May 26th at Sea</p>
<p>Quiet day band at night</p>
<p>Tuesday May 27th at Sea</p>
<p>Sports in day &amp; concert at night : we expect to be in Capetown for Sunday though</p>
