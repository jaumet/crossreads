  
 August, 1916 Wednesday 23 doing nothing & getting a bit "fed up" rains nearly all the time & everything is cold & dismal If this is the summer, God help the winter 
 Thursday 24 wrote letter in the morning played cards until tea time & walked to Tidworth to the theatre to see the "Play" Tidworth about 3 miles from our camp. Located old Tom Kennedy & his crew
