 Sunday March 3 New Zealand band at Richon. Only band in Richon division which sticks to Division Monday March 4 Rode over 1ST Brigade to see Sgt Beny[?]. found he had gone to hospital with bad knee. Received paper &amp; mail from Home Tuesday March 5 1ST Brigade moved out to day. Supposed to be returning in few days. Will be glad when we move for a change. Wednesday March 6 Very windy to day. Rumours of moving in few days.
