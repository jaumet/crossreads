<p>DEC. &nbsp; SATURDAY 2 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine. Cleaning harness all the morning. We had an inspection &amp; it passed all right. I was allotted the lead of the firing battery harness &amp; it was not too clean so I was cleaning it all afternoon &amp; evening. Sent a cable home &quot;Letters &amp; cable received. Glad all well. Love&quot;</p>
