 Monday 6 December 1915&nbsp; Travelling South in the Red Sea. Temperature somewhat high, but easily bearable.&nbsp; Short Marconi Message posted up.&nbsp; Distance travelled 306 miles in 24 hrs. 49 mins. 
 Tuesday 7&nbsp;December 1915&nbsp; Very hot night yet not unbearable&nbsp;  Wh &nbsp;Weather fine. Steady head wind all day.&nbsp; Distance 291 miles. Time 23 hours 50 mins. 5 seconds. 
 Wednesday 8 December 1915&nbsp; 8 a.m. Almost at the Northern end of the St. of Bab el-Mandeb, where so many tears have been shed during the ages that have gone.&nbsp; Cool head wind. Sea not rough enough to disturb this big ship.&nbsp; Served Mass for Father Coleman in the lounge. He told me that it was for me because I had treated him for his sore throat. 8.30 p.m. Off Aden in Gulf of Aden, heading for Cape Gardafui&nbsp;&nbsp; 268 miles. 23 hrs 44 mins 55&quot;.
