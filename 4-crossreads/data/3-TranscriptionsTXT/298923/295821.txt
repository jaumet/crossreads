 4 June 1918 Tuesday Yes I remember Harry Watton carrying me on his horse to Correagh &amp; you greeting me there.  A.W. Pearce letter speaks for itself-   Thank Day &amp; K for Parcel not yet received- but coming- Note all your news-   Nothing doing here yet so no need to be anxious-   Papers coming later on- Dolly well &amp; happy.  Tell me what  name  you would like for the Trumpeter- All my love Your son F W
