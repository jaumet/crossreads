France 17 Nov 18
Darling Mither,
Our mail comes in batches now for we are not in much communication with the rest of the Section. And I have received your end of August letters and mid-end of September.  I see that Dad has had a bad time with a touch of pneumonia, but I am very pleased to note that he is over it and was able to go on holidays. Bess, too, I hear had a touch of the flue.  It's several mails since I've had a few lines from her.
