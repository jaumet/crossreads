<p>Oct. 31<br/> Reported at Vic. Barracks. Pies and tea and cake served out by young ladies to recruits. Marched to showground and issued with dungarees etc; inoculated; final medical examination. Made aware that we would sail for England on 10th Prox. Sworn In .</p>
