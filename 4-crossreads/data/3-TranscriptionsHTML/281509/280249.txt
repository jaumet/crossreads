<p>1916 January 26 <br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>General leave.  Anniversary Day. Duty as usual.  Tony laid up with foot.</p>
<p>Fun in afternoon with horse.  Bob, another chap &amp; self, Bob's spill &amp; knock out.  Horse bolts with three of us up, into bush, tumbles &amp; pitches all, self underneath:  Scratch forehead. For joke carry me up on stretcher.  Scares Tony.<br/>Trio into Liverpool at night.  Park &amp; pictures.</p>
