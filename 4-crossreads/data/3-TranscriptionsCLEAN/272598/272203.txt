Sunday 1.7.17Presentation of medals by General Birdwood & Mr Holman [Australianpolitician] to men of the 8th Brigade.
Mr Holman reviewed the Brigade after the ceremony.  Few bombs & trench mortars fired shortly after for the special benefit of Billy -  Mr Holman under fire, 20 miles behind the line -More reinforcements coming here, chiefly those who were weeded out during the winter including McCabe & Smaile.
Monday 2.7.17Played A Sec cricket won by 6 wickets.  Brawl among the Sergeants.  Reunion of the Ambs of the Div,  Orgie among the officers at night in the form of a dinner.
