TuesdayVisited Billets &amp; went to Canteen Did not go out today, was not too good.
WednesdayWent to Canteen, took stock &amp; handed over management of Canteen to Richmond.  Bible Class this evening, there were 20 present, topic the prodigal son.  It was a good meeting &amp; discussion was free.  In order to back up his statement that it sometimes happens that only disaster will bring a man to himself, one chap quoted "Ginger Mick" by Dennis.
ThursdayVisited Billets today.  Weather very sloppy.  Two young chaps came in this evening for a yarn, Basham &amp; Till.  The front has been fairly quiet but a heavy bombardment is pending.
