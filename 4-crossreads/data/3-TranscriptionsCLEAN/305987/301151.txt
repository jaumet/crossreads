  
  Somme 1918  
 Wed 3rd:  Left afternoon 2 pm marched to Caestre entrained at 6 a.m. arriving at Amiens at 10 p.m. Thurs 4th:  Left Amiens & marched to Allonville arriving there at 1 p.m.  2 p.m. off again per motor to Bussy-le-Daours, thence to Daours: 11 p.m. off again to a position on right flank. Frid 5th: Into Wood for the day. At 5 p.m. left wood & relieved 33rd in supports Sat. 6th:  Moved across the road & dug new support line Sun 7th:  Relieved by Raingers came back to Gentelles Wd Mon 8th:  I am one of 50 to be sent back to Allonville for a rest. Wed. !0th:  To Moullions au bois Thurs 11th:  To Gentelles Wood. Frid 12th:  Front line; relieved 34th at mid night 
 Mon 15th:  Relieved by 19th came back to reserve trench
