<p>1916 February 5<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Bob &amp; self go to Falls Creek for dinner.  Find all well.  Sarah Peterson there. Go round falls &amp; leave for home through bush.  Call at Emery's.  Evelyn greets us warmly.  Regaled with aft tea &amp; fruit &ndash; visit bush house, new engine etc. Arrive at Mayfield about 5 pm.  Mary gives us warm welcome. Tea &amp; talk.<br/>Bob getting better.  Mary arrives from Sydney for a few days.</p>
