 In afternoon went with Smith, Strahan &amp; Sowter to the church at Barford.  It is 700 years old &amp; has fine carvings on the altar.  Has memorials engraved dated 1584, 1618 &amp; numbers in the 1700s &amp; early 1800s.  Altogether was very interesting. 
 Saturday, 7/4/17 Funniest Easter Saturday that I ever had.  Paraded with full equipment, blankets, all belongings &amp; changed our quarters to hut 20.
