<p>Dec. 1st<br/>Sabbath &ndash; Rehearse during the morning &amp; again in the Afternoon &ndash; A new recitation written &ndash; Also letters &ndash; Many French soldiers (picked troops by their appearance) pass through our town on their way to occupy German territory.</p>
<p>Dec. 2nd<br/>A day of rehearsal, and also preparation for to-morrow's move.<br/>- Diary &ndash;</p>
