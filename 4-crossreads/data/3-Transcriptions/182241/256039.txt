<p>the old city we made our way to the Garden of Gethsemane it is now Kept in order by the Franciscan monks &amp; is in <strike>beat</strike> beautiful order At intervals along the garden path arranged the fourteen stations of the cross &amp; in the garden are a few ancient olive trees, though it is improbable&nbsp;that any are remaining here which were in existence in the time of Christ, Near the garden is the grotto of the Agony &amp; a few paces farther is the Virgins Temple, after seeing a few more places of interest we returned to camp our camping&nbsp;place being on the top of Mount Zion</p>
