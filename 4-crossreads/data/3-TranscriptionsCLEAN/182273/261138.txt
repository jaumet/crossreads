June   1918
16 SUN  'Fritz' did not 'hop over'
Fine day little rain.
Amm: going up.
Posted letters to May&radic;. ThelmaX
& Hortense&radic;. Off. & O.R. went
to rest camp. Very quiet
day & night
17 MON  Fine day. Washing &
bath. Sgt. Palmes went up
to Bty. Rec: letters from May.
Amy Mc, & Else P. & 3 Photos.
Amm: going up. Quiet day & night.
18 TUES  Fine day. Warm. Rec
letter from A. Lawrenson. Quiet.
Amm. going up.
Quiet during day. stunt
towards morning. Sgt Mac Q.M.S.
19 WED  Rain in morn. fine aft.
Amm: going up. Plane brought
down 6.P.M. Sgt Gilligan went to Le
Havre. Rec. letter from Amy.
Quiet day & night.
