  
 April 1916 Colombo 17th April   We crossed the "line" about 2 P.M to-day.  We are still having beautiful weather, but a few heavy showers have fallen during the last two days. We passed a ship about 10 A.M. which seemed to be taking a peculiar course, first travelling N.W. & then suddenly changed due North & passing about two miles astearn. 19th   We sighted land early this morning & arrived at Colombo about 9 a.m. & anchored in the bay.
