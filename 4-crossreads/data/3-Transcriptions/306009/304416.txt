<p>1915<br/>Aug. 20 Fri.<br/>Our former Quartermaster &ndash; Capt. C. Gray got the sack when we were out at Mena, and was sent back to Australia for certain irregularities in connection with his department.<br/>To-day a new Q.M. arrived for us from Alexandria &ndash; Lieut. Beers &ndash; he was Q.M. of 1st Field Ambulance.<br/>W.O. Emmett had been acting Q.M.</p>
