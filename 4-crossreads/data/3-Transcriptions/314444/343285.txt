<p>I often times think of the balls &amp; dances that Tamworth folk are having now June being the month.  [Deletion by censor]<br/>Am terrible short of envelopes please enclose a few in your next letter &amp; continue on till further notice we may not be able to get any for a long time  p. cards will have to do in the future now unless of course I drop on a few now &amp; again<br/>Jack Tory encloses a letter home please see that his people get it next letter you will very likley get from them change about like.<br/>Well with the best of love to all<br/>Your loving son<br/>Watson F Adamson</p>
<p>P.T.O.</p>
