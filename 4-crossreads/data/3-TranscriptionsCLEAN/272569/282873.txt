  
 Thurs. 6-7-'16 
 of Dug outs. Had to evacuate Officers Dug out. Finally patched & floored a very dilapidated Dugout & Rats not withstanding got to sleep 12-15 am. Fri. 7-7-'16 Awaked to Stand To 2-30 a.m. - 3-30 a.m. Toogood & self Mess Ord to-day. Allotted Dug-out with Cpl Gorr & 4 of us together. 9 P.M. - Fighting Order very fatigue.  Returned 2  Sat. 8-7-'16 Returned from Fatigue of Sap digging at 2 a.m. We had 6 Casualties. Bullet grazed me too. M.G. trained on us all time.
