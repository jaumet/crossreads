<p>June, 1916<br/>Sunday 4<br/>Church parade over we do some more lazing too tired in fact even to go to town.<br/>go to Matariah in the evening for a blow.</p>
<p>Monday 5<br/>Put in all day on working up the points for tomorrows exam on Care of Gun.<br/>More rumours of our leaving, but no orders.</p>
