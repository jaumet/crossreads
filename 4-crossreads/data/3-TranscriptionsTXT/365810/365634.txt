 There are a few priceless blighters at this game.  We have had far too many cases of officers "swinging the lead" lately.  They make me positively furious.  Fellows who go to England either wounded or sick and dig themselves well into a good fat job there and make no attempt to come back and do their job. 
 There have been a good number of marriages committed by all the officers of the Battalion recently.  The amount of letter writing that is done is amazing.  I have not yet met the girl who was made for me.  I wonder when I shall. 
 Best love to everyone, Affectionately yours C.R. Lucas
