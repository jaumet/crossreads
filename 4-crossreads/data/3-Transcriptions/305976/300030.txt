<p>Thursday Aug 15<br/>There is not much news to be written. Since we came here we have had very little to do.<br/>There are all sorts of rumours current as to our future movements. We have spent the time quietly having occasional sports, concerts.<br/>The spell here has done us all good including the Horses. There are rumours of a big offensive early next month. Tomorrow we are moving to Latron<br/>Friday Aug 16<br/>Left for Latron at 7 AM. On our way passed camp of the Artillery belonging to Australian Division. Arrived at Latron at the foot of the Jordan</p>
