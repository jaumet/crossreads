We crossed the old Jordan river which flows into the Dead Sea which I hope never to see again . It resembles its name as there is no sign of life & nothing can live in it. On taking a swim one is covered in Salt on getting dry & a fresh water bath is desirable. We continued our way to the Foothills & expected to camp here for the night.
