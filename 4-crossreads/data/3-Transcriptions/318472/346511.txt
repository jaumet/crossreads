<p>2 October 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>16 October 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>Started the day by missing the morning train. Caught the 4.45 P.M. &amp; went to Nowra &amp; out to Selwood reaching there about 11 P.M. feeling rather tired. Saw reinforcement march through City Good news from all Fronts.</p>
<p>1918 October 17<br/>Thursday<br/>A very warm windy day. Got up late and the principal event I think was that Arch and I went fishing and caught two fish about this length [drawing] Good news continues to come from all Fronts.</p>
