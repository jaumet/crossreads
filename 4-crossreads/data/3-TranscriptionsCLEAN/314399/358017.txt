  
 Monday 20 January 1919 Up at 2 pm. Went to Bank. YM Kodaks for films & negs. Had a busy afternoon. Went out for dinner. 
 Tuesday 21 January 1919 Up at 4 am & after a lot of bustle we managed to get a seat in second class compt. Still it was a seat. Train was very slow. At 1.15 crowd rushed for dinner waggon & train started leaving about 20 or 30 on a platform. At 7 it waited 2 1/2 so could easy have stopped for them Got to Brussels at 1 am.
