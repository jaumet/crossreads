 21/2/17. Wednesday Parade was ordered for 9-00 & soon we were lined up for a Route march around Sea Points some 7 miles. We moved off at 10 am all our Unit & we were marched for 20" then we had a halt for 10" this was continued until 12 Midday. The Road around a Sea Point is well made & kept in good order & when we were marching at ease all sorts of Songs were indulged in. We followed the Coast line for about 3 mile, & some very beautiful homes were passed & we were cheered by the Residents & at several places Fruit was handed out to the boys.
