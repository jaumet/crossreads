
June 3rd continuedMending & letter writing.  Reading novels & B. L & S Officers revolver practice afternoon, reading night.
June 4th  323M.  WestB. Coy on Duty in charge of own section for Phy. D. till 7am. then on with Canteen Fatigue, very hot & on the go all day till 8-30, good music & singing aft.
June 5th  West.  M.305Usual drill all day.  Outpost in addition.  At reveille we were running along the Socotra Island, very rough & high mountains also passed 2 ships, one 7-30am. other [indecipherable].
