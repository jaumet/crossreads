Miss Miles &amp; I are going to the theatre tonight &ndash; 'Home on Leave &ndash; which is supposed to be very good. We also hope to do many other things, circumstances permitting, all of which I will tell you about when we have done them.
Have a good time in N.Z., &amp; don't worry about the war because it's going to be over in about a month or so.
Remember me mushily to the family
Yours in the most respectful &amp; loving taste
Keith
P.S. If you send letters through the bank, I receive them between a week &amp; 12 months earlier than through the Expeditionary mail.
Another P.S. I sent you a cable yesterday.
