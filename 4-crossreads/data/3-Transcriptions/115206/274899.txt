<p>November 1918.<br/>Monday 25</p>
<p>Southall. Hospital<br/>Feeling fairly rotten today and am looking forward to a good furlough, commencing tomorrow morning.<br/>Temp 101  at 6 am - 103  6 pm. Settled down to a Turkish bath and felt much better at midnight.</p>
