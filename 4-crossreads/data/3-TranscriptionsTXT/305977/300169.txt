 straight up to the wharf.  It is a very busy place crowded with shipping with good berthing accommodation.  The native population are very dirty &amp; old things we chucked overboard onto the wharf were scrambled for and fought over.  We got a lot of amusement out of them.  Nearly everyone has something to sell &amp; they are the greatest robbers under the sun.  It doesn't do to have much dealings with them, as they are so jolly dirty &amp; there is every disease here that is known.  Machine Gun Section &amp; C Squadron landed first &amp; got away about midday.  B Squadron left the boat at 8pm &amp; we got to Cairo early, 2am on Tuesday morning.  The carriages were very dirty &amp; plain, being 3rd class.
