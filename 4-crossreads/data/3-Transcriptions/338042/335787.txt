<p>Panto with great success &ndash; Major Churchill congratulates "Coves," and speaks of a tour which is being arranged by General Birdwood.-</p>
<p>Jan.10th<br/>Memories of Bailleul (July 1916) Skittle alley &ndash; Visit to Y.M.C.A. Book from Library &ndash; Afternoon spent sewing &amp; writing Large mail from Australia.</p>
<p>Jan.11th<br/>Mess Purchases &ndash; rehearsal &ndash; "Tales of Mystery &amp; Imagination".  Earl of Cassiles (Town Major &ndash;) Mild weather. Panto still booming.</p>
