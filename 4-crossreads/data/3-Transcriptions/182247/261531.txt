<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 7</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feel well but sleepy.&nbsp; Slept after breakfast past mail time.&nbsp; Dispensed with one tube to-day.&nbsp; Did nothing but sleep all day.&nbsp; Had shave with new razor.&nbsp; Good.&nbsp; Great improvement on poor Darkie&#39;s shaves.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 8</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Still improving.&nbsp; Ward washed out and my possy and number changed.&nbsp; Slept all afternoon.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 9</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Well.&nbsp; Had good breakfast.&nbsp; A little too good perhaps.&nbsp; Feel over-indulged a bit.&nbsp; My paper supply run out&nbsp; Slept all afternoon.&nbsp; 2 parcels from home.&nbsp; Returned from Company and sent on from London.&nbsp; Glad to get them.&nbsp; Spent good restful night.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 10</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Awakened feeling good again.&nbsp; Spent all morning in slumberland.&nbsp; Letter writing prevented me in afternoon.&nbsp; Big mail from home at night.&nbsp; Had good night&#39;s sleep .&nbsp; 21 men went to Blighty.&nbsp; Did not hear them.&nbsp; Cut my Birthday cake, sent by Flossie, and gave sisters a piece.&nbsp; Good cake.&nbsp; Enjoyed it.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 11</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Awakened to find only 5 in ward.&nbsp; Shifted over to &quot;Canada&#39;s&quot; side as No. 5.&nbsp; Spent most of morning in xxxxxx writing.&nbsp; Spent afternoon in same manner trying to get writing up to date.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 12</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 5.30.&nbsp; Feel well.&nbsp; Wrote Mrs Linley after break-fast.&nbsp; Slept until dressing time.&nbsp; Slept all afternoon.&nbsp; Had good sing song after tea.&nbsp; Enjoyed it.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 13</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feel well.&nbsp; Slept most of day.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 14</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feel fine.&nbsp; Slept after breakfast.&nbsp; Tube hurt at dressing.</td>
</tr>

</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 17</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Good to-day.&nbsp; Spent day much as yesterday.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Nov 18</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Slept all morning.&nbsp; In afternoon, Miss Campbell, a relative of O.G.B. visited me.&nbsp; She called at the request of Lady Brabazon, a relative of O.G.B.&nbsp; Gave me some nice sweets.&nbsp; A most charming lady.&nbsp; Sorry when her visit ended.&nbsp; She proved to be a V.A.D. in a hospital adjoining.&nbsp; Baby Canada sent to Blighty.</td>
