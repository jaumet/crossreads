<p>seals, monkeys birds &amp; etc, then back to town again for dinner at the "Louis" &amp; from here we had to go to the Railway station for they were returning that night I saw them off, then I went down to see my friend of yesterday &amp; arranged to take her home after she finished her work this was not until 10.15 so I went to the theatre to kill the time, &amp; as soon as it was out I slipped round &amp; met Miss Devereaux then we walked home I suppose anyone who reads these lines will think me silly when I say that I am almost gone on her, I cannot understand myself of late, I have met now some hundreds of different girls but of them all this one attracts me most she is such a refined &amp; nice spoken girl &amp; as handsome as they make them in fact she has almost turned my head, &amp; its just as well</p>
