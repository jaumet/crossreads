Friday 1.9.16Fine again to-day: has been raining for almost a fortnight on & off; a treat to see sunshine.
Saturday 2.9.16Walked to Bailleul, about 61/2 miles from here & bought a few more articles to send home.  Feel the effects of the walk, for I have had no occasion to do any walking for the past month.  Bailleul a fine old town of no small dimensions.  Church a beautiful building, but very ancient; spent some time inside the building.
