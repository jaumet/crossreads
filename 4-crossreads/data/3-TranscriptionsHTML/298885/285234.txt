<p>Compared with Ypres, Albert and several other similar towns which have been under hostile fire, Amiens has been spared to a great extent, especially the fine Cathedral which has only been hit by two shells in such places as to cause very little damage</p>
<p>On arrival at Glissy we at once heard rumours of a big stunt coming off in the course of a couple of days.</p>
