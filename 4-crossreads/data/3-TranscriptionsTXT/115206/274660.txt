
March 1918Sunday 31
On the Road - Bycourt.2am detrained at 2.30 am. and proceeded to our billets Rained very hard all the while.The complete outfit whilst en route to billets at Orville, was drenched with rain.We arrived at Orville at .15 am and after watering and feeding up, retired to bed for a little spell before moving up to the line. We are to "standby" to move up any hour.Word came through that the 4th Divvie had smacked Fritz back four miles - heavy casualties.
