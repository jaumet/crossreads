I wrote one & I think did it rather well, & I hear there were very few written, so am living in hopes. Am keeping young Robinsons address & hope to look him up.  I am sorry to hear Grahame Garland is coming poor Mrs Garland. Please remember me very Kindly to her, also to Miss Jean. If I meet Grahame will try & get him some Cricket.
