 Colne about 9 pm. Landing stage Bullelts spitting in the water around now &amp; again on the way. One or two hit the boat. We proceeded up the track towards the trenches &amp; near the top bivouacked for the night to the music (?) of the rifle fire &amp; the sing of the bullets I slept some Our chaps took the whole piece so far jolly quietly &amp; did not show much excitement.
