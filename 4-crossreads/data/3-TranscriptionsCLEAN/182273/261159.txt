 1918   August 
 29 THUR  Fine day. Warm. further 
 advance all along the 
 line. Moved off at 2. PM. 
 & bivouacked near Estrees 
 Guns well forward. Very quiet. 
 Little opposition. Inf. crossed Somme. 
 Guns pulled out at 12 P.M. Quiet 
 Reveille 4 am. Moved off at 6 am. 
 30 FRI  Heard Peronne had fallen. 
 Passed through Foucaucourt, Proyart etc. 
 & camped near Sailly Laurette. Moved 
 in aft on River Bank. Fixing up 
 camp. Rec. 3 letters from Else P. 
 1. Gladys C. Quiet night. 
 31 SAT  Fine day rain on & off. Fixing 
 Camp. Rode over to Behencourt 
 in aft. Did not see Tense. Saw 
 Mme Le Ferme. called at Bavelin- 
 court came home via La Houssaye 
 Bonnay & Vause  Rec. letters 
 from Reg C. Amy. Card 
 from Julie & letter Tense. 
 Home. 9 P.M.
