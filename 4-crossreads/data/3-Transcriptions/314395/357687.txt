<p>Australia.  I fancy these six month base jobs are a compromise.</p>
<p>About 12 months ago I asked you for another photo of yourself, as I had lost the other one, perhaps you never got the letter, as the photo has never come along.  My <strike>one</strike> own have not yet come from Paris, but I will send one as soon as they come.</p>
<p>Russia having thrown in the marble will make things extremely awkward for us, on the west, but the Yanks may make a slight difference when they kick off.</p>
<p>Some months ago I sent home my medal.  Did you ever get it?</p>
<p>Best wishes,<br/>Your affect. Son,<br/>George.</p>
