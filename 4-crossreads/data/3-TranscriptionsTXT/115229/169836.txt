I obtained leave on June 2nd and went into Amiens per foot, it being 12 mile distant.&nbsp;I spent a very good day and bought a few presents, which I had sent home.&nbsp;During the whole of the month spent here we had frequent concerts, boxing tournaments and plenty of sports.&nbsp;Of course these were held outside parade
