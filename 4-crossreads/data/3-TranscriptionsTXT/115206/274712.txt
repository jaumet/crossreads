 May 1918 Wednesday 22 
 Battery Wagon Lines. Ken Gunn went up to Guns, and O.B.Walker came down, also Ernie Halligan. Went down to the Lake for a little swim during morning . Russell arrived during afternoon he looks very well. At 9.30 pm Fritz dropped at bomb on a C" charger dump up at Freshencourt. The whole countryside was illuminated.  Fritz dropped a few shells across the village of Pont Noyelles.
