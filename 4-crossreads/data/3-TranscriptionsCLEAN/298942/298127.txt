
While I was at [indecipherable] our division was on the way to the Somme where they stopped (Amiens) for a couple of days.
No sooner had they had left the northern sector (north of Messines), which was left very thinly held, than the Germans broke through the Portuguese & made their rapid dash on Bailleul.
Neuve Eglise & Bailleul proved to be slight checking places, but after the capture  of Ballleul things were very critical indeed
