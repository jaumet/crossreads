<p>Monday 4th June 191<s>6 </s>7</p>
<p>A lovely day warm with a breeze - I am feeling very tired somehow or another.</p>
<p>Conference (at 5.30pm) of Group &amp; Bn. Bombing Officers at Group Hqrs which I attended. Played cards after attending a good concert given by R.G.A club.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5th June - Conducted bombing all day. Attended Lewis Machine Lecture in Evening after which Smith &amp; myself went to Y.M.C.A. to Conjuring performance.</p>
<p>Entry <u>Thursday 7th</u> - Wrote to George &amp; Reg last night &amp; also wrote several postcards to Jess &amp; the &quot;littlies&quot;.</p>
<p>Sent my Bombing Certificate to Bn. for record. Wrote congratulations to Lt&nbsp;Rose &amp; Lt Parsonage on their M.Cs. Wrote to A.I.F. Headquarters (Casualty Branch) and asked for casualty information concerning about 110 of</p>
