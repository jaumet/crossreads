<p>Wednesday 6.3.18 to Saturday 6.4.18<br/>Very quiet &amp; peaceful month.  Drafted into Quartermasters Store of Group Clearing Hospital day after returning from leave &amp; have now a fairly comfortable position.  Have sole control of messing &amp; rations in connection with hospital.<br/>During the month have spent a considerable amount of my spare time in at the YMCA at Warminster with Frank Grose &amp; have had a fairly good time.
