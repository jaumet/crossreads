<p>30 December, Tuesday</p>
<p>Home again. I amuse D &amp; Mum with piano &amp; get to bed at 9. Go to 430A at 1 pm.</p>
<p>31 December, Wednesday</p>
<p>Go to Top's. We wait &amp; watch for Slaper. Music &amp; [indecipherable] until the Boy Come Home.</p>
<p>Get to bed at 12.30 am. I am again at 430A. Dine with Ellis who cls the bike over.</p>
