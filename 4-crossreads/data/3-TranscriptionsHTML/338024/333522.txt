<p>Mon. 13 Nov. 1916<br/>Sighted lights of Cape Town at 7. 30pm.  Lay in Bay till next morning (arr in bay 9. 30pm)</p>
<p>Wed.  15 Nov. 1916<br/>Route march in the morning.  Sport in oval in the afternoon, concert and tea in the evening at Feather Market.</p>
<p>Thurs. 16 Nov. 1916<br/>Tram ride round lions head.<br/>Left Cape Town 6 am.</p>
