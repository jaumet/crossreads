<p>an apparent total lack of sympathy both with the sporting &amp; recreation pastimes of the unit.</p>
<p>Today was "Sports Day" &amp; it fell rather flat. The cause may be attributed, I think, to the same source, for men have been refused permission to go &amp; prepare certain team races, &amp; to train, on the plea that they were needed for drill. However just such a plea may have been, there is no doubt that a genuine resentment has been caused by it, &amp; that it produced the unhappy sequel of a dead day's sport.</p>
