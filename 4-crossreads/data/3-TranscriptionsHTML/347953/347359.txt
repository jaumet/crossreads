<p>We met two British warships on our way through also Indian &amp; British troops entrenched at various strategic points along the canal banks</p>
<p>I am feeling well at present having developed an attack of influenza.</p>
<p>On our way through the Canal at a place called Ismailia, which had been attacked by the Turks we met some of the N.Z. contingent, two Indians were killed &amp; 5 wounded</p>
<p>Much more fighting is expected</p>
