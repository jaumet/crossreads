<p>soldier will perhaps when he goes on leave to "Blighty" stop as is only natural a few days over &amp; is therefore A.W.L, he is unlucky enough to get caught &amp; in 9 cases out of 10 he will get 90 days 2nd FP. equal to the same number of pays, a pretty big slug in a mans book &amp; it gives him very little encouragement to soldier on for until that money is paid up on his book he draws only 5 franc's a fortnight, &amp; the other joker though he has been convicted of a far more serious crime &amp; is a rotten soldier can draw his full pay, &amp; has more liberty to boot.<br/>11th  Been very stormy most of the day it was very funny when we were on Coy. parade this afternoon I had just got my roll called &amp; the men numbered off ready to march</p>
