<p>Salonica Tobacco Co.<br/>Opera Sq., Cairo</p>
<p>The day is Sunday once again &amp; the commencement of our third week, 9/5/1915, in arms against the Turks.  Things are not as bright as of yore owing to our camp having been located by a Turkish battery on the right flank and at a distance less than a mile.  As they are able to observe the result of their fire &amp; their observation station being under our nose the damage they do is immense.  After each successive battering our chaps dig for their lives, &amp; as it is really necessary it doesn't cause as much merriment as in former days.  One of my dug out friends took occasion this morning to thoroughly renovate his person &amp; removed all his clothes &amp; placed them in a position above his head.</p>
