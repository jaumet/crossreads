<p>19th Sunday<br/>Disembarked by means of lighters at North Pier, Mudros.  Grouped up in respective Regiments then rested for &frac12; hour when orders came.  Moved on for about one mile &amp; then got orders to make ourselves comfortable <strike>for</strike> till daylight as no guide for us &amp; no one seemed able to lead us.  With all gear moved off for our camp which was reached in a worn out condition after marching about 5 miles.  After straightening tents &amp; peging out had combined Bkfast. &amp; dinner 1 meal 11.30 a.m.  Visited Canteen for purchases &amp; slept from 2 to 4 p.m.  Tea 5 p.m.</p>
