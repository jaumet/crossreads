<p>was announced at the college, we were about to the dining room; the director assembled us&nbsp;in the court yard, and made us sing the allied and national airs, and gave to us Champagne wine.</p>
<p>In the afternoon we went out of the college, two by two, with flags. &nbsp;We wandered over the town, stopping all vehicles. &nbsp;We went till the Place of Concorde, took a big gun (there was an exposition of material taken to the enemy) which we drew and brought in the college. &nbsp;The following day, we brought a second gun which we took also at the Place of Concorde. &nbsp;Now we have at the college three big guns and a machine gun.</p>
