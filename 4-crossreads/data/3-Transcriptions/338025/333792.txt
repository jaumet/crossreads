<p>Taubes having a fly over near our lines. Both sides have the aircraft to a wonderful stage of perfection and there is no finer site than to see an engagement, when they get into close quarters, then some marvellous manoeuvers and the machine guns begin to talk from both planes, a sheet of flame, an object taking a header, then quiet.</p>
