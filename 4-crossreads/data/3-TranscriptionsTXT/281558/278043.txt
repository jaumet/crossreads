Tuesday 25th
Napsbury Hosp : nothing doing in morn : sister said I could get up for a shave but had to go back to bunk again : leg still stiff : lady visitor in afternoon & she gave me some fruit, paper & scented soap : Aust Red Cross girls round too but I was asleep so they left some smokes for me
Wednesday Sept 26th
Napsbury Hosp : nothing doing during day : merry boys in the ward & generally end up with a few songs before "lights out"
Thursday Sept 27th
Napsbury Hosp : nothing doing in morn : Salvation Army women round in afternoon & gave me some chocolate & papers to read : drew khaki as we are leaving here tomorrow
