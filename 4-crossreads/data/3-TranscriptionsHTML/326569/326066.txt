<p>Friday, 8/6/17<br/>Went route march for the day to Corfe Castle &amp; back by different road, total 10 miles.  Had dinner near the Castle &amp; had 1 hour off to see it &amp; the town.  Extra good meals today.  After tea went to town &amp; read papers.</p>
<p>Saturday, 9/6/17<br/>Fairly warm.  Feet slightly sore after march.  When came back from bull-ring at 12.30, Reg was waiting for me, had been waiting &frac34; hour.  Stopped about &frac14; hr. then he motored to Bournemouth.</p>
<p>Sunday, 10/6/17<br/>Warm day &ndash; got up 7 a.m.  In morning took stroll up valley &amp; gathered nice wild flowers.</p>
