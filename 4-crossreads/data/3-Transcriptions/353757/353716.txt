<p>I'll send you a photo shortly as I'm having some taken.</p>
<p>Didn't meet Carmichael, &amp; wouldn't know him if I did.  I can't remember much about politics these days.</p>
<p>I had a 14 days leave before coming here and another week at mid term.  Work or study is a &ndash; but one has to stick to it.  We have as recreation Rowing, swimming, cricket, tennis, poker (principally the latter) and debates.  I have a bit of a name at the latter &amp; generally manage to knock them rotten.  But my opponents are generally poor, tho' if I'm up against an Australian I have generally a rough time.</p>
