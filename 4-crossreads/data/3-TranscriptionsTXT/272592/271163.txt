 I can hear the heavy firing quite distinctly down on the "Somme" &amp; the vibration in the air is easily felt everything seems quivering 
 Have had quite a busy day what with booking &amp; inspecting all the firing bays &amp; arranging for the supply of bombs &amp; etc. getting the fatigue partes together, making a dump for the "crater snatching" partys tools &amp; cutting the barb wire into proper lengths for tonights wiring party &amp; over seering a lot of the little jobs &amp; inspecting all gas helmets I have had scarce a minute for myself. 
 28th.   D Coy had 1 man killed &amp; 2 wounded last night on their wiring party, Dave Mason &amp; I put ours out, &amp; got in safely.
