 Off to Blighty [October 1918] 10 Th Wine flows free, the boys are happy song &amp; mirth,"Marching" with the deathless army of the A.I F 11 Fri Left Fignacourt last night, rested at St Vasbri &amp; slept for the night at Saint Savavour then took train to Bolounge at Ailly Sur Somme 12 Sat Bolounge to Folkestone a very nice trip, arrived in London for tea Feet very sore.I am being sent to hospital Signal of distress ulcerated Feet
