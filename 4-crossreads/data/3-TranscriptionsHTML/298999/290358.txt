<p>Tuesday Sept. 11.9.17<br/>Turned out 5.30 A.M. fed up had breakfast cleaned up camp.  fell in for rifle inspection fix and unfix Bayonets.  rested till 12.30 had dinner.  resting and reading all afternoon fed up 5.30 had tea.  saddled up and went on Patrol.  minding Bedouins all night never got much sleep with the noise.  they made grinding grain and Barking dogs.</p>
