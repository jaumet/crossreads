Had 3 of my platoon go to hospital this morning. 15th  Marched out to a new parade ground this morning &amp; practised our new formation in battle order the platoon shaped very well &amp; are coming on fast. While we were out there a lot of Lancers came cantering across the ridge with their sabres drawn &amp; flashing in the bright sunlight.
