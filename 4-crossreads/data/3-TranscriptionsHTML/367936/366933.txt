<p>Monday 14 February 1916<br/>Tempreture normal again.<br/>Beginning to feel hungry.<br/>Heard from Father.  The wheat went 390 bags.  We estimated 400 so were not far out.</p>
<p>Tuesday 15 February 1916<br/>Allowed to get up in middle of day and go out on verandah.<br/>Am very weak, could not walk without holding on to things.<br/>Was allowed to have fish to eat.</p>
