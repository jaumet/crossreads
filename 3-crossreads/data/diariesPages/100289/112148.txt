Monday 10.7.16
Arose 7.30 feeling much better for the nights rest, although we were disturbed during the night by a gas alarm. Received parcel from home, but received instructions to move off to the trenches about an hour after receiving it. Moved away with a party of 20 others marched single file through Flaubaix, at an interval of 100 yards, till we reached the saps. Wended out way for about  of a mile through saps. Four of us were stationed at the R.A.P. remainder taken to various other positions. Now about 250 yds behind front line settled down for work.
