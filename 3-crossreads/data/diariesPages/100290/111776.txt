15 January 1916
Saturday
Morning shift. Pay morning pound 3.15.
Prepare weekend leave. Leave 11.30 am train. Crowded train 20 in box carriage 3 on rack 2 in lavatory. Civilian broke window getting through at Fairfield. Sanitary paper streamers. 
Dinner on pot at Marine Caf Gardens sleep. Meet Mary at Quay go to Central tea. see Janet off by Nowra train Essie Mr Lang. Meet Jack, Ethel Evelyn Essie. Say goodbye to the former. Home to Ashfield. Take Ess to pictures, stay night ? Bed 11 pm.
