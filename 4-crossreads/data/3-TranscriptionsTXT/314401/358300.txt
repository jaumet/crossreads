 Chaplain A memorial service will be held at 11.15 a.m. Monday, 28th.December, while the ship is close to the spot where the "GOOD HOPE" and MONMOUTH sank. All Officers and men not on duty will attend. A service for Roman Catholics will be held at the same time. After the service is complete, the Roman Catholics will be marched forward to the Quarter Deck. The Band will then play the Funeral March. On completion of the March, 3 volleys will be fired, and the "Last Post" sounded. After the "Last post", "God Save the King" will be sung, accompanied by the Band. An officer will be in charge of the Firing Party. A selected Bugler will sound the "Last Post". Rig for Officers  - Frock Coats and swords. Rig for Ship;s Company &ndash; No.8.
