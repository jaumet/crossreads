1918   October
24 THUR  Fine day. Inspection
by the heads. Rec.
paper from Else P. Increase
in leave to 'Blighty'.
Writing at night.
25 FRI  Dull day. raining
Rec. letters from Maggie &
MayAmy. Up village at
night. Secured bed.
26 SAT  Fine day. Football
in aft.
Writing at night.
Posted letters & cards to
Gladys. Else P. W.T.B. C B Dowe.
Gilligan & letter to
Julie. Brown went
to O.T.C.
