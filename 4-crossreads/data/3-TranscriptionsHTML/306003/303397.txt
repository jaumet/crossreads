<p>18-6-16<br/>One fell in the sea near El-Arish &amp; the plane was towed ashore &amp; burned  one of the other planes brought the aviator back, the second came down 5 miles this side of El-Arish the aviator of this machine was also brought back by another plane &amp; the 3rd with the Flight Commandant on board was missing no one knowing what happened to him, but a later report from Kantana said 10 planes had returned &amp; the plane reported missing had turned up</p>
<p>Charlie Linsell received 3 days leave to go to Alexandria last Thursday to see his father who is going to England, he returned today.<br/>The Tommies here cannot stand the work too well on the desert &amp; give us a good deal of kid stakes for the amount we are doing.  There is a very popular rumor afloat that we shall soon go to England but it is one of many;  Leask arrived back from the School of Instruction a few days ago.</p>
