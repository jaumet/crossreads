
Friday 31st March
In regard to the application we "slept on it" & came to the conclusion that it would be wiser to wait for a time.  Both Molloy & I however, are hanking to get back to the Australians.Neither of us is in love with the disciplinary methods as practised here, although, the job being to my liking, I could put up with quite easily.The promotion I am in need of, however, added to the other makes me feel like taking a change.
