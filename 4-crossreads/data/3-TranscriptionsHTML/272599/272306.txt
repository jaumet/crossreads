<p>Feb.  Tuesday 6  1917</p>
<p>Nothing of great import occured.  On duty all day in dressing room.  A few cases through. Feet wash at night of all the strength. Preventive of trench feet &amp; frost bite. --  Scrubbed with certain kind soap, dried &amp; rubbed with camphorated powder. &ndash; Result colder feet than ever. Still orders have to be carried out.<br/>Braised steak for dinner, some class.</p>
