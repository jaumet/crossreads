  
 8 - to be a success - and if she does realize all you've done it may help her to go on on being a brick. How strange to think of Susie Baynton with a son! Your Jaeger is one of my most precious posessions darling mother. Hen "the sands of the desert grow cold" - that's a quotation isn't it? On goes your Jaeger. I wonder if you went to the farm after all. How I'd love to be there again. There are some rather charming cotton rugs here - bright colours that come from Persia - which would look well on the sitting room floor at the farm, not dear and wash & wear well. If you were here - I wish you were - you could get with a few pounds some things to fix the farm up. All Con's news is so charming to me. Because of a life so different from mine - so much more natural - with those adorable babes. But however much I regret not having those joys - I know I couldn't bear domestic life unless I married a man I really loved - and I know I've only loved one - and I cant help it
