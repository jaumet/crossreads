  
 ARQUES Friday 10 A twilight march through wonderful woodland's. Our camp by the canal condemned shifting further out Saturday 11 Woke up this morning with "Christ" at the old monastry of "Clairmarais" situated in a dense forest. Raining again Sunday 12 Living on a farm another such place as Carnsdale the old stables ect recall other day's and other face's A new week beginning still along way from home Just saddled "Nutty" for a long night ride Major Fussell off to BR.H.Q
