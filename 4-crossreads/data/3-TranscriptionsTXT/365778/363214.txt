
Thursday 23/8/17Whole Division marched past General Birdwood along Canal De Neuf-Fosse. Raining but a splendid turn out.Friday 24/8/17Battalion Training in wood, fighting cleared forest of Clairmarais, met Bas who has applied for commission in Indian Army.Saturday 25/8/17Went with Bas to see Birdwood at Hazebrouck afterwards went and had dinner with Victoire at Steinbacque, arrived home at 10.30 pm.Sunday 26/8 17Church parade afterwards went with Wisdom to see ground for Sir Douglas Haig's inspection on Wednesday next - Letter from Doff.
