<p>bit of work by a Fritz Plane. There were (and are everyday) a lot of captive baloons which are used by one or two men for observing, they are attached by a wire rope to a steam windlass, and they go up in the morning to any old height up to perhaps a thousand feet &amp; they stop there until night, when they are wound down again, that is under ordinary circumstances. Today we were on parade and as I said, a beautiful bright day, with some large clouds floating about.</p>
