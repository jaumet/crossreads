<p>Hobart summed up without prejudice is a sleepy old town such as may be expected from a backward country.</p>
<p>In Hobart</p>
<p>Sunday 27th September. Today being the first Sunday on board I expected there would be a kind of service. Not much. Everyone put on their best duds &amp; went ashore. Teddy &amp; I opened up a Phonograph that was to be given to the troops later on. There were some familiar songs &amp; several that caused an outburst of "Cheese it". Altogether the musical programme was a success as the papers say. In the afternoon I went by tram to the "Cascades" but was "sold again" as there are no cascades there. Cascade brewery is the only sight to be seen. The Cascades are at the foot of Mt Wellington, but to look up Mt Wellington is not an invitation to climb it, so I turned back to the ship.</p>
