<p>3rd A.C.C.S.<br/>A.I.H.<br/>France<br/>25.3.17</p>
<p>XOXOXOXOX<br/>XOXOXOXOX</p>
<p>My darling Eddy, XOX</p>
<p>I shall expect a letter from you next week. Suppose you will be going to school soon. Hope you are well   &amp;  strong now and shall be able to join in all the games at school with the other boys. Are you going to Ella's school?</p>
<p>Hope Mummie, Grin, Auntie, Bill   &amp;  Ealter are w ell. Give them a big kiss for me and heaps for you dear little self.  From Auntie Eadle.</p>
