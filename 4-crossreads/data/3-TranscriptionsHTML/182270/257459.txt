<p>TUESDAY, MAY 27, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine in morning but came on</p>
<p>to rain after midday &amp; rained</p>
<p>pretty steadily all night. Had</p>
<p>43 points. Drove down to Walhallow</p>
<p>in morning &amp; saw Wyburn at</p>
<p>the saw mill &amp; then over to</p>
<p>Spring Ridge to see Laurence</p>
<p>but he was away. Down in</p>
<p>the Boundary in afternoon.</p>
<p>It was Linda&#39;s birthday.</p>
