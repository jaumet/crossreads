
[Friday 25 August 1916]
Cairo's Church dayUsual Mohammedans work around Hospital I asked one why he washed his feet etc before "Praying"  His answer was good- viz"Whats use tell you""Christian doesn't believe in his religion !!!  this his conclusion after watching many Church parades etc.Entrained for Alexandria & boarded Red X ship LETITIA for England mostly Tommies on boardWe are crowded but don't mind, fortunately we have No 1 Station Hos Sisters en route for France & not R.A.M.C. sistersCabled England I was coming I have 2 months I believe before considered fit-Left Alexandria at 5.30 pm
