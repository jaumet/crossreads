
Oct 19177 Sun   It is a miserable wet morning but cleared up this afternoon.   Visited the H.M.S.N.Z. in the Firth of Forth   Monday 8thWent to Leith this morning & took the train to Dundee arrived at 4 pm mid drissle[ing] rain.   Went to a very good Variety show the best I have seen in England.   This is a great place for girls there are 25 for every manTuesday 9thIt is still wet so we could only go for tram ridesWednesday 10th.I went back to Edinburg   This morning my mate went on to Inverness I took the midday to Glasgow it is a fine city & has the finest tram service in the world
