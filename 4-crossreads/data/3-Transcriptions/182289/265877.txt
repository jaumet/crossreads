<p>28.4.18<br/>Slightly increased activity in enemy Artillery especially north of the Ancre.<br/>C.O. (Lt. Col. Blacklow) visited line with a view to locating site for fresh distribution of machine guns, imitating German M.G. tactics.<br/>The following targets were engaged at irregular intervals throughout night &amp; morning, 2500 rounds fired.<br/>(1)  M.G. K.1.a.60.60.<br/>(2)  M.G. K.1.a.95.65.<br/>(3)  Sunken Road K.1.b. &amp; d.<br/>(4)  Hollow K.2.c.05.70.<br/>(5)  Trench K.7.b.10.75.<br/>(6)  Cemetery K.1.a.90.70.<br/>Enemy artillery retaliatory fire between 9 to 11 p.m.  Granatenwerfer at Railway line east of Buire about 10.30 p.m.  Enemy Machine Guns fired short bursts throughout the night.  Enemy aircraft nil.  Weather dull, visibility poor.</p>
