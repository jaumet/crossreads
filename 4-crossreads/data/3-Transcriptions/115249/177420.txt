<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 13 9 4</p>
<p>Time 11 10 am</p>
<p>From Brigadier - Understand Encounter has a Band on board &nbsp;Could arrangements be made for its attendance at Flag Ceremony this afternoon, if so I should be extremely grateful. &nbsp;Parade forms up at Two thirty</p>
