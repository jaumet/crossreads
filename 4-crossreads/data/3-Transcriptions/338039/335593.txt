<p>Through Strazeele.  Steenwercke many aeroplanes.  First signs of German destruction- Destination reached after travelling 9 miles- Quartered in Hospital or D.S.  Formerly a brewery.  Occupied for a day &amp; a night by Crown Prince &ndash; Fort Rompu.<br/>Apr.6th.<br/>Waggon. Guard 3 am to 6 am- myself in party of 36 bearers- kit, blankets etc.- Observation balloons- Lost!- Found!  Arrival at Le Estrade-</p>
