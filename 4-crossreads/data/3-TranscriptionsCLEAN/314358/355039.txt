
Heliopolis3 Nov 1915Darling Teddie,
I have only just received my first letters from you and motherOne from you dates 24th AugustOne from Mother same dateOne from Mother 7th SeptOne from you no date in one of mothers date 14 SeptOne from Mother 14 SeptOne from Dear Edina September one from the [Moyens?] Aug 30th
It has been such a very long wait my darling, but it is good to get the letters at last dear one.I do hope you have got some of my letters by nowI will write offtener dear girl but you cannot know what a little time I get sometimes not enough time to undress for bed not to get a bath for several daysAs Regimental Quartermaster Sergeant for this Battalion I am almost the busiest man hereOur strength now is nearly 1400 men all of whom have to look after generally.My Battalion is only a small part of the vast camp which consists of may Battalions.From 5 oclock in the morning to 12 oclock at night I am generally
