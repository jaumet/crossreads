
[Friday 26 May 1916]
Hotter again &Boils again as well as flies are an awful curse.[Following paragraphs are crossed out]Bob Ronald of Nap Nap from the 6th L.H. came to my Tent & had a yarn.And many other L.H. men of the 2nd Brigade- The rest of the 1st L.H. Brigade arrived today - 2 LH & 3 L.H.   The Ambulance have made a sort of Sleigh out of a Sheet of Galvanised Corrugated Iron pulled along the sand by a Camel & Chains, to collect sick men in- & wounded.Found 6 Tents buried in the sand near the Yeomanry Camp evidently by Bedouins
