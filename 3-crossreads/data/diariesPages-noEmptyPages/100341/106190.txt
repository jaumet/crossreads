May 19th
Physical drill before breakfast. 9-30 Drill order with rifles, Bayonet fighting, lecture on Correspondence, lecture on aiming parts of rifle, saw whale blowing about 9 a.m., came in sight of land on starboard side, low scattered hills first then higher chain later, ran parallel with them all day. Full marching order inspection after dinner, later lecture on Landscape targets with map. After tea, haircut writing reading, sentinel. Concert after deck. Weather beautiful, sun sea, fair wind W.N.W. direction, 313 M.
