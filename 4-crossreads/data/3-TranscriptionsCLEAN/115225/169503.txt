 Nov. 25  th    We went to Church in the morning & for a walk in the afternoon. it was a fine day - & some friends came in in the evening & we had some music 
   
 Nov. 26  th    I had made an appointment with the dentist for 9.15 A.M. which I kept. he did all the work necessary in 13/4 hours (filled seven teeth) & made a very fine job of them for 2.12.6. M  r   & M  rs   S & I went to the theatre in the evening & saw "To-night's the night" (the second time I have seen it -  
 Nov. 27  th    I slept in this morning  got up in time to have a good hot bath before lunch (which we had early) & I caught 12.46 train to Paddington - Left my haversack at Waterloo  & went to tea in Oxford Street with & friend & went back to Kingston at 8.30 P.M. 
   
 Nov 28  th    I went to Victoria & booked a bed for to-night as I am going back to France to-morrow morning a day early as I wish to visit my Sister at her Unit. No 1. Canadian Gen. Hospital. Etaples - I took my Aunt to the Pictures in the afternoon & the theatre in the evening & saw "Search me" it was very good - I got back to my bed in Victoria at 12.30 -
