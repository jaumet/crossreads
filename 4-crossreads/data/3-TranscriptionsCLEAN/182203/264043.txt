To my surprise I got word again that he was coming down Tuesday, as they were leaving Egypt on Thursday. So the dear boy came down again & we had a grand time together until 8.15. p.m.  I have not received any wire since, so suppose he is still in camp at Tel-el-Kebir. No doubt we shall have Saturday on the Lake. I have the whole day - off duty.  We think now that our  Unit will be leaving here in about 3 weeks time.  Poor old George Bower is ill & in hospital.
