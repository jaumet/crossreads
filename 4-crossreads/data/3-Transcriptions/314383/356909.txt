<p>July 21st<br/>Arrived Takan Tepe.  Rather a pretty place for Persia in that it has avenues of poplars leading to the town.  Derives its name from a knoll just outside the town 18 miles.  Major Chaildecott joins us from across country.</p>
<p>July 22<br/>Arrived San Jud a long weary trek of 28 miles</p>
<p>July 23rd<br/>Arrived Sain Kala 17 miles, pitched camp on outskirts of town hear a large River.</p>
<p>July 24<br/>No sign of our friends.  Buy over telegraph operator who agrees to bring us all telegrams to look thru'.  Gov visited camp today.</p>
