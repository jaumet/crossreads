<p>when he came back from Southampton for his kit which he left here.  so everything alright I carried my kit to the offices and explained my position there they could not give me any money but gave me an order to stay at a large boarding house for a week and then report back.  so that evening I went to this place "Peel House" and booked my room a large place billiard tables and all conveniences but still there is no money for me at the bank. But I am alright here now for a week.  I left russel Square on the morning of Feb 16th 16 and same evening started my weeks Lodging at peel House.  that night had a game of billiards there and</p>
