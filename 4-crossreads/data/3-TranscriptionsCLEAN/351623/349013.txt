  
 HMAS Sydney took one of its destroyers in tow at 10 pm & left for base at 23 knots. 
 13 Aug 17 Arrived at Scapa Flow at 11 am anchored & started to coal. Took in 800 tons, average 172 tons an hour. 
 14 Aug 17 Anchored at Scapa Flow. 
 15 Aug 17 Still in Scapa Flow, weather fine. Salvage boat is recovering bodies from Vanguard. 
 16 Aug 17 In Scapa Flow, weather fine. 
 17 Aug to 20th In Scapa Flow doing exercises. 
 21 Aug 17 In Scapa Flow. Tried our tried our moving picture machine for first time.
