<p>to him as I should like to see him.</p>
<p>Saw Geo. Robertson a few weeks back &amp; he's looking fine.  He is a Div. Engineer and was building a shed when I saw him.  Also got a glimpse of Dave Stein some while back, also Raward.  Norm Ballantyne was also asking for me at the Coy. but I was up the line and missed him.</p>
<p>You say This time I'm afraid you'll vote yes".  Very foolish of you to think that.  Your views coincide with mine.  Anyway, there are more men in Ireland than in Ausie &amp; they won't conscript them.  However, this letter has to be censored so I can't tell you what I think as politics are barred.</p>
