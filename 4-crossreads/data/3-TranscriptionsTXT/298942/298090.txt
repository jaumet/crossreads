 On the way I found a piece of shell &amp; put it in my pocket as a souvenir. 
 Since then he has been shelling round there pretty consistently at intervals, but we have had no more cases so far. I notice a cluster of houses in hollows some distance from here have been set on fire.  Metoren &amp; Bailleul are still burning.
