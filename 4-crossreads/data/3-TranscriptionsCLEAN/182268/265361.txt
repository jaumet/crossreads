  
 Thursday 11/10/17. In billets near Steenvoorde, fixed up all shortage for 28 th . 
 Friday 12/10/17. Collett returned & took over, I went back to 26 th  Bn. Currie to 28 th . 
 Saturday 13/10/17. Stirring up things  saw Bridges at Div re. "Dud" Camp 
 Sunday 14/10/17. Finished letter to Doff  saw Bas who has done well. 
 Monday 15/10/17  Visited all billets  went to STEENVOORDE to dinner with Lloyd  applied for leave to England.
