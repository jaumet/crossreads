<p>I was round at the cookhouse this evening having a yarn to Digby &amp; McGrath &amp; while I was there they were cutting up the meat, it was frozen as hard as steel &amp; ice all through  not much fear of getting it flyblown or flabby this weather. We are getting "chestnuts" issued to us now instead of potatoes. I think they get them principally from Spain she is a great country for nuts of all sorts<br/>6th. . Been pretty crook all day &amp; a nasty cold &amp; headaches, it makes one feel so rotten.<br/>7th.. Out on fatigue last night trucking Stokes mortars to the firing line  we took over a 1000 up on 6 trucks, just as we got to the end of our journey hanged if our truck didn't capsize &amp; shot all the boxes of bombs flying       I very near got</p>
