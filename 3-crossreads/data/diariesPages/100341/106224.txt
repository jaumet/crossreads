21st
about 2 miles. Slept in gulley all night, 3 wounded by stray Bullets. Beach shelled where our boys were bathing getting water. Moved off further and took up Bivouack just rear of our trenches, midday (1-30) between heavy Gun duel all afternoon. Navy land batteries gainst Turks shells flying overhead all day. Two Platoons of C Coy moved over to assist in trenches, remainder slept in readiness.
22nd
Violent infantry attack commenced about 3.30 a.m. lasting about 2 hours. Good issue of rations given. Battery duel continues aided still by Navy.
23rd Monday
All day in same Gulley reading sleeping. Water carrying 9 to 10 p.m.
