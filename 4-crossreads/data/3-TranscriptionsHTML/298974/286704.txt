<p>[postcard]</p>
<p>Mrs A Fry.<br/> Northcote Rd.<br/>Lindfield. Sydney<br/>Australia</p>
<p>1 Sep. 15</p>
<p>Dear Mother</p>
<p>Just nipped into a tent where writing material is to send you this card. Am OK. Had 3 days in London. Met Bert Radcliffe, he is a Lieut. Went to Cambridge but Mr. H. was in Scotland. Will probably be in England a fortnight more.</p>
<p>Best love, writing Dene</p>
