<p>&amp; then to the Pack Store to be repaired &amp; re-issued &amp; here is where the Red + can help with pyjamas, hanky, shirts, a toothbrush, soap etc. etc.<br/>No grumbling except when they got no letters or couldn't draw their back pay.<br/>Visited each other &amp; attended concerts in the Hall.<br/>We always knew when fresh troops arrived from Aus.  Prices went up &amp; fresh faces appeared.  They did route marches in the desert &amp; dug trenches &amp; in spare time visit the city of Cairo &ndash; its beautiful mosques &ndash; fascinating shops &amp; bazaars, the old remains of the Roman Aquaduct or take tram &amp; visit the Pyramids &amp; Sphinx.</p>
