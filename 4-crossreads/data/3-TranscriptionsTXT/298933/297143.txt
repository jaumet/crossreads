 Centelles [April 1918] 14 Sun Drinking Rum 15 Mon After 4 hours heavy shelling we were forced to quit our quarters 16 Tues B H Q in a chalk pit.General sent me back to Boves, living in a chateau 17 Wed My home is a musty old cellar my mate is a good sort, Jack Trenbeth.The boys are having a hard time.
