  
 She   &  three other sisters from 3rd A.C.C.S. went together but expect they are back in  France to-day. 
 It is over 12 months since I came to this place (13th Sept 1916) but the weather is perfect. Can hardly think it is the same spot. Everything was so cold, wet   &  miserable. The country is beautiful   &  the weather excellent, one could not feel   blue   under these conditions also the patients all doing well   &  therefore very bright.
