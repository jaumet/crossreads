<p>24th August<br/>During  morning Bean, Wilkins &amp;  self motored out to see the St Omer Aerodrome of the Royal Flying Corps. This great aerodrome consisted of many large hangars &amp; more resembled the extensive edifices of a great Central Railway Station. We expected to see the 69th Australian Flying Corps land here, but as the weather was very gusty &amp; windy &amp; army arrangements are so variable, we were disappointed.   They were to fly</p>
