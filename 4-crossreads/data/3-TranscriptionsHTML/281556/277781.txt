<p>6.30 to 7.30 beautifull 7.30 to 10pm Battn Concert in Town Hall very tiring. Weather fine.</p>
<p>Sep 3rd. Sunday.<br/>Church parade 10.30 Bgde. Afternoon looked through [Indecipherable] &amp; then short while in Cathedral listening to organ. Took several snaps while out. light showers &amp; rather cloudy. Church at Soldiers Home evening just in before exceedingly heavy showers came. Did little shoping after service &amp; then home to beg (Bed ?).</p>
<p>Sept. 4th. &ndash; Monday &ndash;<br/>Wet morning. Duty Coy. Roll Call &amp; orders</p>
