<p>We landed at Dover and just near the wharf was the station where a train was waiting for to convey us to Leicester, which was the first English hospital I went too.</p>
<p>We started at half past twelve noon and as it was in the month of August all the harvest was stacked up in the fields ready to be taken into be stored away.</p>
<p>We steamed on through Kent which I may say is a very pretty place, and just at the time we came through the hops were green and looked very pretty.</p>
