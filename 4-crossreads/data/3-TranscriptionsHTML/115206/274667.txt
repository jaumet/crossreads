<p>April 1918<br/>Sunday 7</p>
<p>Blaney Tronville. D.A.H.Q     [ Div.artillery H.Q]<br/>Met a great number of old companions at 5th Pioneer comp.<br/>Worked on horse lines all morning, and set out for Bdge with despatches at 3.30pm.<br/>Got drenched through before Corbey. Sheltered a little while there and met Jimmy Grant.<br/>Arrived and delivered despatches received and set off again for ?d qrs.<br/>Weather wet. Met Les Fuller in La Nevvillo. 15th bdge captured 1000 yds of trenches, about Corbie Sector.</p>
