The stones are tremendous &amp; it is very tiring climbing them.  We had church parade in the morning &amp; some of us leave from 2 p.m. &ndash; 11 p.m.  We tramed into Cairo (1 pt. for 10 miles, cheap enough) and wandered round a bit.
