  
 Monday 3rd [Dec] Very heavy frosts.  All pools frozen this morning &  trying to snow.  It did a little. 
 Tuesday 4th Another heavy frost this morning.  At football the A Team defeated B Team by 16 - 0.  The B Team were outclassed   I played my first game today with B Team & didn't have anything to do.  Saw Les Powers this afternoon. He is with 15 [?] Fld Amb & also Sammy Doyle. 
 Wednesday 5th Concert by our own boys.  Very good.  First appearance of our orchestra. 
 Thursday 6th Football practice & at night Cribbage tournament.  Weather very cold & everything frozen pools [indecipherable] etc. 
 BOMBING RAIDS BY GOTHAS 
 Friday 7th  Saturday 8th Played football this afternoon against ASC Team (D Cunningham was playing) & won by 6 - 5.  Two goals by Billy Brown against a converted try.  I played but did not shape at all well at full back. 
 Sunday 9th  Monday 10th Fritz was over us bombing with his Gothas this afternoon - five or six of them & he distributed a few papers. 
 Tuesday 11th Polling day.  Reinforcements Referendum.  I voted  "yes" 
 Wednesday 12th A bad day
