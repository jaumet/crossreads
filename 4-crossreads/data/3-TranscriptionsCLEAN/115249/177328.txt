  
 W.T.Signal 
 Receiving ship -  Flag 
 Transmitting ship -  Warrego 
 Date - 11.9.4 
 Time of Despatch - 7.40 pm 
 Comdr Beresford to Brigadier and Flag.  I have no details yet of enemys losses I am sending reinforcements to the front which is about five miles from the pier.  I have just received at six p.m a message from Lieut Bond he is again being engaged but is [indecipherable]?
