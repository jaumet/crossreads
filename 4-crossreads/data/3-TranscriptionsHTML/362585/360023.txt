<p>My dear mother,</p>
<p>This is the place where you can get the worst tobacco in the world.  It looks like smells like &amp; tastes like lucern &amp; I wouldn't be a bit surprised if it was lucern.  Some of the officers have got so wed to it now, that they almost appear to be enjoying it, &amp; with a little more practice, I think they will be able to deceive most people.  You pay 1/- per lb for it &amp; by jove its worth it.</p>
<p>Yesterday afternoon, two of us got leave for a couple of hours &amp; went into the city.  The first thing we did was to find a cafe &amp; order tea &amp; scones.  I can't describe <strike>what</strike> how glorious it felt to be our own masters once more even thought it was only for two hours, &amp; those scones tasted like nothing else on earth.
