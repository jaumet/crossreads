<p>As it was impossible to cook anything we had beef &amp; buiscuits served out. At 9 am we had boat drill. The rolling still continued, though the choppiness had gone down. We were ordered to keep our life belts on all day and there was a very strict lookout kept. During the morning a ball of hay and some timber was seen</p>
