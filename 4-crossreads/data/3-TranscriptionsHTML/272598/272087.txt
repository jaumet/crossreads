<p>to-morrow, had 2&frac12; hours sleep in 75.<br/>Arrived back at H'Qrs at 6 pm.  3324 wounded have been collected during the past two days by the B Field Ambulances of the Division.  The dead, missing &amp; prisoners have to be added to that number.</p>
<p>Saturday 22/7/16<br/>Action which took place here a few days back &amp; in which we were engaged, reported as having taken place at Fromelles (2 miles south of Armentieres).  Feeling very much refreshed after a good nights rest.  Shelled here last evening &amp; landed five shells in the grounds adjoining the billet.  Very few wounded came through to-day.</p>
<p>Sunday 23/7/16<br/>Very quiet day.  Piquet Corporal.  Seven motor ambulance wagons</p>
