 [Message side of post card] Dear Miss Dunlop I was very pleased to get Miss Brewin's and your letters, but am sorry I cannot answer them fully as I am very busy and also because of the censor. As I expect you know we are at last off [indecipherable] I am fit and happy. The men are as pleased as punch that we ar[e] off and very [indecipherable] sharpening up the bayonets.. Hoping all are well love [indecipherable] [Note written next to address:] From dear Tom Cadell who was killed at Gallipoli aged 18
