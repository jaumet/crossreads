<p>Saturday 3 July 1917</p>
<p>Our new Commander in Chief is chief topic of conversation. <strike>Tommy officers say &ndash; "Really rough sort of fellah" &ndash; you men should get on well with him!!</strike>  After we got home from last STUNT I asked Troop Leaders to give me a List of any deficiencies caused through crossing Barrage of Shell Fire, meaning such equipment as NOSEBAGS, SHOE CASES etc. likely to fall off saddle &ndash; one put in<br/>4 pairs of PANTS!!!<br/>1 pair of BOOTS!!!</p>
<p>Going Port Said tomorrow <strike>on continued business &amp; pleasure for 3 days</strike> so will post this with some snap shots. <strike>My Table manners will be lacking I'm afraid &amp; I'll find the BED too soft.</strike></p>
