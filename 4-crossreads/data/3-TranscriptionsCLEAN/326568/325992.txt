  
 January 2nd.  Raining again to-day.  Rumours that some men are going Home on transport duty soon. January 3rd.  Ambulance reduced to two sections, C Section being merged into "A" & "B" January 5th.  A Tommy on sick parade amused me this morning, it being at least five months since his last bath. January 8th  Leave to Charleroi from to-morrow but am going on to Bruxelles to-morrow morning all being well.
