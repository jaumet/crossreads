<p>13th May</p>
<p>Into Australia House at 9 am. Lunch 1.15 to 2.15 pm. Return to Hotel about 8 pm Constitutes my day's hours. There is much work to be done in conjunction with the exhibition, so that I have very little time. After dinner I go to bed to give my severe laryngitis a chance.</p>
<p>14th May</p>
<p>On exhibition work arranging the Palestine Walls.</p>
