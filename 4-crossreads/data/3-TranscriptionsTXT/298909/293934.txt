 &amp; 17 L H F A didn't roar, we counted out a Lieutenant Staff Sergeant etc &ndash; One L.H.F.A Sergeant crawled out &amp; wouldn't come with the rest of us &ndash; I reported at the Palace &amp; needless to add fell foul of the Warrant Officer &ndash; by parading &amp;  almost  on behalf of the Boys almost refusing duty. 
 I was immediately reported to O C. as being insolent etc &ndash; Was ordered to be reduced with option of one stripe &ndash; I told them straight, they could have uniform etc &ndash; as myself &amp; Boys were prepared to walk out immediately, if they would transfer us to any other Corps &ndash; nuff said We all got at it &ndash; &amp; started to pack up No. 1 A.G.H.. &ndash; Only a rumour there is no stuff to pack &ndash; some where about 300 Tons I should say &ndash; It is a lovely building &amp;
