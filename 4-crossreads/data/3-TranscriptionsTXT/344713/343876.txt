 Thurs. May. 4th. Sent in to a "Tommy" Hospital at Hazebrouck. Left here again at night for another British hospital at Milississi just outside of St Omer. (No 7 General Hospital) 
 Monday. May. 29th. &ndash; Left hospital &amp; put-up at Details barracks at St Omer for night. 
 May. 30th 1916. Left St Omer by train to Hazebrouck spent day there  amp; left by train again tonight for Steenwerck from where we walked to Sailly where the Bn. is billeted. Rejoined "D" Company. 
 Sat. June. 10th. Left Sailly tonight  &amp; marched to Chapel Farm about a mile the other side of Fleurbaix. 1st  &amp; 2nd  Bns have gone in firing line. Relieved 2nd Brigade. A  &amp; B Coys billeted in Fleurbaix. D Coy. distributed between Elbow Farm. Smith's Avenue, &amp; Chapel Farm. Our sleeping quarters (old barns) are muchly knocked about by shellfire &ndash; hardly any roofs left  &amp; it is starting to rain. No one allowed outside as we are in sight of Germ. Lines.
