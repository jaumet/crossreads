<p>7 September 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Cartwright, self, Fistz Gerald, Carruthers &amp; Sergt. Clifton with Capt. Lee, chosen to attend 2 days at Div. Gas School. Lectured on Gas, its uses deadliness, helmets, new &amp; old. 800 cas. 300 deaths<br/>Dinner at corner eggs &amp; chips, getting sick of these. Tony happens along; go with him to his camp nearby  Back to school. Lachrymatory &amp; Gas tests. Cart &amp; self meet Tony after 4 o'clock  Go into Pop. Rains  Pictures at railway stat. Good band.   Home to [indecipherable]</p>
