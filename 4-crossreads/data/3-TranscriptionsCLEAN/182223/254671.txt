
Monday 14 June 1915Letters posted to The Girls, Mollie, & Mrs M. M. Knowles. Very busy all day  In Cairo during the evening from 5-15 to 7 p.m. Posted letters to
Tuesday 15 June 1915Letter posted to Ned Sheehan Very Hot today. Flies, small ones, troublous. 
Wednesday 16 June 1915Very hotter day today. Wind of the hottest & dust galore At Heliopolis at Night, had Supper at the Brassiere de l'Avenir at 11-15 pm home 12-30 am. on the 17-5-16 [17 May 1915] Wrote to the girls.
