<p>Spt 9 Much laughter &amp; cries of "give it buksheesh lemonade" &amp; "let me like a soldier fall". The usual Saty night concert, but the audience was small owing to the Polar weather. Several chaps have put up colors &amp; gold bars when they're not entitled to them, so theres to be an investigation by one of the ossifers.</p>
<p>10  Sgt Shinnanikins? Came down at 7. &amp; blew his whistle but was impolitely told to imshi and then in his childish way threatened to crime the lot of us! Pretty fair Sermon this mng by the C of E parson.</p>
