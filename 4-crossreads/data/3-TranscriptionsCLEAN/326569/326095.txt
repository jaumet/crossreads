
Points before, during & after firing.
Before1.  Remove oil from bore2.  Oil all frictional parts behind the Body locking pin3.  Weigh Return Spring (13 lbs.)4.  Test feed mechanism5.  Examine mags & ammunition while filling6.  Examine spare parts7.  See that barrel mouth piece is tightly screwed up
During1.  Replace empty mags. in cases.2.  Oil bolt & striker post & mag. post.3.  Weigh return spring.4.  Turn gas regulator slightly to prevent the seizing of threads in gas chamber.5.  Replace partly used mags.6.  Send empty mags. back for refilling.7.  See that clamp ring is screwed tight.
