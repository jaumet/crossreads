
Sunday 18 June 1916I go out alone to Band Performance and chat with two Tommies & home with Hudson.  Anzac Provost in great array.  Church in morning.
Monday 19 June 1916Billy arrives Home at 7 p.m.  All the Tent gorge on melon.
Tuesday 20 June 1916Parcels arrive, one for me from Bess with chocs., cheese, peaches & Veal Paste.  Ellis receives Cake & Soap & [indecipherable].  Wrote to Mum (44), Marie.
Wednesday 21 June 1916Late lunch day for me.  Warm day with a nice warm breeze.  Billy has no trouble to keep the temperature up.
Thursday 22 June 1916Billy, Dave, Ellis & I go for a sail towards Barrage.  Billy & Ellis have a swim near Ghezerah [also spelt Gezira] Bridge.  We all won a game of Billiards.  All Staff moved again.
Friday 23 June 1916Blandford, Perkins, Slatree, Bolton, Taylor go to Artillery.  Capt. McDowell has his say to Snowy.  Wrote to Ida & Wal & Marion Clarke.
Saturday 24 June 1916Billy, Ellis, Dave & I took gharry to Koobeh Gardens & walked back 21/2 miles.  Very tired.  Wrote Mag. Dobson.  Have written 215 letters to this date.
