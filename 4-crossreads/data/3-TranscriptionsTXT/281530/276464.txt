The chalk covers the clothes and makes all white. The 36 grenade is splendid and used much. The Boche posts and his wire were plain to view and he was putting over on to us a few pineapples & minnies. Hereabouts there is a post very close to us - about 60". Later our 6' Newtons got on this place and blew the Huns out. They had to run and our chaps fired at them. Lennon & a sergeant then crawled out and found some papers in a pack there Carried on along the trench and saw some of our raiders and examined the terrain. Returned
