Evening to Brigadier with C.O. reams of orders - all reinforcements to do musketry. Early mess and then hard at orders, rattled them out and arranged to take back balance of pay but no one came to light, too much of a rush. Interrupted a number of times by men wanting to get away with us. One Ryan from 7th Reinf walked all the way up when he heard we were off.
