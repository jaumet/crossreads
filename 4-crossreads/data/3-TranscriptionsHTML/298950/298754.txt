When the No. 2 scale is ordered <strike>this scale</strike> for a period exceeding 21 days, it is to consist of the average stirabout diet for the first 3 weeks &amp; after the fourth week.  During the fourth week the Pris. under sentence is to recieve the ordinary diet.  The entire period for wich any single term of the No. 2 scale may be ordered is not to exceed 42 days.  No. Pris. under sentence, who has been upon this scale for a period of 21 days continiously can again be placed upon it untill the expiration of an interval of 1 week during wich interval he will recieve the ordinary diet.
