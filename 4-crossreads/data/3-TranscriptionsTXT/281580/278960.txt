The long column winding its way like a great serpent over rocky defiles and plain, made a very impressive spectacle. The distance travelled to our present camp was ten miles - a long trail of slippery slimy mud. I was sorry to leave El Bury so quickly as it is a pictorial little spot, an old tomb on the crest of a hill surrounded by gnarled ancient trees. The country abounds with caves which evidently were used in times distant for residences. I was much interested in the watering arrangements, for I discovered the water was being raised by buckets from wells, with which the country abounds.
