<p>[Telegram form &ndash;  marked Six/2]<br/>[Continuation of Ashmead Bartlet's report on the second combined British/Anzac operations at Suvla Bay and Lone Pine, 21st August]<br/><strike>Daily Telegraph  London  R.T.P.</strike><br/><strike>Cable six section four</strike>   our centre and right were to advance from the ridge in front of Chocolate Hill or Yilghin Burnu as it is marked on the map and from the trenches in the plain south of it and after capturing the Turkish trenches in the low ground in their immediate front where  to converge and assault the main objective hill one houndred  and twelve stop<br/>Ashmead Bartlett / Maxwell Censor</p>
