  
 12 March, Wednesday 
 F. We sit up until 1 am talking about Everything & all. We go to Ramsey & buy some things & I hurry M. Home. It is just lovely this day to be HOME. 
 13 March, Thursday 
 F. Ellis hears of his acceptance of Early Repatriation form. Auntie, M & I go for ride to old Ballaugh Church & the Sun Dial. We come Home for Music & we stay in front room all night.
