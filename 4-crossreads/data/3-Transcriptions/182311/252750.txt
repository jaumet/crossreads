<p>minutes then read for about ten minutes from Powell&#39;s &quot;Vive la France&quot; keeping their lively interest all the time.&nbsp; Oh yes, I&#39;m well/in the boom now, such doings - why last night (Saturday) we had a &quot;Trial by Jury&quot; and I took the part of a woman contriving my costume from just what I could find lying about, some feathers off the wild duck in the refrigerator, two handkerchiefs blue two brown, and my mosquito net made quite a &quot;confection&quot; of my old Khaki hat, pyjama coat as a blouse, rugs for skirt, rope yarn doing duty as hair and blackboard chalk of various <s>v</s><sup>c</sup>olours ground up fine as paint and powder.&nbsp; My eldest child was a friend of mine in every way bigger, but dressed in a sailor&#39;s cap, jacket, short pants and socks, my youngest child our pet wallaby covered up well, and when I declared that &quot;he was a real Australian&quot; I just brought the house down. Lipshut was prosecutor and his ready wit certainly was the feature of the evening.</p>

<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; The morning&#39;s church parade was very fine, the weather warm and sparkling, very little wind, the men very happy and singing well, and the Padre had a rattling food sermon for us.</p>
