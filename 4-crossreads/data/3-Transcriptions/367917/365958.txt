<p>Belgium<br/>Dec 31st</p>
<p>Dear Justice Ferguson<br/>Many thanks for parcel which I rec on Xmas Day The Cigaretts were Glorious.</p>
<p>We spent Xmas in a little Village called "<u>Pry</u>" near "<u>Charlerio</u>" it was very lonesome but still we don't mind as there is no more Trenches &amp; no more Bombs Dropping on us at nights &amp; no more Bombardments to go Through we are all anxious to get back now</p>
<p>Most of the Boys Visited</p>
