delaware Baltimore May 1 and 2 1918 Newport Virginia USA
Wednesday
May 1 and 2nd landed at the town of newport from the boat laying in the midstream by barge townpeople made us welcome this is a dry state but no food shortage; Black race in evidence people speak good English slight drall second day ashore thay gave us a tram ride through the suburbs including Riverview "la salle ave" Battle fought here between arramac and cheaspeak graveyard of soldiers cival war light in town
