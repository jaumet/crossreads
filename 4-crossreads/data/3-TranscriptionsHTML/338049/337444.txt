<p>Thursday 22 August<br/>Began ordinary routine but out on job to Div. with Pip Page &amp; others. Running 14 [indecipherable] cable along water pipe trench. Extremely hot again. <strike>Mail</strike> Souveniring poles. Dug up 50 yds of trench.</p>
<p>Mail in later. Great swim with new spring board.</p>
