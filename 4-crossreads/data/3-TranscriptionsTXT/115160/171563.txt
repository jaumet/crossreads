 I have already thanked you for the draft. &nbsp;But I don&#39;t think you need bother any more. &nbsp;I am holding well and a fortnight ago sent a tenner to Auntie. 
 All sorts of furfies are about as to how soon we will be on the way home, but of course no one has any &quot;guts&quot;. &nbsp;We are beguessing whether we&#39;re on the way to Germany or not, but I think it will come to pass. &nbsp;That will make one more country.
