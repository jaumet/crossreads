 Saturday 18th Had Massage & R C Treatment also time for the paper 
 Sunday 19 Put in about 1/2 an hour on fatigue Writing & reading rest of time Quite hot 
 Monday 20 Went up for classification Do not know what I was marked Had Massage & R C treatment. There is an old Roman fort on the hill opposite here. Heard 3 young Aussies discusing what it had been made for - it is just a rough circular bank of earth about 6 ft high & 150 yds across in diameter - They concludled it must have been intended for a Sports ground & Cycle track & could not understand why it was so far away from anywhere
