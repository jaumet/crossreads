
[Written on YMCA paper]In HospitalBoulogne11-8-18
My dearest Wife,I wish and wish that you did not have to wait so long between the Cable I sent yesterday, and which I am told by a Chaplain should reach you in a few days, and this bit of a letter which you will not get until I am real well again, I know you and Mother will be awfully worried when you get the Cable and will think it is worse than it really is, but I thought it would
