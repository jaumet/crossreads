  
 I wish to make an application for a position on the nursing staff for active service abroad, with the Australian Expeditionary Force. I am 34 years of age & am healthy & strong. I have been ten years nursing   I trained in the Melb. Hospital & obtained my General Certificate & R.V.T.N.A. in Nov. 1907. I was on Dr Moore's nursing staff for nearly two years &  for 12 month was theatre nurse. I have had varied experience in both hospital & private nursing.
