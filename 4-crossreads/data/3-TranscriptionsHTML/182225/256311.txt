<p>JULY &nbsp; TUESDAY 11 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>A cold miserable day. We were dismissed &amp; spent the day cleaning up our lines ect. Had some kicks about with the football in afternoon &amp; then the Battery went on duty. I was amongst those picked out for main guard.</p>
