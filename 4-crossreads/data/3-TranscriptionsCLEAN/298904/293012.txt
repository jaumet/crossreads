
Up in the trenches for the last 2 or 3 days we lived like Kings" for all the stores were being destroyed & you could go & get your pick, the one thing the military were particular in destroying was the rum they broke hundreds of stone jars up & it was the best thing they could have done. For if the men had got among the rum theres no one knows what might have happened.All the tracks leading to the beach were being enlarged & little steep places cut down & making steps in them so as 3 or 4 men could go abreast, the ground was frozen hard & all along the tracks men had been chopping little chunks out
