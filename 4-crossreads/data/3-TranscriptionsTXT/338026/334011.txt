 [18 Th] Grooming and Cleaning harness 
 [19 Fri] 12 noon ordered to harness up. Left Boves at 1pm. and arr. Pont Noyelles. about 4 pm. Had billets to sleep in  Tied mules on to waggons 
  Pont Noyelles  
 [20 Sat] Left Pont Noyelles 1pm. and arr. At our next stopping place Beaucourt about 4 pm. Made a dug out as the billets were very poor.
