<p>25:XI:16<br/>Poor old Jack died on the 10th of this month.<br/>In October he collapsed at Boulogne through ear trouble &amp; after four operations at the Queen Alexandra Hosp. Malo-les-Bains, he developed pneumonia &amp; bronchitis,<br/>The cause of death was septic pneumonia.<br/>Went to London for three days (14th to 17th) &amp; had a few hours with Dad &amp; Lily.<br/>Everything here is as it was before.</p>
