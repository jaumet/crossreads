<p>At the Front<br/>28/9/1915</p>
<p>Dear Ted</p>
<p>Another line trusting it will find you all well leaving me just the same still in the trenches I am still looking for mail but devil a one can I get but it will come some day I suppose</p>
<p>how is poor old Dah getting along now. Hutchy is still away I cannot hear of him</p>
<p>is Sid still thinking of coming he wants to get it out of his head his knee would never stand it as Infantry we may get our horses again after a while I hope so</p>
