27 June1, 2, &amp; 3 Platoons go to the range for rifle shooting.  4 Platoon granted leave for Cairo.  We go and see the Pyrimids and had a spin round Cairo in a motor with Sergeant Pulling &amp; corp. Bradford arriving back home at 9 p.m.
28 JuneNo. 4 on duty.
29 JuneIssued with Kahki shorts &amp; shirts.  Go to the range for rifle shooting.  Fire 10 rounds in the "grouping".
30 JuneShooting on the range.
1 JulyFinish all the ranges.  I score 105 points.  "A" Coy. marches home to Heliopolis.
2 JulySee "Tiny Ryan".  The 7 Brigade arrive at Abbasia Barracks.
3 JulyAm sick.  Go on guard at 7-12 PM.  Sergeant Robertson &amp; Corp. Perrol over guard.
4 JulyGuard dismissed at 8 AM.  Go into Cairo with 4 mates.  We see the zoo etc. &amp; arrive home at 9 PM.  Webb, O'Hara, Taylor.
5 JulyNo. 2 Platoon sent on picket duty in Cairo.  27 Batt. arrive.
6 July"A" Coy. on duty.  Have a yarn with "Tiny" Ryan of the 13th Batt.
7 JulyRoute march through Heliopolis &amp; out into the desert.  5th of the 13th Battalion leave for the front.  "A" Coy. digging trenches under Lieut. Pye.
8 July"A" Coy. on duty.  Address by Colonel Unsworth in the Y.M.C.A.
9 July"D" Coy. goes to the rifle range.
10 JulyGranted leave for Cairo.
11 July"A" Coy. on duty.  Am on guard.  Receive letter from home dated June 7th.
