<p>1916 November 21<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Receive sudden order to pack up &amp; be at Buire by 8.30 a.m impossible to be there in time  Arrive late. Blown up by O.C. We are destined for Biscaudel C.C. Station for duty.  Arrive after tiring march with pack at 12 noon. No dinner. Allotted to our several duties.. Bob, Ridgway &amp; self on night duty in undressing tent.  Strip all the patients &amp; put clean pyjamas on them. Not many thro; during night, cold.</p>
