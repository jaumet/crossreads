<p>Christmas Day Dec 25th</p>
<p>Great preparations were made for a good time in camp, and two large tents were put up together and tables and forms were borrowed and we had a splendid Christmas Dinner at 1 pm. Having had programmes printed of the days fun I am going to write out here a copy of it.</p>
