  
 Thursday 7 October 1915  Had  cholor  Anti-choleria vaccine innoculation this evening at Coonvalescent Camp Mustapha. The C.O. there sent me lots of patients when I was at Abbassia  At  At the Sultan Hassan Club during the afternoon.  Wrote to the girls & many others.  At a Music Hall at night. Sick. Did not enjoy it. Bed midnight.  
 Friday 8 October 1915  Letters from Car, Joseph, Kitty (Home from Cessnock on 8 th  or 9 th  Septbr) DeLargie and Doffie. Answered them at once.  Some men from the Kanowna [Australian hospital ship], dined with me tonight. Met Austin Curtin from Bondi Sydney.  At a rotten Musical Show in the evening  
 Saturday 9 October 1915  Writing all the morning.  Lunched on board the Australian Hospital Ship Kanowna with an officer from Hobart. Saw Curtain, Moss, Fluffie Vickards [Vickers], Hamilton,  and so  Featherstone, & some others.  Received orders to join the ship Ismailia at 10 a.m. on 10.10-15. at Quay 45 to cross the Mediterranean. Jerom also. 
 Sunday 10 October 1915  7a.m. Mass at St Catherines.  Mustapha Barracks at 9-15 am.  At Ship Ismailia at 10 a.m. got on board at 1-30 pm.  Left No   45 wharf at 4-30 p.m. Passed through breakwater at 5.45 p.m. Sea Smooth. Conditions comfortable.
