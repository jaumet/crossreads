  
 Saturday 15 December 1917. 
 More mail arrives. 6 letters  I got  for me. Messages of congratulation read out on parade at 7 am. today. Letters dated as late d  as Nov 6th in today's mail. 
 Wrote letters. 
 Regimental paper the "Cacolet" at last comes to light. 
 Rumors of a move & of Hollands entrance into war against Eng d . News that Clarrie is wounded in France. 
 (NOTE) The mail has cheered everybody &  wh  all seem pleased. I got some very nice letters especially from D'nong.
