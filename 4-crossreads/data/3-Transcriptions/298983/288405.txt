<p>dressing put on &amp; was put in a car on a stretcher. They made me into a stretcher case because I colapsed for a moment in the dressing Station Was taken out of the Car at another Station &amp; inoculated, Put in another Car &amp; brought here Gottweter seems a fair sized Hospital was given Gass &amp; wounds fixed properly Have not had much pain. Had 2nd inoculation</p>
<p>Sunday 31st March<br/>Stayed in bed</p>
