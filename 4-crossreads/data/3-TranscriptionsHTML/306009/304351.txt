<p>1915<br/>June14 Mon.<br/>Parade of the Unit held at 2 p.m. for inspection by the O.C.- most of the chaps turned out in their new uniforms &ndash; jolly warm in the sun &ndash; must have been over 100&deg; in the shade.<br/>Yester an Officer &amp; a dozen men arrived from Sydney &ndash; advance party of the 5 Field Ambulance &ndash; discovered G.P. Stock, Scout Commissioner for N.S.W., among them as a Pte. &amp; also Banks Smith, an old Wahroonga chap.</p>
