He is looking fit. Went to town with him today, had a most enjoyable time. He went back to London this afternoon.<br/>23/2/18.<br/>Went for a run down into Devon during the week; was away two days but was not missed, it is lovely down there; well might they call it Glorious Devon! Had tea down town with Arch McKenzie this evening, and went to a show.<br/>27/2/18.<br/>"X" Carrier went yesterday and I am on the roll of Carrier "D.2." We shall not be long now.</p>
