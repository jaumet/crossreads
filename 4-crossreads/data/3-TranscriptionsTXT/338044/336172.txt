 24th June. Temp 102 &deg; This departure is again delayed. Lord! how long are we to stay in this rotten country &amp; heat? The sooner the next lot depart, the sooner we'll follow. Where? Some say Mesopotamia, or Bombay for the last lot. Isn't  this hot enough? Sergt  Avant says Wednesday is the date 
 25th June:- Sunday Not quite so hot today  About 96 &deg; in the shade. After going to church at 10 am I simply turn in  un  dressed with but an old light pair of trousers &amp; read for the rest of the day.
