<p>After I had <strike>kept</strike> got a few of the men away an English Staff Sergt. came along to tell me that he had got some trucks &amp; an engine on the light railway, &amp;amp that he could take the men right through. I told him to ask for instructions on the way, &amp; if he met nobody to take them right through which he accordingly did with the result that the whole of the cases were evacuated in 1&frac12; hours.<br/>Just about this time a Major Johnson R.M.O. of the 12th Batt   arrived as a relief, &amp; immediately provided half a dozen <strike>str</strike> regimental stretcher bearers who helped me</p>
