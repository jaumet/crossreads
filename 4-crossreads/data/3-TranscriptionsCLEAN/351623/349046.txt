  
 30 Oct 18 Out with 2nd L.C.S. running torpedoes back to anchorage off munition factory.

 4 Nov to 10 Nov Anchored off Charlestown 
 11 Nov 14 Hostilities ceased today on all fronts, result being that everyone is very happy. Served a tot of rum to all hands & we had some singing & band. Ships were blowing syrens & sending up rockets, all round the harbour. 
 12 Nov 14 Went ashore in afternoon to Dumfermline to celebrate.
