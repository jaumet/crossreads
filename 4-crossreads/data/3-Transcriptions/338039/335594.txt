<p>Billeted in barn- Improvised sleeping- Leave to Steenwercke in evening.<br/>Apr. 7th.<br/>Capt. Strachan O.C. of new camp- squad drill &amp; letters   Sudden departure in afternoon.  Strange to say we ride in cars instead of footing it.  "Are we off to the trenches?" (Later) "No!"<br/>Apr.8th.<br/>Our party take up duties in 2nd. Divisional Baths at Erquinghem- Work in drying room- very trying &amp; oppressive- "French friends."</p>
