 352 4 Her husband and other son are both serving in the army &ndash; she brought me a large basket of strawberries and a pot of cream &ndash; While I was out yesterday and Mrs Goodfellow called &ndash; she is cousin of the maters but I suppose you know all about her &ndash; she is coming to take me out tomorrow &ndash; I do hope I see you before you go &ndash; Quite likely I shall be at Blackheath Saturday night 
 Fondest love old girl Ever your  devoted brother Gibson McMillan
