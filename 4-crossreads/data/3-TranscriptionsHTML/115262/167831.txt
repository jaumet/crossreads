<p>Monday 5 March 1917.</p>
<p>Arrived back off leave.</p>
<p>News just come through that embarkation on Wednesday has been deferred till Friday.</p>
<p>Passed&nbsp;final medical exam for embarkation and had hair close cropped.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">In afternoon our unit was taken for a swimming parade.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">Interstate Artilelry, T.M.B. M. Grs, Cyclists, &amp; A.S.C, units marched into camp at Seyr [Seymour] for embarkation this week.</span></p>
