<p>The Turkish Sanitation was practically non-existent I think, as the flies are very numerous &amp; my hands have both gone terribly septic, which is a great nuisance as, besides being painfull, when on the move like this one cannot get them properly attended to.</p>
<p>How the scrap is going today I have not the faintest Idea, we seem to be pretty stationary. Was up at DHQ last</p>
