
November 1917Tuesday 27Got orders to move back to Latron with 5th Mtd Bgd. I and 3 others detailed as DRLs or mtd orderlies and attached to 60th Inf Div. Reported at Enab Hqrs 8.30 am. Cushy job so far
Wednesday 28Still at Enab with 60th Div. 10th ALH to be only Aust troops to go thro Jerusalem.Andrews and Anderson left .Very cold
