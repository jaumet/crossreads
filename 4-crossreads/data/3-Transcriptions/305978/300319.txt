<p>This little book contains a diary of my travels since leaving Sydney on October 30th 1915 on the Argylshire.</p>
<p>Jan 14/1/15<br/>Enlisted at Victoria Barracks for the Light Horse.</p>
<p>16th/<br/>Went into camp at Liverpool. 28 miles from Sydney on main Southern line. We marched down from the station to the camp, there were about fifty of us; The quartermaster soon dished out blankets  &amp; eating utensils, &amp; the next thing was tea oh, plenty of bread &amp; jam if nothing else; then the few old soldiers among the crowd</p>
