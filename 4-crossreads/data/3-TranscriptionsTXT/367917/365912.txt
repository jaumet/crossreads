 So far of course we have not been able to do much as there has been little time since we came out. The men were badly in need of rest, and there has been military training to carry on. Influenza has hindered much, and accommodation for lectures is difficult to get. However it is something to have made a start, which will enable us to get well away  before  when the last shot is fired.
