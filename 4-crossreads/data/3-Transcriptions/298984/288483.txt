<p>this morning &ndash; Cape Otway  Ringbarked hills with green grass &amp; odd patches of cultivation. Went through heads &amp; anchored about 12 midday. Went through the Dr farce &amp; went on up the harbour in the evening Anchored off Port Melbourne for the night</p>
<p>Wed 7<br/>Rivalie at 5 tied up about 7 &amp; soon after the Vic got of &amp; were taken away per Motor  After a fair delay We could get on the wharf if so inclined</p>
