 First impressions of camp good &ndash; given good tea &amp; temporary shakedown in hut. 
 Wednesday   Following morning were allocated work in Hut 83 &ndash; a ward &ndash; after medical inspection etc. etc. I, of course, explained for what I was sent down, &amp; Sgt. said I should in due course be sent into dispensary. Subsequently found out that Cpl dispenser was to be drafted with permanently unfits for Australia on Sunday &amp; I was to take his place (Positively refuse to conjecture on whether I shall get promotion
