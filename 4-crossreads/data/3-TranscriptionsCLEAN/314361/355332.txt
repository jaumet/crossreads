We all expected that we would all be leaving for Tel-el- Kebir to morrow but now it seems that our company is not to go  This afternoon I spent in Cairo, & had a very nice afternoon tea at the YMCA, we had afternoon tea then a short service. This morning I handed a parcel into the orderly room to be sent home to Louie, it was only a tin of chocolates a few cig cards & a small souvenir Wednesday 15 Feb 16 Received a letter from Louie this afternoon the first for about a month.
