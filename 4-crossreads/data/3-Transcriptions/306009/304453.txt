<p>1915<br/>Sep. 28 Tue.<br/>Met Mildred Brown at 3.15 p.m. &amp; after calls at a photo. shop &amp; post office, went off to the Native Bazaars where all kinds of shoes, mats, rugs, jewelry, tinware, etc, etc, can be seen in the making &amp; all sorts of curios &amp; valuables are on sale.<br/>Finished up with a visit to No. 1 Gen. Hospital, Heliopolis.</p>
