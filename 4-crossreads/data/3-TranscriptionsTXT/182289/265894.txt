 16.5.18 Orders received for a Battalion parade on Saturday the 18th.  Instructions given that limbers, equipment ect. should be cleaned. Sections given an hour's lecture on Bombing by Divisional Bombing N.C.O. Orders received at 10.30 p.m. that Company would parade as part of Battalion under 11th Brigade for inspection by the Commander-in-Chief on the 17th at about 3 p.m.
