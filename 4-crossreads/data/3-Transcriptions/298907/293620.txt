<p>&amp; then sketches the size of it on your card &amp; away you go, after this little piece of work I was washed &amp; given us dinner &amp; carted away to the operating theatre, I don't remember a great deal of what happened in here only remember the Dr &amp; nurses in their white aprons &amp; rubber gloves, of a formidable array of glasses, knives &amp; etc, I can remember hearing the nurse say smell this, then a great &amp; tremendous banging which gradually sounded fainter &amp; fainter till it died away in the distance then when I came to I was in my bed again &amp; a nurse was bending over me, but what a headache I had everything was spinning round &amp; round &amp; I lapsed again into unconsciousness &amp; I believe I swore a little when coming round so the nurse told me at any</p>
