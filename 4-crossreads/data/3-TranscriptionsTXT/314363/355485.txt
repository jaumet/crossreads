Also there is a beautiful bunch of wild flowers which we gathered along the railway line yesterday June 23rd. Today I sent off to Louie a nice eddition of Moliere's Plays, some time ago I sent two other "Rapa Mama Babe" &amp; "The Leeland Fisherman". We are still on the hill, have had a couple of windy says with some rain, but today has been fine, read nearly all the day. Tuesday June 25th 18 Have been doing a great deal of reading today, just now ave read Julia de Trecoeur (Feuillet) a very nicely written little book. We go forward tomorrow night. This evening I received two letters from Louis &amp; one from Annie, wrote several this afternoon.
