
ALLAN LINER.M.S.
On Route EgyptDecember 23rd 1915At Sea
Mother DearYou see I got through alright & enclose the last few days of my diary. These have been very fine days indeed & you will see I have Faith enough to be thankful to the Almighty for a calm sea the night we left.I had for 7 hours at [indecipherable] about 600 yards of Trench from Camel Hump to No 1 out post & 14 men to hold it with without any relief - Posted at intervals
