<p>11 May 1918 Saturday   <u>Not for printing</u><br/>Said goodbye to Dolly &amp; caught the 6 o'clock train for Palestine once more, the little girl is wonderfully cheerful &amp; well I am glad to say. Wrote Mrs Ish, Laura &amp; fixed up Dolly's a/cs etc:- Address all letters to the wife to<br/>c/- Manager Anglo Egyptian Bk Cairo  as she changes her address.</p>
