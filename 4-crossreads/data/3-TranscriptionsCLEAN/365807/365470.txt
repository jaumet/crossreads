 SYDNEY, 29th June 1917. P.O. No. A. 17-3987 
 Dear Judge Ferguson, 
 I have to inform you that the following cablegram respecting your son has been received from the Premier (now in London), dated 26th instant.- 
 "Please inform Judge Ferguson Premier has seen his son before he left for Scotland. Greatly improved, no visible effect of shock, speech apparently quite restored; am writing fully." 
 Yours faithfully, F.C.G. Tremlett Acting Secretary. per J.N.G. 
 The Honorable Mr. Justice Ferguson, Supreme Court, SYDNEY
