<p>Thursday 2 August 1917.</p>
<p>The camels again. After breakfast equipment fixed up for 4 hrs march into the Desert tomorrow.</p>
<p>During evening a party of us went to Heliopolis. Saw right through Luna Park for 5 P.T. &amp; then went up through the principle streets. The buildings are beautiful &amp; the latest type having been rebuilt during the last 10 yrs. Prior to this these buildings were some of the oldest in the world.</p>
