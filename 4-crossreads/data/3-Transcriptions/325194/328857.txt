<p>50 piatres to the good about 10/-  now that's all gone and we have to go out again to night to try our luck .<br/>It was given out on parade the other day that the Russians had had some very marked successes and the turks are on the run, we gave em three cheers . If its true I wouldn't be surprised if we got a move on to france, do me, fed up with Egypt and its dirty inhabitants, they get on my nerves.</p>
