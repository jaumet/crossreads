<p>at 11.30 p.m. and left at 1 oclock this morning. We disentrained and marched to a camp about 2 miles from Poperhinghe, at breakfast time 26.8.16</p>
<p>We are moving up to firing line tonight 1.9.16</p>
<p>We entrained about &frac14; of a mile from camp and proceeded for about 6 miles in the direction of Ypres. We marched from the southern portion of the town, through it to supports to the rear of hill 60. 2.9.16</p>
