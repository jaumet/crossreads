<p>As I was hurrying down New Street yesterday afternoon who should I spot but Kelleway, but I never had a chance to speak to him, Jack Hayes told me he was over here sick but I never thought I should meet him in Birmingham</p>
<p>For tea last night in addition to the bowl of soup each man had a plate of strawberries sugar &amp; cream they were the gift of some people &amp; very nice eating they were.</p>
