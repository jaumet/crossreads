<p>Monday 18th June<br/>Nothing much has happened but something is about to happen<br/>We are to leave this beautiful town for the miseries of camp on Salisbury Plains<br/>The original rumour said last Wednesday, but fact will make it this Wednesday or Thursday.<br/>Last Wednesday I gave a lesson on Australia to the kiddies of Sandford School.  They were interested &amp; greatly responsive.<br/>I have been out frequently to Sandford &amp; spent very happy times there discussing</p>
