<p>April 30, Mon.<br/>Warned for the line in real earnest, issued w/- iron rations, amtn. etc.</p>
<p><strike>April</strike> May 1, Tues.<br/>Weather very hot.  Altered draft from a small one of returned men to large one of reinforcements about 300.</p>
<p>May 2, Wed.<br/>Windy, still standing by.</p>
<p>May 3, Thurs.</p>
<p>May 5th, Sat.<br/>Drew 48 hrs. rations and proceeded by rail to "Albert".  Saw the famous hanging statue of the Madonna &amp; child on Albert Cath.  Stayed in rest camp all night.</p>
<p>May 6th, Sun.<br/>Marched to a village near Bapaume viz:-  "Ligney Thilloy" [Ligny Thilloy] where we joined the 14th M.G.C., received a hearty welcome from officers &amp; men.</p>
