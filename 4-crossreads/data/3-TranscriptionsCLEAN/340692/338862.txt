Everywhere lies the wastage of war, both in material & men, for all around is equipment of every description, & in all stages of decay.  Amunition of every calibre & distruction lies wasting in the mud.  Here a pile of rusting hand granades intermingle with with  belts of  machine gun belts, cartridges keep company with a number of broomstick bombs, everywhere can one see all formes of unexploded bombs & shells both English & German.
