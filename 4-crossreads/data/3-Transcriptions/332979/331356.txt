<p>went to the Pavilion, a very nice Theatre with a full Orchestra &amp; a good programe we remained there until 10.30 Pm &amp; then we adjourned to a Restaurant for Supper <strike>at</strike> after having some very nice Fish &amp; Bread &amp; Butter &amp; Coffee I went along with my friend to his Hotel were I booked a Bed &amp; Breakfast for 5/-. As I was tired I <strike>soon</strike> turned in &amp; soon was fast asleep.</p>
<p>Sunday 29th/4/17-<br/>I awoke at 8 am &amp; after having a Hot Bath I adjourned for Breakfast. Then I went into the Shaving Saloon &amp; has a Shave &amp; off I went for the Day as my friend had made previous arrangements &amp; bade him Good Bye &amp; went along for a Walk. I decided to look up a family whose address I had, so after taking a Bus &amp; then a Train I soon found</p>
