<p>12 March, Wednesday</p>
<p>F.<br/>We sit up until 1 am talking about Everything &amp; all. We go to Ramsey &amp; buy some things &amp; I hurry M. Home. It is just lovely this day to be HOME.</p>
<p>13 March, Thursday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Ellis hears of his acceptance of Early Repatriation form. Auntie, M &amp; I go for ride to old Ballaugh Church &amp; the Sun Dial. We come Home for Music &amp; we stay in front room all night.</p>
