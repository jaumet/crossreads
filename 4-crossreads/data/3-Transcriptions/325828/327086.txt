<p>Probably by the time this letter reaches you it will be both Mother's &amp; your birthday once again.<br/>May you both be blessed this Birthday, People dear, &amp; this letter carries with it every loving wish for Very Many Happy Returns of the Day with the hope that by next <strike>time</strike> birthday I may be able to Kiss you both at home in Sunny New South Wales.<br/>My time is up.  My love to you all at Home, &amp; love to you &amp; Mother<br/>Ever your loving son<br/>Leo.</p>
