<p>Thursday 20/4/16<br/>Fatigue work practically all day.  All camps about the desert given Australian names such as Duntroon, Narrabri, Brighton Beach.</p>
<p>Friday 21.4.16<br/>Good Friday; water fatigue.  Blankets fumigated by a sanitary squad, who travel from camp to camp for such duty.  Church parades during day, but was unable to attend owing to fatigue duty.  Paid 150 piastres = &pound; 1-10-9</p>
<p>Saturday 22/4/16<br/>Mail day.  Route march afternoon.  Scratch football match at night.</p>
<p>Sunday 23/4/16<br/>Very warm.  No duty to day  Meeting at night of Officers &amp; men</p>
