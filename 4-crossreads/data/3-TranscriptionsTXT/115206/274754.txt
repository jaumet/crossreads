
July 1918Wednesday 3
Vaux sur Somme.1st Bty Position.Weather glorious , fine and clear: Received a long letter from Mother. Wrote one or two to different folk.Final preparations made for tonight's stunt . Tom Codeand Da Costa are to hop the bags tonight. I wish themboth the very best of luck.  Georgee Baker and Fitters North to hospital with Trenchfever.I am Assistant linesman in the company of Ken Gunn for the stunt.
