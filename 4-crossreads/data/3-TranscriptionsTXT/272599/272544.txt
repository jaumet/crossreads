 1917 Tuesday 23 October 
 MacB. Drum. &amp; Self to Loch Lomond by tram electric. Foggy cold morning. Clyde Bank &amp; celebrated ship building yards. Dumbarton Rock &amp; Castle. Loch Lomond. Ballock. Park Rabbit, Arvie half. Mac &amp; lassie in train. Fun on the Bristol  Arr. back Glasgow for lunch. Browns Restaurant. Good O. Get photos taken, fire light. Cinema rain heavily. Home for tea. Mrs. D a very nice hostess &amp; kind, fine tea. I leave G--- at 10 p.m. for Birmingham arr. B&mdash;at 7.10 a m.
