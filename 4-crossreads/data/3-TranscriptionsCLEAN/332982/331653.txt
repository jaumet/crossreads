  
 Nov. 15th (Wednesday) Route march in the morning, and back in time for dinner. After dinner we were marched to the oval where we had sports, Tea and cakes provided by the Ladies of Capetown. About 5 O'clock we marched to the Feather Market where the Y.M.C.A. provided us with Tea, scones, cakes and fruit, then a concert after, but I don't think many of the Boys remained
