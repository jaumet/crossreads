However after 1/2  an hours restive waiting, we were handed our certificates.  It was a big relief to find I had got through as I could have cried with disappoin disappointment had I failed.We came up to Oxford here the following morning (last Monday) but were granted 2 days leave to get our outfits.We were allowed 9 for our outfits, but on an average they run into 30 or so.  I have got all mine & it has run me into between 23 & &pound26.It is a five weeks course & we are kept at it for it is a stiff exam at the end of the course.
