<p>16th Tuesday<br/>Weather very bright &amp; hot.  Naval Guns directed toward Beachy Bill with heavy shells during afternoon.</p>
<p>27th Wednesday<br/>Sky clouded at times but very windy.  Combined Naval &amp; Artillery bombardment all along line for about 1 hour midday.</p>
<p>28th Thursday<br/>Weather Bright &amp; hot.  Fairly quiet along front.  Evening commenced early with bomb throwing &amp; kept up very furiously all night.  390 Bombs in all used on whole Post during the night.  Small English Mail.</p>
