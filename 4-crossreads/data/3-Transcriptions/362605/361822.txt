<p>&amp; at 12.7, Ellis noticed that there was just 13 at the Table. Is it not strange &amp; a good omen for us after such a long run of lucky 13's.  We arrived home at 2 a.m. having to walk of course.  I go out every night now at 6 pm down to the town to a Concert or a sing-song at the Diggers' Flat as it is called then scramble &amp; push onto a tram for Sanvic.   The cars are single ones of the Pitt St type &amp; 1st &amp; 2nd class on the one car.  No one is allowed to stand inside the Car so you can guess how many get on the front &amp; back platforms.
