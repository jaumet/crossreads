<p>Tuesday 9 October</p>
<p>To "K" Supply Column &amp; 5th D.S.C. Workshops<br/>To A.D.M.S. Scottish Lines Busseboom<br/>To Do. and thence to all the Battalions of the 8th Brigade up near Ypres.<br/>Coming home tonight I met with a slight accident due to a loaded Ambulance Car stopping in front of me.  Resulted in smashing of the front wheel of my bike.  One of the lorry wheels passed over the instep of my left foot.  Only slight</p>
