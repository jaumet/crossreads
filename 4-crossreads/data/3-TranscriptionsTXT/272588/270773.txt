 Saturday 21 September Americans are appearing on this front &amp; soon we are to be relieved &amp; I think for a good long spell behind the war area. 
 Sunday 22 September Amid much handshaking &amp; good wishes fourteen 1914 men left the unit for Australia this morning. In the Orderly Room as "A" section clerk from to-day. Wet.
