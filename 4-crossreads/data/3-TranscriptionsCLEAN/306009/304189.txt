  
 1915 3 Jan.  Sun. Attended Communion Service conducted by Staff Sergt. Start in Music Room at 7 a.m.  Fire alarm sounded at 4.50 p.m. & Unit paraded with lifebelts on, on promenade deck starboard. ( Practice only ) Wrote several more letters during afternoon. At 7.15 pm. attended a sing-song conducted by Chaplain - Col. Nye, followed by Service at 8 o'clock & Communion at 8.30; Then to bed.
