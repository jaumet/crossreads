<p>Tues. Mar. 27.  Yesterday wrote Lill &amp; today to Mother &amp;  <strike>Arthur</strike> David.  Wet, windy day.</p>
<p>Wed. 28.  Hospital ship "Asturias" sunk by the Huns.  Another wet day.</p>
<p>Thur. 29.  Still got the Huns on the move.</p>
<p>Fri. Mar. 30th  Went into Albert with Billy Williams &amp; had a good look round the Town.  Quite a number of civilians have returned &amp; the place is looking quite civilised.  Got Letter from Ruth B.</p>
<p>Sat. Mar. 31  Some H.Q. Staff has moved into Albert &amp; the Town is full of Red Caps.  One has to be careful about saluting these days.</p>
