  
 When we got back to the ship the pilot had just come aboard.  He resides in Hull & he told us that that the raid made by Zeps on the East Coast the other day was at a place 20 miles North of Hull.  The people of that town believing the Zeps could not get so far N. & W. used lights as before the war.  There were 22 killed I believe.  Since he brought us up several steamers travelling along the same route have been torpedoed, some travelling close inshore too.
