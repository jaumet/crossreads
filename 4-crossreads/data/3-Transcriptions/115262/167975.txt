<p>Friday 27 July 1917.</p>
<p>6 am reveille.</p>
<p>Read out in orders that the action I took regarding the letter yesterday is contrary to censorship.</p>
<p>Notified that tomorrow we move out to Abbassia &amp; the list of men to go read out on parade. I am included in original crowd.</p>
<p>Boys having a blow out tonight &amp; the bulk of them merry celebrating the moving out to Abbassia.</p>
