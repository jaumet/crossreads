
Monday 5 March
Marched off to Edgehill - between Dernancourt and Buire - and witnessed the burial of Lt. Col. Williams.  A party of about 26 from our unit attended.  Bob Troup the batman who left Aust. with Lt. Col. Williams fainted at the graveside & Capt. Irvine & Major Donald were deeply affected.Pierrot Show in Albert after tea.
