 27 Friday Same as above slept till 7.40.  Cold day. 
 28 Saturday Went to 41 Russell Square at 10 cut a stencil and left at 1.30.  Typed during afternoon.  Cold day but spasmodic sunshine.  Plane trees in height of glory.
