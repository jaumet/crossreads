<p>1917 Wednesday 4 April</p>
<p>Companions of the "Bath" get to work after breakfast. &amp; put through most of patients before dinner. <br/>The W. O. tells me that I am transferred from "A" Sec bearers to "B" Sec. Tent Division.  Sorry to move out of A. Sec. but know I'm not much good for bearing for a while. <br/>MacBurney, Johno' &amp; self get ride into Albert for paper.  Parade the streets &amp; put in evening. News still good from front line. <br/>America about to declare war.</p>
