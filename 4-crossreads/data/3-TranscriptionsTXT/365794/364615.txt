
Thursday 1st August 1917 [1918]Morning star is the most beautiful thing these nights, no moon now.Miss Creal sends introductory note with Gen. Cox to me.  Miss Cheetham takes him on, I discover later & shows him round.Treatment for malarias differs slightly.  Capt. Hyrons - Intra-muscular inj. Quin. grs. xii daily, Quin. orally Dixon.  Other pts., Quin. 4 hrly then ch. to 6 hrs., then 8 hrs.
