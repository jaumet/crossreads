<p>July Wednesday 18 1917</p>
<p>Raining last night &amp; continuing this morning. Half afternoon off &amp; Loaf. Pay day. Get destination for leave taken. <br/>Skinner goes to Blighty. <br/>Meningitis broken out in M &amp; N Ward. Boisterous weather.</p>
