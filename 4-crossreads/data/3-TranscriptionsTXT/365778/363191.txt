 Monday 23/4/17 Had good look around things normal. Tuesday 24/4/17 Letters from Doff enclosing 4 lovely snaps of Billie &ndash; Normal. Wednesday 25/4/17 Orders to relieve Norrie half tonight &amp; half tomorrow night. Wrote to Doff &amp; General. Thursday 26.4.17 Took over line from Norrie, usual shelling. Friday 27.4.17 Usual shelling by both sides otherwise normal. Saturday 28/4/17 Stapylton wounded, heavy shelling by huns and us. Enemy bombarded our trenches after Gas. Sunday 29/4/17 Quiet by day and usual shelling by night. Cable from Doff
