<p>idea of a pleasant time.  It is easy enough to get a companion but I like to be free &amp; alone so I always come here.  We are right in the country here, 22 miles from the railway Station &amp; 1&frac12; hours by train to Douglas, but Marie &amp; I ride in to Ramsey to the Pictures or do any shopping that we require.  I had a good crossing on the old "Douglas" &amp;amp the weather at present is just tres bon.  I am well &amp; all Here are fine, &amp; send all the best of loving greetings.
