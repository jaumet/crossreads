
120Still on the stunt of billeting & this morning have to revisit a Dr Courmontagne re soldiers billets Madame implores me to leave her home alone if I can & she is ably supported by her father a Maire of a neighbouring municipality I promise to do my best & on learning that I am an avoca] invites me to her home any time I am free to make the acquaintance of her husband.
This afternoon I am deputed to take a statement from a French girl Germaine Bandelin who witnessed the death of one of our men who fell from a horse & a pleasant interview it was & laughable when a baby on the floor gave a representation of the Mannicken Pis.
This afternoon I am on a Court Martial a pleasant way of spending Saturday afternoon so we sentenced him to 12 mos hard labour which is subsequently commuted to 90 day F.P. Then I review the French girl & obtain her signature to the typewritten copy.
20-4-19 (Sun)This being Easter Sunday we decide to dine in style & really do ourselves well. Dinner consists of Soup Devil kidney & Macaroon, Roast Duck, Roast Pork Roast Beef Potatoes Green Peas
