  
 Sunday 9 January 1916 Monday 10 January 1916 
 Tuesday 11 January 1916 Put in the cells for disobeying the guard.  Guard gets into Hot Water & I go free. 
 Wednesday 12 January 1916 Halfway between Fremantle & Suez.  Mock Trial. 
 Thursday 13 January 1916 Crossed the line this morning. 
 Friday 14 January 1916 Saturday 15 January 1916
