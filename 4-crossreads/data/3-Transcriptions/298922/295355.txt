<p>Monday 29 January 1917</p>
<p>6 of our planes just flew over en route <strike>Bersheba</strike> &amp; well laden with bombs, with the aid of Glasses, these can be seen tied on under the body of Car &ndash; poor Johnnie Turk 5 for 1 is our arrangement in bombing. [The following crossed through]   Clive Norton drew out my Power of Attorney &amp; I forward here. With love to you all.</p>
