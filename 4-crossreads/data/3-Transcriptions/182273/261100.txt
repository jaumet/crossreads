
<p>February &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>3 SUN &nbsp;Fine day.</p>
<p>Packing wagons ready for</p>
<p>moving. drew 4 guns. Orders</p>
<p>out re moving on Tuesday.</p>
<p>Writing at night.</p>
<p>4 MON &nbsp;Posted letter to Amy &pound;</p>
<p>Right Section moved off in aft</p>
<p>relieved by 113<sup><u>th</u></sup> Bty. Preparing</p>
<p>to Move out tomorrow. Rec.</p>
<p>letter from Tense &amp; letter, photo &amp; card</p>
<p>from Tom. U.S.A.</p>
<p>5 TUES &nbsp;Moved off 1.P.M. went</p>
<p>on bicycle. called at Mme Duean&#39;s</p>
<p>etc. had a good aft. Au: Vieu Berquin</p>
<p>4.P.M. Went into Au Progr&egrave;s at&nbsp;</p>
<p>night. Had a bonne time. called</p>
<p>at Mme Larinets&#39; for J.</p>
<p>6 WED &nbsp;Home 6.30. Fixing up</p>
<p>billet, Stables scattered - also</p>
<p>billets. fine day. 4 Passes</p>
<p>for Paris. Lively night</p>
<p>with boys.</p>
