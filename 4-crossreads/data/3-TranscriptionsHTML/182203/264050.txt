<p>me to "The Belgian" for dinner. He was very lonely  &amp; I was very tired, so said a Dinner away from hospital was the best thing for us both. Returned in good time.<br/>Saturday, six of us, three Officers &amp; three Sisters had a delightful time on the Lake. Left the pier about 6. p.m. Took tea    &amp;  sandwiches and a jolly good thing we did. We intended returning at 8. p.m.  Dinner was ordered for six at Belgian" &ndash; when we set sail there was a great wind &amp; we went along beautifully. Got off &amp; had some nourishment</p>
