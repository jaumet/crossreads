
<p>MARCH &nbsp; FRIDAY 31 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>If in this life you wish to win</p>
<p>And rise above the common chump,</p>
<p>Take off your coat &amp; pitch right in,</p>
<p>Don&#39;t wait, lay hold, hang on, &amp; hump.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>It ain&#39;t no use to grumble &amp; complain</p>
<p>Its just as cheap &amp; easy to rejoice</p>
<p>When God sorts out the weather &amp; sends rain</p>
<p>Why, rain&#39;s my choice</p>
