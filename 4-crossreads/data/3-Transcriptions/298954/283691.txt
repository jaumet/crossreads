<p>The doctor is a most effeminate looking individual, wearing a frizzled white goatskin collar over his green uniform.</p>
<p>We spent a not too respectable but joyous time in the town &amp; we sailed home in the evening &ndash; I linked arm-in-arm to do the red-cockaded posten who called me "brother" and sang loudly songs of Vaterland and freedom.   I responded with the Hamburger "Holdrio, is geht nach Heimat", which was quite appropriate.   My red-cockaded "brother" begged me to throw my cap-badge into the gutter, or at any rate to remove the Crown &ndash; the "badge of servitude!"   I didn't.</p>
<p>Mon. 18th.<br/>My birthday.   Amused ourselves by snow-balling in the heavy snow.   Photos in the football field.</p>
<p>Tue. 19th</p>
<p>Farewell concert by the old stagers.</p>
<p>"The Price" a dramatic trifle from one of le Quesne's works.<br/>Rooney was excellent as the "heroine";  looked very distingue but his teeth quite spoiled him.   I was dresser &amp; "maker-up", "doing" Aird for his "Harry Lauder" turn, Limb for sentimental tenor songs &amp; the cockney duo for ragtime.</p>
