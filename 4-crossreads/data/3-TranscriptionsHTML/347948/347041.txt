<p>24th  5 a.m.  Still on table boat taking waves broadside on like an exaggerated see-saw.  Am sliding up &amp; down the table and waiting for the extra big one that is going to slide me off over the end on to the floor.  Second thoughts I get dressed.  Signaller Jones "C" Company died during the night with pneumonia.  Word was quietly passed round to hold the day in respect as much as possible. Service</p>
