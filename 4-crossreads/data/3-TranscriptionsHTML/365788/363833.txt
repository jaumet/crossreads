<p>2<br/>he was very good but very busy. He gave notice to all the troops that he would give prizes of &pound;4 &pound;3 &pound;2 &amp; &pound;1 for 4 descriptions of the destruction of Xmas Gifts. I wrote one &amp; I think did it rather well, &amp; I hear there were very few written, so am living in hopes. Am keeping young Robinsons address &amp; hope to look him up.<br/> I am sorry to hear Grahame Garland is coming poor Mrs Garland. Please remember me very Kindly to her, also to Miss Jean.<br/>If I meet Grahame will try &amp; get him some Cricket.<br/>No! did not see anything of Major Vernon. Will send you an Egyptian Gazette if we go to Egypt. I enclose another Copy of the Peninsular Press. Keep</p>
