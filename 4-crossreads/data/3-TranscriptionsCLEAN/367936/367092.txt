  
 Thursday 28 December 1916 Out on out post all day.  Very cold and windy with some rain. Did some helio work when sun was shining. More rain at night. 
 Friday 29 December 1916 Nothing much doing. Parcels mail in & got three.  Nice to get some good tucker.  Getting ready for stunt tomorrow. Posted letter home which I began on Christmas day. Rain at night.
