 Sunday 31st 
 Left Port Said early in the morning.  Steamed very slowly as we didn't want to get to Alexandria until Monday.  Beautiful fine day.  Full dress parade in the afternoon &amp; a lecture by the doctor.  All hustle getting ready for landing tomorrow.
