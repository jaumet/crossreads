  
 Sunday We had a somewhat troubled night.  We went to Bed about 10.30 & at 11 I heard the hum of an aeroplane & got up.  It was very low down & almost overhead.  The night was bright moonlight, full moon.  Suddenly I heard the Bombs fall & seven in quick succession fell round our Billet.  They were big bombs & shook our place very much.  During the early morning shells began to fall into the town & all day it has been very lively. Today is Easter Sunday.  I had a very good parade at the Billets.  The weather has been beautiful, clear & warm.
