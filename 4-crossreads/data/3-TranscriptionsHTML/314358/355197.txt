<p>24<br/>The broken frames of men.</p>
<p>Assistants round them are patiently carrying our orders with the same care and attention that nurses show to the sick</p>
<p>Shattered bones of steel are removed and replaced by new. Crumpled wounded parts of iron and brass are taken away and new parts put in place</p>
<p>Twisted joints are righted.  New wheels are put in place of the old and splintered</p>
<p>Adjustments are finally made during convalescence, and the brave old guns go back to France once more with the brilliance and paint and burnish of youth.</p>
<p>It was late in the evening when we embarked and drifted away from the wharf</p>
