<p>15 October 1918 Tuesday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Jerusalem   X Temp normal.  Feel off-   Had a glass of <strike>Champagne</strike> this morning &amp; good food- only a bit weak now X<br/>Hear Kaiser has abdicated &amp; all German troops gone back to Rhine is it true?<br/>Hope you won't worry when you see or hear I'm ill <strike>Really nothing</strike> &amp; soon OK   May cable if I go as far as Cairo.<br/>Jerusalem is full of prisoners &amp; captured GUNS &amp; war material   I saw some labelled 1st L.H.</p>
