 1919 May 28 2/6 per time &amp; were very slow.  Seeing that the dinner consisted of a cup of  tea, bread, meat, &amp; lettuce, I think they made good thing out of it.  It was the first time though that I had seen a clergyman increase his stipend by such means.  We landed at Hampton Court &amp; looked through the grounds, entered some of the rooms, &amp; saw the grape vine 100 or 1000 years old &ndash; I forget which, the tennis court in a room &amp; the Maze.
