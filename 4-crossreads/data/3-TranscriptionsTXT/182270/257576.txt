 SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 21, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; hot. Turned all my horses 
 into wheat paddock in morning 
 Tickle lopped in Top paddock &amp; 
 drew water for Darby. Al &amp; Jo 
 went to Coomoo Coomoo for 
 the day. I went into QuIrindi 
 to go on to Taree&nbsp;re some 
 grass for the cattle but found 
 there was no train, so came home again.
