<p>The American Sisters (Nurses) cannot be praised too highly - They love their work, and have much sympathy &amp; consideration for our sick &amp; wounded Anzacs, all of whom laud the hospital &amp; its staff to the utmost<br/>- The end of a day made perfect by French scenery &amp; seaside &ndash; American fraternity &amp; hospitality and sincere Australian Appreciation -<br/>A new acquaintance of importance is Bunny Southwick late of the "World" newspaper</p>
