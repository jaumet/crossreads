<p>I can see if I am in England much longer I'll come a gutser, for I am now meeting so many nice girls &amp; a man is only natural he must get trapped by one of them for instance the girl this afternoon quite took my fancy &amp; I am fool enough to think that the attraction was mutual, at any rate she was a splendid type of girl &amp; one that anyone could be proud of she is an only child, somehow or other I can't get her out of my mind she has stirred me as no other girl has ever done, she is not pretty far from it, but there is an undefinable something about her which seems to attract me like a magnet I suppose I am a fool to be thinking like this of her for I doubt if ever I shall see her again, but there</p>
