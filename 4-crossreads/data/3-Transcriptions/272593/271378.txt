<p>special pains were taken to get them up as well as possible   we even had "irons" brought over to smooth the hats &amp; tunic collars out, some Guard I can tell you   the Adjutant said they were the best Guard that has mounted since coming here,  Jock Mackie had charge of them  Jock made a bloomer or two where the "old" &amp; "new" Guard were changing, but it is a ticklish thing taking charge of a Guard, I am not looking forward with any great amount of pleasure to my turn at the job, the whole Battn, you might say looks on &amp; critizes while the Guards are changing.<br/>Went to the pictures tonight, they are quite close, they showed</p>
