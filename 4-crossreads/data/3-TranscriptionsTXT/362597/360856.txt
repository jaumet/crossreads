
Saturday 8.1.16 (17)It is a lovely day & we are looking forward to the final of the  sports. The sports were held & I lost my heat being beaten by Roy Farnsworth, the League Footballer. He eventually won the race. Ellis beat the gong & was disqualified in the sack race which was won by Capt Rickson, a Salvation Army Captain but a private with our unit. The Sergeants won the Tug-o-war.
