 2nd April 1914   For Easter Saturday Last   Good Friday Yesterday we marched out for many miles into the country, and bivouacked amidst a large grove of palm trees. Apart from sand, through which we had to march many miles the trip was a very enjoyable one, for in the evening the band came out and gave us a concert. It is funny to see the old ploughs, &amp; the primitive water wheels, for methods of cultivation have apparently not changed for  2000 yards  thousands of years.
