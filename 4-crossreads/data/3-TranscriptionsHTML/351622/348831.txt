<p>School. Jimmie left about 7 oclock. This has been a great day for me.</p>
<p>20 May 1916 Sat<br/>Jimmie came to see me from Erquinghem. A great meeting adjourning to the estaminet at 11 am &amp; stopping there till 7 p.m. Poor old Jim very broken up concerning the death of Frank. Several grogs all day &amp; Bell joins up with us after he had finished his work at the gas [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>21 May 1916 Sun<br/>Up at 5 am a most beautiful day. Started work with Cecil Davis in S. Ward. Cec has argument with [Brem] &amp; leaves to go on night duty. After dinner went into Steenwerck with Ken where we made our usual stay in the estaminet. Bottle of Cider. Arrived back early &amp; enjoyed a good bath &amp; a change of clothing.</p>
