<p>I was out with a dynamite fishing party this afternoon &amp; we got some great hauls of red pointer, blue striped garfish &amp; big spotted trevally &ndash; The Commander &ndash; Skeete - self &amp; a lot of us went &amp; had a grand time &ndash; Once inboard I got a note from Corporal Nunn &ndash; He'll take anything I like to Sydney &ndash; You'll get the big silver N.G coin Mab the small one &ndash; R. the triangular bandage &ndash; I've written an article to the M. Journal on our ptomaine stunt &ndash; A second that I've sent to Turner &ndash; on Xmas day in New Ireland &amp; on the natives. He passes it on to the Nat. Hist Soc of Brisbane &ndash; Its for them &ndash; I am going to wind this up here &ndash; Carry on as per usual &ndash; See that you get my pay 29&pound; p m &ndash; We maynt be home for months &ndash; All is quite well &amp; I'm most careful &ndash; We had fish &amp; a bit of steak for dinner &ndash; Fancy putting your teeth into fresh meat &ndash; Lovely -  Fred &ndash;</p>
