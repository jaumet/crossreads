  
 Monday 12 February 
 In morning went to Whiteleys and had a few Bus rides round Paddington.  Ride in tube from Charing Cross to Kings Cross.  In afternoon went to a performance at The Colliseum.  This is a magnificent Theatre of marble and about 24  dozen  boxes.  At 9.45 p.m. caught train to Berwick from Kings Cross. Passed York & Newcastle arrived Berwick just over the border at 8.30 a.m. (over).
