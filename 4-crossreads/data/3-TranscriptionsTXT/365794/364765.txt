
Thursday 27 December 1917Day full of changes of atmosphere, pt. duty & poor Adams (N.Z. pt.) gradually sinking, then off duty 2-5 & dressed in new frock went to "at home" in Sisters' quarters & enjoyed it immensely, talked to 2 Aust. & 1 N.Z. man & they were very nice, then ward 5 to 8 & pt. Adams died after a hard struggle for life, and 8 of his countrymen leave tomorrow for N.Z. where he will never return again.Rouse, Pilmer, Burnet, Cooper, Foster, Riddell, Hall & Curtis are our 8 off at 6.45 a.m. [indecipherable].
