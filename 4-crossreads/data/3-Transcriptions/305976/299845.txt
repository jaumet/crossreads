<p>Tuesday March 27<br/>Easter leave talk of the camp. Many N.S.W say they intend to make complaint on going to Sydney.<br/>Wednesday March 28th<br/>Sailing date postponed again until April 18th. We are fed up again. Nothing unusual here.<br/>Thursday March 29th<br/>More NSW reinforcements marched in to day. Saw George Steed &amp; Billy Williams. We informed them of our good home.?<br/>Friday March 30th<br/>Usual route march to day. Rained heavily coming home<br/>Distance about 18 miles<br/>Saturday March 31<br/>Did not go on leave.
