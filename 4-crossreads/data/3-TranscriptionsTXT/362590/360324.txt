 R.A.S.G. 
 6.2.16 My dear Father 
 I am glad you are enjoying your trip so well; &amp; when you come back I expect to hear great tales of men falling overboard, millions of cheering troops, exciting adventures in gold mines etc &ndash; especially from Dorothy. As I have nothing to the contrary, I conclude that you are all well, &amp; have no idea of returning yet a while.
