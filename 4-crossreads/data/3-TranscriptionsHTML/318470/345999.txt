<p>Sunday 30 January 1916<br/>Working "Pontoon" bridge on Canal.  Great preparation going on for supposed coming attack.  Nothing else of importance.</p>
<p>Monday 31 January 1916<br/>Working Pontoon bridge.  Very windy making handling bridge very difficult.</p>
<p>Tuesday 1 February 1916<br/>Working bridge as usual.  Nothing fresh reported.  7th, 8th Batts. crossed Canal today.</p>
<p>Wednesday 2 February 1916<br/>Working "Pontoon bridge".  A great deal of traffic crossed today.  Also much traffic on the Canal.</p>
<p>Thursday 3 February 1916<br/>Windy today making work on Pontoon bridge very hard.  Nothing of importance going that we have heard of.</p>
<p>Friday 4 February 1916<br/>Working on Pontoon bridge.  Windy making work difficult.  Not so much traffic as usual across Canal.</p>
<p>Saturday 5 February 1916<br/>Very windy today, work on bridge being very hard.  Heavy rain in evening &amp; early last night.</p>
<p>Sunday 6 February 1916<br/>Not much doing today.  Took walk down Canal for about a mile in the morning to view scene of last years attack by the Turks.</p>
<p>Monday 7 February 1916<br/>Had easy day at work today.  Nothing of importance going.  Beautiful day.  Working on "Pontoon bridge".</p>
<p>Tuesday 8 February 1916<br/>Usual work on Pontoon bridge.  No news of importance going.  A lot of shipping going through Canal.</p>
<p>Wednesday 9 February 1916<br/>Working on Pontoon bridge.  Nothing of importance reported.  Very heavy wind &amp; a little rain.  Wind made Bridge difficult to work.</p>
<p>Thursday 10 February 1916<br/>Usual bridge work this fine day.  Nothing of importance going.</p>
<p>Friday 11 February 1916<br/>Usual work on Pontoon bridge.  Nothing out of the usual reported.  Much preparation going on across the Canal.  Very windy.</p>
<p>Saturday 12 February 1916<br/>Working on Pontoon bridge as usual.  Bridging Train to take over tomorrow.</p>
