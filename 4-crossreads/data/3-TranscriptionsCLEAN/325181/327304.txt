  
 26th is a small palace, (showing pure white in the moonlight) & a few humble native residences while the foreground is composed of one beautiful  green  carpet of green herbage. 20th Batt arrived - 27th Usual parade. Saw Jack Curtis (20th) & he looks A1 Had surprise attack 5 to 7 Showers & then bed 28 Parade as usual. Put in good work Pay day today 200 piastres. Expect leave for front shortly Washed some clothes. Had good shower 29th Parade 6 to 7 then full kit inspection Half holiday. Visited Zoological gardens. Simply beautiful. Well laid out far before Sydney Zoo. Tiled walks
