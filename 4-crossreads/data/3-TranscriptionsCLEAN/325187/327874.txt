Mch 19th & gumleaf  Mrs Cotton April 17th  Mrs Mack Mch 28th ISH. Glad to hear etc: Alls Well Laura April 12 Norman Turner re Sheep Skins Note Day is sending me 2 Skins- many thanks but do not spend your  S.D. Glad Joe met Stead I very seldom get the Papers. Am glad the old Paters grave is kept so green Soks & Handky from Maurie Phillips please thank.
