<p>Returned back to other section 5.30 p.m. &amp; then comforts issued.</p>
<p>18 February 1916 Fri<br/>B Section Struck tents at 7.30 &amp; with the waggons loaded &amp; packs up moved off across the desert. Arriving at destination practice tent &ndash; pitching. Afterwards underwent field operations of bringing in wounded &amp; treatment of wounds. My job in the operation theatre. Lectures by Captain Savage &amp; inspection by colonel.</p>
<p>[Note in margin]<br/>Ali Hassan shots a doubler in theatre.</p>
<p>19 February 1916 Sat<br/>Nothing happened out of the ordinary save eight hours picquet. Hair parade by Captain Shannessy which resulted in Steve cutting mine. Vickery leaves suffering with mumps. Ali Hassan plunges to-night spending 15 piasteres in biscuits. A good old sing song to-night dont some of the old songs bring back fond memories. Wont a man like talking about these days after over.</p>
