<p>turned up <strike>this morning</strike> safe and sound this morning and gave an interesting account of how he managed to hit Dieppe accidentally and helped <strike>the</strike> a French lady in that fine city sweep <strike>up</strike> out a drain pipe and push a perambulator for some other woman.  Having done both those jobs to his own satisfaction and, I have no doubt, to the satisfaction of the good dames themselves he proceeded to help some French yokels to load a cart with wheat or some other grain.  The Yokels finding assistance so ready and cheap left him to do the lot on his own or as much as, for he avers that they did the grunting</p>
