<p>19 September, Friday</p>
<p>Work late. Go to visit Julie but no luck.</p>
<p>20 September, Saturday</p>
<p>Feeling best ever. Cut grass &amp; dig in garden. Go to theatre with Mum. Very good although I nearly fight with Brooky mob.</p>
<p>Go Dobbees first.</p>
