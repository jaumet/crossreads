<p>In proportion to the number of medicoes registered, I am told Australia has the record, of supplying most followers of the ancient art.</p>
<p>The Imperial custom is for M.Os. to sign on for 12 months<br/>With the A.I.F. the contract is &ndash; when the job is over or three months longer if required.<br/>Davies &amp; Connington's almanac for 1917 had better be sent to my present address.<br/>Our surburban allotment at Anzac was extended a while ago, to a homestead selection with <u>full time</u> residential conditions &amp; no allowance for improvements but, [dash]. Are we downhearted? NO!</p>
<p>We had a fall of snow 5 days ago &amp; the last of it is just disappearing.<br/>It was that cold we could not sleep &amp; had to get up &amp; mark time of a night.<br/>Those artists who paint landscape snow-scenes, never include chilblains, wet feet &amp; rheumatism on the same canvas.</p>
