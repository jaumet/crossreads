<p>Sunday  -  Sept 13  - 14</p>
<p>Mail goes off 4pm to  - day by the Melbourne  -  Flag ship sent that message round the fleet last eveg  -  The whole Australian Fleet is here &amp; we look very fine &amp; large  -  It's a little like comic opera our overwhelming force &amp; these defenceless places  -  However its war &amp; got to be done  -  Thank heaven its mainly bloodless  -  Poor Pockley will feel the loss of his boy  -  Mab &amp; I shook hands with him at that Congress in Sydney &ndash; at the close of the ball in the Town Hall  -  I was up last night looking after a Chinaman &ndash; one of the crew of the captured Sumatra  -  His guard made me laugh  -  The guard was an immense man &amp; the Chinaman very small &amp; inoffensive looking &ndash; guard had loaded rifle &ndash; fixed bayonet &amp; was in full marching order with contraptions hanging all over him  -  I had to consider the question of chloroform &amp; an operation by a tallow candle  -  Eventually I sent guard &amp; all over to one of the war ships with a note to the doctor  -  Its raining  -  No leave of course  -  Ribaul is occupied  -  We saw the troops going ashore a bloodless occupation  -</p>
