<p>up five camels  fed up had Breakfast all ready now to move  some of our wounded are coming in  I feel OK now after a good sleep nice and warm now  issued with more rations the Country is terrible rough here all rocky hills  took camels to graze  had tea on guard to night  stood to 2 A.M  raining heavy<br/>Saturday March 30.3.18<br/>the Place would Bog a duck  raining very heavy I am all wet and mud  dinner 12 took camels to graze very cold and showery they are still fighting here  had tea turned in<br/>Sunday March 31.3.18<br/>turned out 5.A.M.  Packed up rest of the Coy are Back  left here 7.30 went two miles had Breakfast moved off again 9.  very slow</p>
