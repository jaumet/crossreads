<p>August, 1917. Thursday 16<br/>Fine day some clouds. S.M. only allowed us half day off. In time I washed equipment very thoroughly. In aftn. L.C.'s &amp; 2nd C under Beck for drill. Tried to smooge. Very slow work on elementary rot. In evening played cards. Feel tired.</p>
