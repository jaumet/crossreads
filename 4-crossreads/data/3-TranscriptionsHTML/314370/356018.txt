<p>April, 1916<br/>Thursday 27<br/>After another day on the Harbor &amp; a beautiful swim &amp; a couple of good meals we caught the 6 pm train for Cairo.</p>
<p>Friday 28<br/>Had a lazy day &amp; in the evening went out to Choubra w/- my chum who was discharged from Hosp today.</p>
