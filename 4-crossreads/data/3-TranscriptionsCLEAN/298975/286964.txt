
Oct 1917Tuesday 9thPacked the our Kit Bags this morning & put out in readiness for our move on Thursday . We are doing practicaly nothing these days just putting in time
Wednesday 10thSwinging the Lead very sucessfully today Put on to load Kit bags in morning finished at 10 Fell out after dinner and stayed in hut all afternoon
Thursday  11thRevalie 5.30 Breackfast 6 Drew dry rations 2 pieces bread each 1 tin salmon to 5 men Fell in stood about & all the usual performance At 8:50 about 1,000 statuard Ausies marched
