<p>Now we are to go up the line.</p>
<p>Everybody is packing up, and a new excitement is abroad. We shall discontinue the classes after tonight, because some of us will go up tomorrow afternoon. Plenty of hard work is required to outline some sort of scheme for continuing the classes up the line. Correspondence will meet the difficulty in the main.
