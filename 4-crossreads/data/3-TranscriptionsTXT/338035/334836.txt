 He had gone thro' Gallipoli as Staff Sergt. in A.S.C. and joined the Infantry as a Lieut. (13th Batt.) only a month before his death.  In cricket baseball &amp; other sporting bodies he was always interested and was Secry. of Centennial Park Cricket &amp; Baseball Association for many years. 
 Monday 13th A very busy day.  The whole Third Div. practised marching out.  We had our wagons all packed yesterday so at 11.30 we fell in (full kit) and moved off.  Then a long march commenced.  To Amesbury we came  thro  up past Stonehenge and on to Winterbourne Stoke, Shrewton and back  to  thro' Rollestone to Camp arriving at  7  6.15 p.m. 
 W. Pearson
