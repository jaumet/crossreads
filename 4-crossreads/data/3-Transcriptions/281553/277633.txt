<p>June 20<br/>Church Parade 6-15 a.m. got Pass &amp; left for Cairo 10-30 with 4 others.  Spoke to 1st woman since 12/5/15 at Resturant.  Visited Pyramids after dinner, got back Cairo 6-30 p.m., after tea had a look round city getting to camp 10 p.m.</p>
<p>June 21<br/>Coy. on Duty.  Cpl. &amp; 6 men to Guard Nurses Home.  Ibraheim [Ibrahim] Palace.  Mounted 10 a.m.  Visited Heliopolis shopping 5 p.m. to 6.</p>
<p>June 22<br/>Took films to be developed &amp; printed 9-30.  Visited by Capt. Kirke, all well.</p>
