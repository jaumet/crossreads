<p>with aunt &amp;amp: D.W.A. next catching train at Euston for Glasgow at 11.30. Carriage not crowded so had a good sleep going up.</p>
<p>Sunday 2nd March  Reached Glasgow about 10 am &amp; received the usual welcome from the Harper family, it was like getting home.  Spent the day indoors &amp; yarning with friends at night.</p>
<p>Monday 3rd March  Weather fine but cold.  A long walk with Mr. Harper in morning &amp; pictures with Elsie during afternoon.  Had trouble getting in people being simply mad on pictures up this way.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4th March  Foggy and cold.  Visited the city in afternoon with Elsie and bought a couple of things for home also chose a book thy wanted to give me for a gift.  Afternoon tea at Craig's and then home.  In the evening</p>
