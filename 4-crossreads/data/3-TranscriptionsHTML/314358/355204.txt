<p>5<br/>Who has to face it, when he suffers is known as 'shell shock'</p>
<p>The strongest and bravest of men can be so mentally terrorized and nerve shaken that all the powers he has for the minds control are useless and vain</p>
<p>Mans intellect has made for him such terrible machinery and missels that long before he meets his enemy in battle &ndash; before he is even in sight of him &ndash; he has to  face and crave the most terrific forces of natures cruel and unrelenting powers harnessed and loosened by his cunning to wreck and ruin and to slay.</p>
<p>This, of course, applies to both armies in contest</p>
