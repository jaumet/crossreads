Nov 15/17 The Chateau, Fauconberguearrived here last night & are stopping here a few days. The Chateau is a very fine place but the owners are away in Paris & it is given over to a H.Q. Billets when troops are moving through. We have got our horses tied up practically on the front lawn
The Country is now quite different to flat Flanders it is hilly with running streams.
