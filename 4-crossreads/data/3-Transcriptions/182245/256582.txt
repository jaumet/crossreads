
<p>301</p>
<p>MARCH 1917</p>
<p>8 THURSDAY</p>
<p>Snowing most of the day</p>
<p>with a very cold wind</p>
<p>blowing. I was on stableman</p>
<p>all day. Hear the Germans</p>
<p>put a few shells into</p>
<p>the factory near the baths</p>
<p>in Point de Neippe &amp; killed</p>
<p>a few civies. Heard from Poss Moison</p>
