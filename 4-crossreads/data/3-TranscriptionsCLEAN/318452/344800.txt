 Saturday Dec 9th The past 2 days have been very cold but clear & light. Sharp white frosts which lie unmelted all day Today is warm & beautiful. Last night it rained again The news yesterday was not too good We evacuated Bouston Wood & have to withdraw & straighten our line in the Cambrai Salient
