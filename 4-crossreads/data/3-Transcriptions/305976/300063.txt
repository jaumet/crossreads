<p>&amp; down the line to the base camp.<br/>Friday Oct 11<br/>We have heard the good news to day. The Germans in France are being badly beaten &amp; hostilities are likely to cease at any time.<br/>There is some kind of Plague in Amman. It is estimated 40 are dying daily. It is a big percentage for a small village.<br/>Saturday Oct 12<br/>We are losing men every day going to Hospital with Malaria. We opened this station with 12 of us &amp; O.C. Today there is only 3 of us &amp; O.C. Our duties are heavy. Every day we are up at 4 AM &amp; generally finish about 11pm We are getting more patients</p>
