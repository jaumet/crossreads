<p>From Capt M M Sinclair<br/>c/o Bank of North Q'land<br/>4 Queen Victoria St<br/>London EC</p>
<p>No 3 Australian Hospital<br/>Brighton 9/Jan/17</p>
<p>Dear Mr Ferguson</p>
<p>I was glad to get your letter last week &amp; to hear you were all well. You seem to have been having a lively time in Australia, with politics &amp; coal strikes &amp; what not &ndash; we were all sorry the vote was for "No" conscription but all the same fee the men will come along yet. Australia has done very well so far &amp; must keep it up.</p>
<p>I am told the 3rd Australian Division which left Salisbury Plains for the front a short time ago is one of the finest &amp; smartest divisions ever put into the</p>
