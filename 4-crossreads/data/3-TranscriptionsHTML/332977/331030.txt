<p>APRIL 23<br/>Sunday we go to church</p>
<p>25 &ndash; 25 Anzac Day<br/>we have lovely sports and concert in Hospital Ground. Receive cigarette case from Red Cross fund boarded by colonel for Australia</p>
<p>May 1 st<br/>still in Hospt. large body of wounded men arrive from England, on there way to Australia, British Yeomanry lose 400 dead 7 mile out Austrians and Germans surround them and shoot their horses,160 ladies captured 40 suffering from V.D.</p>
<p>May 5th<br/>Afternoon off to go to Anzac Hostel had a look around city</p>
<p>May 3rd<br/>8 German Aeroplanes attack Suez drop 40 Bombs, try and wreck wharf 140 killed 230 wounded</p>
<p>10th<br/>all Light Horse leaving for Palestine &amp; Syria, Infantry at prison</p>
<p>13<br/>Transferred to Luna Park, Hospt<br/>16<br/>Concert given by the Countess Jasinsky in Dining Hall lad dies with heat</p>
