From the zoo, we hired an aged heathen & his boat & continued our journey towards Cairo, which we reached after much hard work on the boatman's part. I would have given the old dog a spell, but on second thoughts came to the conclusion that a little work now & then would not hurt him. I am led to believe that these natives have become utterly spoiled since the Australians came to Egypt, & I don't doubt it the average Australian soldier believes that money was made to spend and acts accordingly.
