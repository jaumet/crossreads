
Sunday 2 September 1917On Q.M. Fatigue (water) and saw Keith Slade & Russel Gilbraith.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 2].
Monday 3 September 1917Firing in morning & exercise in evening.
Tuesday 4 September 1917Exercises & shooting, on Mess duty.
Wednesday 5 September 1917Nothing doing:  on picquet at night.
Thursday 6 September 1917Quiet day.  Good concert at Y.M.C.A., best ever held this side of the Canal.
Friday 7 September 1917On beach guard, got books from Mrs. Walker & Reg letters from Jess.
Saturday 8 September 1917Nothing much doing, good concert by the Welsh Choir at Y.M.C.A. at night.
