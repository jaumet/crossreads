1916   FRIDAY 28   JULY
Fine & warm. Mother & Alan & Self had our photos taken together, then Alan went off to see some mates. Mother & I went for a walk round town. Alan brought a couple of Officers to dinner  Went for a run round in car after dinner & then on to Auntie Florence's for tea & I caught the 10 minutes to 6 train & got back to camp about 8.30 pm & found half the brigade gone away to different instruction schools.
