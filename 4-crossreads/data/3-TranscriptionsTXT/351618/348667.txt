I had to take refuge on my returning as the shelling was very free. I decided to take teas with Olly G and went the sun set. I started on my return to our Headquarters. The sap through which we generally travelled was full up of water &ndash; the result of last night's storm &ndash; and it meant the road in the open had to be traversed.
