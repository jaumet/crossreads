  
 Feb. 10 Wed. Worked till  11 p.m.  11 a.m. in office - then went off to Cairo on leave - about an hour's run in tram & only costs 1 pastre. Walked through Ezbekieh Gardens & visited British Soldiers Cafe, Roller skating rink, Head fire station & Greek Boy Scout Headquarters, very smart lads - saw & spoke to a B-P Scout - Had haircut & visited Dentist- teeth alright - Arrived back at Mena at 7.15 p.m.
