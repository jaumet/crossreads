<p>Friday 23.3.17<br/>Quite a demonstration in the town to-day; full of officials &amp; troops; getting busier each day; 5th Brigade band played in town.</p>
<p>Saturday 24.3.17<br/>A number of sick coming through but very few wounded.  Two of the old buildings blown up to make way for roads.  Most of craters filled in &amp; traffic running  as usual.  Still no news of relief.</p>
