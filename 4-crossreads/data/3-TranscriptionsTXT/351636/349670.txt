 The Centre Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers 28/10/15 &ndash; 
 Dear Mr. Layton &ndash; Do you think you could give Mrs. Hilder some help today? She has been to you before &amp; so feels shy about troubling you again. She seems badly in need of assistance. her children have no clothes &amp; not enough to eat. 
 I am going to make inquiries about the separation allowance from Liverpool 
 Yrs sincerely E. M. Wallack One child is to go to Hospital. A Doctor says she is ill from want of food
