Thursday 26.7.17Played 31st Btn Cricket & won by 6 wickets & 1 run.  Rugby team also beat 1st F.Amb at League by 14 to 9, this is the first time the 1st F Amb have been defeated.
Friday 27.7.17Detailed in charge of Guard to proceed to Aveluy siding to guard unit gear until unit entrains.  8th Btn & 1st Pioneers entrained for North.  Met Cec Stoddart & Stan Haggerty
Saturday 28.7.17Plenty of life about the siding with all the Anzacs entraining.  Second Division entrained.  Day very hot, spent most of the day near the lake. At night
