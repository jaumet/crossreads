
Sunday 11 March 1917 [10 March 1918]S. Peile ill so I take Connaught on for the day.  C. Grant claps his hands softly when he finds we are running it together.  Church 11 a.m. & very good address "respect our Mothers".1/2 day & went Zoo with 7 Aust. in C. Garland's charge."Frank" Blundell of the party - this man interests me slightly being older than the average soldier, & has travelled a lot having left home, England, at 16 years & has lived in Aust. (S) a good deal since.
