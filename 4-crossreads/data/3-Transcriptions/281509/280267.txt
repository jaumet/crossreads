<p>14 February 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>A day of surprises &amp; unfortunate happenings.<br/>Strike by soldiers at Casula &amp; Liverpool for shorter hours of drill.<br/>Procession from Casula  after breakfast to Liverpool. Broke up hotels &amp; cleared out the drink.<br/>Lighthorsemen came out after dinner. Grave position &amp; [indecipherable] disgrace to the Army.<br/>Thousands go to Sydeny &amp; play havoc there. <br/>Tony, Bob &amp; self into Liverpool to view damage.  Meet Bob Abernethy. Concert at Methodist Tent. Hotels wrecked &amp; refreshment [indecipherable]</p>
