<p>8.11.18</p>
<p>Dear Judge,<br/>Your very meaty letter of 7 Aug. has lobbed. I suppose you contracted your habit of writing as small as possible in the days when you were saving paper for the purpose of helping that struggling family the Fairfaxes to earn their modest livelihood. Nowadays it is of course a duty not to waste paper but I am glad that I have been able to keep a pair of glasses unbroken.</p>
