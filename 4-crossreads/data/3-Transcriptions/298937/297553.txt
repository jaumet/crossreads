<p>are advancing in the Dvinsk [?] region as well as in the Caucasus.<br/>Nothing has turned up in regard to my promotion, &amp; although the doctor seems satisfied with me, I get absolutely no privileges in respect of my work.<br/>I hear that several Aust. units have left Egypt, &amp; taken with them a couple of hospitals.  I want to get back to them now:  for there seems no hope of promotion here.<br/>I am sadly disappointed in my progress since I became a soldier.<br/>I have worked hard &amp; intelligently here on responsible work, but nothing seems to come of it,</p>
