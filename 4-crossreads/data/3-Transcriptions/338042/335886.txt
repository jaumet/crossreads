<p>June 3rd<br/>Major portion of day spent in pick &amp; shovel work &ndash; To comply with Army Orders we excavate interior of bell tents to escape probable destruction from enemy bombs &ndash; News of Harold Williams &ndash; "The Pemberton Billing" case causes much controversy amongst the troopers who are interested.<br/>June 4th<br/>Feeling rather seedy &ndash; Rehearsal &ndash; Having obtained a new Cook we again commence our Mess</p>
