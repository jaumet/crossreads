<p>1916 &nbsp; MONDAY 18 &nbsp; SEPT.</p>
<p>Raining in morning but fine in afternoon but very cold. With the director class all the morning &amp; M<sup>r</sup>&nbsp;Neylan gave us a lecture in the afternoon on gun pits ect. The 27<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;beat the 25<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;battery by 6-0 at football in evening.</p>
