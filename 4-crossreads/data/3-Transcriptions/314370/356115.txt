<p>December, 1916<br/>Sunday 3<br/>Church parade in Canteen<br/>Parson was a huge, fat man who did not seem to suffer by England's food shortage.<br/>Played football against Tommies<br/>They won.</p>
<p>Monday 4<br/>Very cold again<br/>Am feeling very tired after yesterdays march<br/>Receive letters from Mum &amp; rich</p>
