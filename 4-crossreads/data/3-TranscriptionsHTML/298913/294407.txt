<p>news from Trickett who cannot keep his face closed.</p>
<p>16/8/16<br/>Brown arrives 4pm terribly hot day &amp; many flies.</p>
<p>17/8/16<br/>Heat like a furnace. Taube visits us &amp; hovers to see how many men will leave the Hod in a hurry but is dissappointed with the result.</p>
<p>18/8/16<br/>Inoculated against cholera which has caught a bit of a hold on the troops here. Water condemned for drinking purposes. Build a neat little dugout to sleep in.</p>
<p>19/8/16<br/>Capt. Downing arrives with Brown's station who relieve us. 9am leave for Romani  Travel all night to Bir Negligeb where we rest for 1 hour &amp; meet WA5 station. 3am both leave again &amp; arrive at Romani</p>
<p>20/8/16<br/>8am.Pass half buried Turks &amp; great craters &amp; junks of shells two inches thick. Have a great sleep</p>
<p>21/8/16<br/>Lie about all day doing nothing</p>
<p>22/8/16<br/>do ditto today. Receive mail from home. Celebrate the anniversary of my enlistment.</p>
<p>23/8/16<br/>Glorious loafing &amp; great  food. Inoculated again against cholera. From our position which is high up we can just see the ocean &amp; all over Romani. Having great time so far but seems too good to last.</p>
<p>24/8/16<br/>Nothing but sleep all day.</p>
<p>26/8/16<br/>Go to Mohammidiya for swim &amp; have to walk home owing to trucks off the railway. Get some what sunburnt. Big mail does not turn up as expected, NSW portion gone on to Endamid</p>
<p>27/8/16<br/>Nothing doing at all-</p>
<p>28/8/16<br/>Nil</p>
<p>29/8/16<br/>Two aeroplanes visit us &amp; drop 100lb bombs that are snifters. Fired at by anti-aircraft guns. Wind of this had been received by our spies &amp; we were ready for the attack although we expected a bigger one. Taube gets away as usual</p>
<p>29/8/16 (Note duplication)<br/>All quiet again</p>
<p>30/8/16<br/>Go to Kantara to get kits at NZ Transport Park  Sturman tears my head off for going above his head. Getting few letters now. Have to sleep Kantara Rwy station over night</p>
<p>31/8/16<br/>Arrive Romani 8am with gear</p>
