<p>February, 1916<br/>Tuesday 15<br/>First thing exam in care &amp; cleaning &amp; I have pleasure in stating my class had highest average.<br/>Have tea w/- Dad, evening cold &amp; inclined to rain.</p>
<p>Wednesday 16<br/>Work as usual &amp; at night I visit the Casino Kursaal.  The "Revue" is absolutely the prettiest thing I have yet seen staged, the music &amp; costumes &amp; really fine &amp; altogether a show worth seeing.<br/>Turn in at 1 am</p>
