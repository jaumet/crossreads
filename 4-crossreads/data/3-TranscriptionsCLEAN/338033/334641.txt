Cattle and horses areAll stabled and tethered when feeding in the fields.There are no dividing fences or hedges, and I can't understand how they manage to keep other pheasants from gradually taking over their property. Every crop is rolled after being sown and ground is well worked after each crop. All farm implements are kept in the village and carried out on iron frames upon wheels, whenever needed.
