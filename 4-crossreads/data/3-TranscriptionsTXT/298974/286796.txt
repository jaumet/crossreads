 [postcard - apparently censored] 
 A Fry Esq. 
 Northcote Road. Lindfield. N.S. Wales Australia 
 Sund 19th Nov. 
 Dear Father 
 My word its cold here. The water is frozen all day sometimes. There is hail &amp; sleet. It snowed on the hills nearby but not here yet. This is [censored] 
 We wont be here many days &amp; will then go into the line. Tommy came &amp; spent the evening with me before leaving England. Am very well. Love. Dene.
