Rot!! "Gmynastics" to-night. Had gloves on with Jack Clark  Good time. &ndash; Sea gone down to heavy swell and ships rolled all night. I have no hammock and am sleeping on the troopdeck so which ever way the ship rolled I rolled too Nov. 4th Heavy. Swell. Dull day,  Dead horse thrown overboard from the "Shropshire"  Boxing tournament to-day at 2.30. It was a bit of a freeze. Too many forfeits  Bob Mayman had a victory.  A very close night. Nov. 5th Glorious morning  I am up on winch deck enjoying morning Sun while the rest are drilling We are in the tropics now and are beginning to feel the Sun The food was pretty bad to-day although it has been improving.
