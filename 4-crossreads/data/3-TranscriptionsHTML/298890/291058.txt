<p>22. Sunday<br/>Miss Smith &amp; I at home.  Many Indians.  Ethel &amp; K. Adams. F. Post. Mr. &amp; Mrs. Carpenter &amp; others.  Wet dull day.</p>
<p>23. Monday<br/>Worked in office &amp; went home to Miss Hodge at night.  Miss Wren had nice dinner waiting.</p>
<p>24. Tuesday<br/>Worked in office &amp; went home to Miss Hodge.</p>
<p>25. Wednesday<br/>Lovely frosty morning but grey later.  Dinner with Miss H &amp; Miss W.  Supper alone &amp; went to bed.</p>
