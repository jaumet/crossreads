 Wed. 28-6-'16. sought shelter in our Dug-out until Bombardment ceased. We then slept soundly Thanks be: Thurs. 29-6-'16. 50 Casualties last night &amp; many suffering from Shock. Lt Paynton killed cruelly. Both Hips broken &amp; Spine inf. 5-6-'16 (see Pay book for Etaples) To-night another heavy bombardment same as last night. Felt easier to-night. Moustache vs smoking scheme Frid. 30-6-'16. 10 p.m. Scrty Gen, C.R.A. &amp; self mounted as all night patrol. Heavy bombardment going on. Saw a good many shells close enough in all.
