  
 3 July, Thursday 
 I am outed at 3rd round of Crib Tourney. 
 F. A gale blowing & sea is very angry. I nearly go without brekkie but I am too hungry. 
 Shave & can't find a place to work. I go to English lesson on greatness of English Language. Walk on promenade with Jack Craven. Go to bed in hammock. Big swell on boat rolling fiercely. Write & read & go to bed next to table at 11 pm. 
 4 July, Friday 
 F, 
 Feeling very onkers until Tea-time. Pushed about all morning. Cadge at trousers for packs. No Book-keeping. Listen to lecture on Light Horse to Gaza. Play Bridge Tourney.  Win . Go up starirs early. Cadge Trous, have supper. Wash tops in bath. Turn in about minuint. 
 Sea fairly smooth.
