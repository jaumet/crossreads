 Dearest Eddy 
 Yr lovely card  &amp;  dear   Mummies just come Thank dear Grandma, Aunt Isabel, Ethel   &amp;  Gae for their cards   &amp;  tell them I shall write next mail. It is very cold here   [indecipherable] I have a cold &ndash; in bed yesterday with fire in my room so poor Mrs Barrie will   have to come to see dear Gae again soon  &amp;  get in the sun! Fond love to you   &amp;  Grandma   Mummie   &amp;  Auntie - 
 4 Morrab Place Penzance 
 From W. Loving Friend C. Barrie
