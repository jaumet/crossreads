<p>14-1-15</p>
<p>funeral.  There was no lamp practice to-night &amp; an enterprising cinematograph firm has built a large tent here capable of holding practically the whole camp &amp; it was opened to-night for the first time.  the house was filled right up &amp; it should do well as they charge only 1&frac12;  PT to go in;  The pictures were not as good as Spencers, but not too bad considering we are here on active service;  Stan &amp; Mills put in A class</p>
<p>Thur 14th<br/>Sig as usual in the same old spot.  McGregor &amp; Pope has been put out of the signallers &amp; Cabban &amp; Geo. Hitching has been put in, in their place &amp; are starting off scratch.  Stan received a pass &amp; went in to Cairo to</p>
