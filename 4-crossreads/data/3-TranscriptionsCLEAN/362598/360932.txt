
Sunday 2 July 1916Wrote Home (49).  Spent a quiet evening outside our tent listening to Phonograph.  Night Matron again falls to my lot.  Hooted out of Bed at 10.45 p.m. to go to Billy's Ward.
Monday 3 July 1916Ward Master all day.  Wrote Cards to Mem, Edie Tory, Ada Cohen, I. Westaway, Jess Mather.
Tuesday 4 July 1916Bill, Dave & I go to Cairo for a run.  Visit all the Popular places.
Wednesday 5 July 1916Ellis, Bill & I go to Heliopolis to meet Dick Rowe.  Also meet McKenzie, Dinkum Soldier McKenzie.  All Patients come inside the Ward.  A kid of 5 talks French & English with equal ease.
Thursday 6 July 1916Everything as usual, had a very lazy day.  Lay in bed at evening & thought of Home & Friends.
Friday 7 July 1916Late dinner day.  Wrote Harry Taubman & Edie Taubman.  Tried hard to rain.  Staff gives a concert in Y.M.C.A. Hut.  Very hot day & Earn malingers.
Saturday 8 July 1916The 4 Jews go out to Y.M.C.A.  I met Roy Ingram.  Ellis wins 3 games Billiards.  Dave & I beat Billy & Ellis 105 to 50.  We dine at Y.M.C.A. & have a sing-song with McKenzie from Manly.  Went to Anzac Hotel for Tea & concert afterwards.
