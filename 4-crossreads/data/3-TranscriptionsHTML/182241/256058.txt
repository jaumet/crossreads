<p>Jan 13th<br />The rain came down in torrents all night &amp; has continued to day accompanied by heavy lightening The Bdge in Bivouacs (Bivvys) were washed out &amp; are having a miserble time. We are now attached to desert Coys again</p>
<p>Jan 14th<br />Heavy rains &amp; thunderstorms continued through the night but cleared into glorious sun shine during the morning Cpt H___&nbsp;secured two rooms to day for <s>the use of del</s>&nbsp;his photographic requirements close to hand &amp;&nbsp;we spent part of the day fitting them up The new workrooms are part of a house occupied by a Yiddish family&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
