The day after we reached Ferry Post we crossed the canal and went to an Ordnance Store and change our rifles went then went on to Moascar on the seventh of March and we stayed there till the Seventeenth. We left in open trucks at midnight. 
 We arrived at Alexandria about midday the next day, and embarked, on the Ascania (Cunard Line) but did not sail till the next day.
