<p>Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>France<br/>27 Aug. 18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>It is just about time for another letter to you so as I have a bit of time on my hands I will at least get a start on.  One of the boys is expecting to go on Blighty leave soon so I will most likely give it to him to post over there.  We have just had <strike>a pretty</strike> another pretty stiff week in the line.  I say another as we have had a good many of late down</p>
