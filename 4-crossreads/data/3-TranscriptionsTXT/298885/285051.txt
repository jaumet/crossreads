Wed. Mar. 1st -16Usual routine things very slow.
Thur. Mar. 2nd - 16Horses stampede causing great excitement.
Fri. Mar. 3rd -16Drivers put through test with hurdle.
Sat. Mar. 4th -16Had an enjoyable evening at YMCA Cairo
Tues Mar 7th - 16All reinforcements warned at 6 P.M. to be ready to leave next day for Tel-el-kiber.
Wed. Mar. 8th -16Left Zeitoun for Tel-el-kiber and every company
