<p>SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 22, 1919.</p>
<p>Had a storm in the afternoon</p>
<p>&amp; had 60 points at Tickles.</p>
<p>Lopping in Top paddock and</p>
<p>cut a salt trough for the</p>
<p>Middle paddock. Tickle cut</p>
<p>his arm badly &amp; had to go in</p>
<p>to the doctor &amp; had 9 stitches</p>
<p>put in it.</p>
