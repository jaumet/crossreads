<p>2pm the sea had a big swell on &amp; owing to the Afric being well down she pitches somewhat but nothing out of the ordinary.8/1/16 during the morning great amusement was provided by the men compelling those with long hair to have it cut off. Forcible haircutting certainly is funny &amp; those who refuse to come up &amp; have it done have two straight grooves cut across their head like a hot cross bun &amp; were left thus for a couple of days. Others submit themselves &amp; are let go with a solitary curl or wisp hanging from one side, not knowing it is there for some time.<br/>The sea  by now had risen somewhat &amp; at 3pm a heavy sea was running, into which the Afric plunged at times sending showers of spray over the decks. The wind too was very stiff &amp; cold. Some amusement was caused by the <strike> gulls</strike> albatross that hung over the poop. Occasionally a gust of wind would catch them whilst on the wing &amp; almost upset them, to the amusement of the onlookers. During the afternoon the Queensland  "Fatty" took sick &amp; all he could say was For Heavens</p>
