<p>Thursday 12th April 1917<br/>Building a hut for John and self, with empty ammunition boxes. Kept going till about 6pm. Got it very comfortable, plenty of hay on the floor and have a good fire inside so we are A, hut about 8x6<br/>Australians were the first to enter Bapaume. In the small hours of St Patricks day, Australian patrols found that the German were retiring from the trenches north of Bapaume and by 6 o'clock N.S.W.and Victorian troops were well out into the country behind the German lines.2 hrs.
