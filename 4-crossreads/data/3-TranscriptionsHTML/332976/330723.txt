<p>October 1916<br/>our troops &amp; we are issued with goggles for protection against it.<br/>We passed the German prisoners hospital on our way &amp; also a few prisoners being marched into camp.<br/>13th  We had another long route march to-day from the camp, (which was changed last night to a new camp about 500 yards away) to Paris Plarge which is on the sea shore about 3 &frac12; miles from Etaples, &amp; <strike>which is</strike> connected to Etaples by train.  I took a party to the Bull Ring at 6 P.M. &amp; returned at 11.30 PM.</p>
