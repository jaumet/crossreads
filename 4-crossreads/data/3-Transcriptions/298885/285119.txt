<p>1917<br/>Jan 6th<br/>This morning we were ordered back to DAHQ which was a great relief for us as we leaving the "Chamber of Horrors" behind.</p>
<p>Here at DAHQ it is almost in comparison with our last possie a "Home" away from Home although the mud is equally as bad, we have duckboards to walk on from one door to another</p>
<p>Jan 9th &ndash; 17<br/>Experienced a heavy supply of Fritz's high velocity shells which were falling in all directions</p>
