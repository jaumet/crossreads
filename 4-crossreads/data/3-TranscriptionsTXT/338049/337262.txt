 Thursday 21 Feb. Pay at 10 am G. W. Stanbury. 
 Visited joint GR &amp; bury. Returned via BN &amp; fixed up re gas doors being done by Spr Worthington. 
 Afternoon out to joint in paddock. Obtained pump but finally fished out cable and jointed. 
 Fine day. Evening bit crook &amp; had Alex Lilburn down for an occasion.
