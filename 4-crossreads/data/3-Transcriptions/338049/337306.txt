<p>Saturday 6 April<br/>Parade 7.45 &amp; marched off to [East?] Camp. Issued dinner &amp; tea tickets.</p>
<p>Marched Fontinettes Station drew dinner &amp; tea after great push boarded train thro. window. Left about 12 &amp; went via Boulogne Abbeville to Canaples. 6.30 p.m. Marched in rain to Corps H.Q. well lost &amp; stop in duty stable wet through.</p>
