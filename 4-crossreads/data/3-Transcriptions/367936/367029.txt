<p>Thursday 24 August 1916<br/>Mess orderly for troop.  Grand concert in Y.M.C.A. in evening.<br/>General Cheval [Chauvel] and all the big wigs there.<br/>Wrote to Boys.</p>
<p>Friday 25 August 1916<br/>B Squadron down for a swim.  Had a jolly good day.<br/>Met Barton from Wellington at the Canteen Momadehea [possibly Mahemdia].  He is in the 6th.</p>
