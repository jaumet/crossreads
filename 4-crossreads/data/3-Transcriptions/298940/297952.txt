<p>We stopped a short time at the civilian platform, (normally our halts are outside stations on sidings) &amp; were the [indecipherable] of numerous civilian feminine eyes. One spectacled mademoiselle annoyed me because of the sneering with which she regarded us. Possibly her pince nez  were responsible for her screwed up nose, &amp; certainly the appearance of myself with lap comforter &amp; unwashed &amp; unshaven countenance was liberal in the way of counter battery work.
