<p>will see an organ playing a jig or rag time and about 20 girls using the road as a <br/>ballroom.  They go over to the pub and get a few in and come out and let themselves <br/>go.  They dance without losing their dignity and I really think that those women are, <br/>inspite of their drinking &amp; smoking, good, and the tide of immorality is comparatively <br/>low.  If that is so, then good luck to them &amp; May they spend many another</p>
