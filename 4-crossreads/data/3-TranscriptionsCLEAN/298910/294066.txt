11/10/16  Wednesday
Drizzling rain - Was sitting outside of Aid Post when 3 bursts of shrapnel came over on top of Aid Post - how I missed getting a blighty is hard to say. Had a few calls during the night.
12/10/16  Thursday
Dull day - Packing up stuff ready to shift out at a moments notice - rumour has it to the somme. Heavy bombardment for about 3/4 of an hour about 7.15 pm. General raids all along the front - As far as we know they were generally successful - 59th probably most successful - every man returned, some dozen fellows hit shrapnel & bomb fragments - all very slight - 57th never got over - as far as we know only one man killed, some of these were badly cut about - one Sailor bloke hard doer & made us all laugh - hit the Doctor on the back & said give us a tot Doc - never mind the ------ wound.
