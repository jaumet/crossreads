<p>17-1-16</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton &ndash;<br/>Mrs. Addison the bearer of this note whose husband has been killed &amp; who is in receipt of pension finds it very difficult to meet a few debts Grocer 2-00 Rent 1-16-0 otherwise she can manage</p>
<p>Do you think you could assist. Mrs. Addison has once had help from you</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>B. Booth.</p>
