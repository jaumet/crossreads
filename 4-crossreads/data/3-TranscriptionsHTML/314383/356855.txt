<p>Major Staines D.S.O.  Our destination I believe is Hamadan somewhere in North Persia, where we will meeting General Dunsterville. I was to go with the Transport section but am changed over to Capt Kay's section.</p>
<p>April 19th<br/>At last the tent is comfortable &amp; well banked up to keep the flood waters out so am able to record a few more incidents.</p>
<p>We left Baghdad at 9.30 on the night of the 18th inst &amp; got aboard a train.  At some unearthly hour we are transferred to a train of open trucks which stick until 11am today and as it rained the whole time the trip was not too interesting.
