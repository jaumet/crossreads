<p>Saturday. Oct 12.10.18<br/>am still in bed and taking quinine  I am felling quite sick from it.<br/>Sunday. Oct. 13.10.18<br/>am still in bed and feeling anyhow to day.<br/>Monday Oct. 14.10.18<br/>am still in bed and feeling quite miserable all day  had a letter from maud to day.<br/>Tuesday Oct. 15.10.18<br/>am still in bed and feeling a little bit better to day.</p>
