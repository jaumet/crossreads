  
 July 1917 3rd   The big day of the Regatta.   The finishing touches were put to the decorations etc, & everything looks "tip top"   The sports opened up at 2 P.M. with  the  our Officers 4 oar, winning a victory over the 8th Field Coy.   The company 4 oar was next & my crew got an easy victory over the 14th.   The 10 oar "galley" race was run at about 3 P.M. between the 8th & us.   Both teams were in the pink of condition, & the race was a good one.   We kept fairly even until within 200 yards of the finish, when I shot out with my team & beat the 8th by over half a length.
