<p>1917 June</p>
<p>18 Monday</p>
<p>Cooked &amp; served at Minerva. Hot day with thunder showers. Miss McMillan &amp; Mrs Cobden-Sanderson came in for lunch. Kit bag &amp; haversack arrived. Nell came late at night with [indecipherable] for my vaccination &amp; told me to go to Dr Canthi.</p>
