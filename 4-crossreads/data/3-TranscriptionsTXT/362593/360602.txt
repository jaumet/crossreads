Thursday 19 September 1918Rounds fired 600, bombs droped 7.  2 Reccos done.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 3 h.  In air 2 h. 25 m.Tonkin &amp; observer shot down, captured and recaptured.Dowling wounded in leg, Mulford wounded in shoulder.
Friday 20 September 1918Rounds fired 1000, bombs dropped 16. 2 bomb raids on Turkish [indecipherable] retreating, great execution done.Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 2 h. 10.  In air 2 h. 25.Bomb raid Semakh, bombed 2 Huns on ground, 1 rendered useless, confirmed by Lt. Vernon.
