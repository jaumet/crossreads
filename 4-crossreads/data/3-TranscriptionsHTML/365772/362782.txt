<p>Hospital London<br/>3rd Sept 1916<br/>My dearest Sister,<br/>What oh, this morning the mail girl came in with the usual mail &amp; nearly all of it was for me, didnt I have a time! 2 from your dear old self, one from little Beres, the Mater one, Ulmarra one &amp; Mrs Pollock wrote me enquiring about Ralph whom she states is missing &amp; asking me to try &amp; find out anything I can about him no doubt she thinks me still with my Battalion, if such was the case I could help her, as it is I will look up any of his crowd who are inmates here &amp; get what information I can about him, poor woman she seemed very much cut up about him, you know "missing" is such an unsatisfactory</p>
