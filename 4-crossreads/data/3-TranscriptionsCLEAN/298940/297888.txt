
After I had kept got a few of the men away an English Staff Sergt. came along to tell me that he had got some trucks & an engine on the light railway, & that he could take the men right through. I told him to ask for instructions on the way, & if he met nobody to take them right through which he accordingly did with the result that the whole of the cases were evacuated in 11/2 hours.Just about this time a Major Johnson R.M.O. of the 12th Batt   arrived as a relief, & immediately provided half a dozen str regimental stretcher bearers who helped me
