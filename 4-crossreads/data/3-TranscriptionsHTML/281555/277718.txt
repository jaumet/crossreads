<p>10th. Wednesday. May 1916<br/>Weather more fair, leave interfered with, promised next N.C.O. Orderly Sgt with Cpl. Simpson. Indexed photos sending Kitty from England also negs.</p>
<p>11th. Thursday<br/>Weather unsettled. Bathing afternoon. Engineers Fatigue of 4 men 7.45pm. to 3am.</p>
<p>12th Friday<br/>Slept in till dinner time Bath afternoon. trenches, Relieved 19th 10pm.</p>
<p>Anniversary of Sydney Farewell 12/5/1915</p>
