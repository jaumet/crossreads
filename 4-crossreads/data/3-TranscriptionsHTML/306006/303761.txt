<p>4-3-17<br/>Sun 4th<br/>Did not get up till 7.30 this morning then had breakfast &amp; Church Parade at 8.30; I went to the Church of England Service, the Service was rather interesting &amp; very amusing to the Australians present as the Chaplain gave an outline of a man life who left England a member of the Church choir &amp; with every prospects of a successful life But it was the old story, as soon as he got to Australia he got in with rough company &amp; his life been wasted with drink &amp; gambling; There was a general chuckle from the Australians &amp; a good many of the Tommies, it was so pointed but the Chaplain apologised to the Australians before the last hymn &amp; said he did not it for a slur on</p>
