<p>29 May 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>Nothing of importance from here to report Germans still advancing. Weather still nice &amp; fine and hot.</p>
<p>1918 May 30<br/>Thursday<br/>Just jogging along in the same old way. Getting ready today to go on Leave tomorrow.</p>
