
December 1916(25th Continued)   During the day we received a lot of private parcels & also parcels sent to the company from people in Sydney which we divided amongst ourselves.  The officers of the company treated us to Champagne & Wine & we had a fairly merry time.26th   Another free day & we were at liberty to roam the country (within a mile radius.) but we were not lax in our celebration of Xmas.27th   We started work to-day.
