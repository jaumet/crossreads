  
 30 June 16 At sea 
 1 July 16 ) 2 July 16 )  At sea 
 3 July 16 Arrived at Bermuda & tied up alongside dockyard pier. H.M.S. Ceaser & Isis and a French Cruiser are here. Received a mail today.

 6 July 16 Alongside dockyard. H.M.S. Carnarvon came in & anchored in Grassy Bay.
