<p>16) Sari Bair</p>
<p>hard luck in the attack as we were right on top of some turkish guns [wich?] they Succedded in getting away. But never mind Better luck next time perhaps. [O well?] on august 10<sup>th</sup> I was just preparing to cook some Dinner when I was wounded. the Bullet went in the right <s>thighi</s> thigh and through to the left were it Remained until aug the 20<sup>th </sup>when I was operated on at No 1 Australian Hospital Heliopolis&nbsp; I have Beaten the turks again and they will have to [use?] another Bullet on me to Kill</p>
