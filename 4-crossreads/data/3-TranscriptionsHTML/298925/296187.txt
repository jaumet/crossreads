<p>My Hero<br/>(Chocolate Soldier)</p>
<p>I have a true and noble lover, he is my sweetheart, all my Own<br/>His like on earth who shall discover his heart is mine &amp; mine alone<br/>We pledged our troth each to the other and for our happiness I pray<br/>Our lives belong to one another (O happy happy Wedding day)</p>
<p>Come Come I love you only my heart is true<br/>Come Come My life is lonely I long for you<br/>Come Come naught can efface you<br/>My arms are aching now to embrace you<br/>Thou are divine<br/>Come, Come, I love you only come hero mine</p>
<p>It is my duty to bow before thee, It is my duty to love, adore thee.<br/>It is my duty to love thee ever, to love thee ever, love thee ever<br/>We pledged etc.</p>
