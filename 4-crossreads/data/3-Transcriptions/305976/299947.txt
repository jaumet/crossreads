<p>Tuesday Dec4<br/>Raining today. My old Neddy looks picture of health owing to good spell &amp; ample rations.<br/>Wednesday Dec 5<br/>Moving back to Richon Le Zion tomorrow for good spell out of line.<br/>Thursday Dec 6<br/>Sent on advance guard Reported  Col Cave 1ST LH.FA. Heavy rain &amp; set in &amp; spent miserable night in rain.<br/>Friday Dec 7<br/>Still raining heavily. The rainy season has started in Palestine &amp; we understand it is on the cards we will be here until after Christmas. Saw Camel Corp &amp; some of my old pals trekking back to Raffa</p>
