The brother (16) broke down completely, & cried as though his heart would break.  He flung his arms round his dead brother's body & cried, "Oh Jack, Jack, come back to me."  Some of the scenes here are terribly pathetic.  There is another young fellow  with  who will be carried into the Morgue before morning, I'm afraid. Will send you word, Father dear, as soon as I hear the results of the Reinforcement list. With love to all at home, Your loving son, Leo.
