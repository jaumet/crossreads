 Naval Signal 
 Ship of Origin - Flag 
 Date - 14/9/14 
 Time received or Sent - 7.55 
 To General 
 The following message was received from the Governor General of Australia. Begins. The Governor General congratulates the Admiral in Command on the success at Simpsons Haven &amp; desires that his high appreciation of the achievements shall be conveyed to the forces engaged . R.A.M. Furguson. Ends 
 The following reply to the above has been sent. Begins. Please thank the Governor General of Australia in the name of myself &amp; the force engaged for his kind message which is much appreciated (0747.)
