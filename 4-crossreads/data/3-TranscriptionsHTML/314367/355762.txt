<p>name &ndash; thro his steward &ndash; on the beach we secured 3 extra bottles of whisky at 2/6 each (This was our own stuff sold &amp; resold) We shall sell it tot by tot to the mess &amp; make a good profit on each bottle &ndash; No other incident occurred save that on passing Matusi point where a battery is being erected we waved our pocket handkerchiefs to some friends &amp; Fitz reproved us &ndash; saying it was orderless &ndash; No one said anything but we cursed him afterwards for a silly blighter full of gin &amp; whisky up to his top waistcoat button &ndash; Really I think it was silly of him &ndash; I got up on the poop &amp; had a last lingering look at Rabaul &ndash; It was absolutely empty save for the Una &ndash; Both town &amp; harbor are deserted &ndash; The blight &amp; curse of war is on them &amp; there is nothing doing &ndash; I've had some real good times in Rabaul but I don't want to see it again &ndash; in the torrid summer &ndash;</p>
