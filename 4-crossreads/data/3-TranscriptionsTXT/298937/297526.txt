Sunday 6th Feb.
Paid on Friday last at Intermediate Base & met Land in evening, & spent happy time at pictures.  Saw others of 3rd Field Ambulance & had good chat with Kennedy who is coming out to see me.Last might night Jack Buckle came to see me & we had a cup of tea in the hospital.The dispensary is now reorganised
