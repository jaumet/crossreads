<p>Saturday 28 April 1917</p>
<p>HESIA</p>
<p>Left Camp at 0530 i.e., 5.30 A.M. &amp; patrolled NO-MANS-LAND got shelled but home without loss &ndash; by sundown &ndash; each man carrying a bit of Firewood from some HUTS &amp; no doubt many Fleas.</p>
<p>Too cloudy to Helio.</p>
<p>I just overheard the following "If you want good Bully &amp; milk look for an Australian brand it beats Argentine Yankee or any other, but the wrappers are not so gay.</p>
