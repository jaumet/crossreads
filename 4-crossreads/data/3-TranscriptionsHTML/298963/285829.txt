<p>April 1st &amp; 2nd</p>
<p>Left Amiens 3.45 a.m. in troop train for Romescamps, spent day there where we saw very pretty hedges &amp; a rather quaint church.  Some of tombstones in yard have date of 1780.  <strike>At</strike> Waited in Y.M.C.A. which by the way was like an island, surrounded by mud knee deep.  Left Romescamps at 11.30 p.m., arrived at Edgehill at 3.30 a.m., <strike>spent</strike> slept till 7 in an empty carriage, rose, breakfasted with some Indians, reported to R.T.O. &amp; set out to find No. 1 F.A. which was billeted at Buire, found them on the move so came through the shell shattered town of Albert, to Aveloy.  We were quartered in a ruined Chateau once the residence of Baron Rothchild.  Cavelcade billeted in the old stables.</p>
