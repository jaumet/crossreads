<p>(No 3" Divers.)<br/>Ministry of Finance,<br/>Cairo</p>
<p>Australian Comforts Fund.</p>
<p>17th. July 1915.</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Read,</p>
<p>At a meeting of the above Committee held on Thursday July 15th. it was proposed and carried that you should be asked to join our Committee and I was delegated to write to you on the subject.</p>
<p>I should be glad to know if you would &ndash; kindly consent to the proposal of the Committee.</p>
<p>Yours sincerely,</p>
<p>James F. S. Hopkins<br/>Hon: Secretary.</p>
<p>Mrs. Read,<br/>c/o Dr. Madden,<br/>St. Davids Buildings,<br/>Cairo.</p>
