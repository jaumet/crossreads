<p>A rank of sergeant is to be conferred on me, &amp; ( the question having caused me much thought &amp; no little [indecipherable]) I have decided to continue to organise the classes until the falsity of such a promise may be proved.  This will not prevent me I am hopeful &amp; feel almost sure, from joining a scholarship for a university course in England,  which is my immediate objective, for I have decided to continue teaching until jouurnalism is possible</p>
