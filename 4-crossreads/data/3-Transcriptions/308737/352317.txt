<p>Well we left the aerodrome just before dawn at 4a.m. &ndash; I forget the date &ndash; about the end of June.</p>
<p>Four of us Ron. Austin, Clifford Brown, Jack Potts, &amp; myself, were flying machines of the BE2E type &amp; each carrying 12 16pndr. bombs. The other four:- Capt Murray Jones, Lts., King Cole, Peter Drummond, &amp; Bill Bowd were flying "Martinsydes" carrying both small &amp; large &amp; incendiary bombs. The trip was about 250 miles there &amp; back &amp; was supposed to take us about 3&frac12; hours.</p>
<p>Well we flew in formation &amp; arrived there safely, in about 1&frac12; hours. We were about</p>
