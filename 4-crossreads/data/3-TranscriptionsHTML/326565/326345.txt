<p>Friday 14/4/16<br/>Windstorm subsided this morning but was followed by very heavy rainstorm. <br/>Did very little duty today.</p>
<p>Saturday 15/4/16<br/>Feet inspection at the early morning parade.  31st &amp; 32nd Battalions moved from here this morning.  Caught sand adder, while pitching a tent; although small, they are considered very dangerous.</p>
<p>Sunday 16/4/16<br/>Sandstorm which prevailed here a few day back; known by the Egyptians a "Khamseen", which means 50, as it has been known</p>
