  
 Thursday  8  29th April 1915 
 Joined my own bn. & dug in.  Men joining bn. from all points.  Reorganising.  Many of my pals dead.  Tommy Bell, a sig., was struck with shrapnel early on Sunday.  Tommy Glew through back of head & into r-eye.  Moved out of our dug out to another spot.  We found by experience that it was too exposed to shrapnel, spending a very bad 10 mins.  Worked late & made another dug out.  Just as we finished Bert Travers was picked upon to help dig another trench in the firing line.
