 Nov 1917 15 Thurs    good.  Pulled out &amp; anchored in the stream there are 700 soldiers &amp; 200 civilians aboard. Friday 16th Still anchored out &amp; will be for a few days Sat 17th No movement yet Sunday 18th Monday 19th Left at 2.30 pm 4 boats in the convoy a load of Canadians a passenger boat an Aux. Cruiser &amp; 3 [indecipherable] B.B.   Very rough so a lot of sick Tuesday 20th Safe so far we lost one mine through the night Wed 21st Safe yet we are through the worst now still rough &amp; cold   Thursday 22nd Still rough &amp; cold.   A lot of stuff given out today
