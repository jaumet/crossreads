<p>It is absolutely the limit. Two cases of whiskey materialised in our truck on the way back. Wonderful fellows some of these men are!!<br/>Now we are at Abasan el Kebir again, first reserves, but there is a very strong rumour that we are going back to the beach again as the horses are in extraordinarily poor condition  &amp;  are not fit for work.</p>
