<p>of Spain seeing the mountains of Sierradevarda -</p>
<p>16/12/18 Day broke with N/East coast Algeria of Africa in sight &amp; followed along same during the day experiencing dinkum weather &ndash;</p>
<p>17th day light broke with several small islands to starboard<br/>18th Passed Malta on Port Side &ndash;</p>
<p>21 Pulled int Port Said &amp; dropped anchor about 4.30. Troops not allowed ashore -  broke ship &amp; went into Port Said &ndash; Saw main streets &amp; 2 horse trams &amp; returned   layed in canal all the time &amp; weighed anchor 2.30</p>
<p>on 22/12/18  both entering Port Said &amp; canal mast heads funnels etc could be seen of sunken vessels &ndash; At head of canal passed a big Armenian refugee camp on east bank &ndash; big suction of canal at midship &ndash; Passed through Suez Canal a very low ratio of speed arriving at Suez &ndash; about 6 am on 23 rd &ndash; layed in Port Suez &amp; spent Xmas  took L.H. of sisters aboard &ndash; leaving at 9.30 on Xmas night</p>
