Went for a long march this morning started out at 7.45. & finished 1.15. during this time we had 4 halts only, none being 10 minutes we covered about 16 miles All the hedges round here now are covered with elderberries in places they area just black with the berries.9th. Woke up this morning to find drizzling rain falling, & it is very little better this afternoon & being Sunday makes it seem all the worse.10th.
