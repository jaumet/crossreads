  
 July 1918 Friday 12 
 Vaux- sur- Somme.2nd Bty.Position 4 am. Weather fair to good. Fritz doing a big duty shoot on right. At 9 am the weather became very much cooler. Salvaged one mile of wire, and had a fair amount of graft. Finished plenty of  graft on dugout,  Played cards and finished a fair amount of cash. Wrote letters to Marguette and [indecipherable] for Smithy.
