<p>Revue, done by Amateuers on Alexandria  &amp;  things Alexandrian which was quite clever in parts. I also had a much-needed Turkish Bath.<br/>Now no one knows where we are going though I fear it will be Bir el Abd.<br/>23rd<br/>While at Alexandria I contracted with one Khalil Ibrahim Saffi for him to send me some cigarettes fortnightly, the first insallment to be sent "le dimanche' it is now Monday  &amp;  elles ne sont pas arrive. The transaction was carried on in bad French which probably explains.</p>
