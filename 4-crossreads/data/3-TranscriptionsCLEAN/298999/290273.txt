 Thursday May 17.5.17 Stand to 6 A.M. fed up went for a swim had Breakfast cleaned up went to doctor to see about getting my teeth fixed up.  rested till 12.  had dinner rested till two.  done some writing went on 1/4 guard 3.30.  had tea 5 P.M. turned in 9.30 windy night. 
 Friday May 18.5.17 Stand to 6.30 A.M. had Breakfast and cleaned up.  Still on 1/4 guard warm morning am doing some writing to to day warm day had dinner 12.  rested all afternoon was relieved from 1/4 guard 6 P.M.  had tea and turned in 7.30 very windy night.  wrote to maud Shorter.
