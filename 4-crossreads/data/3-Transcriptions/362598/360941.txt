<p>Sunday 3 September 1916<br/>Piano in case &amp; I play Hymns.  Very awkward &amp; very faint sound.  All the singers get out of Tune.  I play a wrong note in opening Hymn.  Pack up for Sedy Bishr.</p>
<p>Monday 4 September 1916<br/>50 of us left Abbassia for Sidi Bishr at 6.30.  Arrive at base camp about noon.  Swim as soon as Dinner was over.  Everything lovely.  Meet C. Gibson on Cairo Station &amp; he goes to Sidi Bishr &amp; camps.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5 September 1916<br/>3 tents away after 17 years.  We have Lecture &amp; S.A. Parade &amp; Concert at night.</p>
<p>Wednesday 6 September 1916<br/>The Party goes to Alex. to see the Catacombs.  We find them not &amp; go to Baths at Ras el-Tin.  Afterwards go on Pier &amp; listen to music, afterwards Tea &amp; Cleopatra Street.  I ride on the motor to camp.</p>
<p>Thursday 7 September 1916<br/>Capt. Orchard &amp; party go sailing.  Tea &amp; a lovely day's outing.  All Donkey ride Home.  I see Miss Brooks party &amp; hear Her sing.</p>
<p>Friday 8 September 1916<br/>Nothing doing.  We go on big beach &amp; enjoy surf shooting morning &amp; evening.  We all have a blow out at the Refreshment Room.  Concert at Y.M.C.A.  We get news to go.</p>
<p>Saturday 9 September 1916<br/>We have issue of Cigarettes &amp; leave Sidi Bishr at 9.30 &amp; march to Tram.  We join up at 10.30 &amp; pitch our moving Tent.  10 in a tent.  We have a swim &amp; the black flag goes down.  I receive letters from Amy Cocks, Auntie Annie, Home, M. Westaway.  Play Cards &amp; have soup in Recreation Room.</p>
