 10/Mr. Henley M.L.A. of Burwood came over from Alex. with gifts from Aust: he issued them personaly &amp; every man got something. He then gave us a little lecture &amp; I can say the boys gave him some rousing cheers in not forgetting us so far out in the desert. 
 We are all busy in exchanging letters &amp; notes that were enclosed in the parcels &amp; writing thanking the senders.
