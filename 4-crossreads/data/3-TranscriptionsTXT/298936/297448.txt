 October 1915 
 Tuesday 19 
 a.m. fatigues. 
 Artillery duel in morning. 
 Had first narrow escape.  Sitting in dug out after breakfast when a thud on loose earth startled me, sending a shower of earth down. 
 Turned out to be a shell cap. 
 A foot to the right &amp; I should not be writing this.
