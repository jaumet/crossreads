<p>There were men of all different forces viz. Australian, Canadian, New Zealand, England, Scotland, Portugal &amp; Italy  I slept there for one night &amp; next morning I went before the American Dr &amp; he sent me straight of  to the 6th General Hospital.  When I got there they put me straight to bed again &amp; on 28th April I was sent to England, I arrived at Southampton on the 29th &amp; went from there to the 5th Southern Gen Hospital, Milton at Portsmouth  It turned out to be a good hospital &amp; I had a good time whilst I was there  I was kept in bed  for three weeks then I was put out on the balcony to sleep..
