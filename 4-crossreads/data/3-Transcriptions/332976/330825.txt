<p>March 1917<br/>26th. (cont)   The weather has been very miserable all day, rain, snow &amp; with a bighting cold North wind blowing.<br/>27th.   The weather is just the same today.   I have had a fatigue party salvaging German material for use in our work.   There is abundance of material &amp; ammunition lying about, especially sawn mining sets &amp; barbed wire.   The majority of the section &amp; No. 2 are working along the road building huts for D.H.Q.   They have been on it for some days now &amp; expect to have it finished to-night.
