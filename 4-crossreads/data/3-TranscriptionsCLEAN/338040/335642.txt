  
 March 23rd B and B very cold, no sleep previous night, well into North of France, agricultural country still continues. Souvenirs requested by French children. Monotony of journey broken by most beautiful landscape scenery. Journey completed 10pm. Rejoin baggage party. Guard baggage while remainder of troops (1500 march to encampment). Spend night in snow out in the open, fall of about 6 inches (Thiennes) March 24th Guard baggage
