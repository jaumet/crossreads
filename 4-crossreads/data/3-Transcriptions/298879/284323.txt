<p>January 1915<br/>Saturday 30<br/>Returned from outpost duty. Half Holiday. Went to Cairo &amp; saw a splendid programme of pictures at American Biograph Cinema<br/>Most important battle of the new year won by British at Chiensky [?]. Lord Kitchener pays attention to it.</p>
<p>Sunday 31<br/>Church parade. Beautiful weather. Wrote several letters.</p>
