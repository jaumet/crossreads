<p>Sunday 3rd Sept 1916<br/>No letters from Lily since arrived back, nor from Hammonds.<br/>Billy is going to town tomorrow &amp; I hope to go for next week-end.<br/>Went to see "Within the Law" on Thursday last,<strike>but of</strike> yesterday afternoon went onto Tidworth per motor (traction engine).<br/>Bought 9 rolls of films for the V.P.K. camera which I have bought for 15/-.<br/>I have also bought today, some printing frames &amp; dishes, but I shall not take these away with me.  They can keep them for me at home.  3/6 was not dear for them</p>
