  
 My Darling Mother, No letters to hand from home since arriving in France am anxiously looking forward for Some.    Hope you are all well and in the best of health & that business is keeping up.   I see Malcolm McPherson nearly every day, he is camped about 1/2 mile away from us & we drift together of an evening & swop news.    he is in the 3rd Engineers, wrote to you a few days ago.
