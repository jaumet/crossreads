
31/5/18FranvillersAnother beautifully fine day with warm sun. met all the company commanders and gave them full details for our reconnaissance and we then set off on horseback. Rode through Heilly and Mericourt. It was very pretty and shady down in the Valley of the Ancre where the green leaves of the many trees gave splendid shade. The village of Heilly is not badly knocked about but that of Mericourt is different indeed. There nearly every house is bashed about and notices are up telling people to keep out as the whole place is full of gas. The Boche did not shell at all as we rode up to a deep chalk pit where a number of dugouts were located. Here I saw Colonel Pat Currie and got full details for our take over tonight.
