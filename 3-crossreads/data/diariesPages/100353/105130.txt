Farmers were abroad working among their vines in the fields the red roofed houses scattered over the intensely cultivated hillsides among the poplar groves orchards, formed a very pleasant contrast to the mud manure huts of Egypt. Everything spoke peace, were it not for the uniformed guards at every tunnel, groups of German prisoners working on some railway works it would have been hard to believe that this was portion of a country engaged in a fight for its existence.
