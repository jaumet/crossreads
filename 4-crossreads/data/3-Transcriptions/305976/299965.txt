<p>heavy storm<br/>Monday Jan 28<br/>Still raining. Flat under water. Heavy going on horseback Have usual cold<br/>Tuesday Jan 29<br/>Weather cleared up. Saw beautiful Air duel over camp to day. 2 Turkish planes attacked one of ours &amp; after a very exciting duel, one of the Turkish planes crashed &amp; the other had to fly for home.<br/>Wednesday Jan 30<br/>Major Leggitt from corps inspected today. All satisfactory.</p>
