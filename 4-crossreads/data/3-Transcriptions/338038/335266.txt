<p>I hope to be able to get about on sticks in a few days and should then be able to give you more interesting descriptions of this wonderful country &ndash; There is a car attached to the Hospital here for the use of patients so I am looking forward to some nice jaunts round the country.</p>
<p>Fondest love to selves &amp; Pat from<br/>Your loving Son Terence</p>
