 5th Apr.  Received orders for the line, which is at present just outside Villers Bretonneaux. On our way we passed dozens of dead horses also some  of  Tommies who had got it.  Round where two big navel guns had stood were scores of holes made by whizzbangs. The bridges we passed were mined ready to be blown up in case we had to fall back  Along a little further Fritz nearly caught us with a big shell.
