<p>Thursday 20th April</p>
<p>News in paper of fall of Trebizond &amp; today I hear that America has declared war on Germany.<br/>If so things are indeed brighter.<br/>No letters yet.<br/>Hope to be on the way to France soon.<br/>Foot is &ndash; like I imagined for weeks &ndash; nearly better.</p>
<p>Friday &amp; Sat.</p>
<p>Nothing happened to get excited about.</p>
