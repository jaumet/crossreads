<p>of a blow &amp; I had very little dinner or breakfast but made up for it at tea time.  I have some medicine but I only carry it about, I don't take it.  I have not been sick yet but I feel funny in the stomach.  A life on the ocean wave is no good to me.  She is a very steady old boat &amp; is making good pace now.  The waves to-day are huge but it is only a long swell &amp; she glides over them like a duck.  We have lost our piano somewhere so we only have a little organ &amp; a flute &amp; violin for our own private concerts.  We have our own concert room at the stern of the boat but the big concert room is forward where there is a good piano.  We have been down the holds looking for our own</p>
