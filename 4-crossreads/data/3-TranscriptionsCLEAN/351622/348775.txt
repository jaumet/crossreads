  
 [Roumanall] killed - midnight Charge 
 2 February 1916 Wed Route march before breakfast. Lecture by the Colonel on treatment of wounds on the desert at 10 a.m. Afternoon off & Ken & myself went to the the historic graveyard of Tel-el Kebir. Coldstream-Guards, Life, Dragoon, [indecipherable] buried. Met [Anthony] canteen. [age] 
 3 February 1916 Thur Signalling before breakfast. Lecture on Sanitation by Major Phipps at 10 a.m. A lot of Kid given by Major Moseley who dismisses us a quarter of an hour early. After dinner lecture on the contents of an ambulance waggon followed by loading of wounded into waggon.
