<p>2.  Sculptor committed susanside over this statue.  After the job was finished to the approval of all concerned, the plans having been handled by many persons the sculptor happened to be standing looking at it.  He noticed a small boy looking at it at the same time &amp; for a joke asked the youngster what He thought of it.  The child innocently remarked that it was very nice but not quite finished yet.  He said there are no spurs on the Prince's Boots.  The Sculptor &amp; all concerned had not noticed it, so He straightway went &amp; blew His Brains out.
