<p>15th/8th/19. On which lies Emden. 9.30 Cocos Group on Starboard passing 4 miles away. Whale 20 yds from Port bow. 5pm msg from W/T Stn apologising for not seeing Burma pass &amp; returning greetings &amp; salute. Also outline of Emden fight.<br/>16th Cooling down. Concert<br/>17th Occasional raining. Bank addresses handed in to Pay Branch. Visit engine room of Burma<br/>18/8/19 Cool to moderate<br/>20/8/19 Rough &amp; squally<br/>22/8/19 4am bearing into Fremantle</p>
