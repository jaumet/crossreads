 Thursday 29&nbsp;July 1915&nbsp; Letters to Pearl Drury, &amp; some others.&nbsp; In Cairo for an hour this morning at chemical laboratory &amp; other places.&nbsp; 
 Friday 30&nbsp;July 1915&nbsp; About hospital all day.&nbsp; The cry is still they come.&nbsp; 
 Saturday 31&nbsp;July 1915&nbsp; Pres 658 patients today.&nbsp; General Legge called this afternoon, about 6 p.m.&nbsp; 
 Sunday 1 August 1915&nbsp; Heard Mass at the Basilica 9.30 am.&nbsp; Father Vrigille S.J. lunched with me.&nbsp; Went with the padre to visit a French family at Heliopolis stayed for two hours&nbsp;
