19th. This morning I was up for classification what a farce it is you all stand in a row they run you through like a mob of sheep. I don't know what he marked me nor care much for I have no desire to stop in this Camp long, it rained like hell all the morning but this afternoon things look much brighter, just before dinner Len came over took me back to dinner with him to his Mess, he is Catering Sgt has the best Mess on the plains I was told this several times by various chaps its true alright for it is very nice the food judging by the dinner plentiful good everything is run on a system so there is no hitches
