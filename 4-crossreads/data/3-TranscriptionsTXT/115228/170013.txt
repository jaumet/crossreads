  Nov 18th :- Moved on to within 2 1/2 miles of Jaffa, the New Zealand Brigade having already entered the town &amp; taken up positions beyond. Jaffa out of bounds to us unfortunately. 
  Nov 19th :- Raining all day. 
  Nov 20th :- Outpost duty last night, terribly wet &amp; cold &amp; miserable. 
  Nov 21st :- Our Brigade was relieved by the 2nd Brigade &amp; we moved back a few miles (about 12 miles today). 
  Nov 22nd :- Moved back a further six to eight miles
