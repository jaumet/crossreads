<p>5.4.16<br/>Out on Parade to day. same old things drill and sat down &amp; smoke  mail arrive to day but did not receive any letters parcels etc. nothing doing afterwards other than inspection by Colonel at 11 A.M.</p>
<p>6.4.16<br/>Told last night or rather early this morning.  Went for swim about 7 oclock which means I missed a parade but that did not matter, it was beautiful.  Two French <strike>boa</strike> steamers &amp; one English tramp <strike>went through the canal </strike> passed whilst I was swimming wonderful how the go through.  no parade this afternoon half holiday  Greeks opened Canteens in rear of our camp to day it is murderous the price they charge for their articles.</p>
<p>7.4.16<br/>Mail to day papers &amp; letter from home also Mrs Sheath &amp; J Ashtory Dusty days  Tell Mother Dad</p>
