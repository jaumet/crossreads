<p>February, 1916<br/>Monday 21<br/>The whole school went out on an attack scheme in the early morning<br/>After dinner we put in a couple of hrs on stoppages.<br/>Draw P.T 200 pay &amp; have a long chat w/- an old pal now a Lieut.<br/>Weather getting warm again</p>
<p>Tuesday 22<br/>Machine gunners go out on a Tactical scheme returning at 8.30<br/>Morning on stoppages &amp; afternoon reviewing notes.<br/>At night I go to see a big old Roman picture "Cabiria" very good.</p>
