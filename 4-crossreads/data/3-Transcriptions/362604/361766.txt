<p>saying good-bye to Mr. Charles, I went to Madame Tussaud's with <u>Miss</u> "Bob" Davis one of the buyers.  She is returning Home via America &amp; will be Home just on X'mas.  Madame's place is very fine but the celebrated "Chamber of Horrors" is non-existant for there is not a blood-curdling thing in it &amp; not to be compared with Sydney Wax-works for Horrors.
