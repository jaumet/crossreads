 1916   MONDAY 13   NOV. 
 Fine & warm & the Sun shining all day, & by the Lord we were glad to see him. All the third division went out on a route march of about thirteen or fourteen miles in full marching order. The general was there & inspected us. We had a real good day, although we came home very tired about 6 pm.
