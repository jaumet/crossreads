<p>August, 1915<br/>Saturday 21<br/>Fortnight today wounded &ndash; Thigh much better but not allowed out of bed &ndash; We get no news but Troops are being sent along as Fast as possible &amp; every man is wanted.<br/>From all I read living must be very expensive in Australia now &amp; chaff dear.<br/>Letters from Aunt Louise May &amp; Daisy July 12th ere this arrives May will have gone again &ndash; Poor old girl I wish I could help her.</p>
