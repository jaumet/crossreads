 16 Spring St. Sydney, 2-Janry 1918 
 My dear Judge, I received your letter and thank you very much for copy cable. We have not had one &amp; Mrs. Lucas was much disappointed but we are quite satisfied now. We may of course receive one yet. 
 With best wishes from Mrs. Lucas &amp; self to you Mrs. Ferguson &amp; family for the new year. 
 I am Yours very sincerely, E. Lucas
