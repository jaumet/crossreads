 Thursday 15 February 
 Walked to Ayton Station in time to catch train for Berwick.  Left Berwick 8.24 a.m. and travelled to York then across country past Sheffield &amp; Derby to Birmingham arriving there at 7 p.m.  Met by Uncle Harry &amp; Bessie.  Tram to their house at 238 Willow Av. Edgbaston. The country round Sheffield is full of ammunition works.  Saw where 2 Zep. bombs had dropped.  Leaning tower near Sheffield.
