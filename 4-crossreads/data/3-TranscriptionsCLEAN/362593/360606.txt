
Friday 27 September 1918Recco to Damascus.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 3 h. 55.  Landed at A'Fuleh.  Total distance 320 miles.Damascus is a great city from the air.Took photos.  The wonderful stunt is drawing to a close as regards bombing.  First machine over Damascus.
Saturday 28 September 1918Bomb raid to Hun aerodrome at Damascus.  Huns had flown except one machine, it was well bombed & gunned.  Fired 800 rounds, dropped 8 bombs, in air 3 h. 40.Pilot Lt. McGuinness, did splendid shooting at Hun, must have damaged him.Engine very dud, glad to arrive Home.
