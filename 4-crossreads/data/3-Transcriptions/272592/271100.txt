<p>just a bare existence, it takes a lot of hardships to kill a man since we have been in here we have not averaged 2 hours sleep per night &amp; only 2 meals a day, &amp; the men have worked fairly hard</p>
<p>This afternoon the whole Coy. is on fatigue cleaning out an old communication trench that the Canadians let got to ruin, we are doing it so as we can have a safe passage down to the dumps a 4th Bn. runner was shot dead yesterday &amp; an Engineer wounded through not having this sap.</p>
<p>Our Bombers killed 3 Germans last night they attempted a small raid on one of our Sections &amp; fell in the muck.</p>
