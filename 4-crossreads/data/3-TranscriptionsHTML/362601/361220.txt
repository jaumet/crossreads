<p>16 June, Monday</p>
<p>F &amp; lovely cool day, fair swell.</p>
<p>I give up Hospital duty. Take English lecture &amp; read all afternoon. I go up on Duty for 2 hours to lelieve. A chap gets 106. What a fuss! I read &amp; Jack &amp; I play German whist all night. I also play Patience but cheat like m--------. I see M est grande on French Blackboard.</p>
<p>17 June, Tuesday</p>
<p>F.<br/>No duties. I go on deck to study, come down below to study. Boat has heavy list to port. She rocks surprisingly for such calm weather.</p>
<p>Partnerships again all afternoon. I do no bon neither do the others. Jack puggarees my hat. Our Boys win the special prize for cleanliness. Another boat drill. I larrickinise with no boats.</p>
<p>Wrote M 4 X Wait for reply X</p>
