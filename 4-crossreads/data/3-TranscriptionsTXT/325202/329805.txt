It was 10 p.m before be finally halted & by the time we had unharnessed - stretched our drag ropes from waggon wheel to waggon wheel (to tie the horses on to) watered & fed our horses ditto ourselves - It was midnight & we were quite ready for our blankets - one of the longest days I ever remember on the go for a full 23 hours.
