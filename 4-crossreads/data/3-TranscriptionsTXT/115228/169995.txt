 escorted a camel convoy out 8 miles to Kalassa, handed the convoy over, and returned as escort to another camel train to Esani.&nbsp; Arrived in camp at 12 midnight &amp; had to stand to arms at 4.30 in the morning. 
  Oct 28th: &nbsp; The Brigade moved out at 4.30 p.m. &amp; arrived at Kalassa about 9 p.m. where we camped the night. 
  Oct 29th :&nbsp; Moved out to Asluj a further 8 miles where three mounted divisions were encamped ready to &quot;kick off&quot; for the attack on Beersheba.&nbsp;
