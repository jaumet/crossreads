For the past, week month our Allies ourselves have been victorious everywhere, but at the same time I fancy that it will still be a long while before the Germans are thoroughly beaten. Yesterday afternoon we witnessed many exciting aerial combats. A Taube managed to get through our lines talk about excitement. From every quarter aircraft guns blazed out kept him at a tremendous height so that he could not observe anything. 2 of our fast chasers or Battle planes then gave chase to see them fighting for top position was wonderful to behold. We eventually lost sight of them in a thick cloud do not know whether they got him or not.It has just started to rain heavily again is pouring into our bivouac like fun, such is soldiering
