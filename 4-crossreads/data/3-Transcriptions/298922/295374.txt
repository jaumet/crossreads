<p>Saturday 17 February 1917</p>
<p>Brass*</p>
<p>Been around on Patrol, saw *Brass Cartridge Cases &amp; dead Camels on the ground of old fights.</p>
<p>I was glad to get all the Home News ere this you will all be in Melbourne.</p>
<p>Foster Rutledge is the C.O. of Flying Corps here but I've never met him.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through]<br/>How strange that old Sir Sam should go to CANADA at his time of life.</p>
<p>Hard as it may seem I cannot possibly see poor JOE could leave you all, I know I have the best of it, still he knows <u>he is doing his job</u>. I note dear old Day is sending me some SOKS. Thanks for information re Bank quite RICH I am &amp; get more pay as Major 30/- a day now.</p>
