<p>slowly improving &amp; we are settling down very comfortably. With the exception of attending a church parade we have done no hard work yet. The food is good all round that is both for officers &amp; men. I'm afraid this will have to be the end, not only because of want of news, but because all letters have to be handed in <strike>to</strike> within one &amp; a half minutes. I'll write again at the next port of call.</p>
<p>Your loving son<br/>Arthur.</p>
<p>If you don't get this letter you'll know it has been censored. This is not my own.</p>
