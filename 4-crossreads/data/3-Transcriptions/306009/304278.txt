<p>1915<br/>Apr. 2 Fri.<br/>Attended Service in the chapel at 10.30 a.m.<br/>Went into town in the afternoon &amp; visited Greek, French &amp; British Boy Scout Headquarters.<br/>Big row in Cairo in Waza area - commencing about 4 p.m. &ndash; troops started to pull houses to pieces &amp; light bonfires in them &ndash; Military police fired on soldiers &amp; killed 2 &amp;  &amp; wounded several &ndash; Fire Brigade called out to extinguish fires.<br/>Very interesting!</p>
