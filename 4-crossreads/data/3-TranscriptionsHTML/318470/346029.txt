<p>Sunday 27 August 1916<br/>A quiet day &ndash; only a little bombardment on each side.  Usual work strengthening positions.</p>
<p>Monday 28 August 1916<br/>Heavy Artillery Bombardment of enemy line.  Eighth Brigade made a Raid at night.  No success.  One casualty.</p>
<p>Tuesday 29 August 1916<br/>Very heavy rain.  Usual work on Dugouts &amp; generally strengthening positions.  Rain impairing work.</p>
<p>Wednesday 30 August 1916<br/>Nothing unusual.  Fair amount of Artillery Fire on both side.</p>
<p>Thursday 31 August 1916<br/>Still on Revetting work &amp; strengthening positions generally.  Nothing unusual.</p>
<p>Friday 1 September 1916<br/>Uneventful, a few Bombs thrown from both sides.</p>
<p>Saturday 2 September 1916<br/>General bombardment on both side.  Usual revetting &amp; strengthening work.</p>
<p>Sunday 3 September 1916<br/>Large Aerial Fleet fleeting to be seen over Enemy position.  Said to have bombarded Line near Lille.</p>
<p>Monday 4 September 1916<br/>A good deal of Artillery preparation on both side.  Work going on as usual.  Showery droopy weather.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5 September 1916<br/>Very heavy showers making work very difficult.  Fairly heavy bombardment both sides.</p>
<p>Wednesday 6 September 1916<br/>Still very showery.  Continuing work revetting &amp; strengthening generally.  Fairly heavy Artillery Firing.</p>
<p>Thursday 7 September 1916<br/>Heavy bombardment by our Artillery &amp; trench Howitzers.  Fairly heavy retaliation by Enemy Guns.</p>
<p>Friday 8 September 1916<br/>Short heavy bombardment by our Guns about 5 p.m.  Nice fine day.  Work as usual strengthening.</p>
<p>Saturday 9 September 1916<br/>Usual Artillery activity &amp; stokes Howitzers.  Still revetting &amp; strengthening.</p>
