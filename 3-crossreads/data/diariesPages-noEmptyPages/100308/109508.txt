5/5/17  Norreuil
the barrage was thick only dim forms could be discerned progressing through it. The barrage was well timed and did not necessitate many halts. Some say that many of our chaps went charging through it ? the reverse to the Tommies. Once in the trenches there was desperate fighting. Jo Slater was killed in No Mans Land and some men who later tried to get his body found nothing but a leg with an officer's boot on it. Evidently his body had been blown to bits. Scammell was killed by a sniper who shot him through the head and Filmer had his brains blown out. Hogarth has not been seen since he fell wounded. Griffen was killed on the road near Brigade H.Q. Bennett got a bomb fair in the face but is still alive Fraser is missing. Gordon Maxfield went right through to the second objective and got his leg shattered. He is said to have died there. Jones 21st died of his wounds 57 officers killed and wounded in the four battalions and about 1000 men. The padre pushing round burying
