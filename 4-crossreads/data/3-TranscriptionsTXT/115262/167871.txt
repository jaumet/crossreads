 Saturday 14 April 1917. 
 Stayed Federal Coffee Palace on Fri eveng 
 Up at 7.30 &amp; after a time out to Burnley. 
 In city on Sat morning. 
 Out to Toorak in afternoon 
 Met Joy at 5.50 in city &amp; after tea went to &quot;Canary Cottage&quot; at &quot;Her Majesty&#39;s theatre&quot;. (Joy lost her purse.) 
 Bed at 12.30 am. at Burnley 
 Most enjoyable evening.
