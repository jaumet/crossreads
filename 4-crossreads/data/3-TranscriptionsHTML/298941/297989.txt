<p>I am tremendously glad to get them! Splendid!<br/>I am in the truck on the way up, but I doubt if I shall be able to write much. These trains are the limit for jolting &ndash; (we have paused.)<br/>We are passing through the beautiful (once again) country between Rouen &amp; Abbeville (2) Wooded valley with a smooth road at the bottom running parallel to &amp; not &frac14; mile from the line.</p>
