<p>March Monday 12 1917.</p>
<p>Rain last night. <br/>Reg turns up &amp; meets me. He is only about a mile down the road.  Took me along to see Ern. Murray who is staying a few days at a collecting post preparatory to going to Amiens about his eyes.<br/>'Twas great to meet them. Had not long for a yarn, but Reg invited us down to tea Wed. evening. <br/>Also met Netherly &amp; Bill Johnston. <br/>Receive big mail 18 letters. Baghdad fallen.</p>
