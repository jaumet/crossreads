<p>Quentin &amp; charged the position in extended order (5th Bde). We encountered a strong resistance, &amp; fierce machine gun fire! In my opinion there was one German M G to every 100 sq yds of ground, &amp; before us were a few rows of trees some marshy ground &amp; scrub We quickly dislodged the enemy from a trench in front of this marsh, &amp; at this place the boys advanced shouting</p>
