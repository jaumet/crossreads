<p>Dec. 25 Saturday 1915<br/>Woke up at Mena House to find all sorts of provision made for our cheer.<br/>Lottery tickets were give &amp; parcels with all sorts of dainties handed round.<br/>Concert at night.<br/>Went for a walk with a few friends today as far as the Pryamids, there are crowds out to see the sights &amp; the camels &amp; donkeys are having a lot of work.</p>
<p>Dec. 26 Sunday 1915<br/>Not  too bright. Resting all day.<br/>6th A L Regiment arrived at Alexandra.<br/>More letters from Home I wonder how they are putting in their Xmas.</p>
<p>Dec. 27 Monday 1915<br/>Dr says I can go to Helonan<br/>Regiment intraining for Maadi<br/>Am feeling better &amp; really do not think that I am bad enough to be sent back.</p>
