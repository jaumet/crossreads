
[News cutting inserted in diary]14.12.1918
SOLDIERS' DIARIES
The MITCHELL LIBRARY is desirous of Purchasing for the State Archives, ORIGINAL DIARIES of Australian Soldiers taking part in the Great War.  Good prices will be paid for good material.
Address, Mitchell Library, Macquarie-street, Sydney.
For the Trustees,W.H. Ifould,Principal Librarian and Secretary
