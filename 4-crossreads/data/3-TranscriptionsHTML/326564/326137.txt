<p>16th. Lectures again this morning up till 11 oclock then we had physical jerks till 12.<br/>Most of the afternoon was taken up in writing up our lectures, its astonishing the amount of writing one has to do at these schools, after tea I had a walk down to Merckenghem to see the watchmaker, it was a lovely evening nice &amp; sunny &amp; all the country looked nice &amp; green, young crops coming on all</p>
