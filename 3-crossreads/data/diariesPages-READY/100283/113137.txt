There are about 80 of us at this School 1 from each Battn, the rest form Trench Mortar Bdes etc. we are living in Nissen huts which are lit by electricity, the Camp seems fairly comfortable, though I'm afraid we are going to do a perisher with only 2 blankets, for the nights are very cold long. 
15th. Nearly froze to death last night I can see it's a case of turning in full marching order unless the nights get a little warmer.
