  
 Saturday 16 December 1916 On engineer escort with troop.  Captured turk walking about near camp. Saw tracks where turks had come in at night and looked at our camp. Saw 3rd Regt. Patrol, thought they were turks so went out to capture them;  but did not. 
 Sunday 17 December 1916 Out on patrol as signaller.  Saw Turkish patrol of about 300 men & camels.  Found some good Hods.  Saw dead Turkish horse shod with steal plates right over hooves.
