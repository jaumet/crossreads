 March, 1916 Saturday 4 The men for next school march in all day. A large detachment of British West Indies Natives are here this time I have managed to get a beastly cold. 
 Sunday 5 Muster Parade at 9.30 am Find again that we have a lot more men than we had arranged for, about 750 Machine Gunners Had tea w/- Dad &amp; went to Pictures at Helmia camp
