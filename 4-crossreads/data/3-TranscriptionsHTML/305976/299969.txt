<p>Sunday Feb 17<br/>Ground in this part of Palestine worked on following principle. Owner of ground lets it out to inhabitants who get paid according to harvest Baron Rothchild the American millionaire subsidised a lot of this property.<br/>Monday Feb 18<br/>Transports N.Z returned roads too bad.<br/>Tuesday Feb 19<br/>N.Z. bombed at Jericho. Inspected as usual. 10 days stunt on. Out of this little lot.<br/>Wednesday Feb 20<br/>Anzacs &amp; N.Z. reported fighting out of Jericho</p>
