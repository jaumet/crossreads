<p>very successful only two of lads killed. They brought back a number of Prisoners, some of whom were only lads of 16 yrs &ndash; also got a lot of information &ndash; Our point is already too far advanced &ndash; so gain nothing taking his trenches yet. Sgt Holt who was killed, was just about to get a Commission &amp; had secretly inherited &pound;12,000. He died in our charge, so we had to t6ake charge till Padre &amp; M.O. took all particulars etc. Had very little sleep.</p>
<p>18/9/16  Monday</p>
<p>Raining very hard all day &ndash; Got orders to go to "Noveau Monde" for a weeks blow. Got wet through walking in &ndash; boots full of water &ndash; Had a change of everything bar boots &ndash; Very cold.</p>
<p>19/9/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Raining off &amp; on. Had the morning off went to Divisional Baths &amp; had a good hot bath. On Gas Picquet evening &amp; night. Very cold</p>
<p>20/9/16  Wednesday</p>
<p>Dull day &ndash; Had the day off. Had a good old read. A few casualtys came in through the night.</p>
