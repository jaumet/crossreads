
13 Feb.  Pay day Troop deck Picket
14 Feb.  On guard looking forward to Colombo
15 Feb.  Arrived Colombo midday sweltering heat.  Went ashore half past two went to Cinnamon Gardens, Buddist Temple, Fruit Markets around city in rickshaw, nightime went out to Grand Pass returned to ship about ten o'clock
Sunday  Finished coaling and taking water  left Colombo three o'clock very hot.
