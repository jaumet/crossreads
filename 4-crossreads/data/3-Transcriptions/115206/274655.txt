<p>March 1918<br/>Tuesday 26</p>
<p>Hollebeke. Zero Road<br/>3.30am Fritz shelling at intervals 10 seconds, - road.for the past few days. I have made no mention of a terrific battle in progress down south. The latest news today regarding the crisis is that Fritz is in Albert. Our Divvie is going down at any time now.<br/>Read a circular issued by Sir Doug Haig, in which he appeals to every man to do his utmost in the critical battle of the war.<br/>Weather fairly cool; Charlie  Smith died of wounds at 3pm.</p>
