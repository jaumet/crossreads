<p>Sunday 4 June 1916<br/>Working on Camp all day.  Weather very hot.</p>
<p>Monday 5 June 1916<br/>A little drill in morning.  Pontooning in afternoon near Ismailia.</p>
<p>Tuesday 6 June 1916<br/>Working on Mess Hut in our Lines today.  No. 1 &amp; 3 Pontooning on Sweet Water Canal.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7 June 1916<br/>Working again today on Mess Hut.  No. 1 &amp; 3 Pontooning on Sweet Water Canal.</p>
<p>Thursday 8 June 1916<br/>Word that Lord Kitchener was downed on Tuesday when H.M.S. Hampshire went down off Orkney Islands.  Torpedoed or mined.</p>
<p>Friday 9 June 1916<br/>Pontooning on Sweet Water Canal.</p>
<p>Saturday 10 June 1916<br/>Pontooning on Sweet Water Canal in morning.  Sports in afternoon.</p>
<p>Sunday 11 June 1916<br/>Had easy day today.  Only work on Camp fatigues.</p>
<p>Monday 12 June 1916<br/>Pontoon bridging on Sweet Water Canal.  News of big Russian victories against Austrians.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13 June 1916<br/>Squad &amp; Rifle Drill in morning.  "Kitchener" Memorial Service in afternoon, also took charge Guards.</p>
<p>Wednesday 14 June 1916<br/>Had charge Guards today.  Nothing fresh going.  Expect to move before long.</p>
<p>Thursday 15 June 1916<br/>Pontooning today on the Sweet Water Canal today.</p>
<p>Friday 16 June 1916<br/>Pontoon bridging, trestle forming and other bridging work.  Very hot.</p>
<p>Saturday 17 June 1916<br/>Left "Moaskar" at about 10 p.m. for Alexandria preparatory to Embarking for France.</p>
