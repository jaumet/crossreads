<p>numbers of young men in offices, who will not go to the front &amp; here, my case &ndash; threatened with punishment for trying to do so &amp; determinedly. Australia &amp;  whoever will may go to worse places, before I enlist as a   <u>volunteer</u> to serve my country again after this &amp; the various rebuffs I have had in trying to get to the front. Meer <u>dirty</u> opposition    &amp; dislike is influencing this <u>fellow</u> Parker in his actions towards me. Cold footed as he is &amp; no desire to go to the front &amp; he stops others. Legge &amp; I hold a council of war, with Palliser &amp; are going to see the thing out, will fight this against his ignorant worship Parker, as I know the letter he has in hand is granting my application for transfer &amp; asking for me to be sent without delay. Await to-morrow</p>
