<p>June 8th  N.N.W.  314M.<br/>Burial Service 7am.  Drill as usual mainly lecture by Commander.  Hot morning, cooler afternoon, stiff breeze night.  Mending afternoon to reading &amp; sleep, no land in sight.</p>
<p>June 9th  N.N.W.  317M.<br/>Usual daily routine, land very indistinct outline.  Hot am., much cooler pm., considerably cooler all day.  Reading morning till 9am., also evening.  Ship getting her windlasses set for unloading fore &amp; aft.</p>
