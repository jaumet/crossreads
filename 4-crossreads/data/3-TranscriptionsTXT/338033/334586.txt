The Kangaroo and Emu were not over welcome for the girls informed us that they could only receive 18/6 in the pound for it. Our difficulty was overcome later on, when the paymaster came along and exchanged it for us. On making a few inquiries, I was told that every village has its church, a fact I had noted on the way up, and a resident parson, who is allowed a farm to use for his own purposes as Well as receiving the collection.
