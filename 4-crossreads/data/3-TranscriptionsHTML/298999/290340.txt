<p>Tuesday Aug. 7.8.17<br/>turned out 4.30 A.M. saddled up and returned to camp fed up had Breakfast cleaned up camp rested till 12.30 had dinner very hot day.  rested till 5 P.M. fed up had tea fell in for rifle inspection went up to camel hospital had a Bath done some washing had a Beer am feeling swell again now.  returned to camp 10 P.M. can hear Bombs or Big guns firing.  turned in.</p>
