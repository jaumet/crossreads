<p>2 of our men they are harmless take them all round &amp; unless they lob right in the trench you are right, but you have got to throw no chances away with the "Minnewerfers for some of them weigh 240 lbs &amp; are highly dangerous</p>
<p>What do you think of this a couple of days ago a Belgian battery sent a big dud over &amp; by some mischance it fell short, &amp; our fellows out of curiosity they dug it up &amp; opened it &amp; to the surprise of all it contained not explosives as you would expect to find, but cleverly sketched plans of the positions of our guns &amp; etc. it was reported at once &amp; after a short examination shot the Major at the gun, lucky wasn't it</p>
