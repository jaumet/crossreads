<p>Wednesday 14 November 1917.</p>
<p>At 10 am. moved to the foot of the hill on which is the village of Yebna. Given an hrs. leave in the village. At our Collecting Station wounded Turks are <s>coming in</s> being brought in.</p>
<p>Took snap of 91 Turkish prisoners and a couple of the village.</p>
<p>The oranges we got here are good oh and are a change needed in the diet of bully &amp; biscuits ration.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Got a little rest tonight apart from 40 mins picquet from 11.20 pm to midnight.</p>
