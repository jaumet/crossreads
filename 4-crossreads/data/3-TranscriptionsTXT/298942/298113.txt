 Meteren burning, tried to see where our shells were bursting &amp; generally explored. 
 As soon as it was daylight it commenced to rain heavily so I went inside.  Then I went down the road &amp; looked over the cottage which is about a quarter of a mile nearer Fletre.  A shell had smashed almost a whole side of it, &amp; with the rain dripping outside &amp; half of the inside, the effect was very eerie in the half light with ghostly tappings &amp; drippings. . . .
