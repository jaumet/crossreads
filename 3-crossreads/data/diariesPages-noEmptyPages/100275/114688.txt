After we had got our gear off we went into the little Belgian caf where we had a good tea on bread butter coffee eggs the Belgian girls are very funny with their broken English. They are pretty smart at their work.
30th. It rained like cats dogs all night fully 2 inches must have fallen, we got up at 8.30 shaved cleaned up had our breakfast in the caf.
