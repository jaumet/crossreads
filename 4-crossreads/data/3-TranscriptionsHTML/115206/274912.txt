<p>December  1918.<br/>Sunday 8</p>
<p>Wembley On furlough.<br/>Mrs Rainer came across to see Olive and she stayed all day. <br/>I certainly think considerably of Mrs. R.<br/>Roy wrote a rather sarcastic implicating letter and I answered it with one in a similar tone.<br/>Dear O. is so very tired and she seems to be becoming very queer.</p>
