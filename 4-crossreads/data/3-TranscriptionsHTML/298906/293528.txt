<p>began, all the units got hopelessy mixed together so we let them alone drawing the rations was the next trouble &amp; the 4th Div hopped in &amp; nearly got the lot, &amp; we had to argue till 12 oclock that night before we could get anything for our men to eat, it was a rotten system they worked the indents on that caused it, at any rate we all had short rations that night &amp; went to bed hungry.<br/>7th. Short issue of rations again this morning &amp; plenty of grumbling moved out of this camp at 10.15 &amp; marched down to the railway station there was a great crowd of people in the streets to see us march by, entrained in trucks there were a fair number of German prisoners aboard also</p>
