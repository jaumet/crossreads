 Saturday 8 September 1917. 
 Out at 5 a.m. as usual. Grooming and feeding as usual. 
 Gas parade at 7.30 am. in full kit for&nbsp;&frac12; hr. 
 Generally fooled around till midday. A little time to myself later in day. 
 My dugout companion has gone to Malaleh Beach on duty. Have &quot;Terra Domus&quot; to myself now.
