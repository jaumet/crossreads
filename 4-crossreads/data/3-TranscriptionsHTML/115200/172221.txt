<p>8&nbsp; Dead mans ridge</p>
<p>Catching hold of one of my Dead comrades feet. This made me as if I wanted to vomit. anyhow we reached the top of the Cliff and we started to Dig ourselves in&nbsp; I had to remain on top of the trench all night and the Bullets were coming very fast. how I Escaped getting hit I Don&#39;t Know. in the morning 3<sup>rd</sup> of may I could see The Enemy coming up to attack us&nbsp; Some had Indian turbans round their Head and some had Big Bushes on their Back so as to try and make us think were trees.
