
<p>953</p>
<p>THURSDAY, JANUARY 2, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine but showery towards the</p>
<p>evening. Went out to the bank</p>
<p>with Alan; &amp; round to see Sir</p>
<p>Charles Wade re my getting</p>
<p>back, but could not see him.</p>
<p>Went out &amp; had tea with</p>
<p>Cam Williams. Went to</p>
<p>dinner at the Pic. Grill room</p>
<p>afterwards with the mob.</p>
