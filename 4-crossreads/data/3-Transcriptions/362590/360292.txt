<p>Mr O'Reilly came out to see me at camp this afternoon, &amp; has promised to come &amp; have a chat whenever he feels disposed, which I hope will be fairly often.</p>
<p>There is no news, so I can only make this letter a note to let you know that I am still alive &amp; kicking.</p>
<p>Your loving son</p>
<p>Keith</p>
