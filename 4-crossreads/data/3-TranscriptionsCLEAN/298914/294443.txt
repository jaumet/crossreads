Up to 24th 11/16  Having good time walks along the breakwater and cards all day. 2nd December Sad blow comes - leave hospital for Details Port Said or rather Anzac Rest Camp.  Parade round Port Said and go to pictures. 3/12/16 Receive 10/- and go on the bust meet Ahzeen and later on Mr Lowder missionary who takes us round to his house. 4-12-16 A MEMORIABLE DAY. 5/12/16 Round Port Said and over to see Sister Atkinson at the hospital. 6/12/16 Leave Anzac Rest Camp for Kantara 2nd LH details.  Arrive 2 pm and find some of the Sig Sqn boys guard over stores. 7/12/16 Spend the day writing letters. 8/12/16 Leave Kantara 4 pm for Mazar arriving midnight being met by George Hamilton and Trickett. 9-12-16 Take watch on long wave set with Sturman and receive press from Vienna (OSM) wireless. 10/12/16 Visit Eric & Hopkins and during late afternoon Taube pays a visit.
