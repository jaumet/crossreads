<p>June 25th<br/>News of Jack Fexton's Death &ndash; a supporter &amp; friend of the "Coves" &ndash; Early morning walks Continuation of "Eastern Scene"<br/>Great news from Italian Front &ndash; Austrians rapidly retreating &ndash; Almost a million American Troops now in France June 26th<br/>Good news from Italian Front Continues &ndash; Austrians driven back over the [indecipherable] &ndash; Major Berry (our new Commandant) has occasion to speak to the Boys.</p>
