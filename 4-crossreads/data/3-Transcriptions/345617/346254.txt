<p>Novembre 28<br/>Part of my Section working in camp &amp; I took the remainder out at night to work on a strong point near our Piquet Line. Nothing of great importance to note. We were badly tested while working last night by machine gun bullets but sustained no casualties.</p>
<p>Novembre 29<br/>Part of the Sec. working in camp &amp; part went out as last night. I returned early as I have to be out early in the morning to go &amp; give evidence in a case Court Martial.</p>
