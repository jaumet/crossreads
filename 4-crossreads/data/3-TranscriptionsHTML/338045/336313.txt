<p>&amp;spent some time looking round this really ancient &amp; famous spot.  Coins, old china of the Romans, Roman walls, statues &amp; baths.  Fronts of houses of the 14th Century carried wholly into the garden of this place, rare specimens of Henry VIII &amp; other English Kings &amp; I left well pleased.  Legge &amp; I afterwards found the old tower in which Joan of Arc was imprisoned but, being closed we did not gain admittance.  However, we had a good view of it &amp; the moat, &amp; walked around many curious ancient streets</p>
