
June 1918Monday 10
Battery W.Lines.  near Pont NoyellesWeather dull, heavy rains. No swim today.Charlie Adey a little better. Received a long letter from Len Clements, - quite adecent one.Practiced for concert, postponed until Thursday owing theavy rains. At 10pm Fritz dropped his barrage, lasted until 11.10 pm. A beautiful display of fireworks.Regst. Another 40 francs to O.J. Expect to go out for aspell very shortly.Had a decent yap with Percy. Posted art studies from LaVie Parision to M.J.Wh. also wrote Ead.
