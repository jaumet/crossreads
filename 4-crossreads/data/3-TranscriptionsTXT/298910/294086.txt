 Tuesday  7/11/16 
 Rain off &amp; on &amp; very cold &ndash; blowing a treat &ndash; Road contract hangs on &ndash; while excavating at Albert today, I found amongst the bricks etc a little afternoon tea cup &amp; egg cup to match &ndash; strange to say they were unbroken &ndash; so I will try &amp; send them on to Laurie Bourke. A fair number of wounded coming in. Very poor rations all day. 
 Wednesday  8/11/16 
 Trying hard to be fine &ndash; Were relieved from the Road Contract about 9 am &ndash; came back to our own Ambulance, was told off to No 3 Preparatory Tent, here we have to cut all clothes away from wounded &amp; check any belongings &ndash; Started to send a few cards for Xmas &ndash; could only get one class in Albert &ndash; our tired Officers will only allow us to send one card away per day.
