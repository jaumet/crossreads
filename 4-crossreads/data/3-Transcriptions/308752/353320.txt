<p>Monday 28th February to Sunday 5th March<br/>(Monday) Startling orders read out on parade by the Colonel, we are to start our training all over again, right from the beginning again, from A to Z. this was very disheartening news for the men. So we started with squad drill by <u>numbers</u> in the scorching sun, saluting, &amp; right &amp; left turns by <u>numbers</u>, kept at this all the week. It was God send when the week end arrived, so as to have a day &amp; a half spell from this very monotonous training.</p>
<p>Monday 6th March.<br/>71 horses &amp; 8 ambulance wagons arrived to day, we certainly looks like an Ambulance Corp now, &amp; it is about time to. Weather gradually becoming very hot.</p>
<p>Tuesday 7th March to Wednesday 15th March.<br/>Monotonous training changed a little by wagon drill every day. The first drill of this kind since leaving Melbourne. You cannot imagine how sick &amp; tired everyone is of the whole affair.
