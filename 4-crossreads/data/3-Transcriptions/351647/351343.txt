<p>It seems funny to hear the old guns going again but so far no shells have arrived anywhere near here.  Touch wood.</p>
<p>I heard there were some casualties at one of our Batteries today &amp; I'm rather anxious as I have a couple of pals there.  But the wire isn't working so I shall have to wait till tomorrow &amp; then stroll up &amp; see what's happened.</p>
<p>By the way when you write next address me c/- 5th Australian <u>Artillery</u> Brigade.  At present all the letters without Artillery go to Infantry &amp; its a toss up if I <u>ever</u> get them.  At present I'm missing quite lots of your letters &amp; I can't afford to do that.</p>
<p>I was so glad to get your two tonight 37 &amp; 38.  I'm sorry you hadn't heard from me that mail.  But don't be too hard on me.  You are evidently having a trying time.  I was afraid mosquitos &amp; fly would be bad this year on account of the moisture.
