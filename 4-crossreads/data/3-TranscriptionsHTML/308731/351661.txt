<p>Cronulla<br/>12.1.1918.</p>
<p>Dear Dad.</p>
<p>Mum has bort me a little bike to ride to school on.  on Saturday and I will be glad when school starts and Ruth has got a Segent Mother. <strike>to.</strike> and I hope you have a nice time. And I am getting lots of blackberrys and my legs area chacht to bits so I cannot go In to the water.</p>
<p>Much love from frank<br/>xxx etc.</p>
