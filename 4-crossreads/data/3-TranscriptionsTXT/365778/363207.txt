
Sunday 15/7/17Church Parade - Bas had cable from Doff - went for ride with Bas & Martin.Monday 16/7/17Field Firing, new horse arrived named him "Banjo" - meeting of C.O.'s.Tuesday 17/7/17Wrote to Doff. Company Training.Wednesday 18/7/17Route March - Full order 111/2 miles - very good work Via Transloy - Hapincourt.Thursday 19/7/17Attack practice - Trench to Trench conducted by Currie.Friday 20/7/17Field Firing morning, night stunt meeting of C.O.s.
