<p>Friday July 13<br/>Handed in all surplus kit to day. Saw Camelian to day. Funny little animals something like a lizard. Peculiar point is that they can change colour. Useful as fly catcher. Egypt commonly called land of Flies, Sand &amp; Dirt. All well<br/>Saturday July 14<br/>Usual routine. Hot windy day<br/>Sunday July 15th<br/>Isolation lifted today. Now we shan't be long.<br/>Monday July 16<br/>Leave to Ismalia today Tom Charley, Jimmy &amp; my self went in</p>
