<p>Sunday, 8/7/17<br/>Up 7 a.m. &amp; while at breakfast was taken sick.  Had to be relieved from Runner job &amp; laid down all day.  Could not eat any dinner or tea.  About 7 p.m. got up &amp; had tea &amp; cheese sandwich at canteen.</p>
<p>Monday, 9/7/17<br/>Feeling better all day.  Skirmishing on top of hill all day, having dinner there.  Rained for &frac12; hour or so.  After tea went to Fovant &amp; got change from grocery shop.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 10/7/17<br/>Very sick all night &amp; paraded sick to Dr. at 7 a.m.  Got dose castor oil.  Went top of hill again for skirmishing but had to leave at dinner time as got very sick again.</p>
<p>Wednesday, 11/7/17<br/>Not feeling too good on awakening.</p>
