
March 1918Monday 18
Messines2 am. Fritz throwing them about rather liberally, very few in our direction.Another glorious day.Washed all the towels in the dugout.O.B.W., returned to W.L. Laurence arrived up at position.Fritz did some good ranging at 53rd and roadway, with H.E.s and 4.2 air bursts.11pm. Fritz shelled road with gas, and 5.9 H.E.s.Grayling out at O.P. Had a F.P.C. from A.D.S. who imparts information that he is well.Wrote R.L. and Mum
