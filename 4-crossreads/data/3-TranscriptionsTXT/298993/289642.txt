 leave here now. 1.P.M issued with rations nice warm day. am waiting for the camels now. 2.PM camels have arrived loaded up and went back a mile went down into a waddie. I was detailed to load five camels away, got a start at 4.30 P.M  trecked as far as Bir-Bola. a distance of five miles which place we reached 7.30.PM. boiled billy had tea turned in 9.P.M cold night Monday.
