 At last my diary is up to date & I am thankful 
 20-1-1919 (Mon) This day we hear from Colonel in an address to a muster parade in this grand place the scheme of demobilisation & during afternoon I wash & have a wash in the shower which consist of 5 holes in a pipe. However it is something. Scotson has gone & I am unable to get a fire alright so freeze while my washing remains wet. 
 21-1-1919 (Tues) Usual routine - afternoon Scotson room in warm sunshine. The country is delightful when the sun is shining. This is now late in January & there has been no sign of snow as yet. 
 The style of house architecture is curious & involves a large expenditure of [cash without] The back of house is always in front & often obscures a pretty building. The rear of house is always a garden enclosed with a strong high brick wall.
