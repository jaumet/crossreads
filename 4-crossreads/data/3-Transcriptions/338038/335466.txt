<p>[Printed letter-head]<br/>Commonwealth of Australia<br/>Governor-General<br/>Melbourne, 3rd May, 1918.</p>
<p>Dear Madam,<br/>I am desired by His Excellency the Governor-General to transmit to you the following message from Their Majesties The  King and Queen:-<br/>"The King and Queen deeply regret the loss you and the Army have sustained by the death of your son in the service of his Country.<br/>Their Majesties truly sympathise with you in your sorrow."<br/>In addition to the above message, I am desired by the Governor-General to express their Excellencies' heartfelt sympathy with you on the death of this gallant officer.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>Geo Steward<br/>Lt.-Col.<br/>Official Secretary</p>
<p>Mrs. W. Garling,<br/>Burns Bay Road,<br/>Longueville,<br/>N.S.W.</p>
