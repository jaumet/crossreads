20.1.15
Wed 20thHad a very heavy dew the night before last &amp; last night &amp; the nights are very cold &amp; quite chilly in the morningsThe Brigadier came along &amp; inspected us while we were working.Tucker from B Sqd. started with the signallers this morning.
Dawes the Armourer Sergeant was found guilty of breaking arrest &amp; was sentenced to the ranks; this morning his stripes were taken off before the Regiment: he was tried by court-martial, on several charges.  the only one he was found guilty on, was for breaking arrest, but he has been giving trouble all along &amp; has been in the guard room most of the time on the boat
