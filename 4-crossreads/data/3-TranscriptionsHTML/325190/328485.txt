This afternoon I participated in a game of football as half back, &amp; thoroughly enjoyed &amp; fatigued myself.<br/>Two days ago I was approached by Tom Smith to help form a concert party &amp; in lieu of a better I am to be pianist.  That afternoon I went with Scotty MacNoll &amp; Tom Smith &amp; Les Bennett to Longvillers to try to find a piano.  We were <strike>put off</strike> unable to find one in that village, but were told to try Madmoiselle</p>
