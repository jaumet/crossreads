 Friday, 12 January 1917 
 [This sentence crossed through]  Posted an old Pay Book &amp; some photos to you today. 
 A few wounded have died. I'll give you more particulars later on. We had no time to get anything except Rifles as Turkish Reinforcements are all coming up &amp; we were 20 miles from food &amp; water &amp; first dark, so we collected our Prisoners &amp; guns &amp; got [The following crossed through] 1470 Prisoners counted 120 Prisoners wounded 200 buried in field &amp; left behind. [total] 1790. Bombed again today &amp; the broken shells from our Anti Aircraft fell around us. Our BUS always arrives too late &amp; we have not the supremacy of the air here YET.
