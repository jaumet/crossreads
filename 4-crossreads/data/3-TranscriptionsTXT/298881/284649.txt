 Swanage 10.9.17 Arrived here yesterday in company of two of my Australian girl friends from Hendon.  Had a bon sleep this morning and rose at 7.30.  Had breakfast with the ladies at 8, went for a stroll along the sea front at 9 and afterwards took a carriage to Studland.
