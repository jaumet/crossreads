<p>Saturday [19/10/'18]<br/>Off 3-8, drove Cairo more dead than alive, very weak &amp; health cond. not good &ndash; anaemic looking.<br/>Sanitary tl's Pt. 15 a doz.!!<br/>Clothes pegs for papers for Capt. Jobson.</p>
<p>Sunday, 20th<br/>Letter from Coles asking about H.F., wrote Mother.  Off 2-5, rested &amp; did washing.</p>
<p>Monday<br/>Capt. Loddiges doing our ward, very smart man.  All pts. put on Diaphoretic 4 hrly.  May get up when temp. 98.4 for 48 hrs. then iron &amp; arsenic tonic.  Convoys still roll in &amp; no abatement in the death toll here or up the line.</p>
<p>Tuesday 22nd<br/>Busy day as usual.  I ask for S. Dowie's day, crushed at once.  Obtain tonic, cough clings hard after "flu" &ndash; very white-faced for me.  4 out of its 20 Indian sisters still at 27th, they don't like it, spoiled as our 16 are by India.</p>
