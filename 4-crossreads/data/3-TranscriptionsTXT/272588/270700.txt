
Thursday 2 MayThe Boche has been fairly quiet again to-day but what few he has sent over have landed close to our billett, "duds" everyone.  Very warm. Playing cricket all day.Hankin wounded up the line this morning.
Friday 3 MayStill on the Northern outskirts of Hazebrouck, doing little else but play cricket all day.Beautiful weather.  A walk to Hondenghem this evening.
