  
 June 1918 Tuesday 25 
 In Billets. Erronville Weather fine and clean Usual routine of stables etc. watered near by.  Walked with Smithy to Camoun through Bailleul. Enjoyed a decent feed in a decent shack. Village about 10 Kilos distant.  From top of hill  [indecipherable] flashes of bombs [indecipherable] About Flexicourt way. Received parcel from Dot Watson also one from O.J.
