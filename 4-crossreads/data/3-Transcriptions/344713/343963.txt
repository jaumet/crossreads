<p>Sat. 27th Oct: -<br/>Marched from Signal School at Aveluy through Bouzincourt, &amp; Hedauville to Varennes &amp; entrained to Candas. After having a bit of an evening there, got train to Abbeville &amp; stayed night at Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Sun. 28th. Oct.<br/>Train from Abbeville to Etaples &amp; spent afternoon at Paris-Plage, a little seaside resort a few miles out by electric tram.</p>
<p>Mon. 29th Oct.<br/>Train from Etaples through Boulogne, Calais, St Omer, Hazebrouck &amp; on to Caestre where we put up at C2 Camp of the Anzac Refts Camp. Don't know whether we are to rejoin the Bn. which is just before Ypres or to wait on their coming back.</p>
<p>Wed. 31st, Oct,<br/>Left Caestre by train to Poperinghe &amp; stopped night with our transport near Cafe Belge.<br/>Battalion has just moved up to railway dugouts the other side of Ypres.</p>
<p>Thurs. Nov. 1st. 1917<br/>Left transport &amp; went through Ypres out of Menin Gate, past Hill 59 where B &amp; D Coys are in tunnel &amp; then on to Westhoek Ridge where Hqrs are in an old Pill Box.</p>
