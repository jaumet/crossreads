<p>and, I fear swearing in about equal parts, knowing that I had to get up at midnight  &amp;  every was lost if not asleep.<br/>However I got in about three hours  &amp;  then came on here our section being Camel escort which duty consists in driving the camels  &amp;  saying "iggerre"  &amp;  "Etla &ndash;a-a Ia-el Kel-p-p" to the natives.<br/>Reached here about 4.30 am  &amp;  found everyone but the Camel escort had been  in bed an hour - very  amusing.<br/>This is a very pretty little grove now on account of the ripening</p>
