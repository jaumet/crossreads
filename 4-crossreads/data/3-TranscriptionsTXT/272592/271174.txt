 To-day Len has been gazetted full Corporal &amp; he should have had it long ago but for pure accident 
 I have withdrawn my Platoon from the firing line &amp; am now in "supports  We are still dropping bombs &amp; shells on old Fritz &amp; rolling his barb wire up lovely, they are making a good job of it. 
 I done a bit of firing last night with a Lewis gun they are little bonzer's &amp; very light &amp; deadly. 
 Got a quantity of stuff up from the Bde canteen this morning for we run one almost in the firing line &amp; it is a great success
