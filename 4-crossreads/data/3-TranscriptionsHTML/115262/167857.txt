<p>Saturday 31 March 1917.</p>
<p>In camp. Duty again. This time Sanitary fatigue. Did washing in afternoon. Detailed to give A.M.C. a hand in the ward. Everyone going crook at this caper. Trust me I did very little work in the ward.</p>
<p>After 7 p.m. turned in early.</p>
<p>(Met Corp. Gray (cook) at Gar<strike>r</strike>isson in evening.)</p>
<p>L. J Laversha tentmate transferred to Base for treatment.</p>
