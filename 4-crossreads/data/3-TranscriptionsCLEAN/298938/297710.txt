  
 Saturday 16th Sept 1916 On Tuesday I started work at the dispensary I have to keep a few stock mixtures supplied, make up special prescriptions for the hospital, & other things such as ointments. There is not a great deal of dispensing to do, & I am under the Corpl. L/C --- Cap. Binger    There are three others who attend to the dressings; among them a Lance Corpl.
