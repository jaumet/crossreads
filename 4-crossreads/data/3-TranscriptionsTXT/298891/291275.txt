 1917 July 
 11 Wednesday 
 Dropped anchor in Corfu about breakfast time. Very near to the land. Rode at anchor till dinner time &amp; then got fouled on a buoy &amp; had to be disentangled by [indecipherable] the Jap. We &amp; our Italian companion pulled port again with the Japs Q &amp; R. racing ahead of us like a pair of lively terriers. &amp; a little French boat capering hither &amp; yon. During afternoon the Japs had target practice. Two creatures put at our table one a jackass &amp; the other a [indecipherable]
