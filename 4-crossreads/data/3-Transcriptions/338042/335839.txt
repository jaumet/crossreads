<p>Mar.29th (Good Friday).<br/>A day off from duty -. The solemnity of London &ndash; A day of serious devotion &amp; sincere worship &ndash; Denomination quite rightly thrown to the winds &ndash; Visit friends at Wood Green &ndash; Return "Elm Bank" 8 p.m. and meet &amp; enjoy company of Mr. &amp; Mrs and Misses Sargent &ndash; English beauty &amp; refinement.<br/>Mar.30th (Easter Saturday) Renewal at Ambassadors &ndash; Most successful shows of the week &ndash; Many friends present at matinee &ndash; Meet &amp; have dinner in town</p>
