
14. ThursdayWorked at removing things from drawing room a.m. at 3 met Nell at Kew & had tea down there. It rained I got wet & sat 20 min on open station. Result:- rigor, temp, malaria.
15. FridayIn bed with malaria all day. Temp. Not able to see Nell off.
16. SaturdayUp again but feeling desperately depressed & weak.Fogs for days
