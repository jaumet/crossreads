<p>17) No 2. Auxiliary Hospital Cairo</p>
<p>Egypt</p>
<p>26/9/15</p>
<p>today has been very quiet&nbsp; There has been Church Service twice. I am still in Hospital at Heliopoli. to day there is a rumour going round that Bulgaria has [got?] in with the Central powers. This is rotten news as I thought She would come in on our Side and now that She has gone against us it remains to Be Seen if we can [Lose?] the Dardanelles or not. We will certainly have a greater task than Ever in front of us now. Than Ever. Britain have in</p>
