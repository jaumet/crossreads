<p>Monday 19 August<br/>Glorious weather still.  Walk along the river almost to Corbie &amp; it is very pretty flanked with poplar trees.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20 August<br/>Glorious day &amp; still at Vaux sur Somme, where we daily indulge in a swim in the river.<br/>Glorious moonlit night. We move up again shortly.</p>
