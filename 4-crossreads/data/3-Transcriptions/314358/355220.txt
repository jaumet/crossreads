<p>21</p>
<p>Am well<br/>Dont worry about me for all is well and all will be well.</p>
<p>I will write to the children when time allows</p>
<p>They must be growing now and will soon smile when I call them children</p>
<p>There is so much to write about if one could only do so without thinking of the Censor</p>
<p>So much is retained in my mental note book, and so much more lost by forgetting</p>
<p>I shall write to my kind friend Walter when I can</p>
