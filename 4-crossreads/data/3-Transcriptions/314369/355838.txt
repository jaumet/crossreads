<p>4 June 1915<br/>We hear this morning the attack has been successful but we can get no details.</p>
<p>1915 June 5<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>"C" Coy detailed for inlying piquet, nobody being allowed to leave the lines &amp; to be ready to fall in at a minutes notice.<br/>Nothing doing all day.</p>
