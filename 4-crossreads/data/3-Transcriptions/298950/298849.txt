So the members belonging to this Socity wher allways informed of the Outside World.  One fine Sunday afternoon ,,The International Workers of the World" Members, inside the Compound called a open General Meeting ,,Every body wellcome".  This Meeting only had a short live, for it hardly had started, when the Military Police came on the Scene arrested the 3 Oraters &amp; ordered the Internees to disband, wich they of course dit, singing I.W.W. songs.  The 3 Oraters wher never permitted to enter our Camp again, but wher keept interned in the formerly explained ,,Singh-Singh".  During the later part of 1916 we noticed that our food got far inferior quality &amp; less quality, instead of Potatoes we recieved mostly Rice for dinner, sometimes Peas or Beans.  Every week we got about 2 smal potatoes each Internee, ones.  During May 1916 the whole Internees, under orders of the Military had their photos taken.  My turn wher May 21.
