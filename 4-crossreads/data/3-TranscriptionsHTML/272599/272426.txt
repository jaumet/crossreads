<p>1917 Tuesday 5 June</p>
<p>Sleepin till 7 a.m. After breakfast go out all morning  Seek a shady green dell away from everyone &amp; enjoy a lovely old laze. Read &amp; write. A beautiful quiet &amp; peaceful morning. Flowers, butterflies, birds, frisky little foals. War seemed an unheard thing. <br/>Back to dinner. On duty at 2.30 till 10 p.m.</p>
