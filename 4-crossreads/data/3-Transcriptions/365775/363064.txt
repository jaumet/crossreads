<p>Now a little of the general surroundings &amp; people of France.-<br/>Solely a farming community carried on by the women &amp; old men of France, to the same extent as in peace time almost. The harvest just now is in full swing, a pretty picture to stand at the window of our billet, which is upstairs, &amp; look across the fields, with here &amp; there loaded waggon of hay, a group of girls tieing up the sheaves, the sun setting in the west,</p>
