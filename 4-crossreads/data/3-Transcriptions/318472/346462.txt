<p>25 August 1918<br/>Sunday<br/>13 After Trinity</p>
<p>A beautiful fine day with a very cool breeze blowing. Good War news. British advancing capturing Albert &amp; gathering in 14,000 prisoners. About 10.30 A.M. one of the Mental cases ran amok &amp; climbed to the cross-head on the mast a height of about 50' A couple of men went up after him &amp; after a short struggle he fell striking on of the winches &amp; was killed instantly. Buried in the afternoon!</p>
<p>1918 August 26<br/>Monday<br/>Nothing unusual to note. Good War news continues to come through. Capt. Hunter late of Mawsons Arctic Expedition gave a Lecture with Lantern illustrations Interesting. Making good progress with fine weather.</p>
