<p>February 1918<br/>Sunday 24</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Had a fairly quiet day, and visited Gunn, and Da Costa at Anti Tank position.<br/>Garfott on F.O.O. my turn tomorrow.<br/>Fritz shelled the Corner Dump like hell for an hour<br/>Received letters  from Mrs Ladd, M, Mother and Uncle Graham the former via Mr [indecipherable]<br/>Weather dull, but with clear patches. Great aeriel activity.<br/>Won five francs form Russell.  Laurence returned form Leave.</p>
