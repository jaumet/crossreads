 We were not long getting over to Kehopalos as this part of Imbros is called. It was dark &ndash; I soon found Eltham a Gunner from our Brigade who was over with Captn Edwards as I said before in my notes days ago. I made myself comfortable in his tent &ndash; got blankets etc. 
 Friday 3rd December To-day I made up my list &amp; as Lieut Sparrow in charge of our Party was going to Panaghia I arranged with him to include me in his party on the pass which had to be obtained.
