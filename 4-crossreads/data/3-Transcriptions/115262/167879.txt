<p>Sunday 22 April 1917.</p>
<p>In camp &amp; on fatigue duty. On A.M.C. QM&#39;s fatigue.</p>
<p>A little spare time in afternoon.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Went down to service at the lines in evening.</p>
<p>Wrote 2 letters in afternoon &amp; did some mending &amp; sewing. Soon got tired &amp; assisted the Mess orderlies.</p>
<p>More washing done</p>
