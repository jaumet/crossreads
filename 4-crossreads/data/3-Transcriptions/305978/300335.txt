<p>Cairo &amp; the train was rushed by a crowd of niggers selling all kinds of fruit &amp; cigarettes.<br/>The noise of them shouting out oringars &amp; other pecular names. I shall never forget.<br/>There was no tucker for us when we arrived at the camp so we had to go into Heliopolis for a feed &amp; the niggers did try &amp; do us on the prices as we had English money.<br/>We learnt next day that we were not reconized for 24 hrs so we again to Heliopolis for breakfast &amp; then proceeded to see this wicked Cairo that we had heard so much of.
