<p>1915<br/>Jan. 22 Fri.<br/>Next troop letter commence from here No. 5.<br/>Kept busy on clerical work from about 6.15 a.m. to 11.15 p.m. with intervals for meals.<br/>As men were scarce for camp picquet did 3 hour shift. Turned into bed  (i.e. blankets) at 10 minutes to 4.a.m. (23rd)<br/>Food poor quality and scarce &ndash; Men worked very hard night and day - Lot of grumbling going on.</p>
