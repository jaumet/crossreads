 20/2/19 Cologne express and got into our bunks. Slept comfortably all night and then awoke to find that we were out of Germany and nearing Liege. Namur we passed about midday and ran down to Charleroi about 4.30 pm. At my billet I found a good Aust. Mail waiting and soon settled down again. 
 21/2/19 Marcinelle A quiet day. In the morning walked round the billets talking to different folk and for the remainder of the day read and wrote. There was some rain after dinner and a walk into Charleroi revealed nothing much except that the conduct of some of the officers at the ERC Club leaves a good deal to be desired. This town is very well lit by electricity.
