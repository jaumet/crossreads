
BUZINCOURTFriday 15Awakend from a sound sleep by a gas alarm early this mornCamped between two hills "Dolce Far Niente"Saturday 16We are the reserve division. Summer Sun good rations, & beautiful nights. Lifes worth living. Plenty of "baloons aboutSunday 17Not a single shot fired on this front for 48 hours there must be something in the air for tis very unusual, either a calm before the next storm or peace terms in view
