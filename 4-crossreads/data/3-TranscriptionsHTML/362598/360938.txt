<p>Sunday 13 August 1916<br/>General Duties.  Dave, Jeff C. &amp; myself go to see the Tennis Finals at Cairo.  We end up at Luna Park.</p>
<p>Monday 14 August 1916<br/>I go for a route march for the first time all round Heliopolis.  It is no good &amp; I get back hot, tired &amp; dopey.  Wrote to Os. Merrett.  Join Tennis Club.</p>
<p>Tuesday 15 August 1916<br/>Pay Day.  Wrote Dad (63).  Wrote Mum (64), Hilda, Mrs. Whitehead.  Night Ward Master 11 p.m.</p>
<p>Wednesday 16 August 1916<br/>Take Patients to Cairo at 6 a.m.  Boys go on march.  Wrote to Mrs. Stephen, May Coppin &amp; 2 Sydney Views to Jennie Lawton.  I play Cards all night with B. E. T. &amp; myself.</p>
<p>Thursday 17 August 1916<br/>Leave all day.  Buy Ink Stand in Mouski &amp; play Billiards with D.  Get badly beaten by D.  Tea at Petrograd with E. &amp; D. Lord.</p>
<p>Friday 18 August 1916<br/>Go off General Duties.  Start night Duties from 8 p.m. to 7 a.m.  We all move over to Sydney Pav. with the Bugs.  Sleep between 3 &amp; 4 &amp; 4.20 &amp; 5.  Wake all time.  Wrote Dot.</p>
<p>Saturday 19 August 1916<br/>Go off Duty at 7 a.m.  Sleep? till noon.  Sargents have a bust up till 11.30.  I sneak 4 hours sleep.  All Sergeants well oiled.</p>
