  
 March 1918 Friday 22 
 Messines Spent the day at F.O.P. Did a shoot during afternoon. Weather fine but misty. Fritz put down heavy barrage on the Warncton Sector. News came to hand per Major to the effect that Fritz had recaptured Bulle and Lagnicourt Had tea and sujpper with some fellows of the Wireless section at right Battaln. I did L'aison with "Dope" Uneventful night. Created a record for duckboard trotting.
