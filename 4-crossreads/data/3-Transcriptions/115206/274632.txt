<p>March 1918<br/>Sunday 3</p>
<p>Messines<br/>During the gale, in early hours of the morning, one of our wires went. Found it necessary to repair same toutes suite. Left at 4.20 pm arrived back at 6.30 am. A great quantity of snow fell during the night.<br/>Went to Bdge Hdqs for pigeons and returned with two birds. &ndash; blue checkers, which are used in carrying work when lines go.<br/>Stunts in all sectors last night at 11.15pm., beautiful display of fireworks. Our battery was in the Barrage. Work. Have not yet heard how things progressed.</p>
