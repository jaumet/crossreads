 1915 Mar. 30 Tue. Went into Cairo in the afternoon &amp; got from Post Office a parcel containing Two novels Two prs. socks 1 diary toothbrush Cake chocolate 
 How glad I was to get hold of it &amp; how decent of those at home to think of me. 
 Also had a haircut &amp; went for a walk through Esbekieh Gardens. Beautiful moonlight night again.
