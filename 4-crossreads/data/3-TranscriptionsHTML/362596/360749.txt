<p>so of course I had to carry on with the good work but we done very well and we got compliments for it so that is very nice to know.<br/>We are out of the line now resting and having sports meeting but the sports are very tame as you might easily guess, but it breaks the monotony of war.<br/>We had one fellow out of this Battalion go to Aust. on leave this morning, I wouldn't mind taking his place for 6 months, what a time one would have.<br/> Well May I will have to ring off for this time. Trusting you all are enjoying the best of health as this scrawl has me at present.<br/>I am<br/>Your Aff. Uncle<br/>Clarence</p>
