<p>Sunday 25<br/>Last night the 33rd had a raid, the Barrage was put on about 10.30 &amp; was very solid.  The raid was successful they say, but we lost about 6 men killed &amp; 20 or so casualties.  They say they found the bosch fastened to wires on his front line.  (Not true 27th)<br/>I have been at Houplines today to the cocoa stall.<br/>There was a good service at the Y.M.C.A. &amp; we had Sacrament afterward to which over 100 men stayed.  The weather is foggy, clearing occasionally &amp; not very cold.</p>
<p>Monday 26<br/>I went to Div. Baths this morning &amp; had a nice hot bath.  Brigade this afternoon where I told them of having lost my bicycle.<br/>I went to Houplines this evening.  There was a big raid on in which nearly 800 men</p>
