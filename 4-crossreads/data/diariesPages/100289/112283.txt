Saturday 11.8.17
Full day route march to Arques back then on through Racquinghem home about 4.30 pm.
Sunday 12.8.17
Leave to St Omer, spent rather a good day, town crowded, band played in the park during afternoon; returned via Ronesque 
Monday 13.8.17
Second Anniversary of formation of unit. Sports programme carried through concert at night; Leave warrant for Blighty came through. Rode to Blaringhem to endeavour to get a new rig out but failed, given authority to get same at Horseferry road London.
Leave to-morrow.
