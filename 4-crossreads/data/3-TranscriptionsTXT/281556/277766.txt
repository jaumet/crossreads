 Bgde. left Gully about 9pm. &amp; bivouaced some 3 miles back but were scattered by shellfire about midnight &amp; made for trenches at hand  y  where we slept till morning. One man was killed within 6 yds of where I lay &amp; all I knew was the feeling of a shower of dirt. Never heard the report I had well dosed myself with Rum before retiring &amp; was surely doped &amp; dead sleepy with same. 
 6th &ndash; Sunday. Bgde reviewed by Gen. Birdwood midday. Making up Coy Casualty
