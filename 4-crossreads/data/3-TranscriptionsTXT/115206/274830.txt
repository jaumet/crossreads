September 1918.Tuesday 17
Bouvincourt and Bty Position nr. Poeuilly.Weather, very heavy storm accompanied by the heaviest rain I have seen in France. Had a fair pickle with tarpaulins.Left with guns for Bty Position at 6.15pm. and arrived in action at 10pm.Fritz planes across our lines all day.I have never seen so many Fritzies across at one timea regular series of showers of bombs. Very few shells and heavy concentration of artillery around the district.
