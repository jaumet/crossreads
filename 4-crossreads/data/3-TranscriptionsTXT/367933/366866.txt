
POSTCARD  18/1/17
Am writing to acknowledge receipt of parcel sent through your Depot to me containing food stuffs, milk, cheese,  asparagies etc, which reached me safely and in good condition - I would also like to take the opportunity ---You all that is good in this New Year which I hope will see us home again
Yours sincerelyCpl A Metcalfe  1030B Squadron1st Regiment 1st A.L.H.BrigadeEgypt
