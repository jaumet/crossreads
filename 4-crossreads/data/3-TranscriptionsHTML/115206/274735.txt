<p>June 1918<br/>Friday 14</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather changing, laid up throughout day and last night. Feel very stiff and sore through being in bed.<br/>D.A.C. and H.A.C.sports commenced. Line went under pressure of shell mended same<br/>Received no mail at all. Smithy and Ken Gunn still hors-de-combat at Guns.<br/>Mr Mann arrived from guns looking like a hero. Boils over his face and head caused the decoration. Mr Ken down for the afternoon.</p>
