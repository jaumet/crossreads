<p>Thursday July 17<br/>Some of the Light Horse Reinforcements leaving to join units. Since we left Ausy we have lost 10 of our unit (medically unfit &amp; in hospital accidents)<br/>Wednesday July 18<br/>Inoculation ParaTyphoid 24 hrs off duty wrote letters.<br/>Thursday July 19<br/>Mail in today. Disappointed only Victorian letters.<br/>Friday July 20<br/>Usual routine<br/>Saturday July 21<br/>On night duty some of the boys gone into Ismalia.<br/>Sunday July 22<br/>Heard we are moving about Wednesday. Will be glad to get out of here.</p>
