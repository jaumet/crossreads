<p>The Brigade has just returned from a 5 days Bivouac.<br/>We of the reinforcements did not go. I managed to have a fairly easy time but unfortunately got snapped for guard one night.</p>
<p>One of our chaps refused to do it. used a lot of obscene language to an officer &amp; will very probably get sent back to Aust, with other who have become medically unfit &ndash; Cairo is a hot bed of certain diseases &amp; has been for years, one can't be two careful here, we are told.</p>
