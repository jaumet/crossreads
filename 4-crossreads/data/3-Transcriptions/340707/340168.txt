<p>spend the night at Hotel du Centre near Porte di St Denis near which a little girl has been singing for my especial benefit. "Pres de la porte de St Denis till I imagine that I am among my beloved isles again with Mdlle Bozon.</p>
<p>24-1-19<br/>After turning in about 6 am this morning I remain in bed till 3 pm &amp; then visit Bank to see if my money is across &amp; find that I am still without pay &amp; may be stranded any day.</p>
<p>I then dine at our little restaurant &amp; then go a dance organised for American officers by the French Homes. After being without a partner I am introduced a lady with a [indecipherable] &amp; Mdlle Madelon Le Neef Rue de la Sorborne. She is a dental student &amp; after a dance or two we adjourn to the garden in the house which was once the home of Napoleon physician &amp; then the residence of M. Ribot.</p>
<p>We talk over philosophic matters &amp; become firm friends I accompany her home &amp; then we decide to visit the town &amp; see if there is any excitement We have a very pleasant promenade &amp; then walk home</p>
<p>The town is still crowded</p>
