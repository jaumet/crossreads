<p>Sunday 29 April 1917</p>
<p>HISEA</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]  2 Letters from Mother 19/3/17, Day JOE Auntie, all very welcome &amp; with me still, 19/3/17 Jim Ayre told me of GORDONS engagement also heard from Winnie MILLS. Into all home news &amp; thank you for cutting of Paper also Vegetables to come &ndash; in Parcel.</p>
<p>Am posting this today as do not want it to fall into enemy hands.</p>
<p>[The following crossed through to end of page.] Love to all, your son, Frank.</p>
<p>I am wondering if this Diary will see the war out &ndash; better post me one about 1st August I think.</p>
