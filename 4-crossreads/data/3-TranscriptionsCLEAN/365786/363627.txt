  
 There the line  branches  divides, one branch going to Suez the other to Cairo.  On the way we passed numbers of Indian troops both cavalry & infantry encamped & entrenched.  At Kantara we saw signs of the early fighting for it was here the Turks made their abortive attempt to cross the Canal.  We saw the trenches & dugouts, the sandbag barricades & the station protected by earthworks & more sandbags.  We also saw the dummy gun hear here.
