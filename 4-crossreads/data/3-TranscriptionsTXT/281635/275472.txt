 Wrote to Auntie Effie &amp; Minnie Luscombe. 24/6/18 Recd 2 parcels &ndash; one containing 1 pr Brown Sox, &amp; 1 ctn Griffiths Chocolate, &amp; other Cake, Biscuits, Sardines, Strawberry Conserve, Pineapple, Tomato Soup, Chocolate &amp; Milk &amp; Butterscotch. 25/6/18 Receiving wounded today who were in a stunt near Meteren during which our people advanced. Wrote to Muriel. Raining today.
