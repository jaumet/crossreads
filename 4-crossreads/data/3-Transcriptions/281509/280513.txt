<p>17 October 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Up at 8 a.m. Breakfast. Stroll up town &amp;amp down road. Parade at 10 a.m  Vote on Conscription &ndash; YES.  Fancy the Infantry will poll a big majority NO   Rest all day. Look Tony up in afternoon &amp; visit his possie after tea &amp; yarn. Up to our hay loft early. Bob not to well</p>
<p>Bob gets small parcel from home</p>
