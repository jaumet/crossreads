<p>Serg Carmody again sinks in the "bluff stakes" but finds he has spoilt his own little game.  He has been seen slightly on the booze, that he gets for making us carry stores for the stewards. He was caught nicely in the cookhouse with doors locked, mopping up whisky. He consequently finds the wind out of his sails.</p>
