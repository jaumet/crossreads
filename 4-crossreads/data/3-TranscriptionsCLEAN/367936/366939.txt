  
 Saturday 26 February 1916 Dick plowing in Front pdk. till dinner time.  I went over to Barham and got "Tiger" and 2 sets of harness. Father & Geoff fixed hoist for wheat bag.  In afternoon we worked at seed drill putting axel in.  Cool day. 
 Sunday 27 February 1916 Ten horses to feed.  Fixed seed drill and took 2 furrow plow to Front pdk.  Got cart and took bags down and covered up wheat from rain. Had rest after dinner then fixed plow and had a hit at pots. Easterly wind with clouds.
