
Sunday 11 June 1916Church.  Major Morris enjoys the Photos & decorations in my ward.  Billy goes on Parade in full rig like the Camel Corps.  Wrote to Mrs. Stephen & Wal Fisher.
Monday 12 June 1916Billy goes to Alex. for a Holiday.  Water melon on the run home from Cairo.  Met McCoogi.  Went through the Belgian Count's Home at Helio.  Wrote to Hilda & Bert Cocks.
Tuesday 13 June 1916Night Matron.  Concert in Patients Hut.  Lady Godiva present.  Late night.
Wednesday 14 June 1916Sick Parade because I was too tired to get up.  Went to Choubra [also spent Shubra and Shoubra] & eat water melon.  Spent day at Soldiers Club & Dave won at billiards 100 to 86.  Have only a few milliemes left.  Eat Bessie's Peaches in Esbekiah.
Thursday 15 June 1916Pay day.  Received letter & Photo from Cousin Jennie.  Wrote to Nettie & Home (42), Mum (43).
Friday 16 June 1916Wrote to Jennie Lawton 5 pages.
Saturday 17 June 1916Night ward master.  Concert by Camel Corp in Y.M.C.A. Hut.  Everybody goes out but me.  Still getting lovely weather.
