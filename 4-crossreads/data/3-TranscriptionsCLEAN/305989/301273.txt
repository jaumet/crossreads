  
 Sunday 9 September 1917 Sky Pilot parade:  swapped jobs & "slipped";  wrote Nos. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 2].  Swimming a good part of the day. 
 Monday 10 September 1917 Preparing for inspection by G.O.C., got all gear clean, on picquet at night. 
 Tuesday 11 September 1917 New Zealand band gave a concert in evening at Y.M.C.A., very good. 
 Wednesday 12 September 1917 Sports all day.  (Schoolers Benefit).  Saw Gudge Brown:  concert and prize giving at night.  Brig. in great form. 
 Thursday 13 September 1917 2nd Brigade gave us a concert at night, very good.  On Sanitary fatigue. 
 Friday 14 September 1917 On "Pump fatigue", exercising horses & picquet at night. 
 Saturday 15 September 1917 Concert by Pyjama Pianos very fine turnout.
