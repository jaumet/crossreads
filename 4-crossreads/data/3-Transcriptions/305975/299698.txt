<p>This is Joseph Bolger's last diary and it follows his hospitalisation period after suffering malaria, his return to duty and subsequent return bout of Malaria.   He is at Jerusalem where he spends a month ill in hospital until they change his medication and he returns to duty.   The end of hostilities and return to Australia is documented up to his discharge from the Light Horse.</p>
