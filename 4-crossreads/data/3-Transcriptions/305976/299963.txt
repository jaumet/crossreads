<p>Sunday Jan 20<br/>Heads expect Jacko to start raiding. Ordered to dig in. Clipped horse. Made bivvy comfortable.<br/>Monday Jan 21<br/>Football match between NZ v Div team. Good game. Football competition commenced in Division. Every prospect of long spell here.<br/>Tuesday Jan 22<br/>Old [indecipherable] rejoined unit usual routine<br/>Wednesday Jan23<br/>Football again. This afternoon Band Concert. Everybody getting fit again</p>
