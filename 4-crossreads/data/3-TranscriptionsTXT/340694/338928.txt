 43 neath the drooping Virgin. It was a lovely ride &amp; the view from the top of the hill was wonderful. Star shells lit up the sky also the roadway. Had a cup of coffee when I got back it went down well. The Adjutant secured a German Howitzer shell case for me, the only souvenir I have of this Front (Somme). 
 4/9/16. Am still in Senlis but am expecting a sudden move any minute. 
 Last night about 30 shells were dropped near by landing amongst some horses &amp; doing a fair amount of damage. This afternoon I rode up the hill &amp; witnessed a German bombardment. They were lobbing them on top of the next rise
