
10-2-17Rumours of a move to take place.  Lads play 3rd Brigade football.  1st Brigade move to Hassamya.  W5 arrive from Bayud.  Go to lecture on Australia in YMCA at night.11-2-1712/2/Stormy night with vivid lightning & rain.  Taube over.13-2-17Camel riding & breaking in.14-2-17Rainy & cloudy day breaking in more camels.15/2/17Pay day.16-2-17Stunt with trench sets -17-2-17Nothing doing18/2/17Out with trench set all day on new camels.  Hostile passes over very low.19-2-17Over El Arish taking photos.20/2/17Orders to pack up.21-2-17W3 & W5 leave El Arish for El Berf with all camels and gear.  Go for a swim in morning - 12-4 pm watch on W.2. 22 - Leave El Arish at 8 am and meet at rendezvous on Wadi.  Taube over as if by arrangement.  Arrive El Berf at 1 pm and leave at 2 pm.  We are followed by the Intelligence Dept consisting of a Captain (Gipps) S/M Bedouin and several bedouin men who are spies & police.  Arrive Sheik Zowayid at 5.36 pm.  Beautiful sunset over the great flats & swamp.23/2/17Lie about all day.  3rd Bde. & N.Z.'s return from reconnaissance having met with Turks.24-2-174-6 am watch.   Taube over during morning.  Make human arrow for our plane.  Take cooking instead of going to El Berf for W3.
