<p>20th  Paid  &pound;1-0-0</p>
<p>21st  Boxing Tourney aboard begins.  Ref Major Cooke-Russell  Information gleaned from seaman. The temperature of the water is taken every 4 hours from a canvas bucketful hauled up from the ocean to the upper deck.  By this they can tell to a certain extent where they are also the kind of weather to expect they will run into.  The log is taken every 4 hours and readjusted as the clock only registers to 100 miles</p>
