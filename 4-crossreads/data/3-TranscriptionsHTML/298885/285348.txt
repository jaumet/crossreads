<p>June 29th &ndash; 19<br/>After a run through a heavy storm we steamed into Melbourne at 12 Noon where the majority of the boys were leaving us.</p>
<p>Anyhow the boys from "Our 'arbour" and Bananaland were allowed ashore till midnight.</p>
<p>I had no sooner got off the boat when I met Cliff Kennard. who left us in Belgium 12 months ago and was invalided Home last Nov</p>
