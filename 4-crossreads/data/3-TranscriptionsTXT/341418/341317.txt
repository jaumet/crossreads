MiniaIn a small town on the banks of the inevitable Nile a small colony of white people live there, government officials  &amp;  their families I imagine. The stock phrase to colonial population which is fairly large is "good night: at all times of the day  &amp;  night small urchins  &amp;  grown men  vociferate  goodnight to you  &amp;  even aged  venerable  looking old "Hags" murmur this magic greeting to you in your ear as they pass.
