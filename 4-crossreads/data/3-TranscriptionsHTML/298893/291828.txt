<p>19th Batt<br/>Popes Post<br/>5-12-15</p>
<p>Sgt Frankhardt<br/>5th Field Engineers<br/>Will you please me have 250 sand bags for trench repairs. The shells got on to our position this morning &amp; cut up the bags<br/>A Gray<br/>RSM</p>
<p><u>Lieut Boyden</u><br/>Will you kindly let me have 4 men to go to 5th Field Engineers for Sand bags. Order enclosed to Sgt Frankhardt<br/>A Gray<br/>RSM</p>
