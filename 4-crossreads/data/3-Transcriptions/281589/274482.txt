<p>Sunday Oct 21st cloudy but lovely afternoon church parade this morning. I went for a walk after tea.</p>
<p>Monday Lovely day, put on guard but as I didnt know <strike>what</strike> the drill I was taken off &amp; put on mess orderly for the guard, went to the pictures in the camp they were good.</p>
<p>Tuesday, rain last night, off duty today, fine today Gen. Haig made his 6th attack in Flanders, pictures again tonight (Apples).</p>
<p>Wednesday cold showery weather, rumours are about regarding Q boat roll, we got some Apples tonight in an orchard, not feeling too good tonight.</p>
<p>Thursday cold showery weather, was called out with nearly all the Codford lot &amp; told us to hold ourselves ready to go to Salisbury any minute for permanent work, but was told that any man could stay here if he volunteered for permanent guard, a few did, but I didn't.
