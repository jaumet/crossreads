28/6/19 London
After breakfast tubed up to the Bank and drew some money and then hurried back to Piccadilly and took a taxi to Buckingham Palace. Went into a reception room there and found myself drafted in among many generals and a few other colonels. Colonel Frybery VQM GDSO two bars was also there. Flunkeys stuck small hooks on our coats and the sound of "Gorsave" from outside announced that George was on the dais. There were three VCs and these filed ahead first. I was about twentieth and stepped up to Geo. wearing the slouch hat and getting quite an ovation from the crowd who were seated on chairs listening to a Guard's band playing. The King is small
