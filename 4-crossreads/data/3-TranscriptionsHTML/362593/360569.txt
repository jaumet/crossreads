<p>Tuesday 25 June 1918<br/>Total Hours flown,<br/>Over lines &ndash; 305 h. 17 m.<br/>Tests, etc. &ndash; 11 h. 35 m.<br/>Total &ndash; 316 h. 52 m.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26 June 1918<br/>H.A.P.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 2 h. 50 m., no excitement.<br/>Murphy &amp; Farquhar scrap a Hun over aerodrome.  Hun escaped but was on the point of landing.  Farquhar killed.  A very fine chap and a good sport.</p>
