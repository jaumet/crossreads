<p>Monday 11 September 1916<br/>Started away to go on leave to Port Said for 48 hours but had to come back owing to line along coast being washed away.  Wrote to Mother (17).<br/>Helped clean up lines.<br/>West &amp; other chaps came back in evening by Cantarah.</p>
<p>Tuesday 12 September 1916<br/>Loading limbers in the morning.  In afternoon had a mounted parade and practiced assault in action.<br/>West and I went over to Machine Gun Section to see any new chaps.<br/>Heard we have to go on 72 hours stunt.</p>
