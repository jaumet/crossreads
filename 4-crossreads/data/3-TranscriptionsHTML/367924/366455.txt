<p>40 Tuckfield Dr</p>
<p>Jun 11</p>
<p>Mrs Dear Mrs Bilney</p>
<p>I feel so sorry to har about your Dear son I know how you will feel but we must look on the bright side &amp; pray that God &amp; his holy angles will be with him to mak him well to be sent home to his dear wife &amp; little one as now the time he will feel Dear for them then evr poor sole I will pray for him but I pray for you all &amp; all thoes dear men &amp; boys at the front &amp; that this Grat war will soon be ovr</p>
<p>A friand of mine her husband has been Gased &amp; I praid for him thay wear looking for word evry day to say he had past away but thay got word that he was in proven &amp; I am glad I put a pray up for him I dont feel that he is very badly wounded but will get better let us hope &amp; pray that this will come true</p>
<p>Some day I may tell you how I have so much fath in my prays now I think this will be all hoping that this letter will find you all well &amp; do not frat for yur now thoughts travels so try &amp; be happy &amp;he will try &amp; be the same allmos say he will get better &amp; I think you will find it will help bouth you &amp; him I no if my son is unhappy</p>
