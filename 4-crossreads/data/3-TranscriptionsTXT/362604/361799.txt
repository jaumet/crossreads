We have a chap to make our Beds &amp; generally look after us &amp; someone calls for our washing &amp; returns it, so we are faring even better than at Bulford.  I was very sorry to read that Mum has been so ill.  This influenza has been awful &amp; the deaths in the British Isles &amp; in fact in all parts of the world has been appaling.  We occasionally get a case here but we are keeping it under now.  How did you celebrate Armistice night?  My word the people went mad over this part.
