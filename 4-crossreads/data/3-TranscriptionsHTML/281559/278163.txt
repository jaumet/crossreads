<p>Wednesday April 2nd London</p>
<p>stroll in morn : dinner with Edie &amp; very nice indeed : museum in afternoon : took Mother &amp; the girls to "The Naughty Wife" after tea &amp; it was very funny :</p>
<p>Thursday April 3rd London</p>
<p>stroll with Marjorie in afternoon to St Pauls &amp; she was a good kid &amp; gave no trouble : everyone was admiring her beauty &amp; colour : stroll with Edie after tea &amp; had a good little time</p>
<p>Friday April 4th London</p>
<p>stroll in morn &amp; afternoon : "Evening" after tea &amp; pleasant said "Good bye" to all at 10.15 pm &amp; the girls came to the train with me : They are both good sorts &amp; well worth a second look :</p>
<p>Saturday April 5th</p>
<p>Train got to Codford at 8 am &amp; then to camp : about 9 hours overdue but nothing was said about it : roll call in afternoon :</p>
<p>Sunday April 6th Codford</p>
<p>roll call in morn &amp; inoculation &amp; medical inspection : wrote after dinner :</p>
<p>Monday April 7th to Wednesday April 16th : Codford</p>
<p>nothing much doing : we only have a parade each</p>
