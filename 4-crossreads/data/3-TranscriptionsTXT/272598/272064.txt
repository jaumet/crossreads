Retained our sheep skin vests up till the present, all through the hot weather, &amp; now when we are off to a cold country, of course it is quite in keeping with the ways of the military to get rid of them , which we certainly had to do.
Friday 2/6/16Foot &amp; hand inspection.  Tedious day on piquet, for they again enforced the four hour shift.  Returned from piquet at 7 pm.
