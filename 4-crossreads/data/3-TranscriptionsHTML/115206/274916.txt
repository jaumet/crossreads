<p>December  1918.<br/>Saturday 14</p>
<p>At Sea. H.S."Karoola."</p>
<p>Weather very moist and a slight sell running. A great number of the lads aboard feeling very seasick and looking it.<br/>The food is excellent in quantity and quality. Had a game of Loto. Purchased some stores for the trip.<br/>Left Sthampton roads at 11.30pm. last night and proceeded down stream<br/>We are fast entering the Bay of Biscay. &ndash; 7.50 pm.<br/>Wrote to D.B.F.</p>
