Wednesday 18th August 1915.   Though we were well in the famous Bay at 2am, there was nothing to distinguish it from a land locked sea as the ship was only making the slightest trifle of a roll.  There is a marked difference between the atmosphere now, and that of a few days ago, the chilly atmosphere of the North Sea being replaced by the warmer of a more temperate clime.
