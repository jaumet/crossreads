<p>Have not received the   <u>Cake</u>  yet, hope it will arrive son, am longing for a good feed of home made cake. Make me a big one when I return, Oh to think of those nice sponge cakes Belle   &amp;  Ethel make. You'll think that sounds healthy &ndash; will I am.</p>
