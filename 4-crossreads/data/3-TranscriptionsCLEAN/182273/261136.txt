 June   1918 
 9 SUN  Fine day. Went into 
 St. Omer on byke. called at 
 Aerodrome saw Capt. Malley. 
 Went on to town. Had glorious 
 time with Sgt. Savage & girls. 
 Stayed overnight. Posted 
 letter to Julie. 
 10 MON  Fine day. Heavy storm 
 at noon. Moved camp. Amm 
 going up. Quiet. Arr: home 
 7.15 am. Quiet night 
 Lt. Donoghue arr. back. 
 11 TUES  Fine day. Rec. letter 
 from Amy. Amm: going 
 up. Fairly quiet. RS.Clarke 
 & Brown went before C.R.A. re 
 commissions. Writing.  Courtney &  Neville 
 Wdd. 
 12 WED  Posted letter to Amy. 
 Fine day. Amm. going up. 
 Quiet day & night.
