<p>July 1917<br/>Friday 13th<br/>Fritz paid us another visit last night &amp; dropped bombs on the Army Workshops &amp; blew up a couple of houses they arrested two Belguim spys this morning.<br/>Saturday 14<br/>It has been a cold wet day &amp; it continues so tonight so we should have a peacefull night.  There was a fair number of wounded admitted today<br/>Sunday 15th<br/>A big evacuation today<br/>Monday 16th<br/>More wounded admitted today so we dont have a very long spell.  There as been a continuous stream of guns going through the town the last few days    They are going up the coast for the last big push to finish the war so the optimist say</p>
