  
 New Years Day - January 1st 1917- It was a dull grey morning as we moved slowly to our anchorage in Plymouth Sound. It was apparent to the most perfunctory civilian observer that the place was strongly fortified, batteries & barracks peeping unassumingly from the stony heights commanding the harbour.
