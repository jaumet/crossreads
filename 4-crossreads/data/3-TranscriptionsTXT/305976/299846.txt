 make use of Sydney leave next week. April 1 Sunday I &amp; some of the boys went for a swim to the Goulbourn River Monday April 2 Leave for Sydney from Good Friday night. Deputation paraded to OC asking for permission to go Thursday &amp; be home for Good Friday Informed department too busy carrying Tourists to supply trains for soldiers. Anticipate trouble. Wednesday April 4 All hands have to pay 38/- before going on leave for fare. Another roar [possibly war]. Only way out for us to have a Kentucky which we did &amp; raised sufficient for our unit.
