<p>Battle Planes brought two of enemy planes down in direction of Moorslede.  None of ours down.  Enemy damaged <strike>toni</strike> during night with Minenwerfers.</p>
<p>22.10.17</p>
<p>The 29th Aust. Bn. relieved our Battalion last night.  We left the front line under a hail of machine gun fire and 5.9 H.E. shells &ndash; a farewell from Fritz the like of which I have never before experienced.</p>
