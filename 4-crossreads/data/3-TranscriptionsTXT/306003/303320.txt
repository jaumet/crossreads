 We have been in the habit of having cocoa every night before retiring but tonight we found that our cocoa supply had run out, so we had poached eggs instead, &amp; were just about to make our bed down when Abdul Moolah appeared on the scene with a kettle, teapot, glasses &amp; Egyptian tea I then remembered asking him to come &amp; have tea with us, so we soon had the kettle boiling &amp; by 10.30 was filled up with sweet tea, &amp; enjoying a very amusing evening with Les the interpreter &amp; Abdul Moolah; D Troop supplied a very fair programme tonight;  Was issued with a watch today
