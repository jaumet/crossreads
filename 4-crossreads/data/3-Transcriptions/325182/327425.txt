<p>July 5th<br/>Mrs. Smith and a few others and myself went over to Lord<br/>Byron's home, Thrumpton Hall and were welcomed by the old boy.  Lord Byron is a jolly <strike>of</strike> old sport.  Lieut. Woods and myself had a game of tennis with his nephew and a <strike>f</strike> very pretty girl a Mrs. Nielson  We won both sets.</p>
<p>Sun. 7th<br/>A beautiful day.  I had a wander round this <strike>big</strike> lovely old place with its beautiful rosary and clustering ancient elms and firs.  My last day here.  It makes one think of odious comparisons</p>
