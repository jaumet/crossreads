<p>24.2.18<br/>Lieut. R.B. Coleman proceeded on 14 days leave.  At 4.15 p.m. a concentration was put down on Twin Farm, C.12.a.73.  At 5.20 p.m. Right group A.F.A. [Australian Field Artillery] reported by wire as follows, <br/>"T.M. emplacement at C.5.d.28.93 and C.5.c.70.55, considerably damaged by our 45 Hows. to-day with the object of inflicting casualties on working parties, a Machine Gun emplaced at C.15.b.20.44 kept up harrassing fire throughout the night, expending 2000 rounds.<br/>Between 6 p.m. and 4 a.m. 4500 rounds were fired on the following targets, <br/>(1)  Cemetary Drive, C.12.C.00.40 to C.12.C.40.30, <br/>(2)  Roho C.11.b.35.90 to C.5.d.65.20, <br/>(3)  Roho C.12.a.00.62 to C.12.a.30.50, <br/>(4)  Sap C.12.b.00.95 to C.12.b.30.95, <br/>(5)  Junction of Trenches (Creeping Barrage)</p>
