  
 Australian Army Medical Corps, A.I.F. letterhead as before. Bulford, Salisbury. Address reply to Staff 12th November Dear Dad, & Mum & George, I have Dad's letters of the 19/9/17, 2/9/17 10/9/17, 16/9/17, 23/9/17, Mum's of the same dates & Georgies of 11.9.17 to answer.  I have about 20 others to answer as well.  I have not written for about 9 days.  Ellis being away & then the busy time I've had since His return has left me in a pretty tired state after the day's work.  On Saturday afternoon I had a game of Football & it was O.K.  I am stiff all over in consequence but that is nothing.  I was on Duty 3 nights last week also, & now I've got heaps of letters to answer.  I had to go to S'bury on Tuesday to stand my Trial for riding without a Tail light.  I left the Hospital at 8 am & had a Cup of Tea with my Friends at 11 am.  I went into the Court with a lot of other Soldiers at 12 Noon.  About 12.45 we were all charged & fined & dismissed   It cost me 2/6 only, rather a cheap day's outing.  After dinner I cycled out about 7 miles to a place called Downton.  It is a straggling country Village with Rustic Bridges & Mills & Village greens, preaching Crosses & old Curiosity shops & Market Square.  I only cycled through, had a look over the church & then returned to my friends in the Close.  I slept at the Y.M.C.A. that night & caught the Train at 7.45 am next morning to attend at D.C.
