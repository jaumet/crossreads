<p>Thursday 21st February<br/>As usual Bill &amp; I went to the Divisional Baths &amp; came back clean &amp; bright. Good supply of hot water &amp; new underclothes. Bill is not a bit windy. <strike>But he</strike> was very funny aalday about a 9 in shell that lobbed quite close to him</p>
<p>Friday 22nd February<br/>Finished our Observation Post proper still a few days digging &amp; cleaning up to</p>
