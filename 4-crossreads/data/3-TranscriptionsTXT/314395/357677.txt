I am enclosing a pamphlet showing all the places I visited, and on New Year's day, I sent you a book of views.  By this mail I am also sending my Xmas &amp; N. Year souvenirs which denote another milestone safely passed.  Great preparations are being made in France by the enemy &amp; ourselves, for next spring.  It will be something colossal when it eventuates.  The number of guns to be employed will run into millions.  All the most modern chemical and scientific horrors, will be brought into play and a great many people are of opinion that it will be the deciding factor of the war.
