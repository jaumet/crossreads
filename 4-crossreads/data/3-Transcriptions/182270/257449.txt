
<p>SATURDAY, MAY 17, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Round Ben Ledi and</p>
<p>the Middle Paddock in the morning.</p>
<p>I got 13 rams out of my ewes</p>
<p>&amp; put them in 4D. Peter &amp; I&nbsp;</p>
<p>&amp; Linda went down in the</p>
<p>little car &amp; lopped in the</p>
<p>Point &amp; then on to Wheelers</p>
<p>shooting rabbits.</p>
