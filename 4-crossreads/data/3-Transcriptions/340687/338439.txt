<p>May, 1917. Tuesday 15<br/>Day cloudy and one shower. Parade at 7.30 &amp; drill order at 9 for squad drill &amp; then company drill with transport. In aftn. went to a gully close by and selected M.G positions for practice. Good football match with 3rd A.S.C &amp; were unlucky to lose by 4 points. Read in evening Motor Manual.</p>
