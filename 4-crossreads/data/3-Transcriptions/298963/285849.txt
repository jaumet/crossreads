One big shell &ndash; probably 9.5 &ndash; landed about 15 ft. behind us.  The concussion nearly knocked us all, the mud nearly buried us, but no one was hurt.  Came straight out then at the "tout suite" thankful to the powers to have escaped from so imminent a death.  We came out thro still swept ground to [indecipherable] where we are resting.</p>
