<p>5.<br/>surprising breadth of view. The specialists say that they find that Monash knows as much about their work as they know themselves. Taking his staff all round I am uncommonly lucky to be with them.</p>
<p>You say you are almost sorry that Keith is with the infantry. There is something worse, &amp; that is being in a Trench Mortar Battery.</p>
<p>We have had beautifully mild weather for a fortnight, but it is bitterly cold now. The mud stays with us all the time. I can't tell you how much we hanker after warmth &amp; sun</p>
