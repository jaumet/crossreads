  
 It is the 1st Div Reinforcement Camp. A very pretty place at the back of a Chateau About 3 or 4 Ac with very fine trees & a hedge around it. The tents are pitched between the trees & there is nice short grass in the centre Have passed through a lot of cultivated  grass  land to day very good crops. Can hear the guns in the line tonight A great number of our planes keep going up & down to the line. Had a blanket issued this evening 
 Tuesday 30th
