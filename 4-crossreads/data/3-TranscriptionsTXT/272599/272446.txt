 1917 Monday 25 June 
 A fair night, Pittams quite recovered.  Skinner &amp; Medhurst almost out of wood, also Plant,  Farrow new admission bad case &amp;  KELLY .  A typical Irishman, an awful worry, high querelous voice calls 'orderly' incessantly. Bunny hug.   Lena Ashwell gave concert but had to leave after first 2 items
