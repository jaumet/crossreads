 January, 1916 Saturday 22 Exam "Mechanism" this morning Spend afternoon w/- dad &amp; make arrangements for going out tomorrow Get a letter from Aunty Emily 
 Sunday 23 Go into Cairo w/- dad &amp; have a long walk through the Native Bazaars &amp; watch the Arabs making rope Have dinner in town &amp; turn in about 10 pm.
