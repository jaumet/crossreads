<p>[from OC 13th MG Coy]<br/>Lieut Weston<br/>No2 Section</p>
<p>Herewith Operation Order No 1<br/>Reference getting into touch with C.O. Battns, this is impossible until they form up but word will be sent you who are Coy Commanders of left flank 49th &amp; right flank 51st.</p>
<p>Also herewith Extracts from Bde orders which concern you.<br/>Advanced CoHQ will be at Garter Pt from about 3a.m. &ndash; will you please make arrangements to reserve space now occupied by you for Holloway &amp; myself.</p>
<p>Rations (probably 48 hours &amp; 1 extra water bottle per man) &amp;</p>
