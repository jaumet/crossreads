<p>Saturday 1 December 1917.</p>
<p>&quot;Jacko&quot; came over with 2 Taubes which 2 anti-aircraft guns which our troops brought up during night opened on him. His long range guns then opened all round us. A.S.C. officer killed 2 men <s>&amp; 2 camels</s> also and 3 wounded. 1 of our camels at the dump killed.</p>
<p>Sent 2 Field Service cards acknowledging receipt of 2 parcels.</p>
<p>On picquet till 6 pm.</p>
