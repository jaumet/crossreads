  1917  
 Jan. 1  st  &nbsp; Another beautiful day, sports were continued, we have only one parade a day on account of the sports. The ships company gave a concert in the evening but I didnt attend it I wrote some letters instead. 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan. 2  nd  &nbsp; Rainy &amp; a very lumpy sea. we all stayed below all day with port holes closed. played 500 in the evening &amp; then had to sleep below. 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 3  rd  &nbsp; Beautiful day &amp; the sea has gone down a lot. We had a death on board &amp; he was lowered over the side with ceremony at 11.A.M.&nbsp; We watched the boxing in the evening when Darkie M c Leod fought Morris. 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 4  th  &nbsp; Very hot day. I sat in the sun all morning perspiring, then had a shower. In the afternoon I lay in the shade &amp; read &quot;The Quaker Girl&quot;&nbsp; We played 500 in the evening. 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 5  th  &nbsp; Very hot sun &amp; choppy sea, also strong wind.- on sweeping fatigue in the morning. We sighted land at 6.P.M. &amp; cruised off Durban all night.
