<p>Friday 23 March 1917.</p>
<p>Survey work &amp; general drill.</p>
<p>Tents rearranged on acct of sickness breaking out in the camp. 4 <strike>men</strike>&nbsp;to be in 1 tent.</p>
<p>Moved kit and settled again.</p>
<p>No lecture at night.</p>
<p>On acct of a clerical error I was granted leave from tonight until Monday night. Declined to go until morning train.</p>
