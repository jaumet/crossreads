Best Day Since arriving back in Sydney, I have frequently met people who evidently think that, when our boys leave Australia for the front, they can never experience many happy moments until they arrive back home again, now I would like to disabuse this impression which I know exists in many minds; certainly I must admit, that when one reads through the lists of Dead &amp;amp; Wounded, it must create a few misgivings, but I can assure you, there are happy moments.
