<p>Beautiful weather prevailed all through the week which helped the Allies along considerably. Many an air duel was witnessed on the 8th &amp; 9th. inst, rather imposing sights.</p>
<p>Once again the Ambulance Bearers came through another Stunt with very few casualties, may God Grant that this state of affairs will continue.</p>
