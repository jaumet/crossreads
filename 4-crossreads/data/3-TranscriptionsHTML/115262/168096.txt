<p>Sunday 25 November 1917.</p>
<p>Moved at dawn across Jaffa road 2 miles &amp; bivouaced.</p>
<p>I.C.C. Bgde moved into front trenches. On watering 1/2 way to Jaffa.</p>
<p>Oranges picked off the trees are exceptionally &quot;good&quot;. 40 for 3 P.T&#39;s I got at one grove which was about the best I have seen&nbsp;so&nbsp;far.</p>
<p>Jews return from Ramleh to Jaffa. From all accounts the Turks gave the Jews a rough time.</p>
