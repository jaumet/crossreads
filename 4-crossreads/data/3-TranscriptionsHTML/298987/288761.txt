<p>Thursday 9th Sun 12th May '18<br/>Hand dressed every day doing nothing but reading &amp; loafing about. Think I forgot to mention that Quex Park was run entirly by women The Dr visited 3 or 4 times per week The only man was a deff &amp; dumb Belgan (been gassed early in the war) he got through a lot of work. The out of bed patients helped a good deal.</p>
<p>Monday 13th<br/>Nothing as usual wet day for a change</p>
<p>Tuesday 14<br/>Was marked B.A. <strike>to</strike> as my hand is quite healed up Walked to Fovant &amp; back about 4 miles the longest walk I have had for some time</p>
<p>Wednesday 15<br/>Dental inspection Had a tooth stopped</p>
<p>Thursday 16th<br/>Moved from Camp 5 No 1 Co to Camp 7 No 5 Co only a few hundred yds</p>
<p>Friday 17th<br/>Am on Massage &amp; R.C. Treatment (exersising</p>
