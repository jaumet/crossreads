<p>After a period of quiet a small strafe has broken out but I'm quite used to them by now.  I told you about the first night near Albert didn't I, I often smile to think of it now.</p>
<p>This is calm compared to there.  I got my first wigging today, very nasty but I deserved it.  Still it's a tricky job &amp; I have to remember things lots &amp; its so hard.</p>
<p>But the importance of me.  If I only had somebody here to talk &amp; giggle over things but I have to go on being very 'orty &amp; never a let down.</p>
