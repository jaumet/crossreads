<p>Thursday 23&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />About hospital&nbsp;<br />650 patients&nbsp;<br />Had dinner with Lieut Col McGlynn ar <s>Shep</s> the Continental Hotel.&nbsp;<br />[Major (Honorary Lieutenant Colonel), later Brigadier General, John Patrick McGlinn CBE VD CMG OBE (1869-1946)]</p>
<p>Friday 24&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />Saw Lt Col McGlynn, he had come from Galipoli, where he has been since April.&nbsp; He looks very well.&nbsp;<br />Lunched with Col. Manifold &amp; Col. Hunter at the Continental Hotel.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Saturday 25 September 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo during the morning.&nbsp;<br />Sent 128 men to Australia, all correct.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sunday 26&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />At Basilica at 9-30 a.m.&nbsp;<br />At Matarieh with Major Young&nbsp;at 6 p.m.&nbsp;<br />Letters from home this morning.&nbsp;</p>
