<p>3rd A.G.H.<br/>B.E.F.<br/>France.<br/>9.10.17.</p>
<p>My dearest Belle,</p>
<p>Just received a lovely Aust. mail &amp; your nice long newsy letter with Equity Transfer enclosed.  I have signed where the pencil mark was so hope it is right.</p>
<p>Am very pleased you bought the shares &amp; that my banking account is quite alright.</p>
