  
 Saturday 29 June Up at 5.30 am. & plenty of work doing. 
 Finally stables moved out to Cosy but with B. Det. stopped behind with D.A. Moved horses to cookhouse site and set up camp thereabouts. Fodder ration small. 
 Arranged camp all day. Living separate from the others, cookhouse of our own. 
 All very good.
