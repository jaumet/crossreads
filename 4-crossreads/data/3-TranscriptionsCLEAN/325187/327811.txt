
[Monday 6 March 1916]
We are not supposed to bathe in Canal here or in fact any where in Egypt owing to a hook worm called BILHARDSI entering the System- But I prefer to chance the hook worm to going dirty-General move on further South to Ass[indecipherable] but we remain where we are I am always afraid I say too much in my notes to you, but it would be a conscientious Censor who is able to [indecipherable] thro all this.  I sent you from the Dardanelles an English Army 1note to raffle at ALLIES Day, with ANZAC on it this must have gone done with my other letters & notes in the Trawler
