I have built me an house of palm sticks &amp; sand bags &amp; am now very comfortable, have a fire outside the door every night and a beautiful washing basin hollowed out of solid Limestone &ndash; as much as I can lift and Lord knows how old &ndash; I picked it up close here &ndash; one finds all sorts of queer remnants of pottery &amp; such like on the desert. 
 Nov. 9th At present I am trying to solve a great problem namely, how to beg, borrow or steal if need be a potatoe to go with some bacon which I am cooking for tea tonight.
