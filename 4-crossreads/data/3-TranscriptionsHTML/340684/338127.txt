<p>435<br/>2<br/>by an explosion of a shell &ndash;<br/> it has been a jolly dull week for me stuck in bed all the time &ndash; The weather has been beautiful and yesterday morning there was quite a heavy snow storm. The first one many of the fellows have ever seen</p>
<p>J and R came up last Saturday looking splendid as usual &ndash; its jolly good of them to give up their Saturday afternoon to come up here, especially now that I can't get out.</p>
<p>I haven't heard from Sir G.Reid since I wrote last &ndash; I will send him along a reminder in a day or two but there</p>
