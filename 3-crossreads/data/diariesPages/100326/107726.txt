29/8/15 The operation consists of first spreading a garment or mat on the ground facing East and then standing with bowed head clasped hands, meditating. Then down on knees and touch ground with forehead muttering prayers. Repeated 2 or 3 times and then up.
Train then to Cairo and switched on to Alexandria line. It is now 9.35 am we may be on board a transport 120 miles away in a few hours before dawn any way. Looking at Cairo passing through the mind runs back to the Mouska the Arabian Nights. Slept two hours. At midnight we are passing at great speed through irrigated country, the plains stretching on either side a canal running all the way along the right hand side. The area full of a dank smell like and we rushing along through the at express rate
