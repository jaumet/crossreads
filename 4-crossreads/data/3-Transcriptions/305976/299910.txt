<p>Thursday Aug 23<br/>Rose at 6.45 AM. Breakfast 7 AM. On duty until 9 AM. This Camp will do me. Food is good. Duties light &amp; plenty of surf bathing.<br/><strike>Wednesday</strike> Friday Aug 24<br/>My first view of two German Taubes today. Flew over camp, very high in air. Heard Monitors shelling Gaza &amp; could see flashes up coast<br/>Saturday Aug 25<br/>Cricket match today between staff &amp; patients of rest camp. Patients won by four runs.<br/>Sunday Aug 26<br/>Two Taube overhead today. Flew much lower than on previous occasions. Wrote letters afternoon.</p>
