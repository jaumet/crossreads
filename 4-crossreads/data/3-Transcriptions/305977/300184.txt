<p>March 1915<br/>afternoon.</p>
<p>Friday March 5th<br/>Same as yesterday.  Alf Outridge came out to the camp at night.  He looks very well.</p>
<p>Saturday 6th<br/>Dismounting for action drill Squadron working independently.  Did more good bayonet work.  No parade in the afternoon.  Several of the troop went on leave.  Went to the boxing tournament in the Ma-adi Stadium at night.  Sports in the afternoon.  The 6th did very well.  Won the wrestling on horse-back &amp; the balaclava Mele&#232;.</p>
<p>Sunday 7th<br/>Went to C of E Church Parade in the morning.  Had leave in the afternoon  &amp; went into Cairo.  Spent most of the afternoon in the soldier's caf&#233; &amp; in the Park listening to the Manchester Band.  Had tea in town &amp; caught the 10.30pm train bank to camp.</p>
