 8th/ Arrived at Railhead at 10-3 am after travelling all night. New Zealand soldiers camped here also Egyptian camel corps. Wattered horses at well the water was putrid. We then started to ride for our destination &amp; did about 25 miles &amp; camped near well gaurded by Indians. We watered horses &amp; had tea Up again next morning at 2am.
