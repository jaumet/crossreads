My own beloved MotherWe left Rabaoul on Oct 4th Monday at 3.30 pm and arrived here at 10.30 pm on the following Monday - we had a very rough trip - fortunately we only had 8 patients - nearly all the sisters were very seasick quite incapacitated - Sister Clouston and I did the work, there was not much to do. We were able to sleep on the deck all the time, the spray came in a little but the deck was screened off. The weather was hot, but  such a strong wind blowing that I had to a [indecipherable] a [indecipherable] all the time. The day before we reached Suva, the weather was perfect. I enjoyed every minute of it.The entrance to the harbour was very lovely and we went on shore and had afternoon tea at a good hotel. Went for a motor drive. The vegetation is beautiful.I am very well - but long to be home again. I am writing now
