I found myself on the extreme right of the line having come up Shrapnel Gully.  Very little happened here during the morning although we could hear very heavy firing on the left flank.  As it was bullets were constantly passing around us and we grew exasperated when we could not locate where they came from.  All kinds of rumours were passed along and one of the most welcome was that the artillery had landed.  It proved to be true too for early in the afternoon one of the Indian Mountain Batteries came along and got into action.
