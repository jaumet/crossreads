
Off to Blighty[October 1918]10 ThWine flows free, the boys are happy song & mirth,"Marching" with the deathless army of the A.I F11 FriLeft Fignacourt last night, rested at St Vasbri & slept for the night at Saint Savavour then took train to Bolounge at Ailly Sur Somme12 SatBolounge to Folkestone a very nice trip, arrived in London for tea Feet very sore.I am being sent to hospital Signal of distress ulcerated Feet
