<p>Monday 3 December</p>
<p>Frosty &ndash; all water is frozen &ndash; Bitterly cold.<br/>Went by Amb. Car to Dranoutre and walked the remainder of the distance (9 kilos) to the workshops.<br/>Worked all day cleaning the engine of the Triumph Cycle.  Returned to camp at night per motor lorries passing through Locre &amp; Kemmel<br/>One of the houses in Bailleul was last night levelled to the ground by a German bomb</p>
