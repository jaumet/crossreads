
<p>132</p>
<p>SEPT. &nbsp; TUESDAY 19 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Bitterly cold all day &amp; showers &amp; a little sleet. I went up to the dentist in the morning &amp; he finished my teeth. With the director men ect in afternoon with M<sup>r</sup>&nbsp;Neylan. Poss Nivison &amp; self went into Amesbury to tea with Eileen Ross &amp; a Miss Scott at the Abbey Hotel in evening &amp; had to walk back, &amp; it was no joke as we had to do it in 40 minutes but we did it in 35 min &amp; just got in in time for tatoo.</p>
