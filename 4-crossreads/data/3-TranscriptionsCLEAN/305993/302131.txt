 15.11.18 The Caladonia arrived today from the Dardanelles after taking Indian troops to man the forts & came back direct here.  She is one of the P & O Transports.  Good news this day re the Germans retreating everywhere & leaving all guns ammunition & war materials behind at the front & of the Kiasar having gone & abdicated to Holland also rumours re the Crown prince having been shot whilst trying to cross the Dutch frontier & escaping from Germany.  Also of a great revolution in Germany & of the Rulers leaving their respectful countries & of the Socialists governing the different German states.  All the Naval  warlike  war ships & appliances are here in Egypt very quiet since the Armistice was signed, a great change from a few weeks ago.  All the hospital ships & Prisnors of war ships are exceedingly busy bringing troops that have been captured in Turkey & the whole coming back in a very poor condition. 
 16.11.18 In the City of Alexandria the British troops on leave of which thousands are to be seen about the streets a large number being drunk are commiting great disturbances the breaking of glass windows & doors & actually robbing several shops wholesale to the disgust of the respectable soldiers & not a single case of any trouble amongst the blue jackets of which hundreds are to be seen in the City going about peacefully.
