<p>LILLE GATE<br/>[OCTOBER]<br/>Monday 1st<br/> The fighting is harder than ever, every gun in the army's seems to be on this front, the casualtys are few consd.<br/>Tuesday 2<br/>I feel well &amp; strong but the least shock or excitement makes me tremble, "ah" well a chap cant go on forever<br/>Wednesday 3<br/>Tomorrow at dawn the 1st 2nd &amp; 3rd Div's of Australians go over the parapet, may good luck go with them<br/> Thursday 4<br/>Over they went and met with great sucess 4000 prisoner's and much booty, good work</p>
