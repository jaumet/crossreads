 Sydney Y.M.C.A. Australian Expeditionary Forces 
 14.5.1917 
 L. Cpl. Maurer No. 1 Hut No. 4 A.G.H. Randwick 
 Dear Miss Clark 
 Having just returned from leave have just received your letter re Cpl. Gordon.  I am very sorry to say that I can tell you very little about how he received his wounds as I was wounded at the end of July &amp; he was going along well when I left the lines &amp; it was in Hospital in England when I heard he was badly wounded &amp; learned later that he had died of his wounds.  I can only say how sorry I was when I heard of his death because a finer lad never lived than poor Gordon &amp; he was thought the world of by all the boys in the Bombers, especially those under his command.
