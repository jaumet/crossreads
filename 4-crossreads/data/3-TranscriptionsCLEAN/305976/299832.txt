
My first visit to this City. Tom & I spending week end at Oakleigh (Toms Uncle) Caught 4.30 pm train to Oakleigh then cab to Notting Hill. Spent pleasant week, end driving & viewing district.Sunday Jan 28At OakleighMonday Jan 29Rose at 4.30 AM to catch 7.30 AM train back to Seymour arrived camp about NoonTuesday Jan 30Semaphore Signalling today. Advised not to throw our arms about like scarecrows.Wednesday Jan 31Concert down time in evening.  Very good show.
