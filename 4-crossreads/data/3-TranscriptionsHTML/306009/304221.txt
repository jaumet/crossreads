<p>1915<br/>Feb. 4 Thu.<br/>Fine swimming bath at Mena 11 yards <strike>long</strike> wide 35 yards long &ndash; must have a dip or two when weather gets a bit warmer.<br/>3 men (motor drivers &ndash; Sgt. &amp; 2 Ptes) arrived from Australia to join our unit &ndash; they came with the second force, 13000 men in 18 Transports &ndash; No leave granted to them on voyage &ndash; Left Melbourne on 20th Dec. &ndash; 3rd Force left on 5th Jan.</p>
