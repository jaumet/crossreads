<p>Tuesday Feb 2-15 &ndash;  I was up early &amp; studied my mail &ndash; Yours of Jan 10 &amp; 17 I've gone thro &ndash; Also Wetwan &amp; Turner &ndash; I was very glad indeed to get yours &ndash; You had Journal part 3 &ndash; 1 &amp; 2 are still in Rabaul I know &ndash; I'm so glad all goes well &ndash; you need not put 'please ford' &ndash; just Garden Island &ndash; They'll come &ndash; in time &ndash; I swam at 6.30 &ndash; then back &ndash; then route marching &ndash; men in full rig &ndash; Rifles &amp; all gear &ndash; Maxim gun &ndash; Ammunition &ndash; I need not have gone but it's learning something &amp; in addition I know round Rabaul &ndash; I made J. bring Stretcher &amp; Haversack &ndash; We filled a launch &amp; a cutter with men &ndash; We marched for an hour &ndash; I took them to the celebrated Fig tree in the gardens &ndash; I like to hear the steady tramp of armed men &ndash; Two officers besides myself &ndash; We marched right thro Rabaul on our way back &amp; got to a pier &amp; let the men strip &amp; swim &amp; then we all went inboard &ndash; I went off at once to the Warrego &amp; Yarra &amp; saw</p>
