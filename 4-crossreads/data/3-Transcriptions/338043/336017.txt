<p>Nov. 12th<br/>Awake &amp; feel much refreshed after a wash and a cup of tea &ndash; the first of both for 36 hours &ndash; mere detail!</p>
<p>Le Cateau. A town of fair size with large church &amp; municipal buildings and important factories &amp; machinery works &ndash; Captured or taken in 1914 by the Germans and recaptured by British in October of this year &ndash; The town is badly damaged and the Hun has deliberately destroyed houses  of manufacture together with plant &ndash; Streets and dwellings</p>
