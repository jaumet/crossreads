<p>Australian Light Horse Camp<br/>Ma'adi<br/>Egypt<br/>B. Squadron<br/>2nd Light Horse</p>
<p>16 Dec, 1914</p>
<p>Darling Mum &amp; Kids</p>
<p>We're just settled down here now. We got news that we were coming here to spend the winter, just after we had left Aden. This camp is composed of Light Horse only &amp; Army Service Corps, about 2000. The rest 26,000 are camped under the large pyrimids just 8 miles from Cairo. We are about 6 miles from Cairo in a sort of desert. There are some large stepped pyramids to the East of us but the large ones with the Sphinx are the ones the main body are camped under. I hope you got my previous letters, but of course you "might" not have, because everything was so strictly censored &amp; the officers told us it would be a long time before they got to Aus. Now, of course the censorship is over. Of course you've heard by now of the "Emden" affair.
