FEBRUARY 1917
14 WEDNESDAY
Fine & sunny & warm. Was
called out at 4 AM to go
up & shift our gun out to
the trenches sniping. We
took it right down the
Houplines to within about
800 yds of German trenches.
Came back to waggon
Lines & went up again in
evening & brought it
back to the pit. Got
letter from mother.
