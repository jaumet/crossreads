<p>I cannot speak too highly of the kindness which I have received at the hands of Mrs. Abel Smith and her family.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Left Cole Orton Hall and reached St. Pancras Station about 10.15 p.m.  Went to the Club in Piccadilly.</p>
<p>8th<br/>Received my move order from Hqrs. and went down to Warminster to the officers training Battalion where I am now installed.</p>
<p>9th<br/>Proceeded with a draft over France as Conducting Officer.</p>
<p>10th<br/>Left <strike>Bologne</strike> Folkestone with the draft and arrived at Bologne about 12 noon after about 2 hours across the Channel.  Marched to a rest camp above the town where we stayed</p>
