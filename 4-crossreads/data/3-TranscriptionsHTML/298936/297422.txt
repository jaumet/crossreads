<p>September 1915</p>
<p>Thursday 23</p>
<p>Put on fatigue work fixing up tents.</p>
<p>It is very healthy here but the rough wind makes the sand a nuisance at times.</p>
<p>A collection of Turkish prisoners is here, and plenty of Ghurkhas French &amp; Tommies.</p>
<p>The island evidently belongs to Greece for there are a few Greeks in the mud hovels of the village.</p>
<p>The hospital seems to be arranged on good lines for all the marquees are in orderly lines &amp; paths are edged with white stones.</p>
