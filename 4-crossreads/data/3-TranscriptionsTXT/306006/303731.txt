 8-2-17 Thurs 8th Have decided to wait till we get settled again before I go to the Dentist we leave next Saturday morning ; the 1st Bde do not relish the idea of going back &amp; Gen Cox put in today at Division to see if he could get it cancelled; The Railway line is going down but not as fast as we expected.
