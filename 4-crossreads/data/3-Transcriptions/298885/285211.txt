<p>Apr 10th &ndash; 18<br/>At dawn of day we arrived at our entraining station and entrained in miserable weather as it was raining unceasingly and being made worse by the strong cold winds.</p>
<p>We arrived at Steenwerck near Hazebrouck early next morning and felt pretty miserable, after a sleepless night.</p>
