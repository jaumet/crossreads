
<p>MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 8, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine all day, but terrible cold. Had</p>
<p>a couple of showers at night &nbsp; &nbsp;points</p>
<p>Barry &amp; I rode round bottom end.</p>
<p>I lopped in Mountain. Tickle</p>
<p>lopped in Top paddock &amp; 4D and</p>
<p>started ringing yellow jackets</p>
<p>in Middle after dinner.</p>
