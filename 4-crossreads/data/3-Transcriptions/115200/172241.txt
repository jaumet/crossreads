<p>28) the landing</p>
<p>Becaus I was not my nearves had gone completely&nbsp; any how we got orders to get ready for a Bayonet charge from Major Brown&nbsp; This officer belonged to the 3<sup>rd</sup> Battalion and he was a Brave officer&nbsp; When I first seen him he was covered with Bandges on his head and face. Come on Boy he Said get into these turks and rely on the Bayonet. Dont fire a Shot if you can help it.
