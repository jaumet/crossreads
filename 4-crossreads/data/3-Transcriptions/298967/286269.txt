<p>25 &ndash; Tincourt Wood &ndash; Transport left Tincourt 3 days "trek".</p>
<p>26 &ndash; 7 P.M. &ndash; Longpre &ndash; Bn. left Tincourt by train moved to Brucamps 11 P.M.</p>
<p>262/30 &ndash; Training and resting.</p>
<p>30 &ndash; Strength 35-482 casualties for month 240.</p>
<p>Oct 1/31 &ndash; Brucamps &ndash; Training, sports.</p>
<p>11 &ndash; 12.00 &ndash; Bgde. Transport competition 10th Batt. won cup.</p>
<p>16 &ndash; About this time Ostend, Lille, Douai, Roubaix, Turcoing was captured.</p>
<p>30 &ndash; Div. musketry competition 12th Batt. first, 10th very good shooting.</p>
<p>Nov. 5 &ndash; Brucamps &ndash; Preparing to leave Brucamps moved postponed for 24 hours.</p>
<p>8 &ndash; 2 P.M. &ndash; Left Brucamps for Hangest to entrain 6 A.M. 9th.</p>
<p>9 &ndash; 17.00 &ndash; Train late left 5 P.M. in evening.  Rumours regards Armistice.</p>
<p>10 &ndash; Stopped at Ham, St. Quentin &ndash; Roisel &amp; <strike>Ephey</strike> Epehy.</p>
<p>11 &ndash; 06.00 &ndash; Detrained at Epehy.  Armistice Signed.</p>
<p>11 &ndash; 11.30 &ndash; Left Epehy 11.30 Bn. by buss transports by road.  Bn. went to Mazinghem transport to Premont.</p>
<p>12 &ndash; 13.00 &ndash; Mazinghem &ndash; Transport rejoined Batt.</p>
<p>14 &ndash; 9.30 &ndash; Bn. left Mazinghem for Bohain arrived 16.30.</p>
<p>22 &ndash; Bohain &ndash; Marched Bohain to Mazinghem again.</p>
<p>23 &ndash; Cartignies &ndash; Marched Mazinghem to Cartignies.</p>
<p>24 &ndash; Beugnies &ndash; Marched Cartignies to Beugnies.</p>
<p>Dec. 1 &ndash; Beugnies &ndash; Inspection by H.M. the King.</p>
<p>2 &ndash; Beugnies &ndash; Lecture by Bishop Long on the British Empire.</p>
