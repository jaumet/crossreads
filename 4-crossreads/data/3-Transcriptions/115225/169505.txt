<p>December 1<sup><u>st</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Yes Bailleul at last - I went into an egg &amp; chip joint to get warm &amp; have breakfast &amp; then went to Marie Louise&#39;s joint as I expected she would know the whereabouts of the section. I met some of the boys at her place on the way to Brulooge Railhead for supplies, so I stayed in the warm till they got back again at 3.P.M. my buss with a broken spring - Our park is just outside Bailleul on the Hazebrouck road parked with 5<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;D.S.C. &nbsp;I feel very sorry to be back again -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dec 2<sup><u>nd</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Took the broken spring off &amp; left it in the blacksmith shop to be repaired &amp; being Sunday we had a half day off. Weather very cold &amp; windy -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
