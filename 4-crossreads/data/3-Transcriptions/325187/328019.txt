<p>[Tuesday 3 October 1916]</p>
<p>Went to British Australian &amp; put marriage in paper<br/>Answered dozens of letters &amp; telegrams, &amp; bought various necessaries-<br/>Money does go in London but we are very happy &amp; both the wee wife &amp; I have sense.</p>
