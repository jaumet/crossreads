<p>Friday 2nd Nov 1917<br/>Working Party. Did my first bit of Sandbag work They make such a neat job</p>
<p>Saturday 3rd<br/>Working Party Went to Warminster in evening what a dull place Weight 11st 3lbs</p>
<p>Sunday 4th<br/>As usual. Attended evening service at the Home Hut Miss Osbourne spoke A splendid little sermon</p>
<p>Monday 5th<br/>Same old Working party same old work</p>
<p>Tuesday 6th<br/>Hard job finding work on the working party. Have a very good little mate on the job an old hand. He is no loafer like so many are. His brother is a VC man &amp; he did not tell about it untill we had been 2 weeks together</p>
