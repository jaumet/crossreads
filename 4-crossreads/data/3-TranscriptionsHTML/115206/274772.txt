<p>July 1918<br/>Sunday 21</p>
<p>Frechencourt.<br/>Weather wet and showery. Rode my bike to Villers Bocage, Rainneville, Cardonette, Coisy and St Gratien. At the last place I ran across Ken Gun and Palmer, at the 5th Divvie Sig School.<br/>Stayed for lunch and had a quiet chat with Ken.<br/>Walked to Behencourt with Smithy and had a nice little snack.<br/>Weather fine, full moon. <br/>Had some polite visitors during the evening.</p>
