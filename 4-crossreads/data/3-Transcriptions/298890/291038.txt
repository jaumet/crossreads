<p>13 Sunday<br/>At Miss Hodges till 8.30 then returned to 22 Harley Rd.  Typed all day.</p>
<p>14 Monday<br/>Wet drizzling day.  Went to dinner with Nancy at night at Lyceum Club as Mrs Bagis guest.  Literally wept with boredom  escaped at 9.30</p>
<p>15 Tuesday<br/>Office routine.   Did some of my own typing.  Matron Nye came &amp; I took her to lunch at Minerva.</p>
<p>16 Wednesday<br/>Office routine.  Lunch at Minerva.</p>
