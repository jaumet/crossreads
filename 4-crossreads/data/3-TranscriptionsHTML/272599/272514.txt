<p>1917 Friday 7 Sept.</p>
<p>A cloudy night. Fritz never plagued us with this at  [indecipherable] <br/>Patients fairly well.<br/>Pathenden &amp; Burt only two seriously ill.<br/>Former developed pneumonia but is a splendid patient &amp; is fighting well.<br/>Parcel from Jessie neice of sox &amp; booklet from J. Hunt &ndash; <br/>Also received Alice's parcel few days ago.</p>
