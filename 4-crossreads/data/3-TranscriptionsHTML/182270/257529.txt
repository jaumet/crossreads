<p>TUESDAY, AUGUST 5, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm, but heavy frost in</p>
<p>morning. I rode round bottom</p>
<p>end &amp; then started stacking</p>
<p>up wood in corner of Middle</p>
<p>paddock. Tickle drawing water</p>
<p>and mending the fence between</p>
<p>4D and Wheelers.</p>
<p>The Bore down 137 ft &amp; no water</p>
<p>yet.</p>
