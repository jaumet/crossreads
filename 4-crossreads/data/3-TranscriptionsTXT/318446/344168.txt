It is a very miserable existence now for one cannot light a fire after dark &amp; it gets very chilly in the morning &amp; evening. Thank the Lord there is a full moon. Today is the second anniversary of our arrival into the land of bondage. 
 Dec. 12th Two 'planes over today but no bombs on us.  I wish I had Vergils VIth Book here now, I am just in the mood to read about pater Aeneas' traversing the 'tenebras' Umbras of Hell.
