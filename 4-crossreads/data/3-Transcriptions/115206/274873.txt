<p>October 1918.<br/>Wednesday 30</p>
<p>Hosptl. Birmingham<br/>Last night the Yank and Pedderson were taken to the tents. Inspection by some long streak of a Capt. Played  Brad draughts and cards all day. Have come to the final conclusion that the biggest wag and best sport on night duty is Nurse Macdonald &ndash; an Irish nurse.</p>
