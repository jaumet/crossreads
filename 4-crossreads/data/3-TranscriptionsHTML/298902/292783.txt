<p>contingent of British Soldiers with Australians made a good show &amp; a large number of Yanks with Y.M &amp; Red Cross workers also made a good show.  Much enthusiasm was not shown until later but a number of aeroplanes stunting overhead made the people very excited.  All types of machines were represented, the scouts flying low whilst some affixed magnesium lights under their wings which looked very pretty leaving a trail of fire behind. It being cold did not wait to hear President Poincare speak so returned to Passy for tea when I said goodbye to my friends.  Had dinner at the Leave  Club &amp; then did a tour with 3 others later taking part in the jollification &amp; celebration which was now at its height in the Boulevards.  They were dancing singing &amp; kissing all they could &amp; girls would snatch our hats &amp; several times I had to race</p>
