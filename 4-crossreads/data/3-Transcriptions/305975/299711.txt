<p>Cleansed on Parade  Went to ismalia in afternoon  had Photo taken.<br/>Tuesday Sept. 24.9.18<br/>turned out 4.30 fell in  am marked to go out  went before doctor and dentist  took all day  never saw such a lot of messing about  had letters from maud S. mick, dorrie, Josie mabel  3 from maud.<br/>Wednesday Sept.25.9.18<br/>turned out 4.30  fell in 5  went to Range as a marker again  very hot day.</p>
