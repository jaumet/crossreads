  
 J.B.F. in the dressing station. 20/10/17 Got shelled off our job yesterday. Scuttled for shelter when we were ordered to cover. I made a bee line for Jack's dug out, and paid him a call. Found the 33rd last night but Bob has been wounded. They have decided to work at night. So we went out at 4 A.m. this morning and got back early. Went across to the "D.A.C." again. 22/10/17. Had a day off yesterday. Rested. Went up on a train; a most exil arating ride; one of the Hun batteries tried to snipe us, we rattled along the line.
