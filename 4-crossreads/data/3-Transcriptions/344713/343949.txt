Our residence here in one of the houses looks too good to be safe but must take a risk for one nights decent sleep.<br/>"Fritzs" mines are liable to go up months after they are laid as he works them with a wire laid in an acid bath &amp; when the wire has lasted according to its guage &amp; material the fireworks start.<br/>We are suspicious of any decent looking domicile now.</p>
<p>Tues. April 24th: -<br/>Relieved in reserves at Beumetz by "Tommies" &ndash; the West Yorks.<br/>They are getting artillery up now the "Tommies" are taking the over.<br/>Marched into Bapaume arriving just before midnight.</p>
<p>Thurs. April. 26th: -<br/>Moved along to Vaulx-Vraucourt &amp; relieved the 21st Bn. for 24 hours so as they could practise for some "stunt" of theirs.</p>
<p>Fri. April 27th: -<br/>Back in Bapaume again.<br/>"Fritz" putting some fast naval shells over on the Railway station.<br/>He "outed" about 6 men &amp; four horses.</p>
<p>Sun. April. 29th: -<br/>Up into reserves again at Vaulx-Vraucourt to relieve 21st Bn. for more rehearsals of their "stunt".<br/>Liable to be here 4 or 5 days; Wish they would get their job done or else let us to it so as we could get out for a bit of a spell.<br/>We always seem to get dragged in</p>
