 Wednesday 16 July 1919 Rushed around to make sure I was on the quota but found that I had missed.  Saw orderly &amp; rushed my leave thro' &amp; got the 11.30 am to Newport &amp; from there to Brecon. Arrived at Brecon at 5.30. Went to Gamitige Place &amp; had dinner. We talked till 11 &amp; then went to bed. I was tired too.
