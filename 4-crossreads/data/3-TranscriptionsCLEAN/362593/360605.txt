
Wednesday 25 September 19183 h. 5 m.2 h. 50 m.2 trips.Pilot Lt. McGuinness, dropped 16 bombs, fired 1900 rounds.Turks now retreating from Amman.We now have Amman which gives us his whole front.  Only a few thousand are left, we have bombed all today.
Thursday 26 September 1918Bombing Mafrak.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 2 h. 30, dropped 8 bombs, fired 1000 rounds, did considerable damage.Pilot Major Addison.Flew up to Haifa, landed there, picked out new aerodrome.  In air 2 h. 5 m.
