  
 It was a lovely room with a big double bed & Coog & I tossed in together. Madame was waiting for us in the morning with a cup of coffee she was exceptionally nice. We are now billeted in the Village school have two front rooms & use of kitchen we are in clover the folk are goodo. Have secured two angle folding beds, tres fron. Went for a stroll with our Interpreter & visited quite a number of places the folk are tres tres gentile, hope our stay is not limited it is the nicest Village we have yet been in, the people are all so clean & the place is spotless. After we made our beds this afternoon I asked the 3 mademoiselles to have a look at our handiwork & they promptly tore into them
