<p>Thursday 27 September 1917<br/>Seas still fairly rough &amp; boat pitching.  Fiddles in use on tables.<br/>Saw our gun at stern of boat only small (submarine), 2 engineers in charge, capable of damaging enemy at 1 mile distant.<br/>Distance eye can reach at sea about 12 miles.<br/>One member of crew afraid for last few days to go up nose of boat for fear of being washed overboard.</p>
