<p>felt for his loss my expression of sympathy to Mrs. Read &amp; yourself.  I saw Fletcher early in January.  He is attached to the 14th Batt. He came up to them about Xmas.  Ben &amp; Ron Mackenzie &amp; Mayo all at last have come to Field Ambulances since December. Morris however is still holding his own.</p>
<p>We have had a pretty severe winter over here &amp; today it is snowing &amp; then shining intermittently, but the wind is cold.  However another month will see fairly respectable weather.  The trees will bud &amp; the weather warmer the days longer.  I have been in France now 11 months &amp; a half so the seasons ought soon to recapitulate.</p>
