<p>a surprise as mine.  Ellis is reading, just fancy Him reading or playin' cards.  He's gone to the dorgs.  I was getting some hot-water by our patent Electric cooker to-night &amp; there was a short circuit somewhere &amp; when I took the can of water off the stand, I was getting a terrific shock &amp; could not let the confounded thing go.  After hopping round like a Hottentot for nearly half-a-minute, I managed to chuck it up in the air.  You aught to have seen the mess.  It was all over the floor &amp; walls, in all the drawers &amp; cupboard.  The place looks like a 2nd Hand shop with all the gear out to be dried.  It is raining like the deuce but</p>
