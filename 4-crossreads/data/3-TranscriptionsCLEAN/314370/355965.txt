  
 January, 1916 Wednesday 12  Mum's Birthday  After the mornings work was over I went onto Cairo to try to get some Bostock Books the afternoon being a holiday But owing to the wreck of the "Persia" these books are not available. Everything they cannot find any other excuse for they blame on to the "persia" 
 Thursday 13 Very dull & signs of rain Work as usual Go with dad over to L.H. lines & score a pair of leggings. The 1st L.H. Regt are to ride to Wardan starting at daybreak tomorrow
