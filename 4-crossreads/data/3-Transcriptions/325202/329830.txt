<p>on Tuesday evening reported to D.E. billetted in a brewery at at little village of Tilques about 5 miles from St Omer</p>
<p>Too late to get anything hot to eat or drink in the village &amp; as we were cold &amp; famished I told Bob to look after my horses &amp; I would do some foraging. Appealed to a Mlle in the Brewery Yard about making us some hot coffee &ndash; She came back &amp; said yes come into the house</p>
<p>So in a few minutes we entered the back &amp; found the Mlle who turned out to be the servant &ndash; seated in the back living room with the Brewer &amp; his wife who were just finishing</p>
