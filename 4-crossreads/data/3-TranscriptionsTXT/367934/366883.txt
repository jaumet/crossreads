 [Transcriber's note:  Sapper Vasco of the Field Company Engineers joined the Army on 11 May 1916 and embarked on the Suevic on 11 November 1916.  He was a Caricature Artist and Draftsman and there are many of his drawings in the following letters to his wife from England and from France.  He died of illness on 3 August 1918 and is buried in Hatfield Cemetery, St. Albans, United Kingdom.] 
 Australian Red Cross 
 "This side of the fence is out of bounds to all ranks and patients." 
 "A study in Blues" 
 Yours truly "Vasco on the Rocks" 47th General Hospital France June 20th 1918
