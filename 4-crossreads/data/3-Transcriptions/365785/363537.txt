<p>it may not be true, but if it is is certainly surprising, but one gets many surprises &amp; shocks.  The Staff Sargeant appeared this morning in three stars &amp; looked patronisingly around on the V.A.D. workers when we arrived.<br/>The Aus. Comfort Funds meeting to-morrow is to arrange all about Xmas gifts, and is to be undertaken on rather a large scale.  Mr. Budden will be away at Anzac and is taking 5000 cases over with him.  The work is growing rapidly and I think successfully now.  Mr. B. is full of energy and a good organiser, a man of fine personality &amp; I think is very decidedly "The right-man in the right-place".</p>
