 June, 1917. Thursday 28 Rained a good deal last night and flooded some out. Fine day and very heavy thunderstorm in evening our roof like watering can. 6.30 parade &amp; 8.30 down to river races late at 10.15 instead of 9.45. Parade at 1.30 and home 5.30. and after finished letters Tug of war won by 2nd Coy I pulled rottenly. N.S.W. interstate. H section inter company race.
