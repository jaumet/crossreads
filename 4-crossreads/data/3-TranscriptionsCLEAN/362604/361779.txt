
I went up to Mr. Edmonds.  We had music & chatter until 9 p.m., more supper, & one of the Daughters & myself took the two prentice Girls Home.  To learn their trade which is Stationery or Haby., their mother pays 10 per annum & although the Girls receive no pay, all the Firm find is food, worse than Army tucker, & lodging.
