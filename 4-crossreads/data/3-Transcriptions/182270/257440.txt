
<p>THURSDAY, MAY 8, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Working round house in</p>
<p>morning. Peter &amp; I had an early</p>
<p>lunch &amp; took the big car into</p>
<p>Quirindi &amp; caught the 9.40</p>
<p>train to Sydney.</p>
<p>I inspected some X bred</p>
<p>ewes of Kimbers on the way</p>
<p>&amp; bought them at 30/-</p>
