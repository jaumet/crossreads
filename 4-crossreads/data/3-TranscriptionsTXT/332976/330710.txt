
June 191625th   Sunday- First Sunday at Christchurch.  Church Parade was held & I went to the Priory- an old church of great notoriety.  It was built first by the Normans in the 11th century & added to during the preceding centuries, & is of great interest.  It is situated on the banks of the river & has on its left the ruins of an old castle, destroyed hundreds of years ago6th, 9th, 10th 12th  see further on
