 THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 4, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; hot. Lopped in Top paddock 
 &amp; Mountain in morning and cutting 
 cotton bush in the afternoon. 
 Linda came down in afternoon 
 and we had some tea in Wheelers 
 Tickle poisoning in Wheelers &amp; 
 Middle paddock. 
 Barry came out &amp; got here for 
 lunch.
