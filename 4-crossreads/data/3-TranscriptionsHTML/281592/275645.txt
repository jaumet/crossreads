<p>On duty in No. 6 ward.</p>
<p>Most of the British troops left hospital to-day.</p>
<p>Came across large numbers of camp stools which had pieces of carpet with patterns on them the same as I have often seen woven in Forthbank</p>
<p>July 1st 2nd Frid 3rd 4th Sun.</p>
<p>Went to communion in the Scots Church.</p>
<p>Large turn out of the Military.  Womens Guild belonging to the church are going to entertain the soldiers who are in the habit of attending the church.</p>
<p>Walked along the promenade on the bank of the Nile.</p>
