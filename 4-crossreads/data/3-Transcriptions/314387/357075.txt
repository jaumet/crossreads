<p>Sunday 27th<br/>Regimental guard all day<br/>Monday 28th<br/>Came off guard nine o'clock went on kit inspection<br/>Tuesday 29th<br/>Paraded morning full marching order afternoon fatigue.<br/>Wednesday 30th<br/>Marched out of Derres 3-45 in morning arrived Famer about seven entrained and left about eleven arrived &amp; Neuve Eglise six marched to Billets</p>
