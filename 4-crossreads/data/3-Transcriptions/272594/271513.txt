<p>In the trench you never saw such a mess up in your life there were men from every unit in the 2nd Div. they were thoroughly disorganised &amp; the trench itself the wonderful Hindenberg line supposed to be impregnable you should have seen it, it had once been a fine &amp; deep trench &amp; well supplied with the big dugouts which every German loves to hide in, but after the battering our guns had given it, well it resembled a series of holes in the ground</p>
