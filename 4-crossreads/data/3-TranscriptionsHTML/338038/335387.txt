We are extremely fortunate as the man who has been appointed to succeed Gen Holmes in command of the Division (Gen MacLagan) is a thorough good sort.  Gen Rosenthal had gone to command an infantry Brigade, but I think it's only a preparatory step to his promotion to a Divisional Commander.  They are so many changes going on these</p>
