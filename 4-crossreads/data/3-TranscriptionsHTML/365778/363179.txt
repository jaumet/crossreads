<p>Tuesday 6/3/17<br/>Fixed things OK, our shelling a bit short, handed line over to Norrie &amp; move into Support at Le Sars &ndash; Letter from the General.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7/3/17</p>
<p>Moved back to Reserve in 26th Avenue &ndash; men looking well. Letters from Doff.</p>
<p>Thursday 8/3/17</p>
<p>Move back to Acid Drop for a rest, relieved by Reid.</p>
<p>Friday 9/3/17</p>
<p>Went to Division, had visit from Whiteman of R.F.C. wrote to General &amp; Doff.</p>
