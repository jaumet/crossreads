<p>Hospital London<br/>23 Sept 16<br/>My dearest Sister,<br/>A huge mail again yesterday from the Regiment viz:- 3 from you 2 Mater 1 Dad 2 from Ulmarra 1 Sydney &amp; others from London, I had a grand time wading through it.<br/>So pleased to hear you are all well at Las Palmas &amp; that Ralph is bucked up I hope he gets through his exam this time O.K.<br/>I am sending you some cards in separate cover the group of three &amp; one in Park taken during my leave. The chap sitting in group was a great pal of mine he was killed at Pozieres.<br/>I am getting on fine now dear, go out for lunch every day &amp; matinee every afternoon, having a fine time, I have struck a London chap who knows his London well</p>
