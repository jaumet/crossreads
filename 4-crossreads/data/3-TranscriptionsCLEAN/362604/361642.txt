  
 Australian Red Cross 
 Bulford 20/4/18 
 Dear Dad, Mum & George, 
 We have had several letters from you all but Ellis has taken them all away to answer & He's out to-night.  I'll get them all & answer later in the week.  We have had a horrid week of rain, hail, snow & ice.  I hope it clears up soon because we want to play cricket & Tennis.
