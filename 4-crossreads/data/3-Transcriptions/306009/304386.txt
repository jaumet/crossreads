<p>1915<br/>July 19 Mon.<br/>Got in a good French lesson this afternoon from an Italian chap working in the Officers' Mess.<br/>The Mahomedan Fast of Ramedan is now on involving the abstinence from food &amp; drink between sunrise &amp; sunset. Still 24 of the 30 days fast to go.</p>
