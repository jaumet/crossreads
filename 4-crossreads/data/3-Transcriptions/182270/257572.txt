<p>WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 17, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Linda &amp; I went for</p>
<p>a ride round Ben Ledi &amp; Seven Star</p>
<p>&amp; 4D in morning. About the</p>
<p>house in afternoon &amp; we all went</p>
<p>into Quirindi (in&nbsp;<s>(</s>&nbsp;the two cars)</p>
<p>for dinner at night. Went to the</p>
<p>pictures afterwards. Linda &amp; Myee</p>
<p>&amp; Graham caught the 9.30 train</p>
<p>to Sydney. We came back</p>
<p>after.</p>
