<p>caught the train back to<br/>Ludgershall. I felt pretty tired after my three days walking, and didn't enjoy the walk of about a mile back to camp. I reached camp just after midnight and was handed a few letters by my pal corporal Corkhill. After reading my mail, I had to turn round and pack up all my gear, as we were leaving next day.  I had very little sleep before being turned, out at fire, and being an NCO.<br/>I had had quite a lot to do.<br/>We marched <strike>finished</strike> fifteen miles to Rollestone camp</p>
