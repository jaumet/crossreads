<p>1915<br/>Feb. 2 Tue.<br/>Worked until 8 p.m. - Then visited S.M. Doyle (A.M.C. detail, 2nd Inf.) &amp; S.M. Francis (Pte. 3rd Inf.) who were extremely surprised to see <strike>him</strike> me.<br/>Wrote a couple of letters.<br/>Number of patients in Hospital 658.</p>
