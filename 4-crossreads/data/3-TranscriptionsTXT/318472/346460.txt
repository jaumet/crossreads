 23 August 1918 Friday A fairly heavy breeze &amp; a rising sea getting up to quite a considerable roll by bed time. Nothing extraordinary though &ndash; just a few things being dislodged (seasick ch &ndash; Serg?) R.M. 
 1918 August 24 Saturday A nice fine day &amp; the sea has calmed down considerably only a small roll now running. Nothing of importance to note.
