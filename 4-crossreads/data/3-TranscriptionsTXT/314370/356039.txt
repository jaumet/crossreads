 June, 1916 Thursday 8 Work as usual all morning &amp; after dinner get the guns &amp; Amtn ready for the range tomorrow. Rigged an old belt filling machine &amp; it did very good service in filling 20 belts in an hour 
 Friday 9 Reveille  4 am Breakfast  4.15 " Target Party  4.30 " Waggons  4.45 " Range  5.30 " first shot fired  6.15 " Each man fired a group of 20 rds &amp; a traverse of 60 rds at Khaki targets at 400 Gun works perfectly fired 1300 Rds without a stoppage.
