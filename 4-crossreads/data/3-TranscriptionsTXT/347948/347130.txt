
18th  Have been out watching a trout-fisher. It appears that the trout cannot be caught unless they rise, as when they are to be seen at the bottom they are feeding and will not bite.The method of fishing for them with a rod continually casting, the line only staying in the water about 2 seconds, rather mystified us.
