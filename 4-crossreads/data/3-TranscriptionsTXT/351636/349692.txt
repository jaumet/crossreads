 The Centre for Soldiers' Wives &amp; Mothers, 92B. Pitt Street, Sydney. 7-3-16 
 Dear Mr. Layton Could you assist Mrs. Saunders in the payment of debts. She is owing rent &amp; grocer's bill. She was left in debt when her husband sailed &amp; has been paying off gradually &ndash; Having an invalid son &amp; delicate daughter she finds it difficult to pay up in full 
 Yours faithfully B. Booth
