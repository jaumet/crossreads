<p>At 11 a.m. Roy &amp; I leave the Rio Pardo and walk into Cape Town.&nbsp; After a stroll along the main thoroughfare (Adderley St) and a spell in the Anzac Lounge (an institution which is under Y.U.C.A. supervision) we spend an hour of observation.</p>
