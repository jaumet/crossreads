<p>The Honorable<br/>Mr Justice Ferguson<br/>Judges Chambers<br/>Supreme Court<br/>Sydney<br/>New South Wales<br/>Australia</p>
<p>Camped at X passing thro Egypt.  The building is the Heliopolis Hotel a place out of the Arabian Nights over 1000 rooms &amp; now a huge hospital.  Hear Mr. Justice Street's nephew is better.  Am still fit but would sometimes like a quiet morning with an undefended ejectment suit.</p>
<p>Regards to [indecipherable] &amp; Byrne<br/>Chas G. Addison</p>
