<p>September 1918.<br/>Tuesday 24</p>
<p>Peouilly and La Vigure &ndash; Bty Pos.<br/>Weather very showery during the morning, toward afternoon the weather cleared. Relieved by the 231st and returned to W.L. I've made a slight mistake we are still in action.<br/>Had another night of liveliness 6 inch How. Bty pulled into position in rear of Bty.<br/>Fritz still barraging ridge and recaptured a little village Palavie.<br/>Great success by Allenby in Palestine. Troops seem to be going well in Salonika</p>
