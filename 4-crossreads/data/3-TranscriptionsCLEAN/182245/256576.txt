 MARCH 1917 
 2 FRIDAY 
 Fine & misty. Cleaning harness 
 all morning & out exercising 
 horses in afternoon. Campbell 
 came up & was quite affable 
 & asking about the RFC & my 
 chances of getting into it. 
 got three letters from Linda 
 & some from home & Coomoo 
 Coomoo & was jolly glad to 
 get them. Also a couple 
 of Xmas cards from the 
 Camerons. 
 
 Friday 2 nd  March. 
 Fine & Sunny. Lay in till 11 oclock 
 & then did stables. Had a half 
 holiday in afternoon & lay 
 in bed & read. Got an 
 accordian from Steenwerck 
 for the hut.
