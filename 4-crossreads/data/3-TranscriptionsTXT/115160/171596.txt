Dear Lock,
Yours of 30th now to hand, altho' Bess and Dad's of 12 May beat it.  I see you have turned up trying to pick em.  Just as well when 40 & 50 to 1 chances win.  Bess says Miller still tries to pick 'em too, and his promises are thereby delayed indefinitely.
That wasn't bad going - 5 gross of buns.  You seem to keep up your past performances in this annual line.
