  Justice  will not be denied him Flight is impossible. That bayonet  is  gleams bright chill and merciless. A bullet rending and crashing through human flesh &ndash; horrible. That Limb of Justice is swift and possesses powerful hands. How powerful he, the accused,  alone  well knows. No; he cannot escape and with this knowledge comes a deluge of Fear which floods his brain. Hate squelches and gurgles in the Deluge and is drowned and now the satellites of Fear, Mercy and Pity have come. But who will be Merciful and who will Pity? Only the Gods.
