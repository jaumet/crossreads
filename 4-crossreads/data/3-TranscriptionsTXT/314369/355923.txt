 21 November 1915 Sunday 25th After Trinity 
 More excitement, all English troops are recalled to join their regiments in the event of having to move out and take a hand in the expected native rising Composite Light Horse Regt. leave camp mounted Destination unknown 
 1915 November 22 Monday 
 Things as usual at school. No more news. Receive two parcels from home
