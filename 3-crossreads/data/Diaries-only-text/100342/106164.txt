18th 
Notice at 1 p.m. to be ready. Heavy marching order at 3.30 p.m. Paraded 4.15 after organization moved off for beach, viz., Rest Gully, embarked about 7.45 aboard H.M.S. Mars, small Gun Boat. 5th 6th Bgds. 13th L.H. represented. Closely missed collission with Tug astern of Hospital Ship. Beachy Bill very quiet, no shots while we were near. Bread Coffee freely issued while supply lasted. Canteen open but sold out by 10.30, snatched couple hours sleep on Gun deck. Good drink hot tea, biscuits cheese 3 a.m.
No. 2 Post.
