<p>1915<br/>Apr. 8 Thu.<br/>Got another letter to-day bringing up this week's total to 14.<br/>The 5th. &amp; 8th. Batteries of Field Artillery left Mena about 6 p.m. &ndash; destination unknown.<br/>Went to Pathe Freres Picture Show at Mena Camp in the evening.</p>
