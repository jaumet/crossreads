 It makes a great hit -  Booking duties  &amp; rehearsals during the day &ndash; The criticism of Major Maclaine, a "Gentleman of Scotland", who is interested in theatricals. He says (despite the fact that he is in charge of a very popular Concert Party): "Saa-eeda Digger!" has been well written &amp; produced.  It is the most original &amp; artistic show I have seen in France. The artists have been excellently cast for the several characters. If there is any fault it is that in parts it is a little too artistic for troops.
