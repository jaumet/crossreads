 [Wednesday 29 November 1916] 
 Arrived safely presented Skipper &amp; Chief Officers with silver smoking outfits- in recognition of their skills.  Ordered report Ismalia Regiment near Bir el Abb  Stayed the night at Windsor Hotel  Alexandria  &amp; with a party from Ship heard old Ada Raeve sing- The Long Long Trail a very pretty popular sad song I shall leave a lot of Kit with the Base Stores- Gezirah  Cabled the wee wife &amp; also Mother.
