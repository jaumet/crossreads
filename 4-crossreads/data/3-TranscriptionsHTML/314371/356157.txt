<p>May 7th Tues.<br/>Grand Concert by Aussies in City Hall.<br/>Have been going all over the country seeing everything worth looking at.<br/>Went one day w/- large party to town of Stellenboseh &amp; had a fine time.</p>
<p>Sun. 19th May<br/>Orders to pack up.</p>
<p>May 20th Monday<br/>Embarked on TSS Tofua.</p>
<p>May 21st Tue.<br/>Pulled out at dawn &amp; sailed about 11 a.m., very rough &amp; stormy.</p>
<p>May 22 &amp; 23, Wed., Thur.<br/>Still very rough &amp; rolling, tucker &amp; quarters pretty good.</p>
