<p>August -21 &ndash; 25</p>
<p>Aug 21st<br/>Left N'mine for Sydney  Exped. Force, had a good reception all along the line in troop train</p>
<p>Sat 22nd<br/>Arrived <strike>Redfern</strike> Darling Harbor at eleven oclock was met by Mrs Pen, Jessie &amp; Mylie Edwards &amp; family, arrived at Rosebery about sundown</p>
<p>Sun 23<br/>Found out at 8 o'clock we had to supply our own cooks, one cook put a knife in his leg, &amp; I volunteered &amp; had breakfast ready by dinner time, it saved cooking dinner</p>
<p>Mon 24th<br/>Resigned the cooking in favour of better man, &amp; started drill.  Uncle Jack came in &amp; saw me for half an hour &amp; had a job to get out again  no passes were given &amp; some of the lads scaled the fence &amp; went to the city</p>
<p>Tue 25th<br/>Passed in all accoutrements belonging to the 9th L.H. our horses examined &amp; Idleboy bought &pound; 16-0-0, we received a lecture for [indecipherable], &amp; trampling the caretakers garden</p>
