
23rd Aug Arrived Cape Town
Ashore this evening with Padre
23rd -29th  Ashore on leave every day
30th Left at 2.30 pm for Durban. Boat rolled badly.  Many sick.  Almost stopped opposite lighthouse
31st 218 miles (Sunday)
Going better but still rolling heavily.
Services as usual
Meeting in afternoon. " Reconstruction from Christian Standpoint"
1st  248 miles. Classes recommenced. Rumoured pay tomorrow. Badly needed on all hands.
2nd 263
3rd Arrived Durban last night.  Anchored all night off harbour, noticing the twinkling lights of town & wharves.  Ship rolled pretty badly.  6 am started to move towards land from which we had drifted a considerable distance, and went at very slow speed.
