 Aug 26 (Sunday) Our 1st Division has done its bit on the Somme &amp; has pulled out. 
 Thursday we trekked back to Rubempre, Friday on to within two miles of Doullens where we entrained yesterday morning &amp; came North again. We detrained last night at Hopontre Siding near Poperinghe &amp; are bivouaced close by waiting to march to our billets on this sector of the Ypres front.
