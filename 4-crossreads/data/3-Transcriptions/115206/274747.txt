<p>June 1918<br/>Wednesday 26</p>
<p>In Billets. Erronvillle<br/>Weather glorious a perfect day.<br/>Rode to Abbeville with Smithy as horseholder. He is the luckiest person I know to be granted ten days leave to Paris.<br/>Abbeville is slightly bent in places owing to the attention paid to it by Fritzer planes.<br/>Paid a visit to my friend of yesterday  Suzanne , and had some oeuf good mangy.</p>
