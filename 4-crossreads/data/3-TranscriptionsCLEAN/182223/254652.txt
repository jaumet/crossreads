  
 Thursday 8 April 1915 Acting O. C. today Colonel Martin at Alexandria   Letters from Mrs Reardon of Lock Gur, Kilmallock Co. Limerick Ireland 
 Friday 9 April 1915 Colonel Martin returned at 12.30 pm. He took over duties of O.C.  Riding between 5 & 6-30 pm. Letter from College of Surgeons & George Reid. 
 Saturday 10 April 1915 Colonel Martin away most of the day, acting O.C. for him   Not out during the day  Posted letters to Girls, Mollie & Doffie 
 Sunday 11 April 1915 Mass at 7-30 a.m. A padre from S.A., Adelaide diocese, a Tipperary man, officiated.  Wrote David Storey M.L.A. Randwick  Riding on dessert during the evening 1 hr 5-30 to 6-30 o'ck
