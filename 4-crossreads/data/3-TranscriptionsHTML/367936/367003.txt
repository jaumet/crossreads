<p>Monday 3 July 1916<br/>Dose of caster oil!</p>
<p>Tuesday 4 July 1916<br/>Rostron, Nelson, Vint &amp; I went for a swim in afternoon then Rostron &amp; I went to Alex.  I got my films &ndash; picture show not bad.<br/>Lecture on Ancient Egypt by Dr. Arnold very interresting.  Had a talk to Lin Alworth.</p>
