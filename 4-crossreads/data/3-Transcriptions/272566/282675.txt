<p>Sunday 14 October</p>
<p>To "K" Supply Column 3 times today and once to Steenvoorde, passing through Abeele and past a fine big aerodrome<br/>To A.D.M.S. office at Lille Gate   Ypres along the main Poperinghe-Ypres Road<br/>Filled in the evening by playing cards near a good fire.  It has been a lovely day but got cold towards dusk.</p>
