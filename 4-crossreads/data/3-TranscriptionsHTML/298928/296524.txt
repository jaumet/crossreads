<p>31st Jan.  Left Meteren for canteen Corner, a muddy camp about <br/>2 kilos from Steenwerck.</p>
<p>3 rd Feb.  Played at church parade and presentation of prizes by <br/>General Birdwood, over 20 men were decorated with D.C.M's <br/>&amp; M M's  one with both.</p>
<p>5th  Left Canteen Corner for Blighty Leave.</p>
