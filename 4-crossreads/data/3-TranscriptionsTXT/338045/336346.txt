 Nov. 3 &ndash; Friday Awake at 6-30 a.m. &amp; make my bed.  Still raining &amp; blowing like fury.  I enjoyed the most comfortable sleep for some time in a nice warm bed.  Hardly believe it to be true.  Examined by the doctor &amp; history of my case taken &amp; from what I can gather have not much chance of getting away for a month at least.  Feeling pretty well knocked up, feverish &amp; head aching, so don't want to leave.  Am so thin that the sister of ward gives all sorts of food to build me up.
