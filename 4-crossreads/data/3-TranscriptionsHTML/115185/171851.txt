<p>drive up to the hospital building a three winged building &ndash; Asiatic type &ndash; a college originally &ndash; at the gates our Australian soldiers kept guard &ndash; real bushmen &ndash; sleeves rolled up &amp; riding pants held on by a scarf &ndash; shirt open &amp; felt hats. "Halt! Who goes there!" I could hear from my bed all hours of the night from their lusty lungs. Last night I was looking over my balcony &ndash; watching them "Two french off &ndash; i &ndash; cer" was the reply one time. They didn't care who it was the Kaiser himself wouldn't have perturbed</p>
<p>their calm ownership of the way. The Mediterranean was away to my left up a dusty road. Dust &amp; sand &amp; fleas everywhere. The Gatehouse was where I slept &ndash; a house rented at the hospital gates for a nurses home. Very prettily furnished. Arabic servants &amp; all comforts. Piano large chairs &amp; chesterfield sofas &ndash; roses in bowls - &amp; meals served in dainty China &ndash; if not very well cooked.
