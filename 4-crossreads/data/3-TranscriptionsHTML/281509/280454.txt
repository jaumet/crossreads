<p>1916 August 19<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>The night proved of varying incidents. Part of the stunt was successful &amp; part failed. Fritz was particularly active &amp; sent 2 shells on to Casualty Corner. Capt. Graham received leg wound &amp; shellshocks. Slept fairly well &amp; felt much better this morning. Artillery very quiet on both sides this morning. back to Lee3's Post this afternoon, carry back to Corner. Patient shell wound in back, horrible sight.<br/>Birthday cake arrives from Jean a magnificent beauty in perfect condition. Give half it to boys, rum &amp; cake up in German dugout. How pleased Jean would have been if she could only have seen we  It could not have come at a better time.</p>
