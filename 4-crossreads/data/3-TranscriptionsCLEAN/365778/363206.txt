
Monday 9/7/17Umpiring for Div Scheme, going all day, very tired.Tuesday 10/7/17Field Firing, Pow Wow of Umpires during the afternoon.Wednesday 11/7/17Training continued, saw Bas, went to 28th Bn concert.Thursday 12/7/17Reconnoitred ground for scheme tomorrow with all officers.Friday 13/7/17Brigade Scheme, General sent us to repeat where ignorance is bliss etc -Saturday 14/7/17Corps School near Albert for demonstration very good - won a rifle match.
