<p>Arrived England (Plymouth) midnight Feb. 20th 1915.  Stayed in Leinster Sq., Bayswater &ndash; 4 rooms at &amp;pound;7.7.0 per wk. fires extra 8d. per scuttleful.  No light &ndash; when light came on a man in dinner jacket suspenders &amp; socks was seen in full view going to the bath.</p>
