 April &amp; May &nbsp; 1918 
 28 SUN &nbsp;Dull day. 
 Very quiet. 
 Posted letter to Maggie &amp; (Amy 
 enc.) Washing. Heavy bom- 
 bardment by us at night. Bty 
 adv. about 1500 yds Lt. Short &amp; Dr Kld 
 29 MON &nbsp;Dull day. Heavy 
 firing day &amp; night. 102 Bty 
 shelled out at W.L. &nbsp; W.O. White 
 &amp; W. Rump. paid us a visit 
 Turned in early. 
 30 TUES &nbsp;Dull day. Rec. letter 
 from Else P. Quiet. Went 
 to D.A. at night called 
 at Hondegem on return. 
 Good time. 
 1 MAY WED &nbsp;Dull day. Heavy 
 firing. Went 
 to Hondengem in aft. Met S.S. 
 Rec. letter from Amy. Quiet 
 Writing at night.
