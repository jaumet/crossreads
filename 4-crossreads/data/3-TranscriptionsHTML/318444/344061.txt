<p>Mon Nov 17th:-<br/>286 miles to midday getting around Cape Verde</p>
<p>Tues Nov 18th:-<br/>The mileage has dropped to 259. The heat must be playing up with the trimmers.</p>

<p>Thurs Nov 20th:-</p>
<p>288 miles. "Crossed the Line" at 2pm The usual foolery.</p>

<p>Sat Nov 22nd:-<br/>287 miles.</p>
<p>Sun Nov 23rd:-<br/>285 miles</p>
<p>Mon Nov 24th:-<br/>281 miles. We are about level with St Helena now.</p>
<p>Tues Nov 25th:-<br/>286 miles</p>
<p>Wed Nov 26th:-<br/>280 miles</p>
<p>Thurs Nov 27th:-<br/>262 miles. The weather is getting windy &amp; cold now.</p>
