<p>22 &ndash; E deM<br/>may be seen some of the beauty of England &amp; what with them in their cool summer dresses the lovely weather and the band all add to the charm of passing a pleasing afternoon in such pleasant company as one lady cousin.<br/>During my stay here, I see a great deal of Brighton &amp; the surrounding Country. The downs at this time of the year looking beautifully and green, such a green that is never seen in Australia.<br/>One Saturday afternoon Uncle Harry took me out to a place called Kingscote some distance away to see the Cruickshanks, Mother's &amp; Aunts cousins. They live in a very large mansion with an enormous library, polished oak floors with skins of different animals that he had killed in Africa he being a big game hunter, he has also a large room filled with stuffed animals including two lions both of which he killed the same day they being together at the time were brought down with two consective shots.
