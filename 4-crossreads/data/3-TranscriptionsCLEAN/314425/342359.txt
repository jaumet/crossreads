  
 Thursday Dec. 19. 1918. 39. I feel much better this morning. My temperature respiration & pulse are again nominal. My temperature ranged from 103. down to 97. There is not much news in the papers now. President Wilson got a great reception in France. Haig & some of his Generals are coming to London today. 
 Friday Dec. 20. 1918. 40. I feel much better today. Though I had a bad night. I could not sleep though I was given Brandy and sleeping draughts, but all in vain. 
 I coughed most of the night. I slept several this this forenoon. There is a registered parcel for me at the P.O. The parcel turned out to be from Mrs A. Pavey. After having my signature witnessed on a card & sending up my pay book I was able to obtain it. The parcel contained a tin coffee biscuits 1 pkt. butter scotch. 1 pkt. loaf sugar. 1 tin tea tablets & 1 pkt spearmints. 1 tin veal & tongue paste. 1 large cake chocolate 7 items in all.
