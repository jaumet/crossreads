<p>ordered to sort ourselves out and rejoin our own regiments.  This was accomplished by the following day and then it was exciting to find old chums still unscathed and ready to swop lies.  Very few of us had been able to wash since the landing and this together with our torn muddy clothing gave us a very 'distinguished' appearance.  As very little fighting was taking place parties of us were allowed to go on the beach for a swim.  After I had shaved and had my hair cut with a pair of blunt nail scissors and mended a big rent in the seat of my breeks I felt quite spry.
