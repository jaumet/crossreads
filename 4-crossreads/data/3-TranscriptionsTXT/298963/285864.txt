 We must bear our lot uncomplainingly &amp; make our own fun.  Played chess with Brownie, whom I beat after a good fight I finished a "perfect day" &ndash; I don't think &ndash; with a small gain of 1d. Poker with Metcalf's crowd.  We must do something here on these gruesome days.  Harold &amp; Billy, Toby &amp; Frank moved to Amiens on leave. 
 12 June Joe has fallen in love with the Marquises daughter &ndash; lucky dog &ndash; a skit has been put up.  I hope he takes a hint in time.
