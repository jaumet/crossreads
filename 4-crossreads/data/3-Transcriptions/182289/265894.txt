<p>16.5.18<br/>Orders received for a Battalion parade on Saturday the 18th.  Instructions given that limbers, equipment ect. should be cleaned.<br/>Sections given an hour's lecture on Bombing by Divisional Bombing N.C.O.<br/>Orders received at 10.30 p.m. that Company would parade as part of Battalion under 11th Brigade for inspection by the Commander-in-Chief on the 17th at about 3 p.m.</p>
