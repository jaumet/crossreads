<p>"Grazier Office"<br/>233 Elizabeth St<br/>Brisbane<br/>2/5/17</p>
<p>Dear Arthur<br/>When we got the news of Dene's death, we both realized what a grip the boy had on our hearts. He is at peace. You and yours are left to suffer, &amp; I realise how deep &amp; bitter is the tragedy of the war to you all. And the whole wide world weeps with your woe.</p>
<p>The subject is too deep and awful</p>
