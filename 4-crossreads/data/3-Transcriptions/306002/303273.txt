<p>31.8.15<br/>Tue. 31th.  Last night the Turks mounted a machine gun on the earthworks they have lately been building &amp; trained it down this gully, but they started it a little too late as we had all just retired to our dug-outs for the night.  This morning our artillery again opened fire &amp; blew a lump off the top of the works.  Murray who was sent away sick a few weeks ago returned yesterday &amp; said the boys sent to Lemnos with this disease which is so prevalent amongst us, get no attention paid them at all.  They are put in tents &amp; when they wake up in the morning they have to fold &amp; stack their blankets neatly on one side of</p>
