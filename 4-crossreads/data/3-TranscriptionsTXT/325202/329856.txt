 Monday (April 23 [about]) We left Etaples on  Wednesday   Thursday  Wednesday morning &amp; arrived at Albert during the afternoon &amp; camped there the night. Next morning marched down to the gas school &amp; were issued with box Helmets &amp; put through the gas test left there about midday &amp; marched through Contalmaison to Bazentin where we camped the night in some huts.
