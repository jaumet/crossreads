  
 Juillet 23 Coy on usual Section Drill & getting ready for a move somewhere in the near future. Painting transport etc. 
 Juillet 24 8th 14th & 15th F Coys journeyed to Bray & played the 1st Div. F Coys football. 1st Div. won all games. I met a lot of the old boys of the 1st F Coy with whom I came away from Sydney 18/10/14. Had an enjoyable game.
