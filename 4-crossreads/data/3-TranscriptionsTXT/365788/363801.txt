 This is Sunday  Morning &amp; I have just been to Church Parade &amp; Holy Communion &amp; thought of you all &amp; sent up a Prayer for you all. Will you please ask Arch  to send me a statement showing how I stand, &amp; ask him to give Fred Sherwin my address &amp; please ring up Miss Rossitter &amp; tell her of my new address &amp; ask her to tell Beatrice Glasson. We hear that Rommania  &amp; Bulgaria are at war. Very much love dear old Girl to you all &amp; Kisses to the [indecipherable]. Your loving Brotheer Fred
