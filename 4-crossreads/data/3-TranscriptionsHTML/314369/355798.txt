<p>16 March 1915<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Nothing in sight today<br/>We are nearing Aden  &amp;  the straits<br/> Weather very warm   &amp;  sultry.<br/>An Irishman's Gale blowing that is straight up   &amp;  down the funnel.</p>
<p>1915 March 17<br/>Wednesday<br/>Bank Holiday, Ireland</p>
<p>Making better speed again, we can just see the Bee ahead so we must be gaining on her<br/>Pass Aden   &amp;<br/>enter the red Sea<br/>Start the big fans in our end of the ship today<br/>Our company on duty<br/>Irish concert in the evening</p>
