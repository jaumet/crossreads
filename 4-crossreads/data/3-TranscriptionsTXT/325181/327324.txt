
19 Spt 1915general Service (CE) at 8.3020 More patients by train last night. Have a nice new bed now - spring- mattress. Toil fairly plentiful. 1/2 piastre left. How will I invest it?21 Slept soundly on top of bed. Nurse came & covered me with a blanket I was too tired apparently to get into bed. Many cases rupture here. Our Surgeon Capt Teague is excellent. Has not lost a case yet at this Hospital. Off duty at 2 pm today Having a rest just by way of a change.(22 ) Bad night. One of patients came in shickered - Went Dr yesty got medicine. Ordered back to bed today. Feeling weak Pay day today. Drew 4-5/-.  Batch of about 90 new patients came in last night.
