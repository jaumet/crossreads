<p>Feb. 9. the first week in February 1916 saw some heavy divisional training for the men of N.Z. &amp; Austrn. Div.<br/>Marching out brilliantly from camp and accompanied by the Band, the respective Batts. moved out in succession of fours. Having received instructions we moved off in artillery formation &amp; when within 2000 yds of our objective we extended to open order of fighting finishing up with an assault by storm.<br/>On Friday night at 9 we left camp &amp; returned in the morning at 8 after walking all night &amp; with practically no rest at all<br/>This most severe training after the strenuous work of the Peninsula [indecipherable] was unnecessary &amp; was too much for the men's stamina</p>
