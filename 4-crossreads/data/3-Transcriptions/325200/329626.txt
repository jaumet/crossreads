<p>Turks strongly entrenched with loads of artillery, had a two hours scrap  &amp;  had to give it up  &amp;  came back with their horses pretty done up.<br/>Tonight I believe we go to Bir el Abd  &amp;  home tomorrow.<br/>I am down with the signallers now, to take any messages or orders which may arrive.<br/>It is too dark to w rite any more.<br/>Nequilliet Monday 9 am<br/>In all we took 20 prisoners  &amp;  nine camels  &amp;  sustained in casualties two killed  &amp;  20 sick  &amp;  wounded. We collected the whole crowd at Salmana from the other two</p>
