<p>December  1918.<br/>Saturday 28</p>
<p>At Sea. Red Sea. "Karoola."<br/>Weather very hot, but tempered a little by a fair breeze from the Sth East.<br/>Played cribagge all day and read a book of Stevenson's poems. Travelled 27 miles for the 24 hours trip . issued with tobacco and chocolate, very decent too.</p>
