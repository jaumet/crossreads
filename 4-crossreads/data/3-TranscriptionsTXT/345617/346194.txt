 Septembre 29 Camped at the Butt last night &amp; were heavily shelled during the night. We are engaged in placing wire &amp; plotting it in on the map for the information of the incoming troops. The shelling is still very heavy on our positions. Our artillery is also very active on the enemy positions. 
 30-12-17 Tommy troops are relieving us tonight. Lt Wagstaffe-Sap[indecipherable] &amp; I surveyed the track back from the Butt to Chateau Wood. The shelling is still very heavy. We have returned H.Q. &amp; do not expect to go out onto this Front for awhile again.
