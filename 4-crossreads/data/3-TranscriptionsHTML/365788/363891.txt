<p>1st Field Amb.<br/>1 Aus Brigade<br/>1 Aus Division<br/>Aus Intermediate Base<br/> 27/2/16<br/>My dearest Irene,<br/>Yes- I think most of your letters &amp; parcels reach me eventually.<br/>Received a letter since I last wrote to you for which thanks.<br/>The parcel you mention having sent containing Tooth-brush, soap, camphor &amp; matches has not yet come, but will turn up within a day or so, I expect. Thank you very much for sending it. We got several tooth-brushes &amp; soap lately in Aus. gift stuffs &amp; are now getting matches as an issue</p>
