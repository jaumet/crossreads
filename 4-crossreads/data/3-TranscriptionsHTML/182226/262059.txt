<p>Dunday 6 February, 1916</p>
<p>Tucker getting very scarce. 1/4<sup>th</sup> tins of jam &amp; 1/6<sup>th</sup> of a tin of bully per man with biscuits &amp; about 1/4 lb of meat for one day.</p>
<p>An aeroplane flew over the camp this morning. She was a big British machine &amp; was flying very low, evidently scouting for Turks.</p>
