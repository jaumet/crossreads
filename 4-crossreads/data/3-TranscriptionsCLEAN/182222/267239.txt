 "white Coolies". These black Jamacian troops are dinkum soldiers, all speak English, are very polite and dignified and are loyal because they think they hate the Huns a degree worse than the British & Portugese. The local nigs are a semi-starved race of bandits & would cut a throat for a 1 d  as cheerfully as they will adopt any named creed for 21/2 d  . They dont like silver money (wise people!) knowing that copper does not pay the counterfeiter The men will undertake to do any work named & prefer that you dont state a figure --- the women then are sent out to do the work while the nig sits back & makes out a bill of costs that would shame a Pitt St shark to death.
