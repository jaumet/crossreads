<p>16 February 1918 Saturday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Brigade moved off &amp; by <u>clouds</u> we should be well wet tonight.<br/>X Secret orders but as no one save you will see this I hope- we are off to Jericho.X<br/>We expect to be away about 8 days- I left all but what I stand up in at Richon-<br/>X Camped tonight at the Junction of the Turks railway line where the Jaffa &amp; Beershebah lines join before Jerusalem.X<br/>All interesting country Purple iris  Almond blossom &amp; Anemone's everywhere.<br/>Tomorrow we will be among the stones of the Judean Hills.</p>
