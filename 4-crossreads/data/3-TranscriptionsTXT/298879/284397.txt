 June 1915 Sunday 27 Hot. A church service was held at the church tent this morning at 9 AM. Also a confirmation service at 6.30 AM. I attended neither I am ashamed to say. 
 Monday 28 Hot   Many warships have moved out of the harbour here. Judging from the noise last night there is evidently a big bombardment going on at Cape Helles.
