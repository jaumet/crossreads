<p>April, 1915<br/>Wednesday 14<br/>Baedacker's Egypt gives a good history of this country which like Ireland has been a most distressful one.<br/>Promised 8 day trek tomorrow. Have been feeling off color, cold throat; etc. etc:- but do not want to miss that. Altogether the health of the men &amp; horses has been very good -<br/>I am thinner but no worse for that.</p>
