<p>1918&nbsp;&nbsp; December</p>
<p>26 THUR&nbsp; Fine day Out with</p>
<p>the boys. morning &amp; aft. Took</p>
<p>girls to Dance at W&#39;Loo T.H.</p>
<p>said Goodbye to them.</p>
<p>Mrs Mooney etc. at 8.30</p>
<p>called at Capt Mooneys &amp; Mac Kinlays</p>
<p>said Goodbye to all. Left home at</p>
<p>11.PM. with Amy. Left Liverpool</p>
<p>12.30 am for London.</p>
<p>27 FRI&nbsp; arr. Euston. 7.30. Went to Brondesbury</p>
<p>met Polly 10.30 came into</p>
<p>London for lunch Maison Lyons. said</p>
<p>Goodbye to Pol &amp; met JWC. at 2.15</p>
<p>called at Horseferry Rd etc, went</p>
<p>to Pictures at night. Left for</p>
<p>Plymouth at 11.30 P.M.</p>
<p>28 SAT&nbsp; arr. Davenport 7. am. went</p>
<p>on to St. Budeaux Camp</p>
<p>place empty bar one Capt</p>
<p>Picked up seventeen in all</p>
<p>Secured warrant &amp; left at</p>
<p>1.30 for Davenport &amp; 2.34</p>
<p>for Salisbury &amp; on to</p>
<p>Codford. walked out to</p>
<p>Heytesbury. fixed up for</p>
<p>Blankets etc. by 8.45.</p>
<p>Good feed. turned in. dead</p>
<p>tired</p>
