<p>30th July (Sunday)</p>
<p>Up at about 10 o'clock.  Only two cases to carry down to Wye Farm.  Our Artillery battered the German trenches about and we were surprised that Fritz didn't retaliate.  Aircraft busy.</p>
<p>31st July (Monday)</p>
