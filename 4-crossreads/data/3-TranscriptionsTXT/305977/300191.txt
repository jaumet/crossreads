 Tuesday 23rd The whole Brigade went out to shamfight the 1st Brigade.  The 6th Regiment occupied the centre &amp; in the days operations did fairly well.  We finished up about 4 in the afternoon.  The B troop did particularly well &amp; the umpire gave us the Red flag.  The 1st Brigade bivouacked at Maadi for the night.  I was on guard &amp; was on first shift.  It was a good rest after the days work. 
 Wednesday 24th Got paid.  Drew 28/-.  Came off guard at 6.30pm &amp; went to bed early. 
 Thursday 25th Had a short mornings work attacking positions &amp; retiring under cover.  Did our second course of field firing in the afternoon.  The results of independent &amp; controlled firing were demonstrated.  We did fairly well, especially at the 500 yards
