<p>30/9/17<br/>Enemy aeroplanes bombed round here last night. There was a great deal of antiaircraft gun activity and the searchlight got onto several of his planes but the gunners failed to bring any down.</p>
<p>We expect to move from here tomorrow and go towards the lines. The Brigadier stated on</p>
