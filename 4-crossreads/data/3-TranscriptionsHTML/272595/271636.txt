<p>sitting in the seat just in front of us was a well dressed man with a short beard but we took no notice of him, though I could see the tram conductor was nearly killing himself with suppressed laughter, we got off at the terminus &amp; as we were walking away the guard said do you know who that was sitting in front of you, of course how should we know, well he said that was Von Bissing, pity we did not know it before for he would have learnt something about himself &amp; country, but he heard quite a lot of truths all the same.<br/>We left the next morning for Liverpool &amp; had a very enjoyable run across the boats are nicely fitted up, lounges dining &amp; smoke rooms &amp; etc, on the way across we got in with a nurse from Townleys Hos.
