 24-12-1917 Sick, in bed all day. 
 25-12-1917 Still off colour, Dogs disease.;The troops had an excellent dinner, consisting of, (turkey roast) 3 Francs 70 per lb. Peas, boiled potatoes, cabbage, and ham, also plum pudding and sauce, and one pint of beer per man 
 26-12-1917 Heavy fall of snow. Terrible wild night, About 4 m of snow; on the ground. Leather jerkins issued, Fit George Reid, Big stunt on 
 27-12-1917 Enemy planes over, dropped a few bombs. Weather mild. Snow still on ground. Great trouble with frost coggs. 
 28-12-1917 Cold, and windy. Enemy aeroplanes busy. 11 drivers evacuated to hospital in four days, suffering from Dysentery. All horses, harness, rugs, and nose bags to be dipped; Mange amongst the horses.
