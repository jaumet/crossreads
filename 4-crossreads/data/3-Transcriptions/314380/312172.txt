<p>The Butte le Warlencourt Martinpuish Somme Area Mar 3.17<br/>Martinpuish. Left here for Trenches again Arr in trenches on the 1st March 10PM to night raid the German trenches capturing a few prisoners including an officer having sprung a surprise on them they left behind cigars   &amp; conigac in their water bottles, 4 30 AM Mar 2nd Germans counter attack  in force &ndash; receive a shrapnell wd in L knee   &amp; fall to the rear arr at D station Martinpuish 8AM from there to C.C.S operated on the 3rd March left C.C.S by H Train for Etaples left Etaples on the 7th for Le Harve</p>
<p>England March 9th<br/>Arrive here Southamton on the 9th 3.17 left Southamton by train for Bristol arr 4PM proceeded by Motor Ambulance to Beaufort War Hospital In Hospital till 15th June was feeling a bit frisky   &amp; asked for leave which was granted Reported back to Hurdcott camp 7th June. Being seven days over leave was parade to C.O   &amp; remanded for further evidence from R.A.M.C Dr   &amp; Dental Surgeon in Newcastle on Tyne. Case concluded 28th Stoppage of pay 6 days R.W. Transfered to B1  A 4 Coy on the 9th July Dental parad 10th for a new plate fitted with plate on the 20th July</p>
