<p>The air is beautiful, cool enough to sleep on deck . At 8 pm I put up my hammock and soon fall to sleep. I woke at 12 as they finished coaling.</p>
<p>17/9/14<br/>I feel terribly thirsty. A bottel of beer makes everything alright again. I sleep till 4 o'clock and have a wonderful dream &ndash; I am once again in Lens. Would have liked to sleep on but duty calls. "Starboard watch fall in for muster."</p>
<p>18/9/14<br/>About 4pm a cloud of smoke is seen on the starboard side. That's surely the English we thought, for we had already been searched for for some days. We won't go for her</p>
