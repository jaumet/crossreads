<p>Most of the vehicles in this</p>
<p>Country, are hooded carts drawn, as a rule by two oxen.<br/>The Rickshaws are all numbered and licensed. The men wearing the license badge round the upper arm. These men are of fine physique, their job necessitates this, as they trot at a good pace for many miles during the day.<br/>Council guides can be had, you see them dressed like a European, with a large brass badge on the left breast marked M.C.G.</p>
