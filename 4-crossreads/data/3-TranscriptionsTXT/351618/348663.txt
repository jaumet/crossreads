 Wednesday 17th Nov It was another angry day. Cloudy and a stormy wind blowing which made the sea a wonderful sight. The waves lashing into heaps of spray &amp; coming onto the beach in large breakers carrying everything before it. The jetties could hardly be seen &amp; it looked as if they would go any moment.
