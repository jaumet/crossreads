<p>15 May, Thursday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Up at 4.30 am &amp; I declare that I am not awake when called. Say good bye. It is very very bad touching. I arr Doyles' but should have caught 9 train as boat didn't leave until 10.30. I walk around to Port Skillion &amp; write letters. Sleep on boat nearly all way. Catch 5.20 train. Dinner on train have good trip &amp; grand meal. Stay at 10 Waterloo Rd. taken there by Y.M.C.A. girl. Fire somewhere near at night but I sleep well.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L. (2), Auntie Annie (2), Dad.</p>
<p>16 May, Friday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Leave London 6.10 am. Front 11 am. Recd 2 letters from Home. Lottie, Marion Clarke, M.R.L. (2), M Mckinnon. I am very lonely. Meet Billy &amp; go by Y.M.C.A. to write. Go pictures after tea. I have to wangle for bed &amp; get down on hard floor, &amp; go to sleep before the mob gets in. I sleep all night but my neck aches next morning.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L.</p>
