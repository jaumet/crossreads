
22-12-16packed with natives apparantly a service was in full swing we took several snapshots & it was 1 pm when we got back & Taube flew over very high this afternoon. received word at 4 oclock this afternoon that we are to go out to attack the Turks at Maghaba leaving here at 5.30 this afternoon; found the rations could not be drawn before 6 oclock & it was decided to issue them out to the Brigade where they had formed up.
