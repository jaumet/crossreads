Some of the nurses went to Lemnos quite a lot are on Transport duty and twenty six went to England;  just after you left for a few weeks, after the by engagement 7, 8, &amp; 9 of August.  They were all very busy but for many weeks things have been slack &amp; wearying with &ndash; almost &ndash; more Drs. &amp; Nurses than patients.  However there are now three of us sewing &amp; mending and there is no slackness in our Department, pyjamas are wearing out &amp; we are everlastingly patching;  in addition the Red Cross has established Aux. Stores at all the Hospitals &amp; I have charge at Gezirah &amp; there is a fair amount of work attached
