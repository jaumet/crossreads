<p>back  to France any more<br/><strike>I may even get</strike> <br/>Well dear Sis I will not write more for I am sure there must be a letter on its way from you. I got four today but none from you. I have not had one from you since before I was wounded.<br/>Give all my love to little or big now Noni  &amp; remember me to Vol  &amp; his people.<br/>Will send you a card on furlough<br/>Fondest love from your brother Hal</p>
