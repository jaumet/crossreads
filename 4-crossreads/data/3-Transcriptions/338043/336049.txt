<p>Greatly enjoy a French Xmas Breakfast, &ndash; included in the many dishes is an English pudding for the two guests.  &ndash; A pleasant afternoon and a Xmas Dinner at 8 p.m. given to a dozen Australians and a Canadian by our dear friend Miss Butler &ndash; A very merry and light hearted Party.<br/>- Midnight celebrations in the Boulevards &ndash; The end of a very happy Xmas Day &ndash;</p>
