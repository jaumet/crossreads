<p>December  1918.<br/>Wednesday 18</p>
<p>At Sea. H.S."Karoola."<br/>Weather fine and splendid sailing sea &ndash; like a mill pond.<br/>Played Auction and commenced my entry for the best trench yarn. Travelled for the 24 hours 324 miles  making a total mileage of 1408 miles.<br/>With the sea as it is I think we should be at Malta or rather Port Said on the 23rd.</p>
