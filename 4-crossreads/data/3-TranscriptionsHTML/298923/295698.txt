<p>1 February 1918 Friday (Whole page crossed out)<br/>Rode to Deiran &amp; had a hot bath &amp; attended Gas Lecture.  This country is so much better than Jerusalem was-   One here rides for miles between Orange &amp; Lemon groves.<br/>3 Letters from Laura &amp; Papers oldest letter dated Dec 19 &amp; Jan 2nd /18  Also letter from May redirected from England &amp; addressed to <strike>CAPTAIN</strike> Weir dated 5/11/17 please thank her for me-<br/>One of Lauras was undated.  May has never addressed me as Major yet!!</p>
