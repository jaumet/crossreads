Am avidly awaiting more mail as it is weeks behind.Friday 13th April '17A very heavy day finishing off at night very tired.  Weather good & went for a stroll round the village in the evening.  Much knocked about & practically empty.  Had a look at the church & found it "knocked rotten" as usual the centre being absent.  The cemetary was a large one & contained representatives of all regiments among the number being about six colonels & one honourable.  An Indian officer had been buried here the day before but we found that he had been removed & reburied by himself about 100 yards away.
