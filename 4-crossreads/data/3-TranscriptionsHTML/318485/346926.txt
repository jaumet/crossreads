<p>During their periodical cleansings they offer a fine subject.  Two or three hundred I have seen formed up in rather irregular fours stark naked in the sun their yellow pink bodies telling splendidly against the background of red Palestine earth with green cactus almond trees in blossom &amp; flowers giving decorative properties.</p>
<p>But now having left the Canal behind I must hurry on in the attempt to overtake the Light Horse for be it known I was considered a too luxurious branch of War records to be sent out promptly and my work was sometimes merely that of <strike>one attached to grave registration</strike> a graveyard artist instead of being carried out in &amp; about the firing line.</p>
