<p>May 1918<br/>Thursday 30</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  posted Noyelles<br/>Weather very nice indeed, had two swims in Lake very enjoyable indeed. Wrote four letters and acted as M.O. during day. Should have visited Andy but was called away early in afternoon and did not arrive home until late.<br/>Percy Grayling arrived as usual from O.P.<br/>Walker and Knipe for three days F.O.O. and L'aison respectively.<br/>11.35 pm Fritz putting a few down on the villages in the district. Stan Laurence had a close shave at W.L..when going</p>
