<p>February 1918<br/>Sunday 17<br/>Messines<br/>Fritz's planes have been very active &ndash; throughout the day. One was brought down on our right. One of ours was was brought down in flames, the wing following the body in descent.<br/>Crossed one of our howitzers fire.<br/>Went on L'aison with Mr Kerr, to left Batt. <br/>Les paid me a visit, and remained here for tea.<br/>Weather glorious but a little frosty. Smithy getting quite well again, and looks much brighter.</p>
