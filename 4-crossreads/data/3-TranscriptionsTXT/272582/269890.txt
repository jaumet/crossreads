 Feby 1918. 1/2/18 Rouen France Strength. 1 officer. 86 Other ranks, not including 5 in hospital, 1 on detached duty at Aust Base Post Office, London. 1 on leave to U.K, and 1 attached in hospital. 64 N.C.O's and men baked 43200 lb rations. 1 man evacuated to hospital (N.Y.D). 1 rejoined from leave in France. 1 man proceeded on leave to U.K. Temperatures. Bakehouse 50&deg;  Fah. Flour. 40&deg;  Water 108&deg;   Mean 40&deg;  . 
 2/2/18. 1 man proceeded on leave to U.K. 64 N.C.O's and men baked 35840 lb rations. Temp. B. 50&deg;  Fl. 40&deg;   Water 108&deg;  . Mean 44&deg; . 
 3/2/18. No baking. The personnel were employed on general camp fatigues during the forenoon. Nothing further to report. 
 4/2/18. 64 N.C.O's and men baked 37700 lb rations. Temp B. 56. Fl. 58. W. 108 to 102. Mean. 50. 
 5/2/18. 64 N.C.O's and men baked 52040 lb rtns.
