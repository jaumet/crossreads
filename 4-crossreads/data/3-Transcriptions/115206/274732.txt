<p>June 1918<br/>Tuesday 11</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather fairly cool, not feeling exactly brilliant. <br/>Went for a swim with P.L.C. Charlie Adey got up, and<br/>seems to be a little <br/>Better. Went down with trench fever during the <br/>afternoon, - feel pretty rotten, <br/>Percy Gray being also effected, he has the same<br/>complaint.<br/>Saw John Harvey on the road to his home. Expect to go out for a spell on the 18th. only a rumor so far</p>
