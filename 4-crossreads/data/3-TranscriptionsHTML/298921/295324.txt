<p>Friday 1916 December 31<br/>Very busy getting everything into going order again &ndash; Had my first ride today.<br/>Also got a letter from <u>old Mrs Faulkner</u> per Agnes Cooper I am sure very good of her.<br/>Also Mother &amp; Joe 24/10/15<br/> "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;" &amp; Day 29/10/15<br/>Note all you say re Parson Rogers Chaplains have a great time 1 in bed every night &amp; only 1 parade a week Church &ndash; carry the rank of an officer &amp; no responsibility &ndash; the free lancer every where they go &amp; recognize no C.O. Yes I got your cable Mother &amp; thought I had acknowledged before &ndash; Lunched at Villa Montrose &amp; am going Concert with the girls tonight.</p>
