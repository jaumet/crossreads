<p><strike>after</strike> arriving in the City I having some time to spare I <strike>took</strike> took a stroll around the block I mean when I say the Block 4 streets in the Centre of the City of Perth Western Australia which is the usual promenade. I met several of my chums &amp; being a True Soldier I naturally spoke on <strike>the</strike> Recruiting &amp; asked them when were they prepared to come in &amp; join of course their answers were that they were not ready, two told me straight out, that they did not want to go which proved to me that they were afraid others said when their turn came they were prepared to go.
