 THE ABERDEEN LINE&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;T.S.S.MARATHON&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;15-IX-15, 
 Dear Judge, 
 So very many thanks for your two letters &ndash; all the more welcome as they arrived when I was in Hospital. As you know by now we had a very big fight, &amp; the first Brigade had to attack some Turkish trenches known as Lonesome Pine, took it all right, but our casualties of necessity were heavy. I got a  H  crack on the knee &ndash; very slight &ndash; it was more the concussion of the shell bursting that bust my knee up.
