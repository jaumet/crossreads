<p>11 July, Friday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Very rough sea &amp; strong gale. Sea roughest yet. Jack Cramer gets a WAC Conigrams. I I read about H &amp; M become separated though married &amp; return to one another. Read.</p>
<p>Mar. wom. 28 falls 1 &amp; L with single M of 04. Reads [indecipherable].</p>
<p>Read a Bachelors love Story Collects. Single girl 82 m. grey haired bandman of 04[?] go to bed in hammock. Just as well as there is another flood. Distribution on music &amp; wardrobe.</p>
<p>12 July, Saturday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Spend whole day reading. Lectures on Fruit pests &amp; [indecipherable] to Jerusalem, Allenby means "Bearer of the Law".</p>
<p>Description of Kaiser &amp; Allenby's entrances to Jerusalem the blessed. Read at night. Awful list on boat &amp; swell still troublesome. I am hungry but can't eat ships munfaces[?]. We have another kit inspection for dutiable goods.</p>
