

<p>12<u><sup>th</sup></u> April.</p>
<p>Well here we are again; since dipping into you last, little notebook, we have been all round the world.&nbsp; At least so it seems to me&nbsp; We were supporting the French at Gentilles Wood&nbsp; we <s>bib</s> <s>bivouced</s> bivouaiced there for three days.&nbsp; We were then marched six miles across country to the village of Aubigny&nbsp; Tis indeed a pathetic sight, these hastily abandoned villages.</p>
