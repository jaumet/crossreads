<p>Monday 29 October 1917.</p>
<p>Picquet today.</p>
<p>Tonight a very nice letter to hand from Joy. Dated Sept 4<sup>th</sup>. Wrote a reply.</p>
<p>Expect to be in the thick of the fighting within 48 hrs at Beersheba <s>to right of Gaza between Gaza and Beersheebah</s>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Went to confession tonight.</p>
