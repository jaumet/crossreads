<p>been detailed as "specialists".  Left Sanvic for Le Havre at 3.30 P.M., entrained at 9.30 P.M. for firing line.</p>
<p>Thur. Apr. 6th -16<br/>Arrived at Hazebrouck at 8 P.M. and proceeded to Castre a distance of 4 miles and after losing our way several times we arrived at our destination at 4 A.M. where we are billeted in a large barn in the villiage.  Can hear big guns firing from here, a noise which gives you a funny feeling in the bread-basket.</p>
