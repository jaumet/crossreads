 During night a few shells came over but did not disturb us. 
 11th Recd. orders to move &amp; were later relieved by No. 3 field Amb.  Went  into  for a rest into dugouts on the right flank &ndash; where I write this &ndash; late in afternoon fritz shells batteries on our ridge, with coal boxes but none came within 200 yds. of us &ndash; but close enough.  4 of us are here living in a comfortable little dug out &amp; are in the best of spirits, rations have come along &amp; we have had a good hot meal.  Our position is exposed to the enemy but all are so tired that we are prepared
