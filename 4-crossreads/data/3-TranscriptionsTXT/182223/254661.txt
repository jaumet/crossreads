 Monday 10 May 1915 Sent 2 bottles of Champagne to Col Martin today in payment for bet made some time ago. saying that we should be away from Mena meaning Egypt by 9 th  May 1915 Ride between 7 &amp; 8 am. About 12 patients came in up to 6 p.m. today. Some forty patients bewteen 6 &amp; 7 pm In town for 1 hour at eight. 
 Tuesday 11&nbsp;May 1915 About the Hospital all day. Patients coming &amp; going all day. Everything all right&nbsp; A good assistant in Captain Plant not lazy, or too Swell. 
 Wrote to F.&nbsp;Fletcher, Col Martin, &amp; Rene Silk 
 Wednesday 12&nbsp;May 1915 
 Ride before breakfast&nbsp; 
 Posted letters F. Fletcher, Col Martin , Rene Silk.&nbsp; 
 Not out during the day. &nbsp; 
 Reading &amp; writing, &amp; attending to patients all the day
