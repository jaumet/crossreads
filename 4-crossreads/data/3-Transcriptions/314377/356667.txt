<p>Upon getting out of the vehicle wanted 3/-! Well I told him I was prepared to give 2/- but he stuck out for 3/- Told him that he could take the 2/- or none at all</p>
<p>Getting nearer the Barracks could see some of the natives selling pineapples and bananas. White people probably hear of altercations scuffles etc between natives and members of the A.I.F. let me say that in this instance there could easily have been trouble and it wouldn't have been altogether the soldier's fault had it been so.</p>
