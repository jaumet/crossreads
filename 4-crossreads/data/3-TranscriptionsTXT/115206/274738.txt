
June 1918Monday 17
Battery W.Lines.  Pont NoyellesWeather  very decent, bright and sunny. 14th Bdge went in to reserve during morning.Jack passed by the camp at about 9.30am.6th Bdge went out. They take the 14ths old campinggrounds. Looked up the whereabouts of Gun Adam, and am going to visit him tomorrow. Feel moderately pleased at theprospect of meeting my dear Elie's brother.Received one or two letters included a schedule of thecontents of my wardrobe in Blighty. Went to a concert in the eveningwith D.Ack.
