<p>9: Skating at Rue Rosette, had a good time &amp; had supper with American naval men who were in Alex.</p>
<p>10:  Driving around town in gharry,sight seeing Went toSultans Palace but could not get inside.</p>
<p>11:  Went to Alhambra to see an English company; had a good night.</p>
<p>12:  Left Sid Bishr 9 a.m., reaching Kantara  5 p.m., after a good holiday.</p>
<p>17:  English ladies visited camp, serving fruit salad to the boys.  Funny to see them Marching up with dixies, etc., &amp; we gave the ladies some rousing cheers for their generosity.</p>
<p>27:  Rugby v. 1st regt., lost by 6 nil, this being our first defeat.</p>
<p>November</p>
<p>2:  Left Kantara 6 a.m. halting at Duedar for the night.  Moved on at 9 a.m. the next morning, arriving at Romani in pouring rain at 4 p.m., carrying one blanket only &ndash; no oil sheets.
