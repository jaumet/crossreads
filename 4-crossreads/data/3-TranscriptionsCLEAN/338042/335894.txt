  
 June 15th Letters written to London - English Diary completed - study - Autographing Photos - Last day & last show at Bertangles - Commence reading "Evangeline" - June 16th Sabbath - Complete "Evangeline" - "The Day of a presentiment which does not take place" - Preparation for departure - Gothas pass perilously close to our camp but are beaten off by our aircraft.
