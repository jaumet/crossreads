 [Sunday 13 August 1916] 
 Received your cable Mine must have been delayed. Getting on A.1 but if I could find out which is going to happen to me re going to England or not.  Weather cooler now.  A big lot of men leave  for Australia &amp; England today- invalided.   [indecipherable] is here.  Roddy Hyland was shot through the head on the 7th at Katia- Killed. We are doing alright &amp; giving Johnny Turk a bad time - but many Casualties    The Turks say the Germans told them the Australians took no prisoners Today Mrs Norman Robertson has actually volunteered to  nurse  wounded Turks Went to Church &amp;  meant it today  When I say went  outside  to Church, to Ward on verandah.
