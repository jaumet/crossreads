  
 Tuesday 4 June Things commenced as usual. 
 Just on feeding up evening large shell (9" - 11") landed near lines. Sid Monks mare killed, another horse wounded destroyed later. Alf House severely wounded losing his chin. Brooksby arm cut off. Dad Harris arm hit. All off to CC.S. 
 Cleared horses away to different place and about 7 p.m. moved to new lines toward Camon. Fixed tent, transferred gear etc. Bed very late.
