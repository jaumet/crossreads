<p>Friday 8 October, 1915</p>
<p>Ran into a regular thunderstorm at 9 a.m. All our bedding got wet but we managed to keep our blankets dry.</p>
<p>The rain cleared off by dinner time &amp; the things soon dried off.</p>
<p>Am getting a bit tired of the ship &amp; will be glad to get ashore.</p>
