<p>Tue. Mar. 6.  Archie Deed is assisting me in the Blanket Store.  They are tossing the Patients out wholesale &amp; we are crowded out with Blankets.  Had a rehearsal after Tea.  Bobbie Roberts was coming to give us a hand but he was taken sick.</p>
<p>Wed. 7.  Got some new songs for our Show also a new opening Chorus which is some class.</p>
<p>Thurs. Mar. 8.  Heavy Frost.  Jolly cold.  There is a rumour going round that Leave is starting again.  Hope to the Lord its true.</p>
<p>Fri. Mar. 9.  Went out to Meaulte &amp; put our Show on in the Y.M.C.A. Hut.  "Props" Hargraves went over about mid-day &amp; fixed up the stage.  Sgt. Major Scanlan of the 2nd D.A.C. the M.T's (Empties) had made arrangements for us &amp; the show went off A.1.  The Col was there &amp; enjoyed it very much.  The Building was packed.  After the Show the D.A.C. took us to their Camp &amp; gave us a Banquet in their Sergeant's Mess.</p>
