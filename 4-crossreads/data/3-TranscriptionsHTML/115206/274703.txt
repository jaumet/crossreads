<p>May 1918<br/>Monday 13</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daours d.y.<br/>Finished rampart for moke, trotted same out for a feed of grass. Wounds opened again .Bill boozed again. Weather glorious. Rode across to see Walton Pip but as he was out came home via W.L. without seeing his cheerful [indecipherable] Received a letter mail. Am due for a run to Villers Brettoneaux again to expect Fritz across tonight avec bombs, an ideal day. Rode the chestnut out.</p>
