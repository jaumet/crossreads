<p>[Page 1 is front cover]<br/>Daily Intelligence Report 9th. Machine Gun Coy.<br/>1st. December 1916 &ndash; 24th. February 1917.</p>
<p>1 The attached intelligence reports were compiled daily at 6 a_m. from information received from section Officer in the line.<br/>2 These reports were forwarded by [Driver?] at 6.30 to Company Hdqrs, and also to Brigade Hdqrs.<br/>3 Each report contains information as to work carried out by the various guns in the sector and report of any enemy activity noted during the 24hours.</p>
