It would certainly be difficult to show anyone a less profitable way than that in which I have spent the last two years.<br/>I have fitted myself sketchily for the position of Groon. Crossing sweeper, stevedore, railway porter &amp; warder for a lunatic asylum. In addition I have picked up enough Arabic to swear at the 'wallads', a certain discrimination in the matter of wines  &amp;  liqueurs  &amp;  a possibly useful faculty for living, sleeping,  &amp;  eating anyhow  &amp;  anywhere.</p>
