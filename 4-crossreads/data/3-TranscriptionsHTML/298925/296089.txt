<p>Alongside the path is the grave of Sheppard, who died 1825 aged 85 who is supposed to be the blacksmith spoken of.</p>
<p>On entering the church, on each side is the plaster figure of a knight in armour lying, and at the feet of one is crouching what appears to be a dog.</p>
<p>It is a beautiful church made of flints and appears to be very old. The oldest tombstone we could discern was dated 1724.</p>
