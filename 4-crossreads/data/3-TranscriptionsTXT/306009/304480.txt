 1915 Oct. 25 Mon. In the afternoon went for a walk, round Gezira &ndash; through the leafy lanes of the English quarter, past creaking shadoufs worked tirelessly by muscular fellaheen, through the tall dhoura &amp; along the edge of the Nile &ndash; A chap wants to see all he can of Egyptian life while he is here.
