<p>April, 1916<br/>Wednesday 5<br/>work as usual in the morning<br/>Half holiday.  I went to Luna Park Hosp. w/- dad's bags which he was glad to get.<br/>Afterwards went w/- my chum to see friends in Choubra.  spent very pleasant evening returning 11.30 pm.<br/>Have another boarder.</p>
<p>Thursday 6<br/>Easy day.  morning spent stripping description<br/>After dinner I walked over to see dad.<br/>Afternoon, Lecture, then we marched out to Matariah to carry out J.D. tests.</p>
