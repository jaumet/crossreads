<p>Tuesday 13th November 1917</p>
<p>Posted notes yesterday &ndash; telling you I'm here till 19th. Laura comes at 2.30 to see us, &amp; goes Hospital tonight.</p>
<p>Brace is coming to see her &amp; she says Connie is ill too but Laura is a bit of a Hypochondriac if that spells it correctly.</p>
<p>Foggy morning my cold better &amp; the wife very fit. A luxurious life at present.</p>
<p>In Palestine several of my friends have been killed.</p>
