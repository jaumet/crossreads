 Friday May 4 Everybody stiff &amp; tired. Had easy day. Informed we are sailing on 10th inst by Booarra Saturday May 5 Weekend leave to Melbourne arrived 4.15pm. Spent afternoon strolling round Town evening visited St Kilda Sunday May 6 Went down to Port Melbourne to see transport. Monday May 7th Many rumours in train. It is said 21 Transports sailing on Thursday. Tuesday May 8th Packing Black kit bags, final medical examination Wrote letters.
