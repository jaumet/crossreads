<p>May, 1917. Empire Day. Thursday 24<br/>Fine day. Check parade at 7 and drill order 8.30 and marched to Bresle &amp; back arrived at 11.30. Fell in again at 2 and were dismissed. Played bridge in afternoon and cleaned up thoroughly. Leave to Amiene to-morrow.</p>
