<p>Seeing amongst others Ellen Terry &ndash; Bransby Williams &ndash; Florence Smithson- Return to Highgate &ndash; Meet Mrs Wm. Scales &ndash; Pay a call to Dr. Bousefield &ndash; with a sore throat. Correspondence &ndash; The night on which &ndash;<br/>Mar.7th<br/>Meet Fred Reade in Town &ndash; Together we go to Daly's Theatre &amp; meet Mr. Todd (Actor &amp; Manager) then on to Samuel French's where we select Plays &amp; Sketches &ndash; Return to Highgate. In the evening</p>
