 November 1918. Tuesday 26 
 Hospital and Wembley Left hospital at 10 am and spent a very quiet time at Hsferry Road. Arrived at Wembley in due course. Met O on the platform Had a very pleasant evening &ndash; a musical one with Vic and a few others. Had lunch with Bert in town. Received a letter of some character from D.B.F.
