Changed trains at Waterloo Station in London a distance of 70 mile from Netley and proceeded to Croydon where there were Motor Cars awaiting us and brought us on to Addington a distance of only 3 mile arrived there about 4 oclock in the afternoon after travelling six hours, Addington is only a few mile outside the great city of London.  We had an enjoyable Christmas here.  Great Christmas dinner was given including bottles of ale, people coming into us with presents and comforts of all kinds.  There was telegrams from Australia with Greetings, posted up everywhere around the hospital.  Also had concerts, pictures and dancing nurses and everybody joining in the jollification, on Christmas night
