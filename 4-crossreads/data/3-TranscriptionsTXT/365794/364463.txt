
Saturday 3 March 1917 [2 March 1918]A case of thypus in 27th G.H.  Removed to infectious hosp. on 2nd & today 4 sisters are isolated in a tent & no pts., friends allowed to visit 27th.  Off duty 2-5, went & shopped for Marge & met Mrs. Davies & tea with her at Groppi's - friendly woman.  Spent Pt. 75 for M. Woods, Pt. 25 remaining.
