 1916   THURSDAY 2   MARCH 
 Fine. Got to town early and had breakfast at Petty's with father  buzzed round town all day  Linda & Jo had tea with us at the [Grey?] Salon in morning and dinner at Petty's with us  Father & I saw Ashford in afternoon and he promised me I would not be molested after I came back from the war. Met Jo & Linda again and came back by boat. Had tea with father at Petty's and he went home after. I went up to the Criterion Theatre and met the 15/1 & 15/3 theatre parties and we saw "Under Fire". it was very realistic
