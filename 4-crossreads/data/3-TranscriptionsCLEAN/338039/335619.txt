  
 July. 1916. 
 July 1st. Departure from "good old Home" 10.30 a. m.  Destination, Bailleul- Strenuous March owing to rapid pace & heat of day.  Arrive at new quarters- V. tired July. 2nd. Beautiful surroundings- Aircraft in evidence- Filthy state of Hospital etc- News of Big British offensive on Somme- Great Success.  Visit to Eddie Cornish- disappointment.  Air duel. July 3rd. Bailleul important military Base.  Impressions of Town.
