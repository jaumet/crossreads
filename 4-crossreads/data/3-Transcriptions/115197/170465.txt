<p>Disorganisation - Evening trip to Wimbledon and return to &quot;Elm-Bank&quot;.</p>
<p><u>4th</u>.</p>
<p>Taking things easy as a convalescent would do.&nbsp; Letters written &amp; parcels posted.</p>
