<p>30 July 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Not much better this morning but get over the issue of rations all right<br/>Lie down nearly all day &amp; feel better in the evening<br/>Get orders to supply several Guards &amp; fatigue parties for tomorrow. "C" coy finding  <u>Bgde</u>  duties<br/>Rum 6 pm.</p>
<p>1915 July 31</p>
<p>Guards go on at 8 am &amp;  so relieve me of my worry for the day.<br/>I go  &amp;  have a look at the new 60 pounder field guns landed during the night  &amp;  apparently intended for the big general advance to take place shortly</p>
