 Went for a short route march this morning, &amp; done a little squad drill, as if we have not had enough of both, according to Battalion order's A. Coy. marched the best the other day &amp; C. Coy the worst. You can see quite plainly our observation balloon's up from here especially of an evening Out again this afternoon, the boys were in a bad humour  &amp; so was the Captain so things were anything but pleasant the boy's don't like the idea of the two parade's daily &amp; are kicking a bit over it, I can see some fun shortly if the parades are continued Our Colonel has gone to the trenches today to have a look at things This evening 2 other chaps &amp; myself went over to the Flying Corp's
