<p>Sunday June 24th<br/>Church parade in morning. settling down in camp. Feel the heat very much. Am not at present very much impressed by this Country.<br/>Monday June 28<br/>Inspected by OC &amp; Camp Commandant. Troops pouring in at main camp.<br/>Tuesday June 26<br/> Moved camp about &frac12; mile Suppose they wanted to give us something to do Saw what they termed a mirage to day. Caused I am told by the heat on sand. It is hoped that the promised land Palestine is superior to this.<br/>Wednesday June 27<br/>What beautiful Sunsets &amp; Moonlight nights they have in this country Makes one think of home.</p>
