<p>12.11.16<br/>Dear Judge Ferguson,</p>
<p>Thank you ever so much for your kind letter and the telegraph forms enclosed.  I shall find them most useful.  It is awfully good of you to think of it.  Mother wrote that she had seen you and Mrs. Ferguson lately &ndash; this was in a letter dated 24 Sept &ndash; and thought you both looking so much better.  I was very pleased to hear it.  They enjoyed the dinner at the Queen's Club and Mr. Curtis' lecture afterwards.  You referred to it in your letter.  Father's letters have been full of the business about conscription and the various party factions connected with it.
