  
 God - we saw an enemy 'plane yesterday, it came over from the west hotly pursued by three of our 'planes and beyond that nothing doing. After the next move - reported only 5 miles ahead - we should be right in the line 'o stoush;  even here they are constantly sighting Turkish Patrols.  I believe. 
 Bir Arnusi Dec. 3rd Move on tomorrow some 5 or 6 miles to a new camp.  Enemy 'plane over Mazar this afternoon dropped two bombs wounding 8 men.  Two of which are not expected to live.
