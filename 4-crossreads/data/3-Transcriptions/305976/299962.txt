<p>lying on side of line. Believe they met Head on Collision. Arrived at Yebnah 7pm slept in Scotch camp.<br/>Thursday Jan 17<br/>Had breakfast with some Scotch lads. Fine fellows. Waiting for wagon to come over to Dump for Rations. Arrived at unit 12 noon. Our new camp situated just outside village. Wadi [indecipherable] some 3 miles from Richon<br/>Friday Jan 18<br/>Tom gone on leave to Cairo. Best camp site we have had yet. Jacko was over  observing today.<br/>Saturday Jan 19<br/>This camp will do for me for some time. Good climate plenty of fruit &amp; food.</p>
