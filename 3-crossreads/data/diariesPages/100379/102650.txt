9th February 1917 Cont. Went on duty at 10 till 12. Received a letter from Rana papers from Charlie. Had supper before going on duty.
10th February 1917. Woke at 8 for breakfast. Had an easy day off duty. Walked up the town in the morning. Had a sleep afternoon. Went on duty at 8 till 10 at night. Put through SOS tests in record time.
11th February 1917. Went on duty at 6 till 8.20. Had breakfast fooled around with the wood etc also got one of the boys to clip my hair. After dinner went on duty at 12.30 till 5. Had tea sat by the fire reading Aust. papers I have just received. Received letters from Rana Nance also Made supper of porridge cocoa and after having a feed went on duty again at 10 till 12. A Good deal of strafing going on tonight on our side.
12th February 1917. Woke at 8.30 had breakfast. Walked up to town bought coke etc Had dinner there. Left battery at 2.15 for R L the F.O.O dugout on the 3rd line of trenches. I stay here for a week amongst the shot shell. Fritz strafing today and made it hot for a while on our journey down. Went on duty at the telephone in the infantry dugout a sniper one practically shellproof. Different from the old one
