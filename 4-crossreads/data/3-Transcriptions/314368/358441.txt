<p>Well Mother I had a new experience the other evening, I went for a fly in an Aeroplane, another chap and I went for a walk down to an Aerodrome just out of Oxford, and we had no difficultly in getting a pilot to take us up. I went up with an American pilot, and we flew from Oxford down the Thames to Reading and back again.  I was in the air for about 2 hours  I think, all told.
