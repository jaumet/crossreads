<p>1915<br/>Jan. 27 Wed.<br/>Commenced work this morning in general admission &amp; administration office at Mena House. Five clerks kept busy all day.  Knocked off at 9.30 p.m.<br/>Had a hot bath at Mena House &ndash; the first since home (first hot bath, not first bath) just the thing.<br/>Our complete unit is made up as follows :-<br/>Officers - 22<br/>N.C.O.s &amp; Men - 148<br/>Nurses - 93</p>
<p>Total - 263</p>
