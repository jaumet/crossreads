<p>early &amp; later rec'd our accumulated mails, 35 letters &amp;, 6 parcels, 1 Journal &amp; 1 Paper.  After dinner had good <br/>[indecipherable]<br/>bath &amp; change.  Had long walk round Camp roads &amp; turned in about 9 p.m.</p>
