<p>less time than it takes to tell the Tommy police tried to stop us, I can see trouble ahead for them if they are not very careful for the boys are not be trifled with &amp; are liable to do nasty things when once started.</p>
<p>At present we are camped in long &amp; low huts built of galvanised iron &amp; canvas roofs, they are dry &amp; that is the principal thing these times. We have no blankets, as yet.</p>
<p>The guns can be heard quite plainly from here belting away for all they are worth.</p>
