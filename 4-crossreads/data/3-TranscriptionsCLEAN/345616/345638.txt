 Sunday 8/9/18 up at 5 ready to chase Jerry again when order cancelled so still staying here. Sun just rising 6am. Jerry was dropping bombs early this morning & shelling just beyond us during the night. Lovely morning & hardly the sound of a gun. Letter from Maud yesterday & Paper from Nell. Posted letter to Nell. been raining heavily clear now 6.30Pm. 58-17-0 - 113/4 
 Monday 9/9/18 Warned for Blighty leave this morning up at 5 reported B.O.R 6.30 been walking ever since now 1 Pm. can't find H,Qrs. great Bat/ this. Done up & [indecipherable]waiting for Bat/ to come out this is Le Misnet. Boys came out 4 Pm. going H.Qrs by Limber then off toCorbie.  58-18-6.  115/4 
 Tuesday 10/9/18 Just caught Sgt Jenkins with my Pass as I got here, he was off to Jim's Bat/ walked fully 20 miles. He wants me to stay here till tomorrow but am off caught G.S waggon went almost 6 miles then walked 2 miles & caught motor lorry got to Villers Bretonneux & caught another got to Amiens at 12 midnight up at 7 no time to shave, am in train for Boulogne 9.a.m, slept on floor of empty house last night with only Water Proof sheet on me Rained all the way out & raining now. 59-0-0 - 117/4 
 Wednesday 11/9/18 Am in Blighty 6-30 Pm. Rough Passage just finished up 7-30 staying at YMCA tonight Belgrade Rd off to Scotland in two days 59-1-6 - 119/4 
 Thursday 12/9/18 Very tired & weary not fit to go anywhere going to Bank for money & will get fitted up generally theatre tonight Perhaps. 59-3-0 - 121/4 
 Friday 13/9/18 Rotten head this morning stout too heavy going Scotland tonight had enough of London too vast etc 59-4-6 - 123/4 Saw Dr/ at HQs Have to report on 23rd re my Eyes & Ear. 
 Saturday 14/9/18 Arrived Glasgow 6-30 Train to Prestwick 8.20 got here about 10 went to Ayr with Maude this afternoon Pictures & Pastries etc 59-6-0 
 Sunday 15/9/18 Raining hard & cold stuck in all day. Went for walk with Alex in the afternoon to see Burns' Well. etc. 59-7-6
