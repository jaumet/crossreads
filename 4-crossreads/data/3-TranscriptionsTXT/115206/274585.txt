
January 1918Tuesday 15
At BeutinUsually daily routine .Weather fine.Played 53rd Bty a game of Rugby during afternoon result draw 5-5I went along to Etaples with Dick then to Paris Plage. The latter is in Peace Time a pleasure resort and is indeed a pretty little place.There are quite a number of W.A.A.C.s camped at the former place.Etaples is a peace time big fishing centre, and possesses a very large square, not to mention an electric tram service to Paris Plage.
