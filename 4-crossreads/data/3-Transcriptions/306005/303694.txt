<p>10-1-17<br/>doing in this war. It was a treat meeting Jessie &amp; the other nurses &amp; I was very lucky in not going to Port Said to await the boat as I intended.</p>
<p>11-1-17<br/>Thurs 11th<br/>Rose at 5 AM. Had breakfast at 5.30 &amp; was at the Cairo Railway Station at 6 oclock &amp; had to wait till 7 for the train to Kantara to move out, played bridge till we reached Ismalia with some other Australians I met on the Cairo platform; here I decided to get out to buy films &amp; send a cable, had lunch &amp; put in a couple of hours moping around the town sent a cable from the Railway Station,  &amp; readjusted the photos putting them in a smaller</p>
