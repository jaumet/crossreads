<p>May 1918<br/>Sunday 12</p>
<p>Bussy-la Daours<br/>Worked at building a small protective wall for splinters<br/>Weather glorious.<br/>On duty at General's office during afternoon and evening, and all night. Sketched two maps of Egypt and Suvla Bay for my American pal.<br/>Intend to visit him at some time or other. Spent a very quiet evening.</p>
