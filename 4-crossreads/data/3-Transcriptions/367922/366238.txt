<p>L/Cpl E K Burke<br/>Wireless Training School,<br/>Moore Park, Sydney.<br/>17-9-1917</p>
<p>Though I am not quite sure whether you are the father to whom <strike>what</strike> this news will be of some value I am writing to you as the only "Fry" of Lindfield, mentioned in the directory. If it does not apply to you, I trust you will be able to pass the information along.</p>
<p>The letter which I enclose was recently received from France &amp; the post-script contains the information to which I refer.</p>
<p>Eric K. Burke.</p>
