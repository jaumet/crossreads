 [4th Sept 1916] I fulfilled the first requirements &amp; six others were selected including Jimmy Hyde, both of whom were in the dispensary at Marseilles. Given charge of party by seniority under Capt Rhodes also travelling with us to No 2 Command Depot, Weymouth. Went by 12-10 pm train via Andover &amp; Southampton West, at both of which places we changed. Arrived at Weymouth at 4 pm &amp; after 2&frac12; miles march, reached the camp which is at Monte Video out near the sea west of Portland Bill, at tea time.
