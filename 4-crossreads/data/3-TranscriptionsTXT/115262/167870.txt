 Friday 13 April 1917. 
 &quot; The day &quot;. Reville 6.30. Roll call 6.45 
 Breakfast 7.30. Moved off 8.15 a.m, Trawool bridge at 9.30 a.m. 
 Turned on to Tallarook road &amp; after another hours march were invited to a farmhouse for &quot;morning tea&quot; which we had in about&nbsp;&frac12; hr. or over. 
 Tea very acceptable &ndash; new life in us. Pushed on &amp; reached Tallarook at 12.30 pm. 
 &frac14; hrs spell.&nbsp;then on again. 
 Dinner by the road at 12.45 
 On again at 1.30. Reached Seymour at 3.30. SPell of 20 min. 
 Straight through to camp &amp; reached there at 4.30. 
 On special leave from tonight till Monday morning. 8.15 train from Seyr. Expect&nbsp; now &nbsp;to move out on 23rd inst 
 Won competition for sketch of country etc.
