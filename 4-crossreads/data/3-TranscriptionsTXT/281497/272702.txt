 Just before we left, Colonel Foote came round &amp; gave us some useful hints as to our future behaviour while training, &amp; when we are made officers, he is a fine sort of chap &amp; a strong advocate of getting the officers from the ranks. 
 At half past 6 we left our camp &amp; made off towards Valdemaison about 2 miles distant, as we were going along the sun suddenly came out &amp; brought 2 pretty rainbows with her, it seemed to me a good omen just as we were starting off on our journey.
