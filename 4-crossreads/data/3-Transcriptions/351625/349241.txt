<p>from here.  I suppose you are having fine weather out there now.  How is Bruce getting on at Woodville.  I wish I was in his place but I am jolly glad to hear he has go out of the City, it will do him the world of good.  It is foolish of him wanting to go to the war at 18 when one gets over here &amp; sees the thing properly it is easy to see 20 is quite young</p>
