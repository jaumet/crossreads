
Naval Signal
From - Berrima
To - Sydney
Date - 7 9 14
Time - 7.5 pm
From Brigadier.  My orders are to seize all German wireless stations in Pacific and occupy German Territory soon as possible under Admiral's instructions or failing an emergency failing such instructions in any emergency carry out objects of expedition.  Further hoist British flag in all territory occupied with success and make suitable arrangements for temporary administration.  I have plans prepared for giving effect to this
