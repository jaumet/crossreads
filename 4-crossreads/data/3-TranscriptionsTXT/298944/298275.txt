Sunday night
Got through most of my correspondence last night.
Today has been very successful but I got a very small amount of sleep &amp; feel consequently, rather sleepy tonight.
Odd jobs before going to bed - overtime (about 10.30) &amp; called prematurely early to receive &amp; play for a big dry singer of the 1st Field &ndash; Sgt Sellars &ndash; who is to help us next Thursday.
