
Monday 7 May 1917.
Up at 6 a.m. Caught 7 pm [a.m.] troop train to Seymour. At camp at 11.30 a.m.
Very little doing but we were drilled in cold wind & misty rain.
Expect to go on Thursday
Advance guard for Lt. Horse on A42 leave camp at 6.15 a.m.
A.M.C. (re-allotted men) about 30 arrive from No 5 & No 11. A.G.H. at 9.30 p.m.
