 The whitemen had been sent into the interior but the Indians were kept digging trenches etc &amp; this party of 6 were in a trench which was shelled by a warship The Bedouin fled so the Indians took their chance &amp; ran towards our men crying "Indian &ndash; Ingleese". They'd had a rough time living in daily fear of having their  coats  throats cut any minute &amp; fed on a handful of dried peas daily &amp; clad in sacking.
