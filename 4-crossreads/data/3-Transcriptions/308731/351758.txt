<p>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>1.9.1918</p>
<p>Dear Old Mr. Jam-Tin.</p>
<p>We got a letter for Francis yesterday and we went down to-day and gave him the letter with a post-card in-side and we Wished him many re- happy turns of the Day.  Mum has too read us Three parts of a book of Lord Clive we get them from the School we get our names put down and we have to <strike>see if we</strike> bring it back on Monday we only get them on a Friday I will put down the names of them for you.  Water Babies.  The two prisoners.  The Coral Islands.  Don Quisite.  And Lord Clive in India.  we get them all from School; and I forgot to put in Uncle Toms Cabin. and Picture Land.  Frank is painting for you on the back of his letter and I am going to do some sums for you.</p>
<p>Much love from Ruth.  To Dad.<br/>xxx etc.<br/>ooo etc.</p>
