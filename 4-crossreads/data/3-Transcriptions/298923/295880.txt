<p>2 August 1918 Friday</p>
<p>Wadi Hanein<br/>Posted Notes yesterday with Granny Isherwood letter.  Still resting &amp; men who were wounded are drifting back to our base at Moascar where some of our best Horsebreakers are put on WOODEN HORSES &amp; taught to ride by Imperial Instructors!!! just to freshen them up, like!!<br/>I am still C.O. &amp; have been able to get Beer &amp; Wine for all Ranks at a nominal price.<br/>C.O. returned &amp; I go back to my Squadron tomorrow.<br/>Still evacuating sick men, no Officers left in my Squadron at all</p>
