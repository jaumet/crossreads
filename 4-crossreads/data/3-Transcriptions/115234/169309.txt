
<p>But I have digressed.&nbsp; A dozen German prisoners have just tramped down the road under escort.&nbsp; They were taken last night &amp; look pretty miserable.&nbsp; Three of them wear spectacles, a couple are mere boys.&nbsp; A nondescript lot.&nbsp; Will stop now &amp; await a favourable opportunity for further scribbling.</p>
