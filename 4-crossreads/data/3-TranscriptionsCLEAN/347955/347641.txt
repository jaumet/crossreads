  
 Monday Dec.10.12.17. turned out 7.A.M. had Breakfast fell in 8.30 roll call so one called it  went down to town  returned to camp 12  dinner 12.30.  rested till 4 P.M. went to Y.M.C.A. had a hot Bath returned to camp for tea went to Pictures turned in 10.P.M. tired out. Tuesday Dec.11.12.17.  turned out 7.  Breakfast 7.30 fell in 8.30 roll call got a Boat and went to Armenian camp.  Saw all the girls at work they are very shy they do some wonderful work all by hand.
