7th August (Monday)
Went to Elbow Farm for rations & mail.  Fritz shelling the batteries behind us also Fleurbaix which is already in ruins.  Numbers wounded in the village.British squadron of planes passed over.  There were 8 altogether & Fritz failed to hit any.  No doubt we have the superiority here in aeroplanes.
