This worked out satisfactorily, for the orderlies became efficient at their duties which was far better for all than taking it in turns to carry the food down from the galley. I, for one was so terrified at the prospect of collapsing with the stew in the hatchway when the ship was on the roll that I would have paid anything to dodge the job of mess orderly.
