<p>30 March 1918 Saturday<br/>After midnight tomorrow any one of Greek religion or Russian can kiss any pretty girl he meets till mid-night Monday, so long as you say Christ is risen.  Our soldiers are looking forward to it.<br/>Just heard definitely the wife cannot get Permit but as a Colonels wife is with her alls well &amp; the event does not take place till <strike>Je</strike> end of July.  Then we may all come home together   Love to all-   Wild anemone from Rachael's Tomb</p>
