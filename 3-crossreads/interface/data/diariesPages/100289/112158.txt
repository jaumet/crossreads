Monday 31.7.16
About 60 of us have to toe the Carpet this morning for having in our possession, government property ? to wit, blanket (spare) which should have been handed into the quarter store. Gave a satisfactory explanation my charge was withdrawn.
Tuesday 1.8.16
Weather has been rather warm for the past few days. Advance party left for Divisional Rest Station at Doulieu.
Wednesday 2.8.16
