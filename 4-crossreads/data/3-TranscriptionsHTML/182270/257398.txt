<p>THURSDAY, MARCH 27, 1919.</p>
<p>27</p>
<p>Fine but cold. Playing games &amp;</p>
<p>reading on deck all day. Played</p>
<p>&quot;Pontoon&quot; at night.</p>

<p>boat deck &amp; were very good, especially</p>
<p>some of the fancy costumes.</p>
