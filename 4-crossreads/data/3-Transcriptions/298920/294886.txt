<p>boats 1st Class Single berth cabins.  They have a bunk, settee, wardrobe, writing desk and toilet basin &amp; mirror, also 2 electric lights, electric fan, and steam room heater.  The Saloon is not a very large one being 16' x 11'6 x 10.  The walls and ceiling are panelled, there is a settee running athwartships at the head of the table, and a long table placed fore and aft.  A big mirror faces the bottom end of the table &amp; is placed over a tiled room heater.  Three large ports and several electric globes illuminate the room.
