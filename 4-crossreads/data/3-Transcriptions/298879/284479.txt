<p>December 1915<br/>Wednesday 8<br/>Weather changeable. The Navy is belting something at Achi Baba.</p>
<p>Thursday 9<br/>Dull. The Navy still firing heavily down at the Cape. Heavy firing has been going on there for about a week now. Also in Suvla Bay.</p>
