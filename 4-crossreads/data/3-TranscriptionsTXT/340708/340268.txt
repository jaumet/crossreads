
July 1917Sunday 8thWe shifted our quarters today to the attic over the Hospital.Monday 9thFritz flew over our way again tonight the anti aircraft guns were going to some [indecipherable]   He dropped a lot of bombs around a munitions works but did no damage.Tuesday 10thVery cold & rough today so we were free from aeroplanes to night.Wednesday 11A big lot of patients evacuated today so we ought to have a quiet time for a day or twoThursday 12thThe Hun has been putting it into Baillieul again so they have sent all there patients in here so our good time was short lived
