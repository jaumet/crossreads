<p>I can see a row or two here if the Tommy police are not careful how they order us about, they, or I should say some of them, are very officious, &amp; seem to take a delight in their rotten crawling cold-footed job, how the Infantry hate these police for as the old saying has it; "They neither spin nor do they reap" I reckon they make the boy's play up &amp; you can't blame them.</p>
