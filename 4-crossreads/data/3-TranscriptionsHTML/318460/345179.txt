<p>Saturday 6/1/17.<br/>16 months to day since I went into camp at Show ground.<br/>Cold, Rained all night but fine today feels like snow<br/>that other Job no good so must stick to Police after all worst luck.<br/>Pay &pound;2.1-1 &pound;18-19-0. Amiens bombed last night by Taubes.</p>
<p>Sunday 7/1/17.<br/>Nice &amp; fine at last hope it remains so<br/>Sergeant actually came out on duty last night at 8 PM. first time for months.<br/>Church parade to day.<br/>Pay &pound;2-2.1. &pound;19-0-0</p>
<p>Monday 8/1/17<br/>Pay &pound;2-3-1. 19-1-0<br/>cold with sleet snow not far off<br/>had a hot shower this morning &amp; clean clothes stopped scratching for awhile, here for another fortnight I think.<br/>Football match this afternoon.<br/>Sergeant in one of his usual snaky moods again, did not appear to close up Pubs  &amp; did not see him at 9. so went to bed at 20 Past he came in later.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9/1/17<br/>Snowed during the night everything white this morning<br/>A new man joined us in Viv's place.<br/>Bapaume &amp; Peronne are being bombarded we go in again in a few days (Friday) 2 Coy's go Tomorrow our usual luck.</p>
