
and we soon arrived in London and none the worse for our fortnights experiences through Scotland.  then we caught tube trains from King's cross to Russell square were we were staying before and nice place in Grenvill St in the heart of London.  next morning went to the commonwealth to see if any money had arrived from home for me for it was a fortnight or more since I sent a wire for some and now I am broke nothing to draw for its all gone the few pound I had which was 8 and as it had not come I went to head offices in London and explained but they could do nothing and I have another
