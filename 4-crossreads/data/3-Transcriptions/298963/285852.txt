<p>June 3rd<br/>Still in Billets at Henencourt.  Training continues.  Everything is <strike>roped</strike> robed in spring vesture &amp; very beautiful.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Interambulance sports held yesterday.  More reinforcements joined up.  Among them is Wilkie.</p>
