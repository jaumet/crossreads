This morning we took a car into Bournemouth for the day.  It is a large town, though very uninteresting just now with the exception of the gardens which are a blaze of colour, beautifully laid out.  Bournemouth can boast a splendid beach for bathing and a very elaborate pier built on the same plan as the one at Swanage, only much grander.
