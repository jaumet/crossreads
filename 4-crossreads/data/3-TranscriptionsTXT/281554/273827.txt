26thRather dull but not cold.  Church parade 11 a.m. opp. our own Lines.  Leave from midday, visited larger village West Mudros.  Busy but very dirty place, plenty eatables, fruit, sweets, etc. but prices very stiff, home to tea spent night in tent.
27th MondayOrderly Sgt. for day.  Woke early to hear rain but day turned out bright later on.  Haircut, reading &amp; card playing.
