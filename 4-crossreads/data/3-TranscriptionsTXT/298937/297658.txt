 6 p.m. 
 Stopped at Montelimar &amp; had a yarn with 2 French soldiers, wounded at Verdun. One was at the Peninsula &amp; helped at the Australian evacuation. He thinks highly of the Australians. 
 Friday 18th Aug. 
 Passed Lyons in the night &amp; breakfasted at Ma&ccedil;on.  Very cool in morning but as we neared Dijon the sun came out &amp; it became warmer.  Rain finished.
