 15 August 1918 Thursday A fairly heavy sea running and rather a heavy swell. Weather cloudy and inclined to be wet &ndash; just a little rain! Boat only doing about  8  miles per hour owing the heavy head wind &amp; sea. 
 1918 August 16 Friday A very nice fine day &amp; a beautiful sea with just a little roll. Nothing important happening on the boat. Another concert at night to cheer the lads up. Good war news. Reported 30,000 prisoners &amp; 1,000 Guns since 4-8-18
