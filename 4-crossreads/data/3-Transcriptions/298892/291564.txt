<p>Stirling's address. In all probability he is in London also. I wrote to Mrs. Sharpe at her old address, just a post-card, but possibly it will not reach here. If you see a small black note book of mine kicking-round you might find his address or at least the No. of Brigade and Battery in it.<br/>With best love to all at home &amp; next door<br/>Eugene.</p>
