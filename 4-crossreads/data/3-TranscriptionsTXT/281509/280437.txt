 2 August 1916 Wednesday 
 Eight o'clock breakfast. Recd &amp; wrote letters all morning. Route march in afternoon. thro' Vadencourt.  Colonel's message of praise &amp; commendation read at 9.30 parade We visit photographers &amp; see proofs of snaps taken at Doulien order a doz. Spinning &amp; weaving Buy 2 scarfes &amp; send them to Mrs. Mac.T. &amp; G.R. Bob comes up to visit us. Walk part of way home with him. 2nd Anniversary of declaration of war between Germany &amp; France. Two days later Germany &amp; Britain
