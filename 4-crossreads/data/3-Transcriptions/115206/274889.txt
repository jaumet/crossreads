<p>Hospital. Birmingham<br/>Weather fine and clear and a happy afternoon was spent at Sutton Coldfield with  -<br/>Had a very fine afternoon in town playing billiards. Went to a picture theatre with Sgt Gott and enjoyed myself immensely.</p>
