<p>The War news lately is very encouraging indeed. The German Press, is warning the public not to be alarmed if their gallant troops retire nearer the Rhine, which really means evacuate Belgium, to still have faith in their leaders etc. etc. Belgium Factories are being pulled down &amp; furnaces are being destroyed.</p>
<p>The Italians have at last made a stand &amp; captured a considerable number of prisoners. The Flanders fighting is still in progress &amp; the British troops are having great victories in Palestine.</p>
<p>The weather lately has been very mild, quite pleasant - why, this time last year the weather was most severe, plenty of snow &amp; ice. The news certainly looks promising,  it is to be hoped, that this kind of news continues &amp; that the near future will see the end of this terrible war. Rumours about that our Unit will be here for a month or more &amp; then we go right out till after Christmas. Our hearts are gladdened by this prospect.</p>
