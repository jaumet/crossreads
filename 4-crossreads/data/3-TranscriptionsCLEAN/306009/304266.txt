  
 1915 Mar. 21 Sun. A Sergeant & 4 men to-day detached from No. 2 G.H. for Orderlies on the ship "Ulysses" returning to Australia with men unfit for further active service. Obtained leave & at 10.30 a.m. set off to Sakkara on donkey with guide - 10 miles distant round the edge of the desert - On arrival saw 6 or 7 Pyramids & went inside 3 tombs - one very fine - had a good feed of oranges - saw Lieut. Hugh Pulling of Nth. Sydney - & came back again via the Sphinx & past the Great Pyramid - Good exercise, a 20 mile ride! Passed a native funeral & went through a native village.
