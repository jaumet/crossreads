He seems to be of the mechanic type, not intelligent, but amiable.  When the daughter enters into a little brisk raillery he follows it closely with all attention & helps her along with a smile & a word while not comprehending one word that is spoken.  She is pretty, intelligent & fairly well educated, judging by her pronunciation of French.  To hear her speak English brokenly is in itself a delight.
