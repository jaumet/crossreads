I saw 3 English nurses walking through the streets this morning. 20th. Well after all I did not give my diary to the parson, as I was too late so I decided to chance it & carry it myself. We left our billets in a hurry, we got 10 minutes notice to pack up & fall in, we carried our  blanket  waterproof & overcoat rolled in bandolier fashion. Had a pretty long & tiresome march & just outside Albert we pulled up & had tea on the roadside, we were now getting close to the scene of conflict for all around us great guns were firing.
