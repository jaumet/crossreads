<p>marching through her streets just outside Ypres runs the Yser &amp; the bridge over her for a wonder still stands, we crossed this &amp; made for the firing line, everything about here shows the signs hard usuage &amp; there are great shell holes everywhere in the roads, fields, the railway line torn up &amp; such like gentle reminders that shells hurt.</p>
<p>As we went along the German flares lit the whole country up &amp; from these strings of flares we could see we were in a huge horseshoe otherwise the "Ypres Salient" it was round about the part that the Germans made their first gas attack on the Canadians &amp; French</p>
