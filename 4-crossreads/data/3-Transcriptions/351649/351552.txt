<p>[The beginning and end of this letter appear to be missing.]<br/>photo which I suppose is like most other things, lost at Bose &amp; put down as being lost through "Hostile shelling", not that a shell has ever been within 50 miles of the Bose.  The Heros there take fine care not to get in range of Fritz's guns.</p>
<p>You must have had a great time at Xmas with all the Kiddies.  We amused ourselves by waiting until Fritz was at Dinner &amp; then pretty well every gun on the Somme opened on him at top rate, so he most likely failed to enjoy his dinner &amp; many will never have another.</p>
