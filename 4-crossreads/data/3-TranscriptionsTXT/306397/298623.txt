 Thursday 20/8/14 About 5 pm. "Geier" meets us accompanied by a collier "Ulm" which is at least three times the size of "Geier". The commander of "Geier" boards our ship. "Geier" is going to Japan. 
 Friday 21/8/14 We are going south . Weather good. 
 Saturday 22/8/14    do 
 Sunday 23/8/14 Passed the line at 4.30. a little later a steamer is in sight. She carries the Japanese flag. After showing the English flag we let her pass. 
 Monday 24/8/14 We have entered the Indian Ocean through the Malucca strait.
