Packed in like Sardines  this time went to a Place called ------ which Place we reacked 5.AM  Saturday morning feeling tired bed Saturday July 27.7.18. had beakfast horses was sent for us went to Bde H.Q had a bit of messing about  had dinner and a swim in a well  I dont like those horses at all  had tea and turned in Sunday July 28.7.18 turned out 5.30 groomed horses fed up  Breakfast  fell in church Parade  had dinner 12.  rested till 4.  groomed fed up  went to bed 8.PM had a drop of good wine
