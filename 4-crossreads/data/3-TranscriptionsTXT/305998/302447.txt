Stayed two days in London. It took a day to fix up things things.
Arrived home at 12pm had a great welcome. Had to leave home again for France on the 28/1/17. It was hard but had to be did, arrived back to France to find my bty. the 58th Bty divided up. half with the 9th Bty the other half with the 16th Bty. I was some time getting back to the 16th where A &amp; B sub. were attached. I arrived back the day they pulled into a new position. I went across to the 9th
