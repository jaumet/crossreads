<p>1917 Tuesday 17 July</p>
<p>Usual duties to mid day. <br/>Afternoon "off". Jock Cart &amp; self into B &ndash; to get photos. Oh horrors. They are atrocious. Tear mine up. save one for curio. Back to tea. Concert &amp; play in Y.M.C.A. in evening. "Miss Honey's Treasure".  Cliffs. Meet Misses ---- Blast 9 p.m. roll call.</p>
