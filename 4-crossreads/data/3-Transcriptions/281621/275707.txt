<p>Sunday 15<br/>The country about here is made up of undulating downs dotted here &amp; there with woods &amp; villages &amp; on the</p>
<p>Monday 16<br/>whole just now is very pretty, especially in the woods. I am debating within myself as to whether I should apply for a com &amp; if augmenting my chances of being killed or wounded, at least being able to live decently &amp; get out of this rotten life.</p>
<p>Thursday 19<br/>Still here doing nothing &amp; enjoying books and fine weather. One book Ernest Maltravers by Lytton has impressed me very much.<br/>I have continued my walkings about the country &amp; have found some</p>
<p>Friday 20<br/>very pleasant spots.<br/>This Commission business has a good hold of my mind</p>
<p>Sunday 22<br/>Visit the neighbouring town of Andover which is very slow &amp; quiet</p>
