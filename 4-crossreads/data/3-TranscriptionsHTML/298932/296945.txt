<p>Friday 24<br/>Here we are arguing the point over girl's, but it is a very difficult subject, I love all good girl's but Bill wont have it<br/>Saturday 25<br/>I do not think that I will leave this place for it is the best I've yet came across since leaving Carnsdale. Visiting "St Omer"<br/>Sunday 26<br/>Just had a good dinner Roast Beef carrots and turnips followed by Pheasant, potatoes and seasoned sauce and beautiful beer<br/>The 17th batt, picked by General Birdwood as the crack regiment and chosen to lead the Anzacs in the review by "F.M.Haig"</p>
