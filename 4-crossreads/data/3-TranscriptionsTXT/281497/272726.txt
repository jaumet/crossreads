 After breakfast this morning 4 other chaps &amp; myself went out for a bit of practice at detailing a very necessary thing &amp; not easy to master by any means we took several books of Instruction with us &amp; a rifle to demonstrate how it is done, we took it in turns to do the detailing, &amp; we used to also illustrate the writing of problems on the sand. Not a bad way I think we all learned something or other. Just before dinner a smart little shower came up &amp; we had to do a get for all we were worth.
