 Saturday 5 May 1917 
 Looking over the A.I.F. Casualty Lists in Australian Papers one notices that the Lists are printed in small type &ndash; not much space wasted. Do you remember the promincence of the first lists from Gallipoli &ndash; I suppose a 9 days wonder to many &ndash; yet it's the young men of a young nation going.
