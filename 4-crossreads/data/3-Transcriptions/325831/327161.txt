<p>6.<br/>-shine. There was a discussion the other night at mess about where to go if we got leave &ndash; most unlikely thing in the world &ndash; &amp; after everyone had had a shot, I suggested the Riviera. Carried unanimously. I have a cousin nursing in a French hospital down there, &amp; she talks of warm days &amp; magnificent flowers &amp; fruit.</p>
<p>However things are not very bad with us after all. I heard the other day of a Div. H.Q. which was carrying on in a chalk quarry.</p>
<p>I am posting you a Vie Parisienne &ndash; not for the tiresomely naughty pictures, but for a little</p>
