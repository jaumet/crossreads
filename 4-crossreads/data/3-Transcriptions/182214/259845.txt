The first of the main body arrived about 15 minutes earlier a glance back across the desert saw the men streaming in from all directions some in partys of two threes &amp; fours and at 9 PM they were still coming in. The New Zealanders who were in camp at Moascar&nbsp;did all they could to relieve the sufferings of the men who were absoulty done up First they sent their ambulances out into the desert with eight horses, &amp; we only had four horses to our waggons for the full journey, the N.Z. infantry gave up their tea &amp; marched out into the desert</p>
