 Saturday 1.6.18. Meeting of C.O's at Bde re stunt. Saw Yanks again. 
 Sunday 2.6.18  Reconnoitred Martins front- very tired on return, saw Sadler  Beiers, Forbes, & Christie 
 Monday 3.6.18  Bn working, visited 6 th  Fld Ambulance  had long talk with Robinson re Thorn
