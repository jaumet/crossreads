  
 Wednesday 16th 
 Clasification Marked B.  A lecture on soil in afternoon.  Pictures at night.  The remarks one hears are better than the pictures themselves.  Some of them take quite a personal interest & allways stick up for the right side.  The villan gets no symphity. 
 Thursday 17th 
 Heard that a man was wanted in Y.M. so asked the S.M. & was put on the job.  It is to help cleaning up serving tea etc..
