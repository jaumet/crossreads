<p>Septembre 7<br/>Working on Refugee Hutting. Reported that we have made another big advance. Bad news from Russia. Germans have taken Riga &amp; the Russians are still retiring.</p>
<p>Septembre 8<br/>Working on Refugee Hutting Ebblinghem. Nothing fresh to report.</p>
