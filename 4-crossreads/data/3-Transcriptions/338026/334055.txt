<p>Edinburgh</p>
<p>[12 Th]<br/>Arr Edinburgh 8 am. Uncle met me at the station.</p>
<p>[13 Fri]<br/>Paid a visit to the Forth Bridge, Art Gallery &amp; Sir Walter Scotts monument. Went to Pictures with Jim &amp; Meg at night</p>
<p>[14 Sat]<br/>Went to the castle. Home for dinner</p>
