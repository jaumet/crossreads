<p>I must tell you I came away in Capt Ferguson Platoon No. 8 B Coy &amp; he was the Finest officers we had him &amp; Major McCallam who is now on Div. HQ. The Battalion got a shock when he went at "Bois" <u>Granier</u>" (<u>Armentiers</u>") he was allways willing to do any-thing for the old Hands (Little) his Batman got a nasty crack at "<u>Hangard Wood</u>" &amp; I think is on his way to Australia</p>
