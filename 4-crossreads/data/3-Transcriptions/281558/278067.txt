<p>Monday Dec 3rd</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : fatigue at Y.M.C.A. : concert at night but not too good : classification in afternoon &amp; marked down for skipping exercise at1</p>
<p>Tuesday Dec 4th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : fatigue at Y.M.C.A. : Reg &amp; I went to pictures at O.T.B. &amp; they were very good : met Fred &amp; he is to leave for France tomorrow morning</p>
<p>Wednesday Dec 5th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : fatigue at Y.M.C.A. : with Reg &amp; Morrie after tea : met Dave Roberts also Bill Dwyer &amp; both look well</p>
<p>Thursday Dec 6th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : fatigue at Y.M.C.A. : V.D. lecture in afternoon &amp; very interesting : Morrie &amp; self to Warminster after tea : got a</p>
