<p>2 February, Sunday</p>
<p>F &amp; ice<br/>I rush off to P. House in new ambulance &amp; walk from cross roads. Meet Aubrey Hickson &amp; go to No 2 for Tea play Bridge until car arrives. Hurry down to YWCA dirty unshaven. Stay until Supper time &amp; rush home to shave &amp; write. Music &amp; singing very good &amp; 40 miles &amp; home from P. House.</p>
<p>Wrote MRL</p>
<p>3 February, Monday</p>
<p>F<br/>E. goes to C.O. &amp; has a long yarn about ourselves. I stay on office &amp; room for while others go up to mess for dancing lesson. We muck up generally, &amp; Dick gets a bang on the forehead. I take another fellows girl Home while he looks on. Nuryance[?] &amp; long yarn in the flat on my return.</p>
