
15 May 1918 WednesdayMove tomorrow towards our old Camp Solomons Pools-   Here we are on the Wadi Kelt, camped on the old Roman road to Jericho near where Elija was fed by the Ravens,   Down this Wadi the Christians fled from Jerusalem in the days of their persecution & long before that the caves were inhabited by prehistoric man now by Bedouins.I see by "Bulletin" May Forth sends Papers.
