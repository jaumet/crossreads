<p>In the meantime the action between the Turkish reinforcements and the Yoewaury division had developed a bit and the Tommy general was getting very frightened keeping on sending in reports that he couldn&#39;t hold the enemy. As a result an order was given for all the mounted troops to withdraw and we started back about nine that night. That was <s>about</s>&nbsp;one of the worst rides I have ever had. I was leading a spare horse with a pack, and every few minutes we would have to gallop to catch up. It was very dark and we were riding into other chaps and getting bumped about terribly. One has to keep up in these&nbsp;<s>things</s>&nbsp;rides.
