<p>When we move out of here tonight we are leaving our blankets &amp; overcoats behind which is just as well for they are both as wet as can be.<br/>The sun is shining nicely this evening for a while &amp; the stars come out quite thick but when morning breaks it is gloomy looking &amp; raining cats &amp; dogs.<br/>There were dozens of air fights this evening but I only saw one machine brought down &amp; she was a Taube.<br/>Old Fritz had a few balloons up &amp; the consequence was he shelled our reserve trenches pretty heavily with 5.9's &amp; shrapnel but he only wounded 2 men for all his shell's &amp; strafing.<br/>To-night I watched the Germans searching for some of our 18 pounders who were firing like blazes on his trenches they put shells all round them, but still</p>
