<p>The Boys</p>
<p>1.  "Do you know what makes our casualties so heavy?"<br/>2.  "No"<br/>3.  "Well it's so many blokes getting killed and wounded!"</p>
<p>1.  "By G, - this b--- gin is heavy"<br/>2.  "Yes, it's its weight makes it so heavy".</p>
<p>Liberating pigeons from trenches waist deep in mud says:-<br/>1.   "I say, Joe wouldn't it be a b----if the pigeons had to walk through this&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  mud.<br/>2.  "Yes: but wouldn't it be a b--- if we had to fly wearing steel helmets!"</p>
<p>English officer describing Australians:- "Those queer long people, who wear wrist watches and call each other bastards!"</p>
<p>French demoiselle's exclamation when she saw our little podgy regimental baby officer, Kellaway "Piccaninny"!</p>
