<p>15-2-16<br/>behind us amongst the cultivation fields; Les goes on the patrol tonight &amp; Reid tomorrow night, as we are the only 3 sigs available will will have it pretty constant; The troops are getting a good deal of work to do getting the camp in order, fatigues, Etc. the whole Sqd on fatigue this morning  Tents were erected last night after tea; yesterday afternoon we put down breast lines for the horses &amp; dug up several skulls &amp; pieces of skeletons while digging the hole, we can dig down a couple of feet anywhere here &amp; strike skulls;  Dave Lindsay turned up here this afternoon after his trip to Suez; The Col. Adj. &amp; Nelson went away early this morning by train &amp; arrived back tonight ; This afternoon Les. Reid &amp; I went out to the well &amp; put the lamp up, we intend to leave it there day &amp; night</p>
