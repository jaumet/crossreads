
17:5:18On the last day on which I wrote we reached Talut ed Dune, a spot -  & a very dirty one at that - in the hills about a mile or two in the hills towards Jerusalem.Here we languished & Hope nearly died but finally on the 15th we left for Jerusalem which we reached just as it was breaking dawn after a very tedious & slow trek.
