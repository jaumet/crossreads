<p>6/3/18  Boulogne</p>
<p>We pulled into Boulogne at about 3 pm and found that the new officers club was full up so had to put up at the old one with McConnaughty Harwood &amp;Herrod all Colonels. Went and saw the R.T.O. regarding the train for Bailleul tomorrow. Met Col. Travers in the street and had a yarn with him. With McConnaughty &amp; Herrod walked up the street and made some purchases All the streets thronged with people and very dark. Had an amusing conversation in one lingerie shop.
