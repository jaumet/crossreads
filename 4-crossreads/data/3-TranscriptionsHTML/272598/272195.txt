<p>unburied.  Party of the 9th Btn at present engaged in the work of burying the dead.  Salvage parties hard at work also, salvaging the whole of the traversed country.<br/>Very little remains of Gueudecourt as is the case with all the other villages which have been fought through in the Somme area.<br/>Big push up North with good results.</p>
<p>Saturday 9.6.17<br/>Played 5th Div. Hqrs in afternoon &amp; defeated them; unable to strike form.  Two more went on leave.</p>
<p>Sunday 10.6.17<br/>Team played 4 D.A.P &amp; won by 7 wickets.  Church parade for all hands.</p>
