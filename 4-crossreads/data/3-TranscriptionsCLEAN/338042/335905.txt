  
 July 1918 
 July 1st Pack up costumes, properties etc. & leave for Bertangles - We have had a very decent week at Vignacourt - Back to the bell tents in the woods - Bed made under the trees - July 2nd Rehearsal & re-opening show in the "hangar" of 48th Squadron R.F.C. - At present very busy on long, new sketch - Heavy bombardments along the Front.
