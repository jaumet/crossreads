Preparations are being made for Christmas. The ward is being decorated by the patients. A Salvation Army Padre was around distributing writing paper. 
 Sunday Dec.22. 1918. 42. A very cloudy day. I finished reading today 
 "Feats on the Fiord" by Harriet Martineau an interesting story of life in Norway. 
 Monday Dec. 23. 1918. 43. Feel much better today, I am not yet allowed out of bed. My temperature is nominal. A new doctor was around today. The lads have finished decorating the ward for Christmas. Among the decorations are small branches of gum trees &amp; wattles. This afternoon two red cross ladies visited the wards and distributed tobacco &amp; cigarettes, also sweets.
