  
 1916 
 Fri Sep 1st To day we are releived by the Canadian A.M.C. and are preparing to take our departure. We handed in all blankets & are paraded in marching order and are awaiting orders to move off. 
 I have a gathering in my ear through having a cold, weather is again fine we slept under the trees at back of chateau, we are warned to move off in the morning. 
 Saturday Sep 2nd Did not move off to day filling in the time cleaning up the camp & billets. 
 Sun Sep 3rd Paraded at 6 a.m. 
 After breakfast marched off at 10 beginning with a number of haults through the congestion of traffic on the road.
