<p>Dec 27 Sun<br/>Mess orderly<br/>Communion .<br/>All day ashore looking round Colombo etc<br/>7 letters</p>
<p>Dec 28 Mon<br/>Ashore all day<br/>Look round city, rickshaw rides, visits to Buddist Temples Cinnamon Gardens, Museum, Fruit Markets. Mr Stevens etc. etc.<br/>Return to Kyarra at 6 p.m.</p>
<p>Dec 29 Tue<br/>Ashore from 9.30 a.m. to 2 p.m.<br/>Another good look round<br/>Worked in office in afternoon until 7.30<br/>Ship left Colombo at 8 p.m.</p>
<p>Dec 30 Wed<br/>Kit inspection</p>
