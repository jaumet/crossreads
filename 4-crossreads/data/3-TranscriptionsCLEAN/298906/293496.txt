  
 & I walked to Shrewton a pretty little village about 6 miles from here, it was a long walk but worth the trouble, we had our tea in there & I got quite a lot of snaps, got back towards 9, I had a cup of tea at the canteen, then went & had a bath & I am going to bed in a few minutes after I finish this entry. 22nd. Have been inoculated again this morning a double dose this time, my arm is stinging already. Beautiful & sunny today one of the best days for a long while.
