The streets were lined with people fairly well dressed and a sprinkle of khaki.  The place boasts a couple of theatres that have two performances nightly.  Trams run along a couple of the streets & there is a service of omnibuses.  We went to the Second show at the Empire, a variety show, at 9pm.  We had booked the seats while the first performance was in progress.  A long queue stood outside the entrances, but we just strolled in and sat down.
