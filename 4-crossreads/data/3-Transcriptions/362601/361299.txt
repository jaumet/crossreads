<p>18 November, Tuesday</p>
<p>Nettie comes down. We miss tram but go to M &amp; meet G &amp; Possie. We go in show on beach &amp; laugh though specialley at possie. Catch 11.50 tram Home.</p>
<p>19 November, Wednesday</p>
<p>Come Home &amp; go to bed early. Dad's seems very very bad.</p>
