<p>Dec. 7th Tuesday<br/>Paper &amp; Parcel mail.  Sheepskin vest from home.  Changed relief now on 12 to 6.  Mild.</p>
<p>Dec. 8th Wednesday<br/>Weather rather cloudy but mild.  Remainder of 3rd run arrived also many returned sick.</p>
<p>Dec. 9th Thursday<br/>Weather dull &amp; mild.  Day off, spent in gully.  Rally 9-30.</p>
<p>Dec. 10th Friday<br/>Weather Fair.  Sgts day off.  Naval bombardment afternoon on Road Mados [Maidos].</p>
<p>Dec. 11 Saturday<br/>Weather still dull.  Nothing startling till 13th.  <strike>14th</strike>.  strong ideas of evacuation were expressed &amp; 14th orders were issued at midday.</p>
