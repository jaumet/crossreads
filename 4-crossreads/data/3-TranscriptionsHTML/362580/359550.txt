<p>also one from Annie Howard &amp; a few from other friends.  The <u>Cake</u> is excellent &amp; we did enjoy it last night &amp; shall for a few days to come.  The short bread was nice too.  The Ginger is still going strong.  I think I wrote Birthday Greeting to you but if not, I wish you a very, very Happy Birthday, heaps of Luck &amp; present.</p>
<p>Fondest love &amp; kisses to you all.</p>
<p>From<br/>Your loving Sister<br/>Edith.</p>
