 Thursday 15 February 1917 
 On Duty last night but I don't think there is a TURK within 100 miles of us. Raining slightly. 
 [The following paragraph crossed through] Many were drunk from the Canteen on the Railway &ndash; Like South Africa greed is an awful curse to the young soldiers altho' I am no wowser. (Clive Norton in trouble.) 
 Had a bath &amp; put on a pair of "judpores", i.e. (Indian riding Pants), old Tommy Orford my Batman was greatly taken by the cut [The following crossed through] &amp; called a Tommy Officer &ndash; this is not a compliment out here. Hear a mail is coming along &amp; our pay &ndash; neck nearly allright.
