<p>16th. Bob Modral, Cameron, Braithwaite &amp; I have been arguing for 4 solid hours this morning, the subject was the fighting qualities of the French &amp; British soldiers, I don't think we got very far ahead or brought out anything out of the common but we killed an otherwise slow morning very comfortably for the time passed wonderfully quick  We passed another ship this afternoon she hung onto us for a good while but our old tub gradually forged ahead &amp; we have now left her far behind</p>
