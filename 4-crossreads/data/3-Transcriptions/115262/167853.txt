<p>Tuesday 27 March 1917.</p>
<p>In camp. Drill &amp; signalling in morning and in afternoon, tents struck and everything well aired on acct. of 2 more cases of C.SM. today.</p>
<p>Lines picquet<strike>s</strike> after this till 5.15 p.m.</p>
<p>Detailed for Hd Qrs fatigue tomorrow.</p>
