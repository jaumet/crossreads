<p>Tuesday 14 January 1919<br/>Visited Champs Ellyses Place de la Concorde  &amp; Arc de Truimphe</p>
<p>Wednesday 15 January 1919<br/>Visited Notre Dame, Hotel de Ville, Napoleans tomb &amp; Invalides. Went to see Casino de Paris &amp; saw Mistinguett &amp; Chevalier. They were very good</p>
