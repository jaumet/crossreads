
(This place is called Ammon.) firing line  camped down Behind a Big stone hill had a Bit of dinner  we are going into action from this Place.  I am left Behind to hold Camels the rest moved up to the front  had tea on guard tonight can hear the firing turned in 10 P.MThursday March 28.3.18turned out 5.30 AM.  fed up Breakfast grazed my five camels the fight is in full swing now a big shell came where we are  moved back a mile had dinner watered camels and grazed them had tea 5.P.M. had a good Bath in a Bit of a hole in the gully  on line guard tonight turned in rum issue and rations we wanted them too.Friday March 29. 3.18.turned out 4.AM saddled
