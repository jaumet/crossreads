<p>Sept. Tuesday 4 1917</p>
<p>Another bright moonlight &ndash; night.   Fritz aeroplanes over Calais again.<br/>He also visited us &amp; dropped several bombs in Boulogne killing 12 people.<br/>Heard he got 80 convalescents at Calais last night &ndash; dropped bombs right on camp -   These nights are ideal for attack s.   He is also attacking coast towns of England.</p>
