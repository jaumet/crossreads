At last I could not stand it any longer, &amp; I hops in &amp; exposes him properly to the boys he nearly fell through the ground &amp; turned all the colors imaginable what a shock he got he was one of the biggest squibs imaginable. I'll bet he is more careful next time he starts skiting. Went over &amp; slept with Len last night for we have no blankets as yet. I have had none now for a fortnight &amp; more &amp; last night was the first time I had my clothes off for that period, &amp; it was bonzer, anyone who has not had these sort of experiences have no idea the relief it is to strip off.</p>
