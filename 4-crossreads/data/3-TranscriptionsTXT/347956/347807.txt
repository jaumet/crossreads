 just heard from the bridge that we are not to move into the wharf till to-morrow.  at 6.30 to-night Orvieto bade us goodbye &amp; good luck &amp; started on her way to England, I am satisfied we are far enough north as last night was quite chilly it was also showery yesterday  this afternoon &amp;  last  to-night.
