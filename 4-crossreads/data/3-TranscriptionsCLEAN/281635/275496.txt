Went to Trafalgar Sq. to meet Cohen but he did not turn up so took bus to kings Cross & enquired about trains going north. Decided to leave for Hull by 1.30pm train & go to Filey tomorrow. Went back to lodgings & got my gear & boarded train at 1/4 to 1, getting very nice 3rd class corner seat. Train left at 1.30pm and travelled via Hitchin, Huntingdon, Peterboro', Grantham, Newark, Retford & Doncaster to Selby where I changed to Hull train, left there at 6.5pm & arrived at Hull almost 7pm.
