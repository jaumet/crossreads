<p>Monday Sept 10th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 6.30 am : we were on M.O.s parade in morn : went out to see the "stunt" ground after dinner &amp; received our instructions etc</p>
<p>Tuesday Sept 11th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 3 am : fell in &amp; marched to the ground : went through the "stunt" &amp; all the Bgde were there : it ought to be a good one &amp; the boys seem in good heart so Fritz ought to get a towelling up : specialist parade after dinner</p>
<p>Wednesday Sept 12th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 5 am : packed up &amp; moved off about 8 am : billeted at Winnezeele : good clean up &amp; then bed</p>
<p>Thursday Sept 13th</p>
<p>Winnezeele : reveille 4.30 am : handed in our packs with spare gear : moved off through Steenvoorde :</p>
