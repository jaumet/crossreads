<p>25th<br/>Have not gone out today &ndash; but are still hoping to go soon.  You had better addrss 1st Training Regt until I tell you for certain that I am in the Regt.<br/>Had a letter from Aunt Min &amp; Aunt Beatrice May 28th    none from you since May 14th but expect one every day.<br/>Rob.</p>
