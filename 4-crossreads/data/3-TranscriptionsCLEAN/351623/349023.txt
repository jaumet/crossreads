
8 Dec 17Left S. Flow in company with Light Cruiser Birmingham & 7 Destroyers. We are guarding a convoy.
9 & 10 Dec 17Sweeping North Sea & convoying 4 ships.
11th Dec 17Arrived back at Scapa & coaled.
12 Dec 17Made false start & had to return & anchor.
14 Dec 17Coaled.
15 to 18th Dec 17Anchored at Scapa.
19th Dec 17Left to convoy food ships. 30 in number.
20 Dec 17Picked up 20 more ships & escorted them into harbour (Norway). Seas rough. 30 odd more ships left to come back with escort.
