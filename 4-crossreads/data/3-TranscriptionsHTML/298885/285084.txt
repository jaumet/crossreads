<p>Thur Aug 3rd &ndash; 16<br/>Left Bouzincourt at 8.30AM and it was dusk when we reached St Leger where we are likely to stay 2 or 3 days.</p>
<p>Fri Aug 4th &ndash; 16<br/>Although we are supposed to be out for a spell we were up again at 4AM to go out and practice manouveres.</p>
<p>Sat Aug 5th &ndash; 16<br/>Inspection by General Walker. OC 1st Aust Div</p>
<p>Mon Aug 7th &ndash; 16<br/>Up again at 5AM. for sham fight lasting all day.</p>
