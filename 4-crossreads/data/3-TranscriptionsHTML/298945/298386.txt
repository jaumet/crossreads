<p>a shell torn wall or tree mars the scene, but <u>comparatively</u> it is splendid. I have found a hollowed out bivouac foundation large enough for four men, &amp;have sheltered one corner from the dew to make me a beautiful fresh resting place. Under me as I write is straw freshly gathered from the field on the edge of which, in the fringe of the wood, I am sitting. Beyond my waterproof sheet roof are trees whose foliage is prettily pierced by the light of the setting sun.</p>
