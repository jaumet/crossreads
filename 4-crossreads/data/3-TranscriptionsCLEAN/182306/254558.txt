Feb. 7th Friday
Very hot to day all lying on deck to cool off. Passed a few ships also a small Island
Feb 8th Sat.
Big swell on to day. Held sports on the boat deck which were fairly lively. Just passed a big Island Had boxing to night which gaveus a couple of hour of excitement. All the boys scrambling for possies to sleep on deck.
Feb 9th Sunday
Last night we passed the Twelve Apostles
