 Sunday 17 December 1916 Rather quiet today.  Very heavy fog in afternoon &amp; very cold.  Usual improvement work. 
 Monday 18 December 1916 A good deal of Artillery fire on both side.  Very cold.  Usual improvements. 
 Tuesday 29 December 1916 Same work as usual.  Revetting trenches &amp; generally consolidating position. 
 Wednesday 20 December 1916 Freezing very hard, very very cold with a little snow.  Nothing unusual. 
 Thursday 21 December 1916 A little rain, very muddy again &ndash; cold.  A good deal Artillery fire. 
 Friday 22 December 1916 Not much work today.  A fair amount Artillery fire both side. 
 Saturday 23 December 1916 Move back about a mile to a new Camp.  Handed over to 5th F Coy. 
 Sunday 24 December 1916 Started build huts "Nissen Bow" on new site for camp.  Things quiet. 
 Monday 25 December 1916 Not much doing today &ndash; a very quiet Xmas &amp; not too much to eat.  Visited 1st F Coy. Engrs. 
 Tuesday 26 December 1916 Working on new camp.  Heavy fog, very cold &amp; light rain.  A little shelling. 
 Wednesday 27 December 1916 New job supervising road formation on Road &ndash; Montauban Quarry Siding Bazentine. 
 Thursday 28 December 1916 Working going well on road &amp; Railway Siding.  Things quiet.  Very little shelling. 
 Friday 19 December 1916 Same routine as yesterday forming roads &amp; working at Siding. 
 Saturday 30 December 1916 Nothing fresh going.  Conferences still discussing peace.
