The young Donkey which I mentioned was born some weeks back is growing fast and frolicking around our lines quite at home with its old Mother. A male donkey has joined the mother a few weeks back &amp; our lads make use of it for transport purposes. 
 Tuesday 24th August Still gusty and blowy. I received some papers but no letters &amp; was a bit disappointed. Am still acting as Quartermaster Sergeant &amp; Pay my daily visit to the beach for victuals etc.
