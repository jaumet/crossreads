
February 1918Saturday 23
MessinesSpent the day in usual duties, and had some visual work in morning.Weather dull.On L'aison with Kerr and listened in at 59th Batt. wireless receiver, to Kiel Wireless Station and also a couple of Dutch Stations at Zeebrugge.Camped with Bombers and found them good chaps.Day a very quiet one had a couple of shots with 3 star rifles at an old turnip.
