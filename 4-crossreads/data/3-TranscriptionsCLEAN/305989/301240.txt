  
 Sunday 21 January 1917 Church parade in morning, outpost at night.  Scotties took over the redouts in front of us. 
 Monday 22 January 1917 Cruel day, rained & blew periodically, rained at night. 
 Tuesday 23 January 1917 Making targets all day for M.G. practice. 
 Wednesday 24 January 1917 General fatigue. 
 Thursday 25 January 1917 Nothing in particular doing. 
 Friday 26 January 1917 Clarrie went to M.G. School Zeitoun, took horses to 152 & led them back. 
 Saturday 27 January 1917 Took horses for exercise.  Mail in.
