<p>Monday Jan 14<br/>Went to Heliopolis &amp; Luna Park. Heliopolis as I have mentioned before is a beautifully laid out city &amp; fine buildings. One would not think War existed in Gay Cairo.<br/>Tuesday  Jan 15<br/>Leave finished only 3 day flutter. Caught 11 AM train back to <strike>Cai</strike> Kantara. Arrived just after two. Crossed Canal &amp; once again boarded the old trucks. Left Kantara 5.25 pm to rejoin unit.<br/>Wednesday Jan 16<br/>Shivered all night in truck 3 days leave not worth trip. Arrived Raffa 6 AM. Khan Yunis 7 AM Bulah 8 AM Gaza midday &amp; Meged 3 pm. Held up here owing to wash away on Railway line. Drew rations. Moved on again 5pm. Saw 2 engines derailed</p>
