<p>May, 1917. Tuesday 22<br/>Day very wet. Up at 6 and moved off in rain through Albert, Millencourt to Henincourt. Fair billets &amp; 2nd Coy also here. Arr at 11 &amp; went for a walk after dinner dinner. Mail in. Slept well on grass in open. Some drunks about.</p>
