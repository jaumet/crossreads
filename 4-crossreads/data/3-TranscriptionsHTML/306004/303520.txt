<p>11.9.16<br/>Mon 11th<br/>The railway now runs to Debabis &amp; Infantry troops are on the move going out, a strong rumor is circulating that 3,000 Turks are at Bir-El-Salmana &amp; we shall be going out shortly to try and hunt them out; The Camel Corps are stationed to the South of us &amp; are to be the advance guard when we attack; Daily parade are now held from 9.30 to 11 AM &amp; from 2.30 to 4 PM. I am giving the Sigs buzzer work during the day time &amp; flag a lamp practice after tea. All leave to Port Said is stopped from today &amp; the line along the coast is washed away, &amp; the men have to return through Kantara.</p>
