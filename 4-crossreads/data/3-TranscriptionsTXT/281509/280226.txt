 3 3 January 1916   Monday 
 Start night duty tonight.  Receive letter. Mrs Wheatley &amp; Misses Gibb come up to camp in afternoon  Bring tomatoes &amp; a fish  Gave them tea in Rec. tent &amp; saw them off by train at 6.30  Rob &amp; Tony also. Rob &amp; self to recreation hall nothing doing.  Home to ward at 9 pm.  Write to Mary Mitchell &amp; Marget Graham 
 Mrs M. E. Waights c/o G. D. Pickering 28 Acherhon St Durham City England
