<p>Wednesday June 20.6.17<br/>turned out 5.30 went to feed camels they were fed had shower had Breakfast cleaned up read till 10.30.  watered camels had dinner 12.30 reading all afternoon had tea 5 P.M. a cool day went down to canteen had one mug Beer turned in 7.30.  the Boys are Playing cards for matches.</p>
<p>Thursday June 21.6.17<br/>turned out 5.30 fell in with blankets went and saddled up and exercised camels had Breakfast 7.15.</p>
