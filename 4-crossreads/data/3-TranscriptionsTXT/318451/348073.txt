 Sunday 25 Last night the 33rd had a raid, the Barrage was put on about 10.30 &amp; was very solid.  The raid was successful they say, but we lost about 6 men killed &amp; 20 or so casualties.  They say they found the bosch fastened to wires on his front line.  (Not true 27th) I have been at Houplines today to the cocoa stall. There was a good service at the Y.M.C.A. &amp; we had Sacrament afterward to which over 100 men stayed.  The weather is foggy, clearing occasionally &amp; not very cold.
