<p>7<br/>Of course I was well aware of that.<br/>But there might yet be a chance I thought, so I hung around in wait for the unexpected.<br/>It came, as I somehow thought it would<br/>One man failed to answer his number<br/>"Where is that man who wants to go" said the Sergeant<br/>"Here" and with that I stepped into the vacant place</p>
<p>We were all examined by the M.O. furnished with kits, equipment and ammunition and that night lodged in one hut under instruction to be ready to move off at 3 o'clock next morning.</p>
<p>That was to be the last marching out of many of those brave men.</p>
