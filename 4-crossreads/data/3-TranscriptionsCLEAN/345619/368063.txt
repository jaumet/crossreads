  
 [Reverse of the above postcard] Mrs A. J. Burrowes (Mum) Franurra Rooty Hill N.S.W. Australia. 
 Dearest & Best We are just of back to our camp after a real fine time, I have been wanting to write a letter to you here, but there has not been a chance, I hope to fill a long one between now & Tuesday. from your husband.
