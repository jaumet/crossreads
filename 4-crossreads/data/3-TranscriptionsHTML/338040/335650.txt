<p>Destination reached after 9 miles travelling (20m in all). Encamped in Hospital previously a Brewery. House adjourning occupied for one day and a night by Crown Prince. Quarters very comfortable-Village of Sailly.<br/>April 6th<br/>Baggage guard 3am -6am (3hrs) chosen in a party of 36 bearers march of 3 miles with full kit and [Blank]ets. Observation Balloon march lengthened to 6 m owing to error of our guide. Arrived at Red Cross Station. Billeted in</p>
