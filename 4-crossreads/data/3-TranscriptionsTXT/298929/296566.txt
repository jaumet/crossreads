 4th July.  Big stunt here by our army corps. 2nd  4th &amp; 5th Divs. Advance 2500 yds on a 5 mile front.  1500 prisoners &amp; countless guns. T.M's. &amp; M.G's.  Our div in close support. 
 8th  Fritz counter-attacked on a large scale but was easily beaten back. Played at Aus. Army Corps H'qrs. Complimented by General Sir John Monash at Bertangles. 
 9th  Stand to 8.30 pm to 1 a.m.  Fritz expected.
