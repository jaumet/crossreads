  
 June 11th Early Rise - Rehearsal - An afternoon walk to Flesselles where I pay a call on Harold Williams and spend a very pleasant time conversing about the English Tour & "Little" personal items - Have tea with my "Brother Wag" & return to the Aerodrome for the Show - We are patronised by Gen. Monash - Gen. Featherston - Surgeon Gen Howse - Major Berry - After the show I again meet Harold Love who is returning from the Base to his Unit.
