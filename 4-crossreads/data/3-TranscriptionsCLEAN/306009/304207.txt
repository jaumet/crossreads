  
 1915 Jan. 21 Thu. Early mornings are extremely cold. Camp still in a state of disorder. Reported again to Lieut. Col. Nash at Mena House. Working daily now at Mena House with Sgt. Clerk in Lieut. Col. Nash's office. Men working very hard in our camp which is somewhat apart from the big one, erecting tents & field Hospital which our unit will also look after. Big bag of mail arrived - very busy sorting - received 10 letters, etc. - mighty glad to get them.
