<p>January, 1916<br/>Sunday 16<br/>Make another move this morning.<br/>All the M.G. Instrs. are put into several new tents in another part of the camp<br/>Have tea &amp; play cards w/- Dad.</p>
<p>Monday 17<br/>Morning, Instruction Care &amp; Cleaning.<br/>&amp; Afternoon Judging distance out on the desert.<br/>Very tired so turned in early<br/>Very windy all day</p>
