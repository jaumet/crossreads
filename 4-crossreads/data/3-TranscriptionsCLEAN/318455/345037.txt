 Sept 24. We marched  yest  back yesterday to Bray-sur-Somme some 18 kilos where we are being assembled with the men from the other Divisions - We are beginning to feel that we are really on our way. 
 Sept 26 Made a start at 9.30 this morning from Bray on our long train journey -  30 of  There are 30 of us in one horse truck. - There is a kitchen truck - one half of the train draws tea at midday & the other half stew - then vice versa
