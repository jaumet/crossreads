<p>WEDNESDAY, JUNE 4, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Down at Middle</p>
<p>Bore all the morning with</p>
<p>Peter trying to fix the engine</p>
<p>on to it, but had no luck</p>
<p>Round Hill paddock in the</p>
<p>afternoon. M<sup>r</sup>&nbsp;&amp; M<sup>rs</sup>&nbsp;Carter came</p>
<p>over to afternoon tea.</p>
<p>Went after bunnies at night</p>
<p>&amp; got 20 &amp; 4 hares.</p>
