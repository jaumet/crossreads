<p>The Medical Officers here, got very ill   &amp;  had to be transferred to another hospital. Poor old Chap was fearfully ill but is much better. I received a letter from him yesterday, but he may not get back to us for months. He was one of the Original Staff &ndash; the others are always coming   &amp;  going. I'm in another ward now.
