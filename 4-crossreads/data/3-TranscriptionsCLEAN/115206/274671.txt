
April 1918Thursday 11
Blaney Tronville   D.R.Weather fine. Went across country to Daours, La P--- Bussey and reconnoitred bridges and position forretreat if necessary. This was carried out under Cap Mellor.At 2 pm. We left Chateau for Bussey and stayed at new artillery Hdqs.Plenty of taubes Gothas and etc. flying around unloading .N.16.C.Heavy bombardment by Fritz.Feeling fairly fagged.
