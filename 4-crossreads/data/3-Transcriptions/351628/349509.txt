<p>I have seen a lot of my old Condobolin mates since I came here and lots of other fellows that I know. I haven't been to see the Pyramids or any of the sights of Egypt yet but I think Norm &amp; Artie &amp; I will go out there next Sunday if we all get leave.</p>

<p>I remain<br/>Your loving nephew<br/>Frank</p>
