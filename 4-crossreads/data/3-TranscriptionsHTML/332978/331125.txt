<p>46 August 26th<br/>their efforts, we are slow but sure.</p>
<p>August 27th<br/>Had bath today &ndash; foot bandaged. It is just the same as it was 2 months ago &ndash;</p>
<p>I was cook today, preparing my favorite menu once again the dishes meeting with the approval of all &ndash;</p>
<p>Made my pay Book up today &ndash; I have a credit of &pound;400, this includes everything also allowing for <strike>debits that</strike> costs of cakes &amp;c also money that has been sent me from Australia &ndash; Capt Robinson &amp; Lt Baerline tried for their offence today <strike>August 28</strike></p>
<p>August 28th<br/>Recd letter from Bert &ndash; also 2 Parcels from A.R.C. &amp; Gertie &amp; Eileen over 300 parcels in today &ndash;</p>
<p>Spent morning in the sun reading &ndash; Played Bridge &ndash; Debate tonight Subject being that all land &amp;c be under state control the motion was lost. Burke is now President of the Debating Soc.</p>
<p>August 29th<br/>Slept in till 11 am &ndash; Raining</p>
