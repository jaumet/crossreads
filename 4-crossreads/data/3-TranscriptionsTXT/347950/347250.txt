 26th Sept. 3.30 P.M. Captured S.S. Hitachi Maru after firing 15 shots at her &amp; killing 13 of her crew. She had a 4."7 gun but never got a chance to use it 27th Sept Both ships proceeded to an anchorage inside the Maldive Islands. About 200 tons of coal was taken from Hitachi Maru &amp; some of her cargo such as rubber, linseed &amp; hides etc. All our passengers &amp; men over 60 years of age were put on board her.
