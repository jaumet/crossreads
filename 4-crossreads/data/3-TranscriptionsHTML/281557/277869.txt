<p>off now &amp; the new names were read out: dinner: kit inspection in afternoon: tea: posted a letter to Ethel at Heliopolis: went in with McCrop &amp; Tom: weighed 10 stone 10 lb: had a feed of oranges &amp; ice cream: packed kit &amp; then bed.</p>
<p>Tuesday March 14th<br/>Revellie 4 am: breakfast: fell in &amp; moved off 6 am to Zeitoun siding: got into open trucks 40 in a truck : about 35 in length &amp; a bit of a bumpy ride : left at 8am &amp; arrived at Ismalia 12.30 pm: very sandy: had bully beef &amp; O.F. biscuits for dinner&amp; tea: had a bosker swim in the canal lagoon: very deep &amp; was quoice katura: a few war ships are lying off the shore here &amp; I seen my first observation balloon: like a big sausage: close to flying sheds: welcome bed;</p>
<p>Wednesday March 15th<br/>hot day: revellie 6 am: fell in 7 am &amp; inspected: breakfast: fell in &amp; went for a swim: quoice katura: dinner: men required picked out: I &amp; Bert Berry schemed in: some good views of aeroplanes rising &amp; landing: medical exam &amp; V.D. exam: tea: bully beef stew: marched up &amp; joined our Batt: all our mob in no 7 plat B Coy: met Ern Beatty: bed: only about 60 tramway lot picked</p>
<p>Thursday March 16th<br/>hot day Revellie 6 am fell in 6.30 am rifle inspection: breakfast: fell in &amp; done some platoon drill: lecture by one of the officers &amp; instructed as to our embarking routine: issued with "pamphlet": rifles &amp; web equipm't: dinner: went &amp; seen the 20th boys. J Schulstad, J Marsland, Rogers, McNeil, Morris &amp; others: F Poole is in the 7th: packed up kit: ammunition &amp; rations issued: [indecipherable] things burnt in incinerator: tea: fell in about 6 pm &amp; marched clear of tents: bivuaked till 2 am: Schulstad, &amp; Marsland Poole &amp; self had a good old yarn: doze: 3rd V D inspection I have had. seen Gen Birdwood &amp; he looks a fine chap.</p>
