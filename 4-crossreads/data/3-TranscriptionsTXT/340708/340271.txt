 July 1917 Sat 21st Pay day so we are financial again. Sunday 22nd Received a lot of cases today otherwise nothing happened to brake this monotony. Monday 23 Our friend disturbed the peace again with the aeroplanes &amp; of course our guns opened up savagely so that ended sleep for a time Tuesday 4th&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; [this should be 24th.] Fine weather again we received more cases again. Wednesday 25 Another visit from Fritz he dropped a few bombs but did no damage. Thursday 26th Still keeping fine &amp; nothing much doing
