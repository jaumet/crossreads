<p>Saturday 26 Jan<br/>    Transport moved out today as they were going by road all the way.   Sent off all my equipment &amp; only left enough to carry on sick parades &amp; send some stuff to battalions.</p>
<p>Sunday 27th Jan<br/>    Sent the last lot of stuff out to the battalions this morning:   in the afternoon walked to Etaples &amp; went to Paris Plage again.   Had a good afternoon capped by good feed in Paris Plage.   Felt quite fit on my return.</p>
<p>Monday 28th Jan<br/>   Our last day in "Cormont" so did final packing and returned all surplus &amp; empties to the main depot for the 15th Field.   Needing a haircut badly, took the car on into Boulogne &amp; had the luxury of a first</p>
