<p>No &ndash; the Eastern went to Pleasant  island right on the Equator &amp; may have gone on to Ocean island &ndash; The probability is we wont melt.<br/>"May the Lord &ndash; stiffen - <u>at an early date</u> - the commander who sent us on this stunt" is the pious prayer of Mr Bumpus &ndash;<br/>If the Eastern does not turn up we must go to Rabaul &ndash; for coal -  &amp; we are ? 3 days journey  E of Rabaul -Its rather a difficulty to put in time &ndash; Reading is almost out of the question in the heat &ndash; The smoke room &amp; wardroom are sweat boxes &ndash; The best thing is early bed on the poop with the stars in the velvety blue above you &ndash;<br/>Our dinner on New Years eve was &ndash; some ham, beans &amp; rice pudding &ndash; Then we passed the Port &amp; drank &ndash; The King &ndash; then we had several more ports &ndash; the bottle going round freely &ndash; then we adjourned &amp; had the gramophone out on the rail of the ship &ndash; Pay came along &amp; said he had an announcement to make &ndash; We went into the wardroom &amp; found he had N. Year greetings from the Ship's company &ndash;</p>
