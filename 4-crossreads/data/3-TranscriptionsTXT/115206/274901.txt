 November 1918. Wednesday 27 
 Wembley. On furlough Spent a very pleasant day up town, avec chere Vic and O. Wired to Smithy to come on down Weather wet and miserable. Met quite a number of O's pals at the office. Had a very nice musical evening in honor of Vics departure of France tomorrow. In bed at 3 am tomorrow.
