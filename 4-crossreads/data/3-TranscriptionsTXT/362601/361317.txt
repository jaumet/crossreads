 24 December, Wednesday 
 Not very busy. I buy wines &amp; send Home. We catch 10.10 tram &amp; after good journey although that &amp; heavy job parcel carrying we reach Forest Home. Bed at 2 am &amp; up at 5 am. We have nice merry day. 
 25 December, Thursday 
 Up at 5 am &amp; spend a Merry X'mas. 
 Plenty of music &amp; merry arie.
