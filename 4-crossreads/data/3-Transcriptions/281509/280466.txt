<p>1916 August 31<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Sanitary fatigue. Finish early. Cart, Fitz &Lamp; self go to Reminghelot to <br/>Y.M C.A in morning. A very big concern there. caters for Canadians &amp; Tommies. Pay day. Go into Poperinge after tea. Cart, Mac. Jock &amp; self. Rained very heavily all time. Are removing to Rest station tomorrow. A couple of miles away. C &amp; self camp in barn on return from Pop. to escape the noise of the men who come home shickered after pay</p>
