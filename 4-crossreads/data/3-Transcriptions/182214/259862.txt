<p>mention that we had our pet or mascot monkey with us &amp; she cause plenty of amusement on our journey. once near paris she broke away only to be recaptured by Les Kerr as she was getting over a big wall, The first morning after we left Marseilles found us at Arles &amp; from there to Lyons the country looked beautiful one can hardly describe the valley of the Rhone the beautiful flowers Vineyards the pretty Villages &amp; old Churches looked beautiful, as we did not travel fast we were able to see quite a lot. Whether it was the fact of being stuck in Egypt so long with nothing but sand to gaze upon, made us appreciate the scenery better, but I have never seen better scenery in all my travels</p>
