 Saturday 20 April 1918 Photos.  Pilot Lt. Lewis, in air 3 h. 5, all photos spoilt at the wing photo section. Photos.  Pilot Lt. Mc, in air 2 h. Films auto. camera jammed. On first photo trip we (2 machines) met 4 Huns 1000 ft. above us. Very [from opposite page] disquieted and dissapointed that our pilots would not engage, though I urged mine to do so. fired 50 rounds at Huns. 
 Sunday 21 April 1918 No photos, too cloudy, spent day digging in tent.  First hot day.
