Sunday 17 December 1916Rather quiet today.  Very heavy fog in afternoon &amp; very cold.  Usual improvement work.
Monday 18 December 1916A good deal of Artillery fire on both side.  Very cold.  Usual improvements.
Tuesday 29 December 1916Same work as usual.  Revetting trenches &amp; generally consolidating position.
Wednesday 20 December 1916Freezing very hard, very very cold with a little snow.  Nothing unusual.
Thursday 21 December 1916A little rain, very muddy again &ndash; cold.  A good deal Artillery fire.
Friday 22 December 1916Not much work today.  A fair amount Artillery fire both side.
Saturday 23 December 1916Move back about a mile to a new Camp.  Handed over to 5th F Coy.
Sunday 24 December 1916Started build huts "Nissen Bow" on new site for camp.  Things quiet.
Monday 25 December 1916Not much doing today &ndash; a very quiet Xmas &amp; not too much to eat.  Visited 1st F Coy. Engrs.
Tuesday 26 December 1916Working on new camp.  Heavy fog, very cold &amp; light rain.  A little shelling.
Wednesday 27 December 1916New job supervising road formation on Road &ndash; Montauban Quarry Siding Bazentine.
Thursday 28 December 1916Working going well on road &amp; Railway Siding.  Things quiet.  Very little shelling.
Friday 19 December 1916Same routine as yesterday forming roads &amp; working at Siding.
Saturday 30 December 1916Nothing fresh going.  Conferences still discussing peace.
