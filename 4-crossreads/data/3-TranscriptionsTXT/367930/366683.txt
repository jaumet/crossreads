 18 Orders to assemble the 11th &amp; 12th LH Regts. 
 Visitors from Meadi: &amp; 11th &amp; 12th 
 19 Capt Hudson  arrived  reported back from Minich. I attended Col Cox at Cont'l Hotel but [indecipherable] on &amp; off from 10.30am till 11.40PM to Helman in afternoon. Orders to 11th &amp; 12th to concentrate at Oasis Camp. 20 Sunday Col Cox arrived in camp 9.30 am; attd Ch Pde left before lunch. 21 Gave orders to 11th &amp; 12th re moving into Oasis Camp: tentage [indecipherable], &amp; entire tents brought in by them worked out. Cheques made for full proportions of all registered funds 22 11th &amp;amp 12th March out: Some tentage over Scale taken by 11th LH. Major Parsons &amp; I had Garrison Adjt over re matter, he was not in so I ask for Lt Col Gant to come with me on Wed: also asked Capt Mc[indecipherable] for his CO.
