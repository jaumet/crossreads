<p>Confusion reigned.</p>
<p>Reinforcements arriving by lorry were told to fix bayonets at once &amp; hurry into the line, whence <strike>they</strike> many shortly returned wounded, to be taken away by the lorry that brought them up. Infantry men wandered about the fields, some going towards the line but many away from it; all of them having lost their units.</p>
