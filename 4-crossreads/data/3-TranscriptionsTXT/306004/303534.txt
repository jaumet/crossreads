 22.9.16 Kings Regulations say the President must be a married man, put in some time tonight fixing up tables, stools etc &amp; it is ready to commence tomorrow. A few Turks still come in occasionally on Camels &amp; give themselves up to the troops on patrol around Salmana 
 23.9.16 Sat 23rd Got a supply of goods in the Sergeants Mess today &amp; opened it tonight; The Officers went down to the Hod below our camps &amp; had a practice game of cricket this afternoon; they are to play the 2nd L.H Regt next Saturday; Nothing startling happens here lately.
