In the meantime it aches as only a very bad tooth can, and the only remedy I can find is pure Carbolic Acid and I have burned my cheek & gums and lips & tongue with the terrible stuff - but it cures the toothache. Toothache takes a good deal of good humour and optimism out of a man and these things are very much needed out here where we are having a hard life of it. I suppose the upshot of the whole thing will be that I shall have to get that infernal molar yanked out.
