 Tuesday July 11.7.16 doing fatigue left Melbourne 3.30pm large crowd on wharf one girl looked broken up. Passed through heads about 8 PM calm sea Wednesday July 12.7.16 Very cold day to day a little rough. Thursday July 13.7.16 arrived in Adelaide went ashore outer harbour went into the town very nice town.
