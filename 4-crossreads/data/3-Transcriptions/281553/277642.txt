<p>31st Tuesday Aug. &amp; Sept.<br/>Continued sapping till 1 p.m. and on returning to Reserve had orders to Stand to to change camp.  Returned to own Bivouac Gullie 4 p.m. &amp; left here 7 p.m. with all gear lower down valley where we spent night.</p>
<p>1st Sept. Wednesday<br/>Remained all day near 4th Red Cross Dressing Stn., moved off 8 p.m. for Popes Post.  Halted for night at Shrapnel Gullie about 10 p.m.  Sea Bath midday.</p>
<p>2nd Thursday<br/>After many fatigue jobs moved on to Popes Post about 5 p.m. where found rest &amp; comfort in fine large dug-out for night.</p>
