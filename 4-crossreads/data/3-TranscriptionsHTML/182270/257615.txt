<p>THURSDAY, OCTOBER 30, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Shore 452 lambs at</p>
<p>Abbottslea and started them</p>
<p>home after. Got some to the</p>
<p>4D &amp; had to leave the others in</p>
<p>Point.</p>
<p>Measured up Kenny &amp; Gosden&#39;s</p>
<p>burning off and paid them off.</p>
