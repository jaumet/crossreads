<p>After that we raced home, across <strike>a</strike> country &amp; done the trip in 20 mins arriving home wet to the skin with rain &amp; sweat, but a good wash altered things &amp; after that dinner, &amp; the rest of the afternoon off. I don't know now whether I will have to go without my leave, for the boil broke this morning &amp; things feel quite easy tonight.<br/>Sent a packet of snaps off to Miss Duke this afternoon &amp; 2 of my diaries home.<br/>4th. Not much doing today out it is nice &amp; fine.<br/>5th. Today we had a defence scheme to work out, this did not take us long.<br/>In the afternoon 4 of us took our gear over to Larkhill", save us carrying it about with us</p>
