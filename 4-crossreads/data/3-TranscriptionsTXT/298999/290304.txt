 had wash and clean up fell in 8.30.  went to Beach with camels give them a wash in the salt lake had dinner 12.30.  rested till 3 P.M. got some Packing ready for my saddle had tea 5 P.M. finished stuffing saddle.  turned in 7 P.M. 
 Friday June 22.6.17 turned out 5.30.  went down and fed camels had shower and had Breakfast.  fell in 8.30 for a rifle inspection inspected rested till 12.30 had dinner reading all afternoon very hot had tea 5 P.M.  on line guard to night nothing about turned in had a good night sleep.
