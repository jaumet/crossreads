 Paris on the 25th  October  Nov 1918 
 My dear Sir; 
 There is already a long time I have received no news of you. &nbsp;I hope that you are always in good health. 
 I suppose that you have not received my two last letters that I have sent to you before the armistice. 
 At last! the war is over, and it shall be possible for you to go back at home. You and your fellow soldiers have deserved well of the country.
