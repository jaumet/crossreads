 Monday 15/4/18. Intercompany relief&nbsp; caught prisoner, visited posts 
 Tuesday 16/4/18&nbsp; wired half front &amp; commenced to connect posts. Wrote to Doff. 
 Wednesday 17/4/18. Finished wiring &amp; connecting posts&nbsp; dug a C.T. Herbert Killed. 
 Thursday 18/4/18 Relieved by Currie- moved back to Support 
 Friday 19/4/18 Remained in support&nbsp; 2 Coys digging in front area 
 Saturday 20/4/18. Relieved by Dawson&nbsp; moved back to Reserve&nbsp; one Coy bathed- Two letters from Doff.
