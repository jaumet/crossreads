<p>7 August 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>Reported "Ambulance" Transport torpedoed &amp; 123 lives lost. Should have crossed the "Line" some time today We are having remarkably cool weather considering our position Just a nice lively sea &amp; a cool breez. Nothing of importance to note.</p>
<p>1918 August 8<br/>Friday<br/>A beautiful fine cool day with a light breeze blowing. Nothing of any importance reported. We "lifted" the Southern Cross last night &ndash; the first time I have seen it for nearly four years. A very welcome sight.</p>
