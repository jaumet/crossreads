<p>It is good job for me for I do nothing only make arrangements for the mens food &amp; etc. &amp; detail the parties off as wanted.</p>
<p>It was freezing cold last night but this morning is a beauty nice &amp; sunny.</p>
<p>Through some mistake of the Q.M. we were not taken on to-days rations strength &amp; so that meant that we had no tucker to go on with but I done a bit of scouting &amp; got bread, tea, sugar, salt, jam &amp; a box of "pork &amp; beans &amp; the cook bandicooted some potatoes from a neighbouring field so we did very well.</p>
<p>Len came down with a guard this morning, he stops for 24</p>
