<p>As for Sanitary etc. The camp is far ahead of my wildest anticipations.  We are all in one hut and very comfortable at present.<br/>At Exeter Station a committee of ladies handed us Tea and cake and a card with the Compliments of the Mayoress of Exeter.  This was very acceptable.<br/>The people here seem to be very open and trusting even to the extent of leaving a change counter in a remote corner for 10 minutes surrounded by soldiers.</p>
