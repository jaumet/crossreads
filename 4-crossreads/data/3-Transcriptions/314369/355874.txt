<p>15 August 1915<br/>Sunday<br/>11th After trinity</p>
<p>Our day off sleep well all night   &amp;  have a good spell through the day<br/>The heat is a great nuisance we not having means of making shelters except w/- Brushwood which is very scarce.<br/>Not having any means of shaving we are beginning to look like wild men. x</p>
<p>1915 August 16<br/>Monday</p>
<p>After a very quiet night we receive a little mail   &amp;  I get Richies letter dated June 18th glad to hear all well<br/>We are getting very sick of it all.<br/>The mens health is breaking down under the constant strain   &amp;  hard work 'C' company's Sick list averages about 10 men to Hospital each day.<br/>Later Receive Mum's Letter but we are not able to answer them from here. At 2 pm a man in my trench got his head nearly blown off by an explosive Bullet.</p>
