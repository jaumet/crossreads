This afternoon we finished our cricket match in fine weather it was an easy win for us for they only scored 63 in 2 innings. I kept wickets the whole time, which made my hands very sore for we have no gloves or pads as yet. out of our collection we bought a football but the cricket set has not arrived as yet.
