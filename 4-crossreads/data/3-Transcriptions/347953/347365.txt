<p>17</p>
<p>The Zoological Gardens contain all sorts of animals including the Giraffe<br/>The Museum including all mummies &amp; Egyptian antiques</p>
<p>Our training here is most severe &amp; overdone. We are overworked, given unnecessary hardships &amp; deprived of privileges due to us. Long &amp; difficult rout marches almost daily, night bivouacs, reviews, divisional &amp; brigade &amp; company drill, and various strategic attacks on specially located towns. The sand is very unhealthy &amp; has caused defective lung trouble to many of the men.</p>
