<p>Australian Imperial Force</p>
<p>Base Records Office,<br/>Victoria Barracks</p>
<p>Melbourne 6th September 1919.</p>
<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>Forwarded herewith are two photographs and description of the locality of the grave of your bother, the late 2nd Lieutenant F.S. Phillips, 1st Battalion, which I trust come safely to hand.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>J.M. Lean<br/>Major,<br/>Officer i/c Base Records.</p>
<p>Mrs. W. H. Read,<br/>C/- A. D. Stobo,<br/>Alliance Association,<br/>Pitt Street,<br/>Sydney, New South Wales.</p>
