<p>reaching road before Waterlot Farm got shelled on the duckboards about 8 came over two combined shrapnel &amp; H.E and fell very close to us and we were lucky. At the A.M.C. 2nd Division men were in a car singing &amp; celebrating the passing of the old year. Coming across the plateaus all the guns in the whole sector opened up for about 10 minutes to give Fritz a new year greeting</p>
