<p>Wed 3rd<br/>Working dismounted close to camp.  believe there has been some fighting close to the canal &amp; are likely to be called on at any moment to go there.<br/>The proceeds of the Kursaal tonight are to go to the sick &amp; wounded fund &amp; have got out of signalling to night to go in to see it;</p>
