
<p>WEDNESDAY, JULY 30, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Round town all the morning</p>
<p>with Alan. Had lunch at the</p>
<p>Wentworth and back to Lisheen</p>
<p>Linda &amp; I came in to dinner at the</p>
<p>Wentworth &amp; went to the &quot;Silent</p>
<p>Witness&quot; after. It was not much</p>
<p>of a show.</p>
