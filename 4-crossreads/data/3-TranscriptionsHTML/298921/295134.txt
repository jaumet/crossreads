<p>June, 1915<br/>Saturday 12<br/>Came up to Alexandria on the Coast to visit our wounded. Many of the men I enrolled have lost limbs and eyes but are quite cheerful. Cooler here by 10 degrees than Cairo &amp; the Transport A42 is in the Bay some go there tonight to see<br/>Daisy &amp; Joe's letter of May 9th just recd have not seen Toby Phillips yet but mentioned Jack Lorimer Glad to hear all is well &amp; so am I.<br/>Am posting this to<strike>night</strike> morrow<br/>Love to all<br/>F.W.</p>
