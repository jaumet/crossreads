<p>Saturday 24 July, 1915</p>
<p>came back to life at day break &amp; had a look round, we have big tents to live in &amp; a wooden mess shed to have our meals in. Should have a good time if it were not for the sand &amp; heat.</p>
<p>Had a look round Heliopolis. Just getting to know our way round.</p>
<p>Had a trip into Cairo arrived home 10.15 p.m.</p>
