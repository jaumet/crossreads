<p>4th Saturday<br/>Still fatigue duties, Water, Wood, Rations, etc.</p>
<p>5th Sunday<br/>F. Meat &amp; Swim.  Fatigue continues.  Letter writing.  Had to sleep handy to trenches in case of attacks.</p>
<p>6th Monday<br/>Fatigue, Sleeping at Arms, Alarm midnight for &frac12; hour.</p>
<p>7th Tuesday<br/>Fatigues.  Mails in 8th &amp; 9th.  8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14 [indecipherable] Fatigues.</p>
<p>15th Wednesday<br/>Reported to Quins Post 10 a.m. as Cpl. of Trench Mortar Section under Sgt. Watson.  Demonstration later of day.  Quartered in sheds, D Terrace.</p>
