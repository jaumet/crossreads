 Novembre 8 Had a nice day. Had stroll around City in afternoon principally in the Strand &amp; at night went to the Palladium with Mr Smith. The show was very good. It has been raining heavily most of the afternoon. 
 Novembre 9 Went up to Town about noon &amp; had lunch there. Afterward returned to Leytonstone arriving there about 6.30 Went to see Zig Zag at the Hippodrome &amp; it was a very good show indeed.
