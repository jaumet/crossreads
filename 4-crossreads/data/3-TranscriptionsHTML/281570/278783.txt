<p>Bde Orders 10.5.15<br/>Bde Order 162<br/>20.5.15  Div orders 73-81 93-90 Foll extracts from Div orders are published here for information &amp; guidance<br/>73/13/5/15 Intelligence S.H.Q reports that in the South the 29th Indian Bde made a success advance on the left on the Southern Force during the night of 12th-13th May &amp; maintains itself in the position then gained.</p>
<p>2nd Aus Bde has arrived from Cape Helles disembarks today &amp; rejoins Aus. Div machine G Sec of 2nd &amp; 3rd LH Bde &amp; 4th LH &amp; Otago M.R. arrived yesterday &amp; are being kept in reserve for time being.</p>
<p>Clearing Battle Field. Arms accout[rements] etc Wounded men will when possible be taken to F Amb &amp; thence to Casualty Clearing Stn belong to [the men. On transfer of wounded to H Ship  Arms Accoutrem &amp; such articles of Equip as are not required by patient will be handed over from Casualty C. Stn or F Unit &amp; handed to nearest Ord Ofr. Staff Ofr from Each Section will be detailed to Superintend the cleaning of that Zone of Battlefield in which Div is operating &amp; will arrange for the collection of all abandoned to or material at suitable positn from whence the stores should be taken to nearest [Indecipherable]</p>
