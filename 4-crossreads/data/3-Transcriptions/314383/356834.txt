<p>Feb 10<br/>Embarked on the "Malwar" some 40 nurses on board.  Food good &amp;  beds lovely.</p>
<p>Feb 12.<br/>Splendid trip.  We commence Russian classes, the Russian teaching us is a full blown Count who escaped after the Revolution, was one time an airman, walks lame on  a/c of a bad crash he once had.</p>
