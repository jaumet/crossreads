Great indignation is being caused here by the deportation into Germany of large numbers of French &amp; Belgians from the country captured by the Germans. One of the towns of the Department we are in (Pas-de-Calais), Arras has been bombarded for a long time by "les boches" &amp; they are still at it, I see from to-day's paper. It is too far away to hear the guns. The zeppelins have been very busy over England lately &amp; the coasts on both sides of the Channel are now alive with searchlights as well as the patrol ships.
