 AUGUST   SUNDAY 20   1916 
 Fine & windy. The 27 th  Bat was on duty so there was no church parade  I made a Signalling flag for myself in morning and read the Quirindi papers in afternoon & wrote some letters in the evening. 
   
 One of the sweetest enjoyments of life is true friendship - it divides sorrows, couples and doubles pleasures.
