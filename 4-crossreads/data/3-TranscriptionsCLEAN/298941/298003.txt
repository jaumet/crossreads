  
 In the third range on way lay along a ridge overlooking a flat stretch & on this we observed what looked very much like the Pyramids of Egypt. They were not true Pyramids, one face being long than the other, but they showed just as prominently on the landscape as the fashion was in Egypt. What they were I could not conjecture. I am writing this in a semicircular roofed hut. We got soup & afterwards
