<p>Thursday 3 June 1915<br />In Cairo during the morning&nbsp;<br />Working rest of the day&nbsp;<br />Many letters from Home carry dates from 20<sup>th</sup> Jny 1915 to 4<sup>th</sup> May.</p>
<p>Friday 4&nbsp;June 1915<br />Not out all day&nbsp;<br />Busy. Office. Reading. Writing.&nbsp;<br />50 patients in one bunch from Alexandria tonight.</p>
<p>Saturday 5&nbsp;June 1915<br />Posted letters Watkins, Arthur, Knowles, Armit, Pharmacutical Secty, Victoria&nbsp;<br />In Cairo, Med school, chemical laboratory &amp;c. between 10 &amp; 12 noon.&nbsp;<br />Office work. Sleep from 3 to 5 p.m. Wrote to Flowers, Fitzgerald, Usher, Maitland.</p>
<p>Sunday 6&nbsp;June 1915<br />Posted to Flowers, Fitzgerald, Maitland, Usher, Girls, Paton, &amp; Mrs Fraser.<br />Mass 10 a.m at Bassilica in Heliopolis. Benidiction at Sisters Church (Africian Mission) at Zeitoun at 7 p.m.</p>
