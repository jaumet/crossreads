 19/6.16 Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers 
 Dear Mr. Horsfield Can you assist Mrs. McGregor, who temporarily as she has only received &pound;1-3.11 this month &amp; no certificate from Melbourne to say when next pay will be made &amp; with 2 little children cannot get along until money will come &ndash; 
 Yours truly E. H. Bennie
