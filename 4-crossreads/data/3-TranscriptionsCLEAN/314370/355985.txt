  
 February, 1916 Monday 21 The whole school went out on an attack scheme in the early morning After dinner we put in a couple of hrs on stoppages. Draw P.T 200 pay & have a long chat w/- an old pal now a Lieut. Weather getting warm again 
 Tuesday 22 Machine gunners go out on a Tactical scheme returning at 8.30 Morning on stoppages & afternoon reviewing notes. At night I go to see a big old Roman picture "Cabiria" very good.
