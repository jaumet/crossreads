<p>4 September 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>Still getting good news from the Front where things appear to be going very well with the Allies. Nothing of importance to note on board. Reported Sir John Forrest  died en route for England.</p>
<p>1918 September 5<br/>Thursday<br/>We still have a fair breeze &ndash; bright weather - &amp; a nice lively sea. Things going well &ndash; but journey getting very monotonous.</p>
