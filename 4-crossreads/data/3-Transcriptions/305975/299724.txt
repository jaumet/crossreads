<p>they carry out their duties  got a Change of Pyjamas this morning  have been wearing what I have on for 7- days  was told to get up  feel very queer in the head.   had a letter from maud S and mick<br/>Wednesday Oct. 23.10.18<br/>feel a little better to day while I am in the shade.   if I walk about in the sun I feel very giddy and weak.   wrote to Mick.   went to D H to day for meals</p>
