
21/9/16  Thursday
Nice day. Got a parcel from Mrs O'Kelly. Got letters from Ammi, Eileen, Frank, Aunty Lyons, Mrs O'Kelly & J. Hagan. On Gas Picquet night & day.
22/9/16  Friday
Nice day. Went down for some ashes for filling up around the Camp. Very Cold. Went to evening devotions at the Church.
23/9/16  Saturday
Nice day. Got orders to pack up & be ready - Finished up by taking over Aid Post at "Rifle Villa" - at last some of us are with our own brigade - few calls during the night. Most comfortable post we have been in yet.
Sunday  24/9/16
Nice day. Lively trench warfare - Fritz putting them in freely to V.V. Sap. Very few casualtys.
Monday  25/9/16
Nice day - Fairly quiet - Very little sleep last night. Aeroplanes busy.
26/9/16  Tuesday
Nice day. Have a nasty cut in my hand, two stiches. Very quiet.
