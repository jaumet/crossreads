Thursday 11 July
To-day some of the 1st Battn. found Fritz asleep went out and collected eighty of them brought them in with only one casualty. They say it was done so quietly that the Germans in the rear are ignorant of the event, 4 p.m.
Friday 12 July
Wet and windy all day. Ye Gods the 1st 4th Battns. are taking Bosches galore the total is not about 150 and our casualties are very slight. In fact there is very little fighting over the matter at all.
