<p>these vehicles were very funny. By the way a Rickshaw is like a small Light Sulky Drawn by a nigger. When we tired of this we decided to go down &amp; have a look at the native markets. Here for 1/- you could purchase a tree of sugar bananas that many, that it took one man all his time to carry it, &amp; that night when we went back to the boat weary &amp; worn out, but never-the-less happy, we felt that we had eaten a mountain.</p>
<p>Next morning we left Colombo &amp; set out for our next stop Suez. The weather from Lenin had been delightful all the way to Colombo but now it was awfully hot, &amp; we were all getting as brown as the niggers themselves. The sea was as smooth as glass &amp; now &amp; again a solitary ripple &amp; a black fin showed</p>
