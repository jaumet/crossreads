<p>fails to do well.  One of the schemes is to turn your shirt inside out once or twice a day, so as to give them so much route marching that it breaks their little hearts.  Some have a method of leaving them serverly alone so that they get so big &amp; fat that they eventually burst.  There is a little story told of a London cockney, calling out to his mate in a neighbouring trench, "I say 'arry, 'ave you got any lices over there?"  "Lices! not 'arf we aint!  Bags of 'em."  "Garn! I don't mean them 'ere lousey lices, I means boot lices."</p>
