 January 1918 Tuesday 8 
 At Beutin &ndash; France A heavy blizzard commencing at 6.30 am and ceasing at 7 am placed 10" of snow over everything. Drifts in places were 5 to 6 feet deep. Smith and Thompson's leave passes &ndash; for Paris via Etaples, in high glee. Went in evening to rendezvous and played cards. Wrote to Roy and Eve.
