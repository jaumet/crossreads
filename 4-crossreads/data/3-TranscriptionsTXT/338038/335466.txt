 [Printed letter-head] Commonwealth of Australia Governor-General Melbourne, 3rd May, 1918. 
 Dear Madam, I am desired by His Excellency the Governor-General to transmit to you the following message from Their Majesties The  King and Queen:- "The King and Queen deeply regret the loss you and the Army have sustained by the death of your son in the service of his Country. Their Majesties truly sympathise with you in your sorrow." In addition to the above message, I am desired by the Governor-General to express their Excellencies' heartfelt sympathy with you on the death of this gallant officer. 
 Yours faithfully Geo Steward Lt.-Col. Official Secretary 
 Mrs. W. Garling, Burns Bay Road, Longueville, N.S.W.
