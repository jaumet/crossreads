Wednesday 4th/4/17.After arising at 7 am & had Breakfast I took my leave I said Good Bye to my sister thanking her for making my stay enjoyable & proceeded to the Station in fine weather I did not have far to go & was soon on the platform, I had a word with the Engine Driver after introducing myself & he invited me to ride on the Engine to Barnham Junction which was only a few miles on reaching there I had to change into another Train which soon arrived & off I went to Victoria Station through a different Route to what I did going to Bognor as the Weather was fine although Cold I interested myself in viewing
