MONDAY, JUNE 9, 1919.
                                            X
Fine & hot. Went down & got the
Middle paddock bore pumping
again then went over & fixed
the Mountain bore mill up. Got a
fox in Seven Star as I was coming
home.
Mr Innis & his mate came over
to start burning ect in Wheelers
Tom Day also came across
to look at the rest of it.
Mrs Tickle took a strip at the
end of the cultivation fence &
started.
