One of them remarked that he supposed he would soon be selling newspapers in the streets. Another discussed the quality of the Hotels de luxe in London as if he contemplated an early visit. Returning to the K&ouml;nigsberg in the British destroyer Oak, they played at Auction Bridge as the best way of passing the minutes of intense constraint. That surely was one of the strangest card parties in history.</p>
