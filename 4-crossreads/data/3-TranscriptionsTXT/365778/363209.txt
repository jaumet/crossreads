 Friday 27/7/17 Muster parade and general clean up for move. Saturday 28.7.17 Marched to Miramont in intense heat, entrained at 4.45 pm for Cassell. Sunday 29/7/17 Reached Cassell 3 hours late marched in a terrible thunderstorm to Woesterne. Monday 30/7/17 Battalion in Camp H.Q.s at Billets in large farm, looked for training ground, wrote to Doff. Tuesday 31/7/17 Visited by Generals irdwood, Smythe, &amp; Wisdom, had a sleep during afternoon.
