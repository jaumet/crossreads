
1916 4 January 4Tuesday
Slept till 11.30 am.  Get up have shower, dinner & dress for parade.  Governor General reviews troops in afternoon.Rob & self go to English Hall to concert. The lassie with the goo-goo eyes. Negro. Confidences. Night duty  Mosquitoes bad.  Write to Geizer. Receive notes from Miss Palser & Janet.
