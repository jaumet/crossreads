 4th December 1916 GERERAT Monday 
 packed up and vacated our previous camp at 10.30 am. Had to carry our blankets on the saddle and there was some fearsome looking bundles too. I borrowed a Kodak and took a photo of my horse. I don't think I could have found room for anything else I even had a shovel. Trakked across the desert and came into this camp at 10 00. There is nothing to write about at this camp - just a camp among the undulating sand dunes. We have a long way to water rather too far I think.
