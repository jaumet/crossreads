<p>Thursday 2 May<br/>The Boche has been fairly quiet again to-day but what few he has sent over have landed close to our billett, "duds" everyone.  Very warm. Playing cricket all day.<br/>Hankin wounded up the line this morning.</p>
<p>Friday 3 May<br/>Still on the Northern outskirts of Hazebrouck, doing little else but play cricket all day.<br/>Beautiful weather.  A walk to Hondenghem this evening.</p>
