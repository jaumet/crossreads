Moreover we could clear out of camp from 5 o'clock till 10 at night, so that we could club together & take a motor to Wilton or Salisbury & revel in the delight of ham & eggs or an expensive fillet of steak. Half on to pay day the motoring would be retrenched & the heroes would saunter to the village of Barford-St-Martin & get in front of a great fireplace such as that described in Barnaby Rudge as the old Maypole Inn.
