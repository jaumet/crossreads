<p>[Wednesday 19 July 1916]</p>
<p>[Following paragraph crossed out]<br/>I note what you say re being "Godmother" to a lonely soldier etc, don't you think you've got your hands full with me- but you were always such a busy little Mother &amp; it is very good of you.<br/>Clive Weston is here a Sergeant, a brother of Mrs Blake's.<br/>Word has just come that 8000 Turks are approaching us so we expect a fight in the morning.<br/>All my love will post this.</p>
