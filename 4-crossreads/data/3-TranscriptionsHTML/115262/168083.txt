<p>Monday 12 November 1917.</p>
<p>In early hours of morning a terrific thunderstorm broke &amp; continued <s>till</s> for an hr. Ground impassable for camels. Halted till dawn and moved off again till 7.30 a.m. Now camped in an orchard of olive trees etc, 4 miles from the beach and beside the village of Julis. Are now Nth of Gaza.</p>
<p>&quot;Stand to&quot; all night. <s>Moved off at dawn in a Nth to NthWest direction</s></p>
<p>Turks counter attacked strongly at dusk, 14 planes go over on a bomb raiding expedition at 4 pm.</p>
<p>News that (Hebron) El Khalali has fallen.</p>
