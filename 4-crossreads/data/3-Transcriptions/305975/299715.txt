<p>maud. Josie Mabel mick Dorrie maud S.  had tea  turned in<br/>Monday Sept.30.9.18<br/>turned out 4. AM Packed up  Breakfast  got in Big Brumby Motor Lorries and left for Jerich a long ride  we got there 11. AM  terrible hot day and dusty  had a cup of tea  got very sick  got malaria again  was sent to field ambulance feeling bad.<br/>Tuesday Oct.1.10.18<br/>Feeling a little easier this morning  cannot eat</p>
