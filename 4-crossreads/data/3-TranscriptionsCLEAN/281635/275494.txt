
Rochester & Heine Hill to Victoria Station London arriving about 12.30pm. Were escorted across Channel by 3 destroyers & an airship. Saw Dover castle in distance. Went to Horseferry Rd & got fixed up with clothing & money etc. Left H.Q, about 2.30pm getting bus in Victoria St for Tottenham Court Rd, & from latter went to YMCA & was recommended to lodgings in 50 Bloomsbury St where I went & got  room to myself, bed & breakfast costing 4/-. Then went to Kings Cross & had pass altered to Aberdeen by the RTO & found out about the train. Went by tube then to the bank (of England) & then walked to Basinghall
