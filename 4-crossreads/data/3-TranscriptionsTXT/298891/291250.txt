 1917 June 
 16 Saturday 
 Arose at 8 feeling very weak &amp; went to Shoolbreds to buy boots &amp; get Wellingtons. Home. Rested during afternoon &amp; typed "On D Street" &amp; went to bed at 10. Street children annoying. Warm day. Thunderstorm during late afternoon with hail &amp; much thunder.
