
May, 1917. Whit Monday. Monday 28Fine day but shower in evng. 7 Parade & Marching order at 8.30. Had to return to shave & clean-up. Came back &. had to return again & discard packs. Marched to Sports ground & 3rd Bgde Gen Leslie inspected us. Savage was not congratulated. Fell in at 12.15 and had a bath at Bresle & rest of aftn & evening wrote letters. No 2 on picquet to-night.
