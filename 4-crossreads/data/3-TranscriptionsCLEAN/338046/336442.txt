The convoy anchored off the town about a mile out.   While groping our way in to the anchorage, we had to have the lead line going all the time.   We rolled about in the swell a good deal all the time that we were there.   The town, Jamestown comes straggling down to the sea in a valley between two precipitous mountains.   The chief building appeared to be a church with a tower.  I had a good look at it with a telescope while on the bridge.   I
