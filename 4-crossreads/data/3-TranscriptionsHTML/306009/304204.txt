<p>1915<br/>Jan. 18 Mon.<br/>Still beautiful weather &ndash; perfect day.<br/>Plenty of work in connection with disembarkation<br/>First batch of 54 left for Camp at Cairo at 7.30 a.m.<br/>Men discharging stores from holds all day.<br/>Went ashore on leave from 7 &ndash; 10 p.m.  Went for drive through city &ndash; visited Greek Boy Scouts Club, very interesting, felt quite at home although few could speak English &ndash; About 300 Greek Scouts in Alexandria; 100 Italian, 40 British, International 50, French 20.  A Greek Scout went round with me &amp; could speak 5 languages:- Greek, Italian, French, Arabic, &amp; English.  Next to Arabic, French is the language most in use in Alexandria &ndash; Changed 9/3d into 40&frac12; large piastres at a money changers.<br/>Did &frac34; hr. work before going to bed.</p>
