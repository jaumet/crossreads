  
 8 - that Gibson wrote from Manchester 2nd Western Hospital a card to Wildwood Rd. our first information of his being here. I then wired and found he was at Royal Eye Hospital Manchester. Just the words "Come tomorrow" - and the address. Archie Scot Skirving had come up to see me. I had an hours talk with him at the flat and tore off to catch the 6.10 train to N.W. from Euston. The girls both wanted to go but I thought I better see first how bad he was, and the chance of his coming to a nearer hospital soon.
