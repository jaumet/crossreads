<p>We had a lovely view from the hotel bedrooms, over the Casino &amp; gardens, to the sea beyond with a portion of coast cliff.  We used to eat our breakfast in leisurely fashion attired very airily &amp; gaze out upon this vista.</p>
<p>The tram rides from Monte Carlo to Nice on the one hand &amp; Mentone on the other were along a glorious piece of coast.  We only saw two theatres whilst we were away, one in Paris &amp; one in Nice.  The French acting is admirable.  They really do live their parts and act so naturally.  I enjoyed both of the pieces very much.</p>
<p>We met some charming &amp; very interesting people at Monte Carlo &amp; saw the world's Lady Champion tennis player, M'selle Lenglen at Nice.</p>
