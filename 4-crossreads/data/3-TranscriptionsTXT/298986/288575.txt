 to the Tivoli, a good show.  There are Pictures, Concerts &amp; Lectures in the different Y.Ms &amp; Halls every night.  Sports one day, Football &amp; Baseball at other times.  There are Yanks as well as all sorts of English &amp; Oversee Troops here, other camps over the road with W.A.A.Cs, Indian, &amp; Black troops, a horses convalese home not far away.  We are high up &amp; look over the town with higher hills all round.  Have been marked out to day. 
 Wednesday 2nd
