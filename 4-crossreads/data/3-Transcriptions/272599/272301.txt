<p>1917  Thursday 1 Feb.</p>
<p>Put in sleepless night with back. Very painful to move crawling in &amp; out of bunk half hours work.<br/>Fritz puts over several shells round about mid-day no damage results.  A bit of a hop over by 4th Brigade last night.</p>
