<p>[JULY, 1916.]<br/>117th day from home, and the 17 week, be leaving for Aus next January 1917<br/>Fuesday 4<br/>I was dreaming, I was dreaming, of a nice little girl sweet persons of Nellie Breen<br/>Wednesday 5<br/>All day long the big guns keep speaking and very loud at that<br/>Thursday 6<br/>Still living in hopes, big hopes</p>
