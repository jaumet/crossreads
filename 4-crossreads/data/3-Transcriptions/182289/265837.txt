<p>Le Bizet<br/>Weather hazy and dull all day with light drizzle about 7 p.m.</p>
<p>5.3.18<br/>Advance parties of 22nd M.G. Coy reported and were guided to positions.  C.O. being taken round the line.  Main body arrived about 6 p.m. &ndash; relief completed by 8.30 p.m. when command of the barrage position passed to the 22nd Company.  Transport was provided for taking the men to the rear lines, and a hot meal served on arrival.</p>
<p>6.3.18 &ndash; Moquet Lines<br/>The day was spent in packing limbers, cleaning camp and disposing of surplus.<br/>During the night several amendments to the original Move Order were received from the 9th Brigade.  See Appendices.</p>
<p>7.3.18<br/>Reveille at 6.30 a.m. &ndash; men on parade at 7 a.m.  Final packing of stores completed and all transport "hooked in" at 8.45 a.m.</p>
