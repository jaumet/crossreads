<p>27-4-17<br/>relieved us at 5 oclock this afternoon &amp; we went a mile up the Wadi to Camp. I broke my pen yesterday &amp; was given another by Vern Harris, this evening ; The 6th Regt has patrols out in front today who are in touch with the enemy.</p>
<p>28-4-17<br/>Sat 28th<br/>The 3 Squadrons of this Regt went out at 6 oclock this morning &amp; kept in touch with Abdul all day ; A few shells were flying from each side &amp; the Turks fired a few rifle shots but did no damage; I ran out &frac12; mile of wire this</p>
