<p>28 Monday<br/>Discharged from Hospital at 12.20 pm. caught 4.50 train home  Bought ticket to Helensburgh.  People pleased to see me at home.  Got till 2/3/16 sick leave.  Went to Liverpool to get dirty clothes, got a letter from Doris.  Went round the street at night, got home at 9 Pm.  nothing doing, rain fell during the evening.</p>
<p>29 Tuesday<br/>Pay day in camp (&pound; 3-10-0) never got mine.  Spent a quiet morning home.  Went for a surf. Went round the street in the afternoon.  Saw Jeff Turners sister-in -law.  Would like to make her acquaintance.  Played Dad cards in the night beat him 4 games to 1 (euchre) a few showers of rain fell. Very cloudy.</p>
<p>1 March  Wednesday<br/>Very cloudy &amp; raining in the morning stopped at home in the morning, got some cartridges  &amp; had a few shots in the afternoon, 2 hits out of 3 shots, very restless at night time, played dad 3 games euchre &amp; he won the lot.  Went to the pictures until interval, then went round to Station, Saw Frank Moran &amp; George Makin, got home at 11 pm.</p>
