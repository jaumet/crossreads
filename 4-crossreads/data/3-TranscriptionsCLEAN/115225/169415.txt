 Jan 15  th    Still standing out in the bay with five other transports. latest latreen wire is that we will sail to-morrow. we played crib & 500 - I slept below on account of a cold on my chest. 
   
 Jan 16  th    We weighed anchor at 1.30 PM. with the five other transports. "Berimar" "Orsova" "Kenilworth Castle" "Port Littleton" & one other. escorted by the cruiser "Cornwall" - The sea was smooth as a mill pond, the weather beautiful & we watched the flashing of mirrors from all along the shore they looked like diamonds flashing in the sun, the Cape's farewell to the convoy - 
   
 Jan 17  th    Sea still smooth & weather lovely, we played cards in the afternoon & evening - The port holes are closed daily at sunset also the dead-lights - I slept on deck under the awning. 
   
 Jan. 18  th    The "Orontes" caught us up to-day & stayed with the convoy - the weather is still fine & the sea smooth - we played, draughts & cards -
