
H.M.T.S. "NESTOR"Hospital Ship.
[22 Sun]Paid 2. (Port Said.)Arrived Port Said 9am, Natives all round the boat selling Fruit & silks etc  Left P.S. 4-30 pm. Had search Light on bow going through canal
(Suez)
[Diarist has written SUEZ. along the left hand side of this page.][24 Tues]Arrived Suez 8am. Argyleshire left Suez at 3am this morning for Australia. "Burma" arrived two hours after the "Nestor". Boys 11-30pm boys officers & sisters singing carols on Every deck. Ringing ships bell etc
[25 Wed Christmas Day]Received parcel from Red X. containing tobacco 3 pk. cigarettes, jam (1 lb tin) chocolate Sultanas etc.Xmas Dinner. Roast meat, Goose, apple sauce, Plum pudding  oranges, chocolate & cigarettes & beer. Left Suez 8-30pm. All night nearly all the officers well gone making speeches etc. Spent an enjoyable day.
