After eating it I felt horribly sick but the other chap seemed all right so of course I didn't say anything.  It may not have been the pastry anyway. Yesterday we had a great meal. a tiny round of roast beef with roast potatoes & boiled cabbage, a rice pudding as a sweet & then a dessert of two apricots I had bought that afternoon off a little village kid for a pinch of tobacco.  Breakfast is a good meal too.  Usually porridge, bacon, fried bread & chips.  Lunch is usually of the cold variety bread & jam etc.  We get one of the [Arabic word] to get us tea.
