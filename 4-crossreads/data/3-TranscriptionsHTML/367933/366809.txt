<p>"A" Squadron<br/>1st. Light Horse Training Regt.<br/>Egypt<br/>26th Oct.1916</p>
<p>Mrs. Vernon Secretary<br/>1st A.L.H. Comforts fund<br/>Sydney</p>
<p>Dear Madam,<br/>By latest mail two parcels of Champion tobacco came to hand.<br/>On behalf of the men of our Regiment I desire to thank you for the above, which are still another proof of the kind thought of the people at home and of the benefit of your fund.<br/>thanking you and wishing you every success<br/>Yours faithfully<br/>J Norman MacMillan</p>
