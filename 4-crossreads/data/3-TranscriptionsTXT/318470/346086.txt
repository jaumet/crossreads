
4/5/17Company Pontooning & general training.
5thDiv. Sports today.  Horses were looking beautiful & turnover was very good.
6thChurch Parade this morning.  Coy. played 55th Soccer in afternoon & won.
7thCompany Pontooning & other training.
8thToo wet for training.  Getting ready for entraining.
9thLeft Becourt for and per foot & train travelled to Vaulx & relieved 7th F. Coy. Engrs.  Lively Artillery firing along Line.
9thVery heavy Artillery firing along this Front.  Enemy firing a lot of shells on positions right back here.
10thCoy. doing a little training & a little Front line work.  12th, 15th Brigade & a Tommy Brigade hopped over.
11-5-17Party from my Sec. out wiring tonight in Front of Line.  Very heavy bombardment by our Guns.  Expect hop over in morning
