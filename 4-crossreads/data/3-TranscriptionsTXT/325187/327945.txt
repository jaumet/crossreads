 [Friday 21 July 1916] 
 Travelled all night &amp; shelled enemy, usual Taubes &amp; Scouts sniping-   I could tell you more but dare not. Casualties I must not mention they were not many but Johnny Turk is in form alright. [Following paragraphs are crossed out] Received Mail which went to France also Ointment from Chemist Deniliquin thank you very much- "Bystander" This nearly = BAIRNSFATHER   While we were bustling to get away, the Supply Officer sent a message that all Hoop Iron off Forage &amp; empty bottles (Pickle Gift) were to be collected &amp; returned to Dump at Railhead &amp; not to the Supply Depot!!!
