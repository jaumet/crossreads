<p>December, 1915</p>
<p>Thursday 9</p>
<p>Arrived at Mudros last night.</p>
<p>Could not see anything of course.  Anchored all night &amp; left at 10 this morning for Alexandria.</p>
<p>Food plain but simply delicious after those biscuits &amp; awful bully beef.</p>
