
Tuesday 27thOn fatague makeing a dugout for the cooks 3 Aussie letters
Wednesday 28thAs we missed the bath yesterday went to day but alas the bath had taken unto its self wings Went on to a little village Sillay le Cic & salvaged a few ods & ends useful about the dugout. Saw a Frenchman & his wife gathering a few of there cherished belongings. But am afraid that after the usual bombardment & then the men camping in these villages there is nothing much left any good Six of us were told of to take the wash Cooker to the canall & wash it Much better if we had taken the cooks.
