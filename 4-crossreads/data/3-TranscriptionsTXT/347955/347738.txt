 [wrote to Alma ang aggie] sent to 31st G.H. Cairo which Place we reached 2.P.M. after a lot of messing about  had dinner and rested  had tea 4.30 Bread a dripping Tuesday Aug 13.8.18 feeling a little better  getting a good dose of quinine Wednesday Aug.14.8.18 feeling better to day it is very hot here. Thursday Aug 15.8.18 feeling better to day. Friday Aug 16.8.18 felt a little dizzy in the head to day have been lying down all day.  was examined By a lady Doctor I don't know what the next generation will be like  she is a nice Dr dressed in military uniform all Pockets
