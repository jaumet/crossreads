<p>marched to camp : looked some of the boy's up : T Goodhead, Scotty, S Brown, Sid Moore, C. Lewis, also met W Barham &amp; Williams :</p>
<p>Friday June 15th</p>
<p>Bapaume : hot : reveille 6 am : early parade 6.30 : got wood for cooks during the day : pass to town after tea : been knocked about some : cemetery very nice &amp; plenty of soldiers buried there of both sides also Huns from 1870 war : got some flowers to send home :</p>
<p>Saturday June 16th</p>
<p>Bapaume : hot : reveille 6 am : early parade 6.30 : "wood" racket again : walked over &amp; seen Tim's grave &amp; it is to the Hun's credit that he has had a decent burial : the country here looks splendid just an occasional shell hole having</p>
