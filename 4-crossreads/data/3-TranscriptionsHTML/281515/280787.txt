<p>Jan 8th<br/>Big review ordered, we all parade and march up &amp; form up in quarter Column in marching order, but we only get liquid quinine &amp; march back again to quarters.  All previous arrangements re going to Sydney off, our stores given out we feed on Tin Herrings &amp; Biscuits no Tea sugar or flour.</p>
<p>Jan 9th<br/>more quinine, rumour that Berrima is coming here with reliefs, rained all afternoon.  Governor Hands over to Colonel Pethridge the new Governor,  Colonel Holmes <strike>Colonel</strike> goes on board the Eastern</p>
<p>Another big review, good bye speech by late Governor Cinemetagraph Picture man takes us on the move &amp; Giving a General Salute on arrival, our late Governor &amp; people say the salute was like one man.  Eastern sails away at 3 pm  Colonel Watson &amp; staff &amp; 250 men go on her, mostly sickly ones, we must be hard stuff keeping us to the last, some one played Home sweet Home as she was leaving &amp; that took some of the hardness out of us</p>
<p>Jan 10<br/>Merinda arrives at 3 am in the morning having passed the Eastern going home, got 2 letters</p>
<p>Jan 11<br/>Merinda goes to Herbertshohe for cargo (copra) &amp; comes back in 2 or three days time</p>
<p>Jan 12<br/>Oil Tank steamer arrives which is a sure sign that the destroyers are not far off rumour says we are to be escorted to Sydney by the destroyers Yarra, Parramatta, Warego</p>
