<p>1915<br/>Sep. 12 Sun.<br/>In Hospital.<br/>The Medical Officer of the Ward, Capt. Dyring &ndash; told me that I could get out of Hospital to-morrow.<br/>Mildred Brown came over this afternoon &amp; stayed at the Hosp. for dinner &ndash; I escorted her back to Choubra afterwards &amp; got roused on by the Night Sister in Ward 6 for being out after hours.</p>
