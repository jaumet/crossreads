We went to one shop in particular answering to the above description, &amp; run by a family of people who had lost their home owing to the war.  There were three  old  women, evidently sisters, a younger lady the daughter of one of them &amp; a husband, all Belgians.  The three elderly ladies run the refreshment side while the daughter speaks excellent English &amp; does all the work including a certain amount of cross talk with facetious soldiers while pa looks on &amp; smokes his pipe.
