<p>Well I must go &amp; find a spot on the deck to sleep. It would never do for Dad to be on this ship. I have never slept in the same place twice since I have been going on deck. Last night it looked like rain so I slept in the tween decks which is cooler than we are in the Hold. The place where our quarters are fixed is No 4 Cargo Hold.</p>
<p>Saturday 15.1.16 (24)<br/>Ellis &amp; I ran the ship this day, Ellis was Orderly Corporal for our Unit &amp;  was Orderly Corporal for the whole ship. I had a good job. I just see that the Bugler blows</p>
