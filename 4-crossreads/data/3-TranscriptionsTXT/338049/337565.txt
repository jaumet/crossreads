
Saturday 21 Dec.Up & away by 9 am train to Belfast. Nearly missed train owing to cab driver.
In Belfast 12 noon. Saw B. Had dinner. Went to Laws 3 p.m. Had afternoon tea - pleasant afternoon. Then to Stn to go to Glasgow. Farewelled by B. Good trip to Larne.
Very cold to Stranaer but sea calm. Arrived Glasgow 2 a.m. after sleepy warm trip. Bed reserved at Grand. OK.
