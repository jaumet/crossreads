<p>Turkish Delight over went the baskets. Most of the railway stations on the run to Cairo are small, some haven't any fencing round them while others are enclosed by wire. There are, however, a few very fine stations such as Zag-a-Zig, Tel-el-Kebir &amp; Benha. Cairo station is rather a fine one.
