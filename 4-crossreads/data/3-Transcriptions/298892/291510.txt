Letters take a much shorter time going from here than from Sydney. I hardly thought you had received my letter when the book arrived.<br/>I also received your wire to-day about Willie, &amp; Mrs. MacDermott. They both, together with Mary &amp; Dermaid, call on me at the Camp on Monday last. I was very surprised to see them here and was in much the same state as when you called at Liverpool but dinner was just over and not just beginning.<br/>I was informed by one of the men, last week, that some people who knew my people were anxious to see me.</p>
