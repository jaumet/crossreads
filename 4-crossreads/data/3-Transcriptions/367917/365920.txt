<p>Now that the war is over goodness only knows what will be done with us. I expect it will be a long wait with not much to do, until we are finally wound up as a going concern and sent home. Personally I should like to get a couple of months leave to England but that is not likely to come my way &ndash; I think first come first go is the fairest policy &amp; I expect I shall be amongst the last to leave this country. However that is all in the blue and a great way ahead.</p>
<p>At present I am living in a tent. Most of the Hdqrs are housed in the town. I was also for a week but the DDMS decided to put his office &amp; mess under canvas &amp; perforce I came too. Tents are very nice &ndash; compared with bivouacs but they're awful when people of the same unit are warm in</p>
