<p>A boat left yesterday &amp; one goes to-morrow from this depot - I was on both boat rolls &amp; for some unknown reason was taken off them - one gets used to these small things in the Army.<br/>With fondest love to self R &amp; B from your loving brother<br/>H.MA.S. Dunluce Castle<br/>6.5.19</p>
