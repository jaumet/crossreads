<p>[Thursday 2 November 1916]</p>
<p>(The following paragraphs are crossed out)<br/>Joes Birthday  Good luck to him<br/>Dolly left by 2 pm train for Brooklands Hotel as her Fathers home is not finished- Laura saw me off from Paddington to Devonport where I join my boat for Egypt<br/>Dolly is a good Soldiers wife-<br/>I am leaving my new Uniform Case with Mufti &amp; nice things with Dolly &amp; taking only service things with me to Egypt-<br/>I wonder how long it is all going to last.</p>
<p>Went on board at 9 o'clock &amp; all night long troops were stamping about <strike>(somewhere about 2000)</strike> odd details are on board, Scots, Welsh, Yeomanry, etc.</p>
