<p>5<br/>on several occasions to see her brother. She was so kind as to ask me to have lunch with them twice. First time we had it on the Rway platform &amp; last Sunday we had it on the grass in the Cemetery. There is no where else to go as girls Cant very well come into the Camp. We had quite a nice time &amp; on the last occasion Mrs Connor, (wife of Dr Connor) a very nice woman who used to live on the Richmond &amp; a girl named Hariett from Parkes, and the brother of Miss Hindmarsh several other soldiers &amp; I all sat down together &amp; formed quite a nice luncheon party.</p>
