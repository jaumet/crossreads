<p>24.9.16<br/>Sun 24th<br/>Had a very quiet day there was a Church parade this afternoon but did not go. leave has been stopped to Port Said with the exception of the men allowed to go in for a weeks spell at the Rest Camp &amp; these have to be recommended by the Dr.</p>
<p>25.9.16<br/>Mon 25th<br/>Was told confidentially this morning by Sutherland that the 1st L H Bde leave here Thursday morning for Hill 70 &amp; are going to Maadi;<br/>The Sergts mess is now going strong &amp; it is good to have nothing to do but stroll up to the Mess for meals</p>
