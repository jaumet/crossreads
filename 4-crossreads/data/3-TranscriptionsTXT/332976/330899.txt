The "Divisional Train" arrived in Corbie this morning &amp; passed through the same procedure as the three brigades.   General Birdwood also, was here to-day, but was unable to attend the inspection on the previous days. 21st   I am at Senlis now in the 8th Field Hospital &amp; the dentist has made a start on my teeth We had very heavy rain during the afternoon
