<p>No doubt you will all mist him but he is a boy  &amp;  it would be dreadful to have him girlie.</p>
<p>Annie, I should think must have a busy life with her step-family but still much better than her being at home working for everybody else.</p>
<p>I wonder if dear old Ed. got out to see you. He was awfully good to me  &amp;  we did so enjoy ourselves. I'm sorry he wont be</p>
