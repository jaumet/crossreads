<p>here I had to put my kahki much against my will over the counter to be stored until I was fit to come out   they gave me a suit of blues in return &amp; I was put to bed, feeling about the worst I have ever felt in my life, didn't care what happened.<br/>27th  Passed another wretched night no sleep, heavy sweats, bones aching head splitting, &amp; as weak as a kitten a pretty state of affairs, &amp; my chest &amp; throat are red raw plenty of blood comes up, they say I have this "dog disease" (Spanish flu) in a very bad form &amp; I believe them, this epidemic is raging throughout England &amp; it attacks anyone &amp; knocks you in a few minutes its that sudden,  I have eaten nothing all day only lay &amp; squirmed in my bed with the intense heat, &amp; they don't seem to</p>
