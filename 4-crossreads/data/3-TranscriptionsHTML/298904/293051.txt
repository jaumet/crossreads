<p>fight so as we could get a bit of our own back on them for say what you like they are Turks at heart, &amp; if it were possible would fight with them against us. I reckon we would dish that chicken hearted crowd up in no time. Nearly every lady in Cairo wears the veil even if they are not Mohammedans for it protects them from insult, there are quite a number of Turkish high class ladies there &amp; some of them are fine looking &amp; white as snow they all have splendid covered in carriages, &amp; keep pretty well to themselves, all the high class ladies wear a white veil which extends from the bridge of the nose</p>
