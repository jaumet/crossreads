<p>But what an ungrateful beast I am &ndash;<br/>For there's always plenty of bread and jam<br/>Which is probably lemon and melon &ndash; Damn.<br/>That's the Army's way of saying "Ah, me!"<br/>But, as nobody quite knows who's to blame,<br/>We jog along happily all the same;<br/>And I think we're all of us glad we came<br/>To join Australia's Army.</p>
