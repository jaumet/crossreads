<p>[Saturday 18 November 1916]</p>
<p>Off the coast of Algeria again, quite hot did some big gun practice with our 4.7 at the stern of the boat.<br/>Understand we are to go to Salonika first, this will be interesting-<br/>Must be many letters waiting for me in Egypt.<br/>Am getting fat &amp; soft on board altho' there is the usual Gymnasium of mechanical riding Horses etc etc:-<br/>The poor Tommies get little room for exercise.  I told you we had many different Regiments aboard.  One Scotty Officer at Gibraltar when I admired his Kilts offered to lend them to me to be photographed in- he said my cost 5/-/- with Scotch</p>
