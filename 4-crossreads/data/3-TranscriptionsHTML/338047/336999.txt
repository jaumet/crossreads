<p>1919<br/>Apl 13<br/>Pont Alexandre.  We went down the river as far as the junction of the Marne.  We disembarked at St Cloud &amp; had our photos taken at the Cascades.  On special days the water is turned on &amp; fountains &amp; waterfalls make a great show.  We climbed to the top of the hill &amp; had a splendid view of Paris.  Just as we reached the top the sun came out &amp; shone on the church de Sacre Coeur.  It looked very beautiful in the distance.  We then returned to the boat &amp; came back to Paris.  This trip was specially arranged for soldiers, as the river fleet was not running at this time, the boats being all tied up &amp; rusting at their anchors.  At night we went to the Olympic theatre &ndash; a variety show.  We made up our minds we would not be caught this time by the local sharps, so I bought the tickets for the promenoir.
