<p>April 1915<br/>1 Thursday<br/>Saw B Sqn AHQ swim their horses<br/>Gardens very full in afternoon had a quiet loaf about and took some snaps<br/>C Sqn arrived</p>
<p>2 Fri<br/>Rode back with Clark, on Merrington's horse, from Barrage &ndash; arrived 1.30 pm road all had cultivation as far as Shukra. Took photo of village St and sent home.<br/>The big riot in the Wazh-el-Birka from 4.30 to 9.30 PM</p>
