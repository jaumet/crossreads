<p>last hospital.</p>
<p>Saturday 19  Sunday 20.1.18<br/>Very quiet in hospital, no amusements of any kind, time drags awfully.</p>
<p>Monday 21.1.18<br/>No food in preparation for operation "no bon"  Put under anaesthetic about 10.45 &amp; had part of my left little toe cut away.  Felt very queer after coming around. &amp; for rest of the day.  Food awful.  Given cold piece of tough meat &amp; vegetable about 1&frac12; after coming to, asked for drink of milk, none to be had was given hot water which I refused to drink with thanks.  Foot very sore to-night.</p>
