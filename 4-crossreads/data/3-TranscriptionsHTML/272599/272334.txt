<p>March Tuesday 6 1917</p>
<p>No further word has come as to when we are to go out. All the 2nd &amp; 3rd bearers have gone, but the 1st have been temporarily attached to the 2nd Div.<br/>Stayed in bunk nearly all day nothing else to do. <br/>The battery overhead pulled out tonight so we'll have a bit of quietness</p>
