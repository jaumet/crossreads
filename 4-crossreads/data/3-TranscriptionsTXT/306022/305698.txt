
Monday 2ndGot our first war news from Mauritius, which is cheering us up a bit. The British are still doing well.
Saw more flying fish.
Tuesday 3rdThe usual dreary day's doings, though livened up a bit in the afternoon by some good boxing events, our fellows all doing well.
Wednesday 4thBoat going along very steadily. Water just like glass though a nasty sort of day.
