WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 17, 1919.
Fine & warm. Linda & I went for
a ride round Ben Ledi & Seven Star
& 4D in morning. About the
house in afternoon & we all went
into Quirindi (in ( the two cars)
for dinner at night. Went to the
pictures afterwards. Linda & Myee
& Graham caught the 9.30 train
to Sydney. We came back
after.
