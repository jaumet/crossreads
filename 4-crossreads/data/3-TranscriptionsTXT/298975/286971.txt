
Friday 2nd Nov 1917Working Party. Did my first bit of Sandbag work They make such a neat job
Saturday 3rdWorking Party Went to Warminster in evening what a dull place Weight 11st 3lbs
Sunday 4thAs usual. Attended evening service at the Home Hut Miss Osbourne spoke A splendid little sermon
Monday 5thSame old Working party same old work
Tuesday 6thHard job finding work on the working party. Have a very good little mate on the job an old hand. He is no loafer like so many are. His brother is a VC man & he did not tell about it untill we had been 2 weeks together
