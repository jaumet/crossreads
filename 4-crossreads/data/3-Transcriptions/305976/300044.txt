<p>Thursday Sept 19<br/>Left Jerusalem Rest camp for Jericho. Arrived about 11 AM &amp; camped near dump until horses arrived. We had the wind up today. They started shelling. We were camped next to ammunition dump &amp; this is evidently what they were after. Every minute we expected the lot to go up in the air but once again we were lucky.<br/>Friday Sept 20<br/>Our Horses came today &amp; I rejoined our unit.
