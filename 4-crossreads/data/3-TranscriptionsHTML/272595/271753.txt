I am going to write &amp; tell his mother for Bill has a job to write at all, after leaving Bill I went back to London &amp; went to the Criterion to see "You never know you know" its rather funny &amp; I enjoyed myself.<br/>17th.  This morning I put in several hours down at Horsferry Rd enquiring about different chaps &amp; etc. I found out about Harry Kelly &amp; was personally speaking to an officer who was a prisoner of war with him, according to reports he is in pretty good health is not likely to leave Holland until after the war is over, they also reckon there that this 1914 stunt is a dead cert so you can guess how delighted I am over the fact, I have been certain these last 5 months as this diary can prove that I</p>
