The sculpture of Animal forms is perfect and seems to have been developed to a perfection and strength that is quite as fine as any modern work. You will know what joy it gave me seeing these beautiful things. Of course I shall go often - and hope to be able to tell all about them to my small nephews & nieces when I go home again. 22. Feb 1916 Yesterday I went out to No 1 Aust Gen Hospital at Gesireh Palace Hotel. We drove there - crossing the Nile over a wonderful bridge with huge lions on each corner - made of bronze I think. Our soldiers stand on picket duty at each end. The Gesireh Palace is in a lovely garden on the banks of the Nile. We first asked for Colonel Dick. He was up - in his dressing gown.
