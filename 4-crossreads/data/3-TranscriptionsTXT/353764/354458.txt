At this juncture some Australian Light Horse arrived on the scene & gave pursuit inflicting many Casualties on them.The Royal Flying Squadron then took a turn (8 machines participating) & dropped 60 bombs each on his camp which was utterly demolished. Altogether out of an attacking Force of 2500 the enemy lost 500 killed 80 prisoners & a large amount of Ammunition together with a few Machine Guns.I do not know the exact Casualties on our side but they were heavy.  I am afraid if they keep this going that we will not be allowed to leave here, which is just what the enemy wants.
29.4.16 SaturdayAnother rather monotonous day in which I never went out of Camp.
