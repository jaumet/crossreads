<p>Tuesday 25 December</p>
<p>Only one run today &ndash; To A.D.M.S. at Samer.<br/>Christmas Dinner in the big tents passed off A1.  Plum Pudding turned up alright too.<br/>Concert in the big tents from 5 to 7<br/>Afterwards my friend Fred Williams and I went for a stroll<br/>It was as clear as daylight tonight and occasional snowstorms fell.<br/>The usual "matter of form" greetings arrived from Douglas Haig Birdie etc.</p>
