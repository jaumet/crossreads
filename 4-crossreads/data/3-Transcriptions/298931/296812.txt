<p>Monday 20<br/>Once more with the Battalion this time in the line, in front of Bapume. A sea of M.U.D<br/>Tuesday 21<br/>Being releived tonight by the 5th division half of the batt are away in hospital<br/>Wednesday 22<br/>The trenches and road's are what we Australians term "A fair bastard" Now at Ribemont<br/>Thursday 23<br/>Last night I had the first genuine meal after ten days of bully &amp; biscuits</p>
