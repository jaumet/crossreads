 November 1917 Thursday 29 At Enab with 60th Div on hill over 2000 ft above sea level. Quiet day. Very cold night. Dull morning Taube flew over and sent our observation balloon down with machine gun fire. Observers landed in parachutes. 
 Friday 30 Went into village Enab bought grape jam. Village fairly decent different from Egyptian villages. Quiet day.
