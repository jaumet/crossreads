<p>Pyramides Hotel &amp;c.  The main street Rue de San Stefano contains the chief business houses run by Egyptians, Frenchmen, Greeks, Armenians &amp;c.  They all ply their trade in the street to the cry of "Verra goot, verra nice" &amp; are a constant source of worry &amp; trouble.  But it is the boot-black who will worry one especially if he happens to notice &amp; he of course invariably does &ndash; that your boots are dirty &ndash; as they, also invariably are.</p>
