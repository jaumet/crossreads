Nothing further happened en route, the weather became fair and on</p>
<p>Oct 14th  6 AM we passed thru Sydney Heads once again,-  we tied to a buoy in Double Bay and ere long a coal hulk was place alongside of us and we again completed with coal after which we again proceeded to anchor off Farm Cove where we leisurely remained until</p>
<p>Oct 17th when we were very hurriedly ordered to prepare for sea and consequently at 2 PM we surrepticiously departed steaming South &ndash; we steamed very slowly about 5 knots and "Argyleshire" troopship which left Sydney after us passed us on &ndash;</p>
