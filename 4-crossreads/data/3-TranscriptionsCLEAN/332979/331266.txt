 Monday 5/3/17. The Weather still being hot & close although now & again we have a good shower of rain still it is very close, down below we we have our meals it is very stuffy, although we have two Wind Sails which come down below. I am very fortunate as I took up a position on the floor to sleep & have slept there since leaving Fremantle & as one of the Windsails is directly over my head I have it very cool all night. As there is still a doubt when we will cross the Equator I believe we must be almost on the Line.
