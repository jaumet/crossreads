  
 [Postcard - drawing of a wreath of mauve flowers with a German message:] Gruss aus dem Felde [Greetings from the Field] Res. Feld. Artl. Regt 29 
 An die Heimat muss ich daenken Stets im wildem Kreigsgebraus. Gern Mocht ich die Schritte lenken Nach dem teuren Vaterhaus, Deutsche Heimat, sei gegrusst, In der Ferne sei gegrusst, :.: Sei gegrusst in weiter Ferne, Deutsche Heimat, sei gegrusst! ;.; 
 [Translated I think roughly as follows: 
 Of the homeland I must think always in the wild brew of battle Glad I'd be to take the steps To the dear Father's home German homeland I greet you ;.; I greet you in the far distance German Homeland, I greet you;.;]
