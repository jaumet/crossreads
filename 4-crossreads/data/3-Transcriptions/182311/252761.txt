<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; Then the Regal Party were ducked and everyong was wet and joyful.&nbsp; The whole thing was a great success, exciting continuous roars of laughter from our 1,500 audience and was said by the Skipper to be the best show ever made on the ship.&quot;(By the way, we earned another superlative on the previous night, that of being &quot;the wildest lot of officers they had carried&quot;).&nbsp; We then left the show to the men and almost every man on the ship was dicked, it was very funny and there were only three minor accidents.&nbsp; I gave my camera to a soldier and posted him in a very good position.&nbsp; I expect that the results will be good, I hope so.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; Today, yesterday, and even last evening were distressingly hot but we are running away from that weather now and look for cooler regions soon, although &#39;tis summer at present up North.&nbsp; For one day I was off colour and life seemed not worth while, but only for one day, and now I&#39;m my old smiling self.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; Last Sunday we issued a newspaper, &quot;The Sunday Times&quot; only a limited edition issued and only available to contributors.&nbsp; I am sending my copy for you to see how although there are only a dozen officers out of our forty mentioned, I&#39;m there in several places, for I&#39;ve/kept myself well to the front.</p>
