 7/10/16  Saturday 
 Dull day &ndash; On Picquet. Fritz hopped the parapet at Levantie &amp; Armentiers &ndash; he got particular hell in both places &ndash; Very few casualtys came in. 
 8/10/16  Sunday 
 Dull day &ndash; Went out to Rifle Villa about 9 a.m. Lively trench warfare &ndash; Had a few calls. 
 9/10/16  Monday 
 Drizzling rain. Heavy bombardment from both sides &ndash; Got a letter from M. Single. Fritz very busy with deadly minniewurzen, he got some M.G. fellows &ndash; one fellow named Jim Harsthorn, although badly injured injured, was a model patient, he was actually singing &amp; joking &ndash; as he said to keep himself awake. Les Bowden took on Groom to A.D.M.S. 
 10/10/16  Tuesday 
 Nice day &ndash; A few calls during the night &ndash; Fritz putting over more minnies than shells on supports &ndash; Wrote to Frank, Anne &amp; M. Single.
