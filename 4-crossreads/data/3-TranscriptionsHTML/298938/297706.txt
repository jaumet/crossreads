<p>night on duty in Hut 40.  Last night we only had four new arrivals &amp; tonight about the same will come I was told.<br/>Was told by the Corporal in change of the dispensary, (<u>not</u> the Corp. dispenser) that I start there next week.<br/>It should be a change for the better.<br/>Am now going to write a letter to dear old Rita.</p>
