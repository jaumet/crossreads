The show lasted from 9 till midnight &amp; a special tram was run to enable all to get back to camp<br/>Having glorious weather here with plenty of sun every day which ripens the cherries very quickly. We have several fruit laden trees in the camp grounds but I don't think the fruit will be able to last forever &ndash; not at the rate it is going.<br/>2000 British &amp; colonial troops have just arrived in camp here where they are isolated. They have just come off transports on which have been various outbreaks of infection.<br/>Monday 22nd May:- Yesterday was the day of my visit to Nice &amp; Monte Carlo. (Who said Australians aren't tourists?) Up at 4.30 a.m. &amp; caught the 5 to 6 tram from  Bonneveino, leaving Marseille Station (Gare St Charles) at 7.20 by the Paris express</p>
