
<p>245</p>
<p>JANUARY 1917</p>
<p>11 THURSDAY</p>
<p>Cold &amp; dull with light snow falling</p>
<p>all day. Grooming all our new</p>
<p>grey horses all the morning</p>
<p>and we went to a gas</p>
<p>lecture in the afternoon, &amp; cleaning</p>
<p>harness when we came back.</p>
<p>Got a lot of parcels ect in</p>
<p>the evening, but I got none</p>
<p>except a letter from mother</p>
