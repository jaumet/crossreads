FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 12, 1919.
Fine. Barry & Al & I & Graham went
down to rocks early after
foxes. I got one, & it was the only
one got. The girls brought our
lunch down & we had it in the
mountain. Tickle lopped in
Top paddock
Barnes came out & brought Darby's
tools and 1 ton of chaff for me.
