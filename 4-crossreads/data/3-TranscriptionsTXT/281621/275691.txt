Sunday 25Beautiful day. Like last Sunday only spent under different circumstances.
Monday 26Raining, snowing &amp; windy. In village of Buire met a Parisian girl who knew Brighton &amp; Hove well. There on holidays.
Wednesday 28Anniversary of our arrival in France. Celebrate it with a bottle of Haumont &amp; drink to "next year at home". Unsettled weather.
Friday 30Very unsettled weather. A strong rumour hath that "B" Section is to go to form part of a new ambulance in the new 6th Division but no confirmation.
Saturday 31The advance generally continues &amp; now the line must be 20 miles away.
