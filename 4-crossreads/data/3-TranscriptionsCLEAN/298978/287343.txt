 Apr; 16 Practising signalling - I feel very anxious as I do not think I am at all efficient. 
 Apr; 17 Ditto. 
 Apr; 18 Landed at Lemnos, a very picturesque island. I have made several studies - it is great to be once more amongst green meadows and to hear the cow bells jingling. All is very fine colour. 
 Apr; 19 Manoeuvres on the Island. 
 Apr; 20 Strudies.We are not permitrted to take more than a bottle full of water a day, there is not enough for washing with - the sea is all around us, yet we are not allowed to bathe, the whole thing is damn rot. 
 Apr; 21 A deal of wind; very cold on deck, where I have been sleeping as there is so little room on the ship and the atmosphere in the hold is appalling - I also eat all my meals on deck, such as they are; bully beef, biscuits, Dutch cheese, Jam and occasionally ham; also at times we had some weird concoction consisting of biscuits and bully beef formed into a kind of stew. Take the Colonel in one of the ship's boats to a Council of War. Midday, a concert. Studies.
