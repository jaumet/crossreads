<p>I have come across for a considerable time, where my acquired tact has failed me. I have been unable to cope with the difficulty, chiefly, I suppose, because I was not humble enough to avoid treading on his corns &amp; wounding his tremendous self pride. Perhaps by working myself into his affections I could have done more.</p>
