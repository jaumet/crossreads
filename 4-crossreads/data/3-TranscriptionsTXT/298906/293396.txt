After this we marched home unfixing bayonets while on the march. Just before dinner today I gave Depina a nasty bang, split his lip badly, it was done accidently while sparring &amp; fooling about with the gloves poor Dip" had to go down to the field Ambulance &amp; get it stitched up, I was sorry for it must be painful but the occurence was purely an accident.
