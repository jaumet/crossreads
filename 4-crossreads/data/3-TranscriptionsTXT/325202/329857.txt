 We left Bazentin early Friday morning &amp; after about a mile  joined  got on the main Albert-Bapaume road &amp; joined the column of traffic passing through Le Sars &amp; Warlencourt at the latter place the Germans had blown up a mine in the middle of the road &amp; so the track had been taken round it. Seemed a waste of labour as it was an easy matter to run the road run &amp; could hardly delay our troops. 
 Halted on the outskirts of Bapaume for dinner
