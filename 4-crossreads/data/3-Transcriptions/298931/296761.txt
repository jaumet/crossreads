<p>Friday 26<br/>Marsailles, looks alright, we are just going in time 4.30 A M<br/>Very glad to be here for I am not to kean of the water, Balldale, 3 months ago<br/>Saturday 27<br/>France beautiful France<br/>I am now travelling through some of the most magnificent country imaginable tell you all about it S.O.D<br/>Sunday 28<br/>God guide me home, to tell my loved ones of this trip, still in the train bound for Paris &amp; then Bolounge<br/>The French girls are like our own pretty sweet good sociable and they like the boys</p>
