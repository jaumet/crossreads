 Sunday 20/2/16 
 Very nice day &amp; very calm. Had a lovely sleep in our new home, Electric Fan, etc. Very quite on board. Great anticipation about going ashore Colombo tomorrow, expect to arrive 9 am. All things being equal &ndash; Drs inspections &ndash; authoritys to land etc. A party of 20 are committed to my charge, while on shore. 
 Monday 21/2/16 
 Very hot all day &ndash; arrived in Colombo 8 am. We were just through the fine breakwaters when we heard the bum boats cry of Di Di Di &amp; Ta-a-ra-ra Boom De Ay &ndash; etc &ndash; all li all li all li. Dozens of the boats offering pineapples &ndash; Bananas., the pines were  English  variety but of poor size &amp; not nearly the same quality or taste of our own Ripley Queens, the Bananas
