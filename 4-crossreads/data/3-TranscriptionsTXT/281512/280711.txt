The enemy are now on the slope of the ridge immediately ahead &amp; we look down on him.  About 3,000 prisoners were captured &amp; several machine guns. Our casualties regrettably were higher than usual.  The battle was fought in a misty rain - heavier falls having taken place overnight, so that the entire battlefield was a great quagmire of mud. It's marvellous what conditions our fellows can &amp; will fight under. There are no troops in the whole fighting force equal to the Australians for storming troops.
