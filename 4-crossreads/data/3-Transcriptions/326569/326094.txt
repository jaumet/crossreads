<p>Contd.</p>
<p>After<br/>1.  Unload &amp; press trig. to ease return spring<br/>2.  Oil bore<br/>3.  On return to billets &ndash; strip gun &amp; clean thoroughly<br/>4.  Carry out any necessary repairs<br/>5.  Wash, dry &amp; oil dirty mags<br/>6.  Examine barrel for metallic fouling &amp; remove same<br/>7.  Lower tension of return spring to zero</p>
<p>Duties of gun crew</p>
<p>Duties of No. 1<br/>1.  Carry gun with tripod attached &amp; spare parts bag into action<br/>2.  Fires in accordance with orders &amp; repeats all commands of NCO in charge.</p>
