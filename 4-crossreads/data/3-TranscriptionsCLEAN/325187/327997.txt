
[Monday 11 September 1916]
Dolly & her Mother came tonight, they want me to go to St Annes as soon as well & are willing for me to be married soon!!!  I am sorry Laura is not in town told Mrs Cotton what was going to happen.Medical Board this afternoon recommended me 6 weeks sick furlough, but don't know from what date.Cold is much better & I had tea in town with Dorothy   All London is streaked at night with search lights above, but the streets are dark.  Expensive place London, everything has increased in price tremendously.
