<p>October Friday 12 1917</p>
<p>Wind &amp; rain.<br/>Billy Leeming arrived at hospital in I ward.   Shrap. Wound thro. Arm into abdominal wall, not penetrating fortunately.   He is doing well.<br/>Lightowler very much worse.   He will not last long I'm afraid.<br/>Make final preparations for my leave.   Cartwright arrives back from his.</p>
