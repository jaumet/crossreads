<p>5th<br/>Transferred from Ghezireh to Mena House Hospital, situated about 200 yards from the Pyramids.  Mosquitoes &amp; flies galore.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Took Sergeant Smith to Cairo.  Saw a wedding procession, headed by an Egyptian brass band, &amp; also another procession in honour of a woman who was coming home from hospital, cured.  She had two bands.</p>
<p>8th<br/>Went for a walk up to the Pyramids, &amp; got some alabaster.</p>
<p>9th<br/>Went to zoo with McCourt.  Had refreshments at "Island of Tea".</p>
<p>10th<br/>Went to Ma'adi.  The camp is greatly altered, the horses now having shelter sheds.  My old horse in great condition.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Went to dentist, and afterwards had a walk round cotton fields.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Had teeth patched up temporarily.</p>
<p>15th<br/>The hospital grounds all lit up at night, and decorated with flags, for a fine concert</p>
