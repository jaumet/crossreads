 27.2.15 
 Sat 27th Had to clean our equipment this morning &amp; pulled our tents down to air the ground &amp; everything was in a right mess at dinner time, as a breeze was blowing &amp; the dust horrible; we put the tents up at dinner time &amp; I received a pass out for the afternoon, went to the Soldiers Home &amp; put the afternoon in writing, had tea &amp; hunted the town for Kiwi polish, at last found it &amp; bough 6 tins for 6/-.
