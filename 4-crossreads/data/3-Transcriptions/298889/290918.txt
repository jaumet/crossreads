<p>Left Afion Kara Hissar on the 5th of Nov at 5 p.m. with officers<br/>Arrived at Ouchak at 5 a.m. on the 6th and left again same day at 4 p.m.<br/>Arrived at Smyrna on the 7th at noon and put up at American School at Paradise to await for ship coming in to take passage to England</p>
