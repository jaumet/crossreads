<p>March 1918</p>
<p>Messines<br/>C.R.A. and col paid a visit to Bty position, and inspected our pit.<br/>Fritz threw a few about, and sent a few waves of chlorine  down about the Kemmel end of the line. Met Donnellan, Morell and a few more of the boys in the 30th on the duckboards.<br/>Weather beautiful.<br/>Met Dick Pernell, at P.gun, believe he is going to Blighty on tour with 5th Aus Div concert party. He looks jolly well. Had a decent feed at Pip with Reuss, Ivan, and Ken Brown and Corp Moncur from 51st came across on a visit.</p>
