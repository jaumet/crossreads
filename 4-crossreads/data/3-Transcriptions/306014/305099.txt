<p>At Salisbury the weather was simply perfect &amp; the sun was shining brightly &amp; we seemed in quite a new clime; you may therefore guess how surprised we were upon finding the the temperature in the middle of the afternoon was 4.6&deg;F. Goodness only knows what it must be at "Rollestone" at times.</p>
