SATURDAY, AUGUST 2, 1919.
Fine. Round the bottom end with
Alan in morning. We lifted the pipe
in Middle paddock bore with a jack.
Darby down about 125 ft in blue
rock.
Al & I & Pete went over to
Coomoo Coomoo in evening. The
employees gave Clyde a bit of
a Social evening there & we had a
fine time.
