
10 - friend Watt who were getting commissions in Scotish regiment asked if they could postpone commission till after attack on Dardanelles. Gibson hid his bad eye which has white scar on eyeball. Gibson broke out in measles day troops sailed & Watt was killed. The Dr discovered Gibson's eye which of course became worse without care etc and he helped to nurse his [indecipherable] regiment on their return to hospitals. All his kit is in Alexandria. He was put on a hospital ship to clean base hospital and discovered Fred Addison with his shattered leg.I found Gibson in a cold dismal auxiliary ward, also stone, attached to R. Eye Hosp
