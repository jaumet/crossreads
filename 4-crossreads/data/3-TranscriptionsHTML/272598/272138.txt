<p>Monday 11.12.16<br/>Off duty.  Met H. Mortimore &amp; spend the evening with him talking of old times.  Raining.</p>
<p>Tuesday 12.12.16<br/>Snowing &amp; raining.  Much against wood gathering in Bernafoy Wood the job which I had to supervise.</p>
<p>Wednesday 13.12.16<br/>Day free from duty.  Called out at 5.30 pm to place a man under arrest &amp; take him to A.D.S.  H Mortimore came to Camp &amp; spent a couple of hours.  Men coming out of the line in an awful state with the mud.</p>
<p>Thursday 14.12.16<br/>Reported to A.D.S. to give evidence re last nights trouble &amp; again in afternoon.  New Sargeant appointed in Sgt Thompsons stead, evacuate , again</p>
