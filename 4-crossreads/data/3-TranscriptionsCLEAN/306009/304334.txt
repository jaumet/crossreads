  
 1915 May. 28 Fri Another mighty warm day to-day - must have been fully 110 but Cairo heat is very dry & therefore bearable. Another chap - Birch Roberts - came into the Office to-day from Mena- now 3 of us. Our daily state shows 434 in Hospital. Egypt contains a wonderful variety of nationalities & many people can speak English, French, Arabic, Greek, & Italian which are the languages mostly in use.
