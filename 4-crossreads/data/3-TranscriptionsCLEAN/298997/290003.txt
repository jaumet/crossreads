 Jan 2 1918 We are now in "the line" our outpost being a post known as 5011.  It is merely a semi-circular series of mounds, with really no protection at the back.  I have not been able to analyse what it had been, because I am only in this position in the night time.  At night all but two of us move back to a pillbox.  The strength of these places is enormous.
