<p>16th<br/>Passed the Leeuwin Light house at 9 a.m.  The boat rolling a good deal.  At 6 p.m. we passed Albany, and met a troopship A49, heading towards Fremantle.  Raining all day.</p>
<p>17th<br/>Boat rolling badly.  Plenty more broken crockery.  A lot of albatrosses flying about following the ship.  Crossing the Bight.</p>
<p>19th<br/>Met a large mail steamer at 4 p.m.  Looked like a P. &amp; O. boat.  Trooper Kerr, of 8th Light Horse, Victoria, died after an operation, and was buried at sea at 10.30 p.m.  The bugle sounded the "Last Post".  Raining.</p>
