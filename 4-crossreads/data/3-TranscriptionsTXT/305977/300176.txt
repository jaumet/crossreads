Monday 15th [Feb]Got back the the old troop drill on foot, sections right & sections left.  Capt White gave us some instructions in the use of the rifle.  Got our saddles out after dinner & cleaned our rifles.  Went on stable picket at 6pm.  Had a quiet night.  Horses gave no trouble which was just as well, as I wasn't feeling too good.  Had an attack of indigestion.
