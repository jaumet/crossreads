
Saturday 14 April 1917.
Stayed Federal Coffee Palace on Fri eveng
Up at 7.30 & after a time out to Burnley.
In city on Sat morning.
Out to Toorak in afternoon
Met Joy at 5.50 in city & after tea went to "Canary Cottage" at "Her Majesty's theatre". (Joy lost her purse.)
Bed at 12.30 am. at Burnley
Most enjoyable evening.
