<p><u>Diary while in France.</u></p>
<p>Mar. 20th.<br/>Left vessel &amp; marched to our encampment- Site of Indian G.H- originally a German residence- fine grounds with abundance of fruit trees &amp; luxuriant vegetation- great contrast with Egypt.<br/>Mar.21st.<br/>Bully-beef &amp; biscuits- Inspection by a French General- Break camp at Marseilles- Baggage guard.  Leave by train for unknown destination.  Sleep. (or at least try to sleep) on train.</p>
