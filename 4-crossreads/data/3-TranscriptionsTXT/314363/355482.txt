 May 22nd 18 Near Daurs. We are getting some very hot weather real Australian, a lot of the boys have been going over to the canal for a swim, have been there twice. Being moonlight at night we get a good many bombs round about us, but so far they don't appear to have done much damage. 23.5.18 Sudden change in the weather today, very cold wind 24./5.18 Very cold today &amp; raining Sunday, May 26th 18 Beautiful day today. Am having a very quiet time, am still in details. May 27th.18 Detail Camp. Beautiful day again. Last night we had enemy aeroplanes over Dropping bombs very near, expect him over again tonight. This afternoon I went for a walk over to a neighbouring village, on the way back watched for a while, some of the 3rd Div. having sports on the river (Somme). May 29th. Details Yesterday I received some Australian mail, two from Louie, four from home one from Kallard &amp; an English one. Last night the bn. went into the line, relieving the 5 on the left of old posie.
