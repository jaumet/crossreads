<p>No further mention made about a transfer &amp; relations between Dr. Scrimgeour &amp; myself continue in the same state.  X<br/>Feel fit, but for the foot, which is extremely painful.<br/>We ordelies <strike>see</strike> rub along fairly well with not more than one brush a day, which is not bad considering.<br/>Letters slow in turning up again.  Am anxiously waiting for Rita's photograph.<br/>In regard to the war it seems from the news in the papers that we are in for a long stay before finally going home.<br/>The homers will be lucky.</p>
