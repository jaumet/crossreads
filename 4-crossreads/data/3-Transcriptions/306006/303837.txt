<p>21-4-17<br/>This afternoon Hop James Troop has been having some fun with Turkish Cavalry; taking it turn about chasing each other trying to draw each other within range of machine guns some shots were exchanged during the afternoon &amp; several enemy saddles were emptied &amp; prisoners taken. Taubes were very active all day &amp; at 4 PM 3 of ours came out bombing &amp; only two returned apparently he was brought down as we could see a big fire some miles away just as the other two returned. The pilot must have fired it after falling in the enemy lines she was a Martinsyde (one of our best) We were relieved by the 22nd MW Bde just before sundown &amp; watered our</p>
