
<p>367</p>
<p>MAY 1917</p>
<p>13 SUNDAY</p>
<p>Fine but came on to rain at</p>
<p>night. Cleaning harness all</p>
<p>morning &amp; we had a half</p>
<p>holiday in afternoon. I went</p>
<p>up to Erquingham in evening</p>
<p>to get my watch &amp; got it.</p>
<p>Fritz sent 5 eight mil.</p>
<p>shells into a farm house about</p>
<p>half a mile from our waggon</p>
<p>lines.</p>
