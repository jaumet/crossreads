<p>Out with Sarre, Stott who was ill and came in early and Hosking.</p>
<p>15.3.18<br/>Stayed in bed all day but started after breakfast and read and wrote a good deal.  One letter to Melv.  Out at 12 with Sarre, Clow &amp; Hosking.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>Fairly lively going up and also some minnies and M.G.  Couldn't get a party after waiting a while at dump so worked on our own and dug a small trench to pill box and filled in a possy.  Rest of evening quiet and stopped at 10.30 after starting at 7.30.  Took out ten men and spent a quiet evening on two possys although other shift was fairly rough and home at 4.</p>
<p>No party for us and we were supposed to use garrison.  However, no garrison.  Sarre &amp; Hosking went out to platoon headquarters for awhile to fix up a pump and on return we did a little camouflaging for Jensen and came home at 3.  T.M. [trench mortar] barrage.</p>
<p>16.3.18<br/>Slept all day and going out at six.  Went out but couldn't get a party so went up and at 7.30 started on trench to pill box and after filled in a possy.  Stopped at 10.30.  Fairly lively going up and quiet afterwards.  Home to bed.</p>
