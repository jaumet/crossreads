<p>Napoleonic exhibit is however most  instructive  &amp;  includes the famous Waterloo carriage with its effects  various sets of clothing  &amp;  accoutrements, table services, besides hundreds of other items of interest. The various Tableaux are very fine  &amp;  include the Murder of the Princes, Death of Nelson, Wellington at Tomb of Nap, Cinderella Babes in Wood  &amp;  Jack the Giant Killer. <br/>We Left London on the 14th June the day of the big air raid. As we lay in Folkstone we could hear detonations to the south.<br/>Arrived back with the Batn at BuIre on 15th June</p>
