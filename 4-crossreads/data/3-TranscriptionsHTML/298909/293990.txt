<p>7/6/16  Wednesday</p>
<p>Not so hot &ndash; but still very dusty &ndash; On Guard &ndash; We got news &ndash; that we would probably have to stay here till about the 15th inst. We got paid &ndash; but instead of getting what was due about 250 pt we only got 50 pt, they also advise that we will only draw (most probably) 1/- per day in future &ndash; the Officers tell us that they are cut down by one third also.</p>
<p>Last night the lads wrecked all the Canteen &amp; went mad generally &ndash; I laughed to see them cooking their own eggs etc at the Gypo Canteen &ndash; they pinched all fancy goods &ndash; to day their was a surprise Kit inspection to try &amp; locate some of the goods they got nothing in our lines, they were too slow. 8/6/16  Thursday</p>
<p>Had the day off &ndash; Rather a pleasant day. Went for a swim &ndash; while marching along we noticed a despatch rider coming along the sand at the water's edge &amp; next we saw that the horse had shied &amp; both horse &amp; rider were in deep water &ndash; Major Moseley &amp; Capt</p>
