The trees have all lost their leaves &amp; it does not look its best. I have a very nice room &amp; the owner is very hospitableIt is a great change after the noise of war no sound of it ever reaches us. The position is South of Boulogne &amp; between it &amp; Etaples about 43 miles from Etaples off the railway &amp; about 6 miles from Samer.
Tuesday 20thWent round the grounds for a walk this morning &amp; then wrote letters
Wed 21stHave not been out. British made a push near St Quentin  Successful
