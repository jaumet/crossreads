<p>9<br/>Now something more of London.:-<br/>I am standing on the paved open space in front of St Pauls<br/>City people and a few touring soldiers are round me, all interested in the fearless pigeons there.<br/>It has become quite a custom with many people to feed these birds.<br/>I borrow some indian corn from a person near me, and the birds flutter to my hats and shoulders and arms.<br/>They feed out of my hands. There are two or three hundred of them altogether amongst the people<br/>'just anybodys birds I suppose" I ask of the colourless old man near by</p>
