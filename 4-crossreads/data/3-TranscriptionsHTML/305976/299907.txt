<p>Thursday Aug 16th<br/>Camels &amp; equipment handed in today. Moving on Saturday<br/>Friday Aug 17<br/>Packing up leaving early tomorrow.<br/>Saturday Aug 18<br/>Reveille 3.30 AM. Told off for baggage Guard stations. Main body left at 7pm. I. J.B. &amp; Cpl M leaving by luggage train at midnight. We placed all gear in truck sealed it &amp; spent a wild day in Cairo. Who knows we may not see it again. A small half starved little dog followed us about all day so we took him on the strength &amp; took him with us.</p>
