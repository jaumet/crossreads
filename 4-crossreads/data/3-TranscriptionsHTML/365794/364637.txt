<p>Friday 24 [23] August 1917 [1918]<br/>Went Cairo 4.30 p.m. tram, met Mrs. C. Harris.  She does not look very happy but evidently is on honeymoon but is over &amp; hubby up the line one should not wonder.<br/>27 pts. in Officers ward &amp; very light.</p>
