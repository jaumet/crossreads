 1916 June 14 Wednesday 
 Letter from Mary No 3. dated 16th Apr. giving later news. Go for route march thro' Etaples &amp;amp halfway to Paris Plage,  cold. Big Serg. &amp;amp quick walker. Inspection parade by O.C. Several chaps crimed for not shaving.  Afternoons nothing doing. not usual. more at night Time put on hour. Summer time daylight saving Made marker. exempt from fatigues
