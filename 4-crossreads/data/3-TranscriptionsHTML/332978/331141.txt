<p>62 September 17th<br/>The Bosche had dogs here so as to try &amp; track them &ndash; our walks stopped today as a result.</p>
<p>Several rooms punished for making noises after 10-30 pm &ndash; one room having all walks stopped for 4 walks &ndash;</p>
<p>Read book &amp; played Patience German Professor commenced giving lessons tonight &ndash;</p>
<p>September 18th<br/>Released from Prison at 1 pm &ndash;</p>
<p>Recd PC no 10 from Kate &ndash;</p>
<p>Cornish admitted to Hospital yesterday to have shrapnel ball extracted from his leg &ndash; the Dr estimates that his absence will be about 10 days. He has had endless pain &amp; trouble with his leg &ndash;</p>
<p>Recd letter from Annie &ndash; Gertie Rushton Bert &amp; Aust Red X &ndash; Bert's letter conveyed the very sad news that Poor Les Poole has been killed in action &ndash; I feel so sorry for his Dear People &ndash; Berts letter also conveyed the very pleasing information that Mother mentioned in her last letters to him that she had received his cables saying I was a Prisoner &ndash; Played Bridge &ndash;</p>
<p>September 19th<br/>Walk today &ndash; the invalid officers did not go, on account of our long promised bath chairs not arriving &ndash;</p>
