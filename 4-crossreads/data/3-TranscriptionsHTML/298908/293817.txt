<p>In a few minutes I was comfortably installed &amp; sitting down to a good tea which I thoroughly enjoyed.  We then pulled our seats up to the fire &amp; started yarning about the topics of the day.  About 9 oclock while we were having a heated discussion over such silly subjects as war brides &amp; etc., one of Mr. Dukes friends blew in &amp; helped the talk along.  About 9.30 Miss Sparks brought some fine pears &amp; nuts &amp; coffee along so you can see spent the evening very pleasantly as I always do down here.  We turned in about 11 &amp; I was dead to the world in no time for I had had no sleep for 24 hours or more &amp; my bed was as soft as a feather.</p>
