<p>Thursday 13th Sept. lovely day, went for a stroll, I received some Ausie letters last night. Friday fine day, went for walk, head still bad, ever since I left Weymouth.</p>
<p>Saturday Ada &amp; Lily went Home, fine day</p>
<p>Sunday fine, all the family went for a walk after tea, round to Lancing where I seen Thomas A' Becketts place, I went to Aunt Sally's in the morning &amp; met Mr Pelley I also met some Cousins name <strike>d of</strike> same as ours.</p>
<p>Monday 17th Sept changeable weather. I received my photo proofs, they are not bad, I went &amp; bought the girls some presents. I met Mr Chipper an old friend of Dad's last night.</p>
