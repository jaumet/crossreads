<p>2.11.17</p>
<p>Dear Judge Ferguson,</p>
<p>Very many thanks for your letter of congratulations.  It was most kind of you to write to me.  Father &amp; Mother were awfully bucked by your writing [?] them up &amp; wrote specially about it.</p>
<p>I hope you are all keeping very fit.  Mother mentioned that you had a car which Mrs. Ferguson &amp; Dorothy were learning to drive.  That must have been great fun.  When I was up at Division, it was always a great grief to me that G.R.Os [General Routine Order] forbad officers to drive cars, otherwise there would have been ample opportunity to learn.</p>
