<p>Fri 9th July. To Walkers Ridge in morn with Arthur Foster for rice &amp; curry also scored some beans &amp; beef. [Note in margin] Bathe. Quiet noon. Cooked a good tea with curry beef onions &amp; rice. Best feed for weeks We go into outpost about 8. Persistent rumours of a move soon by our Brigade.</p>
<p>Sat 10th On outpost all day Heavy shelling of Kabid Baba</p>
<p>Sun 11th. Fatigue 6 &ndash; 10 finished in good time Bathe Heard from Mater &amp; Mr B Guard in evening 9 till 4 am</p>
<p>Mon 11th. [12th] Fatigue 2 &ndash; 5 contract so finished fairly early. Tile came &amp; had a curry tea with me &amp; later I went down to get water can Crowd damnable poor devils</p>
