
Division under orders to move out at 1 hrs notice.Monday Oct 8Usual routineTuesday Oct 9Saw fine scrap in air to day. Two Taubes came over & one of our planes gave them a go. Brought one Taube down near Somerset Battery lines & drove other back to Turkish lines.Wednesday Oct 10Mail arrived today after a long break. All well at home. Heavy sand storm in afternoon. Column moving up closer to GazaThursday Oct 11Heavy bombardment in progress. Jacko over observing in planes all day.
