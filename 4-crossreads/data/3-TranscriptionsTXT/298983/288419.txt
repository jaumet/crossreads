 Wednesday 8th May Fell in twice but did nothing 
 Thursday 9th to Sunday 12th May Hand dressed every day. Beautiful weather. Doing nothing but reading &amp; loafing about 
 Monday 13th May Nothing as usual Wet day again 
 Tuesday 14th May Was marked B.A. today as my hand is quite healed. Walked to Forant &amp; back this evening about 4 miles longest walk I have had for a long time
