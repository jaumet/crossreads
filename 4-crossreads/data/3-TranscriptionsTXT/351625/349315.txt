 A Gift from The Australian Comforts Funds. 
 France 18 July 18 
 Dear Mum, 
 I must try &amp; write you a few lines so that you will know that I am still going strong &amp; keeping well.  I sent you a cable 3 weeks or so ago I hope you got it, let me know if you did, also I never heard whether you got our cable sent
