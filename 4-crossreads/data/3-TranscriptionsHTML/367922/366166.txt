<p>and generally some of the big guns of the Ministry have an hours firing at the banquet: but I want my <u>pop gun</u> to be heard too.</p>
<p>I was really sorry to hear that yr. health has not been too good: but pluck up, keep going and all will come right. I felt next door to a dead man a few months ago, but I delved on and now I am as fit as ever I was in my life. I agree with you Stephen Mills is a good'un alright and I am sure he will prove it down here. With all good wishes &ndash; In much haste</p>
<p>Yrs ffly<br/>D. C. McLachlan</p>
