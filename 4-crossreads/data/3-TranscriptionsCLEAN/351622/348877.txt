20 August 1916 SunThe 12th Field Ambulance relieve us & by ten oclock we were on the road bound for Contay. On arriving at Contay sometime after dinner I was laying down on a paddock feeling pretty tired when someone said Well Sting how are you doing & there in front [text continued at top of page]
21 August 1916 MonReveille 6 am. Most of the boys leave for the trenches. I am not going out this time but doing night duty with McGee. Said farewell to Ack, Geoff, Toby & Cliff as they left. Did some washing & indulged in the old game of pontoon before going on duty. First Anzac Rest Station
