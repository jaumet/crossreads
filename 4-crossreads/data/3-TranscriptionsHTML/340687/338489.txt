<p>July, 1917. Wednesday 4<br/>Heavy and long thunder shower this morning and I got wet &amp; had to shift. Dull with a little sun. 6.30 check 8.30 same parade and home at 10.45. Read in intervals all day. 2.30 drill order and did a little squad drill until 3.30. 2 &amp; 3 VI Rugby in evening won by 1st 9 to 6 after a good game.</p>
