<p>September 1915<br/>Monday 13<br/>Cool    We began Regimental duties yesterday. Our section had to help pull down some tents and pack them up as they were required by another Brigade. Did nothing else all day. Played "Nap" for cigarettes last night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 14<br/>Cool. Waiting to hear what our orders for the day are. We overhauled our signalling gear today. It was in fairly good condition considering the working about it had</p>
