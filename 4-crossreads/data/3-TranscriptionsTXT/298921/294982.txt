 Dec 25 Xmas Day I know the little Mother will be at Church &amp; the lines she has written me 'Till we meet again' I have just re-read. Capt. White is O/C &amp; there are only the Q'land Reinforcements &amp; my own on board. Our boat is a German Prize boat originally the Pfalz &amp; had only made one voyage when we boarded her &ndash; Wool, Ores, frozen Meat Crew are all strangers to each other, the Skipper a good man concert in the evening
