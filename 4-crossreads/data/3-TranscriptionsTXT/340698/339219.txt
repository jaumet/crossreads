 10/9/17 We travelled on the Great Western to Fishguard from Paddington via Newport and Cardiff. 
 Went by boat to [Rosslare?] about 3 &frac12; hrs. and took the train again to Mallow changed trains at Mallow and arrived in Killarney about noon of the 11th. 
 Done a trip across the lake to Innisfallen Island and viewed some very romantic places.
