<p><strong>Eclipses, 1916.</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>January 20th. - Partial Eclipse of <em>Moon</em>, partly visible in Australia.</li>
<li>February 4th. - Total Eclipse of Sun, invisible in Australia.</li>
<li>July 15th. - Partial Eclipse of <em>Moon</em>, invisible in Australia.</li>
<li>July 30th. - Annular Eclipse of <em>Sun</em>, visible in Australia. The central line of Annular Eclipse passes along the southern portion of Australia. At Adelaide the Eclipse will be Annular.</li>
<li>Dec. 25th. - Partial Eclipse of <em>Sun</em>, invisible in Australia.</li>
</ol>
