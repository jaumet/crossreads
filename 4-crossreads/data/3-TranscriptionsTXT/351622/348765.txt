 13 January 1916 Thur Nothing of any importance to report to-day. Went to see a few pals close by in the infantry during the night. On my way home I stopped at one of the Crown &amp; Anchor schools. Lost &pound;3 backing the lucky old Heart. 
 14 January 1916 Fri Went to see Jack Kater QM.S. A long chat with Lieut Taplin. We have a [indecipherable] in the camp which claims a great number of the boys after the days drill is over. Crown &amp; Anchor &amp; two-up schools are to be seen everywhere. There seems to be plenty of money about.
