 FRIDAY, AUGUST 8, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; warm. Round Cow paddock 
 &amp; Mountain in morning &amp; lopping 
 in 4D in evening. Tickle poisoning 
 in Mountain &amp; Top paddocks. 
 Darby bust both his bits &amp; sinker 
 bars &amp; has to stop work while he 
 has them sent to Sydney &amp; fixed up. 
 He is down 150 ft [bi?].
