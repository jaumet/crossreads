<p>Mon. 21st Aug. 1916.<br/>Marched from Albert through Senlis to Warloy &amp; billeted.</p>
<p>Tues. 22nd Aug,</p>
<p>Marched from Warloy through Contay, Herrisart &amp; Valdemaison to the old apple orchard at La Vicogne &amp; bivouaced.</p>
<p>Thurs. 24th Aug.<br/>Left La Vicogne &amp; marched through Candas to Fienvillers where we billeted.</p>
<p>Sat  Aug. 26th<br/>Marched from Fienvillers to Doullens &amp; entrained to Poperinghe &amp; camped in huts at the Scottish Lines about 4 miles the other side.<br/>Our turn in Belgium now.</p>
<p>Thurs. 31st  Aug.<br/>Left Scottish lines which are between Busse Boom &amp; Ouderdom on our advance guard &amp;  marched through Vlamertinghe to Ypres &amp; took over underground billets in the cellars of remains of houses which the Bn. will come &amp; occupy tomorrow as reserves for front line.<br/>The town is absolutely a heap of ruins &ndash; Cathedral &amp; Cloth Hall have only parts of wall left.</p>
