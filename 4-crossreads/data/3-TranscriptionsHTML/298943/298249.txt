<p>Vic still under treatment in isolation tent, &amp; no better.<br/>Finished Tom Pollocks physical jerks diagrams, &amp; tomorrow have to prepare a board for their reception.<br/>Wrote article for "Daily Mail" "Civilian Rearguards", &amp; owing to difficulty over censor regulations, the Colonel has sent it down to the Base censor.</p>
