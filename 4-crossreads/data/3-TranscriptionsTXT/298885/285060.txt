 Sun. Apr. 16th -16 Out of bed at 3 A.M. and left at 5 A.M.  After losing our way several times arrived a short distance behind the line at 3 P.M.  All gunners taken at once to position. 
 Mon. Apr. 17th -16 Myself and 5 other specialists (commonly known as "spooks") stationed at O.P. (observation post) and 1st line of trenches, our duties being Telephony.  Firing going on all round and too close to be comfortable.  Today is exactly 4 months since leaving Sydney.
