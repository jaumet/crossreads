<p>21 July, Monday</p>
<p>We go to Town. Dinner. Mum goes to Julie &amp; I go to Anzac Buffet. I meet at Julies. We pull down decorations. I meet Doris &amp; ring Mary.</p>
<p>22 July, Tuesday</p>
<p>Stop Home. We visit Dobbies &amp; Mrs Stephen &amp; White's place. I r------</p>
