<p>1st Dec.  Received a very large mail of letters &amp; parcels just now, and as we all are <br/>broke with scarcely a cigarette, it came at an opportune moment.<br/>Our rations now are very bad or rather scanty, for we have not had a decent feed since <br/>we left Larkhill.<br/>One great relief to us is we do not have to wash our clothes now, as, coming out of the <br/>firing line we are sent back to the baths where we have a hot bath and a change of <br/>clothes supplied.</p>
<p>2nd Dec  Paid this morning</p>
