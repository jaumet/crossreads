At intervals in the sides of the passages are small recesses marked with sign of cross cut out of the stone. Said to be for use as tombs but not large enough. At end of passage is a slightly larger recess with a small well about 3 feet deep and 3 feet diameter sunk into stone. Usual stories about Holy Family resting there. They seem to have rested everywhere! The whole place shows no sign of damp but there is a dank smell like a cellar everywhere. Came up again into body of church and inspected screens which are said to be wonderful work. The design is in ivory of which small pieces are let into the wood like mother of pearl. The woodwork is being restored and, in some cases, there will not be much of the original left. Hailed by coloured gentleman in Tarboush and carrying offertory plate embossed with cross.
