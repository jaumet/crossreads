<p>They have to stamp their mail apparently./ You will find it stuck to the top of this letter. I have a book of Views taken from a Fritz it is views behind his line principally his Head qr staff but will be a good souvenier if it reaches you shall send them across the first chance I get.  Did the piece of the Aroplane reach you safely.Well Father I am behind to blazes with my writing so shall have to cut all short until I catch up Shall write again shortly. Havent had any thing from Sloanes people as yet.  Have you seen the will he left. Find out just what it is for me will you one is working in the dark. I shant do anything about it until I get back to Austo again  No more just now must close with love &amp;amp: best wishes to all<br/>Your sincere son  Clyde</p>
