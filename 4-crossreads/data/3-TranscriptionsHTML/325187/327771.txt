<p>[Wednesday 26 January 1916]</p>
<p>The natives are very shy out here &amp; at sunset string in to water each leading an animal a lamb to a Camel-<br/>Women are never seen between the ages of 8 or 80, they appear to be kept in absolute seclusion.  Field Officer today, this is the work of a Major, but Captains do it- practically Orderly Officer to the Brigade.<br/>What women we do see are very modest!! &amp; generally wear only one garment, so when they unexpectedly meet a European they throw this over their head feeling sure they have done the correct thing, but the rest of their body does not even boast a fig leaf-</p>
