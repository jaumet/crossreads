<p>1916 February 21<br/>Monday<br/>Reinforcement drill min morning as usual. In afternoon dusty dirty march &amp; company drill. Back a shower.  Still heavy heat.  Slight rain.<br/>Concert at Rec. tent. Sid MacDonald &amp; party. Bob &amp; self go down lines. former [indecipherable] <br/>Y M.C A. &amp; [indecipherable] ring up Sr tripper at canteen.<br/>Receive acc from Marriot &amp; Oliver re Power of Attorney. Hot.</p>
