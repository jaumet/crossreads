<p>Saturday 10th June</p>
<p>I am writing this on my kit bag in a tent at the Miscellaneous Details Camp, Tel-El Kebir.  We left Maadi yesterday morning, had breakfast at Mrs. Nyland's, catching the 11 oclock train for here, which arrived at 1.15 p.m.  They took our medical history &amp; also informed me that I stood a good chance of getting dispensery job, for there were plenty wanted.  Rumours are circulated that 2 days should see us embarked for England.<br/>The advance guard is to be picked to-day I understand.<br/>Today I have struck the</p>
