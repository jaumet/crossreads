<p>Le Bizet<br/>Between the hours of 6.30 and 9.15 p.m. a total of 2000 rounds were fired at the following targets:-<br/>1.  Thatched Cot., C.17.b.<br/>2.  Screened Fm., C.5.b.<br/>3.  Road, C.11.b. &amp; 12 a.<br/>4.  Road, C.18.c.<br/>Owing to better visability, more activity was shown on the part of the enemy, a few shells landing round the Station during the afternoon.  Quiet at night, except for retaliation to our fire.  Machine guns very quiet during day and night.<br/>The following is the Fighting Strength return to noon today:-</p>
