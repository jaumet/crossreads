  
 Square where I adjourned to have a rest after my long days walk. This is only a small square but very pretty indeed. I had tea and returned to Horseferry road, to be marched back to Waterloo Station. I might mention that at several of these underground railway stations, there are escalators to take you down. These are revolving staircases, and you simply step on and off. We marched back across Westminister bridge to the station and caught the train back to Ludgershall. I felt pretty tired after my three days
