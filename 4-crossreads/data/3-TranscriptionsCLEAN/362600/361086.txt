  
 F 19 Sun - 1235 letters to 21.5.18. Very hot day. Play cricket in afternoon. Band plays on oval. Sit in pine all night &  write long letter to M.R.L. Home supper when Ellis comes home. Meet J. Vernon Wrote M.R.L. 
 F & Hot 20 Mon Go to S'bury Tea on Lawn meet Frank. Go to Wyndham Rd. Go to meet Cis   & baby. Have supper & just catch Train. Arrive home pretty late rush and charged on Quarters. 
 F & hot 21 Tues. Very hot. Dick gets sick & we all [indecipherable] Him. I have a big clean up & I am in a terrible mess. Have supper & write to Aunt Annie. Send parcel Aunt Annie. 
 F & hot 22 Wed Recd 2 letters from Aunt Annie. I am inoculated. E. goes to waac Dance. Dick in bed all day but much better. I read after writing. Wrote Mum. Nancy Denam. Aunt Annie 
 W 23 Th Rain & hail. [beautiful] day. Go to room & read all night. We learn that we are to go to France very soon. Send home photo of Hobden's Dance. 
 D & windy 24 Fri Received 6 letters from home. Nettie. Peg. Nell. Grandma. Mrs Niness. Read all night. Wrote M.R.L. 
 F 25 Sat Nice cool day. Play cricket   & get 9. We win by 26 runs. Read all night and get supper afterwards. Recd letter from W. Ha  & Norm Upton. Stan Hodge killed. meditate at home &  after the war. Who goes back with me   & in what relation etc.
