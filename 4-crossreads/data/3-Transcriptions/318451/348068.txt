<p>and our own Colonel &amp; 7 of our officers were present.  My text was "How shall we sing the Lord's song in a strange land".  Tonight I preached at the Y.M.C.A. so altogether I have had a full day.  Weather still freezing.</p>
<p>Monday<br/>Today a slight thaw set in but it began to snow again.<br/>I went to Houplines this morning.  A lad came in this afternoon for a chat &amp; tonight Jenkin one of our Vic. probationers also dropped in.</p>
<p>Tuesday<br/>The weather was a little milder today but cold enough, the ground has become quite muddy, but tonight I noticed before dark that the water in the streets was coating with ice.  I have been down to the trenches today &amp; had lunch with Capt. Connell &amp; called also at the Canteen.</p>
<p>Wednesday<br/>Went to Trenches &amp; canteen today.  Bible Class tonight, 18 present, parable Lost Sheep, Lost Coin.</p>
