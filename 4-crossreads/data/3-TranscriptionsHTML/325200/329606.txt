<p>In the afternoon we went to   <strike>Katia</strike>   Bir el Abd which the Turkeyman had now evacuated. Here we found evidences of a large camp, a number of wells  &amp;  pumps  &amp;  notice boards &ndash; the german touch &ndash; announcing that this was Bir el Abd  &amp; Katia</p>
