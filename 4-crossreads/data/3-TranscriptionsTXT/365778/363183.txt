
Saturday 24.3.17Made more patrolling and further preparations for the attack, visit from Osborne.Sunday 25/3/17Attack tomorrow at dawn, everything should go well.Monday 26/3/17Attack a great success but fierce opposition met. Cherry & Corner killed. Enemy made a big counter attack which we beat off.Tuesday 27/3/17Relieved by Pye of 19th at 3 am, very tired indeed. Met Gen Gough who congratulated me most heartily on work of Battery.
