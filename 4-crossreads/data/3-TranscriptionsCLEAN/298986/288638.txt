  
 Thursday 27th 
 Cloudy & much cooler today.  The Y.M. opened a libery today.  Got a fair book, was just about out of reading.  The Educational Officer took names for Classes yesterday.  Am down for Bookkeeping & General Agriculture.  Put on my slacks & sand shoes today much comfort. 
 Friday 28th 
 Beautifull day warm enough to sit in the shad.  Passed an island on port
