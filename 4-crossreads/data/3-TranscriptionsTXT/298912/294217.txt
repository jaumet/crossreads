The others were equally absurd but they were fitted up at last, &amp; we trained it up to Casula, on purpose missing the train arranged that we should catch. Hop was put in charge  --- but we took him there ok &amp; got out of the train at Casula Railway Station &amp; had a nice little walk back &amp; up hills in the heat of the sun with full equipment on board  packs on our backs.. We arrived here at last &amp; of course nobody knew we were coming &amp; there was no preparation made for us, &amp; no dinner. However we took up our positions in tents at our own liking &amp; in our tent we had Eric, Hop, Pierce, Charlie Williams, Fat, Balfour, &amp; self, quite a happy little family.
