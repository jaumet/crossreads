<p>boy, I do hope he is getting on alright and having a real good time &ndash; He is one of Our Heros &amp; I'm awfully proud to think he is our friend. Give his mother &amp; father my love. Tell Mr Capp I receive the Church papers &amp; find them very interesting. Shall try &amp; write to them soon. Poor old Keith must be on his way to Australia I think, I cannot find out where he is. He has not written to me since, the one I got from the Hospital Ship. The mail closes in the morning so a few more hurried lines. Well, Dorothy &amp; I went into Cairo this afternoon &amp; had tea, returned early &amp; found a note from Eric Baker saying he was on Picquet duty for 7 days, so could not keep appointment. Dorothy received a lovely box of eatables from her folk, so we sampled it &amp; had a goo feed in our own bedroom &ndash; then walked down to Gordon House to see</p>
