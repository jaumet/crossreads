  
 Sunday, July 1st 1917 Up 7 a.m. - Church Parade 8.30 a.m.  Bought pr. breeches from Pratley for 7/6 - nice fit.  Afternoon went for stroll down Fovant.  Fine sunshine all day. 
 Monday, 2/7/17 On Lewis Guns all day.  Left them 3 p.m. & came to hut & got cleaned up & received leave pass for Barrow.  Caught 4.50 p.m. train from Dinton & 6.23 p.m. from Salisbury & ran to W'loo [Waterloo] without a stop.  Went per tube to Euston & booked bed at Y.M.C.A. & looked up trains at station.
