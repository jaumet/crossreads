<p>Well old chap, I hope you are doing well.  Remember me to old Jim, Jack Fraser, &amp; any of the old hands that I knew.</p>
<p>I am afraid this has been rather an egotistical letter, but I will give you the general news when I get back to where the Sun shines every day.  It is deucedly cold &amp; foggy here.</p>
<p>Believe me<br/>Yr old pal<br/>C  Boccard  Lt</p>
