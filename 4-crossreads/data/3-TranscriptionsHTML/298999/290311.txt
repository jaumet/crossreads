<p>got cleaned up and left anzac 5.30 got a garry and went to Cairo Station.  my leave has finished got a ticket caught 6.15 P.M. train.  J.K. came on the Platform looking Pretty full arrived ismalia 9.30 got some tea got a car and returned to ferry Post 10.30.  turned in feeling very tired.</p>
