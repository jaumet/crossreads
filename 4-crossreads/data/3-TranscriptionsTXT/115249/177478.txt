
Naval Signal
From - Berrima
To - Flag
Date - 21 9 14
Time - 6.50 PM
From Brigadier.  Governor surrendered with four Officers fifteen Officials Thirty Seven White Non Commissioned Officers and men and one hundred and twenty natives all of whom are at Rabaul.  Governor staying night at Herbertshohe reports here early tomorrow.  Half Company Infantry at Toma to receive reinforcements sent from Wilhelmshafen
