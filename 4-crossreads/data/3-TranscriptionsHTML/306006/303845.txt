<p>26-4-17<br/>Thurs 27th<br/>At 3.15 this morning we formed up &amp; the Bde moved off &amp; daylight found us close to Shellal but no sign of the enemy soon after sunrise we watered our horses,&amp; returned to Camp arriving here at 10 AM &amp; slept till mid-day; Then received orders to move at 3 oclock &amp; arrived back at Shellal<br/>Just before sundown &amp; had a busy time laying wire to the 3 squadrons.
