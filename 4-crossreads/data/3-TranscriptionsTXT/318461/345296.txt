 Sunday 17/6/17. very hot just like Sydney in Jany. Got the two extremes here. Got letter from Syd &amp; Trustee Coy. also Parcel from Nell. Tasmania. 
 Monday Tuesday Wednesday.  Thursday  Nil 
 Thursday 21/6/17 cannot get leave yet so (umpty.) letter from Viv, Broke, so am I, can sympathise with him. Pay 22/9 &pound;27.7.0. sweet place this no Money &amp; no Pay for 9 days may as well have kept us in the line. 
 Friday 22/6/17. Nothing much doing Boys in C Coy going out to day for stunt, taking Village, Pictures I expect. Pay 23/9 &pound;27.8.0 
 Saturday 23/6/17 same every day. all chaps Broke, no money, no Booze, good behaviour. Bat. gone out on stunt this morning must write to Viv to day, also Nell &amp; Boys. Pay 24/9 &pound;27.9.0. Bully Beef stew every day, can't come at it at all. 
 Sunday 24/6/17 Glorious morning 3rd Brigade went through on their way to the front again expect we go soon. nothing important, sent letters to Viv &amp; Nell. Pay 25/9 &pound;27.10.0. Post Cards to Jack Carol, Tony Nell. letter to Trustee Coy for &pound;10-0-0 ought to get it end of August. been lovely day getting warm again. Pay Tomorrow.
