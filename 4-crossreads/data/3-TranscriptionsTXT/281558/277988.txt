Tuesday May 1st 1917
Tidworth : good day : mounted guard 9 am : No 2 Post
Wednesday May 2nd
Tidworth : good day : mounted 9 am : our passes did not come through so we went for a stroll after dinner : pictures after tea
Thursday May 3rd
Tidworth : good day : mounted guard 9 am : No 3 post : photos
Friday May 4th
Tidworth glorious day : relieved 9 am : caught noon train to Swindon : put in a great time &amp; caught 9.40 pm home
Saturday May 5th
Tidworth : windy &amp; dusty : mounted guard 9 am : M.O.
