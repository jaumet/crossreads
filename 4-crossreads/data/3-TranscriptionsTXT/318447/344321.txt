 Esdud. Nov 12th. 22  15-30  miles up the coast from Gaza 
 We have caught up with the Brigade &amp; are now camped just behind a village. There is a battery firing just a few hundred yards in front of us &amp; a half battery of heavies firing a few hundred yards behind us so I expect we shall get shelled out of here soon. 
 The Turks have made a stand here &amp; held us up for two days &amp; now the 52nd Inf Division is up here attacking them. There is an enormous amount of material &amp; guns abandoned back on the road &amp; there must be at least &amp;pound;3000000
