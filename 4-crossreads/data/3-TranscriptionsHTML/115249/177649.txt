<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>Ship of Origin - Hebertshohe</p>
<p>From - Brigadier</p>
<p>To - Berrima</p>
<p>At the request of the German Commandant Military Force I have despatched one Motor Lorry with Infantry Scout and one officer to bring in large quantities of ammunition from Toma. &nbsp;Have also sent to Pitapaka Wireless Station two officers and five men to bring in second motor lorry for same purpose if in working order. 1525&nbsp;</p>
