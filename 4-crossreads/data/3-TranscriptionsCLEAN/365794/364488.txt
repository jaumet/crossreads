
Wednesday 28 March 1917 [27 March 1918]Off duty 5 p.m. went church 6 p.m.  Mr. Horan preaching, very good lessons - "A Christian is always known by his behaviour to fellow men", was one very true saying.  I think of dear Dr. F. & of his treatment of people all the years I've known him.Whist drive 8.45 p.m. at A.S.C., room crowded & very gay evening spent.S. Reed & I return together in garry escorted by Mr. J. Andrews and arrive 11.50 p.m.
