 9th.  Another lovely morning & its quite a treat to be out.  There is not a cloud in the sky & things look very beautiful. We handed in all our blankets this morning & quite time too for they were absolutely filthy you could go to bed with clean pyjamas on & get up in the morning real  &  dirty, I don't think they could have been changed for ages. I got another nice batch of letters from Aussie & letter & a Xmas card from Dick Duke & a parcel from New S. Wales, letters totalled 13 without cards.
