 Greek Houses impressed me. One scene in the street interested me &amp; it was the settlement of a dispute. It was in the Street &amp; the Governor seemed to be the Arbitrator. 
 To-day was the commencement of their fasting extended till over Xmas &ndash; the great 40 days fast &amp; early in the morning I noticed a goodly crowd of women folk coming out of the church all carrying their tapers which I was informed they purchased there &amp; carried home to burn before their own little altar. 
 Sunday 5th Dec This morning Eltham &amp; I got busy &amp; I marked cases &amp; we managed to get transport down to the wharf. I spent the day getting final matters fixed up
