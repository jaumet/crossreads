  
 12.30 p.m.  Heavy Machine Gun and Rifle fire and Shell fire encountered from North Eastern end of Peronne. 
 The adjutant Capt. J. Haddon and the Intelligence Officer wounded;  both rather badly.  Lieut. Veal has been wounded slightly and gone out.  Enemy dead testify to the havoc wrought by our barrage earlier in the morning. 
 4.30 p.m.  Major Kerr received a slight but nasty wound between the eyes. 
 5.30 p.m.  Orders have been received for our brigade to relieve the 14th Bde. who have suffered very heavy casualties.  We are to relieve the 53rd Bn. 
 Major Kerr went forward
