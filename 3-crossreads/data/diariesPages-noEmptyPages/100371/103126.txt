Saturday raining (light) as soon as it was dark we (7) went to relieve a squad at the Batts post, 5 in the squad a Acting Cpl. a Sergeant went with us as guides, but we lost our way, we had about a mile to go, The Infantry were changing over Fritz was a bit jumpy, machine gun bullets kept whizzing by two shells lobbed very close to us, we had to pick our way between the flares having to repeatedly take cover in shell holes we had a rough passage then we had a carry as soon as we got here, it being the first stretcher case for 6 days, though the going was heavy, Fritz left us alone. also on our return journey. The front line is two hundred yards away
Sunday 18 Feb. I was nearly sniped outside the dug-out. Relieved about 6.30 PM Fritz was quiet. Monday 19
