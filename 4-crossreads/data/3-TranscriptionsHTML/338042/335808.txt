<p>Visit War Chest then on to Highgate Hill (Mr. &amp; Mrs Scales) a charming,  sincere &amp; kind hearted Lady. Mr. Scales a benevolent, pious &amp;  sterling Gentleman &ndash; "Home Sweet Home" &ndash; Meet Arthur Brooker (48th Batt. A.I.F.) who also is a guest of Mrs. Scales. An Interesting afternoon walk over Highgate Hill etc During which we enter House of Nell Gwynne &ndash; I see the Home of Richard Cromwell and the House in which Bacon died after performing experiment with the "snow" etc.<br/>(Home of Grand Duke Michael of Russia)</p>
