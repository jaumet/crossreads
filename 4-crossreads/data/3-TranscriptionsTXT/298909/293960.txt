 20/4/16  Thursday 
 Very cold last night. Pay day &ndash; as it was a &pound;2 pay the lads consider themselves well off &ndash; Attended a lecture at Y.M.C.A at night on Discipline etc. At 6 pm we got marching orders to march over to No. 1 A.S. Hospital &ndash; which is only a couple of camps away &ndash; Camped their overnight &ndash; Orders to move at in the morning to some unknown destination. 
 21/4/16  Friday 
 Good Friday - &amp; well myself &amp; 150 A.M.C. men that moved out from Telekebar will remember it. At 9.30 am after a severe medical examination, we boarded a Hospital-train which runs along the Canal daily &ndash; Our destination was Ishmailia &ndash; here we got out &amp; had to carry our home or anything we wanted with us, that is blankets &ndash; etc, &ndash; 48 hours rations, water, etc, it was beastly hot &amp; talk about dust. We marched from Ismailia to Ferry Post &ndash; crossing the Suez Canal by hand Ferry, we arrived at our destination
