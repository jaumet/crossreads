We hear nothing of interest later as we have no record of another visit to Torre abbey for some years.  The last abbot was Simon Rede elected and confirmed by the King in Aug. 1523.  He and his fellow canons surrendered the monastery to Henry VIII in August 1539 before the Commissioner William Petre.  The abbot and his religious sect each received a pension.
