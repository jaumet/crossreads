Beautiful &amp; sunny morning after last nights showers.<br/>Have just come back from being medically examined, every one who goes out of this Camp if only for a couple of days has to be examined by the Dr. before going &amp; after, though it is only a farce to say the best of it, the object of it is to control that terrible scourge "venereal disease".<br/>England is full of it &amp; especially London, &amp; unless you have a strong will, you are a "goner." Received a few letters this morning but judging by the dates of these &amp; the last lot I got, there must have been some sunk.</p>
