<p>19th May At about six a general armistice was made by Turks &amp; white flag show all along the line Our Amateur officers as I always expected made a hash of it at a critical time &amp; allowed the officer of the Turks a German to come right up to our advanced trench with several Turks Three came in But after an hours parley &amp; the enemy having seen all there was to be seen</p>
