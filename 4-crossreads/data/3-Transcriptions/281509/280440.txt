<p>1916 August 5<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>We relieve 4th Field Stretcher bearers at Clearing Station, who go up to trenches. Cartwright &amp; self take over 2 tents &amp; attend to patients till 9 p. m. Several German wounded pass thro' hospital well fed and very fine specimens they are too. The quest for souvenirs</p>
