  
 Aug 5th Good news of previous nights attack all along the line. Continual stream of patients pass through our hands. 
 Aug 6th Two more of our boys (making 4 in all) killed - Tragic but courageous end of Harry Smith - He, together with Pte Griffiths are recommended for D.C.M's Success of 2nd Division. 
 Aug 7th. Return of our bearers from Albert - The boys have done excellent work. Losses killed 4 wounded 8 Praiseworthy speech by our Colonel & General Legge. 
 Aug 8th Heavy losses in Fourth Division - 1st Div reorganised - Patients galore 
 Aug 9th. Pay-day - Departure of 20 men from our section for Puechvillers (?) Arrival of Reinforcements to supplement our Amb.
