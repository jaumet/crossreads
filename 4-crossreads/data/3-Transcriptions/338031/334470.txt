The superior coloured gentry ride about in sedan chairs &amp; they comport themselves with a stately magnificence that denotes the aristocracy of colour as compared with the common black trash. There was a public washing place at the foot of a rocky cliff &amp; here the women were rubbing out their clothes with flat stones.
