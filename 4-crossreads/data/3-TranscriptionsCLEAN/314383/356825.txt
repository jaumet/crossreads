
Jan 12Left France. Lt. Turner from 7th Bde called at 6 am to go to Bolongne in Gen Smythe's car.  We set off at 7am after breakfast.  Turner just out of line.  Picked up Hitchcock at Wisques school.  Arrived Bolongne 11am after an awful trip through the snow.  Road all slush and mud.  Arrived London 4pm, reported Horseferry Road, told report in morning.
