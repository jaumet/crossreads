<p>Apl 8 the next one as she is marked with 8 black crosses on white grounds Only a single hollow canvas plane, beautiful engine, fairly long tail held up by steel supports, 2 pneumatic wheels under the main body &ndash; which is of a sort of marked &ndash; aluminium, &amp; single seated. The MG is controlled by the propellor blades which it fires past but does not hit. Everything is intact so I guess we wont be long in getting a few Fokkers going. The chap who made the Capture is a "Tommy &ndash; Cook", &amp; I believe gets a months leave, &amp; a reward "Lucky devil"</p>
<p>9 Chewing kisses yesty &amp; a filling pulled out. Stiff luck today on BHQ guard from 9 am to 7 pm 2 hrs on &amp; 4 off. Went on from 9 to 11 &amp; chucked a few salutes &amp; presents to sundry officers &amp; armed parties   Weather as usual, cloudy &amp; cold. Batt leaves for ----- tomorrow morning; closer to the firing line I think. [in margin] Sunday</p>
