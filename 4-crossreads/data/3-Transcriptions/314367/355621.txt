<p>HMAS Fantome<br/>Dec 22 &ndash; 14</p>
<p>Dear girl &ndash;<br/>Ship's company including self are all in the best of health &amp; spirits &ndash;<br/>To duly economise the time of the censor I wont say more save carry on as usual &ndash;</p>
<p><u>- Fred -</u></p>
