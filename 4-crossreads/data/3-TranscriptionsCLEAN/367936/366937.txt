
Tuesday 22 February 1916Train very crowded but Mother and I had a fair trip up and were met by Father at Warrobil.  I rode Zaida home, nice to be on a horse again.  Place looks terribly dry.  Thunder storm in evening.  Started to rain.
Wednesday 23 February 1916Good storm at night and rain during day;  had 70 pts.Father & Mother drove to Wonga in evening;  I went shooting rabbits in back paddock and burning hollow logs.Rabbits are fairly thick but very wild.
