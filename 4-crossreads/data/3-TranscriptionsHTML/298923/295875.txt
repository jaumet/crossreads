<p>28 July 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>X Instead of going to Church Parade I had the unpleasant job of taking a Summary of evidence against 5 of our Officers caught out of bounds in Jerusalem-X   Not 500 yards from my "Bivy" is a Cement Reservoir used by Jewish Syndicate to water orange groves-<br/>Jackarandah Trees are all around &amp; I bathe there every morning- such a Garden of Eden after the Jordan Valley.<br/>Just received a big batch of Australasians with local papers enclosed, I gave Sullivan the paper re his brothers death X Wagga as he is generally called may get a trip home soon. X</p>
