<p>There was a beautiful harvest moon showing and the nights were lovely. We had one disadvantage from this however &ndash; It showed the landscape up so clearly and "Mr. Fritz" was very active in this time with air raids.</p>
<p>Indeed we were all gathered together in the Y.M.C.A. but when we heard the wank-wank wank of exploding shells or rather bombs, making us put out the light very suddenly a light in a tent is a grand target for a bomb to be aimed at</p>
