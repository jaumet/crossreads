<p>ensued in a desultory manner for about 3 hrs: the only casualties - on our side at any rate &ndash; being two horses slightly wounded by strays.<br/>I myself heard three bullets whistle over, the first I have heard since Anzac, - I was forgetting that there was a war on.<br/>The whole thing was very funny Officers would ride up smiling  &amp;  rubbing their hands "there is a beautiful little engagement going on up on the hill there" "Anybody hit yet?" " Oh no not when I left" It seems so incongruous after</p>
