This morning Mr. Edgely came round wanted to know how I was feeling. I told him not too good, so he said "well you had better take it easy for the next 3 or 4 days give yourself a chance to get over it.
25th Xmas Day my 3rd away from Australia, one in Cairo, one on the sea coming back to Egypt after the evacuation of Gallopoli this time the scene is laid in France for me at any rate it has not been a very pleasant one
