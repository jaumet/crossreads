On 23rd we went out to Beaulenct but on Sunday 30th April we moved into the reserve line near the Vaulx. On Wed 3rd May we went up dug a communication trench from Noreiul to the front line to facilitate the movement of troops for the big attack coming off the following morning. We got back to Vaulx at about 3.30 AM on 4th just as the bombardment commenced. The sky was lit by the flashes of the heavy batteries. The field guns
