<p>2 July 1918 Tuesday<br/>Got well strafed wiring last night-    Knees &amp; hands full of thorns this morning.<br/>Expected attack on June 8th now, which is about end of Ramadan.  We hear we get relieved after that, those that are left.  Very hot &amp; not much sleep.<br/>Very thankful for Victoria Racing Club gift of Tobacco Tucker etc. it helps the men at a critical time-<br/>Got to bury 8 horses now, killed by shells in our lines- mine are all OK still.</p>
