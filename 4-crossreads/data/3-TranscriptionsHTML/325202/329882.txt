<p>Sept 17<br/>Yesterday &amp; today have been taking rations up to advanced HQ near Dickebush &amp; then gone on to the R.E. Dump near Ypres for sandbags to complete the H.Q Camp. Hot &amp; very dusty weather.</p>
<p>Toady between Dickebush &amp; Cafe Belge &ndash; Fritz was sending some heavy stuff over at one point of the road &ndash; and we went through at a gallop for about 400 yards.</p>
