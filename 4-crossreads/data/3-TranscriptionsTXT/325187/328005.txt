[Tuesday 19 September 1916]
St Annes We have Hot OXO on the Pier every morning crowds of people there &amp; a WOMAN BAND.Dolly is a dainty little girl like Mother.Going to see old Aunt Anna &amp; Mr Ferrar at Ireland at the end of the week &amp; then coming back here &amp; on to London to an  Hotel next to where Laura lives, I don't think Bruce wanted me at Portman Square somehow, so Laura took a room at Somerset Hotel.
