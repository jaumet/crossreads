<p>30 March 1915<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Have the pleasure of meeting several old friends namely Leuit Stan Perry   &amp;  Sgt Jack Holman   &amp;  Cpl Nelson all from Auburn   &amp;  In our Bat. All very glad to see us.</p>
<p>1915 March 31<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>13th Bat out field firing   &amp;  Outpost work so have the camp to ourselves.<br/>do a bit of drill   &amp;  have a half day off<br/>Make first trip to Cairo. Find it the dirtiest   &amp;  most immoral place on the Globe.<br/>Have a delightful dinner of Soup, Roast, Fish, chicken, salad,   &amp;  dessert<br/>Return to camp 9.30pm</p>
