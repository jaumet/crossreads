 29/8/15 went. Completing a whole day without sleep. A crowded day 
 30/8/15 Mitchell arrived and carried on. Had a look round ship and like her well. 24th embarking on HM Nile. Alexandria natives we notice as before differing in dress etc from further inland. A busy port transports all round and the high masts of Arab dhows sticking up everywhere. Busy round ship. Went into Alexandria at 10pm for morphine for the doctor and had great difficulty in getting same. Eventually got  200 grains for 3 pounds 5 pence.
