<p>July 1918<br/>Tuesday 9</p>
<p>Vaux sur Somme.2nd Bty.Position<br/>Weather bright and clear during morning, aftenoon rained very heavily, and was still raining at midnight. Have had no word from Ken Gunn, as yet. <br/>Mr Mays left for Sig School at St Evatien, the same one as Ken has gone down to. O.Beer, commenced work on the dugout's spare entrance. Fritz and a decent duty shoot. Won 100 francs at poker, suppose I will lose same tomorrow.<br/>Repaired two breaks on the O.P. line Major returned</p>
