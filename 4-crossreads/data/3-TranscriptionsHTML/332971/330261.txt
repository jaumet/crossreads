<p>that way until we come to a corner &amp; then listen to an argument between two Tommies as to where we have to go.  Eventually we find ourselves going up flights &amp; flights of dark steps.  Then we are in the top story of a block of buildings with light &amp; bright fires burning.  Mitch &amp; I are there but where ever in Rush &ndash; she doesn't come &ndash; I secure a bed for her &amp; all we can do is to wait.  At ten we are given nourishment.  After that Matron comes along and calls a roll.  There are 5 S Nurses missing.  Paul is one!  Dora another'  Later on in the night Paul appears.  The five of them after a lot of difficulty were directed to the Sisters Home.  They found it after going down a lane &ndash; over a large cabbage patch &amp; down a hill &ndash; and were about to snuggle in with some of the Sisters when Matron finds them &amp; makes them all get up &amp; follow a guide back again.</p>
<p>Twas laughable this morning the tales the different ones had to tell &ndash; Groups had been getting lost in all directions.  One Sister twice went to knock at what she thought in the darkness was a house &amp; when the got up close felt it was a haystack.  Later she found a cottage &amp; coeed &ndash; but no response.</p>
