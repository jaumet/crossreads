<p>A little further along we passed a <strike>little</strike> tiny cemetery with its small wooden crosses so common in France. On the starboard side little of interest could be seen beyond the primitive horse drawn tram: and yet we all gazed and gazed. There is something very fascinating about this Egypt. What it is I cannot define &amp; I have never struck a man yet who could.</p>
