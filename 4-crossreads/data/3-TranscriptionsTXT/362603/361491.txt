Well Mum, I am enjoying life as well as I can as you can see, but don't think we forget our Home Folk.  We meet only Homely People &amp; I am quite "on my feet."  I just go when &amp; I like &amp; as often as I like &amp; have a "Home from Home." Give my Regards to the Neighbours &amp; to All enquiring Friends especially Mrs Dobson, Ali &amp; Stephen &amp; the Parks. Best of Love to You All from Harry XXX
