<p>Wednesday 22nd May 1917 [1918]<br/>1st day at Bulkeley.  Breakfast 8 a.m. (in bed), out of room 10 a.m. to 12 m.d.  Went Alex. &frac12; hr. ride in double-decker tram, drew pay &pound;10-0-0 at Pay office, lunch &amp; 2 hrs. rest at Nurses' Club, paid 2 frocks Chalons &pound;4-10-0.  Khedival Hotel (Sisters quarters) &amp; 4 p.m. tea S. Roberts.  No. 9 Conval. 6 p.m.  Lovely flowers in gardens here &amp; days fine &amp; not too warm.</p>
