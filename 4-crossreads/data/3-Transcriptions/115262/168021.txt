<p>Tuesday 11 September 1917.</p>
<p>Taubes visited us several times today, and were shelled. &quot;Poor shooting&quot;</p>
<p>Detailed for guard duty tonight till 6.30 p.m. tomorrow night.</p>
<p>Saddle repairing again all the day.</p>
