<p>16.11.16<br/>Thurs 16th<br/>It is rather exasperating to us hearing of the coal strikes at home. The Sigs at Divisional Head Qrs heard a message in reference to it going through to the enemy on wireless yesterday &amp; they put in the day jamming it. It is a pity they cannot work along without strikes while the war is on<br/>[Sentence deleted]<br/>The usual grazing parades today; &amp; this evening we got word to say we are to move at 9 oclock tomorrow morning &amp; are to go to Willegha about 2 miles from here. busy tonight packing panniers 3 getting ready to move</p>
