 We have a number of enthusiastic cricketers in our unit. They spend more time on cricket that at work. 
 A number of the wounded who have passed through the Palestine Campaign unscathed have been brought in to the C.C.S. severely wounded. 
 Aug 10th. Had a set to with Major.C. Taken up before the Col, who asked me to make an apology to him for being rude. 
 Trying to get a proper system into the O.T. 
 Very good news from the Somme Front 
 The road to the West of the C.C.S. has been put out of bounds to us. A fracas between some of our men and some Chinamen had taken place the other night and the thing being reported we were all made to suffer for the misdeeds of a few, as this road was our favourite evening walk. 
 Aug 11th. Got instructions to go on Leave to Paris with W.I. Left Blendecque at 9.36. a m.
