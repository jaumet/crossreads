<p>Well to quit this Flying Corps business, I am hale &amp; hearty except for a rather trying cold I have at present. I hope you are all well darlings &amp; things are going well. As soon as I get settled down, &amp; the money business, of my commission is settled, I will be able to increase the allottment Mum.</p>
<p>Address future letters to<br/>2nd Lieut JHB<br/>20th Reserve Wing R.F.C.<br/>Aboukir<br/>Alexandria</p>
<p>You will have had a cable from me I expect before you receive this letter.</p>
<p>Cheer-oh darling Mum &amp; Kids &amp; God bel you all, Very Best Love<br/>John</p>
