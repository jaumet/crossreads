
<p>THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 6, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Lopped in Top paddock in</p>
<p>morning and Boundary in the</p>
<p>afternoon. Tickle at the fence in</p>
<p>morning, then he cut a salt</p>
<p>trough for the Top paddock. He</p>
<p>brought the dray to the station in</p>
<p>afternoon &amp; got some flour ect.</p>
