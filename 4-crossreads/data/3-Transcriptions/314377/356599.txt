<p>ress of a Mr.Philpott as a dispenser of hospitality to Australian soldiers Mr. Philpott was a member of the Croydon Adult School acting as Secretary for them. It was to him then that I phoned to obtain particulars as to hospitality scheme.</p>
<p>He was to send me to a friend of his a Mr. Grace who lived fairly close to him I rang up and was invited to Mr. Philpotts own home where I was received as one of themselves They even had an hot water bottle in bed for me &ndash; One can imagine the change of circumstances brought about by their kindness</p>
