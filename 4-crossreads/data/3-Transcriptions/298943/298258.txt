<p>where trench fever patients (if any come along) are to bath &amp; have their clothes ironed.<br/>Les Burnett &amp; I have the dispensary &amp; this on our own, &amp; I help by dispensing in the morning.<br/>Last Tuesday, we had a variation with the Literary Circle. We held an [historical evening, &amp; papers were read on "Fred Great", "Franco Prussian War", "Clive", History of Edinburgh". Noticed one or two new faces there &ndash; chaps who had not taken any interest in our doings previously. Good discussions on papers, &amp; also a decision in regard to the study carried (the starting of which had been discussed at a meeting a few days before).</p>
