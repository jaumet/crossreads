<p>28-3-15   "Inflexable" in Harbor appears to be a good deal damaged    Supposed to have been 26 men locked in compartment while ship was in sinking condition.    All lost.   Still in light duty.</p>
<p>29-3-15   Pipe laying for new water supply commenced today   More Battle ships arrived in Port today  There are now a great many large boats in the Harbor    We expect [indecipherable] Dardanelles in about fortnight   Boats cannot proceed in "Narrows" much further without Co-operation with land forces.   Still light duty.</p>
<p>30-3-15.   Nothing fresh in the way of news, work going on steadily.   Still on light duty.</p>
<p>31-3-15.    Camp work water System now being installed &amp; other improvements   Still light duty   Cold very prevalent &ndash; a few deaths from Pneumonia</p>
