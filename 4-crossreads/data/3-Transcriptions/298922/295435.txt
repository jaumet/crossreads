<p>Thursday 19 April 1917</p>
<p>Marched all night, in action at 0400 &ndash; shelled by enemy big fight on all around hope live through it. Two of my friends Killed <strike>MAJOR CHISHOLM &amp; LIEUT CHAMBERS of 2 L.H. SINCLAIR (some PUCKAWIDGEE relative</strike>. We have many casualties 71 Horses. Bombed by enemy air craft again &ndash; cold blooded business. One of our planes shot down by enemy this afternoon.</p>
<p>Many Fleas &ndash; its harder to catch than LICE.</p>
<p><strike>MAJOR CHAMBERS of 12 also killed today attack 2 of same day</strike>. I hope Cuth is alright.</p>
