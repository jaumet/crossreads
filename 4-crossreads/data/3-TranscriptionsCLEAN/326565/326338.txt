Saturday 1/4/16Piquet Corporal for the day, returned at 6 pm.  Very nearly in trouble for leaving camp while on duty. Received parcel from Mrs Robison.  Sing song in tent at night, had rather an enjoyable few hours.
Sunday 2/4/16On the move again, being in this camp only 4 days.  Struck all tents & packed & loaded all material
