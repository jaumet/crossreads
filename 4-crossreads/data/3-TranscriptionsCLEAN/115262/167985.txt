
Monday 6 August 1917.
[Bank Holiday.] No holiday here though.
On the camels once more. We are getting a little used to them by now.
During afternoon went to 14 A.G.H. to see Ted Holden (Sgt) & Tom de Gruchy. While there saw several of the other boys on the Staff & as patients there.
After tea & feeding parade went to Cairo with H. Flavell.
Back for "tattoo" roll call at 9.30 pm at camp.
Made a few purchases in the part of the "Mouske"
