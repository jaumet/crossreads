<p>April, 1916<br/>Saturday 1<br/>Soon after breakfast the men for the new school marched in.<br/>Keep coming in batches all day.<br/>After tea we went for a walk to Zeitoun</p>
<p>Sunday 2<br/>Men continue to come in all day.  I was on duty from 12 to 4 pm.<br/>No word from dad.<br/>At 4.30 pm I went into Cairo for a final ramble before starting work tomorrow.<br/>When I returned about 9.30 pm, I found a note from the store to come &amp; get dad's bag as they were going to Tel-el-Kebir at 6 am tomorrow.  I got 'em.</p>
