<p>May, 1915<br/>Wednesday 5<br/>Hospitals all full<br/>[indecipherable]<br/>Today we got orders for dismounted drill, Horses to be sent afterwards, as no use for mounted men at present at &ndash; a good deal of heart burning at losing horses but glad to get into action.<br/>Sent all surplus horses to depot &ndash; Where we go I cannot tell you, but it is not far away, &amp; when we go will be advised by letter to you.</p>
