<p>July 1918<br/>Tuesday 30</p>
<p>Ribemont. Bty Position<br/>At 12.30 am I was awaiting to hear S.O.S. but it did not <br/>come off. Weather all day muggy and over warm.<br/>Fritz commenced to make things very lively indeed. O.P. wire went in twelve places and Smithy  and I mended it. A deuced amount of gas prevalent.<br/>During afternoon line again drifted in three places. Smithy could not come with me unfortunately so I went by myself. Clive spent day at O.P. B.Malcolm and Walker came up from W.L.</p>
