
Monday 10 December 1917.
Turned out at 5.30 am. A mouthful to eat. Saddle up, in full gear and on the trek once more.
Crossed Waddi Guzzi at 11 am - 12 noon and travelled to camp site near Shellah Junction, where we bivouaced.
Passed enemy fortifications near used during early part of stunt.
In bivouac at our destination at 3 pm.
What a satisfaction known only to those who came through with the stunt.
