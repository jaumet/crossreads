  
 Sunday 11 August 1918 Met Mrs. H. in morng.  Went for a stroll.  Very warm in the Valley.  Hear that we are not to go back until enquiry is over. 
 Monday 12 August 1918 Still hanging about.  Went for a walk in morng. with Mrs. Hendry.  Very hot in the Valley.  Bridge & Billiards all day. 
 Tuesday 13 August 1918 Usual work in the morning.  Concert in eveng. at Hospital & anor. one at the Y.M.C.A.  Reced. my orders for returning to Blighty. 
 Wednesday 14 August 1918 Embarked at 9 a.m. & laid in the harbour at Havre all day.  Left at 9.30 & made a good crossing. 
 Thursday 15 August 1918 Reached S'hampton at 4 a.m. & landed at 7.30.  Train to Warminster at 9.10 & reached home at 11.30.  Rup was out but came in for lunch. 
 Friday 16 August 1918 Met Johnny Watt at OTB & he came along for tea.  Nothing doing & no work if I can help it.  Tennis in afrnoon. 
 Saturday 17 August 1918 Into Warminster to get my new glasses repaired.  Went in and back by 'bus.
