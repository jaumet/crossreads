<p>Things forgotten</p>
<p>At Port Said the natives made a disturbance by refusing to carry on with the coaling of the boat.  One of the ships cooks forgot himself &amp; emptied a bucket of hot water over the side &mp; it went all over the niggers below.  He thought he was still at sea.  The strike was soon quelled &amp; the work went on again.  The water police were kept hard at work chasing the natives from the side of the boat they were trying to sell things to us &amp; the authorities had forbidden it.  A Sentry was given orders to fire on them if they did not obey his first warning he gave</p>
