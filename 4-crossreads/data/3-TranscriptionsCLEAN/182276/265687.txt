  
 9th. M.G. Coy No 12 Intelligence Report from 6am 11th to 6am 12th:- Right Sector:- No 5 gun fired - indirect - from I 10 D 42/75 on Level Crossing I 12 D 04/64 - 6 pm to 7.45 - unobserved. No 6 gun fired from I 10 B 26/11 - [works?] round RUELLE DE LA NOIX- midnight to 1.45 pm- unobserved. Line quiet all day- Aerial activity on part of enemy, one machine flew very low - 200 ft- over HAYSTACK FARM and No 6 gun position. Left Sector:- No 7 - C.F - fired on C. Central - Tramway & Trenches - No 8 - S.P.X - fired on I 6 a 5/0 Road & Dump at INTACT FARM. Enemy plane s  seen to fly low over our line on right sector- fired at by rifles & Lewis guns 
 B.J Carey Capt 9th M.G. Coy 12.12.16
