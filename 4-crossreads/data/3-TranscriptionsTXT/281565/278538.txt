Farming &amp; fruit growing appeared to be the sole pursuits of the rural inhabitants, &amp; the old cow, so inevitable in Australian scenery, disappearing practically altogether.  Cherry orchards appeared a mass of white blooms while peach trees were also showing in blossom.  At 9 a.m. on Tuesday morning we ran into Lyons where we stopped for about an hour &amp; breakfasted.  Hot water was supplied by the French Red Cross, so that we were able to make tea &amp; bovril.  Between Lyons &amp; the Loire beautiful stretches of dark green spruce forests alternated with the farmlands.
