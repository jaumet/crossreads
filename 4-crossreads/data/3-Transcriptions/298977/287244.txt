<p>-2- 3rd July  1916.</p>
<p>to Ralph Mace.  It certainly was a fine tribute to a man who lived like a man and died like a man.</p>
<p>Am sorry to hear Mrs. Forrester has not been too well.  Give her my kindest regards and hope she will soon be about again.</p>
<p>Hope Father is O.K. again now, I'll have to take him away for a holiday when I get back.  I'll have a lot of Army money to draw and bust.</p>
<p>With love to all at home<br/>From<br/>Your loving Son<br/>Roy.</p>
