Wich "Promisse" he also keept.  This wher two fences covered the entire lenghth with Barb Wires &amp; each 6 feet apart. To this sort of "Visits" the Internees dit not comply with &amp; simply refused to see their "Wifes &amp; Family". So they wher all forced to return to Camp without even hawe had the chance of having a fond handshake, sadly to the dismay of Internee &amp; his Family.
