<p>Sunday 6 May</p>
<p>Fine but windy.<br/>Claude Le Quesne paid me a surprise visit.<br/>Football match in evening V Rugby team resulting in a win for us.<br/>Match V 15th F. Amb. postponed.<br/>35 to 40 casualties in the 7th Field Amb. in the last day or so's fighting, and 15 casualties in the 14th F.A.  9 of the 7th F.A. have died.</p>
