We paper man the the point until the matter was taken up by a shiny ebony skinned Soudanese policeman in top boots a smart blue uniform it was funny to see how he dealt with the case the paper man came in for a lot of shaking and was spun round like a top two or three times he argued the point to the very last the Black gent in the boots then hustled all three across the street to an Egyptian police officer a smart looking man wearing red fez who pronounced judgement on the case. the paper man came off second best and was sent off at the double receiving a kick behind and a slash across the back from the officer's cane. this appears to be the method used by the native police to the natives.
These police are a smart body of men to look at they generally carry a short sword. I saw three men tried by an officer here in camp for selling cigarettes without a licence. the three were lined up against a wall after a deal of jabbering each received a smack alongside the ear and a kick behind were sent running somewhere out over the desert.
