<p>17-2-1917<br/>Wet and muddy, a big straff on 5-30 a.m.  Big fog all day.  The dump blown up yesterday &pound;1,000,000 damage done, several men killed and wounded.</p>
<p>18-2-1917<br/>An extremly quiet day, nothing doing, plenty of mud about.  The Baziltin  dump almost blown up, shells all around.  Lieut. Benham is in charge.</p>
<p>19-2-1917<br/>Fine but foggy;  the mud is increasing.  A few shells over at 8-30 p.m.  Lot of men going sick.  The price of eatables very high, small tin of pineapples Fr. 1-60, a large tin 2-6, a small</p>
