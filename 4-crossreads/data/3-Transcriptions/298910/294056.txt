<p>3/9/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Very fine day &ndash; Our artillery giving Fritz Pepper, his replies were very vicious, but with poor results &ndash; 15 out 25 duds. Shifted over &amp; relieved the squad at Foray House &ndash; very poor dug out &amp; leaking badly. No calls.</p>
<p>4/9/16  Monday</p>
<p>Raw day &amp; drizzling rain &ndash; Received a parcel of sox &ndash; soap &ndash; chocolates &amp; Handkerchiefs from Mrs O'Kelly. Capt Bullin came out &amp; paid us 40 francs. Fritz peppering the saps &amp; coming dangerously near our dug out &ndash; terrible lot of duds. We have a dud on exhibition 60 pounder &ndash; nice souvenir to think it just missed us. Lucky enough to score two good books. The Sowers &amp; Countess Tekla &ndash; to keep me occupied when not busy.</p>
<p>5/9/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Drizzling rain &ndash; Fairly quiet till late evening, then things fairly lively. Had a few calls about midnight.</p>
