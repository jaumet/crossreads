<p>The 3rd Army north of Arrar, took 10,000 prisoners &amp; advanced to a depth of three miles &ndash; The Canadians assisted in taking Vimy Ridge.</p>
<p>Wednesday  11/4/17</p>
<p>Boisterous &ndash; Rain &amp; Snowstorms night &amp; day. Squaring up the Camp &ndash; The order still to be in readiness. 4th Div &ndash; we heard took several Villages. But were repulsed with losses. Had a bit of a sing song at night &ndash; one of the Boys found a Mandolin in one of the Villages and as there are many players we have some fairly good music.</p>
<p>Thursday  12/4/17</p>
<p>Dull to raining &ndash; a little snow &ndash; last nights fall of snow was the heaviest for a long time. Odd jobbing about the Camp &ndash; We have not been called out yet &ndash; but we are still in readiness. Got a letter from Bob Plummer.</p>
<p>Friday  13th</p>
<p>Rather a nice day &ndash; sunny all day. Just fatiguing about the Camp. General</p>
