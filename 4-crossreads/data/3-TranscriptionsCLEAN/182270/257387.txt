SUNDAY, MARCH 16, 1919.
1026      16
Fine & cool. I was orderly
officer for the aft of the ship. Its
not a bad job though as the
duties are light. Had a
boxing tournament in the
afternoon & it was very good.
Also had arough dance at
night, but as there were only
about nine ladies it was
not much of a success.
Days run 330 miles
