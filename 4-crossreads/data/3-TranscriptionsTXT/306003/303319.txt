
14-4-16Fri 14thThe wind has dropped & the weather is glorious today   Two up is played all day here by some of the lads;  A Troop celebrated the birthday of a couple of their men tonight & the majority of them drank more beer than they could very well carry & were rather noisy as they had an impromptu concert
