
27.10.16Fri 27thParaded to the quack this morning & was much relieved when he said it was nothing serious & I would not have to go away; Another football match was played this afternoon between A & B Sqdn, a win for A; applications have been called for, for transfers to R.A.F.C. needless to say a good many applications went in but I did not apply as I am in hopes of a Commission in this Bde before long.  tradesmen, clerks etc were called for.
