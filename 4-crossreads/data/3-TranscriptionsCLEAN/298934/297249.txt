We also make small wells on the beach & drain the sea water.  The transports also condense water & this is brought ashore in kerosene tins or by water boats.  We get rum given to us (3 spoonsfull 1 tot per man) practically every day & much appreciate it.  We are having some tobacco & cigs. given us.
