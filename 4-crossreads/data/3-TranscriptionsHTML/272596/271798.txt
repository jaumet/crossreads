<p>An airman came over &amp; done all sorts of fancy tricks &amp; waved his hand to us in greeting they still have an aerodrome in the desert.  I took a few snaps coming through.</p>
<p>This afternoon Bill May turns up   came down by train &amp; then came across in a picket boat  what a tale Bill had to tell  Braithwaite &amp; I were very pleased to see him.</p>
<p>We have been taking in water &amp; goods all afternoon getting ready for our run to Colombo.</p>
<p>19th. Laid in the harbour just off Suez last night, about 6 oclock we got 70 more men aboard nearly all light Horse from Palestine all the morning the ship has been taking in stores, "Gyppos" come out with their boats &amp; our lads have great fun with them, for they are just like a lot Kiddies</p>
