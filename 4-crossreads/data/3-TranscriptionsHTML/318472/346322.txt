<p>7 April 1918<br/>Low Sunday<br/>1 after Easter</p>
<p>Very showery weather. Nothing of much account doing in the afternoon took a walk with Sgt. Lench into Barford. Had a lunch there &amp; got back to camp about 4 P.M.</p>
<p>1918 April 8<br/>Monday<br/>Same old camp routine Still on massage treatment Went Pictures at night Nothing of importance doing.</p>
