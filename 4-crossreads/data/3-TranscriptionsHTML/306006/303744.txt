<p>20-2-17<br/>lucks way; The Sigs are getting a good deal of work now, &amp; it will be heavy when 5 of us are away; A few reinforcements arrive ed for us this evening including one Sig; Frost &amp; Leask are going in at 8 oclock tomorrow to go on leave; Frost's Commission is expected at anytime.</p>
<p>21-2-17<br/>Wed 21st.<br/>I went in to El Abd with the men going on leave today &amp; were in there at 10 oclock; had dinner with Tancred, Poile, McKeown,&amp; Frost. The men going on leave caught a train leaving Abd at 12.30 &amp; as their leave does not commence till tomorrow morning they are getting "Backsheesh" as they will get to Cairo some</p>
