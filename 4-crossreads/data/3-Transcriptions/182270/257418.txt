
<p>1057</p>
<p>WEDNESDAY, APRIL 16, 1919.</p>
<p>47</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. We lay out in</p>
<p>Quarintine Bay all day. On</p>
<p>deck reading &amp; playing</p>
<p>games all daa. Got no</p>
<p>letters or news from home at</p>
<p>all. One of the troops on</p>
<p>board hung himself during</p>
<p>the night.</p>
