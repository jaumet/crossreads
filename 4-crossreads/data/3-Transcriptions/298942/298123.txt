<p>Raining &amp; lack of preparation made arrival miserable, &amp; caused me to address Sergt [indecipherable] (rejoined) &amp; incidentally Major Routledge (unwittingly) rather bluntly not to say rudely.</p>
<p>Got a shake down &amp; a cold feed in a barn &amp; slept two nights' sleep in one, making a good job of it.</p>
<p>Spent yesterday (Wednesday) in cleaning up.</p>
