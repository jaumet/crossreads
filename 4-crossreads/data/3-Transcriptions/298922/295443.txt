<p>Friday 27 April 1917</p>
<p>SHELLAL</p>
<p>On outpost again last night, it actually rained &amp; after the heat turned quite cold. I washed &amp; shaved this morning. Sullivan of Deniliquin, Bullock driver is my Batman now.</p>
<p>We are camped in a rugged water worn WADI but no water.</p>
<p>Hear that a mail is in Camp.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]  My substantive Rank of MAJOR came through in Orders so am Temporary no longer &amp; if go sick or wounded I do not revert. Don't know when I'll post this.</p>
