<p>16 killed British 55 wounded 3 killed Turks brought into Cairo</p>
<p>Feb 10th<br/>Lousy dodge drill lost &amp; cannot carry pack</p>
<p>12th<br/>Fordham dies with smallpox re vaccinated</p>
<p>13th<br/>Shamfight back of Pyramids inspected by Major General Maxwell</p>
<p>17<br/>Vaccinated again Day off after night of outpost duty</p>
<p>25<br/>Visited Mosque seen Jacobs well, long chat with Indian wounded from Suez, Received pull through and coins from Indians Visited Kursall and Museum</p>
<p>Feb 28<br/>Struck camp 6.30 AM taken off prison Guard, cleaned up all day, left Mena Camp 8 PM. Marched in to Kas. El. Nil Barracks arriving 1. PM. Bivouacted</p>
<p>March 1st<br/>Left barracks at, 3 AM. marched to Central Station embarked left 5 AM. for Alexander good trip arrived 11. AM hung up outside station for one hour, embarked on Ionian left gear and went for a trip around  Alexander, took on 100 tons firewood</p>
<p>March 2nd<br/>Sailed from Alexander noon toward Dardanelles conditions on board awful, packed 2,500 soldiers, had to eat sleep and do everything on decks no hammock, terrible  poor tucker, Indians and Irish leave for Marselles U.S.A</p>
