By this time the moon was well up & the picture we cut would be more easily imagined than described. We had Coffee & milk & it was great also French bread & issue butter & biscuits, I don't remember ever enjoying a supper so much before. Jimmy Lean sprang a cigar each coming back & the three of us seated in the front looked the real thing. We arrived back about 10 pm after a very enjoyable evening.
