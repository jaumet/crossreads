<p>driven mad by requisitions for men he couldn't supply &amp; so on.<br/>My chief, the D.S.M., serenely told me to draw on the remaining make shift companies for stop gaps.  For instance I could squeeze a corporal for the guard out of C. Company, &amp; even rake up a few duffers out of D. Company to help the Quartermaster.  If three men were told off to a job that savoured of down right solid work they would languish seat themselves, light a "fag". Each one murmuring "No good to me."   Coaxing might succeed but bluff or threats were of no avail,</p>
