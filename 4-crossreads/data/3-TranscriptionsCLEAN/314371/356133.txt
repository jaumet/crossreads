
About 7 o'clock I started for the clearing Stn.Went from there by motor to Bapaume where we spent the night.
Wed. 16thAt 9 a.m. we were put aboard the hosp. train, made a very slow passage to Boulogne where we were carried by motor to No. 3 Canadian Gen. Hosp.
Thur. 17thAfter a good sleep woke up feeling real good & Hungry.After breakfast the doctor had a look at us & we had our hurts dressed.The weather is perishingly cold again.
Fri. 18thStill resting.  Cut now almost O.K. again.
Sat. 19thSame again, getting restless & want to get out.
