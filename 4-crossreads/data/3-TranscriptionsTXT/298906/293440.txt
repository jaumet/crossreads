 attacked my right flank in force but never got through our fire was too hot &amp; the men placed as it happened in the right joint before the attack began a whole crowd of people collected round us to see the fun, motor cars &amp; all were there, how some of the women screamed when the blanks began to go off, I don't know what they would do if they heard the real thing. After it was all over we marched back again, &amp; this morning there was a discussion on last nights operation in the Y.M.C.A., after both the defence  attacking force had spoken, the verdict was given to the defenders, the Colonel reckoned my force had wiped them out, (a "feather in my cap")
