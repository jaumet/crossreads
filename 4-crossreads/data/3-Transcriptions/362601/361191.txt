<p>19 April, Saturday</p>
<p>F &amp; Hot</p>
<p>Meet Hassells &amp; Les Luker also Len O'Brien. Meet Bill Allinors. I go to Bulford &amp; meet the boys in Mess. It is a lovely hot sultry day &amp; we cut out the fire. We all go to Boozer &amp; celebrate, but I have a row &amp; buzz off. I am very put out but eventually go to bed easy in my mind.</p>
<p>I see H. Hey &amp; M. taking off. C.H.H. has Sheperds Plain &amp; M.C.R.[?]<br/>20 April, Sunday</p>
<p>F but windy.</p>
<p>I go to Tidworth all morning with a victim of sand-bagging. E.T. is drunk horrible. Recd letter from Jennie. I go to T'worth Delhi after Tea with a Patient. It is a lovely ride in the cool of the evening.</p>
<p>Wrote Mum, Mr Edmonds, Con, Marg, M. McKinnon, Aunt Annie.</p>
