 How do I live? Well never mind. Thats a secret I can keep. I manage a sort of meal to find and a place where I crawl to sleep & see in a fitfull dream the wraith of a face once fair and a woman's eyes whose tender gleam once dazzled old Devil May Care. To wake & wonder what might have been  if  had the game been fairly played. To wearily fumble the tangled skein that a woman's fingers made. We waltzed together the night
