<p>out &amp; there they have perished miserably "poor devils," but I am rambling &amp; must get back to where I left off, the remnants of the Batt. charged gamely but we had not the ghost of a chance for the machine guns mowed us down in rows, I could have cried with disappointment &amp; rage when we got the order to get back to our own trenches it was the first time we ever had to acknowledge defeat &amp; I can tell you it hurt some, I can quite understand how a beaten army feels after this affair, the worst of it all was that in spite of the bravery displayed by the boys all our losse's were in vain, the truth of the matter was that they underrated the strength of the position &amp; they sent a couple of Coy. to do what it would have taken a Bde &amp; the result was that we got cut to pieces our casualties were about</p>
