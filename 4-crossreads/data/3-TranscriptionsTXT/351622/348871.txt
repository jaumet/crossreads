 8 August 1916 Tues Left Rubempyre 10.am not far out on the road the 1st Field Engineers passed us on their way back to the trenches. With them I saw Harold White just having time to bid him good-day as he passed by. Passed through [La Bicogne], Naours &amp; the big aerodrome. Arrived Canaples 5 p.m. all weary &amp; knocked up after covering [text continued at top of page] 
 9 August 1916 Wed Inspection of clothes &amp; equipment 9.30 am. We were then dismissed for the day. I enjoyed a good bath &amp; washed my shirt the only one I have. During the afternoon 13 of us were told to get ready as we were pushing off somewhere. Left Canaples by motor ambulance at 6.30 &amp; arrived Val-der Maison about an hour after. A very quiet place &amp; no shops
