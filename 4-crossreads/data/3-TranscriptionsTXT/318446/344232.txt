 2nd Feb. El Arish The Turkish 'planes came over the other morning when it was very misty &amp; dropped about 10 bombs &ndash; two in the sea. On clear days they usually come over but are kept at a distance by our A.A. guns.  I got a fright last night, I had just blown out the light &amp; turned over when I thought I heard a 'plane coming.  I heard it for about 5 mins. but did not like to say anything for fear that I had what is known here as taubitis. Finally it became unmistakeable &amp; turned out to be one of ours.
