  
 letter from Viv yesterday. Plenty of Stout & Laager in canteen sore heads tomorrow quite mild again gale blew itself out cloudy & like snow very warm to night & getting clearer 
 Thursday 17/1/18 Snowing again & getting colder lot of drunks about last night. nothing much doing deferred Pay 41-5-0 Owe 7-13-0. wrote to Viv Happy & hillarious time tonight not so many drunk to night. 
 Friday 18/1/18 cloudy & warm like rain lot of fat heads this morning mine O.K. sports to day. deferred Pay 41-6-6 Owe 7-11-10 Monsieur & I friends for life shouts every time he sees me gave him 11 dz instead of 8. Reg letter for me at Post must get it tomorrow I suppose its from Poor old Nell bless her. feel a bit squalmy. quite warm again tonight funny weather. 
 Saturday. 19/1/18 got letter from Nell with Pound 1- inside also one from Jack.  Paid Joe Dempsey 20 francs out of debt at last except for 7-9-0 owe Government deferred 41-8-0 went down to see Jim he's pretty bad & will have to go to Hospital. Writing to Nell (No 3). By the 31st of March will owe 9/- if I do not draw any money at all. the 55th won nearly everything at Brigade Boys very happy tonight so am I but not zig-zag all night when the Boys dont play up to bed 11-30
