  
 Amiens [April 1918] 4 Th Arrived at Amiens 5 A M Marched to Allonville 5 Fri  Left Allonville yesterday morning now at Bussy-Le-Daux.Blangy-Tronvill 6 Sat Just got settled last night when along comes the order to shift, Marched 10 weary miles to a Village named Brettone, then into action at Gentellen
