 Thursday 31 May 1917. 
 Up at 5 a.m. Mounted guard on No 7 post from 7 am till 9 a.m. (No 6 &amp; 7 decks aft) 
 Air very bad on No 3 troop deck. 
 Dismissed at 9.15 am. Did washing 
 Marching order parade at 1.30 pm I was minus 1 cap &amp; 1 boot, found cap later but not other boot. 
 Awning taken down altogether as a monsoonal disturbance is expected tonight. Hatchways closed over. 
 G. S. S. Crmn &amp; I went down to Engine Room, propeller shaft tube, &amp; stokehold at 6 pm. 
 Sleeping on deck &amp; chancing rough weather. 
 3 wks now since leaving Port Melbne.
