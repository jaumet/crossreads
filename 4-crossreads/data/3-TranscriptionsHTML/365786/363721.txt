<p><u>these men if we vote NO?</u><br/>To date they have done wonders, their bravery is unquestioned &amp; their initiative &amp; grim determination unsurpassed, but our small force is only holding a very small portion of the whole front &amp; should we fail to reinforce our men, this little bit of the line, Australia's proud position at the front, will be defended by other Britishers &amp; our volunteers at the Front will be absorbed <strike>in other</strike> as reinforcements in other Imperial regiments.
