<p><u>9th AMG Coy</u> No. 45<br/>Intelligence report from 6am 8/2/17 to 6am 9/2/17<br/>The following indirect fire was carried out:-<br/><u>Gun at C 28 c 60/45</u>: on 1) X ROADS south of L'AVENTURE C30c 90/90 2) enemy communication trench C 29 b 90/35 to C 30 a 90/50 3) CROSS ROADS at C30a 15/75. 1000 rds<br/><u>Gun (FRY PAN) at C22a 75/15</u> on 1) Communication trench C23d 30/60 to C 24 c 20/60 2) BRUNE RUE Rd C 29 c 80/60. 3) CROSS ROADS at C 30 a 10/80. 1500 rounds.<br/><u>Gun (SS88) at C16d 15/40</u> on 1) XRds Fme du <strike>CHASTEL</strike> Gd. VERQUIN C12b 90/60. 2) LIENART Fme Dump C18b 80/55 3) X ROADS C18d 60/50. 1000 rounds fired between 6.30pm to 8.30pm.<br/>One gun fired throughout the night on gaps cut in enemy's wire.<br/>Visibility too poor for daylight firing.<br/>Quiet during day except for hostile bombardment on [Locs?] 10 11 &amp; 12, lasting from 2pm till 3.15pm &amp; 4pm till 4.30pm. Machine guns fairly quiet during night.<br/>Wind N.E. fairly strong. Visibility poor in morning, fair in afternoon.<br/>Frost continues</p>
<p>D. Whitehead<br/>Lieut<br/>9th. AMG Coy</p>
