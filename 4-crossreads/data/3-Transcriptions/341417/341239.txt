<p>Stacks of stores near the jetty were saturated with paraffin &amp; made ready to fire.<br/>At midnight on 16th Dec these caught fire by unknown means &amp; made a great blaze. It was smouldering when we left 3 days later. Probably it was set fire by some thoughtless person for the sake of excitement or else by a shell.<br/>Anyhow it looked as though all arrangements would be upset for it lit up all that portion of the shore where we were getting men &amp; stores off.<br/>It could also be taken that we were intentionally destroying stores.<br/>Although the scheme was arranged no dates was given but only that the final stage of "shooting the moon" would be spread over 2 nights.</p>
