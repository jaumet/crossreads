<p>30.1.15</p>
<p>of Cairo, but went through only the outskirts.  we arrived at Heliopolis &amp; found the lines down, mess rooms up &amp; everything ready for us, even had all our tents up;  I believe there are about 10,000 Terriers here &amp; about 10,000 colonials at present;  Heliopolis appears to me to be a much better place than Maadi &amp; a large skating rink is very handy &amp; Heliopolis seems more of a town than Maadi.</p>
<p>Sun 31st<br/>No Church Parade this morning, applied for pass out from dinner time but could not get one so put the afternoon &amp; evening in writing;  I received a letter from mother</p>
