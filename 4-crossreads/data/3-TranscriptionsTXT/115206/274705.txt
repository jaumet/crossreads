 May 1918 Wednesday 15 
 Bussy-la-Daours Weather &ndash; very hot, and muggy, evening fairly mild. Wrote one or two letters. Acted as guide to several Tommies.  2 98 DAC changed places at 6 pm with the 13th FAB. Paid 60 francs and had a run across to Villers Bretonneaux. At 9pm Fritz visited Amiens, Querrie and Daours. Had a swim during afternoon. A 3rd Division chap was drowned in the big pool.
