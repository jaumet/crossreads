Still alive &amp; kicking had a little better rest last night for it ceased raining about 6, our chaps bombarded something terrific all night it was one constant roar &amp; rattle, old Fritz poked a few over among us, &amp; one 9.2 lobbed about 15 yards to the left of our little shanty throwing dirt &amp; mud all over us, it was wonderful no one was hurt for it did not lob more than 3 yards from where the 2nd Batt.
