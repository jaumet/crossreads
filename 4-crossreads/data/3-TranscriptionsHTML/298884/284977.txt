<p>Everybody seemed to be enjoying themselves a treat. Rickshaw after Rickshaw dashed past us, some on their way to the Cinnamon Gardens, some to Mount Leviniere others to Galle Face Hotel &amp; many other places. All wore a broad smile &amp; I think the natives enjoyed themselves equally as much as we did.</p>
