 &amp; bullet fire most of the time &amp; it was most interesting, but as no one owned us we were ordered to reembark for Lemnos &amp; here we are. I think we were intended for the 2nd Stationary Hospital at Lemnos Is. so please address letters to me Cp. 2nd Aus Stationary Hospital Intermediate Aus. Base Alexandria. Please Forward. till  forward  further notice. I have a friend with the Stationary hospital &amp; if I am not there he will redirect them. I trust you are all well &amp; happily at home at the Wurley. Wish to God this awful war would end.
