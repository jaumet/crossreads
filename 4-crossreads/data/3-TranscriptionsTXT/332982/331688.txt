
Sunday 3rd Jan 1917About 4pm the H.M.S.Kent left us     then the Transports all went separate routes. At five o'clock 10 destroyers came alongside but only two remained, the remainder going with the other transports.Mon 8th Jan 17Very rough we have to hang on to the rails, as it is impossible to stand without holding on to something.About 8.30pm we sighted the first lighthouse in England
