<p>Mon 13th Jan. 1919<br/>Kit inspection and filled in form for the Repatriation Dept.</p>
<p>Sat 18th Jan.<br/>Arrived Albany 11am. Laborers refused to coal our boat, as they have got the "Flu" scare. Not a case of "Flu" on the "Nestor"</p>
<p>Sun 19th Jan<br/>Some of the Troops coaled the ship. They put 600 tons on board, Finished about 4pm.</p>
<p>Mon 20th Jan\W.A. Boy, disembarked at 6-15pm and we sailed at 6-30pm.</p>
<p>Wed 22nd Jan<br/>I have got the job of Table Maid, otherwise known as Mess Orderly for A.7. table, Cutting up the meat and washing up.</p>
<p>Thur 23rd Jan Medical Inspection (B. Class) Blanket &amp; Hammock inspection in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Fri 24th Jan.<br/>Sighted land at 3-30pm and passed</p>
