<p>12 Thursday<br/>Showery day.  Feeling very tired but struggled on at novel &amp;  just a little shorthand  Walked with Bessie Allin before supper.</p>
<p>13 Friday<br/>Had a little walk with Miss Woledge.  Worked as above.  Wet afternoon.</p>
<p>14 Saturday<br/>Wet day.  Had to stay indoors  Worked on shorthand &amp; novel.  Leaves coming down thickly on the lawn.  Creepers turning red.</p>
