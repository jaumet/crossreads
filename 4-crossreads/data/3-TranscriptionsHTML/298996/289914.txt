<p>6.6.15<br/>We had to stand to arms again last night because <strike>they</strike> we expected the Turks to <strike>counter</strike> attack us, but they did not so we had a pleasant sleep when we got the chance, mind you we sleep in our equipment. Church parade this morning. Nothing doing this afternoon other than the Destroyer giving the Turks on the Left flank a shell or two.</p>
