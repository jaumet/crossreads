<p>July 1917<br/>quite a climbe. A splendid view from here. Have just 6d left &amp; its borrowed</p>
<p>Tuesday 24th<br/>On butchers fatigue so did not get off untill midday. Saw Picture Gallery not much and <strike>Old</strike> Koopman De Wit Collection old Dutch articles not interesting Had a Hot Bath best item on the trips programme Payed 10/- today. Needed muchley</p>
<p>Wednesday 25th<br/>Moved out from the Wharf this morning &amp; have been anchored all day It has been raining &amp; every one is sleepy. Take it all round men have behaved very well</p>
<p>Thursday  26th<br/>Still in harbour. Showery all day. Two Parraides. Fishing chief occupation. Fair number caught. Cook charges 3' each for cooking them.</p>
