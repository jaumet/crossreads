<p>24 Oct 16<br/>Anchored at Rosyth with fleet.</p>
<p>25 Oct 16<br/>Anchored at Rosyth. Watch   [indecipherable]  in all stokeholds, in readiness to out at two hours notice.</p>
<p>26 Oct 16<br/>Went to sea in afternoon watch &amp; patrolled all night, accompanied by two destroyers.</p>
<p>27 Oct 16<br/>Returned to Rosyth &amp; anchored during forenoon watch. Coaled ship from Collier Ursside.</p>
<p>28 Oct 16 )<br/>30 Oct 16 )  Anchored with Fleet at Rosyth</p>
<p>31 Oct 16<br/>Coaled ship, took in 67 tons. All fleet, Battle Cruisers, Light cruisers &amp; destroyers, heaved anchor &amp; proceeded to sea in first dog watch.</p>
