
Sept 15th The 1st A.L.H. Brigade moved out of camp at 7 p.m. for Bir-el-ab, 22 miles distant where we arrived at 4 a.m. on the 16th inst. Early on the night of the 16th the Second & Third L.H. Brigades moved out from their camps near Bir-el-ab to a point about 22 miles East. The 1st Brigade left Bir-el-ab at mid-night on the 16th inst. but being only the supports to the reconnoitering force went only about 14 miles East, where we took up our position & waited in case we should be required.[Bir-el-ab should be Bir-el-abd and is now Bi'r al abd]
