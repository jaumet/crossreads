I believe it was very amusing this morning an English Colonel came over with Cox &amp; wanted 3 Sqdn of Light Horse to protect working parties in from of our lines.Cox blew him up properly &amp; told him a Troop was quite sufficient; He then wanted to have our camp shifted as he said we interfered with the right flank of the 127th Bde &amp; Cox told him to go to Jericho.
