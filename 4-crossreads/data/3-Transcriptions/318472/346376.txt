<p>31 May 1918<br/>Friday<br/>Left Dinton about 9.30 &amp; got to London about midday. Did some business went to see Mr Smith at the office and later went to Leytonstone to see Mrs Smith &amp; Daisy. Stayed with the Smiths for the evening &amp; left for Glasgow at 11.45 P.M.</p>
<p>1918 June 1<br/>Saturday<br/>Arrived Glasgow about 9.45 a.m. Had a clean up &amp; did a bit of business &amp; then went to the office to see my friend Mary Boyd When I got there found that she and her two sisters a friend had a trip arranged to Gourock and expected me to go too so away I went nothing loth. Got back to Glasgow about 9.45 P.M. after an enjoyable day. The girls too enjoyed themselves thoroughly. Very warm weather.</p>
<p>2 June 1918<br/>Sunday</p>
