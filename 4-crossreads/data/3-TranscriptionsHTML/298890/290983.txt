<p>4.Thursday<br/>Went down town to Kodak for prints. Evelyn Isilt &amp; M.M. Moffatt for lunch. Had to type Lyceum  general stuff  for Isilt  got none of my work done consequently.</p>
<p>5. Friday<br/>Cooked dinner for Mme Jallay. Rev. Arnold came for afternoon tea. got nothing done.</p>
<p>6. Saturday<br/>Two strange girls called and asked for water. Cold showery day. Struggled vainly to get on with work. Cooking &amp; bell calls made it very unprofitable.</p>
