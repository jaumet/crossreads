Jan 21stLeft Dieran at 7 am in a ford car for Jerusalem. With a fine beautiful morning favouring, we had a fine through bumpy run to Latron which is at the foot of the hills &amp; we began the assent of the fooothills along, the road to Jerusalem after a four hours run through mountanious country we arrived @ the old city of Jerusalem. The Jerusalem of today cannot be termed a typical oriental city inside its walls its Eastern character has been generally preserved but in recent years European influence has been great.&nbsp;Without the walls a new town has grown.
