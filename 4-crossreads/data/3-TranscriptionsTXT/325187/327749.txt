 [Tuesday January 4 1916] 
 Stayed at Windsor Hotel the old Hall Porter bemoans the lack of Tourists- which were worth 250/- /- a year to him. All the survivors of the SS Persia, which was torpedoed are staying here men, women &amp; children, they are fairly cheerful but lost everything &amp; most of them had been picked up out of the water.  Returned Cairo 4 p.m. train. Cutter is here &amp; well.
