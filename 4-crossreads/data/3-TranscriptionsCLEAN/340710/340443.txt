We have just discovered that we are really going to Egypt for training, we hear it is a place called Mena near the Pyramids but that at present conveys nothing to me.  Last night we had an awful epidemic of ptomaine poisoning and about 1200 of the fellows were down to it.  I was lucky enough to escape but I was up nearly the whole night doing what I could for the poor devils.  I hear the Ras-el-Tin Lighthouse is in sight so Ill close as I want to get the first glimpse of Alexandria.  Hal thinks he has ptomaine but he hasn't, he's eaten too much Turkish delight.
