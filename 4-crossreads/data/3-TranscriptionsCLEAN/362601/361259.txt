  
 30 August, Saturday 
 I stay Home at night but Dad, Bess & I drive to Dee Why in afternoon & go walk on beach. We do not notice that the House has been moved. 
 I am very tired & Bess is not well. Dad, George, Mum & Lily go to theatre. 
 31 August, Sunday 
 Bess is down. I potter in garden all morning. We go gathering wild-flowers in the afternoon. E & E come out for tea.
