 10 a.m. passing through Villiers arriving at "H.Q's" &ndash; Bertangles at midday. The glorious weather the only saving quality &ndash; After dinner we erect bell tents and once again dwell under canvas. May 19th Sabbath &ndash; Letters to Home. "Elm Bank" &amp; Wimbledon &ndash; Again meet Bill Smith &ndash; Jack Shaw &amp; Algie Cooper &ndash; Original Poems &ndash; Clean &amp; Pack costumes etc &ndash; Our new camp is in an ideal spot.
