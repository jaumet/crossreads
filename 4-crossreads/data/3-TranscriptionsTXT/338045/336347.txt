 Nov 4th &ndash; Saturday 
 Still raining &amp; cold, but we are comfortable. Rest all day. Very nice sister in my ward &amp; have a long chat with her. Play in theatre rather good. 
 Nov 5th &ndash; Sunday 
 Raining all day &amp; winds blowing like fury. Writing &amp; rest all day, not wishing to venture out.
