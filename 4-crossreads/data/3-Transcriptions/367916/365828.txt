<p>If we hold him now we may at any time look for a very attractive peace offer. I doubt if we can yet drive him very far. Only very foolish people insist on having it both ways. Had we driven him as he has driven us our tails would be scraping the stars.</p>
<p>Depend upon it we are in a pretty groggy state in France today. Our wastage must have been appalling &amp; our loss in material is beyond calculation. There is on that front a very wide gap between the strength to resist &amp; the power to drive; or on the enemy's side the incapacity for a triumphant offensive pushed right home</p>
