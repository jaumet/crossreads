<p>1-6-16<br/>but only killed one of ours although they dropped within a few yards of some of them &amp; there was some very amusing incidents as both men &amp; horses were knocked down from the shock &amp; as some were mounted there were some comical spills;  Reid led the pack horse off the lines &amp; I took both his horse &amp; my own, but had to let Reid's go early in the game &amp; had an interesting time holding my own as none of the horses had bridles on, but were led from the head stalls &amp; scores of horses soon got free &amp; stampeded across the desert;</p>
<p>The sixth &amp; seventh bombs were dropped close to the anti-aircraft gun but did not get a bully;  The eighth bomb also fell amongst the 2nd &amp; 3rd Regts &amp; was the last &amp; the Taube then made off &amp; we returned to our lines to find 4 horses between us &amp; the wireless that were wounded in the 2nd Regt &amp; managed to go a few yards before dropping were being finished off with bullets;  We had breakfast &amp; then</p>
