<p>[The beginning of this letter appears to be missing.]<br/>Still any old time will do &amp; the sooner the quicker.  I've had quite enough soldiering.  There was a stunt today, very successful.  I saw several Hun prisoners, poor brutes.  I wonder how things will be when this reaches you.</p>
<p>Oh I wish you knew the Colonel, he's a trick.  I think he's just the thing, witty &amp; musical &amp; humorous.</p>
<p>Oh about my brother, I'm really rather worried, you know he told me he hadn't been well &amp; he'd met a Mrs. Fisher who had been good to him &amp; had him week ending &amp; dining &amp; theatreing.  I imagined a portly kind hearted middle</p>
