<p>The war.  By reducing our forces at the Front we reduce both our own power in the peace negotiations &amp; Britain's strength in any future conflict.  To stay at home &amp; allow our foreign enemy to grow in striking power is to commit national suicide.  In modern war we can only beat the enemy by seeking him out &amp; destroying him.<br/>Therefore send Reinforcements to France.<br/>The majority has no right to compel the minority to fight.  But is not the principle of compulsion applied to all citizens in every direction?
