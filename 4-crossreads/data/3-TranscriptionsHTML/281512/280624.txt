Afternoon went to Steenvorde. The darkroom and living quarters are nearly finished &amp; I intend taking them over tomorrow morning.  Weather just as bad as it can be. Incessant rains &amp; wind, &amp; the roadways quagmires with the grinding of the  continuous procession of transport lorries &amp; troops.</p>
<p>30th August<br/>Morning packing, &amp; forwarding my equipment to Voormezeele. Afternoon, went to the [blank] with Major Gibbs to take photographs for instructive purposes. This section of our forces has an enterprising Major, who is always on the alert for new &amp; original methods</p>
