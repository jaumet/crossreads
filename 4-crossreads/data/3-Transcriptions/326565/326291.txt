<p>Saturday 22/1/16<br/>Mail from home.  Col Shepherd arrived to take command of 8th Field Ambulance.<br/>Excitement at night when HMS Implacable passed up canal. Looked fine throwing her searchlights.</p>
<p>Sunday 23/1/16<br/>Sargeant of 29 Btn Camping here, killed by train at Serapeum Station.</p>
<p>Monday 24/1/16<br/>Exceptionally cold morning.  Discharged from hospital, after 3 weeks detention with mumps.</p>
<p>Tuesday 25/1/16<br/>Coldest morning to date, rained heavily</p>
