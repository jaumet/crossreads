 even though often hard worked of course we all are inured to Rifle fire &amp; the fall of Bullets &amp; occasionally shell &amp; shrapnell fire too. I must say I most distinctly do  not  like shrapnell &amp; dive for shelter when I hear it comming. Sniping if we are at long Range is treated by all with Contempt but if our ardent friend is at short Range then one &amp;  all are wary &amp;  so Casualties are avoided by care &amp;  common sense I trust you &amp; the Dr. are both well. Diarrhoe seems to be the chief complaint if a man is sick, I am glad to say that I have kept well up to the present we have a Dentist here &amp; I have had the novel experience of having a Tooth stopped in the open [with] the Trenches active 
 Grace will give you my address Best wishes to you all G R Short
