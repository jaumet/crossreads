  
 Saturday 1 December 1917. 
 "Jacko" came over with 2 Taubes which 2 anti-aircraft guns which our troops brought up during night opened on him. His long range guns then opened all round us. A.S.C. officer killed 2 men  & 2 camels  also and 3 wounded. 1 of our camels at the dump killed. 
 Sent 2 Field Service cards acknowledging receipt of 2 parcels. 
 On picquet till 6 pm.
