<p>We passed through Pont Remy Longpre Ailly Sur Somme, Haugest St Roche and finally pulled up at Amiens where we had a truck taken off which had a squeaking wheel and was red hot and smoking. <strike>A</strike> Had it been left on much longer it must have set the truck on fire. A new truck was put on in its place and we started off again about 3 p.m.<br/>From here onwards for a considerable distance the ground was very familiar to almost every man on board; for we were passing through the now famous <strike>f</strike> battle- ground of Villers Bretonneux, the Waterloo of the German Army</p>
