<p>bright full moon<br/>The hun has been very active all the night. He was one plane every quarter of an hour from 7. till 12. &amp; dropped a great number of bombs Scores of machine guns rattled at him but seemed to do little damage. He shelled <strike>use</strike> at us occasionally. My batman happened on the rum ration for several HQ men this afternoon &amp; felt "another little nip wouldn't do him any harm" till he was awfully familiar with all &amp; sundry, and finished up by spending the night in the Clink leaving my bed not made and things in disorder generally</p>
<p>Friday Oct 30th<br/>The war situation is not very bright</p>
