Dec 17th  The weather was wild & snowing in the morning - I had to get a tow to start my engine again, but carried on with the Supplies - Fritz is bombing near here very heavily - our guns are bombarding continuously -
 
Dec 18th  Fine day but a bit of wind made it very cold - I got a tow to start my engine again, after the engine is warm, I can start it by hand. Our guns are still hard at it - To-morrow we draw at Steenwerck railhead as the Brigade is moving towards De Suel - The 1st D.S.C. took the place of the 5th D.S.C. & we remained attached to them.
 
Dec 19th  Beautiful day but cold, freezing all day - We drew to-day at Steenwerck . the brigade moved to-day - 106th & B.A.C. up at Neuve Eglise & 16th 17th & 18th Batteries at De Seul & on Bailleul.Armentieres road near La Creche - We did'nt get back to park till 5.P.M. The 12th Brigade moved into the waggon lines which the 6th vacated -
 
Dec 20th  Very cold, ice bearing everywhere there was a thick rhyme on the trees all day. I went up to K column
