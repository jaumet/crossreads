<p>by the morning I'll have about half my pillows &amp; brooms, buckets etc.  "San fairy anne" as the froggies say which means "what does it matter.  They can't make me pay &pound;200 &amp; men don't eat buckets brooms &amp; pillows.  Ellis has gone to a whist Drive to-night but I am orderly Sergt. Major so I cannot go out.  Last night a party of "diggers", Australian soldiers for your information, under the management of Stan McKay who produced Bo-Peep</p>
