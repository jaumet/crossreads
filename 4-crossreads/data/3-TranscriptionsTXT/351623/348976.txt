 10 April 16 Anchored at Castries 
 11 April 16 Left Castries St Lucia in morning watch &amp; went to Martinique. French Cruiser Conde is here. Four of our ships company, that we left in Bermuda &amp; Jamaica, came round to Martinique on the Conde &amp; they came aboard today. Left Martinique for Trinidad. 
 12 April 16 Arrived &amp; anchored a few miles off Port o Spain Trinidad. Done sub-calibre firing at anchor today, &amp; also night firing. 
 13 April 16 Run two torpedoes during morning watch. Came up close to Port o Spain &amp; anchored. Filled up with coal, from lighters.
