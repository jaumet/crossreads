<p>March 1918<br/>Friday 15</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Another fairly quiet day. Weather splendid, very little hostile artillery.<br/>Hun got 1 target from 40 rds of 5.9 down the 51 way. Battery did an aeroplane shoot. <br/>Mr Mays parti to the A.T.P. 6.35pm.Fritz sent a few decent ones across.<br/>Received balance of articles from customs department.<br/>Knipe went back to W. Lines. <br/>Finished my Australia, and engrave same with penknife.<br/>Received paper form Stella.<br/>11.30 pm Fritz shelling fairly regularly.</p>
