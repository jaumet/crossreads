
June, 1915Saturday 12Came up to Alexandria on the Coast to visit our wounded. Many of the men I enrolled have lost limbs and eyes but are quite cheerful. Cooler here by 10 degrees than Cairo & the Transport A42 is in the Bay some go there tonight to seeDaisy & Joe's letter of May 9th just recd have not seen Toby Phillips yet but mentioned Jack Lorimer Glad to hear all is well & so am I.Am posting this tonight morrowLove to allF.W.
