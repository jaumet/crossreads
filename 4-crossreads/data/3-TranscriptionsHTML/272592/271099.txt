<p>was hurt, there was a dead man buried in one of these old saps, &amp; I tried to dig him out with my gas helmet on but I could not finish him I did not like the idea of driving the pick through his body you could feel the point striking his bone's so I chucked the job talk about stink "Geewhizz"</p>
<p>During the night Capt. Price came round &amp; he reckoned the work I was doing was excellent I ought to know a bit about trench building by this time for I have been long enough at it, &amp; have had a pretty varied experience of the game.</p>
