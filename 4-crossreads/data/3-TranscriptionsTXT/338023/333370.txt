 [Letter written on paper from the Australian Red Cross] Cty of Middlesex War Hospital 9/10/17 
 Dear little Mother 
 Because you see the address as above you must not let yourself imagine heaps of horrors &amp; gallons of gore &ndash; as a matter of fact your hopeful wandering boy is quite alright with the small exception of a game leg that has to be rested and occasional 'Verey Lights' in the head. These things are to be expected &amp; don't last, thank the Lord!
