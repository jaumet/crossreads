<p>January 1917<br/>Wednesday 3<br/>Still raining. Wet blankets every night. expecting to have colds and rheumatism. Well so far. Ordinary routine of camp . Rumours of big battle to come off at Rafa</p>
<p>Thursday 4<br/>Plenty of work at this QM job.<br/>Fine day but very cold.</p>
