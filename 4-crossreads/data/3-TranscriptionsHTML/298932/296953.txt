<p>GLENCORSE WOOD<br/>Friday 21<br/>The great battle over our boys triumphed right along the line. Lt Haigh wounded twice, we lost good men, it is wicked<br/>Saturday 22<br/>This battle was the 6 Brig greatest victory yet achieved by the Anzac's<br/>Battalion releived last night, now at Ypres<br/>Sunday 23<br/>Left Ypres at 4 this morn and marched to Halifax camp near "Vlaamantinghe Our casualties numbered about 250. The sights witnessed in a day on the field, are awful blood and iron, pain and suffering</p>
