<p>CALAIS<br/>Friday 9<br/>At last, I report to B.H.Q for leave to the United Kingdom my destination is  "The land of the Shamrock<br/>Saturday 10<br/>Left Godesvalle at 2 this morning arrived at Calais at 6, camped in tents, awaiting a boat and its very cold<br/>Sunday 11<br/>Left Calais by the S.S Dieppe at 8 arrived at Dover by 10 and London was reached by 1 (one)<br/>London is very quite The Trip across was rough almost everyone sick, I was not ill</p>
