 December 1915 in them here.  The sergeants mess in this camp is very good &amp; I am putting on condition daily. 
 January 1st 1916 New Years Day;  No holiday for us. We are down to hard work reorganising &amp; there is a lot of work to do.  Received more letters &amp; parcels.  Wrote to Doles.  Had a splendid dinner.  Turkey &amp; ham &amp; Xmas pudding &amp; drinks.  Many of the lads got leace &amp; went into Cairo on leave.  Met Les Whipp of Croydon Park.  He recognised me first.  Was jolly glad to see him as I hadn't met a cobber for a long time. 
 Sunday 2nd Got leave for the day &amp; with SQMS Cheater &amp; Cpl Suffolk hired a car for a fiver &amp; had a day out.  Drove into Cairo
