
14 August 1918 Wednesday (X to X crossed out)Usual training, Posted notes yesterday-   Just received word we move on Friday so once more a Mobile force.X Had letters from Mrs Cotton & Ferrer-   Nowell is lending me "after many days" to read.  He is now with an English Regt-   Australian Flying Corps entertained us with a Travelling X Pierrot Show, very good performance.X Parcel arrived safely thank you for all good things.  Oleac has cured my Skin but it recurs after some months-   I have plenty for a long time yet.X
