<p>along beautifully paved &amp; clean streets to the city.  The streets &amp; footpaths are very narrow, but as there are no trams or busses &amp; very little vehicular traffic of other descriptions, this is not a great consideration.  The business quarter is confined to one long street.  Every description of business is carried out and there is a good sprinkling of Eastern goods shops.  Went to GPO which is about as big as a NSW suburban office, &amp; then walked down Main Street which is the principal thoroughfare, to the landing stage where the bumboats from the mailboats come ashore.
