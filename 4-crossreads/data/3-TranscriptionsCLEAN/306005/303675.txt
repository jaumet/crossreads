
29-12-16Fri 29thMore rain again last night & this morning; I never get out of bed here before 7.30 or 8 oclock as I have not to attend "stables", & do not think of getting out of bed before Bat Pearson (the Sergts batman) calls me for breakfast; It has been very cold the last few days, today all the gear we left at Gererat came along & we are pretty right again for gear; Took a ride to Bde Head Qrs again this afternoon we leave here tomorrow on a 20 mile "Stunt" to Sheikh Towait.
