<p>April 1917<br/>29th Sunday.-  The section were split up in to four or five parties as started on the work which we are to do here, consisting mostly of carpentry work &amp; general accommodation for troops as the chateau is to be used as a signal school.   At dinner time we recorded our vote, for the election of candidates for the Senate &amp; House of Representatives in Australia   All Australian soldiers in France &amp; England etc are to record their vote on the matter &amp; according to general oppinion  the Ministeralists are in favour.   This afternoon we had a half holiday &amp; had a chance of looking around the country.   A couple of others &amp; myself walked out to Tootencourt &amp; Warloy.</p>
