<p>Tuesday 8 May 1917.</p>
<p>Very little drill doing but things busy about embarkation.</p>
<p>Returned stores &amp; struck some tents during morning.</p>
<p>Afternoon ―&nbsp;drill ―&nbsp;packed kit bag (universal) and placed in &quot;store&quot; for A.S.C. &nbsp;At 8pm. placed on G. S. Wagon for removal tomorrow morning at 4 a.m.</p>
<p>Advance guard for A.C.<s>B</s>.F<s>d</s>.A<strike>mb</strike>.&nbsp;detailed.</p>
<p>Wrote 10 notes&nbsp;tonight &amp; posted same.</p>
<p>A cold as result of Monday. 1st cold since last October.</p>
<p>A.M.C. (2 lots) leave for various transports.</p>
