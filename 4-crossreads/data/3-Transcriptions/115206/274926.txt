<p>December  1918.<br/>Tuesday 24</p>
<p>At Sea. and Port Said."Karoola"<br/>Weather unsettled and inclined to be very cool. After turning around all night, and we entered the port at 7.30 am <br/>Purchased a few articles and despatched three post cards to England. The boys had quite a good team in the purchasing of goods from the bumboat kings.<br/>I had quite a job as interpreter and caught quite a deal of things for nothing. <br/>R.M.S. Mandala passed out. Two wrecks just outside.</p>
