
An Afterthought  7.6.16.The mails for Australia close here as followsJune 1stJune 15thJuly 13thJuly 27thSo that after my next letter which (OV) will be dated June 14th there will be a spell of 4 weeks instead of only 2 as it is now.  You will know then if you do not receive them for 3 or 4 weeks after the 15th mail closes.I will write every week tho' in case it is alteredLove from Leo.
