<p>8.1.18</p>
<p>My dear Judge,<br/>I have just received your letter of 31st October. Mails from Australia have been rather scarce lately, I hope they will again begin to arrive with their usual regularity.</p>
<p>I am very much interested in your motor tour through Muswellbrook etc. I have covered almost the whole of that route on a pushbike and remember it very well, especially from Sydney to Maitland which I have ridden over about 7 or 8 times. I think the road from Wiseman's Ferry to Wollombi is about the worst I have ever seen. I once did the greater part of it in the dark.</p>
