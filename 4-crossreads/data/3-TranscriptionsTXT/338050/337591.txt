Jan,No parade in the morning &amp; a short route march in the afternoon.12th JanRoute march in the morning &amp; went to rifle range in the afternoon where we fired fifteen rounds each 13 th Jan,Went out to the bull ring where we did a little training out there.14th Jan.Church parade in the morning &amp; spell in the afternoon. This evening I found Reg Edols he was warned for draft tonight &amp; I believe he is going in the morning.15 th Jan.Bull Ring parade today &amp; trained there all day.16th Jan.Two short route marches today &amp; nothing else.17th Jan.Snow has been falling all day. We had clothes washing parade this morning &amp; no parade this afternoon.18 th. Jan.Bull Ring parade today &amp; tonight I got a parcel from home posted on the 10th Oct 19th Jan,Bull Ring parade again today.19 thBull Ring Parade.21st.
