<p>4/2/16</p>
<p>Dear George</p>
<p>This is the way they shave in Cairo it is no good to me, they also clean your boots going along in trams or any other thing  most of their ways are funny but you get quite used to them very soon.<br/>Fondest love to all<br/>Your loving brother<br/>Ellis</p>
