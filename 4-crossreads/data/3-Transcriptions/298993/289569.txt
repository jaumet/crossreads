<p>Saturday Dec23 16 travelled all night very cold reached destination magdoba day break  Bovocked camels and had a snack  Prepared for action we formed first line&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    screen enemy entrenched 1000 yards in front we advanced and took cover &amp; opened attack fierce fighting all day very hot had no dinner nearly hit number of times    one shot plowed up sand by my side   I changed position  took large number of prisoners artillery and [indecipherable] done good work we cleaned them all up by 5pm[indecipherable]wounded retired  mounted our camels and went back  had tea just got to feed when we was ordered to move off saddled up and moved about 2 miles had two hours sleep moved off again</p>
