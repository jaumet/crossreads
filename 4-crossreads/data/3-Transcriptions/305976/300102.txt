<p>Sunday Dec 29<br/>There is very little to do. We have made ourselves comfortable &amp; expect to have a good easy time here.<br/>Monday Dec 30<br/>Dad Williams returning to New Zealand. The boys are beginning to move for home now.<br/>Tuesday Dec 31<br/>The last day of the old year. The boys have a celebration on to night, a&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Concert party is coming over tonight.</p>
