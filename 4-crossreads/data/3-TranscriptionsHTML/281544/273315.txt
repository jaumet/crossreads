<p>&amp; I made my way to the show, having to await my turn &amp; form up in line 2 deep, after a little while the doors were opened &amp; I eventually got a Ticket for 3d and went in &amp; got a seat near one of the Windows, which were of wood &amp; opened &amp; in between each picture I would open the windows for fresh air likewise to watch the endless stream of Traffic, at one interval along came 8 Caterpillar Traction Engines drawing each a big Gun, &amp; they made a terrible noise rolling over the Cobble Stones. After seeing a good show I came out at 8 Pm &amp; made my way home after purchasing a kilo of Sugar for 2 franc 2d. &amp; I lit the fire &amp; had some Supper &amp; turned in.</p>
<p>Sunday 23rd<br/>I was booked on duty at 6 am until 4 Pm on a Emergency job but did not get a run out of the yard. During the Night there was a Collision up the line, the Armoured Eng was taking up an ammunition Train, propelling it when a light Eng coming down crashed into it, telescoping 2 trucks of Ammunition, the latter</p>
