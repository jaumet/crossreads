 December  1918. Monday 16 
 At Sea. H.S."Karoola." Weather fine and clear. Nice smooth sea and a few newcomers are noticeable on deck. Up till non today we had steamed another 326 miles for 24 hours. Played poker and lost &pound;1. Passed five ships going northl. We are at present off the coast of Spain and should reach Gibraltar by 5 pm tomorrow. George better today.
