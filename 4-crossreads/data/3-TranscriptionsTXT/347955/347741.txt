 Colonel 10.30  asked a few questions and Humed and hared not a Bad old fellow at all  asked if I was fit  I said yes Monday 26.8.18 feeling much Better to day was discharged from hospital went to Boulac Convalescent home Tuesday Aug.27.8.18 feeling Better to day there is a fair amount of fat here too and Plenty of medicene and Beer Wednesday Aug 28.8.18 feeling much Better to day went for a route march till 11.AM returned to camp 11.30.  Thursday Aug 29.8.18  had a good drink of Beer and had dinner slept all afternoon  friday Aug 30.8.18  had tea and turned in cool night.  I am feeling well Joe.M.Bolger
