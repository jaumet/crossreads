
<p>SUNDAY, JUNE 8, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Went down to the rocks</p>
<p>fox hunting but only got one.</p>
<p>The block one was seen again</p>
<p>Got the Middle paddock bore</p>
<p>going at last, &amp; left her pumping</p>
<p>Jo &amp; I went round Hut paddock</p>
<p>shooting after tea but got nothing</p>
