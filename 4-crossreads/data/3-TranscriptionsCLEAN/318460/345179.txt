
Saturday 6/1/17.16 months to day since I went into camp at Show ground.Cold, Rained all night but fine today feels like snowthat other Job no good so must stick to Police after all worst luck.Pay 2.1-1 18-19-0. Amiens bombed last night by Taubes.
Sunday 7/1/17.Nice & fine at last hope it remains soSergeant actually came out on duty last night at 8 PM. first time for months.Church parade to day.Pay 2-2.1. 19-0-0
Monday 8/1/17Pay 2-3-1. 19-1-0cold with sleet snow not far offhad a hot shower this morning & clean clothes stopped scratching for awhile, here for another fortnight I think.Football match this afternoon.Sergeant in one of his usual snaky moods again, did not appear to close up Pubs  & did not see him at 9. so went to bed at 20 Past he came in later.
Tuesday 9/1/17Snowed during the night everything white this morningA new man joined us in Viv's place.Bapaume & Peronne are being bombarded we go in again in a few days (Friday) 2 Coy's go Tomorrow our usual luck.
