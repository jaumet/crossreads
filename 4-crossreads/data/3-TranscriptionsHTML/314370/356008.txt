<p>April, 1916<br/>Friday 7<br/>More lectures &amp; description<br/>After tea went for a walk into Zeitoun along the avenue of gun trees for a breath of fresh air<br/>had a nice cup of tea &amp; cakes &amp; got home about 9 pm</p>
<p>Saturday 8<br/>Exam all morning.<br/>After dinner went to have a chat w/- dad &amp; hear that he is booked for Australia to go by the "Karoola"<br/>After that my chum &amp; I had dimmer in Town went for a chat to Patti's &amp; then finished up at the Kursaal getting home at 1.30 am</p>
