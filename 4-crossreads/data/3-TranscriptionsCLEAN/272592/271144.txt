  
 After the service was over General Birdwood presented some medals to those who had been fortunate to win them most of them were M.M.'s.   After he had finished he as per usual gave us a short talk on the war situation, to-day he told us of a great victory that had just been won by the Roumanians & Russians & he also said that he hoped the next time we went over the parapet we would be accompanied by some of these wonderful "tanks what a sensation they have caused they must be a marvellous invention 
 You should have seen the swag of papers I received today 48  differe  papers in all & from such places as Tasmania, Walcha, Armidale
