Saturday 15.6.18&nbsp; Very sick stayed in Bed, Bn. relieved
Sunday 16.6.18&nbsp; Better, joined Bn at Frechencourt&nbsp; still a bit seedy
Monday 17.6.18&nbsp; stayed in bed all day&nbsp; made appln to be returned to Australia
