<p>DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS</p>
<p>Arrived Mudros.  Bitterly cold.  Effective strength 15/11/15, 25 officers 540 other ranks.</p>
<p>Dec. 20 &ndash; 10th Batt. left Lemnos on S.S. Seang Bee.</p>
<p>29 &ndash; Arrived Alexandria.</p>
<p>30 &ndash; Arrived Tel-el-Kebir.</p>
<p>1916</p>
<p>Jan. &ndash; Tel el Kebir &ndash; Bgde. General McLagan resumed command of Brigade.</p>
<p>15 &ndash; Review by Sir Archibald  </strike>Murry</strike> Murray</p>
<p>25 &ndash; Serapeum &ndash; Moved to Serapeum.</p>
<p>28 &ndash; Habieta &ndash; 2 Coys moved to Habieta.</p>
<p>30 &ndash; Strength 27 officers 950 other ranks.</p>
<p>Feb. 1 &ndash; Bn. Head Quarters and 2 remaining Coys to Habieta building trenches.</p>
<p>9 &ndash; 10th Batt. invented sledge box for removing sand.  Water scarce.</p>
<p>11 &ndash; General Birdwood visits Brigade.</p>
<p>26 &ndash; 500 all ranks proceeded to join <strike>50</strike> 50th Bn. Major Lorenzo to command 49th Bn.</p>
<p>28 &ndash; 6 officers 461 other ranks joined.</p>
<p>March 4 &ndash; Col. Weis returned to Battalion.</p>
<p>9 &ndash; Serapeum &ndash; 10th Batt. arrived Serapeum staging camp.</p>
