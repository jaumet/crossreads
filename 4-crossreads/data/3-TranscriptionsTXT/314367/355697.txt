 I had them all in hand &amp; pushed off to the Fantome &amp; landed my sick five &ndash; I soon made them comfortable inboard &amp; gave them all chocolate &amp; milk &ndash; We've 14 in all, in the garrison hospital &ndash; I go up daily for I must keep in touch with them. The destroyers wont be here for 10 days &amp; I want them to have their men fit &amp; well &ndash; I saw Dr Martell this morning &amp; asked why the hospital was so badly organized &ndash; I said I know a man who'd get you 100 stretchers made inside 2 weeks &ndash; He said it was due to a congestion in the Administrative department &amp; they couldn't help it &ndash; I wish I were PM O &ndash; I'd alter all the organization inside a week &amp; have &amp; have my sick look &amp; be respectable in a bed &amp; not lumped on the floor anyhow &ndash; Another lovely bit of maladministration &ndash; All the garrison in Rabaul is on  half  rations &amp; no fresh meat in the place until the Eastern arrives &amp; she has not left Sydney &ndash; At a time when
