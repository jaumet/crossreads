<p>[Written on the back of the postcard on the previous page, which shows The Hard, Brightlingsea.]<br/>April 17th.</p>
<p>At the Anchor hotel I met two naval officers off U. Boats who were both caricatured on the C.P.R. Boats in British Columbia on the vary same pad I have now.</p>
<p>The beer here is excellent and drawing great fun.</p>
<p>Talk about types!!!</p>
<p>Vasco<br/>April 1917</p>
