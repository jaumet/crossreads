<p>Dec 20<br/>rumours of us going to sydney soon   letters arrive by Metunga &amp; all letters are being censored by company officers    more growls from the Troops about letters being censored   we are supposed to leave for sydney tomorrow</p>
<p>Dec 23<br/>we are still here  the latest rumour is we are going in a weeks time one of the sailors died during the night of Black water fever &amp; was buried this morning</p>
<p>Dec 24<br/>Preparing for Christmas, giving out parcels which had arrived for the men  also letters  every one  seems brighter as we are promised a Bottle of <u>Beer</u> a man on Xmas day</p>
<p>Dec 25th Xmas day<br/>We get 1 Bottle of Beer 2 of Burton ale &amp; 1 Tin of Fruit 1 Cigar &amp; 8 Packets of cigs each, we have for dinner 3 small chickens some sliced Ham, shoulder of Mutton, &amp; Xmas Pudding &amp; roast Paw Paws  we made some cornflower custard &amp; put it over the Xmas Pudding;  The day passed off nicely the boys are teaching the niggers to sing "It's a long long way to Tipperary"  God save the King &amp; other British songs</p>
