
HeliopolisEgypt.23/1/16
Dear Mrs. Read,Many thanks for your letter & card.  It was good of you to remember me & I must apologise for not having written before.  When I got back from the Peninsular there was a pile of letters for me & I have been working them off by degrees.  I have been back at work for a fortnight now & we are so hard at it that I don't get very much time to write especially as I spend most of my spare time with mother & the girls.
