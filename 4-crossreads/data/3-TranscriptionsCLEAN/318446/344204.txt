Sure to be plenty of stoush anyway.The railway embankment has now reached El Arish, where it will probably stop until they bridge the wadi.A trawler ran ashore the other night & is now lying on her side in a big surf.  Would make an excellent photo if the sun would come out.  The wind has been cold as charity lately & I am seriously considering the advisability of two shirts.
