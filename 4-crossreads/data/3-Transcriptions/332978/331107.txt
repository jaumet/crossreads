<p>July 18th 28<br/>All the wounded will be given preference, so it is very probable I will soon be in Switzerland.</p>
<p>There was a whist tournament tonight I did not play, only looked on.</p>
<p>Received 2 more letters today one from Bert dated 14/[indecipherable]/17 &amp; one from Mary &ndash;</p>
<p>July 19th<br/>Good sleep. The Beds here are very crude &amp; old fashioned, not to mention their hardness; Had a bath this morning. Received my first tobacco parcel today 1000 cig &amp; 4 ozs tobacco. Played Gallagher chess &amp; beat him. Wrote Bert no 4 letter. Wrote the Commdt, asking him to cause enquiries to be made concerning the whereabouts of Sgt Wynne, who has been missing since our stunt.</p>
<p>The new German Chancellor is to express his views today re peace terms, so all hands are looking forward for tomorrows communique &ndash; 12 Dutch Doctors are being sent to England &amp; Germany to examine all prisoners who are to be sent to Holland &amp;c.</p>
<p>My wound not healed yet, it is still sore.</p>
<p>Rained heavily today. Recd my money <strike>July 20th</strike> I requisitioned for on 13 Inst &ndash; 160 marks.</p>
