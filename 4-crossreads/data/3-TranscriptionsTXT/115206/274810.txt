 August,1918 Wednesday 28 
 Bty Position ahead of Proyart. 
 Weather fine and clear, with showers at intervals. We did practically nothing during the mornin. The gunners dad a duty shoot of 100 rounds at 4 am. The enemy has evacuated a considerable amount of property and ground. At noon we were ordered to move up to an advanced position. We were in action in front of Lappin Wood, which is 3000 yds from the front line or [indecipherable] our outposts which have penetrated beyond Heseyvillers and seems to be still going on merrily.
