
May, 1915Saturday 15Letter from Joe 13/4/15 - W'bool   "        "     Kathleen 28/3/15 so you see how erratic the Mails are probably some of yours have gone on to the Regiment.Myra Nyse very little better has been transferred to Ghezirah Palace Hospital on the Nile & is to be sent to Australia poor girl she has had a very bad time. Sincerely hope Joes' eye gets no worse, note he has sold [indecipherable] when rain does come all classes of stock will be very dear. Thank Kathleen for her letter & give Aunt Louie my love.
