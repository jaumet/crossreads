<p>Monday 27&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />Col. Featherstone. P.M.O. for Australia visited the Hospital and made an inspection.&nbsp;<br />Dined with Mrs Newmarch at the Occidental Hotel.&nbsp;<br />Spoke with General Ford and some others.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Tuesday 28&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />In town this morning called at Chem Laboratory, Med. School, and other places.&nbsp;<br />Writing, reading &amp; sleeping for the remainder of the day.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Wednesday 29&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp;<br />About hospital all day.&nbsp;</p>
