<p>The German Pilot got a bullet in his leg and was forced to land, the machine being captured intact.  This was carried out by Australian airmen.</p>
<p>30th Apr  The batt'n has been broken up and the band has been placed on the strength of 33rd, 34th &amp; 35th batt'ns for administration purposes 8 to each.  I am allotted to 34th.</p>
<p>1st May  We have been transferred to Brigade Hqrs as Brigade band and to do other duties that may be required of us</p>
