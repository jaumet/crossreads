<p>22nd.  Left Harefield this morning for London &amp; after a short run in the train arrived at Marylebone  here we caught the tube &amp; went as far as Charing Cross, changed there &amp; got out at St James from we had only a short way to walk to Horsferry Road   on arrival there we went through the usual lot of red tape before we finally drew our money &amp; got off,  after clearing Horsferry I went round to Peel House &amp; dumped my bag &amp; overcoat had a meal &amp; then struck out for Victoria, but I could not catch a train here for Eltham for I was going to see Mrs Oliver, so I had to take the tube again &amp; get to Charing Cross, had to wait a while here so I walked off up the Strand &amp; bought myself</p>
