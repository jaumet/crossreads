<p>We achieved something, but the cost was great<br/>On our left the new landing party made 4 miles inland &amp; when this letter reaches Australia &amp; reports from the Dardanelles are open &ndash;<br/>I lost [indecipherable] Kit, but that is a detail, when so many good fellows lost their lives &ndash;<br/>Now don't worry about me I'm writing in bed hence bad writing &ndash; I'll soon be about again &amp; am in good hands<br/>Love to all &ndash;Your fond son<br/>Frank<br/>Keep the same address<br/>1st L.H. Regiment &amp; Egypt</p>
