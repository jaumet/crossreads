<p>I hope some final victory comes before next Christmas.</p>
<p>I have heard nothing of Keith but am hoping very much I may see him; I had a line from Jim Lee who is at the front &amp; he is going to try &amp; look me up next short furlough. Syd Lee was killed not long ago: so was Young Woodhill of Wentworth Falls; the toll is very heavy.</p>
