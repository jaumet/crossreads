 Thursday 1 April 1915 Ride before breakfast.&nbsp; About the hospital all day. Writing, reading, &amp; sleeping.&nbsp; Riding between 6 &amp; 7 p.m.&nbsp; Sent a cable to the girls at 8 p.m. Nash, Sydney. Happy Easter. Well. Love&nbsp; Cost 28.5 Milliemes 
 Friday 2&nbsp;April 1915 [Good Friday] Not riding this morning.&nbsp; O.C. away during most of the day.&nbsp;  1  st  Brigade &amp; other troops under orders to move out tomorrow.&nbsp; Reading and writing all day 
 Saturday 3&nbsp;April 1915 About hospital most of the day&nbsp; After dinner went with Dr Purdy to Cairo. At Continental &amp; Sheppheards Hotels. Left Cairo by 11-45 pm tram.&nbsp; 1 st  Brigade Inf left camp &amp; Cairo. Col MacLaurin, Col. Braund, Captain Maguire &amp; others. 
 Sunday 4&nbsp;April 1915 [Easter Day] Wrote Holman, Flowers, Paton, Mrs Thompson (Cooma), Fallon, Father Perrin, Pat. Watt., The Girls.&nbsp; 2 nd  Brigade of Infty left Camp &amp; Cairo Ride during morning &amp; afternoon
