<p>Thursday 11th April<br/>Went for a walk in Vilage Cabled home (Wond very slight cable 5)</p>
<p>Friday 12th April<br/>Another walk got to the beach The first sunnie day we have had here</p>
<p>Saturday 13th April<br/>Usual thing</p>
<p>Sunday 14th April<br/>Explored the grounds. Found a Bell tower but could not get in to ring the Bells</p>
<p>Monday 15th April<br/>Started out for Margate did not ask the way so landed</p>
