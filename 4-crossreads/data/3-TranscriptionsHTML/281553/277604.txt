<p>May 18th<br/>Physical drill before breakfast;  after drill order with rifles, musketry, triger pressing, aiming all positions.  Afternoon given for mending clothes.  I spent it reading, writing and sleeping.  After tea spent time on deck till 7.30 then writing.  Letters Kitty &amp; Eva, Cards to. Mother, &amp; Sanders, letter to solicitors.  Weather much better sun all morning cloudy afternoon, evening clear &amp; mild.  Speed full, Course W.N.W. 290 Miles.</p>
