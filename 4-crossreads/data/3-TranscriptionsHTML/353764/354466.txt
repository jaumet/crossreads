They crawl all over you &amp; make life a perfect burden.<br/>The Ceramic came through the Canal to-day loaded with Troops &amp; it was very funny to see the way our fellows greeted them.  Told them that it took them a Hell of a long while to make up their minds.<br/>When the Cooees came through one of our fellows asked them who they were &amp; on the reply "We are the Cooees" our chaps replied.  Well we have been Coo'ing for you for 18 months so now come &amp; do a bit.<br/>We are under orders to Sail for France &amp; expect to leave here for Alexandria at any time &amp; embark on the 27th of the month.<br/>To-morrow is my Birthday, the first ever I have spent away from all my People.  Plenty of Troop ships are coming through the Canal with troops of the 3rd Division, so I suppose they are going to relieve us.</p>
