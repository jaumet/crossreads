<p>I sent you a view.  I'll send a book all about it later on &amp; it will explain things in a proper manner.  It is raining now so I suppose Ellis is standing under the shelter of some trees somewhere, as verandahs are unknown in these parts &amp; are not very frequent even in the big cities.  We were having a very dry spell &amp; the crops were beginning to fail but we have hardly had any spell since last Tuesday.  We have only had rain on 48 days this year including snow &amp; many times it was only a passing shower for perhaps 2 or 3 Hours or even less.
