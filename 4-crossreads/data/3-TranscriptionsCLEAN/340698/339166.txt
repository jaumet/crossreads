  
 26/1/17 Went up to line today. I was in the advance party. We are releiving the Fifth Yorks tomorrow night. Bn Hd. Qrs. is in [Heyam?] Road. 
 28/1/17 Went into line today. The enemy are only about forty yards away and everyone seems neutral. This sector lies between Flers and Warlincourt Butt.
