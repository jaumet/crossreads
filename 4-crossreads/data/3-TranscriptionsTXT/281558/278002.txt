 Loupart wood in afternoon : 
 Wednesday June 20th 
 Bapaume : showers &amp; thunder : cleaned up &amp; roam around : "P" of Bieuvillers church &amp; yard : bath in afternoon &amp; disinfected our clothing : got a few letters also a parcel from JBJ (rolls) 
 Thursday June 21st 
 Bapaume : dull : reveille 4 am : cleaned up &amp; wrote during morn : Alf &amp; self roamed down to Le Sars &amp; had a look at the "Butte" &amp; malf trench : ride home in motor waggon : 
 Friday June 22nd
