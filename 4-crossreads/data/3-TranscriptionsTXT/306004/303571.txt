22.10.16Sun 22ndReceived 10 more remounts in Head Qrs this morning , &amp; as we are a long way over strength they make a good deal of extra work; This evening more men returned from Sidi Bishr; Lieut Sutherland took bad with Appendicitis yesterday &amp; was send to Hospital. I saddled up a good sort of remount this afternoon to see if it was any good, &amp; she gave me a few lively minutes as she apparently thought she was only an ornament but I managed to come out on top; A couple of football fields are handy to the Camp &amp; a good many lads go down soon after tea practising
