 July 1915 Sunday 11 Fine. Drew rations for the Camp. Attended a church service conducted by Rev. Mackenzie on the hill near the wind mills. Had to pull a barge inshore to be cleaned out ready for storing water. 
 Monday 12 Fine. Eight men of us went down and cleaned the barge out this morning. Drew rations again from the 29 Divisional Transport tent.
