<p>[Pages 14-15 envelope]<br/>Again I have been neglecting you &amp; again the same old excuse - too busy etc. Had a very decent batch of mail from home a week ago, including several from you &amp; Beres scarf &amp; I thank the dear little kiddie very much indeed for her kind thought of me &amp; convey my fondest love to her.<br/>Well my dear I am a busy man nowadays, I rejoined &amp; was at once placed in charge of a Company - am now O.C. A Coy. have four other officers with me &amp; am having a very good run, a Captaincy is looming up on the horizon &amp; will come along with any luck at any old time</p>
