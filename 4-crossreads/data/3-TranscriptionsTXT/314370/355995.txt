 March, 1916 Sunday 12 As soon as dinner was over Dad &amp; his boy came down &amp; had a look at my "dugout" which is plentifully decorated w/- pictures etc. Going into Cairo we went on the tram out to "Vieux Caire" (old Cairo) to have a look round Finished up by going to the Kursaal 
 Monday 13 Start seconds weeks work Put the class through a final run over ready for tomorrows exam.  All seem ready for it.
