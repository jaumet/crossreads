<p>8.10.16<br/>Hospital to see a few of our lads who are there but when we got to the gate we were  held up by a guard as we had no pass to enter the grounds, so we rang up Sister Evans whom we had heard about but had never seen &amp; she got us in, put in a hour yarning with them. went on to Aboukir passing an Aviation School; at the Hotel here we met a Frenchman we knew who was employed in the Russian Bar (Cairo) while we were in Heliopolis,  coming back to Alexandria we drove through the Nouza Gardens &amp; it is the best I have even been in, we landed at the French Restaurant for dinner at 7 oclock</p>
