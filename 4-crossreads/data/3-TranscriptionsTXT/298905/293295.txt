At 8 oclock last night we held a full muster parade, & were ready to move off, but we had to wait for the 45th. to come in, they kept us waiting there till 12.30. real old military style.We reached our old billets behind Sailly after a march of about 8 miles most of us were nearly knocked out when we got here which was about 4.a.m. some of us no sooner had our packs off when we made a break for some cherry trees that we knew were here, we had a good feed
