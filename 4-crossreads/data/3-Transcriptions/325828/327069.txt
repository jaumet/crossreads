<p>It is getting very cold up here now (October) &amp; I think we are in for a severe winter<br/>I am keeping well, &amp; am anxious to get to school of Military Aeronautics so as to classify for my Pilots Certificate   Meanwhile I am working quietly, for it will all come in handy.<br/>I am glad Walters arm is getting on satisfactorily, &amp; trust it will continue to improve.<br/>Must close now as I am trying to squeeze in a note to Ol.<br/>With much love to each &amp; all of you at home, &amp; regrets that I am not able to give it you personally<br/>Ever your loving<br/>Leo.</p>
