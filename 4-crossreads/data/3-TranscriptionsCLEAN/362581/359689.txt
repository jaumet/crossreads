  
 3rd A.C.C.S. A.I.H. France 25.3.17 
 XOXOXOXOX XOXOXOXOX 
 My darling Eddy, XOX 
 I shall expect a letter from you next week. Suppose you will be going to school soon. Hope you are well   &  strong now and shall be able to join in all the games at school with the other boys. Are you going to Ella's school? 
 Hope Mummie, Grin, Auntie, Bill   &  Ealter are w ell. Give them a big kiss for me and heaps for you dear little self.  From Auntie Eadle.
