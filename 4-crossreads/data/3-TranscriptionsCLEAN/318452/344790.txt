They were 10 hours covering 8 miles owing to the rain & the shelling of the tracks many of them got bogged & some of them died in the mud, bogged & exhausted. Our own men had a terrible time coming out. Frequently they sunk to their waists in icy cold slush & had to be pulled out by their comrades.
