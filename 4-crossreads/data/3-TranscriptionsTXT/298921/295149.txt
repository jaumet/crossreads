 June, 1915 Sunday 27 Went to Church this morning in Y.M.C.A. recd covered Armour  No  Galvanized iron is used I am glad to say in Egypt &ndash; No rain means flat roofs &amp; a handy place to keep the wood  &amp; the fowls etc:- I hear our 5th Reinforcements are here so something may be doing shortly to give us a more  I hear that Typhoid &amp; measles are prevalent at Gallipoli &amp; hope it is not true for our mens sake  I keep fit by exercise, &amp; a bucket of water Bath morning &amp; evening &ndash; flies not so bad now.
