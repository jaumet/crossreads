The work is tiresome &amp; very fatiguing &amp; demands great strength and endurance. The heat of the sun is terrific &amp;at times almost unbearable but as usual the night are delightful, and freshy. Light horse camps are situated at different points quite close to the firing line &amp; so are always handy in case of an attack.
