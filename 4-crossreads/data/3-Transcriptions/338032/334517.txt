<p>by this time beautifully mild getting warmer &amp; warmer every day, &amp; instead of sleeping up in the barn where there were a tremendous lot of rats, I and a few others slept under trees in the orchard.   For over a month we had practically nothing to do but recuperate &amp; lounge down to the Divisional Canteen for ger &ndash; lorcious beer 12 to 130 and 6 to 9.   The estaminets, where wine was procurable at 2 and 3 francs a bottle, were well patronised, and the troops right through comforted themselves, with the utmost respectability.   I used to invest considerably</p>
