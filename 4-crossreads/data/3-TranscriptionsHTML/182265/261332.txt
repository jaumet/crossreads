<p>miles in 11<sup>1/2</sup>&nbsp; &amp; 12 minutes.</p>
<p>Had a sponge bath on our return. During the walk we enjoyed the scents of many flowers, carnations, roses honeysuckle, hedges &amp; many others.</p>
<p>We had a long drink at the Pelican Inn at Stapleton &amp; then ate our <s>lunch</s>&nbsp;tea which we carried, on the grass near the bridge above Stapleford.</p>
<p>We saw at Hanging Langford three Australian soldiers conducting a religious meeting, the whole village almost attending in their Sunday best <s>and</s>&nbsp;providing forms and joining vigorously in the singing to the accompaniment of the two cornets played by our soldiers.</p>
<p>Monday - Rained slightly this morning.&nbsp;- I had about 60 Mills&#39; Castings half filled with cement to make them the correct weight.</p>
