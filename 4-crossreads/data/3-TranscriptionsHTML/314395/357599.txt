<p>England<br/>27.3.17</p>
<p>Dear Dad,<br/>Since my last letter, the amount of &pound;20 has arrived from the Bank and I again thank you sincerely for your prompt response.  I have made two applications for leave since it arrived but both have been refused.  I will try again and if not successful, will have to put up with it, and wait till my next leave from France.</p>
<p>I felt rather sorry to miss the capture of Bapaume, after the stiff fighting we had in front of it, but I suppose there are plenty more places to take, and plenty more open fighting to be done yet before the war is finished.
