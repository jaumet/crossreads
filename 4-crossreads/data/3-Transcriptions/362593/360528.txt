<p>Sunday 17 March 1918</p>
<p>Monday 18 March 1918<br/>Recco Tul Keram the 19th.  Pilot Lt. Taylor.  In air 2 h.  Dropped message from Hun observer on Nablus.<br/>Archies severe and accurate, propeller and top plane shot through.<br/>Capt. Austin &amp; Oliver Lee in B.F.  Capt. Evans in a Martinside missing.  A chance that they may turn up later on.</p>
