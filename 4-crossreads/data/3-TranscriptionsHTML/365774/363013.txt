<p>Alick know I am doing alright.<br/>Well mother I am afraid to give you an account of our landing as the censor might cut the whole letter up &amp; I want this to reach you.<br/>I will give you an account in next letter &amp; chance it.<br/>Well mother will close now with fondest love to all from your loving son<br/>Ken</p>
<p>23.5.15<br/>My dear mother<br/>Just a line this week to let you know I am doing alright.<br/>My wounds are doing fine, all except the dum dum hit on left thigh which will take a fair while to heal up, the Doctor is talking of grafting skin on to it shortly, when its finished discharging.<br/>A lot of our chaps are being sent back as unfit for further service, they have broken legs etc.<br/>Well mother a short note this time so ta-ta<br/>With fondest love to all &amp; self, from your loving son<br/>Ken</p>
