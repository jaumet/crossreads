
Sunday 20 August 1916Play for Church.  No sleep.  D., E. & I go to old Cairo to see cutting of Khalig.  We meet two Gentlemen in the Royal Salon.  I come home for Duty & E. & D. go Feasting.
Monday 21 August 1916I play Tennis & play the Piano.  Ellis goes to Cairo for Films & comes Home in a side car.  Wrote to Marg. & Maisie Carter.
Tuesday 22 August 1916Mail Day.  E.T. gets 4 letters.  Letters from D. Vernon, Dad, Bess, Otto, H. Hey, Mum & George.
Wednesday 23 August 1916Wrote to H. Hey & D.C. Vernon, Otto & Annie (5) & Jess Mather, Bess.  Ellis plays tennis & hurts his foot.  The mob have Physical Jerks.
Thursday 24 August 1916Wrote Home (65).  Very windy, dusty day.  E.T. Condon & I dine in Chanchie [?] Alley on Tomatoes, apricots, Bread, butter & coffee.
Friday 25 August 1916Ellis goes to No. 3.  Dave & I go to Cairo & Old Cairo Cemetery.  I take Photo & Beat D. at Billiards.  I still go on Night Duty yet no sleep.  Hot Fom. from 3 to 6.30 on one chap.
Saturday 26 August 1916Leave all day starts from 9 a.m. to 9.30 p.m.  I go to Cairo for Film.  I go off night Duty & have no Duty at all.  I receive letters from Dad, Mum, Maisie Carter, Julie, Nettie.
