 25 Aug 15. Cairo Dear Mrs. Read, I sent a parcel last Saturday containing 3 Camels for Nurse &amp; two for Children. One high one with soldier on top for Miss Margaret &amp; one for Miss Tingey, &amp; the Key of toys box tied around neck of camel. Hoping you arrived quite safely &amp; are all quite well &amp; house is all right. Yours faithfully [indecipherable] from Mahomet
