<p>July 29th<br/>Return of Pte Stock (Wounded) &amp; news of Bert Mallet's death etc.</p>
<p>July 30th<br/>Weather hot &amp; depressing &ndash; The Anzacs continue their excellent work round Pozieres &ndash; I pay a visit to Eddie Cornish 45th Batt and have a pleasant evening &ndash; More casualties among our Ambulance.</p>
<p>July 31st<br/>Australia Day &ndash; First hand news from the trenches &ndash; News of Harry &amp; others &ndash; A Hell on Earth &ndash; "Gallipoli insignificant" &ndash; Another visit to E.C. [Eddie Cornish] wherein I meet Capt. Perry &amp; Lieuts Murray. Fergusson. McIntyre Storer.</p>
<p>Aug. 1st<br/>Mail from home &ndash; Presence of numerous bomb-dropping Taubes. Quiet night on Duty.</p>
