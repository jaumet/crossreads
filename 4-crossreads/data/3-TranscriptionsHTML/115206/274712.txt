<p>May 1918<br/>Wednesday 22</p>
<p>Battery Wagon Lines.<br/>Ken Gunn went up to Guns, and O.B.Walker came down, also Ernie Halligan.<br/>Went down to the Lake for a little swim during morning . Russell arrived during afternoon he looks very well.<br/>At 9.30 pm Fritz dropped at bomb on a C" charger dump up at Freshencourt.<br/>The whole countryside was illuminated. <br/>Fritz dropped a few shells across the village of Pont Noyelles.</p>
