Tuesday. 3rd Nov
Most of the men left the barracks to bring in a wireless pole. At two o'clock two parties left here, - one (so I believe) to raise the British flag at Petershafen the other as a punitive expedition to Alexishafen. Only two men were taken out of "H" of course I was not one of these.
