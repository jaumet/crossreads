Being much refreshed we set out on a four mile march to our camp, but got lost and roamed around till nearly midnight before reaching our desination, which having been found we turned in to sleep until late next morning. Here we were issued with the white and green diamonds, of which I am very proud, and alloted to companies mine being B.
