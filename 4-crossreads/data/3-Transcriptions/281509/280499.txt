<p>3 October 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Wet weather set in again last night. More mud. Do nothing all day on account of wet.  To bed early &amp; read.<br/>Bob. Mac &amp; self into Pop. buy, some souvenirs. Tea &amp; Cinema. Some Sketch.  . 29th Div. leaving for Somme tomorrow.</p>
