 14.11.16 Tue 14th The 'planes that came over yesterday left Salmana at midnight &amp; bombed a  town near Maghara early this morning, a good many lads are suffering from  dysentry  diarrhoea &amp; I have a touch of it myself today Our planes have been very active lately &amp; go out bombing regularly they say they get great targets in Palestine, the aeronauts here are either Australian or New Zealand on this front, Mad Muir (New Zealand) &amp; Mad Roberts (Aus) have the greatest names for dare-devilness Roberts took Capt Hudson (our Bde Capt) up at Kantara just before we came here they went up three thousand feet
