<p>After breakfast comes Sick Parade  &amp; those who are seedy, fall in &amp; dead march it up to the doctor at Victoria Barracks where one would wait till 12 oclock before receiving attention.  This medical advice was wonderful. A man has a sore throat for instance . The doctor puts a glass shovel down his throat, feels his pulse, &amp; looks down the parade ground- Result, some terrible tack with about 100 per cent of quinine in it. It would appear by the medicines given that quinine was very plentiful.</p>
<p>Anyhow, when a route march was on the orders, it was very handy to go up to the doctor until it was all over.
