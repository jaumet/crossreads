<p>20.11.17. Reveille &amp;  check 7.30  Parade 9 Usual routine chopped wood in evenings. N.C.O.s drill. Paid. &amp;  played games under Bremner.</p>
<p>21.11.17. Usual parades and N.C.O.s drill in front of Coy wrote in evening.</p>
<p>22.11.17. Usual parades. Wrote with Chapman in Orderly room all day. From 17-22 cloudy &amp;  rain threatning. Read in evening</p>
<p>23.11.17. Finer day &amp;  sunny  chaff cutting all day  Samer in evening. Anzac book</p>
<p>24.11.17. Usual parades. Fine.</p>
<p>25.11.17. Usual parades &ndash; Fixed cookhouse in aftn</p>
<p>30.11.17. Usual nothing of importance. Guard &amp; Loading trip to Samer. Paid 30. 29 Visit to Desvres  good time. 28 Fine bath.</p>
<p>1.12.17. Windy and cold   usual parades  squad drill in morning  Railway guard at 2. Raining. Easy time in a truck  mail in  Elliott back.</p>
<p>2.12.17. Timber taken away at 9 &amp; arrived back at 11  No parades. Read &amp; cleaned up. Windy &amp; cold.</p>
