After this we all "fell in" &amp; were marched off to the baths, it is a fair step there, but it is worth the trouble for you feel ever so much better after it  they have a very cute way of driving you out of the baths when you have been there long enough, each batch is allowed a certain time, &amp; when that is up, down come's the cold water, &amp; By Jove it is cold the room is cleared in a couple of shakes I can tell you, for no one will waste time arguing about the cold water.  Captain McKenzie was sent away suddenly today to England he is going into a School of Instruction"   I am sorry he has gone for he was pushing Len &amp; I along, now I expect we will be forgotten.
