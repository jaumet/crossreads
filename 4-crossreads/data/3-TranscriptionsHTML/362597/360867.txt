<p>Friday 14.1.16 (23)<br/>Still no special duty but I must cop out tomorrow but Saturday is an easy day.</p>
<p>I have just finished a French lesson. I do not think that I will ever pick it up. I forget the pronunciations. I have been writing a lot to-day. I cannot finish any letters. Just a few pages</p>
