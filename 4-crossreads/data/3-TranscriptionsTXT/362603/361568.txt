
4.  still tumbling down.  The temperature at 4 pm was 42%   so the snow only runs away.  We had about 2 inches covering this morning but the sun came out & it all thawed.I must go to bed now.  It is after 11 pm & I do not remember anything else.  We are going strong at Hockey.  The Team won on Saturday.  We are both well also Dave, Ern the Valentines.Lots of Love to You all & kind remembrances to all my Friends.  Tell them I cannot possibly write to them all.  If I were very much busier I would have to give up corresponding to a lot.  I am always writing & never get time for reading   or anything. Love to You allHarry
