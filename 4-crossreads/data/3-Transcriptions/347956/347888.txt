<p>14.2.15</p>
<p>received passes out from 2 till 11 PM. &amp; had a bath at the National Hotel &amp; went to the Kursaal at 6.30 &amp; came back to camp at 10 o'clock; the Kursaal was very good as half the actors were English also a Japanese troupe was very good;  The Kursaal is opened from 6.30 till 9 oclock every Wed. Sat &amp; Sunday night to suit the soldiers.  9.30 is the ordinary time it opens for the aristocrats</p>
