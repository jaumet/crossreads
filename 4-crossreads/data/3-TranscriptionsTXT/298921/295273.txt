 October, 1915  Friday 29th October  Along the road to Suvla, we can watch men walking  - the Turk Snipers are very active, &amp; it is one of our amusements to see how close the bullets go &amp; if the men get through &ndash; run, duck, walk &amp; [indecipherable] Day &amp; night firing goes on &amp; bombing, but my nerves are as good as ever. Started wearing Sheep Skin [indecipherable] coat I like the smell of the Jumbucks. Fortunately my mate Lieut Harris who was in same Region Africa does not take  sugar  Our  water  ration is &frac34; Gallon per Die
