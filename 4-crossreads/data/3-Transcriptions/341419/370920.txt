<p>11th Sept. 1918<br/>This is an unconscionable way in which to make use of a book given to us by a beneficent government for the express purpose of winning the war.  But I justify myself with three reasons.<br/>X. The Army should not provide a book so obviously made for a diary.<br/>B.  You cannot win a war on paper, which is what we seem to be attempting.<br/>Y.  I cannot get anything else suitable to use.</p>
<p>However, enough, to it.  A day or two ago while dozing in the early dawn I was woken up by a Turkish gun which started to shell the flat in front of us about &frac14; mile off.  It continued for about an hour</p>
