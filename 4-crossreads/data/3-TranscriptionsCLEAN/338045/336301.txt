  
 1916 Sept. 28th - Thursday Drizzling rain, but not cold.  Very heavy thunderstorm, hailstones & lighting at 6 p.m.  More casualties coming in & as a consequence, plenty of work.  Meet the Belgian & have a cup of coffee & cakes with him & learn some French.  Still raining, I see him off in the tram, which is late & return to the office to secure my greatcoat, which left there.
