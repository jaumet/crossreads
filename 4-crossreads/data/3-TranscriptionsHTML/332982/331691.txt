<p>We were then put into a hut (40men). About 3am our biscuits and tinned meat, arrived from the Station, and we did enjoy them as we had nothing to eat after 12o'clock dinner on the "Borda" except the bun that we had at Exeter.<br/>Wed.10thJan17<br/>Did not get out of bed till 9am as we were all so tired after our march the night before.</p>
