 WEDNESDAY, MAY 7, 1919. 
 Fine. Shifted the Cow&nbsp;paddock 
 sheep to Piccadilly in the 
 morning. Cut chaff &amp; watered 
 rams in afternoon and rode 
 round the Hill paddock. Jo &amp; I 
 got a vixen fox in the Woolshed. 
 Went round after bunnies in 
 the car at night &amp; got 8 of them.
