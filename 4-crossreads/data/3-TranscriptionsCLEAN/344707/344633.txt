  
 Novr Sunday 28 1916 We will be at this time tomorrow am sending you a Cable to say "Arrived Safely" A smooth passage & we have picked up another escort so should be safe now - Weather warm again - Communion Service in [indecipherable] room, I took part - I was thankful to be still alive - Novr 29 Arrived safely - Presented Skipper & Chief Officers with silver outfits  in recognition of their skill - Stayed the night at Windsor & with a party from ship heard old Ada Reeve sing, "The Long Long Trail", a pretty popular sad story - Novr 30th Arrived at --  & got leave to go Cairo to store Kit - I am here at the Base for a few weeks but do not know yet Dec 1st You may expect to hear of a scrap within the next few weeks - Dec 2nd Returned to Camp no news of Kit so got some more in Cairo, met many old friends there but very few soldiers mostly Tarbrushes now - Got another batch of letters returned from England - I am so "[indecipherable]"
