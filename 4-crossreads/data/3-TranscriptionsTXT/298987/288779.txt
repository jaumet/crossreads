
Sunday 7th July 18Stayed at Training Camp all day it is a change to be with men one has known for a time well
Monday 8thOn Parade once more Drew Overseas Kit. Payed. Start for France tomorrow Went to Sutton V & sayed good bye to my mates The Hospital trip last week was bad luck for me as I should have had another 4 days leave before going to France
Tuesday 9thVery little sleep last night as the troops had money they made the most of there last night & mostly got drunk. Being on Draft have just mucked about all day F There was a free Picture Show for us this afternoon Did not go as I wanted to do some writeing Fell in about 7 PM usual rool calls & inspections "Two up" being played on every possible chance.
