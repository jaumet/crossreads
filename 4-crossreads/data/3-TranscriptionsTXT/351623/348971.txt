
24 Mar 16Arrived off Grenada Island in forenoon watch & done sub-calibre firing.We then went closer in to the town of St George & anchored.
25 Mar 16Left Grenada for a cruise round Venezeula.
26 Mar 16At sea
27 Mar 16Passed Curacoa, Dutch West Indies.
28 Mar 16Arrived & anchored at La Guaira a town in Venezeula not far from Caracas, the capital of Venezeula.We fired a salute, which they answered from the forts. La Guaira is built mostly on the slopes of a high range of mountains. There is a railway line zig zags round the side of the mountains, we could watch the train going up to the top.
