<p>No 5.</p>
<p>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>1.5.18</p>
<p>Dear Dad.</p>
<p><u>Puppy is very Bad</u> he was playing with my coat and pance and as son as I got down he was chacing me and I could not get them an tell he went out side. and I can not write strate because this paper has no lines on it <u>and I hope you do</u> not mind do you I <strike>ick skeked</strike> expect you will notoice wont you.</p>
<p>muck love from<br/>Frank Burrowes.<br/>xxx etc.</p>
<p>[Sketches of soldiers at the top and bottom of the letter.]</p>
