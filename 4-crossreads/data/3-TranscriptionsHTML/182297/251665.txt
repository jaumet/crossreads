<p>Narrative up to 12 noon, 6/2/15</p>
<p>Yesterday morning, a small mixed detachment from EL KUBRI, reconnoiting GEBE MURR came in contact with a small hostile force. We lost 1 Sowar, killed. The night passed without incident.</p>
<p>Hostile force behind GEBEL HABEITA was unchanged this morning.</p>
<p>The enemy&#39;s losses south of TUSSUM on the morning of 4<sup>th</sup> inst, were:- 59 killed, 58 wounded, 190 prisoners, and 3 maxims.</p>
