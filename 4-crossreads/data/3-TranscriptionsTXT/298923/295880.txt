 2 August 1918 Friday 
 Wadi Hanein Posted Notes yesterday with Granny Isherwood letter.  Still resting &amp; men who were wounded are drifting back to our base at Moascar where some of our best Horsebreakers are put on WOODEN HORSES &amp; taught to ride by Imperial Instructors!!! just to freshen them up, like!! I am still C.O. &amp; have been able to get Beer &amp; Wine for all Ranks at a nominal price. C.O. returned &amp; I go back to my Squadron tomorrow. Still evacuating sick men, no Officers left in my Squadron at all
