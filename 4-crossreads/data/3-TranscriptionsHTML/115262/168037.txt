<p>Thursday 27 September 1917.</p>
<p>On watering parade to the Waddi 6 miles out. Since my last visit, things have changed wonderfully. &quot;The line of communication&quot; is altogetlher improved in every way. Telegraph lines, entrenched roads etc. form a great change &amp; addition to the defences. There are now thousands of troops on the Waddi in expectation of the &quot;big push&quot; shortly.</p>
<p>Artillery covered by battle&nbsp;<s>aero</s>planes was seen moving out towards flank of <s>Beersheebah</s> Beersheba.&nbsp;</p>
