Friday 6th August 1915.  Spent all the morning and afternoon writing letters home in reply to mail which came to hand yesterday.  During the morning the monitor which has been having the finishing touches put to her in the dock a couple of hundred feet from us was taken away for her speed & gun trials.  The purser photographed her as she lay broadside on to us entering the lock which opens on to the river.
