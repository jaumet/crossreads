<p>1917 MAY</p>
<p>12 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; Warm, the hottest this</p>
<p>year, the thermometer went up</p>
<p>to 80&ordm;. Cleaning harness all</p>
<p>day; &amp; 10 of us got tickets &amp;</p>
<p>went to the pictures in</p>
<p>Steenwerck in evening. There</p>
<p>was a battery turn out in</p>
<p>evening.</p>
