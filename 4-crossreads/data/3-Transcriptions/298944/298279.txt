<p>I have been losing some sleep lately owing to the fact that a substantial portion of the day is taken up with necessary attention to flagging details of the concert preparation. With Bill Drew as music man, &amp; Ploughs &amp; Wheat as stage managers there is not a great deal for me to do as there has been in the past: Still they have not yet acquired a sense of responsibility.</p>
