<p>The gratified doctor passed him, &amp; on Sunday, day before yesterday, he left home to accompany the day's recruits to Liverpool by the midday train. The last two nights have been very cold (first of the season) &amp; we have not heard yet how he got on, but he took my old grey overcoat, so was probably better off than many.</p>
<p>Edgar &amp; Carol Greenwell were about, as well as Olly, &amp; Helen [indecipherable]; but somehow the house seemed very dull. Olly is a good chap, but all the same I dont care how soon you are both back. And Dene hasn't gone yet! Ah! well, we must all do our best to pull through somehow.</p>
