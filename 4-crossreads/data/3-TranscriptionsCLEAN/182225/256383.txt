 SEPT.   THURSDAY 21   1916 
 Fine & fairly warm for this place. Out with the signallers all day, but did not do much. We had a very hard football match in afternoon. Probables V Possibles for the brigade team and the Possibles one 8-4 after a hard game. I am that stiff tonight that I creak every time I move.
