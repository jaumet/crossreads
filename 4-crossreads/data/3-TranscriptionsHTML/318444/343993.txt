<p>Sun Ap 28th :-<br/>Left camp &amp; relieved 12th BN in supports on left of Meteren.</p>
<p>Mon Apr 29th :-<br/>Shell on farmhouse wounding 6 of Bn Hq signallers including Sgt Campey &amp; Corp. Middleton so I have to go back to Bn Hqrs tonight to take charge of the sigs.</p>
<p>Tues Apr 30th :-<br/>More shells on our barn tonight. Corp Burns killed &amp; Corp Southcote &amp; Thomas wounded. Sgts Mackie &amp; McInnes killed out in trench</p>
<p>Wed May 1st :-<br/>Things are very quiet around here today. Leaving us alone as far as shelling is concerned.</p>
<p>Fri May 3rd:-<br/>Relieved 3rd BN. In line at 11P.M. tonight. Went up at 9P.M. to have a look around. We are South of the Fletre &ndash; Meteren Road &amp; directly East of Meteren</p>
<p>Sat May 4th:-<br/>Things quiet around here today. Estimated casualties up to 6. pm. K. 1; D of w. 1; w 21.</p>
