<p>Several chaps are dead shik, one lying on the deck, another took sundry fits, &amp; altogether they were a damned disgrace to Australia. Some of the rotters seem to think of nothing but booze &amp; ---- , &amp; yet they are tip top fellows when they're sober. There will be some beauts of heads in the morning ---.</p>
<p>Aug 31 Up at 7 am &amp; am feeling A1 &ndash; but would like a good hot bath. The Miltiades had left the Dock &amp; is lying in the harbour. Rumored that we leave at midday. The Mountain tops are wreathed in mist which is here &amp; there pierced by the rays of the sun. Smoke belches</p>
