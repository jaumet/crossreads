<p>17 August 1918<br/>Saturday<br/>Nothing of special import. Beautiful weather &amp; everything going well. Expect to reach the Cape about Monday. A little war news but nothing of great importance We are making a little progress.</p>
<p>1918 August 18<br/>Sunday<br/>12 After Trinity</p>
<p>Nothing exciting happening. Usual Church Services in morning &amp; "sing song" Service at night &amp; afterward a discussion of ways &amp; means of getting a living after returning home. O.C. Major Ross prisided &amp; a few legislators on "would be<strike>s</strike>" spoke.</p>
