<p>1917 Tuesday 13 March</p>
<p>Duty all day. <br/>Ern Murray comes up for yarn in afternoon. <br/>Party of us go into Albert to Anzac Entertainers in afternoon. Harold Williams performing.<br/>Wrote home letter No 32 &amp; posted it. also cards to Glad R. Carmen H. Joan Doherty &amp; Jan MacT.</p>
