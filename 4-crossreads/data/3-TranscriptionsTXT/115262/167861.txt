
Wednesday 4 April 1917.
Field day till 12.30 pm.
Afternoon easy.
Big riot of 1500 men in evening, on account of general camp conditions & Easter leave. Not much outcome of it. Men, especially interstate, riotous.
Wrote 2 letters & turned in feeling not too good.
P. J. Fogarty transferred for duty to No 11 A.G.H. (Downhearted.)
Allo AM.C Reinfts unallotted now.
