
Monday 13 September 1915 Prof Watson called about 6 pm  Went with him to Sheppheards hotel & dined with Barrett at 8.30 p.m. Home at 11-30 p.m. 
Tuesday 14 September 1915 In Cairo this morning for two hours. Letters from all at home  Answd these letters by 2 A.M. 15-9-15 
Wednesday 15 September 1915 About the hospital all day Work not being done to my satisfaction by my medical staff.
