 Fri May 17th:- Plenty of air-raids on St Omer at night times &amp; it is the usual thing for all inhabitants to get out to the Park Cellars at 9 P.M. with their pillows &amp; chairs. 
 Sat May 18th:- Had my first fly this morning in an R.E.8 Biplane. They'll only give you a quarter of an hours joy ride. Left for Bn. By motor passing through Arques &amp; on to where they are now situated on road between Hondeghem &amp; Hazebrouck. 
 Sun May 19th:- Joined the Nucleus Bn. On outskirts of Hazebrouck as it is my turn to remain out of the line. 
 Tues May 21st:- Left Nucleus Bn. &amp; relieved Sgt. Osborne on Lt Guan's Hazebrouck piquet. 
 Fri May 24th:- Relieved of picquet duty by the 5th Bn. &amp; went back to Nucleus at farmhouse on road between Hazebrouck &amp; Borre. 
 Sat May 25th:- Fatigue duty today building machine gun "possies" around Borre.
