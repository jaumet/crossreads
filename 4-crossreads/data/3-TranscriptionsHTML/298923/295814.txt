<p>28 May 1918 Tuesday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Days pass quickly &amp; the Gyppy Labour Corps sing<br/>"How many days &amp; How many"<br/>"nights must pass ere"<br/>"I see my loved ones again"<br/>Am afraid you often have difficulty in reading my notes but pens &amp; ink are all bad in this country.<br/>X I wrote Aunt Louie this morning X   The cake from Boomerang Stall arrived safely &amp; in good condition thank you very much for it.<br/>X More letters Mother March 3, May 1st April-   Laura saying you will be a great grand Mother if all goes well ere long-X</p>
