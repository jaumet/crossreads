He laughed when he said it for he knew I had mistaken him, things like this often occur in the Army &amp; go to make up the humorous side.<br/>I believe there was a big fight down in one of the dancing halls a couple of nights back, a Yankee insulted a girl &amp; the M.C. went up to him &amp; asked him to apologize, this he refused to do &amp; sang out for his mob to gather round him.  This was the signal for the Jack Tars &amp; they on to the Yanks.  When the Aussies saw what was happening they joined the Tars &amp; pitched the Yanks out neck &amp; crop.
