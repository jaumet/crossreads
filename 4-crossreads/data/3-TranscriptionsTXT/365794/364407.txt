
Sunday 7 January 1917 [1918]Day set apart for prayers for peace & thanksgiving for mercies already received.  1/2 day & went to 14 A.G.H. first then to 70th G.H. & Heliopolis for 4 p.m. tea - then Cairo & tried shopping but no decent places open.  Club for letter writing then "All Saints" & a crowded church with a good sermon & hymns.
