<p>5th Apr.  Received orders for the line, which is at present just outside Villers Bretonneaux.<br/>On our way we passed dozens of dead horses also some <strike>of</strike> Tommies who had got it.  Round where two big navel guns had stood were scores of holes made by whizzbangs.<br/>The bridges we passed were mined ready to be blown up in case we had to fall back  Along a little further Fritz nearly caught us with a big shell.
