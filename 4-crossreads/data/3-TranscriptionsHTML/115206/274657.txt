<p>March 1918<br/>Thursday 28</p>
<p>Daylight Corner<br/>Old Chas took over his W.O.s job on Hdqrs.<br/>Busy throughout day on little odd jobs getting ready for track expect to leave for the Somme at any time now.<br/>Da Costa in billeting party &ndash; lucky.<br/>Walked to Dramoutie with Percy, and Ivan, had a decent feed. Arrived home at a respectable hour.</p>
