 Dear Judge 
 Do not think because I have not written that I have forgotten because I have not. 
 You have probably heard of what happened - anyhow I am now between Durban & Cape Town - & today is Christmas Day - a time of peace & goodwill towards men etc: (in view of the times somewhat satirical) - anyhow I wish you & yours the best possible & that 1917 may be the happiest & brightest you have yet had.
