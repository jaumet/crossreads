 Sat Nov 10 The bridge across the Gully had been mined &amp; blown up by Jacko before leaving thus rendering railway useless. Sunday Nov 11 Dust storm today Attended wounded &amp; cleaned up our camping ground Monday Nov 12 Four of us spent day foraging for horse feed &amp; food. The Turk left a complete Bakery, Ordnance &amp; Field Hospital in his hurried retreat. We found a number of wounded Turks in the Hospital &amp; some of the Gun positions. We came across Medical supplies &amp; in a store near to the station a quantity of onions &amp; wheat
