
Monday 16thWhen Went on a days leave to Amiens had a good timeTuesday 17thWent on a route march to Corbie passed through villages of Millincourt Ribbmont Heilly Boundy arrived Corbie midday camped out & La Neuville for night.Wednesday 18thA Brigade marched back from Corbie same route as previous day left 8-15 arrived Senlis about midday
