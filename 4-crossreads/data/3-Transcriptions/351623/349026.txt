<p>Jan 15th 18<br/>Left Scapay with 2nd Light Cruiser Squadron &amp; picked up convoy with 2nd B.C.S.</p>
<p>Jan 16th 18<br/>Took them to Bergen &amp; picked up one to return.</p>
<p>Jan 17th<br/>At sea with convoy.</p>
<p>Jan 18<br/>Arrived at Scapay &amp; coaled. Second advance party were snowed up while returning to ship.</p>
<p>Jan 19 to 23rd<br/>Anchored at Scapay Liz</p>
<p>Jan 24th 18<br/>Getting ready to dock</p>
<p>Jan 25th 18<br/>Left Scapa Flow for Rosyth at full speed.</p>
<p>26 Jan 18<br/>Arrived Rosyth &amp; anchored out in the stream.</p>
<p>27 Jan 18<br/>Went into dry dock &amp; went on 10 days leave.</p>
