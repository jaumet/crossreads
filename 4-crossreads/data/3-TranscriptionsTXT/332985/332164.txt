
Lines as clean as a new pin.  All manure carted away in G.S. wagon.
31-5-1917Contests, Picking out best men for certain events.  Weather fine.
1-6-1917, FridayBoxing contest.  Driver Cook, heavy weight 1st Bty.
2-6-1917Fine day.  The 3rd Bty. won the tug-o-war.  We meet the 101st Bty.
3-6-1917Lieut. Hogan, to be Captain.  Harness competition on this evening.  Horses looking well, Singing to be carried out on a few with long hairs.  The horse master here almost every day.  King's birthday.  Lost the Tug of war to the 101st Bty., who will pull the 3rd Bty.
4-6-1917Harness competion, One section from each Bty.  Col. McLaughlan, assisted by Capt. Little, Capt. Lucas V.O. and Lieut. Strong, judges.
