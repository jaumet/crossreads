<p>Friday 4 August 1916<br/>Battle of Romani.<br/>We left before dawn to reinforce, got it very hot.  Poor old Bill killed bring in wounded man, Hull's arm blown off.  Rested in middle of day.  Rode out west in evening &amp; slept beside horses without removing saddles.</p>
<p>Saturday 5 August 1916<br/>In morning advance under rifle fire most of morning.  Turks kept surrendering.  Found lot of them left behind by Turks.  Got split off from Regt. &amp; tried to follow it out towards Katia but horse knocked up.  Came back, found camp pretty knocked about by bombs and shrapnel</p>
