 Am at present entering upon the stage of demobilising stores and up to date things have happened more smoothly than I had hoped. 
 I have got a camera over from England &amp; have taken dozens of photos but the place does not lend itself much to photography. 
 Some rather unpleasant excitement this morning. One of the French boys at the farm was bitten by a dog a few months ago &amp; developed rabies Today he had a recurrence &amp; [indecipherable] wants me to send him to Paris in my ambulance car.
