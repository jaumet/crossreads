 Thursday 25 November 1915&nbsp; Some arrivals from Australia today, parsons and doctors. 
 Friday 26 November 1915&nbsp; In p.m. room all the morning. In Cairo about 4 pm.&nbsp; At 4-30 pm. met Miss Delohery on the balcony at Shepheards Hotel. Had a talk with her.&nbsp; A train load of sick &amp; wounded in at 4.30 pm today. 
 Saturday 27&nbsp;November 1915&nbsp; Another Morn Aurora rosy daughter of the Dawn has risen with splendour in Egypt&nbsp;&nbsp; Reading &amp; writing all day. 
 Sunday 28 November 1915&nbsp; At Basilica at 10 a.m. Mass. Afternoon tea at No 4 Auxillary hospital&nbsp; Big batch of delayed letters from home, they&nbsp;went after&nbsp;me to Lemnos.
