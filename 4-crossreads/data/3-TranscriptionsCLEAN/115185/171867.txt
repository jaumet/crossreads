  
 6 - I am lying on it. How I wish I had brought a stretcher bed as some of the sisters have. Later when our delayed store ship comes I  shall  have one. I have today a staff nurse for the first time & am off duty for a few hours - this afternoon. The wind has been blowing all day & I am lying on grit. The flies persist to sit on my face & walk up my legs, so please forgive this disconnected epistle. I cannot get my washing done.
