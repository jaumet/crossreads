I have seen men from English regiments come into hospital one night, and away the following day.  The nurses in the Calias hospital, packing up and sending their gear to England, ready to clear out. 
 17-4-1918 Parade before M.O.  Classed as fit for active service.  The doctor did not want to let me go, said stop here another week.  Glad to get away from here. 
 19-4-1918 Arrived at St. Martins detail camp Bolougne at 12-30 p.m.  16 Sgts. in one tent.  Had dinner in Sgts. mess.  Weather cold, snow and wind. 
 20-4-1918 Left camp, 1,600 men marched to Bolougne station, en route for base camp.
