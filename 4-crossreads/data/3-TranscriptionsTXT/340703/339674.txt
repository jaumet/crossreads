
December 1916Thursday 14Left Gara per camel for oasis junction. 5 miles entrained camels and baggage and left at 4 oclock for Cairo
Friday 15Arrived Cairo station 1030 am had lunch and left for Kantara at 11.30. Passed thro' Tel el Kebir Moascar Ismaelia and arrived Kantara about 5pm. Unloaded camels and baggage and camped for the night.
