<p>Inspection by Mjr Gen McKay today. Mild weather still. Destroyer changed places with another of the same class about 6 am (name &amp; number not visible). Large Shire Line Transport with troops on board passed us in Eastern direction. Destroyer 77, same class as ours is convoying them. We passed South of Malta at 6 a.m. and had a fine view of the Island with its magnificent buildings.<br/>Numbers of steamers on all sides. Passed the Island of Pantelaria, between Africa and Sicily. Cape Bon light</p>
