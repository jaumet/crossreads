 June 22 9 am Hove up anchor & proceeded into the inner harbour of Taranto. Orders to remain at anchor until 3 pm. At 5 pm hove up & proceeded to inner harbour & moored at 6,30 pm. Troops remaining on board for the night. The harbor full of Italian dreadnoughts. cruisers. French cruisers. torpedoe boats & also British destroyers. 
 June 23 Sun. As per usual cargo discharged & loaded this day. The last batch of officers & troops who were on home leave disembarked. News from the Italian front that the Italians British & French had stopped the Austrian advance. 20,000 Austrian prisnors taken. 
 June 24 Loading gun carriages, travelling kitchens & hydroplanes & aeroplanes for Palastine & Mesepotamia. Cleaning up the troop decks for another 2000 troops for Egypt. Splendid weather prevails throughout.
