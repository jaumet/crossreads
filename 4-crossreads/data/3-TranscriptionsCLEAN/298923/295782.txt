  
 26 April 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out) Refugees (all Christians) Arabs) continue to pass, as the Circassians & Turks are killing & robbing all who try to come in.  Sheriff of Mecca is not far away now, & our armed Motor boats on the  Dead  Sea cease firing this morning- so we keep on. X I not only have that old affliction of Ezcema but Dobie: itch as well cannot cure without disinfecting under clothing- impossible on A.S. but the water in Jordan & Dead Sea is full of sulphur & good for these troubles-  Went out to cover a Troop which was ambushed coming from Madeba.X
