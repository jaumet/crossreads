<p>4<br/>Could you understand how utterly sick and weary I am of humanity. And how despairing I am of that same humanity ever becoming less deduceable to degradation than it is.</p>
<p>When you have to stop a grand old dog to have someone clean to talk to, you will quite understand how I feel at times.</p>
<p>Most men dont understand this for they soon become a fitting part of the great mass.</p>
<p>They have no inclination to stop the dog, for soon he is too clean minded for their pleasure.</p>
<p>Now that I have had my say within sane and reserved limits I must get on with matters of more interest to you.</p>
