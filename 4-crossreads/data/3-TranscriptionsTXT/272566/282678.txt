 Wednesday 17 October 
 Fine Runs to 5th Div. Workshops (Hillock) A.D.M.S. Ypres &ndash; through Busseboom &amp; Ouderdom &amp; Vlamertinghe 3rd Aust. Field Ambce 5th Div. Workshops again 1st Anzac Corps Head Quarters Sgt. Roberts &amp; Briggs evacuated "gassed" Cards again tonight as usual. Saw two Fritz planes brought down.  Our young 19 year old pilot descended &amp; took the hat and gloves from one Fritz.
