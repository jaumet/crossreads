<p>8th &ndash; A splendid Concert &ndash; Row in Boat.<br/>10th Finished Dental treatment one tooth out &ndash; another <strike>tooth</strike> swim.<br/>11, 12 &amp; 13 &ndash; Wandering about.<br/>13 - Put in for 14/-.  R. Ackett Discharged .<br/>14 - Received pay &ndash; discharge B Duties.<br/>On 16th arrived at Cairo 4 o'clock.  Taken to Overseas Base Ghezia.  Went to Aerodrome Camp to see about mail.<br/>17th &ndash; Got up 6-30 fell in names taken had breakfast, fell in names taken about twenty different times then dismissed.  Marched to A details camp.<br/>18th Taken to Aerodrome Camp back with same reinforcements met Alick &amp; Murphy.<br/>19th &ndash; Rained all night wet to skin.<br/>20th &ndash; Paid 10/3.  Sent home silks.  Given Sergents Orderly Job, had Photo taken with Alick &amp; Murphy 25 piasters.  Wrote Dolly Ralton.<br/>23rd &ndash; Very warm.<br/>25th Native funeral &ndash; Washing day.  Rumors we are going to be transported on Monday morning.  Experience in Cairo.</p>
