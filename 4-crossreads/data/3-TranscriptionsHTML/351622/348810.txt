<p>12 April 1916 Wed<br/>As usual coffee at 6.30 am. After breakfast Ken &amp; self turn in together enjoying a good rest. Raining some to-day. Had a hot bath which was great. At 2 p.m. made our way to the estaminate having coffee half a dozen times with Mrs. Weillaert. These [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>13 April 1916 Thur<br/>17th Battalion sustains their first causalities 2 wounded &amp; one killed. Very cold to-day. Met Lieutenant Tims who told me of Jimmy &amp; went around to our friends place &amp; sipped coffee all day nearly. In the wards Marsh &amp; I are looking after are mostly highlanders. Northumberland Fusiliers &amp; many other well known regiments</p>
