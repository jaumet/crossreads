<p>October, 1915<br/>Sunday 3<br/>Holy Carpet starts for Mecca today &ndash; pity so many soldiers persist in carving their names wherever they go &ndash; Here the chalk rocks around the Garden &amp; buildings are all disfigured by Tom JONES WOOLOOMOLOO. etc etc the same at Pyramids &amp; other places.<br/><strike><u>Carbuncle</u>will block me now for a day or two</strike><br/><strike>(DID you get Medal I posted Registered ere I went to Gallipoli &amp; have Receipt let me know please? Also Pictures from HAY.!</strike></p>
