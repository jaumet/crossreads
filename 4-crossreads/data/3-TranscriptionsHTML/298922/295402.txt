<p>Saturday 17 March 1917</p>
<p>You remember me telling you last year about the way the Palms are utilized, this work is now being done among all the TREES. Weather glorious, &amp; although there is no word in the Irish language for Gratitude, I am thankful I did not spend a Winter in France.</p>
<p>There is plenty of corn in Egypt still.</p>
