<p><strike>Mar</strike> April. 2nd 18 Near Fouilloy.<br/>We moved into this front relieving a cavalry bde. it was a very dark night when we took over &amp; very muddy. These Tommies appear to be a shiftless lot, there only a couple of posies they seem to have just hung about with their horses.<br/>We did four days in, then were relieved by the 56th &amp; came back to the road, there we were much better off &amp; made ourselves very comfortable.<br/>We went in again &amp; were to have been relieved again by the 56th, but the night before, the enemy heavily gassed the village Villers Brettonoux   the wind changing blew on to the</p>
