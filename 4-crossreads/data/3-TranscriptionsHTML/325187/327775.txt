<p>[Sunday 30 January 1916]</p>
<p>"Dates" are now being issued in lieu of jam, this may be good tucker for the Arabs &amp; Bedouins but the meat eating Bushie does not take to vegetarian diet.<br/>[Following paragraph is crossed out]<br/>Today we hear of a bit of a rumpus in Cairo owing to Gyppy Reserve Soldiers being called up for service.  The 9th L.H. went in &amp; soon quietened with the aid of Machine guns in armoured cars- etc:- No place for women here at present Omit.<br/>Attended Church Parade- We have now a fine fat Priest attached to the Regiment in addition to C. of E.</p>
