<p>go through the same performance  I'll bet one white man will do the same work as 10 of these mongrels they have to have task masters over them he has a good stout stick or a whip, &amp; he can belt them how he likes they never say anything they are poor spiritless dogs with scarce an ounce of courage, how on earth they managed to build the Pyramids with this class of laborer gets me. What a difference Mudros" was when we left it compared to when we first landed there just before we tackled the Dardanelles" wherever you looked now there were nice roads, &amp; 3 or 4 good piers had been built, small houses</p>
