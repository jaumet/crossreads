<p>5-3-16<br/>Sun 5th<br/>It is reported that we leave here (Bahnasa) next Tuesday or Wed. &amp; are to go to Minia &amp; that English troops are coming here to take our place, nothing out of the ordinary happens here the days &amp; [are] rather warm to be comfortable but the nights are quite chilly, I brought the set of boxing gloves &amp; we make use of them pretty well every evening</p>
<p>6-3-16<br/>Mon 6th<br/>I heard yesterday that last week an order came to the Regt. asking for applications for Signalling Officers, Nelson was rcommended, also Traill by the Colonel.
