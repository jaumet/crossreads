  
 Monday 30 August 1915  300 Australians set out from here for Australia today - 8 am.  x Mrs Ramsay Smith, from Adelaide, called upon me this morning. She looked well  Letters to Girls, Mollie, Strickland, Paton &c  
 Tuesday 31 August 1915  Saw Ramsay Smith & his wife in Cairo this morning. The festive Ramsay looked upset.  At Chemical laboratory.  Sent 270 Australians away today, to be shipped for home.  
 Wednesday 1 September 1915  Nothing out of the Common  Patients in & out slowly  About hospital all day
