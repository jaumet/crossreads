<p>Monday 18<br/>Pay day, "Reville was sounded this morning at 7 oclock tis very hot &amp; quite<br/>Tuesday 19<br/>A daylight gallop to D.H.Q. at Bancourt with Capt Ronald who caught a motor to Bolounge thence to Blighty for 6 month's<br/>Wednesday 20<br/>"Glorious Gully" is the name I have goven to our camping ground it lies in the centre of Bapaume. Biefvilliers and Sapignies</p>
