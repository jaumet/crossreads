 War Diary 1916  13th June damage &ndash; 3 casualties on Canal &ndash; pieces picked up point to bombs being converted "Mills" 
 14th June Convoy of 650 camels with half Sqadn. escort arrived in camp at 0300, remainder of column between 1600 and 1830 &ndash; men tired but both men and horses fit &ndash; operations a complete success
