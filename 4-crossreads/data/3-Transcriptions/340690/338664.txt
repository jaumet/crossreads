<p>putting in partitions.  Home alright and wrote some letters in evening.  Tug of war stunt which we lost to 1st Coy who <strike>were</strike> beat 2nd.  Nothing of import.  Rained in evening.  Frosty rest of day.</p>
<p>21.2.18<br/>Finished partitions with Rosewarne and nearly completed hut.  Some H.V. shells on Neuve Eglise Road.  Frosty and misty.</p>
<p>22.2.18<br/>Out on new Y.M.C.A. at Vierstraat.  Fiddled about on doors and roof till 11.30 &amp; came home.  Past Kemmel Straight road.  After dinner were to have our photos taken at 2.30 but parade dismissed as too wet and dull.  Wrote letters in evening and guard at 6.  Dola and White.  Very quiet.</p>
<p>23.2.18<br/>Quiet all day and wrote more letters and did sundry odd cleaning up jobs.  Off at 6 and played bridge in evening with Phillips &amp; Edmonds who came home in morning.</p>
<p>24.2.18<br/>Cleaned up in morning and wrote letters for rest of day.  Day mild.</p>
<p>25.2.18<br/>On to Vierstraat job and coverd kitchen roof and odd jobs.  Fairly fine with high wind.  Some surveyors gone out.</p>
<p>26.2.18<br/>Down to compound at Corps Farm and put up latrine.  Home at 4 and played bridge in evening.  Fine &amp; windy in aftn.  Frosty</p>
<p>27.2.18<br/>Fine slight frost, was to go out with Fraser at 9.30.</p>
