
Our next move was towards the line, dragging the gun carts over very rough roads.  We reached Fricourt huts that night which were situate in a sea of mud guaranteed to break the heart of a saint. On the 6th we moved up into reserve for three days, and I am sorry to say we suffered pretty heavy casualities. We then moved into the line of Flers, the battalion gunners going into reserve on the second night there
