
1 July 1918 Monday(Residents abroad should now order this diary for 1919.)(Wonder whether I'll want another)At 0400 or 4 am we were well & truly shelled a nice introduction to our new Camp-  Just warned to go out on a Wiring party tonight in front line so must try & dodge shells but a man is never free in this SectorWe remain in support at least a fortnight those that are left - evacuations heavy thro' Malarial etc:-Lost 10 horses killed 26 wounded by shell fire-   ABU. TELLUL
