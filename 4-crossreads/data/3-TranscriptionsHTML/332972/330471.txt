<p>The Sun, Tuesday, April 15, 1919.<br/>Huns To Go<br/>4000 Aliens to be Packed Off<br/>Commonwealth Takes Action<br/>Ships Now On The Way<br/>Melbourne, Tuesday.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It is understood that vessels for the deportation of 4000 Germans, Bulgarians, and Turks from Australia and 300 enemy aliens from New Zealand have been arranged for by the Commonwealth Government.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Two of the vessels to be employed will leave Italy shortly, and several are now on their way from Great Britain, carrying troops on board. Among these is the Wyreema.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The departure of the aliens from Australia should take place in about eight weeks time.</p>
