 Fri July 21st - 16 Today the guns have been going from daylight to dark and since being here we are just beginning to realise what is ahead of us. 
 Sat July 22nd - 16 At 8.50 this morning all batteries (with which the fields are covered) have started a continuous shelling for the purpose of barbed wire cutting.
