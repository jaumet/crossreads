<p>From Surafend to Portsaid</p>
<p>Sept 17<sup>th</sup> - Nov 12<sup>th</sup></p>
<p><u>with</u> <u>the</u> <u>5<sup>th</sup></u> <u>Signal</u> <u>Troop</u></p>
<p>Surafend</p>
<p>17<sup>th</sup> Sept. 1918.&nbsp; Time 6 pm.&nbsp; A beautiful bright Palestine moon shines on a troop of dismounted horseman.&nbsp; They are hastily adjusting their accoutrements before moving out on <s>what proved to be</s> that memorable advance against the Turks when three Turkish Armies where anihilated in less than two weeks and which ended the <s>war</s> great war as far as Turkey was concerned.</p>
