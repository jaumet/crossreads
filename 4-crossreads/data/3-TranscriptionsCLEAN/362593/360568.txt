  
 Sunday 23 June 1918 H.A.P.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 25 m., dud engine, set out again, in air 2 h. 25 m. Saw 4 Hun scouts, our escorts gun jambed so we took them on single handed.  Addie gets one with front gun, I get one good burst in, rest driven down under low clouds. 
 Monday 24 June 1918 Escort Bomb raid on Amman.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 2 h. 10 m. R.E.8 forced landing at Jericho, bombing good.
