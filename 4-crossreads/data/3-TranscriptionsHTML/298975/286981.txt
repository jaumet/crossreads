<p>Church this morning About an hour of it The sun came out afterwards &amp; made things very slushy</p>
<p>Monday 17<br/>The slush froze hard last night &amp; made it very hard to get about &ndash; one sliped &amp; slid so much. <strike>Issued</strike> Lectures on Bombing Mills 23 Mark 21 &ndash;  hand grenade Hales Rifle grenade Etc</p>
<p>Tuesday 18<br/>More bombing threw 2 live bombs We used to get spattered with mud &amp; ice if the bombs were not thrown far enough</p>
