<p>April 1918<br/>Sunday 14</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours      D.R.<br/>Weather dull, - no rain in day  - rained all night.<br/>Witnessed a good horse race for five pounds over 5 furlongs &ndash; easy win for Hdqrs . 4 lengths.  Bob Butcher came around had the usual yap.<br/>Tom Gallaghers horse wounded in off hind with shrapnel.<br/>Fritz put down a fairly heavy barrage on Abinqy Rd [Aubigny ?], and lengthened to Daours &ndash; Rumours of pay tomorrow; Had a win at midnight.</p>
