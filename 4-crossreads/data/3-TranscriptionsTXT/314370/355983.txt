
February, 1916Thursday 17Instruction as usual, Semaphore exam & get guns ready for range practice tomorrow.Go & have a chat w/- Dad & play a couple of games of Bridge.
Friday 18A most distressing accident occurred this morning.  An Instructor was demonstrating w/- a Garland Grenade Mortar & live grenade, when by some means the grenade exploded in the gun C.S.M Kirton was killed & 5 others more or less severly wounded.
