  11 A.M.   A tremendously big shapeless human form known as the Health Officer has arrived to see us at last. He was greeted with loud cheers by the boys for having taken the trouble to look us up at all. He inspected everybody &amp; then gave us the glad news that we would be kept in quarantine on board here for seven days.
