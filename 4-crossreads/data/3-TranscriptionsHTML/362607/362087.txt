<p>Dear Dad &amp; Mother,</p>
<p>I am just returning from 14 days leave &amp; I catch the train for Sarum where I will stay the night. I had a lovely time although very quiet &amp; I will tell you all about it when I feel settled down in two or three days time.   Best Love  Harry</p>
