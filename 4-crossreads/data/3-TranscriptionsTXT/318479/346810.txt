 June 4 Artillary activity.  Enemy bombard two salients heavily. 
 June 5 Wet day.  Very quiet.  We are on fatigue in firing line.  "Stand-to" all night as we are expecting the 27th Batt. to raid the German trenches on the left. 
 June 6 Wet day.  Very quiet. 
 [Private Middleton was killed in action this day]
