<p>lady-in-charge there, did not get on too well with our O.C. or M.O's. so vented her temper on us. We are awfully sorry because before <u>she</u> came we were all very happy  &amp;  agreed awfully well.</p>
<p>Anyhow we are now separated from 29th C.C.S. but can invite our friends to our Mess room. Most likely we shall have living rooms soon. Hope I can stay here for sometime longer.</p>
