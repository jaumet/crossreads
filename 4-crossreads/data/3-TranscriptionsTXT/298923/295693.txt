 27 January 1918 Septua-gesima Sunday (X to X crossed out) Raining again Church service this morning,  but men wont sing when cold blooded.  Busy clipping horses mending saddlery etc &amp; getting ready for our next push. X Post this with love to you all- &amp; don't forget to tell May I often think of her but do not write.  FW  X
