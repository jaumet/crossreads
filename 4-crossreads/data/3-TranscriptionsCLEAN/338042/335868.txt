
May 9thLeave Bournemouth most reluctantly for Portsmouth - Give a matinee at Theatre Royal - Large audience, much appreciation - Manager, Mr. P. Davey - This Theatre (one of the most important outside of London contains a great deal of fine new scenery all of which is being prepared for Drury Lane etc)  The "Green Room" contains a fine large portrayal of late Sir Henry Irving.Leave for London.Back Home
