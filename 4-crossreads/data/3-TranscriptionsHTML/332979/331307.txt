<p>very open &amp; cold Country &amp; coming back I went up into <strike>the</strike> another village which was crowded with  Soldiers it being their pay night &amp; the few Shops in the Village seem to be doing a fair trade. On coming back again I went into the Salvation Army Rest Room &amp; had a cup of Coffee &amp;1 piece of Cake for 3d which is very reasonable these hard times afterwards I adjourned to my quarters tired after my days work.</p>
<p>Saturday 31/3/17<br/>When all hands awoke this morning we got a surprise, it was Snowing hard outside everything was covered &amp; look a picture, it was a Picture in reality of what I have often seen in print. It caused quite a Commotion amongst us Australians as the majority of us had never seen</p>
