Nov 27th:- Moved out to Rechon le Zian (about six miles South of Jaffa) & camped there till Dec 4th. On the night of Dec 2nd we were trench digging all night some miles out for the I.C.C. who are holding part of the line. Getting plenty of oranges & brown bread (at 1/- per loaf) from the inhabitants of Rechon le Zian, which town by the way is the head-quarters o the Jewish community in Palestine.
