 Nothing said about me being overdue. 
 Tuesday 19th Nov  Set about fixing up things for the move, making up lists and sending back to salvage, all we could possibly do without as we expect to do a lot of marching. 
 Wednesday 20th Nov.  Very little doing as we are still marking time in connection with the move.  In the afternoon had a kit inspection in order to see that all the men had all their requirements.  Did some writing at night having received a huge mail on my return. 
 Thursday 21st Nov:  Spent the day issuing clothing etc &amp; making arrangements for the move.
