
1st F. Amb.Egypt22 Jan 16My dearest Irene,Young Docker joined our unit today. Came over on a Hospital ship, I think but am not sure.Have not yet managed to get to Cairo.We may leave here soon. A rumour to that effect is current.Have not yet seen young Garland. Hope to see him soon.Got three letters from you one day lately & was very delighted thereat. I am writing this out on the desert during
