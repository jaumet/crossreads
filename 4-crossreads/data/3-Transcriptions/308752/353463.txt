<p>He is lucky missing that training in Egypt &amp; not being in France during the Winter.</p>
<p>July 12th 1917.<br/>The Military Tournament held last Wednesday was such a success, that the display was given again today, on the occasion of the Inspection by His Majesty King George.</p>
<p>July 15th 1917.<br/>A great sports afternoon, three cricket matches &amp; a soccer match. I was playing with the 2nd Team against another Field Amb. They prepared tea for us &amp; gave us a good feed. We had a win for a wonder.</p>
<p>July 17th 1917.<br/>Brigade route march to C ---  a distance of about 12 miles. Stopped the night in the town, &amp; returned on the 18th. Met Stan. Purse</p>
