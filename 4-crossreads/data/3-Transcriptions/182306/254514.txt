
<p>38</p>
<p>day and night we came back from there to Amiens &amp; had a good spell for two weeks. Fritz put some High Velocity shells over every day into the town. They just sounded like a clap of thunder when the Exploded in among the emty houses. We held our sports &amp; swimming Carnival in the Civilian Baths The water was nearly freezing. We left here for the Line again</p>
