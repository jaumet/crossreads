<p>March, 1916<br/>Tuesday 14<br/>Care &amp; Cleaning exam took up to dinner time<br/>After dinner we carried on as per syllabus<br/>Weather very hot during daytime</p>
<p>Wednesday 15<br/>Work as usual nothing to disturb the peace<br/>After tea I went to Heliopolis to put my watch in for repairs<br/>Met an old chum &amp; had a long talk over old times (Fred Williams)</p>
