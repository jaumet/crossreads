 Meanwhile boats allotted to us: Prinz Hubertus to sail July 3rd Quevic&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; July 9th Main&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  "   16th  21st 23rd 
 Then came another four days in London after the 9th was arranged.  Spent with Kit again &amp; Hills. 
 Arrived back &amp; found again put back to 16th.  Strike of men against continual postponements.  One day's fatigues not done.  Was chief delegate &amp; did not enjoy it. 
 Final date given as 23rd &ndash; more leave granted.  Did not take leave.  Instead I went to Stockton got a room, and started studying &amp; working on the Short story course which I had paid &pound;10 for. Worked until 20th. 
 Left Watford for Plymouth on 23rd morning &amp; embarked by tea time
