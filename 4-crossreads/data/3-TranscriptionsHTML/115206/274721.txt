<p>May 1918<br/>Friday 31</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines. near Pont Noyelles.<br/>12.30 am Came off shift relieved by Scottie Malcom.<br/>Weather delightful, pleasantly warm. Had customary swim.<br/>Met Alex Francis who is off to a "poisoners" school at Pont Remy. He's in luck right enough.<br/>Bombs during the night and a few shells. Fritz having a little "stunt" down south near Soissons &ndash; seems to be successful, Rheims fell to the Hun today. He is up against a large body of troops.</p>
