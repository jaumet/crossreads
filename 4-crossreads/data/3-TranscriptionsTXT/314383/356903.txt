 The Sgts are distributed over the general work. 
 July 10th Still awfully slow same old work etc. 
 July 17th Nothing of interest to note except the arrival of 2 troops  of British Cavalry.  We hear rumours of something doing up about Lake Urmiah with the Armenians.  Starnes is fed up owing to the hanging back of the Kurds who will only join us thru the Sidah who wants a big present. 
 July 18th.
