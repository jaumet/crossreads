<p>February 1915<br/>16 Tuesday<br/>Field off. Of day<br/>Visited Abassika Detention Barracks. 151 Aust and NZ soldiers in. Lts Weir and MacMillan joined with 51 reinforcements.</p>
<p>17 Wed<br/>Capt Ison came to mess afterwards went to Helipolis House &ndash; met Col Everett and Genl Douglas the E.Lancs Div'l Cmdr.</p>
