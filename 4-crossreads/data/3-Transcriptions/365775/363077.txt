<p>of course, the rum undoubtedly is a great health preserver.</p>
<p>I shall not dwell just now on our stay on this front, suffice to say, it was cold, bitterly so. after the first few weeks the country was enveloped in a mantle of white &amp; then frozen, remaining so till we pull out to be reorganized to six Gun batterys. this was about the end of January 1917.</p>
<p>I shall never forget that day. the road leading from our camp was just like glass, our horses could not keep on their feet falling &amp; get up &amp; fall again although we had most of them shod with spiked shoes,</p>
