
September 1918.Tuesday 3Bty Position close to the Somme -14th A.1.Bgde.Weather fine and clear, plenty of work one way andanother.Salvaging line all day for use in laying to Regt Section.Laid line to Regt Section and used all the line wesalvaged. Met Jack Harvey in the eveningand spent a very pleasant time. He and his colonelwere the only two officers left in his Batt. The casualties have been heavy in taking Mont St.Quentin aplace that Fritz hung on to until the very last.His right of [indecipherable] is the pushing point. RgtSection went well forward.
