 SATURDAY, AUGUST 30, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; hot. Finished shearing at 
 10.30 in the morning &amp; got the 
 sheep to their paddocks in afternoon 
 Will have just over 34 bales. 
 Tickle took Mountain sheep as 
 far as Wheelers in morning &amp; I 
 took Cow paddock to the 4D. 
 Al &amp; Jo &amp; Marj. came home &amp; 
 Al &amp; Linda &amp; Hilda went over 
 to Coomoo Coomoo for the 
 night.
