  
 Thursday 20/9/17 Stunt a great success am sorry to be out of it. Every one jubilant. Friday 21/9/17 Casualties for Div only 2,000 mostly slight. Saturday 22/9/17 Started a sports meeting for Monday got Francs (1200) from Officers. Sunday 23/9/17 Orders received to rejoin units on Tuesday. Monday 24/9/17 Sports meeting a great success, contests very keen indeed. Tuesday 25/9/17 handed over to 5th Div (Denchy) and after lunch at Fletre came to Div - Renighelst - now living with 7th Bde HQrs.
