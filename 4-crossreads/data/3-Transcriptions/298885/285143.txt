<p>June 9th &ndash; 17<br/>After a sleepless night we again entrained and left Abbeville at 7.15AM and arrived at Boulogne at 10.30AM. From the station we were marched to St Martins Rest Camp a distance of about 2 miles from Boulogne.</p>
<p>Here we stay all day and night.</p>
