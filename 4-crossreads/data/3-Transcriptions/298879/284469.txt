<p>November 1915<br/>Thursday 18<br/>Fine Trenches drying nicely. Boys all busy making dugouts waterproof. My dugout escaped the wetness pretty well.</p>
<p>Friday 19<br/>Cool wind blowing. Did nothing all day. Our anti-aircraft gun nearly hit an enemy aeroplane today.</p>
