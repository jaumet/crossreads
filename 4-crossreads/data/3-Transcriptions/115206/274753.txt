<p>July 1918<br/>Tuesday 2</p>
<p>Vaux sur Somme.1st Bty Posit.<br/>Weather glorious, wwarm with a very gentle breeze. Ken and I prepared our little whiz Bang proof home, - not of very much value.<br/>Tom Code volunteers to hop the bags with the F.O.O. on the morning of the stunt.<br/>Registration etc. the duties of the day. Slept very cold last night. Pas bon.<br/>Received a letter from Mother and Adrienne. Looking forward to shoot which commences tomorrow morning.</p>
