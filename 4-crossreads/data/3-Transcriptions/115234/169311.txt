
<p>Colours.&nbsp; Bands of shining youths paraded the streets, plentifully bedecked with the rosette &amp; tricolour ribbon,&nbsp; Their enthusiasm was <s>gold</s> good to behold&nbsp; They chanted the &#39;Marseillaise&#39; &amp; other martial melodies: A band of these youths surged into the Estaminet where we were indulging in a &quot;Wee &quot;Doch &amp; Dorrick&#39;&quot; &mdash; Singing all <s>they</s> the time, they linked hands &amp; danced around us &quot;Tres Bon Australie&quot;</p>
