<p>Saturday 7 April 1917.</p>
<p>On Easter Leave.</p>
<p>Met Joy in City. Out to D&#39;nong in afternoon. 7.30 from D&#39;nong to Lyndhurst. Home&nbsp;once more.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Father &amp; I went to Mr. Stephens for evening. Had games of billiards. Played fair game.</p>
<p>Handed V. P. K. A. K. to &quot;Kodaks&quot; for repair on Sat morng.</p>
<p>Raining.</p>
