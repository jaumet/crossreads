
Friday 13th AprilDefinite news today that we go to Bulford on Tuesday for King's Review.Heard from Dad today that he cannot come down just now; I fancy it will not be possible to see him before I go over.Were drilled all the afternoon by a Capt.Feeling fairly fit & happy & am quite anxious to get over to France to try my luckIt will need to be good I fancy for the boys who left in the draft after the one I should have gone on have lost several wounded already
