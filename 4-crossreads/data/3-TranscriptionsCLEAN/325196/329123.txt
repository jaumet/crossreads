  
 23-2-16 Wed 23rd Some English troops have taken over from our machine gun men at Sequela & are guarding the bridges. B troop do nothing but mounted patrol work; The Tommies are making their post very strong for defensive purposes & barbed wire has been laid down through the crops; Nothing happened today beyond the usual routine of stables Etc. 
 24-2-16 Thurs 24th Was on mounted patrol duty last night; & this morning I was kept busy getting things out of the black kits for B Troop; This evening 8 men under Capt Higginson went out to try & surprise some Senoussis who come in close every night & there tracks are very plain
