<p>May 1915<br/>7 Fri<br/>Told that I was to take the regiment and we were to leave Sat night. Short notice.</p>
<p>8 Sat<br/>Left camp at 11.30 pm and marched with heavy packy to Helmieh entrained at 4.20 for Alexandria.</p>
<p>9th. Issued with puttees and infty pants. Embarked on DEVANHA with 30 on the Kingstonian. Fine weather.</p>
