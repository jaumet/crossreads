
Aug 5thGood news of previous nights attack all along the line. Continual stream of patients pass through our hands.
Aug 6thTwo more of our boys (making 4 in all) killed - Tragic but courageous end of Harry Smith - He, together with Pte Griffiths are recommended for D.C.M'sSuccess of 2nd Division.
Aug 7th.Return of our bearers from Albert - The boys have done excellent work.Losses killed 4 wounded 8Praiseworthy speech by our Colonel & General Legge.
Aug 8thHeavy losses in Fourth Division - 1st Div reorganised - Patients galore
Aug 9th.Pay-day - Departure of 20 men from our section for Puechvillers (?) Arrival of Reinforcements to supplement our Amb.
