<p>13 August 1918<br/>Tuesday<br/>The same old Boat routine &ndash; 3 meals per day &ndash; ships Inspection and put in the remainder of the day as best one can.</p>
<p>1918 August 14<br/>Wednesday<br/>"Concert at night"</p>
<p>A very considerable swell developing &amp; a rough choppy sea running. The weather is now very much cooler than in the early part of the voyage &amp; one can do comfortably with a blanket at night.</p>
