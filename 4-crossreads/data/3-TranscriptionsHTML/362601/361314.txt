<p>18 December, Thursday</p>
<p>Go to Inksdale. We laugh &amp; talk. I try to put the acid on Bill but fail.</p>
<p>19 December, Friday</p>
<p>Fairly quiet. I go to Ot's Solicitor &amp; get Riding Licence. Meet Bob Ward. Try to fix make but fail.</p>
<p>Recd letters from Connie &amp; Mr F.</p>
<p>Buy P.C's of Birds &amp; animals but do not buy dinner.</p>
