<p>1917 Monday 5 March</p>
<p>Furphies are flying round that we are to be relieved in a day or two. <br/>The good news is confirmed. The 2nd division is taking over tomorrow, &amp; we should all be out in a day or two. The 2nd  &amp; 3rd Field Bearers are all being withdrawn from the relays &amp; going out tomorrow.   1st Field bearers are to carry on by themselves till relieved. Hayman, Maloney &amp; self 3 dressers at Edward's Post had to go up to Factory Corner, after tea for duty. Hayman &amp; self put on Gas Guard. I doing from 10 p.m. to 2 a.m. H. 2 to 6 a.m. Up in a possie in the sap not much protection. 18 pounder battery barks just a few yards away now &amp; then enough to give anyone shell shock. Wrote home letter.    [indecipherable]</p>
