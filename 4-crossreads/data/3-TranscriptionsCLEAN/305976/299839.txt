Sunday Feb 25 Last day at home, spent quiet day and caught Melbourne Express back to Camp Monday Feb26 Back to the old home again with its stew, bread & jam & plenty of work. Tuesday Feb 27 Usual routine Wednesday Feb 28 Riding morning, afternoon resting night, sham fight collecting wounded not permitted return to camp until supposed patient brought in.
