<p>Octobre 23<br/>Sec. out laying sleeper Rd at Birr X Rds. Very nasty weather. Good news tonight about French advance on our Left.</p>
<p>Octobre 24<br/>Sec. out laying sleeper Rd at Birr X Rds. Shelling normal. Reported French have captured 6,000 prisoners &amp; 60 guns. Somewhere near Graon. Also all their objectives. French have brought 4 Zeppelins &amp; 4 more are wandering about in a crippled condition. They were returning from a raid on England.</p>
