 April, 1915 Sunday 11 Returned to Camp travelled all night in wretched train but I am glad to have had the opportunity to see so much of Egypt &ndash;  Hear today Myra Nyse has had a relapse I am sorry for her. We are all grieved to think the Infantry have gone &amp; we are left behind no doubt our time will come tho; it is hard on the men to damp their ardour;
