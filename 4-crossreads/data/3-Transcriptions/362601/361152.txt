<p>31 January, Friday</p>
<p>F &amp; ice<br/>The Stunt comes off &amp; the supper, dancing &amp; games are tres bon. Recd running shoes from George. I am very quite at show &amp; cannot get to sleep at night for thinking of I.O.M.</p>
<p>1 February, Saturday</p>
<p>F &amp; ice<br/>Go down to YWCA to dance but do very little. Come Home take 2 WAAC's Home. Dance mostly with Alice who is very nice dancer. Came Home &amp; read until Ellis comes Home. Dicky &amp; Dicky go Home for first time.</p>
