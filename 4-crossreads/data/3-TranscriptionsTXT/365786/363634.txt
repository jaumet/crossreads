Europeans of course use wood & coal which are imported.  The land is wonderfully fertile & in many places will give four crops of lucerne in a year.As we went along fields of cotton plants & clover looked flourishing & the ripe barley quite golden in the bright Egyptian light atmosphere was a lovely sight.
