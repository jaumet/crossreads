Just told today we are to go up near Ebblinghem. Marching up I think. Wrote home. 1/9/18 recd letters from Mother dated 5/11/17, 25/11/17, 7/12/17, 23/12/17, & a Xmas Card for 1917 from Mrs Doyle. Taking down the tents today. During afternoon went on top of hill & as atmosphere was very clear had a magnificent view. Could see Cassel, Mont des Cats, Mont Rouge & Kemmel in the distance; & to the Sth East could see a long ridge right out past the three slag heaps.
