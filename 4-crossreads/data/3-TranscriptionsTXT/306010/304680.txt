(100)Sat. 28" Oct 1916 (8.30 pm)My six days leave to Paris -IOn Saturday evening, 21st October I received the necessary permission to proceed on 6 days leave to Paris commencing from 22nd inst. I caught the train at 1.30 a.m. from Gare Central, Boulogne and arrived at St. Roch at 7 a.m.
