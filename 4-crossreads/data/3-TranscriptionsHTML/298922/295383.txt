<p>Monday 26 February 1917</p>
<p>ZEITOUN<br/>Cavalry School on Wesminster Dragoon Horses great collar <strike>maketed</strike> marks &frac12; draft animal, very suitable for Gunners or Poison Cart but awful brutes to ride. Only <strike>3</strike> 2 paces are known WALK TROT anything over Trot is called galloping!!  However these are side lines &amp; a man can learn a lot.</p>
<p>[The following sentence is crossed through]<br/>except if he is told to shorten his stirrup leathers, use both hands on them, put foot in now not ball of toe, etc:-  rather amusing after all these years.</p>
<p>IN Cairo for dinner &amp; to buy uniforms etc. I am going to lead a simple life &amp; save my pennies.</p>
<p>Tommy Officers are united up with us.</p>
