<p>June 10th  N.N.W.  320M.<br/>Orderly Cpl. Company, reading novels most day, passed many ships several transports &amp; Hospital Ship towards sunset.  Running up kind of narrowing channel from midday.  Rec'd Identification disk &amp; Pay Book to make out Will Form.  Beautiful weather cool, good breeze.</p>
<p>June 11th<br/>Reached Port Suez about 3 a.m.  Usual Morning Parades.  Afternoon given to returning blkts. &amp; getting Baggage ready for landing.  Awnings [indecipherable]</p>
