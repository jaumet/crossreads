 Wednesday 24 January 1917 [1918] Sister Peile's day off so I on the ward myself &amp; then &frac12; day.  Went 14 A.G.H., met Dick &amp; went Barrage &amp; had happy afternoon, then dinner St. James Restaurant (very good) full of soldiers &amp; maybe Sisters are not supposed to be there but all best hotels are "out of bounds" for troopers. Dick's colors  black &amp;  red &amp; blue [see image for drawing of colour patch] for 2nd light horse.
