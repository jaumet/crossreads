
Sunday 6 May
Fine but windy.Claude Le Quesne paid me a surprise visit.Football match in evening V Rugby team resulting in a win for us.Match V 15th F. Amb. postponed.35 to 40 casualties in the 7th Field Amb. in the last day or so's fighting, and 15 casualties in the 14th F.A.  9 of the 7th F.A. have died.
