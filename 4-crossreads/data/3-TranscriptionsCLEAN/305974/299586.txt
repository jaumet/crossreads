  
 [14 Monday [Feb]Some of Casula & Liverpool troops Mutinied.  No A.M.C. men in mutiny.  Trooper Keefe killed on Sydney station & 5 more injured by bullets fired by Infantrymen from show ground.  A terrible lot of damage done in Liverpool. 3 hotels smashed up, & all goods inside plundered.
