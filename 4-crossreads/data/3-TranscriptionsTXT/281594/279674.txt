 May 21st. Went to Bas. S. Was in the room of the refugees. What a contrast to what they once had. This room was only four bare wall, bare floor a huge mattress in the corner of the room on which they all slept at night time. Had dinner with them and  spent a few pleasant hours. 
 On our way back via St Omer we saw a good deal of the damage done by the air raids on St Omer lately. 
 May 25 
 Had wandered through the woods and amongst the hills a great deal lately W  &amp; I went into St Omer to day. The market was on  There the country people had gathered, just like as at a fair at home, and produce of the farms were being sold. There were also many stalls at which all kinds of wearing apparril and household utensils were being sold.
