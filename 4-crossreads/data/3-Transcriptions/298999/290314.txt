<p>could not find them I think I walked out 5 mile the silly goat in charge camped off the beat on top of a hill where he could not be seen his name was N.M.  I Poked about over sand hills and gullies could not find them.  I returned to where I started from and woke the next man.  I turned in again 2.40 A.M. very tired,  it is very thick sand to travel on foot.</p>
<p>Sunday July 1.7.17<br/>turned out 5.30 rolled Blankets went to the canal waited for the motor Boat which turned up 6.30 A.M. returned to ferry Post</p>
