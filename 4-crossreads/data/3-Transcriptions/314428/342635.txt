A photographer took a snap shot of the crowd at the station. Outside the station motor cars were waiting to drive us home and another snap shot was there. <p>Wednesday May 21. 1919. 191<br/>Fine in the morning but showery in the afternoon.</p>
<p>I was over at the school this morning with a large tin of lollies for the children. I spoke to the children at the request of the teacher describing some of my travels in England.</p>
