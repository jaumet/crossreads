 The conditions down here are very much different  than  we have been used to . Our first camp is in the famous Frecourt wood or whats left of it. I should say, We got bogged many times with the guns before we reached the spot for the horse lines  &amp; wagon park. Wallace Lee &amp; myself hunted round the wood for a dugout that would keep the rain out: We found one that we thought would suit &amp; patched it up, made a fire up to have a warm before turning in bunk.
