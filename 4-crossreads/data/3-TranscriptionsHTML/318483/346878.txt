<p>Copy of Monthly Force Report</p>
<p>Estate.  Haverfordwest.  Administration<br/>As on last Saturday of Month of Dec. 1st 1917</p>
<p>Employment &ndash; Men Govt. &ndash; Horses etc. Govt. &ndash; Remarks on Weather etc.</p>
<p>Hauling to Mill<br/>Horses &ndash; 4 &ndash; 19 &ndash; The horses stabled in H'West do chiefly hauling on road, material Plant etc.  As soon as stables in Wood Completed this Plant will be moved out.  3 horses entered on Wiston Sheet there are 12 Govt. in all.</p>
<p>Sundry<br/>Office etc. &ndash; 3 &ndash; Accountant, Transport Clerk, Cleaner.<br/>2 &ndash; Girl Clerks<br/>Other Labour &ndash; 5 &ndash; Finishing Loading, Treffgarne Fuelwood</p>
<p>14 &ndash; 10</p>
<p>Signed. G.W. Lambert</p>
