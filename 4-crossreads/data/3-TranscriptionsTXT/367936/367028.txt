 Tuesday 22 August 1916 All hands enoculated against Cholera again.  Got three letters.  One from Mrs. Nicholl &amp; one from Aunt Katie 15th May. 
 Wednesday 23 August 1916 Day stable-man for D Troop.  Wet canteen opened also A.I.F. canteen. An awful lot of men got tight but were not very unrully.
