Thursday 6/8/14
The watch must keep a sharp outlook as there is a possibility of enemy warships or torpedo boats being in Tsingtau.
We arrive at 4 o'clock in the bay and give a signal for recognition. A minelayer guides us through the minefield . We make fast at the coaling station.
