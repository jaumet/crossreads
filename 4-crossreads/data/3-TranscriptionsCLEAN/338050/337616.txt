  
 5th June We crossed the line some time this morning 7th June Sports today.Wonderful sunset tonight, the finest I have seen. 8th Passed Cocus Island today. 14th Reached Fremantle at about 7.30 am and the ship was quarantined. 15th The men for W.A. went off on a barge to the quarantine station, this morning at 8 oclock and we pulled out immediately after, 19th Arrived off Adelaide at 10 pm. And anchored until morning. 20th The ship was passed as clear and we pulled into the warf at 10am when the S.As Disembarked. They were met on the warf by their people who travelled up to Adelaide with them on the train.
