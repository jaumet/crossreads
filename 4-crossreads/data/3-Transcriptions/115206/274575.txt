<p>January 1918<br/>Saturday 5</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Repeated yesterday's operation and ride, had my tooth pulled by a very capable Staff Sergt in the dental corps attached to A.A.M.C. <br/>The frost again hardened the roads, and they are indeed in a very treacherous state for uncogged horses. Our little village is still our home; and the Estaminet [indecipherable] an rendezvous. <br/>He the boys gather to played billiards, sit by the fire, and drink coffee, or vin Rouge. Both excellent drinks on cold days.</p>
