Early this morning prepared about a dozen breaks in our line. About midday to day, I was sitting on a stool near the door, when a chap who was cleaning his rifle just outside, accidently shot himself in the foot, it gave me a good start. This posy that we are in now I think is the warmest in France, or anywhere for that matter, &amp; the enemy has concentrated his artillery.<br/>Shall be very glad when we are out of it, we are having the biggest shell fire we ever had. This afternoon has been comparatively quiet, but I think that the bus. is about to start, for we are sending over some big stuff, we are getting plenty of rations<br/>NOREUIL 17 .5. 7<br/>Last night &amp; the night before has been fairly quiet, especially the night before.
