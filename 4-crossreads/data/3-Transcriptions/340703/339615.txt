<p>March &ndash; April 1916<br/>Friday 31<br/>Medical lecture</p>
<p>Saturday April 1<br/>Arrived Colombo 6am. Got ashore 3.45 pm. Marched to barracks (40 min) Stayed G.O.H. Went all around also to Circus.<br/>Beautiful sight coming in breakwater, natives very interesting .Very hot.</p>
