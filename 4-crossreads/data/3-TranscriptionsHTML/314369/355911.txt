<p>28 October 1915<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>An easy morning. Musketry instruction   &amp;  Bayonet work<br/>No parade in afternoon but a night advance from 7 to 9.30</p>
<p>1915 October 29<br/>Friday</p>
<p>I am orderly Sgt  &amp;  lucky enough to miss the route march over the desert in about 125 &deg;  of heat<br/>I make a draw of P.T. 500</p>
