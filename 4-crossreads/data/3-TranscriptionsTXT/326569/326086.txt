 Sunday 5/8/17 Up 6.45 a.m.  Went to farmhouse with 4 others &amp; each had pint of milk.  After dinner went to Teffont via Fovant going over grounds of the Manor.  Called at farmhouse on way back &amp; had pint of milk.  Went to Y.M.C.A. after tea &amp; posted booklets etc. to Bourke. 
 Monday, 6/8/17 Very muggy all day &ndash; foggy early in morning.  Out all day skirmishing.  After tea went to Y.M.C.A. 
 Tuesday, 7/8/17 Skirmishing all day.  Bought tin peaches &amp; had peaches &amp; cream at Farmhouse.  Wrote to Australia &amp; C/Wealth Bank.  Had Camp Pie for supper.  Recd. parcel groceries from Australia. 
 Wednesday, 8/8/17 Marched out 7.30 a.m. on a stunt
