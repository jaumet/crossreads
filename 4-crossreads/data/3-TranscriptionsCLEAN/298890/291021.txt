  
 15 Thursday Fine day pottered & went in afternoon with Miss Day to a fete in aid of cot in Barnados Homes 
 16 Friday Pottered in house all day - did a little shorthand.  Miserably depressed.  Clouds came up in the evening. 
 17Saturday Fine showers.  A Miss Fawkener came.  Pottered around.  Miserably depressed.
