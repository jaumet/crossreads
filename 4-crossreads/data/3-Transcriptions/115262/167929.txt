<p>Monday 11 June 1917.</p>
<p>Still on duty and fairly solid too</p>
<p>Sea rough &amp; resembling the bight. Transport shipping seas a good deal. Fire in bunker or hold started again. Foul air starting again also.</p>
<p>Wrote letters in spare time in morng &amp; afternoon.</p>
<p>I am now looking after 42 out patients,&nbsp;<s>&amp;</s>&nbsp;mumps &amp; measles, 3 scabies &amp; 6 V.D&#39;s. patients.</p>
