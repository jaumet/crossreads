Following fortnight was spent in usual parades. The weather was warm we had little to do. On Thursday 27th we shifted up to an encampment in the wood near Mailly. Here we remained doing field work in the neighbourhood of Beaumont Hamel Serre until early in July when we returned to Beure leaving in turn for Bray on 14th July.
Towards the end of July we entrained for Cassel billeted at Nieppe until 9th Aug. shifting to Sec Bois via Hazelbrouck. As we passed the latter place the Germans were shelling it with a long range gun
