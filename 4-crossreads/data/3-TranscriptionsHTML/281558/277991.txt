<p>more old boys</p>
<p>Sunday May 13th</p>
<p>Perham Downs : dull day : reveille 6 am : Church parade : went over after dinner to the No 62nd &amp; seen Harry &amp; some of the old 17th</p>
<p>Monday May 14th</p>
<p>Perham Downs : fair day : reveille 6 am : physical march 6.30 am : "jerks" 8.35 am : piquet in afternoon &amp; night : met a few old boys in the village</p>
<p>Tued<br/> May 15th</p>
<p>Perham Downs : fair day : reveille 6 am : march 6.30 am : "jerks" etc 8.45 am : lecture on bombs in afternoon</p>
<p>Wednesday May 16th</p>
<p>Perham Downs : fair day : reveille 6 am : march 6.30 am : physical jerks etc 8.45 fatigue in afternoon : I am on the draft to leave about Friday : went to a concert after tea but</p>
