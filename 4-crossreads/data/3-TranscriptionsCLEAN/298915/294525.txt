  
 19th - 11th & 12th LH due to board Morvada but fire- breaks out in coal bunkers. 22nd 11th & 12th sail per Morvada. 23rd News that we sail per Burma on 25th 25th Break camp & send gear away. Sleep a la     in mess huts 26th 7am March out to train Moascar  0805 Leave for Kantara Board about 10am. Leave 1230 Arrive Suez Midnight. 27th 9 25am sail outwards. Pretty hot. Some love-birding. 28th Hot. 29th Getting hotter. (of possible concert). 30th Cool Breezes in morning  Hot in afternoon - 5 malay stokers out to it.
