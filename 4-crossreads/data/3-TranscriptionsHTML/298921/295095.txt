<p>May, 1915<br/>Sunday 2<br/>Went to church under the Bough Shed of VMCA &amp; could not but think of our dead mates I sent you a letter <strike>saying flourishing</strike> yesterday as you May not realize that the 1st L.H. were still here &amp; had <u>not</u> crossed the water. <strike>Donald Campbell I hear is mortally wounded &ndash;<br/>Daisy letter 28th March<br/>Mothers  "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;4th April  &amp; papers arrived safely tonight so will post these notes now &ndash;<br/>Love to you all. I see Joe has gone to Melbourne I trust nothing seriously wrong with eyes &ndash;  </strike></p>
