<p>I have nothing to read despite the fact that I have done a house to house canvas for books.  Hence my soul yearns hungrily for pithy descriptions of Hell, its inmates &amp; its tortures such as are ably described in The Aeneid Book VI.  I am even deined that dog-in-the-manger consolation of going to bed for Bernard has to be fed again at 8 oclock &ndash; I am always feeding that beast, it is nothing less than adding insult to injury after such a meal as I have had.<br/>There is not a soul here that I care much to converse with, that is, with common interests;  anyway I am too lazy to hunt him up.<br/>In fact I have descended to a mental and physical apathy from which I have only strength to rail feebly at fate.  I hope &amp;</p>
