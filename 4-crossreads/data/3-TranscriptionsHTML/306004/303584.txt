<p>3.11.16<br/>Fri 3rd<br/>Reveille at 6 oclock this morning &amp; the Brigade moved off at 9 oclock, we were leading the Regt yesterday &amp; last today, stopped for dinner (by the same Hod as we breakfasted at when we were coming in from Romani) at midday &amp; arrived at our old camp at Romani before sundown, we also found Romani deserted at least the part of Romani where we camped<br/>we bivouaced for the night to the East of the old Camp just above the water trough, tonight Laney &amp; Sergt Scott went to Railhead &amp; came back with &pound; 2 worth of tinned fish, fruit etc they had a mile march each way &amp; we have a good supply on hand, the weather is cool &amp; we are having a very decent trip</p>
