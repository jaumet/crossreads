 FEB. &nbsp; TUESDAY 22 &nbsp; 1916 
 Fine &amp; very hot. Foot slogging in Park all morning. Got the last of my issue in afternoon &amp; got the early train to Sydney. Haardy Bros did not have any colours finished, so they are posting them. Linda &amp; M rs &nbsp;Lloyd in town all afternoon so are pretty done up. 
 Got an early boat back &amp; got the 9.45 train back to camp.
