I am just beginning to know &amp; understand London a lot better now, still one can easily lose himself in her maze of streets &amp; endless traffic. I got 2 days extension to my leave, I pitched them an awful yarn &amp; they fell for it, bit of a wonder too. The day I had to report I felt very rotten its pretty rough coming off leave &amp; with the training &amp; drill in front of one, a fellow now has 1 foot in England on a banana skin &amp; the other in France, not a very cheering prospect a chap does not mind going back to France in the slightest but I strongly object to drill &amp; etc. I positively hate it.
