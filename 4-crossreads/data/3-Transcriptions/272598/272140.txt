<p>Wednesday 20.12.16<br/>Fritz shelled very heavily &amp; found some good marks not far from us.  Taubes above directing their fire.  Disappointed again as regards our relief.  Snow still on the ground.</p>
<p>Thursday 21.12.16<br/>Our lads had to go out &amp; relieve 14 F.Amb.  Met young Kelly from Henley &amp; Walter Jarvie, who I had quite a long yarn to.  Rained all day.  Fritz rather quiet</p>
<p>Friday 22.12.16<br/>14 F Amb marched away early this morning.  5. F.Amb. went out to relieve our boys on the line.
