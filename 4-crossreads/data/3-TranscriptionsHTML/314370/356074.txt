<p>August, 1916<br/>Wednesday 23<br/>doing nothing &amp; getting a bit "fed up"<br/>rains nearly all the time &amp; everything is cold &amp; dismal<br/>If this is the summer, God help the winter</p>
<p>Thursday 24<br/>wrote letter in the morning<br/>played cards until tea time &amp; walked to Tidworth to the theatre to see the "Play"<br/>Tidworth about 3 miles from our camp.<br/>Located old Tom Kennedy &amp; his crew</p>
