<p>Saturday 5 October 1918<br/>Photographic recco to Rayak.  Turks have completely gone.  Dropped 8 bombs on Bedouins looting.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 2 h. 40 m.<br/>Went shooting partridge on Mt. Carmel, quite fine spot.  Ginty &amp; self got 5 lovely birds.</p>
<p>Sunday 6 October 1918<br/>Shooting again, very bad shooting, only got 1 bird.<br/>Hours flown on active service, 469 h. 27 m.</p>
