Got in &  anchored at 1/4 to 2. Coming in noticed a very big brick building like a large Hotel, then saw the wireless plant &  a lot of big houses. Mostly white. Plenty of ships in. the NZ &  a few of ours besides a few cruisers &  the Empress of Russia. The Sydney &  Melbourne were in &  the Sydney only received one shot. Plenty of green trees about right down to the water A big breakwater wall made of stone protects the ships. The country here is low but about an hour before anchoring it was hilly. There was a Russian 5 funnel cruiser in & just after we came she came out. The Hampshire sent her pinnance over with their Capt. &  a couple of motor boats with only natives in came out too. One bringing the Governor. Saw a couple of big schooners &  a few more catamarans only this time without the outriggers. Well nothing much more happened today. 
 Monday 16th Nov 1914 A couple of tugs keep coming here. They are the Lady MacKay & Lady Inchcape both have only black men to manage them. The smaller craft (the motor boats) are all painted white. The boys threw money down  &  the niggers grabbed it well.
