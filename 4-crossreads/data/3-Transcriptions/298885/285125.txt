<p>Feb 2nd &ndash; 17<br/>With an early start we proceeded to our next position in action which happened to be at Fricourt Farm</p>
<p>Feb 7th &ndash; 17<br/>We have now been here at Fricourt for several days and are still looking for the FARM. About the only remaining landmark left identical to a farm is the stone wall of a room and an ancient well where a stagnant pool of water rests</p>
