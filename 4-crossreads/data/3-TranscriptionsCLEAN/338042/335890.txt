
In the evening the 8th Brigade Band under Mr. Wellings (late of Manly) proved very fine music which is much appreciated by the Boys assembled in its vicinity.Jan.10thStill at Bertangles - Rehearsal - Fatigue Duties & Usual Daily Routine - An hour spent with "Con Clarke" - I am still rather depressed in mind but again well in body - Dave West & Charlie Schwab (D.C.U. & M.M.) both from 7th Fld Amb visit our Camp & bring news of the "7th" Boys.
