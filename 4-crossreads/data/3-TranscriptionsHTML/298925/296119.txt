<p>to me, and lastly, if he intended to keep this letter as a keepsake and momento of his giddy days she was sorry that his request did not fall into the hands of Someone with a more romantic nature than himself.  Finally she said "I know how you boys are fond of smoking and I pity you.  So if you will write I will send you some "Smoker's Cure" which is said to be very good."  Not knowing that the letter was written by me &amp; sent to a friend down the line to be posted, he has asked me to write a scathing reply to her which I am going to do this morning.  I will not put any address inside</p>
