<p>Frosty, very cold. 9 mules recieved from D.A.C. real culls. The Major very annoyed. General Walker expected tomorrow, great preparation, A Sgt from D.A.C. attached, Sgt Bourne.</p>
<p>4-12-1917<br/>First snow this winter, very cold. Frosty morning. Hun aeroplane over this morning at 4-30. General Walker did not arrive.</p>
<p>5-12-1917<br/>Hard white frost, sunny day. Half holiday Wednesday, Great activity along the front, We can hear the guns firing. General Coxon and staff in our lines.</p>
<p>6-12-1917<br/>Frosty morning, glorious day, Concert to-night</p>
<p>7-12-19<br/>A bit of a thaw set in, Weather fine, Concert last night rotten. Nothing doing, very quiet</p>
<p>8-12-1917 Sunday<br/>Wet all day. Referendum voting tomorrow,</p>
