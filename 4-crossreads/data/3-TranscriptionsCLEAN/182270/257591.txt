MONDAY, OCTOBER 6, 1919.
Fine & hot, came up cloudy but
got nothing out of it. Lopped
in Top paddock in morning.
Shot Robin & Tommy & skinned
them in afternoon. Took Sulky
up to Hill paddock in afternoon
& the horse there covered her.
Tickle drawing water to Darby
Darby cased the bore to the
bottom 109 ft 9 in; he had no
water at lunch time.
