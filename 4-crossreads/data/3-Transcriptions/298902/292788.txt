<p>Tuesday 26th Nov  Last night at about 9 oclock orders came through cancelling the first arrangement owing to a delay with the trains.  The new orders stated that our trains would move out at 8.30 A.M. which meant that we would have to march out at 1 A.M.  Altered the cooking arrangements to suit this &amp; then decided not to wait any longer, so took a car in the afternoon &amp; went to Abbeville.  Stayed at the No 3. General Hospital the night, had a good supper, yarn &amp; game of Ping Pong.  Turned in, in the W.O's bed about 10 P.M.</p>
