
F 28 SunPut in for leave. Col. Y.M.C.A [blows?] me [indecipherable] only Hallett. Ride to Tilshead. Lovely ride & come Home in time for walk & supper.
Wrote. Roy. Mem. Mrs Niness. Dad.
F 29 MonGlorious Day. Go for a run to Durrington & back again on bike.
F 30 Tues.Go to see The Glad Eye. Very good show. We all come Home & talk.
F 31 WedGo to Sarum old castle, the Wilton Factory. Have Gipsy Tea on the old castle & rather a nice time. We have a rare feed at [indecipherable]. Darkie is worried
W 1 Th AugustWe have a large Party in our room & everything goes off well eve to Supper for all concerned.
W 2 FriGo to see who's the Lady. It is very fine. We come Home & have a very nice Supper. I play Billiards afterwards.
W 3 SatI go to Sarum by Train. Go to [indecipherable]  Go on River.  Leave at 20.13 at 9.05.  & arr  B'ford at 10.25 sopping wet. Have nice time generally. We get wet a little on the river also.
Go to D.O.C.
