
Sunday 26 August 1917Swimming horses, very bad headache from inoculation.  Picquet at night.
Monday 27 August 1917Swimming horses in morning.  Pictures at Y.M.C.A.
Tuesday 28 August 1917Swimming horses & a good surf:  thumb pretty sore.
Wednesday 29 August 1917Exercising horses.  Got paid 30/-.
Thursday 30 August 1917Exercising horses in morning:  swimming horses afterwards.
Friday 31 August 1917Firing practice in morning.  Exercise in evening.
Saturday 1 September 1917Firing practice in morning and exercise in evening.  Picquet at night.
