<p>[Friday 10 March 1916]</p>
<p>Yesterday met Judge &amp; local prosecutor <strike>[indecipherable] </strike>  very worthy no doubt but could not understand a word they said, however enjoyed the day.<br/>Just heard my leave granted from tonight till Sunday morning so ride in to <strike>Samalut</strike>- wired for a bed to Continental Hotel &amp; arrived at 9 pm.<br/>Cairo was a gay city after the Desert, &amp; I intend to combine business with pleasure. <strike>Rang up Mardi but hear she is out.</strike><br/>Got reserves commissions for A Squadron done &amp; heard all latest news-   Not many old friends Cairo is full of Soldiers.</p>
