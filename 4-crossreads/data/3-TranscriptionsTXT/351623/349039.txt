
11 Aug 18Sunday. Anchored at Scapa Liz. Second advance party returned off leave.
12 Aug 18Our squadron, Second Light Cruiser squad, Birmingham (Flag), Sydney, Melbourne, Dublin & Yarmouth held their regatta today. It resulted in even honours for Melbourne & Birmingham.
13 Aug 182nd advance party went on leave this morning early. Birmingham won cup for regatta. Melbourne second.

18 Aug 18Squadron & Destroyers went to sea with mine layers.
19 Aug 18Standing by mine layers while they blow up a mine field. Can see mines exploding all day. Came back to Scapay Flow.
