<p>Friday 8th Dec.<br/>Things very quiet the last two days but have had a few shells over with a few casualties as a result. Have written several letters as had plenty of time to spare The flow of trench feet has stopped. Very few cases now coming in pointing to more care being taken.</p>
<p>Wednesday 13th Dec<br/>Nothing much of importance to mention for the last few days. On Monday we had a fall of snow, a very pretty sight indeed. A wind was blowing which caused the snow to collect on one side only of trees etc. making a very nice shaded appearance. Had rather an exciting night. Several shells landed quite close throwing mud etc. all over our residence. The first was very close indeed &amp; landed about 11.30 P.M. when everyone was asleep.
