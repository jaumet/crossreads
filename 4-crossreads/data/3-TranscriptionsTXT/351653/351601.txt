 Egypt 13.9.1916 
 Mrs Robertson 
 Dear Madam 
 Your welcome letter to hand the mail before last very pleasing.  All were well as this leaves me at present. 
 I am very pleased you are haveing such a good year as it is wanted badly.  We never see a bit of green grass about hear.  I have not seen a decent bit of green grass this last 4 months.  I had a letter From Tom last mail, he was telling me he had his old job Back again, he said he liked it well.
