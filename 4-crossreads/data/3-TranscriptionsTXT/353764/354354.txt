It was here that the "China" ran ashore. In between Perim &amp; the main-land (Arabia) is what is termed "Hells Gates" at the entrance to the Red Sea, &amp; I can tell you it is very appropriately named.  The Coast  for a long way is studded with abrupt &amp; steep Cliffs, the same absence of vegetation making it look very harsh &amp; forbidding. 
 We are now several hundred miles  away from the Coast &amp;  along the Red Sea &amp; hope to reach "Port Suez" next Saturday night, which we understand is to be our destination. We picked up our first bit of War News by wireless to-day, &amp; it did not brighten us up, as it was a slight reverse in the Balkans. We have several contagious diseases in the Hospital, &amp; think that we will be quaranetined on arriving at Port Suez.
