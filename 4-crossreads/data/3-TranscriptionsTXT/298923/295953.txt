 14 October 1918 Monday Jerusalem   66&deg; Casualty Clearing Hqs arrived here midday-   "Tummy" still on strike but feel better goodness knows where we go next- Great rumours of peace but I believe nothing-   All Hospitals &amp; trains full so stop here -for present- Heard our Brigade is coming back to Richon way soon to reorganise so expect will join up there later on- This Hos. overlooks the Walls of old Jerusalem &amp; was a Monastery-   SISTERS here.
