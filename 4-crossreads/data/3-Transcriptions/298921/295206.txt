<p>August, 1915<br/>Monday 23<br/>Last night had horrible nightmare 1000s of Germans Barb wire 6" long &amp; saw all my mates who were killed, I shot the Germans but they would not stop &ndash;<br/>I did not take my camera to Dardanelles, but the scenes are impressed on my mind for all time &amp; it seemed too serious a job to be photographing.</p>
