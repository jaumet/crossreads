<p>The Army Medical Corps</p>
<p>To the Army medical corps is due much credit and skill in saving many valuable lives under most trying and difficult conditions.  Our own Battalion medical officers did splendid work, &amp; their untiring energy &amp; unbounded attention saved many whose condition appeared hopeless.  From the battalion they were transferred by stretchers if unable to walk to the nearest field dressing stations which in the early part of the campaign were situated on the beach &amp;  were subjected to much heavy bombardment.
