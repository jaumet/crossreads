<p>1916 February 1<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Morning shift.  Get our final leaves made out &amp; our allotment papers signed.  Down to Sydney in evening.  Bert Edwards up to see me.  Go out to Bondi with Bob. Stay the night. Miss Astbury &amp;<br/>[indecipherable]ora &ndash; in later. Sing supper &amp; bed<br/>Bob still very unwell.</p>
