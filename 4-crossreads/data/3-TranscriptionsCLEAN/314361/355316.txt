  
 Lou yet. Thursday. Dec. 30th 15 This school breaks up on Saturday just now I am going through an exam. Yesterday I received a parcel from Louie. It was first rate, just a few things I needed. 1st January 1916 Saturday New Years Day We leave school today. return to Coy. at  Abbassia. Last night I registered parcel with three silk [indecipherable]. Louie. This morning at   mea  breakfast we each received a parcel from the Sydney War chest. In mine was a tin of boiled lollies, & a tin of (50) shamrock cigarettes. Have a full supply of cigarettes just now, a tin of 50 from home & a tin of shamrock. Have received no word from Lou yet, & so far only two from Louie. New Years Night Received a letter from Lou tonight. & from Home. This afternoon I spent in Cairo. Am now back in Abbassia Camp. Sent Pos. off to Lou Home & Nell Sunday Jan 2nd 16 This afternoon I & some friends went to the dead
