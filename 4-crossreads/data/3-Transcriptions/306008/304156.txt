<p>1914<br/>Dec 10 Th<br/>Still unwell<br/>Reported sick to Medical Officer for day<br/>Fine day</p>
<p>Dec 11 Fri<br/>Feeling fairly good.<br/>Wrote several letters<br/>Arrived at Fremantle at 9 p.m.<br/>Very pretty by night<br/>Rained during day &ndash; Read Magazines</p>
<p>Fine sunset<br/>Sea smooth</p>
<p>Dec 12 Sat<br/>Lovely day<br/>Worked in office &ndash; Place very pretty<br/>Did some signalling to H.M.A.S. Pioneer.<br/>Read first newspaper for a week.<br/>Went ashore at  3 pm. Saw Fremantle &amp; Perth &ndash; In touch with scouts &ndash; Good look round.<br/>2 letters</p>
