 Of war so far. This is up to Sunday night 
 Monday 28th January Had a very short march this morning &ndash; without Packs Nothing in afternoon Can hear the guns from here &amp; saw some shell bursts around a plane this morning &amp; some to night behind us. They were a good distance away.
