28/11/18 Parisbeer &ndash; 11 francs and 1 fr. tip (10/-). The rain was coming down quite heavily as I walked down to the Place de la Concorde all beflagged and crowded with captured Hun guns. Crowds were assembling and there were plenty of soldiers of the Republican Guard with their brass helmets. Walked down the Champs Elysees where enterprising people were selling chairs and other stands. After a fairly long wait a cannon sounded cheering started and His Insignificant Majesty King George V drove past with M Poincaire as companion. Following him came the Prince of Wales and Prince Albert. In front were soldiers of the Guard and blue helmeted mounted poilus  lined the street.
