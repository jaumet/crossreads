  
 March, 1916 Wednesday 22 Forenoon on the written paper which towels 'em up  The questions set are far more severe than last exam. Afternoon on Drill & stoppages Spent evening giving dad a lesson on the M.G. he was an apt pupil 
 Thursday 23 Exam Drill My boys do very well, as also on their note Books, all but 3 got 100. Afternoon on stoppages.
