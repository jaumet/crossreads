7 September 1916
Thursday 
Cartwright, self, Fistz Gerald, Carruthers Sergt. Clifton with Capt. Lee, chosen to attend 2 days at Div. Gas School. Lectured on Gas, its uses deadliness, helmets, new old. 800 cas. 300 deaths
Dinner at corner eggs chips, getting sick of these. Tony happens along; go with him to his camp nearby Back to school. Lachrymatory Gas tests. Cart self meet Tony after 4 o'clock Go into Pop. Rains Pictures at railway stat. Good band. Home to
