<p>Sunday, 22/4/17<br/>Sunshine in morning but cloudy afternoon.  During morning an aeroplane alighted in paddock close to hospital, but rose again before we had time to go &amp; see it.</p>
<p>Monday, 23/4/17<br/>Nice fine but cloudy day.  Expect to go out in day or two.  Concert was given in dining hall during afternoon by visiting professionals &amp; was very good.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 24/4/17<br/>Nice fine day.  Went for walk in scrub adjoining hospital with "Gunner".  In afternoon went over railway line &amp; watched some "Tommies" playing Soccer.</p>
