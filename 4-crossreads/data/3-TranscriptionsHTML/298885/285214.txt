<p>Apr 19th &ndash; 18<br/>In order to allow ourselves to see a few more summers we have shifted our camp, in other words we have been shelled out</p>
<p>Now we are occupying a farm about 1000 yds from the villiage. From here we can see shells dropping into villiage and causing great havoc.</p>
