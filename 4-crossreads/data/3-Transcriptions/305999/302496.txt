<p>7/8/15<br/>At 10 AM we where taken over to the left of Lone pine  we took over a lot of the firing line for the few men we had left    we lost about 300 men the night before   we had a lot of work to do</p>
<p>8/9/15<br/>We where releaved by the 22nd Battn  we went out just behind the firing line and sleept for the night  a few shells came over and it was new to the 22 Battn</p>
