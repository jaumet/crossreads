  
 Tuesday Dec4 Raining today. My old Neddy looks picture of health owing to good spell & ample rations. Wednesday Dec 5 Moving back to Richon Le Zion tomorrow for good spell out of line. Thursday Dec 6 Sent on advance guard Reported  Col Cave 1ST LH.FA. Heavy rain & set in & spent miserable night in rain. Friday Dec 7 Still raining heavily. The rainy season has started in Palestine & we understand it is on the cards we will be here until after Christmas. Saw Camel Corp & some of my old pals trekking back to Raffa
