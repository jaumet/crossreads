  
 30. Thursday Lovely day.  Pottered and did a little shorthand in forenoon.  Afternoon went to hear Dr. Harold Williams at Lyceum on Russia.  Not feeling very strong. 
 31. Friday Pottered around.  Went up to Miss Hodge in evening on message   Only Greta there. 
 June 1918 1. Saturday F.P. rang up at noon & invited me to Hambourg Chopin recital in Cent. Hall.  Tea at Victoria &  went to Kensington Gdns. & brought F. home with me for supper  He stayed till 12.10.  I showed him photos. Miss S. away for week end.  Lovely, lovely weather. Visited Sir James in morning
