
October, 1915Thursday 7People will want to be careful of Imposters, posing as Heroes a hateful word when bandied about -As in Africa a lot of wasters are making back to Australia when they should be returning to the trenches -One man with an ingrowing Toenail has never done a days work since he embarked another gone to England, one day shot himself thro' the foot.  Tis true tis Pity & pity this be true.A lot of the best men are also going back unfortunately - minus limbs & broken. They know the wasters & do not like associating with them.
