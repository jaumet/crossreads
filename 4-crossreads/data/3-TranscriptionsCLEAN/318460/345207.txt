
ContinuedTuesday 27/2/17glorious fire tonight Petrol Tin, top cut off & tin Jabbed with Bayonette full of holes about 3 lbs coal rest damp chips etc, still do great.great artillery activity on both sides.
Wednesday 28/2/17definetly known that Fritz is retiring good luck to him may he keep it up.Cloudy & cold at 5.30 amterrific barrage put up by us on the Right must have gone over. no news yet. Fritz sending a lot of Shells over here but doing no damage,hear that we dont go out till April now.Pay 3.4.5. 21.12.0
Thursday 1/3/17Letter from Syd saying Uncle George dead about the 5th Jany. left Loo &  Maude Property but nothing to us Boys Uncle Jack gets the bulk.Wrote Maude, Syd & Viv & Posted themfine & clear plenty of aircraft about & plenty of artillery tonight will be worsePay 3-5.5 21.13-0
Friday 2/3/1755th came out last night, going back to train for the big stunt coming off soon this will not be a healthy spot, when it doesgot letters from Maude & Viv Maude did not know of legacy when she wrote it no mention of it. my letter will surprise her.severe frost & fog this morning we get them together here very liberal in France tried hard to snow this afternoon sago fell for awhilePay 3.6.5 21/14/0Posted letter to Loo to day mail goes out tomorrow for Ausi.dug-out fell in this morning lucky we were up or might have been killed one poor chap was, up in the trenches
