<p>[Follows on from page 255.]<br/>Battalions of our Boys hopped the bags with the Tommies the Balloons have gone up so things have gone ahead.<br/>We have not much shelter the country is like waves up &amp; down the night we advanced just dug the gun in &amp; it rained so had to sleep on the ground I got near a big log &amp; rolled up in my waterproof sheet but you can sleep even with a wet shirt tail. It brings back happy memories when the children were babies. It gets very muddy &amp; you get in a hell of a mess.<br/>It has been a bit quite to day had a wash &amp; chatted my shirt had a change &amp; washed it but only muddy water out of a shell hole water is very scarce here. Fritz has gone &amp; poisoned all the water in wells in the villages He has gone through &amp; most other water has to have Chloride in it a rotten taste even when its made into tea.<br/>Well George I have put you in another shoulder strap off a German Lieutenant also a disk off another dead Fritz.<br/>So I will ring off now there is a good stunt going on on our right just now.
