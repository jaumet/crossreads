 Wednesday 28 November 1917 Connaught (settled) evidently 39 pts. &amp; all dressings.  Must have septic or desert soles, some foments, many splints both arm &amp; leg, 4 baths, 2 arms, 2 legs, 2ce. daily. Eusol &amp; peroxide most used. Off for &frac12; day, went 14 A.G.H., no one to go out with so went alone.
