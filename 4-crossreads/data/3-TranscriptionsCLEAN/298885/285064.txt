 Mon May 1st - 16 Everything quiet all day except for snipers who are continually on the strafe. 
 Tues May 2nd - 16 Another gas attack reported but too far away to cause us any anxiety. 
 Wed May 3rd - 16 Enemy trying to shell Sailly Station all day but with no result
