<p>Acting is splendid &ndash;<br/>- Congratulations all round &ndash;<br/>* German delegates arrive in Paris "re" the Armistice &ndash; and now the entire world awaits the outcome of this conference &ndash; Excitement in our town of "Eu".</p>
<p>Nov. 8th Rehearsal of Panto &ndash; Many letters written &ndash;<br/>- The conference is over and Germany has 72 hours in which to submit to terms &ndash; Failing that the War will continue and the longer it lasts</p>
