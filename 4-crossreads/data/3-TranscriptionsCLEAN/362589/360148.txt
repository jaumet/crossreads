
POST CARD[Postage stamp is 1d stamp for UNION OF SOUTH AFRICA]The HonMr Justice FergusonJudges ChambersSupreme CourtSydneyAustralia
28.9.15 Have spent two very pleasant days at this hotel in Durban. It makes a welcome break after the weary run across from Fremantle. I am posting you a local newspaper to give you some idea of both journalism and politics in this part of the Empire. With kindest regards to all at 'Wimbledon"Yrs very sincerelyLt. S. Whitfeld.
