 26 Aug, 1916 And then he reached Et Maler And started his advance But found against Australia He had no ------ chance. 
 He thought that with his rifles He would easily get through But found that these were trifles To old Man Kangaroo. 
 He brought up reinforcements And snipers by the score But found this to his loss meant Our rifles spoke the more. 
 He tried to get around us, But here he tried in vain And very soon he found us In front of him again. 
 He cried out allah! allah! As through him cold steeled seared But discretion more than valor So Allah disappeared. 
 And Abdul taking Allah As leader good &amp; true He also did a 'yallah' From the wily Kangaroo.
