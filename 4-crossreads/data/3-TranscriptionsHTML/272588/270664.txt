<p>Friday 22 February<br/>March winds are commencing.  The "Sentimental Blokes" in Ballieul are still very good, the lady being more charming than ever. A violent bombardment on our left to-night.<br/>Papers profesy a big German attack on this front but I don't know whether it will eventuate.</p>
<p>Saturday 23 February<br/>Walk over to 4th D.S.C. &amp; find George Petersen in &amp; have a good evening with him.</p>
