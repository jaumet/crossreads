<p>&amp; find my Quinine gone &ndash; Also every patient gone &ndash; Rabaul is washed out &ndash; As we steamed back to Rabaul twixt 3 &amp; 4 pm we saw the Navua (Union S.S.Co) go out &ndash; She had over 1000 souls on board &ndash; Skeete was on her so I'm the only Naval Dr in Rabaul &ndash; The men on the Navua cheered the Fantome vociferously but we were not allowed to cheer &ndash; Naval discipline of the wrong sort &ndash; We should have returned cheer for cheer &amp; the Captain &ndash; if necessary &ndash; should not have heard it &ndash; No other officer would I know &ndash;<br/>Well then N &amp; I went up to the top Hospital to say good bye to Fitzherbert &amp; Flood &ndash; We did it over bumpers of Moselle &ndash; Back to the town (1 mile walk) Aboard &ndash; Dinner &ndash; Then Walker the navigator came off &ndash; 4 sheets in the wind &ndash; with 3 white cockatoos which he had promised to take to Sydney &ndash; We other 4 officers sat on the ship's rail &amp; abused the birds &amp; him</p>
