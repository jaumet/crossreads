<p>3.  that I know?  Hang London, It is a dirty rotten dreary Hole.  The English Country is pure &amp; lovely but the cities are too awful.  Just like all big cities, women &amp; wine, sorrow &amp; poverty.<br/>I know what Mrs Clarkes Home is.  I hate to write 'cos Ted &amp; I were such old chums &amp; it must only stir it all up every time that She gets a letter from me.  Glad to hear that Nette Nelson was down for a couple of days.  I never find Her too quiet.  She is much more lively than Hilda.  We have seen Eric Hynde only a week or so ago, at least</p>
