
Y.M.C.A.With TheMediterranean Expeditionary Force.
14th May  1916
Dear MotherI have once more a chance of sending you a line to let you all know how I am progressing. This is about our 8th day in Egypt and I suppose I am now getting acclimatised to a certain extent. The camp conditions are not of the best as we have been moving about a good deal and the dust and sand worries a chap a bit, where we are the whole countryside is composed of sandy gravel and when the wind blows etc. mind your eyes.I cant say that I like Egypt or rather all  I've seen as yet, we are camped in sight of the Nile but owing to some reasons are not allowed to either drink or bathe in the water.
