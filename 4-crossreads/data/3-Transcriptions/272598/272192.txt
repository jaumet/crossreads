<p>afternoon, G.O.C. inspected.</p>
<p>Friday 1.6.17<br/>Cricket Match A v C Sec.  Played with C but were badly beaten by A.  Have had no practice, consequently in very poor form</p>
<p>Saturday 2.6.17<br/>Colonel went on leave to Blighty for the 3rd time.  A sec played B cricket &amp; won by 4 wickets  Still no sign of a move</p>
<p>Sunday 3.6.17<br/>Church service in Church Army hut.  C sec played B &amp; won by 10 wickets.<br/>Scored 0-10 not out also took 5 for 25.  Left at 5 pm by car for the Divisional Gas school at Becourt.  Walked from there to Albert at night.  Expect to be here for about 3 days.</p>
