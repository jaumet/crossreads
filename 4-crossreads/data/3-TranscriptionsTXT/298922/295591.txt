 Sunday 7th October 1917 
 The Olives &amp; Vines give way to Wheat &amp; then very pretty hilly country. Spent half today at  FERANZE  an out of the way old pretty town,  cabled Dolly . We found a mineral Bath (thermos) &amp; bought eggs, Chianti, fruit etc. - Cobble stones Cathedrals &amp; Pottery. 
 Boarded the train at 7.45 p.m. again. All day we passed Castles on the hills &amp; could hear the big guns on the Austrian border. TRAIN fumigated &amp; disinfected here.
