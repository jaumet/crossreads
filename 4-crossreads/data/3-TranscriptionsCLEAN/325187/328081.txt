  
 [Monday 4 December 1916] 
 Col  L Meredith  has been in Australia a month  & may not return - Major  Granville  now O.C. & his fiancee arrived from Australia & married yesterday.  We go  Ismalia   & give him a dinner tonight . I speak of WE I am at present in Detail Camp near  Ismalia  awaiting instructions.  Still Kit missing. Am a bit stiff after riding been so long away- Hear young Charlie is a Captain now. Found a Mongoose in my Tent a pet one of the Bengal Lancers   I have seen them before [indecipherable]
