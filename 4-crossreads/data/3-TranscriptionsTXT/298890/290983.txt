 4.Thursday Went down town to Kodak for prints. Evelyn Isilt &amp; M.M. Moffatt for lunch. Had to type Lyceum  general stuff  for Isilt  got none of my work done consequently. 
 5. Friday Cooked dinner for Mme Jallay. Rev. Arnold came for afternoon tea. got nothing done. 
 6. Saturday Two strange girls called and asked for water. Cold showery day. Struggled vainly to get on with work. Cooking &amp; bell calls made it very unprofitable.
