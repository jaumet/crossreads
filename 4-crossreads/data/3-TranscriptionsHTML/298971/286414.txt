<p>10th October (Tuesday)</p>
<p>On Waggons touring round the streets after rubbish.  No more souvenirs from Fritz.  Heated arguments against conscription, in our billet till late at night.</p>
<p>11th October (Wednesday)</p>
<p>On Waggons again today and visited dentist.  Debate on Conscription at Y.M.C.A. 6 pm.  Only 5 votes for conscription from the whole audience.</p>
<p>12th October (Thursday)</p>
<p>Off duty on dental leave</p>
