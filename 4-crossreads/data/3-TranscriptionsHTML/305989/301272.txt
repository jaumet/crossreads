<p>Sunday 2 September 1917<br/>On Q.M. Fatigue (water) and saw Keith Slade &amp; Russel Gilbraith.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 2].</p>
<p>Monday 3 September 1917<br/>Firing in morning &amp; exercise in evening.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4 September 1917<br/>Exercises &amp; shooting, on Mess duty.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5 September 1917<br/>Nothing doing:  on picquet at night.</p>
<p>Thursday 6 September 1917<br/>Quiet day.  Good concert at Y.M.C.A., best ever held this side of the Canal.</p>
<p>Friday 7 September 1917<br/>On beach guard, got books from Mrs. Walker &amp; Reg letters from Jess.</p>
<p>Saturday 8 September 1917<br/>Nothing much doing, good concert by the Welsh Choir at Y.M.C.A. at night.</p>
