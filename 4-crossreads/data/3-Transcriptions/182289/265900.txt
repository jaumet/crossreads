<p>23.5.18<br/>O.C. (Capt. D.A. Whitehead) visited Batt. Hdqrs with reference to siting of M.G's.<br/>Position chosen for C.H.Q. being considered suitable, O.C. took Pioneer Officer and O.C. Batt. Signallers to reconnoitre the position.  Capt. Whitehead visited several houses in Villers-Brettonneux [Villers Bretonneux] with a view to their employment as a Temporary Hqrs., none found suitable.  Visited Batt. in the line of Left Bde. and accompanied C.O. Brigade (Colonel White) round forward system and discussed guns there.  Three existing gun positions were slightly altered and arrangements if possible are to be made to protect the re-entrant on the left flank of the Bde. in P.19.b. &amp; d.<br/>Two thousand rounds were expended by gun situated at O.29.a.80.85 at irregular intervals throughout the night on the following targets:-<br/>(1)  Fork Roads P.26.c.85.38.<br/>(2)  Trenches P.32.a.9.2.<br/>(3)  Road Junction P.31.c.85.65.<br/>(4)  Road Junction V.1.a.73.05.<br/>(5)  Trenches V.1.a.9.7.<br/>(6)  Crossing V.1.b.5.6.<br/>Enemy Artillery shooting was normal throughout the day &amp; night.
