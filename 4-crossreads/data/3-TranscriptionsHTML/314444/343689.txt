<p>Feb 17</p>
<p>My dear Arthur and Catherine<br/>I am very late but must send my love &amp; congratulations on dear Tom's achievement &ndash; such success that his conduct has he tells me been mentioned in "Despatches home" &ndash; I don't think he knows half what that distinction means!<br/>Loving Aunt<br/>F.W H</p>
