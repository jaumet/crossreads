Your news about Mr Lewis Curtis is welcome. I am glad he is doing so well now & hope he will soon be his old self again.
The war is still dragging on at this end though the Hun has got some nasty knocks lately on the Western Front. Our casualties though heavy are not so great as anticipated: I know the Medical Corps made arrangements for coping with a much larger number than we had,
