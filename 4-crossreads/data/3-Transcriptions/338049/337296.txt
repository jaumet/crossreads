<p>Wednesday 27 March<br/>Around streets in morning. Wharves shops etc. Afternoon to [indecipherable]. Saw nose caps being made 15" shells being machined. Practically all girl workers even in 15" room.</p>
<p>Evening met last nights friends &amp; landed in pictures. Home early &amp; a good bye.</p>
