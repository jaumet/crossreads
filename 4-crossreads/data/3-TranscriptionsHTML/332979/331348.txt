<p>arranged by the South African Entertainment Committee. The Artist were a first class lot. There were 4 Ladies &amp; 2 Gentlemen, one Lady gave us a treat on the Violin another had a beautiful Soprano &amp; was encored 3 times before the boys were satisfied one Gent was very amusing as a patter Comedian &amp; had us in roars of laughter likewise one of the Ladies a Comedienne who gave us turn Cecil in the Tube, the Boys could not get enough of her &amp; she received a grand reception. The Second part of the Programe was as good as the first portion. The Artillery Band played selections during the evening, this is a first class band &amp; their playing is always welcome. After having Supper I turned in.</p>
