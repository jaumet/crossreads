<p>Sunday 25 March 1917.</p>
<p>At home on week-end leave. Went on the &quot;Indian&quot; in the morning to McLellands to take snaps.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Camera went crook&nbsp;&ndash; no results&nbsp;expected 8 exposures.</p>
<p>Stayed at home in afternoon &amp; evening. Bed 9 pm (New time.) (Clocks altered.)</p>
