<p>Decembre 12<br/>Getting ready today to move out to Rest Billets tomorrow. 2nd F Coy taking over our work on the Messines Front. Things very quiet on this Front.</p>
<p>Decembre 13<br/>Entrained at De Hanebeek to go out to rest billets near Boulogue. 14th Bde all on the move.</p>
