<p>4.2.15</p>
<p>were very good &amp; arrived home early;  Stan is in the orderly room to-day &amp; Nelson &amp; I represented C Sq. as we were all out for the day &amp; got through some good work;  There is no signalling to-night as "signalitis" reckons we had done enough for one day;  I am putting the night in reading.</p>
<p>Fri 5th<br/>I thought I was in for a good days writing to-day, but made a mistake as Nelson was transferred to Brigade signallers &amp; Stan &amp; I had to take the C Sq. station;  The whole brigade was out to-day &amp; went out nine</p>
