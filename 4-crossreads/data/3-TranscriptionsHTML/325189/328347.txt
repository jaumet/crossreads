We were inspected etc. etc. &amp; then at 12.30 p.m. we marched off to Abbassieh tram terminus, passing en route through the barracks of the garrison.  We tramed to Cairo &amp; then 10 miles further to Mena Camp.  This 10 miles ride only costs 1 piastre 2nd class.  Mena Camp is just near the largest pyramid Cheops.
