
Wednesday 26/9/17Spent day with 26th Bn who are very pleased with stunt 2 Off Killed 3 W.Thursday 27/9/17Rode over to see Bas also had a talk with Hotchkiss the General's old groom.Friday 28/9/17Had letter from Doff & also wrote to her. Dinner with 2nd Div farewell to A.D.Ms 28th Bn bombed - took over command.Saturday 29/9/17Pow-wow about new attack made all arrangements for move.Sunday 30/9/17Left by Car to reconnoitre front line near Zonnebeke [Belgium] returned & rejoined 28th at Canal Area.
