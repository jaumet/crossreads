<p>also use the dogs for drawing purposes you will often see a small hand cart coming along the road &amp; underneath pulling for their lives is one or two big dogs &amp; I can tell you they are willing workers &amp; very strong, they don't believe in keeping useless animals here they all have to do their bit.<br/>They started our tug of war team to-day for the sports which are coming off shortly.<br/>1st June. Last night we had a concert at our billets, the band turned up &amp; played some tunes in between the item's we had a fair crowd present.<br/>This morning we went to the School of Instruction again, the school is a very fine residence easily the best I have seen in France so far, they say the Crown Prince made it his Head-</p>
