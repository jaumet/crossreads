<p>Wednesday<br/>500 Civilians were taken to the hospital as a result of the gas attacks in Armentiers   one pathetic case was that of a mother suffering from gas effect who gave birth to a child in the Ambulance on the way to hospital<br/>The new offensive in Belgium has begun, our troops operating with the French on their left went  over the top this morning &amp; occupied all their objectives taking about 5,000 prisoners the front was about 15 miles long, the depth is not great but heavy &amp; continuous</p>
