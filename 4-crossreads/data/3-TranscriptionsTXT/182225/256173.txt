 1916 &nbsp; TUESDAY 18 &nbsp; JAN. 
 Spes&nbsp;Wharton &amp; Jack Keep are in 15/1 st &nbsp;Brigade &amp; they have a good lot of fellows there &amp; get&nbsp;away 9 th &nbsp;February so I applied for a transfer to their lot &amp; to their tent &amp; got it &nbsp;There are 11 in the tent but they are all real good coves &amp; mates.
