<p>but we did not stay long for it rained fairly heavy, so we were dismissed &amp; had to carry on with "machine gun instruction" in the huts.<br/>There has been a lot of rivalry lately between A. &amp; C Coy. over their platoon drilling &amp; etc so this morning each platoon selected their 6 best men &amp; they were put in a "special squad" &amp; they shaped real well, so there is a promise of a competition coming off.<br/>A few of us went over to the Y.M.C.A. hut tonight to see Charlie Chaplin &amp; a concert which was being run by Old Mac. What a man  he is a man can't give him too much praise  absolutely the finest man in the Division</p>
