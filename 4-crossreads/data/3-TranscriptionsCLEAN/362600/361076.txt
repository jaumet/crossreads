
F 10 SunGlorious Day. Heads from L'don visit Hospital. Recd. Letters from Lottie Watman & Roy. D, E & I go for a stroll around the block. We are all dopey because of early heat. Wrote Mum.George. Dad. Edie.Taub. Maisie Carter
F 11 MonMeeting re dance. I go to S'huy with Bill Tranter. We run all way to station. Visit Mrs E.  & go to close. Have supper at Guest House. We come home via Railway line for last time
F 12 Tues.I go to see Mup Rees at the [indecipherable] others come Home to dear bouts etc. & generally clean up. Darn socks & D & I talk  &  read
F 13 WedWrite Invitations all night. D & I walk around Block Nothing doing much.
W 14 ThArr Ord Corp. Mess around all night. I write invitations all night. I have lots of writing.
I sew on Buttons & darn socks again.
F 15 FriMeeting about Dance. E & I go for walk round Block after Tea. I have to wait up all night to call Roll. We go to see the [indecipherable] It is OK except for D-Tommy, who laugh like a Hyena at everything funny! Recd letter from Reg.
F 16 SatGlorious Day. I go up to get hall ready. Dance a success. I scale a lot of my parties. Have a great time but I am very tired.  Plan Dancing, supper a treat. I go to bed very tired & could not sleep. Receive 4 letters re Dance & let from France with [indecipherable] in French dates from Capt. Robinson. No drinks at dance except E, G & his pal.
While I am at the Dance a Telegram for Dave today. He is leaving for Aussie X
