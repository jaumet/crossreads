  
 October, 1915 Sunday 3 Holy Carpet starts for Mecca today - pity so many soldiers persist in carving their names wherever they go - Here the chalk rocks around the Garden & buildings are all disfigured by Tom JONES WOOLOOMOLOO. etc etc the same at Pyramids & other places.   Carbuncle will block me now for a day or two   (DID you get Medal I posted Registered ere I went to Gallipoli & have Receipt let me know please? Also Pictures from HAY.!
