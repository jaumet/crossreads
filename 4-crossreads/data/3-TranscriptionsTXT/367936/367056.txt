 Tuesday 17 October 1916 Went to Museum then round to tin smiths and got rings. Kursol in evening and home early. 
 Wednesday 18 October 1916 Swim first thing.  Dick &amp; I went into town then out to Nouzha Gardens.  Picture show in evening then dinner and Concert at Alhambra. Did not get back to camp till after one a.m.
