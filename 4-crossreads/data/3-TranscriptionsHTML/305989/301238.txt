<p>Sunday 7 January 1917<br/>Quiet day:  2 taubes dropping 10 bombs among the E.L.C. doing a lot of damage had a go at us but missed.  Had a night air raid:  no damage.  Out on outpost.</p>
<p>Monday 8 January 1917<br/>On "pumping fatigue" started on a divisional stunt to Rafa:  camped at Shiek Zowaiid till 12.30 &amp; on to Rafa.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9 January 1917<br/>Arrived at Magruntein at sunrise &amp; then Rafa just after, crossed the Syrian border &amp; charged into our position carrying ammunition all day:  bad country.  Turks chucked it in at sunset.  Went back to Shiek Zowaiid.</p>
<p>Wednesday 10 January 1917<br/>Arrived at Zowaiid about 1.30, camped till 5.30 &amp; proceeded back to El Arish.</p>
<p>Thursday 11 January 1917<br/>Had swimming parade &amp; went down to the Field Ambulance to see Tonie.  Mr. Harris &amp; Major Irwin all wounded.  Picquet at night.</p>
<p>Friday 12 January 1917<br/>Day stableman:  two Taubes over but did not drop any bombs.</p>
<p>Saturday 13 January 1917<br/>Muster parade.  Gen. Choueval [Chauvel] gave us a lecture of appreciation for the work at Rafa &amp; gave us an idea of our captures.</p>
