<p>July<br/>Sunday 1st<br/>Twelve months since Somme hop over.  Drill day but marked by increased artillery activity in our section and seemed to be a large majority of our 'heavies' pounding Fritz.  Ossy Wood in hospital with gall trouble.  11th Field Amb are losing  number of men recently 6 have come</p>
