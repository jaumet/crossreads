<p>Mon July 24th &ndash; 16<br/>The Germans made a counter attack last night but were repulsed by our artillery.</p>
<p>Wed July 21st &ndash; 16<br/>The enemy is still making attempts to recapture Pozieres but our boys still hold on although they are having a bad time.</p>
<p>Fri July 28th &ndash; 16<br/>Enemy is still attempting to retake village and is shelling our communication trenches very heavily and our boys have dug another to allow fresh men to reach the line  Our infantry have had a fair amount of casualties</p>
