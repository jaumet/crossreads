<p>4th<br/>Rested.</p>
<p>5th<br/>Wood fatigue morning, mail fatigue afternoon.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Firing line.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Firing line.</p>
<p>8th<br/>Water fatigue.  Trench-digging at night.</p>
<p>9th<br/>Water fatigue all day.</p>
<p>10th<br/>Had a birthday.  On water fatigue all day to celebrate it.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Water fatigue.  Firing line at night.  Supposed to be Enver Pasha's birthday.  Was inoculated.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Had a demonstration of our own to-day.  Was under very heavy shrapnel, machine gun &amp; rifle fire for over half an hour, with no cover at all.
