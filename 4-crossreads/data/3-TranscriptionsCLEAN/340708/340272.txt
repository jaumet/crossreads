  
 July 1917 Friday 27th Filled pretty well up today   Fritz has sprung a new sort of gas, it is pretty crook stuff & quite a number have passed Saturday 28th There was a terrible lot of women & children brought from Armentieres today suffering from the effects of this new gas   They look terrible scalded all over they are dying like rats. Sunday 29th We are busy now just about filled right up today. Monday 30th A big evacuation today so we will be slack for a day or so Tuesday 31st The second advance on the Messines sector skirted this morning & so far is progressing favourably.
