<p>Near the mouth of the bay is Robertson island on which is a lighthouse.</p>
<p>16th  At nine o/clock two escorts arrived.  The "Laconia", a boat after the Orient type, about 11 or 12,000 tons, on it are about 12 x 6" guns (auxiliary cruiser)<br/>The H.M.S. "Kent" a light cruiser about 11 or 12,000 tons also arrived.<br/>The "Thermistocles" arrived last night with the 41st (Queensland) Battn.  It is said to have had 36 deaths through Smallpox coming out.<br/>Three nurses came aboard this morning</p>
