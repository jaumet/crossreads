<p>This is Sunday  Morning &amp; I have just been to Church Parade &amp; Holy Communion &amp; thought of you all &amp; sent up a Prayer for you all.<br/>Will you please ask Arch  to send me a statement showing how I stand, &amp; ask him to give Fred Sherwin my address &amp; please ring up Miss Rossitter &amp; tell her of my new address &amp; ask her to tell Beatrice Glasson.<br/>We hear that Rommania  &amp; Bulgaria are at war.<br/>Very much love dear old Girl to you all &amp; Kisses to the [indecipherable].<br/>Your loving Brotheer<br/>Fred</p>
