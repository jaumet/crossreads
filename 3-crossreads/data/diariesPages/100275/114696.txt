I then reported to the Company was put on the strength, was handed out a fine batch of letters which I was very pleased to get.
31st. This morning is a beauty after the rain the sun is shining brightly mud is drying fast as ever it can. I saw the officer got off parade this morning, am now writing this in a fine Y.M.C.A. within a hundred yards of our camp. You can buy apple tarts etc in here not bad is it.
