<p>Saturday 13 January 1917 [1918]<br/>Cold days again &amp; a fine mist &amp; dust in the air.  Ward still very slack only 42 pts. &amp; all convalescent.  No home letters yet for me.  So far 2 from Mother, a p.c. Edith &amp; letter card from Eva comprise all.<br/>Went 14th A.G.H. 8 p.m.</p>
