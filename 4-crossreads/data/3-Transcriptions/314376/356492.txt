<p>181<br/>into bell-tents making ourselves comfortable enough on the wooden floors. There was a Y.M.C.A. erected and I obtained permission from the Secretary in charge of it too to give a hand in the serving. I put in some time at it and I liked the work very well indeed. From here we were moved to the village of Vaux. We heard rumours that the 2nd division were going to bring off a big attack which they did &ndash; but which failed.</p>
<p>We were intended as 1st Division to be supports to the 2nd Division. Owing however to the failure we too had to attack.</p>
