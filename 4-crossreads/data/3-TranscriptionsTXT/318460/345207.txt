 Continued Tuesday 27/2/17 glorious fire tonight Petrol Tin, top cut off &amp; tin Jabbed with Bayonette full of holes about 3 lbs coal rest damp chips etc, still do great. great artillery activity on both sides. 
 Wednesday 28/2/17 definetly known that Fritz is retiring good luck to him may he keep it up. Cloudy &amp; cold at 5.30 am terrific barrage put up by us on the Right must have gone over. no news yet. Fritz sending a lot of Shells over here but doing no damage, hear that we dont go out till April now. Pay 3.4.5. &pound;21.12.0 
 Thursday 1/3/17 Letter from Syd saying Uncle George dead about the 5th Jany. left Loo &amp;  Maude Property but nothing to us Boys Uncle Jack gets the bulk. Wrote Maude, Syd &amp; Viv &amp; Posted them fine &amp; clear plenty of aircraft about &amp; plenty of artillery tonight will be worse Pay &pound;3-5.5 21.13-0 
 Friday 2/3/17 55th came out last night, going back to train for the big stunt coming off soon this will not be a healthy spot, when it does got letters from Maude &amp; Viv Maude did not know of legacy when she wrote it no mention of it. my letter will surprise her. severe frost &amp; fog this morning we get them together here very liberal in France tried hard to snow this afternoon sago fell for awhile Pay &pound;3.6.5 &pound;21/14/0 Posted letter to Loo to day mail goes out tomorrow for Ausi. dug-out fell in this morning lucky we were up or might have been killed one poor chap was, up in the trenches
