It is a case of every man for himself. God Helps those who help themselves. If it was not for the comforts which are sent to me from Australia I would be very badly off. A fair number of Bearers went away to Hospital today.</p>
<p>Wednesday 13th December.<br/>At mid-day we proceed once again to the trenches, to the furthest post. I believe this will be our last time in, as we are supposed to be relieved on Monday 18th inst.<br/>A very quiet night thank God, but at 10 a.m. the following day Fritz started shelling heavily. We were kept busy all day. Bob Roberts &amp; Esbert Smith had rather a trying experience picking up wounded men out of an old trench, whilst Fritz was shelling heavily. Six men were killed near by. Engineers I believe. Raining</p>
