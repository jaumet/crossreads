
Wednesday 16th Dec 1914Very pretty sunrise. Nothing startling happened. Knocked off guard at 2 o'clock &  went into the city at 3. Went out to Heliopolis. Very good & large stone buildings. Had a look all over Luna Park. Has tea &  didn't pay for it. Bought a fez (cap). Came home at 11 P.M.
Thursday. 17th. Dec 1914Worked on Sergeants Mess all day. 4 of us went into city in the evening &  bought &  stole a few items. Payed 100 piastres for a shawls &  50 for others. Payed altogether 150 piastres for 4 silk shawls & 1 silk handkerchief. Am sending 1 to MG. & 3 home to mother & Chris & Madge. Got home at 11.
Friday. 18th. Dec 1914.Worked on Sergeants MessEgypt made a British Protectorate &  a 101 shots were fired as a salute from the citadel. All leave stopped for us on account of a man being absent in the city without leave. Tom Norton's brother came over to see me.
Saturday. 19th. Dec 1914Al leave was stopped yesterday because a sapper was away without leave.
