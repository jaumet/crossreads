<p>about embarking for Aussie. I hopped out had breakfast and buzzed off to Bde where I was told to report to No1 Command Depot Sutton Veny to await embarkation on the Derbyshire for Australia. 750 men are leaving this depot for the same boat next Sunday. Altogether she is booked to carry 1700 troops.  Lieuts Cockram Austin and Langford and myself were allotted one large cubicle.  We are a very fine crowd of fellows Ahem.</p>
<p>Herb came along from Bde Hqrs mid afternoon where he is working in the Records Office</p>
