Experienced a very touching good-bye at Vieux Berguin they were very sorry we had to leave. I was sorry too it was a very pleasant village all hands enjoyed their stay there. 29-5-17. Had the afternoon off & sauntered forth on the bike to try & find Charlie Griffith & Acko & met with success. Found Acko on the corner near their camp & was told Griff would be already with his team any minute so we sat down on the edge of the ditch & waited.
