<p>Tuesday 25th</p>
<p>Napsbury Hosp : nothing doing in morn : sister said I could get up for a shave but had to go back to bunk again : leg still stiff : lady visitor in afternoon &amp; she gave me some fruit, paper &amp; scented soap : Aust Red Cross girls round too but I was asleep so they left some smokes for me</p>
<p>Wednesday Sept 26th</p>
<p>Napsbury Hosp : nothing doing during day : merry boys in the ward &amp; generally end up with a few songs before "lights out"</p>
<p>Thursday Sept 27th</p>
<p>Napsbury Hosp : nothing doing in morn : Salvation Army women round in afternoon &amp; gave me some chocolate &amp; papers to read : drew khaki as we are leaving here tomorrow</p>
