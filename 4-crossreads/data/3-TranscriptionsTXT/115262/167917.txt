 Wednesday 30 May 1917. 
 Fell in with guard at 8.45 a.m. 
 Inspected on boat deck above and then marched to guard room in the forecastle. 
 Mounted duty on No 9 post port aft. at 1 till 2 pm.; 2 to 3 pm (No 5 deck) No 8 post.&nbsp;relieved at 3 pm. marched back to guard room &amp; dismissed until 7 p.m. when I go on till 9 pm. 
 Tea at 5 pm. &nbsp;Mounted guard at No 9 post&nbsp; port aft &nbsp;at 7 pm till 9 pm.&nbsp; 
 Slept on deck for 1 &frac12;&nbsp; &nbsp;hr s &nbsp;or so &amp; then a monsoonal wind sprung up in 5 mins &amp; was compelled to go below &amp; sleep in guard room&nbsp; 
 Air stifling here &amp; could not sleep
