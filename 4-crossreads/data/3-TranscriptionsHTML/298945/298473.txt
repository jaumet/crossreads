<p>The predominant thought in my mind in competition with the wondering about our Corps rest, is of leave. I am now about 30 on the list so, with five going per week I have only 6 weeks to wait for mine.</p>
<p>Ones thoughts are almost always divided between leave, the end of the war &amp; returning home. Yet it has been said that there will be no possibility of the Australians being all home under eighteen months from the declaration of Peace.
