<p>7 Th June<br/>Still going   wondering when it is going to cease  too tired to do anything  Usual on front</p>
<p>8 Fri June<br/>Very quiet day. Although Aeroplanes had a duel or two  Leaving for Cairo on duty [as a groom?]</p>
<p>9 Sat June<br/>Arrived in Cairo at 5.30.  very tired and sleepy</p>
