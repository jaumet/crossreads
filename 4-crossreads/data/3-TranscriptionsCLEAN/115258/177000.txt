You see! we had only carried an over coat and a bivvy sheet with us, and, on account of the hot days, our saddle clothes were pretty wet with sweat by night time. 'C' Sqdn had to do a patrol out along the line on the 28th so we left camp at 4.45AM, watered our horses, and rode along the wadi down to Kulat Zerka. That was one of the prettiest rides I have ever had I think.
