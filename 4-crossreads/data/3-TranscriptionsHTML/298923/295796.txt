<p>10 May 1918 Friday<br/>Leave.  Spent the night on the Nile, cooler &amp; today did some more business for Dolly- an expensive luxury Babies!!!<br/>We go to Dinner tonight at Continental Hotel it is a change for Dolly from Pension &amp; she loves going out.<br/>The "Trumpeter" is well &amp; I'm sure you will be pleased to hear about the event!<br/>How about a name?  Ive got real Jordan Water for the Christening!!</p>
