This was all great fun for us But the range was shortened &amp; we got them one or two quite close It was our turn to duck &amp; someone elses to laugh. It was all over in about 10 or 15 minutes &amp; no one hurt. These are the first shell that have burst any way close to me so far. One or two Hun Planes over this evening so we will probably get more shells in a day or so</p>
<p>Sunday 10<br/>Same job. Pross &amp; I went salvaging this afternoon got a full tin of Biscuits 8 tins Bullie 3 of Jam. Besides other odds &amp; ends to furnish the dugout. You can pick up almost anything about here. What a terrible waste war is</p>
<p>Monday 11th<br/>The daily round as usual Go out at 6 AM &amp; back at Midday. It is about 1&frac14; miles to the job</p>
