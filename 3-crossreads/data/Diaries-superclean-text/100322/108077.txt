17/5/19
Southampton
A fine sunny morning, the precursor of a hot day. The water still and calm and the gentle slopes of English shores covered with low woods and houses embowered in trees. We got off the boat at 7.30 formed up in a big shed and got breakfast there. At the same pier was the mammoth Aquitania and in a few minutes gallant Anzacs of an exploratory turn were all over her until ejected by an anxious ship's staff. One Billjim excited much mirth by putting his hand in his pocket and solemnly asking a decorous and dignified purser how much he wanted for the ship.
