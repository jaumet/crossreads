<p>25th.  "Oh what a night we had never a wink of sleep, &amp; nearly frozen to death &amp; it rained like blue blazes, so when we crawled out this morning we were wet to the skin &amp; aching all over, a more miserable looking lot of men you never saw, for the rain beat in on us all the night &amp; the roofs of our waterproofs leaked &amp; our overcoats sucked up the cold moisture from the ground, it's a wonder to me how on earth men stand it for the conditions are awful at present &amp; show no signs of bettering for the rain is still pouring down &amp; we are like blocks of ice, some of the chaps are lucky Len among them for they have decent dugouts.
