
7 May 1918 TuesdayArrived Kantara & telegraphed Nowell to meet me at Ishmalia for a few minutes yarnArrived Cairo midday, found Dolly well- she met me on the railway station & looks splendid-   Has a nice cool pension & looks far better than I expected the event takes place around 12th August-Did not see Nowell at Ishmalia.
