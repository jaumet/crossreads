  
 Sunday Feb 17th This is my first entry since I left England. With 8 vessels on Convoy & 5 or 6 destroyers & Cruisers, we left Plymouth at 12 Sunday 3rd. The sea was pretty lumpy for 3 days & many were sick. Regulations were very strict as regards lights & lifebelts. No lights on deck not even allowed to smoke, & Port holes closed day & night & life belts to be worn all day.
