
[Letter from Maurice Alfred Fergusson]France7.4.18
Dear Mrs GarlingFergusson is my name, poor old Terry and I were the best of pals and fellow officers for a long time; so I am writing to you to give you my deepest sympathy.Terry was a son to be proud of, I can't say enough for him, he was just about the bravest man I have ever known and the 'whitest'.
