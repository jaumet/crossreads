<p>November 1915<br/>Saturday 20<br/>Very cold wind blowing from south &amp; changing rapidly to S West &amp; North. We are in splendid trenches here. Our aeroplane did some daring reconnaissance work this morning. Some more fine shooting at an enemy aeroplane.</p>
<p>Sunday 21<br/>Wind still blowing from the north. On orderly duty today at the Signal Office</p>
