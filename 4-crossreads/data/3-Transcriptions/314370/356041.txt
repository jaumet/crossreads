<p>June, 1916<br/>Sunday 18<br/>wake up in our new home, completely disgusted<br/>No rations yet.<br/>At 4 pm tents arrive &amp; are erected 14 men per tent</p>
<p>Monday 19<br/>Went to Tel-el-Kebir Rifle Range w/- a company of reinforcements.<br/>shooting was simply awful men have never had any proper instruction.<br/>splendid walk out &amp; on the way back I sniped a large bird in a tree at 60 yds w/- my pistol</p>
