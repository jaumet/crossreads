 Thursday 4th November. Joined our respective companies in the battalion. Captain Stacey in charge of Company "D" We are in reserves &amp; have our quarters dug into the sides of the communication saps. "Jacko" had a bit of a demonstration affair our first night here. Bullets like bees. 
 Tues. 9th Nov. Our first payday on ANZAC today. Paid the large sum of 10/- by Capt. B.V. Stacey. 
 Sat 13th Nov: -  The first casualty the 8th regts of the 4th occurred today pte A J Stanhope got a bit of a shell up against his ankle as he was lying in a dugout. 
 Thurs 20th Nov. Paid &pound; 2.10.0 today by Cpt. Stacey. Received letter from Mother  &amp; the first letters I've had since I left Aust were only received just a week ago. Written from Anzac to everyone but the outgoing mails are reported to be capsized  a Lemnos. I wonder if we'll receive a Christmas mail. Still in reserves.
