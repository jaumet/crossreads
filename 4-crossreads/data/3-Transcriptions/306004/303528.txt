<p>evening; to-day has been rather hot, but the nights get quite chilly here coming on morning;</p>
<p>17.9.16<br/>Sun 17th<br/>Rose at midnight last night &amp; left Bir-El-Abd at 1.30 arrived at Hod &ndash;El-Salmana at daybreak watered our horses here &amp; pushed on arriving at  our destination 18 miles from Bir-El-Abd &amp; 7 from Bir-El-Mazur where the  Turks are entrenched at 10 AM.   We could hear the Artillery guns banging away all the morning as we came out &amp; at &amp; oclock this afternoon we heard the attack was a failure as the Turks were too strongly entrenched to be driven back; The 2nd &amp; 3rd Brigade attacked &amp; had very few</p>
