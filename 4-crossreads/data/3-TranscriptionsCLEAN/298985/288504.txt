  
 Thursday 18 Only one dose of greese today Went over to Training Ball in evening & went to Pictures with Hall. They were good. 
 Friday 19th Had a bonnie bath & was turned out of isolation. Equiped for over sea & put into C. Co for next Tuesdays Draft 
 Saturday 20th Fell in with full packs for kit inspection It consisted of seeing we all had our disks stamped properly
