<p>7177 miles, 1119 to go.</p>
<p>Sunday 5/12/15<br/>Very warm.  Overtook &amp;amp: passed in very close proximity to large steamer (name unknown), when abreast signalled wished us good luck.  One of our boys signalled back &amp; thanked them for their good wishes.  Our band played a few selections while abreast,  Actually saw a lady on board, first of fair sex seen for a month.  <br/>Mail closes in orderly room 9 pm tonight, all very busy writing letters, as it will be last opportunity for writing on board.  Very heavy wind &amp; rain storm during afternoon.  Thunder exceptionally heavy.  Church Parade</p>
