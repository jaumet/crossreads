
Sunday 9 September 1917Sky Pilot parade:  swapped jobs & "slipped";  wrote Nos. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 2].  Swimming a good part of the day.
Monday 10 September 1917Preparing for inspection by G.O.C., got all gear clean, on picquet at night.
Tuesday 11 September 1917New Zealand band gave a concert in evening at Y.M.C.A., very good.
Wednesday 12 September 1917Sports all day.  (Schoolers Benefit).  Saw Gudge Brown:  concert and prize giving at night.  Brig. in great form.
Thursday 13 September 19172nd Brigade gave us a concert at night, very good.  On Sanitary fatigue.
Friday 14 September 1917On "Pump fatigue", exercising horses & picquet at night.
Saturday 15 September 1917Concert by Pyjama Pianos very fine turnout.
