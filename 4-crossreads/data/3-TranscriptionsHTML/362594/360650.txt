<p>[Mars 16 Dimanche]<br/>Church Parade<br/>Very calm<br/>Passed islands</p>
<p>[17 Lundi]<br/>Arrived at Colombo 5 p.m.  entered port with the yellow flag flying.  Disappointed at not going ashore.  Cable from home very pleased.  Lost &pound;3 at [indecipherable] let this be a warning useless waste of money.</p>
<p>[18 Mardi]<br/>Coaling &amp; watering.  Officer of the watch.</p>
