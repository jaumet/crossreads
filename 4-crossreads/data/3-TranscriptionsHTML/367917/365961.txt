<p>Northern France Disgraceful with their Dirty work it made our Blood Boil when we passed Through the Villages, they left some Villages &amp; Towns in a shocking condition Some Towns had <u>Certain Desease</u> which I cannot tell you &amp; the military put them out of Bounds to all Troops</p>
<p>The Civilians were left Starving they stole all their Cattle &amp; when we passed Through their Cry was "<u>Mungie</u>" (Bread), we gave them our Rations Poor Devils</p>
