<p>[Tuesday 28th November 1916]<br/>We will be at <strike>Alexandria</strike> by this time tomorrow &amp; I am sending you a Cable to say arrived safely-<br/>A smooth passage &amp; we have picked up another escort so should be safe <strike>now- &amp; the weather is quite warm again.<br/>Communion service in the Smoke room I took part as I was thankful to be still alive.<br/>Rooke's grandfather was James Cochrane but he knew nothing about the Weir family.</strike></p>
