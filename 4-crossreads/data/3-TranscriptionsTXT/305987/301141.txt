 Tuesday 17th:  Relieved 1st Div one Outpost on our right Wednesday 18th:  Relieved. Came back to Vaulx. Friday 20th:  In sunken road in rear of Lagnicourt in supports to 7th Bgde Tuesday 24th:  Relieved. Came back to trench in rear of Vaulx. Sunday 29th:  Back to Biefvillers Tuesday May 1st:  To Vaulx Wednesday 2nd:  Moved up to front line 10-30 p.m. for Bullecourt. Thursday 3rd:  Out to "hop off" tape 3 a.m Barrage opened 3-45. Got cut up. Had to retire. 6 a.m. advanced again &amp; occupied O.G.1. Bombing all day.
