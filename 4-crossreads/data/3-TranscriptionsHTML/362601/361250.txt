<p>10 August, Sunday</p>
<p>Chop wood in morning. Geo &amp; I go to Narrabeen see Livo. Come Home &amp; stay. Moonlight &amp; I think of Island. I play piano until Dad &amp; Mum go to bed. Mum is melancholy.</p>
<p>M 19</p>
<p>11 August, Monday</p>
<p>Mum &amp; I go to Manly shopping. Visit Mrs Stephen, Dobbies &amp; Whiteheads &amp; Creightons. We buy Ellis's presents &amp; soda syphon.</p>
<p>Wrote to Mr Paul.</p>
