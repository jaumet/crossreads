<p>Sunday 30 June 1918<br/>Went out to Findon in morng.  Tead at Perivale in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Monday 1 July 1918<br/>One of the Yankees crashed on the beach this morng.  Propeller was smashed but pilot not hurt.</p>
<p>Tuesday 2 July 1918<br/>My eyes are still giving me trouble.  Am wearg. dark glasses occasionally.</p>
<p>Wednesday 3 July 1918<br/>Bathed in sea afternoon, not too warm.  Breakers not so good as the Manly ones!!</p>
<p>Thursday 4 July 1918<br/><strike>The water was top hole &amp; warm.</strike>  Took Rup &amp; Muriel to Salvington Mill in a trap &amp; very small pony!!  Tennis with Chicken later.</p>
<p>Friday 5 July 1918<br/>Tead at the Atkinson's &amp; beaucoup Tennis afterwards.</p>
<p>Saturday 6 July 1918<br/>Leave up to Town by the 2 p.m.  Stayed at Highgate.</p>
