<p>Memoranda</p>
<p>Enlisted on August 17th 14<br/>Embarked on October 19th 14<br/>Arrived in Egypt Dec. 9th 14<br/>Left Maddi for Heliopolis &ndash; 29/1/15<br/>Left Heliopolis for Gallipoli &ndash; 7/5/15<br/>Left Gallipoli for Malta &ndash; 17/9/15<br/>Left Malta for Lemnos &ndash; 23/10/15<br/>Left Lemnos for Anzac &ndash; 3/11/15<br/>Left on avacuation for Lemnos &ndash; 19/12/15<br/>Landed in Alexandria &ndash; 29/12/15<br/>Left Heliopolis for Wardan &ndash; 14/1/16<br/>Left Wardan for Bahnasa &ndash; 26/2/16<br/>Left Bahnasa for Minia &ndash; 27/3/16<br/>Left Minia for Kantara &ndash; 14/5/16<br/>Arrived at Romani &ndash; 18/5/16<br/>Left Romani for Kantara &ndash; 29/9/16<br/>Left Kantara for Guila &ndash; 1/11/16<br/>Guilia to Arnoussi to Yerarat to El Arish &ndash; 23/12/16<br/>Left El Arish for Bayud &ndash; 10/2/17<br/>Left Bayud for El Arish &ndash; 17/3/17<br/>Left El Arish for Khan Yunis &ndash; 6/4/17</p>
