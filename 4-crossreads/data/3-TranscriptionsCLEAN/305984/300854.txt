  
 26/12/17 until 12 oclock plenty of fun amongst ourselves 
 27/12/17 Snow again today  not nice  it was very cold all day 
 28/12/17 It is hard to walk around the compound because of the frozen snow.  The boys sliding on the ice 
 29/12/17 Nothing to note  the ice still in all water holes 
 30/12/17 The old Wabbler & young on leave the Q Master is in charge. a beauty he is watchmaker would stop until one out paraded three times today hellish cold 
 31/12/17 More left for holland exchange today. 
 1/1/18 Midnight service last night  still cold  Boys throw bucket of water on the ground & have made a fine slide  they were at it all day  80 more men for holland
