<p>Dover<br/>[DECEMBER]<br/>Monday 3<br/>Marched to wharf and boarded the S.S Villa De Leigh but after waiting an hour, the port was closed<br/>Tuesday 4<br/>Spent a good day in Dover a very nice place. Left for home (France) Arrived at Calais for dinner<br/>Wednesday 5<br/>Stopped at Calais for the night which was severly bombed by the enemy<br/>Thursday 6<br/>After a ride of six hours in the train we got to Castre and then motored to Steenwurk</p>
