 I have been tonight how to play draughts &ndash; you would be amused if you saw me. I have read two of Masefield's books &ndash; Multitude &amp; Solitude &amp; Captain Margaret &ndash; I liked them both. Am reading the Price of Love by Arnold Bennet as usual he depresses [indecipherable] his sordidness. Dr Jekyl &amp; Mr Hyde I read without much interest. I have the Higher Critisism of the Bible on board in case I fall short of mental stimulus. Devoted love to you my precious mother. Do rest and come home refreshed &amp; strong. Devotedly your daughter Betha
