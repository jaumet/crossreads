  
 Sept. 17th More Australian & English Letters, all of which contain good bright news - "Non-Suited" still going strong. Rehearsal of "Eastern Romance". 
 Sept. 18th Rehearsal & Show - Change of programme - Letter writing etc. - My memory plays me false for the first time in the History of "The Coves" - I recite part of Xmas Carol, and then suddenly came to an abrupt stop.
