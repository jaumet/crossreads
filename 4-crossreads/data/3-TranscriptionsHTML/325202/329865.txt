<p>The people in these little village have never had such a shaking up as this war. It is like some irresistible force to them <strike> to have some</strike> for a sleepy hollow of a place <strike>to ha</strike> with perhaps a couple of hundred inhabitants to suddenly have 1500 rollicking soldiery billetted on them. They live half way between fright &amp; laughter</p>
<p>The other night I saw a 'fresh' soldier in the crowded room of an estaminet juggling with 2 empty beer bottles &amp; 2 glass tumblers the faces of Madame &amp; her daughter were a picture</p>
