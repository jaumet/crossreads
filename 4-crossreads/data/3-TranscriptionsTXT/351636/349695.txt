 Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers 11.2.16 
 Dear Mr. Layton, Mrs. Lindley who has been told by you that the debt on her sewing machine was paid by L.M.F. is threatened by Singer to have her machine removed as it is not paid for. Could you give her some receipt to show to them &ndash; 
 Through sickness after childbirth 7 wks ago she has got a little into debt could you assist her with that &amp; oblige 
 Yours truly E. H. Bennie.
