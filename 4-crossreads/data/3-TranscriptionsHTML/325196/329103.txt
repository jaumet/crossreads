<p>2-2-16<br/>for another man to be sent out &amp; Les left here by the 7 oclock train; Nelson received a pass to visit Cairo today &amp; we had the same old parade of mounted work till 2.P.M. &amp; then finish for the day;  This afternoon C Troop of A.Sqd played D troop football; The 2nd Regt. also had a match going</p>
<p>3-2-16<br/>Thurs 3rd.<br/>Mounted drill again this morning; this afternoon 2 teams from this Regt played a football match I was playing &amp; managed to get filled up with sand &amp; was beaten by 3 points to nil. Received a parcel of medicines from Edie today</p>
