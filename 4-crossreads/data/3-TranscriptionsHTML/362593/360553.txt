<p>Wednesday 22 May 1918<br/>Photos, Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 2 h.  Uneventfull.</p>
<p>Thursday 23 May 1918<br/>Taking aspect photos of Hun which was driven down over Turks line.  Pilot Lt. Paul.<br/>Excellent photos.<br/>1000 ft. over Hun lines firing like hell at us, fired 200 rounds.<br/>In air 1 h. 30 m.</p>
