  
 Tuesday June 5 Sailing to day no leave granted. OC & some officers went ashore. We were told our name stank in Colombo. Much discontent on board and a very indiscreet remark for an officer to make. Boys busy exchanging coins & Buying fruit Left Port at 2pm Wednesday June 6 OC. Transport made himself unpopular by prohibiting Queenslanders to enter sports. Result all hands pulling out. Thursday June 7 Fire broke out in coal bunker no cause for alarm. Flying fish Sword fish very numerous. Friday June 8 Full dress inspection on a scorching Summer's day O
