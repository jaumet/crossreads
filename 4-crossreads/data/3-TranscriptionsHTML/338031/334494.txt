<p>an official inspection was announced there was an all round improvement in the fare provided &ndash; both in quantity and quality; &amp; then of course there were no complaints. One day, however, Colonel Abbott unexpectedly swooped upon the mess room at dinner time to investigate a complaint about "dishcloth" tea. He at once sampled it &amp; made a wry face in doing so.<br/>"Taste that," he said to the officer in charge.<br/>The officer did so &amp; said he did not think there was anything wrong with it.<br/>"Would you like to drink it yourself?" pursued the Colonel</p>
