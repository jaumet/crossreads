<p>Memoranda<br/>7th Jan 1917<br/>About 5 pm the Kent left us and all the transports went in different directions.<br/>5. 30 10 destroyers met us but only one remained.</p>
<p>Mond. 8 Jan.  very rough spray coming on to the boat deck.  Passed destroyer this morning and sometimes the waves went right over her. (45)</p>
