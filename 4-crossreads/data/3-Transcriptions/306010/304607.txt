<p>had a good look through &ndash; very pretty. After dinner in town I went off to a French lesson. Have put in 5 evenings of French fag last week with various people who are helping me &ndash; seem to be making pretty fair progress.<br/>Tomorrow a Sgt. &amp; 2 of our men leave Marseille for  Australia via Le Harve &amp; England to be discharged from the A.I.F. as permanently unfit &ndash; all after some months of active service.<br/>Yesterday we got 20 rank &amp; file as replacements from Base Depot, Etaples. (80)<br/>Friday 9" June<br/>Yesterday afternoon paid a visit to the Church of Notre Dame de la garde situated on a great hill from which one looks down on the whole city with the green clad hills in the background and the harbour</p>
