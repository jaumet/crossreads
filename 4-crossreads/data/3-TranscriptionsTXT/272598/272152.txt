 Monday 12.2.17 to Wednesday 14.2.17 Weather getting warmer, snow &amp; ice beginning to thaw.  Several heavy bombardments have taken place.  Received parcel from home containing putties.  Rations very poor &amp; scanty.  Reg evacuated to C.C.S. Lads again went into line for a week.  Walked to Albert to try &amp; find 3rd Div Artillery, but was misinformed for they are still up North. 
 Thursday 15.2.17 Hospital full.  Ground getting very muddy owing to thaw setting in.
