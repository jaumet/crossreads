<p>The boys who were to proceed per "iron-horse" (bike) were ordered to start at once for St Leger for new stores on our return to call at Bertacourt where we slept in a hay cart for a couple of nights when our main party joined us again.</p>
<p>It was just at this time that we heard Fritz had broken through that portion of the line we were holding in Flanders a few days previous, and now we have to go back again tomorrow.</p>
