  
  
 30th  Left Cape Town.  Outside the harbour started rolling.  Many interested  in the  hugging the railings in a loving embrace appear interested in the water. rolling increased  Learning  expression appears in many faces.   Speed of boat  As one of our poets has expressed it 'the eternal main thing is to see the main spring"  The spring is, of course, upwards & not forwards.  In fact at one time it appeared doubtful she was moving at all, for a lighthouse off our [indecipherable] she seemed to be holding its own. 
 31st  This being the day of rest the Main is to be commended in having made better headway.  She is now pitching as well as rolling.  General financial depression as a result of gay life in Cape Town. Unaccountable popularity of pay staff.  Many offers of refreshment in harbour.
