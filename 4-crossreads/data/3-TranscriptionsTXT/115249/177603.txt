 Naval Signal 
 From - &nbsp;R.A. 
 To - Brigadier 
 Date - 14/9/14 
 Time - 6.20 
 Following received from Comdr. Beresford. Have had no news whatever from Colonel Watson nor has firing been heard. German residents&nbsp; here &nbsp;are of opinion that no resistance will be offered but enemy will keep retiring for many miles (1745)
