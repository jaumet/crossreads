<p>I shall return to Australia when the War ends &ndash; No word of going back yet.<br/>Collected 214 Bedouins today. They live in caves, Donkeys &amp; all together, &amp; are very poor &ndash;<br/>See Diary<br/>March 19th<br/>I do love to get home news &amp; to hear All's well &ndash; Can't understand my cable not reaching you I sent it Dec 15th &ndash;<br/>20th<br/>Move postponed owing to Jordan being in flood anyway I am staying here &ndash; with details &ndash; the regiment will be away some days &ndash; Still wet &ndash; Rain saved the men a lot during the last fortnight.</p>
