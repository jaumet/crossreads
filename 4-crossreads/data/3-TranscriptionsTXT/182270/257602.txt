 FRIDAY, OCTOBER 17, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; hot. Lopping in Mountain in 
 the morning &amp; measured up the 
 Boundary scrub for Radcliff in afternoon. 
 Darby down 175 ft &amp; no water yet. 
 Tickle drawing water to Darby 
 all day. 
 Peter &amp; I shifted my office desk into 
 my room at night.
