  
  Saturday 21 April 1917.   
 In camp and on fatigue duty. On ASC. fatigue lumping stores & loading & unloading wagons during morning and in afternoon on AM.C. QM's fatigue.   
 Rather easy in afternoon   and was able to get washing done.   
 Camp seems deserted. All on extended leave. 2.400 troops marched out on leave today.    Did some w a shing
