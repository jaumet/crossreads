 at Armentieres : the old town has been badly battered &amp; is in ruins : walked to Bois Grenier &amp; seen the lads graves from Lithgow : old "streaky bacon" &amp; other places knocked down : caught train to Hazelbruick &amp; had a look round there : home to Dunkirk about 9.30 pm : 
 Tuesday Dec 10th to Thursday 12th 
 on duty 10 &ndash; 6 nothing much doing : going on Blighty leave on the 15th 
 Friday Dec 13th Mardyk 
 went on sick parade &amp; seen the quack re being clean for leave : packed up during day 
 Saturday Dec 14th Mardyk 
 caught the 7 am train to Boulogne &amp; went to the rest billet : couldn't get on the boat mob so had a look round the town : not a bad sort of place : 
 Sunday Dec 15th Boulogne 
 caught the first boat to Folkestone &amp; thence to London per train : got away from Horseferry Rd about 4 pm &amp; went to the Aldwych hut &amp; got a bed &amp; clean up : pictures at night 
 Monday Dec 16th London 
 made some enquires at H.Q. in the morn &amp; went out to see Bel Muir's people in afternoon : made very welcome indeed &amp; have to call again :
