 1917 June 
 28 Thursday 
 Arose at 8 &amp; went to 66 Victoria St to report. Met Jean Lindsay &amp; Nurse Margaret Scott Russell. Then we went the rounds at B. Red Cross. Dinner at Wilton Hotel with Jean Lindsay. Then home &amp; had a rest. Nell came &amp; did my arm filling broke out of tooth again.
