<p>to the wharf &amp; we lined up &amp; marched up to the barracks. I was warned for duty <strike>at</strike> for 2 p.m.  As it was then 10A.M. I didn't have much time to look round &amp; buy things at the same time. I decided to do the shopping.  I went off with Lts Young Reynolds &amp; McIntyre to the Galle Face Hotel in Rickshaws along a good asphalt road built along the sea beach.</p>
<p>Rickshaws with their dusky human beasts of burden were dashing about every where.</p>
