<p>Sep 11 &ndash; 17</p>
<p>Fri 11th<br/>Arrived in camp at 9 AM  &amp; had some fun watching riding test; had the day off on account of vaccination.</p>
<p>Sat 12th<br/>Raining a treat;  Passed the riding test signalling the rest of day, went to the city after tea</p>
<p>Sun 13th<br/>Church Parade, issued with extra kit bag, bandoliers etc. raining strong went Felicia for tea &amp; then to station &amp; saw mother &amp; father off</p>
<p>Mon 14th<br/>Squad drill morning  flag drill afternoon.  went to Redfern &amp; passed the evening at a benefit</p>
<p>Fri 15th<br/>Flag drill morning innoculated against fever at dinner time, to Felicia evening &amp; had bad time from innoculation</p>
<p>Wed 16th<br/>Flag drill all day, had photos taken at the Crown Studios evening saw Jessie for awhile coming back from Burwood</p>
<p>Thurs 17th<br/>Raining all day.  no drill wrote letters all afternoon, went to Felicia</p>
