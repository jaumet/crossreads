<p>[Reverse of postcard on previous page.]<br/>France<br/>8/1/17</p>
<p>Dear Frank</p>
<p>The picture on the front is replica of what we are doing to Fritz's trenches with our mortars. Every day we give him some to keep him busy. He does not love us. I can assure you.<br/>Am keeping well and in the best of condition<br/>Things generally here are good. Can't say much. We are giving Fritz hell<br/>Your affect Bro<br/>Eddie</p>
