  
 Charles Hargreaves was born on Sept 7th 1886 in "Westbrook Cottage" Ilkley, Yorkshire - sixth & youngest son of Charles & Ellen Hargreaves. 
 he came to Australia in 1911 & spent the first twelve months working on "Mimosa Station", Temora, N.S.W. From there he went to Tasmania & together with a friend bought an apple orchard in the Tamar Valley. 
 In need of more money Charles Hargreaves went to Melbourne, but decided to seek work in Sydney & so began with Messrs John Sands Ltd. printers as he had worked in Yorkshire & London with Messrs Percy Lund Humphries , printers, friends of his parents.
