The orange orchards are a mass of golden fruit & every lane or road is hedged with wattle or prickly pear on either side. The farther north you go the prettier &  more thickly populated the country becomes.
At Tel esh Sharia where the "fertile plains of Philistia" start the country is rolling flat & monotonous.
