<p>left in by the Dr at Etaps by Jove it stung when it was coming out but what a relief the side started to go down straight away &amp; the pain left as if by magic, the nurse took the little piece away &amp; washed it &amp; then <strike>then</strike> put it in a little cardboard box &amp; wrote my name on it, I think I will send the piece home for a souvenir</p>
<p>The second day we were here it snowed like one thing nearly 2 ft deep it laid for the next couple of days but after this the weather cleared up &amp; it is now beautiful but we are not allowed out of the Ward as yet today I got up for the first time &amp; I am now toddling about with the aid of a walking stick, I shant be long getting convalescent now for</p>
