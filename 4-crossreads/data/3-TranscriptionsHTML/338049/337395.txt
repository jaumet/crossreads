<p>Thursday 4 July<br/>Heavy barrage in morning and attack made by Austn. Divs. 1 &frac12; miles advance on 4 mile front 1,500 prisoners. Very satisfactory.</p>
<p>Whytecross relieved. Moroney forward. assistance of horses refused.</p>
<p>Wrote a little.</p>
