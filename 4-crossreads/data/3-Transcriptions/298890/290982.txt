<p>31 Sunday<br/>Ethel Bull &amp; I spent it together. Cold &amp; dull. I worked hard.</p>
<p>April 1918<br/>1.  Monday<br/>Foggy &amp; Showery. Worked late. Shoulder ached. Miss Smith returned.</p>
<p>2 Tuesday<br/>Showers &amp; sun. worked more.</p>
<p>3. Wednesday<br/>Worked some between getting meals.</p>
