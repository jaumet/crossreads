<p>&amp; joined at Folkestone the Channel ferry. Arrived at Boulogne 4.30 pm where I found Wilkins waiting with the car. We reached Steenvorde at 10 pm.</p>
<p>21st Octr.  Sunday.   [Photo Nos 62, 66-70] <br/>Went to Ypres &amp; made several pictures of the Cloth Hall. A great number of Canadian troops are now parsing through to take over the section held by the Australians &amp; New Zealanders. As the town is under balloon observation by the Boche, it received a heavy shelling, especially the Poperinghe gate. Here he dropped shells throughout the afternoon &amp; killed some 40 men, as well as holding up traffic. On my way home I had to motor by this way, &amp; raced the car through the shell fire.
