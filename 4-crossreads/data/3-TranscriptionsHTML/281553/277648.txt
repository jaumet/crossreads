<p>8-30 &ndash; 9-15 with Naval assistance.  Rumoured Turks using Gass.</p>
<p>8th Friday<br/>Bread &amp; Meat issued daily this week.  Turks Artillery very quiet lately.</p>
<p>9th Saturday<br/>Weather threatening all day culminating in severe Gale about 8 p.m. with rain about &frac12; hour, clearing later.</p>
<p>10th Sunday<br/>Weather settled but Beach much effected by gale also water supply pumps damaged.</p>
