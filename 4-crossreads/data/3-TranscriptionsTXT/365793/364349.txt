 Spt 4 Sun-kissed waves, for there is a stiff westerly blowing, &amp; the white horses are out for keeps &ndash; All piffle about the propeller shaft. The men played the Officers cricket, &amp; towled them up by an innings &amp; 6 runs. And after the match the Nurses had a little game. A couple of waves came well over the saloon deck &amp; hunted the majority of the boys below. 
 5 Still running before a heavy sea &amp; making the pace a welter. Good luck to it too &ndash; every day sees us a few hundred miles nearer home. Played
