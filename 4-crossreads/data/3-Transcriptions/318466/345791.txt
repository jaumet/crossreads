<p>Sunday 12 May 1918<br/>Went home to Highgate for the day.  Have to wear dark glasses &amp; shade when I am out.</p>
<p>Monday 13 May 1918<br/>Rumours of an Air Raid which did not eventuate.</p>
<p>Tuesday 14 May 1918<br/>The bus conductors are very decent &amp; always let the wounded chaps on before the rush.  Am gettg. to know one of 'em on the Charing X run!!</p>
<p>Wednesday 15 May 1918<br/>Went with a party from the Hospital to George Robey's concert at the R.A.C.  He gives one every week for Colonial Officers.</p>
<p>Thursday 16 May 1918<br/>Read a bit in the morng. to Fox our patient.  Doc says there is no hope of him gettg. his sight back.</p>
<p>Friday 17 May 1918<br/>Most of the ward are up and walkg. now.  All wearing dark glasses.</p>
<p>Saturday 18 May 1918<br/>Leave for week end from Hospital spent at Highgate.</p>
