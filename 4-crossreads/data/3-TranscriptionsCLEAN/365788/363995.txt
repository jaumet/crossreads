  
 5 Oct 16 My dearest Irene, You will know ere this that I have been awarded a Military Medal. Major Barney Somerville got me 10 days leave & we are here together, just making a hurried visit. I had dinner one evening at the Elsmie's & very charming they all were. - It is raining very hard here, but is a change from the trenches I have been in twice since I received my Commission & am glad to say am A.1.
