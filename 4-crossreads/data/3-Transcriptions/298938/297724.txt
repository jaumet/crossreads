<p>9;XII:16<br/>In the new hut am well installed with a dispensary to myself<br/>Plenty of work &amp; no reply yet concerning establishment &amp; my promotion<br/>Nurses arrived today<br/>The camp is slowly but surely being turned into a hospital.<br/>Hope the nurses will turn out good, &amp; not of the interfering kind.</p>
