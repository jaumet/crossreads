<p>Anecdote</p>
<p>The Stuttering Man &amp; the bomb<br />An artillery team on a road got into trouble.<br />Clergyman (Anglican standing by) standing by said to the officer in Charge &ndash; Can I be of any service?</p>
<p>Officer: If you will kindly get away so that we cannot see you then we shall make use of the language that the horses understand.</p>
