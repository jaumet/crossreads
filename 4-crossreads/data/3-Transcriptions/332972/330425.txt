Than what wonderfull unity is proven by the fact that &ndash; without premeditation, without plan, without leaders &ndash; All Civilians in the German Compound refused to attend roll call as a protest. IV I feel shure your aim, <strike>is</strike> as it must be mine is to consider what will make for the welfare of the Internees as a whole  &amp;  not of any particular section, no matter how noisy or agressive <strike>they</strike> it may be. - I  &amp;  each Member of the Comittee, can assure your Honor that in every step we haive taken, we have only one end in view, the good of the whole Camp, and not of any section, least of all the noisy section.
