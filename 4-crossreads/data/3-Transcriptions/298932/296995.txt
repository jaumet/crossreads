<p>Bolounge 15 Dec 1918<br/>Leaving today for ever the hallowed soil of France after an experience that will never be forgotten, and although at times I would have willingly sacrificied a limb to get out of it, I thank Providence that I came through with a whole skin &amp; now I find that I bid adeiu to France a wee bit sorry to go.</p>
