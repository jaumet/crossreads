 The 14th June. Military sports carnival, our brigade, the 14th being well to the fore in most of the events. 
 15th June. Thursday finds us busy loading guns &amp; equipment &amp; trucking horses, expect to entrain about 5 p.m. to commence our journey across Egypt to Alexandria. We the 5th Div. are now considered fit for service, right glad we are too, to be away from here it is as hot as Hades. 
 17th June. Busy imbarking at Alexandria after an all-night journey from Moascar, all guns &amp; horses aboard
