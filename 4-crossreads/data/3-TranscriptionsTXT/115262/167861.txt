 Wednesday 4 April 1917. 
 Field day till 12.30 pm. 
 Afternoon easy. 
 Big riot of 1500 men in evening, on account of general camp conditions &amp; Easter leave. Not much outcome of it. Men, especially interstate, riotous. 
 Wrote 2 letters &amp; turned in feeling not too good. 
 P. J. Fogarty transferred for duty to No 11 A.G.H. (Downhearted.) 
 Allo AM.C Reinfts&nbsp; unallotted  now.
