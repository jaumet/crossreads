<p>Thursday 17 May 1917.</p>
<p>Still at sea and we are of the opinion that we are just now in &quot;bight&quot;&nbsp;&nbsp;judging by the very rough weather we have run into</p>
<p>The roughest day yet of the voyage,&nbsp;since leaving Melbourne.</p>
<p>1<sup>st&nbsp;</sup>.Cl<sup>&nbsp;</sup>.&nbsp;<s>Bgde&nbsp;</s>&nbsp;Fd Amb. does guard. &amp; police duties on board.</p>
<p>Medical inspection in afternoon</p>
<p>A very rough night. Sat on upper deck till 9 p.m. &amp; then down below.</p>
<p>Have veered&nbsp;<u>slightly</u>&nbsp;in a Northerly direction but are stll about 500 miles from land.</p>
