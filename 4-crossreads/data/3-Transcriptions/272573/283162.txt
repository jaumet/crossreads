<p>Landing into Laventie again we had a good repast of coffee and eggs which sent us on our way rejoicing, to walk back to Estaires, three miles away</p>
<p>April 9th. Mon<br/>The Village in F&ecirc;te, Easter Monday<br/>O.T. changed into the Hut in order to have the O.T. proper, whitewashed.</p>
<p>April 11th Wed<br/>Owing to a raid being on we were kept very busy. I am not feeling too good. Have not got energy for anything.</p>
<p>April 13th. Friday.</p>
<p>Everything in the C.C.S. in a hubbub. General House Surgeon General to A.I.F. is to inspect us to-day. We made everything look spick and span but the "General" only put his head inside the door as the saying is and went away again.</p>
<p>Poor old Col D. very disappointed that more notice was not taken of the place.</p>
<p>Heavy shower of Hailstones.</p>
<p>Capt Owen has left for another unit, what a relief!</p>
