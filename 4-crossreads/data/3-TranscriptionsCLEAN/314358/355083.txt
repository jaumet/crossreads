
26 May 1916Dear BeatriceJust a few lines to say all is well.I cannot say what my address will be in the near future.Have not had letters from any of you for agesThe constant movement from one place to another must have put postal arrangements out.I have directed them at this end to deliver my letters care of my O.C. but still no letters.Am living in hope of better times and more pay for your sake.Keep well and happy and don't let the children
