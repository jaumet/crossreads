<p>2nd Years Diary<br />1916<br />May 12th.<br />Engineering till 5am, slept in till midday. Relieved 15th Battn 10pm same night. all OK.</p>
<p>May 13th.<br />Wet morning. engineering from dinner till 6pm.</p>
<p>May 14th Sunday Cold.<br />Our trucks shelled &amp;amp; we also retalliated later. Mail in. On wiring party night.</p>
<p>15th. Monday.<br />Cold &amp;amp; wet. again shelled and parapet had to be repaired from same. With &#39;full kit&#39; left trenches on Furlough to England. marched to Steenwerk where we stayed till 5am next day.</p>
