 Sunday 2nd June Cycled to Hollbrock for tea, D, Nab Miss A & self. A very pretty wood with a nice sheet of water 
 Monday 3rd June Went through the Boys Naval Training school in the morning It was most interesting the Boys made a very poor attempt to learn to swim Went to Captain Lynes for tea & tennis 
 Tuesday Nab & I Cycled about 7 miles to a friends Cap Ellis I think was the name to lunch He had been blown up on his mine sweeper & had his leg broken
