On making a  Study of their Faces & hands I came to the Conclusion that they followed all Sorts of occupation's, some had very clear Complexions & their hands were solf & White which proved to me that they were from  The Office & Business Establishment then again was the Man with a Complexion which showed that he had been following the Call of out door work & then came the Way Back I mean by that Expression the Man who born & bred in the Country & was not used to the Town Life but nevertheless he was like the rest Anxious to go forth & fight for his Country's Freedom.
