<p>been doing the odd jobs that are always waiting with a pair of horses &amp; a waggon &ndash; For instance groom horses, pull them a bag full of green grass from the hedge bottoms as a treat, the my "bits", grease the waggon wheels &ndash;</p>
<p>Aeroplanes very active overhead today as is always the case on a fine calm day</p>
<p>June 1/16<br/>Very heavy artillery firing last night &ndash; one of the Artillery Brigades was moving out into the Reserves &amp; another Bgde taking its place The Germans had probably got wind of the change &amp; chosen the opportunity for bombarding the gun positions</p>
