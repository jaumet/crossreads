  
 15 February 1918 Friday  Letter from Nowell & 2 from the wife alls well  We have a BAND for the 1st L.H.B. mounted on little white DONKEYS- it adds to our pleasure in off duty hours-  Moving on tomorrow  to Jericho!!!  Boils are better but I spend a few hours every day getting them dressed. 13 Parcels from Australian Comforts Fund arrived- Cakes Vegetables Sweets all very welcome.  By a strange coincidence my Parcel was from Miss Rose Clapperton of Brookong, I have sent her a Post Card
