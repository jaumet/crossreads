  
 437 3 Is no hurry as if he did want me to go and see him I wouldn't be able to for a while - I believe all our men are off the peninsular now and there is talk of the mounted Rifles taking their horses to Servia - By the look of things  at present there won't be much left of Servia unless something unforseen happens - I am afraid this is a very uninteresting letter - One doesn't get much to write about stuck in bed all the week - I hope Harold Bule is sending you my allotment money - its not much  - but as much as I can do at present I hope to do a great deal more when I get another start
