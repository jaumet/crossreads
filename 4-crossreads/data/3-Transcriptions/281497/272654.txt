<p>Captain Jacobs is back again in the Company he has been away a very long time now, I think Capt Mckenzie is going to Hospital in the morning he has a small piece of shrapnel near his eye.</p>
<p>Sent a few letters away to day the first chance I have had for some time</p>
<p>1st August. Another beautiful &amp; glorious morning not a cloud to be seen in the sky, &amp; the birds are singing sweetly in the shady trees all around me, the world is not a bad old place after all.</p>
<p>Last night the boys were very riotous &amp; noisy, they kicked up a terrible row to well on to 1 in the morning</p>
