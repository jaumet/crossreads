 12-3-16 The name of the place we are stationed at is "Hassan-pasha"; The Regimental Head Quarters are at Tukh-el-Khul also two troops of B Sqd, the other two troops of B Sqd are at Nazlet-El-Adib with the machine gun section, &amp; A Sqd is at Ezbet-Sou-Doon 
 13-3-16 Mon 13th The native have been fighting amongst themselves all the morning as one or two outsides come on the scene &amp; squatted down by the Canteen &amp; started opposition &amp; the owner of the Canteen strongly objected, about dinner time Capt Higginson hunted them all away except the Canteen owner; We were paid &pound; 1-10-0 last night &amp; the Troopers &pound; 1-0-0 &amp; tonight several lads went into the village looking for adventure the natives complained that they were making a row in the village &amp; a party was sent to arrest them at 8 oclock; Lieut Snow received a telegram tonight asking if he would consider a transfer to the artillery Ammunition Column, at Tel-El-Kebir
