<p>Owing to his peregrinations in leading me to Poperinge, Eva is now the "surveyor" or the "guide", &amp; we laugh a good deal over such quips.  While Gray &amp; I are in the refreshment room, talking war to a semi-religious French Canadian with no great insight into the ways of nations; Pinky &amp; Eva are playing "house" in an estaminet.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13th Nov 17<br/>Still in camp at Caestre.<br/>Tomorrow the 1st Div Newforces camp moves to a new site up the main road, &amp; we (A.M.C.) are to parade with full marching order at 8.30 perhaps to move</p>
