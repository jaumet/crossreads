<p>Coronation service was played.  The Midland Bank with a great square dome.<br/>Boot Repair notices read Mens 2/3 Hand sewn 3/6<br/>Saw several curiously shaped chimneys. [sketch of angled chimney]<br/>The conductors of all Buses &amp; Trams are women<br/>These have to under go 2 weeks training at the yard Then go to Scotland Yard to pass the test.  Their licence costs 5/- and their clothes are provided for them.  The age is from 18 to 35, minimum height 5? and wages &pound;1 week.  They must live within 10 minutes of the yard</p>
