 To Long for money &amp; to Lonpre for new clothes. Took train from there &amp; arrived at Pepiniere Barracks at Paris at 8.30 pm. Put up for night at Hotel D'Eina 
 Tues Oct 29th:- Left Hotel D'Eina &amp; put up at the Hotel des Deux Gares in the rue du Faubourg between the Gare du Nord &amp; the Gare L'Est . Follies Bergies at night. 
 Wed oct 30th:- Visit to invalids &amp; Museum &amp; Napoleon's Tomb. 
 Thurs Oct 31st:- Around the Madeleine, Place de La Concorde, Champs-de-Elysees 
 Fri Nov 1st:- All Saints day &amp; as it is a holiday here all of the places of historical interest are closed. 
 Sat Nov 2nd:- Ceremonies held here today &amp; wreaths placed on all monuments. Crowds of people around the captured war trophies on the Place de la Concorde. "All Souls Day" 
 Mon Nov 4th:- Left Hotel des deux Gares &amp; have put up at Army &amp; Navy Leave Club "Hotel Moderne" in the Place de la Republique.
