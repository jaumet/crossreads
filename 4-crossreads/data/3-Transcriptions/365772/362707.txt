<p>"Egypt<br/>7.2.16<br/>My dearest Sister,<br/>Again our mail seems to have gone astray we havn't had a mail from Aust. for over 3 weeks, our Brigade is separated from the division for a time so conclude the mail has gone to the latter we will pick it up when we rejoin.<br/>I think I thanked you dear for your parcels &amp; Xmas cheer which you sent along but in case the letter has gone astray I do so again.<br/>Our correspondence is still under a very strict censorship so you will have to be content dear with brief letters until the embargo is lifted.<br/>I am afraid with the trouble of changing camps &amp; being in this place of "Revel" again I have</p>
