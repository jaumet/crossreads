<p>Minister for Defence&nbsp; 214/9/14<br />Troops under my command occupied Kaiser Wilhelshafen to-day without opposition - Flag hoisted proclamation issued principal official absent&nbsp; Four officials and Thirteen other Germans surrendered&nbsp; All subscribed oath neutrality&nbsp; The officials will. be temporarily engaged assist administration others are planters&nbsp; Missionaries business men - Forty fighting men left Wilhelmshafen fortnight ago reinforce German troops New Britain but arrived after capitulation now prisoners at Rabaul&nbsp; Found private stores well stocked - Health of troops excellent - Returning Rabaul</p>
<p>Holmes</p>
