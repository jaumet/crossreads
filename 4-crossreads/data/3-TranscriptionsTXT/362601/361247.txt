 4 August, Monday 
 Nette, Mum &amp; I go shopping, Millinery etc. I am very dopey &amp; feel off colour, meet Charlie Pearson. 
 I am very cranky. 
 Wrote Miss Dickson, Mrs Bell &amp; organist. 
 5 August, Tuesday 
 We catch 7.45 boat. Meet Jim Gosper, Mabel, H Belle, Nick. See lots of others. We go to Windsor. Good trip. Nice day. See boys &amp; go on river banks for walk. Have a good day. Arrive Home 11.30 pm.
