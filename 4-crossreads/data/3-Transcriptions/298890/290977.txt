<p>14. Thursday<br/>Worked at removing things from drawing room a.m. at 3 met Nell at Kew &amp; had tea down there. It rained I got wet &amp; sat 20 min on open station. Result:- rigor, temp, malaria.</p>
<p>15. Friday<br/>In bed with malaria all day. Temp. Not able to see Nell off.</p>
<p>16. Saturday<br/>Up again but feeling desperately depressed &amp; weak.<br/>Fogs for days</p>
