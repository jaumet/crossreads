  
 Tuesday 18 April 1916 Father, Dick & I branded Countess and the Colt. I walked up to the store and rode back on Dick's bike. Mother, Father & I drove over to Warrobil;  Dick took sulkey home.  Saw Miss Smith & Mrs. & Dr. Nicholl. 
 Wednesday 19 April 1916 Very crowded train.  Found Alan at Lyttleton. Father, Alan & I went up to Menangle Camp to try & get my back pay, had to wait nearly all day & then was told to go to Liverpool. In evening we all went over to Telarah.
