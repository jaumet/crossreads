<p>The fellows reckon that we did great execution with our two batteries  &amp;  also that the 'planes did excellent work bombing at about 500 feet with a battery of AA guns on her all the time. I expect we shall continue our journey back to Romani late this afternoon.<br/>Romani also Monday 11.30 pm<br/>Have just taken off my clothes for the first time since Friday morning washed, drunk a cup of tea  &amp;  crawled into my blankets, where I am about to luxuriate for a short time with a priceless   [indecipherable]  Ramses<br/>We left Nequilliet at 5.30 pm  &amp;</p>
