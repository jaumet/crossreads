<p>March, 1916<br/>Thursday 30<br/>After dinner we went into Head quarters to get a couple of application for Com. forms.<br/>Went to friends at Choubra &amp; then to pictures<br/>returning to town at 11.30 we had supper at the Obelisk</p>
<p>Friday 31<br/>Just after dinner dad sent for me  He is to be boarded for Australia &amp; we had a nice job sorting out his gear, what he should take what to leave me &amp; what to discard.<br/>He is to be at No 3 General Hosp. at 9 am tomorrow.</p>
