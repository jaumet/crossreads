 10th. Wednesday. May 1916 Weather more fair, leave interfered with, promised next N.C.O. Orderly Sgt with Cpl. Simpson. Indexed photos sending Kitty from England also negs. 
 11th. Thursday Weather unsettled. Bathing afternoon. Engineers Fatigue of 4 men 7.45pm. to 3am. 
 12th Friday Slept in till dinner time Bath afternoon. trenches, Relieved 19th 10pm. 
 Anniversary of Sydney Farewell 12/5/1915
