<p>a  good deal of amusement in camp such as boxing  &amp; wrestling twice a week  &amp; always two concerts a week so you see there goes four nights out of the seven. The weather here is very changeable, raining one minute  &amp; fine the next, but I think we have more rain than sunshine.<br/>Well dear old kid I will finish this now sending you, Noni  &amp; Vol my fondest love<br/> &amp; I remain Your loving brother Hal</p>
