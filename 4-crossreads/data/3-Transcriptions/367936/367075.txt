<p>Friday 24 November 1916<br/>All hands saddled up and left camp about 11-30.  Rode out due east for about eight miles.<br/>Got to camp after dark.  Very cold at night.</p>
<p>Saturday 25 November 1916<br/>Up early and rode on all day to somewhere near Mazar and South of Rail Head.  Allinson "looped the loop" down sand bank with his pony.  Jolly cold at night.</p>
