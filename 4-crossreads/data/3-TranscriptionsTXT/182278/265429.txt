 Monday 22.7.18&nbsp; More Haymaking&nbsp; wrote some letters 
 Tuesday 23.7.18&nbsp; Raining heavily, Wrote to Doff telling her of my intended return. 
 Wednesday 24.7.18&nbsp; Orders received to report for duty at H.Qrs London, arrived at 6pm from Godalming. Went to the Alhambra 
 Thursday 25.7.18&nbsp; President of D. [C.in&#39;s?] on 9 cases. Went to National Sporting Club at night. 
 Friday 26.7.18&nbsp; Took Pattie Bamford to tea &amp; afterwards spent the night at Malden
