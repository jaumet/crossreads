 MONDAY, JULY 7, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; warm. Drove down to Boundary 
 well with old Cy &amp; Tickle &amp; I were 
 trenching there till 3 pm. Then we 
 went up to Mountain &amp; shot some 
 parrots &amp; laid a trail for foxes. Percy 
 George came out in his car &amp; brought 
 my things. Mum &amp; Jo went over 
 to Coomoo Coomoo in the car.
