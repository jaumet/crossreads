After sundry delays I at last got fixed up but had to buy a tunic as they would not give me one &amp; then took a taxi to the National Hotel, Bedford Place, rather a decent place. I had a bath &amp; was fixing up things at the office when Miss Andrews, Miss Le Marchand &amp; Mrs. Furber walked in much to my surprise &amp; to theirs and as they were just going to the Coliseum they asked me to join them, so putting in a good night for a start. The show was a good one &amp; I felt made as things were going so well.</p>
