<p>The atmosphere created by the afternoon's demonstration put the boys in a happy &amp; thoughtful mood, and correspondingly we spent a pleasant evening together "chez-nous".</p>
<p>July 4.    No parade today on account of the wet.</p>
<p>At 5 P.M. "A" Coy. was warned to get kits ready and at 6 P.M. we moved off, tents, kitchen &amp; transport complete, and camped again about four miles on the safe side of Bapaume.   Directly the tents were set Alan &amp; I went off in search of anything we could find and soon managed to fill ourselves with fresh red currants.</p>
