<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 11 9 4</p>
<p>Time - 12.0</p>
<p>Reply from O/C troops. &nbsp;Balance of Herbertshohe garrison are ready to leave with 2 days provisions and 150 rounds,&nbsp;&amp; could be landed in 1/4 of an hour. &nbsp;Submit that rest of 60 days provisions could be sent down from Rabaul as convenient.</p>
