<p>Sunday 10 Feb.<br/>Out to RD2 to test thro. Y route. Could not get Y so walked there. Finally bunched each cable &amp; test with galvo at Y. Got no loops. So Y route is blown out. Returned to RD2 &amp; fixed up UR, SN lot walking to U.R. Finally put thro 9 pair.</p>
<p>Raid on at 10 p.m. Very successful.</p>
