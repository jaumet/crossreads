<p>Wednesday 21 August<br/>At the usual stable routine.</p>
<p>Cable wagon out 11th Bde to a DS Sailly-les-Sec to Sailly Lauerete [Laurette?]. Extremely hot. Finished line in good time. Swim. Out again over line. Washed Snowy on return.</p>
<p>Great stock of fruit in.</p>
