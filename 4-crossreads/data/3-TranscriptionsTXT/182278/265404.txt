 Friday 15/3/18&nbsp; More demonstrations &amp; revolver competitions, very good concert at night. 
 Saturday 16/3/18&nbsp; Left by bus for St. Omer [Saint-Omer] no trains owing to Shelling Hazebrouck- left St. Omer by Car &amp; arrived home at midnight. 
 Sunday 17/3/18&nbsp; Quiet day- saw Colette&nbsp; had a concert at night 
 Monday 18/3/18. Orders received to relieve 19 th  Bn.- Finals of boxing competitions- won  all  the brigade contests. 
 Tuesday 19/3/18. Collett going to England&nbsp; am taking charge of Brigade- &quot;C&quot; Coy. Concert at night very good
