<p>Tuesday 3 December<br/>Two lectures to-day, in connection with the A.I.F. education scheme, one by the Bishop Frodgem (?) on Democracy &amp; one by Lieut. Lowe, the Educational Officer on the scheme generally.  It is indeed some scheme &amp; all is to be done to fit a man to recommence civil life.</p>
<p>Wednesday 4 December</p>
