<p>I hope you are flourishing, we have had some Australian Spring weather here lately it has been delightful.  England is beginning to look lovely &amp; in a few months will be glorious.</p>
<p>I must away, wish me luck, love to John &amp; the nipper &amp; heaps for yourself.</p>
<p>Yrs.<br/>J.B. Metcalfe</p>
