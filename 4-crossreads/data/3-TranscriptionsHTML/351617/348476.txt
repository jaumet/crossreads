<p>All along the road were villages some of them uninhabitable while others were in good condition. Here and there a few crosses in a heap told where there had been a stand up fight but for most of the way it had been a running battle.</p>
<p>Everywhere one looked in a place at all inhabitable were french civilians already settling down to work to build up the old home while out in the fields they were already ploughing. About 5 pm we arrived at Favril and went straight away into Billets and then went looking for some tea as we were just about famished after our long march.
