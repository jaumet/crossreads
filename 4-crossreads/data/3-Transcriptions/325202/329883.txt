<p>Sept 22<br/>Our Infantry have been over the top &amp; taken their three objectives &amp; are to pull out in a couple of days as the 1st Division is now a 'Storm Division' kept for offensives only. Behind the lines we have had a lively time with air raids the last day or so. The night before last Fritz put a lot of heavy shells over <strike>near</strike></p>
