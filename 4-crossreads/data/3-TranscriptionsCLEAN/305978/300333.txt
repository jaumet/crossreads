  
 28/ Two ships passed, one was the Ballarat which had left before us & had called into Aden. Two birds like Kesterels are perched upon the yards all day. We were paid the sum of 14/- so there will be a little game of two-up & cards going again, about two men have got the last pay now. Won it at two-up etc. The final of the boxing tournament carried out. Some good fights occurred. I was picked out for Ships gaurd when we reach port. 
 30/ Passed an island of rocks with a lighthouse upon it
