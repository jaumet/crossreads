At the Cross roads General Birdwood &amp; Gen Walker watched the Batt. pass by.<br/>We reached our destination in the neighbourhood of Grand Sec Bois about midday, having done about 10 miles only one man who was sick fell out.</p>
<p>Friday Aug 10th<br/>Last night or early this morning, I was awakened by heavy bombardment &amp; later heard a plane overhead which proved to be a German the Searchlights found it &amp; the Anti aircraft batteries fired furiously. He stayed about for some time then went away. Today is dull &amp; fresh</p>
