<p>&amp; cut the Crop of Oats during the day so now we have a nice patch of ground to amuse ourselves on, it is about 1 &frac34; acres. At 7 Pm I was sent to see a Sick Lad in one of the Huts, after seeing him I gave him a good rubbing with Camphor Oil also a cold Mixture, he had pains on the chest, back &amp; legs &amp; I took his Temp which was 102, I felt a bit uneasy about him so decided to take his Temperature at 9 Pm which I did &amp; it read 102.6 so I decided that it was a case for the Hospital &amp; saw one of the Officers who gave me an order to the Medical Officer in the Hospital at Steenjemill about 1 mile away, asking them to send along an Ambulance Motor for the Sick lad. I straight away put my Tunic on &amp; Gas Bag &amp; off I went along the line to the Hospital.
