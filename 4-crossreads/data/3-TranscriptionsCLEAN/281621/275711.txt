
Sunday 12Walk round a few villages about made up of thatched cottages etc. & pretty garden
Tuesday 14At dentists one tooth out & three filled which finishes the contract.
Wednesday 15Have come the proverbial "gutser" & am now marked as A3 meaning medically & dentally fit for France.Move to another camp
Thursday 16Move from command depot to Overseas Training Brigade from where infantry go on draft to France.We still have to go to our depot at Park House before crossing over.
Friday 17Rehearsal for big review on Monday next
Saturday 18Mess Orderly for guard & detention a rotten job
