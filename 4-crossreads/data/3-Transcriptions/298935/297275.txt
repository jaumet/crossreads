<p>much drilling.  Lectures were frequently given by the Officers and non-Coms and we had to take our turn of guard and fatigue duty.  Every Sunday a Church Parade was held.  Concerts were arranged weekly and one or two sports meetings took place.  When we crossed the Equator Father Neptune and his assistants visited us and shaved and baptized many.<br/>On the 22nd February after sailing along the coast of Ceylon we anchored in Colombo Harbour in order to obtain more fresh water.  Several fishermen were near to us and attracted attention on account of their peculiarly shaped boats known as catamarans.  At a distance most of the Cingalese appeared to us to be women owing to the cloth which is worn round the waist</p>
