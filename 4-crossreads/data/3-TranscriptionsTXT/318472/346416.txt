
10 July 1918WednesdayNothing in particular doing today. Expect a final Board on Friday Showery weather.
1918 July 11ThursdayPut in a quiet day. We had exceptionally heavy rain last night and this morning & all day it has been squally Went with Sgt Clark in the evening to the Pavillion Theatre & saw Who's the woman. Only just fair Had some tea in town & got back to camp 9 P.M.
