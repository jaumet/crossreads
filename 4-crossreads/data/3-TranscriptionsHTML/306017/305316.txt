<p>Monday 14th  There is to be a Brigade competition &amp; every troop is to be judged to see 1st, the best troop of each squadron. 2nd, the best troop of each regiment &amp; 3rd, the best troop in the Brigade.  There is to be a cheque of &pound;28 divided amongst the winning troop which must consist of 1 officer 2 sgts &amp; 24 men.  We have a fortnight to prepare for it, so we started today.  We had a good bit of rain at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 15th  Received a letter from Arthur Blomfield.  He is in the 1st Infty Bgde, so I wrote to him to arrange a meet.  Peter Gosling of Dorrigo came out here to see us.  He is the same old spark.  We went to the picture show but didn't stop long as we didn't like the programme.</p>
