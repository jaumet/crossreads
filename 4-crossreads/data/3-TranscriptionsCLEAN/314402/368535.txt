  
 REPORT NIGHT Sept 17/18 1915 D Coy 12th BATT 
 Events of night 7-8pm Quiet generally. Few bombs on LONE PINE 
 8pm-12 midnight Quiet, Desultory rifle fire. Intermitent bombing on LONE PINE. [Signed] PH Weston 
 12mid -4am Quiet Generally. Desultory Sniping. 4am-8am Quiet 
 Ammunition used Casualties NIL Bombs used 5 [signed] PH Weston
