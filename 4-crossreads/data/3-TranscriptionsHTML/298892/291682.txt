<p>letter. The weather has been behaving itself a little better lately and the medicines &amp;c have thawed out. The snow on the ground is commencing to melt and the river has partially thawed, the ice now being too thin to walk on.<br/>We might be hundred of miles away from the firing line for all the evidences of war we see here. We are reminded of its proximity however every now and then by the intermittent boom of the guns at the front or the continuous roar when a strafe is taking place.</p>
