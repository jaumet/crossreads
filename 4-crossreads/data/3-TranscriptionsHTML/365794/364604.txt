<p>Sunday 21st July 1917 [1918]<br/>(Malaria cont'd)<br/>Pt. on duty in a few days, never send to Base Hosp. if possible to avoid it.  Many pts. have carbunkles &amp; boils, etc.  If the former <u>cut deep</u> swab with carbolic or perch, let pus out, same treatment to abscess if occurs after quin. inject.<br/>Many cases of thorns (prickly pear) occur, cut deep &amp; app. foments 4 times a day.</p>
<p>Sunday &ndash; Remonstrated with 2 V.A.D's keeping me awake, their sole object in life is Groppi's, at 5 p.m. never cease talking.  Not much sleep to be had.  Church 6 p.m., good address.</p>
