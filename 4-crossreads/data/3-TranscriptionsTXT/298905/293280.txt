
We are now supposed to be resting & I will give you our daily routine  Our Coy. is cut into 2 parties one a day "fatigue" & the th other night, I will take the night fatigue" for that is the one I am on, we leave our billets at 9.30. each night & walk about 3 miles to our job which might be any thing from pushing & loading trucks to putting up barb wire, we arrive home from these jobs about 4 in the morning we then have what sleep we can snatch till breakfast, about 8. oclock, after breakfast, we have to fill 250 sand bags, then there is a gas helmet parade or lecture, after dinner we "fall in' for musketry" which generally lasts about an hour, the rest of the afternoon if nothing else turns up we have for ourselve's, so
