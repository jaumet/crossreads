 Jan.  Thursday 25 1917 
 We left our school house for the line at 11a.m. B &amp; C section bearers left 9 a.m. Their "sisters" come up with us, we marched up thro' Pozieres &amp; Contalmaison such a change has come over the scene since last we were here. Railways &amp; hutments are now over the site of our recent varied experiences.  We stay the night at M.D.S. Bazentin le Petit. Go up the line tomorrow. Given a squad for this stunt. Skating rink in a shell hole. Rumoured we are in this stunt for "40 days &amp; 40 Nights".   Impromptu concert round brazier --- Rum loosens tongues
