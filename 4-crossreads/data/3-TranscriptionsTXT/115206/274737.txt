 June 1918 Sunday 16 
 Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles Very peculiar weather prevails at present. Our  experience here today was quite a typical tropical one. Did usual shift on phone. 6-8 &ndash; and then noon, thoroughly cleaned my saddle etc. During afternoon Battery sports was held, my sub carried the highest points for the day, winning the tug-o-war on foot and horseback. I was in both teams.  They also won the Rescue race, high jump, and Bending race. Received a few letters one from Home.
