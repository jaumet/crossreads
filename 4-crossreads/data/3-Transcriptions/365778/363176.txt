<p>Tuesday 20/2/17</p>
<p>Enemy minenwerfer active, things otherwise normal.</p>
<p>Wednesday 21/2/17</p>
<p>Relief by 18th Bn completed 12.30 am very tired.</p>
<p>Thursday 22/2/17</p>
<p>Returned to Scotts Redoubt at 5 am slept in until mid-day &ndash; meeting of C.O's.</p>
<p>Friday 23/2/17</p>
<p>Went to Corps Demonstration and afterwards to Bazieuse.</p>
<p>Saturday 24/2/17</p>
<p>Meeting of Battn C.O's visited Divison also 5th Fd Amb.</p>
<p>Sunday 25/2/17</p>
<p>Enemy evacuated front line, plenty of work, visited line returning 3 am Monday.</p>
