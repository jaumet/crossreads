<p>1. &nbsp; The general object of the Med: Ex Force is to assist the Fleet in forcing the passage of the Dardanelles by the occupation of important positions in the Gallipoli Peninsula. The 29th D<sup>ivn</sup> will operate from CAPE HELLE and the Naval Divn near BULAIR.</p>
<p>The Aus &amp; NZ Army Corps will land North of KABA TEPE - the landing will probably be opposed.</p>
<p>2. &nbsp; The D<sup>ivn</sup> will land between KABA TEPE and FISHERMANS HUT. Its first objective will be the occupation of the ridge extending from Sq: 212 (i) to point 971 in Sq: 238 (m).</p>
