
<p>354</p>
<p>1917 APRIL</p>
<p>30 MONDAY</p>
<p>Beautifully fine &amp; warm. Caught</p>
<p>the 7 AM train from Longueville</p>
<p>to San Martin &amp; then the tram</p>
<p>into town. Had a look round</p>
<p>&amp; tried to get some information</p>
<p>of Alan at the RFC but could not</p>
<p>Got a car &amp; went out to</p>
<p>Wimreaux in afternoon</p>
<p>Got the train back at night</p>
<p>The Brigade had sports</p>
<p>ect all day</p>
