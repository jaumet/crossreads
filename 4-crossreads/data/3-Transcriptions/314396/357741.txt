<p>Sunday 23rd Aug 14</p>
<p>Got up at 6  &amp;  <br/>fixed things up. Went to Adelaide in the afternoon. Saw Uncle Jack &amp;  got gold ring from him. Went on the river rowing. Had tea in town &amp;  pinched Knife, fork  &amp;  spoon. Came back to Glenelg in the evening &amp;  <br/>got back in camp at 8.15.</p>
<p>29/8/14<br/>Left Adelaide for Melbourne &amp; arrived on Sunday morning.</p>
<p>Broadmeadows<br/>30/8/14<br/>Marched to Camp but was not expected so had to march back to station &amp; then went back to Melbourne. Took Owens to Hospital (or FBM Hosp) went onto camp at Alexandra Avenue. Engineers Depot</p>
<p>3/9/14<br/>Transferred from 3rd Field Coy to 2nd Field Coy as Quarter Master Sergeant.</p>
<p>23/9/14<br/>Had a terrific fall came back as Corporal in No. 4 Section. Didn't like it very much<br/>Opened Banking account</p>
