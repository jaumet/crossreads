<p>Afterwards I got into Cheapside &amp; bought 2 picture albums one for home &amp; the other for Aunt Amy posted &amp; got them away, &amp; came down per medium of a bus &amp; had tea, I think I will go to the Adelphi tonight &amp; see what she is like I heard to night that there are a lot of Australians at Salisbury Plains, they reckon they came from Egypt.<br/>14th. Last night I was talking to Lady Murray &amp; she asked me if I would care for a ride in her car as far as Picadilly, so away I went she is a real nice lady her husband was wounded the day before Kut fell &amp; is now in India, she takes a great interest in Peel House &amp; spends nearly all her time</p>
