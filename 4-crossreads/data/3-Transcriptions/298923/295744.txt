<p>19 March 1918 Tuesday<br/>Bethlehem again.<br/>Letters from home Mother Jany 19th I do love to get home news &amp; to know all's well, also Mother &amp; Day Jany 13.  Can't understand my Cable not reaching you I sent on Dec 15th from Port Said.  Also another letter Jany 28th from Melb:-  So glad you are there.<br/>Love to Aunt Louie- Dolly sails again for England end of April I <u>must</u> see her ere she goes- it is hard after all my arrangements, &amp; I wish she had gone Bronte.</p>
