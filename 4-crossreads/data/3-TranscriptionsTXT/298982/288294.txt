 Great number of dead buried &amp; the stench was awful. The position gruesome. Turks largely in the majority. Godley &amp; many staff men up all morn twigging the field. Only heard one shot fired all day. Concluded at 4.30pm Shooting commenced again about 5 pm. 
 [Note in margin &ndash; E. Norman. Nelson.?] 
 In supports all night on ammunition guard Nothing special, heavy thunder &amp; some rain. 
 Saw Scan &amp; another a Corp of Engineers this evening yarn re battleship &amp; the guns a little ruse which scored about 1200 of the enemy.
