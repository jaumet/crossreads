<p>Jan 12/16<br/>The Light Horse Regt have left this morning &ndash; they are returning to the railhead (el Dabaa) &amp; thence by rail to Cairo to rejoin their various regiments from which they were drawn to make up this Comp. Regt. &ndash; As far as is known in the ranks their various regiments will be moving <strike>shortly</strike> down soon to the Canal where there seems to be big trouble coming. <strike>All the Divis</strike> A squadron of them are left behind &amp; are to follow in a week</p>
<p>All the Divisional Train waggons have gone with them carrying forage &ndash; supplies &amp; water &amp; I expect will stop at el Dabaa &amp; then return with the Complete</p>
