She left about 2 oclock & I went down below & started to write up the days doings & by now I am beginning to feel pretty rotten so one can guess what kind of a time I am going to have.  I am dreading the first few days for I am no sailor, anyone whe can have my rations for the next 3 or 4 days or I will be greatly mistaken.  Braithwaite has just given me a dose of Beechams pills & what a job I had to get them down they insisted on sticking in my throat & the coating started to wear off & my mouth began to feel like or taste like a chemist shop so I had to dash downstairs & swallow some water which thank heavens shifted the obstruction.
