Across the sand to Heliopolis Car Very fine & up to date Electric Railway carrying 1st & 2nd Class passengers. The journey is only 10 minutes from Heliopolis to Cairo. Tom Charley Jimmy & I went to view the sights. On arrival at Cairo we were surrounded by Gyppos of all ages wanting to clean Boots, sell all sorts of rubbish.
