 55 September 6th today as a result of yesterday inspection &ndash; 
 Received my tunic Breeches &amp; Boots that I had in France, I feel quite Happy now 
 Had a bath today &ndash; 
 September 7th Had a special shoe made for my left foot today. It is really grand, &amp; fits me very well I can walk 80&#37; better now, having only a very slight limp &ndash; all I am afraid of now is that my foot will get better too quick for the commission &ndash; 
 All Dressed up &amp; nowhere to go today. Looked a little bit like an officer It is really quite a treat to be respectable, it is the first time since I was wounded. I cannot say how happy I am &ndash; 
 Recd letter from Annie also some Snaps &ndash; also parcel from Army &amp; Navy Stores &ndash; 
 Listened to the Communique today our people at Home are undergoing a very anxious time as 1/3 of our crops have been ruined by the extremely long spell of bad weather &ndash; The populas have been specially warned to economise. 
 Played Bridge 
 September 8th Good sleep &ndash; Sent for by the Doctor in connection with our complaints
