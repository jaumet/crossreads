<p>25<br/>Military life has become quite the accepted thing now</p>
<p>Everything goes with the smoothness (of a business) long established.</p>
<p>For us the moment came at last.  We were ordered to embark</p>
<p>There was the necessary movement and bustle of getting everybody and everything on board, but no fuss or excitement as of commencement of the war.</p>
<p>The hungary grey troop ships swallowed a great mass of animated kahki which trailed slowly up its gangways, and gobbled up everything which the cranes dangled near</p>
