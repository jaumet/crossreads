 1 April 1915 Thursday 
 Batt off on route march   &amp;  Bivouac until tomorrow Go for a stroll into Heliopolis   &amp;  watch the pictures   &amp;  have several cold drinks   &amp;  Ice-creams as night is very warm. 
 1915 April 2 Good Friday 
 Early morning parade only. Holiday for the rest Battalion returns to camp about 11 am The Australian and New Zealanders take charge of the rough part of Cairo   &amp;  wreck   &amp;  Burn the contents of about 6 houses   &amp;  cut the hoses of the fire brigade. In the riot which ensued several of our men were shot   &amp;  a couple were shot dead by the M.P.   &amp;  Red Caps.
