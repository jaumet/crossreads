
2.  T'-other.  I met some of my Friends as soon as I touched the City so I went for a stroll in the Park with them & Home to Tea.  After I had my fill I cycled down to my other Friend's Place & we went for a walk on the Downs away to the back of the Cathedral.  I rode Home the 11 1/2 miles in 65 minutes.  About 5 miles of the way is very steep upland so I was not too slow.  I went looking for a couple of new villages on Thursday night but after riding all over the Country side I missed them.
