  
 I have mentioned his credulity which will be exemplified in what follows.  He has also, a little trick of repeating little parenthetical sentences ad lib, forgetting that the rather unimportant point referred to be made is in danger of being unduly brought forward.  Thus in referring to his wife he hardly mentions her in connection with any episode but  which  he adds "My fiancee which she was then" or "of course she was my fiancee at that time", or "my wife or fiancee as she then was".
