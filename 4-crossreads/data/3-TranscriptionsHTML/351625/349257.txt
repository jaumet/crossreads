<p>within a few yards of the line &amp; even in the line, the Y.M.C.A. has its free coffee stall where troops can always get a cup of tea or cocoa, they are great.  <strike>I can</strike> I am afraid this letter is not very newsy but now we cant say anything about our own doings.
