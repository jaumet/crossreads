<p>Sunday 16 September 1917.</p>
<p>1 Taube over this morning. Went to Church Parade. The first time since leaving Moascar Isolation Camp. The rest of the day very little doing.</p>
<p>At the Aerodrome &frac12; mile from our position things are going ahead and very busy too.</p>
<p>No Aust. mail yet.</p>
<p>Received parcel from Ted Holden from 14 A.G.H.</p>
