<p>We left Bristol docks at 7.30 this evening and went a few miles out into the channel  and lay to until morning , waiting for instructions. The weather was fine and the sea calm so all hands had a good night. We drew our hammocks and blankets and most of us went to bed  early.  We could see the lights of two high towers on the North side of the channel.<br/>A sailor fell overboard before we left the docks last evening. But beyond getting a good wetting he was nothing the worse for the bath.</p>
