<p>I might mention here that a few days before leaving for Claremont I saw in the Daily paper an adv. calling applications to form a Railway Corps, composed of Engine Drivers Fireman, Steam raisers, Fitters Boilermakers, Station Masters. Now I being a Locomotive Engine Driver in Civil life I decided there &amp; then to apply for a Transfer into the newly formed Corps. Naturally I was anxious when in Claremont for some word, well after a Week or two, I received a Message to report my self as soon as possible <strike>to</strike> at Headquarters which I was not long in doing. After my interview with the Officer concerned I was accepted as an Engine Driver &amp; seven days later was transferred back to Blackboy with the Railway Corps, I was given two Stripes which</p>
