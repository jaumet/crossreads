<p>&amp; it seemed to me that each performer was trying to make a worse show than the preceding one, at any rate they caused a lot of fun.</p>
<p>We had a lecture this morning from a gas expert.  All the Batt N.C.O's had to attend with their helmets &amp; had to do all sorts of drill &amp; work in them didn't it make us swear all in our own time too.</p>
<p>Had a fatigue party this afternoon they were working for the Pioneers</p>
<p>This evening I went to the "pictures" &amp; after that I attended a lecture given by Capt McNicholl on his recollections of the S. African War, it was very good &amp; the Y.M.C.A. was crowded, we have 2 "picture" shows each night in the same hut</p>
