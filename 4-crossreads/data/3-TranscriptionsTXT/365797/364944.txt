 England. He reached Plymouth about Aug. 26th. Ran is in England too. 
 Nurse Woods has returned to Sydney &ndash; I think I told you &ndash; &amp; is in charge of a hospital for wounded here. Am going to write &amp; see if we may call. 
 Hazel Father &amp; I went to Ingalara last night for tea &amp; had a nice time. Dorrie Ward the singer was there &ndash; a girl with a lovely voice, &amp; very like Hilda Trugeon only much fatter &amp; not such a pretty complexion. 
 No word this week from Harold. Reg has changed into Motor transport but I dont know his exact address. Ernie was in [indecipherable] Battalion but has been attached to some special company over there. 
 With much love from us all hoping you are still unhurt. 
 Mother.
