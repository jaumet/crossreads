<p>Thursday 15 March 1917.</p>
<p>1st <u>Sham </u>fight that we (A.C.F.A) took part in.&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">The &quot;white hats&quot; were forced to retreat over the hills &amp; across the &quot;Trawool&quot; bridge. Our No1 dressing station had to be disbanded in the retreat.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">After dinner on the banks of Goulburn River we were marched in a round-a-bout way to the camp. Dist. 20 miles.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6em;">No lecture. &quot;Furphies&quot; still going strg.</span></p>
