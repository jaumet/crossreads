 infantry swam ashore at Colombo &amp; the guard that was sent ashore also played up.  Nobody was allowed ashore here &amp; by the look of the place there doesn't seem much to go for.  We started to coal &amp; take in water &amp; they expect to be ready to go by Saturday.  There seems to be a lot of shipping here, boats continually in &amp; out.  The fortress looks very forbidding.  Saw some of the Garrison artillery.  They seem to be a fine lot of men. 
 Thursday 21st  [Jan] Still coaling.  Usual work on stables.  3 or 4 chaps including the 2 Heckleys' left the boat having been discharged.  They will be sent straight back to Sydney.
