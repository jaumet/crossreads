<p>But then are we not privileged in being allowed there?&nbsp; It is not everybody that can go to North Head - and I am sure it is not everybody that is anxious to remain there after a few hours have passed.&nbsp; On going ashore we have hot baths and all clothes and belonging are cleansed and fumigated.</p>
<p>Then we graciously retire to our huts.</p>
