I knew the way quite easily & I was in Brighton at 5 p.m. free & with a 5 days leave pass in my pocket.  I walked along the Prom past the two huge & magnificent Piers.  The front is very grand & the Hotels huge.  I was surprised to find some advertisements posted up, so & so, merchant, 32 Steyne Street.  I arrived in London at 7.30 p.m. & after one supper at the Y.M.C.A. as much as I could get, & another immediately following at Lyon's, I felt a bit better.
