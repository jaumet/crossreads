
Sunday 28 July 1916Inspection by outsider at 5.45 p.m.  I meet Mr. Nicholas, go to the Band.  Came Home with Annie's Room.
Monday 24 July 1916Mac goes to Ras el Tin.  33 men come from Sydney Pav.  I have jelly & cocoa in No. 8 & play Bridge.  B. & D. go on leaf.  Get letters from Home, George, Bess, Marg., Jess, Julie & Mem.
Tuesday 25 July 1916Full Dress Inspection.  Molly picks her Boy.  I ride to Abbassia & call at McCougi's.  Dinner & go to Cairo.  Meet Roley Hale & play Billiards.  Met McCougi & go to Pictures with Maltese Dames.
Wednesday 26 July 1916Went to see Dick Rowe at Pyramids Bar.  Ellis & I, we talk with two Pommys & listen to Gippo songs on Phonograph.
Thursday 27 July 1916Wrote to Jess, Julie & Mem.  Ellis & I go to Cairo, meet Cougi & ride back to Heliopolis Hotel with Maltese Lady.  It is very nice.
Friday 28 July 1916I go to Cairo for my Photos.  Meet Kinna & Ice Cream, Lemonade & Peanuts.  Wrote to Bess, Marg., Lily, Mum (58).
Saturday 29 July 1916I play Cricket at 6 p.m. & nothing else is worthy of note.  Send Photo to Jennie.
