<p>21.3.19<br/>Up 9 am. After stroll around went to Red Cross and had lunch with Hawkins. We then both went by No 16 tram for about an hours ride, going thro' the industrial part of Frisco. Passed all the railway freight yards and shipbuilding yards, also numbers of factories. Came back a different route via Mission St and went back to canteen.</p>
<p>Stopped around there till about 8 pm then went home and had bath and off to bed.</p>
<p>After lunch rang up Mrs Demarest at Berkeley/ an Australian lady/ and have to go to her place on Tuesday.</p>
<p>22.3.19<br/>Up 8.30 am. After breakfast had a bit of a stroll around street then went down to canteen and met Hawkins.</p>
<p>Stopped about there till 2 pm when Mrs Stephenson came for us in motor and took us and an American naval ensign for a grand drive up to the Twin Peaks &ndash; we went right to the summits of both of them. Then drove on through a forest to the Ocean Beach, going to the Cliff House and seeing the seals again. Then drove thro' Golden Gate Park, passing several artificial lakes, and went to the Japanese Tea Gardens and had afternoon tea of strong green tea, but did not like it too well.</p>
<p>The gardens are laid out just as they would be in Japan. Then drove on to the Presidia, passing several forts, and the entrance of the Golden Gate. Also passed the Letterman military hospital.</p>
