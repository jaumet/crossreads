 Parade by Capt. Ferguson and various instns. received. 
 Afternoon exercise &amp; grazing along main Villers Brettoneaux &ndash; Amiens Rd. 
 Visited with Harry Richards various woods &amp; were finally too close to a bombardment so came away at gallop. Saw many French, &amp; tanks.
