  
 As we have a large amount of Coal stacked on the Promenade Deck on both sides it is impossible to hold any Sports or Concerts & as we have practically nothing to do there is little of importance to nots so I will just mention any thing of importance in brief. On 
 Thursday 1/3/17 We had the pleasure of our 4 Point 3 Gun being fired for the first time since she was placed on board. There was a Target thrown overboard it was a Barrel with a fled Flag attached to it & the first Shot fired off just went over the Top & the second hit it fair & blew it to atoms.
