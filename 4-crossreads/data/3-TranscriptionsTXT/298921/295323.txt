
30 December Thursday 1916Received a shoal of Letters, Papers & Parcels 2 from Joe enclosing Diary 1 long one from Mother 10/11/- Farror, H.L. Austin, saying he would see me fixed up on the Murrambidgee to quote he says -I will do my best to get you settled on the Murrambidgee near here after the war & we can talk sheep & Station Management."I also got Australasians soks & cards galore from many people - can give some I am posting you Place of Anzac, you'll see where we withdrew from, some day I hope to explain it all. Old Sam also wrote saying he had sent you too (Parcel soks etc will you Please thank H.L.A. & wife too).
