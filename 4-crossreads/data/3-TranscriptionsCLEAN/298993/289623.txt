  
 dug mine 4 feet deep the soil is quite moist someone dug up a snake and put it in my dug out  had dinner 12. Had a rest till 4PM went for fire wood got some down a well lowered A.H down on a rope had an argument with bedowin followed us home we split up and bluffed him he did not know who to follow had tea issued with rum air craft firing at a taube went up to confession 7.P.M. turned in 8.P.M. Sunday April 9.4.17. stand to 4AM easter Sunday went to mass and holy communion 7.A.M. returned to camp had breakfast 8AM. Saddled up and went out trench digging it is the first easter Sunday I have ever worked and I hope I wont
