 Sunday 9 April 1916 No leave again. 
 Monday 10 April 1916 Mail in.  One from Bess &amp; Os Merrett.  None from Home. 
 Tuesday 11 April 1916 Leave all day starts. 
 Wednesday 12 April 1916 Donkey ride, Helouan to Memphis.  Tea at Anzac Hostel &amp; Concert.  Billy, Ellis &amp; I twice across the Nile. 
 Thursday 13 April 1916 Dust storm No. 1.  No Treatment afternoon.  Dust everywhere &amp; in everything. 
 Friday 14 April 1916 Wrote Roy, Marie.  Received one from Marie.  Papers from Dad.  Went to Heliopolis for milk &amp; meat paste &amp; matches.  Poor jelly died in Ice Chest. 
 Saturday 15 April 1916 Pay Parade.  Met Italian Consul's daughter &amp; L. McLean.  Boys gave show at Anzac Hostel &amp; played No. 3 Aux. Hosp. Heliopolis cricket, 188 to 90 lost.
