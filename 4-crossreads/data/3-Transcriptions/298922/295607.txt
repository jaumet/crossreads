<p>Sunday 4th November 1917</p>
<p><strike>(Kelman)</strike><br/>Busy packing &amp; Major !!! Prince of Millawa called to see me.</p>
<p>Had afternoon tea with Cottons yesterday.</p>
<p>We leave London tomorrow so post this with love to you all.</p>
<p>Hope to go to St. Pauls this afternoon.</p>
<p>Love from Dolly.</p>
