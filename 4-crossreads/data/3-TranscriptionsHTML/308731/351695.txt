<p>30.4.18<br/>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill</p>
<p>Dear old Dad.</p>
<p>Miss Heffernan has come home at last it is a wonder isent it. and I should of fiven pupy a bath yet I chould of thiths him. and salky was broken and to day we went to get polly shord and then went up to get the salky. and I hafta go over to grandma to get the mail and take the milk over to Grandmas <strike>with the milk</strike> and I hate going and Grandmas with the milk.</p>
<p>much love from<br/>Frank Burrowes<br/>xxx etc.</p>
