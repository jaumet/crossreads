<p>P.P.S.<br/>As soon as we arrived at Southampton we wrote to Auntie.  Of course from the very first day, she kept saying now I wonder when they'll come.  Marie kept saying that they are coming next Monday Mother, &amp; Auntie said, How silly of you Marie.  3 days before we did actually arrive there, Auntie again said, now I wonder when those Boys will come.  Marie</p>
