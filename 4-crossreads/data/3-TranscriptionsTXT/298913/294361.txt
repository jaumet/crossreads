(4/3/16)  Take up a pass & go to Cairo with Bon Gow & to the Citadel.The CitadelThe Citadel, an old fort & place of worship stands on a hill that overlooks Cairo & the Tombs of the Mamelukes. It is reached by a short tram ride from Cairo. A short walk brings one to the Citadel which is garrisoned by English Tommies. One has to enter by a side entrance that leads up a pathway to the courtyard. At the entrance here one has to pay 1 piastre (in future called
