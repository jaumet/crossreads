<p>France<br/>7-11-16<br/>My dear Judge</p>
<p>Still fit with only a middling sort of a cold after a very trying fortnight nearly to our waists in mud.  We are now out of it &ndash; for a little while &ndash; resting.  On our way out I had the pleasure of seeing my old Brigade going in.  They looked really splendid in their clean clothing and with their full complement of men officers &amp; equipment.  I was surprised to see how great a number of my old brother officers and men were still on deck and they all looked in such good condition.  It was indeed a pleasure to see them all.  I nearly got my arm torn out as I rode along and even made an opportunity of having a short yarn to a good many of the officers.
