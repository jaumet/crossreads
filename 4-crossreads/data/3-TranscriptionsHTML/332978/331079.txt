<p>cutting away a great deal of the soul. He made an excellent job of it, as the boot did not show any sign of being tampered with.</p>
<p>The balance of diary was concealed behind the back of a hand mirror. When being finally searched prior to leaving Germany the mirror was subjected to a careful examination, &amp; luckily for me passed the censor; my boot which I wore, was not even looked at.</p>
<p>Had my diary been found I would have been kept in Germany for duration of war &amp; in addition confined to cells, for wilful defiance of orders.</p>
<p>That portion of diary concealed in boot was slightly damaged by water, &amp; the paper still bears the marks of the boot tacks.</p>
<p>[Written vertically in left-hand margin:]<br/>On arrival in Switzerland, the whole diary was rescued &amp; preserved carefully ever since.</p>
<p>Donald. F. Wells Captain<br/>13th Bn A-I-F.</p>
