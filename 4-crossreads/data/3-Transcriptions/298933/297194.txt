<p>Off to Blighty<br/>[October 1918]<br/>10 Th<br/>Wine flows free, the boys are happy song &amp; mirth,"Marching" with the deathless army of the A.I F<br/>11 Fri<br/>Left Fignacourt last night, rested at St Vasbri &amp; slept for the night at Saint Savavour then took train to Bolounge at Ailly Sur Somme<br/>12 Sat<br/>Bolounge to Folkestone a very nice trip, arrived in London for tea Feet very sore.I am being sent to hospital Signal of distress ulcerated Feet</p>
