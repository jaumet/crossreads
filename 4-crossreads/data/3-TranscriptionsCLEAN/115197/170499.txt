Heavy fall of snow.
Arrive Marchienne-au-Pont - then to Charleroi.  Necessary demobilisation cards returne obtained.
Returned to Mont-sur-Marchienne for the evening and night.
Mar. 31st
Reveillez 5.30 a.m.  Early breakfast.  Tearful farewell.  Leave Monsieur & Madame whose generosity & kindness will always bring pleasant memories of Belgium - Charleroi - Speech by General.  Our quota "No 23" (1,000 strong Infantry & Details 2nd Div).
