<p>1916 April 13<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Ward orderly this morning. very hot steamy. Shaved of moustache &amp; had hair cut by A. M. C. barber. Geo. Laurence.<br/>Grainus pudding. Debate on "Early Closing"  Tony led off &amp; made a good speech. others followed.  The six o'clocks won hands down.</p>
