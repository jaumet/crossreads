<p>sundry articles. Burial service at 5 pm (a Q'ld light horseman)<br/>Boat hove to for 3 minutes. Imagine the ever moving waters of the Indian ocean (luckily free from Monsoonal weather &ndash; so far)<br/>The sharp rattle of rifle fire as the dead hero's mates fire a parting volley. The long lines of Khaki clad figures standing firm &amp; erect at the "present" arms as the "last post (our sweetest bugle call) breaks upon the tropical stillness. As if in keeping with such a sad event the sun lies hidden behind the ever thickening cloud veil whilst the remains of our first hero glide swiftly to a watery grave</p>
