  
 March, 1916 Friday 10 Work as usual during the day After tea several of us went for a walk to Zeitoun & had coffee & cakes Wrote a letter & sent some stamps to Amy Bryant & finished up by giving one of my pupils an extra hours work. 
 Saturday 11 Exam on Description takes up all morning. After dinner went along & had tea w/- Dad & go to pictures Make arrangements about going out tomorrow
