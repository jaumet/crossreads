
<p>MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 15, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, with W wind. Round Boundary</p>
<p>&amp; lopping in Mountain &amp; Top</p>
<p>paddocks. Linda brought my</p>
<p>lunch down to Middle bore</p>
<p>&amp; we were burning off there</p>
<p>afterwards.</p>
<p>Tickle went down to the farm</p>
<p>&amp; borrowed a horse from Kimber</p>
<p>Ringing yellow jackets in</p>
<p>Middle paddock after.</p>
