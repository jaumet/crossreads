<p>Anxious to go &amp; do their duty for their Country &amp; King. On making a  Study of their Faces &amp; hands I came to the Conclusion that they followed all Sorts of occupation's, some had very clear Complexions &amp; their hands were solf &amp; White which proved to me that they were from  The Office &amp; Business Establishment then again was the Man with a Complexion which showed that he had been following the Call of out door work &amp; then came the Way Back I mean by that Expression the Man who born &amp; bred in the Country &amp; was not used to the Town Life but nevertheless he was like the rest Anxious to go forth &amp; fight for his Country's Freedom.
