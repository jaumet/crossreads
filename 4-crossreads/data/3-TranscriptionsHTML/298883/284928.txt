<p>Managed to secure a lorry which brought me out to St. Maxent and I soon reached Tours en Vimeu once again Unfortunately it was then about 12 midnight and I found the gate to my billet locked and another place where I did manage to enter the yard I was angrily refused a bed by an extremely irate lady who did not appreciate being dragged from her bed very late <strike>at</strike> on a clear starry Winter's night.  I managed to find a bunk in the Canteen however, thanks to the Corporal there (Burgess by name, and another man named Taylor.</p>
