<p>Sunday 13th<br/>Church Parade We were  colected into various gangs this morning for a Fire Brigade all Camps are supposed to be organised in this way &ndash; and told just when the fire was to start as some Head wanted to see <strike>how</strike> what the organization was like. Off course it was perfect under the circumstances. But it would not have been if a real fire had occured  yesterday Walked to Halesburey in afternoon</p>
<p>Monday 14<br/>Got into a Squad for a Bayonet Fighting Competion <strike>Tuesday 15</strike> Snowed all last night about 8 or 9 inches deep Snowballing is the order of the day. An organized fight between the 2 4th &amp; other companys on Parade ground</p>
<p>Thursday 15th<br/>Training for Bayonet Fighting</p>
<p>Wednesday 16th<br/>Competition came off No 2 Co won Our Guard Mounting Squad also beaten The winner of these events got 3 or 4 days leave</p>
<p>Thursday 17&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As usual</p>
