 In the Field France 26/3/18 
 Mrs Parker Senr from nephew Bomb. O. H. McIntyre 
 My Dear Auntie 
 Just how pleased I was to receive your letter of Jan 23rd I can hardly say, I love getting your letters, they are always so newsy &amp; Bright, I only received it a couple of days ago, &amp; am going to answer it right away, You will have known that the great Fritz offensive has at last been launched &amp; so far my Unit is not in the fury of it, but expect our time will come, so will get this off while a good opportunity offers.
