17 July 1916 MonThe main body moved off from [Picquingy] at 9.30. I stopped behind with the Quartermaster till 5.30 pm. During the day I spent most of the time in the village. Our way out we passed the village of [Breilly], Ailly on Somme & Saint Sauveur. Arrived Longpre - Amiens 8.30 [text continued at top of page]
