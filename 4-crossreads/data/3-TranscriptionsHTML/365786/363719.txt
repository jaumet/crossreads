<p><strike>The Pyramids.  They face the cardinal points.</strike><br/>This huge mass of stone covers an area of nearly 13 acres.  It is constructed of a yellowish limestone quarried in the vicinity.  The space on top is about 12 yards square.  The pyramids face the cardinal points.<br/>Kephren looks higher but is a few feet lower &amp; about 100 feet less at the base.  It was covered with limestone slabs &amp; partially polished granite.<br/>Mykerinos is only 204 ft. high &amp; its sides about 350 ft. long.  Near is the Sphinx hewn out of the natural rock augmented by blocks of stone.</p>
