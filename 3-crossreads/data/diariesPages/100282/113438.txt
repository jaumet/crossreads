Got a few chocolates from the Comforts Funds this morning.
7th. A big thaw seems to have set in this morning, for it is very muddy fresh outside the ice is melting fast not too promising for our trip in the line.
A small mail came in last night I got 3 letters out of it.
8th. Left "Rose Wood" early this morning for the line, had breakfast at 3.30 moved out just as the late moon was rising wasn't it cold, we had no trouble relieving for all "posts" were told off they
