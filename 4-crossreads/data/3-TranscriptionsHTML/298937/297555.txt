<p>Friday 31st March</p>
<p>In regard to the application we "slept on it" &amp; came to the conclusion that it would be wiser to wait for a time.  Both Molloy &amp; I however, are hanking to get back to the Australians.<br/>Neither of us is in love with the disciplinary methods as practised here, although, the job being to my liking, I could put up with quite easily.<br/>The promotion I am in need of, however, added to the other makes me feel like taking a change.
