<p>of these 2 we would have a decent Camp, at any rate we are working hard to get them out of it, but they will both take some shifting.<br/>21st. The 1st Battalion reinforcements came in last night about, 150 strong got here a t 11.30. Have had a stiff mornings work. Battalion drill &amp; old Tubby kept us going all the morning, very funny at times to see the mix's up's &amp; hear the arguments, some more "Fragments" came across last night, Taplin brought me a batch of letters from Len.<br/>Pay day round once, &amp; I needed mine for it was a fair while since I drew my last money.<br/>This afternoon 2 other Sergts</p>
