<p>Wednesday 21st March 1917<br/>Boulogne (France)<br/>The Artillery (about 200) all slept in the same hut. Revielle 6am.Breakfast 7am (bacon, cheese, jam and tin meat, bread and tea). Issued with rations, 1tin meat, biscuits and cheese and marched off about 10am, arr Boulogne station 10.30am. Put into trucks and left about 11.50am.<br/>Arr. Etaples (E-taps) about 1.20pm then marched to camp about 2 mls. From station. Slept in Bell tent 12 men in each tent.</p>
