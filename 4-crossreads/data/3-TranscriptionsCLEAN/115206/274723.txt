
June 1918Sunday 2
Battery W.Lines.  near Pont NoyellesBeautiful weather. Jack H. did not arrive as I expected. had usual swim. After Church Service had a practice on the piano salvaged from the debris of Corbie. Numbers of brass instruments have appeared as also has a violin, clarinet and mandolin. The orchestra is having a flying start. At 11.30 am, Fritz commenced dropping salvoes of H and Gas down in the gully. He continued to do so until 12.30 am, when he suddenly ceased. O.B. and Knipe returned. Cpl Kelly down for a spell.
