<p>Sun Aug 11th<br/>Moved forward tonight to gullies near Lihons &amp; n.e. of Rosieres.</p>
<p>Mon Aug 12th:-<br/>Mick Carmody &amp; Evans killed today by a shell.<br/>Rsm Robertson had his ankle shattered by Colonel Sasse's groom letting a rifle go off.<br/>The hostile 'planes are laying plenty of eggs around us at night. Owing to the casualties of the 2nd &amp; 3rd Brigades the 1st division is too weak to do a divisional stunt.</p>
<p>Thurs 15th Aug:-<br/>Moved back at 7pm</p>
<p>to bivouacs along railway line between Vauvillers &amp; Harbonnieres.<br/>"Fritz" giving his usual free issue of aerial bombs tonight. He must not like getting his "knock-back" for 11 miles.</p>
<p>Fri Aug 16th<br/>Moved further back this morning passing through Harbonnieres, Bayonvillers, Hamel, Lamotte, Santerre, Vaire-sur-Corbie, to Vaux-sur-Somme where we encamped on the river bank.</p>
<p>Mon Aug 19th<br/>Tank demonstration in front of Hamel in preparation for our coming "hop-over".</p>
