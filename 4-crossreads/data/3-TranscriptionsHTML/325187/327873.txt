<p>[Wednesday 10 May 1916]</p>
<p>Still Cairo &amp; enjoying every hour of it, Plenty to do fixing up affairs for the Regiment  Very Hot.<br/>Met an old Bushman Sgt who has come over to open a Branch of the Commonwealth Bank- [Following paragraph are crossed out]<br/>but too late as I hope in 2 months we will be in France or England (Private)<br/>Horses, Kit, &amp; men arrive 9 pm &amp; I leave at 7 AM in the morning<br/>DIVISIONAL C.O. Gen CHAUVEL<br/>BRIGADE C.O. Gen COX<br/>Reg C.O. Col MEREDITH</p>
