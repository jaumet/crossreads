<p>home.  The nights are bitterly cold now though fine &amp; starry but the breezes seem to cut to the very marrow.  I shall be glad when we get a move on.<br/>The Australians held Sports here yesterday afternoon but I never attended them.<br/>All leave has been cut out, so we must be on the point of going.</p>
<p>17th.  Took my friend to our usual Sunday evening concert at the Royal, the Royal Marine Band were the principal ones tonight &amp; they were very good, especially their playing of one of my favourites "Land of hope &amp; glory".</p>
<p>18th.
