7th Feby /18
Photographed various stunts - Battery going into action - machine gun drill - and ambulance turnout.  Afternoon, two regiments turned out and re-enacted their famous charge at Beersheba. The scene was filled with excitement; and I can well imagine the demoralising effect on the enemy, of two regiments with bayonets drawn sweeping down on to them. In same small degree I sensed the excitement myself, for the charge was directed against theposition. which I occupied.  She charge of the regiment at Beersheba
