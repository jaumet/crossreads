<p>6 March 1918 Wednesday (X to X crossed out)<br/><strike>14th R.F.C. (articled)</strike><br/>I hope to send you some photos taken from above the Turks line next mail.<br/>We are well treated here, electric light in every Tent generated on the ground- great comfort after a Cavalry Mobile Brigade.<br/>X The planes send messages by wireless &amp; a XLAXON HORN-   We send by a Black &amp; white PANEL on the ground- MORSE.<br/>Men from all English Regts are here quite young men &amp; they treat us well, this is <u>not</u> the Australian Flying Squadron- I send you some photos of Machines-X</p>
