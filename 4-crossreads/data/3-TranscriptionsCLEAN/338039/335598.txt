  
 Apr.20th. Our A.D.S. a couple of hundred yards behind reserve trenches.  "FIRST DAY" in action- Stretcher bearing in support trenches.  Our dug-out comfortable-  Part of Line called Shaftesbury Avenue- "Jenolan Caves"-  Danger of shells "M's S"- Action of British Guns.  Leave trench at 6 p. m for A.D.S.  Great Destruction of village. - no inhabitants-  No Red Cross or Brassards.  Batteries around our Stations encourages shelling from Fritz.
