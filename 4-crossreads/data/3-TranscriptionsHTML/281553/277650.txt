<p>15th Friday<br />Turks very quiet indeed. Heavy Naval Bombardment South most of day. Small mail in.</p>
<p>16th Saturday<br />Demonstration given by us 4 a.m. with Artillery &amp; Navy. Poorest yet witnessed, turned in to sleep at 5-30 a.m. Much Artillery &amp; Naval fire later morning, answered weakly by Turks Guns. Very heavy bombmt. South.</p>
<p>17th Sunday<br />Line very quiet. Weather very dull &amp; cold.</p>
