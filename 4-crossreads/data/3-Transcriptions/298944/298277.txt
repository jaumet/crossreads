<p>Buccanara, the rather dryly humorous old bearer of the 3rd, who is now an officer in one of the battalions, visited us tonight for a chat. He is coming to the concert next Thursday. Only when up the line is there anything wrong with the life, thanks be, &amp; that is not for long. At the back they get well looked after.</p>
