 pictures after tea with Yvonne at Eastleigh. Y. is a lovely little French girl married to Langston Earls about 3 months; comes from Bethune &amp; is now my cousin. They gave me such a lovely time, would have liked to stay instead of visit elsewhere. stayed all night again retiring late. 
 19th. Friday. Caught midday train reaching London where I was kept very busy at bank etc. By 6pm train went to Bolton and reached &quot;North Leigh&quot; about midnight finding them waiting up for me. Never felt so at home anywhere in my life before as here, they were both so loving and homely &amp; nice. Beautifull room and surroundings.
