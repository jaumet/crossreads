<p>Wednesday June 26th : Mardyk</p>
<p>On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : stroll with Gill after tea : plenty of girls kicking around &amp; we got a few glad eyes thrown at us :</p>
<p>Thursday June 27th : Mardyk</p>
<p>On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : made a bunk so did not go out after tea</p>
<p>Friday June 28th : Mardyk</p>
<p>On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : stroll to the aerodrome after tea &amp; had an interesting look round : flying is now a conquered art &amp; the progress of a few years is wonderful :</p>
<p>Saturday June 29th : Mardyk</p>
<p>On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : did not go out after tea : another raid after dark &amp; a second one before morn &amp; Fritz got a very warm reception :</p>
<p>Sunday June 30th</p>
<p>My day off so I had a roam round : Gen Birdwood inspected the boys but I have heard enough of his soft soap talk so went out &amp; missed the parade : went to church at night &amp; stayed for Holy Communion : very nice :</p>
<p>Monday July 1st : Mardyk : Mardyk</p>
<p>Nothing doing in day : on duty 10 pm</p>
<p>Tuesday July 2nd</p>
<p>Nothing doing in day : on duty 10 pm</p>
<p>Wednesday July 3rd : Mardyk</p>
<p>Into Dunkirk after tea &amp; had a stroll round to my friends place : one of the Fritz 'planes that came down the other night was on view in the square &amp; it is some wreck : just a few splinters &amp; tatters of cloth left : rather a hideous death</p>
