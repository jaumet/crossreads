 [Easter Day 23 April 1916] 
 CAIRO  Rused  Rushed up here from Assuit got some clothes; leave again 11 o'clock Express  Sitting in train was a man I last met in Portugese East Africa at  Beira  BEIRA. Had only a few hours to get a few nessesaries and in my hurry left Watch in lavatory- You'll think I'm losing my head, Belt at Lemnos &amp; now Watch here. I may get watch but the Native servants steal anything  of  from " FOOL SAHIBS "
