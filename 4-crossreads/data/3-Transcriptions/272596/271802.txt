I have one witness only against me &amp; if we can't beat him I'll eat my hat but the indignity of the thing is what troubles me for I know they will think it a fearful thing home whereas in reality it is a mere nothing, at any rate it has spoilt my trip &amp; enjoyment entirely for do &amp; say what I will I can't get the unfortunate occasion out of my mind, however if they make a fuss over it, &amp; come &amp; cut up rough, I will pack</p>
