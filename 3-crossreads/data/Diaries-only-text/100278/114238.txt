Had charge of 60 men on a big fatigue party carrying timber, iron, etc. up to where they are building a lot of new huts on a little rise.
Saw a fine smash this morning down on the railway line, 4 trucks loaded with rails come tearing down the line completely out of hand she never struck nothing till she arrived at the dump just close to where I had a party working, here there was a big string of trucks being unloaded you should have seen the boys leaping when they heard the shouting they fairly flew through the air, just in time for the runaways hit them with a terrific crash, you could see nothing for the next few minutes but flying metal logs amp splinters, when the stink settled down there was a pretty sight, trucks all over the place piled on top of one another in a most bewildering way, the line
