<p>April, 1915<br/>Tuesday 6<br/>Divisonal Hd Qts &amp; infantry leave today for Alexandria thence across the Mediterranean it seems useless omitting names etc: when none of my Diary notes apparently have reached home.<br/>Cooler after the Dust storm. Here is one re Nurses &ndash;<br/>When the Sultan heard of the 100's of pretty!! Australian Nurses he sent 4 of his Court to Port Said to pick out 6 &amp; if they did not obtain 6 as pretty as where in the Harem, they  need not come back &ndash; They came &amp; saw, &amp; then jumped overboard!!</p>
