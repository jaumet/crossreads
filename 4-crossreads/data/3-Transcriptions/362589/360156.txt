I told them I would collect tickets if there was nothing else to do, but so far I have been very lucky &ndash; Barring Salisbury Plain Camp where my plank bed will always be in memory of what other many better men than myself have to get used to.<br/>We are getting a lot of wounded from the front; some of the recent pushes have been very successful but at a considerable sacrifice of our own Australians. Regarding England itself I must say that I came home with the inform belief that we must win somehow some time, &amp; my impressions now I am here more than increase ones confidence.</p>
