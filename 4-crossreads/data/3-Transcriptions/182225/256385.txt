
<p>136</p>
<p>SEPT. &nbsp; SATURDAY 23 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Signalling all morning and we had another possible v&nbsp;probable football match and the probables won&nbsp;by a cricket score. Poss &amp; self went to the pictures at night &amp; they were rotten. M<sup>r</sup>&nbsp;Neylan was transferred to the 23<sup>rd</sup>&nbsp;Brigade, &amp; we were all very sorry to loose&nbsp;him.</p>
