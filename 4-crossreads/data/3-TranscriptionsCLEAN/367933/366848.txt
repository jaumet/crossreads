  
 6.11.16 To Hon Sec. 1st LH Comforts Depot 
 Dear Madam Many thanks for parcel of comforts sent by the committee to me. They arrived in good order. I am no longer with the old regiments having been seconded for duty with the Australian Squadron of the Royal Flying Corps . Thanking you again I am  Yrs faithfully G K Mills Lieut
