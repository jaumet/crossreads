<p>chap from 31st btn &amp; he told me Reg N had been killed at Polygon Wood on about Sep 27th. He was first of all wounded then a shell came along &amp; blew him to pieces. Last Sunday received letters from Muriel, Mrs Nancarrow, Minnie Lusc[ombe], Guild, Uncle Harry &amp; Amanda.<br/>18/10/17 Could not get to sleep for a long time on account of the vermin crawling about &amp; going to bed too early viz 5.30pm. Received change underclothes &amp; sox so had cold bath in creek alongside camp &amp; changed. Went to canteen in afternoon &amp; bought some chocolate. Some of the chaps arrived</p>
