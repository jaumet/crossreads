I returned to Holborn for Tea and later on I went out to Finsbury Park and met my friend and paid another visit to the Marliborough Theatre Picture Show and again spent a splendid evening seeing a good programe, as usual the place was crowded, and had to wait a little while for a Seat. After coming out we went and had some supper and took the Train back to Harrangay, where I said bye bye to my little friend and once more returned home to my Flat.
