<p>11 June 1916 Sun<br/>More news of big Russian victories the total of prisoners within a week totals 64,000. To say nothing of the killed &amp; wounded. Mr. Minter leaves for Hazebrouck also Captain Needham both good fellows &amp; we spent some rare old times to-gether. After tea with Keith Bill Fordyce &amp; Tom Buck.</p>
<p>[Note in margin]<br/>Vic Carter rejoins us.</p>
<p>12 June 1916 Mon<br/>Captain [indecipherable] Arthur comes in to hospital. He is commanding officer of the 17th Battery Field Artillery. Herbie goes for his D.C.M. &amp; gets out of it. The Russians are still pressing on. The great advance taking thousands more prisoners. Spent a happy night with Tom Buck, Keith at the old inn on the [night]</p>
<p>[Note in margin]<br/>Sid Bragg returns.</p>
