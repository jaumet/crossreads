We lost no time in availing ourselves, and Nurse Locke and I promptly climbed to the nearest hill and lost ourselves midst the maze of battery communications. After a few hours welcome walk around the town we dined at the Westminster Hotel and returned to ship 9 pm.
6th December /17
Passengers were allowed ashore, and there was no scarcity of the small curious double prowed skiffs to ferry them. ?Jillock? and I visited the city and climbed to the outlook of Fort St Elmo from where we had a transcending outlook over the grand harbour and the Blue Mediterranean.
