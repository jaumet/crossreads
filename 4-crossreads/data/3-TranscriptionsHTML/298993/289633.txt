<p>it fell a good way out from where we was it was a terrible sight a large number of our Coy put out of action we stuck it till it got dark then cleared out went back we seemed to have got lost we went around in a circle no one seemed to like it at all we found camels about midnight we must have been wandering about 3 hours mounted and rode about a mile and unpacked I had something to eat the first meal all day turned in 1.30. Bolston Byfield Rhall hit<br/>Friday April 20.4.17. stand to 3AM saddled up filled water bottle got some tucker got all gear on and walked about a mile took up a position in a creek or waddie dug ourselves in the battle has started</p>
