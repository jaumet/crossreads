  
 March 3rd Harry Elliot & self have a pleasant morning walk down & under the Dean Bridge & Gardens - In the afternoon an Australian (in company with a Lassie) set out for Crammond - a grand walk in which much picturesque scenery is encountered - The [indecipherable] & the Ocean. Returned Edinburgh. March 4th Letters written & received - "Poems of Sentiment" (Wilcox) - O. Henry's - Last day in Edinburgh - Evening spent at "59".
