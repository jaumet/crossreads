Sunday Nov. 10.11.18got up to day  saw Dr.  am feeling very much better to day  raining heavy and cold-  wrote to Josie BellMonday Nov. 11.11.18am feeling much better to day  it is Raining cats and dogs this day  water running all through the tent.   am waiting to hear what germany will do now.Tuesday Nov. 12.11.18went to concert to night  word come through 8.P.M germany has signed the Armistice.  Chreeing any song god save the King not me
