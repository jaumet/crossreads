  
 Tuesday 29 May 1917. 
 Flying fish seen today by the hundred. Another very hot day and sea a little rougher today. 
 1 st  sports meeting this afternoon. 
 Physical jerks during morning - a practice for the competition in the sports.  One of the 15. Japanese Cruiser moving very quickly into various positions yesterday & today.  
 Sports caused amusement among the boys. 
 Wrote 2 letters. 
 Detailed for guard duty tomorrow to mount at 9 am. 
 Sleeping on deck again tonight. 
 G.S.S. Cmn & I went down below into stokehold & engine room, & right along to end of propeller shaft. 
 Speed now Full as indicated on dial above log book below.
