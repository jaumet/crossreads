<p>Thursday July 5<br/>Mail arrived. Our first mail since leaving Ausy. All the boys excited. Pleased to hear all are well at home &amp; with Glady. Charlie tells me Dulcie is sending him a parcel<br/>Friday July 6<br/>Riding test. Practically all passed. Saw first dummy gas attack to day &amp; Scotchmen manoeuvering.<br/>Rumoured that big attack coming off.<br/>Saturday July 7<br/>Usual drill. Looking forward to getting out of isolation Started Bridge tournament to day to pass away long evenings.<br/>Sunday July 8<br/>Inspection by A.D.M.S.</p>
