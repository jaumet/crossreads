
May 12  Same old thing always cloudy & cold. Some argument in the tent last night, but I kept well out of it - you bet - Must get a bath today.(Later) Hoo-blooming-ray discovered the bath room & had a bonza shower. Red hot & then icy cold. Gee it was great. Tomorrow the board sits & I'll hear the verdict. These Frenchies breed beautiful cows, & draught horses. Would like to own a few in Australia like them. Wonder how Harry is getting along up the line or if he has had the bad luck to stop one. (Thus I write from the top of Napoleons Column). For sheer peaceful beauty the scene from this vantage point is absolutely the best yet.
