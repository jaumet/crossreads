 3 Heard from Edie Budden the other day. She seemed to be having a very good time. How awful of them to have made Meagher Lord Mayor &ndash; Such an absolute rotter too. We had a church parade today at which I was present. The Officiating chaplain was not much of a preacher. Rather a ninkumpoop, I think. Well, au-revoir, old girl. Very much love to you all. Your loving brother Fred
