<p>every day plenty of Transport work to attend to &amp; little time to do it in.<br/>All Horses have to be tested for Glanders in the next couple of days &amp; as the test kills them within 24 hours if there is anything wrong with them, I am anxiously awaiting the result on my little Pony, whom I have become genuinely attached too.<br/>We had a change of W O's today, ours going to the 27th &amp; theirs coming here.<br/>Two Turkish Spies were captured on the Canal this morning &amp; sent along.  They were dressed as Arabs, &amp; were found to have a copy of our Canal Defences on them.  It is now 6 months since I left home, but it seems far longer.  We are getting no news of the War so I dont know how things are going.</p>
