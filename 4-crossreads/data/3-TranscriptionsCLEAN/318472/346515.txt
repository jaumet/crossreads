
18 October 1918FridayToday the same as yesterday - lazing about and having a good time generally. Reported Germans evacuating the whole of the Belgian Coastline Good news coming in from all Fronts.
1918 October 19SaturdayPut in the day pottering about the farm. Hilled a few rows of potatoes in the morning and in the afternoon Arch & I did a little fishing in the Currumbene & I caught one fish as the result of our efforts. Good news continues from all Fronts. Lille has been evacuated by the enemy - occupied 5th Army
