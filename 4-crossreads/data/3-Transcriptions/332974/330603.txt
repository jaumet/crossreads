<p>German Concentration Camp<br/>Liverpool. N.S.W. June 27th 1916</p>
<p>To the Consul General of the Unites States of America<br/>Melbourne</p>
<p>Sir<br/>We beg to notify you that any correspondence sent to you by Mr Walter Emde has not been authorised by <u>all the men interred here</u> who formerly were prisoners of war on Torrens Island &amp; we beg to inform you that we are representatives of more than 350 men formerly detained on Torrens Island &amp; and a list of the men we represent is at your disposal. We do not think there are any men who are against theses representatives with the exception of W. Emde &amp; perhaps one other man.</p>
<p>We are<br/>Sir<br/>your respectfully<br/>Siegmund I. Villiers<br/>J Frank Bungardy<br/>C. Behrendt<br/>H. Thomziek<br/>Representatives of Men formerly interned on Torrens Island</p>
