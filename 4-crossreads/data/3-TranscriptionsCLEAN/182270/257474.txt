 WEDNESDAY, JUNE 11, 1919. 
 Fine & hot. Got the mares & foals 
 & stalion out of Hill paddock 
 & put them in Strand. At the 
 Middle paddock bore again. 
 Tried to lower the pipe & got it 
 stuck I watered Joe Perry's cattle 
 at the 4D troughs. 
 Father got a summons from 
 the P.P. board for not killing 
 rabbits.
