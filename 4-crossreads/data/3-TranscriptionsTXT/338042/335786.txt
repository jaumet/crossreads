
Jan. 8th.Early rise - Pack up & Load Lorries - leave Sheenwerck, which proved to be safest town in which we have had the pleasure of showing - Arrive at Bailleul 11 a.m. (last in this town in July 1916) Afternoon spent in preparing Hall & Stage for Panto. - For first time in France I become the proud possessor of a Private Billet.
