
Thursday 13 July 1916Rostron and I went to Comforts fund but did not get anything.  Then took photo to police of man whom I had photographed.  Policeman took us over barracks & showed us the court house.  Left films to be developed.
Friday 14 July 1916In town with Nelson.  Went to Comforts fund depot but they had gone to France.  Sent registered packet to Stella.
