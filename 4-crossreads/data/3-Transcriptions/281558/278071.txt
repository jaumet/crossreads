<p>(first real fall of snow today : very cold)</p>
<p>Holy City by the Christians once more : home about 9.30 pm</p>
<p>Monday Dec 17th</p>
<p>O.T.B. : Mass parade in morn &amp; presentation of medals : we almost froze to death on the parade ground : freezing all day &amp; very cold : signallers were called forth I fell out &amp; I think &amp; passed the test alright so with a bit of luck may get to the school yet :</p>
<p>Tuesday Dec 18th</p>
<p>O.T.B. : hurt my hand &amp; it is a bad sprain so got off parade : went to see Reg after tea :</p>
<p>Wednesday Dec 19th</p>
<p>O.T.B. : two years today since I left Aust : pictures after tea : very cold this week &amp; everything is frozen up : paths &amp; roads are very slippery</p>
