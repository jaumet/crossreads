 beginning to roll up in consequence.  a muster parade before dinner;  writing letters after dinner &amp; went nowhere 
 Mon 15th Signalling close to camp all day;  Stan went to Heliopolis after tea  The reinforcements arrived to-day; also some extra horses from Abbassiah I went on feeding party to-night in Yules place as he wanted to go to town 
 Tue 16th Were told this morning that we had to parade dismounted, &amp; just as the Regt. was moving off on manoeuvres the Adjutant rode up &amp; told us the saddle up
