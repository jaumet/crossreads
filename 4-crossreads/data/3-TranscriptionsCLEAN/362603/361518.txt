  
 Red Cross letterhead 13.9.17 Bulford Dear Mum, Your letters of the 1st & 8th July have just arrived.  I had 11 altogether this mail.  I've only 30 to answer now.  Everyone expects a nice long letter.  How the deuce I am going to answer them all I don't know.  I am Orderly Corporal To-night.  It is just after 9 pm.  I don't have to report now till 10.15 pm.  Ellis is snug in bed asleep.  It is cold To-day & the wind is howling a Treat outside.  I have had a nice supper of Home-made apple pie & cocoa, & bread, butter & Golden Syrup.  We look after ourselves.  You will wonder from whence came the Pie.  My mate is isolated with Measles & I call at His Friend's also my Friend's place & I bring these dainties Home .
