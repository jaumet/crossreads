<p>London every time I see her.  I got lost over &amp; over again this afternoon, &amp; often I found myself walking in an entirely different direction to where I wanted to go she's a big place alright, I had a job to get home I never saw the tube so crowded before especially the "inner circle", it was packed &amp; the platforms were swarming with people.<br/>7th.  Sunday &amp; things are very quiet I even went round to Euston this morning to make enquires about the boats running between Liverpool &amp; the Isle of Man, they start tomorrow so tonight I leave London &amp; will spend a day with the Allens in Blackpool all going well.<br/>Left Euston at 11.45 AM. for Liverpool &amp; after a 6 hours run arrived at destination, walked</p>
