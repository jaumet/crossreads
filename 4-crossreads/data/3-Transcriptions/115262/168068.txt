<p>Sunday 28 October 1917.</p>
<p>On again at dawn travelled all day till 2 pm when we reached a point between Shiekh Nuran Stn &amp; Shellal. Camel Brigade <s>Brigade</s> bivouacked.&nbsp;<s>and</s>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Another batch of 18 letters I received by 2 lots of mail that must have gone to Engd<span style="font-size: 10.8333330154419px; line-height: 19.2592582702637px;">&nbsp;</span>before coming to Egypt. This is good, oh!</p>
<p>On picquet tonight.</p>
