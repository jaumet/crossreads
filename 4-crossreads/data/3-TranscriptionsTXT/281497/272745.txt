 Len tells me this morning that his groin is getting sore again &amp; that he is going to see the Dr. in a couple of days. 
 26th.  Last night was a stormy one &amp; the wind howled &amp; raged like fury &amp; now &amp; again heavy storms of rain would come down, but luckily we are camped on sandy ground &amp; that saved us from being flooded out though we had gutters dug round our tent. Heavy firing could be heard yesterday evening I think it was Verdun way for it was East from here.
