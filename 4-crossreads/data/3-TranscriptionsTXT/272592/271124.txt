 We have football &amp; cricket going again, you can't beat a bit of sport to liven things up a bit 
 Len &amp; a few more of the boys have gone into Poperinge today 
 I heard this evening that the 3rd Division had been sighted at last &amp; were about Hazebrouck its time they done something for they have not struck a blow as yet, they have had a good time in England. 
 Leave for England started again the other day its about time I should think. 
 Received a big parcel of sweets from home today they were in splendid condition &amp; had kept well.
