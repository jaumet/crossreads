<p>Tuesday Ap23 Gale blew this morning Two planes from school came croppers. Field Amb drill dismounted Communication Drill Lecture Guard Duty What a farce these schools are. Some of the things we have here we never use.<br/>Wednesday Ap 24<br/>Posting Guards, Discipline, Rifle Drill. Afternoon Lecture on map setting.<br/>Thursday Ap 25  Anzac Day<br/>In morning we had a memorial service. Sports rest of day.<br/>Concert in evening by Lena Ashwell Concert party. These coves in base camps have a good time.</p>
