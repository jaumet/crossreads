
December  1918.Tuesday 24
At Sea. and Port Said."Karoola"Weather unsettled and inclined to be very cool. After turning around all night, and we entered the port at 7.30 am Purchased a few articles and despatched three post cards to England. The boys had quite a good team in the purchasing of goods from the bumboat kings.I had quite a job as interpreter and caught quite a deal of things for nothing. R.M.S. Mandala passed out. Two wrecks just outside.
