 He is a nice kiddy   &amp;  has quite won the hearts of the Sisters here. 
 No doubt we are a jolly crowd,  but can work some. Must stop now think I have written enough for a time. Must make myself tidy for Afternoon tea   &amp;  duty. 
 Fondest love to you all   &amp;  kisses. Love to all my friends. If any of you have any spare cash   &amp; want me to buy furs etc for you I England, let me know soon, in case I get to    Blight   (means Home). If you heard the dear boys say it you would love the word. 
 From Edith
