<p>Sat 31st Decr<br/>Hospital recommended for England, wrote a letter from Etaples 26, AGH Home, not feeling up too much, helped in the ward all day, AD nurses here, plenty of Australians here of all battns, some 3rd Divn in already, Eddy Craven gone to England and all men over 40 years of age.</p>
<p>1st Janr 17<br/>At Etaples Hospital all day, done nothing, in evening got ready for blighty,  after tea left in motor ambulance and arrived in train travelling all night to Le Havre getting very poor tucker on this hospital train, had a bit of a sleep, few tommies in here.</p>
<p>2nd Janry 17<br/>After travelling all day, very weary journey, arrived at Le Havre at about 6 oclock, went on the Braemar Castle and went down below, very good, sail started about 11 oclock, very smooth, very good staff of AMC nurses here and look after a man well.</p>
