Tuesday March 5.3.18 turned out 5.30.  Breakfast went out grazing camels any amount of feed here was relieved 12  had dinner rested till 5 P.M. had tea had a letter from Bell turned in Wednesday March 6.3.18 turned out 5.AM fed up had Breakfast cleaned up the rest of the coy are out on a march again   went to canteen got a Bit of stuff had dinner very windy to day rested till 4.30 had tea on line guard to night
