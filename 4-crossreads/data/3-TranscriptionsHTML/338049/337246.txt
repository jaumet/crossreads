<p>Tuesday 5 Feb.<br/>Read in bed before breakfast. Then read afterwards all the morning being wasted on reading.</p>
<p>Had visit from Lieut Stanbury &amp; Capt Ferguson. Put a line through to UR Sappers Frost &amp; Chapman proceeding through to RD2.</p>
<p>Dinner late. Ration wagon arrived in midst of business.</p>
