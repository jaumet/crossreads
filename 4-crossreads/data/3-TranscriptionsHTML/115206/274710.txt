<p>May 1918<br/>Monday 20</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash;la-Daours<br/>Weather beautiful. Had an inspection at 9am by Bdge Major, Received orders to promenade to my unit and stand by there as an emergency.<br/>Should a hostile bombardment take place to report immediately. At 3 am this morning an aerial torpedo fell on a B,A.C's Lines &ndash; made a mess.<br/>Arrived at W.L. at 3.30 pm and reported to Capt Redmond.<br/>Ran to Fuilloy during the morning.</p>
