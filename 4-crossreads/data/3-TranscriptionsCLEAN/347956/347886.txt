  
 12.2.15 
 Fri 12th This morning we were out bright & early & were out on parade at 7.30 AM.  The whole of this camp took part in a big scheme;  We were attacking a hill a few miles from here, the artillery first bombarded it & their shooting was very good  The Light Horse was attacking the left flank & the infantry the right & centre;  we were advancing under cover of the artillery fire, until it was too dangerous  to fire of hitting us & then we had to finish the battle with rifle & bayonet;  Cardboard men were stuck up in the ground to represent the enemy & by the time we were given the order to charge with the
