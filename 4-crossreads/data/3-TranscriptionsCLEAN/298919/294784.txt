A few minutes later he came up to us & asked for particulars of destination etc.  At 4.10 being on watch I was sitting down in office chair quietly reading an novel and at the same time turning the variable condenser around with my left hand when I heard a station quite near us sending out SOS, I immediately dropped my book, got the best adjustment & stood by.  I had not long to wait.  It was the SS City of Edinburgh, & reported that a submarine was chasing them and shelling them at the same time.  Four men were killed.  He gave us his lat & long which was 40 miles behind us, and right where we would have passed had we taken our original course.
