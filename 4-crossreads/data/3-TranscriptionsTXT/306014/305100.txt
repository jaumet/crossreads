"The Gold fish". Later we had tea &amp; then went to the evening service at the Cathedral which we enjoyed; the organ music was especially fine. The organ itself has wonderful power &amp; the gothic nature of the building's architecture added a wonderful echo effect to its music. As we walked away from the place after the service the throb of the organ followed us until the houses intervened &amp; brought us back to the consciousness that we had to return to our muddy camp.
After a nice drive through the country with a young moon shining down upon us, we arrived home at 9.30 &amp; were soon sound asleep in our little bunks.
