<p>November 1918.<br/>Sunday 3</p>
<p>Hosptl. Birmingham<br/>The weather today is the limit. There has been a heavy Scotch mist all day making the place simply miserable. Had one letter only, from London. Am waiting for news of my approved transfer to the same hospital as Roy is a patient of.<br/>Hungary has signed an armistice.</p>
