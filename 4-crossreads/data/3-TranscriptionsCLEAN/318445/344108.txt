Thus was the whole trench system East of Clery  was now  cleared of the enemy, and therefore, a line was established from which the troops might make the general attack on Mount St Quentin. The men now rested in the trenches, received their issue of rum, and smoked cigarettes, the Germans in the meantime keeping the neighbourhood in a state of illumination with  their  flares. Thus was the situation of affairs as midnight approached Early  on the morning of the 31st August the 5th Brigade was assembled in readiness to make the grand attack designed to turn the German positions from the North-West .
