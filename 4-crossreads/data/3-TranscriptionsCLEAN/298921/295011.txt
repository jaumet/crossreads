  
 February, 1915 Sunday 7 Straightening up Cargo & held Church parade all the Reinforcements are here together -  arrived crowd of infantry, artillery, & Light  Horse, Camels, Guns, Wagons Remounts, - Hospitals, Veterinary Sections etc:- Gravelly pebbly sand the Desert has no vestige of grass or herbage & "boots" wear out very quickly.
