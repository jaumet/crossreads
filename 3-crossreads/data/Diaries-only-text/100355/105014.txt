Very little traffic was on the Cantillon road but after walking as far as Bazuel we secured a French lorry driver by Madagassan which took us to Catillon. We arrived chez-Delatte just as they were participating of their Xmas dinner but having eaten a tin of bully at Bazuel we refused to eat with them despite the protestations of Madame mademoiselles.
