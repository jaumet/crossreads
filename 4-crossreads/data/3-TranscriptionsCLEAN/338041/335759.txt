  
 Dec. 1st. Another raid on London - Unsuccessful - Fatigues - A trip to Picquigny and a visit to the "Duds" Vaudeville Show - A versatile comedian & a "bon Orchestra - A meal earned by Patience but paid for by cash.

 Dec. 3rd. A free day granted us after strenuous (?) guard duties - Letter writing - Better weather & the result on our boys - Assisting Q.M. Sergt.
