
Thurs. 15-6-'16.turned down  [indecipherable] J. McMahon & self evaded Dr's inspection & are holding ourselves in readiness for the Trenches "Up the Line". Gas helmets etc issued late to night.Frid. 16-6-'16Reveille 2.45 a.m. Bk'fast 3 a.m. Marched away withBand playing 3.30 a.m. Down Etaples road past Hospitals (#26 Genl. Canadian) Night Nurses bade us God-speed at Dawn. Entrained with hundreds of all Regiments & got away at 6 a.m. Our crowd in Horse boxes. Weather cold. No money. Mac & self carrying extra blanket contrary to orders. Steenwerck our destination reached
