
May 1918Friday 3
Bussy-la-DaoursHad a run to F.H.B. at Bonnay. Saw three Fritz's dropped near P.O.W. camp, from La Neuvelle.Received several letters,two from cousin Mary and one from Jack Harvey, also several others including one from Geo Drury.Received also, a letter from Adrienne.Paid Ken Gunn 15.  Rect   Alex Mouab, at battery baggage lines had an agreeable talk. He is in in 24th A.S.C. 3rd divvy.Weather glorious.
