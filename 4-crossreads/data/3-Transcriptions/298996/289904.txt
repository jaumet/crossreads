<p>24.5.15<br/>Last night went to Cairo &amp; had a good time. re catery Ice cream arc, &amp; listening to the Orgister Orchestra playing.</p>
<p>25.5.15<br/>We received news that were are to go to the front tomorrow so we are packing up <strike>now</strike> today.</p>
<p>26.5.15<br/>We left Zutoter for Alexander 6.15. AM. this morning. It was a hellish hot day to travel to day all along the line all one can see is ground</p>
