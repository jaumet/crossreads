The net result of many invasions was the Egyptian civilization as it existed under the Pharaohs.The greater number of modern Egyptians form the Fellahin Class.  There are the peasants - literally tillers.  There are 7 or 8 million of them.  Poor & miserably housed in dwellings of four low walls made of crude bricks of Nile mud thatched with straw to form a flat roof on which is stored their fuel, goats & fowls & where their children play.
