<p>Monday 24 January, 1916</p>
<p>We marched on to our camping ground dead beat &amp; fell down where we stood. About an hour later the tents came so we pitched tents &amp; had some breakfast after that I payed a visit to the canteen &amp; bought a few eatables.</p>
<p>Went to bed at about 6.30.</p>
