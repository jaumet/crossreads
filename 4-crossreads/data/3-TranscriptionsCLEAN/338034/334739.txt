We were relieved from outpost about 4 P.M. by a Yeomanry Brigade & we then rode into near Khan Yunis where we arrived at 11 P.M. & camped till morning. Apl 22nd :- This afternoon the Brigade trekked back again to the front cavalry outpost line where we expect to be camped for awhile at least. We have been bombed frequently of late, in one raid three Taubes accounted for 70 odd horses & seven men killed & wounded in our Brigade alone.
