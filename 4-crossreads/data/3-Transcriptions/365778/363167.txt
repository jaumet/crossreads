<p>Thursday 11/1/17</p>
<p>Visited Rifle Range now almost complete. Cable from Doff, also letters.</p>
<p>Friday 12/1/17</p>
<p>Visited by Colonel Smith 5th Bde, also Babe, they both were delighted &ndash; went for a spin to Amien returning at 6.30 pm.</p>
<p>Saturday 13/1/17</p>
<p>Usual Training &ndash; men shaping well &ndash; lecture at night to Officers &amp; N.C.O's on Map Reading.</p>
<p>Sunday 14/1/17</p>
<p>Inspection of 6th Bde. No bad &ndash; visited by Col Reynolds &ndash; went to see Rifle Range. Men played football in afternoon &ndash; Wrote to Doff.</p>
