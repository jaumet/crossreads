
Thursday 1 July 1915Joseph's Birth anniversary [Josephine Nash]Sent her a cable last night Very busy all day Admissions about 80 patients.
Friday 2 July 1915Busy all day Temperature comfortable 3  450 odd patients
Saturday 3 July 1915Horse at 11 a.m. Sent off to Australia 131 patients All went well Captain Plant went to Alexandria
