<p>15 February 1918 Friday<br/><strike>Letter from Nowell &amp; 2 from the wife alls well</strike><br/>We have a BAND for the 1st L.H.B. mounted on little white DONKEYS- it adds to our pleasure in off duty hours-  Moving on tomorrow <strike>to Jericho!!!</strike><br/>Boils are better but I spend a few hours every day getting them dressed.<br/>13 Parcels from Australian Comforts Fund arrived- Cakes Vegetables Sweets all very welcome.  By a strange coincidence my Parcel was from Miss Rose Clapperton of Brookong, I have sent her a Post Card</p>
