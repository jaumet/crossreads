  
 Octobre 5 Coy still resting. Good news continues to come to hand. It is now reported that the N. Zealanders & 3rd Div have done very well & have captured large numbers of prisoners. About 2000 prisoners passed here today. Germans are putting up very little resistance. 
 Octobre 6 Good news continues to come from Front. Between 4,000 & 5,000 prisoners have been taken so far. We are still resting near Reningheltz. The weather is now very cold & windy - having rain also.
