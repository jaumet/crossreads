<p>Thursday  8/3/17</p>
<p>Strong winds blowing &ndash; bitterly cold, snow fell at intervals during night &amp; day. Light artillery very busy. Very few casualtys through.</p>
<p>Friday  9/3/17</p>
<p>Snowing &ndash; foggy etc &ndash; Very quiet. A few trench feet came through.</p>
<p>Saturday  10/3/17</p>
<p>Dirty foggy day &ndash; Snow thawing. Excepting for local artillery duels very quiet. Very few casualtys. Wrote to Mrs O'Kelly giving more particulars of Hubert.</p>
<p>Sunday  11/3/17</p>
<p>Miserable sort of a dull day. Busy ay night. Fritz's artillery giving us extra special attention &ndash; also throwing over great numbers of Minniewwurfers etc.</p>
<p>Monday  12/3/17</p>
<p>Dull to drizzling rain &ndash; Got letters from G. Nock &amp; C. Hagan. Very busy all night, nearly all cases, were very badly wounded. Performed several urgent operations at M.D.S.</p>
