<p>Tuesday 25.1.16 (33)<br/>The crew put all the boats in the water this morning &amp; tried to get the launch to kick for about 2 hours. Like my old bike she went for about a mile &amp; for the last 1&frac12; hours she has been on the drift. She must be down near the canal entrance by now.</p>
<p>A P &amp; O liner from Australia came in while we on Roll Call this morning.</p>
