<p>My Diary from 11th May 1916 Tel-el-Kebir</p>
<p>Orders came for us to be prepared to move out to the Canal where a big fight is in progress. General Muster Parade for inspection by the Colonel. I had my squad out. Col complimented us on our work.</p>
<p>Friday 12th May<br/>Anniversary of my landing at Gallipoli celebrated quietly. We had our first meeting of our Sergeants Debating Society. The subject Town versus Country life. I took Town<br/>Life and Country</p>
