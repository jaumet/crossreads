<p>107<br/>France.</p>
<p>The work the Battn was on was to unload 2 train loads of blue metal in the day. I kept to my sanitary work. One Sunday afternoon the Padre, Chaplain Wilson came over from H.Q. and had a little Sunday Afternoon service in one of the huts. There were not many men who volunteered to come along and listen.</p>
<p>That night I was approached by one of the men out of No 18 platoon and asked to conduct a service for them in their hut. I approached Horace Horsewell and together we went along and started a Song Service.</p>
