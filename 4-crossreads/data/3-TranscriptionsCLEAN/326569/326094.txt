 After 1.  Unload & press trig. to ease return spring 2.  Oil bore 3.  On return to billets - strip gun & clean thoroughly 4.  Carry out any necessary repairs 5.  Wash, dry & oil dirty mags 6.  Examine barrel for metallic fouling & remove same 7.  Lower tension of return spring to zero 
 Duties of gun crew 
 Duties of No. 1 1.  Carry gun with tripod attached & spare parts bag into action 2.  Fires in accordance with orders & repeats all commands of NCO in charge.
