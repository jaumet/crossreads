<p>April, 1916<br/>Tuesday 25<br/>Caught the 4.15 express to Alexandria.  Arrived about 8 pm &amp; put up at the Windsor Hotel<br/>After dinner went  roaming round town till 12 mid.  Turned in very tired<br/>Anzac Day</p>
<p>Wednesday 26<br/>had a lovely sail on the Harbor &amp; a swim off the breakwater.<br/>in afternoon went to see the Catacombes &amp; Pompey's Pillar<br/>Both are splendid pieces of work  one on top of a hill &amp; the other inside the hill.</p>
