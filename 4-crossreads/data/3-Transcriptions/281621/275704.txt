<p>Sunday 24<br/>Afternoon at Kingston on Thames.</p>
<p>Monday 25<br/>I have come to the conclusion that this alleged hospital is at the lowest ebb as regards the conditions of food &amp; general affairs. The district is filthy &amp; I will be pleased to get out of it.</p>
<p>Wednesday 27<br/>I have asked to be discharged on Friday.</p>
<p>Friday 29<br/>Discharged from Hospital &amp; receive pass for 14 days furlough.<br/>"Maid of the Mountains" at Dalys. Weather wet &amp; generally miserable but things are OK. It is good to have meals at your own time etc.</p>
<p>Saturday 30<br/>Richmond this afternoon after going to the Bank this morning</p>
