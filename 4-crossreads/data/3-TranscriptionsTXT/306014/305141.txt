Thus have I seen the original of the Gothic design, and also one of the finest monuments of Gothic architectural culture; the former, a country grove; the latter, Salisbury Cathedral. One is strongly tempted to ask 'What is  art ?' or 'what ought art to be?' but the solution of such problems is hardly commensurable with the demands of war &amp; so I must make my little bunk on the floor, get my equipment ready for tomorrows' parade and dream sweetly of a country far away. (I forgot, unlike Joseph &amp; Daniel; I  don't  dream at all!).
