<p>Very few troops are left here.  The heat is intense &amp; we are right in the desert with very little shade.<br/>There is a refreshment booth run by N.Zealand ladies the other side of the railway lines.</p>
<p>Monday 12th June 1916</p>
<p>Today a Captain has arrived to take charge of us.<br/>An orderly room tent has been erected &amp; we seem to be here for some time.<br/>Every day rumours come thro' of intended "movings off", in fact several times a day.</p>
