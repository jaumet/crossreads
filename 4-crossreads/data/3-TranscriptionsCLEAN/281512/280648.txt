  
 Port a distance of about of 55 miles. We had dinner together a Boulogne & I returned to camp where I arrived at 10.30 p.m. 
 7th September I visited the Australian Mining & Boring Corps this morning & lunched with Capt Hunter. I secured from him a mine exploder & Electric Cable. 
 Afterwards to Renescue & made arrangements to take pictures of the 2nd division tomorrow at Routine drill. Thence by way of Cassel, through the beautiful tree groves to Camp. We then went to Hoograaf where are our headquarters. On the way, we passed the engineers of which my dear friend Major Webb is O.C.   Also immense columns of transports & artillery trains drawn by caterpillar
