<p>All the guards on the ship now wear life-belts constantly, but this is merely a precautionary measure in case of accident. Turned in at 8 P.M.</p>
<p>Nov 22<br/>Not feeling too good to-day and the weather is somewhat rough &amp; stormy but appears to be clearing up again,<br/>This afternoon is our 'washing day' and we have to wash all our dirty clothes ourselves &amp; hang them on lines just like the sailors do on the men-of &ndash;war.</p>
<p>We have been putting our watches back every night 21 minutes and so we must be quite a considerable time behind you by now. When we get up at 6</p>
