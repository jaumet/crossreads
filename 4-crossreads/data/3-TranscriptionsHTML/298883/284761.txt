<p>To<br/>Lieut. Hector Brewer<br/>Head Quarters Signaller<br/>54th Battalion<br/>14th Brigade<br/>Australian Imperial Forces</p>
<p>Sent to you with best Love &amp; Wishes</p>
<p>From<br/>Your Loving Sister &amp; Nephew<br/>Flo &amp; Roy Knight<br/>April 25th 1918<br/>This is "Anzac Day" Dear Hec, the Day that will be well remembered in the History of this War.<br/>The Landing of our dear Lads in Galipoli &amp; my Brother.<br/>This Day should be kept as a Sunday &ndash; to my mind.</p>
