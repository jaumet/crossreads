<p>16-2-16<br/>Wed 16th<br/>The signallers have some work before them now; The Military have taken over the telephone service is this part of the world; Head Quarter Sigs are now working a telephone from the house of the Mayor of this village  A Sqd Sigs are to work a phone from Shusha where they are now camped &amp; B Sqd from Ezbet Bughra; These stations are off the main railway line to Khartoum. The Head Quarter Sigs are to leave here &amp; work the phone on the main line at Fashan, Maghagha Beni Mazar &amp; Samalut, &amp; our Brigade H.Q. are at Minia &amp; have communication with the 2nd Regt. further South.  There are two villages here one on either sides of the canal Sandafa on the East &amp; Bahnasa on the West where we are camped, the C.Sqd Sigs are to work the phone from</p>
