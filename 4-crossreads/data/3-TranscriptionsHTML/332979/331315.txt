<p>where I <strike>was met on by</strike> had the pleasure of seeing my Sister again who lives there, after some 3 years. On arriving at her home on the Esplanade I soon made myself comfortable &amp; spent the rest of the Afternoon <strike>Chatting</strike> &amp; evening Chatting over old times. As the Weather next day was very bad in the Morning I did not venture out till the Afternoon where I had my  Photo taken by myself &amp; also with <strike>myself</strike> my Sister &amp; after having a Walk around the Town &amp; seeing the sights which I very much enjoyed. Bognor to my mind is a very pretty place &amp; during the Summer Season is largely patronised the Beach &amp; Pier &amp; Esplanade is beautifully laid out.
