
<p>970</p>
<p>SUNDAY, JANUARY 19, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, but cold and foggy. Did</p>
<p>not get up till dinner time.</p>
<p>Went round with Alan &amp; the</p>
<p>mob &amp; had dinner at the Pic</p>
<p>after dinner we went to see</p>
<p>Dot Brunton and then</p>
<p>back to the flat, &amp; sat by the</p>
<p>fire.</p>
<p>Poss went up to Scotland at</p>
<p>night.</p>
