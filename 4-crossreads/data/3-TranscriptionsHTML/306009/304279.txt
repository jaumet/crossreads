<p>1915<br/>Apr. 3 Sat<br/>Great bustle &amp; activity &ndash; The remaining two Brigades of the 1st Division are to leave Mena for unknown destination about 12 or 13000 men &ndash; First Troops to march out were 500 of the 4th Infantry who left about 4 p.m. to-day &ndash;<br/>Troops marching out all through the night.<br/>Record day in Hospital. 115 patients admitted &amp; 205 discharged leaving us with 429.<br/>No word concerning move of No. 2. G.H. has been received yet.<br/>Said good-bye to S.M. Doyle (2nd Inf.) &amp; S.M. Carpenter (2nd F. Amb.)</p>
