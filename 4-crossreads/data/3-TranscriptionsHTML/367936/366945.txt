<p>Thursday 9 March 1916<br/>Dick &amp; Geoff finished Front pdk.<br/>Father, Mother &amp; I mustered sheep from Long pdk. down here.  Draughted them in afternoon and took 8 &amp; Leis ewes to Wonga.</p>
<p>Friday 10 March 1916<br/>Dick &amp; I plowed up front road.<br/>Father &amp; Geoff riding round.  Dick started discing 40 acres.<br/>Mother &amp; Father drove over to Barham.<br/>Geoff &amp; I mustered Front pdk.</p>
