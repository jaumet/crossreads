Pretty heavy gunfire tonight our heavies and howitzers are engaged.
The weather is very miserable, dull gloomy skies with a few scattered showers during the day and at night it rains fairly constant but thank goodness we have a dry bed and a good fire when the days work is finished. I pity the poor devils in the firing line where we will be shortly I expect.
16th Out on the road job again this morning and it was real rotten for the sleepers were covered with mud and the men naturally did not like handling them, they were dodging away at every opportunity and it was over this that I had a few words with one of D Coy's officers.
