<p>Thursday<br/>still on water</p>
<p>Dear Ted</p>
<p>Just a line to let you know I am alright hoping this will find you Grace   &amp;  Mavis still the same we stayed in the harbour until sunday evening I have had a fair trip I have a bad cold so there is nothing to tell I will say good bye for the present with best wishes from your loving brother Charlie</p>
<p>Address: C Bathersby<br/>A. Squadron<br/>B. 12 A.4.House<br/>C. no 94<br/>D. C/O Victoria Barracks</p>
<p>[Written on side of letter at bottom 'they will send it along']</p>
