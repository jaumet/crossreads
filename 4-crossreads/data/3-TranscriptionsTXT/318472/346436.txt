 30 July 1918 Tuesday Making a good trip but nothing very interesting happening No war news through So do not know how things are going. Still on Submarine lookout on Bridge. 
 1918 July 31 Wednesday Nothing very exciting Sighted a convoy of four boats bound Northward from Sierra Leone. Sighted a few whales &ndash; porpoise Weather unpleasantly warm.
