<p>Christmas card  dated 8.12.18<br/>GREETINGS AND BEST WISHES FROM<br/>Handwritten:-.  Clyde,  Beaumeuse, Belgian Border</p>
<p>Text:-  CHRISTMAS 1918<br/>We greet you friend, where'er your distant home,<br/>In Britain, France, 'neath Austral's sunny dome;<br/>Now there is peace, we'll see you all again-<br/>For soon we hope to leave this cold Bohain,<br/>To safely dodge the Hun slow-action mine<br/>And spend our last war Christmas on the Rhine</p>
