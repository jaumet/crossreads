<p>10th Jan 1916</p>
<p>[Note: Date should be 1917]<br/>My Dear Mother<br/>Here I am again right back at the canal in a Tommy hospital under observation for diphtheria which I dont beleive a bit I've got.<br/>It really is rotten luck as the day I left I think the regt went out on a sort of raid again and there was likely something doing I think.</p>
<p>12th Jan.<br/>The day I began this they took a swab of my throat to test</p>
