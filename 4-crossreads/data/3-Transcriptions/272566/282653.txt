<p>Tuesday 25 September</p>
<p>Twice to 5th Divisional Hd. Q. (A.D.M.S.) at Dickebush<br/>To 1st Anzac Corps Head Quarters<br/>Dickebush is not a choice place for motor cycles &ndash; At one spot I have to plough through mud &amp; water about a foot deep.</p>
