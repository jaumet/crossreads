
July 1918Tuesday 30
Ribemont. Bty PositionAt 12.30 am I was awaiting to hear S.O.S. but it did not come off. Weather all day muggy and over warm.Fritz commenced to make things very lively indeed. O.P. wire went in twelve places and Smithy  and I mended it. A deuced amount of gas prevalent.During afternoon line again drifted in three places. Smithy could not come with me unfortunately so I went by myself. Clive spent day at O.P. B.Malcolm and Walker came up from W.L.
