<p>Oct. 26 Tuesday 1915<br/>went up to P. M. Bag secured &amp; the net closing in. After work went out to Mustapha with Rev Foley &amp; Dick Playdell for Kit Bag. Met Mr Lamb (Scotchman) from Alexandra Light House. Invited me to got out there on Thursday to see him.</p>
<p>Oct. 27 Wednesday 1915<br/>on guard today from 12. 30 PM to 12. 30 PM tomorrow.<br/>Working all day. Pay 7/-</p>
<p>S. J. Watkins<br/>333<br/>!st Batt.<br/>94 Exelsior St<br/>Leichardt<br/>Sydney</p>
<p>Harr<br/>Milford told DP &amp; I that Grice took away so many things that he asked him if he intended to start a shop</p>
<p>Oct. 28 Thursday 1915<br/>Working all day commenced at 8. 30AM<br/>Grice taken again to Mustapha by Police. Went &amp; interviewed staff of Police tonight &ndash; arranged to see Head man tomorrow &ndash; 9 AM at Mustapha. working back tonight. W. O placed me in charge</p>
<p>Oct. 29 Friday 1915<br/>Working at 6 AM<br/>went out at 8. 30 AM to Mustapha made a statement which was put down in writing<br/>Arrangements made to see Pethard, Pladell &amp; Stewart by the staff tonight. Grice pretending to be mad &amp; created quite a sensation  tonight.</p>
