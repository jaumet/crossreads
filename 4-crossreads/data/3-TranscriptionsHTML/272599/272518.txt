<p>1917 Tuesday 11 Sept.</p>
<p>Nothing special.<br/>Stomy of Hun block house.  12 ft. walls thick. captured by some Tommies.   6 ft water in it.   Dead Huns afloat.   built staging one end.  Stayed in there a whole platoon for 3 days &amp; nights. French fire &amp; patient no wonder.</p>
