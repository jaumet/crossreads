We are in another German dugout much the same as the last. It is rather quiet. It was at this position that we lost Logan our OC, who was the sig sgt. before he got his commission. Fritz is evacuating a lot of his trenches, falling back I believe.<br/>11 Pm 16. 1. 6. This evening two of our sigs Wolrige &amp; Roberts went out to an advanced position to keep up communications.
