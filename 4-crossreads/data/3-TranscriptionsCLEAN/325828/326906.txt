I thought he was looking well & Ol also was looking better than when I last saw her.  My trip was lengthened out to 4 days & I saw Ol every day.  One day we went to the Zoological Gardens & had a good time: another time we went to the Citadel Cairo, & did some exploring there After I got back to Serapeum we were kept very busy packing up & then on the Monday 29th March we started shifting.  We trained the horses & ourselves to Alexandria & at dinner-time on Tuesday went on board the "Knight Templar" (Transport Z 49)   It is a big job shifting horses (we left the waggons behind) & we lost one horse whilst loading.  This brute started to kick when about half way up the gangway with the result that he booted part of the side out & fell over.  He fell between the boat & the wharf & after struggling for 1/2 an hour we were unable to get him out & he was drowned.  We had a good trip across the Mediterranean.
