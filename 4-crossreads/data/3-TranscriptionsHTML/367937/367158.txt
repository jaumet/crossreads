<p>I suppose by the time you get this it will be mid-winter and the boys will be home and Father down at the sheep sales.<br/>Don't suppose another letter will reach you in time for your birthday so Mother dear I must wish you many happy returns and hope that the next one we will all be at home and have a really big bon fire.<br/>I wonder will Aunt Katie be up with you for the holidays?<br/>I wont be able to write to the boys this time.  Did you get my telegram and letters from Fremantle?<br/>As soon as I can I will send you a cable when we get to Egypt but don't suppose I will be able to till we get into camp.<br/>Will you send me the dates of the birthdays of some of the girls; they would think it awfully sweet of me to remember their birthdays right over here, but I have really forgotten them all now; only you must not tell them of course.</p>
