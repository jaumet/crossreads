<p>Wednesday 6 August<br/>The journey through the night was accomplished quickly, but it seems to me a terribly long way round. Since Jock took command (it may be merely coincidence) we have been subject to better treatment on the march or move from place It may be that Monash, who replaced Birdwood in command of the Aust. Corps. recently, is responsible for this. He is renowned as a advocate of the minimum amount of marching for troops; &amp; he put it into practice with the 3rd Division,</p>
