
Novembre 14Went up to town in afternoon. Went to Australia House to draw money & met Cpl Gough there. Had a knock round with him - made some purchases & got back to Leytonstone at about 7 p.m.
Novembre 15Went to town about noon & in the afternoon Mrs Smith & I went to the Lyceum & saw "Seven Days Leave". It was very good though perhaps not quite up to the usual London standard. Met Mr Smith on the way home & got back about 6.30 p.m.
