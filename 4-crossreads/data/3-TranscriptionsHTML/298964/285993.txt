<p>11 Sun March<br/>Bivvie down during the night blowing like Hell  Covered with sand when I woke</p>
<p>12 Mon March<br/>Bivvie down again pouring rain wet to the bone buggar of a day blowing awful  Taubes over  Son back from Cairo started to train B</p>
<p>13 Tues March<br/> Had Baldy out 4 a.m  working otherwise very quiet  Taubes over in morning</p>
<p>14 Wed March<br/>Baldy out with Boxer B going well  Taubes over  Australian mail arrived  Rum Issue</p>
