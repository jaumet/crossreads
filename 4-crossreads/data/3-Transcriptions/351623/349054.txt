<p>Liverpool.</p>
<p>8 Jan 19<br/>Arrived Liverpool &amp; embarked on Orsova Orient Line. There are about 1300 soldiers, &amp; some soldiers wives &amp; passengers besides about 80 of us sailors. Had a few hours leave in the Pool before we left. Shifted out from wharf in afternoon &amp; anchored out in stream. A Tommy band played us out.</p>
<p>9 Jan 19<br/>Heaved anchor &amp; proceeded to sea we are going by Suez Canal route.</p>
<p>10 Jan 19<br/>There is a bit of a roll on as we are crossing the Bay of Biscay &amp; good many of the soldiers are sick.</p>
<p>11 Jan 19 )<br/>12 Jan 19 ) At sea. Proceeding to Gibraltar</p>
