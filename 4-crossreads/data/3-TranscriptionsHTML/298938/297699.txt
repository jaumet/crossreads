<p>I could not have wished for a pleasanter reunion of all my old associates  -- except School, -- they were on holiday.<br/>London is not much changed &amp; everybody was much as before, except the girls who of course are growing up.<br/>It was strange to have the streets dark after about 8 &amp; to see so many searchlights about.<br/>There was a raid somewhere about Woolwich the night I reached London, or the night before --- I am not certain which.</p>
