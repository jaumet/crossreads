<p>Nov. 18th Thursday<br/>Weather again simply perfect with slightest breeze drying up things wonderfully.  Made some improvements to dug-out against rain.  6-30 p.m. to 10-30 p.m. on Ammunition Fatigue to Beach &amp; back with bomb mule transport.  Shelled.</p>
<p>Nov. 19th Friday<br/>Perfect day.  Writing morning.  Gass apparatus inspected.  Writing evening.</p>
<p>Nov. 20th Saturday<br/>Morning reading &amp; writing, afternoon issuing underwear for Reserve.  Ammunition Fatigue 6-30 to 9-30 p.m.</p>
<p>Nov. 21 Sunday<br/>Eileen Pcl.<br/>Weather very dull and bitterly cold with wind.</p>
