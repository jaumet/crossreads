Friday Oct 5th
Dartford : cold day : up at 8 am : helped to clean up : went to Dartford with Monty in afternoon for a stroll : thigh still stiff after walking any distance at all : cold night 
Saturday Oct 6th
Dartford : cold day : changed the wards linen in morn : wrote in afternoon : splendid "comedy play" in theatre after tea entitled "Jane". I have seldom enjoyed such a laugh before the acting was splendid
Sunday Oct 7th
Dartford : wet cold day : read most of the time 
Monday Oct 8th
Dartford : inspected by doctor : my scabies are pretty right so I got marked out to B 29 ward so as to be able to stroll round get used
