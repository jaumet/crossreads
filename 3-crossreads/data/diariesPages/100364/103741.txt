13th 
Left Rafa at 10 am. and arrived at Medjdel 4 pm. (Lunched with 4th Brigade at Bela). Made arrangements and arranged i programme with Major Williams for taking a series of photographic of the 67th Flying Squadron from the ground and air. Looked over the aerodrome which is the most complete I have visited. Every branch of craft essential to a Flying Squadron has its separate shop. I anticipate the results will be most spectacular I am likely to take here, and will well repay all the time and trouble I can believe.
