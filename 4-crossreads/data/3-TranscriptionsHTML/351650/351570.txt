<p>There are 1400 troops aboard going Egypt herded in like penned cattle, or tinned sardines &ndash; what ho when they feel ill.</p>
<p>I knew a lot of people on board.  Geo. Stewart &amp; family who sold us B'bogie, Tony Hordern, a western district Robertson, Mrs. Carse who was a Boonoke Falkiner and many others.</p>
<p>Tonight we go ashore for a square meal in Perth which is 20 minutes run up from Freemantle [Fremantle].  The steamer cooking and food is very poor stuff after what we Ellis' are accustomed to.</p>
