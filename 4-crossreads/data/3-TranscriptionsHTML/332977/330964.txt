<p>and Brand have to wait 3 weeks before attacking Turks, everyone sorry shoot on White flag Lizzie gets a shell into her bows, warships awaiting munitions</p>
<p>Friday 26 March<br/>Eleven miles route march ashore more sore feet only a rumour</p>
<p>Sunday 28<br/>Ceremonial guard man who built Forts in Dardanelles comes aboard turns out to be a spy, shot</p>
<p>29th<br/>A. B. and D. Company march to capital to hoist British flag. 18 men buried off Inflexible, drowned in bows through being hit by shell, Inflexible in Lemnos</p>
<p>30th<br/>we do a little foraging caught stealing sugar got some jam and cheese had a lovely bust up</p>
<p>31st<br/>On prison guard we receive our first mail for 5 weeks great rejoicing</p>
<p>April 1st<br/>Fools day received 13 letters &amp; papers everywhere<br/>2nd<br/>Company training ashore<br/>3rd<br/>Church Parade Officers Mess, to get out of rain</p>
<p>5th<br/>Received news to land on Friday, great rejoicing getting full equipment very rough<br/>6th</p>
