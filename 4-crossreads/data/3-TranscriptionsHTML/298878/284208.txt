After driving about for two hours, we were just making for the passenger jetty to return to the ship when we espied our Vet. plodding along by himself; we left our carriage and caught up with him, and all adjoined to the nearest place for refreshments.  Most of these refreshment rooms do not have much soft drink traffic, apparently, because it is a trouble to get even a lemon squash sometimes.
