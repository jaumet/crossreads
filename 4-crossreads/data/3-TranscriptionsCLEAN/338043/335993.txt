
Oct. 16thRehearsal & Show. Good house. Splendid reception. Commence the preparation of the "Monkey's Paw".
Oct. 17thExcellent War News. - Important towns recaptured. Brilliant achievements of General Foch - Rehearsal Shows and study.
Oct 18thChange of programme. - Many Australians in town - In taking Lille & Douai General Birdwood has his wish of "1916" gratified.
