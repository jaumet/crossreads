 2. Battle started. It was the 1st Brigade's turn &amp; we had some exciting work. I managed to survive the days of fighting that followed. We had a lot of bomb fighting at which most of our men were new but in time we were able to return the Turk more than he gave. While I was over there the 17th btn landed &amp; I made enquiries about Caesar. 
 3. I was unable to see him as the 17th were a good way up from the right of Lone Pine but I heard that he was quite well &amp; doing good work.
