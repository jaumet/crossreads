  
 March 1918 Saturday 9 
 Messines Spent the morning from 4 to 6 am on watch and from that hour until noon in bed. Fritz shelled O.P. and front line fairly heavily all day. Smith nearly sniped with a 4.2 H.V. whilst enroute to the line. Paid a visit to Pip's crowd and had a fairly decent time. 9.45 pm Fritz doing a fair amount of shelling on dump. 11 pm.Clocks advanced 1 hour. Fritz still having a shoot. And is expected to come over in lump tonight.
