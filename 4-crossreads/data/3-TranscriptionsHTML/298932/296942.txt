<p>"CLAIRMARAIS"<br/>Monday 13<br/>We will be here for another winter, but it cant be any worse than the last one so we will battle through it<br/>Tuesday 14<br/>Just as soon as I get some money I'm off to England Having dinner to night on this selection with the cooky &amp; his family.<br/>Wednesday 15<br/>Pay day. Dinner last night was fine dainty dishes but what they were composed of I know not but if twas frogs I'll have more<br/>Thursday 16<br/>Rabbit lovely rabbit for tea real Australian rabbit, sure it was great. A lovely meal</p>
