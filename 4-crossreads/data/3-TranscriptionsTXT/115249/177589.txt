 W.T.Signal 
 Receiving Ship: Berrima 
 Transmitting Ship: Sydney 
 Date: 14 Sep. 
 Time of Despatch or Receipt: 7.55am 
 &nbsp; 
 Captain to Brigadier 
 A large number of native canoes were crossing from Matupi island to main land this morning as I went out. They may be removing stores.
