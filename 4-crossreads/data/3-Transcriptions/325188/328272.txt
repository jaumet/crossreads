<p>21st March<br/>We are camped in a Russian Monastery tonight &ndash; I move into  Franciscan Convent about 4 miles away &amp; take over Brigade details for a week &ndash; We hope after this stint to get back to __ warmer there &ndash; Boots only last a short time on these rocks</p>
