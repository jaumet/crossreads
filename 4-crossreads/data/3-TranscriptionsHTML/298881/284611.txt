<p>Two of the signallers whom I took with me also had a flight and they enjoyed it immensely. The officers were extremely courteous to us and told us anything which we wanted to know <strike>and</strike> in that refined and precise manner which only an English Gentleman knows how to use correctly. I met two Australians belonging to that squadron one of whom was a qualified pilot and an M.C.</p>
<p>Today's war news is very good all round. The Russians have started again and taken 7000 prisoners, 38 guns and their cavalry have pushed through.</p>
