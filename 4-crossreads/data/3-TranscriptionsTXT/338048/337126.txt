
AugustI was hopping on & off my horse hanging on my one leg and getting a pretty hard time in general.
Got back at 1-30 and had dinner changed uniform and attended sports held by "A" Coy. Australians were allowed entry and they we did very well. As a team we won the Boat Race, Tug of war & Relay Race and generally showed our prowress.
Received another letter but shortly I suppose I will have received all outstanding letters.
Sunday 13thChurch parade to Wilstead again Remainder of morning & afternoon writing Walk to village in evening.
Monday 14thOut with wagon as No. 3. We went in direction of Houghton Conquest and had a pretty good day. Evening spent in writing greatly interrupted by chatting. Had a bath first since being here. Rained pretty heavily for short time
Tuesday 15thRepairing cable & practise of cable numbers in the grounds. Rained again. To a village in evening.
Wednesday 16thOut with cable wagon as No. 5. Went in direction of Southill a distance of about 5 3/4 miles. The country out this way is very pretty. In evening went for a bike ride around the district.
Thursday 17thRaining. Lecture on phones - Everybody very sleepy.
