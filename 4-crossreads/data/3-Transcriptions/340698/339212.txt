<p>Dropping more shells into Hazebrouck.</p>
<p>The rumour of the original first Division returning home seems to be confirmed now as we hear arrangements are being made to that effect.</p>
<p>19/8/17<br/>The enemy airoplanes bombed a dump near here last night but did very little damage. They also got on to a labour battalion camp and wounded about 55 men</p>
