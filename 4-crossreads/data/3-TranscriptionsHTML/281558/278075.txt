<p>Friday January 4th</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades &amp; lectures : very hard frost &amp; bitter cold all day : YMCA at night</p>
<p>Saturday Jan 5th</p>
<p>Fovant : route march (in full pack) of about 8 miles : leg very sore after it so I will have to drop long marching : Sing song in YMCA at night :</p>
<p>Sunday Jan 6th</p>
<p>Fovant : went on sick parade &amp; the fool painted my leg with iodine : Holy Comm in morn : rain set in so wrote in our hut after dinner : service in the YMCA after tea &amp; prayers for intercession :</p>
<p>Monday Jan 7th</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades</p>
