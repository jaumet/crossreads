<p>in our rough bivouac but it snowed nearly the whole time. Arch Hobden &amp; Ern Victorsen popped in to see me one night I did get a surprise. Had a great yarn round the fire (an old drum) you can imagine how our tongues would wag. Saw some great air fights Fritz is pretty game in the air.<br/>We left Bapaume yesterday 14th April 1917 &amp; arrived here (Albert) same day &amp; occupied the same billet we had before moving up. Our Division lost pretty heavily &amp; are now in back area reinforcing.<br/>25-4-17 Anzac Day Am going to an Orgie at the 4th Field Amblce to-night on invitation from Joe Hainwright Q.M.S. Was up</p>
