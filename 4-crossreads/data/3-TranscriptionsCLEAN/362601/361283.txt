  
 17 October, Friday 
 We are fearfully busy & take 28. Meet E & E on boat. I am Hooker in meet W.H.A. 
 18 October, Saturday 
 Go to D Why. Fix door & layout the ashes. I go for a swim alone & then for a stroll to then rocks. We have dinner at Guys & Tea on the beach. Two talking women drive me nearly mad, come Home at 7 pm & write & play piano. 
 Wrote Kev, Pine & H Hey & E & K Naple.
