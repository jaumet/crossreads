  
 May 1918 Saturday 11 
 Bussy-la-Daours Paid a visit to Eglise with bob. During afternoon acted as a guide to an American Sergeant and rode across to Glisy with him. Weather wet. On arrival home found all the boys including Kenn Gunn, and Albie Garford, the former with my mail. One parcel from Eva one from Roy and one from Toots. Also a few letters.
