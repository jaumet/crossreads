
Monday 17 March 1919Very dull all day today. Hotel De Ville picquet tonight. Went for a stroll & did the usual work. Things very quiet tonight. Bus called early so we were home in camp at 10 pm.
Tuesday 18 March 1919Special patrol today. Bill, George & I went to Avelings & stayed there till midnight dancing & singing. Walked home via Rouelles & Les Acacias. Got home at 1.45. Good going. Do me. Have a bonny cold.
