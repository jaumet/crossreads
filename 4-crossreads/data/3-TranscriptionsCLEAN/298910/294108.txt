 28/2/17  Wednesday 
 Dull & foggy - a little bit sharp. Good news coming in from all along the line, still nibbling at Fritz & advancing - with remarkably few casualtys. Got rumours of the fall of Rut. 
 All the Boys are singing out at not receiving mail for quite a long time. 29th Batt made a dashing raid last night - our Artillery put up a beautiful barrage for them - the results we have not yet got. 
 March 1st / 17  Thursday 
 Very nice mild day - The sun tried hard to make itself felt. Got news that our recent gains extend eleven mile in length, to some two miles in depth. Saw a mob of some 60 prisoners going thro - Very heavy Artillery firing - Very few casualtys. A Taub was brought down in our sector, after setting fire to one of our observation Balloons.
