But they got caught in a barrage &ndash; mostly gass &ndash; about half way out &amp; had to spend some hours in a dugout then came home without doing anything 2 or 3 got gassed slightly &amp; have been evacuated. A few shell came about the camp but no damage was done. Have done nothing all day 
 Friday 22nd Went out at 6 a m &amp; nearly finished the job. Quite a hot day. Put some sand bags &amp; dirt on our humpie to make it more secure from shrapnel &amp; shell splinters- not sea shells &ndash; 
 Saturday 23rd Three of us went out to put the finishing touches on the Machine Gun post on Hill 60 mostely riveting a trench to the door way 
 Saturday 24 Same as yesterday only the job is completely finished this time. The rest of Platoon started on a new job yesterday.
