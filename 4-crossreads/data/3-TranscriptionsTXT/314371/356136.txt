
Mon. & Tues. 4 & 5 JuneSame job.
Wednesday 6th JuneWarned for Base.
Thurs. 7th JuneMarched out & went by train to Dannes, Camiers.
Friday 8th JuneExamined by M.O. (3 days L.D.).
Mon. 11th JuneMet two Sgts. from our Coy. down on course of Inst.  Had quite a celebration.  (The Coy. suffered terribly at Bullecourt.)
Thurs. 14th JuneWarned again for line draft.
From June 15thReturned to unit by way of Achiet-le-Grand, Albert & Becourt, found 'em at Warloy-Baillon.Had a good time there practising for a competition (which, by the way, never came off).  Aug. 17, J.C.L.Went from Warloy to Mailley [Mailly]
