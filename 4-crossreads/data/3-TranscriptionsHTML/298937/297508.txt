<p>[This is the diary of Oliver L.S. Holt from 1st January to 23rd August 1916.  It begins at the Prisoner of War Camp at Maadi, Egypt. On 2nd August he left Alexandria by boat and disembarked in Marseille, thence by train through France to Le Havre, then across the English Channel to Southampton.  He travelled by train to Amesbury and then to Parkhouse Camp.<br/>Includes a description of landing at Anzac by 3rd Field Ambulance (page 170 to end)]</p>
<p>O.L.S. Holt<br/>A.A.M.C.<br/>Prisoners of War Camp, Maadi</p>
<p>Diary &amp; Notebook<br/>No. 2</p>
