<p>About 5 oclock the Town Major came down to our camp &amp; gave me some more orders as regards the tents.</p>
<p>There are 30 of us altogether from the 1st Division, &amp; I believe we are on our way to Bristol or Scotland, where we will go to our Training School, how lucky were we to get away for a big scap is coming off.</p>
<p>16th. This morning General Walker inspected us &amp; gave us a lot of advice &amp; told us where we were going to, Etaps the great Australian training Camp is to be our first stop &amp; there we are to have platoons &amp; Company's to drill before</p>
