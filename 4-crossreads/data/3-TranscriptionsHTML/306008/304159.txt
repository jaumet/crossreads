<p>1914<br/>Dec 20 Sun<br/>Skipper went out of course so that we could pass by Cocos Islands<br/>Wrote letters Communion Service at 7 a.m.<br/>Sighted Cocos Islands at Midday<br/>Church Service in evening</p>
<p>Dec 21 Mon<br/>Lantern Lecture by Lieut Col. Nash in evening<br/>Ordinary lecture at 4 pm</p>
<p>Dec 22 Tue<br/>Concert in evening<br/>Lecture at 4 p.m.<br/>Wrote several letters</p>
<p>Dec 23 Wed<br/>Sports meeting in afternoon<br/>Kit inspection in morning<br/>Mess orderly for day</p>
