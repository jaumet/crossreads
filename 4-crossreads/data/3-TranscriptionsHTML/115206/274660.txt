<p>March 1918<br/>Sunday 31</p>
<p>On the Road &ndash; Bycourt.<br/>2am detrained at 2.30 am. and proceeded to our billets Rained very hard all the while.<br/>The complete outfit whilst en route to billets at Orville, was drenched with rain.<br/>We arrived at Orville at .15 am and after watering and feeding up, retired to bed for a little spell before moving up to the line. <br/>We are to "standby" to move up any hour.<br/>Word came through that the 4th Divvie had smacked Fritz back four miles &ndash; heavy casualties.</p>
