 23 Sunday  [Sept] Up to Town &ndash; Lunched at Highgate. Stayed at the   [indecipherable] Hotel 
 24 Monday To HQ  &amp; altered allotment with Rup. Dorothy came to tea also saw Gladys [indecipherable]  Arlette in eveng. Air raid. 
 25 Tuesday For a walk in the Park in ming.  to the "at Home" in aftern. at H'gte.   anor Air Raid but not much damage done. 
 26 Wednesday down to Folkestone at 11.0.c. tried to get to Dover to see Katie but we were taken for spies!!! Listened to the band in eveng. 
 27 Thursday Brekker in bed!! Topping day. Walked along the Leas  &amp; listened to band saw airship &amp; plane. Katie came to tea 
 28 Friday caught the 3.30 pm boat to France &amp; the 12.54 train up to Pop. [Two lines deleted] 
 29 Saturday Got off at Caestre &amp; joined the Details camp  &amp; had a good sleep.  Heard of the rotten casualties in the   [indecipherable] 30 Sunday Went over &amp; saw Morrisset &amp;  some of the 8th   fellows. Rumours of going up tomorrow 
 1 Oct. Monday Went for a walk &ndash; a route march  &amp; we did not move up. 
 2 Tuesday Paraded early &amp; marched up &amp; joined the Bn.  My first 2 lres from my wife. 
 3 Wednesday Am appointed Sig off again &amp; endeavd to straighten things out. 
 4 Thursday Bathing parade in morning  &amp; was turned out damned early.  Ripping little note from Rup. 
 5 Friday Got busy with tho Sig's.  Courtney came back from the T.B.   [indecipherable] &amp; I had tea in village at Reninghelst 
 6 Saturday Raining all blessed day. Two topping lres from Rup &amp; the proofs &ndash; Return same
