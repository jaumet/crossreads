<p>August,1918<br/>Saturday 3<br/>Ribemont and Lamotte.<br/>Weather wet and miserable. Journeyed over the lines with the fellows taking over, and mended two lines that were down.<br/>Packed up kit and proceeded to W.L. arriving there at 4 am. Had a fairly long walk up to Lamotte.<br/>Battery position at the new place visited by Ammo wagons. Thompson lost his best horse and there were seven horses killed one man wounded Taylor. Weather still wet.</p>
