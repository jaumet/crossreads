<p>59<br/>day of it. It would be about 4/30 when we finished for the day. Route marches proved a good part of the training and it was very hard work trudging through the sand.</p>
<p>The Instructional staff at the Bullring comprise officers and men who had seen service up the line and were out for a spell to recuperate although there were some who had specially been trained and picked for exceptional ability. We did a lot of skirmishing in extended order &ndash; bayonet fighting &ndash; our opponents being bags and then what I would term a practice turn of trench warfare, complying to the conditions we would have to undergo in France.</p>
