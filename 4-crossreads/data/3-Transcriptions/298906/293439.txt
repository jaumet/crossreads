<p>Got a letter from home today dated on Xmas Day, so that has been delayed somewhat. Fairly hot today, the country wants rain badly.<br/>16th. Night operation's last night &amp; with packs up officers &amp; all we were the defending force &amp; wore white bands round our caps.
