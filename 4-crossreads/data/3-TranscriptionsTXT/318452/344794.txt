Monday Nov 19We left Blequin this morning at 8 oclock our trip was about 18 miles I came on the Motor Lorry through very pretty country & some nice towns & villages. We arrived at our destination "Frencq" at about 11.30We are billeted in a fine old Chateau 160 years old The grounds and woods are well kept. In summer it must be delightful
