<p>4.<br/>Australians are in favour of conscription but cant bring themselves to vote for it because they don't like to send other people's sons having none of their own to send &amp; similar reasons, but would like the Government to have taken the bold course &amp; introduced it through Parliament &amp; not by referendum.</p>
<p>I fear this is not much like an ordinary letter but for this forgive me as I have little to say of myself &amp; my doings.</p>
<p>Goodbye, kindest regards to you all,</p>
<p>Yours very sincerely<br/>A [indecipherable]</p>
