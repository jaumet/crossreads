 I was hit in the arm  &amp; ribs, leg &amp; ankle. The one on the leg was the worst as it has left a lovely bruise. The ankle one cut right through my boot so I got a new pair of boots out of it. We had a great dinner &amp; sports last week. Also one of the bands in attendance. The dinner consisted of soup, fish rissoles, roast beef, ham, peas, beans, mashed &amp; baked potatoes, plum pudding &amp; sauce, canned fruit, custard, jellies, strawberries, cake, biscuits, lollies, chocolate &amp; cigarettes with rum punch, beer &amp; coffee to drink.
