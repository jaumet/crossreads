 29 August 1918 Thursday Nothing of great importance to note. A Little Boxing in the afternoon to keep the boys fit. Nice fine weather &amp; a beautiful sea. Good news continues to come from Western Front N. Zealanders enter northern outskirts of Bapaume. French &amp; Americans doing well. 
 1918 August 30 Friday A little war news through per wireless via Mauritius. Still progressing well on all Fronts. Nothing startling on Board still getting nice fine weather and making good progress &ndash; about 250 miles per day.
