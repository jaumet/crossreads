Got my legs arms burnt dreadfully yesterday, tonight they are giving me something to think about I rubbed some "Hazeline Snow" into them hoping to steady the pain but so far little impression has been made
31st. Have been very busy all the afternoon, took my tunic which I had washed a few days ago up on top sat in a nice breezy place sewed all sorts of things on.
There was a debate this afternoon on Prohibition or otherwise. I never heard it but I believe it was very funny so were a lot of the argument they brought forth.
