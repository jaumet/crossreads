 Sunday 7 September 1919 Much milder again. Read a lot &amp; played two or three games. Colonel told O.C. companies today that there must not be any noise on deck till after 5 pm. that the 'subs must keep away from the fire &amp; that cigarettes &amp; matches must not thrown on the floor. 
 Monday 8 September 1919 Not so windy today. When playing with a life belt we threw it overboard. It is only about our fifth. Hurt my finger.
