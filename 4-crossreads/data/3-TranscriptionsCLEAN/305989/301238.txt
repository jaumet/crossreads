  
 Sunday 7 January 1917 Quiet day:  2 taubes dropping 10 bombs among the E.L.C. doing a lot of damage had a go at us but missed.  Had a night air raid:  no damage.  Out on outpost. 
 Monday 8 January 1917 On "pumping fatigue" started on a divisional stunt to Rafa:  camped at Shiek Zowaiid till 12.30 & on to Rafa. 
 Tuesday 9 January 1917 Arrived at Magruntein at sunrise & then Rafa just after, crossed the Syrian border & charged into our position carrying ammunition all day:  bad country.  Turks chucked it in at sunset.  Went back to Shiek Zowaiid. 
 Wednesday 10 January 1917 Arrived at Zowaiid about 1.30, camped till 5.30 & proceeded back to El Arish. 
 Thursday 11 January 1917 Had swimming parade & went down to the Field Ambulance to see Tonie.  Mr. Harris & Major Irwin all wounded.  Picquet at night. 
 Friday 12 January 1917 Day stableman:  two Taubes over but did not drop any bombs. 
 Saturday 13 January 1917 Muster parade.  Gen. Choueval [Chauvel] gave us a lecture of appreciation for the work at Rafa & gave us an idea of our captures.
