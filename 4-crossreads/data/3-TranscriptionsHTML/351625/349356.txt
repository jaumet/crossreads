<p>I wished you could get some good photos of the place taken &amp; send me, get someone with a camera on the go.  <strike>It is</strike> I think I have just about run dry now so will have to say au revoir. <strike>with the</strike> I may put some more on later.  Give my love &amp; kisses to all &amp; whips to yourself from your loving Son,</p>
<p>Douglas</p>
<p>26 Sept. 18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>I have at last got a chance of posting this.  I am in hospital in Blighty just near Birmingham.  The greatest bit of luck out as I got over with nothing worse than a ceptic finger as they were clearing all the hospitals over in France getting ready for a big stunt later.</p>
<p>Doug</p>
