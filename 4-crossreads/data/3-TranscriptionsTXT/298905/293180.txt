
mile after mile of what they call the Down country, of which Sussex is one of the Counties, & it is splendid I never saw better grassed paddocks in my life, & the stock were all rolling fat, the journey took about 2 hour's reaching Littlehampton about 10.30 that night Mr Duke was there to meet me, arrived at his (9th)  house which is a bonzer, & has lovely gardens & lawns, I was introduced all round, & made to feel at home, had some supper & went to bed, the bed I slept in was of solid oak, & wa is I think of great age, it was the best bed I have slept in since leaving Australia, I never woke no more till about 8 o'clock next morning I then got up had a bath, dress-
