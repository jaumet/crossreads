 1915 Oct. 10 Sun. Spent the afternoon instructing 2 Patrols of Armenian Scouts in first aid for a Display to be given by the Troop &ndash; The language problem presents a little difficulty. Went to the Kursaal (variety show like Tivoli) at 6 p.m. &ndash; Splendid programme. Had good dinner &amp; then train to Gezira.
