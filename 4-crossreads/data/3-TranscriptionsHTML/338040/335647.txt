<p>April 2nd<br/>Sabbath. Mass at 9am. Pleasure walk- Issue of two "respirators for combating gases" - Salmon sauce &ndash; Pears and Lime juice for lunch. Letters written during afternoon.<br/>April 3rd<br/>Sick parade Marching Orders- Preparation of baggage and Examination of kit- Day of Rest Generally.<br/>April 4th<br/>Leave Chateau with 7th Field- Join 7th Brigade- Depart from Morbecque.</p>
