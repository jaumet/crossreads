<p>Thursday 4th<br/>Went on quartermaster's fatigue in the morning.  Loaded wagons of maize &amp; bran.  Had an easy time afterwards.  In the afternoon fixed up our saddlery.  After tea Gordon Edmondson came round &amp; we had a bit of a yarn.  Was on stable picket at night.  It was bitterly cold &amp; the horses gave a lot of trouble.</p>
<p>Friday 5th  [Feb]<br/>Our transport arrived last night.  Clear, fine day.  Cleaned up stirrup irons &amp; bits in the morning.  In the afternoon exercised horses.  Went up to town Maadi at night with McCarthy.  Got back early.</p>
