<p>30th Day out<br/>I missed all the fatigues for the first 3 weeks of the trip but this last week I catch it every time.  This morning I had a terrible job.  A Sergeant &amp; two Corporals are generally put on to do the job I had this morning.  When I say do the job, I mean see that the men that are given to me do the job.
