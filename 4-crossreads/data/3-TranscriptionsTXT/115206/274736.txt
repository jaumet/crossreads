 June 1918 Saturday 15 
 Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles Weather wet, during morning fine and clear, in afternoon.  Had a very enjoyable concert at W.L. a very good tenor, and ebcontionist.  The Star items. O'Kelly the tenor, sang several very sweet songs. Polished up old "Acks" bit and irons "Smithy  still feeling bad. Had a run to Bussy; and saw folks there. Second divvie pioneers and 5th Bdge came out. Met Harry Rich now Lt  Rich, - congratulated him on his progress.  Received 3 letters from Australia  20/4/18
