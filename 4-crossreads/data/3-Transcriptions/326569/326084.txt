<p>Friday, 27/7/17<br/>Fairly warm all day.  Went out from 9 p.m. to 12 midnight on night stunt.  Coffee &amp; biscuits supplied for supper.</p>
<p>Saturday, 28/7/17<br/>On Lewis Gun stoppages all morning.  In afternoon went to Tisbury with Smith, Meldrum &amp; Dawson.  Had tea there &amp; arrived back 9.15 p.m.</p>
<p>Sunday, 29/7/17<br/>Reveille 6 a.m., up 7 a.m.  Went with Palmer to farmhouse &amp; had 2 each of pint mugs of milk, cost 2d. per mug.  Rained in afternoon.  Took laundry to Fovant.  Posted photos to Australia etc.</p>
<p>Monday, 30/7/17<br/>Went skirmishing over the hill all day.  Afternoon &amp; evening rained continuously.  Paid after tea.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 31/7/17<br/>Rained all morning so did not go out on parade.</p>
