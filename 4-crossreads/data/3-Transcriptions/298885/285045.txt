<p>Sun. Jan. 30th -16<br/>Warned twice to be ready to quell native rising.</p>
<p>Mon. Jan. 31st -16<br/>Spent most of day exercising horses with the result I am feeling pretty stiff.</p>
<p>Tues. Feb. 1st -16<br/>Light Horse called out to quieten unruly natives in Cairo.  Several being killed besides a number wounded.</p>
<p>Wed. Feb. 2nd -16<br/>On guard extremely cold at night.  Inspection of kit.</p>
<p>Thur. Feb. 3rd -16<br/>Went into the desert with old howitzers and had gun-drill.</p>
