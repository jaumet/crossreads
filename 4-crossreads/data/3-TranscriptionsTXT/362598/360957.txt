 Sunday 24 December 1916 Lovely warm &amp; balmy Day.  Go to Kirk as usual.  Practise Xmas Hymns.  Receive Red X Gift.  Write to Home, Bess, Ida Wilson, Ida Dean, Dulce.   Bess  
 Monday 25 December 1916 Had 3 good meals.  Unfurled the flag.  Play Hymn O Come all ye Faithful wrong Intro.  Had bonnie day.  Nice weather.  Wrote Aunt Ann, Home, Doris, Esme, Alma, Marg. 
 Tuesday 26 December 1916 D. E. I go to see A Pair of Silk Stockings.  Play very good. 
 Wednesday 27 December 1916 Recd. Toffee &amp; Chocs. from Auntie Annie, very good. 
 Thursday 28 December 1916 I buy an "Onoto" pen.  Wrote to Aunt Annie.  Canteen Corporal again. 
 Friday 29 December 1916 Wrote to M.R.L.  Canteen Corporal again.  Ellis goes on a joy ride to Fargo. 
 Saturday 30 December 1916 Sent Parcel to Roy.  Recd. letter &amp; Photo from Bert C., P.C. &amp; letter from Jennie.  Letters from Mrs. Whitehead, Maisie Carter.  Go to Pictures with Dave.  Meet Bill Drummond again.  Ern Thomas meets C.D. Bell at Lark Hill.  Wrote to H. Hey.  Send Book to M.R.L.
