
3/1/19 Gourdinneand Robertson. Conversation was on demobilization and League of Nations. Upon the later question Clemenceau and Wilson seem to be at variance. The amalgamation of units seems to be the next step when strengths still further fall. General Hobbs was wearing his 1914-5 ribbon and on my return I found a letter from General Birdwood enclosing a piece for me. Rode back at a good pace through the dark and was rather late for mess. Afterwards went to a Euchre tournament which all the officers were taking part in a cafe
