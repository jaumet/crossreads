 7. Sunday. Terrible depression &amp; irritable. Got up 7 went out a little to post a letter. F.P. came &amp; talked to me. Had attack. Rigor temp. head. 
 8. Monday Got up &amp; did things all day. F.P. rang up.  claude came in evening. 
 9. Tuesday. Got up again. Met Mrs Fisher at Victoria at 5. &amp; went home for the night. Had a lovely fire, entirely necessary in this climate. 
 10. Wednesday. Came on to rain in the early morn. Went up to town with Mrs Fisher &amp; got home between showers. Fire again at Fishers.
