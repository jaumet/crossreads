<p>13 September, Saturday</p>
<p>Dinner at Esplanade Caf&eacute;. Go to Sports which are great. Aeroplane &amp; motor stunts. Go to Ellis' with crowd for Tea &amp; meet Billy. Meet Amy Bert &amp; Myra Brown. Go to Pictures see "Woman".</p>
<p>Recd letter from Lodge &amp; Doris.</p>
<p>14 September, Sunday</p>
<p>Go to Dee Why. Fix Linos &amp; clean up outside. An awful windy day &amp; it is a terrible drive Home. Sit &amp; talk after chopping wood &amp; cleaning up etc. Go to bed after a bath.</p>
<p>Wrote Doris.</p>
