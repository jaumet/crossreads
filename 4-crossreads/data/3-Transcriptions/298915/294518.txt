<p>Great  excitement over the loaded biscuit tin &amp; a Bosch auto-Starter.<br/>3rd Sunday.  More fun with the biscuit tin. Arabs willingly give bread for this tin but can't carry it away. Cold and cloudy.<br/>4th Cloudy &amp; cold<br/>5th Change sets with Singo. Move off at 7pm for Tripoli. Camp at midnight and<br/>5/11/18 move off at 6am crossing the crater of an extinct volcano. Wish the big German car would smash &amp; it did Camp in a hell of a place away from water and among boulders.<br/>6/11/18 move again 6 am and camp 2pm on beach near Tripoli.<br/>7/11/18 Move 8am through Tripoli camping 5 miles S &amp; up on the hills</p>
