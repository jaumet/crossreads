 Sunday 9/9/17 Bean here all day for accounts of Lagnicourt &amp; Bullecourt. Monday 10/9/17 Handed over to Currie &amp; proceeded to Ca&euml;stre on job of Reinf Camp. Tuesday 11/9/17 Arrived at Ca&euml;stre with 100 adv. Party of 26th Bn commenced on camp, wrote to Doff &ndash; very sick. Wednesday 12/9/17 2100 all ranks marched in saw General Smythe, trouble with water supply. Thursday 13/9/17 Straightening up Camp generally things now shaping well. Friday 14/9/17 Commenced Schools &ndash; others route marching.
