 Wednesday 3 October 1917 A quoits tournament began, every player plays everyone else.  H.M. scores &amp; refuses to reveal any scores, therefore all interest in the game vanishes. Have now written 18 letters. This boat heavily laden with mutton &amp; great quantity metal.  Cargo worth about 1&frac14; millions not to mention members A.A.N.S. [Australian Army Nursing Service]
