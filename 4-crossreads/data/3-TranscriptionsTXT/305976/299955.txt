We made our way to the Sandhills &amp; arrived absolutely exausted. Thus we spent Boxing day 1917. Thursday Dec 27 Bitterly cold today. Have heavy cold &amp; cannot speak. We straightened up camp. Friday Dec 28 weather clearing up, spelling. Saturday Dec 29 Received some mail, wet but readable. Sunday Dec 30 On water guard Ezdud.
