<p>Melbourne, I have been a Charge-Sister at Caulfield Military Hospital, now Repatriation.<br/>On account of my Mother's bad health, I am obliged to try and obtain a position that will allow me to sleep at home.<br/>Enclosed, please find copies of four testimonials &ndash; Sorry I have not any since my service with the Military but shall forward some as soon as possible.<br/>E.S.Thistlethwaite                                                                                                                                                                                       Sister.                                                                                                                                                                                       Repatriation Hospital.                                                                                                                                                                       Caulfield.</p>
