 February, 1917. Monday 26 7 Parade &amp; reported at 9.30. Nos 3 &amp; 4 section moved to Factory Corner at 9 &amp; other sections to follow when billets ready. Moved off at 10 with Mackenzie Mitchell &amp; Groth per railway line to C.R.E. at Tricourt Farm in easy stages and thence to destination about a mile away arriving at 2 and got some tea and found billets after reporting in Armstrong huts. After tea visited canteen at 2nd F.A at old chateau and went to bed at 9 (Lights out). Slept rather cold and floor hard.
