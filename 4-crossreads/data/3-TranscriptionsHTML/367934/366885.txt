<p><u>opposite side</u>!  The folorn expression of Napoleon on St. Helena has vanished and the slight spinal curvature from the nape of the neck to the base as shown in the sketch has straightened out considerably.  Even the malacca cane has been discarded.  I hope you received Murger's "Vie de Boheme" posted about fourteen days ago, several French papers, two or three letters and "Vive Le Quartier Latin".  Shall write a good long letter as soon as things are a bit settled.  Love to Jack, Jen and all at home.</p>
<p>Toujours a toi<br/>Tom Vasco<br/>[line of kisses]</p>
