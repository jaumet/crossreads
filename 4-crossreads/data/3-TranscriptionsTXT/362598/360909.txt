 Sunday 23 January 1916 Monday 24 January 1916 
 Tuesday 25 January 1916 Went alongside Wharf. 
 Wednesday 26 January 1916 Left Suez at 7 a.m. for Cairo.  Arrived at Abbassia at 4 p.m. 
 Thursday 27 January 1916 Watched Dressing in Morning &amp; started work at 2 p.m. 
 Friday 28 January 1916 Bubs Dressings all day. 
 Saturday 29 January 1916 Went to Cairo for first time.  Met C. Board &amp; McDonnell.  Came home in Chariot with H. O'Keefe &amp; Roy Farnsworth, Geoff Clarke.  A Gallop all the way.
