<p>Australian Branch<br/>B.R.C.S.<br/>Bulford<br/>9/6/18</p>
<p>Dear Dad, Mother &amp; George,</p>
<p>There is little worth writing about this week.  I rode to Salisbury on Tuesday.  It is such a long while since I did so.  We have had the bike tuned up &amp; a new saddle &amp; now she goes like a young horse.  I took me an Hour each way, but it was a very hot day &amp; I walked most of the Hills to save myself knocking up.  I played Cricket on Wednesday against the pick of the Tommy Garrison.  I was only chosen because all the good players were away.  Anyway I fielded</p>
