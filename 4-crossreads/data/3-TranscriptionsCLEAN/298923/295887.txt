
9 August 1918 Friday   Hospital room.Am arranging for Dolly to go on Oct 1st to the same Pension as Cuth Fetherstonhoughs wife,   Spent day at Hospital.  Flying men had a dinner to celebrate arrival of a Squadron which flew all the way from England-  A little dog came with them he had a fight in CRETE & was hopping about Shepherds Hotel on 3 legs next day-Had dinner at Gezireh Sports Club in the open.
