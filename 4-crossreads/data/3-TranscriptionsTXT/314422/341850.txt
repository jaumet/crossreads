
Thursday Jan 24. 1918. 1270.12 day of leaveI wqas up at 7 this morning had an early breakfast and caught the 7.30 train my Un cle seeing me off at the station. I changed trains at Bundorn junction and when I got to Enniskillen stopped and went into the town had a shave called at the Post Office and saw Kitty then I went to where she is staying - Mrs Creswell - and  wrote letters to dinner time. I sent my sister a packet of Enniskillen post cards also sent a card to my wife Mother and Mrs Murphy also Tom Brown. Kitty Gilmore got
