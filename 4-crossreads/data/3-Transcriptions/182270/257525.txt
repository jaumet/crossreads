
<p>FRIDAY, AUGUST 1, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Peter met us in big car, but</p>
<p>had to get another for the luggage.</p>
<p>Got home for breakfast. I was</p>
<p>down bottom end after. Darby</p>
<p>down 110 ft in the blue rock, he</p>
<p>had a soakage at 80 ft of 30 gal an</p>
<p>hour. Ewes in Mountain have&nbsp;</p>
<p>started to die worse luck, &amp;</p>
<p>ewes in Cow paddock to lamb.</p>
