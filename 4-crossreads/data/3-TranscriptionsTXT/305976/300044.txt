 Thursday Sept 19 Left Jerusalem Rest camp for Jericho. Arrived about 11 AM &amp; camped near dump until horses arrived. We had the wind up today. They started shelling. We were camped next to ammunition dump &amp; this is evidently what they were after. Every minute we expected the lot to go up in the air but once again we were lucky.
