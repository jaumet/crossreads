
Dec 1917Thurs 6thLeft this morning at 11 am got clear of the Harbour at 1 pm getting very warm but it is pleasant after the cold.Sun 9thVery warm we are in the West Indies   The sea is dotted with islandsThursday 13thReached the Panama Canal at 5 am pulled up outside by the destroyers   The harbour is closed at night so we couldn't got in till daylight  The town of Colon is at this [end]    The Tahiti Manyanui Willockra came through with the 31 Reinfts.   The canal is a marvellous bit of work the boat is lifted 4 ft above sea level & by [lock] & the lowered down the other side
