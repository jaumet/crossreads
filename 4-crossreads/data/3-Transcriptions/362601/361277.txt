<p>Go to Ottos's for plants. Ida &amp; Nat come down &amp; kids. I take 'em all paddling in creek. Gee they get in a mess. I dig &amp; plant all day.</p>
<p>Wrote Auntie Annie.</p>
<p>6 October, Monday</p>
<p>Dig &amp; plant all morning. We go to Dee Why in afternoon. Meet Geo Lil &amp; Myrtle. I am very quiet. I am better after [indecipherable] &amp; watering my garden. Amuse Dad &amp; Mum until nearly 9 pm.</p>
