<p>June 3rd continued<br/>Mending &amp; letter writing.  Reading novels &amp; B. L &amp; S Officers revolver practice afternoon, reading night.</p>
<p>June 4th  323M.  West<br/>B. Coy on Duty in charge of own section for Phy. D. till 7am. then on with Canteen Fatigue, very hot &amp; on the go all day till 8-30, good music &amp; singing aft.</p>
<p>June 5th  West.  M.305<br/>Usual drill all day.  Outpost in addition.  At reveille we were running along the Socotra Island, very rough &amp; high mountains also passed 2 ships, one 7-30am. other [indecipherable].</p>
