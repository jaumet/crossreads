  
 Jan.12th Receive Birthday Parcel from Home - "Mystery & Imagination" - Again meet Mr. Jack Kirby - Votes granted to the Women of England. 
 Jan.13th My 21st Birthday - Genuine congratulations & Good wishes from my Pals - A special Dinner for the occasion (Soup, chicken, ham, vegetables, fruit salad, ginger etc.) - Letters containing Birthday greetings from Australia arrive right on time - A glorious day - Correspondence
