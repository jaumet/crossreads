<p>Sunday 9 July 1916<br/>Went to Communion before breakfast.<br/>Afternoon picture show in Alexandria with Rostron and then concert on pier at Anfouchy.<br/>Longworth here, gave exabition of swimming.</p>
<p>Monday 10 July 1916<br/>Museam hunting.<br/>Rostron &amp; I took tram out and went past museam, took tram back but could not hit it again so came home.<br/>Concert in Y.M.C.A. given by 36 L. M.T. Coy, A.S.C. [Army Service Corps]  Very good.  Sent Registered photos to boys, Post Cards to Mother.</p>
