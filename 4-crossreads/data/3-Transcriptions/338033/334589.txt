<p>part consisted of route marches<br/>Round the countryside, viewing the beautiful spots, which only a cool climate can produce. I struck me how well all the roads are kept, and there is no end of roads in England. On the 17th we drew up in review order and inspected by Mr Andrew Fisher. Several days later we did a forced march to Bullford, a distance of eight miles, to rehearse a grand review which took place the following day at the same grounds. Mr Hughes accompanied by Mr Fisher took the salute as we marched past.<br/>We had carried our great coats but just before the officials were expected</p>
