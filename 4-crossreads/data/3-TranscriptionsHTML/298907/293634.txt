<p>Last night we had a concert in our Ward some concert believe me I have seen better one's in our platoon billets many a time, it was that rotten that I was real pleased when the show as over.</p>
<p>Had a bit of a lark with the sister this evening when she was taking our temperatures while her back was turned I dipped the thermometer in a basin of hot water which was on my locker this had the effect of driving the temperature up to an alarming height, she got quite scared when she looked at the figures but I could not help myself from laughing &amp; so gave the whole show away.</p>
