<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 13 9 4</p>
<p>Time 6 20 am</p>
<p>From Brigadier - As you have no doubt reported successful occupation of Herbertshohe and Rabaul and the other steps taken to date and furnished list of killed and wounded nothing further is I think required to be sent by wireless except that all are well no sickness whatever. &nbsp;I will send despatch by mail today.</p>
