<p>My address is still<br/>1ST Training Regt ALH<br/>Interm. Base Depot<br/>Eygpt<br/>I cant help thinking of all our chaps in camp with me here with pink pyjama pants on too.<br/>I have never had such a swell trip in a train as I had this morning in the Red Cresent Hospital train; it is beautifully easy, you have a bed to lie on and an orderly brings you any cool drinks you like to order.<br/>I am looking forward to getting Father's letters with the news of home.<br/>Love to Father<br/>Your loving son<br/>Rob.</p>
