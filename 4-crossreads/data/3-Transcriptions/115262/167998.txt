<p>Sunday 19 August 1917.</p>
<p>Gave up trying to sleep at 4 a.m. Had a little hard rations &amp; water. Are now passing over captured ground &amp; through stations built by Allies on occupatn.</p>
<p>Every mile or so there is a redoubt on a sandridge and surrounded by barb &amp; springwire entanglements. Soldiers graves often seen here &amp;&nbsp;there near Stations.</p>
<p>Arrived 7.40 a.m. at No 4 Staty Hospl<span style="font-size: 10.8333330154419px; line-height: 19.2592582702637px;">&nbsp;</span>on shores of the Mediterranean Sea. Hosp. not yet staffed as it is new. First sighed Medit. Sea at 7 a.m. Rly line follows shore now.</p>
<p>Arrived El Arish 8.45 am. Left 9.30 a.m. &amp; crushed still more in trucks round meat boxes.&nbsp;H<s>ead for open desert</s>. Passed through fortified &amp; entrenched country. Taube flew over at 10 a.m.</p>
<p>Arrived Raffa 11.15. Shiekh&nbsp;Nuran at 12.20 (due at 12.17 pm) Transferred our kit on camels to Scottish Horse Amb. bivouac at 2.30 pm. Light ration at 3 p.m. &amp; at 3.30 started to build our own bivouacs. Bully &amp; bread ration for tea. At dusk watched from a distance an aerial&nbsp;engagement. Shells seen&nbsp;bursting round the 6 planes up.</p>
<p>Guns on Gaza front heard plainly through the night.</p>
