
Oct. 5thAn abrupt ending to morning rehearsal - Orders to move -  In two or three hours we make preparations for departure and load three large motor lorries with personal & Theatrical properties -A journey of 17 kilometres (almost 11 miles) brings us to Bray-sur-Somme where we are to enjoy the privilege of entertaining our "1914 Boys" who are just about to leave for Australia.
