 Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers 29.10.15. 
 Dear Mr. Layton I am sending Mrs. Thorne to you. Asking if you could give her some assistance got behind in rent through serious illness of children &ndash; &amp; if the &pound;1 she is owing 10/- for rent &amp; 10/- for eggs principally could be paid she would be alright. Mr. Hyde knows of Mrs. Thorne 
 Yours truly E. H. Bennie
