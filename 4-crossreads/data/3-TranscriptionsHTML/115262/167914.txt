<p>Sunday 27 May 1917.</p>
<p>Crook during early morning. &nbsp;It rained at 5 p.m. All sleeping above came below. Speed at 7 a.m. 11 knots an hr. Good sea all day. Travelled well. Church parade at 10 a.m. up on boat deck. &nbsp;Wrote letters down on troop deck. Entered for sports events for Tuesday. 2 more shoals of &quot;flying fish seen by those on upper deck in afternoon. Sing-song after dark &amp; turned in at 8 pm.</p>
<p>Another case of &quot;mumps&quot; today.</p>
<p>Today we passed near Cocos Is. where Emden &quot;was&quot; sunk by Sydney&nbsp;<s>H.M.S.</s></p>
