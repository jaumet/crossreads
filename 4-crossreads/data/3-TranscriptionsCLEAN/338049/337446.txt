  
 Saturday 24 August Things as usual - busy cleaning saddlery - 
 Called out about 7 p.m. with wagon - Two wagons mine - Chancellors. Up along main road & a fair way up one of the wheels of my wagon stuck - Could not repair. Shelled rather heavily. Horses withdrawn. Finally removed wagon to side of the road & took stores on cable card. Laid line. 10th Bde - 291 QFA.
