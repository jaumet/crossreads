 Sept. 4th. Spent quiet day in Longuevillette prior to entraining for (?) &ndash; (Apple fights) 
 Sept 5th &amp; 6th. Night march commenced 1am &ndash; arrive at Doullens station 3a.m. entrain 5am and after uneventful journey arrive at Proven at 2p.m. (First glimpse of Belgium March to Poperinghe &ndash; We encamp at Canadian Camp on outskirts of town in the vicinity if Ypres. 
 Sept. 7th. Very pleased with new Camp &ndash; As we are in a very prominent position as regards German gas attacks, a most vigilant watch is maintained. "Impressions of Belgium"
