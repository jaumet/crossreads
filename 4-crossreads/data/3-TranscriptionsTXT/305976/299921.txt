 how they are all spending the holiday. Would give anything to be able to spend a week at home &amp; see them all again. Tuesday Oct 2 Wrote mail. no sign of any mail yet. Rode over to Bedouin camp with Tom to give our neddys a feed of grass. Would not change my horse now we know one another. Wednesday Oct 3 Rumoured big air raid coming to morrow night. Two planes over lines observing to day. Rumoured big stunt on 10th. Hope there is anything is better than this dull monotony. Thursday Oct 4 New Zealand mounted band gave us a tune today, usual routine.
