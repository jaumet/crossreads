<p>May 1917</p>
<p>Monday 7<br/>Went to Aust Gen Hosp saw Dave, Fr Goodman and host of the boys also Vic Holland Vic Masters. Dave looking well. Was at Hospital from 2.30 till 8.15 pm.<br/>Col McIntosh (12 LH) died of wounds received at Gaza</p>
<p>Tuesday 8<br/>Went in to National with Jack Gratham to see Olsson</p>
