<p>27 May 1918 Monday (X to X crossed out)<br/>We have been given foods stuff from Comforts Fund &amp; the AIF Canteen and YMCA all sell stuff but none can help in the wood difficulty-  Charcoal from Lebanon is now 30/- a donkey load = to about 75 lbs.<br/>X Received English Papers from Laura- anxious days these-  Also Australian Mail- Mother March 19th with enclosures- Lorna &ndash; May do. and of course the little wife every 2 or 3 days-  Colonel McLaren is a Medical man &amp; will be able to get leave to see <u>his</u> wife so Dolly will get good advice too, alls well. X</p>
