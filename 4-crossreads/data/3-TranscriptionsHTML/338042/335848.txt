<p>Then to Theatre - &amp; afterwards seek "digs" quite unsuccessfully &ndash; Return to Club &amp; decide to spend week there &ndash; Having a free afternoon  I accompany Les Williams to 9th Park Rd where he calls on some "old friends" &ndash; These friends are then kind enough to take us to Paisley where we are entertained by Mr.&amp; Mrs Wood &ndash; Return to Club.<br/>April 14th<br/>Rehearsal with Orchestra at "Coliseum" &ndash; and preparations for evening -</p>
