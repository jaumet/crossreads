<p>and also a very old place.  The building's there are nothing to write home about.  I also had a good look around<strike>Heliop</strike> Heliopolis which is a very fine place.  Before I go any further this is the town our camp was nearest.  Heliopolis can boast of having some very fine buildings.  The Palace Hotel which was turned into a hospital is absolutely the prettiest building I have ever seen or at least up till that time.  It was first built for a Monte Carlo, other wise a gambling house, but I think it fell through, and was then turned into a hotel, from that into a hospital.
