<p>February 1918<br/>Monday 25</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Rained like the very deuce this morning. Left home at 6 am, with Kerr for F.O.P.<br/>Spent a rather chilly forenoon and registered Baab Farm<br/>In afternoon, whilst registering Code F, saw a Fritz arise like a spirit under the persistence of an 18 pounder H.E.<br/>Arrived home, 6.15 pm. Glorious moonlight night .<br/>Fritz dropped a few eggs on supports.<br/>Received letters from Doug H, and Mother</p>
