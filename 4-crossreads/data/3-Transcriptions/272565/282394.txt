<p>Flers, and to the right.  On the way out we passed another abandoned Tank to the right of Flers.  Met Benson, now a Lieut. in the 56 Batt.  As soon as we reached our post business commenced.  Three trench feet patients were there to be carried out.  They poured in for hours &amp; our 4 squads were carrying all night till we could hardly stand up.  Today water will freeze if left in the open for an hour.  The fog is also thickening.  For days now the weather</p>
