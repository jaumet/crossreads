<p>making others miserable &ndash; Reading &amp; Letter writing  Leave Boulogne at 6 p.m. for Etaples. Arrive there 9 p.m. Camp Etaples for night.<br/>May 17th<br/>Leave Etaples 9 a.m. &amp; travel in cattle trucks through decent country till 8 p.m. when we leave train at Canaples &amp; walk to Vignacourt &ndash; Considerably bucked up by hospitality of Australian A.P.C. Boys who though strangers to us all provide beds &amp; meals.<br/>May 18th Leave Vignacourt</p>
