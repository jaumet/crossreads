<p><u>IX  Wittness</u>  Eduard Newman being duly sworn states "I remember the day Ratch was shot. I wher in the same tent, as he.  I saw the commandant pass near the fence.  He had passed about 20 stepps, and while lighting a cigarette I called out to him"  Can I have a cigarette"  I dit not hear anyone calling out or insulting remark to him, nor dit I do so myself.  If any one behind our tent had called out to the commandant "The bugger want shoot us" I must hawe heard it.  "When I had called for a cigarette, he turned and fired.  One shot struck the Pole in the middle of our tent.  I heard four shots being fired in all.</p>
