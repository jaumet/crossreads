  
 Raining heavily again. Signallers below us flooded out. The Arabic name for Richon is Ayn[?] Khua[?] Thursday Dec 20 Still raining had to move horses Rode to Canteen for Christmas supplies Friday Dec 21 Saw Squadron of our planes flie over today laden with pills for the Turks. Saturday Dec 22 On half rations & roads impassable on account of heavy rains which is also destroying Railway Sunday Dec 23 Prospect for fine Christmas very poor. Cold, wet, miserable
