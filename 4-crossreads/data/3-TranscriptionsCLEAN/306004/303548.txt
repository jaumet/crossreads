  
 3.10.16 folded & neatly packed by the tent door & all our belongings & equipment  packed tidy for inspection. the men got out fairly well & there were no complaints today, as soon as the men were dismissed from stables practically all of us made for the Canal & had a dip before breakfast, but as we have only half an hour between 'stables' & breakfast, reveille & stables will be 15 minutes earlier in future to give us more time for swimming, parade at 9 oclock, but it was to put the finishing touches on fixing up the Camp
