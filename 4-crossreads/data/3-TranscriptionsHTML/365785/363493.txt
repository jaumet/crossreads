<p>P.S.<br/>I hope I shall be lucky enough to see you soon in Egypt.</p>
<p>Australian Imperial Forces.<br/>No. 2 Hospital,<br/>Training Depot,<br/>Seymour, Victoria<br/>21.viii.15</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Read,<br/>Your very welcome letter containing picture of the youngsters and the kind congratulations arrived yesterday.  I did not lose much time in volunteering when I got through but I did a little hospital work and then volunteered here in Melbourne as nothing was doing in Brisbane in A.M.C. at the time.  They tell me I am to go to a Hospital to assist some of the older men, and I was hoping it might be the one Dr. Read is in so I am letting</p>
