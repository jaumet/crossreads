<p>43<br/>neath the drooping Virgin. It was a lovely ride &amp; the view from the top of the hill was wonderful. Star shells lit up the sky also the roadway. Had a cup of coffee when I got back it went down well. The Adjutant secured a German Howitzer shell case for me, the only souvenir I have of this Front (Somme).</p>
<p>4/9/16.<br/>Am still in Senlis but am expecting a sudden move any minute.</p>
<p>Last night about 30 shells were dropped near by landing amongst some horses &amp; doing a fair amount of damage. This afternoon I rode up the hill &amp; witnessed a German bombardment. They were lobbing them on top of the next rise</p>
