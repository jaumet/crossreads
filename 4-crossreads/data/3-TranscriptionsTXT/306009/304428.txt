 1915 Sep.1 Wed. The Australian Intermediate Base is the main base of the A.I.F.  &amp; has a staff of about 250 men of which a large number are N.C.Os. Staff Pay Office, Int. Base Post Office, Ordnance Depot, &amp; Records &amp; Correspondence Depot, are all at the Intermediate Base &ndash;They must get through a tremendous amount of work.
