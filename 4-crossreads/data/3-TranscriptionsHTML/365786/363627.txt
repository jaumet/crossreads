<p>There the line <strike>branches</strike> divides, one branch going to Suez the other to Cairo.  On the way we passed numbers of Indian troops both cavalry &amp; infantry encamped &amp; entrenched.  At Kantara we saw signs of the early fighting for it was here the Turks made their abortive attempt to cross the Canal.  We saw the trenches &amp; dugouts, the sandbag barricades &amp; the station protected by earthworks &amp; more sandbags.  We also saw the dummy gun hear here.</p>
