
November 1915Friday 26Weather very cold. Wind blowing strongly from South. Expecting Turks to attack. Received a parcel of smokes from Aunt Annie per the "Sun"
Saturday 27Very cold and windy with heavy showers. Navy fired some heavy shells at some object down on Gaba Tepe  Point. I go on the "phone tonight at 2100 until 0100 AAA. It began to snow about 8/30 P.M. It is very cold.
