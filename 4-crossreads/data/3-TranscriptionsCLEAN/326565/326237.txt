
Cove boat for Fig Tree.  Walked from Fig Tree to Alexandra St & Caught boat back to town.  Had tea Sargeants in Market St; Went Bondi at night.  Arrived home 11 pm.
Monday 8/11/15Went to town in morning to buy some articles necessary for trip.  Returned to Balmain visited Mort St met Mother corner of Darling St & Weston Rd. 3.20 pm visited Mrs Hancock.  Home 5.20 pm.  At night paid a few farewell visits.
