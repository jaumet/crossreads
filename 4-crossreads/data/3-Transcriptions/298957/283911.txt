<p>9th Jan (Sunday) On guard all day. Big steamer Marwarri sailed down to Suez.</p>
<p>10th Jan (Monday) Issued with Jack Knives. Otherwise nothing of importance.</p>
<p>11th Jan (Tuesday) Digging trenches and dug-out over on the soft sand hills all day</p>
<p>12th Jan (Wednesday)<br/>Light Duties again. Preparations are now advancing rapidly on both sides of the Canal. Natives are busy cutting a roadway to the water edge, removing the sand in small baskets. Railway to Serapeum also advancing.</p>
