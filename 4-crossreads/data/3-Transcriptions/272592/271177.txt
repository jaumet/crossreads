<p>we had done splendidly, when we are filling the bags we often get the bones &amp; clothes of men that have been buried about here, &amp; the earth positively stinks.</p>
<p>Last night one of our patrols was out &amp; spotted a German patrol the instant the Fritz's saw our boys they bolted for their trench &amp; never offered to put up a scrap</p>
<p>Len &amp; I received parcels of socks from home this morning, &amp; strange to say Len's parcel was wrapped in the same paper that I had sent a parcel from London, home, &amp; the address was still on it, so it has travelled right round the world you might say &amp; finished up in Belguim.</p>
