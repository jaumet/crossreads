<p>Monday 1 October 1917.</p>
<p>1 Taube appeared and chased 2 of our planes out of sight. As soon as they were gone 2 of our planes went up <u>very daring</u>. (<s>I don&#39;t think</s>.)</p>
<p>Inspection today by A.D.M.S. The Lt Col &amp; &quot;Gagget&quot; made a bosh of the whole affair. Result &ndash; Instructional parades from this on.</p>
