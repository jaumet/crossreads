 Sunday 14 October 
 To "K" Supply Column 3 times today and once to Steenvoorde, passing through Abeele and past a fine big aerodrome To A.D.M.S. office at Lille Gate   Ypres along the main Poperinghe-Ypres Road Filled in the evening by playing cards near a good fire.  It has been a lovely day but got cold towards dusk.
