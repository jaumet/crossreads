<p>43 August 19th (Sunday)<br/>Slept in till 11 am &ndash; Played Bridge.</p>
<p>I have been appointed as Pres. of our mess being responsible for all Stores &amp;c. Have instituted a Duty Roster in our mess much to the annoyance to some &amp; amusement to others of our mess.</p>
<p>Recd 2 letters, one from Staff Paymaster &amp; other no 3 from Bay. Not feeling well my foot bandaged with the usual rotten paper bandages.</p>
<p>August 20th<br/>Had hot shower &ndash; Sent for by Doctor once again this morning, apparently there has been another alteration in the conditions &ndash; He, the Dr dictated quite a lot of <strike>inform</strike> instructions to the Medical orderly.</p>
<p>An issue of food from Red Cross Soc today including Butter Beans, Dates, Bully Beef, Sausages, Army Rations, Cheese &amp; margarine. &ndash;</p>
<p>Recd P.C. from German Red Cross referring to Sgt Wynne. &ndash; He is now reported as [a] Prisoner &ndash; Played Bridge, wrote letter no 12 to K, A, &amp;, B.</p>
<p>August 21st<br/>Recd 5 letters &ndash; no 3 from Annie, PC from Kate, one from Gertie &amp; Gertie Rushton also Aunt Sally &ndash; also recd Pcl from Red Cross &ndash; Over 200 parcels in today &ndash; <strike>Made mess</strike></p>
<p>Guest evening today Had tomato soup &ndash; salmon, meat Pudding Date &amp; Jam tart, Date Pudding &ndash; &amp; Coffee I was cook, It was an excellent turnout &ndash;</p>
<p>Played bridge &ndash; Debate tonight &ndash; Subject that Kissing be abolished &ndash;</p>
<p>A Raid on our Room today by the Germans &ndash; looking for tools &amp;c &ndash;</p>
<p>My foot very painful &ndash;</p>
<p>Germans are retiring on Western &amp; Italian front &ndash; French having advanced <strike>just 22</strike> 7 mile on a 20 mile front &ndash;Peace overtures all over the world &ndash; I think Peace is bound to come soon</p>
