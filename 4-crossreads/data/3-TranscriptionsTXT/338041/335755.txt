 Nov. 21st. Return of Bearer to the Quarry &ndash; Preparations for departure &ndash; A miserable train trip followed by a more miserable march &ndash; "Where are we? &ndash; Somewhere in France". The thoroughness of Fritz's positions "avant l'offensive"! (Dugouts etc) &ndash; Back to the good old Huts. 
 Nov. 22nd. We lose our O.C. much to our regret. We are all decidedly pleased to be away from the war &amp; its Horrors &ndash; A trip to a neighbouring village - "Vaudeville". 
 Nov. 23rd. General duties &ndash; News of "Britannic" &ndash; An expensive bath in Albert. &ndash; Promotion of Capt. Donald &ndash; Our air-service. 
 Nov. 24th. Mail from London &amp; Home &ndash; Our recent camp &amp; its surroundings
