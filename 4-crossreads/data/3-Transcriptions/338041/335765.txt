<p>Dec. 18th.<br/>At 4a.m. we move off &amp; make first journey to again proceed to the Line &ndash; Entrain at Vignacourt at 9a.m &amp; after 3 hours slow travelling we leave train &amp; are billeted at Buire &ndash;</p>
<p>Dec. 19th.<br/>Fatigue duties &ndash; Y.M.C.A. &ndash; Roy Mc</p>
<p>Dec 20th<br/>Snow storm &ndash; "A bath we did not get - &amp; have we earned it" - Orderly room runner &ndash; A trip to Mericourt where we enjoy a vaudeville show &ndash; Charlie Chaplin again &ndash;</p>
<p>Dec. 21st<br/>Orderly Room duties &ndash; Y.M.C.A. &ndash; bad weather &ndash; marching orders &ndash; L. George</p>
<p>Dec. 22nd<br/>Our Ambulance leaves Buire at 8.30a.m</p>
