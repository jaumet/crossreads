 THURSDAY, OCTOBER 16, 1919. 
 Fine & S. Wind. Lopping in Mountain 
 in morning and ringing in the 
 Middle in afternoon. 
 Darby down 140 ft in the new 
 bore and has got rotten rock & clay 
 all the way. Tickle drawing 
 water to him. The 200 ft of casing 
 came along & was dropped at 
 Tickle's.  
 I got my new sheep dog. "Wallace" 
 from Tickle's & brought him home 
 Dad came back in the mail car.
