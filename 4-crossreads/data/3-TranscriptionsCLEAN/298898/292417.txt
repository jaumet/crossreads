Tis indeed a fine town & very prosperous looking, crowded with soldiers, the few men in civilian clothes being well dressed & the women especially so. Certainly it was a sight for sore eyes. The Cathedral was a fine sight the front & main entrances being protected by sandbags also parts inside the building. Reached home about 9 P.M. just in time to go on duty for the night. 
 Tuesday 19 December Went in to Albert & Becordel for something to do as I did not feel very sleepy. Took in a meningitis patient to hospital & had dinner there going onto Albert.
