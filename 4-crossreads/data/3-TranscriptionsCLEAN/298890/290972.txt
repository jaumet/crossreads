  
 Miss de Lissa, M Charland 
 24 Sunday to tea with Miss H. and Miss N. 
 25. Monday In afternoon went to Miss Smith 22 Harley Rd. N.W. 
 26. Tuesday At Miss Smith. Writing 
 27. Wednesday. Writing. Went down town to buy Pollard's materials & then on to Usshers' for tea. Nell and her brother there.
