<p>Frenchies for a while, then Spaniards for a couple more days, Gibraltar for a couple of days, Teneriffe and Las Palmas for 3 or 4 more days, a couple of stations on the West African coast up to today, but very faint and with little or no business.  This region is not well adapted for long distance wireless working on account of the atmospheric conditions.  There is such a small volume of shipping that it is not utilised to any great extent.
