  
 Friday 21 December 1917 H. Communion 6.30 a.m. & such a nice service, sisters Brown (E.M.), Crane, Heathcote & self.  In charge of Connaught for day.  All is peace & quiet, dressings, etc., finished 12 m.d.  C. Grant & I exchange opinions re line of hymn to fit "Connaught".  I think, O day of rest & gladness.  He, "Peace Perfect peace" - both are appropriate.
