<p>September, 1917. Monday 3<br/>Usual parades. Perfect day. Last night awakened several times by bombs, machine guns &amp; art bombardment. Little's F.G.C.M. in morning with Christian and pleaded guilty. In aftn. &frac12; hr fatigue &amp; rest of day read.</p>
