<p>Tuesday 26 March<br/>Up early for 8.40 train to Balloch then boat up to Loch Lomond calling at various little villages until Inversnaid. A couple of hours to spare so climbed hills instead of having dinner. Great fine views. Good trip but slightly cold. Met Australian Civilian whose views of us were interesting.</p>
<p>Home by about 6 p.m. &amp; after much coaxing etc. visited Whist Drive &amp; Dance at Blackfriars Church. Fine gathering. Won the Drive; Prize fountain pen. Thanked people.</p>
