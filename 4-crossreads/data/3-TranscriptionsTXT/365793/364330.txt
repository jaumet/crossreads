 Aug 18 rigged Schooner on the Starboard Bow so cut in behind her &amp; left her to Port. She passed very close &amp; dipped the salute in passing. Counted 27 sails in all &amp; could distinguish several figures on deck (12 Noon) averaging 300 a day &amp; just 3,170 off Capetown. Every night the piano is in full request &amp; the voices of "the boys" float out over the Atlantic, and it is the  good old  songs they love the best of all.
