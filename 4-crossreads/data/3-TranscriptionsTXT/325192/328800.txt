So all letters found upon him wher confiscated by the Camp authoritys &amp; destroyed. On rainy days, whe wher permitted remain in Camp, &amp; work wher dispensed with for that day at least. So the Motto "No work, no pay" wher hier also the Rule however the loss of 1 shilling made us wishe it would rain daily. End of May a Internee &amp; his fiance a lady residing in New Zealand decidet to get married, allthough the daily Newspapers had never a good word to say either for a German personally or the "Hun-Race" in General. But owing thos two lovers hawing being enaged previous to the outbreak of the "European upheaval" &amp; the lady knowing the better side of her Fianced German Husband, begged him to marry her, so as to show australia that their wher at least 1 women hier, wich did not beleave in Newspapers fairy tales &amp; Gossip. So she came across the "blue water" to Sydney &amp; engaging their the American Minister of the U.S.A. Church &amp; wendet their way to this Camp wher the Ceremony toke Place.
