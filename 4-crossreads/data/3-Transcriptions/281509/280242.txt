<p><br/>19 January 1916<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Up betimes homecoming.  Meet 9 other at [indecipherable] at Prince Alfred at 8.30 am. Dr. Willis takes us in hand.  View 11 adenoid operations, 3 circ. in forenoon.  Go to lunch at Brodies.  Back in afternoon to Hospital.  View 3 major operations viz Adhesion of stomach, hernia &amp; lump on neck.  Tea with boys at Newtown &amp; back to hospital after; Venereal. Back to Macs for supper.  Home at 10.30</p>
<p>Ashley &amp; Sid Woodward leave for front.</p>
