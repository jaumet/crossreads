<p>17<br/>Here they are, just landed from France.</p>
<p>Put out of commission by the enemy</p>
<p>Brave old guns which have done their duty and are back as hospital cases</p>
<p>Look at them! See this one has a breech which cannot open on account of a lucky hit to the credit of the Hun.</p>
<p>The solid brassworks of the block is kneeded into shapliness as though it were dough</p>
<p>Next is a smaller field gun with a wrecked carriage and its bullet proof sheild crumpled up and torne like brown paper.</p>
