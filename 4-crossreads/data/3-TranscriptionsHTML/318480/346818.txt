<p>I have been in Englan [England] 4 months &amp; I have only had two letters from home, you don't how anxious I am to hear how you stand and how you got on with the harvest.</p>
<p>I haven't any more to say now.</p>
<p>Your loving Brother<br/>Frank Hassall</p>
<p>A Coy<br/>17 Batt.<br/>5 Brigade<br/>15 Section<br/>No. 4 Platoon</p>
