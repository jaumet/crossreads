<p>12.3.16<br/>We got paid on the 10th  I collected &pound;2.11.4 I put a chit in the pay book humbly requesting the payment of a fiver but I guess that chit hit the waste paper basket, but 50/- was better than nothing,<br/>We had a very good feed in fact we had a couple and then sat on the balcony of an hotel and drank beer looked at the picture and smoked 1/-</p>
