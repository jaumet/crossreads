<p>POST CARD<br/>[Postage stamp is 1d stamp for UNION OF SOUTH AFRICA]<br/>The Hon<br/>Mr Justice Ferguson<br/>Judges Chambers<br/>Supreme Court<br/>Sydney<br/>Australia</p>
<p><u>28.9.15</u> Have spent two very pleasant days at this hotel in Durban. It makes a welcome break after the weary run across from Fremantle. I am posting you a local newspaper to give you some idea of both journalism and politics in this part of the Empire. With kindest regards to all at 'Wimbledon"<br/>Yrs very sincerely<br/><u>Lt. S. Whitfeld.</u></p>
