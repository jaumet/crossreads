
27th June  Tuesday:-From letters received from London it seems rather certain that we are all going to be packed off to the front when we reach there. This will do me. A few of those originally chosen seem rath inclined to prefer staying in Egypt on strength of this, so there is a chance for me, after all. To-day is cool, only 98  . After a short stroll & writing a few letters, retire.
28th June:- WednesdayMore rumours, but it is evident we are off to the front after a short stay in England. This only serves to show what a mistake the general idea of the inexperienced in Australia make. One never knows the time &  we just do as we are told &  go
