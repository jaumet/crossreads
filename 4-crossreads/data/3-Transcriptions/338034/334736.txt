<p>named Erk on the Turkish left flank. The whole of the mounted troops &amp; artillery shortly after daybreak attacked the Turkish flank, the engagement lasted all day &amp; at times was pretty severe. The Third Brigade had a fairly large number of casualties &amp; <strike>a</strike> two companies of the Camel Corps were badly cut up. <strike>our casualties however on the whole were light.</strike>  We captured a number of Turkish positions &amp; our artillery must have inflicted severe losses on the enemy, it completely outclassing the enemy batteries.</p>
