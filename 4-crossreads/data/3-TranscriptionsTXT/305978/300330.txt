
Arrived at Albany at 2pm. left again at 5pm not allowed off the ship
10th/Raining. Passed Freemantle sea freshened.
11/Church Parade. Several good fights on board.
13/Thirteen days out at sea. Man died at sea he was out of the 4th of the 17 Batt.Beural Service at 5pm.
14/Twenty four of us on gaurd looking for submarinesI lost my bayonet overboard came off gaurd at 8.30
15/Were all paid 14/-Saw a big shark. Sea very calm.
17/Went to church again.
