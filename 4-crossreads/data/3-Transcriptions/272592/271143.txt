<p>24th.   Sunday today &amp; we held a Brigade Church parade, we had to march about a mile along a very pretty little road, lined on either side by green hop-fields &amp; young growing crops, we formed up in a square in a nice little grass paddock where we held our sports the other day, the 3rd Bn. band was there &amp; played for the service which was conducted by old Mac, we had quite an important morning, for General Birdwood &amp; Walker were there &amp; also Andy Fisher &amp; someone else in civies</p>
