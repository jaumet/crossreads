<p>more have just come in this last minute. One of them Cpl 'Dick' Dawson &ndash; with no kit at all but his simple uniform, not even great coat &ndash; but guess'll he 'pick' one up as he goes on.</p>
<p>Dec 12/15<br/>Havn't had a single minute to call my own since last entry, except those required for sleep &ndash; am now writing this on the box seat of a waggon by candlelight at 11.45 pm while on horsepicket</p>
<p>am on duty from 10 p.m. until 1.30 and was up at 4.30 this morning &amp; have to be up tomorrow at the same hour. Will never forget the last <strike>few</strike> 3 days &ndash; since leaving the rail head &ndash; a little name on the map only containing a mosque, a well &amp;</p>
