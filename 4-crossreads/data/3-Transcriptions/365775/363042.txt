<p>be reequipped &ndash; or our battery, I might mention here, that our battery left Egypt only partially equipped, only having a few horses &amp; our own personal equipment, no guns wagon, horses etc., the horses we have handed over to the three batteries in our brigade &amp; now we are to draw everything from the big Army Ordnance Stores in Harve. I happened to be one of the party sent down with our O.C. to draw the equipment necessary for the working of a battery in the field. If I were to stop &amp; explain fully the working &amp; enormity of these stores, well, it would take a bigger book than this &amp; more time than I can spare to do so, suffice</p>
