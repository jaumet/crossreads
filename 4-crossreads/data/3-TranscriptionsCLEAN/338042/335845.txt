  
 Mrs Rice (with whom we are lodging) certainly knows the Art of Cooking - At 7 p.m. the curtain rises & the Anzac Coves are tendered a wonderful reception by the Manchester people - Very difficult programme for few of us - Enthusiasm & Appreciation of Audience. April 9th Mail from London - Read O.Henry - Visit to Food Controllers Office for purpose of procuring Pickels for meat, butter, & sugar and tea etc. - See the matinee at Hippodrome - on the Bill are:- Ruth Vincent. Lupino Lane. Herbert Waring etc.
