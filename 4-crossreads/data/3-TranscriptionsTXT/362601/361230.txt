 1 July, Tuesday 
 F. Major has liver &amp; makes all the others buzz around. Go to Boo-Keeping. Sleep downstairs on the floor. I go in Crib Tourney &amp; miss first round we fasten Ports as Gale is expected. We are warned to parade come on deferred pay. 
 Sit in Oper Theatre &amp; read M's letters. I am very much overcome. 
 2 July, Wednesday 
 W. Gale raging. I mess around all morning. Go to Book-Keeping in afternoon. Play draughts before going on Duty. We develop films in pantry after [?] pm. 
 Go to bed on seat &amp; pack myself in like a parcel about midnight. Arise at 6 am &amp; have K bustle. Music makes me very thoughtful about my chere solin. Lecture on deciduous fruits.
