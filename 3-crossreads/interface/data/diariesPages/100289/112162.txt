Wednesday 16.8.16
Quiet as usual, rained slightly, Paid 40 Francs, 1.8.8
Thursday 17.8.16
Still raining, roads very muddy.
Received letters parcels from home. Had a look over a fine old church in Doulieu a few days back, which was completely burnt out by the Germans, with the use of petroleum.
Friday 18.8.16
A B Sec came here route marching this morning, appear to be getting a rough time with squad drill again.
Saturday 19.8.16
Rained hard during morning. Dozens of Tommies come here direct from Somme look much the worse for their experience; they tell some very appaling tales of the Slaughter
