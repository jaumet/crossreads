<p>Not feeling in the pink lately for some reason. Had four letters from Rita in a fortnight much to my pleasure.<br/>Have been writing to her every day lately, as I know what great pleasure they will give her.<br/>We are supposed to be going either in one or three months, which is not known for certain.<br/>We made our wills this week</p>
