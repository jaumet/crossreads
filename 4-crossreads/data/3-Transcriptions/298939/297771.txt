<p>Monday 7th May<br/>Inoculation today which I missed, &amp; on that account Capt Straun (who is likely to be our major) lectured us on bleeding wounds &amp; antiseptics.<br/>Both were interesting the former characterised by the undressing (partial) of various of of number, &amp; consequent stoppage of all kinds of arteries with numbers of imaginary lives saved<br/>A story brought up incidentally is worth recording: -<br/>Two men had their legs shot off in a dressing station &amp; the only stretcher bearer</p>
