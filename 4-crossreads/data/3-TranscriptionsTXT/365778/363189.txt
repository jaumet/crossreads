 Saturday 14/4/17 Marched to Favrieul arrived by 3 pm commenced to rain &ndash; saw Vernon, also passed the General &amp; had a long talk. Sunday 15/4/17 Hun attacked Lagnicourt, Fifth Bde did the job splendidly &amp; pushed him out. Moved at 7 pm to Corps Line. Monday 16/4/17 Raining heavily all day, men all in bivouacs. Tuesday 17/4/17 Still raining &amp; snowing, bitterly cold &ndash; meeting of C.O.s at 7th Bde H.Q. Had dinner with Black 7th Fd Ambulance.
