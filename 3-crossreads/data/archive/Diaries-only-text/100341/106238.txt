16th Tuesday
Weather very bright hot. Naval Guns directed toward Beachy Bill with heavy shells during afternoon.
27th Wednesday
Sky clouded at times but very windy. Combined Naval Artillery bombardment all along line for about 1 hour midday.
28th Thursday
Weather Bright hot. Fairly quiet along front. Evening commenced early with bomb throwing kept up very furiously all night. 390 Bombs in all used on whole Post during the night. Small English Mail.
