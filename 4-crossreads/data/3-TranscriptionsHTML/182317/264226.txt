<p>[Telegram dated Boulogne Sur Mer, Pas de Calais 14-4-17]<br/>London 42876 40 14 6H13 SR<br/>Very anxious about you ask surgeon telegraph me fully for transmission to Sydney also ask transfer Millbank as soon as possible have arranged bed for you Major Skirving 189 Coleherne Court Earlscourt.<br/>[Annotation on telegram Out of danger; Wound of arm &amp; Head, not penetrating Brain]</p>
