
Monday 10/12/17. Training generally. "C" Coy Concert & Supper Party.
Tuesday 11/12/17  Feeling a bit off color  quiet day, meeting of C.O's.
Wednesday 12/12/17. Battalion Scheme afternoon f/ball match against 5th Bde. Had dinner with C.n. Smith with 10th F. amb.
Thursday 13/12/17  Reconnoitred front Line with Lloyd, going in front of [Warneton?]
Friday 14/12/17. Another trip to Front line, returned by The Shrine where the General was killed
