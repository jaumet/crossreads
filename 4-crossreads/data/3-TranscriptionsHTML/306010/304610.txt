<p>following day we learnt that no. 2 G.H. was to move to Boulonge and the O.C. with 2 officers &amp; 25 men left the same night as an advance party while the remainder of us are still here.<br/>On Wednesday night at 11 o'clock all clocks &amp; watches throughout France were put forward on hour as the result of a day-light saving bill passed by the French Parliament. And it is indeed daylight saving &ndash; not being dark now until sometime after 9 p.m.<br/>Yesterday evening went for a stroll through the slum area of the city. Marvellous how men, women &amp; children can live among such surroundings, the streets being nothing more than dark, dirty, rough &amp; narrow lanes into which the sun never shines.
