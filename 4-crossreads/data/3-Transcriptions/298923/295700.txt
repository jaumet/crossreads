<p>3 February 1918 Sunday<br/>Warm &amp; fine.  Field Officer today &amp; instead of Church attended a lecture on Jerusalem-<br/>Good news tonight re Berlin strikers hope it is all true.<br/><strike>Recd. parcel from the wife she has gone Port Said.</strike><br/>Have been busy fixing up mounted sports for the men.  Am attending a Topographical course under Captain McLean (who is a Licensed Surveyor) tomorrow</p>
