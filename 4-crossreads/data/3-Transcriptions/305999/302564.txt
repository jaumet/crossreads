<p>just going to march away when I got chucked out    they went and sent men that had only been away from Australia a short time.  And I an Anzac can not go   this is the way all the old men get treated here</p>
<p>31/3/17<br/>I was taken to orderly room and tried for not answering a statment when called upon  I told them the position I was in and they remanded me to the Comidant</p>
