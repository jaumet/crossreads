<p>Saturday 5 May 1917.</p>
<p>In camp in the morning as not having a vote was on &quot;fatigue&quot; cleaning up the lines.</p>
<p>2<sup>nd&nbsp;</sup>troop train to Melbourne from Seymour at 1.45 pm.</p>
<p>A very dirty &amp; dusty march from camp to station.</p>
<p>1/3 of the boys had to ride in cars as result of march on Wed. &amp; Thursday.</p>
<p>Arrived City. 4.10 pm. Caught 4.35 pm. train from Flinders St.</p>
<p>Had tea in D&#39;nong. &amp; caught train later (7.30) to Lyndhurst.</p>
<p>Home at 8.15 pm. Spent evening at home &amp; bed at 11.30 pm.</p>
<p>Present from &quot;Gannons&quot; Deniliquin.</p>
