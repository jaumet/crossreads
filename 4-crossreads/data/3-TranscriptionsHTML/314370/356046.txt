<p>June, 1916<br/>Wednesday 28<br/>12 instructors were sent on the H.Q. staff of the CTB &amp; BD,  we have here a splendid mess which sells splendid cheeroots.</p>
<p>Thursday 29<br/>This morning there was a huge split up of the camp.  All details were cut up into Bdes. ready to go to their units.<br/>about 4 of us are now without a job for which we are truly  thankful.</p>
