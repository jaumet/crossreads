<p>Wednesday Nov 13<br/>To day was wild. The wet season is upon us &amp; we had plenty of wind &amp; rain. All our tents blew down &amp; a nice job it was putting them up again. As far as we can understand we are here until after Christmas.<br/>Thursday Nov 14<br/>Still we &amp; cold nothing doing. New Zealand sports were supposed to be held today but were postponed on account of rain.<br/>Friday Nov 15<br/>New Zealand sports were held today in fine weather. General Allenby attended &amp; we all witnessed a fine days sport.</p>
