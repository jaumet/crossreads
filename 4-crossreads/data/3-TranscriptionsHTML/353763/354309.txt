<p>most useful I admit but not the place for an energetic medical man who wants to evolve his mind by experience &amp; actual work  -  No &ndash; the science for the Navy &amp; every war ship is the practical application from deck to keel of science &ndash; is physical science, mathematics, physics, mechanics  -  The Beach is the happy hunting ground for the Biologist &ndash; Chemist &ndash; Hygienist &amp; practically applied medicine &amp; surgery  -</p>
<p>This aftn I went to the Beach about 2 pm  -  I posted my letter to you &amp; P.  C's to England for Xmas  -  I then bought some P.cards for you which show Suva &amp; give some detail of Fijian life  -  I then went to the library &amp; looked over some books about Fiji &amp; picked up some facts about the old times here  -  King Thacombau was a cannibal Chief &amp; then converted  -  Many tales are told about him  -  I then took a 5 mile walk right round</p>
