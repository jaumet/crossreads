They seemed a bit frigid at first but we have got pretty thick 'hides' nowadays &amp; they soon thawed unde the influence of my "Parlez-vous ing" as Bob &amp; I made our selves at home &amp; put 2 tins of Maconochie [Transcriber's note: a vegetable stew] into the oven to heat They put a jug of beer in front of us &amp; then gave us some hot coffee &amp; cognac.</p>
<p>Billetted in Tilques for 3 days &amp; spent each evening in their house &amp; they told me if ever passing through again to be sure to go &amp; see them.</p>
