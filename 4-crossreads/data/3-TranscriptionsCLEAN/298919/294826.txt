The grounds are magnificent with beautiful flower gardens & grass lawns.  We had afternoon tea in the grounds & returned home across some fields & thence along the main road.  We had tea about 7.30 & then gathered our baggage for departure.  Mr Newton had taken some snapshots of us during the afternoon & Mrs Newton took my home address & promised to send them on if they proved a success.
