This morning we moved from Brisle to Gricourt camp & it was a long & wearisome march & the roads were in a very bad state swimming in water in some places 23rd  Saw Jack Gribble last night he has been in England at a School & got his commission. Early this morning we had to turn out for fatigue & was 1/2 an hour late over it.
