 [Wednesday 19 July 1916] 
 [Following paragraph crossed out] I note what you say re being "Godmother" to a lonely soldier etc, don't you think you've got your hands full with me- but you were always such a busy little Mother &amp; it is very good of you. Clive Weston is here a Sergeant, a brother of Mrs Blake's. Word has just come that 8000 Turks are approaching us so we expect a fight in the morning. All my love will post this.
