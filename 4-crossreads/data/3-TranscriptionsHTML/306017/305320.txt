<p>Allies being represented in the performance  Finished up with a good supper &amp; a motor car back to camp.</p>
<p>Thursday 24th  We had a very busy day packing loading &amp; cleaning up ready to march out tonight.  There was a good concert in the Recreation Room after tea given by Cairo residents.  Had a good supper at the Soldiers Club &amp; snatched a couple of hours sleep.  We saddled up at 11 oclock &amp; marched off at 12.10 am.</p>
<p>Friday 25th  Arrived at the Railway trucking yards at 2.30 am &amp; soon got everything on the train.  A few ladies very kindly provided tea &amp; cocoa, sandwiches &amp; cakes.  I got another hours sleep before the train moved off   We had an uneventful journey reached Serapeum about 11 am Saturday.  We had a busy afternoon getting the lines down &amp; the tents pitched.  We were all pretty tired at night &amp; lost no time getting to bed.</p>
