<p>July 1915<br/>Saturday 3<br/><u>Fine</u>   Heavy shower of rain last night. We often get a thunderstorm here. None of those light showers so common in Australia but big drops of rain that wet one through in quick time. Had a nice ramble with a fellow patient, of the Kings Own Scottish Borders &amp; named Walker</p>
<p>Sunday 4<br/>Fine Another shower of rain this morning. I am some others are to go to the Provost Marshall tomorrow</p>
