 30 September 1918 Monday Went to Randwick in the morning &amp; was measured for surgical boots. In the afternoon saw the eye specialist &amp; have to report back in a fortnights time. Ruby &amp; I went to Carey St later in the evening 
 1918 October 1 Tuesday Went to A.A.H. Mosman this morning &amp; got 13 days Leave granted came back to 204 Victoria St &amp; Ruby and I went out to Carey St later in the evening. Expect to Leave for Canberra in the morning.
