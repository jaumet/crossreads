<p>Range Sutton Veny Heavy frost nice day had light.</p>
<p>Thursday  13<br/>Shooting another nice day Good light Did not shoot well Issued with new rifle last night N. 61277 for active service</p>
<p>Friday 14<br/>Finished a Musketry Course Scored 97 thus being classed as a First Class Shot. Did not do as well as I should have done.</p>
<p>Saturday 15th<br/>Route March with Packs about 10 miles</p>
<p>Sunday 16<br/>Started snowing when we were in</p>
