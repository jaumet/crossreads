March  1.3.15
&amp; had a good time there, we had a cup of tea at the New Zealand Caf&eacute; &amp; arrived back in camp at 8.30;
Mon 1stDismounted parade to-day &amp; were signalling amongst ourselves close to camp.  this morning we had a test in reading; it is said to be a test to see who gets made Corporal of A Squadron.  Lamp practice after tea &amp; it is time we started again as we are not to good with the lamps;  We were reading groups &amp; when we were about to come in some of the lads would not march properly &amp; we were marched around the flat for ten minutes before coming in. was writing until bed time
