
we were to have gone out on a night stunt but it was put off at the last moment.2nd. Full marching order parade this morning & I was platoon Commander, but I managed things alright & it went off smoothly the Colonel inspected us & we marched past him afterwards on the Barrack Square.3rd Went to Church this morning a military Church.In the afternoon I went for a stroll took my Kodak & got a few snaps round about  Has been a rather nice day. I hear that Colonel Abbott. is camped at Wareham, so I am going to look him up shortly.Have had a very sore mouth for the last week got a cold
