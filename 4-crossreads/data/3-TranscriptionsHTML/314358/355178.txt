<p>5<br/>Australians seem to handle so carelessly.</p>
<p>Very few words are spoken by the six as they loll amongst their equipment and handle their cards like a gathering of friendly sultans.  Six clouds of blue smoke rise from pipe and cigarette to simple and waft and fail above the heads of the Tommies standing by.  The flutter and flick of cards is almost constant as the game proceeds.</p>
