<p>We arose at 6 a.m. &amp; had a good breakfast.  I was Mess Orderly as we were all Sergeants to-gether, Len Valentine &amp; Billy Hanrahan being with us.</p>
<p>We had a lovely sea journey but of the details of that I am unable to tell you anything at present as the Censor is sure to busy [?] on this.  We saw the Belgian Coast in the Distance.</p>
<p>Now, I must finish now as it is getting on towards the time of departure.</p>
