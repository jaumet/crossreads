
Australian Red Cross letterhead28.4.17BulfordDear George,It is answer to Your letter of the 28th Feb. that I pen these few lines.  You were very fortunate, Son, in getting Your chance so Young.  Stick to it & never mind if You do have to work a bit of overtime & have to miss some pleasures.  It will be a great treat for You to get some outdoor life.  Indoor business is up to putty & I would not stay inside 10 minutes if I could get out.  I must write & thank Mr Frost for His interest in You.  I knew Dick Guyot well.  Give Him My regards & tell Him what ever He does to keep out of
