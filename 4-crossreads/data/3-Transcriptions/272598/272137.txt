<p>to top of list.  Took up posts again to day reinforced by 14 F.Amb.  Sent cable home, deferred rates.  "Xmas Greetings"  Found traces of H Mortimore, also saw Ern Hawkless.</p>
<p>Friday 8.12.16<br/>Fairly busy night.  Fritz shelled batteries near to our post, also expended a great amount of ammunition by straffing a part of the front line which is unoccupied.</p>
<p>Saturday 9.12 16<br/>32nd Btn raided last night but were not very successful.  Weather hardly so cold as it has been.  Feet giving me a lot of trouble.</p>
<p>Sunday 10.12.16<br/>Relieved at 3 pm.  After 3 day &amp; arrived back at camp a little after 4 pm.  Fritz took advantage of a clear day &amp; shelled heavily.</p>
