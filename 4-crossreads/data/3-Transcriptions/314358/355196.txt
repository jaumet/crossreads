<p>Let us look in te long wharf sheds close by.</p>
<p>See they have been converted into work-shops for the rapid repair of guns</p>
<p>There you can see the bright, well kept machines lathes, hammers, cutters benches, cranes, and the numerous tools and fitting.</p>
