<p>29 March 1918 Good Friday<br/>Best news Ive had is that the little wife has nearly decided to remain in Egypt another Major's wife would be with her in a Nursing home.<br/>Went Jerusalem to Holy Sepulchre, everywhere Bells are ringing &amp; the Jews, Syrians, Armenians etc are dressed in their sunday best.  Found on arrival at Church we would have to stay for 5 hours, the service being translated into 7 languages so we scratched &amp; went to the Mount of Olives.<br/>4 Guns, 300 prisoners 17 Motor lorrys captured East of Jordan.</p>
