
Out on stunt over big hill all day.  Had feed blackberries.  After tea went to Tipperary Hut.
Thursday, 16/8/17Fine day.  On Q.M. fatigue all day so missed all night stunt.  Issued with new tunic & w/proof sheet.  Went to Y.M.C.A. after tea.
Friday, 17/8/17Had easy day - men returned at dinner time.  Heard today leaving for France next week.  Recd. 10 from Bank through Paymaster.
Saturday, 18/8/17Muster parade this morning & detailed off for draft for France - 400 going from this btn.  After dinner had hot bath & fixed up some of my clothes for France.  Sent 12 to C/Wealth Bank per registered post & posted
