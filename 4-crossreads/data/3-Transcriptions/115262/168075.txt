<p>Sunday 4 November 1917.</p>
<p>Brigade Orderly despatch.</p>
<p>At 6 p.m. I.C.C. Bgde &amp; Bgde Ammun.&nbsp;Column concentrated at Larykn Caves. 2 Bedouin watering wells close by, to which the Bedouins in Larykn Caves water their flocks &amp; herds.</p>
<p>[The following two&nbsp;sentences are struck through:]</p>
<p>Reported back to Fd Amb at 10.30 am</p>
<p>2.30 pm Fd Amb moved on to main road &amp; relieved N.Z. L.H.F.A. Mtd Fd Amb at 5 pm when A&nbsp;Sec.</p>
