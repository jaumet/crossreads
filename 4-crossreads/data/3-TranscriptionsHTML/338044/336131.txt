<p>13th Dec., Monday<br/>Start on night duty 6 pm to 12 pm. Very much better than day work in my opinion, but prevents me from visiting our friends.</p>
<p>18th Dec. Saturday:-<br/>During the week nothing of much consequence has happened outside the regular nightly routine of work  Sleep during the morning &amp; walk after dinner &amp; go on duty at 6pm. Our friendship with Madame Alice &amp;  the girls has been proceeding favourably &amp; we are invited to tea on Xmas Day, next Saturday.</p>
<p>19th Dec: Sunday</p>
