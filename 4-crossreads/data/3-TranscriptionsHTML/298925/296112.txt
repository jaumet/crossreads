<p>We got back to camp at 1.30 a.m. marching through mud up to our ankles.</p>
<p>21st  Church parade in morning and parade &amp; programme in Durrington in aid of Salisbury Infirmary for Wounded Soldiers.</p>
<p>22nd  Got Telegram from Harry to say he was leaving for Aus on Thursday</p>
<p>23rd  The goat which was presented to us at Torquay, being a Billy has undergone an operation to-day, but is progressing favourably.  A.M.C. Lec to-day.</p>
