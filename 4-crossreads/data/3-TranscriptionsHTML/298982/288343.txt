<p>Sat 28th Woke up stiff in joints &amp; sore of head</p>
<p>Sun 29 Still feel old &amp; rotten. Rested all morn</p>
<p>Mon 30th Went to doctor in morning. Pills &amp; to lie down <u>Evening</u> come tomorrow</p>
<p>Tue 31 Sent down to Hospital in evening 103 &deg; Sun sent aboard<br/>9th Sept arr in Cairo No 2 G.H. off S.S. Salva</p>
<p>Oct 16th Went into Cairo &amp; drew &pound; 10. First trip into the city since return Met Mr C Sinder.</p>
<p>Oct 24. To Ghiza Zoo with Brookhouse &amp; Baird Met Sisters Lehmann at 2.30 &amp; had a most pleasant day.</p>
