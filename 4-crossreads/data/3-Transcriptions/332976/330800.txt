<p>Febuary March 1917<br/>28th continued   We received the news through orders that Krupps were out on strike (official).  We bombarded the German lines heavily this morning &amp; there was also a heavy bombardment on our right towards Le Transeloy.<br/>1st March 1917<br/>News came to hand this morning that the 29th Division (Tommies) took Le Transloy early this morning &amp; are holding it, but have suffered heavy losses.  We also took another line of trenches on our sector this morning   We have made a general advance along this sector within the last week (see map at back) &amp; it is rumoured that the Germans are retiring to strong points about 13 Kgs. back.</p>
