<p>But even here my old Luck refused to leave me as one of my old school mates was Corporal Nicholson in charge of Battalion section, and although I did not know him till he came up &amp; spoke, and asked me how I liked my new Job. I said "no Bon", and he then told me I was to go on Battalion Head Quarters with him. "No picnic", he informed me "but better than shoving ones head down a Fritz machine gun to see what sort of a barrel it had"</p>
