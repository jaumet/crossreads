Thur. Mar. 9th -16Made Cook's Fatigue for the day.  Peeling spuds carrots etc.
Fri. Mar. 10th -161st Division Artillery assembled 4 P.M. on account of presentation of medals to a driver and a gunner in recognition of their bravery in saving an officer's wife at Alexandria.
Mon. Mar. 13th -16All batteries of 2nd Bde. out for battery manouveres on Saharah desert.  Clouds of dust and boiling hot sun

Tues. Mar. 14th -16Gun-laying tests all day.
Thur. Mar. 16th -16Warned to be ready to leave at any notice.
Fri. Mar. 17th -16Commencement of Gun  firing practice.  Excellent results.  6th Bty. easy winners.
Sat. Mar. 18th -16Going out for more practice and when about half way, were ordered back to pack up to leave same night for Alexandria.
Mon. Mar. 20th -16Orders cancelled for leaving, everybody disappointed.
