<p>Sunday 19 March 1916<br/>Bill, Ellis &amp; I went to Mary's well &amp; Virgin's Tree &amp; to Kursaal after tea.</p>
<p>Monday 20 March 1916<br/>Dave Niness took Photo E. R. I.  Roy &amp; I went to Kursaal together.  Roy came out to dinner but got very little.  Came down to my ward to talk to Corbett.</p>
<p>Tuesday 21 March 1916<br/>Ward-master.<br/>Home, Nettie, Bess, Laura, Nell &amp; Pa, Ma, Cock &amp; Ida.<br/>Went to Pyramids &amp; round the Houses of Cairo.  Bill, Dave, Charlie &amp; E. &amp; I.  Rode from Mena in motor car.</p>
<p>Wednesday 22 March 1916<br/>Met Johhno &amp; went to Pictures.  Special leave from 5 to 10.</p>
<p>Thursday 23 March 1916<br/>Wrote to Mem &amp; Cohens.</p>
<p>Friday 24 March 1916<br/>Went to Blue Mosque.  Met Roy in Gardens, Tea at Caf&eacute; de la Paix.  Tea Misrquash.  Roy, Dave Niness &amp; I went to Pictures.  Pictures Misrquash.  Pictures Colosse.</p>
<p>Saturday 25 March 1916<br/>Our Theatre Experts gave concert in Esbekiah Gardens.</p>
