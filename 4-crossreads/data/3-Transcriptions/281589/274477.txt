<p>Wednesday 26 Sept raining, the chaps whose papers came with them were given there bands (red)  mail came today.</p>
<p>Thursday  fine day, boat roll came out. I was in a physical jerk squad this morning, but this afternoon we were all taken up the lane, went for a walk. Friday 28th Sept. dull threatening to rain pay day, went for a walk after tea, was reclassified this morning, still C1.</p>
<p>Saturday lovely day went to see two N.Z. football teams play, good game. Walk.</p>
<p>Sunday lovely day, church parade. Walk</p>
<p>Monday 1st October very foggy but lovely afternoon, still taken for walks up the lane.</p>
<p>Tuesday very foggy, good afternoon.</p>
