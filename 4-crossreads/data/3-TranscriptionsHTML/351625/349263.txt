<p>peace by Christmas.  Quite a number of the boys of the 25 Reinf. have been wounded lately, quite nice Blighties some of them have got.  That cobber of mine Frank Bailey is getting along alright.  I had a letter from him at the Base hospital (France) &amp; he expected to get to Blighty any day, his knee was pretty bad as he nearly lost it but it is safe now, although they told him he will not get back to France, which is a good</p>
