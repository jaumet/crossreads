 5.  Home with Genuine  British  People like ourselves, not the reserved cold people as we find most of the    English.     Fancy the Willetts' Family getting to London &amp; then having to get out again.  Gee they soon snavel You for the army over here.  No eligible men that can possibly be spared can miss it.  All Tube railways &amp; Motor Buses, lifts, wagons etc are tended by women.  If You see a man You can bet Your life that He is too old or absolutely unfit.
