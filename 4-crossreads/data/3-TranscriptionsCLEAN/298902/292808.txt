  
 Orders out for us to move a distance of 35 miles in 3 stages & the Colonel has given me absolute freedom to make what arrangements I think best for the whole move, could anyone do otherwise than his best when so treated? 
 Monday 16th Dec.  Orders through for our move to take place in 3 stages starting from the 18th & to end at Sivry in Belgium which is to be our winter quarters.  Had an easy day, just making arrangements for the packup tomorrow.  Wrote some letters thus making a start on my back correspondence. 
 Tuesday 17th Dec  The 4th Anniversary of my enlistment in the Army.  Spent the day collecting all our gear from the different departments such as dentists, dispensary etc etc & taking it to the wagons for loading.  Completed all loading by dark ready for the march tomorrow.
