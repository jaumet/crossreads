<p>Jan 1918</p>
<p>to the Butts with a markinh party</p>
<p>Thursday  3<br/>Still at Butts Have Hegarty for a mate. Hall on next target. Backed our score against his for suppers. We won</p>
<p>Friday 4th<br/>Got another cheap supper</p>
<p>Saturday<br/>On the Butts Won again Went to Warminster in afternoon, Banked &pound; 5 Saw the Pictures &amp; had 2 fair meals</p>
