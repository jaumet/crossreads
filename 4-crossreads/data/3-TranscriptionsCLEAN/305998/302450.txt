
Thiloy, cleaned horses of a little mud.
29th Pulled out for spell. Stayed two days near Albert, then had sudden order to go into action at Ervillers between Baupaume & Arrass. The guns were pulled into St Leger & we were there till the big stunt came successfull,  then went forward a few miles.
