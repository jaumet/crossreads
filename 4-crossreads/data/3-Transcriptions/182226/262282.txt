<p>Friday 17 September, 1915.</p>
<p>On duty again.&nbsp;Very hard work carrying sandbags up the Hill &amp; finishing the mending of the roof of the big shed. Finished that &amp; am building a shelter for the cook.</p>
<p>Scored one egg. Great rejoicing. Very slight shower of rain.</p>
<p>Wrote to Ida.</p>
