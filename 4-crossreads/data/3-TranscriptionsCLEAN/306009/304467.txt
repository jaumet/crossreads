  
 1915 Oct. 12 Tue. Looked in at the French Scouts' clubroom & then went for a stroll with 2 or 3 No. 2 G.H. chaps I met. Getting very decent weather now - days not hot & nights not cold. The Military authorities have decided that as during the winter months the climate of Egypt and the Dardanelles is so good, no patients will be sent to England. They may go to Cyprus perhaps, for a short stay.
