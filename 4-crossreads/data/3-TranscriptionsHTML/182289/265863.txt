<p>Lieut. Wright reported back to unit.  Horse Lines moved to approx.. C.5.d.70.75.  During the last 24 hours enemy attitude has been particularly quiet.  Only artillery activity, a few 77 mm. in Marrett Wood and T. Roads near the village.  Enemy aircraft very little, a few planes attempted to cross our lines.</p>
