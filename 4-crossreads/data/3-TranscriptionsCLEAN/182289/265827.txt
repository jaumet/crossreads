  
 Le Bizet (8) Cook House, C.18.b.12.23, 2.30 a.m. At 6.5 p.m. Right group A.F.A. reported by wire as follows.  "Cook House at C.8.b.12.23 and Pineapple Emplacement at "C.17.a.55.65 damaged to-day by our 4.5 How's." With the object of inflicting casualties on Working parties a Machine gun emplaced at C.15.d.50.90 kept up harrassing fire throughout the night expending 1,000 rounds. 
 26.2.18 C.O. (Capt. D.A. Whitehead) attended Divisional Commanders Conference, the subject under discussion being, Tactics and training with a view to the probable employment of the Division in the near future.  Points of the greatest importance were brought up, and thoroughly discussed.  It was suggested by the Divisional Commanders that each Unit Commander should hold similar Conferences with his Officers.  This will be done as soon as Company moves into reserve.  The main point emphasised throughout the whole Conference was that of "Uniformity of Thought".
