<p>[Wednesday 23 August 1916]</p>
<p><strike>Days are</strike>  Days are <strike>still</strike> still warm even hot here,  I don't know how I'll get on with the cold of England but I haven't gone yet.<br/>My Chart is normal now <strike>not so much sore back</strike> , Pulse good every thing good- my wound was nothing to what some chaps got.</p>
