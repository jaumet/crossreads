<p>resting.  An occasional address was made by a great Belgian preacher.  In fact everything was Belgian.  After the ceremony we visited the Criffot[?]  Chapel where Cardinals Wasenia[?] &amp; Manning are buried.  The streets in front of the Cathedral were densely packed  and as the Duchess of  Yvonne &amp; Princess Clementine (Sister of the King of the Belgians &amp; a most beautiful woman both who had attended the Mass) drove away in their cars great enthusiasm prevailed the crowd cheering frantically calling "Vive la Princess' &amp; "Vive La Belgium".  I was quite close to the cars.  As they moved away slowly the Princess smiled &amp; bowed</p>
