<p>October 17th &ndash; One of our Sisters who was with us for some months at 3rd A.C.C.S. is to be married   &amp;  she wants Kelly   &amp;  me to be at the wedding.</p>
<p>Dorothy is at a C.C.S. quite close to her cousin Alan, so she is lucky &ndash; also near some of our other friends.</p>
<p>Very few Aust. Wounded have come here but they are "In" now, so we shall surely get them. Of course it is much brighter to nurse are own dear boys   &amp;  they love to get their own boys   &amp;  they love to get their own Sisters -   <strike>the</strike>  very few English sisters understand the Aust. boy.</p>
