<p>[Saturday 19 August 1916]</p>
<p>Turk prisoners have brought <strike>CHOLERA</strike> with them so every one is inoculated once more.  I am getting quite white I was burnt black when I came in, &amp; getting very soft lying in bed.<br/><strike>Woke up as a new orderly fainted &amp; fell over the Surgeon DOCTORS dressing tables couldn't stand seeing dressing done so I thought it was a Howitzer.</strike><br/>Sent you a sketch to replace the one lost in "trawler" but am afraid you must wait till I come home to fill in details &amp; explain-<br/>Was in Hospital this time last year at No.1 A.G.H. (Palace)</p>
