<p>and still able to do his share of Talking!  You know he can Talk me out of Time easily!<br/>The weather today has been rather stormy, and there have been two or three rather smart showers   I do hope it does not break alltogether, It has been such good Sunny weather for chasing fritz,  The Corporal has just come for the Letters So I will have to ring off with all good wishes to all at Overstrand from Chas G. Pryce.<br/>Please thank Elsie for Card.   C.</p>
<p>R. Pofe [presumably censor.]</p>
