  
 11 June 1916 Sunday 
 Fourth Sunday in Etaples Camp. Church parade as usual at 9. 30 a.m. Chaplain's address on Lord Kitchener. Two chops play cards during service, sacrilege. Write letters to Bess Haas & post cards to Nell, Jessie Mon. Jack & Arch. Bob Tony & self go for walk to Camiers, blocked by military police. Same old story. Anzacs barred. French peasant, chicory. Rain, shelter in dug out. Tony receives parcel from England. Cake & apples. Former crushed but nice.
