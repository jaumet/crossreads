 The monotonous call of the cuckoo here haunts one for it is unceasing & has no music like the lark now trilling so clearly in the sky above. 
 31-5-19 Leave camp at 9 but do not catch train till 1 pm & then pay my fare to Salisbury as there is a train for France via Southampton which leaves from this station & so if I go by this route I need not change my baggage 
 I leave my baggage in the cloak room & have dinner at Chinese restaurant (4/10) where I met 2 African airmen who volunteer to take me up in their plane if I can get leave to go up to Northolt junction before I leave. I then find Lily Brookes & take her to the pictures after which I go home to Baywater Rd 
 1-6-19 (Sunday) Get up this morning at 11.30 & take a walk in the park & listen to the speeches There is a big police meeting here to day to decide if the London police should strike. I ask a policeman what I can do to fill in time & suggests going up the river in the boat & I add visiting Kew Gardens to myself.
