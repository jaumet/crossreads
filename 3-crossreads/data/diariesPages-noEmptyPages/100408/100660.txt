in beer tent. Had yarn to a chap today who is in the Aust. 9.2" siege battery. He was at the Cambrai push nearly got cut off when the Germans broke through. Told me a 11" shell fell just under his loading platform but fortunately did not burst.
25/1/18 After tea went to an estaminet had 2 eggs chips beetroot bread butter costing 2 francs each. Had coffee rum to wash it down. Saw 2 airships along the coast. 
26/1/18 Recd parcel from Australia containing 4 pcs chocolate, tin unsweetened milk tin milk coffee. Little colder not feeling too good. Heard 4th 5th divisions hopping over at Ypres tomorrow night. Fritz
