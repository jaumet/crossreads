 Saturday May 11th 
 Dunkirk : on duty till 8 am : wrote at night &amp; sent a book of Sydney views to Auntie Turner in Scotland 
 Sunday May 12th 
 Dunkirk : on duty till 8 am : stroll after tea round the docks &amp; watched the monitors etc : very interesting : met an "Ausie" chap working on one of the motor "M.L" [Motor Launch] &amp; he took us on board &amp; we had an interesting time there : 
 Monday May 13th 
 Dunkirk : on duty till 6 am : round to my friends place after tea &amp; had a yarn : brother just home from Verdun :
