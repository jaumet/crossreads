<p>Colonel Simpson gave an address to the massed troops on the eve of our departure.</p>
<p>20th Have been issued with Gas helmets, 2 Blankets, Tear Shell goggles, and bullet <br/>proof steel helmets.</p>
<p>21st Parade with Marching order, Equipment with pack, 2 blankets, 150 rds ball, and <br/>rifle. Makes one feel like a packhorse. This is our last night.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    <br/>A 6 guinea pair of cymbals and several new marches including "The great little Army" <br/>"Colonel Bogey", and Blighty have been bought.</p>
