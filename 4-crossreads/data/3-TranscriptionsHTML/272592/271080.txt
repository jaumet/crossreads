<p>it seems impossible that human beings can live in it, I'm afraid my nerves are beginning to give a little, but can you wonder for I have been through something that would make 99% of men go under &amp; I can't complain about them.</p>
<p>This bombardment continued for an hour &amp; I was very lucky for I had just got an "order" to join the Coy &amp; bring my men in when one of these enormous "oil drum" bombs landed right where our outpost" was stationed, Providence again I reckon for had we been there it would have been good bye the lot &amp; we should never have seen Australia again, but there you are luck again</p>
