
6 December, Saturday
Carnival. I am Seating Steward. I turn in, Hood down. Good sports & swimming events. We come Home for Tea. Mum, Hanna, Bess & I go to Manly for a walk after Tea. B & I miss Mum & L in Political Meeting.
7 December, Sunday
Bess, Laura & I do gardening. We go to Ellan Vannin after dinner. Have a very cold swim & get weather bound. Home for tea. I see 'em off on boat & meet Dave & Misses Jones at Ellis' place. We laugh & sing & play.
