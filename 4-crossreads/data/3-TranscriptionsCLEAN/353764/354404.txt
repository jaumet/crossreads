 13.2.16 Attended Church Parade this morning & have spent the rest of the day having what I call a well earned rest. Uncle Charlie called on me this afternoon & I have promised to visit him at the latter end of the week. There is a big movement of troops on.  All the 5th Training Battalion are going down to The Persian Gulf. Personally I think that the British intend striking at the Turks in this direction, anyway whatever they do, I think the Australians are destined to play a very important part in it. In all probability we will be fixed up this week, & then off we go to the front Turkish   Hip Hip Hurrah.
