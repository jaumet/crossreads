On the 27th a very hot day we packed up and marched eight miles in the excessive heat to Beaucourt to entrain for the north.&nbsp;Our battalion band had been formed by this and we had music on the march for the first time in France.&nbsp;When we finished the march we had several hours to spell so after cooling down and having tea</p>
