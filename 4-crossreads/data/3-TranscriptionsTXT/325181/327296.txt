 12th Just read farewell letters 9 letters recd from Dad &amp; Mum on my enlisting. All the country with exception of 2 headlands appears be flat &amp; sandy. Left Aden at 5.25 for the Suez. Convoy &ndash; (Auxiliary Cruiser) at the lead. 117 in the shade. First aid lecture 13th Oppressively hot Early parade only. Not even a lecture. Everybody spread out on deck under cover. Entered Hells Gates last night  Passed island port side about 7 am. Water reeking with lime &amp; almost undrinkable. 12 Noon. Beautiful breeze getting us head on  Passed group islands at 2 pm (lighthouse) Sea bright blue few porpoises swimming
