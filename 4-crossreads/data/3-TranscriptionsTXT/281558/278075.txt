 Friday January 4th 
 Fovant : usual parades &amp; lectures : very hard frost &amp; bitter cold all day : YMCA at night 
 Saturday Jan 5th

 Sunday Jan 6th 
 Fovant : went on sick parade &amp; the fool painted my leg with iodine : Holy Comm in morn : rain set in so wrote in our hut after dinner : service in the YMCA after tea &amp; prayers for intercession :
