 XOXOXOXOXOX 29th C.C.S. Oct 28th 1916 
 My darling Eddy, 
 A Merry Xmas   &amp;  A Happy New Year. Hope you will like the book. Will you take this little dress to Auntie Berries baby, Margaret and give her a big hug   &amp;  kiss from Auntie Eadle. Hope you get lots of presents from Santy   &amp;  some money out of the Pudding. Heaps of kisses   &amp;  love  From Auntie Eadle  XXXXXXX
