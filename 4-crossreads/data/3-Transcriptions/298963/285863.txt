<p>If the Germans were better looked after than we were their chats, we mighty hungry.</p>
<p>11th June<br/>Camp life grows pretty monotonous, yet if we told to go into the line, I suppose would curse our luck.  I think we must find something to occupy our time.  Today we drilled for 4 hours while poor old "Kirkie" Capt. Kirkwood watched us.  In the afternoon "Lord Brasso" &ndash; our O.C. &ndash; otherwise known as Col. Brennen rode out to inspect us.  We cursed him up &amp; down but it didn't make much difference.</p>
