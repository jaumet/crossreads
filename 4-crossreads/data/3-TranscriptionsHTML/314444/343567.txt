<p>Somewhere in France<br/>13/12/17</p>
<p>Dear Mater<br/>Just a few lines to let you know that I am still in the land of the living I wrote to you last week also Miss Kelly thanking her for those socks she sent me<br/>we are having beastly cold and wet wether over here now. Tomorrow is my birthday I cant keep it up has we are out of civilization just now will keep it up at Xmas Day<br/>Ma I will soon be away on furlough again I cabled to you to send me some</p>
