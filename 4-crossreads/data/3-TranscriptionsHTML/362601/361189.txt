<p>15 April, Tuesday</p>
<p>F but Windy.</p>
<p>I make my first inspection. Pay Parade. Do some tailoring &amp; read &amp; talk all night in Orderly Room.</p>
<p>Wrote to D.J's &amp; Davey Lord. Coins to M.R.L. &amp; letter.</p>
<p>16 April, Wednesday</p>
<p>W &amp; Windy.</p>
<p>Go to B'ford &amp; fix up a lot of matters. Recd letters from D.J's, M.R.L. &amp; Auntie &amp; P.C. from Ellis stating that he was sailing on the 13th to send cough dope to Margaret. M.C. Sent Parcel to Dick Tappin. Hear of Percy Bliss.</p>
<p>Wrote D.J's, Home, M.R.L., Aunt Annie.</p>
