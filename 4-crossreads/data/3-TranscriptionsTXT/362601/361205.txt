
17 May, Saturday
F.Billy & I walk to Hundcroft in morning & with Reg to village with washing in afternoon. Stay in at night, play cards & write. Go to bed early.
Wrote Dick, Jennie, M.R.L.
18 May, Sunday
F.Miss parade & with L/C Dunn after waiting Compton-Chamberlain Cemetery we walk to sarum. It is a lovely walk through beech avenue.i arrive at 11 & go walk with Percy & Mr E. Music & chatters after dinner. Marion is very lively & I have never seen her so price of the devil before. She kisses me while I am packing book for Mell, Dol's hubby & Harry Marshall "(Ir.2 a.a.c.). W & F come to station with me. I slip through to train & get Home OK with Vic Wright.
