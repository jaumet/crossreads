<p>how they are all spending the holiday. Would give anything to be able to spend a week at home &amp; see them all again.<br/>Tuesday Oct 2<br/>Wrote mail. no sign of any mail yet. Rode over to Bedouin camp with Tom to give our neddys a feed of grass. Would not change my horse now we know one another.<br/>Wednesday Oct 3<br/>Rumoured big air raid coming to morrow night. Two planes over lines observing to day. Rumoured big stunt on 10th. Hope there is anything is better than this dull monotony.<br/>Thursday Oct 4<br/>New Zealand mounted band gave us a tune today, usual routine.</p>
