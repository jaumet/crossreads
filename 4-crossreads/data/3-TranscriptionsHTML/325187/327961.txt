<p>&ndash; whole page crossed out<br/>Camel Caselet is like in a Baby's Perambulator &amp; with a little hood I thought I was right, but the ground slipped &amp; I was emptied out- so the MO sent me to a [indecipherable] in a Sandcart then in a Goods Train to the Canal then Red + Train great luxury &amp; tinned RABBIT (Australian at that) &amp; landed here No 3 A.G. Hospital Abbassia once more.  Lungs a bit sore, but soon alright<br/>Dr MacKnight is here on duty but not in surgical unit Duty &amp; is Anti Tetanus Serum one more puncture but same tunic was broken in by shell-<br/>-copied</p>
