 Snowing [4/2/17 Sun] Play for Church again. Wrote Home. Jess. Nette. Dave. Vernon. Arthur Little. Margaret. Slept 14 hours last night 
 Ice [5/2/17 Mon] Snow Still on ground Have long hard Day. Ellis returns from Weymouth &amp; we have sausages for supper 
 Ice 6/2/17 Tues] Snow Still on Ground We change our style of organisation from Ward Treatment to Hut Treatment. 
 Ice [7/2/17 wed] Snow still on ground. Write to Dulce Butell Dressing in C Ward Starts at 2pm. 
 Ice [8/2/13 Thu] Recd letters from Marg. Doris. &amp; 4 from Home 
 Ice [9/2/17 Fri] recd. letter from Auntie Annie Scribbled notes to Jennie &amp; M.R.L. 
 Ice [10/2/17 Sat] Went for a walk with J. Rob. to Milstone &amp; Durrington
