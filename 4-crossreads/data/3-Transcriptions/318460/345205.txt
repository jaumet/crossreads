<p>Saturday 24/2/17<br/>Frost this morning first for more than a week hope it continues &amp; freezes the ground again anything better than the mud.<br/>Corp got drunk yesterday.<br/>Our shift now is 3 hrs 12 min on.<br/>still foggy.<br/>Pay. &pound;3.0.5 &pound;21.8.0<br/>fearfully cold wind blowing.</p>
<p>Sunday 25/2/17<br/>Frost &amp; very cold with the usual fog very thick<br/>Pulling dug out to pieces. suddenly discovered that it is too low, too wet &amp; generally speaking up to Putty [Transcriber's note: "up to Putty" soldier slang for "no good"] after being in it two weeks "Oh these Officers"<br/>Pay &pound;3.1.5 &pound;21.9.0.</p>
<p>beautiful, clear sunny afternoon first for about a month.<br/>expect plenty of Artillery duels tonight.<br/>letter from Viv, in hospital Southampton getting alright.<br/>Bapaume evacuated by Huns also 4 Villages.</p>
<p>Monday 26/2/17<br/>fine &amp; getting clear. rained a little in early hours<br/>writing to Viv today.<br/>Artillery active last night Fritz sent about a doz close handy.<br/>Pay &pound;3.2.5 &pound;21.10.0<br/>first touch of spring, Larks singing this morning &amp; myriads of Birds about. sun quite warm when the clouds get out of the way getting cool in the evening.<br/>Our guns going all day.<br/>wrote to Viv &amp; Posted it.<br/>Got duck-boards in our dug-out now changed to another dug-out to day. got Bosker fire quite compfy. [Transcriber's note: "bosker" a variant of "bonzer".]</p>
<p>Tuesday 27/2/17<br/>A Beautiful Morning sun shining brightly but clouds coming up.<br/>One of our Planes brought down yesterday Pilot killed, observer got off wounded,<br/>Gun blown up last night 4 men killed 2 drivers &amp; Horses got off<br/>Pay &pound;3.3.5 &pound;21.11.0<br/>Artillery going all night &amp; still going fritz doing the same.</p>
