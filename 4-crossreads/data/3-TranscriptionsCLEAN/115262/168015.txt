
Wednesday 5 September 1917.
Sports today at Shiekh Narang Nuran and to be concluded tomorrow Friday. A Taube came over about 11 am. & although shelled from all directions escaped unharmed to his own lines. Wrote letters in time between reliefs on the picquet. Did first lot of cooking - a success too.
Boys returned from Marakeb Beach, & another detachment went to take their place (Tent Division.)
