 19 May 1918 Whit Sunday Nothing of much importance to note Australians reported to have taken a Village near Albert and 360 prisoners Very warm weather Went to Service in Y.M. in the evening. 
 1918 May 20 Whitsun Monday Bank Holiday This appears to be something different to what the English people usually have on Whit Monday. Usually there is a holiday but all appear to be working today. Same old Camp routine. Very warm. Air Raid on London last night.
