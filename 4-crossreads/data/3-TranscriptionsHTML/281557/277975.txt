<p>Tell me is Lithgow still the same<br/>Just write and let me know<br/>I am weary of waiting for news from home,<br/>Of Mother dear sister and Jelo<br/>Tell the boys I'll soon be back<br/>And no matter where I roam<br/>My thoughts daily turn<br/>And my heart fondly yearns<br/>To Lithgow my dear old home.</p>
<p>[names not transcribed]<br/>(Gems from Ella Willcox)</p>
