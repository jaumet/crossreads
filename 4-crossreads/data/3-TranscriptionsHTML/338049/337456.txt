<p>Tuesday 3 Sept.<br/>Things easy &ndash; Afternoon suddenly recalled to Suzanne. So packed up &amp; off.</p>
<p>Arrived, fixed tent, cleaned busied to show opposite D.HQ. Saw Press Representatives, German Prisoners goose-stepped past HQ. Visited vault desecrated by Fritz.</p>
<p>Then to Cooees 7.30. Prisoners Pioneers busy all day on building.</p>
