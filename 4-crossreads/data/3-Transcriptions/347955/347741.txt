<p>Colonel 10.30  asked a few questions and Humed and hared not a Bad old fellow at all  asked if I was fit  I said yes<br/>Monday 26.8.18<br/>feeling much Better to day was discharged from hospital went to Boulac Convalescent home<br/>Tuesday Aug.27.8.18<br/>feeling Better to day there is a fair amount of fat here too and Plenty of medicene and Beer<br/>Wednesday Aug 28.8.18<br/>feeling much Better to day went for a route march till 11.AM returned to camp 11.30.<br/><strike>Thursday Aug 29.8.18</strike><br/>had a good drink of Beer and had dinner slept all afternoon<br/><strike>friday Aug 30.8.18</strike><br/>had tea and turned in cool night.  I am feeling well<br/>Joe.M.Bolger</p>
