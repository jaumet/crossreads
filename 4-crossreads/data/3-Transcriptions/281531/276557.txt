<p>25/7/18<br/>Edinburgh<br/>the biggest proportion of the crowd would I am sure be foreigners to Edinburgh. After performing various commissions we returned to Abercromby Place and, after afternoon tea, we strolled into the gardens for a yarn. Before dinner we had great conversation en famillie and after dinner a little music.</p>
