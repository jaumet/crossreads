  
 Thursday 25.1.17 turned out 6.30AM had breakfast and tidied up not much doing Friday 26.1.17 turned out 6.30AM had breakfast. Fine warm morning two taubes over to day aircraft firing at them one appeared to be hit. circled around a lot Saturday 27.1.17. turned out 6.30AM had breakfast went a few messages 2.PM fine warm day not much doing made a new dug out . ordered to rejoin company had good issue of rum. Sunday 28.1.17 rejoined company stand to 5.30A.M two taubes over this morning dropped bombs
