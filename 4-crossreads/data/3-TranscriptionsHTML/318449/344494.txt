<p>It is every CO's duty  &amp;  his "honour" (sheref) to render the maximum assistance to guns  &amp;  carts in difficult ground. On such ground Limbers  &amp;  carts will have to be emptied if necessary</p>
<p>Infantry must assist artillery in the above manner. Art.  Officers  must inform Inf. Officers of extent of their requirements in turn for haulage, and Inf Officers must obey instructions issued under this head, issued by their COs or by Artillery Officers</p>
