<p>The Centre Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers<br/>28/10/15 &ndash;</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton &ndash;<br/>Do you think you could give Mrs. Hilder some help today? She has been to you before &amp; so feels shy about troubling you again. She seems badly in need of assistance. her children have no clothes &amp; not enough to eat.</p>
<p>I am going to make inquiries about the separation allowance from Liverpool</p>
<p>Yrs sincerely<br/>E. M. Wallack<br/>One child is to go to Hospital. A Doctor says she is ill from want of food</p>
