The work got very tedious & persistent. Monotonous was no name for it, & not being used to it we thought we were going to die. 
 The first week end I was Keystone Policeman, that is I had to stop in & look after the lines. All the others left for home so I had to spend the week end alone - very cheerful. The church societies at Casula have many tents for amusement & for writing letters. Concerts were given every night, some were good & others were very poor. About this time the soldiers were beginning to get rather noisy & had riots in town over German premises & germans in positions in the city.
