<p>Monday 16 April 1917.</p>
<p>Up at 5 p.m. Caught troop train at 7 pm from Spencer St.</p>
<p>Arrived in camp at 11.45 a.m.</p>
<p>Was marched over to races in afternoon and then back to camp at 6.15 pm.</p>
<p>Tea in the dark at 6.30 p.m.</p>
<p><s>Wandered round doing nothing at the racecourse</s>. Wrote a letter after tea &amp; turned in at 9.15.</p>
<p>Heard Pat Lynch has been wounded.</p>
