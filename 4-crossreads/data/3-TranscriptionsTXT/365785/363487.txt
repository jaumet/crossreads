 From Douglas Ferguson 13 Batt. 1662 Reg't. C Coy. Ward No. 7, Room 83 Mena House

 Would you please if possible send the few items mentioned, for which we will be very thankfull.  2 Razors 2 Shaving brushes, Soap &amp; Strop.  Cigarettes, 2 Tooth brushes &amp; paste. We are Gunner Fahy &amp; Private Ferguson late of No. 8 Ward.
