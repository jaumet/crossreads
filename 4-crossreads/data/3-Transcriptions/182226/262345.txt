<p>Friday 19 November, 1915</p>
<p>Went in for our pay &amp; came out to Valletta again in the afternoon.</p>
<p>We met a corporal out of our hut &amp; took him round with us. We went to the Olympia Club &amp; played billiards.</p>
<p>Sid &amp; the corporal went back to Camp, I stayed at the Hotel De France.</p>
