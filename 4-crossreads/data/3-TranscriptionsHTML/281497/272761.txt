<p>However I reached the Batt. &amp; it was not so bad as I thought it would be for the boys welcomed me back heartily.  I am not skiting when I say that they think a fair bit of me for I have always acted as fairly &amp; squarely to all sides as it was possible for me to do &amp; I am sure I have very few enemies.   I reported at once to our "orderly room" &amp; here I saw the Colonel &amp; Major they were surprised to see me, they knew nothing whatever of us being recalled, however the Colonel said he would make a note of it.</p>
