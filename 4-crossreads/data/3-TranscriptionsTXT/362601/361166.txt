 28 February, Friday 
 F. Lovely Day. I go to meeting Place &amp; meet Jack the N.Z. pianist &amp; L/C Nuttell from Dampreis show. Friend of Maud Hughes. We all get scaled &amp; I come back to Mess &amp; talk to Nuttell &amp; go through wards. 
 Recd letter from M.R.L. 
 I dream about all Skeletons. 
 1 March, Saturday 
 F. I am bit better &amp; get washing &amp; go to Dance. See Alice &amp; we have chat about scaling one another I am very lively &amp; play up considerably. 
 Recd letter from Jennie.
