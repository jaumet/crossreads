<p>24/7/17<br/>Marched from camped near Bray to Dernancourt and expect to entrain about a kilo from here.</p>
<p>25/7/17<br/>Rode on bikes to Rebempre about 225 kilo's in search of 3 lost fullerphones.</p>
<p>26/7/17<br/>Entrained at Edge Hill siding Dernancourt at 1945 tonight</p>
<p>27/7/17<br/>Left Edge Hill at 8.30 p.m. and arrived at Amiens</p>
