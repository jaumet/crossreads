<p>No 2 Camp<br/>PARK House<br/>Salisbury,<br/>13.4.19</p>
<p>Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George,</p>
<p>Here once again on the 13th I have been sent from Bulford to this Camp, this one where I landed from Egypt &amp; later from France.  I come over here on a better job as W/O in charge of Nursing &amp; Treatment.  It is a very clean &amp; compact Camp &amp; is about 1/3 of the work at Bulford.  I have a room to myself, a servant &amp; every privilege just the same as an officer.  I have not yet</p>
