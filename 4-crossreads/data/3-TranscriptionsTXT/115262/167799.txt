 Information got from &quot;Tommies&quot; who are here from Salonika:&ndash; 
 On Saturday 30th June at 4 a.m.&nbsp;&frac12; a division of &quot;Tommies&quot; arrived here from Salonika. Up to this date 30,000 &quot;Tommies&quot; have left Salonika &amp; have come to this camp. Some are now at Gaza. The French troops have taken&nbsp; or&nbsp;a this  over the position there. These &quot;Tommies&quot; were in the trenches in France for 6 mths. &amp; were then taken to Salonika where&nbsp; they merely held the line  8 miles from Bulgarian frontier. Only one advanse was attempted &amp; in this on Hill 535 the 22nd division lost 8000 men so they withdres. They believe the position to be impregnable.
