 THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 4, 1919. 
 Fine & hot. Lopped in Top paddock 
 & Mountain in morning and cutting 
 cotton bush in the afternoon. 
 Linda came down in afternoon 
 and we had some tea in Wheelers 
 Tickle poisoning in Wheelers & 
 Middle paddock. 
 Barry came out & got here for 
 lunch.
