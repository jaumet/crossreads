<p>31st October (Tuesday)</p>
<p>At mid-day after removing a large number of wounded we were ordered back to A.D.S. as Turk Post would not be used again on account of the sledges.  At A.D.S. we had to pull down a large dug out and were then sent out to McCormack's Post to assist in digging new accomodation for a dressing station to be used when the advance comes.
