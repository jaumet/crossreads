
Sunday 9/9/17Bean here all day for accounts of Lagnicourt & Bullecourt.Monday 10/9/17Handed over to Currie & proceeded to Ca&euml;stre on job of Reinf Camp.Tuesday 11/9/17Arrived at Ca&euml;stre with 100 adv. Party of 26th Bn commenced on camp, wrote to Doff - very sick.Wednesday 12/9/172100 all ranks marched in saw General Smythe, trouble with water supply.Thursday 13/9/17Straightening up Camp generally things now shaping well.Friday 14/9/17Commenced Schools - others route marching.
