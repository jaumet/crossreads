  
 March 1918 Sunday 24 
 Messines Arose at 6.30 and prepared breakfast, worked on souveniers until 10 pm. Another glorious day, Charlie Adey and Gunn went to W.L. At 12.15 pm orders came across wire that M.G.s had to report home again At 1.30pm we packed up all our kits in readiness to "Emshee" to another Bty possie near Oostenhaven Wood. 11.45 pm arrived to our destination and took over from some fellows of the 15th Bty .Damn rotten possie with no dugouts.
