 Naval Signal 
 From - Berrima 
 To - Flag 
 Date - 21 9 14 
 Time - 6.50 PM 
 From Brigadier. &nbsp;Governor surrendered with four Officers fifteen Officials Thirty Seven White Non Commissioned Officers and men and one hundred and twenty natives  all of whom are at Rabaul . &nbsp;Governor staying night at Herbertshohe reports here early tomorrow. &nbsp;Half Company Infantry at Toma to receive reinforcements sent from Wilhelmshafen
