 [June 1918] 23 Sun Been to church 24 Mon Another full moon but the hun as been very quite with his aeroplanes 25 Tues He threatened to have the town of Rouen in flames this moonshine but so far so good 26 Wed I have, at this place, as at all others made good but I daresay that I will soon get Marching orders
