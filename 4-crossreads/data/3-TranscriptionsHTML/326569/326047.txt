<p>east wind sprang up &amp; developed into a real Darling River dust storm, also making temperature very cold.  Just before bed walked down to Ludgershall for cup of coffee.</p>
<p>Saturday, May 6th<br/>Very cold this morning but nice fine sunshine although windy.  After dinner went to Perham for hot shower &amp; after tea to Perham to Y.M.C.A.  Still cold.</p>
<p>Monday, 7/5/17<br/>Frosty early in morning. Practising bombing morning parade &amp; afternoon ditto.  After tea went to Ludgershall Y.M.C.A. for some stamps.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 8/5/17<br/>Very cold.  Making new bombing course during day.  After tea an aeroplane fell 1&frac12; miles from camp &amp; was smashed.</p>
