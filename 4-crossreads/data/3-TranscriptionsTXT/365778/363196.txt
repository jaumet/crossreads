
Saturday 19/5/17Training, and Sports Football, tugs-0f war, and running. Officers had a jump.Sunday 20/5/17Wrote to Doff, missed church parade. Robinson to Paris for sports material.Monday 21/5/17Arrangements re dinner, played football against 7th Fld Coy & won.Tuesday 22/5/17Rode to Senlis re staff with Bde, very stiff & sore.Wednesday 23/5/17Sports meeting in afternoon, visited by General Smythe; letter from Doff.
