No. 2518Pte. J.V. Armstrong5th Rein., 39th Batt.10th Inf. Bn.A.I.F.On Active Service Abroad.
Armstrong.
Some Boxing. Gambling a treat on board. Got game of draughts, dominoes, 3 sets of gloves other games given out to us.Sunday, 12th Nov.Getting into Australian Bight which means rougher. Result of Melbourne Cup by wireless also other news. Church parade spars. Very rough in the evening but not sick yet.
