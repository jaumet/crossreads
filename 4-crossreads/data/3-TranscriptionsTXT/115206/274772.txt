
July 1918Sunday 21
Frechencourt.Weather wet and showery. Rode my bike to Villers Bocage, Rainneville, Cardonette, Coisy and St Gratien. At the last place I ran across Ken Gun and Palmer, at the 5th Divvie Sig School.Stayed for lunch and had a quiet chat with Ken.Walked to Behencourt with Smithy and had a nice little snack.Weather fine, full moon. Had some polite visitors during the evening.
