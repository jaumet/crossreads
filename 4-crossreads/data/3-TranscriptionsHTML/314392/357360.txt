<p>Amongst us which caused some with sore eyes. After a bit he sent over liquid fire and incendiary shells. Gee what a night Messives blown up at 12.<br/>6th Bombardment still on. Boys are now past the miseries, the object of the attack. Fritz continues to shell camp.<br/>8th left camp at 7pm for trenches, with a convoy of water, bombs and ammunition. Arrived at Bayles Farm at 8.15 but could go no further as Fritz was counter attacking the 13 Bde. The din was awful. Race into a barrage.  Several men and animals knocked. Dead lay all over the place</p>
