Got our new Colonel today he comes from the 4th Bn. does not bring too good a name Stacey's his name, when Major Jacobs went away our band played him out to the tune of Auld Lang Syne. 
26th. Last night all the clocks were put on an hour, so we lose an hours sleep by it.
Sunday again we had a Brigade Church Parade, but could not hear a word, where we were, right at the rear of the Battn.
After the parade was dismissed there was a football match between 1st 2 nd Bns
