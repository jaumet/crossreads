 Monday July 9 Inspection &amp; address given by General Royston. Some 3000 men in Isolation camp Riding exercise in afternoon. Smash of two planes to day. Both Pilots killed Tuesday July 10 Riding again today. Was thrown &amp; dragged across sand with foot in stirrup. Lucky was not hurt. Concert &amp; sing song in evening. Getting more accustomed to hot weather. Wednesday July 11 First pay day drew 350 Piastres 1 Piastre = 2&frac12; in English money Usual camp routine Thursday July 12 Very funny today.
