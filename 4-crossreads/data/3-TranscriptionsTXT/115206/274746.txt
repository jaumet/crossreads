
June 1918Tuesday 25
In Billets. ErronvilleWeather fine and cleanUsual routine of stables etc. watered near by. Walked with Smithy to Camoun through Bailleul. Enjoyed a decent feed in a decent shack. Village about 10 Kilos distant. From top of hill  [indecipherable] flashes of bombs [indecipherable]About Flexicourt way.Received parcel from Dot Watson also one from O.J.
