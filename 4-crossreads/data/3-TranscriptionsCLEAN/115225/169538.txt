 Mar 8  th    Fritz was over here with bombers early this morning - We took the labour corps party to work in the morning but did'nt have to wait for them. we went back to Park & cleaned the lorry - Frank & Bud walked up to the B.A.C. at Neuve Eglise for the mail in the afternoon. 
   
 Mar 9  th    We did'nt go out to-day - Last night one of the 12  th   brigade lorries had an accident at the bottom of the Bailleul hill by the Hazebrouck road. the drivers (Poor old Blue was one of them) were both killed & also the dispatch rider. Whom they were trying to avoid when they ran into a tree & went over into the ditch. In the evening Frank & I went to Bailleul & saw the Smart Set at the concert hall 
   
 Mar 10  th    Sunday - We went to take the labour co y  to work, but they did'nt want us  they dont work on Sundays - We got back & greased the lorry. Fritz put two shells into Steenwerck railhead to-day killing two diggers - our time advanced 1 hour last night -
