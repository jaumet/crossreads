  
 From - Brigadier. 
 To - Rear-Admiral C. 
  12th Septr. '14.  
 (7-5 a.m.)  
 Have already recommended withdrawal of Troops from Wireless Station to Kaba Kaul, thence to Herbertshohe as garrison.  Infantry now at Herbertshohe to come on board "Berrima", which should then proceed to Rabaul to carry out original intention.  If approved please instruct Beresford.
