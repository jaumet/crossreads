<p>shortage of nourishing food.   At one estaminet I found a wholesome red wine that was a good tonic.  Nearly all these places were very cleanly kept, &amp; the wife who is absolute boss invariably wears a cheerful face.  The husband who <strike>is invariably</strike> is generally an old soldier is seen but not heard.  He knows his pace as the under dog in the establishment &amp; is supremely happy if he is allowed to sit in a quiet corner occasionally &amp; discuss a glass with the local postman or gendarme.  On one occasion, I saw an old warrior harnessing up his horse &amp; cart in order</p>
