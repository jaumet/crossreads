  
 Last two shows at the Olympia - Second House proves most enthusiastic of the week. April 7th Take 10.30 a.m. train for Manchester - Arrive there at noon - After two or three hours tramping in the rain (which is not devoid of humour) we at last find lodgings - Very comfortable we hope to be in this new place - A trip out towards Cheadle - Early to bed. April 8th Report at Theatre Royal 9 a.m. - Rehearsal & Preparations for Evening - Meet new friends.
