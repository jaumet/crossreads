<p>Thursday 17th Aug.</p>
<p>Went on duty at dispensary alternate days with a Warrant Officer dispenser &amp; although he had the rank I did most of the [indecipherable].  Visited town on Wednesday the day the 5th Div. left.<br/>We left this morning.  Reveille at 5 a.m. left camp at 9 a.m. (some of the hawkers of fruit cried as we left) &amp; entrained after 2 mile march at 10.<br/>Left at 10.30.</p>
