  
 Tuesday 19 September 1916 Nothing doing all day;  on picquet at night. Dick got two parcels from home. 
 Wednesday 20 September 1916 John Bay and I got 48 hours leave to Port Said. Went to see Mr. Trelawny but found he was in England. Tried to get rooms in Continental Hotel but it was full so went to Great Brittian Hotel.  Rotten place!  Went to Empire Club and picture show in evening.
