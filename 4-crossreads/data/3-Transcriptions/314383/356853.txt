<p>Mar 31<br/>Explored the city again today picked up two Jewish boys who spoke English as our guides.  Looked over the old Turkish Barracks on the further side of the city.  They cover an extensive area of ground &amp; are well built with fine parade grounds inside.  The place is now used as an ASC depot by us.  We strolled into a native dancing hall &amp; there booked seats.  The smell of the natives in the heat was terrific, but nevertheless we got good seats &amp; saw the show, which commenced by two Arabs striking up some very wonderful music on their native fiddles and drums.
