<p>He asked me to write to him.  He said that he had not been in the garden for 6 days but God had made him come today to translate what we said to his father.  He intends being a Doctor.  Whilst talking about the cinema he said the "lightning made the pictures".  The old man gave us some strange flowers from which scent is made &amp; also showed us the engine he had for pumping the irrigating water.  They had never heard the word 'calf'  but said that their two cows had sons.  We had a ride on one of their asses.</p>
