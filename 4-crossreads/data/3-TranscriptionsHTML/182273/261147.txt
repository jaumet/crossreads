<p>1918 &nbsp; July</p>
<p>18 THUR &nbsp;Fine day. Blowing hard</p>
<p>Posted letter to Amy<sup>&radic;</sup>.</p>
<p>Amm. going up. Very quiet.</p>
<p>Writing at night.</p>
<p>Heard of news of Foch&#39;s</p>
<p>offensive.</p>
<p>19 FRI &nbsp;Fine day. Posted Card to</p>
<p>Amy Mc<sup>&radic;</sup>. &amp; Aussie to May. Very</p>
<p>Quiet. Went into St. Omer at</p>
<p>6 P.M. Stayed overnight. had</p>
<p>a good time with Cic. Rec.</p>
<p>letter from Tense.</p>
<p>20 SAT &nbsp;Fine day. very warm.</p>
<p>Market day in St Omer. French</p>
<p>celebrating French success</p>
<p>17.000 prisoners &amp; 230 guns.</p>
<p>Heavy storm in aft. Had</p>
<p>a good time. Aitken &amp;</p>
<p>Snowy went mad. Drove</p>
<p>[cart?] home. left Aitken in</p>
<p>town. arr camp 11.30 PM</p>
