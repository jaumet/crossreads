<p>right to our door, we only had to walk a few yards &amp; we were in our huts, it was a bonza lift, we passed through Bailleul she is a deserted town now, all the one time life &amp; bustle of her lively little streets having disappeared, this was on account of the Germans bombarding it so heavily, all the civilians were ordered to move out at once, &amp; just as well they did for old Fritz appears to have knocked a good few houses, shops, &amp; churches  <br/>rotten, he dropped some big shells in the square &amp; as we passed through they were busy filling them in, old Fritz has got a lot to answer for one of these fine days.<br/>Things seem to be in a very tangled state round this part of the front they have got some of our Light Horse &amp;</p>
