 7. Sunday Heavy showers.  Finished Macedonia sketches.  Sig. Siga. Signora Bambacionis called for tea &ndash; Read History. 
 8. Monday Sunshine now &amp; again.  Went to Miss Hodges Indian tea at Minerva.  Terribly boring.  Home &amp; washed hair.  Dr &amp; Mrs Clark called 
 9. Tuesday Such a dense fog, lights necessary at 10 a.m.  Wrote article for S.M.H.  Pottered had a snooze etc. 
 10. Wednesday Showery &amp;  grey.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10 returned home at 4 &amp;  pottered on odds &amp;  ends.
