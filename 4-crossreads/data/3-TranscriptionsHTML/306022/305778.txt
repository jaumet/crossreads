<p>The Tommies, I believe have all gone to France. The next reinforcements for our battallion go tomorrow, so that I suppose we will be going fairly soon.</p>
<p>Saturday 18th<br/>Snowed last night; about 2 inches on ground this morning. No drill done and nothing of any importance occurred.</p>
