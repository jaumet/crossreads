<p>25 March 1918 Monday<br/>Bethlehem   Went again to Church of Nativity &amp; the Milk Grotto the latter place crowded with pilgrims with BABIES, you know the legend i.e. no need for Foster Mothers if the Mother goes to Marys Milk Grotto.<br/>Later went to Typhus Hospital run by Armenians the Doctor was at QUINNS POST GALLIPOLI opposite me Aug 7th 1915. <strike> His saddle was lost after we evacuated</strike>-   Pretty Syrian nurses here who had nursed our prisoners in Jerusalem before we came.</p>
