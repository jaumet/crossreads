No HQ sigs were hit, but a couple of coy sigs were killed.<br/>While at Baucourt I paid another visit to Bapaume &amp; had a look at the site where the town hall was. Fritz has blown up some dugouts &amp; cellars  in around Bapaume, including the town hall, the place was blown right up, not even a cellar left, there were a good number of deaths.<br/> Le Barque April 8th  17<br/>After being relieved by the 53 rd at Daignies, we retired to Helu, there I slept two nights in one of the stables of the chateau.
