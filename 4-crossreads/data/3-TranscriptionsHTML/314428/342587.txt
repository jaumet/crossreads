<p>The shorthand class is now in full swing and some of the students are coming on fast. The colonel is also making good progress.</p>
<p>The distance travelled for the 24 hrs. ending noon today is 312 miles, an average of 12.2 miles per hour.</p>
<p>Wednesday April 30 1919. 171.<br/>Wet and stormey with a high sea running.  The ocean is covered with white foam.</p>
<p>The following has been posted up today by the ship's officers.</p>
<p>Longitude 39&deg; 53' South Longitude 55&deg; 50' East. Course South 87 East. Distance travelled 315 miles. Steaming time 23 h. 33 m. Average speed 13.4 miles. Temperature 62&deg; Wind West. Total steaming time: 146 hours 24 min. Distance travelled from Cape Town 1872 miles. Average speed 12.8 miles. To Adelaide 3873 miles.</p>
