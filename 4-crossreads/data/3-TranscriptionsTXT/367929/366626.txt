 May 1915 20 Thursday After 6am went into bivouac in valley. Bullets lob in our dugouts and sound very dangerous. Casualties 20th 2 killed 4 wounded. Armistice white flag by Turks. We lined trenches at night 
 21 Friday Went to Anzac Cove for a swim &ndash; 1st bath since landing and 1st time boots and socks off. Casualties in valley 4 wounded. 
 22 Sat Fairly quiet day. We landed and sent up 2 Japanese bombs howitzers. High general led on to our beach for armistice. Cas 1 killed and 2 wounded. Wrote a letter to M.V. no incidents. Turk dead piled in acres. Rained 4th rain since leaving Str of Victoria
