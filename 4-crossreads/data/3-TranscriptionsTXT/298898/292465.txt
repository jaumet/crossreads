Tuesday 13th Feb. 1917Fine weather all day. Sent in my particulars to the Colonel to see if he would recommend me for a W.O. so now will sit back to see if anything comes of it. I fear there is little hope as there is great competition for promotion in the A.M.C. &amp; previous service counts etc.
Wednesday 14th March 1917Heavy rain during the night &amp; more during the day. Very little doing altho' some new guns have come along to disturb our peace by shaking the place to pieces. Good news concerning 'Bagdad' being taken. We seem to have retrieved our failures there, also news from home that Ian has a son.
