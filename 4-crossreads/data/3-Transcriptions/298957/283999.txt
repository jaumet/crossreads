<p>23rd April (Easter Sunday)<br/>Fairly hot. No Church Parade Sports meeting held to make arrangements for a good time on Anzac Day.</p>
<p>24th April (Easter Monday)<br/>Hot. Carpentering and Lecture in morning. Enemy aeroplane flew over the other day dropping a few bombs on Serapeum. It is believed that a few were killed.</p>
<p>25th April (Tuesday &ndash; Anzac Day)<br/>After carrying an appendicitis case to Road Head North where he was met by an Ambulance Waggon, we came back to a fine dinner of Pineapples,</p>
