<p>1915<br/>Jan. 24 Sun.<br/>All as busy as previous days.<br/>Patients streamed in from the three Field Hospitals all day. We are looking after all the hospital work for the whole of the Troops here.<br/>Worked in the Orderly Tent at camp all day up to tea-time &ndash; Worked at Mena House (Col. Nash) from 6.15 to 10 p.m.<br/>The complaints about food bear fruit and tucker is improving.<br/>Am working longer hours than ever before.<br/>Hospital inspected to-day by Gen. Bridges, (G.O.C.) Australian Troops &ndash; who pronounced everything as excellent.</p>
