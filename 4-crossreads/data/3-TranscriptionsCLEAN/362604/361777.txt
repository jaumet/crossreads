
Australian Red CrossBulford20/10/18
Dear Dad,
Your letters of 21/7, 28/7,4/8, 25/8 have arrived & I'll answer them before I knock off for the evening to enjoy myself.  I went to Salisbury on Wednesday & visited the Close first.  I helped Mr. Freemantle tidy up the lawn & paths & trim the creepers around the House.  Autumn is well on now & the leaves are falling very fast.
