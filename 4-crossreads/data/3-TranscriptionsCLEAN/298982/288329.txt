  
 Fri 6th Some bombardment at night & action on left 
 Sat 7th Things very lively early Heavy musketry rifle gun & battleship fire. Many dead brought down to Morgue a false attack early to screen operations on left cost the 1st & 2nd Light Horse dear on Popes & Quinns also the Welsh Fulisiers up Pope's gully & the Light Horse on the left Walkers. Our little corpl did not turn up all day & it is said he has gone over parapet with his brother who was a bomb thrower. The losses are pretty heavy on this front at least 26 in Mortuary & 8 or more
