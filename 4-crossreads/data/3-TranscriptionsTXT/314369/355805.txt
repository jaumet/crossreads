 30 March 1915 Tuesday 
 Have the pleasure of meeting several old friends namely Leuit Stan Perry   &amp;  Sgt Jack Holman   &amp;  Cpl Nelson all from Auburn   &amp;  In our Bat. All very glad to see us. 
 1915 March 31 Wednesday 
 13th Bat out field firing   &amp;  Outpost work so have the camp to ourselves. do a bit of drill   &amp;  have a half day off Make first trip to Cairo. Find it the dirtiest   &amp;  most immoral place on the Globe. Have a delightful dinner of Soup, Roast, Fish, chicken, salad,   &amp;  dessert Return to camp 9.30pm
