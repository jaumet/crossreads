
Tuesday Jan 11th My  Birthday (23rd)a very calm day : hot : Revellie 6 am : congratulations from my cobbers (Birthday) & Just make a small sailing boat we had passed early in the morning : parade for physical drill 10 am : attended physical class after tea : slept on deck : no land sighted & only the one boat as above :
Wednesday Jan 12thA hot windy day : Hazy & a big swell on : We were supposed to have passed through the straits of Babel Mandeb early hours of morning. All day we keptRevellie 6 am
