<p>Australian Red Cross<br/>Bulford<br/>26/5/18</p>
<p>Dear Dad,</p>
<p>I've just completed a letter to Mum &amp; now I'll proceed to answer your three letters of the same dates as Mum.  I am greatly pleased that you are so very much freer of those dreadful screws.  I should not have liked Georgie's task of carrying that boat to Narrabeen.  It is a dreadful distance to walk without carrying anything.  I envy you your nice little outings at Dee Why, but D.V. we'll be Home very soon &amp; then we can make up for lost time.  I was just wondering &amp; had forgotten</p>
