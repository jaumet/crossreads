<p>8 August, Friday</p>
<p>Mum &amp; I drive to Manly &amp; do a lot of shopping, meet Percy Hickson &amp; Amy.</p>
<p>We drive to Dee Why. I play piano all night &amp; amuse Dad &amp; Mum. Ellis &amp; George out. Recd April letters back from Eng &amp; France.</p>
<p>Wrote to M.R.L. 18</p>
<p>9 August, Saturday</p>
<p>Doris Park brings in music "Till we meet". We chop the tree down &amp; I work like a nigger. I wash &amp; go to Otto's place for Tra &amp; Talk.</p>
<p>Recd invitation to Party on 21.8.19.</p>
