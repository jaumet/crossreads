
Flanders5-7-17Dear JudgeI was awfully glad to get your letter of 23rd May - more especially as my letters from Australia have been few and far between of late. You are one of the most regular of my correspondents and I enjoy getting your letters more than anything.I am afraid I must have made a mistake, when I wrote you that I had seen Keith with General Holmes, when the latter came down to make some presentations. I was some distance away and seeing a tall figure with Holmes, asked if an officer near me if same was Ferguson. I know he wasn't certain about it, but thought it was - I am awfully glad to hear that Keith is better, and hopes soon all will be
