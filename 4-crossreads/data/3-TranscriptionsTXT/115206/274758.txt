
July 1918Sunday 7
Vaux sur Somme.2nd Battery PositionLaid gun line and fixed up a couple of emergency lines to53rd and 55th, in company of Tom Code.Laid a line to O. Pot. 4pm and generally squared upeverything.Weather fine. Fritz still shelling Vaux and the vicinity.Battery registered Heard today Mr Sheedy was comingback to the Bty. Smithy arrived back from Paris leave, upto time. Hear we are only in for a week. Capt Nicholsona little better. Had a letter from Bully, he's going home.
