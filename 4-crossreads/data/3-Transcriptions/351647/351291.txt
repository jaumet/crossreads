<p>Things are really much better.  I haven't much to tell you.</p>
<p>I saw dear old Brian Mac one day, he has his 2nd star &amp; looks well.  We had a great little yarn.  I haven't seen Bert for ages.</p>
<p>My pal Tansey also has a star up, it seems to be my fate to see all or most of my best pals pass me in the race but I'm not a bit military or smart or good at bossing.</p>
<p>In a job like my present one I'm pretty fair but having those 8 fellows here &amp; bossing them bored me to tears &amp; so does being senior N.C.O. down here.</p>
<p>I have had quite a number of letters of yours to acknowledge;  they went to Infantry &amp; then circled round &amp; came here in a heap, 5 of them.
