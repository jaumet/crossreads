<p>[Thursday 7 September 1916]</p>
<p>Cold grey morning   X rayed today quick &amp; lively everything is done, will try &amp; get a photo of my chest for you.<br/>There are hundreds of Australian Officers in England- &amp; many thousands of wounded men.<br/><strike>Expect to see Laura this afternoon &amp; post this.</strike></p>
