<p>and were about to leave when we ran into a bunch of them.  We collared one each, the others bolted so we got off with our catch.  The citizens were extremely nasty &amp; tried to raise Cain but we pulled out our revolvers which quietened things.  These bucks told the interpreter that they were down from Baku &amp; had been wandering about for months living on a handful of grain a day.</p>
<p>Packing up to move on tomorrow, we take over a wireless group of one Sgt. &amp; 4 men.  When we looked for our camels we discovered that they had bolted without their wages when they heard we were bound for Bijah.</p>
