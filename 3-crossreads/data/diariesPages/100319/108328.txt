28/8/18 Dompierre
Awakened early to speak on the ?phone to the General and found that conditions were abnormally quiet on our front. Decided to push the line forward once more and to use B D Coys for so doing. Issued the necessary orders and having taken a very little breakfast set off to a new headquarters up near Dompierre which Harricks had just occupied. In the meanwhile Chalmers pushed ahead having occasional bombing scraps, until he reached Black Wood. From our headquarters we could see the bursts of the bombs they threw and Fritz was sending up flares from in front of Herbancourt and using machine guns. Chalmers soon established his line and connected with the 21st. Harricks then went through the village of Dompierre and the 24th connected up with him. We now had a Corporal of the German guards
