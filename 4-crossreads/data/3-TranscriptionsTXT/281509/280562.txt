 1916 December 5 Tuesday Up betimes.  After breakfast move off once more for our allotted stations. A different track on duck-boards thro, Delville Wood. much more comfortable. Rain hail &amp; sleet &amp; snow as we go in enough to wet us. Our squad elected for advanced dressing post. Arrive in front line about 2 p.m. 12 in a German dugout, pushed for room. Could not make tea. Fritz no sport, would not let us gather any fuel. One carry only
