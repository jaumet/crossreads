<p>1st A.G.H.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Egypt.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       June 19th  1916.<br/>My dearest Mother,<br/>Just a few lines to wish you Many Happy Returns of your Birthday. Hope I shall be with you next year. I really thought I would this time but the <u>powers that be</u> willed it otherwise. I hope you get lots of presents &amp; have a real happy day. I have just finished a long letter to you all but this is just for your dear old self.<br/>I'm enclosing a few snaps that I took about here a few weeks back. <strike>Some</strike> The little spots that are very dear to me, where my beloved &amp; I spent many a happy hour. The other one no doubt you will know.<br/>I'm having a day &ndash; off duty. I was looking very tired &amp; feeling so yesterday so Matron decided to give me to-day off    &amp;  I'm</p>
