<p>We have a lot of arguments with the A.M.C. [Australian Medical Corps] staff &amp; there seemed to be serious trouble brewing. Everything has been patched up now &amp; we are the very best of friends.</p>
<p>Last night we had an open air concert on the Fore-deck just under the Bridge. About 50 men sat &amp; sang all the songs that they knew. The Nurses &amp; some of the Officers were seated on the steps &amp; along the Promenade Deck.</p>
