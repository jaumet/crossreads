<p>Oct. 23 Saturday 1915</p>
<p>Working as usual, after work Lieut Playdell &amp; I went for long walk.<br/>Got some post cards for Harry wrote Home to Maude &amp; Harry,</p>
<p>Oct. 24 Sunday 1915<br/>Church both morning &amp; night<br/>Afternoon went to 17 Gen Hosp to see Lieu Drummond.<br/>This morning at 6 AM went for a swim with Pethard &amp;Watkins.<br/>After Service went to Y. M. C. A. to hear Chaplain Green speak about temptation. very good address.</p>
<p>Oct 25 Monday 1915<br/>working all day.<br/>Lieu Cunningham returned from Cairo. Went for train ride to Noosa Park with D. Playdell.<br/>Posted letters &amp; Post Cards Home to Maude &amp; Harry.</p>
