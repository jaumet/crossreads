 Sunday 16th:  Up the line to Dickebusch. Joined Btn. 
 Wednesday 19th:  Btn moved up to front line. Thursday 20th:  Btn "hopped over" this morning &amp; took every objective. Saturday 22nd:  Relieved early this morning. Sunday 23rd:  Came back from Ypres to near Reninghelst. Sunday 24th:  Shifted to near Abeile. Friday 28th:  Back to Halifax camp near Reninghelst Saturday 29th:  To Ypres Thursday Oct. 4th: To Railway Wood, thence to Westhoek Ridge Friday 5th:  Back to [indecipherable] at Railway Wood. Tuesday 6th:  Battalion hopped over this morning Wednesday 10th:  Came back to Belgian Chateau Thursday 11th:  Btn Relieved
