  
 Saturday 1/7/16 Route march of about 8 miles to the village of Le Panc [?].  All L Cpls in Corp reduced to the rank of Private.  In the distance could see the enemy aeroplanes under fire of our aircraft guns. 
 Sunday 2/7/16 Our Artillery very active the last few night.  Commenced firing very early last night & made things warm the whole of the night.  Church parade 9.30 AM. 
 Monday 3.7.16 Appointed L/Cpl & transferred to C Section.  Piquet Corporal for the day. Capt Lind transferred to 14th F. Amb & Major McCartney attached in his stead.  Practical experience of a gas attack
