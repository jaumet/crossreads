
Octobre 29Section working between Westhoek Ridge & Anzac Ridge strengthening "Pill Box" & building splinter proof shelter for 329 Siege Battery. Artillery not so active as usual.
Octobre 30Bad day for the Section. While working on shelter for 329th Siege Battery 7 sappers were killed and four wounded. Killed Sap Rae, Busby, Tuffice, Lyall, McShie, Harman, Burton.  Burton lived for about 1 hour.Wounded - Sap Noss, Willey (leg wounds), Rogers & Arnold. Rogers and Arnold were seriously wound & I have fears that they may not live. The shell burst right amongst us & 11 out of 13 were hit.
