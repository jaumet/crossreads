  
 3am got 4 letters 1 from Kitty & full of cheer & cheek. Parcelled up Souvenirs etc for K. sending by Courtney Sgt for posting in London. 
 [A space has been left in the page indicating a gap in the diary, then towards the bottom of the page is written:] Much wet weather followed. nightly trench fatigues, on mining & trench reconstructing. Left Ypres first week October.
