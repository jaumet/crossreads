<p>September<br/>24th.   Two Zeplins were brought down near London by anti-aircraft guns &amp; I saw the first of the two fall in flames.<br/>The reports returned from the Esher school &amp; I was informed that I came out top of the school.<br/>27th.  Left for another leave in London &amp; returned on the 1st October.<br/>1st October. 1916<br/>Another Zeplin was brought down near London by gun fire.</p>
