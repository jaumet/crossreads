<p>September 1917<br/>Thursday 20th<br/>Went to Kafa in the morning to get some canteen stuff.  It was nearly dark when I got back.  The Sergeants mess got in a supply of stuff.</p>
<p>Friday 21st<br/>It is still very windy &amp; dusty.  There is nothing much to do here excepting feeding grooming &amp; watering.  I took over SQMS in the afternoon.   We are expecting a turn out  at night to see how long it takes to form up in full marching order with 48 hrs feed &amp; rations limbers &amp; waggons loaded.</p>
<p>Saturday 22nd<br/>The dust was not so bad today.</p>
<p>Sunday 23rd<br/>Went over to the 7th in the morning  Got some mail English.  Wrote a letter home.  There is a Brigade band now &amp; they entertain the various camps here.  The weather is fairly warm but there is always dust in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Monday 24th<br/>Usual sort of day.  Nothing doing.</p>
<p>Tuesday 25th<br/>The Regiment paraded in the morning, full marching order &amp; the Brigadier inspected us.  He said he was well pleased with the turnout.  We are expecting one at night.  In the afternoon we played</p>
