
9th July Left camp for Calais to get [indecipherable] arrived there same night.11th Had a good time in Calais and am now returning by road to Neuve Eglise14th Arrived at Neuve Eglise.  Beautiful day19th Left Neuve Eglise for Vieux Bergin where we arrived about 1pm Beautiful day27th Today we were reviewed by General Plummer who is in Command of the 2nd Army. Also attended a lecture rendered by the Rev. Arch. Deacon Ward in the dangers of V. Disease. It was indeed a splendid address.3rd August Wet and miserable.
