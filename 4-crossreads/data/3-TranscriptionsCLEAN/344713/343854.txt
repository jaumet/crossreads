 Sat. 4th. Sept. 1915. Sighted Aden at dinnertime today. Never stopped, the "Persia" overhauled us. Don't think much of the Arabian Coast. 
 Sunday. 5th: - Out of sight of mainland passing a lot of islands. The "Persia" overhauled us again. She is carrying Victorian troops. Only sighted one warship - a converted cruiser "The Empress of Asia" barely moving along. 
 Thursday. 9th Sept. 1915. Anchored at Port Suez 9 A.M. Cannot land till next morning after getting everything ready. Boys catching sharks - got one over 10 feet. 
 Friday 10th: - Landed at Port Tewfik & entrained & after enduring our long train journey & experiences with the niggers arrived at Zietoum & put up there in the hutments just outside Heliopolis. 
 Sunday.  19th. Sept: - Getting fed up with Egypt. the Gyppos etc. Have had all the Cairo I want & most of us are anxious to get away from this everlasting drill.
