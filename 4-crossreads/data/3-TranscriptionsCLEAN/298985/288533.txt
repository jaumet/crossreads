The 2 Observers jumped out & looked very quaint comeing slowly down in there white parachoots. The blazeing balloon seemed almost to touch them as it fell leaveing a great straight coloum of smoke 
 Wednesday 14th Moved of at 9.30 & have camped in an open hill side near a small ruined town Hammill I believe it is or was.  There is a fine big water hole here sort of swamp. We had a fine swim this afternoon
