<p>I was O/C Codford Picquet one evening (Saturday) from 6 to 10. I stayed at George Hotel after handing my party over until they were ready to return. We returned in a heavy thunderstorm.</p>
<p>Yesterday afternoon I borrowed Capt. Macpherson&#39;s bicycle &amp; rode through several villages to Wilton and then to Salisbury. At Wilton I inspected the beautiful church erected by Earl of Pembroke at a cost of over &pound;80,000. It contains 4 pillars from Pompeii, mosaics from various places, stained glass of great antiquity &amp; other fine work.</p>
