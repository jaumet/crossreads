
6 - joined the army - "confined to Barracks" is one.I was very sorry I did not get to Castos. I had the garry one day and everything arranged - and no leave was given owing to the expecting imediate orders to move. This is a boring letter - tomorrow I shall be at Alexandria 10am 22. Jan 1916 -We are having a lovely smooth trip - and it is much warmer now.Much love to you all - I long for letters and hope you will write me often - all your news. I expect I shall get some tomorrow - as our mail has been kept in Alexandria - also that all the parcels that have been sent me will be there.Always your devoted daughter & sisterFMcMillanI am glad about Sir George ReidI have not heard from Mother since 2 Nov - & the girls the 7th Nov - so I really know nothing about you all.
