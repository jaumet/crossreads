All this was leaving us very short handed, which meant so much less amtn. with each gun.<br/>On the night of the 25th we drew rations &amp; water &amp; proceeded back to our gun store.  There the whole attack was explained to the gun commanders &amp; we were given maps of the ground over which we were to move.  At about 3 a.m. we got out our gear &amp; started to move forward to the front line.<br/>Shells were dropping occasionally but did no damage.
