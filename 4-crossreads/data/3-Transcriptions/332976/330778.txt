<p>December 1916<br/>(27th continued)   ascertain the extent of repairs required &amp; after dinner I distributed the party to the billets &amp; we started on the work.  There is a lot of work to be done, consisting of chiefly the repair of bunks, construction of extra bunks &amp; accomidation for the troops in general.<br/>28th   We continued with our work to-day &amp; have made considerable progress.  To-night I &amp; three others went to the vaudeville show about a mile distant from our billet.  Being held every night &amp; financed by the Australian Comforts Fund, it is a great success &amp; appreciated by the boys after the spell of the trenches.</p>
