 April 1916 Colombo 17th April   We crossed the "line" about 2 P.M to-day.  We are still having beautiful weather, but a few heavy showers have fallen during the last two days. We passed a ship about 10 A.M. which seemed to be taking a peculiar course, first travelling N.W. &amp; then suddenly changed due North &amp; passing about two miles astearn. 19th   We sighted land early this morning &amp; arrived at Colombo about 9 a.m. &amp; anchored in the bay.
