<p>Monday 5<br/>Still playing soldiers and playing the game<br/>Tuesday 6<br/>Raining like 1 thing and here we are like tea towels after a big wash up, very damp<br/>Wednesday 7<br/>Writing this in full view of the North Sea with a German air ship overhead about 2 mile high<br/>Thursday 8<br/>Feet still warm but I hope I dont get shot, bless my heart I am game , so Cheers Jack</p>
