<p>Monday 29 November, 1915</p>
<p>The weather is much warmer now. We opened our tent up &amp; pulled our beds out to give them an airing in the sun.</p>
<p>Sid has been floating round Valletta on his own account for the last couple of days. He landed up in the guard tent this morning.</p>
<p>The Maltese&nbsp;Malitia [Militia]&nbsp;set on some of our boys &amp; nearly killed one, he was hit on the head with an iron chair.</p>
