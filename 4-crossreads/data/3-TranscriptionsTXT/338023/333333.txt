The pigs are the same as others, save that they occupy a higher social plane. The fowls are the same save that they hide when soldiers are passing. Cattle and horses are noticeable when you discover any animals that tally with a vague memory of the Aussi animals. The rabbits are many & haughty. They stare at you as tho they expect a salute. Possibly they do - for they have other privileges.
We came back by dawn light - a nice cool walk, made all the better by the assurance that the rumble of our passing would wake numerous Aussis in numerous camps & bring on us the blessing of their curses. I understand that it did.
The letter - 24th May - Just arrived.
Love.
