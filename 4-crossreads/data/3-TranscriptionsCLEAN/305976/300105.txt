
Wednesday Jan 22Left Rafa in the usual old Trucks about 5pm last night. It was a cold trip down & we arrived at Kantara about 6 AM. We walked across to the other side of the Canal & had breakfast at Mrs Chisholms at the Canteen then catching the 8.30 AM train to Cairo.We arrived in Cairo about 1pm and stayed at Soldiers Club.Tuesday Jan 28Arrived back at Rafa about 6 AM.
