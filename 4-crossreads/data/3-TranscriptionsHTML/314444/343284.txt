<p>G.P. sent never came to hand it seems to be of no use sending such things  somebody else only gets them  that's 3 lots sent &amp; never a sign of one.</p>
<p>I struck Smith the chemist chap, sometime back he wished to be remembered to Dad.  Dad used to call him some nick name but I just forget what now.  Have also seen G. Palmer, &amp; Leathly, they are O.K.</p>
<p>John Willie is large as life their is some talk of them going away for a spell they have had it very solid</p>
<p>we have 2 mails out weekly Monday &amp; Friday but only get an occasional one in, all out are passed by censor so am not allowed to give any particulars [indecipherable &ndash; obscured by censor's deletion on next page] rumours about us going back for the horses but that's as far as it goes.</p>
<p>We get an issue of tobacco &amp; cigarettes pretty well every week some of the chaps have lost their pipes &amp; I have heard of a chap paying as much as 2 sov's cash for a pipe from another chap so a stock of pipes would bring good money I guess.</p>
