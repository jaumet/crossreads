<p>13-2-17<br/>Tue 13th<br/>Told the Dr. this morning that I was fit for duty &amp; he said he would get me away on the first train tomorrow &amp; advise the Regt. Got dressed this afternoon &amp; went to the E.F.Canteen &amp; bought a supply of cigarettes &amp; sauce for the lads here. Aeroplanes have been going out today loaded with bombs</p>
