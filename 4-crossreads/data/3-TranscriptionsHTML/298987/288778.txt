<p>Wednesday 3<br/>Went to Sutton V</p>
<p>Thursday 4<br/>Sport at S.V. Had afternoon off to attend them. Hall &amp; I went to a concert afterwards. While the Sports were on the Officers Quarters caught fire &amp; mostly got burnt It is generally reported they were fired with the idea of looting.</p>
<p>Friday 5<br/>Still the Guard</p>
<p>Saturday 6<br/>Went sick this morning as I have a slight cough got some stuff for it Went to S-V- &amp; Hall &amp; I went to Warminster &amp; saw a picture show Stayed in S-V- camp all night as I have a week end pass</p>
