  
 Wed Aug 30th - 16 Everybody very much excited when told that Italy and Roumania had declared war. 
 Thur Aug 31st - 16 Our Right Section moved forward at 7PM to take up battery position. I was detailed to do despatch work between wagon lines and battery. 
 Fri Sept 1st - 16 Had my first exciting ride on  horse as we had to gallop off on account of shells falling too close. 
 Sat Sep 2nd - 16 A dinner held by the boys as a surprise for my birthday
