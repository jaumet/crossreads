<p>10 January 1918 Thursday<br/>Only 4 Officers left in the Regiment who were in it originally viz-<br/>Lt.Col Granville<br/>Major Irwin<br/>"&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    Weir<br/>"&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    Harris<br/>Altho' many Armenians have been killed &amp; forced to work for the Turks the Jews have not been killed as stated &amp; are <u>not</u> starving so far as we can see here-<br/>Usual aerial strafing goes on Huns over daily but no bombs dropped of late.</p>
