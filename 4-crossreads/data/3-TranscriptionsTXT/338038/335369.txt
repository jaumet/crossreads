 I am expecting to go on leave in about 3 weeks now  Have a little programme mapped out including a few days in Ireland, but of course its such a total change to get over in England at all that I may decide to stay down with Sis &ndash; I will go down there for a few days anyhow &ndash; England is beautiful now from accounts of some of our fellows who have just returned including Percy Ross who spent most of his time down in Cornwall &ndash; 
 Maurice Fergusson is over on leave at present from my Battery. He returned to duty from Hospital much too soon so the rest was badly needed in his case.
