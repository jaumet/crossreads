<p>Nov 10th  Rested at V.B. for the day.</p>
<p>Nov 12th  Left V.B. at noon in motor waggons for the line and <br/>arrived at Rossignol Camp near Nieppe at 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Nov 13th  The men went into the line at 5 p.m. <br/>The band remained out to do fatigue, guard &amp; to practice.</p>
