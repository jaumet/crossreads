 Thursday June 27.6.18 am feeling well to day Roll call 8.30.  was paid 10.30 they only Pay one Pound Per week here  I am not short of a few bob anyway  wrote to maud. Friday June 28.6.18 am feeling well to day went for a route march to garden had a feed of strawberries returned to camp for dinner had a Pint rested till 4.30 had tea very hot day.  turned in Saturday June. 29.6.18 am feeling well to day went up before the Doctor  had  dinner 12 rested all the afternoon had tea 4 P.M. turned in Sunday June 30.6.18 am feeling alright fell in 8.AM went for a walk to the gardens returned for dinner
