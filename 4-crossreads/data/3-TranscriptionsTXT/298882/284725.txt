 20th Gen. Hospital 27/11/17 
 This afternoon I visited the C.O. of the Hospital and he asked me if I felt fit to return to my unit.  I told me I did not.  He then examined my left ankle and promptly marked me for a board at my Divisional Base.  He  was  is a thorough gentleman and very reasonable and considerable to any patient.  I am to leave here tomorrow.  and  I leave behind me good companions I have received every comfort from the Sister in charge
