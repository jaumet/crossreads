 2-6-19 (Mon) I report at Georges Square &amp; am sent on to DAAG at Horseferry Road who wants to send me back to Georges Square but ultimately finds the papers in his own office. 
 As I must wait 4 days to report back on Thursday for my orders. I then try to collect the allowance payable last march &amp; I am told that it has been posted to my unit. When I tell them I shall never see the unit again they consent to enter the amount in my pay book. 
 I then visit Harrowby &amp; Lady Francis strafes me for not going to Mrs Wilkinson. 
 I visit the Daily News &ndash; a daily &amp; see all through the printing works from the reproduction of photographs with their dots to the machine which prints &amp; rolls &amp; turns out 20,000 copies in 19 minutes &ndash; This is a double machine. 
 From the linotype I get my name set up. I see the linotype block made from paper &amp; the molten metal does not burn it though it is only paper covered with a solution. It is cast in cylinder form &amp; put in machine
