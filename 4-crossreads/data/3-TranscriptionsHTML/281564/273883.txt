<p>Sunday 4th Oct<br/>Church parade.,<br/>Saw some real savages. Dark &amp; ferocious.  Hair worn in high arching fringe &amp; trimmed with strings of rat's teeth &amp; shells. The broad flat nose rendered more picturesque by a long shaft of sharpened bone thrust through it.</p>
<p>[Drawing of a soldier under a palm tree entitled "A Picket on the road outside Wilhelmshafen]</p>
