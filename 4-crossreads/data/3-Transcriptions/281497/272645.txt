<p>you know it is summer time here &amp; the middle of the day is very warm especially when you are marching &amp; carrying everything you possess, &amp; the dust kicked up by the troops nearly chokes you, Saw some beautiful crops of wheat round this part, they were a picture Halloy where we are billeted is the same as most of the little French towns, very pretty but I fancy this a prettier one than most of them, there is a nice little stream running through her, &amp; a very fine chateau &amp; beautiful green trees line the roads &amp; stream, &amp; flowers, flowers, every where especially rose's</p>
