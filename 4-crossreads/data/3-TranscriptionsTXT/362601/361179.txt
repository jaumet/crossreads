 26 March, Wednesday 
 F. Lovely Day 
 I referee Hockey Match. Ellis goes away by 2 pm Train. I go to English Class &amp; then to office to write &amp; study, get to bed at lights out. 
 Wrote Home to dad. 
 27 March, Thursday 
 F. but very windy. End of Period. 
 I go to B-Keeping Class &amp; English Literature Class. 
 Wrote Ida Dean, Dulce, Bert, Esme, Lottie &amp; Bert Cocks. 
 Wrote M.R.L. &amp; sent Books.
