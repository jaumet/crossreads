<p>Friday Mar. 2 [no entry]<br/>Sat. Mar. 3.  Busy day in Blanket Store.  Got 2 patients to assist me &amp; we put in some good work.  After Tea went to see 5th Div. Concert in the Church Army Hut.  They used our Stage &amp; Hargrave assisted.  Wrote long Letter to M.S.  Got word that Col. Williams was severely wounded.</p>
<p>Sunday Mar. 4/17  Glorious day.  Aeroplanes about in droves.  5th Div. Band played for Church Service.  Fine music.</p>
<p>Mon. 5.  About 40 of the 8th with Maj. Wooster &amp; Capts. Irving, Catford &amp; Riley marched to Edgehill &amp; attended the funeral of Col. Williams.  It was a very simple ceremony &amp; he was put in a trench just the same as a Private.  The  Night birds" got another knock tonight.  Bob Roberts is going to the Anzac Show, so we are left without a Producer.</p>
