<p>[April 1918]<br/>25 Th<br/>Our Boys did not go into action the order was cancelled Spent the day on poker I won a few francs<br/>26 Fri<br/>Another full moon and a big fight soon the Brigade went into action today<br/>27 Sat<br/>Taking things easy reading writing and gambling and eating good tucker, but Im lousy I scratch my self to sleep every night</p>
