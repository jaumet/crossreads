<p>Our Engineer Dvr. Smith leaves for Long Service Furlough in Australia &ndash; After the show I write a new dramatic sketch for what is called "The Burglar".</p>
<p>Sept. 15th<br/>Sabbath &ndash; Further decoration to the Blue Room. &ndash; Wash clothes &ndash; write letters. Weather warm &amp; oppressive.</p>
<p>Sept. 16th<br/>A never to be forgotten storm &ndash; Thunder &amp; lightening as I have never seen before heard or witnessed it. Show as usual.</p>
