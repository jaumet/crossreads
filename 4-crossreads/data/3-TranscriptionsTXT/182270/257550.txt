 TUESDAY, AUGUST 26, 1919. 
 Cold &amp; windy with a couple of very 
 light showers. At the shed all day 
 had five shearers going after 
 lunch. Took what was finished 
 of wethers down to Middle paddock 
 Got the ewes &amp; lambs from Mountain 
 up &amp; into the shed. 
 Al &amp; Jack Charley, Jo &amp; Maisie went 
 down to Sydney.
