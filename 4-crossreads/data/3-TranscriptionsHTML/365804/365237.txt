<p>September 4th. 1916 [Different handwriting, presumably date of receipt of letter]<br/>R.A. MESS.<br/>WOOLWICH.</p>
<p>Dear Judge,<br/>Only a few days ago I heard by letter from a friend of your son's death</p>
<p>Please accept my deepest sympathy at the very great loss you have suffered which time alone can assuage. The knowledge however that your son met his death in the service of his country</p>
