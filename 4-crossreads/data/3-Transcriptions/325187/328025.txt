<p>[Monday 9 October 1916]</p>
<p>Examined<br/>14 days extension sick leave.<br/>Laura here to Tea- Met Harold Ryrie.  Had breakfast in bed- Am not returning to Ireland it is hardly worth it.<br/>Went &amp; saw the "Bing Boys" at the Alhambra last night.<br/>To <strike>day</strike> morrow we go to Hendon Hall where Dorothy was at shool for some years.<br/>I am taking Laura &amp; Connie to something next Saturday night.<br/>Had afternoon Tea with Laura at Trocadero</p>
