 24-2-17 A.G.H. &amp; was told that Jessie was out for a walk &amp; was off duty, so I caught a tram &amp; went to Abbassia saw Hills &amp; Jessie before they had dinner &amp; found Leask &amp; Frost had been out &amp; had only just left per motor to catch the 6.30 train back; Hills is on night duty I went over to the Hospital with her from the Sisters Quarters &amp; then went for a walk with Jessie; It was 10.30 when I left the Hospital &amp; eleven before I arrived back at the Anzac had another dinner &amp; went up to bed, the Anzac is filled right up on account of the men coming down on leave.
