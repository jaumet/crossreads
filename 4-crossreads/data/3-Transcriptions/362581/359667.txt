<p>2nd A.A. Hosp.<br/>Southall<br/>25.11.17</p>
<p>XXXXXXX</p>
<p>My darling Eddy,</p>
<p>Received two lovely letters from you this week. The cuttings a beautiful. You must take me to a Picture Show when I return. Hope you will be able to go all this term to school   &amp;  get stronger. We will have some fun when I come home. Fondest love   &amp;  kisses From OXOXOX Auntie Eadle.</p>
