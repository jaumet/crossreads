<p>Thu. Jan. 13th -16<br/>Mess Orderly.  Fell downstairs with can of coffee, dish of porridge and dish of stew.</p>
<p>Fri. Jan. 14th -16<br/>Innoculation for third time.  First rifle drill and also inspection of kit.</p>
<p>Sat. Jan. 15th -16<br/>Fire and boat drill in case of fire or emergency.  Concert at night.</p>
<p>Sun. Jan. 16th -16<br/>Church Service 10.30 A.M.</p>
<p>Mon. Jan. 17th -16<br/>Passed another ship.  Also passed Cape Guardefui on east coast of Africa.</p>
<p>Tue. Jan. 18th -16<br/>Passed Aden at present entering Red Sea.</p>
