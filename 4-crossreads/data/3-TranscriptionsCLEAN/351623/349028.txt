  
 Feb 18th Dep anchor & went to sea to meet convoy. 
 19 Feb 18 At sea screening convoy, weather bad. 
 20 Feb 18 With convoy 
 21 Feb 18 to 4th March Arrived Rosyth, coaled & went alongside the dockyard pier to have aeroplane platform completed. Also camaflouged the ship all colours of the rainbow. 
 4th March 18 Left dockyard & anchored in stream off Charlestowe plenty of leave breaking. 
 6th March 18 Went out past May Id to do firing & returned to Rosyth. 
 8 March 18 Went to sea to meet convoy.
