  
 Jan. 5 Tuesday 1915 Another horse died Sea smooth, very hot Trooper died and buried from "Samata" 
 Jan. 6 Wednesday 1915 German merchants"gun boats" in the vicinity. Firing during night Weather still sultry & hot. 
 Jan. 7 Thursday 1915 Distance 60 miles passed Cocos Island early 7 AM 2 horse died a little rain fell, but no apparent difference in temperature. Fleet doing a little "manuvring during afternoon,   1 hour's delay expecting to reach Colombo next Thursday morning. 
 Jan. 8 Friday 1915 Horse died making 13 to date. 
 Heavy clouds rising very sultry Fleet stopped 2 PM. "Servic"approached flagship & sent off a boat. "Ceramic"did likewise, within 1/2 hour both boats returned to their respective ships. Signal full steam ahead.
