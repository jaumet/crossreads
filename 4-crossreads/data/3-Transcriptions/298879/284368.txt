<p>April May 1915<br/>Friday 30<br/>Fine<br/>Roll call. Several missing men have turned up. Heavy artillery has been landed to deal with Mr. Turk &amp; his shrapnel which is considerably superior to ours. We make a little pot of tea any time &amp; have plenty of rations. Cable from Lord Kitchener encouraging us in our work.</p>
<p>Saturday May 1<br/><u>Fine</u><br/>Enemy poured shrapnel down to our camping place early this morning and off and on all day. Many narrow escapes. Our warships very quiet today. Orders to move from this position.</p>
