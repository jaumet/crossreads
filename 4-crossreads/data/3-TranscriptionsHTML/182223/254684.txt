<p>Thursday 29&nbsp;July 1915&nbsp;<br />Letters to Pearl Drury, &amp; some others.&nbsp;<br />In Cairo for an hour this morning at chemical laboratory &amp; other places.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Friday 30&nbsp;July 1915&nbsp;<br />About hospital all day.&nbsp;<br />The cry is still they come.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Saturday 31&nbsp;July 1915&nbsp;<br />Pres 658 patients today.&nbsp;<br />General Legge called this afternoon, about 6 p.m.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sunday 1 August 1915&nbsp;<br />Heard Mass at the Basilica 9.30 am.&nbsp;<br />Father Vrigille S.J. lunched with me.&nbsp;<br />Went with the padre to visit a French family at Heliopolis stayed for two hours&nbsp;</p>
