  
 27 July, Sunday 
 Drive to Dee Why. Go walk on hill. Duck for dinner. George to Marathon at Long Reef. Cold & windy. 
 I look out all letters & P.C's & we sort them up & sit to talk after tea. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie 
 28 July, Monday 
 Mum & I go out in rain to H'ville, meet Dick's people & then go to Nelsons. Arrive Home at 9 pm.
