<p>"OCHTEZEELE"<br/>Friday 17<br/>Peace floating about but I think it will float over the bulldog as his teeth well in and is not going to let go now<br/>Saturday 18<br/>Harvest time in Flander's Ripe is the golden grain, And within my heart a feeling To be back home again A long route march to Arniques<br/>Sunday 19<br/>An Australian mail in 2 letters for me, Els &amp; Mue I wrote a little article of prose and sent it to the editor of "Blighty"<br/>Down on the farm making butter and doing a bit of haymaking</p>
