<p>Thursday 25.1.17 turned out 6.30AM had breakfast and tidied up not much doing<br/>Friday 26.1.17 turned out 6.30AM had breakfast. Fine warm morning two taubes over to day aircraft firing at them one appeared to be hit. circled around a lot<br/>Saturday 27.1.17. turned out 6.30AM had breakfast went a few messages 2.PM fine warm day not much doing made a new dug out . ordered to rejoin company had good issue of rum.<br/>Sunday 28.1.17 rejoined company stand to 5.30A.M two taubes over this morning dropped bombs</p>
