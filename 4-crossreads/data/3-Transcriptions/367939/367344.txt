<p>Gallipoli<br/>8/10/1915</p>
<p>Dear Ted</p>
<p>Just a line to find out how you are all getting on leaving me very well at present I had a letter from home the only one I got they were saying Da was not too good when they heard from you last I hope he is better by this their letter was dated 10th august so that is a good while ago Dear Ted by their letter their not paying any money from the Barracks. I wish you would go to the Victoria Barracks   &amp;  see the Pay Master   &amp;  shake them up Well Ted I have no more news this time hopping to hear from you soon with fond love to you all from Your Loving Brother Charlie No 94</p>
<p>I don't know where all my mail is going I cannot get any</p>
