<p>6 October 1918<br/>Sunday<br/>19 After Trinity</p>
<p>1918 October 7<br/>Monday<br/>Heavy rain for a few hours. In the afternoon Ruby &ndash; Mac &amp; Malk &amp; I went for a row on the River. There is nothing in particular to note. Things are still going well on all Fronts.</p>
