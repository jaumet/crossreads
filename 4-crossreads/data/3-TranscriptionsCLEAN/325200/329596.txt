
Several more horses got bullet wounds  &  another man belonging to this outfit got hit in the foot.Then until 5 oclock pm. we stood by back in camp while the infantry held the turks. All this while the Turks could shrapnell all the camp,  &   - which was a great surprise - had a couple of 6" g howitzers in Katia with which they bombarded the redoubts  &  railway unmercifully but fortunately a great part of their effect is lost in the soft sand.
At 5 pm we started off  & made a big detour to the N  &
