
12February, Wednesday
F & iceI arise at 7.30 in the attic. Brekkie & catch 8.50 train to Bulford, met an N.A.C.B. [Navy & Army Canteen Board] Civi[lian] B- on Train. Arr Camp 10 am & recd letter fromM.R.L. E & I go to see "The Happy Day" Musical Comedy by S Hills - Tresbon. Im very sick, we see WWoody at the Theatre & Dicky's Two on the Corner.
13 February, Thursday
F & iceRecd letter from Jess, Mother & Doris. I am very sick but get all my work square at last. E. gets ready for London & I get my issue of Clothes. I write my Diary up & pack up my dirty togs for the wash. I am very tired waiting for a shave to go to bed.
Wrote M.R.L.
