<p>Monday 2 July 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille 4 a.m. Parade as usual. I am Mess Orderly today. Major Whitford gave us an address on 1<sup>st</sup>&nbsp;and 2<sup>nd</sup>&nbsp;battles of Gaza. I took notes &amp; a copy of his sketches. He has just arrived from the front &amp; is taking up position of Adjutant of A.C.B.F.A.</p>
<p>A trainload of Turkish prisoners came through here from Gaza this morning. 3 hospial trains came through yesterday &amp; today from the front.</p>
