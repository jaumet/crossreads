 Monday 22 January 1917 
 I have about 160/-/- in Bank in England. I have about 293/-/- Anglo Egyptian Bank Cairo [Total] 453/-/-. My deferred pay 100 about [Total] &pound;555/-/- 
 How much is in Savings Bank in Deniliquin please!  Interest could be entered in Pass Bk. if you have it. 
 Khamsin blowing &ndash; odd Turkscome in they know we will treat them decently &amp; not feed the vultures on them like they do the Armenians, some terrible stories are told of what they have been doing to these unfortunate people. 
 One Turk had 2 bullets in him, shot by the Bedouins who got a lot of Rifles etc. from Rafa &amp; Magdaba.
