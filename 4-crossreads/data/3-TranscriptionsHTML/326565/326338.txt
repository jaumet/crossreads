<p>admitted into hospital here suffering from the effects of the march. Paid 150 piastres (&pound; 1-10-9).  Turkish spy caught out a few miles past railhead, &amp; brought in here blindfolded.</p>
<p>Saturday 1/4/16<br/>Piquet Corporal for the day, returned at 6 pm.  Very nearly in trouble for leaving camp while on duty. Received parcel from Mrs Robison.  Sing song in tent at night, had rather an enjoyable few hours.</p>
<p>Sunday 2/4/16<br/>On the move again, being in this camp only 4 days.  Struck all tents &amp; packed &amp; loaded all material</p>
