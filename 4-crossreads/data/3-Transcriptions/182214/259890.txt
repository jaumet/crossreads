
<p>Oct 28 a rainbow after the Somme Battlefield what a contrast. Oct 29 Rained nearly&nbsp;all day I got wet through helping Scotty fix up our&nbsp;dugout&nbsp;which&nbsp;had almost collapsed. Let the water in I also fixed fixedup] unknown soldiers grave which was getting knocked about. Made [?] a new wooden cross as the old one was broken, drained the water off the top as well as I could &amp; moulded it up a bit. October 30 beautiful sunny day put out clothes out to dry and mended our dugout counted 24 observation balloons &amp; 30 aeroplanes up in the air at the same time.
