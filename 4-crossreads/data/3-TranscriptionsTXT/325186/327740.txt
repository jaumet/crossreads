 Things forgotten 
 At Port Said the natives made a disturbance by refusing to carry on with the coaling of the boat.  One of the ships cooks forgot himself &amp; emptied a bucket of hot water over the side &mp; it went all over the niggers below.  He thought he was still at sea.  The strike was soon quelled &amp; the work went on again.  The water police were kept hard at work chasing the natives from the side of the boat they were trying to sell things to us &amp; the authorities had forbidden it.
