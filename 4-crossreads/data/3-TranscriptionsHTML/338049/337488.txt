<p>Saturday 5 October<br/>Up &amp; preparing for move off. Packed up &amp; ready to move early but <strike>delay</strike> to wait. Finally had dinner &amp; moved off 12.30.</p>
<p>Moved to near Halle &amp; had to wait for 9th Bde transport. Detailed for billeting party so rode forward with officer 6 others to Marcourt. Division of transport on rode.</p>
<p>Very longer. Fixed up gully at Marcourt. Colum arrived about 9-30.</p>
