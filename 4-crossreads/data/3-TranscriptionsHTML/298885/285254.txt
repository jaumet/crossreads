<p>Well we arrived at Corbie at 3.30PM and after a good feed, we soon made use of the blankets</p>
<p>Oct 8th &ndash; 18<br/>With brighter weather and a beautiful track we soon covered another 20 miles to St Sauvier.</p>
<p>Our good track was the towpath of the Canal du Nord. These paths are without an incline of any degree at all and are very smooth</p>
