<p>Wednesday 30 May 1917.</p>
<p>Fell in with guard at 8.45 a.m.</p>
<p>Inspected on boat deck above and then marched to guard room in the forecastle.</p>
<p>Mounted duty on No 9 post port aft. at 1 till 2 pm.; 2 to 3 pm (No 5 deck) No 8 post.&nbsp;relieved at 3 pm. marched back to guard room &amp; dismissed until 7 p.m. when I go on till 9 pm.</p>
<p>Tea at 5 pm. &nbsp;Mounted guard at No 9 post&nbsp;<s>port aft</s>&nbsp;at 7 pm till 9 pm.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Slept on deck for 1<s>&frac12;&nbsp;</s>&nbsp;hr<s>s</s>&nbsp;or so &amp; then a monsoonal wind sprung up in 5 mins &amp; was compelled to go below &amp; sleep in guard room&nbsp;</p>
<p>Air stifling here &amp; could not sleep</p>
