 at Mousset Camp, a few miles out of Marseilles. (69) Monday 3rd April :- Put my foot on French soil for the first time this morning when I spent several hours in town. What a fine city (600,000) inhabitants). Pretty streets with many trams on some of which are female ticket collectors. Quite decent to see so many civilians after Cairo &amp; its military hordes. Australians quite a novelty &amp; many a curious glance cast at us. Plenty of French troops about &ndash; some with battle scars.
