<p>Tuesday 5 March 1918</p>
<p>Wednesday 6 March 1918<br/>Recco, Tul Keram.  B.F.  Pilot Lt. Fraser.  Returned dud engine, in air 35 m.<br/>Started in another machine, in air 2 h. 30 m.  engine very bad indeed.  Congratulated on work done.  Report on railway bridges.</p>
