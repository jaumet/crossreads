<p>Dec 16th/<br/>Out pattroling, found enemy near camp, so we had a cut in at them.<br/>They must have thought they had us nearly under. Seeing us retreat in disorder so got much more game &amp; came closer, they soon bolted back to the hills when they found us with plenty of bullets for them<br/>We cannot do much till reinforcements arrive from Alexandria. The track that we came by is now blocked by the enemy &amp; they have been trying to take the Railhead; but they will find the</p>
