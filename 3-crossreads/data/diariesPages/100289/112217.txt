B C sections remain out in line. About 1000 patients here; quite a large concern.
Sunday 4.2.17 to Wednesday 7.2.17
Getting used to the new surroundings. New job on baths going smoothly. Fritz's planes generally pay us a visit each night cause a sensation. Unit concert party "Nightbirds" gave an entertainment to patients which proved a great success. Five more went on leave to Blighty "7.2.17". Received
