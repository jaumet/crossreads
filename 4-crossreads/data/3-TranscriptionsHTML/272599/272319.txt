<p>1917 Monday 19 Feb.</p>
<p>Set off after breakfast for Factory Corner with Brown &amp; Cassin.  Arrive without incident but plenty of mud in hours time.  5th Batt changing over to the 7th Batt. <br/>The 7th M.O. Capt. Barlow makes things uncomfortable. Inexperienced &amp; youthful. I take on cook for day. Make up bully &amp; cheese hash for dinner &amp; cut up bonser leg mutton for tea, cutlets &amp; thick gravy. Pronounced best ever. Fortunately no carries during night.<br/>Incident of mistake with the tea water instead of tea. by previous mob.</p>
