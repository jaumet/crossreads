  
 August,1918 Friday 16 
 Cayeau Still at this rather pretty place. But somehow after last night I can't claim to like it very much. Out salvaging wire all day. Ken Gunn rode across to new Bty position near Morecourt, and he arrived home looking fairly tired. Made up the old tarphouse again. Had a few Fritz's  across also a boots enclisers. Inspected a captured gun
