<p>Monday 13 March 1916<br/>Dick working disc Harrows.  Geoff took seed drill.  Father &amp; I sifted lucerne seed.<br/>Cut chaff in afternoon.  Father took honey from bees.</p>
<p>Tuesday 14 February 1916<br/>Mother &amp; I drove to Mudgee.  Saw Dr. Nicholl.  He won't let me go back for a fortnight as my lung is not quite healed.<br/>Dick &amp; Geoff working in 40 acres.</p>
