 23/2/17. Friday When I awoke next Morning I did not feel to well, having a Head ache I remained on board  until  although the troops went for another Route March, & after Dinner I dressed & went ashore at 3 Pm & caught a Train a Train & went up towards the Mountains & spent an hour looking down over the Town. On Returning I met a Chum Kept a precious appointment & went out to Tea to a friend of his place,  I  a Mr Taylor who is  in the  a Black Smith in the Harbour Works. Mr & Mrs Taylor live in one of the Dock Cottages
