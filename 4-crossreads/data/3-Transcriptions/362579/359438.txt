<p>Porch House.<br/>Torrington.<br/>Devon.<br/>4.11.17</p>
<p>My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>you will be surprised to see the above address. Last Sunday I wrote to you from 3rd A.G.H. France now I am in England on   <u>Leave</u> . I know Miss Walker had put in for our Leave for last Monday but we had been disappointed so often that it came as a very pleasant surprise. We were told at 8 p.m. Sunday   &amp;  we to leave next day at 8 p.m. for Boulogne. Went on duty until mid-day, then packed   &amp;  were ready for car at 7 p.m. To our horror we nearly missed the train. The Colonel forgot we wanted the car, he was out in it   &amp;  did not return until 20 mins to 8 o'clock. It takes nearly 15 minutes to get to Station by car. Anyhow we did it.
