 67 Sept 28th Did not go out on walk today as my chair has not been returned &ndash; Those who did go out enjoyed themselves as they went for a row on the lake, there were several naval actions &ndash; 
 7 officers tried to escape last night but, unfortunately the Germans were informed &amp; consequently were waiting for them &ndash; They are now in cells &ndash; as a result another search for tools &amp;c took place today &ndash; 
 Speech made by Asquith published today it is a darn fine one, in which he cleared stated our terms, if our people act up to his speech we will eventually benefit by this war &ndash; The speech by the Imp. Chancellor was not published &ndash; 
 In the leading article in todays paper, the German people are now complaining about the secret way in which the Reichstag transacts Germany's affairs &ndash; They demand that the speech of the I.C. be published &amp; they also want to know the actual state of affairs as regards the prolongation of this war &ndash; There is at last signs of interal trouble in Germany &ndash; this is a good sign &ndash;
