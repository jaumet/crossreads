Thursday I went to Gy. &amp; returned last night.  Well, Dad, I've given you all the news &amp; I must scribble a note to Mum &amp; then go on Duty.  I am glad to Hear that you are all keeping so well.  We are just tip top.  I go out on the Bike nearly every night for an Hour or so.  Kind Regards to all &amp; Fond Love to George, Mum &amp; yourself. 
 Your Fond Son Harry
