<p>They worship the Cow. They have no religion. A Cow is the most valuable possession of a Dinka man, before wife child or anything else. Tall stout&nbsp;&amp; black. Very good fighters with the spear. Plenty of lions, leopards, hyaenas, giraffe (especially)&nbsp;&amp; Elephants.</p>
<p>They live on maize, roots of trees,&nbsp;&amp; especially milk.</p>
