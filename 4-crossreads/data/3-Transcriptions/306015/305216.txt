<p>May 31.   Drill now takes the form of practise stunts, or attacks.   We are organised either as a platoon or Company &amp; at the given signal, go through the process of taking an imaginary trench with fixed bayonets etc.</p>
<p>Evening kindly granted me a few quiet hours in the Y.M.C.A. writing room.</p>
<p>June 1.<br/>We spent the day in the fields in order to see a demonstration trench-mortar barrage.</p>
<p>It is said that the Americans have an enormous class of gun ready for use in France.   Its shell will kill anything within 8 miles of the burst &amp; the recoil of the gun will bring up provisions for the troops.</p>
<p>June 2    Alan, Ron, Norman &amp; "Bindy" spend the day at Amiens.</p>
