<p>Sunday 15 September<br/>Not very busy to-day or yesterday &amp; nothing at all happened to break the usual routine of M.D.S. Railway broad gauge is now past here.</p>
<p>Monday 16 September<br/>A big stunt is impending on this front, to come off in a few days.<br/>Glorious night, moonlit, with Boshe 'planes in dozens.</p>
