<p>a lot of large steamers going up &amp; out of the "canal" they seemed to be going over the land. Some nigger boats came round but got pelted with spuds &amp; a "red cap" police boat appeared &amp; kept them off. A boat from the Karoola came over a few times crowded with "our lads" who came to see if they could spot some mates : They told us that the Dardenelles campaign had been given up &amp; that the troops were all along the canal expecting an attack : Taylor's sister came over from the hospital ship &amp; they let her on board. I was introduced to her &amp; felt quite shy; a month since I had seen a girl : another boat from the hospital ships with a lot</p>
