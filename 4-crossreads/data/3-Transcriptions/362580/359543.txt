<p>We are still in Egypt &amp; seem likely to remain, so perhaps I wont need my savings for pretties.</p>
<p>It is fearfully hard to try &amp; not worry but the mails from France are so irregular.  I'm longing for news from him but must wait.  It is nearly three weeks since I got my last letter.  I hope he has got some of mine by now.  I have not heard from Fred nor any of my other friends since they departed.  The French girls must be taking-up all their spare time, so forgetting their Australian Sisters.</p>
<p>My godfather, if you could hear the noise some of the other sisters are making, it is awfully hard to write at our Quarters, women</p>
