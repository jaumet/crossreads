<p>The Sub. came up end first &amp; the destroyers soon fixed her.<br/>The weather is holding remarkably fine &amp; it is quite pleasant down here.<br/>Am Orderly Sgt. today, but theres practically nothing to do here, she's some Camp.</p>
<p>26th.  Well it seems as if we are not going to leave this Camp for some time.  This morning on parade we were told that 20&percnt; were to be given 4 days leave so that about settles Aussie by Xmas for us.  I put my application in &amp; got my warrant by 12 oclock.  I am going to London &amp; intend if I can get money enough to get some new clothes.<br/>This morning 4 other Sgts. &amp; I went to see Miss Wasterns funeral.  She has been a wonderful good worker for the sailors &amp; the Navy were burying her with Naval honors.
