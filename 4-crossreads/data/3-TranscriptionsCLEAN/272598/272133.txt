  
 Fairly quiet.  Rained hard this morning. 
 Sunday 26.11.16 Fritz shelled heavily practically the whole day.  Relieved at 3 pm, arrived back at camp about 5 pm, where a mail was awaiting our return.  Roads in an awful state, mud up to our knees. 
 Monday 27.11.16 Rested all day.  Sgt Thompson & Sgt Roberts evacuated & being only N.C.O. left in C. section was put in charge.  Wrote long letter home; issued with top boots.
