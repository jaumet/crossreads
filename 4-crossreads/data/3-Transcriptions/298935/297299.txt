Our boatload escaped all the bullets but we received a literal baptism of fire from the water they threw up.  The boats grounded a few yards from the beach and we had to wade, waist deep in water, to the shore.   A few casualties we noticed on the beach but not so many as we had expected.<br/>The boys all hoped to be in Constantinople by night and consequently having once got Johnny Turk on the run they did not stop to "dig in".
