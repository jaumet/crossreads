<p>Feb 17th &ndash; 18<br/>At last found Norrie this morning and put the day in together</p>
<p>Mar 10th &ndash; 18<br/>Since my last entry nothing startling has happened except for the rapid descent of a bomb which fell near the graperies of Bailleul fatally wounding 3 Aussies</p>
<p>Also a few days later a little excitement was caused by 4 12 inch shells which fell in the town. One shell wrecked</p>
