<p>Meanwhile boats allotted to us:<br/>Prinz Hubertus to sail July 3rd<br/>Quevic&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; July 9th<br/>Main&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  "   16th  21st 23rd</p>
<p>Then came another four days in London after the 9th was arranged.  Spent with Kit again &amp; Hills.</p>
<p>Arrived back &amp; found again put back to 16th.  Strike of men against continual postponements.  One day's fatigues not done.  Was chief delegate &amp; did not enjoy it.</p>
<p>Final date given as 23rd &ndash; more leave granted.  Did not take leave.  Instead I went to Stockton got a room, and started studying &amp; working on the Short story course which I had paid &pound;10 for. Worked until 20th.</p>
<p>Left Watford for Plymouth on 23rd morning &amp; embarked by tea time</p>
