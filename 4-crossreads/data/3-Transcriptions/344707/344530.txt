<p>Shrapnel &amp; bullets fell all round &amp; by the blood on the donkeys neck [indecipherable] he had been hit earlier in the morning &ndash;<br/>At the beach the Dr &amp; orderlies worked like Trojans.<br/>I felt very faint &ndash; &amp; commenced vomiting so they put me on a stretcher on piles of biscuit cases &ndash;<br/>Beef Tea was given round &amp; water &amp; after about an hour a string of barges towed us out to the Royal Yacht LIBERTY which in turn took us to EMBROS ISLAND transhipped us to the Atlantic Liner CALEDONIAN &amp; thence to Alexandria &amp; then by train to where I write from my No1 AGH Heliopolis &ndash; Shot on Saturday, Heliopolis Monday - pretty good &ndash;</p>
