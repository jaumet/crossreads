7177 miles, 1119 to go.
Sunday 5/12/15
Very warm. Overtook amp: passed in very close proximity to large steamer (name unknown), when abreast signalled wished us good luck. One of our boys signalled back thanked them for their good wishes. Our band played a few selections while abreast, Actually saw a lady on board, first of fair sex seen for a month. 
Mail closes in orderly room 9 pm tonight, all very busy writing letters, as it will be last opportunity for writing on board. Very heavy wind rain storm during afternoon. Thunder exceptionally heavy. Church Parade
