<p><u>Monday 4th March '18</u><br/>Last night there was another raid, with usual accompaniment of big shells, bombs &amp; machine gun fire. A few shells fell round here, but not within yards of <strike>here</strike> the dug out. It happened on the right of here, that is the right arm of the A salient, held by the 10th Batt., of which the left arm is held by the 9th batt. In the latter are several old 3rd boys, including old Charlie Taylor of Anzac days &amp; Harry Barnes, recently transferred.<br/>We had rather a funny case last night.
