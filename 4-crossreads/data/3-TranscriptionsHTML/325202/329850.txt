<p>entrained for Etaples to [Base] Details we left Havre at 10 p.m &amp; got to Rouen at about 10 next a.m. we had to go to a Rest Camp there to wait a train for Etaples In fact we were hung up there a week for some reason or other Another nights travelling &amp; we reached Aust General Base Details there to wait a draft for the 'line' again. &ndash;</p>
<p>Was at Etaples 5 days &amp; taken sick again this time German Measles &amp; rushed off to Hospital. My chief 'grouse' about this time was the <u>early</u> morning wash &ndash; In fact they seem to be always washing down</p>
