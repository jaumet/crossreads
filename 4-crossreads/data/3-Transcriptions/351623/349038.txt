<p>1 August 18 )<br/>2 Aug 18 ) Anchored at Scapa Flow<br/>3 Aug 18 )</p>
<p>4 Aug 18<br/>At S. Flow. Four years today since war started. Went ashore to an open air church service. There were sailors there off each ship here.</p>
<p>5 Aug 18<br/>At S. Flow. Our galley's crew raced &amp; beat Minotaur's galley this afternoon.</p>
<p>6 Aug 18<br/>At Scapay</p>
<p>7 Aug 18<br/>Steamed down the Flow &amp; done torpedo running &amp; sub-calibre firing. Anchored again.</p>
<p>8 Aug 18<br/>Went to sea with convoy.</p>
<p>9 Aug 18<br/>Came in &amp; anchored at Scapay Flow.</p>
<p>10 Aug 18<br/>Coaled ship.</p>
