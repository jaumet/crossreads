<p>Monday Aug 27<br/>Boys tell me at this camp that losses at Romani, first &amp; second Gaza were much heavier than officially reported<br/>Tuesday Aug 28<br/>Captain Sutton arrived in camp to day. Gave offer to join his unit as Sanitary Inspector. May take it on if Tom is willing.<br/>Wednesday Aug 29<br/>G. Cameron came over with patients Informed us our holiday would be over next week as we would be recalled to unit. Received mail.<br/>Thursday Aug 30<br/>Swimming nearly all day. Beautiful moonlight night. About 9.30 pm two Taubes came over dropped two bombs. Put the wind up one.</p>
