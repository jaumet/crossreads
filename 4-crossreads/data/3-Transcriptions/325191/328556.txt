All the others also recieved 28 days Darlinghurst Jail &amp; returned into Camp after being compelled to giwe the word of Honor never to try to escape again.  13/  On this day an Austrian Internee got released to attend &amp; look after his children. About 2 weeks ago this Internee recived communicationes that his Wife layd seriously ill &amp; not expekted to live.  This Internee applied for permission to visit his Wife under guard before it was too late.  This however wher refused by the Camp Authorities.  When this Internees got informed that his Wife had died, he applied to be permitted to <strike>go to b</strike> be present at the funeral, wich also got refused.  Now their the Wife has been burried for over a week.  He wher told by the authoritys to return to his Children to protect them &amp; to see that they didn't want.  "Another instance of the treatement meted out to Internees in case of dead in their families. He applied for permission to goe &amp; see her under guard.  This request got refused by the Comandant.
