<p>We are not in same tent, because she got here about 2 days before us, so had settled in one with Annie Bell. I'm with a Sister Macdonald (Queensland) who  was with me at the other C.C.S.s. to my joy quite a number of letter here for me. Alice's of 21 May. Ethel   &amp;  Belle's of 27th May. The only one from Belle from Talbot, also must have missed some from home &ndash; suppose they have gone for ever to the bottom of the sea.</p>
