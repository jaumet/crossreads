<p>A wireless message brought more good news of the War. Near the Peronne a Squadron of the Dragoon Guards accounted for a detachment of the enemy. This is the first opportunity of using Calvary in action since 1914 and signifies further victories ahead, as the Germans must be on the run &amp; past the trenches.</p>
<p>Tuesday July 18. 1916<br/>A dull cloudy day. The course is 10 points East of North with a strong head wind and a rough sea.</p>
<p>[Shorthand not transcribed]</p>
<p>I have done a lot of reading lately. Several magazines &amp; short stories. The Strand is a particularly good magazine, also several parts of the Times History of the War.</p>
