
13th Dec., MondayStart on night duty 6 pm to 12 pm. Very much better than day work in my opinion, but prevents me from visiting our friends.
18th Dec. Saturday:-During the week nothing of much consequence has happened outside the regular nightly routine of work  Sleep during the morning & walk after dinner & go on duty at 6pm. Our friendship with Madame Alice &  the girls has been proceeding favourably & we are invited to tea on Xmas Day, next Saturday.
19th Dec: Sunday
