 Friday 2/11/17&nbsp; Left at 5.30 a.m for Camiers for musketry demonstration with Paton &amp; Miles&nbsp; returned at 11.30 p.m stayed night at Division 
 Saturday 3/11/17. Joined Battalion, just relieved from line, at Walker Camp, some of the men slightly gassed. Two letters from Doff. Wrote to Doff. sent Cable to Billie. 
 Sunday 4/11/17&nbsp; Quiet day- saw Wisdom&nbsp; had a game of cards. 
 Monday 5/11/17&nbsp; Very quiet day&nbsp; raining slightly- men resting. Night raid by Huns very close.&nbsp;
