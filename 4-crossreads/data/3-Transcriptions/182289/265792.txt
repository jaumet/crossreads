<p>Armentieres<br/>billets in Armentieres.  Billets were found for them by this Company and arrangements were made for the handing over.<br/>Lieut. T. Graham sent ahead to Locrehof Farm with an advance party to find some billets if possible.</p>
<p>4.1.18<br/>The 57th Div. Machine Gun Co. arrived and took up their temporary billets.<br/>Report received from Lieut. Graham that Locrehof Camp had been denuded of most of its portable material, and was in a very bad state of repair.</p>
<p>5.1.18<br/>Our guides led 3 Sections of the 57th Div. M.G. Co. to their respective Section Headquarters from whence they were taken to their barrage positions.  The remaining Section took over the A.A. Defence position.</p>
<p>Locrehof Camp<br/>Relief was complete by 11 a.m. and the Company proceeded back to Locrehof Farm Huts.</p>
<p>6.1.18<br/>Repairs effected to the Camp by the Duty Section while the remainder of the Company carried on with training.  Syllabus attached.</p>
<p>7.1.18<br/>Training continued and work on camp.  Indent for material required submitted.</p>
