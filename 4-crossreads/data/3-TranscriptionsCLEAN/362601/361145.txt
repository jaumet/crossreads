  
 17 January, Friday 
 F Go to Bulford by Car & go to Dick's Room all night. We yarn & sup & go to mess. Afterwards I play Billiards a bit yarn with all the old Boys. We have a lovely walk Home in the moonlight. Recd letter from Manly Predday. 
 Send telegram to Auntie. 
 18 January Saturday 
 F Lovely day, we got to Bulford by Car so to mess & Dick's room. E goes to Gibbys with Tap. I go to YWCA & Post Office. So to meet Hilda & Corrie & get scaled. So back to Dance & Dance with Dick Dolly Moore who I squeez & also Dorrie Webster. We set out & walk home. I laugh at Billy & Ellis sliding on the frosty hill side.
