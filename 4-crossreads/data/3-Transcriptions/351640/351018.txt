<p>I notice that Martin has had to go back to Lieut Col., he was appointed Colonel for the voyage on the Kyarra and he has kept it since. I fancy that he is not in good odour with Ford Maxwell and the rest, &amp; it would not surprise me to learn that he will be travelling south before long. From my office at Abbassia General Ford rang Martin up a few days ago, and had a chat with him, during it he said "You have not attended to this", You have not completed this though I have been at you for more than three months to get it done". It may be that the conversation at the moment was for my benefit, because Ford promised to protect me from Martin if he attempted</p>
