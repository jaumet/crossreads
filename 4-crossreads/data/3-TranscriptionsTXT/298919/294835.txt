 alongside a wharf where were several movable cranes.  In trucks alongside them are piles of rails that we are to take to Australia.  During the morning preparations were made for everything to be ready for working tomorrow morning.  Rain fell during the morning &amp; afternoon so perforce had to stay aboard.  After tea as the rain had ceased and there was every prospect of the weather remaining fine, Witt and self went ashore.  We walked along the business streets and to the park which is a beautifully laid out one.  A band was playing and large crowds of people were sitting down, standing and walking about, enjoying the beautiful twilight.  We returned to the City
