<p>stroll along to Weymouth &amp; it is a very nice place in daylight : after the style of Manly or Coogee though the "sands" are not in the same street : tea in town &amp; then went to Church : nice place &amp; service but rather high for my liking : home about 8.30 pm : leg a bit sore &amp; another bruise has worked up on my calf</p>
<p>Monday Oct 15th</p>
<p>Westham : Reveille 6.30 am : parade in morn : parade to Q.M. after dinner : Morrie came in on a new draft : met Jim Howard &amp; Allan Amey : three letters from Aust : stroll to town after tea &amp; left my films at a shop : shifted huts</p>
<p>Tuesday Oct 16th</p>
<p>Westham : wet day : Reveille 6.30 am : roll call then wrote till dinner time : after tea went to Y.M.C.A. pictures</p>
