<p>78<br/>out of the water and ever after that there was a board put up "dangerous" don't swim.</p>
<p>We had a fatigue party to carry what are called pudding bombs or 60 pounders.</p>
<p>They are also called broomstick-bombs. It was my first experience of what is called heavy carrying and I thought I should have swapped in two before finishing the work.</p>
<p>There is one thing I will remark on here and that is the fact whenever the artillery or Engineers required a fatigue party the infantry were obliged to provide. My first experience in the line I can say was rather an experience as I think others will agree.</p>
