 There are always 2 army divisions 40,000 British troops here.   It is  They are called the Army of Occupation in Egypt.  They are  the police  responsible for the safety of the route to India and  now  with all our men coming &amp; going &amp; with territorials &amp; Indian regiments with New Zealanders &amp; Hospital units the war seemed very close.
