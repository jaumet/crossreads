 All the guards on the ship now wear life-belts constantly, but this is merely a precautionary measure in case of accident. Turned in at 8 P.M. 
 Nov 22 Not feeling too good to-day and the weather is somewhat rough & stormy but appears to be clearing up again, This afternoon is our 'washing day' and we have to wash all our dirty clothes ourselves & hang them on lines just like the sailors do on the men-of -war.
