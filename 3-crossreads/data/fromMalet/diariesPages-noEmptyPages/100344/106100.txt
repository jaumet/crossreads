List rec?d about 40 reinforcements to the Coy. Moved at 1pm. for Warloy which we reached were billeted in about 5pm. I was on the Billeting party and went through on Transport motors.
7th. Wednesday.
Up 7am muster parade later. Recd mail.
Requisitions made out, completely Nominal rolls also completed nominations for promotions rec?d. Shoping to bed early.
8th. Tuesday.
Orders for moving at 9am. Left on bike 8.30
