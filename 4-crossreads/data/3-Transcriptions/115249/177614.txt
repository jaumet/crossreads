<p>W.T. Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship: Flag</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship: Parramatta</p>
<p>Date: 15 9 4</p>
<p>Time of Despatch or Receipt: 6.12 am</p>
<p>Submit passed round Duke of York Island nothing seen searched to north west with Yarra could motor schooner proceed and examine Credner and Duke of York Island and northeast coast New Britain at daylight whilst we search seaward to North</p>
