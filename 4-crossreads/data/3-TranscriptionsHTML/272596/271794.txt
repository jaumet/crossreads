<p>said John, what else said the Adjutant "Willis" said Harry &amp; dodged away but the Adjutant recognized him, our luck was dead in one way for we had the luck of the world to catch the ship nothing could have been finer, for 2 minutes more &amp; she would have been away &amp; we would have been stranded properly we would either have had to engage a motor boat to catch her up or try to catch the Suez train both extremely doubtful things, when all the excitement had died down we went below &amp; found to our sorrow that the others had not so far turned up, so we knew for a certainty that they had missed the ship &amp; were stranded in town, we went to bed after this mad escapade wondering what</p>
