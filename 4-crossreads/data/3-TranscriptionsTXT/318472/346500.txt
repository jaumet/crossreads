 2 October 1918 Wednesday We took motor from our digs to Central &amp; took train at 9.45 a.m. Madge and Neila came to see us off Arrived Goulburn about 3.30 P.M. &amp; left there about 4.40 P.M. Arrived Queanbeyan about 9. P.M. and motored out to Canberra. Had a good trip up but very slow. Found all home well. Very keen weather. Good news from all Fronts. News Confirmed that Bulgaria has turned it in. 
 1918 October 3 Thursday Got up late and did nothing in particular except just stroll about the place. Had a look along the River and put in a lazy day generally. Good news continues to come from all Fronts.
