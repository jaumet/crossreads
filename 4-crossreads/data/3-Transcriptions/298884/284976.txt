<p>There was method in his apparent madness though for arrived <strike>at</strike> back in the town <strike>he</strike> we all strolled into another branch of Ramsamy's business near the Bristol Hotel &amp; he compared the prices of the same silken articles there before buying anything.</p>
<p>We entered jeweler's shops, 'milliners &amp; men's wear' shops and several other places going from one to the other in Rickshaws. We always managed to have a small argument with the Rickshaw man about the fare but we invariably won or so imagined.</p>
<p>We went to the Grand Oriental Hotel for lunch &amp; had a very good meal</p>
