
April 1918Wednesday 24
Bussy - la - Dours     D.R.3.50 am. Aroused by heavy drum fire in the region of Montididier - packed up kit, lines went at 5.15am.Stand to until noon.Weather dull and misty.Battery W.L. all returned to Franvillers, and are having a fairly rough spin.Had a run through Bonnay at 1pm. A very putrid sight arms and legs and mules scattered over the roads in a horrible array.6ath Battery caught coming out of village, mules left on road.Fritz shelling fairly heavily
