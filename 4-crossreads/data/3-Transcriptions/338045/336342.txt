<p>where I stayed a day &amp; night.  The scenery all along is beautiful, typically Northern French, especially Wimereux, a pretty seaside place, where No. 2 Aust. General Hosp. is now stationed.  Reached Calais at 12-30 p.m. &amp; detrained for H.S. "Cambrian".  I cannot help thinking here of the different circumstances under which I made the trip to England before &ndash; from Le Havre, Calais, judging by my limited view, is after the style of most French towns &amp; quite different from what I expected it to be.
