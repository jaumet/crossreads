Commonwealth of AustraliaDepartment of Defence.Melbourne.  4th November, 1914.No. 19462
Dear Colonel Holmes,Yours of the 9th October to hand.Many thanks for the set of stamps of the late German administration.Your despatches are read with great interest, and I look forward with pleasure to further submissions from you from time to time.
Yours faithfully,
G.F. Pearce
Colonel W. Holmes.Administrator,Rabaul, New Britain.
