<p>Hassaniya, situated about 5 miles North West of Bayud. This is a small camel depot &amp; our work here will be to escort the supplies from Bir-el-abd to the different outposts. The supplies coming from Bir el abd are transferred here from one line of camels to another which distributes them to the various small encampments.
