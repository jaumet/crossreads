<p>Passing onwards &amp; upwards to Escalier Daru rests the Victory of Samothrace without head or arm but yet of arresting workmanship &ndash; clothed in fine linen &amp; not in heavy wrappings &ndash; Her marvellous body was not designed for daily tasks but was of the gods &ndash; a beauty that was in itself an ideal. Her movements though vigorous were always harmoniously balanced</p>
<p>This the best somewhat dimmed the undoubted beauty of latter] works but one in special took my fancy [Deane by Houdin] a light &amp; almost living figure in bronze another was the Young Neapolitan [Teisher] by Rude a child with laughing face playing with a tortoise.</p>
<p>All the picture were not yet in their place Titian &amp; the [indecipherable] school are missing But Van Eyck, Rembrandt &amp; who can write of the play of light &amp; shade that the master has shown in the two philosophers. Alone in the centre of a room stands Mona Lisa with her mystic smile of ever bewildering fascination.</p>
