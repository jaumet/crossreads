<p>No. 1 Section Div. Ammunition Column &ndash; A.I.F.<br/>My dear Father,<br/>I was very surprised to hear from Major Rabett on Wednesday that you had not been getting my letters (He had a cable from his Father to that effect) so I sent you a cable on Wednesday night just to let you know I was O.K. and hoped this reached you safely.<br/>Your letter of the 18th Jany was most welcome. Reached me yesterday. I am</p>
