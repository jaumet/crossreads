<p>From Tuesday until Friday 18/1/18 things were very quiet, very little fireing going on.    We were issued a new Tunic, before lunch &amp; as the sleeves were rather long I took the opperunity being off duty &amp; went into Poperinghe to my friends where the young lady, altered the sleeves &amp; put new <strike>Chevons</strike> Chevrons (stripes) on my sleeve also made me two new colours &amp; sewed them on.   We expect our Riding Breeches &amp; New Hats any time now.   After spending the Evening with these people I left by a special troop Train at 10 Pm for Camp.</p>
<p>Tuesday 22nd/1/18.   As I was not on duty until 4 Pm I caught a Train at 10 am for Peselhoek to go &amp; have a hot bath &amp; after a good run arrived at Peselhoek at 10.45 am &amp; straight away had by Hot Bath, then I had Dinner &amp; spent a couple of hours with my Pall in his Hut, then I left at 3-15 Pm &amp; arrived in Camp at 4 Pm &amp; straight away signed on &amp; went up to St Jean Yard.  Today Wednesday 23rd turned out to be a fine day &amp; up went our Observation Balloons, which Fritz soon started shelling with Schrapnel also Fritz Machines were continually over for Observations purposes &amp; our Anti Air Craft Guns were kept fairly busy blazing away at them.   This was kept up all day until eventide</p>
