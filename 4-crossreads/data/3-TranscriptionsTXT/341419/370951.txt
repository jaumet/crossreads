This is a terribly dull boat.  Nothing to do, nothing to read, and I believe that they will fete us in Melbourne &amp; Sydney which means more time lost. 
 14.12.18 I personally have now given up all hope of spending Xmas on land.  We have only just reached Freemantle [Fremantle] today owing to a heavy head wind &amp; sea most of the way from Colombo.
