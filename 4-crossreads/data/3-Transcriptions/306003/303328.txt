<p>Morrice &amp; Reid reported back in Minia this afternoon &amp; are to ride out this evening;  About a dozen lads made the pace a welter on the beer this afternoon &amp; took a goose &amp; a turkey from the village the goose I managed to get back for the native but it was a different matter with the turkey &amp; it was reported to Mr Garbett;</p>
<p>Had a swim this afternoon &amp; then played bridge &amp; five hundred till bed time, we play cards every night &amp; have cocoa at 9.30 &amp; then turn in;<br/>Received a parcel today from Ede</p>
