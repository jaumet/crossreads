<p><u>NEWS BULLETIN</u><br/><u>Via Wireless per Thursday Island</u>  <u>28th.Oct.1914</u><br/>Violent battle still proceeding in North France and West Belgium,at present 'undecided.<br/>The Allies are generally holding their ground, but the enemy have crossed the River Yser<br/>Germans reported to have sustained enormous losses here.<br/>Russians reported doing well and have crossed the Vistula, but there is little news from this quarter.  First Wireless received at Madang</p>
