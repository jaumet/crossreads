 Monday 16 Coaling finished this morning. The work is done by Blacks more noise than work Native sellers of Fruit & Post cards did a great trade from the Wharf sending there stuff up by a basket tied on a string Several white people came & gave us a lot of fruit a Miss Cambell of the YM did most of it also took letters & P.C. to post.
