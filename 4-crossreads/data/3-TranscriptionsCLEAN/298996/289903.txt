22.5.15I was sent to Palias De Kubbuha to guard fodder, with 14 men, which was a weary job nothing to do. I was very hot here today I had to change the posts every 2 hours. The native were carting the fodder away for the army & what a sight it was to see them loading up their narrow guttered lorry. These lorries are drawn by mules or Arabian horses which are small & dainty.
