<p>1917 Friday 6 April</p>
<p>Good Friday &ndash; no recognition.   <br/>Get our packs fixed for moving off after dinner.    Work the baths in forenoon.   Fall in at 2.30 p.m.   14th Field arrives &amp; takes over.  We move out immediately with full packs &amp; blankets up for Bazentin-le-Petit &ndash; 6 miles further up where the Dressing Station was during the late stunt.  Have 2 rests on the way.  Pass Casualty Corner Pozieres, &amp; thro Contalmaison once agains.   Point out the places of history to the new reinforcements.   Arrive at destination on 5.30 p.m. tea.  Pretty tired.<br/>Receive bundle of papers from Harry &ndash; very welcome as no letters have come to hand for a month past for any body some hitch in the postal service apparently.   <br/>To bed early &amp; have a good old read of local papers, come upon many interesting tit bits.</p>
