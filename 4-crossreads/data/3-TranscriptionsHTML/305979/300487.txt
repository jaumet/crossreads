<p>Dec 10   Fifth Sunday at Sea.<br/>Durban is only a memory now, and once more we are tossing on the deep blue sea, and waiting expectantly for the next port of call, which is Cape Town &amp; which we will most likely reach some time tomorrow morning.</p>
