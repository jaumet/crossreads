<p>August,1918<br/>Monday 5</p>
<p>Villers Brettoneaux. Bty Position<br/>Arrived at possie at 5 am after a tour of the district. Fritz shelling spasms [indecipherable] during the journey up.<br/>At 12 noon orders came to hand to evacuate the trench and Smith and I set to to build a home. Rain settled down at 4 pm and twas still raining at midnight.<br/>Laid O.M. and Gun line . Fritz shelling pretty frequently all day and we had a little gun fire during the night.</p>
