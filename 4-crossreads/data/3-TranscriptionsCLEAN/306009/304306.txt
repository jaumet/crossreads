
1915Apr. 30 Fri.Patients com Wounded commenced to arrive at 9.30 a.m. & kept coming in all day by motor ambulances until 5 p.m. when 250 had been admitted - nearly all minor wounds. Everone excited - wounded have plenty to say about the engagement on the previous Sunday - Large numbers of extra beds are ready - We are also to mann a hospital at Ghezireh Palace Hotel which is to be converted into a Hospital. We are to have 300 beds in it by Sunday ready for more wounded.Number of sick patients admitted since No. 2 G.H. took over Hospital is 3,750.
