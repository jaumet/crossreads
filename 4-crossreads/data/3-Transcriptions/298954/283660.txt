<p>Wed 5th June (cont.)<br/>Spend the afternoon lounging, sleeping, &amp; so forth.</p>
<p>Do without afternoon tea today, being lazy, and at 5 pm serve up.<br/>Fried bacon (a thin shaving each.)<br/>Fried bread.<br/>Bread with treacle or dripping.<br/>Cocoa (<u>with</u> milk &amp; sugar)</p>
<p>These luxuries are not always served.   We either play or watch football from 7.30 to 9.30 p.   More cocoa at 10 p &amp; a walk round our odorous lake telling each other our sorrows beings us one day nearer "Holland or home."</p>
<p>Thu. 13th June.<br/>A big explosion at the Meser (munition factory) about six miles away across the river.</p>
<p>Swimming parties were taken out today for a swim in the river.   I went, of course.</p>
<p>Sat. 15th June.<br/>About 30 English "tommies" arrived today from East Prussia.</p>
<p>Poor devils!   Gaunt, hungry, filthy, &amp; in rags.</p>
<p>Today is bath day, an Event of fortnightly occurrence.</p>
