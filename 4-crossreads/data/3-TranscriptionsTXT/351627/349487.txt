 with the Turks, exchanging cigarettes etc. One (live) Turk had a lot of money on him &amp; he distributed different Turkish coins among our chaps &amp; told them to keep them as souveniers. Don't you think it is funny being so friendly with one another &amp; then firing at each other the next. 
 I am writing from No. 2 Auxiliary Hospital Heliopolis, Egypt, I was wounded on 16th June hit in the hand with a piece of shrapnel. I was lucky I was not blown to pieces, as two shells exploded within five yards
