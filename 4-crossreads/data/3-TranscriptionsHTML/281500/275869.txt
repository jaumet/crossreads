<p>It has been a very miserable day, dull &amp; overcast, &amp; tonight it is raining.</p>
<p>1st January 1917.  We gave the Huns a heavy bombardment last night, &amp; he retaliated but not so fierce as ours he dropped shells all round our trench but hit no one luckily.</p>
<p>This morning the Sergt.
