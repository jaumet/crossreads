<p>52 September 3rd<br/>Invalid officers so as to enable us to go for walks of a Friday. The Commandant when approached on the subject said that this suggestion could not be entertained as we would not be getting exercise by going out in bath chairs &ndash;</p>
<p>September 4th<br/>Spent morning in rearranging cupboard</p>
<p>It is glorious sitting in the afternoon sun I am taking advantage of all the sun I can get, because we will soon have winter on us &ndash;</p>
<p>Exercised my limbs today on parallel bars The Commdt has at least introduced the means whereby we can exercise ourselves occasionally &ndash;</p>
<p>Played bridge &ndash; Recd letter from Mary</p>
<p>September 5th<br/>(See end of Sept 5th)<br/>Good sleep. Had a visit from the Dutch minister today &ndash; He was specially instructed to visit this camp to enquire into state of affairs which at present exist &ndash; needless to say he received complaints from nearly every officer in the camp &ndash; He openly admitted that there was far too many officers in this camp &amp; that we were quite justified in complaining how we were cramped &amp; also as regards having no reading Room &amp;c.
