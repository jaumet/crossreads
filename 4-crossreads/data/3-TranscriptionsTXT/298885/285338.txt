 Cinamon Gardens 
 I am not going to describe the beauty of these gardens because to be frank, I cannot do it. 
 On either side we saw large, beautifully, constructed, cool looking bungalows, the properties of either a Cingalese or a European gentleman. Here &amp; there we saw a bungalow which in pre war days was german property. 
 Passing by the Galle Face Hotel the College and Asylum, all fine buildings we pulled up at the Gate of the Buddhist Temple.
