  
 WINNIZEELE Monday 15 Just been through the dead mens mail it is bad enough for us, but to the folks at home "Oh what anguish Tuesday 16 Had a clean change of clothes, and parted with my little shirt that I've worn for four months Wednesday 17 On a visit to the villiage of Winnizelle. A lot of the old boys have rejoined the battalion. Posted Xmas cards Thursday 18 730 days of soldiering 2 long year's of life days of anxiety nights of fun, a queer mixture still going strong
