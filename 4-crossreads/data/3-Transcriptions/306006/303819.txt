<p>13-4-17<br/>with steep banks &amp; only crossable at certain places, we came to the edge of a small village &amp; found a patch of Echalots &amp; in a mud building alongside two men apparently the owners; About this time machine guns began to rattle, &amp; looking up we found quite close to us &amp; only a few hundred feet in the air, two of our BE2C planes &amp; a new enemy plane above them, our planes exchanged shots but were driven down, one landing at the 2nd L.H.Outpost, the other returning to its hangar; The Bosche had an easy win against odds; Our BE2C planes are too slow for fighting machines &amp; are used for bomb droppings; The new German planes</p>
