<p>WINNIZEELE<br/>Monday 15<br/>Just been through the dead mens mail it is bad enough for us, but to the folks at home "Oh what anguish<br/>Tuesday 16<br/>Had a clean change of clothes, and parted with my little shirt that I've worn for four months<br/>Wednesday 17<br/>On a visit to the villiage of Winnizelle. A lot of the old boys have rejoined the battalion. Posted Xmas cards<br/>Thursday 18<br/>730 days of soldiering 2 long year's of life days of anxiety nights of fun, a queer mixture still going strong</p>
