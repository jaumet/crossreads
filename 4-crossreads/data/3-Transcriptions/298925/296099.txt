<p>The scenery is delightful.  Bathing machines and canvas dressing sheds line the seaside resorts which abound here.<br/>We passed Axminster where the famous carpets are made also an old castle such as we have read about in a forest in which we could see quite a number of deer.  We arrived at Torre station at 7.30 a.m.  Where we were met by the mayor and aldermen of the town with a special 2 decker</p>
