  
 10 "Who are you" we ask of the moving dragon of shadows as it passes "The twenty seventh battalion" it answers "Going in." we enquire Ah that, going in, what does it mean We know, so does every mortal member of that great creeping shadow of the night. Going in - each one to do his bit in the trenches Going in - each one to the battle Going in - some to deeds of daring and to glory Going in - many to that given door which closing opens no more The answer comes in a single word from the ceaseless moving shape "yes'
