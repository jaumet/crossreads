We spent the remainder of the 2nd all day the 3rd arrived at Le Havre during the morn on the 4th at night obtained leave to have a look round the city which is a very decent place but very dirty. Next day we were issued with all line kit left for the Battalion also put the night in, in the train.
Next Saturday the 6th we detrained at Caestre joined the battn. details spent a rather miserable night. Next morning it started to rain we were ordered to pack up marched down to Caestre station where we waited for about eight hours then entrained detrained on the western side of Ypres at dark. We then had to march after going for about an hour discovered we were lost so had to about turn reached the Bn transport lines late at night had a sleep in a tent.
