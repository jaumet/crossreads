<p>3. cool and clean looking, tiled floors, high rooms &amp;c.  We had tiffin as they call it, there and afterwards set out in a motor car to find this young lady.  The Y.W. Secretary came with us as a guide &amp; on our way stopped us at the other two branches of the Y.W.C.A and showed us through them: they also were very fine and representative of a great work being done there.  At last, after about an hour's drive, which by the way, showed us a very good deal of the city, we arrived at the Missionary Settlement for University women where we found the desired lady.  After a little time spent there we proceeded in our car to the top of the hill- Malabar Hill it is called- where we obtained a charming view of the city &amp; harbour.  It really was a beautiful view, with its orientalisms.</p>
