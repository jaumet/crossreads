16 December 1916 SatBad news again from Geoff saying he had had his arm taken off. 10 am. A battalion of highlands with pipes &amp; drums playing passing by on their way to the line
17 December 1916 SunWrote to McAlpine. Spent some time with Norm Burgess. He is going to England to night on furlough, whisky &amp; milk. Stapleton an old school chum comes through sick.
