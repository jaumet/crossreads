Patrols were out searching for him and word was brought in by a Russian to the effect that he had stumbled across a German Machine Gun Nest on one of his journeys. This may have been true. The Germans, whilst they were on the retreat were in the habit of posting a Machine Gun and crew in addition to snippers to retard the advance of our troops.
