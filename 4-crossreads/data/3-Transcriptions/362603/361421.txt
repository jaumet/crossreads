<p>7.  I could write for hours on their ways &amp; their goodness to me &amp; to all wounded Soldiers.  They spend every minute that they can in entertaining Soldiers, &amp; visit Hospitals &amp; take Parties all over London.  Thank Mrs Rogers very very much for Her Kindness in giving me Mrs Harberts address.  I told them what she said about being Careful not to fall in Love &amp; bring one of them back to Australia.  They were very highly amused.  I do not think that it will happen though so tell Mrs Rogers I unfortunately will have to disappoint Her.</p>
