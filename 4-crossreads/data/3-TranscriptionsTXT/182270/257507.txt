 MONDAY, JULY 14, 1919. 
 Went into Quirindi with Peter &amp; Dad 
 in the big car. Collect Rev. E. Pocknall 
 there &amp; got back for lunch. Took the 
 big car down &amp; Pocknall picked 
 a site near M c Ginnity&#39;s at back 
 of my new house. Picked another 
 near Mountain, Boundary &amp; Wheelers 
 junction. He said Mountain bore 
 was no good. He got a site at 
 back of House &amp; one in Woolshed 
 for Dad. 
 Tickle working at fence &amp; trough 
 in Cow paddock. 
 Quainton &amp; two mates started 
 burning off in Wheelers. 
 W. Clarke delivered 360 X bred wethers 
 from Pat Hurley at 4D gate. Tickle 
 took delivery &amp; counted them.
