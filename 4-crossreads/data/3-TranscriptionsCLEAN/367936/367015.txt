
Thursday 27 July 1916Trench digging again in morning.Lecture & bomb throwing in evening.Had to parade in evening and go to station to unload horses and bring to camp.
Friday 28 July 1916Bomb throwing.  Lecture & trench attacks.  On guard for 1 hour & a half in evening as Infantry were rioting prior to leaving for France.  Voulenteered for Machine Gun section.
