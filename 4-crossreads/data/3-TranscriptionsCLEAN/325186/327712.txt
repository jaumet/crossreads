Taking Portsmouth all round I enjoyed myself during the six weeks I was there. I was sent from Portsmouth to Harefield on 12th June.  I went to Glasgow on 17th & came back to London on 19th June.  I had a good time on leave & reported back to No 1 Com Depot  I was classified B. I. A4. Which meant that I was medically fit but dentally unfit  Australia House
