<p>16 October 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Get leave  &amp;  knock round Helouan for a couple of hours but soon get full of it.<br/>The usual pest of locusts have invaded Egypt again just as it did when Moses was here.</p>
<p>1915 October 17<br/>Sunday<br/>20th After Trinity</p>
<p>Church at 10 am<br/>Dad  &amp;  I go for a drive to the Nile   &amp;  see Sakkara Pyramids in the distance<br/>We catch 5.30 pm to Cairo for a ride   &amp;  I get back home about 9 pm</p>
