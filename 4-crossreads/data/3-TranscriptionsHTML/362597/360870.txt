<p>We had a boxing tournament this afternoon. Two of our sergeants fought the preliminary fight. All the other sergeants split into 2 sections, each section taking a man. Neither of the pugs have ever had the gloves on &amp; one was as tall as Mr Park &amp; the other about as tall as Lily. Each party coached his man &amp; had the two combatants as excited as two kids.</p>
