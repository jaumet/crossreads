<p>Tuesday 14 May<br/>Instructed to move out with composite detachment to be attached to D.A. Drivers Hopkins etc. &amp; my own sappers etc.</p>
<p>Fixed re pay etc. Moved off with No. 1 wagon to Longpre arriving 4.30. Parked wagon &amp; spent evening cleaning up.</p>
