 Rafa &amp; were then only 4 miles away &amp;  at 5 p.m. it seemed that we would have to withdraw otherwise we should  run  run a big risk of being surrounded ourselves. Just as the orders to retire was being signalled to the various Brigades, (in fact some units had already received the order &amp; were retiring) the New Zealanders by a most gallant attack captured the main Turkish redoubt nearest the sea. This immediately changed the whole position &amp; a general assault from all fronts on to the remaining earthworks was ordered &amp; ended in our taking  of  the whole position.
