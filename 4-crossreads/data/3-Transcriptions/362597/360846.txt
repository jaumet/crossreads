<p>Sunday 2/1/16 (11)<br/>Church Parade as usual &amp; then read all day. Sacred concert at night in F ward. No officers present. Men &amp; Nurses only. Very nice time.</p>
<p>Monday 3.1.16 (12)<br/>Nothing doing. Troop-Deck Sergeant in morning, looking for piano &amp; played cards.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4.1.16 (13)<br/>I have found out, that to write an interesting diary is very hard work. We have Revailie at 6 am &amp; parade at 6.30 just for Roll Call. Then nothing till Breakfast time at 8 pm [?am]. Fall in at</p>
