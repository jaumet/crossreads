<p>Sunday 23 September 1917<br/>A typical Sunday, sparkling water as far as the eye can reach and birds gracefully skimming over the surface all round the boat.<br/>H.C. 7.45 a.m. in Smoke room, only 4 communicants.  Matins 10 a.m., 14 worshippers.  Where are the others?</p>
