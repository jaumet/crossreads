
Wednesday 31 July 1918Road recco.  In air 2 h. 55 m.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  Went as far as Semakh on the Sea of Galille [Galilee].  Flew most of distance at 2000 ft., gunned many cavalry and stations and aerodrome at Semakh & caused a complete panic.Fired 800 rounds, a good day, felt very fit.
Thursday 1 August 1918Standing by for Hun alarm.Alarm at 12.30 p.m., off ground first & catching up with Hun front when engine went dud frightfully sick.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 25 m.
