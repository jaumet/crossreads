<p>On Jan 2/1921 Charles Hargreaves &amp; William Blake began business as printers &ndash; Blake &amp; Hargreaves Pty Ltd 53/5 George St. Sydney. In 1952 Charles Hargreaves was elected President, Master Printers' &amp; Allied Trades Assn. of N.S.W.</p>
<p>This compliment he greatly prized &ndash; it crowned his life as he died on August 25th 1953 at the age of 66 years during his term of office.</p>
<p>During the whole of his life after his Army discharge in 1919 he was an active member of the R. S. League &amp; was a foundation member of the Sub-branch, Killara.</p>
<p>He had an intense love of Australia &ndash; the land of his adoption.</p>
