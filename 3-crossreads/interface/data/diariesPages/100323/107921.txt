1/7/19 London
A busy day. Went up to the Bank but found it closed owning to a general holiday for balance purposes. Mr Blogg retired from the service yesterday ? Mr Jeans was there but I deemed it wise not to bother him on Balance Day. Went to Belgrave Square on top of a bus and there got orders from Plant. To the Grosvenor Hotel for lunch. Meeting of the Red Cross ? off to Berlin. Then to R P HQ and saw Generals Monash Wisdom and ? the later gave me orders. Then to Horseferry Road to arrange other details. Had an early tea at the Great Western Paddington and went by tube to King's Cross catching a train for Hull. Our journey was a good one and in Lincolnshire and Yorkshire we passed through
