<p>Monday 25 March 1918<br/>Very heavy rain.</p>
<p>Tuesday 26 March 1918<br/>Recco, to start from Jericho, landed at Jericho but recco not done owing to clouds.  Jericho a very small place and not very interesting.<br/>Pilot Lt. Headlam, in air 2 h. 20 m.</p>
