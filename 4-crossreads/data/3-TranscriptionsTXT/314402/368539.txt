 Report night 27/28 Sept. 15 D Coy 12th Inf. Events of night Generally very quiet. Aircraft passed over travelling low towards sea, 2245, returning 0005. Fired on with rifles &amp; also by ship. Remainder of night quiet. Casualties - Nil Bombs - Nil Patrols - Nil Ammunition used - 115 P. Weston L. 12th Bn
