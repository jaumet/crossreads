<p>3 May Saturday</p>
<p>F &amp; Showery</p>
<p>I take Marie to Ramsay. We go to Picture &amp; shopping. I lose her &amp; get wind up about coming home alone. Arrive home &amp; very tired. I am off my tucker &amp; quiet. All others play cards while I watch puzzle. We go to bed &amp; I get my face painted &amp; tucked in. a lovely good night by -----, while all others look for Venus &amp; the moon.</p>
<p>4 May, Sunday</p>
<p>F.<br/>We work at Puzzle especially Marie. I go up to make my bed &amp; M. comes to fix my blind. In afternoon M. sews while I puzzle.</p>
<p>We all sit &amp; try puzzle at night. I on a buffet in front of fire.</p>
<p>Auntie ticks me in this night, elddoc.</p>
