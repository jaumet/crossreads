<p>1916 December 3<br/>Friday<br/>Where a man finds how little he can really live with in a cold winter it makes one think of the large extravagant homes many people keep up &ndash; I see War taxes are now very heavy &amp; all cost of living.<br/>Sailors off the War ships now ashore &amp; bring Chocolate Milk etc. they retail at 500% - viz 1/- Chocs; 30/- the Packet, Milk 5/- [indecipherable] you can always send [indecipherable] if you wish - [indecipherable] save money [indecipherable] life on the Peninsular [indecipherable] the trouble. flies and Fleas nearly all gone.</p>
