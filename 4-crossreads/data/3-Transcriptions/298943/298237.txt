<p>There was a bombardment however, &amp; a pretty heavy one too I heard from Caesar Hood of the transport (motor) who, by the bye, was once the hero in the Cormont burlesque.<br/>The first thing I knew of the results of the raid, was when I rose at about 5 Am when there were many cars coming &amp; going, bringing cases from the line, &amp; taking cases on to C.C.S. The lorries batch, attached to us, did good work in the latter sphere with walkers</p>
