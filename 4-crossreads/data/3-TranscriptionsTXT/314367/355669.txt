
Friday Jan 8-15  I've reopened my Journal - The Eastern awaits the Morinda coming in & we are waiting as well - It means mails - Wednesday it rained heavily & I remained inboard. We started Auction bridge - We thought we ought to learn it because when well played it seems a pretty sure source of income & we must look to the future - One man played with farmers ashore & got 15 - It involves much more cunning & insight as to the declarations & opens up the road for what is known as Bluff - A Machiavellian game - I don't quite know whether my intelligence department approves of dissipating its energy that way -I was the cause of a rumpus among the military on Thursday - It came to my knowledge that my Bluejackets got thro the pickets ashore to Houses of ill Fame - Japanese - I promptly reported to Fitz & he as promptly told the Military - They denied that this could happen & went into the matter but Fritz & I stuck to our guns - told them what
