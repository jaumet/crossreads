<p>&amp; Tommy Depy were pals at Anzac in the dug out next to mine. Now Splinter is in the 9th with Charley, &amp; Tommy is driving a motor ambulance attached to the 3rd.<br/>A typical instance. Tommy got up to Vermoziele when he heard Charley was gassed, &amp; slipped 10 francs in his wallet.</p>
