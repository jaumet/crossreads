<p>The world is so mad &amp; the pace so fast nowadays that when one pauses in the hectic advance &amp; thinks of all the unbelieveable &amp; indelible events that have taken place in the so short time one is assailed by a feeling of unreality. And when all is over &amp; one has time &amp; leisure to plan &amp; look about one, the events of the years of war will assume the blurred proportions of a feverish dream. Only the results will (it is to be hoped at any rate)</p>
