 29.1.18 
 My dear Judge, A mail today brought me your letter of Nov. 30 with the newspaper cuttings of Hughes' treatment at [indecipherable], &amp; C.J. Brennan's letter about mannixism. 
 Those two cuttings were the only piece of journalistic news of Australian doings that I have had for a long while and were an exceedingly interesting peep into what was happening out with you before the recent referendum was taken.
