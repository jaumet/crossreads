 It has been simply a glorious day the sun has been shining nicely, & there has been plenty of heavy shelling & scores of air fights but the Germans have no chance now either with our artillery or aeroplanes, our guns paste them day & night, while as long as there is a streak of light left our planes are flying backwards & forwards & all round their lines. 
 Just about starting to get my voice back again, I have been that hoarse for the last 6 or 7 days that I could hardly speak & when I did it was more like a crow.
