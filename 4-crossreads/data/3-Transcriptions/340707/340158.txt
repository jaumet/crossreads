<p>Thence we adjourned to a Bohemian Club "Nostre Logis" 10 Rue Couston where admission is restricted. Here we had the Bohemian world of Murger &ndash; big hats, craving look, large cravats all proclaimed that status. On old bird [Anoten] is the reputed spendthrift [scion] of the old nobility. Guitar is very much used here &amp; song of <strike>the</strike> love are the common type.</p>
<p>Goyard]recites "J'aime tes cheveux blonds" We talk &amp; yarn &amp; drink (one drink only a glass of water with 3 slices of lemon &amp; sugar 3 fr) &amp; leave about 2.30 am. for a four mile walk home. Still evening has been a pleasant glimpse into Bohemia &amp; I shall often revisit it.</p>
<p>In fact one of the party is a girl with a lovely voice who sing me songs of Chaminade &amp; Verlaine. I agree to continue our acquaintance to morrow. This girl we accompany home &amp; in spite of her education &amp; high poetic soul find she is reduced to ironing by night &amp; washing by day Until 2 years ago she was a dancer but when stagework fell off her old Bohemian instincts reasserted</p>
