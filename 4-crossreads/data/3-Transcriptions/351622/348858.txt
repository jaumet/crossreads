<p>Sat night I came across Jack Cartwright. Just as I arrived home Arnold Markey came to see me.</p>
<p>13 July 1916 Thur<br/>Attended sick parade by Capt Chapman who said I was suffering with a bad heel. The result was that I was put on light duty. Went for a short ride with Bellemey after dinner in hopes of see Jimmy Fay. Couldn't see him as he is about 5 miles away. Met Frank Noble on the way home. In the village [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>[Note in margin]<br/>Received tin of chocolates Mother.</p>
<p>14 July 1916 Fri<br/>Saw Captain Savage on sick parade who put me on no duty. 9.30 general inspection by Major Aidy. The River Somme runs through the town &amp; I very often take a walk down to the bridge to watch the hospital barges coming up. Mail arrives to night receiving letters from Mildred, Thelma, Mother &amp; a card from the Occidental. 19 cases Comforts arrived.</p>
