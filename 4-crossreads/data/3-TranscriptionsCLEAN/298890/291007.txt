  
 27 Thursday Slept & rested & read & in the afternoon Maud & I went for a nice walk. Nice day but cold. 
 28. Friday Same routine. Went to Bedales after tea to see Girlie Smith. 
 29. Saturday. Lovely day. Miss Whishaw Maud & I walked  to Frox  [Froxfield?] in afternoon Girlie Smith & a small companion came.
