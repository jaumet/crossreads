<p>2<br/>What lovely maps of the children. I was delighted with them. I wonder if they will decide to use Moree baths? It makes my mouth water to hear you talk of the lovely peaches etc. Have some for me.<br/>Hope the youngsters sickness did not prove serious. What a dear little letter Margaret wrote me, &amp; was I not just pleased to get it. I will write her one in reply in a few days. I am very sorry to hear</p>
