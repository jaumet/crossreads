  
 Uxbridge [October 1918] 13 Sun Now in paradise at Harefield 14 Mon My tootsies were lanced this morning, May be I will be here 1 week 15 Tues Peace Peace let there be peace, but  on our own terms, for the day is ours by conquest 16 Wed A fellow soon forgets the hardships of war when out of the fighting zones.
