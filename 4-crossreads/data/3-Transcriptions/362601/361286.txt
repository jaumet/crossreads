<p>23 October, Thursday</p>
<p>Ellis &amp; Etta to tea. We play whist. Dinner with Roy. Roy comes Home to sleep. Much argument at Home. Recd letter from Doris &amp; Staples.</p>
<p>Wrote Auntie Annie.</p>
<p>24 October, Friday</p>
<p>Frightful busy day. I cut 10 suits by 5 pm &amp; Otto 23. Dick comes Home but I can't go up to see Him.</p>
