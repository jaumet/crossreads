<p>1915<br/>July 7 Wed.<br/>Another 20 patients were boarded to-day &amp; recommended for various changes.<br/>Kept pretty busy &ndash; the clerical work in connection with admissions is pretty heavy.<br/>A meeting of the Aust. Scouts Assn. was held to-night &ndash; the first for over 3 months. The name was changed to "Imperial Scouts Assoc." so that Scouts serving in the British forces could be admitted as members. 15 were present at the meeting which was held in the Y.M.C.A. rooms, Cairo. 20 new members were enrolled bringing membership up to 89. Meetings are to be held every Wednesday at the Y.M.C.A.</p>
