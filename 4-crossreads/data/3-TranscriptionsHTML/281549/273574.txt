<p>Afterwards we flew the Union Jack at half-mast but were made take it down as flags are not allowed on troopships at sea.</p>
<p>We had a little more Lifebelt &amp; Boat Drill to-day</p>
<p>10th of November Friday<br/>Received Wireless news that the R.M.S. Arabic was torpedoed in the Mediterranean Sea on November 6th at Noon. Kit inspection to-day again.</p>
<p>To-day has been a very hot day &amp; the sea has been like a sheet of glass not even a ripple to disturb the surface, The wake of the ship is visible for about</p>
