
Friday 5thAs usual
Saturday 6thDid about 1 hour P.T. then messed about. No rifles to use as they have been packed for our move to Sutten Veny next week. Collier & I walked to Tesbury a small town 5 1/2 miles away. Half the town is very old the other part near the Railway more modern. A very pretty walk
Sunday 7thA lot of us were about 1/2 a minute late getting on Parade this morning so caught Main Guard
Monday 8thGuard has been quite a good job fine fire in the Guard Room & plenty to eat. The C O sayed we were the worst guard B- Guard he had ever inspected & that we must get extra training We wont so all is well Was in a raffle for a Bike but did not win
