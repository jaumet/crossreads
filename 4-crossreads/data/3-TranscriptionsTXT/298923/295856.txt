
9 July 1918 TuesdayOut wiring last night Jacko shelled the adjoining hill pretty badly & made good fireworks, we dont get home to until midnight or 1 0'clock & stand to arms again at half past 3, so can do a sleep[indecipherable] gone "phut" again-  Reinforcements come, stop a week or 10 days & go, evacuated sick, only a few of the hard old survivors hang on & make the back-bone of each troop-  permanently my nerves are rottenAnyway the type being sent from Australia now are a poor lot & don't seem to last more than a few nights wiring etc:-
