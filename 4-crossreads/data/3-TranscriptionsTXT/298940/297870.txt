
Monday 4th March '18Last night there was another raid, with usual accompaniment of big shells, bombs & machine gun fire. A few shells fell round here, but not within yards of here the dug out. It happened on the right of here, that is the right arm of the A salient, held by the 10th Batt., of which the left arm is held by the 9th batt.
