<p>Sunday 23 July 1916<br/>Wrote home (No. 9).<br/>Heard that the regt. has had a fight &amp; want us as reinfs.  Hurrah!</p>
<p>Monday 24 July 1916<br/>Went to the rifle range in morning but had to return suddenly to get ready to go.  All horses &amp; some men left.</p>
