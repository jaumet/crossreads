<p>Heard that last night a German Plane swooped down near our quarters and a traffic policeman was shot.  More shells dropped round us early in the morning.  Making roads all day.</p>
<p>12th November (Sunday)</p>
<p>Church Parade in morning.  Moving tents and baggage all afternoon, then ordered out after tea to put tents up.    When the "Fall In" blew it was a sight to see a crowd</p>
