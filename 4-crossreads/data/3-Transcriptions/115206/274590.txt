<p>January 1918<br/>Sunday 20</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Macully left for Blighty on leave, Macaffery and Meredith went to report as instructors at R.B.A.A.<br/>Weather very fine.<br/>Big parade on tomorrow general inspection.<br/>Very busy polishing up limbers, harness and etc.<br/>Weather beautiful.<br/>Received a small parcel of socks from Mother and some papers from Blighty and magazines.</p>
