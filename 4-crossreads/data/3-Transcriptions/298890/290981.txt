<p>28. Thursday<br/>Got little work done. In and out of bed, very weak &amp; shivery. Cold &amp; grey &ndash; showers. Miss H. insisted upon reading to me.</p>
<p>29. Friday.<br/>Cold, grey day. Miss Hodge read to me interrupting any attempt to work.</p>
<p>30 Saturday.<br/>Went back to Miss Smith's. Had to go up on top in rain on tram. Found Miss Smith gone to Keighly with Nina Bogle to ask nom. in bye election.</p>
