Inspected Bazaar at Cairo paid guide 10 piastres &amp; driver 4 piastres. Little or no news from The Dardanelles. Drill about 5&frac12; hours &amp; one hours lecture.<br/>20th Parade 6 am &amp; 5pm Lecture. Plenty of ices &amp; drinks. Tucker plain but fairly good.
