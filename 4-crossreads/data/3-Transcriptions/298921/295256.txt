<p>October, 1915<br/>Tuesday 12<br/>If peace Turkey I pay Weston 2/-/-  if not I receive &pound;11/-/-<br/>JB owes me 200 P.T.<br/>The above is a bet re the war made many months ago you see I won it.<br/>Went Cairo to Aerodrome Camp &ndash; there is a difficulty in getting to Anzac owing to trouble in the Canal again &amp; submarines outside Alexandria <strike>a lot of boats have been torpedoed lately</strike><br/>Inoculated again for Cholera, no ill effects from this virus.<br/>Posted notes yesterday.</p>
