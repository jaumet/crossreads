<p>should not be surprised to see anything happen.<br/>8th. Down at Littlehampton again &amp; having a good time on the beach, nearly got run in through taking snap shots of the beach &amp; harbour, but wriggled out of it neatly, the beach is crowded nearly all people from London, a lot of them have rushed down since the raid, got a bit of a fright I expect<br/>9th. Mr Duke &amp; I went to Arundel Castle &amp; Park this evening &amp; strolled all through them the grounds are very beautiful &amp; full of deer.<br/>Ran up to London later on &amp; saw some of the havoc caused by the raid, not as much damage done as you would expect to see</p>
