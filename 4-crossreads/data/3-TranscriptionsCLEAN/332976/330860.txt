
May 191720th.   To-day the section was working about 1/2 a mile this side of Noreuil at the advance dressing station, putting in cupola dugouts in place of rough shelters the which they have had of late.   No 2 section have been working on them for the last three days with the help of a few of our section.   Fritz shelled us during the afternoon but no casualties resulted.The weather continues fine & the aeroplanes have been very active.21st.
