<p>to my wife and one to my sister. At 3 o'clock I boarded a train at Amiens station Dublin &amp; arrived at Great Northern Station Belfast at 10 to 6. I went for a walk after tea tonight purchased some P.C. and some writings &amp; packing of P. Cards for Posting.<br/>I am staying at the Belfast Sailors &amp; Soldiers Service Club. Waring St.</p>
