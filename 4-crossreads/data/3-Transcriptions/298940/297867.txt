<p>Two huge slabs of concrete from the pill box had been lifted en masse &amp; blown a few yards where they lay on their sides in a heap of mud &amp; rubbish. There had once been a Fritz light railway past the pill box, but all that remains to testify to it are half buried rails twisted into grotesque shapes, in shell holes.<br/> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<br/>Nothing doing during the afternoon, but we were just settling down to a final read in the evening about 8.30 p.m. when the Germans raided our trenches</p>
