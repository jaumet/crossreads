 8 Aug, 1916 
 "Pinched from Rowdy book" my Creed 
 I would be true for there are those who trust me, I would be pure for there are those who care. I would be strong for there is much to suffer. I would be brave for there is much to dare. I would be friend to all the [pore?] the friendless I would be giving &amp; forget the gift I would be humble for I know my weakness I would look up &amp; laugh, and love &amp; lift. 
 The Road to El Arish 
 On the road to El Arish, Oh it aint the place youd wish For a picnic with your [indecipherable] or a spell For the sand flies over all &amp; your horse can hardly crawl And your twenty miles from  what they call a well And the water when you get it well you try hard to forget it For it taste as if it 'ad been drunk before And the sun is over head pourin down like molten lead Why the 'ell did El Arish come in the war   Why the 'ell did El Arish come in the war
