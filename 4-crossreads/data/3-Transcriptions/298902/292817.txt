From the Canteen we obtained 3 turkeys and our Christmas boxes from Australia we collected by car from Solre le Chateau so that we are practically now set up.  Other matters such as the drawing up of the menu etc also had to be done.  Funds to the extent of 600 Francs we drew from our funds at the Pay Office.</p>
