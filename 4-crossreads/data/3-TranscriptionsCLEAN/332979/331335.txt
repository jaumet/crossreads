
This took up the morning & .in the Afternoon we had some more drill, as it was very Cold in fact Snowing on & off all hands enjoyed it as it kept us warm. After Tea I & my chum went to hear the Artillery Band give a Instrumental Concert from 7 to 8 Pm & then we This band is composed of Returned Heroes & they are all masters of their Instruments. They played splendid together & received a good reception from the large crowd of Boys, it was held in the Garrison Theatre a very fine building afterwards we went into the Salvation Army Reading Room where a Royal Engineer was playing the Piano, after listening to several Singers I obliged the Company with two Songs at 9 Pm after having a Cup of Cocoa & Bun we made
