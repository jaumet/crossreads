<p>Wednesday 13 February 1917 [1918]<br/>&frac12; day &amp; went Muski with Sister Watson at 2 p.m. till 4 p.m. &amp; bought brass &amp; various table cloths, runners (colored all) for sending home as brass is so heavy to send then joined Y.W.C.A. Suliman Pasha, Connaught House.<br/>Afternoon tea Groppi's very full.  Sister inclined to be tearful but does not complain of her hard luck.  Garry back to 27th for 7.15 dinner.  All electric light disappears 7.10 p.m. but candles are stuck in plant pots &amp; the meal progresses as usual.</p>
