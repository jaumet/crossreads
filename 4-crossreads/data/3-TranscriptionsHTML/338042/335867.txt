<p>West Cliff, not forgetting a lift &ndash;ride up the side of the Cliff &ndash; Eric &amp; I discuss Old Days &amp; Predict future events. A word must be said about the  Municipal Orchestra at the Winter Garden &ndash; Under Mr. Dave Godfrey they are responsible for fine musical treats &ndash; The "Coves" (between shows) are entertained by "Miss Mac &amp; Mrs Fullarton (our friends from "Overseas Club, Glasgow) &ndash; In five shows I recite "Sari Bair", "Singing Soldiers", "Prayer" &amp; "The Tent &ndash; The Original Wests Picture House is to be found in Bournemouth</p>
