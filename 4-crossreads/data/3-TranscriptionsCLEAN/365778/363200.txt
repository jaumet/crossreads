  
 Sunday 10/6/17 Tactical schemes all day without troops. Battalion at musketry. Monday 11/6/17 Training generally, rumours of move. Tuesday 12/6/17 Usual training, orders to move on Saturday. Wednesday 13/6/17 Training in morning, cricket match against 27th in afternoon, won by us. Thursday 14/6/17 Training generally. Concert at night at Bouzincourt - purchased the Anzac Coves for the night. Friday 15/6/17 Defeated 7th Eng at Cricket. Field firing in afternoon, dined at Corps School.
