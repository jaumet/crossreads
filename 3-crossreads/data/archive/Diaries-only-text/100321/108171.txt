8/2/19 Gourdinne
Bitterly cold. Ears feel it. The snow lies inches thick on the ground and a light North wind cuts like a knife. A bright sun devoid of warmth makes a glare. Towel like a board ? hairbrush and toothbrush also. Could not muster energy enough to do anything but cling close over the fire in my room. In the afternoon essayed a walk but going in the snow was heavy and the uncomfortable cold soon drove me back home. Life here is getting rather a bore and I shall not be sorry when our move makes its appearance. Pat Gorman is still hard at work on the history and has this evening gone to Corps H.Q. to see if he can find out the why and the wherefore of a premptory summons there timed for noon tomorrow.
