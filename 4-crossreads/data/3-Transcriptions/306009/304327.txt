<p>1915<br/>May. 21 Fri.<br/>Busy all the morning getting out Returns etc.  Lance Cpl. Hill came out of Hospital (influenza) to-day so that now there are two of us in the Admin. Office.<br/>Went into Town (Cairo) in the afternoon &amp; was measured for an issue of khaki drill coat &amp; long trousers.<br/>During the summer which has now commenced, the shops are closed from noon to 3 p.m. &ndash; the hottest part of the day.<br/>Saw a very pretty grotto in the Esbekieh Gardens to-day. We also have a very interesting grotto in the grounds of Gezira Palace.</p>
