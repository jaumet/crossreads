 At Sea 
 Dear Mother 
 We have left our first port of call in Springbokland and are nearing our second. The weather bids fair to be calm round the Cape &amp; I hope it is. So far I'm feeling very well indeed. 
 Give my love to all 
 Your loving son 
 Dene 
 Mrs. Arthur Fry  Northcote Rd Lindfield New South Wales Australia
