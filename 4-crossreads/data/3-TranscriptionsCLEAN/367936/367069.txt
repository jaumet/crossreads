  
 Sunday 12 November 1916 Got ready for church but parade was cancelled owing to having to clean camp for General's inspection.  Harry Jackobs and I went away and practiced signalling.  On telephone from 6 to 10 p.m. 
 Monday 13 November 1916 Buzzer practice in morning then flag drill.  Telephone from 10 p.m. till [indecipherable].
