The King arrived at 11.15 on a coal black horse When the whole of the troops came to the "present" and the Massed Australian Bands played the Royal Salute of 6 Bars of "God Save the King".  With the king came a group of about 20 military officers and a banner bearer carrying a flag with the King's coat of arms.  On the flagstaff at the saluting base also floated the King's flag.
