<p>[Wednesday 20 December 1916]</p>
<p>Anniversary of our evacuation of ANZAC 20/12/15.<br/>Bombed again &amp; a few casualties among Infantry.<br/>( The rest of this page is crossed out)<br/>I see by Day's letter Mother walked to church &amp; back how splendid &amp; I am so glad to think the way home is so pretty.  I felt a bit selfish getting married, but it may be the best for all of us some day-  I hope.<br/>Please thank Aunt Louise &amp; K for letters I'll write when [indecipherable]<br/>The Irish relation said they all thought I would marry K as I used to speak so much about her 16 years ago in Ireland.<br/>To Mazar on a Board re a lost Bag of Mail, shifted Camp to Gererat.</p>
