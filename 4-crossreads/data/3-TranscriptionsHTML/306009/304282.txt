<p>1915<br/>Apr. 6 Tue.<br/>This afternoon 1 officer, 1 Sgt. &amp; 3 Privates were detailed for duty on a Troopship &amp; left the Unit.<br/>Mail day to-day &ndash; received 6 letters.<br/>Another dust storm blowing  to-day.<br/>Very little work to do &ndash; things are very slow &ndash; Would have been more active had I <strike>joined</strike> enlisted in a Field Ambulance or the Signal Engineers.<br/>So far there have been 27 deaths since we took over the Hospital.</p>
