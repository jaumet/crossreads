
11. ThursdayDrizzle in morning &  Dense fog in afternoon.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10.  Got home at six.
12. FridayPartly grey but warm & sunny in afternoon.  Went to Miss Newcomb in morning & to see Maud in afternoon.  Came home tired & went to bed.
13. SaturdayDull &  damp.  Was at Mrs. Newcomb's all day.
