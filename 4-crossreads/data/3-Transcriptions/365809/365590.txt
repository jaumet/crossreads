<p>I had a most delightful, though all too short, visit to Worcestershire the other day . Unfortunately my host was not able to be there  His Parliamentary duties kept him in town. He is Financial Secretary to the Government, and a junior Lord of the Treasury &ndash; rather an interesting job these days &ndash; I got back to London just in time for this Zepp. Raid.</p>
