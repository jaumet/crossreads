<p>There is also a fair bit of a moon showing tonight, &amp; the night working parties can easily do with it for it is rotten working in the pitch dark.</p>
<p>Well this is the end of another small diary making me 5 in all I must try &amp; get these 3 home safely somehow, for they are much better than letters &amp; are absolutely true in every respect, neither being exaggerated nor understated but plain unvarnished facts of my life &amp; every days doings &amp; what we see going on around us from day to day, &amp; if this book gives anyone as much pleasure reading it as I have had writing it well it will be time well spent on my part.</p>
