<p>Boy's High School<br/>Darilevu, Fiji<br/>12th Feb 1917</p>
<p>My very dear Father.</p>
<p>Herewith please find my cheque for &pound; 3:4:0 made up as follows</p>
<p>Premium Mercantile M. Co<br/>Accident Policy  2:3:0</p>
<p>Payment of Loan (Instalt)  1:0:0</p>
<p>Exchange 1:0</p>
<p>3:4:0</p>
<p>Re Total Abstainers Abatement &ndash; I can hardly sign declaration as upon one occasion recently I was induced to try a glass of light Danish Lager which was being taken by one of our men as a Tonic. I notice too in the Declaration that no provision is made for Stimulants ordered by Medical Adviser to.</p>
<p>I want to get this business letter safely out of the way in case there is a rush later for mail.</p>
<p>With much lolma</p>
<p>Your loving son,</p>
<p>J H L Waterhouse</p>
<p>P.S.</p>
<p>Under separate cover am forwarding per Registered Post 1 Pkt Photos &ndash; was much interested in them &ndash; my own from  Lau are being developed in Suva &ndash; some may be in time to forward this mail.</p>
