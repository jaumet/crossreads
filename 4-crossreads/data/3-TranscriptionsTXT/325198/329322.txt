 25 June, 1916 Etmaler After stables this morning I came over to the seventh camp about ten minutes walk, where there is a Y.M.C.A. tent its a fine institution, one can do a bit of writing here its a bit quiet &amp; cooler than the tent. 
 ---  "An' square an' all, no matter 'ow yeh' start, The commin end of most of us is &ndash; Tart"
