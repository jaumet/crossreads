<p>Saturday 22 June<br/>Move up to A.D.S. at Borre. A stunt is coming off to-night or to-morrow night.<br/>Still fine weather.</p>
<p>Sunday 23 June<br/>Glorious weather &amp; to-night a stunt is to come off to the left of Meteren as far as the 1st Aust. Division is concerned.  Some Americans, with full packs have gone past here to-day, up to the line.</p>
