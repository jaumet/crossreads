<p>are strapped on their back, something after the "thermos" style are these dixies &amp; will keep food warm for 24 hours so you can see they are a good thing, what a struggle it is for the men who have to carry this food to the firing line I pity the poor devils for they have to force their way through a regular glue pot &amp; it drags the life out of them besides they keep on falling in to big shell holes for it is almost impossible to dodge them in the dark the land is just honeycombed with them &amp; 9 out of 10 are half full of water so the state this particular party generally arrives home in is more easily imagined than described.
