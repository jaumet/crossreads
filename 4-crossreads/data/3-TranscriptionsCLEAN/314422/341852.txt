  
 arrived at Holyhead at 12.30 in the night and boarded a train which was waiting for us. On our way to London we passed through Crewe and arrived at Euston Station soon after 6. Then I went ,after having a cup of tea ,by the tube railway to St James Station en route to the War Chest Club where I had breakfast and done some writing.
