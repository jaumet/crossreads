 Saturday 12 August 1916 Still waiting. Took my boots off for first time &amp; found my feet still there. 6th &amp; 7th Regts. passed in evening going back to camp. 
 Sunday 13 August 1916 Left Ogratina in morning and came back to camp.  Lovely to change &amp; have a wash again. Colin Sams came over in evening.  Heard that we are to be sent back for a rest.
