<p>1915<br/>June 17 Thu.<br/>Wrote letters in the afternoon &ndash; Have nearly every afternoon clear now as get all work practically done during the morning.<br/>Took Commissioner Stock into Cairo in the evening &amp; showed him round the city &ndash; he was interested in the strange people &amp; their curious customs.<br/>We are all just about full of Egypt &amp; anxious to get a "bit nearer". Perhaps we shall as it is too hot for wounds to heal properly &amp; quickly.</p>
