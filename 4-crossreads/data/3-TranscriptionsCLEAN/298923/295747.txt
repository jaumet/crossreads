
22 March 1918 Friday (Whole page crossed out)Bethlehem    Also Australasians-3 more letters  Mother & Day Feb 3rd & one from May addressed as usual to Captain Weir.  I did Cable sorry never delivered.You would enjoy all these old Holy places, am afraid the average soldier then is past much fervour now here-One soldier blew an oil lamp out in the Holy Sepulchre that had been burning 2000 years or more.  Such is notoriety-   The poor give oil to keep the lamps burning in the Tabernacles-
