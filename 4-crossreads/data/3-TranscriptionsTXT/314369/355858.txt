14 July 1915Wednesday
Well this is a rather personal day for meI am 20 yrs old today so have reached my first score.We receive an advance of 10/- pay in anticipation of our visit to Imbros.Weather beautiful

Weather still HotProvide working parties for the new water works which is being undertaken by the Engineers so as to be able to supply all the outlying posts without having to carry the water long distances
