<p>11 June 16<br/>At sea</p>
<p>12 June 16<br/>Arrived at Grand Cayman &amp; anchored for a few hours &amp; left again for Jamaica.</p>
<p>13 June 16<br/>Arrived at Kingston Jamaica &amp; tied up alongside pier in morning watch. Received a mail from Australia. HMAS Sydney is here.</p>
<p>14 June 16<br/>At Kingston. Coaling ship all day.</p>
<p>15 June 16 )<br/>16 June 16 )<br/>17 June 16 )  Alongside pier at Kingston<br/>18 June 16 )<br/>19 June 16 )</p>
<p>20 June 16<br/>Went down &amp; anchored at Port Royal. Sydney also anchored at Port Royal.</p>
<p>21 June 16<br/>Sydney done firing, while we towed target for her. We came back &amp; anchored off Kingston pier. Sydney</p>
