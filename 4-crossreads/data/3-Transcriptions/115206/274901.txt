<p>November 1918.<br/>Wednesday 27</p>
<p>Wembley. On furlough<br/>Spent a very pleasant day up town, avec chere Vic and O.<br/>Wired to Smithy to come on down<br/>Weather wet and miserable.<br/>Met quite a number of O's pals at the office.<br/>Had a very nice musical evening in honor of Vics departure of France tomorrow.<br/>In bed at 3 am tomorrow.</p>
