<p>Monday 25 September 1916<br/>Out at Rifle range all day near Mount Meridith [Meredith].  Shot fairly well.</p>
<p>Tuesday 26 September 1916<br/>Getting ready to leave.  Got fountain pen from boys.<br/>Went out and took a photo of Bill Graham's grave which Haliday had found and put a cross on.</p>
