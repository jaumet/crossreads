<p>Round the inside of the roof where the supports join are leering, grinning, heads looking down hideously at you.<br/>Another addition, The Chapel of Jesus has beautiful vaulted roof and is accounted for in the following manner.<br/>In 1280 Queen Guinivere, not wishing her eldest son to come to the throne, her youngest being her favourite, she determined to have him killed.  She gave him wine to drink out of the Loving Cup and while he was drinking had him stabbed in the back from behind</p>
