 preliminaries over we repaired to the bath room originally the laundry wash house The floor was covered with tubs filled with hot water from a hose in which we had our bath 
 Finished that we went under a hot shower &amp; then a cold plunge dresses into our clean underclothing &amp; pressed tunics etc &amp; we came out feeling as fit &amp; fresh as possible 
 Bac St Maur Railway Station was shelled this morning &amp; a number of our waggons which were drawing supplies had to make a hurried exit luckily no one was hit.
