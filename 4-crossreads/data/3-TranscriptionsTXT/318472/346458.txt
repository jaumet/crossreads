 21 August 1918 Wednesday Left Cape Town about 11.30 a.m. Soon after leaving Port we ran into a very dense fog &amp; had to slow down for about an hour "Sussex"  left just in front of us &amp; we passed her just before dark. Past a Boat about 9 P.M. Beautiful clear night Left one man in Hospital. 
 1918 August 22 Thursday An uneventful day. There is a fairly heavy swell at times and the weather is very much cooler. At times the wind is almost cold. We are making good steady progress doing a little over 10 Kts pr hour.
