
October 1915
Monday 4
"Shell Green" dressing station for 24 hrs duty. Received letter from Prof. Helmy of Cairo & letter from J.W.Holt !
6 casualties up till sunset, all resulting from a violent artillery attack in the morning.
The shells burst all round the dressing station & there was a constant rain of pellets & lumps of [indecipherable] all round, right up to the very the door of the shed.
Demonstration at 8.15 p.m. with vast amount of artillery & lot of rifle fire but no casualties (only a sham).
