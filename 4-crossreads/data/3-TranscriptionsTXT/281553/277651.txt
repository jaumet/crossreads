19th TuesdayAnother 'Parapet scene' & exchange as on 18th, also seen on Courtneys Post till Germans opened fire with artillery & Rocket Bombs of which they placed 17 along their lines with much apparent effect, remainder of day fairly quiet.  Evening at Cards.
20th WednesdayBusiness as usual, nothing out of ordinary.
21st ThursdayFair amt. of Artillery fire from both Navy & Batteries.  Cards.
