<p>[Saturday 26 August 1916]</p>
<p>Calm.  Bound for Southampton [indecipherable] Blind &amp; unfit to travel  100's of Stretcher cases be good to crippled men after this war.  I'm thankful I'm able to get about-<br/>Still we are all merry &amp; bright.<br/>The boat is a blaze of light at night &amp; so different to a Troop ship.<br/><strike>Of course I'm going to Dotty but there's only one [indecipherable] &amp; you Mother I hope you get my letter &amp; photos from Alex.</strike>  Only 4 Australian officers on board so we must be careful per example</p>
