<p>Thur. Apr. 20th -16<br/>Done 24 hours duty as telephonist at Battalion Hd. Qtrs. 2nd Infantry Bde.</p>
<p>Fri. Apr. 21st &ndash; 16<br/>Back at O.P.  Numerous air duels.  Shells dropped about 50 yards away which started us thinking.</p>
<p>Sat. Apr. 22nd -16<br/>At Battalion Hd. Qtrs. again.  Machine-gun firing on both sides extra heavy.  Our snipers broke 7 enemy periscopes.</p>
