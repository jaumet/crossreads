<p>Saturday 18 January 1919<br/>Did Versailles with the YM &amp; gee it was great. also went to the opera with them &amp; saw A Ida. Was exceptionally good specially the orchestra.</p>
<p>Sunday 19 January 1919<br/>Went to see the Australians play rugby against the french. Very poor game. Diggers won by 1 try to nil.</p>
