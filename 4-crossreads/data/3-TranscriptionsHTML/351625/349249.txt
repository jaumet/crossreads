<p>can get back to Shellharbour &amp; the war over or the end in view any rate I think this spring will bring things to a close.  England &amp; Germany cant last for ever.  I am writing this sitting on my bunk in a large tunnel, we are always living in tunnels lately just like rabbits, they make good billets as we can sleep without fear of a blooming shell coming through on us.</p>
