 22 April 16 Received mail this morning. Left Trinidad &amp; proceeded to Scarborough, on the island of Tobagos. 
 23 April 16 Easter Sunday Anchored at Scarborough Tobago 
 24 April 16 East Mon Heaved anchor &amp; left for British Guiana 
 25 April 16 At sea 
 26 April 16 Arrived off Georgetown on the Demerara River in British Guiana. There is only leaf to Petty Officers &amp; Chief Petty Officers, so us common sailors could not go ashore. 
 27 April 16 Anchored off Georgetown. 
 28 April 16 Left British Guiana, bound for St Lucia. 
 29 April 16 At sea
