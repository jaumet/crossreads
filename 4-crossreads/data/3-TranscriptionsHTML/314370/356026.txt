<p>May, 1916<br/>Saturday 13<br/>Exam Mechanism, my class very anxious  one Sgt got no marks at all.<br/>After dinner went into town &amp; out to Ghezireh garden   later to Kursaal<br/>Temp 119&deg;</p>
<p>Sunday 14<br/>After lunch I went out to Patti's &amp; later to another friend with Miss Patti<br/>Met several nice young ladies &amp; 3 more British N.C.Os.<br/>All to leave Cairo &amp; Heliopolis stopped to A.I.F &amp; N.Z.E.F.<br/>Temp 115&deg;</p>
