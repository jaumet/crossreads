<p>Diary of Private James E. Bell<br/>1st Pioneers, A Company, 1st Battalion, A.I.E.F.</p>
<p>December 11th, 1915:<br/>Left Liverpool 9.10 train, on to Rhodes, embarked on ferry, down Parramatta to Sydney aboard Mooltan;  sailed 1.30;  dinner 2 p.m. Retired 8.30, slept on deck, shifted.  Issued Hammocks and life-belts.</p>
<p>December 13th:<br/>Melbourne 9 a.m.  M.O. Inspections.  Leave granted, 10 a.m. to 12 midnight.  Look round town;  afternoon, car through Doncaster, back to town had tea, marched through St. Kilda to Luna Park, aboard again 11 p.m.</p>
<p>December 14th:<br/>Sailed 3 p.m.  Port Adelaide.</p>
<p>December 16th:<br/>Arrived Port Adelaide 7.30 a.m.  Left 6.45 p.m.  Lovely trip.  Good weather.</p>
