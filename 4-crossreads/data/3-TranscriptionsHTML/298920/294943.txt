<p>early this morning and by 9am we were abeam of Table Mount the top of which was enveloped in its table cloth of mist, a little later we passed Slangkop where the Cape Wireless Station is situated.  The whole coast line surrounding it is very rugged, and some of the peaks are a good height.  The sea (following us) hereabouts was very rough, and the P&amp;O branch liner Geelong which was sighted astern about 11.30 could be seen rolling heavily, though our boat was only rolling slightly.
