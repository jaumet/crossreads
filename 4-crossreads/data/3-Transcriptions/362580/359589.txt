<p>1st Australian General Hospital<br/>Egypt 8/11/15</p>
<p>My dear Mrs. Thistlethwaite,</p>
<p>Every good wish to you all for Xmas &amp; the best of luck for the New Year.  Edith &amp; I are still together, both very well, it is so nice still being together like this &amp; only hope we will remain together.  The weather now is just beautiful quite like an Australian Spring</p>
<p>Lots of love to you all</p>
<p>Yours very sincerely<br/>Dorothy Newton</p>
