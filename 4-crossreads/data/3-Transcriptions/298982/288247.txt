<p>May 12th<br/>Came to anchor on Eastern side of the peninsula at some Bay Heavy bombardment down coast We moved up the coast noon off a supply base They were giving the Turks hell on a coast ridge all noon &amp; night. Heavy &amp; continuous rifle fire above supply base Also the base was being shelled by shrapnel for a while.</p>
<p>We landed per Torpedo boat</p>
