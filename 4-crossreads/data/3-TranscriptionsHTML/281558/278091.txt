<p>Saturday March 2nd</p>
<p>Omicourt :cold bleak day : on at 9 am ran hospital train to Brie : off 11 pm</p>
<p>Sunday March 3rd</p>
<p>Omicourt : Got a bundle of 25 letters : packed up &amp; went to Misery : "phones on at noon so I had to open up &amp; start working : Gill came along in afternoon : when we get fixed up properly it ought to be a decent job : had a sleep after tea :</p>
<p>Monday March 4th</p>
<p>Misery : on till noon : done a few odd jobs in afternoon : bed after tea : light snow during day :</p>
<p>Tuesday March 5th</p>
<p>Misery : on till noon : straightened things up till tea time then bed : cold bleak day :</p>
