24/3/18 ? La Basse Ville
An officer of a special Gas company called in early. An enthusiastic youth whose preparation caused recipients to vomit and so pull off their masks thus getting their lungs full of poison. A fine day but a thick haze is hanging about. Went up to the O.P. of from there got a good view of the country as far as the haze permitted. The village of Warneton looks like Warlencourt used to when viewed from Gallwitz Tr. . A number of 5.9's were being chucked into La Basse Ville and sending up big columns of dirt and debris. Had a talk to a Tommy sound ranging
