<p>Sunday 3.3.18<br/>Very cold &amp; quiet in the town. Did very little other than writing my usual weekly letters.</p>
<p>Monday 4.3.18<br/>Played billiards during morning.  Went Whist Drive at night at Hospital &amp; won "Booby" prize; had a grand night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5.3.18<br/>Went to "Boots" to lunch in morning &amp; to pictures in afternoon.  Caught 5.25 pm train arrived Bristol 6.18 pm.  Had to wait Bristol till 8.45 pm to catch Warminster</p>
