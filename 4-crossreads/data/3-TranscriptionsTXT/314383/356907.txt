
July 19thUp till 2am last night arranging details.  Reveille 4.30 am.  Took over money ammunition guns  & horses for officers, mules for Sgts.  Set off at 8 am escorted by one troop of Cavalry Major Moore & Capt Reid who arrived at Bijah 4 days ago set off with us.  Lt Col Bridges in command of Cavalry (14th Hussars) Camped at Ponja 20 miles.
July 20thArrived at Kizil Bulahk 18 - 20 miles.  Had a go at Crawley-Boevey.  He is years senior to me & tried to come on seniority  bounce & take command.  Showed him my orders & threatened to send him back if he gave any trouble.
