<p>Tuesday 20 August 1918<br/>Road photography.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 2 h. 35 m., camera jamms, it is cleared.  No excitement.</p>
<p>Wednesday 21 August 1918<br/>Bombing &amp; gunning at Amman &amp; Kissir.  Pilot Lt. Towling.  In air 2 h. 50 m.<br/>Bombed armoured train, horses etc. with splendid results from 1000 ft.<br/>Gunned transport at Ain es Sir.<br/>Murdered the horses and caused a Hellish panic from 500 ft., fired 1000 rounds.</p>
