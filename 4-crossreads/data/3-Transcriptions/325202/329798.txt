<p>Borealis. Corlette was back at Strazeele 8 miles in the rear with his waggon &amp; says there are big bodies of cavalry now billetted there waiting for a chance which I hope they'll get.</p>
<p>July 3/16 Monday<br/>On Saturday afternoon 'Scottie' (N.R. Scott) look me up he had just come out the day before from 2 months in the trenches. He was my great pal at John Sands Ltd in Sydney &amp; this is the first time we have met since I left Australia, although we have been chasing each other for the last 12 months <strike>ever since he arr</strike> We celebrated the "meet" by an extempore supper at the nearest 'Estaminet'</p>
