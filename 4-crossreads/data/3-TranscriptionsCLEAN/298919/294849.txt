When the British monitors were bombarding the German lines at Nienfort last Spring the Germans made many unsuccessful to torpedo some of them.  After tea strolled down town.  Was walking aimlessly along when the 4th Mate coming from the opposite direction stopped me.  At that moment I noticed the 3rd Mate standing on the corner, so we went over to him.  We had not been talking two minutes when the purser happened along.  So the four of us stood there yarning till the 3rd despairing of his girl turning up suggested we should walk along the street.
