 4 February 1918 Monday (Whole page crossed out) Censored Letters from Mother Dec 23rd no mention of receiving my cable on arrival Egypt. Hope ere this you are in Melbourne. Just had my PAY Book debited  half pay  during time I was away- did not anticipate this- [indecipherable] thank you &amp; acknowledged muffler long since &amp; am wearing Days soks now- but cold weather nearly over now-  I hope Joe got to Warrnambooll  Some day, we may go again to the Hopkins.
