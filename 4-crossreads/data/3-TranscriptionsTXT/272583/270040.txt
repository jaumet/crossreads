Also mail from home Moved into stream at 6.30 pm to make way for the remainder of the fleet. 8 pm Are now anchored in Mediterranean.  4th Very Dull day. Sea dead calm. I have been ill for past two days and am still feeling bad. Will be moving off some time to-day. We have been ordered to pack our kits as we will be disembarking to-morrow at 7 am. Moved off out 5.30 pm. Very cold. Another mail, one from    indecipherable   5th Raining steadey, Heavy sea Arrived at Alexandia at 8 am  Everything ready for disembarkation. Later :- As usual there is some hitch. We are not to land till Monday There are innumerable sea gulls flying about here. The most I have seen so far.
