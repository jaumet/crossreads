<p>AUSTRALIAN CLUB</p>
<p>Sydney<br/>September 22. 1916</p>
<p>Dear Judge Ferguson<br/>My wife and family and I am very much touched by your kind letter. Your high estimate formed from your experience of Bert [indecipherable] has been justified in the manner of his death. The chaplain who was in the field with the 53rd Battalion in writing</p>
