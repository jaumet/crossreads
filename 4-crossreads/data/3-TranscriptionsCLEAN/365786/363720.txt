  
 What does Conscription mean? The dictionary tells us that Conscription means "obtaining recruits by compulsion" & this is what we are asked to vote "for or against" next Saturday.   Now what is it that we are asked to decide.   We are asked if we are willing to keep up our A.I.F. of 100,000 men to its full strength or no.  To do that we require 16500 men every month & under the voluntary system at present in vogue we are only securing 6000 men per month.  We have given the voluntary system a fair trial & it has failed;   so it  so it remains for us to decide whether we shall honour our promise or let the name of A.I.F. be but a memory.
