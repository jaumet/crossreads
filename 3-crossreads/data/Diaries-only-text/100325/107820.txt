19/8/15
He treacherously killed all the Beys one hundred years ago and then built this mosque to recover his reputation. The alabaster gives the mosque an ancient look. The courtyard and washing fountain Clocktower from king of France. Inside Tomb of M Ali. The floor richly covered with carpet. The chandeliers glittering with glass ? the centre one enormous ? 2000 lights in mosque at night. Reading desk and pulpit. Rising sun near pulpit, setting over door. Four enormous pillars near supporting dome ? not alabaster on account of great weight. The stone cornerwise quite transparent when lighted match held behind it. Outside through Gurkha and Sikh wounded to ramparts. The whole city spread out at our feet. The Nile like a silver ribbon and the green band of palm trees
