 16-12-16 plane was sighted &amp; the Mazar guns opened fire &amp; 3 of our machines rose the Taube dropped her bombs doing no damage &amp; ran, one of our planes though much lower gave chase they exchanged shots at long range &amp; after they were out of sight we could still hear their machine guns at work, our 'plane returned half an hour later flying very high.
