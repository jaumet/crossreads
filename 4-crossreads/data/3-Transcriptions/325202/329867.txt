<p>on detachment with Colin Hall with 'Don Ack' (Divisional Artillery) expect to pull out in a few days &ndash; it is common rumour that we are going North to the Belgian Coast for a big push against Ostend &amp; Zeebrugge a few days will show whether rumour is right or wrong &ndash;</p>
<p>Things very quiet here this last hew days hardly any artillery fire &amp; even aircraft duels are rare enough to awaken ones interest.</p>
<p>July 11/17. Wednesday. Sarton<br/>We 'pulled out' to near Albert five days ago &amp; I rejoined the Coy. with my limber. Yesterday afternoon we picked up our loads of Rations after Reveille at 3 a.m. We left</p>
