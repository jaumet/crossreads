However I learned a little this morning.In the afternoon we collected together some gloves, and a medicine ball (a football stuffed with rap, for throwing & catching) & also a punching bag, & rallied for the sea shore. There we indulged in antagonistic games with the medicine ball, walking, wrestling & so forth.Being Easter Monday there were some girls, out for the afternoon, resting in the sand
