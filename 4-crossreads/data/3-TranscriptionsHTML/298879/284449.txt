<p>October 1915<br/>Saturday 9<br/>Fine. Rained heavily last night. We were snug in our tents though although we had about 27 men in the tent including a guard and two prisoners. There is not much fear of the rain coming in now.</p>
<p>Sunday 10<br/>Fine Church parade at C. of E. Service. Had a little cricket practice on the oval sports Ground with W. Coy.</p>
