<p>1915<br/>June 24 Thu.<br/>Work has been pretty easy lately  - Start about 8.15 a.m. with "Shay" (tea) at 11, knock off at 12.30 - dinner &amp; usually  no more work for the day although there must always be one chap in the office out of 3.<br/>After dinner got busy on a big juicy watermelon. Pretty good &ndash; Betcher!<br/>Spending the afternoon at French &amp; Arabic.<br/>4 of the Westminster Dragoons including a Covent Garden singer, gave a concert at 8 p.m.</p>
