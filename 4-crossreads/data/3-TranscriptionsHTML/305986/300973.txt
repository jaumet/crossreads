<p>"Dinkum Oils"</p>
<p>The "dinkum oils" come pouring in,<br/>Morning noon and night;<br/>Startling news of every kind,<br/>All about the fight.<br/>Heres a sample of the kind,<br/>Given by those who know,<br/>Based on a batman's whisper soft,<br/>Then Rumour's on the go</p>
<p>Russians Capture twenty towns;<br/>Five thousand tommies killed;<br/>No recruits are offering now,<br/>'Cause their feet are chilled.<br/>No more houses to be cleaned;<br/>Leave to Ireland stopped;<br/>No more beer is sold to troops<br/>Dage's [?] cut and chopped</p>
<p>We are going home quite soon</p>
