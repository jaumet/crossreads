 &amp; since my coming have taken things easily &amp; much appreciate the quiet &amp; the surroundings in a country that is British &amp; speaks one's own tongue.  The contrast between the English &amp; French towns is very noticeable.  I am going to Glasgow on Monday &amp; am returning to London on Wednesday &amp; then back again to the Front. The "push" that I spoke of earlier in this record has taken place &amp; so far has resulted in the capture of Messines &amp; the Ridge &amp; 7000 prisoners.  My old unit has been in the thick of it.  How I would have loved to have been there with them.  I am anxiously watching for the casualty returns
