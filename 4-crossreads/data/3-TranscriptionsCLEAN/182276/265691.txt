  
 9th M.G. Coy No 16 Intelligence Report from 6 am 15th to 6 am 16th:- Right Sector:- No 5 gun fired from 7.3 to 11.45 to I 12 Central - I 12 d 0/7 X Roads, Trenches, Tracks, etc round RUELLE de la NOIX. No 6 fired at intervals from 12.30 to 2.30 on I 17 d 8/4 - I 18 C 3/0 road from LA BLEU a BASTROU. Enemy attempted to bring M.G. and rifle fire to bear on No 6 gun during the night firing. Two small shells also landed in rear of position. Left Sector:- No 7 (S.P.X.) fired at irregular intervals from 5.30 - 11 pm on I 12 b 9/8. Roads, Trenches, etc.- Unobserved- light dark - wind calm - Quiet night 
 B J. Carey Capt 9th M.G. Coy 16.12.16
