<p>[Major Gibson] went</p>
<p>15 July 1916 Sat<br/>On duty in the dispensary did a few dressing also having my sore foot dressed. All leave in the village stopped to-day. After dinner I attempted the route march when we had gone about 3 miles we were made double three of us suffering with bad feet fell out. We had our names taken &amp; were paraded before [text continued at top of page]</p>
