<p>very beautiful but could not get very near to see it well. There were about 4 Bishops, Canons etc  &amp; great ceremony but most impressive. We then went to buy Gum-boots for France, so I'm quite ready to leave in the morning.<br/>Then we made our way to Fleming's Restaurant &ndash; we then realised how hungry we were &ndash; had a good dinner at 6. 30. p.m. Afterwards finished up with a Picture Show and we laughed until we ached. It was a very good show &amp; some most interesting pictures. Then made tracks for Station &amp; arrived in time to see 10.30. p.m. train go out. So had to wait until 11.10. p.m.  At once said we must have <u>a drink</u>, so got to a Caf&eacute;    &amp; had Coffee. We got back to our Diggings nearly mid-night, sleep soundly &amp; feel splendid this morning. Two of our party Bonnily &amp; Munro have been sent to "Bagthorp", Nottingham, this morning, so we are all divided</p>
