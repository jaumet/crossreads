<p>Tuesday 22/1/18&nbsp; Wrote to Doff and then went to Malden</p>
<p>Wednesday 23/1/18. Spent the day at Malden and went for a long walk</p>
<p>Thursday 24/1/18&nbsp; Returned to Town&nbsp; saw Howse, had lunch with Gray&nbsp; afterwards met Hardie- had dinner at Club</p>
<p>Friday 25/1/18&nbsp; Got some new clothes finished, sent wire to Doff asking for &pound;50 which I will repay. Went to &quot;Romance&quot; very good</p>
