 While we were out drilling  this  yesterday afternoon a message came up saying that I had to report to the Adjutant at once so down I goes wondering whats the matter now, on arrival I was shown into his room, &amp; he told me I had been nominated for the position of Bombing Instructor  at the Divisional Reinforcement Camp, &amp; also that I had qualified for this  from the School at Volkeringhove very nice news if it comes off, but the Adj. said there was no certainty of it for every thing was in such an unsettled state.
