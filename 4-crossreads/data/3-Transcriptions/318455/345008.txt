<p>We reached our camp that evening about 6 p.m. &amp; as Fritz was not too far away we were issued with extra ammunition &amp; we fully expected a call out that night. But it did not come &amp; yesterday we moved again &ndash; we were ready by 10 a.m. but did not eventually move off until 1 p.m. making towards Sercus &ndash; we were halted for some time about 1 kilo from the latter place at a crossroads just the 4 waggons in charge of the Sgt. &ndash; When the Sgt drew our attention to a man dressed as an officer standing at the X roads with a map in his hand having a good look</p>
