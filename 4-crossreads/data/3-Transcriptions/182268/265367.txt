<p>Tuesday 6/1/17. Rode to Reninghelst and had tea with Lloyd.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7/11/17. Raining heavily all day- O&#39;Hara Wood came along for game of cards.</p>
<p>Thursday 8/11/17&nbsp; Orders received to move&nbsp; saw Bas had letters from Hetty &amp; Pattie.</p>
<p>Friday 9/11/17. Battalion moved by bus to Steenvoorde&nbsp; I rode with Little. Had dinner to celebrate Billie&#39;s Birthday with Robinson.</p>
<p>Saturday 10/11/17&nbsp; Quiet day- a bit off color- presumably Billie is the cause.</p>
