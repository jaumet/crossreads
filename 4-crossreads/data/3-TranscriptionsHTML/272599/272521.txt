<p>Sept. Friday 14 1917</p>
<p>Down village after tea.<br/>Jock tricked out in new tunic &amp; strides.  Khaki links disappoint.   night work<br/>Patient in today.   Wounded 10th Aug. lay out till next day crawled into shell hole &amp; there a month subsisting on biscuits collected from dead comrades.</p>
