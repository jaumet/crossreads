<p>in reinforcement camp at Peronne.  Fifteen men from 2nd Bty.  Raining a treat, trying to find shelter.  Put the night in, in a dugout (German).</p>
<p>23-9-1918<br/>On the road, making for Bray.  Arrived there 12 noon, after an 18 Kilometre march.</p>
<p>24-9-1918<br/>Still at Bray, Billeted in a concert hall.  Nothing doing, filling in time until the 27 inst.  We then entrain for an 8 days train journey.</p>
<p>25-9-1918<br/>Farewell by General Birdwood.  We move off tomorrow.  New pay books issued.</p>
<p>26-9-1918, In train<br/>Left Bray 10 a.m.  Passed through Amiens.  27 men in horse truck.</p>
<p>27-9-1918<br/>Going strong, passed through Versailles and Troyes.</p>
