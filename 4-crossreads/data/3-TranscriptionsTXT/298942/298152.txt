 It makes me long to be back with Rita more than I have longed before unless it was at Dorrigo. 
 Cricket claims attention most afternoons, but for the past two or three days we have worked all day.  The cricket pitch is in front of one line of four marquees  &amp; enclosed in the  indecipherable  rectangle formed by this line &amp; a line of bell tents.
