<p>Gallipoli<br/>25-10-15</p>
<p>My dear Mrs Ferguson</p>
<p>I was very pleased to receive your letter of 6th September.</p>
<p>It is very slow here at present and it looks as though we would be here all the winter which is just about to start. I am afraid it will be very severe but the men are fairly well equipped with warm clothing and we should be fairly comfortable</p>
