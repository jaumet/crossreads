<p>was a cold bitter day here, almost like winter, as soon as it becomes cold, I feel it in my rheumatic joints.</p>
<p>Wednesday 8th Nov.<br/>Put in for leave to Cairo expect to go up on Friday.  Boxing at Welsh, our fellows went very well, one winning the bowman.</p>
<p>Thursday 9th Nov.<br/>Sham fight on to-day at Canal.  I told the Maj. I was too old to play soldiers so got out of it.  Our chaps drove "enemy" right back on the Canal.</p>
<p>Friday 10th Nov.<br/>Pass through left camp early in a.m., eventually arrived at Cairo, busy first day.  O.Rs welcomed me, stayed Anzac Hostel.  Cairo very quiet.</p>
<p>Saturday 11th<br/>Hearing of possibility of Hospital ship jobs returning home went out to Abbassia to see Herb E., introduced me to a Cpt. who was willing to swap jobs if my Dr. signed transfer, so I will probably come up here for a month before getting the job, am highly excited at probability, met Gwennie in p.m. took her to Nuns thence to a Matinee, had Xmas Cards printed, they are splendid, my own idea, printer said if he had idea earlier</p>
