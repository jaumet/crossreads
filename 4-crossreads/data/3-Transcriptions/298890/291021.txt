<p>15 Thursday<br/>Fine day pottered &amp; went in afternoon with Miss Day to a fete in aid of cot in Barnados Homes</p>
<p>16 Friday<br/>Pottered in house all day &ndash; did a little shorthand.  Miserably depressed.  Clouds came up in the evening.</p>
<p>17Saturday<br/>Fine showers.  A Miss Fawkener came.  Pottered around.  Miserably depressed.</p>
