<p>Sunday 16 December 1917</p>
<p>Reported Head Quarters &amp; got KIT, Cuth Fetherstonhough has the D.S.O.!!!  I met his wife. <strike>May get a job as O.C. 1st L.H. Training Regiment at Moascar but nothing certain yet.</strike>  Went Hospitals &amp; saw all the wounded &amp; got news. My Sergt. Major has been killed. I brought a watch from England for him, &amp; many other good men. <strike>I feel going back after missing all the recent fighting, D.S.O.s &amp; M.C.s have been won while I was away.</strike></p>
