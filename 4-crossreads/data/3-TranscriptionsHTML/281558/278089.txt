<p>walked around &amp; had a look about the country : home through Mont St Quentin &amp; it has been knocked about a fair bit :</p>
<p>Friday Feb 22nd</p>
<p>Peronne : looked about the yard &amp; done a bit in the box : not a bad place here &amp; civillian comfort in comparison to the infantry :</p>
<p>Saturday Feb 23rd</p>
<p>Peronne : done a bit in the box : warned to go to Omicourt to-morrow as guard for a while :</p>
<p>Sunday Feb 24th</p>
<p>Peronne : packed up &amp; caught the 7 am to Omicourt : put into huts &amp; warned for duty 4 pm : done two loads to peronne &amp; back : pretty cold :off about eight</p>
<p>Monday Feb 25th</p>
<p>Omicourt : off about 5 am &amp; into bed : not a bad job though a bit cold :</p>
