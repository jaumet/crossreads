
Sunday 4 March 1917Quiet day:  Col. Granville inspected us:  & wrote No. 4 [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 5 and 6].
Monday 5 March 1917Out on exercise in morning, played the N.Z.rs football won 3-0.
Tuesday 6 March 1917Out on exercise:  had a fine ride.
Wednesday 7 March 1917Not feeling too well;  went & got physic from doctor.
Thursday 8 March 1917Went to Bir-el-Abd for a ride with Clarrie:  saw all the boys.
Friday 9 March 1917Not feeling too well:  bad cold & sore neck:  quiet day.
Saturday 10 March 1917Quiet day:  played football:  M.G.s against A & C troop A Sq., draw 0-0.
