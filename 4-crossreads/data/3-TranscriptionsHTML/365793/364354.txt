<p>Spt 7 its too cosy <u>here</u> 'tween decks, &amp; this book is too good to leave</p>
<p>8 'Latest Wireless' &ndash; this ship is searching for Shackleton. Think we'll be sighting icebergs soon. The 'Funnel' Captain produced sundry apples from his pocket, &amp; I happened to get in the line of fire &amp; stop one.<br/>Last night saw the playing of the Euchre Tournament, &amp; now there's another quoit match in full swing.<br/>So heavy was the hail storm that the Nurses &amp; some of the boys pelted one another with it as with a snowball</p>
