 Sunday 4th Oct Church parade., Saw some real savages. Dark &amp; ferocious.  Hair worn in high arching fringe &amp; trimmed with strings of rat's teeth &amp; shells. The broad flat nose rendered more picturesque by a long shaft of sharpened bone thrust through it.
