<p>Tuesday 27th<br/>On fatague makeing a dugout for the cooks 3 Aussie letters</p>
<p>Wednesday 28th<br/>As we missed the bath yesterday went to day but alas the bath had taken unto its self wings Went on to a little village Sillay le Cic &amp; salvaged a few ods &amp; ends useful about the dugout. Saw a Frenchman &amp; his wife gathering a few of there cherished belongings. But am afraid that after the usual bombardment &amp; then the men camping in these villages there is nothing much left any good Six of us were told of to take the <strike>wash</strike> Cooker to the canall &amp; wash it Much better if we had taken the cooks.
