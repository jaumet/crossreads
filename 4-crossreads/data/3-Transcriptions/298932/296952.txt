<p>BRISTOL. FARM<br/>Monday 17<br/>If the rain keeps off this week will be one of the wildest weeks in history. Guns and munitions haunt me<br/>Tuesday 18<br/>Three oclock in the morn I am on picket, horses very quiet but the front line one blaze of fire and the fight as just began<br/>Wednesday 19<br/>The Batt. go into the fray tonight which will  be the greatest battle of the war<br/>God be with them and lead them to "Victory"<br/>Thursday 20<br/>It would have broken Germanys heart, if they'd have seen our boys marching into action singing &amp; laughing like school boy's</p>
