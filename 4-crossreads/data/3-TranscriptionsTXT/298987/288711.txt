 Wednesday 31st The Clinck was not just a success as the Birds pulled some of the fence down last night &amp; got away We are putting up a Bayonet course in the same camp 
 Thursday 1st November Bayonet Course quite a job. Ground to be levelled Trenches dug Sand bag brestworks put up &amp; bags of straw hung at intervills to run ones bayonet into 
 Friday 2 Had my first go at Sand bagging They make a real good job when properly done
