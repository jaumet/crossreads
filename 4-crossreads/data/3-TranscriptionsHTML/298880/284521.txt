<p>28th July<br/>Sun condescended to shine today. Fritz fired some heavy shells over the village today. They all fell close to the old church and one hit fair on it.<br/>29th July<br/>Enemy bombarded the church tower and billets today with the result that the church tower was destroyed and set alight and many houses wrecked &amp; burning. Our batteries replied.<br/>30th July<br/>Gave a plan of our company telephone lines to BDE today. Only one Company line in use at present.<br/>31st July<br/>Lovely day. One of our little section gone to hospital. About 10PM gas alarm was sounded. We put our gas helmets on for a while till GAS OFF was passed along street</p>
