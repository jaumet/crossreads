In fact, we have not had a decently fine day for some time &amp; it is getting too cold for swimming. Nevertheless I haven't yet tired of France. When is the war going to be over? No one knows! The Germans are jolly strong &amp; I think it will last at least another year. When the war started I said it was going to last 3 years. The French are thinking of emptying their prisons &amp; sending all criminals to the front. It would give them another 15,000 troops.
