<p><u>Sunday, July 1st</u><br/>This week has been full of changes. The weather even has been changeable From hot sultry weather we passed to dull steamy, Thundery days which gave place to cold winds and driving wintry rain making it unpleasantly cold.<br/>We have changed our address also, on Thursday we moved up here in the vicinity of Mailly, &amp; are now in huts &amp; dugouts, &amp; improvised shelter in part of the old lines of 1916 before</p>
