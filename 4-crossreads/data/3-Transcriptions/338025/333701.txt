<p>Thursday 31st May 1917<br/>Nr Pozieres  Aveluy<br/>Got out of bed about 8am. Watered and fed mules as we have to return to our own unit, we harnessed up about 1pm,emptied our waggon and moved out at 2pm. Picked up our own unit at 3pm which were camped near Bouzincourt (nr. Albert).</p>
