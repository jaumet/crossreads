 There is a comfort and a companionship in the snow which make it a kindly and merciful part of nature Nothing can fully describe the lush and beauty of a snowfall. One almost wants to whisper instead of speak while tat white silence descends. This morning it comes with a sense of blessing and a promise of peace. The ground is covered everywhere with a thick depth of its soft whiteness. The roofs of villas near by are so completely covered that against the whiteness in the air, they disappear altogether, leaving the chimney shafts strangely fixed and suspended as black blocks in the sky. The great queen of whiteness has trespassed upon the world. She claims the fields and hills and valleys
