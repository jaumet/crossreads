 Tuesday Mch. 20th The weather is very cold again with sleet & snow occasionally.  It cleared up towards the evening. The Retreat of the Germans continues & it has spread over 100 miles of front.  We shall soon know what it means.  The Cavalry are operating & aeroplanes are very busy. I visited the Hospital this morning.
