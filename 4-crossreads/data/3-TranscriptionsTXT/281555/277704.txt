 March 26. Sunday. Wet morning reached sea coast 6.30am. small ship sunk close in shore masts visible from train. Passing Calis 8.15, small place apparently &amp; nothing striking, later 9.30 reached St. Amiene much like Calis &amp; general townships 
 Running more South East reached Hazebrouck 10.40 country very wet &amp; cold. farms mainly. seriel crops 
 Tommies seen along latter end of journey making railways etc.
