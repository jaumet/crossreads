<p>took part, in what promises to be one of the most successful "stunts" of the war. Moved back 500 yds, into Abancourt.<br/>Frid 9th:  Moved up to 5 Div "objective" &amp; hopped-off with the tanks at 4.20 p.m. Advanced 3000 yds, captured Framerville &amp; dug-in 300 yds on other side (9000 prisoners)<br/>Sat10th:  Bombarded by our own heavy artillery for [indecipherable] hours<br/>Sun 11th:  19th  &amp; 20th moved through our position at 4 a.m. &amp; advanced 1000 yds. 150 prisoners. Total advance about 10 miles. Total of prisoners for 2nd Div 9000. Relieved by 10th Bgde. came back to reserve trench.<br/>Sat 17th:  Relieved by "Tommies". Came back, per motor, to Fouilloy near Corbie.</p>
