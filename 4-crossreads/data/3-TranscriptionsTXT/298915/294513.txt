 12 midnight arrive Ludd camp in an orchard and camp. Spend the day moving from one place to another doing silly jobs. 26th Still moving 27th Moving again. 28th Not yet settled. Bivvies &amp; lines moved round and round the camp. Dismantle W14 (Kaesehagen's) Station &amp; Shellac Condenser tubes. 29th Wonderful! Still moving the bivvies round the orchard. Men growling &amp; cursing. A job that a boy scout could do in a few moments cannot be done by us in 4 days. 30th Dismantle W14. Overhaul HF condensers. 31st Dismantle W13. held up for gear. Write stiff report 56 cases backsheesh for sqdn.
