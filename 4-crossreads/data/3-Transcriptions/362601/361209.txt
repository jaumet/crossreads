<p>25 May, Sunday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Up at 7. Brekkie, chat, music. I wake at 4[am] because of motors. Say Good Bye. Percy &amp; Sis come to Wilton. Arrive home O.K. Miss Jacks O.K. Arrive home 3.30. read &amp; lay down until Tea. I am off my tucker, very sad &amp; lonely. Write &amp; go to Post. Meet Cullman &amp; Parry &amp; come home quite cheery. Recd letter Esme. Hawker is found.</p>
<p>Wrote long letter &amp; newest M.R.L.</p>
<p>26 May, Monday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I just miss fatigue &amp; give anals party to miss it. I walk down town with Les Nett. He goes away to Glasgow bound for Home. Read most of the day. Go down to [indecipherable] with Billy, off my tucker &amp; hear its Luvley when Lovlete we got. Picture &amp; get "Beams". Recd letter from Jennie.</p>
<p>Meet Les Hamil &amp; Craven in Pictures.</p>
<p>Wrote Auntie Annie &amp; M.R.L.</p>
