<p>to be treated any how lately trecked out to a place called bel el Jemie we have been here before very hot day there is some heavy gun firing there must be a fight on rested till 4.30 P.M. had tea saddled up and went on an out post one mile away on 2 till 4 all clear turned in again 4.15 they are pumping water at this place<br/>Sunday April 15.4.17 stand to 6.AM had breakfast got some wood also got plenty of fleas in a hut returned to the camp at bel el Jemie 8.A M unpacked let camel go grasing saddled up again 9A M and was relieved went back to camp which</p>
