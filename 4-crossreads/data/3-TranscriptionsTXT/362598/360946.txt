
Sunday 8 October 1916Church Parade.  We walk to Shipton & motor to Tidworth.  Motor runs out of Benzine at X Roads.  We arrive in London.  Visit Fleet St., see St. Pauls.
Monday 9 October 1916Arrive L/pool.  Visit Town Hall.  Dine & Play billiards at Angel Hotel.  Arrive Douglas at 5 p.m.  Meet Marie.  8.5 train to Sulby.  Arrive Home 10 p.m. at Vicarage.  Meet Grandma, Auntie & Uncle.  Wrote Mum.
Tuesday 10 October 1916Blackberry Day.  Uncle takes us for a walk.  I sprain my wrists.  Ellis & Dave go out & I sit at the Fireside on the mat not on the seat.  We have plenty of music.  Aunt Hannah arrives.
Wednesday 11 October 1916I do not go out.  Ellis & Dave shoot & gather mushrooms.  I again sit by the fire & more music at night.  I say Good night & sleep for the first time & dream of Grandma's other farm.
Thursday 12 October 1916I arise at 4.20 & make fire.  We drive with Auntie to Railway.  Meet Uncle Harry for an hour.  We reach L'pool after a rough passage.  Leave L'pool at 5.30, arrive London 10.5.  Union Jack.
Friday 13 October 1916Leave London 6.15, arrive Andover & motor to Parkhouse, too late.  We join our ranks at Bulford, start Duty at 2 p.m.  Everything very nice.  I write Mar. 6 pages.
Saturday 14 October 1916Have easy time.  Some black bags arrive.  I write Home.  I had letters from Bess (2), H. Hey (2), M. Hudson, 5 from Home, Jess Mather, Mem.
Motor breaks down on Friday.  I learn of Ted Clarke's death.  I was very sick until this morning.  I can't sleep in M's bed but sleep on the floor very well.
