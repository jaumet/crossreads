<p>Monday 31 July 1916<br/>Grenade throwing in morning.  Lectures.  Saw Charlie Rayner and Lin Alworth.  Nearly whole Squadron detailed to go to Machine Gun Squadron.</p>
<p>Tuesday 1 August 1916<br/>Left Tel-el-Kebir by train at 7 a.m.  Got parcel from Stella &amp; Aunt Katie.  Passed Ismalia [also spelt Ismailia] and crossed Canal at Cantarah [also spelt Kantara].  Came up to Romani in open trucks.  First order on arriving was "Scatter" on a/c of Taube overhead.  No tents 1st night.  Pass a lot of wounded at Kantarah.</p>
