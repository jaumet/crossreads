
Sunday 5/8/17Up 6.45 a.m.  Went to farmhouse with 4 others & each had pint of milk.  After dinner went to Teffont via Fovant going over grounds of the Manor.  Called at farmhouse on way back & had pint of milk.  Went to Y.M.C.A. after tea & posted booklets etc. to Bourke.
Monday, 6/8/17Very muggy all day - foggy early in morning.  Out all day skirmishing.  After tea went to Y.M.C.A.
Tuesday, 7/8/17Skirmishing all day.  Bought tin peaches & had peaches & cream at Farmhouse.  Wrote to Australia & C/Wealth Bank.  Had Camp Pie for supper.  Recd. parcel groceries from Australia.
Wednesday, 8/8/17Marched out 7.30 a.m. on a stunt
