 Tuesday 7 August 1917. 
 Instruction in full marching order for the field where it appears we will move out to shortly. 
 To Cairo in evening to have envelopes containing silk&nbsp;handkerchiefs censored at Anzac Hostel. Had to leave them there till tomorrow.&nbsp; 
 Supper at Y.M.C.A. &amp; back to camp. 
 During afternoon we were issued with the rest of our equipment for the field &amp; had to fill water tanks &amp; dhurra bags for tomorrow.
