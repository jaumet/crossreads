 July 1915 
 Thursday 22 
 Vaccinated this morning. 
 Granted home leave for four days. 
 No pay &amp; no pennies so not much chance of getting to Grafton. 
 Wired return for 30/- &amp; walked around with Francis. 
 He met his friends so I was left. 
 Saw Curry, Kenney, Miss Jepson &amp; Mrs. Bruce 
 Went to Soldiers Club in evening &amp; wrote a lot of letters. 
 No word from Rita yet.
