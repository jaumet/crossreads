 Thursday 20&nbsp;May 1915 Went in Motor Car to Mahdhi &amp; Hellowan with Dr Will during morning.&nbsp;&nbsp; At No 1 G. H. with Jesuit Father at 6 p.m.&nbsp; Otherwise about the grounds &amp; looking to business 
 Friday 21&nbsp;May 1915 At Matarieh Church this morning at 7 o&#39;clock. &nbsp;heard Mass. 20 French wounded &nbsp;from the Galipoli peninsula &amp; about 100  Egypt  Cairo catholics, all men attended. About 20 received H. Communion.&nbsp; The head of the Jesuit College in Cairo asked me to be present. Breakfasted with them. A priest delivered a sermon in French during the Mass. At office 9-15 am. In Heliopolis from 8.30 p.m. to 12 midnight. Williams &amp; [indecipherable] 
 Saturday 22&nbsp;May 1915 Not out during the day All going well. 
 Sunday 23&nbsp;May 1915 At Matarieh Church. St. Famile &ndash; at 7am. No Mass till 8 a.m. owing to special celebrations for Pentecost&nbsp;Sunday. Said my prayers look round &amp; back at 8-15 for brkfst. Went to the Barrages below Cairo, between 4 &amp; 6-30 &nbsp;p.m. Captain Williams &amp; two privates went with me.
