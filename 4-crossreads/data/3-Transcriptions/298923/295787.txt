<p>1 May 1918 Wednesday<br/>Land of Moab<br/>Much cooler on high land feed green but too interested in a big battle at presents going on-  We did our job yesterday so today we are Div. Reserve.<br/>Our horses have not had their saddles off since 10 oclock Monday night.<br/>This diary helps me to keep track of time.<br/>Attacked at 5 p.m. &amp; with<u>drew</u> at 9 pm<br/>On outpost dys to 3 AM then marched towards River again.<br/>Big strafe going on all around <u>we</u> AMD took 250 prisoners.</p>
