
4th Field AmbulanceN.3 & Australian DivisionAnzac, M.E.F.
My dear ReneI have already stated in black & white, and I again assert with the utmost vehemence - you are a real good sort!  Those two parcels arrived and have caused unbounded happiness in this Ambulance.  The men were like a lot of kids and your ears must have burned a bit.  Gawd bless you did not express it at all.Now I want you to do something else.  Send me 1 oz. of Acid Salicylic.  I'll pay you when I see you if not the Neesons [?] will.  This constant diet of Bully Beef & no vegetables makes my skin very irritable & I can have this made with an ointment which fixes it.We have had a pretty rotten time here this last scrap as you will have seen by the Casualty lists but we have got a good bit of Turkey in return, and where we are now, we are away from that awful shelling we used to get daily in Anzac.The second day after this scrap started I had my tent division in a dry creek bed.
