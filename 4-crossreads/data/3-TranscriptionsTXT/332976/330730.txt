 October 1916 on the strength of the 15th Field Coy (5th Division Engrs) as Sappers, &amp; settled down.  I have "dropped" my stripes on joining the company according to A.I.F. Orders, &amp; will now be paid as a Sapper. 26th  The Company is quartered in rough built huts of tarpaulins about 5 miles behind the front line   We are surrounded by batteries &amp; all last night the guns were firing over at Fritz.   At about 8 o'clock last night Fritz sent over about a dozen shells which landed a few hundred yds
