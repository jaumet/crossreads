
<p>98</p>
<p>AUGUST&nbsp;&nbsp; WEDNESDAY 16&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine except for a few showers&nbsp; Out signalling all day with the class. And had a lamp parade at night&nbsp; I got a letter from mother &amp; the photos of myself &amp; the group taken in London. They are not bad at all I thought.</p>
