
<p>335</p>
<p>APRIL 1917</p>
<p>11 WEDNESDAY</p>
<p>Fine in morning, but blowing a</p>
<p>bitterly cold gale, &amp; it came on to</p>
<p>rain in evening &amp; to snow later</p>
<p>We had an inspection by</p>
<p>Lieut Watt in morning, full</p>
<p>marching order, &amp; we marched</p>
<p>round the square by the D. AC.</p>
<p>I took Capt Callow&#39;s luggage &amp;</p>
<p>batman into the train at</p>
<p>Steenwerck in afternoon, in</p>
<p>Maltese Cart.</p>
