 [Easter Monday 24 April 1916] 
 Court all day today as  all evidence   Evidence is given through an Interpreter HINDUSTANI &amp; again sometimes in ARABIC with the Native witnesses it is a long tedious case. Did I tell you it was a case of a Sikh soldier murdering his Native Officer shot him in the night while asleep but evidence is difficult to get, &amp; the Witnesses swear Black is White &amp; vice versa.
