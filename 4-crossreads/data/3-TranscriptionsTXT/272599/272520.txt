 1917 Thursday 13 Sept. 
 In to Boulogne with Jock.  Cinema.   Call at Tailors for my tunic, took it to be altered slightly. Wanted to change 15 frs.   Refused to pay it &amp; left tunic there.   Exorbitant price &amp; an English firm too.   He got his character detailed to him by me. Bought Hugo's French Course   Good issue of pr. books from Q.M.
