<p>Sunday 26th<br/>Went to Braid Hills afternoon night time came back to London ten o'clock train.<br/>Monday 27th<br/>Arrived London morning had a look round night time went to Garrick Theatre<br/>Tuesday 28th<br/>Visited Tower London and St Pauls Cathedral nightime went to Lyceum Theatre<br/>Wednesday 29th<br/>Saw changing of the guard had a look round the city</p>
