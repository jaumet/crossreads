But it is wonderful how this stuff brings back your strength and enables one to sleep. These terrible conditions would kill a horse. I had not dreamed the human frame could stand such hardship. A german aeroplane came over and observed the entrance to this tunnel this morning. His gunners are busy dropping shells on the entrance now; he is making poor practice.
29/12/16.
