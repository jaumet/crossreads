I have given you the sum total of the week's happenings except my daily routine.  I am very thorough in my work (different to what I was in Civi life) &amp; it is a lot of trouble to be so in Military life.  But one has the satisfaction.  I have a Clerk who is fine steady old chap from Melbourne.  He was strange to say in Georgie's line &amp; also retail so we are of kindred Trades.  He is very thorough &amp; conscientious &amp; a very travelled &amp; well read man.  Another of my Clerks is an M.A. of Oxon.  I hope to go out for a spin with Tommy (Tam) or Wee Tammy as well Him, on the Bikes.  We
