There was a very decent supper & shortly after this, just after midnight, the crowd dispersed. Yesterday afternoon I came across a new Scout Organisation in Cairo - the "Young Macabees". They are all French Jews & are about 60 in number. They are much interested in photos & particulars of Australian Scouts. March 8" 1916 Wednes. On Monday evening the O.C. went down to Luxor for a weeks holiday; the first he has taken since arriving in Egypt.
