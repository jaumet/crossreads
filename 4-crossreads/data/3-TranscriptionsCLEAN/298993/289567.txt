
Saturday Dec 16.12.16Stand to 6AM had breakfast cleaned up watered camels had blankets fumigated took half day very hot on Picquet to night the blankets wanted scalding am sure there is lice in themSunday Dec 17.12.16Stand to 6AM had Breakfast rifle inspection fell in for church parade no church. Finish of sports  afternoon boxing and camel wrestling I looked on had seven letters from maud s Jose. Alma. Ailie maud 3  answered themMonday Dec 18.12.16Stand to 5.30AM had breakfast saddled up and went down to dump as fatigue returned to camp and went back for water.
