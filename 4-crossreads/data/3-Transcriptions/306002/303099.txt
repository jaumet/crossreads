<p>4.4.15<br/>Australian soldiers on board going to Alexandria also a train load of Gurkhas.  It is rumored that practically all the troops from Mena was sent to Alexandria last night &amp; this morning.  No leave was allowed for Cairo, on account of the riot on Friday night but we were allowed to go to Heliopolis.  A couple of nights ago the New Zealanders burnt their picture show down, as they were not satisfied with it.  Put this evening in writing home &amp; to Edie T.</p>
