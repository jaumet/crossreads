<p>Frid. 28-7-'15<br/>Camp 1 p.m. &amp; marched to Vadencourt (near Contay) slept in huts. March cruel on men whose feet have grown tender in trenches.</p>
<p>Sat. 29-7-'15<br/>3.45 am. Reveille. Off 5.15 am. Another cruel forced march to Lavicogne beyond Talmas) Billetted in Caf&eacute; stable 11 a.m. Everybody finished. Slept until 5.30 p.m. tea &amp; clean up to bed again. Sneaked away &amp; wandered around outskirts of very pretty village with decided Home atmosphere.</p>
<p>Sun. 30-7-'16<br/>Reveille 7 a.m. Left 8.30 am. Very hot but everyone seems fit. Scenery most delightful yet. Valleys Cultivated every square inch of them.</p>
