Our food control officers employ 5000 people, 20000000 food tickets printed weighing 68 tons.   Queues now expected to be finished those we have had being for Butter, Meat, picture shows &amp; whisky.   Tea has been scarce only expensive brands being available but Government have taken over all stocks &amp; made a blend which sells at 2/8 per lb, Manchester has been made the Clearing house for tea &amp; tea experts have been sent there.   Other lines are treated in a like manner.   Potato flour being now used to better effect as experts have demonstrated its use to the bakers.   Sugar beet cultivation also commenced.   Sinkings of U Boats now said to be faster than the enemy can build them.
