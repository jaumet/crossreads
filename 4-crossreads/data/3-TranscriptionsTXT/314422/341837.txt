At 5pm I caught a train to London travelling via Lyn and Cambridge arriving at Liverpool Station about 8.30 then I took the tube to St James station travelling several miles underground and walked from the station to Horseferry Road and stayed at the War Chest club for the night.I obtained 12 views of Norwich and gave 2/- to a stranded Australian on the train.
