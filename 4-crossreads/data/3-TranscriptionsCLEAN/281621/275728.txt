
Sunday 9Wet & miserable.
Monday 10Beautiful weather
Tuesday 11Beautiful day. A dinner tonight was very decent.Rumours are about that we move back to the line at Messines in a couple of days
Thursday 13Weather is still fine. We perhaps move on Saturday morning to somewhere in the vicinity of Loere but the journey is to be accomplished in stages.
Saturday 15Move to-day at sunrise to Senleques via Samer for Reserves. A beautiful day & a decent march
