<p>It is the desire of all Pris of War, wich have been interned in South Australia, to have all <u>complaints investigated</u> up to August 19 not only a few, as granted to us by the Military Department.</p>
<p>The Complaints are the following</p>
<p>No 1. The treatment of Pris in General.<br/>" 2. Short of Provision, Food unwholesome &amp; bad<br/>" 3. Cooking Utensils inadequate. No Axces. Kitchens<br/>" 4. Pris wher compelled to pay Postage &amp; freight on letters, parcels, and luggage.<br/>5. Neglect of Authoritys to inform Pris of arrival of Luggage, and has been lost.<br/>6. Kitchen Material.<br/>7. Handcuffing Muller &amp; John Bergmann &amp; Lunatic Becktenwald.<br/>8. Treatment of Pris while under arrest.<br/>9. Shooting of Ratch.<br/>11. Sentencing Pris to fail, and in wich state.<br/>10. Treatment of Pris in regard of convenience<br/>12. Bayoneting of Pris on their way from Latrines<br/>13. Housing accommodation, (tents) ectra)<br/>14. Threatening Pris to shoot &amp; [indecipherable]<br/>15. Shooting into Borchards tent.</p>
