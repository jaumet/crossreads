<p>11 September, Thursday</p>
<p>Go Home to cut out suit. Ellis &amp; Ette out to tea. I have lost measures &amp; go to bed instead.</p>
<p>12 September, Friday</p>
<p>Work all day &amp; I have a rush at night where People are lined two-deep. Go down &amp; see Doris &amp; buy coat see Ellis Roy &amp; Wal at dinnertime.</p>
