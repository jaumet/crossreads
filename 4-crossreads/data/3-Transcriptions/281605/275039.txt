<p>left ship at 9AM &amp; returned MIDNIGHT.  Had good look round City &ndash; entertained by Red Cross &amp; went to MAJESTIC THEATRE at night.</p>
<p>5th Left Adelaide about 7 AM</p>
<p>6th Arrived outside heads Melbourne &amp; waited till dawn</p>
<p>7th under quarantine &ndash; during mist pulled inside about midday &ndash; restriction lifted &ndash; stopped opp Williamstown &ndash;</p>
<p>8th Pulled into New Wharf Port Melbourne about 8 AM &amp; received leave about 11 AM.  Had good look round city &amp; St Kilda &ndash; stopped in town at night</p>
<p>9th &ndash; round city &amp; St Kilda &amp; returned to ship about mid night</p>
<p>10 &ndash; left Port Melbourne about 6 AM &ndash;</p>
