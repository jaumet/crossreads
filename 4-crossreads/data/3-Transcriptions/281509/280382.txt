<p>1916 June 8<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Linesman again.  Sick to death of these endless . worthless fatigues. Bob gets position in orderly room as assistant pay clerk. He intends moving on with us when we go. Write a letter to Stan re enlistment. Customary tea &amp;amp cake before turning in.<br/>Lord Kitchener drowned by sinking of H.M.S. Hampshire blown up by a mine in a great storm off the island of Orkney.</p>
