<p>everything so peaceful, but a short distance behind the lines, scarcely a shot to be heard. you stop &amp; ask yourself is there a war on.</p>
<p>Although the girls work hard here in the fields, it does not effect their figure or looks so very much, for there is no doubt you will find some very pretty girls amongst them. a pleasant pastime to go out after stables in the evening &amp; help them loading the hay or pitching sheaves of wheat for the thresher.</p>
<p>Another thing one notices here is the fine stamp of horses they have &amp; how well kept they are, just such a picture as I used to see in my boy hood</p>
