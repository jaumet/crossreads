  
 Friday 18 May 1917. 
 No sight of land yet. Right up close to "Encounter" (cruiser) this morning. Rough at first but not quite so later. 
 I finish up my mess orderly duties with G.S.S.Crmn today, for a month or so. 
 Expect to. sight land tomorrow evening.  
 Still in a northerlyly direction as on Thursday 17  th  inst 
 Saw with 2 of the boys a  "transparent"   "flying fish". An uncommon sight so far south in a cold climate.
