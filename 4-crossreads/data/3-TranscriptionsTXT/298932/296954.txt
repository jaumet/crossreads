 POPERINGHE Monday 24 On the move, again got up at 3 and rode to Winnipeg camp and then marched to Wickenham living in tents, nice day Tuesday 25 Hopping about in the hop field's. Lt Allan Doig is now Nuttys pilot and O.C. at B. Company Visiting Poperinghe. Wednesday 26 Waiting patiently for money from home so as I can go to England This is my 567th day of A.S a long stretch Thursday 27 Moonlight on the battle fields of Belgium "ah to be back at Carnsdale a night like this would be soothe a soldiers mind
