Home with Genuine <u>British</u> People like ourselves, not the reserved cold people as we find most of the   <u>English.</u>    Fancy the Willetts' Family getting to London &amp; then having to get out again.  Gee they soon snavel You for the army over here.  No eligible men that can possibly be spared can miss it.  All Tube railways &amp; Motor Buses, lifts, wagons etc are tended by women.  If You see a man You can bet Your life that He is too old or absolutely unfit.  Chaps with one arm &amp; one eye cannot get their discharge from Home service Duties.
