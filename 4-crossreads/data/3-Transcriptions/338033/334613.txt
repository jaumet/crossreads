<p>we had a march past. This<br/>Was carried out every Sunday, a number of civilians coming to see us. Sunday afternoons we had to ourselves and I mostly went out for a walk with mates. A few days later all blankets were withdrawn owing to being in a plate of vermin, and we all had a disinfectant bath.<br/>The following Sunday we were invited to a private house to spend the afternoon, and evening and I can assure you we enjoyed ourselves very well, singing and talking.
