<p>Saturday 5 Jan.<br/>Up on stables as usual then out on the line. Completed line to Divn. by about 11.30. Returned to Outtersteen &amp; altered line to D.A.C. Returned home. Dinner.</p>
<p>Evening reading remainder of letters. Billet too inconvenient for writing etc.</p>
