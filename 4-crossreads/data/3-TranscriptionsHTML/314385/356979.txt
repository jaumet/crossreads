<p>Sun Sep 10th<br/>We are still living at our leisure doing as we like.</p>
<p>There is also part of the 7th FA with us.</p>
<p>I tried to see the Popge church but they were all locked up owing to the constant shelling but there are many nice shops here &amp; everything is cheaper and better than in France, beer is far superior also cigars &amp; other things. There is also some beautiful lace things to be had.</p>
<p>Mon Sep 11th<br/>To day we take over the rest Station &amp; start duties. I am detailed to one of the wards. The 7th are carrying on in conjunction with us.</p>
<p>In the afternoon a football match was played between the 6th &amp; 7th 7 AS but we beat them out of sight.</p>
<p>Weather fine but inclined to be foggy &amp; chilly, the ground here is very low lying all roads ligned with deep ditches.</p>
<p>Tues Sep 12th<br/>We are getting well into our duties.</p>
