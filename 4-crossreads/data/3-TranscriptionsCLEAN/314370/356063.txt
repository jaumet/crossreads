  
 August, 1916 Tuesday 1 Muster Parade 5.30 am.  strike tents 9 am stack Baggage & clear up the old ground. Parade & march to train at 4.30 pm.  train consists of open trucks Reach Gabbari dock, Alex at 1 am Embark on R.M.S. "Franconia"  All Sgts allotted 4 berth cabins. 
 Wednesday 2 are woke up by steward at 7 am Good breakfast inspect ship  play cards till dinner Still got my little red Box "Boite Rouge"
