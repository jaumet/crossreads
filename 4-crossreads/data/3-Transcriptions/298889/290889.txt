<p>On the 8th all A.E.2s Crew received 3 Pounds per man from the High Commissioner of Australia<br/>On the 11th received one letter from Sister dated 13 Feb<br/>On the 18th received one letter from Wife dated 22 Feb two from Sister dated 16th &amp; 21st and two from Barrow dated 16th &amp; 20th also it rained for 48 hours very hard on the 18th &amp;19th<br/>Three Russian Prisoners escaped at the beginning of the Month and got clear away, two more tried to escape, but got captured and sent to Stamboul<br/>On the 23rd received two letters from Sister dated 27 &amp; 29th<br/>On the 1st of April more clothing arrived from the U.S. Embassy<br/><strike>On the 6th</strike><br/>On the 7th received one letter from Wife dated 6th March, one from Barrow dated 6th and two from Sister dated 5th &amp; 12th<br/>On the 12th all British Prisoners received 1&pound;; per man from U.S.Embassy<br/><strike>On the 16th a Dove visited the camp and we catched him and kept him as a pet taking it as an emblem of peace</strike><br/>On the 28th March started work in the Central Power Station &amp; on the 8th April started night work 12 hour shifts up to the 22nd<br/>On the 18th received one letter from mother dated 23rd of March one from Wife dated 20th two from Sister dated 20th &amp; 19th</p>
