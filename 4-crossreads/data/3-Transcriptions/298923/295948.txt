<p>9 October 1918 Wednesday<br/><strike>Still marking time</strike>  Collecting war material &amp; sending Fever patients away-  the earth is not the kindest place when bones ache with fever but I think I'll beat the microbe yet <strike> Got some Ma[indecipherable]  from the M.O.</strike> Temp 99.4 Swallowing quinine <strike>Quinine</strike>  Amman is full of Armenian refugees without food-  they are dying at the rate of 12 or 13 a day with fever etc &amp; want of proper food.</p>
