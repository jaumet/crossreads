 Thursday 5/4/17. After a good sleep I awoke at 8 am & dressed & made my way to the Dining Room for Breakfast. As this was my last day's leave I intended to make the Best of it. I first took a Bus for the Tower Bridge, & after passing through several Streets  I passed  I noticed large numbers of Women waiting in a crowds to buy potatoes which in England are very scarce & dear at the present time. I left the Bus at the approach to the Tower Bridge & walked over the Bridge at this moment it was opening in the Centre to allow a Steamer to pass.
