Fri June 2nd - 16Fritz continued today with "coal-boxes" (15 inch shells) and gave us a lively time.
Sun June 4th - 16Last night about 11.30PM a heavy bombardment began, all variations of guns taking part. we lay in our bunks awake for a while and hearing a shell coming close we all began running to our dugouts in our little short shirts.
