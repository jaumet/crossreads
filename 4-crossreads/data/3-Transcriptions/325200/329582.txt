The Second Brigade went out last night  &amp;  engaged about 3000 of them at Oghratina  &amp;  a brigade of New Zealanders went out to Bir el Bayud  &amp;  held 1500 there or so they say. the latest rumour is that we have fallen back on Katia but I don't know whether that is right. We shall probably go out this evening.<br/>22nd July<br/>The alarm came at 1 o'clock am. on the 21st yesterday. We went out as far as Katia. the idea, I believe, being to get in touch with the turks  &amp;  retreat in front of them to Romanih</p>
