 April 1918 Wednesday 24 
 Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Dours     D.R. 3.50 am. Aroused by heavy drum fire in the region of Montididier &ndash; packed up kit, lines went at 5.15am. Stand to until noon. Weather dull and misty. Battery W.L. all returned to Franvillers, and are having a fairly rough spin. Had a run through Bonnay at 1pm. A very putrid sight arms and legs and mules scattered over the roads in a horrible array. 6ath Battery caught coming out of village, mules left on road. Fritz shelling fairly heavily
