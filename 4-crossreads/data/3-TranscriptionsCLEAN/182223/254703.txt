
Monday 4 October 1915 Left Cairo at noon today Arrived Alexandria at 4-30 pm. Reported to Act.G. 3rd Echelon at 6 p.m. At Hotel Majestic in Alexandria at 5 pm. 
Tuesday 5 October 1915 In Alexandria. Arranged about departure for Mudros & Anzac on Friday. Telephoned Mrs Rudd about letters & papers. At Mustapha. Ride around in small trams with Jerom. Ramble round the city at night. Wrote many letters Bed about midnight 
Wednesday 6 October 1915 In Alexandria Went as far as 17. Gen. Hospital, end of the Ramelah [Ramleh] tram line. Between 9 & 11 p.m. rambled round the Native quarter of the city midst lanes, & roads, narrow dirty, at Egyptian music halls playhouses Very interesting but horribly dirty. How is it that some epidemic does not come & Sweep the whole people off the face of the Earth?
