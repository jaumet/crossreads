 21 July, 1916 Friday 
 Still fighting 
 Went out after them today &amp; had a real good go we lost one man shot in the head, the artillery got on to them at Oghratina &amp; shelled blazes out of them  prisoners are arriving daily, we expect them to attack today &amp; are ready for them. 
 Two of the enemy's planes came over &amp; gave battle to ours, we drove them off Romani but they came over us &amp; I thought they intended giving us a few pills, enemy towards the close of the day came at us but we couldn't wait for them. They have 8 battalions of rifle, 2 regts of cavalry also with a few mountain guns. 
 Got in at 12.30 tired out 
 22 July, 1916 Saturday 
 The Second Bde kept them busy today at Oghratina &amp; attacked at dawn inflicting losses on the enemy at all points, prisoners continue to come in &amp; one is supposed to be a German sergeant who is supposed to be with the German Battalion.  Their force now consists of 12 Bns of 800 men, 8 batteries of 10 pdrs, 2 regts of cavalry as well as two companys of camel corps who are supposed to be 1000 men per company at Bir-el-Abd they have established a large Hospital &amp; an ammunition pack &amp; at Salmana they have a aerodrome &amp; today there were 2 Fokkers at rest. They continue to consolidate their lines everywhere, expect the big fight any day, good-oh.
