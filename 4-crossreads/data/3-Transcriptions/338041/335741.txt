<p>Oct. 19th<br/>Motor trip to Poperinge (Car orderly). Wet &amp; cold weather.</p>
<p>Oct 20th<br/>Australian Mail &ndash; Route march to village of Eecke &ndash; outgoing letters still prohibited.</p>
<p>Oct 21st<br/>Our Ambulance leaves "Steenworde" &amp; after passing through Cassel &amp; other villages we finish our days march at Noordpeene still in doubt as to our final destination &ndash; Our billets the worst we have had in France &ndash; Weather very cold &amp; unsettled &ndash; Farmhouse &ndash; Concert</p>
<p>Oct. 22nd<br/>Resume march from Noordpeene via the large town of St Omer and we are billeted at Pilques &ndash; Australian Mail arrives.</p>
<p>Oct 23rd<br/>Football etc &ndash; further mail</p>
