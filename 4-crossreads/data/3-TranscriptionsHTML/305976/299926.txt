<p>Thursday Oct 18<br/>Troops moving up towards Gaza. Every appearance of something doing shortly. Tom came over today attached to Battery's as Inspector<br/>Friday Oct 19<br/>We heard to day that Turkish were suffering heavily from sickness.<br/>Looking like rain<br/>Saturday  Oct 20<br/>Division moving up to Shelal to night Battery moved up to Shelal returned to unit. Struck decent patch of grass to day. Neddy had a good time<br/>Sunday Oct 21<br/>Visited old pals at Ambulance. They are attached 20th Army Corp. 2nd Brigade moved up. We are moving tomorrow. Tom rejoined unit from 1st Brigade</p>
