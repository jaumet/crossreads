
Have been doing a fair bit of washing this afternoon getting things cleaned up in the flannel & sock line.Went down to Warminster this evening to get my prints but they had no glossy paper so did not print them but waited until they saw me, I am now having them done in semi matte.  Warminster is a great soldiers town practically nothing else but then there are of course a good number of girls there of a week end they come from Bristol & Bath especially & are out to catch the mugs & their money.8th.
