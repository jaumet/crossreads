<p>Later on we passed ABUAIR [could not identify this island] a small island with a lighthouse</p>
<p>Four hours later on main land of Arabia we passed the town of MOKKA from which formerly the finest coffee in the world was exported.</p>
<p>Three hours later we passed through the Straits of Perrim into the Gulf of Aden.</p>
<p>Cape Aden being about 100 miles from Perrim.</p>
