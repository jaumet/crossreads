<p>Sat 23rd<br/>Signalling morning received a pass out from 6 till <strike>dinner</strike> 11.15 PM &amp; went in to the Ezbekiah Gardens to see some boxing contests, only one good contest between Bartlett (New Zealand) &amp; Joe Lee (Melbourne)  Bartlett won fairly easy;  Davidson went with me, we had tea at the Standard bar &amp; caught the 10.30 train home.</p>
