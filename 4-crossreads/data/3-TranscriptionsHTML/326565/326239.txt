<p>mess orderly first day.</p>
<p>Wednesday 10/11/15<br/>Reveille 6 am.  Sea choppy but wind dropped later in day &amp; sea became very smooth.  Rained late in day.  Still fairly well.  Commenced ward duty 10 pm till 6 AM following morning.  Whales noticed spouting in close proximity to the ship, caused great excitement.</p>
<p>Thursday, 11/11/15<br/>During early morning sea became very rough, bottles &amp; crockery began falling about causing rather an unusual disturbance.  Relieved from duty 6 am feeling queer from effects of rough weather.  Missed meals during day.  Passed Cape Otway about 5 pm</p>
