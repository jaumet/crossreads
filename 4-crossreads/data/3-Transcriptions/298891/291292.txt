<p>1917 July</p>
<p>28 Saturday</p>
<p>Cooked in morning. In afternoon Brownie &amp; I went riding with two Italians &amp; stayed to dinner with French officers under a tree &amp; were brought home in French Lorry. Inoculation got very stiff &amp; painful.</p>
