 Fri Long march all day &amp; fiasco of an affair 
 Sat 6th Quiet day Wings died Posted to Mater &amp; Cousin 
 Sun 7 Quiet day Wings funeral Mr &amp; Mde Snow came out at 5 am Went into Heliopolis with them and Butcher in evening. Watched the pictures from Pension Verandah Back for feed up. 
 Mon 8th. Off mounted in good time to the range only fired five rounds when called in.
