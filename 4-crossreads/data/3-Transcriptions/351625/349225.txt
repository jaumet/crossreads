<p>so rotten but I am writing lay on my stomach in my bunk which is very narrow &amp; very low.  We are living in one of Fritzs huge underground houses a place large enough to hold hundreds of men &amp; it is deep down under the ground, the dugouts &amp; pill boxes he makes are wonderful, no end of work &amp; trouble</p>
