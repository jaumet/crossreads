<p>Tuesday 4<br/>Still walking the countryside of an evening.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5<br/>The new arrivals from Australia have been responsible for introducing the meningitis germ into this camp &amp; a section of it is isolated. One or two cases have been diagnosed as C.B.M. &amp; there are many suspects of carrying</p>
<p>Thursday 6<br/>Warned for draft to France.<br/>Barnby is engaged to be married.</p>
<p>Friday 7<br/>Medical exams etc preparatory to going on draft.<br/>The divisions in France are still out of the line &amp; have been so for the past four months, since Bullecourt.</p>
