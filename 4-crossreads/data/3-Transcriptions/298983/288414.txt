<p>at 8.20 after many farewells &amp; good wishes arrived Dartford at noon &amp; realized I was still in the army (Yesterday afternoon went home to tea with Nurse Stone they have a nice old home a big farm) <strike>Her parents are like</strike></p>
<p>Friday 26th April<br/>Went for a walk but there is no where to go as the only road leads through Dartford &amp; it is out of bounds</p>
