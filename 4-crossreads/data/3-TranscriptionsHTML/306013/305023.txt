<p>To the Ocean.</p>
<p>Roll on, thou deep &amp; dark blue Ocean &ndash; roll.<br/>&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;<br/>Thou glorious mirror where the Almighty's form<br/>Glasses itself in tempests;  in all time, -<br/>Calm or convulsed, in breeze or gale or storm,<br/>Icing the pole, or in the torrid clime<br/>Dark heaving &ndash; boundless, endless &amp; sublime.<br/>The image of Eternity; the throne<br/>Of the Invisible&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;.</p>
<p>Thou goest forth, dread, fathomless alone.</p>
<p>Lord Byron.</p>
