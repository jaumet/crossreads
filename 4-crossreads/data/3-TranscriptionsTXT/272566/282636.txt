They are small vicious little good-for-nothings and are quick to flash a knife, but the Anzac knows how to deal with them.St. Omer is a nice place over-run by these W.A.A.C. girls from Eng.  (The Womens Army Aux. Corps.)  Aire is also a nice place.  The fruit in the orchard surrounding our Orderly Room Tent is now ripe & of course we get a good share, though only by pinching it at great risk.My bike is now in the Workshops at Morbecque being overhauled.
