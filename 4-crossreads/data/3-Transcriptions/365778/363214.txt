<p>Thursday 23/8/17<br/>Whole Division marched past General Birdwood along Canal De Neuf-Fosse. Raining but a splendid turn out.<br/>Friday 24/8/17<br/>Battalion Training in wood, fighting cleared forest of Clairmarais, met Bas who has applied for commission in Indian Army.<br/>Saturday 25/8/17<br/>Went with Bas to see Birdwood at Hazebrouck afterwards went and had dinner with Victoire at Steinbacque, arrived home at 10.30 pm.<br/>Sunday 26/8 17<br/>Church parade afterwards went with Wisdom to see ground for Sir Douglas Haig's inspection on Wednesday next &ndash; Letter from Doff.</p>
