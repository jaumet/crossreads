<p>Tuesday 3 September 1918<br/>Photos.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 2 h. 10 m.  Successful.<br/>Received D.F.C.</p>
<p>Wednesday 4 September 1918<br/>Standing by for H.A. alarm.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 1 h. 40 m.<br/>Took off twice but could not get at Huns, they were too quick in getting back to earth.</p>
