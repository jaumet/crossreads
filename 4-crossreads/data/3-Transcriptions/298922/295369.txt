<p>Monday 12 February 1917</p>
<p>TILUL <strike>BIR-el-ABD</strike></p>
<p>Still on the road &amp; raining hard &ndash; but all hands are happy. Passed several Catipillar Tractions pulling loads of 10 tons over the sand. Country gets more Desert like &amp; has edible bush as we go back. A man could make a do of 20,000 acres or less of Rafa country &ndash; all Bedouin Stock were fat there.</p>
