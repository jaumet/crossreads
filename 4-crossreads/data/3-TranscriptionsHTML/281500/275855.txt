<p>light job to do such as cleaning up the grounds, collecting tools &amp; etc. one thing &amp; another you are kept going all day &amp; well into the night though I could not complain about my job today.</p>
<p>Saw 2 Taubes &amp; one of our machines brought down today.</p>
<p>Fritzie has been dropping some more heavy shells quite close to us tonight they make the ground fairly tremble.  Was rather a nice day, but tonight it is raining once more.</p>
<p>Put all the evening in playing Bridge again, it passes the time away.  Lindsay Graham &amp; Digby came back from the School today, &amp; Arthur Sands sent on "leave".</p>
<p>The Band was going strong again tonight up at the Pioneers Batt.  I suppose they were having another spread.</p>
