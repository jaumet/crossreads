 F 14 Sun Awful windy day. Worst this year. D &amp; I stay in room all afternoon &amp;  try to read. 
 Wrote to Mum. 
 W 15 Mon Read all night 
 Wrote to Dad and Aunt Annie 
 W 16 Tues. Read all night 
 Snowed all day long 
 W 17 Wed Recd. Letter from M.R.L. Go to see "A pair of silk stockings". Come Home &amp;  read till Bed time 
 F 18 Th Top &amp; I write &amp; laugh &amp;  talk all night. Arr ord. Corp for last time. 
 Wrote Dulce. Mrs Mel. Roy. Marg. Alma. Nette. 
 W 19 Fri Snowy. Snow at night. Go to see "The Windfall" &amp; come Home  &amp;  go to bed straight way. 
 W 20 Sat Go to[ Handcatton?] Duty. It is horrid day. Visit CC &amp; Cho Edmonds. Catch 7.10 train Home  &amp;  go to bed early Wrote to MRL.
