  
 October, 1915 Monday 4 This is my average day - BreakFast 8 a.m. Medical Officers inspection 9 a.m. thigh dressed, arm carbuncle ditto 10 A.m. Sulphur Bath 38 degrees for 25 minutes by Sand glass 11 Return Hospital in one of many gift Ambulances which are in daily use all over here - 1 Lunch 2 p.m. Write letters, read, go sleep as an extra, got a tooth filled. 4 p.m. Dressings 6    "      Massage 7 Dinner. With various trips to the Frenchman for a POT of Beer this being a prohibition establishment & we don't all follow the King in that way - tho' no intemperance.
