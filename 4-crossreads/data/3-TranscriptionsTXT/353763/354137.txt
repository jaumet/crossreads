 We steamed along &amp; then the rain came on  -  Tropical rain &amp; we dived for the cabin I can tell you  -  Native huts &amp; compounds &amp; naked men &amp; women, save for loin cloths went past us but off we went to the Upolu &amp; landed wet thro  -  We changed &amp; got up top side &amp; then the sky cleared &amp; a lovely eveg came on  -  Presently we got orders to weigh anchor &amp; steam 5 Cables astern of the prize  -  We steamed  -   Protector led  the prize in the middle &amp; we astern  -  Presently the Australia loomed up &amp; came close in &amp; we 3 went round her  -  We cd  see the Melbourne &amp; the Berrima at Herberts Hohe but that was a long way off  -  The Flag ship &ndash; 3 funnelled &amp; grim with her 800 men aboard &amp; the admiral on the bridge looked fine  -  There was a immense mob of men watching us as we went slowly round  -  Our old cow is bad to handle but we managed to keep in line  -  Herbert took a snap shot at my instigation 2 in fact &amp; then we 3 went to Raboul
