<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Sydney</p>
<p>Date - 7 9 14</p>
<p>Your 1720. &nbsp;From Brigadier. &nbsp;I consider Kanowna detachment as&nbsp;at present constituted and equipped unfit for immediate service and&nbsp;in view of todays <strike>action</strike> events and your action in ordering ship back&nbsp;to Townsville recommend disbandment and reorganisation if Admiral&nbsp;considers further troops necessary. Request that arrangements be made for Captain Fry loaned from <strike>the</strike> Berrima to Kanowna as adjutant return to this Force earliest opportunity</p>
