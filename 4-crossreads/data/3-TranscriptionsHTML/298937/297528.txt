<p>Devons were called out &amp; half a dozen Egyptians were killed &amp; a number wounded.  A sign of the times.<br/>C. arrived home dead drunk to-night &amp; caused trouble.</p>
<p>Tuesday 15th Feb.</p>
<p>Am making headway with French under tuition of Dr. Cochenos.<br/>Latin I am also going on with in order to get up to matriculation standard as soon as possible.  Dr. Boutazy thinks I should stay in Egypt after the war &amp; go to Brerut College to take medical degree.  He is giving me all the practice he can here.<br/>Last Sunday I vaccinated about 40 prisoners &amp; every day I pull</p>
