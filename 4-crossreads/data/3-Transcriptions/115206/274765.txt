<p>July 1918<br/>Sunday 14</p>
<p>Vaux- sur- Somme.2nd Bty.Position<br/>The weather was as it usually is, wet and miserable. Our relief arrived here and we are moving today at about 10 pm.<br/>At 6 pm we left our old position and journeyed via Heilly and Ribemont to Bresle. Here we relieved the 11th Battery.<br/>Met  Tom Macarron, and had a little pow wow. Plenty of heavies well in evidence, some even forward of where we are. Retired to roost at 2.20 am. Still raining</p>
