<p>16 July 1918<br/>Tuesday<br/>Chief excitement was the returned of the boys who were on the Torpedoed D16. I was one of the Conducting N.C.O.s &amp; we had them all in camp about 9.20 P.M. looking rather rough in all kind of clothing</p>
<p>1918 July 17<br/>Wednesday<br/>Was drafted onto Boat Roll D18 today &amp; transferred into No 3 Company. Expect to leave about Sunday A wet squally day Went to the Palace &amp; Montys Flapper played Fairly good.</p>
