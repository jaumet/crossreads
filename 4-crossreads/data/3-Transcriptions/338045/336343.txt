<p>number of destroyers &amp; torpedo boats here flying around give one an idea of our naval activity.  Searchlights playing all night in the sky present a fine spectacle, the old Castle on the hill being silhouetted against the darkness beyond.  At 8 p.m. turned in for the night to disembark in the morning.  Heavy gale still blowing.  We sleep on mattresses right in stern (no blankets) &amp; make best of it till morning.  Dead tired, I am not long going to sleep.</p>
<p>Nov. 2nd &ndash; Thursday<br/>Awakened at 6-30 a.m. to dress &amp; wash.  On deck &amp; find the weather has abated a little.  At last after hours of waiting</p>
