<p>at Sec Bois There were to have been sports during the day, but owing to bad weather these were postponed.<br/>The concert was a success and, although briefly prepared, was noted the best given yet. After the show the Colonel came along &amp; complimented me on it very sincerely, in his general manner.<br/>The literary circle, meanwhile, has grown a great deal. Since the first literary evening we have we have had three meetings: <strike>one was</strike> a debate on "should sexual instruction be taught in schools; a Literary papers night &amp; a debate on land settlement.
