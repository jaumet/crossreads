
Stretcher drill.  All No 4s of squad brought out in turn to drill the section, my first opportunity of drilling the section.Excitement at night; gambling school composed of men from the Infantry raided within our lines.  Some of the men arrested.
Saturday  12/2/16Went on dental parade & had tooth refilled.  Large mail from home after a fortnights interval.
Sunday 13/2/16Church parade 9 AM.  Two British Monitors, carrying 2 large guns, one fore & one aft & two ante aircraft guns passed
