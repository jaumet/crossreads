<p>Sun.  26 Nov. 1916<br/>Sighted land about 6 pm (Freetown Sierra Leone) Lay off till next morning.</p>
<p>Mon. 27 Nov. 1916<br/>Went into bay not allowed ashore.</p>
<p>Tues. 28 Nov. 1916<br/>Went ashore in ships boat.  Granted leave from 10 am till about 4 pm.</p>
<p>Wed. 29 Nov. 1916<br/>No leave granted.<br/>Thurs. 30 Nov. 1916<br/>No leave granted.</p>
