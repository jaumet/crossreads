<p>Sunday 7th<br/>The Batt. Came back to supports today.</p>
<p>Monday 8th<br/>We moved back to Dickebush huts, &amp; the Batt. came out to here, tonight in the driving wind &amp; rain.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9<br/>We left again this morning, by Motor busses, for Connaught, lines near Abeele, where we are in tents We are back from the line about 8 miles. The wind is terrible &amp; living in tents with no floors is anything but comfortable.<br/>We have had a great many changes during the last month The sleeping valise is ones home as long as it turns up things are OK, but the continual moving</p>
