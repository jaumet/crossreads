<p>Wednesday 21 at break of day we sailed through the entrance. Saw trenches made against Turk on the canal banks. Indians &amp; Tommies stationed all along.</p>
<p>Thursday 22 arrived Port Said &amp; received a great ovation by the sailors on british warships.</p>
<p>Sailed for Alexandria Friday reached Alex entraining for Heliopolis camp. Visited Heliopolis at night.</p>
<p>Saturday paid my first trip to Cairo.<br/>Saw [indecipherable] tree &amp; well on Thursday 29th.<br/>Friday 30th. Saw Cleopatras needle<br/>Sunday 8th August church parade on the sand 2000</p>
