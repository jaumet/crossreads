Leaving Salisbury at 4.20 we simply flew along and I do not remember being in anything that covered the ground quicker.<br/>In swerving to avoid a rabbit the tire came completely away and it was only a miracle that the car was pulled up without mishap.<br/>He had another wheel and soon remedied the defect and we were in Camp at 5.<br/>A short sleep of half an hour and up again.</p>
<p>Saturday 29th<br/>It was fortunate that the morning was devoted to washing etc. so we had a sleep.  Letter from home &amp; M.B.</p>
<p>Sunday 30th<br/>Very hot weather of late and today was exceptionally hot, about 80 in shade.<br/>church in Y.M.C.A. Rollestone again, also at Durringstone [Durrington] at night.</p>
<p>Monday 31st<br/>Received letter from cousin Jim.<br/>Commenced training again.  Route march.</p>
