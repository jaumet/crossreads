  
 October. Oct. 1st Our Engine & Dynamo being under repair we deem it inadvisable to show - A visit to the "Pedlars" (a neighbouring British Council Party) - A new poem & two new concerted items written. 
 Oct. 2nd Bulgaria no longer a belligerent - Unconditional surrender - Depression throughout Germany - Rehearsal & read "Shelley" - Letters answered.
