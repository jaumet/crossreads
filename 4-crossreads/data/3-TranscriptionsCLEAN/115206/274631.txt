
March 1918Saturday 2
MessinesReceived letter from Geo Lang--- who is in hospital in Blighty.Fritz strafed us with H.V.s all the morning. To be sure I had ze vind up. He dropped one in front of our Pill Box, scattering same with sand.Journeyed to P.gun for compass snowed and sleeted all the afternoon - bitterly cold.Garfoot trotted down to H.Q.for the pigeons, and we now have deux pigeons for company.Had a long letter from Roy.
