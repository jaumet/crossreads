<p>I have been fortunate in many things   &amp;  feel quite satisfied with the work I tried to so, Don't say anything about this, there are many more who deserve it better than I do.</p>
<p>Did not go to the Pantomime, could not get seats &ndash; all booked up until February, so my chance is not much.</p>
<p>Had a quiet day on Wednesday. Washed, mended   &amp;  wrote</p>
