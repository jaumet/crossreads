It was a very rough trip & I felt tired when we arrived there - having had little to eat since the day previous. I slept at Headquarters making a bunk in the underground store wh had been dug.
Sunday 7th NovTo-day we fixed prices to allow of a little profit for breakages etc & then called for orders from the Batterys. Needless to say the orders far exceeded the stock I had secured. In the evening I made up the orders working late to have them ready early.
