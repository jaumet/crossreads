 [Thursday 1 June 1916] 
 Killed 15 Turks wounded 2, Prisoner 1- New Zealanders had 2 men wounded, they did the work, we were in support fortunately the Weather was cool- We took our Wireless Plant &amp; had aeroplanes to scout. This  afternoon  morning their Planes came over, &amp; bombed us well- Killed 1 officer,  4  5 Troopers, wounded 16 others &amp; Killed 38 Horses-, I think Col  Meredith's  ------ horse among them. I got thrown &amp; about 10 horses galloped over me- but only got sand in my ears etc. Honours are easy again- Tell H.L.A. my Horses are O.K.
