<p>Sunday 6 October<br/>Church, Dr. Jowat.  Richmond for the afternoon &amp; evening. Once back in London one feels that one has never left it so completely does it banish France from the mind.<br/>Of course rationing is in force, but there is no shortage of food.</p>
<p>Monday 7 October<br/>Windsor all day. "The Boy" at night. Windsor &amp; <strike>Eato</strike> Eton is a fine &amp; scrupulously clean town.  The Castle is fine &amp; the view therefrom, of the river &amp; the country for miles is glorious.  Eton College stands out well.</p>
