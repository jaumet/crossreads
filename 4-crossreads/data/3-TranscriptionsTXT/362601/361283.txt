 17 October, Friday 
 We are fearfully busy &amp; take 28. Meet E &amp; E on boat. I am Hooker in meet W.H.A. 
 18 October, Saturday 
 Go to D Why. Fix door &amp; layout the ashes. I go for a swim alone &amp; then for a stroll to then rocks. We have dinner at Guys &amp; Tea on the beach. Two talking women drive me nearly mad, come Home at 7 pm &amp; write &amp; play piano. 
 Wrote Kev, Pine &amp; H Hey &amp; E &amp; K Naple.
