
Letter RegisterFeb 2 - Gladys Noble - Galongditto - Nelly Slattery - dittoFeb 6 - Gladys Noble - dittoDitto - Nelly Slattery - ditto
Tuesday. We went back to Belah to water & feed  Infantry still attacking town with heavy losses  a great assault made by NZ and 2nd Brigade on Eastern end  captured 2 guns and 300 prisonersWednesday  Very quiet so far  a big offensive coming off  40,000 Turks on our front
