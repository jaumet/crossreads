<p>February 1915<br/>Sunday 21<br/>Roys BIRTHDAY<br/>Church parade as usual. Weather very warm</p>
<p>Monday 22<br/>No parade until 2 PM. Marched about three miles &amp; the C.O. told us we were off to Europe in a fortnight's time Bivouacked all night. Did a few charge &amp; dug a huge trench</p>
