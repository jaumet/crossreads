19th March 1918Left Fremantle this morning at 6.30. Passed the Ionic coming in.
23rd March 1918Arrived at Adelaide late this afternoon. Got into the bay at 6 oclock. All the South Australian Victorian &amp; Tasmanian troops left at 8 for the city. The balance of the troops aboard were entertained by the people with a good feed &amp; concert.
25th March 1918Left Adelaide tonight at 10 oclock after a pleasant 2 days with the people and their hospitality.
