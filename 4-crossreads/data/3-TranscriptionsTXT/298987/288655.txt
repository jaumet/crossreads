 HMT'S. A 72 formaly the P &amp; O S.S. Beltand We were then payed up to date &amp; at 10 pulled out from the Wharf which was imeditally crowded with the friends etc who had been locked out side There was a mixture of cheering &amp; crying as the tugs pulled us out to the Bouy Another L/C &amp; self were detailed as guard for the gangway. Quite a number of boats &amp; launches around to have a last word &amp; was more disapointed than I would like to admit when at 12-30 we made a start &amp; my people had not turned up This was the start propper for the great adventure &ndash; What will it bring forth Wyh trouble about it &ndash; Dinner was our main consideration at the time &ndash; or rather the want of it.
