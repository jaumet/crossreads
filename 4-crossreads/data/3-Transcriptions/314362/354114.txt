<p>4 Sunday  [Nov]<br/>off to our new billets with vague map reference  &amp;  plenty of bluff !!! Fixed them up o.k. tho</p>
<p>5 Monday<br/>spent the day getting everyone  more or less settled. Three lres. from Rup Cheers !!</p>
<p>6 Tuesday<br/>Have quite a decent billet myself. Am savg one for Pennie. Visited the Aust. Hosp. &amp; saw Sis. Shepheard.</p>
<p>7 Wednesday<br/>Much rain  &amp; cold. Ugh &ndash; the coming Winter.  Am to be counsel for Usher ! my hat !!! Parcel for birthday from Rup.</p>
<p>8 Thursday<br/>Hear we move on the 10th Watts came to dinner.  Heard from [indecipherable] at Havre. Paid in Baillieul</p>
<p>9 Friday<br/>Had rather a good dinner of chicken etc !  lres from Rup. Alb. &amp; Muriel.  we leave tomorrow for [indecipherable]<br/>10 Saturday<br/>Born 1891&amp; still going strong !! moved to Locre in Belgium.</p>
<p>11 Sunday<br/>Moved on to Linchenhoek [?] Camp from Locre. Quite a good place &amp; camp.</p>
<p>12 Monday<br/>Played cards most of the day &amp; won a few francs !!! Photos of Rup &amp; I came from her.<br/>13 Tuesday<br/>Went ahead to sun new support position at Wijtschaete Ridge.  Pretty quiet spot.</p>
<p>14 Wednesday<br/>HQ have some dugout ! Oak fittings &amp; feather beds. also enamelled fire grates. snaps from Rup.</p>
<p>15 Thursday<br/>Lovely day &amp; no shelling !  Cheers !!  Went up forward with MacDonnell &amp; saw our line</p>
<p>16 Friday<br/>moved to new HQ in Ravine Wood in aftrn.   Communications rather rotten but soon fixed them up</p>
<p>17 Saturday<br/>Shifted again back to [indecipherable] Farm &amp; established myself there. Rather a cramped sleep !</p>
