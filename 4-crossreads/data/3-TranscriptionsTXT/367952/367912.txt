 Transcribers note: Typed on Letterhead of Australian imperial Force, Base Records Office Victoria Barracks 
 Dear Madam, 
 Forwarded herewith is one of the late No. 3770 Private T. Bell, 20th Battn. received  ex "Barambah" I shall be much delighted if you will kindly let me know whether same (Enclosure.) comes safely to hand, by signing and returning the enclosed printed slip. 
 Yours faithfully, 
 J M Lean Major, Officer in Charge, Base Records
