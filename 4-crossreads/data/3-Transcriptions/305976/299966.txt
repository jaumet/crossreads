<p>Thursday Jan 31<br/>Nothing doing<br/>Feb 1 Friday<br/>Kicked by mule at Water Trough. Usual Routine. Oranges plentiful.<br/>Saturday Feb 2<br/>Jacko over today. Getting monotonous here with nothing to do.<br/>Saturday Feb 9<br/>Last week there was nothing much doing. We have had plenty of windy, cold &amp; wet weather. We hear a Bachelors tax has come into force in Ausy. New Zealanders Brigade moved out into trenches somewhere round Jerusalem .</p>
