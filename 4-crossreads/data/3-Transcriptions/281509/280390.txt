<p>1916 June 16<br/>Friday</p>
<p>March out to old hulks on beach &amp; stay there all morning.  Bathing parade in Aft. March up to Russian Steam Baths. Good. Enter vapour room first. Get good sweat up. Then hot &amp; showers. Feel nice &amp; clean.<br/>Tony appointed aerial expert.</p>
