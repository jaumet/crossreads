<p>Nov. 8.<br/>Another visit by German Aircraft &ndash; no damage &ndash; We lose Capt Hamilton &ndash; Despatch of Parcel (cards) &ndash; Quiet night</p>
<p>Nov. 9.<br/>Day duty &ndash; Acquaintances renewed &ndash; 8th Fld. &ndash; Aust Mail.</p>
<p>Nov. 10th<br/>Another quiet day &ndash; Rumours of Peronne &ndash; Still another visit by Taube &ndash; 37 patients killed or wounded in the Rest Station &ndash; No bon!</p>
<p>Nov. 11th<br/>Quiet day (for us) &ndash; fine night unfortunately &ndash; German armoured train shells our camp without doing damage &ndash; lively time while it lasts.</p>
<p>Nov. 12th<br/>News of the Arabia &ndash; Reuben Lovelock &ndash; Our Camp No mans land prior to offensive! Our tent originally German Officer's</p>
