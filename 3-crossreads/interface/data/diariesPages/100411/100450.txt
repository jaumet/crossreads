Arrived at Waterloo Stn. at about 12 midday then walke to Horseferry Rd. had a nice meal at War-Chest Club. After dinner went walked down out on Victoria St. branched off into Hyde Park. In Jan. it looked like a swamp would. Thick with snow all the trees old dead looking. Now it is all out in leaf bloom. The ducks on the ponds chaps pulling-boats being rowed about too. It was beautiful. Saw Rotten Row too. Went back to War Chest for dinner. Had a smoke read in lounge room took a taxi from Picadilly to Mme. Tussauds Wax Works.
