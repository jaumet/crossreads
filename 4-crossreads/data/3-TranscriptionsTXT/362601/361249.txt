 8 August, Friday 
 Mum &amp; I drive to Manly &amp; do a lot of shopping, meet Percy Hickson &amp; Amy. 
 We drive to Dee Why. I play piano all night &amp; amuse Dad &amp; Mum. Ellis &amp; George out. Recd April letters back from Eng &amp; France. 
 Wrote to M.R.L. 18 
 9 August, Saturday 
 Doris Park brings in music "Till we meet". We chop the tree down &amp; I work like a nigger. I wash &amp; go to Otto's place for Tra &amp; Talk. 
 Recd invitation to Party on 21.8.19.
