<p>or rather privilege our uniform earns for us.<br/>The only thing of course is the fact that my not going back isnt fair to you dear people at home after being away so long - dont think too bad of me my dear sister I will be over to see you as soon as possible &amp; if I go to India it will be close to home anyway &amp; leave will come round.<br/>Now for my present doings. A Colonel D.V. Stacy of Sydney is my companion on this trip, at present we are in Scotland 4 miles from Edinburgh being guests of a Mrs Wauchope who by-the-bye traces herself back to William the C. - living in one of the oldest homes in Scotland - from here we go on to Aberdeen &amp; then down the West coast to Glasgow the whole tour taking a month, then Xmas in London &amp; another tour this time in England.<br/>I had what is called here Armistice week in London</p>
