 3-2-1918 Three guns drawn from 16th Bty. Right section move out tomorrow at 2-30.P.M. Relieved by the 50th Bty 
 4-2-1918 Fine but cloudy. Right section of the 50th Bty arrived at 1.P.M. The remaining sections of our Bty move off tomorrow. Our front very quiet. 
 5-2-1918 SEC. BOIS Left KAMEL at 1-30.P.M. Weather fine. Arrived at MERRIS aeria 9-30.P.M. Horses in one large stable mud up to their hocks, in a filthy condition. Trying to get a local farriers shop to work in. Today is cold and cloudy. 
 6-2-1918 Everything upside down, Baggage all over the shop. Today I was awarded the Croix De Guerre, Belgium.
