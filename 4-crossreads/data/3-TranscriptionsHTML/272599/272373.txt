<p>1917 Thursday 12 April</p>
<p>Fatigues per usual.    another cold day with wet underfoot due to the snow of last night thawing.<br/>Rumour of a reverse in the 4th Div. line.   Fritz counter attacked  we captured a village &amp; secured some prisoners &amp; a couple of tanks.   A heavy bombardment on our left this evening, view it from a distant hill.<br/>Full mail arrives this evening.<br/>Letters from Mary, Cis, May, Harry, Wall, Ess, Ethel Mac &amp; Miss W, Nurse Swiney.   All so welcome after a month's silence.</p>
