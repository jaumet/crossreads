After that, while at the Base, men coming from the unit always spoke of him, and his work. <p>Within a few days of the stunt, wounded were arriving at the Base, from hospitals, and great were the tributes paid to him. I was not at all surprised to learn that the Colonel had embraced the Catholic Church before his death, God rest his soul. What man, of intelligence, who gave a thought to religion could fail to be impressed by that <strike>upheld</strike> preached and upheld by such a man.</p>
