Thursday 18.10.17
Compelled to stay in bed for the day, passed my time letter writing. Very pleased with this place, being treated like kings.
Friday 19.10.17
Given my blues allowed to get up, some swank in my new rig out; - Blues, pair old civil boots, Tommy cap, red tie, stick
