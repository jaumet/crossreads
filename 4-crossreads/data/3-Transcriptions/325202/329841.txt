<p>frosty day with a slight fog All the trees &amp; grass white with the frost &ndash; about 20 miles riding &amp; alternately walking to keep warm.</p>
<p>The 2nd Inf Bgde who have been spelling at Vignacourt were sent on by train instead of the usual marching as <strike>there</strike> their feet are so bad through the frost &amp; wet of the trenches</p>
<p>Have just heard that Montauban Railhead where we draw the supplies from was heavily shelled a few days ago &amp; amongst other casualties Bob Kennedy, George Hyde &amp; G Brandie of our Company were 'blesse' slight ones only;</p>
<p>We go back</p>
