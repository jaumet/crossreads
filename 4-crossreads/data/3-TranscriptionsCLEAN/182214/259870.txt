 women work hard & we could not help admiring them as they did a mans work, while the men were at the war.  The Butter was very nice & tasty.  The dog would be placed in a large wheel & keen running around churning the butter.   one often saw a digger (nickname for an Australian soldier) helping to milk the cows.  It was therefore not surprising when one hears of a marriage taking place.  Some of the French girls are very pretty & have a style all of their own, we got a gas alarm that night but it was only our sentry with a touch of nerves we saw several aeroplaines being shelled.
