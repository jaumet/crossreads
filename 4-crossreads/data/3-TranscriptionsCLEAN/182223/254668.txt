  
 Thursday 3 June 1915 In Cairo during the morning  Working rest of the day  Many letters from Home carry dates from 20 th  Jny 1915 to 4 th  May. 
 Friday 4 June 1915 Not out all day  Busy. Office. Reading. Writing.  50 patients in one bunch from Alexandria tonight. 
 Saturday 5 June 1915 Posted letters Watkins, Arthur, Knowles, Armit, Pharmacutical Secty, Victoria  In Cairo, Med school, chemical laboratory &c. between 10 & 12 noon.  Office work. Sleep from 3 to 5 p.m. Wrote to Flowers, Fitzgerald, Usher, Maitland. 
 Sunday 6 June 1915 Posted to Flowers, Fitzgerald, Maitland, Usher, Girls, Paton, & Mrs Fraser. Mass 10 a.m at Bassilica in Heliopolis. Benidiction at Sisters Church (Africian Mission) at Zeitoun at 7 p.m.
