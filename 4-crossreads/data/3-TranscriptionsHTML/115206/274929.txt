<p>December  1918.<br/>Friday 27</p>
<p>At Sea. Red Sea. "Karoola."<br/>Weather warming up slightly, and the sea has been like glass. Roy has been appointed Secretary to the Sports Committee.<br/>Travelled 254 miles from Suez, and 90 miles through the canal.<br/>Played cribbage and lost. Played Auction bridge for a little while.<br/>Issued with a clean change and handed in my contribution to the "Karoolian's " Editor.</p>
