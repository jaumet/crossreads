<p>13 October 1916<br/>Friday<br/>Busy preparing for departure of morrow. Down to baths &amp; get change of underclothing.  Bob &amp; self post our souvenirs &amp; presents home. Y.M.C.A. before turning in. farewell prayer by Y.M.C.A. manager.  Glee Club practice. Piccaninny's Lullaby.</p>
