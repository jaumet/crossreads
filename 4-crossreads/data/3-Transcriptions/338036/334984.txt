<p>[13th Dec. contd]<br/>much more closely contested than the scores indicate.  The mud was awful &amp; at best was ankle deep but that didn't matter so very much.  After scraping some of the mud off &amp; having a bath I took part in a 500 tourney and spent an enjoyable night.</p>
<p>Friday 14th<br/>All haste being made in packing up wagons &amp; arrangements being completed for 'B' Section to move off first thing tomorrow for L'Estrade DRS which we were to take over.  Our concert party however found time to give  a concert at night.</p>
<p>Saturday 15th<br/>At 9 am the 7 Fld Amb marched in &amp; at noon our Section completed the movement by marching to L'Estrade where we occupied almost the same billets as before.  The place however had improved since our last occupation.</p>
<p>Sunday 16th<br/>In afternoon Fred Cooke &amp; I went for a stroll into Steenwerck &amp; had tea in a private house under very pleasant conditions.  I purchased a torch also.</p>
