<p>having fled from their own country 10 months ago when the Huns first came into their land.  I immediately took the kiddie into my arms much to the amusement of everyone, especially the good monk.  We stayed for an hour or so &amp; had tea.  We then walked back to the station &amp; caught a train to Stratford where we changed &amp; caught a Customs House train 40 minutes later.  Arrived back at ship at 8.30 after spending the most enjoyable day of our stay.<br/><u>Thursday 21st[22nd] July 1915.</u>  Raining all day stayed aboard &amp; helped clean wireless gear up &amp;  get it spick &amp; span ready for use.<br/><u>Friday 22nd[23rd] July 1915.</u> Very miserable day raining intermittently.
