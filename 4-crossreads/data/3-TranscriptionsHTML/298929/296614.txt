<p>But Look! a wisp of smoke goes curling high,<br/>Towards the glorious sun which gave it life,<br/>And bears aloft the spirit ne'er to die,<br/>And triumphs still o'er season's bitter strife.</p>
<p>O noble life, so handsome and so brave,<br/>That cheered less happy fellows with a smile,<br/>Till duty's finger pointed o'er the wave,<br/>And pangs of parting gripped his heart the while</p>
