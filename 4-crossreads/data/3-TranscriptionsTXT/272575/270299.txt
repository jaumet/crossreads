 No. 3598 8th R. [indecipherable] At Sea January [indecipherable] 1916 
 My Dear Mother We may reach our journeys end now in a few days but we do not know our destination yet, we have been very fortunate, the weather has been perfect, and ever since we left Freemantle the sea has been calm.   I was hoping that we would have a little rough weather to make things lively, but so far we have struck none.
