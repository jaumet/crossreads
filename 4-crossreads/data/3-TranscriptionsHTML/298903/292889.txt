<p>had a gathering of friends &amp; had a real good evening with dancing etc. which found me trying to demonstrate some new steps.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5th March  Had a quiet day remaining by the fireside the whole time.  Cold and wet.  Telegraphed for an extension of 4 days &amp; received answer same day granting 2 so will remain here until Friday evening.</p>
<p>Thursday 6th March  Remained in all the morning, foggy and wet.  Afternoon cleared up &amp; went for a walk to Rooken Glenn, a fine Park a few miles out of the city.  Went to Mrs. Hunters at Newlands for the evening &amp; had a real good time, feeling at home all the time.</p>
<p>Friday 7th March  A cold day and snowing so stayed at home by the fire.  My cold much better.  Caught the 8.45 train to London being farewelled by Elsie A. Meg K. &amp; Mr. &amp; Mrs. Hunter.  Collected some Chaste Salutes</p>
