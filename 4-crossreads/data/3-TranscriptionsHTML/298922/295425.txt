<p>Easter Monday 9 April 1917</p>
<p>Bombed again. <strike>"Australian" arrived &amp; letter from the wife.</strike>  We are still waiting for the big fight, Egyptian word BUCKRAH meaning tomorrow applies, but I think it will be tomorrow. Enemy planes over every day now. Will not post this for sometime as Censors are very strict out here. <strike>Good thing my writing so bad, can't read it.</strike>  Had a letter from Nowell Forth he is O.C. Camel Corps at ABBASSIA a suburb of Cairo .</p>
<p>Cut all my hair off &amp; had a bath ready now for Constantinople if caught.</p>
<p>See Bible AMOS, Chap. 17 verses 6 &amp; 7.</p>
