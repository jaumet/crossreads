<p>before I was out of bed. We both went for a bath this morning. Went to Horseferry Rd &amp; bank with Bill. Then to lunch. Rushed off &amp; caught the 1.30pm to Reading. Went round to the hotel &amp; met Ada. Went into the park for a walk &amp; it rained. Last time I was there it rained. Caught the 5.10pm back &amp; went out for Vi, at 7.30. Took her to see Marie Lohr in Nurse Benson &amp; was late as usual for the start. Liked the show immensely.</p>
<p>Saturday. 26.10.18.<br/>Took Harry &amp; the gang to lunch. Went to Marie's for tea &amp; created the usual disturbance by having a good cushion fight. Had pancakes for tea. Took her out some lard. Took she &amp; Janet Stewart to see Dennis Eadie and Sidney Valentine in "The Freedom of the Seas".  Gee it was goodo. Put Janet in a tube &amp; took Marie home &amp; we had supper. Soup, Ham &amp; tomatoe &amp; some real cakes &amp; coffee.</p>
