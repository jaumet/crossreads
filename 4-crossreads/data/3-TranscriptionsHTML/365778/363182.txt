<p>Tuesday 20/3/17</p>
<p>Took over line from 23rd . Hdqrs in Vaux-Vraucourt, adjusted line of ourposts.</p>
<p>Wednesday 21/3/17</p>
<p>Handed over &frac12; line to 27th Bn. Great air fight. Prince Frederick Charles of Prussia forced to land &amp; taken prisoner by us wounded.</p>
<p>Thursday 22/3/17</p>
<p>Made arrangements for attack on Lagnicourt, visited Right Battalion.</p>
<p>Friday 23/3/17</p>
<p>Attack on Lagnicourt postponed, saw the General and was sniped at in his company with 77 mm shells, feeling very tired.</p>
