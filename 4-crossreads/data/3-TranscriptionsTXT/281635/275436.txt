
Games in reception swim after tea. Did not go, stopped at fire all night till 9.30pm. Recd 2 letters from Mother, 1 from Aunt jean, & p/card from Minnie Luscombe. Recd Xmas boxes from British Red Cross & Aust Red Cross.26/12/17 Boxing Day - 4 or 5 inches snow fell during night so everything is now white. After dinner YMCA gave a concert in Reception room & supplied biscuits cake  & tea making very enjoyable afternoon. Had salmon & chicken for tea.
