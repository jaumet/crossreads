
<p>321</p>
<p>MARCH 1917</p>
<p>28 WEDNESDAY</p>
<p>Fine but dull &amp; cold. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness ect all day. I was</p>
<p>stableman for the day. Getting</p>
<p>ready for a turn out tomorrow</p>
<p>in full marching order &amp; planting</p>
<p>all our surperfluous gear ect</p>
<p>so as the salvage people</p>
<p>wont get it. I put mine in</p>
<p>one of the barges up the river.</p>
