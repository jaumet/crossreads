<p>(1)</p>
<p>21/9/15</p>
<p>Cairo</p>
<p>Yesterday I had leave and I went to Cairo and The Pyrimids and Sphinx&nbsp; Left Cairo By motor car after lunch time. we arrived at our Destination we had a Drink and then we hired a guide and he explained everything to us. we went under the mids and also through the Temples and the Buried City.</p>
<p>Things are very interesting here&nbsp; There was three of us and we had a Splendid time on Donkeys motor cars and Carriages and Trams.</p>
