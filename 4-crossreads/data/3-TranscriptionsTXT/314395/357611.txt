After a thorough inspection, during which I was deeply interested in architecture, colour, antiquities etc., my soldier said to me, "By Christ that was a - fine tier of sandbags in there".  (You know the huns used to bomb Amiens and some bags had been erected before the altar to shelter it from splinters.)
I was mistaken when I said Greg had gone to England.  I had been misinformed.  A letter from him recently shows his location to be some base hospital in France.  I note your demand for a full length photo by myself, and will have it done at the first opportunity.  I had one taken when I was in town but it was also a group.  I had not then received your letter (which was readdressed from England).
