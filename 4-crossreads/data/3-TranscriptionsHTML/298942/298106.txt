<p>to our home.</p>
<p>Noticed two Fritz observation balloons up not far away, &amp; can easily see from that how dangerous is the observation.  Still we have better I believe, for we have all the commanding positions.</p>
<p>Monday 6 May</p>
<p>The result of the observation balloon's ascent yesterday was evident during the night in the very frequent shell fire.  One shell fell in the farm house on the other side of the road &amp; aroused suspicions</p>
