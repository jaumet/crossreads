<p>strafing going on. Just as I write this Fritz has started his evening contribution which are landing round the road a few hundred yards away, two Yanks out of seven so far. Work during the day has been very slack &amp; I hope to get away tomorrow to go up &amp; see a special dugout near the line. I can combine business with pleasure as I really want to know what stocks they hold.</p>
<p>Thursday 1st March 1917</p>
<p>Things very quiet the last two days very few cases coming through. No one yet knows the extent of Fritz's retirement, our chaps moving forward cautiously &amp; consolidating as they go. Went out to day to the advanced possies one of which is a most elaborate affair. 'Tis a series of tunnels extending for miles, very deep down &amp; provides accomodation for ambulances, signallers</p>
