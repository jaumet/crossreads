<p>his opponent badly rattled would have cut loose &amp; punished him severely, but Cossey chose the more manly action, &amp; at the finish he was loudly cheered by the crowd.<br/>Had a letter from Lindsay Graham today &amp; he tells me that Len has been recommended to come to England on the 6 months stunt, it would be alright if it comes off.<br/>22nd. Hot &amp; muggy &amp; dust blowing all over the place, have been fairly busy what with the early morning parade &amp; being Duty Company, keeps the Orderly Sgt going.<br/>23rd. There was a flash concert last night here, the party cost about 30 guineas</p>
