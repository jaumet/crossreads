But when I received my mother's, father's, brothers and sisters letters full of love and anxiety on my behalf my heart yearned for home, and from that time forward I have been most anxious to return with as little delay as possible.  Though I have been on the ship for 7 months I have not described her, nor my fellow officers.  The ship itself is a German prize, captured in Melbourne.
