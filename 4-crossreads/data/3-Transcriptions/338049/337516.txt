<p>Saturday 2 Nov.<br/>Big roundup parade morning. Pay also.</p>
<p>Afternoon football V 38 B. Beaten after hard game. Additional chaps detailed to Stables.<br/>Saw Lieut Brisenden re education. Wrote to High Commr. &amp; [E E?] London.</p>
<p>Study. Very good evening's toil.</p>
