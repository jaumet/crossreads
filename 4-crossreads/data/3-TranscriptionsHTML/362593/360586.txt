<p>Saturday 10 August 1918<br/>Photos &amp; road recco.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 2 h. 20 m.  Photos from 9,500 ft. under heavy A.A. fire.<br/>Gunned transport, horses and killed a good many.<br/>Height 300 to 1000 ft., fired 700 rounds, also gunned transport.</p>
<p>Sunday 11 August 1918</p>
