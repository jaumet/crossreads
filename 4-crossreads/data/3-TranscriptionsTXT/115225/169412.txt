1917
Jan. 1st  Another beautiful day, sports were continued, we have only one parade a day on account of the sports. The ships company gave a concert in the evening but I didnt attend it I wrote some letters instead.
 
Jan. 2nd  Rainy & a very lumpy sea. we all stayed below all day with port holes closed. played 500 in the evening & then had to sleep below.
 
Jan 3rd  Beautiful day & the sea has gone down a lot. We had a death on board & he was lowered over the side with ceremony at 11.A.M.  We watched the boxing in the evening when Darkie McLeod fought Morris.
 
Jan 4th  Very hot day. I sat in the sun all morning perspiring, then had a shower. In the afternoon I lay in the shade & read "The Quaker Girl"  We played 500 in the evening.
 
Jan 5th  Very hot sun & choppy sea, also strong wind.- on sweeping fatigue in the morning. We sighted land at 6.P.M. & cruised off Durban all night.
