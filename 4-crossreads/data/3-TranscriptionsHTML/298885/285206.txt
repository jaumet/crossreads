<p>At 9AM we moved off on our trip via Boulogne  The whole journey being very slow, occupying 12 hours at which time we reached Amiens and were at once accorded a hearty welcome from Fritz in the way of dainty 9.2 shells dropping all round our detraining centre</p>
<p>It seems as if Fritz makes a speciality of locating the Aussies as the following will show.</p>
<p>In the first place it is the general knowledge that Fritz does not like to oppose the boys from the land of the Southern Cross</p>
