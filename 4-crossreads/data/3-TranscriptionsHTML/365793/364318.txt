<p>Aug 8 and now we are "home-ward bound" for the <u>same </u> dear old Harbor. Practically our only danger now is from Submarines &amp; by the morning of the 10th even <u>that</u> danger will be "nil" &ndash; because it is not until then that the destroyer leaves us. All the time &ndash; she is crossing &amp; recrossing our bows, <u>ever</u> watching for the deadly periscope.</p>
