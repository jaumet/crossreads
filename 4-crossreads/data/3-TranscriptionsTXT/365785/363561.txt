 6 weeks on business and will be pretty busy all the time. I am losing all my hair, tis falling out in waste paper baskets full and the Hairdresser says it will cost &amp;pound;5.5.0 to restore it to its usual beauty.  It is either that or a toupe &ndash; which do you recommend? And how are the infants?  I suppose my little man is in trousers and has applied for a commission by now and is Tingsy still irrepressible and Margaret tinier than ever? Tell Trix I got her letter and enclosures which I gave to one of the Hospital days which recur twice a day in London. Goodnight old girl and take care of yourself. 
 Yrs. plainer than ever Nan.
