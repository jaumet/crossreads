Nov. 1stOur "Section" - taken on strength of C.C.S. - Under canvas once again - Preparation of an "Operation tent" - We relieve 29th British Division.
Nov. 2nd.Transferred to No.1 Operating Tent which is run by 15th Aust F. Amb - Long & gruesome days work - 8.30a.m. to 9.30p.m. - Operations.
Nov. 3rd.Continue duties in Tent. No.1 - Assisting Capt. Brown, who impresses as being a thorough gentleman - Capt. Colyer of Newcastle also of Tent 1. - German "strafing" of Fricourt Wood. Remainder of our Ambulance relieve 15th F.A. up the line.
Nov. 4thPrevalence of bad weather continues.
