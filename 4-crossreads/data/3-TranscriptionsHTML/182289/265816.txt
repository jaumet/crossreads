<p>Le Bizet<br/>The following targets were engaged at stated times between 6 p.m. to 12 midnight at the request of the Infantry.<br/>(1) Enemy Dump on Road, C.12.a.00.61.<br/>(2) Searching Cemetery Drive from C.12.c.00.40 to C.12.c.40.30.<br/>(3) Approach Track, Screened Fm., C.5.b.60.38 to C.5.b.85.20.<br/>(4) Approach Track to Ceylon Fm., C.12.b.00.95 to C.12.b.30.95.<br/>(5) Approach to Duriez Fm., C.5.d.30.90 to C.5.d.42.81.<br/>Enemy's Artillery activity more active than usual, chiefly registrations.  Enemy machine guns fairly quiet.<br/>Certain enemy aircraft crossed our line flying high.  Weather clear and bright, visability good.</p>
