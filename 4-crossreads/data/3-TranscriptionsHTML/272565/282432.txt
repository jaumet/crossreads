<p>Saturday 30th Dec.<br/>Into Vignacourt again for the day.  Boxing tournament on tonight in the local hall, the Follies Show having disbanded temporarily.  Went inside local Church.<br/>Sunday 31st Dec.<br/>Writing all day otherwise things were very quiet.  The weather for the past few days has been dull and drizzling rain.<br/>Monday 1st Jan. 1917<br/>New Years Day.  Xmas</p>
