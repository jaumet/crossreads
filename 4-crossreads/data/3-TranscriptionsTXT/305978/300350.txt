SundayOn stables then fatigue. We gave the horses a salt water bath &amp; had one ourselves, it was glorious &amp; the horses enjoyed it after their long &amp; dusty trip.We are lucky to be near the sea; as there is nothing else to see but sand &amp; sand.
Dec 13/Went out with 5 men &amp; a corpral as a water gaurd 3 miles towards the enemy's position. We searched every old house for enemy but only found five rifles &amp; some old pistols &amp; a few coins.When we arrived
