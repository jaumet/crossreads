<p>Saturday 8 March 1919<br/>In town arranging Permits to travel, money matters, etc.  As I told you, have sent Dolly's money to Commonwealth Savings Bank, Melbourne, with specimen signature so she can draw on arrival.  With her I am sending the following kit,</p>
<p>1 New Steel Uniform Box 4" x 2"<br/>1 old ditto<br/>1 Wooden Box about 4&frac12;" x 2&frac12;"<br/>1 Big leather Trunk<br/>2 Black Tin Trunks<br/>1 Gladstone Bag<br/>1 Brown leather Trunk 4&frac12; x 2&frac12;<br/>1 Hat Box<br/>1 Suit Case<br/>1 Dressing Case<br/>1 Cradle</p>
<p>The first 3 mine.</p>
