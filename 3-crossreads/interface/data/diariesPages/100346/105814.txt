Tuesday Jan 15th
Bristol : wet day : caught the 4 pm back got to camp about 8.30 pm : very heavy rain : got a parcel from Aunt Myra also socks from J B J mittens from C H.
Wednesday Jan 16th
Fovant : cold day : usual parades during the day had a couple of reading tests : wrote at night :
Thursday Jan 17th
Fovant : usual parades : all inside work on account of the sloppy ground rain
