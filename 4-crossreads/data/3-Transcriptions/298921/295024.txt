<p>February, 1915<br/>Saturday 20<br/>Divisional day today 20000 men out, including Batteries Hospitals etc. etc:-<br/>The 1st Brigade of Light Horse did some smart work in attacking blank ammunition being used.<br/>Horses getting more used to Rice Straw or Tebren &amp; as the Camels bring us green alfalfa daily the horses are becoming more used to them &amp; the Donkeys. I have told Grazier people not to send Grazier after [indecipherable] I think about March.</p>
