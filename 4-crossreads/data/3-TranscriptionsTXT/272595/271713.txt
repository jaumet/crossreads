Lindsay &amp; I paid Len a visit over at the OTB. last night, we played "bridge" till fairly late then came home in the moonlight.  I hear just after we left there was a big row there, &amp; Len threw a couple of them out by the scruff of the neck, he went on his final leave today before going to France.
