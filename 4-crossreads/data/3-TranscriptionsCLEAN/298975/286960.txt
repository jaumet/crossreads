
SeptThursday  27thEarly breckfast Busseled about without any reason caught train at Dinton 9 AM Arrived Waterloo about 12 Marched to Horsefury Rd & were turned loose. Had dinner at War Chest an exelent instituon Wandered around (4 of us) & got a room at 150A Southamton Row Near Russel Square. A Salvation Army place & very good. Wandered about some more & went to a rotton play A Little bit of Fluff
Friday 28thWent to The Bank & Wax Works Etc Lindfield Gardens in the evening
Saturday 29thMet Miss Murray had a look at Picture gallery & spent most of the day at Kew Gardens
