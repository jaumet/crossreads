  
 Hospital Ship Grantala 
 Oct 4th 1914 My beloved Mother - I wonder if you will have posted me a letter before you left. I suppose you left in the [indecipherable] & the girls too. My last news of the family was a letter from Father dated the 17th Sept - day before Jocelyn's birthday also a letter from Rosamond who seemed very happy and excited at the idea of leaving.
