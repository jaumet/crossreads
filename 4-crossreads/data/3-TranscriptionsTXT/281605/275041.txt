
26 when daylight broke we had entered Port Said & land was visible on both sides -
29 passed 12 apostles & Hells gate on port side - during rough sea
31 passed along coast of Africa & late afternoon passed Cape Gardafewue the most easterly point of Africa.
1st 1919 had fancy chess parade & Ball on board
Pulled into Colombo & lay in side break water about 2AM on 7/1/19
Weighed anchor after coaling & watering at 12 NOON 9/1/19 -
11th cross the line just before midday -
14th passed Cocos Islands during the night -
21st pulled into & dropped anchor at Fremantle about 6 AM.  afternoon all W.A. troops left in launches    took fruit & vegetables aboard -
22nd Weighed anchor & left Fremantle about 8.30 AM & run into fairly big Sea. -  & started [sprecy?] for Influenza -
