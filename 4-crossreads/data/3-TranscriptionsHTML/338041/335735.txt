<p>Sept. 24th<br/>Sabbath. "B" Section take over Divisional Baths &ndash; We are billeted in a Convent &amp; have every convenience tending to make us comfortable, - Our handy position to town a valuable asset &ndash; but demanding a power of wealth. &ndash; Sports</p>
<p>Sept. 25th<br/>"Talbot House" &amp; Rev Clayton &ndash; visits to Y.M.C.A &amp; town. Photos posted home &ndash; Daily hot &amp; cold showers.</p>
<p>Sept. 26th.<br/>A busy day - Good news from England &amp; the front.</p>
<p>Sept. 27th<br/>"Our Canteen" &ndash; Parcel from Home &ndash; Aerial combats. Rumour of Combles &amp; Thiepval.</p>
<p>Sept. 28th.<br/>Rumour confirmed &ndash; "Confession" &ndash; Belgian Priest &ndash; Card etc</p>
