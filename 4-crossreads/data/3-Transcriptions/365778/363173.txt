<p>Tuesday 6/2/17</p>
<p>Took over line from 28th Battalion everything quite.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7/2/17</p>
<p>Usual shelling visited Companies in front line sent out patrols at night one casuality.</p>
<p>Thursday 8/2/17</p>
<p>Enemy shelling heavier 4 casualties &ndash; good work done at night &ndash; went out to Bde for about 2 hrs.</p>
<p>Friday 9/2/17</p>
<p>Quiet Day one casualty relieved by 18th and returned to Cinque Ports Camp.</p>
<p>Saturday 10/2/17</p>
<p>Slept well mid-day went &amp; saw the General who leaves for England for 10 days tomorrow.</p>
