<p>&amp; I laid down in the cool green grass &amp; blessed old England for being such a glorious place, whatever her faults may be there is nothing that can rob her of her lovely &amp; beautiful places of interest all hallowed with age &amp; memories of long ago, I can quite understand people calling England "home" for she is worthy of it.</p>
