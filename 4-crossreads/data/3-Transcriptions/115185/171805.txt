<p>18 - They get regular letters from Robert in trenches at Dardanelles. Archie is fretting to be off. Clapin rang up Aunt Isabel the day I arrived. She is married to Basil Greatrix &ndash; he is in the A.S.C. Lieutenant and goes Dardanelles this week too. I expect we'll all go together. I mean under the same escort.<br/>She is offering her services to Australian hospital in London<br/>Clapin so have, her husband is a charming young man, full of grit &ndash; has been fighting in German East Africa. I hope he comes back safe and sound. He told me he went to see Father for employment when he was in Sydney &ndash; but Dad asked him what he could do? I think he said he</p>
