<p>1915<br/>Mar. 18 Thu.<br/>Took 4 patients in motor ambulance out to No.1 General Hospital at Heliopolis &amp; then took them through Heliopolis camp to the Aerodrome Hospital. I like these motor trips somewhat, I guess, although it makes a chap somewhat late for tea.</p>
