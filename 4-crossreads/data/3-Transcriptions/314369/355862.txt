<p>22 July 1915<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>More mail arrives but no letter for me.<br/>A Turkish shell wounds seventeen men on the beach this afternoon none killed.</p>
<p>1915 July 23<br/>Friday</p>
<p>The Turks put four high explosive shells on to a road close to our bivouac but do no damage</p>
