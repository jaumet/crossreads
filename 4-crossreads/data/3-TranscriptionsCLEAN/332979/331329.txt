 Food Friday 6/4/17. After Breakfast we were paraded for teeth inspection by the Dentist & then dismissed for the Day, after resting I went for a Stroll in the Afternoon along the usual walk till tea time & after Tea I spent the evening writing home. Good Friday was a red letter day for our Corps it being our first Mail Day from Australia, there were great excitement in the morning when our letters were handed out to us.
