 21 January, 1916 Soldiers Club  Maadi We were on the parade ground in full marching order at 8.0 this morning &amp; went up to Heloun again, this time we went by way of the desert &ndash; had a pretty decent trip &amp; got back about four oclock.  Evans left my section &amp; Joe Woods came in his place &ndash; old Joe is a thundering good sort. Got eight letters this evening so am well in the [indecipherable] according to Vic &ndash; the troops are playing up in Australia, they want to send the beggars right into it, that would quieten them.
