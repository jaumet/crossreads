 31 December 1918 Unable to leave camp as we are under orders. During morning draft is cancelled & then is changed to a stand by & finally we report 7 pm to Camp Adjutant No 12 camp - get into motor lorries & whizz off to station Gare des Maritimes & find the train a collection of goods wagons with one 3rd class carriage & 1 first class with the windows broken. 
 I find myself in a 3rd The 3rd class here either are a long carriage with wooden seats & backs or else these backs are continued for 5 ft & thus make some little privacy. However sleep save from exhaustion is impossible The soldiers are happier because they can stretch their limbs at full length & have a brazier whilst officers must perish as compartment is too small for a brazier
