
5354Poperinghe this afternoon. It was A.1.
20/10/16First frost this morning by jove it was cold. The duck-boards around the place looked lovely under a snow white crust. It has turned out a lovely day, beautiful in the sun. The Aeroplanes took advantage of the clear morning & were fairly active.
21/10/16Frost heavier this morning the house tops were snow white & glistened in the early morning sun.
23-10/16Am just about to turn in after a busy day. We leave to-morrow for Steenvoorde but only for a day or so from there we are making for the Somme
