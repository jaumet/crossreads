Last night my feet were very sore so I paraded sick this morning.  The doctor a major, ordered me to the hospital and I was driven in this afternoon by one of the 40th Bat. Transports to the 10th. Field Ambulance Hospital in Armentieres for another rest and medical attention.  I took all my kit and gear with me.  The name of the village where our transports are located as well as other transports of the Brigade is A. L'Epinette.</p>
