<p>Left Albany on the 31st with 17 Transports, the 2nd Contingent, about 20 thousand Troops and Horses<br/>Arrived at Colombo on the 14th of January<br/>Left Colombo on the 16th arrived at Aden on the 23rd and left again the same day after taking in water and Provisions<br/>Arrived at Suez on the 27th<br/>Proceeded through the Suez Canal on the 28th at 9, a.m at Full Speed, 14 knots, as fighting had commenced in the Canal arrived at Port said at 8,30 p.m and tied up alongside of Custom House to await orders<br/>Transports came through as quick as possible and proceeded strait on to Alexandria to disembark Troops<br/>Left Port said on the 1st of February for Dardanelles arrived at Tenedos on the 5th<br/>Started Patrol Duties with B. Submarines on the 6th also French Submarines<br/>On March the 17th ran ashore at entrance to Lemnos at 9,45 p.m returning from Patrol Duties, Part of the Crew was taken off by Battleships Steamboat and Lifeboat H.M. Destroyer Chelmer towed us off at 2.a.m next morning, came off successfully went into Lemnos tied up to Hindustan</p>
