<p>We left Amiens &amp; proceeded to Ballieux where we visited the Australian 68th Flying Squadron. This squadron which has just recently arrived In France, is undergoing a scouting &amp; training course, preparatory to more offensive work. We had a glorious run home through Doulens, St. Pol &amp; Hazebrouck.
