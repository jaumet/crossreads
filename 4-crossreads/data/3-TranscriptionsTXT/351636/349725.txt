 Centre for Soldiers' Wives &amp; Mothers 6.7.16 
 Dear Mr. Layton &ndash; Could you give Mrs. Monahan some temporary assistance, as she has not received any pay for 3 wks &ndash; The Patriotic Fund gave her &pound;1 a wk ago but she has nothing to go on with until pay comes on 15th July &amp; she has two small children 
 Yours truly E. H. Bennie.
