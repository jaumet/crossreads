<p>22 August 1918 Thursday<br/>Put 80 men into trenches again where Germans attacked us last &amp; moved Camp up the Wadi.  Dust worse than ever, but certainly cooler.<br/>We are staffing the <u>B.W.I.</u>   <strike>I don't suppose you know who they are</strike> but cannot write it plainer.  <strike>Posted notes yesterday hope they arrive &amp;</strike> ate your PLUM PUDDING on arriving into our new Bivouac area-  I have now done 4 years of war = 2 years S.A. makes 6 years active service.  I suppose the world will owe us at least a living if we come out &amp; are willing to work.</p>
