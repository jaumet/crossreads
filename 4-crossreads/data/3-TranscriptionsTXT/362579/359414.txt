 3rd A.Gen.H. B.E.H. France 7.10.17 
 My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters, 
 Had a lovely lot of home letters this last week. One from dear old Belle 16th July with darling Mother's of 17th July. I am so pleased you had such a happy day  &amp;  everything is so successful. 
 How I should love to be home again, but there is heaps of work to be done.
