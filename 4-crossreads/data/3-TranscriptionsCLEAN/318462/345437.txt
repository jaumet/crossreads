Thursday 6/12/17.Big frost again last night the ground is like iron now fritz was over again but not near us Big gun fire near Armentiers. a lovely morning clear & fine. deferred Pay 38-3-6. - 85/11 Wine 2.50. getting warmer so suppose rain is comingWrote to Dessie Dehms (Sox) & the Asst Sec. of Mrs Cole Hamilton's
Friday 7/12/17no frost this morning, Cloudy & like rain Boys on fatigue & drill. nothing much doing Fritz was over again last night reported that one of his planes was brought down. deferred Pay. 38-5-0 - 90/5. turning colder 12 noon am very lucky in this camp am living in the canteen with Cpl Siddons & Srng Williams both friends of mine & we have a good time together break up the boxes for a fire & have suppers last night Pork Sausages & Wine to night Pork chops. Sometimes we get fresh fish & salted Herrings. Anyway its a break to have a fire this weather. (Locre, Denoutre, Lindenhoek) Posted letters to Ladies from Y.S.C. fund. Wagga.
