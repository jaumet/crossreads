<p>Friday 28</p>
<p>Back to the old camp life.  Parade at 9 A.M. &amp; 2 P.M.  Some fatagues folded a few blankets this morn.</p>
<p>Sat. <strike>28th</strike> 1 March</p>
<p>Paid this morning &pound;12.0.0 to go on leave on Monday morn.  Met my old mate N. Bell went to Warminster for a walk.  Had the studs taken out of my boots &amp; rubber heels put on a great improvement.</p>
