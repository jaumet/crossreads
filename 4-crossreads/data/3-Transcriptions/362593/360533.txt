<p>Wednesday 27 March 1918<br/>Recco, Katiani.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 3 h. 30 m.  Got a direct hit on a body of 200 cav. with a 20 lb. bomb, caused a tremendous scatter and stampede.  Very pleased with result.  Also fired 200 rounds at same target.</p>
<p>Thursday 28 March 1918</p>
