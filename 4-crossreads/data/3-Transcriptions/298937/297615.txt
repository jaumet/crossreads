<p>Medical Stores man, you will be gone in 2 or 3 days.<br/>Our group is composed of Cpl. Weir, an easy going good fellow apparently &amp; Molloy, Stewart &amp; Nunan.  The latter is a cousin of the Nunan of 6th F.A. with whom I travelled to Gallipoli.  The Doctor &ndash; a newcomer &ndash; is Dr. Voss, &amp; I spent some time with him today making out a requisition for drugs &ndash; all in vain.
