Wednesday June 21. 1916.A nice day with the wind from the South West. The course is 4 points North of West. One of the Pioneers "Darkey" Silver was sent to the Hospital yesterday evening and this morning I was told he was suffering from measles. The Sports are to be held this afternoon.
The Sports were held this afternoon & were a very tame affair. They consisted chiefly of Rope Quoits, Deck Quoits, and Deck Billiards.
They will be continued to morrow. I wrote 2 letters today.
Thursday June 22. 1916.A nice day, with a fairly strong head wind on the starboard bow. The course is 2 points North of West.
Short arm inspection this afternoon.
Friday June 23. 1916Another nice day with a head wind. The course has not changed. I have finished reading a great lady detective tale entitled "Joan Mar" by Marie Connor
