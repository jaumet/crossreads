 Tuesday 30 April 
 Definitely decided that we go up the line this afternoon or evening.  We are supposed to be relieving a section of the other ambulance. Some say there is a [indecipherable] for Meteren, &amp; others that is only just an ordinary relief, so that it is impossible to say.  Feeling just an ordinary. Weather has again become misty. The waning moon perhaps prevented a raid of any proportions last night. Very light shelling today: one or two heavies onto Caestre at intervals.
