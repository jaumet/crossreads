<p>Thurs. 13-7-'16.<br/>Slept Under Apple tree last night. Cold at dawn. Rain my reveille 6.30 a.m. Enjoyed wash in Farmyard. Pet Rabbits Little Chickens. Dog called "Coquette" Purchased bread &amp; Butter for our bag. Baker &amp; wife &amp; Grand Mere Vin Rouge et Citron<br/>[indecipherable] <br/>Got new gas helmet after Lunch. Left Talmas 3.30 p.m. Arrived 7 p.m.   &amp;    Rainneville. Billetted with Mme &amp; Mon Clement. Dog called "Tambour". Paul Gente 19 + Gaston Clement 16 very bright boys. 4 cows to milk we helped old Grand Mere Clement</p>
