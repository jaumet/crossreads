<p>Somewhere in France</p>
<p>My dear Wife</p>
<p>We are now resting in a village after a term at the 'front'</p>
<p>I am correct in saying 'the front' for we were as near to that business place of war as our particular work required us to be</p>
<p>Without giving details, which after all would not make pleasant reading nor be of much avail as the censor might object, I may at least be permitted to express myself in some measure and say I have experienced some of those rare and intense moments in which one really lives with the vital thrill of being.</p>
