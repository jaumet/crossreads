
11th August:- FridayAwoke late & having intended going to the doctor, as I feel right played out, was too late arriving to do so. Found plenty of work awaiting, as the casualties of 19th Batt have been very heavy & I am on this work. (380 wd in 8 days) Manage to pull through the day well & have lunch & tea at Soldier's Home as usual & a walk around town & again seeing the monument of Joan of Arc & site of her execution  Talk at home after our walk around the town & go to bed late  We received 15 francs each for expenses.
