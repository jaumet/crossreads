Rear Admiral Goodenough of the "Southampton" came on board and all the men fell in aft whilst the Admiral welcomed us as a unit of the Grand Fleet also as a unit of his squadron - the 2nd Light Cruiser Squadron, and after a long speech and promising us the front place in any future action, he departed.
