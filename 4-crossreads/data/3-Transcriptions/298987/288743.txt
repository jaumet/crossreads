<p>Sunday March 3rd 1918<br/>Same as yesterday</p>
<p>Monday 4th<br/>On the job for about 3 hours a dark wet night</p>
<p>Tuesday 5th<br/>Weather has cleared up again. All hands went out this evening did a bit &amp; got home about 10 PM</p>
<p>Wednesday 6th<br/>Went to the Divisional Baths this morning. Am now waiting until 9 PM as I am one of the second shift that goes out then. Have just got fresh orders do not go out untill 3 AM &amp; work untill daylight</p>
<p>Thursday 7th<br/>Same as yesterday. It seems to be a good time for work as things are quiet</p>
<p>Friday 8th<br/>Same as yesterday only did less work than usual. A little frost lately beautiful days. Pay was on hand Did not draw as I have most of last pay on hand</p>
<p>Saturday 9th<br/>Went out as usual. Just after starting some</p>
