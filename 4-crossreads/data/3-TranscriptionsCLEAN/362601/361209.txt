  
 25 May, Sunday 
 F. Up at 7. Brekkie, chat, music. I wake at 4[am] because of motors. Say Good Bye. Percy & Sis come to Wilton. Arrive home O.K. Miss Jacks O.K. Arrive home 3.30. read & lay down until Tea. I am off my tucker, very sad & lonely. Write & go to Post. Meet Cullman & Parry & come home quite cheery. Recd letter Esme. Hawker is found. 
 Wrote long letter & newest M.R.L. 
 26 May, Monday 
 F. I just miss fatigue & give anals party to miss it. I walk down town with Les Nett. He goes away to Glasgow bound for Home. Read most of the day. Go down to [indecipherable] with Billy, off my tucker & hear its Luvley when Lovlete we got. Picture & get "Beams". Recd letter from Jennie. 
 Meet Les Hamil & Craven in Pictures. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie & M.R.L.
