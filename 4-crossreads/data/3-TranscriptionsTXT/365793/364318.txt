
Aug 8 and now we are "home-ward bound" for the same  dear old Harbor. Practically our only danger now is from Submarines & by the morning of the 10th even that danger will be "nil" - because it is not until then that the destroyer leaves us. All the time - she is crossing & recrossing our bows, ever watching for the deadly periscope.
