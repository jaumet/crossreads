However we made a fuss &amp; stopped the train. About a battalion of Portuguese marched into the siding at Etaples while we were there. <p>The country we are now passing through - between Calais &amp; Belgium is very low, level &amp; marshy with plenty of old fashioned windmills like one sees in Dutch scenes. Apples &amp; pears grow well, there are large fields of silver beet hay &amp; lucerne. Hedges line all the roads which are good, and fields.
