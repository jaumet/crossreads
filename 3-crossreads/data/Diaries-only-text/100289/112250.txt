Wallis Arthur.
Saturday 12.5.17
Boys came in during the early hours of the morning reported having a rough trot. From all accounts its "no hon" where we have to work now. 58th Btn had a very successful hop over this morning, many Fritz prisoners wounded have passed through as well as 58 casualties. Tommies relieved 15th Brigade at night.
Sunday 13.5.17
