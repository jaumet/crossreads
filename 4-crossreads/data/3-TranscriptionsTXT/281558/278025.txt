
Friday Aug 17th
Monasterie : showery " reveille 6 am : parade for us : went for a walk down the stream & got some lettuce " hired a punt in afternoon & went down the canal to the junction : Morrie & self caught 16 eels after tea
Saturday Aug 18th
Monasterie : reveille 6.30 am : route march to Arneke & back : not too bad : back about 4 pm : bath after tea
Sunday Aug 19th
Monasterie : reveille 7 am : H. Com 9.30 : P.C. from Aunt Myra & some wedding cake from Jess & Harold : stroll after tea with Rock & Alf to aerodrome : flying has certainly be mastered & I would be delighted to go up in a plane
Monday Aug 20th
Monasterie : good day : reveille 6 am : we had to go on parade : fell in
