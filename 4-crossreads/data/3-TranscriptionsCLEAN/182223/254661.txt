
Monday 10 May 1915Sent 2 bottles of Champagne to Col Martin today in payment for bet made some time ago. saying that we should be away from Mena meaning Egypt by 9th May 1915Ride between 7 & 8 am.About 12 patients came in up to 6 p.m. today. Some forty patients bewteen 6 & 7 pmIn town for 1 hour at eight.
Tuesday 11 May 1915About the Hospital all day.Patients coming & going all day.Everything all right A good assistant in Captain Plant not lazy, or too Swell.
Wrote to F. Fletcher, Col Martin, & Rene Silk
Wednesday 12 May 1915
Ride before breakfast 
Posted letters F. Fletcher, Col Martin , Rene Silk. 
Not out during the day.  
Reading & writing, & attending to patients all the day
