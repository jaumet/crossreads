 [24 Sun] Fall of Bapaume Peronne. etc. Log hauling, 
 [25 Mon] Q.M.S. Ration waggon Enemy reach near Maricourt their original line of July 1916. 
 [26 Tues] Up before the Lieut Col. for overstaying leave  Fined 14 days pay 4.R.W. Sent to 2nd. D.A.C. HQ to relieve Dvr. Wigful. Took my two mules &amp; harness. MAIL Driver. 
 [27 Wed] 1st Parade 7am. rugs off &amp; fed mules. 2nd. parade 9am. Grooming 10am. Harnessed into single Pill box &amp; went to field P.O. in Steenwerck Rd. Took mail to 2nd. section. Back in time for dinner. Harness cleaning in the afternoon. Stable piquet on from 8 to 10
