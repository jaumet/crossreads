Two graves in the sand, one containing officers and the other men, held more than 700 bodies and testified to the difficulties overcome in gaining a foothold.<br/>We disembarked, marched along the Krithia road for some distance and then made dugouts in one of the fields.  Owing to the ground being practically waterlogged we could not make our shelters very deep.  We spent the remainder of the day principally in watching the French operations against Achi Baba.  At night we were nearly frozen, the air and ground was so cold.<br/>At 3 p.m. the following day the Brigade advanced to the attack.  We were told to carry our packs</p>
