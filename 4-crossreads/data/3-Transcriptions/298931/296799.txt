<p>Friday 6<br/>Good Bye Ypres entraining to night for France where we are going to spend a whole months holiday<br/>Saturday 7<br/>Last night was a fair buggar we got lost, marching for six hours, and then slept in the rain<br/>Sunday 8<br/>Living on a farm tis just like being at home, and I am sure that the stay will  be enjoyable<br/>Mary, nice girl, but today we move to another selection<br/>Denice a sweet little thing of 17 summers</p>
