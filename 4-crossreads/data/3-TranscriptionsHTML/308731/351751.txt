<p>[Note from Ruth's mother.]<br/>It is quite true about the fox &ndash; he came into that yard on left going towards Lovegroves &amp; must have got over the fence &ndash; I am too upset to do any thing 50 odd he has got in 2 or 3 months</p>
<p>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>22.8.1918</p>
<p>Dear Old Mr Jam-Tin.</p>
<p>Poor mum had the fox last - night and it has got 18 of mums fowls and she is in a state? she sent two of them down to Francis place I don't know if she is going to give some to Grandma or not.  We are staying at home because mum is going to have dinner at a place and has a little boy who allways likes Frank one Sunday George that is the little Boys name he said to his mother can I take some biscuits for Frankie and I and sit on the door-step and eat em.</p>
<p>Much love from<br/>Ruth.<br/>oxox etc.</p>
