 20 March 1915 Saturday 
 Sea continues choppy Weather cool w/- strong strong north wind Our company is paid 20 days at 1/- per day. Large Japanese steamer comes up astern of us about 2pm. We get news of the sinking of the Dresden. 
 1915 March 21 Sunday 5th in Lent 
 Arrive at Suez Make first aquaintance w/- The Bumboat men No leave or orders
