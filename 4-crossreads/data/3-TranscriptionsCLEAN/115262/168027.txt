
Monday 17 September 1917.
Biplanes coming to new Aerodrome in large numbers th today. All new machines too.
Duties:- Despatch rider for week.
It is said that the Aerodrome is being opened today.
3rd & 4th Brigade on the rising ground towards Marakeb start to move out to the other side of the "Waddy". 
Wrote a couple of letters.
