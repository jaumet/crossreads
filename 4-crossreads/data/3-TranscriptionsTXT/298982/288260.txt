 a fairly quiet day. 
 16th Mon QMS fatigue in morn 5 15 then on road fatigue from 8 am &ndash; 12 noon. our mtn guns gave the trench of enemy at head of valley a lively time all morn &amp; we saw some lovely hits. 
 One man flew in the air &amp; his clothing apparently in shreds after him, another was later deprived of his rifle which went away in rings into the gully. 
 The smell of the dead.
