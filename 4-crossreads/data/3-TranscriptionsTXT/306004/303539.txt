 28.9.16 I was fortunate enough to get a tent to myself here, I dine in with the R.S.M. Frost, &amp; have mats all over the floor, a table &amp; a bed in my own tent &amp; as I brought a blanket with me from Romani I should be comfortable. We had a decent trip across, &amp; as the day was cool it would have been good only we got a fair amount of dust; put in some hard work at stables this afternoon making some improvement on the horse lines. 
 29.9.16 Fri 29th Reveille at 6 oclock &amp; stables at 6.30 as usual The Regiment fell in at 9.30 on the horse lines &amp; put in till midday stables cleaning up
