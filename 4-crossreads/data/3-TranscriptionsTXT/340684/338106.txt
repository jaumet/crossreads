 393 New Zealand Hospital Walton on Thames 5.9.15 
 Dear Aunt Isabel Very many  thanks for asking me to stay at your house last weekend &ndash; I  enjoyed  my stay immensely, but sorry you weren't there &ndash; I have been  looking forward so much to meeting you and hope I shall have the opportunity before long &ndash; The specialist came up and examined my eye.
