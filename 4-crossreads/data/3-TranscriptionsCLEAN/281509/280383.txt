
9 June 1916Friday
No fatigues today for a wonder.  In morning we go for a route march to Canniers, a village about 3 miles down the road.  New church, single street, Pond. Rest beyond the village & retrace steps. In aft. retire to sandhills & loaf.  Bob getting tired of being separated from his mates.  After tea Tony & self go to Army P.O. & post Stan's letter, green envelope. Sergt. secures us a supply.Russians begin a big offensive about this date. Capture many Austrian prisoners.
