  
 12 - at that special spot. Mrs S Skirving is sending me packets of singlets and underpants for the Australian wounded - at intervals. If they are sent to private individuals they are more likely to arrive - at the destination or for the soldiers they were meant for. I hope Constance is well and strong - and was grateful for the news you gave me of her in your last letter. It all seems so strange and far away that peaceful adorable land of Australia.
