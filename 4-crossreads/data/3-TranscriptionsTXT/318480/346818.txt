 I have been in Englan [England] 4 months &amp; I have only had two letters from home, you don't how anxious I am to hear how you stand and how you got on with the harvest. 
 I haven't any more to say now. 
 Your loving Brother Frank Hassall 
 A Coy 17 Batt. 5 Brigade 15 Section No. 4 Platoon
