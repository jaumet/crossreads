<p>business &amp; got a chit to chain money, in Boulogne. That town looked dismal under a heavy rainstorm, &amp; the effects on the people of the late air raids, but I managed to get a reasonably fair time by eating.<br/>Visited a couple of <strike>the</strike> "maisons tolerees" in the Pare de St. Paul &amp; found them as bad as the worst of Marseilles which are in turn worse than in Cairo, always excepting where blacks are kept.<br/>A man must be very hardened not to feel the effect of the awful hollow laugh of the poor girls who live in these</p>
