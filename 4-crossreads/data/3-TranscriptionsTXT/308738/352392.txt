
Feb. 13 Saturday 1915Half day Parade for RegimentMajor Fuller & Col Cox out together in afternoon.I went to Cairo by 2. 30 PM train got tents paid balance.Met Mr. Victor Convas who took me around to see the sights. Back camp 11PMOrdered lime to be sent to Maadi Station Monday
Feb. 14 Sunday 1915Major Fuller & other Officers went to PyrimadsChurch ParadeGeneral Walker visitingMr Victor Convas visited campWent to church tonight with Jack Lister.
Feb. 15 Monday 1915Regiment foot drill all dayWhite washing stones 6/- worth lime received from Cairo
