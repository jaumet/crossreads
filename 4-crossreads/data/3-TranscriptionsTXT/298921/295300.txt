 191 6 5 December 7  Tuesday Court Martial still on &ndash; a good deal of "Malingering" takes place among the rotters while other men half dead but not paraded the Sick List to the Doctor  "Taube" missed our Pier by about 5 ft with a Bomb today, it only fizzled in the water &amp; the plane flew off &ndash; "Rum" issue now nearly every morning &amp; an extra blanket per man but we all have cold feet. Had to have a swim felt so dirty &ndash; I'm not more of a coward than the average man, but between the shrapnel bursting all around &amp; the cold &ndash; I did not waste any time &ndash; We scrub each with Sand.
