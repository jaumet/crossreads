 MONDAY, AUGUST 25, 1919. 
 Showery most of the day  had 29 
 points altogether. Started shearing 
 at breakfast time & had a good run 
 all day. Only four men turned 
 up but all are pretty good. Got 
 through 368 for the day. 
 Tickle lopped in  point  4D 
 & was round Cow paddock after. 
 put shorn sheep in Middle paddock
