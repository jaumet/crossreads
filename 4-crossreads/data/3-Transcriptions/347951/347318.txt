<p>27/1/17 Saw Doctor for bad cold sent to Hospital. Admitted 12 am Malarial Fever to spend two days at least</p>
<p>28/1/17 Hospital Quinine twice a day.</p>
<p>29-1 17 Lot of men leaving for Pretoria Hospital to day. Temperature 100 Sent to bed. dosed for fever still a rotten cold</p>
<p>30/1/17 Still in bed &ndash; allowed up in the afternoon met some Australian Ladies Visitors.</p>
<p>31-1-17. out of Bed but not allowed down to Town. Inspection of Hospital today</p>
<p>1-2.17. Train for Pretoria 11-20 am</p>
<p>Standerston 8-30 am 2/2/17<br/>Germiston Pretoria 2 pm<br/>Cantonments 3.30 pm (For ten days)</p>
<p>3rd 4th 5th 6th asked to be boarded promised to day or tomorrow</p>
<p>8th Particulars taken for Boa[rding]</p>
<p>9th 10th 11th 12th 13th 14th 15th To be Boarded practically 2 pm.</p>
<p>Home to JoBerg Saturday 17th Feb. 1917</p>
<p>Rejoined May 4th for G E Africa</p>

<p>[Transcriber's notes:</p>
<p>Bibi spelt BiBi<br/>Dar es Salaam variously spelt Daresaalam, Daresalam, Dar essalaam, D E Salaam, D E S etc<br/>German Commander Von Letto-Vorbeck spelt Von Letta-Vorobeck<br/>Iringa spelt Iranga<br/>Kilimanjaro sometimes spelt Kilimangaro<br/>Kilosa spelt Kilossa<br/>Mgeta spelt MGeta<br/>Mombasa spelt Mombassa<br/>Morogoro sometimes spelt Monogora or Morogora<br/>Mpwapwa spelt Mpapau or Mpapua<br/>Moshi spelt Moschi<br/>Rufiji River spelt Rufige<br/>Taveta spelt Tavita</p>
<p>Place names are often misspelt and not all place names mentioned are positively identified or confirmed<br/>There is frequent mention of friends Sive and Jimmy (or Jimsy/Jinny/Jinmy) Fraser but the spelling of the latter's first name is not clear.<br/>With regard to the diary itself:<br/>The writing is often difficult to read and some parts appear to have been overwritten, possibly at a later date. Entries are brief and sometimes cryptic and punctuation is erratic. ]</p>
