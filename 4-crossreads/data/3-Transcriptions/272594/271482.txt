<p>Bazentin up through Martinpuich, Le Sars &amp; so up to Baupaume, we pulled up about  a mile outside Bapaume at Div. Hqrs. left our officers here, &amp; I then took charge of the Bde &amp; with a guide brought the Bde just on 5 miles further to a place called Velu, we reached this just at dark &amp; the men were strung out for miles knocked right up, here I separated them &amp; sent them on to their different Battns, after I had finished I</p>
