<p>Tuesday 2 April<br/>Up at 6 a.m. breakfasted &amp; off for Victoria Stn.</p>
<p>Caught train for Dover. Marched to House billet, had dinner, marched to boat &amp; across to Calais. Marched to various camps until finally lodged.</p>
<p>Had tea late. Turned in early.</p>
