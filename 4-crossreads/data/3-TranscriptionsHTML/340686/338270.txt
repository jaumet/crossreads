<p>positions. Later this place was shelled by whizz-bangs but no shots were right in the camp and no damage apparently done. Last night George Clarke of No 3 cook came part of the way with me dead drunk but soon fell off the duckboards into a shell hole where he wallowed and made a perfect beast of himself. We left him behind in a convenient dugout.</p>
<p>26-12-16<br/>Out at midnight &amp; work as usual.</p>
<p>29-12-16<br/>Laid in bed all day slept &amp; wrote letters. Go to work to-night at 4-30. Set out and branched off outside Delville and at length after 1 &frac12; journey came to Needle Trench Communication where we started digging a shaft 5 x 3 for a machine</p>
