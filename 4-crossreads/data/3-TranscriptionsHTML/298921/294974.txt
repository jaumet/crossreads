June 1919<br/>Military engagements, wounds, distinctions received, etc.<br/>S.African war *2 years 2 mos, Kings Queens Medals 7 Bars &ndash;<br/>Mentioned in Despatches &ndash;<br/>Wounded bullet [indecipherable] head at<br/>Broukhurst Spruit Transvaal<br/>Promoted 1st Lieut:-</p>
<p>German War<br/>Lone Pine, wounded, returned to Peninsular in Sept 1915 remained till evacuation, was in charge of one portion of the Rear Party &ndash; Dec 20 ? 1915 (I think or was it Dec.22nd) Anyway there [indecipherable] the last as per Diary &ndash; Then on Western Frontier, till Canal was crossed wounded again at Romani, thence rejoined in time for Rafa Magdhaba [indecipherable] &amp; all fights up to occupation of Jerusalem, thence took part in raids on Essalt &amp; AmMon &amp; received the D.S.O. for counter attacks on Germans July 14 1918 at Mosselabeh</p>
