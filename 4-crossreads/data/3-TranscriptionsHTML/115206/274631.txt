<p>March 1918<br/>Saturday 2</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Received letter from Geo Lang--- who is in hospital in Blighty.<br/>Fritz strafed us with H.V.s all the morning. To be sure I had ze vind up. He dropped one in front of our Pill Box, scattering same with sand.<br/>Journeyed to P.gun for compass snowed and sleeted all the afternoon &ndash; bitterly cold.<br/>Garfoot trotted down to H.Q.for the pigeons, and we now have deux pigeons for company.<br/>Had a long letter from Roy.</p>
