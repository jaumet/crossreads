20.9.18Up at 7.30 and parade at 8.30 and spent day making windlass with Batten, Dola and Reid.  Good job well done.  In evening finished diaries, letters, etc. and expect pass in morning.  Bed at 10.30.
21.9.18Fairly fine.  Some rain during night.  No pass through all day and in morning went out to Roisel and salvaged timber flooring and 3 x 2.  Got some good mineral water.  Rested in afternoon and played bridge in evening.
22.9.18Fine.  Timber stunt again to Roisel & brought gear back to camp.  Read in intervals.  Batten a nuisance.  Only 4 went on leave this afternoon.
23.9.18, Monday.8.15 parade usual and only some odd jobs.  Spent rest of day playing cards and reading.
24.9.18Some odd jobs and usual Sappers came home late in evening.
25.9.18Washed wagons in morning and other part of section, loaded pontoons in aftn.  Drill order parade at 4 and inspection of gear for back areas.  May move at midnight but probably to-morrow.
26.9.18Breakfast at 8 and after cleaning up moved off about 9.30 to Tincourt railway Sta.  However had to wait till 12 before getting away and crowded in truck.  Moved
