<p>10 July 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Duty Company<br/>We have to find about a dozen guards   &amp;  fatigue parties<br/>14th leave during the night so as not to run any risk of shrapnel like the unfortunate 16th</p>
<p>1915 July 11<br/>Sunday<br/>6th After Trinity</p>
<p>Church parade this morning<br/>Turks make a rally  &amp;  make a devil of a row but do no damage<br/>The Monitor fires about a dozen large shells (10") over our heads during the afternoon.</p>
