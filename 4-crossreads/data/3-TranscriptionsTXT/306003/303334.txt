
1-5-16Mon 1stLes & McFarlane went into Minia today to see if they could draw money through the bank;  All passes have been stopped to Cairo & Sohag for a week;  It is rumored that a troop of the 9th L.H. was captured a week or so ago on the Sinai Peninsula,We seldom get papers here & are not too well posted with newsA swim & cards after tea
2-5-16Tue 2ndWe got orders from R.H.Q. to make preparations to move at short notice;  Abdul Moolah was here when I received the message & was very downhearted when he hear the news  I gave him 1 to get Sundays dinner ready for but cancelled the order;  The Officers fired away a good many rounds of gun cartridges this evening shooting birds
