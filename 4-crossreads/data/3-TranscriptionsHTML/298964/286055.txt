<p>14 Sun<br/>Very quiet</p>
<p>15 Mon<br/>3rd &amp; 4th Sqd team had a very funny match   4th won by 3  Arrival of beer very little sold  more water than hops</p>
<p>16 Tues<br/>Another Albatros brought down by Capt Steele  machine smashed to atoms pilot killed.  Plenty of fatigues  1st &amp; 2nd played 16 to nil</p>
<p>17 Wed<br/>Had a great night getting plenty from the Sergts  very dusty</p>
