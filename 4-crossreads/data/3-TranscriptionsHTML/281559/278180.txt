<p>Monday June 9th at Sea</p>
<p>Nothing much doing : band at night &amp; a dance</p>
<p>Tuesday June 10th at Sea</p>
<p>Quiet day : plenty of birds hanging about : dance at night</p>
<p>Wednesday June 11th at Sea</p>
<p>Quiet day : concert at night : I printed a lot of gaslight prints &amp; they turned out pretty fair  :</p>
<p>Thursday June 12th at Sea</p>
<p>Still a bit choppy : quiet time band at night with a very good programme</p>
<p>Friday June 13th at Sea</p>
<p>Quiet time : drizzly rain :</p>
