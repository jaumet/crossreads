
Friday 8/3/17.After Breakfast Land was sighted at about 10 am away on our Left Right & everyone was interested in making our next port. We continued along the Coast until about 12 noon when we altered our Course & our fleet formed into Single Line our Armed Cruiser Orcoma leading then came the Walmer Castle 2 then our Boat Militiades 3 then the Omrah 4 Waitani 5 - Qta. Ultimaroa 6 and the Auchices 7. Number 5 & 6 has all New Zealand Troops & the 3. 4. & 7 Australian Troops & No 2 has th English Troops returning from German East Africa also a large number of Civil passengers.
