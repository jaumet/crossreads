 28 March, Friday 
 F. but snow at intervals 
 Recd letter from Ellis. 
 I go to W.A.A.C.'s Dance in Sgts Mess. I play &amp; dance &amp; we have chocs, wine trifle &amp; fruit salad. At midnight it is snowing hard EW &amp; I are left to carry on alone. 
 Have bath &amp; clean up after dinner. 
 29 March, Saturday 
 F. 6" covering of snow &amp; much snow-balling in consequence. 
 Recd letter from Ellis &amp; Miss Scroggins. Promotions come out as 14.2.19. 
 Sit &amp; write until 8.30 pm &amp; then go to bed. D. &amp; D. come down at 9.30 while I am in bed &amp; jam until lights out. 
 Wrote M/R.L. 
 Sent Mag to M.R.L. &amp; S. Mail &amp; A.B. to Auntie. Wrote Ellis &amp; Miss Scroggins.
