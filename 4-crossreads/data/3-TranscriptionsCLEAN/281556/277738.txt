  
 30 h. Tuesday May Kit inspection. Letter writing etc. Truck fatigue at night 
 31st Wednesday Dull weather. Mail in & letter writing. Much airial activity. 
 June 1916 1st. Thursday. Lovely day. Fritz shelling our batteries nearby also Armontieres. Also got onto our fatigue party & caught several. 
 2nd. Friday. Nice day. letterwriting etc. Trenches on fatigue at night. 
 3rd. Saturday. Nice quiet day. Packed up belongings & relieved 19th. Battn in Support Trenches. Plenty of fatigue here, Engineering by day & ration
