  
 Sunday 20 October 1918 Still in bed & crook. 
 Monday 21 October 1918 My flu changes to pneumonia, at least I  think  it was today. 
 Tuesday 22 October 1918 Matey came up from Worthing. 
 Wednesday 23 October 1918 Gettg. rid of the pneumonia. 
 Thursday 24 October 1918 Getting better & sitting up & taking notice.  The Doc. looks quite happy!! 
 Friday 25 October 1918 Still in bed.  Rup called at C.P.R. but we could not get double berth yet.  Doc. says illness due to gas but don't believe him. 
 Saturday 26 October 1918 Got up today, feeling somewhat groggy.
