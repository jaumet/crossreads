We came to the village of Fouilloy , & my nose bled a bit, - strange to say, it bled this afternoon for a while too. He was putting over high explosive shells that were bursting to our left with an awful roar, & we noticed a strong smell of gas that burnt the nose & made us sneeze, so we put on our gas masks. We came out on the road by the Somme Canal, & halted where the cookers were, & had a hot meal., tea & stew. On reaching there we heard the rotten news that No15 platoon of our company were amongst the shells & Bartholomew was killed, Warne died later of wounds, & 5 others were wounded. It is cruel  luck after doing 7 days in the front line without mishap to be knocked going out for a spell. Tuesday 27 June After having our meal, we went on to the place near Daours where we are to rest, it is a nice spot among green trees & grass, & a river & ponds near.
