<p>[Page 22]</p>
<p>A man sells his wife or child as he likes.</p>
<p>The wife does not care.</p>
<p>The wife goes home to her own people if she is turned out.</p>
<p>A woman who wants to get away gives to him whatever he gave to her father mother or brothers for her.</p>
<p>The husband has no obligation to keep the woman.</p>
<p>While she is with him he feeds her&nbsp;&amp; keeps her in a hut. The huts are well raised from the gro<span style="line-height: 1.6;">und as protection against wild animals. The woman grows no corn the man does no work.</span></p>
