<p>June 25th<br/>"We drank the Libyan Sun to sleep<br/>We lit lamps which outburned Canopus <br/>Oh! My life in Egypt, Oh! The<br/>dalliance and the wit!<br/>The flattery and the strife!"<br/>"Oh!!!" indeed.<br/>Well, despite the monotony &amp; the general unpleasantness of war &amp; conditions I suppose some day I shall look back with pleasure on my life in Egypt.
