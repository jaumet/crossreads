 Thurs Apr 24th:- Got train to Warminster &amp; back to Sutton Veny Camp. 
 Fri Apr 25th:- Anzac Day commemoration service on the grounds of Green Hill House Sutton Veny. Addressed by Lt-Col James &amp; Fred Norwood. 
 Fri May 2nd 1919:- My n.m.e. orders have come along at last. Left No 7 Camp Sutton Veny &amp; went by the 1.36 pm train from Warminster via Reading to Paddington Station London. Starting my Motor Course. 
 Sat May 3rd:- March of the overseas troops through London. Fine weather &amp; I made one &ndash; of the spectators &ndash; up in the Strand near Australia House. Coliseum theatre at night. 
 Tues May 6th:- Started my motoring course at 10 Heddon St off Regent St Piccadilly. Know now why the boys call this stunt  bon  &ndash; Military  Enjoyment . 
 Wed May 7th:- Fixed up lodgings at 73 Belgrave Road near Victoria Stn.
