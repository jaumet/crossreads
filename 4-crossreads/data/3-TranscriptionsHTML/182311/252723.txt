<p>PETERS, CHARLES HAROLD&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp; ML MSS. 1887</p>
<p>&#39;SOME WAR RECORDS.&nbsp; From June 1916 to Aug. 1919&#39;.&nbsp; 1930.&nbsp; MS., typescript and carbon&nbsp; typescript</p>
<p>1v.</p>
<p>Captain Charles Harold Peters of Melbourne enlisted 21 July 1915, served with the 38th Battalion, A.I.F. in France where he was awarded the Military Cross and Bar.&nbsp; He also served as an instructor.&nbsp; He was discharged in Australia 19 Aug. 1919</p>
