<p>January, 1916<br/>Sunday 30<br/>Nothing to do<br/>Men returning to Ismalia march out<br/>After tea go to pictures</p>
<p>Monday 31<br/>In the morning shift out of tents into Mat huts, 3 men in a room<br/>Very nice &amp; private<br/>Catch 4.15 express to Alexandria arriving about 8 pm &amp; put up for the night at the Royal Hotel</p>
