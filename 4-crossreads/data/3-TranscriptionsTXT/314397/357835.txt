Friday. 18.10.18Left Belfast at 10am. The girl I caught yesterday came to see us off. Was a very nice & interesting trip. Arrived at Dublin at 1pm, got a cab & went straight round to the Royal Hibernian Hotel. After having a walk, had dinner & then went out to have a look round. Went into Sackville St. & saw all the damage the rebels had done in 1916. The buildings are only now being started to get rebuilt. Went to see JW Elvery & Co for a boy in France. From there we went to the station & got our tickets for Brecon etc. Got an Irish Jaunting Cart from there & did some shopping & went down to Guinness Brewery.
