<p>[14 Monday [Feb]Some of Casula &amp; Liverpool troops Mutinied.  No A.M.C. men in mutiny.  Trooper Keefe killed on Sydney station &amp; 5 more injured by bullets fired by Infantrymen from show ground.  A terrible lot of damage done in Liverpool. 3 hotels smashed up, &amp; all goods inside plundered.</p>
