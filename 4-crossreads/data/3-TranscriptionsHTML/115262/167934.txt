<p>Saturday 16 June 1917.</p>
<p>Still in Red Sea and a very hot day but breeze is fairly strong making all the difference.</p>
<p>A number of boats seen today&nbsp;Most of them going in direction of Aden.</p>
<p>No land yet today at 3.30 pm. Several shoals of &quot;dolfins&quot; seen right against boat. Mail steamer passed at 3 p.m.</p>
<p>Sickness on boat decreasing</p>
<p>Wireless news of last air raid on London published today. 397 killed &amp; 439 injured. (mostly women &amp; children.).</p>
