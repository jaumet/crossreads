
December 191624th    also a fair number of Tommies who are attached to us.The civil population here is not very big but we are well off for shops & estaminets.Being Christmas Eve we had a fairly merry time under the circumstances & got to bed about 11 P.M. (our usual bed time is about 6 or 7)25th   Christmas Day-   It has been fairly quiet but we are getting settled down in our new abode for a while.Reveille was blown at 7 a.m.  Breakfast at 8, & we fell in for parade at 9 a.m. but were dismissed immediately after the roll call.
