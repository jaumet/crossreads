 pipe, karki handkerchief, playing cards, cake of chocolate   &amp;  a Auxt. match-box. Then the Y.M.C.A. gave each one a little leather pocket book with envelopes, paper   &amp;  lead pencil. the sisters of the wards also gave them all a little present, so they did very well. 
 The patients here get a good time   &amp;  have lots of amusement   &amp;  invitations out to parties   &amp;  theatres. 
 I was helping in D &amp;  E Wards. They both looked very pretty, with Red, Pink   &amp;  White cherry blossoms. Shall tell you more about it when I return. 
 Plenty of Smokoes all day. An excellent dinner Roast Goose, cabbage, potatoes   &amp;  ham, cooked well &ndash; a lovely plum pudding with white sauce   &amp;  custard. Jellies   &amp;   tin fruits. A pint of Beer each. Sweets, apples nuts   &amp;  crackers. 
 Their tea was cold ham, beetroot, cakes   &amp;  jellies &ndash; bread   &amp;  tea.
