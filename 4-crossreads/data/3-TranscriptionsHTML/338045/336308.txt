<p>walk to Pont Corneille &amp; take tea in the Restaurant Patisserie in Rue de la Republique.  Return home &amp; to bed after a chat.  During the day we observed some Chinese who had arrived for munition work, some weeks previously in the native clothes, &amp; now in European dress, really smart, soon civilised.  Also some South African natives of the contingent for wharf work, who speak English well.</p>
<p>Expenses &ndash; 2 f. for tea.</p>
<p>2nd October &ndash; Monday<br/>Cold &amp; raining all the morning, but fine in the afternoon.  Some of us of A.I.F. Headqrs. (four) are taken</p>
