 Sunday 15 December Move this morning at 11 o'clock &amp; arrive at Solre St. Gery this afternoon. Solre St. Gery is just out of Beaumont &amp; is a very quaint old village. We move on again to-morrow at 8 a.m. The country is fine, undulating &amp; seems to be of great agricultural wealth &amp; well inhabited. We are now out of France &amp; in Belgium which is a different Belgium to Ypres!! 
 Monday 16 December Move again to-day to Gourdinne on a very circuitous route via Walcourt, which is a decent small town.  Wet. The country we are passing through is magnificent &amp; very fertile &amp; closely inhabited &amp; cultivated.
