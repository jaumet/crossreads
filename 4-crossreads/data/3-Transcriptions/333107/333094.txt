<p>Sauce.   Mild Indian.</p>
<p>45 Gallon Boiler<br/>26      "     Vinegar        3-2 Gall. Bkls<br/>19      "     Water<br/>Fill Bkls with mixture from Boiler.</p>
<p>27 lbs Onions  (Minced Fine)<br/>16  "   dates<br/>16  "   Salt<br/>16  "   Sugar<br/>Boil for 90 mins.<br/>1 lb 10 oz mustard<br/>6 ozs celery seed<br/>6 ozs Vermlion Ball.<br/>2 ozs Cayenne Pepper.</p>
<p>Boil &frac34; hour<br/>1 Gall Black Treacle.<br/>16 lbs Rice Scones or Flour<br/>(mix in Buckets)<br/>Boil for 30 mins<br/>Draw Fire. 60 Gallon Cask.</p>
<p>On Truck. Place near Boiler<br/>With Fine strainer over Top.</p>
<p>Gallon Can for Ladling.<br/>Let Stand over night.<br/>Next morning remove 1 Gal from Cask and Boil.<br/>Add preservative 2 ozs.<br/>Then replace in Cask and Stir.</p>
