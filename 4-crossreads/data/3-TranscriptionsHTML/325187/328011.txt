<p>[Monday 25 September 1916]</p>
<p>Went 160 miles per car with Mr &amp; Mrs Ferrar to the Giants Causeway- went via Coast road &amp; returned via Ballamena &amp; saw Sir Sam McCaughy's birthplace-<br/>Ireland's green is very restful after the Sinia Desert &amp; I enjoyed it all immensely.<br/><strike>so</strike> Had lunch  at Larne where the guns were landed by the Carsonites before the war- you remember the gun running- I'll make one to sign the Covenant against Home Rule in the North.</p>
