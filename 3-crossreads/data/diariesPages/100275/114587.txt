I finished this about 8 in the evening A lot of Scottish regiments passed through here this evening bound for Albert the Somme, they are fine fellows are the Jocks our chaps get on famously with them.
The boys are getting the beer wine alright in spite of all the orders forbidding it, I am sorry to say that a lot of them are making hogs of themselves.
A small mail came in this afternoon I got a couple of letters out of it very welcome they were too Saw Ferguson again tonight had a good long yarn to him he is a fine fellow, a mate of mine he is one of the old origionals
