
10th. Wednesday. May 1916Weather more fair, leave interfered with, promised next N.C.O. Orderly Sgt with Cpl. Simpson. Indexed photos sending Kitty from England also negs.
11th. ThursdayWeather unsettled. Bathing afternoon. Engineers Fatigue of 4 men 7.45pm. to 3am.
12th FridaySlept in till dinner time Bath afternoon. trenches, Relieved 19th 10pm.
