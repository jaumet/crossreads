<p>Small boat left at 6pm so one of the planters took us to the boat in his launch.<br/>4th Ashore again. English residents put their cars at our disposal and we were driven around the Cinnamon gardens and shown all the beauty spots of Colombo. After the drive we wee entertained at the Y.M.C.A. by the local Concert Party. There is a rumour that Austria is asking for an armistice. Our Congolese shopkeeper said today that if Austria was granted the armistice, Germany would crumble in a week, I wonder &ndash;<br/>Left Port at 11.30pm</p>
