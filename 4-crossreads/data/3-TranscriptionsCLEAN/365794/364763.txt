  
 Tuesday 25 December 1917 Such a bright sunny day & soft pink clouds at sunset. H.C. 6.30 a.m. in Recreation room & a fair amt. of pts. present, then long busy day feeding pts., etc.  Menu - turkey, veg., plum pudding, mince pies, etc., 12.30 p.m. then 4 p.m. cakes, tinned fruit & so forth.  Whist drives & singing in some wards. 6.40 p.m. visited No. 14 A.G.H.  Bright & gay. Australian flags everywhere, aeroplanes, models of boats, mottos, even a gunyah with Aboriginal sitting before his billy & little electric bulb hidden in old sticks. Pt. kiss sisters under miseltoe, etc. & general air of comradeship prevails throughout.  How different to our cold & sedate wards.
