
Thursday 26 August 1915 Col. Manifold lunched with me today. About hospital during the remainder of the day. Wild rumours from Galipoli A paper from Mrs Reynolds Catholic Press. 
Friday 27 August 1915 About hospital all day. All going well. 
Saturday 28 August 1915 At the Citadel during the morning. Visited the Indian hospital and saw the asphalt that is laid upon the sand as flooring for the tents. 
Sunday 29 August 1915 Took Sister Draper, Captain Cameron, & another officer to Matarieh this evening. Finished letters to Girls & Mollie. Dr Hollywood partook of tea with me.
