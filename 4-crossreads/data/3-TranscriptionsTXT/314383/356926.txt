They took a M.G with them &amp; soon we heard it in action, so pushed up the other one &amp; the rest of the men &amp; got into action against about 75 of the enemy, who galloped over the ridge to the left.  It was now dusk so I pulled back to Chal Kanain where we could get inside a house on the outskirts near water &amp; protect ourselves far easier.
