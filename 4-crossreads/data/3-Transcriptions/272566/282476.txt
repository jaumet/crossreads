<p>Friday, 2 February</p>
<p>Set off at 8 a.m. &ndash; packs being carried on a limber.  Arrived Bellevue Farm about midday.  Some of the 6th and 2nd Field Ambulances are here.  Our unit has not yet arrived from Vignacourt.  Met Bill Bailey.<br/>Heavy shells were thrown into Albert about 6 p.m.</p>
