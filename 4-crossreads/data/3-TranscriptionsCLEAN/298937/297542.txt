 Friday 10th March 
 Dr. Scrimgeour has gone away for the week-end. My ankle was sprained last Sunday & now is giving me trouble for I got about on it too much.  This afternoon the General (Gafrah Bashir) came into the hospital to have his arm dressed & Dr. Boutazy, he & I had quite an interesting conversation about Anglo-Turks-German affairs.
