 3 Dec 18 Went on long leave from Devonport & went to London. Spent just over a month in London including Xmas & New Year. Had a good time. This was the first Xmas I have spent ashore for 5 years, alltho' Xmas away from home is not much under any circumstances. Myself & mates had a good time on New Years night. All the soldiers & sailors Landgirls Waacs & Wrens took possession of the West end & we made some sport.
