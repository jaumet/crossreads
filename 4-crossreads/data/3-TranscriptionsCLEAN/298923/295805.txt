  
 19 May 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out) 2 Bottles of Beer per man at 4 PT = 1/-s bottle issue  Does the men a world of good & keeps them from drinking local Koniac. Church parade as usual X Letter from Dolly including Mothers to Mrs Isherwood which the latter had sent Dolly. X Yes we will both walk in at the garden gate before many months now I hope. X My pen sometimes works & other times I have to use pencil   Do hope you can read. X Painful trying to get men to sing at a Church Parade better cut Hymns out if no music -  X Stayed for communion-X
