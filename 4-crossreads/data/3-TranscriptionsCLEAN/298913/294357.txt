Opera square is the main part of Cairo & here are Ezbekia Gardens, a small park, with a bandstand & restaurant. The YMCA are here too & have a skating rink amongst their amusements. This place is always full of soldiers. A light meal can here be obtained for a few disasters. In Cairo however restaurants are few & far between & the food obtained there is questionable. The price of food anyhow is very high. Anything made with flour is very dear, & a few cakes soon eat up the "disasters", trains are very few & far between & travel well up to time & are fast.
