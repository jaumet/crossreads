Crash all around me. I thought my time had come, I looked out & I saw a Teut & Pole in pieces go up in the Air & then several cries of Help .Help from poor unfortunate Men injured. & shouts of Stretcher Bearers wanted here, about 20 Explosions took place in a space of about 2 minutes, Another Taube followed & he dropped his bombs but did no damage, No damage was done to the Eng or Trucks, & when the Taubes cleared out, I got down & saw two holes near the bank about 12 ft away, so it was close enough for me.
