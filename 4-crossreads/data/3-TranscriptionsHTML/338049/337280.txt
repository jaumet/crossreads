<p>Monday 11 March<br/>Called for first parade and on a bit late.</p>
<p>Out at 9.30 to lay a line to 11th Bde. Velinghem. Beautiful day so had a great job but cable ran out about 2 miles from end. Got home about 6.30 after a very pleasant days hard work. Evening disappeared before I knew I was home.</p>
