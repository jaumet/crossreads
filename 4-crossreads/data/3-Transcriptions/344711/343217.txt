<p>26th<br/>Ship rolling a lot.  Saw a big school of whales, spouting and splashing about.</p>
<p>28th<br/>Met Transport A47, with Australian troops on board on their way to the Front.  They gave us a great reception as they passed by very close.</p>
<p>29th<br/>Pay day.</p>
<p>31st<br/>Sunday.  Passed the island we saw when we were on our way over, 14/1/15 &ndash; Sea a bit rough.</p>
<p>November<br/>1st<br/>Sea very rough.  Raining all day with cooler weather.  Deep-sea sounding going on.</p>
<p>2nd<br/>Arrived Colombo at daybreak.  Saw the steamer Nubian aground near the entrance to the harbor.  Her skipper tried to come in at night without a pilot, and made a mess of things.  She is fast on shore.</p>
