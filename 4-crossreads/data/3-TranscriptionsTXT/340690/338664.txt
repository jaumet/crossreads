21.2.18Finished partitions with Rosewarne and nearly completed hut.  Some H.V. shells on Neuve Eglise Road.  Frosty and misty.
22.2.18Out on new Y.M.C.A. at Vierstraat.  Fiddled about on doors and roof till 11.30 &amp; came home.  Past Kemmel Straight road.  After dinner were to have our photos taken at 2.30 but parade dismissed as too wet and dull.  Wrote letters in evening and guard at 6.  Dola and White.  Very quiet.
23.2.18Quiet all day and wrote more letters and did sundry odd cleaning up jobs.  Off at 6 and played bridge in evening with Phillips &amp; Edmonds who came home in morning.
24.2.18Cleaned up in morning and wrote letters for rest of day.  Day mild.
25.2.18On to Vierstraat job and coverd kitchen roof and odd jobs.  Fairly fine with high wind.  Some surveyors gone out.
26.2.18Down to compound at Corps Farm and put up latrine.  Home at 4 and played bridge in evening.  Fine &amp; windy in aftn.  Frosty
27.2.18Fine slight frost, was to go out with Fraser at 9.30.
