<p>they carried me over hills &amp; hollows, round shell holes &amp; god knows what.  The shaking almost drove me crazy w/- pain, but could not complain.  It was very hard work even for 4 of them.  On reaching a dressing stn. the doctor had a look at it and bandaged it carefully &amp; sent me on to the motors about a mile further on.  The trip in the motor was even worse than the bearers, theirs was a slow swing, the motor was a succession of violent jerks which nearly shook the remainder of my life out.  After a long trip through Ypres we reached the C.C.S. in Poperinge.  I was immediately put to bed, my clothes taken away to be burnt, they were all covered in mud &amp; Blood.  That same afternoon</p>
