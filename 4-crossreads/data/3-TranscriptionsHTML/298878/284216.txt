A most extraordinary effect noticeable in the water at night is a phosphorescence which lights the sea up as though there were myriads of small lights underneath.<br/><u>Friday 5th March</u>  A haze which hung over the water since sunset yesterday cleared away as the sun rose, and presented a most extraordinary and beautiful sight; the sea was absolutely without a ripple and as smooth as glass, the rising sun a red ball, shining through the haze cast a golden tint over everything, and looked more like a painting than a reality, later the wind arose, the sky became overcast and the sea choppy, and the atmosphere decidedly cool.<br/><u>Saturday 6th March.</u>  Weather again cool and squally with a choppy sea.
