
July 1918Sunday 28
Ribemont. Bty PositionWeather still very moist. Worked on lines until midnight last night, and until midnight today.At 2.30pm went out to F.O.O. with Clive Western and mended a couple of breaks en route.Fritz shelled O.P. with 4.2 H.E. and some fairly nice 5.9s. Smithy received some letter from Marguerite and they were answered by myself and himself.Syncronised time was at 10.20 pm. no idea what time stunt commences.Smithy recd his photos. Heavies shooting gas.
