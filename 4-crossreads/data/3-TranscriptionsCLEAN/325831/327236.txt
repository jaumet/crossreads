 4. same troubles to contend with. 
 As January came on, severe frosts set in & the ground became frozen. Just now the place is clothed in frozen snow which has been there now, I think, for about twelve days. The cold has been rather intense but our fellows are fighting it very well under the circumstances. 
 At the same time they would give a lot to be back on the deserts of Egypt. We have had to take great care of their feet; certain precautions are taken every day in this matter & strict attention enforced. Of course we are having a fair amount of sickness - colds, rheumatism, bronchitis, sore feet &c. I myself had been sick for some weeks & I was finally sent off for a while to this place which is up in the fighting area & well within hostile shell fire
