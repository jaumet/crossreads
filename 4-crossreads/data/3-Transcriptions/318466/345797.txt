<p>Sunday 2 June to Wednesday 5 June 1918<br/>[No comments recorded on these days.]<br/>Thursday 6 June 1918<br/>Hospital sports.  Quite a good show.  London Police Band came along &amp; played for us.</p>
<p>Friday 7 June 1918<br/>Toddled down to Maidenhead for the weekend.  Stayed at the The Hermitage (Taplow side).</p>
<p>Saturday 8 June 1918<br/>On the river with the Tibbitts for tea.  The Candain [Canadian ?] Hospital at Cookham looks quite a comfy place.</p>
