We stayed there for eight days, then moved into the line, our Coy being in supports to the other three. <br/>Stayed in supports four days &amp; then went out on outpost on the bank of the river Lys. On outpost four days, then we were relieved by 20th &amp; came back to Pont-de-Nieppe. New Years Eve.  <br/>1918<br/>Jan 4th moved up to reserve trench; stayed there four days, then moved up to supports; stayed there four days, then moved up to supports again. On the 16th we were relieved by 20th &amp; came back to Le Rossignoll near Steenwerke. <br/>On the 20th I was sent to a Brigade N.C.O. School at Nieppe. 22nd the Bn moved into the line again. I left the School on the 27th &amp; joined the Bn at Neuve Eglise. 31st we entrained at Neuve Eglise for Lumbres, &amp; from there we marched to Coulomby. Feb 2nd the school re-assembled at Bayinghem. I stayed there till the 16th &amp; then rejoined Bn. On the 28th I got a trip to Boulogne</p>
