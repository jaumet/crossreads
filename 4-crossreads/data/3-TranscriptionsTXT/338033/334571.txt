At Kantara we had to wait for the pontoon bridge to swing open before we could proceed. Port Said was reached at midday on the 14th and just as we arrived, a British troopship left for the Persian Gulf carrying troops for Mesapotania. Owing to the excessive heat, we had to remain on board until dusk before landing and entraining, which we did in open cattle trucks.
