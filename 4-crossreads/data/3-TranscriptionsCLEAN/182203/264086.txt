Oh, it is a marvellous place but have promised ourselves to pay another visit - The jewels are magnificent & we had a most interesting day but as Annie T. said, she sat down & cried at feeling so tired because there is still such a lot to see. Sister Stapley & I left two other sisters, we could not stand any-longer - my legs ached for two days afterwards - the steps here are fearful going from one platform to another is quite enough. We two returned to Southall just in time for tea, then had a nice hot bath & got to bed, to write, but alas, Bonnily & Munro returned so our tongues wagged of the wonders we had seen until 11. p.m         Friday .
