 Went to see Comforts Fund rep. re issue of comforts. 
 Friday Funeral this afternoon.  Weather fine. 
 Saturday Meeting of Div. Chaplains re cemetry arrangements.  Weather dull &amp; cold. 
  Saturday  Sunday, Jan. 7th Church parade this morning was small about 80 men present.  Fatigue parties from both Battalions went out.  About 15 stayed to sacrament.  Mills came in during the afternoon.  He had tea with me and stayed all night.  We went to the Y.M.C.A. for service at 6.30.  It was a fine service.  Cutriss gave an address.  Lew came home with us after church &amp; we had supper &amp; discussed Methodist conferences &amp; Australian Methodism. 
 Monday Visited the Billets today of C &amp; D.  Most of the men were out near the trenches doing fatigue work stacking bricks from ruined buildings &amp; clearing old buildings out.
