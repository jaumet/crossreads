
Tuesday Dec4Raining today. My old Neddy looks picture of health owing to good spell & ample rations.Wednesday Dec 5Moving back to Richon Le Zion tomorrow for good spell out of line.Thursday Dec 6Sent on advance guard Reported  Col Cave 1ST LH.FA. Heavy rain & set in & spent miserable night in rain.Friday Dec 7Still raining heavily. The rainy season has started in Palestine & we understand it is on the cards we will be here until after Christmas. Saw Camel Corp & some of my old pals trekking back to Raffa
