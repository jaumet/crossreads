  
 Friday Jan 11. 1918. 1257 
 On parade this morning Mr Hunter told me that Bil Kiley and I were going on Blighty leave tomorrow. This afternoon we got our pass, warrant & my our ticket directions and an order for an advance of 10 from Pay Master Horseferry road. Tonight I packed my things, polished my boots & had a shave ready to go in the morning at 9.a.m. Saturday jan 12, 1918 Left the camp at 9 a.m and motored to Baileul 1258
