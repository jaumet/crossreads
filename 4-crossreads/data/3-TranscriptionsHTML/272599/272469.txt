<p>July Friday 20 1917</p>
<p>Sister Simpson takes command. 4th Div. Band gives open air concert this afternoon. A beautiful calm evening. Band first class &amp; gave us all a treat. Off at 5 p.m. Receive parcel from Mac. T. Jock also gets one from home. Supper in his ward. Write up "Australia in War" to send home.</p>
