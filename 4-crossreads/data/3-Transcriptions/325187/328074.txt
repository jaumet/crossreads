<p>[Monday 27 November 1916]</p>
<p>( Following paragraph is crossed out)<br/>Posted a letter to the wife from Salonika but would be no advantage posting to you so send these notes on Arrival at Alex about Thursday  next -  All dressed up in our Lifebelts again  better chance now that only 100 Officers are left on board with 2400 Tommies we had little chance.<br/>I am not sure <u>where</u> my Regiment is, but will soon find out-<br/>After 6 months have passed I shall try &amp; get leave or a trip to Australia somehow.</p>
