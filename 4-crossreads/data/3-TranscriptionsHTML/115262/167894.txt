<p>Monday 7 May 1917.</p>
<p>Up at 6&nbsp;a.m. Caught 7 pm [a.m.] troop train to Seymour. At camp at 11.30 a.m.</p>
<p>Very little doing but we were drilled in cold wind &amp; misty rain.</p>
<p>Expect to go on Thursday</p>
<p>Advance guard for&nbsp;Lt. Horse on A42 leave camp at 6.15 a.m.</p>
<p>A.M.C. (re-allotted men) about 30 arrive from No 5 &amp; No 11. A.G.H. at 9.30 p.m.</p>
