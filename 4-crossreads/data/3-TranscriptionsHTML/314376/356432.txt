<p>121<br/>attended after Church parade.</p>
<p>We held a concert in a place especially built or altered for the purpose close handy. Here all the "local" talent was given a chance to try their vocal and other abilities. The men enjoyed the performance well enough.</p>
<p>I was called in and rendered an item, Horace Horsewell, a friend of mine also sang. Horace had a good voice and was not afraid to use it.</p>
<p>One feature that I can well remember was a fall of snow which made the whole scene similar to Fairyland. The leaves of course fall off the trees in France, unlike our own trees, in winter and the snow being light remained</p>
