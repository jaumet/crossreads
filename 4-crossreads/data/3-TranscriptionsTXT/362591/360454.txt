 I almost forgot to acknowledge a tin of almonds &amp; raisons you sent.  An envelope was all ready addressed, just waiting to be filled in &amp; posted, but during a slight rumpus I mislaid the beastly thing.  It was probably burned in a hurry with some orders &amp; things, which must on no account be seen by our beloved foe.  Still that did not detract from the excellent quality of the goods.
