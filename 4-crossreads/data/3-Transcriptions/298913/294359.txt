<p>Fares are very cheap indeed &amp; in some cases run into a few millemes only. Cairo Railway station is very large considering the traffic</p>
<p>(27/2/16)  Take another trip, to the Zoo this time. The  Zoo is on the other side of the Nile &amp; is not very much to see. There are not the same amount; nor variation of animals as in Sydney, but in some respects, the outlay is superior. Soldiers get admission for one disaster but the public must pay 5 "disasters". An amusing sight there however is an Egyptian boys band of bagpipes, big drum &amp; kettle drum. The music is passable for bagpipes.</p>
