
Monday 20 September 1915 Letters posted to girls Wrote Watkins, Estell, Doyle, Manon  M. Knowles. Not out all day. 
Tuesday 21 September 1915 In Cairo this morning. 600 patients today. Wrote several letters 
Wednesday 22 September 1915 Examining patients all day today for Australia. Purdy & Campbell Smith here for lunch
