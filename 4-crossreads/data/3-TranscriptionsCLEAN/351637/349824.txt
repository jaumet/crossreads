 Saturday May 29th At sea in the "Ardroasan"     Sunday May 30th At sea in the "Ardrossan". The voyage was quite uneventful and we sighted no more of the enemy's boats. I passed the time writing accounts of recent events for the purpose of having them sent off from Malta, but I very much doubt if anything will be allowed to pass, about these disasters to the battleships.
