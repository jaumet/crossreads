<p>Memoranda<br/>Roy, my beloved brother paid me his first visit on 2-11-18<br/>Arrived in Blighty on 27/10/18<br/>Wrote a 6000 word "the life of a telephonist" and forwarded same to Cassell's Ltd London for publication on 10/11/18<br/>Commenced 14 days furlough on 26/11/18<br/>Ran up to Bristol on 30/11/18<br/>Returned up to Bristol on 30/11/18<br/>Ran up to Birmingham on 8/12/18<br/>Returned from furlough on 12/12/18<br/>Left England on H.S"Karoola" on 14/12/18<br/>Arrived.</p>
