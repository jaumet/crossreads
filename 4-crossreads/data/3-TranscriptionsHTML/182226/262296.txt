<p>Friday 1 October, 1915</p>
<p>Another glorious morning. We are truly in lucks way as far as the weather is concerned. At 7 a.m. there was a great cloud of mist over the completely hiding it.</p>
<p>Am on guard again.</p>
<p>Mr Wright came round &amp; was examining the gun position.</p>
<p>Mac went to see Capt Fitzgerald in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Things in general have been very quiet today. The artillery in particular has been very light.</p>
