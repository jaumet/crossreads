<p>Decembre 8<br/>Out again last night wiring. Fairly successful night. Coy mostly engaged wiring &amp; putting in strong points.</p>
<p>Decembre 9<br/>Nothing but wiring &amp; general defence work. Coming into wet weather again. Nothing of importance to note.</p>
