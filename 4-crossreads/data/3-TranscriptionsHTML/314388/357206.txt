<p>13 Feb.  Pay day Troop deck Picket</p>
<p>14 Feb.  On guard looking forward to Colombo</p>
<p>15 Feb.  Arrived Colombo midday sweltering heat.  Went ashore half past two went to Cinnamon Gardens, Buddist Temple, Fruit Markets around city in rickshaw, nightime went out to Grand Pass returned to ship about ten o'clock</p>
<p>Sunday  Finished coaling and taking water  left Colombo three o'clock very hot.</p>
