
April 24 Saturday 1915Tents pulled down & insides cleaned & disinfected in morning planting flowers & making up gardenAfternoon Half Holiday.Col Cox & Major Fuller went out with Brigadier Ryrie tonightClipping Horses throughout the regiment
April 25 Sunday 1915French Fete in Cairo [indecipherable] Gun 75  to Church Parade this morningAustralians landed at Anzac Cove.Major Fuller having his horse clippedWent to Service  tonight Rev. Robertson preached.Col Cox & Major Fuller went for trip up the Nile.
April 26 Monday 1915Mounted parade in morningAustralia mail closed 4 PM posted letters for Major Fuller & also to M & mother.The Australians fought like tigers & died like heroes. The stories of this maiden fight will be ever remembered by those of us who came later to reinforce.[ References to landing at Gallipoli are in darker print than rest of text showing that these references were added later. ]
