15 September 1916 FriSlept from 8 am till 4 pm. I then went down & had tea at the Belgium estaminet on the Ypres Rd. Received a letter from Dad was mother saying that his health is very uncertain & as much as saying he did not think he would ever see me again. 16 September 1916 SatSid Bragg caughtAnother day of sleepHad a fairly large number of wounded passed through last night. Close by adjoining the mill their is a fine piece of ground which is used by the boys for football. It seems hard to believe playing football within 2 1/2 miles of the front line of trenches. It is now 8 p.m. & the sky is lit up like day over the trenches.
