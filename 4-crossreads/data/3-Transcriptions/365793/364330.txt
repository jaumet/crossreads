<p>Aug 18 rigged Schooner on the Starboard Bow so cut in behind her &amp; left her to Port. She passed very close &amp; dipped the salute in passing. Counted 27 sails in all &amp; could distinguish several figures on deck<br/>(12 Noon) averaging 300 a day &amp; just 3,170 off Capetown.<br/>Every night the piano is in full request &amp; the voices of "the boys" float out over the Atlantic, and it is the <u>good old</u> songs they love the best of all.</p>
