  
 Friday 19 Good Bye for a while to the good old 10th Jackie the first man of that brigade to leave for the firing line Saturday 20 Aboard the Cunard liner S.S Ivernia, at Alexandria left T.E K last night at 7 minutes past 10 still going strong, feeling sleepy Sunday 21 Bound for France, left Alex. This morning, a destroyer on the port side with cruiser in the distance, Manly to the Mediteranean a long way I am getting nearer to this "ere" war" by jove - yes for we have to wear life belts, night and day
