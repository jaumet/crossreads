<p>1916 February 23<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Heavy rain breaks up parade this morning. due to thunder storm.  Gives the camp a good wash.  We spend the morning in tent writing letters &amp; general loaf.<br/>Rain again interferes with afternoon drill. Good job. Came down in torrents. Cleared about 4 pm.<br/>Go down to Fairfield for tea. returned to camp at 7.30 pm.<br/>Mrs Wheatly going to Orange for health trip.  Write letter to A.G. in YMCA.  Home to bed at 9.30 pm.</p>
