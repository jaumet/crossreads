Had tea at Y.M.C.A. in Esbekiah Gardens, then wound our way to station to catch train leaving at 7.15 pm.<br/>First news I received on returning, was to the effect that Captains North &amp; Donald had been transferred to the 7th Field Ambulance.  Their departure from the 8th Field Ambulance was keenly felt by all concerned.</p>
