<p>She took me out to see Alice Vernan but she wasn't home so we went back to town, had tea &amp; then rushed to the station (St Enoch's) to catch the train to Stranraer about 8.30 &amp; went straight to the hotel, had dinner &amp; went to bed. It is a fairly large sea port. Gee I love the scotch people &amp; their quaint accents. Can't understand some tho.</p>
<p>Thursday 17.10.18<br/>Left Stranraer at 8am &amp; arrived at Larne (Ireland) at 11.15. Was sick on the way. A transport was sunk by a submarine. Saw the whole show. The first explosion we thought was a gun firing but soon after the stern went down &amp; a little later the bow stood straight up &amp; then the boilers blew out &amp; the whole lot sunk.
