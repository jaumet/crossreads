Sultan Hassan's Mosque was built of stones stolen from the Pyramids & of is no mean size. The Citadel was once a fort & during the battle of the Pyramids by Napoleon, shots fired from the Citadel landed in the walls of Sultan Hassan's Mosque. The holes & the cannon balls may still be seen embedded in the walls of the Mosque. We intended to go through this Mosque but our financial position would not stand it.
