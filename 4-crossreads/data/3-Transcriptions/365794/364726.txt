<p>Sunday 18 November 1917</p>
<p>Another Sunday &amp; I am in "Maxwell".  All day our old familiar hymns sound along the corridors &amp; the Tommies troop in to church like the good boys they are.<br/>Matron enquired very kindly how I was &amp; seemed pleased to get "quite well" at last.</p>
