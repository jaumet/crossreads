<p>Saturday June 8th<br/>Lovely day again. Rode into Bethlehem to see some of the boys. Mail arrived.<br/>Sunday June 9<br/>Rode out towards Hebron on message to Wellington &amp; Canterbury Regs. Very windy day. Good news arrived from French front<br/>Monday June 10th<br/>Looking like rain today. Rode out to Hebron. Usual type of Palestine City.<br/>Tuesday June 11<br/>Fresh issue steel helmets issued to day Received mail &amp; papers from home. We were inspected today by Gen Clayton. This means an early move I suppose.</p>
