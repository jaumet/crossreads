Sunday 27.5.17
Church parade in church Army hut at 10 Am. Fritz again shelled the town caught a number of the 29th 30th Btn
Monday 28.5.17
Spend day in Camp as Orderly Corp. Went to pierrot show at night. Rained very hard while show was in progress as the building was in a very bad state of repair we were almost washed out.
Thursday 29.5.27
Leave granted to N.C.O 7 men to Amiens each day. Great competition among the boys in their endeavours to decorate the vicinity of their tents. Expect a visit from the G.O.C. Division any day.
