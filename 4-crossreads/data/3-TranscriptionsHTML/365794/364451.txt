<p>Monday 19 February 1917 [18 February 1918]<br/>Day the same as all others.  Pts. vary from 37 to 40 odd.  All wards quiet.<br/>My "black eye" has faded but arm is still massaged &amp; very stiff on waking.<br/>A tit bit from Alex [Alexandria] &ndash; 2 V.A.D's sitting on beach with 2 officers refused to retire 9 p.m. as desired &amp; finally swore at policeman, consequence, all dinner leave stopped for Sisters &amp; V.A.D's.</p>
