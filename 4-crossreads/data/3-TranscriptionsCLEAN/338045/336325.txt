
from the front, the French have again made a big advance on 3 mile front.  Austro-German offensive against the Rumanians stopped.  Expenses, Cigarettes 8c.  Cold night.  Write a few letters & turn in.  Bugles are always blowing here & "last post" sounds for miles around, for at least an hour.
14th Oct. - SaturdayReveille 5-30 a.m. & awake feeling cold & tired after an uncomfortable sleep.  (Two blankets.)  Still blowing hard & drizzling rain.  No parades & awaiting orders.
