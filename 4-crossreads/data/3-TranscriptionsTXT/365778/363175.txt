 Thursday 15/2/17 
 Left at 7 am &amp; went by car to FIESSELLES giving a lecture Div School, returned about 10 pm, very tired. 
 Friday 16/2/17 
 Visited front line Battn &amp; made preparations for taking over. 
 Saturday 17/2/17 
 Took over front line from 28th &ndash; one casualty &ndash; applied for leave of absence raining slightly. 
 Sunday 18/2/17 
 Wrote to Doff &ndash; Thaw set in &ndash; very muddy and wet. 
 Monday 19/2/17 
 Visited front line posts. Enemy machine guns very active.
