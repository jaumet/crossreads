Wednesday June 25th at Sea
Quiet time : arrived off Adelaide about midnight
Thursday June 26th Adelaide
Dr aboard about 10 am had our temperatures taken again : we have had them taken each morn since leaving Albany : drew in to the wharf about 2 pm : good crowd : got a few snaps : after our "S.A.s" had disembarked we were given leave till midnight : Gordon self went up to town it seems a decent city : met Gibs he took us round to his people's place we had an enjoyable couple of hours :
