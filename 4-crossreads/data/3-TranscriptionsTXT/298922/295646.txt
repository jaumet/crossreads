 Thursday 13 December 1917 
 Did practice shooting with our 6" gun astern &amp; also Howitzers at floating branches it is a novelty for P. &amp; O. passengers. Start to pack up tomorrow, I'm rather tired of this wandering &amp; living in "bags", expect I'll turn up at home some day. 
 About 12 passengers get off at Port Said some for Soudan &amp; other parts.
