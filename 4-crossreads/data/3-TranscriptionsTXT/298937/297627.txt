
Friday 28th July
Capt. Earle Page of Grafton came here & took charge this morning.He is a good sort & had quite a long chat with us.He has gone to Alexandria on a few hours leave so Dr. Van Epen has not left us altogether yet.Today we had a big riot in the Camp.All the 1st Division, 14th T.B. & some of ours & A.M.C. details are leaving & there was a lot of pay about.
