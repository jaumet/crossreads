<p>Sur-Somme.  We left our reserve position and marched back in artillery formation so as to minimise the danger of casualties from bombs or shell fire from the long range guns which the enemy is using to harass our communications though with practically no results whatever, as there are acres and acres of country in which the shells may drop without doing the slightest damage to anything or anybody.  After a hot march we reached some fields just behind Fouilloy.  French shelters were provided and <strike>we</strike> the battalion camped</p>
