<p>26 December 1918 Thursday<br/>Australasians arrived safely &amp; letters from Dolly- alls well!<br/>There is talk of the PRINCE coming out to review us- educating a man for a job I doubt if he will ever get-  &amp; our men are restless &amp; hard to handle at present, so many are only just joined up &amp;  are an irresponsible crowd.<br/>Paid all ranks today.  Altho' I cannot dance the recent steps I would like to have gone to  Xmas dance when I hear the Band playing.<br/>The wife is one of the <u>best</u> dancers I have seen &amp; everyone in Cairo wrote to say how pretty &amp; [indecipherable] she was</p>
