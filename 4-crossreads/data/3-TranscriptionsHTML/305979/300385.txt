<p>1916<br/>Oct. 5</p>
<p>Offered self at Victoria Barracks for third time. Accepted as fit except for teeth and referred to Major Donald Smith re same and passes as 'fit' by him.</p>
<p>Oct 6    Vaccinated</p>
<p>Oct 15  left by 7.25 P.M. Melbourne express and proceeded to Hobart.</p>
<p>Oct 20   Acted as 'best man' at Bob Williamson's marriage.</p>
<p>Oct 23   arrived home after a rough trip on the 'Morraki'</p>
<p>Oct 24   Examined in English 111 A</p>
<p>Oct 25   Examined in History 11</p>
<p>Oct 27   Examined in English 111 B</p>
