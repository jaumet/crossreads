<p>Fanurra<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>18,8,18</p>
<p>Dear old Dad.</p>
<p>Mum just went to church and we are staying at home.  And last night I dremter that I killed a magpie by hitting it dont yo think I was curel to think that.  We went to Sunday school this morning.  I have riten 45 words and Ruth has riten 81 words Ruth has ritter more then me and we got a little coury house</p>
<p>After we finnish we are going down to play.  We have rulled the side lines and we have rulled the top lines.  Only some of them are blotty only I could not. and on thirsday at school I had to write out I saw a ship a-sailing on the sea and Ruth is writing now. and bing is chasing flies. and bing I a good watch dog but blucher is lazy. and Mr Casles killed a fox in our bush the other day it was on fryday I think he killed it and yesterday wile we were going past they had it up on the wall drying and mum said I would like to see six of thos</p>
