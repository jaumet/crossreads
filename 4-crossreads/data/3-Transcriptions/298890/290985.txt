<p>11. Thursday<br/>Drizzle in morning &amp;  Dense fog in afternoon.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10.  Got home at six.</p>
<p>12. Friday<br/>Partly grey but warm &amp; sunny in afternoon.  Went to Miss Newcomb in morning &amp; to see Maud in afternoon.  Came home tired &amp; went to bed.</p>
<p>13. Saturday<br/>Dull &amp;  damp.  Was at Mrs. Newcomb's all day.</p>
