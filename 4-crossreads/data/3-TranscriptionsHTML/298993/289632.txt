<p>done. Orders to be ready to move out had tea 3.30PM packed up and left Khan yunis 4.30. trecked out beyond yel el jemie trecked till 12 o'clock rested for two hours had a snack got ready for action left camels 3.AM<br/>Thursday April 19.4.17 in enemy country we advanced smartly the turks artillery fire was very heavy the shrapnel burst mowed everything a tank in action it did not last long was soon put out of action we dug ourselves in and laid there all day the heat was frightful. I felt quite done saw one of our planes come down it seemed to go wrong and spin around like a top as it come down</p>
