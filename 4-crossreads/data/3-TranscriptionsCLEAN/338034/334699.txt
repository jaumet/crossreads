After a sleepy night march, daylight on the 9th inst found us once again in fertile country, about 5 miles South of Rafa & two miles from the Turkish entrenchments. our aeroplanes arriving soon after daylight reconnoitred all the surrounding country & soon after their reports being received by the Commanding General we  commenced to  deployed and encircled the Turkish positions which  were  commenced about 1 1/2 miles in from the coast.
