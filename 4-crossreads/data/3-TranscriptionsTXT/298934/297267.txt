 10th August Tuesday 1915 
 Treated very well indeed at Paddock House.  Excellent food (Matron &ndash; Miss Yoxall Sisters Higson, McKechnie (night sis.) Barlow, Wildblood, McGronin.  John Willie Bainbridge N.Z.R., Wilfred Kaye 'Dimple' Jim Bak Never-Never, Jock Rae, Addison, Mary, Whip, Bleazard, Dad Garton, Dunaclaes.  After leaving this hospital I spent some months in the Australian Hospital at Harefield during which time my arm was operated upon.  On 12th December 1915 I sailed from Portland on the "Star of England" for Melbourne.  We arrived here safely &amp; I was discharged as permanently unfit on 5/7/16.
