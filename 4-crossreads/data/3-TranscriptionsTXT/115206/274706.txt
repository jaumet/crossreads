
May 1918Thursday 16Bussy -la-DaoursWent for a swim during the morning.Weather very warm, with no breeze. Had a morning run to Fuilloy at 7 amAt 7 pm Fritz commenced the first aerial bombardment  since we have been in the village. We had a frisky time under the strain, a very big pool down by the Daours church greatly patronised  during the heat of the day.
