<p>turned out 2.30 had Breakfast  Packed up and moved out 4.AM trecked up to a place called Dieran have been here before  arrived 11.AM had dinner took camels to water 2.P.M very hot and dirty  all men sick tea 5.P.M turned in<br/>friday May 17.5.18 turned out 5.30 they are very regimental here fell in for roll call fed up had breakfast fell in 9 P.M rifle inspection issued with clothing and rations a treat to get Bread  rested till 5 tea  on line guard.  had Papers from home.<br/>Saturday May 18.5.18<br/>turned out 5.AM dissmissed from guard  camels inspected by G O.C. breakfast  inspection 8 dinner 12 rested all afternoon tea 5 turned in 7.30<br/>Sunday May 19.5.18</p>
