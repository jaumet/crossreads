<p>Sat 3rd<br/>Fell in twice this morning for inspection. Went for a walk around some of the villages in afternoon</p>
<p>Sunday 4<br/>Church parade went about 1 Kilo allong a very muddie road. A good little address.  Quite a good Searchlight display last night. Saw no Planes.</p>
<p>Monday 5th<br/>A great lot of messing about this morning to be inspected by Maj Gen Glascow</p>
