 52 Sunday 8-10-16. Bill &amp; Rodger called &amp; we went to Church. It was great, the Archbishop of Perth preached, Chaplain in Chief of the Aust. Forces. Had a cup of coffee at Juliette's place across the road. 
 10-10-16. Promoted to Acting Corporal 
 11-10-16 Had some great games of cards last night. Geo. Veitch came down &amp; the Skipper joined in &amp; with the W.O. made a four hander. 
 Things are very quiet on this front no excitement at all lately, not even mail to break the monotony. 
 Am a constant visitor across the road for coffee, they are very nice folk. The only lad has just turned 18 &amp; he left this morning to join the Army. His mother is very much cut up so is Juliette, he was a nice lad.
