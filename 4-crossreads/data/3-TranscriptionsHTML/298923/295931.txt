<p>22 September 1918 Sunday<br/>5600 prisoners 60 Guns including 56 Divisional Hd Qts &amp; going strong-<br/><u>NOON</u> Now 6000 &amp; 95 guns 60 Motor Lorrys etc etc:-<br/>Old BANJO Patterson came around our lines for a trip he is stationed in <strike>Moascar</strike> Remount Camp   I gave him particulars of my wounded horses to look them up &amp; keep an eye on.  He looks old but game still &amp; was much interested in the old original horses.  <strike>Mc L[indecipherable] is sick &amp; at Port Said</strike></p>
