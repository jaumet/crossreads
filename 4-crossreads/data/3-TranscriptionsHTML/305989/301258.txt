<p>Sunday 27 May 1917<br/>Church Parade in morning, did not go:  did fatigue instead.  News of a move back to Abassa el Kebir.  Wrote No. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 6 and 7].</p>
<p>Monday 28 May 1917<br/>Left Shelal at 8.30 arrived at Abasan-el-Kebir late, camped the night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 29 May 1917<br/>Early breakfast &amp; went through Khan Yunus to the beach for a swim.  Saw "Towser" Nevison arrived home late.  on picquet at night.</p>
<p>Wednesday 30 May 1917<br/>Went to Khan Yunus, changed our rifles for new Mark IV  very dusty day. got some canteen stuff.</p>
<p>Thursday 31 May 1917<br/>On pumping party, but had a quiet day.</p>
<p>Friday 1 June 1917<br/>Gordon Birkbeck changed with Yapper Brown to F.A.</p>
<p>Saturday 2 June 1917<br/>Quiet day:  nothing doing very dusty &amp; hot.</p>
