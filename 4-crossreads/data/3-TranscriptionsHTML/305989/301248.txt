<p>Sunday 18 March 1917<br/>Left Tilul early &amp; proceeded to Mazaar arriving at about 2.30, bought some stores.  Tommy buried killed in accident.</p>
<p>Monday 19 March 1917<br/>Left Mazaar at 8.30 and proceeded to Bardwell:  got into camp early.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20 March 1917<br/>Left Bardwell at 7.30 &amp; came on to El Arish:  did not come right on to the town but near "152".  Pitched tents in morning.</p>
<p>Wednesday 21 March 1917<br/>On fatigues all day.  Picquet at night.</p>
<p>Thursday 22 March 1917<br/>Squadron Canteen fixed up.  Renee's birthday.</p>
<p>Friday 23 March 1917<br/>Quiet day.  Went in for 2 swims during day.</p>
<p>Saturday 24 March 1917<br/>2nd Camel Corps came through &amp; camped quite close.</p>
