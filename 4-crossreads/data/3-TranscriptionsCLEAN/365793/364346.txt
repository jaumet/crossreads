
Spt 1 box of cigars for 10/-, & they are a tip top smoke. Patiently waiting for pay, expect to get 1 quidlet.Still plenty of birds keeping us Company, some of 'em only little uns too.Boat parade at 2 pm & of course I fell in on the wrong boat. Recd the large sum of ten bob.
2 Head wind & sea, & rather chilly. Paraded to the Doc & he gave me some pills of aloes. Played several games of deck quoits just to fill in the time like. Good cigars which I got in Capetown. Concert tonight, & better
