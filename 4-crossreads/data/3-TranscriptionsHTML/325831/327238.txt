<p>6.<br/>weather is particularly favourable to the artillery &ndash; the hard ground causes the shells to burst ever so much more effectively.</p>
<p>The mud, on the other hand, tends very much to limit the spread of a burst. Again the hard ground affords good opportunity for "stunts" in the line. We have just had a small advance down on the right of the line &amp; raiding had been pretty frequent.</p>
