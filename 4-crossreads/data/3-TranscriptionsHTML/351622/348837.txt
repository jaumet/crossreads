<p>2 June 1916 Fri<br/>Herbie returns from London under armed guard being 7 days overdue. Looking forward to the great day when I leave for London with Keith. After tea played cricket &amp; football till half past 7 &amp; then made my way up the old stairs to the domicile. Put the word on Mr. James for a new pair of trousers which were granted. Verdun</p>
