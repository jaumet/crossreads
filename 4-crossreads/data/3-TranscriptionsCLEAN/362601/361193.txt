  
 23 April, Wednesday 
 F. Livo & I go to village for walk. We develop photos & make a mess of them by fogging the lot. 
 I receive Parcel & note from M.R.L. I am anxious to write, I sit up till mid-night writing. I am continuously interrupted & I write all the things I did not want to write. 
 Wrote M.R.L. 
 24 April, Thursday 
 F. Recd letters from Dad, Laura, Nettie, Marion C. I go to B'ford & arrive back at 3 pm. Pack up for dear life. Go down to village to get washing. Recd letter from Mum. Rack up all things & get ready. 
 Sent Wire to auntie. 
 Sent parcels to Auntie, Uncle, Marg McKinnon, wrote Dave Lord, Dick, Jennie.
