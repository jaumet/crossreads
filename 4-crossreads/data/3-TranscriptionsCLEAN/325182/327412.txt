
28thWent down to Hqrs. again today and raised hell about my pay book.  It was produced after a short argument and I drew my money and left.  Had dinner at the Club again and again went to Hqrs. to receive my meat tickets and sugar ticket and God knows what.  I believe tea tickets are to be next on issue to the public.
