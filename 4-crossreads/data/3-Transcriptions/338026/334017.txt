<p><u>Pont Noyelles.</u></p>
<p>[9 Th]<br/>1pm. Went with Limber to the 27th Batt. for 12 boxes of bombs. Took them to the 25th Batt. Mounted Piquet 7 pm.</p>
<p>[10 Fri]<br/>Orders to pack up &amp; move out at 9-30 am  Arr. back to section at 12 noon. Camped about 1 Kilo from <u>Pont Noyelles.</u></p>
<p>[11 Sat]<br/>Went to Querreul for canteen stock. Sports at the section in the afternoon</p>
