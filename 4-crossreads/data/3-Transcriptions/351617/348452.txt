<p>About 7.30 George Williams said he was going up to see what was wrong and asked me if Id go with him. From Bussy-Les Dours we cut straight across the Somme in a bee-line for Corbie but half way ran into a gas cloud and had to don our our helmets when nearing the town we saw it was foolish to go straight in so we cut across the river again to the left until we hit a road</p>
