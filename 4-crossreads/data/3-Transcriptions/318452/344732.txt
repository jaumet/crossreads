<p>there has been little of interest We have been in the same camp all the week the weather has not been too settled It has been raining almost every day. I had a reading room built which has been well used. There has been no mail this week.</p>
<p>Tuesday July 31st<br/>We returned to Buire last Tuesday, on Monday I went to Amiens It is a very fine town the principal sight is the Cathedral. It presents a bold fa&ccedil;ade to the square &amp; is very picturesque with carvings &amp; statues around the entrance it is sandbagged to preserve it against Ariel Bombardment. Inside its high Gothic Arches are magnificent &amp; its stained glass windows comprise both very old &amp; also modern</p>
