<p>Sunday 15 April 1917.</p>
<p>Up at 8.30 a.m. Caught 11.5 train from Flinders St to Dandenong.</p>
<p>Spent till 4.15 pm at &quot;Elstre&quot;.</p>
<p>4.25 train to Lyndhurst.</p>
<p>6.10 pm train from Lyndhurst.</p>
<p>Till 8 train spent evening D&#39;nong</p>
<p>Back to Melbourne by 8 train.</p>
<p>Arrived Burnley 9.45 pm.</p>
<p>An enjoyable week-end.</p>
