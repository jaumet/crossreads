<p>14 June 1918<br/>Friday<br/>Nothing of much importance going. In the evening Sgt. Howlett &amp; I went to the pictures. Nothing else to excite one.</p>
<p>1918 June 15<br/>Saturday<br/>Have not been given any duties so far. Just walking about and having a good time generally.</p>
