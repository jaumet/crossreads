
F 8 SunRecd Cards from Fat Laura White. Lottie. Mrs Merrett. Letter from Bert & 2 from Home 30/9 & W.H. Smith. An Orderly Sgt. Major. Wrote to M.R.L. H Rayner. M Carter. White. Jennie. Laura. Claire. Lottie. Mrs Edmonds. Bertha
F 9 MonGo down Town to YMCA Concert. See man with Marionettes. Nearly catch a Waac Tommies riot at Honfleur & [indecipherable] Canteen & Messers etc.Wrote W.H. Smith & sent parcel to Aunt Annie.
F&W 10 Tues.Go down Town to YMCA hear R.G.A. Band play rotten tunes. Meet Cpl. McDonald. Harry Ch. Pal. We have heavy rain storm & then sunshine
W 11 WedGo shopping [indecipherable] buy nothing. Go up on Top Gallery listen to concert. Some good tunes. Meet YMCA cook & tell [indecipherable] anniversary [Christal?] Palace opening. 2 PKTS Bank [indecipherable] cigs. Meet A.P.M. Hard to get Home.
W 12 ThE & I meet awsome Girl & have lovely long chat. I go & see [Havianis??]  & have a good view of Home from [indecipherable]. Meet 4 amer. YMCA Girls with Holly [Benies?]. I am very tired walking about. Wrote Mother. Entertain home Book Seller.
W 13 FriGo down YMCA [indecipherable] & sentimental Tunes being [indecipherable]  & I am disgusted & rush Home. I go looking for shortages. We move into better quarters & we are now together. Wrote MRL. Recd 2 Letters & Parcel from M.
F14 SatRecd 2 letters from Home & from H. Rayner. 1 Card each from Mr E & Mr F. I do gardening in front of our Hut. Go down to YMCA & see Best Gym. Display [indecipherable]. Meet [Sandle?]  & Others. Catch 8 pm train Home but it is too Hot to write in mess. Play cards instead. I get a very lonely feeling hearing [indecipherable]  in staff mess Hut. Wrote Auntie Annie
