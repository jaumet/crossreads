<p>31 May 1918 Friday (Whole page crossed out)<br/>I am beginning to realise that as in all spheres of life a man without influence or money is looked upon as a man of "straw" &amp; of no consequence when Decorations go around.  I don't say I deserve any but I do say nearly all those who "pull the string" get them-   A whole "hide" &amp; peace will be sufficient  for my share I think.<br/>We will be at it again inside a fortnight I hear.  I <strike>hear</strike> wrote HLA last mail to let him know the horses are OK.  After all I'd sooner have a good wife &amp; man child than many Baubles.</p>
