  
 April 2nd Sabbath. Mass at 9am. Pleasure walk- Issue of two "respirators for combating gases" - Salmon sauce - Pears and Lime juice for lunch. Letters written during afternoon. April 3rd Sick parade Marching Orders- Preparation of baggage and Examination of kit- Day of Rest Generally. April 4th Leave Chateau with 7th Field- Join 7th Brigade- Depart from Morbecque.
