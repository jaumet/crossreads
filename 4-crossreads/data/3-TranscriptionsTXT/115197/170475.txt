Afternoon with Miss Doris at Mr 7 Mrs Harold Scales' Home.  Peggy & Phoebe, their pretty little children.
A heart to heart talk with my dear English Mother - "Elm Bank" and my great love for it!
A new Light!
Bed at midnight.
Feb. 17th
Arise at 4.30 a.m. - the parting.  Take 6.18 a.m. train from Victoria to Folkestones (?) where we embark at 9.30 a.m.
