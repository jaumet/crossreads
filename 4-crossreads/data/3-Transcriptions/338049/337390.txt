<p>Saturday 29 June<br/>Up at 5.30 am. &amp; plenty of work doing.</p>
<p>Finally stables moved out to Cosy but with B. Det. stopped behind with D.A. Moved horses to cookhouse site and set up camp thereabouts. Fodder ration small.</p>
<p>Arranged camp all day. Living separate from the others, cookhouse of our own.</p>
<p>All very good.</p>
