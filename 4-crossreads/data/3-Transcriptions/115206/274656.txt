<p>March 1918<br/>Wednesday 27</p>
<p>Hollebeke  Zero road. <br/>6am. Weather very dull and cold, <br/>Very quiet in this sector,  News came through that Fritz was in Albert and had very severe losses in getting there.<br/>Ardey Kolar died of wounds at 1st F.A. Kemmel at midday.<br/>Packed up kit and four of us were relieved by the 36th Imp B.A.C.[Brigade Ammunition Column]<br/>We journeyed to Daylight Corner, arriving about 9 pm. slept in hut used by [indecipherable] sub as mess room.</p>
