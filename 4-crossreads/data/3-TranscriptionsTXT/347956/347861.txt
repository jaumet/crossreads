 18-19.1.15 
 Mon 18th Signalling as usual Chasper Moore, Plushy &amp; Gwilliam overstayed leave last night &amp; were arrested in town;  to-day they were before the Colonel &amp; were sentenced to 7 days cells &amp; 10 days C.B. Lamp practice after tea, I bought some fruit from the canteen &amp; lost my purse shortly after but did not miss it till going to bed 
 Tue 19th Found my purse a few yards from the canteen door &amp; was lucky to get it as it was trampled into the sand were signalling in same old spot &amp; doing the same work.  had lamp practice again to-night.  Stan &amp; I stayed out after the others came in and were practicing on our own  Sergeant Mack sent to hospital at the Citadel
