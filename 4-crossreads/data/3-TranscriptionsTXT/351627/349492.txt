 319 6th A.L.H. Ma'adi Egypt 
 4th Aug 1915 
 My dear Aunty I received your welcome letter last mail, was very pleased to hear from you, I have received a good many letters from you I could not say how many, I am turning over a new leaf &amp; writing as many letters as possible while in camp. 
 I will only be here another few weeks, as I am going back to the Dardanelles again, I have quite recovered from my wound &amp; my hand is just as good as ever. I was in hospital nearly six weeks on account of septic poisoning setting in, also the tendon of my first finger was damaged &amp; the bone behind the knuckle penetrated but not broken.
