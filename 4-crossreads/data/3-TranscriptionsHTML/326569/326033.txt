<p>Friday, 30/3/17<br/>Light fall of snow during day.</p>
<p>Saturday, 31/3/17<br/>Very cold, only &frac12; day drill.  Did some sewing in afternoon.</p>
<p>Sunday April 1st, 1917<br/>Still very cold &ndash; no church parade for contacts &ndash; sent washing down to farm at back of hut.  About 3 p.m. went with Sowter over hill to south of camp.  Small flakes of snow falling on top of hill.  Splendid view of camps &amp; scenery right along the valley, including some villages.</p>
<p>Monday, 2/4/17<br/>About 6 inches snow fell during night.  Came through window thus leaving good coating snow on my blankets.  Drilled in hut during morning &amp; route</p>
