<p>nor is the tower destined to remain long, for but a few weeks after our entrance on this front, two or three of us are standing on a rise in front of our waggon lines watching Fritz shelling "Fleubaix", lobbing them all round the tower, when a few seconds later he lands one right on the old tower igniting the whole; a flame leaping feet in the air, burning for a few minutes when the whole falls to the ground amidst a shower of sparks &amp; mortar, leaving us standing watching across the fields but a few hundred yards away, the injustice &amp; damage done, &amp; a feeling of sadness followed by one of revengefulness passes through us that so noble</p>
