<p>23rd<br/>Fatigue in morning, &amp; had a swim.  Trench digging night.</p>
<p>24th<br/>Rested all day.</p>
<p>25th<br/>Rested in morning.  Digging in afternoon.  Lord Nelson came up and bombarded Kilid Bahr.</p>
<p>26th<br/>Trench digging in afternoon.</p>
<p>27th<br/>Quarter-master's fatigue.</p>
<p>28th<br/>Went into reserve trenches in support of the 5th, who made an attack on the right.</p>
<p>29th<br/>Trench digging at night, but the Turks opened out on us with machine-guns, &amp; we retired.</p>
<p>30th<br/>Shifted into reserve trenches on the right.  Colonel Cox returned.  Big rainstorm at night.</p>
<p>July<br/>1st<br/>Rested all day.</p>
<p>2nd<br/>Water fatigue.</p>
<p>3rd<br/>Firing line.</p>
