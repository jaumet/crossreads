 Sept Tuesday 18 Have been B.O.M today not a bad job but you are kept moveing 
 Wednesday 19 Did 15 minutes PT for the days work Rained most of the day. Concert in evening quite a good one. Payed 10/- 
 Thursday  20th As usual. The hut was very quiet last night considering it was pay day. 
 Thursday  21st As usual. Good concert in evening. Had 2 Aussie letters today 
 Saturday 22nd Went over the hill in front of the Camp as a start for the march did about 10
