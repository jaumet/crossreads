<p>1917 Saturday 9 June</p>
<p>Stretcher bearing today. Evacuations to Blighty. <br/>After tea go into Boulogne. Visit Eng. tea shop. long yarn to [blank]  <br/>Home by convoy of Fritz wounded  Some of mid-age boys making themselves nuisances, they are not worth their salt here or up the line  Got 2nd Field punishments meted out to them</p>
