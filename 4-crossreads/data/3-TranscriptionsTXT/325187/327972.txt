 [Thursday 17 August 1916] 
 Doing well &amp; sleeping well Long looked for letters have arrived  2/6/16 July 9 &amp; June 18 Mother Joe Day &amp; Laura Mrs Cotton &amp; English with enclosures-  also Mrs McKinnon's muffler &amp; Cap the Bandage she sent me I used on our Trumpeter Sergeant where he was shot. BHQ [BHA inserted] means Brigade Head Quarters . [Following paragraphs are crossed out] Tell Joe May can do just whatever she likes with Land I told her so-  Rash all went till it comes again. As you will have seen by subsequent letter, I omitted to cross out 2nd line in F.S. Portland I was  not  in Hospital before this. Glad pictures are put up &amp; safe
