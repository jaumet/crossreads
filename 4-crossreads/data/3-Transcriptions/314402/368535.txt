<p>REPORT NIGHT Sept 17/18 1915<br/>D Coy 12th BATT</p>
<p>Events of night<br/>7-8pm Quiet generally. Few bombs on LONE PINE</p>
<p>8pm-12 midnight Quiet, Desultory rifle fire. Intermitent bombing on LONE PINE.<br/>[Signed] PH Weston</p>
<p>12mid -4am Quiet Generally. Desultory Sniping.<br/>4am-8am Quiet</p>
<p>Ammunition used<br/>Casualties NIL<br/>Bombs used 5<br/>[signed] PH Weston</p>
