<p>11th  Had a good sleep &amp; a fine bed last night &amp; we were very tired, but a good wash fixed us up, had all our shortages taken  &amp; then went down to the sitting room attached to the "Sergts Mess" a Sergt over here is someone I can tell you &amp; they live like fighting cocks, &amp; their "lounge" is a bonzer room all plush &amp; leather seats &amp; lounges, beautiful carpets on the floor fine pictures on the walls plenty of small tables &amp; flowers &amp; regimental</p>
