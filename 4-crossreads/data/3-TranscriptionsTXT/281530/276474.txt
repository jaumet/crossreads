This failed to eventuate although later a prisoner who had lost himself said they had been pulled out of bed to do a counter attack. It never developed. Dawn broke with wreaths of grey mist hanging round the "wodongas" as one of our runners said. Waited until 6 a.m. and things then having resumed normality I transferred my H.Q. back to the rear of Marett Wood and turning in slept the sleep of one who has done a good job of work.
