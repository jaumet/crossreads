<p>May, 1917. Monday 14<br/>Day a bit rainy and cloudy. Check parade at 7 &amp; drill order at 9. No work and got my boots mended. In afternoon went near Tricourt for a good hot bath &amp; passed rest of time reading chronicles, and Motor Manual. Gramophone spring broken.</p>
