<p>August, 1915<br/>Wednesday 11<br/>[indecipherable] 1 top S.A. 1900 today <u>15 years ago shot in the head</u><br/>Here I am Ward 14 the same as Sister Wyse had &amp; train loads of wounded are<br/>arriving &ndash; I have cabled you "slightly" wounded flourishing &amp; should be out again in a months time &ndash; with luck.<br/>Some of the Reg &amp; Colonel came to see me latter much cut up &ndash;<br/>2 more train loads arrive Have a draining affair put on wound, will be Xrayed  tomorrow.<br/>Poor Tommy Redford was killed I hear.<br/>Every attention here, a Barber came &amp; shaved me for 2 P.T.</p>
