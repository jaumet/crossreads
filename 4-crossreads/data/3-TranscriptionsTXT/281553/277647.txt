 about 8 a.m.  Turks opened very fierce Artillery fire on Posts &ndash; Courtneys, Lone Pine, Walkers &amp; Pope's, particularly from 9 a.m. till about 10 a.m.  Our Guns also retaliated and things quietened off again.  No rifle fire whatever near our posts during bombardment.  Remainder of day very quiet. 
 5th Tuesday Bombardment continues toward south (Naval).  Mails in. 
 6th Wednesday Visited Chaplain on Beach &amp; had good bathe &amp; wash, getting back noon, felt the exertion very much, slept all afternoon. 
 7th Thursday Quiet during day.  Rally at Lone Pine
