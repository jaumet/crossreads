20thReveille 6.30.  Blkt. Inspectopm later.  Bkfst. Bacon &amp; Tea 8 a.m.  Tent pitching from 9 to midday.  Bread, jam &amp; cheese dinner.  Rifle inspection 2 p.m.  Cleaning up lines, stones &amp; other litter, &amp; trenching round tent, then off to well for wash.  Tea 5.30, Stew, fresh meat &amp; veg's.  Canteen after tea but nearly sold out, home 9 p.m., then turned in.  Gale of wind blowing all night.
21s TuesdayGale continues but changed to rain about 9 a.m. when wind dropped.  Bacon &amp; tea.  B. Coy. for duty.  On Guard till 4.30 p.m., Wednesday.
