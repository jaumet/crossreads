
11th Sept. 1918This is an unconscionable way in which to make use of a book given to us by a beneficent government for the express purpose of winning the war.  But I justify myself with three reasons.X. The Army should not provide a book so obviously made for a diary.B.  You cannot win a war on paper, which is what we seem to be attempting.Y.  I cannot get anything else suitable to use.
However, enough, to it.  A day or two ago while dozing in the early dawn I was woken up by a Turkish gun which started to shell the flat in front of us about 1/4 mile off.  It continued for about an hour
