<p>Orderly. Very busy night.<br/>April 25th<br/>ANZAC DAY "Good luck to all our comrades in Khaki". The fine day is well in keeping with anything Australian. Stretcher Bearing duties at College Green Trenches.<br/>Return to our base at Ft. Rompu at night.<br/>April 16th Detailed for one weeks hospital work. I take up my duties.</p>
