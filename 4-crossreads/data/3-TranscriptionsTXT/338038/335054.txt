 5 The Balutchistaus and the Punjaubis besides numerous troops of Jourkas. We drew up at Port Said on Wednesday morning the 2nd Decbr. This place at present is simply like a beehive, being the Headquarters of the troops operating in this part, besides having a harbour full of shipping, French &amp; British warships etc. The City looks rather attractive from the Harbour, but the native quarter &amp; those parts back from the water front are very squallid and dirty. A message came from the Orvieto that one Officer could have leave to go ashore for mails, and as I happened to be picked out, I put in a couple of hours seeing the sights.
