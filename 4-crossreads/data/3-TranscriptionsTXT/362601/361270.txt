
21 September, Sunday
Very dopey. Think I have got Jo. Pick peas. Go to Manly to Ellis' place. Meet Dave & Doris also Jeffes from 39 Gen. Catch 8 pm tram Home. Take Argyrol Treatment.
22 September, Monday
Go for treatment twice. Go to Inksdale via P'sham. No Tea. Go for stroll. Laura & I sit & talk also Bess.
Write Queenie.
