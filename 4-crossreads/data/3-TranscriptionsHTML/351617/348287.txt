<p>Next day we were up early &amp; all looking spick &amp; span as there looked a chance of being independent leave ashore for the day but the time went on &amp; no word came along so the boys decided to take things into their own hands. There were a number of coal &amp; water lighters moored to our boat &amp; some of these had been emptied. Before anyone could tell what was happening, the crowd had taken possession of these &amp; away we went ashore to have a good look at Colombo.</p>
