<p>Wednesday Aug. 15.8.17<br/>turned out 5.30 A.M. saddled up returned to camp and fed up.  had Breakfast cleaned up and went fishing never got a Bite.  <strike>reste</strike> Put camel in the cart and carted fodder.  had dinner 12.30 very hot day rested till 5.30 P.M. fed up had tea went fishing caught nothing turned in 10 P.M.</p>
<p>Thursday Aug. 16.8.17<br/><strike>Thursday Aug.</strike> turned out 5.30 fed up had Breakfast cleaned up went fishing got 6 Bites lost one hook.  cleaned saddle and had dinner 12.30 hot day.  rested till 5.30 P.M. fed up had tea.  Played cards turned</p>
