<p>Aug 28 A night of rolling, much wind &amp; cold as b-----s<br/>Tomorrow night we will reach Capetown. an extra blanket has been dished out so we wont rattle the timbers any more with our shivering. Pte Wilson is shortening my greatcoat. &ndash; which should look OK.<br/>No sign of any pay yet?</p>
<p>29 "Darwins Missing Link" "Chidley" &amp; sundry others! They're all on board this boat, in fact it's a panto' in itself. A fairly large whale appeared for a few seconds &amp; then sounded. Noticed several birds: - Albatross, stormy petrel &amp; Cape pigeon</p>
