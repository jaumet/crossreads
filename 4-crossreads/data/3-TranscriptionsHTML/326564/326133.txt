<p>14th. Reported at 7 A.M. this morning &amp; it was raining steadily it having set in during the night after a lot of messing about we got away at last, &amp; after a tiresome &amp; dreary voyage (for these military trains are absolute killers the way they eat the miles up is surprising I don't think) we reached Hazebrouck here an argument started for no one knew</p>
