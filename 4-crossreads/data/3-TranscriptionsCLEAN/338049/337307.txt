  
 Sunday 7 April Could not slept owing to the cold. Someone built fire & so we warmed at it & then went away for a few minutes sleep. Managed during whole of night about an hour. 
 Up at daylight and dug up Corp H.Q. Met Joe Smith, obtained location of Divn. & marched off immediately. Had couple eggs in a village. 
 Arrived Divn. about 8.30 am. Received mail gear etc etc. & carried on with work. Boys have been very busy. Walk with Hoppy. Good sleep.
