<p>Sunday 14 January 1917 [1918]<br/>No church but went to dinner 8.30 p.m. at Continental &amp; had a gay time.<br/>12 of us &amp; filled a round table.  All bright &amp; gay &amp; pretty roses &amp; colored liquer glasses made a note of color &amp; our 6 red capes &amp; the karki of the men must have made a bright spot.</p>
