In the darkness my only guide was the lights on the distant shipping and making straight for these I reached a gully down which a soldier took me to a field dressing station.  Here I remained till daybreak when feeling a little stronger, I managed to reach the next dressing station.  Here I was bandaged and also met a friend who had been shot through the thigh.  He was playing patience but stopped when I suggested trying to reach the beach.  A red cross man offered to help my chum so off we set for the Krithia road a quarter of a mile away.
