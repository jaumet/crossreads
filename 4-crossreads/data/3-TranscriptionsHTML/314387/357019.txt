<p>Tuesday 29th<br/>Cooks Fatigue all day.<br/>Wednesday 30th<br/>Fatigue all day went down to Bazentin on A.L.R. <strike>for</strike> went up on top of the Butte we Wartencourt loaded wire for Fremacourt finished &amp; night came back to Bapaume and shifted out into tents.<br/>Thursday 31st<br/>Usual drill morning and afternoon<br/>Friday June 1st<br/>Morning Route march afternoon bath.</p>
