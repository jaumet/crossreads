<p>1917  Tuesday 30 Jan.</p>
<p>Visit from Major Wilson, Capt. Hunt &amp; Lieut Neve. We are to be relieved this aft.  Bert Reynolds &amp; I cut who have to go. I lose, so we pack up &amp; report back to Edwards Post. Leaky dugout &amp; odious Flynn  <br/>Sergt. Cliff visits us.</p>
