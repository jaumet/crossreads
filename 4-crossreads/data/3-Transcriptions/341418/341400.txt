<p>for the freedom of Europe returned home not having heard a shot fired  &amp;  being kept out there a day extra in order to bring back one of our aeroplanes they discovered. This is one of the three we lost in the last raid on El Arish.<br/>It appears very much as if we are not going to be relieved here for another month according to the common report. How the horses will stick another month I don't know they are all terribly ticked up now: It means a lot of deaths if this is so. We have ten horses over in the 1st</p>
