<p>was <strike>where</strike> the southern Gibralter.  <strike>was</strike>  The first sight of habitation was a house or two near a signal station;  then a reservoir on the crest of some seemingly inaccessible hill;  a wireless station perched on the top of some beetling cliff.  Rounding the western point of the cliffs we beheld portion of Aden itself.  A few strange looking houses with red roofs beside a broad harbour sheltering several steamers &amp; dhows.  Far away to the north was the low long coast with its trees &amp; verdure in utter contrast to the volcanic outcrop we had just passed.</p>
