Met a lot of the boys "at Waterloo" & we returned together reaching Amesbury at 11 oclock that night, & by midnight I was in bed thoroughly tired out. 10th. Medical examination this morning after returning from the School, have not been on parade yet am fixing up my cubicle. Yesterday I met quite a number of chaps whom I knew, a couple of them were out of my old platoon one of them had his leg off. Saw Stewart this morning he went back to Australia just after Lone Pine" & has come back again he used to be in old H. Coy.
