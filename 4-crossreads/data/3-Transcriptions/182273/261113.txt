
<p>1918 &nbsp;&nbsp;March</p>
<p>21 THUR &nbsp;Fine day. Battery in</p>
<p>action on &amp; off day &amp;</p>
<p>night. Fritz very active</p>
<p>H.E. &amp; gas. Writing letters.</p>
<p>Posted one to W. Williams</p>
<p>Quiet night.</p>
<p>22 FRI &nbsp;Fine day. Quiet. Posted</p>
<p>letter to Amy &amp; encs. M<sup><u>rs</u></sup> Mooney</p>
<p>Amy Mc &amp; Celia Halsall &amp; letter to Julie.</p>
<p>Quiet night.</p>
<p>23 SAT &nbsp;Fine day. feeling very</p>
<p>sick from gas. stayed</p>
<p>in bed all day.</p>
<p>Very quiet. Heard about big german</p>
<p>offensive on Somme.</p>
<p>Leave to Blighty stopped</p>
<p>boys sent back from</p>
<p>Calais.</p>
