<p>Sunday 28 January 1917<br/>Took horses down to "Kilo 155" for reinforcements but missed them.</p>
<p>Monday 29 January 1917<br/>No. 1 Sectn. on shooting practice firing a belt of ammunition.</p>
<p>Tuesday 30 January 1917<br/>On a muster parade to 155 for "Delousing", pretty chilly while clothes were being fumigated.</p>
<p>Wednesday 31 January 1917<br/>Foggy in morning.  Two Taubes over:  dropped several bombs.</p>
<p>Thursday 1 February 1917<br/>Quiet day:  saw Guth &amp; got my disk made.  Outpost at night.</p>
<p>Friday 2 February 1917<br/>Windy, dusty day.</p>
<p>Saturday 3 February 1917<br/>Found a Turko mine on the coast, pulled it in &amp; put a guard round it.</p>
