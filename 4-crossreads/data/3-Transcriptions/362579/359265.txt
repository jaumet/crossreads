<p>the soil is fearfully hard &ndash; The ink is in lumps even   &amp;  our pens have to hold over heat before one can write.</p>
<p>Of course water is very scarce. Every drop has to be melted. You never could imagine this cold. My poor feet are suffering   <u>some</u>  felt about  <u>100</u>  yesterday but am slightly better today.</p>
<p>We have just evacuated so must scribble a few lines to catch the mail.</p>
