 May 30th 12 miles, thro pass evidence of Russians being massacred in that we passed three motor vans overturned on the road.  Supposed to be dangerous country, so far so good.  Plenty of food.  A wagon pulled into the village this evening with 4 horses abreast &amp; outfits like the Russian conveyances one sees on pictures.  Feeling pretty fit. 
 May 31st Camped at a village called Abba Garm, the native for boiling water.  It is really a wonderful place.  The road leads thro a big valley with the village on the right of the road.
