<p>23rd cont. Airoplanes take advantage of fine weather "Mad Major" teaches the Germans (1) how to fly (2) how to use a machine gun in flight and (3) that British pluck and daring is unparalleled.<br/>April 24th Easter Monday Germans still busy shelling. No1 squad duties, badly wounded Australians, popularity and coolness of our officers in dealing with cases. Our FIRST BIG BOMBARDMENT<br/>Motor ambulance run to Fort Rompu as Waggon</p>
