
28.12.15Tue 28th.Had no parades this morning but looked up the Sig. gear & found the pannier just as I left it in the transport last May, gave Arthur a visit before dinner; After dinner there was a muster parade & the Regt was reformed; 2 Sqd is cut out of the Regt. as the 12th L.H. are to be reformed; Ally Hargreaves is now in D Troop C Sqd; Andy Butter & Mc Farlane  & Cole turned up this evening they have been away sick McFarlane said he saw Stan in Malta & he was then knocking about on crutches; Having finished tea this evening I took a walk down to the billiard room had a game & a chat & then came back & turned in early
