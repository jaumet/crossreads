<p>Dad moves His possie. He sleeps so well that He does not hear S &amp; I come in (because we don't come Home at all). Dick &amp; I go to Zoo &amp; Bot. Gardens. Meet Harry &amp; Mrs Hey. I go to P's with two Nurses after meeting Mr Lord in Macquarie St. I play piano until Girls go to sleep.</p>
<p>26 October, Sunday</p>
<p>Have stayed at P's. Get up awaked by Jackass &amp; Kid. Go walk Brekkie etc. 45 minutes along the Forest Rd, sweet smelling rose to Beulah. Dinner &amp; catch 2.30. I come Home to bed. Mum &amp; I go to Ellis' place after tea. Go down to Band. Last tram Home.</p>
