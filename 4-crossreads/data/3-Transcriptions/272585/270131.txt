<p>Jan 19 1918<br/>F2 Ward 14<br/>Kitchener Hospital <br/>Brighton Eng</p>
<p>Dear [A]linda</p>

I was very badly gassed in action on Nov 17 behind passhendaele ridge while firing on 18 pounders Batteries which were holding our front line on that date but I would not give in until the battery (the 15) was relieved 3 days after and after after dragging</p>
