<p>Thursday 17 June 1915<br />Busy all day. Patients in and out. &nbsp;Examining those to be sent to Australia.&nbsp;<br />Not so hot today.</p>
<p>Friday 18&nbsp;June 1915<br />Dr Hollywood of W. Maitland brought a parcel &amp; letters from Mollie for me.&nbsp;<br />Dinner,&nbsp;at 10-30 pm. at Borrills Cafe at Heliopolis &ndash; Hollywood, Garde, Leary, Plant &amp; others&nbsp;</p>
<p>Saturday 19&nbsp;June 1915<br />Heaps of patients today&nbsp;<br />Over 350 in hospital&nbsp;<br />Letters from Macquarie&nbsp;St, Maitland, &amp; other places. Dates Febry &amp; up to 18<sup>th</sup> May</p>
<p>Sunday 20&nbsp;June 1915<br />Mass &nbsp;at Basilica (Byzantine Church) at 9-30 a.m. Working hard all day.&nbsp;<br />Had dinner with Dr Schuber at the Chalet Mena at 7.30 pm.</p>
