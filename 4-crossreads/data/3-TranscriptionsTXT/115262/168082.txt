 Sunday 11 November 1917. 
 On again till 9 am.&nbsp;Halted 3 to 4 hrs. &amp; then on again in direction of Gaza &amp; coast. An old mail somehow arrived. Letter from Arthur Thompson just in Egypt. 
 Travelled through till midnight with one stop for 20 mins. and numerous halts  for 5  when passing through villages when it was done in single file.  Line of  Country looking more settled &amp; civilized. Orchards here &amp; there, owing to war have been neglected.
