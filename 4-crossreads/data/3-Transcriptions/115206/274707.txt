<p>May 1918<br/>Friday 17</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash;la-Daours<br/>At 3 am Fritz came across in full swing and unloaded some tens into the district.<br/>Weather very warm but a little cooler than yesterday.<br/>Mounted parade at 9.30 am. Chestnut getting better very rapidly &ndash; will soon be fit to ride.<br/>Rode across to La Neville at 10.30 pm.<br/>Have received no mail for quite a considerable time. Fritz shelled Daours.</p>
