 28 early in morning. My friend A R Tait still Missing. &ndash; All pleased to read of great victory in France. 29 Dull &ndash; cool weather. Just like a cloudy winters morning in Australia. Reading Lord Lyttons works &amp; doing a little short-hand. Rumours of fighting  Gallipoli 30 Had 5 fillings put in on Tuesday in 45 min's. Only small holes mostly &ndash; Excellent appetite of late. Another recurrence dysentery. D&mdash;it. Dont think I will return to Office Oct 1 Shower makes one feel tip top. Must write Eric. Number chaps leaving for Alexandria. Dys gone. 2 Some wounded came in last night. Heard Narrows were rushed.
