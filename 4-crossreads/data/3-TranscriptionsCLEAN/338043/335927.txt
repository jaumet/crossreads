
July - 1918
July 22ndReveille 6 a.m. - Load Motor Lorries and accompanied by the 27th Battalion Band we leave Bertangles for the Old French Town of Rouen - Having Australian Drivers on the Motors can you wonder that we travel at a reckless pace through country ad village on a wild endeavour to prove each Lorry the fastest - Beautiful Landscapes and Farms that are very rich in Crops - Rest at Neufchatel - Lunch - Journey resumed - German prisoners working on the roads -
