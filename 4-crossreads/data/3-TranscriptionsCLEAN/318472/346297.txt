  
 [Transcriber's note:  Sergeant Ernest Murray of Nowra, N.S.W., served in the 1st Field Company Engineers.  This is Sergeant Murray's sixth diary and begins when he was on furlough in Scotland. He returns to his camp in Hurdcott - Wiltshire. He is transferred to Brightlingsea - Essex. Left Bristol aboard the 'Boonah' bound for Australia.]
