<p>22.3.16<br/>Wed 22nd<br/>Major Smith had to go to Tukh-El-Khail today to attend a court martial &amp; brought my pen back with him  Had a very quiet day in camp;<br/>Horse troughs were erected today by the river to water the horses at;  Capt. Stucky came with the reinforcements last night &amp; is to take charge of H Troop.</p>
<p>The canteen caught fire about 8 oclock tonight &amp; the roof which was made of corn stalks was burned. the rest of the building is made of mud bricks, several of the lads raider the canteen a little later &amp; got away with about 30 bottles of beer;  The natives went to the Major but the beer could not be found;  Received a message from Gen. Head Quarters at Minia tonight to try &amp; get in touch with the Signal Stn at Beni-Suef at 10 oclock tomorrow morning;  Beni Suef is about 40 miles N.E. of us, &amp; we are to use the helio.</p>
