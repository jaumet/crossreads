<p>November 1917<br/>Tuesday 13<br/>Dodging shells today up and down a deep wadi. No water or rations.<br/>Left for Arak at 1.30 pm to go to water and rations. Fed up and came back to a wadi, concentrated and took up outpost, left of Summeil. Turcos retired.<br/>Horses and men getting knocked up. Very little fodder and only 1 drink a day for horses. Men on short rations. My hands very bad septic poisoning.</p>
<p>Wednesday 14<br/>Advancing<br/>Fires of dumps burning in distance fine view of country. Buildings getting more modern. 2 story red tile roofs. Troops moving up on our left. Very quiet.<br/>Did outpost at night. Our Regt lost. Own troops away out.<br/>Went to Duras for water. Modern village while people speak English tiled roofs. 2 oil pumps at well.<br/>Pinched sheep , cut it up and carried it back on saddles.</p>
