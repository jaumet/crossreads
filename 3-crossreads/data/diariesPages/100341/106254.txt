18th Saturday, December
Day very dull, heavy cloud, fire still fierce. Water bottles filled midday. Iron rations afternoon. Paraded 4 p.m. ready for Beach. Moved off at dusk.
Continued on loose leaves (18/12 to 6/1/17) thence to Back of Page with 22nd May's records.
By 618 B Coy, 17th Bn.
Donald MacDonald
If found return to above or ?Avondale?, Victoria Parade, Manly, N.S.W., Australia.
