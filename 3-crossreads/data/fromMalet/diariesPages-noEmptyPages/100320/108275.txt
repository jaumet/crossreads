
4/12/18 Nice
Another fine day but inclined to be hazy and here is how I loafed it away. Stayed in bed till 9 and then had chocolate and a roll before going up the Avenue de la Gare to buy a local paper. Returned to Promenade des Anglais there to bask in the sun and read the latest news till lunch. Watched the crowd passing and repassing and then went to the Hotel Westminster for lunch.
