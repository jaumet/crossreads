  
 Tuesday 6th  Feb.  Great activity in the air today several encounters taking place. In one which I watched the Hun was forced to descend in his own lines. Went for a ride during the afternoon about 15 miles. Received by this mail Mums photo which gave me a great shock. It looks like a person years older & much saddened by great worry. 'Tis really a good photo but I cannot get the sad look out of my mind, I only hope the war will end soon that I can get home & try & alter it. Dads photo was just what I expected, practically no difference at all. 
 Wednesday 7th  Feb.  Had a few cases through otherwise very quiet. Strong wind & bitterly cold all day, managed to write a few letters.
