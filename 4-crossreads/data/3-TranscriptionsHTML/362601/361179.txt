<p>26 March, Wednesday</p>
<p>F. Lovely Day</p>
<p>I referee Hockey Match. Ellis goes away by 2 pm Train. I go to English Class &amp; then to office to write &amp; study, get to bed at lights out.</p>
<p>Wrote Home to dad.</p>
<p>27 March, Thursday</p>
<p>F. but very windy. End of Period.</p>
<p>I go to B-Keeping Class &amp; English Literature Class.</p>
<p>Wrote Ida Dean, Dulce, Bert, Esme, Lottie &amp; Bert Cocks.</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L. &amp; sent Books.</p>
