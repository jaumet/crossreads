<p>Heavy fall of snow.</p>
<p>Arrive Marchienne-au-Pont - then to Charleroi.&nbsp; Necessary demobilisation cards <s>returne</s> obtained.</p>
<p>Returned to Mont-sur-Marchienne for the evening and night.</p>
<p><u>Mar. 31st</u></p>
<p>Reveillez 5.30 a.m.&nbsp; Early breakfast.&nbsp; Tearful farewell.&nbsp; Leave Monsieur &amp; Madame whose generosity &amp; kindness will always bring pleasant memories of Belgium - Charleroi - Speech by General.&nbsp; Our quota &quot;No 23&quot; (1,000 strong Infantry &amp; Details 2nd Div).</p>
