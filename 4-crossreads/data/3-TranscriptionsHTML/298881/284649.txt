<p>Swanage 10.9.17<br/>Arrived here yesterday in company of two of my Australian girl friends from Hendon.  Had a bon sleep this morning and rose at 7.30.  Had breakfast with the ladies at 8, went for a stroll along the sea front at 9 and afterwards took a carriage to Studland.  It was a very pleasant drive through valleys bounded on either side by high and beautiful green hills; past patches of trees with their queer twisted branches and smooth trunks under which the bracken lay thick in pretty shades of gold and green,</p>
