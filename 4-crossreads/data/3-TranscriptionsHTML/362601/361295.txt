<p>10 November, Monday</p>
<p>Go up on boat with pel &amp; Lighthouse. Come Home. Played cards with Dad till 9 pm &amp; wrote to M. Mum comes into the Shop. Sent paper (Smith's) &amp; almanac to Auntie.</p>
<p>Wrote M 30</p>
<p>11 November, Tuesday</p>
<p>Recd letter from Auntie &amp; Card of wedding from Marion. Go to Smiths &amp; meet lots who know me. Tea at Railway with O &amp; B. act guide to Smiths. Nice evening. Played the empty stockin' game.</p>
