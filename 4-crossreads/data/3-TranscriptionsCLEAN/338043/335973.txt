
Our Engineer Dvr. Smith leaves for Long Service Furlough in Australia - After the show I write a new dramatic sketch for what is called "The Burglar".
Sept. 15thSabbath - Further decoration to the Blue Room. - Wash clothes - write letters. Weather warm & oppressive.
Sept. 16thA never to be forgotten storm - Thunder & lightening as I have never seen before heard or witnessed it. Show as usual.
