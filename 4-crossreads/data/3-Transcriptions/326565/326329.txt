<p>Saturday 25/3/16<br/>Piquet Corporal for the day.  All stores &amp; equipment being packed ready for removal.  Majority of tents struck &amp; removed to railway siding; had to sleep in the open.</p>
<p>Sunday 26/3/16<br/>All busy making final preparations to move off early to-morrow, rumoured we are to march to Ferry Post, 6 miles further on than Ismailia.  Issued with puttees &amp; lanyard.  No church parade owing to 8th Brigade having moved off a few day previous.  Fatigues till late at night.</p>
