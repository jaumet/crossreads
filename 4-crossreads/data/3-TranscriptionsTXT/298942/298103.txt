 Now to return to our later visit. 
 Rogers &amp; I went down in the late afternoon &amp; visited the brewery residence first.  Every room was in the same topsy turvey &amp; dilapidated condition.  From what I saw here &amp; other places I think the residents must have had barely time to clear out with what they stood up in.
