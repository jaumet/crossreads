
Tuesday 10/4/17Did some shopping bought Bag, boots, watch, etc. Went to see the Bamfords at night.Wednesday 11/4/17Had letter from Doff & Florrie, wrote to Doff. Left by 2.55 pm train after having Mrs Wisdom to lunch, very rough trip across - seasick, stayed at Hotel Folkestone Boulogne.Thursday 12/4/17Left by 2.15 pm train & arrived at Amiens at 7 pm, reach Battn by car at 9 pm, very tired.Friday 13/4/17Looking around generally meeting of C.O.s, moving forward tomorrow, letter from Doff.
