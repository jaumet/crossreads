<p>April 27th to 29th (inclusive) Hospital duties 7am to 7pm Dublin riots news.<br/>April 30th to May 5th Hospital duties- many letters written home and friends- Aeroplanes again plentiful. Weather perfect.<br/>May 6th German bombardment has telling effect on 20th Btl. Courage work of R.Bearers- Our Ambulance has a very busy time- true stories of courage, humour and fortitude in and after bombardment. I take part in Y.M.C.A. concert</p>
