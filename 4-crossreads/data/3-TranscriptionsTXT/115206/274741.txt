
June 1918Thursday 20
Battery W.Lines.  Pont NoyellesWeather wet and miserable. orders for Rgt and CentreSection to pull out at 11.p.m. tonight . peculiar coincidence that rain always falls when we pull out.Gun Adam paid me a visit at W.L. we went for a longstroll to Bussy, Querrie and Pont Noyelles. Had a verynice food , and awbon parle.Grayling, D a Costa left, Charlie Adey has gone to aschool as instructor to a  Tommy School, at Havre
