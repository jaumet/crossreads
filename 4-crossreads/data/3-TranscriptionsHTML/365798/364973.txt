<p>On leaving the train we did about 9 miles through lovely country to a little village down in a hollow called Dormart, where we stopped 3 days. Left Domart 16/7/16 and marched some 10 or 11 miles to Naours arriving same day 16/7/16.</p>
<p>Rested for 8 days in billets and moved off again 25/7/16 at 8 am to a village some 6 miles away where we stayed for one day and rested. Next day on the go again and had a stiff march to [indecipherable] some 10 miles nearer the firing and went into Billets. 27/7/17.</p>
<p>Moved out again on 4/8/16 and made for Albert, where we arrived at 1 am 5/8/16 &amp; camped on a hill some &frac14; mile from the Town. All civilians have left &ndash; the place is in ruins, including a lovely cathedral. It is bombarded every day by the Germans, &amp; the narrow escapes are many.</p>
