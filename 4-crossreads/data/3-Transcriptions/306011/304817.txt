<p>We are back at work again and I am at present on general duty. This morning was an inspection of gas helmets, a lecture &amp; company drill. This afternoon there will be a route march.</p>
<p>Monday 31st Dec. 1917:-</p>
<p>Yesterday I went into Boulogne on leave and visited various friends there as well as out at Wimereux. It was a rotten kind of day, cold &amp; windy with plenty of snow on the ground. There are 6 or 7 sent daily from the Ambulance to Boulogne, being taken in and brought out on lorries.</p>
