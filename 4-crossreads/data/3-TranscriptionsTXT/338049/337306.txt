 Saturday 6 April Parade 7.45 &amp; marched off to [East?] Camp. Issued dinner &amp; tea tickets. 
 Marched Fontinettes Station drew dinner &amp; tea after great push boarded train thro. window. Left about 12 &amp; went via Boulogne Abbeville to Canaples. 6.30 p.m. Marched in rain to Corps H.Q. well lost &amp; stop in duty stable wet through.
