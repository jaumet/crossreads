 up aerial.  Very busy scene on deck this morning, grocers, butchers, fruiterers &amp; all sorts of tradesmen bringing victuals aboard besides laundry people clothiers etc fulfilling orders.  A couple of Board of Trade men were also down examining lifeboats, lifebelts, and seeing that the ship is altogether sea worthy.  Owing to two of our old boats being unsatisfactory two new ones were made during the week &amp; were brought alongside this morning.  The ship is now painted grey all over hull, masts, derricks included.  Altogether she looks a very fine boat.  During the afternoon I assisted the Purser to fill all the "National Registration"
