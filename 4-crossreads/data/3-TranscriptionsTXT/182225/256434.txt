 1916 &nbsp; SATURDAY 11 &nbsp; NOV. 
 Fine &amp;&nbsp;cloudy but fairly warm. Cleaning harness all morning &amp; the Colonel made his weekly inspection of harness &amp; stables ect. Heard of several fatigues in afternoon&nbsp;so Bill Hunter &amp; self went over to the YMCA and I wrote Linda a letter. Heard from Jo &amp; Linda in morning. The battery went on duty at night &amp; I was on horse picket.
