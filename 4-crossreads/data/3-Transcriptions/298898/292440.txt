<p>only 3 days before they had had an accident in this spot killing 12 &amp; injuring about 20, all going on leave. However we at last reached Albert we the majority of us disembarked &amp; I set off for 'Becordel' about 2&frac12; miles away trusting to luck to find my unit there or if not I would be amongst friends so could get a feed &amp; wash. It was bitterly cold, snow being everywhere &amp; I was glad of the walk in order to get warm. When I reached Becordel I found my unit had arrived the day before so all was well altho' I could eat nothing for breakfast.</p>
<p>Saturday 20th Jan.<br/>About the first thing I did was to have a wash &amp; shave after which I began to feel myself again but the cold was very severe &amp; I felt very cold &amp; miserable all day, even near a fire my back would be cold so altogether my return was not of the</p>
