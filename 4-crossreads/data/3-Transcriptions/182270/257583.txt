
<p>SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 28, 1919.</p>
<p>Hot &amp; thundery. Had 10 points</p>
<p>at Tickles. Round the bores</p>
<p>with Darby in morning and</p>
<p>went over to Kickerbill and</p>
<p>Mooki Springs in afternoon.</p>
<p>I decided to shift Darby out</p>
<p>of hus present site down on</p>
<p>to the plain near the 4D mill</p>
<p>There is a bonza stream there.</p>
