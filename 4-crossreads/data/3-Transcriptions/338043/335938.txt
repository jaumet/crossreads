<p>- August. &ndash; 1918 &ndash;</p>
<p>Aug. 1st<br/>Rehearsal of "Grafters". Washing &ndash; Diary &ndash; Parcel from Maggie &ndash; Free afternoon &ndash; Once again I have pleasure of a very interesting walk around Rouen &ndash; Museum &ndash;<br/>In the evening we entertain the Sergeants of 1st A.G.H. &ndash; A good friend, advocate &amp; supporter in  Pte. A. Wilkins who makes all arrangement[s] for the Coves.</p>
