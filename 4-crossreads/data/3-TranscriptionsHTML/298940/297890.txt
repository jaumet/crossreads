<p>thought they looked bad.<br/>Soon a new squad arrived to take the place of those evacuated. Snowy Eva was in charge, but the other three were very little better than useless.<br/>Finally Capt Grieves decided to evacuate the whole of the original bearer party except our friend Gregg, the cook, &amp; just when he was writing our tickets out a new M.O. arrived with word from the Col. that we were to be returned at once. He brought up 2 new squads &amp; so we are packed up, &amp; returned to Vermozelle a little the worse for our 24 hours hard labour with gas.</p>
