<p>went back and stayed &amp; B.H.Q.<br/>Friday 28th<br/>Remained at B.H.Q. all day enemy shelled fairly heavy some casualities<br/>Saturday 29th<br/>In same position till three o'clock then got relieved arrived back near Dickebush about seven and camped<br/>Sunday 30th<br/>Rested all day had a muster roll call about fifty per cent casualities.</p>
