<p>There was a big Y.M.C.A. close by and a mixture of "Tommies" and Australians. They sang splendidly. It is a great sight to see such a body of men. A scotch minister remarked to us later it was always possible to get a crowd of Australians to attend services but a great difficulty to get the Scotchman</p>
<p>There was a Military cemetery close to the camp. Many mens graves were visible.</p>
<p>A feature to be remarked was the airman's graves each of their graves being surmounted by a cross made from the propeller of a machine he had evidently been piloting</p>
