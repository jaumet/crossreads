<p>Long before we sighted Port Said the familiar neat triangle sails of the Jippy cadamanangs could be seen <strike>in</strike> dotted along the horizon.</p>
<p>The splendid break-water showed up clearly. Everybody was on deck to see whatever could be seen.</p>
<p>The town showed up at fist like a hazy white blob then as we drew nearer, distinct outlines of streets &amp; buildings kept everybody's eyes <strike>glued</strike> occupied trying to see more. We passed a very old warship which has been stationed in Australian waters.</p>
