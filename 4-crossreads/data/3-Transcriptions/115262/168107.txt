<p>Thursday 6 December 1917.</p>
<p>Bivouaced at 12.45 am. No stand to. In bivouac 1 mile from village of Tebna. Cold &amp; bleak day. My camel&#39;s back crook after last nights movements.</p>
<p>Bgde got lost last&nbsp;night and only travelled 8 miles direct although the track they took was about 15 miles. Stayed here <s>most of</s> the day &amp; tried to move out at night but rain made country impassable.</p>
<p>Ration convoy was lost &amp; rain came on again so Bgde could not move.</p>
