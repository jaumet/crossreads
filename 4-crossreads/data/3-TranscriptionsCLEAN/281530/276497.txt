
25/6/18Another day in bed with nothing very special to chronicle. Our new Dr. proved a cocky little person, who found his own level after a while. Two new officers Mallinson and Miller reported today and I gave them some sage advice I do wish they would not send to a fighting unit at this stage of the War, officers who have never smelt powder. On the afternoon we had a Battalion Sports meeting which went off very well and resulted in "C" Coy winning the Battalion Championship. Hughes had the job of running the sports and did it right well. In the morning we had held a competition for the champion Lewis gun team and H.Q. won it. Received an order for Major Matthews to go to Aldershot to attend a Senior Officers School.
