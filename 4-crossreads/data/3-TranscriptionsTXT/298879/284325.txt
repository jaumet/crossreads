 February 1915 Wednesday 3 Rifle Range shooting. 
 Thursday 4 Shooting practice for the battalion. 7th &amp; 8th Batt. gone to Ismaelia. Paid weekly wage of 68 piastres or 14/- English. Received photos of myself &amp; a friend. Posted them to the various recipients. Our Battalion won the brigade shooting competition
