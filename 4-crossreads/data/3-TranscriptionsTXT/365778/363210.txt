
Wednesday 1/8/17Raining heavily all day, place a regular bog.Thursday 2/8/17Went for a route march towards Bouchasne, raining all the tine 81/2 miles.Friday 3/8/17Looking for 30 yards range site - splendid concert at night by A.S.C. Coy billeted near here at Distillery.Saturday 4/8/17Owing to heavy rain shifted men from Camp into Billets - mud up to knees.Sunday 5/8/17Brigade Church parade, meeting of C.O.s at Bde H.Qrs at Renescuse in afternoon.Monday 6/8/17Band coming back to Battn, Wisdom gone on leave again.
