 9-3-19 (Sun) This is Sunday & we have been told that there will be lectures rather unusual & unnecessary we think but orders are orders I intended to go out to day but rain has set in & I do not move out at all 
 10-3-19 This vet Hospital has been the disposing centre for army horses for some time past & horses are regularly offered for sale Mon & Fridays. The mules average about 700 fr & the horse 1300 to 1400 Many people advocate that these horses should not be sold to french as they do not know how to treat an animal.
