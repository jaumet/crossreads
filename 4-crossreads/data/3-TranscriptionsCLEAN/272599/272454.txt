  
 1917 Thursday 5 July 
 Queen Mary visits the hospital at mid-day stays 3 minutes. No fuss made. French hang out flags thats all.   Jock MacB & self go for walk after tea to Happy Valley & Hidden singer, hoeing cabbages. We have a little impromptu operatic duet. 3 in one refreshment.  A beautiful calm evening & a most enjoyable stroll.  Call at Mde Hunch at Wimille raspberries & sugar.
