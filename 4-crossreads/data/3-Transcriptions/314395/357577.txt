<p>Australian Branch<br/>B.R.C.S.</p>
<p>Park House<br/>Salisbury<br/>1-2-17</p>
<p>Dear Dad,<br/>Although now quite recovered, I am still being kept in Convalescent Camp and may be here for a couple of weeks yet, before getting my 14 days sick leave.  I despatched a cable to you which I learn was sent on the 27th Jan. for some financed assistance to tide me over the leave.  I think I explained in my last</p>
