<p>29th  An observation balloon is continually on the lookout for Zeppelins close by the Camp  There are Raids almost every day on the South Coast.</p>
<p><u>Roumania</u> has now entered the war by declaring war on Austria.  Italy has also extended her declaration to Germany.</p>
<p>30th  One of our boys is very musical.  He has drums in his ears, internal organs, &amp; his feet hums.</p>
<p>Bread is 1/0&frac12; per 4 lb loaf  steak 1/8 lb and Rump is priceless.</p>
<p>Harry left for Australia yesterday.</p>
