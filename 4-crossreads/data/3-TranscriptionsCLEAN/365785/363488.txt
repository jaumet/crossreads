
No. 7 WardRoom 87Mena House, 278/7/15
To Mrs. Major ReadYou must excuse us for not thanking you before this, for the very usefull presents we received alright at Ghizerah.  Sorry to heare the comforts funds are (Mafish) as we would like you to always feel like a millonear but not at Major Reads expence.  We have to thank him for his kindness.  We got such good treatment in Hospital.  Under his care we did not expect him to get us comforts for the trinches.Hopeing you will soon be a millionear again.
We are respectfully yoursDriver FahyBr. Ferguson.
