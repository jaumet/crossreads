<p>1916 March 2<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Do nothing much all morning. Heavy rain on 9 o'clock parade. After 4 o'clock Tony &amp; self go town get several little items &amp; pull up at Petersham to tea. after which we go to Audley Pictures all hands poor program, home supper &amp; catch 11.15 pm train back to camp.  Safely arriving about 1 pm</p>
