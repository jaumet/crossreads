<p>1st Field Amb<br/><strike>Tel. el. Keber]</strike><br/>Egypt<br/>10/1/16.</p>
<p>My darling little Tingsie,<br/>Thank you very much for the lovely card you and Peter sent me at Christmas time. I was so pleased &amp; surprised when it came.<br/>There are not many camels here but some dear little donkeys like the dear little one you rode on at the Pyramids.<br/>It was just like this,<br/>[drawing of a donkey]<br/>and he says he-haw</p>
