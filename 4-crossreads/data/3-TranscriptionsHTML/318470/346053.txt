<p>14th<br/>Still acting S.Maj. getting plenty to do and things not too satisfactory.  Expect to leave it.</p>
<p>15th<br/>Saw the Colonel today &amp; intend leaving today.</p>
<p>16th<br/>Left A.C.L.R. last night &amp; came back to the old Coy.  Taking No. 2 Sec.</p>
<p>17th<br/>Snow set in last night &amp; continued to fall lightly all day.  Very cold.</p>
<p>18th<br/>Half the Coy. &ndash; No. 1 &amp; 2 Secs. shifted camp in 8th F. Coy's Camp &amp; took over their work.  I took over C.R.E. defence work.  Still snowing.</p>
<p>19th<br/>Working on C.R. Defences.  Cold &amp; freezing.  A fair amount of shelling.</p>
<p>20th<br/>Same as yesterday.  Very cold but fine.</p>
<p>21st<br/>Heavy shelling &ndash; large shells falling close to our work.  Cold but fine.</p>
<p>22nd<br/>Still working on C.R.E. defences.  Weather very cold.  A good deal Artillery fire on both side.</p>
<p>23rd<br/>C.R.E. defence work.  Intensely cold.  Aerial activity and a good deal of Artillery both side.</p>
<p>24th<br/>Same work as yesterday.  Still very cold but fine.  Heavy shelling.</p>
<p>25th<br/>C.R.E. defences.  One of Fritz's aeroplanes brought down near where we are working.</p>
<p>26th<br/>3 more of Fritzes Planes brought down.  Heavy shelling both sides.</p>
<p>27th<br/>3 more of Fritzes Planes brought down within sight.  29th Div. hopped over last night &amp; took some trenches &amp; about 300 prisoners.</p>
<p>28th<br/>Still very cold.  Heavy shelling both side.</p>
