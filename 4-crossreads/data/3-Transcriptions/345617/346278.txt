<p>Decembre 22<br/>Coy engaged on Sec. &amp; Coy drill. Still freezing heavily. Nothing of importance to note.</p>
<p>Decembre 23<br/>We are doing a fair amount of Section and Coy drill &amp; other training. We are having cold weather, the roads being frozen hard &amp; make travelling very hard for horses.</p>
