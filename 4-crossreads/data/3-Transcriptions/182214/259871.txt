
<p>Shells burst about two hundred yards away &amp; then began to think we were not in such a safe place after all but when we saw the old French woman still go ahead with her work it restored confidence &amp; at the sme time greater admiration for them. , a firework display could be seen every night if one looked toward the lines where the star&nbsp;shells we continually going up. Armentieres was only a mile or so away &amp; we could see its ruined church which was only a week previously destroyed. &nbsp;On July 11 th&nbsp;we left Erquinghem&nbsp;for Fort Rompu only a short distance away &amp; took over from another Field Ambulance This was the dressing station. In a Brewery much to our satisfaction. Been all day</p>
