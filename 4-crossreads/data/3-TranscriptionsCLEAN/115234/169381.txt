 A stranger in a foreign land 
 I boast no bright regalia 
 Bronzed of cheek, keen of eye 
 Chips of the old block all 
 We take our place by John 
 Bull's side, with him we rise 
    or Fall. 'Adieu' 
 ___ 
 Written by 
 Jom O'Donnell 
 "Soldat De Australie" 
 ----------  "   ---------- 
 at "Hamelet" 
 April 1918 
 "In front of Amiens"
