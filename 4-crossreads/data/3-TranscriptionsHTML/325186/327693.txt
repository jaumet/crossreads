<p>when the platoon sergeant comes around with the order to roll our blankets in bundles of ten to be taken out to our  Quarter-Masters Stores in Armentiers. That was just the Military Red Tape all over<br/>The first week in the trenches proved to us what the real active service was going to be. Hardly any food &amp; when we did get it, it was stone cold. We were in the line about when Fritz fired a shell that we got to know as a (minny) &amp; blew my pack to ----. Nothing happened of any importance &amp; after seven days in the line we were relieved by the 36th Battn. we came out to billets</p>
