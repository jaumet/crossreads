
7 January 1916 FriHad a fair number of dressings this morning afterwards breasting the officers bar & securing light refreshments. Met Warrant officer of 20th Battalion who resides at Petersham. Never before have I witnessed such beautiful sunsets as these parts offer.
8 January 1916 SatSgt Bellemey before Colonel
Arrived Alexandria 4 p.m. Hover by mid stream. Played penny banker with the W.O also indulged in hot bath. First decent bath for six months Thousands of sea-gulls around the ships. Also H.M.S. Mars.
