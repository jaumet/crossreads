<p>16 February, Sunday</p>
<p>W. Very Rainy</p>
<p>Get my work absolutely square. Go to Y.M.C.A. &amp; go straight up to D.M. I witness Pictures of D.A.M. on the Beach in Costume &amp; Pictures of Doris. I am very dull &amp; dopey. I speak behind fellows Backs. Dicky T's is very exuberant &amp; plays up generally.</p>
<p>Siddie comes Home at 11 pm.</p>
<p>17 February, Monday</p>
<p>W.<br/>Sgts mess meeting which is attended by C.O. Write by Mess Fire &amp; go down to Dick's flat to Play Cards.</p>
<p>Got some at 9 pm</p>
<p>Wrote M.R.L., Mrs Clarke</p>
