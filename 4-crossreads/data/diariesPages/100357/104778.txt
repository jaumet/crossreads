97 20/5/15
Arrivals
The NZ Inf Bde having arrived at Anzac Cove rejoins NZ A Div from today inclusive. Will biv in the gully S.W of Walkers Ridge and will form Div Reserve, the Ottago M.R. having disembarked at Anzac Cove rejoined the NZ A Div today are attached to the NZMR bde The 3rd ALHBde on disembarking today will be attached to this Div will move into Biv in the valley S. of the Area now occupied by NZ I. Bde will form part of the Garrison of No 4 Section of the Defence under Brig Gen AH Russell
