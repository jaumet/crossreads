<p>only feature of interest here is the old battle-ground of Tel-El-Kebir of about 35 years ago.  The old formation of trenches   &amp; the British square are still in evidence   &amp; are marked by bones   &amp; skeletons, old cartridges (in some of which the powder will still explode   &amp; a few other pieces of uniform etc.  I suppose you are all settled down in the new place now.  I have had no letters for a couple of months,   &amp; cannot apparently get back to the regiment or a transfer</p>
