 April 1918 Thursday 25 
 Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours.    D.R. Had a tour to Bonnay after inspection, - received new gas bag. 1200 Fritz's captured at Villers Brettonvibne. Some fine big fellows amongst them also some youngsters very heavy fighting for village of Villers Brettonvibne. Fifteenth Bdge did the damage. Rode up to W.L. at Franvillers and had a yap with boys. James knocked in thigh.  Fleming, Bill Aldred, Davies and three others killed. Weather mists.
