<p>Shot in the Back<br/>Internees Missed from Camp</p>
<p>Liverpool, Wednesday<br/>Two internees of the Sing Sing compound at Holdsworthy attempted to escape on Tuesday night.  One of them Schwartz, was shot in the back, and is now in the hospital.  The other man was recaptured.</p>
