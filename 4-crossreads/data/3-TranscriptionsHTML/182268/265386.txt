<p>Saturday 2/2/18. Went for Pattie took her to lunch &amp; then to Queen&#39;s Hall Concert Dance at A/asian Club Piccadilly at night.</p>
<p>Sunday 3/2/18. Went down to Bamfords for a couple of days.</p>
<p>Monday 4/2/18. Received cable from Doff [sent?] on 12/1/18. Got a new pr of Alpine boots. Wrote Doff</p>
<p>Tuesday 5/2/18&nbsp; Still at Bamfords&nbsp; Went to a picture Show &quot;The Greatest Wish in the World&quot;.</p>
