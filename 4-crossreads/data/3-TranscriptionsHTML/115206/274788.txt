<p>August,1918<br/>Saturday 24</p>
<p>In Rear of Proyart. Bty Position<br/>Weather dull, but nice and cool. Fritz planes flew fairly low all day.<br/>News from all fronts seems pretty good. Built a nice <br/>dugout under the road. Received a letter from Uncle Will<br/>&ndash; a nice interesting one.<br/>Ken Gun and Red Palmer left for Waggon Lines early in <br/>the evening. Had trouble with Bdge line and walked it .<br/>Bty pulled out on the main road. Fritz bombing all roads<br/>fairly heavily. Slept in a<br/>dug out near the road.</p>
