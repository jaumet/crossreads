23rd Nov:-Quiet day in Hospital  Two hospital trains arrive, bringing one hundred stretcher cases (wounded) &amp; the hospital is full. Seen by Capt Kay (x-ray) who expresses the opinion from x-ray picture that my shoulder was broken &amp;  not properly set.
24th Nov:-Very nice &amp; cool day. Doctor reports on shoulder as opinion of last night. Go out for a motor drive this afternoon round Mataria &amp;  Hellmich to the residence of an English gentleman who is in a high position in Cairo.
