<p>Tuesday 3 [2] July 1917 [1918]<br/>&frac12; day &amp; spend it with Cpl. Shaw &amp; my camera, go Kodak Island &amp; take street hawkers, women in the Nile etc.<br/>Have 70 pts. in 2 wards, mostly malarias, debitory, [debility ?] throats etc., and we do not feed them properly &amp; practically no outings.  Dr. Wilson is good &amp; does her best but some lady M.O's are no good for the welfare of the pts.
