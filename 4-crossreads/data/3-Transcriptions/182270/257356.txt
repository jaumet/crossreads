
<p>995</p>
<p>THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 13, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; cold. Reported at</p>
<p>20 Belgrave Square in the</p>
<p>morning &amp; got my marching</p>
<p>orders to go to Sutton Veny</p>
<p>tomorrow. &nbsp;Went round &amp;</p>
<p>fixed things up afterwards</p>
<p>&amp; got a big mail at the bank</p>
<p>&amp; packed everything up.</p>
<p>Went round &amp; said good</p>
<p>bye to Dot Brunton</p>
<p>after tea.</p>
