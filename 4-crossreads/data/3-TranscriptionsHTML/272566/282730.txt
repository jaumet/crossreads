<p>Thursday 13 December</p>
<p>Misty &ndash; Greasy Roads<br/>Dark at 4.30 p.m.<br/>Working at the bike all morning.<br/>After dinner I took the A.D.M.S. Run and continued on to Bailleul via Ravetsburg<br/>Took the 6 a.m. casualty wire and another this afternoon to 15th Bgde. Sigs.<br/>1st Div. Troops are now arriving to relieve our 5th Division &ndash; 14th Brigade &amp; 14th Field Ambulance moved today for a destination near Etaples.  The 8th Brigade &amp; our Ambulance move on the 17th inst. (Monday)</p>
