<p>Monday 23 September<br/>To-morrow we are to move to Herbecourt preparatory to entraining for the West of Amiens.<br/>Yanks why they are here by thousands the 30th Division relieving us. Tanks are also here by the <strike>the</strike> dozens  &amp; soon I think the Hindenburg line is to be attacked &amp; I think carried by the enthusiastic Yanks.</p>
<p>Tuesday 24 September<br/>Glorious day.  Move to Biaches or remains thereof via Peronne &amp; thence across river at Halle.  Good march on good roads.  Possibly entrain to-morrow.</p>
