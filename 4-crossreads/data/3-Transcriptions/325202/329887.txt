<p>Oct 6 (Saturday) Dickebush<br/>have been up here the last 4 days &amp; am <strike>kept</strike> taking up the rations to our advanced headquarters in Ypres. it must have been a beautiful old town but now after three years of artillery fire is just a mass of debris.</p>
<p>I pass through the Lille Gate <strike>and</strike> between the solid old ramparts of the town which still stand though bearing the marks of many shells &amp; across the moat upon which I noticed <strike>swimming serenely</strike> amidst all the noise of traffic &amp; of our guns &ndash; two swans <strike>swim</strike></p>
