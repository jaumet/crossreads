  
 Letter Register Feb 2 - Gladys Noble - Galong ditto - Nelly Slattery - ditto Feb 6 - Gladys Noble - ditto Ditto - Nelly Slattery - ditto 
 Tuesday. We went back to Belah to water & feed  Infantry still attacking town with heavy losses  a great assault made by NZ and 2nd Brigade on Eastern end  captured 2 guns and 300 prisoners Wednesday  Very quiet so far  a big offensive coming off  40,000 Turks on our front
