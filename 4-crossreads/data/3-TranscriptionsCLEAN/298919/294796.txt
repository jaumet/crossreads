The continuous whistle by porters calling cars & taxis denoted we had no chance of getting a taxi so we braved the elements & ran along to the nearest tube.  We took it as far as Charing X thence underground to Mark Lane & caught our train in nice time.  Wednesday 7th July 1915.   After lunch went ashore with W. Visited Marconi House where we were taken in hand by Mr Harris the Editor of the W.W. who showed us over all this beautiful not to say gorgeous building which[?] was originally built as the Gaiety restaurant next door to the Gaiety theatre.
