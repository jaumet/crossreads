<p>[Sept.-Oct., 1916.]<br/>Friday 29<br/>Living in comfort at the ramparts sleeping on a real live bed wire matress and alive with chats, but its good<br/>Saturday 30<br/>Now at the barracks Singing to night at the battalion concert nice stew for tea (meat beans spuds tomatoes &amp; bovril)<br/>Sunday Oct. 1<br/>In a cafe with Irish English Scotch Welsh Australians and Canadians<br/>Drunk as old Dave on a farmers picnic<br/>This little book as been in action seven months to day Blue Eyed Nell 1.3.16 [indecipherable]</p>
