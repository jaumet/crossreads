 29/3/16  Wednesday 
 Very Hot &ndash; Got orders to be ready to march out at 7.30 am, needless to add, we didn't waste any time time to get away from the wretched A.G.H &ndash; which all us L.H.F.A were very loathe to go to even on fatigue.
