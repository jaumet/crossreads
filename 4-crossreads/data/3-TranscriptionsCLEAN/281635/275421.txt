I then looked around for our camp as too fatigued to go back to the line then, & then walked 3 or 4 miles pretty well all around Ypres trying to find it. I struck some Victorians from 60th Btn & they gave me a good feed of stew & bread & jam as was famished with hunger & cold & wet through with rain having lost overcoat waterproofs everything.
