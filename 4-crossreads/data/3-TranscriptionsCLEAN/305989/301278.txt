  
 Sunday 14 October 1917 Quite day, hands very sore. 
 Monday 15 October 1917 Had snap taken of the Dumpites:  nothing doing. 
 Tuesday 16 October 1917 Taube over:  one brought down in Wadi, nothing doing in particular. 
 Wednesday 17 October 1917 Got two parcels from Aunt Rose & Dorothy.  Saw a good concert given by The Tanks at Y.M.C.A. had good supper. 
 Thursday 18 October 1917 Quiet day, got a lot of new gramophone records, quiet night, read after supper. 
 Friday 19 October 1917 1st Anzac Mtd. Dump moving into Shellal Crossing;  helping the lads to pack up. 
 Saturday 20 October 1917 Quiet day.  Matt Ward came back from Port Said.
