<p>Sept 1917<br/>Fri 28th My mate &amp; I went to Billets today we struck good accomodation &amp; very nice people<br/>Saturday 29th<br/>Went to Paington [Paignton] today it is another very fine place 3 miles from Torquay by tram<br/>Tuesday Oct 2nd<br/>I got my teeth fixed up so I will be going on leave soon<br/>Wednesday 3rd<br/>Put in for leave &amp; got it will be going tomorrow<br/>Thursday 4th<br/>Started on our journey at 4.25 pm.<br/>Friday 5th<br/>Arrived at Edinburgh at 4.40 am my word they are some trains here &amp; travel for some time.   Put up at the Soldiers Club.   Had a look round the town this morning it is a very fine town &amp; puts me in mind of Melbourne    They have the old cable cars</p>
