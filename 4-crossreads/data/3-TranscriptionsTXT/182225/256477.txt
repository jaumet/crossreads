 DEC.&nbsp;&nbsp; SUNDAY 24&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916 
 Fine &amp; Sunny. Mother, Peter &amp; Self went to church in the morning &amp; then for a walk along the Woodford road. Stayed in the Hotel in afternoon waiting for Alan &amp; he did not come. Talking to Major &amp; M rs  Brewiss from New Zealand &amp; they are very nice people.
