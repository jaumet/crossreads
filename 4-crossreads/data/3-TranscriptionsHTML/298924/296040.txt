<p>Thursday 27 February 1919<br/>Rafa<br/>Khamsin still blowing &ndash; everything smothered in Dust like a Darling Shower, in a Tent, water becomes mud, food sand etc.<br/>I am the Lecturer today.  May the men listen &amp; curse in silence &ndash; topic Court Martial procedure.  Heard old <strike>Sam</strike> Sir Samuel McC. had gone west.  I wonder if true &ndash; somebody will come in for many millions.  Sorry to hear Hunter Landale gone.</p>
