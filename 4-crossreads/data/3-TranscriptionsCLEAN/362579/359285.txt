  
 Long for letters, they would  write a few lines. 
 My papers are not sent on now, so think you had better stop sending the "Argus" until I get to Base. 
 It is not quite so cold   &  our tents are fairly comfy. 
 The horrid noise of the firing worries us sometimes but never feel afraid. 
 Sister Annie Bell is back. Poor old Sister Pilkington has appendicitis   &  may have to be operated upon. So she won 't be back again.
