
December 1916Saturday 30Squally weather
Sunday 31Terrific gale blowing no shelter. Everybody wet. Rained during night
New Years day . All 1917Went down to beach bombarded by German Taubs. 3 shells fell down into the sea. Remainder went into palm groves. 13 casualties amongst British troops. Terrific wind blowing.  Had the coldest swim I ever had.
