 Lieut. F. Thompson proceeded on leave. Weekly inspection of Transport took place on the 3rd.  Report received today stating that everything was correct. Concentration of fire, Nil.  Two thousand rounds fired on the following targets:- Ceylon Farm, C.6.c.90.05., Traversing &amp; Searching of Road, C.5.d.77.38 to 86.60., Junction of Trenches, C.11.d.65.56., Traversing &amp; Searching Road to Deulemont C.12.c.00.40 to 37.30., Working Party C.11.d.30.50.  Observed from O.P. flash lights.  No movement seen after fire was directed against them. Both enemy's Artillery and Machine guns were very quiet throughout day and night.  Weather dull, slight rain, but observation good. Concentration of fire during day, Nil.
