<p>it in the papers.  You won't forget to try &amp; send about &frac12; a lb of Circular Head Potatoes for Uncle Tom to try.  My word it is lovely at Uncle's House.  I have not seen much of England or of the Isle of Man for that matter but I have had better times for all that.</p>
<p>I am glad that Mum is on the mend &amp; trust that she will be foolish enough to relapse.  I have no news to give you so I'll ring off as I want to write to Auntie &amp; Uncle who have just been nursed by Marie for the Flu &amp; they are only just getting about now.</p>
<p>Best Love to you all<br/>From Harry.</p>
