 Monday 2nd April. Very cold again. Fritz shelled Bapaume today for the first time several places being knocked &amp; he also put a few over on to the roads. Very strong wind blowing which dislodged bricks etc. &amp; caused several walls to collapse one tall one we had to pull down for safety. Towards evening had a heavy fall of snow a very pretty sight. 55th, 56th Battalions had a hop-over today &amp; took two villages the stunt developing into a bigger thing to aid the French lower down.
