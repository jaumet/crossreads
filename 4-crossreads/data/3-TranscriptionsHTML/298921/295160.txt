<p>July, 1915<br/>Thursday 8<br/>Letter from Daisy enclosing one from Mother an old one sorry to hear Hilda's brother is dead. I noticed it &amp; was wondering if were he.<br/>The C.O. has promised that I shall take the next draft to Gallipoli, <strike>120</strike> go today all without horses &ndash; I am posting Joe some of my Orders shewing I circulated the Melburnians good wishes in Regimental Orders.<br/>Also Mays enclosed June 6th  - I am very glad she is home again.</p>
