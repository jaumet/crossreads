
Tuesday 5th AugustLeft (Questede) Quiestede at 3 p.m today in rain.
During the morning, while I was drawing some water from a water bore in the courtyard of the farm, the Colonel came along and in passing asked me if I had noticed the Chapel in the corner. I went in with him, through the ordinary cottage door of an ordinary whitewashed cottage, which formed one side of the quadrangle, whose other sides were barns, and then through to the right.
