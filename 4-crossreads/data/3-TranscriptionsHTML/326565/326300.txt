<p>Thursday 10/2/16<br/>Route march to Bitter Lakes, about 4 miles &amp; swim.  Lakes very shallow in parts; can wade out for fully a mile.  Returned to camp for lunch about 1.30 pm.  Interesting lecture during afternoon by Capt Wooster, dealing with particulars of Geneva Convention</p>
