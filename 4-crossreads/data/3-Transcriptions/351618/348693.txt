<p>I had a hair cut 7 shave after dinner &amp; felt quite refreshed. We went into the few shops that Panaghia boasted &amp; some of our party made purchases. I was content to take notes till the following day &amp; I afterwards found it was the best procedure as I got the best values &amp; low prices &ndash; the competition in the little place being very keen. During the afternoon the children came trooping out of School &amp; what memories that brought back to me with their happy gambols.</p>
<p>At tea we had another spread &amp; several Imperial Subalterns who were staying at the London came along &amp; gave us nuts mandarins &amp; figs &amp; in their own words "Let us have a dam good feed".  We got a few bottles of fiz (Champagne)</p>
