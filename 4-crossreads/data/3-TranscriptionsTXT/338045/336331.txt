Oct. 18th &ndash; WednesdayReveille as usual at 6 a.m. &amp; parade 6-30 a.m.  Raining hard all night but not so cold.  Sleep well.  Cold day, cloudy, but no rain.  I have charge of a fatigue party during the morning on lines &amp; a picket for lines afternoon.  Most of the A.M.C. at the "Bull Ring".  Three trains pass the camp &ndash; the 5th Division Australians &ndash; going from Ypres to the Somme.  Scott goes to England.  News from the lines scarce.  Another "push" coming.  A walk to 4th Div.
