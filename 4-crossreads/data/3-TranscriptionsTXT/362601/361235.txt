
11 July, Friday
F.Very rough sea & strong gale. Sea roughest yet. Jack Cramer gets a WAC Conigrams. I I read about H & M become separated though married & return to one another. Read.
Mar. wom. 28 falls 1 & L with single M of 04. Reads [indecipherable].
Read a Bachelors love Story Collects. Single girl 82 m. grey haired bandman of 04[?] go to bed in hammock. Just as well as there is another flood. Distribution on music & wardrobe.
12 July, Saturday
F.Spend whole day reading. Lectures on Fruit pests & [indecipherable] to Jerusalem, Allenby means "Bearer of the Law".
Description of Kaiser & Allenby's entrances to Jerusalem the blessed. Read at night. Awful list on boat & swell still troublesome. I am hungry but can't eat ships munfaces[?]. We have another kit inspection for dutiable goods.
