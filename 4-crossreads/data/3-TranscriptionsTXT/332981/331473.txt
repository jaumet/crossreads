 After continuing on for a few moments we arrived at the camp &amp; once again amongst our old chums, &amp; what a difference only about a quarter of the original Company left all rest away, some back in Ausie, others in Hospital &amp; some waiting in Blighty to leave, all the old men over 50 have left &amp; a lot of reinforcements had come along &amp; now we are close on 300 strong.
