 24-5-1917 Innoculation 2 p.m.  Arm very sore.  Can't write, weather fine, no Bty. manoeuvre in this camp.  Sports coming off second week in June. 
 25-5-1917 Feeling rotten, terrible headache.  Effects of innoculation.  Sgt. Todd rejoined Bty.  Nothing doing, weather fine. 
 26-5-1917 Competition coming off, best subsection in Bty., then in Brigade, and then in Division.  Sgt. Gibson next for seaside leave.  Leave to Paris, 4 days is also rumoured.  Recovered from innoculation. 
 27-5-1917 Sunday.  Sgt. Gibson on seaside leave.  things very quiet.  We have a gun with us now, that was in action on Cape Helles.
