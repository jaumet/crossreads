<p>Sunday 6 May 1917</p>
<p>[See images for remainder of lettering of inscription.]</p>
<p>Bombed again &ndash; it's just like a Hawk in a chicken yard the way we scatter when the HUN flies over us.</p>
<p>No Church today only digging Trenches. Hear Russia is getting full up, but we know little of the true state of affairs.</p>
<p><strike>Letters from Laura &amp; Dolly</strike> - MAIL has been regular of late. Had your vegetables for Tea tonight very good.</p>
