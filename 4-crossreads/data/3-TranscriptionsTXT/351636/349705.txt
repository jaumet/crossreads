Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers17.3.16
Dear Mrs. LaytonCould you assist Mrs. McPherson to get a prambulator for her very heavy baby. She has got along very well on her pay with 3 children &amp; so far asked for no help &ndash; It would be a great help to her to have some means of taking the child about
Yours trulyE. H. Bennie.
