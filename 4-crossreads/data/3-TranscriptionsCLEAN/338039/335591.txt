  
 April. 1916. 
 Apr.1st. "Fools day"- no jokes or false alarms- Football & Boxing under Capt. Brownell- lecture by Capt. Hamilton- "Veneral Diseases"- Continued bombardment on British front- Half- holiday (?). Apr. 2nd. Sabbath- Mass 9 a. m. - Pleasure walk- Issue of two gas respirators- Salmons. Sauce.  Pears & Lime juice. No! not issued-  Letter-writing- "R.C faith in France."
