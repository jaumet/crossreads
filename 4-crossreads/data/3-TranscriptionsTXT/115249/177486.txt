 From - Press 
 To - News 
 Date 23 9 14 
 No news of operations of War received today except great fighting in progress on Aisne. &nbsp;Kitchener states good grounds for quiet confidence but struggle bound to be long. 
 Great Britain has in the field more than 6 Divisions of Troops&nbsp;&amp; 2 Cavalry divisions kept at full strength by reinforcements. 
 21st. Fighting still going on Aisne. &nbsp;Galicia evacuated by Austrians. 
 Imperial Parliament prorogued. 
 22nd. &nbsp;Fighting Aisne considered of utmost importance no decisive results yet due Allied positions satisfactory. 
 Some little progress made by left wing of Allies in lower Aisne. 
 Cusack 
 0230
