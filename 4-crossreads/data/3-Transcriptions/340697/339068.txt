<p>In building a pier in the harbour here a small steamer was sunk to make a breakwater 21.6.15</p>
<p>Left Embros on El Hihira and took up our position in firing line the following day 7.7.15</p>
<p>The turks dropped about 40 8 inch howitzer shells in 2nd Bn trenches today causing about 10 casualties.</p>
<p>H.Q. sigs made full strength today 3 B. Coy. sigs joined including myself 3.7.15</p>
<p>A great number of howitzers have been landed here lately 14.7.15</p>
