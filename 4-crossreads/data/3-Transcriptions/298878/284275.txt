Lizzy fired a couple of her big guns at it, with the result that the gun has given no more trouble; at another time the airship reported that there was a big Turkish transport sneaking down from the Sea of Marniona towards the Narrows.  Lizzy got into position, and fired three salvos; though the transport was miles away out of sight behind ranges of hills she was struck by some of the shells and sank in 4 minutes taking 2000 men to their doom.  The Adjt showed a telegram which stated that the English forces landing at some of the beaches around the Hellis had landed without much opposition and were progressing favorably.
