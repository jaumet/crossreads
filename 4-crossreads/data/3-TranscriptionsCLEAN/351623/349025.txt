
Jan 1st 19183rd advance party lost 1 days leave.
Jan 2nd to 5th 18Anchored at Scapay Flow.
Jan 6th 18Left Scapay Flow for Bergen Norway. Picked up convoy & escorted them to Bergen &
Jan 7th 18escorted back returning convoy to England. Ran into very rough weather & blizzard & lost a seaman overboard.
Jan 8th 18Arrived at Scapay Flow.
Jan 9th 18Coaled.
Jan 10th 18Shifted to North Shore.
Jan 11th 18Torpedo running.
Jan 12 to Sat 14 18Anchored at Scapay.
