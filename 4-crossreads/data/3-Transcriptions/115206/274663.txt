<p>April 1918<br/>Wednesday 3</p>
<p>In Billet &ndash; Y.M.C.A. Orville<br/>Spent morning in cleaning harness, and afternoon in excersising on hills.<br/>Weather very wet, and cold.<br/>Received p.c. from O.J. and Smithy received some mail from M.V.<br/>Received a letter from A.R.T, <br/>Went up to Sav-on in evening with P.G.<br/>Had the usual and spent an enjoyable evening.<br/>Gunn still bed mate.<br/>Rained heavily last night.<br/>Had lecture by Major.</p>
