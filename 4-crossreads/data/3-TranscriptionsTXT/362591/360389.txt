I suppose you've heard that Caesar has gone to the 54th or some such outlandish battalion, & has consequently been left behind.  I've heard nothing of Evan Richards, & Walter is still the Brigade's Orderly Officer.
Having just been handed orders which require immediate attention I'll have to close.  From them I gather that by the time you get this we will have been two or three weeks in the trenches.
