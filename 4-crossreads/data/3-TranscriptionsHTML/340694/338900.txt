<p>15<br/>I'm left behind as Transport Clerk but will join up again over there. I leave with the 14th Coy.</p>
<p>9 p.m. Thin brown lines have been passing by since tea time. The lads look in splendid nick, &amp; as brown as the clothes they're in. Each Battalion has its' band &amp; everyone seems very cheerful. It's the thought of leaving this country I think. The Tommies are arriving to take over I don't envy them a bit this is not a white man's land. We are kept at it supplying transport as there's only two Coys here to do it.</p>
<p>1/6/16.<br/>The 12th Brigade marched out to-day &amp; looked very fit. Our Coy shifted the whole Brigade in record time.</p>
