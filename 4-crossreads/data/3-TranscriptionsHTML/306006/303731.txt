<p>8-2-17<br/>Thurs 8th<br/>Have decided to wait till we get settled again before I go to the Dentist we leave next Saturday morning ; the 1st Bde do not relish the idea of going back &amp; Gen Cox put in today at Division to see if he could get it cancelled; The Railway line is going down but not as fast as we expected.</p>
<p>9-2-17<br/>Frid 9th<br/>Found my left foot slightly swollen this morning &amp; rather sore went to Lumb &amp; had it painted with iodine &amp; knocked about barefooted this morning packing up ready to move, at dinner time my foot had swelled</p>
