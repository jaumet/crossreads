
1915Feb. 1 Mon.Number of patients in Hospital - 674.Turned into bed after working all day at 11 p.m.Bugle calls are sounded daily in No.2 G.H. camp as follows:-Reveille - 6.15 a.m.Breakfast - 7.45Dinner - 12.45Tea - 5.15 p.m.First Post - 9.30Last  Post - 10.0Lights out - 10.15
A number of patients medically unfit for further service in the A.I.F. sent back to Australia in the old Kyarra.
