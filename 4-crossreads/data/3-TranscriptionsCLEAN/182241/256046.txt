  
 Jan 21st Left Dieran at 7 am in a ford car for Jerusalem. With a  fine  beautiful morning favouring, we had a fine through bumpy run to Latron which is at the foot of the hills & we began the assent of the fooothills along, the road to Jerusalem after a four hours run through mountanious country we arrived @ the old city of Jerusalem. The Jerusalem of today cannot be termed a typical oriental city inside its walls its Eastern character has been generally preserved but in recent years European influence has been great.
