<p>however we got out alright. We are now the 54th Bty, half of our original battery the 60th coming to the 54th the other half going to the 53rd &amp; so making a six gun battery. I might state here the idea of this in the first place, the four gun battery were formed to cover a wider front not having too many guns, but now we have the guns we are able to revert to the original six gun batteries.</p>
<p>Well after about a week out, we are ordered in again for some special stunt, on the Poiziers ridges, our wagon lines being in the town of "Albert", after this</p>
