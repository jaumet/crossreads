 WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 5, 1919. 
 Fine & windy. Lopped in the Middle 
 paddock and then shifted the ewes 
 & lambs to Top paddock and 
 lopped there for them. Lopped in 
 Boundary in afternoon, & round 
 Cow paddock. Tickle at the fence 
 in afternoon, in Top paddock. 
 Dad & Peter went to Quirindi.
