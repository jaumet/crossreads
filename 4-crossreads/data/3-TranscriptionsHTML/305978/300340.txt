<p>landing in Alexandria at 8.30 pm. I was put on gaurd over ammunition till 4 pm next day, we had no rations left us, so had to go into town to buy.<br/>We had the name of the Regiment given to us as No 2 troop B Squad 1st Composite Cavarly  Regt.</p>
