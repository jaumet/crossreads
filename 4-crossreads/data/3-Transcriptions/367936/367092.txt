<p>Thursday 28 December 1916<br/>Out on out post all day.  Very cold and windy with some rain.<br/>Did some helio work when sun was shining.<br/>More rain at night.</p>
<p>Friday 29 December 1916<br/>Nothing much doing.<br/>Parcels mail in &amp; got three.  Nice to get some good tucker.  Getting ready for stunt tomorrow.<br/>Posted letter home which I began on Christmas day.<br/>Rain at night.</p>
