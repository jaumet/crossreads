<p>July 1917<br/>12th continued    prepared for driving.   The remainder of the section were making ready the "pile driver" &amp; we hope to make a start to-morrow.   The cyclists of the <strike>section</strike> Company went to Heinencourt [Henencourt] this morning to attend the review at which the King was present.<br/>Various Battalion or Unit competitions were held in the presence of the King, &amp; were a great success, &amp; proved a credit to the Australian troops.<br/>13th   This morning, final preparations were made by the section for the pile driving.   The 8th Coy have already started &amp; have a considerable number of piles driven.   Owing to an accident</p>
