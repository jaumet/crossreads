<p>26 Thursday<br/>Same as day before slept till 7.30 and consequently very tired no time to type.</p>
<p>27 Friday<br/>Same as above slept till 7.40.  Cold day.</p>
<p>28 Saturday<br/>Went to 41 Russell Square at 10 cut a stencil and left at 1.30.  Typed during afternoon.  Cold day but spasmodic sunshine.  Plane trees in height of glory.</p>
