 Jan 21  st  &nbsp;&nbsp;I did&#39;nt like too well the place I was staying &amp; I met a couple of fellows out of 31  st  &nbsp;Canadian Battalion in the same way so I went with them to Hotel D&#39;Egypte, they took a double room between them &amp; I took a single room. they were nice rooms &amp; nicely furnished in the most central position two hundred yards from the opera. We went &amp; saw the Eifle Tower &amp; went up in the great wheel in the afternoon &amp; went to the Olimpic theatre in the evening 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 22  nd  &nbsp;&nbsp;We got up at 9.A.M. &amp; had breakfast at our hotel. We went &amp; saw Napoleons tomb &amp; Hotel d&#39;Invalides in the morning &amp; in the afternoon to &quot;Mus&eacute;e Gravin&quot; which consists of waxwork tableaux of historical interest. cabinette fantastique. conjouring etc &amp; Palace des Mirages - We had tea at the Soldiers &amp; Sailors club &amp; stayed to the concert in the evening. it was splendid &amp; consisted of 1  st  &nbsp;class talent only - 
 &nbsp;
