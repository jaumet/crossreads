<p>During the weekend I visited Weymouth &amp; had a few happy hours with [indecipherable] travelled on to Chichester &amp; spent a fine time with Lily, watched her at work, then spent Monday night at Caterham with Mrs. Palmer [indecipherable].</p>
<p>On Tuesday I arrived in London, met Bobby &amp; spent the evening with her at "The Boy"; got a day's extension of leave, &amp; spent the best part of the following day with her ending up at the  "Empire" to see Clara Butterworth in the "lilac Domino".  I enjoyed the play more than any other, for Clara Butterworth is a real actress.</p>
