<p>I struck trouble as soon as I reached the hospital on account of having somebodys wheel-chair It wasn't my fault. I found the chair in the ward and in it went for a tour round the ward to see if I could find anyone who had been in the 3rd Battalion</p>
<p>Arriving at "A" Ward where all the double amputation cases were kept somebody taking me unawares that is coming up at the back of the chair laid hands on it. I foolishly lost control of myself.</p>
