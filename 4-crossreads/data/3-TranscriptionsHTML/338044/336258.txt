<p>2nd September:- Saturday<br/>A beautiful day &amp; just the day to be out. The casualties are still very heavy for the 2nd Division &amp; there's tons of work. The news in the paper is making us all feel. Roumania's intervention is making things fly &amp;  they're doing great work against Austria.
