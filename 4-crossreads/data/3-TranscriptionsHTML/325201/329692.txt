<p>July 3rd<br/>"I'll 'ave a little of your jam if you don't mind". He promptly begins to hog into our jam when Bernard, who was also having breakfast near by, neatly, deliberately &amp; with no little aplomb, kicks him in the back of the neck. Wonderful horse!<br/>Apparently it must have been a caterpillar which disturbed us last night as no one got bombed.<br/>I am a back number  now I am afraid. I got orders last night to go over</p>
