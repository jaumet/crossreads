
<p>MONDAY, MAY 12, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Went into town early</p>
<p>&amp; left my kit at cloak room.</p>
<p>Got mine &amp; Linda&#39;s tickets ect</p>
<p>Had lunch out with Kieth</p>
<p>&amp; went &amp; saw Barry after.</p>
<p>Met Linda &amp; M<sup>r</sup>&nbsp;Lloyd &amp; had</p>
<p>dinner at Hill&#39;s &amp; caught</p>
<p>the 6.50 train up from Sydney</p>
<p>Peter joined us at Hornsby.</p>
