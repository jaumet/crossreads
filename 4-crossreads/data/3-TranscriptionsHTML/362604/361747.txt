<p>Australian Red Cross<br/>Bulford<br/>15/9/18</p>
<p>Dear Dad,</p>
<p>I am answering your letters of 30/6, 7/7 &amp; 14/7.  It is Sunday as usual &amp; I have finished rather early.  I intended writing this afternoon but I met an acquaintance in here &amp; He was telling me a bit about life on the other side.  He was lying in a Hospital gassed when the Germans raided all the Hospitals by Aeroplane &amp; hundreds of Patients, Orderlies &amp; Nurses were killed.
