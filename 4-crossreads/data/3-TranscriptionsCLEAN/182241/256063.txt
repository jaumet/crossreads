  
 Jan 9th Went across to the beach @ the Wady Nahr Sukereir where a vessal is being discharged the beach was teeming with natives engaged in the work & was one of the most animated & noisy secens I have witnessed here, 
 The vessal lay out about 1/2 mile & a large number of small craft were ferrying the cargo ashore, 
 When the bows grounded a line was hooked on & about 100 natives hauled the craft inshore Imense dumps were formed which were being transported by thousands of camels,
