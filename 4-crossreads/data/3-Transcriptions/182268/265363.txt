<p>Saturday 20/10/17. Spent day buying clothes and then at Wandsworth, went to the Coliseum</p>
<p>Sunday 21/10/17. Went to Australian Club at Piccadilly and had lunch &amp; dinner there with Hardie. Wrote Doff</p>
<p>Monday 22/10/17. Went to see &quot;a Kiss or two&quot; and then to the Bamfords for the night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 23/10/17. Came to London in the Evening to book seats&nbsp; returned with Patie to Malden.</p>
<p>Wednesday 24/10/17&nbsp; Took Patie &amp; Hetty to &quot;Arlette&quot; and then left them went</p>
