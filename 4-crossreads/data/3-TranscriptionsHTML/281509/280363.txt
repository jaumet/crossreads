<p>20 May 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Pass round Paris in early hours thro' Versailles, oval lake statue St Cyr. aeroplanes wrecked, hangars aeroplane over train.  French woods. <br/>Arrive at Armiens at mid-day. on to Epaple &ndash; our destination as things fell out. arrive 5 p.m. disentrain. march thro, hospital to camp out on sand dunes. Recover bags &amp; blankets. erect tent scratch for tea, to bed tired &amp; worn out with my cold.</p>
