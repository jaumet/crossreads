<p>July 13 sides is plainly visible. There's a demented chap in our hut who makes a darned nuisance of himself, but he goes into Hospital tonight. Its jolly hard luck &ndash; especially when a man gets blown up with a shell.</p>
<p>14 Dumped my stick &amp; pinched another from the woods, but its too heavy so guess I'll have to buy a decent one.</p>
