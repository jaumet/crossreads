<p>10<br/>"Who are you" we ask of the moving dragon of shadows as it passes<br/>"The twenty seventh battalion" it answers<br/>"Going in." we enquire<br/>Ah that, going in, what does it mean<br/>We know, so does every mortal member of that great creeping shadow of the night.<br/>Going in &ndash; each one to do his bit in the trenches<br/>Going in &ndash; each one to the battle<br/>Going in &ndash; some to deeds of daring and to glory<br/>Going in &ndash; many to that given door which closing opens no more<br/>The answer comes in a single word from the ceaseless moving shape<br/>"yes'</p>
<p>Much love to all. No letters yet<br/>Love to you children and mother<br/>Geoff</p>
