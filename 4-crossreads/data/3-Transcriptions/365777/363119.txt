<p>some few miles beyond Amiens on the Albert road where we rest our horses, have a bit to eat ourselves. here we rest for a couple of hours whilst the C. O. goes forward to [reconnoitre &amp; select a position for the batteries.</p>
<p>Midday finds our wagon lines in the little village of Bonney], close to the town of Corbie &amp; the guns in position on the top of the hill which rises from the river that flows alongside of Bonney.</p>
