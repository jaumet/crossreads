<p>Friday 19<br/>Good Bye for a while to the good old 10th<br/>Jackie the first man of that brigade to leave for the firing line<br/>Saturday 20<br/>Aboard the Cunard liner S.S Ivernia, at Alexandria left T.E K last night at 7 minutes past 10 still going strong, feeling sleepy<br/>Sunday 21<br/>Bound for France, left Alex. This morning, a destroyer on the port side with cruiser in the distance, Manly to the Mediteranean a long way<br/>I am getting nearer to this "ere" war" by jove - yes for we have to wear life belts, night and day</p>
