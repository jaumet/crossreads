<p>a picturesque one. One our way we passed through Amiens the town which was saved by the Aussies</p>
<p>It has had a miraculous escape compared with other towns.</p>
<p>Now it is full of life again and busy as all the civilians are back and carrying on as if no war ever existed. They realise what the Aussies have done are very thankful and cannot do enough for us.</p>
<p>Oct 10th -18<br/>Moved of again at 9AM and still making use of canal path we covered our last 25 miles in good</p>
