 He also went on the boat-deck. I sneaked up there to watch the pillow fight with all the Doctors. 
 We held our "smoko" & it was bonza. Some more good talent came to light. The nurses & Doctors had a dance on their deck. I & 6 other chaps had to go up & prepare the floor & move all the chairs etc that had been put there for the sports. I had a good chat to the nurses.
