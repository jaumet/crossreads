
train Great Western Railway arrived London 12.40 pm, took the whole of the remainder of the day getting fixed up at Headquarters.  Found accommodation at Shaftesbury Hotel.
Thursday 3.1.18Again had to go to Headquarters.  Spend other part of the day about city.  Met "Lad" Jarvie at H'qrs.  Went to "Zig Zag" at the Hippodrome at night.
Friday 4.1.18Day passed quietly in London.  Went to "Maid of the Mountains" at night.  Sent home Cable
Saturday 5.1.18Left London 11 Am (Paddington) for Penzance, joined my dear nurse at Bristol
