The following <strike>is</strike> is a stanza of a French song equivalent to our "Tipperary". &ndash;</p>
<p>"Apres la guerre est finie<br/>Soldats Anglais partis<br/>Mam'selle Francais<br/>Beaucoup pleurer,<br/>Apres la guerre est finie!"</p>
<p>Raucus voices, some of the owners being "well "vin-blanced"+, chanted the above popular verse very frequently this evening, or the following adaptation of it, wherein the ingenuity of the Australian in twisting and localizing things, is apparent:-</p>
<p>"Apres la guerre finie<br/>Soldat Australien depart<br/>Mam'selle Francais<br/>In the family way,<br/>Souvenir Australien."</p>
