<p>May, 1915<br/>Monday 3<br/>Just received long letter from Daisy dated 28 March &amp; yours of April 4 with Grazier &amp; Australasian, so glad to hear all is well at home &ndash;<br/>I posted more snapshots &ndash; some of myself, hair cut a la jail bird &amp; moustache you can hardly recognize.<br/>Went Heliopolis where all our wounded are from &ndash; Sister Myra Nyse also there poor girl 13 weeks in bed from Pneumonia.<br/>You will have news in Australia of Casualties long before we see the correct list -</p>
