<p>guard 12.30 very hot plenty of feed here returned to camp again 3.30 saddled up went to beach for a swim  place we went was to rocky could only have a wash there was a beautiful fresh water spring on the beach I had a drink the best water I have had since I have been on the desert it was like ice filled water bottle returned to camp had tea turned in 7.30 cool night circle around the moon  Friday Mar. 30.3.17 stand to 5A.M put saddle on had to take it off again to getting rugs  handed in there is a lot of messing somehow had breakfast rifle inspection 8.15 very warm the 4th Brigade L H are passing</p>
