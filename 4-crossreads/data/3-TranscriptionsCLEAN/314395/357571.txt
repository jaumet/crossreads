 That's a general summary of the situation & might give some idea of war.  The tanks - I never saw in action but have thoroughly examined the half-dozen which are bogged & abandoned in the ruins around Flers. 
 Now another explanation of a point on which you do not seem too clear.  Every bty. has a certain number of men trained to the use of the technical instruments and all kinds of signalling.
