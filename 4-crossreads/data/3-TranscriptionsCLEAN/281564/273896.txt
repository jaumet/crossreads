  
 About 11 o'clock the rest returned after accomplishing nothng but catch a pig with the cas-cas & we returned tired out with a very tall tale of cannibals & cannons & precipices & impregnable swamps & bush. 
 Tuesday 20th Oct Went for wood into the bush. 
 Wednesday 21st Guard 2 M.S. 
 Thursday 22nd Oct  Went and helped to put up the new wireless pole. After much tugging & swearing the first section was raised & by 5 P.M. the second in position for hauling. 
 Friday 23rd Oct Tried to haul up the rest of the pole & succeeded in getting half up by 5 P.M.
