<p>Feb. Friday 16 1917</p>
<p>The thaw has set in in earnest all the snow is melted &amp; now slush &amp; mud are everywhere. Rain has set in again too. T  There is a green tinge appearing over the ground, the young grass shooting.<br/>A furious bombardment on our left began about 4 a.m. last night, probably a hop out by the Tommies<br/>Three weeks in the line today</p>
