
18/4/17Section pegging out position for Defences round Bievilliers.  Very cold windy wet weather.
19thSec. standing by today.  Expect to leave early in morning for unknown destination.  Reported French have captured 19,000 prisoners and 90 guns.
20thNo. 2 Section marched out of camp & proceeded to "Bazentin" followed later by the whole company and camped for the night.  Nice fine weather.
21stMarched to Becourt & took up camp there.  Expect to leave here soon.
22ndNice fine day.  Went into Albert with Sgts. Banks & Barnes.  A few civilians coming back.  Saw Reg. Monaghan.  Still good news coming in from all allied Fronts.
23rdA little training & camp Fatigue.
24thNothing much doing.  Training & Fatigues.
25thCarrying on as yesterday.
26thNothing fresh doing.
27thJust a little Coy. Fatigues & Drilling.
28thWent to Albert & saw a Picture Show, wasn't too bad.  Nothing fresh going to report.
29thStill out resting.  Nothing fresh of importance to note.
30thDoing a little fatigues & Drill.
1-5-17Same routine as yesterday.
2ndNothing fresh going.  Having beautiful weather.
3rdStill having lovely weather.  Sections doing Pontooning & fatigues.
