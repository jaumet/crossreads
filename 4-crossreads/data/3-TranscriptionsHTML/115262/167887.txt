<p>Monday 30 April 1917.</p>
<p>On leave. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Caught 10.45 am train and went as far as Dandenong. &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>In afternoon went along Melbne road for walk for a mile or so. Came back at 5 pm. &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>An enjoyable walk too&nbsp;and a fine day. &nbsp;</p>
<p>7.46&nbsp;pm&nbsp;train to City. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Stayed at Federal Palace Hotel &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Crook during night.</p>
