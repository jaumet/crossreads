<p>9th M.G. Coy No 15<br/>Intelligence Report from 6 am 14th to 6 am 15th:-<br/>Right Sector:- No 5 gun fired &ndash; indirect &ndash; from I 12 Central to I 12 d 0/7 X Roads, Trenches, etc. at irregular intervals from 9.45 to 12.15. Result unknown.<br/>No 6 fired from I 10 b 2/1 to I 17 a 8/4 to I 18 c 3/0 Road from LA BLEUE to BASTROU, irregularly from midnight to 2.30 am.<br/>No damage done to M.G. Positions during enemy bombardment. Enemy M.G's active during night on front line.<br/>Left sector:- No 7 (S.P.X.) fired indirect on I 6 a 5/0 Road and dump at INTACT FARM. (from 5 to 11 pm) Result unobserved. <br/>Day was dull &amp; hazy, early evening and night drizzly &amp; dark, morning, moonlight  Wind light.</p>
<p>B. J Carey Capt<br/>9th M.G. Coy<br/>15.12.16</p>
