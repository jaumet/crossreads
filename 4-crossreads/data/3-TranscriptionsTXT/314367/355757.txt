 By Jove it was funny &ndash; I laughed till I cried &ndash; Fancy taking white cockatoos to Australia!! &ndash; He wanted them up on the poop &ndash; Bumpus &amp; I swore we'd send them adrift inside 10 minutes &amp; we would too &ndash; Eventually he got them away forward &amp; pays a man to look after them &ndash; After this &ndash; quiet &ndash; cider &ndash; at Walker's expense - &amp; bed &ndash;
