Battalion  This man was taken prisoner by a german and while he was taking him away, our soldier had the german with his fist, a fight ensued, our man got the best of it, succeeded in getting the germans rifle and bayoneted him through the neck.  He then returned to our trenches triumphantly bringing the rifle with him as a souvenir.  The rifle is lighter than ours.
