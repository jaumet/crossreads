By the time this reaches you, you will understand the price that some have paid. 2 6/9/16: And now for the news. Well, I have just had my name taken-- and rumour has it     well, more that that or I should not write is, that I may be coming home shortly. Of course I could do lighter work,but their policy is to give that to the Tommies. When we were out in the Field it seemed as though settling down to civil life would be an easy thing.
