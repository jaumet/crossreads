 Apr 10th - 18 At dawn of day we arrived at our entraining station and entrained in miserable weather as it was raining unceasingly and being made worse by the strong cold winds. 
 We arrived at Steenwerck near Hazebrouck early next morning and felt pretty miserable, after a sleepless night.
