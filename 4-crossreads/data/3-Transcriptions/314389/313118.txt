<p>Friday, July 21st:<br/>Arrive to quarters about 4 a.m.  We march to billets at Albert where we rest for the day. Retire 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Saturday, July 22nd:<br/>Rise 8 a.m. &hellip;.</p>
<p>Sniped 9 a.m.  Shot in thigh.  Taken to Dressing Station, thence to 5th General Hospital, Rouen.</p>
<p>Died July 28th, 1916.</p>
<p>Buried, St. Sever Cemetery, Rouen.</p>
