 Under an hour the prophet was vomiting badly himself &amp; then came to me for a cure &ndash; I told him I hadn't one &ndash; I've just had a bottle of Tasmanian Cider &ndash; A most excellent drink &ndash; I had no idea we had it &ndash; 
 Coaling is just starting &amp; we shall be dirty before its over &ndash; Niggers coal us &ndash; Mr Bumpus views this with disgust  - 'Kiki &ndash; Smoke-O &ndash; Rest O' &ndash; says Mr B &amp; winked solemnly &ndash; "Better use our own men" We had a thunderstorm last eveg. Temp at 6 am was 89o F &ndash; Generally its like a wet February in Q-land with a breeze from the NW &ndash; N.W. monsoonal weather &ndash; 
 Sunday. Dec 20-14. Since I last wrote a few things have happened &ndash; of gossiping interest mainly &ndash; About noon on Friday we went up alongside the Eastern who lay at the Rabaul pier &ndash; where the old Berrima lay in Sept &ndash; We were to take coal from her &ndash; This interesting but dirty operation continued all Friday aftn. &amp; eveg. till 2 am Saty. &amp; finally ended about 1 pm on the Saty. Slow work but we couldn't help that &ndash; Any rig is allowed &ndash; Bathing costume is a favorite one &ndash; Shouting &ndash; Singing &ndash; Language &ndash; It's an ordered pandemonium of half naked men &ndash;
