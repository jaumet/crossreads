<p>here, they belong to the Mining Batt. &amp; are tunneling under the German lines, they reckon the whole line is mined Ypres to Armienties &amp; is almost ready to blow up, there will be something doing then I'll bet.</p>
<p>Great big dugouts are being built along the line they are about 20 feet deep &amp; are big enough some of them to hold a hundred men nicely.</p>
<p>The trenches here are in a most disgraceful state, the Canadians don't seem to have made any attempt to keep them up to the scratch, you can wade through mud &amp; slush a foot deep &amp; if you dig down you will come to duckboards &amp; galvanized iron that they have let be swamped &amp; in some places you can dig them</p>
