<p>(No.3.)<br/>Troopship Kyarra<br/>Melbourne<br/>4th December 1914<br/>Dearest old Mrs,<br/>I received your short letter advising me that you had written to Adelaide &ndash; I will not of course get that letter as we go straight on to Fremantle. Our ultimate destination is not yet known some think we will go to Egypt but the majority fancy it will be France.<br/>We are expecting to get away from here tomorrow Saturday afternoon &amp; we will all be pretty glad as we are tired of Melbourne. I called in to see Charlie Patterson yesterday and got &pound;10 from him for which I gave him</p>
