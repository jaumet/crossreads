Put the morning in drilling etc in the afternoon the Coy. were marched down to Hopoutre to get a bath but only cold water was on so very few had a wash, as soon as the men were out we collected them marched them home without our officers we knew this would not go unnoticed so it proved for this evening the Capt came round wanted to know why we did it etc, we told him because we thought it was the best thing to do rather than let the men stand about in the rain we had a good 5 miles to walk
