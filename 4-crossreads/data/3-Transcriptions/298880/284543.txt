<p>Mon 6-11-16</p>
<p>We arrived here at Ribemont yesterday per boot sore footed and utterly tired out. We were allotted to our billet a very drafty one but with comfortable bunks made out of <strike>of</strike> wire netting. This morning we awoke feeling jolly sore and stiff after our hard Somme experience. The French have retaken Fort Vaux without a casualty. The Italians are still tuning up the Austrians in the Carso region. The sun managed to glimmer through the low hanging clouds today, but it is still very <strike>c</strike> chilly.</p>
