<p>Thursday 6 September</p>
<p>There has been little or nothing to report for some time, hence the vacant pages.  There has been the usual daily paper run to St. Omer a distance of 10 kilos.  At times there might be two runs a day to Bailleul with swabbings for the Laboratory.  Called at the Estaminet in Strazeele where we had such a good time whilst en route from Armentieres to the Somme last October.  The weather has been very changeable &ndash; Perhaps fine for a few days then wet for a few days.
