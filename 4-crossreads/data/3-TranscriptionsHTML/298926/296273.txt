<p>3rd Wet and muddy. Went on a route march and got wet through.  It appears our artillery <br/>have failed and we are going to have "Pommy Artillery" and are leaving within the next <br/>10 days.</p>
<p>4th We have been issued with new clothing throughout and are sending some to <br/>relations.</p>
<p>5th Inspections by company officers and colonel.</p>
<p>6th Floundering about in the mud, supposed to be on a route march.<br/>Came home with wet feet</p>
