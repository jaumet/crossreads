<p>January, 1915<br/>Saturday 16<br/>We marched over the desert to the rifle range &amp; the remainder of the Batt fired their practice. We did not work hard &amp; then marched back about three o'clock. Wrote to Flo. Every man issued with a field service dressing containing fine gauze, bandage wool &amp; waterproof.</p>
<p>Sunday 17<br/>Church parade Methodist<br/>Equipment inspection</p>
