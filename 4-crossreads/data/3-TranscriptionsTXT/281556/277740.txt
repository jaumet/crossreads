
8th. Thursday.Weather fair front quiet. Midnight artillery stunt by our guns.
9th. Friday.Rec'd 3 more Reinforcements for platoon.
10th. SaturdayFritz shelled us heavily again killing several.
Wet night & usual artillery stunt.
11th. Sunday.Heavy rain. Mail in. Battn Hqrs shelled.
12th. Monday.Heavy bombarding on both our flanks. 1st Bgde raided Fritz.
13th. Tuesday.To wet for engineering fatigue & too much shelling to sleep. (on 6th Brigade)
