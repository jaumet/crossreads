<p>France<br/>15.4.17</p>
<p>Dear Mother and Father<br/>It is now some time since I sent my last letter home and I hope you have not been anxious about me as I have been fairly well all through this trip. Since coming back from hospital I have been in the line pretty constant and could not keep up my usual correspondence.<br/>I am pleased to say that lately I have been getting my letters regularly and so have been in touch with home.
