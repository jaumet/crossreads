<p>NOV. &nbsp; SUNDAY 12 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; dull. Took two horses out to exercise them &amp; graze them on the plain. It was great to get a ride round on your own again, something like old times. Stayed in the huts all afternoon &amp; got ready for the big parade tomorrow.</p>
