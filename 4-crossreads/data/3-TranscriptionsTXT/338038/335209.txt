 P.S. Look out for some Photos. I will send a few along as soon as I have a chance of registering them. 
 Had a great concert here the other night in the Y.M.C.A. tent. Colonel Rosy in great form, &amp; sang two songs. Our Brigade again to the fore in latest list of honors. Major Burgess of 9th Battery awarded DSO and Capt Waite (Adjutant) a Military Cross. Not bad!
