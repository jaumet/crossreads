<p>&amp; saluted the Colonel, there was only the Major Adjutant &amp; himself there, he started to question me, &amp; I was wondering what on earth he was driving at, when he had finished, he asked me if I would care to enter an Officers Training School" I nearly fell through the floor, as you may imagine, I thanked him for the offer &amp; accepted it.</p>
