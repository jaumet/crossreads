<p>&amp; asking them to respect our graves&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; In one place they had a gramophone set so as soon as they touched it it would play "The Turkish Patrol" not bad was it, &amp; in other places the tables were all left just as if they were going to sit down to dinner, &amp; had been called away hurriedly, but as soon as they lifted the bottles of wine a bomb went off what a shock they would get  The Turks never jerried that the birds had flown till morning broke, &amp; to make sure they gave it a terrific bombardment &amp; rifle fire, Ill bet in one way they were mad especially the officers to think they had let us get off</p>
