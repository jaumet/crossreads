<p>[Letter from AIF to Mr Giles]<br/>H.W.</p>
<p>AUSTRALIAN IMPERIAL FORCE</p>
<p>Base Records Office, A.I.E.F.<br/>Victoria barracks.<br/>Melbourne. 18th September 1916.</p>
<p>Dear Sir,</p>
<p>I now beg to advise you that information has been received to the effect that No. 2837, Private A=Giles, was admitted to the Ist.. Eastern General Hospital 25/8/16 suffering from Gun Shot Wound (mild).</p>
<p>His postal address will therefore be:-</p>
<p>No. 2837 Corporal A.C. Giles,<br/>"Wounded" Ist. Australian Infantry,<br/>C/o Australian Imperial Force Headquarters,<br/>130 Horseferry Road,<br/>Westminster, LONDON. S.W.</p>
<p>Any further information coming to hand will be promptly transmitted.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>J.M.LEAN.<br/>Major.<br/>Officer i/c Base Records.</p>
<p>[Addressed to]<br/>Mr. J.C.Giles,<br/>20 Marshall St.,<br/>STANMORE. N.S.W.</p>
