<p>Dec</p>
<p>so spent some time in a Picture Show</p>
<p>Tuesday 25<br/>Went to St Pauls &amp; several other places. Lunched at Trocadro quite a swell place 4 courses</p>
<p>Wed 26th<br/>Wandered about considerably &amp; saw a good deal Aunt stayed at home</p>
<p>Thursday  27<br/>Westminster Abby etc left London at 7.55 reached camp about 11 PM. Have not bothered about details of all the things I did &amp; saw on leave. The days are short for sightseeing especially</p>
