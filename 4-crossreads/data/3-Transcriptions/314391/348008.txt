Inverness battery opened fire   &amp; drove them temporarily off the hill. During the night enemy threw up a good deal of new trench on this point.<br/>1100 2nd L.H. Bde report. About 0900 small party of Turks attempted to rush one of our advanced posts but were driven back by rifle fire. Considerable trench work has been done on long ridge about 1000 yds N of 265.
