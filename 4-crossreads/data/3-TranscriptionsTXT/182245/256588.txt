 MARCH 1917 
 14 WEDNESDAY 
 Fine most of the day, but rained 
 a little during the day. Cleaning 
 harness all the morning and 
 we went up &amp; brought out our 
 guns from the old pits, &amp; brought 
 them back to the waggon lines 
 for a couple of days spell before 
 going to Ploegsteert (Plug Street) 
 The 31 st  Battery relieved us at 
 our pits.
