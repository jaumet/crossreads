<p>The Company is still <strike>tak</strike> working at night on the advanced road work</p>
<p>Bert Ridewood was killed up there &ndash; Devine &amp; Bill Baird have been wounded while we have had over 20 horses killed, a number wounded &amp; lost some 30 waggons &ndash;</p>
<p>Quiney &amp; Macrow have been given the M.C. while Humphreys, Col Hall, L. Collen S. Dunstan &amp; Percy Dixon have got the Military Medal.</p>
<p>They dropped a bomb in the Company lines a few days ago &amp; killed the Majors Hack &amp; wounded 15 other horses luckily no men touched &ndash; It was the worst</p>
