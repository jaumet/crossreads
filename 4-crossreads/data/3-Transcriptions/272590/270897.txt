<p>"'Tis better to be nobly remembered than nobly born."<br/>Ruskin.</p>
<p>The Ashfield District Band will render selections before the Concert, from 7.30 till 8.<br/>At 8 p.m. Concert arranged by Mr. Percy Herford.<br/>At 9.10 p.m. His Worship the Mayor of Ashfield, Alderman F.O. Hedger, J.P., will address the returned men, and present the certificates given by the Municipality.<br/>After which there will be dancing in the town Hall;  Ms. C. &ndash; Alderman G. Watson, J.P., and Mr. C. Winn, J.P.  Music by the Railway and Tramway Institute Orchestra, under the conductorship of Mr. Leo. M. Grouse.<br/>Refreshments provided for the Sailors and Soldiers, and their friends, in the Supper Room, attended by members of the Ashfield, North Ashfield and Western Suburbs Voluntary Aid Detachments.</p>
<p>Committee:- Chairman, John Russell.<br/>Hon. Treasurer:  O.W. Brain.<br/>Hon. Secretary:  S.H. Mawson.</p>
