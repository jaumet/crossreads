<p>5 August 1918 Monday<br/>Arrived midday &amp; went to Anglo American Hospital where I found Dolly as lively as a cricket no trouble at all &amp; both doing well  She is such a strong girl never felt anything much &amp; never took any Chloroform-  I am sleeping at the Flat &amp; go out each day twice to the Hospital.<br/>Cabled you &amp; Mrs Ish this evening.  We are christening the Kiddy Kathleen Dorothy- the former after good old Day.</p>
