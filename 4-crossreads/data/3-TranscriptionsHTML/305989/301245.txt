<p>Sunday 25 February 1917<br/>Left Kantarah by the 5.45 train &amp; came back to Bir-el-Abd &amp; then into Bayud.</p>
<p>Monday 26 February 1917<br/>On day stableman.</p>
<p>Tuesday 27 February 1917<br/>Played N.Z. football &amp; <strike>won</strike> played a draw, hard match.</p>
<p>Wednesday 28 February 1917<br/>Nothing doing in particular, had to get my flipper dressed.</p>
<p>Thursday 1 March 1917<br/>Quiet day:  Billy Ahearn got his com.</p>
<p>Friday 2 March 1917<br/>Billy A'hearn left for Bir-el-Abd.  Very windy day.  M.G. &amp; H.Q. v B. Sq. &ndash;  won 9-3.</p>
<p>Saturday 3 March 1917<br/>Got my hand bandaged in morning.  A Sq. v M.G. &amp; Q.V.</p>
