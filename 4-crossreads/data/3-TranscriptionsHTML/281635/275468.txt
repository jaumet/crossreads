<p>Has been over every night during the last week.<br/>20/5/18 Several trainloads of American troops passing through going towards Lumbres.<br/>22/5/18 Wrote home to Uncle Fred<br/>27/5/18 After lunch walked over the hill to Bilques.<br/>29/5/18 Wrote to Base Post Office.<br/>30/5/18 Recd letter from Uncle Fred. Nice fine warm day &amp; foggy early this morning. Very heavy artillery fire during night.
