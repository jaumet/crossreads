<p>Sun. Apr. 9th -16<br/>I was made Brigade Orderly and not a bad job either.</p>
<p>Mon. Apr. 10th -16<br/>Witnessed first air-duel between balloon and 2 aeroplanes about 4 miles away.</p>
<p>Tues. Apr. 11th -16<br/>Horses excercised in morning and inoculated during afternoon.</p>
<p>Thur. Apr. 13th -16<br/>Heavy Snow storm.  SOME cold believe me.</p>
<p>Fri. Apr. 14th -16<br/>Still snowing on and off.  Groomed horses in a heavy fall.</p>
<p>Sat. Apr. 15th -16<br/>Warned to be ready to leave</p>
