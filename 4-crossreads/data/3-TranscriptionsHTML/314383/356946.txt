<p>my heart is in a very bad way due to the straining of a valve, but I hope to get home pretty soon now.  It's heaven here to have the gentle touch of a woman's hand as she ministers to one. What a curse this world would be without women.</p>
<p>Jan 27th 1919<br/>Egypt.</p>
<p>Just received notification today that I've been award the D.S.O. for the Urmiah stunt. Left Bombay Jan 1st to join the 24th but stopped here &amp; am due to leave for Aust any day.  What will home mean after the last 4 years.</p>
