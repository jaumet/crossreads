<p>4/9/17 cont<br/>Germans say that a telegram came into camp about the fall of Riga but they do not believe it. said  I expect they want another war loan.  Bread issue raised to day from 6 &frac12;  to loaf to 5 &frac14; per loaf the difference is hardly noticable.  We are off to another camp tomorrow at noon.  Where to?</p>
