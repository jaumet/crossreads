<p>Anzac Army Corps which is controlled by General Godley.</p>
<p>19th May (Friday) Cool. Brushes issued for cleaning boots, buttons &amp; uniforms. All buttons &amp; boots have to be clean for every parade &amp; the boys don't like the idea. Lecture in the afternoon by Capt. Nance.</p>
<p>20th May (Saturday) Cool. On Cooks Fatigue. The camp was prepared for an inspection by Major General McKay but he didn't arrive.</p>
