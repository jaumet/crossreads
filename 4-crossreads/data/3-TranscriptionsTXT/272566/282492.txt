 Sunday 18 February 
 Went to the Wesleyan Chapel with Harringtons &amp; Edgbaston people afterwards had dinner at Uncle Charlie Allsop's, called at Auntie Lizzie's and had tea at Auntie Lunts.  Returned to Uncle Charlie's for a few hours then back to Ridding House with Bessie &amp; Madge.  At Auntie Lunts place there must have been 15 people this evening.  A crowd also gathered at Uncle Charlie's.
