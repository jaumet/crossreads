 on to the Hospital ship. It was a nice crisp trip across to Imbros. We called in at Cape Helles &amp; went right round into the Straits. We saw the ruins of Sed dol Bah Fort and went in near the Aiasiatic Coast. The celebrated SS "River Clyde" which was run ashore full of Troops was on the Beach a monument to the many brave lives that were lost in the landing which was effected from her. Every one will remember how she was run up &amp; all her sides opened to provide a quick exit for troops. Unfortunately the "Clyde" did not beach as expected &amp; it meant certain death to go out on the planks provided for the landing on the lighters.
