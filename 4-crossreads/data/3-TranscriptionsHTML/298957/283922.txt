<p>30th Jan (Sunday)<br/>Beautiful day. Lying on bank of Canal all day reading. New Zealand troops arrived including Artillery. On guard at night.</p>
<p>31st Jan (Monday) Another lovely cloudless day. On Guard. Artillery and long line of transport waggons crossed over the Canal. Cartloads of timber and limbers loaded with new stretchers &amp; material replacing that which was lost on Gallipoli also went across.</p>
<p>1st February (Tuesday) Whole unit went out for the day with tents, medical supplies</p>
