<p>Fri July 21st &ndash; 16<br/>Today the guns have been going from daylight to dark and since being here we are just beginning to realise what is ahead of us.</p>
<p>Sat July 22nd - 16<br/>At 8.50 this morning all batteries (with which the fields are covered) have started a continuous shelling for the purpose of barbed wire cutting.</p>
