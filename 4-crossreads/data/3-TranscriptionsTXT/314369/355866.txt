
30 July 1915Friday
Not much better this morning but get over the issue of rations all rightLie down nearly all day & feel better in the eveningGet orders to supply several Guards & fatigue parties for tomorrow. "C" coy finding  Bgde  dutiesRum 6 pm.
1915 July 31
Guards go on at 8 am &  so relieve me of my worry for the day.I go  &  have a look at the new 60 pounder field guns landed during the night  &  apparently intended for the big general advance to take place shortly
