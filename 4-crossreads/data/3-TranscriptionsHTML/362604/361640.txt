<p>We have had no mail for some time.  There aught to be some forthcoming in a few days time.  All the trees are out in blossom &amp; leaf now.  Very shortly we'll have beautiful May weather with us.  I want to go to the Island again on the 1st May.  Perhaps Ellis will come with me.  He has had no leave for a long while.  We have been so short of cash.  I have the wherewithal to buy a cup of Tea to-night.  But pay-day to-morrow &amp; I'll be set for about 12 days then.  I never seem to be able</p>
