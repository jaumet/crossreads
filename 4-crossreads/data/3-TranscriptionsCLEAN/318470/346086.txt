  
 4/5/17 Company Pontooning & general training. 
 5th Div. Sports today.  Horses were looking beautiful & turnover was very good. 
 6th Church Parade this morning.  Coy. played 55th Soccer in afternoon & won. 
 7th Company Pontooning & other training. 
 8th Too wet for training.  Getting ready for entraining. 
 9th Left Becourt  for  and per foot & train travelled to Vaulx & relieved 7th F. Coy. Engrs.  Lively Artillery firing along Line. 
 9th Very heavy Artillery firing along this Front.  Enemy firing a lot of shells on positions right back here. 
 10th Coy. doing a little training & a little Front line work.  12th, 15th Brigade & a Tommy Brigade hopped over. 
 11-5-17 Party from my Sec. out wiring tonight in Front of Line.  Very heavy bombardment by our Guns.  Expect hop over in morning
