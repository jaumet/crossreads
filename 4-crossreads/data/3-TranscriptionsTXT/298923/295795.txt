 9 May 1918 Thursday Leave.  Dined last night at Shepheards with Dolly as that is the place I met the little girl. Hot in Cairo now but they have cool rooms-   The little wife is sitting on my knee now &amp; joins in love to you all, someday we will walk into the old garden gate Love from F M  Address  Dolly to Anglo Egyptian Bank.
