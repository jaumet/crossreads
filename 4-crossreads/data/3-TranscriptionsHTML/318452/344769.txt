<p>but missed it by 10 minutes so turned towards St Omer about 85 miles distant I had not gone far before I got a lift in a fine minerva car, right ot St Omer. Here I found there was nothing till 8.30 it was now about 4 oclock. As I did not want to wait so long, I got on a Horse Hospital train (350 Horses, wounded &amp; sick) &amp; reached Calais at 8 PM. I stayed at Hotel Sauvage till Saturday.</p>
<p>Friday 12<br/>Visited Hospital &amp; found Col Green had returned to England I then visited the other hospitals in search of any of our men It was showery all day. Calais is rather a nice town &amp; its beach is very nice.</p>
<p>Saturday 13<br/>returned to unit rainy all day</p>
