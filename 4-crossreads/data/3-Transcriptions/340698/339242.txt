<p>0230. Entrained there for Liverpool arrived here at about midnight and were met at the station by private cars. Were driven out to a place called Oakdene near the village of Rainhill.</p>
<p>16/10/17<br/>This is a V.A.D. hos. and is first class. Did not get to bed until 3 this morning as I had to have wound dressed.</p>
