Saturday 24thYesterdays morning stunt was most successfull 2100 Prisoners counted up to t early in the evening   Advanced 11/2 miles on a 4 mile front. Some bombs droped about last night & a few shells on the road An Obsv Baloon came into our Valley this evening. It was lift up about 200 ft for the night & gave off a most pecular echo from when some of our guns fired. A fair lot of bombs dropped about last night none close
