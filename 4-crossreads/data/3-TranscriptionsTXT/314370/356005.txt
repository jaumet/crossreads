
April, 1916Saturday 1Soon after breakfast the men for the new school marched in.Keep coming in batches all day.After tea we went for a walk to Zeitoun
Sunday 2Men continue to come in all day.  I was on duty from 12 to 4 pm.No word from dad.At 4.30 pm I went into Cairo for a final ramble before starting work tomorrow.When I returned about 9.30 pm, I found a note from the store to come & get dad's bag as they were going to Tel-el-Kebir at 6 am tomorrow.  I got 'em.
