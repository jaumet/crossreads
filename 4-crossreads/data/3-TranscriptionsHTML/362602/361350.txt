<p>take a long time.  We have 4 or 5 cats &amp; one of then climbs up the rigging sometimes.  How is the garden &amp; the fruit trees?  Did the fruit ripen &amp; what sort of tomatoes did you get from those that were planted near the nectarine tree.  How is Sandy &amp; the cats.  How is Mrs. Allison getting on?</p>
<p>We are in the land of Golden Sunsets.  A couple of nights ago the whole of the heavens were a mass of gold.  I have never seen anything like it before.  We feel the abscence of fruit very much but we have plenty of lime-juice to keep us from scurvy.  We have nobody ill, nor have we had any ill since we left Sydney.
