<p>16th<br/>We left our kind friends and reached Rouen here by Serquigny and Oissel about 8 p.m.</p>
<p>We drove to the Hotel D'Angleterre and were soon comfortable again.</p>
<p>17th<br/>We went for a drive round the town after lunch in a taxi and through the big pine forest.  Thousands of these fine trees are being cut for war purposes.</p>
