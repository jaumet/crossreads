Here we found out that most of the ones arriving about this time went to a place called Tel-El-Kebir, where there was a big training camp &amp; which was situated about 100 miles from Nowhere in the middle of the Desert. About an hour after stopping we got "all Clear", &amp; away we went again.</p>
<p>Next morning we entered the Harbour at Port Said &amp; Dropped Anchor among all sorts of craft; from French Submarine Chasers to Big British Cruisers &amp; Dreadnoughts. No sooner had we dropped anchor; than word was received that we were to disembark, &amp; for an hour or two everything was hurry &amp; bustle.</p>
