
<p>MONDAY, MARCH 17, 1919.</p>
<p>17 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;1017</p>
<p>Fine with a very strong head</p>
<p>wind. On deck reading and</p>
<p>playing games all day. It was</p>
<p>too cold up there at night so</p>
<p>came down to bed &amp; read.</p>
<p>Days run 316 miles.</p>
<p>Got paid &pound;10 today</p>
