30/8/15 outside the breakwater a heavy sea was on and the pilot boat and tug were tossing very heavily. The process of dropping the pilot full of excitement, he swinging about on the end of a rope ladder a few feet above the water, and then the drop into the pilot boat at the exact pychological second.
10pm. We are bowling along in pitch darkness with all lights out on account of submarines. Two machine guns are mounted with crews standing by all the time and guards with ball are also on sentry. It is a quaint feeling to know that any minute may mean a good big torpedo into the centre of the keel
