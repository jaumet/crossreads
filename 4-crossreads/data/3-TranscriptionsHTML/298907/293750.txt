<p>13th. Have had a fairly quiet day nothing doing except a couple of runs into town to take a few snaps of the streets for it has been a very nice bright afternoon &amp; suitable for photography, its a wonder I have not been grabbed for I have taken my snaps right under the policemans very nose &amp; I know I am not supposed to have a camera in this district without a permit for it is a big munition area but nothing venture nothing have is a true old saying, &amp; so far I have come off alright.</p>
