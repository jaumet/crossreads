It was awfully hot &ndash; tearing about in the desert with stretchers &ndash; we also had the Camels out with  stretchers  catalecs attached. Got back to Camp about 12 noon &amp; had a good old swim. The whole thing was an eye opener to me to see two  Divisions  complete &ndash; it was just men &ndash; men horses &amp; men everywhere &ndash; I daresay there must have been some 38,000 men mustered. 
 7/5/16  Sunday 
 Terribly hot day, hardly a breeze. I went off to first Mass &amp; Communion &ndash; got back in here to bolt a piece of bread &amp; just fall in for General Church parade &ndash; so I had to go to 8.30 Mass again. Were busy all day with men or malingerers going before the Medical board &ndash; most of them were ordered back to their units.
