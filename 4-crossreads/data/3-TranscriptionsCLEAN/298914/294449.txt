
15-1-17Go for a swim and find it very cold. Turkish mine exploded by gunfire.16-1-17Swim again.  Nothing doing.18-1-17Leave to Cairo to be granted. Great fuss over the rum issue.  We are supposed to line up & drink it before an officer because someone gave some to some natives & got them drunk.  All refuse to line up.19-1-17Nothing doing.20-1-17ditto.21-1-17ditto -24-1-17Pay day - draw 4/10/-25-1-17Orders to move, but Capt squares division.27-1-17Visit WDJ about generator.28-1-17Charging accumulators29-1-17[indecipherable]30-1-17Bombed by two Taubes bombs dropping within 100 yds of Station. 5 horses killed in a small Tommy section passing. Cut a hole through for mast.1-2-3-4Nothing doing.  Lot of dust blowing about and being bright moonlight all are dwelling on a night raid.  Tanks arrive Mazar - Pom Pom guns on armoured train.6/2/16Take out trench sets & lose temper over camels which play up considerably7-2-17Go for a walk through El Arish taking photos.  Visit the old fort alongside the mosque.  This old fort has been shelled to a mere heap of stones from the sea by monitors & not a brick has been misplaced around the outside.  Remark accuracy in gunnery.  This fort commands a wide view.  Much talk about peace.8/2/17Photos and making small long wave set.9-2-17Out on Wadi El Arish with W7 trench Wireless set.
