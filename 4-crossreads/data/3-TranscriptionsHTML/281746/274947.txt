<p>Sept 21st 1918 to Dec 5th 1918</p>
<p>Sept 21st&nbsp;<u>1918</u></p>
<p>Walked on the hills. A lovely view of the valley of the Somme is got from these hills.</p>
<p>Had to do fatigue work fixing up a tumble down cottage as a [__llet] for the Major, this afternoon.</p>
<p>Walked along the banks of the River Somme. A number of Aussies were out on the river in a flat-bottommed boat fishing.</p>
