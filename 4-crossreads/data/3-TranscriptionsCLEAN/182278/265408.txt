
Wednesday 3/4/18  Moved by circuitous route to Meteren by Bus- billeted
Thursday 4/4/18  Heavy day entraining early in morning  bought some lace for Doff
Friday 5/4/18  Left Meteren at 3 am entrained at Caistre 6 am arrived Amiens 9 p.m. marched to Allonville
Saturday 6/4/18 marched to La Neuvelle & billeted for night.
Sunday 7/4/18 Marched to [Bazieux?] [Baisieux] & relieved Imlay's lot, moved forward to Support at night relieving Christie.
Monday 8/4/18  Raining heavily all night  Hun shelling all day  about 10 casualties
