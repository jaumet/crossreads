<p>March, 1915<br/>Thursday 25<br/>Started on return journey &amp; expect to Camp Maidi again tonight &ndash; Camped in the sand again, a beautiful starlight night like the Next: &amp; as I lay in my blankets I thought of you all at home.<br/>Friday<br/>Also got letter from Walter Campbell &amp; Lizzie Davy England - [indecipherable] had 5 inches rain at Xmas. Papers come from Mrs Cotton, &amp; Joe &amp; Mrs Ferrark. We hear <u>next</u> week we are really off, but I hope I am not committing myself &amp; so getting Diary stopped by Censor I will post this tonight -</p>
