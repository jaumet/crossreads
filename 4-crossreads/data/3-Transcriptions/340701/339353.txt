<p>7-4-16  Friday<br/>We passed a ship today the Neuralis probably a hospital ship.  A small bird like a canary came on board yesterday while we were out of sight of land we were also visited by Grasshoppers.  We were medically inspected today and informed that we would disembark on Sunday next.  The 17th will be duty company on that date.</p>
<p>9-4-16  Sunday<br/>Arrived at Port Suez this morning &amp; found all the transports that were at Colombo here &amp; in addition the two russian Transports. We did not land here but left at 6.00 P.M. for Alexandria.  Shiels said we</p>
