<p>The artillery only played on the German trench for 3 minutes, but if you had seen it you would have thought it had been playing for a week or so.  Well as soon as the 1st Batt got news the artillery had lifted on to the second line of german trenches they were up and at the Germans who were left in the front line, then the news came back to us that they had captured the first line and that was all we got the order to get over the top and at them well we had to cover 5 hundred yards to get to their front line and it was no easy job in the dark I also had a pick on  my shoulder which we used for digging our selves in with.</p>
