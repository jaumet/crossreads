 Monday 2 July 1917. 
 Reveille 4 a.m. Parade as usual. I am Mess Orderly today. Major Whitford gave us an address on 1 st &nbsp;and 2 nd &nbsp;battles of Gaza. I took notes &amp; a copy of his sketches. He has just arrived from the front &amp; is taking up position of Adjutant of A.C.B.F.A. 
 A trainload of Turkish prisoners came through here from Gaza this morning. 3 hospial trains came through yesterday &amp; today from the front.
