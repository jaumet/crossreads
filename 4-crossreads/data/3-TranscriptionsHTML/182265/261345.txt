<p>I spent the Sunday evening walking through the Gardens along the beach walks &amp; on the pier.</p>
<p>Yesterday morning (Monday) I walked the streets - bought a few small articles - then did the &quot;Circular Tally Ho&quot; drive - which takes one through Bournemouth into the country, along the River Stour &amp; back again in about 2 hours for 1/-. It was a beautiful drive &amp; thoroughly enjoyable.</p>
<p>Leaving Bournmouth at 2pm it began to rain heavily &amp; is still (Tuesday evening) very unsettled &amp; showery.</p>
<p>Arriving at Lyndhurst Road, an ambulance wagon brought in several officers - to the Camp.</p>
<p>Here we are in tents.&nbsp;Very bleak &amp; damp last night.&nbsp;</p>
<p>It promises to be wet again this evening. Am writing to Jess &amp; <s>home</s>&nbsp;</p>
