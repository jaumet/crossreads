<p>July, 1915<br/>Thursday 15<br/>Went to Mess to 17 Infantry Battalion, very hot all day &ndash;<br/>City of Cairo very quite now that Police close up 10 a.m.<br/>Pay day today I'm getting quite good at Piastres. <strike>Letter from James, Charlie is alright, but the Major is doing nothing.</strike></p>
