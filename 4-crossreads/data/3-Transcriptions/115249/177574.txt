<p>W.T.Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship: [indecipherable, possibly abbreviation for Head Quarters]</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship: T28</p>
<p>Date: 12 9 4</p>
<p>Time of despatch or receipt: 4 25</p>
<p>Comdr Beresford to Brigadier</p>
<p>The wireless engineer estimates that it would take 4 months to erect iron towers. Lieut. Bond reports 3 of his men wounded whom I am sending down to the Beach for transport to the Ship.</p>
<p>My men are somewhat exhausted &amp; I shall require provisions and water today.</p>
<p>I shall send what provisions &amp;&nbsp;water I have to the WT station at daylight &amp; await your instructions as to whether I am still to hold the station.</p>
<p>I have received a communication from Colonel Watson which states that he will junction with me on the Kabakaul Road leaving his present position. at Herbertshohe&nbsp;at 5 am today one of the wounded above referred to has succumbed Time received 2.45 a &nbsp;0425&nbsp;</p>
