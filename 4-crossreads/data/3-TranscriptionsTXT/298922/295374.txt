 Saturday 17 February 1917 
 Brass* 
 Been around on Patrol, saw *Brass Cartridge Cases &amp; dead Camels on the ground of old fights. 
 I was glad to get all the Home News ere this you will all be in Melbourne. 
 Foster Rutledge is the C.O. of Flying Corps here but I've never met him. 
 [The following paragraph crossed through] How strange that old Sir Sam should go to CANADA at his time of life. 
 Hard as it may seem I cannot possibly see poor JOE could leave you all, I know I have the best of it, still he knows  he is doing his job . I note dear old Day is sending me some SOKS. Thanks for information re Bank quite RICH I am &amp; get more pay as Major 30/- a day now.
