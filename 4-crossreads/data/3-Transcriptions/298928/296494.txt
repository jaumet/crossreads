<p>21st  Left rest camp to go back to Leddingham, there to <br/>reorganise.  It is a small scattered village 40 miles from Ypres. <br/>We went out in motor buses and were shelled going out.  We <br/>passed through Poperinghe, Steenworde, Caselle (army h'q'rs) <br/>and got to Leddingham 10 p.m.</p>
<p>23rd  billeted in barns of French peasants, and play a <br/>programme every afternoon.  Mac cooks for us so are living <br/>well.</p>
