<p>16 June 1915<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Continued work in reliefs   &amp; make good progress.<br/>Shrapnell drive the men out of the water during the afternoon</p>
<p>1915 June 17<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Inlying piquet<br/>A lot of rifle fire through the night but nothing comes of it   &amp; we do not have to turn out until 3 am   &amp; stand to arms until 4.30</p>
