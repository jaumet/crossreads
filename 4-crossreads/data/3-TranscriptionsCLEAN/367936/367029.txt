  
 Thursday 24 August 1916 Mess orderly for troop.  Grand concert in Y.M.C.A. in evening. General Cheval [Chauvel] and all the big wigs there. Wrote to Boys. 
 Friday 25 August 1916 B Squadron down for a swim.  Had a jolly good day. Met Barton from Wellington at the Canteen Momadehea [possibly Mahemdia].  He is in the 6th.
