
<p>FRIDAY, AUGUST 8, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Round Cow paddock</p>
<p>&amp; Mountain in morning &amp; lopping</p>
<p>in 4D in evening. Tickle poisoning</p>
<p>in Mountain &amp; Top paddocks.</p>
<p>Darby bust both his bits &amp; sinker</p>
<p>bars &amp; has to stop work while he</p>
<p>has them sent to Sydney &amp; fixed up.</p>
<p>He is down 150 ft [bi?].</p>
