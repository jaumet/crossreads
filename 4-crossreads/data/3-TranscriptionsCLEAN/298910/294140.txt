  
 In order to celebrate Anzac Day etc, we C. Section brought out a paper, with the title of "The Somme Chat" a real live paper, it consisted of some very fine Articles & puns on Officers & men & things in general. We expect other Sections to come out with one now & hit us off. 
 Thursday  26/4/17 
 Very nice day - Drilling all day - Good war news coming thro to us - some 80 Aeroplanes have been brought down this week - Very heavy bombardments on our front - Our Boy's 4th Division got as far as the Hindenburg line, but were driven back not being strong enough. Several pitched Battles in the open. 
 Friday  27/4/17 
 Very nice day - Section went for a route march to Meaulte. Good news still comes in from our front, of more Aeroplanes & Prisoners. 
 Saturday  28/4/17 
 Very nice day. On Picquet - Complemented on the way we turned out. General Hough gave us a surprise visit. Wrote to Mrs O'Kelly, M Single & Eileen. Big mail in but never got any.
