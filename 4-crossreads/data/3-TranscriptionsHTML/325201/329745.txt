<p>Oct 22nd<br/>My last night in pyjas &amp;   comfort, we move out at dusk the day after tomorrow I believe on a big stoush. All moving to be done at night henceforth which is particularly uncomfortable. I am getting too old for war.<br/>Oct 27th<br/>"Essani"<br/>We are at the second stage of this adventure now.<br/>We left the beach three days ago at 5 pm reached Abasan el Kabir at 9 pm stayed there until yesterday at 4 pm when we moved out here arriving here</p>
