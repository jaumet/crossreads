<p>Australian Red Cross</p>
<p>Bulford<br/>26/5/18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>Here is Sunday afternoon again.  I have been sewing &amp; washing Clothes (Hankys only of which there were 30) &amp; washing myself &amp; now I've 40 minutes before Tea-time I am going to walk to the village Church after Tea.  I've not been to the Kirk for months.  Ellis has gone out for a walk with a Sergeant &amp; Dick is playing Tennis.  I have received your letters of 17.3.18, 24/3/18 &amp; 31.3.18.  You have spoken about the banishment of snakes from "auld Ireland"</p>
