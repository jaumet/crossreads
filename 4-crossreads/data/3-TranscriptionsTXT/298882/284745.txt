 25th Dec. Xmas Day. 
 Today has been spent in trying to make a real happy Xmas for the men and I believe we have succeeded fairly well.  The Mairie of the town kindly allowed us the use two School Rooms for the men's dinner.  Tables and chairs were hired from Ordnance and crockery etc from various firms in Etaples.
