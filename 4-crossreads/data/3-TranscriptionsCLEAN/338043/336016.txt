Amiens - and between Cambrae and St. Quentin we arrive at Le Cateau at 9 p.m.During the journey  evidence of the good news is shown by the display of flags & bunting - and the hope -Last of all are the German prisoners working on the roads, who wave and smile as we pass - Our own lorry is well decorated and attracts considerable attention -Spend night in shell shattered & deserted shop -
