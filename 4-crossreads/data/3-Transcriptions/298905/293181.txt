<p>ed &amp; went down to breakfast; where I found them waiting for me.<br/>After breakfast Mr Duke took me all round the town. She is a fair sized place &amp; everything remarkably neat &amp; clean, the houses to are very fine &amp; solid, &amp; most of them have ivy climbing all over them, he took me all round the promenade which is a very fine one there were hundred's of kids on the beach building castles with the sand &amp; so on, he also took me round to his offices &amp; workshops, we then went back for dinner, after dinner we went to Portsmouth the great naval base she is a big town &amp; is well fortified as we all know, from the station we went onto a ferry boat &amp; had a trip over to Gosport, here we engaged</p>
