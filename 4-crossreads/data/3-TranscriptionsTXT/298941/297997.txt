 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;10.30 p.m After Wimereux, to my surprise came Calais. Here the signs of the present battle were more noticeable in the trainloads of salvaged material, smashed guns, &amp; light railway stock that passed us.
