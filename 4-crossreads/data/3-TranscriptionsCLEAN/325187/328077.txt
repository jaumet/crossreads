  
 [Thursday 30 November 1916] 
 Arrived  Ismalia  & got leave to go to Cairo to store Kit- Received a lot of letters  one from Mother Sept 14 Dolly - Joe & Day, Laura- May Sept 14th- please tell May I did receive goggles, Fly Nett   & wrote & acknowledged them.  I note she has written a SONG!!! Address letters as of old but leave out A Squadron  I am here perhaps at the Base for a few weeks but do not know yet.   Also letter from Mrs Cotton, Ferrar & others. I send the cuttings of Diary onto Dolly they are of interest to me too .
