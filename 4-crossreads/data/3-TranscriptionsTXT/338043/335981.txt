 October. Oct. 1st Our Engine &amp; Dynamo being under repair we deem it inadvisable to show &ndash; A visit to the "Pedlars" (a neighbouring British Council Party) &ndash; A new poem &amp; two new concerted items written. 
 Oct. 2nd Bulgaria no longer a belligerent &ndash; Unconditional surrender &ndash; Depression throughout Germany &ndash; Rehearsal &amp; read "Shelley" &ndash; Letters answered.
