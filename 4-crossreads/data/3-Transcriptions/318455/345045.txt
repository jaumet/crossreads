<p>I am writing this at midday in a siding at Termoli where we are having one of our many halts to let another train through.</p>
<p>Bully Beef &amp; Cigarettes are better than money in this part of the world &ndash; we have been exchanging them for grapes &amp; tomatoes all the way down the line. Constantly <strike>passing</strike> meeting troop trains of Italians from Albania &amp; Salonica dirty, slovenly looking men they are.</p>
