<p>24 Th May<br/>Very hot and dusty  cleaning gear  aeroplanes very busy.  Otherwise on front very quiet</p>
<p>25 Fri May<br/>Very quiet went to DADOS [?] with White  Taubes very busy also Artillery  Aus mail arrived</p>
<p>26 Sat May<br/>Usual activity in the air  General visited the whole of the front line  Getting warm and dustier  The natives are still harvesting</p>
