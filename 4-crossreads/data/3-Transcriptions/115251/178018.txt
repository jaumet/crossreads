<p>[Report from Lt. Col. J. Paton]</p>
<p>S.S. &#39;Komet&#39; at Sea<br />13th Oct 1914</p>
<p>The Administrator<br />Rabaul</p>
<p>Sir<br />I have the honor to report that pursuant to your instructions I proceeded in the &#39;Nusa&#39; with Lt Comdr Jackson and a party of Naval &amp; Military members of the Expeditionary Force at 4 am on the 9th inst for the purpose of<br />(1) clearing up the situation in regard to the Wireless Station working on the Islands<br />(2) Effecting the capture of the K.G.S. &#39;Komet&#39; -<br />The &#39;Komet&#39; has been captured undamaged and the Wireless plant is on board and in working order.</p>
