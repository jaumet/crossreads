<p>We were all paid this afternoon those who had enough on their books got &pound;10, this ought to carry us home there was also a brief medical inspection, but we have a clean bill of health a rather unusual thing I should think for a ship with so many troops &amp; coming so far, on board, we have not a single patient</p>
<p>23rd. Reveille at 5.A.M. we are now lying at anchor off Queenscliff, the quarantine staff have come aboard with masks on to have a look at us I have just been done with the Tasmanians we fell in on the boat deck &amp; a Dr came round &amp; took every mans temperature, it all depends how we get on whether we go into quarantine or not, on looking through the paper which came aboard I was surprised</p>
