 TUESDAY, JANUARY 7, 1919. 
 Fine. Knocking about town 
 in the morning. Went down & 
 put in an application for an 
 early return to Australia at 
 Horseferry Road in the  evening  afternoon. 
 A big mob of us went down to 
 the Pic for  lunch  dinner & got 
 a couple of boxes for the "Boy" after 
 & had a good evening.
