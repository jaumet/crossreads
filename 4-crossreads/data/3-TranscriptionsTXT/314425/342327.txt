I went for a short walk this afternoon before tea. The weather was very threatening and it had been raining lightly most of the afternoon. 
 Monday Nov 25 1918. 15. Dull &amp; cloudy. Since I came here I have had a bath every morning with the exception of yesterday. The time is slipping away fast. It is only a month to Christmas today. 
 and I hope to be about returning to Australia by then if not already on the way. 
 I wrote to Tom Douglas this morning in reply to a letter I received in France. The Doctor was around this morning &amp; and asked if there were any patients who wished to be discharged. The lady Doctor N. O. Walk does not visit this hospital now. I went in to Devonport this afternoon and got the hands fixed on my watch. There was a very good concert here in the Hall tonight. It was given by some Naval Officers &amp; their friends.
