<p>quite cutting, &amp; to kill time I went &amp; had a look in at the picture show in Sutton Veney &amp; then back to camp where I done some writing <br/>22nd.  At last the papers are beginning to publish the true accounts of the fighting during March &amp; April when the Australians so splendidly acquitted themselves &amp; saved Amiens yes &amp; the British Army when things looked their blackest, how proud the people in Australia will be when they read of how their boys stood up to Fritzie &amp; fought him to a stand still when the other troops had chucked it in, it does one's eyes good to read the accounts so long withheld from the public I don't know what they must think of the Aussies now after what they have been reading &amp; its only</p>
