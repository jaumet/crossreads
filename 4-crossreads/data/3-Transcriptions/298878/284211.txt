Those present had not recovered from their surprise when I reached the deck, but the Dr who was one of the party was soon busily engaged attending to them both; after binding up Walker's leg he ordered him off to the hospital; and then turned his attention to the Mate; after making a careful examination of the wound, he concluded there was no immediate danger, so he brought some of his gear on deck, and packed up the wound.
