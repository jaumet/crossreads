<p>June 1915</p>
<p>2 Wed<br/>Brought A and B Sqdns to Popes and with C filled the two left sections, in 3<br/>Sqns. Quiet night.<br/>2 wounded. Lt Birch of 3rd killed.</p>
<p>3 Thursday<br/>On Popes.<br/>Generally quiet. Lce Corpl Grimson in charge of the sniping posts. With 6 others</p>
