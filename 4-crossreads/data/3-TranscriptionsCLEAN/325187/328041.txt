
[Wednesday 25 October 1916]A.I.F. HeadquartersStill counting Ballot Papers 10 hours a day-   The Soldiers fight a lot better than they fill in their voting papers but so far only 1% are informal & the majority is in favour of NO conscription.Laura called today at Berners Hotel but only saw Dolly-   She is coming to dinner tomorrow night I hope.  I may be given some leave after this job I hope so.Had lunch at Army & Navy Club.Bad news from Roumanians & the war is a long way from over yetThe Votes here goes against conscription.
