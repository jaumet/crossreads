<p>Dear Fergie,</p>
<p>I am sending you, just as a curiosity a French paper dropped by a German balloon in our lines.  The holes punched in the paper are apparently made for some automatic arrangement for pushing off the papers one by one.  I don't see anything very serious in the printed matter, which seems chiefly to consist of a collection of nasty things which the Entente nations have said about one</p>
