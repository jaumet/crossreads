 25:XI:16 Poor old Jack died on the 10th of this month. In October he collapsed at Boulogne through ear trouble &amp; after four operations at the Queen Alexandra Hosp. Malo-les-Bains, he developed pneumonia &amp; bronchitis, The cause of death was septic pneumonia. Went to London for three days (14th to 17th) &amp; had a few hours with Dad &amp; Lily. Everything here is as it was before.
