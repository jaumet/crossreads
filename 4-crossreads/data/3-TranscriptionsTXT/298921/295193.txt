
August, 1915Tuesday 10Not far from Alexandria beautiful weather to be killing men - The Turks fought fair but shelled us going out to Hospital ships, probably could not tell we were only for the meat ships, they hit 2 with Shrapnel.Out of the Boat into Hospital Train.No.1 Australian General Hospital Heliopolis the same place I used to visit the sick ere I was one -SIKHS attended us on the Train & nurses were very good -A good many died coming over.
