<p>The Sea:-</p>
<p>The Sea!  the Sea!  tho open sea!<br/>The blue, the Flesh, the ever free!<br/>Without a mark, without a bound,<br/>It runneth the earth's wide regions round.<br/>It plays with the clouds;  it mocks the skies;<br/>Or like a cradled creature lies. B Proctor.</p>
