<p>arrest him too for all he cared. Hop like a stupid owl sat down near the Head's tent waiting for orders  - to go on the route march evidently. Charlie Williams however soon settled that &amp; we coaxed him back to our tent &amp; told him to keep quiet or  he would get us all into the march.</p>
<p>At 2pm the drums &amp; musical instruments played Tiddley Winks &amp; other high class music, whilst the whole camp &ndash; bar us &ndash; left for the route march that returned at almost midnight. All the tents of the Signal School were old condemned ones &amp;were marked for repairs so we chose the best whilst they were absent &amp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;replaced ours with it, the new one being absolutely perfect. We then "shook"  a table &amp; made ourselves comfortable thus in the tent</p>
