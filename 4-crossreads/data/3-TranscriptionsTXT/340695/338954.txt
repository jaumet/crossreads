 18th Nov.1916. A year ago today the old Persic steamed out of Sydney Heads. What a day it was, one that I'll never forget. It snowed this morning for the first time gee it was cold. Everywhere looked beautiful shrouded in the purest white. I indulged in a bit of a snowballing. Our fire is kept going all day, we would freeze only for it. Make cocoa before turning in.
