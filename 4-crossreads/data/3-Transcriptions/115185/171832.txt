<p>3 &ndash; main deck, a most comfortable dining room with small round tables.<br/>We had a very good dinner at 7pm. There are troops on this ship, we are, I suppose, the etc. We had life belt drill at 9pm, and then I went to bed &amp; slept well, noticing the quiet after the noise of the great city. There aren't any stewardesses. The sisters have tables to themselves and the officers also. There aren't any passengers. I have started a diary but I think it will be less interesting than my letters. I am sorry I haven't a copy sheet with me because this letter may be censored. I am now seeing and doing just the same</p>
