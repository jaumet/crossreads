<p>in fact when I first closed my eyes She seemed to stand first on one end &amp; then on the other. The Hatch was covered with a tarpaulin to keep the water out &amp; it used to flap up &amp; down in the wind making a great noise.  A good many men were sick &amp; most of them did not reach the deck to be sick we had shipped a little water so the floor was in a horrid mess by morning.</p>
<p>Monday 18/6/17<br/>Weather is still farly rough &amp; cold the sick are recovering slightly &amp; the messes filling up</p>
