
December, 1915
Sunday 5
Never slept a wink all night for the ache of my leg, mostly due to the rotten stretcher.
No hospital ship in so will remain at Clearing Hospital until one comes
Heard that no ship nearer than Alexandria.
How heartily I cursed the man responsible for this blunder not on my own account so much as other & worse cases.
There is an amputation opposite me & a loss of sight to the left,
