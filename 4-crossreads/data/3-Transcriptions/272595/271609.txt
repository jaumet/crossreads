<p>It is a nasty sort of day, plenty of sunshine but the wind is blowing a gale &amp; dust is flying everywhere  Went back at 4.15 but still no sign of Mrs Oliver so I decided then back to London,  I left a note for for Mrs Oliver with the gatekeeper, arrived in London &amp; went by tube to Victoria I got a taxi &amp; run round to Peel House where I booked my bed &amp; had tea, I then decided to go to the Opera at Drury Lane  Cavelleria Rusticania &amp; Pagalacciao was on was luck for I got a single seat in the Grand Dress Circle &amp; the 2nd row cost me 9/- but the performance was easily worth it the music &amp; singing was lovely, the theatre was packed by a most enthusiastic</p>
