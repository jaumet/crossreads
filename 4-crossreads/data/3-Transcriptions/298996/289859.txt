<p>Notes of <strike>the Run</strike> trip to the front after leaving Liverpool Camp Australia</p>
<p>17- 3-15<br/>We left Liverpool camp. 8.A.M. arrived Sydney R. Station 10. AM. After some trouble.<br/>Formed up and marched to No 1 Wharf, Woolloomooloo Bay arriving there at 11. AM. Embarked aboard T.SS. Shropshire.<br/>Moved from wharf to midstream 12.AM. Sailed from there 5 PM. As soon as we passed out of the heads some of the Lads began <strike>to get sea sick</strike> to feel funny about the inner man there being a good swell on. <strike>9 PM</strike> 6 PM. All men alloted to their sleeping <strike>points</strike> positions. This is how they are alloted off.</p>
