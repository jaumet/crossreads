  
 11. Thursday Drizzle in morning &  Dense fog in afternoon.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10.  Got home at six. 
 12. Friday Partly grey but warm & sunny in afternoon.  Went to Miss Newcomb in morning & to see Maud in afternoon.  Came home tired & went to bed. 
 13. Saturday Dull &  damp.  Was at Mrs. Newcomb's all day.
