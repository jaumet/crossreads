
<p>380</p>
<p>1917 MAY</p>
<p>26 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness all morning &amp; Eric</p>
<p>Mackellar &amp; self went up</p>
<p>to headquarters in afternoon</p>
<p>&amp; the Colonel interviewed us</p>
<p>about commissions &amp; said</p>
<p>he was sending my name</p>
<p>on, for one. Poss Nivison</p>
<p>was here looking for me</p>
<p>but I was not home.</p>
