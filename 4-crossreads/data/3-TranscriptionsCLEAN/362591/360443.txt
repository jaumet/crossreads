  
 All ripping good books, & moreover ones that leave a clean taste in your mouth - somewhat of a rarity in present day fiction. 
 I shall have much to talk over and tell you when we meet, and shall much enjoy coming round some of the Concentration Camps with you, if you will allow it. 
 C. Macnaghten 
 We expect to sail on Saturday 27th - & should arrive soon after this note.  The ship is the Itonus on which I came back to Alex. after the landing, & which was a veritable shambles.
