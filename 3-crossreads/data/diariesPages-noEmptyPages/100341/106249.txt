Nov. 28 Sunday
Snow followed Saturday afternoon storm from about 10-30 p.m., still snowing this morning, everything covered thickly, bitterly cold, slight fall even in evening.
Nov. 29 Monday
Snowfall ceased but threatening to return, everything frozen still too cold to thaw. Registered 22 below freezing point. Night clear crisp and biting bighting.
