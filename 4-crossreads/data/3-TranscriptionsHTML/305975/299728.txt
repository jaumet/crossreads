<p>Wednesday Oct. 30.10.18<br/>went for a walk this morning I don't see to be able to stand the heat of the sun  it makes me feel quite shakey and giddie if I stop in it long<br/>Thursday Oct. 31.10.18<br/>am feeling a little off to day  it is very hot to day  kept in the shade a much as Possible.<br/>Friday Nov. 1.11.18<br/>am feeling a good deal better to day  am keeping as quiet as Possible  it is very hot here to day-</p>
