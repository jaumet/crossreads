
Diary while in France.
Mar. 20th.Left vessel & marched to our encampment- Site of Indian G.H- originally a German residence- fine grounds with abundance of fruit trees & luxuriant vegetation- great contrast with Egypt.Mar.21st.Bully-beef & biscuits- Inspection by a French General- Break camp at Marseilles- Baggage guard.  Leave by train for unknown destination.  Sleep. (or at least try to sleep) on train.
