<p>November 1918.<br/>Sunday 17</p>
<p>Hospital. Birmingham<br/>Weather wet but not too bad. Accompanied by Sgt we strolled to a recital by an orchestral society. The turn out was excellent.<br/>Had afternoon tea at the [indecipherable], only a small affair .<br/>Scaled wall and found a nice French Bed and pajamas all ready for me.<br/>Sister P. helped me to straighten out the muddle like a thorough sport.</p>
