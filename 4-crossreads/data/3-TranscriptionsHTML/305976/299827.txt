<p>1917</p>
<p>Jan 22<br/>Left Sydney for Seymour Camp Victoria by express 7.25pm<br/>Big crowd on platform to wish us good luck and safe return.</p>
<p>Jan 23 Arrived Seymour 1 pm after a tiring journey due to overcrowding. Met at Station by Officer &amp; Sergeant Major &amp; marched to Camp some 3 miles distant.<br/>Arrived at Camp, pitched tents &amp; turned in very tired. First impressions this camp unfavourable &amp; from remarks made by occupants could be better ("Time will tell")</p>
<p>Jan 24th<br/>Reveille 6.30 AM.  Allotted</p>
