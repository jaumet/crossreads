April 13th  A draft releif ratings arrived on board from Australia during the forenoon and the relieved men departed at 4 PM. - they had a warm send off from the ship and they were some of our very old boys, that it was like breaking up a home to part with such long standing shipmates April 19th  We coaled ship during the forenoon constantly having to keep steam up ready for emergency necessitated continually refilling bunkers.
