<p>[Thursday 21st September 1916]</p>
<p>Went Manchester met Dolly Grandmother &amp; Aunt all very nice, Champagne dinner etc:- also saw Sister-in-law Mrs Aldwyn Isherwood whom I met in Cairo.  Feel as though I had been "Shot at" rather as an exhibition of a future relation.</p>
