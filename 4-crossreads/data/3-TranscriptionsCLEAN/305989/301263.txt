
Sunday 1 July 1917On "Sky Pilot" parade in evening.  Instruction on stoppages.
Monday 2 July 1917Quiet day, packing up for move, usual routine.
Tuesday 3 July 1917Brigade left Abassan el Kebir for El Gamli at 7.30:  was left in charge of dump.
Wednesday 4 July 1917On dump at Abassan-el-Kebir.  Taube came over, and two shells fired by taube fell near our camp & exploded.
Thursday 5 July 1917Limbers came in from Gamli & took remainder of stuff from Dump, went out to new camp on "Ahmed" at El Gamli.
Friday 6 July 1917General Routine, took horses down to water (3 miles).  Beer issue;  on picquet at night.
Saturday 7 July 1917Went to Dentist at F.A. had a tooth bored out & nerve killed pretty crook rest of day.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 7 and 4].
