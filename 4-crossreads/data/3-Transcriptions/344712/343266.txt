<p>Leaving the ship at the docks Southampton <strike>and</strike> we entered a train and arrived at Clapham Junction, London.  We have just arrived at Fulham Hospital, Hammersmith, in private motor cars, mine being driven by a lady.</p>
<p>This little book would hold insufficient space for me to give an account of my experiences, impressions formed, and the wonderful kindness extended to myself and comrades after our arrival here.</p>
<p>F.C. Burgis<br/>1363 D Coy.<br/>20th Batt.<br/>5th Brig.</p>
