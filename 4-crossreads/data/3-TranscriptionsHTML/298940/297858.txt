<p>last night (got Chestre I believe &amp; wounded a man &amp; killed a child). Over again tonight &amp; dropped bombs somewhere in the vicinity.</p>
<p><u>Thursday 28th Feb. 18.</u><br/>Up the line in a dugout at present, &amp; as near the front line as it is possible for them to put me.<br/>Got over the piano difficulty by getting Capt Peel, the Artillery Chaplain, to let us have the one he has borrowed from the Church Army, with the promise of returning same to Bailliue.<br/>Consequently we were able to give a concert last Monday night</p>
