  
 8 Dec 17 Left S. Flow in company with Light Cruiser Birmingham & 7 Destroyers. We are guarding a convoy. 
 9 & 10 Dec 17 Sweeping North Sea & convoying 4 ships. 
 11th Dec 17 Arrived back at Scapa & coaled. 
 12 Dec 17 Made false start & had to return & anchor. 
 14 Dec 17 Coaled. 
 15 to 18th Dec 17 Anchored at Scapa. 
 19th Dec 17 Left to convoy food ships. 30 in number. 
 20 Dec 17 Picked up 20 more ships & escorted them into harbour (Norway). Seas rough. 30 odd more ships left to come back with escort.
