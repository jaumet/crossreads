
1915Feb. 28 Sun.We are not to take any more patients in - getting them out in large numbers.In the afternoon 11 S.Ms, Scouts, & ex-Scouts from various Units met on the sandhills with the Great Pyramid as a background & had photos taken.  Spent the rest of the afternoon with V. Marshal & R.A. Doyle.Was over at the Melbourne Y.M.C.A. after tea.3rd Brigade of 1st Division struck camp & marched out from Mena about 5.30 p.m. destination unknown. The 1st & 2nd Brigades are very busy getting ready for a move - likewise ourselves.
