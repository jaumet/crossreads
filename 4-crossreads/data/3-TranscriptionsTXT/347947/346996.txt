
evening went out to a place called scone. met a friend by appointment and it started to rain heavy so came home early it was bitter cold. next morning went out and had a few whiskies at the salutation hotel during restricted hours as we often did during our stay in perth so at 12.30 that day Mon 7th  Feb left perth for Aberdeen Edinburgh which was on our way to London and only stayed at Edinburgh till the following day arrived there at 4 oclock on the Monday after 4 hours ride in the train which passed through some more beautiful country and large villages. we stayed there a day and a night it was too cold to stay any longer the country and the great city which is the capital of scotland
