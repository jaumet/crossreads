 1915 May. 8 Sat. In afternoon went out with motor ambulance load of patients to Light Horse Camp, Heliopolis Race&ndash;course &amp; base Details Camp, Zeitoun &ndash; Called in at No. 1 General Hospital, Heliopolis &amp; left a few useful odds &amp; ends with Val. Marshall (to-days good turn ! What!).  Very fine run through Gizeh, Gezireh, Old Cairo, Cairo, Abbassia, Heliopolis, Zeitoun, Abbassia, Cairo, to Palace Hotel, Gezireh. Left car here &amp; caught a tram back to Pyramids. In evening Y.M.C.A. organised a fine concert for the patients in smoking lounge at Mena House.
