 September 24th.   Two Zeplins were brought down near London by anti-aircraft guns &amp; I saw the first of the two fall in flames. The reports returned from the Esher school &amp; I was informed that I came out top of the school. 27th.  Left for another leave in London &amp; returned on the 1st October. 1st October. 1916 Another Zeplin was brought down near London by gun fire.
