<p>Abberville. That Sergeant is now in charge of B section in place of Sgt Canvin.<br/>Vic Hall, Bert Emmott &amp; I have been leading a pleasant life. Hall is exempt from parades, &amp; so is Bert every third week, being on nursing duties with A section, while I attend all parades. &ndash; Yet spare time is not meagre.<br/>On fine mornings we rise an hour before reveille &amp; get kits and cleaning up done by the time we go on physical drill parade</p>
