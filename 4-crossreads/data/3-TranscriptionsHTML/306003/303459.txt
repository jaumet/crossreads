<p>4-8-16<br/>We retired back behind the trenches that were manned by Scottish Infantry &amp; the Turks only made a small half-hearted attack here on the trenches &amp; then retired.<br/>One or two of our men were hit with shrapnel before we got our horses out of range &amp; at last pulled up about a mile behind the first line of trenches &amp; waited there till 4 oclock this evening;</p>
<p>We then received the order to mount &amp; went west &amp; pulled up for the night in a Hod (or palm grove) close by Mount Meredith which was in the hands of the Turks here we met a big body of infantry (Manchesters)  who had come down from Hill 70;  Our casualties today in H.Q. were Capt Weir (Adj) &amp; Capt Fitzpatrick (QM) both seriously wounded &amp; Lt Nelson wounded  Sig Edwards wounded through left shoulder Sgt Selff [Self] seriously wounded</p>
