<p>We are led to believe we are only here for a short time as rumour has it we are going out for a spell and then proceeding up north of France again A move we are all looking forward to as we have now been on this front almost continually since Oct 1916 and through the worst of weather</p>
<p>May 20th<br/>At last we have come out for a spell and later go up north.</p>
<p>Leaving Velu Wood at 9AM a party of 9 of us proceeded on bikes to Bavilencourt a distance of 30 odd miles</p>
