  Oct.  Nov. 1st - Wednesday After a cup of Bovril at 5 a.m. we three (from 26 Ward) move out to the Evacuation Tent & once aboard a Motor Car feel safe & sound for "Blighty" (England) on that journey to which all good soldiers aspire.  On the Hospital train at 6 a.m., in a comfortable "Pullman" Car, back to Etaples for a short stay & then straight to Calais for the Hospital ship.
