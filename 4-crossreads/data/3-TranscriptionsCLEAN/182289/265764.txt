  
 Perham Down Camp 4 Officer, 59 O/Ranks attached to "A" Group. 4 Officers, 58 O/Ranks attached to "E" Group. 
 12.5.17 - Course held 13th to 17th May (inclusive).  Returned to Perham Down, 18.5.17. 18.5.17 - No. 7332, Pte. Setchfield, J.C., marched in & was taken on strength. 23.5.17 - No. 3353, Pte. Hanley, J.S. & No. 1995 Pte. Sowter, P.G., taken on strength. 1.6.17 - Pte. Cook promoted L/Corporal. 2.6.17 - The Company marched out from No. 7 Camp, Perham Down to No. 1 Camp, Parkhouse. 
 Parkhouse 5.6.17 - No. 2910, Pte. J.J. Griffin, marched in and was taken on strength. 8.6.17 - Cpl. and 4 men returned, having completed Signalling Course at Grantham. 15.6.17 - Twenty-four Drivers appointed, Driver, Dee, Kalucy, Powell, promoted L/Cpls. Twenty-one Light draught horses and seven cobs drawn from Remount Depot, Bulford. 15.6.17 - Drawing of Mobilisation Stores from Imperial Ordnance, Tidworth, commenced. 18.6.17 - Twenty-four light draught horses and two mules received from Shenbourne.
