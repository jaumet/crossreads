<p>I was too occupied to feel anxious but all went well &ndash; There was a big explosion but no fish &ndash; However Fitz had learned how to chuck the hand bomb &amp; that was good &ndash; When we left the Fantome I said what about a bandage &amp; some wool? but we didnt need it &ndash; Only one soldier has lost his hand up here so far &ndash; by dynamite &ndash; We got back to the Fantome &amp; were then ordered out to sea at 8pm to patrol &ndash; The Yarra called me to see some sick &ndash; more malaria &ndash; Off I went &ndash; Dinner &ndash; Up anchor &ndash; Darken ship &ndash; When that happens I creep &ndash; into my cot &ndash; I'm useless &amp; so is everyone else &ndash; on a darkened ship &ndash;<br/>I didn't mention I lunched on the Elax with the Captain &ndash; I christened him Ajax &ndash; He's a Hull man &ndash; Yorkshire to the back bone &ndash; A good bold man &ndash; very powerful &ndash; shrewd but ignorant &ndash; English to the back bone &ndash; He told us the latest news when we first met &ndash;</p>
