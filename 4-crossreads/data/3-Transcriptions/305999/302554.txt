<p>and travilled 4 hours through Armiens  and  and billited at a town called Vignacourt  plenty of drill</p>
<p>30/11/16<br/>At 30 we where aroused and woke and had to pack up and at 4 AM we marched about 1 mile and &frac12; to the railway station  we stood about till 8 AM then we intrained  we disimbarked at a village called Abiel  we remained there the night and a day  very cold</p>
