<p>Thursday 23 December, 1915</p>
<p>Drew our pay.</p>
<p>I went into Piet&acirc; to see Paula. Now that Sid Moss has gone back I have to get out on my own. It was a hard job beating the picket as none of those in isolation are supposed to have passes but I got home all right at about 12.30.</p>
