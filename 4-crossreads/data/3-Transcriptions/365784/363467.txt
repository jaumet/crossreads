<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; handkerchiefs, soap, tooth brushes &amp; such like things<br/>(2) that our wounded here in Egypt should have everything they want in the way of suitable clothing &amp; every comfort &amp; luxury that the heart could desire.<br/>(3) that our wounded who have been sent back to Australia &amp; who had to face monsoon season in the Indian Ocean should be well supplied with suitable summer clothing &amp; should have everything to make the voyage home as pleasant comfortable as possible.</p>
<p>All these good things were provided &amp; donated often at great sacrifice by the people of Australia &amp; were handed over to the representatives of the Australian Red Cross Society for distribution but not to Y.M.C.A. building funds.</p>
