<p>War Diary<br/>1916  13th June<br/>damage &ndash; 3 casualties on Canal &ndash; pieces picked up point to bombs being converted "Mills"</p>
<p>14th June<br/>Convoy of 650 camels with half Sqadn. escort arrived in camp at 0300, remainder of column between 1600 and 1830 &ndash; men tired but both men and horses fit &ndash; operations a complete success</p>
