<p>19 April 1915<br/>Monday</p>
<p>The fourth Reinfs arrive   &amp;  are sent into camp at a place by the name of Zeitoun<br/>We are set to work cleaning up the   <strike>the</strike> whole camp at Heliopolis on account of an order that we may have to go on to Zeitoun but later the order is cancelled.</p>
<p>1915 April 20<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Finish cleaning   &amp;  burning   &amp;  have our first proper parade with our new officers</p>
