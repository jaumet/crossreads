
<p>? Guns&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 12/ Officially informed of the why &amp; wherefore of things We&#39;re here because its better to be hot &amp; hungry than cold &amp; stiff. We&#39;re to go on &frac12; ration forthwith. We&#39;re to wash no more in fresh water nor are we to shave until further notice on pain of instant death &amp; dismissal from the A. I. F. (I don&#39;t think) we&#39;ve got to help unload cargo &amp; coal ship (we smile) We&#39;re to stoke fires, launch boats, learn navigation &amp; be prepared to duck overside &amp; swim ashore &amp; take to the tall timbers tother side the hills --- if Fremantle says so. Tonight there are 20 ships &amp; Hear that many thousand men bottled in this jar of pickles</p>
