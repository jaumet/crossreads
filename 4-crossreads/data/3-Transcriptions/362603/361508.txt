<p>Red Cross letterhead as before<br/>Bulford<br/>12.8.17<br/>Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George,<br/>I have very little to write about having been very busy &amp; the weather has been very unsettled just lately.  I had &pound; 10  from Jones' &amp; 7 days leave granted last Friday so I'm off to the Isle of Man again on Tuesday.  I have been for a stroll over the Downs to-night with Dave &amp; Ellis has been playing Billiards all night in the Mess.
