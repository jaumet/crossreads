<p>Got back through the fence at 1am next morning.</p>
<p>Wednesday 18th  [Nov]<br/>Nothing about McDermott.  Looks as if he isnt coming back.  Bayonet drill all the morning.  Captain McLaghlen Instructor.  In the afternoon independent troop drill mounted.  Fairly cool day rather cloudy.</p>
<p>Thursday 19th<br/>Full regiment parade in uniforms with rifles.  Cinematographer operator took pictures of the regiment marching past.  In the afternoon went out to Holsworthy with an advance party of 9 from B Squadron to help get things fixed up.  All leave stopped at night.</p>
<p>Friday 20th<br/>Struck camp at Liverpool.  Went out with guard over the transports.  We got our horse lines</p>
