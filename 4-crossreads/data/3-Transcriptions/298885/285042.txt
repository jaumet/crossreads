<p>Wed. Jan. 19th -16<br/>In the red Sea, coast very pretty passed 18 boats during the day.  Searchlights played on us last night by 4 battleships.</p>
<p>Thur. Jan. 10th -16<br/>Last night it blew a gale result a heavy sea.  Thorough inspection of whole ship also kit.  Passed Dutch East Africa trading boat.</p>
<p>Fri. Jan. 21st -16<br/>Blowing gale all day but quietened at night.  Very cold, passed two islands called "The Brothers," on one stands a big light house.</p>
<p>Sat. Jan. 22nd -16<br/>Arrived Port Said at 6.30 A.M.  The Sun sets for the last few</p>
