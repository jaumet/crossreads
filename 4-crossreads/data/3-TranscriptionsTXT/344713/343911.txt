Sat. 21st. Oct.: -Marched from Le Gandspette to St. Omer & entrained.Passed through Calais, Wimmereaux, Etaples, Boulogne, & Abbeville & disentrained at Longpre. Marched to L'Etoile & billeted.  Leaving by Motor busses tomorrow for - We don't know where.
Mon. Oct. 23rd: -Marched from L'Etoile to Mouflers where we got aboard large buses & passed through Flixecourt &  St Sauver &  dismounted near Buire & then marched to near Fricourt where we put up in improvised shelters for night.
Tues. Oct. 24th: -Marched to a hillside near Mametz wood & we are having a horrible time with the water & mud.Caught up to the 56th Bn (our sister Bn) for the first time since we have been in France.
Thurs. Oct. 26th.: -Weather slightly cleared though there is nothing to be seen but cursed mud for miles round here.Men & Mules struggling & splashing through it.
