<p>19th. 'Was at a concert last night given in the Y.M.C.A. &amp; I could not help feeling wild at the injustice of things, for here were hundreds of men in the pink of condition loafing round about the camp &amp; huts, while up in the trenches their mates were being killed &amp; wounded in hundreds &amp; many &amp; many a poor devil up there in the trenches was only fit to be in hospital but their indomitable spirit kept them where they were wanted, &amp; you hear very few complaints from them as regards tucker &amp; hardships, while down here they <strike>lit</strike> live like Kings compar</p>
