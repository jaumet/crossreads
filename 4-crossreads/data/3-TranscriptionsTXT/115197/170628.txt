  May 28th  
 Reveille 6.30. 
 Account of Hawker&#39;&#39;s &amp; Grieve&#39;s rescue &amp; return. 
 Australians to the front! 
 Stirring scenes. 
 At 11 a.m. we draw out from the wharf at Devonport and sail slowly down the harbour of Plymouth.&nbsp; While our band plays patriotic airs - &quot;Auld Lang Syne&quot; .&nbsp; &quot;Good-byee&quot; and lumps rise in our throats (for after all we are human &amp; have human sympathies).
