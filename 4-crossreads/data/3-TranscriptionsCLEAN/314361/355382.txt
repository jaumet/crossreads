There are not so many shops, there is a large cooperative store. This afternoon we had some practice at signalling, for this we went outside the town, my party went on to a hill, which gave us a fine view of beautiful country. From the hill it reminds me of the Blue Mountains NSW, but here we have in the distance cultivated fields, with hay stacks & the town in the distance. Our billet this time is not in a loft, on the ground floor it would be very nice if not so cramped. there is plenty of good straw on the floor. The people are different to those of Flanders, in fact it is a different country all together. The well water is good. It has been a very interesting & enjoyable day. We must be some distance from the fighting, for we cannot see or hear anything of it, which is in is self a treat, for now there is exceptionally heavy fighting going on "on the Somme" Oct 19th Pont Remy Ballot was taken this morning for conscription.
