<p>27.4.19</p>
<p>My dear Judge,<br/>We are just getting everything ready for the move from here on the 6th May. Apart from the work of getting ready there has been nothing to do &amp; we have been having a very slow time. The weather has been very disappointing after its promises of a beautiful spring and the cold winds have made it beastly uncomfortable to do anything except sit down and read.</p>
<p>Yesterday in spite of the weather we went out to see the Crecy battlefield</p>
