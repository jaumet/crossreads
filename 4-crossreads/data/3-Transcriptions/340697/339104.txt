<p>There are four of us on this kind of work. Our duties are to keep the telephone lines in good order. 19.4.16</p>
<p>The enemy shelled the 9th. Battalion in one of their billets. Their casualties were 18 killed and 35 wounded 20.4.16.</p>
<p>There are a good number of our aeroplanes up observing today and "Old Fritz" is wasting thousands of shots at them 21.4.16</p>
