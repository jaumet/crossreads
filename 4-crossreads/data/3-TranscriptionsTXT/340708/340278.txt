
August 1917Saturday 18thFritz planes in evidence this morning one brought down in flames also one this evening.  There was another successfull advance by the English & French in Ypres sector this morning   I am still having a holiday.Sunday 19thAnother batch of C men left this morning en route for N.Z.   Fritz still carrying on with the bombing he got 8 horses & 30 men this morning early.    Monday 20thGot paid today & got word that we are going back any time two Hun airmen brought in   they were driven down in our linesTuesday 21stPlanes active through the night & early morning another brought down
