 Tues Aug 8th &ndash; 16 Spent all day getting everything ready, cleaning harness and waggons etc as we may have to return to Pozieres at an hour's notice 
 Wed Aug 9th &ndash; 16 Continued cleaning and at 4PM we are to move out at 10Am following morning. 
 Norrie wounded in knee 
 Thur Aug 10th &ndash; 16 Instead of 10AM we were pulled out at 3AM and it was some hurry up too as we had to get away by 4AM and after a wet trip we arrived at Bouzincourt again but in a different camp.
