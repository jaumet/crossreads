
19/3/17Roads between Fremicourt & Beugny & returned again at night to Fremicourt & Bivouaced for night.  Good progress was made during the day altho' strong opposition was met on both flanks.  Beugny is now strongly held by us.
20thWe got out early again this morning & started work again on the Rds. & water supply.  We camped this evening in the Lebucquiere & at night our Sec. was out digging strong points & M.G. positions.  We are now holding a position in front of Bometz.
21stBusily engaged on well sinking today & at half night half the Sec. was engaged digging strong points in front of Bometz.
22ndEngaged in Well digging today.  Things rather quiet with the Inf. but Fritz shelled the Village Lebucquiere rather heavily & put a lot of shrapnel on our left.
23rdAt daybreak Fritz made a heavy attack on Bometz & the high country on our Left.  He got possession of the Village but was driven back as he was in all positions with heavy loss but our casualties were fairly heavy also.  Capt. Booth was killed & also another
