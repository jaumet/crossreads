<p>Relations with Cpl Merrilees &amp; Major Strachan have a little improved lately.<br/>Fancy latter is inclined to have favourites &amp; does not care a rap about the rest as individuals.  If he gets a dislike for a chap he works off his feelings by mean actions.<br/>Gertie has developed his eccentric mannerisms &amp; is a little less popular than before  In some ways has improved &amp; in others not<br/>Generally less noisy.<br/>Means well but fails lamentably.</p>
