9th July:- SundayTo-day is the day &  it is hot too. About 101 . Parade in full marching order at 6.30 am & inspection follows. The rest of the day is taken up with doing odd jobs. Parade at 2.30 pm & at 10 pm we again fall in, parade & dismiss to again; parade at Cairo station at 10.30 pm. This is an awfully messed up affair. Talk about hot.
