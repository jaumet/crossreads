<p>Thursday 20/8/14<br/>About 5 pm. "Geier" meets us accompanied by a collier "Ulm" which is at least three times the size of "Geier". The commander of "Geier" boards our ship. "Geier" is going to Japan.</p>
<p>Friday 21/8/14<br/>We are going south . Weather good.</p>
<p>Saturday 22/8/14    do</p>
<p>Sunday 23/8/14<br/>Passed the line at 4.30. a little later a steamer is in sight. She carries the Japanese flag. After showing the English flag we let her pass.</p>
<p>Monday 24/8/14<br/>We have entered the Indian Ocean through the Malucca strait.</p>
