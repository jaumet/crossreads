<p>Friday Jan. 10. 1919. 61.<br/>Dull &amp; cloudy but fine. I finished reading "Pan's mountain" a classical story by Amelie Rives (Princess Troubetzkoy.<br/>The scene of the story is in Italy &amp; it is rather weird tale and it ends unhappily. The lovers do not get married.<br/>[shorthand symbols] I wrote to my wife to day. Letter 153<br/>A new patient came in this evening. The ward is now full up.<br/>End<br/>of<br/>Book XI</p>
