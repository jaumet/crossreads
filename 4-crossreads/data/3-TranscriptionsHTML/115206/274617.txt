<p>February 1918<br/>Saturday 16</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Went to F.O.O.with Garfott with Mackensen's lunch. Battery fired a few rounds at a Fritzer plane. Great deal of aeriel activity throughout the day and night.<br/>Weather beautiful, a little frosty, shell holes covered with ice.<br/>Visibility very good.<br/>Had a rather disastrous evening at cards.<br/>Wrote to Doug Fraser and Ike Steinthal.<br/>Had the 10 to 12 watch last night.</p>
