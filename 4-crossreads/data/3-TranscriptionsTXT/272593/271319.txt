Sometimes after this we turn in for an hour or so, but generally work starts immediately, one of the first duties is tidying &amp; cleaning up the trench, empty bully tins, biscuits paper &amp; etc form the greater part of the rubbish, then set to &amp; clean our rifles, after this breakfast. As a rule each man cooks his own, though in some of the quieter places the cooks do the work.
