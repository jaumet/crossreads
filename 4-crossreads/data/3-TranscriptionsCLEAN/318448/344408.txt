The stunt out here has not been an unqualified success, we advanced 40 miles & blew up 15 miles of the Hedjaz railway but could not get the tunnel which we were after & and had to retire with the Turks close behind. This morning they could be seen coming down the hills on to the plane valley through the glasses & now we can see the shells bursting over on the other
