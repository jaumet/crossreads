<p>Feb 23</p>
<p>Usual drill in morning. As it rained in the afternoon we were issued with service rainproofs, &amp; of these the boys are very proud. Y.M.C.A. in evening.</p>
<p>To my surprise my weight, without great coat, was 11 stone 2 lbs. This is not bad considering that I was 9-6 upon date of enlistment on Oct 5th last year.</p>
<p>Feb 24</p>
<p>We had a very enjoyable route march in the morning. In the afternoon Alan &amp; I went to Shrewton and after walking right through that place, continued along the main street into the country beyond. After going for about 1 &frac12; miles we discovered between two hills a quiet little hamlet &amp; immediately made straight for it.
