<p>Feb. Wednesday 14 1917</p>
<p>A fairly busy night. 6 or 7 cases through.  Sprains due to the slippery ice common. <br/>A squad of 6 men go up to a sap to make a relay shelter tonight. <br/>Word has just been brought that Capt. Teague has been killed up the line --- a fine man gone. <br/>Dope Drummond &amp; the case of the man with the broken leg due to a fall.! <br/>Receive parcel from Mary. Biscuits, socks &amp; balaclava.</p>
