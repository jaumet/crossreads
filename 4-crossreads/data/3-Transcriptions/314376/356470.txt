<p>When we reached the railway cutting we started to make dugouts to sleep in. it was not easy work on account of only having our entrenching tools to dig with. I remember I spent my 27th Birthday here. It was a rainy night Apl 6th. I was put on top of a railway cutting with my waterproof wrapped round me. The rest of the platoon had gone off to reconnoitre a village close by to see whether there were any Germans in occupancy there.
