<p>14-11-1917<br/>Tomorrow our guns go to workshops for repairs. Likely to stay in these lines one month</p>
<p>15-11-1917<br/>Fine, The enemy shelling, almost all night. A few bombs dropped early this morning. Col Dean in lines this evening Birdwood made a full General.</p>
<p>16-11-1917<br/>A few isolated showers. General Anderson inspecting our horses. 11 remounts recieved. Terrific drum fire up in the line. We hear of a bit</p>
