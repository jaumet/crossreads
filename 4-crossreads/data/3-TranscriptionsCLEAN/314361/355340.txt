Saturday April 8th 16 Have made another shift, we are now on the desert about one mile above the camp on canal, Do not know how long we shall be here. A & B Coys moved off again this morning to a camp outside Ismailia, which was previously occupied by N. Zealanders, who they say have left for France. Camp, Near Ferry Post  Ismailia Egypt April 11. 16. Had another ride into Ismailia this morning, took a few photos.
