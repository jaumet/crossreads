
Little moor Dec. 9- 18Having very miserable weather now, shall be glad to get out of it. So far two lots for two different boats have left & another is now ready to go, hope to be on the next, went into Weymouth again, last Saturday night
Little moor. Dec 12- 12 18was told yesterday that the 15 was in the pay office here, but will not be able to draw it untill I get my pay book back from London. Little moor Dec 19-12-18Am now on a Boat Roll, the Takada to sail on the 24th.Tuesday last I drew my remittance of 15 & in the evening went to Weymouth & bought a ring for Louie & registered it home straight away. The ring cost 14. 10- a diamond cluster. I also sent the french book on Zoology.
