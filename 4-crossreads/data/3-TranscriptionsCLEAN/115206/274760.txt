  
 July 1918 Tuesday 9 
 Vaux sur Somme.2nd Bty.Position Weather bright and clear during morning, aftenoon rained very heavily, and was still raining at midnight. Have had no word from Ken Gunn, as yet.  Mr Mays left for Sig School at St Evatien, the same one as Ken has gone down to. O.Beer, commenced work on the dugout's spare entrance. Fritz and a decent duty shoot. Won 100 francs at poker, suppose I will lose same tomorrow. Repaired two breaks on the O.P. line Major returned
