
25th March 1917Battle of Gaza45 miles from Jerusalem  First shots fired by Turks about 11 am at Anzac Mounted Div  Infantry started to attack Gaza about noon heavy fighting all day with considerable losses inflicted on the 53rd Inf Div  their total casualties were 25% of their total strength  The casualties of the Mounted were about 70  We were attacked in our rear by 3000 Turkish reinforcements
