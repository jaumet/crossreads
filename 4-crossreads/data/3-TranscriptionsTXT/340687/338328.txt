 January, 1917. Wednesday 24 Usual &ndash; one pontoon although another should have come up in afternoon. Intense cold &amp; keen enough to cut. Fine all day and much aerial activity. Home at 4. Fritz shelled railway siding where are 4 naval 4&ndash;7" very heavily all the afternoon. Section not gone out to-night. May move on Friday so got my kit together to-night. Very great amount unfortunately.
