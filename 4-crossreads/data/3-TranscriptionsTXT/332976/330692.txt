 April 1916 2nd April-   We are traveling South &amp; have passed Wilson's Prom.  So far the weather has been splendid &amp; we have had a very plesant time. 3rd   We have passed Melbourne &amp; the weather is beginning to get bad. 4th   We are now in the Australian Bight &amp; the weather is very rough &amp; a heavy sea is running.  Many of the chaps are sick &amp; the parades have been cancelled. 5th   The weather is getting worse &amp; heavy showers of rain are falling at intervals
