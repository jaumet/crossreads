  
 Went to London & Edinburgh, had a good time but was glad to get back. 
 March 13th Left Upwey Jnct. for Plymouth & embarked on H.M.S. Dunvegan Castle same day. 
 March 14th Pulled out into stream to await completion of convoy. 
 March 15th High wind & choppy sea, issued w/- Red X kit. 
 March 16th Sailed at 5 p.m.  Large convoy & escort. Passed Eddystone Lighthouse at 6 p.m., ship rolling a bit.
