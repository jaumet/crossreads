<p>14th Order<br/>Orders issued midday to pack &amp; brand valises &amp; blankets.  Weather fair.</p>
<p>15th Wed.<br/>Alloted to specially divided &amp; equal strength companies;  no movement yet made.  Silent stunt from 2 to 6 a.m.</p>
<p>16th Thursday<br/>Very cold.  Day quiet, nothing doing.  Beautiful sunshine and much milder.  Silent 1-4 a.m.</p>
<p>17th Friday<br/>Day quiet &amp; beautifully bright.  Orders restricted re evacuation.  Mines connected with wires to No. 2 tunnels.  Tea the only cooked rations for evening meal.  Big fire at Anzac from midnight.  Weather Cloudy &amp; Foggy.</p>
