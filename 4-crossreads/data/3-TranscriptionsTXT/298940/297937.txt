On the way back we took a short cut across sand hills, where grass &amp; low bushes are growing sparsely. Our feet disturbed the virgin smoothness of the sand; in some places hard, &amp; others soft yielding &amp; in waves driven by strong winds. In one place it sloped away below us at a sharp angle right down to the little grassy creek 100 feet beneath.
