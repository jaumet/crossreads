  
 9/4/18 - Amiens-Albert Road The men round the trenches here are all miserable and dirty and very tired out.  The day has cleared up a bit and the sun has come out a bit.  Round here there are a fair number of dead lying about and we have been collecting any gear from the bodies.  Am considering the advisability of shifting H.Q. to another hole in the ground as this one is too consistently strafed at all hours.
