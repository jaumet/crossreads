Great things are expected of the forth coming Spring. The British hope to completely crush Germany. Tanks are [indecipherable] in galore. They are much faster than the first ones & much more deadly. Cavalry are to be in readiness  & from all reports we shall have Fritz on the run very soon.  Of course we are doing nothing now but hanging onto our captured positions, but there's going to be a scatter as soon as the fine weather starts.
