
<p>WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 13, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Got to Lisheen for</p>
<p>breakfast &amp; found them all</p>
<p>well. Went to town after &amp; met</p>
<p>Alan &amp; we went up to the King&#39;s</p>
<p>School Old Boys day at the</p>
<p>School &amp; stayed there for tea.</p>
<p>I got home early &amp; went to bed.</p>
