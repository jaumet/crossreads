<p>22nd Friday<br/>Guns very busy all round.  Dull all day with light rain from about 3 p.m.  Turks using Grenade Mortar on Courtneys Post.  We returned fire with our Mortar &amp; they immediately stopped theirs.</p>
<p>23rd Saturday<br/>Dull &amp; bitterly Cold, later sunshine but wind kept shaded places very chilly.  Much heavy firing at Narrows all morning.</p>
<p>24 Sunday<br/>Weather as 23rd.  Guns still busy.</p>
<p>25 Monday<br/>Weather finer &amp; warmer.  Front quiet.</p>
