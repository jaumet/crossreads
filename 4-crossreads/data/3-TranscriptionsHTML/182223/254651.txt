<p>Monday 5&nbsp;April 1915<br />A severe dust storm today. The wind began about 2 a.m and continued till about 6 p.m.&nbsp;<br />In Cairo for an hour. Spoke with General Williams at Shepheards hotel&nbsp;<br />Writing &amp; reading all day.</p>
<p>Tuesday 6&nbsp;April 1915<br />Acting O.C. today Colonel Martin away.&nbsp;<br />Letters from Car, Joe &amp; Kitty.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7&nbsp;April 1915<br />Acting O.C. today Colonel Martin at Alexandria&nbsp;<br />Letters from Mrs Fraser of New Port. Isle of Wight.</p>
