 August 1917 Monday 27th Still wet &amp; rough. Tuesday 28th It cleared up for a bit this morning but came on worse this afternoon. I am getting better now   I don't think I will have to go under an operation after all Thursday 30th Weather still rough. Friday 31 Fritz paid us the first visit for a week owing to bad weather. Saturday Sept. 1st I am pretty right again did a bit of work the first for 3 weeks Sunday 2nd Shifted back to Hazebrouck this morning &amp;amp then on to Wiskque this afternoon they are building it is well out in the country so we should be safe from airraids.
