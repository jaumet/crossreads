Dec 11th  We made our trip on supplies & returned at 2.15 P.M. to Park & cleaned the lorry up a bit. Weather fine & not too cold - We saw Fritz come over & get two baloons, forcing the observers to descend by Parachute shooting at them the while with the machine gun however they all landed alright - The boys voted on the conscription issue - mostly against it - Fred & I went to see "the Barn Owls" in Bailleul in the evening  they were very good - I had to put Joe to bed again when he came home.
 
Dec 12th  I stayed in to-day & cleaned the buss - fine day & not too cold - we went up town in the afternoon , I bought some silk & lace table centres to send away  Fritz came over in strength in full daylight & dropped bombs along the Bailleul-Armentieres road. he got seven men of the 5 A.S.P. & put the wind up a good many more
 
Dec 13th  I went out on Supplies this morning, got back 3.P.M. I sent parcel away - also received a parcel from Glasgow friend. which included shortbread etc. Fred came in & spent the evening with me  he is going on a trip to-morrow to Boulogne  Poor old Tom & Jackie were killed to-day
