<p>19/12/17.<br/>70 N.C.O's and men baked 35760 lb rations. 1 man proceeded on leave to U.K. and 1 rejoined unit from hospital.</p>
<p>20/12/17.<br/>70 N.C.O's and men baked 36860 lb rations.</p>
<p>21/12/17.<br/>70 N.C.O's and men baked 38400 lb rations. 1 conviction of an offence of A.W.L. in London from 7.30 a.m. 3/12/17 to 10.15 a.m 6/12/17. Punishment awarded. Deprived of 4 days' pay.</p>
<p>22/12/17.<br/>70 N.C.O's and men baked 39280 lb rations.</p>
<p>23/12/17.<br/>47 N.C.O's and men baked 25860 lb rtns.</p>
<p>24/12/17<br/>1 man proceeded on leave. 70 N.C.O's and men baked 38720 lb rations.</p>
<p>25/12/17.<br/>1 shift, viz., 6am to 2pm of 23 N.C.O's and men baked 13120 lb rations</p>
<p>26/12/17.<br/>2 shifts, viz., 6.am to 2p.m and 2pm to 10pm, of 46 N.C.O's and men baked 21120 lb rtns.</p>
