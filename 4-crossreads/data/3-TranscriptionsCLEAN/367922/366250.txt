  
 The Australian Museum, Sydney, 1st. May, 1914. 
 This is to certify that Mr. Dene B. Fry has been in the service of the Trustees of the Australian Museum for 6 and 1/2 years as a Cadet & Junior Assistant in the sections of Invertebrate Zoology and of Reptiles, Snakes and Fishes, during which period he has had opportunities of making himself proficient in the subjects. 
 He has passed his professional examinations in Zoology at the Technical College & the University with honours and performed his Museum duties to the entire satisfaction of his Superior Officers. 
 H. H. B. Bradley President 
 R. Etheridge Curator 
 S. Sinclair Secretary.
