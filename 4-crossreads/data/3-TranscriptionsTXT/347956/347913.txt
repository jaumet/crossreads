 8.3.15 
 Mon 8th Paraded sick again this morning &amp; my sickness was named Diptheria by the old quack.  I do not think he knows enough to be able to tell what it is, but the tent I camp in had to be pulled down &amp; disinfected this morning I hope none of the others catch dip. although I do not feel too bad myself &amp; am enjoying a good holiday on the strength of it &amp; I am satisfied to let it go as diphtheria until I get proof that it is not.
