The Veterinary Surgeon has been having a very busy time the whole trip; nine horses have died up to date, and the carcases consigned to the deep; for this operation the ship is brought to a standstill, the carcase hauled out on a rope, and dropped; as soon as it is clear of the propellers, the ship is set in motion again, and what once was a valuable horse, left to satisfy the greed of sharks.Thursday18th Feb.  The sea still turbulent, and every now and then a squall, with the sky a dirty grey, and the atmosphere muggy, makes a person feel rather ill-disposed towards the tropical part of the Indian Ocean.
