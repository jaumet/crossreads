
<p>1054</p>
<p>SUNDAY, APRIL 13, 1919.</p>
<p>44</p>
<p>Fine. Got inside the heads at</p>
<p>Melbourne &amp; dropped anchor off</p>
<p>Portsea about 6 AM. Got all the</p>
<p>troops off &amp; they went into</p>
<p>quarintine. We got away again</p>
<p>at 1 pm. Read &amp; yarned to</p>
<p>the sisters all day</p>
<p>Days run 204 miles</p>
