 Have heard no news of Kollosche or Dickson &amp; Stafford, &amp; received no letters from the unit. 
  Monday 1st April 1918  Yesterday &ndash; Easter Sunday &ndash; we had a church parade in the morning and in the afternoon I volunteered for a job which was done in five minutes, &amp; so had a slack afternoon. This morning I turned up at the boxing ring and did some club exercises &amp; swinging, after which the Sergeant instructor &ndash; one, Mick Walsh &ndash; gave me some hints on how to defend against a straight left.
