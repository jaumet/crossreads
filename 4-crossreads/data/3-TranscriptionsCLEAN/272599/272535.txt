  
 October Sunday 14 1917 
 Parker sent on leave today.   My turn it should have been.   Am wondering why it was altered. Afternoon off.   MacB & I out for walk.  tea in Wimereux.   Met Jock.  Malaga & Porto.  Back church.   [indecipherable]  Billy Leeming in I ward.   Marked up for Blighty tomorrow.
