
No 3 Australian General Hospital Mudios - west - Lemnos23.8.1915My beloved Mother & Father - Brother & sistersI am sorry I have not been able to write before.The tremendous difficulties in which we have started to nurse 800 wounded. The sickness of our staff - Surgeons - Sisters, & Orderlies leaving us short handed - no equipment - but what we took from the Red Cross stores on Store ships in the harbour - including the marquees & tents for patients & sisters & officers.The lack of any food - but Bully beef & hard ration biscuits - Badly made tea - Kerosene for Primus Stove or methylated spirits - condensed milk - Bread - sour made at the Ration bake house
