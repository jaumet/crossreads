<p>19 Th April<br/>Travelled all night in Position at dawn terrible bombardment with B. Guns  tanks &amp; Gas used  Fokkers doing damage with bombs  no advance made</p>
<p>20 Fri April<br/>travelled until 1 am to 3 miles W of [indecipherable]  dog tired and very sleepy</p>
<p>21 Sat April<br/>moved to new camp about 3 miles W of J.  water very scarce  General Chauvel left  Anzac Div for Desert  Column Headqtrs  also sent with him</p>
