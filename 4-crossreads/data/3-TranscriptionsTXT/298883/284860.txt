 25th The 53rd Bn. held their Bn. Sports today. 
 Our boys still on strike. 
 26th This morning I watched the boys of the 54th that is the strikers as they might be termed hold a battalion parade under the command of a full blown private and they behaved splendidly.  Bde sports were held this   evening  afternoon and went off O.K.  I was assistant starter and bawler out until my throat got sore when  the  Major Lucas took on the job.
