 20 Nov 18 Proceeded up the river &amp; anchored off Charlestown with the Fleet 
  21 Nov 18  Proceeded to sea with Fleet in early morning. In the Forenoon we met &amp; took under escort a portion of the German Fleet off May Id consisting of 50 Destroyers, 8 Light Cruisers, 5 Battle Cruisers &amp; 10 Battleships. We brought them back to Scotland &amp; anchored off Inchkeath. It was a grand sight after 4 &frac12; years of war to watch the German ships going by in single file ahead, &amp; to realise that the war was really over.
