
Sunday 22Church parade
Monday 23Warned to move to another part of the camp tomorrow.
Tuesday 24Move to No 3 camp, 1st Command Depot, a hundred yards or so, where I believe we will go through preliminary training
Wednesday 25Training in earnest, physical jerks etc & I think I will enjoy it, even though it is taking perspiration out of me.Loafing about makes one sluggish & the jerks are just what I need.
Friday 27Still in No 3 camp, No 1 Command depot where we are doing physical jerks & route marches daily. Saw Barnby Brown to-day
Saturday 28Concert at Y.M.C.A. by Lena Ashwell. Very decent, classical
