
Centre for Soldiers Wives & Mothers29.10.15.
Dear Mr. LaytonI am sending Mrs. Thorne to you. Asking if you could give her some assistance got behind in rent through serious illness of children - & if the 1 she is owing 10/- for rent & 10/- for eggs principally could be paid she would be alright. Mr. Hyde knows of Mrs. Thorne
Yours trulyE. H. Bennie
