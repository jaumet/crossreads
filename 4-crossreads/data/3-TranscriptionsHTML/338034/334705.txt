<p>very little to eat during this time, so we had breakfast at Sheiks Zowaiid, after which we again saddled up &amp; rode in a further 16 miles to El Arish arriving there at 2 p.m. on the 10th inst. very weary &amp; tired. I had the luck to be one of a party of 24 men under Major Irwin, who early in the fight were detailed off to rush what appeared to be some snipers in dugouts about 60 yards in front of our position, there was no one there as it happened, but 15 yards further on we encountered a small Turkish trench occupied by 25 men.
