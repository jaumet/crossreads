<p>&amp; then after dinner we went to the rink for some skating &amp; had quite a good time from the rink we went &amp; had a wash &amp; then tea in town &amp; finally landed home 15 minutes late through having to wait so long for buses I suppose there will be a row over it in the morning there was a concert here tonight but the majority of them get on my nerves so I lit a cigar &amp; strolled round the lovely gardens till it was all over, I enjoy this far more than the average singing.</p>
