30-12-16Spent most of next day resting and drying clothes which wasn't a success. Throat very sore yet & glands swollen. Going out again to-night. Spent the day in bed and cleaned up a bit and went out at 4-30 working with Alver who is not yet an efficient sapper. I had to do all the digging in the shaft and got a good deal behind the others.
