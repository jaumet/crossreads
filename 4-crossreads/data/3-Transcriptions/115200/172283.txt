<p>70&nbsp; 20/12/15</p>
<p>mudros island.</p>
<p>today we were inspected By the C.O. of the Convalescent Camp and he marked me C. This makes it the 2nd time that I have been marked&nbsp; This camp is called the Base Convalescent Camp and it is Situated at mudros west about 60 miles from Gallipoli Peninsula. This island is one mass of tents&nbsp; When the french get wounded they go home to france and get cured there of their wounds. There is no french hospitals on this island at any rate</p>
