 Saturday 10 August Fine day. 
 11th Bde line still crook. After much investigating &amp; annoyance ran D3 piece from pole crossing to Bde. OK. 
 Evening just on dark 23rd M.G. out. Nosing about until late but found M.G. moved up. Field Engrs &amp; Pioneers took the line. 
 To bed late.
