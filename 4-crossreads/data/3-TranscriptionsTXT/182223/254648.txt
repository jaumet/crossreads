 Thursday 25&nbsp;March 1915 Ride between 7 &amp; 8 a.m. &nbsp;Not well.&nbsp;Poor wine at St Joseph&#39;s upset me.&nbsp; Went to the Competitions,  at &nbsp;in drill &amp; physical&nbsp;exercises,&nbsp; at the  beteween the Primary Schools of the Egyptian govmnt, at the Saidieh&nbsp;secondary school from 4 to 6 pm. Six teams competed. &nbsp; Drill  Dress neat, drill good,&nbsp;exercises&nbsp;in some cases very good. The&nbsp;Under Secty&nbsp;for Education &amp; many teachers present. &nbsp;Youngsters in the teams enthusiastic. &nbsp;Much applause from onlookers.&nbsp; Home 7 p.m 
 Friday 26&nbsp;March 1915 Writing&nbsp;&amp; reading all day.&nbsp; For a ride  dur  between 5 p.m &amp; 6-20 pm. &nbsp;Across dessert, beyond the pyramids.&nbsp; Had photograph taken of Morgan the Nubian land.&nbsp; Photo taken of self at the Sphynx, on my horse 
 Saturday 27&nbsp;March 1915 Ride before breakfast&nbsp; Reading and writing most of the day&nbsp; Posted letters to The Girls, Mollie Dr Armitt [editor of the Medical Journal of Australia],&nbsp;Ted Buckley, Dr Arthur. Paper,&nbsp;Wenkley [Weekly] Dispatch&nbsp;came from Mrs Fraser (Mrs Bradley)&nbsp; Ride during the afternoon.&nbsp; Father McAuliffe called here during the morning.&nbsp;  Reading &nbsp;Letter from&nbsp;Dorothy Williams&nbsp;of date 21/1/15. It came via London &amp; the Brit. W. deptmnt. [British War Department?] 
 Sunday 28 March 1915 Mass at 9 a.m. &nbsp;Father McAuliffe.&nbsp;Full Congration&nbsp; A  dust  storm filled the air with sand all day, with the dusk &amp; moon the wind fell &amp; now 7-30 p.m.&nbsp;all is calm.&nbsp; Not out during the day.&nbsp; Wrote to Doffie Williams Mrs Knowles &amp; comm letters to Mollie.
