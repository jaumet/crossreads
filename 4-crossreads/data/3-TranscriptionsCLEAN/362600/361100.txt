  
 W 25 Sun Recd letters from Home 4. Nettie. Bess. Lottie. Edie. T. Peg. Super Ford Print Photos all night with Ellis & Tam. Card party afterwards & Ellis & Tam do not get or speak Wrote to Mum 
 F 26 Mon Dull & Windy. Have a clean up & go up to mess & play Billards & cards against Tappin & win Easily for the first time. 
 Wrote MRL 6 
 F 27 Tues. Pack up all Books for sending Home. Wash room out & generally tidy up. Go to bed & read a little. I read good news about France from George. Wrote to Dad. 
 F 28 Wed Go Bike ride & puncture. Take off tyres twice & can't manage. Come Home & Bath & play Cards all night till 11pm. Recd letter from Marie. Dream all night travelling everywhere in train. [indecipherable] 
 F 29 Th Mend Puncture & write all night. Recd letter from Auntie. We hear of am going into Barracks Rooms. Dream of [indecipherable] as Y.M.C.A workes & all Monty Girls. Peg. M & Bert. In Blighty. Much [indecipherable] in Public Wrote Auntie Annie. Miss Rowley. Dulce. Bess 
 F 30 Fri We play Billards all night & get wollapped & also win. I dream of 10.M & Ollie Cardle. Ellis is with me & [indecipherable] is very lovable & we sit on [Tailors Brand]. O is also amorous
