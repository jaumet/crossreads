<p>I was up early, had breakfast a haircut &amp; a shave. Then I took a bus(h) to Liverpool St. Station which is about half an hours ride from Horseferry Road. I called on the London bank of Australia and found that Willie was still up at Norfolk War Hospital. At 10am I took a train for Thorpe Norwich</p>
