<p>August Saturday 19 1917</p>
<p>A fine beautiful day. Religious festival in Boulogne. <br/>Jock Mac &amp; I walk into B &ndash; Esplanade. Listen, to Band &amp; ogle the lassies. Home to tea. Down to Wimereux after to celebrate Jocks birthday which we do with due ceremony. Walk on beach. Catch two Khaki  -----  Future appointments.</p>
