<p>May 1915</p>
<p>Sunday 16<br/><u>Fine</u>. Went into fire trench again last night &amp; stayed there till 6 PM tonight when we were shifted up to a supporting trench for a night's rest. We had to draw water for the cook's before turning in. Enemy very active of a night time. They have more machine guns very close to us &amp; a battery of big guns on our right.</p>
<p>Monday 17<br/><u>Fine</u> Came back to reserve trench this morning about 5 AM. Enemy busy with new battery on our right. More arguing the point with them.</p>
