
Deiran15/2/18Pack up and leave camp with HQ men and proceed to Latron.16thLeave 7 am and proceed.  Wagon nearly lost near Motza through break giving way.  Arrive Jerus 5 pm and meet Kenny & [indecipherable].  Arrive Bethlehem 6 PM and camp in an old Greek Monastry.17thWindy dirty Day18thPack up ready to move out.19thLeave Bethlehem 10 am with pack animals and have a very rough trip through the narrow streets.  Crowds of people line the way.  Wind up and down the bridle path and eventually get a glimpse of Jerusalem over all the rough country.  Awful rocky country having to lead the animals most of the way.  Have lunch 3 PM in a little grassy patch & move on and at dark camp up on the hill overlooking the Dead Sea.20th3 am move on again in rain and wind up and down, reaching the flats near a big redoubt which is being shelled heavily.  Spread out and get across as quickly as possible.  Hide under cover of the redoubt all day and get the wind up over a few close shots.  Erect station at dark and do some traffic.  Men set out
