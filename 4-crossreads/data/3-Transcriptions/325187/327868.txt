<p>[Thursday 4 May 1916]</p>
<p>Court again.  Finished CM at noon &amp; came Cairo with Judge Advocate worked it well to see Nowell &amp; stay 2 days, till Court is Dissolved-<br/>Nowell is thin but well has 3 months Holiday &amp; goes to England lucky beggar he is a Colonel very like his old Father &amp; a great row of Ribbons for Gippy decorations.<br/><strike>He wishes to be remembered to you all-<br/>I have taken a couple of Photos which I will send you-<br/>We have had a great yarn, but he tires easily-</strike></p>
