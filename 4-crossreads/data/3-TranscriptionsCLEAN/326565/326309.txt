
Thursday 24/2/16Issued with tin of tobacco, gift from Victorian Racing club.  Fatigue work practically all day.  Scratch concert arranged, at night which passed a very pleasant evening.
Friday 25/2/16Fell in on parade at 10 AM. in full marching order with blankets rolled & kit bags.  Parade dismissed & camp thoroughly cleaned up.All goods & equipment loaded on to 20th A.S.C. waggons & transported to railhead Serapeum West.  Issued with 24 hrs rations (1 tin
