  
 26. Thursday Frosty morning but grey afterwards.  Miss H. & I went down to Piccadilly to see President Wilson.  Great crowd so I missed Ethel & had tea with Mrs. French & the Sawyers in Club 
 27  Friday Worked so late had no dinner.  Went to see Ethel at Dysart Hotel at 8 &  then for night with Miss H. & Miss Newcomb 
 28 Saturday Worked all day & got to Dysart Hotel just in time to have dinner with Ethel and went to bed early & talked till past midnight.  E. had a cold & snored so I cd. not sleep
