<p>here whereas we have learnt how unecessary it all is &ndash; as one chap <strike>side</strike> up the line was boasting about his personal cleanliness &amp; said "he always had a wash once a week whether he wanted it or not"!</p>
<p>Well at this place it depended you were first or last on the night orderlies round But if you were unlucky enough to be first (&amp; I was for 1 week) he used to <strike>wake you up &amp; tell you</strike> bring a basin of hot water &amp; <strike>tell</strike> wake you &amp; tell you to sit up &amp; have a wash at 3.30 a.m. If you were lucky last it was 5. a.m.</p>
