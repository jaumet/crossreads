<p>Sunday 28th Jan<br/>Cold conditions still continue. Nothing much doing &amp; have at last got recq. through for supplies. Plenty of strafing going on with Taubs over to see what it means.</p>
<p>Monday 29th Jan.<br/>Drugs arrived at last so all is well. Report through which explains the bombardment. We had an advance yielding over 350 prisoners very good indeed. Weather still cold. Albert &amp; surroundings heavily shelled today. Divisions going through to take over more of the French front.</p>
<p>Tuesday 30th Jan<br/>Cold still continues with addition of snow, papers today stating that these are the coldest conditions experienced for 20 years. Our Christmas parcels arrived today, belated but welcome.</p>
<p>Wednesday 31st Jan</p>
<p>Still very cold. Usual duties but a little trouble in keeping up supply of ointment for the scabies. Am</p>
