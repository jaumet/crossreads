<p>Tuesday 2 April 1918<br/>News of Oliver on 30th March false &ndash; refers to officers captured in Mesopotamia.</p>
<p>Wednesday 3 April 1918<br/>Escort to bomb raid.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 3 h. 5 m.  A good trip to Amman, fired 500 rounds at troop train and Amman station at height of 1000 to 2000 ft., train put on steam and went like the Devil.</p>
