<p>92 Pitt St.<br/>April 27th 1916</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Horsefield &ndash;<br/>I am sending Mrs. Cassidy wife of a man who went into Dubbo Camp last night after enlistment 3 weeks ago</p>
<p>He was out of work for some time previously &amp; the wife is without funds until pay day.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>B. Booth.</p>
