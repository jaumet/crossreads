 27 Sunday Very tired.  Tried to work a little. Had sleep in afternoon. 
 S. McKay came for supper &amp; stayed till 11.30  grey day. 
 28 Monday Office routine  S. McKay called at 6 &amp; we had dinner at Colonial restaurant &amp; thence to Wyndhams to see 'The Law Divine by Esmond.  Grey day 
 29 Tuesday Finish grey day.   Office routine.  Ran about on errands a good deal.  Very exhausted. So bathed &amp; went to bed early. 
 30 Wednesday Delightful morning.  Plane tree leaves down a glorious carpet in Bedford Sq.  Alister came in evening.  I typed a little but still very fatigued.
