 There was a good deal of messing about transferring from one boat to another. As we were anchored out a splendid view was obtained of out [our] Battle Zone &amp; ridges &amp; the scene looked pretty dotted here &amp; there with white tent. The trip up to cape Helles was enjoyable. There one could see the wonderful preparations made on building a landing stage etc. a wall of stone &amp; then some large ships were sunk to make a breakwall.
