<p>We miss the Australian sunshine and warmth.  I hope you enjoyed yourself at Xmas did the children have a picnic how is the little girl with the dolls and Ethel I have not had leave yet and I have not been near Franks Batt yet may see him some day excuse this short note as I am to sick to write. Best of luck from your Brother Gnr Oscar Melly<br/>     </p>
