

February  191817. SundayAir raid at night. No damage near us.
18. MondayMiss H & IWent to hear Litvinoff in Cent. Hall, Westminster. Guns began firing at 9 and kept on a long time. Delayed us getting home.
19. Tuesday Sun At Miss Hodges. Slept at Mrs Richards 10 Farm Walk.
20. WednesdayWent with Mrs Oliver  to see Com. Kitchen at Child's hill. Tea with Mrs O. Rained a little.
