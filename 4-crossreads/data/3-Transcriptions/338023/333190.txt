<p>[Transcriber's Note: Henry Weston Pryce was born at Woolway Station in Monaro, NSW. He joined the 9th Machine Gun Company of the A.I.F. on 16 June 1916 and sailed from Melbourne in October 1916. He returned to Australia on 25 November 1917. Both his brothers died in the war. He was a salesman, insurance agent, clerk, soldier, journalist and poet. After the war he contributed verse and stories to Australian literary journals for several years and joined the staff of the Sydney Sun.]</p>
