 [May, 1916.] Monday 1 10th Reinf 17th Batt Ellis Skinner the first of above company to depart to the great unknown R.I.P Tuesday 2 I hardly know what to jot down, for today I am here right in the mess officers mess writing letters Wednesday 3 Strolling across the desert with Letty good company for Letty is a Terrier Thursday 4 I wonder, yes, I wonder where she is who she's with and what they are doing, good thoughts
