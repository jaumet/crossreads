<p>24th  Passed a real bad night &amp; this morning I feel as if every bone in my body wants to drop out, my head is aching like mad &amp; I feel weak all over so I have been in bed practically the whole day, I can eat nothing only drink a little port or a cup of coffee now &amp; again they wanted to send for the Dr but I stopped them, for I kept thinking it would go as suddenly as it came but no fear as the day wore on it got worse &amp; worse, about 2 oclock I thought I would get up &amp; go downstairs out into the bright sun &amp; air, but this did no good so I started unpacking all the parcels which I had sent Mr Duke during the past year I stuck at this till finished, then I had to go back to bed again, Mr Duke was beginning to get alarmed but I made out that I would be right in the</p>
