<p>Aust &amp; Amiens<br/>[November 1918]<br/>17 Sun<br/>A fine ride to Rebumfrie<br/>18 Mon<br/>An evening with the school mistress Mollie Albertine very interesting.(The germans shall never pass)<br/>19 Tues<br/>Waiting to go home, is going to be very monotonous, but when we get on the move the time will soon slip by<br/>20 Wed<br/>The weather is not to bad, it is very dry for this time of the year, but by jingo it is cold heavy frost and dense fogs</p>
