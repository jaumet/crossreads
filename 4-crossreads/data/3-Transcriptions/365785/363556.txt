<p>Tis awfully nice, so clean and good and I think that having to put on a pretty dinner dress and prink up does not really do any harm.<br/>I've got a perfectly dinky dinner gown I bought at Russells, dark brown ninon and lace &amp; brown glace and perfectly ducky brown shoes.  Got it at the sale for 49/6 reduced from 5 guineas.  Just fancy talking clothes these times but it is so long since I wore any that it is quite exciting to even think about them.<br/>Our Club is a steady success &ndash; we make no meteoric flights as to numbers like the Anzac but are just growing by degrees.
