<p>June &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>2 SUN &nbsp;Warm.</p>
<p>Fine day. Very quiet</p>
<p>Posted letter to</p>
<p>Else Parker. Preparing for</p>
<p>stunt tonight. Went to</p>
<p>Staple at night. Quiet.</p>
<p>3 MON &nbsp;Good success in &#39;hopover&#39;</p>
<p>250 Huns &amp; m. guns</p>
<p>mortars etc. Fine day. cool</p>
<p>Amm: going up. Quiet.</p>
<p>Stayed in at night.</p>
<p>4 TUES &nbsp;Fine day. Cool. Amm.</p>
<p>going up. Out on byke in</p>
<p>aft. Very quiet no bombs.</p>
<p>Playing bridge at night</p>
<p>5 WED &nbsp;Fine day. Cool. Bath</p>
<p>Washing. Posted letter to Gladys</p>
<p>Rec. letter from Julie. Quiet</p>
<p>Amm. going up. Writing</p>
