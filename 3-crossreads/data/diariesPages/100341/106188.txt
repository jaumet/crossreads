May 17th
Physical drill at 9 p am. Medical inspection at 10. and lecture on boat drill Boat drill at 2 Pm. given positions only in case of alarm. games from 3 till 4 pm. After tea singing and writing. Bed 8.30 pm. Weather still showery, wind sea steadier but still cold, full speed again, W.S.W. direction WNW 195 Miles.
