 August,1918 Saturday 3 Ribemont and Lamotte. Weather wet and miserable. Journeyed over the lines with the fellows taking over, and mended two lines that were down. Packed up kit and proceeded to W.L. arriving there at 4 am. Had a fairly long walk up to Lamotte. Battery position at the new place visited by Ammo wagons. Thompson lost his best horse and there were seven horses killed one man wounded Taylor. Weather still wet.
