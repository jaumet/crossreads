Probably Jan 29 or thereabouts - I suppose you got my last bit of journal - Did R get his Ambulance triangular sling - Get & give your Mother a bottle of Port wine - One glass +oz  ter die sunendus [three times a day] - with or after food - She's very strong & of good constitution - After paying off, in the eveg I went to see Hedley & got a joy ride back to my wharf with Dr Kestevery - Old Brick wanted me to do a Locum but it wd be too flat to leave the fighting men unless compelled to - Love to all - Fred -
