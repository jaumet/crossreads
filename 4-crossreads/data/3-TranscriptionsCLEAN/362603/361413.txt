
3.  Ellis & Ern. & Myself are still here & are likely to stay here for some time.  Dave is also with us.  We expect to be here for a long, long time.  I was so sorry to hear about poor Sandy in Dad's letter   It is to be hoped He is quite better & not suffering any ill effects.  Well Mum, I have no news at all.  I am well as is Dave & Ellis also.
