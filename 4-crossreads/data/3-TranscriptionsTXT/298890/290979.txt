
21. Thursday.Glorious spring weather. Feeling weak in morning. Better during day and did good work. Went for a walk & called on Mrs Oliver. Sun all day.
22. FridayLovely weather. Offensive of Germans in France.
23. SaturdayWent down town to bank etc. Lovely day came home on top of bus. Quinine [indecipherable]
WeekendTerribly depressed by quinine.
