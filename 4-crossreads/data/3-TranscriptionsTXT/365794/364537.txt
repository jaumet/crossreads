
Wednesday 16 [15] May 1917 [1918]Town with Sisters Soar & Furnifull.  Tea at Club then shopping.  Drew pay Pt. 300.Wrote Nell long letter.Wrote Mrs. Simpson.Wrote S. Leed.Questioned M.O. re this last attack.  No dysentery germ but a lot of colitis going around, probably that, no fear of recurrence.  Going out to Bulkeley in a day or two.
