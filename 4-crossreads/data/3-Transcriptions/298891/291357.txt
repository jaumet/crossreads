<p>1917 October</p>
<p>October 1 Monday</p>
<p>Miss Stephens &amp; I went over to take a little photograph of the Serb. Cemetry.  Not bad excepting for leg.  Miss Stephens, Sister Kerr &amp; I had a lesson with Milesov &amp; then I had a talk with Dr. De Garis.</p>
