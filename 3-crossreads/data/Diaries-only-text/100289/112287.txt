Gradually getting to know my way about. Again wrote home.
Saturday 18.8.17
Drew money at Adelaide Bank; saw St Pauls, Mint Bank of England. Went to see the changing of Guard but owing to the rain did not wait; saw Buckingham Palace St James Palace. Afternoon went to Putney by bus which is perhaps the best way of seeing the city. Went to the "Globe" at night saw Gaby Deslys in "Suzette". Again wrote home sent a few books of views.
Sunday 19.8.17
Wended our way to Middlesex St commonly known as Petticoat Lane, pays one to keep their hands well down south on their money while there. Before lunch went to Hyde
