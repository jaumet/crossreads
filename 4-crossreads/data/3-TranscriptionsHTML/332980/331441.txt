<p>down hill to Barliu where another long delay took place, as I had then been on duty some 14 hours I was getting tired &amp; during these delays I had a good sleep, leaving Barliu I soon arrived at Hirson close on 8 am where I had to shunt &amp; place my train for unloading &amp; then started to prepare for my return journey leaving there attatched to another Engine at 10 am. we made a splendid run through Barliu, Bruay passing in each place large numbers of Trucks of Coal waiting to be lifted, there are a large number of Coal Mines round about &amp; the Country being very hilly &amp; all under cultivation dotted here &amp; there with small clumps of Trees, &amp; with the large number of large Chimneys &amp; rows of Houses with their tile roofs all assisted to make a beautiful picture, &amp; a lovely panoramic view, there is no mistake the <strike>country in</strike> France is a beautiful Country &amp; very charming to the Eye.
