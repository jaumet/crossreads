 Jan 28 Went to Major T J Lynch &amp; Lt P Potts, Austr. Overseas Base Gherzich &amp; Ghizeh. Official opening of Mess with a Great night. 29 Adjt. Lt. Norris 2nd LH sent for by Major Glasgow: apptd Lt Mossop with Lt Chaffey as assistant Adgt. 30 Sunday Todays state 1935: Church parade 1750(?) Motored to Maadi with Vaughan to see Geof: require another Med Off. [Imp?] Bus &amp; a LH Regt sent out in connection with Egyptian reservists mobilization. 31st Musketry classes going. Major Granville called at Nile Cold Storage. Cadillac car peremptorily called in &amp; report asked for by GOC as to why I did not do it before. I had had Gen'l Spens promise, &amp; Bde instructions. Went to town &ndash; Major Maxted: Stein &amp; B.C. Patches. Budden: Capt Belfield; Major Taylor: tools: trumpet mouthpieces Lt Norris left 5 a.m. with horses for WARDAN
