 Thursday 24th Aug. 1916 
 Received a wire from Lily to effect that Dad is at Eastbourne &amp; she has wired him. No more news of leave yet. Luna Park Hosp. crowd were told today on parade that they are disbanded now &amp; to be split up. Everybody went to Tidworth RAMC sports today &ndash; no parades &ndash; I stayed in in case dad should come. Writing letters &amp; cleaning up generally.
