  
 Dec. 25th Xmas day - No Armistice - but absence of shells, gives us a peaceful day. Xmas pudding issue - Nothing startling occurred. 
 Dec. 26th. Boxing Day - Asher Chowne - night duty - as usual - Fritz's erratic firing - very few wounded cases but scores of "trench feet" pass through us. Capt. Power. 
 Dec. 27th. German shells & bombs near our camp. "peace" negotiations continue - night duties. 
 Dec. 28th. A Taube that caught us napping. Our bearers leave for [Indecipherable] - Sleep during day. 
 Dec. 29th. I meet one of the Fountains - trench feet - Artillery very quiet - Anxiety for mail - few cases
