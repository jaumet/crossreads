<p>Friday 18 May 1917.</p>
<p>No sight of land yet. Right up close to &quot;Encounter&quot; (cruiser) this morning. Rough at first but not quite so later.</p>
<p>I finish up my mess orderly duties with G.S.S.Crmn&nbsp;today, for a month or so.</p>
<p>Expect to. sight land tomorrow&nbsp;evening.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Still in a northerlyly direction as on Thursday 17&nbsp;<sup>th&nbsp;</sup>inst</p>
<p>Saw with 2 of the boys a&nbsp;<s>&quot;transparent&quot;&nbsp;</s>&nbsp;&quot;flying fish&quot;. An uncommon sight so far south in a cold climate.</p>
