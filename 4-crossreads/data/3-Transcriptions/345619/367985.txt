<p>[Reverse of the above postcard. No stamp or date]<br/>Miss Ruth Burrowes<br/>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>N.S.W.<br/>Australia</p>
<p>One of the colors off my coat is for you &amp; one for Frank. Mum might sew it on your dress or hat, if you would like her to, cos she is a nice Mum. Good-bye little girl for a little while. lots of love and kisses from your old Dad.</p>
