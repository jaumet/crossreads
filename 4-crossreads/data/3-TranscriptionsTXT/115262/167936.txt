
Monday 18 June 1917.
Still in the Red Sea. A hot day but breeze makes the day a little pleasant. No boats seen today.
Universal kit bags brought up from "hold" & during afternoon we were given access to them.
Special parade at 1.45 pm. on account of a letter written by E.P.O'R. of A.C.B.F.A. I was exempted from parade and so arranged my kit ect [etc] prior to disembarkation which we expect will be Wed. morning. Reveille will be at 5 a.m. tomorrow morning.
Passed the "Deathless" lighthouse at 5 p.m.
