  
 No. 12039 Priv. W. Pearson 9th Field Ambulance 3rd Aust. Division (9th Brigade) 1st May 1916 
 2 Short St. Leichhardt. 
 May 1916 
 Leaving Sydney 
 Monday 1st Arriving at the Agricult. Ground after leave at 2  p.m.  a.m. & slept for about half an hour. Reveille at 3 a.m. when it was fairly cold. After a roll call & a tedious wait we finally commenced to move at 5 a.m. & friends & relations joined us all along the route which was along Randwick Rd., Oxford St. to Darlinghurst thence Bourke St. to Wolloomoloo [Woolloomooloo] Mother, father, Harry, May (sis), W.B., Arch Flack.
