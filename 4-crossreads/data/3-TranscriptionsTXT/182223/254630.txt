 Thursday 21 January 1915 At Mena House Everyone working hard getting hospitals into working order 
 Friday 22 January 1915 Same as Thursday Two operations,&nbsp;inguial hernias 
 Saturday 23 January 1915 Camp being erected rapidly. Officers &amp; men working very well, especially Major Read, Captain Mackenzie, and Captain Clayton.&nbsp; Col. Springthorpe officious &amp; boyrish [boorish?]&nbsp;as usual. Young officers slow &amp; not punctual 
 Sunday 24 January 1915 Camp ready for inspection. Major General Bridges inspected the camp at&nbsp;11-15 am. Said &quot;excellent&quot;.&nbsp; &nbsp;Not able to be at Mass. &ndash;&nbsp;Operation.
