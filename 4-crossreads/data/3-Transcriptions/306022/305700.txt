<p>Friday 6th<br/>Disappointment for nearly all those who got up early to get the first sight of land. It was not sighted till about 8 o'clock and we were in at the wharf by 11.30 am, where were several ladies with cases of fruit which they threw to us as soon as we were anchored.</p>
