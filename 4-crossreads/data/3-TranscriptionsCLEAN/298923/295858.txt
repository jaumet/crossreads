  
 11 July 1918 Thursday So Joe has sailed!!  Never mind Mother dear we will  all come back soon-  Received letters from you April 28 May 5 & May 12 May 19th- & note all your news.  Also from May & Joe & Mrs Strange.  Note all you say re House   Sir Sam etc, it is brave of you little Mother & I cannot advise so far away   One thing certain Joe will profit by it.  His job is there for him & he needed a change badly will write c/- Laura.
