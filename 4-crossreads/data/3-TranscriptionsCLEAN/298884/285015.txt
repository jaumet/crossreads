  
 A medical officer came on board & inspected the hands & arms of everybody on board to satisfy himself that no small pox existed on board. Despite the fact that we have so far a very clean health bill it was reported in the W.A  newspaper  Times that a case of small pox had broken out on our boat soon after leaving Colombo.
