<p>15/3/17<br/>A cold day.  Still waiting at C.C.S. for eye treatment.  Charlie Monaghan, Reg &amp; I went to see a Show in Albert at night run by an Australian Troupe.  Very good.</p>
<p>16th<br/>Did not go to Amiens for eye test after all.  Got discharge from Becordel &amp; rejoined Unit at Guidecourt.  Expect to move forward tomorrow.</p>
<p>18th<br/>15th Div. moved fordward of the German Lines on the ridge at Bapaume today.  Nos. 2 &amp; 3 Sections, Capt. Carr in charge, were attached to the 59th Battn.  We moved right forward with very little resistance as far as Fremicourt &amp; were held up at a point about 1 mile forward of this village and after about 2 hrs. skirmishing this position was taken with few casualties.  Beugny was entered late in evening &amp; a little resistance met with &ndash; patrols going in at night but it was abandoned &amp; will be enveloped tomorrow.  The Huns have destroyed everything possible in there retreat &ndash; Churches, houses &amp; deep Dugouts have been mined and destroyed.  Fruit trees cut down &amp; long avenues of trees destroyed along the Main Rd.  Much wilful destruction has been carried on in all the villages.</p>
