Shops close now on their own at 6 to 7 p.m. instead of 10 p.m.  Honestly the state of affairs far worse than I should care to expose in this diary.  Personally I have seen mean things done of which a Britisher would be ashamed off.  The riots mostly done by Englishmen & Scotchmen.  We went to a Welsh concert tonight given by Welsh troops.  They sung beautiful & the Hall which held thousands was full & well patronized.
17.11.18Still refitting altering our Crew quarters for the Indians & extra cooking places fixed on deck & refitting the troop decks for white troops.  Several hospital ships arrived with sick troops from Beyrout, Palastine & several left for the Dardanelles for returned & exchanged prisnors of war.  Foodstuff transports arriving daily otherwise things are quite re naval matters.  British, French & Jap ships of war laying peacefully at their moorings & several troopships preparing for to take the troops away from Alexandria of which there are here alone upwards of 80,000 in camp ready for shipment.
