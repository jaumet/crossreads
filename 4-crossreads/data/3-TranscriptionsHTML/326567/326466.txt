After remaining down for a few Moments it arose again &amp; the fun was all over. Those Balloons go up every evening also a large number of Aeroplanes, &amp; Battleplanes, the latter fly around in number from 6 to 10 in numbers, they travel very fast &amp; very high. After getting a quart of Fresh milk for 5d &amp; 4 Ginger Breads for 6d my Tent Mate &amp; myself had cupper. There is no mistake I enjoy a good drink of Milk before retiring &amp; it is about the only thing reasonable here, Biscuits are sold at 2 &ndash; 3 a Penny about 5d for a &frac14;  of a lb.<br/>Saturday 16/6/17.<br/>I was awakened about 4 am by quick fireing close at hand &amp; I jumped up &amp; looked out of the Tent &amp; saw shells bursting up in the air, Fritz was over the lines &amp; the Air Craft guns were trying to bring him down with what result I could not say. It only lasted a few moments &amp; it ceased. During the afternoon an Observation Balloon came down in Flames, the pilot jumped with his Parachute</p>
