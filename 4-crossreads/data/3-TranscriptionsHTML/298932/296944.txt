<p>Staple<br/>Monday 20<br/>The hun, in planes was over this way last night dropping bombs not much damage done<br/>Tuesday 21<br/>To St Omer with Lt. Maynard in this town there are girls of the woman's army, poor little English girls, at the mercy of the troop's<br/>Wednesday 22<br/>A perfect day, bright sunshine and blue sky's, spent by me in farm labour, just like being at home<br/>Thursday 23<br/>Not yet one &amp; washing done, but it was hard work for Madam kept me at it all the time</p>
