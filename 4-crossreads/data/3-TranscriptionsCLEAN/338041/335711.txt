  
 June (1916) 
 June 17th Nothing unusual happened during day 
 June 18th Frenchy Sunday -attend Mass at 5.30pm (6.30 by watch) - Artillery at Ypres silent - 2 Taubes brought down - I leave with party for Bois-Grenier 
 June 19th Day duty in Dressing Room - 
 June 20th Huns shell Armentieres - Church set on fire by their shells - Seen by us at Bois-Grenier - wilful destruction. Very few cases - Death of Von Moltke
