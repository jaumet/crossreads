 The big armoured cars, or tanks as we know them by are all the talk at the present time, I believe they are fearful &amp; wonderful things &amp; are going to play an important part in this present war. 
 20th.    Another miserable rainy morning &amp; the ground around is swimming with water &amp; is that slippery that you hardly dare trust yourself on it, you have no idea what it is like.
