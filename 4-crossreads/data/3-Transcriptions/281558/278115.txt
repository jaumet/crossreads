<p>Gill to Malo after dinner &amp; there was a fair crowd there : on duty 10 pm :</p>
<p>Monday June 3rd</p>
<p>Dunkirk : stroll after tea with Gil : on duty 10 pm :</p>
<p>Tuesday June 4th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : went round to my Frog friends house after tea &amp; had a yarn : on duty 10 pm : air raid :</p>
<p>Wednesday June 5th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : went out to the Aerodrome with Gil after tea : very interesting : the H. P. are a monster machine alright &amp; Fritz must get a rough time from their mighty roams over his way : on duty 10 pm : air raid &amp; very lively : plenty of bombs &amp; a heavy barrage too :</p>
<p>Thursday &amp; Friday June 6th &amp; 7th</p>
<p>Nothing doing : stroll after tea with Gil : raid alarm</p>
