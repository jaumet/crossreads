 Monday 4.9.16 Sent parcel home (registered) containing paper knife &amp; brooches,  Expect to have all patients evacuated &amp; be away from here by end of week. 
 Tuesday 5.9.16 Bleak &amp; Cold; rained practically the whole of the day.
