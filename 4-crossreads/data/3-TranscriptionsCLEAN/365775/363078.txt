  
 however we got out alright. We are now the 54th Bty, half of our original battery the 60th coming to the 54th the other half going to the 53rd & so making a six gun battery. I might state here the idea of this in the first place, the four gun battery were formed to cover a wider front not having too many guns, but now we have the guns we are able to revert to the original six gun batteries.
