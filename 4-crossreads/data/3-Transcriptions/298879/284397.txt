<p>June 1915<br/>Sunday 27<br/>Hot. A church service was held at the church tent this morning at 9 AM. Also a confirmation service at 6.30 AM. I attended neither I am ashamed to say.</p>
<p>Monday 28<br/>Hot   Many warships have moved out of the harbour here. Judging from the noise last night there is evidently a big bombardment going on at Cape Helles.</p>
