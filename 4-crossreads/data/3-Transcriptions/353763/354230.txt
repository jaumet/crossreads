<p>one goes one should first of all follow up your own game &amp; see &amp; learn all you can   -  then those adnexa that be nearest    -  in my case &ndash; Natural history &ndash; finally a strong interest in other mens pursuits &ndash; both mental &amp; physical  -<br/>I walked along the edge of the bay over black sand &ndash; really cinders to the pier I saw 2 sandpipers  -  Talked to Cumberledge of the destroyers  -  He's Chief there  -  The Warrego is his home  -  Then our boat came alongside &amp; off we went to the Upolu  -<br/>I wrote to you &amp; sent a P.C  as well  -<br/>To wind up Rabaul for good let me summarise &ndash; we've been here 3 weeks &amp; 3 days &amp; taking all in all I consider I've not done amiss  -  Collecting is <strike>was</strike> quite out of the question in war time but I saw &amp; observed a great deal  -  I spent more time on shore than any sailor man &amp; went further &amp; did more  -  Now we're off &amp; I have a good general idea of New Britain &amp; Rabaul  -  Its inhabitants its geology &ndash; its flora &ndash; its dry weather &amp; glorious sea breezes &amp; its coco palm &amp; fringe of greenness<br/>Vale Rabaul</p>
