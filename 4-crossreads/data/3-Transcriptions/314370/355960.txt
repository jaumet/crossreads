<p>January, 1916<br/>Sunday 2<br/>Went to church parade &amp; then had dinner w/- dad<br/>We went into Heliopolis Music Hall<br/>Had supper at 9.30 &amp; turned in about 10.30 pm.</p>
<p>Monday 3<br/>All men returned from M.E.F. are paraded with a view of returning to our battalion. But hear no further news except that the 4th Bde is at Tel-el-Kebir</p>
