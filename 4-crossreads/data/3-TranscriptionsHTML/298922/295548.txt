<p>Sunday 26 August 1917</p>
<p>The Y.M.C.A. TENT is on the Beach &amp; is a good boon to the men &ndash; PIANO, writing material a CINEMA at night &amp; free issue of cocoa at night. Besides selling Biscuits, cakes, etc:  I notice H.L.A. is doing a lot in HAY to help the Y.M.C.A., it is a worthy institution.</p>
<p>Went Church but the sound of the waves drowned the Preacher's voice.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>Our Artillery is daily remains the Turk effectives but at least 7&percnt; &amp; very large preparations are being made here for our advance out this time. Cavalry will be used for proposed job we hope. No more frontier attack recommended at entrenched TURKS.</p>
