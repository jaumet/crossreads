<p>5.10.16<br/>this morning to ride to the station at Kantara &amp; had a good trip to Sidi Bisha arriving here at 4.30 I slept a good part of the journey as the Sergeants &amp; Warrant Officers had a carriage to ourselves we were not crowded.<br/>On arrival at the station we were marched into the Camp &amp; orders were read by an English Officer who seems a decent chap (but by rights he should still be at School) it was too late to get passes to visit Alexandria tonight so we went in without  after walking a couple of miles we got a carriage &amp; drove to a tram terminus &amp; went in by tram;  Sidi Bisha Camp is</p>
