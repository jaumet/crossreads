 We returned via the old tow path along the Somme River all the way.  The water was dark and sombre looking with a quiet smooth surface except where it rushed over the weirs. 
 28th News still good.  The germans have retired a long way and the 8th Bde. is following up.  I came off parade at 12.30 p.m. and was told to report B.O.R. as soon as possible which I did and after a hasty meal bolted off on a cycle with L/cpl. Grinter to meet the Staff Captain in Proyart
