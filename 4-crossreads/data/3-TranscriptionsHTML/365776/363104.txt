<p>forget, never a day went by but some comrade or other paid the supreme sacrifice.</p>
<p>here again, our Battery is wiped out, or very nearly so. I will just tell you one of the close calls I had. I was in charge of a party of our drivers packing ammunition, all ammunition being taken to the guns by pack horses, being impossible to get waggons up to the guns &amp; far too dangerous.</p>
<p>well as I was saying, we were pushing through one of Fritz's barrages that he kept up continually, along the roads &amp; tracks, when a great piece of shell that burst a few yards from us. went right through the throat of the horse</p>
