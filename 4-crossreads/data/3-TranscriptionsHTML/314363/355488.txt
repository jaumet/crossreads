<p>&amp; had the task of squaring things up, we remained in the sap for a couple of hours, shifting along as he shelled, then went over to <strike>new</strike> old position, &amp; reached the sig. office in another dugout close by the old one.<br/>It was not a great while before we had most of the lines good again.<br/> We changed over with the 56th that night. I &amp; another remained up at the relay untill the changeover was completed. We left about 12.15Am, &amp; hurried back to HQ.<br/><strike>The next evening was told</strike> We had about 60 casualities including a good few killed including one officer Mr Stafford, who was killed while doing some good work in the german lines.<br/>The next evening was told that I had to leave at once for the school, so left shortly after, &amp; soon obtained cart which took me right into Tehoussoi where the real HQ was.<br/><strike>Tha</strike> While there I recommended the Sgt. for decoration for the work he did. Then rode over to the Div. pay-office at [space left blank] to draw money for the school I drew 150f.<br/>Arriving back I turned</p>
