  
 Tamworth, & England. 
 After we marched home from the service the great event of the day came off & that was the final of the tug of war between C. & A Coy. great  was the excitement as the two teams took the strain the first pull was a fair one & was won by C. Coy amid great cheering they then reversed end's for the 2nd pull but it was a short one for C.
