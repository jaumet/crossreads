<p>Friday<br/>I have been round part of the lines today.</p>
<p>Saturday<br/>Went to Batt. H.Q. in the trenches &amp; on to B Coy &amp; A Coy.  I had a good view of the German lines through a telescope.<br/>There is an old ruined railway station there in the lines.  A train engine &amp; carriages is standing at the station.  It is riddled with Bullet &amp; shrapnel but it is standing on the rails as if ready to go out.  The Farmhouses are very close to the lines here &amp; the farmers plough their land right up to the Support trenches.</p>
<p>Sunday<br/>Today one service that I arranged for fell through but I had two others, both very near the front line, about 25 men at each, the first in an old cowshed &amp; the second in an old house.
