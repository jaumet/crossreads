7.I am hoping my evacuation to hospital will not cause me to miss either opportunity as I have been bent on getting on to staff work.
Colonel Mackenzie of the 19th has been here in this hospital with me. He has some cough trouble which seems to be chronic. He did not stay very long but hurried back to his battalion.
I am afraid, however, he will have to come away shortly for a long spell. He now has two ribbons - the D.S.O. & a Russian distinction.
