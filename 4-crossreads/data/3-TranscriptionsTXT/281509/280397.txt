 23 June 1916 Friday 
 Dispensary again. Taken off all parades form henceforth. &amp; put on permanent staff. Nettleton &amp; Cartwright endeavour to get into Aerial Corps. prospects good. Talk of former patients of Harve hosp. 4,000 men. 200 officers. 4 parsons. Man &amp; his sister. Camp hospital started at Ditails Camp. Tony, Bob &amp; I chosen by Staff Smith as orderlies.
