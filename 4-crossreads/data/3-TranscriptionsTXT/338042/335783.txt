January 1918
1st: New Year's Day &ndash;A Happy, Bright &amp; Peaceful New Year  to friends &amp; enemies &ndash; Our Hall graced by the Gen. Sir Wm Birdwood who shakes with one &amp; all after the show and is mighty pleased with his Concert Party &ndash; General Coxon also present.2ndTwo Australian Letters received. A strange coincidence concerning one &ndash; Our Panto has a most disastrous ending this evening &ndash; A fire takes place in the engine room &amp; two engineers are badly burned
