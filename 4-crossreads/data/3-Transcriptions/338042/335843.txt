<p>April 5th<br/>Meet Mr Davison friend of Mr &amp; Mrs Scales with whom I converse, have luncheon &amp; proceed to his Home at Oxton (across the River) &ndash; The Suburbs of Birkenhead &amp; Port Sunlight &ndash; Liverpool fine buildings &ndash; Exterior &amp; interiors of St Georges Hall Walker Art Gallery. Liner Building &ndash; Cunard Liner Offices.<br/>April 6th<br/>Writer new Original Monologue "The Opium Den" &ndash; See &amp; hear "Madame Butterfly" at the Shakespeare Theatre &ndash; Meet Beatrice Waycott (member of Opera Co).</p>
