SouthamptonArrived Southampton 7.30 a.m.  Stores and Equipment loaded on ship "Archimedes".4 p.m. - Company less 2 Officers, 70 O/Ranks marched into Rest Camp, Southampton.5 p.m. - 2 Officers, 70 O/Ranks embarked on ship "La Marguerite" and left for France.6.9.17 - Company at Rest Camp, Southampton.7.9.17 - 12 noon - Orders received for embarkation on ship "Archimedes".  3 p.m. - Company embarked.
