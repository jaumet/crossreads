  
 [Thursday January 6 1916] 
 Cold here now & it would be Blizzard at Anzac.  Please thank Day for Bed soks & May for her Parcel; all received safely. Had 2 teeth filled today. Busy getting Squadron equipped & ready to move any moment [Following paragraph is crossed out]  Now that we are reforming men & officers are turning up from all points of the Compass  They kept quiet while a Winter at Gallipoli was expected & enjoyed themselves in England & Scotland-  All ranks say they have been treated like princes in the old country
