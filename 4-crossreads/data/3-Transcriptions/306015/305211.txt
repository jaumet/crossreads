<p>Such new manifestations of the bounds of humanity come as revelations of a bigger universe, and one can only stand, aside in awe, &amp; utter inarticulately, "O, <u>in</u>comprehensible humanity";  or join with Scott in the words of "Solitary Reaper".<br/>"Behold her single in the field<br/>"Yon solitary lass.<br/>&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;.<br/>&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;&hellip;.<br/>"The lake, the boy, the waterfall<br/>"And <u>she the spirit of them all</u>".</p>
<p>May 28.   It was necessary to get an early start on this morning, for four of us had to escort 2 prisoners to Albert, which is ten miles away.   On the road we were lucky enough to pick up, or to be picked up by motor lorries &amp; so had an easy time.   Our platoon was on town picquet in the evening.</p>
