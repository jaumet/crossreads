 19 It was very pretty the little kiddies looked great. This village (Merris) has suffered heavily in this war nearly everyone was in mourning. Heavy bombarding was heard yesterday &ndash; we are not many miles from the line. It rained a good deal yesterday but to-day has been perfect. Am writing this by daylight &amp; it is now 10-15 p.m. the twilight is great over here.
