<p>Dear Miss King</p>
<p>Just a few lines hoping you are in the best of health as it leaves me the same at present.</p>
<p>I have not received a letter from you for some time but I suppose I will get one any day now.</p>
<p>You will be pleased to hear that I have been promoted to full Corporal and I can assure you Miss King I feel very proud of it.</p>
<p>My pay now is 10/- (ten shillings) per day so if I have the good luck to pull through and reach Australia again, will have sufficient money to establish my self in some little business.</p>
