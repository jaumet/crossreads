  
 One morning I got out with Eric to do Physical Training & after running for three miles round Centennial Park I returned almost dead. That was the extent of my physical  training. At times we would have to get out for a few minutes so as to get our names marked on the roll but after that, well, we just faded away. Well, at 9 oclock the fall in would sound & we would get squad drill all the morning.
