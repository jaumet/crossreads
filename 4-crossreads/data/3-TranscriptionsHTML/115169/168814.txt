<p>Aug 11th [1915]  at 8 am.we dropped anchor at Barbados but our whaler had been lost during the night otherwise schooner was safe.  At 1.30 PM we departed for Trinidad</p>
<p>Aug 12th  10 am. we anchored off Trinidad &ndash; we remained here several days, during which we coaled and oiled ship, and carried out various exercises, torpedo, calibre firing etc, and leave was given</p>
<p>Aug 19th  at 9 am we again departed for Barbados.</p>
<p>Aug 20th 7 am arrived Barbados, and there remained a few days, having good time,</p>
<p>Aug 24th 9 PM we left Barbados, weather fine</p>
<p>Aug 25th 10 am till 11 am was occupied in firing full charge projectiles at a rock, at long range, experimental, off Martinique, after which we proceeded to Port of France</p>
