 Somewhere in Belgium this time. 16 September 
 Dear Mrs. Jack, 
 I only wish I could write to you when I felt like it but somehow or other I never can, always something important  to be  doing or to be done, when we're in I'm going like made &amp; when we're out I'm still going, clearing &amp; squaring up things.  Consequently the only time I get to myself &amp; my pleasures is very late at night and say what you like without whisky or champagne I'm not at my best at night.   light  
 Excuse mistakes &amp; corrections but this is a rowdy old place.  Gawd knows how many people all round &amp; all yapping &amp; the place has as many echoes as a cave.  However perhaps it will get quieter later meantime I'll struggle along.
