 Thursday Dec 20th 
 O.T.B. : warned to go to signalling school tomorrow : V.D. &amp; dental inspection in morn : issued with our U.K. kit too : went with Tom Clunie up to see Reg after tea : 
 Friday Dec 21st 
 O.T.B. : Reveille 5 am : fell in 6 am &amp; marched to Station : train to Dinton &amp; marched to Fovant camp : camped at 5th T.B. &amp; met many of the Batt N.C.O.'s etc : stroll with Jock Craig after tea : 
 Saturday Dec 22nd 
 Fovant : medical &amp; dental exam in morn : concert at night : 
 Sunday Dec 23rd 
 Fovant : church parade in morn : stroll through village in the afternoon : Xmas carols hymns etc in YMCA at night &amp; were very nice :
