<p>"Ah God one sniff of England<br/>To greet my flesh &amp; blood<br/>To see the hansoms slurring<br/>Once more through London Mud"</p>
<p>Jan 20 &ndash; (I think)<br/>Hurrah, have got out of that Postal orderly job and am back again for duty with my Company. <strike>It was a bit difficult getting away &amp;</strike> It means harder work &amp; none of the "sweets of office" in the way of soft living but it is more satisfactory to be back with the Company with which I started with.</p>
<p>This diary seems to be dying a natural death, but nothing seems to have happened worth recording. We got some new arrivals last night in the shape of 180 men &amp; 5 officers from the South African</p>
