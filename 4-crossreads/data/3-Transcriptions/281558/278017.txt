<p>Tuesday July 31st</p>
<p>Arques : reveille 4.30 am : packed up &amp; marched off about 6 am : we carry no blanket or pack so the muckup of a march to Beaucourt must have got the "heads" into a scrape : nice march &amp; we struck a garden of lettuce &amp; eschalots at breakfast time so I squared the old boy to sell some : he soon cleaned his garden out : got to our billets about 2 pm : heavy rain set in : fair billets</p>
<p>Wednesday August 1st</p>
<p>Westbecourt : wet day : "stunt" postponed : reveille 6.30 am : rifle inspection in billets in morning : Gib came back &amp; is taking on S.B. again :</p>
<p>Thursday Aug 2nd</p>
<p>Westbecourt : raining : "stunt" off again : reveille 6.30 am :</p>
