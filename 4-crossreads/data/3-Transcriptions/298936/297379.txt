<p>July 1915</p>
<p>Saturday  24</p>
<p>Visited casualty ward of Prince A. Hospital during morning.</p>
<p>Fingers torn off, sprained wrist  4 other minor affairs. Judging by the nurses attitude there I should soon get sick of Hospital work &amp; I now find I am going in the field.</p>
