<p>"get the lay of the land".  The 72 hours were spent mainly in seeing St. Paul's, the Vatican, Piazza Venezia &amp; Vitt. Emanuel Statue, Forum, Coliseum, Palatine; visiting British Club, Italian &amp; English people, Scouts, restaurants &amp; cafes, Mefistofele (Opera) at Teatro Constanzi and a musical concert at the Quirino Theatre.<br/>At 11.5 p.m. on the third day in Rome two of us (picked up an Aust. 3rd Div. Sergt. for this trip) left for Naples &ndash; 250 kiloms. where we arrived the following morning at 7.30.  After breakfast at the station buffet a horsed carriage was secured and we had a look round the town &ndash; a fairly dirty place from what we saw of it.
