 I must apologise for not acknowledging the receipt of the &quot;treasure&quot; before, but I was exceptionally busy on the Monday &amp; somewhat harassed. We were trying to squeeze 2lbs into 1lb space. My people were urging me to sail as soon as possible, if not before, &amp; I was tearing between the Office &amp; ship rushing&nbsp;the stevedores attending shipping office, tactfully squaring&nbsp;differences with the crew, &amp; trying to please everybody witht he main object of pleasing my employers representative in getting away from the wharf &amp; we managed after a struggle to leave at 7 pm &nbsp;with some of the Sydney cargo remaining on board. 
 Oh these days of Rush. I look back on the days when I first came out here 1885 in the old Sailing ship. Then we made fast to the wharf &amp; discharged our cargo without the rush we have now Good old days those.
