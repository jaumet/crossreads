 [18th Nov. contd] &amp; was at present at D.R.S. Bailleul.  Pushing on to Steenwerck we made tea of herrings &amp; biscuits in Y.M.C.A. &amp; returned to M.D.S. about six in time for duty. 
 Monday 19th After tea went with Chas Hamblin to Steenwerck &amp; visited his cousin (Charles) at the private billets.  Spent a very interesting evening. 
 Tuesday 20th Held the first Gathering of the evenings arranged by Recreation Club &amp; it took shape of a Five Hundred Tournament &amp; was very successful there being about six officers &amp; about 90 men competing.  Major Wilson won First Prize with 21 wins out of a possible 25. Eric Cunningham 19  Alf Moss 18 filled the other places.  The officers provided supper after the affair. 
 Wednesday 21st  Selected   Rugby Football match between B &amp; C Sections resulted in a draw 3 all.  It was wretched day &amp; ground was quagmire.   Zocchi [?] scored a try for 'C' &amp; Swinnerton for 'B'.  For 'C' Zocchi [?] Campbell  C  Egan Burdon Rawlins Wagstaff all played well.  [Page 152] [21st Nov. contd] Swinnerton &amp; Garner, Moodie Astbury Wilson Craig &amp; Murray Rawlins Frank &amp; Zocchi [?] Thursday 22nd Impromptu concert at night.  The Reps team trained.  In the morn I toured Romarin YMCA in hope of finding Bert Forrest but he was in Bailleul.  I saw Abel Sheath (18th Batt) Lieut &amp; M.C. in the afternoon &amp; had a good chat to him  Told me Perc Mayhew was killed.  Jack Harris (also a Lt) is in same battalion as Abel. 
 Friday 23rd Raining.  Team training for Saturday. 
 Saturday 24th In morning I took a run down to see Bert Forrest again &amp; was more successful.  He is looking OK &amp; is pretty busy just now in arranging certain functions.  In the afternoon witnessed the Rugby Match between our Ambulance &amp; the 5th whilst resulted in a draw 3 all.  Brown scored for us.  It was one of the most evenly contested games I have seen &ndash; the Fifths forwards being particularly heavy fast &amp; strong.  Our backs played well also the forwards with one or two exceptions.  In the backs Wagstaff played safe at full back.  Swinnerton Brown Burdon Williams all distinguished themselves whilst Zocchi mc Bean Campbell Astbury were pick of forwards.
