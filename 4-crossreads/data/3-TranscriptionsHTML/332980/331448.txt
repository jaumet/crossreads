<p>I was called at 4.45 am &amp; signed on at 5.30, taking the Staff Train from the Camp up to St Omer, reporting there at 6 am &amp; was told to wait in the Mess Room until called, I passed the time away resting as I had drawn 24 hrs rations &amp; one never knows on this work where &amp; when you will finish, I had by Breakfast &amp; a smoke &amp; laid down on a form &amp; had a sleep until 11 am, when I obtained permission for 15 minutes to go out &amp; into the Canteen for some Envelopes &amp; Cigarettes also over into an Estaminet for a Drink, on my return, my services were required to pilot a Driver from the Main Depot to Boulogne, so at 11.30 the Engine arrived &amp; I boarded same &amp; the Driver was to proceed Light Engine to Wizernes to assist a Coal Train waiting there over the Hills to Boulogne, we arrived at Wizernes at 12 noon &amp; soon was coupled on &amp; on our journey, our first stop was Lumbres were both Engines took water, it is necessary on these heavy grades for one Engine to be in front &amp; the assisting Engine in the rear, in case of the Train breaking away, after 15" stop at Lumbres, we were off again were we</p>
