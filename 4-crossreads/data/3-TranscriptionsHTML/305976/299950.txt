<p>reminded. We went to Jaffa &amp; bought two small pigs today in hopes that we will be here for Christmas. Many of the Jewish children had never seen pigs and they were a source of amusement to the children. How pleased the Kiddies seem to be that we are here. Our immobile section coming up. We lost 20&#37; of our strength last stint<br/>Friday Dec 14<br/>Beautiful sunny day. Spent day grazing my Horse &amp; cleaning &amp; repairing saddle. Referendum notices posted up. Presentation of Medals in Village today. Population village estimated 600.</p>
