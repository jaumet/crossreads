<p>One of the engines broke down again (for two hours).  After tea some music from band.  Weather dull, strong wind.  Got another inoculation (Flue).</p>
<p>Wednesday July 2nd<br/>9 a.m. Parade.  9-30 Boot class.  Passed the "Euripidies" 10-30.  Saw a shoal of porpices.  Had a fun stunt during afternoon on well deck.  7 oclock.  Concert by Smart Set.  8-30 went down into stoke hold alongside furnaces (21 of them), did a gentle sweat, very hot, had to have a bath after it.</p>
<p>Thursday July 3rd<br/>Up 6-30.  Parade 9 oclock.  Boot class 9-30, finished pair of shoes.  2-30 p.m. Boxing contests on well deck.  Some very good also funny contests.  Got some snaps.  Saw a couple of large sharks.  Filled in the evening playing draughts.</p>
<p>Friday July 4th<br/>Started another pair of shoes.  Weather much cooler.  Heavy head wind still blowing.  Played crib all night.</p>
<p>Saturday July 5th<br/>Started another pair of shoes.  Weather cool, strong wind.  4th Divy sports during afternoon.  Blindfold boxing very funny.  Played crib during evening.</p>
<p>Sunday July 6th<br/>Went to Church 10 a.m.  Had to wait until 1-30 for dinner.  More complaints about grub.  Slept all afternoon in a boat.  Weather getting colder.  7 p.m. Church service.</p>
<p>Monday July 7th<br/>9 oclock Parade.  9-30 Boot class;  finished pair of boots.  Weather good; cool wind.  After tea crib tournament, afterwards had an hours dancing.  Saw a comet.</p>
<p>Tuesday July 8th<br/>9-30 Boot class, made a watch pouch.  Just after dinner passed a large steamer.  2 p.m.  Got paid &pound;1-1-0.<br/>Started raining; thick mist, rain cleared off just before going to bed.  Put in a cold night. Strong wind and boat rolling from side to side.</p>
