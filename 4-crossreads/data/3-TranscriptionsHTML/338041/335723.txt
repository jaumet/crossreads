<p>Aug 2nd<br/>Contay in afternoon. We receive many more of our boys from the trenches. Vivid stories from the line. Day of vast preparation by British. Enormous shell supply goes to Albert</p>
<p>Aug 3rd<br/>More letters. Fierce night's bombardment of Somme Front &ndash; incessant 12 hours shelling by our Artillery.</p>
<p>Aug 4th<br/>Early morning (1a.m) celebration of Second Anniversary of Dec. of War.</p>
<p>Menu<br/>Mashed Potatoes<br/>Salad<br/>Salmon<br/>Cocoa<br/>Biscuits<br/>Fruit</p>
<p>Toasts<br/>"The King"<br/>"The Allies"<br/>"The Day" x<br/>"Our Fallen Comrades"</p>
<p>Second Division successful in a big objective &ndash; Fritz badly beaten.</p>
