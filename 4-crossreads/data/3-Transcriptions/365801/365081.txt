<p>Lake View<br/>Cowper CT.<br/>Randwick<br/>June 27 1916</p>
<p>Dear Judge Ferguson,<br/>I trust you will pardon me for addressing you, being as I am a stranger to you in all but name.  As a Grammar School Master who taught your son, Arthur, during part of his School career, and as one who saw him at Liverpool during his training, I was deeply grieved to read in the Paper of his death in France.  We Masters are so proud of the doings of Old Boys who have joined the Australian or Home Forces &ndash; parents, I am sure, will permit us</p>
