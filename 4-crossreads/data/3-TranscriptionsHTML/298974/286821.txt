<p>2<br/>I'm O.K., &amp; feeling fit. I have been with Cleve all the time and he is now a real experienced soldier, and a great help. We had an Xmas Eve gift of a heavy bombardment, which fell all around us. Just how one did not drop in our nest I don't know, but beyond a whack on the shoulder from about a pound of dirt, nothing lobbed on me. My gracious its cold. The ground is now white with snow &amp; ice, &amp; we are all in our sheepskins &amp; gloves. I don't know what</p>
