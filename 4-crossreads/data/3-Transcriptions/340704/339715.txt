<p>January 1917<br/>January 10 (cont)<br/>back in old camp El Arish, dead weary and hungry. Had no sleep for 58 hours and no food. Big casualty list. all the camp fast asleep.</p>
<p>Jan.11<br/>Still very tired  - Had an easy day &ndash;overhauled camel saddles and camels &ndash; some have sore backs.</p>
<p>Friday 12<br/>Quiet day</p>
