 On your post. Going in you are loaded up with bombs ammunitions machine guns buckets and machine guns, but coming out you, generally only have the gun as all the rest is left in the line for use in a big stunt. The buckets being handed over to the relieving gun teams. Having two small primus stoves we, the gun team, used to load ourselves up with material to make hot drinks and meals in the line.
