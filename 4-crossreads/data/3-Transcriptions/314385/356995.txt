<p>We pushed on till we reach a rural village at about 4.30 pm the name of the township is called [Nordenesque?] &ndash; here we camp and are distributed in various billets. I <strike>was</strike> in a party of 100 am alloted a large brick barn with plenty of clean straw which is recherche as we <strike>were</strike> a all tired, weary &amp; [indecipherable].</p>
<p>Sunday Oct 22nd<br/>We rise at 8 feeling refreshed with the sleep, but we are faced with an intense frost and the wind tho light is cutting.</p>
<p>Breakfast over R.S. &amp; I go exploring &amp; after one or two at the estaminet we find our way to the church as usual &amp; stayed to Service &amp; hear a very eloquent sermon.</p>
<p>On returning we find a mail has arrived &amp; I am fortunate in getting a couple of letters 1 from C, 1 from A Leggo also 1 from Paris, these keep me occupied for the rest of the day especially that written in French.</p>
