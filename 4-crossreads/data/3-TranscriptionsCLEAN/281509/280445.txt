  
 10 August 1916 Thursday 
 Quiet on front. Started to rain this morning first for 3 weeks. Dust has been terrific. Go to Albert on service waggon for coal. Very misty. not much doing on front. Pass through Henencourt & Millencourt 
 The King, Prince Wales, Generals Haig & Birdwwod visit us. Nearly missed them, thro' having shave. Caught them up at Hospital  Good view.
