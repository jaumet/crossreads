<p>mountain side I should not care to live on these islands for life must be pretty dull, there are light houses everyhere &amp; there along the coast, we were now getting closer to the coast of France &amp; everyone was on the lookout for it.<br/>We passed several warships about here French I think they were, they were patrolling the sea's for submarines, we had a wireless just before we reached Touloun of the torpedoing of the Minneapolis just outside Alexandria we were lucky for she followed us out a few hours after we sailed, so we were lucky were we not.</p>
