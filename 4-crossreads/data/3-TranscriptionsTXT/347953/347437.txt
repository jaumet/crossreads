But the houses were far from clean and respectable, &amp; seemed to be owned by the poorer classes.  The sunset here was most wonderful illuminating the sky with its many radiating and glorious colours.  At [indecipherable] were glorious &amp; ancient Grecian baths whose interior was paved with marble.  The water from the ever flowing wells was naturally hot &amp; gave very invigorating effects.  This island is certainly very valuable and would make an ideal locality for residential flats &amp;  private houses.
