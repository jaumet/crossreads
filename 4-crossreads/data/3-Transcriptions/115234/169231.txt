
<p>&quot;Devil-May-Care&quot;</p>
<p>----------&nbsp;&nbsp; &quot;&nbsp;&nbsp; ----------&nbsp;&nbsp; &quot;&nbsp;&nbsp; ----------&nbsp;&nbsp; &quot;&nbsp;&nbsp; ----------</p>
<p>Fly if you see me in the street - Leave me to drift - to drift.</p>
<p>Turning aside if by chance we meet. Pray that the end come swift. Once I was spick &amp; span like you had money &amp; all to spare. Do you remember? Yes, you do. Dear old Devil May Care. Devil may care who paid your debts when you&#39;d gone the pace too fast. Devil may care, whom</p>
