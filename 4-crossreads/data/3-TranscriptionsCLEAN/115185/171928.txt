
2 - grumble. I told you about my kitten Hellas from the french trenches at Helles. I decided to accept the kind offer of Lieut Gravely to keep the kitten and take her to his own home in England. It was kinder to the little thing - but I hated giving her up - and at the time we did not know where we were going - or what convenience there would be for a little pussy cat.At 3 pm we got into a hospital train - sent right on to the docks - just in the same way as wounded are taken from the ships - and my heart was full as you may imagine at the thought of all the pain & suffering that had gone before on that same rail jogging & swaying.It was an Indian Hosp train & the major med. off and attendants were indians - The two sisters English women. The Major hid for our journey - but the attendants were charming, helping the sisters to give us tea & bread & butter & oranges - and when the evening came, insisted on covering us with blankets.
