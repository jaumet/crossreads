<p>to El Arish &amp; perhaps beyond. All the mounted troops are to be sent <strike>indecipherable</strike> off the desert &amp; all the Camelry will be kept here to do the Desert patrolling etc. We are all looking forward to the campaign in Palestine &amp; shall be glad to once again get into fertile &amp; interesting country. <strike>Some of our mounted men have already penetrated to within 15 miles of Jerusalem.</strike> The Turks have lately been getting a bad time on all fronts &amp; during last week the British had a great victory in the capture of Kut-el-Amara &amp; Bagdad.</p>
