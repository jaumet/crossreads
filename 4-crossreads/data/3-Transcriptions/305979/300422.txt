<p>Nov 20<br/>This same chap is very friendly when speaking to us, and keeps patting Allen or me on the shoulder &amp; knee to inforess  his words ; he seems a  bit countrified, but is delighted with the trip, and on one occasion exclaimed, with gusto, "I never et so much in my life".</p>
<p>There is another 'bird' down in our mess-room a real 'pommy' with a voice that can be heard everywhere, He is evidently a born worker for besides his ordinary duties he &amp; his mate (Bottle &amp; Murphy by names) have arranged for a small consideration to act as mess orderlies for their table all the trip .
