Tuesday 25thCame out of supports at 5 am & turned in at 5 30 & slept till 7 & bed again in dug out Had a good sleep till 12 30. During the morn a battle ship was sunk (torpedoed?) just in the bay. I did not see it. Noon the torpedo destroyers & the airship were making great search while the battle ships went out to sea.HMS Triumph.
In the trenches again at 4 pm. After a wet after noon. Things damned cloggy.
Quiet night. On alert but little doing. [indecipherable] hardly awake all night.
