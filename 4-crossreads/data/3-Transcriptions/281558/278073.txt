<p>Monday Dec 24th</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parade : had two teeth filled : concert at night in the YMCA :</p>
<p>Tuesday Dec 25th  Xmas day</p>
<p>Fovant : church parade &amp; Holy Com in morn : good dinners considering the difficulties under which cooking is done in the army : concert in afternoon : watched games etc in YMCA at night :</p>
<p>Wednesday Dec 26th Boxing Day</p>
<p>Fovant : drizzling cold day so didn't go out at all :</p>
<p>Thursday Dec 27th</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades buzzer, light &amp; flags : interesting : Wrote at night :</p>
<p>Friday Dec 28th</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades : freezing all day : Tom came in today :</p>
<p>Saturday Dec 29th</p>
<p>Fovant : route march in morn</p>
