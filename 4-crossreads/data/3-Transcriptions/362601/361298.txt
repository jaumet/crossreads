<p>16 November, Sunday</p>
<p>Dig &amp; make wall around top garden. Go to Dee Why &amp; fix ashes on front of house. Swim &amp; Home again. E &amp; E out to dinner.</p>
<p>Wrote Auntie Annie.</p>
<p>17 November, Monday</p>
<p>Go to E &amp; E for Tea &amp; meet N at 8 pm. Meet R Charles &amp; go walk along Lovers Walk to Condamine St.</p>
