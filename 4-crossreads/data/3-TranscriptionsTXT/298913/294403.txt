 Feen drinks himself sober, loafs. 9pm all square &amp; ready for work. Traffic at 7pm &amp; of course Trickett gets excited &amp; mucks up message. Working with Kantara  Light Horse arrive &amp; leave by score. 
 30/7/16 3rd LH Bde. Arrive under Brig Gen Anthill or as he is generally know as Gen Bull Ant. Do some cooking &amp; make awful failures in way of pancakes &amp; scones. 
 31/7/16 Taubes up again Kill 2 men at Kantara. Band music at night. Order sent for the wireless station supplies. 
 1/8/16 Taubes over Kantara 
 2/8/16 More aeroplanes up doing damage 
 3/8/16 Pay day draw &pound;2.Scorer &amp; Jeffreis arrive from the squadron 
 4/8/16 Leave Bally Bunion after LH regts On camels &amp; wireless gear. Arrive Hill 70 at 1am 
 5/8/16 sleep in YMCA. On again to  Duieda where we spend dinner with WA2 wireless staff. Proceed &amp; arrive Bir-el-nuss at 3pm. Leave with instructions to meet orderly on telephone who would direct us further. Go on, followed by AMC waggons &amp; camel back stretchers. Meet 3LH men &amp; no orderly  who had misheard. 
 Battle going on at Katia See shells bursting &amp; anti-aircraft guns firing at Se Bristol plane. Decide to chance sleep in oasis of Bir-abu-rame.
