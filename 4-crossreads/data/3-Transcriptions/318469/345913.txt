<p>To be forwarded to<br/>Mrs. E.  Murray<br/>Berry St<br/>Nowra<br/>N.S.W.<br/>Australia</p>
<p>8.4.15<br/>First Field Coy boarded Ionian</p>
<p>9th<br/>Nothing important  [indecipherable].</p>
<p>10<br/>Preparation for landing Dardanelles &ndash; forming barrel Piers etc.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Little work - barrel Piers etc.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Nothing interest &ndash; Piers &ndash; All going well.</p>
<p>13th<br/>Same as yesterday</p>
<p>14th<br/>Did a little barrel Piering. Things quiet.</p>
<p>15th<br/>Still on Ionian.  Nothing fresh.</p>
<p>16th<br/>Still doing a little work. Nothing of interest.</p>
<p>17th<br/>Awaiting orders, nothing fresh.</p>
<p>18<br/>Usual routine.  Tired waiting</p>
<p>19th<br/>Transferred to "Malda".  Got Webb equipment.  Heard that a Torpedo boat "Turkish" attempted to torpedo a transport coming in a few nights ago but missed &amp; was in turn beached. Panic on transport resulted in a good many being drowned</p>
<p>20th<br/>Nothing of much consequence today. Was told that we are to form part of first landing party.</p>
