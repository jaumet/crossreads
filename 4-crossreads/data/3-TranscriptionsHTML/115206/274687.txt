<p>April 1918<br/>Saturday 27</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours.   D.R.<br/>Weather misty.  500 Fritz's grabbed by 14th Bgd.  English landing at Zeebrugge, - first public of news !<br/>Very heavy fighting up north and at Hangard. French lost Hangard after seven counter attacks. <br/>Another inspection. Cleaned up saddle again<br/>13th Battalion went through.<br/>French lost Kemmel.  Whole division being wiped</p>
