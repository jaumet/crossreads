<p>Next morning we embarked on waiting trains and commenced a 52 hours journey in the train across France.</p>

It was very restful for the eyes to gaze on the beautiful green fields and the sparkling waters of a brooklet flowing through the land. The scenery is altogether different to Australian scenery.
