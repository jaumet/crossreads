<p>Tomorrow we get leave to the town, but all pubs will be closed to Khaki, so the boys will not be likely to get lively. It's a good precaution as we are not the only crowd by a long chalk.</p>
<p>13th Just heard that all letters from D. are to be censored so am entrusting this to a returning wounded man who will post it in Aus.<br/>Will describe the marvels of this wonderful (?) city later on.</p>
<p>Bearer waiting. Thus for present will say so long.  Harry</p>
<p>Sending to Betty small packet contg shells from Bluff Beach &ndash; not much chop.</p>
