Sunday 12/3/16Church parade 9.30 AM. conducted by Capt Chaplain  of the 30th Btn.  Cricket match continued from yesterday.  Engineers compiled 59 in their second innings leaving us winners by an innings & 11 runs.  I took 2 wickets for 6.
Monday 13/3/16Innoculated again for Enteric.  No 4s of each squad given medical haversack to go through & see contents were all correct.Afternoon off in consequence
