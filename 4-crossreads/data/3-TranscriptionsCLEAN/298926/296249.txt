  
 Oct 15th Church parade on Common presided over by Chaplain - General Rentoul who has  just arrived from Australia with a message from Australia that the "Home fires are kept  burning" for us 
 16th  We voted to-day on the conscription question in Australia. Each man received a  printed manifesto from W.M.Hughes stating the terms of the proposition.
