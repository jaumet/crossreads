<p>Friday 2 November</p>
<p>Dull &ndash; Muddy and Slippery roads.<br/>2 runs to A.D.M.S. and other branches of the 5th Div. Hd. Q. at Scottish Lines Busseboom.<br/>To "K" Supply Column.<br/>To 1st Anzac Corps Hd. Q.<br/>Airman flying over the C.C.S.'s today doing fancy moves in the air.  He dropped 4 messages to the nurses who were out looking at him.  This is a daily occurrence and doubtless essential to the successful prosecution of the war.</p>
