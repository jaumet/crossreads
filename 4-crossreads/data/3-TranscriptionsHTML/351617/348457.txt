<p>On the 6th of May I once more went up to advanced Div at Tulloy to be in readiness in case of immergency and with me were Billy Lickiss and Jimmy Hamilton. Before leaving Rear Div I was having a game of cards one day and Sammy Holden said to me Hold on Tom I wont be a minute, and as I was, with my pipe in my mouth, my tunic open, and my hat on the back of my head, he sketched me. I would have liked to have kept that but the girls got it and I never seen it again.</p>
