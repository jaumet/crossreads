  
 Jan 25 Released Pte Hughes 1st LH from Aerodrome Guard Room: he had been there about 6 weeks: the FGCM had awarded him 3 day detention. Jan 26 QM Hockey & VO Guyer in camp with authority to take stores. Received cable from Mrs Vernon sending 30.00 in answer to my appeal for 'A' Sqn (cost 90 PP) Attended Garrison office Zeitown; saw Col Hall & Capt Phillips who promised mess huts, latrines, tents to their ability. Being Anniversary Day the 2.30 parade was handed over to Sqn Cmdrs to dismiss if they did not require the men. 27 Nothing of Special import.
