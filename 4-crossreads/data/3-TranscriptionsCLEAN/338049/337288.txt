
Tuesday 19 MarchUp at 6.30, breakfast immediately. Fell in about 9-30 & though my pass was not marked for the early boat I schemed through where I should have waited. Marched to boat with many productions of passes & packed below. Went up on deck just before leaving. Fine weather.
Left Calais about 11 am & arrived Dover about 11.15 (Clock 1 hr different). Managed to scramble into first train & arrived London about 2.40 p.m. 20 Horseferry Rd & fixed up bath, change etc.
Met Norm. Richardson. Rang Will up & arranged to meet for tea. Got paid etc. Had tea with Will went to Chu Chin Chow. Good seats great show. Stopped with Will at a neighbouring soldiers home.
