<p>Australia in July of that year, it seems possible that I shall be sent back in a few months &ndash; possibly four.</p>
<p>We are supposed to go to Germany in two day's time.  At least we are fairly certain to go to Germany, &amp; to have march most of the way, but when we shall start is not yet known.</p>
