<p>29/5/17<br/>Bde sigs. School started today 10 days course.</p>
<p>24/6/17<br/>Bde church parade today. Gen. Birdwood and Walker were present issuing out decorations. I received my M.M.</p>
<p>29/6/17<br/>Marched to Englebermer from Lavieville via Hellencourt and Senlis. We expect to remain here about 10 days for a course of field training.</p>
