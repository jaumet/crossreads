<p>11th<br/>Left Boulogne by train about 9.30 A.M. with the draft.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Arrived at Harfleur Camp (just outside Le Havre) about 4 A.M. and marched straight to the camp where I handed the devils over and turned in for a couple of hours rest.</p>
<p>I left Le Havre this evening by <strike>the</strike> a trooper at 8.30 p.m. and after a rough trip across in which I was darned nearly sea sick reach Southampton <strike>on n</strike> next morning.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Back in camp again.</p>
