  
  15th  An easy day - Censored letters. Visited Dentist & received preliminary filling for a tooth.  
 Went for a walk with Jock Willis to Rouelles Village - Saw model of the Brigand Calabrais. 
  16  Censored letters. Visited Ordnance at Havre & purchased Map Case & electric torch. Posted vase to Merlie. Spent about 1 1/2  hrs after dinner near the entrance to Havre harbour watching the shipping - quite an astonishing amount mixed up with destroyers & submarine chasers. Spoke with some American soldiers - Went to Picture Show until 9.50.  
  17  Left base at le Havre at 8pm. - motored to Entraining Station - Had a good journey to Rouen.
