  
 Thursday 30 September 1915  About Hospital all day  Captain Bourke left hospital, as he was ordered for Alexandria.  He was insulting when going away.  
 Friday 1 October 1915  Notification received that I was to hand over hospital to Captain Bourke & that I was to go to Alexandria on Monday the 4 th  inst.  General Ford called with Major Bagshaw. Had a talk with the General  
 Saturday 2 October 1915  In Cairo during morning. Called on General Ford. He took me to General Maxwell.Had brief chat with the latter.  Settled up accounts.  Handed over to Captain Bourke at 1 pm.  At Confession in Heliopolis at 8 p.m.  
 Sunday 3 October 1915  Rosary Sunday. Mollie  Communion 7 am at Bassilica Heliopolis.  Packing all day.  At Zeitoun Convt between 6-30 & 8-30 pm.  Clearing up everything.
