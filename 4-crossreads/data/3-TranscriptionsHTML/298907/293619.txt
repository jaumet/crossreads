<p>but it was of no use, how glad I was when morning came &amp; the orderlies came round to wash us, by this time the whole of my side was swollen dreadfully &amp; I could scarce stand any one to touch it, after nurse had dressed it again I was sent out on a stretcher to go under the X Rays, they strip you for this &amp; lay you on a cold table the room is then darkened &amp; the X Ray a great bluish looking bulb all radiating with light is swung over your body all spitting &amp; fizzing millions of little sparks, there is no feeling while this on you, all of a sudden the operator switches her off for he has discovered the piece of metal, the lights are then turned on &amp; he marks the place on your body with a pen or a piece of pencil</p>
