 TUESDAY, JULY 8, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; warm. Round Boundary &amp; Mountain 
 in morning &amp; picking up in the 
 Middle Paddock in afternoon. 
 Tickle took waggon down to 
 Walhallow &amp; brought back the 
 cut timber for the mill. Left it at 
 the bore &amp; brought waggon up to 
 Station and the &quot;Pop&quot; with it. 
 Percy George doing the Boundary 
 troughs again.
