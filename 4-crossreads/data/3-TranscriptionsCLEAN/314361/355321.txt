The purpose of this work is to hold back the water until it reaches a certain level for flowing into the large canals, which then flows northward & so irrigates miles of fertile land. The Barrage was began by Mohammed Ali in 1835 but unfinished till 1890. At barrage are beautiful & well laid out gardens, & a delightfull place for a picnic. The land on both sides of the railway are under cultivation as far as I can see & the whole country looks well.Tuesday Jan. 18th 16Monday & Tuesday I have been on the rifle range at the back of our old range camp at Abbassia.
