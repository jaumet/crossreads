<p>Has Ritchie left yet? He was thinking of it before I left, &amp; his last letter suggested that he might be coming over. Mitch was a pretty considerable surprise to me, &amp; I wrote him a letter to the effect that he was a dam fool. It was no trouble to compose it as I only had to remember the things people used to say to me.</p>
<p>Don't stop writing merely because my letters are tedious. I can say very little of interest, you know.</p>
<p>Peace negotiations looking</p>
