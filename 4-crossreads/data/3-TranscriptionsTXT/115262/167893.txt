 Sunday 6 May 1917. 
 On leave. Home during day. 
 Writing wallet&nbsp;given me by Mother in evening before leaving,&nbsp;Wallace&#39;s from Bayswater over for dinner &amp; left at 6. 15&nbsp; pm. 
 Caught 6.10 train from Lyndhurst. Stayed 1&frac12; hours in Dandenong &amp; then got 8 pm train to Melbourne. 
 Wrote note &amp; turned in 11 pm. at Fed.Coffee Palace.
