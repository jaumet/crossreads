<p>for a spell. The camels have the mange &amp; most of the boys badly need a spell so they are to be isolated<br/>Saturday Dec 8<br/>Mud everywhere, raining heavily. Unit arrived today wet through.<br/>Sunday Dec 9<br/>Camped in open. Rain &amp; mud , everything in a hell of a mess.<br/>Monday Dec 10<br/>Beautiful fine day so we proceeded to make camp. We secured a lovely position on a slope among some good old saplings. The village lies some 560 yds at the back of us and it looks very pretty sheltered among the trees.</p>
