<p>DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS</p>
<p>22 &ndash; 22.00 &ndash; Proceeded via Black Watch Alley to attack Pozieres.</p>
<p>22/25 &ndash; Pozieres &ndash; 1st Pozieres Capt. Blackburn recommended for the V.C.</p>
<p>25 &ndash; Releived by 5th and 17th Battns.  Total casualties for operation, Killed wounded &amp; missing 350.</p>
<p>Augst. 1 &ndash; Bereaucourt &ndash; Bn. at Berteaucourt.</p>
<p>9 &ndash; Bonneville &ndash; Marched from Berteaucourt to Bonneville.</p>
<p>14 &ndash; Toutencourt &ndash; Marched from Bonneville to Toutencourt.</p>
<p>15 &ndash; Vandicourt &ndash; Marched to Vadincourt Wood.</p>
<p>16 &ndash; Brickfields &ndash; Left Vadincourt for Brickfields near Albert.</p>
<p>18 &ndash; Sausage Valley &ndash; Marched to Sausage Valley.  Relieved 3rd Battalion.</p>
<p>19/23 &ndash; Mouquet Moquet Farm &ndash; 2nd Pozieres.</p>
<p>23 &ndash; 24.00 &ndash; Relieved by 21st Battalion.  Entrained at Doullens.</p>
<p>Belgium &ndash; Detrained Proven.</p>
<p>Sept. 1 &ndash; Renora Camp &ndash; Near Poperinghe (Belgium).</p>
<p>2 &ndash; Ottawa Camp &ndash; Marched to Ottawa Camp.</p>
<p>7 &ndash; Col. Weis left for England.</p>
<p>8 &ndash; Bomb accident 21 casualties.</p>
<p>9 &ndash; (T) Capt. Blackburn V.C. left Batt.</p>
<p>13 &ndash; Chateau Belge &ndash; Moved to reserve position in Chateau Belge.</p>
<p>19 &ndash; Rlwy. Dugouts &ndash; Moved to railway dugouts.</p>
