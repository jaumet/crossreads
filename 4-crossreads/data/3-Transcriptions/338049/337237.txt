<p>Sunday 27 Jan.<br/>Work during morning.</p>
<p>Afternoon rode into Bailleul to play football for D.A. sigs against DA. Harry Richards &amp; George Leverett also came in. Chaps very good to us. Had a good game but got beaten by about 17 points.</p>
<p>Had tea returned home &amp; was advised about going forward tomorrow. Spend evening packing &amp; arranging.</p>
