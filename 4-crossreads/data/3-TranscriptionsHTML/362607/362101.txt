<p>Oct 5 1911</p>
<p>13 Junction St<br/>North Sydney</p>
<p>Oct 5 1911</p>
<p>Dear Freda<br/>This is the place where we get off in the City of Sydney. Well we are having a lovely time over here. We will be away a month by the time we come home again three week in Sydney   &amp;  a week going   &amp;  coming over &amp; I was twelve years old over here here had my birthday in Sydney Mum took me to a play it was lovely love from your cousin Roy</p>
