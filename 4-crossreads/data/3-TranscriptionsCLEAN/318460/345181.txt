
Wednesday 10/1/17More Rain & snow bitterly cold,getting another man for PoliceD. & C. Coys moved off this morning the rest of us go on Friday.The Trenches are awful so they tell us. Pneumonia, fever & Trench feet have pulled out the 2nd divisionSergt on duty last night from 8 oclock & drill all day to day.had best Tea since I joined the Army Roast Beef Bld & Bkd Potatoes & Turnips dish fullPay 2-5-1 19-3-0 Paid to day 14/4 (20 francs)
Thursday 11/1/17heavy frost, commenced to snow at 11.30 still at it 1-30 but very light, cold.Pay to date due 1-10.9 - 19-4-0snowing hard when we went to bed.Viv returned last nightTwo letters & a Parcel for him still no letters for me.
Friday 12/1/17Rained during the night so the foot of snow I expected was melted.Pay 1-11-9 19-5-0looks like a day of rain, much mildernot going to the front to day.Lobster, coffee Beer & Whiskey for supper tonight living high for once.Snowing.
Saturday 13/1/17Rain, Sleet & Snow bitterly cold,going back tomorrow by Train.Got 2 letters from Nell & Jack. They are thinking of going to hobart, let em. I wont.Viv still in rest Hospital.Pay 1-12-9. 19-6-0wrote to Maude & Nell not Posted,last night in the Barn with the cows.
