<p><em>RATES OF POSTAGE</em>.</p>
<p><strong>WITHIN THE COMMONWEALTH.</strong></p>
<p>Letters, per &frac12; oz. .. .. .. .. .. 1d. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Post Cards, each. 1d.; Newspapers (each), &nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;every 10 oz. .. .. .. .. .. &frac12;d. &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;Letter Cards, single1d., reply 1d. each half. &nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;Books and Magazines:- &nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p><em>(a)</em>&nbsp; Printed in Australia, 1/2 d. for 8 oz. or fraction. &nbsp;</p>
<p><em>(b)</em>&nbsp; Printed outside Australia, 1/2 d. for 4 oz. or fraction.</p>
<p>Catalogues printed in Australia, each, 1/2 d. per 4 oz. &nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>Printed Papers, every 2 oz. or under, up to 5 lbs. 1/2d.</p>
<p>Patterns, Samples, eacch 2 oz. up to 1 lb. 1d.</p>
<p>Commercial Papers, every 2 oz. or under, up to 5 lbs. 1d.</p>
<p>Parcels, Inland, 1lb. 6d., and 3d. for each additional lb. (limite 11 lbs.); Inter-State, 1 lb. 8d., 2lbs. 1s. 2d., and 6d. for each additional lb. (limite 11 lbs.)</p>
<p>Merchandise, every 2 oz. or under, up to 1 lb. 1d.</p>
<p>Registration Fee&nbsp; 3d.</p>
<p>Postal Notes, 6d., 1s., 1s. 6d., fee 1/2d.; 2s. to 4s. 6d., 1d.; 5s., 1&frac12;d. ; 7s. 6d., 2d.; 10s., 10s. 6d., 15s., 20s., 3d.</p>
<p>Money Orders, not exceeding &pound;5, 6d.; &pound;5 to &pound;10, 1/-; &pound;10 to &pound;15, 1/6; &pound;15 to &pound;20 (limit) 2/-.</p>
<p><strong>UNITED KINGDOM AND ABROAD.</strong>&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>Letters to all places within the British Empire, New Hebrides, Banks and Torres Islands, 1d. per &frac12; oz. ; all other places, 2&frac12;d. per &frac12; oz.</p>
<p>Letter Cards, all places with British Empire, New Hebrides, Bank and Torres Islands, 1d. each ; all other places, 2&frac12;d. each.&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
