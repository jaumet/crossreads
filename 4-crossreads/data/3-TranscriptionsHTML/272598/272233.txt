<p>1.8.11th of July.  Also parcel 20 June. Few of the boys transferred to 3rd A.G.H.</p>
<p>Friday 14.9.17<br/>Orderly Corporal. Walked to Wardriques at night. Brawl in camp late.</p>
<p>Saturday 15.9.17<br/>Had to give evidence in connection with last nights disturbance.  Camp struck &amp; all patients evacuated; expect to move on Monday.</p>
<p>Sunday 17.9.17<br/>General fatigues, preparing for to-morrows move.</p>
<p>Monday 18.9.17<br/>Early reveille.  Moved away 9 Am. early part of march very nasty &amp; choppy, probably owing to bad arrangements; improved after passing saluting base at about 10 Am.  Trouble among transport, one wagon</p>
