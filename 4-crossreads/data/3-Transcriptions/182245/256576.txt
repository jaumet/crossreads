
<p>295</p>
<p>MARCH 1917</p>
<p>2 FRIDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; misty. Cleaning harness</p>
<p>all morning &amp; out exercising</p>
<p>horses in afternoon. Campbell</p>
<p>came up &amp; was quite affable</p>
<p>&amp; asking about the RFC &amp; my</p>
<p>chances of getting into it.</p>
<p>got three letters from Linda</p>
<p>&amp; some from home &amp; Coomoo</p>
<p>Coomoo &amp; was jolly glad to</p>
<p>get them. Also a couple</p>
<p>of Xmas cards from the</p>
<p>Camerons.</p>
<hr />
<p>Friday 2<sup>nd</sup>&nbsp;March.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; Sunny. Lay in till 11 oclock</p>
<p>&amp; then did stables. Had a half</p>
<p>holiday in afternoon &amp; lay</p>
<p>in bed &amp; read. Got an</p>
<p>accordian from Steenwerck</p>
<p>for the hut.</p>
