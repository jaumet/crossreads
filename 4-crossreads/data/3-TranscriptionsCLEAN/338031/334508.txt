  
 Jany 22 to 30 Hard solid drilling & fixing up our equipment for France. To give an idea of the thoroughness of British Army methods we were furnished a few weeks before our departure with special strong boots for the campaign. We wore them for a week to break them in & have defects corrected, for on the march feet are more important than stomach. A man can survive the loss of a few meals but if his feet are such that every step is an agony he can't possibly last for fighting purposes.
