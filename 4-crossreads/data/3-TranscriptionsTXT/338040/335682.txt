
June 10thGerman guns silent. We witness eight of our planes chasing 2 Taubes one of which was brought down. Truth of Naval Engagement.June 11thWhitsun day. Verdan fiercer than ever. Burial of a 20th lad. Miserable weather (N.D.)June 12thEnemy guns very silent in our quarters. Ypres battle going strong. Artillery heard by us. Our party returns from Bois Grenier to Ft. Rompu. Quick march under wretched conditions.
