
May, 1915Thursday 6A thunderbolt fell today when I heard that Col. Meredith remains here o/c Camp & I am detailed to remain with him - The Reg. leave their horses & go as Infantry - Both the c/o & I feel it very much & he is selfish in keeping me, when he was going I was one of his Officers that would go at any price, now that he stops he keeps me as his aide -Letters from Laura   (23/4/15               (10/5/15  "          "         "        12/5/15Also from Lorna & Ralph I shall probably see him now, my job is to come on with the Brigade Horses & 600 men.Laura sent scarves & Tobacco
