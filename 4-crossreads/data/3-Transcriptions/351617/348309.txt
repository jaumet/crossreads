<p>Everyone was happy &amp; singing until we got into the Suburbs when we all got to the windows to acknowledge the waves &amp; shouts of people all along the line &amp; on the stations as we flew through</p>
<p>About 11 am we got out of the train at Waterloo Station London &amp; were marched round to Australian Head Quarters Horseferry Rd Not far from Waterloo Bridge &amp; Westminster Abbey. There we were addressed by some Staff Officer on Saluting, Soldierly appearances, &amp; numerous other things, the Riot Act was read to us &amp; we were then taken round &amp; paid whatever money was due us.</p>
<p>Offices for any information at all about London were there for the use of the troops &amp; it took us no time to find out</p>
