 TUESDAY, JANUARY 28, 1919. 
 Fine but very cold. Round 
 town all the day. Had lunch 
 up at the Bank with Kieth and 
 Barry. Barry &amp; a couple of mates 
 went to &quot;Us&quot; at night, but said 
 it was a pretty bum show. Kieth 
 &amp; I went home. As we were both 
 very tired.
