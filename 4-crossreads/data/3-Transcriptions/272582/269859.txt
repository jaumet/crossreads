<p>Date<br/>Aug 4th 1917<br/>3 N.C.O's and men proceeded on leave to the United Kingdom.  1 N.C.O. rejoined from leave<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men employed in bakehouse baked 30,512 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 5, 1917<br/>Strength: 1 Officer 82 O.Ranks, not including 1 P.B. man attached, 2 in hospital, 1 in detention and 11 on leave.<br/>69 N.C.O.'s and men baked 42,920 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 6 1917<br/>One conviction of crime of Absent without leave.  Punishment awarded: Deprived of 1 day's pay.</p>
<p>Aug 7 1917<br/>One conviction of crime, i.e. interference of the duties of the Regimental Police.  Award:  Deprived two day's pay.<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 39,800 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 8th 1917<br/>5 N.C.O.'s and men proceeded on leave to the United Kingdom.<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked</p>
