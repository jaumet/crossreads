 Monday 1 November 1915&nbsp; In Cairo during the morning. Back at 1-45 p.m.&nbsp; Reading, writing, &amp; sleeping during the afternoon. 
 Tuesday 2&nbsp;November 1915&nbsp; All souls day.&nbsp; Visited Old Cairo today with Drs Henry of Grafton &amp; Cunningham of India. Coptic Churches, one Mosque &amp; the Nilometer.&nbsp; Letter from Car&nbsp;Joseph. Wrote to them as also to Mrs Keohan &amp; Mrs Ryan. 
 Wednesday 3 November 1915&nbsp; Writing during morning. From 2 till 6-30 p.m. in Cairo  most  around the bazaars off the Moushky with Drs Henry, Hollywood and&nbsp;[blank].&nbsp;Felt rather miserable, and about 6 p.m. wretched&nbsp;noises in ears, rushing in the head, heaviness in legs below the knees, miserable all over, too talkative words flowing correctly &amp; with much facility; worse coming home in tram at dinner wretched, 8 p.m. same but becoming worse inclined to go to bed at 8 p.m., some visitors too talkative to them; during night no sleep a great battle in progress all over body between lucocytes and some invading organism. Passing much urine 6 times in 12 hours, large amount each time. Imagining all sorts of things.  Not fit to get up for breakfast . Treat &ndash;&nbsp;Salacine &amp; x grs, .  Dormoporde grs.&nbsp;Phenactin [phenacetin] xx grs&nbsp;[indecipherable] Laxative 3 tabs.
