Copy of Report from Preparatory Staff Course.

held at Kandahar Barracks,
Tidworth, Hants.
 




General Report
on Class.

They are well selected and intelligent with ability above the average.  With a little more experience and training they should make good staff officers.  Their industry and behaviour have been all that can be desired.


Individual Report.

Lieut. C. H. Peters, M.C.
Likely to do good work; did very well in the practical scheme.




 
Note.  Composition of Class, 2 Officers from 1st, 2nd, 4th and 5th Divisions, A.I.F., and 1 only (Peters) from 3rd Division A.I.F..
