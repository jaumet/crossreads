  
 Was out this afternoon experimenting with my camera, it was a fairly hot day, had a good look over the ancient church at Amesbury, she dates back from the 12th Century & the carving is quite solid in her today, but very peculiar the parson took me over it & explained all its history which is very interesting. Another batch of N.C.O.s arrived from France tonight they are sending the boys over now alright.
