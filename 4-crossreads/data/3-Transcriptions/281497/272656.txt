<p>2nd. A picket from our Coy. were out last night patrolling the roads for everyone is supposed to be in their billets by 9 oclock, needless to say this rule is <u>strictly</u> observed, hence the picquets.</p>
<p>There was a musical evening last night at Headquarters.</p>
