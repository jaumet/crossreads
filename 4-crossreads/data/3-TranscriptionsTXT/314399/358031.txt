 Monday 3 February 1919 Nothing to do at all today. Very cold all night. Still on the floor. Moved to a new hut. Tons of mud everywhere. Into Havre today. List went up showing our positions etc on the staff. In an HQ &amp; [indecipherable] allotted. 
 Tuesday 4 February 1919 Still cold. Moved again to another hut. this time to a bed &amp; the hut partitioned. Much better. Into Havre again.
