
<p>987</p>
<p>WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 5, 1919.</p>
<p><s>Fine</s>Snowing all day &amp; as cold</p>
<p>as charity. About town</p>
<p>in morning. Met Major Glover</p>
<p>&amp; Eric MacKellar &amp; we went</p>
<p>up the city to lunch. I came</p>
<p>back &amp; sat by the fire all the</p>
<p>afternoon. Alan &amp; I went to</p>
<p>Buzz Buzz again at night</p>
