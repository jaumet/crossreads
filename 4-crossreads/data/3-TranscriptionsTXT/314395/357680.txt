 I'm going to make another prophecy as to the duration of the war.  I give it until October 1918. 
 Best wishes for your welfare during the coming year. 
 Your affect. Son George. 
 P.S.  Shortly I will receive 10 days leave and as I expended that other money in Paris I will shortly cable you for some more.  If it is not convenient, I hope you won't send it.  But I am asking on chance, and, being confident of your approval, express my sincere gratitude in advance. G.
