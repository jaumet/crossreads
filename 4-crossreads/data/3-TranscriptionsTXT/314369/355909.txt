
24 October 1915Sunday21st After Trinity
After church parade go into Cairo with Dad   &  visit the ancient   &  Historical mosques of Sultan Hassan   &  the Coronation MosqueLater we are shown the old Coptic church   &  the Crypt in which the Holy family hid from Herod. it contains Pictures 900 yrs old.
1915 October 25Monday
This afternoon all details in the Bn. were lined up to find out how many were fit   &  ready to return to the front
