We have been going to the range this week to do a bit of shooting the result being a good majority of the men shooting very fair. 
 29th January 7th & 8th Batt. Left camp for Ismailia to engage with the Turks who were reported to be on the banks of the Canal but it seems when they got there the enemy were on the retreat being pursued by the Sikhs Ghurkas & an English regiment.
