<p>Nutty a big raking chestnut 17.1 hands, three white stockings star on forehead. Branded on shoulder [diagram] also under saddle [T with 21 under] and [arrow pointing upward] on rump, dont look much but beleive me he is a great old plodder &amp; worth his oats<br/>20. 6.17</p>
