It ultimately came to light &amp; was found on end of Pier. The weather seemed to be getting bad &amp; things were busy all around the landing Pier getting things secured. The wind &amp; sea rose &amp; in less than an hour everything was disturbed. I had to ship the cases again to side of bank. A guard came down &amp; relieved me &amp; I went to tea with McLaren Lowland Battery. It rained heavy &amp; I got coats for my men from McLaren.
