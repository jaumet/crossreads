
there - by 'they' I mean these American mission people -  &  their system of education at any rate must be excellent, boys of 14 or 15 could speak English almost perfectly  &  indeed most of them were Christians too.Though why they should convert him  &  condemn him to have only one wife while he has an excellent religion which suits his temperament  &  makes allowances for his nature I cannot imagine. I believe we gave the teachers there a very awkward time indirectly as awkward questions were asked by these youths when they saw us working on Sunday. We had an excellent camp there on a clover field with shade for the horses  &  a canal was by, Alas too good to last: we abode there
