  
 18 May 16 At Kingston. Our concert party & band gave an entertainment ashore in aid of Red Cross.  They had a large audience & it was a great success. 
 19 May 16 At Kingston 
 20 May 16 Left pier in forenoon watch & proceeded to Port Royal where we anchored. 22nd May anchored at Port Royal. 
 22 May 16 Proceeded to sea a short distance from Port Royal & done sub-calibre firing. Came back and anchored at Port Royal. 
 23 May 16 Anchored at Port Royal. 
 24 May 16 Done 6 in firing & came back & anchored off Kingston. 
 25 May 16 Anchored off Kingston.
