 Feb 3. On once more, much refreshed after a good bath and rest in the camp.  The journey along the valley of the Rhone simply magnificent as spring is just making itself felt. 
 Feb 5th Run thru Monte Carlo, Nice &amp; Mentone, unfortunately it was dark, still the beautiful moonlight allowed us to see to a certain extent.
