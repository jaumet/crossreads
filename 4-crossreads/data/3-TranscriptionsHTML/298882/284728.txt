<p>although my position as O.C. Train entailed considerable responsibility there was no need to worry.  We passed through Abbeville, Noyelles Romescamp and just before reaching <strike>Rouien</strike> Rouen were met with the prettiest sight imaginable &ndash; a long sweep of high country rising to a graceful but steep summit along which were perched a cluster of gaily painted houses, green trees above and fields below perfected the picture.</p>
