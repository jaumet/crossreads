<p>so I just sit in the Stern-sheets amongst the Cushions while they alternately do the rowing.  They are nice light skiffs that we hire, with outriggers but no sliding seats.  There is only about 35 minutes rowing to the Top of the River but it is delightfully pretty.  It was a cool still moonlight night &amp; the road was as smooth &amp; clear as glass.  I was alone but it was a delightful ride.  I always feel nervy about passing through Amesbury.  My lamps get cold in 10 seconds if the light goes out.  Ugh! it is raining.
