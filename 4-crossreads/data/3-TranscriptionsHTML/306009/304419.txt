<p>1915<br/>Aug. 23 Mon.<br/>Ray Smith admitted to Hospital to-day with influenza so I had my evening walk alone &ndash; My word!  My French can do with a tremendous amount of polishing.<br/>In Cairo &amp; district at the present time there are 10 Australian Hospitals &amp; Convalescent Depots of which Nos 1 &amp; 2 Generals are the largest. The personnel of the 10 places is 111 Officers, 789 N.C.Os &amp; men &amp; 297 Nursing Sisters.<br/>Some Canadian A.M.C. men are now stationed in Egypt.</p>
