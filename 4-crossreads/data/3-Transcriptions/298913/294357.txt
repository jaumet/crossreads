<p>&amp; donkeys. Camels are not seen here.<br/>The streets are, generally speaking, narrow. The buildings are generally small &amp; dirty. Opera square is the main part of Cairo &amp; here are Ezbekia Gardens, a small park, with a bandstand &amp; restaurant. The YMCA are here too &amp; have a skating rink amongst their amusements. This place is always full of soldiers. A light meal can here be obtained for a few disasters. In Cairo however restaurants are few &amp; far between &amp; the food obtained there is questionable. The price of food anyhow is very high. Anything made with flour is very dear, &amp; a few cakes soon eat up the "disasters", trains are very few &amp; far between &amp; travel well up to time &amp; are fast.</p>
