 30 June 1915 Wednesday 
 Put in part of the day in going along our firing line   &amp;  having a look at the dead Turks who are lying in heaps in front of our trenches 
 Heavy thunder storm overtakes us while returning from a sapping job at 8.30 pm   &amp;  wets us   &amp;  our camp while we are away 
 1915 July 1 Thursday 
 Sun shines brightly again this morning   &amp;  the wet is soon forgotten 
 Six men wounded while getting water this morning by shrapnell 
 Sapping again 4 to 8 pm
