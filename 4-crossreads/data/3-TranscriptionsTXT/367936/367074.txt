Wednesday 22 November 1916Mr. Edwards (A Squadron) and party of us rode about 10 miles to where the scrap was at Bir-el-abd and fixed up Ronald Macdonald's grave, also Boughtons.  Fenced them in and put sandbags over them.On telephone from 6 to 10 p.m.
Thursday 23 November 1916Practiced signalling and got ready to shift.  On the lamp from 10-30 till one at night.
