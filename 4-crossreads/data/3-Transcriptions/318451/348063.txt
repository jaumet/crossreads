<p>Tuesday<br/>Have not been out much today.  The weather continues as cold as ever.  People say they have not had such cold weather for many years.  Snow is falling again.</p>
<p>Wednesday<br/>Went to Bailleul to see Mr. Mann at the Hospital.  Cemetry this afternoon.  Class tonight, small 10, but a good meeting.  Still very cold.</p>
<p>Thursday<br/>Went to Houplines today to Canteen Brigade at 2 oclock.<br/>Then spent the rest of the afternoon at the Canteen purchasing stores.</p>
<p>Friday<br/>Went to Houplines to Canteen.  Weather fine, clear &amp; very cold 10&deg; below zero Centigrade, that is about 12&deg; Farenheit.  Canals &amp; rivers all frozen over.  Paris coal supply threatened.</p>
