<p>June Saturday 2 1917</p>
<p>General duties all morning <indecipherable /> off at 3,30 p m. <br/>Jock &amp; I go down Wimereaux I for tunic at tailors not done, blast him had it a week already. These French are the most casual crowd I've ever met<br/>Tea of omellete &amp; bread butter, took &frac34; hour to serve us. Caught tram into Boulogne. Pictures, Cinema, Pastry supper, so long.   Heavens, took several home,  Catch motor back  [indecipherable]  &amp; arrive only &frac14; late.   Fix it up, &amp; bed.<br/>Letters from Bob &amp; Johno.</p>
