<p>was sighted about 12 noon &amp; by about 2 we anchored of Freemantle The W.A. got there bags out &amp; gave there hammocks Etc in  The Dr came on &amp; spent about 2 hours on a farcical inspection put a thermometer in each mouth &amp; eventually about 7 PM it was given out than no one was to land &amp; the W.A. are to do 7 days quarantine One more case of the rotten incompitence &amp; incapacity of the army &amp; Gov officials generally  From here about1 mile out Freemantle does not look much</p>
