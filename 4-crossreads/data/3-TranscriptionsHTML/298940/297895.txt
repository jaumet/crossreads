<p>Beyond the continuation of the railway, &amp; a tramway to Boulogne, nothing seems to head the view of the sea just showing over the brow of the hill in front of us.<br/>Instinctively ones eyes always turn to Wimereux when out in the open, for it represents all that is possible of the joys of modern civilisation in the neighbourhood. Yet we shall possible see Boulogne before we see Wimereux, for unlike this camp, the convalescent camp  nearer  Boulogne holds out hopes of leave.</p>
