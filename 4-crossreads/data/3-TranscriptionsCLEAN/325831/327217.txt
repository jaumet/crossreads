Another reason for our delay was the alleged activity of the Hun Untersee Boats German [U Boats - Unterseeboots] in the region of the Canaries.
It was stated that quite a number of Transports were kept at Durban & Cape Town, pending the provision of an Escort. But no one knows the real reason, & Rumour was ever a lying jade.
Durban is a pretty place - & I went for several train drives round about. The trams are Municipal, & soldiers were allowed free - a grateful concession, which was much appreciated. I saw the Zoo, Museum & Art Gallery. The latter is nothing like up to the Sydney one.
