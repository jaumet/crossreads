 Friday 31/5/18 Another glorious day, sun very hot, fritz dropped Bombs by the Ton round here last night &amp; this morning also shells got very close to here at times. had a go in with Sgt OBrien &amp; Sgt Maj Harrison over lights last night there will be trouble if they repeat it tonight. Our men coming out tonight for spell of about 10 days, going about 6 or 7 kilos away deferred Pay. &pound;51-7-0 Owe me 68/6 
 Starting new diary to day. So good Bye to old one
