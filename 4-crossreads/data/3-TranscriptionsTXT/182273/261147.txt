1918   July
18 THUR  Fine day. Blowing hard
Posted letter to Amy&radic;.
Amm. going up. Very quiet.
Writing at night.
Heard of news of Foch's
offensive.
19 FRI  Fine day. Posted Card to
Amy Mc&radic;. & Aussie to May. Very
Quiet. Went into St. Omer at
6 P.M. Stayed overnight. had
a good time with Cic. Rec.
letter from Tense.
20 SAT  Fine day. very warm.
Market day in St Omer. French
celebrating French success
17.000 prisoners & 230 guns.
Heavy storm in aft. Had
a good time. Aitken &
Snowy went mad. Drove
[cart?] home. left Aitken in
town. arr camp 11.30 PM
