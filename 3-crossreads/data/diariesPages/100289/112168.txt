Monday 11.9.16
As the happy home has been broken up, had to revert to Fatigue work of which there is any amount just at present. This camp is being closed down for the winter.
Tuesday 12.9.16
Fatigue work practically the whole of the day. Very quiet on front out from where we are at present. Generally a lull before a storm.
