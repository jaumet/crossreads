Sunday Nov 24th to Sunday Dec 1st Mardyk
on duty 2 ? 10 pm so didn't have a chance of going out at all : plenty of prisoners rehabilitated civilians going through lately also refugees going back to their old homes.
Monday Dec 2nd ? Sat 7th Dec Mardyk :
on duty 6 ? 2 : went to the pictures a couple of nights : our long week end off so Gil I are going to St Andre at midnight :
Sunday Dec 8th : Lille :
slept in a railway carriage : up at 6 am, washed then strolled into the City : got a room at a nice little estaminet : fine city in the afternoon the streets were crowded with people as everyone was on foot owing to Fritz having taken away the tramcars : the city hasn't been knocked about much though all bridges in around it have been destroyed : got a few photos of them : went to a French cinema at night : small paper notes cardboard money very amusing :
Monday Dec 9th Lille
