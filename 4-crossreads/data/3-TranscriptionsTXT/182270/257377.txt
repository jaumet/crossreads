 THURSDAY, MARCH 6, 1919. 
 1016 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;6 
 Fine &amp; beautifully warm. Played 
 gmes for a bit &amp; then lay in 
 the Sun &amp; baked. It was just 
 glorious. Passed H.M.S Challenger 
 in afternoon. Saw birds today 
 for the first time following us, also 
 a whale &amp; some flying fish. 
 Had a impromtu Jazz band 
 on deck at night &amp; it was 
 a good break in the monotony 
 Days run 348 miles.
