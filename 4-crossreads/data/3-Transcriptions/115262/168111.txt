<p>Monday 10&nbsp;December 1917.</p>
<p>Turned out at 5.30 am. A mouthful to eat. Saddle up, in full gear and on the trek once more.</p>
<p>Crossed Waddi Guzzi at 11 am - 12 noon and travelled to camp site near Shellah Junction, where we bivouaced.</p>
<p>Passed enemy fortifications <s>near</s> used during early part of stunt.</p>
<p>In bivouac at our destination at 3 pm.</p>
<p>What a satisfaction known only to those who came through <s>with</s> the stunt.</p>
