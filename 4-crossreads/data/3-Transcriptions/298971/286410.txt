<p>3rd October (Tuesday)</p>
<p>On incinerator.  More shells dropped at 3 pm some near our billet.  A number of casualties including an old man killed.  Our guns retaliated.</p>
<p>4th October (Wednesday)</p>
<p>On incinerator.  Early in afternoon Fritz started to drop shells all over the town.  When we returned to our billet the others were missing, having gone to the brewery cellars.  At 9 pm more shells dropped all round us.  Three times</p>
