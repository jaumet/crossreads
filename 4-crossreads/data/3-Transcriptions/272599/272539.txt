<p>October Thursday 18 1917</p>
<p>Mac goes to Stirling friends. Mrs. Tony takes me round Cathedral &amp; castle. River walks.  History of castle.  Norman Chapel 1076. think of it? Neville's Cross.<br/>Stroll round town . <br/>Sing song at night all the girls sing &amp; play nicely.</p>
