<p>Australian waterside Workers would be vindicated &amp; they certainly succeeded.  <strike>Five</strike> Nine men working on each hulk (six men filling, one man on the winch &amp; two on the trolley &amp; basket above, tipped coal into the old Derbyshire at the rate of about 2 &frac12; tons to the minute. At six o'clock they knocked off again for tea.</p>
<p>During this time the health officer had held a long conversation with the ships captain O.C troops (Major Jacobs) &amp; the adjutant (Captain Rossiter) at the foot of the gangway.</p>
