Sep 3rd. Sunday.
Church parade 10.30 Bgde. Afternoon looked through then short while in Cathedral listening to organ. Took several snaps while out. light showers rather cloudy. Church at Soldiers Home evening just in before exceedingly heavy showers came. Did little shoping after service then home to beg (Bed ?).
