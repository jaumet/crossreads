<p>31.1.15</p>
<p>to-night dated 29th November also a couple of magazines &amp; a tit-bits from Edie Tomlins dated the 16th Jan.   Broncho Finn who is to be sent back to-morrow went in to Heliopolis to-night &amp; commenced playing up, Corporal Balhausen was sent in to arrest him &amp; brought him back &amp; put him in the guard room for the night.<br/>There is no post office here &amp; I am afraid  that I cannot get the letters posted to catch the mail</p>
<p>Mon 1st  [Feb.]<br/>Well heres the commencement of another month &amp; still on the same old thing.  I thought when I enlisted the war would be pretty well over now &amp; things are just the</p>
