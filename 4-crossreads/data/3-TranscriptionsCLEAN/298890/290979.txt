  
 21. Thursday. Glorious spring weather. Feeling weak in morning. Better during day and did good work. Went for a walk & called on Mrs Oliver. Sun all day. 
 22. Friday Lovely weather. Offensive of Germans in France. 
 23. Saturday Went down town to bank etc. Lovely day came home on top of bus. Quinine [indecipherable] 
 Weekend Terribly depressed by quinine.
