May 23rd N.N.W. M.314
Up usual time 6-30. 7-30 physical drill. Dress for Church Parade. Dress with Cap shoes. Parade at 9-5 a.m., Service about 9-30 on Port side Boat Deck, Band in attendance. Good service. After mending till dinner. Afternoon C S reading and resting about deck. After tea quiet talk with Goode on N.Z. and present occupation, spent remainder of evening at Service, good singing, lecture on Drinking habits. Weather morning changeable, fair after dinner.
