<p>July 1918</p>
<p><u>July 1st</u></p>
<p>The last month in the Army!</p>
<p>Tuesday evening class.&nbsp; A new book commenced:&nbsp; &quot;Our Boys Beyond the Shadow&quot; by noteworthy clerics of England proves good reading and is productive of much thought.</p>
<p>The second stages of &quot;deck sports&quot;.</p>
<p>Officers triumph in tug of war.</p>
