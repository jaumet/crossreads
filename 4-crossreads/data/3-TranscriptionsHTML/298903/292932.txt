<p>Wednesday 7th May.  At daylight we went alongside the new pier &ndash; (a fine affair) &ndash; at Port Melbourne.  All hands had an early breakfast &amp; the Victorians &amp; Tassies (590) commenced to land at 8 A.M.  Soon a fleet of cars &amp; charabancs arrived and all were taken to the Barracks where relatives were in waiting.  It was rather an inspiring sight, the cars were decorated and a band was playing, which gave a festive air to the scene, the old tune "Pack up your troubles in your old kit bag" being our welcome in.
