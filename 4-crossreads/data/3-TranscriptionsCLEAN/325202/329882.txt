 Sept 17 Yesterday & today have been taking rations up to advanced HQ near Dickebush & then gone on to the R.E. Dump near Ypres for sandbags to complete the H.Q Camp. Hot & very dusty weather. 
 Toady between Dickebush & Cafe Belge - Fritz was sending some heavy stuff over at one point of the road - and we went through at a gallop for about 400 yards.
