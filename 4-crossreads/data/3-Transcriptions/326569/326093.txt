<p>3.  Cleans gun<br/>4.  Attends to points B, D, A firing<br/>5.  Should have some knowledge of tactics<br/>6.  Reports any breakages to Sergt</p>
<p>Duties of No. 2<br/>1.  Assists No. 1 man<br/>2.  Always beside No. 1 when firing<br/>3.  If signals used, watches for them<br/>4.  Replaces No. 1 if No. 1 is casualty<br/>5.  Examines spare parts to see if they are complete<br/>6.  Carries 4 mags into action, piston rod, spare barrel, spare cylinder &amp; cleaning rods for barrel &amp; cylinder &amp; carries entrenching tool</p>
<p>Duties of No. 3<br/>1.  First link in ammunition chain &amp; carries 8 mags full<br/>2.  Follows at distance of 30 yds<br/>3.  Always brings back empty mags</p>
