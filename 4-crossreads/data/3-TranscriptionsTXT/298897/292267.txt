 Wednesday 19th A cool change as cement had arrived I completed the cookhouse range &amp; was glad to get it finished.  Usual swim during afternoon although 'twas rather cool. 
 Thursday 20th On guard but managed to have my usual swims.  Held a few tests for the men who wished to enter for Anzac sports. 
 Friday 21st Good Friday Church parade in morning.  Had a letter from Morris written in France this being my first authentic information that our men are there.  Usual swim. 
 Saturday 22nd On 2. m's fatigue went down for a practice swim &amp; was timed for swimming 33 yds with manacled hands in 25 seconds.  How this time compares with what it should be I don't know but will probably know more about it if I enter the race on Tuesday Anzac Day.
