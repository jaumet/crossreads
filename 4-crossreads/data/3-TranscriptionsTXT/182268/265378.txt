
Sunday 30/12/17  Hun aeroplane flew over very low  went up to arrange for take over, again wrote to Doff.
Monday 31/12/17. Took over from Colette again, no strafe.
Tuesday 1/1/18  mentioned in dispatches, a bit of enemy shelling on both sides.
Wednesday 2/1/18. Visit from Smythe  sector getting lively
Thursday 3/1/18. Good patrol work done by the Battn
