<p>7 May 1918 Tuesday<br/>Arrived Kantara &amp; telegraphed Nowell to meet me at Ishmalia for a few minutes yarn<br/>Arrived Cairo midday, found Dolly well- she met me on the railway station &amp; looks splendid-   Has a nice cool pension &amp; looks far better than I expected the event takes place around 12th August-<br/>Did not see Nowell at Ishmalia.</p>
