
Thursday June 6Went out to N.Z. Brigade Field Amb. Temporalily attached. Camp situated at Solomon's pools about half way between Hebron & Bethlehem. These pools are there in number of considerable depth & size. At present they contain water so we are in a handy position as regards watering. There are snakes in these pools & in vicinity. Solomons  gardens all that is left of them lie a little lower down from the pools.Friday June 72ND Brigade came in to day from the valley looking absolute wrecks. Heard we will be moving in few days
