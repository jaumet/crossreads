The fish (chiefly herring) is sent up in baskets, & the fisher wives seize their prey  &  hawk it in the streets or in the markets. The best  & cleanest part of Cologne is within the walls of the citadels. The top of the rampart is about 30 yards wide & tree planted as it is forms a fine walk.
