Cairo Zoo 13.2.16
Received certificate for signalling and returned to Tel-El-Kebir. We entrained at Helmieh Siding and went to Tel-El-Kebir on cattle trucks, by a branch line which struck the main line at Zag-a-Zig 26.2.16
Leaving for the Canal arrived at the Canal this afternoon and rigged camp at a place called Serapeum 27.2.16
We expect to go on outpost duty this afternoon. We are camped about 1 &frac12; miles
