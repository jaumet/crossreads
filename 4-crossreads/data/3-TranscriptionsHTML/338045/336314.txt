<p>8th Oct. &ndash; Sunday<br/>Raining &amp; cold, but fine afternoon.  Church at St. Sever at 8 a.m.  After work at 12-30, start on a most interesting tour.  Meet our gallant young Belgian friend &amp; take a tram to Maromme.  Here a fine monument to the French soldiers of 1870-1.  This &amp; a walk right into the country along the hills to a little village St. Jean du Cardonnay a distance of 4&frac12; kilometres</p>
