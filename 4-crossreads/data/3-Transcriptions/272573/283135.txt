<p>Oct 19th. Thurs</p>
<p>The cases in hospital are mostly medical at present.</p>
<p>Saw an aeroplane brought down.</p>
<p>An Aviator who had been brought down by the Germans came into the D.R. wounded slightly.</p>
<p>He said that the clouds that he had passed through this morning were snow.</p>
<p>The peasants are working in the fields lifting the potatoes and the beetroot.</p>
<p>The country round about is taking on a bare wintry appearance. The sky is cloudy, the earth is damp, the air is cold and the trees and fields are bare.</p>
<p>Saw a large number of Carrion Crows flying about, also Starlings, Larks, Yellow Hammers are pretty plentiful.</p>
<p>Oct 22nd. Sun.<br/>There has not been very much dressing to do lately.</p>
<p>Football match to-day. Our team defeated by three points.</p>
