<p>Tuesday 8 February 1916<br/>Mother and Father both came down.<br/>Father went back same night.<br/>Dr Mason says with care will stop pneumonia.  Tempreture still over 103.</p>
<p>Wednesday 9 February 1916<br/>Having very bad nights, no sleep and half delirious.  Living on ice and milk.  Nice having Mother to look after me instead of being in hospital in camp.</p>
