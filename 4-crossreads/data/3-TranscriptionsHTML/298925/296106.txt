<p>we were given tea on the Peir.  We played another programme from 7.30 till 9.30 when even larger crowd were there.  They lined the slope down to the beach and encored every piece "Moonlight Bay" we couldn't play enough for them.  Next day we were due to catch the tram at 9.30 to go back but the Mayor was so pleased with our performance &amp; behavior that he wired for an extension of leave.</p>
