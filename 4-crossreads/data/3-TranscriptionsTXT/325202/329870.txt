 July 26/17 Cycled over yesterday afternoon to Neuve Eglise &amp; saw both Colling &amp; Jimmy Shaw. Coming back at dusk I saw a procession of  many  tanks moving up nearer the line, today there has been heavy artillery all day &amp; the air alive with lanes The offensive is evidently not far off.
