There is some complaint about Australian soldiers undergoing this punishment, while they are in British Detention camp; it is not allowed in the A.I.F. 16/5/16. Took rations and marched to a place called "Berke". A very long march. More Australian mail today. Leuit Lacy is now O.C. of the Company. 18/5/16. Field Marshal Sir Douglas Haig; Commander-in-Chief of the Army in France; visited us today and made a hurried inspection. He is a smart figure of a man; though rather small in statue. There were an enormous number of troops at the "Bull Ring" when he came.
