They finished up by singing "Auld Lang Syne" &amp; in the end half the people were crying, the street by this time was blocked from end to end almost, for Fore Street is quite short &amp; it was with difficulty that we were able to move, it was a great day for everyone.  I made out towards Plymouth &amp; Union Street was simply blazing with flags of all the "Allies" &amp; people were going silly.  The Aussies were jumping on the horses backs in the cars &amp; on anything that came along, the whole place was a mass of excited people waving flags &amp; singing, little bands of kids were parading the streets with tins Kettles old drums &amp; anything they could lay their hands on, in most of these</p>
