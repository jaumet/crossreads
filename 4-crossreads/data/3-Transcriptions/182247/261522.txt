<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">July 10</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">On leave to Amiens again.&nbsp; Herb and Tim came with me.&nbsp; Left Corbie by train at 9.15 and spent day in the beautiful city.&nbsp; Bought souvenirs and had them sent home from there.&nbsp; Had good day and arrived back at Corbie about 9.30.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">July 11</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Pontooning all morning.&nbsp; Concert practice all afternoon.&nbsp; Had group photo of section taken at the Chateau at 5.&nbsp; Concert in hall at 8.&nbsp; Did &quot;Lasca.&quot; and Lichtenberg&quot; which were well received.&nbsp; Good show.&nbsp; Good house.&nbsp; Did &quot;Lasca&quot; in character.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">July 12</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">On fatigues.&nbsp; Heard that the King would pass through Corbie this morning.&nbsp; Waited for an hour or more but he did not show up.&nbsp; Did washing.&nbsp; Spent rest of day writing.</td>
</tr>
<tr>

</tr>

</tr>
