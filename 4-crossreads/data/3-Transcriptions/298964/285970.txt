<p>On 22nd 23rd 5.30 moved en route to attack Maghabda  Arrived Turkish position 5.30. 24th.  23 miles from El Arish hard fighting all day.    Victory was ours at 3.30.  Brigades left for El Arish at 9.30.  1300 prisoners 4 guns. Machine guns etc.<br/>issue</p>
