<p>Monday 10<br/>The equipping &amp; examinations of the draft have been completed &amp; we expect to move any moment. We have parades every few hours to assure out not being out of camp.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11<br/>The weather is beautiful but the pack is heavy.</p>
<p>Thursday 13<br/>Still training for draft to France &amp; dancing about the country with packs.</p>
<p>Saturday 15<br/>Another military funeral at Tidworth, 2nd in my army career.<br/>We are under marching orders &amp; tomorrow we move over to France. The O.C. is giving a supper tonight.<br/>Meet Major Wilson on the road.</p>
