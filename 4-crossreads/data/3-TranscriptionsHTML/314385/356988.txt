<p>1916</p>
<p>Fri Oct 6th<br/>Weather improving.</p>
<p>I have been playing chess nearly all day. Received two letters from C 3 from Paris 1 from [Rev Ctgn?]</p>
<p>Sat Oct 7th<br/>Usual duties.</p>
<p>We are feeling the benefit of the rest here. I played some more chess.</p>
<p>In the evening 2 of us walked to Reninghulst for a change of scene also went to the pictures there.</p>
<p>Sun Oct 18th<br/>Day broke beautifully fine took a walk in afternoon towards Ypres some of our bearers are up there.</p>
<p>Mon Oct 19<br/>Routine duties, in the afternoon I went into Pge to make some purchases.</p>
<p>There are rumours that we will be leaving here for where I do not know but will be glad of a change.</p>
<p>Tues Oct 20<br/>Fine weather but everything very flat.</p>
<p>Some of our ambulance were detailed to work on a dump not far off.</p>
