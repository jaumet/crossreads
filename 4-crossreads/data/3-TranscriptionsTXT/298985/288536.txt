 Sunday 18th Had a very quiet night. A little rain this morning. The dugout is very comfortable &amp; warm. Some Indian troops passed yesterday they look well on horseback. 
 Monday 19th Did a little Drill this morning. Swiming Carnival in afternoon. Too cold for me.
