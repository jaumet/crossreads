<p>15th Another fine day. Fritz takes advantage of this and comes over during the day and night dropping. Such occurrences almost makes one wish for wet weather again.<br/>16th Out to Zonnebeke at 4am. Cold. Few shells flying about including mustard oil shells Fritz dropped some bombs near the road. Reached camp again at noon. Very seedy today.<br/>17th Fine. Eyes becoming worse everyday. Fritz came over to have a look at the sights but say! He did not return.
