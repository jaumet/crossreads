 1917 APRIL 
 4 WEDNESDAY 
 Fine but dull & we had a 
 storm of sleet in afternoon 
 Cleaning things round stables 
 ect all morning & we went 
 up the river (Lys) & grazed 
 the horses for a couple of hours 
 in afternoon. Pay parade 
 in eveninv. We are paid 
 at the rate of 1 franc = 81/2 d  & 
 all the civilian people reckon 
 1 franc = 10 d  so we make 11/2 d  
 on each franc. I got two 
 letters from father 26 th  Mar &  
 6 th  Feb. & a letter 26 Jan & a 
 "Bulletin" from Linda.
