<p>No 3 Canadian C.C.S.<br/>B.E.F. France<br/>Sep 25th/17</p>
<p>Mrs C. Hill<br/>Euterpe<br/>Kurraba Road<br/>Neutral Bay<br/>North Sydney<br/>N. S. Wales</p>
<p>Dear Mrs Hill<br/>It is my sad duty to inform you of the death of your Son Sgt Hill 874; 19th B'n, 2nd Aus Div: on Saturday Sep 22 at 2.10 p.m.: I had worship with him a short time before he died &amp; he asked me to write to you giving his love: He was seriously wounded but everything possible was done for his comfort &amp; recovery: His End was peaceful: He will be buried in the Lejssenhoek Cemetery, Belgium:<br/>You have my deep sympathy &amp; I pray that God may Comfort you in your hour of sorrow:</p>
<p>Yours sincerely<br/>A. H. Devon</p>
