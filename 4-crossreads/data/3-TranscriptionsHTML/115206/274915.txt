<p>December  1918.<br/>Friday 13</p>
<p>Southall and H.S.S. "Karoola"<br/>Weather as usual &ndash; dull and miserable. Handed in kit and wrote a few letters. Received an answer pre paid cable from Elsie. I was certainly very pleased to receive same. <br/>Entrained and left S'thall at 1.30pm.Went aboard and was allotted a cot up at the fos'cle head of the old "Karoola".<br/>Quite a large number of patients aboard.<br/>Left the berth and anchored in mastream off battery.</p>
