 JAN. &nbsp; SATURDAY 29 &nbsp; 1916 
 Fine &amp; warm. Linda, Jo and Self went to town in morning. Linda &amp; self had our photos taken at the Burrlitz; we met Jo&nbsp;again and had dinner at Tissots and caught the 2.15 boat to Clifton Gardens &nbsp;We saw M r &nbsp;&amp; M rs &nbsp;Allison and the M c Masters (Binnia) &amp; had a swim then had tea with M rs &nbsp;Allison and home for dinner at night. It was a long day, but think we all enjoyed it. Miss Earle was at Lisheen&nbsp; in &nbsp;afternoon &amp; stayed to tea. Linda &amp; Self sat on Tennis court for a bit after tea
