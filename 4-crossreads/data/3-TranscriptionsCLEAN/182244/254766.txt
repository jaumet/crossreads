  
 They worship the Cow. They have no religion. A Cow is the most valuable possession of a Dinka man, before wife child or anything else. Tall stout & black. Very good fighters with the spear. Plenty of lions, leopards, hyaenas, giraffe (especially) & Elephants. 
 They live on maize, roots of trees, & especially milk.
