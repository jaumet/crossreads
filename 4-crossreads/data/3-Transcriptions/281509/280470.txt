<p>1916 September 4<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Day overcast &amp; somewhat cold.  More carpentering(?) 11 o'clock tea &amp; biscuit at Y. M. C. A. Leave pocket on counter carelessly Returned by Y.M.C.A man. Thanks. Leave again this evening. C Mac &amp; self go into Pop. Tea &amp; bargains. Cinema at Station. Good orchestral band. Enjoy entertainment. Fysh's razor strop. Pretty girl. Home &amp; cold  <br/>Cinema. praying chairs out of cathedral</p>
