<p>June 1915<br/>Thursday 3<br/><u>Fine</u>.  Our artillery roaring again Indian Batt. doing good work again. I with three others went down to the D.A.D.O.S. had a swim in the sea and brought up some bombs for the trench mortar. Cpl. Lyons was in charge of us. Had three good meals today &amp; went into fire trench at 6.45 PM.</p>
<p>Friday 4<br/>Fine. Navy bombarding Kaba Tepe Hill very heartily. Nothing else to write about. Came out of trench about 6 PM. On quartermasters fatigue until 11 PM bringing stores for our Reg. up from the beach. Our chaps took a trench on the left under heavy fire. Many Turks were captured.</p>
