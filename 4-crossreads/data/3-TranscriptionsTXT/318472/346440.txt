 3 August 1918 Saturday A very quiet uneventful day. The weather continues to be very hot making things very uncomfortable. No news of any kind through. 
 1918 August 4 Friday Cloudy &amp; squally with a choppy sea &amp; a fairly heavy swell. Church Parade in the morning &amp; nothing else of much importance. Expect to get Sierra Leone tomorrow
