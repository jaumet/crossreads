<p>Sunday 9 January 1916<br/>Monday 10 January 1916</p>
<p>Tuesday 11 January 1916<br/>Put in the cells for disobeying the guard.  Guard gets into Hot Water &amp; I go free.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12 January 1916<br/>Halfway between Fremantle &amp; Suez.  Mock Trial.</p>
<p>Thursday 13 January 1916<br/>Crossed the line this morning.</p>
