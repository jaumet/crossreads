  
 Seeing amongst others Ellen Terry - Bransby Williams - Florence Smithson- Return to Highgate - Meet Mrs Wm. Scales - Pay a call to Dr. Bousefield - with a sore throat. Correspondence - The night on which - Mar.7th Meet Fred Reade in Town - Together we go to Daly's Theatre & meet Mr. Todd (Actor & Manager) then on to Samuel French's where we select Plays & Sketches - Return to Highgate.
