<p>thinking it would be over each minute but it kept on coming down the faster &amp; I could feel the wet all over, at last I could stand it no longer but leaped out of the sodden blankets gathered them in my arms &amp; joined the crush making for below like drowned rat, we looked a pretty lot for there were dozens in the same boat as myself,  we hung our hammocks on the hooks, &amp; got what rest we could in the hot stifling atmosphere of the lower deck</p>
