  
 [31st Dec. contd] a path to the cookhouse as we expected all our ground to be flooded tomorrow.  Had on gum boots & saved some salvage.  This afternoon had afternoon tea with Madame in tuck shop in Pont de Nieppe with Sam & the boys.  Walk to Nieppe. A terrific bombardment is just commencing probably to usher in the New Year (11 pm)  Forgot to mention that 5 of boys were transferred to A.D.S. today 
 1917 Monday Jany 1st New Years Day More bombardment - and at 2 am this morning a daddy one which lasted some time. Water still rising though not so quickly. 
 Tuesday 2nd River has gone down a little so danger at present is averted. 
 Wednesday 3rd Very cool & strong wind today.  To Pictures at night  Saw Chaplin in "Pangs of Love" & "Mary Queen of Scots"  awful [indecipherable]  Then had fish & eggs at tuck shop.  Very tricky the bowls without handles to drink out of.  Was up in night - not too well 
 Thursday 4th Spent morning in bed  Did not eat anything
