  
 8.  Change of Camp.  Zeitoun to Ferry's Post:- Left Zeitoun March 5th 1916-  Took train to Cairo & thence to Ismailia which is 100 miles from the Capital town- Good train journey- cultivated country- Nile Irrigation scheme- Ismaila a large village- A march of 5 miles chiefl'y on sand & in which we cross Suez Canal brings us to 7th Aust. Fld. Ambulance- Sleep on desert sands under wagon.  Very cold- "Ferry Post Camp.
