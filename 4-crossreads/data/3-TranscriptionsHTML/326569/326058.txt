<p>via Park Lane &amp; Grosvenor Place to Victoria St. Stn. which we looked over.  Tram then for about &frac12; mile &amp; walked to Horseferry Rd. where I inquired about Roy but found he sailed for Australia on Feby. 13th.  Walked then past Parliament Houses &amp; Westminster Abbey along Whitehall for tea at one of Lyons Rooms.  Then got overcoat from Y.M.C.A. &amp; went along Strand to Waterloo Brge. (passing Somerset House) &amp; so to Waterloo Stn. when I caught 7 p.m. train for Wareham.
