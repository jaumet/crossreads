
One of our fellows has been indulging in extremely luxurious pastimes lately. The other day he was on a fatigue party to Khan Yunis & whilst there he nipped off for a swim. He was discovered & details of the event were made known to the O.C., The result being that that swim cost him 35/- shillings.Again he was sent a few days ago to take some fellows over to the Clearing Station.
