<p>4.   rid of it.<br/>We do not know what Fool it is that Has been writing such rubbish to You People.  Dave has nothing whatever to do with Boots except His own Private or Govt. Property.  There was some Talk of a D.C.M. for Billy in Egypt but none of us Know whether it was a yarn or the Truth.  We do have Lieutenants in the A.M.C. Dentists &amp; Q'master are always Lieutenants.  Fancy hearing from Frank Hemming again &amp; now at Brisbane.  What did He want to Enlist for.  You will see that I have also visited Mrs Rogers Sister at Richmond.  It was just like walking into a Friend of life-long standing.  I was made quite at Home.  All are such sincere People just like ourselves.  No pomp no ceremony just An English gentleman's</p>
