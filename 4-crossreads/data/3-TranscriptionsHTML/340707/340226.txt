<p>saluting a policeman in George St. Sort of makes you think what Equality means here.</p>
<p>Passing along Boulevard St Germain (which contains many princely mansions &amp; courtyards which are lost by the system of building in that is rampant here) I notice several demolition for rebuilding purposes in each case there is a single wall between each property &amp; not a wall to each house as we possess.</p>
<p>There is one thing I forgot to mention ie that cats are not expected to kill a rat. Any person claiming that his cat has killed a rat must produce the rat as evidence before he will be believed. What on earth they keep cats for in this rat infested city I do not know.</p>
