<p>doing my best to fall in with my daily routine but not without thinking every now and then of my 14 day's leave.</p>
<p>Sept 9th &ndash; 18 [this date is wrong, it is either the 10th or the 11th]<br/>Yesterday we moved to this new position nearer the line a small village called Halle all that remains of the village is the name</p>
<p>Sept 12th &ndash; 18<br/>Off again at daybreak and found our way through torrents of rain to Lincourt Wood where we are now occupy huts which only a few days ago had german tenants</p>
<p>We are likely to stay here for a couple of weeks.</p>
