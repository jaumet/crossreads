<p>2<br/>enough just post this letter on to Arch &amp; ask him to ring them up &ndash; also it might be as well to let the Secretary of the King's Old Boys union know as they always like to hear these things. I don't know his telephone No but if you ring up the King's School Parramatta they will tell you. &ndash; Well old girl I do hope you are well again &amp; that the old trouble will not recur. &ndash; I expect you will be wondering</p>
