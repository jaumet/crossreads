
I see by last nights "orders" that they are going to send me to Candahar Barracks to a School my luck must be in surely.To-night I am Canteen Sergt. my duty is to preserve order & see that it is closed at the proper time, & then I have to report on the Staff parade. I have a Corporal & 6 men with me, they patrol the joint & report anything wrong to me.26th.
