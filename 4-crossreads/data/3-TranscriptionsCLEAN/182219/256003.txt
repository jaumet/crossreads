In the store tent thing were more serious as the waters had damaged a large quantity of perishable goods. Still there was nothing to be done. The rest of the day we spent in the little bivouac looking out over the flooded waters & to the far Judean hills reasonably dry & comfortable & consoling ourselves that it might be worse.
