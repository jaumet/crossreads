<p>16 Sunday</p>
<p>After lunch Dr De G. took a number of us including Col. Gordjevic to concert in Vodena. School exercises by children &ndash; Immensely interesting cosmopolitan audience. Home in time for dinner. Big Italian officer stopped in road &amp; announced how he loved the English girls.</p>
