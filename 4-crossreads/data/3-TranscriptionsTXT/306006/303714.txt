 21-1-17 working in conjunction with one of our spies, but as the Turks were getting very poorly fed &amp; our planes were too fond of dropping bombs, he decided to come in with the spy &amp; surrender; When 2 miles this side of Rafa Bedouins attempted to rob them, they showed fight the spy was badly wounded when the jew left him he says he killed 5 Bedouins &amp; wounded three, on arriving at Sheikh Towaud he paid a jewish boy &pound;1 to take him to El Arish on the donkey, he had a roll of Turkish notes besides several English &amp; Turkish sovereigns; Good water can be obtained by sinking a couple of feet in the sand on the beach. Had orders today that we are to remain here indefinately. Captured several deserters today
