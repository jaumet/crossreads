
<p>June 16th We took our load to Wagga dump &amp; then went to Saleux sailhead &amp; put on another load &amp; got home at 1 P.M.I had dinner &amp; camouflaged the lorry a bit. I&nbsp; had a bath &amp;&nbsp;we went to Church in the evening with Zita &amp; Marie. Going home with them after serbive - I was on guard so had to leave at 9. P.M.</p>
<p>June 17th We left park at 4.45 A.M. &amp; took our load to Daours, returning to Saleux railhead we got another loat to take back to Park, we picked as many strawberries as we could eat &amp; took a lot home also plenty of rhubarb for the cook house &amp; a huge bunch of beautiful roses for Zita. We spent the evening with Zita in her home as usual Jno &amp; I -</p>
<p>June 18th We dumped our load of ammo &amp; went out on supplies to Frichencourt. On the way home we picked up a very nice girl (Gisele) &amp; jer mother at Montreses &amp; took them to La Chausse where they were living. Jno was too busy in the evening to come to Zita&#39;s. but I was with her all the evening. Jno looked in just before bed time. Frank is on guard tonight.</p>
