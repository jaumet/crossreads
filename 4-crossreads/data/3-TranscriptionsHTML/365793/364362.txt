<p>Spt 14 has broken. Beyond a slight Westerly &amp; a few scattered clouds there is nothing to denote that the rain-god held full sway for <u>days</u> past. The fc'stle head is a regular "flapper", clothing of all sorts &amp; sizes hung out to dry, and there are even blankets, &amp; a pair of leggings. &ndash;<br/>There was a <u>farcical</u> hammock inspection at 2 oclock. In a great many case the Sgts didn't even see our hammocks &ndash; let alone say how many blankets they contained. Six devil dodgers we have on board &amp; not a damn one of them</p>
