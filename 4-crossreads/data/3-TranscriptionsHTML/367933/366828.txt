<p>packet Jan 4th 1918</p>
<p>Otaika Road<br/>Whaugaree<br/>Auckland N.Z.</p>
<p>The Secretary<br/>1st Light Horse Comforts Fund<br/>28 Moore St.<br/>Sydney.N.S.W.</p>
<p>Dear Madam<br/>Enclosed please find P.O.O.for 15/- and I shall consider it a great favor if you will despatch a parcel of that value to my son. I shall be glad if you will send a similar parcel to the last to include 2 lbs butter, cake, toffee, asparagus, chocolate, milk, fruit, salmon etc omitting tobacco.<br/>In your letter you mention a balance of 1/10. Please make use of it in any way you consider advisable.</p>
<p>Again thanking you for your very great kindness in the matter</p>
<p>Yours Faithfully<br/>Chas. A. Falkiner.</p>
<p>Please address parcel to<br/>No.955 Sig. Sergt. G.F.Falkiner<br/>13th Battalion<br/>4th Infantry Brigade.<br/>4th Australian Division<br/>Australian Imperial Forces.<br/>On Service Abroad.</p>
