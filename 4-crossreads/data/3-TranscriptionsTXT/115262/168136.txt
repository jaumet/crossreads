 Brig. Gen. of our Bgde. Concert for 1st Battn. in the Recreation Hut tonight &quot;Vernons Concert Party&quot;. 
  Jan 31st . Inspected by the Brig - Faults found in various places throughout Amb. He spoke to me about having my hair &quot;long&quot; as he termed it. Result - haircut. Word of change of our Reg l  colors &amp; others. Troops in Egypt and Palestine. Wrote home. 
  Feb 1st . &nbsp;Football again otherwise nothing unusual. Concert in Recreation Hut. 
  Feb 2nd . Nothing out of the ordinary. Signs of being here longer than anticipated. Concert again in Recreation Hut. 
  Feb 3rd . Went to Church Parade. Unusual for me. 2 letters from Aust. dated Dec 23rd &amp; 24th 1917. Football again. 
  Feb 4th .  Received parcel from home . As usual. 
  Feb 5th . Received parcel from home.
