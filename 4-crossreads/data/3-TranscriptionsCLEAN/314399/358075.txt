  
 Wednesday 19 March 1919 Rained all day & night. Went to dancing class at the gym & learnt the foxtrot & Missouri waltz. Went to Mountvilliers. Went to dentist again. Becoming quite a permanent thing now. 
 Thursday 20 March 1919 Nice & sunny all day. Have train picquet tonight. Wrote 5 letters. Bill Cowper left for Huppiy. Can't speak at all today. lost my voice.
