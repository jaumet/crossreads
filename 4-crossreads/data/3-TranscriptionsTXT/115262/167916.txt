 Tuesday 29 May 1917. 
 Flying fish seen today by the hundred. Another very hot day&nbsp;and sea a little rougher today. 
 1 st &nbsp;sports meeting this afternoon. 
 Physical jerks during morning&nbsp;&ndash; a practice for the competition in the sports. &nbsp;One of the 15. Japanese Cruiser moving very quickly into various positions yesterday &amp; today.&nbsp; 
 Sports caused amusement&nbsp;among the boys. 
 Wrote 2 letters. 
 Detailed for guard duty tomorrow to mount at 9 am. 
 Sleeping on deck again tonight. 
 G.S.S. Cmn&nbsp;&amp; I&nbsp;went down below into stokehold &amp; engine room, &amp; right along to end of propeller shaft. 
 Speed now&nbsp;Full&nbsp;as indicated on dial above log book below.
