<p>Thursday 3 October 1918<br/>Having a great spell in our lovely new camp at Haifa by the sea.<br/>First shower of rain about 20 points.<br/>McGuinness &amp; Self have record hours flying for the stunt, 55 hours.</p>
<p>Friday 4 October 1918<br/>Tent fixed up, all camp in order.  Maughan, Tonkin, Ross Smith, Mustard &amp; Ned Henry get D.F.C., very glad to hear it.  Smith &amp; Mustard got it very backsheesh.</p>
