<p>Wareham<br/>Xmas Day 1916<br/>[in right margin] No letters yet<br/>spent dull xmas never mind dear</p>
<p>Dear Beatrice</p>
<p>I did not forget that the anniversary of our wedding day was in November close to cup day, but being at the busy battle front I was unable to get an opportunity of writing at the time.</p>
<p>The pleasant and dearest events of our lives are indelible in memory<br/>The ordinary incidents of trouble around to most people battling against fortune, we can look back upon merely as so many [indecipherable] patches in the road of life<br/>And though there remains a never to be obliterated memory of tragedy sad and bitter to us both and which even now at times</p>
