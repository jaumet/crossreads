The camp is quite new and when thoroughly in order will be some place our Div has Two fine Dining halls and Canteen run by . There is also a new Y.M.C.A. Hall near it is a double Building so that one part can be used for reading writing etc. and the other can be used as concert and amusement Hall at the same time.
