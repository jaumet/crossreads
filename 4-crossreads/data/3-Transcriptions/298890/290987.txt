<p>18.Thursday<br/>Bitterly cold day.  Went to Miss N. at 10.15.  Home at 6.  Little clearer towards evening.  Cruelly depressed.</p>
<p>19. Friday<br/>Worked at 22 Harley Street.  Showery &amp; snowy day.</p>
<p>20. Saturday<br/>Same kind of day.  Worked better at night went to 'Old Vic" to see Henry VIII with A.A.S.  Sybyl Thorndike v. good Queen Katherine.  Russell T. a thin &amp;  dyspeptic Wolsley</p>
