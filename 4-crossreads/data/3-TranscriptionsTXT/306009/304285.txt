 1915 Apr. 9 Fri. On leave all day - left Mena at 9 a.m. &ndash; called on French Scout President &ndash; Caught 1.30 p.m. train to Barrage (10 miles) &amp; went across the Nile Bridges there &ndash; locks &amp; sluice gates great feats of engineering skill and very interesting - walked through the beautiful gardens there &amp; also looked over the Museum in them, containing working models of bridges, locks, &amp; sluice gates. &ndash; Returned to Cairo about 5.30. p.m., had tea, followed by walk  round  Cairo  including Esbekieh Gardens. Returned to Mena at 11 p.m. &ndash; Day cost me about 30 piastres.
