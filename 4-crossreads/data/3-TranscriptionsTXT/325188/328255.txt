 Novr 17th Personal Novr 18th 
 1916 Off the coast of  Algeria  again quite hot , did some big gun practice [indecipherable] ours &ndash; Understand we are to go to &ndash; first this will be interesting Must be many letters waiting for me in Egypt . &ndash; We have the usual Gymnasium of mechanicals riding horses etc We have many different regiments on board &ndash; One Scotch officer at  Gibraltar  when I admired his kilts offered to lend them to me to be photographed in, he said they cost &pound; 5 in Scotland. He thought he was doing me a great favor
