<p>December Saturday 1 1917</p>
<p>Received a big mail yesterday Xmas greeting from 18. Also parcel from Elsie Gollan, Emmie Gulliford, Nurse James &amp; Mrs. Wheatly. Wish I had plenty time to reply to all at length. <br/>Our ward a Maturnity Home for the hosp cat today. <br/>Heavy gales &amp; rough seas usual these days. <br/>Met Geo Aldous yesterday. A patient in hosp. convalescent.</p>
