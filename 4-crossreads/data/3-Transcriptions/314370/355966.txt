<p>January, 1916<br/>Friday 14<br/>Work as usual during the day<br/>Go to Helmia camp pictures at night w/- Dad.<br/>Nights very cold &amp; it is hard getting up at 5.30 am</p>
<p>Saturday 15<br/>Exam in Description takes up best part of the morning<br/>My class do rather will.<br/>After lunch I go into Cairo have dinner &amp; go to Pictures<br/>Turn in about 11 pm</p>
