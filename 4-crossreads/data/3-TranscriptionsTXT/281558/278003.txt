 Saturday June 23rd 
 Bapaume : fair day : up at 4 am fell in for our passes at 5 am " walked to Achief La Grand &amp; got the train : landed Amiens 10 am : put in a gread day roaming round the place : markets, etc : had another look at the Cathedral : it was pansy day for the wounded &amp; prisoners of war &amp; there were some pretty girls selling the pansies : caught 8 pm train home &amp; landed back a little after midnight 
 Sunday June 24th 
 Bapaume : up at 7.30 am : dull day : Holy Com at 9.30 am : roam over a village &amp; fed up on red currants : looked over a Hun cemetery &amp; they have certainly had some casualties :
