<p>June 28 this morning, &amp; was put on QM's fatigue, but did nothing &ndash; just fell out &amp; went to Medical Hut. Am applying for leave tomorrow but don't know how I'll get on Several chaps were boarded today, some for Australia, &amp; others for home Service.</p>
<p>28  On QM's fatigues again this morning. Am taking on hut orderly tomorrow, as it's a damn good job. Cold as blazes; just like winter in fact. Cleaned up a few things, but nicked away at 10 oclock &ndash; presumably to see the Quack. As it happened the Doc did see me, &amp; said i'd have</p>
