<p><u>PERIOD X plus 10 DAY to X Plus 14 DAY (inclusive)</u>.</p>
<p>Practice over Replica Trenches.</p>
<p>Equipment of Party; fitting, checking and use.</p>
<p>Testing of vital equipment under as nearly as possible actual conditions.</p>
<p>Night operations.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Night Operations</u> will consist of:</p>
<p>Scout tactics on Field and Parade Ground.</p>
<p>Training of the power of observation, both hearing and seeing.</p>
<p>Moving under alternate darkness and light (Verey Lights to be used.)</p>
<p>Night Tactics.</p>
<p>Night Emergencies.</p>
<p>Work by night over Replica Trench and Course.</p>
<p>Trench clearing by night.</p>
