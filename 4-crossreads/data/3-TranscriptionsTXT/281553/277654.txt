 29th Friday Weather still good.  Heavy Gun fire on both flanks also some battery work on &amp; about our front.  Turks began fierce Bomb throwing again 4-30 to 5 p.m., remainder of night very quiet. 
 30th Saturday Weather good &amp; bright.  Bread &amp; meat been off since 27th.  Gale. 
 31st Sunday Weather continues good.  Bread &amp; Meat issued.  Front quiet.  Used Japs Mortar 9-30 p.m. to quieten Turks with their Broom Stick Bombs playing on Pope's.  Out for same reason again 1-30 a.m.
