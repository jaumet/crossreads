 Aug 19 midnight tonight. Only 288 during the last 24 hours. Buksheesh Concert ,&amp; as usual the Colonel caused much laughter, but the best singer was a deaf chap. 
 20 Heavy seas last night &amp; a strong head wind. Crossed the line about midnight.
