<p>Mafrah and shows you a few German planes with that hated cross on them (it gives me a shiver just to look at it even on paper) and the others are all our planes; many of which I have seen on this front. It is wonderful what they know about them, they have even got the English names written there in German. You will see &quot;Martinside&quot; and &quot;Doubledecker&quot; etc. The turkish writing is directions as to how the different part of the plain should be when it is flying level. The bit you may care for - I got it at Mafrah. Those chains are&nbsp;for fastening it onto the headstalls the <s>tl</s> turkish cavalry use on their horses</p>
