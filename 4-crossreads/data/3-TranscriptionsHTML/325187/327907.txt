<p>[Tuesday 13 June 1916]</p>
<p>Hot again today 20 c/s of Comforts from Mrs Bradden arrived so the men are well fed for this week.<br/>I got a parcel from an old Wingalea neighbour soks thick woollen ones   Temperature 115 in the shade!!!<br/>One of our Aeroplanes treated us to a good exhibition of flying this morning on a powerful  machine  I hope to see an aerial fight some day<br/>1 Gallon of Water per diem per Military Train to Railhead thence by Camel to us-<br/>Australasian from Joe &amp; Independent.</p>
