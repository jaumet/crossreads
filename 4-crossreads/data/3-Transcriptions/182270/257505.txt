
<p>SATURDAY, JULY 12, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, but cloudy. Went down to</p>
<p>Kimbers farm &amp; picked out</p>
<p>286 X bred wether weaners &amp; brought</p>
<p>them home &amp; put them in 4D</p>
<p>Paid 26/- for them.</p>
<p>Tickle helped me.</p>
<p>Heard Alan would be</p>
<p>home about 28<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;July.</p>
