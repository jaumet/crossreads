
May, 1915May 21-22 missingSunday 23Spent the day in orderly room busy with Pay Sheets etc:- Very hot & our horses will be glad of the reed roof shelters being built - Rumour that 500 of our Brigade are back at Alexandria having had a tough time in the trenches but always successful.Here with the Horses we all endeavouring by Lectures in the heat of the day to instruct the Reinforcements as well as possible.
