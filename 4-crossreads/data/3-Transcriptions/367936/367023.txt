<p>Saturday 12 August 1916<br/>Still waiting.<br/>Took my boots off for first time &amp; found my feet still there.<br/>6th &amp; 7th Regts. passed in evening going back to camp.</p>
<p>Sunday 13 August 1916<br/>Left Ogratina in morning and came back to camp.  Lovely to change &amp; have a wash again.<br/>Colin Sams came over in evening.  Heard that we are to be sent back for a rest.</p>
