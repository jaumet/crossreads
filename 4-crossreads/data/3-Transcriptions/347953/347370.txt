<p>23<br/>Tuesday 29 June. Enemy commenced heavy rifle fire right along our front at 12p.m. lasting until 1 p.m. &amp; attacked over left front vigorously with bayonet &amp; Bombs. They fell into our secret saps &amp; were polished off by our machine gun and rifle fire. Their dead numbered about 500. They attacked again about 3 a.m. &amp; were badly repulsed. 30 men came over the parapet at Quinn's Post &amp; were severely dealt with.</p>
