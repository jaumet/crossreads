<p>-- Mud --</p>
<p>While were fighting for the nation, in our Present situation,<br/>Weve example in the good old common mud,<br/>For it sticks with desperation, and makes quite a decoration<br/>Till a soldier man looks something like a spud<br/>When you meet an old relation, and he starts the conversation,<br/>It's the same &ndash; unless you nip it in the bud &ndash;<br/>For we curse the whole creation for its slimy old foundation,<br/>Drives a man to Aviation &ndash; does the mud &ndash;<br/>When with frantic jubilation, we shall hail the fermentation<br/>Of the war, then e'en forgetting.</p>
