 27 Thursday Slept &amp; rested &amp; read &amp; in the afternoon Maud &amp; I went for a nice walk. Nice day but cold. 
 28. Friday Same routine. Went to Bedales after tea to see Girlie Smith. 
 29. Saturday. Lovely day. Miss Whishaw Maud &amp; I walked  to Frox  [Froxfield?] in afternoon Girlie Smith &amp; a small companion came.
