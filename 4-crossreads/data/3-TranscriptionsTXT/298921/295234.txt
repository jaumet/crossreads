September, 1915Monday 20In these grounds a very fine leafy flowering tree Golden Mohur Flamboyant, I believeComMon in N Q'land will not stand frost well but is most picturesque &amp; should suit the back country &ndash;Col Manifold P.M.O. inspected Hospital &amp; says a fortnight will see me right. I hope so: Heard today that the L.H. Brigade is knocked on the head, &amp; all available men go back to Gallipoli &ndash;I will not be fit for some 10 days yet at least.
