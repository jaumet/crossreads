
Sunday 10 February 1918Went up to Bde. & saw the Col.  Am to go up when Adjt. goes on leave.  Wrote a good deal of mail & played Bridge.  Finished reading Thomas.
Monday 11 February 1918Nothg. doing all day 'cept Bridge & reading.  Deadly life this.  Wrote few letters.  Very windy.  No lrt from home yet.
Tuesday 12 February 1918And no Pancakes!!!  Took the parade at Belgian Farm & had lunch at the Div. Club.  Played Bridge rest of day & wrote a few lres.
Wednesday 13 February 1918Went up the line with Musgrove & stayed at Omad Farm.  First lre. from Rup.  Shake some of these bally sigs. up.
Thursday 14 February 1918Went to all stations & had a look round.  Called on Major Lloyd & lunched there.  Muddy walk.  Anor. topping lre. from Rup.  H.Q. did a turn on all night.
Friday 15 February 1918Had brekker at 11.15 owing to late rising & duty during the night!!  Paid a visit to the "Archie" battery.  Put in some good work on the writing tablet.  Big front in the eveng.
Saturday 16 February 1918Day broke clear & frosty.  Splendid observation.  Went all round the 'phones & put them straight.  Archie battery had a busy day.  'Phones all going well now.  New signallers gettg. settled down to their job.
