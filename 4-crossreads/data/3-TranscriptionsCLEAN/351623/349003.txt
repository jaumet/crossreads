  
 11 Dec 16 Left Rosyth with Second Light Cruiser Squadron, HMS Southampton, HMS Dublin HMAS Sydney & Destroyers. Proceeding to Scapay Flow. 
 12 Dec 16 Arrived at Scapay Flow at 9 am. Coaled ship. Left again at 3.30 pm with 2nd Light Cruiser Squadron in search of German armed Merchantman. 
 13 Dec 16 Cruising in vicinity of Iceland searching for German Raider. 
 14 Dec 16 Searching for Raider. Heavy seas running 
 15 Dec 16 Arrived at Scapay Flow at 5 pm & coaled ship
