
24.4.18Enemy artillery directed light harassing fire on roads in vicinity of Treux about 12 4.5's were dropped in valley in D.23.b. & d. between 10.45 & 11 p.m.  At 4.30 a.m. about twenty 4.5's dropped in same locality.  About 20 gas shells fell in T.5.b.60.70 between 3.30 & 3.40 a.m.Enemy Machine Guns fired short bursts throughout the night.  Enemy Aircraft, Nil.
