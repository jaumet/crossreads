  
 Friday 5 July [indecipherable] attacks severely [refused?] 
 Parade by Capt. Ferguson and various instns. received. 
 Afternoon exercise & grazing along main Villers Brettoneaux - Amiens Rd. 
 Visited with Harry Richards various woods & were finally too close to a bombardment so came away at gallop. Saw many French, & tanks. 
 To La Motte concert. Very good 7th Bde - Met two little Bailies.
