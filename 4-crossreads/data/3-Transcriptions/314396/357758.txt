<p>Went to the city &amp; got [indecipherable] etc. Got a few stamps from a Maltese.</p>
<p>Saturday. 12th Dec 1914.<br/>Put up tents &amp; took notes from old papers I had. Got a tent for the corporals (only 7 of us). Got payed in the night. Collected &pound; 2 = 195 piastre</p>
<p>Sunday .13th. Dec 1914<br/>Swimming parade &amp; general leave from 1 P.M. till 10 PM. for us.<br/>Orderly Corp. again tomorrow. Went into the city at 3.30. went around a bit &amp;  came home at 10. Was told off  by Mr Dawkins.</p>
<p>Monday. 14th Dec 1914.<br/>Things getting stirred up in the company. All the sergeants reported for neglect of duty, (for not reporting men absent.) Nothing much else doing so far. Went into the city &amp;  had a good feed of oranges.</p>
<p>Tuesday. 15th. Dec. 14.<br/>Same as usual. Went down to the rifle range &amp; worked all the morning &amp;  got hooked for corporal of the Quarter Guard. Had 9 men. On for 24 hours from 2 PM to 2 PM tomorrow. Stayed awake most of the night. Got a prisoner sent in. Saw the morning Star &amp; a very pretty sunrise.</p>
