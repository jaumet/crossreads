 March, 1916 Monday 20 Day begins with the usual "scheme" starting with reveille at 5.30 am, but as soon as the men march out we were free to go back to bed if we wished. After breakfast we had a long lecture. Afternoon "Standard Tests" 
 Tuesday 21 The early part of the morning we went out on the desert "Tactical handling" Very warm &amp; quiet all day.
