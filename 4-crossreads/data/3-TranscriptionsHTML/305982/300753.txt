<p>same had quite an enjoyable evening.  usual joke etc  Parcel from Mrs Ashton  Batt sports day  musketry squad  tug of war</p>
<p>27/12/16<br/>Freezing cold last night needless to say  like wise to day  ground quite hard</p>
<p>28/12/16<br/>Raining today visited 8th G.H.Q. mess Sgt  amm park.</p>
<p>29/12/16<br/>Raining hard today mud &amp; slush all over the place.  Went to Amiens on special duty bought prisoners etc muf &amp; fur for Mother  also handkerchief  left same behind at 8th G.H.Q. on way home</p>
<p>30/12/16<br/>Light rain today mud etc  Parcel from Mrs Coombs very nice   nothing interesting doing otherwise</p>
<p>31/12/16<br/>Nothing doing today until later in the evening  New Years Eve tonight</p>
<p>1/1/17<br/>I went over to 8th G.H.Q. with B. Master Copp  what a time we had a musical evening &amp; arrived here about 11 oclock   Very cold today of course   the boys spilt some beer etc  Brigade sports  I did not go to tired</p>
