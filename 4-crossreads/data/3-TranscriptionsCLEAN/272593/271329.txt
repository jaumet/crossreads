When we reached Bresle there was a great rush for the boozer's & for a while they did a brisk trade. 17th  The Coy. were out drilling this morning & we tried a few likely men for NCO.'s & in the afternoon we went for a route march & they made it a welter coming home. Very dull overcast today & tonight it is raining steadily.
