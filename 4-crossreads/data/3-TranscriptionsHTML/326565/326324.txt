<p>Wednesday 15/3/16<br/>Resumed duties after 48 hours off duty, through inoculation.  Field work carried out during the morning with waggons.  Sgts Fry &amp; Kidson from 15th Field Ambulance, transferred to our unit from 14/3/16.  Number of the boys practicing for Quoits tournament, which is to be commenced on Friday next.</p>
<p>Thursday 16/3/16<br/>Exempted from morning parade in consequence of piquet</p>
