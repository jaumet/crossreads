16/1/19 Brussells
leafless) we came on rather ugly suburbs. Soon ran into the fine town and put up at the Palace Hotel. I thought the buildings in many cases looked as if painting and renovation were crying needs. The Rue Neuve is a crowded and fashionable street and here we located Hislop and the Brigade rest house. Plenty of theatres and cinemas. Had afternoon tea and cakes for 4. 20 francs. Promenaded after dark and located the Hotel de Ville which seen by electric light seems a splendid square bordered with old buildings all of maedival architecture. Saw a monument to Nurse Cavell and her hospital. Theatre Royal or Opera House is not imposing viewed from the outside. Shops well stocked but seemed dear
