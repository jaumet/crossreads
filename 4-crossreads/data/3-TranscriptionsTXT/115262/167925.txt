
Thursday 7 June 1917.
Fire not yet located but smoked has ceased to issue from air vents. Watch & precautions still being taken. One boat in sight at 7 a.m. but now as far as we know we are "unescorted and alone in open ocean".
Weather moderate. Did washing during morning & more in afternoon. Wrote home in aftern. 
Cpl in charge of Isolation & no fault found in any way at the inspection.
