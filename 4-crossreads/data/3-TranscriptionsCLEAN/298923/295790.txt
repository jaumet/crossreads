  
 4 May 1918 Saturday (X to X crossed out) Hope to get back across J- tonight & will not be sorry.  Cannot give details of prisoners or anything but we did fairly well, our wounded have a rough time if I get through the next 24 hours I am right. X We are rear guard & we are all near the end of our powers of [indecipherable]   A sad sight to see 2 Australian Divisions galloping away out of the mountains on a Bridle track with Turks firing all around & Aeroplanes bombing & machine gunning us X-   crossed J- at  0  3 AM & lay down minus kit or Tucker bearded & dirty but alive oh.
