<p>[1914]<br/>August 21st Passed the doctor to join the Exp. Force to help England at W. Maitland Drill Hall</p>
<p>Aug. 24 Left Maitland by Taree Mail &amp; joined the 2nd Batt at Randwick Racecourse<br/>3rd&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Left Randwick &amp; came to Kensington Racecourse.</p>
<p>Sept 25th B. Coy went on board the S S Suffolk</p>
<p>Sept 26th Arrangements made to go on board cancelled. Great disappointment</p>
<p>Sun 18th Oct. Embarked on board the S S Suffolk A23 transport about 9AM.  Steamed through the Heads about 4PM the same day. The sea was fairly rough. Food on board clean &amp; enough to eat. Hammocks comfortable to sleep in</p>
