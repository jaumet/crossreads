<p>Feb. 13 Saturday 1915<br/>Half day Parade for Regiment<br/>Major Fuller &amp; Col Cox out together in afternoon.<br/>I went to Cairo by 2. 30 PM train got tents paid balance.<br/>Met Mr. Victor Convas who took me around to see the sights. Back camp 11PM<br/>Ordered lime to be sent to Maadi Station Monday</p>
<p>Feb. 14 Sunday 1915<br/>Major Fuller &amp; other Officers went to Pyrimads<br/>Church Parade<br/>General Walker visiting<br/>Mr Victor Convas visited camp<br/>Went to church tonight with Jack Lister.</p>
<p>Feb. 15 Monday 1915<br/>Regiment foot drill all day<br/>White washing stones<br/> 6/- worth lime received from Cairo</p>
