<p>76000  - 2464 ozs</p>
<p>Becourt to Senlecques.<br/>Senlecques (3 AM) to station about km &amp; thence through Bailieul to rail head.  Marched from rail to Locre where we established 1st Div Rest Station.  Locre is behind Kemmel &amp; Messines.</p>
