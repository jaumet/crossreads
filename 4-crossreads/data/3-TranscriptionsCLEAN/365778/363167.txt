
Thursday 11/1/17
Visited Rifle Range now almost complete. Cable from Doff, also letters.
Friday 12/1/17
Visited by Colonel Smith 5th Bde, also Babe, they both were delighted - went for a spin to Amien returning at 6.30 pm.
Saturday 13/1/17
Usual Training - men shaping well - lecture at night to Officers & N.C.O's on Map Reading.
Sunday 14/1/17
Inspection of 6th Bde. No bad - visited by Col Reynolds - went to see Rifle Range. Men played football in afternoon - Wrote to Doff.
