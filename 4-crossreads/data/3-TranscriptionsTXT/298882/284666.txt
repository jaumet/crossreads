 Belgium 20.9.17 
 The 1st and 2nd Aust. Div. attacked this morning at 5.30 am.  Our brigade ordered to be in readiness to move but 2 hours notice. at 12 pm. word was received by aeroplane that attack progressing favourably. 1st and 2nd  Div  objectives gained with slight losses.  At 2 p.m. word received 1st and  D  2nd Div's. proceeding towards 3rd objective. 
 We are still standing to expecting to move.
