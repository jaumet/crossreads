  
 I reached Vaire with the Staff Capt. and he allotted the eastern portion of the town to my Bn.  to  as billeting area.  I accordingly began the task of allotting the different habitable  d  houses to the companies.  In this I was ably assisted by Lieut. Mountford of 'B' Company.  We then waited along the tow path of the canal for the incoming tired battalion.
