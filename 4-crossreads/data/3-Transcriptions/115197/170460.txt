<p><u>Jan. 29th</u></p>
<p>Leave Jeumont at 9 a.m. with Rupert Brown for London.</p>
<p>First stage of journey in motor-lorry to Charleroi - where we spend afternoon in looking round town before entraining in the Evening.</p>
<p>Meet Sid Alder, Capt. Potts etc.&nbsp; Entrain in Calais bound troop train at 10 p.m. and leave Charleroi at midnight.</p>
