<p>over last May I think he said.  At the C.C.S. the other day I met an old school fellow there, Morgan his name &amp; he comes from Epping, he is in the 1st Batt.  Crane was telling me Harz of Burwood St. James, you doubtless know of them, is in his Battery.  I did not see him but will look them up sometime when we get out.  I think I told you Hosier was wounded last time in &amp; Huxley took ill suddenly while we were out &amp; was taken away to hospital.  I have heard from neither since.</p>
