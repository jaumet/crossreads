  
 Sunday 23rd Jan. Struck tents afternoon, had to sleep out all night that that transports may be loaded, were to leave 2-30 a.m. but postponed till 9-30 a.m. 
 Monday 24th entrained 9-30 a.m. steamed out nearer 10-30 in open trucks reaching Ismalia 11-30;  later marched through same along canal, Scenery just charming with its Canal & tropical growth.  Had midday meal at flood gates opening into Suez Canal & after another hour's tramp reached Punts where we all put hands  [See Page 30 for continuation of this entry.]
