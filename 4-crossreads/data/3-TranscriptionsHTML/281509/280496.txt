<p>1916 September 30<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Usual dittoes.  Half holiday. Nothing striking of note to chronicle the past two days.  Reveille, breakfast, duties, read papers &amp; book. Dinner duties, tea &amp; wander walk. etc.  Frequent adjournments to Y.M.CA. for coffee chockolats.</p>
