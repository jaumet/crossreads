  
 September 1918. Thursday 5 Attached to 15th A.1. Bdge. Bty position. Pulled into a position ahead of Birleau to carry on with sniping work. On road last night we managed to collar 3 miles of metallic wire, a very good find.  Salvaged a few miles of wire with Smithy. Major returned to Bty position with 3 L'aison spooks, - not in an excellent humor. A heavy thunderstorm came across telephone put a slightly damp home to rest in  Walked to 5th Div Pioneer Camp and saw numerous fellows I knew. Met Eliza Thoresby and Bill Grant, old Bridg Train fellows. Prepared to leave in the morning.
