Turkish cigarettes writing notes. Day night following quietest yet known.
19th Tuesday
Another ?Parapet scene? exchange as on 18th, also seen on Courtneys Post till Germans opened fire with artillery Rocket Bombs of which they placed 17 along their lines with much apparent effect, remainder of day fairly quiet. Evening at Cards.
20th Wednesday
Business as usual, nothing out of ordinary.
21st Thursday
Fair amt. of Artillery fire from both Navy Batteries. Cards.
