Oct 17 My Blighty leave pass arrived on the 17th October but I could not go owing to the boils. Bill Collins came over to see me one afternoon. He had been presented with a Military Medal for good work in the line at Foucaucourt. He was I/C of us at that time &ndash; George Ogle being away. We were all pleased except George, who was rather jealous. Bill said that we all really had a share in it as it was on account of the work of our section generally that it was give. Oct 21 On 21 October I got my clearance from the AMC, though far from well and saw Sgt Collis about my leave pass.
