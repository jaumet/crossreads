 May 1916 Egypt 1st   Suez was sighted about 7.45 PM &amp; we arrived in Port Ismail (adjoining Pt. Suez) at 8.15 PM &amp; anchored in the bay. 2nd   We expected to go ashore today but received orders during the day that we are to dissembark to-morrow &amp; dry rations have been issued. 3rd   The ship drew alongside at 7.30 A.M. and the first train load (of troops) left the ship at about 9 A.M. &amp; entrained, but the train did not move off until 11 AM., with the 1st 3rd &amp; Sigs. of the Engrs. in charge of Lt. Nangle.  I entrained with my reinforcement at 11.30 A.M. &amp; moved off at 12.30 PM en route for Tel-el-Kibir, where we arrived
