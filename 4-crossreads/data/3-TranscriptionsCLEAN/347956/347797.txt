  
 Nov  29 - 30 
 come on the bridge;  A fairly stiff breeze has been blowing all day & it is rather on the cool side to what we have been having, in fact it could not have been ordered a better day;  slept out last night  
 Mon 30th Slept out last night as usual & it was chilly enough with both blankets wrapped around us.  were cleaning the steel parts of the saddlery all day & they were as rusty as it was possible to get them.  Am on the bridge from 4 till 6 P.M. with Howard.  we have put in a good part of the day finding lost clothing.  I believe we get to Suez to-morrow morning.  Passed the Hampshire (cruiser) (just as I went on duty) & was signalling to her.  The boxing championship not contested
