<p>8th June<br/>Went to Amienes where we spent an enjoyable day.  Saw the cathedral &ndash; now the most beautiful in architecture (Gothic) in the world with its many towers &amp; steeples, its pictures, paintings of the old masters &amp; its time honours slabs of marble.  It is now all sandbaged up round the entrance in case of attack from aerial craft.</p>
<p>Truly Amiens is picture of war.  Trades pursue their usual crafts, girls &amp; women fill the offices of the men who are fighting for honour &amp; home.</p>
<p>Few places are damaged but devastation is a mild effect of war, nearly every woman is clothed in deep mourning, few indeed they are who</p>
