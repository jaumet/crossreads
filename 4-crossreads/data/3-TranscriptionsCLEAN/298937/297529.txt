If the 3rd Field Amb. are again sent on active service I shall be faced with a  dillem  dilemma, for it will be hard to stay behind & leave the boys to go ahead & face the old dangers. On the other hand it would not be right for me to go on desert fighting with my eyes as they are.  In addition I might be altering the whole course of my life for the better, by staying here.
