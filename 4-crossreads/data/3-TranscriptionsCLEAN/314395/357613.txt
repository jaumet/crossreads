 Reckoning up a few nights ago we found that 21 original 2nd Bty. men have obtained commissions and that 11 have been awarded honours. 
 News tonight of a good advance at Messines by Australians and N. Zealanders, which position is of great importance to us.  I hope we can hold it. 
 The Russ. seems to have quitted altogether and large bodies of troops (german) are coming over to the western front to annoy us.  We are expecting to take part very soon in a big stunt in the North. 
 Love to yourself an Delphine when you see her, Your affect. Son George.
