<p>While waiting for the second trip I walked into Poperinge to see if I could get a paper, &amp; who do you think I saw as I was coming back, you'll never guess so I will have to tell you, why Bob Rex of Ticehurst, I would never have known him, he stopped me &amp; asked me if I knew the way to the "Divisional Baths" &amp; I told him, he then to my surprise asked me if I was not one of the Barwicks from Campania I was astonished, he then told me who he was, fancy meeting here in one of these little Belgian streets, he is in the Artillery</p>
