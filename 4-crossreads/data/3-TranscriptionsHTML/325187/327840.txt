<p>[Tuesday 4 April 1916]</p>
<p>Sandflies have bitten one Scotch Officer so badly he has gone to Hospital.<br/>Camels even are feeling the heat now poor brutes get hell- if they lag behind, the natives pull out a knife &amp; cut a hole in <strike>their nostril</strike> Camels Nose &amp; tie a piece of leather or hide on &amp; drag 'em along.</p>
