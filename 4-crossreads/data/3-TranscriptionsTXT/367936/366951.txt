
Tuesday 21 March 1916Drove Father into Mudgee to the Land board and then went up to Show ground.  We won, Fat whrs., Fat ewes, Fat lambs, and Corriedale ram & ewe & 2 Corriedale fleeces.  Drove out with Dick & brought 2 draught horses.
Wednesday 22 March 1916Father in Mudgee at Show.  Dick and I mustered up horses in Long pdk. and took them to Wonga.After dinner nailed up cases of honey, shod Galina on front feet, then rode round looking for horses but did not find them.
