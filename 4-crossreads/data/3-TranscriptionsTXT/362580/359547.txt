 3rd A.G.H. B.E.F. France. 9.10.17. 
 My dearest Belle, 
 Just received a lovely Aust. mail &amp; your nice long newsy letter with Equity Transfer enclosed.  I have signed where the pencil mark was so hope it is right. 
 Am very pleased you bought the shares &amp; that my banking account is quite alright.
