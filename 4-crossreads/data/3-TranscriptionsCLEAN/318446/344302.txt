
28thI think I shall have to put down "situation unchanged" as in the official reports as nothing seems to happen much, we live in a world of trivialities.Our positions have chrystalised into my friend being stable boy & myself cook at which I am beginning to fancy myself not a little.  I must spend quite 10 minutes of or more every day working out the menu for the day as it is quite a business to devise different dishes from much the same materials.  Also we are somewhat lacking in the matter of cooking utensils as they consist of one mess tin & 4 billies (ex fruit tins).I went so far as to try my hand at pastry the other day (I have rigged up an indifferent oven from a big biscuit tin.I decided to make a jam tart.
