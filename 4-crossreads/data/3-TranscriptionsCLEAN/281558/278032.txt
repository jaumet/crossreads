Wednesday Sept 5th
Monasterie : reveille 6.30 am : good day again : boys on a "stunt" : we had an A.M.C. parade & lecture : wrote in afternoon : went with Morrie to Arques after tea : met Jim & Bert & gave them the Mercury's : had a feed & then home : more 'planes about & heavy gunfire from the line way
Thursday Sept 6th
Monasterie : stormy : reveille 6 am : boys on parade Morrie & self left off to fill water cart : boys on a "stunt" after dinner : very heavy gunfire from up the line way so there must be some thing doing : read after tea
