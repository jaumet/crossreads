<p>Plenty of Bully &amp; Biscuits some jam &amp; cheese. Not a cold night had a fair sleep The march this morning took it out of most of us it was quite hot &amp; the road was bad<br/>Traveled slowly all day &amp; left the train / 4 Oclock at Castre &amp; marched about 1 &frac12; miles to camp getting there at dusk<br/>Put in tents &amp; given an extra blanket. It is flat uninteresting country saw a few shell holes at Hasellboch a fair sized place about 6 miles back. The only real signs</p>
