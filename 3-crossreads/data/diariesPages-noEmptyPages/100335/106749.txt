After the Gangway was lowered the Town Picket went ashore followed by the Troops. The Boys caught the Authorities napping as our hotels were opened and soon were filled and very busy. Two of us Myself and chum made for an Hotel and had a cool Glass of Ale and then caught a Tram coming out, as they would be quickly filled at the Terminus, the Tram was almost filled before it reached its destination, and soon started again for Town, the first Car was packed and on arrival the flinfish's with their Rickshaw's soon heard that we had returned and started in large numbers to make for the Boat, our Car passed them going full steam for a Fare, they looked a sight with their little lights and ringing their little Bell, we arrived at the Post Office at 8 Pm and caught a Tram for a run along Musgrave Marriott Roads up along the Hill for a View by Night of Durban.
