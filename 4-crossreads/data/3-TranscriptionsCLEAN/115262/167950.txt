
Monday 2 July 1917.
Reveille 4 a.m. Parade as usual. I am Mess Orderly today. Major Whitford gave us an address on 1st and 2nd battles of Gaza. I took notes & a copy of his sketches. He has just arrived from the front & is taking up position of Adjutant of A.C.B.F.A.
A trainload of Turkish prisoners came through here from Gaza this morning. 3 hospial trains came through yesterday & today from the front.
