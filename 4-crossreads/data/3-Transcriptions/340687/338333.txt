<p>January, 1917. Monday 29<br/>Bill Thomas woke me at 4.10 having slept in so I arose and entered diary, had a shave and cleaned the hut up and then woke the cooks up which took some time. Finished cleaning hut in morning and chopped some wood Large consignment of comforts came in afternoon and besides these I procured a new tunic a little small &amp; took a parcel to Ingrams who comes over to-night while I get mobile. Breakfast at 7 Fall in at 8 &amp; move at 9. Drill order parade at 9.30 in morning. Day fine and not quite so cold. Moving to Bazentin-le-Petit.</p>
