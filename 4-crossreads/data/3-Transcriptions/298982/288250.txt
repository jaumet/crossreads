<p>Turks having gained by force of numbers.</p>
<p>13th<br/>We moved up a gully about 8 or 9 am &amp; camped in old dug outs which we improved The Sharp shooters made it pretty hot down the main vally about 10 &amp; got 6 of B Squadron. The bullets sing continually I went for water for a dozen or so &amp; It was pretty lively about then. Don't like the Shrapnel or Mixed</p>
