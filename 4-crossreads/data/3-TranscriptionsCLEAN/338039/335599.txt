  
 Apr. 21st. "Good Friday"- No buns.  Plenty of rain- Night duty in trench- 5 p. m- 9 a. m sleep in dug out. Apr. 22nd. Easter Saturday- quiet day- letters- Night duty at "College Green." Apr. 23rd. Easter Sunday proves a bad day for our boys- 250 shells from Fritz in 40 minutes*- Four killed & many wound pass through us- Indescribable wounds- Fortitude of our Boys- Aircraft
