<p>August 1917<br/>29th   which are fitted out with bunks &amp; very comfortable.<br/>31st   We started work yesterday &amp; to-day have almost compleded a 60' Inglis Tubular Bridge which we began this morning.<br/>The company has been divided into 8 parts, which have been lettered from A to H    (I have got G)</p>
<p>September 1917<br/>1st   We finished the Inglis Bridge this morning &amp; started on a heavy girder bridge.   Fritz raided the town early to-night but was driven off without doing any damage, by the anti-aircraft guns.<br/>3rd   One of our men went to Bethune &amp; got into the hands of the military police last night.</p>
