<p>9th M.G. Coy No 19<br/>Intelligence Report from 6 am 18th to 6 am 19th:-<br/><u>Right</u> <u>Sector</u>:- No 1 fired from B. Post and traversed enemy trench and wire. Fired on enemy M.G. No 5 fired from I 10 d 42/75 on LEVEL CROSSING- I 12 d 04/64 from 7-10 pm. No 6 fired from I 11 c 05/69 on enemy front line and wire, also M.G. which was [treated?] by its flash. Result [uncertain?]. All these front line guns had to cease fire at 10.30 owing to patrols being sent out.<br/>An enemy M.G. was located in this Sector on night of 18th one of our guns fired on it with result that enemy gun did not fire from that position again.<br/>Left Sector:- No 3 fired on enemy front line and trenches at intervals. No 8 fired- indirect- on I 6 b 8/9 roads &amp; trenches round LE TEMPLE, from 7-10 pm. No retaliation  Enemy rifle and M.G. fire very consistent up to about 10 pm</p>
<p>B J Carey Capt<br/>19.12.16</p>
