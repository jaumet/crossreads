I am writing Mother by this mail also, I have instructed her to draw any amount of my cash & buy me some winter underwear "Dr Jaegers" heavyweight 2 singlets & 2 underdrawers a heavy cap comforter mittens & cardigan vest & gloves, give Mother a hand Lizzie dear.
