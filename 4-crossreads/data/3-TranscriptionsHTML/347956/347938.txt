<p>will find it a good deal warmer in the tropics than we did; &amp; if they are landed in Egypt they have my pity, as it will be no joke training here in a month or two, it is quite unpleasant enough contending with the dust here now &amp; it would be horrible in the summer time;  England will have to keep a large army here, as the Sultan is far from popular with the natives &amp; they would rise &amp; fight with the Turks if they get half a chance;<br/>Well Mother I will have to say Good-bye again for the present, with heaps of love to all at home from your loving son<br/>Fred</p>
