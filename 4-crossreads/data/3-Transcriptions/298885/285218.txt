<p>Apr 21st &ndash; 18</p>
<p>Great excitement has held sway all day caused by numerous air duels between Fritz's planes and ours which have come out in great numbers to make the best of a fine day.</p>
<p>Shells also continue to pick out cottages &amp; the villiage church.</p>
<p>May 3th &ndash; 18<br/>Today has brought me the unpleasant news of my brother Stan being wounded yesterday</p>
