<p>A few nights after this, we get our first "gas alarm", word was flashed through from the front line that Fritz was putting gas over, but after standing to for thirty minutes or more &amp; no appearance of any gas we roll back to bed, the gas clouds not reaching as far as our wagon lines. we had several gas alarms after this but very rarely any of us used to take much notice of it, might just see our respirators were handy.</p>
