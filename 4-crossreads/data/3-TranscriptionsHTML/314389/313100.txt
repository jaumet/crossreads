<p>Thursday, May 4th:<br/>Rise about 4.45 a.m.  Ride out on wagon to job.  Return to Camp about 5.30.  1st Battalion shelled in their billets to-day &ndash; a few casualties.  Fine day.  Retire about 8.30 p.m.</p>
<p>May 5th:<br/>Rise 4.30.  Ride out to work.  Fine day.  Return to Camp about 5 p.m.  Gas alarm at night.</p>
<p>May 6th:<br/>Rise 5 a.m.  Ride to work.  Work all day.  Return 6.30 p.m.</p>
<p>Sunday, May 7th:<br/>Rise 5.30 a.m.  Work all day. Billet in bombing school quarters.  Retire about 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Monday, May 8th:<br/>Rise 6.30.  Work all day up till 11 at night.  Cold and drizzling rain.  Retire about 11.30 p.m.</p>
<p>Tuesday, May 9th:<br/>Rise 7 a.m.  Work all day till 4 p.m.  Rainy day.  Retire at 8 p.m.</p>
<p>Wednesday, May 10th:<br/>Fine day.  Work all day building new huts.  Return to camp 4.30 p.m.  Retire 8 p.m.  Write to Mother.</p>
