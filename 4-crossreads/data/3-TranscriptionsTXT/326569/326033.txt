 Friday, 30/3/17 Light fall of snow during day. 
 Saturday, 31/3/17 Very cold, only &frac12; day drill.  Did some sewing in afternoon. 
 Sunday April 1st, 1917 Still very cold &ndash; no church parade for contacts &ndash; sent washing down to farm at back of hut.  About 3 p.m. went with Sowter over hill to south of camp.  Small flakes of snow falling on top of hill.  Splendid view of camps &amp; scenery right along the valley, including some villages. 
 Monday, 2/4/17 About 6 inches snow fell during night.  Came through window thus leaving good coating snow on my blankets.  Drilled in hut during morning &amp; route
