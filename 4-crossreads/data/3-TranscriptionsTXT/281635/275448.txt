 21/2/18 Went to pictures with Murray &amp; then to Canteen &amp; had supper of Tinned rabbit. Temperature much milder. 22/2/18 Issued with new boots &amp; tunic. Wrote home. 23/2/18 Met palmer of 6th Btn. He was wounded on Oct 31st at Ypres &amp; arrived yesterday from England on his way back to the line. Told me Burrell &amp; Snowy James both going to Australia. 24/2/18 Sunday Palmer went up to line this morning, also Murray. 25/2/18 Recd letters from Uncle Fred &amp; Mr Southwell. Latter says he will probably be made a chaplain. 26/2/18 Met 2 Railway unit chaps who left Melbourne on Nov 7th.
