The Wages however wher not collected, but got taken out of Entrance Money.<br/><u>Educationes</u>  The Military presented the Camp in August 1916 with 3 marque tents in wich to hold "shools".  These wher ocupied at every hour of the day &amp; Evening by the various Classes.  Their wher Classes to enable Pris. to learn allmost any Language gratis.  Ingeneering Classes, Prospecting, Short Hand Writing in fact allmost anything wich could assist a Men in future Life.  These "Shools Cursus" wher all gratis, the teachers having given their services free.<br/><u>Cantine</u>  The Camp only boasted of one Shop.  The Canteen.  This shop wher run entirely by the Military &amp; untill than nothing of the "proffits" made their off ever wher noticeable to come the Camp to credit.
