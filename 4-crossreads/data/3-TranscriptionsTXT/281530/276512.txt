We turned out through the village down a side road and found that we were well ahead of the Battalion headquarters of the firing line and had to go back some distance to locate them in deep dugouts dug into the white chalk and liberally camouflaged. Our destination lay in the support positions and we located them similarly in a deep place well behind.
