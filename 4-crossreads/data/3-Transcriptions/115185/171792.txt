<p>5 &ndash; the end. I suppose the mere fact of being here as they are, is a lifelong advantage to them &ndash; if they don't do any work. They are most sweet and affectionate and awfully keen about my work. I tried to get J attached to us &ndash; but we are <u>not</u> taking any one in the unit but those who came with us. Matron was quite keen on a few probationers coming, like Jocelyn but the war office wont permit. So that's all about it. The doctors wives cannot come even</p>
