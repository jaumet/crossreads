  
 9/7/16. Paraded in full marching order at 8 A.M. Marched all the morning covered some 9 miles. We have halted at a billet near Merrio and we march on again tomorrow. Whither we are bound I know not. 10/7/16. Did not get our blankets last night, the transport that is carrying them has moved on; but it was warm enough in the straw of the barn. Rested here all day waiting for the order to move. It will come some time tonight I suppose. Marched off at 11.P.M.  11/7/16. Marched 5 miles last night, to the railway at Bailleul.
