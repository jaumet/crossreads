<p>I am very pleased to hear that you get about such a lot.  I will tell you a little about our Island Trip.  We had a rather tedious journey over &amp; back so I'll leave that part out of it.  We just missed motors, &amp; trains &amp; boats etc. &amp; we were unable to get any meals on the way either so we were quite fed up when we reached our destination.  But it was a nice journey in the Tynwald &amp; Douglas was crowded when we arrived.  The Boats were packed too.
