<p>27 September 1918 Friday<br/>Moved <u>North</u> to get touch with enemy who have gone that way-  Guns are now up with us- also a motley crowd of mounted Arabs who call themselves Hedjaz Cavalry- rather picturesque in their flowing robes &amp; Turbans- 2 Horses died from eating "Oleander" which grows in thick scrub along Wadi-  Heavy dew last night, we are 2500 feet <u>above</u> sea level again &amp; in a country where every one is armed &amp; quite ready to kill his neighbour for a bit of tucker-  400 more prisoners &amp; 4 machine guns<br/>Zerka is full of wounded Turks- wide Pilgrim Road to Mecca.</p>
