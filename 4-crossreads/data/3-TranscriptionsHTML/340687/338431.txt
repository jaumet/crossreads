<p>May, 1917. Monday 7<br/>Day cloudy but pleasant. Up at 9.30 and at 11 a lorry came with men from 5th Army RE workshops and took away my plant by 1. Saw Savage and he may close too. Have to wait till to-morrow for everything to go and then return to 148 Coy in evening to await new job. Went to tea at Savages and saw pictures in evening Home at 8 and wrote letters. Very good pictures. Send Kit by ration wagon to-morrow</p>
