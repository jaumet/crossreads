 August&nbsp;&nbsp; 1918 
 25 SUN&nbsp; Fine day. Warm. Posted letters 
 to Amy (&amp; Amy Mc) Julie &amp; Maggie 
 &amp; Tom. U.S.A.&nbsp; Very quiet 
 all day. Balloons going 
 forward all the time 
 Heard Bapaume had fallen. 
 Successes all along the line. 
 Writing at night. rain. Quiet 
 26 MON&nbsp; Posted letters to Gladys. 
 Zelia. Else P.. Mrs W.T.B. Fine day 
 Cool &amp; windy. big movement 
 of troops &amp; artillery. ready 
 for further operations. Quiet 
 Rec. letter from Celie H. Gilligan ret to 
 Battery 
 27 TUES&nbsp; Fine day cool, rain in evening 
 Posted letter to Celie. rec photo from Cic 
 Amm. going up &amp; troops. very quiet 
 day &amp; night. Broadhurst &amp; Thomas 
 went to Australia. Reading 
 28 WED&nbsp; Dull day &amp; cool. raining 
 on &amp; off. moved off 3 PM to Proyart 
 &amp; then to Fontaine les Cappy. Guns 
 moving forward the whole time. 
 Shelling roads at night. 1. horse kld 
 3. wdd. very narrow escape. Bivouacked 
 finished 12.PM. Quiet
