<p>May 1918<br/>Thursday 9</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daours<br/>Thank goodness the old juice has stopped at last.<br/>At pm. Had another run out to Bonnay.<br/>Weather still uncertain.<br/>Fritz shelling slightly <br/>New horse now<br/>Met Charlie Frey and had a little pow pow .<br/>Bill boozed badly , visited Madame.<br/>Boys very cheerful under influence of rum issue.</p>
