No work after 12-30 pm    & Legge & I, take take a great tram ride right out of the town for about  three  seven miles, right along the Seine, viewing Bonsecours en route, to St Etienne  a nice walk to the river & take a boat across to a pretty little village resting on the banks of the old river Seine    & the foot of some hills, St Adrien. Here we have some tea in a garden cafe on the river bank & inspect a very ancient little church, built in 13th Century & cut right into the rock. Most interesting.
