Battalion, they killed 40 odd Germans took prisoners 26 men &amp; 2 officers &amp; brought back 2 machine guns &amp; a trench mortar, that will take some beating Our Battalion received to-day a congratulatory letter from the 2nd Army Corp on the successful raid we made though we did not get so many prisoners as some of the others, we got a lot of valuable information.
