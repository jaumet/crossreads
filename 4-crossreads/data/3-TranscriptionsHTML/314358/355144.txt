<p>5<br/>This fall of snow is fine<br/>There is as much as four inches of the magic whiteness everywhere over the ground, and of course deep drifts of it in places.<br/>The khaki boys are making great ugly snow men.<br/>Oh dear, Oh dear, how ugly and vile is everything which the masses do.<br/>The horrible and base the ugly, the crude and the sordid.<br/>All this is theirs to be thoroughly enjoyed.<br/>The man how drew our bones in the old stone age was an artist nobler and greater and cleaner than any one of the masses of today.</p>
