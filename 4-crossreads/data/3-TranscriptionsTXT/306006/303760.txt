 3-3-17 at 8 oclock the guard from Zeitoun arrived to take us back, we caught a tram to the Railway Station &amp; then a train to Zeitoun where we arrived at 9..40 &amp; told by the Orderly Officer that we were under open arrest &amp; would be for the Orderly Room at 9 oclock Monday morning; We 2 met Satchell in "Clink" he was put in the night for being drunk, he serves in the Sergeants Mess at Camp so we were well represented; Harris &amp; I had rather an amusing day but cannot say we enjoyed ourselves as much as we expected to; Kingsford went out to the Hospital afterwards &amp; saw Ossie Hayes &amp; reported
