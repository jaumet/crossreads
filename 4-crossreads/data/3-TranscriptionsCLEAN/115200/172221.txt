  
 8  Dead mans ridge 
 Catching hold of one of my Dead comrades feet. This made me as if I wanted to vomit. anyhow we reached the top of the Cliff and we started to Dig ourselves in  I had to remain on top of the trench all night and the Bullets were coming very fast. how I Escaped getting hit I Don't Know. in the morning 3 rd  of may I could see The Enemy coming up to attack us  Some had Indian turbans round their Head and some had Big Bushes on their Back so as to try and make us think were trees.
