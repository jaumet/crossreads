<p>Novembre 6<br/>Went to Codford to look for Jack but did not find him as he had been shifted to Tidworth Training School a couple of days ago.</p>
<p>Novembre 7<br/>Went up to London with Mrs Smith, met Mr Smith &amp; we all had lunch together. We all went to the "Empire" Stratford at night &amp; saw a fairly good show.</p>
