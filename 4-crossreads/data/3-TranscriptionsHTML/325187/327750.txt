<p>[Wednesday  January 5 1916]</p>
<p>Regimental Drill mounted &amp; in afternoon went No.1 Hospital to get a tooth fixed up-  There I met Riley from Deniliquin.<br/>Did I tell you Norman (G[indecipherable] Robertson &amp; his wife is here as Dr in the 1st L.H. Field Ambulance<br/>Cold today &amp; raining part of the 2" we get in the year.<br/> We will not be leaving here for a week yet so post this with love</p>
