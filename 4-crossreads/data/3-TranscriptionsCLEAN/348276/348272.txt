
War Diary1916  13th Junedamage - 3 casualties on Canal - pieces picked up point to bombs being converted "Mills"
14th JuneConvoy of 650 camels with half Sqadn. escort arrived in camp at 0300, remainder of column between 1600 and 1830 - men tired but both men and horses fit - operations a complete success
