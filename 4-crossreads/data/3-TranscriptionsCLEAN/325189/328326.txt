 24th February Tuesday 1915 
 The 'Chilka'  & 'Machubra'  sailed about 1.45 a.m. and the 'Hessian'  at 10.15 a.m.  At breakfast we were told that we were to go ashore on a route march.  By 10.0 a.m. we had all boarded a tug and 4 lighters and were towed to the landing stage.  The march was about 41/2 miles and it was quite far enough.
