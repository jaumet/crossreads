<p>for all directions, but first move was for Dinner &amp; after a little while our party which I was in charge off came across a Dining Room &amp; soon <strike>we</strike> the under man was satisfied. Afterwards an inspection of the Main Street was made, &amp; I must give Credit for the way the buildings are laid out some of them are very high 4 &amp; 5 storeys. The Railway Station &amp; Offices are very imposing structures built of Stone, &amp; is in the Main St. The Post Office &amp; the Standard bank of South Africa is likewise both Splendid buildings. Gradually our party got separated &amp; only two us were left, &amp; as we were advised to take a Train ride <strike>ride</strike> to Camp Bay which is situated behind Table Mountain we soon was aboard a Car, &amp; the Trip was an eyeopener to both of us.
