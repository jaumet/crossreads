<p>so will be very careful. Both Dorothy &amp; I are going to save as much as we can to spend afterwards. <br/>I wonder where we are off to &amp; when I shall get another letter from home. Give my love to all friends. Tell dear Mrs Barrie that I shall write her a long letter &amp; send it as soon as I can. Not just now, must hurry or will not get this home. Fondest love &amp; kisses &amp; hope you are all well &amp; happy<br/>From<br/>Yours lovingly<br/>Edith</p>
