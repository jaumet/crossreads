<p>[Tuesday 29 August 1916]</p>
<p>Sister Calvert says I look 10 years older &amp; she would not know- we made no such ungallant remark to her.<br/>"Gyppy Tummy" last night, I hope for the last time &ndash; sort of Colick caused by sand &amp; Bully beef etc:- I suppose- but that is a month ago nearly-<br/>Lovely weather- all [indecipherable] the swell.<br/>Just heard by Wireless that ROUMANIA has joined us this will help immensely</p>
