<p>Since leaving home (eggs, porridge, toast, tinned fruit, rissoles, puddings etc.)<br/>May 25th<br/>More cribbage &ndash; 10 letters, good news. Still well, plenty shells.<br/>May 26th<br/>A few more cases than usual, cleaning up day. Our Gooseberry picking expedition brought to a hurried close by shell landing about 15-20 yds. away.</p>
