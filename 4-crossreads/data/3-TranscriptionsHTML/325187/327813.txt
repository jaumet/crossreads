<p>[Ash Wednesday 8 March 1916]</p>
<p>This Canal must not be confused with SUEZ<br/>We were startled today by seeing the body of a dead Soldier floating down the Canal-   He turned out to be a Queenslander 2nd L.H. drowned 3 days before higher up the Canal.<br/>A sad end for a fighting man &ndash; it appears his [horse?] fell off one of the Ferries &amp; being weighted with Rifle &amp; ammunition had no chance-<br/>We wired for Motor Ambulance &amp; sent him to Minna for decent burial.<br/>Poor old Sef Bey's brother died last night- much wailing in the village just like our Aboriginals.  He was attended by native Doctor who told us this morning "That he died <u>accidental</u></p>
