To-day Len has been gazetted full Corporal & he should have had it long ago but for pure accident
I have withdrawn my Platoon from the firing line & am now in "supports  We are still dropping bombs & shells on old Fritz & rolling his barb wire up lovely, they are making a good job of it.
I done a bit of firing last night with a Lewis gun they are little bonzer's & very light & deadly.
Got a quantity of stuff up from the Bde canteen this morning for we run one almost in the firing line & it is a great success
