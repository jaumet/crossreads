<p>Wednesday 10th. Put on the Water fatigue which is rather easy so had a fairly easy time writing and doing some photos. A football match was held during the afternoon. N.C.O.'s &amp; officers against the privates. The privates won.</p>
<p>Thursday 11th. The great event happened we were reviewed by Lord Kitchener. We were formed up with the second brigade &amp; a few others &amp; he walked slowly down the line, towering above all his companions. In appearance he has altered somewhat being older &amp; not so virile, his face having a bloated appearance. We then formed up into a hollow square &amp; he said a few words to us Australians, I am here to thank you today in the name of the King, the People, the Army, &amp; myself for the good work you have performed. It has been excellent &amp; although has not had the required effect, it has undoubtedly prevented the Turkish Army from helping our enemies elsewhere.<br/>The speech was thus very moderated &amp; he described our work as excellent which describes things much better</p>
