<p>Monday 4th March<br/>On the job for about 3 hours a dark wet night</p>
<p>Tuesday 5th March<br/>Weather has cleared up again All went out this evening &amp; got back about 10</p>
<p>Wednesday 6th March<br/>Went to the Baths this morning Am now waiting until 9 PM as we are to go out then as a second shift The time was changed for going</p>
