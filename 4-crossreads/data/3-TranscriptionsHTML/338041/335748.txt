<p>-Old friends Cecil Moore &amp; McDade &ndash; Letters despatched Home &ndash; Still on duty in Tent. 1. &ndash; "Aye indeed and 'tis a bloody business &ndash; (At least Shakespeare would say so.</p>
<p>Nov. 5th<br/>Transferred from No.1 to No. 3 operating Tent (Night Duties) &ndash; Under our own M.O's. Very much regret leaving Capt. Brown who on my leaving makes a most kind proposal on my behalf &ndash; Afternoon free</p>
<p>Nov. 6th.<br/>Night Duty under Major Brendan &amp; Capt Hamilton &ndash; Zeppelin raid 3 miles from camp. Loss of Ammunition. rain and Death of over 200 men in C.C.S. Caused by Zeps bombs &ndash; Busy night.</p>
<p>Nov. 7th<br/>Alick Barr &ndash; Seventh Brigade stunt &ndash; Death of Ray Walters &amp; other of Sixth F. Amb.</p>
