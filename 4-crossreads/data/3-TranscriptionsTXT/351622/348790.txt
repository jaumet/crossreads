 3 March 1916 Fri Murphy at last owns up that that he is full of the whole affair reciting pieces from [indecipherable]. 
 A very funny think happened this afternoon. A squatter Lance Corporal wandered into our camp with a throat as dry as a camel &amp; asked for a Stimulant of some sort. The Q.M. sent him to me who came &amp; saluted me thinking me an officer &amp; didn't he put the kid in for a whiskey [text continues at top of page]
