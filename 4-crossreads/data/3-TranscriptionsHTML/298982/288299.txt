<p>&amp; kept going up &amp; down till about 3 pm. NZs walked over us &amp; then we went right to top Stayed there about an hour then came down again &amp; settled into dug outs again. The messing abt cost 4 NZs their lives at one corner.<br/>[Note in margin &ndash; Bailey killed.]</p>
<p>Saw the Aeroplane in evening drop four indications over Turks lines. Picquet all night. McDolly kept in her posse all night Shes a great officer.</p>
<p>Sun 30th Quiet morn about 1 pm we all stood to arms a fairly heavy attack on our front shrapnel, shells &amp; machine guns which was kept</p>
