  Oct 3rd '17 :  The Brigade moved in to a camp on the beach near Rafa, our horses having been declared unfit for the Front line.  Rumours are current to the effect that the Brigade is to be sent to Upper Egypt, & I am inclined to believe that there is a big chance of us going there.
