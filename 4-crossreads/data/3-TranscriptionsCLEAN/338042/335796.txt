  
 Jan.30th. British Air Raids on German Towns - During the day I meet Madame's daughter (age, about 45) and other of Madame's friends - Political Unrest in Germany & Austria. 
 Jan 31st. Change of Programme - "Fuzzy Wuzzy" - strikes in Germany- War rationing in England - In a leisure half hour I run across a gold mine in the shape of a shop containing Shakespeare - Homer. Dickens - Tennyson & Kipling etc - Weather colder.
