
Sat. 14th. Oct: -On the march & went around Poperinghe &  Hopoutre railway siding, along main road & billeted for night at a farm house outside of Godeswaervelde. (I hope I've got the name O.K)
Sun 15th Oct: -Marched through Steenvoorde & Abeele, skirted around Cassel (pretty place on hill-top with its large Monastery) went through Bavinhove, Zuytpeene &  billeted at Noordpeene.
Mon Oct. 16th. -Marched through the village of Lederzeele &  Les Cinq Rues; the town of Watten (a lovely view from the high range we came down as it lay on the banks of the river down below &  hundreds of miles of fertile country open to our view) through a couple of small villages to Le Gandspette &  we are billeted in large barns. Think we are here for a month's drill & reorganisation.Getting very cold of a night.
Thurs. Oct. 19th. 1916: -Voting on the Conscription Referendum today. If the voting depends on the troops here it won't be carried.
