<p>9th M.G. Coy No 13<br/>Intelligence Report from 6 am 12th to 6 am 13th:-<br/>Right Sector:- No 5 gun fired &ndash; indirect &ndash;on I 17 b 3/1 to I 17 b 9/6 road behind enemy supports from 6 &ndash; 7.45 pm.<br/>No 6 fired on Enemy defensive position I 18 b 3/3 from 10 pm to 10.45  Stopped by "Stand to" order.<br/>Four Minnewerfers were dropped near No 5 position at noon.<br/>Left Sector:- No 7 (S.P.X) fired I 6 b 8/9 road &amp; trenches round LE TEMPLE, from 6 &ndash; 11.30 pm<br/>No 8 fired on I 12 a 4/9 trenches &amp; buildings round RETALIATION FARM. from 6.30 &ndash; 10.45. Result unobserved. Light wind (westerly)  Light dull - snowing during day</p>
<p>B J Carey Capt<br/>9th M.G. Coy<br/>13.12.16</p>
