
<p>1918&nbsp;&nbsp; November</p>
<p>7 THUR&nbsp; Fine day. Rec. letters</p>
<p>from Julie &amp; Tense. Heard</p>
<p>of Tom Magee&#39;s death</p>
<p>on Oct 26<sup><u>th</u></sup>. Rumours out</p>
<p>re declaration of peace.</p>
<p>Writing at night.</p>
<p>Evening after. Boys well oiled.</p>
<p>8 FRI&nbsp; Raining on &amp; off during</p>
<p>the day. Dental Inspection</p>
<p>Writing at night.</p>

<p>Sgts. Posted aussies to May</p>
