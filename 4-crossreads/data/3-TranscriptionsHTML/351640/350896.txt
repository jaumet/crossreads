<p>One I am told belonged to the Emperor of Austria.   This was a very select German seaside resort.   I have a lovely room looking right on to the sea &amp; the ride to Nieuport Dixmude [Diksmuide] etc. so see the battles by land &amp; sea.</p>
<p>I have one 75 shell for you don't know yet about the helmet, I think I promised Mr. Hyman first.</p>
<p>Believe Dr &amp; Mrs. Fiaschi are in London, think he took a risk coming over at his age.   There are no old men or women here.   The work is too tiring.   Dr. Defage does a big abdo [abdominal] case in less than an hour.</p>
