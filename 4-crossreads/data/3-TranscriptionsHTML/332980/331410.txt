<p>at St Omer, &amp; signed off &amp; after Tea I went up the Street to the New Theatre to see a Variety Show which as <strike>fair</strike> good &amp; the Theatre was fairly crowded &amp; the Artists received a good reception, after a Drink at the Estam I returned to the Depot, &amp; turned in,</p>
<p>Sunday 30/6/18.<br/>I did not go over any Roads as I received permission from a Officer for a day in Camp so after Breakfast I went over to the A.V.C. Barracks &amp; had a Hot Shower Bath which was Bon &amp; then into the Estaminet for a Drink, where the Madam invited me to come back at 1 Pm &amp; have Dinner with the Family which I accepted &amp; returned to the Depot to have a Shave &amp; Brush up &amp; at 1 Pm duly arrived at the Estaminet where a splendid Dinner was provided for me consisting of Soup, Roast Beef Peas &amp; Potatoes, a Glass of Ale, Wine &amp; then Rum &amp; Coffee, &amp; I did not enjoy it, not half, After a smoke &amp; a chat &amp; thanked Madam for her Hospitality, I went for a Promenade through the Town to the Park where I expected a Band would be playing but was dissapointed, anyhow I was not there long when</p>
