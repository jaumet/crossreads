<p>One could boil about 2 dixies of water on it. They were a great boon during the winter months. It was not allowed to have fires in the front line for fear of attracting the enemy. I was a flare pistol carrier for 3 months. I don't know why I carried it but the fact remains I had a little adventure which brought down a shower of abuse upon my devoted head and that was in trying to clean up my flare pistol.
