<p>Copy of a notice in "B" Coy firing line<br/>PALAIS DE SHRAPNEL.  Gallipoli<br/>Accommodation for 6 bob a day tourists.  Ground floor.  Meals at all hours<br/>Apartment a loner</p>
<p>Newly married couples must have luggage.</p>
<p>Visitors are requested to keep off the parapet.  Offenders will be shot.</p>
<p>Visitors must not use the drinking water for washing.</p>
<p>Gentlemen wishing to change their hose are requested to do so at night and as close as possible to the enemy trenches</p>
<p>Fleas lice &amp; flies gratis.  Not extra charge if served up in tea</p>
<p>Turkish delight dealt out daily.  New arrivals can get instruction free (at the rear) in the use of firearms.  No close season</p>
<p>The management will not be responsible for barbed wire etc removed from the plateau</p>
<p>No credit given to "stiff" Turks</p>
<p>Bullets, bombs grenades &amp; shells will be exchanged here day &amp; night free of charge</p>
<p>Positions vacant on our staff for Lady Snipers<br/>Apply within</p>
