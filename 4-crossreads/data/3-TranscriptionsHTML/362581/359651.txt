<p>XOXO<br/>3rd A.C.C.S.<br/>A.I.F.<br/>France<br/>17.3.17.<br/>XOXOXOX</p>
<p>My Darling Eddy,  XXX<br/>XX We have some Jocks in our Wards and they are very good patients also excellent soldiers. I like them best in their Kilts, I should love to take you to Scotland when you are a bog boy   &amp;  show you the interesting sites we saw. Hope you are well   &amp;  strong now. Fondest love   &amp;  kisses. XXXXXX From XXXX Auntie Eadle XX.</p>
