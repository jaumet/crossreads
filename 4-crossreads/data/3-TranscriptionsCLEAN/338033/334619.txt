However after four punctures we arrived home safely at five o'clock, having been delayed four hours.Coming through Salisbury we had to go round the back streets in order to avoid the MPS. August 6th I met Horace Thurston and Harrington Crough, who came with me through Netherhaven to Tiheledean  Fiheldean where the village smithy stands.
