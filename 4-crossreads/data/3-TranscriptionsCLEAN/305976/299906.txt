
Saturday Aug 11thOn dutySunday Aug 12thOn dutyMonday Aug 13thLast night in Cairo before leaving for the line. Sent home parcels & had a good timeThursday Aug 14thInspection of unit by Major BuxtonWednesday Aug 15thNight ride on Camels. Saddling in dark. Rode 5 mile unsaddled & saddled 10 minutes allowed. Capt. S. informed us if anybody had a spill they would hear from him. He was the only one who fell due to his Camel putting his foot in a hole. What Joy among the boys.
