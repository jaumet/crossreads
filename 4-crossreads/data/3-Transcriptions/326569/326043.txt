<p>Saturday, 28/4/17<br/>Up early in morning &amp; marched to No. 11 with our full packs.  Left our packs there &amp; marched back for breakfast, then to No. 11 again &amp; then marched to Dinton with packs up.  Entrained there &amp; went via Salisbury &amp; Andover Junction to Ludgershall.  Got out there &amp; marched &frac12; mile to Windmill Hill Camp &amp; joined up with D company 62nd Btn. of 6th Division.  It is only a new camp of tents with wooden floors &amp; everything is very rough so far &amp; food very scarce.  There are 13</p>
