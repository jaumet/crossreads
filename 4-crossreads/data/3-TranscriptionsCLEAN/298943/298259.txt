  
 Result of study circle discussion was that we commenced a series of classes a couple of days ago after wards: English, & Arithmetic (which I am taking); Shorthand & French. Regular attendance & good earnest spirit on the part of the dozen or so in each class. Plane flights in two sessions lately, near balloon. The last one was rather amusing. Several squadrons of our bombing planes had been going over, when, during a time of overhead quiet, a Hun plane suddenly swooped down from above & attacked our balloon quite near.
