<p>February, 1916<br/>Thursday 3<br/>We rise at 10.30 am &amp; after breakfast went for a long tram ride<br/>Walk along the sea front during the afternoon<br/>Catch 11.30 pm train back to Cairo</p>
<p>Friday 4<br/>After a very cold ride of nearly 7 hrs we arrived back into Cairo &amp; getting home about an hour later went to sleep till 11 am</p>
