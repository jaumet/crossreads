<p>1915<br/>Apr. 13 Tue.<br/>Yesterday received a letter from District Scoutmaster George Rainsford of Kidderminster &amp; Dist. B. Scts. Assn. His address is 89 Wood St., Kidderminster.<br/>Still very busy.<br/>Have received 12 letters &amp; a newspaper this week.<br/>Learnt from Aunt Louies's letter to-night that it was appendicitis Kitty was operated for on in December.<br/>Feeling a bit crook to-day about the "innards".</p>
