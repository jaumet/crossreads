<p>Wednesday 23rd May 1917<br/>Watching mules while grazing.<br/>Cleaning harness in the afternoon and stable Piquet at 6pm. On 3rd shift (2 till 4am)<br/>One of Fritzs'planes over our camp at 5am this morning but had to return to his own lines as our guns made it too hot for him.</p>
