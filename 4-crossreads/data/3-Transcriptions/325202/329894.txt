<p>Nov 18/17 Samer &ndash;<br/>arrived here today after about 18 mile trek and are billetted on a farm about 1 mile out of the village. It is we are only 15 kilo from Boulogne. It is typical November weather &ndash; mild grey days</p>
<p>Dec 14/17<br/>For the past month six of us have been billetted on the farm of Lance-Lelin, Breuil W. Samer &amp; it has been quite a home &ndash; The most kindly people we have met in France</p>
<p>We have had a table put on one side for us in the big farm kitchen &amp; had the use of their fireplace in the kitchen while we have been able to buy milk from them &amp; they have given us</p>
