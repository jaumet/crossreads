 Office. Dubbo. Tuesday Mng. 19:1:15 
 Dear Alan. We have all heard with great excitement that you have volunteered for active war service and that your leaving for the front is not far off. 
 That "for the front" makes my heart go a bit pit a pat while I write it &ndash; not with fear I hope, but with the imaginations it pictures up of the great things being done there.
