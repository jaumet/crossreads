<p>9 September 1916 Sat<br/>Slept the best part of last night. After Dinner I went into Poperinghe with Cliff Searl &amp; Frank [indecipherable]. Motoring both ways. Eggs &amp; chips &amp; wanted to know if Bob was my father. 8.30 pm. B section joins us up. Hot baths change of clothes.</p>
<p>10 September 1916 Sun<br/>Slept the best part of the night. Sgt Beatties wants to know how everything is at 6.30. The Answer is all had a good night. Wingrove from the Anti-craft came to see me &amp; Sid &amp; I went with him to his quarters. Big pig sitting in old trenches all the afternoon.</p>
