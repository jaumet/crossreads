 22 February 1916 Tuesday 
 Called out on tattoo this morn.  First time since being in camp.  Absent from parade on Sat &ndash; last. Let off. 
 Field Amb. Being picked today. Drill in afternoon &amp; hand seat drill. Trio go down to Deans to tea.  Mary arrives too from Manly &amp; Cis &amp; Ess Hewlett after tea. Nice evening.  Dorothy &ndash; Charlie recite. Rescue soldier from having been run over by train at Strathfield Station.
