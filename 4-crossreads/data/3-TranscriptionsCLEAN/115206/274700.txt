
May 1918Friday 10
Bussy-la-DaoursGlorious weather.Had the usual journey at 4 pm, to Lailly Le Sec, via Vaux sur Somme and Corbie.During afternoon drifted out avec Muller, to Querrie Pont Noyelle, and La Neuvelle, as Mounted Orderley.Wrote a French epistle to Adrienne, aided by Lemon, at Madame Isobel.Harry came in from Aubigny.
