<p>The main Turkish position at Bir-el-Mazar was not attacked, but the 2nd &amp; 3rd L.H. Brigades in conjunction with the Camel Corps reconnoitered all the country &amp; gained the information required. They also succeeded in capturing two Turkish outposts (about 30 men). The Turks brought up some artillery &amp; shelled our fellows fairly heavily for awhile, however we had three <strike>Barr</strike> Batteries of artillery there &amp; they silenced the enemy. We had very few casualties on the stunt. The 1st Brigade acted as<br/>[Bir-el-Mazar is now Bi'r al Maza]</p>
