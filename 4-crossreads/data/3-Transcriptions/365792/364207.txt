<p>May 3  but they may turn up later. Never know your luck. Believe im to go to Marseille although the Doc has said nothing yet. Cold &amp; dull all day. Ward gradually being cleared out &ndash; possibly in view of the big offensive. Been polishing the floor &amp; feeding the bed patients, &amp; etc; helps to fill in the time.  The Leather Hosp slippers have no backs &amp; I cant manipulate them, so Nurse presented me with a pair of size 12 green &amp; red slippers, &amp; gave me strict instructions not to use them outside, &amp; of course I don't. Oh No! Anyhow they do stay on mostly, although I look more like a 'Roo than ever. [in margin] Ess</p>
<p>4"Australia" the Tommies call me, &amp; I call them &ndash; well &ndash; the first name available.</p>
