 23/7/17. Still exercising vigourously. Route marching mostly. Everybody is in the pink judging by the distance some go in search of diversion. Killed a snake today the first I've seen in France. 24/7/17. Moved from Maricourt back to Buire. A fair march. Am in the same old billet. Spent the evening in Ville-sur-Avere. 25/7/17. An inspection today and a march past, full marching order. I suppose we are pronounced fit for war, and this gay life is finished.
