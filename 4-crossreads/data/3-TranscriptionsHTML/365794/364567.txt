<p>Friday 15 [14] June 1917 [1918]<br/>Convoy in 4.30 p.m., 8 pts., 7 beds, confusion reigns for a few minutes then peace as one is transferred to lines.  A case of typhus in one ward has closed that one pro tem &amp; another, Victoria, is being fumigated &amp; all cockies taken to pieces (bugs) so we never have an empty bed for one day.</p>
