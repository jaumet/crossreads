 May, 1916 Sunday 7 Got up late  After Church parade we had dinner &amp; a good sleep. about 5 pm I went into town &amp; had Tea &amp; went to the Ezbekieh Gardens to hear the R.W.F. Band. Monday 8 Start work again preparing for the C &amp; C exam tomorrow. Weather still scorching. Get several letters &amp; answer them
