 Thursday 17th Aug. 
 Went on duty at dispensary alternate days with a Warrant Officer dispenser &amp; although he had the rank I did most of the [indecipherable].  Visited town on Wednesday the day the 5th Div. left. We left this morning.  Reveille at 5 a.m. left camp at 9 a.m. (some of the hawkers of fruit cried as we left) &amp; entrained after 2 mile march at 10. Left at 10.30.
