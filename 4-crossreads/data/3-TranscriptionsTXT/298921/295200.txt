
August, 1915Tuesday 17The Routine here is as follows White Robes, Red fez Arab brings tea & Biscuit at 6.30 -I then get my crutches & proceed to Bathroom & wash all but my Right thigh -Then a Tommy makes bed (3 officers one ward), another sweeps, a nigger swabs out with a Bucket of water. Then a R.A.M.C. orderly comes along with Thermo : counts pulse * finish this in  letter.Just heard Aust Mail closes So send love to all - Frank
