Saturday 3 March 17This morning I went to Canteen & after lunch left Houplines for Armentiers.  My kit came up & for another spell I am back in old billets.  Our men came out early in the morning.  The weather has turned very cold again, sharp white frosts & keen winds.
Sunday 4thWent to Billets for a few minutes, lunched at "au Boef" with Capt. Blake.The weather has been nice & clear for a couple of days & quite cold.  I had a service at the Y.M.C.A. tonight.  Had a good time.  Three men decided for Christ.
