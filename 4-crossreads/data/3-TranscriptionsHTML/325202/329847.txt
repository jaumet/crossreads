<p>Funny I had been told of it but couldn't believe it until my own experience but the first 2 night[s] could hardly sleep in a bed also I used to be fond of pastry &amp; fancy cakes but found now I couldn't look at them at all &ndash; had lost all taste for them. &ndash;</p>
<p>Got back the end of Jan. &amp; took over my team again &ndash; When I had left for leave everything was a sea of mud but on returning it was frozen hard &amp; was in the grip of <strike>of</strike> the keenest frost for over 40 years I had never experienced anything like it</p>
