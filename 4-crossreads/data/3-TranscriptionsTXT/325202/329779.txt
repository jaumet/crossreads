 April 23/16 Easter Sunday &ndash; Doilleu We arrived here late yesterday afternoon on the pouring rain &ndash; soaked through &amp; covered in mud. "Here" is a large roadside farmhouse about a mile from the village &ndash; it is also an "Estaminet" or inn &amp; we are billeted in the various outbuildings wherever we can get in. It reminds one of the old song "Fifteen of us on the parlour floor" except the parlour is a disused stable. 
 It wasn't very long we were crowded into the kitchen &amp; Madame was at the stove frying eggs  if  as if her life depended on it
