   My meals I took out always trying a new place, Dinner at Romanos, at the Maison Lyons, a Dinner & a Lunch at Simpsons the modern survival of an old time coffee house; & teas at fashionable little tea shops.
   Fairweather took me visiting a friend at Lancaster Gate Miss Portia Wheeler a Queensland heiress, she & here mother are a real friends to Anzacs in London & particularly the Queensland Lads, who are her special care.
