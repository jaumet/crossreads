This will give 1 as a Rough idea - but every brig builds them their own pattern & fancy-land in many places - Tunnels take the place of risk cubicles and support trenches if the Shelling is bad-The Turk holds his trenches lightly with men and heavily with M-guns which are all over the place, concealed in Parapets & in rear in the higher ground - we have to hold them heavily with men as we are so badly off for m.gs. - we have about 1/5 the numbers as They - imagining our men are never half united - Bayonets always fixed in F.T. except the Snipers and these by away all day from loop holes -No men are spended as are the N.Zs - but at any time by shorts of officers Fancy a private of 5 months experience being a Sergeant Major. I saw Fitzpeach &' Holmes took over Vickers from us & F appeared in beautiful clothes with all sorts of weapons and badges sticks over him - he felt quite ashamed - Whitlaim has some will - Foster ditto - But we rarely see anyone who of our own section - There is no time to wander - We have to walk miles - is it in every day and it is becoming winter now - we have no covering - not a sheet of iron in the peninsular and no Timbers or tools - But no use frosting -  Food excellent - Give my kind regards to the friends - I was one of the very few who has not been on a sick list or absent an hour and not as strong as
