<p>Nov 7th &ndash; Tuesday</p>
<p>Raining &amp; cold again to-day. Do just as I wish in hospital. Out at 6-30 am &amp; spend the day in various ways, reading, writing &amp; walking around. Am here for rest &amp; get it. Concert in theatre to-day at 3 pm &amp; another at 8 pm.</p>
<p>Nov 8th &ndash; Wednesday</p>
<p>Cold to-day, but clear. Really a nice afternoon. Usual resting around the wards. Obtain my greatcoat from the store in a beautiful condition. No concert. Have a game of billiards. Bed 9 pm.</p>
<p>Nov 9th &ndash; Thursday</p>
<p>A grand day. Awake 6-30 am</p>
