<p>Nov. 22nd, Wednesday<br/>We leave this pleasant little camp on Friday morning I believe.  I, for one, am very sorry:  really it has been an ideal little place as things go out here.  However &ndash; we are going about 20-25 miles further out to Bir el Mazar a place about 20 miles from El Arish, where I suppose we shall have to "stand to" in the grey light of early dawn every day &ndash; a miserable proceeding.  There is no humour in turning out at that hour.  Also we shall have perpetual lights out &amp; other unpleasant messes.<br/>I wonder if Marcus Aurelius ever had to do this sort of thing?</p>
