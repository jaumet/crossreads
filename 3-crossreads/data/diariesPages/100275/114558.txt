We were about an hour here then we moved on again, we marched about 3 miles nearly into Albert bivouacked out on an open piece of ground for the night we just simply threw ourselves down slept as we were, , to many of us it was the first time we had closed our eyes for 6 nights so you can imagine how we looked We rose when we liked next morning had a good breakfast
27th. That day we rested except for a few fatigue parties, one of which had to go to the firing line they were pretty stiff, for it was a very long walk.
