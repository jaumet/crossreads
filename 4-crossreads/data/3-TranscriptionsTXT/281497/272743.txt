Went for a swim this morning &amp; the water was fairly cold. Done some more detailing this afternoon. I can now run through all the rifle exercises correctly so I reckon I am getting on alright, &amp; I know a fair bit of the other work.
They have a great way of expressing themselves over here when they fall in, they call it a "gutser" pretty expressive isn't it.
This morning I tasted some cockles there are tons of them on the beach here.   I can't say that I care much for them.
