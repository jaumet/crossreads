Defence scheme. From this green ridge covered with yellow flowering plants there was a splendid view of all the positions opposite us. The rooves of Morlancourt could be seen plainly in the next gully. We are confronted with another ridge but in a few days time the 7th Bde. will attack and capture the one in front of us and give us command of the valley. Behind the nearer ridges rises the blue line of La Boiselle and Pozieres. It is rotten to think of once more assaulting these places after so much war. Walked over to the 21st and found things very quiet with them. They have an American attached for experience and he appeared a fine clean cut stamp of youth.
