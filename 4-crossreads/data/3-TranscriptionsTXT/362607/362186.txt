 W.G. Nicholls Andrews Bros 40 King St Sydney N.S.W. Australia 
 14.5.18 
 Dear George, I arrived safely at Liverpool. I am now on my way to London. The Train is rocking about. Don't think I'm shica. I had a glorious time. Note George's  Hall on left  &amp; Wellington's Monument at top   &amp;  hotel on right is North Western Hotel outside the Lime St Station. I've been up   &amp;  there lots   [indecipherable] . George, there's a long letter coming  &amp;  want to post   [indecipherable]  Love to all  Harry
