
March 1915Wednesday 3Field Day today and the 20000 men and Guns of Division out - Good training but very hard on horses, flinty Desert & no water.Recd letter from Laura Dated Feb 11th from England. Arranged for  110 to go to Gov: [indecipherable] London making 210/-/- there close a/c Anglo Egyptian Bank also arranged Thos Cook & Sons to send surplus luggage to C/- [indecipherable] Union BK of Australia Cornhill London Laura's address, as we go Constantinople in about 10 days or so. Am still a bit lame & hand in a sling result of fall off horse.
