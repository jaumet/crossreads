<p>Dec 3rd</p>
<p>Russia captured 70,000 Germans the Crown Prince included.</p>
<p>Thur 3rd<br/>Slept on deck last night but it was a bit on the cold side &amp; woke up to find ourselves anchored at Port Said, nothing very exciting happened last night &amp; we were passing troops all night stationed along the canal.<br/>this morning we are taking fresh water on board;  during the night we passed  a ship with the Royal Engineers on board &amp; they said they expected to be fighting any day now.</p>
<p>A French hydro-plane rose off the water a couple of hundred yards from our boat this morning &amp; flew around us in a circle reconnoitring this morning &amp; landed again on the water.  The boat has been surrounded by boats containing musicians playing for what they can get &amp; natives selling</p>
