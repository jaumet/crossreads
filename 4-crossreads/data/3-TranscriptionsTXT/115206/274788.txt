
August,1918Saturday 24
In Rear of Proyart. Bty PositionWeather dull, but nice and cool. Fritz planes flew fairly low all day.News from all fronts seems pretty good. Built a nice dugout under the road. Received a letter from Uncle Will- a nice interesting one.Ken Gun and Red Palmer left for Waggon Lines early in the evening. Had trouble with Bdge line and walked it .Bty pulled out on the main road. Fritz bombing all roadsfairly heavily. Slept in adug out near the road.
