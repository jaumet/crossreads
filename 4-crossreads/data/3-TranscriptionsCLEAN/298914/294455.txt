  
  29/3/17  All quiet - go for swims all the afternoon - mail arrives 30/3/17  Move over Move over to HQ position and do not erect.  At tea we shell a Turkish working party at Gaza. 1/4/17 Jacko shells us during morning.  Lovely surf in morning 2/4/17 Our bombardment of Gaza starts. 3/4/17 Still shelling. Chandler & I leave 9 am for Rafa for goods.  Pass through KHAN-YUNUS.  Busy little market place here.  Take photos and have look round.  Arrive Rafa 4 PM and bivouac with some 52nd Division  A.S.C. men.  Go to Flying Corps & see a number of photos of Turkish positions. 4-4-17 Leave 9 am for Bela arriving 4 PM.  Seven of our planes up for a Taube - no good. 5-4-17 Go down to Bela & see the tanks.  Tiger-Kia-Ora.  Pincher Sir Archibald, Knutly and three others - 8 in all.  Male tanks carry two 6lb guns & female 4 maxim and smaller arms. 6 to 10th Nothing doing. 14th Jacko shells railhead & busts up things.  Blows away half the Clearing Station. *See the tanks go out 10 PM.* 15th Shells camel corps & wings our 60 pdr of 90th batter.  Stunt on
