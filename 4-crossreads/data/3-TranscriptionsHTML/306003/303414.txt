<p>1-7-16<br/>Sat 1st<br/>The 3rd, 6th &amp; 7th Regts returned today from their jaunt to Bir-El-Abd &amp; they did not get in touch with any of the enemy;  The formation for the railway line is now out some miles in front of this camp (Romani) &amp; the work is going along lively,  Strong posts have also been made of any hills here that will be of any value in case we are attacked;  Nothing out of the ordinary happened today.</p>
<p>2-7-16<br/>Sun 2nd<br/>Church Service again this morning &amp; nothing out of the ordinary happened today;  Tonight Batty (Lt) raided the two-up school &amp; took several names;  Too much gambling goes on in camp &amp; it would be better if it was cut out altogether</p>
