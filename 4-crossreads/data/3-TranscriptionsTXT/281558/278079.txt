
Friday Jan 18th
Fovant : usual parades : theory exam in afternoon but we will not get our papers back till Monday :
Saturday Jan 19th
Fovant : still showery : boys went on the route march : I was exempted by the Dr : wrote in the afternoon : concert at YMCA at night & pretty good :
Sunday Jan 20th
Fovant : wet : church parade in morning : wrote in afternoon & night :
Monday Jan 21st
Fovant : usual parades : got our papers handed back to us & I got 25/30  25/30  & 35/40  total 85/100  so did not do too bad : wrote at night & had a bitter of practice on my "buzzer" :
