 Mon. Apr. 3rd -16 Arrived Le Havre 10 A.M. and marched to No. 2 Camp at Sanvic (6 miles distant). 
 Tues. Apr. 4th -16 Very comfortable in our new camp but weather extremely cold, heavy fogs.  After usual routine we visited the villiage. 
 Wed. Apr. 5th -16 Equipped with new guns and limbers and warned to leave for front at any minute.
