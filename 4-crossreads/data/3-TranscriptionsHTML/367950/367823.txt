<p>the ocean and we were hung up for a full fortnight at Aden then we had to go at a pace of only 8 knots because the Cruiser "Doris" was not capable of going faster. Left to ourselves we could have easily gone at least 16 knots. So through hanging about on the water for so long, the poor fellow died  He could easily have been home in the ordinary course of events that privilege must be for ever denied.<br/>He suffered great pain and prayed death to relieve him. It came at last.<br/>There was to have been a concert tonight &ndash; a good one &ndash; to wind things up so to speak, but it has been cancelled.<br/>My head has been aching today don't know if it's the weather or not. But there it is.</p>
