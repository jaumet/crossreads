  
 Wednesday 19 July 1916 Left Ras-el-tin, caught train at Alexandria for Benha.  Had to wait 3 hours at Benha then came on to Tel-el-Kebir. Arrived in Camp & got back with my mates. 
 Thursday 20 July 1916 Reported to Dr. for inspection, O.K. Allotted a horse. Received 30 letters from Australia. Father has bought a car. Letters (1) from Father & (2) Mother.  No. (2) Mrs. Nicholl.
