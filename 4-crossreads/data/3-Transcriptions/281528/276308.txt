<p>12/4/18 &ndash; Lavieville<br/>A lovely sunny day.  The Boche shelling very close to here and sending up debris &amp; smoke.  Found the dead bodies of a Scotch officer and a private at the local church.  They had evidently been there some days and had bad wounds.  Met a Major of the Tanks and with him made a reconnaisance of a new line forward.  The weather was so clear that the observation from Albert was absolutely wonderful.  After lunch met Rodda and Kennedy &amp; made yet another reconnaisance, this time of the right flank.  In a little copse there are now many</p>
