So far the weather is nice & mild bar for the rain, but we have struck no cold as yet & I don't think we will before November after that I'll bet she's a freezer.Since we have been in here we have received a fair lot of reinforcements, so that now the Coy is fairly strong again which is much better, for it lightens the work all round.
