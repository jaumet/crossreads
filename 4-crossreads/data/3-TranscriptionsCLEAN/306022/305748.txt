 Thursday 2nd Nov. All along the coast we could see searchlights playing around on the watch for Zeppelins, and others on the sea. 
 We entered Plymouth Haven about 2 am the entrance being under the light of over 30 searchlights all focussed across from side to side. At last dawn came and with it a greeting from the "Clan McGillivray"
