 Thursday 19 April 1917 
 Marched all night, in action at 0400 &ndash; shelled by enemy big fight on all around hope live through it. Two of my friends Killed  MAJOR CHISHOLM &amp; LIEUT CHAMBERS of 2 L.H. SINCLAIR (some PUCKAWIDGEE relative . We have many casualties 71 Horses. Bombed by enemy air craft again &ndash; cold blooded business. One of our planes shot down by enemy this afternoon. 
 Many Fleas &ndash; its harder to catch than LICE. 
  MAJOR CHAMBERS of 12 also killed today attack 2 of same day . I hope Cuth is alright.
