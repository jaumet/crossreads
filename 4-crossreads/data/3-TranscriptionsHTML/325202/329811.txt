<p>Albert about &frac12; mile away &amp; we can see what was the church The spire has been hit &amp; its back broken &amp; although it has not fallen it is bent in half &amp; the top of the spire is pointing to the ground. <p>July 22 (Sunday) Senlis<br/>moved here to a bivouac 2 miles from Albert &ndash; Cpl C[dash] had been celebrating St Patricks Day (he does it every pay day!) and was fast asleep in the corner of a dark barn when some practical jokers suggested holding a 'wake' on him.</p>
