From here we had to pick up our packs to walk, &amp; by Jove it was a snorter we were bound for Fricourt 9 miles away &amp; to me it seemed one of the hardest marches as ever I have done, we had had no tucker since breakfast so you can guess how we felt, &amp; we were a very straggling tired lot that eventually pulled up at some long low huts where we are spending the night 75 men to a hut, we can hear the guns booming away &amp; the horizon is lit up with the flash of the guns. As we came in tonight I counted 27 balloons all in a line &amp; any amount of aeroplanes, one I saw was a brand new design &amp; was double engined she was one of our fast planes 120 miles per hour.
