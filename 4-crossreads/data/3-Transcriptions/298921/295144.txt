<p>June, 1915<br/>Tuesday 22<br/>Court martial today I am Prosecutor.<br/>Received letter from Laura &amp; a few cards from the front some are very funny I have some good snap shots &ndash; of dead Turks etc &ndash; but will not trust them to the tender mercy of a Censor, who no doubt would like them himself .<br/>Some quaint Post Cards come back from Dardanelles written on Cardboard or Brown Paper &ndash; one from one of my men was just addressed<br/>Mr Weir.<br/>Heliopolis &amp; I got it send it on to you with this to keep for me.</p>
