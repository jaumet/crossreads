Sunday May 6thHave packed on again today ready to leave tomorrow Monday, Have been able to write with the R hand for last few days.
Monday 6th MayLeft Dartford about 10.30 & Marched to the station Changed trains at Waterloo for Winton. The train took us right up to Forant & Cars from there about 2 miles to No 3 Command Depot Hurdcotte. Went through the usual perforfmance
