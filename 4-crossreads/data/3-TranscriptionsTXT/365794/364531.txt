 Thursday 9th May 1917 [1918] Feeling myself after 2 p.m. "Niobe-fied" name &amp; passing breeze with Miss McAlpine re pan or rather permission to go W.C. (which I obtain), pity we just don't understand one another yet.  Other Aust. visits me &amp; 1 lends L.E.  Wrote Capt. Stillman A.I.F. H.Q. re pay, also Mother, Miss McLean, Choubra &amp; S. Leeds.  Temp. etc. now normal &amp; once again I am over 3rd attack since coming to Egypt. Particulars as to 19th G.H. Sisters &ndash; 17 or more can be adm.,  quite separate  from other wards, own mess room, only 10 in at present.  Medical &amp; Surgical Officers  on  in this hosp., 2 rooms for mids (civilians) tommies Hosp. in 2 divisions under same O.C. Pts. 1000 in each division.  Belonged to Germans in pre-war days.  Gypos on gr. floor.
