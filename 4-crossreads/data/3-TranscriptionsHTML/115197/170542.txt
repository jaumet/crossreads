<p>Thus truly may we say with Byron -</p>
<p>&quot;Thou glorious mirror,</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; where the Almighty&#39;s form</p>
<p>Glasses itself in tempests;</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;in all time,</p>
<p>Calm or convulsed - in breeze,</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; or gale, or storm,</p>
<p>Icing the pole or in the torrid clime</p>
<p>Dark-heaving - boundless, endless, and sublime.</p>
