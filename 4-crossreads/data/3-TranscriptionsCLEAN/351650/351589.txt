
At the present moment it is likely I may go to Mesopotamia:  anything for something new.
I have been advised to be ready to go at any moment to any of our three fronts, but have not yet got any definite orders as to the date.
The only place I'm dead against is Salonika.  It seems from all accounts to be an extraordinarily dull spot.
Rules & regulations due to the war are becoming pretty stringent here now, a wretched soldier in uniform seems to be the most penalised of all.
Do you know, Wilkie was over in London on leave two weeks ago and I never knew about it, only just found out by accident two days after he went back.  Wasn't it bad luck missing him?
