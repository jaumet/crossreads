
<p>1918&nbsp;&nbsp; September</p>
<p>5 THUR&nbsp; Posted &#39;Aussie&#39; to May Webb.</p>
<p>Arrived back about 6.15 am</p>
<p>Fine day. very warm. Battery</p>
<p>manouvres in morning</p>
<p>2 Bde Sports in aft. Writing at</p>
<p>night. Fresh successes everywhere</p>
<p>Quiet</p>
<p>6 FRI&nbsp; Fine day. Bty manouvres</p>
<p>in morning. Packing up for</p>
<p>moving off tomorrow. Quiet</p>
<p>Posted letter to R G. Clarke.</p>
<p>Received letters from Amy &amp; Zelia</p>
<p>7 SAT&nbsp; Fine day. Warm. Moved</p>
<p>off at 9 am. via Bray to</p>
<p>bivouack near Zusanne. Fixing</p>
<p>up camp. &#39;<u>Fritz</u>&#39; reported</p>
<p>to be evacuating. Received</p>
<p>letters from Amy Mac &amp;</p>
<p>Celie. Writing at night.</p>
<p>Very quiet.</p>
