<p>V.A.D. Hospital<br/>Alcester, Eng.</p>
<p>13 Oct. 18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>I have just received a large batch of mail, twelve altogether, three from you, one from Father, Bruce &amp; Jean, the rest from different of my regular correspondants.  As you see by the above address I have shifted from the other hospital, this is a tip top place, they feed us up on plenty of the best tucker excellently cooked so if I don't get fat I never will.  We are allowed out morning &amp; evening &amp; although there is nothing to see in the quite little town we can always enjoy a</p>
