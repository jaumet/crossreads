<p>Friday 2/3/17</p>
<p>Attack on MALT TRENCH at 3 am a great success by Bn, and Bde 2 M.G 4 T.M. and 75 prisoners out casualties 5 Officers 90 O.R.</p>
<p>Saturday 3/3/17</p>
<p>The Bn was relieved by Reid &amp; we went out to Reserve in 26th Avenue &amp; Crumpet Road.</p>
<p>Sunday 4/3/17</p>
<p>In accordance with the wishes of the G.O.C. went &amp; took over again from Reid to readjust the line.</p>
<p>Monday 5/3/17</p>
<p>Took over all brigade, Front part from Norrie and made all dispositions. Had letter from Doff.</p>
