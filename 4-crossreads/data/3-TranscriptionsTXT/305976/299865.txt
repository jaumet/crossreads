Sunday May 27 Church parade Quiet day Monday May 28 Received Wireless on board that Transport Transylvania sunk in Meditteranean. Perhaps ('Furfie') Had mock trial on deck in afternoon very amusing. All hands well. Tuesday May 29 Sea beautifully calm. Have had good weather since leaving Fremantle. Flying Fish numerous. Ships Sports start to day Fire alarm started during progress
