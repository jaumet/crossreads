<p>Monday 7th December 1915.<br/>Am now at Helouan. Have been out here about 11 days. &hellip;&hellip;&hellip;.. at Al Hayai Hotel. This is a good sized building. It is built in a generous fashion. The rooms are large and lofty and the halls wide. It is built of stone brick wood &amp; plaster. Is has a good piazza &amp; colonnade. The piazza is tiled. The paths are bordered with ornamental low&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    of a rustic fashion. There are 3 fine tennis courts. The hotel is built on 2 wings. We have our meals in the dining rooms here. There are a number of animals here in an enclosure. I think they are ibex and are native to Africa. We have breakfast at 7.30.</p>
