 Gallipoli Peninsula 20.9 15 
 Dear Mrs. Dunlop 
 Just a line to say I am well &amp; happy in wearing your knitted socks. The Autumn is here &amp; soon I think we will get real Winter but as  for  we have had only 2 months of cool weather with a snap of cold in it for the past 10 &frac12; months it will be a change. We are engaged in plenty of daily work some of it grim, some pleasant, usual lights &amp; sounds of active warfare one sees almost daily but our men are cheery 
 [Note written along the left-hand margin of the page:] Don Dunlop 9.8.32
