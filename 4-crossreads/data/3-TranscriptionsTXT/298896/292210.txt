Next caught a tram to Le Bar walking on to Gorge du loup, a lovely spot with waterfall etc & fine restaurants. There we had dinner a splendid spread with local wines which are renowned. We were seated on a balcony overlooking a stream & I thoroughly enjoyed it. After, I visited a shop to purchase some postcards & had a great yarn with the proprietor, a horticulturist & collector of stamps, who became very friendly on my giving him some Australian stamps. He gave me some extra postcards, also a bulb of some famous lilly which he had grown. Had tea here & caught the train back at 6 and reached Nice about 8.30 P.M.
