Bally Bunion is about 5 miles from the Canal, it is very strongly fortified &amp; inside the barb wire entanglements are sufficient shelter sheds for some thousands of horses. While here we should be most comfortable, quite a treat after Romani &amp; the long stunts we have done from there. However we expect to be here only a few days, a camp is being prepared for us elsewhere. Our duties while here are to patrol out about ten miles East of here.
