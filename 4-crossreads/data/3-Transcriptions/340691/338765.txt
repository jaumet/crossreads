<p>Bed at 8 not feeling very well and slept well. Dull.</p>
<p>22.10.18. Up at 6.30 and set off for parade ground after a preliminary run round and marched in drill order through Surcamps, Brucamps and Ailly-le-haut-clocher about 2 kilos beyond town  Did some company ceremonial drill and back again at 11.30. Very dull and rotten roads about 10 kilos each way. I aftn. rested and issue of blanket, gloves. Paid in evening and rest of evening spent in writing diary and getting mobile. Bed at 9.30.</p>
<p>22.10.18. Check parade at 7.30 and drill order at 9. Dismissed and only some fatigues going and model section etc. In aftn. game of tennis, and evening read. Dull</p>
<p>23.10.18. Dull. Usual. Dismissed in morning and played cards. In aftn. read and played on game of tennis and after tea caught lorry to Port-Remy via Ailly. Lobbed at 6.30 and good show by Sentimental Blokes. Home by 9. through Long. Bed tired.</p>
<p>24.10.18. Dull should have gone with Tuck but went down &amp; marked Sports Ground with Robb. In aftn. played good tennis game while tryouts on. In evening bridge.</p>
<p>25.10.18. Guard. Quiet One cycle lost. Dull</p>
<p>26.10.18. Off guard at 9. and wrote diary and read in morning. In aftn. game of tennis and read in evening. Dull.</p>
<p>27.10.18. Parade at 9 and few fatigues and dismissed in aftn. and played tennis and read</p>
