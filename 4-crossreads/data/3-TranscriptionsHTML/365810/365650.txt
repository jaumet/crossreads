<p>Some time ago you wrote me a very kind letter in appreciation of my Cartoons, and I can assure you I was very flattered by it.  But it chanced to be put away among my papers, and I have only this moment turned it up with a <strike>trill</strike> thrill of consternation, to discover that I never answered it at all.</p>
<p>This is a wretched admission, but there is no option but the bald truth permitted me.</p>
