<p>[June 1916.]<br/>Friday 2<br/>I am thinking to night of the girls I love ELSIE Nell Muriel Dolly distance only lends happy thoughts<br/>Saturday 3<br/>Time passes quickly will be leaving for the trenches any day, just as well do my bit and get it over &amp; hope for the best S.A.G<br/>Sunday 4<br/>Ah, me, life is a queer game one never knows what is next<br/>Carnsdale is the place I'd like to be to day it is raining here miserable day</p>
