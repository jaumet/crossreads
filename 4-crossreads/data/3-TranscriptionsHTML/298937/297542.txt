<p>That ended the sightseeing in Cheops &amp; we quickly got out of the humid interior &amp; left less some 10 piastres, mulcted by the guides.</p>
<p>Friday 10th March</p>
<p>Dr. Scrimgeour has gone away for the week-end.<br/>My ankle was sprained last Sunday &amp; now is giving me trouble for I got about on it too much.  This afternoon the General (Gafrah Bashir) came into the hospital to have his arm dressed &amp; Dr. Boutazy, he &amp; I had quite an interesting conversation about Anglo-Turks-German affairs.
