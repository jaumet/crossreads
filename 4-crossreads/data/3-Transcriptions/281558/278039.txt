<p>was some barrage : rain cleared up about 2 am : moved off about 8 am : no casualties for first 1000 yards in our coy though shells were plentiful : struck it rough after that : shell blew my squad up &amp; killed Bill Rowe wounded Murrell &amp; Morrie but not me : Morrie stuck out with me &amp; we went to the front line : told that S.M. Watson &amp; Alf were out in front wounded so went out &amp; brought them in : rough carrying through Fritz's barrage of 5.9 how : took Watson down &amp; returned for Alf but a shell had again got him &amp; took both legs off : put tourniquet on &amp; was bringing a stretcher when a shell landed behind me &amp; blew me down : bit through thigh &amp; back, &amp; Morrie also got another one : lost a lot of blood &amp; was very weak but managed to hobble out to near : Hell Fire corner" : officer gave me a</p>
