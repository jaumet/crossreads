<p>Nov. 14 Sunday<br/>Lovely day, felt very tired after Saturday's walking.  Mail in.  Reading &amp; resting all day.  Rec'd beautiful Cake.</p>
<p>Nov. 15 Monday<br/>Weather good.  Still very weak.</p>
<p>Nov. 16 Tuesday<br/>Weather very windy but fine.  Quite strong again, out gathering firewood for self during afternoon.</p>
<p>Nov. 17 Wednesday<br/>Weather changeable with high wind ending in terrific burst of rain lasting about &frac14; hour but flooding everything.  Was able to relay my bed after shower &amp; sleep remainder of night from about 8.30.</p>
