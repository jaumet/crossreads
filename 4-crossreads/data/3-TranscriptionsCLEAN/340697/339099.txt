  
 Left Toulon at 6 pm yesterday and were towed alongside wharf at Marsielles at 3 p.m. Disembarked at 8.p.m. Entrained at 10.30 p.m. (in second class carriages) and moved out at midnight. 28.3.16 
 We expect to do a 3 days train journey to somewhere north of Paris. One man fell off train last night and had both legs cut off. Had breakfast at [Orange?]. Dinner on train and tea at - Passed through Lyons at about 4.10 p.m. 29.3.16
