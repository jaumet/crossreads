<p>4th October 1917<br/>[Photo Nos 36, 41]<br/>.Punctually at 6 a.m. our infantry attacked along an eight mile front after first it had been battered by our artillery barrage. By a remarkable coincidence the Boche had also planned a counter attack at precisely the same hour:  but our frightful barrage descended &amp; absolutely demoralised &amp; almost annihilated his attacking line.
