<p>Friday 17 May<br/>Conducted parade 7 a.m. Grooming etc.</p>
<p>Off to Abbeville in afternoon with Whytecross &amp; Robinson by motor wagon. Stopped but got through on a yarn.</p>
<p>Knocked about the town but afterwards called on Miss Priestman. Had tea very tediously. Left about 7.45 after further knocking about the streets. Very hot.</p>
