 March 1918 Friday 1 
 Messines Spent a fairly quiet day and registered for Warneton help in S.O.S. through 5 boards. Weather, snow and sleet during morning, very cold in afternoon. Went to L'aison with Kerr at 7 pm turned in at 8 pm S.O.S. went up from Orange Trench, at 20 to 10 pm. Guns were all in action 5 sec later. Fritz put his barrage down on supports. Smashed up the wires badly . 
 Fritz came a "cropper" and did not reach our wire. Ceased fire at 11 pm.
