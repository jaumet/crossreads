<p>September 1917<br/>easily &amp; got back a little after 8.  Some were not in till 9 oclock.</p>
<p>Friday 14th<br/>Had the exam before breakfast.  The results were out before 12 oclock.  I got the possible the papers were not very hard &amp; they mark very liberally.  We got a car into town &amp; had lunch at St James.  Went visiting in the afternoon.  Had to take a few Australians to spend the afternoon with Mrs Price so I took my own friends.  We met quite number of people &amp; Mrs Davies Bryan took us off &amp; showed us her collection of curiosities which are very good.  Mr Bryan being a keen collector &amp; an old resident of Cairo.<br/>Jim Nevitt (one of ours) was celebrating his birthday, so a little party of us had dinner together &amp; drank his health in champagne.  This was our last evening, but I was satisfied with my spell at the school.  We got a car home about 11 oclock.</p>
<p>Saturday 15th<br/>School broke up this morning &amp; went away by special train.  Got to Moascar</p>
