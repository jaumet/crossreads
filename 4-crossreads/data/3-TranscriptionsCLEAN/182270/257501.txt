TUESDAY, JULY 8, 1919.
Fine & warm. Round Boundary & Mountain
in morning & picking up in the
Middle Paddock in afternoon.
Tickle took waggon down to
Walhallow & brought back the
cut timber for the mill. Left it at
the bore & brought waggon up to
Station and the "Pop" with it.
Percy George doing the Boundary
troughs again.
