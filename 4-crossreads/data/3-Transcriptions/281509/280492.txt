<p>1916 September 26<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>1st Field Amb. Dinner. Canadians Scots, &amp; English. Song &amp; quip. 6 to 10 p.m. Sang 'Melisande'.<br/>Great victories for the Allies on the Somme. Saw a balloon fall in flames this evening. Not sure whether it was one of our own or Fritz's.</p>
