<p>I have seen also a French sub marine in which the people go and subscribe to the loan.</p>
<p>A friend of mine has taken photographs, therefore it shall be possible for me to give you views of this exposition.</p>
<p>In the newspapers I have seen that an Australian corps had been celebrated at Denain. &nbsp;I address to you for this all my congratulations. &nbsp;I wish that an equal celebration be made in my native country.</p>
<p>I hope that you are always in good health.</p>
<p>With my kindest regards</p>
<p>Yours faithfully</p>
