<p>Sunday 19-11-16<br/>Very wet yesterday evening ground thawed a bit which made everything sloppy &amp; messy.  Double Rum Issue last night.  On Water fatigue today.</p>
<p>Monday 20-11-16<br/>I had to go out yesterday on the line.  We went out at 12 midday &amp; returned about 7.0 pm.<br/>Letter to Mac</p>
<p>Tuesday 21-11-16<br/>Trucked Rails out to line running parallel with Turk Lane trench where it leads on to Right Strong point.</p>
<p>Wednesday 22-11-16<br/>The Battalion is "on Spell"  We are working on Railway today.</p>
<p>Thursday 23-11-16<br/>Pay day yesterday.   My new Pay Book has not come to hand yet.</p>
<p>Saturday 24-11-16<br/>Raining all day.  Working on Railway Depot</p>
<p>Wednesday  29-11-16<br/>Same Job.  Foggy &amp; cold very little daylight.  Dark at 5</p>
