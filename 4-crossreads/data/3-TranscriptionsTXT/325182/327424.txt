
1st JulyArrived at Waverley Station just in time to see my train wriggle out out and a damn fool of a porter informed me that I was too late.  So I caught the 10 A.M. train for London and Leicester.  am I arrived at Leicester about 8.30 p.m. after changing at York, Cormanton and Sheffield and put up for the night at the Wyvern Hotel.
