<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Flag</p>
<p>To - Berrima</p>
<p>Date - 13-9-14</p>
<p>Time - 9-0</p>
<p>Following received from Encounter following received from shore. &nbsp;Pass to Berrima. &nbsp;I have received no copy of proclomation and should be glad to receive one. &nbsp;Delay in informing you of this was owing to this afternoon operations my reports have also been delayed for same reasons. &nbsp;I <s>would</s>&nbsp;will endeavour to send them in the morning. &nbsp;1855</p>
