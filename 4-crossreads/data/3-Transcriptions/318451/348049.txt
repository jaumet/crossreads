<p>Thursday 28th<br/>Visited Billets in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Friday 29<br/>Took reading matter to Billets.<br/>Weather fairly fine.  Made arrangements re services for Sunday.<br/>Found 13 cases of comforts &amp; had them taken to store.</p>
<p>Saturday<br/>Took a walk round Billets, prepared for Sunday.</p>
<p>Sunday 31st<br/>We had a fine parade this morning, about 500 men present.  "[Indecipherable], Jesus [indecipherable] the same yesterday, today, forever".<br/>About 40 men stayed to communion.  Tonight we had a good service in the Y.M.C.A.  Mills gave address, I led singing.  At 8.30 the officers of the Batn. met at Au-Boeuf Cafe at a dinner, a reunion of the officers of our mess.
