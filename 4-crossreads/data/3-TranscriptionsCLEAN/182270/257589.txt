 SATURDAY, OCTOBER 4, 1919. 
 Fine with cold Southerly gale blowing 
 Lopped in Top paddock in morning 
 & burning off track in Wheelers 
 in Afternoon. 
 Darby's bore fell in & he had to 
 punch it all out again & case it 
 as he went down. Down to 
 about 106 ft in rotten "mush" & 
 no water yet. Tickle drawing 
 water to him all day
