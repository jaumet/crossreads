<p>Xtracts from letters to Military Authoritys</p>
<p>I have received no pay since my husband left nowhere"</p>
<p>My Bill has been put in charge of a Spitoon, shall I get more money</p>
<p>In accordance with instructions on No 9 Paper, I have given birth to a daughter on April 1st</p>
<p>You have changed my little boy into a little girl will it make any difference.</p>
<p>I am writing these few lines for Mrs Morgan, who cannot write herself &ndash; She is expecting to be confined, &amp; can do with it.</p>
