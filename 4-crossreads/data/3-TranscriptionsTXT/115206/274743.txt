 June 1918 Saturday 22 
 On the Road. Bourdon. Weather wet and miserably cool. Cleaned harness in the morning generally swanking up for the road tomorrow. Walked to a neighboring village, where we spent a fairly Convivial evening in the company of Smithy and Gunn. Believe we pull out at [indecipherable] can't say with any certainty. Feel fairly happy.
