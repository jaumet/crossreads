<p>Monday 20 January 1919<br/>Up at 2 pm. Went to Bank. YM Kodaks for films &amp; negs. Had a busy afternoon. Went out for dinner.</p>
<p>Tuesday 21 January 1919<br/>Up at 4 am &amp; after a lot of bustle we managed to get a seat in second class compt. Still it was a seat. Train was very slow. At 1.15 crowd rushed for dinner waggon &amp; train started leaving about 20 or 30 on a platform. At 7 it waited 2 &frac12; so could easy have stopped for them Got to Brussels at 1 am.</p>
