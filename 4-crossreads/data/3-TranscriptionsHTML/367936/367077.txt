<p>Tuesday 28 November 1916<br/>Nothing much doin all day.<br/>Wrote some letters.</p>
<p>Wednesday 29 November 1916<br/>In line Squadron all day.  Had to wear our gear and have horses saddled for the whole day and night.<br/>Got photo taken.<br/>Did half Dick's picquet at night.<br/>On telephone from 6 a.m. till 10.</p>
