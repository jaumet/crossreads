 1915 June 17 Thu. Wrote letters in the afternoon &ndash; Have nearly every afternoon clear now as get all work practically done during the morning. Took Commissioner Stock into Cairo in the evening &amp; showed him round the city &ndash; he was interested in the strange people &amp; their curious customs. We are all just about full of Egypt &amp; anxious to get a "bit nearer". Perhaps we shall as it is too hot for wounds to heal properly &amp; quickly.
