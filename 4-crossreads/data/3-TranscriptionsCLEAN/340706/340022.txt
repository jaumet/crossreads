
68at HQ 10 minutes late was told that Major wanted to see me. This put the wind up & more when on asking for CO orderly asked me if I had had my breakfast. I thought CO had got to know that I had been away without leave. However it was to act as interpreter. One of our men being accused of stealing from train - the same who had rushed into mess previous night - & french police wished to interrogate him -
After this went on duty as orderly officer. Sky is peculiar a greenish yellow & we hurry off guard in fear of hail but none came.
28-2-19 (Friday)[indecipherable] has interfered & stopped my going to Rouen but Redfern rights matter. Get Melhuish for an orderly - & await end of day but have not yet asked permission to go & may be thrown back till Sunday yet.
29-2-19Received permission to start & got away by 11.15 train Little Melhuish is almost invisible with his kit 3 blankets, helmet rifle & my pack He reminds of the old cartoon in Punch of the father who would only hear his sons voice out of the mass of material in front of him.
