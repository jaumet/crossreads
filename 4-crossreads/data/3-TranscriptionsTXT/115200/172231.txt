 18)  Bulgaria  
 my opinion left things to late. I hope we get moveing quick as it is our only chance of Success. We have lost a lot of men and it would be awful to think that we had lost them in vain let us all hope that it will not be the case. no Doubt we will have Greece and [Romania?] in with us against Bulgaria and Turkey and I think that the former can Deal with the later allright.
