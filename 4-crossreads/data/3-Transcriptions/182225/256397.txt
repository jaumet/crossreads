
<p>148</p>
<p>OCTOBER &nbsp; THURSDAY 5 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine but came on to rain again in evening. Out with the signallers all day; and Capt Campbell gave the headquarters a parade at night &amp; a bit of practice&nbsp;<s>at night</s>. Poss, Bill Hunter &amp; I went up to the pictures at night &amp; they were pretty rotten.</p>
