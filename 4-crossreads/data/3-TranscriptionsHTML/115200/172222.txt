<p>9) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Dead mans ridge</p>
<p>see one chap in particular crawling up to our trenches with Bombs. I jumped into the trench and fired. I Don&#39;t think that chap will tell any more tales he was no more than 10 yards from our trench. I reported to the Seargent that The Enemy were coming up&nbsp; But he would have that they were our own men. any how it was not long before every rifle in that trench was [Speaking?] The attack had Been repelled. Soon after I was sent off on a message and when I was returning a Corpral called me over into the Support</p>
