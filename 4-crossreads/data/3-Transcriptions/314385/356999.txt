<p>We eventually arrive at 10 oclock at night at a town called [Ribmont?] which is about 6 miles from Albert where we leave the lorries &amp; are paraded while the officers locate the alloted Billets for the ambulance the fun begins, some other unit has jumped our billit &amp; we have no where to sleep but I slip away &amp; find an upstairs loft filled with infantry so I jams in between a couple of them how the others fare I cannot tell.</p>
