 Very dreary marching across sand.  Accompanied by camel train of 70 camels with provisions.  Had tea 5 pm, bully beef &amp; biscuits. 
 Monday 20/12/15 In the desert, not a tree or growth of any description to be seen.  Infantry trench digging &amp; constructing wire entanglements.  Meals still of bully beef &amp; biscuits.  Easy day, lying about the most of the day.  Few dressings to do. caused while opening tins of bully beef.
