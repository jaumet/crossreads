<p>Friday Jan 18th</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades : theory exam in afternoon but we will not get our papers back till Monday :</p>
<p>Saturday Jan 19th</p>
<p>Fovant : still showery : boys went on the route march : I was exempted by the Dr : wrote in the afternoon : concert at YMCA at night &amp; pretty good :</p>
<p>Sunday Jan 20th</p>
<p>Fovant : wet : church parade in morning : wrote in afternoon &amp; night :</p>
<p>Monday Jan 21st</p>
<p>Fovant : usual parades : got our papers handed back to us &amp; I got 25/30  25/30  &amp; 35/40  total 85/100  so did not do too bad : wrote at night &amp; had a bitter of practice on my "buzzer" :</p>
