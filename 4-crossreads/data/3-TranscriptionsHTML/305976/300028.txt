<p>Thursday July 25<br/>Left Enab 7 pm last night. It was a beautiful moonlight night and the road was clear. The journey only occupied four hours to the foothills as the travelling was all down hill &amp; the road in good order. We are moving tonight to Wadi El Neins[?] near Richon.<br/>Friday July 26<br/>We moved last night about 7 pm. It was a beautiful night. We arrived at our old camp site which I had not seen since March. just on daybreak, Today I put up my bivvy and am looking forward to a good spell here.</p>
