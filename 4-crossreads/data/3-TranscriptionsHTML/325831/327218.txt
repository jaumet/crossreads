<p>5.<br/>place for surf bathing.</p>
<p>At night it is all lit up with various coloured lights &ndash; very pretty. Mixed bathing of course.</p>
<p>It is a very expensive town. Lately they have had thousands of troops passing through &ndash; New Zealanders &amp; Australians, all of which must be good for trade.</p>
<p>There is a great shortage of Literature on board &ndash; so I laid in a supply. How long it will last I don't know, for the borrowing on board of every article is <u>very bad</u>.</p>
<p>We have of course had the usual amount of sickness &ndash; colds, measles &amp; influenza, &amp; two or three cases of Meningitis.  I have not yet visited the doctor, &amp; hope I never shall. You never know what the Medical gents mat discover; and</p>
