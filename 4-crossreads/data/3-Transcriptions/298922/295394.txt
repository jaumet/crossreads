<p>Friday, 9 March 1917</p>
<p>Called on Mrs. Cuth Fetherston Hough but did not seen Cuth &ndash; Mrs. Cuth is rather a foolish woman (talks too much).</p>
<p>Old Pearse has been running the Comforts Fund &amp; I got 150 Billys for my Squadron out of him.</p>
<p>My address &ndash; MAJOR WEIR 1st L.H. now. Am feeling very fit &amp; well again &ndash; this 3 weeks has done me so much good. Letters Mother, Daisy, WIFE, Jany. 20th. Glad alls well at home. Thanks for Parcel, no doubt following on.</p>
