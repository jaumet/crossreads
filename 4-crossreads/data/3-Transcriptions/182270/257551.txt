
<p>WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 27, 1919.</p>
<p>Cold with SW gale blowing.</p>
<p>Cut out the wethers after breakfast</p>
<p>&amp; took them down to Middle. They</p>
<p>wont start on ewes till Friday</p>
<p>morning so I turned them out</p>
<p>in Point. Tickle lopped in</p>
<p>4D for cattle &amp; later we shifted</p>
<p>cattle into Middle &amp; lopped</p>
<p>for them there.</p>
<p>Linda, My&eacute;e &amp; Hilda were</p>
<p>down burning off.</p>
