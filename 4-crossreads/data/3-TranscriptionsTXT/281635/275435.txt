Out gathering Ivy for decorations this morning.25/12/17 Xmas day &ndash; fairly cold. Had Xmas dinner in YMCA hall tent; being waited on by Officer patients &amp; Nurses. Had Roast Beef, Potatoes, Beans, Xmas Pudding (Plum) Stout &amp; Beer Nuts &amp; Fruit &ndash; Colonel carved. Whist drive in afternoon but did not go &ndash; first prize was Gillett Sfty Razor. Tea laid out in ward consisting of jelly fruit trifle etc all mixed up, biscuits etc.
