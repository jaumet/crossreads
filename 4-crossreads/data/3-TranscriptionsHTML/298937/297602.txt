<p>Railways is very good to get on with.<br/>He is a splendid worker &amp; always doing more than his share of work to avoid under doing it.<br/>The two smallest men are of a size &amp; consequently called the twins;  one is a Scotch man rather quiet &amp; slow &amp; the other is a typical bushman, with very red brown face &amp; hard voice &amp; a good heart.<br/>Lastly excepting myself is Monsieur Mony a Frenchman.  He had a French University education &amp; passed his barrister's degree, but, being too young to practice</p>
