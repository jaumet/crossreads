<p>13 January 1918 Sunday<br/>Khirbet Deiran<br/>No Transport till 2 o'clock 4 inches rain fell &amp; one of the coldest nights I ever experienced-<br/>This evening in the wind I put my Bivy up it got torn to pieces I am now in a dugout &amp; hope no Bronchitis will result-  Joe's leather waistcoat saved me from Pneumonia because water will not go through it-<br/>Feed is growing &amp; soon the wild flowers will be blossoming again-  The Bedouins are busy putting in their Crops.</p>
