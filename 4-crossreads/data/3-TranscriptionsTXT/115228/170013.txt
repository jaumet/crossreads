Nov 18th:- Moved on to within 2 1/2 miles of Jaffa, the New Zealand Brigade having already entered the town & taken up positions beyond. Jaffa out of bounds to us unfortunately.
Nov 19th:- Raining all day.
Nov 20th:- Outpost duty last night, terribly wet & cold & miserable.
Nov 21st:- Our Brigade was relieved by the 2nd Brigade & we moved back a few miles (about 12 miles today).
Nov 22nd:- Moved back a further six to eight miles
