Wrote to J.N., M.P., B.C., & Home. A bit of a scrap over the organ in the night but there will be nothing doing till we leave Fremantle.
Thursday 30.12.15 (8)Sea very calm & are making up for lost time. We came up with Rott Nest Island about 12.30 pm. It is a long narrow island about 41/2 miles along. We arrived at Fremantle at 5.30 & went ashore at 7.30. straight to Post Office & some sent Telegrams. Had some Ice Cream & a walk round. Very clean little city laid out in squares. Fruit all sold
(Rott Nest Island. German  Camp).
