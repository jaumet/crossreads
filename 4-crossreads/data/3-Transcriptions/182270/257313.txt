<p>WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 1, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. With Alan all day. We</p>
<p>buzzed about in the morning &amp;</p>
<p>went out to see Cam Williams</p>
<p>in the afternoon, but he was out.</p>
<p>Alan &amp; I went to the Lang&#39;s for</p>
<p>afternoon tea &amp; then back to</p>
<p>Hatchetts for dinner.</p>
