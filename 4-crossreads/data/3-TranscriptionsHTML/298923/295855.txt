<p>8 July 1918 Monday<br/>We had been warned to expect an attack today but it did not come off only an enemy plane over<br/>Blood test very simply taken- prick tip of finger &amp; smear a slide<br/>Have not heard of Nowell Forth for some time   His crowd are getting Horses in lieu of Camels.<br/>There are still a good many Partridge around here notwithstanding the shelling &amp; <u>they do drink</u> whatever the "Field" may say about the home birds.</p>
