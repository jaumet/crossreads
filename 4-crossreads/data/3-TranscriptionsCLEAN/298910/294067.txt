 13/10/16  Friday 
 Dull to drizzling rain. Ready to move out from 7 am. New Zealanders coming in all day relieving F. Amb never turned up till late - so we all had to stay back & introduce them to Saps etc. we had to do a bit of a perish as we had sent all our belongings in. Only one came through the night, a N.Z. who had been all thro the Somme. All these men have come from the Somme - Rumour has it that we are to make for the Somme. 
 14/10/16  Saturday 
 Dull & cold - Not relieved till midday - by the N.Z. Fld Amb - Our Motor Cars took us to where the rest of the Ambulance are billeted in Estaires - I had a good old hot bath & were allowed the freedom of Estaires - Thompson, Westwood & I went to the Tommys Divisional Picture Show & really enjoyed it, as it was such a change - a few Tommy Artists - Conjuring etc. Some of the lads made it a welter late at night & told the Colonel off when he tried to quieten them.
