<p>November 1915<br/>Tuesday 2<br/>Cold wind. artillery duel this morning &amp; evening. Bombardment going on at Cape Helles</p>
<p>Wednesday 3<br/>Fine   Grand day. Wrote a few letters. Had a look round the firing line. One man in B. Coy of our Bn. hit with a piece of a shell from a high explosive. He was taken to the Beach Hospital. Had a game of cards with some friends in the M. Gun Section.</p>
