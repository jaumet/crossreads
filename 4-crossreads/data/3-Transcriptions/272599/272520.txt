<p>1917 Thursday 13 Sept.</p>
<p>In to Boulogne with Jock.  Cinema.   Call at Tailors for my tunic, took it to be altered slightly.<br/>Wanted to change 15 frs.   Refused to pay it &amp; left tunic there.   Exorbitant price &amp; an English firm too.   He got his character detailed to him by me.<br/>Bought Hugo's French Course   Good issue of pr. books from Q.M.</p>
