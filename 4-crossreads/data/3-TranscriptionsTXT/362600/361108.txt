 20 Sun Dull &amp; [indecipherable] rain. Recd. parcel &amp;amp letter from Doris &amp;amp Parcel from Dulce. (Tea &amp;amp Sugar) we play cards all night. 
 Wrote Mum &amp;amp Dad 
 F 21 Mon Jim Robby comes over &amp;amp we sit &amp;amp chat by the fire all night. I go walk as far as [Shing?] Y.M.C.A. Recd letter from Doris &amp;amp White &amp;amp Roy. We have supper at Miss Peak's Home. 
 F 22 Tues. Lovely Day but very cold all night. Sent off P.C.s to Jess. Dulce. Maisie. Ruby. Edie. Bert. Nancy Niness. Muriel. Eilleen. Aunt Fanny. Ida.  Mrs [Styshen?]. Jessie Walker Lottie. Peg. Mrs McBuike. Nette. Mrs Merrett. Atto. Jim Robby [indecipherable]  Au revoir 
 F 23 Wed Lovely Day. Go to Dance but feeling very sick &ndash; too ill to dance to second half. Lovely show nevertheless we have a royal time. Recd letter from Doris &amp;amp Ethel Wheelhouse. Tele. from M. sent one. 
 F 24 Th Go to Dance in Y.W.C.A. Very good time Ruth [Preent?]. Make a mess of music for [indecipherable]. Do not dance very much for I'm very tired 
 F 25 Fri Nothing doing. Go down Flat &ndash; straighten gear up. Play cards &amp;amp have supper at camp fire etc. 
 Wrote Mr Love 
 Dull 26 Sat Play Hockey we win 3 &ndash; 1. I am very stiff. Walk D'ton stay an hour. Hurry back &amp;amp go to Dance. Only one dancer. Playing [indecipherable]  &amp;amp [indecipherable].
