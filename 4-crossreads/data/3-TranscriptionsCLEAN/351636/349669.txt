  
 The Centre for Soldiers Wives & Mothers 92B Pitt St Sydney 22.10.15 
 Dear Mr. Layton I am sending Mrs. Sullivan to you to see if you can give her some help. 
 She is the wife of a Reservist of the R. Irish - & her allowance has been cut down 7/- per wk. & is short of any money to go on with till paid again. on Nov 1st. 
 Yours sincerely E. H. Bennie -
