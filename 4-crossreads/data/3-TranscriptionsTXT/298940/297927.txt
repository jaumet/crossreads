
Easter Saturday 30 March 18This morning Walter & I struck Sanitary fatigue, which involved shovelling incinerated tins about on a heap under the impression that we were levelling it. Unfortunately (for the fatigue) the boxers were training in a ring established near the scene of our labours, and we watched the skipping and boxing antics more than we shovelled.
