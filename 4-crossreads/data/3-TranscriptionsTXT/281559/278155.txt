 Tuesday Dec 17th London 
 roam round in morn &amp; afternoon : went to "Box of Tricks" at night &amp; it was pretty good &amp; very laughable : caught the 11.30 pm train from Euston to Glasgow arriving about 10 am on 
 Wednesday Dec 18th Glasgow 
 had a clean up &amp; went to Paisley : made very welcome by Uncle Auntie &amp; Mary : company after tea &amp; very interested in my souvenirs 
 Thursday Dec 19th Paisley 
 roam during day &amp; seen the big cotton mills : Gil came along &amp; is also stopping here : seen over a ship building yard &amp; it was very interesting : play at night &amp; pretty good : 
 Friday Dec 20th Paisley 
 strolled round in day &amp; pictures at night 
 Saturday Dec 21st Paisley 
 roamed into Glasgow &amp; seen the U boat : company at night including "cousins" Nellie &amp; Daisy : good fun 
 Sunday Dec 22nd Paisley 
 went to Loch Lomond with Aunt &amp; Uncle : bad day snow &amp; rain so we didn't see much : however it must be a glorious place in summer time 
 Monday Dec 23rd Paisley 
 roam round in day " went with Gil to Daisy &amp; her mate's flat
