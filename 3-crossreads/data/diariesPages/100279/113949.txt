From Bn Hqrs we had one of the most trying marches as I have ever tramped on, it was about 6 miles if ever men were knocked out by the time we reached Bendigo "Camp" well we were, we all got into huts had a bit to eat simply threw ourselves down went to sleep tired right out Next morning we rose early put
