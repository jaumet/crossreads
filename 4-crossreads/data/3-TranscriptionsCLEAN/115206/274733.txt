
June 1918Wednesday 12
Battery W.Lines.  near Pont NoyellesWeather dull and dismal.Fritz shelled presumably after balloon but without anyresult. A few lobbed ahead of W. Lines. H.V. 4.  H.E.Laid up throughout day did not do my shift last night.Feeling rotten on Strength of Trench fever. Percy Gray being 103 degrees. Myself 102 degrees, nottoo dusty.Can't admit any tendency on my part to do any ragtime dancing as I am at present.
