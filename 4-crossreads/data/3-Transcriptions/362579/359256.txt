<p>3rd Aust C.C.S.<br/>B.E.F.<br/>France<br/>20.1.17</p>
<p>My dear Mother   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>Last night I got two letters from home. Alice's 20th Nov   &amp;  Belle's 27th, You cannot imagine how upset I was to hear of poor little Eddy's illness. I do hope the darling was not very ill   &amp;  has quite recovered. What an anxious time it must have been for  you all.
