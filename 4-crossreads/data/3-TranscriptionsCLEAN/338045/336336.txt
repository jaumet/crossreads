  
 1916 adjutant.  Very tired & bed early.  Expenses, Tea etc. 1f., Cigs. 8c., Sundries 3c. = 2f.10c. 
 Oct. 24th - Tuesday Raining all day & as a consequence, not so cold.  Parading all the morning in mud & rain but afternoon in the tents.  No duty.  Inspected in full marching order by the O.C.  No pay.  Sergts' mess meeting at 6-30 p.m. & elect new office bearers.  Not feeling too well & turn in at 8-30 p.m.  Expenses 3frs. 
 Oct. 25th - Wednesday. Rumours of moving camp to Le Havre.  Raining all day & keep to our tents after two parades.  Usual routine of line duty.  No news yet of moving to the front (no drafts going).  Paid & received 70fr. (2-10-2).  Also 5 francs from David.  Bed early.
