<p>Its purely a question now of who is going to starve first.  The allies or the enemy.  Address my letters to France as before, for I will be back there by the time your reply comes. Hope you are somewhat reconciled to Junee by this time, and are not too fretful for the surf.</p>
<p>Best wishes from<br/>Your affect. son<br/>George</p>
<p>Love to Delphine if you see her.</p>
