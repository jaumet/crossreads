Both Bill and I enjoyed it. It was a far better Coy than we gave them credit for at first. After the show we went to a Fish & oyster saloon called The Dive & had a feed & then went home to bed. 
 Saturday. 19.10.18 Got up about 9.30 & did the usual (shave, bath & brekker) & then went down to the brewery arriving there about 11am. Got a book on the place & a guide was provided we went all over the place. Met Reg Downey & his wife there & they came over with us. Gee it is a magnificent place & covers acres. Think it marvellous. They have all sorts of brainy ideas for saving labor.
