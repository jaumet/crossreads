<p>2/4/17<br/>I was tried by the Comidant I told him the position and he said that no man can be crimed to not telling them what crimes we had had before so for the sake of not making a fool of the Sargent Major he gave me three days CC.</p>
<p>3/4/17<br/>I was classefied by the doctor and marked B2 A that means a good spell  I have been marked for six months home</p>
