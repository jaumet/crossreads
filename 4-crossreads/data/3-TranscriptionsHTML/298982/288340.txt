<p>Some demonstration at 3.30 by our front as a general attack was taking place on left flank in day light Heavy firing on left all noon &amp; night. The bombing by our men on Quinns seemed specially good. Absence of machine gun fire noticeable. 29th Div I believe it is have landed on left also 5th Aust reinforcements.</p>
<p>Sun 22 Before breakfast did Walkers saps &amp; firing line Courtneys &amp; main sap. Former of last two very unsanitary.</p>
<p>Snipers had two goes at me early. One close. I am pleased with notice re saps</p>
