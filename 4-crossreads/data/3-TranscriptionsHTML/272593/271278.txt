<p>one another as if they were the best of friends, one of their Sergts is getting a Court Martial for going over to their lines &amp; coming back drunk. I can easily believe it for it is the German policy to let quiet alone. <br/>On the way back our guns opened up a terrific bombardment on our right somewhere about "Grease Trench" the bursting shells looked very pretty as they flashed purple, gold, &amp; fiery red on the frosty horizon, I think the snow white ground made it show up more distinctly.<br/>Didnt we get a shock coming back going up we kidded ourselves we could walk the frozen ground nicely but I think the dew had settled on the steel like ground which made it absolutely dangerous to walk on. I was getting on nicely</p>
