<p>June. 4th<br/>Sabbath- Mass 7.30 am- news of naval engagement- Reported German Victory Easy day.<br/>June 5th.<br/>British Victory off Jutland- Wet &amp; miserable weather- I take part in concert at Y.M.C.A. "Australian Corroboree"-<br/>June 6th.<br/>Pay-day- Another journey to Bois-Grenier for a week- D.R. duties- Fierce &amp; prolonged British bombardment- Aerial activity.</p>
