 22 June 1915 Tuesday 
 Nothing to report Several men hit by shrapnel at ASC Deport in Rest Gully. A battery of the City of Glasgow Territorial Artillery land with 5 inch Howitzers 
 1915 June 23 Wednesday 
 Very easy day hardly any firing both parties seem to be resting on their laurels   &amp;  waiting for the other to make the next move Enemy fire a few shells over but do no damage
