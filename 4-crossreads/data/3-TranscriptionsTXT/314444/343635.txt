 Thomas Hedley Hill enlisted end of Jan. 1915 at Victoria Barracks, Sydney &ndash; Trained at Liverpool &ndash; at landing &amp; evacuation of Gallipoli one of the last to leave the Peninsula &ndash; Wounded there &ndash; Went to France &amp; died of wounds at Paaschendaele 
 Presented by Mr. A. H. W. Hill Euterpe, Kurraba Rd Neutral Bay.
