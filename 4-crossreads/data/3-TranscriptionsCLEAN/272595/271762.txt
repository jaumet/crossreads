  
 Rain seems to have set in this evening & it is blowing & howling outside a treat, but I am quite comfortable sitting in our well appointed billiard room in a nice easy chair beside a good coal fire, I am just counting the days now till we get our trip home to "Aussie."  I see the 1st & 4th Divisions have just put up another splendid stunt & the papers are giving our splendid troops great praise, I wonder how Len is getting on he will be in all this heavy fighting. 21st.
