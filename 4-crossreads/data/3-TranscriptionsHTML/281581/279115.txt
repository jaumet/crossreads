<p>21st June to 10th July /18</p>
<p>As this gap indicates, very little of change or importance has transpired. I have neither visited nor taken further interest in the exhibition and am so embittered against the High Commissioner's office, that relations have been entirely broken off.</p>
<p>I am now negotiating with a Mr. Mar tin, late of the Fraser Films of Sydney and my old friend. Fred Gent, of the same firm, London, to form a small syndicate to exploit the Mawson, Shackleton, and Scott film throughout Australasia.
