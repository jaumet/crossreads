
March, 1917. Wednesday 28No pumping as only a little water was taken. Carried another sack of wood in aftn. and visited Savage in even. Better rations with 4 fire buckets wrote letters in intervals. Rumours of german concealed mine blowing up 60 Australians & 7 german spies as civilians caught. Day fairly fine with squalls of rain. Heavy gun firing close here. Evidently on train.
