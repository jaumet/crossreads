 1918   Feb & March 
 28 THUR  Arr Lime St 6.20 am & home 
 at 7.30 am Raining. Writing 
 letters. posted same to Julie 
 Hortense. Else P.  & P.C. to L Berry. 
 Visiting friends at night 
 1 MARCH FRI  Fine day. blowing 
 hard. Went to Shakespeare to see 
 Marriage of Figaro. 
 2 SAT  Rec. P.C from L Berry. Went 
 to see match Lpool v. Stoke. crowded 
 gate. good game. Went to 
 Olympia to see G Formby in 40 
 Thieves. very good. Maggie 
 came home. Mild night
