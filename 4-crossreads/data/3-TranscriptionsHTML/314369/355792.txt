<p>4 March 1915<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>The first serious case of sickness brought into hospital Find it is appendicitis. Make signals to Seang bee about 2 pm for their doctor. Bee does not respond until we send up rockets about 6.30 we come together about half an hour later<br/>The operation is successful.</p>
<p>1915 march 5<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Getting very close to Equator now Weather Horribly hot. There is not a breath of wind Sea like a mirror not even a swell on.</p>
