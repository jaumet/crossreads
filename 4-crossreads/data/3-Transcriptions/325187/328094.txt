<p>Decber. 17th   [-This page is written in a different hand]<br/>Ten of our planes came along today so things should be exciting in the Air-<br/>Turks dropped a message saying "Thank you for building the Railway we will take delivery of it on Janry 6th"<br/>No service today-<br/>Decber 18th [Monday] I am always afraid something will crop up to make me lose these notes so will post today- Alls well-</p>
