
Naval Signal
From - Berrima
To - Flag
Date - 11 9 4
Time - 5.15
Commander Stevenson considers it inadvisable to go to Kaba Kaul tonight, as could not return here before dark.  Propose leaving here 5.15 tomorrow & will commence attack from both Herbertshoe and Kaba Kaul immediately you have shelled as arranged .  Propose landing stores tonight for Herbertshohe garrison
