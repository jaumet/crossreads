<p>[Wednesday 16 August 1916]</p>
<p>As young Rochefort is going to Australia tomorrow I'll send these notes with [indecipherable] he will deliver-<br/>He has been with this Hospital a long time Young Allanson is here slightly wounded through the leg, nothing serious &amp; he will soon be out again.<br/>Have no idea when I shall be sailing for England probably in a week-<br/><strike>Address c/- Laura please<br/>Love to all</strike></p>
