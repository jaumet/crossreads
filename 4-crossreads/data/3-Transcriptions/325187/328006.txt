<p>[Wednesday 20 September 1916]</p>
<p>1.30 Lunch Lady Reid.<br/>Went for a lovely Motor drive around Country parts of Lancashire.<br/>Cold better.<br/>Just got word that Nowell made me an Honorary member of the Junior Army &amp; Navy Club 96 Piccadilly- very good of him.<br/>Wound &amp; Ribs quite alright again but Im soft &amp; "Short a gallop."</p>
