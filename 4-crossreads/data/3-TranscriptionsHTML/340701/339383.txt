<p>At Breakfast this morning one of the Peninsula men said that their Seargent of the M.G.S. had to mix Phenol with the water for the machine Guns to prevent the men from stealing it   also he said that firing at Aeroplanes spoiled the Guns.</p>
<p>22-5-16<br/>More artillery firing today.  My Watch cost me 30 P.T. Today  it is becoming an expensive luxury.  Received letter from A. Gales.  Answered Arthur's letter this evening.  The artillery firing was really Bombs.</p>
<p>23-5-16<br/>Seen the Bombs School practicing today<br/>Sent a Cable to Mollie on the 22-5-16</p>
