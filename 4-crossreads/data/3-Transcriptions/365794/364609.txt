<p>Friday 27 [26] July 1917 [1918]<br/>Finish moving my goods into single room &amp; very enjoyable is the fact of being alone once again although room is noisy being just next kitchens of main house.<br/>Went town 5 p.m. &amp; bought picture, Pt. 65, one of Jacope's.<br/>Coins given by shop girl (opposite Shepheards).<br/>Have now arranged to have all linen washed including sheet &amp; p. slip &amp; towl for Pt. 14 per wk.<br/>Our boys play many tricks to break hosp.  One A.W.L. quietly asked Cpl. on guard at 5.45 p.m. if too late to see visitors? &amp; so got safely in.<br/>Others make up figures &amp; lay out on couches to trap women doctors if possible.  All pts. to be in bed 9 p.m,.</p>
