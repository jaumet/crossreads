<p>January 5th &amp; 6th<br/>Snowing steadily, the countryside looks grand in its white covering &amp; the boys have taken to snowballing &amp; guy making as if they had been brought up to it from childhood of course a percentage of Australian troops, about 20% I think, were reared in some part of Great Britain, so what the Australian natives don't know they can rapidly pick up and I must say that snowballing is an exhilarating, healthy pastime.
