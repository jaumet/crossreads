 17th 2nd Reinforcements arrived at Post &amp; begin trench Duty. 
 18th Saturday Another small mail in early, 2 Parcels from Lindfield.  U/load Duties very easy.  Very sharp attack with Artillery &amp; Rifle fire from Turks about sunset &frac12; hour. 
 19th Sunday Things very quiet on Post all day.  Wrote letters. 
 20th Monday Quiet.
