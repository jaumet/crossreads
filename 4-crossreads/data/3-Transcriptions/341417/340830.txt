<p>Give us fresh horses we have had one good long night's sleep &amp; we are better now than a month ago. The second day we fought in a hot sun on one bottle of brackish water &amp; nothing to eat from mid-day the day before. This on top of 17 days skirmishing is a good performance.<br/>I am writing to Mrs Stribley telling her what arrangement is being made re the cash I am holding.<br/>Yrs affcty<br/>R. A. L. Macdonald</p>
<p>[Lieutenant MacDonald was killed in action the following day.]</p>
