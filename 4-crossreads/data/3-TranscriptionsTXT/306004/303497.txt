 24.8.16 Thurs 24th On account of our horses being innoculated against glanders we were not allowed to ride them yesterday, today a party from B &amp; C Sqdn went swimming; Got my teeth back from the dentists this afternoon; Last night Ted Oates &amp; Maybury came over &amp; paid me a visit, it is rumoured we are to go to Bir-El-Abd at the end of this month to relieve the 3rd Brigade, a concert was held in the YMCA tonight &amp; was one of the best I have heard; Renaldi was sent into Port Said a few days ago to get goods for the Officers mess &amp; was held there on account of Cholera, but managed to get back today.
