
25 May 1918SaturdayNothing of importance to note. Still carrying on in the Canteen job.
1918 May 26ThursdayJust the same old Camp Sunday. In the afternoon LCp Trevena, Sap Brown and I went for a walk out onto the Dinton Rd & on to Barford. Saw an old church there part of which dates back to Magna charter.
