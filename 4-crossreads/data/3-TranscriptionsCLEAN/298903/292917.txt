 Sunday 13thour hottest day, the perspiration simply poured from us.  At 6AM we reached Colombo & a storm working up the air became cooler so we will perhaps have a decent night even if we do commence coaling.  Went to Communion in the morning & had a good Song service in the evening. 
 Monday 14th At Colombo.  We commenced coaling & taking on water but at a leisurely pace so there is apparently no hurry.  The whole crowd were given leave for the day, the announcement being received with loud cheers, & at 8.30 A.M. 10 large barges came alongside upon which we flocked & were slowly towed ashore.
