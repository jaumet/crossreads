Entered Magdalen College and had a good look round the beautiful cloisters of its secluded interior. Being winter time the creepers have no leaves ? when all is green these walls and towers must present a lovely sight. The Chapel dates from 1480 and is very lovely with a stone reredos. Some cadets were drilling in the garden the paths to river side and the walks were not open. Passed University College All Souls College without entering and next pulled up at the Church of St. Mary the Virgin. All turrets pinnacles and a high tower. The door has twisting stone supports surmounted by a statue
