<p>Yesterdays order was a bit out as it is now dark &amp; no land in sight. Have put our blankets &amp; hammocks away &amp; have kit bags &amp; equipment out all ready for landing Sea rough Passed 5 small steamers</p>
<p>Saturday 25th<br/>Slept in the Orderly room so was better off than a good many but spent rather an uncomfortable night not being used to the floor &amp; having no blankets. Land was in sight at daylight &amp; by 7 oclock we were anchored off Plymouth Every boat even little <strike>sail</strike> fishing one seem to be armed &amp; mostly have wireless Destroyers seem to be every where Saw a seaplane for the first time No time was lost (considering we are in the army) in disembarking One big barge took the lot &amp; landed us right at the train. It was a great for all old hands &amp; new &amp; if it</p>
