
Thursday 11 February 1915Ordinary days work.Surgeon General Williams here between 3 & 4 p.m. Looks as well as ever he did, even thirty years ago.Letters from Car [Caroline Nash], Joseph [Josephine Nash], Kitty [Kathleen Nash], Doffie & Mr Clarence Bridge. Walk around excavations at base West of great pyramid.
Friday 12 February 1915rdinary duties during the day. Prof Watson left a card for me.Walked around the excavations during the evening.Letters
Saturday 13 February 1915Ordinary duties.Op. appendicectomy during at 3-45 p.m. Wrote to Car, Joe & Kitty
Sunday 14 February 1915Mass at Mena House 6.30 a.m. Attended Work all day  Walk over sands during 5-30 to 6-15 pm.
