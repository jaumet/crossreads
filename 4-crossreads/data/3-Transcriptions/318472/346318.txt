<p>3 April 1918<br/>Wednesday<br/>Left Edinburgh about 1. P.M. &amp; went to Glasgow In the afternoon Dr Eddy &amp; I went to the Municipal Building to have a look at them. It is a beautiful Building. Saw my friend Marie Cameron there. In the evening went went to Govan to see some people named Boyd. They proved to be very nice Sociable &amp; homely people. Left Glasgow about 10. P.M.</p>
<p>1918 April 4<br/>Thursday<br/>Arrived Euston about 10. a.m. Went to Leytonstone about mid day. Left Waterloo about 6. P.M. Arrived Hurdcott about 9.30 P.M. The end of a very pleasant Furlough especially as regards my visit to Scotland.</p>
