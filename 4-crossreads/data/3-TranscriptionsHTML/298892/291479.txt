<p>position. This hours drill in the early morning is by far the most enjoyable in the day.<br/>We received our pay to-day for the last three weeks  viz. &pound;5.5.0. Not too bad is it.<br/>I got last week end off and went in to town and stayed with Sharpe's. On Saturday night Stirling, Stuart, Maggie, Douglas &amp; myself went to the theatre. An American play - called "Kick in" was the attraction. It wasn't much good but I am enclosing portion of the programme containing a glossary of American slang terms which I know will interest Kit and Douglas. I hope there not too populars.<br/>With regard to your Christmas trip I think it would do you all good to have a run down. I would willingly supply &pound;20 towards it. I am not</p>
