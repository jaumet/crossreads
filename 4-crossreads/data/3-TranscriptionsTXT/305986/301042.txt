 And then theres all that saddle soap And soap of common kind You know they both are waste of cash To any frugal mind The cheese that we are issued with When things are as I hope, Would do to wash our hands &amp; face And do away with soap. 
 Then why waste all that ration jam, With fancy names galore, When axle grease would do for both And save a little more, Or put some poison in it too And add a little fat &ndash; And we could use it very well In killing off the chat.
