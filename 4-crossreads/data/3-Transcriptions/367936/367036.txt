<p>Thursday 7 September 1916<br/>On as day stableman all day.  Read about six Stock &amp; Station Journals.  West went to Cantarah to load trucks.</p>
<p>Friday 8 September 1916<br/>Ayre and I down swimming.  Got a parcel from Stella.  On picquet at night.</p>
