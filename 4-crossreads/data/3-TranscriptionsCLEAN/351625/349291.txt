  
 France 9 June 18 
 Dear Mum 
 As I have time on my hands at present I will drop you a line.  You will be surprised to hear I am away from the Batt. at last but I don't think it will be for long, a week or so most likely.  I developed very sore eyes after coming out of the line last time & as they seemed to be getting worse after a week or so I went & saw the doctor & was sent away immediately to the Casualty Clearing Station where I have anchored.
