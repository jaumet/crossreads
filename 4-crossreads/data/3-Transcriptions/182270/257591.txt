
<p>MONDAY, OCTOBER 6, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot, came up cloudy but</p>
<p>got nothing out of it. Lopped</p>
<p>in Top paddock in morning.</p>
<p>Shot Robin &amp; Tommy &amp; skinned</p>
<p>them in afternoon. Took Sulky</p>
<p>up to Hill paddock in afternoon</p>
<p>&amp; the horse there covered her.</p>
<p>Tickle drawing water to Darby</p>
<p>Darby cased the bore to the</p>
<p>bottom 109 ft 9 in; he had no</p>
<p>water at lunch time.</p>
