  
 19-1-17 Fri 19th Nothing out of the ordinary happened till after tea tonight & then we received orders to be ready to move out at 8.30 tomorrow morning; 
 It is rumoured that the New Zealanders are at present getting 7 days leave & that we are also to get it in a few weeks time.
