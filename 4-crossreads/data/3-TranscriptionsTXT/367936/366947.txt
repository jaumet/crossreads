 Monday 13 March 1916 Dick working disc Harrows.  Geoff took seed drill.  Father &amp; I sifted lucerne seed. Cut chaff in afternoon.  Father took honey from bees. 
 Tuesday 14 February 1916 Mother &amp; I drove to Mudgee.  Saw Dr. Nicholl.  He won't let me go back for a fortnight as my lung is not quite healed. Dick &amp; Geoff working in 40 acres.
