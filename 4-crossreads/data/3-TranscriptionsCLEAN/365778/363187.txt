  
 Friday 6.4.17 Good Friday snowing heavily, went to Eltham with Mr Gray & played Billiards. Saturday 7/4/17 Lovely spring day, went again to Eltham & then returned to London at about 6 pm. Had dinner with Sir Thomas & Lady Robinson from Qld. Sunday 8/4/17 Met "George" Smith went to The Tower with him, had Albert Hall concert, afternoon tea & Trocadero & went to picture show. Monday 9/4/17 Went to Horseferry Rd for 1 day's extra leave, went to Cheereo with George in afternoon. Zig Zag at night. [At Hippodrome Theatre]
