<p>June 25 Pr'aps it will go on to Australia. Went &amp; had 6' worth of Church, in the pit. The School Kiddies, teachers &amp; others sit in the gallery. Good organ and choir &amp; not a bad Parson.<br/>Jolly nice gardens here; plenty of shady spots &amp; any amount of girls. Met Fred again &amp; wandered around for an hour or so. Kit bag arrived<br/>[in margin] Sunday</p>
