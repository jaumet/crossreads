<p>with all my kit in company with Lts. Meehan and Carrick for a little town on the main road where we were to pick up a bus for Charleroi. We found that Lt Adams had already arrived so we all jumped aboard a Motor lorry &amp; were driven to Charleroi. We left our kits a the station and had a lunch at the Expeditionary Force Restaurant then had a stroll around the town after which it was time to catch our trains for Brussells as we had made up our minds</p>
