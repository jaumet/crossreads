<p>beautiful morning.  Large number of Albatros following the ship, very peculiar bird, never leave only when approaching land.  Visited engine room, machinery very fine, though temperature below very warm.  Physical drill for morning parade.</p>
<p>Tuesday 16/11/15<br/>Very calm. Mess orderly as well as Orderly sick parade.  Had a little practice in dispensary, very interesting.  Passed five islands off West Australian coast about midday.  Rained heavily during afternoon.  Passed Hospital Ship. Kyarra about 4.30 in afternoon.  Very bad head, went bed early.</p>
