 WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 5, 1919. 
  Fine Snowing all day &amp; as cold 
 as charity. About town 
 in morning. Met Major Glover 
 &amp; Eric MacKellar &amp; we went 
 up the city to lunch. I came 
 back &amp; sat by the fire all the 
 afternoon. Alan &amp; I went to 
 Buzz Buzz again at night
