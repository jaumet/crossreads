
Tuesday, May 1st 1917Nice fine spring day.  A big battle plane flew over in morning.  After tea went to Perham Downs with Mooney for a hot bath.
Wednesday, May 2ndReveille 6.30 a.m. & food still very scarce.  Several more aeroplanes flew over.  Route march in afternoon about 4 miles.  Paid 30/- after tea, then went to the town with Dawson to post some letters.
Thursday, May 3rdStarted at Bombing School this morning, also voted for Federal elections.  Pay Books collected again in morning on account of shortage
