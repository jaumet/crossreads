This magnificent property has been acquired by the L.C.C. &amp; is open for the use of the public.  The grounds are magnificent with beautiful flower gardens &amp; grass lawns.  We had afternoon tea in the grounds &amp; returned home across some fields &amp; thence along the main road.  We had tea about 7.30 &amp; then gathered our baggage for departure.  Mr Newton had taken some snapshots of us during the afternoon &amp; Mrs Newton took my home address &amp; promised to send them on if they proved a success.
