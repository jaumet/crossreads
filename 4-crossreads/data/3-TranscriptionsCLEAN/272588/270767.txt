
Monday 9 SeptemberMove this afternoon to Buire where we are to establish a M.D.S. in bow huts which our people built in 1917 & had to evacuate again. About nine kilos from the line.
Tuesday 10 SeptemberWorking about M.D.S. fixing things up generally for work. The division takes over to-night at midnight & our bearers are already up, with the battalions.
