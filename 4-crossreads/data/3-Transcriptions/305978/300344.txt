<p>at 9 am.&amp; we ought to get to our destination to-night sometime &amp; I can tell you we will not be a little bit sorry as it becomes very dull seeing nothing but sand day after day.</p>
<p>Dec 11th/Saturday<br/>Arrived at out base camp at 5-30 pm. The name of the small town is Matruh.<br/>It took us four days &amp; a night to get here from El Dabba the Railhead.<br/>We soon set to work &amp; got the horse lines down &amp; gave the horses a good feed.<br/>A squadron went out patrolling eight miles from here, The aeroplanes</p>
