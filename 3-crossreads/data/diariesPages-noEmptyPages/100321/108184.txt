a fine rising pillar here supporting the tower. Lunched at Kaiser Wilhelm Hotel with the A.F.C and met young Schafer and another. Out in front is a statue of the present Kaiser's father and the irreverent airmen recently crowned him with an appropriate vessel which the Fire Brigade had to remove later on. Walked down once more past the Dom and came down past the station to the Hohenzollern Bridge which is a fine structure. Walked out on it and got our first view of the Rhine. Fine and broad with a swift current her waters were carrying little flots of ice. We walked right over the other side of the Rhine. A dull low mist prevented our view from being a good one.
