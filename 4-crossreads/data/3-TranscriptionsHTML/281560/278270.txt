<p>9/7/16.<br/>Paraded in full marching order at 8 A.M. Marched all the morning covered some 9 miles. We have halted at a billet near Merrio and we march on again tomorrow. Whither we are bound I know not.<br/>10/7/16.<br/>Did not get our blankets last night, the transport that is carrying them has moved on; but it was warm enough in the straw of the barn. Rested here all day waiting for the order to move. It will come some time tonight I suppose. Marched off at 11.P.M. <br/>11/7/16.<br/>Marched 5 miles last night, to the railway at Bailleul. Entrained 40 in a truck. Journeyed for some hours and detrained at a nameless</p>
