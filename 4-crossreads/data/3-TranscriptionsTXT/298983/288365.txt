 23rd January 1918 Have fared well for tucker 5 of us got 4 tins of Bully between us &amp; were able to buy good bread on the Boat 
  Thursday  23rd Wednesday Had a very light breackfast Bacon &amp; Bread at 6 &amp; started right away for the Boat Southampton seems a fine place Went straight on to the Boat &amp; anchored in the Harbour until 7.15 P.M. There are a lot of horses on board
