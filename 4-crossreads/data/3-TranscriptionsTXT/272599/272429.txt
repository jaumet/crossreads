 June Friday 8 1917 
 Coal transport today. 4 of us shift 20 tons, - a new role. coal lumper  Egyptian &amp; Chinese coolies.  Finish 3.45 p.m.  Shower, tea &amp; down to Wimereux blowing.   Great British offensive being carried on near Ypres, Wycheate &amp; Missines.  Wounded beginning to come in, in large convoys operations in the line are always reflected here
