
<p>FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 12, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Barry &amp; Al &amp; I &amp; Graham went</p>
<p>down to rocks early after</p>
<p>foxes. I got one, &amp; it&nbsp;was the only</p>
<p>one got. The girls brought our</p>
<p>lunch down &amp; we had it in the</p>
<p>mountain. Tickle lopped in</p>
<p>Top paddock</p>
<p>Barnes came out &amp; brought Darby&#39;s</p>
<p>tools and 1 ton of chaff for me.</p>
