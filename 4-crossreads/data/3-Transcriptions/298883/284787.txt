<p>7.50   The day has opened up fine again.</p>
<p>We buried the dead germans lying <strike>a</strike> near by and the graves were marked with little wooden crosses and their steel helmets placed at each end.  No names were put on the graves as it was impossible to distinguish one word from another as being names.  How many fallen heroes on either side have thus passed into complete oblivion during this terrible war.</p>
