<p>White D.S.O. as C.O. 36th  Capt. C. Doig second in Com.<br/>Capt Tedder, adjutant.</p>
<p>17th  News of Allied offensive gain of 12 miles on 40 mile front into Alsace.<br/>Made trench cooker. Req. a tin, candles melted down &amp; bag as wicks.</p>
<p>18th  Battalion is now a counter attacking party stationed at V.B. sleeping by day &amp; carrying on at night.  40 including 3 officers were gassed.  It was sent over in shells and the constant replacing of masks carried a little gas</p>
