 43 August 19th (Sunday) Slept in till 11 am &ndash; Played Bridge. 
 I have been appointed as Pres. of our mess being responsible for all Stores &amp;c. Have instituted a Duty Roster in our mess much to the annoyance to some &amp; amusement to others of our mess. 
 Recd 2 letters, one from Staff Paymaster &amp; other no 3 from Bay. Not feeling well my foot bandaged with the usual rotten paper bandages. 
 August 20th Had hot shower &ndash; Sent for by Doctor once again this morning, apparently there has been another alteration in the conditions &ndash; He, the Dr dictated quite a lot of  inform  instructions to the Medical orderly. 
 An issue of food from Red Cross Soc today including Butter Beans, Dates, Bully Beef, Sausages, Army Rations, Cheese &amp; margarine. &ndash; 
 Recd P.C. from German Red Cross referring to Sgt Wynne. &ndash; He is now reported as [a] Prisoner &ndash; Played Bridge, wrote letter no 12 to K, A, &amp;, B. 
 August 21st Recd 5 letters &ndash; no 3 from Annie, PC from Kate, one from Gertie &amp; Gertie Rushton also Aunt Sally &ndash; also recd Pcl from Red Cross &ndash; Over 200 parcels in today &ndash;  Made mess  
 Guest evening today Had tomato soup &ndash; salmon, meat Pudding Date &amp; Jam tart, Date Pudding &ndash; &amp; Coffee I was cook, It was an excellent turnout &ndash; 
 Played bridge &ndash; Debate tonight &ndash; Subject that Kissing be abolished &ndash; 
 A Raid on our Room today by the Germans &ndash; looking for tools &amp;c &ndash; 
 My foot very painful &ndash; 
 Germans are retiring on Western &amp; Italian front &ndash; French having advanced  just 22  7 mile on a 20 mile front &ndash;Peace overtures all over the world &ndash; I think Peace is bound to come soon
