  
 Wednesday 4 July 1917. 
 Usual parades during early morning till 7.30 a.m. Special duties - (stretcher bearing today). Instruction in elementary riding etc. and at  11 p  10.45 a.m. was one of party to water the horses at fresh water canal at back of Ismailia. By time horses were fixed up - groomed etc. & we had marched back to Isolation area it was 2 pm. Parade at 3-30 pm & reallotment of tents. More fooling around & of course in heavy clothes. 
 More N.C.O's from  Gaza  in the field arrive here. 
 Saw Johnson N.A.G today (VIC.) L.H.F.A.
