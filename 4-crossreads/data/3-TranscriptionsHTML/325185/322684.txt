<p>I have a great bundle of papers here. Aunt Nell has been sending the Guardian along also I saw in it where Mrs Charlie Craft had died, I went along to see Norman Tayt last night. They are not far from us. He &amp; another young fellow are sleeping in a dug out they have made it very comfortable too I sat with them for some time we had a game of 500 am going along to see if I can find Harry Cross. The division he is in is here. I hope I can find him cant think of any more just now so will close Hope you have been receiving the letters I have been writing you  You see it takes some time for a letter to get across from here</p>
