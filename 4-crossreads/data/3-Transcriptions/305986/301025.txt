<p>high wind blowing but its not too cold. have just had my dinner Mac's ration and Mud its very hard to get wood for a fire and when you do get some you've got to go some to get one going.  Been sending a few shells over this evening got 4 men 1 killed ( LT Turnbull) others wounded. lines are all down and they can stop down its raining like the big flood and Im stopping right here till morning</p>
<p>24.12.16 Ran the lines again this morning both OK. Fritz has been pretty quiet</p>
