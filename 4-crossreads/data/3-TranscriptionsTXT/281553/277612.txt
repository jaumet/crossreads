 May 24  N.N.W.  M.320 Physical drill as usual.  9-30 Lecture on sleeping on deck and alteration in future parade hours during tropical weather which from now are:-  6-30-7-15, Physical, 9-30-11 a.m., 2-30-4 p.m.  No one allowed to stay below deck between meals except special cases.  Sections lecturing individually on parts of rifle;  aiming;  muscle exercises.  Afternoon lecture on Fire discipline etc. &amp; Landscape targets.  Several sharp showers till 10 a.m., later very nice, beautiful afternoon.  Very warm below deck still.  [See May 25th on Page 25.]
