<p>Thurs Dec 11th:-<br/>297 miles still raining &amp; making things uncomfortable for the women &amp; children owing to the restricted space on the decks.</p>
<p>The few first class passengers have 2/3rds of the ships.<br/>Protest &amp; deputations are unavailing.</p>
<p>Fri Dec 12th:-<br/>Mileage 298. Raining still. Received the King's letter of thanks. Sarcastic remarks prevalent.</p>
<p>Sat 13th Dec:-<br/>Mileage 306 &amp; weather is beginning to clear up.</p>
<p>Sun 14th Dec:-<br/>305 miles of good weather.</p>
<p>Mon 15th Dec:-<br/>315 miles today.</p>
<p>Tues 16th Dec:-<br/>Fancy Dress Ball on deck tonight. Mileage 308.</p>
<p>Wed Dec 17th:-<br/>Getting within "Cooee of Aussie" today's mileage of 309 bringing us within 625 miles of Fremantle.</p>
<p>Thurs Dec 18th:-<br/>Mileage 310 so we are sure to be straining our eyesight tomorrow.</p>
