<p>Friday 16 March 1917.</p>
<p>In camp.</p>
<p>Parade called by C.O. troops at Camp Hd Qrs regarding w-e [week-end] leave for embarkation men.</p>
<p>Afternoon parade in full marching order. Inspection of universal &amp; sea kit.</p>
<p>Paraded for week-end leave &amp; were turned down.</p>
