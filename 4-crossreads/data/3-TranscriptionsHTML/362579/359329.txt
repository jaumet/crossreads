<p>Back to December 28th 1916. So the news is somewhat old when it reaches me. Still we get some interesting reading out of it.</p>
<p>So glad Eddy is growing stronger, poor wee chappie must have been very ill.</p>
<p>Do you see or hear anything of Nurse Ferguson? Give her my love   &amp;  tell I shall write to her now I'm on day-duty. Received two letters from Miss Dixon (Thomas friend) so answered the second. She belong to B.E.F. but would like to join the A.I.F.</p>
<p>Young Cutherbson (Charlie's friend) often comes for Tennis</p>
