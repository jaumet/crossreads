<p>in full marching order this afternoon &amp; wasn't it cold the wind cut like a knife, shortly after we came in a heavy snowstorm set in &amp; continued for an hour or so, things just at present are very wintrified indeed, &amp; tonight we have only 1 blanket each the other having gone in to ordance for we are moving tomorrow.<br/>3rd Rose at the usual time this morning had breakfast &amp; got our platoon blanket's away &amp; the billets cleaned up ready for inspection.<br/>While we were waiting for the rest of the Brigade to clear the town we went in &amp; had a cup of coffee &amp; said goodbye to the old French couple, my word they have been good &amp; kind to us every night they used to make coffee for us, lend us plates &amp;</p>
