<p>(2)<br/>No doubt the circumstances of his death are well known to you so I won't touch on them except to say that on April 5th &ndash; from my battery &ndash; I could see the terrible time they were having &amp; the way they all stuck it &ndash; well it was worthy of a V.C.</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>H.C. de Low<br/>Major</p>
