<p>Sunday 18 November 1917<br/>Left Safiyre with 3rd Regt. &amp; went out on a reconnesence, camped in an olive grove, went back to Ludd to water up, came back to Safiyre &amp; then proceeded to Yazur.</p>
<p>Monday 19 November 1917<br/>Rained heavily in morning, Clarrie came back.  Stayed at Yazur till 4 p.m. and then proceeded on outpost with 3rd Reg. about 5 miles East  Cruel night, rained incessantly.</p>
<p>Tuesday 20 November 1917<br/>Still threatening rain, windy, Regt. doing reconnesence, rained off &amp; on all day, relieved off outpost about 5 p.m. by 1st Regt. came back to Yazur &amp; rejoined S<br/>Squad;  rained heavily, mail in (on picquet).</p>
<p>Wednesday 21 November 1917<br/>Very cold morning, left Yazur 6.30 &amp; proceeded back to Yebna, &amp; stayed the night, cruelly cold night.</p>
<p>Thursday 22 November 1917<br/>Left Yebna at 8.30 and proceeded back to Esdud through Butani.  Camped at Esdud, very chilly put in a cold night.</p>
<p>Friday 23 November 1917<br/>Very chilly morning:  usual routine a good way from water.  Lawson a little lame:  lads came over from 1st to see me.</p>
<p>Saturday 24 November 1917<br/>Chilly morning &amp; night, took horses down for swim to beach:  very good.  Went over to 1st at night.</p>
