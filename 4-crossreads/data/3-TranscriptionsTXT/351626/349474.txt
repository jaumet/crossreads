 Australian Imperial Force Base Records Office, A.I.E.F. Victoria Barracks. Melbourne.  22nd August 1916 Dear Madam, I now beg to advise you that No. 500 Pte. C.R. Sherwin, 6th Light Horse Regiment, has been reported admitted 3rd Australian General Hospital, 11/8/16, suffering from gunshot wound elbow and back (severe) and in the event of further information coming to hand, you will be promptly notified. In the absence of further reports it is to be assumed that satisfactory progress is being maintained. It should be clearly understood that if no further advice is received this Department has no further information to give. Yours faithfully, J.M.Dean Major. Officer i/c Base Records. Mrs L. A.Sherwin "The Moorings", Gibb St.,  Croydon, near Sydney, N.S.W.
