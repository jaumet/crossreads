<p>[December 1918]<br/>5 Th<br/>Nutty the old b&mdash;is having a royal time good grass &amp; stable, he reckons it is just like being at home.<br/>6 Fri<br/>Letters from home ar pleadings of "amour", but that sort of stuff with me is dead a mans mind as been altered with the war<br/>7 Sat<br/>It matters not what men assume to be, good or bad, they are but what they are a statement with a lot of meaning some men think or assume to be wise and clever, and others big things with talented ideas but I (<u>Ca ne fait sien</u>)</p>
