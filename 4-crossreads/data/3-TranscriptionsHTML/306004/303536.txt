<p>26.9.16<br/>Tue 26th<br/>Some of the troops have been away from Camp doing musketry lately &amp; this morning Head Qrs had orders to go, we left here at 9.30 &amp; went down with A Sqdn, after we had finished we went down into a Hod &amp; had a good feed of fresh ripe dates &amp; got back to Camp at 1.30 this afternoon.</p>
