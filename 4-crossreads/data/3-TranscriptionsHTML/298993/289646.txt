<p>Friday May 4.5.17. stand to 6.30 fed up had breakfast had a read pumping water for camels  10AM watered camels had dinner 12.30 reading all afternoon had tea 5.PM turned in 6.30 was woke up in the night sometime two taubes over dropped about 5 bombs a fine moonlight night could hear taube flying very low could not see it<br/>Saturday 5.5.17.<br/>Stand to 6.AM. am having a good rest here all night feeling well had breakfast and a clean up cool morning  went to canteen got some fruit sausages and matches</p>
