<p>Wed 11th September<br/>7-15am, Drew my kit and left Harefield at 9-50am. Got my pass, money, etc at Horseferry Rd, then spent the rest of the day in London. Caught the 10-15pm. train from Kings Cross for Edinburgh</p>
<p>Thurs 12th Sept.<br/>Arrived at Edinburgh 8am. and received a great reception from my relations.</p>
<p>Fri 13th Sept<br/>Paid a visit to the Forth Bridge, Art Gallery and Sir Walter Scotts monument.</p>
<p>Went to the pictures at night, and I was glad to get into bed, as I had been flying all over the place during the day.</p>
<p>Sat 14th Sept<br/>Chumed up with a Canadian Officer, while looking over the Castle, and we had a very enjoyable day together.</p>
