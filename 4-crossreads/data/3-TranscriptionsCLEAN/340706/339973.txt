However at this moment I am quite unconscious of the fact & go forth & visit for 1 fr the Music Anatomique a collection of wax figures & pictures illustrating VD in all its hideousness. I used my French to explain to a party of diggers the import of the explanation especially in a fictitious development of the embryo
Then went on to Musee Archaelogique but it closed at 11.30 am & as it was now 11.20 am it had taken time by the forelock & entry was impossible. I then met two girls & asked my way to the university. They were going to Mons in a car & offered me a trip. The ability to speak French does wonders for a man here.
I visit the university to which is attached a Technical school but do not go through the building minutely as it has recently been a German hospital & there are numerous officers still in occupation there. I then return to cafe Cristal & Cafe La Rose near canal.
