<p>After visiting a few of the shops we walked through to the village of Buizencourt &amp; there had some eggs &amp; caf&eacute;.   We returned to our camp by motor lorry.</p>
<p>July 16.</p>
<p>At 11 A.M. we packed up kits &amp; marched back to the Battalion at Bapaume.</p>
<p>Our Battalion now has a band of its own.   It is true that man cannot possibly live or even exist upon bread alone.
