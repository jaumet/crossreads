Well I must go & find a spot on the deck to sleep. It would never do for Dad to be on this ship. I have never slept in the same place twice since I have been going on deck. Last night it looked like rain so I slept in the tween decks which is cooler than we are in the Hold. The place where our quarters are fixed is No 4 Cargo Hold.
