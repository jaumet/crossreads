  
 Saturday 19th May 1917 Bapaume Cleaning lines. Left camp about 10am and went to Dump at Favreuil. Filling boxes with empty 18lb. shell cases and stacking. Came back to camp for dinner in the afternoon. 
 Sunday 20th Left camp at 9am for dump. Took dinner with me. Arr. back in camp about 5.30pm.
