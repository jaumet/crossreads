Wednesday 14.3.17
Kit inspection. Went to pierrot show at night.
Thursday 15.3.17
More bearers to stand by in readiness to go to line; expect to go at any time now; don't expect to get the leave granted me under the circumstances
Friday 16.3.17
Closed camp, owing to having to stand by. My leave cancelled
Saturday 17.3.17
Heavy bombardment during night. 20 men left for the line at 7.15 Am 
Ordered to move of at 7 Am in the morning. Bapaume captured early this morning.
Sunday 18.3.17
Further details re taking of Bapaume
