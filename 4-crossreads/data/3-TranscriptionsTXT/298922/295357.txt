 Wednesday 31 January 1917 
 El Risa [The following paragraph crossed through] Note what you say re Laura &amp; Mardi &ndash; woman is always looking for slights etc. &amp; its very tricky about many things. Hence I did not see as much of Mardi as I should have liked. 
 Another Khamsin sand blowing &amp; altering the landscape, dunes come &amp; go in this country of hummocks. 
 [The following paragraph crossed through] Fancy keeping horses SHOD all this time?!! simply coz the Army says Cavalry are to be shod so work for men &amp; misery for Bush horses &amp; great big iron shoes at that are used. 
 Moonlight again &amp; enemy planes over last night, they will come every night for a week now &amp; BOMB us.
