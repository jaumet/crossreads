<p>3<br/>to England stating reasons etc. &ndash; I put in   &amp;  mentioned the two Aunts at Surbiton &amp; that Mary Stobo was to leave Sydney in April for England.<br/>8 days after arrival in England, is to be allowed &amp; I am living in hopes. Each night the Star shells make a very brilliant illuminative effect. &ndash; I don't know why the Germans don't shell us here as we are within very easy range &amp; they shell</p>
