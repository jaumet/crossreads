<p>Mrs. Lobjoit also had swings erected in the trees and the boys took it in turn to swing one another. They didn't forget me either. I organised a game of football as well.</p>
<p>All the time I was in the wheelchair except to sit down on the grass. I brought the crutches too We spent a delightful afternoon The sun was shining practically all the time God blessed the day Coming home the boys sung and trudged along pushing me with them.</p>
<p>At night upon returning went to the Salvation Army Concert, called a "Popular" These were held every saturday night at the Hall, and we used to enjoy them very much</p>
