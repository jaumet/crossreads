<p>The director of Graves Registration   &amp;  enquiries</p>
<p>War Office<br/>Winchester House<br/>St. James's Square<br/>London S.W.1</p>
<p>Reference: - A. A/21/10347</p>
<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>In reply to your letter of 28th July  1918 in which you make a request for a photo of the grave of Private T. Bell and the grave of Private T. Bell who is buried a point just North North West of Hangard, south of Corbie and whose grave is fully registered in this office, and marked by a durable wooden cross bearing his name, rank, regiment, and date of death. I regret to have to inform you that at the present time it is not possible to take any photographs in this area.</p>
<p>A note, however, has been made of your application, and when it is possible to operate in the area referred to, a photograph will be taken and the desired number of copies sent to you as soon as possible thereafter.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,</p>
<p>Singed:   [indecipherable]<br/>Major<br/>For Major-General,<br/>D.G.G.R.  &amp;  E.</p>
