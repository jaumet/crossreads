<p>10.11.15<br/>Wed 10th<br/>The Turks have been giving up some shells again today; killing 4 lads at Bde.H.Q.: The 2nd L.H. also lost a good many men to-day by the same cause, &amp; a good many shells fell into Div H.Q. but hurt no one: As a third of the Regt. is going away tonight also 3 Sigs Peters, Caban &amp; Winterton &amp; the FH 'phone is cut out during the day time in consequence, &amp; I came on duty at M.H at 3  o'clock this afternoon with Tomlinson we work 5 hrs on &amp; 10 off here &amp; have a good many messages to run: Made some apricot  tarts soon after dinner &amp; set some jelly: It looks as though we are about to have some rough weather &amp; it is sprinkling a little tonight.</p>
