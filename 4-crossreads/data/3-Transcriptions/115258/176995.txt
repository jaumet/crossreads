We entered the hills just at dusk and rode or rather walked and led our horses most of the night up the steep mountain tracks and over the same country where we had been scrapping in our former Es Salt stunt. On reaching the top of the ranges we camped till dawn and watered our horses in the head waters of the Wadi <s>M</s> Nimrin then had a few hours spell. About mid-day we rode on through Es Salt and out along the Ammon road for about eight niles to a deserted Sacassian village where we watered up and camped for the night.
