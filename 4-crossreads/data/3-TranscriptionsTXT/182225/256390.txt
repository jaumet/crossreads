 1916 &nbsp; THURSDAY 28 &nbsp; SEPT. 
 Fine &amp; warm. Out with the signallers all day. We had a test in the morning &amp; I passed all right. The battery went on duty in the evening. Capt Campbell took some of the headquarters in a gun laying class in the evening. Old George Curry came over to see us in the evening &amp; he &amp; Poss &amp; Self went to the pictures in the evening.
