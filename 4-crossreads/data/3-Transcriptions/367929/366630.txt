<p>May 1915<br/>29 Sat<br/>Got up at 3.10 am and at 3.30 heavy mine explosions and the Turks shelling bombing and machine guns; they rushed Quinns Hill and took trenches; reported  L-Cpl Grimson's  [indecipherable] took 12 prisoners himself and joined the trenches again after searching in them for Turks</p>
<p>30 Sun<br/>Some 60 of 10th ALH and some 30 or 2 NZ inf rushed out from Quinns  to pull down the sandbag building in front and fill in the trench only 4 of 10 who were not casualties. Result more successful than not. No swim.</p>
