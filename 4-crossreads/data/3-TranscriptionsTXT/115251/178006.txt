 Royal Australian Navy 
 H.M.A.S. &quot;Australia&quot;, at Simpsonhafen. 26th September, 1914. 
 Sir, I have the honour to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of 26th September forwarding me a copy of your despatch t the Minister of Defence dated 23rd September on the subject of the terms of surrender of German New Guinea. 
 2.&nbsp; I wish to thank you for your courtesy in forwarding the same for my perusal. 
 3.&nbsp; I have no doubt that the future will prove the correctness of your opinion. 
 I have the honour to be, Sir, Your obedient Servant, 
 George Patey Vice Admiral. 
 The Administrator, Rabaul.
