What a remarkable thing it is that of the last three deaths, two of them were very prominent members of the Literary Circle, &amp; the other was one who was becoming identified with the Sunday night services. We have lost three of the best men in the unit. There is food for thought &amp; conjecture in that.</p>
<p>There were 13 casualties: all but two by the same shell; a high explosive gas one. Out of a group of bearers resting or waiting in a trench only two were left untouched, &ndash; <u>Eva</u> &amp; Maynard. The latter had huge gaps torn out of his tunic.</p>
