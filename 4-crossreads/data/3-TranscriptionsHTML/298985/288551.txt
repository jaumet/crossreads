<p>through Tencourt to Rosiel Showers started just when we got here have not amounted to much. Once more we have made a home for our selves. Up among the guns again. He has been putting a good deal of stuff over. One or two small ones close to our cook house.<br/>Went out to work at 7. Had to go through a corner of the Village about 400 yds from camp &amp; he caught us in a straff. There was a bank about 3 ft high on the road side so we made our selves small beside it One shell hit the road</p>
