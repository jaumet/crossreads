<p>You remember I was telling you of Gavan Aspinall &amp; young Lloyd joining the Brigade? &ndash; They were both hit at our last position. Only very slightly in Gavan's case. Lloyd has gone to Hospital but will be O.K.  I think.</p>
<p>Father's letter I have handed to General Rosenthal. He was in rather a hurry &amp; did not make any comment beyond to thank me &amp; said he would be&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; glad to read it.</p>
