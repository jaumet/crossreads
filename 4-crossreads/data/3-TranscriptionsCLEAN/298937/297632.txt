
Wednesday 2nd August
Arrived Alexandria at 4 in morning & on board by reveille.Found 5th Div. & Luna Pk. crowd already settled down.  Stuffy cabin in aft of lower deck, but lucky to get cabin instead of hammock.  "Franconia" is a big boat a Cunard I believe & by the time we sail (in an hours time I believe - i.e. 6 p.m.), there will be 5,000 troops on board - a nice haul for a submarine.Lt. Col. Ramsey Webb is the P.M.O. I believe, so
