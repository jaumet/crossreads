 8 December 1916 Fri Raining some to-day. Very cloudy skies of late no aeroplanes. Poker again lost 12 francs. 
 9 December 1916 Sat Took up our quarters in the newly made huts. Played poker till lights out. Dick Powter placed under arrest for having light by Sgt Smith. Poker school won 20.
