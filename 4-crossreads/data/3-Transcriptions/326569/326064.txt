<p>Sunday, 3/6/17<br/>Still having cold nights &ndash; did not rise till 7 a.m.  After dinner walked to Corfe Castle 4&frac14; miles away &ndash; it was built about 792 A.D.  Walked there &amp; back taking 1&frac14; hours each way.  After tea went to town &amp; posted number of post cards.</p>
<p>Monday, 4/6/17<br/>Beautifull day.  In morning had practice with Box Respirators, also Visual Training &amp; Bombing.  After dinner Bayonet fighting</p>
