They polished off some of our stew and cheese with avidity.   They said that they had been living mainly on "bully" and biscuits.<br/>Jan 13.    On 13 January the HMS Glasgow steamed off in the afternoon saying good bye to us by semaphore.   The Almanzora, acting as a cruiser, a converted liner, took us in charge.   A gun was also put onto each <strike>boat</strike> transport, usually in the stern.<br/>Jan 14.   On the following day the 14th January we sailed at 8 am.   None of us were sorry as it was very hot while we were in port.<br/>Jany. 18.   On 18th January, a man named Walker died of heart failure and an impressive service was held for him.
