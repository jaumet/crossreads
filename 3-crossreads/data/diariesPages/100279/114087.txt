Our rations are a little short lately the Sergts (all hungry dogs) are generally a day in front of their issue worst of all we cant buy any bread for love nor money. Yesterday was pay day again 
Had old Mac for our parson on this morning's church parade he preached quite a good sermon General Walker Leslie were also there, they were to have made an inspection of our huts but they have not done so as yet.
