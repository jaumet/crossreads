<p>15th  In the words of the immortal M.W.T.Horan "It is damnable cold"<br/>Did Gas Guard and nearly froze.<br/>Shell fire from the surrounding hills was a magnificent sight.</p>
<p>16th  Back to woods nr. V.B.  Germans have extended their line through Merris and Meteren, and Neuve Eglise has been retaken by us.  Camp shelled 2 cas. This morning.<br/>Appointment of Major</p>
