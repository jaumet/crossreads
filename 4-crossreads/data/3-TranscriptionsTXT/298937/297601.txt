 Saturday 17th June 
 We drew lots (3 of us) for the dispensing position in L. Horse &amp; I lost. On account of the good friends in this tent I was not sorry;  also it would probably mean a life in the desert for ever, &amp; as for the details, we expect to leave soon. In our tent are widely different characters.
