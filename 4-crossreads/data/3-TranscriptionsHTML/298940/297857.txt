<p><u>Monday 18th Feb.</u><br/>Committee meeting of concert party yesterday decided to attempt concert very soon.<br/>Went be Osteasteene, where the rest of the Ambulance is, today &amp; fixed up for the chaps to prepare their stuff up there.<br/>Meeting of literary men tonight &amp; was proposed as Pres. But had to refuse on account of concert party work &ndash; put on the committee.<br/>If piano arrives soon, will be able to give a pretty good show in a few days.<br/>Cold, clean &amp; very fine.<br/>[indecipherable] over bombing</p>
