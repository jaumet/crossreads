<p>18th October (Wednesday)</p>
<p>About 10 o'clock marched off in drizzling rain 10 miles to Erguies, a little village out on the hills.  The roads are all frightfully muddy.  It was a very tiring march especially as we carried all our gear including packs &amp; blanket.  Reached Erguies</p>
