 1915 June 27 Sun. Went into Cairo in the afternoon &amp; visited Greek Scout Headquarters - The Greek Scouts are even more friendly than the British though they speak little English = Had tea at the Soldiers' Caf&eacute; &amp; then with 2 young Egyptians as guides went out through Shoubra . Saw a pidgeon shooting match &amp; then walked down to the Nile &ndash; Watched the fellaheen  making  cutting up straw &amp; also had a good look at a Sakya which is used for watering the fields (as well as the shadouf) &ndash; Got back to Gezira about 9 p.m.
