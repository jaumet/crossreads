  
 GLENCORSE WOOD Friday 21 The great battle over our boys triumphed right along the line. Lt Haigh wounded twice, we lost good men, it is wicked Saturday 22 This battle was the 6 Brig greatest victory yet achieved by the Anzac's Battalion releived last night, now at Ypres Sunday 23 Left Ypres at 4 this morn and marched to Halifax camp near "Vlaamantinghe Our casualties numbered about 250. The sights witnessed in a day on the field, are awful blood and iron, pain and suffering
