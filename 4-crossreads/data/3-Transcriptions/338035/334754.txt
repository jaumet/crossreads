<p>Thursday 11th<br/>Excellent weather though very warm since we are getting into tropics now being in meridian 20.<br/>For 24 hrs. ended noon we did 302 miles.<br/>Ship in sight on starboard side practically all day but towards night she passed us.  She was "Kastigar [?] P &amp; O boat.<br/>Pay (10/-) today.  Pay on board ship is every 10 days and 1/- is all that is paid.</p>
