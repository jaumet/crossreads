  
 Mon 17.  stand by all day & go into reserves in evening.  past guns etc. hell-fire corner gas-alarm (false), bags of tucker. 
 Reserve trench very rough.   plenty of shelling all the time, day & night.   very little sleep. 
 Tues.  18   Artillery  bombardment  duel for about 1 1/2 hours continuous.   shells everywhere, planes directly fire   men very cool, walk about in open.   member of one party reading paper on the way up, dirt & water flying  three  three of our boys buried in dirt.
