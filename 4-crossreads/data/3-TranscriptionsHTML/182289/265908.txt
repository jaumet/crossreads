<p>During the night 5,000 rounds were fired on the following targets:-<br/>(1)  Roads &amp; Trenches P.32.a.<br/>(2)  Railway Crossing V.1.b.<br/>(3)  Road V.1.c. &amp; d.<br/>(4)  Cross Roads P.31.c.<br/>(5)  Enemy Tracks in P.26.c. &amp; d.<br/>(6)  Enemy Tracks P.32.b.<br/>Enemy Artillery activity normal, consisting chiefly of harassing fire in valley O.15.c. &amp; d.  At 11 p.m. 20-10.5's were dropped around &amp; in vicinity of Orchard O.29.b.  About 1 a.m. enemy threw a number of gas shells in vicinity of Cross Roads in O.24.a.<br/>Enemy aircraft active throughout day.  During mist about 8.30 a.m. enemy planes flew over our battery areas &ndash; heavily engaged with A.A. fire without result.</p>
