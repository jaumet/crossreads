<p>the train. here we are billeted at farm houses: the whole of our division being mobolised here to undergo about eight days final training, being put through gas ect.; here too we of the 60th Bty are transferred to another brigade, the 25th H.W.B.</p>
<p>A little incident, which caused us a good deal of amusement at the time, was, the day we were given a lecture &amp; practical demonstration, under gas &amp; through tear shells. of course the gas was of a mild form but still, quite severe enough to kill without the helmets. the tear shells are only a bit uncomfortable to the eyes making them smart like anything, nearly blinding one</p>
