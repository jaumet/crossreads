<p>25,000 Austrian prisoners.</p>
<p>5 June 1916 Mon<br/>Severe cannonading going on. Tragic end of England's greatest soldier Lord Kitchener who was lost with his staff in a cruiser on his way to visit the emperor of Russia on important military &amp; financial questions. He has died in harness as truly as if he had died on the field of battle. Russians take</p>
<p>6 June 1916 Tues<br/>Got the gramophone. News came through to-day that the Russians have taken 13,000 Austrian prisoners. Paid to-day. Paddy Connors, Les Hall, Marsh Rice Keith Harris Snow Portus &amp; [Tiny] O'brien &amp; myself went to Steenwerck. Had a good time round at Lawrences. Keith &amp; I in officers tent</p>
