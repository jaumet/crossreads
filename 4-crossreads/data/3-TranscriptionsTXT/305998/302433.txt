 Received three letters today no more letters accepted for outward post. 
 13/6/16 Kit inspections by the dozens &amp; getting ready for France. 
 14/  Packed most of the harness except the gun teams harness which will be used to pull the guns &amp; waggon to the station. 
 15/ Drawing guns etc to station, came out in Brigade orders gazetted as Driver so a extra 1/- a day now on. Put on B.H.L. gaurd &amp; marched prisoners down to train &amp; took them on boat at Alex.
