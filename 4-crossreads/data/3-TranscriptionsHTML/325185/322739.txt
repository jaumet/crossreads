<p>[Letterhead]<br/>Telegrams &ndash; KING'S WALDEN &ndash; Telephone No. Ten HITCHEN</p>
<p>KING'S WALDEN BURY,<br/>HITCHIN,<br/>HERTS.</p>
<p>9.9.16</p>
<p>Dear Father</p>
<p>You will see by the heading I have moved again. Am convaseleint now. The hand is The hand is getting along splendid is nearly healed up now. It is a bit stiff yet but shan't be long getting the use of it again. Am having it massaged. I got myself into disgrace at the Hospital kept asking the sisters when I would be allowed out. The Matron said isint the treatment to your satisfaction I told her the treatment &amp; attention were all one could want but I couldint stand being caged up. At last I asked the Dr. to let me out so he said they</p>
