 Sunday 2 June to Wednesday 5 June 1918 [No comments recorded on these days.] Thursday 6 June 1918 Hospital sports.  Quite a good show.  London Police Band came along &amp; played for us. 
 Friday 7 June 1918 Toddled down to Maidenhead for the weekend.  Stayed at the The Hermitage (Taplow side). 
 Saturday 8 June 1918 On the river with the Tibbitts for tea.  The Candain [Canadian ?] Hospital at Cookham looks quite a comfy place.
