<p>spread and I can tell you we did wine in and after that meal was over we went up in a special train to Adelaide which lays a few mile from the port, anyway we had a good day at the capital with of course plenty to eat free of charge, and after getting all we could we went back to the ship.  Like all soldiers get all you can for nothing.</p>
<p>We did not get away till next afternoon but our dinner was provided by the V.A.Ds on the wharfe so we were in luck's way.  We got away from this port alright and after a few days we came to our destination or rather the boat did that was port Melbourne.</p>
