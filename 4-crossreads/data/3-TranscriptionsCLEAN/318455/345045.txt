I am writing this at midday in a siding at Termoli where we are having one of our many halts to let another train through.
Bully Beef & Cigarettes are better than money in this part of the world - we have been exchanging them for grapes & tomatoes all the way down the line. Constantly passing meeting troop trains of Italians from Albania & Salonica dirty, slovenly looking men they are.
