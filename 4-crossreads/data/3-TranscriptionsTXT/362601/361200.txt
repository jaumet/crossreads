 7 May, Wednesday 
 F. We all walk over fields up to Gorse patch. I take photos. We sit in cosy room &amp; look at books, etc. I am surprised to be back at old games as in Sydney 6 &amp; Mel. 4 in Egypt. Elddoe &amp; Korse. 
 Wrote Home, Mr Edmonds, Mr Freemont 
 8 May, Thursday 
 F. We go up Snaefell much against my will but it is lovely &amp; we have a real good time, we tell stories about one another &amp; have a lovely cycle down hill. Tea near Sulby station. We are very confidential. I help Uncle sew peas &amp; dig garden all morning. I watch uncle at puzzle &amp; won't sit down.
