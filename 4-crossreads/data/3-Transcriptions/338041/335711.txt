<p>June (1916)</p>
<p>June 17th<br/>Nothing unusual happened during day</p>
<p>June 18th<br/>Frenchy Sunday &ndash;attend Mass at 5.30pm (6.30 by watch) &ndash; Artillery at Ypres silent &ndash; 2 Taubes brought down &ndash; I leave with party for Bois-Grenier</p>
<p>June 19th<br/>Day duty in Dressing Room &ndash;</p>
<p>June 20th<br/>Huns shell Armentieres &ndash; Church set on fire by their shells &ndash; Seen by us at Bois-Grenier &ndash; wilful destruction. Very few cases - Death of Von Moltke</p>
