<p>Fortunately the discharge went off into the ground.</p>
<p>We had a raid whilst in the line.</p>
<p>A certain number of men were selected amongst the platoons and the objective was the German front line.</p>
<p>The raid was hardly a success. It rained rather heavily just before the men were timed to start.</p>
