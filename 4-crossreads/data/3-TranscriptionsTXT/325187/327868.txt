 [Thursday 4 May 1916] 
 Court again.  Finished CM at noon &amp; came Cairo with Judge Advocate worked it well to see Nowell &amp; stay 2 days, till Court is Dissolved- Nowell is thin but well has 3 months Holiday &amp; goes to England lucky beggar he is a Colonel very like his old Father &amp; a great row of Ribbons for Gippy decorations.  He wishes to be remembered to you all- I have taken a couple of Photos which I will send you- We have had a great yarn, but he tires easily-
