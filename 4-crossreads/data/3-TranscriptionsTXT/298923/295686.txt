 20 January 1918 Sunday second after Epiphany  (X to X crossed out) Went to Church Parade Sermon Text  "I AM THE DOOR"  Here the sheep are put in a corral, but no  door  is used, a Bedouin sleeps on guard, hence simile-  A Taube flew over during service- This afternoon rode into Richon-Le-Zion &amp; had a  hot  bath at a Jews house for  10  P.T.  5  Majors  Ryrie  &amp;  Irwin  came with me. X More letters Dec 2nd &amp; 13th &amp; cutting Dairy from Mother also 3 Australasians 1 snapshot of Jack Mathews playing polo- &amp; my usual letter from the little wife, alls well.X
