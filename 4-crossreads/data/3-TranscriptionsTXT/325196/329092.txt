 19-1-16 Wed 19th This morning orders came out that two Australian Camel Corps are about to be formed &amp; volunteers are asked for who would take Ser-Majors jobs also commissions &amp; about 80% of the C.Sqd. put in applications today;C.&amp; B Sqd.Sigs went for a ride this morning to exercise our horses &amp; this afternoon we had some message sending with H.Q. Sigs. We have been having very poor food here &amp; been putting in complaints daily &amp; are faring a little better at present. At 12 oclock today A Troop (A Sqd.) left for their new job, Lieut Mack went in charge of them, &amp; by all accounts they should have a good time; This camp is named Beni Salaam.
