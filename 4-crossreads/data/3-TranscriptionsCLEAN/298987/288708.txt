Monday 15thWent with a working party of 100 to a camp about 2 miles off The Officer in charge could find nothing to do so marched us back & told us not to go on Parade as we would be wanted Order obeyed, have spent the afternoon in the Hut
Tuesday 16thA little musketry & a lot of loafing Put tags on my overcoat too as we keep the tails out of the mud have to wear them on all parades now
