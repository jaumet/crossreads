
Hosking & another.  On to support line outside Battalion where we waited for Price.  About a dozen 4.2 shells fell quite close and we had to move down trench.  Then up trench and working close by Battalion on deepening dugouts.  Worked until 12.30 and home very quietly.  A lot of new wire out and our path crossed in places.  Bed after supper.  Mail.
17.6.18Fine day.  Slept till 11 & had a wash & slept in afternoon again.  Up for tea and afterwards wrote letters.  Out at 9.15 as usual although not warned till late.  Heavy barrage just as we got to Battalion on support line but rest of night very calm.  Same job and some more digging and home at 12.30.  Indignation at Linton and others.  Home to supper & bed.
18.6.18After breakfast up and wrote Pelman.  Fine with one shower in morning.  Some of section busy on dugout frames.  In aftn. wrote more Pelman and out at 9 with Boot, Sparkes, Burnop, Williams & Stott and walked round trench to new switch support.  Turned to left to Bde. O.P.C.T. until we came to Rouge Croix Switch.  Trench dug by 2nd Coy and very bad.  Had a spell here & then got 4 shovels each and back after one spell overland past Cowshed and Estaminet quietly.  Going got some M.G. very close.  No parties for us so we got to work on putting bridge for horse traffic across trench at Battalion, taking us till 12 and then home.  Supper and bed.
