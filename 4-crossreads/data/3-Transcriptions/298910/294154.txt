<p>Left Queen Alexandra Hospital end of July for Southall, where I stayed three days &ndash; then went on to No 2 Command Depot, Weymouth. Humbugged about sick leave &amp; furlough till Aug 30th, when owing to the kind influence of Mrs Slade &ndash; was told I could have 7 days special leave. I arrived in London about 1 pm &amp; caught the Flying Scotsman for Dundee 6-30 am August 31st.</p>
