 26th &ndash; Slip for leave - 27/28/29 30 nil &ndash; 1/st Opportunity of leave.  Leave Khan Yunus in truck arrive Kantara 6.30 am 3rd/7/17 Arrive Cairo 2 PM and go to Hotel Metropole and kit stores.  Get new suit. 4/7/17  Mess about in morn and use afternoon in motor to the nile delta barrage. 5/7/17 To Cheops  and  in morn &amp; Matarieh in afternoon 6/7/17 To Citadel morn &amp; bazaars with Said in afternoon. 7/7/17 Leave Cairo 11 am and arrive Kantara 4 pm.  Leave 6 pm and arrive Rafa 6 am 8th after awful night in a crowded truck.  Leave Rafa 11.45 for wadi Shiek Nuram and arrive camp 5 PM. 9-7/17 Work again &ndash; usual stables 10/7/17 Parade to major over sig instructors job at Zeitoun. 11-12th 12th all quiet and very hot. 14th Lecture on Palestine by Col Maitland Woods. 15-16-17-18 nil. 19th. Jacko comes down in force and leathers into us over the wadi.  All turn out.  Wireless leaves in 17 minutes from alarm.  return 11 pm.
