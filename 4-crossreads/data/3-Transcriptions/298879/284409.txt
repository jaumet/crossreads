<p>July 1915<br/>Wednesday 21<br/>Dusty. On the brakesmans job again today with a new driver. A little Lancashire lad too. He always believes in taking short cuts through his sentences. We started to cart water to No 1 Aust. Hospital about 6 AM &amp; finished about 1.30 PM.</p>
<p>Thursday 22<br/>Mighty clouds of dust. Beautiful evenings</p>
