<p>5.5.18<br/>O.C. visited Battalion Hdqrs. and received information of operation by 9th Bde. (Bde. on our right) whereby our line was to be advanced about 500 yards on a 1500 yds. front.<br/>Arrangements were made for this Company to co-operate by dealing with selected localities of enemy activity and posts.  To this end special targets were given to 8 guns for firing during the night.  Programme carried out was as follows:-<br/>(1)  Dernancourt &amp; Railway.<br/>(2)  Quarry E.14.c.<br/>(3)  Enemy front line E.13 &amp; 14.<br/>(4)  Suspected H.Q. E.2.b.90.15.<br/>(5)  Bridges at Dernancourt.</p>
