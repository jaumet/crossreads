<p>on through very nice country to Blequin, where are to stay the night. Weather dull but fine cold &amp; raw. No Services possible today 10 miles today</p>
<p>Monday Nov 19<br/>We left Blequin this morning at 8 oclock our trip was about 18 miles I came on the Motor Lorry through very pretty country &amp; some nice towns &amp; villages. We arrived at our destination "Frencq" at about 11.30<br/>We are billeted in a fine old Chateau 160 years old The grounds and woods are well kept. In summer it must be delightful</p>
