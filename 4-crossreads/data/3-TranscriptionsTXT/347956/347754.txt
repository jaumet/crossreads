
Sep 11 - 17
Fri 11thArrived in camp at 9 AM  & had some fun watching riding test; had the day off on account of vaccination.
Sat 12thRaining a treat;  Passed the riding test signalling the rest of day, went to the city after tea
Sun 13thChurch Parade, issued with extra kit bag, bandoliers etc. raining strong went Felicia for tea & then to station & saw mother & father off
Mon 14thSquad drill morning  flag drill afternoon.  went to Redfern & passed the evening at a benefit
Fri 15thFlag drill morning innoculated against fever at dinner time, to Felicia evening & had bad time from innoculation
Wed 16thFlag drill all day, had photos taken at the Crown Studios evening saw Jessie for awhile coming back from Burwood
Thurs 17thRaining all day.  no drill wrote letters all afternoon, went to Felicia
