<p>14 July 1915</p>
<p>My dearest Mother &amp; Father</p>
<p>I received a letter from Mother addressed to St Cloud. It was lovely hearing from you. I'm glad you had a safe journey to Sydney and found the family well. It must have been strange such a small one. I do hope you still at Darrah, I wish I was home again and the war over. I went out with the girls to tea at Cousin Marian's on Sunday.
