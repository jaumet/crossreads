<p>Le Bizet<br/>6000 rounds were expended in night firing on the following targets:-<br/>1.  Trench and road junction, C.5.d.30.90<br/>2.  Crescent trench, C.11.d<br/>3.  Enemy tracks, C.18.a.25.30<br/>4.  Twin Farm, C.12.a.60.50<br/>5.  Road leading to Frelinghien in C.5.d.<br/>No activity displayed by either enemy Artillery or Machine Guns.<br/>Weather very foggy, observation nil.</p>
