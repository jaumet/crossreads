<p>2nd. Sunday. July<br/>Church parade at Bttn Hqrs also Band out.</p>
<p>Football match between B &amp; C Coys. after lunch.</p>
<p>3rd. Monday.<br/>Physical &amp; Coy drill all day with lecture on Gass.</p>
<p>4th. Tuesday.<br/>Parades cut short about midday by heavy rain.</p>
<p>5th. Wednesday.<br/>Very dull weather. Foot ball match between 7 &amp; 8 platoon postponed owing to bad weather. Gass alarm.</p>
<p>6th Thursday.<br/>Physical jerks. Drill contest between Coy's of 17th. Mail in.</p>
<p>7th. Friday.<br/>Very dull weather. Parade stopped by rain.</p>
