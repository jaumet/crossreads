Jan 13th  We cleaned up the buss in the morning & I read "McClusky the reformer" A.G. Hales in the afternoon - Harold just back from Paris leave spent the afternoon in the lorry with us, talking needless to say mostly about the gay city.
 
Jan 14th  We had another fall of snow last night  Nothing to do to-day - I read all the morning & made an ascetylene gas lamp in the afternoon & wrote letters in the evening - We are getting our rum issue every night again -
 
Jan15th  We stayed in all day with the back down ascetylene lamp burning (works tres bonne) & read in comfort - we also pinched a five gallon drum of "metho". it rained nearly all day & was a bit windy -
 
Jan 16th  Very stormy night lots of rain & a high wind. ditches & holes were full this of water this morning. but it is'nt cold. I stayed in all day & read "Quinneys" by Vachell - Had letters also a book by Post. Joe goes into Bailleul every evening -
 
Jan 17th  It was snowing & raining more or less all day & we spent a real lazy day, we lay down all afternoon & slept - Joe stayed in & read in the evening for a change
