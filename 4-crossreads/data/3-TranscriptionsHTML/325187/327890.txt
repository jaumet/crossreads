<p>[Saturday 27 May 1916]</p>
<p>Recd 2 Australasian April 1st &amp; 8th thanks.<br/>Some of the Regiment went to the Sea for a swim I have not been yet.  Very little news reaches us here of what is going on in the world &amp; Censorship is stricter than ever.  Been busy making a place for Aeroplanes to land.<br/>The Heat is quite moist so different to the dry Upper Egypt heat &amp; consequently the Flies are worse &amp;  especially the larger varieties the same Green Bronze Fly that causes so much damage to sheep, is plentiful here.<br/>Sandflies bad.<br/><strike>Laura never mentioned a word about Charlie's 21st Birthday</strike></p>
