THURSDAY, JUNE 12, 1919.
Fine & warm. Jo drove Dad & I into
Quirindi in the big car to the
Graziers Meeting. Nothing much
doing at the meeting, but saw
most of the Plains folk. The red
buss jibbed coming out at bottom
of the Who-d-thought-it, but got
her home all right.
Daly was over and handled
the stalion. Tickle round the
paddocks. He skinned two cows
in the Boundary.
Joe Perry shifted cattle
