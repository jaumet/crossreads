<p>23. Thursday<br/>Grey cold showery &amp; windy.  Stayed in house &amp; wrote some articles.  Went to bed a little after 10.</p>
<p>24. Friday<br/>Cold and grey.  Went to Miss N. at 10 &amp; left at 2.  Mrs. Nelson Smith left after tea.</p>
<p>25 Saturday<br/>Cold dull miserable profitless day.  Alone in house so slept in afternoon. Weak for want of nourishing food.</p>
