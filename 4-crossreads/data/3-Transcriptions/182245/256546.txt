<p>JANUARY 1917</p>
<p>31 WEDNESDAY</p>
<p>Snowing all day off an on, but</p>
<p>the sun also shone a good part</p>
<p>of the day. Had an inspection</p>
<p>of horses in morning. by some</p>
<p>[ret? vet?] major &amp; exercising</p>
<p>horses in afternoon. Got a</p>
<p>couple of letters from Linda, &amp; one</p>
<p>from M<sup>rs</sup> LLoyd &amp; father in morning</p>
