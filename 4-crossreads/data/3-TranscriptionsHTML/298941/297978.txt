<p>In the busy shopping centres &ndash; round the square &ndash; although the shops were shut, there was a busy &amp; animated scene,. <strike>to</strike><br/>Leaving the square &amp; walking towards the docks towards the docks along the broad main Rue de Paris, it is surprising how the change from friend shops to mean ones, &amp; dirt both on the shops &amp; their inhabitants, is effected. It would be hard to fix any point of change for it is gradual</p>
