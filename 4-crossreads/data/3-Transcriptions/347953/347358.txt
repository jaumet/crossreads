<p>A few deaths have unfortunately taken place since we left Colombo. Most of them being from pneumonia.</p>
<p>We passed by a P &amp; O boat and a warship while travelling the red sea.</p>
<p>A few cases of measles have occurred on board <strike>boat</strike>  No less than 8 from our own company.</p>
<p>Met on board C.C. Dight an old boy of the Kings School.</p>
<p>Arrived at entrance to Suez Canal on Thurs.27th Jan &amp; learnt to our astonishment that fight had taken place at Kantach on the Suez Canal. So we did not proceed</p>
