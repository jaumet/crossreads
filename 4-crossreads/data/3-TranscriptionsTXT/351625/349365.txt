I think the division is out now for a good long spell, in fact they say all the Australians are going to have a ninety days spell &amp; quite time too as there would soon be no Australians left if they kept them going like they have been.  The Batts. have all been cut down to three Companies &amp; the Companies to 3 platoons &amp; even now the platoons are very weak. 
 I have a good collection of souvenirs here to send you at the first opportunity.
