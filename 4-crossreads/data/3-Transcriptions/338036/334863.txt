<p>[14th Jan. contd]<br/>At night I went to Armentieres with Ron Ponton [?] &ndash; staying at Marie Choselet's near Pont de Nieppe (Bridge) for cup of coffee.  The method of partaking of .  coffee is worth noting.  The beans are put thro' the machine in ordinary way and after making is poured into the peculiar no handled bowls and a dash of rum added.  Instead of sugar you are given a small lolly to hold in your mouth whilst drinking and the coffee goes down well.  On arrival at Armentieres we went to M.D.S. thence to tea &amp; Church in YMCA.  The Rev Capt Mills preaching.</p>
<p>Monday 15th  [Jan]<br/>Large Australian mail  I received about 14 letters.  The snow &amp; heavy frost of early morn made it exceptionally cold.</p>
<p>Tuesday 16th<br/>Another frost &amp; cold</p>
<p>Wednesday 17th<br/>nil</p>
