
Started wearing shorts. It has been showery & overcast for some days today is bright but not unplestaly hot. Lectlure Why the Church is so Divided by Padre Knight a good speaker & has evidently studied a great deal. Ships Orderly C/pl today
Monday 6thStarted training for Physical Drill competion. A lecture by Captain Knight - Messopotamia. Our gun fired 4 shots all misses. We have crossed the Line at least 6 times in the last 48 hours according to roumer
Tuesday 7thDid quite a lot of Physical Drill this morning & feel all the better for it. Lecture by Let Hart - The War in East Africa. He has been there for some time a poor speaker. Altered our course slightly more to the West. Our deck got first prize for cleanliness & 2nd for best table again
