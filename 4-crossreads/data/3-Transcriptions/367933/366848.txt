<p>6.11.16<br/>To Hon Sec.<br/>1st LH Comforts Depot</p>
<p>Dear Madam<br/>Many thanks for parcel of comforts sent by the committee to me. They arrived in good order. I am no longer with the old regiments having been seconded for duty with the Australian Squadron of the Royal Flying Corps .<br/>Thanking you again<br/>I am  Yrs faithfully<br/>G K Mills Lieut</p>
