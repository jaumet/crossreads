<p>-2-<br/>Thanks very much for the parcels I am writing o Arthur.</p>
<p>Rochford got a letter from Mother last night and I think has answered it already.</p>
<p>Everything is going alright here and am as usual in excellent health.</p>
<p>Best Love<br/>Your Affectionate Son<br/>C.R. Lucas</p>
