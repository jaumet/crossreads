I thoroughly enjoyed the run, but it rather unnerved the driver, & my timid & always scared Camera bearer. During the night Bombs were exchanged from both sides. 
 22nd October Weather unpropitious Wilkins & I went to Savy to our 69th Squadron (Flying). We enjoyed the two hours motor run, though it was infernally cold. The avenues & foliage are taking on those wondrous shades of Autumn & the whole country is a riot of marvellous colorings. The leaves are falling & winter is fast coming.
