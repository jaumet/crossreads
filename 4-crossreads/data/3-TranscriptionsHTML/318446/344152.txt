A hostile plane appeared the other day but was winged by our A.A. guns &amp; forced to unload his bombs on nothing &amp; fly ignominiously, which was exceeding good to see.<br/>I find the pleasure of solitude &ndash; my house contains me &amp; me only &ndash; very pleasant.  I have no intimate companion now, they are all gone &ndash; Threlfall killed on the Peninsula, Yeats gone to England, Tommy Rutherford, England, etc. etc., and I find that it is better to be in a position to please yourself than have to consult one whose wishes do not always run with yours.</p>
