<p>Monday's Daily Mail of Nov 4th contained even better news &ndash;</p>
<p>Standing out in large headlines &ndash;</p>
<p>Austria out of War<br/>Armistice Signed</p>
<p>New German front open to the Allies<br/>Berlin within 80 minutes by air.</p>
<p>The following Official Note was issued in Paris last night &ndash;</p>
<p>The Armistice has been signed with Austria. Hostilities will cease at 3 pm tomorrow Nov 4th. In five weeks all Germany's Confederates have laid down their arms as the result of disastrous defeats in the field.</p>
<p>The Order of Surrender has been.</p>
<p>Bulgaria. Entered the War October 1st 1915. &ndash; Armistice took effect on Sept 30th 1918.</p>
<p>Turkey. Entered the War Nov 5th 1914 &ndash; Armistice in force October 31st, 1918.</p>
<p>Austria. Began the War July 28, 29, 1914 &ndash; Armistice in Force November 4th, 1918.</p>
