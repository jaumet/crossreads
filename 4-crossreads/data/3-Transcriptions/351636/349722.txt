<p>19/6.16<br/>Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Horsfield<br/>Can you assist Mrs. McGregor, who temporarily as she has only received &pound;1-3.11 this month &amp; no certificate from Melbourne to say when next pay will be made &amp; with 2 little children cannot get along until money will come &ndash;</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>E. H. Bennie</p>
