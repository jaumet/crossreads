We stayed in this village for a fortnight during which time our transport joined us up from the Base.  They seemed to have had a great time at Marseilles also along the road from Abbeyville.  Our Battalion band used to play in the market square of an evening, we got paid while we were here & most of us sampled French Beer and declared it quite harmless, no so was the case of the French wine & headaches were plentiful next day.
