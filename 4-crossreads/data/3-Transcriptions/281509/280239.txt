<p>1916 January 16<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Rise 9 am. Shower. breakfast. Stay in pyjamas till 1 pm.</p>
<p>Meet Mary at Quay at 4 pm. Go out to Robbins at Waverly to tea.  Pleasant  afternoon &amp; evening.  Leave for camp at 9.30.  Home without incident at 11.30 pm.  Fast ride in motor.</p>
