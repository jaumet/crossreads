<p>Wednesday 16 January<br/>Weather very boisterous this morning but died down this afternoon &amp; turned out sunny. A fatigue trip to Lindenhoek near Kemmel.</p>
<p>Thursday 17 January<br/>An "evening in Egypt" with a lecturer at Y.M.C.A. who gave us a lantern lecture on Egypt, Cairo &amp; surroundings prominent. Weather still bad, wet and snowing.</p>
