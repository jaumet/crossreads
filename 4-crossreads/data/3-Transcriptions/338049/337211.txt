<p>Tuesday 1 Jan.<br/>New Years Day once more. This time in Steenwerck on the stables. Up at 6.30 morning stables at 7 finish about 7-45. Parade 9-15 &ndash; Grooming. Afternoon on at 2. Harness cleaning &amp; preparing for move. Evening writing &amp; study. Weather exceptional cold. Billet over Estaminet near YMCA. &amp; mess at Sergts mess.</p>
