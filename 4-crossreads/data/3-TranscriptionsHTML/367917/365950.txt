<p>Notwithstanding the scarceness of things in these parts we all managed to have an exceptionally good "feed" on Xmas day, including Turkey, etc. but of course, as it was our first peaceful Xmas Day in this part of the world, we rather laid ourselves out to have a good time.</p>
<p>Up till Xmas the weather here had been exceptionally mild but the last few days have been decidedly cold, so expect we will be having a proper Belgium winter shortly but as we are all in very fair billets these days it probably won't worry us much.</p>
