 Fritz came over flying low, &amp; dropped a few bombs round about. We soon found a reasonably comfortable cellar, &amp; the others got to bed while I went on the 10-12 shift. No more bombs came anywhere near, &amp; I turned in about 12 &amp; got to sleep somewhere near 4 oclock. 
 This morning when we got up, orders had arrived for us to move to a flour mill (where I am writing this)
