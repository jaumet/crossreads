<p>Saturday 4 August</p>
<p>These 4 days were passed in fine weather.  Runs included the Daily trip to St. Omer for papers &ndash; 3 or 4 trips to Blaringhem where the 5th Div. Hd. Q. are situated with the A.D.M.S. &amp; Signallers office included.<br/>Brigade Head Quarters are at Wardreques. </p>
