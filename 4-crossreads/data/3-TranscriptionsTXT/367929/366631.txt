 May and June 1915 31 Mon Yesterday quieter. I placed C Sqd on Popes Post in right section. Swim in morning. Met Clarence Pyke. 
 1 June Tuesday Quiet last night but we were to have made an attack on Quinns and Popes. Found 1 off 1 sgt 2 cpl and 24 men as picquet in NZ Valley. 4 wounded
