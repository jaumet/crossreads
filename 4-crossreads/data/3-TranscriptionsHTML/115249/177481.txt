<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Flag</p>
<p>To - Berrima</p>
<p>Date - 21-9-14</p>
<p>Time - 9.33</p>
<p>VA to Administrator</p>
<p>I think it would be much better if German officer was placed onboard Encounter so that he might go ashore with the flag of truce and thereby facilitate matters. &nbsp;This was put down in the orders. &nbsp;Request he should be sent there at once.</p>
<p>0925</p>
