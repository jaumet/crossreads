<p>21st<br/>Left the hospital at 1 A.M. this morning and came on to Havre by Amb. Train <strike>to</strike> where I was carried aboard the hospital ship Granturny Castle.</p>
<p>22nd<br/>We left Havre last night and arrived at Portsmouth this morning about 6 a.m.</p>
<p>Since coming to hospital I have had umpteen tickets attached to and detached from my valuable person and nearly everybody <strike>conc</strike> concerned has found a new way to spell my name.</p>
