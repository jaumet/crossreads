 FEB. &nbsp; TUESDAY 8 &nbsp; 1916 
 Fine. All horse picket got leave off&nbsp; from &nbsp;till 12 oclock &amp; as there was nothing doing we did not drill in afternoon. Came out through the fence about 4 oclock &amp; came in by tram &nbsp;Got over to Lisheen early. Luke Boyd, M rs &nbsp;Rorke &amp; Miss Nickol there. Got back to camp by 9.45 train. Took Miss Nickol back as far as the quay &amp; put her on tram for Metropole
