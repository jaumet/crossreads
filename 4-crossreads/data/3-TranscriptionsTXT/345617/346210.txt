
Octobre 15Building dugouts on West Hoek Ridge & draining "Pill Boxes". The weather is still very disagreeable. Nothing of unusual importance to note.
Octobre 16Working on drainage of roads between West Hoek & Anzac Ridge in morning & on dugouts all day on "Hoek Ridge". The shelling today has been exceptionally heavy & we are getting a lot of casualties. Fritz also sent over a lot of gas shells last night.
