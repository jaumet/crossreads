<p>Fri Long march all day &amp; fiasco of an affair</p>
<p>Sat 6th<br/>Quiet day Wings died<br/>Posted to Mater &amp; Cousin</p>
<p>Sun 7 Quiet day Wings funeral Mr &amp; Mde Snow came out at 5 am Went into Heliopolis with them and Butcher in evening. Watched the pictures from Pension Verandah Back for feed up.</p>
<p>Mon 8th. Off mounted in good time to the range only fired five rounds when called in.</p>
