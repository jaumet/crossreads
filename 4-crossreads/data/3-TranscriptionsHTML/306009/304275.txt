<p>1915<br/>Mar. 30 Tue.<br/>Went into Cairo in the afternoon &amp; got from Post Office a parcel containing<br/>Two novels<br/>Two prs. socks<br/>1 diary<br/>toothbrush<br/>Cake chocolate</p>
<p>How glad I was to get hold of it &amp; how decent of those at home to think of me.</p>
<p>Also had a haircut &amp; went for a walk through Esbekieh Gardens.<br/>Beautiful moonlight night again.</p>
