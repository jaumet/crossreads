<p>February 1918<br/>Wednesday 27</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Had a very quiet morning. Strolled down to Daylight Corner and "bagged" a horse. Rode to Dranoutre to [indecipherable] Baths. Had a good bath and got a clean change of clothes. Had lunch at Waggon lInes. "Tommo" pretty well screwed.<br/>Albert and Charlie were my companions during the visit. Saw Jamie, cleaning limbers. Fritz shelled the house at F.O.P. and the occupants drifted off. <br/>Garfoot on L'aison</p>
