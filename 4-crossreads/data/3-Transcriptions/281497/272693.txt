<p>I was wanted for, most of them thought it was for billeting N.C.O. but I had a pretty shrewd idea what it was for.</p>
<p>Orders from the Adjutant were that we were to report at Divisional Hqr's at 3 oclock sharp that meant a march of 4 &frac12; miles to Rubempre after receiving these orders I went back &amp; shook hands to all the boy's who by this time had guessed what was on, &amp; many were the congratulations showered on me</p>
