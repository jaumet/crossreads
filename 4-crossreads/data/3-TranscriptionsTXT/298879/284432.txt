 September 1915 
 Sunday 5 Cool. Had some trouble to get some rations today on account of my imminent transferment to Head Quarters Signallers. Had a cool wash last in the sea and washed some clothes in the briny  Repeated the process this morning. A sniper amused himself with pot shots at me and several others. Very quiet 
 Monday 6 Beautiful day. Our new soldiers have arrived to relieve us. They call themselves the  "Dinkum Australians" . they are the 21, 22, 23, and 24th Batt of the 6th Bgde. It is nice to see fresh strong faces again.
