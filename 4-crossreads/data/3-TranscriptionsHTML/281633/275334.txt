<p>27.8.18<br/>Plenty planes &amp; Bombs again last night.</p>
<p>29.8.18<br/>Moved up to Froissy last night &amp; got a dug out near the Lagoons &amp; Canal.  Fritz has left tons of munitions about here.  There are also some crashed british planes.</p>
<p>1.9<br/>Seen big Fritz gun over 15" near Chuignes.</p>
<p>3.9<br/>Arrived in Paris from Froissy via Boulogne.</p>
<p>4.9<br/>Seen Keller.  Went to Bois de Boulogne &amp; afterwards to Alhambra.</p>
<p>5th<br/>Trip on the Seine to the Marne &amp; through Paris, quiet Evening.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Malmaison, Montmatre, &amp; Sacre Couer  Church.  Dance at Moderne in the Evening.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Notre Dame de Paris, law Courts, Church of St. Just (Oldest Church in Paris).  Handed over diary to Censor.<br/>x<br/>9th.9<br/>Hotel D'iena A.M.  Dance in the Evening.</p>
<p>x 8th<br/>Quiet Day.  Concert at Moderne in the evening.</p>
<p>10th<br/>Afternoon, Musee Grevin.  Evening Dined at Saulnerin Caf&eacute; &amp; went to Folies Bergere.  At the Folies the singing is no good the Chorus Girls not up to Australian standard for either looks, Dancing or Singing.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Fontainebleu.  While on the way there we seen numbers of small French Tanks only about 9' long.  Fontainebleu Palace is lovely but most of the Art Treasures have been removed for safety.  The surrounding forest is the largest in France.  It contains some fine trees one 1400 years old.  In the town there is a Bull (Sculptured) by Rosa Bonheur.  The 1920 Boys went on a jag the other night &amp; painted it red.</p>
