<p>2.2.18 Saturday   Walked about 20 KM. today looking for Jack McMahon &amp; then found that he had gone away about a month or 6 weeks ago with a Broken ankle. He was going up to the line  <strike>indecipherable</strike>  when he slipped on the ice &amp; broke his ankle as stated. He made a nasty job for the Bearers who took him back. The N.C.O. &amp; others fixed things up for him O.K. He is probably in "Blighty" now. I won't go looking for anyone in a hurry again. My feet are so bad that I will only just be able to hobble about tomorrow.<br/> <u>3.2.18 Sunday</u>  The Taubes were over 5 times last night but they were not after our place fortunately. They were so close overhead that the noise of their motors awakened me before the "Archies" &amp; machine Guns opened up. Feel very sore today. Letter to Clare.  Last Friday night I had a sort of Heart Attack that nearly finished me off. I really thought my Heart had burst. Have had a dull ache there ever since. I</p>
