 I will finish off this letter now and begin a new one for the same mail. Much love dearest Mother &amp; Father. I long to see you and be home again &ndash; though not till the war is over. I am sorry Mr Allen has not been well. I am sure he must have great anxiety about Dennis. Goodnight my heavenly parents. Ever your devoted daughter Betha McMillan I think if Roslyn gets my letter it should be shown to Colonel Jamieson too. Betha
