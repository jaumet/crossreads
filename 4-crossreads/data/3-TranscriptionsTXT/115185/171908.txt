 3 A.G.H. Lemnos 26.11.15 
 "If I die here I die by accident! don't I?" Oh I'm sure it would be  - cos only a bomb would kill me! 
 My own beloved Mother 
 Just a few lines. I've been writing to Constance &amp; Ernest tonight &ndash; I have not written them a separate letter often. I posted you or rather gave Capt Boyes R.E. letters to post to you in England. I sent you little letters from men here to me. They give you a little idea of their cheerful spirit and braveness. It's a ghastly affair this [indecipherable] the tradgedy of loosing these wonderful young lives. I was looking at Major Hershel Harris photographs today. He has a splendid collection, when he goes home &amp; we are all home, I must get him to show them to you. He went to Anzac &amp; Suvla Bay for a few days. He has one picture showing the landing place &ndash; the beach where the wounded lie, the hospital ship standing off in the deeper water &amp; Imbros coast line in the distance.
