<p>Sergeant Major &amp; the allotment of fatigues to do the work for the Camp was a tantalising job instead.<br/>The Sergeants &amp; I were in constant turmoil.  For the Sergeant of A Company had a certain number of men graded for hard work, others for light work;   there were cripples, &amp; "exempts" from all duties, shell shock nervous debility cases.   A &amp; B companies did the lion's share of the work besides taking it in turn to find the guard &ndash; 9 men a corporal &amp; Sergeant + "A" Company Sergeant said the exactions on his men were too severe.
