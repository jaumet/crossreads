<p>[YMCA Letterhead]<br/>Mudros. West<br/>Lemnos Is<br/>1 Field Amb.<br/>30 Sept 15<br/>My dearest Irene,<br/>I am still with the 1st Field Ambulance but still expect to be attached to the 2nd Stationary Hospital. The life here is very monotonous but I suppose must put up with it. A few of those small tuition books you used to send me would be most welcome &amp; Cadbury's Blue Paper Choc. a tin of tooth <strike>Pap</strike> Powder &amp; a brush of the same ilk about each 3 mos would also be acceptable. It is necessary always to sew up in some stuff to ensure parcel holding together.<br/>Did I tell you I saw Capt. Jim Simpson here the other day &amp; that poor George was killed?<br/>They give us a fair supply of Cigarettes &amp; Tobacco but some Cigarette Papers would be welcome.<br/>I here Colonel Berston is expected here soon &amp; I think when he</p>
