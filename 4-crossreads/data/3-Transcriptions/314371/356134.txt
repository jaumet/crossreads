<p>Sun. 20th<br/>Beautiful day.  It is an awful pity to be shut up in Hosp.<br/>Doc. not satisfied with my head yet.</p>
<p>Mon. 21st<br/>The M.O. operated on my head this morning &amp; removed a piece of steel helmet. *</p>
<p>*Unfortunately lost during Ypres offensive, Sept. 1917.  J.C.L.</p>
<p>Tues. 11th<br/>Making very satisfactory progress.</p>
<p>Wed. 23rd<br/>Still doing O.K. expect to get out in a couple of day.<br/>Another man died of wds. in the ward this morning.</p>
<p>Thur. 24th<br/>Discharged from Hosp. to No. 7 Con. Camp.</p>
