<p>Each man has but 15" in which to lie as the hammocks all touch one another. Numerous Heads were bumped, &amp;  owing to the heat one can scarcely imagine the din &amp; the laughter caused by the struggle to get into bed.Lights out at 10pm &ndash;I was asleep long before this.</p>
