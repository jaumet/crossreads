<p>This evening I am about to attempt the answering of "twenty-seven" letters which came to hand yesterday.</p>
<p>Oct.21st<br/>A "heavy" day &ndash; Dress Rehearsal &ndash; Preparation of stage &amp; properties &ndash; Booking &ndash; In the evening "Saa - eeda Digger!" (A Romance of the East) is played for the first time to a very small audience &ndash; I am neither satisfied or disappointed _ I must wait &amp; see. &ndash; Our manager<br/>(?)</p>
