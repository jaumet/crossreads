<p>Wednesday 26 June<br/>Nothing eventful to-day at Borre except great rumours about the Austrian retirement across the Piave after their offensive is only six days old.</p>
<p>Thursday 27 June<br/>Nothing eventful all day, which is glorious.  It is said that there is a hop over to-morrow morning.</p>
