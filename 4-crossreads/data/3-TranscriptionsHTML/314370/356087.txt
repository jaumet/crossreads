<p>September, 1916<br/>Monday 18<br/>Work all day &amp; at 4 pm was warned to be ready to march out to Harrowby camp at 5 pm<br/>arrived there we were dumped into wet tents<br/>awful night COLD ----</p>
<p>Tuesday 19<br/>Start work on course<br/>Have found room in huts for us, so we are again snug.</p>
