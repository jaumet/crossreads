<p>15.9.16<br/>Fri 15th<br/>A shower of stars fell on this Regt this morning as Johnny Keegan, McKeown, Hordern, Guthrie, Gray &amp; Sutherland from Bde Head Qrs came out in orders &amp; are going out on this stunt in charge of Troops. McKeown is Q.M. Sutherland came over from Bde this evening to take charge of the Sigs; the Regt formed up at 6 P.M. &amp; moved off at 6.30</p>
