  
 Tuesday March 27 Easter leave talk of the camp. Many N.S.W say they intend to make complaint on going to Sydney. Wednesday March 28th Sailing date postponed again until April 18th. We are fed up again. Nothing unusual here. Thursday March 29th More NSW reinforcements marched in to day. Saw George Steed & Billy Williams. We informed them of our good home.?
