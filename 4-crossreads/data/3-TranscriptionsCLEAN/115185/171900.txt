They have worked and slaved and demanded for their sick soldiers comforts - and Col Fiaschi is very proud of them. The matron is very young and has not been a matron for very long in Queensland hosp, which I believe is not a very good training school - 6 months matronship she saw before taking over this job - which has really run itself as far as the sisters are concerned. She is bright and likes company - has a very charming manner - a fine brow on which Denis remarked - I wonder how or where Denis is. I heard a rumour from Brownie Dangar - But I could not bear to think anything had happen - that sweet boy. So I try not to think of such a thing until I hear definitely. It is very cold here. I'm sitting in a marquee surrounded by sleeping men and wearing Mother's fur coat. It must be nearly midnight. The wind is howling.
