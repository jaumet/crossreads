  
 Thursday 21 January 1915 At Mena House Everyone working hard getting hospitals into working order 
 Friday 22 January 1915 Same as Thursday Two operations, inguial hernias 
 Saturday 23 January 1915 Camp being erected rapidly. Officers & men working very well, especially Major Read, Captain Mackenzie, and Captain Clayton.  Col. Springthorpe officious & boyrish [boorish?] as usual. Young officers slow & not punctual 
 Sunday 24 January 1915 Camp ready for inspection. Major General Bridges inspected the camp at 11-15 am. Said "excellent".   Not able to be at Mass. - Operation.
