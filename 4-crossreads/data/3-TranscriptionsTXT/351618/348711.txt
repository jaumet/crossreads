 Friday 17th December Our Steamer went up close to the Headquarters Steamer &amp; received instructions &amp; soon two Barges were alongside &ndash; one to take the men from other units off &amp; the other for our Baggage &amp; Party. It did not take very long to get the lot of stuff on her &amp; off we went to the shore &amp; were dumped onto a wharf. There were other Units besides those over which I had charge (the 3rd F.A.
