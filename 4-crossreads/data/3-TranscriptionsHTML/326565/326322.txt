<p>Sunday 12/3/16<br/>Church parade 9.30 AM. conducted by Capt Chaplain  of the 30th Btn.  Cricket match continued from yesterday.  Engineers compiled 59 in their second innings leaving us winners by an innings &amp; 11 runs.  I took 2 wickets for 6.</p>
<p>Monday 13/3/16<br/>Innoculated again for Enteric.  No 4s of each squad given medical haversack to go through &amp; see contents were all correct.<br/>Afternoon off in consequence</p>
