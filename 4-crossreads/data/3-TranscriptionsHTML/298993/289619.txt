<p>Saddled up camels and went digging trenches again went to a different place this time this country reminds me of ingleburn it is lovely soil with plenty of lime in it  as far as I can see it is all green barley and other food stuff  had dinner 12 started work again 1.30 taube over air craft and H.S. battery firing at it never got him had tea 5PM there are two mazim [maxim]guns here manned with scotch infantry we are on out post to night I drew last post 3. Till 4 turned in 7.pm cool night. Tuesday. 3.4.17 stand to 4.AM  all is clear lied down till 5. Started trenching</p>
