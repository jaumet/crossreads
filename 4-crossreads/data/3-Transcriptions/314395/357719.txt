<p>best wine it has been my fortune to come across, some very old port it was, and excellent.  I don't know whether I mentioned it before but I met a lad named Tierney here some months ago who said his Dad was a friend of Uncle Jack's.</p>
<p>The war news lately has been good and it looks as if the hun will soon have to make a big retirement.</p>
