<p>left my velour hat at a dyers to have its atrocious colour made into something civilized.  After dinner again went  ashore, &amp; had a tram ride to Norton Green a few miles out of the city, passing on the way Thornaby &amp; Stockton the latter being rather a large sized town.  As rain was falling I did not alight from the tram but came back again.  After tea Watt &amp; self went for a walk through the City &amp; to the gardens.  Returned to ship about 8.30pm.<br/><u>Saturday 30th[31st] July 1915.</u>  Assisted Purser during morning  &amp; afternoon went ashore after tea &amp; to Confession at St Marys Cathedral  a very fine church &#188; of an hours walk from the Docks.
