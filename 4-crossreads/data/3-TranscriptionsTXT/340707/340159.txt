17-6-19 (Tues)Met the [little fairy] in Cafe Legrand Place Blanche which still holds the Moulin Rouge & decide to spend the evening visiting the same places as the previous night.
Notre Logis is more interesting & more talent to present [indecipherable] plays nocturne after nocturne from Chopin. I met a young painter named Caillous & accompany him home leaving the girl among her friends who will see her home
18-6-19There is a dinner tonight at Military Cercle Rue di Volney the aristocratic French military Club of Paris to receive 2 Americains. I have asked permission to be present & so make a friend of Duke an American on GHQ once stationed in Paris who goes out of his way to make friends for me.
Dinner costs 13 fr & we get champagne & an excellent concert The Club rooms were the War Office of 1849 & are spacious. All servitors wear white stockings. The court is a green lawn & cool & inviting. There are 2 courts.
