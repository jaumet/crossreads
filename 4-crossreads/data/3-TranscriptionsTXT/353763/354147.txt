 Borrowed a helmet &ndash; got my gloves &amp; looked all right as a civilian medico  -  Captain &amp; I then stepped over a submarine into our little motor launch &amp; off we went &ndash; the only 2 men off the Upolu anyway  -  We landed on a very fine wharf &ndash; 1000 foot wharf &ndash; astern of the troop ship Berrima  -  Then we landed on erstwhile German territory but now British &ndash; (may it ever remain so)  -  We saw pickets everywhere &amp; they saluted as we past  -  Everyone was going down a certain roadway Casuarina trees flanked it  -  I noticed many trees &amp; Hibiscus &ndash; glorious double Hibisci  -  After all was over I went into a garden &amp; got 3 superb beauties &amp; brought them off  -  The verandalled villas seemed bare &amp; empty  -  on a few lay the kit of Tommies but no nice seats or verandah chairs  -  I fancy the Germans had cleared out early  -  I looked thro French windows but saw no furniture or draperies which confirmed my notion  -  I looked &amp; observed hard for 10 minutes till
