<p>Tuesday May 20th<br/>Parade 9 oclock, all our huts had to be scrubbed out.  30th Quota played and beat the 35th at Cricket.</p>
<p>Wednesday May 21st<br/>The last of the 4th Division came into camp (52nd Quota).  Evening went to lecture in Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Thursday May 22nd<br/>3-30 p.m. Had to go on Guard.  7-30 went to Concert.  11-30 p.m. went on two hours shift.</p>
<p>Friday May 23rd<br/>Came off shift 1-30 a.m. and went to bed.  5-30 a.m. on Guard again till 7-30 a.m.  Third shift 11-30 to 1-30 p.m.  A Glorious day.  Got our paybooks back.  4 p.m. came off guard.  Went to Concert.</p>
<p>Saturday May 24th<br/>Another warm day.  Finished blacking my leggings in Shop.  Went to Pictures.</p>
<p>Sunday May 25th<br/>A dull day.  Went for a walk through Barford St. Martin, towards Dinton, came back through Lady Hurdcott's estate.  Wrote letters during the afternoon.  Evening Pictures.</p>
<p>Monday May 26th<br/>Wrote letters all afternoon.  Evening went to pictures.</p>
<p>Tuesday May 27th<br/>9-30 Muster parade all ranks.  Stitched leggings all afternoon.  Evening, Concert in Y.M.</p>
<p>Wednesday May 28th<br/>9-30 Muster parade.<br/>After tea went to Salisbury by car and got box from station.  Went through the Cathedral.  Very old and very fine.</p>
<p>Thursday May 29th<br/>Another muster parade, had our names read out for roll.  Evening went to Concert, best concert so far in camp.</p>
<p>Friday May 30th<br/>9 a.m. Parade.  10 a.m. Final medical inspection.  5-30 went to pictures.  7-30 Dance in Y.M. until 10 p.m.  Very warm.</p>
<p>Saturday May 31st<br/>9 a.m. Parade.  Names of men on advance boat party read out.  Weather very warm &amp; dry.  Did some washing.  Went to Concert in Y.M.C.A.  Had hot bath before going to bed.</p>
