<p>7.30 had a wash and got Breakfast fell in 8.30 again cleaned up camel lines.  rested till dinner time 12.30.  had dinner very hot day.  rested all afternoon had tea 5 P.M. turned in 7.30 P.M.</p>
<p>Monday Junly 2.7.17<br/>turned out 5.30 fed all camels had shower had Breakfast fell in again 8.30 washed camels in the Bitter lakes.  washed two and contrary one at that <strike>had</strike> watered camels had dinner 12.30.  had a Parcel from home also about 20 Papers.  rested till 4.30 P.M. had tea 5.30.  wrote to maud, Josie, dorrie aggie Bell alma.  went to canteen had a Pint turned in 9 P.M.  Saw a sea Plane.</p>
