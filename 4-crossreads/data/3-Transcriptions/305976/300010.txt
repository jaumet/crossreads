<p>Wednesday June 12<br/>Heard rumour to day that our division may be going to France. Causing mild excitement.<br/>Thursday June 13<br/>Saw Monastry built on old site of Solomons gardens. Parts of the old garden still intact. Moved out from Solomon's Pools to Telat El Doon [Talat ed Doon] at 5pm. Sorry to leave this camp. Passed through Jerusalem about 10pm arrived Telat el Doon[?] 7 AM. Friday Very tiring trip with Camels<br/>Friday June 14<br/>Scorcher today 4th Brigade came up from Jordan Valley 125 &#37; degrees to day &amp; no shade. Fine choking dust flying owing to constant stream of horses watering Telat El Doon[?] also known</p>
