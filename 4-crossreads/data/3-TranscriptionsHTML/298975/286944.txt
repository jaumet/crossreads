<p>Aug 1917<br/>passed today. Got our water bottles out today have to carry them full all the time, but not of Rum</p>
<p>Wednesday 22nd<br/>Had a full Marching Order Parade today Got my fiver out of Orderly room today. Small steamer passed this evening Fair sea running strong W wind. They are making out a <strike>long</strike> list of all our trades or kinds of work we have done. Mine is a fairly long list</p>
<p>Thursday  23rd<br/>Fair sea and much cooler. But am still wearing shorts</p>
