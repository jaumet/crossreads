
18 May 16At Kingston. Our concert party & band gave an entertainment ashore in aid of Red Cross.  They had a large audience & it was a great success.
19 May 16At Kingston
20 May 16Left pier in forenoon watch & proceeded to Port Royal where we anchored. 22nd May anchored at Port Royal.
22 May 16Proceeded to sea a short distance from Port Royal & done sub-calibre firing. Came back and anchored at Port Royal.
23 May 16Anchored at Port Royal.
24 May 16Done 6 in firing & came back & anchored off Kingston.
25 May 16Anchored off Kingston.
