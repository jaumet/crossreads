  
 He then ordered the natives to pull their rickshaws off the stone barricade & on to a path which was studded at intervals with blocks of stone in the centre. 
 Again the natives demurred & again Brolga burst forth into a torrent of abuse which had its effect. We were now quite near a bridge or a main road on which we could see a group of Aust soldiers & a number of natives gathered greatly interested in this sudden appearance of a string of rickshaws being pulled over this forbidden ground.
