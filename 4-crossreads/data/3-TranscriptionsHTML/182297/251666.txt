<p>Narrative up to 12 noon 8/2/15</p>
<p>The enemy behind GEBEL HABEITA remained in the same camp until the night of 6<sup>th</sup>/7<sup>th</sup>, when they vacated it. During the 7<sup>th</sup>, their camps were seen along the WADI MUKSHEIB, and their retirement v&icirc;a GEBEL MUKSHEID and BIR GIFGAFFA is confirmed today. To the north, their retreat towards EL ARISH is believed to continue.</p>
<p>Until important events render it advisable, the issue of Narrative will not be continued.</p>
