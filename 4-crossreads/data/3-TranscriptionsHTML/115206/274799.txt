<p>August,1918<br/>Saturday 17</p>
<p>Morcourt, Bty Post, in front of.<br/>Weather nice for travelling. Left Cayeau at 8.30 am, and proceeded via Wiercourt, Bayonvillers and Eithevanem, to a position outside  of Morcourt. <br/>we relieved the 79th Bdge R.F.A.<br/>En route I met Lance <br/>Raymond, a very old pal of mine.<br/>Received a package of tobacco from Olive, and Young Francis gave me an Auto Strop razor.<br/>Laid O.P. and O.M. lines.<br/>Have 8 lines in use and Smithy is experimenting with a Fritz phone.</p>
