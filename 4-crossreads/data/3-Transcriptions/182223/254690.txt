<p>Thursday 19&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />Major Purdy of Sydney. Here for lunch with me.&nbsp;<br />Korno taken by the Germans&nbsp;<br />Teutons victorious all along the Eastern front&nbsp;</p>
<p>Friday 20&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />About hospital all day.&nbsp;<br />Young Coppin from Elsternwick Victoria took tea with me at 8 p.m.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Saturday 21&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo during the morning&nbsp;<br />Heard that Ramsay Smith had left No 1 A.G.H., and that Barrett had been deprived of his posts except the Red Cross business.</p>
<p>Sunday 22&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo dng mng, saw Ramsay Smith at Sheppheards Hotel.&nbsp;Mass 7 a.m.&nbsp;<br />Purdy to lunch <s>&amp; tea</s>. Purdy &amp; Paton to tea. Drive round Matereh [Matarieh]&nbsp;between 6 &amp; 8 pm..</p>
