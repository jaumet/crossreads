 Tuesday took rations out to the R.E yards at Montauban &amp; Longueval 
 Roads in a terrible condition &ndash; transport &amp; guns of every description &ndash; infantry going through both ways in single file. Rations &amp; Ammunition going up on Pack mules &amp; horses &ndash; Drivers ride one horse with 4 shells &amp; lead another horse with 8 shells 
 Undulating country &ndash; devastated by shell fire  which  by day it is one mass of men &amp; horses and at night just a dark screen pricked out with twinkling camp fires &amp; the flash of guns.
