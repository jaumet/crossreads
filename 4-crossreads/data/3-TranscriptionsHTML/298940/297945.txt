<p>Altogether I was very glad to fall in on the parade at 4 p.m. to move off for Harfleur even if it was raining hard &amp; I, having lost my hat, was under the doubtful cover of a cup comforter<br/>After the normal amount of delay taken up with roll calling set out for the station, which turned out to be North Bologne station &ndash; Walter &amp; I still together.<br/>The train was already comfortably full, so we proceeded to make it uncomfortably so.
