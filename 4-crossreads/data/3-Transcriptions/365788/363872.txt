<p>In an open Railway Wagon on the line to &ndash;</p>
<p>Tel-el-Kebir<br/>14 Feby 16.</p>
<p>My Dear Irene,<br/>It was lovely to receive a letter from you yesterday dated 26 Dec. 15 &ndash; I am getting your letters now fairly regularly &ndash; They seem to come better when addressed 1st Field Amb. Received also a nice parcel containing 3 cakes chocolate &amp; 4 novels from you - &amp; 1 pair sox from Mrs Carter. It is very good of you old girl to send me so many parcels.<br/>Mrs Fletcher, Mrs Connor (wife of Dr Connor) &amp; Miss Hindmarsh were down at Tel-el-Kebir yesterday &amp; we had a nice luncheon</p>
