
Friday Oct 12Two new Batteries arrived to day moving into position midnight. Tom came up from Rafa. All dumps being moved up from Rafa & El Arish to Shelal. Getting Horses fit for coming stunt. Beer a Sheba rumoured our objective.Saturday Oct 13Sultan's funeral in Cairo. Soldiers armed in case of native rebellion. French, Indians, Jews, British West Indias, Tommys, Irish, Welsh New Zealanders Italians & Ausys on this front.Sunday Oct 14All ranks warned of prevalence of Spies. A snake came in Bivvy today & curled up in Blankets. Finish Snake
