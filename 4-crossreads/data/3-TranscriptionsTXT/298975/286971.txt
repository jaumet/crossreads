 Friday 2nd Nov 1917 Working Party. Did my first bit of Sandbag work They make such a neat job 
 Saturday 3rd Working Party Went to Warminster in evening what a dull place Weight 11st 3lbs 
 Sunday 4th As usual. Attended evening service at the Home Hut Miss Osbourne spoke A splendid little sermon 
 Monday 5th Same old Working party same old work 
 Tuesday 6th Hard job finding work on the working party. Have a very good little mate on the job an old hand. He is no loafer like so many are. His brother is a VC man &amp; he did not tell about it untill we had been 2 weeks together
