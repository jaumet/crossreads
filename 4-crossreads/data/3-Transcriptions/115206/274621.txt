<p>February 1918<br/>Wednesday 20</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Fritz shelled in our direction from 7 am to 11.15 &ndash; no damage done.<br/>Weather wet and fairly cold<br/>Went down to O.P. with Jone's lunch and had a little pow pow pow with Gun.<br/>Wrote an informal applic. for leave to Australia and handed same to Bty Clerk.<br/>Visibility purely rotten, so the guns did very little shooting. Barrage put up for raiding partly last night. <br/>Did the 2 to 4 shift.</p>
