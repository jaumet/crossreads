<p>Edith will have told you he going to be marred it came a shock to me I never praid for him to get a good wife as I nevr thought thire was eny one good anuf for him but when I got to know I started to pray that she will be a good true wife to him as he has been one of the best sons to me &amp; all of mine</p>
