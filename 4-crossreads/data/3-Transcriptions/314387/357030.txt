<p>Thursday 12th<br/>Went to Divisional sports &amp; Herieancourt Sports attended by the King for about an hour marched back in afternoon Friday 13th<br/>Instructions in live bomb throwing! Saturday 14th<br/>Fatigue filling in trenches morning and afternoon<br/>Sunday 15th<br/>Church parade in morning afternoon went for walk to Warloy came back got paid forty francs.</p>
