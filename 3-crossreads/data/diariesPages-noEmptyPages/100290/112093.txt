28 November 1916
Tuesday
A very busy night. Bad management of this place is deplorable. Congestion needlessly caused by bad organisation. Get cleared up about 2 p.m. Change on to day shift, remain on duty till 2.30 p.m. 18 hours. Visit Tony in aft. take him great coat. Tea in Y.M.C.A Biscuits cold cocoa. 3 cents for tallow dip to eat it by.
