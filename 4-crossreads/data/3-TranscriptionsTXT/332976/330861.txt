 May 1917 21st continued&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;move off at any time.   We have been "standing by" waiting for orders, but so far none have come through 22nd. The Tommies are moving up &amp; relieving our lads  of  on this sector, but there are still some of the 5th Div. in the line. 23rd.   This morning our rifles &amp; equipment were inspected, &amp; later we went for a route march.
