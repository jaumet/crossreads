<p>Octobre 11<br/>Working at West Hoek putting in shelters. Coy engaged in various kinds of work from Hooge to Anzac Ridge. Sap Scott of No.3 Sec. killed today. Still having nasty weather.</p>
<p>Octobre 12<br/>Fourth Div: NZealanders &amp; some of our other Australian troops in conjunction with British &amp; French made an attack this morning. Our objectives were gained &amp; it is reported that English &amp; French troops did well. Cannot get full particulars. Lft Brydon was killed this afternoon &ndash; my Sec. Officer. Still putting in shelters at West Hoek.</p>
