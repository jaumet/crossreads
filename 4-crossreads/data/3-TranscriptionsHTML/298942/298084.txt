<p>All day today we have kept to our little room, &amp; the guns have barked &amp; crashed. <strike></strike> Our artillery have been very active, but Fritz's has not replied much.  A few shells fell the other side of the hopfield facing us, but so far we have been lucky enough not to have taken shelter in the trenches by the side of the road.</p>
