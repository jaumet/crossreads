<p><u>April 14th</u></p>
<p>Parcels made up &amp; posted.&nbsp; More letters written.&nbsp; Poems copied.&nbsp; A busy day.&nbsp; Spend evening at Wimbledon where the great Tennis Courts are - Wimbledon Common.</p>
<p><u>April 15th</u></p>
<p>Morning in town.&nbsp; Book buying.&nbsp; Parcel posting.&nbsp; Visit the Canadian Beaver Hut.</p>
