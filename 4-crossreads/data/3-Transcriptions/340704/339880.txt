<p>December 1917<br/>Friday 7<br/>Enab.<br/>Very cold morning dawned started raining and most bleak. Wind would cut one in two.<br/>Quiet morning started raining. Turks shelled road again no one hurt tho shells landed near monastery. Roche and Fraser going with despatch at [indecipherable] to 10th regt. Dangerous jorney for them and very cold and wet.</p>
<p>Saturday 8<br/>Infantry 60th Div started attack on Jerusalem.<br/>Heavy artillery fire on Turco defences.<br/>Hope to be in Holy City tomorrow am doing important work to and from front line. Wet and cold. Artillery doing great work. Went down to Kulomieh got shelled and fired on. Turks shelled Judea. Went out to old camp.<br/>Rumours city of Jerusalem fallen</p>
