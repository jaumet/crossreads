Most of us were temporarily blown out when we reached the shore where a large concourse of natives of all sorts &amp; sizes awaited us. There were fruit sellers, cheap ornament vendors, traders in French silks &amp;c clamouring to trade their wares. There were also a good few of sleek advance agents of the local brothels &amp; sly grog shops &amp; they secured not a few customers.
