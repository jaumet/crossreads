
<p>SATURDAY, AUGUST 30, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Finished shearing at</p>
<p>10.30 in the morning &amp; got the</p>
<p>sheep to their paddocks in afternoon</p>
<p>Will have just over 34 bales.</p>
<p>Tickle took Mountain sheep as</p>
<p>far as Wheelers in morning &amp; I</p>
<p>took Cow paddock to the 4D.</p>
<p>Al &amp; Jo &amp; Marj. came home &amp;</p>
<p>Al &amp; Linda &amp; Hilda went over</p>
<p>to Coomoo Coomoo for the</p>
<p>night.</p>
