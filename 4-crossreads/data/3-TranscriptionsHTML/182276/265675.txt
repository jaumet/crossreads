<p>9th M.G. Coy No V<br/>Intelligence Report from 6 am 4th to 6 am 5th<br/>Right Sector:- Fired from No 7 S.P.X position indirect on I.6.B.8.9. Time 10 pm- Result, unknown. Gun [crews?] stood by with gas masks from 8.30 to 10.30. <strike>Listening posts were withdrawn at 8 p.m &amp; replaced at</strike>.<br/>Left Sector:- From No 5 position searched roads in rear of enemys line 3&deg; elevation. No 6 position searched roads and communication trenches in rear of enemys line 3&deg; elevation. Notice of discharge of gas by 10th Bgds received, necessary precautions taken</p>
<p>BJ. Carey Capt<br/>9th M.G. Coy<br/>5-12.16</p>
