<p>Monday 17 February 1919<br/>Last night we trucked the remainder of our horses to the Remount Depot, Moascar.  O.C.s Squadron keeping one horse only.<br/>Wet &amp; cold &amp; I cannot shake my cold off.<br/>Polo team in Cairo doing well, winning all before them, against the pick of the English players &amp; the pick of all the Ponies in Egypt.<br/>Stilling living in uncertainty in regards to embarking but I do think the end of the month will see us away.</p>
