 1916 January 24  Monday 
 Come down to Sydney again this morning.  Knocked around all day, bought 2 prs. pyjamas &amp; bathing suit. Went to Annies &amp; Idas to tea.  Thence called at  Robbins about 9 pm.  Found them out but they came along in a few minutes. Had a yarn. &amp; some passion fruit Home to camp at 11.30 pm.  Two peculiar dogs on street Go to Swiss Studios to get photo taken in overcoat
