<p>Snow [16/12/17 Sun] Our First Snow. Recd. Letter from M.R.L. Dick. Ellis &amp; I go for Walk. Take Snaps. Dave comes us for Talk. Have good supper am Canteen Corpor.<br/>Wrote Home.</p>
<p>Ice [17/12/17 Mon] Hard frost. Ground to slippery to walk. Norm. comes up &amp; we talk till Supper Time over the Fire</p>
<p>Ice [18/12/17 Tues] Run all way top Train Pains in legs all the way. Buy presents &amp; distribute same. Go up London Rd for music. We all write letters, very cold. In Train. Wrote M.R.L by fireside</p>
<p>Ice [19/12/17 Wed] E. goes to Sarum. Go down with [Indecipherable] until 8.15pm. We rearrange the Tables. Have Bath &amp; go to bed at 10.30pm. Sent cards to Fremantle, Aunt Annie, Jennie, Mrs Edwards.</p>
<p>Ice [20/12/17 Thurs] Still icy. We alter stove. Pay two German Prisoners. Get nasty cold. Room lovely &amp; warm. We have nice quiet time Photos. to Rube. Gwen. L. Robson.</p>
<p>Ice [21/12/17 Fri] Begins to thaw at 4pm. I am Ord. Corp. [Indecipherable] write a lot of P.C's.</p>
<p>F [22/12/17 Sat] Nice Day. Go to Sarum. Go to Mrs E. &amp; also to Cou8ncil Chambers &amp; Mr F. Meet Frank again. Meet Civic for Bulford Bridge in Train. Get Home at 12.45am. See Winnie.<br/>Recd. Letter from Aunt Annie.</p>
