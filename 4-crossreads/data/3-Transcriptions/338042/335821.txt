<p>Brunswick St. &ndash; Sad partings &ndash; a weary train journey to London.<br/>Mar. 5th<br/>Arrive "Kings' Cross" 7.30 a.m. Train to Crouch End &ndash; Return to "Elm Bank" &ndash;Home Again. Meet Mr. &amp; Mrs Matthews &ndash; Afternoon trip to London on business &ndash; a heart to heart talk with Mrs Scales &ndash; Correspondence.<br/>Mar.6th<br/>Feeling ill &ndash; Breakfast 11 a.m. &ndash; A run into Town &ndash; Spend afternoon at Coliseum.</p>
