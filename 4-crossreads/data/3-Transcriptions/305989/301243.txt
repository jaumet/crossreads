<p>Sunday 11 February 1917<br/>Left "139" early &amp; "trekked" to Mazaar, stayed the night &amp; left the 2nd Regt. there.</p>
<p>Monday 12 February 1917<br/>Short days "trek" from Mazaar to Tilul &amp; stayed the rest of day, heavy shower of rain, on picquet.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13 February 1917<br/>Left Tilul early &amp; proceeded to Bir-el-Abd.  Saw some pictures of Charlie Chaplain, bought some stores.</p>
<p>Wednesday 14 February 1917<br/>Left Bir-el-Abd for Bayud arriving there about 2.30.  Relieved the Suffolk M.G. Section:  they went back to Bir-el-Abd.</p>
<p>Thursday 15 February 1917<br/>Quiet day:  a bit windy.  Got good mail:  this wallet from dear old Jess : Saw Guth &amp; Charlie Wright. (Mail  from ?) Mary, Mother 2, Theo, Elsie, Dulcie, Grace, Lucie, Miss P., Enid, Norma, Jess.</p>
<p>Friday 16 February 1917<br/>Quiet day:  just getting settling down.</p>
<p>Saturday 17 February 1917<br/>Very quiet day:  ordinary fatigues:  wrote Enid, Elsie.</p>
