<p>The Centre for Soldiers' Wives &amp; Mothers,<br/>92B. Pitt Street, Sydney.<br/>31-1-16</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton &ndash;<br/>Mrs. Machron who is now receiving a small weekly allowance from your Fund is in difficulties about meeting payment on a [indecipherable] on furniture. Could you assist in this case? Mrs. Machron is under heavy expenses in trying to pay off her small home as well &amp; could manage tho' with difficulty to get along if this debt were paid as they are pressing her</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>B. Booth</p>
