<p>12.30 p.m.  Heavy Machine Gun and Rifle fire and Shell fire encountered from North Eastern end of Peronne.</p>
<p>The adjutant Capt. J. Haddon and the Intelligence Officer wounded;  both rather badly.  Lieut. Veal has been wounded slightly and gone out.  Enemy dead testify to the havoc wrought by our barrage earlier in the morning.</p>
<p>4.30 p.m.  Major Kerr received a slight but nasty wound between the eyes.</p>
<p>5.30 p.m.  Orders have been received for our brigade to relieve the 14th Bde. who have suffered very heavy casualties.  We are to relieve the 53rd Bn.</p>
<p>Major Kerr went forward</p>
