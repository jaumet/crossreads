
<p>TUESDAY, MAY 20, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine with SE wind, but started</p>
<p>to rain at night &amp; we got 25</p>
<p>points steady rain from N.W.</p>
<p>I was round Point &amp; Boundary</p>
<p>lopping ect then through</p>
<p>Mountain onto Ben Ledi</p>
<p>&amp; met Linda &amp; came home</p>
<p>through Scrub.</p>
