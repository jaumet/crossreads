 Sunday 18th Had a very quiet night. A little rain this morning. The dugout is very comfortable & warm. Some Indian troops passed yesterday they look well on horseback. 
 Monday 19th Did a little Drill this morning. Swiming Carnival in afternoon. Too cold for me. 
 Tuesday 20th Dull in morning. At least we are supposed to drill. We do a few movements & then stand about for some time. The Sgts don't want to do anything
