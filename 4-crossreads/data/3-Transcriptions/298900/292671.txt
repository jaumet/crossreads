<p>Monday 8th April<br/>    Matters fairly quite;  a few wounded &amp; sick coming through also Trench feet brought about by the recent wet weather.   During the afternoon went over all my [indecipherable] &amp; put them in apple pie order by getting some extra things.   Matters running very smoothly altho' this is the first time in the history of the Amb. that A Section has been able to run a dressing station with its own equipment &amp; personnel.  This is due to the fact that I control the nursing as well as the Dispensary duties &amp; have been working for some time past improving the equipment &amp; personnel of the Section.   It means hard work but I don't mind that.   Turned in about 10.30 after reading the local paper sent by Syd where I was pleased to see his name figure so prominently.</p>
