<p>October 1916<br/>22nd   I volunteered for the draft which will probably be leaving in a day or so.<br/>23rd   Warned that the draft will leave in the morning &amp; I have drawn all equipment from the Q.M. Store.  A draft of 20 is leaving for the Engrs &amp; also drafts for the Artillery A.M.C &amp; A.S.C.  We were issued with 120 rounds of ammunition each, &amp; rations &amp; slept in the mess hut for the night, ready to move off in the morning<br/>24th   We left camp at 6.30 AM &amp; proceeded to the station at Etaples.  We entrained in <strike>a</strike></p>
