<p>351<br/>3<br/>most charming man and is calling for me tonight at a quarter to six and taking me out to dinner &ndash; Mrs Evans called this morning she also is a charming person. Terribly sorry that she didn't see you and that her letters to you had gone astray &ndash; she wrote 3 times to the "Grantala" &ndash; I hope you will see her sometime as she was so cut up about it.</p>
