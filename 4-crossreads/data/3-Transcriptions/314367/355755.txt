<p>Thursday Feb 11-15 &ndash; Part 10 Rabaul</p>
<p>Heavens &ndash; All my 3 lbs 6 oz of quinine away on board the Navua &amp; I with but an ounce left &ndash; Rabaul &ndash; empty swept &amp; garnished &ndash; not a soul in her &ndash; The Eastern &amp; Navua gone &amp; everybody with them &ndash; The Fantome &amp; Una left &ndash; We are off on Saty.<br/>I'm mess caterer &amp; put 8 bottles of Beer in the ice chest &amp; one was stolen inside 12 hours &amp; I talked freely &amp; insisted on the fact that some steward <u>stole</u> this (I'm certain) &ndash; I've been told that my thoroughness &amp; strictness wont be liked to which I've retorted that when thieves fall out honest men come by their own &ndash; That makes 104 bottles <u>stolen</u> on the Fantome &amp; <u>129</u> stolen on the Eastern &ndash; value Eastern bottles 6-17-6 for which <u>I</u> have to pay my whack &ndash; I'm out &ndash; looking for honest men &ndash; now for a connected story &ndash;</p>
<p>W-day 10th   To Matupi to coal &ndash; started 6 am. got ended by 8 pm &amp; back to Rabaul (4 miles) by 4 pm &ndash; Ashore with Nelson (No 1) &ndash; To Lauris to sell our Soda water machine &ndash; I induce .L. to come &amp; see it &ndash; I to meet him 10 am at pier &amp; [indecipherable] him off &ndash; Then to Garrison Hospital</p>
