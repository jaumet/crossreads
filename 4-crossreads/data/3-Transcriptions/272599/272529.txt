<p>Sept. Sunday 30 1917</p>
<p>Another Gutser.   Leave list revised &amp; we are put back to 7 &amp; 9th Carty went this morng. &amp; Jock was due tomorrow.  I on Thursday.   Some dirty under hand work going on.   Oh the rotten Army.   There's corruption every where.   Is it any wonder it takes so long to win the war.  The Army is rotton throughout.     [indecipherable]  conscientious one is the greater fool.</p>
