<p>February 1915<br/>14 Sun<br/>In camp</p>
<p>15 Mon<br/>Caught 7 am train with Bdr A Cols of 2nd and 3rd Rgts to Kantara examined the advcd post 1m out where fight was. To Tussum and Serapeum by boat; saw several Turks remains. Back to Temalia and returned camp 12 midnight.</p>
