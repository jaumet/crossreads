<p>the spectators sitting round the edges were all pushed in, clothes &amp; all : One of our boys 8th of the 17th just a fraction over ten stone divided the first prize of the scratch pulling with a chap over fourteen stone as neither could gain an advantage. In the heats he pulled men over fourteen stone &amp; beat them. For his size he is a marvel. After tea our platoon fell in on the boat deck for a voluntary physical drill parade under our Sgt Wheatley. It was a success &amp; is to be held every night. Another concert on</p>
