<p>today. Marched to St. Omer and entrained there for Longpre. We travelled to Longpre via Calias and Bolougne.</p>
<p>21/10/16<br/>Arrived at Longpre about 2 a.m. and marched to Bruchamps 8 miles.</p>
<p>Bn parade to read out the sentences given by D.C.M. to two of our men one man got 15 years for desertion and the other 2 years.</p>
