<p>93<br/>across any inequalities in the roadway, ruts or small shell holes and were unfortunate enough to tread in these, the upward jolt which the container got would cause some of the contents to insert themselves down my back. This would be bad enough but by the time we had reached our destination the food would have got cold and made us feel very uncomfortable.</p>
<p>On one journey a chap who was carrying a container fell into a shell hole of water which was situated on another side of tree trunk.</p>
