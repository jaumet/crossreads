<p>1915<br/>June 11 Fri.<br/>Secured the two tunics alright &ndash; they are better than the originals but can hardly be said to look respectable.<br/>Have now over 700 in hospitals. No Australians coming in &ndash; only Imperials &ndash; it appears <strike>only</strike> all former are being sent to Malta hospitals &amp; then sent back to Egypt when alright to join new units at the Base.</p>
