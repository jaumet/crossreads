<p>Thursday 6 December 1917</p>
<p><strike>Spent today on Malta. Dolly bought 2/10/- worth of Handmade Lace.</strike>  We visited the Cathedral of St. John a beautiful place &amp; after went to the Palace where the Knights of St. John lived. I took tickets for the Opera TOSCA &amp; returned tired out. <strike>It is no use trying to save any pennies so Boomly Busted &amp; Co.</strike>  A Torpedoe is on view here that did not explode rather interesting under the circumstances to us.</p>
