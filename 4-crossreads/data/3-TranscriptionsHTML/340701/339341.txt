When we were paraded to be paid the officer said open your pay Books at page 8 every man did &amp; while they were standing by with their pay Books in their hands some one started "Onward Christian Soldiers" &amp; every man started &amp; they sang the Hymn right through despite every effort to stop them, the Officers had to laugh.  We had the opening rounds of a boxing Tournament last night.</p>
<p>18.3.16 Saturday<br/>Nothing doing</p>
<p>19.3.16<br/>Church parade. No priest on board so the Colonel presided &amp; we said the Rosary &amp; sang God save the King.</p>
