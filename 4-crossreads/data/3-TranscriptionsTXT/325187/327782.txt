
[Sunday 6 February 1916]
Church Parade good sermon always by C of E Padre  Not far off a Native road maker was also praying looking towards Mecca they bow & touch the ground with their heads repeating the Koran, this they do 3 times a day generally just as you want their services.Heard today that we are likely to go to Mersa Matrue, up along the Mediteranean for some distance towards Tripoli- via Alexandria-Letter from Lorna tonight all well with her & hersWe also have a Priest from Belgium a most interesting Padre, who knows Father O' Connell & has been all over Australia he is a good Sport, & soldier.
