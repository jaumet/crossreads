  
 Anecdote 
 A priest on his rambles round Galipoli. Well know as one of the best. Coming up to a sentry he said:/ 
 Are you an R.C.? 
 No but I am a bloody sentry sor, & if you dont hurry away them Turks over there will shoot you sure. 
 Told me by Jerrom on S.S. Demosthenes going south in the Aegean Sea. 25th October 1915, St Crispin's Day Agincourt 1915
