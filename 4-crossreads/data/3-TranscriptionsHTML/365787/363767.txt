<p>4th March, 1919</p>
<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>I have to acknowledge with many thanks receipt of your parcel, containing a donation from the Wahroonga-Warrawee War Chest Branch of 117 pairs of socks and 1 knitted muffler.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>"WAR CHEST" SUPPLY DEPOT,<br/>[Signed] J. M. Antill<br/>Hon. Superintendent<br/>per Cu.E.R.</p>
