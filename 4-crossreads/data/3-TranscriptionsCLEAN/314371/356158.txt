
Friday, May 24th
June 7thSighted land 11 a.m.  Picked up Fremantle Pilot at 1 p.m., berthed about 2 p.m.After tea Perth Ladies came on board w/- fruit & cake for us & we had a great feed.Pulled out about 9 p.m., sea very rough.
June 13thArrived Port Melbourne about midnight.  Disembarked from "Tofua" & reembarked on "Suevic" same morning, 14th June.  Sailed at noon.  Beautiful weather.
June 15thWeather continues "Bon".We are getting quite excited.
