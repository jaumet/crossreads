  
 1 January, 1916 Maadi I left the peninsular with the 1st Div Head Quarter's Baggage on 16 Dec (Thursday) (two days before the final evacuation) 'Ence to Lemnos, Had a deal of trouble with the stuff, we had to handle it seven times all told. We landed at Lemnos on Friday 17 Dec & got into camp with stuff that evening. Coulstock & Tetley & I had a good look about for a few days, visiting the villages & eating nuts & chocolate.  Some horses came however & they wanted us to groom them so we just paraded & Hastie told us that we had done good work & he did  not wish us to take on a job that we did not care for so we could go to the 4th LH & go to Egypt with them & rejoin our unit there.
