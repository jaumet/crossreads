<p>Steeplechase &amp; fortunately that was not serious. We spent one of the best days sport we had spent in this country.<br/>Sunday Dec 15<br/>It rained again to day. We received Reinforcements to day. To morrow is an inspection by G.O.C. General Allenby. We anticipate some trouble over the Bedouin affair.<br/>Monday Dec 16<br/>Inspection was held today in the square just outside the Artillery camp. The General Turned us down.
