
Tuesday 26 December 1916Bit of telephone work in morning.Inspected by G.O.C. Desert Colum in afternoon, very funny turn out;  our general got nervous, gave wrong orders.  Heavy storm.
Wednesday 27 December 1916Nothing much doing in morning.Harry Jacob, Cpl. Leaske & I on out post at night.Rained a good bit which short circuted telephone.
