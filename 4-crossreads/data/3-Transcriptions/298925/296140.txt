<p>We then went down to Amesbury where a collection was being made for the Star and Garter home for disabled soldiers. We also saw a lot of german prisoners being marched to a camp. Among them were two of the Prussian Guard.  They looked insolently happy and carefree.  They were marched to Rolleston where they were placed in a camp surrounded by barb wire entanglements.</p>
