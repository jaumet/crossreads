<p>Sunday 21 October 1917.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Fell in at 2 pm &amp; saddled up with all our gear, equipment etc &amp; 5 days rations. Thousands of troops are on the move.</p>
<p>The whole of the Imperial Camel Brigade in Palestine and the A.C.Fd Amb. are on the move where we do not know but only have an idea.</p>
<p>Halted &amp; formed up at 4.30 p.m 5 miles from Shiekh Nuran. Moved off at 5 p.m. and travelled right through <s>the</s> till midnight. No smoking or lights of any description and no noise whatever allowed.</p>
