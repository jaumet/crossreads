<p>Tuesday March 13<br/>Another day on horses. Had some good gallops which finished up in a race. Charley[?] Woods winner. Jimmy Woodcock leading when his neddy misjudged jumping trench &amp; landed him in it. The evenings drag here<br/>Wednesday March 14th<br/>Day out in sham fight with L H. Regiment. Officers simply do not know what to give the men to do to fill in time while we are waiting. Everybody fed up including myself.<br/>Thursday March 15<br/>Rain heavily all day did our best to keep dry<br/>Friday March 16<br/>Inspection by General Featherstone leave granted from tonight. Arrived</p>
