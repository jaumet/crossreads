<p>Saturday 10 August<br/>Fine day.</p>
<p>11th Bde line still crook. After much investigating &amp; annoyance ran D3 piece from pole crossing to Bde. OK.</p>
<p>Evening just on dark 23rd M.G. out. Nosing about until late but found M.G. moved up. Field Engrs &amp; Pioneers took the line.</p>
<p>To bed late.</p>
