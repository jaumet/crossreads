<p>packed up &amp; moved off for billets 6 pm : reached an old monasterie about 8 pm :</p>
<p>Saturday Aug 11th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 6 am : boys on a route march : after dinner Alf, Morrie, &amp; self went on leave to St Omer : had a good time roaming around : the "Abbey" must have been a beautiful building : gardens tres bein : had a nice feed &amp; also got some fine P.C.s : plenty of canals about this place &amp; the farmers go round their plots in boats : storm broke as we were going home &amp; the lightning was very vivid : got wet a bit :</p>
<p>Sunday Aug 12th</p>
<p>Monasterie : cloudy : Holy Com 9.30 am : stroll after dinner : went to "our house" &amp; had coffee : asked to tea tomorrow night : strolled through village &amp; then home :</p>
