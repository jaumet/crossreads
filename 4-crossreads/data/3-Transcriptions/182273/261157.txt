
<p>1918&nbsp;&nbsp; August</p>
<p>22 THUR&nbsp; Fine day very hot. rec.</p>
<p>letters from Gladys.</p>
<p>Else P.&nbsp; &amp; M<sup>r</sup> W.T.B. final prepar-</p>
<p>ations for stunt tomorrow.</p>
<p>Quiet. resting in aft. Moved</p>
<p>forward. 9.PM &amp; bivouacked near</p>
<p>Morecourt &amp; Mericourt. very quiet</p>
<p>heavy bombing at night.</p>
<p>Thomas &amp; Clingham went to Aussie</p>
<p>23 FRI&nbsp; Reveille 2 am. Fine day. Warm</p>
<p>Stunt opened 4.40 am. very success-</p>
<p>ful. prisoners in galore. very quick</p>
<p>Objective reached &amp; consolidated</p>
<p>Guns moved forward. Rec. letters from</p>
<p>May &amp; Amy &amp; watch. Heavy shelling</p>
<p>&amp; bombs at night on both sides.</p>
<p>24 SAT&nbsp; Fine day warm &amp; cloudy.</p>
<p>Fairly quiet on both sides</p>
<p>Rec. PC. from Celie &amp; letter</p>
<p>Amy Mc. Few small batches</p>
<p>of prisoners coming in.</p>
<p>Horses grazing. Heavy</p>
<p>bombing at night. &amp; heavy</p>
<p>firing towards daybreak</p>
<p>(Sun) Fritz reported to be</p>
<p>evacuating. Writing.</p>
