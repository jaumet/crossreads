 February 1918 Thursday 28 
 Messines Walked across to Tank &ndash;pos and spent day with Gun. Received a quantity of mail including the remains of a parcel passed through customs. Weather delightful. Great scare here, O.P. manned at 4 am. Fritz had a stunt near Ypres.- no results to hand yet. Fritz bracketed our position  with.5.9, and two 8 inch. He also bracketed the 53rd Bty. He also executed quite a considerable amount of registering on the road and  Wewique. 1 Fritz plane down
