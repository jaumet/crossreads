
In the Trenches13 Oct 16My dearest Irene,Was very delighted to receive your letter dated 20 Aug & am glad you are so pleased - You will long ere this have heard that I have been awarded a Military medal for stretcher bearing at the first Posieres action.Well - I have just come back from England after having 10 days leave there.
