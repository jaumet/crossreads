The battalion is undergoing some solid training physical drill in the mornings &amp; route marches in the afternoon. Yesterday we went for about a five mile march, the country is lovely here. Last night I got my eye hurt which meant I had to stay inside today. The weather is beautiful Pont Remy Wed. Oct 16 &ndash; 16 We left Outtersteene about 2 pm, marched to Bailleul, &amp; entrained. We did not enter Bailleul, entrained at outskirts. We left Bailleul about 4 pm &amp; arrived here about 4 Am the next morning. This is real France &amp; is much prettier than Flanders. In Flanders the country is flat, the towns are uninteresting, but here it is an entire change, hilly country &amp; beautiful towns.
