14/8/16Nothing doing other than making a nuisance of oneself about the place  Band played here today Aust old tunes which our old band used to play 31stup early morning shoulder torn muscles  not fractured
19/8/16Went before Board & was passed for Blighty  will be on the move tomorrow  went for a walk
20/8/16Woke up about 4 oclock this morn for to get ready for the old Dart  train to leave about 6 oclock into ambulance to train put in OK fine carriages on train  long one  we got a cup of tea & piece of bread & then we started as the saying goes for Calais  we arrived there about 12 oclock after a beautiful journey we passed through Boulogne
