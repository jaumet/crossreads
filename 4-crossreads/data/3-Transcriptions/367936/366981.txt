<p>Saturday 20 May 1916<br/>Fairly cool.<br/>Fell out of my hammock last night but landed on my feet alright.<br/>Sergt. Smith in hospital.</p>
<p>Sunday 21 May 1916<br/>Rained all day.<br/>Had afternoon off and wrote letters home.<br/>On picquet at night.<br/>Had to give horses lucerne feed at 8 p.m.<br/>My hammock broke down in the night.  I did <u>not</u> land on my feet!  Worse luck!</p>
