
16 June, Monday
F & lovely cool day, fair swell.
I give up Hospital duty. Take English lecture & read all afternoon. I go up on Duty for 2 hours to lelieve. A chap gets 106. What a fuss! I read & Jack & I play German whist all night. I also play Patience but cheat like m--------. I see M est grande on French Blackboard.
17 June, Tuesday
F.No duties. I go on deck to study, come down below to study. Boat has heavy list to port. She rocks surprisingly for such calm weather.
Partnerships again all afternoon. I do no bon neither do the others. Jack puggarees my hat. Our Boys win the special prize for cleanliness. Another boat drill. I larrickinise with no boats.
