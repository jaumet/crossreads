 Sunday. 25.8.18. Brekker at 9.30. Went for a swim in the morning. Went for a stroll after tea. After dinner played football & did a bit of a sing song. It started to rain about 9pm rained extra heavy. 
 Monday 26.8.18. Rained to some order last night. Started our routine this morning. Standing orders & fitting of equipment etc. Had squad drill (turning by numbers etc) Physical training & a lecture on Leadership. Went to some pictures after dinner & they were very good.
