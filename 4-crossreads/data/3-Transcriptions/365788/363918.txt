<p>8<br/>No happenings of any importance to report on today. We are voyaging without an escort, but a warship appeared to port astern of us about &frac12; an hour ago. - Darkness begins to enrap us &amp; soon the light will be too bad to write by.<br/>No lights are allowed above deck after 5.P.M. &amp; all portholes are shut at that hour &ndash; Lights out on troop deck at 9.P.M.
