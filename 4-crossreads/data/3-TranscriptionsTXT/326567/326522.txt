Monday 23rd/7/17 At 3 am this morning Fritz was over again, but this time I did not stir I simply sat up &amp; put my Steel Helmet &amp; lit a Cigarette &amp; had a smoke &amp; listened to the play outside after awhile things quietened again &amp; I dosed off &amp; slept until 7 am, feeling tired as I had very little sleep all night.  Tuesd   Friday 27/7/17 After a quiet morning the Bombardment increased in violence &amp; continued practically all night, word came from the Front line this evening that Fritz has cleared back 3 or 4 miles but our Lads are not advancing for a while as a trap may be set for them anyhow we are pummelling his Trenches to make sure We can guage how things are advancing by our Observation Balloons.
