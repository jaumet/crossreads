 October 1916 Saturday 7 Left Qara 8.30am for the 150 or so miles across the desert . Train broke down at 12 noon after climbing the escarp. Another engine sent on and we left at 12 MN arrived Sherika 6 am Sunday . No camels for my section so will have to wait. 
 Sunday 8 We formed the full comp' to a company of camel Corps . 180 strong.
