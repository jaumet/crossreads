<p>We have 2 seaplanes and 2 observation balloons up observing all day and directing the fire of our guns. The turks keep up a continual fire at our planes with their antiaircraft guns rifles and machine guns, but up to the present have failed to hit any of them.</p>
<p>All transports left here on the 13.5.15. It looks quite desolate without them. Caught sight of the first</p>
