<p>4-11-20<br/>"Nevlill"  30 Aug<br/>Kogarah</p>
<p>Age 6 mths<br/>Weight 20 lbs</p>
<p>Dear Auntie<br/>Received your welcome letter today and will be very pleased to come over and see you. We have promised to go with the kiddies to their SS Picnic next Sat the 6th so I expect we will come over Sat the 18th I will ring up and let you know for certain later hoping uncle is quite well again  Love to all Ida  &amp; Wal,</p>
