<p>heard she is not coming back, so am rather scared that I shall not be allowed to return. I have been the longest here. Hope I shall be left in England if I'm moved from here.</p>
<p>Have written to Mts. Barrie's sisters this week also Mr Copps brother at Porch House Yorrington so may visit them if get to Blighty again. Received a nice letter from Charlie to-day   &amp;  he is very well, they are a long way from us now but think we may follow soon.</p>
