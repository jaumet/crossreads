<p>1917 July</p>
<p>22 Sunday</p>
<p>Staggered out at six to get early breakfast with Tretin &amp; had a little nap at nine &amp; got dinner. Had a sleep in afternoon &amp; arose to fry potatoes for evening meal. Dr Bennett conducted a quaint service in mess tent attended by 6 women plus Major Groden. Very hot &amp; mosquitoes a curse also flies wasps &amp; fleas &amp; ants.</p>
