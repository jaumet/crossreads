<p>7/12/17.<br/>5 N.C.O's and men rejoined unit from leave to the United Kingdom. 1 man evacuated to hospital (sick) and 1 man rejoined unit from hospital.</p>
<p>8/12/17.<br/>Nothing to report.</p>
<p>9/12/17.<br/>3 men reported to Unit from leave to U.K, and 1 man evacuated to hospital (sick). 1 section of 13 N.C.O's and men baked 6920 lb rations.</p>
<p>10/12/17.<br/>40 N.C.O's and men baked 23040 lb rations, and 33 N.C.O's and men were on duty at the Base Supply Depot: the balance on staff and camp fatigues. Notification was received from Officer i/c Records, 3rd Echelon. Anzac Section that 2 men were invalided to England from hospital and thereby struck off strength.</p>
<p>11/12/17.<br/>40 N.C.O's and men baked 25040 lb rations.</p>
