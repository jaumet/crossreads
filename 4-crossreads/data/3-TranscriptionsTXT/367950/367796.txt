 17th March 1917 H.T. WILLOCHRA 
 We have been detained here since the 12th instant on account of a German Raider which has got loose somewhere in the Indian Ocean. This raider had another ship with her which was laying mines &ndash; dropping them anywhere. The minelayer has been captured &ndash; or sunk &ndash; but the raider is still at large and she is armed with 6 guns. Several ships came in yesterday from Colombo escorted by a French Battleship.
