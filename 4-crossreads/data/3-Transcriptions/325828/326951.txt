<p>I haven't seen Rid since he &amp; I went up to see Wal in his dug-out.  He had a cold in his chest at the time, which caused his voice to give out but he was recovering the use of it gradually<br/>I am keeping well &amp; everything is running smoothly here.  I hope you are all keeping in good health, &amp; free from the usual winter complaints.<br/>About a fortnight ago I sent a parcel containing all my Egyptian negatives which I hope reached you safely.<br/>Must close now   With love to you all at "Cairnleith,"<br/>Your loving son,<br/>Leo.</p>
