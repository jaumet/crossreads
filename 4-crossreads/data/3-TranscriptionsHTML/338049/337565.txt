<p>Saturday 21 Dec.<br/>Up &amp; away by 9 am train to Belfast. Nearly missed train owing to cab driver.</p>
<p>In Belfast 12 noon. Saw B. Had dinner. Went to Laws 3 p.m. Had afternoon tea &ndash; pleasant afternoon. Then to Stn to go to Glasgow. Farewelled by B. Good trip to Larne.</p>
<p>Very cold to Stranaer but sea calm. Arrived Glasgow 2 a.m. after sleepy warm trip. Bed reserved at Grand. OK.</p>
