<p>Left Cairo for unknown destination on 3.4.15. We marched to Cairo station, a distance of 12 miles and entrained for Alex. the following day. We embarked on board Minnawaska which has a cargo consisting of Div. Hd.Qrt. Staff a Company of Engineers a company of A.S.C. First Bn. and about 600 horses and mules. Alex harbour is crowded with transports which are packed with British Indian and Australian troops.</p>
