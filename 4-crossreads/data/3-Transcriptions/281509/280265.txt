<p>12 February 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Reveille 6 a.m. Physical drill first time for months. Tony explains absence from holiday.  Rotten time at Gundagai.<br/>MacIndoe had a bad time with roughs in Sydney last night.  Lost spurs, numerals &amp; everything movable.<br/>11.30 parade &amp; down to Ashfield for lunch.<br/>Took it easy for the afternoon &amp; night.  Ess &amp; self went to Pratten Park about 4 p.m watched long-jumping &ndash; 20 ft 10 1/8 ins. &amp; bike race.  One chap fell &amp; sustained severe gravel rash.</p>
