<p>move to morrow<br/>Wednesday Dec 26<br/>It was raining &amp; cold &amp; the roads were in an awful state. We were bogged several times crossing a flat near Yebnah. Yebnah is the usual type of filthy village. Our Horses were absolutely bogged at times &amp; there was nothing else for it but to get off &amp; sink in the mud ourselves. A few Horses were drowned . The Artillery we passed seemed to be hopelessly bogged in the mud. We arrived at Ezdud just on dark and what it a mess it was. It was under water A train there was practically marooned.
