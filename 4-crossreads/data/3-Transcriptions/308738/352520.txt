<p>Sept. 25 Saturday 1915<br/>Working all day. Heavy lists coming in again from different hospitals. Sheppard one of the Staff leaves today for Base Mustapha where he will prepare for the front.<br/>Saw Shell on Grices dressing table similar to one taken from office.</p>
<p>Sept. 26 Sunday 1915<br/>Worked from 6 to 8. 30 knocked off for the day went to Church with Will Pethard both morning &amp; night &ndash; afternoon spent in taking Watkins out to Noosa Park. We both enjoyed the beautiful scenery.<br/>After church at night went round to Y. M. C. A. &amp; heard Rev Watson preach. He spoke simply &amp; yet with great force on resolutions. It was a fine address to men.</p>
<p>Sept. 27 Monday 1915<br/>Wrote home to Maude also to Miss Sarginson of  W Maitland re papers books etc sent.<br/>Received 2 letters from Maude dated 15 &amp; 22nd August also one from Mother &amp; Mr Palethorpe.</p>
