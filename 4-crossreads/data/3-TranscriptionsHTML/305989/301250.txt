<p>Sunday 1 April 1917<br/>Church parade in morning.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic number for 2 and 5].</p>
<p>Monday 2 April 1917<br/>1st Regt. v F.A. &amp; M.G. combined draw no score.</p>
<p>Tuesday 3 April 1917<br/>Played 1st Field Troop lost 3-0, two other games in progress.</p>
<p>Wednesday 4 April 1917<br/>Brigade Races in the Wadi did not go:  on picquet at night.</p>
<p>Thursday 5 April 1917<br/>Clarrie came back off leave.  4th Brigade came through:  saw all the boys:  getting ready to move eastwards.</p>
<p>Friday 6 April 1917<br/>Left El Arish in morning coming through El Arish &amp; passing 4th Brigade, proceeded to Shiek Zowaiid, camped the night.</p>
<p>Saturday 7 April 1917<br/>Left Shiek Zowaiid early &amp; got to Rafa about dinnertime &amp; saw places familiar in the scrap, arrived at Khan Yunis about 4 o'clock:  &amp; camped after watering up.</p>
