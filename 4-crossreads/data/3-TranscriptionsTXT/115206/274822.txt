
September 1918.Monday 9
In Reserve position 4 miles from Peronne.A very heavy downfall of rain from 7am to 11am.Received a letter from home, also a visit from Fritz 7 planes - no damage.Had another nice little spell today. Salvaged a little wire, not very much.Major inspected guns.Wrote to Doug, Ken Gunn and Cousin Stell.Had a feed of good quaker oats.Rumors that the Corps is pulling out very shortly. Hope that is correct.
