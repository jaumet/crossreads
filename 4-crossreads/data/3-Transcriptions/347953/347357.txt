<p>Received letters from home &amp; friends and was extremely pleased.</p>
<p>We heard some news here &amp; were sorry to hear the fate of the warship "Formidable" in the English Channel but nevertheless things seem to be going on satisfactory.</p>
<p>We arrived in Aden in the 23rd Jan  in the morning &amp; left in the evening nothing to see there except the Arabs who sold cigarettes &amp; dates to us from their dingies.</p>
<p>We are now passing through the red sea &amp; expect to reach Port Said by Monday.</p>
