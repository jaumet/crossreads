<p>German has always something up his sleeve.  They had flooded many of their trenches with water, and our men had to go through the muddy water up to their waists.  Hour after hour the enemy guns were playing right and left, and during the night the right and left flanks were forced to withdraw.  This left the Australians "in the air" and the Boches began advancing down their communication trenches on each flank until they almost enveloped our men, who by this time had fully occupied the German works in the centre.
