<p>Monday 9 June 1919<br/>Beautiful morning. Got 2 letters from Bill. He crashed for leave. Real stiff. Went into town twice. Second time to post some letter. Rained in the evening.</p>
<p>Tuesday 10 June 1919<br/>Took Arthur to the dentist &amp; he had a tooth out Has to go again on Tuesday to get two filled. After lunch went to Queens Park &amp; heard the Scotch Guard Band play. Was bonnie. They played the 'Missouri Waltz' &amp; Chu Chin Chow. Went  to the pictures in the evening. Rain again this evening.</p>
