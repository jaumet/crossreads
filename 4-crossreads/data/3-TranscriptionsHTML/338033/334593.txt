<p>taken to tea to Lyons by<br/>Mr Rowe, who then drew out a programme for the four days.<br/>Lyons are the Sargeants of London and about as good a place as any in London. I was taken to the YMCA at Tottenham Court Rd where I lodged for the night. After booking my bed, I took a stroll round Regent St way. After a good night sleep, between sheets, which seemed a bit strange at first, I had a light breakfast and then set out for morning sight-seeing. I made the Bank Of England my starting point and went down King William St</p>
