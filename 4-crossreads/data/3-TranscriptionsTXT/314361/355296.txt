 This Diary begins in Aug 1915 Sunday Aug. 8th   15th Liverpool Camp Have volunteered, joined camp last Thursday week. Just now am in Q 3 Coy, but expect to get transfer to Signallers tomorrow. Camp life not too pleasant, food bad &amp; far too rough Aug 25th Contact Camp Am now in Contact Camp. There was a case of measles in my tent, so we had to come to this place. Will be here for ten days. Then we have to have four days leave. There are only two in my tent in this camp. We each drew 24 hours rations.
