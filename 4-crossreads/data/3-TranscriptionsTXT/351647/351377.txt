 But its an enthralling thing &amp; a great gift &amp; I'm sure will be a great thing for you.  I hope you will let me know how it is getting on.  Really I am pleased &amp; so interested.  You must not say you have no brains, you certainly have &amp; rather more than most women so there.  How is the new book keeper, I am dying to get your next letter to hear about him. 
 Fancy the river being so high &amp; how did the shearing go. 
 I must to bed my dear its after 12 &amp; I have to get up early as I sleep in the office-cum-mess room.
