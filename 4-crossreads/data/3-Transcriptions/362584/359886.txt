<p>Desert<br/>28-1-16</p>
<p>My dear Mother,</p>
<p>This is the first chance I've had to write since we moved from Tel-el-Kebir, but as no mails have been dispatched since then it really makes no difference. We are supposed to be on active service again, but have as yet seen very little enemy, although we are breaking our necks to do so. They are not so very far away, &amp; with any luck we will have some good sport &ndash; the sort of stuff that ought to suit us fifty times better than squatting down in trenches being bombarded with big guns.
