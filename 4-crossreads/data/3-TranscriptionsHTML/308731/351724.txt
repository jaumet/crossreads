<p>[Comment added by Frank's mother.]<br/>This is a very old one of Franks he has been away so much this week did not write any fresh ones.</p>
<p>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill</p>
<p>Dear old Bill Burrowes.</p>
<p>George and Bill went up the hill to fetch a pail of water.  George fell down and broke his crown and bill came tumbling After and Anna told me that. and Mum has gorn to Sydney To day and I am home with Anna and Anna said she found a pair of your garters. and do you know who is George George is Mr flowers and dont you think I am cheeky to call him georg in sted of Mr flowers and calling you Bill Burrowes.</p>
<p>So good bye</p>
