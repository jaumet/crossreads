The first few days were very rough. One man fell over board & was drowned. Lt Christiansen died while in Sierra Leon & was buried ashore. Stewed in the harbour at Sierra Leon - Temperature generally about 100 degrees & rain nearly everyday - till October 2nd, when we left in company of "Orontes" & Durham Castle & escorted by Battleship Afric.
October 15th  Arrived in Table Bay about daylight & stayed still Saturday the 20th. Allowed ashore every day till 11 pm. The Capetown people were just kindness in the very best
