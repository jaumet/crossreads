<p>Monday 8 November, 1915</p>
<p>Went into Valletta &amp; made arrangements to have our teeth done.</p>
<p>Had a good time.</p>
<p>Put R.M.L.I. [Royal Marine Light Infantry] badges in our caps so we could get beer served to us but it wouldn&#39;t work much.</p>
<p>Visited the Admiralty arrived home early 8.20 p.m.</p>
