
9-4-17No. 2 Section again on road repairs at Grevillers.  Very bad weather.  Still doing well on all Fronts.
10thNo. 2 Sec. engaged building huts for Coy.  Good news through of big advance by British near Arras, 10,000 prisoners taken & about 100 Guns includ. 8" & 4.9 Guns.
11thStill good news from British Front where the big advance started on the 9th.  Still working on Rds.
12thStill working on Rds.  It is now said that British near Arras have captured 200 Guns.
13thStill working about Bapaum.  1st & 4th Divs. got a rough handling this morning near Hernicourt but eventually turned defeat into victory on being reinforced by the 2nd Div.
14thStill on roads etc. about Bapaum.  On our Front yesterday the Hun captured 16 of our 18 [indecipherable] destroying 2 of them but they were all recaptured.
15thIt is now reported that the British have captured 14,000 prisoners & 194,000 194 guns.
16thIn the engagement on our Front we captured 300 prisoners & it is estimated that at least 1500 of the enemy were killed.
17thBig French success in the Champagne district on 25 mile Front.  Things still going well on the whole British Front.
