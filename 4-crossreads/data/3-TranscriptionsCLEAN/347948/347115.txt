Leading from the wood is a good, metalled road (all the roads are good) about 30? wide and on each side of it is, respectively a field of rye and oats. We crossed the River Avon which at this end, is only a narrow stream, and is literally teeming with trout. There are houses here 3 & 4 hundred years old  Built of flints & looking as solid as ever.  This one was built 1600 A.D.  [sketch of houses]
