 1st Sept. 
 2nd Sept Nearer &amp; nearer to land so that Durban should not be overlooked. 
 Nearly put into East London in mistake, because of similarity of their town halls. 
 Can this be the main which is forging ahead at a found speed of 11 knots? 
 3rd  Found Durban last night but such was the speed of the boat that it was found impossible to stop her short of Zanzibar.  Turned around &amp; made Durban by morning.  Dutch boat sighted overhauling us rapidly.  The [indecipherable] let her get level, then with a speed which shook everybody off their feet  she dashed ahead just in time to prevent miss Campbell signalling a hearty welcome to Herr Hertzog, who was on the Dutch boat.
