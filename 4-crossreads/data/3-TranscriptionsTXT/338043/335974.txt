 Sept. 17th More Australian &amp; English Letters, all of which contain good bright news &ndash; "Non-Suited" still going strong. Rehearsal of "Eastern Romance". 
 Sept. 18th Rehearsal &amp; Show &ndash; Change of programme &ndash; Letter writing etc. &ndash; My memory plays me false for the first time in the History of "The Coves" &ndash; I recite part of Xmas Carol, and then suddenly came to an abrupt stop.
