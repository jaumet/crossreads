 December 1  st    Yes Bailleul at last - I went into an egg & chip joint to get warm & have breakfast & then went to Marie Louise's joint as I expected she would know the whereabouts of the section. I met some of the boys at her place on the way to Brulooge Railhead for supplies, so I stayed in the warm till they got back again at 3.P.M. my buss with a broken spring - Our park is just outside Bailleul on the Hazebrouck road parked with 5  th   D.S.C.  I feel very sorry to be back again - 
   
 Dec 2  nd    Took the broken spring off & left it in the blacksmith shop to be repaired & being Sunday we had a half day off. Weather very cold & windy -
