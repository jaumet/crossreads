<p>coming out this way; at 12 noon we struck the foothills of the Judaean mountains. We are now in a country of rocky escarpments &amp; giant boulders amongst which grow the most beautiful and varegated wild flowers a great number of English flowers interspersed with others more delicate subtropical species. Altogether this promises to be a most interesting trip.</p>
<p>We are now camped by the side of the Jaffa-Jerusalem road</p>
