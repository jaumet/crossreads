<p>29th Friday<br/>Weather still good.  Heavy Gun fire on both flanks also some battery work on &amp; about our front.  Turks began fierce Bomb throwing again 4-30 to 5 p.m., remainder of night very quiet.</p>
<p>30th Saturday<br/>Weather good &amp; bright.  Bread &amp; meat been off since 27th.  Gale.</p>
<p>31st Sunday<br/>Weather continues good.  Bread &amp; Meat issued.  Front quiet.  Used Japs Mortar 9-30 p.m. to quieten Turks with their Broom Stick Bombs playing on Pope's.  Out for same reason again 1-30 a.m.</p>
