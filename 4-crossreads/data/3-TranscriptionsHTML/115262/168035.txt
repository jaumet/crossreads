<p>Tuesday 25 September 1917.</p>
<p>1 Taube today came over &amp; immediately he was gone up went our planes as usual.</p>
<p>Accident at aerodrome.</p>
<p>R.F.C. from Ismailia moving into same grounds as the Australian aerodrome here at Shiekh Nuran. An active fighting unit on arrival.</p>
