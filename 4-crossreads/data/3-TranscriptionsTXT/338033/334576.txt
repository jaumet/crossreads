
We saw quite a number of human bones and wondered if anyone really knew who was buried there. I might add that this is where the British square was first broken in the year 1881.The following day I had the great pleasure of meeting Bob Uright and having a good meal at a native restaurant  and  going to the pictures afterwards. On the 21st I went to service in the Y.M.C.A. and took the Sacrement for the first time in my life. Two days later we went for a route march along the canal and sawThe irrigation farms worked by wooden ploughs drawn by oxen
