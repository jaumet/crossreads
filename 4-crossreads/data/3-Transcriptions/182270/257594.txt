
<p>THURSDAY, OCTOBER 9, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Round bottom end in</p>
<p>morning &amp; brought all the cattle up</p>
<p>to Kimber&#39;s Yards. Darby brought</p>
<p>me in to Quirindi in the car in</p>
<p>afternoon and I got the 9.30 train</p>
<p>to Sydney.</p>
<p>Tickle shifted Darby up to</p>
<p>Middle paddock bore.</p>
