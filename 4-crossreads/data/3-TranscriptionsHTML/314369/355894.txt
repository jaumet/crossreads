<p>24 September 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Proceed to make myself at Home which is not difficult. There are very few restrictions. Leave is granted to a limited number each day   &amp;  the remainder go out without leave if they feel so inclined</p>
<p>1915 September 25<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>After dinner today I make a tour of the Ghizeh Pyramid district.<br/>Visit the Pyramid of Cheops  &amp;  the smaller ones in the vicinity as well as the Sphinx   &amp;  excavated tombs, some of which are covered with Heirogliphics   &amp;  carving</p>
