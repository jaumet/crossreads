 July 1918 Wednesday 31 
 Ribemont. Bty Position Punctually at 12.30 the line parted company under Fritz barrage. Robbie our best chap was knocked through the shoulder. We spent well over an hour on the lines and eventually fixed them all up.  Weather very wet and misty spent afternoon on O.P. line. Fritzie having very many duty shoots.  Out battery got on to a convoy of amm wagons and made them move at the hickory and some Francs. Fritz shelled and bombed the wood up till midnight.
