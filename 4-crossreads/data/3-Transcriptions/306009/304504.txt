<p>1915<br/>Nov. 18 Thu.<br/>Another big batch of 93 reinforcements arrived from N.S.W. &amp; Queensland for us last night. Goodness only knows what we are going to do with them all, there being fully 150 occupying their time with drill &amp; marching only. There is nothing doing on the Peninsula &amp; A.M.C. work in Egypt is pretty slack.<br/>Our staff at Gezira to-day is :-<br/>Officers - 34<br/>Men - 385<br/>Nurses - 75<br/>Natives - 75 = 569<br/>There are only 400 patients in Hospital.</p>
