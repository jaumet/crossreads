  
 [Letter from Australian Military Forces - 3rd Military District Headquarters, Melbourne, 23 Mar 1920] Miss E.S. Thistlethwaite,                                                                                                                                            "Ilkley"                                                                                                                                                                                                       Cr. Orrong & Alma Roads,                                                                                                                                          EAST ST KILDA    Dear Madam, With reference to your claim for Extra Duty Pay as Sister in Charge "Llanstephen Castle", I have to inform you that same has been approved of, and a cheque for 2.7.0 will be posted to you in a few days time. This amount is made up as follows -  14.2.18 - 16.4.18  -  62 days @ 1/- .........3.2.0 Less allotment overpaid 26.11.18 - 28.11.18 - 3 days @ 5/-...............15.0 2.7.0 Yours faithfully, H.W.Cooley Lieut-Colonel, District Finance Officer, 3rd MD
