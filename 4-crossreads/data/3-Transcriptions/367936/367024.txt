<p>Monday 14 August 1916<br/>Wrote to Father (11).  Was enoculated for Cholora in afternoon.<br/>Heard that there are a couple of cases of Cholora in Tommy camp at Katia.</p>
<p>Tuesday 15 August 1916<br/>Got eighteen letters in one bunch.  Three from home 6th June, 8th June, Father 8th July (7) and 5th July No. 6.<br/>From Vida 26 June, Mrs. Nicholl.<br/>Issued with a lot of backsheesh tucker from War Chest Fund.</p>
