<p>A &amp; B Section engaged in a friendly football match during afternoon.  A Sec won 10 points to 5.</p>
<p>Sunday 9/1/16<br/>Cold morning; still in hospital.  Church parade held, but unable to go owing to being in hospital.  Col Tivey inspected camp.</p>
<p>Monday 10/1/16<br/>Out of bed first time for a week. Went for a stroll along the canal, great activity on both banks.  Received pay.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11/1/16<br/>Grand morning.  Arab died in hospital  Met Cpl Brabner of 7th Aust Field Engineers.<br/>Pts Fisher &amp; Priest reported missing from previous day; search party organised &amp; found them 10.30 AM.</p>
