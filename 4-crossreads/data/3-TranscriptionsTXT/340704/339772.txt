 May 1917 
 Saturday 5 Left Regt near Gamli for bomb school Biltoun. 5 hours ride before reaching train Khan Zunus Travelling all night in the train 
 Sunday 6 Arrived Kantara 5.30 am. Had breakfast saw Olsson. Arrived Zeitoun 4 pm. 
 Mrs Chisholm and Miss Mc Phillamy are doing great work for the boys at Kantara in their refreshment room
