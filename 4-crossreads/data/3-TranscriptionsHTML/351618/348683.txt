<p>It took me a long time to get warm when I went between my blankets to-night but I pitied the Infantry who were not so well placed as we were. Some of the poor beggars had only arrived from Lemnos &amp; had to dig positions while it was snowing.</p>
<p>Monday 29th Nov.<br/>To-day I received orders for Canteen goods fixed up lists in preparation &amp; issued the Headquarters lot. It was devilish cold &amp; the snow was still thick on the ground.
