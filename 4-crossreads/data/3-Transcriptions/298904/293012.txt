<p>Up in the trenches for the last 2 or 3 days we lived like Kings" for all the stores were being destroyed &amp; you could go &amp; get your pick, the one thing the military were particular in destroying was the rum they broke hundreds of stone jars up &amp; it was the best thing they could have done. For if the men had got among the rum theres no one knows what might have happened.<br/>All the tracks leading to the beach were being enlarged &amp; little steep places cut down &amp; making steps in them so as 3 or 4 men could go abreast, the ground was frozen hard &amp; all along the tracks men had been chopping little chunks out</p>
