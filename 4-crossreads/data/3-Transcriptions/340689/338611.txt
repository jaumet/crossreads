<p>12.10.17. Windy &amp;  wet all day but not so cold and I wrote letters after putting house in order. Bed early. Rest went for a bath at 2.30 but I dodged it and we also have to-morrow off before starting on Dump to load.</p>
<p>13.10.17 Fine  &amp;  not cold but thunderstorm in morning. Carried some wood  &amp; spent rest of day writing in dugout.</p>
<p>14.10.17.  7 men out for loading &amp; 4 carpenters. I stayed in and during day wrote &amp; cleaned up   fine day</p>
<p>15.10.17. Fine. Some loading   I off and wrote &amp; read</p>
<p>16. Out with No 3 at 11 and motor'd to Birr X Road from Kruisstraat  Walked to job on line between Westhoek &amp; Zonnebeke laying mule tracks. Shelled out. Penny wounded by H.E.shrap'n  Left at 4. Day fine.</p>
<p>17. Out at 4 and onto job. Finished to Switch. Walked to Zonnebeke to look for breaks. Shelled out and home at 10.30 after sheltering in Pill boxes.</p>
<p>18.10.17. Worked hard from 8 to 5 building our new dugout. Fine day.</p>
