<p>May 1916<br/>3rd  (continued)   at 4 PM. &amp; proceeded to Camp.  The "Royal George" with Indian troops, which arrived yesterday, proceeded through the Canal this morning &amp; the "Star of Victoria" arrived in port at about 9 a.m.<br/><strike>I may state here that</strike>  I was not sorry to leave the ship, although we have had a wonderful voyage &amp; like most of the troops, have enjoyed it well.  The weather here is moderately cool &amp; the last two nights have been very cool, different to what we have had the other part of the voyage.<br/>7th   This is Sunday &amp; we are still at Tel-el-Kibir.  The weather is very dry &amp; hot during the day &amp; cold at night.  The camp is a very large one &amp; we have about 800</p>
