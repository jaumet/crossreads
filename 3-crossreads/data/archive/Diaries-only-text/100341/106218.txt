June 20
Church Parade 6-15 a.m. got Pass left for Cairo 10-30 with 4 others. Spoke to 1st woman since 12/5/15 at Resturant. Visited Pyramids after dinner, got back Cairo 6-30 p.m., after tea had a look round city getting to camp 10 p.m.
June 21
Coy. on Duty. Cpl. 6 men to Guard Nurses Home. Ibraheim [Ibrahim] Palace. Mounted 10 a.m. Visited Heliopolis shopping 5 p.m. to 6.
June 22
Took films to be developed printed 9-30. Visited by Capt. Kirke, all well.
