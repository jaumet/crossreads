<p>parliament, Generals and admirals are a few of the many<br/>Figures to be seen. The two most real are a policeman standing at the entrance, in an attitude of showing someone in and a porter sitting down just going off to sleep. I caught a motor bus back to Piccadilly and went in to have a look at the art gallery. Quite a number of artists were here taking copies of different works of art they fancied. Coming out I had a look at Trafalgar Square which has a monument in the centre and four lions at each corner. A Short distance from here is <strike>Leishesie</strike>  Leichester</p>
