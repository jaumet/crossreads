  
 The hot wind, the Khamasin causes a yellow fog to prevail owing to the sand & dust it brings from the desert.  It may blow for three days at a time.  In spite of this all plants flourish.  The land is truly a land flowing with milk & greenstuff.  The only honey we saw was imported & very poor. Cairo is like a dirty mixture of Naples & Colombo.  Cairo is the largest city in Africa & contains the residence of the Khedive.  It is a mixture of old & new.
