The Officer in charge of the A hospital staff on board is Lieut. Col. Fuhr, D.S.O.
We also passed the Lisbon on the coast of Portugal today.  On the other side of the Bay of Biscay is a point called the Ushant.  Anchored in the stream off Southampton 4 a.m. 27th.  We can't see land for a heavy fog.
