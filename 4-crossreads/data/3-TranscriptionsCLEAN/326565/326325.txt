  
 of the Balmain boys.  Supper Y.M.C.A. 
 Sunday 19/3/16 Rained heavily during night; five of the tents swamped out; quite an unusual occurence in this part. Church parade 9.30 AM 8th  Brigade parade ground; service conducted by Capt Chaplain Beverage. Played team from 30th Btn & won by six wickets & 6 runs - Scores 30th Btn, 52 & 67.  We scored 66 & 4 for 59.  Scored 14 first innings 32 not out second innings & took 3 for 9.  Went on piquet duty as acting piquet corporal at 6 pm.
