  
 Had charge of the coy, again this morning, today a lot of the Guard regiments marched through here,the Irish Guard had their band playing as they came along, they are splendid looking troops alright all over 6ft and spick and span, they appeared to me to just have come over from Blighty. The Coldstream and Grenadiers also went through.  Saw some great train loads of fresh meat go through for the French Army .
