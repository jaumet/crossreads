1916   MONDAY 16   OCTOBER
Fine & fairly warm & Sunny  I went out with the right section & headquarters in morning as a horse holder We came into action in Fargo Clump, but the Colonel did not seem to be quite satisfied with it. We all had a vote on the Conscription referendum in afternoon. Cleaning harness in the rest of the afternoon. Poor old Poss went into hospital with measles in the morning. He has had no luck at all.
