<p>July 1915</p>
<p><strike>Saturday 31</strike></p>
<p>the men along the wharf officers powerless to quieten men.  Mutiny the order of the day.</p>
<p>Two casualties brought to me for first aid.</p>
<p>Gillam pierced in lung &amp; butted in stomach.</p>
<p>Somerville fell in water but rescued.</p>
<p><u>Saturday</u></p>
<p>Men refused to fall in</p>
<p>[indecipherable], minister for defence arrived &amp; gave a speech to men.<br/>I went to Base Hosp. with sick men &amp; on return after sending wires to Rita &amp; Grafton</p>
