<p>September 1916<br/>Sunday 19<br/>Fine lovely morning. Had church parade every man shaved and with his best gear on and belt and bayonet. felt quite brand new again<br/>Had another issue of clothing</p>
<p>Monday 20<br/>Fine Cool breeze. We did  some station[?] work with flags from 9 AM. A team of sailors played a team of Australians soccer but I do not know who won. The sailors were very fast.</p>
