<p>W10 Sun<br/>&amp; Dull. E &amp; I go all around The Old Cathedral. Dinner in Hotel very good.<br/>Go to [indecipherable] &amp; I [indecipherable] which E. likes hot. Go to [indecipherable] met [Howard] Cpl Burke several other NCOs that we know. Have bonnie time [indecipherable] after very good day.<br/>Wrote Dad.</p>
<p>W 11 Mon<br/>Dull &amp; wet after 9 am. We go down the Town but return to march out. Only 20 men on parade &amp; we can't so we all go down Town again together. I go all around the Place &amp; drink. 2 glasses vin blanc<br/>Wrote M.R.L. P.C's to Dad M.R.L &amp; Home. Sleep in [indecipherable]</p>
<p>F 12 Tues.<br/>Leave Boulogne at 12.30pm &amp;am and march to Wiemere [Wimereux] about 5 Kilos. Train at 2.45. We are comfy &amp; warm &amp; it is a lovely day. Pass Etappes [Etaples] Abbeville Rouen [indecipherable]. See Ruins  &amp; [indecipherable] etc.<br/>Wrote M.R.L. a long farewell.</p>
<p>13 Wed<br/>We have a feed of apples from Truck van. at Etoulles &amp; base at 10.15.<br/>Meet Roy. Get issued with gun. March out again at 5 pm &amp; spend 1&frac12; hrs in house. [indecipherable] in [indecipherable]  No 39.<br/>Place lit up &amp; we have warm welcome from [Ranec?]<br/>14 Th<br/>F Ice. We have a Turn all around the Hospital.  We have a riot at night &amp; bottles &amp; other things fly pretty strongly. I sit in mess with all others &amp; write. No leave for anyone.<br/>Wrote M &amp; Mum.</p>
<p>F 15 Fri<br/>Ice. Everyone stands to Guards C.G. &amp; G.G. but come up to take away rioters. No leave again. We are both sick &amp; awfully fed up. Just stand by all day as usual. Joe comes up again.<br/>Wrote Con &amp; Mr Edmonds &amp;  Doris (Good Bye)</p>
<p>F 16 Sat<br/>Ice M, D, E&amp;I  I go down Town together &amp; return alone.<br/>Meet two Waacs no [indecipherable]. [indecipherable] if Auntie saying how like &amp; too near KIM. M &amp; one other on long blue ribbon is I go for a swim</p>
