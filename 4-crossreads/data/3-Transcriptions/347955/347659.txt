<p>Saturday Jan 26.1.18<br/>turned out 6.15 Breakfast groomed camels 8.30 went to dentist 10AM fell in 11.30 was inspected by colonel dinner 12.  led 8 camels to MVS 1.30 to be clipped tea 5.  turned in<br/>Sunday Jan 27.1.18<br/>turned out 6.30 fed breakfast 7.30 fell in 9.30 for church Parade went to mass 10 A M dinner 12.  rested till 3.P.M. wrote to Bell very windy day tea 5.P.M. fixed my tent up raining turned in 8.P.M. it is very black.<br/>Monday Jan.28.1.18.<br/>turned out 6.15 fed up Phisical Jerks Breakfast fell in 9.30 rifle inspection rained fell in again 10 for bayonet charging charging at Bags of grass  dinner 12 raining a treat  led four camels to be [indecipherable] dressed still raining tea 5.30 turned in Blankets</p>
