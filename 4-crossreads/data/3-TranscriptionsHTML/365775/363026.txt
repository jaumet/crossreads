<p>expect to leave in the morning. 7.30 anchor hauled. we sail out from Alexandria for France 18th June 1916. -</p>
<p>Just before going further I will endeavour to give you a brief outline of our journey across Egypt. packed in trucks like so many sheep, the shades of night are falling as the last of us are hustled aboard, &amp; consequently getting cooler, tis the one thing that makes life worth living in Egypt, is the cool nights, one is always able to get a good nights rest. Well, the whistle blows &amp; away we start on our nights journey across Egypt, tis a beautiful moonlight night &amp; for the greater part</p>
