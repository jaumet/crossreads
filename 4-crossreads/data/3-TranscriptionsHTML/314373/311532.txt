<p>near Albert.<br/>The next day Keating Thomas Stroud and myself started off at 7.30 a.m. for the new position we were taking over from an English battery. We arrived  here about 11a.m. and went from then till the following tea time with only one meal and no blankets, not forgetting the wet feet, which I managed in my case to keep warm by putting them both inside about four sandbags.</p>
