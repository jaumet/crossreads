 Sunday 18/2/17 dense fog all day yesterday could not see 50 yds away, ended in rain last night, found our dug-out flooded &amp; had to pack up &amp; shift at 11 PM. no sleep all night. Blankets &amp; Waterproof sheet wet heavy firing all night. fog again this morning same as yesterday snow all gone &amp; nearly all ice but mud, a regular sea of it. Pay 2.14.5 &pound;21.2.0 
 56th came out tonight dead beat had a good raid 2 killed. took Prisoners &pound; killed a lot of huns 
 Monday 19/2/17 Rained a lot during the night foggy again this morning. The thaw [indecipherable] in Properly nothing but mud now till spring Fritz seems very jumpy so the boys say, food very poor all through the Army Aus &amp; English. Ought to get out of this front in 11 days sent Nell's &amp; Maudes letters yesterday. 
 Tuesday 20/2/17 Raining &amp; miserable water dripping in dug-out, also cold wind blowing, same old routine nothing much in Artillery line last night too foggy that dense you could cut it with a knife. 
 Wednesday 21/2/17 Still fog &amp; slight Rain, not much artillery fighting, can't see 50 yds away. 54th going in tonight. Pay. 2.17.5. &pound;21.5.0 
 Thursday 22/1/17 Gas alert again last night, same every night. 55th went in last night to supports. fog &amp; Rain today. Artillery busy. Pay 2.18.5 &pound;21.6.0 
 Friday 23/2/17 same as yesterday fog. very little gun firing Pay 2.19.5 &pound;21.7.0 Cold &amp; miserable.
