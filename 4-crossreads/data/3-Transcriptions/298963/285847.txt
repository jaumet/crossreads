The barage was awful, but soon all fell asleep after one of the most eventful days in our lives and one that we shall always remember with horror &amp; sorrow as long as any of us live.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Not called out yet, feeling much refreshed after a sleep &amp; a meal.  The bombardment continues but on a small scale.  At 5.30 p.m. went back to sunken road &amp; learned that we were held in reserve for the "hop over".  10.30 load of [indecipherable] blown up.  Went to rescue the wounded in the face of a most awful barage, met the Gordon Highlanders going in</p>
