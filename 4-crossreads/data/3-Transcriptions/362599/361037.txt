<p>F [23/12/17 Sun] Lovely Day. E Tops &amp; I go to D'ton. I serve Tea for them. We all get Home tired, I play the Piano a little. Letter from Jim. Roddie Wrote W.H.A. George, Aunt Annie. Roy Wrote George Millar. Nurse Hilda. Jim. Reg Duke Wellington.</p>
<p>F [24/12/17 Mon] Go with D &amp; E to Albany Ice. Wards. Not bad show. Am Canteen Corporal &amp; have a hell of a Time &amp; get in a very bad Temper. Have good supper &amp; go to bed.</p>
<p>F Ice [25/12/17 Tues] Lovely Day. Good Dinner. Go to D'ton with Med for May. Round through Milstone with Clancy. P.Cs to Dot. Dulce. Mam. Amy. Edie. Ida. &amp; Wal. Jess Walker. Jess Matthew. Maynie Frances. Chris. Nette, Mrs Mel OSG Otto Bess Alice, M.R.L. Mum.</p>
<p>Ice [26/12/17 Wed] E &amp; I go to S'bury. Go to Victoria Park. Tea at Mrs E. E &amp; Dolly come after Tea. We have a good Train Trip Home.<br/>Recd. Cards from Laurie, A's, Mrs E</p>
<p>Ice [27/12/17 Thurs] Am Ord Corp. Go on [Indecipherable] parades for Defaulters. O.S. Bertie goes on his own. Do Canteen Corporal &amp; Night Corp. to m'night. Practice Hockey. Have a very busy night &amp; miss Concert.</p>
<p>F [28/12/17 Fri] Recd. Letter M.R.L. E goes to bed ill. I practice Hockey. Cleaned all my Boots etc. Go to bed fairly early</p>
<p>F [29/12/17 Sat] H of a bustle to get away. Ride Bike Hang on Back of Amesbury Bus. Go to [Indecipherable] &amp; then to Dance. Have lovely Time. Slow flow, fast music. I know all the girls out. It rains at 11pm. I stay at No 13. Lovely soft bed. Rise at 6pm next Day. Yanks make first appearance in Sarum Public life. Win is Home &amp; I have a realy good Time till 12pm.</p>
