<p>Tuesday 20th November 1917</p>
<p>Moved on to DEAL &amp; anchored there.</p>
<p>Passed a big lineR with stern blown in &amp; being driven by TUGS.</p>
<p>We have at our table a Capt. HURLEY who has been twice to the Pole, &amp; has been on the French salient photographing &amp; is now en route to Palestine.</p>
<p>Cold &amp; foggy.</p>
<p>The tucker is good &amp; no restrictions like in England. All the Stewards are Indians &amp; we have passengers for ports as far as Japan.</p>
