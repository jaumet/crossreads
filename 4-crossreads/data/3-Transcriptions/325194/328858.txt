<p>I went gay in Cairo a few days back and stoushed a few of them up and got 10 days F.P. for it, never mind Ill take it out of them if we ever get busy in real ernest gee I'd enjoy insinuating my bayonet into the vitals of the brutes and then giving it nice deft twist before pulling it out and handing another chap his dose.</p>
<p>23.2.16<br/>Oh this army is a beaut we have to mess about all day and every day</p>
