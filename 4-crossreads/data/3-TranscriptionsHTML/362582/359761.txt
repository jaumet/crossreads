<p>The Palace,<br/>Heliopolis.<br/>Sept 6th 1915.</p>
<p>My dearest Jane, Received your delightful letter after Belle's, so it is your turn for a letter. I think you are very good at writing so often &amp; hope you will continue doing so. I spent my birthday in bed but felt splendid, was up yesterday and was alright, suppose I shall soon be off to Alexandria now. I'm so glad to think that I shall soon be quite well again. So sorry that you have all had Influenza trust you are all well again. It is a horrid complaint but seems the usual thing at Ilkley about July &amp; August. I had such a number of letters last week, had two Australian mails in. Funny I received No. 17 before No. 16. Thank you all very much for birthday wishes also the presents, which I'm sure you cannot afford this year.
