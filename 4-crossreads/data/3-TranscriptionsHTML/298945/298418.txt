<p>Sunday 1st Sept. 1918</p>
<p>Yesterday we were turned out of our tent, in which we had dug down a foot or so for protection against bomb splinters, &amp; so, in preference to going into a marquee most of us decided to build bivvies. Joe Ritchie &amp; "Kak" Smith had one already.</p>
<p>I asked Freddy Cocks <strike>at which</strike> if he would join in with me, but, as usual lately, he hesitated agreat deal, so George Goode joined forces with me. Fred Grey gave us some very valuable help &amp; we made a very good one with sand bags &amp; curved iron, salvaged from an old artillery position on the opposite slope.</p>
