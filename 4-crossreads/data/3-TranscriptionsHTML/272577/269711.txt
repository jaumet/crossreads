<p>6th October 1916<br/>Early morning stables.  Signalling &amp; Buzzer.  Battery went out on all day manoeuvres.  Signallers race today.  H.Qrs. of Brigade disqualified also 25th &amp; 26th.  107th went to pieces.  Result win for our Battery.  I was on disc station.  Our Battery won all events today.  Had sundry wads at Canteen tonight.</p>
<p>7th October 1916<br/>Early morning stables.  Got ready for our Sports.  They were on all day till 5.30 and turned out a success.  Went to pictures at night &amp; saw C/ Chaplin in The Tramp.</p>
<p>8th October 1916<br/>Early morning stables.  Went out to exercise horses in morning.  Answered sundry letters in afternoon.  Canteen &amp; shower at night.</p>
<p>9th October 1916<br/>Early morning stables.  Signalling &amp; station work.  Did not go out.  Perce came up to canteen.  Very surprised that he has returned from London.</p>
<p>10th October 1916<br/>Early morning stables.  Signalling &amp; station work.  Did not go out.  Wrote letter for Aust. mail.</p>
<p>11th October 1916<br/>Early morning stables.  Signalling &amp; Lectures.  Received letters from Australia.  Did not go out.</p>
<p>12th October 1916<br/>Got ready early for mounted manoeuvres of Horses.  Staff left at 9 for the trenches.  Out all day &amp; returned to Camp at 6.30 very tired.  Got ready for moving off again tomorrow morning early.</p>
