 After that a desultory scrap started but neither hurt each other much, and as for Molden he hadn't the faintest Idea, I am sure, about what he was fighting. Thank goodness at last we have something here in the Aeroplane line which will beat these enemy planes. This afternoon our latest type of monoplane went up and engaged a taube, flew rings round him &amp; sent him back with a bee in his ear, though not succeeding in bringing him down.
