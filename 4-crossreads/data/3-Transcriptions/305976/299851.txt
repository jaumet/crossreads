<p>Monday April 16th<br/>More Drill putting in time<br/>Tuesday April 17th<br/>Riding exercise,  Lecture.<br/>Concert in lines in evening<br/>Wednesday April 18th<br/>Guard duty to day our S.M is considered a Military crank. Very amusing. Going through procedure changing guard. Everybody fed up. S.M at his word. Jim B supposed to be on duty at Guard room. Your humble expected to march up with guard, halt &amp; proceed with Military manoeuvres. Jim B. supposed to challenge &amp; call out guard. This is what happened. Marched up to Guard room. Jim B. instead of challenging said in a dramatic voice (Who are these people) Little Ernie a Corporal appeared from nowhere &amp; said I'm <strike>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</strike> if I know</p>
