  
 Saturday 24 July, 1915 
 came back to life at day break & had a look round, we have big tents to live in & a wooden mess shed to have our meals in. Should have a good time if it were not for the sand & heat. 
 Had a look round Heliopolis. Just getting to know our way round. 
 Had a trip into Cairo arrived home 10.15 p.m.
