<p>As our Colonel says &ndash; "they live in damned fowl houses") However the camels became excited and got mixed up a bit, and Bunny Brett fell off. The camels bucked about a bit  and we only lost one &ndash; the one with rations &ndash; v.e. rations<br/>(V.E. is pronounced VICK &ndash;E and in sig's language means "The end") Bunny lay on the ground quite hysterical laughing &amp; crying alternately so we got  him back after a lot of persuasion &amp; set off again. At about 8pm we reached Hill 40 and found the Sig Squadron left as it stood. We then set off along the main road to Hill 70 some miles farther on. Bunny suddenly became too sick when half way. so George had to half carry him back to Hill 40 where he was dumped in a shed and left &ndash;</p>
