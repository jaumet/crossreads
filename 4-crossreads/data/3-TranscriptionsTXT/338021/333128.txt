 To OC 13th M.G. Coy Situation report 
 New gun received &amp; mounted last night.  a.a.a. Am returning Pte Neal as his leg is too stiff to be of any use in action. O.C. took away spare parts last night. My co-ordination was wrong apologise a.a.a. 
 Thanks for letter &amp; cigarettes. 
 Casualties Nil S.A.A. expended Nil Heavy firing occasionally during night by enemy artillery. No damage 
 CHP Weston Lieut 8.15am 25/9/17
