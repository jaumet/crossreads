
Monday Sept 30We are expecting to move in a few days to a better camping ground. It is impossible to stay here for long or all hands will be in Hospital. The weather at present is beautiful & large numbers of Turkish prisoners are coming in daily.Teusday Oct 1We rode down to the Water troughs about one mile from station with a number of prisoners to bury the dead Horses.
