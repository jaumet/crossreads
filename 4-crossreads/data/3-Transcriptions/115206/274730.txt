<p>June 1918<br/>Sunday 9</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather dull, and fairly close. Attended Church Parade<br/>in the morning, had a nice swim after lunch.<br/>Sports very successful l"F" Sub second 15 pts. "C" first<br/>20 pts.<br/>Bob and Mick Fargo rode very well in Y.C. and Bending <br/>races.<br/>"F" Sub won wrestling on horseback.<br/>Walked over to Bussey with "Pip" C, and "Don Ack". Had<br/>some eufs. Met John on way home testing? Mills Bombs.</p>
