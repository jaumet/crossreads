<p>At night Bastian &amp; I &amp; friend went up to see Ossy Wood but he was at an Officers School Tidsworth.  Sent note to him.<br/>Letter from Ernie.</p>
<p>Wednesday 20th<br/>Route march to Figheldean, Upperavon &amp; Milston.  Saw a Church at Milston which dates back to 1299.  Saw some good flying.<br/>Afternoon, Pay day.</p>
<p>Thursday 21st<br/>Morning.  To Dentists and had tooth filled.<br/>Afternoon.  Stuffing palaisses in preparation for cold weather.<br/>Slept very comfortable on them at night.</p>
<p>Friday 22<br/>Our Section on fatigue duty all day.</p>
<p>Saturday 23rd<br/>Walk to Rollestone to No. 5 Training Battalion and met Abel Sheath.  He was going on leave however and I promised to visit him one night.  Walk to Flying Depot thence to German prisoners Camp &amp; back to tea.<br/>After tea to Amesbury &amp; back.</p>
<p>Sunday 24th<br/>Church Parade, morning.<br/>Afternoon.  W. &amp; Eric Cunningham &amp; self to Stonehenge thence to Amesbury to tea &amp; Church.  Heard Brilliant sermon on "Repentance".</p>
<p>Monday 25th<br/>Practising near the trenches for next Wednesday's review by His Majesty the King.<br/>Afternoon.  Route march.</p>
<p>Tuesday 26th<br/>Full dress rehearsal of tomorrow Review.<br/>An enormous number of troops took part including Third Aust. Division and all the Training Battalions.</p>
