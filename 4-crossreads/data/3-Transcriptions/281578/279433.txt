<p>in the house, &amp; a heap of manure lies in front of the house. I had a yarn to the French woman &amp; the kids. Some of the episodes were funny. One chap said to the woman whose barn we occupy &quot;Bon soir, Madame&quot;, then &quot;you needn&#39;t say anything that&#39;s all I know.&quot; I must mention one other incident. A woman was chasing a fowl that got away, &amp; an Australian joined in &amp; caught it. The woman was jabbering in French, &amp; throwing her arms about, &amp; the Australian thought she wanted it killed, so he wrung its neck. She did go crook then, it turned out the hen was clucky &amp; she wanted to catch it &amp; put it on some eggs. Turned in at dark, as there was nothing to do, &amp; slept in on the straw till 7a.m.<br /><u>Saturday 6 April</u> Fell in in the street in morning, while a sergeant lectured on the gas mask, &amp; we looked to the different points he mentioned to see the respirator was O.K.
