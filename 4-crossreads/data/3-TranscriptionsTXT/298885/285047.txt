 Thur. Feb. 10th -16 Made permanent brakesman to C.I. team. 
 Fri. Feb. 11th -16 Accompanied C.I. team to Abbehsiah, Waila, and Slave markets for break. 
 Sat. Feb. 12th -16 First lesson at riding school.  "Some Circus". 
 Sun. Feb. 13th -16 Church Service 7.45 A.M.  Received first mail since leaving Fremantle and feel quite excited. 
 Mon. Feb. 14th -16 Inspection of D.A.C. by Colonel Hobbs.  Weather very cold and windy with clouds of dust. 
 Tues. Feb. 15th -16 Received first instruction
