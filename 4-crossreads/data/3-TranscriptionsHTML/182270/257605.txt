<p>MONDAY, OCTOBER 20, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; very hot &amp; windy. Darby got</p>
<p>a real good supply at Middle paddock</p>
<p>at least 12000 gal per day at 186 ft.</p>
<p>I brought Cy back at lunch time</p>
<p>and took the block &amp; tackle ect to</p>
<p>Mountain bore. Tickle &amp; I got all</p>
<p>the pipes ect up &amp; the sails of the</p>
<p>mill down.</p>
