7/9/17 On skirmishing stunt all day having lunch out on field. After tea went out for blackberries.
8/9/17 Battalion sports all day. Left just after dinner for billets washed hdcfs then bathed in stream alongside billet. Heard going to line on Tuesday. Few more casuals arrived from Havre. Heavy fog til 9am. Went blackberrying after tea.
9/9/17 Sunday ? beautiful sunshine, not a cloud to be seen. On  rations today. Numbers of aeroplanes about. 2pm walked into Lumbres with Ashwood. Bought maps of France in there, then had steak eggs for tea ? cost 3 francs. Left 6pm arrived back 7.30pm. Sent ppcards to Australia England in green envelope also with 2 whiz-bangs.
10/9/17 Lewis Gun all day.
