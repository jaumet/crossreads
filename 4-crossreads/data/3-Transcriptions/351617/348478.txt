<p>to take us back to Division which had now shifted from Favril to Solre-Le-Chateau about 12 Kilos from the Belgian Border. Here we went into billets in an old german hospital with beds and matresses a nice big stove in the centre of the room &amp; each big room holding 12 men</p>
<p>Here we were told that there was nothing to do but keep our billets clean and take exercise which we did every day to the tune of about a 12 Kilos walk. Most of our time we put in making souvenirs and I have two shell cases of Fritzies made into Vases at home today.</p>
