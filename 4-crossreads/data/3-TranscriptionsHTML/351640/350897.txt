<p>Prince Alexander also came to assist but one never mistakes him nowadays.</p>
<p>The King is always shabbily dressed but what matter about clothes when ones country men are watering the earth with their life's blood.</p>
<p>The other evening about six a great Zeppelin sailed over here.   On its way to England, The war ships fired at it but it sailed majestically on.   It knew of course that the guns could not reach it nor [indeciperable] about.</p>
