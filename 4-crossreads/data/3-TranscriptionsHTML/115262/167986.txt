<p>Tuesday 7 August 1917.</p>
<p>Instruction in full marching order for the field where it appears we will move out to shortly.</p>
<p>To Cairo in evening to have envelopes containing silk&nbsp;handkerchiefs censored at Anzac Hostel. Had to leave them there till tomorrow.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Supper at Y.M.C.A. &amp; back to camp.</p>
<p>During afternoon we were issued with the rest of our equipment for the field &amp; had to fill water tanks &amp; dhurra bags for tomorrow.</p>
