
Sunday 21 January 1917Church parade in morning, outpost at night.  Scotties took over the redouts in front of us.
Monday 22 January 1917Cruel day, rained & blew periodically, rained at night.
Tuesday 23 January 1917Making targets all day for M.G. practice.
Wednesday 24 January 1917General fatigue.
Thursday 25 January 1917Nothing in particular doing.
Friday 26 January 1917Clarrie went to M.G. School Zeitoun, took horses to 152 & led them back.
Saturday 27 January 1917Took horses for exercise.  Mail in.
