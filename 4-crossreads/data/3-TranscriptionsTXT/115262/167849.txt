 Friday 23 March 1917. 
 Survey work &amp; general drill. 
 Tents rearranged on acct of sickness breaking out in the camp. 4  men &nbsp;to be in 1 tent. 
 Moved kit and settled again. 
 No lecture at night. 
 On acct of a clerical error I was granted leave from tonight until Monday night. Declined to go until morning train.
