<p>10 March 1915<br/>  <strike>Wednesday</strike> it. A boat load of men more or less drunk would come aboard about every 10 minutes.</p>
<p><u>Wednesday</u></p>
<p>Take aboard a quantity of provisions   &amp;  also change the crew taking a black crowd instead of the Chinamen.<br/>Troopship "Nile" w/- a regiment of Punjabis ties up near us amid much cheering   &amp;  playing of bagpipes which the native troops play very well Clear out of port at dusk.</p>
<p>1915 March 11<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Take up the usual routine again.<br/>Time is beginning to hang heavy the novelty has worn off   &amp;  we want to be at it again.</p>
