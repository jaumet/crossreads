
1919July 27passed by Groote Shuur, once Rhodes residence, at this time General Botha's residence, who had arrived home a few days previously.  On this account we were unable to inspect it.  We caught the train at Rondebosch & returned to Capetown.  I went with Jim Copland at night to a concert in the Grand theatre (Buckshee).
