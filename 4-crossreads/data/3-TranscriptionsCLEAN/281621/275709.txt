
Sunday 29Church parade. Wet weather
Monday 30Wet day & cold.
Tuesday 31Tonight I spent very pleasantly, in a old Pozieres gathering Docker & Barnby & also James & Robby were there & we had a good talk over old times.I don't know, but in spite of paper talk etc I think peace movements are getting a hold.
August, 1917Wednesday 1If this country & the people are realizing the utter absurdity of driving Germany out of Belgium & France. Soon too, think we will compromise with Germany perhaps this year, as an outcome of this peace feeling.Wattle day at homeThird battle of Ypres began yesterday & has been made out to be a huge success. What are our casualties in these stunts?
Thursday 2Change in the weather which up to yesterday was beautiful is now wet cold & windy.Three very interesting lectures this morning on "Economy in War Time" principally. Also on the eternal topics.
Friday 3To go by coach with Dock & Barnby to Salisbury tomorrow.
Saturday 4Salisbury trip cried off on account of bad weather & Barnby not being able to come.
