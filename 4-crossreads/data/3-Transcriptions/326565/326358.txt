<p>Friday 5/4/16<br/>Received word about 10 AM to strike camp &amp; move to railhead immediately.  After assisting to strike the camp, left with the advance guard shortly after lunch; march very unpleasant owing to the heat of the day.  Arrived at the site of our new camp about 2.45 pm.
