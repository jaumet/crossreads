<p>open truck.  Struck coast at daylight, country along route fine.  Loaded gear on to  lorry about 12.30 pm &amp; travelled with it to our new camping area at Racquinghem [about 25kms SE of Calais], showed a profit on trip as I escaped route marches each end.  Received mail, first, for seven weeks.  Plenty fatigues erecting camp.</p>
<p>Wednesday 1.8.17<br/>Orderly Corp.  Trying day with fatigues etc. getting camp into running order.  Rained the whole of the day, got drenched early in the morning &amp; was wet the whole of the day.</p>
<p>Thursday 2.8.17<br/>Four went on leave to "Blighty"  Leave granted to St Omer &amp; Hazebrook  Orderly Corp &amp; Piquet Corp as well  Camp in a very bad state owing to the rain.</p>
