<p>again at 12.30 &amp; again at 6.30pm.  We caught a fairly good shelling at tea time, but there were no casualties.  The parapets of the trench were broken down.  Again in the morning we got another dose of shrapnel &amp; poor Freddy Isted got a splinter across his eye &amp; head.  He was pretty bad but the doctor thinks he will survive.  We heard later he had lost an eye but was progressing favourably.</p>
<p>Thursday 27th<br/>Went on fatigue in the afternoon &amp; deepened our trenches a bit.  We had all night in the rest trenches &amp; didn't have to turn out although there was heavy firing just after dark.  We don't take much notice of it as the Turks do that every night.</p>
