<p>Anyway there is some very heavy stuff being bandied about here now. We are up here in reserve acting I believe as divisional troops for infantry.</p>
<p>I am a pitifully sordid object just now, bandages are very scarce now &amp; so we have to keep our ad infinitum.</p>
<p>I spent most of the halts on the trek up here tenderly undoing one sceptic sore after another &amp; spitting on it to keep the swelling &amp; pain out of it. Imagine me, all one</p>
