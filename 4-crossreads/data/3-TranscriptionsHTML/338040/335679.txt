<p>June 1st<br/>Discharged from hospital. Inspection by Messrs. Am. Hughes and A Fisher.<br/>June 2nd<br/>Digging Trenches in our own grounds (in case of German Bombardment), good work. Parcel (handkerchief (11) silk apron, disc etc.) sent home, writing letters.<br/>June 3rd<br/>Digging trenches, Stretcher case from Erquingham (use wheels) weather wet.</p>
