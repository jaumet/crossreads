<p>Aug 1917</p>
<p>smoke screene if attacked</p>
<p><strike>Friday</strike> Saturday 11th<br/>Still admireing the well washed green hills of Serra Leone. Coaling has finished &amp; the duty Niggers have left us Australians make the great mistake of being to friendly with them</p>
<p>Sunday 12<br/>About 10 AM the "HMS Affric" went out with 4 Boats going Cape town way &amp; We followed imediately after. There are 9 Boats includeing 1 Battleship &amp; 1 Aux Cruiser in our lot now. I wonder if they will all get through No one was <strike>glad</strike> sorry to leave Serra Leone. As we were not allowed any leave</p>
