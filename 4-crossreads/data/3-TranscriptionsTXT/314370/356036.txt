 June, 1916 Friday 2 Final run over stripping &amp; description &amp; getting exam papers ready. Go for a walk into Zeitoun after tea.  The cool breeze is very refreshing after the hot sweaty day. 
 Saturday 3 Description Exam takes up all morning, my little class do very well. After dinner I went out to Patti's spending a very pleasant afternoon &amp; evening, returning at 12 pm.
