 Saturday Oct 20th 
 Westham : parade &amp; V.D. inspection in morn : went on leave 2 pm &amp; met Ern : walked to the "White horse" &amp; then to his Aunt's for tea : Ern tells me that I have been recommended but I suppose that will be my share of it 
 Sunday Oct 21st 
 Westham : fair day : church parade in morn &amp; nice too : leave 2 pm : stroll along promenade in afternoon : nice service in Parish Church after tea : rained as I came home
