Sunday Dec 9 Camped in open. Rain &amp; mud , everything in a hell of a mess. Monday Dec 10 Beautiful fine day so we proceeded to make camp. We secured a lovely position on a slope among some good old saplings. The village lies some 560 yds at the back of us and it looks very pretty sheltered among the trees.
