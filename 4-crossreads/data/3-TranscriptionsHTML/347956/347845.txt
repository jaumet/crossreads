<p>4-1-15</p>
<p>we went to the Recreation tent &amp; wrote to Tyson &amp; then had hymns till 9 oclock, when I had to leave to feed the horses &amp; answer the roll call before turning in for the night</p>
<p>Mon 4th<br/>Signalling with "helio" all day &amp; was out from 6 till 7 PM with the lamps &amp; then went to a concert in the Recreation tent, but as it was rather tame, Stan &amp; I came back &amp; played draughts till bed time.</p>
