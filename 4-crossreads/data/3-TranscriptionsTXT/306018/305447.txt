 Thursday 19th Took over SQM Sergt from Halloran who is going Sergeant Major.  The job is fairly easy &amp; I for one am not going to make it hard. 
 Friday 20th This Quarter Master's job is too easy.  There is absolutely nothing to do for most of the day &amp; one doesn't know what to do with oneself.  Besides the weather is too  hot to sleep or read.  In the cool of the evening went for a walk along the canal &amp; on our return went to the pictures. 
 Saturday 21st Got some more men into camp, some from hospital &amp; others from the School of Instruction.  It will make the duties much lighter.  Today was very hot fully 108&deg; in the shade.  Applications were called for the Flying Corps.  I put in one, but don't expect anything will come of it.
