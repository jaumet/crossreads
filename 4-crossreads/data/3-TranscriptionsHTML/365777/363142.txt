<p>"Where's your bivey". following the course of the river we could hear them overhead when suddenly, could be heard the swish of the bombs falling &amp; then crash &ndash; crash - crash, bombs dropping all round us the place lit up as bright as day. I jumped out of bed, heavens, that last lot must have been right on the horse lines. A lot of the lads were camped just behind the horse lines too, I pulled on my boots a hurried outside &amp; run into Sergeant Major</p>
