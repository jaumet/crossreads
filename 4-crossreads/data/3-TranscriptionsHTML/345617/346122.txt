<p>Juillet 19<br/>15th Brigade marched into Corbie today &amp; will camp for the night as did the 8th &amp; 14th. Nothing of importance to report. Company carrying on with Section drill.</p>
<p>Juillet 20<br/>Company just carrying on with Section drill &amp; training in general. Nothing of importance to note.</p>
