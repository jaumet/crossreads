<p>Wednesday 14.3.17<br/>Kit inspection.  Went to pierrot show at night.</p>
<p>Thursday 15.3.17<br/>More bearers to stand by in readiness to go to line; expect to go at any time now; don't expect to get the leave granted me under the circumstances</p>
<p>Friday 16.3.17<br/>Closed camp, owing to having to stand by.  My leave cancelled</p>
<p>Saturday 17.3.17<br/>Heavy bombardment during night.  20 men left for the line at 7.15 Am <br/>Ordered to move of at 7 Am in the morning.  Bapaume captured early this morning.</p>
<p>Sunday 18.3.17<br/>Further details re taking of Bapaume</p>
