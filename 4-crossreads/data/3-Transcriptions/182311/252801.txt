
<p>2&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 2</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; We (5 Officers of A Coy) were very glad to see his pictures &amp; his grounds too were glorious for birch &amp; elm, maple &amp; beech were either burned or bleached with the fire of autumn.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; After this visit we went on to Salisbury had a quiet civilized dinner &amp; then <s>went </s>after making a few purchases back to our Hut on Lark Hill.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; From Friday the 10<sup><u>th</u></sup> to Monday the 14<sup>th</sup> November has been leave; &quot;Indulgence Leave &#39;twas called &amp; &#39;tis our final leave for embarkation approaches very closely.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; &quot;Up to London to look at a King&quot;, &amp; for other purposes too I went, other sights, purchases of Kit to make, friends to be met, &amp; shows to be seen.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; &quot;The City of the World&quot;, London, so huge, so wonderful &amp; how important the great clearing house of the world&#39;s commerce, the heart of thr Empire &amp; since &quot;mud built London by the Thames&quot; it has been the cradle of our race.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; It really does <s>thrill</s> give one a thrill</p>
