<p>This is the first big scrap I have had since I got my Com. One has too much to watch &amp; think of now to get the tit bits as when a ranker.<br/>The second day was interesting, but mostly a case of endurance for horses &amp; men. I will ma[il] a separate letter of it.<br/>I am writing to Father to ask him to send a cheque for &pound;7.10 to Mrs Kate F Stribley 99 Sheppard St Sydney. He had given this amount to mind in Egyptian notes. The O/C told me to arrange things by keeping the money &amp; paying him if possible from the other end. Poor chap was a married man &amp; gamer than</p>
<p>[Private William Henry Stribley, No 1666, 1st Light Horse Regiment, killed in action 4 August 1916.]<br/>[This letter concludes at the top of page 7.]</p>
