<p>at Vivier Mill.  This mill has been worked all through the war and is owned by the Mayor of the villiage</p>
<p>We notice neither this mill or the villiage have been troubled with shells  For divers reasons we are all inclined to take the mayor and his crowd for pro germans.</p>
<p>We are only here for 3 days and at last to our relief are proceeding up north which will be almost as good as a spell in seeing different country</p>
<p>July 11th &ndash; 17<br/>Left Vivier Mill and started on our run to Belgium which is to take</p>
