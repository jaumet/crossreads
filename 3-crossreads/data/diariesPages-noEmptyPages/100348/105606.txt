Some one ought to be shot over this; for we cannot stop them. We are in a very exposed position here, if we are shelled no one will survive. Percy Wright was killed last night.
19/8/16.
Had another thrill last night went and lined the front parapet in preparation for an attack on Moquet Farm. The stunt was cancelled at the last moment.
