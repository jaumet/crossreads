One of the first things to strike in Cairo is the smartness of the native police.<br/>Also the funerals.<br/>Our men buried with Chaplain &amp; firing party in the English Cemetaries in Cairo or Alex.<br/><strike>Several sails on the Nile by moonlight.</strike><br/>Carry on!</p>
