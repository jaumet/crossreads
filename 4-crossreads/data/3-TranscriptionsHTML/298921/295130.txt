<p>June, 1915<br/>Tuesday 8<br/>Mrs Ferror sent me another Cake &amp; a tin of Chocolates very good of her &ndash; I am very fit &amp; getting plenty of office work at present which is a change after Field days on the Desert, I am doing Brigade Major or Camp Adjutants work now, which may mean promotion later on.</p>
