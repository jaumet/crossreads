 April. 27th.  March out this evening to report to London.  Sleep at War Chest Club.  Wonderful weather &amp; as late as to-day, London had six inches of snow. 28th.  Join "China" at Albert Docks, &amp; this afternoon went to London.  We will be very comfortable on this boat &amp; are due to arrive in Sydney on 12th June. 29th.  Leave docks &amp; wend our way down the Thames.  We are now in Channel opp Dover, en route for Plymouth.
