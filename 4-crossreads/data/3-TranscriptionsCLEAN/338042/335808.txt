  
 Visit War Chest then on to Highgate Hill (Mr. & Mrs Scales) a charming,  sincere & kind hearted Lady. Mr. Scales a benevolent, pious &  sterling Gentleman - "Home Sweet Home" - Meet Arthur Brooker (48th Batt. A.I.F.) who also is a guest of Mrs. Scales. An Interesting afternoon walk over Highgate Hill etc During which we enter House of Nell Gwynne - I see the Home of Richard Cromwell and the House in which Bacon died after performing experiment with the "snow" etc. (Home of Grand Duke Michael of Russia)
