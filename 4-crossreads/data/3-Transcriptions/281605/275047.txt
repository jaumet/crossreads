<p>post &amp; then went back &amp; saw my officer &amp; then left for the Aid post where I remained for 2 day. On the afternoon of the second day I was sent to the advanced loading field ambulance station &amp; was put on a motor &amp; taken to the field ambulance at Boerre &amp; had all particulars etc recorded. Our stay there was very short for we were again put on a motor and taken to the 3rd Field ambulance at [indecipherable] and it was here I was put into pyjamas &amp; made a stretcher case &amp; was again put in another motor about 12 midnight &amp; arrived at No. 2 Aust. C.C.S. Arques at 1.30am on the 23.6.18. The stay there was 36 hours when I was again put in a motor &amp; taken to St Omer station &amp; put aboard an ambulance train &amp; went to Boulougne. On arriving at Boulougne motor again met us &amp; I was taken to no. 53</p>
