<p>TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 4, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, &amp; foggy. Went up to the Bank</p>
<p>in the morning and to a</p>
<p>matinee of The Maid of the Mountains</p>
<p>in the afternoon. Went to bed</p>
<p>early. Alan came down &amp;</p>
<p>is staying with me. He has</p>
<p>got his gdischarge all right. I</p>
<p>gave him my two tickets</p>
<p>for &quot;Oh Joy&quot; &amp; he took Marj.</p>
<p>Lang &amp; enjoyed it very</p>
<p>much.</p>
