<p>Saturday 29th September 1917<br/>Painting boxes for the Captain.<br/>On sick lines on Piquet. First shift 6 till 10pm.<br/>Fritz droped great number of bombs between 7 and 9pm the nearest about 200 yrds.</p>
<p>Sunday 30th<br/> Painting a cross for no.549 R. Freyer T.M.B who was killed on the 22-9-17<br/>Half holiday</p>
