<p>1917 Thursday 19 July</p>
<p>Medhurst &amp; Hall marked for Blighty too. Good to see all these bad cases so much improved. <strike>but</strike> <br/>Sister White leaving ward for night duty. Sister Simpson succeeds. <br/>Army Pay Corp. "Frivolities" give a first class concert in Y.M.C.A.<br/>Afternoon "off".  Jock H &amp; to W--- fool round back to Canteen &amp; concert. <br/>No more convoys to come in for 10 days.  Clearing hosp. in anticipation of coming big stunt.</p>
