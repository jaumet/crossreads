
1915Sep. 7 Tue.For the past week have not been feeling too good. Paraded sick about 6 p..m. & admitted to Ward 6 with tonsilitis.My case sheet in the ward says for the last 4 or 5 days has had a sore throat - to-day feels feverish - no headache - felt inclined to be sick from the stomach - no general pains - constipated.On admission:- T 99.6, P120, R24.
