
Saturday 1st September 1917Went out in W. Flinns team as lead driver carting 4.5s from Spoil Bank to Dump near camp. One load in the morning and one in the afternoon.
Sunday 2ndFed mules in the morning 2nd parade 10 am. Church service 10.30am. 3rd parade 4pm.10pm Tubes dropping bombs. They kept coming over till about 3.30am.
