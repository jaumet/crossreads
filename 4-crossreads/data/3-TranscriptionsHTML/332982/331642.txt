<p>After a very pleasant voyage, we sighted land at 10.15am on the 9th of Nov.<br/>Always to the front the Y.M.C.A. welcomed us   at Port O'Call No1 (Durban) even before the ship was made fast.<br/>From far out on the Jetty a young lady Semaphored the greetings of the ubiquitous and ever helpful institution. Come to the Y.M.C.A. Hut close to the Town Hall.<br/>Arrived in Wharf at 12 noon but were not allowed ashore till a guard of 50 Artillery men</p>
