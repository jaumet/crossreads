<p>78  Overseas Training Bgde<br/>A.I.F. Warminster<br/>14/1/1918</p>
<p>My Dear Father &amp; Mother<br/>Am in camp again &amp; although not in harness, it comes as a rude shock after the lazy time I've been having these last few months &ndash; Read &amp; I arrived here on Thursday night &amp; are now under orders to proceed over to France at short notice &ndash; I will be glad to get back among the old crowd although the winter over the water does</p>
