 A long &amp; miserable march under wretched conditions &ndash; We pass through Meaulte, Becordel. Fricourt. Mametz, and pass the night in the last named. 
 Dec. 23rd. Reveille 3.45a.m &ndash; We march off in the dark through rain, mud, slush on a road half under water &amp; stray mettle &ndash; After a solid march we arrive at a miserable spot in Bannefay Wood &ndash; Take up duties in A.Div. Much improved service directly behind the Line light railways. 
 Dec. 24th. Xmas Eve &ndash; A fine day but cold &ndash; distribution of Gifts - Taubes once more &ndash; Application for new position &ndash; News of Eric Gordon. A midnight meal.
