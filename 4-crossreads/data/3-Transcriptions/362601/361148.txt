<p>23 January, Thursday</p>
<p>F<br/>Long busy worrying day. We straighten our room up &amp; then the lights go out. We go to Tops room for an hour then I find Hurricane lamp &amp; we get ready for bed after a lovely Hot bath. Our troubles never cease but we are set at last in a new room.</p>
<p>24 January, Friday</p>
<p>F &amp; ice<br/>E &amp; I go to Pictures &amp; they are bon. Come Home for supper at Top's flat &amp; then off to bed later on. Recd 2 letters from Auntie Annie. I sleep in spring bed &amp; rather don't sleep. Dream of hurricanes &amp; volcanoes etc on Esplanade.</p>
