<p>Continental caf&eacute;, Swanson St, caught 6.10 PM train to Kew, found all out.  Returned to camp by 8.30 pm.</p>
<p>Monday 1/11/15<br/>Raining hard.  Early morning march to Port Melbourne.<br/>Pay parade 2 pm.  Late leave at night.  Tea at Francatelli Caf&eacute;.  Went Her Majesty's theatre to "The Old guard".  Supper at Boulevard.</p>
<p>Tuesday 2/11/15<br/>Cup Day<br/>Made preparations during morning for trip to Sydney.  Left Melbourne (Spencer St) 4 pm train, arrived Albury 10.10 pm, left 11.5 pm</p>
