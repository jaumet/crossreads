  
 October 1916. 30th.   I have been on water picquet all day to-day.  Water is very scarce here as it has to be carted for miles & consequently is guarded.  It has been raining very hard all day & a strong wind has been blowing. 31st   Pritchard & myself went out to High Wood this morning.  High Wood a few weeks ago was the scene of a very severe battle & many hundreds of lives were lost in its capture.  The German lines can be seen from there & he is continually bombarding the place.
