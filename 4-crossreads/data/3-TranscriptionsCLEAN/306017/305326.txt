 Sunday 6 oclock reveille  It was much better than 5 am & 5.30 am get-ups at the school  Did practically nothing all day except go to church.  Took over orderly sergeant in the afternoon.  It is a fairly easy job.  The weather is warm & the flies are getting very thick. 
 Monday 27th  Usual mounted parade & food for man & horse.  There is nothing much doing in the way of drill.  All spare horses are taken off the lines & exercised the squadron often pulling up at the canal for a swim.  Received some letters 6 altogether & answered one or two
