 Feb. Thursday 22 1917 
 Stay in bed all day. not much change either way. Word comes that Harry Dingle has died of wounds received from bomb./ There, both poor old Phillips &amp; he have gone. The two I carried with during the Pozieres stunt, where they got their M.Ms. The pity of it all. The futile waste of fine manhood.
