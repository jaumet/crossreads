 [Thursday 19 October 1916] 
 Wrote Mother yesterday packed up for Blackpool. Raining &amp; Cold Dolly well. Mrs Cotton here to Afternoon tea; took her to Trocadero good music, but London is cold &amp; I'll be glad to go to a warmer climate if possible. Address all letters as before 1st Light Horse Reg Egypt. Laura comes back from Walsall on Tuesday 24th October so I hope to see here again- Enquired for Jones as requested by Joe &amp; enclose all information I can get here.
