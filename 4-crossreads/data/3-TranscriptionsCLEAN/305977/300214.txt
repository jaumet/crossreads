 Tuesday 18 [May] We had a most interesting day.  We were in sight of land most of the time.  We reached Cape Hellas at dusk & saw four aeroplanes making observations.  There were a lot of transports & warships.  We watched the shells bursting during the night & there was rapid machine gun & musketry fire. 
 Wednesday 19th Stayed most of the day at Cape Hellas & were beginning to get tired of looking at the shore & wondering what was doing there.  However towards evening we got away to go down to the place the Australians landed.  We watched more bombardment of Turkish batteries by the "London".
