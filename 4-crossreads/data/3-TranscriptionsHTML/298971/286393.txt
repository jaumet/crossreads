<p>Thursday 14th September</p>
<p>On job.  Bitterly cold.  Weather dull for observation purposes.  2000 loads of ammunition coming across bridge behind us tonight so probably things will liven up.</p>
<p>Friday 15th Sept.</p>
<p>Bitterly cold last night.  Day turned out very clear so aeroplanes made the best of it.  12 of them were visible, all ours.  Guns broke out in the evening.</p>
<p>Saturday 16th Sept.</p>
<p>On job in morning.  In afternoon went to Estaires</p>
