<p>Monday 26/2/17</p>
<p>Moved to Acid Drop Camp. Bde taking over the line, wrote to Doff.</p>
<p>Tuesday 27/2/17</p>
<p>Received orders to proceed to line and attack, Battn moved up to get into position.</p>
<p>Wednesday 28/2/17</p>
<p>Attack timed for 3 am with artillery postponed owing to guides losing themselves &ndash; Battn returned to Acid Drop Camp, men very tired.</p>
<p>Thursday 1/3/17</p>
<p>Orders received to have another attempt with artillery preparation, all arrangements made and Bn got up well early.</p>
