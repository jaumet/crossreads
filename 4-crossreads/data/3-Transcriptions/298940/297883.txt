<p>Remarkable to state that was the last we had that night, &amp; we went to sleep in our dug out which was slightly tinged with the gas, <strike>no</strike> &amp; did not wake until Harry Barnes came in next morning with the news that a good number of the 9th Batt. were gassed, above all,  -- the Machine Officer Capt Phillips.<br/>Koll &amp; Mick Stafford went down with a couple of patients &amp; I went straight to the R.A.P. where I found to my astonishment that medical details, engineers, &amp; patients had all spent the night in these gaseous dig outs.</p>
