<p>1915<br/>May. 19 Wed.<br/>Up to to-day there another Private (commonly known as "Tango") &amp; myself running the Administration Office for the Hospital but this morning Tango moved into the Secretary &amp; Registrar's (Capt. Mackenzie) office leaving me on my "ownio", what?<br/>Changed my sleeping quarters from upstairs to the office which is a fine, well lighted room with stacks of ventilation. Am dossing on a pretty comfortable mattress.</p>
