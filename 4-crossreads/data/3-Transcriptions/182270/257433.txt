
<p>THURSDAY, MAY 1, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, with a light shower</p>
<p>in evening. Fixed up the Boundary</p>
<p>bore in morning &amp; the Seven</p>
<p>Star in afternoon. Eric Cadell</p>
<p>&amp; Jack &amp; Fairly Snape came out</p>
<p>to dinner.</p>
<p>Got a lot of mail readdressed</p>
<p>from England.</p>
