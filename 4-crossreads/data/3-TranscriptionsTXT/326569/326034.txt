 Tuesday 3/4/17 Drilled in the slush &amp; snow on parade ground making feet very cold.  In afternoon route march through Compton Chamberlain, Favant, Dinton &amp; Barford back to camp;  a distance of 9&frac12; miles with full packs up. 
 Wednesday, 4/4/17 Still cold but drilled outside all day. 
 Thursday, 5/4/17 Had Battalion drill as supposed to be reviewed by King at Bulford on Saturday. 
 Friday, 6/4/17 Good Friday &amp; had a holiday.
