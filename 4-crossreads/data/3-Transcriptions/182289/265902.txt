<p>and set preliminary distribution of guns prior to the issue of a map to various units.  Sketch was to be sent in to Batt. on the 25th showing dispositions of guns on secondary tasks.<br/>The following targets were engaged at irregular intervals throughout the night, 2000 rounds being fired:-  Gun position at O.30.a.19.73.<br/>(1)  Trenches P.32.a.9.2.<br/>(2)  Fork Roads P.26.c.85.38.<br/>(3)  Road Junction P.31.c.85.65.<br/>(4)  Road Junction V.1.a.75.05.<br/>(5)  Trenches V.1.a.9.7.<br/>(6)  Crossing V.1.a.5.6.<br/>Enemy Artillery.  Activity throughout the day normal.  Light harassing fire during night, chiefly in O.28.a. &amp; b., probably searching for single guns of ours which were active about this time.  Two of our H.V. guns (about 6") were seen to move well forward into the valley O.22.c. and fire during the night.  Fork Roads in O.27.b. lightly shelled about 11 a.m.</p>
<p>25.5.18<br/>Sketches as required by Batt. completed and sent on at 12 noon.<br/>Capt. Chambers visited three Batt. Hqrs. of Left Bde. and also certain guns and new positions in O.24.a.</p>
