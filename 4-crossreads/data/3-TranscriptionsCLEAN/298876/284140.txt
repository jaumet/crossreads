
Cape Guardafui came to view about 5 pm, we were quite close to some Hundreds of Porpoises & Dolphins.Semi finals of a Boxing Tournament took place - two AMC Boys gave a good account of themselves, one got a draw & the other skied the towel after a good exhibition in the third round. Land is lost to sight again about 9 pm.
Tuesday29.2.16
Fairly warm. Very Calm. Saw some half dozen boats, one Cruiser in the distance.Lights of Aden came to view about midnight - also several other light Houses - A very pretty sunset - very red.
