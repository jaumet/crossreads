  
 a jerry to some of these gentlemen. Left Folkestone this afternoon & marched through the streets to the wharf, the streets were very lively & full of girls & women down for the holidays I expect. Had to wait on the boat for a couple of hours while the rest of the drafts were embarking, we had quite a small fleet going over with us & a small fleet of destroyers as escort, it was a very fine sight & to make it better a big airship cruised nearly all the way over with us, she used to almost come within touching distance & we could see everything as plain as anything, I wish I had my Kodak what a snap one could have got, an officer who was next to me had his but
