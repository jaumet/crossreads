<p>12.  Now Mum, It is very late &amp; I've taken about 2 &frac12; Hours to write this so I'll have to buzz off to bed.  Give My regards to all the neighbours &amp; do what you can for the poor widows who take up their residence on the Hill.<br/>Heaps of Love to Dad Mum &amp; George<br/>From<br/>Your own Boy<br/>Harry<br/>XXX</p>
