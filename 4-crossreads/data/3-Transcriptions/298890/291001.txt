<p>6. Thursday<br/>Warmish day.  Hardly able to crawl but went to Minerva &amp; to Caxton Hall to serve tea in afternoon.  Thence crawled home to bed.</p>
<p>7. Friday<br/>Cold grey day.  Wrote a little. Quite a [indecipherable] with [indecipherable]</p>
<p>8. Saturday<br/>Lovely day.  Went to Clara Butt &amp; other's concert in Albert Hall with F.P.  Then we had tea at Knightsbridge &amp; put A.A.S. on bus for Charing Cross.  F.P. &amp; S took her rubbish to Despard Arms  then rode on bus to Mrs. Mills.  Had supper at Marble Arch &amp; got home at 10.  F.P. left at 11.20  He did not like C.B. &amp;  was cross.</p>
