 Wednesday 26th 
 The day perfect went through the Straights of Gibralta last night &amp; are now in the sparkling Mediteranean.  Warm bright &amp; calm.   Can  Could see the mountains in Spain clearly in morning.  Enjoyed the warmth &amp; read most of the day.
