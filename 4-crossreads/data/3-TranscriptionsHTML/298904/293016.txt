<p>&amp; Australian papers had been gassing about it for some considerable time.<br/>Most of our trenches were heavily mined especially "Lone Pine" Quinns Post '<br/>&amp; the trenches round " Walkers Ridge" they reckon there was just about 7 ton of Ammonal under the " Neck" close to Walkers Ridge, &amp; Lone Pine would be blown to Smithereens &amp; a good few Turkish trenches with it for our chaps had mined well under their positions.<br/>There were tons upon tons of explosive destroyed so as the Turks could not get it.<br/>Len &amp; I were in the second last party to leave we volunteered</p>
