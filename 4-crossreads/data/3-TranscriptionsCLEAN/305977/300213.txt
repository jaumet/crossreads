
Sunday 16thWe arrived at Alexandria early, just after daylight & embarked on the Late N.D.L. Steamer "Lutzow" .  The space allocated to us was very small but we were all a happy crowd so it doesn't matter, as we will only be a couple of days on board.  We got our first issue of biscuits & bully, which in future will be the main feature in our menu.  We had a good swim in the harbour in the morning.  The water was beautiful.  There were dozens of stowaways from practically every regiment, even some Tommies.  The weather is delightful & it looks as if we will have a good trip.
