<p>Our Battn came out on the 6th  &amp; we finished up in one of the Bazentin[?] Camps after marching a mile or so out of our track.<br/>I carried a great big shell round with me which must have weighed 14 or 15 lbs  she was a walloper. <br/>We left Bazentin early next morning &amp; pulled up at our old Camp at Fricourt, where we are at present.<br/>7th My birthday  Spent it in Camp for it was fairly cold &amp; it took us some time to get tidied up again after coming out of the line<br/>8th Parade today &amp; the ground nice &amp; hard for it froze during the night. Got a few more reinforcements today.<br/>9th  Went for a bath today &amp; we badly needed it for</p>
