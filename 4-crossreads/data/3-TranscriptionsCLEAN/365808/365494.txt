He was always wandering about the front line. I know in the early morning after the second push at Messines, I had been with a Carrying Party taking up stuff to the front line - and on making one trip saw Holmes coming back from a visit up there. He was a fine soldier. I wrote Claud Reading a short account of our doings in the late stunt. Since then we have been employed on Fatigues - digging mostly at night.
