<p>The Springs<br/>Dubbo</p>
<p>Dear Father<br/>I hope you and all the rest at home are all well. The heat at Dubbo is dreadful. When I was coming up I felt as sick as possible. We folks think it nothing to go 9 or 10 miles a day, and sometimes go 30 miles a day. Three of the girls and I went to Goonoo. I was sitting at the back and got sick. We stayed</p>
