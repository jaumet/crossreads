<p>11.11.16<br/>Sat 11th<br/>Watson &amp; I left Ge'eila at 7 oclock this morning &amp; proceeded along the line mending it as we went, our journey half completed we were held up by some Tommies who told us to look out for a German spy dressed as an Australian riding a bay horse with a long mane &amp; tail he is carrying maps &amp; tells anyone he meets that he lost the main body while on patrol &amp; is trying to find his camp. we arrived at Geisi for an early dinner &amp; then I rode back on my own &amp; rather enjoyed the ride as the weather is perfect, arrived back in Camp early &amp; had some more boxing after tea, while some of the lads amused themselves kicking a football about</p>
