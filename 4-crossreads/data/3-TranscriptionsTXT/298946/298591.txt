 6th Ashore with Bill Adams &amp; Paterson Trip to  Umgeni.  Bucksee tea at Crown Hotel.  Went to Mr. Burton's with Hume in the evening. 
 7th  Church in morning.  Lunch with Mrs. Browning &amp; walk with family over hills in evening. 
 8th  With Adams &amp; Paterson 
 Wesley hut as usual &ndash; then wandered around writing letters &amp; searching for books till tea.  Popular pictures with Peters &amp;  Jes Figtree, Bill &amp; Pat. 
 9th Writing letters &amp; feeding with Pat &amp; Bill &ndash; and Peters &amp;Figtree  Dinner evening at Downings again.  Farewell.
