 13.9.17 Yesterday we drove over to Studland again to have a bathe, but it was trifle too breezy so returned and had lunch in Swanage.  In the afternoon we went for a long motor drive out to Corfe Castle through Lyneham and Lulworth and back to Swanage again.  The scenery around these places is simply gorgeous.
