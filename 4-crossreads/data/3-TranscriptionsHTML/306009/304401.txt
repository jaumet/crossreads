<p>1915<br/>Aug. 5 Thu.<br/>Great linguistic possibilities in Egypt. This evening I was talking to an Armenian refugee from Constantinople &amp; while I was with him he spoke in English, Arabic, Armenian, French &amp; Italian. He is a boy of 16 or 17 &amp; belongs to the Armenian Boy Scouts of Cairo. He lived for many years in Russia.<br/>Mena Convalescent Depot from to-day is an altogether separate Unit from us. Major Grey is O.C. &amp; 2 other Officers and 8 Nurses as well as men have been detached from No. 2 G.H. to constitute the personnel.</p>
