<p>25. Thursday<br/>Went to bank. Met Fred Post at corner. Struggled thru the day &amp; went to a couple of  [indecipherable] with Mrs Fisher while having attack &amp; temp. Crawled home to bed. Torrential rain.</p>
<p>26. Friday.<br/>Heavy showers. Struggled thru day waiting &amp; cooking &amp; crawled home to bed.</p>
<p>27. Saturday.<br/>Pelting showers. Wrote letters in morning. In afternoon Nan took me to tea at Lyceum Club to meet Mr Ford, just back from Scandinavia. I suffered horribly having an attack right there. Temp &amp; rheumatic pains. Stories of atrocities in Finland awful.</p>
