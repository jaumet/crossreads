  
 April 1918 Tuesday 9 
 Blaney Tronville   D.R.  [Despatch Rider] Weather much about the same, fine during the morning - rained all the afternoon. Fritz shelled fairly lightly, roads and villages. Have so far not had a run to do today. Wrote a few whzz bangs. Fritz planes flew over chateau and home again attended by machine gun and anti fire 19th Batt captured Decaguaville
