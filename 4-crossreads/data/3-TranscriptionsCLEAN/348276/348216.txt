  
 War Diary 17th Sept. Work continuing in Trenches and Saps. 
 18th September Do.  Do. 73 Reinforcements for 8th, 9th, and 10th - arrived from Heliopolis. 
 19th Sept. Inspection of Trenches and saps - In afternoon enemy made demonstration at 1730 for 1/2 an hour accompanied by Rifle and M.G. fire from Battleship and Snipers Ridge - one of our own 18 prs landed in our trenches few feet from Col. Abbott - failed to explode - Norfolks marched without reference to B.H. Qrs. evidently in instructions from their own C.O. - Work going well in Trenches and Saps.
