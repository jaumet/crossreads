Tuesday 30.10.17
Received registered parcel from home containing gloves, very pleased with them. Went to pictures in afternoon, had tea at Co-Operative store.
Wednesday 31.10.17
Inquiry came through from home regarding my condition.
Thursday 1.11.17 to Wednesday 7.11.17
Have received quite a number of parcels during past week. Also received official notification on 4.11.17 that I have been awarded the M. Medal. Again played in a whist drive
