 8 Dec 17 Left S. Flow in company with Light Cruiser Birmingham &amp; 7 Destroyers. We are guarding a convoy. 
 9 &amp; 10 Dec 17 Sweeping North Sea &amp; convoying 4 ships. 
 11th Dec 17 Arrived back at Scapa &amp; coaled. 
 12 Dec 17 Made false start &amp; had to return &amp; anchor. 
 14 Dec 17 Coaled. 
 15 to 18th Dec 17 Anchored at Scapa. 
 19th Dec 17 Left to convoy food ships. 30 in number. 
 20 Dec 17 Picked up 20 more ships &amp; escorted them into harbour (Norway). Seas rough. 30 odd more ships left to come back with escort.
