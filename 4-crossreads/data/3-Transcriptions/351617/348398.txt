<p>Leaving "Half Way House" Dugouts we cut across country until we hit the Menin Road and then up the Road to Hooge. The guns on both sides were going as hard as they could and all along the Road were bursting shells, Dead mules &amp; men, broken Lorries and every minute I expected to get hit with a piece of flying shrapnel that was falling like hail.</p>
