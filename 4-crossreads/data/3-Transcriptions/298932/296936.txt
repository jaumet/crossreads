<p>ACQUIN<br/>Monday 23<br/>The boy's are out on a midnight stunt charging imaginary foes, they won easy<br/>Tuesday 24<br/>My arm is like a summer sunset for five lovely boils give it a lovely color<br/>Wednesday 25<br/>Rain again, this old tent of ours is standing it very well<br/>Thursday 26<br/>Had my arm lanced this morning, I have it in a sling but still do my work</p>
