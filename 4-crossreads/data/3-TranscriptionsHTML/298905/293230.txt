<p>from the Brigade canteen &amp; on the way back had to run the gauntlet of a high explosive bombardment, anyone looking for thrills, haven't far to go for them when high explosives are about.<br/>While we were away the Germans shelled our trenches pretty heavy &amp; caused a few casualties, our Coy. suffered pretty heavy during our stay in the trenches this time, but nothing to what old Fritz has suffered for he has had no rest night nor day since we came in, &amp; every here &amp; there his trenches have been levelled to the ground, with artillery, &amp; "Tadpole" bombs &amp; grenade's<br/>22nd. Was out last night with a reconnoitring &amp; fighting patrol, there were 9 of us all told, Sergt Howarth took us out, once outside our barb wire we formed into fighting</p>
