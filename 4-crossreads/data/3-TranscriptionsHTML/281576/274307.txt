<p>Tues. Dec. 4.  Things much the same.  Weather jolly cold.  We sit round the Fire &amp; tell yarns, &amp; smoke.  My Blotter Cover is nearly finished.</p>
<p>Wed. 5.  Serg. Rolfe, Woods &amp; Watkins went away to Weymouth today.  Finished my Fancy Work.  Com. Letter No. 46.</p>
<p>Thur. 6.  Fearfully cold.  Another Air Raid over London.  Not much damage done, &amp; two Gothas brought down.  Wrote France &amp; Sam Ibbs &amp; finished Letter 46 to B.E.</p>
<p>F. S. S. M.  T.   W.  T.<br/>Dec. 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13</p>
<p>Things about the same.</p>
<p>FRI. Dec. 14.  Sister Cummings went away today &amp; Sister Davies came back.  She will no doubt shake things up.  The first thing was to mark a number of us to have our meals at the Mess Hut, which is rather hard this cold weather.  Capt. Fox continues in good humour.  He has nickname for us all. I am "The Professor".<br/>Com. Letter No 47 to M.S.</p>
<p>Sat. 15  Great excitement in the Ward.  9 of our family were issued with their Khaki, in prep. For going away by the hospital Ship tomorrow.</p>
