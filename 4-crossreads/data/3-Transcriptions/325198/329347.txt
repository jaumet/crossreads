<p>20 Aug, 1916<br/>Hot Summer Nights<br/>"From the Bully"<br/>Dead ring for old "Coogee"</p>
<p>Hot summer nights;  Hot summer nights!<br/>The beach &ndash; a splash; soft tarts in tights;<br/>A glow of distant city lights;<br/>Ti-tree in silhouette; a white pearl sky;<br/>Oceans of lovers, lovers pass my eye;<br/>Soft, shaded grass; pale, shining sands;<br/>Romantic sighs; holding of hands;<br/>White tracks, with stealing shades of trees;<br/>Sweet scents of earth; a gentle breeze;<br/> A glimmer of white; a sigh, a sneeze;<br/>Full lips of red, a kiss, a squeeze;<br/> A stealthy voice, a laugh, a rustling skirt,<br/>A world of whispers, &amp; the world aflirt.<br/>My oath, hot nights<br/>Hot tarts in tights<br/>Hot views, hot sighs &ndash;<br/>Real hot, my oath! Hot summer nights!</p>
<p>Hal [indecipherable]</p>
