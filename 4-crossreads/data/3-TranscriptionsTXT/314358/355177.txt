 4 Are fast asleep, with their heads upon their packs 
 Very early rising and many hours in the train have brought complete weariness.  Sleep takes charge for a time and is in command. 
 Not all are in the forgetfulness of sleep. 
 One or two, are, like myself, sitting each on his pack, and writing home 
 Half a dozen are playing cards, gambling, if you will have it so; but they are good sports, which is excuse enough.
