<p>By now I was about six miles from Camp &amp; once again took to the road &amp; two miles further on, at Wishford Village, picked up another wayside wanderer from another A.I.F. Camp, who also came from Newtown, and with him I journeyed towards Salisbury City.</p>
<p>However we had to pass through the town of Wilton ere reaching our destination &amp; so had to tack across country in order to dodge the M.P.'s for we were by then "out of bounds" without a pass. The sally was successfully negotiated &amp; by 6.30 we arrived at Salisbury &amp; proceeded to the Soldier's "Guest House" &amp; had tea in a room known as "Tipperary Room".
