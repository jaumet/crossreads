<p>Tuesday, May 1st 1917<br/>Nice fine spring day.  A big battle plane flew over in morning.  After tea went to Perham Downs with Mooney for a hot bath.</p>
<p>Wednesday, May 2nd<br/>Reveille 6.30 a.m. &amp; food still very scarce.  Several more aeroplanes flew over.  Route march in afternoon about 4 miles.  Paid 30/- after tea, then went to the town with Dawson to post some letters.</p>
<p>Thursday, May 3rd<br/>Started at Bombing School this morning, also voted for Federal elections.  Pay Books collected again in morning on account of shortage</p>
