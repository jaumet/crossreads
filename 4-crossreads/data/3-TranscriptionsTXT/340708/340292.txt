 Nov 1917 23  Fri Very rough it increased to a Hurrican this evening Sat 24th Calmed down tonight Sun 25 It came up for another blow this evening Monday 26th Very rough all day but calmed down this evening Wed 28th Hove to this morning for the Naval man to go back to his boat passed a big convoy.  Came up squally with a heavy beam sea Thursday 29 Still rough cruiser left us Sunday Dec 2 Arrived outside Newport    Newport News this evening Monday 3rd Pulled in the Health office gave us a clean bill.   We went right up &amp; anchored of the wharf as there was not berth
