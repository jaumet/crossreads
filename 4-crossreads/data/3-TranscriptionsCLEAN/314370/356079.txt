
September, 1916Saturday 2Route march for troops. but I am detailed for other work & miss it.After dinner went into Grantham.  evening to pictures, returning to camp at 11 pm are startled by loud hooter, all lights doused & traffic stopped.  "ZEP. RAID"But it never came near us.  English people panic stricken at the very thought of it.
Sunday 3No report out yet about ZEP.  Morning cold & dark.
