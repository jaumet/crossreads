19/10/16 Crossed the line
24/10/16 Arrived at Dakka had feed of fish
2/11/16 Arrived at Pymouth &amp; went to Wareham
3/11/16 Went to Hurdcott via Southampton
24/11/16 London four days leave. Met Jack Wirth &amp; went to Butchers Ernie dead.
11/12/16 London again to bank. Good trip home.
14/12/16 France at Boulogne (Blanket Hill)
15/12/16 Etaples
23/12/16 Buire to battalion Good Xmas dinner. Spent New Years Day with (Archie) Griff, Ben &amp; Strange.
