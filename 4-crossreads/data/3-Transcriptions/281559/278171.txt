<p>by the YMCA : good canteen on board so we ought to do well : concert at night &amp; band in afternoon :</p>
<p>Thursday May 15th at Sea</p>
<p>lovely day &amp; one can feel the heat coming along now : passed a couple of boats : band in afternoon :</p>
<p>Friday May 16th at Sea</p>
<p>usual routine : expect to be in Las Palmas tomorrow :</p>
<p>Saturday May 17th Las Palmas</p>
<p>dropped anchor about 6 am : the island looked well as we drew near with the white city in the foreground &amp; the towering mountains in the back-</p>
