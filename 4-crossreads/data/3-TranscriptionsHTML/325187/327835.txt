<p>[Thursday 30 March 1916]</p>
<p>Today I had some business with the Principal of Technical College- There are 14 of these &amp; include all Trades- &amp; Agriculture.  The Native students are making me a pair of boots for 80 P.T. &amp; presented me with a hand worked silk handkerchief.<br/>Slept last night in a double Bed - sheets- Cold shower-etc etc.<br/><strike>Recd letters from Laura Mch 1 [indecipherable] Joh:-</strike><br/>Heavy Khamsin or Dust Storm &amp; <u>very hot</u><br/>On Court Martial of SIKH Indian Mountain Battery who shot his Sgt Major pure Murder.</p>
