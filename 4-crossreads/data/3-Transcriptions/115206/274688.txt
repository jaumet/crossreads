<p>April 1918<br/>Sunday 28</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours.    D.R.<br/>Weather wet throughout day. Rode across to La Neuville as guide to a new runner.<br/>Met wagon lines in new position out from Pont Noyette. Fritz shelling village with gas.<br/>Rode up to W.L. with wire and cigarettes, in the evening.<br/>Fairly heavy shelling in the region of Viller Bettnovino, numerous tanks passed through .<br/>Met Charlie.</p>
