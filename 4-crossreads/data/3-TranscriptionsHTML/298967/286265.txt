<p>DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS</p>
<p>March 1/2 &ndash; Hollebeke &ndash; B. took over line raid at time of relief.  Mjr. Henwood Killed.</p>
<p>9/10 - <strike>Hollebeke</strike> Crater Dugouts &ndash; Releived by 12th proceeded to Crater dugouts.</p>
<p>24/25 &ndash; Line Hollebeke &ndash; Releived 12th in line.  Casualties for March 141 other ranks.</p>
<p>April 3/4 - Hollebeke &ndash; Bn. releived by British Div. moved to Dezon Camp strength 39-764.</p>
<p>4 &ndash; Dezon Camp &ndash; Bn. left Duzon camp for Caestre by buss.</p>
<p>5/6 &ndash; Caestre &ndash; Bn. entrained at Caestre and left for Somme.</p>
<p>6 &ndash; Doulanville &ndash; Detrained at St. Rock (Amiens), moved to Doulenville, First Fire.</p>
<p>7 &ndash; Resting Second Fire.</p>
<p>8 &ndash; Resting third Fire.</p>
<p>9 &ndash; Vignacourt &ndash; Marched to Vignacourt.</p>
<p>10 &ndash; Rainneville &ndash; Marched back to <strike>Raie</strike> Rainneville.</p>
<p>12 &ndash; Bivouacked outside Amiens.</p>
<p>12/13 &ndash; 9 A.M. &ndash; St. Roch &ndash; Marched to Station in morning 13th, entrained for the North.</p>
<p>13/14 &ndash; 11 P.M. &ndash; Hondeghem &ndash; Detrained at Hondeghem about 11 P.M. very dark and Germans near Strazeele.</p>
<p>14 &ndash; Borre &ndash; Batt. marched to near Borre in supports.  T.L. Morbecque.</p>
<p>19 &ndash; Meteren Sector &ndash; Bn. releived support Bn. 33 Div. near Besace Farm South.</p>
<p>23/24 &ndash; Line &ndash; Bn. releived 11th Bn. in line.</p>
<p>24/25 &ndash; Line &ndash; Metren Stunt.  Total casualties for April 169 other Rnks.</p>
