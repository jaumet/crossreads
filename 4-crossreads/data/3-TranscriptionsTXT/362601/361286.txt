 23 October, Thursday 
 Ellis &amp; Etta to tea. We play whist. Dinner with Roy. Roy comes Home to sleep. Much argument at Home. Recd letter from Doris &amp; Staples. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie. 
 24 October, Friday 
 Frightful busy day. I cut 10 suits by 5 pm &amp; Otto 23. Dick comes Home but I can't go up to see Him.
