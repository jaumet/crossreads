<p>2<br/>from the trenches in relays leave their soiled clothing &ndash; have a hot  &amp;  cold bath  &amp;  put on clean clothes.<br/>It is a great institution for the poor beggars.<br/>Expect we will take our turn out at the Trenches stretcher bearing when the other section come off duty. So far none of the 1st Field bearers have been wounded.<br/>We have been told to put in for leave</p>
