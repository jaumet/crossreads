<p>Some very big shells dropping around. We expect to be relieved this afternoon.</p>
<p>7/11/16<br/>We were releived at 6 p.m. and marched to a camp on the Bazentin-Flers road. We moved on to Pommiers Camp.</p>
<p>It has been very wet all day and we had a miserable time.</p>
