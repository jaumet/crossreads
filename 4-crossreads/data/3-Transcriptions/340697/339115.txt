<p>"Fritz" bombed our front line today with 14 lb bombs. There was also a severe artillery bombardment on our reserve line. We had 11 Casualties 22.6.16</p>
<p>We were releived by 3rd bn and went into billets at Fleurbeaux 23.6.16</p>
<p>I left bn at Fleurbeux and went to bomb school on Lys. to practice for raid which we expect to take place in the course of a few days. We are billeted at a sawmill on the opposite side of the river to Sailly. 25.6.16</p>
