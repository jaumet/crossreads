Wednesday 30/5/17Cool &amp; cloudy but fine,Just heard that Viv has been boarded by the Drs &amp; sent home to Ausy. hope its true &amp; good luck to him.Hear that Austria is seeking a separate Peace, hope so.Fenton &amp; Hankinson the new men began yesterday on duty.Pay. 15/5 &pound;26.5.0.had a bath &amp; clean change yesterday &amp; what a treat after the vermin &amp; sweat covered shirts &amp; how they did Pong. its a great life.A little rain this afternoon thundery weather.Cant get a late Paper so don't know how the war is going.Going on a 90 mile march in a day or so to Alberville can see a lot not finishing.
Thursday 31/5/17lovely morning cool &amp; nice Breeze.A terrific Barrage put up last night all along the line &amp; another this morning early must have been attack &amp; counter.lots of Parties going away to schoolPay. 16/5 def. &pound;26.6.0.Been a lovely day, no letters.
Friday. 1st June 1917A glorious morning, France at her best marred by the Presence of two Taubes cruising overhead.Boys just gone but some on fatigue &amp; some to drill leaving here either tomorrow or Sunday.Pay 17/5 &pound;26.7.0
Saturday 2nd June 1917another fine morning warm means Hot dayheavy gun fire on our leftPay 18/5. &pound;26.8.0.Been Raining this afternoon now cold enough for a fireBoxing on at 54th linescricket &amp; football here.
Sunday 3 June 1917A very cold night but a beautiful morning, church Parade,Heavy fighting all nightrumours that Tommie's took Quent.Fritzs planes over head this morning. Shelling BapaumePay 19/5 &pound;26.9.0.no word of leave yet &amp; no letters.Seems rummy to be called Corp now.Had an exciting time exactly at &frac14;  to 10 PM.
