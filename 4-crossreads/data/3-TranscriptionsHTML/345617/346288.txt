<p>Notes du trimester<br/>1-1-18<br/>Company had an enjoyable time as far as possible. We were able to procure fowles etc. from the farming people &ndash; so we were able to make a good New Year dining.</p>
<p>2nd<br/>Sgt [indecipherable] &amp; I went into Boulogne.</p>
