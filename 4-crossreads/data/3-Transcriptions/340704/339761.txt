<p>April 1917<br/>Friday 13<br/>Concert in YMCA tent Dave sang ora pro nobis. He got a great reception.</p>
<p>Saturday 14<br/>This camp shelled by Turks. Several landed  in Hospital 14 casualties . Also bombed by Taubes no damage no casualties.<br/>First drill with gas masks</p>
