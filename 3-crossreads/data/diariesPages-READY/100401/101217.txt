Sunday 29
 Church parade. Wet weather
Monday 30
 Wet day cold.
Tuesday 31
 Tonight I spent very pleasantly, in a old Pozieres gathering Docker Barnby also James Robby were there we had a good talk over old times.
 I don't know, but in spite of paper talk etc I think peace movements are getting a hold.
August, 1917
 Wednesday 1
 If this country the people are realizing the utter absurdity of driving Germany out of Belgium France. Soon too, think we will compromise with Germany perhaps this year, as an outcome of this peace feeling.
 Wattle day at home
 Third battle of Ypres began yesterday has been made out to be a huge success. 
 What are our casualties in these stunts?
Thursday 2
 Change in the weather which up to yesterday was beautiful is now wet cold windy.
 Three very interesting lectures this morning on "Economy in War Time" principally. Also on the eternal topics.
Friday 3
 To go by coach with Dock Barnby to Salisbury tomorrow.
Saturday 4
 Salisbury trip cried off on account of bad weather Barnby not being able to come.
