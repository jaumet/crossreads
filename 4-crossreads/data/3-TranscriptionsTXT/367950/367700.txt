2nd December 1916 Arnussi SaturdayWent out on patrol today. Escort to Brigadier General Cox to our new Camp GERERAT - about 5 miles further out in the desert towards El Arish - we shall be going there in a few days - should have been there for some time now but the water was not satisfactory. The Engineers have been working at deepening the wells.
