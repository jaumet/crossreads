 The boat has a fair amount of deck space, but this is taken up by fixtures, and in any case is insufficient for the numbers on board. 
 We have a promenade deck on both sides amidships open to troops and families alike.  Above that is the boat deck forward half of which is for officers 2&amp; first class passengers ( who are few), and the aft half except on isolation sections for troops above.
