
September, 1917. Wednesday 26Fine day a bit misty. Didn't get up till 9 and found the work cancelled for the day. 50 prisoners came down this morning and heard 5th Div had captured their objective in spite of all. Spent morning cleaning up and went over to 12 gun and watched it for some time Other gun smaller. Also watched observation balloon. In afternoon wrote diary and some letters. Bed about 10 and have to go out to work again at 4.
