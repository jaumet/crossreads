
May 1916Meditteranean Sea31st   We have passed numerous ships to-day & yesterday, some with convoys, both British & French.  We have no convoy yet but have passed four or five warships since we left.  At night all lights are shaded & none are to be seen outside at all.  We passed Malta about 10 P.M. on our Starboard side about 5 miles away.1st June.  We passed Cape Spartivento (Sardinia) about 5 P.M. & are on a N.N.W. course, but still zig-zagging about as we have been doing since we left Alexandria.  Later at night we changed our course to W.S.W.
