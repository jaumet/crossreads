 1916 May 9 Tuesday 
 Parade at 10 a.m. &amp; told to be ready at 9p.m. to entrain.  Bob Tony &mp; all prepared. Muster to time  Farewell to the boys. Regrets.  Underwood still sick. MacBurney, Bill Parsons &amp; Jockey help us with our packs &amp; see us off. 20 of us all told.  Train loads off infantry engineers etc move off.  Leave Tel-el-Kabir at 11.27 p.m. in trucks
