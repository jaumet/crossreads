<p>27 Feb. Broke down in ocean drifting about nine hours  Osterley passed midday</p>
<p>28 Feb.  Going very slow.</p>
<p>1 Mar.  Arrived Fremantle midday on guard went ashore and up to Perth at nightime  very tame.</p>
