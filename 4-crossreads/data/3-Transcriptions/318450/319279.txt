<p>We left Cape Town about half past five on the 28th August and headed straight out to sea.</p>
<p>I may say we had about nine ships with us till we reached Cape Town of course including a man o'war but after leaving the port we were alone, and in one way I was glad as we could make our own pace, where when we had the rest we had <strike>the</strike> to go to suit the others which were very slow.</p>
