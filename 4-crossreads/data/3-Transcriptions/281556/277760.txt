<p>29th. Saturday.<br/>Stayed all day in support trench we made &amp; felt very much knocked up. Slept most of day.</p>
<p>Platoon strength now 15. Coy., about 60 strong. Fritz shelled our support &amp; reserve lines much during night. 1st line geting no fire at all.</p>
<p>30th. Sunday.<br/>Spent all day in the same place only duties to carry our own rations from kitchens, Sausage Gully. 2 Casualties since night. 2 Cpls of my platoon. 35 planes of ours flew over toward Fritz's lines together this evening, about sunset &amp; dense smoke was seen rising from rear of his platoons.</p>
