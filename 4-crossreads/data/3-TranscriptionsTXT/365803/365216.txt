He had called his officers into his dugout to confer with the, &  issue his orders when a shell smashed through. Fortunately, your son was killed instantly, but Lt. Campling lingered for some hours. Both officers were buried next day with Military honours (June 15th) &  crosses erected by the men.Lieut McCallum, &  Harper despatched the valises, etc to you.I can write no more, but believe me the officers &  men of this battalion grieves with you  in your great loss &  extend their deepest sympathy to you &   yours. And none so sincerely or so deeply asYours sorrowfullyR.F. FitzGerald
