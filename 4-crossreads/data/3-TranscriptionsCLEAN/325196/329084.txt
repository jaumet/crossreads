
10-1-16Mon 10thHad more Helio work again this morning & put this afternoon in packing up & getting our gear ready to go away, it is to be a muster parade tomorrow & and an in  inspection by Col.Cox to see that we are properly equipped .The Signalling panniers were packed on the transports this afternoon, I put the evening in writing
11-1-16Tue 11thThe Bde was on parade today & inspected by Col. Meredith, came in late off parade & put the evening in doing nothing; At 3 oclock Nelson & I went to Cairo, had a bath at the National & I arrived back in camp before 8 oclock & heard Stan was out to see me I had no idea he was in Egypt & very much surprised to hear the news
