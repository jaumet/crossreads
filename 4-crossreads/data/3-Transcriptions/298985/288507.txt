<p>us throw live Bombs as we should have done Fell in about 7 PM &amp; moved off about 8 Entrained at Warminster (5 miles march) and started at 11 <strike>arri</strike> good cariages 8 in each compartment arrived Shorncliffe at 5 AM &amp; marched on to Folkistone about 1 mile were put into resideantial Flats there &amp; given a drink of tea right of Breakfast at 7 (Bacon Potatoes Bread Jam &amp; tea) not a particular good meal. Fell in again given 24 hours ration and marched straight on to boat not far. Started at 10</p>
