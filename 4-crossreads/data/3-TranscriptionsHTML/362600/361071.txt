<p>F 3 Sun<br/>I write all afternoon. Get ready for Devonport  &amp; chase around after Rations etc. Say au Revoir to Norm.</p>
<p>Wrote. Doris.Bess. Norm. Hilda Hickson. Chris. Jennie. Lottie.Con.</p>
<p>W 4 Mon<br/>Catch 8.45 am I go down Town  &amp;  to museum. Visit Cis  &amp;  Con.Catch 11.17 to Exeter. Go all round  &amp;  to Cathedral. Dinner at Cellers  &amp;  go tram ride. Hand over Pat. at 9 pm. an P'mouth  &amp;  walk around Town for a room. Stay app. Continental Hotel.</p>
<p>F 5 Tues.<br/>Lovely Day. Go on Pier  &amp;  through Aquarium. See Girls Bathing Round old Plymouth to Fishing Docks. Have Cider no good. Take Photos on Pier. See  Guild Hall &amp; Church.<br/>Catch 2.38 ran. At Mrs E. at 1.15 pm music etc.<br/>Wrote M.R.L &amp;  Dad. Stay all night.</p>
<p>F 6 Wed<br/>Lovely Day. Catch 1.50 train to B'ford. Get fixed up &amp; catch 12.27 Back again. Jean [indecipherable]  &amp; practise piano. Go to Dance at 7 pm. Have jolly time. Photo taken. Go home 2 am.  [indecipherable]  hit Longley across the head. Show not complete without a drunken Australian</p>
<p>7 Thurs.<br/>Sent parcel to Auntie Annie yesterday</p>
<p>Dull Arr. B'ford. 10 am come up with Hobdro Girls. Recd. Letters from Mavis. Carter. M.R.L &amp; Nancy.   &amp; parcel from Bess. Tidy up room  &amp;  generally mess about.<br/>See Moran whose sister gave me present of Pen Knife when in Condor St Shop.<br/>Wrote to M.R.L.</p>
<p>F 8 Fri<br/>Except for a storm of half an hour Duration. E goes to Sarum to buy Ring. D gets ready for Weymouth. I get new shirt &amp;  breeches. Go to Theatre Nah Poo with Top. E goes to Walk Coast.</p>
<p>Recd P.C. from Gen Roberts.</p>
<p>F 9 Sat<br/>Very Windy but not cold. Dave goes away. He gives me two parcels before he goes. I develop 2 films. I sit   &amp;  read all night until Top   &amp;  Robby come down at 9 pm. E goes to Theatre.</p>
