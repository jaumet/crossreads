 11.35pm 25/9/17 
 Glencorse Wood 
 We relieved the 55th Bn from this position last night.  Our relief effected with few casualties about 11 A.M.  Soon afterwards the enemy opened up a terrific barrage of large calibre H.E. shells and caused a good deal of damage to transport and moving troops.
