<p>2.9.16<br/>Sat 2nd<br/>4 more men were sent away for leave today but owing  to a breakdown on the line the men had to wait a [at] Railhead till mid-day for a train instead of leaving at 8 oclock;  Mr James returned from Cairo today &amp; said Nelson &amp; Smithers both left for England yesterday  We have only 3 Sigs &amp; one Cpl (Sig) left in the Regt today as Law got leave &amp; Nelson &amp;Locke left today to proceed to School of Instruction<br/>Bassan's court martial was continued today but we have not got the verdict yet. I am taking a shift on the 'phone from 8 till 12 tonight; &amp; applied last night for leave to Port Said tomorrow. James sent a cable home for me from Cairo.</p>
