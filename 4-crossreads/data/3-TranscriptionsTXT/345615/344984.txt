 Jan 27 (Friday) The aeroplane had an accident at noon today on its return from its daily patrol. Was watching it while watering my horse &amp; just as it was 30 or 40 feet of the ground it fell 'head first',  just  on the beach within a few feet of the water &ndash; Luckily both aviator &amp; observer were only severely shaken &amp; were able to walk to the ambulance which took them to hospital to be attended to
