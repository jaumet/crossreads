  
 I take Mrs. Scales to Drury Lane where we witness & hear "Cavalleria & Pagliacci (Beecham Grand Opera Company) - Rosena Buckman, Robert Parker, Frank Mullings, Jeanne Brola the most important of a "great cast" - glorious singing - splendid acting - the Old & Historical Drury Lane Theatre - On our return journey to Elm Bank we are held up in the Tube for 3 hours owing to German Air Raid.
