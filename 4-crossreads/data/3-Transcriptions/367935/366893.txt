<p>BRIEF SYNOPSIS OF WAR-DIARY.<br/>O.L.S. HOLT.<br/>32 Boyce St.,<br/>GLEBE.</p>
<p>DIARY No.1.  1915</p>
<p>June 14th. to July 27th : Camp at Enoggera<br/>July 27th  to Aug.31st. Sydney to Suez &ndash; S.S.Suffolk.<br/>3rd Sept. et seq.: Description of natives.<br/>Cairo and Alexandria.<br/>16th " to 19th  Voyage to Mudros.<br/>26th " Anzac. to 4th Dec. (N.B. snow and blizzards: 28th Nov.)</p>
<p>DIARY No.2.   1916<br/>Description of native feeling in Egypt.<br/>30th. Jan. to 23rd . June: Maadi. Prisoners-of-War camp.<br/>7th March: Turkish prisoners plotting.<br/>Interview with Jaffa Pasha (brother of Enver Bey)<br/>9th March: Pyramids.<br/>25th April: Memorial service at Anzac Hostel&mdash;Cairo.<br/>8th June: Sidelights on Turkish methods in Syria.<br/>24th June: Tel-el-Kebir.<br/>2nd to 8th Aug.: Alexandria to Marseilles per S.S. Franconia.<br/>End of diary: Notes on the landing; sketch map of Anzac and sketch</p>
<p>DIARY No.3  1916-1917<br/>24th Aug. to 23rd. March: Salisbury Plains and Weymouth.</p>
<p>DIARY No. 4  1917<br/>27th March to 25th. Aug. England &ndash; Parkhouse, Fovant, and Wareham.</p>
<p>DIARY No. 5  1917<br/>26th Sept. to 18th Oct. : Parkhouse<br/>18th Oct, : "The story of Dingo".<br/>20th Oct,: Havre.<br/>23rd., and 24th Oct.: Rouen, and description of approaching the line.<br/>26th et seq First impressions of the Belgian war zone.<br/>(behind Ypres)</p>
<p>DIARY No.6.  1918.<br/>5th Jan. et seq: Behind the Messines sector: Locre and Sec Bois.<br/>28th Feb "  "  In the Ypres-Comines canal sector.<br/>Detailled description of the Ypres country.<br/>12th March: Description of a gas attack, and "no-man's land".<br/>17th March: In hospital.<br/>20th    "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hospital bombed. V.C. man described.</p>
<p>DIARY No. 7.<br/>9th to 16th April: Havre.  Conjectures on German advance.<br/>Preparations to leave for line again.<br/>17th to 20th  " : En route to line.  Rouen.<br/>18th : Description of communications during retreat.<br/>20th Arrival in Strazeele sector, near Hazebrouck.</p>
