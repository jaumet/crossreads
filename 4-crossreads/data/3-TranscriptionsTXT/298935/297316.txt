The docks were full of French warships and we visited one of our own the "Agamemnon" which was being repaired after being slightly damaged in the Dardenelles.On the 23rd June I sailed in the hospital ship "Nevasa" bound for England.  En route we sighted some of the snow-capped Sierras of Spain and, of course, Gibralter.  We berthed in Southampton and the Australians were taken to Manchester.
