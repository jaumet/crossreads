<p>6.3.15</p>
<p>morning; at stables this afternoon I paraded to the hospital &amp; found Dr Idione was not in camp, but had my throat painted, I was not too sick to parade at tea time, although I am suffering from a swelled head;  no parade to-day on account of the work we had to do last night;  The second rounds of the boxing contest are to be contested to-night;  I am sorry I am too sick to witness them; <strike>paraded to</strike> went to bed early</p>
