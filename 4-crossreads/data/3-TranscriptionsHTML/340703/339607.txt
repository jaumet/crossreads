<p>March 1916<br/>Wednesday 15<br/>March to Albert Park, guests of Red Cross Ladies. Excellent lunch. I had lovely 20 mile drive in car. Back to Melb at 1.30 special leave to purchase .<br/>Saw the block and the styles. Girls smostly done up, but still cold and distant.</p>
<p>Thursday 16<br/>Left Melb at 3pm for Colombo. Only got 1 wine from home. No letters or word from anyone else.  Aust Flying Squadron joined us here before leaving</p>
