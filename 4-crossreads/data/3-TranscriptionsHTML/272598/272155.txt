<p>Thursday 1.3.17<br/>Heavy bombardment during night. Beautiful day, quite a treat to feel the warmth of the sun after all the recent cold weather.</p>
<p>Friday 2.3.17<br/>Another day over.  Went to pictures in Albert at night.  Wrote home.  Lt/Col Williams who came away with our unit; badly wounded while inspecting his outposts.  Has lately been transferred to 1st</p>
