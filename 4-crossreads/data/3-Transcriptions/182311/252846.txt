<p>it increases the activity tremendously; bright skies &amp; clear air mean excellent observation &amp; the artillery of both sides is very busy <s>directing</s> their firing being directed by Aeroplanes &amp; great Sausage Balloons</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; One day last week the wind being very favourable Fritz had a balloon which looked right into our trenches &amp; every movement drew artillery fire but an aeroplane of our flew in that direction &amp; the German Sausage was hauled down without any delay.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; There is no doubt of the Air Supremacy being British daily we witness encounters between planes &amp; tis always the German who is broken &amp; falls &amp; where the observation balloon is used always you see British, once nineteen were counted, the Germans perhaps a few.</p>
