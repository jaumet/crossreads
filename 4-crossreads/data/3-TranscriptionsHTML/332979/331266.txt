<p>Monday 5/3/17.<br/>The Weather still being hot &amp; close although now &amp; again we have a good shower of rain still it is very close, down below we we have our meals it is very stuffy, although we have two Wind Sails which come down below. I am very fortunate as I took up a position on the floor to sleep &amp; have slept there since leaving Fremantle &amp; as one of the Windsails is directly over my head I have it very cool all night. As there is still a doubt when we will cross the Equator I believe we must be almost on the Line. We have most beautiful Sunsets now it is a glorious Light As the Sun Sets the Sky is lit up brightly it reminds me of large</p>
