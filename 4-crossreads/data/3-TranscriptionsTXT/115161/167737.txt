 Thus ended my military career. 
 For four and a half years I had worn khaki, and my life during the greater portion of that time had been spent amongst men, - men who have won the respect and admiration of the whole world, - men with whom it was an honor to be associated, - men to whom cowardice was a stranger, cruelty foreign and to whom fear was unknown. 
 The brave, gentle, fearless, high-spirited and chivalrous man with the &quot;turned up hat&quot;. - The genuine Australian soldier.&nbsp; The ever smiling and &quot;don&#39;t-care-a-hang&quot; AUSSIE. - The &quot;genial&quot; Digger. 
 May he ever enjoy what he fought for, - right - freedom - and liberty. 
 May good health - good luck - and prosperity, attend him and his.
