<p><u>12 midnight</u> The C.O. received an urgent message to report to 53rd Bn H.Q. to attend a conference.  He trudged off through the mud slush and rain and pitch dark. <strike>to</strike>  He returned about 2.30 A.M. and had a game of cards.  The rations having arrived during his absence he had a good meal and turned in after a very strenuous and worrying day after first informing all Coy. Commanders that our Bde. was to do a hop over, but that our Bn. would not as at first intended be one</p>
