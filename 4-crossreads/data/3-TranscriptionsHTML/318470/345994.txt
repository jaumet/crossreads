<p>Saturday 1st January 1916<br/>Staying Kedievial Hotel.  Despatched three large photos of Pyramids &ndash; 1 to Dorrie, 1 Anne &amp; Charlie, 1 to Ruby.  Bought wristlet watch P.T. 100.  "G. Lekegian, Photographer".<br/>Bought "Vest Kodak" double lense &amp; cover P.T. 300.  Day too cloudy for snapping.  Left Cairo 6.15, arrived "Tel El Kebir" 9 p.m. after an enjoyable day in town.</p>
