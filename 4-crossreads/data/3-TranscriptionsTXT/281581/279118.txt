The scenery is theses parts is delightful, great rolling hills with a tiny stream &ndash;"The Esk" winding through the valley, and typical sleeping villages nestling amongst the trees or clustered on the hillsides.  We are staying at a delightfully comfortable little Hostel with the Cognomen, The Fox and Hounds displayed over the entrance.
