 S.S. Finland. Off Holyhead March 6/16 
 My dear Ferguson 
 I can quite sympathise with the Children of Israel &amp; understand why they did not please Jehovah.  Forty years in the wilderness  was  tough!  Of course our pilgrimage has not been so bad or so long:  but its been bad enough &amp; long enough.  It  seems  ages since I left Sydney.  And it has been  over   6 weeks ! 
 Through Canada we travelled in state.  The Government cars &ndash; 2 &ndash; at our disposal &amp; everything very well done.  From Vancouver to the Atlantic seaboard all the country is covered with snow.  The Rockies are a glorious sight &ndash; through a steam-heated window.
