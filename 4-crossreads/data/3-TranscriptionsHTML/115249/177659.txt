<p>From - Brigadier.</p>
<p>To - Rear-Admiral C.</p>
<p><u>12th Septr. &#39;14.</u></p>
<p>(7-5 a.m.)&nbsp;</p>
<p>Have already recommended withdrawal of Troops from Wireless Station to Kaba Kaul, thence to Herbertshohe as garrison. &nbsp;Infantry now at Herbertshohe to come on board &quot;Berrima&quot;, which should then proceed to Rabaul to carry out original intention. &nbsp;If approved please instruct Beresford.</p>
