Wednesday Sept 12 Applications called today for Engine Drivers, Firemen, Station Masters. Rumoured men who have had 1000 days service to go home on leave (they deserve it) Thursday Sept 13 Two Taubes overhead today dropped a few pills but did no damage. Transferred to Sanitary Inspector section Anzac Mounted Division. Tom & I transferred. We were told Sanitary Inspectors were needed & as we held Certificates were transferred. Friday Sept 14 Left Shuk Naran 10 AM for Marakat
