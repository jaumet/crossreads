  July 9th  
 The sports are continued - Tug o'  war competitions - A ship of 3,200. 
 Further study of "Principles and Practice of Elocution". 
 Perfect weather becomes our lot as we draw nearer to our native-land.  Our position on the map is conducive to much good humour amongst the troops. 
 Evening hymn service proves highly popular.
