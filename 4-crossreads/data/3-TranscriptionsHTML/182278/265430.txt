<p>Saturday 27.7.18&nbsp; Took Hetty for the Week end to the farm at Bramley.</p>
<p>Sunday 28.7.18&nbsp; Went for a walk &amp; groomed &quot;Mayfair&quot;</p>
<p>Monday 29.7.18&nbsp; Went back to Malden with Hetty, reported to H.Qrs, orders to proceed to Plymouth as O.C. Troops tomorrow</p>
<p>Tuesday 30.7.18&nbsp; Had lunch with M<sup>r</sup> &amp; M<sup>rs</sup> Gray, bought a watch for Doff, left by 4 p.m train from Paddington for Plymouth.</p>
<p>Wednesday 31.7.18. Took over as O/C Troops on [D17?] (Malta)</p>
