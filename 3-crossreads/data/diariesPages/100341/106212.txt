June 3rd continued
Mending letter writing. Reading novels B. L S Officers revolver practice afternoon, reading night.
June 4th 323M. West
B. Coy on Duty in charge of own section for Phy. D. till 7am. then on with Canteen Fatigue, very hot on the go all day till 8-30, good music singing aft.
June 5th West. M.305
Usual drill all day. Outpost in addition. At reveille we were running along the Socotra Island, very rough high mountains also passed 2 ships, one 7-30am. other .
