<p>-Rumours of England Salonika, Ireland. Gallipoli, Palestine &amp; Somme.</p>
<p>Oct. 16th<br/>Departure of first section of our Second Division &ndash; now the time has arrived we are very loath to leave our home in Poperinghe &ndash; Nothing happened of importance.</p>
<p>Oct. 17th<br/>Day of Preparation &ndash; Abandonment of Shorts.</p>
<p>Oct. 18th.<br/>We take to the road once more and in departing Poperinghe we leave many kind friends &amp; the nearest approach of Home or Home Living, since leaving Australia &ndash; Only pleasant memories shall remain &ndash; "Belgium we salute you." Arrival at Steenworde where we are billeted.</p>
