<p>Lt Cochrane had been discovered walking around the deck this morning quite oblivious of the fact that a large notice had been pinned to his coat tail bearing the inscription SUNDAY SERVICE.  It was no wonder then that people began to get <strike>a bit</strike> uneasy this afternoon.  After a while they began to feel their coat tails &amp; Major Steele had the good luck to find the mirth provoking notice firmly fixed in his stern sheets.</p>
