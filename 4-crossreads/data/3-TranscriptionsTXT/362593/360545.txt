
Friday 26 April 1918Flew over to Junction Station with E. Headlam in our 2E to see John Webster who is with No. 14 Sqdn. A.F.C.  In air 18 m.John looks well but much knocked about by bomb.
Saturday 27 April 1918H.A. [Hun Aircraft] patroll over the Jordan.  Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 3 h. 10 m., no excitement.
