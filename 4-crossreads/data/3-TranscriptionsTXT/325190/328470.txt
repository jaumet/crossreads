 Saw Bob Fry with whom I used to carry on the Peninsula, who is now a Sergeant D.C.M. &amp; also M.M.  He referred to me at once as the Bishop of London &amp; seemed pleased to see me.  Jimmy Garland seemed less free &amp; easy as W.O. but he also was pleased. The bearers were just starting for the line at Belgian Battery when we arrived, but we had time to chat to some of them.  They were going up into a stunt.
