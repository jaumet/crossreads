<p>Started to rain last night &amp; this morning it is raining heavily &amp; looks rotten outside.</p>
<p>We were issued with new box respirators this morning &amp; every one had to go through the gas test again, but there were very few defective helmets among them &amp; now we have to hand all our P.H.G. helmets in</p>
<p>19th.   Yesterday evening all the N.C.O's of the Brigade had to meet over at the 3rd Battalion for a lecture on gas &amp; the new helmets after waiting for nearly an hour in the mud, we had to go home for the lecturer never turned up</p>
