 1917 July 
 5 Thursday 
 Changed trains &amp; went on to Turin where we arrived at 3 &amp; remained till 3.10. Had charming afternoon tea of fruits &amp; tea at Hotel Rome &amp; took up with Dr McLaren of R. Army Medical Corps. Got seats but not sleepers for Rome. Trainned &amp; walked around Turin to the amazement of beholders &amp; took some things with us to eat on train. Saw the Alps. Very warm. Got a little rest during the night
