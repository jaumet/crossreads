<p>Jan.12th<br/>Receive Birthday Parcel from Home &ndash; "Mystery &amp; Imagination" &ndash; Again meet Mr. Jack Kirby &ndash; Votes granted to the Women of England.</p>
<p>Jan.13th<br/>My 21st Birthday &ndash; Genuine congratulations &amp; Good wishes from my Pals &ndash; A special Dinner for the occasion (Soup, chicken, ham, vegetables, fruit salad, ginger etc.) &ndash;<br/>Letters containing Birthday greetings from Australia arrive right on time &ndash; A glorious day &ndash;<br/>Correspondence</p>
