<p>Thursday 5 June 1919<br/>Garage all day. Bill came home with me &amp; we went to Marie's for tea. Stayed there till 9. Came home &amp; wrote &amp; read. Also did a bit of packing.</p>
<p>Friday 6 June 1919<br/>Packed. Squared up. Took my trunk &amp; valise to Pritchard Place. Went to Horseferry Rd for &frac12; fare vouchers. Got 3rd class voucher &amp; ticket. The old lady gave me a sponge cake for the train. Went early to the train &amp; got a corner seat. Had 2 naval boys &amp; 3 ladies in the compartment. Wasn't comfy tho. Thought I'd be found out. Wasn't tho &amp; arrived Glasgow OK.</p>
