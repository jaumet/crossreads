<p>First impressions of camp good &ndash; given good tea &amp; temporary shakedown in hut.</p>
<p>Wednesday   Following morning were allocated work in Hut 83 &ndash; a ward &ndash; after medical inspection etc. etc.<br/>I, of course, explained for what I was sent down, &amp; Sgt. said I should in due course be sent into dispensary.<br/>Subsequently found out that Cpl dispenser was to be drafted with permanently unfits for Australia on Sunday &amp; I was to take his place<br/>(Positively refuse to conjecture on whether I shall get promotion</p>
