<p>our divisions were sent one by one down south to the scene of our past glorious successes to help stem the hun tide - the old 1st Division were the last to leave the line to go down &amp; no sooner there than what-oh! back again at the double detrained at a siding &amp; told to defend a certain big town - it wasnt certain where our line was at the time - by a strange co-incidence the sector given my company lay near a village "in fact I had to march through it" where we spent a happy month resting last</p>
