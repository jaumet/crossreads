 December 1  st  &nbsp;&nbsp;Yes Bailleul at last - I went into an egg &amp; chip joint to get warm &amp; have breakfast &amp; then went to Marie Louise&#39;s joint as I expected she would know the whereabouts of the section. I met some of the boys at her place on the way to Brulooge Railhead for supplies, so I stayed in the warm till they got back again at 3.P.M. my buss with a broken spring - Our park is just outside Bailleul on the Hazebrouck road parked with 5  th  &nbsp;D.S.C. &nbsp;I feel very sorry to be back again -
