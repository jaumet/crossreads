 Wednesday Oct 17th 
 Westham : wet : fell in for roll call before &amp; after dinner : walked to Weymouth after tea &amp; bought Xmas cards &amp; also got my films &amp; prints : not too bad 
 Thursday Oct 18th 
 Westham : better day : fell in for roll call before &amp; after dinner : sent a Xmas card to all the family, all at Lithgow, Muir, Moyle, &amp; Aunt Myra &amp; Grandma : Ern Beale is over on leave &amp; looked me up : the "boys" have had a bad time &amp; the names of those who are dead shocked me : went to his peoples place &amp; had a pleasant talk
