
Thursday Aug 16thCamels & equipment handed in today. Moving on SaturdayFriday Aug 17Packing up leaving early tomorrow.Saturday Aug 18Reveille 3.30 AM. Told off for baggage Guard stations. Main body left at 7pm. I. J.B. & Cpl M leaving by luggage train at midnight. We placed all gear in truck sealed it & spent a wild day in Cairo. Who knows we may not see it again. A small half starved little dog followed us about all day so we took him on the strength & took him with us.
