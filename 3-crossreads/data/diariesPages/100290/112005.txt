1 September 1916
Friday
Nearly missed parade tho? sleeping in barn. Dress in record time. Breakfast move off in forenoon. 3rd Field take our "possie". Arrive at Remi Siding take over "Rest Station" a good "possie" so outward seeming. Baths, washing places, mess room, library "tres bon". 4 Casualty Clearing hospitals adjacent. also Y.M.C.A Tent only 1 mile from Poperinge. Live in tent once more. Yankee sanitary method. Each man his own carrier. ogh!
