<p>52 Lemnos island</p>
<p>troops are coming on the to island every day from Gallipoli&nbsp; They are giving up the Suvla Bay and Anzacs fronts. I dont know if they will do the Same at Cape Helles or not but they will I believe bring them all away from the whole front on the peninsula. While I was there this month it snowed and it Knows how to Snow here when it Starts.
