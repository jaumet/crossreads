<p>Sunday 17 June 1917<br/>Hot &amp; windy quiet day, church parade late did not go packed up for move onto beach.  Wrote No. [see images for possible Arabic number for 7].</p>
<p>Monday 18 June 1917<br/>Left Razar for the beach at 7.30 arrived early &amp; put up lines &amp; took horses for swim.  On picquet at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 19 June 1917<br/>Cleaning up in morning, swim in afternoon.</p>
<p>Wednesday 20 June 1917<br/>Cleaning up in morning &amp; swim in afternoon.</p>
<p>Thursday 21 June 1917<br/>Inspection by G.O.C. had great preparations.  All seemed to be quite satisfactory.  Concert at Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Friday 22 June 1917<br/>Quiet day, feeling a bit off after being caught in undertow.  Debate in Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Saturday 23 June 1917<br/>Concert by the Leisk Battery, quiet day, plenty swimming.</p>
