 1916 May 23 Tuesday 
 Another inspection parade at 9.30 a.m. address by camp commandant on "Playing the Game" Washing parade in  fore noon.  Route march to Etaples &amp; back 6 miles. Etaples a dirty hole, fishing village, inhabitants poor, common &amp; ugly. Squalid &amp; filthy streets stink.  Stroll round after tea. meet &amp; yarn[indecipherable] to Gog Aldous Alexander, Piper &amp; Elliot. The latter to be boarded for medically unfit.
