<p>Saturday 18 November 1916<br/>Stood to arms at 5 a.m.  Greased saddle etc.  Big mail in but I only got one letter.<br/>Went to Dr. about boil on chin which he lanced.</p>
<p>Sunday 19 November 1916<br/>On telephone from 10 till 2 p.m.  In afternoon did some washing.</p>
