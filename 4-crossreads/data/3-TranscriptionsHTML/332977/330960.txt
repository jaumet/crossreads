<p>Battleship plays the National Anthem as we pull out to stream</p>
<p>March 3rd<br/>Passing Athens conditions rotten, cannot even get drinking water, nothing to wash our mess tins with, no forks plates or mugs issued us</p>
<p>March 4th<br/>Arrived Lemnos Island, beautiful harbour scores of Warships, transports, Destroyers and submarines,</p>
<p>March 5<br/>Lying in Harbour awaiting orders</p>
<p>6th<br/>we row Queenslanders ashore to make room</p>
<p>7th<br/>Admiral of the fleet comes aboard<br/>We form a boat crew, to row men ashore</p>
<p>8th<br/>Rowed 10 men ashore back in evening, buy nick nacks, good feed</p>
<p>9th<br/>Long route march ashore, pulled ashore in boats, have a good day<br/>10th<br/>great rejoicing cant go ashore to rough</p>
<p>11th<br/>On prison guard, Warships &amp; Transports arrive</p>
<p>12th<br/>French General visit our ships</p>
<p>13th<br/>Rowing men ashore, 1&frac34; hrs to do a trip, to rough to handle boats</p>
