<p>6/3/16<br/>I was made Lance Cpl of C companys signallers</p>
<p>10/3/16<br/>We left Serapium East and marched back to the canal we where issued with new rifles and new ammunition</p>
<p>10/3/16<br/>Camped on the canal</p>
<p>26/3/16<br/>We embarked on train for Alexanderia and boarded SS Ballarat</p>
<p>27/3/16<br/>We sailed for Marcails all the boys where glad to git over there</p>
