<p>A free afternoon spent at the Garrick Theatre where Madge Pitheradge, Arthur Wontner &amp; Hugh Buckler are playing in "Pigeon Post" (a War Drama) "Good acting in an ordinary play &ndash; We are getting great Houses at Wimbledon &ndash; very busy these days &ndash; Large Australian Mail (including 20 letters &amp; two parcels) comes to hand.<br/>April 28th<br/>Mr. Wilfred Inman &ndash; Local Paper gives the "Coves" a great notice.</p>
