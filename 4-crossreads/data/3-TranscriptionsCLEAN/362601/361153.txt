  
 2 February, Sunday 
 F & ice I rush off to P. House in new ambulance & walk from cross roads. Meet Aubrey Hickson & go to No 2 for Tea play Bridge until car arrives. Hurry down to YWCA dirty unshaven. Stay until Supper time & rush home to shave & write. Music & singing very good & 40 miles & home from P. House. 
 Wrote MRL 
 3 February, Monday 
 F E. goes to C.O. & has a long yarn about ourselves. I stay on office & room for while others go up to mess for dancing lesson. We muck up generally, & Dick gets a bang on the forehead. I take another fellows girl Home while he looks on. Nuryance[?] & long yarn in the flat on my return.
