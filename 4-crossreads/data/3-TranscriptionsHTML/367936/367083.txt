<p>Sunday 10 December 1916<br/>Got back from outpost to find 35 letters waiting for me, all been to 14 A.G.H.<br/>Church parade.  Read letters most of day.<br/>Taube over in afternoon dropped some bombs somewhere but no damage.</p>
<p>Monday 11 December 1916<br/>On telephone from 6 till 10 a.m. then signalling class rest of day.<br/>Taube over at dinner time dropped three more bombs.<br/>On telephone from 10 till 2 at night.  Wrote letters.</p>
