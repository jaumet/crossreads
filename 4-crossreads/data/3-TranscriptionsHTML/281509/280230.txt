<p>7 January 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Cooler, Advanced stretcher drill in afternoon. Loading wagons.  New dixie dishes.</p>
<p>Rob &amp; self attend Capt. Willis' Lecture in "C" ward 7 pm.  Enemata.</p>
<p>Night ward duties per usual.</p>
<p>Garden started in front of ward. <br/>Tony Waights in charge. To be made corporal.</p>
<p>[Page 8 January P.O. Q from camp 6 am train, arrive Hewlett's for breakfast --- strike McTavishes for dinner. Came on into town en route for Watson's Bay &amp; Deans. Find 3 girls at home.  Eddie &amp; Charlie also.  After tea go for walk on Cliffs.  Search lights ---Home &amp; sing.  Leave by 9.30 pm boat.  Arrive back camp with out incident at 11.30 pm.</p>
