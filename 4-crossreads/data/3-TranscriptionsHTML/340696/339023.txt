<p>121<br/>26-12-17. Arrived home safely last night after a most enjoyable day everywhere was covered in snow a typical Xmas.</p>
<p>1-1-18. Rather a quiet day still situated near Peronne.</p>
<p>2-1-18. Leave to-day with Horace, Charlie Ferguson Cridland for 8 days leave to Paris so great is the joy.</p>
<p>13-1-18  Arrived back to find the Div. near Reninghelst in huts &amp; by jove its cold snow everywhere. Had a wonderful time in Paris stayed at the Hotel MONTANA Avenue de L'Opera near Place de L'Opera a lovely place. Saw Hamlet as a grand opera it was great the building is gorgeous. No time to detail my trip don't think I shall ever forget the beautiful City, the <strike>City of my dreams</strike>.</p>
<p>30-1-18 Spent the day in handing over my duties at T.H.Q. to [Coog?].</p>
