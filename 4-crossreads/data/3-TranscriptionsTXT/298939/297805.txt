Am advanced in a good many ways. &ndash; a little firmer in character &ndash; less prodigal with money &ndash; more inclined to do any work at hand &ndash; and happierGreat lapses from time to time, but am fighting as hard as I ever fought before to once  &amp; for all make something of myself.  The glimpses I get from time to time of what I might become if I persevere make the fight well worth while.
Yesterday we attended a brigade battle manoeuvreReveille 5 AM, moved off 6.15 &amp; picked up 3 battalions marching about 3 miles from here.
