At 10 I had enough so retired to rest. 13th November Wednesday  Up at 9 oclock & had breakfast in a cafe close by writing a letter home at the same time. Did the town thoroughly per tram & foot it being very busy & prosperous looking Had a midget photo taken. After a good lunch visited a famous church, Notre Dame, on a high rock overlooking the city, reached by funnicular railway. Here again the view was magnificent the city lying beneath me, the sea being on my left & the surrounding hills to the right. The Cathedral & curious punt could plainly be seen.
