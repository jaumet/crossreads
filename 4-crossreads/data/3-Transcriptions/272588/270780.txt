<p>Friday 4 October<br/>At Bolougne in rest camp after a decent days journey, En route for my leave to England.  Quite a decent day &amp; evening in Bolougne.  Go to Calais to-morrow &amp; embark there.</p>
<p>Saturday 5 October<br/>Crossing Calais to Dover rough. Dover to Victoria St. &amp; get a glimpse of Canterbury Cathedral. Charing Cross Hotel.  Accommodation is hard to get, the Strand Palace &amp; Hassells Hotels being crowded.</p>
