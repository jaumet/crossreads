
7/10/16  Saturday
Dull day - On Picquet. Fritz hopped the parapet at Levantie & Armentiers - he got particular hell in both places - Very few casualtys came in.
8/10/16  Sunday
Dull day - Went out to Rifle Villa about 9 a.m. Lively trench warfare - Had a few calls.
9/10/16  Monday
Drizzling rain. Heavy bombardment from both sides - Got a letter from M. Single. Fritz very busy with deadly minniewurzen, he got some M.G. fellows - one fellow named Jim Harsthorn, although badly injured injured, was a model patient, he was actually singing & joking - as he said to keep himself awake. Les Bowden took on Groom to A.D.M.S.
10/10/16  Tuesday
Nice day - A few calls during the night - Fritz putting over more minnies than shells on supports - Wrote to Frank, Anne & M. Single.
