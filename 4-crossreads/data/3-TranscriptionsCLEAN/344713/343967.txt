Fri. Nov. 9th: -Relieved by 1st Bn. & went to Anzac House on Anzac Ridge, a concrete "Fritz" observation point & stayed night.S.M. Bock & Corp Gillam of "D" Coy were "blown-out" at back of the "House" & Corp Searle badly wounded.
Sat. Nov. 10th.Relieved here by "Tommies" & went back to old "pill-box" on Westhoek ridge & stayed night.
Sun. No. 11th: -Left Westhoek & passed through Ypres to "Vancouver Camp" near Vlamertinghe.This is the finish of our part in the 3rd Battle of Ypres.We are starting tomorrow on a 3 day's march back "somewhere".
Tues. Nov. 13th.Left "Vancouver Camp" by motor - Bn. marched - & passed through Poperinghe, Watou-Abeele, Boeschepe & Berthen & billeted for night outside of Shaexken.
