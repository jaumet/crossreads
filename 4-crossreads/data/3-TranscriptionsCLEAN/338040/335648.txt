  
 in marching order 10.30am Destination Unknown- pass through Hazebrouck and other French villages- lunch on the road- march proving strenuous to many, encamped at 3pm in a barn at village of Ottersheene (11 miles  31/2 hours) April 5th March resumed in Marching Order at 10.30am suitable weather but bad roads pass through Strazeele, Steinberg(?) etc. mainly aeroplanes visible- signs German destruction in village.
