Yesterday a chap fell from the deck right through the hatch & down to the steps of the lower deck it's a wonder how he never broke his neck or some bones for it is a very nasty tumble he was knocked unconscious & they carried him away to hospital.
While we were having tea this evening a Sgt who is attached to the Steward of this boat to help with the rations came down & wanted to know which sort of fruit we preferred, for he said that a lot of fruit was going to be bought at Port Said for the troops & the steward wished to know what we would like, the steward suggested dates & figs but this did in no way agree with our tastes & ideas we promptly turned this down & voted
