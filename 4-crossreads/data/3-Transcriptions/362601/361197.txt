<p>1 May, Thursday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I am up [?]times &amp; very hungry. I make a hard confession quite easily. M &amp; I go for a walk. We clamber over the hedge &amp; have to get over the water ditch. We sit by the fire &amp; talk all evening. I do ironing.</p>
<p>2 May Friday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Hot shower in afternoon. I get up early &amp; light fire etc. I print P.Cto M. do ironing &amp; Auntie &amp; I go for a walk all around by back road. We get caught in the rains. I sit by the fire &amp; write. I have my nails manicured &amp; thank manicurist.</p>
<p>Wrote Dick &amp; D. Op</p>
