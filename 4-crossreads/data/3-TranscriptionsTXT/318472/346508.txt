
10 October 1918ThursdayLeft Sydney by the 4.45 P.M. & got to Nowra about 9 P.M. where we were given a welcome by the Welcome Com: at the Station and on reaching Nowra in the Motor was given a Civic welcome by the Mayor. Got to Sydney from Queanbeyan about 6 A.M. & went out to Waverly where we stayed till afternoon. Good News continues from all Fronts
1918 October 11FridaySlept most of the day at least we didn't do anything of importance.
