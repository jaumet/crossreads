<p>Decembre 20<br/>Had a trip into Boulogne with C.Q.M.S. for stores for our Sgts Mess. Rode into Boulogue on bikes &amp; found it a very hilly road indeed. Still very cold.</p>
<p>Decembre 21<br/>Coy training in the morning &amp; the afternoon was devoted to sport. Still freezing heavily.</p>
