<p>Hope Mrs Copp is quite well again. Trust Charlie is safe  &amp;  well,.</p>
<p>Two of the sisters here have had word this week that a brother has been killed. It is awfully sad for the,   &amp;  oh, so many have been wounded &ndash; one is very very anxious for news of friends in firing-line.</p>
<p>Eddy seems to be very happy at school   &amp;  it will make  him stronger.</p>
