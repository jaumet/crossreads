<p>May, 1915<br/>Thursday 27<br/>My experience of working men teaches me when they cannot stand the strain of too much domineering in a Hot climate &ndash; Here they are working at Concert Pitch all thro' the day for 1000s of years the people of Egypt found they could not do this thing &ndash; warm English clothing most unsuitable.</p>
