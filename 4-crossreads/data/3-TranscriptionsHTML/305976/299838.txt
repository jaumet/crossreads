<p>Wednesday Feb 21<br/>Raining like <strike>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </strike><br/>Woke up in water.  Sham fight on today. Plenty of fun<br/>Thursday Feb 22<br/>Final leave through. Left for Sydney 6pm. Had good trip over<br/>Friday Feb 23<br/>Arrived Sydney 12 noon<br/>Saw Mother, Fred &amp; Glad again. How pleased they were to see me. I really think those who love us feel it more than we do on parting. Spent  quite evening at Home &amp; enjoyed it.<br/>Saturday Feb 24<br/>Visited Garrison Hospital in morning surprised to hear poor old Jack Smith dead. Buried today, went to <strike>funo</strike> funeral</p>
