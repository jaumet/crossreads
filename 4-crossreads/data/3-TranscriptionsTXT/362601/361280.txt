 11 October, Saturday 
 Dad &amp; I dine at B. Go to Ida's place. Bert gives us some Yiddisher. We get Home at 10 pm. Are upset by drunks on boat. 
 12 October, Sunday 
 I go to N. Steyne for 2 dips. It is lovely &amp; I get very sunburnt. Go to Manly afternoon. Swim again. Dine with E &amp; E. Home at 7.30 pm. 
 Wrote all night.
