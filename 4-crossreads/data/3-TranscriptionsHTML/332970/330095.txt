There seems to be a general feeling abroad that <strike>the</strike> now we are embarked on the real thing minor discomforts don't count. On board our transport are three companies of the 13th, B, C &amp; D and the 7th Army Service Corps. Of course it is an absolute impossibility under the circumstances to keep clean, yet the Colonel complains that the men are not turning out spick &amp; span. Yesterday each man was served with 200 rounds of ammunition, and we are ready for anything.</p>
