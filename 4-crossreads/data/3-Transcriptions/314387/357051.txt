<p>Saturday 6th<br/>Usual drill in morning afternoon went to Bailleul<br/>Sunday 7th<br/>Did nothing wet miserable day<br/>Monday 8th<br/>Marched out of camp to near Poperilnghe then got into Motor Lorries and went <strike>to</strike> close to Ypres through Vlameringhe got out of lorries and marched over to Huts.<br/>Tuesday 9th<br/>Left camp about eight</p>
