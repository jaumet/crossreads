<p>Have a new Hut leader Capt. Pike.  We now get 5 francs per week as pay.</p>
<p>Tuesday 17</p>
<p>Attended Bookkeeping Class from 10 to 11 A.M.  My day off went for a walk with Martin (he has been helping in the Library lately) through Montevellen had several Vermecassies in a Boozer a nice afternoon except for a shower or so.  Martin is a decent Chap a bushie like myself</p>
