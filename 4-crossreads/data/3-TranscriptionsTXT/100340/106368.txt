I got a few souveniers this time, four different decoration ribbons, 20, 5, 2  &amp; 1 mark notes watch, ring  &amp; various other articles,  &amp; some of the filthiest post cards imaginable, but I soon gave them away. Charlie Lewington wishes to be remembered to you  &amp; Sis. Give my kindest regards to all your folk  &amp; fondest love to Noni, Sis &amp; self from your brother Hal. 15526 Pte F H Vick
