<p>4th August</p>
<p>Pte. Guilliaume Casimir W. has come to me as my batman.</p>
<p>Bill, as he is called, is a Frenchman with a varied and extremely interesting career.  Born at &hellip; in 1862 he was educated at <strike>school</strike> and given every opportunity to study by a very wealthy and indulgent parent of whom he still cherishes affectionate memories.  But Bill early showed a roving disposition.  When quite a young man he entered the French Army where he soon rose to Commissioned Rank.  He was of course too young to take part in the war of</p>
