<p>July 1917<br/>nice tea &amp; cakes. Examined the Pier in the evening &amp; came home early</p>
<p>Monday 23<br/>Marched up town. Went around gardens very small &amp; nothing out of the way to look at. Went through Museum. Fine collection of animals minerals etc <strike>fo</strike> did not take time to examine fully.</p>
<p>Plaster figures of Native Bushman are near lifelike Took Train to Newland &amp; went over Groote Schuure in afternoon. Fine massive old house nice grounds did not go into house Good collection of animals running in large enclosures except the caged ones Went to the Memorial</p>
