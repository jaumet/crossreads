 Some of the men were watching an officer taking the soundings.  One of them said "I see they are still looking for mines". Since we left Capetown no lights are shown of a night.  On the troopdeck no lights are lit excepting 4 bulb eyes and these are obscured with a towel. A whistle has taken the place of the bugle for sounding "Reveille".
