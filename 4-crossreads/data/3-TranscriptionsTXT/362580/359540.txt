 We finished long before them &ndash; had another stroll &amp; then returned to duty at 9 p.m.  Feeling very happy &amp; contented.  He stayed all night but left early next morn.  Most likely I shall see him for a few moments on Tel-el-Kebir Station on our way to Cairo. 
 Am getting another cotton crepe dress, it is fearfully &amp; hard to find dressmakers.
