The Melbourne has now joined up the second light Cruiser Squadron which consists besides Melbourne of Southhampton, Dublin & Birmingham. H.M.S. Southampton is our flagship under Admiral Goodenough. The second light cruiser squadron is attached to Admiral Beatty's Battle Cruiser fleet. 
 22 Oct 16 Anchored at Rosyth. Admiral Goodenough came aboard & spoke to ships company. 
 23 Oct 16 Anchored at Rosyth. Admiral Packingham of H.M.A.S. Australia, & Admiral of the Australian Navy in home waters, came aboard today & spoke to ships company.
