<p>26-3-17<br/>Mon 26th<br/>The attack on Gaza commenced this morning &amp; all day we have been able to hear a continual rumble from the warboats shelling the enemy positions &amp; a continual stream of aeroplanes going out loaded with bombs &amp; coming back empty has been Kept up, all the mounted troops in the front line are at it besides the infantry, there are rumors of a good number of prisoners taken but get no official news through, tonight a message came to Bde Headqrs asking how many men we could raise as escort to prisoners, the reply was 17 officers &amp; 250 men; Headquarters played a team from</p>
