<p>March 1915<br/>&amp; went into Cairo with  Jean McCarthy in the afternoon.  Had tea at York House &amp; came back to camp fairly early as I was feeling tired.</p>
<p>Monday 6th  First day instruction.  I shall never forget it.  2 hours solid marching &amp; platoon drill in the morning &amp; I was breaking in a pair of new boots.  It was slightly better after breakfast in the musketry class &amp; we had a fairly solid afternoon at rifle exercises &amp; it was very hot too.</p>
<p>Tuesday 7th  We were given 2 hours company drill in the morning.  More musketry instruction &amp; a lecture on discipline.  In the afternoon usual training &amp; more musketry instruction.  The day was fairly warm.  It is a big cram here &amp; you have to concentrate your mind on the work if you dont want to be left behind</p>
