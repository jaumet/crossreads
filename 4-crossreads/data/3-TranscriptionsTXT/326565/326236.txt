 Friday 5/11/15 Caught 8 am tram &amp; met other boys at railway.  Caught 9.30 am train for Como.  Hired boats &amp; rowed up river about 5 miles, returned to Como in time to catch 5 pm train.  At night went Bondi Junction 
 Saturday 6/11/15 Remained home during morning.  During afternoon played tennis, rather out of practice, but games very enjoyable.  At night went to farewell party given to J Martin by his parents. 
 Sunday 7/11/15 Remained home during morning.  Met friend 2.40 pm.  Caught Lane
