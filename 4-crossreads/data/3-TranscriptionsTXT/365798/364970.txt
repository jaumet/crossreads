 We did not have the promised storm, but instead the wind and sea went down &amp; the whole trip to Colombo was as calm as could be. On crossing the Line, of course there were the usual celebrations, &amp; all the Officers had to go through the mill. Some of the lads got a bit of their own back then. 
 No leave was granted in Colombo, though [indecipherable] &amp; myself managed to get one whole day ashore. We had a real good time &amp; saw everything there was to be seen. It was quite a new experience to ride in a "rickshaw" &amp; the coolies who pull them seem tireless. Dined at the G.O.H. a beautiful Hotel, better than any we have in Sydney.
