<p>At our own Camp but it is very easy   &amp;  I'll have a good rest.</p>
<p>We are away from all our own soldiers, they are all on the move too.</p>
<p>Yesterday afternoon (Friday) to my delight some officers motored from Bapaume to see me. It is a Battalion that thinks something of this  <u>Old Bird</u>   &amp;  I'm great pals with them. I get a good deal of teasing about one of them but we are only pals, though he is very nice but</p>
