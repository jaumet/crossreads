<p>Resting headquarters),  to a little village just off the main road to Estaples, called Cormont.  Here, we discovered the 3rd, &amp; very shortly after my arrival, (nearly two years after I left them) we were paid received mail &amp; a drop of tea; showing how they look after the boys.</p>
<p>Nov 24th<br/>Since I have been with the boys I have got to know some of the new ones, &amp; had good yarns with the old ones.<br/>We are billeted in an old farm building in a hay loft over the main denuded rooms, &amp; are fairly comfortable.  We rise at 6.45 for parade at 7, march a short distance &amp; then have breakfast.<br/>There are parades at 9 &amp; 2</p>
