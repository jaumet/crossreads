<p>Then there seems to be a chance that we shall be withdrawn from here and shall go to Europe. That is the current rumour,  &amp;  some hardy liars (I think) even declare that the 4th Regt has already gone.<br/>Then we shall have no more of these unpleasant stunts, the gentle reader has (probably) no idea how unpleasant it is to go riding on  &amp;  on over endless sand using all one's muscular energy in a futile attempt to keep ones eyes open.<br/>June 17th<br/>We have had very dull days lately nothing at all has happened, no aeroplanes, not even a rumour</p>
