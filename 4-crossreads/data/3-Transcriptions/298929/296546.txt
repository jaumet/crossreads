<p>23rd Aug  Well, I have been in the big push but owing to a G.S. Waggon running over my foot nr Bray I find myself in 2nd C.G.H. at Triport wondering if I will get to Blighty.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    Marked for Blighty</p>
<p>31 Aug  Left Triport for Le Havre.  S.S. "Grantilly Castle" to Southhampton</p>
<p>1st Sep.  Birmingham University, 1st Southern Genl Hosp.</p>
<p>4th  Transferred to Rugby V.A.D. hospital</p>
