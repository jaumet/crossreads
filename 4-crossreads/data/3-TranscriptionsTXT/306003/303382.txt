 5-6-16 Mon 5th This morning at 4 oclock A Troop went to Hill 110 &amp; I also went &amp; Les &amp; Reidy had to work telephone station from above water troughs, had a good bit of work to do  this morning as I had 3 stations going pretty regular &amp; came in at one oclock;  Les &amp; Reid are on till dark;  Bob Butter was transferred from the 6th to this Regt. &amp; arrived today;  Divisional Head Quarters also came here today &amp; a fatigue party from this Regt had to go &amp; pitch their tents; Received an account of battle in North Sea today saying 14 English &amp; 13
