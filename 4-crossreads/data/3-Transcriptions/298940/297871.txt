<p>walking &amp; one stretcher case: both wounded on outpost duty. It was pitch dark, except for the fitful illumination of star shells &amp; gun flashes, which really left us worse off than before, for when they had gone all was blacker than ever. As our heads stood out in silhouette above the saps in the ghastly star shell  light, one felt that one was by far too conspicuous. Part of our track to the next relay is under direct observation from Fritz, yet he does not trouble us much.<br/>Well we started. - Mick Stafford. - rough yet</p>
