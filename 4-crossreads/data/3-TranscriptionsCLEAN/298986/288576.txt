  
 Spent most of the day getting settled in K. Co.  Was asked out by a lady in the Y.M. to see an ancient Church but could not manage it. 
 Thursday 3rd 
 Saw Dr. am moveing again tomorrow.  Marched to Tivoli, Rouen, good veriaty show. 
 Friday 4th 
 Left per ambulance at 9 for Rouen entrained at 11 reached Hafleur - 5 miles from Le Havre - about 15 marched to Con. Camp 1 mile.  Had dinner also ate more than was good for me on the train.  Have a bow Hut
