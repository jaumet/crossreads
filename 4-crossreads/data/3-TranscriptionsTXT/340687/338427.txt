 May, 1917. Thursday 3 Fine day but unpleasant dry wind. Up at 8 and cooked breakfast and cleaned up and ran engine in morning. Slightly better &amp; pumped tank about 1 ft from top. Officer round yesterday &amp; intends to close this point. Pumped again in afternoon and only would go for about &frac12; an hour &amp; also cleaned carburettor. Went to pictures. Change of programme in evening &ndash; Very good. Read some surveying before bed.
