He never improves his system of farming & when he has done what he needs must, he rests, smokes & gossips & trusts that Allah will do the remainder of his work for him.  They are a peaceful folk & kindly to their neighbours.The next large body are the Copts undoubtedly the most direct descendants of the ancient Egyptians - in 1914 there were 700,000 of these.  They mostly live in the big towns & are engaged in the more refined handicrafts - embroiderers, weavers, goldsmiths, cabinet makers, etc.
