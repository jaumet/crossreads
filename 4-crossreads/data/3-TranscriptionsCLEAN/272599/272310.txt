  
 Feb.  Saturday 10 1917 
 D.D.M.S & Col. Williams up at 6.30 a m. on way to inspect all aid posts & dressing stations in anticipation of an offensive shortly.  Satisfied with things here.   A raid is anticipated tonight by our side. Extra squads sent up & all preparations to start ready.  Ridgway & Brown with 2 new men sent up to front line for night.
