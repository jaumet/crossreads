<p>Are closed on Sunday. We had to be content with cakes and soft drinks both for lunch and tea. In the afternoon we took a bus to Hampstead Heath a very pretty suburb and where the wealthies class reside.  We returned to Charing Cross by train, meeting a lady and her daughter who gave us their address and asked us to be sure and call on them anytime we were in London.
