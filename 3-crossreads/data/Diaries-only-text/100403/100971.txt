February 1919
16th. Leave Paris at 7.30 per Brussels express via Amiens Arras, St Pol Lille, arriving Brussels at 12.15 in night.
17th. Morning at Galleries in Brussels leave per 2 p.m. for Charleroi, arriving at unit at 8.30 p.m.
18th. Back at Biesmes to find I had missed a draft for Australia which left on 12th. However am well up on the next, which rumour has will leave on 22nd.
