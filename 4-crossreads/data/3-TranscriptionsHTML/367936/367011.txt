<p>Wednesday 19 July 1916<br/>Left Ras-el-tin, caught train at Alexandria for Benha.  Had to wait 3 hours at Benha then came on to Tel-el-Kebir.<br/>Arrived in Camp &amp; got back with my mates.</p>
<p>Thursday 20 July 1916<br/>Reported to Dr. for inspection, O.K.<br/>Allotted a horse.<br/>Received 30 letters from Australia.<br/>Father has bought a car.<br/>Letters (1) from Father &amp; (2) Mother.  No. (2) Mrs. Nicholl.</p>
