<p>W.T.Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship: Encounter</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship: Flag</p>
<p>Date: 14 Sep</p>
<p>Time of Despatch or Receipt: 7.40 am</p>
<p>In view of message from Colonel Watson your action in shelling is fully approved.</p>
<p>If required your maxim may be left on shore tonight. I may require Encounter this evening or tomorrow morning</p>
