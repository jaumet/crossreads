<p>Monday 11/2/18&nbsp;&nbsp; Came back to Hotel about noon&nbsp; went to Picture Show- Queen Alexandra present- Wrote Doff</p>
<p>Tuesday 12/2/18. Went to see &quot;When Knights were bold&quot;. Received letter from Doff</p>
<p>Wednesday 13/12/18. Received proofs of photo- not much good&nbsp; met Jack Playfair had lunch &amp; went to &quot;A Little Bit of Fluff&quot; with him</p>
<p>Thursday 14/12/18&nbsp; Going back tomorrow&nbsp; leaving this book with Mrs Bamford tonight</p>
