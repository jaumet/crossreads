 Captain McNabb of 'A' Coy. Q.M.S. Macdonald and two other N.C.O.s all 1914 men are to leave for "Aussie" tomorrow morning. 
 Capt. McNabb was carried home on the last night of his stay with the 54th Bn. well oiled and properly stunned after making a futile effort to pummel the old Doc's head and  losing  winning 12 francs at poker.
