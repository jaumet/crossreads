 18/5/16 A little cooler to-day no hot wind. Rode up to YMCA hut on orderlies' horse to get a couple of tins of peaches to give the lads a return treat for cocoa last night. 
 Just before tea the "Mooltan" went through & looked great. There ought to be more mail in a day or so. 
 Received a few papers yesterday amongst them was a Bulletin from Ruth with note from Cecil. 
 The lads came across & we had quite a party on the chaff stacks of the Depot (2 tins peaches & tin of milk). 
 19/5/16. Did some washing this morning in the same water
