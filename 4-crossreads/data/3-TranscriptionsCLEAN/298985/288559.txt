We unloaded at Rosile & had a Mule to draw each Tank to the front line about 3 Kilos One Mule bolted & I believe broke his tank another lost a wheel & had to be left  I was with No 1 & we got through allright  There were 6 all told. Don't know how the others got on. Pulled ours up past our outposts & came back to a bit of a trench to wait for our barrage. Rain started about 11 & we were in it untill morning.
