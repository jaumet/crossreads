<p>June 1918<br/>Friday 21</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather still raining and a very driving mist with a cold<br/>breeze.<br/>Went up to Bty with a horse for Smithy.<br/>Left section pulled out at 9 pm and we eventually arrived<br/>at a small village on the outskirts of Amiens, where we<br/>billeted for the night.<br/>Smithy and Gun slightly inebriated under the flue of Vin<br/>rouge et Vin Blanc.   <br/>Still enroute at midnight.</p>
