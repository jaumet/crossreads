We can see the fleet (away on the horizon) that left Colombo first; there are only ten boats here, & as far as we can see, only the Japanese cruiser escorting us, but probably there are some others out of sight scouting.I believe we have the the prisoners of the "Emdem" on board the "Omrah"A dinner time to-day the Reg.
