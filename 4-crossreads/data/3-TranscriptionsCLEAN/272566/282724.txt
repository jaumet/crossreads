 Saturday 8 December 
 Fine, but roads very greasy.  Cold outside. Run to A.D.M.S. Dranoutre thence to 1st Anzac Corps at Flete. Night casualty wire at 6 p.m. Our unit today played the 14th Fld. Ambce, at Locre - Rugby League.  Resulted in a win for us as usual  20 to Nil. Lt. Col. Makin reported back from sick leave to England.  He is our new O.C. & not a bad sort.
