 MONDAY, JULY 7, 1919. 
 Fine & warm. Drove down to Boundary 
 well with old Cy & Tickle & I were 
 trenching there till 3 pm. Then we 
 went up to Mountain & shot some 
 parrots & laid a trail for foxes. Percy 
 George came out in his car & brought 
 my things. Mum & Jo went over 
 to Coomoo Coomoo in the car.
