
<p>273</p>
<p>FEBRUARY 1917</p>
<p>8 THURSDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; Sunny. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness all morning &amp; Bill</p>
<p>Hunter &amp; self went for a ride</p>
<p>through Neippe in afternoon</p>
<p>The place has been knocked</p>
<p>about more than Armenti&egrave;res</p>
<p>&amp; is almost deserted.</p>
<p>There was a fair bombardment</p>
<p>going on all day.</p>
