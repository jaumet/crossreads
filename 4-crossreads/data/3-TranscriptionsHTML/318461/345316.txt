<p>to Nell, Jack, Carol, Tony, Syd. Arthur, Jean.<br/>Raining again makes it miserable went this afternoon 3/- Trip on drag Westminister Abbey a Marvellous Place. Buckingham Palace mostly seeing Horses stables &amp; the state coach weighs 4 &frac12; tons &amp; a marvellous carriage, each horses harness costs anything between 100 &amp; &pound;200. No wonder there's taxes etc.<br/>The Kings state carriage is simply all gilt &amp; is magnifiacent &amp; his Horses are just <u>it</u>.<br/>Going to Theatre tonight<br/>rang up Newton go to see him tomorrow at 11 am 60 Belsize Park. (Bus 24.)</p>
<p>Tuesday 31/7/17.<br/>went out to see Newton at Belsize Park he was not there saw Mrs Moore had a chat &amp; came into the strand &amp; made appointment for 3 P.M. Am now here Just saw him &amp; he recognised me straight away.<br/>Raining like hell Glorious country this Oh that I was back in Sydney<br/>went out by Taxi cost me 4/6 same back by bus 3d The curse of it all is you don't know what bus to take &amp; where it is going to when you do take it.<br/>Saw Newton he called for me for &pound;10. ought to be here in a day or so wrote to Maude &amp; Viv.<br/>Rumest thing I have struck is the women drinking in the Pubs there are more women than men at it &amp; its quite the custom no one minds it.</p>
