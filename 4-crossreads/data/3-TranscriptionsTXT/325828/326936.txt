I will send Ol a line or two during the week.  I hope &amp; trust that you are all keeping well &amp; that the winter may not leave its trade mark at "Cairnleath" in the way of colds &amp; coughs etc. Am sending Anna a couple of arrangements that I suppose she would call handkerchiefs that I saw in a village shop one day I am keeping in good health &amp; strength.
