
<p>138</p>
<p>SEPT. &nbsp; MONDAY 25 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. We had an inspection (full marching order) in morning before Campbell. Out with the signallers the rest of the day and we had a lamp parade at night. Cleaning up all our gear at night as we have to march to Bulford tomorrow I believe.</p>
