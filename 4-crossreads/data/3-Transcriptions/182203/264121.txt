<p>3rd A.C.C.S.                                                                                                                                                        c/o 29th C.C.S.                                                                                                                                                B.E.F.                                                                                                                                                                          France.                                                                                                                                                                              (Nearly mid-night) 2. 12. 16.<br/>My dearest Mother &amp; Sisters.,<br/>No Australian mail for me this week again, no doubt shall get one beginning of next week, but it seems a long time between drinks. Well, I'm on night-duty still &amp; likely to be for a few weeks longer. Oh, it is cold, you would be surprised at the amount of clothing I'm wearing. Bought a pair of Kharki knitted leggings, could not get gaiters nor black ones but these are great &amp; go alright with my tan boots.<br/>We are having some heavy frost the roads are quite hard, the water</p>
