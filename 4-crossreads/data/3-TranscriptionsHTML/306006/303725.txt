<p>1-2-17<br/>trying to trace 8 miles of wire we left at Gererat &amp; which has gone astray, had no success bought some goods from the E.F.Canteen &amp; returned ; Lieut Sutherland was to have arrived today but did not turn up</p>
<p>2-2-17<br/>Frid 2nd<br/>My throat is still a little sore, &amp; half the Regt are suffering the same complaint.</p>
<p>Lieut Sutherland came back this afternoon looking very thin, &amp; half dead; Browne went to the Camel Corps today &amp; saw Jock Davidson &amp; Moylan; A day or two ago the 5th Mtd. Bde sent in half a mile of wire in exchange for what we left out</p>
