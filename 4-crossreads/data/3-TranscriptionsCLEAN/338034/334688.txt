
We have taken many Bedouin & camel caravans prisoner during the last couple of days.Dec 23rd :- last night about 11 p.m. the whole of the Anzac Mounted Division with the exception of the 2nd Brigade, accompanied by the Australian Camel Corps & two Batteries of English Artillery (18 Pdrs.) & one Battery of Indian Mountain Guns marched out along the Wadi for about 20 miles in s Southerly direction.
