<p>Troopship "Vestalia"A44<br/>Major C. D. Fulller in charge left Sydney  with 50 Queenslanders &amp; 50 N. S. W. (414 horses) 13 Vet.<br/>2 A. M. cleared the <u>Heads</u> 5 P. M.</p>
<p><u>Thursday 17th December 1914 arrived Melbourne Saturday 19th night at Victoria Docks 9 P. M. </u>  Left again<br/>22nd <u>Tuesday afternoon 4. 30 </u><br/>anchored at Ree Light all night ; <u> Left for Albany 12. 30 Wednesday afternoon 23rd </u><br/>passed Queenscliff -4 P. M. dropped pilot 5 PM<br/>distance from, Sydney to Melbourne by water 576miles<br/>from Port Phillip Heads to Melbourne  warf &ndash; 45 miles.<br/>"Vestalia"average 12 knots per hour.</p>
