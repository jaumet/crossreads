 SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 20, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; hot westerly gale blowing. 
 Lopped in Top paddock in 
 morning. Set a few fires in 
 the Middle scrub. Tickle 
 lopped in Mountain and then 
 drew water to Darby. 
 Went into Quirindi in evening 
 to see Laurie Cadell about 
 grass down the coast.
