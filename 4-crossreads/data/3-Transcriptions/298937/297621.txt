<p>Tuesday 25th July</p>
<p>Since last entry the Turks have attacked on the Canal at Katia, but it was not an action of any great consequence.<br/>The Light Horse reinforcements are moving off in consequence, &amp; rumours did flow along that we were to go too, but they have dropped now, for new ones are afloat.</p>
