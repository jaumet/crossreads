<p>They had the choice of valuations of good standing; they nominated Richardson &amp; Wrench who were unknown to me except by repute, &amp; both the Red Cross &amp; myself were satisfied with the result.</p>
<p>The place was never on the market at any time; 3 different medical men approached me asking the possibilities of partnerships but I preferred to hold it myself &amp; negotiations were never even started; they were turned down at once.
