<p>Monday 15/1/17<br/>Training progressing slowly &ndash; interviewed N.C.O's.</p>
<p>Tuesday 16/1/17</p>
<p>Rifle Range completed commenced firing. Concert at night very Good &ndash; letter from Bas.</p>
<p>Wednesday 17/1/17</p>
<p>Inspection of 7th Coy quite good &ndash; Rugby and Victoria Rules football matches.</p>
<p>Thursday 18/1/17</p>
<p>Murphy arrived for C.M. Cases went into Amiens for dinner with him.</p>
<p>Friday 19/1/17</p>
<p>Murphy left &ndash; snowing heavily, sports tomorrow.</p>
<p>Saturday 20/1/17</p>
<p>Excellent Sports Div Cmdr present marched past in forms very good day but bitterly cold.</p>
