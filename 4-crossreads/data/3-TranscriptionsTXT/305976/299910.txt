 Thursday Aug 23 Rose at 6.45 AM. Breakfast 7 AM. On duty until 9 AM. This Camp will do me. Food is good. Duties light &amp; plenty of surf bathing.  Wednesday  Friday Aug 24 My first view of two German Taubes today. Flew over camp, very high in air. Heard Monitors shelling Gaza &amp; could see flashes up coast Saturday Aug 25 Cricket match today between staff &amp; patients of rest camp. Patients won by four runs. Sunday Aug 26 Two Taube overhead today. Flew much lower than on previous occasions. Wrote letters afternoon.
