<p>were supplied with Bread &amp; Jam &amp; Tea which was readily devoured as we were very hungry &amp; tired, a few moments later we were issued <strike>Bla</strike> 5 Blankets &amp; as there were Iron Beds Provided all hands made a shakedown on the Iron Beds &amp; retired for a little Sleep it being then 5.45 am.</p>
<p>Wednesday 28th 1917.<br/>After having 3 hours rest we were all up again being anxious to have a look around the Camp. In describing the Camp, I am afraid I am unable to do it justice, but I will do my best. Of what I have seen this Camp which is named Bordon is built on both sides of a long road enclosed with a hedge on both sides as far as the eye can see &amp; the Huts are built of <strike>Iron</strike></p>
