<p>Sep 18 &ndash; 23</p>
<p>Fri 18th  Doing nothing but looking at riding tests all day witnessed some lovely falls.  on picket from 10 pm till 2 am.</p>
<p>Sat 19th<br/>Races at Rosebery. went out on the hills for mounted drill with signallers  Coleman made the pace a cracker.  Stan stopped a kick from Cob but was not hurt  Coleman's horse bolted, we saw Jessie at Hospital evening</p>
<p>Sun 20th<br/>Church parade morning.  were told in orders that we start to embark on Thursday.  received a pass from 2 PM till 6 PM  went to Felicia, met Jess &amp; Eva Stokes there &amp; went to Church for a change</p>
<p>Mon 21st<br/>Lying about all morning getting instructions how to fold overcoats</p>
<p>Tue 22nd<br/>Innoculated against typhoid  mounted drill.  to Felicia evening</p>
<p>Wed 23rd<br/>Races at Rosebery,  mounted troops inspected by Govenor General, saw Jessie evening at Hospital</p>
