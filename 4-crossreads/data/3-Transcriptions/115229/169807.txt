<p>out of our cramped positions into slush well above out boots.&nbsp;Here we shivered till we raised a perspiration and then marched to billets with beds made with wire netting.&nbsp;We ate a couple of hard biscuits and then turned in and slept until rations arrived at eleven next morning.&nbsp;From then until the 4th November we put in training and being issued with sheepskins and rabbit skin gloves.</p>
