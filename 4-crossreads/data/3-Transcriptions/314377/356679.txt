<p>The tents were right on the top of Quarantine Reserve. There was no special accomodation for cripples. We were put into ordinary tents. One man filled me a mattress &ndash; another got me tea.</p>
<p>Next day representations were made for a separate marquee for us. This was supplied also wire mattresses with legs on them We slept very well for the rest of the stay in Quarantine. A separate marquee was used for serving the meals.</p>
