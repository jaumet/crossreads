 Nothing changes in this damp little village except the weather.  Ten minutes sunshine perhaps then a dull misty atmosphere followed  from  by a drizzle.  Occasionally one is lured  on  out of doors without a rain coat by a bright blue patch in the sky, but before going many hundred yards that blue patch has mysteriously disappeared, completely changing ones outlook on life.
