<p>18th August 1918</p>
<p>My dear Mother</p>
<p>here it is Sunday again and I wrote to Fred last week but forgot to get it censored straight away and so missed the post, which closed earler this time than it generally does before one of our moves. You see we are on track again, left our old camp early yesterday morning and I&#39;m writing this on Sunday afternoon under the shade of an Olive tree on one of the rocky hills near Kuryat el Enab. We are on our way back to the valley again you see. It is very interresting sitting here on this hill and watching all that is going on below me.
