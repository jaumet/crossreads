<p>Frank Beaurepaire is here as 3rd.Div.YMCA Rep., amongst other things he runs a jolly good Cinema and six of us paid our 50 centimes, about 4&frac12;d and went into the special reserve, the pictures were great.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp; Oh, well, Cheer-oh and good luck!</p>
