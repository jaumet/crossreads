<p>Paris on the 12th October</p>
<p>My dear Sir:</p>
<p>I have received your kind letter there is a few days, and I am glad to know you in good health.</p>
<p>At the beginning of October, I have gone in at school again. &nbsp;I study chemistry, physics, natural history and principaly philosophy. &nbsp;Ths last matter, at the beginning, is very difficult to understand and&nbsp;to read.</p>
