
26.8.16Sat 26thAs Brig. General Cox came back from England today Col Meredith has once again taken command of the 1st Regt.   at stables this afternoon Cox inspected the horses & men, I handed in all the Begbie Signalling Lamps today & are to be issued with Electric Sig Lamps tomorrow,nothing out of the ordinary happened today
27.8.16Sat 27thNo Church parade this morn held tonight instead & had a good congregation;  Both Jeffries are still on the spree and put under open arrest tonight, Frank was on day stableman & is being wheeled for leaving the lines I can see no charge against Fred, & as he was transferred to C Sqd yesterday he does not affect me
