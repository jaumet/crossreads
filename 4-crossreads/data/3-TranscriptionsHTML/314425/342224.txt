<p>The Potts brothers also put in applications for transfers to the Pioneer Battalion. Later we were told to hold the transfers for a few days as at present the C.O. could do nothing until it was decided what was to become of the 37th.<br/>I was at a concert tonight Sgt Westbrook gave an exhibition in boxing &amp; showed the various hits and their counters.</p>
<p>Tuesday Sept24. 1918. 1512.<br/>A beautiful morning. We heard a rumour last night that the authorities had decided to cut up the 37th at all costs &amp; that the men would not get a win after all. This cheered up those who came here, many of whom coming &amp; were very anxious to get back. There has been great victories on 3 fronts. The Western, the Balkin states and in Palestine where the Turkish army is anilated &amp; 18,000 prisoners taken.<br/>The 38. Batln is preparing for another stunt today.<br/>There was a heavy bombardment last night and again this morning. It is reported this evening that St Quintin has fallen to the Yanks. I had a bath in a big dam this afternoon. The enemy brought down 2 of our baloons in flames today.</p>
