<p><u>Saturday March 25th.</u><br/>Well, we went to the Bazaar, (Billie &amp; me), it was all wright, we went for a "Bonzer" drive all round Killara for 3 we must have been away for nearly 15 minutes. I have not heard how much they got yet over &pound;100-0-0 I should think.</p>
<p>Bill is enclosing a letter to you, I don't think there is much reading about it so you wont have to put your glasses on.</p>
<p><u>Wednesday 29th 1916.</u><br/>I am going to a swimming carnival to morrow afternoon.</p>
