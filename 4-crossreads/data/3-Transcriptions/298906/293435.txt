<p>near them, after we had thrown our bombs we sneaked back with the rifles popping at us, we then opened fire on them &amp; rockets were sent up by both sides, then the whole lot of us attacked under cover of a smoke bomb barrage when we got to their wire we heaved our bombs in the trench &amp; at the spectators which consisted mainly of old Volunteer Majors &amp; Colonels, they enjoyed the whole thing immensely, as we were laying in front of the wire a bomb landed fair under my nose, I grabbed it &amp; was going to throw it back it just left my hand &amp; burst, scattering powder &amp; dust all over me, it never hurt my hand in the slightest</p>
