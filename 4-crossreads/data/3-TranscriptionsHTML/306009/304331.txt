<p>1915<br/>May. 25 Tue.<br/>Have been issued from Quartermaster's store with 3 prs. socks &amp; 1 pr. puttees - the first re-issue of clothing since enrolment; but at same time not exactly necessary &ndash; have plenty of togs, pretty complete outfit.<br/>Issued with khaki helmet with blue &amp; <strike>back</strike> black diamond shaped cloth badge on side. Seems to be an Imperial idea to do away with the slouch hat.<br/>Went in to town to-night with "Snow" Freeman for a couple of hours.<br/>On return wrote a couple of Scout letter - and then feeling snoozy&ndash;woozy Off to bed.</p>
