<p>3<br/>Heard from Edie Budden the other day. She seemed to be having a very good time.<br/>How awful of them to have made Meagher Lord Mayor &ndash; Such an absolute rotter too.<br/>We had a church parade today at which I was present. The Officiating chaplain was not much of a preacher. Rather a ninkumpoop, I think.<br/>Well, au-revoir, old girl. Very much love to you all.<br/>Your loving brother<br/>Fred</p>
