<p>Thursday 4 February 1915<br />Hospital in the morning.<br />Writing &amp; reading most of the day&nbsp;<br />Walked to top of sand hills just before dinner.&nbsp;<br />Air very cold.&nbsp;<br />Expected letters but none came.</p>
<p>Friday 5 February 1915<br />One operation this morning Haemorrhoids&nbsp;<br />A beautiful morning 12 noon.&nbsp;No letters<br />In Cairo during the afternoon<br />At Heliopolis for half an hour.<br />Shepheards to dine with Major Barrett.<br />Left for home at 10.30 pm.</p>
<p>Saturday 6 February 1915<br />At Mena House all day.<br />Working, writing, and reading&nbsp;<br />10.45 p.m. Must go to bed early.</p>
<p>Sunday 7 February 1915<br />Mass. 9 a.m. Padre McAuliffe &nbsp;preached against use of&nbsp;immodest&nbsp;profane &amp; vulgar words in use about Camp.<br />Called 3<sup>rd</sup> &amp; 4<sup>th</sup> Infty&nbsp;Battalion L. Col Owen L. Col. Onslow Thompson also 1<sup>st</sup> F &nbsp;Ambulance L Col. Newmarch.&nbsp;<br />Col Beeston &amp; other officers, from the 3<sup>rd</sup>&nbsp;Exped. force visited Mena House. They are camped at Heliopolis</p>
