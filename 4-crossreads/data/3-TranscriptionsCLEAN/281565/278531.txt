 Instead of landing as we had expected at Toulon, we again set sail on Tuesday morning for Marseilles & arrived off that city a couple of hours later getting into berth about 1-30 p.m.  A driving rain obscured a good deal of the shore, which like Toulon was high & Pine clad.
