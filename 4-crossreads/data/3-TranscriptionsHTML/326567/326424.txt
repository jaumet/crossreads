On passing through Ropley Station the Ornamental Trees were trained &amp; cut into the shape of a spire with an imitation Rooster on Top, other Trees were like a bottle shape doing great credit to the Station Master in charge. A Station called Northam roused a cheer from the Boys as nearly all hands were acquainted with a large Town in Western Australia of that name. After passing through several stations we arrived at Southampton at 7.50 am after twisting &amp; shunting around several buildings we entered the Dockyard Gates &amp; into a large shed when we received the order to detrain &amp; form up into our platoons, after Orders of our Boundarys were read out we were dismissed &amp; allowed to ramble about the Docks for a while &amp; see the Sights.
