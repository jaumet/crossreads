<p>APRIL 1917</p>
<p>27 FRIDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; sunny. Cleaning harness all</p>
<p>day getting ready for inspection</p>
<p>tomorrow. I got all mine</p>
<p>clean again &amp; it was very</p>
<p>good. We put our horses out</p>
<p>in paddock all day &amp;</p>
<p>again at night. I did not</p>
<p>hear any more of Alan.</p>
