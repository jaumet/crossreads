<p><strike>Noordhemhoek</strike> Broodsiende 18.10.17</p>
<p>Fine day.  Enemy planes very low and active during early hours of morning.  Our guns silenced an enemy gun and blew up an ammun. dump in direction of Moorselede.</p>
<p>19.10.17</p>
<p>Our guns fired heavily in direction of town of Passchendaele on left last night.  Enemy opened barrage on our lines shattering front line system for a short distance.</p>
