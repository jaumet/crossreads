<p>January 1915<br/>Friday 22<br/>Fatigue at Ordnance Store. Left the store with a few useful articles of clothing.</p>
<p>Saturday 23<br/>Muster parade. Half holiday arranged. Football match arranged at Khaazaar en nil [Kasr&ndash;el&ndash;Nil] Barracks Cairo between team out of our Batt &amp; the Terriers. We won by 6 goals to 1</p>
