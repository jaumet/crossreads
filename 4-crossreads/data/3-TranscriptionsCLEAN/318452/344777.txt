 Sunday Oct 25th Last night the hun airmen were very active dropping bombs. About 6 oclock he came over head and unloaded about a dozen bombs he got about 30 men in the 2nd Bn who are close by us 12 men were killed. We had a good parade this morning at the Hall. It was a combined parade of non Anglicans. I took part & Padre McKenzie gave address We buried 14 men this afternoon. Bombs were dropped all round us again this evening
