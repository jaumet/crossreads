<p>Oct. 5th<br/>An abrupt ending to morning rehearsal &ndash; Orders to move -  In two or three hours we make preparations for departure and load three large motor lorries with personal &amp; Theatrical properties &ndash;<br/>A journey of 17 kilometres (almost 11 miles) brings us to Bray-sur-Somme where we are to enjoy the privilege of entertaining our "1914 Boys" who are just about to leave for Australia. Bray &ndash; (quite recently in German hands) presents</p>
