 October 1916 4th   Left for leave in London &amp; returned on the 6th at Midday. This was my final leave before proceeding to France. 6th.   We were supposed to leave for France to night but it was postponed. Since I have been in England I have had an interesting &amp; most enjoyable time &amp; I like the country very much.
