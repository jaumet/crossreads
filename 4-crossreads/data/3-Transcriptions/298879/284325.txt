<p>February 1915<br/>Wednesday 3<br/>Rifle Range shooting.</p>
<p>Thursday 4<br/>Shooting practice for the battalion. 7th &amp; 8th Batt. gone to Ismaelia. Paid weekly wage of 68 piastres or 14/- English. Received photos of myself &amp; a friend. Posted them to the various recipients. Our Battalion won the brigade shooting competition</p>
