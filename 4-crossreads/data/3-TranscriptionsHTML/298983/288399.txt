<p>Thursday 21st March<br/>Did nothing all day</p>
<p>Friday 22nd March<br/>Went out this morning at 6 &amp; &amp; nearly finished the job. Quite a hot day. Put some sand bags &amp; dirt on our humpie to make it safe from Shrapnel or pieces of Shell &ndash; not sea shells &ndash;</p>
<p>Saturday 23rd March<br/>Three of us went out to put finishing</p>
