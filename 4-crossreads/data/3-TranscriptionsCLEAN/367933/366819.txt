
At Sea8th Nov.1917
Mrs Vernon1st A.L.H. Comforts Fund, Depot28 Moore St. Sydney. N,S.W.
Dear MadamPermit me on behalf of the boys of mine and 32nd Reinforcements to record the pleasure it gave us to receive the parcels of comforts so kindly sent by your committee in response to my wire from Broadmeadows . The goods were distributed on the wharf at Port Melbourne just prior to our leaving the shores of Australia.
