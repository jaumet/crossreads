<p>Oct. 24th<br/>Letters from London - and to Australia &ndash; Congratulations on last night's performance of "Saa-eeda Digger" &ndash;<br/>President Wilson and his judicious replies to German Peace Talk -<br/>Despite the time of the year the weather is fine and far from being cold.</p>
<p>Oct. 25th<br/>We leave the Old Theatre and take over the New &ndash; Our Eastern comedy is still on the bill and the audience consisting mostly of Australians</p>
