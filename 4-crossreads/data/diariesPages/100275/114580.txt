Last night all estaminets; were placed out of bounds to British troops till the
10th of August. the boys are going crook over it, but I'll bet they find some way to get the beer, unknown to the authorities Just before dinner some of us went down to the little river, had a good swim the water was icy cold but it was beautiful all the same the reason why it is so cold is that it is fed from the Alps, the snow from Alsace the mountains there abouts she runs fairly fast, is a dark blue color there are plenty of fish in her too.
