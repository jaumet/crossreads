Smythe &ndash; two  soldiers  in the most true sense of the word. &ndash; Many happy returns of 29th August. &ndash; Please God we may all be together on 29th Aug 17. 10 June 16 Since writing the foregoing was very pleased to receive a letter from you, Will &amp; dear little Margaret. In anticipation I want to thank you dear for sending me such a fine parcel &amp; have not many doubts that it will turn up in due course. &ndash; Had such a nice letter yesterday from Mrs Reid of "Gowan Brae". She worries a lot about May, because the Zeps have been raiding Newcastle-on-Tyne. &ndash; Thank you for the  slip  newspaper cutting about Bob Stobo. &ndash; I think it was his first Command, so am very glad he was able to come out on tip. &ndash; How good it was of Will to repay you  for  the money you paid in to Harry Budden's office.
