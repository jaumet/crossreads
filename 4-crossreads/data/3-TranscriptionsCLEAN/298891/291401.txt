  
 14 Wednesday 
 Kaimacktchalan still clear - came up dull after lunch.  Leaves coming down in dead earnest now.  Nothing to report.  Dick, Saunders, Davis & I went into Ostrovo to dine with some Serbs and Bogaljub & Milan brought the camion for us at 9.15.  Dick quite drunk. Menu:  Slaw, tinned fish, sheeps' tongues & paprikas;  pungent soup;  cabbage & meat, chicken & fried pot.;  slaw & pastry;  Turkish coffee;  pankakes, Malta & Turkish coffee wine.
