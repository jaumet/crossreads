<p>Sunday Oct. 27.10.18<br/>am feeling off colour to day  the quinine makes me feel very dopey at times  went for a walk along the Beach  a hot day.<br/>Monday Oct. 28.10 18.<br/>am feeling a little better to day  very hot.  wrote to Josie Alma Dorrie Walter Bell Maud S.<br/>Tuesday Oct 29.10.18<br/>am feeling a good deal better to day  wrote to Alie stella aggie.</p>
