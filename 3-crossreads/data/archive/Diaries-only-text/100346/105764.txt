order : home about 3 pm : wrote after tea
Tuesday Aug 28th
Monasterie : still showery : reveille 7 am : "brasso" issued we had to doll up our equipments : rations have been very short lately so the boys have lodged a complaint : but for what we buy we would by no means grow fat
Wednesday Aug 29th
Monasterie : showery : reveille 4 am : fell in inspected " our Coy were overstrength so we were left behind much to our joy : cleaned up the billet wrote : Morrie self went to St Omer after dinner for bread got eight loaves so the boys gave us a welcome : 2 letters from Lithgow :
