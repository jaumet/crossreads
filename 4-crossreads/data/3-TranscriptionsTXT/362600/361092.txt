
F 30 SunTam & I go to [Pewsey?].Tam's chain breaks & we tow one another Home. 12 miles in 1 3/4 hours. Get home in a filthy state & drinks are my only consolationWrote Dad. P.C.  & Mum 10 page letter.
F 1 July MonWe have Magic Lantern Show & I play the Piano. Generally mess around &  tidy up
Dream all night that I've got 42
F 2 Tues.Recd letter from Doris. B.2's play Bulford & win through on Bad Playing. Chain up bike. We have jellies etc. I first try the [sharing?] water pocket and it is O.K. May comes back from Port Hole strike.Wrote Mrs Williams. Nancy Dewan. Roy.
