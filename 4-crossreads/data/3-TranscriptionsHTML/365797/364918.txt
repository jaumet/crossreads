<p>"Dene Gully"<br/>Lindfield<br/>March 23rd. 1916</p>
<p>My Dear Allan,<br/>I have just had a "fair monster" cut, I have been chip carving, &amp; trying a new patten, it stung some I can tell you, still it did not stop me from going on withit.</p>
<p>Dene has gone down to Duntroon, he went a couple of days ago. He Went just "Bong tong you know" He said appearance was everything</p>
<p>We received your letter &amp; photos on tuesday, they were very interesting indeed. but we have not got the photo's you sent out, with the boy, I suppose he has "nabed" them, A?</p>
