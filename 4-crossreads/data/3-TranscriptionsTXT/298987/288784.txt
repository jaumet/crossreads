 Wednesday 17th July 18 Mess Orderly not much work attached to it. Have bathed &amp; greased myself twice today. Was awake a long time last night &amp; it was like being in a big rat hole to hear the others scratching The irritation has almost left me Just been to quite a good lecture by an Indian. There was nothing very special in it but he spoke well &amp; brought in many funny stories so it went off well 
 Thursday 18th Only one greasing today The AMC Cpl who is in charge of this turn out is a rum little bird very serious &amp; most particular about the treatment He seems to have studied the Scabie It appears it is a minute insect &amp; was introduced into this country by the mules from S.
