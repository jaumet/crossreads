  
 27/3/18 - La Basse Ville About 1 a.m. Braithwaite rang up to say that Gorman was wounded and sure enough the fighting Pat soon came in with a white face, a useless arm and blood all over his face and tunic.  He joined our standing patrol and they worked close up to an enemy post on the railway line.  There were plenty of Huns moving round and our chaps got well on to them with sniping.  Their post is at the foot of a sign post.  A couple of hedges were running alongside the railway and presently Sgt. Fraser yelled "Look out" and, in the words of the poet, the "box on"
