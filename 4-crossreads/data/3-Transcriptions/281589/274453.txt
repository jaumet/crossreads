<p>Wednesday 28th March we (5th,6th &amp; 7th Field Ambulances marched out at 10.30 for Bazentin Le Petit about 7 miles or so, where we put in the night. Thursday we marched about 10.30 AM for Becordal about 5 miles, it rained very hard on the way. Friday 30th Fred Little, Macaulliffe &amp; I we detailed to guard stores at Aveluy about 1&frac12; miles the other side of Albert. we went by motor. We are camped in a marquee, close to the river, can draw on certain parts of stores (food) the chaps who we relieved left eggs &amp; other stuff and so we are living like Lords, spring mattresses to sleep &amp; every convenience we are short of a few food necessities. Saturday I made a sago pudding it was alright, showery today, having great times. A couple of weeks back when</p>
