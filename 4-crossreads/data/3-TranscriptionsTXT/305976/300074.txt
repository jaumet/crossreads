We camped at the foothills preparatory to resuming our journey across the valley to morrow.Sunday Nov 3We proceeded from Shunet Nimron to day & crossed the Jordan Valley probably for the last day. It was very hot and a heat haze hung over the Valley. What a desolate place the Jordan Valley is. Practically barren with mountains of Judea or the Judean Hills they are termed facing us
