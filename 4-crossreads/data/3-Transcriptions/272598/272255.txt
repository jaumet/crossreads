<p>Wednesday 16.1.18<br/>Reported at Headquarters 10.30 Am from furlough.  Missed first batch, caught 3 pm train from Paddington arrived at Warminster Sutton Veny" about 7.30 pm.  Spent a awful night on the boards, never slept all night.</p>
<p>Thursday 17.1.18<br/>Snowed heavily, waited about in the snow all morning to be classified, marked B1 A2.  Drew kit from store.</p>
