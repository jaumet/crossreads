  
 Wednesday 30.5.17 Went on leave to Amiens,  Left 7.30 Am by car for Albert & caught 8.15 Am train, arrived Amiens 10 Am  Spent day wandering around city & suburbs   Very little to rave about in the town after having once been there especially with funds rather low  Returned by 7.40 pm train from Amiens, arrived at Albert 10 pm.  Unable to get back to Bapaume till morning which spoilt the whole day.
