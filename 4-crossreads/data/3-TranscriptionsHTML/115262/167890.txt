<p>Thursday 3 May 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille at 6.10 a.m. Breakfast at&nbsp;<s>8 p</s>&nbsp;8 a.m. &nbsp;Left Yea 8.30 a.m.</p>
<p>Homewood 10.20&nbsp;<s>p &nbsp;</s>&nbsp;a.m.</p>
<p>Setting for final plunge to &quot;CAMP&quot;.</p>
<p>Dinner at Kerrisdale &amp; some more dropped out. Horses in G.S transport wagon knocking up.</p>
<p>Now gone 12 miles. Next stop at&nbsp;Trawool Hotel at 3 pm. Left Trawool Hotel at 3.15 pm. [After lunch we left Kerrisdale at 1.37 p.m.]</p>
<p>Arrived in camp at 5.15 p.m.</p>
<p>After&nbsp;<s>tea </s>&nbsp;a&nbsp;shower then tea &amp; turned in at 8.30 p.m.</p>
