  
 April, 1916 Monday 3 We have got a motly crowd in this time. It took us two hours to sort them into squads I am given 19 men & a gun from the 13th Bde M.G. Coy. See Theo Perry down for bombing course. 
 Tuesday 4 Continue the good work. Most of my lot are working men & object to so much writing. We are working on the summer programme 6 am to 8 am 9.30 am to 12.30 pm 4.30 pm to 6.30 pm
