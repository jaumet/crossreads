1/8/16No stunt last night as rumoured though there was plenty of shells flying around last night & early this morning  Germans shelling Albert all around the church  Out again to day cleaning up.  two of our chaps wounded.  we arrived back home after a few shells we thrown at us.  Very very hot.
2/8/16Lively night again last night wonderful sight to see gun firing 12 inch gun fired near us to day what a mess wonderful  Plenty of aeroplanes about.  only seen two up to date of the enemys.  big guns on railway  Letter from home today.  I did not go out today.
3/8/16Thing quiet to day of course shells still falling about
