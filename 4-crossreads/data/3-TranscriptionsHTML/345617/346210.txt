<p>Octobre 15<br/>Building dugouts on West Hoek Ridge &amp; draining "Pill Boxes". The weather is still very disagreeable. Nothing of unusual importance to note.</p>
<p>Octobre 16<br/>Working on drainage of roads between West Hoek &amp; Anzac Ridge in morning &amp; on dugouts all day on "Hoek Ridge". The shelling today has been exceptionally heavy &amp; we are getting a lot of casualties. Fritz also sent over a lot of gas shells last night.</p>
