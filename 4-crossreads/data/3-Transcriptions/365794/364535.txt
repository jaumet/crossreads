<p>Monday 13th May 1917 [1918]<br/>At 6 a.m. hot bath, sew, read, etc.  7 a.m. breakfast, 9 a.m. cocoa, 11.30 a.m. M.O. cool &amp; calm.  What is causing diarrhoea Miss Holloway?  Taking any Aper.?  I suggest 2ce daily, I swallow salts, "leave it off".  12.30 p.m. lunch.<br/>3 come my 2 Aust. friends, Jones &amp; Furnifull &amp; at last am free &amp; a happy 2 hours spent shopping etc. in Alex. make me feel myself once more.<br/>No M.O. tonight (first time the poor man fails to visit us).  Borrowed &pound;1-0-0 from Miss Jones.</p>
