<p>This started the other brothers with the desire to enlist and they too shortly joined the ranks.</p>
<p>Speaking of myself, I was not satisfied with the verdict. I went back again a few months afterwards and this time passed flying at the Sydney Town Hall.</p>
<p>At the examination I was confronted by 2 detectives with the intention I suppose of seeing whether I was a desirable character or not. They noticed the vaccination marks on my right arm and thought I had probably been a deserter after offering my services previous by.</p>
