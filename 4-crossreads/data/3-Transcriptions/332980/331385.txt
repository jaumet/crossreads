<p>Sea Beach &amp; the Walk's are laid out well, there are two Walk's before you come to the Beach &amp; as it was a nice &amp; pleasant Evening, there were a large number of people promenading, &amp; my Pall &amp; myself soon met two nice Scotch Lasses for a chat &amp; stroll, &amp; we had a Bon time for a couple of hours, I must admit these Scotch Lasses are grand, they delight in showing the Australian's around &amp; pointing out the places of Interest &amp; as the Evening's are so long being quite light &amp; hardly any darkness this month the Twilight is beautiful, these Lasses kept us company until 11 Pm when we returned by Tram &amp; saw them home, making an appointment for the next Evening, we strolled about until midnight, as there were still a fair number about &amp; we were not tired,</p>
