<p>10. Sunday<br/>In House. Audrey came up from down stairs &amp; Miss Smith for dinner. Read Miss H. "By Far Kaimacktchalan" at night.</p>
<p>11. Monday<br/>Went to meet Miss Hodge at Minerva &amp; helped her buy a hat at Maxim. Almond trees almost out.</p>
<p>12. Tuesday<br/>Came back to Miss Smith. Nell came at night.</p>
<p>13. Wednesday<br/>Miss S. &amp; I went to Suffrage celebration in Queen's Hall Fawcett in chair. Haydon  Henderson &amp; Zylton  speakers</p>
