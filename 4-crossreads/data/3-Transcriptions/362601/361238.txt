<p>17 July, Thursday</p>
<p>F.</p>
<p>Lovely Day. We sail at 6 am. We have a big clearance in Hospital. I expect to go overland from Melbourne so I pack all my bags &amp; bring 'em up to Hospital.</p>
<p>18 July, Friday</p>
<p>We get news at 3 pm that we go ashore at Melbourne &amp; overland. All excited &amp; tons of work. I sleep in Hospital on top story.</p>
<p>We sight the heads at 8 pm.</p>
