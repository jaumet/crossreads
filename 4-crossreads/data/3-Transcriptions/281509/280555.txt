<p>28 November 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>A very busy night. Bad  management of this place is deplorable. Congestion needlessly caused by bad organisation. Get cleared up about 2 p.m. Change on to day shift, remain on duty till 2.30 p.m. 18 hours.  Visit Tony in aft. &amp; take him  great coat. Tea in Y.M.C.A Biscuits &amp; cold cocoa. 3 &amp;cents for tallow dip to eat it by.</p>
