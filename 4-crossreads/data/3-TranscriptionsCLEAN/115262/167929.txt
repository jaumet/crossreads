  
 Monday 11 June 1917. 
 Still on duty and fairly solid too 
 Sea rough & resembling the bight. Transport shipping seas a good deal. Fire in bunker or hold started again. Foul air starting again also. 
 Wrote letters in spare time in morng & afternoon. 
 I am now looking after 42 out patients,  &  mumps & measles, 3 scabies & 6 V.D's. patients.
