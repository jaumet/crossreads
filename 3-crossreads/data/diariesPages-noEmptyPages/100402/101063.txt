Saturday 27 April
Quiet to-day, no casualties all day.
News to hand that Kemmel Hill has been taken by the Boche after a big fight with the French defending troops.
Sunday 28 April
Last night the Boche shook things up round here caused us much uneasiness with his shells. One direct hit on our cow shed. This afternoon too, he straafed here dropped some 5.9's on the road. Heavy bombardment on our left again to-night.
No casualties except two.
