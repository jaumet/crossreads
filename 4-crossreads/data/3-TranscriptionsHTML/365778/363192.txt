<p>&amp;wishing me many happy returns.<br/>Monday 30/4/17<br/>Received pair of sox from Doff. Relief orders received.<br/>Tuesday 1/5/17<br/>Relieved by True and Forbes came out to Favreuil, relief completed at dawn, had dinner at Division with Heritage.<br/>Wednesday 2/5/17<br/>Men bathed and fitted out again, going in tonight for stunt. Saw Bas, wrote to Doff.<br/>Thursday 3/5/17<br/>Things very warm, 5th Bde did not get in, 6th OK. My Battn switched in carrying bombing etc. Came back at night after being relieved by Norrie.</p>
