<p>Thursday 8 August<br/>Up at 4 a.m. and awaited the stunt. At 4-20 up she went &amp; noise was terrific. Everything went well. Lines held. Artillery chased up infantry very quickly. Day foggy.</p>
<p>Out in morning around about. Plenty of prisoners. Casualities very slight. News better as day goes on. Out in afternoon looking about.</p>
