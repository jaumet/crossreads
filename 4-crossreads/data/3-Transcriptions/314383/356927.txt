We grilled the meat on long wires, had a drink of water &amp; rolled up in some hay after feeding &amp; watering the horses &amp; posting an Armenian as sentry at the gate.  At about 3am the mules turned up, Nimmo having gone out for them.  At dawn we were astir &amp; posted Cameron on the roof to keep a look out &amp; looked to breakfast.  I intended staying here until noon thus giving the refugees a start &amp; resting the horses after the hard day before.</p>
