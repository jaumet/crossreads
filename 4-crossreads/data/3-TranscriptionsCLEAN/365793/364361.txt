
Spt 13. Cold, wet & windy today as yesterday, but we still hope to see "old Sol' score a victorySeveral fairly decent seas have been shipped on the Port Saloon deck & the well decks. Q'ld pineapple dished out again last night; a tin to 3 or 4 men. No 7 table - noted for hums & appetites. When we eat all our own tucker we hum some more from the other tables. The mail for Melb' closes at 8 pm on the 13th ins't. Expect to reach Melb' in time for the show.
