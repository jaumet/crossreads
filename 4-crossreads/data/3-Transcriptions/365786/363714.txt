<p>in Egypt.  <strike>Saladin built parts</strike> They also </strike>made</strike> built a huge aqueduct to bring water to the Citadel from the Nile.<br/>Saladan [Saladin] built parts of it, Napolean Bonaparte occupied it for several months &amp; wherever one turns something historical can be seen.<br/>The Island of Roda has the Nilometer in it &ndash; a wooden post in a slimy green well marked with Arabic signs.  When the Nile reaches a certain mark on this the sluices are opened &amp; the inundation commences.</p>
