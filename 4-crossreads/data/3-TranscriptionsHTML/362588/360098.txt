<p>Leave<br/>3 &ndash; The District Commandant directs that Officers are not to be granted leave except with the approval of the Commandant, A.I.F. Camp, Liverpool.</p>
<p>Leave is not to be granted to men except in special cases certified by C.O.s.</p>
<p>All applications for Officers leave must be in Headquarters for the approval of the Commandant not later than 10 a.m. daily.</p>
