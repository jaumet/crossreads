<p>February  1918<br/>17. Sunday<br/>Air raid at night. No damage near us.</p>
<p>18. Monday<br/>Miss H &amp; I<br/>Went to hear Litvinoff in Cent. Hall, Westminster. Guns began firing at 9 and kept on a long time. Delayed us getting home.</p>
<p>19. Tuesday Sun<br/> At Miss Hodges. Slept at Mrs Richards 10 Farm Walk.</p>
<p>20. Wednesday<br/>Went with Mrs Oliver  to see Com. Kitchen at Child's hill. Tea with Mrs O. Rained a little.</p>
