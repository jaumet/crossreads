 General Concentration Camp Holdworthy 28th June 1916 
 To, Messrs. Bungardy, Thomzick, Korth &amp; Villiers Prisoners of War. Holdsworthy 
 Sirs, Your letter 28th Instant is received, the contents of which are noted. 
 Your letter, with the two previously received will be forwarded to the American Consul General in Melbourne. 
 Yours faithfully W S. Stuart Major President. Court of Enquiry.
