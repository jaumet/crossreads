<p>Sunday 20 May 1917.</p>
<p>Still no sight of land.</p>
<p>Everyone writing letters today as mail closes. tomorrow morning.</p>
<p>Sat up on the top deck reading during morning after church parade.</p>
<p>At 12 noon. (exactly) the&nbsp;<s>destr</s>&nbsp;cruisers 2 now turned completely North from West &amp; continued so in that direction. We have now rounded the coast at the Sth Wstn<sup>&nbsp;</sup>extremity of Australia. A good sea &amp; a smooth trip today.</p>
<p>At 10.30 p.m. last night another Cruiser picked us up &quot;Doris&quot; an imperial cruiser. &nbsp;A nice appearance&nbsp;too&nbsp;and bringing up the rear of our fleet.</p>
<p>Expect to be in Freemantle tomorr-ow morning&nbsp;early&nbsp;now.</p>
<p><u>6 pm&nbsp;</u>travelling very slow in calm sea</p>
<p>4 Knots an hr</p>
