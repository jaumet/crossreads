  17th  
 We relieved the 7th Border Regt. from the support position in  behind  front of Mericourt about 11 p.m. 
 17th I slept in the chateau last night in one of the roomy cellars.  Like all other Chateaux in the battle area this one has been sadly torn by shell fire.  Only the ground floor remains intact.
