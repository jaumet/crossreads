<p>Sunday 20 February 1916<br/>Got up early and went to church with Mother to 8 o'clock service at St. Chads Cremorne.  Was going to Manly in afternoon but all went to sleep instead.  Went down to Nohovel in afternoon then round to Sams and got some beans &amp; helped Vida wish up.</p>
<p>Monday 21 February 1916<br/>Went to town with Mother.  Drew some money out of bank then got railway tickets.  Got present for Sam &amp; Minnie (Mother did).  Had dinner in town and got some music etc.  Left Aunt Katie's by 8 o'clock tram &amp; caught train for Mudgee.</p>
