
March, 1916Tuesday 28Got up fresh as daiseys, had a wash & breakfast then went up to see dad.After dinner went out w/- an old Pal from Liverpool Harry Grovesent 10 home & had a real good timeturned in 11.30 pm.
Wednesday 29Very quiet all day reading & poking aboutVery hot & sultry after sundown cools offPlay cards w/- dad & the boysWrite letter & up my DiaryTurn in at 11 pm.
