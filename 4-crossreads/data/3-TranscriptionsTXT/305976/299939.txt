About dusk we moved from Jil Gaza bound for Julius.Wednesday Nov 21We rode all through last night & arrived at Julius just before daybreak Julius is usual type of native village consisting of one or two decent houses where the head men of the village lived the rest being hovels.We are now getting into decent country & Oranges are plentiful.
