<p>Sunday Sept.1.9.18<br/>am feeling well  very hot day  had a good cold shower  dinner 12 issue of cigarettes  they were all mouldy  threw them away  sent dad some more Papers  had tea turned in<br/>Monday Sept. 2.9.18.<br/>discharged from Boulac This morning 8.30 AM  went to Cairo  caught train arrived moorear 3 PM.<br/>glad to get away from Boulac a cow of a Place</p>
