<p>Tuesday 16th<br/>Wet &amp; Cold. 14 Patients left &amp; 14 new ones took there place. Did not go out. One is well out of France just now. The news is not good but we are shelling a <strike>n</strike> great lot of Huns acording to the chaps who came from the fighting</p>
<p>Wednesday 17<br/>Walk to Buckingham as usual had afternoon Tea. Matches are very hard to get</p>
<p>Thursday 18<br/>As usual Have a job in forenoon now helping the little Nurse with the dressings</p>
<p>Friday 19th<br/>Walked to Margate quite a fine place. Been hit in a few places by shells in the Hun raids Hun Officer prisoners are kept there now as protection Bus home</p>
<p>Saturday 20th<br/>Quite a busy day Went to church &amp; christened Christopher Powel-Cotton the son in heir</p>
