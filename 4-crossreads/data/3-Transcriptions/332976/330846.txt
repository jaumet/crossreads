<p>April 1917<br/>23rd   Another fine day to-day.   We had an early morning parade before breakfast &amp; the usual 9 o'clock parade on which we had the inspection &amp; then drill until<strike>l</strike> dinner time.<br/>This afternoon was <strike>out</strike> our turn at the bath house which is about a mile or so away, &amp; it was the first bath the majority of us had had for months.<br/>News came to hand to-night to the effect that on the Arras front, the 3rd Army have advanced 1500 yards on a 1600 yards front.</p>
