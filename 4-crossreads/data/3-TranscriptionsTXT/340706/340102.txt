 I dine tonight at Chinese caf&eacute; &amp; find it very agreeable to be eating bamboo again. The meal they give is one of the biggest in London. 
 The previous evenings I have dined at the Restaurant Espagnol 27 Dean St. Soho Sq.W. Bill Coverta 3 Tortilla 1/6 Staeck 1/9 Fritto 6 Compot 9 Coffee 4d = 5/1. Previous evening Coverta 3 Sopa 6 Filet Plaice 1/6 Cocido 2/6 Compot 9 Coffee 4 = 5/10. In evening I visit Socialist Club &amp; am present at a big fire in Tottenham Ct Road. 
 20-5-19 (Tues) Visit tailors in Savile Row &amp; find their prices for uniform is 11 to 12 gns far beyond my purse &amp; then catch 1.15 train to Weybridge which is about 2 &frac12; miles from the Manor House but is nearest station I am met by a South African officer who is staying there &amp; driven in a little cart with a small wee pony just like a big dog.
