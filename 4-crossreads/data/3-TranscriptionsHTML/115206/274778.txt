<p>July 1918<br/>Saturday 27</p>
<p>Ribemont. Bty Position<br/>Weather wet and miserable throughout the day.<br/>Laid four lines including O.P. which is quite some distance away from our home.<br/>Fritz doing quite a considerable amount of ranging and gunfire during afternoon and night. I think he has pulled some more guns in action up this way. Our trench home completely flooded out during day.<br/>Had a very bombs during the night. <br/>Mended O.P. wire during the evening.</p>
