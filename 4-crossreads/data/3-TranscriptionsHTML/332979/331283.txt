<p>Ammunition in the Magazine. They face the Sea &amp; must not take their Eyes off it. If they see any Suspicious Objects <strike>they</strike> or Lights in the near distance they must Fire one round &amp; point in the direction &amp; Shout out to the Corporal of the Guard. If it is a long way off they let the Corporal know who reports same to the Officer on the Bridge. On the Starboard Side there are 6 positions. The Corporals duty is to place the Men &amp; Inspect them every &frac12; hour to see if they are keeping a sharp look our as we are well in the Danger Zone &amp; no chances are taken. The Ship also has a Man posted right at the Front of the Forecastle Head also in the Crow's Nest. After doing duty on the Port Side all night I was transferred to the Starboard Side at 6. 1m in the Morning</p>
<p>Saturday 17th/3/17.</p>
