  
 Sunday 18/3/17 and Monday 19/3/17 passed off quietly nothing happened out of the ordinary. 
 Tuesday 13/3/17. This day was our saddest of the long voyage, we had the misfortune to lose  off  one of our comrades who was attached to the 24 Reinf of the 11th Battalion. He had been ailing for some time & was in the Hospital 4 days & he gradually sank & Died early this morning. After preparing him for his last resting place his Mortal Remains was Commited to the Deep at 8 am.
