  
 Sept. 19th Rehearsal & Show - Derive much pleasure from reading short biography of R.B. Sheridan & his play "The rivals" - A visit by General Carruthers. 
 Sept. 20th Further British Advance in which Australians greatly distinguished themselves - Commence reading "The Bancrofts" - a book of delightful Theatrical Reminiscences - and I also read for the first time the "Monkey's Paw".
