<p>69 October 1st<br/>the navy &ndash; especially Commr Hewitt for his persistent attacks on me last Saturday evening &ndash;</p>
<p>Recd 2 letters from Gertie, 1 from Bay &amp; 1 a/c from A &amp; N Stores &ndash; Played Bridge with Commr Olphert &ndash;</p>
<p>Thite left this camp for Heidelberg tonight &ndash; we will miss him as he used to read us extracts from the Daily papers, &amp; was of the greatest service to us all as his experiences during his 2&frac12; years captivity, was of the greatest value &ndash; But we are glad for his sake, as on account of ill-health a change to a more congenial climate was necessary &ndash;</p>
<p>Paid today 46-30 marks.</p>
<p>October 2nd<br/>Had cold shower this mng &ndash; Played Bridge &ndash; a Debate held tonight It was most humorous, as several motions had to be considered by the house, one being that a vote by Censure be passed on the President for abusing his presidential powers, some most eloquent speeches were made in connection with this motion, also for the motion that a vote of Confidence be passed on the president &ndash; a new vice president was elected, <strike>my</strike> I was nominated but withdrew.</p>
<p>There is a chess tournament in progress, I have entered but to date have lost all my games &ndash;</p>
<p>October 3rd<br/>Slept in till 11 am &ndash; no walk today</p>
<p>Played Bridge &ndash;</p>
<p>Recd 4 parcels &amp; 1 letter from Mrs Guthrie. Skipper visited Cornish today, He has been operated upon &amp; is doing fairly well</p>
