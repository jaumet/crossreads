<p>26 29 30 31 27<br/>tea 5 turned in<br/>Monday May 27.5.18<br/>turned out 5.AM fed up Breakfast saddled up and went to Beach washed camels had a good swim nice day dinner returned to camp 4.30.PM  some one had a look at us.  had tea turned in<br/>Tuesday 28 May 5.18<br/>turned out 5.AM. breakfast kit inspection took camels to graze returned 12.  dinner camels to water had a letter from Tessie Bell Alyth two maud Josie.  tea 5 wrote to Tessie Josie maud S<br/>Wednesday May 29.5.18<br/>turned out 5.AM. Breakfast fell in 8.30 bayonet exercises dinner 12 rested till 2.PM done washing tea 4.30 I went on guard on last shift 4 till 6.</p>
