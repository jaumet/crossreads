<p>Tuesday 17th<br/>Planes over again no harm done. Saw one in the Secret light. A very rough little thunder storm early in the morning. Had to make a little dam inside our door way to stop the water running in.  Going out with some Dummie Tanks tonight for a big attack in the morning. Left camp about 8 &amp; loaded the Tanks on the light railway at Tenecourt. They are excellent imitation. Wooden frame covered with canvas. They run on 3 wheels. That was there weak point They had used small</p>
