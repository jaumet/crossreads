<p>There is a certain amount of relief when one hears him get rid of his bombs. &ndash; We have been lucky he seems to have peppered the surrounding villages but we have escaped his attention so far.</p>
<p>Sept 3/17.<br/>Another visit from the Taubes last night in the surrounding neighbourhood</p>
<p><strike>Heard</strike> The Company up at Dickebush has had its first fatal casualty in France. Hogan of the supply being killed by a bomb &amp; two <strike>of</strike> Drivers wounded (Seymour &amp; Turnbull) S. is away at present on a months rest!</p>
