I did not think it possible to see so many Australians busily engaged all over the place at all kinds of work, without striking someone I knew. Im feeling very fit at present, but have a bad heel, through some rough marching. I think Im not far from the spot where old Alan met his death, but things alter as the push goes on, & I have found no one so far, who knows anything about it. However, I will not cease trying to find out. I had a letter from Noel Cuthbert t'other day. & he told me about poor old Nim. I wish I could express to Mrs Cuthbert
