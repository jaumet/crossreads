There were two main air shafts &amp; one shaft which was used for a chimney &amp; it was so constructed that the smoke came out through the chimney of a neighbouring cottage, so designed to mislead the enemy.<br/>Various bones coins &amp; other relics were on view, also monuments placed<br/>to commemorate the visits of different genealogical societies.<br/>Wangled some afternoon tea from some Tommies (2 francs) &amp; it was most welcome as the circulation was turning me up.  Rested in the shade until about 6, then walked back to Villers-Bocage &amp; had tea.  Here we were fortunate enough to pick up a lorry which took us to within 1&frac12; miles of home so we finished fit.  Had a sponge down &amp; turned in feeling well.  Weather good.</p>
