<p>Even the sea seems to have conspired to defraud us of its usual interesting sights.</p>
<p>Occasionally we run into a shoal of porpoises. Flying fish are to be seen skimming over the<strike>water</strike> glassy sea at a terrific speed for such small fish.</p>
