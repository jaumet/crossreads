 Oct 26 arrival of Mail steamer &amp; return of men who went as guard to Sydney with some more german prisoners sent from here 
 Oct 27 arrival of Masina with stores 
 Oct 28 drill &amp; marches are the order, getting stale 
 Oct 29 nothing happens now only the usual Parades until about Nov 10th 
 Nov 10th when we hear that an English missionary has been flogged  by Germans in New Ireland G Coy are to provide 50 men to go &amp; arrest the 6 Germans who took part in the flogging we hear that they have 200 Native Police &amp; are ready for us 
 Nov 11th getting ready for the Trip which is about 100 miles away the Germans call it New Mecklenberg  we call it New Ireland 
 Nov 12th We embark on a small wooden steamer called the Medang bound for a place called Namatinia  all well until 9.30 pm when they discover that she is leaking badly, there is a rough sea on, we fire rockets &amp; burn Blue lights &amp; Turn back  she is making water fast, we have 50 native Police on board &amp; 7 maries [women?]  besides 52 whites, the niggers are calling out &amp; are very frightened as the water is still coming in, the Pumps are working hard some of the boys are sleeping through it all, some men are sick &amp; few realise the danger we are in, our signals
