 February 3rd All things inspected &amp; deficiencies adjusted we marched to Wilton, the townspeople turning out to give us a hearty send off. The officers who had brought us from Australia- Lieuts Heath &amp; Heugh &ndash; said good bye &amp; hoped to be over after us soon. We woke up at Folkestone about 2 o'clock in the morning &amp; were marched to an empty terrace on the marine parade, unfolded our blankets, made pillows of our packs.
