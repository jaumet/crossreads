<p>Wednesday March 13th<br/>Usual work &amp; night<br/>Thursday 14th<br/>Went to Kemmel to see mates nightime working strong post one man killed couple wounded<br/>Friday 15th<br/>Shelled out of dug-outs by enemy went into Kemmel nightime usual work<br/>Saturday 16th<br/>Removed camp to Walverghlem<br/>Sunday 17th<br/>Usual work<br/>Monday 18<br/>Same as usual</p>
