<p>There was a great kick up here last night, for most of the boys got full as expected, &amp; played up top ropes.</p>
<p>The last 2 mornings here have been very foggy a very fine Scotch mist falling till 7 oclock &amp; after that a hot day.</p>
<p>The King is supposed to be in town today, Loutencourt is General Haigs Headquarters so it is a very important place, plenty of cars &amp; swell officers, to much saluting for my liking, &amp; you can't very well dodge it, though we do our best to do it.</p>
