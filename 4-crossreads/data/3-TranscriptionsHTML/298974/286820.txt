<p>France<br/>16-1-17</p>
<p>My Dear Mother,</p>
<p>Here we are out of the line. There has been a long time in which I have not written, but we are not allowed to when we are "in". Well, we spent Xmas &amp; N.Y. "in", and it was not all beer &amp; skittles. However, we are now out and beyond a few things like chill blanes</p>
