
<p>381</p>
<p>MAY 1917</p>
<p>27 SUNDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. I was stableman</p>
<p>all day. Went down to the 3<sup>rd</sup></p>
<p>DAC in evening &amp; saw Poss</p>
<p>Nivison &amp; he is in great form.</p>
<p>Went up to the pits as a</p>
<p>gunner during the night &amp;</p>
<p>we had a raid &amp; fired 134</p>
<p>shells, raid was not all the</p>
<p>success we hoped for.</p>
