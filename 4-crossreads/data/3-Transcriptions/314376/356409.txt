<p>All that I have related here took place part during the early part of 1916 (November). After being relieved by 7 and 8 platoons we came back to the sunken roads dugouts and foraged around for a "possie". I was among the last to reach the dugouts but managed to secure a fairly decent place although it was not worth much.</p>
<p>On the side of the road were dugouts as well but we were told to stop away from there owing to the likelihood of a shell finding us.</p>
