<p>November 1918.<br/>Friday 1</p>
<p>Hosptl. Birmingham<br/>Official news today declares the unconditional surrender of Turkey.<br/>Austria may collapse at any time now, and then perhaps a month will see the v.e.<br/>The food in this place is rather poor considering that it is a hospital.</p>
