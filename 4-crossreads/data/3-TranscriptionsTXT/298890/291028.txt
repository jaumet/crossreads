 8 Sunday Showers &amp; wind.  Went to Vile Church in morning.  Beastly old men sat in front in armchairs.  Tired.  Wrote &amp; studied and walked on beach before supper.  Leaves coming down 
 9 Monday Worked on shorthand &amp; story all day.  Wild &amp; showry 
 10 Tuesday Fine day excepting short thunder shower.  Similar routine to above.  Walked on London road for an hour 
 11 Wednesday Wrote &amp; studied shorthand.  Tired &amp; not very well.  Food not sufficiently nourishing.  Walked to Felpham with Miss Woledge
