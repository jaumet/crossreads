 Naval Signal 
 Ship of Origin - Hebertshohe 
 From - Brigadier 
 To - Berrima 
 At the request of the German Commandant Military Force I have despatched one Motor Lorry with Infantry Scout and one officer to bring in large quantities of ammunition from Toma. &nbsp;Have also sent to Pitapaka Wireless Station two officers and five men to bring in second motor lorry for same purpose if in working order. 1525&nbsp;
