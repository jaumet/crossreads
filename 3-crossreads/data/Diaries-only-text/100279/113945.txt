"No mans land" is covered with German dead they are laying all over the place I cut a fine pair of wire cutters off one chap also brought a rifle, bomb and gas helmet for curios. 
The next thing I had to do was to report to Mr Edgeley, then I had to write a "report" out on my night's work and it was sent down to Hqrs at once, the Capt reckoned I had done
