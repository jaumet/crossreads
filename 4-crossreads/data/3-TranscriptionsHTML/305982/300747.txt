<p>Cold again in fact it is snowing up the line a bit.  trains with snow on them  Hail stones it is that cold that the rain turns into ice same all over the place  Bull ring to day again  through gas &amp; tear shells practice  Germans in hospital near by they look sore &amp; sorry  Hellish cold today  I am horribly chafed about the legs.</p>
<p>19/11/16<br/>Sunday to day did not go to church service</p>
<p>20/11/16 to 24/11/16<br/>Nothing of much interest during the last four days other than the weather has been a little warmer  News of the Hospital ship Brittania being sunk by mine or torpedo</p>
<p>21/11/16<br/>Usual cold weather today</p>
<p>22/11/16<br/>Letter from Mother.  news of another hospital ship being sunk</p>
<p>23/28/11/16<br/>Cold weather during last <strike>few</strike> four days</p>
<p>29/11/16<br/>Nothing doing other than it is now</p>
