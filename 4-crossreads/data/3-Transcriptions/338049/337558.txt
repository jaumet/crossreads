<p>Saturday 14 Dec.<br/>Up at 6 a.m. &amp; after breakfast caught boat to Larne. Train then to Belfast 11a.m. Went through city in jaunting car with a chap I had met. Afternoon went out for a tram ride &amp; during evening knocked about with a couple of friends.</p>
