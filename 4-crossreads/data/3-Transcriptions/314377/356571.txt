<p>At the farm was a dairy The method of churning was as follows. In a big wheel which was revolved by a dog put inside on the principle of a treadmill This dog was very cunning when he saw the master coming along he would go very fast, if the girl &ndash; it would not be so quick His usual method was a regular "government" stroke or tread.</p>
<p>Everyone works in France Horses men women and dogs and children owing I suppose to the great scarcity of men caused by the war.</p>
