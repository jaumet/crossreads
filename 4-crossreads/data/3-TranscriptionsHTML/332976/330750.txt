<p>November 1916<br/>18th   We had a light fall of snow early this morning, (This is the first snow I have seen) &amp; a very cold wind has been blowing all day.  About 9 a.m. it started to rain &amp; <strike>has b</strike> continued until about 3 P.M.  Combined with the wind it has made things very miserable.<br/>I am still putting the culvert in the drain but progress has been slow.  We have seen no aeroplanes up to-day &amp; although our artillery has been bombarding incessantly, Fritz has not troubled us.<br/>19th   After heavy rain last night the mud is just as bad as ever if not worse.  The 5th Division inf.</p>
