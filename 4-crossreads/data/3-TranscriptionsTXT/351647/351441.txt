Alison is well & just the same, I'm glad he got his stripe.  Stewart Walker is here but I haven't seen him.
16 Jan.  Have just got your letter, No. 1.  Thanks so much, it was great.  I haven't much time to write as they are calling out for all mail.  I was much interested in the account of my successor & glad he doesn't appear likely to cut me out.  I often think of the dear old place & you all & wish myself back but there's a lot to be done & gone through yet.
