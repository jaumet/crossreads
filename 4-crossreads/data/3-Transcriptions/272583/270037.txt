<p>Leuit Col. Dobbin presented the prizes for the rifle competion.<br/>Mileage 257.<br/>29th<br/>Very windy but fresh Sea becoming very rough. The air is quite cold at night to-night being the coolest we have had so far.<br/>30th<br/>Still blowing very hard  Sea a bit rough. Expect to land very shortly. <br/>The wind to-night was very severe and during the night it blew a hurricane  I was sleeping on deck and the wind cut like a knife in fact it was impossible to get warm<br/>December 1st<br/>Sea choppy  Air cool. Heavy wind still blowing.<br/>There is land on either side of us. It is rough and sandy  not a tree or bit of green stuff to be seen. But there are plenty of lighthouses and beacons.<br/>Arrived at Suez 12 noon  Dropped anchor at 12.35. There are two warships here. There are also to be seen plenty of sharks in the water near the boat.<br/>Some native craft came<br/> </p>
