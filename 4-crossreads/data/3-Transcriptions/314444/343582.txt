<p>[On letterhead of the Red Cross Society, Moss Vale Branch.]<br/>6th. Feby: 1918.</p>
<p>Dear Mr.Garbutt,</p>
<p>I received the enclosed letter to day and send it on to you with pleasure. I am sure you will be grateful for the further evidence of the esteem in which your son was held by those who were associated with him at the last.</p>
<p>With best wishes, Yours sincerely<br/>Ernest E Row</p>
