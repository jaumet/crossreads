Last night George Clarke of No 3 cook came part of the way with me dead drunk but soon fell off the duckboards into a shell hole where he wallowed and made a perfect beast of himself. We left him behind in a convenient dugout. 
 26-12-16 Out at midnight &amp; work as usual.
