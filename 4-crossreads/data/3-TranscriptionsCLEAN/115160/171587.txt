Things are very easy with us, and we are still detached.  Our mail comes in dribs and drabs and altho' Headquarters of the Column are about 10 kilos away, we hear little indeed of t'other part of the Section.  I expect tho' they will be back up here in a week or so.
The detached part of us with the lorries went to the Column for Xmas Dinner, where there was plenty of beer (crook), turkey and so-called cake.  It wasn't so bad, but not as good as our little turnout last time.  This time last year, you will remember I was in Paris.
