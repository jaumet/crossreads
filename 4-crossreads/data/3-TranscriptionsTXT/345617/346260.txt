 Decembre 4 Went to Steenwerk &amp; Bailleul work shops &amp; try &amp; get some material. Most of the Section are still working on Corps Reserve &amp; Support defences wiring etc. Set-back near Cambrai has not been so serious although a fair lineup of [indecipherable] &ndash; some guns which were turned against us &amp; a good number of prisoners to the enemy. 
 Decembre 5 Section nearly all out wiring. I have been in camp today just looking after a little  more  camp work. Haven't heard anything further from Cambrai.
