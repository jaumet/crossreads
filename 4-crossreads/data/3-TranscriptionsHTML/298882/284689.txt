<p>Sat. 29/9/17  Half Way House</p>
<p>Our Bn came back here last night.  The fight which was very successful along the front attacked is now over for us, for a little while at any rate.  The poor chaps were dead beat but turned in wherever they could find cover without a murmur.  Fritz shelled this camp heavily during the night and wounded a couple of our men.
