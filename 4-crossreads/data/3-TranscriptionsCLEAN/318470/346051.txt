  
 7th January 1917 Still on Road Supervising.  Cold & wet.  Things going well.  Nothing fresh going. 
 8th Things going well on Road work.  A good deal of shelling going on. 
 9th Transferred from 14th F.C. Engrs. to 1st Anzac Light Railway Operation Corps. 
 10th Took up duty with C.L.R.  Things very much mixed up at present.  Expect to go better before long. 
 11th Been having a very strenuous time.  Things are very much mixed up owing to overlapping of the old & new formations. 
 12th Things are not working at all smoothly. Having a rough time trying to cope with work. 
 13th Still doing plenty of work trying to get things going on the Railway work.
