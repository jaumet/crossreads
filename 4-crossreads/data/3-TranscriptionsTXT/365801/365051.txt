 You were asking me to send along snapshots, but we are not allowed to carry cameras.  I got yours &amp; gathered from [indecipherable] that the German prisoners were having too much of a good thing.  We have heard stories here about the treatment they deal out to our men, which will not improve matters for themselves if we get into their trenches, which I hope we will do. I will write again in a day or two. Your affectionate son Arthur
