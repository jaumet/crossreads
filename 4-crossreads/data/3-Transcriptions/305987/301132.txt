<p>Tuesday 22nd:  Transferred to No. 1 A.G.H Palace<br/>Wednesday 23rd:  Operated on. Tonsils out.<br/>Wednesday March 1st:  Discharged.<br/>Monday April 3rd:  Left Heliopolis. Arrived Tel-el-Kebir same day.<br/>Sunday May 28th:  Embarked on "Megantic" at Alexandria<br/>Monday 29th:  Sailed mid-day.<br/>Saturday June 3rd:  Arrived at Gibraltar. Sailed midnight<br/>Wednesday 7th:  Arrived at Plymouth 6 a.m. Reached Perham Downs same day.<br/>June 30th:  Left Perham Downs for Rolleston</p>
