 [Sunday 24 December 1916] Magdhaba Xmas eve &amp; our job is to help clean up the Battlefield collect rifles &amp; ammunition Bury the dead, succour the wounded &amp; burn what we cannot take away- Any amount of loot- Oil engine pumps ordnance. Two Armenian Doctors help our own- We go back to El Arish today- This fight about finishes the Turks in SINIA for some time anyway- I just got back in time to help capture the guns that wounded our men in August. 3rd night without a Blanket freezing cold but all hands cheerful at result of Battle.
