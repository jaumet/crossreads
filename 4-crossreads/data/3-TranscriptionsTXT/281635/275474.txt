 7/7/18 Recd letters from Mother, Amanda &amp; Mrs Brown. Still feeling very off &amp; cannot eat. 8/7/18 Feeling much better. Had first food for 60 hours. Very hot. 9/7/18 Much cooler. Wrote home &amp; to Uncle Fred 10/7/18 Wrote to Mrs Brown. 11/7/18 Up this afternoon for first time. Head still sore &amp; a buzzing in the ears. last Friday met a Froggy up on top of hill at back of camp &amp; he told me the three mounds are for French soldiers who died about 1839.
