
14 February 1916Monday
A day of surprises & unfortunate happenings.Strike by soldiers at Casula & Liverpool for shorter hours of drill.Procession from Casula  after breakfast to Liverpool. Broke up hotels & cleared out the drink.Lighthorsemen came out after dinner. Grave position & [indecipherable] disgrace to the Army.Thousands go to Sydeny & play havoc there. Tony, Bob & self into Liverpool to view damage.  Meet Bob Abernethy. Concert at Methodist Tent. Hotels wrecked & refreshment [indecipherable]
