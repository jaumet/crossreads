<p>20th<br/>Returned to unit &amp; afterwards shifted to 1st Relay, did a little fatigue.</p>
<p>21st<br/>On water fatigue, went into village of Hermies.</p>
<p>22nd<br/><strike>11th Div Field Amb. relieved us in the line at 1 p.m.</strike>  Went back into line at Hermies, very quiet all day save for a bit of shrapnel which came over at intervals.  At night big demonstration for half an hour.  Taube brought down into our lines at 8 p.m.  Raid (aerial) on Cambrai by 22 of our planes , all returned.</p>
<p>23rd<br/>11th Div. Field Amb. relieved us.  Duke of Wellington Light Infantry relieved Infantry at midnight.</p>
<p>Went back to La Bouchigne.  Fritz shelled it all day.  Left at 4 p.m. for Bapaume.</p>
