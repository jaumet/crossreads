<p>January 1918<br/>Sunday 13<br/>At Beutin<br/>Gun [Gunn?] returned, back at stables. Marched to Church Parade. Divisional sports decided at Longvillers Battery one Tug-o-War, Broad Jumps, 400 yard championships , and Corbett was beaten by an inch in the Sheffield.<br/>The weathr throughout was rotten, - raining hard, and making everything sloppy. <br/>Received a letter from smith who is in Paris. He seems to be having a pretty decent time.</p>
