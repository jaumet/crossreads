5-8-19)6-8-19)Still the same - am attended on almost night & day with an attention that is beyond comprehension till secret is revealed
7-8-19Still the same - state - if matters do not improve I shall have to call in a Doctor.
To night my little friend who has wished to give me a substantial birthday present has invited me to Opera Comique where one plays what is known [indecipherable] as Grand Opera which here is reserved for the Opera. The seats are excellent & cost only 10 fr but there is a box for poor, a war tax - a supplement - 2 fr for booking & in sum one pays 15 francs.
