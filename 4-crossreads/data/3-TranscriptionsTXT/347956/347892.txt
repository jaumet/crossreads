
19.2.15
get back to camp till late went nowhere after tea but stayed in the tent & spinning yards
Fri 19thWas sent to A Sqd. again this morning as the whole division was out on a scheme all day & were handed out dummy cartridges to fight the New Zealanders with;  we had dinner about two oclock & had the battle shortly afterwards.  It was nearly dark when we returned to camp; & was rather weary, as we had a lot of running during the afternoonwent nowhere after tea & went to bed early
