<p>21) The landing</p>
<p>But it Did not Come Back for us, much to our Dis<s>s</s>appointment&nbsp; at night we were Still on Board. There we were waiting to land&nbsp; Everything ready for the fray. We had on our Backs Bundles of wood as we believed wood was very scarce ashore. while we were <s>a</s> waiting and watching the Shore there flashed out a Signal that [told?] us that our Boys had gained the Hill&nbsp; at this we cheered and cheered untill our throats were Sore from Shouting&nbsp; rain it Did that night and on our Ship you could see the restless</p>
