<p>Sept<br/>miles. Nice cool day so came home quite fresh Concert at YM more high class than any of the Camp tournouts I have been to.</p>
<p>Sunday 23rd<br/>Open air Service to day. Walked up to top of hill in front of Camp &amp; examined Riseing Sun &amp; other badges. The turf is cut out the right shape &amp; filled in with chalk They look very well. Especially the sun it has been layed out very well &amp; is about 50 yds across the base Had an extra good tea in the vilage Ham &amp; Egg 1/8&frac12; with Tea &amp; bread &amp; butter</p>
<p>Monday 24th<br/>Lice were found</p>
