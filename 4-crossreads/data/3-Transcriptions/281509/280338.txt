<p>1916 April 25<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Anzac Day. Holiday in camp. Sports. Did not go. Party went for walk up line. Very hot, back for dinner. Fatigue work on motor wagon drawing stores. Go to pictures at night &ndash;peanuts, cake &amp; beer. Clever club swinged.</p>
