<p>Sunday 16th June : Dunkirk</p>
<p>Went to Church at night : nice service &amp; about 50 there : yarn with the Minister's daughter &amp; got an invitation out to tea :</p>
<p>Monday June 17th : Dunkirk</p>
<p>On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : shifted to our new camp after tea : not too bad but too far for the 12 hours a day idea :</p>
<p>Tuesday June 18th : Mardyk [Mardyck]</p>
<p>Not booked for any duty so wrote a few letters : felt a bit off colour at dinner time so went to bed : I expect it is the "flues" that are going about :</p>
<p>Wednesday June 19th : Mardyk camp :</p>
<p>Went on sick parade &amp; go no duty : feeling pretty crook</p>
<p>Thursday June 20th : Mardyk</p>
<p>No duty again so stayed in bed : felt a bit better so got up after tea</p>
<p>Friday June 21st : Mardyk</p>
<p>Feeling pretty good but still groggy :</p>
<p>Saturday June 22nd : Mardyk</p>
<p>Feeling pretty good so went for a stroll :</p>
<p>Sunday June 23rd : Mardyk</p>
<p>On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : stroll afternoon &amp; the country here looks splendid : we can just see Cassel in the distance with its prominent windmills :</p>
<p>Monday June 24th : Mardyk</p>
<p>On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : stroll after tea</p>
<p>Tuesday June 25th : Mardyk</p>
<p>On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : felt a bit off so stayed in the hut after going back home</p>
