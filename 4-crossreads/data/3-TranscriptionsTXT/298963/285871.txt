 while the rank &amp; file bleed in the cold wet trench with scarcely enough to wear at times.  I used to be on the side of the officer &ndash; once &ndash; seems however make one think &amp; now I am for the private always. 
 26th Aug. Nothing has happened yet.  I am lying here still, no more enamoured to English hospital than I was yesterday.  Today the "quack" said "Well whats wrong with you?" 
 I said I don't know.  He said "what are you suffering from?"  I said, shell shock, gass &amp; wounds.
