<p><u>November</u></p>
<p>The reports also show that we have taken two large town, Valenciennes and Tourney.</p>
<p>The weather is getting very cold lately, All the trees have lost their leaves and the country looks very bare and wintry looking.</p>
<p>The reports of the 4th of November are that Austria-Hungary has concluded an armistice with Italy, It is not mentioned that England and France have been included in the Armistace but I expect they have.</p>
<p>The defeat of Turkey and Austria-Hungary has had a very demoralizing effect on the German people and Army.</p>
<p>I expect that Germany will give in very soon. I hope that the Allies will refuse to give her the Armistice that she is asking for. If Germany gets the Armistice it will save the country from ruin &amp; revolution,</p>
