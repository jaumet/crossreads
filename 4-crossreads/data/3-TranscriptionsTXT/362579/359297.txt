 C/O Major Cunningham Aust. Base P.O. Horseferry Rd, London, 3.6.17 (12.10 a.m.) 
 My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters, 
 Was so pleased to get Al's letter dated March 25th about 2 days ago. I also received one from Mr Balfour, Alice Bower   &amp; I this Annie Thomas. I was beginning to think all my friends had given me up.
