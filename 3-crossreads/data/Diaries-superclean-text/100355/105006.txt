Monday 11th Nov I spent in London it being armistice day. Tuesday I left for Notts, returning to London on the 21st. I reported to Sutton Very on the 26th Nov. Left Sutton Very on Friday 6th Dec arriving at Boulogne via Folkstone on 7th going the same night to Camiers, M.G. Base.
