<p>October.<br/>Oct. 1st<br/>Our Engine &amp; Dynamo being under repair we deem it inadvisable to show &ndash; A visit to the "Pedlars" (a neighbouring British Council Party) &ndash; A new poem &amp; two new concerted items written.</p>
<p>Oct. 2nd<br/>Bulgaria no longer a belligerent &ndash; Unconditional surrender &ndash; Depression throughout Germany &ndash;<br/>Rehearsal &amp; read "Shelley" &ndash; Letters answered.</p>
