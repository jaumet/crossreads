<p>I was later on after admittance and upon request allowed to have a course of bootmaking in the Red Cross Workshops. This was a good idea for I was soon able to repair boots fairly well.</p>
<p>We were visited by different people some Australians and officers. I take it belonging to the Administrative Staff Sir Joseph Cook also visited us. We were visited by Mr. Hughes as well who made a speech and shook hands</p>
