<p>May Tuesday 29 1917</p>
<p>Transport duty today. Early breakfast &amp; walk thro' fog into Boulogne at 7 a.m.   Motor lorry load of  goods &amp; medical stores. huge bulk stores,  Lunch  in town at canteen<br/>off duty at 2 p.m. for rest of day.  Read a bit &amp; write a few letters</p>
