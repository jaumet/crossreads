<p>Friday 4 August 1916<br/>Still summer weather. During the morning was kept busy with clerical work &amp; in the afternoon lectured to all company Gas N.C.O.s, choosing a grassy spot close to the main road under the shade of some tall poplars. Writing home in the evening, several home letters having come to hand during the day.</p>
