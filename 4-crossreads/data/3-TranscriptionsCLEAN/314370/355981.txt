
February, 1916Sunday 13Write letter home in the forenoon.  It is so long since I have heard from mum that I find it hard to put anything into letters.After dinner take dad to the Zoo, & see the new wonders of a hippo & several splendid Giraffes.Have dinner in Cairo & return at 8.30 pm
Monday 14Peg away at the care & cleaning subject; the men a very keen to learn.Have great hopes for them.
