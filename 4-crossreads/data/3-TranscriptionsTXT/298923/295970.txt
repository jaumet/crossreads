
31 October 1918 ThursdayStillmanMother's letter with enclosure from HLA dated Aug 18thI hope you will let Bronte for the summer too hot to take Babe & Dolly there for a startVery glad to think Joe will be home when I come- can't say anything definite yetSnakebite Antidote came along safely but no one lives in the Jordan Valley nowI enclose cutting from Times almost verbatim what I wrote you in my Diary re J.V.
