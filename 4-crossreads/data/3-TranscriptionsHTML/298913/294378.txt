<p>21/3/16<br/>Dream am alive with Chats &amp; awake to find that two drunken sergts are crawling around outside looking for diamonds with matches. Hell of a day</p>
<p>22/3/16<br/>Payday &ndash; as usual with a great deal of messing about. Try to write letters at night but Bon Gow's voice too continuous.</p>
<p>23/3/16<br/>Pay at last  Have a bit of a splash in Cairo after tea using blue band of signal service as a pass. Pay a visit to the Y.M.C.A. &amp; have a good feed.</p>
<p>24/3/16<br/>Hot as Hell &amp; flies very bad</p>
<p>25/3/16<br/>Pay a visit to Heliopolis with Hudson &amp; take a few photos. Then go to Museum</p>
