<p>We have to report to Bayud again on the 5th inst. Caught train at Bir el abd at 2.20 p.m arriving at Kantara 6.30 p.m.. As I wished to do some private business at Port Said, I parted from the others at Kantara &amp; caught the 10.10 p.m. train there, arriving at port Said about mid-night.<br/>Mch 2nd After satisfactorily concluding business caught the 12 o'clock (mid-day) train to Cairo, where I arrived at about 5.15 p.m. Went straight to the National Hotel &amp; found most of the lads there.</p>
