<p>March 1918<br/>Sunday 17</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Went down to F.O.O. at 6.30 am with rations and etc. <br/>Spent all day until 7 pm at the O.P. Battery doing some good sniping. Some beautiful bursts by M.T.M.<br/>7.15 pm Seven Fritzer planes sailed over our lines but were driven back by A.A.F..<br/>At 10.30 pm Fritz put down a barrage of "Mustard" shells and shelling, and shelling Left Batt fairly solidly all the while for at least half an hour.<br/>A "pigeon" bomb lobbed in a W.C. possie of 32nd Batt, 5 men became casualties.</p>
