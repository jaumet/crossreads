 Saturday  22nd Food not too good.  Could see hydroplanes patrolling channel also balloons &amp; destroyers as well.  Later in morning had a beautiful view of the whole cliffs of England and made us wish we were there.  They really seemed about 5 miles away.  The main portion of camp was struck in the morning.  At night we walked to Wissant &amp; had good look at the village.  It was excellent.  Easily the prettiest village I have seen.  Belgium Soldiers billeted in village &ndash; looking very neat &amp; clean.  Sir Doug Haig inspected the Div.
