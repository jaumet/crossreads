<p>Bay at 8 am on the 20th. Here we transhipped aboard the Campenolla. There are small parties from nearly every battalion of the 1st and 2nd Aust. Divisions aboard here 20.12.15.</p>
<p>We went ashore at Sarpi Camp last night but only expect to remain here a few days 21.12.15</p>
<p>Went to Thurma today and had a grand bath in the hot springs there. On returning to camp we found our Xmas billies and pudding awaiting us 22.12.15</p>
