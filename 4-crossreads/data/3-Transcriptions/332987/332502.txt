<p>2-10-1918<br/>Still in train, making for the town of Taranto, which is at the gulf of Taranto.  Weather changed, raining.</p>
<p>3-10-1918<br/>Nearing train journey's end.  Arrived at Taranto rest camp 4 p.m. after 8 days and 7 nights on train.  8 p.m. warned for baggage guard, up all night.</p>
<p>4-10-1918<br/>On board boat Ormonde, 15,000 tons.  All Sgts. in cabins.</p>
<p>5-10-1918<br/>Raining.  No word of sailing.</p>
<p>6-10-1918<br/>Taking on board 250 English Officers, bound for India.  Now on board approximately 2,000 troops.  Boat stations and life belt drill this morning.</p>
