<p>Six of the 2nd Reg. were caught by 30 turks when patrolling yesterday afternoon this fellow was hit  &amp;  they brought him under cover over their saddles.<br/>We pumped 10 rounds from a battery of 18 pdrs onto Oghratina  &amp;  then came home reaching here about 12 oclock. Stand to again at 3 - I shall sleep well today.<br/>24th July<br/>Went out to Katia again at 2 am yesterday morning  &amp;  came back again at midnight. We all lay on our backs for about half an hour in the morning watching</p>
