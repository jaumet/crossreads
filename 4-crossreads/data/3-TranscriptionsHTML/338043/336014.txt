<p>Nov. 11th<br/>As it is decreed that we must rise at 5 a.m., we do so, and are fortunate enough to see a wonderful &amp; inspiring "red dawn". Could there be a more fitting &amp; majestic herald of /to the joyous tidings which we receive a little later. At 11 a.m. hostilities cease, the Armistice has to be signed, and after four years of War &amp; bloodshed, during which the Divine Laws of the Almighty have been/are ruthlessly corrupted, "Right prevails over Might &ndash; and &ndash; Honour,<br/>Truth,<br/>[added in a different ink] MISERY &amp; DISTRESS</p>
