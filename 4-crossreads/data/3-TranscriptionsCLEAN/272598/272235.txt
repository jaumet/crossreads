 Thursday 20.9.17 Good number of slightly wounded coming through C.C.Ss, from all accounts stunt was a great success.  Orders through to stand by.  Given 20 minutes warning & left by motor lorries at 1.30 pm for the line  Slept in dug-outs some distance back from the line for the night.  All objectives gained this morning.
