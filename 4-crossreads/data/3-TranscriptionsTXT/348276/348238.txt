 War Diary 1915 
 [Sketch of a typical pattern of support and fire trenches] 
 Showing useful type where men have to occupy &ndash; self contained &ndash; strength weak. 
 No two patterns of trenches are the same &ndash; no one pattern is possible &ndash; Accommodate Resting men near their firing positions &ndash; Sqn. cook house handy &ndash; Recesses here and there for everything.  In Paradoss such as  spare ammo., Bombs; Flares. 
 Label everything and every place name Roads and alleys. 
 [Sketch] Biscuit Box partitioned for various types of Bombs:  They should be replenished and cleaned daily.
