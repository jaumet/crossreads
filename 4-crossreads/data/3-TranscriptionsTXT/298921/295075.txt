 April, 1915 Monday 12 Received papers from Ferrar &amp; have just read that Heliopolis is known in the Bible as "ON" see Genesis Xll 45. re Pharoah &amp; Joseph.  Many troops have left us. Papers from Joe. Grazier Pastoral Times &ndash; Sorry drought still so bad note HLA may buy property near Hay &ndash; I expect I shall be back some day &amp; might be glad of a job if he wants a man.
