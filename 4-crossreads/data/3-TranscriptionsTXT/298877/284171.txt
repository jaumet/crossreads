 Friday 8th Jan. 1915 Nothing of interest 
 Saturday 9th Jan. 1915 Nothing of interest 
 Sunday 10th Jan. 1915 Nothing of interest 
 Monday 11th Jan. 1915 Crossed the line this morning. 3 P.M. another horse died today 
 Tuesday 12th Jan. 1915 13 of the boats left us today, going to Colombo, we &amp; the other two White Star boats are going to coal up at Aden. 
 Wednesday Jan 13th 1915 Passed two Indian Troopships to-day. 
 Thursday 14th Jan 1915 Passed the Maldive group of islands. Inhabitants are black, we passed quite close. Saw a party of them putting out to sea in their fishing smacks. 
 Friday 15th Jan. 1915. Nothing of interest. 
 Saturday 16th Jan. 1915. Holding a concert to-night, conducted in first-class style. 
 Sunday 17th Jan 1915. Nothing of interest. 
 Monday 18th Jan. 1915 Passed another island today, a very rugged coast.
