<p>29/5/17<br/>Coy. played 20th Div. Gloucesters A.S.C. Rugby League &amp; won 3 to nil.  Rough game.  3 of Gloucesters had to leave field.</p>
<p>30th<br/>Sec. digging trenches near Villa de Flos.  Other Secs. doing the same.</p>
<p>31st<br/>Still on "Strong points".  Doing a little training.</p>
<p><strike>1st</strike> 1/5 [17]<br/>Nothing fresh going.  Training &amp; Strong.</p>
<p>2nd<br/>Training &amp; diging trenches but having easy time.</p>
<p>3rd<br/>Same as yesterday.</p>
<p>4th<br/>Nothing fresh going.  Having easy time.</p>
<p>5th<br/>No. 2 Sec. played No. 3 &amp; won with two wickets.  Nothing fresh.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Coy. had route march today about 5 miles.  Played 8th F. Coy. Soccer &amp; won.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Working on Sports ground &amp; doing camp work &amp; a little training.  Played a Tommy A.S.C. &amp; won easily.</p>
<p>9/6/17<br/>Had Div. sports today &amp; 14th F. Coy. came out on top with 47 points to 15th Coys. 36.  8th F. got 23 &amp; Sigs. 22, got Silver Cup.</p>
<p>10th<br/>A little squad drill &amp; Coy. Fatigues.  Nothing of importance to note.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Good news from North  British &ndash; including our 3rd &amp; 4th Divs. &amp; N. Zealanders advanced on a 9 mile front taking a lot of prisoners &amp; a good many guns.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Still good news from Front North of Armentiers.  All Enemy counter attacks beaten off.  Lost Soccer to 15th yesterday.</p>
<p>13th<br/>Having general clean-up today preparatory to moving out.</p>
<p>14th<br/>Getting ready to move out tomorrow.  Played 15th Rugby today &amp; won 14 to 9.  Some more ground won near Arras.</p>
