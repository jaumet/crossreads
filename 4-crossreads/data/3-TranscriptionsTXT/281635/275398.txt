Issued with 1 blanket &amp; then marched 7 kilometres to the village of Wizernes to billets &amp; with 24 others was billeted in a farmer's shed by 10.30pm. Issued with cup of tea each. 2/9/17 Slept soundly &ndash; 3 of us in 1 bed &amp; rose 8am. Breakfast then shaved. Billet is wooden framework filled in with sand and straw plaster. Had good feed blackberries. Tasted green walnuts for first time &ndash; rather pleasant to taste. 3/9/17 Fine day. Inspected by Colonel then summoned to new billet &ndash; 8 of us in a stable. Squad drill etc in afternoon. Heard gun-fire all last night like muffled thunder &amp; all during the day as wind changed. Saw several aeroplanes &amp; an observation balloon.
