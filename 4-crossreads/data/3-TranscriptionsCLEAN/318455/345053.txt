 Oct 14 Kantarah Still on board ship We reached here at dark the last mile or so we were passing one huge Base Camp - an endless series of stacks of  fodder & cases  bales of fodder & bags & cases of supplies. This is  I believe  the main base for Palestine & also I believe for Salonica. It is a monotonous looking place to look at this morning
