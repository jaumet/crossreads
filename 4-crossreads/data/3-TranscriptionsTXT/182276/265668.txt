 [Page 1 is front cover] Daily Intelligence Report 9th. Machine Gun Coy. 1st. December 1916 &ndash; 24th. February 1917. 
 1 The attached intelligence reports were compiled daily at 6 a_m. from information received from section Officer in the line. 2 These reports were forwarded by [Driver?] at 6.30 to Company Hdqrs, and also to Brigade Hdqrs. 3 Each report contains information as to work carried out by the various guns in the sector and report of any enemy activity noted during the 24hours.
