<p>7th January '19  up feeling fairly bright &amp; at 10 was on my way to Charleroi per car with C Section medical gear to hand over to medical stores also Mj Birch &amp; two females who were getting a buckshee trip there.  Had two punctures on the road &amp; arrived about 1.  Had a dinner of Beef (horse) &amp; chips coffee &amp; bread for 4&frac12; francs.  Then visited an Anatomy museum open to both sexes over 18.
