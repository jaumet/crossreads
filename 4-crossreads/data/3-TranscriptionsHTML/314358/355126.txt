<p>Crash!</p>
<p>It is only the fall of brickwork. It comes to the mind of each of us that the explosion seems dangerously close to the secret lair of our twelve pounder.</p>
<p>We hold our peace in the shattered darkness.</p>
<p>If the shell has had the luck to find where the grand old gun is placed, nuzzling grimly beneath a flimsy covering of splintered timbers and sacking dragged from the wreckage and rubbish heaps of ruin to disguise, - well, it is but the fortune of war.</p>
