This afternoon we had an inspection of feet, for I believe we are going on a long march tonight or early tomorrow morning we also had the band up here this afternoon, it was a lovely day it put one in mind of being in a park for the piece of land was covered with lovely green grass where we were laying about, & bonzer trees were scattered all over it & plenty of fruit trees were among them while on all sides of us were tall trees green with leaves from the bottom to top, they look splendid.
