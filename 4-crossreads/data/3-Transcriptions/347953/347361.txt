<p>Our camp is just outside Heliopolis situated on a desert of alluvial soil rather than sand as they call it here. We have every convenience as regards mess, &amp; leave but we feel horribly unclean in this dirty sand.</p>
<p>Today I met some of my old school pals who came in the first contingent.</p>
<p>The natives here are peculiar in manners &amp; everywhere in the streets the little youngsters want to brush your boots for &frac12; piastre. They crawl under the table when you are having meals &amp; clean your boots &amp; then ask for money</p>
