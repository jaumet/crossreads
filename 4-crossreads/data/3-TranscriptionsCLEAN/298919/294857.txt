This vessel was in Colombo when we were at that port.  Sunday 15th August 1915.   Awakened about 5am by sailors & mates walking about the decks, blowing of the whistle, etc.  At 5.30am we cast free, & steamed down the river arriving off the entrance at 6am.  Here the river Pilot handed over to the North Sea pilot who will pilot us to Deal (if we get thro').  As she sailed during my watch hours I crawled out and cut in.
