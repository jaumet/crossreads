<p>Nov 22nd &ndash; Wednesday</p>
<p>A beautifully fine day but very cold. Sleep in until 7-30 am. Late breakfast on a/c of heat being off. Write several letters &amp; at 7.30 pm "Whist drive" in theatre. I don't take part in it. Still awaiting developments Doctor seems to do nothing.</p>
<p>Nov 23rd &ndash; Thursday</p>
