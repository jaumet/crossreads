<p>OCTOBER &nbsp; WEDNESDAY 11 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine but windy &amp; cloudy. Carting ashes &amp; chalk all the morning and loading manure ect in afternoon. They finished off a few events left over from last Saturdays sports &amp; the 27<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;won the Kicking the football.</p>
