 1917 Friday 7 Sept. 
 A cloudy night. Fritz never plagued us with this at  [indecipherable]  Patients fairly well. Pathenden &amp; Burt only two seriously ill. Former developed pneumonia but is a splendid patient &amp; is fighting well. Parcel from Jessie neice of sox &amp; booklet from J. Hunt &ndash;  Also received Alice's parcel few days ago.
