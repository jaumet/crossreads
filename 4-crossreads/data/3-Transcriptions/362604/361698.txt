<p>Mum carrying George about in a shawl at Mount Pleasant &amp; I remember all the crowd trying to making me kiss a little Girl, old Waites the fruiterer's Niece or something.  I have been doing a lot of cycling &amp; cricket etc. so my wait is down a bit but I usually go 11.2.  You are much heavier than of yore.  I think you generally went about 11.7.  We are always pleased to hear that you have company.  It would be very dull out there alone always.  Food is short over here.  In Scotland they only one 1 lb Tin of Jam a month &amp; sometimes, in England &amp; Scotland they can't get any for a longer period.  It is very very hot to-day.  I tried to write last night but I had to go</p>
