<p>Sunday 21st April<br/>Had tea with Aunt S. found Mrs Matthews. Very nice people live about a mile away</p>
<p>Tuesday 23rd April<br/>Just an ordinary day except for getting 4 Aussie letters</p>
<p>Wednesday 24th April<br/>Got the sad news that I am to move tomorrow Letters from Ann dated 18/3 Farewell concert in evening &amp; we gave Master Cotton a silver backed C &amp; Brush cost us 9 each it was quite nice</p>
<p>Thursday 25th April<br/>Left Quix</p>
