<p>Thur. Mar. 9th -16<br/>Made Cook's Fatigue for the day.  Peeling spuds carrots etc.</p>
<p>Fri. Mar. 10th -16<br/>1st Division Artillery assembled 4 P.M. on account of presentation of medals to a driver and a gunner in recognition of their bravery in saving an officer's wife at Alexandria.</p>
<p>Mon. Mar. 13th -16<br/>All batteries of 2nd Bde. out for battery manouveres on Saharah desert.  Clouds of dust and boiling hot sun</p>
<p>[Page 22]</p>
<p>Tues. Mar. 14th -16<br/>Gun-laying tests all day.</p>
<p>Thur. Mar. 16th -16<br/>Warned to be ready to leave at any notice.</p>
<p>Fri. Mar. 17th -16<br/>Commencement of Gun  firing practice.  Excellent results.  6th Bty. easy winners.</p>
<p>Sat. Mar. 18th -16<br/>Going out for more practice and when about half way, were ordered back to pack up to leave same night for Alexandria.</p>
<p>Mon. Mar. 20th -16<br/>Orders cancelled for leaving, everybody disappointed.</p>
