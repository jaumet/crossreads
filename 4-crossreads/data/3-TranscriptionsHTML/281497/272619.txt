<p>While all this was going on an aeroplane flew pretty low down over us &amp; blew the cock-a-doodle-do on her horn again &amp; again this sort of cheered us up.</p>
<p>We had a fine tea tonight just before we left the firing line the cooks brought it up as much steak &amp; bacon as you could eat &amp; to spare, tea, boiled potatoes &amp; onions mashed together, this was the first square meal we had for some days &amp; we enjoyed it.</p>
<p>Its wonderful how they kept the supply of food up to us during this fearful bombardment.</p>
