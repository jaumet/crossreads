
Committee was formed in the Camp for purpose of celebrating Anzac Day. This was done, and the evenings entertainment took the form of a social evening, participated in by members of the 4th   & 5th A.I.F. bakeries, and members of the W.A.A.C. of the bakery, and a pleasant evening was spent by all.
1 man rejoined unit from hospital.
26/4/18.39 N.C.O.'s and men baked 23,568 lb rations. Owing to a slight fall in the issue, the 10 pm to 6 a.m. shift did no baking.
27/4/18.58 N.C.O's and men baked 39,600 lb rations. 1 man rejoined unit from A.A.S.C. Reinf Depot. Ex Inv to Eng.
28/4/18. 39 N.C.O's and men baked 23,240 lb rations  Sunday.  The usual change was effected in shifts, there being no work done by the 2 pm to 10 pm shift.
