<p>19<br/>the parapets.</p>
<p>Amongst the brave men in the battle field those who tend the wounded should have special praise</p>
<p>If I wrote as I could of things as they are the Censor would only blacken out, and perhaps no good end would come of it.</p>
<p>This soldering is too cramping to the mind, I think most thoughtful men find it so, but of necessity it must be so when one considers the average capacity of human beings</p>
