 Wednesday 30 May 
 Lovely Day. Tomorrow our party is due for a trip to Amiens.   4 German Prisoners today escaped from the Prisoners Compound in Bapaume. The A.D.M.S. inspected our camp today &amp; was so impressed by the gardens we have made all round the tents and throughout the grounds that tomorrow he intends bring General Tivey along. Met Bill Cannon today.
