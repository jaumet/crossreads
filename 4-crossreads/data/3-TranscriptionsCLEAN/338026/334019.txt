
[16 Th]Mounted piquet at 6 pm.
[17 Fri]Grooming, and fixing the jumps etc. for our sports tomorrow.
[18 Sat]Sports started at 1pm but had to prospond them on account of rain  3.30 pm harnessed mules into mess cart and took Lieut Mar's [kit?] to rest camp near Longchamps. Arr there just on sunset. Tied mules to trees and slept under [cart?] as it was a lovely night
