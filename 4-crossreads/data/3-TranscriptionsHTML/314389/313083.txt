<p>January 2nd:<br/>Arrived Bombay 8 a.m.  Sail 4 p.m.</p>
<p>January 3rd:<br/>Quiet day.</p>
<p>January 4th:<br/>Fire alarm practice.</p>
<p>January 5th:<br/>Quiet day.</p>
<p>January 6th:<br/>Quiet day.</p>
<p>January 7th:<br/>Arrived Aden 7 a.m.  Left 2 p.m.</p>
<p>January 8th:<br/>Sports aboard.  Concert in evening on Second Saloon deck.</p>
<p>January 11th:<br/>Arrived Suez 6 p.m.  Disembarked.  Left by train from wharf 12.30 p.m.  Ride in train, arrive in Camp about midnight.  Aerodrome Camp, Heliopolis.  Keystone speed and Keystone carriages.</p>
<p>January 12th:<br/>New quarters &ndash; jumble up day.</p>
