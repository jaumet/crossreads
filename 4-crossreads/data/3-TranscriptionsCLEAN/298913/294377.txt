No satisfaction can be given if enquiries are instituted. Our troop funds should now total about 150, but it too either disappeared or else it may  be lying some where. It should be spent in the troop.
17/3/16Dust storm & heat, followed by heavy rain that soaks through the tent. At midnight have such a happy family in the tent that I cannot move.
18/3/16Saturday. Have a glorious sleep all the afternoon & night.
19/3/16Sunday. Another loaf all day & sleep. Write few letters & take few snaps.
20/3/16Cold night that makes us shiver all night, followed by beautiful morning. Early morning (2/30 am) moon shining
