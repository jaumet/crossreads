 Friday 16.6.16 Camp taken over by 14th F. Amb.  Remained in vicinity of Camp till 9 pm, when we moved away to Moascar Siding, where we entrained on open goods trucks & left about 10.45 amid great rejoicing. 
 Saturday 17/6/16 Spent rather a cool night on the goods truck; arriving at the end of our journey about 7 AM, none the worse for our trip.  After a considerable delay we embarked & were stowed right below in very unhealthy quarters.
