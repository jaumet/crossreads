<p>Sunday 30 June<br/>Stable routine much the same as usual. Marching order parade 9-15. Swim afternoon.</p>
<p>Evening engaged on fodder matter. 4 days ration 260 lbs oats &amp; 1 bale of hay (11 horses). Report submitted.</p>
<p>Home very late amid air raid.</p>
