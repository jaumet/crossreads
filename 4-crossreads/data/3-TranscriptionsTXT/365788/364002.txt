 2 Ireland four days &amp; went to Killarney which I thought very lovely. Nancy Norris gave me &pound; 15- from you dear. It was very very good of you old girl &amp; I am very grateful. I had a lovely Holiday &amp; went &amp; saw Mrs Elsmie &amp; her daughter &amp; very charming they were. Had dinner there. Also had tea with Mrs Norris &amp; Nancy. What a dear old lady Mrs Norris is. Barney Somerville &amp;amp I had our Photos taken in Dublin 12 for 1/6d &ndash; awfully cheap &amp;amp not half bad. I enclose one for you, one for Trish &amp; one for Arch with my love &amp; Fred the two brothers one for you -
