
Nov. 22nd, WednesdayWe leave this pleasant little camp on Friday morning I believe.  I, for one, am very sorry:  really it has been an ideal little place as things go out here.  However - we are going about 20-25 miles further out to Bir el Mazar a place about 20 miles from El Arish, where I suppose we shall have to "stand to" in the grey light of early dawn every day - a miserable proceeding.  There is no humour in turning out at that hour.  Also we shall have perpetual lights out & other unpleasant messes.I wonder if Marcus Aurelius ever had to do this sort of thing?
