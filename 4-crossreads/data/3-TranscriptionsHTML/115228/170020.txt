The 3rd L.H.Regt. supported by the 2nd Regt. &amp; in conjunction with the Infantry attacked positions on our right (Bald Hills) at 1o&#39;clock this morning, gaining all their objectives with few casualties. At 8 a.m. after more heavy artillery prepartion the Infantry attacked on a four mile front running from the coast - inland, and advanced something lke five miles in depth during the day, the Turks retiring without offering any serious resistance.
