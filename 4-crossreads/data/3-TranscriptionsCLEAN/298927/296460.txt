  
 Aldershot Oven First prepare site  Then place front arch in position mouth facing prevailing wind   place in position second arch  Flange overlapping.  Wire centre through 2 rings  place back in position  drive in stake and wire to back ring  drive 2 stakes side save spreading.  Front collar brick    Pug from front to rear 1" slope allow rain to run off.  Front arch set back 3"  2 arches  2 ends  4 bars  9 tins  1 peel                             weight 33/4 cwt
