<p>February, 1916<br/>Saturday 5<br/>The men for next school march in at 10 to 12 am.<br/>Write letter home.</p>
<p>Sunday 6<br/>have a muster parade this morning &amp; try to sort the mob out a bit<br/>At 9 pm several hundred men march in from Tel-el-Kebir &amp; Ismalia<br/>Meet several; old cobbers two are now officers.</p>
