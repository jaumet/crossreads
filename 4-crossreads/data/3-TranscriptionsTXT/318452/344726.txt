It is simply churned up with shell holes Barbed wire lies in a tangle of shattered ends. Deep dug outs run down almost straight into the earth &amp; the old trenches are terribly smashed. The Bosch held on to this high point as long as possible &amp; contested the position to the last His dead lie in scores all over the field. Time has reduced them to almost skeletons Skulls are to be seen gruesome &amp; grinning peeping out of deep shell holes. Boots with feet in them here &amp; there, &amp; where the village once stood is a scene of utmost desolation.
