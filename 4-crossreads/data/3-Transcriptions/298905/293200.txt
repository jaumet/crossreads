<p>&amp;etc, splendid chandeliers hanging from the ceiling are everywhere we went through a lot of bonzer rooms, &amp; at last we pulled up in the House of Lord's it is not such a very big room but got up splendidly  we were shown where the Lords sat &amp; the Bishops the King &amp; Queen, Speaker, &amp; the meaning of the word Woolsack was explained to us, from the House we went into another room a sort of Hall it is &amp; in it are 2 paintings one of the meeting of Wellington &amp; Blucher at Waterloo, &amp; the other the Battle of Trafalgar &amp; death of Nelson, they are most enomorous things they are easily 40 feet long &amp; about 15 feet wide &amp; are worth seeing every little</p>
