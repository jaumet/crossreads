 leg. having lost his at the Somme. Went to the mess and met all the chaps again and afternoon was devoted first to a conference and then on odds and ends. At dinner we had formal mess and they drank my health on my return from leave and also that of Matthews. A good pleasant mess. After returned to billet and did some transfers  &c &c and read screeds &c. Yarned with the family and had a cafe with then - three rather nice mademoiSelles the eldest being somewhat of a beauty. Matthews and Sutherland came along with a good many things that needed attention so the evening was a busy one towards its finish. A dark cloudy night and looks like snow.
