 [Thursday 30 November 1916] 
 Arrived  Ismalia  &amp; got leave to go to Cairo to store Kit- Received a lot of letters  one from Mother Sept 14 Dolly &ndash; Joe &amp; Day, Laura- May Sept 14th- please tell May I did receive goggles, Fly Nett   &amp; wrote &amp; acknowledged them.  I note she has written a SONG!!! Address letters as of old but leave out A Squadron  I am here perhaps at the Base for a few weeks but do not know yet.   Also letter from Mrs Cotton, Ferrar &amp; others. I send the cuttings of Diary onto Dolly they are of interest to me too .
