<p>Jly 29  And tomorrow is <u>Sunday</u> the day of rest, &amp; thank goodness we don't get up until 7. Hope there are no drunks to disturb my rest tonight &ndash; else the extras hour in the morning will be wasted. Chased all over the place for a car but managed to get one before 10.30.</p>
<p>30 Our lovely SM tried to make us go to Church! But theres nothing doing Came down town with Fred (about 3 oclock) but failed to do any good; so after wandering along the Prom &amp; through the gardens I caught the first car home. Three [in margin] Sunday</p>
