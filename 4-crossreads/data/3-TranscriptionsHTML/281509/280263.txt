<p>10 February 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Second day of show.  Dull weather.  Met lots of friends, talk all day to first one &amp; then other.  Rain in afternoon.<br/>Bob goes home by afternoon train.<br/>After tea visit Percy's Hayli's &amp; Morisons.  Get wet.</p>
