<p>1917 September</p>
<p>25 Tuesday</p>
<p>Pains in stomach &amp; feeling very weak and depressed.  Not well enough to do anything.  Head thick from quinine &amp; leg helpless from same cause.  Matron in bed with malaria.  Mrs. Green having an injection.</p>
