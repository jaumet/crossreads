<p>January 10<br/>Thursday 10</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Dick and Wattie to Montreuil, to repair magneto. Report having had a good time.<br/>Sir Douglas Haig's Hdqrs at above town, 6 kilos from here.<br/>Plenty of "Waccs" in village close by, numbers of fellows have made their acquaintances and pay frequent visits to their camp at La Cotolierie<br/>Went for the Dr. at 9.30pm one of the pretty bad. Rode some 15 kilos before I found him.</p>
