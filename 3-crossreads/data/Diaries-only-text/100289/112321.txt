I. Noakes came by 2.1 pm train from London. Went to Hospital at night saw them all again.
Saturday 12.1.18
Spent afternoon in Cheltenham the night at the Hippodrome
Sunday 13.1.18
Very cold. Went for a long walk in the morning.
Monday 14.1.18
Played Billiards at Y.M.C.A. in afternoon. Had a fine night at hospital fancy dress whist drive at night.
Tuesday 15.1.18
Caught 9.30 train Gloucester arrived London 1 pm. Went to "A Little bit of Fluff at criterion at night.
