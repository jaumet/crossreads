<p>June 23rd 1917.<br/>The Anniversary of our landing in France. To celebrate the occasion a big mail arrived which was welcomed by all specially as we have been waiting over a month for news from home. Looking backward one can't help thinking of what an eventful year it has been, probably the most eventful 12 months in our lives.
