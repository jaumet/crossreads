 A little incident, which caused us a good deal of amusement at the time, was, the day we were given a lecture & practical demonstration, under gas & through tear shells. of course the gas was of a mild form but still, quite severe enough to kill without the helmets. the tear shells are only a bit uncomfortable to the eyes making them smart like anything, nearly blinding one
