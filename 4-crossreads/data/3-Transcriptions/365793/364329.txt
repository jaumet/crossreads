<p>Aug 17 the Canteen. Senator Mc came down on the mess deck to test our tucker</p>
<p>18 Rain chased us in off the foc'stle last night, &amp; by gee it did rattle down. Beautifully cool this m'ng but fairly rough sea. Went before the Quack today, &amp; was asked quite seriously if I could see out of the left eye. And he didn't half like it when I told him it was a "glassy".<br/>Just finished one of Haggards novels. Have read two good books "Lytton", &amp; "Dickens" works, but am afraid they're aren't too many others about . Overhauled A full</p>
