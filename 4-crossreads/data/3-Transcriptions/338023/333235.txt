<p>5pm We got in about noon, escorted over the bar by about two battalions of hammer-head sharks. The brutes are very daring (or hungry) &amp; hang persistently around the boat waiting for a morsel in khaki to fall over. I'm not swimming any you may bet. Arrived at the wharf we had cold lunch &amp; then went on a route march &ndash; round the whaling station to the ocean beach &ndash; a very treacherous looking surf - &amp; sat down there for an hour or so. The place is rather pretty; the harbor is formed by a long breakwater on the city side &amp; on our side by a long, steep ridge jutting far into the sea.</p>
