  
 1915 Jan. 28 Thu. Still very busy.  Knocked off at 8.30 p.m. Number of patients in hospital yesterday was 476. After knocking off went round lines & visited Scoutmaster Joyner, 1st Woollahra - Paddington Troop (Pte. 1st  Brigade  Div. Signal Co.) & S.M. Marshal 2 Waverley Troop  (2nd Battalion, Infantry Scout). Very pleasant evening with them.
