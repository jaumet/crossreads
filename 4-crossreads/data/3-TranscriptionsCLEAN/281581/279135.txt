Quite a tall order to make each perfect. In addition two copies of 5,000 feet film each have to be made for each expedition. 30,000 feet film, and all to be carefully toned and stained. 
 29th July Monday 
 With Mawson morning, with Ponting on Scott film afternoon, with Mawson again evening till midnight. Shackleton film and lecture bridging in spare intervals. Am tired of the sight of films and lectures. Was paid my outstanding account with Military after some four months.  A  balance of &pounds;22 still owing will be paid in Novr by Paymaster, Victoria Barracks, Sydney.
