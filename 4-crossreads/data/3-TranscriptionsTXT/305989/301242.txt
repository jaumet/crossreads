 Sunday 4 February 1917 Paraded sick to get out of "Pilot parade" heard the Turko mine blown up. 
 Monday 5 February 1917 Quiet day:  picquet at night. 
 Tuesday 6 February 1917 On field firing all day over at Y.M.C.A. 
 Wednesday 7 February 1917 Read Ginger Mick:  had concert at Y.M.C.A. 
 Thursday 8 February 1917 Gordon Bourkebek [Birkbeck] went on leave to Cairo.  Went to Y.M.C.A. 
 Friday 9 February 1917 Got news of move, got things ready for "trek".  Bought a few things at the Canteen. 
 Saturday 10 February 1917 Left El Arish:  trekking west on trough fatigue, stopped at 3rd Brigade.  Saw Sgt. Moore 303.  Camped the night at "Kilo 139".  Saw Billy Campbell.
