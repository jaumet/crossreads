  
 1916 June 20 Tuesday 
 T. loses his job as "Aerial Expert". thro' a contriatry of Staff. Sergt. going on leave last night without reporting to him. Mad Demonstration on loafing & first aid this morning. In aft. go for a route march to Paris Plage for surf bathe. feel tired. Handy swim at end of march.  Hundreds of men bathe on beach opposite to where [2 indecipherable] is wrecked boats washed ashore. Woman selling chocolates  among the naked bathers, think nothing of it. Home 7 pm. Tea, cold & to bed & read.
