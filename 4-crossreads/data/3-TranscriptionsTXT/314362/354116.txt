 2 Sunday  [Dec] Went into Baillieul &amp; bought more gear.  First snow this year. Bought new British warm [overcoat] 
 3 Monday Played for B. Coy. at soccer &amp; licked C-4-1 ante Room opened. 
 4 Tuesday Things going quite well in the anteroom.  Went to Baillieul again for stores &amp; reached home in [indecipherable] 5 Wednesday Very Cold !!! Freezing in fact !   Am longing for my Hurdcott Hut  &amp; someone to Rup me warm. 
 6 Thursday Cohen goes on leave. Inquiring into the "listening  dugout" by Stevens. Went to Kookaburras. 
 7 Friday Dined out with Woolrych at the club in Kemmel. Weather turned much milder. 
 8 Saturday appointed to look after some more court martials. Had  a hot shower at the club. 
 9 Sunday Attended a meeting at the officer club 
 10 Monday Court martials all day. Interrupted by air Raids  &amp;  bird !! [?] Boxing tournaments. 
 11 Tuesday Brackpool Gordon &amp; Wittleton came back. Heugh [?] went to hospital. Getting short of cash!! 
 12 Wednesday Another meetg at the club &amp; paid out  30 fr for them  &amp; 10 fr for the bar. 
 13 Thursday Left Rosignol camp at 11.30  &amp; entrained for Desvres. Reached there about 7o'c. &amp; rested well &amp; truly 
 14 Friday marched on to Tingry. and billetted with   Padre  Cure = confound him. 
 15 Saturday Changed my billet toute suite !!! The cure &amp; the 9 o'c touch got on my nerves !
