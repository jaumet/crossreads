<p>Les [Wakeford] on Board</p>
<p>17 March 1916 Fri<br/>Arrived Alexandria 11 oclock &amp; lost no time in getting aboard the S.S. Arcadia. A fine ship &amp; very clean. 16 to each mess. 17th Battalion 13th Light Horse, Engineers all on board. Jimmy Fay here. Left Alexandria 5. pm. All precautions taken against submarines as usual.</p>
<p>18 March 1916 Sat<br/>(Boys) Slept at the foot of the companion way last night. A bit of a sea running consequently a lot of the boys are down to it. Bell Sid Bragg &amp; myself indulge in a fair amount of light refreshments through the day. Busy with typhoid innoculations very nearly all day. We were also done ourselves.</p>
