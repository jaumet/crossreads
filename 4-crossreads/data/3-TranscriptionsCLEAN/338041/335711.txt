
June (1916)

June 18thFrenchy Sunday -attend Mass at 5.30pm (6.30 by watch) - Artillery at Ypres silent - 2 Taubes brought down - I leave with party for Bois-Grenier
June 19thDay duty in Dressing Room -
June 20thHuns shell Armentieres - Church set on fire by their shells - Seen by us at Bois-Grenier - wilful destruction. Very few cases - Death of Von Moltke
