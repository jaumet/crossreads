<p>Programme:  Kursaal, Music Hall</p>
<p>["Kursaal" is a German word which literally means "Cure Hall".  In this case, it refers to a place where concerts, plays, musical evenings, recitals, etc. were organised to entertain troops or mark occasions such as Christmas and New Year.]</p>
