Monday 4.9.16
Sent parcel home (registered) containing paper knife brooches, Expect to have all patients evacuated be away from here by end of week.
Tuesday 5.9.16
Bleak Cold; rained practically the whole of the day.
Wednesday 6.9.16
In spite of the rain our Artillery still keeps up a heavy bombardment.
