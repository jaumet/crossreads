<p>to forget some of it. Otherwise it went well.<br/>It is not so hot here as I would have thought for the equator but it is very close and stuffy.  We saw a lot of flying fish to-day.  Man said to have been lost overboard off the "Marathon".<br/>The weather is so calm that for days we have not felt a movement in the Ship.  Every dinner time now a bottle of ale about &frac12; pint is issued to each man for 6d.  Needless to say this does not interest me.  Another concert was held to night at 3.45</p>
