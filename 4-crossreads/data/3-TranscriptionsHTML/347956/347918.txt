<p>12.3.15</p>
<p>&amp; arrived in camp at 10 oclock &amp; found a camel had got rid of a load of lucerne in front of the Y.M.C.A. so we set to work &amp; took enough to keep our horses feeding all night.</p>
<p>Fri 12th<br/>Early rising this morning as there is a big "sham" fight on the cards the Territorials are to fight the whole of this division (17000 men)<br/>I went before the quack again to-day, but there was no necessity to as I am as right as rain again &amp; got off parade &amp; put on "light duties"<br/>The troops returned at 4 oclock &amp; said they did not meet the</p>
