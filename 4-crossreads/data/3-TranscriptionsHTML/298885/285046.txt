<p>Fri. Feb. 4th -16<br/>Horses on our guns shied at camels and bolted across desert.</p>
<p>Sat. Feb. 5th -16<br/>Visited Cairo Zoo a very pretty and well laid out.  Animals and birds of every description from an elephant to a flea.</p>
<p>Sun. Feb. 6th -16<br/>Church Service 8.15 A.M.</p>
<p>Mon. Feb. 7th -16<br/>8 men from 13/1st F.A.B. sent to Tel-el-kiber for patrol duty.</p>
<p>Tues. Feb. 8th -16<br/>On guard, weather very cold, wet and windy.</p>
<p>Wed. Feb. 9th -16<br/>Still raining very hard everyone and everything wet.</p>
