<p>7 May 1918<br/>Tuesday<br/>A very nasty wet day &ndash; rain falling incessantly from morning till night. Nothing fresh going to report.</p>
<p>1918 May 8<br/>Wednesday<br/>Weather much improved today. Just going along in the same old way &ndash; the same old camp routine.</p>
