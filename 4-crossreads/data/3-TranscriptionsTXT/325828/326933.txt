 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;we met some fellow-officers of Walter's &amp; stayed yarning; it was too late when we got round to the place: never mind; Gipsy is speaking again tonight &amp; I am hoping to get round to hear him, &amp; I think Wal will be there &amp; also Rid. On Monday Wal called up for me where I was working &amp; as I happened to have the afternoon to spare we set off to hunt Rid up.  We found him eventually, after a mistake as he has shifted to a new position on the line, &amp; by the time we got home we had walked close on 10 miles &amp; my word I  was  wasn't sorry to crawl into bed that night.
