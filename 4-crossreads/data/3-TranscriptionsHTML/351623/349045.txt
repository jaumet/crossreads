<p>11 Oct 18<br/>Coaled ship from collier Constantine.</p>
<p>12 Oct 18<br/>Out firing</p>
<p>16 Oct 18<br/>Went out &amp; anchored for the night in Lagos Harbour</p>
<p>17 Oct 18<br/>Out on patrol.</p>
<p>19 Oct 18<br/>Came in again &amp; anchored off Charlestown.</p>
<p>20 Oct 18<br/>Coaled ship from Transporter.</p>
<p>24 Oct 18<br/>Alongside wall at dockyard Rosyth</p>
<p>25 Oct 18<br/>Coaled</p>
<p>26 Oct 18<br/>Went out in stream to let L. Cruiser Calidon go into dock. Came back to wall</p>
<p>28 Oct 18<br/>Went out from wall to let Aussie &amp; Arjos in to dock.</p>
