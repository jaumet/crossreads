 May 23rd A week yesterday since leaving Blighty &ndash; Memories of that day &ndash; First Mail from London &ndash; Mr. &amp; Mrs. Scales at Essex &ndash; Command performance to General Birwood and staff &ndash; The General is most congratulatory &amp; very pleased with his "Coves" &amp; signifies his appreciation by hearty handshakes with all the boys &ndash; Back to camp &amp; bed. May 24th Rehearsal &ndash; The warm &amp; perhaps oppressive weather gives place to a cold change.
