<p>had dinner'  We were paid this afternoon &pound; 1.8.0 &amp; I went to the skating rink after tea &amp; witnessed some boxing tests &amp; they were very good indeed;  I also had a ride on the scenic railway &amp; the water chute;  Coming home I bought a bottle of Lungkura from the chemist in Heliopolis as I have a slight touch of a cold</p>
<p>I met Col Merrington &amp; Col Plaine (the two Chaplains) there &amp; accompanied the back to camp. they were also at the skating rink they are exceptionally fine men &amp; very well liked by all the men</p>
