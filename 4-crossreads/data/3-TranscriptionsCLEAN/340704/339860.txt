  
 October 1917 Start of the big stunt. Sunday 28 Left camp Shellil 3.30pm on the start of the big push. Arrived on the wadi Imalaga 10pm camped for the night. We travelled 12 miles. Mounted troops everywhere in the moonlight. 
 Monday 29 Hussani Moved about 2 miles to Masalaga and camped for the day. Drew rations etc. Left again 4 pm and travelled till 9pm arrived Khalasa, camped there abt 10pm
