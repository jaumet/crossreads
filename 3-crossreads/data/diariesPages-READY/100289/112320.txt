Clymas Hotel. Only Australian in the town.
Wednesday 9.1.18
Very little to see or do in Truro so went on to Falmouth returned by boat up the River Fal to Truro. Snow thawing, very wet under foot.
Thursday 10.1.18
Visited Redruth spent the day at Lady Carbeck's home where my nurse is staying. Caught 5.12 train back for Bristol arriving Bristol 12 midnight.
Friday 11.1.18
Caught 9.10 Am Midland train from Bristol for Gloucester where I arrived 10.5 Am, arranged accommodation at Bell Hotel for Noakes
