
Melbourne midnight. Had trouble in getting a bed. At last Tom & I managed to get in Coffee Palace. During the night there was a fight in the lane below. We told them to clear out & Tom emptied a bowl of water over them. They abused us the boys hopped down but they cleared out.Saturday March 17thSpent weekend at Dr Moores[?] at Canterbury. Had a fine time. Tom & I spent time playing tennis. In evening music. Fine family.Sunday March 18thHad a walk round district.Monday March 19thCaught 5.57 train from Canterbury to Spencer St. then back to Camp.Tuesday March 20Spent day Semaphore. Good fight at night at stadium
