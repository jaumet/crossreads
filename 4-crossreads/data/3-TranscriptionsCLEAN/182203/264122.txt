I'm very well cared for also  really our Aust. men are great fellows, always thinking of some little extra comfort for us. We have an awfully nice class of orderlies, two Aitchison's, Best    & everyso many other boys of some of our best families. Am sleeping fairly well & feeling very fit. Our hospital is very quite just now, so shall try to write to my friends but my hand gets so cold, cannot hold the pen. Sister Rhoden left here on Tuesday for No 1. A.G.H. Rouen. got orders
