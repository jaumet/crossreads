<p>[Pages 87-90 postcards and envelope]<br/>I haven't had a letter from home for over a month - which makes it doubly hard to write. I am at present doing a 5 weeks course at what is called the 2nd Army School of Instruction<br/>- it is an Infantry school for Company Commanders where one learns the latest methods of drill warfare etc. &amp; incidentally have a very good time - meet some very fine fellows from all regiments, there are about 150 officers all told &amp; the instructors are fellow "officers" who have had a lot of experience &amp; in several cases lost limbs in the war - at the present</p>
