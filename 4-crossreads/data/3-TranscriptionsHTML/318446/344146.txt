<p>About Nov. 16th<br/>I have spent all this evening sitting on the doorstep (for it has a doorstep) of my shanty with my pipe in front of the fire.  A gramophone has been playing about 60 yds. off;  the tunes of which transported me from place to place with bewildering rapidity.  They played "Hitchycoo" and I was in the auditorium of the London Hippodrome with Artie on one side &amp; Jub on the other listening to the beautiful Bonita &amp; her absurd little husband singing.
