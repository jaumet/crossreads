  
 Monday 5 August 1917 [1918] 4 p.m. S. Musgrave & I grab a gharry & drive to A.H.Q., draw 5-0-0, remaining 11 odd.  Then tea at Liptons with Musgrave & her friend, Mr. Broughton. Shopping & so back to 31st G.H. Ematine being given intra muscularly for doses.  By mouth in B.G.I. pdrs., i.e., Bismuth Emetine & Iodide.
