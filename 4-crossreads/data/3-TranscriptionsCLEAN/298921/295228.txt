  
 September, 1915 Tuesday 14 Last night "the lame blind diseased unfit to travel" [next five words circled] (Sale Note or Delivery notes) had a Concert - (I mean sick soldiers) The poor beggar quite blind recited, to me it was most pitiful but the average Tommy loves Sentimental stuff. In this Hospital is Sister - a friend of an old nurse who had heard of you thro' [indecipherable] is it not strange. Weighed today 13 stone 5 1/2 lbs so you see I am not wasting away!!! The Turks are mostly  big  men,  & Kitcheners new army or what I saw of them only boys of small stature round faced unsophisticated youngsters.
