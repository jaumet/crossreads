Had a sponge bath on our return. During the walk we enjoyed the scents of many flowers, carnations, roses honeysuckle, hedges & many others.
We had a long drink at the Pelican Inn at Stapleton & then ate our lunch tea which we carried, on the grass near the bridge above Stapleford.
We saw at Hanging Langford three Australian soldiers conducting a religious meeting, the whole village almost attending in their Sunday best and providing forms and joining vigorously in the singing to the accompaniment of the two cornets played by our soldiers.
Monday - Rained slightly this morning. - I had about 60 Mills' Castings half filled with cement to make them the correct weight.
