<p>on the 4th &amp; had a very good night.  Made a member of the hospital committee.  Played in a billiard tournament on morning of the 6th but was beaten in my heat. Sent home  Xmas cards.</p>
<p>Thursday 8.11.17 to Tuesday 13.11.17<br/>Spent a pleasant afternoon at Conservative club on 8th at Whist Drive organised by Chemists of town.  Invited out to tea on Sunday night.  Have been out each day in spite of the bad weather.</p>
