<p>[Monday.22 May 1916]<br/> [Page headed Personal and crossed out]<br/>Letter from you &amp; enclosure from Dorothy Hungerford- thank you Mother also big box of Cigarettes I think from Harry Austin please ask him if he sent.  I will write him myself but never know our luck think they must be from him.<br/>Just found a note the Cigarettes were from Albert Tyson??!!  Very good of him.<br/>I will not risk posting this Diary till I get nearer Civilization or the chance of getting through are small per Camels etc: &amp; Censors are strict.<br/>Thank you for Printed notes they refresh my memory of where we were.</p>
