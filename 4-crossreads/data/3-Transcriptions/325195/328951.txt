<p>26.9.15<br/>Sun 26th<br/>Hobson &amp; McGee were put sapping in another position today &amp; the work on the 'phone box is hung up; Nelson &amp; I went to Bde HeadQuarters this afternoon to receive telephone instructions &amp; saw in Div. Orders that Arthur Lye has been struck off the strength of the Army Corps; It is a decent step to B.H.Q. but we had an interesting time finding where our lines ran &amp; met etc on a sketch. also fixing telephones; Col Merrington is returning to Australia shortly, &amp; he has promised to take Stan diary also mine back with him; I heard to-day that Stan Creighton has or is returning to Australia; A Rumor has been afloat that the Gurkhas were to attack 971 last night but it proved only a rumor &amp; there was only a small demonstration. On 'phone duty again tonight at F.R.A.</p>
