
Monday 6 September 1915 At D. M. S. during morning, a chat with L.G. Ford 
Tuesday 7 September 1915 Chat with L. Gs Baptie & Ford this morning. They said that I should go to Galipoli soon. Dr Plant left & Dr Aiken came today In Cairo during the mng Major Purdy here. Dr White partook of tea with me 
Wednesday 8 September 1915 Major Purdy to Lunch. Dr Schuber called at 7 pm. asked me to go to the Chalet at Mena on Sunday night.
