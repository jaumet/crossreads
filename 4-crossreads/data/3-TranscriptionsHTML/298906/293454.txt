We gave the girl that has waited on our table a little present last night a nice little wristlet watch, &amp; she was overjoyed with it, but she deserved it for she has looked after us splendidly, &amp; tomorrow she is leaving these Barracks, her time having expired, they signed on for these War Services the same as soldiers, only not for duration &amp; 4 months afterwards, they draw the same pay &amp; rations as Tommies, they don't get the same food as we do down here, they have to have their rations most unfair I think, though there are two ways of looking at it.<br/>30th.
