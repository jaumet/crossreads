<p>Wednesday 19 June<br/>Rainy so not much grooming. Head dressed. Hoppy reverted Sapper &amp; posted 10th Bde kite suite.</p>
<p>Heavy rain dinner time <strike>swamped</strike> wet lower end of tent. Afternoon writing. Evening improved tent with covers from Richy.</p>
