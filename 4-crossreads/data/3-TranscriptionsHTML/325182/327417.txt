<p>13th<br/>Have been amusing myself reading, writing, eating and taking a few photographs with my V.P.K. Camera and playing a little tennis.</p>
<p>18th<br/>Today a couple of us were driven into <strike>Leicester</strike> Nottingham in one of Mrs. Smith's cars.  It is a very rambling old town.</p>
