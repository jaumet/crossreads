Saturday, 28/4/17Up early in morning & marched to No. 11 with our full packs.  Left our packs there & marched back for breakfast, then to No. 11 again & then marched to Dinton with packs up.  Entrained there & went via Salisbury & Andover Junction to Ludgershall.  Got out there & marched 1/2 mile to Windmill Hill Camp & joined up with D company 62nd Btn. of 6th Division.
