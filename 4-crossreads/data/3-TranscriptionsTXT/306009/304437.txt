 1915 Sep. 12 Sun. In Hospital. The Medical Officer of the Ward, Capt. Dyring &ndash; told me that I could get out of Hospital to-morrow. Mildred Brown came over this afternoon &amp; stayed at the Hosp. for dinner &ndash; I escorted her back to Choubra afterwards &amp; got roused on by the Night Sister in Ward 6 for being out after hours.
