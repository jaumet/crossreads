<p>Dec 26th</p>
<p>Weather improved &amp; the glorious sun set about drying up our soaked garments &amp; cheered things up again. The waters on the plains subsided but left a horribly sticky mud behind. During the afternoon Capt Hurley &amp; myself went for a ramble over the sandhills &amp; to the camp of the camel Post, the boundless stretchers of great nothing sand dunes dotted here &amp; there with white tents &amp; rows of camels tethered out in rows impressed one greatly with its uniqueness.
