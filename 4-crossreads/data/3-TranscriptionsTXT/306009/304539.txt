 1915 Dec. 23 Thu. No. 2 G.H. held a sports meeting this afternoon on the Gezira Sporting Club oval. The Meeting went off very well &ndash; an Egyptian Army Band was in attendance &amp; played selections. The prizes won at the Sports Meeting were presented by Gen. Wallack, C.B. at a Concert at 8 p.m. when a very decent programme was contributed. The Palace is looking very "Christmassy" &ndash; decorations, et. etc. everywhere.
