<p>Saturday Feb 9th</p>
<p>Longmoor : parade in morn : standing by to leave at any moment but it didn't come off :</p>
<p>Sunday Feb 10th</p>
<p>Longmoor : packed up again &amp; wrote to Lithgow in afternoon : supposed to leave about 6 pm : caught train to Bordon &ndash; thence train to Folkestone :</p>
<p>Monday Feb 11th</p>
<p>Folkestone : arrived about 2 am &amp; slept in "mansions" : a large block of the up to date private hotels has been fenced off &amp; is used as a rest billet : splendid buildings &amp; the owners must have been hit very hard by the war : Folkestone : fell in 3 pm &amp; boarded</p>
