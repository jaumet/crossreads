<p>Left Serapeum 6 pm 31/5/16 "Transylvania" at Alexandria 4 a.m. 1/6/16. Sailed 2/6/16 arrived Marseilles 9.a.m. 7/6/16<br/>Entrained 6 p.m. 7/6/16<br/>Lyons 6 am 8/6/16<br/>Ballieul 3 a.m. 10/6/16<br/>[indecipherable] 5 am 10/6/16</p>
<p>Left [indecipherable] on 17/6/16 &amp; had a 10 mile march with full packs &amp; blankets passing through Sailly &amp; Bac St Maur &amp; reached our Billet about 9 pm same day. Departed to 13th Bn. Transport 20/6/16 &amp; made Provost Cpl over 12 men to do Police duty in 4th Bgde Transport [indecipherable] and act as [indecipherable] in case of emergency.</p>
<p>Stationed at Jesus Farm. Left Farm on 11/7/16 with amm. guard for [indecipherable] &ndash; arrived 7 p.m. and moved off at 7 a.m. next morning. Entrained at Ballieul at 12 noon and arr [indecipherable] about 7 p.m. 13/7/16.</p>
