<p>Wednesday 1 August 1917.</p>
<p>The camels again. <s>After breakfast equipment fixed up for 4 hrs. march into desert.</s>&nbsp;</p>
<p>In Cairo again last night. Went to Anzac Hostel, Y.M.C.A. &amp; Br. Soldiers Home. It was a pleasure to be served with supper in the Y.M.C.A. in Esbekia Gardens, by the British ladies who were very polite.</p>
<p>A biplane came to grief within 30 yds of our section this morning&nbsp;Pilot escaped with his life. Machine smashed to pieces.</p>
<p><u>Pilot died later from shock</u>. (1/5/18) CCF&nbsp;</p>
