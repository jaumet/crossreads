
25-11-1917
Bitterly cold, sleet and wind. Very rough night. Sgt Buchannon back. Two more Sgts made, Anderson and Massey. Twenty three reinforcements arrived
26-11-1917Cold day. General Anderson inspecting our horses, Nothing doing
27-11-1917Staff Fitter Gyson awarded the D.C.M. Major Selmer D.S.O. Both men well worthy of the decorations. A big stunt going on all day, hear the guns quite plain.
28-11-1917Fine day. 7 mules sent to D.A.C. Three horses in exchange. Stunt still continues
29-11-1917Enemy aeroplanes flying about. Leave to Paris starting again. 4 men per day, from Brigade. Eight days leave, must have at least 10-0-0 in credit in pay book, befor leave will be granted. Great discussion about the referendum, voting take place on Dec 17th.
