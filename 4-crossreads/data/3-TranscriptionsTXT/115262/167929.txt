 Monday 11 June 1917. 
 Still on duty and fairly solid too 
 Sea rough &amp; resembling the bight. Transport shipping seas a good deal. Fire in bunker or hold started again. Foul air starting again also. 
 Wrote letters in spare time in morng &amp; afternoon. 
 I am now looking after 42 out patients,&nbsp; &amp; &nbsp;mumps &amp; measles, 3 scabies &amp; 6 V.D&#39;s. patients.
