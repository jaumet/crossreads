<p>Monday 10 September 1917.</p>
<p>Routine as usual till 9 a.m. Cleaning, restuffing &amp; fitting saddles all day &amp; in the sun &amp; dust with 1&frac14; hrs for lunch. A solid day again. Heard that another Australian mail landed at Suez 2 days ago. Everybody waiting for same.</p>
