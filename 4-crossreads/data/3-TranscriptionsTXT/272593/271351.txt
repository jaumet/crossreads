Evans &amp; Grey were up again today  they have both lost there their steel hats &amp; they made no attempt to replace them  Capt Mckenzie gave them a great talking to. There is a heavy bombardment going on tonight  the guns are raging properly, &amp; flares are going up in all sorts of unexpected places  away back in our rear the heliograph is going, so there must be something doing, they must have located the enemy &amp; are pouring the explosive in to him
