<p>17 March, 1916<br/>Serapeum<br/>Went out on parade as usual this morning &amp; took mid-day meal with us.  We had a good swim before dinner, did no drill at all.  When we got back heard that we had to leave to morrow; later we were told to pack our kit bags &amp; that C squadron &amp; A &amp; B troops from B were to move off at 9 o'clock in the morning</p>
