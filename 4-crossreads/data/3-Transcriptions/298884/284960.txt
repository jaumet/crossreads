<p>Big posters common in every port next presented their advertisements to our eager gaze. As we are a 'dry ship' I heard many groans over the hoarding Ushers famous whiskies Liptons Tea etc.</p>
<p>No one was allowed off the ship but <strike>some</strike> there are <strike>also</strike> usually a number of chaps who cannot be kept on board and this proved to be no exception to the rule.
