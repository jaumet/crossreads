  
 The latest rumour is that - this comes first hand from the Sigs. Troop from a man who saw the telegram - we have captured 800 turks, 45 Austrians & 5 germans & two 4.5" guns & that street fighting is in progress in Gaza at the present time. So what is a man to believe? 
 April 2nd I have refrained from writing anything about Gaza for the excellent reason that I know nothing beyond vain imaginings.  The newspaper report says 900 prisoners, H.Q. staff of Gaza & 2 4.7 guns so the facts must be true.  Of course the paper gives one the impression that it was a brilliant victory but ... I dunno.
