<p>28 December 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Go for bath &amp; change of clothes to Meaulte. Ride in train left Quarry Dump 1. p m. side tracked Fricourt. From there get car to Meaulte' G good luck.  Lee McBurry.  Bath &amp; new clobber. Home 7.30 by train. Bitterly cold. Ice &amp; frost.<br/>Horses bogged &amp; drowned in liquid mud.</p>
