<p>Sund. 31st Dec 1916 (12 noon):<br/>Yesterday I was able to do a bike ride to see the Field of the Cloth of Gold so well famed in History. I left Wimereux about 11 am. And biked through the villages of Wimille &amp; Wacquinghon to the sleepy little country town of Marquise, 10 kilometres from Wimereux. Here I left the Route to Calais and took the more narrow &amp; muddy road to Guines, 14 kilom. away.
