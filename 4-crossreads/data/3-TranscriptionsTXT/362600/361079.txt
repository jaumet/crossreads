 31 Sun W &amp; Fine. Recd letters from Jennie &amp; Marie. Arr ord. Corp. Wrote letters all night. 
 Wrote Mum. Edie T. Mr Horner.  &amp;  Edie Tdais. Francais. F 1 Apr. Mon G  &amp; I go walk round Block &amp; I came Home  &amp;  read all night. Home good supper with Mat [Deldindelles?] who knows a Waac Sgt Cook. Cake very nice. 
 W 2 Tues. E &amp; I go to Salisbury. Stay at close till 8pm. &amp; then go up to Mrs E. We get caught in the rain round [Hashon?]. They all say we will not allow to touch. I find out very different. I am very sick but manage to get Home Ok 
 W3 Wed Am not well. Go to bed at 9pm. D &amp; E talk about Scotland. Top has just returned from here. Recd. Letters from Marion &amp; Maisie H. 
 Go to see "Facing the Music" with [Pene?] &amp; George Slatter. Very funny. 
 W 4 Th Nett. Comes up for a yarn. 
 I stay a bed until 9am. Recd. Letter from Dad [reake?] from Marion &amp;  letter. Views of mountain &amp; Sydney come to hand, also paper. Wrote Masie Hudson Wrote MRL. Marion Edwards. Marion Clarke 
 F 5 Fri I print photos with Top. Write to Jennie &amp; PC.of Hockey match to DAD. D Wall &amp; Nick talk all about Nancy [Meron?] Jane 
 F 6 Sat Arr ord. Corp. Measles break out  &amp;  I have H of time. Work till nearly 8pm. Then go to my room to read. D &amp; E out all night. 
 Nothing doing at all.
