 Monday 14/5/17 Training commenced in earnest, had letter from Fred. Tuesday 15/5/17 Visited by General Gough who came to watch the training. Saw Bas; took men for stiff march. Wednesday 16/5/17 Raining, lectures in huts, had a game of Bridge. Thursday 17/6/17 Musketry, Battalion shot well, raining. Friday 18/5/17 Marched at 10.30 am for Bouzincourt, had midday meal on road &ndash; now in camp near Martinsart.
