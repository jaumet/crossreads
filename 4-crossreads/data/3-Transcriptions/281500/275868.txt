<p>This evening my platoon had to carry our rations from Bn. Hqrs. down to the Coy. it was not a very long job &amp; we were soon finished.</p>
<p>"New Years Eve" &amp; it is being ushered in to the sound of guns, not a very good look out for the New Year which will shortly be on us, but I am confident that 1917 will see the last of this gigantic struggle &amp;amp the complete defeat of Germany &amp; so we enter the New Year full of hope &amp; more confident than ever of our victory for it is certain now, come what will.</p>
