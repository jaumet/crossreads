 Phillips &amp; Pte J. Chippendale went on leave this morning to  Blighty. 8 men from the Company were sent to the Transports this morning for fatigues. [Shorthand symbols]  Thursday Jan 10. 1918. 1256. 
 There was rain last night &amp; this morning most of the snow was melted. I sent a parcel to my wife today &ndash; a flower vause made out of  a shell case. It cost 3/6d to send it. We were paid today I drew 30 francs = &pound;1-2-0 [Shorthand symbols] P. Ryan came back from England to the Transports tonight.
