<p>[Extract from the Telegraph, March 23rd 1917]<br/>Roll of Honor.</p>
<p>Phillips. &ndash; Killed in action in France, November 5th, 1916 (previously reported missing), Second-Lieutenant Frederick Stobo Phillips, youngest son of the late Major Henry Phillips, and grandson of the late Captain Robert Stobo, only surviving brother of Mrs. Edward Owen, Hunter's Hill, and Mrs. W.H. Read, Wahroonga.</p>
