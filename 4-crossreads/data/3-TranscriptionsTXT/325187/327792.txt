 [Wednesday 16 February 1916] 
 Had a swim in Canals   I am getting a bushel of Visiting Cards from the Sheiks &amp; Pashas &amp; enclose some samples- Just a small pkt of Chewing gum would be acceptable in the heat if we have long days in the Desert in April-  On my road today I saw a Traction Engine &amp; 11 furrows Plow on one side &amp; on the other side wooden plow &amp; 2 oxen, the old &amp; the new.
