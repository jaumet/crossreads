Saturday 21.4.17
Suffering a good deal with my teeth. Bearers beat Transport at football 5 to nil. Colonel went on leave to Paris.
Sunday 22.4.17
Things a bit lax while the Colonel is away. 14 F Amb beat our boys at Aust Rules Went to church in Albert at night, 5th Div Band played Hymns a few selections after the service. Appendicitis very prevalent of late.
Monday 23.4.17
Bearers again played defeated the transport. Albert at night
Tuesday 24.4.17
Walked to Poziers during afternoon
