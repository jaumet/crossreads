<p>Saturday 21st July 1917</p>
<p>Took wagon to Busseboom for a load of timber forth 13th battery. Left camp at 12 noon and arrived back at the 13th battery about 4pm where we left wagon.</p>
<p>Sunday 22nd<br/>Great number of our searchlights sweeping the sky looking for Fritz planes. Lovely sight. Half holiday from12noon to 3pm. About 10.30pm a few of Fritz planes dropped bombs around our camp for nearly &frac12; hour. No damage done.</p>
