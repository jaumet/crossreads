
Port Said4th December 1914
My dear Father & Mother.I expect you will be surprised at not hearing from me from Aden, especially when I told Pat I was writing but there was no opportunity of getting more letters ashore so I had to let it slide until now.We stayed a day longer in Colombo than we expected owing to the ship requiring water, and for this reason we had to go in to the Inner Harbour and consequently saw a little more of the Town. Five days sail from Colombo brought us to Aden.
