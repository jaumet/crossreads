So that's that.<br/>There is talk now of seven days leave to Cairo, indeed some have already gone.  I shall probably put in for it too but now that they have taken away all our clothes from our kitbags at Ghezirah, I have only what I stand up in &amp; for me the chiefest pleasure of leave is clean clothes &amp; no helmets.<br/>This promises to be an interminably dull job, Front line reserve, front line reserve in spells of two months each.<br/>I was talking to some Frenchmen the other day &ndash; there are a brigade of them up here &ndash; and they tell us that there are 500 Italians at Port Said so this is going to be a very mixed grill here indeed.</p>
<p>Sunday 10th<br/>There is no doubt that this is wonderful weather that we are having for the time of year.</p>
