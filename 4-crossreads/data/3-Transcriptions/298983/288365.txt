<p>23rd January 1918<br/>Have fared well for tucker 5 of us got 4 tins of Bully between us &amp; were able to buy good bread on the Boat</p>
<p><strike>Thursday</strike> 23rd Wednesday<br/>Had a very light breackfast Bacon &amp; Bread at 6 &amp; started right away for the Boat Southampton seems a fine place Went straight on to the Boat &amp; anchored in the Harbour until 7.15 P.M. There are a lot of horses on board</p>
