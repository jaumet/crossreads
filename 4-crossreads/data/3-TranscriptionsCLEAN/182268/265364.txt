 Thursday 25/10/17. Met Mr Gray & had lunch with him at Cafe Royal  saw "Romance". 
 Friday 26/10/17  Went to "Fovent" to the Training Battalion  came back same night 
 Saturday 27/10/17  Went out to Malden to say Goodbye to the Bamfords  had lunch with them. Had dinner with Gray & left for Scotland. 
 Sunday 28/10/17. Arrived at Dunkeld had breakfast and then went to Amulree Hotel, went for a walk. Good dinner  Wrote to Doff.
