30/8/15 HM Nile pulled out with 24th. Found three stowaways on board and put them ashore. Amused for a long time by the crowd of youngsters scrambling for coins and the very futile efforts of policeman to stop them. Left 5.45 swung out from wharf and tugged out through shipping. The beauty of the harbour forces itself on one. In front the thick shipping and winding narrow channels in rear the white city like a leadpencil straight along the coast. A good bye to Egypt, fascinating, filthy, beautiful repulsive the home of an intense religious people and the mother of vice
