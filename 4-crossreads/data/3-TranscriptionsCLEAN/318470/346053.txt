
14thStill acting S.Maj. getting plenty to do and things not too satisfactory.  Expect to leave it.
15thSaw the Colonel today & intend leaving today.
16thLeft A.C.L.R. last night & came back to the old Coy.  Taking No. 2 Sec.
17thSnow set in last night & continued to fall lightly all day.  Very cold.
18thHalf the Coy. - No. 1 & 2 Secs. shifted camp in 8th F. Coy's Camp & took over their work.  I took over C.R.E. defence work.  Still snowing.
19thWorking on C.R. Defences.  Cold & freezing.  A fair amount of shelling.
20thSame as yesterday.  Very cold but fine.
21stHeavy shelling - large shells falling close to our work.  Cold but fine.
22ndStill working on C.R.E. defences.  Weather very cold.  A good deal Artillery fire on both side.
23rdC.R.E. defence work.  Intensely cold.  Aerial activity and a good deal of Artillery both side.
24thSame work as yesterday.  Still very cold but fine.  Heavy shelling.
25thC.R.E. defences.  One of Fritz's aeroplanes brought down near where we are working.
26th3 more of Fritzes Planes brought down.  Heavy shelling both sides.
27th3 more of Fritzes Planes brought down within sight.  29th Div. hopped over last night & took some trenches & about 300 prisoners.
28thStill very cold.  Heavy shelling both side.
