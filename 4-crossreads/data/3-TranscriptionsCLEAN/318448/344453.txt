  
 11/7/18 I have just been able to have a smoke again now,  I have been unable to touch tobacco for over a week to the great detriment of my temper. There was a concert last night down at the main Supply Dump Jericho,  given by the "Bats" They were extremely good indeed. One of the cleverest & funniest  things being one fellow who came in dressed as rather a moth eaten Prima Donna & proceeded to sing "Il Miserere" in falsetto They had a wonderful stock of clothes & were all good dancers.
