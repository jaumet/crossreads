<p>falling without afterwards moving. Murphy who was near Nicol's horse sent Nimmo back while he covered him.  The horse was killed but Nimmo got clear &amp; Murphy gave up his own horse to Nimmo to have another go.  Again the horse was hit but again Nimmo got clear.    <strike>We then</strike> They then came back to us along a &amp; we had a pretty stiff go before falling back to the next ridge.  Cameron made another attempt afterwards, but the enemy was between him &amp; Nicols in such force that I called him back.</p>
<p>We then fought from ridge to ridge 8 against at least 700 until they almost surrounded us &amp; then would pull back to another position.  Not once would the Christians come to our assistance even tho all the men were armed &amp; when it got particularly hot. The chief &amp; I bailed up 14 at the point of our revolvers &amp; gave them the choice of riding with us in a charge in order to get the guns back to the next position a mile</p>
