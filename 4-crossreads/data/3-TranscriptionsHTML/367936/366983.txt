<p>Wednesday 24 May 1916<br/>In Gulf of Aden.  Sea very calm.  Not a ripple.  Passed two steamers also dhow.<br/>Lost sight of land at dinner time.  Got wireless account of war from Aden press.  They are still fighting at Verdun.  Russian successes.  Very hot day.</p>
<p>Thursday 25 May 1916<br/>Passed Aden in night.  Came through Hell's gates about 10 a.m.<br/>Saw island of Perrim [Perim] with light house knocked down.  In afternoon were able to see Mocha with glasses.<br/>Arabian shore very sharp sandy hills;  poor looking country.</p>
