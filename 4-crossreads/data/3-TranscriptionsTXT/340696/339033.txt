
131Very quiet reception at the New Pier. Had lunch on board then went ashore with full kits. Had a few hours to wait for the train which left about 4 p.m. Good trip overland arriving in Sydney next day a while after 4 p.m.
Great crowd at the Anzac Buffet & one cannot explain the feeling to be "SAFE BACK HOME."
Got my discharge very quickly Edmund [Ferranti?] put me through & I was soon whizzing by car HOMEWARDS with my Home Folk
8408.Sgt. S. Hayne4th AUS. DIV. TRAIN.
