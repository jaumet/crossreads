<p>Of those Australia gazed upon with pride<br/>Lie crippled maimed &amp; helpless uncomplaining through it all&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<br/>Remembering those who perished at their side.</p>
