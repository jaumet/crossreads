<p>Wednesday 4 Dec.<br/>Instructed to be ready for transfer to Horseferry Rd today or tomorrow. Application for 3 days leave rendered unnecessary. Fussed about all the morning arranging for photo of the boys. Taken about 12.30. Packing up in afternoon. Evening had the boys home &amp; made a bit of fun for a goodbye.</p>
