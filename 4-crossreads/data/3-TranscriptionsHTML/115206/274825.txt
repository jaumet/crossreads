<p>September 1918.<br/>Thursday 12</p>
<p>In Reserve. Hebecourt<br/>Weather very wet and fairly miserable.<br/>Walked across to Frise to see old Yank Watson but had no luck. <br/>Wrote three letters, and cleaned the telephones. Spent a very quiet night .Met Fisher of 10th A.L. [indecipherable] he looks well.<br/>Charlie Adey returned to Bty from school and Blighty leave.</p>
