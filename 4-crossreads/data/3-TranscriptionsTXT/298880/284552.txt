 What a pity we cannot have the Australian weather over here. London would be paradise indeed then with plenty of sunshine. 1.30 P.M. We have reached Rouen after a tedious journey. These French engine drivers have a habit of stopping about half way inside  a  every tunnel &amp; as we have no lamp alight in the carriage &amp; no room to sleep it is a dirty trick, very dark deed  indeed  in fact. Nearly all the way we have been crawling along stopping nearly every quarter of an hour for no apparent reason whatever now &amp; then we do a sprint &amp;  the  then the ghostly yell of kiddies calling for bully beef can be heard.
