
<p>TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 16, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Round Boundary &amp;</p>
<p>lopping in Mountain &amp; Top in</p>
<p>morning. Tickle laid posts of the</p>
<p>line</p>
<p>Gosden &amp; Kenny finished their piece</p>
<p>of burning off and started the</p>
<p>next piece.</p>
<p>All the crowd went down</p>
<p>for a picnic to the river.</p>
