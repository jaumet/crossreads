12/10/16.
Our pet mine has not exploded yet; and I still manage to sleep sweetly on top of it. Have been building a dressing station. Near Batt H.Q. I feel better down there, am not afraid to tread heavily. Hope "Hinie" nurses her a little longer for:- Hush!
13/10/16.
