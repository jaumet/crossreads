<p>stuffed right through both holes.  The Maltese are practically all R.Cs. &amp; bells are going all day &amp; all night.</p>
<p>20 May Thursday</p>
<p>Had side stiched up.</p>
<p>Friday 21st May</p>
<p>Had 1 hole in arm stiched up.  Told I was a serious case &amp; should be tfd. to the proper hospital but told later I was to remain here.  Some nurses arrd. today.</p>
<p>22nd June 1915</p>
<p>Had a concert once a week &amp; tobacco &amp; cigs. given us but we are all tired of Malta.  It is an unlovely spot.  Walked to Citta Vechia one day.  Very quiet village with a nice cathedral.</p>
