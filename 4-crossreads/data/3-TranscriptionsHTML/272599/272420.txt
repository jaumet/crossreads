<p>1917 Wednesday 30 May</p>
<p>Transport duty today. Nothing much to do. Finish at 1.30 p.m. for day. Down to Wimereux for tunic not done. Blast. <br/>Do men's mess for Jerry at tea, crook with inoculation. Mac &amp; I tog up &amp; go into Boulogne to see Opera Madam Butterfly. Did not start till 9 p m   Principals good, others very ordinary.   [indecipherable]  Good seats Second act, an hours interval while they fix the stage. Do not finish till 12 30 p.m. French very casual &amp; take everything calmly  <few words indecipherable/> my rude remarks, did not know she compreed English  Brisk walk home  The search light on the waves, an indescribably beautiful effect.  <br/>Harry B &amp; the bed board joke 1 a.m.</p>
