 28 
 It rained like blazes today.  The C.O. and I went for a stroll to Bde H.Q. in Hervilly  We had an  a  interesting yarn with some artillery diggers on our return journey.  We saw a lot of anks in Hervilly  They appear to be a very likely looking lot.
