<p>2480 Tpr R.C. Wilson<br/>17th Reinf 1st Reg A.L.H.<br/>Interm. Base Depot<br/>Eygpt</p>
<p>14th May 1916</p>
<p>My dear Mother<br/>This Sunday is "Mother's day" so it is a good time to start a letter to you even though I wont be able to post this for some time yet.  I did not know it was Mothers day until I opened a letter from Miss Anderson at dinner time in which she told me; was it not a funny coinsidence opening it on a day like that.</p>
<p>Every body is looking forward to when we get our first home mail; but I suppose that wont be till about a fortnight after we land, however my mail bag is still holding out and gives me something to look forward to each day.<br/>It is beginning to get very hot here now, we all sleep up on deck but have to come down below for meals and then the perspiration</p>
