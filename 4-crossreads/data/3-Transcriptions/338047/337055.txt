<p>1919<br/>June 1<br/>the battery in his car was worn out &amp; he could not get a new one.  So we sang at the piano instead.  In the evening we went for a walk &amp; saw the Church in the Woods, build by the devil according to popular tradition.  I wont vouch for the statement, but it has undoubtedly been there a long time.</p>
