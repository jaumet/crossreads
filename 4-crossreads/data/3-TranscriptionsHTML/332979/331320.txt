<p>in it being to crowded so we went along to the Ahlambra to see another Revue (Variety) called The Bingham Girls. After buying a Ticket we went up stairs &amp; duly got a seat. This is another find Theatre not so big as The Hippadrome but very compact &amp; beautifully finished off.<br/>The Orchestra was another large one 33 &amp; the Conductor. The Music was another treat, every performer was a Master of his Instrument.
