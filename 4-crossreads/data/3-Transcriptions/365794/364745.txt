<p>Friday 7 December 1917<br/>Busy day, 2 officers from Aerdrome adm. 8 p.m., accident.  Faces very much injured, one West, injuries to ankle &amp; more.  Other, ... blood pouring from eye (R), nose &amp; several cuts on face.  Removed to Nazereah (?) 4 p.m.  Pt. Gardner foot amputated, condition good.  Went to A. H.qrs 6.10 p.m.  Recd. &pound;4-0-0 pay to date, shopped &amp; ret'd to 27 in garry after tram broke down.  Went in with S. Murphy &amp; found her pleasant.  Now I am living again as of old.  Life is a very good thing when one is fully employed in congenial work and when off duty time comes I know the women with whom I am living &amp; when in their company can laugh &amp; be gay as the old R.P.A.H. days.</p>
