<p>Tuesday Dec 17th London</p>
<p>roam round in morn &amp; afternoon : went to "Box of Tricks" at night &amp; it was pretty good &amp; very laughable : caught the 11.30 pm train from Euston to Glasgow arriving about 10 am on</p>
<p>Wednesday Dec 18th Glasgow</p>
<p>had a clean up &amp; went to Paisley : made very welcome by Uncle Auntie &amp; Mary : company after tea &amp; very interested in my souvenirs</p>
<p>Thursday Dec 19th Paisley</p>
<p>roam during day &amp; seen the big cotton mills : Gil came along &amp; is also stopping here : seen over a ship building yard &amp; it was very interesting : play at night &amp; pretty good :</p>
<p>Friday Dec 20th Paisley</p>
<p>strolled round in day &amp; pictures at night</p>
<p>Saturday Dec 21st Paisley</p>
<p>roamed into Glasgow &amp; seen the U boat : company at night including "cousins" Nellie &amp; Daisy : good fun</p>
<p>Sunday Dec 22nd Paisley</p>
<p>went to Loch Lomond with Aunt &amp; Uncle : bad day snow &amp; rain so we didn't see much : however it must be a glorious place in summer time</p>
<p>Monday Dec 23rd Paisley</p>
<p>roam round in day " went with Gil to Daisy &amp; her mate's flat</p>
