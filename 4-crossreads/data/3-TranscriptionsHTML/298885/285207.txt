<p>Now he made his first attack last month on the Somme where no Aussies were holding the line</p>
<p>Within 7 days all Aussie Divs were at once sent down to do their share in checking Fritz</p>
<p>As soon as we arrived at Amiens we were told that our other 4 Divs that were already in the line, had regained lost ground while Fritz had made an advance on the front we had just vacated</p>
<p>The result being that our Div (1st) after 2 days on the Somme, were rushed back again to the scene of activity in the North.</p>
