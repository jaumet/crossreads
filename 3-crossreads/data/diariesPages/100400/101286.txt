1917
Jany 1st Dull raining and in no wise different from other days.
Jany 3rd Move back to Buire. Saw George yesterday he is OK. Warned to-day for leave on 27th inst.
Jany 5th Still at Buire. Lately it has been nice and fine to-day Fritz has been shelling the ridge above the village, further back than I have ever seen him before. Last night at the Dandies at Merricourt
Jan 10th At Buire weather cold and windy with rain. Visit Merricourt again last night Lately the bombardments north of Thiepval have been very severe rumours of an advance prisoners are many. Rumour has it that Baupume has fallen but it lacks confirmation.
