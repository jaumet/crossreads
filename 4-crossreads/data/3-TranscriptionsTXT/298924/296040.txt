
Thursday 27 February 1919RafaKhamsin still blowing - everything smothered in Dust like a Darling Shower, in a Tent, water becomes mud, food sand etc.I am the Lecturer today.  May the men listen & curse in silence - topic Court Martial procedure.  Heard old Sam Sir Samuel McC. had gone west.  I wonder if true - somebody will come in for many millions.  Sorry to hear Hunter Landale gone.
