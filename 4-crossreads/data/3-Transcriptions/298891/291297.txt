<p>1917 August</p>
<p>2 Thursday</p>
<p>Camp routine by day in evening we were invited to a moonlight pic-nic by Serb officers I drew Svetislov Soldatovich for my partner. Nice young man with one leg very sensitive about his mutilation. Got home about 10.30. Pop had held service under the trees in the morning.</p>
