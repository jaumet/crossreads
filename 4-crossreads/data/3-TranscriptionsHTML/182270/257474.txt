<p>WEDNESDAY, JUNE 11, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Got the mares &amp; foals</p>
<p>&amp; stalion out of Hill paddock</p>
<p>&amp; put them in Strand. At the</p>
<p>Middle paddock bore again.</p>
<p>Tried to lower the pipe &amp; got it</p>
<p>stuck I watered Joe&nbsp;Perry&#39;s cattle</p>
<p>at the 4D troughs.</p>
<p>Father got a summons from</p>
<p>the P.P. board for not killing</p>
<p>rabbits.</p>
