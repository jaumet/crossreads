<p>9/11/16<br/>Two enemy planes bombed one of our clearing stations. They done a good deal of damage. But our antiaircraft guns brought them both down.</p>
<p>Our casualties were killed and wounded 41 and with the exception of the orderly room staff all these were previosly wounded cases.</p>
<p>11/11/16<br/>We expect to be releived</p>
