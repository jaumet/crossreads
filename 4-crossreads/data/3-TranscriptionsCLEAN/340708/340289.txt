
Oct 1917Tuesday 16thHad a general look round went to a coal pit caught the 10 pm express for London arriving at 7 amWednesday 17Had a look at London Bridge The Tower Madam Taussards the strand Marble Arch & various other places of interest.   Went to the Club & met an old mate.   Our boy have been knocked about in this last stuntThursday 18thLeft for camp at 10.15 am arrived at 2.30 pm.  It is the finest day for a fortnightFriday 19thPension Clarification  another fine daySunday 21stVisited Paington & BrixtonMonday 22ndA wet miserable day
