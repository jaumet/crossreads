<p>so he shouted out "a quarter past ten" Again the name was called, again he vociferated "a quarter past ten" The orderly Sgt approached &amp; asked him what he meant by answering in such a fashion. Whereupon our friend after speaking of the Sgt's birth &amp; upbringing in terms of  contumely  he declared that the Sgt was several kinds of a fool-all enumerated at length &ndash; if he did not know that a quarter past ten was ten fifteen.
