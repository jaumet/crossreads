 Dec. 2nd Thursday Glorious day.  Tinned meat &amp; vegetable ration issued just lovely cold.  Rice &amp; tea breakfast, Tea evening.  Letter writing &amp; improving bunk afternoon.  Bomb burst within 5 ft. of 3 of us morning fortunately no injuries.  No. 2 Northern. 
 Dec. 3rd Friday Lovely day, mild night, rations still very low, &frac14; water. 
 Dec. 4th Sat. Day fine, evening not too cold.  Line quiet. 
 Dec. 5th Sunday Weather continues mild. 
 Dec. 6th Monday Weather very nice.  Mail in.  Parcel from Eileen.  Day off duty
