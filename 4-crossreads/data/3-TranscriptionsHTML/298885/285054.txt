<p>Tues. Mar. 21st -16<br/>Ordered again to be ready.  Left Tel-el-kiber 4.30 A.M. in open trucks and after very cold trip arrived Alexandria at 11.30 A.M. and 3 hours later we boarded S.S. "Grampian" for France.</p>
<p>Fri. Mar. 24th -16<br/>Just after passing Malta we steamed back for fresh orders then resumed our original course.</p>
<p>Sat. Mar. 25th -16<br/>Submarine reported 10 miles away last night also report of torpedo fired but doing no harm.</p>
<p>Mon. Mar. 27th -16<br/>Arrived at Toulon about midday.  A very picturesque</p>
