 We went in per Motor Lorry &amp; back by train part of the way the rest per buz wagon. It was in the train I met "her" &ndash; Usual thing I always meet "her" in train tram or boat but this one was indeed a peach growing at Corbie. There were a couple of French Soldiers in the carriage &amp; one was a singer &amp; sang a couple of very pretty songs &amp; taught me the chorus of one. His fianc&eacute;e wrote out the words &amp; sent them to him. He was a jolly spark was sorry when they got out.
