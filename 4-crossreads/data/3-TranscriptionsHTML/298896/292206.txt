American soldiers, mostly officers, predominate, 3000 being on leave there usually whilst occasionally Australians &amp; New Zealanders can be seen rubbing shoulders with the French in their Reds, blacks and greys, African Chasseurs in their Yellow Khaki &amp; Red Fez &amp; Italians in their green grey. The yanks flash their medals like the French. Several Polish soldiers also present mostly from America, being now in a special regiment with the French.<br/>9th November Saturday<br/> up at 6.30 A.M. fresh as paint &amp; after usual breakfast went to meet the car for 8 oclock. it was late however &amp; we arrived at Monte Carlo too late to see the Gaming Saloon. it was a glorious trip  the road being cut in the face of the cliff &amp; winding in &amp; out in some cases passing through tunnels.
