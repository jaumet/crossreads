  
 5 September, Friday 
 Terrific rain storms. I buy fruit etc & have a terrible job getting it Home. 
 6 September, Saturday 
 Dinner in Manly. No 13 ticket & 1/3. Dis-app over marathon. Go Home, back to Dobboes to Ellis place & to Britt Theatre for Fisk Jubilee Singers with Gen, Lil & Mum. Great rain up till 11 pm & then glorious day.
