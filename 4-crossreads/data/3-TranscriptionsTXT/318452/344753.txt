 Monday 17 We remained in the huts today one company has gone up to work in the trenches. We had a man killed today &amp; several wounded. It has been cloudy with light misty showers occasionally Tonight, before dark, I witnessed a very fine piece of work by one of our airman. He looped the loop beautifully just above our heads
