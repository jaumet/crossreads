<p>Friday 23 July, 1915</p>
<p>Arrived at Alexandria at 4.30, berthed alongside the Royal Inniskilling Fusiliers later the Connaught Rangers came into port.</p>
<p>The natives get dirtier &amp; lower in cast all the way along, the best class of Natives are seen at Suez, at Port Said, they are much the same only dirtier &amp; at Alexandria they are just scum.</p>
<p>We only managed to get onto the wharf &amp; saw nothing of the town.</p>
<p>We left for Cairo at 8.30 &amp; arrived at 3 a.m.</p>
<p>When we got into camp we slept on the ground just in our ranks.</p>
<p>We are camped at Heliopolis 8 miles from Cairo, you can see the Cheops Pyramids from here.</p>
