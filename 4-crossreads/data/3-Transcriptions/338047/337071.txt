<p>1919<br/>July 27<br/>look well around us &amp; observe well the position in which we were placed.  The Memorial faces North, &amp; a bust of Cecil Rhodes is placed in the temple part, looking  the same way over boundless Africa. &ndash; A big sweep of plain country rising to foothills, &amp; finally to snow capped mountains.  We then descended, &amp; walked further around the mountain to his private Zoo, where there was a large collection of animals &amp; birds.  Some of the monkeys were very interesting. We<br/>[Three photographs, Groot Schuur, City Hall, Docks]</p>
