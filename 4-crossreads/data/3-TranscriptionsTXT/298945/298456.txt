 When I arrived I found large numbers of wounded lying in the open between the two top marquees &amp; the road, while the lower marquees were being used by the 2nd Field Ambulance for walking wounded. In our dressing tent two cases were dealt with at a time &amp; these were selected from the worst of those in the open. Cases were left as they were, provided that the dressing was clean &amp; comfortable, &amp; that there was no reason to deal with the case at once.
