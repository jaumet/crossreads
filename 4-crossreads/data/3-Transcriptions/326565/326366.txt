<p>Tuesday 16.5.16<br/>Detailed for Quartermaster's fatigue.  Paid 150 piastres &pound; 1-10-9.</p>
<p>Wednesday  17/5/16<br/>Parade hours again altered; hardly worthwhile going to bed at all. Reveille 4 AM, breakfast 4.30 AM, Parade 5.15 AM to 8.45; at leisure till 5 pm when we parade till 6 pm.  Inspection by A.D.M.S. &amp; D.D.M.S. whose report was very satisfactory.<br/>After parade went to canal swimming.  The weather for the past week has been unbearable</p>
