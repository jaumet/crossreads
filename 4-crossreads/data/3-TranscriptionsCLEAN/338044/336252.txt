
24th Aug:- Thursday.Plenty of work. Raining slightly but otherwise nice weather  Take a bath at 5.30 pm in the only place possible to obtain in Rouen & it costs 1 franc. 15 cents (11 1/2), also a tip (Baines, Salle D'Armes). Return to work until 10 pm, nose down to it all the time. Straffe the work. The casualties are enormous.
25th Aug:- FridayJust mechanical work & go it. Yesterday, I was orderly Sergeant for the first time & carried it through well enough. My new restaurant is tip-top    & the dinner to-night was A.1.  See many German prisoners at work. Work again until 10 pm  "This is the life"
26th August:- SaturdayNot feeling too well, do very little work this morning  At 12.30, am off for the day & after lunch we take a stroll
