Oct 31We returned to the bay to anchor with the rest of the boats
Sunday Nov 1stwe got up & were glad to see that all preparations were finished & that the boats were starting off headed by the Orvieto. Such a sight as they went in single file out of the harbour & out to sea will never again be witnessed fromby anybody we were all on deck watching 2 waiting for our turn to come which wasn't long & nowwe are all in sight of one another on the high seas & will have the same sight to see every  morning we get up. There is an escort of 5 cruisers.
