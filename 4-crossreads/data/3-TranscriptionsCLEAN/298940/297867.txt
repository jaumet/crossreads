  
 Two huge slabs of concrete from the pill box had been lifted en masse & blown a few yards where they lay on their sides in a heap of mud & rubbish. There had once been a Fritz light railway past the pill box, but all that remains to testify to it are half buried rails twisted into grotesque shapes, in shell holes.        Nothing doing during the afternoon, but we were just settling down to a final read in the evening about 8.30 p.m. when the Germans raided our trenches
