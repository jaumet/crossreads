
Sunday 20 October 1918Still in bed & crook.
Monday 21 October 1918My flu changes to pneumonia, at least I think it was today.
Tuesday 22 October 1918Matey came up from Worthing.
Wednesday 23 October 1918Gettg. rid of the pneumonia.
Thursday 24 October 1918Getting better & sitting up & taking notice.  The Doc. looks quite happy!!
Friday 25 October 1918Still in bed.  Rup called at C.P.R. but we could not get double berth yet.  Doc. says illness due to gas but don't believe him.
Saturday 26 October 1918Got up today, feeling somewhat groggy.
