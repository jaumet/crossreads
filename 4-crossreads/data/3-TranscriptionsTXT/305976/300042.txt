 ft below Sea Level &amp; here is about 3000 ft above sea level. On arrival we were examined by Medical Officer &amp; then issued with Tobacco, &amp; Comforts from Red Cross Depots. The grounds were covered by EP tents among olive trees. What a fine home this is compared to what we have been used to. Sunday Sept 15 Here there is nothing to do. The food is excellent &amp; we spend our time reading, writing &amp; yawning. I went into Jerusalem to day to have a look round.
