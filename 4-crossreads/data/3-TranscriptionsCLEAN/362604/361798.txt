  
 Corporal in charge of a party of Jerries, or Fritzes (Germans you know) made them push us out again.  I had Tea with Roy & then we came into Havre for a walk around.  I left Him about 8 p.m. as He had to catch the 8.30 p.m. Train.  I met Frank Lascelles in the Y.M.C.A. in Havre last Monday or Tuesday.  We did not have much time together as He had to hurry back to camp.  I hope to see Him again ere long.  It has rained without ceasing for two days but it is preferable to the icy weather for then even the soap freezes hard.
