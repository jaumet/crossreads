<p>9th Feb 1915 To-day I have been Batt Orderly Sergeant, and amongst multituding duties, has been that of giving an hours pack drill to various defaulters <strike>Displ</strike> Discipline is tightening, &amp; in place of the old 5/- fine, it is 4, 8 or even 24 hours pack drill. This means that after afternoon parade, perhaps a long march, these defaulters have to parade with full pack, rifle etc and <strike>drill for</strike> march up and down for an hour without a spell, commands such as "right turn" "left turn" "about turn" etc following at intervals of a few seconds. It is a most effective punishment, but takes practically as much out of the unfortunate NCO</p>
