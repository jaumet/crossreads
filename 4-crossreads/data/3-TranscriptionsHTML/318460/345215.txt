<p>Thursday 15/3/17<br/>Rain &amp; mud, Cold wind from the North this morning, but it's drying up the mud, Fritz's artillery very active this morning.<br/>Pay 8/9 &pound;22.7.0<br/>letter from Viv answered it &amp; <strike>owe 20 francs</strike>. Says Nell has had her hair-dyed &amp; &amp; that Grace Box sent her &pound;2.0 0 for Xmas Box very secretive about it never mentioned it herself.<br/>Paid 20 francs leaves me 5/9 owining them.</p>
<p>Friday 16/3/17<br/>Frost &amp; fog, lovely clear night last night.<br/>Posted letter to Nell, Viv &amp; Maude<br/>owe 4/9. deferred &pound;22.8.0<br/>no more news about going but leave is starting again I hear</p>
<p>Saturday 17/3/17<br/>Beautiful Morning stiff breeze blowing &amp; drying up the ground,<br/>Artillery very active the Big Push cant be far off now<br/>Owe 3/9 deferred &pound;22.9.0</p>
