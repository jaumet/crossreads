 42 It was rotten travelling by bike it rained nearly all the way rendering the roads in parts practically impassable. Encountered troops both coming &amp; going which made matters worse. 
 29/8/16 Last night we had a tip top sing song in the kitchen at the back it was great. Sang all the old songs accompanied by a mouth organ. I turned in about 10 o'c but they still kept it up. I was nearly asleep when one of the chaps brought me in a steaming hot cup of coffee it went down well. The kitchen is just off the office, very handy.
