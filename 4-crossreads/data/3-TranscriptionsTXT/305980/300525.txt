 15:  Eleven of our planes bombed El Arish, blowing up an aerodrome &amp; inflicting heavy casualties on troops.  One machine shot down &amp; captured, another coming to grief in Red sea, killing pilot &amp; observer. 
 20:  Taubes over bombing, but no damage done. 
 28:  Exciting duel which lasted 20 minutes, the taube bringing our plane down with petrol tank perforated, both men uninjured. 
 July 
 2:  48 hours leave to Port Said.  Filthy town &amp; people.  Native quarters disgusting.  Stayed at the Continental &amp; enjoyed the change of diet. 
 4:  Returned to Romani by light railway from Port Said along the seashore to Mahomdayich. 
 15:  One of our boys, Pte. Tucker died today.  Went to Kantara to attend funeral.
