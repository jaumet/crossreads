<p>Friday May 4<br/>Everybody stiff &amp; tired. Had easy day. Informed we are sailing on 10th inst by Booarra<br/>Saturday May 5<br/>Weekend leave to Melbourne arrived 4.15pm. Spent afternoon strolling round Town evening visited St Kilda<br/>Sunday May 6<br/>Went down to Port Melbourne to see transport.<br/>Monday May 7th<br/>Many rumours in train. It is said 21 Transports sailing on Thursday.<br/>Tuesday May 8th<br/>Packing Black kit bags, final medical examination Wrote letters.</p>
