It is two years since we joined the Grand Fleet, & our squadron the 2nd L.C.S. has had its fair share of work. 
 28 Nov 18 Steaming along West coast of Scotland & Hebrides. The scenery along the coast line is for the most part bare & devoid of trees. You catch a sight of heather at times, & you see plenty of small streams, running down from the hills. Passed Bullock & Slade Whiskey Distillerary at 8.30 this afternoon. 
 29 Nov 18 Running along Coast of England. Steaming 16 knots.
