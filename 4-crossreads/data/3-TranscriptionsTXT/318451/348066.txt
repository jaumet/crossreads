I went to see Capt Cuttriss about a Concert programme for Saturday night &amp; have arranged for a concert in our Billets for Saturday. Bible Class this evening, 18 present.  It was a very fine meeting. 
 Thursday Went to Billets today.  Reading room going nicely.  Bought Lamps to light it &amp; got an issue of coal sent round.  I had a funeral this afternoon. 
 Friday Today I have been at the Billets &amp; Brigade Headquarters &amp; arranged for services for Sunday afternoon.  Had two funerals this afternoon.  They recovered a body yesterday that had been buried since the 21st Jan.
