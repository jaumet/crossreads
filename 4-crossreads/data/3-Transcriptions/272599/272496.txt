<p>1917 Monday 20 August</p>
<p>A poor old Frenchy &amp; his little granddaughter fell foul of a Mills Bomb this morning just alongside ward. They were mowing the crop when the old man struck a live bomb which had evidently been thrown into the crop by a patient. Result it exploded &amp; wounded both the old man in a dozen places &amp;  little girl on leg. Both treated at hospital. <br/>Busy with full ward. one poor youngster of 19 with leg blown off  Jocks mother over to see him a typical poor old Scotch lady.</p>
