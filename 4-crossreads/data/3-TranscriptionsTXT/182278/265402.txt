 Wednesday 6/3/18. Went by car to Boulogne had dinner &amp; returned about 11 p.m. 
 Thursday 7/3/18&nbsp; Orders to move forward received, Bn cleaning up. 
 Friday 8/3/18&nbsp; Left Henneveaux at 5Am and entrained at Desvres arrived at Steenwerck at mid-day, took over from Goddard at Canteen Corner&nbsp; very Dirty camp. 
 Saturday 9/3/18&nbsp; Cleaning up- men resting officers reconnoitred  for  forward areas 
 Sunday 10/3/18. Formed Bn Mess again Reconnoitred area around Nieppe.
