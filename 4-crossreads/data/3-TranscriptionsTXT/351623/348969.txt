14 Mar 16Arrived at Castries, St Lucia in morning watch & tied up alongside pier. Filled up with coal, natives doing the coaling in baskets. Anchored out in the stream after finishing coaling.
15 Mar 16Anchored at Castries. Went ashore at 1.30 p.m. till 6.30 p.m. Went & saw a cricket match between our team & St Lucia C.C. Our band was also playing there. They have a good little sports ground here, & it is situated among very pretty scenery.
