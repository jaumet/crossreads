 February, 1916 Friday 25 Early morning we do a little practice in Gun mounting &amp; get our gun &amp; Tripod ready for the standard tests After breakfast we examine all squads at Drill After dinner inspection &amp; in the evening I went into Heliopolis for a walk. 
 Saturday 26 First thing I heard this morning was "Cookhouse" at 8 am. After breakfast we had a muster parade &amp; issue of certificates. After dinner all men for Ismalia &amp; Tel-el-Kebir march out At night I went to pictures w/- dad
