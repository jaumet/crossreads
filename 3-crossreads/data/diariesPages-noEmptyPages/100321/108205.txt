25/2/19 Marcinelle
Orders arrived for Col James to go to England and I spent most of the morning with him. Visited both the printing offices 22nd. and 24th. The latter have a very fine up to date machine. Several mines are around this quarter of the town and their ramifications are said to extend miles under Marcinelle. Conditions are bad men do 10 hours a day at the coal face lying on their sides crouched up with a lamp between their teeth. The air is so hot that they can only wear a pair of light cotton drawers ? for their hard life they get only 10 francs a day.
