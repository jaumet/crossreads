Went to the station to see them off but the train was late, so we had to get back.<br/>To my surprise I got word again that he was coming down Tuesday, as they were leaving Egypt on Thursday. So the dear boy came down again &amp; we had a grand time together until 8.15. p.m.  I have not received any wire since, so suppose he is still in camp at Tel-el-Kebir. No doubt we shall have Saturday on the Lake. I have the whole day &ndash; off duty. <br/>We think now that our  Unit will be leaving here in about 3 weeks time. <br/>Poor old George Bower is ill &amp; in hospital.
