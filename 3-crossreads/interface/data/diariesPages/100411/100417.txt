Mon. 13th Nov. 1916Light exercises, plenty of reading good tucker. Boxing. After dinner exercises with life-belts. Extremely rough weather, a lot of sick.Tues.None rough weather, boxing usual routine. Lost watch.Wed.Reconing on calling at Fremantle to-morrow. Had a spar. A big sports tournament coming off shortly.Thurs.Some fair sport after dinner having perks at Albatross. Fair weather. A couple fr of good boxing contests a fair concert.Fri.More wireless news su dealing with war news the labor coal trouble. Fairly slow to-day. Nice smooth weather. Just beyond W.A.
