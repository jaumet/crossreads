<p>W 8 Sun<br/>Very nasty day. I play for Y.M.C.A Service. &amp; go next door afterwards for supper &amp; chat. A nice Library &amp; nice people. Hear a sermon "Laughing Jackass &amp; dead kangaroo" More Mockery.<br/>Wrote Mum. Mrs Edmunds. Con</p>
<p>9 Mon<br/>Recd letters from [Hilda?] &amp; Esme. Go to Pictures. In 1st see [indecipherable] [married?] with all [indecipherable].  2nd winning a wife 5 Reels. More mockery<br/>Come Home &amp; go to bed &amp; read. D. that someone is trying to steal.<br/>Wrote to Auntie Annie.</p>
<p>W 10 Tues.<br/>Recd money from L.N.W. Railvy. Go to theatre with E. "Her Excellency the Governor" and make love &amp; my double affair as His greatest asset that He sleeps in [indecipherable]. More mockery. It is very cold. I come &amp; go to bed &amp; read.</p>
<p>W 11 Wed<br/>Play for Y.M.C.A lecture Christian Martyrs &amp; finish letter to M.R.L &amp; post it.<br/>Go to Bed &amp; read for a while &ndash;<br/>Write Muriel. Davies. Bess. Edith Tauchner. MRL</p>
<p>W 12 Th<br/>Recd letters from Lottie. Peg. Dulce. Maisie.Carter &amp; Photo. Maisie &amp; Aunt Annie<br/>Go to WAAC show &amp; print Photos. Meet Kilty. She goes too [solid?] so I go to call the Roll at 9.45 pm. We play the game hunt the ring.</p>
<p>W 13 Fri<br/>Read letters from Home 4. Go to Sarum by bike in 50 minutes. Meet Percy Edmonds. Go to Palace &amp; order Dumas' Books. Buy food stuffs at an awful price. Go to Palace &amp; see Girl comedian. Sleep at Cl013</p>
<p>F&amp;W 14 Sat<br/>Recd letter from Con. Get Home a little wet by Car from Station.<br/>Straighten things out all day &ndash;<br/>Tidy up generally at night. Bitch comes down to see us. YMCA.<br/>Moved on 13th to a Division.</p>
<p>Wrote to Mr Gregson. Miss Rowley. Con. Auntie Annie.</p>
