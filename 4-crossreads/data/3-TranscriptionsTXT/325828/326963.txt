Everywhere signs of harvest but labor seems very scarce in this country now. I am keeping in good health &amp; strength &amp; trust you are all well at home. Any amount of work in hand to keep us going, &amp; last few weeks writing of any sort has been impossible other than Field Service cards. Haven't seen anything of Rid for 4 or 5 weeks now but Wal said this afternoon he had seen him recently &amp; that he was keeping well. Must close now with love to all at home Your loving son, Leo.
