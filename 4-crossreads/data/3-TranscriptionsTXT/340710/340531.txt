  The Graves of Anzac  14.12.17. On the shores of ancient Hellespont kissed by the ripples of the Aegean Sea they lie our honoured dead buried in soil which bore the name of Anzac for eight short months.  Neat little crosses mark the spot in that Moslem country where the first of our men paid the great price in the fight for the freedom of the Empire.  Here Leander met his death in bygone days for the love of a woman and here our men met theirs for just that same reason.
