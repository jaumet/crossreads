<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&amp;  are camped near us, so of course, all seem to try to accept as many as possible. We are not so busy, sp generally manage a few to go each day.</p>
<p>I had another Night-off last Thursday   &amp;  went to another Dinner. It was great   &amp;  we had a beautiful time. Such a   <u>great</u>  lot of men. I'm absolutely spoilt   &amp;  having a lovely time.</p>
