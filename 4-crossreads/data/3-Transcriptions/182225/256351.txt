
<p>102</p>
<p>AUGUST&nbsp;&nbsp; SUNDAY 20&nbsp;&nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; windy. The 27<sup>th</sup> Bat was on duty so there was no church parade&nbsp; I made a Signalling flag for myself in morning and read the Quirindi papers in afternoon &amp; wrote some letters in the evening.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>One of the sweetest enjoyments of life is true friendship &mdash; it divides sorrows, couples and doubles pleasures.</p>
