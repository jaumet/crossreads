
2 September 1915Thursday
Get a bit of a surprise when in walked dad.He was overjoyed to see me &  could not do enough for me.He brought a bag of grapes  &  figs  &  gave me another watch mine having gone a bit cronk.He had a bad time physically but is making up for it in other ways.He is drawing Gun. Sgts pay (Viz: 12/- per day)   &  doing nothing for it.{Another victory for me I stuck on the peninsular longer than any of the other Lindsell's}
1915 September 3Friday
About a dozen sisters   &  large number of Patients leave here for Australia this morningAm not feeling too good must have caught a cold or somethingfoot getting on well.Dad comes in about 6 pm   &  brings a tin of Pineapple which goes down very well.Sleep a little better than for several nights past.
