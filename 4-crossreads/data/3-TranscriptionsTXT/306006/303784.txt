 21-3-17 Jessie, Hills &amp; Cobb out. Ben came on the scene a little before 6 oclock &amp; Hills, Jessie &amp; Soar drove up a little later; Hills is on night duty &amp; Jessie  Soar, Ben &amp; I went to the Cosmograph &amp; saw the pictures out; Had dinner at the Nurses Club at 8.30 &amp; then came back arriving in at 10, came back to Heliopolis per tram; Found things merry in camp several of the huts were holding a farewell concert; The Orderly Officer sent the Orderly Sergt over at 11.30 to stop the row but he was counted out &amp; the concert continued; I am rather pleased the School is breakingup as another 3 weeks here in a months time is something to look forward to.
