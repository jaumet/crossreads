<p>November, 1915<br/>Friday 19<br/>One could appreciate some fruit &amp; vegetables now, we lose a lot of men for the want of suitable Tucker apart from the continual sniping. But the men are better fed than in Africa &amp; personally as a Jackeroo &amp; droving in the Back country I've had many a rougher time &ndash;<br/>Honestly sick at all my letters being in the Aegean Sea, I had made a special effort &amp; wrote to most of my friends to bid them Xmas Greetings. To show how necessary Reinforcements are we evacuate <u>each day</u> anything between 350 to 500 men from the Peninsular in the great white Hospital ship.</p>
