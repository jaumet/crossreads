 I finished &amp; went to see Dr. Ireland but he was out  -  I desired tea then but found I hadn't a bean on me  -  All the same I blew in at the tea room &amp; told them how badly I needed refreshment but they'd have to give me the necessary credit which they promptly did &ndash; 9d for aft t here  -  Refreshed I then went for a walk &ndash; on my own &ndash; right round the harbor for a mile or more  -  I spotted a brilliant Kingfisher &amp; a slate colored crane &amp; lots of Indian starlings &ndash; the mongoose is wiping out bird life in Fiji I am told  - The plant life &ndash; trees, shrubs &ndash; herbs all interested me &ndash; Clerodendrons &ndash; Mangrove of the shiny laurel leaf kind &ndash; A very prickly shrub with lots of white flowers &ndash; a Solanum &ndash; Lantana  -  Palms here are dying &ndash; some disease apart from insects affects them  -  I told you Albyzias do splendidly here  -  I walked &amp; observed &amp; listened to the boom of the surf on the reef  -
