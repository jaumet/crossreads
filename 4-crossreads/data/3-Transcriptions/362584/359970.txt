<p>Extract from L.B. Speddings Letter 3rd/7/16</p>
<p>We lost some good Officers, among them was Capt. Ferguson, He used to be my platoon officer, lately he had command of B. Coy., He was a real man &amp; gentleman, our chaps were very sorry to lose him. He did his duty.</p>
