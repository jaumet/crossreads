
1915Aug. 7 Sat.Kept pretty busy to-day as we admitted about 90 wounded - all stretcher cases - last night.One man died to-night, diagnosis: Enteric.In the evening with a couple of others, visited 2 of Cairo's largest & finest mosques - One of them in particular looked splendid: beautiful carpets & rich carvings & ornamentation in a blaze of illumination & the Mahommedans chanting their prayers.
