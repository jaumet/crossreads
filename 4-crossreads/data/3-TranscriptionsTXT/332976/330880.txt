
8th, 14th & 15th arrived in the town to-day & we are the first Australian troope ever billited in Corbie.   It is a large & thickly populated place, & from all appearances promices to afford us a very good time.21st   This morning we paraded in "drill order" & marched down the river (Somme river) to a spot about 3 Km. from our billets where we are to go through a course of Pontoon Bridging.
