 I was talking to Colonel Paton &amp; asked him if the troops wd go to Europe. He said it wd  be best &ndash; They wanted to fight &amp; would not settle down in civil employment until they were fed up on fight &ndash; The vast majority are in good health &amp; cd go at once &ndash; Did I mention that the Eastern has 160 Malaria on her &amp; 1 doctor- The Navua has 3 doctors - !!! &ndash; Ginger Burns is on the Eastern (His hair is ginger) By George its hot &amp; sweaty &amp; at 3.30 I've to see that S.W. machine to the pier &amp; inform the purchaser &amp; then collect fifteen of the best &ndash; Alone I did it &ndash; 
 Friday Feb 12-15  We go to Sydney tomorrow &ndash; General feeling of relief &ndash; I swam &amp; held Sick Parade &amp; then to the Beach &ndash; I collected in gold 15 &pound; for Soda water machine &ndash; I went to the Hospital &amp; deposited safely a man named Bates with Malaria &ndash; He had been landed on me from the Una last eveg.
