<p>Wednesday 2 February 1916<br/>N.C.Os class in morning.  Lecture on outposts.  A very easy day, lectures on musketry etc.<br/>Uncle George &amp; big party came up and brought tea for all hands in tent, had a great feed.  Went back to Sydney with them and saw Alan off to Nyngan.</p>
<p>Thursday 3 February 1916<br/>Church parade before breakfast.<br/>Rifle exercises &amp; bayonet fighting then route march this afternoon with about a mile's run to finish coming home.  Slight thunder storm but not much rain.<br/>Hot day but cold night.</p>
