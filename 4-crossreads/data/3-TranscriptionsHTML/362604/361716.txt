<p>I love the rain when I am well wrapped up so that it cannot harm me of course.</p>
<p>When at Warminster the other day, I met a Padre &amp; got into conversation with Him.  Of course, the usual question, what State are you from.  He said He was a South Sea Island Missionary.  I said did you ever get to Epi [Vanuatu].  He said I have lived there with Mina, who is married to a surveyor, &amp; Mr. Roxburgh.  Oh, what a small place the world is again.</p>
