At dinner I was the only silent officer who refused to take part in the hilarity. Everyone had bon bons which gave either trumpets or caps & paper hats. Champagne was supplied & catering arrangement under Richards 31st Bn excellent. Richards address is Tattersalls Club Adelaide.
After dinner I retire to my room & with memories of [Billie Carleton] whose case is still proceeding in the papers long for the drugs that bring forgetfulness. Strange it is years since this thought crossed my mind & that it should recur on the happiest day of the Church year. Then I went for a long walk among undulating hills & half closed in valleys which destitute of bush or life well accorded with my gloomy thoughts & when on the way back I was lost the unison was complete.
