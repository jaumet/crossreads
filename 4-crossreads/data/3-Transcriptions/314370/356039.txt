<p>June, 1916<br/>Thursday 8<br/>Work as usual all morning &amp; after dinner get the guns &amp; Amtn ready for the range tomorrow.<br/>Rigged an old belt filling machine &amp; it did very good service in filling 20 belts in an hour</p>
<p>Friday 9<br/>Reveille  4 am<br/>Breakfast  4.15 "<br/>Target Party  4.30 "<br/>Waggons  4.45 "<br/>Range  5.30 "<br/>first shot fired  6.15 "<br/>Each man fired a group of 20 rds &amp; a traverse of 60 rds at Khaki targets at 400<br/>Gun works perfectly fired 1300 Rds without a stoppage.</p>
