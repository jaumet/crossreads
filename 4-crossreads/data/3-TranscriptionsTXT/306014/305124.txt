 Mch 27 
 "On Gas" again. We had to gain a fair knowledge of the use of the P.H. Helmet &amp; had also to gain facility in putting it on. After lunch we placed the helmets in position &amp; marched etc for an hour with them on in order to get quite used to them. 
 The mail today brought Alan &amp; me a nice box of home-made cakes from our two girls in London. Needless to say we greatly appreciated their kindness and also very much enjoyed the cakes.
