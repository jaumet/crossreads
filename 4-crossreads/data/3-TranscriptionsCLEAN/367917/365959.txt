  
 "Charlerio" on Boxing Day & things are very dear there Boots are 300 Francs per Pair (11) Meat 6/= per Pound & evan a reel of Cotton is 5/6 a reel & the Town is full of Belgian Refugees coming from England & France. 
 The money used in Belgium now is German Marks The Huns Printed them. there it is all paper money am Enclosing one as a  Souvenir ". The Belgians are very good to us & give us plenty of Coffee
