<p>18.8.18<br/>Being sent over to Taranto singly our escort composes of the Jap. destroyers Katsura, Kaede &amp; Ume. 14 hours out submarine reported on our track, 80 miles south of the island of Create.</p>
<p>19.8.18<br/>Fine weather prevails &amp; strong head wind. Zig zaging course night &amp; day the moon being up throughout the night. Averaging in speed 15 knots</p>
<p>20.8.18<br/>Fine weather prevails &amp; so far have seen nor encountered any submarines. Passed a cargo convoy at 3 am going the same way. It consisted of 5 cargo boats escorted by trawlers &amp; one sloop. During the night some of the military officers were caught smoking cigarettes &amp; striking matches on deck. Court martialed next morning &amp; placed under arrest &amp; detention.</p>
