<p>22 May 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Cross to inspection full marching order to parade ground other side of line at 6. 30. a.m. 4 Batt. Artillery. Engineers &amp;  A S.C included. Breakfast. Med. Exam. pass. Dinner. Light rain breaks aft. parade. Sit in tent &amp; listen to stories related by returned men. <br/>Pay parade wait 2 &frac12;  hours then dismissed till next morning. a la military style.</p>
