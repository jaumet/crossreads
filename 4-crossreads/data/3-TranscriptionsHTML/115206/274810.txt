<p>August,1918<br/>Wednesday 28</p>
<p>Bty Position ahead of Proyart.</p>
<p>Weather fine and clear, with showers at intervals. We did practically nothing during the mornin. The gunners dad a duty shoot of 100 rounds at 4 am. The enemy has evacuated a considerable amount of property and ground.<br/>At noon we were ordered to move up to an advanced position. We were in action in front of Lappin Wood, which is 3000 yds from the front line or [indecipherable] our outposts which have penetrated beyond Heseyvillers and seems to be still going on merrily.</p>
