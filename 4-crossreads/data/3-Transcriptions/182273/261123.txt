
<p>1918 &nbsp; April</p>
<p>25 THUR &nbsp;Fine day. Heavy rain</p>
<p>in aft. Heavy firing</p>
<p>all day. Quiet night</p>
<p>26 FRI &nbsp;Dull day. Amm.</p>
<p>going up. Fritz very</p>
<p>active. Fairly quiet.</p>
<p>Heard of W. Cleary&#39;s death</p>
<p>Writing at night.</p>
<p>27 SAT &nbsp;Dull day. Fairly</p>
<p>quiet. Amm. going up.</p>
<p>Went into Hongendem</p>
<p>in aft. 29<sup><u>th</u></sup> Div. on</p>
<p>the move. Rec. letter</p>
<p>from Maggie. Posted</p>
<p>letters &amp; photos to</p>
<p>Gladys C. Else Parker</p>
<p>&amp; Hortense (2. photos).</p>
