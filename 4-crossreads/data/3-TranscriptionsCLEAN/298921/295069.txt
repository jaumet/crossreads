  
 April, 1915 Tuesday 6 Divisonal Hd Qts & infantry leave today for Alexandria thence across the Mediterranean it seems useless omitting names etc: when none of my Diary notes apparently have reached home. Cooler after the Dust storm. Here is one re Nurses - When the Sultan heard of the 100's of pretty!! Australian Nurses he sent 4 of his Court to Port Said to pick out 6 & if they did not obtain 6 as pretty as where in the Harem, they  need not come back - They came & saw, & then jumped overboard!!
