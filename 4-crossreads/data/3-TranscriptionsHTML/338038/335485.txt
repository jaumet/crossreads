<p>He was one of the gamest men in France.  I have read about men like him but very seldom seen them &ndash; if anyone got wounded he was always one of the first to help him, often without waiting to put on his steel hat &ndash; he was a soldier and a man and his loss will be deeply mourned by all the 37th Battery boys &ndash; we though no end of him.</p>
