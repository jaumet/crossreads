  
 Stayed in dug out all day relieved & night by 53rd Batt. No casualities in A Coy. marched back to Bancourt arrived about two in morning dead tired 29th Thursday Spent a miserable day & Bancourt. 30th Moved back to Bapaume to billets. 31st Street fatigue had a bath Sunday April 1st Moved up to front line again and relieved the 53rd Batt.
