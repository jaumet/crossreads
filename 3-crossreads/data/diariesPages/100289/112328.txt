Sunday 3.3.18
Very cold quiet in the town. Did very little other than writing my usual weekly letters.
Monday 4.3.18
Played billiards during morning. Went Whist Drive at night at Hospital won "Booby" prize; had a grand night.
Tuesday 5.3.18
Went to "Boots" to lunch in morning to pictures in afternoon. Caught 5.25 pm train arrived Bristol 6.18 pm. Had to wait Bristol till 8.45 pm to catch Warminster
