<p>Had a nice letter from Mrs Copp, Mollie, Miss Eyres, 2 Charity, one before &amp; the other after baby arrived. Doris Bower &amp; her dear Mother, Mrs McQuie, &amp; hold your breath <u>one</u> from   <u>Elsie Cock</u>. Quite a number from other friends in the firing-line &amp; nurse friends. It is delightful to hear from one's friends &amp; relations.<br/>Darling little Eddy's drawing &amp; paper cutting arrived safely &amp;  I shall decorate my room with the later. He must be getting a big boy now, what a difference I'll see in him when I return to dear old home. I went to early H.C. on Wednesday-morn (All Saints Day) we have a little Chapel between the two Hospitals. This morning went</p>
