
Monday 24 May 1915Posted letters to Girls, Mollie, Mrs Knowles, Hyman, Travers, Josie Morris, Doyle & Matareiah book. Catholic Press. In Cairo from 4 to 6 p.m. visiting various business places.Cable from Girls awaited me at 6p.m. reporting all well.Wrote to M. M. Bertrand.
Tuesday 25 May 1915Cairo - at bank & other place during the morning At Mena for letters, and to the pyramids & sphinx between 4 & 6-30 pm. Letters from Mollie dated 17 March & from Girls to 20th April. Papers from Mrs Knowles & Dy T [Daily Telegraph] from Girls
Wednesday 26 May 1915Writing, reading & office all morning.In Cairo between 4-30 & 6-30 p.m. Dodging round various places.Writing & reading at night
