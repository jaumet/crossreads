
30 September 1918MondayWent to Randwick in the morning & was measured for surgical boots. In the afternoon saw the eye specialist & have to report back in a fortnights time. Ruby & I went to Carey St later in the evening
1918 October 1TuesdayWent to A.A.H. Mosman this morning & got 13 days Leave granted came back to 204 Victoria St & Ruby and I went out to Carey St later in the evening. Expect to Leave for Canberra in the morning.
