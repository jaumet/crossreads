<p>Thursday Feb 21<br/>Raining again. Davis transferred to 14th A .G.H.<br/>Friday Feb 22<br/>Wet day. Anzac Anzac mounted Div arrived Jericho this morning Casualties light.<br/>Saturday Feb 23<br/>Showery day usual routine.<br/>Sunday Feb 24<br/>1st Brigade &amp; N.Z returning tomorrow. Scottish met with heavy casualties.<br/>Monday Feb 25<br/>Jacko over today. Dropped few bombs.</p>
