Ammunition arrived and placed in dumps.  The following targets were engaged throughout the night & morning at irregular intervals, 3000 rounds expended. (1)  M.G. K.1.a.60.60.   (2)  M.G. K.1.a.70.75. (3)  M.R. K.1.a.95.65. (4)  Cruicifix K.1.a.45.70. (5)  M.G. K.1.a.45.40. (6)  Sunken Road K.1.b. & d. Enemy Artillery directed light harassing fire on roads in & around village of Treux throughout night, consisting chiefly of 77 H.E. Enemy Machine Guns fired short bursts throughout night.  Enemy Aircraft, Nil.
