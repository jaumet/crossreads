<p>the evening it was situated on the side of the hill facing the Tay River and had a beautiful view of the grand city of perth.  Next day Monday made acquaintances at a large and respectable hotel with a large staff, all of which made a great fuss of us and made us at home it was called the salutation hotel , in south St.  although the weather was cold and miserable we had a good time but could not see the country at its best, so on the following Sunday 28th Jan left perth by train at 4.10 in afternoon very cold and went further up north to Inverness a distance of 100 or so mile on the journey up the hills was white with snow.
