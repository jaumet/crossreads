<p>12.5.16<br/>S. G. Jeffes [Lie. Cpt] 5th Fld Amb.<br/>Who first informed Jeff of his best Chums death (Killed on Active Service) Patrol Duty Although he is now above the clouds we will drink to his Bon Voyage above. <u>he served his country.</u> I shall never forget the good work he did at "Quins Post" Gallipoli (One of the B boys)<br/><u>Steve</u></p>
