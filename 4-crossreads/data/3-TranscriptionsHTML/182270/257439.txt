<p>WEDNESDAY, MAY 7, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Shifted the Cow&nbsp;paddock</p>
<p>sheep to Piccadilly in the</p>
<p>morning. Cut chaff &amp; watered</p>
<p>rams in afternoon and rode</p>
<p>round the Hill paddock. Jo &amp; I</p>
<p>got a vixen fox in the Woolshed.</p>
<p>Went round after bunnies in</p>
<p>the car at night &amp; got 8 of them.</p>
