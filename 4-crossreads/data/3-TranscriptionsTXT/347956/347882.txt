
8.2.15
Mon 8thSignalitis, Nelson, Mills, Farrell. Hobson, Cobcroft left here this morning on bikes & had to be a Mena by 10 oclock,  they had twenty rounds of ammunition each & their rifles & blankets besides, the signalling equipment to carry, so they are loaded up & expect to get back tomorrow night.we were signalling under Cpl. Howarth this morning; after dinner the Brigade went out sham fighting till dark, & then dismounted, fed our horses & had one piece of bread & cheese & one piece of bread & jam for tea & then set to work digging trenches & did not finish
