<p>Tuesday 31 July</p>
<p>St. Omer this morning.  I followed a Ford Ambulance out <strike>on</strike> to Racquinghem.  This Amb. was carrying our Colonel.  I was given orders and directions as to where the different parties were to be billetted and then had to see that these instructions were carried out.<br/>Returned to St. Omer and arranged for Daily Papers.<br/>Now living in a barn</p>
