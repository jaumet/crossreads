<p>25/9/17<br/>We are billeted at a farm about two kilo's from Wateau.</p>
<p>There was a good deal of aerial activity last night and Fritz dropped a number of bombs on different villages round here. The battn. marched into billets here this afternoon.</p>
<p>I believe our casualties for the whole affair was about 200 and about 20 of these were killed.</p>
<p>28/9/17<br/>Enemy aeroplanes bombed the tommie camps around</p>
