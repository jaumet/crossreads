It is wonderfull how they stand the bump when they go into a trench & then there nose [indecipherable] straight up in the air as they come out untill they overbalance again & go down on there proper level They can turn easily in there own length. Yesterday a chap near here was boiling his dixie & put a bo Stokes bomb on the side of the fire to rest the dixie on. Of course there was an explosion. The dixie is still missing Fortunately the man had gone away from the fire or he would be the same There are a fair number of accidents but not as many as one would expect considering the way men fool about with Bombs & rifles.
