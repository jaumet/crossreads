<p>[The Diarist has used a page in December to record the following (correct) entries:]<br/>England (Brighton)</p>
<p>August</p>
<p>18 Sun</p>
<p>Arr Southampton 10 am  Left Sth Hampton by train at 5 pm. Arr Brighton 7 pm.</p>
<p>19 Mon</p>
<p>In D.3. Ward Kitcheners Hospital.  Allowed out of bed after inspection by Doctor.</p>
<p>20 Tues</p>
<p>As I was a walking case, I was allowed leave into Brighton from 2 till 9 pm.</p>
<p>21 Wed</p>
<p>All the concerts etc on the Piers free to the wounded  Splendid sisters in Hospital.</p>
