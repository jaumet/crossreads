<p>Decembre 24<br/>We have finished our training stint till after Xmas. Everything points to us having a good Xmas &ndash; although we have had snow &amp; it is very cold. Nothing of importance to note that we have heard of.</p>
<p>Decembre 25<br/>We have had a good Xmas day. All Sections have had plenty to eat for their xmas dinner being able to procure what they wanted from the farming people of the district.</p>
