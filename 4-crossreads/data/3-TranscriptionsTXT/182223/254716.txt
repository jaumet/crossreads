 Thursday 18 November 1915&nbsp; Went to Mena with Dr Henry (from Grafton ) &amp; some others.&nbsp; Climbed the 2 nd  pyramid &amp; waved the Australian flag upon it &ndash; Kephren &ndash;&nbsp; Left Henry &amp; the other men to climb the first pyramid (Cheops) but when I met them again I was told that none of them had reached the top. Brought some pieces of Alabaster from base of 2 d  pyramid. 
 Friday 19 November 1915&nbsp; Board on pyjamas at 12 noon.&nbsp; Went with Major Brown for a trip past the Citadel, the Sulphur Springs to Old ( deserted ) Cairo, near to the Windmil Hills, &amp; home via Gezeirah &amp; Cairo.&nbsp; Writing during evng. 
 Saturday 20 November 1915 &nbsp;  The anniversary of my Fathers death in 1885 R.I.P.&nbsp;&ndash; Posted letters to Girls, Mollie,&nbsp;M. M. Knowles, Geo Varley, &amp; Pharmtical [Pharmaceutical] Journal in Melbourne.&nbsp; At Andebean&#39;s place 6 Shareh&nbsp;El Murah, Heliopolis.&nbsp; Visit [Lieut.] Col. Nicholson at Zeitoun at&nbsp;12-30&nbsp; p.m.&nbsp;Saw L. Col Nicholson at Zeitoun. 
 Sunday 21 November 1915&nbsp; Mass at 9am at Bassilica&nbsp; Writing &amp; reading all day. Visit [Lieut.] Col. Nicholson at Zeitoun at 6 p.m. At Convent of the Africian Mission at Zeitoun from 6-30 to 8-30 p.m. Vespers &amp; Benediction.
