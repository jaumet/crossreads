
Sat. 17-6-'16.Were told that attack of night before had taken unawares a good few - especially 270 Tommies - Ugh! After Lecture we marched to "Lethal Chamber" & were "Gassed & smoked". Then we marched to 1st Div. Baths - a matter of about 10K with full packs up.Here we were given  change of U'clothing & clean Towels. Baths Hot & Cold plunges OK. Another Route march landed us at H.Q. of 7th Batt. Major Jess now Lt. Col. & Capt Bastion addressed us. Mac. Tiche Elliott & self to "C" Coy 10th Platoon (I wanted the 9th). Finally marched off to our respective Companies. Ted Game
