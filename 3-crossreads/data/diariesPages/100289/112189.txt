Saturday 28.10.16
Reported to H?qrs made a couple of sledges which will probably relieve the strain of the 7000 yds carry from the line. Raining hard to-day.
Sunday 29.10.16
Still raining, again reported to H?qrs. Unable to write home for the past fortnight. Issue of rations very poor of late. Fritz shelled heavily in very close proximity to our dug-out. Lads came in after 48 hours.
