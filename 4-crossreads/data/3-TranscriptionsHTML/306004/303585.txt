<p>4.11.16<br/>Sat 4th<br/>Reveille at 6 again this morning had a shower of rain last night &amp; thunder &amp; lightening was heavy &amp; we we expecting a ducking before morning but it did not come &amp; we left Romani at 10 oclock this morning &amp; had a very decent trip to Negiliat arriving at 4 oclock fed our horses, put our horse lines down &amp; made ourselves comfortable &amp; had tea well before dark; It has been cloudy today &amp; looks a little like rain again tonight</p>
<p>5.11.16<br/>Sun 5th<br/>Had a heavy shower of rain at 2 oclock this morning we covered our heads over &amp; managed to keep fairly dry although our blankets got wet &amp; they were rolled in bundles at 6 oclock</p>
