A big force of Turkish cavalry estimated at 6000 men are operating on the plains here guarding the Turkish flank, we have not yet come into touch with them but have to be always on the alert. Tonight we do outpost duty in the Shellal trenches. Apl 19th :- Last night the whole of the Anzac Mounted Division & part of the 2nd Mtd Division with 8 Batteries of 18 Pdr.
