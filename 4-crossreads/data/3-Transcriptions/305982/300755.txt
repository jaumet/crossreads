<p>one continual stream of traffic of all sorts of course military train lines at the right of Albert we arrived here at 5 oclock the usual messing around the place</p>
<p>8/1/17<br/>Nothing of any interest other than the usual noise with the artillery</p>
<p>9/1/17<br/>Supplying fatigue parties etc to day.  plenty of rain during the night darn cold to day ground slightly frozen  I went for a walk &amp; the cold on my face it was not too nice</p>
<p>10/1/17<br/>Same fatigue parties to day  Batt inspected  by Brigadier  plenty of messing around with equipment to the Colonels liking our Coy was picked out by him as the best again.  really it was fine</p>
<p>11/1/17<br/>Very cold today sleet falling off &amp; on during the afternoon  very heavy bombardment last also during the day it was going rather hot.  plenty of mud</p>
