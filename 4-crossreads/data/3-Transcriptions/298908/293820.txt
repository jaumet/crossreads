<p>29th.  Today I went &amp; done some shopping, went up to Gamages &amp; bought some breeches &amp; puttees &amp; a few more things that I wanted.  Gamages is one of the big stores of London &amp; its not a patch on Horderns in my opinion.  My shopping took most of the morning up, so in the afternoon I went &amp; saw "The Maid of Mountains" this is a lovely thing &amp; is drawing great crowds.  Dalys is a nice theatre &amp; has none of those aggravating pillars in the Dress Circle that most theatres are cursed with.<br/>When this was over I made off for Charing Cross &amp; on my way bought some grapes 5/- a lb. I paid for them &amp; also a few white roses 6d. each.  Things are an awful price over here now.  I took these out to Plumstead to the Winters, on arrival there I found Vera in bed with a bad cold.
