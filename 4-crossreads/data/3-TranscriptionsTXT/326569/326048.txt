 Went over to see it &amp; while there met Mr. Southwell. He is in 66th Btn. &amp; was originally in 62nd. 
 Wednesday, 9/5/17 Bitterly cold in morning.  Still working on bombing course during morning &amp; afternoon till 3 p.m. when were dismissed for a football match between B &amp; D Companies.  After tea went to Perham for hot bath. 
 Thursday, 10/5/17 Rained during night &amp; was not so cold in morning.  Bombing practice during day.  Heavy shower rain at dinner time.  After tea went Y.M.C.A. Ludgershall &amp; posted some letters.  Pay Books collected preparatory to disembarkation leave.  Three men shifted to other tents this afternoon.
