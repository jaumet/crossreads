<p>last three years which he has spent in Cairo, have been the most miserable in his life &amp;  this, in his opinion, is the most God-forsaken filthy shameless land on earth, also the East has never produced anybody of intellect   Good man, he merely speaks his opinion (&amp; ours too). The others are not willing to do so</p>
<p>3rd July:-  Monday<br/>Fairly warm &ndash; about 100&deg; &amp; this heat has played on me terribly. Am now feeling &amp; looking wretched. See the doctor about my teeth &amp;  without saying I am sick, he tells me I must be careful &amp; gives me a tonic. Rather cheering, I must look well off colour &amp; feel it too</p>
