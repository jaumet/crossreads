All the way down the harbour we sat on the deck &amp; for the greater part of the time we sat quiet ; as the remark is always being made &ndash; thinking of home? We drew up at the heads at 5pm &amp; anchored, to everybody's dismay as we thought it was to be an indefinite delay. Boats of people then came out in motor boats from Queenscliff &amp; cruised around the Afric securing curious in the way of beer bottle caps, coins, anything in fact that would serve as a memento. Soon after the boats began to take visitors at 1/- each round the troopship &amp; back again. Dense crowds gathered at the wharf waiting, &amp; the boats made a great trade. Hop managed to get 16 notes with my address on, on the various boats. &ndash; one curly haired girl promising to write, As darkness came the men had a sing song on the poop.
