  
 September 1915 
 Friday 3 
 They are very intelligent picking up scraps of English very quickly; especially swear words which they give with great gusto 
 Money is the object of all natives while soldiers are here.  I expect they make a great deal of it. 
 Prices are about the same as at home, but I suspect they should be cheaper. 
 It is hard to know what to pay for things, for one can be sure that natives double the price at least. 
 Safe to give half of what asked I should think.
