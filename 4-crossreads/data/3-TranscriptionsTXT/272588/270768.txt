 Wednesday 11 September Last night the Boshe bombed, causing many casualties. Take over new duties, in A &amp; D  room. The 4th Battn. had a stunt this morning, but was unsuccessful taking their objective but being shelled out again with a good number of casualties. 
 Thursday 12 September Wet off &amp; on all day. Very few casualties to-day.
