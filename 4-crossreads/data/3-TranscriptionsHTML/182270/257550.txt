<p>TUESDAY, AUGUST 26, 1919.</p>
<p>Cold &amp; windy with a couple of very</p>
<p>light showers. At the shed all day</p>
<p>had five shearers going after</p>
<p>lunch. Took what was finished</p>
<p>of wethers down to Middle paddock</p>
<p>Got the ewes &amp; lambs from Mountain</p>
<p>up &amp; into the shed.</p>
<p>Al &amp; Jack Charley, Jo &amp; Maisie went</p>
<p>down to Sydney.</p>
