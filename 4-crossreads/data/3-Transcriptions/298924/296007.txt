<p>Saturday 25 January 1919<br/>Just got a letter from Debrett, England, asking for particulars as to education, birth, etc. etc. for insertion in re D.S.O.<br/>Jim Ayre has a job in Cairo, he sent me some Australian letters he had got &ndash; best rumour that Neilson Mills is going to marry Mrs. Ainslie!!  This from Frank Chase who came into my Brig. for a yarn.<br/>Cold better, may get leave to Cairo tomorrow, this does me good.<br/>3 Australasians arrived together, many thanks for sending same &ndash; also local paper.</p>
