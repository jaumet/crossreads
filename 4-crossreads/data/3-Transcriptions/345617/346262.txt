<p>Decembre 6<br/>Out wiring last night. Very dark making it almost impossible to see. Still freezing hard. Nothing of importance to note.</p>
<p>Decembre 7<br/>Out wiring last night. It was very dark &amp; difficult to work. Nothing of importance to note. Got home this morning about 5 o'clock.</p>
