<p>Tuesday 1 October 1918<br/>Recco, Rayak, Damascus.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 5 h.<br/>Our troops in Damascus 3000.  Turks escaping to the North bombed and gunned, dropped 8 bombs, fired 500 rounds.<br/>For 13 days, 55 h. 25 m., bombs 111, rounds fired 10,500.<br/>[From opposite page] Have done record flying for Sqdn.</p>
<p>Wednesday 2 October 1918<br/>Escort to Recco Rayak.<br/>Pilot Ltd. McGuinness, in air 3 h. 15 m., fired 350 rounds.  Turks evacuated Rayak.<br/>Landed at Haifa at new aerodrome.  A lovely camp right by the sea.</p>
