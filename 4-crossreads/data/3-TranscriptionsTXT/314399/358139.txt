
Thursday 22 May 1919Hot again. Went to Brixton for tea. Missed lecture again. Had some ices with Bill at Arding & Hobbs.
Friday 23 May 1919As usual hot again. Went to the motor class & from there out to Marie's to tea. Bill was there but left at 6 pm. Marie & I took him to the bus & then went visiting but the people weren't home. Went back to the flat & played Rummy & Crib. Moved into new room at the flat. 40/- per week.
