<p>31st Drizzle of rain, nothing much doing. I will be satisfied to stay here until the war is done. There is a church in this village which was built in 1630. Gee! one does encounter some quaint things in the village.<br/>1stNov  still nothing doing<br/>6th Nov Left camp for Boulogne to draw [indecipherable]<br/>(date missing) Arrived back in camp.<br/>20th Left Verchin for Frittemule where we arrived on 25th.What a hole we have been told that we are to stay here over Xmas   .<br/>Dec 3rd Our hopes were dashed to the ground we have under orders to move out up the line. So much for our spell</p>
