<p>1915<br/>Nov. 21 Sun.<br/>Spent the afternoon with the Armenian Scouts &amp; met a Sth. Australian S.M. who came over in recent No. 2 G.H. Reinforcements. The Armenian Scoutmaster has just returned to Cairo after being three months with the British Troops at Suvla as Interpreter.</p>
