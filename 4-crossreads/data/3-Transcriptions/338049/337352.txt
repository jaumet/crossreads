<p>Wednesday 22<br/><strike>Things as usual but a</strike> Up on racecourse to try Grey with Sapper Dodd up. I was on Hopkins Darky which cast a shoe. Milly too strong for Dodd. Had Darky reshod.</p>
<p>After to Races (8th Bde) &amp; watched couple of races &amp; met Jimmy Force. O.W.C. Then 4 p.m. to Abbeville &amp; knocked about enjoyably.</p>
<p>Hot but thunderstorm about 6 p.m. Very heavy rain for &frac12; hr.</p>
