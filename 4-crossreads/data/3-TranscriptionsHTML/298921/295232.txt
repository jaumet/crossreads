<p>September, 1915<br/>Saturday 18<br/>Went Cairo to join the Reg: remnants at a Dinner &amp; had photo taken to send you sort of run into it by Mess [indecipherable] You will think I'm always being photographed <strike>&amp; wasting L.S.D.</strike> I know I'm no oil painting. Quite forgotten all about our diary over Deadman's Ridge at Anzac, yet I hear our men are still lying there unburied, as no burial time has been granted &amp; it is quite impossible to go over the parapet.<br/><strike>"Take his temperature" Sister.!! "More butter Abdul "stretcher Bearer" if a blood &amp; murder scene etc: etc:</strike></p>
