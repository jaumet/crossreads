This evening a runner came round for me with a note to the effect that I had to report at once to Main Hqrs. So over I go was informed that I was to report to the D.S.M. tomorrow at 10 AM. for the object of getting my Croix de Guerre, I am anxious to have a look at the medal for they say it is rather a nice one.
