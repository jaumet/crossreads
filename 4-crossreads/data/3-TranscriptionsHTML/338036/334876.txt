<p>[1st Mar contd]<br/>and just after dinner a Fritz made a dash across to set fire to one.  The occupants seeing him coming got into the parachutes &amp; came down.  It was a pretty sight.  Fritzs missed with his fire bomb continued on at terrific rate &amp; returned &amp; fired with machine guns without avail.  Our anti aircraft made things too hot &amp; he got back to his lines.  Then appeared from everywhere our aeroplanes and I counted quite close to us about 12.<br/>During the afternoon an Aust mail came &amp; I fared very well getting about 15.  At night walk into the village of Erquinghem</p>
