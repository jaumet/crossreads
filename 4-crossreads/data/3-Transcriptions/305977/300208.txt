<p>May 1915<br/>"B" Squadron came back from the Barage &amp; the other squadrons were ordered to leave tomorrow as typhoid had broken out.  Maadi we found just the same, dust &amp; flies &amp; wind.  Austn mail came in.  I got 2 letters from England &amp; 4 from Australia including letters from Violet Blair, A Blomfield, Alice Sturt &amp; Carrie.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4th<br/>Warm dusty day.  There was a rifle inspection parade in the morning &amp; the usual camp stew for dinner.  No parade in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5th<br/>Another rifle inspection parade.  The whole regiment turned out in the morning to hear the sentence (court martial) read out for Tpr Tovey.  Warm day.  Got paid in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Thursday 6th<br/>Regt had to be ready</p>
