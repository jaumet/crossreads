 The smell was almost stupefying.  On the way back to the ship we had afternoon tea at a pretty refreshment room.  2nd, Purser &amp; self only had a light tea and then went into town for dinner at 7pm.  We had a very nice meal at the Royal and then went to a Variety show.  From here we hired a rickshas and caught the 11 oclock ferry to the Ship.  Wednesday 15th Sept.   Coaling having finished 1400 tons having been taken aboard in the one day we were at 6 oclock this morning taken to a loading berth at the town wharf, where cargo for Sydney was loaded throughout the day.  Most of it is cargo landed from the "Benalla" after the fire she had aboard.
