<p>8th Nov. '17<br/>Have in purse in gold - &pound;20.0.0<br/>Have in purse in notes &ndash; 10.0.0<br/>Have in purse in cash, piastres 31&frac12;</p>
<p>20/9/'18<br/>Matron 9 a.m.  My &frac12; day, obtain it but the boys fail to appear so probably have been conscripted as they thought possible.  Town &amp; 7th hair treatment done.  Very difficult days these &amp; am tired.  No mental help to be got from Miss Goode, just goes on her own way &amp; is quietly against me as far as ward reform goes.</p>
<p>Pt. Woodhouse &ndash; Address, Ashton Rd., near Manchester (Mother).</p>
<p>Capt. Jobson syringes ear with Eusol warm &amp; foments app., quantity discharge.  He explains what very sheet of paper thinness lies bet. middle ear &amp; brain &amp; anyone with m. ear trouble is practically sitting on a pdr. box.  A fall from bycycle has set up meningitis, boy is only 24 &amp; so good &amp; patient.</p>
