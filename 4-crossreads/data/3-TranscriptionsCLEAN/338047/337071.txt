  
 1919 July 27 look well around us & observe well the position in which we were placed.  The Memorial faces North, & a bust of Cecil Rhodes is placed in the temple part, looking  the same way over boundless Africa. - A big sweep of plain country rising to foothills, & finally to snow capped mountains.  We then descended, & walked further around the mountain to his private Zoo, where there was a large collection of animals & birds.  Some of the monkeys were very interesting.
