 On Sept 13/1920 he married Alice Edith Mallott, of Sydney. They had four children - 3 sons & 1 daughter - Thomas Cecil, enlisted R. A. Artillery World War 2 1940-1945 Charles Haley - enlisted R.A.A.F pilot Feb. 1942 - Killed Mar: 31st 1944 at Grafenberg nr Nuremberg final burial Durnbach Cemetery Bad Tolz, Bavaria, aged 20 yrs Nigel Walter - died when a baby. Alison Charlotte - born 1933
