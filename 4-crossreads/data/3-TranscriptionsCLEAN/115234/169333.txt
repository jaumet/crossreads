  27 th  March "18  
 Am writing this sitting on the steps of a wayside shrine of the Virgin & Child Just on the outskirts of the village  - Thieushouk -   We are on the march (And a forced one at that)  The long expected German Offensive has come at  last.  Fritz has broken through the British Line on the Somme.  All the places we (the Australians) took from him last year; he has taken back from the "Tommys".  As usual
