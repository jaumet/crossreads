<p>13  Up late of course, about 8.15. Left Leeds for Swindon at 10.55; both Ethel &amp; Arthur were at the Station to see me off. Cold as blazes &amp; like rain. Left Cheltenham at 3 for Swindon. Feeling jolly tired &amp; a bit hungry Uncle Jim met me at Swindon &amp; we strolled down the main Street to Auntie Nances where we had tea with the family Nance is very much like Em, &amp; they are both like Dad. Ike was at Wootton Bassett Station to meet us. I had no idea that Auntie Sue was so small, but she's jolly nice &amp; I can understand why she got on so well with Mum.
