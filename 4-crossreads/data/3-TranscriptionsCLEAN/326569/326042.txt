
Wednesday, 25/4/17Inclined to be cloudy.  Being discharged tomorrow.  Saw Rugby League match in afternoon between two Australian Pioneer teams.
Thursday, 26/4/17Discharged from hospital this morning.  Came in Ambulance motor as far as No. 5 Camp & walked rest of way to No. 11.  After dinner transferred to No. 12 Camp & in hut No. 13.  Feeling very tired after walk.  Rest of mates in No. 20 hut of 11 Camp in contact for another 24 days.  Pennel & Brigden developing mumps.
Friday, 27/4/17Reported sick in morning & given light duty, but in meantime
