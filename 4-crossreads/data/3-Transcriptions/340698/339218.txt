<p>Horseferry Road and were given money and clothing. Stayed the night at the War Chest Club.</p>
<p>Aeroplanes bombed London last night and did a fair amount of damage.</p>
<p>6/9/17<br/>Paid a visit to the Tower and in the afternoon had a look at Kew Gardens</p>
