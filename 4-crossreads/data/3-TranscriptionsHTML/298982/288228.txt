<p>I scored a 12 group at the 100 yds just missed eight. Eat our ration at midday then at abt 3 we all marched out into the desert. Messed about all day until dark Dismounted &amp; waited &amp; then about 9 we started to dig trenches after a feed of bread &amp; cheese &amp; water. It was hard The soil, also d-d dark candles &amp; rags improvised for light. Much remark about the tucker we were expected to dig trenches on. Dig Dig Dig of Dollys. Back to camp 2 am</p>
