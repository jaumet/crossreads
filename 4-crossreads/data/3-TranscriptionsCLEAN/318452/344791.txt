They pointed a rifle at Him he charged with his stick & they both put up their hands & he brought them in. We are moving   back We have a three days march. 
 Thursday, Nov 15th On Tuesday morning 13th we left Halifax Camp and came into Berthew. The day was fine & the roads fair The men were tired but in good heart Valises were left behind, slept in clothes, no towels soap or toilet requisites next morning Wednesday 14th we came in to Le Neippe
