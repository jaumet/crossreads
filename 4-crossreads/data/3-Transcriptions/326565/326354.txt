<p>Friday 28/4/16<br/>Went to railhead on dental parade to have tooth attended to; returned to camp 3 pm.  Sent to roadhead with stretcher party late in the evening; arrived back in camp 8.30 pm.  Mail day.</p>
<p>Saturday 29/4/16<br/>Most of the day spent covering in the mess shed, which was erected by the Engineers</p>
<p>Sunday 30/4/16<br/>Church Service at 59 Battalion.  Capt. Deck attached to A Section from date.  Rumors of forming a C. Sec to be again attached.</p>
