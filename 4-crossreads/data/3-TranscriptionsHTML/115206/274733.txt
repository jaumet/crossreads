<p>June 1918<br/>Wednesday 12</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  near Pont Noyelles<br/>Weather dull and dismal.<br/>Fritz shelled presumably after balloon but without any<br/>result. A few lobbed <br/>ahead of W. Lines. H.V. 4.  H.E.<br/>Laid up throughout day did not do my shift last night.<br/>Feeling rotten on Strength of Trench fever. <br/>Percy Gray being 103 degrees. Myself 102 degrees, not<br/>too dusty.<br/>Can't admit any tendency on my part to do any ragtime <br/>dancing as I am at present.</p>
