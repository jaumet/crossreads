
Thursday 7 MarchOn fatigue at A.D.S., working on the dugout.Last night Fritz shelled the line for four hours with "mustard gas" & about one hundred casualties to 9th Battn. was the result.  Colonel, Adjutant, & Capt. Phillips (1st F. Amb.), M.O., were gassed.  Some cases were very bad & came down on the light railway, eyes & other parts of the body were affected.  The cases were pitiful. Glorious day.Go up to the posts on Sunday.
Friday 8 MarchWorking on dugout at A.D.S. & it is just about complete. Weather glorious.
