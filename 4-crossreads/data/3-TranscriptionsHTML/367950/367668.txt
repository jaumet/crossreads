<p>(2) Corpl A.G.Wilson. no. 11<br/>An ex naval man but has a rooted objection to discipline and so long as he is not asked to take responsibility then so long is he happy . an expert signaller but on occasion displays a temper which is typical of him being Irish. Very secretive &amp;amp: "close" type of man and as a rule never says more than is necessary, but thinks a great deal. Never speaks of private affairs nor past experiences. Is one of my original signallers who joined with me at Ennoggera and has gone through it all to date doesn't like church.</p>
<p>(3) Corpl C.H.Smith No 217<br/>Sometimes called "Smig" ( a corruption for Smith) a conscientious worker and reliable. very particular and careful about his belongings always takes the greatest care of his kit, horse saddle etc. prone to using bad language very frequently probably it will wear off as he gets older. A typical Steele Rudd type of an Australian lad. A good optimist and a good soldier has never had any previous military experience but so far has turned out well. The pity of it is that his education has been neglected somewhat but which could easily be remedied if he only applied himself to it whole heartedly.</p>
