<p>TUESDAY, APRIL 15, 1919.</p>
<p>46</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm we got inside the</p>
<p>Heads about 6.30 AM &amp; anchored</p>
<p>off Clifton Gardens. Got all sorts</p>
<p>of roumers about quarintine. We</p>
<p>moved to quarintine bay about</p>
<p>6.30 pm &amp; stayed there for the</p>
<p>night. It was not a very warm</p>
<p>welcome home, but still it&#39;s</p>
<p>good to be home.</p>
<p>Days run 214 miles making</p>
<p>a total distance of 12761 miles</p>
<p>traveled in 46 days.</p>
