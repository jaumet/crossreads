 May, 1916 Friday 19 Exam "Standard Tests" before breakfast &amp; "Stoppages" after. Weather positively cool only 91&deg; Go for a walk into Heliopolis returning at 10 pm 
 Saturday 20 Early this morning the men started to march out &amp; we were very glad to see their backs Read pretty well all day &amp; go into Heliopolis again on the evening
