<p>Friday 10 January 1919<br/> Went to Sains again &amp; actually got fixed finally. Shoofed it back to Beaurieux. Back at the old billet.</p>
<p>Saturday 11 January 1919<br/> Took up the Sports Officer again &amp; did a few necessary odd jobs. Kerr is O.C. the Coy now.</p>
