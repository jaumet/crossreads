<p>found there was no horse for me. So I decided to go to the Colonel &amp; beg for Bernard. I did not think that I had a chance of getting him but to my surprise the Colonel agreed immediately so now I have him here with me.</p>
<p>The R.M.O. who is of course my O.C. here drinks like a fish &amp; is a most queer person to handle. He is a Scot &amp; while I have been here has given two men the sack &amp; so I expect he will do the same to me any day especially as he recommended</p>
