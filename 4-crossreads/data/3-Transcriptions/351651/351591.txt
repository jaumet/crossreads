<p>18 October 1914</p>
<p>My dear Babs<br/>We are sailing tomorrow so just a few lines to say good-bye to you &amp; John. Thank you so much for the very nice ties &amp; comforter.<br/>I think we are all glad to get away as taking it all around we've had a dashed rotten time in camp.
