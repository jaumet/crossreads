<p>Tuesday 26 Nov.<br/>Managed a new pair breeches for dance. Afternoon cleaning up after bath. Left Hallencourt about 5.15 p.m. &amp; arrived Abbeville just a bit late.</p>
<p><strike>Waac</strike> Dance in Y.W.C.A. at No. 3 Camp. Plenty of Waacs. Had one dance (miserable failure. Afterwards looked after refreshments. Very successful evening.</p>
