<p>10th<br/>I went for a pleasant bike ride through Rogeant and Moyenville this afternoon.  The country round about is extremely pretty and peaceful.  France must have been a happy land in pre-war days.</p>
<p>11th<br/>In accordance with Corps Orders received at Le Mesnil re the re <strike>direction</strike> organising of each brigade <strike>of</strike> into only three battalions instead of four, the 54th Bn. were amalgamated <strike>to</strike> with the 56th Bn this afternoon.  The 56th Bn. marched over to our village here and both Bns. formed up on the one parade ground as one Battalion.</p>
