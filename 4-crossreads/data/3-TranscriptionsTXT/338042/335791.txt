
Afternoon spent with George Martin and evening (including supper) with Harry - I write a sketch (or is it an apology) entitled "Failed to Start"
Jan 20thSabbath - Pay day - Reading & Writing - We journey in the evening to Steenge (?) where we give a show to 27th Batt. (3rd Div. Artillery) arriving back at Ballieul late in the night.
Jan.21st.Lloyd George says "Fight On! - Change of Programme - Letter to M.B. "Les Miserables"- Fred Reade in Paris.
