The first sleep in a decent bed for some months Saturday Jan 12 Morning Took Tram to Abbasia where General Hospital is situated. Afternoon visited Gezeria[?] Kit stores situated some two miles from Town across River Nile. Bought some Bead Snakes  &amp; other work from Turkish Prisoners. Tea at Grappis (some meal) &amp; evening Concert Party. Sunday Jan 13 Spent morning at Bazaars buy few things. Met Cpl Vine at hostel. Evening met some of the boys &amp; had a good time round City
