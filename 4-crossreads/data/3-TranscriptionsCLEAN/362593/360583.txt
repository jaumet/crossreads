
Friday 2 August 1918Photos.Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 2 h., uneventful, photos very good, congratulated.
Saturday 3 August 1918Photos & Escort to el-Afaleh [El Afule] & Turkies aerodromes.Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 2 h. 20 m., crashed a Hun 2 seater machine reduced to a heap of rubbish.  Gunned troops from 800 ft., fired 700 rounds, did good work.  The scrap was great.  McGuinness is a great pilot.
