<p>I am writing this from Villers B. as I came up here last night &ndash; The Division is I believe pulling out for a few days rest &ndash; Our Infantry could do with a few months after what they have gone through.</p>
<p>Hot weather &amp; the dust in the roads terrific &ndash; the traffic on the roads has been enormous but this will be relieved in a few days if not already the railway will be connected through with the advance</p>
