<p>Sept. 19th<br/>Neuralgia &ndash; visit to dentist. Continuation of good news &amp; bad weather.</p>
<p>Sept. 20th.<br/>"Our boys" beaten at Australian Rules. Mail from Home.</p>
<p>Sept 21st<br/>We win our second game of Soccer, beating 4th Field Amb by 4 goals to nil &ndash; I spent the night at Y.M.C.A with Asher Chowne &ndash; Glorious weather.</p>
<p>Sept 22nd<br/>Wet &amp; stormy weather prevents work or pleasure.</p>
<p>Sept. 23rd<br/>A route march gives us our first distant view of the town of Ypres &ndash; In the afternoon we move camp from Reninghelst to Poperinghe.</p>
