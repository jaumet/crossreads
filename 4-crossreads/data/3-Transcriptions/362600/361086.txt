<p>F 19 Sun<br/>- 1235 letters to 21.5.18.<br/>Very hot day. Play cricket in afternoon. Band plays on oval. Sit in pine all night &amp;  write long letter to M.R.L. Home supper when Ellis comes home. Meet J. Vernon Wrote M.R.L.</p>
<p>F &amp; Hot 20 Mon<br/>Go to S'bury Tea on Lawn meet Frank. Go to Wyndham Rd. Go to meet Cis   &amp; baby. Have supper &amp; just catch Train. Arrive home pretty late rush and charged on Quarters.</p>
<p>F &amp; hot 21 Tues.<br/>Very hot. Dick gets sick &amp; we all [indecipherable] Him. I have a big clean up &amp; I am in a terrible mess. Have supper &amp; write to Aunt Annie.<br/>Send parcel Aunt Annie.</p>
<p>F &amp; hot 22 Wed<br/>Recd 2 letters from Aunt Annie. I am inoculated. E. goes to waac Dance. Dick in bed all day but much better. I read after writing.<br/>Wrote Mum. Nancy Denam. Aunt Annie</p>
<p>W 23 Th<br/>Rain &amp; hail. [beautiful] day. Go to room &amp; read all night. We learn that we are to go to France very soon. Send home photo of Hobden's Dance.</p>
<p>D &amp; windy 24 Fri<br/>Received 6 letters from home. Nettie. Peg. Nell. Grandma. Mrs Niness. Read all night.<br/>Wrote M.R.L.</p>
<p>F 25 Sat<br/>Nice cool day. Play cricket   &amp; get 9. We win by 26 runs. Read all night and get supper afterwards. Recd letter from W. Ha  &amp; Norm Upton. Stan Hodge killed. meditate at home &amp;  after the war. Who goes back with me   &amp; in what relation etc.</p>
