Received several letters from Australia &ndash; from Miss P., Ma &amp; Aunt Sarah.  Answered Aunts &amp; after tea went to town &amp; posted it.</p>
<p>Friday, June 1st 1917<br/>Gas Lecture in morning.  At dinner time issued with rifles &amp; bayonets &amp; took them on parade in afternoon.  Rained about 3 o'clock so were dismissed, rained fairly heavily after tea when being paid.</p>
