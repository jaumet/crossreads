<p>Aug. 1916.<br/>Four other Australians from the 3rd Divn Engrs, who arrived in England a few weeks ago, were in the school &amp; two N.Z. Engrs. from Christchurch.<br/>16th September. 1916.<br/>Sat for final Exam of the school &amp; finished up about 12 noon.  We stayed at Esher that night &amp; left for Christchurch about 10 AM on the 17th.<br/>The school was a very interesting one &amp; there was plenty of work to be done   The last week I was there Capt Courtman, the chief instructor, offered me a commission in the R.Es.</p>
