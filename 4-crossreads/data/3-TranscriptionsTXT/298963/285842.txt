
24thLeft Bapaume for Pozieres, marched over the celebrated road & found it very hard after the soft ground encountered in the line.
25thMarched to Albert for a bath.  Went to football match No. 8 V No. 18, met Tod Nicholson, Raon & C. Bowler.  Came back to Pozieres in time for feed up & concert, it being Anzac day.
26thSaw Dentist - old Les Schooles now a captain, had tooth filled.  Big guns - naval - went up, football in afternoon.
Everything
27thFatigues all day, went to Theipval in afternoon.  Saw hundreds of dead bodies lying about.  18 lb. shrapnel capes lay every few inches.  Not an inch of ground that has not been torn up
