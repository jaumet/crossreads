<p>mixed picture show &amp; variety turns quite good. Had 2 good meals. Started home per Buss at 10.30. It was over full so about 10 of us got on a tailboard arangement, which promptly broke as soon as we started  We knocked around for a time &amp; eventually got a car arriveing home after midnight</p>
<p>Sunday 2nd<br/>Hall &amp; I walked to Wylie a village about 5 miles away. Quite a nice walk &amp; the road was new to us.</p>
<p>Monday 3rd<br/>Just pick &amp; shovel &amp; drains again. The cold is starting Heavy frost last night. Ice in sheltered places all day</p>
