<p>again our engines are changed and we travel towards "Rouen" where I believe our army headquarters are. Soon after midday we arrive at "Sotteville", here we get the first glimpse of the billeting area and troops behind the line, there is also a big clearing hospital here and lines &amp; lines of engines belonging to "Belgium". After passing Sotteville we cross a river and entering the longest tunnel during our trip, taking over ten minutes to get through, as we come out of the darkness of the tunnel we steam into "Rouen", we stay here just long enough to fill up with water &amp; coal, continuing our journey on to "Harve".</p>
