<p>Wednesday 20/3/18. Raining heavily all day- relief postponed&nbsp; had a game of bridge.</p>
<p>Thursday 21/3/18&nbsp; Left by car at 6 am to reconnoitre front- heavy gas shelling by Hun. Took over Bde Front from the &quot;Old Bde&quot;.</p>
<p>Friday 22/3/18. Feeling a bit off color from effects of gas. Saw Ralston re vickers.</p>
<p>Saturday 23/3/18&nbsp; Went around whole support line with Read of Engineers. Hun Offensive Bewteen Arras &amp; St Quentin begun.</p>
<p>Sunday 24/3/18. Took over neucleus Garrisons from 5<sup>th</sup> Bde- Went around</p>
