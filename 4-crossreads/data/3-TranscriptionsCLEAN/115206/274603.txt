  
 February 1918 Saturday 2 
 On the road Reveille at 5.30 am very cold but fine Passed through St Omer and Wizernes at a fairly early hour Arrived at our billets a big shed, at 4 pm. Watered and fed. Short speech by Major congratulating Bty on their smart turn out. Went into Staples with Jamie, Smith or orderly Sgt. Had a feed of eggs and chips, also some fun in an Estaminet. Lost ourselves coming home.
