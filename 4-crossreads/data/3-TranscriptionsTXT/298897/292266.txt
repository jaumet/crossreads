Monday 17th AprilMy 28th birthday & spent in Egypt my last being spent in Lemnos. Nothing doing during the day as we ran out of cement & I could not carry on.  Saw the Prince of Wales again going out with the camel corps. Owing to an officer being allowed to photograph him I seized the opportunity & did likewise.  He was looking particularly well &happy & was good to look upon.  Had a good swim during the afternoon.
Tuesday 18thHad some general drill during the morning also a lecture on saluting & general conduct as complaints had been made.
