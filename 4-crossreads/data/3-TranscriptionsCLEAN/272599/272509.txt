  
 Sept. Sunday 2 1917 
 Fairly easy night, most of the bad cases either dead or evacuated.   Sister Stiggins - Carty & inquisition   Beautiful moonlight night  Conway of 3 in at 3 a.m. Sleep soundly from 8 a.m. to 2 pm.   Up & go to church at W.  Good old Pres. service. & communion.    Arrange walk with Miss S. tomorrow.   Home to the  [indecipherable] .   Walk down village with Frank C. & Lyle - Malaga & Porto.   5.25 francs.
