<p>8.<br/>for the agony of that second dreadful cable telling of my Precious little Mothers death. I was sitting quite alone in my hut at Parkhouse when they handed me the cable &amp; I think I was crazy for a while, &amp; didn't know where to go or what to do.</p>
<p>Capt. Tim Lamrock was adjutant of the A.M.E. depot &amp; was very kind to me, putting his own room at my disposal &amp; trying to help me with advice. I asked him to put me on a draft for France at once as nothing really matters now my Darling has gone &amp; he agreed with me that France would be the best for me as the constant activity, danger &amp; work might keep me occupied &amp; there is</p>
