<p>Whilst in hospital I met several of my old pals including several old St Joseph's College boys &amp; some of them who were in camp with me last April in Australia. Jack &amp; Fred Raff, Jack particularly being one of my best friends &amp;  of S.J.C. to wit is attached to the hospital &amp;  we spend a lot of time together  Frank McAdam of S.J.C. has just returned to Australia from here to complete his medical studies &amp;  Jack Ellis of the same old place is still there. Met Billy Cabban also, he is with the 5th Bgde Artillery, at present on the Canal</p>
