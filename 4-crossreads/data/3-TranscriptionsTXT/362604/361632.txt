
Bulford24.3.18
Dear Mum,
I have received your letter of 13.1.18.  It has been a frightful hot day & I went out for a long walk.  I returned Home late & Ellis, Dick Toppin our mate or I have had no tea yet.  But you just wait we'll make it up when one of my Orderlies returns.  We walked to Amesbury & all around the river banks.  We could only get tea & war cakes.
