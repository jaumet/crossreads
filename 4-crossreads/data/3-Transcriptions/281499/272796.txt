<p>I felt that disgusted &amp; wild with myself that I never uttered a single word, never swore once, not that I don't swear oh no" but I felt that full up, to full in fact for words however the night was fine &amp; by the time we reached our dugouts nearly 4 miles away I was beginning to get dry, &amp; as soon as I got the men sorted out &amp; fixed up I turned in &amp; changed my clothes Dave Murray lent me a clean flannel &amp; I had underpants &amp; socks so I was pretty right for the night<br/>4.30  this morning we were all roused out for fatigues &amp; wasn't there some swearing &amp; cursing we had an early breakfast &amp; away we went up to the firing line to work for the Engineer's, arrived</p>
