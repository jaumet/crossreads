<p>a little farther on hedges of holly, Hawthorn and lots of other trees which we don't know the name of.  We ate some <u>elderberries</u> which also grow in profusion, and some<u>sloes</u> which are like small plums only about twice as sour and about 20 times as bitter.</p>
<p>A body of troops were sent forward in advance we did not know what for until we were passing a clump of bushes when all at once the bushes started to spit fire and the troops that were ambushed had us in easy range.  The advance guard</p>
