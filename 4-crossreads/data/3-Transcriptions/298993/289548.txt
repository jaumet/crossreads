<p>Thursday Oct 5.10.16<br/>Stuffing saddles to day<br/>Friday Oct 6.10.16<br/>Branding camels to day number of my camel 2824.<br/>Saturday Oct 7.10.16<br/>Preparing to leave for the desert to day went into heilopolis to night returned to camp and packed up.<br/>Sunday Oct 8.10.16<br/>Revallie 4 o'clock had Breakfast 5. saddled up and rode to olbassia left about 8 o'clock arrived at Alfadan 5.p.m unloaded saddled up crossed canal and camped five miles out.</p>
