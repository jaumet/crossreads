Here we found that we she were not required  &  so leaving our valises at the house of a French family we took a lorry to Le Cateau. Here a French repatriated prisoner attached himself to us & we secured a Red+ car to the Busigny X road on the main Cambrai route. Another lorry took us a few miles along the road  &  at dark put us down at a village, After a good deal of trouble we secured another lorry &  hanging to its splashboard with one hand  &  to our friend the French  prisoner with the other we arrived
