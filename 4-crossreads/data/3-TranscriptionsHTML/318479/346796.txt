<p>27 June<br/>1, 2, &amp; 3 Platoons go to the range for rifle shooting.  4 Platoon granted leave for Cairo.  We go and see the Pyrimids and had a spin round Cairo in a motor with Sergeant Pulling &amp; corp. Bradford arriving back home at 9 p.m.</p>
<p>28 June<br/>No. 4 on duty.</p>
<p>29 June<br/>Issued with Kahki shorts &amp; shirts.  Go to the range for rifle shooting.  Fire 10 rounds in the "grouping".</p>
<p>30 June<br/>Shooting on the range.</p>
<p>1 July<br/>Finish all the ranges.  I score 105 points.  "A" Coy. marches home to Heliopolis.</p>
<p>2 July<br/>See "Tiny Ryan".  The 7 Brigade arrive at Abbasia Barracks.</p>
<p>3 July<br/>Am sick.  Go on guard at 7-12 PM.  Sergeant Robertson &amp; Corp. Perrol over guard.</p>
<p>4 July<br/>Guard dismissed at 8 AM.  Go into Cairo with 4 mates.  We see the zoo etc. &amp; arrive home at 9 PM.  Webb, O'Hara, Taylor.</p>
<p>5 July<br/>No. 2 Platoon sent on picket duty in Cairo.  27 Batt. arrive.</p>
<p>6 July<br/>"A" Coy. on duty.  Have a yarn with "Tiny" Ryan of the 13th Batt.</p>
<p>7 July<br/>Route march through Heliopolis &amp; out into the desert.  5th of the 13th Battalion leave for the front.  "A" Coy. digging trenches under Lieut. Pye.</p>
<p>8 July<br/>"A" Coy. on duty.  Address by Colonel Unsworth in the Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>9 July<br/>"D" Coy. goes to the rifle range.</p>
<p>10 July<br/>Granted leave for Cairo.</p>
<p>11 July<br/>"A" Coy. on duty.  Am on guard.  Receive letter from home dated June 7th.</p>
