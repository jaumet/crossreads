We are closer to winning the war now than ever we were - the morale of the French both army &amp; civilians alike is astounding when you consider the gruelling time they have had - I was talking to-day to one of our Brigade staff who came back from Paris &amp; although the Hun is only 40 miles from them the people carry on gaily in spite of daily cannonades &amp; nightly bombing's - I wish Australia was as much in the war as these poor people - a few air raids &amp; a bit of shelling in the vicinity of say Martins Place</p>
