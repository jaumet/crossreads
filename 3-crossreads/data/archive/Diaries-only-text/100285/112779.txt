Very hot cloudy this afternoon looks like a change setting in. Had a good hot bath a proper clean up this afternoon then went for a walk round this quiet little village of Harefield, one can scarce believe that's its only 20 minutes run by train from London, in the evening I wrote a few letters in a few minutes I am going round to the pictures which they show nightly here.
6th. Left Harefield again this morning arrived in London about 11 oclock Marched round to Horsferry Rd. to see about our leave etc, here we were delayed for a couple of hours as per usual while the red tape was in action, got away about 1 oclock took my things down to Peel House, then
