 Mon. 21st Aug. 1916. Marched from Albert through Senlis to Warloy &amp; billeted. 
 Tues. 22nd Aug, 
 Marched from Warloy through Contay, Herrisart &amp; Valdemaison to the old apple orchard at La Vicogne &amp; bivouaced. 
 Thurs. 24th Aug. Left La Vicogne &amp; marched through Candas to Fienvillers where we billeted. 
 Sat  Aug. 26th Marched from Fienvillers to Doullens &amp; entrained to Poperinghe &amp; camped in huts at the Scottish Lines about 4 miles the other side. Our turn in Belgium now. 
 Thurs. 31st  Aug. Left Scottish lines which are between Busse Boom &amp; Ouderdom on our advance guard &amp;  marched through Vlamertinghe to Ypres &amp; took over underground billets in the cellars of remains of houses which the Bn. will come &amp; occupy tomorrow as reserves for front line. The town is absolutely a heap of ruins &ndash; Cathedral &amp; Cloth Hall have only parts of wall left.
