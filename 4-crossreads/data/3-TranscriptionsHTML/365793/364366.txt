<p>Spt 16 Saw the lights of Fremantle at 7.30, the pilot came aboard at 8.30 &amp; we anchored in the stream.</p>
<p>17 Passed through the breakwater early this morning but haven't gone alongside yet, &amp; don't expect to. I can hardly realise that just about 15 months ago we were in this self same Harbour on board the Karoola &ndash; bound for Egypt &amp; Gallip' and now we are returning to Sunny New Sth Wales. The WA boys have just gone ashore, but we are not getting off, in<br/>[in margin] Fremantle Sunday</p>
