<p>Mar 31st<br/>Early morning march- game of football- host of Aeroplanes "herring and pears" for lunch- stretcher drill.<br/>Apr 1st<br/>April Fools Day- no practical jokes or false alarms- Football and Boxing under Capt Brownell- Lecture Capt Hamilton "Veneral Disease". Continued Bombardment on British front heard throughout day. Half holiday in afternoon.</p>
