<p>Thursday 16 November 1916<br/>On telephone from 6 a.m. to 10 a.m.<br/>Then signalls as usual.<br/>Got parcel and letter from Miss Macphilamy.</p>
<p>Friday 17 November 1916<br/>All up early and saddled up &amp; left Hod el Ge'Eila owing to lice &amp; crabs etc. being numerous &amp; came to Hod Willagha about 2 miles away.  On telephone from 10 till 2 p.m.  Fixed up bivouach in afternoon.</p>
