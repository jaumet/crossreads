5.5.16Papers from P.O.Coombs.  this mornings work the same as yesterdays.a parade to day  this afternoon as we are going out for a couple of hours to night.  Rumours that the Turks are coming towards us in large numbers.
6.5.16Not a bad night last very interesting while it lasted very quaint ways some fellows have. Parades as usual though this afternoon we went a little further than usual it was a terrible dry stunt chewing sand.  Hell of a wind.  there is a concert at the 13th Eng. camp to night. our Batt band is to play
7.5.16Concert was OK band played Grand programme. some good singers  Boys boxing two bouts, yes very good also fiddles made of kerosene tins. beautiful. The band got very good reception  Shouted beer bandmaster & I had a good walk about 12 oclock.  Church service to day splendid service & lesson
