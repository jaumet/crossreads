We had afternoon tea at the Club and then promenaded again until we met Colonel Adey. Went to a restaurant with him and had some Rhine wine amid luxurious surroundings. Then to Simplicimmus where a concert was in progress. Comic and other turns all in German and a richly dressed and chic audience. We invited two French officers to our table and they proved to be tremendous sports. One called ?Disdonc"!. They were both graduates of St Cyr and very hard and severe in their comments on the Boche.
