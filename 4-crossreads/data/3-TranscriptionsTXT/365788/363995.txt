 5 Oct 16 My dearest Irene, You will know ere this that I have been awarded a Military Medal. Major Barney Somerville got me 10 days leave &amp; we are here together, just making a hurried visit. I had dinner one evening at the Elsmie's &amp; very charming they all were. &ndash; It is raining very hard here, but is a change from the trenches I have been in twice since I received my Commission &amp; am glad to say am A.1.
