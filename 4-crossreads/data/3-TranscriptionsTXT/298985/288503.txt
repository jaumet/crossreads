 Wednesday 17th July 18 In isolation greased my self twice Was mess orderley to day. Was awake for a long time last night &amp; it was like being in a big Rat hole to hear some of the others scratching. The irritation has almost left me Have just listened to quite a good lecture by an Indian. There was nothing very special in the lecture but he brought in a great many funnie little stories. They were quite good &amp; well told. So things went off well
