<p>Thursday Oct 17<br/>The weather still keeps fine and the climate at present is very much like our Australian weather. There is nothing of importance occurring. The Division is getting weaker every day owing to Malaria. It is believed we are staying here until the Sherif of Mecca's forces take over this sector.<br/>Friday Oct 18<br/>Went over to Div Headquarters. On the way saw Turkish landing ground &amp; all that is left of machines stationed here<br/>Saturday Oct 26th</p>
