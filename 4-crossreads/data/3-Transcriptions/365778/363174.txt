<p>Had sinner with Wisdom and went to a concert in Y.M.C.A. hut at night.</p>
<p>Sunday 11/2/17</p>
<p>Church Parade, re-allotment of Officers to Coy's, sore throat &ndash; wrote to Doff &ndash; had parcel from Doff Billies cake.</p>
<p>Monday 12/2/17</p>
<p>Meeting of C.O's at Bde H.Q. had letters from Doff &amp; Nellie.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13/2/17</p>
<p>Orders received to move to SEVERN ELMS as Support Battn took over from 23rd relief finished at 8.30 pm.</p>
<p>Wednesday 14/2/17</p>
<p>Quiet day generally &ndash; had a game of cards at night &ndash; Battalion mostly on fatigues.</p>
