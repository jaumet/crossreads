
Assisted Group line bombing all day.
Saturday - Inspection by G.O.C. Gen. McKay during the morning. This afternoon Smith, Ward & myself ate a 3/- box of strawberries & another box this evening, with condensed milk (cream unobtainable).
This evening I played Bridge with Smith against Ward and Freeman for 6 per 100. I lost 8/3. Wrote Reg & Jess. 
Sunday - Church parade in open air under trees near Wylye Creek. Medical inspection of every man in camp after.
