<p>146<br/>that was in anyway intact that we had seen for 3 months or more. The Germans when leaving had left a lot of Pickelhaubes or helmets. One chap found a pr of German Opera Glasses &ndash; others had taken Souvenirs from the dead and wounded Germans. Our officer, Mr Boilleau had secured a Military decoration &ndash; the German Iron Cross, others secured ribbons.</p>
<p>The Germans, naturally enough, had taken a pride in their decorations.</p>
<p>Another man had brought in a German bayonet to which there was a tassel attached.</p>
<p>This was another feature of the Germans love of comfort.</p>
<p>They would have a tassel attachment even on their pipes as well as their bayonets.</p>
