Friday 1.6.17
Cricket Match A v C Sec. Played with C but were badly beaten by A. Have had no practice, consequently in very poor form
Saturday 2.6.17
Colonel went on leave to Blighty for the 3rd time. A sec played B cricket won by 4 wickets Still no sign of a move
Sunday 3.6.17
Church service in Church Army hut. C sec played B won by 10 wickets.
Scored 0-10 not out also took 5 for 25. Left at 5 pm by car for the Divisional Gas school at Becourt. Walked from there to Albert at night. Expect to be here for about 3 days.
