<p>(app. 3 miles) &amp; back.  This was fine &amp; the most pleasant &amp; real French country I have seen.  On return to Rouen, we see the Belge off to his hospital &amp; return home to a talk &amp; bed.  (Belge has just gained the "Ordre de Leopold II" &amp; is most happy.  A fine lad &amp; has now to undergo an operation again.)</p>
<p>9th Oct. &ndash; Monday<br/>A cold <strike>rain</strike> day, but no rain.  To-day is the day after all this time of waiting, at 10 a.m. Capt. Mackay reads the order to depart &amp; to-morrow</p>
