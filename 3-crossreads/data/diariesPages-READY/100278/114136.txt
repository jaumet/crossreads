As we were passing down the road we saw a lot of German working on the road and one of them stood to attention and saluted one of our officers gravely, the officer of course returned the compliment 
The day was nice and sunny and we thoroughly enjoyed the ride, we passed down the high road leading to Baupaume, it is one of the National roads of France and is a beauty nice and wide and well made and it is as straight as a die for miles and miles and both sides of the road is lined with trees, now all lined stripped of their beautiful green leaves for the cold autumn winds have done their work and they look very gaunt and bare, but what a beautiful sight they are in the summer.
