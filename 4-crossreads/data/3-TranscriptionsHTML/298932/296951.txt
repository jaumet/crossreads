<p>BELGIAN BATTERY. CR<br/>Friday 14<br/>Now in the "Valley of Confusion" (near Ypres), guns men horses motors wagons &amp; trains, going and coming like a huge ant bed, the front was quiet<br/>Saturday 15<br/>It is a wonderful war and to folks who have never been in war zones it is beyond realisation "Ah" me it is bewildering<br/>Sunday 16<br/> On our right a heavy artillery duel, above us a fleet of german planes who are dropping bombs and all around us machine guns are firing the din is terrifying Oh what a Sunday</p>
