Nov. 18th  Sunday - I stayed at home all day & had a good rest - Went to Church in the evening with my Aunt.
 
Nov. 19th  I went up to town in the morning & saw The Tower, & St Pauls Cathedral. went up into the whispering gallery & heard the history of same whispered on one side of the dome to be heard distinctly on the other side. I Bought a book I had heard a lot about - "Raymond" by Sir Oliver Lodge. & returned to Kingston - The weather miserably dull but fine.
 
Nov. 20th  I took my cousin to Mme Taussauds in Baker Street in the morning. we had dinner at Slaters restaurant in the Strand & afterwards met Mr M. to go to Bairnsfathers "Better ole" but we couldnt get seats, so we Went to the Palladium & saw Geo Robey - We got back home 6.30.P.M.
 
Nov 21st  I went to Euston to enquire about Glasgow trains etc. I had dinner at Victoria St Y.M.C.A. & wrote some letters. Went to the Bank & back home. Left Kingston at 9.30 P.M. & caught 11.30 P.M. train to Glasgow to see a friend.
