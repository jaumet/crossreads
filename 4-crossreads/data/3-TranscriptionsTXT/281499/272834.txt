 ground almost, you can't beat getting in first Got hold of a sovereign today they are pretty rare things nowadays, so rare that I have almost forgotten what they were like. One of our chaps collared a pretty expensive turnip the other day he pulled it out of a little patch as we were marching along the road, now this is very much against the rules &amp; an officer caught him &amp; sent him up, the turnip cost according to the fine 25/-, so now whenever the boys see turnips they sing out to one another &amp; ask "who wants a turnip" 17th.  This morning we rose fairly late for all were very tired after our trying 3 days march, &amp; I was stiff &amp; sore all over, from the effects of the football
