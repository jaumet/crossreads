  
 [Letter from Brig. Gen. William Holmes] Head Qrs 5th Inf Brigade 14th June 16 
 Dear Mr Justice Ferguson One month ago it gave me great pleasure to write to you in praise of your sons gallant conduct in action on 5th May now it is my sad duty to sincerely sympathise with you and your family on account of his sad death made glorious however from the fact that he gave his young life so full of promise for his King and Country.
