<p>4 July 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Small Turks raid, 2 prisoners-<br/><strike>Got some letters from England &amp; Dolly alls well-</strike><br/>We sleep in our Boots &amp; toujour Pres is the order   Killed 1 centipede 1 Scorpion in "Bivy" interest has gone for fights with Tarantulas<br/>Will post this as I don't want it to be found by anyone-  We go out of the Valley on 13th so worry not mother alls well &amp; I am feeling very fit X considering but in a state of wonder re that "Cable" via America &amp; what you &amp; Day are doing- Love. X</p>
