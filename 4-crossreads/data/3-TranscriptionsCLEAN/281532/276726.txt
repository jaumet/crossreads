
4/10/18 Beaurevoir
The attack in the main was a success as we got to within 200 yards of our objective but during the morning our casualty list grew and grew. In all about 20 odd prisoners passed through us going rearwards. Joe Greene has had a very rough time & men returning from him say all there are wiped out. Dawsett is dead and Billy Braithwaite has also run his race. About 60 wounded and others lying around. Our forward telephone lines were cut again and again and at last I decided to go up myself and find out how things were. Found K. Anderson and his men with our Dr. and R.A.P. on top of a crest and had a very rough passage getting up to the place. A few wounded were lying here - Splatt the driver was badly hit through the body.
