<p>1916 September 10<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Scotch &ndash; Canadian conducts service in mess Room at 10.30 a. m. very good. Fine singing. In aft. we wait for Tony who does not arrive. Cart, Bob &amp; self go for walk [indecipherable] to next village. Fascinating waitress in tea room. Buy two brass serviette rings. Back to service at Canadian hospital. Another good time. Y.M.C.A after, then tea &amp; biscuit.</p>
