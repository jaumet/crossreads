<p>July 1 the shortsighted old lady who industriously studies her newspaper, &amp; mayhap gently strafes "That naughty man The Kaiser, &amp; those <u>horned, horrid</u> Germans. And there is a rather nice girl (with a nice long plait of dark hair) who rides a cycle &amp; smiles sweetly. Apparently she hails from the country or some inland town; &amp; is well looked after by MA! (Hang MA!) And there are many prams &ndash; an occasional one conveying two passengers. Well built men of 45 &amp; upwards are not altogether absent, doggy ladies</p>
