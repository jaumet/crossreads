<p>Give Annie Howard my fond love   &amp;  tell her I shall write to her soon. Did she get my little note I sent her. Suppose you have (Belle) had your holiday with her.</p>
<p>Seem to be spending a fair amount of clothing etc. My clothes are (under garments) needing replenishing. So my extra money is being spent. Still have heaps to go on with.</p>
<p>Fondest love   &amp;  kisses to you all. Heaps of Good Luck</p>
<p>From<br/>Edith</p>
