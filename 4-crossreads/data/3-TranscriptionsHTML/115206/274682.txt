<p>April 1918<br/>Monday 22</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours   D.R.<br/>Went out on reconnaissance with Russell and shewed him plenty of the back areas, - second positions.<br/>Weather beautiful, fine and clear.<br/>Fritz dropped his advance  bombs for supper last night.<br/>Fritz shelling lightly back areas. Cleaned up all my saddle appointments.<br/>Wrote seven letters.<br/>Bomb went up to Aubigny on Buzzer Unit.</p>
