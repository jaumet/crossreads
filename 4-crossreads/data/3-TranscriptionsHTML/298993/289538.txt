<p>friday August 4.8.16<br/>calmer to day<br/>Saturday. Aug 5.8.16<br/>calm to day<br/>Sunday. Aug 6.8.16<br/>Arrived in aden 6 A.M very hot morning went ashore in afternoon saw the camels very bad Beer had one drink.<br/>Monday Aug 7.8.16<br/>Still at Aden went on shore in evening got 2 Pipes very hot had a motor ride out to the town and back, all natives very cute lot white Peigon on boat. Wrote home.</p>
