<p>November 1918.<br/>Thursday 28</p>
<p>Wembley . On furlough<br/>Went up on early train with Mr.Jones and Vic to Victoria.<br/>Met "O" at 1 pm on Marlebone Station and received smithy's wire.<br/>Met Smithy at 2.25 pm at Kings Cross.<br/>Went down to Baker St and met Margeurite Vaugnat also Miss Westport.<br/>Made arrangements for tomorrow. Left cakes in the carriage.</p>
