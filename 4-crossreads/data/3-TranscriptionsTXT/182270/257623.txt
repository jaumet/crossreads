 FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 7, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; hot. Lopped in Top paddock 
 in morning &amp; the Boundary in 
 evening. The casing in the 
 Middle bore slipped about a 
 foot &amp; George &amp; I raised it with 
 a lever. We got one strain of fence 
 wired and strained. 
 Mum &amp; Al &amp; Ray Battye arrived 
 in the car about 10.30 pm.
