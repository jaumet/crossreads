<p>9-6-16<br/>Fri 9th<br/>Received orders today to be ready to leave at 4 oclock tomorrow morning to raid another Turkish camp;  Were issued with iron rations this evening &amp; I went to the Y.M.C.A a couple of times to see if any eatables had arrived but there was nothing but smokes &amp; we were busy this evening getting things ready to move</p>
<p>10-6-16<br/>Sat 10th<br/>We were called at 2 oclock this morning &amp; fed the horses. had breakfast &amp; had to bustle to get on parade;  The 1st Bde of L.H. &amp; a <strike>Sqd</strike> Regt of New Zealanders left Romani at 3.30 A.M. &amp; watered our horses at Qatia about 6.30, &amp; soon after leaving Qatia our plane flew over us &amp; dropped a message, &amp; the went on scouting;  We arrived at Hod. El. Sagia at 11 oclock &amp; fed &amp; watered our horses &amp; rested for the remainder of the day, the water supply here is</p>
