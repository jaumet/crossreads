<p>Saturday 23 September 1916<br/>On guard all day, pretty easy time as we only had to sit down at it.<br/>Wrote to Mother (19).</p>
<p>Sunday 24 September 1916<br/>Roy and I went to Communion in Y.M.C.A.<br/>Did some washing.<br/>Church parade in evening.</p>
