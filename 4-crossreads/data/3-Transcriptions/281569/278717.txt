<p>14 August, 1916 Monday</p>
<p>96 wks from home.</p>
<p>Same old routine during the day, after lunch went down for a swim which was highly successful.</p>
<p>Mail arrived I got 4, 1 from Nell whose mother is far from well.</p>
<p>15 August, 1916 Tuesday</p>
<p>Nothing unusual</p>
