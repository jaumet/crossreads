<p>Meeting quite a number of old friends   &amp;  they are a jolly crowd.</p>
<p>The country is looking beautiful   &amp;  the weather is charming. The nights are passing quickly. The twilight is so late, quite light after 9 p.m.   &amp;  then moonlight at 4 a.m.</p>
