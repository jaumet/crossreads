<p>Royal Australian Navy</p>
<p>H.M.A.S. &quot;Australia&quot;,<br />at Simpsonhafen.<br />26th September, 1914.</p>
<p>Sir,<br />I have the honour to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of 26th September forwarding me a copy of your despatch t the Minister of Defence dated 23rd September on the subject of the terms of surrender of German New Guinea.</p>
<p>2.&nbsp; I wish to thank you for your courtesy in forwarding the same for my perusal.</p>
<p>3.&nbsp; I have no doubt that the future will prove the correctness of your opinion.</p>
<p>I have the honour to be,<br />Sir,<br />Your obedient Servant,</p>
<p>George Patey<br />Vice Admiral.</p>
<p>The Administrator,<br />Rabaul.</p>
