<p>2/3/17<br/>Still doing good work all along the Line.  Germans falling back it is thought to Cambrai.</p>
<p>3/3/17<br/>Still carrying out work about "Bernafoy", "Longueval", "Delville Wood", etc.  Still good news along Front.</p>
<p>4th<br/>"Bavarian Foot Guards" attack 1st Div. last night near Thilloy &amp; were badly beaten nearly a whole Coy taken prisoners beside many being <strike>taken</strike> killed &amp; wounded.</p>
<p>5th<br/>Nothing fresh going.  Still working on Hutting, etc.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Things going well along Front.  Still working on Hutting &amp; general improvement behind the Lines.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Joined Coy. at Bulls Run near Guidecourt.</p>
<p>8th<br/>Section doing work on Brde. H.Q. near Fleurs &amp; Guedecourt also Guedecourt also putting in M.G. positions &amp; doing work on Coy. H.Q.</p>
<p>9th<br/>Left for Becordel for eye treatment &amp; arrived about 10 p.m.</p>
<p>10th<br/>Find I will have to wait for a week for treatment owing to a large number of patients waiting.  Went to <strike>Belb</strike>  Bellevue Farm &amp; saw Reg Monaghan.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Had a stroll into Albert.  Paid a visit to Y.M.C.A. &amp; stayed for Service at night.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Met Charlie Monaghan &amp; had a talk to Reg.  Still waiting for eye treatment.</p>
<p>13th<br/>Went to see Reg Monaghan at night.  Charlie M. also came.  Good news of advancement in Front.</p>
<p>14th<br/>Have made mistake in dates, all should be one day ahead.  Missed a day somewhere.</p>
