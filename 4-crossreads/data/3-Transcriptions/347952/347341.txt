<p>saying that they will compete against any soldiers in the world so thats saying something. Australian people can be proud of their sons. They undoubtedly accomplished the impossible. Well Mother &amp; family I hope you get this alright. I always told you that if my <u>time</u> had come I would get hit, but it evidently hasen't come as I am very much alive. I will know this week what is too be done with me, I have to go before the board for medical examination too see if I am still fit to go back to the trenches, if not they will send me home. I will wish you all good luck &amp; take care of yourself &amp; don,t worry, Give my love to all.  I am always thinking of you good bye &amp; God bless you all<br/><br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I am, Yours<br/><br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   devotedly,<br/><br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  Laurie Hyder.<br/>Big X to Bertha &amp; one X for yourself to the girls X &amp; kiddies XX.</p>
