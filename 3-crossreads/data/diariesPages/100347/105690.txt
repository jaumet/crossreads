Saturday March 22nd Codford
caught the 7.30 am arrived in London at 10.30 : went to the Aldwych cleaned up : joined a party to see the guards procession : the got a good welcome especially the Prince of Wales : concert at night at the Aldwych theatre very enjoyable
Sunday March 23rd London
went to Petticoat Lane in morn had some fun down there : stroll through the Horse Guards parade in afternoon seen the Hun tanks then strolled down Rotten Row around the Serpentine : pictures at night
Monday March 24th London
stroll round during day : out to Harringay at night had a good talk : am going there to stay from tomorrow 
Tuesday March 25th London
went for a stroll in morn : to Windsor Castle by invitation in afternoon were supplied with refreshments by Princess Alice other ladies : nice : took photo of her daughter, Lady May Cambridge : Castle grounds interesting especially the old belfry : went to Eton College seen all through it : out to Harringay had an "evening" of music etc : pleasant
