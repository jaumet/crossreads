<p>20 October 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Still here- Kantara East No.24 Stationery Hospital<br/>This place has grown into an immense Canvas town since the days we first crossed the Canal-<br/>No comforts here, Bully beef for lunch!! &amp;  black bread the <u>best</u> food goes to the front line which is right-<br/>Our Tummies are upset from Quinine- but we are all much better.<br/>X An orderly put the wrong end of the Thermometer in my mouth &amp; was quite satisfied, another took mine this morning instead of another officer who was being discharged &amp; who occupied the bed along side. X</p>
