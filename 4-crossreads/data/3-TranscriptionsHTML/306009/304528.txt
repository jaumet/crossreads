<p>1915<br/>Dec. 12 Sun.<br/>Went into Cairo in the afternoon. Visited Armenian Scouts, had haircut &amp; then afternoon tea at Groppi's. Back to Gezira for dinner.<br/>What a rotten hole Cairo seems after Alex.<br/>[Note, Groppi had restaurants in Alex, and Cairo]</p>
