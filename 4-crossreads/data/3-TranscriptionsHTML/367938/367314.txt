<p>I wonder how recruiting is since you've heard of our toil. I hope the digging out of the men is going on &ndash; Under 21 or 22 are no good &ndash; &ndash; &ndash; &ndash; &ndash; The best that Australia can produce came with the 1st Division &ndash; they had it in the blood &ndash; &ndash; &ndash; &ndash; &ndash; Some are said to have advanced with tears streaming down their faces, as their pals fell one after another &ndash; tears of anger. The Turks got from them quicker than they did from the Bulgarians.</p>
<p>No rain today &ndash; beautiful Mediterannean blue: down the valley I get a glimpse on a hill side of a plateau with 3 Turkish tents still standing &amp; a lot of clothing &amp; things lying about: They went very soon for the 3rd Brigade ran down or slid right on top of them.</p>
<p>I asked mother to tell you I met Chidley &ndash; Chidley the dainty! on the beach &ndash; I admire him. &ndash; &ndash; &ndash; &ndash; &ndash;</p>
<p>Burdett is detached from me &ndash; he is orderly officer to the Lt. Col. (L.H.) who is permanent Commandant of our Post: but is well.</p>
