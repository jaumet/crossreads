<p>19th Dec.<br/>Met Igotz Mendi again somewhere about 50 miles east of Trinidad &amp; proceeded towards the island to coal. But during the night hauled out to Eastward I think they found wireless had been installed on Trinidad or cruisers around</p>
<p>20th Dec.<br/>Hauled to Southrd about 10 am dodging easy</p>
<p><u>Xmas Day</u> 25th Dec<br/>Both ships have been lying stopped since 22nd inst somewhere about 400 miles [indecipherable] E of Trinidad, no chance of getting collier alongside too much swell, boating water  across to her</p>
