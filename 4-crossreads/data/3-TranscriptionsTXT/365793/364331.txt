
Aug 19 midnight tonight. Only 288 during the last 24 hours. Buksheesh Concert ,& as usual the Colonel caused much laughter, but the best singer was a deaf chap.
20 Heavy seas last night & a strong head wind. Crossed the line about midnight. One of the lads (a dentist) extracted a sailor boys tooth, & after the agony a wit advised him to fill the offending molar with sugar & watch it ache. It was a love of a tooth.
