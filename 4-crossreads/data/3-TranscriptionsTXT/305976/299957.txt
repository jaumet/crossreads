
Wednesday Jan 2Visited ruins of Esdud to day. This place was at one time occupied by  Philistines. Was besieged so the story goes for 27 yrs before it fell. Out of Camel Train which left here yesterday for Richon a distance of 10 miles only 120 arrived.Thursday Jan 3Usual routine, Raining like hell.Friday Jan 4Still rainingSaturday Jan 5Heard Conscription failed.
