 Friday 18 October In London in the thickest fog I have yet been in.  Shopping etc. all day.  Dinner at troc. &amp; to the "Maid of the Mountains" in the evening. Return to France to-morrow. 
 Saturday 19 October Leave Victoria St. at 9 a.m. &amp; cross to Calais from Dover.  Crossing fine. Now at rest camp at Calais.
