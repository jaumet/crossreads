<p>30th March 1915 Tuesday</p>
<p>A very hot day.  Hottest we have had up to the present.  Just to show how bright the moonlight is here I have written the above by its aid.</p>
<p>31st March 1915 Wednesday</p>
<p>Had a shooting test to-day.  Five rounds group test &amp; five application.  Pleased to say I passed alright.</p>
<p>1st April 1915 Thursday</p>
<p>Went shooting again.  Failed in the test to-day.  Had a swim yesterday &amp; today in the bath at Mena House Hotel.</p>
