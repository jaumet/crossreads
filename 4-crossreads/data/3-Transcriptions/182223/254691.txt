<p>Monday 23&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />Ramsay Smith relieved of his command at No 1 A.G.H. Why?&nbsp;Barrett relieved of all his Military duties &amp; ordered to confine his energies to Red Cross work. Why? Miss Bell the Matron, formerly, of N<sup>o</sup> 1. A.G.H. is to return to Australia. Why?&nbsp; A real convulsion. I fear some dirty bad work.&nbsp;<br />Saw Smith &amp; Barrett for a few minutes today. <s>Col. Paton</s>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Tuesday 24&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo at Med school &amp; Chemical laboratory during the morning.&nbsp;<br />Major Purdy to lunch.&nbsp;<br />Prof Watson to tea.&nbsp;<br />Placed two men under arrest at 11 p.m., because they brought liquor into the hospital.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Wednesday 25&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp;<br />In Cairo during the morning&nbsp;<br />At No 4 A.H. between 9 &amp; 10 A.M. Talking with Sister Draper.&nbsp;<br />Wrote Watkins, de Largie, &amp; Graham (Hon Mr.)</p>
