During the watch 12 to 4 also heard two German stations (probably in Turkey) working.  Their signals were loud, their business transmitted in German, and the sending typically German, which from what I have heard is indifferent.  At 4am when I was relieved the sea seemed quite black except for the wash from our bows where it was white.
