<p>November 1917<br/>Thursday 15<br/>Went to Beit Duras after water waited all day for water had to go without .<br/>Rejoined Regt. Left here to join B.H.Q. Travelled about 6 miles N.E. camped alongside river plenty of water and ducks, modern villages (one named Nebi Yunus) near</p>
<p>Friday 16<br/>Stayed at river all day. Was plenty of water joined BHQ could see the sea  country most interesting. More modern as we go. Modern buldings people civilised. All land under cultivation. Seems rich soil</p>
