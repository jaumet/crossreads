1915Aug. 20 Fri.Our former Quartermaster &ndash; Capt. C. Gray got the sack when we were out at Mena, and was sent back to Australia for certain irregularities in connection with his department.To-day a new Q.M. arrived for us from Alexandria &ndash; Lieut. Beers &ndash; he was Q.M. of 1st Field Ambulance.W.O. Emmett had been acting Q.M.
