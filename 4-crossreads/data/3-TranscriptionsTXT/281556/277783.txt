 7th Thursday &ndash; Sept &ndash;  Arrived at Poperughe &amp; joined up with Battn there in large big billet holding 4 Coys easily. turned into sleep 5.30am up to bkfst 8am. 
 Roll call &amp; orders recd 9.30 dismissed &amp; wrote Kitty &amp; posted noon. Leave to town from 2 to 8pm. Looked round &amp; spent some time &amp; games in Soldiers Recn Hall then went to dinner. Went to Pictures from 6 to 8pm. very enjoyable. Bought Brooch &amp; Locket souvenirs for K.
