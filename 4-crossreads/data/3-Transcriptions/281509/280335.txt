<p>11 April 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Patient took epileptic fit last night. orderlies take it in turn night duty. Tony has horrible knees thro' sun-burn yesterday. Great water blisters on each.<br/>Pass frequent steamers all day indicating we are drawing near our goal. Red Sea only about 10 miles wide here. Great bare mountain ranges  each side. Most inhospitable [indecipherable] grand looking. Pass 3 more lighthouses. Ward duties all day. Expect  to land tomorrow morning. prepare accordingly. Bob &amp; self take night duty from 3am. to 6. am. 4 a.m. on duty. A most miserable cold night wind nearly blew us out of our hammocks.</p>
