<p>Watching Jeanne at work of an evening I have discovered that she is a pretty girl, slightly sensitive to notice, - that is, not entirelly unaffected, yet very sound.  I think she would respond to well to certain contingensies if necessary, &amp; is deserving of a husband above her station.  I have not fallen in love with her, but I like her a great deal; mostly I think, because she is open &amp; bright.  Even speaking French fairly well as I do, it seems hard to get to know people intimately.</p>
