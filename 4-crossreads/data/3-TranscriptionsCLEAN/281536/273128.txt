Crowds of niggers running up the gangways with their coal baskets and disappearing into the bowels of the ship.
9/8/19 At Orderly Room handed out 28 days detention to as evil a man as ever came before me. Only a bayonet close enough to him kept him in check. Coaling still busily proceeding.
