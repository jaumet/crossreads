<p>January, 1915<br/>Saturday 23<br/>Arrived Aden midday &ndash; high rocky cliffs, not unlike Gibraltar or Quebec:-<br/>As we passed the lighthouse, we noticed the attendants cottage was on fire.<br/>Arab "Dhows" sailing all around but no permission for boats to come near ships &amp; no man or officer is allowed ashore.<br/>Orderly officer today.<br/>Sick men better.<br/>Will post this here &amp; hope you may get it.</p>
