 "Australians" 
 Written by Miss Ethel Campbell (of Durban) after hearing a wealthy Durban Merchant speak disparagingly of Ausies. 
 We are not cotton spinners all, but some love England &amp; her honor yet 
 We stand on the shores of Durban And watch the transports go To England from Australia Hurrying to &amp; fro, Bearing the men of a Nation Who are heroes to the core &ndash; To stand or fall by the Motherland, And they are sending thousands more. We've watched the ships returning With the cripple &amp; the maim With limbs that trail &amp; falter
