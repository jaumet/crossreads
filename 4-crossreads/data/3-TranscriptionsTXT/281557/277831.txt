 Thursday 13th Jan A very calm &amp; hot day : Revellie 6 am : Lecture on venereal diseases by the Doctor at 10 am ; no physical class after tea : no land sighted but passed  a fair few steamers slept on deck : very hot &amp; this is the middle of winter I pity the people in the summer Found that Joe Garbut &amp; S Eather were in Hospital with mumps.
