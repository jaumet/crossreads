 Wednesday 2 April 1919 Did I mention that Bevy Liddles ex husband W. West is on this boat &amp; has now his commission.  I was about the only one who knew his career &amp; kept quiet about it.  Cold better &amp; Diary nearly finished.  Wireless came today saying calling at Albany, Sydney &amp; Adelaide so I will be a week later than Dolly.  I hope some one meets her alright.
