 I haven't seen Rid since he &amp; I went up to see Wal in his dug-out.  He had a cold in his chest at the time, which caused his voice to give out but he was recovering the use of it gradually I am keeping well &amp; everything is running smoothly here.  I hope you are all keeping in good health, &amp; free from the usual winter complaints. About a fortnight ago I sent a parcel containing all my Egyptian negatives which I hope reached you safely. Must close now   With love to you all at "Cairnleith," Your loving son, Leo.
