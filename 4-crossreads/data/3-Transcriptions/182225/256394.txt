<p>1916 &nbsp; MONDAY 2 &nbsp; OCTOBER</p>
<p>Raining all day, but not very cold. With the signallers all day. The Brigade had a concert at night &amp; it was a great success &nbsp;It was good to see &amp; hear some good songs &amp; music again &nbsp;The proceeds were in aid of the sports funds.</p>
