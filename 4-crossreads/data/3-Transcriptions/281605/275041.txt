<p>26 when daylight broke we had entered Port Said &amp; land was visible on both sides &ndash;</p>
<p>29 passed 12 apostles &amp; Hells gate on port side &ndash; during rough sea</p>
<p>31 passed along coast of Africa &amp; late afternoon passed Cape Gardafewue the most easterly point of Africa.</p>
<p>1st 1919 had fancy chess parade &amp; Ball on board</p>
<p>Pulled into Colombo &amp; lay in side break water about 2AM on 7/1/19</p>
<p>Weighed anchor after coaling &amp; watering at 12 NOON 9/1/19 &ndash;</p>
<p>11th cross the line just before midday -</p>
<p>14th passed Cocos Islands during the night &ndash;</p>
<p>21st pulled into &amp; dropped anchor at Fremantle about 6 AM.  afternoon all W.A. troops left in launches    took fruit &amp; vegetables aboard &ndash;</p>
<p>22nd Weighed anchor &amp; left Fremantle about 8.30 AM &amp; run into fairly big Sea. -  &amp; started [sprecy?] for Influenza -</p>
