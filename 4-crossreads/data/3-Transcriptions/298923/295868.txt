<p>21 July 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Fixing up Camp &amp; washing-   Not much of a Church Parade men too tired.<br/>X Got some papers &amp; letter from Laura &amp; the wife   I hope to go down in about 3 weeks.X<br/>Have written to the Mothers of all our killed NCOs &amp; men  a job that is a sad one-   I feel the loss of my Second I/C Capt Badge as we were good pals-  then my horses being all killed after all these years.<br/>X Re my sword-   Wilkinson &amp; Lavender Sydney have it-   any of your friends such as Ernest Officer might collect it. X</p>
