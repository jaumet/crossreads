 The first to go of the fighting units were the 5th &amp; 6th Battalions 4th Light Horse &amp; anything between 40 &amp; 60 guns with artillery, &amp; I think the 8th Field Ambulance luckily the night was fine &amp; the sea smooth &amp; they got away without a hitch each night after this there were big drafts of men leaving, &amp; very soon old Anzac" looked deserted, what few guns were left kept on firing bravely &amp; to us it seemed as if the Turk's must surely notice it, our daring bit of bluff seemed so very thin &amp; poor an excuse, but they were fooled properly. I don't know what they must have been doing for there was a constant stream of small barges  boats all day long
