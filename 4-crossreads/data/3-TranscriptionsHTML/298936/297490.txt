<p>December, 1915</p>
<p>Monday 6</p>
<p>Morphia last night whilst away on the right is a case where the lower part of the jaw has been blown away.</p>
<p>He has to write on paper for what he wants.</p>
<p>One small hospital ship in but that utilized for walking cases, having no accommodation for beds.</p>
<p>Heavy shelling going on all the time around Hospital.</p>
<p>No. 16 Hospital bombarded &amp; one tent smashed to pieces.  About 20 casualties. Poor fellows.</p>
