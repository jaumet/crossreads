<p>In the Trenches<br/>13 Oct 16<br/>My dearest Irene,<br/>Was very delighted to receive your letter dated 20 Aug &amp; am glad you are so pleased &ndash; You will long ere this have heard that I have been awarded a Military medal for stretcher bearing at the first Posieres action.<br/>Well &ndash; I have just come back from England after having 10 days leave there. It was very lovely but all too short. &ndash; Barney Somerville (who is now Lieut Col Somerville AAQMG 2nd Div) took me with him &amp; was awfully good to me. We were in</p>
