<p>1916 August 25<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Breakfast 8 a.m. Do not move off till evening. Spend morning writing letters card etc. have another look at church with Cartwright. Fatigue duty for humpy backed old Frenchy Mean beggar grudges us a bit of wormeaten wood for fuel. Have to carry it back. Mail &ndash; letter from Bill Haas. Put on guard over prisoner till morning  Slight rain.</p>
