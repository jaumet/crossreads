 21 April, Monday 
 F. Lovely Day. 
 Everyone A.W.L. we carry on OK. C.O. arrives unexpectedly &amp; there is a H--- of a scatter to find anyone. All on booze again. I set out for T'worth alone &amp; buy Red Lamp. Home to read bath &amp; to bed. I am thinking by day &amp; dreaming by night. All the time. The boys get rabbits &amp; the game keepers nearly get them. 
 22 April, Tuesday 
 F. I go to Amesbury &amp; Bulford on bike, forget everything. Back for Tea, get medicine but no oil. Senator Pearce makes an inspection of Irrigation Hut but only on the outside. Recd letter from Jennie. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie &amp; Miss Jeffs.
