<p>Friday 15<br/>Back at the ramparts of Ypres, feeling O K and enjoying myself, to day we hold our hands out<br/>Saturday 16<br/>Here I am with 40 Francs and without any means of spending it Today I met Lieutenant Reg Gibbons he looks well<br/>Sunday 17<br/>A nice quiet day, on a nice quiet front. Rather be home, it would be quite alright<br/>Letters from Elsie, Kitty, Emma, Maggie, Miss Caro, Valerie, &amp; Eileen a busy day</p>
