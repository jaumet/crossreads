 Yesterday &amp; to day I have been spraying mumps huts &amp; mounting guard over swill tubs; neither very intelligent, but fairly easy, &amp; leaving plenty of time on my hands. Astonished to find that the infantryman dispenser is my old friend from Brisbane &ndash;Syd Baker. It is rather an awkward position to be waiting to step into the shoes of such a decent chap.
