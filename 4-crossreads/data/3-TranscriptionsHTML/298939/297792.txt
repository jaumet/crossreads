<p>I don't get at all well with A Section officers, however, They dont seem particularly struck with me, &amp; on the whole I am not strangely taken with them so, the feeling is mutual.<br/>From now on I have no desire whatever for stripes.  As long as the war lasts I will work, but then finish with all things military.</p>
