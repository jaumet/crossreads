<p>1917 June</p>
<p>29 Friday</p>
<p>Went to awful Cahil who wd. do nothing. Then to Walsoe's.  Mrs Black took me to Spindloe 10 Portman St. who was charming &amp; then home. Nell came again &amp; I saw her to 'bus &ndash; Wet day. Afternoon tea with Miss Hodge at Caf&eacute;. Such a struggle to try &amp; get a taxi to go to station.</p>
