<p>3.4.15<br/>who got wet also stripped;  &amp; it ended up with 15 or 20 in the water by the time we had finished;  we got the whole of the horses back in 15 minutes, &amp; finished up by one of the lads pushing two of the natives off the boat into the water.  We had the afternoon off &amp; had a look at the Weir &amp; Bridges, also a walk through the gardens, &amp; finished up by a look through the Museum which is full of engineering models.  We heard a rumor to-day of a riot in Cairo last night;  in which 3 Aus. were killed by the Red Caps who fired on them.  To bed early.</p>
