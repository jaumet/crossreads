 MARCH 1917 
 2 FRIDAY 
 Fine &amp; misty. Cleaning harness 
 all morning &amp; out exercising 
 horses in afternoon. Campbell 
 came up &amp; was quite affable 
 &amp; asking about the RFC &amp; my 
 chances of getting into it. 
 got three letters from Linda 
 &amp; some from home &amp; Coomoo 
 Coomoo &amp; was jolly glad to 
 get them. Also a couple 
 of Xmas cards from the 
 Camerons. 
 
 Friday 2 nd &nbsp;March. 
 Fine &amp; Sunny. Lay in till 11 oclock 
 &amp; then did stables. Had a half 
 holiday in afternoon &amp; lay 
 in bed &amp; read. Got an 
 accordian from Steenwerck 
 for the hut.
