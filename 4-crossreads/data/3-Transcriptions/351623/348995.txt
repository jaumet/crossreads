<p>14 Oct 16<br/>With fleet. Fired torpedo for practice</p>
<p>15 Oct 16 )  With fleet<br/>16 Oct 16 )<br/>17 Oct 16 )  With First Battle Squadron of British Navy   [indecipherable]<br/>18 Oct 16 )</p>
<p>19 Oct 16<br/>Lit up all boilers &amp; all ships of fleet heaved anchor. We done 6 inch firing. Fleet all anchored again after few hours.<br/>We got under weigh again in first watch &amp; in company with cruiser Weymouth &amp; some destroyers went to sea.</p>
<p>20 Oct 16<br/>Cruising with destroyers &amp; Cruiser Weymouth in North Sea. In sight of coast of Norway.</p>
<p>21 Oct 16<br/>Arrived at Rosyth, Scotland. Passed under Firth of Forth bridge</p>
