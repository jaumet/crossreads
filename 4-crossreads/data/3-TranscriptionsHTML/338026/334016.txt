<p><u>Rivery.</u></p>
<p>[5 Sun]<br/>Mounted Piquet 7 pm.</p>
<p>[6 Mon]<br/>Cleaning out the mule lines &amp; cleaning harness.</p>
<p>[7 Tues]<br/>2pm. Harnessed up and went with G.S. to the A.S.C. dump for rations.</p>
<p>[8 Wed]<br/>Grooming and grazing, went boating in the evening, <strike>came</strike>  found a drowned soldier. pulled him out on to the bank. Had <strike>nothing</strike> no identification disk. By uniform a Tommy</p>
