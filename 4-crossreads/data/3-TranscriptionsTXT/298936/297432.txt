 October 1915 
 Sunday 3 
 Light duty today for a change. 
 Got some firewood from the hill during day &amp; did little else except write some letters &amp; read "Vanity Fair". 
 Heard that the firing on the left a couple of days ago resulted in 1000 yds gain. 
 Three hospital ships in the bay &amp; several visits from our aeroplanes overhead.
