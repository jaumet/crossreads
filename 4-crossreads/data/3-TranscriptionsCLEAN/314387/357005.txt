  
 Saturday March 24th Relieved of road duty went up and camped in old house 25 Fatigue on road saw great air fight five machine  go  brought to earth night time gas guard 26 Nothing doing in morning after noon moved up to Bancourt. 27 Moved out at twelve o'clock for front line miserable cold [indecipherable] day relieved 53rd Batt. Quiet night a few shells sent over by enemy.
