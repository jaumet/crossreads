<p>Kandahar Barracks<br/>Tidworth</p>
<p>19.11.16</p>
<p>Dear mother,</p>
<p>This letter has been started several times, but something has always cropped up to prevent me going on with it. By this you may understand that though we don't do much here, we take a deuce of a time in doing it. I never worked so hard at nothing before in all my life.</p>
<p>24.11.16</p>
<p>Since writing the above paragraph, all sorts of things have happened. To start with I have left the school &amp; am with the Training Battalion once more.
