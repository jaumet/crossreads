 February, 1915 Monday 15 Moved out at 7 am for Heliopolis to join the regiment, leading out Horses from Remount Depot &ndash; Said Goodbye to Abassia still no Mail &ndash; The men gave me a good Welcome &amp; I am once more beded down in a fresh Camp, Horse feed is bad. Rice Straw a sort of Alfafa &amp; Maize &amp; the horses are getting badly sanded. Hope you can read this scribble.
