Very hot weather with prospect of a change. Sunday 6th May     Cool wind blowing all day.  Heavy gun fire still going on our fellows being mixed up in heavy fighting, 18 casualties in our Amb, two deaths so far, feel it very much as the men lately working with me caught it worst.  Church parade today with a band, the band afterwards treating us to a few pieces one of  of  which recalled Sydney as it was a tune played on every trip of the old Manly steamers called, I believe, "In a Mississippi Cabaret."
