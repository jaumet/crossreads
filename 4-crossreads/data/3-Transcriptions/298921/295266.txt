<p>October, 1915<br/>Friday 22<br/>We are now zig-zagging among the islands, &amp; all hands <u>wear</u> their life belts, boats swing out, etc: etc:  One becomes like a Mohammedan &amp; says to himself if Allah wills it, so be it, still just as well I am a good swimmer. A new name for submarines is <u>Tin</u>Fish:- distinct from Porpoise etc:- which nervous people often mistake for the real thing.</p>
