<p>2 ladies kindly escorted us round to the place on arrival there we were taken out into his garden a beautiful place, &amp; in a few minutes he joined &amp; shook hands all round with us, he then introduced Lady Hamilton to us she is not a bad sort Sir I Hamilton sat down on the grass with us &amp; yarned away quite naturally the talk naturally turned on the old Gallipoli campaign, &amp; many a laugh he had when we told him some of our experiences.<br/>After about an hour spent this way we all made our way to the dining room for afternoon tea was on &amp; a right good spread we had.<br/>Afterwards they started a concert &amp; it was very good we thoroughly enjoyed it, they separated all the</p>
