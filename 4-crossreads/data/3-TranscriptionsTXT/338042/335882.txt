 May 27th Early rise &ndash; As in the old days we pack up our goods &ndash; and after being issued with new gas masks we set out from Bertangles for Flixecourt &ndash; passing through an old residential town of ours &ndash; to wit: "La Chaussee. &ndash; Pirancourt &ndash; Arriving at our destination, we encamp in Huts &ndash; and at 6 p.m. give our "first show" to Officers amp; Men of Fourth Army H.Q's. May 28th:- The "Coves" are being very decently treated but do not altogether care for increased army discipline.
