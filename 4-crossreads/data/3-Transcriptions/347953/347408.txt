<p>The unburied Dead</p>
<p>Of all the ghastly spectacles that a battlefield can bring, the unburied dead in their mangled forms are the most forbearing and compassionate.</p>
<p>These unfortunate heroes fallen in their desperate attempt to vindicate the right are forgotten, and are given practically no sympathy or sentiment which they so thoroughly deserve.</p>
