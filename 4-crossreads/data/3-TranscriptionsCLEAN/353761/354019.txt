The general appearance imparted to the land by this wide-spread pocking and upheaval suggests the visitation of some ill curse of colossal magnitude.Up at the lurid front of raging conflict there is at this moment a sector of the battle-field where several still-rooted trunks of trees splintered and riven, stand the most conspicuous amongst the few remaining indications of a village that had been but is now no more.
