<p>April 1918<br/>Monday 15</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours.   D.R.<br/>Spent a very quiet morning during the afternoon rode to Bonnay via Querrie, Pont Noyelle, La Ne&mdash;and received a fair quantity of mail.<br/>Borrowed a few francs from Ken Gunn.</p>
