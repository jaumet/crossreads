<p>Saturday 6 January 1917 [1918]<br/>On duty evening &amp; Miss Hughes &amp; I fearing she is no gain to our wards.  Matron says I must make her from a 2nd to a 1st class sister but I say no hope, now stuff is not there.  A bad amp. sent in from Kantara &ndash; pus pouring away &amp; very "smelly".  Off duty 2-5 &amp; did sewing etc.</p>
