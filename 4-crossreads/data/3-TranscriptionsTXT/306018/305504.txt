
March 1918Thursday 28thGeneral Chauvel & Staff inspected right through the office this morning.  He seemed to be well satisfied with the way things were carried on here.  Got a letter from Captain Todd in the morning.
Friday Good FridayHad the whole afternoon off.  It was very windy.  Met Mrs Price & a Miss Williams in the afternoon & arranged to call on MondayLeft Bayoud for Hassaniya   Australian mail arrived  English mail arrived
