  Feb Mar 12.  Issued with swords today.  Each of us know about as much about our sword as it knows about us.  Russian classes still continue. 
 The advance party, including Hitchcock and Turner push on upstream today.  Rotten luck I couldn't go with them, still why growl. 
  Feb Mar 15  Rained all night, tent flooded, but all had too good a night at the officers club to care much. 
 Packing up ready to go. 
  Feb Mar 16.  We leave Ashar, by boarding a paddle steamer, our gear is on a raft attached
