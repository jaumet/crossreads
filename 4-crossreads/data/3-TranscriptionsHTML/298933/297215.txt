<p>[December 1918]<br/>22 Sun<br/>At Hammersmith<br/>23 Mon<br/>Wandering about London<br/>24 Tues<br/>Xmas eve I visited the Princes Theatre "Jolly Jack Tar" good show, and then I journeyed to Hampstead and spent the evening with Mr &amp; Mrs Pugh<br/>25 Wed<br/>I attended Westminister Abbey and when the royal party were leaving the church they stopped right by me, "Mary P of Wales looked nicely at me &amp; I looked nicely at her and smiled<br/>Xmas dinner at Wimbledon</p>
