
Thursday 9 August 1917.
Camels in marching order & a trot round the hills near camp. Finished up early this morning at 10.45 a.m.
At 2 p.m. went to 14 A.G.H. to see the boys.
Went to Cairo on leave with Ted Holden & Tom de Gruchy. Had tea in Cairo & then a tram ride to the Citadel just about dusk. Came back to city & went to Groppe's & Liptons & back to camp at 9.45 pm.
Saw where one hole was made & damage done by aeroplane raid by Turks last November on Cairo. Since then the Turks have been driven back 125 miles.
