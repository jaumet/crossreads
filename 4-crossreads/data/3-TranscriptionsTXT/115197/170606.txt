Diary - letters &amp; preparation for to-morrow&#39;s departure.
April 26th
Most reluctantly and with a sad heart I leave &quot;Elm Bank&quot; - and later in town I bid farewell to Mr Scales.
Take 10.15 a.m. train from Paddington to Warminster.&nbsp; Return to Sutton Veny Camp.
Report to Orderly Room &amp; take up new quarters.&nbsp; Blanket
