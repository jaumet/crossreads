<p>solid Dickson, &amp; very strong. His face is reddish with broad effects, &amp; shows more signs of obstinacy &amp; strength than of intelligence. Dickson described his mental attitude well yesterday when he said, "He's got a little box of opinions &amp; when he starts to argue the point he brings it out, opens it, &amp; dishes them out one by one &amp; then ---" I finished the comparison for him. "He shuts the box up, puts it back &amp; goes home, leaving you gasping," said I.</p>
