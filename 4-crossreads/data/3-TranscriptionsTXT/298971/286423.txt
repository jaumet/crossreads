19th October (Thursday)
Resting all day.  Raining &amp; bitterly cold.  Pinching apples and walnuts from the orchards.  Had to cook our own tea in the evening because the transport left last night for Albert.  We follow tomorrow.  Farm people here have a fancy for rabbits,
