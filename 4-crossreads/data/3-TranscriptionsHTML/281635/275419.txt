<p>for good time by machine guns, snipers &amp; shell fire &amp; men then fell very quickly. I lost the Lewis Gun team but when all were held up found the No1 (Tom Eddy) again &ndash; all the others were either casualties or lost. Tom wounded in wrist binding up trench so he made me No1 &amp; I dumped my rifle &amp; took his revolver. After being stuck up about an hour we advanced but I could not keep up with my load &amp; gradually got left behind &amp; kept falling over in mud with fatigue. Fritz started heavily shelling</p>
