 Monday October 22. 1917. (1176.) 
 It is foggy this morning. There are a lot of colds about. I paraded sick & got some medicine for my cough & also got my feet dressed. I was given light duties for the day. I made some [indecipherable] cases today for Jim Lawler & myself out of cartridges. 
 Tuesday October 23. 1917. (1176) 
 Showery this morning. I again paraded sick & was given light duties. There was a wild rumour afloat last night that Holland had declared war on England. I don't believe it & there is no strained relations between the two countries. The papers make no mention of it. 
 There was another air raid on England. 5 Zepelins have been brought down in France, three trapped. They were scattered, hunted, & smashed by French airmen & guns. The Zeppelins were lost in their return from England. 
 To day the war has been raging 42 lunar months - one prophecy of its duration, and yet there is no prospect of its immediate end, though I believe that it will eventually end as suddenly as it began.
