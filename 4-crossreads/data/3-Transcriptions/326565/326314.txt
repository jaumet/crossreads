<p>Walked out about 1&frac12; miles to old field of Tel-el-Kebir; country very flat for miles.</p>
<p>Monday 28/2/16<br/>Morning Squad drill, Afternoon Stretcher drill.  Sgt Ruming transferred to Casualty Clearing Hospital, ill. Went to 30th Btn lines at night &amp; met a number of the Balmain boys.  Meeting of Officers &amp; N.C.O.s in view of organising sports in connection with 8th F Amb.</p>
<p>Tuesday 29/2/16<br/>Tea served out before early morning parade.  Forbidden to drink camp water, owing to outbreak of Enteric.</p>
