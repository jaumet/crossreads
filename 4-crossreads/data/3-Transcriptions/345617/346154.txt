<p>Aout 20<br/>Not much doing today except that in the morning the O.C. Sec. Officers, Sgts &amp; some other NCOs went out on field work &amp; discussed best method of bridging rivers etc.</p>
<p>Aout 21<br/>Doing Coy work in camp &amp; getting ready for another long march tomorrow.</p>
