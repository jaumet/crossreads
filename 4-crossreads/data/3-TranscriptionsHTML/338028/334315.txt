<p>Saturday 13th May<br/>Was fortunate in obtaining 16 hrs leave to visit Cairo and the S M and I rode in early in the a.m. and a man took our horses back. Very hot in Cairo and not enjoyable. Had a busy day, duty and pleasure, arrived back at Tel El Kebir at 10 pm finding everything packed to go the next morning to Kantara.</p>
<p>Sunday 14th May<br/>Packed my Maltese Cart[?] and all away to the stn. I came on later after doing fatigues, boarded train at noon and had pleasant ride through some bestruck[?] ground all the way to Kantara where we unloaded the train.
