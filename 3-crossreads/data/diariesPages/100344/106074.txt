14th-Wednesday-June - 
Raining heavily. Much shelling on front second line.
15th. Thursday.
Very cold weather much fatigue shelling by both sides. great airial activity.
16th. Friday.
Weather good usual fatigues airial activity.
17th. Saturday.
Relieved by 19th Battn. only to go on engineers fatigue till 11pm.
18th. Sunday. Slept in till 10am. Church service in billet evening. Our artillery gave enemy trenches lots later.
19th. Monday.
Dull day. Hot bath after dinner. Truck fatigue
