<p>2 Th August<br/>Very easy Guard at Water.  relieved at 6.30.  Regiment marched out laying cable to Khan  back at 12 night</p>
<p>3 Fri<br/>Very lazy all day.  Leaving for [indecipherable] at 6 pm  about 16 miles<br/>5th Reg carrying out stunt  we took out post on W. Imlay  cold as charity  left W. at 4 am  for Fara</p>
<p>4 Sat<br/>Very sleepy and tired  having a good old sleep</p>
