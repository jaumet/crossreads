
Saturday 20th April
Had a bad time during the night.  Got up for dinner after the Major had seen me.  Watched a football match during the afternoon & about tea time had a recurrence of abdominal pains.  Left me weak & sick, could not even read my mail which I had been longing for for a month past.  Eating only a little toast & some arrowroot.  Luckily have a good bed & am comfortable otherwise.
Sunday 21st AprilHad a fair night & decided to start work again.  Went up to the Dressing room & just as well as we were to be converted into a Main Dressing station & there was a lot to do.  The Major was glad to see me back although still wishing me to take it easy.  Had to increase my staff & make arrangements to deal with more cases, all of which I did during the day, 4 extra cars being attached for evacuation.  The village shelled during the day with several casualties, one French soldier being killed, very unlucky as he was helping some civvies to shift.
