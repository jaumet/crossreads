 [Letter from Garling to his Father, 3rd Aug. 1917. Re allotment &amp; deferred pay 72 France 3rd August 1917 My dear Father, I have been treated very well the last two nights as the mail brought me your letters (3 of 22nd 25th and 26th June) also a number of Heralds &amp; Bulletins &ndash; Apart from the usual pleasure your letters bring, there was the great news of Pats admission to officers school &ndash; Its simply grand, and I know now he's got that far he will come through allright.
