<p>from our destination. tis a freezing night &amp; the ground is all frozen. we get into camp in the afternoon &amp; midnight same night we are harnessed up again to complete the rest of our journey in the early morning, arriving at our waggon lines on the Somme in the early morning of the 18th Nov 1916. there is a sleetie snow falling &amp; bitterly cold. our horses are feeling the cold too after their  journey, some eight days march.
