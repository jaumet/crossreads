<p>June 1917<br/>Saturday 23<br/>Sudden move out after three planes (ours) brought down by Jacks. We rescued the pilots and salvaged all the gear in them. Captured several Bedouins who were thewing and sniping . Set fire to a hut with lots of arms and ammunition.</p>
