<p>September, 1916<br/>Sunday 10<br/>up early &amp; after Breakfast went off round London &amp; over Bridges &amp; looked over buildings etc<br/>returned to Grantham by train leaving Kings X at 8.45<br/>Very tired.</p>
<p>Monday 11<br/>Work as usual<br/>stayed in at night</p>
