<p>our leave again. All Leave is temporary stopped   &amp;  we could not think of leaving while there is so much to be done. We are very busy again &ndash; Have a few  Aust. in but none who, I know or can find out about my pals.</p>
<p>There is great rejoicing over what they have done but at what cost. Anyhow hope it  will hasten the end.</p>
