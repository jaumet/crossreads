 next day &amp; also got out the three days following.  As soon as we got off a mate &amp; I made straight for N.Y. via the Subway.  We first of all changed our money &amp; had just got out when a returned American soldier came up to us &amp; took us off to dinner at his home.  Leaving there we made for Coney Island.  Coney Is. is similar to the White City only on a very much larger scale.  There we had a swim &amp; after tea took a ticket for Steeple Chase Park where we went on all the wild &amp; tame things that seemed worthwhile.  When we came out we had our photos taken in the electric light in 20 minutes &amp; came out like so many Dagoes.  After that we went home. 
 The next day we went up the Woolworth building 793&frac12; ft. high.  It was some view from up there too.  We there met 2 Yankee solders who were expecting to leave that night.  We went with them to the acquarium which is very fine.  After that we visited the Public library &amp; saw papers from 5 June to 26 June, Sydney Morning Herald.
