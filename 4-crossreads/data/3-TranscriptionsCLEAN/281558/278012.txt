  
 the padre from the 20th & a few others : 
 Monday July 16th 
 Bapaume : reveille 4 am : showery : nothing much doing : artillery & 'planes have been more active lately : 
 Tuesday July 17th 
 Bapaume : reveille 6 am : Alf & self went for a long walk in morn : slept in afternoon : bath after tea : 
 Wednesday July 18th 
 Bapaume : reveille 6 am : carried Bob Bradford to the hospital : showery day : 
 Thursday July 19th 
 Bapaume : reveille 6 am : usual parades, etc : 
 Friday July 20th 
 Bapaume : fair day : reveille 6 am : nothing much doing : wrote in afternoon : stand to about 10.30 pm for a "stunt" : we marched over the ridge to take up positions for a counter
