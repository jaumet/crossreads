<p>Nov  23 &ndash; 24</p>
<p>inspected our hammocks this afternoon to see that the ropes ends were properly bound, so that they would not unwind<br/>We passed quite close to the Sokotra Islands early this morning.</p>
<p>Tue 24th<br/>No rain last night.  slept soundly all night in fact too sound, as I found out when I awoke this morning that a handkerchief, I had about 15/- tied up in, had dropped out of my pocket &amp; someone had apparently noticed it before I did, &amp; took possession.</p>
<p>We saw a whale or porpoise this morning about 8.30 A.M. all the boys reckon it was much too big for a porpoise &amp; I believe it was; but did not think whales were to be found  in the tropics; it was within one hundred yards of the boat;  I also saw two lovely fish come up above the water at the same time about [indecipherable] feet long. the</p>
