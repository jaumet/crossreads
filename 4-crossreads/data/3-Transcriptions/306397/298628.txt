<p>6 - 9/9/14<br/>Course to the Bay of Bengal. We cross several trade-routes.<br/>No unusual occurrence.</p>
<p>10/9/14<br/>(At 1.50 am. A steamer came in sight. We approached her and she was stopped. A boat with an examining crew was sent over.)</p>
<p>Nothing new during the day. In the evening I have watch from 6.30 to 8. I promise to shout the steward a bottle of champagne as soon as we catch another steamer which is to be drunk---------.</p>
<p>Middle watch from 12 to 4. About 2 o'clock when I was dozing in my hammock on the signal deck (as the watch was with facilitation) suddenly a white light was seen fore of the starboard side, apparently a steamer.
