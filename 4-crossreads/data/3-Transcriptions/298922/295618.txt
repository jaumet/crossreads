<p>Thursday 15th November 1917</p>
<p><strike>Kelman dinner</strike><br/>Took seats for Dolly &amp; self at Royal for Billeted.</p>
<p>Had lunch at the Club &amp; took rest of luggage to P. &amp; O. Office.</p>
<p>Laura doing well &amp; she has lots of friends who have taken her Flowers &amp; Fruit. We all hope she will be stronger after this.</p>
<p>Foggy days &amp; nights now.</p>
<p>Have read your letter of 23/9/17 to Laura &amp; also one of Aunt Lowes. I hope you will go to Melbourne at Xmas again.</p>
