<p>Military Regulations. One man had to be helped on board by his mates but no trouble whatsoever was caused to anyone in Colombo &amp; no disturbances occurred amongst themselves except a small tiff which ended in a couple of good blows in the barracks &amp; was stopped by Capt. Titford.</p>
<p>We have set a good example for the next crowd to pass this way.</p>
