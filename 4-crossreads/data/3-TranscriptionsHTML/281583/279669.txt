<p>These are some of the prices existing &amp; all food stuffs are practically the same,, Made to suit the pockets of Australian soldiers or as the French people say "Australia plenty money".</p>
<p>I received 2 registered letters from Nina &amp; seeing that I was looking forward to some mail they were very pleasing to me.</p>
<p>Two "tanks" are reported to be up on this front now. So there will be considerable activity shortly. The entire front is at the present time very quiet. Due no doubt to the rough weather we have been having lately. They are taking advantage of the fine weather again down on the Somme &amp; this mornings news contains a fresh advance &amp; a good few prisoners.</p>
<p>10.10.16  Tuesday</p>
<p>The day has passed without anything of any note occurring save that I have had two of my corporals placed under arrest, but hope to get them out of it. Something is about to happen on this front shortly as a French Army Corps has moved up to the Belgian Sector of the Front. Also a couple of English divisions.
