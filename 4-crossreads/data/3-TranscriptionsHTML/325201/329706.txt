<p>Fetch it every day from Bela. Some week ago they peppered Bela  &amp;  the aerodrome with a long range naval gun  &amp;  one shell hole is close beside the road. I hope they don't do it again that's all.<br/>Aug 19th<br/>Quite a number of more or less exciting incidents have happened lately. To start with, one night there was a murderer loose around the camp somewhere. He belonged to the B.W.Is. A rather poisonous crowd</p>
