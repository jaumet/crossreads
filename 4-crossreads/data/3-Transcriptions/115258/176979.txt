<p>round and kick up the dust a bit the YMCA will get dinkum concert parties out from Egypt and will arrange lectures and get up sports. Even out here now they have a lending liberary of books. At times there have been things go wrong but it is generally the fault of having the wrong man in charge ot it. If the YM sevretary is really a man he can do an awful lot of good out here, but when shirkers take the job, to get a safe place away from military service then things soon go to pot. Anyhow we all out here think a great lot of our Y. Emma. and I&#39;m off now to get a drink of iced lemon squash.
