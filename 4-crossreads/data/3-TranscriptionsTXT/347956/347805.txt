Dec Fri 4thI was on duty last night from 6 till 8 with Hobson &amp; from 12 till 4 this morning with Howard; we lifted anchor at 7.30 last night &amp; had a good reception coming through the town, as we passed the French quarters some girls were singing the Marseilles shortly after we passed an English cruiser the band played Brittania &amp; we exchanged cheers with them; a little later we passed two French cruisers.  the band played the Marsailles &amp; I do not think I have heard heartier cheering in my life;  I also heard on the bridge
