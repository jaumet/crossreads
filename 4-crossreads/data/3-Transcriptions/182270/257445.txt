
<p>TUESDAY, MAY 13, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine but stormy all up north.</p>
<p>Round the bottom end in the</p>
<p>morning, &amp; we went up to</p>
<p>the Hill &amp; got some ration (15)</p>
<p>sheep in afternoon.</p>
<p>Linda came out with us in</p>
<p>afternoon &amp; we went for a</p>
<p>drive in the car at night.</p>
