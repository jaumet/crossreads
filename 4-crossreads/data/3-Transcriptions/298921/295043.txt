<p>March, 1915<br/>Thursday 11<br/>Pay day today we get paid every fortnight, it costs me 20 Piastres per day = 4/- for Mess &amp; many of us go to the Continental &amp; Shepherds at week ends. The wags say these are the Bars we will get on our Medals:-<br/>Tomorrow we have a Divisional day, fighting the Lancastrian Brigade &amp; Artillery good instruction, so I'm off to bed early.</p>
