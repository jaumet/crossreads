  
 Feb. 19th Rise 10.30 - Bath & and Breakfast. Report to Horseferry Road - Met Jack Carey, who is minus a leg - Taxi to Waterloo - take 2 p.m. train to Weymouth. Pass through Guildford - Southampton.  Bournemouth etc Destination 6.30 p.m. We are taken to Weymouth (A.I.F. Convalescent Camp) - "Back to the Army Again - (?) - Visit Jubilee Theatre and witness a very poor local show Return to Camp. Feb.20th Breakfast - Walk into Weymouth - Lunch in town
