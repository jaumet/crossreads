<p>19th Batt<br/>Popes Post<br/>5-12-15</p>
<p>To O.C. Ordnance<br/>Will you please replace Garland Mortar out of action with a new one.<br/>We have only the one Mortar on our post &amp; it does good work &amp; we are anxious to have it in place at once.</p>
