  
 Thursday July 5 Mail arrived. Our first mail since leaving Ausy. All the boys excited. Pleased to hear all are well at home & with Glady. Charlie tells me Dulcie is sending him a parcel Friday July 6 Riding test. Practically all passed. Saw first dummy gas attack to day & Scotchmen manoeuvering. Rumoured that big attack coming off. Saturday July 7 Usual drill. Looking forward to getting out of isolation Started Bridge tournament to day to pass away long evenings. Sunday July 8 Inspection by A.D.M.S.
