
26th March Monday 1917
We came here on the 12th and we picked up the anchor this morning at about 9.30 and put to sea escorted by H M S "DORIS" armed with 11 6 inch guns and several smaller guns. The Battleship "EXMOUTH" [4 12 inch guns and 4 6" guns] also left; escorting 3 steamers to Bombay The "DORIS" is only escorting us. We have parted company with the "Exmouth" and her convoy as they are making for Bombay and we to Colombo.
