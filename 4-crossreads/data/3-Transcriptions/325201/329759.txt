<p>Gaza the first day but I don't know.<br/>There are an enormous number of troop up here now &amp;    our communications lie along the main Hebron road, a really splendid metal road, which runs past were we are camped &amp;   while I have been writing <strike>au  our</strike> a neverceasing stream of big motor lorries with stores &amp;    munitions, batteries of guns, long lines of motor ambulances have been passing by. And so, with a  fervent hope for an undisturbed night, to bed.</p>
