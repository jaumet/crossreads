<p>CASH MEMORANDA</p>
<p>Date.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Particulars &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Received</p>
<p>Feb&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For Richmond article<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;in S.M.H.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;17/9.</p>
<p>Sat. 5th</p>
<p>Terribly, terribly cold</p>
<p>Sunday 6th</p>
<p>Did odds &amp; ends in tent</p>
<p>Monday 7th</p>
<p>Serb Christmas. Saw wards &amp; had cake with Treasurer then Dr De Garis. Reid &amp; I went to Salonique Very warm as we got down. Australian dinner at Wht Tower. Staying at 52nd</p>
<p>Tues 8th</p>
<p>Ran around to S.W.H. near camp &amp; back to 52 for lunch &amp; afternoon tea. Concert &amp; refreshment after at night at M.T.O. officers' mess</p>
