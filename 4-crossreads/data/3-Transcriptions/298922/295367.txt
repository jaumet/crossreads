<p>Saturday 10 February 1917</p>
<p>Copied<br/>Packed &amp; said Goodbye to El Arish, going back 4 days march for a spell on the Flank.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through]<br/>Papers from England &amp; magazines from the new wife. Weather warmer but still pampering my neck. And all the way from Bronte came my old African spurs &amp; the lighter racing ones, thank you for posting both.</p>
<p>Also letter card from May saying how busy she is &amp; forwarding her song. Please acknowledge if the Power of Attorney reaches Joe I posted 2 mails ago. A Parcel of Tucker from the wife also came along &ndash; We have a canteen here now &amp; the Railway feed us.</p>
