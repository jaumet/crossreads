It is two years since we joined the Grand Fleet, &amp; our squadron the 2nd L.C.S. has had its fair share of work. 
 28 Nov 18 Steaming along West coast of Scotland &amp; Hebrides. The scenery along the coast line is for the most part bare &amp; devoid of trees. You catch a sight of heather at times, &amp; you see plenty of small streams, running down from the hills. Passed Bullock &amp; Slade Whiskey Distillerary at 8.30 this afternoon. 
 29 Nov 18 Running along Coast of England. Steaming 16 knots.
