
Sunday 27 May 1917.
Crook during early morning.  It rained at 5 p.m. All sleeping above came below. Speed at 7 a.m. 11 knots an hr. Good sea all day. Travelled well. Church parade at 10 a.m. up on boat deck.  Wrote letters down on troop deck. Entered for sports events for Tuesday. 2 more shoals of "flying fish seen by those on upper deck in afternoon. Sing-song after dark & turned in at 8 pm.
Another case of "mumps" today.
Today we passed near Cocos Is. where Emden "was" sunk by Sydney H.M.S.
