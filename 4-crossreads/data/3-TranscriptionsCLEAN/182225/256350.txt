 1916   SATURDAY 19   AUGUST 
 Fine & warm. Out signalling in the morning with the Head quarters. I got leave in afternoon & went in to Salisbury  I had dinner at the Country. Walked round town after dinner & found Harry Armstrong & we went over the Cathedral together after. Had tea at Country with Pat Rutledge - Vern Brown & Watt they have their commissions now & are same class. Armstrong & Self came out in a car in evening. The 27 th  Bat on duty at night
