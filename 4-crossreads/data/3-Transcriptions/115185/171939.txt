<p>Alma Dowe &amp; Patricia Blundell &ndash; both very nice girls. There were two green painted side board affairs in the room &ndash; and I got them placed so they make a screen between Patricia's end and Almas &amp; mine. There are three large windows with shutters and a large two pannel door. The height of the room is enormous &ndash; quite as high as two stories in an ordinary house. The length of the room is 15 of my strides and 8 across.</p>
