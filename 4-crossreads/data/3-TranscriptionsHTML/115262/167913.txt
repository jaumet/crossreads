<p>Saturday 26 May 1917.</p>
<p>A little warmer still today &amp; sea not quite so&nbsp;<s>rough</s>&nbsp;smooth.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Swell as usual and surface a little disturbed.</p>
<p>Physical jerks for 1&frac12; hrs. 10 to 11.30 a.m.</p>
<p>Travelling 10 &frac12; knots an hr.today.</p>
<p>Flying fish (1 shoal) seen this afternoon at the bows of boat.</p>
<p>this morning &quot;Port Sydney&quot; practised with her maxim gun across the water. &nbsp;<s>In afternoon from the stern from boat&nbsp;(A42)&nbsp;the C.O&nbsp;troops on board &amp;another officer practise with revolvers.</s></p>
<p>Cool on&nbsp;upper&nbsp;deck but on troop deck very close tonight.</p>
