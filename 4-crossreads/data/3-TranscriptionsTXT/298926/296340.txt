 19th  We have been shifted to York Place Hospital which is a Tommies Hospital and  seems to be far better managed than Kitcheners Australian Hospital.  There are 3  billiard tables, bagatelle, phonographs, a fine library and scores of minor games. Leave is granted from 2 to 5 pm. but unfortunately for us we cannot get an issue of hat,  coat &amp; boots, so have to stay in.
