<p>1915<br/>May. 2 Sun.<br/>Had a swim before Breakfast this morning.<br/>A detachment of men &amp; some Nursing Sisters were sent off to receive wounded at Ghezireh Palace Hotel.<br/>Examined a Turkish bayonet, waistbelt &amp; pouches secured by one of our patients at the Dardanelles. I guessed I looked some kid in the outfit.</p>
