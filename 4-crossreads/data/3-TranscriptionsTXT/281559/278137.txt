 Wednesday June 26th : Mardyk 
 On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : stroll with Gill after tea : plenty of girls kicking around &amp; we got a few glad eyes thrown at us : 
 Thursday June 27th : Mardyk 
 On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : made a bunk so did not go out after tea 
 Friday June 28th : Mardyk 
 On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : stroll to the aerodrome after tea &amp; had an interesting look round : flying is now a conquered art &amp; the progress of a few years is wonderful : 
 Saturday June 29th : Mardyk 
 On duty 6 &ndash; 2 : did not go out after tea : another raid after dark &amp; a second one before morn &amp; Fritz got a very warm reception : 
 Sunday June 30th 
 My day off so I had a roam round : Gen Birdwood inspected the boys but I have heard enough of his soft soap talk so went out &amp; missed the parade : went to church at night &amp; stayed for Holy Communion : very nice : 
 Monday July 1st : Mardyk : Mardyk 
 Nothing doing in day : on duty 10 pm 
 Tuesday July 2nd 
 Nothing doing in day : on duty 10 pm 
 Wednesday July 3rd : Mardyk 
 Into Dunkirk after tea &amp; had a stroll round to my friends place : one of the Fritz 'planes that came down the other night was on view in the square &amp; it is some wreck : just a few splinters &amp; tatters of cloth left : rather a hideous death
