<p>13th his yarns were amusing &amp; lurid No doubt the landing &amp; driving back of the enemy was a thing which no regular forces would have succeeded in or attempted. Only an amateur army would have been so daring &amp; fool hardy. It must have been a fine piece of work.</p>
<p>14th Saty On reliefs of communication trench digging all day till 4 30 pm Under rifle &amp; occasional shrapnel fire hardly had near</p>
