 as our job was to save the greatest number &amp; it would be hopeless trying to save the weakings as Bijah was the nearest city of refuge.  Next day we rested.  A group of Cavalry rode into a neighbouring village to buy provisions. 6 shots caused them to retire.  In disgust I sent young Wallace &amp; Casey with a couple of our muleteers into the same village where they purchased all we wanted the villages becoming pacified at the sight of our Persian muleteers.  They thought the other people would treat them as the Christians had treated the people of San Jud.
