<p>Dec 26th Thurs:-<br/>My yearly furlough to the United Kingdom has again meandered along so left Bn this afternoon &amp; got leave train from Charleroi for Boulogne at midnight.</p>
<p>Fri Dec 27th:-<br/>Travelling slow &amp; it is raining heavily.<br/>Passed through Ghislain, Douai, Arras &amp; St Pol</p>
<p>Sat Dec 28th:-<br/>Reached Boulogne at 11 am &amp; got the leave boat " SS Victoria" to Folkestone. By train to London &amp; arrived at Victoria Station about 8 am. Stopped night at Maida Vale hostel.</p>
<p>Sun Dec 29th:-<br/>Down to friends at East Dulwich.</p>
<p>Mon Dec 30th:-<br/>Visited the Alhambra Theatre tonight. Bing Boys on Broadway. George Robey &amp; Vi Lorraine.</p>
<p>Tues Dec 31st:-<br/>Coliseum Theatre Variety show</p>
<p>Wed Jan 1st 1919:-<br/>A wet day so off to Madame Tussauds' Waxworks at Baker St.</p>
