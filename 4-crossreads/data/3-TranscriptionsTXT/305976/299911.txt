
Monday Aug 27Boys tell me at this camp that losses at Romani, first & second Gaza were much heavier than officially reportedTuesday Aug 28Captain Sutton arrived in camp to day. Gave offer to join his unit as Sanitary Inspector. May take it on if Tom is willing.Wednesday Aug 29G. Cameron came over with patients Informed us our holiday would be over next week as we would be recalled to unit. Received mail.Thursday Aug 30Swimming nearly all day. Beautiful moonlight night. About 9.30 pm two Taubes came over dropped two bombs. Put the wind up one.
