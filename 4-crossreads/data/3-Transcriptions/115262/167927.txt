<p>Saturday 9 June 1917.</p>
<p>Busy with moving Isolation during morning and in afternoon the 2nd concert of the trip was held at 3 pm. Not much of a success.</p>
<p>Wrote 2 or 3 letters after 2-30 pm.</p>
<p>Rather a very warm day today.</p>
<p>Infectious cases of mumps, &amp; measles are very prevalent on boat now.</p>
