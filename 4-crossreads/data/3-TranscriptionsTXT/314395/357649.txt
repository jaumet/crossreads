He is used in a wheel shaped treadmill, making  crea  butter, washing clothes etc. and is also used very largely in drawing carts.  He is very sturdy, and of great endurance, and intelligence, also a very willing energetic worker, one, two, or three of him will pull a cart, according to size, load, etc., and I have seen him driven six in hand.
