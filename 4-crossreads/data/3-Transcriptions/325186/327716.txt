<p>On parade on 16th Dec we were told by C.O that No 2 Batt of O. T. B comprising 2nd &amp; 3rd Divs would be leaving for Australia during the second ten days of January Christmas soon came round &amp; we were served up with a real good dinner.  The Christmas leave was cut down from 10days to seven &amp; five days  Volunteers for Australia were called for to sail on 3rd January  All those that volunteered had to forgo their leave.  After a lot of fooling around we were finally put in No 6 Coy on a boat roll for the Orsova  Snow fell on Jan 3rd about seven or eight inches</p>
