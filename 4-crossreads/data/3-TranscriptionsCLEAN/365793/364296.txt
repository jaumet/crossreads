
July 15 sundry pots of flowers to brighten things up.(11.30 am) Miss Eileen Boyd (a Sydney girl) & several other artistes entertained us, & handed round smokes. After - the whole party made a tour of the Camp, passing through our mess hut. No 4 Coy (that's us) is now provided with a special rec' room including piano & Billiard Tables. Perched up on the church yard wall & yarned with a School master for about half an hour.
16 Pay day tomorrow, & my leave paper goes in. Church Mafeesh;
