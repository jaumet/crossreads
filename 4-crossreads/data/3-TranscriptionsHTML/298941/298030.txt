<p>Felt very "anyhow" this morning owing to the rough night, &amp; a dose of "jerks" did little to improve matters. At present we are still standing by, so I expect we may be called up at any moment.<br/>Later<br/>The somewhat misty morning has cleared, and it is now a fine Spring evening. It is good for planes, yet at the same time it is a calm &amp; peaceful evening, &amp; makes me almost forget war</p>
