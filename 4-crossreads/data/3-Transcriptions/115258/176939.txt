I hope Father is able to buy James&#39; next year. I suppose he will if the season holds good. We are still back in the peace camp and living pretty high; but it is getting a bit of a bore dont you know - they get us horribly regimental back here. A whole lot of remounts have been coming lately, but so far I have not got a new horse.
