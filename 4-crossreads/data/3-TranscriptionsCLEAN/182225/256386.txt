 1916   SUNDAY 24   SEPT. 
 Fine & warm. Went to the YMCA to church in morning & Poss & self got a car into Salisbury for dinner & then got a car down to Bridport & saw M rs  Suttil & had afternoon tea there & we got back to Dorchester for tea. Had it at the "Kings Arms" hotel & got back here about 11 pm. It was a great run down & very pretty down there. Got news that two more Zeps had been brought down in England.
