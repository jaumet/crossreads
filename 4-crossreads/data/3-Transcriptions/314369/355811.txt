<p>11 April 1915<br/>Sunday<br/>Low Sunday &ndash; 1st After Easter</p>
<p>Sunday morning 5.30 arise   &amp;  get my gear ready mount Guard at 6.45 a.m.<br/>take over 16 prisoners most of which are released to join their Battalion.<br/>Bat moves of at 6.30 pm followed at half hour intervals by the 14th 15th   &amp;  16th with all baggage   &amp;  Kitchens.</p>
<p>1915 April 12<br/>Monday</p>
<p>The work of reorganising the remains begins<br/>All told, there are in camp about 400 of all ranks.<br/>The 1st ALH lend us three officers to help us along as we have only 3 of our own left.</p>
