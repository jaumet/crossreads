Sunday 25 June 1916Wrote Lyd. Goddard &amp; Doris Blackwell.  Matron, 2 men probable enteric.  Ellis &amp; Earn go to Band.  Dave &amp; Billy go to the Kirk.  Night Ward Master.
Monday 26 June 1916Wrote to Mum &amp; George 46-47, Dad 45.  Went to Heliopolis with Q.M.S. Williams.  Saw Pictures &amp; ate Peanuts.  All the other Boys stayed at Home.
Tuesday 27 June 1916Dreamt of Nell &amp; Fanny.  Can't remember anything very startling.
Wednesday 28 June 1916Dreamt again being at Church with Mum.  Played Cards a lot.
Thursday 29 June 1916Wrote to Bess.  Met Dick Rowe.  Remount Unit give concert.
Friday 30 June 1916All day leave.  Saw all the Boys off to Aust. &amp; spent day at Giza &amp; Zoo.  Dave, Bill &amp; I played Billiards &amp; I won one game.  Dreamt of Jess, mother again.
Saturday 1 July 1916Wrote Home (48), Madge, Mrs. Whitehead, Nell, Cuneo.  In charge of Sydney Pavilion &amp; put Patient Blackwell into Mel. 8.
