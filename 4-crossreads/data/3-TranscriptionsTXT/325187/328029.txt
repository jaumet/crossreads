 [Friday 13 October 1916] 
 Gladys Best of Luck Had Gladys Riddler a nice girl friend of Dorothys to dinner at a fashionable restaurant &amp; then to see The Best of Luck at Drury Lane- good thing I had saved my pennies &amp; only have short time in London- couldn't keep going- This morning went to Regents Park for a Blow. Posting you some Cards of the Giants Causeway Mr Ferrar motored me there.  While in Ireland also Dorothys old school now an Hotel &amp; a jolly nice one too Going to see Mardi Ritchie tomorrow.  Expect to leave for Egypt end of this month.
