<p>6 p.m.  Turkey has surrendered.  It is extremely welcome news.  We are now playing the Hun <strike>s</strike> and his Allies their own game &ndash; namely crush the weaker adversaries first.  Both Bulgaria and Turkey have been thoroughly thrashed.  Gen. Allenbury is the leader of the Victorious Army in Palestine which is entirely British.  On top of this is the good news that we are to go out of the line for a long spell.</p>
