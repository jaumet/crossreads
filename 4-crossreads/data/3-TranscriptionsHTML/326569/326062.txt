<p>Tuesday, 29/5/17<br/>Fine morning but rained after dinner so dismissed from parade.  After tea take stroll beyond Rail Stn. towards Poole.</p>
<p>Wednesday 30/5/17<br/>Fine today &ndash; practising with gas helmets now.  After tea went to town &amp; Y.M.C.A. for supper.  They are having boxing contests in the hut tonight.</p>
<p>Thursday, 31/5/17<br/>After going to bed last night was roused up at 10.30 p.m. for telephone in B.O.R.
