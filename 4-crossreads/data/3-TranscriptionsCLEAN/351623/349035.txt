 3 July 18 ) Anchored at Rosyth. 4 July 18 ) A great number of ships company have been bad with influenza. 5 July 18 ) People are hailing with it ashore throughout the country. 
 6 July 18 Admiral of squadron came aboard off HMS Birmingham. Went to sea 7 pm in dogwatches and are flagship of Light cruiser squadrons & destroyers. 
 7 July 18 At sea doing manoeuvres with Admiral aboard. Seas calm. 
 8 July 18 Came in & anchored outside of bridge at Rosyth & coaled. Admiral went aboard his own ship Birmingham as soon as we arrived in. 
 9 July 18 Anchored outside bridge Rosyth. King & Queen of Belgiam visited Fleet today
