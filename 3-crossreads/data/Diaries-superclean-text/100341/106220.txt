Diary of Camp Work.

Aug. 1st
Arrived home from all night manoeuvre about 2nd Tower. Rested all day, letter writing evening.
Aug. 2nd
Lost a/c from 27/6/16 to Aug. 11th.
August 11th
Reveille 4 a.m. Marched through to Citadel, stayed all day, left 5 p.m., arriving Heliopolis 8-45 p.m.
Aug. 12
Bayonet sharpened.
Aug. 25
Packing all day. Left Camp (Heliopolis) 7 p.m. Marched to Zeitoun thence by train to Alexandria, detrained
