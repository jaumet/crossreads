 In the evening Jack came to see me in the Dispensary &amp; he &amp; his friend with Graysmarsh &amp; I, adjourned to the canteen, where to my amazement I disposed of three shandies. 
 Monday 31st January 
 Weather very much better to-day.  Cold nights still. Prisoner brought in this afternoon from the Citadel, who had been a jeweller in town. Arrested &amp; sent to Citadel where he fell sick.  Now in our hospital. Given Turkish rations today of olives &amp; meat cake which he would not eat (of course). Poor fellow is very thin &amp; looks ill &amp; being hours without food
