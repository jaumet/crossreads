
Monday 5 November 1917.
Brigade Orderly despatch till 10.30 am.
At 2.30 pm A.C.F.A moved to main road & relieved N.Z Mtd Fd Amb at 4.30 pm when A Sec. moved out at dusk to the front line for active service. I was in the group as advanced dressing station for the 2nd Battn I.C.C. Bgde.
The whole Bgde was completely lost & never got into communication before midnight tonight when N.Z.'s were relieved.
El JabriEl HawiRas-el NagKuteelfah
