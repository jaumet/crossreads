
September 1918.Thursday 5Attached to 15th A.1. Bdge. Bty position.Pulled into a position ahead of Birleau to carry on withsniping work. On road last night we managed to collar 3miles of metallic wire, a very good find.  Salvaged a fewmiles of wire with Smithy.Major returned to Bty position with 3 L'aison spooks, - not in an excellent humor. A heavy thunderstorm came across telephone put a slightly damp home to rest in Walked to 5th Div Pioneer Camp and saw numerous fellows I knew. Met Eliza Thoresby and Bill Grant, old Bridg Train fellows. Prepared to leave in the morning.
