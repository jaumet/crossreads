  
 April 1918 Thursday 4 
 In Billets - 7pm .12 mid on Road. Spent morning cleaning up bits, and irons. Left for exercise arrived back after dinner. Great number of Tommies miving around village. Went along to Sarton and had a snack; just finished when Percy G. arrived in breathless haste and announced the news that Battery was moving out. Caught up Batt as they were moving out of village and at mid shell on road.
