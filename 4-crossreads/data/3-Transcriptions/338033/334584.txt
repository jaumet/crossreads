<p>And shunted on to a side platform, where generous women supplied us with hot tea, which we appreciated very much, as we felt the cold pretty acutely after the heat in Egypt. While we were out getting tea, more kind ladies placed eight paper bags in each compartment, containing hot buns and a card, with complements from the Mayor and committee of Exeter written on it.
