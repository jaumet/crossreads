<p>Fri 18th<br/>On doing stables this morning   we were told we would have to fall in at 9.30 AM to be marched off for a bath;  We had a couple of miles to march &amp; ended up at a prison where we received the best shower bath I have ever enjoyed in my life.  first cold &amp; then hot &amp; finished up cold;  I suppose colds will be prevalent here now;  after we had finished washing we had to wait till the rest of the <strike>Light</strike> Squadron finished &amp; it was very interesting watching the native prisoners (in chains) being exercised &amp; worked,  by the time we marched back to camp, it was</p>
