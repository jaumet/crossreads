26/8/18 Cappy
extent that we were out of touch with the companies. Went over and had a yarn with Nelligan and Duggan who are mutually relieving. A fine and bright moon tonight. A good amount of ironmongery is flying about. After relief we commenced silent penetration and pushed our line forward nearly  mile. The penetration was not peaceful as Huns were encountered frequently. Harry Wall was killed by a shot through the groin and died almost at once. A veteran of the "landing?, an M.C. winner and a very gallant gentleman. Corporal Rasdell 3 others were killed too and there 5 wounded. We secured 3 prisoners 1 badly wounded from a Mills bomb and covered with bloodstained bandages. The relief went through alright and the succeeding penetration
