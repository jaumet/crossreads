<p>Friday 13 October 1916<br/>Left Kantara early, came through Ishmalia [Ismailia], Tel-el-Kebir, Zagazig, Benha [also known as Banha] and on to Alexandria and then to Sidi Bishr.<br/>Wonderful crops of corn all the way.  Cotton being pulled.  Nile in flood.</p>
<p>Saturday 14 October 1916<br/>Lecture and inspection by Dr. in morning.<br/>Leave from 2 till 11 every night.  Dick West, Aire and I went in for afternoon and had a look round.  Had two good feeds and went to Alhambra vaudeville show.</p>
