 19/10/18 Yzeux 
 A rather dull day. Remained indoors all the morning having a fine boil on the right cheek. Did not go out until afternoon. Rambled about until twilight. Spent the evening indoors. 
 20/10/18 
 Sunday and wet. Remained abed all day. Dog's disease rather rife among the troops again. 
 21/10/18 Stayed in bed. Visit from Genl.Robertson. 
 Still in bed. Winter coming on fast and the air full of falling leaves. Today at 3 pm I was to have kept a rendezvous at Villers Bocage but it must wait until later. Things very quiet in the Brigade and no news of interest to chronicle. Very little sun these days and a lot of gloomy weather. 
 23/10/18 
 Stayed in bed. Visit from General.  A Lena Ashwell Concert Party to afternoon tea.
