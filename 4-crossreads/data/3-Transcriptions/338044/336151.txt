<p>20th Feb to 9th March<br/>To day (9th March) I am being discharged to duty, and am very pleased to be going out  My stay has been very monotonous for, after five days in bed    &amp;  few more days of convalescence I have been within the hospital confines, with nothing to do but write letters &amp; waiting to leave   Have met here an old friend of Sid Banks &amp; a few other Sydney boys; his name Jack Frost. He has had an attack of enteric after leaving the Peninsula    &amp;  we became good friends   On Sunday evg. last (5th)  we obtained leave &amp;  attended a marriage in Cairo. The invitation</p>
