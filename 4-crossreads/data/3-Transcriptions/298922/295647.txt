<p>Friday 14 December 1917</p>
<p>[This page crossed through]<br/>Expect to get to Port Said tomorrow at noon, so busy packing &ndash; just heard a great argument about Miss ASQUITH moving to London &amp; Lord HALDANE subscribing to German prisoners fund, while the nation asks for War Bonds.</p>
<p>The wife is worrying at the prospect of being alone in Cairo but she will be in luxury &ndash; at HELIOPOLIS HOUSE HOTEL, is her address.</p>
