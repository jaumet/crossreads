<p>along about 1.30 crowds lined the street &amp; it was impossible to get to the Hall, so we turned away &amp; paid a visit to St Pauls, which eminent edifice we inspected thoroughly.  After a walk through part of the City we returned to the Ship.  About 3 miles from the City on our line at Stratford Market, is a German concentration camp.  Barbed wire, &amp; soldiers with bayonets are ever watchful to shield the Teutons from having to participate in the turmoil outside their prison.
