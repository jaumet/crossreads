<p>April 27<br/>Still in Camp.  Col. Heyworth D.S.O now in command. He came from Salonica to form this force.  The time is passed by cooking &amp; shooting expeditions among the foothills  &amp; swimming in the river which runs nearby.  The only shooting one can get is a chance shot at a hyena or a fox, still this passes by the time.</p>
