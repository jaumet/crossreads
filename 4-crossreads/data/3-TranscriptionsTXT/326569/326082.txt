 Thursday, 19/7/17 Rained slightly in morning, but turned out nice fine sunshine.  On Lewis gun all day.  After tea went for walk through Compton Chamberlayne &amp; Dinton.  Received letter from Uncle Fred. 
 Friday, 20/7/17 On Parade ground all morning.  Afternoon marched about 2 miles towards Teffont &amp; on outpost work till midnight, having  tea out there.  Arrived back at 12.45 a.m. &amp; had coffee &amp; biscuits. 
 Saturday, 21/7/17 Reveille 7.30 a.m.  Paraded as usual 9 a.m. &amp; finished 12 noon.  Afternoon walked to Teffont &amp; saw Manor &amp; Church.  Came back short cut &amp; called at farmhouse &amp; had drink milk.  After tea went to Fovant for washing &amp; had photo taken.
