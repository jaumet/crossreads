 Mar 8  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;Fritz was over here with bombers early this morning - We took the labour corps party to work in the morning but did&#39;nt have to wait for them. we went back to Park &amp; cleaned the lorry - Frank &amp; Bud walked up to the B.A.C. at Neuve Eglise for the mail in the afternoon. 
 &nbsp; 
 Mar 9  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;We did&#39;nt go out to-day - Last night one of the 12  th  &nbsp;brigade lorries had an accident at the bottom of the Bailleul hill by the Hazebrouck road. the drivers (Poor old Blue was one of them) were both killed &amp; also the dispatch rider. Whom they were trying to avoid when they ran into a tree &amp; went over into the ditch. In the evening Frank &amp; I went to Bailleul &amp; saw the Smart Set at the concert hall 
 &nbsp; 
 Mar 10  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;Sunday - We went to take the labour co y &nbsp;to work, but they did&#39;nt want us &nbsp;they dont work on Sundays - We got back &amp; greased the lorry. Fritz put two shells into Steenwerck railhead to-day killing two diggers - our time advanced 1 hour last night -
