<p>and so past the Madeleine up the Boulevard Malsherbes. The narrow Rue de Monceau, where stands the Prince Murat's mansion which will house the President during his stay in Paris, was lined with the famous 9th Zouaves, no longer in baggy scarlet trousers but garbed in yellow African khaki and mudhued helmets. But nearly every man wears the croix de guerre.</p>
<p>The President and Mrs Wilson were introduced to their new house by M. and Mme. Ponicare who with the rest of the official party, then departed, not however before Mr Wilson had expressed his deep appreciation of the welcome Paris had given them.</p>
