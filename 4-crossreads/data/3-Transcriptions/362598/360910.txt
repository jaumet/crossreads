<p>Sunday 30 January 1916<br/>Ward work &amp; no spell even for a shower.</p>
<p>Monday 31 January 1916<br/>Continue in ward work &amp; Operation Theatre</p>
<p>Tuesday 1 February 1916<br/>Still continue.  Wrote to Marg Millar.</p>
<p>Wednesday 2 February 1916<br/>Wrote Home &amp; sent a few cards.  Wrote to Cohen's.</p>
<p>Thursday 3 February 1916<br/>Ellis &amp; I with Staff Moore, L.C. Winn &amp; Sergt. McIvor went to Pyramids followed by tea at Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Friday 4 February 1916<br/>Harold Filshie, Roland Hale &amp; Eric Davis came up to see us.</p>
<p>Saturday 4 February 1916<br/>Had a letter from Roy Tredrea.</p>
