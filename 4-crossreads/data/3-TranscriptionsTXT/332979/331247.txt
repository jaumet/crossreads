Afterwards an inspection of the Main Street was made, & I must give Credit for the way the buildings are laid out some of them are very high 4 & 5 storeys. The Railway Station & Offices are very imposing structures built of Stone, & is in the Main St. The Post Office & the Standard bank of South Africa is likewise both Splendid buildings. Gradually our party got separated & only two us were left, & as we were advised to take a Train ride ride to Camp Bay which is situated behind Table Mountain we soon was aboard a Car, & the Trip was an eyeopener to both of us.
