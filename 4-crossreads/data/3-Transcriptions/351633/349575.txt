<p>to make you for ever happy &amp; content.<br/>If necessary I will write you in 3 days from now &amp; say <u>"safe"</u> if I am so &amp; when you get this letter you will understand why I sent that wire to you.  I will write &amp; tell you fully this job I am on.</p>
<p>Remembrance to Edie &amp; all at Bellevue &amp; [indecipherable]   &amp; will you thank them all for their kindness to me &amp; also to dear old OB.</p>
<p>Yr affect Son</p>
<p>Percy J Thorn</p>
