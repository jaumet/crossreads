<p>3 Sunday [June]<br/>Took the Bathing Parade to the Div. Baths. Rode over to the 8th Bde &amp; saw Geoff Evans &amp; all the crowd. Wrote to Rup No. 26</p>
<p>4 Monday<br/>Penny took the coy. out on advance guards. Damned hot anyway &amp; was glad to get 'ome!<br/>Brackpool joined up &amp; others.</p>
<p>5 Tuesday<br/>A beastly route march of over 8 miles rather knocked me up.  A Shower bath in evening. Dined at Bapaume Railhead.</p>
<p>6 Wednesday<br/>Out wiring at Villers au Flos.<br/>lre from Rup. No. 32.  Applied for leave to Amiens</p>
<p>7 Thursday<br/>Out on a long route march in the morning. Played cricket for B. Coy in evening. Left for Amiens at 7 o'c &amp;  reached there at 2 am Friday.</p>
<p>8 Friday<br/> stayed last night at the officers club. Bought some new Kit etc. &amp; sampled the cocktails!<br/>Drew 200 Fr off the Field Cashier.</p>
<p>9 Saturday<br/>Left Amiens by the 5.52 train: Arrived camp at 10.30 am.  &amp; adjt. earns my hatred for the rest of his natural. still one cannot help being born "a pig" instead of a gentleman.</p>
<p>10 Sunday<br/>The Padre preached on the doings of "Leave" people. Silly  ass ! Evans came over to dinner. Sports on at 5.30. I was starter.</p>
<p>11 Monday<br/>Hell of a thunderstorm last night &amp; this morn.  Rode in Bapaume to dentist  &amp;  saw Myers at Camouflage.  Had teeth tempy filled</p>
<p>12 Tuesday<br/>usual parades. Played  the Devons at soccer in the eveng  &amp; were licked 2-1. Orderly Dog today. Gippy clothes from Esc [?] came yesterday.</p>
<p>13 Wednesday<br/>Route march at 7 o'c. I had Coy. &amp; bally well didn't have the horse!  Feeling <u>very</u> fed. Somewhat stiff after yesterdays match.</p>
<p>14 Thursday<br/>into Bapaume to dentist &amp; the scoundrel gave me Hell. Met Cherry Slade in there.  My valise came home yesterday.  Beastly toothache.</p>
<p>15 Friday<br/>Moving day.  Boche blew up railway at Achiet le Petit &amp; we are delayed. Reached Warloy just after midnight<br/>16 Saturday<br/>Troubled with rats last night confound them!<br/>Built a nice tarpaulin tent in the afternoon &amp; got a touch of the sun.</p>
