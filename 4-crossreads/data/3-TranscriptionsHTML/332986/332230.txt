<p>16-8-1917<br/>8 horses sent to Vet Mobile section. 5 gunners killed. Bdr Gibson, Sampson, Brady, May, and Ross. and three men wounded. Sgts Forpey Galloway (2nd time) and Bdr Sharrett. A big stunt on starting at 1. AM. Finished at 10.A.M Some wood keeping us back. German planes overhead. Put the wind up our men. A lot of wounded coming down on light railway. Another horse killed, taking up rations.</p>
<p>17-8-1917<br/>Bty coming out of action tonight. Sgt Flett slightly wounded. Lot of Hun prisoners passed here. Enemy planes over, flying very high. Firing time A. X at our baloons, knocking some of the tiles off the remaining portion of my shop. One of the men that landed from a baloon, fell into water and was drowned, got tangled in the weeds</p>
