And I wish to place on record my Best Thanks to all the Ladies who voluntary assist & provide free Luncheons to us Boys from France & there is no doubt it that the generosity of these good Folk inspire us Colonials to return to France & give of our Best to Protect these good People & our Dear Old Flag, I especially desire to thank the Ladies, at Folkstone, Victoria, Euston, Preston, Glasgow, Perth Aberdeen, Edinburgh, Newcastle, York who waited on us & give us a hearty Welcome in these Soldiers Welcome Buffets, where I had the pleasure to visit, These Notes terminate my record of my first Furlough from France,
