<p>1916 January 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Morning shift all this week.  Full ward.  <br/>Lecture by Capt. Willis in Rec. Tent this afternoon --- Venereal Diseases.</p>
<p>Mrs. Wheatley  Mrs. McKenzie Hardie &amp; Miss Napper up.  Bring apricot tart, stewed peaches etc. Tea in Rec. Tent &ndash; bother. Bob.  See them to gate. Feat.</p>
<p>Back to lecture by Capt. Willis.  Digestive System.</p>
<p>Meet  Mrs. Rehardt at entrance to camp on my way to Fairfield.</p>
