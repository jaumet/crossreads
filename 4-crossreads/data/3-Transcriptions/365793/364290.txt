<p>July 7 Rather good concert at the Jubilee Hall. Some, or at least nearly all of the boys stayed for tea, but I imshied to the Prom! &amp; had a look around. Landed home about 8.15 &amp; went for a stroll along the Coast.</p>
<p>8 No sign of Board papers yet therefore no leave will be granted until they arrive or I am reboarded. Have pass from 2 to 11, &amp; it's a topping day. Saw the girl in the ---  Jacket, but alas (Mum).<br/>"The Rosary" (screened at the Paladium) was a beautiful picture, &amp;</p>
