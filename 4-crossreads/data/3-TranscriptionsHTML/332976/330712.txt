<p>August 1916<br/>6th   (Sunday)   First draft of Australian Engineers left for France with Thompson &amp; conducting officer.<br/>10th   Left for London on weekend leave &amp; returned on the 12th at midnight.<br/>13th   Left Christchurch for the Engineering School of Instruction at Esher, a small town  about 15 miles out of London.  We were billeted in Wolsey Rd with Mrs Capel &amp; settled down comfortably.  CS.M Stevens &amp; Sgt Hutton were with me &amp; we had a very enjoyable time</p>
