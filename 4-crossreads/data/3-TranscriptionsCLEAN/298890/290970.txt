  
  
 February  1918 17. Sunday Air raid at night. No damage near us. 
 18. Monday Miss H & I Went to hear Litvinoff in Cent. Hall, Westminster. Guns began firing at 9 and kept on a long time. Delayed us getting home. 
 19. Tuesday Sun  At Miss Hodges. Slept at Mrs Richards 10 Farm Walk. 
 20. Wednesday Went with Mrs Oliver  to see Com. Kitchen at Child's hill. Tea with Mrs O. Rained a little.
