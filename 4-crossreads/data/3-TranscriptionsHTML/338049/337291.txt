<p>Friday 22 March<br/>Passed thro York &amp; Newcastle &amp; had a decent sleep as time went on.</p>
<p>Arrived Edinburgh about 8.30 am. Introduced to Cockburn Hotel by the cobber of [Korahs?]. Took up position at the Hotel. Very nice. At breakfast met Korah. Afterwards along Princes St, then to Holyrood (closed) so visited Castle.</p>
<p>Afternoon could not get accommodation to go to Forth Bdge so had photos taken. Visited Portobella. Not much impressed. Walked along beach.</p>
<p>Evening on walk &amp; to [Granton?]. Very enjoyable.</p>
