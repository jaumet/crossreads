  
 F & w 21 Sun Tommy Sports. T & I go look at 'em at 6 pm. Team up [indecipherable] all night straighten out things a bit. Go to bed early 
 W 22 Mon Have a long day & say Au Revoir to Mark Marsh. Get a horrible Electric shock & wet everything in the joint E. reads a book. Wrote Mum (Photos) Dad (Photos) M.RL. 
 W & F 23 Tues. Wet all morn. I go to. Warminster. Get sopping wet. Meet Sten. Ronie & Jane from Collingwood. St. Chapman bolts & I have to come back [Tranch?] with Amesbury girl to Sarum & Bristol Girl to W'minster. Salis. [indecipherable] back to Salisbury & then Amesbury Girl. 
 F 24 Wed Lovely morning & rain. 3 pm. Arr in bad [skin?] with everyone. Bath. Supper. & write. Recd. Letter from M.R.L. Feeling pretty well again Wrote MRL 
 W 25 Th Recd letter from Jinnie. Rotten Day. Go up to mess & place Billiards all night 
 W & F 26 Fri Tom comes down & we play cards all night in our Room 
 F 27 Sat With one huge storm. T & I go to Rotten W.C. Sports. Go up again after Tea & just get wet a little. Play cards & go to Dunnington Cross Stones afterwards. I come Home Knocked out & T & D come down afterwards & we talk about various subjects for an Hour or more.
