
<p>45</p>
<p>The place was full lit up like Pre war days Left London on the 3rd Jan. for Warminster When we arived there it was pouring rain and had to march three mile to our camp By the time we arived we were wet through &amp; to top all while we were being fixed up with our gear we had the stiff luck to cacth the first snow storm this winter. It was absolutely freezing. We had nothing to do here only a guard</p>
