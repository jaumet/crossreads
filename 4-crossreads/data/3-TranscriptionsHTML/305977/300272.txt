<p>Friday 1st October<br/>The Turks put in a lot of heavy shells in the morning &amp; nearly got a big howitzer shell in the major's dug out.  The day was fairly warm.  We had some bomb throwing practice in the afternoon.  The performance was very creditable.  Am still feeling weak &amp; was marked exempt.  The bombardment in the south continues.  The monitor plastered Gaba Tepe with shells just before dark.</p>
<p>Saturday 2nd Oct<br/>Went on duty again.  Am feeling much better. The 6th received 100 reinforcements this morning.  We got Adams &amp; Fredrickson back.  They look well.
