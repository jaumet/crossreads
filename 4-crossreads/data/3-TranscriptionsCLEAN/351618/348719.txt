 Saw Tom Watson of D.A.Park. Tents were promised to us etc. Guard of Groves - Clark Corp Preston & Thannett returned to the camp at Sarpi [?]. 
 Tuesday 28th Dec Felt much better. Saw Jack Fowles unloading stores from Wagon. A lot of Egyptians left here to-day. I must say my regard for the Australian Aboriginal has greatly improved since coming into contact with the lower order of the Egyptian race.
