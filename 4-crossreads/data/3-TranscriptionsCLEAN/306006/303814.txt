
11-4-17Wed 11thThe Taube paid us a visit again today & opened up with his machine gun on us doing no damage; A supply of goods came in to the canteen today & there has been a rush on in consequence; Sergt Hayes is the gas Sergt of this Regt. & has been putting in a good deal of time daily & the B.H.Q.overhauling Respirators etc. We had a Sigs parade this morning 5 Sigs going to the opposite side of Khan Yunus & sending back to camp per helio. Grazing parade this afternoon.
12-4-17Thurs 12thThe 4th Bde are camped about 11/2 miles East of us they arrived a couple of days ago; This morning at 11 oclock
