<p>May 4/16<br/>5 years today since I left England</p>
<p>Quite a red letter day &ndash; Mail &ndash; &amp; also I had a hot bath at the Nations Expense A big Laundry at Sailly has been turned into the 'Divisional Baths' so as opportunity occurs the troops can have a hot bath. Thirty of us who were off duty went down. When we entered we stripped off our tunics &amp; trousers &amp; these were tied up with our identity</p>
