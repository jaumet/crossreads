 Thursday 23 September, 1915 
 Leon Ferguson wounded. 
 The big shed caught fire, several chaps were burnt out of house &amp; home i.e. their rifles, equipment &amp; overcoats went. 
 The ships were very active during the night &amp; sent a number of heavy shells into the Turks. 
 We are building a new cook house for the cook to keep out the weather. 
 It was very cold during the night &amp; windy in the evening.
