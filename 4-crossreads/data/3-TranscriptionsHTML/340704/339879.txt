<p>December 1917<br/>Wednesday 5<br/>Enab. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quiet day<br/>Rained at night, Kept fairly dry<br/>We get plenty of wine from monastery.<br/> Septics healing rapidly</p>
<p>Thursday 6<br/>Enab.<br/>Nice morning.<br/>Hope to be in Jerusalem within 7 days.<br/>Night turned very cold and rainy.<br/>The coldest night I have ever felt. Just like Katoomba.</p>
