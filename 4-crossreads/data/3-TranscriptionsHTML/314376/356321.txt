<p>10.<br/>to drink it down. This only whetted their appetite for more mischief. They rang up the Sydney Railway Station and ordered the Station Master to send up a Special Train to Sydney. Failing this they said they would wreck the Railway Station at Liverpool.</p>
<p>Arriving at Sydney they formed up and did a lot of damage to shops &amp;c. Overturned hawkers barrows at the Sydney Railway Station entered public houses in Sydney and proceeded to get more drink. At night time the Military Authorities had procured a guard from the O.T. School at the Show Ground and they formed a guard on Sydney Station. This night they had their orders and would not allow anyone to proceed to Liverpool unless he was in possession of a rail</p>
