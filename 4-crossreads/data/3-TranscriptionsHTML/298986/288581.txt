<p>Wednesday 16th</p>
<p>Clasification Marked B.  A lecture on soil in afternoon.  Pictures at night.  The remarks one hears are better than the pictures themselves.  Some of them take quite a personal interest &amp; allways stick up for the right side.  The villan gets no symphity.</p>
<p>Thursday 17th</p>
<p>Heard that a man was wanted in Y.M. so asked the S.M. &amp; was put on the job.  It is to help cleaning up serving tea etc..</p>
