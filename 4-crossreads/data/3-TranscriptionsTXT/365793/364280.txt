 June 30 Half way up the hill I sat down on a heap of stones by the wayside &amp; watched 2 or 3 Companies of Tommies March past. Believe they are trying to crime me again &ndash; for something I don't know anything about &ndash; to wit &ndash; the non return of a tea bucket to the cook house. Girls, did you say? Not for mine &ndash; yet! &ndash;
