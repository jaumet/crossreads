22 September 1918 Sunday5600 prisoners 60 Guns including 56 Divisional Hd Qts &amp; going strong-NOON Now 6000 &amp; 95 guns 60 Motor Lorrys etc etc:-Old BANJO Patterson came around our lines for a trip he is stationed in Moascar Remount Camp   I gave him particulars of my wounded horses to look them up &amp; keep an eye on.  He looks old but game still &amp; was much interested in the old original horses.  Mc L[indecipherable] is sick &amp; at Port Said
