 Dec 17  th    The weather was wild & snowing in the morning - I had to get a tow to start my engine again, but carried on with the Supplies - Fritz is bombing near here very heavily - our guns are bombarding continuously - 
   
 Dec 18  th    Fine day but a bit of wind made it very cold - I got a tow to start my engine again, after the engine is warm, I can start it by hand. Our guns are still hard at it - To-morrow we draw at Steenwerck railhead as the Brigade is moving towards De Suel - The 1  st   D.S.C. took the place of the 5  th   D.S.C. & we remained attached to them. 
   
 Dec 19  th    Beautiful day but cold, freezing all day - We drew to-day at Steenwerck . the brigade moved to-day - 106  th   & B.A.C. up at Neuve Eglise & 16  th   17  th   & 18  th   Batteries at De Seul & on Bailleul.Armentieres road near La Creche - We did'nt get back to park till 5.P.M. The 12  th   Brigade moved into the waggon lines which the 6  th   vacated - 
   
 Dec 20  th    Very cold, ice bearing everywhere there was a thick rhyme on the trees all day. I went up to K column
