I find signalling very difficult to grasp; the others seem to make a much better do of it.</p>
<p>Dec; 23<br/>I am mess orderly &ndash; beastly job, washing up for 22 men. Breakfast &ndash; cleaning up &ndash;flag &ndash;wagging &ndash; dinner; go up on the sergeant's deck, paint all the afternoon &ndash; get hoofed round generally by sergeants and sentries; they resent having a low artist man encroaching on the sanctity of their deck &ndash; however continue painting until I am picked off. Weather glorious, sea like glass.</p>
<p>Dec; 24<br/>'Christmas Eve' - no drills, though I practise signalling myself, am not satisfied with my slow progress. All mess decks decorated with paper festoons, etc., reminds me of a coloured Christmas supplement of some illustrated paper. Spend all day painting.</p>
<p>Dec; 25  Christmas Day<br/>All day painting and reading . Fruit is given us as a little extra &ndash; I presume no day of any importance could be such unless there were something extra for the inner man.</p>
