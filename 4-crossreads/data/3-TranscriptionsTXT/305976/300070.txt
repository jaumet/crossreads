 238  Sunday  Tuesday Oct 29 All hands will have to lead spare Horses. Some of our fellows are done already  God knows how they are going to stand a 7 days trek. We have only two NCOs Bert &amp; myself. The SM is going to hospital. The boss is down but says he will ride &amp; we are 60 &#37; below strength. I will not be sorry to leave Syria. The Tribesmen here are a treacherous crowd &amp; the inhabitants although they state they are pleased with us well away.  Monday  Wednesday Oct 30 We are leaving on Wednesday
