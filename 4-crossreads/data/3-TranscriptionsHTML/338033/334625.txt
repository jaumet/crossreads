<p>Queens Park after tea, and<br/>Then caught the train back to Ammesbury and a lorry from there home. A few days later I had a photo taken at Sheaton to send home, to show how I had grown and put on weight. Sept 2nd Went to Tielshead for a walk, returning by Stonehenge. Stonehenge was originally an open temple, where the Druids congregated to worship their God, which was seen.
