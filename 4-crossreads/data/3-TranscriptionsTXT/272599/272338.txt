 March Saturday 10 1917 
 Up 8 a.m. Meet lot of the other boys who came down from other relays. B section remains at Bazentin. A &amp; C go back to Becordel by train at 12.30 p m. Arrive Becordel in hour. &amp; attached to 7th Field for duty at Rest Station. Hayman, Johno &amp; self go to Meaulte for bath. 1st for 6 weeks.  back for tea, turn in early.  Sergt. Clifton puts Ridgway &amp; self over wards&mdash;an easy job nothing to do only supervise.
