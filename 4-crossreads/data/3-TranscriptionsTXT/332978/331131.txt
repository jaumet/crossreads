 September 4th Spent morning in rearranging cupboard 
 It is glorious sitting in the afternoon sun I am taking advantage of all the sun I can get, because we will soon have winter on us &ndash; 
 Exercised my limbs today on parallel bars The Commdt has at least introduced the means whereby we can exercise ourselves occasionally &ndash; 
 Played bridge &ndash; Recd letter from Mary 
 September 5th (See end of Sept 5th) Good sleep.
