 Sunday 6 May 1917 On "Barb Wire" fatigue all day with limbers, had a big day, were right along our front along laying it. 
 Monday 7 May 1917 Stand to usual time 3.15 : nothing doing in morning.  Grazing horses in evening, new lot of Reinforcements came out in evening. 
 Tuesday 8 May 1917 Usual routine.  Stand to 330.  Just came out to outpost east of Shelal at night  did shift on guns at night. 
 Wednesday 9 May 1917 Usual routine:  camped on outpost, did shift on outpost at night. 
 Thursday 10 May 1917 Got leave to go &amp; visit 2nd Brigade saw Roger, Jock Baird, "Squeaker" Fenners, Nev. Johnson, had dinner there &amp; came home early, on outpost as usual at night. 
 Friday 11 May 1917 Warm &amp; windy day:  quiet got some canteen stuff.  Taube brought down one of our planes quite close to our lines, great shooting with anti. 
 Saturday 12 May 1917 Mail in:  quiet day but windy.  Made a M.G. possy.
