<p>4 August, Monday</p>
<p>Nette, Mum &amp; I go shopping, Millinery etc. I am very dopey &amp; feel off colour, meet Charlie Pearson.</p>
<p>I am very cranky.</p>
<p>Wrote Miss Dickson, Mrs Bell &amp; organist.</p>
<p>5 August, Tuesday</p>
<p>We catch 7.45 boat. Meet Jim Gosper, Mabel, H Belle, Nick. See lots of others. We go to Windsor. Good trip. Nice day. See boys &amp; go on river banks for walk. Have a good day. Arrive Home 11.30 pm.</p>
