<p>1918</p>
<p>First impressions I suppose are worth remembering because they are untinged by experience or analysis and my first impression of Alexandria <strike>was a delightful one.</strike> I can still visualize pleasurably &amp; realistically.</p>
<p>As we approached the buoyed channel to the harbour the water appeared a light chalky blue, the land and the city dancing in sunlight, <strike>and</strike> - most alluring.</p>
