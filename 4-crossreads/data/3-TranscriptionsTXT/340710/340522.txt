 We had an air raid here a few days ago, the planes came right across London dropping bombs at intervals, all the anti-aircraft guns were firing and the sky was dotted with puffs of smoke but the maurauders sailed serenely beneath the mid-day Sun scattering death and destruction in their path.  Three of them were afterwards brought down on the French Coast on their way home. For about a fortnight I have been doing the rounds of the City going to all the theatres, looking up old pals and    generally   seeing the sights generally.
