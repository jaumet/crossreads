<p>doss &amp; squeals from the pig, boot &amp; hair fly rapidly for a few minutes, &amp; then all is quiet once more.<br/>Len is expected back from England to-day they have been ringing up on the telephone wanting to know if he or Sgt Downer is back yet.<br/>The artillery is fairly active again to-day &amp; shells are flying pretty fast &amp; thick while aeroplane's are everywhere.<br/>Yesterday was pay-day here, &amp; great was the quantity of beer got rid of, some of the chap's have not turned up yet, they are making it a bit hot, &amp; I guess they will cop out<br/>11th. Len came back this morning from England, had a bonzer time &amp; was treated splendidly. I am looking forward to my trip to the "Old Country"  Saw George Vaughan this morning he had 3 stripes up &amp; is on Brigade</p>
