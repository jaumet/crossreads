It generally contains some instructive reading which is very necessary in the trenches. Our Commanding Officer, Lieut. Col. Morshead received the D.S.O. recently in recognition of the good work done by the 33rd. Btn. under his leadership. The photo which I enclose with this letter was overlooked and should have accompanied one written some months ago. The scrap of paper is one of Fritz's Field Post cards, (one of the relics I acquired lately) which may prove of interest to you. Unfortunately the enclosed slip is not written on.
