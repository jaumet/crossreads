
Aug 1917passed today. Got our water bottles out today have to carry them full all the time, but not of Rum
Wednesday 22ndHad a full Marching Order Parade today Got my fiver out of Orderly room today. Small steamer passed this evening Fair sea running strong W wind. They are making out a long list of all our trades or kinds of work we have done. Mine is a fairly long list
Thursday  23rdFair sea and much cooler. But am still wearing shorts
