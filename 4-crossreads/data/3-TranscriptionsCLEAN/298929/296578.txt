
10th  At present I am like the lily. "I toil not, neither do I spin" unless it is a few somersaults into a convenient shellhole when Fritz begins his fireworks display.At times blighties are flying about like little wasps, but thank goodness they cannot penetrate 6 ft of earth.                               Often during the day you see blighties are hobbling out and others being carried.
