<p>in Tatts. You maybe lucky or otherwise The Turks are constantly trying to shell the watering area. To get to water you are compelled to cross open country Thus the excitement.<br/>The 1ST Reg are camped at Mersalarba[?]. 2ND Reg in between two hills to the left &amp; 3RD reg with Headquarters behind the hills. Machine Gunners are down in the Wadi bed which is quite dry &amp; not a bad possy. The sick &amp; wounded unless urgent are brought in by night &amp; one needs to be very careful as direction is easily misjudged.</p>
