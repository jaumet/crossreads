<p>Aug 1917<br/>3 Blankets each were given out &amp; we were allotted to tents with board floors. Our kit bags had been left at the railway</p>
<p>Sunday 26<br/>Spent a fair night Good breackfast Medical Exam &amp; Inoculation. Kits issued and just got fixed up nicely when a lot of us had to move into <strike>new</strike> other tents Showerry all day</p>
<p>Monday 27<br/>Rained nearly all day. Did not feel well so stayed in bed most of the day as there was nothing doing the camp is muddy although there is a good fall</p>
