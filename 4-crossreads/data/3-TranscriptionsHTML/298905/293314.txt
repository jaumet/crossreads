<p>later, as soon as we had dinner today we marched out onto the parade ground &amp; the roll was called we then took our packs off &amp; all the billets &amp; ground was properly cleaned up before we went, I saw Graham &amp; Flattley this morning in Outersteene they both belong to the Pioneer Battalion<br/>There was an issue of green envelope's &amp; Field card's on the parade ground this morning &amp; not before they were wanted<br/>All the members of the our band are now back with the Company, all our instruments have been sent to Salisbury Plains in England that's a pretty good sign<br/>This morning all the N.C. O's were issued with a pair of wire cutters, &amp; a lot of the men with bags to carry bombs in sounds nice do'snt it, but the sooner its over the better</p>
