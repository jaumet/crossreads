 24th morning pulled out from Berth, & moved to buoy.  Then in afternoon sailed downed to harbour mouth & again buoyed up after many turns round  to regulate the compass. 
 25th Morning.  Steamed through heads on our way, but had not gone far before steering gear went wrong. Speed down to about 3 knots & slowly steamed up & down coast while they endeavoured to fix it up.  Finally had to return to Devonport. 
 16th  We are still just within the harbour, & understand we are to sail to night or tomorrow morning.
