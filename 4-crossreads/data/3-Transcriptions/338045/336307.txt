<p>1916</p>
<p>35 new men from Alexandria arrive this morning &amp; the likelihood is now of our being sent back to London.</p>
<p>1st October - Sunday<br/>A memorable day of 25 hours.  Rather cold but no rain.  After work at 12-30 p.m., take tram home for lunch with our two Collegian young friends &amp; Madame &amp; Madamoiselle, as per their invitation.  After a very pleasant lunch &amp; talk, the two leave (one to a College &ndash; M. Louis Leudet &amp; <strike>other to</strike> for teaching having finished his studies &amp; the other to the University M. Roger Hautemer).
