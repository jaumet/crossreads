
<p>963</p>
<p>SUNDAY, JANUARY 12, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Poss &amp; I &amp; a few more</p>
<p>went for a walk round the</p>
<p>&quot;Row&quot; in the morning. I was</p>
<p>at the flat all the afternoon</p>
<p>and evening. Alan got</p>
<p>word to return &amp; motored</p>
<p>up to Upavon in the morning.</p>
<p>Had dinner at Hatchetts</p>
<p>at night.</p>
