<p>on the Main.</p>
<p>24th morning pulled out from Berth, &amp; moved to buoy.  Then in afternoon sailed downed to harbour mouth &amp; again buoyed up after many turns round  to regulate the compass.</p>
<p>25th Morning.  Steamed through heads on our way, but had not gone far before steering gear went wrong. Speed down to about 3 knots &amp; slowly steamed up &amp; down coast while they endeavoured to fix it up.  Finally had to return to Devonport.</p>
<p>16th  We are still just within the harbour, &amp; understand we are to sail to night or tomorrow morning.</p>
<p>26th-29th Devonport</p>
