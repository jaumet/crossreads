<p>Friday 1 June 1917.</p>
<p><u>&quot;Crossed the line&quot;</u> at 10 a.m.</p>
<p>Have developed another cold, &amp; unluckily I am mess orderly again.&nbsp;<s>with</s>&nbsp;G.S.S.Cmrn on with me.</p>
<p>Rough weather&nbsp;&ndash; a big swell on.</p>
<p>Air still foul in No 3 deck.</p>
<p>Dinner 11.30 am (early) and preparations for &quot;crossing the line&quot; initiation at 2.30 pm</p>
<p>Great sport &amp; everyone being drenched to the skin. No distinctions drawn between officers &amp; men. Officers faring worst.</p>
<p>I had to go through the performance and have now &quot;crossed the line&quot;</p>
<p>Travelling 8 knots (slow) tonight 7.30 pm.</p>
<p>Very heavy seas. Boat shipping them very badly. WIth heavy head feeling crook, turned in early.</p>
