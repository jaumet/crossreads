  
 Sunday 19 March 1916 Bill, Ellis & I went to Mary's well & Virgin's Tree & to Kursaal after tea. 
 Monday 20 March 1916 Dave Niness took Photo E. R. I.  Roy & I went to Kursaal together.  Roy came out to dinner but got very little.  Came down to my ward to talk to Corbett. 
 Tuesday 21 March 1916 Ward-master. Home, Nettie, Bess, Laura, Nell & Pa, Ma, Cock & Ida. Went to Pyramids & round the Houses of Cairo.  Bill, Dave, Charlie & E. & I.  Rode from Mena in motor car. 
 Wednesday 22 March 1916 Met Johhno & went to Pictures.  Special leave from 5 to 10. 
 Thursday 23 March 1916 Wrote to Mem & Cohens. 
 Friday 24 March 1916 Went to Blue Mosque.  Met Roy in Gardens, Tea at Cafe de la Paix.  Tea Misrquash.  Roy, Dave Niness & I went to Pictures.  Pictures Misrquash.  Pictures Colosse. 
 Saturday 25 March 1916 Our Theatre Experts gave concert in Esbekiah Gardens.
