<p>feeling particularly rotten.  I was washed by an orderly &amp; found myself between white sheets &amp; on a spring mattress the first for over a year.  Was examined by a doctor &amp; so left till morning.  For over a week I could not move without bringing on a fit of coughing &amp; sitting up was impossible.  Then I began to improve but my temperature which reached 104.6 would not come normal &amp; so I spent 3 weeks in bed.  The attention given by nurses &amp; orderlies was splendid &amp; I lost my great dread of ever being in hospital.  The food was also good, chicken ad. lib. &amp; quite a number of delicacies.</p>
<p>During this time my mail came along very well &amp; I was visited by several friends Will &amp; M.D. coming to see my very often.  My word it was a welcome sight to see them both, 10 months since I saw Will &amp; 12 months Morris.
