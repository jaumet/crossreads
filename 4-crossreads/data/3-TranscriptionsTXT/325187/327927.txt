
[Monday 3 July 1916]
Many calls for officers for Camel Corps & Artillery    Many of our best Rankers are getting commissions good luck to them-We carry on much as usual when not out on a Stroll STUNT, we are digging trenches in the sand, which promptly fill up again before the morning-Gen Cox our Brigadier has gone to England for 6 weeks so Col J B Meredith is our Brigadier again-Clive Weston a sergeant with me in Africa & a brother of Mr Blake's was in my Tent half an hour ago.We hope to go back to the Reserve within the next 3 weeks about 7 miles from the Canal
