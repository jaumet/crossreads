<p>we have her on her own territory. &nbsp;There may be a diffent tale but she is not there yet.</p>
<p>I haven&#39;t had any word from Bess for several mails now.</p>
<p>We are back from the forward area although we have not rejoined the rest of our crowd. &nbsp;We are boxing on independently and having it much easier. &nbsp;We were forward about a fortnight and it seemed much longer for we were hard at it.</p>
<p>These back areas are rapidly refilling with the inhabitants who were evacuated when the Hun was so near Amiens. &nbsp;He hasn&#39;t got much of a chance of getting there now &amp; we hardly know there is a war on.</p>
<p>I am as well as ever, and the weather is pretty mild so far,</p>
