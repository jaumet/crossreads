
At our own Camp but it is very easy   &  I'll have a good rest.
We are away from all our own soldiers, they are all on the move too.
Yesterday afternoon (Friday) to my delight some officers motored from Bapaume to see me. It is a Battalion that thinks something of this  Old Bird   &  I'm great pals with them. I get a good deal of teasing about one of them but we are only pals, though he is very nice but
