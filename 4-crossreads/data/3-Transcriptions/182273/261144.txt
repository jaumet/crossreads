
<p>July &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>7 SUN &nbsp;Fine day. Went into St Omer</p>
<p>on bicycle. had a good</p>
<p>time at [Cic&#39;s ?]</p>
<p>Plenty Americans in town.</p>
<p>very quiet. Left 8.30. home 10.30</p>
<p>Rec. letter from May. Webb.</p>
<p>Quiet night.</p>
<p>8 MON &nbsp;Fine day very warm.</p>
<p>sultry. Storm at night Amm.</p>
<p>going up. Very quiet day &amp;</p>
<p>night. Cleaning up at</p>
<p>night: Rec. letter from Julie.</p>
<p>9 TUES &nbsp;Fine day. Amm</p>
<p>going up. Very quiet</p>
<p>day &amp; night.</p>
<p><sup>X</sup>Sgt Savage returned for</p>
<p>trial.</p>
<p>X 10 WED &nbsp;Windy &amp; raining</p>
<p>very hard. Amm. going</p>
<p>up. Quiet day &amp; night.</p>
<p>Writing. Paid Savage 40 Fcs</p>
