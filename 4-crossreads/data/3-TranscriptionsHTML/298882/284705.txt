<p>17th.  A very quiet relief.</p>
<p>We sustained no casualties and the relief was completed early with out a hitch.  Ground in a very bad state.  Fortunately practically no hostile shelling on our sector during <strike>night</strike> relief.  Enemy very active with his artillery later on in night which gradually eased off towards daylight. Enemy machine guns sniping during night.</p>
