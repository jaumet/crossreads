<p>Saturday 15/12/17&nbsp; Relieved 40<sup>th</sup> without casualties, Band went to Division. Cabled Doff.</p>
<p>Sunday 16/12/17&nbsp; Hun fairly active throughout the day, our patrols out- Wrote Doff</p>
<p>Monday 17/12/17. Quiet day generally men working well&nbsp; have attached to me Lt Park of 102 Regt U.S.A</p>
<p>Tuesday 18/12/17. Very cold &amp; frosty. had a good look around the trenches</p>
<p>Wednesday 19/12/17. Hun active with Minenwerfers. One man killed.</p>
