<p>2 March 1915<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Finish the work at 11 a.m<br/>The mate personally thanked us for our trouble. We get 2 or 3 bottles of whiskey for the men from the officers.<br/>We get a sight of the south coast of Java about 2 pm</p>
<p>1915 March 3<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Continue along the Java coast all day. Surprised at the height of some mountains which seem to rise sheer up from the water<br/>Much hotter the wind having dropped to a light breeze.</p>
