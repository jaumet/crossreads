<p>1915<br/>Dec. 13 Mon.<br/>More fighting in the west of Egypt on 11th inst. &ndash; An engagement with 300 hostile Arabs &ndash; enemy driven off with 80 killed &amp; 7 prisoners &amp; 30 wounded &ndash; we had 16 killed &amp; 15 wounded.<br/>Several days ago a Regiment of Australian Light Horse &amp; 2 Regiments of Imperial Cavalry left Alex. for western frontier.</p>
