 Jly 26 Harefield this m'ng, &amp; I met my old cobber from the same place. Photos taken at the Salvo' Army Hall aren't bad. Fire alarm just went &amp; we all had to turn out at the double. It was a dinkum fire too. Half holiday so didn't go down town, must go tomorrow. Went for a stroll round Chickerell. 
 27 Muster parade today? But nothing doing. Rec'd 12 letters from home.  Just rec'd 6 more letters incl' 4 from Australia. The flying Board is marking lots of chaps for active, [in margin] Ada, R, Mill
