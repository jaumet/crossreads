<p>Commonwealth Military Forces<br/>2nd Military District</p>
<p>District Pay Office,<br/>Victoria Barracks, Sydney</p>
<p>20th November, 1917</p>
<p>Dear Sir,</p>
<p>I beg to enclose herewith a Statement showing the balance of Military Pay at date of death, &pound;78.1.5 &amp; cash in effects &pound;8.7.10 which is due to the estate of your late son, No. Rank Capt., Name, A.J. Ferguson, Unit, 20th Battalion.</p>
<p>The above amount will be available at the Victoria Barracks within the next few days.</p>
<p>Mr. David Gilbert Ferguson,<br/>Supreme Court,<br/>Sydney.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>W. Percival Minell, Lieut.-Col.,<br/>District Paymaster, 2nd M.D.</p>
<p>Copy of Statement enclosed is to be kept by you.</p>
