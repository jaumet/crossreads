All along the<br/>Canal were Fuluca's native boats being drawn down stream by men along the bank holding a long rope.<br/>At Kantara we had to wait for the pontoon bridge to swing open before we could proceed. Port Said was reached at midday on the 14th and just as we arrived, a British troopship left for the Persian Gulf carrying troops for Mesapotania.<br/>Owing to the excessive heat, we had to remain on board until dusk before landing and entraining, which we did in open cattle trucks.
