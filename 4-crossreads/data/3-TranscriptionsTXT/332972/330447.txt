 [Newspaper clipping with handwritten date &ndash; 22/4] Der Holdsvorthich Himmel "The German internees in Australia have been reposing in the lap of luxury, . . . There were butchers' shops, fruit shops nine cafes and restaurants. There were numerous athletic and social clubs, dramatic chess clubs, two choral societies, several theatres, and an open-air picture show. " Und how you vos put in der day, &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And how you vos amuse, Vhen out you vos bei Bot'ny Bay In yoal, pecause you disobey, &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und spracht dose Deutschtum views? Ach, not so bad it vos, by Gee, &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und not so bad at all, Far besser als in Germany You been in, Berlin bei der Spree Vhen Kaiser Bill vos call. 
 Weies brot, und blenty fleish zu eat, &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und no hard vorks zu do! Ach, ja! It vos ein lofely dreat Dot surelich neffer can be beat, &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I bet zwel mark mit you. But efenin's, dey vos pooty dull? Ach, nein, Fritz, you vos wrong, Die nachts mit harmony vos full, Und barties meet to hate Yohn Bull, &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Mit speech, und yoke, und song. 
 Und did die guards der fist und boot &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und rifle-butt display, Like in our brison-kampfs so goot? Of course, dey somedimes shtab und shoot &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;In Deutschland's downright vay?  Gott! Hans, you don't not mean to say &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Dot you brefers to be Shut in dot Holdsvorthick avay, Vhere all you do ist eat and blay &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Und schlep?
