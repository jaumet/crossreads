
427New Zealand HospitalWalton on Thames11.11.15Dear FatherThanks very much for your kind letter - I can't make out how you hadn't received one from me written from Epsom about August 6th. Still it may have turned up later -From this on I am going to make an effort to write to you once a week - I don't know why I can't write a decent letter - I read it over after I have written it and it doesn't seem to be worth posting.
