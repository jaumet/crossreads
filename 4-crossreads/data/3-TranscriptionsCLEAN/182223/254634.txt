
Thursday 4 February 1915Hospital in the morning.Writing & reading most of the day Walked to top of sand hills just before dinner. Air very cold. Expected letters but none came.
Friday 5 February 1915One operation this morning Haemorrhoids A beautiful morning 12 noon. No lettersIn Cairo during the afternoonAt Heliopolis for half an hour.Shepheards to dine with Major Barrett.Left for home at 10.30 pm.
Saturday 6 February 1915At Mena House all day.Working, writing, and reading 10.45 p.m. Must go to bed early.
Sunday 7 February 1915Mass. 9 a.m. Padre McAuliffe  preached against use of immodest profane & vulgar words in use about Camp.Called 3rd & 4th Infty Battalion L. Col Owen L. Col. Onslow Thompson also 1st F  Ambulance L Col. Newmarch. Col Beeston & other officers, from the 3rd Exped. force visited Mena House. They are camped at Heliopolis
