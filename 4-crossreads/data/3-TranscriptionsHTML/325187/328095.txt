<p>[Tuesday 19 December 1916]<br/>My "Bivvy" is made of two blankets over a Palm leaf [see sketch], a Canvas Bucket to wash in &amp; 1 change of underclothing- from the door I watched a good aerial duel but the Hun ran.<br/> ( The rest of this page is crossed out)<br/>Letters today from<br/>Daisy&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; }<br/>Kathleen&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; } All sent to England Oct 15th &amp; returned<br/>Aunt Louise&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   }<br/>Mrs Cotton&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  }<br/>Papers from Mr Ferrer<br/>Took out a Norton Spear Pump &amp; drilling Machine on Pack Horse 5 miles in front &amp; in view of the Turks we put down a Well in 30 minutes &amp; struck good water-<br/>This portable plant might be useful in a drought in AUS for beds of sandy creeks etc.  WATER is the Key note of every thing here-  Ate your Plum Pudding tonight &ndash; as if I kept it for Xmas I might lose it- &amp; another is on the way</p>
