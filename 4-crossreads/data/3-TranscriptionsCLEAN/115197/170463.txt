February 
1st -
Awake with nothing more than a cold to my credit or should I say discredit.  Late breakfast.  Return of Mr Scales with whom I journey to Wanstead for purpose of meeting my Dear English Mother - Happy meeting - A long conversation and much laughter.  Return to Highgate.
