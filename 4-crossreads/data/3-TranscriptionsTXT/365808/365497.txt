  5  I heard from one of my old officers the other day, now commanding a battalion. He stated that G.O.C. would restore me to my former rank at once, but he does not want to have any trouble with the Commonwealth authorities; and thinks that I technically deserted from the C.M.F. in that my resignation was not accepted. As I never got any reply to my letter (never could get any replies) I do not know whether this is so or not &ndash; but it hardly seems to me the time to worry about technicalities. I think this all my news. Salaams. C. Macnaughten
