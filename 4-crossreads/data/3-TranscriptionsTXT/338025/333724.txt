
Wednesday 27th June 1917Went to dentist this morning. Left camp at 9am with "Donk" as I had to go to Bell view farm hospital which is on the outskirts of Albert. Arrived there about 10am. Got fixed up at 12 noon then had a ride through Albert,arr. back in camp about 3pm.2nd Div. sports today but I did not go as I was too late in getting back to camp.
