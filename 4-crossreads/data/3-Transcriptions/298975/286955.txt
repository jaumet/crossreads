<p>Thursday  13th<br/>As per usual. Told we are to get leave on the 25th This leave has been a very prominent question ever since we got here. There have been roumers about it every day.</p>
<p>Friday 14th<br/>As per usual. The physical drill &amp; Bayonet Fighting in the forenoon is far the best part of the drill. The instructors are keen on the work &amp; keep us going. The Musketery are just the opisite do as little as possible</p>
