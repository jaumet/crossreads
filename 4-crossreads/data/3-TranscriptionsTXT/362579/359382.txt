
My dearest Mother   &  Sisters,
Have not received any home letters for weeks, the last dated May 27th so y ou can imagine I'm wondering how you all are.
Received a nice letter from Lucy yesterday dated June 27th   &  no other from Aust., so you will know how I feel.
Well, this life is full of comings   &  goings. Kelly   &  I are still here   &  I'm very contended   &  comfortable, this place will do me for some time.
