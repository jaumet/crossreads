 26th Aug. 1000 Matunga proceeded to sea followed by "Wolf". Both ships stopped about 10' off the land &amp; preparations were made for sinking Matunga 130 First bomb exploded port side engine [indecipherable] &amp; ship settled quickly by stern with heavy list to port. 132 Second bomb exploded &amp; ship straightened up with after deck awash.
