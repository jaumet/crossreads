<p>1916 July 10<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Route march occupied morning. Afternoon no parade.  Cricket match between A &amp; B grade of A Sec. Former wins by narrow margin. "Bertha" speaks again at dinner time. Jars our teeth Rumour is that we leave tomorrow morning.</p>
