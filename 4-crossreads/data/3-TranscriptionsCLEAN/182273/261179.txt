1918   November
7 THUR  Fine day. Rec. letters
from Julie & Tense. Heard
of Tom Magee's death
on Oct 26th. Rumours out
re declaration of peace.
Writing at night.
Evening after. Boys well oiled.
8 FRI  Raining on & off during
the day. Dental Inspection
Writing at night.
Went up the village.
9 SAT  Fine day. received
pay 40 Francs. & 50 from
Sgts. Posted aussies to May
& Amy & letters to Amy Mc
Celie Gladys. Else Parker
Went up the village
Negociations on for an
armistice with Germany.
Frost
