It is the only Joseph's coat, I have seen in Egypt &amp; it is a curious coincidence that I should have seen it just when I did.<br/>Also, when I were inside the tomb of Sultan Hasif we saw the great doors which were made of Gold &amp; Silver.  Napoleon took all the gold &amp; silver away except about 6 little pieces which we saw on one door.  From the windows of this place we could also see where Nap fired on this Mosque &amp; one of the round cannon balls is still sticking in a hole in the wall about 50 feet from the ground.<br/>The Great Mosque was so beautiful that the Khedive gave the Architect a great sum of money &amp; then put his eyes out, so that he could not build another<br/>More next time<br/>Love from Harry Ellis</p>
