 APRIL 1917 
 5 THURSDAY 
 A beautiful fine sunny day. 
 We are getting a bit like rabbits 
 when a light is flashed on 
 them at night, now when the 
 sun comes out, it dazzles 
 us & we cant see. Cleaning 
 headstalls all day. Had 
 nothing else to do so polished 
 them up with boot polish
