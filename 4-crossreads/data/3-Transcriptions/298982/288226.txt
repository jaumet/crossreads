<p>Satdy 30th Jan Went from Maadi to Zeitun Marched it mounted Dept abt 9.30 am arr 2 pm.</p>
<p>Sunday quiet day Mess orderly.</p>
<p>Mon 1st Feby.</p>
<p>Tuesday Field day out by an oasis all day went into town.<br/>Monday Major Fulton said possible six weeks to the front.</p>
<p>Wed Out all day. Picket at night</p>
<p>Thur Out all day Met Lance in city</p>
