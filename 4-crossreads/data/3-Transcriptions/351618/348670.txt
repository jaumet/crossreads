<p>and our little ship heaved and rolled. She stood it all very finely and we entered the Harbour about 7 P.M. I was feeling famished as I had had nothing to eat since morning except a few "Gaba Tepe" Drops &ndash; a kind of fried scone cooked by our Cooks at Headquarters.</p>
<p>As soon as I was ashore I got my tent assigned to me &amp; went &amp; had a cup of "Cocoa" as it is called at the Y.M.C.A. Greek place. I tried to sleep at night but it was only a miserable attempt as it was perishingly cold with a high wind blowing &amp; everything seemed to be one the wind with its terrific force.</p>
<p>Sunday 21st Nov<br/>Saw Capt Stowe &amp; arranged to buy next day. I visited Owens Y.M.C.A. &amp; had a chat with him</p>
