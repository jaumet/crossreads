The rifle men concentrate rapid fire on the trench while the 2 Gunners advance about 50 yds on one flank Then they open fire while the sections advance live amunition was used also smoke boms which did not seem a great success Quite an interesting show &amp; hard work for the men doing it<br/>This is St Whitins Day &amp; if it rains &ndash; it has very slightly &ndash; leagon says it will be wet for 40 days. They say it was so last year &ndash; The leagon is that St Whitins who was Bishop of Winchester was buried in the Church Yard &amp; it kept on raining for 40 days untill some bright chap buried him inside the church &amp; the wether was fine once more.</p>
<p>Tuesday 16th<br/>The M Gun Draft left early this morning without me rotten luck. We were all supposed to be in one Hut but as the others went in to it on Sunday while I was away knew nothing about it, Went sick</p>
