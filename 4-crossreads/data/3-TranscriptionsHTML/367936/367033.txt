<p>Friday 1 September 1916<br/>Fatigue on pumps most of day pumping water for the horses.<br/>Also on helping to cart away manure.<br/>Got 4 more letters, one from Joan, one Dora.</p>
<p>Saturday 2 September 1916<br/>Held horses at shoeing forge most of morning.<br/>In afternoon West, Dawson, Aire and I rode out on to the battle field looking for curios.  Only got some copper bands off shrapnel shells.<br/>Saw an awful lot of dead Turks.</p>
