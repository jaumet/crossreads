  
 Wednesday 22nd May 1917 [1918] 1st day at Bulkeley.  Breakfast 8 a.m. (in bed), out of room 10 a.m. to 12 m.d.  Went Alex. 1/2 hr. ride in double-decker tram, drew pay 10-0-0 at Pay office, lunch & 2 hrs. rest at Nurses' Club, paid 2 frocks Chalons 4-10-0.  Khedival Hotel (Sisters quarters) & 4 p.m. tea S. Roberts.  No. 9 Conval. 6 p.m.  Lovely flowers in gardens here & days fine & not too warm.
