<p>I got rid of my men.<br/>Saw quite a number of chaps whom I knew, including Lieut Champion had a yarn with him this morning, he was my old platoon officer &amp; a good fellow. There are plenty of camps round here, a few Tommies but mostly Australians.<br/>There was a couple of heavy showers this afternoon but it cleared up towards evening.<br/>After dinner went &amp; saw the boxing matches at the Stadium here they have it situated in almost an ideal place, the ring itself is built in a little saucer shaped hollow &amp; the sides of the bank slope gently away &amp; thousands can get a good view of the whole turn out &amp; there was a mighty crowd, must</p>
