<p>Friday 25th<br/>I little thought a few months ago that I would be putting in Xmas under canvas, but here I am, it is only too true &amp; putting in the quietest Xmas I have ever had;  applied for a pass, but<strike>I</strike> as Nelson got one I had to stop in, we had a shower at the prison this morning &amp; rode over to have it, we had a very enjoyable time &amp; when we returned sat down to a Xmas dinner of boiled beef &amp; potatoes cooked in their jackets, plum puddings were ordered for us by</p>
