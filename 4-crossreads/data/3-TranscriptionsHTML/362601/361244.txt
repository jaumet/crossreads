<p>29 July, Tuesday</p>
<p>Mum &amp; I go to Granma's &amp; Aunt Fannies. Shopping at Grace Bros &amp; to pictures &amp; to Glebe.</p>
<p>Break up in general. We all strike same boat Home. Meet O.G.H.M. We all race to bed in screams of silent laughter.</p>
<p>Send [indecipherable] love &amp; painting of Kookaburra [indecipherable].</p>
<p>30 July, Wednesday</p>
<p>I go to Burwood. Watch tennis[?] at old Cernt &amp; the Davey Boys. Meet Brigg's &amp; Mrs Haigh. I have a very good time &amp; sleep out on verandah. We write to Jessie.</p>
<p>Wrote M 17. Wrote Jessie.</p>
