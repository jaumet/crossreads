<p>24 December 1916<br/>Sunday <br/>Up at 8 a.m. Xmas Eve. No difference, useless fatigue as usual. Sunday &amp; all.  We have to thank 3rd Field Col. for that.  He is a notorious nigger driver.  <br/>Kept at it till 4.30. p.m. <br/>Fairly quiet day, usual bombardment.</p>
