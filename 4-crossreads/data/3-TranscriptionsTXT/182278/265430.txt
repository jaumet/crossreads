
Saturday 27.7.18  Took Hetty for the Week end to the farm at Bramley.
Sunday 28.7.18  Went for a walk & groomed "Mayfair"
Monday 29.7.18  Went back to Malden with Hetty, reported to H.Qrs, orders to proceed to Plymouth as O.C. Troops tomorrow
Tuesday 30.7.18  Had lunch with Mr & Mrs Gray, bought a watch for Doff, left by 4 p.m train from Paddington for Plymouth.
Wednesday 31.7.18. Took over as O/C Troops on [D17?] (Malta)
