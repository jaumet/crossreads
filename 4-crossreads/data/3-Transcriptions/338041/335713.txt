<p>June 25th<br/>Nothing unusual. Mass 7.30a.m.</p>
<p>June 26<br/>First view of German soldiers. Three pass through our base. Our Boys successful raid.</p>
<p>June 27th<br/>Letter to E.C.</p>
<p>June 28th<br/>"The White Company"</p>
<p>June 29th<br/>I again meet Roger Morgan &ndash; Capt. Strachan annoyed by the Boys.</p>
<p>June 30th<br/>Preparations for evacuation of Ft Rompu.</p>
<p>July 1st<br/>We leave our "Good old home" at 10.30am for Bailleul &ndash; March not enjoyable owing to rapid pace heat of day etc &ndash; We arrive at our new quarters &ndash; very tired.</p>
