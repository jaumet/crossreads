<p>W.T. Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship:&nbsp;Berrima</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship: Parramatta</p>
<p>Date: 14/9/14</p>
<p>Time of Despatch or Receipt: 10 17 pm</p>
<p>Comdr Beresford to Brigadier Berrima</p>
<p>Reply Submit I forwarded explanation by signal 7 5 pm last night re delay and finished my report this morning at 1 am and leaving camp at 6 am not returning till 6.30 pm I&nbsp;submit I have been unavoidably delayed My report will be despatched to you tomorrow morning at 7-30 am &nbsp; &nbsp;2145</p>
