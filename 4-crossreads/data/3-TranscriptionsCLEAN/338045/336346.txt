
Nov. 3 - FridayAwake at 6-30 a.m. & make my bed.  Still raining & blowing like fury.  I enjoyed the most comfortable sleep for some time in a nice warm bed.  Hardly believe it to be true.  Examined by the doctor & history of my case taken & from what I can gather have not much chance of getting away for a month at least.  Feeling pretty well knocked up, feverish & head aching, so don't want to leave.  Am so thin that the sister of ward gives all sorts of food to build me up.
