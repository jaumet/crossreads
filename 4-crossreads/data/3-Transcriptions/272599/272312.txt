<p>Feb.   Monday 12 1917</p>
<p>No cases through last night. Stay up till 2 p.m. then call relief. Hunted "chats" for 1&frac12; hours casualties heavy.-217.   We have no chance of securing a change so have to endure the beggers. <br/>Go off duty at 9.30 a.m coming on again at 8 p m.  Sleep &amp; read &amp; get up for meals during day. Pott's fracture through tonight  Lucky man! He'll be out of this mess for some weeks at least</p>
