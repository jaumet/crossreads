<p>[Friday 3 November 1916]</p>
<p><strike>On board A25 Troopship a Union Castle Liner taken originally from the Germans and named Huntsend </strike>  <u>raining &amp; cold</u> <strike>Have written Dolly &amp; telegraphed to Laura &amp; her<br/>The former is well &amp; plucky</strike>   Very few Australians on board only about <strike>5</strike> Officers, &amp; a few sick dismissed men for Australia<br/>Plymouth &amp; Devonport are all about the same rather a pretty spot, if it were not so cold &amp; wet-<br/>Goodbye old England I wonder when I'll see you again.<br/>We are to be vaccinated again against cholera I weigh 13-7lbs.</p>
