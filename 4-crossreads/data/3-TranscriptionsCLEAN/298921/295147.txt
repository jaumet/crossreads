
June, 1915Friday 25took Sister Nellie Hill late of Burrabogie for a run to Pyramids in Brigade car last night, she had news of Mrs Mills & all Hayites & is a most the most energetic of the nurses sent from Australia. All Cairo is closed now at 10 p.m. I could give you a lot of Military news about our men at Gallipoli & what is doing here, but cannot break faith as an officer hence I fill up Diary with Garden stuff.
