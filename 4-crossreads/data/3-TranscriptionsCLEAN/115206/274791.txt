  
 August,1918 Friday 9 
 Bainvillers.  Bty Position Weather fine and clear. Plenty of air fighting. General advance during the afternoon pretty solid work hit easy going as far as fighting was concerned. 15th Bdge did good work also 2nd Bdge 1st Divvie.  1st Div Art pulled in to a possie alongside of us. Harbonnier in our hands - at 5 pm. Spent night in old German dugout evidently a Hdqrs, electric light laid on and plenty of foodstuffs.
