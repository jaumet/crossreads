<p>Thursday 1 April 1915<br />Ride before breakfast.&nbsp;<br />About the hospital all day. Writing, reading, &amp; sleeping.&nbsp;<br />Riding between 6 &amp; 7 p.m.&nbsp;<br />Sent a cable to the girls at 8 p.m. Nash, Sydney. Happy Easter. Well. Love&nbsp; Cost 28.5 Milliemes</p>
<p>Friday 2&nbsp;April 1915 [Good Friday]<br />Not riding this morning.&nbsp;<br />O.C. away during most of the day.&nbsp;<br /><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 23.1111106872559px;">1</span><sup>st</sup> Brigade &amp; other troops under orders to move out tomorrow.&nbsp;<br />Reading and writing all day</p>
<p>Saturday 3&nbsp;April 1915<br />About hospital most of the day&nbsp;<br />After dinner went with Dr Purdy to Cairo. At Continental &amp; Sheppheards Hotels. Left Cairo by 11-45 pm tram.&nbsp;<br />1<sup>st</sup> Brigade Inf left camp &amp; Cairo. Col MacLaurin, Col. Braund, Captain Maguire &amp; others.</p>
<p>Sunday 4&nbsp;April 1915 [Easter Day]<br />Wrote Holman, Flowers, Paton, Mrs Thompson (Cooma), Fallon, Father Perrin, Pat. Watt., The Girls.&nbsp;<br />2<sup>nd </sup>Brigade of Infty left Camp &amp; Cairo<br />Ride during morning &amp; afternoon</p>
