Sunday 16
 Move from Senleques to Warrams. Snowing tonight prospects of more tomorrow.
Monday 17
 A very cold night last night. Move today to Wizernes there entrain, disentrain at Ballieul thence to Danoutre, about ten kilos behind the line at Messines. The bombardment on the left, Ypres is fairly heavy
Tuesday 18
 Rumour has it that the Germans are going to make an attack on this front. Certainly the artillery is very active, day night.
Thursday 20
 A very heavy hoar frost to-day tonight the hedges etc are covered with frost crystal. No rain the ponds etc are frozen over
Friday 21
 Visit Ballieul again. The town is the same as of old. French customs are queer. To-day I saw a man carrying an empty coffin under one arm the cross under the other. The effect of this hoar frost is just fairy like, beautiful.
Saturday 22
 Visit 9th Field Ambulance have tea with Verne Cocks quite a good yarn. En route passed through old haunts, Neuve Eglise Steenwerck. Still freezing
