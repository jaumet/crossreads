That town looked dismal under a heavy rainstorm, &amp; the effects on the people of the late air raids, but I managed to get a reasonably fair time by eating. Visited a couple of  the  "maisons tolerees" in the Pare de St. Paul &amp; found them as bad as the worst of Marseilles which are in turn worse than in Cairo, always excepting where blacks are kept.
