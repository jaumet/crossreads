 This country is a mixture of many races. The Fellahin or the agriculture class, the Beduin &amp; Town Arabs, Copts or Xtian Egyptians, Montenegrins &amp; Syrians, Jews, Albanians, Greeks, &amp; many other European races. 
 Most of the people are able to speak at least two or three languages. English, Arabic or French.
