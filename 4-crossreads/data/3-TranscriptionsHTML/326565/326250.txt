<p>Thursday 25/11/15<br/>Very warm, nearing the line must expect hot weather.  Very large sick parade.  Getting a nuisance coming after sick parade hours, practically attending to someone all day.  Started to treat some of their corns, had a crowd around before I knew where I was, soon cut it out.  During the night one side of Sgt Matheson's moustache was clipped off &amp; his hair cut at the back.  Caused great stir, extra duties were allotted to us all.  Very poor dinner owing to the</p>
