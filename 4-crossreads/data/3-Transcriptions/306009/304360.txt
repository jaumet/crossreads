<p>1915<br/>June 23 Wed.<br/>Feeling a bit too snoozy for early morning walk to-day.<br/>In the afternoon a cricket match was played between the Y.M.C.A. &amp; a No. 2. G.H. team playing 3 Officers &amp; 8 Privates &ndash; on the Y.M.C.A. ground at Gezira &ndash; we won.<br/>Watched a polo match &ndash; fine riders the players are with fine ponies.<br/>The 1st Army Pay Corps, A.I.F. held a concert in Ward 1 at 8 p.m. It was really good.</p>
