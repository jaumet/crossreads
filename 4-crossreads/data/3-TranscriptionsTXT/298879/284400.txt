 July 1915 Saturday 3  Fine    Heavy shower of rain last night. We often get a thunderstorm here. None of those light showers so common in Australia but big drops of rain that wet one through in quick time. Had a nice ramble with a fellow patient, of the Kings Own Scottish Borders &amp; named Walker 
 Sunday 4 Fine Another shower of rain this morning. I am some others are to go to the Provost Marshall tomorrow
