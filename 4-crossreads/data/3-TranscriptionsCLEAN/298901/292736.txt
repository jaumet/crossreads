 Thursday 6th June 1918 Sent off by car the drugs required by battalions & had a game of hockey at night after tea.  Weather good.  Offensive still going strong but is being held now to some extent. 
 Monday 10th June Nothing of interest happening.  We are just having a rest the men doing a little revetting during the day.  Gave a couple of lectures to my tent subdivision on practical subjects. Honours coming out now think Major Metcalf (deceased) D.S.O.  Capt Fay M.C. (gained for same thing the two M.M's were given for at Daours)  Major Close & Sgt I. Watkinson "Mentions".
