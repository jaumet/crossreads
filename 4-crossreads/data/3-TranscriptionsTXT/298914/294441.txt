Away over towards the sea can be seen the edges and part of the huge salt lake Sabket Bardwil a brilliant white colour and along the edge runs the railway which is being pushed on as fast as possible. 28/10/16 Take the set down for necessary repairs and make it very clean.  All new gear as far as possible used.  Play five hundred after tea and have great amusement. 29-10-16 Taube over again, drops a bomb near railhead and 3 others on Salmana. 30/10/16 Taube over again.  Anti-aircraft guns blazing at him from Salmana lob a few "overs" near us which explode in the sand, the time fuse not acting. 31/10/16 Mazar stunt postponed. 1/11/16 Feel a bit seedy same as on 2nd &amp; 3rd. 4/11/16 Go to AMC NZ MR and get fixed up. 5/11/16 Some rain falls during early morning.  Feel well.  Five hundred in wireless tent and roars of laughter 6/11/16 Feeling very seedy indeed.  Taubes over who chases one of our BE planes 8/11/16 Go to AMC again and get sent to Salmana in red cross van.  Spend the night in English hospital. 9/11/16 Take train to Kantara to be put in isolation.  Tonsilitis.  Have a dreary ride and go to 26th.  Casualty Clearing Station &ndash; English
