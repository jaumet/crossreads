 From - Captain Travers. &nbsp;&nbsp;To - Brigadier &amp; Rear-Admiral C. 
  12th Septr. &#39;14:  
 Have just returned on board &quot;Warrego&quot;. Have brought ten white prisoners and comandeered motor lorry. Lieut. Bond has taken up strong defensive position&nbsp;at Wireless. I understand he has been ordered to withdraw, and motor lorry is returning for his stores, etc. 
 (1300)
