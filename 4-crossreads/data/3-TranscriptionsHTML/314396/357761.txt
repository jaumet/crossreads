<p>out at 8.30. Came home at 11. Eygpt was proclaimed a British Protectorate too Plenty of new Eygptian flags kicking about. The flag has 3 crescents &amp; 3 Stars on a red ground.</p>
<p>Monday 21st. Dec 1914.<br/>NCOs marched up &amp;  down the hill all the morning. A route march in the afternoon &amp; I got a blister on my heel.<br/>Wrote 3 letters &amp; went to bed early.</p>
<p>Tuesday 22nd. Dec.<br/>Cold this morning.<br/>Went for marching both in the morning &amp; afternoon<br/>went to city in the evening.</p>
<p>Wednesday 23rd Dec 1914<br/>Cold again. Big marching order parade all day.<br/>Went all over the desert<br/>left at 9.30 &amp; got back at 4.15. Went out to Sheeshara tonite. Some real lovely granite there. A big hole &amp; all this granite in it.<br/>Coming home saw the American &amp;  his party digging for the ancient mummies etc. Saw 2 lads climb the second pyramid.
