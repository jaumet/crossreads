<p>24/3/18 &ndash; La Basse Ville<br/>be a bit "windy" on account of imagined shortage of men.  Went out to the two right posts and hurried back because at midnight our Stokes opened up and put 50 rounds over on to the Spinney.  The men tonight were in good heart.  A quiet and uneventful walk back to Bn. H.Q. along a tortuous and winding C.T.  We passed one or two ration parties and the sentries of Vickers Guns standing near their emplacements.  Up in the moon was the drone of heavy aeroplane engines and from his trenches climbing [indecipherable].</p>
