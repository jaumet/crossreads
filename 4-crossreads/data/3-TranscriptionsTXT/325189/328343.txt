All manner of things are hawked in the street among these being hard-boiled eggs.  There are many English "Terriers" in the place &ndash; the Lancashire Fusilleers.  The native quarter is the biggest of ramshackles.  We were advised to go about in company &amp; I think it is just as well.  About every five yds. touts surround us.
