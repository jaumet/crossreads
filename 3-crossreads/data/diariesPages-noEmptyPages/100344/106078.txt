2nd. Sunday. July
Church parade at Bttn Hqrs also Band out.
Football match between B C Coys. after lunch.
3rd. Monday.
Physical Coy drill all day with lecture on Gass.
4th. Tuesday.
Parades cut short about midday by heavy rain.
5th. Wednesday.
Very dull weather. Foot ball match between 7 8 platoon postponed owing to bad weather. Gass alarm.
6th Thursday.
Physical jerks. Drill contest between Coy's of 17th. Mail in.
7th. Friday.
Very dull weather. Parade stopped by rain.
