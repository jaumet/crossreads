<p>April 1918<br/>Wednesday 17</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours.   D.R.<br/>Everything fairly quiet.<br/>Rode across to Camon and had a decent feed.<br/>Went on to Amiens. Cathedral does not look too well, under Fritz's bombardment.<br/>Weather very wet.<br/>Fritz in Baille--  latest.</p>
