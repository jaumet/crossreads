<p>7th<br/>I left the 60th Bn and after a long tramp going almost due east the whole way I caught my battalion.  They were at rest in support of the 8th Aust. Bde near the town of Estrees.  I found that the Bn. had suffered heavily during the Peronne Stunt, Lts. Macarthur and Holder being killed and <strike>the</strike> John Adams badly wounded though after doing good work.
