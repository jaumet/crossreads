<p>After dinner, saw Rev. now Corporal Bistock Jones, who is here recovering also.<br/>It was a very bitter day &amp; slight snow fell &amp; a keen east wind blew.</p>
<p>Wednesday Jan 9th<br/>The weather today has again been very cold the ground is frozen hard &amp; the wind is very cutting.<br/>I visited the hospital this afternoon &amp; met several fellows I knew there</p>
<p>Monday Jan 14th<br/>The weather towards the latter end of last week &amp; yesterday was fine cold &amp; clear there is little of interest. I</p>
