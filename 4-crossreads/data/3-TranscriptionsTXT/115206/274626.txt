 February 1918 Monday 25 
 Messines Rained like the very deuce this morning. Left home at 6 am, with Kerr for F.O.P. Spent a rather chilly forenoon and registered Baab Farm In afternoon, whilst registering Code F, saw a Fritz arise like a spirit under the persistence of an 18 pounder H.E. Arrived home, 6.15 pm. Glorious moonlight night . Fritz dropped a few eggs on supports. Received letters from Doug H, and Mother
