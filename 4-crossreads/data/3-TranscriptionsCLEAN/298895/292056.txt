Had a heavy day as we shifted into the new baths which we start properly tomorrow.  Went for a walk along the banks of the "Ancre" & the lagoon tonight a positive treat.  In a months time it will be ideal.  The river is crossed by several bridges also the swamp this part being a part of an old line of defence & was evidently a very strong point, shell holes could be seen in the river itself & at places along the bank & a grave yard near, many of the crosses are splintered with the Shrapnel.
