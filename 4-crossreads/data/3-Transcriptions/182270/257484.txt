<p>SATURDAY, JUNE 21, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Roving round town all the</p>
<p>morning &amp; went up to Richmond</p>
<p>in the afternoon in a car with the</p>
<p>Lloyds &amp; Jack Hunter &amp; Marcia [Craven?]</p>
<p>Linda &amp; I went to Belmont for</p>
<p>afternoon tea. Got home for</p>
<p>dinner at night.</p>
