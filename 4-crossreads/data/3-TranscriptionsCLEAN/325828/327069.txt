
It is getting very cold up here now (October) & I think we are in for a severe winterI am keeping well, & am anxious to get to school of Military Aeronautics so as to classify for my Pilots Certificate   Meanwhile I am working quietly, for it will all come in handy.I am glad Walters arm is getting on satisfactorily, & trust it will continue to improve.Must close now as I am trying to squeeze in a note to Ol.With much love to each & all of you at home, & regrets that I am not able to give it you personallyEver your lovingLeo.
