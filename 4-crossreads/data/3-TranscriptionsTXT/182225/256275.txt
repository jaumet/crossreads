 1916 &nbsp; TUESDAY 6 &nbsp; JUNE 
 A fine warm day &amp; not much wind. It was great lying in sun out of the wind &nbsp;M r &nbsp;Neylan d &nbsp;gave the headquarters staff a lecture in morning and Tom Reed &amp; self did some buzzer work &nbsp;We had a lecture from Capt Samson (Doctor) on venereal diseases gonorhea &amp; syphlis. He gave us a very good lecture indeed. Passed a mob of&nbsp;gulls diving at a school of fish. They were in millions and lots of poiposes. It came up very windy again at night.
