<p>And provides good music &ndash; Apart from a score or so of 'khaki-clad" youths, the passengers are all French Civilian &ndash; Beautiful and inspiring river scenery &ndash; Must surely compare with the World's best &ndash; Arrive &amp; disembark at "La Boville" (a French Village on the Seine of picturesque &amp; natural beauty) After Lunch we divide into parties &amp; make a tour of inspection &ndash; "Old Chateaus" &amp; (Castles) - Statues &ndash; Landscapes - &amp; Rivery Scenery &ndash; Melle Jeanne. Candelibe - A</p>
