  
 Monday Sept 30 We are expecting to move in a few days to a better camping ground. It is impossible to stay here for long or all hands will be in Hospital. The weather at present is beautiful & large numbers of Turkish prisoners are coming in daily. Teusday Oct 1 We rode down to the Water troughs about one mile from station with a number of prisoners to bury the dead Horses. The 1ST Brigade are camped near this area.
