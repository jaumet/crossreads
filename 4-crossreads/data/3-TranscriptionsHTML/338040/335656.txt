<p>April 12th<br/>Eggs and bacon for breakfast. Route march under Cpt.Strachan to Steenwercke. Lecture on Haemorrhage. FIRST MAIL very happy<br/>April 13th<br/>Letters and P.C (12) written home. More wet weather. Route march and lecture. Eggs and bacon a regular.<br/>April 14th<br/>Evacuation of Rest Station. Long and weary march through mud and rain arrived at our Base</p>
