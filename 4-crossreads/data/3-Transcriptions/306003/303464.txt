<p>also kept up a heavy fire &amp; wiper out thousands of Turk coming along the coast to attack Marmadieh [Mahemdia] on the coast;</p>
<p>6-8-16<br/>Sun 6th<br/>The Sigs have a fairly bad time of it during the fighting  Nelson, Smithers &amp; Edwards HQ wounded &amp; poor old Alf Winterton A Sqd Cpl was killed &amp; Richardson H.Q. &amp; Roberts (Nelsons groom) are missing  I went to the Field Amb. this morning &amp; found Nelson &amp; Smithers &amp; Edwards were sent away yesterday  there was some doubts about Winterton, as to whether he was killed or wounded but Bassan &amp; other A Sqd Sig went out this morning &amp; found his body &amp; buried him &amp; put a cross of shells on his grave;  he was one of the finest lads I have known</p>
