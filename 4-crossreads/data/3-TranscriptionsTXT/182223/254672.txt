
Thursday 17 June 1915Busy all day. Patients in and out.  Examining those to be sent to Australia. Not so hot today.
Friday 18 June 1915Dr Hollywood of W. Maitland brought a parcel & letters from Mollie for me. Dinner, at 10-30 pm. at Borrills Cafe at Heliopolis - Hollywood, Garde, Leary, Plant & others 
Saturday 19 June 1915Heaps of patients today Over 350 in hospital Letters from Macquarie St, Maitland, & other places. Dates Febry & up to 18th May
Sunday 20 June 1915Mass  at Basilica (Byzantine Church) at 9-30 a.m. Working hard all day. Had dinner with Dr Schuber at the Chalet Mena at 7.30 pm.
