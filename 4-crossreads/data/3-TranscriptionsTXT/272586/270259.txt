 I am much for  comfortable here than I hitherto have been, the chaps in my tent are all decent lads and better educated than any other tent mates, in fact a better stamp altogether. We had a lot of wasters in the reinforcements. 
 A great number of our lads blame the New Zealanders for a lot of the trouble here they say they were a very wild lot and played up some I can quite believe this some of them look like proper toughs.
