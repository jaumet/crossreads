<p>2 August, Saturday</p>
<p>George &amp; I drive around Manly, visit Marg &amp; see Mrs Taubman. All family Home to tea. Also Nette.</p>
<p>3 August, Sunday</p>
<p>Nette &amp; I go to Otto's. We go up on Hill to see aboriginal carvings. Gramaphone Music after tea. I get warm before I go to bed.</p>
