<p>25 Sunday</p>
<p>Glorious weather.  12 or 14 officers came for afternoon tea &amp; one Serb lady &ndash; Sturdy sample with very big legs &amp; thick ankles.  A. Lindsay &amp; I had coffee with Milevoj &amp; Milan (tents) in their [indecipherable].  Miss Pritchard left also Stephie</p>
