
Raining heavily- men all working.
Saturday 25.5.18  Letter from Doff  Wrote to Doff- moving into line tomorrow night
Sunday 26.5.18  Relieved Raper in Line  had an amusing relief. no casualties.
Monday 27.5.18. Went around line, had a fight in No Mans Land- very warm
Tuesday 28.5.18  Visited by G.S.O III Army showed him front- did a good nights digging.
Wednesday 29.5.18. Went around front with Lee- Bn working very well
