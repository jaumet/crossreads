
<p>A family of Coli Snakes infest its roots. We got one of them 18&quot; long with fangs like a cat. Saw the Boa -- 34 feet long but they say he&#39;s only a small one. They are considered common. Went out to the observatory &amp; saw sev. snakes in boxes. Also&nbsp;chased a monk. Saw big hairy face peering from Gloom of bush. Joe flung a stick at him &amp; then went away, also me. A Baboon, Coast Ape(?), big as they grow &amp; the terror of the nigs! Up the track two Queensland lads had roused one up in the crown of a palm&nbsp;&amp; were shying at him. We went along to see what was up. The old monk got annoyed&nbsp;showed signs of coming down so we left there also nig guide&nbsp;</p>
