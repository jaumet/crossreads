Tuesday 17.10.16
Preparing to move further on to-day. Felt the cold during the night with only 1 blanket. Left Strazelle at 2 pm marched to Bailleul to entrain, about 9 kilos.
After putting wagons on train, moved out about 6 pm. 40 of us to a horse box, quite a tangle when we tried to stretch out.
Wednesday 18.10.16
Travelled all night. Disentrained at Longpre at 7.30 AM. Unloaded wagons marched off at 9 AM. Marched through L?Etoile on to Ergnies where we arrived billeted at 3.30 pm.
