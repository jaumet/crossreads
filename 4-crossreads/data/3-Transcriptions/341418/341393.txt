<p>Yesterday about seven oclock in the morning about 10 or more 'Planes went over to raid El Arish the Turkish base. They dropped 88 bombs  &amp;  did a lot of damage  &amp;  lost three of our 'Planes. So now we are expecting reprisals though no Turkish 'planes have put in an appearance up to now.<br/>June 20th<br/>Nuthing' doin' in the Turkish Planes business yet, I think our people must have put them out of action.<br/>Down in the palms again today  Been occupied sewing a new corner onto my haversack with the</p>
