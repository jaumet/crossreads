After Calais came another stretch of fine wooded country which showed up well as the sun began to burst forth for intervals (quite an April day) Next came St Omer where stayed a long time. A batch of beautiful V.A.D  nurses were seated with their baggage on the main platform waiting to be evacuated. This on account of the retreat &amp; consequent bringing of St Omer into more frequent shell fire.
