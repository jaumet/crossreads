<p>[Printed on card]<br/>"A hearty Signal." &ndash; From across the foam<br/> Flashes this message to our loved at home &ndash;<br/>"May God protect you bless you on your way,<br/>And health and happiness be yours for aye".</p>
<p>With<br/>Sincere Good Wishes<br/>For<br/>A Merry Christmas<br/>And<br/>A Prosperous New Year<br/>From Walter</p>
<p>2nd Australian Division,<br/>Gallipoli, 1915-16.</p>
