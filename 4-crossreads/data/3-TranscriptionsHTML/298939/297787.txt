<p>on the way; but it is not forthcoming.  Probably letters have been written but lost in transit.<br/>The daily life of the ambulance has not varied much, &amp; my position is just about the same.  I am hovering between the tent division &amp; stretcher bearers at present.  If the latter job falls to my lot I shall go through to the last, but I shall not last as long as I should on the tent division.<br/>I must try to scrape together some remnants of shattered faith, &amp; put trust in the workings of providence without fretting from day</p>
