
Monday 17 February 1919As usual its raining Went & saw Harry Brenett. Met Dad & we three went to lunch to a nice quiet little french cafe. Then went out to Marie & we went to see Madam Tressaud's Wax Works. It was very good. Janet came out & we then met Dad & went to dinner & then to 'Yes Uncle' Was goodo. Back for supper to Maries. Janet staying with Marie.
Tuesday 18 February 1919Snow this morning & also rain. Ran round as [indecipherable] Dad went to his Auntie at Colchester. Harry & I went to lunch & also to dinner at the french cafe. After dinner went for a stroll & then to Lyon's Corner House in the Strand for coffee. Music here was excellent.
