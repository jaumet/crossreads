<p>28 Aug 17<br/>In Scapa Flow, weather heavy &amp; blowing. Battle cruisers left for sea at 3 pm.</p>
<p>29 Aug 17<br/>In Scapa Flow</p>
<p>30 Aug 17<br/>Doing Squadron torpedo practice today. 2nd Battle Cruiser Squadron. Australia New Zealand. Inflexible &amp; Indomitable came in today.</p>
<p>1 Sept 17<br/>Anchored off North Shore Scapa Flow. Airships going to sea patrolling.</p>
<p>2 Sept to 6th<br/>Lying in Scapa Flow</p>
<p>7th Sept 17<br/>Draft left for Australia today. Battle cruisers left for Rosyth</p>
