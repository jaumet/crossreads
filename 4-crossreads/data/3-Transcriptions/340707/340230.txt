<p>7-9-19<br/>To day is the fete of St. Cloud &amp; the fountains play to day &amp; also on the 14-9-19 but after Versaille they are a poor show or rather are overshadowed by the number. One jet here rises to a height of 136 ft.</p>
<p>In the afternoon or rather midday we start out for St. Denis the burial place of most of the French Kings The fa&ccedil;ade built in 1140 preserves the old pre Gothic arch while the battlements along the top were built for other than peaceful pursuits.</p>
