<p>Sunday 6 Jan.<br/>Work at home today. Advised by Lieut Stanbury that I had been awarded Meritorious Service Medal.</p>
<p>Began inventory of wagon equipment. Afternoon football &amp; writing. Evening writing in bed, not too successful.</p>
<p>Met Bob Peebles. Artillery in same camp.</p>
