<p>Decembre 18<br/>Coy engaged on Coy work only fixing up camp &amp; making things comfortable generally. Freezing heavily after a small fall of snow.</p>
<p>Decembre 19<br/>Section &amp; Coy drill in the fornoon. Pay in afternoon &amp; nothing further for the day. Still freezing heavily.</p>
