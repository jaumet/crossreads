 March, 1917. Thursday 1 Got all material ready for charge but explosives only came at 1. Blew up at 3.30 in presence of a Brig general &amp; a collection of officers (Lavinia) After inspecting crater went home at 4 and after tea came up to drivers with our gear so as to push off with ration cart in morning at 8 o'clock. Got papers and also hear all our company is up with old camp as base &amp; Dick Lord wounded. Got to bed at 9 after coffee &amp; slept well.
