<p>1st F. Amb.<br/>Egypt<br/>22 Jan 16<br/>My dearest Irene,<br/>Young Docker joined our unit today. Came over on a Hospital ship, I think but am not sure.<br/>Have not yet managed to get to Cairo.<br/>We may leave here soon. A rumour to that effect is current.<br/>Have not yet seen young Garland. Hope to see him soon.<br/>Got three letters from you one day lately &amp; was very delighted thereat. I am writing this out on the desert during</p>
