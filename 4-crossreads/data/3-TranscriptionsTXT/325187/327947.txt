 [Sunday 23 July 1916] 
 "It is a case now of in again, out again, off again "Monaghan" We only come to Camp for Horsefeed &amp; Rations, scrap every day now. One of our chaps who got a crack swore &amp; sang out "The --- wait till Bill comes, he'll give 'em something"- meaning brother.  Several duels every day now &amp; we are not so good as the German Planes unfortunately (I should not say this)  With glasses I could pick up the colored Austrian or German uniform on Officers out on the Desert I suppose they'll get closer yet &amp; well have a big go soon.  We can do with all BILLS BROTHERS-
