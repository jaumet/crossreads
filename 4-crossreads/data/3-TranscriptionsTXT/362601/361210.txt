 27 May, Tuesday 
 XX 1695 letters to here XX 
 F. Go to Y.M.C.A. &amp; write all morning. Go with Billy in afternoon &amp; play Billiards. He licks me by 25. I sit in hut &amp; write all night &amp; then play Bridge until nearly 11 pm. 
 Wrote Mum, &amp; letter M.R.L., Esme, Dad, Ellis. 
 28 May, Wednesday 
 F. We are warned to get ready to march out. 
 Pay Parade. Get &pound;4. Recd 2 letters from Home. I watch B &amp; Ern play Billiards. Win 1 each. I go to Anglo Scotch Concert. It is fine " She's got to be, if she isn't &amp; in She's the loss for me. All the drunks come Home." 
 Wrote M.R.L (2)
