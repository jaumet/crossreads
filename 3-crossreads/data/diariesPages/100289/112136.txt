Our transport entered for the best turned out general service limber, much to the delight of all the unit; were successful in pulling off 1st prize; All looking forward now to the Divisional sports laid down for Tuesday next. Pitched tents again which were struck about 8 or 9 days ago, prior to our anticipated departure.
Sunday 11.6.16
Church parade; reference made to the death of Lord Kitchener
Monday 12/6/16
The whole of the unit marched to the palms on the opposite bank of the lake at Ismailia, where we spent a very pleasant morning at our leisure, returning to camp at 12 noon. Have been given very little work since the postponement of our departure.
