
<p>THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 4, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Lopped in Top paddock</p>
<p>&amp; Mountain in morning and cutting</p>
<p>cotton bush in the afternoon.</p>
<p>Linda came down in afternoon</p>
<p>and we had some tea in Wheelers</p>
<p>Tickle poisoning in Wheelers &amp;</p>
<p>Middle paddock.</p>
<p>Barry came out &amp; got here for</p>
<p>lunch.</p>
