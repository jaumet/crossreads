<p>Panama was formerly a department of Colombia, but in 1903 it proclaimed its independance which was recognised by the U.S.A. &amp; subsequently by othyer powers.  At the same time the New Republic agreed to provide facilities for the construction of an inter-oceanic canal.  The U.S.A. was granted a zone of territory 5 miles wide on each side of the canal, with exclusive control in return for a payment of &pound;50,000.
