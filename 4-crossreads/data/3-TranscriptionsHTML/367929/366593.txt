<p>February 1915<br/>6 Sat<br/>Lt Weir with reinforcements reports arrival at Abassia Reinf.camp.<br/>Dinner continued met several new 2nd [indecipherable] and Onslow. Dinner with R Horden and W Charlie. Dance at Mrs Devonshires.</p>
<p>7 Sun<br/>to town in afternoon for drive round Rhoda Island and past zoo.</p>
