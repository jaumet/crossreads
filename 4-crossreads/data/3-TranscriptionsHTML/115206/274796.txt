<p>August,1918<br/>Monday 12</p>
<p>Spelling at Villers Brettoneaux<br/>Stayed at a little possie near Hamel for the day. Weather warm. All drivers and gunners went down to Hamel Lagoon and spent the day.<br/>There have been several casualties in the Battery, they are O.B.Walker, Jack Hills (killed) and Bdr Ferguson Jack Gorrick, Bill Hart, Harry Marsh, wounded and Roy Lewis missing.<br/>Got some kit from several sources.<br/>Had a very enjoyable swim in the Lagoon.<br/>Rumor we move up again in the morning.<br/>Had one or two bombs Bdr Townsend wounded</p>
