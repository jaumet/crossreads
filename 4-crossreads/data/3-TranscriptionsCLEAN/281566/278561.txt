  
 The 10th saw further activity in the artillery  line. Our guns  &  mortars began to spread German sand bags over the landscape, continues for a few minutes quite unopposed. Then the answer came by way of a rifle grenade, whizz-bangs  &  and heavier shells. This was kept up for the best part of the afternoon but very little damage resulted.
