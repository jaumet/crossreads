<p>December 1916<br/>22 continued   afternoon, Pritchard &amp; I went down &amp; found Smith's grave near Bernafay Wood (in the Bernafay Cemetary) took a couple of photo's &amp; intend sending them to his mother whos address is in the back of this book.  Fritz has been shelling "Lone Pine" very heavily to-day &amp; has caused a fair number of casualties.<br/>We received orders to-day to have our kits packed, ready for an early move on Sunday morning.</p>
