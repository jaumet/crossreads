<p>July 13th 1917. Moascar Camp.</p>
<p>Information got from Pte J. Gould C B F A who was on &quot;Pioneer&quot;.</p>
<p>Notes on German Cruiser &quot;Konigsberg&quot; action.</p>
<p>1st Action commenced 3.30 p.m. Fri 6th July 1915, and finished at 4-30 pm. Fri 6th July 1915.</p>
<p>Cruisers &quot;Pioneer&quot;, &quot;Hacynith&quot; [Hyacinth], &quot;Weymouth&quot; &quot;Challenger&quot; &quot;Pyrimus&quot; [Pyramus], &quot;Trent&quot; (Hosp. Ship.) Aux Cruiser Laconia (recently sunk) 2 monitors Severn &amp; Mersey 10 whalers besides. 2 Br. seaplanes.</p>
<p>Konigsberg fired at random &amp; opened the action 7 miles up Rufugi [Rufiji] River. Cruisers stood at entrance to river. Germans opened fire at 3.30 a.m. Kata<strike>ra</strike>marang used by Germans as range finder. 1st shell missed &quot;Pioneer&quot; by 3 yds. Monitors opened fire &amp; entered river. Went to within 3 mls of &quot;Konigsberg&quot; &amp; cleared banks as they went. &quot;Weymouth&quot; &amp; &quot;Hacynith&quot; [Hyacinth] fired on hills. &quot;Pioneer&quot; went to Sth entrances of Rugi [Rufiji] River (delta) &amp; engaged guns &amp; kept up fire for 13 hrs. firing broadsides &amp; turning round.
