Oct 10th
There is talk of a mail coming soon. we now again hear some war news which is very soon devoured as we are hungry for news have had no letters from England as yet, perhaps all letters stopped by censor
Oct 11th
Fever breaks out a lot of men get sick Malaria is the fever which I have had before in Crete not very pleasant either
Oct 12th
 Feel all right myself although lots look seedy
Oct 13th
Mail arrives get loan of some newspapers have a good read of all the news
