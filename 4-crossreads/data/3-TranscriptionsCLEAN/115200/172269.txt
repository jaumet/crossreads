 These are the rations that [we?] get issued to us at the front 
 Jam 1/4 of a tin per Day. 
 Sugar. One [ounce?] per Day  Perhaps it may be two 
 Bacon 2 Slices per Day. 
 Bully Beef you can get as much of this tack as you want  tea 2 ounces per Day. Cheese about 3 ounces and it is not to good at that 
 Salt very little. Biscuits of these there was an unlimited Supply. Rum about 1/4 of a pint a week. 
 This ration is Supposed to last us 24 hours
