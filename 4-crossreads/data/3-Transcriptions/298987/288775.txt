<p>bed is made another respite &amp; the water comes along for the morning wash then a good spell &amp; then Breckfast turns up. Am a bed patient at present</p>
<p>Monday 24th<br/>Feel a bit better today. Had Fish &amp; Rice for dinner the firs solid meal since Friday Only milk etc while the Tempre lasts. Got up this afternoon Nurse brought some books got one of O Henrys not bad</p>
<p>Tuesday 25th Nothing fresh. A Canaidain in the next bed to me. They have a camp near here cutting timber &ndash; He is a very decent sort another Canaidain in the next bed half bred Indian seems a regular dope</p>
<p>Wednesday 26th<br/>Went out this evening &amp; as there was a very good Lecture on the Panama Canal in the YM close by stayed to it quite forgetting that our time to be in was 7 PM Evidently was not missed as nothing has been sayed about it. At Dartford an Orderly used to come</p>
