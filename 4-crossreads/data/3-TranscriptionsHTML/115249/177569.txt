<p>Ship of Origin - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 8 - 9 - 14</p>
<p>Time received or sent. 2. 5</p>
<p>RA to officer Commanding troops,&nbsp;CO Sydney, Commander D &amp;&nbsp;Yarra, Information has been received as follows. At Kapanga about ten miles South of Cape Gazelle lives a Samoan woman named Gracie Schmidt who is known to be loyal to the British and who would if required send a message to her half brother Henry Cox who lives at Kapopo close to Kabakaul and would willingly provide at least forty native guides for the troops at Kabakaul i[f]</p>
