 22 Sun April moving again to day to a new camp  are amongst the Tommies don't find them so bad 
 23 Mon April Camped about 3 miles from Khan Yunus  watering there terrible dusty nearly chokes me  very quiet on the front  Gaza very hard to take 
 24 Tues April Buggar of a day blowing like Hell from the South.  dust to blind a chap.  Had a drop of J W. otherwise very quiet 
 25 Wed April Very hot &amp; dusty  first wash for Four days  it was great  would give a 100 for a long P. went to the beach about 9 miles swam horse  longing to have a spell   feel done
