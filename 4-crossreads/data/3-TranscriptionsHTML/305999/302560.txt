<p>sent to my Bde training camp</p>
<p>4/1/17<br/>I was tried at orderly room and given 32 days pay and 8 days detention  I was not striped</p>
<p>12/1/17<br/>I was releaved from the detention and was paid 30 bob</p>
<p>13/1/17<br/>we cleared out to London on French leave and had a good time</p>
<p>12/3/17<br/>I serendered to the police warick square</p>
<p>15/3/17<br/>I was escorted to lark Hill Camp to be tried</p>
<p>17/3/17<br/>I was tried and given 28 F P No 2  51 days R W on saint Patricks day</p>
