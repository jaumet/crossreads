 Oct. 11th I make a start to complete "The Xmas Carol" &ndash; Mail closed for several days &ndash; Hospital 
 Oct. 12th. Still a patient &ndash; Wild rumours concerning A.I.F.  &ndash; "Salonica" England etc. Letters &amp; papers from Home. 
 Oct 13th My eye is slowly improving &ndash; Gramophone in the Hospital. Pleasant memories awakened by "Humoreske" &ndash; "Cornish Floral Song". &ndash; More Home letters
