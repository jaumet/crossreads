<p>&amp; introduced me to the girl it appears she does nothing else but go round &amp; visit the Australians in hospital she's an American girl &amp; a real bonza, just after the style of our own girls in Australia</p>
<p>Another concert was given in our Ward last night by 6 little girls they were not bad.</p>
<p>16th. Have been skating again this afternoon got on real well had only 1 buster the whole time, &amp; while there this afternoon I bought a pair of splendid skates a chap sold them to me for 12/- they are <strike>Wilcoxs</strike> "Winslows" make and a pair of beauties they are too fast at present if anything for me they run like greased lightning seems funny don't it being in hospital &amp; yet able to go skating like fun, the old chap who looks after the skates at the rink knows me alright, calls me the mad Sgt.</p>
