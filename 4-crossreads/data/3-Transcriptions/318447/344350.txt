<p>&amp; which had not been heard of since. So we started wet, tired &amp; without rations. I was not told what to do when I got there in fact there was nothing we could do except find out their whereabouts &amp; if merely for that, why six men?</p>
<p>On arriving at Yebnah a native village some halfway back we were overtaken by night which left three courses open to me, to go on to Esdud &amp; in all human probability get bogged in the fertile plains of Philistea &amp; certainly miss the waggon</p>
