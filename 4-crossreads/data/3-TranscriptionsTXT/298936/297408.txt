 September 1915 
 Thursday 9 
 Good bye to the doctors &amp; and such nurses as I know &amp; away by car to Cairo station catching the midday train for Alexandria. The line runs through the very fertile land in the basin of the Nile delta. 
 Corn &amp; other crops as far as the eye can see. 
 Arrived at Alexandria &amp; drafted to Mustapha barracks. Leave in Alex. for evening. 
 Went in with Kennet &amp; Hunter
