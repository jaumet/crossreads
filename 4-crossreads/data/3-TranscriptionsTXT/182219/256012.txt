 31st Dec 
 An so on the last day of the year I find myself way out on the hills of Judea taking part in the campaign against the turks at K &ndash; I &ndash; H. A is a time when all past memories crowd on me, thoughts of home &amp; dear ones &amp; of when I shall see them again. Have been out most of the day &amp; as I am dead tired am now going to turn in for the night.
