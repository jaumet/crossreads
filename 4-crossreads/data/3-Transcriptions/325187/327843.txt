<p>[Friday 7 April 1916]</p>
<p>(Big mail tonight Mother Joe Daisy Jean Mathis Miss Chase Cuthbert Fetherstonehough  May Harrison &amp; Papers from Joe- Cannot answer all.)<br/>I see my missing Diary is one containing &pound;1 in Gallipoli Notes, also a sketch I made of our position &amp; some of my letters must be in Davy Jones locker.  Still I'm not, so that's all right.<br/>Went to see some Howling Dervishes dance tonight mostly naked <strike>men</strike> &amp; Tom Toms &amp; Backsheesh<br/>Senussi chucking it, &amp; we are to occupy Kharga Oasis, West of Oasis Junction.  see map</p>
