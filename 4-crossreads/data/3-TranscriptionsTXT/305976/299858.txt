Friday May 4Everybody stiff &amp; tired. Had easy day. Informed we are sailing on 10th inst by BooarraSaturday May 5Weekend leave to Melbourne arrived 4.15pm. Spent afternoon strolling round Town evening visited St KildaSunday May 6Went down to Port Melbourne to see transport.Monday May 7thMany rumours in train. It is said 21 Transports sailing on Thursday.Tuesday May 8thPacking Black kit bags, final medical examination Wrote letters.
