  
 May 1915 Friday 28 Fine. Our fatigue carrying water for the company. The Turks shelled us with 9.2 in shells and did no harm as usual. Our guns doing a bit for their living again, the enemy replying. We pelt bombs & flares of all sorts at one another of a night time. More reinforcements have reached us 
 Saturday 29 Fine Lively work near the 4th Batt. trenches. A few Turkish prisoners taken. Many men wounded. Lively sniping between our chaps and the Turks. Came out of fire trench at 6 PM. Our aeroplanes very busy doing reconaiscance work. On guard tonight
