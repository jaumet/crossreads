<p>Tamworth, &amp; England.</p>
<p>After we marched home from the service the great event of the day came off &amp; that was the final of the tug of war between C. &amp; A Coy. great  was the excitement as the two teams took the strain the first pull was a fair one &amp; was won by C. Coy amid great cheering they then reversed end's for the 2nd pull but it was a short one for C. Coy again kept up its reputation &amp; pulled A. head over heels amid a scene of great excitement, all the officers were out as usual barracking for their side, Len was one of our team of 8 the win was worth a hundred franc's but that is</p>
