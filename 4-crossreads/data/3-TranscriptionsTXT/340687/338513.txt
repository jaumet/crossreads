
July, 1917. Saturday 28Fine cloudy day. Arrived destination 2.30. Helped unload horses & marched off via Morbecoue past Wallon Cappelle to a farm house. Breakfast & slept till dinner. Slept again till tea time. Bad billets & sleeping under trees. In evening went with Taylor to Hazebrouck. returned at 9. Very nice place. Then bed. Country very pretty and intensely cultivated and everything very clean. Slept well.
