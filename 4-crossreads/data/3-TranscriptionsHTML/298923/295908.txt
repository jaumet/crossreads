<p>30 August 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Good news still comes from France &amp; the World should hear some news in this quarter soon-<br/>Visited dentist again &amp; finished now am glad to say- &amp; am all ready for what is in front.<br/>Our sector has been quiet &amp; deserters come in each day 1 or 2 at a time-<br/>X Had no mail from Australia for some time so a lot of letters must be on the way-<br/>Alls well with the wife &amp; Babe- X</p>
