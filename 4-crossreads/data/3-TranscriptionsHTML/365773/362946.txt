I hope your health you dear old pal of mine is a lot better you are too unselfish in your letters to mention your own ills, all you write about is Owen or myself &amp; both of us are hulking strong fellows well endowed by nature &amp; perfectly fitted to look after ourselves, yet you worry like the deuce about us &amp; never mention yourself - keep me posted about Ralph I will drink success to him when he passes his final - your latest accounts of Miss Perdriau</p>
