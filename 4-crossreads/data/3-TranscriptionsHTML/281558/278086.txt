<p>the boat : rough trip over &amp; plenty of feed for fishes : landed at Boulogne &amp; rested for night at top of "one blanket hill" : blankets very "chatty" &amp; soon made pals with some of them :</p>
<p>Tuesday Feb 12th</p>
<p>Boulogne : fell in about 5 pm &amp; marched to station : train to Harfleur :</p>
<p>Feb 13th Wednesday</p>
<p>French train : arrived Harfleur about 3 pm &amp; marched to A.G.B.D. [Australian General Base Depot] issued blankets etc, then bed :</p>
<p>Feb 14th Thursday</p>
<p>Harfleur : on stone fatigue : warned for draft but did not go :</p>
<p>Friday Feb 15th</p>
<p>Harfleur : gas parade to "Bullring" &amp; went through the gas chambers, both tear &amp; cloud : met C Hawke O Dunstan &amp; Alf</p>
