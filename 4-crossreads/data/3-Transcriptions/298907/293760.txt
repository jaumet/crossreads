<p>Weather is still very unsettled &amp; showery, I wish it would clear up Yesterday afternoon having nothing much to do I went &amp; had a look at Museum &amp; Art Gallery, the former is very good especially as regards birds, but the Art Gallery is nothing out of the ordinary though I fancy most of the good pictures have been removed to places of safety for fear of air raids &amp; etc</p>
