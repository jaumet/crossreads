<p>July 8 Lasted 2 hours solid. This artificial eye of mine is providing some amusement. In the theatre it twisted round some, showing the white, &amp; I was wondering why people were looking so hard. Later, The Provost Marshall pulled me up for not saluting, but he climbed down when I told him that my left eye was <u>minus</u><br/>[in margin] H.C.</p>
<p>9 On Pioneer fatigue &amp; its not a bad job, just a couple of hours in the morning. Inspection this morning by the Colonel &amp; we had to turn our beds up. Had a good hot shower, an equally<br/>[in margin] Sunday</p>
