  
 The harbour was full of steamers tied up 2 & 3 deep at the Quay waiting to be loaded or unloaded.  We had to go to the British Consulate & get passports with our photos attached otherwise we would not have been allowed to land.  These passports were only for the city of Marseilles not even for France.  While here we saw a great many German prisoners at work.  They looked happy & jolly & used to march past our boat in the morning & evening going to & from their work.  There must have been 150 or more.
