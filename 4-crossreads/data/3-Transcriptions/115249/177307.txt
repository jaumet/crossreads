<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Sydney</p>
<p>To - Brigadier</p>
<p>Date - 11 9 4</p>
<p>Time - 4.38</p>
<p>I am informed from the shore&nbsp;<s>that</s>&nbsp;party will return at 4.30. alright. &nbsp;I think you will not be able to get to Kata Kaul and back before dark. &nbsp;In any case Berrima must shift from here as you are masking my fire.</p>
<p>1630</p>
<p><strike>Not intending to move&nbsp;to Kaba Kaul until daylight tomorrow.</strike></p>
