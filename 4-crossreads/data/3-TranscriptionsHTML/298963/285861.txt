<p>I fear sunshine is not in France for him.  Sometimes he used to talk about Carrie &amp; I know that she was ever in his thoughts.</p>
<p>10th June<br/>Jerry came again.  Alf is better &amp; on his way back up the line &ndash; hard luck &ndash; he is not as rough as we are &amp; was cut out for a better calling.</p>
