<p>13 April 1918 Saturday (X to X crossed out)<br/>X Still Bethlehem  Censored 14th-15th<br/>Getting monotonous waiting here for Orders to rejoin-  Hear today the Turks attacked our Pontoon Bridge over Jordan-  If I cannot get leave, would prefer to be with Regt:- but have had a good spell here in B.X<br/>It is getting quite hot &amp; the wild flowers which have been beautiful are going to seed.  Are we to see another summer through I wonder.<br/>Fighting in France is terrific &amp; I see many names of men I knew among the Casualties.<br/><strike>Letter from London  C is loafing again</strike></p>
