
Monday 8 November 1915 Air Temperature ideal this moment 7 a.m.Left hospital ward at 4 pm. Nearly O.K. At dinnerMess meeting after dinner Walk with Henry Dr after dinner.
Tuesday 9 November 1915 As I look out of my window at the central dome of this great building - Palace Hotel - & note its surroundings of architecture & atmosphere, my mind thinks:- How much a good Architect can do to fit puny art into great Nature? -
Wednesday 10 November 1915 Met [Lieut.] Col. Nicholson in Col. Newmarch's office this morning: He has just come from Sydney, in charge of troops. In Cairo for a little during the afternoon
