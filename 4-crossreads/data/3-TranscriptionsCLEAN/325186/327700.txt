  
 I was at the Base at Havre for about five days & was in the Town for one night for which I was up for orderly room but I got dismissed with a caution. I left the base on Friday 24th Aug in the usual troop-train cattle -trucks & no room to move in them  After two days travelling I got back to the Battn after being nearly six months away.  I found a lot of new faces there & a good many old ones missing      The Battn were in the rest area at a small village named  Vandringheim , about 6 kilos from  Lumbres )  We got plenty of drill & had to polish everything up to the knocker  On the whole we had a good time whilst
