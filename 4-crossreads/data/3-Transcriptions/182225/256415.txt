
<p>166</p>
<p>OCTOBER &nbsp; MONDAY 23 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; fairly warm. I was Brigade headquarters orderly all day &amp; heard that all drivers ect had boys up to clean harness ect so I did not show up till 10 pm at night &amp; missed it all. Everybody getting things ready for the inspection by General Brunker tomorrow. Campbell got thrown from his horse &amp; had to go into Fargo hospital.</p>
