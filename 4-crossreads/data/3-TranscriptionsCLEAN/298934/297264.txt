
Pentaloupe a French penal settlement.  650 patients on board, 7 doctors & 80 on the staff.  French battleship passed us.  It was very hot during the evening & the ship was put about to try & get a breeze.
Friday 25th June
Up to now sea has been dead calm but this morning a breeze has sprung up & it is nice & fresh.  At breakfast time 2 submarines & a destroyer passed us.
Saturday 26th June
Land visible on European side.  Sea dead calm.  Very pleasant.  We hugged the coast of Spain.  Saw the snow capped Sierra
