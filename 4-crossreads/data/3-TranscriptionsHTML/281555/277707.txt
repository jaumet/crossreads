<p>1st. April. Saturday<br/>Weather very pleasant, warm visited village 10 to 12 (noon) Recd. parcel from Eileen. N.Z.</p>
<p>2nd. Sunday.<br/>Walked with other mates to Steenbeck for afternoon took 2 snaps returned about sundown, put about 1 hour in Thiennes &amp; returned to billet. Nice day.</p>
<p>3rd. Monday.<br/>usual duty, weather good</p>
<p>4th. Tuesday<br/>Afternoon Snaps along canal in rear. evening develloped same &amp; others beautiful weather bed in Pm. Parcel of Kodak films Fowler N.Z.</p>
<p>5th Wednesday<br/>All day Route march 10 miles with Transports etc. well organised with rests. Developing finished night 11Pm. Results of 10 films very fair. weather dull moderate temperature.</p>
<p>6th. Thursday.<br/>Colder &amp; dull weather, all films</p>
