<p>8th<br/>Went to Norwich to day and saw the castle and Museum, cathedral and Grammar School.  All very wonderful and ancient buildings.</p>
<p>9th<br/>Had tea today with some friends and then had a stroll down to Bungay Station with a Major in R.A.F. who had <strike>p</strike> been through the whole piece.</p>
<p>10th<br/>Left Lady Twiggs place this morning and went to Ashby De La Zouch Station via Ditchingham, Norwich, Ely, Peterboro and Leicester.  I was met at the Station by Miss Smith in the car and driven on here to Cole Orton Hall where I was soon made</p>
