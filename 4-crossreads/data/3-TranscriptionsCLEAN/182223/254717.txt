  
 Monday 22 November 1915  Post mortems during the morning  Not out during afternoon or morning  Writing & reading & sleeping during afternoon 
 Tuesday 23 November 1915  In p.m. room during the morning  At Andebanes for afternoon tea.  Bed 9 p.m. 
 Wednesday 24 November 1915  P.M. room during morning. Posted letters to Potts at H. A College [Hawkesbury Agricultural College] Windsor N. S.Wales.  At Zeitoun & Matarieh between 4 & 6 p.m. with Cpts Henry & Black. 13 letters from Australia at 6 p.m. must read them now.
