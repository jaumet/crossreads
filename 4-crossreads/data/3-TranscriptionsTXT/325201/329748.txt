Asluj Oct 30th '17 This is a queer little place ringed in with hills it has walls, barracks  &amp; a small mosque. The Railway (Turkish) from El Wadia to Beersheba passes through here, or I should say, used to pass here, for last March the Div. stunted out here &amp;  blew up the bridges  &amp;   also the viaduct at this place.
