 Saturday 3 March 17 This morning I went to Canteen &amp; after lunch left Houplines for Armentiers.  My kit came up &amp; for another spell I am back in old billets.  Our men came out early in the morning.  The weather has turned very cold again, sharp white frosts &amp; keen winds. 
 Sunday 4th Went to Billets for a few minutes, lunched at "au Boef" with Capt. Blake. The weather has been nice &amp; clear for a couple of days &amp; quite cold.  I had a service at the Y.M.C.A. tonight.  Had a good time.  Three men decided for Christ.
