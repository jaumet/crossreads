<p>I also had a look at their cemetery they are a wonderful people to look after their dead, I'll warrant some of their vaults &amp; headstones cost a fortune, they are most elaborately fitted up, &amp; no expense is spared some of the vaults date back for a couple of hundred year's.<br/>This evening an enquiry was held before Mr Graham about the loss of the rifles last night on the train, I had to give the main part of the evidence, for I was in charge of the truck at the time, my report practically settled the matter, so I don't expect there will be anything more about the matter<br/>Great rumour's tonight of a great British &amp; French victory at Peronne I hope its true, its up to us to shake them up a bit.<br/>12th. Got orders to pack up last night &amp; be ready to move off at 7 in the morning so we had an early breakfast &amp; off we</p>
