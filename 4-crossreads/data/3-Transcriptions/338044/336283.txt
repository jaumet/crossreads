<p>[The next paragraph is written in a different hand]<br/>15th July  Brek 7 45  Sort of a greasy potato puff puff &ndash; slop tea &amp; bread. Overhauled &amp; passed two tramp steams bound west about 10 0. Sardinia in sight at 8-9 this morn.  Parade 10.0.<br/>Exs Jam <strike>18</strike>1/  chocolate 1/-</p>
<p>[The next sentences are in Hurley's handwriting]<br/>Alexandria &amp; we may go.  Home early &amp; learn French. Then to bed.</p>
