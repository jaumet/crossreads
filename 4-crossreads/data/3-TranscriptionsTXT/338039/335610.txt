 June. 1916 
 June 1st. Discharged from Hospital- Australians inspected at Croix-du-Bac by Messrs. A. Fisher &amp; Wm. Hughes. June 2nd. Trench &amp; dug-out digging in our paddock- safeguard against German shelling.  Parcel despatched to Home.  Our friends in P.C. shop &ndash; Correspondence.  Artillery active. June 3rd. Trench digging- detailed to bring stretcher case from Erquinghem.  Wet w.,
