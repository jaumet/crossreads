<p>Dec<br/>Thursday  4th<br/>The ground was frozen yesterday for about 2 in down in places same today. Very hard to pick Snowed for about 10 minutes today. Do not feel it out of the way cold</p>
<p>Wednesday 5th<br/>Drain makeing</p>
<p>Thursday  6<br/>A fresh party arived yesterday to take our places as we were nearly all untrained men We started this morning in time to miss the 9 train had wait untill 12 arived Sutton Veny about 2. Verry heavy frost this morning</p>
