<p>Up to about 11 oclock the morning was fairly quiet &amp; all the Sergts were busy organising their men &amp; fixing the trench up a little for we all expected to have to fight for it like tigers we got a good number of the wounded out, collected all bombs, fixed posts &amp; reliefs up etc. &amp; soon we were ready for the Hun, we did not have long to wait for he started a bombing attack &amp; things were pretty lively for a time but eventually he was beaten off with</p>
