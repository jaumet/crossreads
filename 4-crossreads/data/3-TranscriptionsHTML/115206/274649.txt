<p>March 1918<br/>Wednesday 20</p>
<p>Messines<br/>2 am Fritz putting a few across the road, very little gas.<br/>Rained throughout the day until 4 pm.<br/>Went down to Regt Battl to see "Wireless"<br/>Cooked the breakfast and was M.O. all day, had a clean up on strength of it.<br/>Pill box leaks on top, caused plenty of amusement and discomfort.<br/>Russell and Adey went out to O.P. the former relieved Gun of his duties.<br/>Charlie Adey went across to L'aison. Not feeling very brilliant</p>
