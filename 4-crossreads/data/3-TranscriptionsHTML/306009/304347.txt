<p>1915<br/>June 10 Thu.<br/>Descended like an avalanche on the factory where the ill fitting uniforms were turned out &amp; got a promise of 2 new tunics the following day.<br/>It was well over 100&deg; in the <strike>sun</strike> shade when we were out but heat very dry, so alright.</p>
