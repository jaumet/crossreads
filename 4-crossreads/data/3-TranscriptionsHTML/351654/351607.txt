Last time we were out I saw Duncan Robertson &amp; Jimmy Wilkinson (both A1) but have not seen "Torpedo" Walker since Egypt &amp; think he must be still down the Canal way.  The noise of the bombardment about here is terrific &amp; a great many of the men in the battery go completely deaf for days but tonight things are fairly quiet &amp; I am off for a bit, but sitting in the dug out lumps of the roof keep falling off from the concussion.
