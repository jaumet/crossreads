
Dear MotherWe are working hard loading transports There are lots of French and Englishsoldiers here. I am prettty well. This is a P.C. issued to the French troops foruse at the Front. love to Jim and Paddy and yourself.Best love to all, your loving son Arthur  XXXXXXXXXXXXXXThere are Zouaves and Turkos here and all sorts of French uniforms. Thetown is alive with soldiers. My letters may be irregular after this as we do notknow when we may move. a lot have gone
