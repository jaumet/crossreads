 Monday 30th Cold day &ndash;  Up &amp; walked horse  Had horses fed &amp; watered as possible during the day. No trips. Living in a tent 7 of us abreast in a 10 yds space. 
 Tuesday 1st October Walked horses about &ndash; fed &amp; watered. 
 After dinner ordered out with full gear. Packed up &amp; with Mr Fountain moved out to 10th Bde again. Still pretty rough, took shelter behind a tank. After waiting about 1 hour, further work found impracticible. Returned home none too sorry.
