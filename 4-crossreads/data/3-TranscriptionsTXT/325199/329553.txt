  Monday  Wednesday July 26. 1916 Here we are at last. There is every convenience. Houses to sleep in, hot &amp; cold baths, a canteen. The number four house is 28 &amp; it will accommodate 30 men. We were allotted to various Houses before dinner, 3 pioneers in each house along with a platoon of B. company. Our House is No 14 &amp; it's accommodating 30 men. There is plenty of room &amp; tables for writing. 
  Tuesday  Thursday July 27. 1916. A hot steamy day, most unpleasant. I was drilling with B. Company. Paraded sick this evening as I had a sore throat &amp; chest. [Following shorthand is not transcribed.]
