<p>13 August 1918 Tuesday<br/>Back once more &amp; got a letter June 16th from Mother &amp; Day glad mine arrived-  Re Sword the Officer who took it said it was taken from him by the Government pretty rotten.<br/>Horses met me (new ones) &amp; the dust is bad   "Flu" has commenced out here it is always a shock getting back after the comfort of a House &ndash; Sheets- a Bath etc.<br/>Parcel also arrived many many thanks for same.  Sorry my eggs did not arrive.</p>
