<p>Expenses &ndash;<br/>Sgts' mess fees 2fr. adm. &amp; 2&frac12;fr. for 1 week., 4&frac12;fr.<br/>Sundries &ndash; &frac12;fr.<br/>5fr.</p>
<p>from Gympie.  Haven't had time to read paper, so don't know the news.  Plenty of mud here &ndash; main item.  Bed early.</p>
<p>Oct. 18th &ndash; Wednesday<br/>Reveille as usual at 6 a.m. &amp; parade 6-30 a.m.  Raining hard all night but not so cold.  Sleep well.  Cold day, cloudy, but no rain.  I have charge of a fatigue party during the morning on lines &amp; a picket for lines afternoon.  Most of the A.M.C. at the "Bull Ring".  Three trains pass the camp &ndash; the 5th Division Australians &ndash; going from Ypres to the Somme.  Scott goes to England.  News from the lines scarce.  Another "push" coming.  A walk to 4th Div. Sgts. Mess &amp; bed 9 p.m.</p>
<p>Oct. 19th &ndash; Thursday<br/>Has been raining hard all</p>
