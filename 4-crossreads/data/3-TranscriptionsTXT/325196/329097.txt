 26-1-16 Wed.26th Eric Dowling was sent to Hospital this morning with mumps; We had mounted parade this morning &amp; dismounted this afternoon; Nelson had to mark off the camps boundaries this afternoon;We have been having showery weather since we have been in Egypt this winter is much colder &amp; wetter  than last year; The patrols now go onto the ridges dismounted as it is hardly worth saddling horses to ride the few hundred yards &amp; it is cold on the horses out all night; I believe there has been some fighting in Egypt &amp; the Artillery from the 53rd Div. left here today
