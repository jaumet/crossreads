<p>transport Horses are done.<br/>Crossing the flats near Edud we were axle deep at times in mud &amp; water<br/>Thursday Dec 19<br/>Today was fine &amp; we reached Gaza about 1pm. Gaza has altered considerably since we were here last. It is now a big receiving hospital. The Town has been cleaned up &amp; the cemetery has been laid out thoroughly. The Australian &amp; New Zealand Forces lost heavily at Gaza at different periods.<br/>Friday Dec 20<br/>We left Gaza about 9 AM moving</p>
