<p>Tuesday 2nd October 1917</p>
<p><u>My Wedding day</u><br/>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>Arrived 5 p.m. last night &amp; hung up, flying "Yellow" Flag only 1 c/- Dysentery on board, but we are <u>not popular</u> at present in this port for various reasons I cannot write of.</p>
<p>The bay is full of shipping Hydro planes Submarines &amp; Battleships. [The remainder of the page crossed through.]  The Italians are very frightened we may bring Cholera from Palestine. Even our drinking water on the Steamer is supposed to be Chlorinated, but the Chief Officer always shows the Medical men the <u>engine</u> Tank &amp; thus we escape &amp; the M.O.s are satisfied.</p>
