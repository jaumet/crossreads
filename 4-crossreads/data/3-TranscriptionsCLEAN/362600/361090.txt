 F 20 Th Yesterday I visited the Green Days, [indecipherable] at Alderburns - Have H- of a day. Tell a yid they are no good & find it is his cousin I'm slandering. Co. goes into every Room in Division. Thomas comes to me to work. Top comes Home. Starts Training write Jennie & M.R.L. Dream of Pats &  Doris  &  DJ's& John Caswell. 
 F 21 Fri But windy. Another hellish day. Another H of a day. I come down to my Room & read all night. Go to bed early. Major Bear the madman goes off Duty & we all get happy. 
 F 22 Sat Bit windy. Tear up paper all night to Top's pleasure. Write letters all night. Roll up pictures from Sydney [Wakly's?] send X'mas Number Sunday Times to Aunt Annie. Wrote to Marion Clarke & Maisie Carter.
