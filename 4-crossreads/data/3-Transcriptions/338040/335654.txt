<p>April 10th<br/>Our party Evacuate Baths at midday and leave for 7th P.A.Base- Dinner supplied "on route" by 6th F.A. Arrival at and departure from Base to Resling Station at le Estrade- Heavy bombardment heard during night.<br/>April 11th<br/>Report 're' German Spies in neighbourhood- letters to home etc. Evening spent in French home eggs, coffee, toast etc. songs. Self "on guard" (3hrs) against German gas attack 9 mths today since enlistment.</p>
