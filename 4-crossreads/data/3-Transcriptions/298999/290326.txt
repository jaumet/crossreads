<p>rising saddled up and went Back to camp fed up had my Breakfast cleaned up the lines and the camp also cleaned out two tents rested till 12.30 had dinner rested till 5 P.M. fed up had tea and read till 10 P.M.  turned in a good Breeze Blowing.</p>
<p>Sunday July 15.7.17<br/>turned out 5.30 A.M. fed up had a shave and wash had Breakfast had rabbit they are giving us rabbit now to eat.  cleaned up camp and rested till 12.30 had dinner wrote to Bell Josie Win Walter had tea 6 P.M.  Saddled up and went on outpost went to Budge on the sweet water canal four mile</p>
