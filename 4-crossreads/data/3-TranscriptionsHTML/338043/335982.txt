<p>Oct. 3rd<br/>Re-opening night &ndash; Small but enthusiastic audience  - Continued allied successes &ndash; Are we approaching the end faster than we anticipated? Study my parts in "The Monkey's Paw" &ndash;</p>
<p>Oct. 4th<br/>Still in Corbie &amp; feeling well satisfied &ndash; In less than three weeks I have written two sketches, two poems, one monologue and four concerted themes. (The latter for the show) &ndash;<br/>Further British &amp; French successes.</p>
