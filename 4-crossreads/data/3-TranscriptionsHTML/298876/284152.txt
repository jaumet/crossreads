<p>Saturday<br/>11/3/16</p>
<p>Very cold last night &ndash; windy &amp; sand blowing today. Went for a Route march to Matarieh &ndash; Visited the well &amp; the tree where the Virgin Mary rested &amp; drank from the well &ndash; got a few chips of a tree &ndash; a Sycamore Fig &ndash; partly alive &amp; dead &ndash; The Virgin rested here on her flight from Jerusalem into Egypt.
