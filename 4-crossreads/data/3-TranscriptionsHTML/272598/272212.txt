<p>Sunday 29.7.17<br/>Flooded out from our improvised shelter by a very heavy storm.  Last of the 6th Bgde left to-day.  All enthusiasm regarding the move died out, just taken as a matter of course.</p>
<p>Monday 30.7.17<br/>Our Brigade on the move  C Section of our unit left at 4 pm, still remained on guard.  Entrained at midnight with the remainder of the boys; travelled the distance in an open goods truck with the goods we were guarding; very cold &amp; wet.</p>
