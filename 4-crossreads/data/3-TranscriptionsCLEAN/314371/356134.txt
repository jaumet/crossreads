
Sun. 20thBeautiful day.  It is an awful pity to be shut up in Hosp.Doc. not satisfied with my head yet.
Mon. 21stThe M.O. operated on my head this morning & removed a piece of steel helmet. *
*Unfortunately lost during Ypres offensive, Sept. 1917.  J.C.L.
Tues. 11thMaking very satisfactory progress.
Wed. 23rdStill doing O.K. expect to get out in a couple of day.Another man died of wds. in the ward this morning.
Thur. 24thDischarged from Hosp. to No. 7 Con. Camp.
