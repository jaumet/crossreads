<p>view of Torbay &amp; the town is obtained. After breakfast we had to fall in at Princes's Peir Where we boarded a special tram and were taken by the Mayor and alderman for a sight seeing tour as far as Paignton.  It was teeming of rain otherwise we could have seen more than we did but we braved the rain a good deal and at 3 different places they stopped the tram and took us for a walk showing us different parts of the coast such as Brabbacombe Beach etc.  The people were delighted with the Australian "Coo-ees" which they insisted on hearing every few yards</p>
