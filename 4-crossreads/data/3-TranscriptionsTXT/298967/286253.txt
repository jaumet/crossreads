 DATE &ndash; TIME &ndash; PLACE &ndash; REMARKS 
 Sept. 2 &ndash; Anzac &ndash; Health of men very bad. 
 2 &ndash; Anzac &ndash; Reports send by M.O. to C.O. A.D.M.S. re need of rest and change of food. 
 11 &ndash; Col. Weis acting Brigadier. 
 16 &ndash; Evacuating 10 men per day dysentory. 
 17 &ndash; 117 men of 7/10 Rienfcmts arrive. 
 18 &ndash; 3rd Brigade now divisional troops.  Strength of Batt. (10th) 21 officers 575 other ranks. 
 Oct. 1 &ndash; Anzac &ndash; Bgde. still divisional troops. 
 13 &ndash; General Walker wounded. 
 20 &ndash; Major Beevor and Capt. R.B. Jacob returned from England.  12th Batt. relieved 10th in line. 
 21 &ndash; Aeroplane over. 
 Nov. 3 &ndash; 9th Batt. relieved by 1st Brigade. 
 12 &ndash; Enemy digging on Gaba Tepe. 
 14 &ndash; 10th &amp; 12th now in line.  9th &amp; 11th in reserve.  Advance parties left for Lemnos. 
 15 &ndash; Move of 9th &amp; 11th Batts. postponed.  Weather too rough. 
 16 &ndash; 21.00 &ndash; 9th &amp; 11th embarked for Mudros. 
 17 &ndash; 10th &amp; 12th in reserve. 
 19 &ndash; Beachy Bill very active (96 hrs.). 
 21 &ndash; Mudros &ndash; 10th Batt. embarked for Mudros.
