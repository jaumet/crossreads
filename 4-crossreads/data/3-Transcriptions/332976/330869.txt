<p>June 1917<br/>5th.   route march of about 7 miles.  I went out with Lt. Oliver to make a survey of the trenches &amp; barbed wire entanglements between Fremicourt and Biefuillers &amp; returned about 2 P.M.<br/>The day was very hot with a temperature of about 80&deg;.<br/>The concert is still in progress but the "Verey Light" changed places with<br/>"The Goods" for the night.  ("The Goods" have a show up near Arras somewhere, &amp; were a great success tonight)<br/>6th   This morning the company went out on barbed wiring in front of the trenches at Biefuillers &amp; returned at 2 P.M.
