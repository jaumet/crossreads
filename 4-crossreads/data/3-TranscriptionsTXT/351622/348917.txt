 Delville Wood instantly lighting fire in the side wound. Broke me up experience of the past. 
 4 November 1916 Sat Met Norman Burgess again &amp; Arthur Davis at night. Hundreds more sick &amp; wounded pass through mud &amp; clothes. Young [Wurik] passes through suffering if I remember with trench feet. Terrible news of my great pals death Norman Sanderson killed in [text continued at top of page] 
 [Note in margin] They are both dead now 
 5 November 1916 Sun Busy as usual. The train brings load of wounded down. Several injections some 40 odd since 4 oclock. Bell pulls up a bottle of rum for me which Dick &amp; myself consumed before retiring for the night. Very cold.
