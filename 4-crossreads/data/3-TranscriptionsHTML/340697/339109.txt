<p>Entrained for Bolougne at 0500 this morning and arrived there <strike>this morning</strike> at dinner time. We were taken on the cross channel mail steamer Victor at about 5.p.m. and arrived at Folkestone at 7 p.m. there we entrained and proceeded to London.
