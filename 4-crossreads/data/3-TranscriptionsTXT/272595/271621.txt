 Very hot &amp; cloudy this afternoon looks like a change setting in.  Had a good hot bath &amp; a proper clean up this afternoon then went for a walk round this quiet little village of Harefield, one can scarce believe that's its only 20 minutes run by train from London, in the evening I wrote a few letters &amp; in a few minutes I am going round to the pictures which they show nightly here. 6th.
