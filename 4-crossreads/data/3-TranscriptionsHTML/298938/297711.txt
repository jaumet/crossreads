<p>know,  it is worrying.   I almost wish she would come over here &amp; marry me.  It would <strike></strike>be better I think.   Anyhow letters should come soon.<br/>This week is the anniversary week of my trip from Alexandria to the Peninsula.<br/>Shall I be here or in France, or back in Australia this time next year?<br/>Either of the last two I imagine.<br/>Have heard from Dad &amp; Lily Vi &amp; Ernest &amp; Frances, also from Rushbrooke, who simply sent the card with "absolved" written on it.<br/>A short way of forgiving me for not calling on him.</p>
