<p>Zonnebeke<br/>Friday 2<br/>Batt go into the line today, releiving the 7th Brigade, front trench a water hole<br/>Saturday 3<br/>RUM marvellous stuff has soothing as an old time melody where there's danger about Doing a lot of buisness<br/>Sunday 4<br/>Rations to Garter Point heavy shelling, my old steed blown over with concussion, self half mad with fear, we got through with one casualty George OLeary had a leg broken</p>
