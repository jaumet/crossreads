 were 3 Turkish Spies in our position, so late at night they got about 1000 Camels &amp; tied Baulks of Timber to them &amp; swept the loose sand clear &amp; then waited.  Sure enough the morning showed the foot prints of these spies &amp; they were eventually caught. The Gurkhas are Constantly going out &amp; bringing in Turkish prisoners.  How they manage to catch them is a big mystery to everyone. One day a party of 80 Turks suddenly appeared at an undefended portion of the defence works &amp; after having a careful look round returned, unfortunately for them some Gurkhas &amp; Sikhs sighted them And -  They did not bring back any prisoners  
 On all the trips of these trains you always see a couple of trucks loaded with steel rails out in front of the Engine.  This is to deaden the effect of a possible charge of Dynamite being placed there. 
 Some facts which the Sydney people do not know &ndash; That the Persic was carrying 5000 Machine Guns &amp; their ammunition when sunk. That another Boat sunk was carrying a full cargo of Water pipes for the Canal defences. That another Boat was ordered to hand over a certain sum
