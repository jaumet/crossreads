<p>Friday 19th<br/>Troop went out on troop drill &amp; firing exercises.  I was put on Regtl Q M's fatigue drawing fodder &amp; firewood.  Took horses up the town about 2 miles for exercise.  Was put on stable picket at night.  Had third shift.</p>
