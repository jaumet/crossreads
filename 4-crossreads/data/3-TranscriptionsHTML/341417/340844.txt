<p>[Gunner, then Bombardier and later Corporal, Oswald Henry  McIntyre, No 8976, a station overseer of Telegraph Point, NSW, joined the Army on 14 September 1915 aged 33, and embarked from Melbourne, Victoria, on HMAT A39 Port Macquarie on 16 November 1915 with the 2nd Divisional Ammunition Column. He served in Egypt and France, returning to Australia on 15 May 1919.]<br/>[Envelope]<br/>[McIntyre O.H.]</p>
<p>O. A. S.</p>
<p>Mrs H. H. Parker<br/>Bridge Street East<br/>Port Macquarie<br/>New South Wales<br/>Australia</p>
