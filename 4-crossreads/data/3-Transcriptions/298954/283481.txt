<p>Fri. 15th June.<br/>Then "Slim" claimed The Chair as his! and told the Old Man his (Slim's) opinion of him.</p>
<p>Uproar!  The O.M. hurriedly despatched a sentry for Mines, &amp;, fearing mutiny at least, "Willie, The Beautiful Hun" arrived on the scene.</p>
<p>"Slim" volubly presented his grievances but his volubility received short shrift from "Willie".</p>
<p>He let out a flood of Prussian cursing in a shrieking tenor (or a tenor shriek), threatened him with "Strafe" &amp; left.  And all was once more Calm.  But the Oldest Inhabitants left off playing Bridge sufficiently long to say that they had had the best entertainment since their capture.</p>
