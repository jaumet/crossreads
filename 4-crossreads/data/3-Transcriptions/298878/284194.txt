The trade winds blowing along behind helping us do a good 13 knots.  Having completely recovered from sea sickness I am now enjoying the trip to the full as we are travelling in company with other ships, it is not necessary to work continuous watch with the wireless, the 24 hours being divided among the fleet, each ship of which carries two operators who work 3 hours per day; consequently I have the pleasure of spending most of my time on the deck.<br/><u>Wednesday 17th Feb.</u>  The sea, this past few days</p>
