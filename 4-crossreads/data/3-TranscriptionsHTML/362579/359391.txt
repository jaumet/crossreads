<p>Yesterday afternoon gave tea   &amp;  presented Miss Wilson with a beautiful L:amp. The Medical Officers were invited but I could only stay a few minutes, was on duty, so had to get back to work.</p>
<p>We are still very quiet   &amp;  very little to so but I'm enjoying the rest.</p>
<p>My Wards are very nice   &amp;  the orderlies good boys shall become quite young again, especially after all the worry.</p>
<p>I'm thinking seriously of applying for Transport</p>
