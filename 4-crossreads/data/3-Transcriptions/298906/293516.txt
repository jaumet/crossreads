<p>&amp; here we had a good old fashioned spread in a private house, after this we walked back through Durrington again &amp; so home, &amp; soon I will be in bed for I am very tired but quite satisfied.<br/>Saw some of the women farm workers this afternoon, at a distance you have a job to tell them from a soldier, for they are dressed something alike, at any rate they look very smart<br/>30th. Very few on parade this morning &amp; all the N.C.O.s had to get rifles &amp; do musketry, this hurts I can tell you for there is nothing we hate worse than this form of drill, it is so uninteresting, so we all think<br/>Looks very much like rain today close &amp; overcast.</p>
