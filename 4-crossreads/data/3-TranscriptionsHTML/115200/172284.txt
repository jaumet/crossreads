<p>71&nbsp; 20.12.15</p>
<p>This place is Some thing awful&nbsp; You can go no where here like you can in Egypt&nbsp; Valletta [wiich?] is the main town our the island and when you go over there you can only go to the pictures and they are not to good&nbsp; The sooner I get out of this place the Better I will like it&nbsp; I hope to be in Egypt Next week.</p>
