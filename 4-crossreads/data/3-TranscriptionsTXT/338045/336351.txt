 Nov 12th &ndash; Sunday 
 Fine day. Out as usual 6.30 am &amp; attend Mass in rec hall. Have a Pass &amp; with Fry take a walk around Orpington. Pretty little place. Green fields &amp; nice residences. Many poultry farms, especially Cook's, the home of famous Buff Orpingtons. 
 Meet two young ladies of the district (my friend Miss Schwerer) &amp; spend a pleasant evening though cold, have an invitation to tea next Saturday evg. Arrive home tired. 
 Nov 13th Monday 
 Rather tired &amp; again raining, spend the day in bed. Fine concert by London Party in theatre
