
F [19/8/17 Sun] We go nowhere except for a walk to the "Rowler". Have a serious talk & I get my deserts. On Monday A & M climb up Bank after uncle
F [20/8/17 Mon] We Bike down to the Beach with our dinner. We have trouble over the Tea. M. tears Stock[ing] & gets all over Jam A & M come down & we go along beach. We have a good Talk at Home & [Indecipherable]. Thinks [Indecipherable] SW&A. Thinks Sleep
F [21/8/17 Tues] Wet as we arrive in L.pool miss 2pm Train by 2 minutes. Go to Art Gallery & go into room with murals & see all nude women. Arr. Sbury 2.30. Leave S'bury 2.45. arr. Bulf 3.55am. Get 1 1/2 hours sleep
F [22/8/17 Wed] Had letters from Jack P. Auntie Annie Roy. Mem. Nette. Jennie. Wrote M.R.L. (2 lett). P.C. Home of Con & I & one to Con. Go to Pictures with Dave & E. goes to S'bury.
W [23/8/17 Thurs] D & E go to S'land [Scotland] by the 10a.m. Played Cricket & got 8 N.O. We won by 30 runs. Very windy To-day. Wrote Home (8) & to Auntie & M.R.L.
W & Windy [24/8/17 Fri] Recd letters from Home (40) with wattle inside. Nette. Marg. Bess (2) Mrs White. Miss Robson Nell. M.R.L. papers from Dad & Mem. Wrote [Indecipherable] 4 lge F'cap 4 of Red. Cross. Paper. Cox & I develop films in one LAV.
F [25/8/17 Sat] I am ord. Corp Print a dozen Photos in morn. Letter came back from Auntie sent by Ellis. Wrote MRL. sent 9 Photos. Wrote Jennie. M.Voland. Roy. Jack Preddey & Doris. Nice day bit windy. Wrote note to Con & sent Photos to be printed. Lent CR the Bike. Dream of all that I wrote to
