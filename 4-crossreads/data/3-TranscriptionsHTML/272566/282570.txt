<p>Saturday 5 May</p>
<p>Beautiful Day.<br/>Still on incinerator.<br/>In the afternoon we were watching an Aust. Rules Football between the 26th A.S.C. and the 15th F. Amb.  We are due to play the 15th tomorrow.<br/>Went to pictures in Albert tonight.<br/>Orders out late tonight telling all bearers to be ready for action at a moments notice.  The 15th F. Amb. have gone out in cars.</p>
