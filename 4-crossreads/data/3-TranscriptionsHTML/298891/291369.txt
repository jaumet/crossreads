<p>1917 October</p>
<p>13 Saturday</p>
<p>Heavy rain at intervals.  Allowed to leave sick tent &amp; go to my own &amp; to mess tent for meals.  Sisters Davis &amp; Maxwell took me for a little walk in late afternoon to try my stiff leg.  Pelting night.</p>
