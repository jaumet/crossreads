<p>[Oct.-Nov., 1916.]<br/>Monday 30<br/>Midnight in an officers mess they are all drunk and yet we privates are crimed for being so<br/>Tuesday 31<br/>A drop of spirits is all we have to make our future cheerful but we never get any, at least very seldom<br/>Wednesday Nov 1<br/>Drunk as a fool<br/>Thursday 2<br/>Sleep and forget the day will come<br/>P.R.I 2 ARMY</p>
