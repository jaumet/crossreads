Returned to Bernafay Wood by the new duckboard track passing under one side of Trones Wood.  Camped in Bell tent.  Drizzling rain.11th December (Monday)No duty today.  Reinforcements arrived for the 14th F. Amb.  Raining and cold.  Good coke fire going in our tent.
