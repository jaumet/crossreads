<p>The cry of stand to was frequent, although in many cases a false alarm.</p>
<p>As regards the direct area affected by us there was plenty of fighting going on around, things on the whole however were quiet.</p>
<p>On the right what we took for attack by (our own) the Germans turned out to be our own men who were going back to a better position to escape from the flammenwefer [flamethrower] which was being used by the enemy in their efforts to approach the trenches occupied</p>
