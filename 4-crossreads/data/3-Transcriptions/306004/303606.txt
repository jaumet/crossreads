<p>22.11.16<br/>which means sigs on the hill all night with a lamp This makes the duties on the Sigs pretty hard; Lieut Smith paid us a visit today; We are leaving here the day after tomorrow &amp; anything going by train goes tomorrow; 17,000 French Calvary have landed in Egypt &amp; I believe they are to go Mecca way. Major Smith arrived back &amp; took charge of C. Squadron the day before yesterday  Enemy planes are never seen in this part of the world now, &amp; there is no doubt we have the supremacy of the air; Busy tonight packing up our gear ready to load on the Camels at 6.30 AM; R.S.M. Frost came here today with 38 reinforcements, we were all surprised to see him. his arm completely cured</p>
