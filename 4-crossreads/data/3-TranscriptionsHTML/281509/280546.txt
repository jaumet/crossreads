<p>1916 November 19<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Rained all night, not so cold  yet bad enough.  B &amp; self go into Albert 2 &frac12; miles, in aft.. there being no parade.  Poke round, wrecked railway station cathedral etc.  Buy souvenir salt spoons. Band, drum &amp; fife in courtyard. Tommies give us tea &amp; we have tin pineapple &amp; condensed milk.  Home &amp; bed. <br/>We get leave to Armiens tomorrow</p>
