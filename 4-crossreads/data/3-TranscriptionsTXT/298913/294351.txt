After dear Sister Morten sat at the piano &amp; gave us some music. The work of these girls must be very tiresome, yet they never complain, &amp; work from daylight till 9pm. Nothing is too much trouble, &amp; they do not  forget . In fact they have a nasty habit of remembering when the next dose of medicine is due, &amp; they see that it goes down. They are as strong as young horses too, so Mac found out. He argued the point one morning over going to bed when sister Whiteman simply carried him there &amp; dumped him in.
