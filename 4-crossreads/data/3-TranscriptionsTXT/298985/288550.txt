Wednesday 11thMoved of at 9.15 & marched through Perone & one or two other smaller places ariveing no where in particular at 12 only had 2 short spells. It was raining slightly most of the time rather an improvement than otherwise. Plenty of iron to be had at close where a dressing station Hospital had been burnt. Made quite a good one home
Thursday 12thMoved of about 8 went about 3 miles
