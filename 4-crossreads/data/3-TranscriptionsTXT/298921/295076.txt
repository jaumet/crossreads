 April, 1915 Tuesday 13 Sister Myra Nyse had relapse &amp; has to be operated on again 10 weeks in bed. bad luck. Very Hot weather now &amp; all hands impatient at being kept in Egypt. Am posting Prints of Karnak Luxor, Camp life etc. and if Censor destroys them. I still have to Negatives &ndash; Have not seen Tommy Redford yet.
