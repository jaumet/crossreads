<p>Sat 28th:  Moved forward 6 miles &amp; bivouacked for the night.<br/><strike>Sun 29th</strike>:<br/>Tues 1st Oct:  Moved forward to Hargicourt. At 6 p.m. moved off again &amp; relieved 32nd in supports at Joncourt.<br/>Thurs 3rd:  Heavily gas-shelled all night. Moved out to "hop-off" tape in front Joncourt at 4 a.m. "Hopped-off" at 6.5 a.m. advanced &amp; took Wiancourt. Held up on other side. [indecipherable] on awaiting reinforcements.  Evacuated gassed.<br/>Sat 5th:  Admitted to 1st  Aust. Gen Hosp Rouen. Gas.</p>
