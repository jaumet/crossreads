<p>1915<br/>June 1 Tue.<br/>We have just received <strike>spec</strike> by the Kyarra, special reinforcements consisting of 32 men.<br/>An officer &amp; 3 more men who were away on transports, reported back to-day looking fit &amp; well.<br/>Lemnos Island appears to be a strong advanced base for the Med. Exped. Force.  There are several hospitals there including 1st &amp; 2nd Stationary Hospitals which came over with us on Kyarra &ndash; lucky dogs.<br/>Received some letters to-day posted in Sydney in January. Wherever have they been?</p>
