<p>threw back dates in exchange.  I might tell you more often they exchange bombs.</p>
<p>In my last letter from home, Flo told me that my brother, Eug, had been accepted and is coming away with the A.M.C.  I should not be at all surprised to hear soon that you have enlisted.  I firmly believe that we need more men that Australia can supply to chase Jacky off this ground.</p>
<p>I also learned that <strike>you</strike> they gave him an evening at home on the 28th Aug, but they didn't tell me you were there; yet I presume you were among the merry party.</p>
<p>Well Charley old chap I shall have to close now as our letters have to be as brief as possible.  I will write again soon and I'll look forward for another humerous letter from you too very soon.  Give Minnie my love &amp; ask her to write.</p>
<p>With best wishes from<br/>Yours Sincerely<br/>Walter.</p>
