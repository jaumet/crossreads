 II last, and altho' bearable in fine weather, the country is so shell torn that a drop of rain turns it into a veritable quagmire &ndash; Nothing very much doing here at present tho' we are busy preparing for the Huns threatened effort &ndash; May he attempt it is our fervent wish, as we are convinced he will get the biggest "slathering" of the war - Had a field service P.C. from Athol yesterday, with a reminder I hadn't written him &ndash; so I dashed off a note &ndash; Peculiar I have never run across him, but expect I shall do so when we next get out
