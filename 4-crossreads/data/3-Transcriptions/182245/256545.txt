<p>1917 JANUARY</p>
<p>30 TUESDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; Sunny, with falls of snow</p>
<p>at different times. It snowed</p>
<p>pretty heavily in evening. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness, ect in morning and</p>
<p>exercising horses in afternoon</p>
<p>We sent one team up with</p>
<p>ammunition. I was on</p>
<p>horse picket at night.</p>
