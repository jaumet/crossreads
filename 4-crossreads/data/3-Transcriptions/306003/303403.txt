<p>22-6-16<br/>fallen machine &amp; at last dropped us a message saying she had failed to locate the machine, &amp; then headed due west for home;  We at once commenced to signal her message to Bde H.Q. but were only half way through it when the plane returned &amp; flying within 20 feet of the ground dropped a bag of tools with a note in it saying he had located the machine to the west of us &amp; to watch for his smoke bomb signals.  we cancelled the message to Bde  &amp; sent another, that the machine was located &amp; watched the aviator drop the smoke bombs above where the wrecked plane was, &amp; then started off to find her.</p>
<p>the sun was now disappearing &amp; we thought she was closer than she was, &amp; after travelling 4 miles the Brigadier (Meredith) caught up to us, &amp; as it was dusk &amp; very rough country the Brig. said the search would have to be postponed &amp; to make for Debabis, the Brig then made back &amp; the advance party were called in &amp; we found they had just come up to the plane &amp; discovered two Bedouin chiefs with it they were taken prisoners &amp; we could do nothing</p>
