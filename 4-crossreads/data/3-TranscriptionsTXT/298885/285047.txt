
Thur. Feb. 10th -16Made permanent brakesman to C.I. team.
Fri. Feb. 11th -16Accompanied C.I. team to Abbehsiah, Waila, and Slave markets for break.
Sat. Feb. 12th -16First lesson at riding school.  "Some Circus".
Sun. Feb. 13th -16Church Service 7.45 A.M.  Received first mail since leaving Fremantle and feel quite excited.
Mon. Feb. 14th -16Inspection of D.A.C. by Colonel Hobbs.  Weather very cold and windy with clouds of dust.
Tues. Feb. 15th -16Received first instruction
