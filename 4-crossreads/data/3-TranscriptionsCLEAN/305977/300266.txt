 Wednesday 15th It began to rain at 3 am & kept up fairly steady till 7 am.  Had a fairly easy day.  Things were very quiet.  Drew 3rd shift at night. 
 Thursday 16th Received letters from Carrie & mother also a packet containing socks & chocolate.  Got leave to go round to the left.  Looked up Capt Edgley of the 19th Battn.  He was very pleased to see me & we discussed old times & Dorrigo identities for some time.  He was looking well & carries the same old happy smile.
