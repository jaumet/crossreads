<p>9 September 1916<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Do nothing much in duty line today. Half holiday  Cricket match between Aust. &amp; Canada former wins. A great pleasure &amp; surprise today. 3 big birthday parcels arrive from Home, Macs &amp; Robbins  Such a grand lot of dainties &amp; things useful.  Very popular today</p>
