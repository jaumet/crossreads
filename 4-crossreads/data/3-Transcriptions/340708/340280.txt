<p>August 1917<br/>Monday 27th<br/>Still wet &amp; rough.<br/>Tuesday 28th<br/>It cleared up for a bit this morning but came on worse this afternoon.<br/>I am getting better now   I don't think I will have to go under an operation after all<br/>Thursday 30th<br/>Weather still rough.<br/>Friday 31<br/>Fritz paid us the first visit for a week owing to bad weather.<br/>Saturday Sept. 1st<br/>I am pretty right again did a bit of work the first for 3 weeks<br/>Sunday 2nd<br/>Shifted back to Hazebrouck this morning &amp;amp then on to Wiskque this afternoon they are building it is well out in the country so we should be safe from airraids.</p>
