<p>1915<br/>Aug. 19 Thu.<br/>Went into Cairo this evening after tea. R. Smith has commenced to fag Italian &amp; wanted some text books.<br/>I ascertained to-night that there are several thousand <u>Spanish</u> people in Cairo. What a cosmopolitan crowd there is in Egypt.</p>
