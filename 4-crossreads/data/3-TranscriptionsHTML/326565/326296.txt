<p>Sunday 30/1/16<br/>Church parade conducted by Capt Cranston, (Chaplin 29th Btn)<br/>Caught ration boat &amp; again went to Ismailia, about 11 miles distance up Canal.  Very pleasant trip; Ismailia a very pretty place.  Caught train leaving Ismailia about 10 pm, arrived back in camp 12 pm.</p>
<p>Monday 31/1/16<br/>Water fatigue.  Interesting lecture by Capt Donald on cause &amp; prevention of diseases prevalent in tropical countries.</p>
