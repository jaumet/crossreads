<p>Friday 6 April 1917.</p>
<p><u>Never dreamt&nbsp;of ever seeing this Good Friday 1917 in Australia.</u></p>
<p>Drilled &amp; a march of 14 mls in the hills. Easy afternoon.</p>
<p>Got ready for Er [Easter] leave at 3.30 pm.</p>
<p>Marched into Seymour in rain.</p>
<p>Entrained 7 pm. Arrived City 9.30 pm.</p>
<p>Spent night at Burnley (74).</p>
