<p>March, 1916<br/>Monday 6<br/>First thing we had to sort the mob out, officers, then N.C.O's. &amp; then all the men in their various units<br/>I got another class of N.C.O's. &amp; my own gun.<br/>Several units brought Vickers guns about 6 in all.</p>
<p>Tuesday 7<br/>Carry on the good work having a fairly good mob including a Cpl from the Royal Flying Corp.<br/>Going to the store tonight I met another one of my old Platoon now a Sergt in the present 13th Btn.<br/>We are all wearing shorts again  days very hot.</p>
