<p>8 May 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Scramble for breakfast. Assist to put up hospital tents<br/>Went up to see Dave Watts to Light Horse Camp. Indian wigwams.  Yarn &amp; restaurant.  Get a ground-sheet.<br/>We get definite news that we leave camp for Alexandria en route for France tomorrow.</p>
