<p>May 2nd &ndash; 19<br/>Word has come through that all 1915 men are to proceed to London tomorrow to take part in a march of 12000 Colonial troops through the city.</p>
<p>Some two hours later we were told that we would also move to another Camp in 3 days time to join our earlier Quota (16th) and will leave Devonport on 12th Inst. On troopship "Soudan"</p>
