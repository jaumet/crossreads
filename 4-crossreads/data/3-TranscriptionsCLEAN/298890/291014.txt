  
 21. Sunday. Showery day again. Went with A.A. to hear Alfred E. Garvie. M.A. D.D. in morning. In afternoon Bambacionis, Yusif Ali & Mrs Aldridge came to tea. Couldn't endure cold. 
 22. Monday. Grey showery day again. Worked all day at Minerva home in pouring rain very tired. F.P. rang up twice but I didn't get him. 
 23. Tuesday. Rain very heavy. Got wet & suffered horribly. Went to bed after getting home from Minerva. F.P. rang up &  had a talk. 
 24. Wednesday. Heavy showers. Went with B. Smith to New Transport Company & home with Mr Gattie for tea. Terribly depressed & in pain.
