<p>in Europe also our local front &amp; great interest is displayed as to the fate of Theipville which we are <u>trying</u> to capture also Moquet farm both of which are almost inpregnable strongholds.</p>
<p>All sorts of roumers (or Furphies as we call them) come in one being a great navel battle.</p>
<p>Thur. Aug 31st<br/>Today we are releived by the Canadian Field Amb and are preparing to take our departure.</p>
<p>The bearers returned from the trenches to day who looked war warn &amp; weary and showed evidences of hardship &amp; hard work, they had carries of a mile &amp; a half as stretches from the <strike>dressing</strike> Residential aid post to the first aid post &amp; worked in 24 hour shifts, being under shell fire all the time. Four of them had been wounded on this occasion one died. There were 3 shellshock.</p>
