<p>Charles Hargreaves was born on Sept 7th 1886 in "Westbrook Cottage" Ilkley, Yorkshire &ndash; sixth &amp; youngest son of Charles &amp; Ellen Hargreaves.</p>
<p>he came to Australia in 1911 &amp; spent the first twelve months working on "Mimosa Station", Temora, N.S.W. From there he went to Tasmania &amp; together with a friend bought an apple orchard in the Tamar Valley.</p>
<p>In need of more money Charles Hargreaves went to Melbourne, but decided to seek work in Sydney &amp; so began with Messrs John Sands Ltd. printers as he had worked in Yorkshire &amp; London with Messrs Percy Lund Humphries , printers, friends of his parents.</p>
