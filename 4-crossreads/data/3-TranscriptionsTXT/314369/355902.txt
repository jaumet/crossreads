 10 October 1915 Sunday 19th After Trinity 
 Great joy I get a packet of letters  &amp;  parcel of cigarettes after nearly three months of waiting Church services at 10am Go for a tour round Helouan with a couple of old friends  &amp;  Dad. See dad off  &amp;  then go to church at 7pm 
 1915 October 11 Monday 
 Very warm   &amp;  sultry during the morning but cool wind from the Nile in evening Pictures at night
