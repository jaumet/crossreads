
<p>1916 &nbsp; WEDNESDAY 23 &nbsp; FEB.</p>
<p>Had a good few storms,&nbsp;showers. In the tent most of the morning &amp; we turned out for parade in full drill order in afternoon. We paraded down on the flat and had a cinemetograph picture taken of us. We made a rotten exibition of ourselves. Came in to Lisheen for the evening</p>
