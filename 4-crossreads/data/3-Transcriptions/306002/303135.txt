<p>9.5.15<br/>Sun. 9th.  At 11.30 last night we fell in at the Camp &amp; gave Col. Meredith three cheers as he is staying behind.  The Col. gave a bit of a speech &amp; said he was very sorry he was not going with us, but he knew we would do our job.  We marched through Heliopolis with our Infantry pack on singing &amp; shouting a treat.
