<p>Wed. 17th Mar</p>
<p>Roll Call 6.30. Water &amp; Feed Bkfst 7.45 abt 9 Saddle up Drill order About to file out when ordered tie up horses again &amp; clean up tents &amp; place all kits outside. This done after much dressing &amp; so on.</p>
<p>Rode out. Some scouting etc On return rearguard action. Officers brought out of walletts, bottles of beer biscuits etc &amp; had a feed in front</p>
