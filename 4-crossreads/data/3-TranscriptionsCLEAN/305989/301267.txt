  
 Sunday 29 July 1917 Stunt across Wadi got home about 3.30, watered up 6.30.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic number for 7]. 
 Monday 30 July 1917 Quiet day:  played 2nd Reg. got beaten:  very hot & dusty. 
 Tuesday 31 July 1917 Very hot;  on mess orderly.  Out grazing all day. 
 Wednesday 1 August 1917 Out grazing all day.  On Fatigue. 
 Thursday 2 August 1917 Out Grazing all day, pretty hot. 
 Friday 3 August 1917 On Pump fatigue, washing parade in Wadi:  (picquet at night).  Reached the dignified age of 24 precisely, Clarrie home for R.G. 
 Saturday 4 August 1917 Nothing doing, on Q.M. fatigue.
