<p>2<br/>feeling more yourself again dear. I am going to try &amp; look up Mrs Norris &amp; Nancy on my return to London.<br/>Did I tell you I somehow lost the photos of you &amp; the dear Kids? If you can I wish you would let me have some more. &ndash; How pretty it is down here &ndash; even in spite of the rain. How are Will and the children.<br/>I hope the next year will be brighter for you all old girl &amp; that we will all be together once more. My ribbon is rather pretty &amp; was presented to me by General Birdwood last</p>
