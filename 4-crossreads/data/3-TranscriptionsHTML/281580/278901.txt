Our discussions hasten along the otherwise monotonous hours.<br/>4th <br/>We sighted          island just after breakfast, and being in an extremely bad zone for submarines, the ships came closer together and the destroyers actively scouted. We received a wireless S.O.S. of a vessel being torpedoed only five miles off and one the other evening further to the North. A homeward bound convoy passed us during the evening; the going and coming fleets making a magnificent and imposing impression.<br/>5th Decr /17</p>
