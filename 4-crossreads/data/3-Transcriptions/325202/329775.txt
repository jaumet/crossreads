<p>Five of us one evening went into the village chemists shop to make a few purchases &ndash; I had to act as interpreter We were evidently the first Australians the old chap had seen &amp; he persisted in our going into his house behind the shop &amp; being introduced to his wife &amp; daughter &amp; drinking a glass of wine with him.</p>
<p>Strazeele April 17<br/>It is a week yesterday since we arrived here by road from Abbeville and our Company is billetted on 3 neighbouring farm houses about &frac34; mile from the little village. Since</p>
