  
 8 March 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out) X Bethania see St John  St Luke chap XXIV X Field Officer today at above named place- Dead Turks, & shells abandoned by them are plentiful. X Strange to relate West, poor Bluey Liddle's man is here in my Regiment and a good man he is too.  Can you read this "BLUE" LIDDLE. X Moved on through very rough country to  Rummon  bivouaced at midnight & moved again at 2 AM escorting a Mountain Battery- to  Taiyebeh
