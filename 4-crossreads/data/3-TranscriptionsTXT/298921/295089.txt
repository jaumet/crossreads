 April, 1915 Monday 26 We leave tonight at 7 pm for a night March to Maidi. Bivouac there, &amp; get back to Heliopolis tomorrow, my orderly is going to ride one of the 2nd Brigade's Buckjumpers at Maidi in the morning &ndash; This trek has been a most pleasant change does the men good - Fleas very plentiful in the sand now.
