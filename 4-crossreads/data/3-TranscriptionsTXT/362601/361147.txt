 21 January, Tuesday 
 W &amp; F We start the Mess &amp; do real well. I meet Norm Gibson again. Use Nette's sugar for Breakfast. I pack up for Bulford. Go over to mess for a while &amp; then off to bed early as I am very tired. 
 22 January, Wednesday 
 F We set out by Car tont suite at 9.30 am for Bulford. Report &amp; begin duties at once. Things in an awful mess. We set our room up &amp; go up to Top's room for a while. We break the stove &amp; chimney falls on my bed &amp; makes awful mess. We go to bed early thoroughly done up.
