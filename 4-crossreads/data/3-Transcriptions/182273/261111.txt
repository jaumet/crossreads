
<p>1918 &nbsp;&nbsp;March</p>
<p>14 THUR &nbsp;Arr. London. 5.30. crossed to</p>
<p>Victoria St. Met Sgt. Dowe. Left</p>
<p>8 am. arr Dover 10.30 had</p>
<p>dinner sailed 2.30 PM arr Calais</p>
<p>4.30 &amp; marched to No 6 Rest Camp</p>
<p>tea &amp; issue of Blankets. turned</p>
<p>in early.</p>
<p>15 FRI &nbsp;Reveille 6.am. Bft 6.30 moved</p>
<p>off 7.30 am issued with dinner &amp; tea.</p>
<p>Left Calais 4 am arr Ballieul 1.15 pm.</p>
<p>Went out to Au Progr&egrave;s had bon time.</p>
<p>Posted PC<sup>&#39;s</sup>&nbsp;to Amy, Celia May Lizzie Amy Mc</p>
<p>&amp; Capt Mooney.</p>
<p>16 SAT &nbsp;Left&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">Au Progr</span><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">&egrave;s 10.45. am.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">arrived Battery 2.30 PM. Glorious</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">day. Guns very active on</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">both sides. Many new</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">arrivals at Battery. Fixing</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">things up. Rec. letters</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">from Amy &amp; photos. &amp; Tense</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">Gladys C &amp; photos, Else Parker&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">&amp; card from Julie &amp; P.C.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 1.6;">Billy Williams from England.</span></p>
