<p>June 1915<br/>28 Mon<br/>On Popes</p>
<p>29 Tuesday<br/>Bourke and Monaghan killed. Turks rushed down gully after reverse on Walkers Top &ndash; about 200 &ndash; 250 of them. Broadbent carried 1 gun to left and we drove them back easily with many casualties about 10.30 Pm on.<br/>4 wounded. Nash of 2nd regt K.</p>
