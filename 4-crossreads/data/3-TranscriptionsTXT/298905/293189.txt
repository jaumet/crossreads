
We reached home about 6 oclock after a most enjoyable ride of about 41/2 hours during which time we covered a lot of ground, & the scenery I shall never forget.12th. Left Littlehampton this morning after a most enjoyable stay, since I have been down there I have done nothing but run about in cars, visiting people, & eating off silver plate of the finest quality for Mr Duke is one of the heads of Littlehampton & he has taken me round to all  the principal people of the town & they have treated me splendidly I must say the English people I have come in contact with are splendid & thorough gentlemen, & the girls & ladies are tip-top, I have been treated almost to well.
