<p>Sunday 11 February</p>
<p>After breakfast started out for a few bus rides, walked round Buckingham Palace &ndash; Whitehall, Hyde Park and other notable places.  In front of Buckingham Palace is the famous statue of Queen Victoria.<br/>Trafalgar Square decorated with notices in connection with The Victory Loan.</p>
