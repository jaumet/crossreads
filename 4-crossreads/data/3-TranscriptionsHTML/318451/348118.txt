<p>Friday<br/>Went to Bailleul.  It is about 10 kilometres form here &amp; for 8 of that distance the road runs through a beautiful avenue of trees.  The Town is like other French towns &amp; does not impress me much.  It was here where we disentrained on our first halt in France.  Everywhere there are signs of a tremendous movement in the near future.  For weeks past huge guns have been coming in day after day</p>
