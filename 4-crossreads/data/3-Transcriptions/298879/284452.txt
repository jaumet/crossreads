<p>October 1915<br/>Friday 15<br/>Breezy still. Batt. on a route march. N.Z. band playing in Y.M.C.A. tent again</p>
<p>Saturday 16<br/>Dull.    Flag waving again. We took the lamps out last night for a little practice. The Div. canteen is in full swing. They have been tremendously busy since opening.</p>
