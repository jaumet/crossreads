Monday 8.5.16Continued fatigues which are anything but pleasant in the heat of the day.  Engineers commenced to build another mess shed in connection with this camp.New officer not very popular among the boys.  Paid 150 piastres  1-10-9;                                   do not expect many more pays in Egyptian money.Six months to-day since we left home.  Piquet to night to celebrate the occasion.  Leave for Cairo starts again to morrow
Wednesday 10/5/16Relieved from Piquet duty at 6 pm,
