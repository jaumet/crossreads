 This camp is said to be able to accommodate 300,000 men. There are supposed to be 12,000 Australians here &amp; there is a rumor that we will only be here 7 weeks.  On the wall of our hut has been written by one of the 2/8 Leeds Rifles "Good luck to whoever comes here" we are off at last after 5 months of hell here" Our beds consist of two tressels 6? high and 3 planks a mattress and 3 blankets.
