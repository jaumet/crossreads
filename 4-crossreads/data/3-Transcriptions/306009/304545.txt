<p>1915<br/>Dec. 29 Wed.<br/>1st Aust.Army Pay Corps Pierrots gave a Concert from 9.30 p.m. to 12.30 a.m. (30th inst.) in the Britannia Theatre, Cairo. Went with Sister Stone. Jolly good programme.<br/>Nights are very cold now notwithstanding 4 blankets &amp; greatcoat.</p>
