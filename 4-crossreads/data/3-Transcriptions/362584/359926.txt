<p>France</p>
<p>14.6.1916</p>
<p>Dear Mrs Ferguson</p>
<p>I have just this minute heard the sad news. One of our men went to see your son &amp; was there when it happened.</p>
<p>Nothing has upset me so much since I left Sydney.</p>
<p>We were all very fond of him &amp; I had rather it had been anyone rather than he.</p>
<p>When we were coming over from Egypt I was on the same boat as your son, &amp; as he was the senior Infantry officer on the boat, he was our O.C. We all got on so well together, &amp; could not have wished for a better man to command us.</p>
