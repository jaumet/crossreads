
<p>322</p>
<p>1917 MARCH</p>
<p>29 THURSDAY</p>
<p>Raining &amp; blowing all morning</p>
<p>&amp; very cold, but cleared up in</p>
<p>afternoon. We all turned</p>
<p>out about 9.30 AM in the rain</p>
<p>&amp; waited on the road till</p>
<p>11 AM &amp; then marched about</p>
<p>quarter of a mile &amp; came</p>
<p>back home. Cleaning harness</p>
<p>ect in afternoon. Got a</p>
<p>letter from mother 29<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;March</p>
