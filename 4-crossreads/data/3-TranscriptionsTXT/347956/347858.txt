 15.1.15 
 get a new bulb for his torch.  I stayed in &amp; wrote to George &amp; Maggie.  quite a pile of letters to-day.  Stan received 14 &amp; I got 8; they are letters that left Australia shortly after us &amp; were sent on to England &amp; have only just turned up.  Bluey Williams got properly Blithered after tea, with his "cobba" Scotty the cook &amp; came back to camp about 8 o'clock with a hole in his leg; it looks very much like a bayonet wound but may have been done with a knife.  some of the lads bound it up &amp; got him to bed 
 Fri 15th Another batch of letters Stan received 6 &amp; I got 4 more &amp; were putting in every available moment reading to-day
