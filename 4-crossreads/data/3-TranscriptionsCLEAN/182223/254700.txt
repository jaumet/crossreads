
Thursday 23 September 1915 About hospital 650 patients Had dinner with Lieut Col McGlynn ar Shep the Continental Hotel. [Major (Honorary Lieutenant Colonel), later Brigadier General, John Patrick McGlinn CBE VD CMG OBE (1869-1946)]
Friday 24 September 1915 Saw Lt Col McGlynn, he had come from Galipoli, where he has been since April.  He looks very well. Lunched with Col. Manifold & Col. Hunter at the Continental Hotel. 
Saturday 25 September 1915 In Cairo during the morning. Sent 128 men to Australia, all correct. 
Sunday 26 September 1915 At Basilica at 9-30 a.m. At Matarieh with Major Young at 6 p.m. Letters from home this morning.
