<p>151<br/>conducted by "Padre" Mackenzie (Fighting Mac" at a place called M&eacute;ricourt [sur Somme]. It was held in a big roomy building. The men always responded regularly to the Padres invitation. He was very well liked indeed.</p>
<p>I lost my pay-book here. Close by was a big Expeditionary Force Canteen. I think, by the look of it that it had been in existence since the commencement of the war.</p>
<p>It was possible to get tea cake preserved fruit in here.</p>
<p>There were special rooms set aside for officers. Although Imperial yet one could be served quite readily.</p>
