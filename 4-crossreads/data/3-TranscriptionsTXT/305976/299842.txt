 Tuesday March 13 Another day on horses. Had some good gallops which finished up in a race. Charley[?] Woods winner. Jimmy Woodcock leading when his neddy misjudged jumping trench &amp; landed him in it. The evenings drag here Wednesday March 14th Day out in sham fight with L H. Regiment. Officers simply do not know what to give the men to do to fill in time while we are waiting. Everybody fed up including myself.
