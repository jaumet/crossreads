
1st June, 1916Went with 18 other chaps to the "Bull Ring" and did sundry courses. The Australian division made a demonstration tonight with mines, artillery, rockets, and other "property". I suppose this is the grand final, as a draft is leaving for the forward area tomorrow. All cameras in the possession of the troops were seized today and all diaries and other writings were ordered to be handed in. I will risk it with this one, I guess.3rd June 1916.On fatigue storing things at the Quarter Master's store. Met Alf Morey the boxer chap there.
