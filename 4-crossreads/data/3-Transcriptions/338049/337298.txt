<p>Friday 29 March<br/>Shop mostly open. Very surprised. Raining.</p>
<p>Visited Dee Bridge, then the Docks. After dinner took the longest tram ride out past Deeside. On return ascertained photos O.K. Arranged evening &amp; Had good time.</p>
<p>Leave going splendidly.</p>
