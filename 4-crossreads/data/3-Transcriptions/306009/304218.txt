<p>1915<br/>Feb. 1 Mon.<br/>Number of patients in Hospital &ndash; 674.<br/>Turned into bed after working all day at 11 p.m.<br/>Bugle calls are sounded daily in No.2 G.H. camp as follows:-<br/>Reveille - 6.15 a.m.<br/>Breakfast - 7.45<br/>Dinner - 12.45<br/>Tea - 5.15 p.m.<br/>First Post - 9.30<br/>Last  Post - 10.0<br/>Lights out - 10.15</p>
<p>A number of patients medically unfit for further service in the A.I.F. sent back to Australia in the old Kyarra.</p>
