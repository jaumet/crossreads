<p>23 Jan 16<br/>3<br/>planted round the paddock next the house.<br/><strike>Young P.H.</strike> There is a rumour current here to the effect that all troops who have been on Active Service Some Aus. 12 mos. are  going back on 3 mos furlough, but I don't believe it. Harry Budden may be here today, so I hope to see him. He works awful hard , old girl for the things he representing.<br/>No news old girl.<br/>Very much love to you all.<br/>Your loving brother<br/>Fred</p>
