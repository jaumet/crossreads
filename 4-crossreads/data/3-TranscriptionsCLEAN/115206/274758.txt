  
 July 1918 Sunday 7 
 Vaux sur Somme.2nd Battery Position Laid gun line and fixed up a couple of emergency lines to 53rd and 55th, in company of Tom Code. Laid a line to O. Pot. 4pm and generally squared up everything. Weather fine. Fritz still shelling Vaux and the vicinity. Battery registered Heard today Mr Sheedy was coming back to the Bty. Smithy arrived back from Paris leave, up to time. Hear we are only in for a week. Capt Nicholson a little better.  Had a letter from Bully, he's going home.
