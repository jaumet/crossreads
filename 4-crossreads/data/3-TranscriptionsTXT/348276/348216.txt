 War Diary 17th Sept. Work continuing in Trenches and Saps. 
 18th September Do.  Do. 73 Reinforcements for 8th, 9th, and 10th &ndash; arrived from Heliopolis. 
 19th Sept. Inspection of Trenches and saps &ndash; In afternoon enemy made demonstration at 1730 for &frac12; an hour accompanied by Rifle and M.G. fire from Battleship and Snipers Ridge &ndash; one of our own 18 prs landed in our trenches few feet from Col. Abbott &ndash; failed to explode &ndash; Norfolks marched without reference to B.H. Qrs. evidently in instructions from their own C.O. &ndash; Work going well in Trenches and Saps.
