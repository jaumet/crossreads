On 28th did my first shooting with Lewis gun on the range and did fairly well. 29th my feet were very bad so I paraded sick and was evacuated and taken by motor to 36th casualty clearning station at Heilly. Here I was attended to and put in a marque to sleep on a stretcher. Once you are given your quarters you are directed to the mess room
