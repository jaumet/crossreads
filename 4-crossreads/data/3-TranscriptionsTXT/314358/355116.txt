 4 Could you understand how utterly sick and weary I am of humanity. And how despairing I am of that same humanity ever becoming less deduceable to degradation than it is. 
 When you have to stop a grand old dog to have someone clean to talk to, you will quite understand how I feel at times. 
 Most men dont understand this for they soon become a fitting part of the great mass. 
 They have no inclination to stop the dog, for soon he is too clean minded for their pleasure. 
 Now that I have had my say within sane and reserved limits I must get on with matters of more interest to you.
