 Wednesday, 25/4/17 Inclined to be cloudy.  Being discharged tomorrow.  Saw Rugby League match in afternoon between two Australian Pioneer teams. 
 Thursday, 26/4/17 Discharged from hospital this morning.  Came in Ambulance motor as far as No. 5 Camp &amp; walked rest of way to No. 11.  After dinner transferred to No. 12 Camp &amp; in hut No. 13.  Feeling very tired after walk.  Rest of mates in No. 20 hut of 11 Camp in contact for another 24 days.  Pennel &amp; Brigden developing mumps.
