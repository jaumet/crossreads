<p>2 March, Sunday</p>
<p>F. Glorious Day</p>
<p>Hop men in mess. I alone get 4 Girls to play Snooker in front of mob. L/Cpl Nuttell sings bonza. We sit with Dot M. M. also at Tea. I slow Dick's "Photos" &amp; my own. N. Z goes at 7 pm. I go Home with Dane. Also have some fun with another Dolly. We all get presents &amp; I get a pin cushion but sister gets a Bondoir Cap.</p>
<p>3 March, Monday</p>
<p>F. Nice Day</p>
<p>We are again inoculated by Dick Top. Recd letters from M.R.L. &amp; Aunt Annie. I go to Y.W.C.A. &amp; they have no musician &amp; I play all night with some measure of success. I am scaled for supper &amp; promenade.</p>
<p>Wrote to M.R.L.</p>
