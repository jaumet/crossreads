<p>195<br/>I put on a German coat, coming round the corner of the trench I found some of our chaps. I said to them "Mercy Kamerade", and they were going to attack me, I didn't try it again.</p>
<p>We were very thirsty and whilst it rained that night we in our endeavour to get some drinking water put out our waterproof sheets obtaining a little in this way.</p>
<p>Some of the chaps also foraged amongst the dugouts and found some German water-bottles, filled with cold coffee &ndash; they as a rule prefer coffee to water owing to the scares amongst the men as the result of many traps</p>
