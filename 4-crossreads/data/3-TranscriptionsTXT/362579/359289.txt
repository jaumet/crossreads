 Now for some news &ndash; We are promoted to Sisters now, so Dorothy, Kelly   &amp;  I are very delighted. Believe it begins from April 4th, so shall get 2/6 a day extra &ndash; not too bad. 
 I think I shall try   &amp;  put it into a Bank in London in case I need any while there. 
 Dorothy is awfully excited about it; of course it was time we got it, 2 years now On Active Service. 
 We have been a bit quieter for a few days, so I've had two days off-duty &ndash; had a sore throat,   &amp;  Col. C. did not want to let me go, so managed to give me a rest   &amp;  I'm quite alright now. Shall be on duty to-morrow. 
 24.4.17
