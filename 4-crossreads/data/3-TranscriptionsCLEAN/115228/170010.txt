Nov 13th :- The Brigade moved back in to the beach about four miles to the rear, evidently with the idea of giving us a short rest. It actually proved very short, for hardly had we our lines (down?) & stripped off for a swim when we were ordered to saddle up at once, & we again moved out.
Nov 14th :- The Turks having retired during the night, we advanced another ten miles today through Askalan & on to (Duran?), a pretty little Jewish village. Only
