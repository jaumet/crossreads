<p><u>Every Soldier A High Commissioner.</u></p>
<p>Every&nbsp;<s>Soldier</s>&nbsp;Australian Soldier in the United Kingdom&nbsp;<s>act</s>&nbsp;is, consciously or unconsciously, acting as a High Commissioner for the Commonwealth. People are facinated with&nbsp;<s>their</s>&nbsp;the stories of their matchless valor in the field. On the average they are 1 biggest men in the British Army, besides being the best paid.
