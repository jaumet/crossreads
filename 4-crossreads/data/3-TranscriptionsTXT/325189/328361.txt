 21st March 1915 Sunday 
 We received part of our pay yesterday (82&frac12; P.T.) &amp; leave.  Tramed into Cairo &amp; had dinner.  Wandered round a bazaar or two &amp; then tramed to the Citadel.  This beautiful place was built in 1179 by the famous Saladin with stones taken from the small Pyramid.  s  .The building stands upon a hill &amp; natural rock forms a foundation for it.
