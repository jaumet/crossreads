<p>1 July 1918 Monday<br/>(Residents abroad should now order this diary for 1919.)<br/><strike>(Wonder whether I'll want another)</strike><br/>At 0400 or 4 am we were well &amp; truly shelled a nice introduction to our new Camp-  Just warned to go out on a Wiring party tonight in front line so must try &amp; dodge shells but a man is never free in this Sector<br/>We remain in support at least a fortnight those that are left &ndash; evacuations heavy thro' Malarial etc:-<br/>Lost 10 horses killed 26 wounded by shell fire-   ABU. TELLUL</p>
