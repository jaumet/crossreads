<p>After breakfast I dress quickly in old clothes which I possess &amp; a pair of Marie Lebretors slippers that I salvaged at my little chateau de 17 Rue Campagne Premier. Then I betake myself into bedroom with a blanket &amp; hot water bottle or brick provided by Brise or her mother &amp; dig into law then the hour of dejeuner (noon)</p>
<p>I am not called till the soup is even in the plate &amp; then I am called. I might add that I am consulted as to if I am ready before meal is served. Everyone must speak in whispers &amp; tread on tip toe if I am working. A King could not be better served.</p>
