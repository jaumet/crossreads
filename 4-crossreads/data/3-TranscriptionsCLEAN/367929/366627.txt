  
 May 1915 23 Sun Fairly quiet day. No casualties from sniping a record - but the usual 1 or 2 sick sent back 
 24 Mon Armistice from 7.30 am to 4.30pm for enemy to bury their dead. Each side buried those half way between ea others trenches. Counted 51  dead opp [indecipherable] of post that C Sqd was at in various stages. Saw Germans Saw sev curios. Many of enemy
