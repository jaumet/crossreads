<p>Feb 4th/ Had a letter from Harry, he was down at Tel el Kebir, he was brought off the Peninsular just before the evacuation with dysentery, was in hospital at Alexandria for some weeks.</p>
<p>Feb 5th/ Played 7th LH Regt football, they beat us badly. March past of 2nd Brigade of consists of the 5th 6th &amp; 7th Regiments. Brig: General Ryrie becomes a General.</p>
