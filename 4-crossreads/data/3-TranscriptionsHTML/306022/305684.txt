<p>We stayed in the stream till about 10.15 am when we took up anchor and made for the Heads. While waiting I had a good look round the ship and it looks as though we are going to have a fairly comfortable time. She is a B. I. 6 mno's boat, the "Mashobra" of about 8000 tons.</p>
