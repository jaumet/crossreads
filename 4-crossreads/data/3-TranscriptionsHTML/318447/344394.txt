<p>18th March<br/>Still; in our good quarters &amp; I have got most of my gear dry. The people of the house are German, they sing lugubrious hymns &amp; just now are singing "There is no place like home." or at any rate something to that tune. They are most obliging drying our book &amp; clothes &amp; washing up our dishes after</p>
