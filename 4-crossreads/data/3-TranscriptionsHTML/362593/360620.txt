<p>Monday 30 December 1918<br/>Cable from Home, telling Uncle Willy died on 12th.  Very sorry about it as I thought a lot of Uncle Willy, he was always a good friend to the ones at Home.  Decide to give up trip to England &amp; America at once &amp; proceed Home at earliest opportunity.</p>
<p>Tuesday 31 December 1918</p>
