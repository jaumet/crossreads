 6 &ndash; I am lying on it. How I wish I had brought a stretcher bed as some of the sisters have. Later when our delayed store ship comes I  shall  have one. I have today a staff nurse for the first time &amp; am off duty for a few hours &ndash; this afternoon. The wind has been blowing all day &amp; I am lying on grit. The flies persist to sit on my face &amp; walk up my legs, so please forgive this disconnected epistle. I cannot get my washing done.
