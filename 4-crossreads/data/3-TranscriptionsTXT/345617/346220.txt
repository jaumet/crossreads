 Octobre 25 Working today on sleeper Rd at Birr XRds. Heavy scattered shelling on gun positions. Sapper Miller was killed today by shellfire near Chateau Wood. 
 Octobre 26 Working on sleeper road at Birr X Roads. Cpl Scotland, Sap's Cook &amp; Hunt were killed today &amp; Sap Hudson was wounded while attempting to bring out the body of the late Sap Miller from Chateau Wood.
