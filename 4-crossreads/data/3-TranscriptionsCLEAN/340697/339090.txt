  
 We jumped aboard a goods train yesterday and set out for a town called Zag-A-Zig about 20 miles from camp. We spent a good deal of time shunting in and out sidings to let specials through and arrived at our destination about 2 a.m. We were arrested by a special picquet at about 2.15 a.m. and found ourselves in barracks for the night.
