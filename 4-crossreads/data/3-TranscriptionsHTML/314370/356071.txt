<p>August, 1916<br/>Thursday 17<br/>Left Carcesson   End of our holiday<br/>Move out of camp to train in the morning<br/>depart amid cheers from population<br/>stopped for tea.  Hot water provided</p>
<p>Friday 18<br/>after a (you know) sort of night we stopped for breakfast  Raining<br/>Magnificent country all farms &amp; gardens<br/>settle down for another night<br/>Pass Paris but do not see it</p>
