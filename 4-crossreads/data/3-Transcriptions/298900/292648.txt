<p>Monday 18th March<br/>    Splendid weather.   Had a busy &amp; hard day.   This was the day we were celebrating our 2nd anniversary on &amp; great trouble had been taken a week past to make it a success.   My particular job was to carry out the decorations &amp; arrange the lighting &amp; stage but I still had to carry on my job outside.   I had made a trip into Bailleul &amp; purchased fancy paper, chinese lanterns &amp; hired a number of flags also purchased some mimosa, the counterpart of our wattle but grown in the South of France.   All day we worked &amp; it really looked well.   The tables were laid out the full length of the mess hut while covers &amp; the cutlery which we borrowed all being new looked splendid.
