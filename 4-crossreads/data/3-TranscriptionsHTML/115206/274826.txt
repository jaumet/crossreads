<p>September 1918.<br/>Friday 13</p>
<p>In Reserve. Hebecourt<br/>Weather showery, inoculated against typhoid fever. Worked during afternoon, Cox leave to Blighty. Paid some money  - no good &ndash; no canteens.<br/>Played cards and lost a little. Weather wet. <br/>My spectacular sight during evening about 9 pm. When a Fritz bombing plane was brought down in flames by one of our planes, near our camp. Five Fritzs in searchlights.</p>
