Saturday 1.9.17
Very quiet, nothing very sensational has happened to create excitement.
Sunday 2.9.17
Spent the day in St Omer. Change from the ordinary routine of camp life. Band usually plays in Park of a Sunday, where crowds of both civilians soldiers congregate. Fortunately left the town early enough to miss an air raid by Fritz, dropped a good number of bombs doing considerable damage
Monday 3.9.17
Fritz again came over bombed St Omer, also tried to bomb but was driven off.
Tuesday 4.9.17
Route march of about 8 miles
