<p>Friday 5 July<br/>[indecipherable] attacks severely [refused?]</p>
<p>Parade by Capt. Ferguson and various instns. received.</p>
<p>Afternoon exercise &amp; grazing along main Villers Brettoneaux &ndash; Amiens Rd.</p>
<p>Visited with Harry Richards various woods &amp; were finally too close to a bombardment so came away at gallop. Saw many French, &amp; tanks.</p>
<p>To La Motte concert. Very good 7th Bde &ndash; Met two little Bailies.</p>
