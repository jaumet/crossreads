<p>September, 1915<br/>Wednesday 22<br/>Much cooler at night now &amp; days shorter, am trying to read a bit of Military Law etc: but have an ex-actor in my room a Comedian with a Banjo - <strike>who does not consider the serious side of our job. He is an Officer of sorts.</strike><br/>Sorry I was not sent to England at first when wounded, but did not anticipate being here so long.</p>
