<p>and proceeded same day with Bty to Bouzincourt where we stayed over night.</p>
<p>Thur Aug 24th &ndash; 16<br/>Left again at 11AM after a good day's march arrived at Hirandel [? see glossary] near Hem [?]</p>
<p>Fri Aug 25th &ndash; 16<br/>There are still some very rich rumours flying round which have got everybody down with curiousity. Some are to the effect that we go to England and then for Garrison duty in India</p>
<p>Sat Aug 26th &ndash; 16<br/>I was picked to go with advance guard and left Hem at 7.30PM. Entrained</p>
