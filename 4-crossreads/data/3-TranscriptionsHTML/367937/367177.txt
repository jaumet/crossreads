<p>No 3 Hospital<br/>Abbassia<br/>Near Cairo<br/>7th June 1916.</p>
<p>My dear Mother<br/>After all said and done I have gone and got mumps, and am up here in hospital, which is a jolly side nicer place than in the contact camp out in the desert at Tel el Kebir.  I started to get them last Sunday; was sent to hospital in camp on Monday and came up here to day.<br/>Yesterday in camp I received twelve letters and My Word! I was pleased.<br/>Two were from you, one from Aunt Katie, and the rest from Telarah, none</p>
