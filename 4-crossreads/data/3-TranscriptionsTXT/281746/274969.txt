Nov. 12th, Tues.No parades to-day in honour of peace but strange to say there is no Divine service being held.
Nov. 13thSong Service conducted by the officer in charge of the Y.M.C.A.
Nov. 17thThese last few days the ship has been rocking a great deal.  There is a tremendous swell on the sea.  About 4 o'clock in the afternoon we reached outside the harbour at Fremantle.
