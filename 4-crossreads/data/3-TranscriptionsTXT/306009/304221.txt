 1915 Feb. 4 Thu. Fine swimming bath at Mena 11 yards  long  wide 35 yards long &ndash; must have a dip or two when weather gets a bit warmer. 3 men (motor drivers &ndash; Sgt. &amp; 2 Ptes) arrived from Australia to join our unit &ndash; they came with the second force, 13000 men in 18 Transports &ndash; No leave granted to them on voyage &ndash; Left Melbourne on 20th Dec. &ndash; 3rd Force left on 5th Jan.
