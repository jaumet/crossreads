<p>204<br/>very long before he shifted his position.<br/>On another occasion we were detailed for a fatigue job taking our rifles with us &ndash; which in trenches we carried at the trail to avoid being a mark for the enemy.</p>
<p>In the hurry of getting out of the trench I did not notice the "safety catch" was forward. Unthinkingly I put my finger on the trigger and pressed it. Instinctively I pressed the muzzle in the direction of the bank and the bullet found a lodgement there.</p>
<p>The chap in front became rather indignant about it as he well might.</p>
