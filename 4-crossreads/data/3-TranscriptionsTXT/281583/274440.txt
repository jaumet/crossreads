 Altogether I am not feeling well but am not too bad. They have shaved the hair away from the wound &amp; I am half bald. 
 25.11.16 Saturday 
 Grove Town Hospital 
 26.11.16 Sunday 
 Was moved today from Grove Town Hospital &amp; taken on small trucks to the Hospital Train on the main Line from where I understand we are being shifted to Rouen Hospital. 
 27.11.16  Monday 
 After spending all night in the train we arrived at Rouen at 9am &amp; were carried out to the waiting ambulance &amp; taken to No 5 General Hospital. I am under observation as my temperature keeps high &amp; I am troubled with headaches.
