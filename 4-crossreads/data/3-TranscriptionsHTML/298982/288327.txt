<p>Sat 31st July Met some our troop at Beach. 48 strong in Squadron now 30 odd for duty.<br/>Rolled overcoats on outpost. Light horse I heard 700 strong out of 1500. Percentage of sick everywhere too heavy. Still hot &amp; dry. Bits of bomb &amp; shrapnel fell near possie at night. Attack on right Two trenches reported taken</p>
<p>Sun 1st August</p>
<p>Quiet day played cards with Morrie noon. Still hot &amp; dry.</p>
<p>Mon 2nd Still nothing doing Made some dice. Called on Tite in evening. Alexander gave me three eggs. Bust 'em don't eggs in your shirt</p>
