  
 Aug 28 A night of rolling, much wind & cold as b-----s Tomorrow night we will reach Capetown. an extra blanket has been dished out so we wont rattle the timbers any more with our shivering. Pte Wilson is shortening my greatcoat. - which should look OK. No sign of any pay yet? 
 29 "Darwins Missing Link" "Chidley" & sundry others! They're all on board this boat, in fact it's a panto' in itself. A fairly large whale appeared for a few seconds & then sounded. Noticed several birds: - Albatross, stormy petrel & Cape pigeon
