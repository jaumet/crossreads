May 27th N.N.W. M.325
Coy Orderly Cpl. for day parading sick 9 a.m. 5-30 p.m. about the only duties realy required. Sighted ship's smoke ahead at 7a.m. over took her at 11a.m. ?Sontay? troopship french aboard. All our men ordered on deck, returned theirs our Nationals also 3 cheers, a very thrilling few minutes, she was very crowded slow, soon left behind. Had to put man through 10" Pack drill 6 p.m. listening to band studying. bath bed
