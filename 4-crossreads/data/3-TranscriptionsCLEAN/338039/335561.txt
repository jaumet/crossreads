  
 [Previous pages not transcribed] 1916-17 Diary of Movements & Events while "On Active Service." 
 Jan. 14th. Final Inspection by our O.C. (Capt. Donovan.  Left Liverpool 2 p. m- Parade through Sydney and Reviewed at Domain by Col. Ramaciotti- Spend night at Show Ground. Jan.15th. March to Orient Wh'f.  Grand farewell- fine spectacle- left Harbour at 1.30 p. m- spent good afternoon- slightly ill at night- slept in hammock.
