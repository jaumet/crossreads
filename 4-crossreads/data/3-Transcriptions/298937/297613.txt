<p>Sunday 2nd July</p>
<p>While at the N.Zealand Canteen the other night Cpl. Foulkes &ndash; Medical Student friend of Mony's &ndash; came to Jack Molloy &amp; I with the information that 3 Corporals each with four men were wanted as regimental details.<br/>He had been chosen &amp; of his four men 3 had been chosen.  Mony of course was the best as fourth for I decided not to leave Molloy.  There remained the chance with the other Corporals.  Molloy &amp; I dashed back &amp; found Corpl. Weir.</p>
