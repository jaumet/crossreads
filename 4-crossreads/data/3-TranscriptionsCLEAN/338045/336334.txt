It is a dirty little town, all side streets & small.  Do some shopping with Sgt. David & return to camp.  Cold night.  Expenses:-  P/cs 2fr.60, Supper 3fr.40 = 6Frs.  Very little occurred at the front.
Oct. 22nd - SundayAnother bitterly cold night.  Heavy frost.  Parade 6-30 a.m. & after breakfast, fall in for church parade at 8-30 a.m.  I have charge of R.C. parade of 28 men & march in (with other units) to the Church in Etaples.  Mass at 9-30 a.m. & a fine sermon
