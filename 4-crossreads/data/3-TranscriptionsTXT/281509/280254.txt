 1916 February 1 Tuesday 
 Morning shift.  Get our final leaves made out &amp; our allotment papers signed.  Down to Sydney in evening.  Bert Edwards up to see me.  Go out to Bondi with Bob. Stay the night. Miss Astbury &amp; [indecipherable]ora &ndash; in later. Sing supper &amp; bed Bob still very unwell.
