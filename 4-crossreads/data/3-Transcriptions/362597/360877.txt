<p>Wednesday 19.1.16 (28)<br/>We are steaming through a glassy smooth sea &amp; it is very warn 'tween decks. Full Dress Parade &amp; the Colonel spoke of our tidyness &amp; good conduct &amp; hoped it would continue.</p>
<p>Semi-finals of Boxing Tournaments held. Some very good fights.</p>
