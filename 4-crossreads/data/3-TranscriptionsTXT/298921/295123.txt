
June, 1915Tuesday 1Joes' letter of 3/5/15Days    "     25/4/15Jack Harrison 2/5/15MOTHER 2/5/15All of which I will answer by letter as soon as possible.Very busy with much [indecipherable] work.Ran over a Nigger today in Brigade Car & killed him they are like ants on the roads & life not of much consequence apparently - still I was sorry, but they do not like the English.
