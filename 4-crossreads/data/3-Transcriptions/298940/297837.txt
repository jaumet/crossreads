He is, I suppose, the nearest akin to me in tastes &amp; ideals of any in the unit, for which reason I hope to be further acquainted with him.<br/>Last Saturday I set out for Caestre which is distant some 8 kilometres, &amp; called on Madeleine Van Uixem who was very pleased to see me again. In helping her to write a letter to Lily I managed to have a confidential chat which I enjoyed immensely. Yesterday Vic Hall &amp; I walked over there &amp; although I was not able to have such a long chat with her on account</p>
