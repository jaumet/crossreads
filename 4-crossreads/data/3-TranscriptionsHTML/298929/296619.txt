<p>12/4/18<br/>There are lots of things that happen that we hear of and forget.<br/>There are memories that we cherish, there are those that bring<br/>regret,<br/>There are tender recollections that the darker hours console,<br/>But there's thoughts of bitter sorrows that will grip your very<br/>soul.<br/>We'd a colonel in command of us, the gruff old veteran brand,<br/>Beloved by his battalion and the idol of the band<br/>The boys all called him Billjim for he'd proved himself their friend<br/>And swore that where he'd lead them they would follow to the<br/>end.</p>
