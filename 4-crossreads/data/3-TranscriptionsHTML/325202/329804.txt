<p>left at 2 a.m for the rail head at Godervaersvelde 10 miles away which after part of our column getting lost (me amongst it) we reached at 5.30 a.m. Entrained and left at 8 a.m. I travelled on the flat truck underneath the waggon it was loaded with</p>
<p>Reached Doullens at 2 p.m. unloaded horses &amp; waggons harnessed up &amp; pulled out at 3 p.m.</p>
<p>Doullens was a good sized country town very straggling but clean &amp; picturesque with good shops &amp; I wished we had been halted there. But we pushed on and at about 6 p.m. halted to water &amp;</p>
