  
 From - Commander Beresford.  To - Flagship & "Berrima" 
  12th Septr. '14  
 (9-30am) 
 Engineer who reported on Wireless is a man who was leading Telegraphist on board H.M.A.S. "Yarra" three years ago, named Rutzon. Submit wireless expert be landed to report. Distance inland about six miles.
