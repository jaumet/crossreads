<p>Destroyer reported seeing both men go into the sea.</p>
<p>22 Dec 16<br/>Run out of the bad weather last night. Picked up with some Destroyers in forenoon &amp; came in &amp; anchored at Rosyth. We were the last cruiser to get to harbour. Coaled ship &amp; oiled ship in afternoon.<br/>HMS Yarmouth reports losing 3 men &amp; 3 boats in heavy seas yesterday. HMS Southhampton had part of her bridge swept away.</p>
<p>23 Dec 16 )<br/>24 Dec 16 )  Anchored at Rosyth</p>
<p>25 Dec 16<br/>Xmas Day<br/>Anchored at Rosyth. Spent a pretty quiet Xmas. We have a new Captain on board. He came on board yesterday.</p>
