<p>No, 7247. Private James Patrick Godfrey Griffin,<br/>13th Battalion,<br/>A Company,<br/>24th Reinforcements.</p>
<p>Born 8th January, 1891, at Bega, N.S.W. Farmer and land-owner.<br/>Sailed from Sydney, 7th February, 1917.</p>
<p>Sent by father, Thomas Griffin. Cathcart, N.S.W.<br/>and mother, Emma Griffin.</p>
