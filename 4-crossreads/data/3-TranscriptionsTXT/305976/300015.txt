The dust gets in everything & is most unpleasant.Saturday June 22Went over to 1ST FA today Just as Tom & I landed there Jericho Jane dropped one in the camp & we had to get down for our lives. We were in a funk hole for nearly two hours while Jacko heaved them all round the camp. Killed several horses.Sunday June 23Jacko shelled us again to day.
