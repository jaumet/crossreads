 5pm, Horses again til 5,45. then tea at 6pm. Lights out at 10 o'clock. Thats our days routine. The drill has been very tedious for we that have come almost fresh from it. All the old men find it new drill, that is all the South African men &amp; such like &amp; I have to go over all the stuff I learnt in the cadets &amp; the 13th Garrison Artillery.
