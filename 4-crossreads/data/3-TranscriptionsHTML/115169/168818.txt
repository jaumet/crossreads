<p>Sep 16th [1915] at 7 a.m. we entered the harbour of St Thomas and anchored between two German steamers, that were laying at anchor, and still flying their national flag but not many persons were seen on board.  We saluted the Dutch authorities at 8 a.m. and at 8.30 our Consul came on board, but did not remain for long, and as we departed he was saluted by 9 guns &ndash; At 1.30 PM we weighed anchor and proceeded to Bermuda</p>
