<p>H Nicholls Esq<br/>Pine St<br/>Manly</p>
<p>12/10/11</p>
<p>Dear Harry,<br/>Many thanks for Photo it is very nice. I had mine taken at Crudens was waiting to give you a surprise will send you one in about a week. I am taken up with Cricket at present. Secretary of the Club. I want you to come out for one week end. Let me know when it will suit you. Hope to see you soon. Hope all are well. Remember me to all. Ted</p>
