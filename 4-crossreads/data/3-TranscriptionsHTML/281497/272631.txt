<p>same paddock, what a fine lot of men they looked. as they marched past us, &amp; us all ragged, unshaven, for you know we had not even our razors or towels since we left Warloy, &amp; had been through all the toil hardships smoke &amp; dust of a most tremendous battle, &amp; had tasted the joys &amp; exhilaration of victory, I can assure you that will make men do almost any thing it is a fine sensation to be flushed with victory especially when you can say that you have taken a good part in it yourself men fighting with the confidence of the Australians will accomplish almost anything nothing seems impossible to one at such a time</p>
