Memoranda
Jan 5th 1917Rained again in showers. Wind very cold. Met a lot of old friends. Lt Lamb, Lt Dawson 5th LH Lt Bell 2 LH all in 11th Coy ICC
Jan 6Nothing of note today.
Jan 7thWork very hard today all bustle whilst issuing rations at 8  pm moonlight our camp was bombed by Taubs 2 bombs dropped 30 yards away. No damage. Moving out to Rafa for battle tomorrow.
