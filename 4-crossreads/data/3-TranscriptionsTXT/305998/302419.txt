 21/ 14th Rfts of 6th Rgt. arrived here 100 strong, a reinforcemt used to be only 50 men. I was short of a fur band for my hat till they came; in fact there is not many of the Rft left with furs. We were issued with helmets for hot weather they are very heavy things after the old felt hats.
