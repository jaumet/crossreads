<p>September 1915</p>
<p>Sunday 12</p>
<p>Church parade at 9 oclock in Garrison church, which I thoroughly enjoyed.</p>
<p>Am now a permanent lance corporal so that will be something to go to Gallipoli with.</p>
<p>Warned to proceed with the next batch which leaves tomorrow or Tuesday.</p>
