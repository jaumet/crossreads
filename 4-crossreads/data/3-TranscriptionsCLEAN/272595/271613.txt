
24th  Passed a real bad night & this morning I feel as if every bone in my body wants to drop out, my head is aching like mad & I feel weak all over so I have been in bed practically the whole day, I can eat nothing only drink a little port or a cup of coffee now & again they wanted to send for the Dr but I stopped them, for I kept thinking it would go as suddenly as it came but no fear as the day wore on it got worse & worse, about 2 oclock I thought I would get up & go downstairs out into the bright sun & air, but this did no good so I started unpacking all the parcels which I had sent Mr Duke during the past year I stuck at this till finished, then I had to go back to bed again, Mr Duke was beginning to get alarmed but I made out that I would be right in the
