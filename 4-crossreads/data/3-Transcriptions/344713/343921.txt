<p>Tues. Nov. 7th: -<br/>Received Aussie mail dated Aug. 27th.</p>
<p>Wed. Nov. 8th: -<br/>Worked on fatigue at hillside neat a "Tommy": howitzer battery building a tunnel for Winter "possies". A shell coming over killed a officer &amp; wounded 4 others.<br/>May as well be in line as then we wouldn't have to be doing hard-graft night &amp; day.</p>
<p>Thurs. Nov. 9th: -<br/>Pay-day. Received 50 francs. Nothing to do it on here except cards &amp; the "National game". The padre ran a bit of a canteen but his stock soon ran out. Received mail dated Sept. 16th.</p>
<p>Mon. Nov. 13th: -<br/>Left the "humpies" on road at Delville &amp; went back about a mile to possies near Bernafay where we are to do the night.<br/>Mud still keeping us company &amp;  we have two 6 inch naval guns firing over us from about 50 yds back.<br/>"Fritz" naturally tries searching for them &amp; two of our chaps got wounded as soon as we got here. I think we go right back for a wash &amp; "spell"<br/>4th Division took over from us &amp; we won't be trailing along Cocoa Lane or Flare Alley for a while.</p>
