 Tuesday 5 March At the Brasserie to-day which is fine in fact beautiful.  Went to Kemmel M.D.S. for mail.  Gas guard tonight. 
 Wednesday 6 March On guard at Brasserie, between Kemmel &amp; Ypres. The A.D.S. at Woormezeele is 3000 yards direct from Ypres &amp; our line is above famous Hill 60.
