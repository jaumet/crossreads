<p>He had gone thro' Gallipoli as Staff Sergt. in A.S.C. and joined the Infantry as a Lieut. (13th Batt.) only a month before his death.  In cricket baseball &amp; other sporting bodies he was always interested and was Secry. of Centennial Park Cricket &amp; Baseball Association for many years.</p>
<p>Monday 13th<br/>A very busy day.  The whole Third Div. practised marching out.  We had our wagons all packed yesterday so at 11.30 we fell in (full kit) and moved off.  Then a long march commenced.  To Amesbury we came <strike>thro</strike> up past Stonehenge and on to Winterbourne Stoke, Shrewton and back <strike>to</strike> thro' Rollestone to Camp arriving at <strike>7</strike> 6.15 p.m.</p>
<p>W. Pearson</p>
