
14 - Sea journey.This letter has to be posted today. There is no more news to tell you. It's the 21st July and the weather is very calm & sunshiny.Much love to you both dear parents. I hope you are both well and trying to get some peace and quiet.I hope Bill is well and that he and Edith have a comfortable house - I tried to see Mrs Myers but I didn't get an answer to my letter. She asked me to tea early in my stay in London but I could not go - will you give
