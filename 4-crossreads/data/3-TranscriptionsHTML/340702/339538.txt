<p><strike>Thursday</strike> 19/2 Tuesday</p>
<p>Sure enough we shut down the lights Fritzys planes seemed to be holding a meeting up above. I took over at midnight &amp; we ran afterward without interruption. I must revise the above. The shunt field of the Dynamo became disconnected &amp; gave an extremely irate electrician (me) a chance to display his proficiency in Australese I went to see some Belgians last night. I thought it was a good chance to learn French, by their questions I think that they thought it was a good chance to obtain some knowledge of the movements of Australian Troops. Needless to relate all of the information they obtained could be put in your eye without causing inconvenience. I must steer clear of that place in future. The church here (La Creche) was practically demolished by a single shell.</p>
