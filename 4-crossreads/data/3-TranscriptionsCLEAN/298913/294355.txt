It lies in a big hollow & faces towards Cairo. In front & a little to one side, are the Temple & Tombs of the Sphinx. There is very little to see here too, as it is in ruins, The photo will explain far more than I can here. The Columns are of granite & look very nice  & the floor is of pure alabaster. The tombs "Hum" of dampness. Here the coffins used to be placed.We took a few photos & then left. Camera fiends are round the Pyramids & the Sphinx in scores. Cheops is the only
