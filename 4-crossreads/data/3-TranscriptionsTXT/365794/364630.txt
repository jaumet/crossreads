 Friday 16 August 1917 [1918] Ordered 2 more overalls at Pt. 60 each.  Ordered 2 night gowns. Very grateful letter from Pt. Angus Bell of Connaught, does one good to read the nice things he thinks of me. M. Woods writes &ndash; very busy at P. Said, hosp. full, 30 sisters gone to France lately, hosp. is short staffed.  We are losing all English sisters &amp; V.A.D's, 15 sisters instead of 22 on n. duty, 75 staff instead of 106. Went Cairo 8.30 a.m. A.I.F. H.Q. &amp; drew Pt. 500.  9 a.m. tram to Old Cairo, snap of Roman wall 2 sakeel (?) 1 Cont. Hotel, 1 Opera Square, back 12 m.d. &amp; slept well.  room shared by S. Gill, nice girl.  S. Gill stayed 2 days only, too noisy.
