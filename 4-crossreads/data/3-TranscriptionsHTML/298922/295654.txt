<p>Friday 21 December 1917</p>
<p>Col. McLeish is writing to General Lee to get job for JOE in L.H. as now I feel he will not be content unless he comes.</p>
<p>PARCEL arrived containing SOKS, Bed Socks, Soap, Toffees, Shortbread &ndash; all very acceptable, the <u>best</u> soap always is COAL TAR Wrights by preference. BANJO Patterson is in camp adjoining me - &amp; many men &amp; Officers I have known for years.</p>
