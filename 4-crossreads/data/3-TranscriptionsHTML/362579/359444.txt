<p>Thursday the Bridegroom arrived from France so great excitement. Went to see "Round the Map"  &amp;  enjoyed it very much.</p>
<p>Next morn (Friday) fixed up my luggage   &amp;  tried to finish shopping. then caught train, about 1 p.m., for Harsfield. At the Station carriages were these waiting &ndash; White ribbon on white   &amp;  flower in buttonhole of coachman. We were driven to the church. Myra was married in uniform Mess-dress   &amp;  she looked so pretty. He is a great fellow (A Major), he is very tall   &amp;  thin, she is short   &amp;  fat. Her sister was bridesman   &amp;  was also in uniform, it was quite a military wedding   &amp;  was very grand.</p>
<p>A lovely afternoon at the Hospital were she had been on Duty. Her friends (the sisters) gave the tea.</p>
