
22 December, Monday
Go to Lodge for my Ring. Sit on beach. Call at Dobboe's about 8 pm for Tea. Home at 9.30 & address X'mas Cards to Nettie, Mrs Mel, M Cocks, Clarke, Cuneo, Aunt G, & F, Granma, Mrs Lawrence, Jack, Preddys, M Harper.
23 December, Tuesday
To I.O.O.F. Lodge. We have music, singing, dancing. I play for dances etc. Catch 11 pm tram Home.
