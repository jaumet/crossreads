<p>Monday 4</p>
<p>Got to Salonique at 7 &amp; got out to camp very cold there. Tea at W. Tower with Reid &amp; Brown.</p>
<p>Tues 5</p>
<p>Went down to Red Cross met Richardson &amp; Dick &amp; went on Board Lafayette 11.30. Sailed at 4 p.m.</p>
<p>Wed.6</p>
<p>Drizzle &amp; slight ground swell &ndash; not feeling happy</p>
<p>Thurs 7</p>
<p>Even keel. Visited Serbs. Talked with old col. Wrote some. Warm</p>
<p>Frid.8</p>
<p>Sea like glass. Ship absolutely steady. Read 3 acts of play to Sister McD &amp; finished the 4th.</p>
<p>Sat 9</p>
<p>Ship routine. Not quite so warm</p>
