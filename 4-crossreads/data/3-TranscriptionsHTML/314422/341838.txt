<p>only 18 miles from Norwich so the shells seem to follow me no matter where I go. The Evening News says this is the 4th year of the war and the 165 day which corresponds with the Daily express reckoning of 1261 days.<br/>I wrote to A. Fisher today asking him to bring under the notice of the War department some inventions of mine. This is the third day of my leave.</p>
<p>Wednesday Jan 16. 1918.<br/>1262 [Shorthand symbols]<br/>Snowing all morning &amp; did not get up until about 9am. After breakfast I took a bus to Euston Station and made enquiries there</p>
