
1st A.G.H.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Egypt.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              June 18th  1916.My dear Home Folk.,We are still in Egypt & feel very disgusted, were told we were to leave last Friday - then for  certain Tuesday & Wednesday next - but now may not move till next month.
