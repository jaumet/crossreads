<p>He came at 4. p.m. &amp; motored me out to Ras-El-Tin Convalescent Home &ndash; Sisters Ross  &amp;  Finlay are there also Captain McLarin (Cris Murray's beloved). I had letters &amp; parcels for them from different ones at The Palace. It is a lovely spin out there. Had afternoon tea there, saw over the hospital &amp; had a beautiful view of the Harbour from the Flat-roof.
