<p>Aug 26 &ndash; 30</p>
<p>Wed 26th<br/>Races held here at Rosebery, we were all allowed out for the afternoon.  Stan &amp; I went to Victoria Barracks for money for our horses, (which we did not get) then to Sydney Hospital &amp; saw Freeman; to Herc Pen's with Jessie for tea, to the Fire Station &amp; saw Greyhurst, &amp; arrived in camp at 11 P.M.</p>
<p>Thurs 27th<br/> Murga Mack joined the camp<strike>Stan &amp;</strike> I scaled the fence after tea, &amp; put the evening in with Mylie Edwards</p>
<p>Fri 28th<br/>Foot drill all day; Stan &amp; I scaled the fence &amp; went to the Girl in the Taxi, evening</p>
<p>Sat 29th<br/>Paraded before Holman who gave us permanent passes from 6 pm until 10 pm.  went to "Felicia" &amp; met Jessie there, arrived home in camp at 11 pm (Foot drill all day)</p>
<p>Sun 30th<br/>Church Parade morning, Mack was made Sergeant &amp; knows nothing, Steven [indecipherable] was made L. Corporal &amp; knows less,  [indecipherable] made Corporal &amp; knows his work well.<br/>Mrs Pen, Mrs Gibson &amp; Mr &amp; Mrs Herc Pen came out went to Felicia for tea &amp; met mother &amp; father there</p>
