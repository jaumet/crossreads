
March 1918Thursday 7
MessinesSpent a fairly quiet day at P., with Smithy. Went along for birds.Marcolm Mills, drifted to Blighty for a spell and also to obtain his transfer to Dental Corps.Have not received any letters for some time. Drifted to L'aison with Mr Mason at 6.10 pm.Fritz tried at raid around Menin Road. 2 Battalions of 31st Prussian Div, went over. Beautifully clear day. Tower at Wewique brought down by 15" How, also a Bosche plane.
