 September, 1916 Saturday 2 Route march for troops. but I am detailed for other work &amp; miss it. After dinner went into Grantham.  evening to pictures, returning to camp at 11 pm are startled by loud hooter, all lights doused &amp; traffic stopped.  "ZEP. RAID" But it never came near us.  English people panic stricken at the very thought of it. 
 Sunday 3 No report out yet about ZEP.  Morning cold &amp; dark.
