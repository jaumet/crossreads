<p>July 22nd &ndash; so you shall be seeing him soon &ndash; the poor boy has really been too long away   &amp;  I believe is unfit for Active Service. His mother will be delighted to see him.</p>
<p>Had a very quiet time at last C.C.S. but had letters so they cheered us a bit. None from Aust. fort ever so long. Hope you are all well   &amp;  happy.</p>
<p>Fondest love   &amp;  kisses to you all.</p>
<p>From,<br/>Yours lovingly,<br/>Edith</p>
