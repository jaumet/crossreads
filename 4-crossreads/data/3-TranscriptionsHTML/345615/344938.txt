<p><strike>which</strike> as once we are on the real move no one who is quite fit wants a 'sweet' job. Not through any bravado but just to be in the swim <strike>&amp; a job in the F.P.O would</strike></p>
<p>Dec 8/15<br/>8.30 am writing this by aid of an inch of candle in an old mess hut.</p>
<p>We are to move off in a couple of hours to the Railway. There will be no sleep tonight as by the time we get to the station <strike>it will</strike> &amp; load up horses &amp; waggons on the train it will be 4 a.m.</p>
<p>We have a great camp fire roaring outside built up of old boxes &amp; the accumulation of months of camp. The men are all sat round</p>
