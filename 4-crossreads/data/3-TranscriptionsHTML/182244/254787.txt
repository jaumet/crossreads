<p>Vulcan Mars &amp; Cytherea</p>
<p>I forged a snare&nbsp;of bands indisoluble, by no art to be untied, durance for ever firm.</p>
<p>Which not the gods themselves could&nbsp;see Journey thence to Lemnos.</p>
<p>Mars flew to the house of Vulcan.</p>
<p>Dawn streamed the net around them, labour exquisite of hands by ingenuity divine informed. Neptune promised to pay the money to Mars.</p>
