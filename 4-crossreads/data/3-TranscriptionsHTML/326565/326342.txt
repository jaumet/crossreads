<p>Friday 7/4/16<br/>Mail arrived today after waiting patiently for 17 days.  Engineers commenced to erect a mess hut &amp; cook house for our corps.  Parade hours altered owing to hot weather.  Reveille 5.30 AM  Breakfast 6.30 AM, Parade 7 AM to 11 AM  Parade again 3 pm to 4.30 pm.</p>
