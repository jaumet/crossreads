Monday Aug 13th
Monasterie : reveille 6 am : boys on route march : sent P.C.s to all at home all the family, also to Spooner St : stroll to aerodrome in afternoon had a good look round : about fifteen ?planes were loaded up with bombs ready for a raid so Fritz doesn't have things all his own way : very interesting to us : went to "our house" had a good time : it makes one wish to be home once more : the girls remind me much of Elsie Mell : she sat on my knee I taught her how to count in English :
Tuesday Aug 14th
Monasterie : reveille 6 am : boys on a stunt but had to return as it rained : swim parade in afternoon to Arques it was very good : Morrie self
