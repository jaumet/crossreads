<p>Sunday 9 April 1916<br/>No leave again.</p>
<p>Monday 10 April 1916<br/>Mail in.  One from Bess &amp; Os Merrett.  None from Home.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11 April 1916<br/>Leave all day starts.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12 April 1916<br/>Donkey ride, Helouan to Memphis.  Tea at Anzac Hostel &amp; Concert.  Billy, Ellis &amp; I twice across the Nile.</p>
<p>Thursday 13 April 1916<br/>Dust storm No. 1.  No Treatment afternoon.  Dust everywhere &amp; in everything.</p>
<p>Friday 14 April 1916<br/>Wrote Roy, Marie.  Received one from Marie.  Papers from Dad.  Went to Heliopolis for milk &amp; meat paste &amp; matches.  Poor jelly died in Ice Chest.</p>
<p>Saturday 15 April 1916<br/>Pay Parade.  Met Italian Consul's daughter &amp; L. McLean.  Boys gave show at Anzac Hostel &amp; played No. 3 Aux. Hosp. Heliopolis cricket, 188 to 90 lost.</p>
