 Saturday 8 December 1917. 
 Rain eased off. Moved at 12 noon &amp; travelled to Julius. The last 3 miles was a perfect bog &amp; this with more rain at the time things were interesting. 
 Today we passed the railhead now at Ashdod. 
 There is a big Aerodrome at Julius. Counted over 25 planes out. Turkish engine &amp; rolling stock are used on the railway line. 
 __________________________________________________________ 
 and went to sleep in rain - soaked through &amp; bitterly cold.
