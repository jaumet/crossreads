 April, 1917. Friday 20 Finer day &amp; pleasant. Up at 9 &amp; got rations. 1 letter. Read in afternoon and got new stove &amp; then tried to pump. No good engine choked with oil. Spent till 6 cleaning her &amp; must continue to-morrow. Man coming round to-morrow for pay &amp; may mean a move. Read till 11. Hodgson came on his way to England for observatn. A.F.C. Very heavy bombardment on.
