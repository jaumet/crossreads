9 June 1916
Friday
No fatigues today for a wonder. In morning we go for a route march to Canniers, a village about 3 miles down the road. New church, single street, Pond. Rest beyond the village amp retrace steps. In aft. retire to sandhills loaf. Bob getting tired of being separated from his mates. After tea Tony amp self go to Army P.O. amp post Stan's letter, green envelope. Sergt. secures us a supply.
Russians begin a big offensive about this date. Capture many Austrian prisoners.
