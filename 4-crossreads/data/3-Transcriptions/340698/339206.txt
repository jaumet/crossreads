<p>about midnight we travelled via Domart, Doullens, St Pol, and Lillers and de-trained at Bavinschove about 5 am. Marched from Bavenshove to Wallon Cappelle, and went into billets surrounding the town.</p>
<p>We hear that most of the towns we were billeted in on our previous garrison of this front have since been blown to pieces.</p>
