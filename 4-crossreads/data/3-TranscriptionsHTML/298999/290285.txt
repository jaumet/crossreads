<p>Monday May 28.5.17<br/>Stand to 5 A.M. had Breakfast had shave done some washing wrote to maud Jessie aggie Johnnie tim dick auntie mae had dinner.  rested all afternoon very windy had tea 5 p.m.  turned in 6.30.  went up the canal on outpost on 2 Post 11.30 till 1 A.M.</p>
<p>Tuesday May 29.5.17</p>
<p>Stand to 5.30 had shower had Breakfast done some washing rested all day and read Papers things are very quiet.</p>
<p>had a letter from Josie had S mail from maud &amp; [indecipherable]</p>
