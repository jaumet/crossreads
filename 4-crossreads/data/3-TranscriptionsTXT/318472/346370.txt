 25 May 1918 Saturday Nothing of importance to note. Still carrying on in the Canteen job. 
 1918 May 26 Thursday Just the same old Camp Sunday. In the afternoon LCp Trevena, Sap Brown and I went for a walk out onto the Dinton Rd &amp; on to Barford. Saw an old church there part of which dates back to Magna charter.
