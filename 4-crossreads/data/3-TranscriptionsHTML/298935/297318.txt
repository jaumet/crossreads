<p>C is the Curried Mouse found in the Stew<br/>D is the Diners getting thinner &amp; thinner<br/>E is the End for which we long<br/>F is the Ling Fish remarkably strong<br/>G is the Ghost we'll soon have to bluff<br/>H if they don't hurry and alter the stuff<br/>I am the man who is writing you see<br/>J is just boys agreeing with me<br/>K is the King of all reptiles called rabbit<br/>L is the Luxury to come through force of habit<br/>M is the Men who are getting 'fed up'<br/>N is the Noise they'll soon start to kick up<br/>O is the Officer (you all know who I mean)<br/>P still Punching tickets is where he ought to have been<br/>Q is the Queerness you feel when you gaze on the table<br/>R is the Rubbish, to eat, you're unable<br/>S is the Stew, a mystery I'm sure<br/>T is the Red Tape that ties us secure<br/>U is the Uselessness in naming the trouble<br/>V very similar &ndash; it's heard &amp; then burst like a bubble<br/>W is the Weakness thro' lack of good food</p>
