 WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 10, 1919. 
 Fine & cold. Round Boundary & 
 Mountain in morning and 
 Dick Tickle & I shifted ewes & 
 lambs from 4D to Boundary 
 in afternoon. 
 Tickle lopping in Top paddock 
 & 4D in morning and went 
 down to the farm to try & borrow 
 a dray in afternoon.
