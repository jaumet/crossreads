<p>Thursday 2 September, 1915</p>
<p>17<sup>th</sup> &amp; 18<sup>th</sup> Batts came to Monash Gully to relieve some of the Regiments who have been here for a long time.</p>
<p>Turkish aeroplane flew over our trenches reconnoitering in the evening.</p>
<p>The enemy kept up a heavy rifle &amp; shell fire all night knocking our parapet about in places.</p>
<p>Wrote to Mater &amp; Mignonette.</p>
