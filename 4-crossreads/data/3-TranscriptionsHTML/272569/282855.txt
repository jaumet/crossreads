<p>Sat. 17-6-'16.<br/>Altho. Tired last night &amp; we retired very early, we were disappointed in our sleep. At midnight the Gas Alarm was sounded. For awhile Confusion reigned until everyone was shaken awake &amp; helmets donned Mac. &amp; self Got up straight away put our helmets on &amp; got out to the roadside along the Canal La Lys. After 2 hours we resumed our broken sleep. We were nicely settled when once again they got us up. (We were sleeping with clothes on of course)<br/>2 Lt. Joint fussed around &amp; soon we were on the road again. Marched to a Theatre in Sailly. Had a Gas Lecture</p>
