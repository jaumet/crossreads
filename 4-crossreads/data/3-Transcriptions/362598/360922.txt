<p>Sunday 23 April 1916<br/>7.30 to Alexandria.  Swim with H. Woolcott at Ras-el-Tin.  Sailed on the Harbour in the Risa.  Put Mr. Charles Verrers aboard the Oxfordshire.  Maltese had King.</p>
<p>Monday 24 April 1916<br/>Letters posted on board.  Had beano Heliopolis.  Wrote Mother (26).</p>
<p>Tuesday 25 April 1916, Anzac Day<br/>Wrote to Dad (27).  Concert at No. 3 Gen.  Wrote to Maisie &amp; Dulcie.  No treatment except for me.  Won 3 Legged Race.  Swim at No. 3 Gen.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26 April 1916<br/>Hot day.  111&deg; Fah. in shade, 258&deg; Fah. on Sand.  Vaccinated again.  3 vaccinated, 8 inoculation to date.</p>
<p>Thursday 27 April 1916<br/>Dave &amp; I walked all round Cairo.  Went to Museum &amp; sat outside Egyptian Caf&eacute; to watch chicken juggler.  Ellis night Ward Master.  Went to Aust. Comforts fund &amp; got 2 Bats, one was Dave Vernon's.</p>
<p>Friday 28 April 1916<br/>Played Cricket, bowling for 2 hours.  Canteen &amp; Recreation Room opened.  Played music for the Boys.</p>
<p>Saturday 29 April 1916<br/>Received mail from H. Hey, Hannah &amp; Nettie.  Went to play Cricket against H.2 Staff but Vic Wright took my place.  Our side 116, other side 4 wickets, 236, Stirling 100 retired.  Night Ward Master.  Messing about with sleeping draughts till 12.45 a.m.</p>
