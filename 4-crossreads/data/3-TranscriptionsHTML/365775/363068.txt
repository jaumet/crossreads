<p>I may mention here that during our stay on this front I took the opportunity of seeing "Armentieres" and the ruined Cathedral there, situated right in a square in the heart of the town. one can ride right round it. the buildings in the vicinity are for the greater part blown to pieces, as indeed is the greater part of the town. there are a few civilians still living in the further outskirts of the town. once has been a fine town.
