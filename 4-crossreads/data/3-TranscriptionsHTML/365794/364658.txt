<p>Thursday 13 September 1917<br/>On duty in B Ward 7 a.m., left for Ball &amp; Welsh's 10.15 a.m., no hope of uniform until 3 p.m.  Met Mag. had lunch, saw Uncle A. &amp; [indecipherable].  Shopped hard as usual.  Out in uniform for 1st time 3.30 p.m.  Barracks 4 p.m.  Got ch. &pound;21-0-0 refund for uniform.  Back at No. 4 4.45 p.m., on duty in B., tea 6 p.m. then sent off finally by S. Reynolds 7 p.m.  Dressed &amp; returned articles borrowed to Boltons.  Said good-bye to A.T.B. &amp; I shall not see him again.<br/>Home 8.40 p.m. &amp; found Mith alone.  Eva's Rob at Freemantle.  Nell over with lovely flowers and all the family pleased with my uniform.<br/>No wearing mufti again I fear me for many a long day.  Leaving &pound;9-0-0 to be paid into C'wealth by Edith, total there &pound;220-0-0 before paying &pound;9 in.</p>
