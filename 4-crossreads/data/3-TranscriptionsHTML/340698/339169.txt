<p>and it is rumoured that our Fourth Division went over.</p>
<p>We expect to remain at this H.Q. about 8 days, doing 4 in support and 4 in front line.</p>
<p>Our bn is training for a raid which we expect will take place in about a weeks time.</p>
<p>The 4th brigade had a hop over last night. It was very successful. They took sixty two prisoners.</p>
