<p>cook house wood chopping fatigue, but so far I have not over-worked, although after soft work for 4 &frac12; mths., the slightest exertion is hard.<br/>Some say Lewes near Brighton is our objective, &amp; some Weymouth.  Those of purely guesswork I consider, but a French chap in our tent, who is rather a smart man intellectually was told by the D.M.S. that those not detailed will most certainly go to England &amp; wait for orders to proceed to France.  At no time since leaving Aust. have the chances of a trip to England been more bright.</p>
