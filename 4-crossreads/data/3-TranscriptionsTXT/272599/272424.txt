 1917 Sunday 3 June 
 Nabbed for Guard on gate. Duties start 2.30 p m morning free.  Take book &amp; go down Wimereaux Beach for mornings quiet &amp; read  A beautiful fresh morning it was  Back to dinner at 12.30 of roast meat, potatoes &amp; rice pudding. Duty at 2.30 p.m. &amp; easy tho' monotonous job. good chance for read &amp; write principal duty is to strike a gong at hour &amp; half hours.   MacB &amp; the chats   Kelly arrives from 1st Field
