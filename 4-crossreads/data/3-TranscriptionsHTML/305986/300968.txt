<p>will strike some bad tempered Australians this trip  29.10.16 Everything is in an awful mess been flooded out wet right through and have to sleep in the open have built a big fire which is a great mark for Fritz shells which he is putting over pretty often I'm cold wet and hungry, they have just shot a horse that got bogged in the mud. just writing this to put in time.</p>
<p>30.10.16 Sun out this morning everything bright once more some entry of mine last night but I'm alright this morning thanks, one of the sigs was blown to</p>
