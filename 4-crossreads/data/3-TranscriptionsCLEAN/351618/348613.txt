Thursday 2nd SeptemberI was bad during the night and had to go to the rear on several occasions. Although it was midnight & later had company on nearly every occasion. Things were still a bit quiet during the day.
Friday 3rd SeptemberThe weather nice & crisp with a cool breeze. Getting the store rebuilt a little in case of Winter protection - a quiet day. Went to Ordnance for clothing etc.
