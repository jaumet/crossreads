<p>4.  It is nearly time for 9 o'clock parade &amp; work &amp; as I have absolutely no news, I will have to dry up.<br/>Dave &amp; Ern &amp; Ellis &amp; I are all in Good Health &amp; spirits &amp; have some good times to-gether now &amp; again<br/>Best of Loving Good Wishes from us all<br/>Your own Boy<br/>Harry.</p>
