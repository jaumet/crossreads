<p>4.<br/>small excitement.</p>
<p>Another reason for our delay was the alleged activity of the Hun Untersee Boats German [U Boats &ndash; Unterseeboots] in the region of the Canaries.</p>
<p>It was stated that quite a number of Transports were kept at Durban &amp; Cape Town, pending the provision of an Escort. But no one knows the real reason, &amp; Rumour was ever a lying jade.</p>
<p>Durban is a pretty place &ndash; &amp; I went for several train drives round about. The trams are Municipal, &amp; soldiers were allowed free &ndash; a grateful concession, which was much appreciated. I saw the Zoo, Museum &amp; Art Gallery. The latter is nothing like up to the Sydney one.</p>
<p>The Esplanade is well laid out, with a fine Swimming Bath, &amp; wired in</p>
