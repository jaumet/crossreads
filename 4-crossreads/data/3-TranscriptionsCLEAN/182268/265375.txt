  
 Saturday 15/12/17  Relieved 40 th  without casualties, Band went to Division. Cabled Doff. 
 Sunday 16/12/17  Hun fairly active throughout the day, our patrols out- Wrote Doff 
 Monday 17/12/17. Quiet day generally men working well  have attached to me Lt Park of 102 Regt U.S.A 
 Tuesday 18/12/17. Very cold & frosty. had a good look around the trenches 
 Wednesday 19/12/17. Hun active with Minenwerfers. One man killed.
