30 MonResting all day  Leaving at 6 pm for W.Imlay
31 Tues1 Wed AugustLeft at 6 pm for W.Imlay   making for rear of Turks lines  our troop was surrounded by T for 2 hours  horses 11/2 m away   A.S. went 2 miles furtherRan into 2 Batts of Turks  we withdrew safely and reached camp at 6.30 am.  very tired and as red as Berries  Doing 24 hours water Guard at Shellal mounting at 4.30
