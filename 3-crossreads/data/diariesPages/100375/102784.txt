1918
at 10.30
Dec 28th. Jan 1stAs a return for the surprise party we were invited down to the farm at Clapbanck for dinner. Spent a very pleasant evening
Everything in our unit is upset at present our messing arrangment are awful.
Jan 2nd.A Good Concert to-night Officers get a "nib up" in song regarding the disgraceful messing arrangements in the unit at present
Read of M Englands death in the "Stirling Observer" I had visited him only a month ago, when I was home on leave.
Jan 16th..
Heavy snow has fallen since the beginning of this year. Terrific Storm of wind and rain last night. 
Still working in "C Ward" Rumour is about, that we are going to Palestine.
Jan 25thMarthe and Jeanne of the farm have gone to a boarding school, or what we would call a Convent, at a place near Cassel, called Norpeene.

Jan 29th.Weather these last few days has been ideal. Very mild.
