There found the boat had been taken up the river to the Victoria Dock at 2p yesterday.  Took the train to Woolwich Arsenal, & then walked for miles along the docks till we eventually found the Ship & and arrived just in time for dinner.  During the afternoon I slept.Tuesday 6th July 1915.  After dinner went ashore with Witt trained to Fenchurch St, wandered around various shops, had afternoon tea.
