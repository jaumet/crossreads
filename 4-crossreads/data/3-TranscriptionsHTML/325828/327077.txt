<p>I must also thank you all very, very much for your Xmas Hamper that you have ordered<br/>It hasn't yet come to hand but I hope that it won't go astray like last years did.  It is awfully good of you dear people, &amp; really I am most grateful to you.<br/>Must close now, with love to you all at home,<br/>Ever your loving,<br/>Leo.</p>
