<p>had a lot of messing about getting fixed up  got into a sort of bed about 9  feel done up  had a good sleep<br/>friday Oct. 4.10.18<br/>feel very dopey this morning  had some Bread and butter and tea  told to get ready to move  got ready and waited all day from 8 till 5 and never got away  I never seen such messing about  feel quite sick again  got into a tent and laid down on the cold ground  had tea  got Blankets  turned in</p>
