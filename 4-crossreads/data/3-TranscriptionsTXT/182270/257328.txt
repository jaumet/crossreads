 THURSDAY, JANUARY 16, 1919. 
 Fine. I went down to Horseferry 
 Road in the morning &amp; got

 About the flat all the afternoon. 
 Poss &amp; I had dinner at Hatchetts 
 in the&nbsp; afternoon &nbsp;evening and went to 
 &quot;Box of Tricks&quot; at the Hippodrome 
 afterwards. Clyde went back
