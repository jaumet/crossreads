 I have seen better stuff thrown into a piggery. Still we can do nothing, for complaints avail to nothing so we must abide by the decisions of the 'powers that be'. This ship boasts a Canteen which makes about 800% profit on each article. Muk may be obtained for 3shillings per tin and small tins of salmon (very inferior quality) cost us 2shillings and 6pence.These prices are exorbitant and I will starve rather than pay the profiteering crew such amounts.
