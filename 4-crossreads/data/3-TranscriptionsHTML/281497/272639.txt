<p>on us, about a mile further on we passed through a complete tunnel of trees made by the trees planted on either side, &amp; they had completely overlapped one another in the middle it was nicely trimmed &amp; not a ray of sun penetrated this canopy of leaves, it was nearly &frac12; a mile in length &amp; lovely &amp; cool.</p>
<p>The march this morning was a very fierce one, we were unlucky for we had to march with full pack up &amp; the other Battalions were in marching order &amp; some in battle-order, the distance was a little over 9 miles &amp; the boy's were as near done as it is possible for men to be, they just simply threw themselves</p>
