 Saturday 7 August, 1915 
 Visited Gezeirch Hospital saw Lindsay Waugh &amp; Nurse Chisholm. 
 Went to see the &quot;Kursale&quot; a big music hall at Cairo. It was a magnificent programme.&nbsp;You are able to smoke &amp; have drinks brought to you while you sit listerning to the performance.
