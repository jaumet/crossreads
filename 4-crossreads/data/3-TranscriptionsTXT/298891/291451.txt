 Sunday 13 
 Camp routine. Tried to get a sleep but Jean had no mercy. 
 Mon 14 
 Serb N.Y. day party at night for Bolnicar's. Very mild. 
 Tues 15 
 Sister Davis &amp; I went to Vodene &amp; saw Dr Petrovic &amp; Major Popovitch &amp; Bozidan &amp; his commandant. Went on engine returned in 1st class carriage. Warm day. 
 Wed 16 
 Sister Davis left. Warm bright day. Tried to sleep in Pollard's tent. Thurs.17 No. 9. Matron Miss Munn &amp; I went to Vodena. Camp routine
