Major Johnson had given place to Major Kennedy, a straighter & more decent chap Craig was still W.O. but the other N.C.O's were new men.A great number of war babys - under aged boys culled from the battalions, & awaiting the fulfilment of age to go overseas with A.M.C. - were in the camp.
