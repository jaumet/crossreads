<p>8/10/17<br/>Was told today that I am going to Blighty. This is the 18th Gen. [Hos.?].</p>
<p>My arm has not been set yet so there is still a bad time in store.</p>
<p>13/10/17<br/>[indecipherable] England again today and have to parade before the Col. in the morning.</p>
