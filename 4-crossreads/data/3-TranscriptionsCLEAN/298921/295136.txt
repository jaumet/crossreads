
June, 1915Monday 14Sand lice are now plentiful on the Desert & the Tents are in want of Keatings Powder. Sent 100 Pipes to the Reg at Dardanelles - Posted Diary yesterday Not much news these days only Sand & Heat. But it agrees with me! & I am very fit - no colds which since Africa have always been my trouble!
I send 2 Silver Piastres other nickel coins of same value make the coinage hard to follow, hope the Censor does not [indecipherable] to them
