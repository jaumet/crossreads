<p>Dec 27th at 1.30 P.M. we were ordered to proceed to Trinidad (Port of Spain)</p>
<p>Dec 28th at 8 A.M. we arrived and anchored in Port of Spain off the town,- we then coaled oiled, and watered, after which we took up a special pilot and departed at 6 P.M. to an unknown destination, - weather very fine &amp; warm &ndash; Our Captain afterwards informed us that we were proceeding to Caribbean Sea to search islands therein for "Karlsrhue" &ndash; This sounded like a pleasant little enterprise, and we were all hoping that there would be something doing and the weather was very fine and warm</p>
