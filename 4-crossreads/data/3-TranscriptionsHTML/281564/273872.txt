<p>(first half of page covered over)<br/>              <br/>soon marked by a flying cloud. They are fine muscular upstanding men, reaching about 5-10 in in height.<br/>Those which we saw this morning were clad in navy loin cloths held in position by a "Gott mit uns," on which hung two bullet pouches &amp; a saw-bayonet. How our troops envied their light equipment! All were armed with Mauser rifle &amp; for headgear wore a grey-green slouch hat, with the turned up side held in place with the German tricolour.</p>
<p>At 5 oclock we returned again to camp at Herbertshohe.</p>
