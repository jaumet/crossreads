 the water was lovely &amp; very easy to swim in on account of the depth  we used to swim right out to the middle, &amp; let the current carry you about, for you could float quite easily, there would be thousands in here of an evening &amp; we used to have some good fun Often when we were down there a big passenger ship would come through &amp; the passengers would throw all sorts of things out to the boy's &amp; there would be a great scramble in the water for them, the boats travel very slow through the canal the reason why is because if they put the speed on the wash from the ships side would bring the sand
