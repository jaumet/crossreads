<p>Wednesday 1 August 1917</p>
<p>Leg better. I hope the Anzac memorial figure of the L.H. man will be with one hand bandaged for Septic Sores.</p>
<p>Got a temperature &amp; sore throat really thinking of taking a longer leave if I can get it.</p>
<p>Turks Bombing Squadron has arrived as the moon is now full. Tonight should be <strike>exciting</strike> exciting. Another year rolled round &ndash; Aug. 4th I was wounded.</p>
<p><strike>John Ayre went on leave &amp; got into Hospital at Cairo . Gastritis.</strike></p>
