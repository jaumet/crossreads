21/8/15
medical inspection c. Lunch and office. 4 pm Left per gharry with Curnow Thomsen for Cairo. Went to Rue de Mousky and strolled up to Bazaars. Met a Greek funeral. Bearers in front carrying wreaths with ribbons denoting donor. Police in white in front. Greek priests and attendants carrying cloth embossed with cross c held at four corners. Then hearse gold painted. The horses covered with black trappings trimmed with gold braid and plumes in their heads. Top of hearse wooden ornament gold painted. The driver the most comical figure conceivable. A negro Egyptian half caste dressed in 17th century coat breeches
