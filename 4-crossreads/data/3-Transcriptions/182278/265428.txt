<p>Thursday 18.7.18&nbsp; On invitation had supper with M<sup>rs</sup> Lathbury&nbsp; very interesting old lady.</p>
<p>Friday 19.7.18&nbsp; Went to London for try on at tailors, had dinner at Club &amp; then went to &quot;Bing Boys on Broadway&quot;</p>
<p>Saturday 20.7.18&nbsp; Went to A.I.F&nbsp; H.Qrs&nbsp; no news of Ship yet, returned to Farm in afternoon</p>
<p>Sunday 21.7.18.&nbsp; Exercised &quot;Mayfair&quot;&nbsp; had a long talk with Daniel Edwards who is peculiarly English.</p>
