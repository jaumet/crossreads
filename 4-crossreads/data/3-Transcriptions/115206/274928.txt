<p>December  1918.<br/>Thursday 26</p>
<p>Suez Canal, Suez, Red Sea."Karoola"<br/>Passed all the old spots, Gereffe, Shallufa, Kubri, and Gurkha and Baluchistan Posts. Weather ideal a nice cool breeze.<br/>Passed the old "Daphne". Had a yarn with Harry Rich who is on board. Left Suez at 1.35pm and commenced our run down to Colombo where we expect to arrive on or about the 10th.</p>
