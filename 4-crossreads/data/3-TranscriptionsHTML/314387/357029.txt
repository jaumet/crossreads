<p>Monday 9th<br/>Morning attended a machine gun lecture washed equipment for inspection by Colonel for following day<br/>Tuesday 10th<br/>Inspected by the Colonel in the morning also had a Retrospective nightime guard in Transport Lines.<br/>Wednesday 11th<br/>Came off guard six o'clock morning then went to Amiens for the day came home by motor lorry &amp; night.</p>
