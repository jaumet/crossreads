 15 May Wednesday 
 Yesterday changed leather equipment for web  leather  &amp; doing a  do  latrine in morning. 
 Warned for guard in afternoon, &amp; as the best dressed man gets off, spent the hours after tea in cleaning up.  Letter &amp; diary in afternoon. 
 Wainwright of my squad, who is a short tempered crotchety, eccentric slightly unhinged chap, untidy &amp; against most in authority caused great amusement by scoring a new pair
