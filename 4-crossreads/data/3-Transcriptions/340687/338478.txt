<p>June, 1917. Saturday 23<br/>No 1st parade as S.M. hadn't recovered from night before Competition for bridging in morning. We were first on time but were disqualified 2nd Coy &ndash; 1st Coy. 2.30 drill order &ndash; Bonza mail &amp; spent rest of day enjoying it.</p>
