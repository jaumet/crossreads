 Wed Sep 13th Head still bad. 
 Myself &amp; others paraded &amp; left here at 12.30 for Hasebrouck by train arriving at 2 pm. After introductory formalities we were left to our own sweet will so I and another took a stroll up the town to see what it was like. 
 To us it is a little city. I should say about the size of Geelong &amp; is in Flanders. The class of shops are better. There is also a fine cathedral. The people are well dressed &amp; as usual there are troops everywhere. 
 Thurs Sep 14th At the eye hospital we were provided with bell tents and blankets &amp; fed of course. There was not much chance of sleep as beer arguments were rife.
