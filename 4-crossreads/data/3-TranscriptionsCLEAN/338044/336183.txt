I am now well played out & not the only one in that condition. But we can overlook all this in the joy of getting away from Egypt at last.
10th July:- MondayAfter a cramped sleepless night in the train we eventually arrive at Alexandria (wharf) at 6.45 am. Strange to say, I slept soundly from 5.30 am to this time, although fully equipped & in an uncomfortable position.
