 25/5/16 fitting itself up at the expense of WA3 on which station I am left. Take over WA3 &amp; set about knocking it into shape, taking up 8 to 12am &amp; pm 
 26/5/16 Tune up WA3 &amp; get good results. Make the place like home &amp; make sandbag couch. Have a glorious feed with Chatty. Blanc-mange smoked - &amp; a la cocoa flavouring with rice &amp; raisins &amp; marmalade jam &amp; peaches which makes a good salad when liberally covered with condensed milk. Hourly calls with WA1 &amp; WA2 cease. 
 27/5/16 Told that we are to be put off WA3 station &amp; let other operators  take place. No supper  owing to the tanks being dry &amp; no water on Hill 40. Issued with tinted glasses 
 28/5/16 Hot as hell again. Troops leave for the front &ndash; aeroplane hovers over Kantara for two hours dropping smoke bombs as targets for warships at practice. Continual rumble of guns. Attack being prepared for tomorrow 
 29/5/16 Aeroplane again about hanging very low. 
 30/5/16 All quiet ready for the attack. Night time code messages come through referring to the time of attack. 
 31/5/16 Result of attack is 15 dead Turks, 7 capture &amp; 2 of our men wounded. The main body of Turks got away. 
 1/6/16 Turks get back one by dropping a bomb on Romani near the sig office killing Lieut Kerr &amp; wounding Lieuts Braithwate &amp;Smith all of the Signal Squadron. Causes some excitement. 6.30 am enemy aeroplane signal sent out from WA1 reporting plane going west. 
 2/6/16 Girlie  returned to camp wounded inside by piece of bomb. Looks rather sick. WA2 dismantles &amp; moves. 
 A3/6/16 Another stunt being prepared &amp; insistent rumors of France. 
 4/6/16 Dust storm that nearly chokes us. Feeling very crook &ndash; overeating I think as Chatty &amp; I have had four eggs each each meal, besides the usual fruit &amp; custard.
