  
 Received your letter from Farm - Great Western Railway 1st Class carriage with 5 sisters - 11.30 am  - July 18, 1915 [indecipherable] love to Father & you  (1)   My own beloved Mother - at last we are off - I am so glad - it has really been very trying waiting when our work is calling to us. I hope to get a chance to post this to you. I write on the back of Gibson's last letter to me - all the paper I have - as my suitcase is in the van. I hope I get it tonight on the ship? Where we are going to get out of the train to embark - I do not know. The stamp on this may tell you - we go to Lemnos as far as I can tell - all our kit is for that place. Mosquitoes & heat.
