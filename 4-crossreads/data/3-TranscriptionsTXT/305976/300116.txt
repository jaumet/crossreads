From Colombo to Fremantle we had good weather. What a difference it was coming home to what it was going. Going there was plenty of work &amp; coming home we had practically none. Between Colombo &amp; Fremantle we were inoculated against Influenza. We were fortunate to have the Flying Corp Concert party on board &amp; they gave some good Concerts.
