
10 Th MayAs usual on front with Aeroplanes very busy with bombs  Aus mail inletter from D. N.G. also writing all lights out at night
11 Fri Maynot feeling too well had a terrible job to finish letters  Usual on frontlarge quantities of stores being removed back.  Big attack expected
12 Sat MayUsual on front  Artillery very active  two Divisions arrived and camped at Khan Yunus  Keeping very warm and dusty
