<p>SATURDAY, JANUARY 4, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Knocking round London</p>
<p>with Clyde in the morning</p>
<p>and went to &quot;Hello America&quot;</p>
<p>in the afternoon. It was a</p>
<p>pretty good show. Had</p>
<p>dinner at Hatchett&#39;s &amp; came</p>
<p>to the flat afterwards.</p>
