
Sunday 29 July 1917.
Reveille 7 a.m. Planes kept us awake from 4 a.m. Still as we were tired we appreciated the extra rest.
Egyptian troops drilling in our troops camp. Muster parade & lecture by Lt Colonel. 25% got leave.
Harry Flavell & I went to 14 AGH. Saw Ted Holden (Sgt), Tom de Gruchy, Chas Hirsefield, Jim Tatterson. Heard that [blank] McKinnon has been mentioned in despatches.
