
Saturday 27 October 1917.
Jacko over again but dropped no bombs on our position.
On picquet all day.
Received moving orders at 2 pm & at 4.30 pm moved from Khalasa. Night came on & Turks & our Light Horse engaged Turks on our right while the Imperial Camel Brigade moved out and the Light Horse Brigade moved in. The Camel Brigade were the first troops to occupy Khalasa. The Turks had evacuated it 3 weeks previously & up to this time it had been patrolled by Aust.Lht Hrs patrol.
Moved all night till midnight when a thunderstorm caught us. Halted & stood to till dawn.
