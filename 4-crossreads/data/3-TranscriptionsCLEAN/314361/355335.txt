  
 the battalion No 2. Signallers Will is now here. Arrived  Wedne  Thursday last. He is doing & looking well. Wednesday 15. 3.  6 Tel-el-Kebir Monday we were inoculated again, for Typhoid I think it was this time. Have taken some very interesting pictures of the place round camp, the camp is on a canal & near the village of Tel-el-Kebir. On the Canal there is quite a lot of traffic in the way of dhows & bargey looking vessels. I was reading in this mornings paper that there are 4,000,000 acres of land under cultivation through these canals, & there is a scheme where with another 2000000 acres could be dealt with. Tel-el-Kebir Tuesday 21st 1916 Still in this place & having a very easy time. Last Friday I went to Cairo.
