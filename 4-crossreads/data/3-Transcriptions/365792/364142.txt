<p>Feb 16 D---- it I say. Every 2nd day is suffish. Wrote couple of letters. Another storm.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  [in margin] M.I.</p>
<p>17 Tent O today. Couple of boys gone to Cairo on leave. No pay yet &amp; am absolutely stony. Have to turn puttees other way More rain &ndash; have<br/>joined Grenadiers &ndash; sick of squad drill. Some chaps trying to grow Mo's [in margin] Mill, Eily</p>
<p>18  This chick is MO today. No pay yet.  Plenty of drill saluting by Nco's etc. Paid 100 pt this afternoon. Went pictures Good show.</p>
<p>19 Bomb throwing today. Not a bad job. Shifted tents again only 10 men a tent now. Rec'd 4 letters. Believe going to Ismalia shortly. Mail dated July &amp; Aug.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   [in margin] R</p>
<p>20 Inspection today. Big Russian Victory in Caucasus; Turks mucked up. Renno paid me 50 pt. Old hands returned from England.<br/>On fatigue putting up O.Cs tent then imshied. Lord - speaks at Y.M. <strike>today</strike>  [in left margin]  <u>Sunday</u>, [in right margin] Ess</p>
