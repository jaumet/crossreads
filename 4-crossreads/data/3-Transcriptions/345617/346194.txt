<p>Septembre 29<br/>Camped at the Butt last night &amp; were heavily shelled during the night. We are engaged in placing wire &amp; plotting it in on the map for the information of the incoming troops.<br/>The shelling is still very heavy on our positions. Our artillery is also very active on the enemy positions.</p>
<p>30-12-17<br/>Tommy troops are relieving us tonight. Lt Wagstaffe-Sap[indecipherable] &amp; I surveyed the track back from the Butt to Chateau Wood. The shelling is still very heavy. We have returned H.Q. &amp; do not expect to go out onto this Front for awhile again.</p>
