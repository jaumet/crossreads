<p>At the Y.M.C.A. we then had tea, which consisted of biscuits &amp; preserved fruit, eaten by <strike>mea</strike> aid of the fingers, &amp; tea, drunk from the fruit tin when it was empty.</p>
<p>June 20.    On account of the mid-day heat, we rise at 4:30 here &amp; cease drill for the day at 11.   Being on 'light duty' I did nothing during these hours.</p>
<p>In the afternoon Alan and I walked about 2 kilos to the Railway town of Achiet-le-grand and commenced our business there by visiting the Expeditionary Force Canteen.   After that we went strawberry hunting, no altogether without success, although I am afraid many of our finds were not as ripe as they might have been.   We visited the motor transport repair</p>
