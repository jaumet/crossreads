We do very little work Our Officer is a new hand like myself a number of the men including the Sgt are old hands &amp; the way they pinch stuff &ndash; material for the job is a revelaition to him An order of any sort is very seldom required</p>
<p>Sunday 28th<br/>Went with O.P.D (other Protestant Denomination) Church Parade A good sermon Text "Man know Thyself" A good straight talk to Men just what is wanted as a straight clean moral life is not very fashionable with us.<br/>Detailed for Flying Picquet leave camp at 8.45 &amp; patrol the Warminster road untill 11 PM to catch Sandbaggers &amp; bring the p'runts home</p>
