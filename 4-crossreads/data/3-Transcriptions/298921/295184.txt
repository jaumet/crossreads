<p>August, 1915<br/>Sunday 1<br/>Here we are on board the Knight Templar <strike>1500</strike> of us packed anyhow. Men just throw themselves down anywhere I have nothing but what I stand up in, but all is well &ndash;<br/>Saw Cuth Fetherston for a minute last night at a riot in the slums of Cairo <strike>the soldiers burnt 5 houses 6 stories high &amp; smashed up 6 others. We call it the battle of the WAZZA.</strike></p>
