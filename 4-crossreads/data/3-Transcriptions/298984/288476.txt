<p>Saturday 26thj<br/>There is quite a swell on this evening &amp; we are rooling about as much as any time on the trip  Am on Picquet. The fresh water tap forward is my post. The only tap I know is at out table so am doing my job well Religion is the main subject under discusion to night, judging from the language the opponents know more of blasphemy</p>
<p>Sunday 27th Rotenest Isle</p>
