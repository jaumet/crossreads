<p>after staying there inverness for three days during which time met Joe and had every evening together very plesent and enjoyable company.  we met on the first night we arrived in Inverness and on tuesday 1st/2/16 Joe came with me to the station and bid me good bye and was sorry I was leaving and in fact I was too, but that day at 10AM left for Aberdeen a six hours run in the train which passed through some beautiful country a the sun was shining made it look almost at its best though it was very cold but I thought what a beautiful place Scotland is, and at 5.30 arrived at Aberdeen.
