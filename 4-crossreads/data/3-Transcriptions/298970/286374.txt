<p>off duty.  Stan Wilson, my particular friend in the Ambulance and who carried with me in our first engagement today got shell shock in the trenches to the right of Wye Farm.  The Regimental Aid Post was blown in by high explosives and a party had to dig themselves out.</p>
<p>12th August (Saturday)</p>
<p>Digging grass from round the wards all the morning.  In afternoon marched to Sailly for a bath and rode back on motor lorries.</p>
<p>13th August (Sunday)</p>
<p>On Water Piquet.  Went with Gillies to a village behind Doulieu and to Estaires in the afternoon</p>
