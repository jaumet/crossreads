 Tuesday 18/4/16 Attached to Fifth Division which consists of 8th, 14 & 15 Brigades & is one of the two Divisions which form the 2nd A & NZ, Army Corps.  The Artillery attached is equipped with 60 guns, 48 of which are 18 pounders & 12 are 4.2 Howitzers.  Struck tents for the purpose of airing the ground on which they are pitched.  Received parcel from home containing Diary & Ink Pellets. 
 Wednesday 19/4/16 Route march morning & afternoon.  Rather a very quiet & uneventful day.
