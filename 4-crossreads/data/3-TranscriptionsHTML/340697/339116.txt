<p>Had some practice with bombs today. Gen Walker watched the manouvre. Heavy bombardment last night lasting several hours 26.6.16</p>
<p>More practice today. Expect to raid tonight. Signallers duties in raid is to keep up communication. Our weapons are a bomb in each pocket and a nullanulla. The nulla nulla is made out of cog wheel on the end of an entrenching tool handle 28.6.16</p>
<p>We raided the enemies</p>
