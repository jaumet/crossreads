<p>Here as elsewhere Death stalked &ndash; four of my comrades passed out within a few hours of each other &ndash; an inert mass covered with the Union Jack is borne away &ndash; thus, one by one, they passed into the Infinite, leaving behind a name that shall ever ring glorious.</p>
<p>As I look into the distant future when the sound of guns is but an echo of the past, in grand array shall I see the spirits of these my comrades marching past, who in the greatness of their souls have handed to future generations a fuller, deeper meaning of the word Patriotism.</p>
<p>Signaller Ellis  Silas<br/>1916</p>
