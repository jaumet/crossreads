<p>BEURE<br/>Friday 19<br/>Oh dearie me aint it cold my hair is all icicles<br/>Saturday 20<br/>All the ponds are frozen over I slipt on my dignity<br/>Sunday 21<br/>Ice a foot thick in the well and the fire's out<br/>Im very dirty and almost afraid to have a wash</p>
