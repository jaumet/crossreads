
Sunday 9 April 1916No leave again.
Monday 10 April 1916Mail in.  One from Bess & Os Merrett.  None from Home.
Tuesday 11 April 1916Leave all day starts.
Wednesday 12 April 1916Donkey ride, Helouan to Memphis.  Tea at Anzac Hostel & Concert.  Billy, Ellis & I twice across the Nile.
Thursday 13 April 1916Dust storm No. 1.  No Treatment afternoon.  Dust everywhere & in everything.
Friday 14 April 1916Wrote Roy, Marie.  Received one from Marie.  Papers from Dad.  Went to Heliopolis for milk & meat paste & matches.  Poor jelly died in Ice Chest.
Saturday 15 April 1916Pay Parade.  Met Italian Consul's daughter & L. McLean.  Boys gave show at Anzac Hostel & played No. 3 Aux. Hosp. Heliopolis cricket, 188 to 90 lost.
