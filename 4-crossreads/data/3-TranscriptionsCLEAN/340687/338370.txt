
March, 1917. Wednesday 7Day cloudy & cold strong S.E. wind. Set off about 8.30 with 3 blankets arrived at Tricourt at 12 & in very draughty huts with broken bunks. Freezed again. Got billet mobile & played bridge in evening until bedtime. Rumours of moving about 11th to Poperingle & waiting here till division comes out.
