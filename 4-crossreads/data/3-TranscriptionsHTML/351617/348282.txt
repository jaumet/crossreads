<p>when we left for the Sydney Show Ground to catch our boat on the 9th (the following day). All the way from the Central Station to the <strike>Show</strike> Domain the Roads was lined with people &amp; we had all the difficulty in the world to get through as everybody seemed to want to shake hands or kiss us goodbye.</p>
<p>When we Reached the Domain we were sorted out &amp; lined up on parade &amp; reviewed by Col. Ramachotti the District Commodant who made a speech wishing us the "Best of Luck" Etc &amp; A safe Return &amp; numerous other things about nothing at all. I was while on this parade that I caught sight of my mother on the edge of the crowd &amp; succeeded in attracting her attention.</p>
