
10.  Books in to the Night Matron wardmaster.I have nothing more to write, Dad, Mum & George.  I have a letter here from Cyril Whitehead who is Southall Hospital near London.  He must be alright again.I must go out for a run now.  It is only 7.30 pm but it is quite light till 9.30 pm.Give my Regards to all the Neighbours to Otto & the Club Boys   We are doing well & having
