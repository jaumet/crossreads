Paddy Costello. --- Auntie Marie - with little Baptiste girl ----  -----] There was no phone to attend to, but we were so far away from Batt H.Q. that nobody bothered about us.  A sergeant came one day to get some men for fatigue, but as we had a good view of the road for half a kilo, when he arrived, the billet was empty (most unfortunate). 
 Apl 4 On the night of 4 April Faddy told me that my Paris leave had come through.
