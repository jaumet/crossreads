<p>1916 &nbsp; SATURDAY 22 &nbsp; JULY</p>
<p>A nice warm day. The place seems deserted with all the fellows on leave. I was on cleaners fatigue, had to clean out the Sergeants hut. Had a game of cricket in afternoon. Got a letter from Mother to say she would wait for me at the Hotel.</p>
