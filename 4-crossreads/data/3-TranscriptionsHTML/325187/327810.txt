<p>[Sunday 5 March 1916]</p>
<p><strike>I got a Teapot from Laura &amp; some Handkies- &amp; more Woollen Soks-</strike><br/>It is quite hot now &amp; about 102&deg;  today.<br/>Our horses are doing well but we have trouble to keep them shod, no coal in Egypt &amp;  rotten iron in shoes-<br/>No fighting only patrol work but one never knows from day to day what our Desert friends are doing-<br/>[Following paragraph is crossed out]<br/>If anyone asks you what to send me as per Mrs W- a fountain pen would be most acceptable.  Posted notes to Deniliquin yesterday, but am glad you and Day are in Melbourne-   Regards to all friends</p>
