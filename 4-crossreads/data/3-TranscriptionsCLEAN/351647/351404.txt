
I think I'll try & arrange to go over there & live & work.  This open air stunt in this climate is a bit much for me.
I am living in a tent with Alleyne Edols (one of the Burrawang clan), he belongs to my old battery & they are next door to us.  H.Q. sleeping quarters are rather crowded & a bit off so when Alleyne who is rather an old pal of mine offered me the shelter of his domicile I rushed it.
