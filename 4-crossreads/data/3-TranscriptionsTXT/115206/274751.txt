
June 1918Sunday 30
Poulonville. - On road and at Bty Posit.Pulled into School yard at 3.50 am. After a farily solid trip all night. The road Palmer, Evayling and Garfott to Bty position 1.25 C.We proceeded at 8 pm for Battery position with the guns.Arrived at position at 2.30am 1/6/18Just arrived when we received two salvoes of whizz bangs. 1 horse killed and Cpl Kelly slightly wounded - Mr Koens horse also slightly wounded.
