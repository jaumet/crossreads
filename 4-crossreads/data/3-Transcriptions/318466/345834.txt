<p>Sunday 13 October 1918<br/>Went to the Wards to tea &amp; on to Sam Hoare afterwards.  Induced Rup to come home by tube.</p>
<p>Monday 14 October 1918<br/>Into Passports office &amp; left applcn. for our p.p.  Lunched at Galvin's on my own.  Tead with Aunty Lu.  Home to dinner &amp; go to bed crook.</p>
<p>Tuesday 15 October 1918<br/>Woke up with 'flu &ndash; feelg. putrid.  Stayed in bed &amp; Rup nursed me.  Had to cancel all this week's engagements.</p>
<p>Wednesday 16 October 1918<br/>Still crook, fed up.  Hang the 'flu &ndash; hope it dosn't put back our sailing date.</p>
<p>Thursday 17 October 1918<br/>Same as 16th.</p>
<p>Friday 18 October 1918<br/>Had the Doc. along.  He's too cheerful.</p>
<p>Saturday 19 October 1918<br/>Muriel came up from Worthing.<br/>Get the 'flu this week of all weeks!!  Feelg. putrid.</p>
