<p>well sprung &amp; up to any devilment    All the estaminet closed early reckoned they ran out of drink, but I think the boys put the wind up them but the funniest part of all took place later on in the evening, we were having a quiet game of cards when Aitchson a batman came amp; started a row he can fight a bit &amp; he started on some of the quieter chaps &amp; wouldn't stop, things were getting very willing when up jumped Lindsay Graham our Sgt. Major &amp; ordered him to clear out, but this made him worse &amp; he wanted to get into him, so without any more ado Lindsay knocked him rotten &amp; he fell on across Prices blanket which we were playing on &amp; the blood started to run over it, Price</p>
