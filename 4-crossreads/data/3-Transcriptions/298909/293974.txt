<p>units were congratulated in having carried out its work successfully &ndash; we were supposed to have done our part very quickly &amp; got credit accordingly. It was awfully hot &ndash; tearing about in the desert with stretchers &ndash; we also had the Camels out with <strike>stretchers</strike> catalecs attached. Got back to Camp about 12 noon &amp; had a good old swim. The whole thing was an eye opener to me to see two <u>Divisions</u> complete &ndash; it was just men &ndash; men horses &amp; men everywhere &ndash; I daresay there must have been some 38,000 men mustered.</p>
<p>7/5/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Terribly hot day, hardly a breeze. I went off to first Mass &amp; Communion &ndash; got back in here to bolt a piece of bread &amp; just fall in for General Church parade &ndash; so I had to go to 8.30 Mass again. Were busy all day with men or malingerers going before the Medical board &ndash; most of them were ordered back to their units.
