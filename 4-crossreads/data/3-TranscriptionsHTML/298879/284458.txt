<p>October 1915<br/>Wednesday 27<br/>Windy Reveille at 5 AM, breakfast at 6 AM, and moved off at 6.30 AM. We marched to the <u>RE PIER</u> waited about two hours embarked on a large steamer and then transferred to the Osmanich. a fine passenger ship now used for transporting stores, troops and wounded to their destination.</p>
<p>Thursday 28<br/>Dull. Still lying in harbour. Our Batt is situated in the 1st class dining saloon on the ship. The boys amuse themselves by playing card games such as; Nap banker, five hundred, euchure and whist.</p>
