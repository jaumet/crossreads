 Monday 3 December 
 Frosty &ndash; all water is frozen &ndash; Bitterly cold. Went by Amb. Car to Dranoutre and walked the remainder of the distance (9 kilos) to the workshops. Worked all day cleaning the engine of the Triumph Cycle.  Returned to camp at night per motor lorries passing through Locre &amp; Kemmel One of the houses in Bailleul was last night levelled to the ground by a German bomb
