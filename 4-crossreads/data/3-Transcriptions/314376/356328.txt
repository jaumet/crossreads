<p>I suffered from tonsillitis on the voyage and was admitted into the sick-bay of the ship. There I came in contact with the Y.M.C.A. representatives &ndash; a Scotchman, I think named Clark. I remember him telling me he knew Bobbie Sinclair of Petersham (Syds friend). Was a very quiet chap, cleanly and methodical in his habits.
