<p>Today passing Peronne &amp; Doigt &amp; camping at Courcelles</p>
<p>Mon Sept. 9th:-<br/>Marched through Tincourt &amp; Marquaix &amp; billeted in hut sat side of Roisel</p>
<p>Tues Sept 10th:-<br/>Moved from Roisel &amp; relieved 39th bn in front tonight. Company not stopping at their position but is going more forward by about 1000 yds &amp; digging in a new position.<br/>Lts Malone &amp; Perkins wounded by shell this afternoon.</p>
<p>Wed Sept 11th:-<br/>D Coy &amp; A Coy went at "Jerry" at 5.30am<br/>Struck strong opposition in parts. C.S.M. Stone, Howarth, Wootten &amp; McCormack among the killed</p>
<p>Thurs Sept 12th:-<br/>Line companies had to move back a bit yesterday afternoon owing to flanks not being up. Moved over with C Company from Hervilly Wood to behind Hesbecourt</p>
<p>Fri Sept 13th:-<br/>C Coy relieved D Coy early this morning &amp; moved to front of Hesbecourt. Moved further along road in afternoon &amp; took up position</p>
