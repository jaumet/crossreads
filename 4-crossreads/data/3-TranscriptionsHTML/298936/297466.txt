<p>November, 1915</p>
<p>Monday 8</p>
<p>Beach dressing station.</p>
<p>Working all day &amp; part of the night, although I felt very ill.</p>
<p>Move on the left side in progress.</p>
<p>Beachy landed shell (from howitzer) clean down the hold of barge at pier opposite one dressing station.</p>
<p>One killed &amp; two  wounded one simply cut to pieces.</p>
