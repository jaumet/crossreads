<p>WESTBECOURT<br/>Friday 20<br/>Just a little luck for I won 100 Francs on four king's<br/>Off to Amiens in the morning<br/>Saturday 21<br/>Now dining at the Grand Hotel De La Paix Amiens some style I can tell you<br/>Sunday 22<br/>A perfect day, just like an Australian spring time, got a new shirt on<br/>Do I think we are winning well to speak the truth I am buggared if I know</p>
