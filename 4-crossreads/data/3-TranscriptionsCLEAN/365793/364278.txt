
June 28 this morning, & was put on QM's fatigue, but did nothing - just fell out & went to Medical Hut. Am applying for leave tomorrow but don't know how I'll get on Several chaps were boarded today, some for Australia, & others for home Service.
28  On QM's fatigues again this morning. Am taking on hut orderly tomorrow, as it's a damn good job. Cold as blazes; just like winter in fact. Cleaned up a few things, but nicked away at 10 oclock - presumably to see the Quack. As it happened the Doc did see me, & said i'd have
