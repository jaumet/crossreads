Saturday 10thWas mess orderly.  No parade today.  Lines were thoroughly cleaned & disinfected.
Sunday 11thGot away fairly early in the morning on 3 days leave, & caught the 11 oclock train for Port Said arriving there at 2.30pm.  Went to the Continental Hotel, had dinner, went out for a good walk around the town.  All the inhabitants seemed to be in the streets, & they it all seemed quite gay.  Bands & orchestras played outside caf&#233;s & the streets were full of soldiers.  Didn't get to bed until quite late.
