[Transcribers note;  indecipherable insert, not clear as to where it should be inserted] That night 9/10th the Division retired to Oghratina. Next day the three LH Brigades took up positions  &  watched the Wily Turk; there was a little shelling but no real fighting. We had our Bivouac at Hod el Kirba about 2 miles to the left of Dubarbos. Patrols were sent out; the remainder did nothing. Immense stores were abandoned at this place, the Turks evidently used it as an advance depot cases of dried apricot compressed into strips like brown paper
