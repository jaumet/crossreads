I was down at the sea last week on my afternoon of we cycled down & had a dip. the sea was rough & a high wind blowing & to put a finish on it the rain came on in torrents & we had no coats & no shelter there are lots of young officers in training down there & they had all to have a talk with us in all the rain they did not seem to think anything about it for a soldier I suppose he has a lot of that to come through & we did not get enough as we fully intended going down again today only it was so wet & it had to be put off for another week.  The Egyptian mail yesterday brought us another letter from John & he is still well & bright he is looking forward to the winter
