 Thursday 21 September 1916 Had a look round the town in the morning and bought a few things.  Sent a parcel home to Mother containing broaches, etc.  Went to Empire Club, had swim and afternoon tea, then went to picture show but did not think much of it so came back and went to bed.  Had fortune told.  Met Miss Huxtible, was introduced by Bay. 
 Friday 22 September 1916 Up early and caught train back to Mohomedya [possibly Mahemdia].  Rode over here. Put on Brigade Guard in evening at Head Qrs. dump.
