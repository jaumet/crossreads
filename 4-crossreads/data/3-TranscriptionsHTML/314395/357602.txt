<p>brigade was well into it.  I also hear that they are now six-gun batteries instead of four which will mean a good deal more in my line of business as I will have to keep communication to 3 sections instead of two.  I hear that Sgt. Gibson is back with the battery.  You may remember him coming home wounded from Gallipoli.</p>
<p>A new division is being formed here in England, with much secrecy &ndash; mystery, it is not yet known, where they are for, but so far no artillery have been called.  I hope to be back with the old crowd, before I get collared for them.</p>
