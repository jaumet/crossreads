30th December 1916 (cont) guns horses picketed horses till late after tea. Left the wharf at 8 oclock for some camp 3 miles away. Battery went column of through Havre. The clatter of the horses guns on the cobbles made a great row. arrived at the camp at 9.30 and picketed horses guns got to sleep for only 4 hours. Reveille 4 and away down to Havre station to entrain for our destination.
31st December 1916. New Years Eve and on Havre station at 8.30. All guns horses we this train at 11 and left at 1120 for our destination wherever that might be. Slow old train. Arrived at 70 miles from Havre at 5.
31st December 1916. and watered horses and got some rations. Through the day we passed hospi tal trains with a lot of Australians aboard. They looked very comfortable. At 8 20p went to bed on the floor of the carriage. It looks comfortable after the lack of sleep of the last few days.
