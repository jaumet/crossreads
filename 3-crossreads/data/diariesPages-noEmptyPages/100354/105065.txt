After a slice of bread quince jam for dinner we stood up in the trench to listen for shells to gaze disconsolately at the bleak desolate ash heap of Geuedecourt. One of the team was struck in the stomach by a piece of shrapnel from a whizz-bang. This relieved the monotony a little. At tea time comes hot stew from reserve. It was brought up by a fatigue party of about 20- strong. Fritz enjoyed himself wonderfully sniping at the party accounting for one casualty. The primus stove which had reducedrefused duty all day was at least coaxed into action a dixie of coffee milk (Nestles best Milkmaid Brand) was made.
