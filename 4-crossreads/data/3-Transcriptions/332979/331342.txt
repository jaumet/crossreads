<p>served around &amp; then the 2nd half of the programe was commenced. As there still more gents than Ladies they were allowed to Dance together &amp; by that way everybody had an enjoyable evening. The Ball terminated at 11 Pm &amp; all hands made their way back to their quarters.</p>
<p>Friday 13/4/17.<br/>As I was Orderly Corporal for the Day I was kept busy with the work attached to that position. I had to parade the Sick to the Doctor also march the Mess Orderlies to the Cook House at the Meal times. I had to stop in the Orderly Room after Tea until Bed time.</p>
