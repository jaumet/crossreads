<p>ARQUES<br/>Friday 10<br/>A twilight march through wonderful woodland's. Our camp by the canal condemned shifting further out<br/>Saturday 11<br/>Woke up this morning with "Christ" at the old monastry of "Clairmarais" situated in a dense forest. Raining again<br/>Sunday 12<br/>Living on a farm another such place as Carnsdale the old stables ect recall other day's and other face's<br/>A new week beginning still along way from home<br/>Just saddled "Nutty" for a long night ride<br/>Major Fussell off to BR.H.Q</p>
