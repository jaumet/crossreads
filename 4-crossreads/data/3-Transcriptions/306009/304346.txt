<p>1915<br/>June 9 Wed.<br/>Staff Sgt. Statt &amp; Sgt. Middleton from Mena became attached to our Administration Office to-day &ndash; trifle condensed with 5 of us but expect S.S. Statt will continue to act as Private Sec. to the O.C.<br/>To-day issued with 2 khaki drill uniforms &ndash; supposed to be made to measure but fit awful  <strike>Descended like an avalanch</strike></p>
