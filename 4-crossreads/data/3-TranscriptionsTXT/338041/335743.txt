 Oct 24th We leave Pilques in very bad weather and after a tiresome march we entrain at 7pm at town of Arques &ndash; spend night in train. 
 Oct 25th &amp; 26th End our journey &amp; leave train at 5a.m. in the village of St. Riquier &ndash; Breakfast on station and march to Ergines. Billeted in Farmhouse barn &ndash; "Apples to burn" &ndash; Episode of the Two Fowls". 
 Oct 27th Reveille at 4.30a.m. Another miserable march &ndash; We motor in French Transports for about 80 kilos, passing through Flixecourt &amp; Amiens &amp; completing our journey in the vicinity of Albert. Frightful Billets - Winter with a vengeance. 
 Oct. 28th. Signs of recent destruction in our village - Respirator drill - Referendum in Australia
