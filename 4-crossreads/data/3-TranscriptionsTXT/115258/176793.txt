 ABP.O.&nbsp; 11.1.18&nbsp; Kantara 
 Dear Pal&nbsp; Just a line to explain the reason why you have had your mail sent to the 67th Flying Squadron.&nbsp; On the 2.12.17 I was notified that you had been transferred to that Core &amp; since then I have heard that you were still in the Regiment &amp; your mail is being sent to the Reg. now. Will you please write to  the  me &amp; let me know if you were ever at the 67th FS &amp; whether you have received any Parcels lately.
