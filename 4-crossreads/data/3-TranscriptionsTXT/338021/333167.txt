 Lt Weston You can tell your men they will be relieved tomorrow. Get everything in order &amp; check all stores preparatory to handing over. The people who relieve you will bring only guns &amp; spare parts wallet, you will take the same out. Careful receipts for everything must be kept. Rum will be sent up later on to-day when it arrives.
