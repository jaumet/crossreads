<p>Visited a few old friends and also went down to the Factory and had a talk with a few old acquaintances.</p>
<p>They seem to think that I am looking very well, rather a compliment to me I think. Went to the pictures with Jane, in the evening.</p>
<p>Wednesday Nov 29th.</p>
<p>Much to my surprise D Zatt arrived home on leave. Little Euphemia I, taken to hospital with Fever.</p>
<p>Went out to minister's for afternoon tea.</p>
<p>Nov 30th Thurs.<br/>Went for a stroll with Jane and in the evening went to the pictures with J. I. D. M.</p>
<p>Dec 1st Fri<br/>Had a look round the book shops buying a few books to take back with me.</p>
<p>Rather a coincidence took place, lying on the dresser at home was a book entitled Gallipoli, naturally I picked it up and what should I see but a full page photograph of the North Beach, Anzac, taken from our dug-out.</p>
<p>Dec 2nd. Sat.<br/>Went to Edinburgh with D. D. A. J. and Father.</p>
<p>We separated at Edinburgh. J, myself and Father went to Prestonpans.</p>
<p>Dec 3rd. Sun.<br/>Went to Church. In the evening spent a few hours with Mr Steele and Mr Christie.</p>
<p>In the evening after Church I went a walk with Ina.</p>
