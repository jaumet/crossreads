<p>18. Thursday.<br/>Went to Minerva &amp; waited at table. Home about 4 &amp; did some washing &amp; typing. Fine day but showers in evening.</p>
<p>19. Friday.</p>
<p>Typed all day at home. Heavy showers all day. A.A,S. came home in evening. Depressed &amp; worried &ndash; money running out.</p>
<p>20. Saturday.<br/>Rain all day again. Went to Lady Byles for lunch met there Mrs Brook &amp; Mrs D</p>
