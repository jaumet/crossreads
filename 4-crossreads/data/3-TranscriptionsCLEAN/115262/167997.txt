
Saturday 18 August 1917.
Reveille 3.45 am. Blankets kit etc. loaded on wagon 4.10 am. Breakfast 4.15. Fall in with equipment & dressed in puttees & shorts & drill tunics at 5 am. Check roll call. Marched to Abbassia tram. Boarded tram & arrived Cairo Rly Stn. at 6.15 Unloaded baggage & entrained 7 a.m. Arrived Zsgazig at 8.25. Tel El Kabir 9.45. Ismailia 10.45. Kantara 11.20. Disentrained & marched to Rest camp 2 miles & had small lunch.
Had a little look round Kantarra camp. Hundreds of troops preparing to move out tonight for firing line. A mere snack for tea & slept on sand till 10 p.m. Kits put on M.T. wagon at 10.40 pm. In marching order fell in & marched to Kantara East Stn. Placed in trucks (open) & 30 to 1 truck & train moved off at 11.25 p.m. Threw our gear down & in an uncomfortable & tangled heap tried to sleep but it was impossible. Train jolted & bumped all through the night.
