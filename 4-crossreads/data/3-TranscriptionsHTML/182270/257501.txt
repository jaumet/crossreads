<p>TUESDAY, JULY 8, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Round Boundary &amp; Mountain</p>
<p>in morning &amp; picking up in the</p>
<p>Middle Paddock in afternoon.</p>
<p>Tickle took waggon down to</p>
<p>Walhallow &amp; brought back the</p>
<p>cut timber for the mill. Left it at</p>
<p>the bore &amp; brought waggon up to</p>
<p>Station and the &quot;Pop&quot; with it.</p>
<p>Percy George doing the Boundary</p>
<p>troughs again.</p>
