We still continued our simple life all this time the weather was getting hotter&nbsp;&amp; the canal saw us very often having a bathe in its cool waters, all good things come to an end so we said good bye to the canal &amp; Indians on Feb 26th after spending 2 1/2 months in that quiet but pleasant spot. We entrained on the light railway &amp; later on to the larger trains &amp; arrived at Tel-el-Kebir 7.30 same day.
