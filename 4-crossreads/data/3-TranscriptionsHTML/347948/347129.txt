<p>17th  Went to Bulford, another small town 2 miles from Camp.<br/>Am struck by the flint houses.  Some have 1 foot of flint work and 3 courses of Bricks or large blocks of stone.  Flint bedded in stone like concrete.   [sketch of brick/flint wall]<br/>Others are completely built of flint.<br/>Also there are innumerable houses with thatched roofs and large overhanging eaves.</p>
