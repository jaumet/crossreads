 [Photograph in left margin of Charleroi with troops] 1919 May 4 to Charleroi, putting up at the rest billets next to the station.  Don &amp; I did not want to sleep there much, so took our equipment up to the town, &amp; got a room in an hotel.  Many others did the same.  Some did not take their gear, &amp; had the pleasure in the morning of finding some of it gone, when they returned. 
 May 5 We paraded at 7.30 am on 5th May.  A lot of our Belge friends had come in from Marcinelle, - Rachel, fat Helene looking as though her next baby was imminent &amp; many others.  Rachel wept over Private Close. 
 [Photo of the troops including Rachel &amp; Don McKinnon] I bought a little bangle made of centime pieces as a souvenir.  We boarded the train, having plenty of room, were given a good stock of "comforts" &amp; at 9 am started on the first part of our long journey back to Australia.
