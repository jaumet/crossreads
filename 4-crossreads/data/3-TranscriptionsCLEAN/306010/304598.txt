A lift takes you up 52 metres but a ladder can be climbed until an altitude of 74 metres is reached. A magnificent view is obtainable from the top - over the busy city  with the mountains in the background, and across the harbour with its Casino & shipping. After going down the lift again, I crossed to the other side by a pontoon affair suspended by chains from the framework, which runs too & fro for passenger & vehicular traffic.
