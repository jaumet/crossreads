 finished up through Calais to Audruicq : into huts &amp; cleaned ourselves up : 
 Saturday March 30th 
 Audruicq : cleaned up, washing etc during day : had a walk round the town after tea &amp; it is a decent joint : 
 Sunday March 31st 
 Audruicq : H.Comm in morn &amp; very nice : stroll round in afternoon &amp; after tea &amp; enjoyed it 
 Monday April 1st 
 Audruicq : nothing much doing : paid 20 francs in afternoon : went tom theatre with Gill after tea &amp; it was a good show too : lady impersonaters very good &amp; one had a splendid voice into the bargain : air raid at night :
