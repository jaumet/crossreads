<p>Aug.  15  On guard all day.   Pay of 40 F.</p>

A "bull-ring" has now been improvised for us in an old quarry about 3 kilos from the billets &amp; here we were put through a series of trench-bombing-raids, including reliefs of bombing sections &amp; platoons.
