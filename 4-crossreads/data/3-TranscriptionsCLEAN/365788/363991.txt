  
 CARTE POSTALE [Red rubber stamp] PASSED FIELD CENSOR 2876 [Black rubber stamp] FIELD POST OFFICE 9 SP 16 F.M. Phillips My dear little Margaret You will see a little house with a cross marked above it. - That is where I lived for two days & it was quite a nice little village. I hope you are a comfort to your dear Daddy & Mummy. With much love from Uncle Fred 
 Miss Margaret Read, "The Wurley" Cleveland Street Wahroonga Sydney New South Wales Australia
