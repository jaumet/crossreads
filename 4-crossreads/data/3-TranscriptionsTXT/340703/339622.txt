 April 1916 
 Friday 14 Sunrise most gorgeous no wonder they write of the wonderful colourings of Egypt. Left Suez 2pm for Tel el Kebir, some in cattle trucks . Arrived at 8pm. (in the desert). Moonlight Marched 1&frac12; miles to our camp  very chilly 
 Saturday 15 Had a look at the desert. Bare barren and sandy. Les Spragg,Tom Reid, Reg King, Barrett and Montring to see me. Vic Masters also
