 Christmas Day Dec 25th 
 Great preparations were made for a good time in camp, and two large tents were put up together and tables and forms were borrowed and we had a splendid Christmas Dinner at 1 pm. Having had programmes printed of the days fun I am going to write out here a copy of it. 
 5th Australian B.G. Railway Operating Coy. 
 Capt. G Morgan Officer Commanding. 
 Australian Imperial Forces. 
 On Active Service. 
 Somewhere in France.
