 From - Flagship. &nbsp;To - &quot;Warrego&quot; 
  12th Septr. &#39;14  
 (9-15am) 
 Commander Beresford per &quot;Warrego&quot;:- Brigadier requests that your party be withdrawn from W/T Station to Kaba Kaul and then to Herbertshohe to form part of garrison there. Make certain that wireless is absolutely out of action before leaving station. 
 0915
