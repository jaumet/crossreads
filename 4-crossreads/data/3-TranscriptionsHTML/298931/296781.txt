<p>[August, 1916.]<br/>Friday 4<br/>Elsie Dean, my girl, 12 months ago to day I made her happy<br/>Saturday 5<br/>The boys went over last night and pushed Fritz back<br/>Sunday 6<br/>Sunday, and out of the fray, for a while, we lost good men<br/>Tis a beautiful day we are camped about four miles behind the trenches</p>
