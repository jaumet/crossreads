<p>France<br/>9th M.G. Coy. as C.O.<br/>28.9.17 &ndash; March to Forward Area continued.  Arrived at "C" Camp, No. B Area, Winnezeele at midday.<br/>"C" Camp, "B" Area &ndash; 30.9.17 &ndash; 4 p.m., 2 Officers &amp; 30 O/Ranks proceeded to Morebecque as nucleus to Company (vide S.S. 135).  10 p.m., Company proceeded to the line East of Ypres.<br/>1.10.17 &ndash; 23rd M.G. Coy. (A.I.F.) relieved the 233rd M.G. Coy. in the Frezenberg Sector east of Ypres.  Guns were in position as follows:- (Reference Map Zonnebeke 28 N.E.I) "A" Section, 2 guns at Van Isackere Fm. (Block House), D.21.a.20.65.  2 guns in Reserve about D.26.b.2.6.  "B" Section, 4 guns about D.26.a.13.17 for S.O.S. Barrage.  "D" Section, 4 guns, at Vampir D.26.a.0.5.47.  Company Headquarters were at Millcott (not shown on above map).  Ref. map Polygon Wood I.5.a.19.20.<br/>The strength of the Company on going into the line was 12 Officers (including 1 attached) and 169 O/Ranks.<br/>The day was quiet except for two practice barrages put down by our Artillery which drew mild retaliation from the enemy.
