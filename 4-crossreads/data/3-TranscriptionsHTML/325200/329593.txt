<p>further on. No 1 section including Kemp esq (honorary corporal) Robertson Fraser  &amp;  I &amp;  two saved carts had to go on.<br/>By this time the fun was fast  &amp;  furious; a battery of mountain guns &ndash; made they say by Austrians was shrapnelling us with really admirable precision and the machine gun bullets  &amp;  strays were landing thicker  &amp;  thicker.<br/>For myself, I might have been drinking bad champagne all night from the state of my mouth  &amp;  I am quite sure I haven't been so mortally frightened since</p>
