<p>1917 Friday 9 Feb.</p>
<p>Duties per usual. Still clear &amp; frosty. A few sick, a few lame through during day.  old sergt of the --- R. Emery got a scalp wound after tea at Dump a short distance away. Capt Hadyn dresses it. A long job on account of haemorrhage.   Faints. <br/>Off duty at 9.30 p m. pretty tired. Wrote letters to Mary Mitch Glad Robbins.</p>
