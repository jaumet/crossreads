
[Date 28th has been crossed out & replaced by 29th October and from here all dates have been altered and the diary is a 1917 diary with date changed to 1916]
[Sunday 29 October 1916]
Went Church with Dolly   Laura & self came to dinnerPosted photos to both families of wee wife & self.Returning to AIF office continue vote recording NOs seem to have it.Rang up Sir Geo Reid & made an appointment for Tuesday afternoon at House of Commons.Thank you for goggles etc & also Mays parcel which no doubt are in Egypt waiting for me.Can only find re Sergt Walter Hood is that he is well & has not been injured or sick- he is not in England-How much LSD have I in Deniliquin Savings Bank please
