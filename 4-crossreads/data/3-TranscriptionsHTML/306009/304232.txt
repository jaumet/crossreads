<p>1915<br/>Feb. 15 Mon.<br/>Am now getting my clothes washed &amp; ironed by natives at 8 piastres a dozen &ndash; done very well.<br/>Number in Hospital &ndash; 784.<br/>Leave in the afternoon &ndash; set out for Sphinx on a donkey at 2 p.m. where had my photo taken &ndash; Came back &amp; went into Cairo &ndash; Went out to Heliopolis &amp; toured Palace Hotel (1500 rooms) where No. 1 General Hospital is stationed &ndash; a splendid building.<br/>Heliopolis is a very pretty place with wide streets &amp; many fine buildings &ndash; about &frac14; hr's ride out in the tram from Cairo &ndash; Fare:- 2nd class &frac12; pt. 1st class 1 pt.<br/>Returned to Mena about 8 p.m.</p>
