Wednesday 25.4.17
Anzac Day. Colonel returned from Paris. Rations very young lately, fortunately we are in a position to buy a few extras. 14th F Amb. again beat us at Rugby.
Thursday 26.4.17
Commenced a syllabus of training consisting of Drill, lectures Signalling.
