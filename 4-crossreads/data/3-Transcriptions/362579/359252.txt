<p>Had to keep our eyes open   &amp;  go slowing but it is horribly wet   &amp;  muddy on the oaths now &ndash; the snow has melted. I caused a little amusement. I thought I looked like a Lady Jockey. Of course I was complimented on my suitable attire. High-gum-boots, South wester   &amp;  dress pinned up, so that I think it was very wise. My work is running from tent to tent   &amp;  my line in the longest from No 4 to No. 10.</p>
<p>Received such a crowd yesterday. In the morning evacuated</p>
