<p>Friday 21 July 1916<br/>Stables in morning.  Went as mess orderly to Patrol on duty at Station.  Got parcel from Telarah &amp; also from Stella.</p>
<p>Saturday 22 July 1916<br/>Orderly for patrol all day.  Went round photographing in morning.<br/>Left two films to be developed.</p>
