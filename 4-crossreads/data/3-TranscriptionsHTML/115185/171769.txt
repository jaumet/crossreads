<p>I lie on the settee in the mess room and think of Grandmother and her cheerfulness to every one however boring they were. The view is beautiful from where we are anchored. Almost like the bay of Naples in formation &amp; color &ndash; the moon &amp; clouds at night and the heavenly peacefulness of it all ought to make the most cantankerous nature at peace with mankind.<br/>The great war ships lie without lights like great unearthly arrivals ready to spring &ndash;<br/>I have not been on shore all the other sisters went to tea with Colonel [indecipherable] &amp; the officers last Sunday</p>
