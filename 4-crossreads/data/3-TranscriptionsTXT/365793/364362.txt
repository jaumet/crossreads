 Spt 14 has broken. Beyond a slight Westerly &amp; a few scattered clouds there is nothing to denote that the rain-god held full sway for  days  past. The fc'stle head is a regular "flapper", clothing of all sorts &amp; sizes hung out to dry, and there are even blankets, &amp; a pair of leggings. &ndash; There was a  farcical  hammock inspection at 2 oclock.
