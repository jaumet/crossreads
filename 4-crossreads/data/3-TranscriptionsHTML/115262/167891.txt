<p>Friday 4 May 1917.</p>
<p>Every<s>one</s>body&nbsp;tired of general breakups. &nbsp;</p>
<p><u>Reveille 7 p.m.&nbsp;</u>for our unit only. &nbsp;<br />1<sup>st&nbsp;</sup>parade at 9 p.m. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Made out &quot;Will&quot; forms, &amp; did nothing in &nbsp;general. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Official word that we are to sail next week, &amp; everyone in A.C.<s>B</s>.F. Amb. granted leave for week-end. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Bed 10 pm. after getting some washing done.</p>
