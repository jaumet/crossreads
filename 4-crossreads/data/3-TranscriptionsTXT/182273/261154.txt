 August&nbsp;&nbsp; 1918 
 11 SUN&nbsp; Fine day. very heavy firing 
 about 4 am both sides. 
 Amm. going up. Inf. overcame 
 &#39;Hun&#39; resistance, 
 &amp; went forward. Heavy firing 
 on both sides. &amp; heavy bombing 
 at night. No mail able to 
 be posted. 
 12 MON&nbsp; Fine day. fairly quiet. 
 preparing for further stunt. 
 Amm. going up. Heavy 
 bombing at night. shelling 
 Received letter from Julie 
 13 TUES&nbsp; Fine day. Artillery 
 active on both sides. Amm. 
 going up. 2  nd   &amp; 5  th   Div. guns came 
 up. Preparing for a stunt tomorrow 
 Shelled out of W. L. in aft. Tanks 
 &amp; cavalry in rear. Bombs at night. 
 14 WED&nbsp; Fine day. Warm. Carting 
 amm. for stunt. Things fairlly 
 quiet. Very hazy. Rec: letter 
 from Gladys. Railway fixed up. 
 Guns moved forward. Rec: letter 
 &amp; P.C. from Tense. Bombs at night.
