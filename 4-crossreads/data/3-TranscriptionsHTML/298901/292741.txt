I took over the station at 11 A.M. with B &amp; A's personnel, most of the bearers going forward to the posts.  During the day we do everything in the tents which are dug in a little &amp; at night we repair to a fine dug out designed to hold 42 stretcher cases with a trolley arrangement for bringing them up &amp; down, it's one of the best I have met &amp; will be used exclusively in case of heavy shelling.<br/>We are all in dugouts, my own being a splendid one being lined with curtains taken from the village and it also has leadlight windows from the same source, so that conditions allowing, we will be very comfortable.<br/>Heavy guns are fairly thick in the neighbourhood but the front is very quiet.</p>
