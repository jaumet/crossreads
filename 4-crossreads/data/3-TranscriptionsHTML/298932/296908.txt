<p>FAVRIEUL<br/>Monday 16<br/>Camped in a tin shed at VAULX<br/>Met Mick Dunn<br/>Tuesday 17<br/>Going to water when the hun spotted us and started to shell wounding McKay and three horses, two dead<br/>Wednesday 18<br/>They are getting very close to my shed with big shells<br/>Thursday 19<br/>The country about here is beautiful grassy plains &amp; wild flowers</p>
