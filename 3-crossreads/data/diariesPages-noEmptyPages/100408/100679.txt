9/4/18 Foggy last night this morning. Very heavy artillery barrage started about 4.30am. Several shells fell fairly close to C.C.S, probably Baillieul. Artillery fire very heavy all day in afternoon considerable machine gun fire. After tea we were told to stand by not leave the camp. No 2 British C.C.S evacuated during today. Saw some despatch riders after tea they gave us the news. Fritz attacked the Portuguese they cleared out leaving our flanks open. Heard Fritz took Sailly reached La Gorgue, also that Estaires has been smashed up. A Tommy told us legs arms were lying about everywhere numbers of people buried in the ruins. About 4pm
