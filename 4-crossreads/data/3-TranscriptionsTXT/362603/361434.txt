 8.  She must know my Taste cos ginger &amp; sultanas are just lovely to me.  I have only been to Richmond on the Thames.  I have yet to visit Windsor. Bess is having a pretty rotten time with Her poor Dad.  I do not think that the Poor old chap will last much longer Well Mum, I am pretty tired &amp; I have to write to George yet so I will wind up for this time  We are having glorious weather now &amp; all my leave the weather was glorious.  I am in good health &amp; so is Ellis &amp; Dave.  Ellis has gone to Salisbury for the afternoon.  Heaps of Love to You all &amp; Regards to Jess Sheldon &amp; the Neighbours.  X  Harry.
