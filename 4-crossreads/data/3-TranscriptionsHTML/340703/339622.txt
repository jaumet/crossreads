<p>April 1916</p>
<p>Friday 14<br/>Sunrise most gorgeous no wonder they write of the wonderful colourings of Egypt. Left Suez 2pm for Tel el Kebir, some in cattle trucks . Arrived at 8pm. (in the desert). Moonlight Marched 1&frac12; miles to our camp  very chilly</p>
<p>Saturday 15<br/>Had a look at the desert. Bare barren and sandy. Les Spragg,Tom Reid, Reg King, Barrett and Montring to see me. Vic Masters also</p>
