<p>Tuesday 21 September, 1915</p>
<p>Still off Duty.</p>
<p>Received a letter from the mater.</p>
<p>Artillery is very active lately. Fleet as well as land batteries.</p>
<p>Rum was issued.</p>
<p>The Island of Cinbros[?] is a beautiful sight just before dark. The sun goes down just to the right of it &amp; it looks a very dark blue &amp; wventually fades away, growin[g] the same colour as the sea.</p>
