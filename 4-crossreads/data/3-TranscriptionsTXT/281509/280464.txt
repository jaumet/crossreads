 1916 August 29 Tuesday 
 Up 7.45 a.m. breakfast. parade 9.30. Write letters all morning. more letters PC.GR. Parcel from Eva. Parade 2 p.m. squad drill. For hour issue of clothing secure overcoat. Pommy get leave to Poperinge. Jock, Mac, Cat &amp;; self again. bob does not come afraid he might be wanted re transfer. Ride in motor lorry. Rain begins &amp; pours down nearly all time. Circama. Rain clears &amp; we get home dry.
