20) Aug 1821 )  Anchored at Scapa Flow22 ) Aug 18
23rd Aug 18Went with our squadron & destroyers to with convoy to Norway.
24th Aug 18At sea with convoy.
25 Aug 18Arrived at Bergen Norway with convoy. We went further in towards land than usual. There were two Danish Torpedo Boats, came close alongside us. Started with convoy to England in First watch.
26th Aug 18At sea with convoy for England.
27th Aug 18Dropped convoy & picked up mine layers & screened them while they exploded more mine fields.
28 Aug 18Arrived in Scapay Flow early in middle watch
