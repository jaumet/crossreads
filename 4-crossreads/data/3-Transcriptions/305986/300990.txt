We Thanked him of course and hit the Trail and after sundry lifts in Motor Waggons etc we arrived in Albert in O'Clock and to beat it back to the unit, they wanted to send us to a reinforcement camp but we werent having any. 24.11.16 back again with the unit and don't like it, had a bunch of letters</p>
