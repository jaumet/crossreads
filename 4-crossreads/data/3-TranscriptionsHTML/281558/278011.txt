<p>round &amp; found most of the old places : still dead lying about : "P" of 2nd Div cross &amp; grave of Black : got a lift in motor transport back : went down a deep pit near Bapaume which is reckoned to be the air shaft of a Fritz underground tunnel :</p>
<p>Friday July 13th</p>
<p>Bapaume : good day : reveille 4.30 am : fell in for "stunt" : march to Warlencourt then the "boys" attached &amp; "took" some trenches then Loupart Wood : seen a taube come down : home about 3 pm :</p>
<p>Saturday July 14th</p>
<p>Bapaume : good day : reveille 6 am : sent map to Aunt Myra &amp; Hun paper to Mum : stroll round after tea :</p>
<p>Sunday July 15th</p>
<p>Bapaume : reveille 6.30 am : Holy Comm 9.30 am : stroll after tea : interesting debate between "Griffin"</p>
