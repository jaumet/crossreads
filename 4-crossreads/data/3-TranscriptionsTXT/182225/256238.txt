 [June 13 continued from previous diary page] 
 MARCH &nbsp; THURSDAY 23 &nbsp; 1916 
 today with more Australian troops aboard and the &quot;Marathon&quot; came in last night with the 8 th &nbsp;Brigade from Victoria on board. They say we wont get away from here till the end of th week, but hope its not true.
