<p>[Page 22]</p>
<p>Monday 6/5/18&nbsp; Had a few casualties by premature explosion of big French Gun.</p>
<p>Tuesday 7/5/18&nbsp; Letter from Doff- also Wrote Doff- Bn digging &amp; reconnoitring.</p>
<p>Wednesday 8/5/18&nbsp; More reconnoitring &amp; digging also Training&nbsp; wrote to Bas.</p>
<p>Thursday 9/5/18. Orders received to move again tomorrow</p>
<p>Friday 10/5/18. Moved to Fransvillers [Franvillers] arriving at 9 p.m. had a game of bridge</p>
<p>Saturday 11/5/18. Reconnoitring positions in Reserve &amp; forward</p>
<p>Sunday 12/5/18&nbsp; Enemy shelled village&nbsp; two civilians killed.</p>
