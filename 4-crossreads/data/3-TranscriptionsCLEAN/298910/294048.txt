11/8/16  Friday
The usual trench warfare going on, & only a few casualys night & day.
12/8/16  Saturday
Trying hard to rain - Fritz shelling villages of Laventie - Fleurbaix freely - of no military importance. Altho still running Eton Hall Dressing Station - we were ordered to also relieve the 8th Field Ambulance & take over the First Aid Post below Fleurbaix - no rest for the wicked or willing - Luckily we can handle all at present very conveniently.
13/8/16  Sunday
Went to Mass & Communion at Nouveaux-Monde   6.30 am. Fritz shelling around "La Bassee". Having a blow from the trenches (Late Parade. Crimed but appealed & got off.)
14/8/16  Monday
Rain off & on all day - Got orders to get back to my dug out at Eaton Hall - Very lively trench warfare, also lively sniping all night.
