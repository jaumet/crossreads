 May 30/17 Wednesday Basieux came on here a few days ago to stop with the C.R.E while we are out here spelling. &ndash; 'Jock' Barclay &amp; I got a pass for a days leave in Amiens for Monday. We got hold of our passes the night before so set off Sunday evening &amp; walked down to Buire 5 miles away so as to be ready to catch the early morning train in 
 Got a camp for the night at Buire Baths &ndash; our camp consisted of a borrowed great coat each
