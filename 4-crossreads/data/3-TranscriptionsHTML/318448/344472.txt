<p>here under the Mount of Temptation beside the Brook  Kerith about dusk. I now hear them say that it is three mins to eleven. We have pitched the tent, set our gear, fed Henry, had a final cup of tea &amp; now the other two are in bed while I am writing this nonsense by the light of the full moon sitting at the table, homemade, folding, 1, in my pyjamas, in the much maligned Valley of the Jordan.<br/>Jericho Jane has been firing</p>
