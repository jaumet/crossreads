<p>31 January 1918 Thursday<br/>JERUSALEM<br/>My Pilgrimage to Jerusalem has at last eventuated &amp; I have written you fully re same.  I left at 4 AM in Divisional Car &amp; returned at night- <strike>along [indecipherable] switchback road.</strike><br/>PEN too bad-<br/>A glorious day &amp; one I shall not forget- if only you could have been with me to tell me more about these HOLY places- I bought a few beads etc just to send <strike>Day</strike> as from Bethlehem-<br/>Let me know if you get my letter</p>
