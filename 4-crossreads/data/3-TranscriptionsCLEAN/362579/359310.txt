  
 Everything is very expensive. Send to England for a new cape (red)   &  it costs &pound 1.2.0 (one pound two shillings) so shall have to be very careful. 
 Consie Murray is in England at Sick-Sisters Hosp. so she has offered to do any shopping for me. 
 Her sister got married to Captain McLaren, relation of Alice Bowers - she is living in England. 
 Our Tennis-Court is very popular but I'm very lazy on night-duty   &  don't play much.
