<p>Slept all afternoon.  After tea went to Soldiers Institute in Wareham.</p>
<p>Monday, 11/6/17<br/>Went to parade ground over the line &amp; drilled.  Knocked off 2 p.m.&amp; mounted guard 7 p.m.  Leave was cancelled at last minute on a/c of camp removing.  Received parcel of eatables from Uncle Fred.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 12/6/17<br/>On guard various times during day one officer under open arrest.  Knocked off 7 p.m. &amp; then went to Y.M.C.A. for supper.</p>
<p>Wednesday, 13/6/17<br/>Route march in morning &ndash; round trip via Holme Lane.  When arrived back had some mail from Australia delivered to me.</p>
