<p>The 3rd Brigade and Light Horse made a charge on our right and captured a trench. They went further than their objective. Consequently our navy put some shells amongst them and caused heavy losses. Their casualties were 12 killed and 70 wounded. We had a great demonstration on our front and fired thousands of rounds. 2.8.15.</p>
