 Thursday 3rd Aug. 1916 
 Everything in regard to arrangements for comfort of troops in order. Everything going like clockwork.  I must admit that there is no room for an accusation of slackness or wilful neglect such as  I have had to make  could have been made in regard to other troopships I have been on.
