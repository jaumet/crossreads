<p>May.<br/>12th.  With the "Karagola" just astern of us, we passed out of the Canal at 5.30, covering 87 miles in 11 &frac12; hours.<br/>However the "Karagola" left two hours or more ahead of us but we caught her at 8 p.m., passing her to strains of "Good Byee!!"<br/>Country of Sinai &amp; that of Egypt along Gulf of Suez is the most inhospitable country I have seen.  Now in Red Sea.<br/>14th.  Very hot two days &amp; a few patients stricken down.<br/>Southern Cross visible to-night!!!</p>
