<p>352<br/>4<br/>Her husband and other son are both serving in the army &ndash; she brought me a large basket of strawberries and a pot of cream &ndash; While I was out yesterday and Mrs Goodfellow called &ndash; she is cousin of the maters but I suppose you know all about her &ndash; she is coming to take me out tomorrow &ndash; I do hope I see you before you go &ndash; Quite likely I shall be at Blackheath Saturday night</p>
<p>Fondest love old girl<br/>Ever your  devoted brother<br/>Gibson McMillan</p>
