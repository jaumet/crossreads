
Monday 4th May 1917Commenced duty week yesterday, & I, being in the tent division, am on the observation Hosp.It is a good jobThere are three of us with a Lance Corp who is an oldish steady old chap, who knows his work fairly well, but is not very quick.  He is a nice chap.The other two are young & very straight kind of chaps & full of life.Went to Bournemouth on Saturday afternoon & saw aunts & uncles.
