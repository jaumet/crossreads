  
 17th First fall of snow, very light just like frozen drops of rain. Weather very cold.        Social evening in our hut for band members as a farewell evening also a recognition of  "Martha" Jones' Birthday. Each man contributed 1/- towards the provender which was laid out &agrave; la banquet    Several Songs and speeches were given throughout the evening by W.O. Flynn,  Sergeant Bignall & members of the band.                                    The whole broke up at 10 o'clock after spending a very enjoyable evening.
