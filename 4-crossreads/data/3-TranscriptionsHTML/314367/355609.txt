<p>To-day we're pretty right but for 48 hours I did nothing save my bare duty &ndash;<br/>Rabaul is pretty &amp; green &ndash; hot &amp; damp &ndash; Not a ship was in the harbor &ndash; war &ndash; or otherwise when we anchored &ndash; Presently a boat came off &amp; Lambson (Morru L) was in it &ndash; No news save about the S &amp; G &amp; Dresden &amp; Nurnbourg &ndash; all sunk &ndash; This is excellent for with it out goes all petty useless resistance by small bodies of Germans who relied on the 2nd advent of their armed cruisers &ndash; no mail leaves here before January but I hear I can wire &ndash; The Eastern &ndash; a store ship &ndash; came in &amp; the Una (Comet) Capt Jackson has come in &ndash; They've been at Wilhelmshaven &amp; we were first here &ndash; we spotted Capt-T-flew the <u>Senior</u>1 officers pennant &ndash; The Destroyers are away up a river in New Guinea &amp; what we are to do we don't know at present &ndash; Coal is the 1st thing &ndash; We've 18 tons inboard &ndash; Food is a difficulty too &ndash; We had 3 sheep sent us from a broken down refrigerator &amp; I promptly condemned the lot aided by 2 other officers (3 have to survey but I do the necessary).</p>
