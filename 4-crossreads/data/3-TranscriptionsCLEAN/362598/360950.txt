
Sunday 5 November 1916Very wild day.  I go to Church.  Receive letter from Jennie.  Send Egyptian inkstand & seeds Home to George.  Toppin comes to Supper.
Monday 6 November 1916Gen. Inspec.  Sent Cards to O. G. H. M. & Gran, Dobsons, Allison, Nettie, Cuneo, Whiteheads, Cohens.  Recd. letters Laura, Mrs. Stephen, Bess & Home.  Dave & I go towards Bulford but turn back & go to Pictures.  Wrote M.R.L.
Tuesday 7 November 1916Letter from Mary, Westy.  Wrote to Dad with P.C's & Aunty Hannah's Card.  Letter to Dad.  Card to Ida & Wal.
Wednesday 8 November 1916Wrote to Laura & talked.  A.O.C. Commis. first.  Wrote Mrs. Stephen.
Thursday 9 November 1916Wrote Marie, Dad.  Sent 4 Photos to Marie & 8 to Dad, Mum & George.  Recd. all films & ordered Book.  Bought P.C's.  Talked A.O.C.
Friday 10 November 1916A letter from M.R.L. & Auntie H.  Edie Taubman writes & Otto puts a note in.  Letters from Dad, Mum, Mem, Ossie & wife.  Parcel from Jess Mather.  Wrote Marie & Auntie, Card Home.  Wrote Mrs. Niness, Edie, Marg., Rube Watson.
Saturday 11 November 1916Wrote Maynie & Bess.  E. & D. go to watch Footy.  All go to theatre, "All aboard", no good.  We cannot have Roll Call on morning parade 'cos it's too dark.  I learn of how to make money by the sale of Egyptian coins.
