<p>Friday 13 September 1918</p>
<p>Saturday 14 September 1918<br/>Recco, Jenin, A'Fuleh, Haifa.<br/>Met large 2 seater Rumpler over Jenin, forced him to land 3 miles away from aerodrome, crashed under-carriage.  Huns jumped out &amp; ran for it, fired 150 rounds at machine &amp; 200 at machine on ground.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 2 h. 45 m.</p>
