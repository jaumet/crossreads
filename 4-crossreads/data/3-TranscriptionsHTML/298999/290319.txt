<p>Friday July 6.7.17<br/>turned out 5.30 fed camels had shower had Breakfast.  the malwa went along to australia.  fell in again 8.30 washed camels had dinner 12.30.  rested till 2 P.M. fixed saddle had tea 5 P.M. went on &frac14; guard 6.30.  nothing doing turned in 9 P.M.</p>
<p>Saturday July 7.7.17<br/>turned out 5.30 had a shower had Breakfast returned to the guard tent.  <strike>reste</strike> took H.P. up to orderley room and he lost his head was going to scatter things about Put him</p>
