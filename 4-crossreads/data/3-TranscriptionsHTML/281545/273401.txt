I then went in next door to a (Couffier) a French Hair Dresser &amp; had a shave &amp; Hair Cut also a good Wash &amp; cleaned me boots &amp; leggings &amp; a brush up which I paid 1 franc for, then I continued on my journey.   The centre of the City is on a Hill when you come into a <strike>small square</strike> wide street where I was directed by a Military Policeman to good quarters for the night.   I went in to the Place which was a Caf&eacute; &amp; paid 6 Franc's for a Bed &amp; Breakfast so I made myself secure for the Night. The Weather was fairly cold &amp; the ground was hard &amp; frosty.   As I intended to visit the famous Notre Darme, a Famous Cathedral I went along just before 10 am &amp; met the Verger who showed me the way inside, &amp; I soon had my eyes opened.   This Cathedral is a magnificient Building part of it was built so I was informed in the 14th &amp; 15th Century.
