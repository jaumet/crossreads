<p>turned out 6.AM fed up cleaned up groomed Breakfast saddled up 9.30 AM went on q. m. fatigue loaded up rations dinner 12.  horses to water 3.30  saw some of my mates out of the 14th Regiment  wrote to Eileen<br/>Monday Aug.5.8.18<br/>turned out 6.A.M fed up and watered nothing much doing.<br/>Tuesday Aug 6.8.18<br/>turned out 6.AM fed up  on q. m. fatigue had a letter from alma and one from aggie  nothing doing to day.  done some washing am very sick this afternoon  dont know what is wrong.  temperature 104.2  went to bed.</p>
