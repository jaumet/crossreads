 12 July 1915 Monday 
 Piquet again About 3 am we are awakened by the arrival of our 5th Reinfs Great disappointment I get five letters from Sydney but none from home   &amp;  can't make it out why it can have missed 
 1915 July 13 Tuesday 
 Officially take over the new men   &amp;  set them making homes for themselves Are called away without any warning to do a four hour job without any tea return   &amp;  have a good old supper at 9 pm.
