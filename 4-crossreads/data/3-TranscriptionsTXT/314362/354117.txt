 16 Sunday  [Dec]  Very Cold church parade Trist fall of snow (heavy) Parcels from Rup chockes 
 17 Monday Jolly cold route march  &amp; the roads were thick with snow. Feeling fed up. 
 18 Tuesday On Court of Inquiry over missing men all morng.  Went to Desvres in aftn &amp; dined with Browne &amp; Graham. Saw Col. [indecipherable] 19 Wednesday Fixed up the line to the Adjt. C.O. Bre &amp; myself.  damned cold. 
 20 Thursday Got a brazier in my room at last !!!  Jolly warm too !  shall never go out now !!! 
 21 Friday Route march in morning.  Finished reading "Bindle" &ndash; topping book. must send it to Rup. 
 22 Saturday lres from Addie Warly &amp; Evans &amp; Rup.  answered them all.  Heath &amp; Sprattt joined up yesterday 
 23 Sunday Had a good sleep in as there was no church parade !!! 
 24 Monday Into Boulogne with Thompson &amp; saw the Smythes  Home in Davies car by nine o'clock 
 25 Tuesday Lunched &amp; dined at B &amp; G [?] mess.  Quite a good day considering.  Hear that Rup has had [indecipherable] 26 Wednesday No parades thank goodness !!  Snow fell again.  Sent books to Rup. 
 27  Thursday Went over the Bde line with linesman.  Came back with Woolrych.  won another 8 frs at Bridge. 
 28 Friday Specialist parade in morng.  Went to B. &amp; bought new tunic &amp; was wanted while away ! just my luck 
 29 Saturday Read &amp; wrote a good deal.  Stunt in morng which was damned cold !!  No mail for day
