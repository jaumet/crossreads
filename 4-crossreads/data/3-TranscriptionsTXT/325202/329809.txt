 Have just walked down to see a batch of German Prisoners who have just been brought in &ndash; about 200 behind barbed wire of course. A smattering of old men &amp; boys but the majority are solid lumps of young men. 
 July 19 Wednesday We got immediate marching orders at 4 p.m. and within an hour were on the road we soon struck the main road &amp; joined the mass of traffic proceeding towards Albert about 5 miles away.
