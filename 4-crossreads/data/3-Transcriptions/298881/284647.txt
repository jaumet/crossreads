<p>Bologne 6/9/17<br/>Yesterday morning the Assisstant Adjutant <strike>was</strike> chased me up on parade and told me that a pass was waiting for me on the Orderly Room table for the United Kingdom.  I dashed back to billet collected a few things, got my pass and got to Ebblinghem Railway Station as soon as possible.  I boarded a train with a crowd of others at 12.24 A.M.</p>
