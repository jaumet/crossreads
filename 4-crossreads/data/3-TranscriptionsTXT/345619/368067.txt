 [Reverse of the above postcard] Master Frank Burrowes Franurra Rooty Hill N.S.W. Australia. 
 26-11-18 Dear old Frank I think it is nearly time I had some more letters from my nice family. I hope they don't get lost when I leave here. I am going on my Annual Leave tomorrow.  Goodbye lots of love from Dad OXO
