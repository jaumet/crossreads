<p>11th November 1916 Hod El Geeila. Saturday</p>
<p>13 of our airmen heavily laden with bombs passed over the camp early this morning on their way to El Arish to give Jacko a real good "strafing". 13 machines carrying 8 bombs each = 104 bombs. They are going to give Jacko a taste of what he delights in giving us. Inspections of the camp by General Dobell. The commander of the Division- The usual farce. We are going now to shift our horse lines to leave more room for the squadron to get out in case of an air raid.
