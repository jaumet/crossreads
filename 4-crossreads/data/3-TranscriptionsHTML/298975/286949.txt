<p>Tuesday 28th<br/>Rained most of the night &amp; blew some of the tents over &ndash; not ours fortunately &ndash; Had a very bad night as I was sore from lying on the hard boards so long. Still she rains. Moved into huts this evening a great improvement Are supposed to be in Isolation. It is the rule when you first land Am feeling fairly well again. The isolation is a fraud as we go about much as we like.</p>
