<p>May, 1916<br/>Thursday 25<br/>Mopped about camp all morning &amp; after dinner went out to Pattis'<br/>Guiseppe had gone to Port Said for a few days to visit some relatives.<br/>After supper several more people came in &amp; we had a glorious time till 11.30 pm</p>
<p>Friday 26<br/>very warm again<br/>very little to do, so do nothing but read &amp; smoke &amp; smoke &amp; read.<br/>After tea I went to a picture show in Cairo.</p>
