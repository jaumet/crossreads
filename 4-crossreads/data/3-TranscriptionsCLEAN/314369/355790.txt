
28 February 1915Sunday2nd in Lent
Work on spuds suspended for the dayChurch parade at 10 am   &  8 pm. Nobody attends.
1915 March 1Monday
The Spud Job commences again on new line. We rig up a set of pulleys   &  set to w/- the steam winch result - about 10 times as quick   &  hardly any labor.Weather continues fine   &  cool.The old man is made Armourer Sergeant
