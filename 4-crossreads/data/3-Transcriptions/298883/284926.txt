<p>Austria is definitely out of the war now and the emperor has</p>
<p>?8/11/18 fled.</p>
<p>A notable feature of this war has <strike>the way</strike> been the dethroning of all the emperors of our enemies in addition to the one-time friend and ally Russia.  We need only to kick the Kaiser out now to complete the list and we shall probably not be long in doing that.</p>
