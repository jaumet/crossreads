<p><br/>17 January 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Morning shift per usual.  D McDonald transferred to C Ward.  Enteric.</p>
<p>Lecture in heat in aft. on Blood stale.  Left early. <br/>Bob &amp; self go over to new trenches &amp; find them remarkably well constructed.  Shown over sanitary depot.  Lecture in tent at night &ndash; Food.: <br/><strike>H</strike> most disagreeably hot dusty day Temp 105.4&deg; <br/>Southerly buster at night.</p>
