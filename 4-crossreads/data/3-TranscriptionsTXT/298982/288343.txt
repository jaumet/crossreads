 Sat 28th Woke up stiff in joints &amp; sore of head 
 Sun 29 Still feel old &amp; rotten. Rested all morn 
 Mon 30th Went to doctor in morning. Pills &amp; to lie down  Evening  come tomorrow 
 Tue 31 Sent down to Hospital in evening 103 &deg; Sun sent aboard 9th Sept arr in Cairo No 2 G.H. off S.S. Salva 
 Oct 16th Went into Cairo &amp; drew &pound; 10. First trip into the city since return Met Mr C Sinder. 
 Oct 24. To Ghiza Zoo with Brookhouse &amp; Baird Met Sisters Lehmann at 2.30 &amp; had a most pleasant day.
