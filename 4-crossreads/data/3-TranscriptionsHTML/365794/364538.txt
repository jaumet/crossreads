<p>Thursday 16th May 1917 [1918]<br/>The Anniversary of our poor little Enid's death, does not seem as long ago as that &amp; yet how easily I too might have been lying today quiet as she is after my recent illness, how many of us will be above the sod this date next year?<br/>Went town with S. Cambridge, ordered 2 white frocks at Salons, Pt. 450 for two.<br/>Shopped &ndash; shoes tan R. Hughes, Pt. 135.  Paid 1 week's subscription at Club &amp; had tea (Pt. 3&frac12;).<br/>Dinner in mess room (1st time).  Concert in Y.M.C.A. Hut, very questionable jokes!<br/>An Aust. sister adm. to Balcony.  All Aust. are going to Salonika from Alex.  Are we?<br/>May go to Bulkeley on Monday.</p>
