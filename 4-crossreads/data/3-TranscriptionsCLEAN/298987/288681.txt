  
 Wednesday 8th The Physical Drill competition came off today. The 18th won we got 2nd. This means a few shillings for our team I need it as my capital is one penny & a Kruger 2/- piece that was palmed of on me in Capetown & can not be changed Boat Drill this morning. Quite an extcitement as we had no warning & there has been a lot of talk about Subs. While we were lined up the Pardres gave out 1 Pkt of Cigs between each 2 men Boats are all in one line tonight 
 Thursday 9th Formed in two lines early this morning & land was in sight anchored about 10.30 in Port Freetown Serra Leone. W Africa.
