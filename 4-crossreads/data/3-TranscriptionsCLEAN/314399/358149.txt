
Sunday 1 June 1919Shaved & washed some clothes. After lunch went out to the hospital to see Bill. He had been sent back to bed on a/c of having a temp. Still was far from well. Marie rang up & then came out. Had tea at the hospital. Took her home & then came home myself.
Monday 2 June 1919Went to see people at Grosvenor Place about Polytechnic. No good tho. Had driving lesson & lecture. Came home & wrote, read & washed a pair of slacks. Looks very much like rain.
