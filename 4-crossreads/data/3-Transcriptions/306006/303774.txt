<p>13-3-17<br/>to the Remounts &amp; then to Abbassia stayed till 9.30 &amp; then came back I went with Ben to his camp which is only about half a mile away &amp; I am to go to the 14th tonight &amp; make arrangements to meet Jessie later on. Ben appears to be a very nice lad;</p>
<p>14-3-17<br/>Wed 14th<br/>Had some tests reading morse flag through telescopes before breakfast, it was very windy &amp; difficult. There is a Field Day on today but does not affect us. But the N.C.O.s course are out &amp; Vern. Harris is on night manoeuvres tonight I went to the 14th A.G.H. &amp; found Hills is on night duty specialising sick Sisters</p>
