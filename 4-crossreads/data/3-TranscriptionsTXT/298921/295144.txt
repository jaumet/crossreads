 June, 1915 Tuesday 22 Court martial today I am Prosecutor. Received letter from Laura &amp; a few cards from the front some are very funny I have some good snap shots &ndash; of dead Turks etc &ndash; but will not trust them to the tender mercy of a Censor, who no doubt would like them himself . Some quaint Post Cards come back from Dardanelles written on Cardboard or Brown Paper &ndash; one from one of my men was just addressed Mr Weir. Heliopolis &amp; I got it send it on to you with this to keep for me.
