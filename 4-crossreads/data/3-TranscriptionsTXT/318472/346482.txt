 14 September 1918 Saturday A very quiet and uneventful day. A nice bright day &amp; a fairly heavy swell. No War news. Making very slow time &ndash; about 9 Knots per hour. 
 1918 September 15 Sunday 16 After Trinity 
 We are in sight of a good deal of Coast here again this evening and are amongst several Light houses &amp; we must be nearing Port Nothing particular happening.
