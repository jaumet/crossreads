<p>May Tuesday 1 1917</p>
<p>Slept in the Leave billet last night. Breakfast wash, hoist our packs get let out &amp; board our tram at Docks for Wimeraux.  A beautiful morning blue sky &amp; bluer sea. sun glittering &amp; countless shipping coming &amp; going to &amp; from Blighty. Tram skirts the sea coast. for 3 miles. so pretty.  Arrive at 2nd A.G.H. report Orderly Room business, carry patients to &amp;amp from X Ray theatre for rest of day. Wimeraux a vast hospital   No less than 6 near each other an ideal spot for convalescents. <br/>Meet Cartwright.  &mp; Jockey, Freda Hewlett.</p>
