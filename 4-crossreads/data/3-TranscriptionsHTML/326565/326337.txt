<p>Moved out of camp 3.40 pm, arrived at railhead, about 5&frac12; miles, at 5.30pm.<br/>Bivouaced here for night.  Hard road most of way out; marching not too bad.  Two new officers attached to corps in place of Captains North &amp; Donald.</p>
<p>Monday 3/4/16<br/>Heavy dew during night which made sleeping out not too pleasant.  As A &amp; B sections are to take up different positions further out into the desert, the day was taken up sorting material &amp; equipment according to sections.  Again fell in &amp; moved of at 5.30 pm to take up a position</p>
