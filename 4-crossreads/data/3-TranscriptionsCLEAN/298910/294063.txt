
27/9/16  Wednesday
Nice day - Witnessed an escaped observation Balloon from over Fritzs lines, the wind was blowing over our way - so it went as far as Estaire & was then shot down by our anti aircraft guns. Very quiet in the trenches.
28/9/16  Thursday
Dull day - Very quiet - not disturbed at all last night. Got the news that Combles & Thiepval had fallen - Tanks doing great work.
29/9/16  Friday
Raining all day. Very quiet in our line. Fritz very busy putting over Minniewurzen & Shrapnel on Supports & entrance to V.C.S ops.
30/9/16  Saturday
Raining - Heavy bombardment - Made raids on Fritz's trench at night time, very successful - very few calls - on N.Z. buried in his dugout.
1/10/16  Sunday
Nice day - very quiet.
2/10/16  Monday
Nice day. Got relieved about 10 am, returned to Nouvea Monde, had the day off.
