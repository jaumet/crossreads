<p>(21)<br/>"Good" and the major passes on continuing his enquiries down the long line of resting troops.<br/>Ten minutes has gone<br/>(It seems but five)<br/>"Fall in" The order is well-nigh hateful for the moment.<br/>The troops stagger up and shuffle to their places in fours on the road.<br/>"At ease; Quick march"<br/>We are moving forward once more with that everlasting tread tread tread.<br/>The boys are soon singing a song that tells of home, or whistling the tune of it.<br/>&nbsp;A glow now faintly glows with rose lights in the East.</p>
