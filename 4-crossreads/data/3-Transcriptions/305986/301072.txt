<p>in the gully where the chaps are walking was where my lines ran to the Front line and back to Brigade, and didnt they get shelled it was lively believe me running those wires, the ground round the dugout was littered with dead when I was there but I dont see any in the Photo. 29.1.17 nothing much doing yesterday &amp; today, its still freezing hard and we move up into the line or close supports tomorrow, by all accounts its fairly lively.
