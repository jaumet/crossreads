  
 July 1918 Monday 8 
 Vaux sur Somme.2nd Bty. Position At 1am Fritz was shelling on a duty shoot, all round district. At 12.20 am all the guns opened for a stunt, of what I do not know.  Smithy came up to position and I was very glad to see him looking so well and happy.  Halligan went out to L'aison at Hamel with Mr. Kerr. Wrote several letters for Smithy and myself. Had a feed of chips made by Tom. Had a heavy thunderstorm
