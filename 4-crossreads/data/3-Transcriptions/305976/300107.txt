<p>the 14th  General Hospital. I expect some to be going home<br/>Jan 29<br/>Great excitement today about 200 men belonging to the Division are going home. I believe I am among the lucky number to go Transport duty. We are eagerly waiting for the news to be confirmed. To night we are having a little celebration &amp; farewell party to some of our pals.<br/>Jan 30<br/>Last night was a wild one Tom &amp; I are leaving by the Train to night to report at Moascar</p>
