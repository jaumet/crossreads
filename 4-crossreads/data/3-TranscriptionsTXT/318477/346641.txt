
28-2-16Passed King Island about 6.30 enroute from West to Melb.  Tonight is our farewell concert to our Melb. Comrades.  Entered Melbourne heads at 7.30 a.m, 29-2-16 enroute for port Melb. Wharf.  arrived in port at 11 a.m.  We arrived at the wharf at the town pier at 4 p.m.  After seeing our Western states off the boat in Motor Cars we were allowed ashore from 6 p.m. till 11 p.m.
1-3-16We moved off from the town pier Melb. to go up to No. 2 wharf up the Yarra river to discharge some cargo.
2-3-16Left Melbourne at 4 p.m. for Sydney after a good two days leave in Melbourne.  Arrived Melb. Heads 7 p.m.
4-3-16Arrived at Sydney Heads at 11.30.
