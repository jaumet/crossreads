
April 1918Tuesday 2In Billet - OrvilleBombed during early hours of morning. No casualties. A great number of R.A.M.C. arrived enroute to line, from Italy.During afternoon I performed the erroneous duty of Marker on Battery manoeuvres.Weather beautiful.Had a swim above the wier with Russell.Went along to Santos with Ken Gunn.Missed the boys so came home very early.Met Adranne - "a bon petite Cherie"
