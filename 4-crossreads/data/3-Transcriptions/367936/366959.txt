<p>Thursday 6 April 1916<br/>Swim in morning.<br/>An issue of one blanket all round.<br/>Bayonet drill.<br/>Advance guard action in afternoon.</p>
<p>Friday 7 April 1916<br/>Church parade before breakfast.<br/>Took my turn as tent orderly.<br/>Had a small route march of about 8 miles.<br/>Was enoculated in afternoon then had a lecture from a Sergeant who had served in South Africa.</p>
