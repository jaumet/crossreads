<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Apl 22<sup><u>nd</u></sup>&nbsp; We had a good nights sleep &amp; got up at 10 A.M. I rewired the engine as I had obtained some cable from the Corps Armouries work shops which we brought down from St Omer. We got back to Park at 4.30 P.M. to-day has been fine, but it clouded up again in the evening -</p>
