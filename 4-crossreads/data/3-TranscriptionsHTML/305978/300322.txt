<p>on my part , after getting to know all the boys, &amp; I must say they were a fine lot in the troop &amp; many were from the Upper Hunter district where I first contracted the war fever.</p>
<p><u>May 30th</u><br/>Other reinforcements formed &amp; I got into the 9lth of 6th Reg with some mates of mine.<br/>We had a very hard time at drill for some weeks under an officer named Prideaux Brune who was once an officer in a English Calvary Regt, he was not popular with the Boys  We are now ready for Egypt when boats are available .You will notice that I miss writing down notes for a few</p>
