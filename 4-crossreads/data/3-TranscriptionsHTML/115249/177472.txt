<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 21 9 14</p>
<p>Time - 10.10 AM</p>
<p>From Administrator - May&nbsp;prisoners be sent Sydney by Murex. &nbsp;Some were allowed on Shore on parole yesterday but they are tampering with natives wish them deported - If they cannot go by Murex would suggest in view of Berrimas departure they be sent [indecipherable ?Asrangi] under guard</p>
