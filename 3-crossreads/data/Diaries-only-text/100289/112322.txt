Wednesday 16.1.18
Reported at Headquarters 10.30 Am from furlough. Missed first batch, caught 3 pm train from Paddington arrived at Warminster Sutton Veny" about 7.30 pm. Spent a awful night on the boards, never slept all night.
Thursday 17.1.18
Snowed heavily, waited about in the snow all morning to be classified, marked B1 A2. Drew kit from store.
