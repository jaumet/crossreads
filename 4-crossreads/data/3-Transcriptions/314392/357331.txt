<p>28th Still freezing. Our artillery is still very active. Nothing much to note.<br/>29th Cold &amp; miserable. A little snow is falling. Fritz sends over a few shells.<br/>30th Nothing doing. Snowing slightly.<br/>31st This is indeed a beautiful day. The sun is shining. Gee! how I wish this dammed war was finished. Life at present is but an existence.<br/>1st Cold and miserable. Our artillery still going strong. 15th Battalion "hop over" tonight 7.30pm.
