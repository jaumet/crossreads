 Oct 1917 7 Sun   It is a miserable wet morning but cleared up this afternoon.   Visited the H.M.S.N.Z. in the Firth of Forth   Monday 8th Went to Leith this morning &amp; took the train to Dundee arrived at 4 pm mid drissle[ing] rain.   Went to a very good Variety show the best I have seen in England.   This is a great place for girls there are 25 for every man Tuesday 9th It is still wet so we could only go for tram rides Wednesday 10th. I went back to Edinburg   This morning my mate went on to Inverness I took the midday to Glasgow it is a fine city &amp; has the finest tram service in the world
