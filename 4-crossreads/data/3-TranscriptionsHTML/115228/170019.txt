At 8 o&#39;clock last night, all the artillery on this section opened a tremendously heavy bombardment, the heaviest I have witnessed, which lasted till about 3.30 a.m. &amp; at daylight the Infantry made a very successful advance on the left flank near the coast, &amp; crossed by means of pontoon bridges, the small river which up to the present had been dividing our line &amp; the enemy&#39;s.
