
Tuesday 26thFell in morning muster parade dismissed straight away visited villages of PPende and Drancourt and St Valery. Wednesday 27-Visited Drancourt again had dinner then returned to St. ValeryThursday 28thAfter parade went across to village of Le Crostnay Crotnay poured and had to remain there till tide went out again went to Cinema returned to camp one o'clock in morning
