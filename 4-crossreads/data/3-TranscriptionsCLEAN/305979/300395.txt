The day was bright and beautiful in every respect, and well suited as an omen to the accomplishment of the task before us. Meals on board are excellent. Our mess-room and sleeping room combined is right foreword. All have hammocks which we sling each night to the ceiling and which are infinitely more comfortable than cabin-beds. 
 Nov 12 Fine day. Allen & self explore much of the ship from end to end. We went down into the engine-rooom and explore nearly everything to be seen down there
