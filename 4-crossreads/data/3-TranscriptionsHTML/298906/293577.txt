<p>wouldn't have this sort of thing Stainton is his name.<br/>Have been busy writing all afternoon letting all know of my change of address.<br/>Had a letter from Len this morning he will be out of hospital shortly &amp; then expects to come over here I hear the Battalion has just gone in the line, the first time since I left her in "Bullecourt." So they had a fair spell this time. The Colonel turned old Jock down this afternoon told him it was no use him parading, as he would go back when he was sent &amp; not before, Jock this evening wanted me to let him take my place so as I could have my leave a thoughtful action but one I would not listen</p>
