<p>the rays of the sun are fast dispelling this haze &amp; soon the whole countryside will be bathed in a flood of golden sunshine, England is now at her best &amp; theres no doubt but it is lovely, or so it appeals to me it is Sunday &amp; over all this birdsong the Church bells can be heard pealing mellowing the whole scene into one of loveliness &amp; beauty, I could sit here for hours drinking in the beauty of it all, &amp; wondering if ever I shall possess such a beautiful place, for it has always been my ambition to do so.</p>
<p>We had a concert here last night an open air one we all laid or sat about on the grass &amp; thoroughly enjoyed the music if nothing else.</p>
