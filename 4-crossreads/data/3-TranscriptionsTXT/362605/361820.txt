 Havre 4.1.19 
 Dear Dad, Mum &amp; George, 
 I have sat down on 4 different occasions to write to you all, but only just now I'm, getting on with it.  We are quite in accordance with your alteration of the Dee Why Property.  Make it up to date for Ellis &amp; I are thinking very seriously of going back to D.J.'s for a while &amp; the money will not be wanted for our Business for quite a while perhaps.  On the advice of many eminent business men that we have sounded
