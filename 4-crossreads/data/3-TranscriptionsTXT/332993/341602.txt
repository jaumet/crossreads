 Last night our iron rations were collected &amp; issued to us for dinner today.  We will shortly be reissued with a fresh supply.  Only the bully beef was called in. There is another revolution in Russia, in fact civil war between two rival sections headed by the Premier on one side &amp; the head of the army on the other. The Swedish counsel [consul] at Buncos Ayers [Buenos Aires?] has imperilled the neutrality of Sweden by sending messages for the Germans there.  A huge system of spies have been discovered in Sweden together with their code book &amp; [indecipherable.] Two more of the transports went on Paris 10 days leave this morning. 
 Wednesday Sept 12. 1917. (1136) Fine but cloudy.  I was shoeing again this morning, with Ryan helping me.  There is the usual half holiday this evening.  There is to be a foot ball match this evening.  Last evening I received a letter from my cousin Kitty Gilmore in which she stated she had written several times &amp; the letters were all returned.  But the number was omitted on the letter  the  I received, &amp; that probably accounts for it. [2 line of shorthand] Thursday September 13. 1917. (1137.) Fine but cloudy.  I was at a very good concert in the Y.M.C.A. Tent last night given by the officers of the 37. Bat.  It was a beaut.  The favorite was Cpt. Toule with his witty Irish songs.
