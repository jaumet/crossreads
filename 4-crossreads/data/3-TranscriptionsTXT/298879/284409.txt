 July 1915 Wednesday 21 Dusty. On the brakesmans job again today with a new driver. A little Lancashire lad too. He always believes in taking short cuts through his sentences. We started to cart water to No 1 Aust. Hospital about 6 AM &amp; finished about 1.30 PM.
