<p>July 1918</p>
<p>July 1st<br/>Pack up costumes, properties etc. &amp; leave for Bertangles &ndash; We have had a very decent week at Vignacourt &ndash; Back to the bell tents in the woods &ndash; Bed made under the trees &ndash;<br/>July 2nd<br/>Rehearsal &amp; re-opening show in the "hangar" of 48th Squadron R.F.C. &ndash; At present very busy on long, new sketch &ndash; Heavy bombardments along the Front.</p>
