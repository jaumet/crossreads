Friday July 27th
Bapaume : hot day : reveille 5 am : boys went to Ligny on a bombing "stunt" : home at dinner time : packed up moved off at 4 pm : solid march very hot to Beaucourt : off packs went into the Ancre for a swim : tres bien : this place has seen plenty of "stoush" by the look of it : got one of a ruined chateau : bivouaked but the "skeetas" were very vicious :
Saturday July 28th
Beaucourt : hot : the boys on loading work : we S.B.s are on piquet work : the rest of the Bgde came in during the day had had a rough time especially
