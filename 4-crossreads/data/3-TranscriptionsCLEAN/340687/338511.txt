  
 July, 1917. Thursday 26 Fine day inclined to be sultry. Missed 1st parade by being too lazy. Fell in at 8.45 and dismissed to clean round huts. Played bridge. Fell in at 2.30 and dismissed & played bridge again. Went for swim in evening. Bed early Marching order to-morrow at 8. Probably moving at 12 from Albert or nearby for 12 hr journey to Steenwerck.
