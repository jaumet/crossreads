<p>April 1915<br/>Tuesday 20<br/>Physical drill. Fine day. Orders to go ashore in haversack and water bottle. Cool wind blowing</p>
<p>Wednesday 21<br/>Very <u>cold weather</u>. Nothing exciting to recite We are still lying idly here with many transports &amp; cruisers destroyers mine sweepers &amp; ships of every description. I managed to change the rest of my Egyptian money to one of the firemen aboard</p>
