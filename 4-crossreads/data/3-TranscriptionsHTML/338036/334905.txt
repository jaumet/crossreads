<p>[21st May contd]<br/>put in our time at playing 500 and the doc (Blumgren) spun a few yarns to pass away time.   When darkness Fritzs machine gun bullets began to whizz across the piece of hill over which we were to carry and later an infantry officer warned us that our light was visible.  On putting a blanket over the door things were quieter.  Time went on and at 10 it began to rain heavily.  At midnight a rumour came down with the news that the raid was over &ndash; a silent one and there were no casualties.  We found it very slippery on return journey &amp; arrived back at Charing Cross at 1.15.  No sooner had we arrived</p>
