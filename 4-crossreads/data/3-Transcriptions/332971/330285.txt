It does them a world of good and they love it.  Sometimes I feel like a kid myself and have a swing too.  I think I am right in saying that these grounds are supported by the Borough of Brighton. A wounded soldier who cannot now follow his old occupation takes care of it for them and he seems very contented in doing it.</p>
