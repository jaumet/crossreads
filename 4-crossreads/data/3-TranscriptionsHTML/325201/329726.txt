<p>I am now versed in the arts of poison gas &amp;  have to teach the young idean  fresh from innocuous  Australia how to put on &amp;  frighten him into a proper &amp;  wholesome <strike>rep</strike> respect for the gas.</p>
<p>Beach at Rafa<br/>Just a bit peevish tonight owing to the crackling of fires everywhere denoting tea &amp;   such like comforts in the making while we have no sugar or milk. All things considered we did quite a good move, down here to the beach. Thank goodness</p>
