<p>lots of clothing &amp; treasures.  The Military are not responsible &amp; she cannot send in a claim.  Rather disgusting to think that we cannot have proper protection from robbers.  It makes one very anxious about luggage.  I know I should be awfully annoyed if my trunk had been stolen.</p>
<p>I sleep upstairs &amp; have my trunk on the Balcony, so do not think the <u>brutes</u> would dare go upstairs.  Now the damage is done the door is locked every night &amp; the M. Police are told to watch our premises.  I shall try &amp; write a few lines to my friends next week.</p>
<p>Sorry I could not write on some of the photos but thought Mr. Censor may get them.</p>
