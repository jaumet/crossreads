<p>May 1918<br/>Saturday 18</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash;la-Daours<br/>Weather very warm, received a little present of eufs from a pal.<br/>A heavy thunder storm broke about 3 pm which greatly freshened things. At 6.30 pm went for a swim and swam Gen's horse.<br/>At 7 pm Fritz shelled village fairly severely with HV, HE and gas .<br/>9.30 pm Run to Bonnay a little rough.<br/>4.30 pm returned from run. Fritz still shelling . Plenty of bombs during night.</p>
