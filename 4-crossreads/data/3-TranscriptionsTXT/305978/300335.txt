 Cairo &amp; the train was rushed by a crowd of niggers selling all kinds of fruit &amp; cigarettes. The noise of them shouting out oringars &amp; other pecular names. I shall never forget. There was no tucker for us when we arrived at the camp so we had to go into Heliopolis for a feed &amp; the niggers did try &amp; do us on the prices as we had English money.
