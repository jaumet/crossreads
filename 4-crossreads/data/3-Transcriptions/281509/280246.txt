<p><br/>23 January 1916<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Duty in morning.  Tony &amp; self go to Watson's Bay in afternoon. Lunch at Marine Caf&eacute;.  Walk along cliffs. Pull up at Dean's. Afternoon tea. Mary &amp; Jean Aberdeen arrive. Stay to tea, a little singing &amp; home again 11 pm.<br/>Motors still blacklisted. <br/>Dwarf man on the Watson's Bay boat.</p>
