After pacifying the french & sending the Aussie away I dispersed crowd & then decided that such an exhibition of Australia must cease & so took him back to his rooms at the Angleterre 
 Meanwhile I had lost my friends but assuming that I should find them waiting for me at the dance made my way back to Hotel de la Poste & find that one cannot enter without a note of invitation from some one. Young however has got into conversation & into difficulties with a slight & delicately formed girl in a scarlet dress with a  red  black domino I am called in as the inevitable interpreter & do it faithfully. A very difficult task with a pretty girl especially as she live over Brasserie St Rue Place Cathedrale 
 However we leave about [indecipherable] go to Charley's Bar a dancing cabaret in Market Place at which one gives mysterious knocks & is admitted along a dark lane or channel for it is covered to dancing & bar room. However we do not stay long as place is crowded. 
 This is night of [indecipherable] & everyone is parading in fancy dress especially the younger element. Leaving Charley Bar we go to Hotel du Nord but find dancing is finished & then go on to  Cafe  Hotel de Paris where we stay for some time & then walk home via No 6 tram road to Casino Rouennaise
