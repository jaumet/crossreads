<p>in marching order 10.30am Destination Unknown- pass through Hazebrouck and other French villages- lunch on the road- march proving strenuous to many, encamped at 3pm in a barn at village of Ottersheene (11 miles  3&frac12;<br/>hours)<br/>April 5th<br/>March resumed in Marching Order at 10.30am suitable weather but bad roads pass through Strazeele, Steinberg(?) etc. mainly aeroplanes visible- signs German destruction in village.</p>
