<p>108<br/>the men soon settled down to a serious and earnest effort at singing.</p>
<p>There were some very good voices amongst them too, especially the cook whose father was a Minister by name of Mr Clydesdale. I read a portion of God's Word and then tried to speak to them on "conduct". They gave me a hearing, I was not much of a speaker but did my best.</p>
<p>The early morning train had to be unloaded at 3 or 4 o'clock of a morning and the chaps did not like to have to turn out so early.</p>
