 It look rainy in the morning but cleared up later.  We could get practically no news of the fighting but heard that the Australians had done well &amp; that casualties on both sides were heavy. 
 Tuesday 10th [Aug] Had an easy day.  Went round the harbour in the motor launch &amp; saw several transports loaded with freshly arrived soldiers &amp; saw the hospital ships transferring wounded to others. 
 Wednesday 11th Did absolutely nothing but rest.  We enjoyed a splendid swim in the bay in the afternoon.  There was a big gun bombardment at night &amp; early in the morning.  We heard the French had captured 2 trenches.
