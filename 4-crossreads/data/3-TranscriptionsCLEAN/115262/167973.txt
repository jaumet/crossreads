  
 Wednesday 25 July 1917. 
 No riding this morning but swimming parade instead, and on return to camp were marched back through Nafisha and Nefiche. 
 The "Tommy" Howitzer Battery is preparing to move out and our unit has now been issued with 24 hrs. rations (iron) as the A.S.C. Depot has moved this morning. 
 Inoculated again against Para-typhoid (a double dose.)
