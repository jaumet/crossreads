<p>11 &ndash; be very useful, she has a niece over there whose fianc&eacute;e was shot in France and who poor girl is trying to forget her sorrow in work. Emmie was going to see three widows, whose husbands' deathbeds she had been at.<br/>Did I tell you Mrs Man asked me to meet her again and we had tea together last Wednesday her husband is in charge of the Ordnance depot at Gallipoli. She is a charming woman. She is trying to send him necessary kit &ndash; but delivery of parcels is very difficult</p>
