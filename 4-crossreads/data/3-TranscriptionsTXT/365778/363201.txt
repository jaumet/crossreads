 Saturday 16/6/17 Two years since leaving Sydney. Marched to Varennes and proceeded by train to Bapaume &ndash; relieving 8th Bde. Sunday 17/6/17 Church Parade. Wrote to Doff. Played 28th cricket &amp; won &ndash; I made 48 top score. Monday 18/6/17 Training as usual. Bas had dinner with me &ndash; rode home with him &amp; saw Martin. Tuesday 19/6/17 Training &amp; preparation for Field Day tomorrow, raining slightly, letters from Doff.
