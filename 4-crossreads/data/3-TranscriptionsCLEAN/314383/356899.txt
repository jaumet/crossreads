
July 4thDiddler [nickname for Captain Wilson] is out holding on a village with a N.Z. Sgt.  Got wind today that the Kurds are going to raid the place.  Diddler's people (12 Kiwis) reckon they will fight so Starnes sent out another Sgt.
Mail today, 39 letters & 1 parcel.  Good O.  This is the first mail yet received which Fisher brought back from Hamadan.
July 5thDead  tired.  The Gov. Sirdar, Com. of Police, Com of Postal Services, Com of Telegraph Service & others came along to dinner.  The Sidah provided the goods, for which we paid.  The meal commenced at 9 pm. & went on with intervals until 3 am
