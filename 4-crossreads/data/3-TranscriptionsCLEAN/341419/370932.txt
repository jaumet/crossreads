 Coming along at a good pace we came about 9 miles past Es Salt towards Ammon & there made camp at about 4 p.m.  They were fighting at  Es Salt  Ammon all day today & I now hear that it fell this afternoon yielding only 150 prisoners. 
 So I expect we shall move on again in a day or two. 
 Ammon is on the Hadjaz Railway hence its importance.
