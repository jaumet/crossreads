 This letter is from No. 169. Trooper Watson F Adamson. A. Co.  D. Troop 1st A.L.H. and is written from Gallipoli.  He was wounded at Lone Pine 7/8/15 and sent to London.  He is  sill  still away 9.8.19. &amp; is Sergt at H. Quarters.  London. T G Adamson. father. Nemingha Tamworth N.S.W.
