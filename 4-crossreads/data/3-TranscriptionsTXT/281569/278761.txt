 12 December, 1916 Tuesday 
 Examined candidates for school. Received 8 parcels &amp; 7 letters, met Cyril Downes. Went to the movies with the Doctor. 
 13 December, 1916 Wednesday 
 Left Moascar for Alex at 2 arrived Sidi Gaba 7.30 was met at station &amp; am now quartered at Cleopatra
