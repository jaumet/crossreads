<p><strike>such my</strike>  My main impression of it is an intense desire, sometimes for water, sometimes for tobacco; and above all an   <strike>overwhleming</strike>  overwhelming sense of irritable  sleepiness inable to be appeased: for from 6 oclock on Monday morning until 2 oclock Thursday morning I only had four hours of sleep altogether.<br/>My only comment is "God forbid that we shall go on many more of these obnoxious affairs"<br/>As you see I tried taking my diary with me and jotting down things as they occurred, but it is not much good as one has not time, inclination or enough light.</p>
