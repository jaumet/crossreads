<p>3rd A.Gen.H.<br/>B.E.H.<br/>France<br/>7.10.17</p>
<p>My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>Had a lovely lot of home letters this last week. One from dear old Belle 16th July with darling Mother's of 17th July. I am so pleased you had such a happy day  &amp;  everything is so successful.</p>
<p>How I should love to be home again, but there is heaps of work to be done.</p>
<p>Poor old Kelly   &amp;  yours truly are disappointed about</p>
