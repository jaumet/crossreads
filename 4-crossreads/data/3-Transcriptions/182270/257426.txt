
<p>THURSDAY, APRIL 24, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Went into town &amp; got</p>
<p>my railway pass ect in the</p>
<p>morning. Met Linda &amp; had</p>
<p>lunch at Farmers, &amp; then caught</p>
<p>the 2 pm train home with</p>
<p>Father &amp; mother. Struan met</p>
<p>us at Quirindi and we</p>
<p>came straight out in the car.</p>
