<p>Paris on the 7th November 1918</p>
<p>My dear Sir,</p>
<p>Not be surprised, if you receive my letter before having your answer. &nbsp;I write to you this letter, for I have much time: I am in vacation for a week.</p>
<p>How the life in Paris has come again thanks to the very good communiques.</p>
<p>Sunday on the 27th October here was a review in the honour of the class 1920 which has passed the revision in August and September. &nbsp;I was summoned for the filing off.</p>
