<p>There are wet canteens here and they are already making a nuisance. On board the boat the chaps including habitual drinkers got on excellently without a drop and all the good condition which they got there has been already lost. If Australian troops had the saying as to their eradication or not I believe we would not have them.</p>
<p>Monday<br/>On arriving here we were placed under quarantine owing to suspected meningitis We were not allowed to leave our little camp.
