<p>1916<br/>Sat. Jan. 1st -16</p>
<p>Train from Black Boy Hill Camp to Fremantle, boarded A35 again at 2.30 p.m.  Sailed 5 p.m. for unknown destination.</p>
<p>Sun. Jan. 2nd -16<br/>Received first mail.<br/>Church Service</p>
<p>Mon. Jan. 3rd -16<br/>Innoculated for second time.</p>
<p>Tue. Jan. 4th -16<br/>Fell out of my hammock in the middle of night owing to heavy roll of boat.</p>
<p>Wed. Jan. 5th -16<br/>We are just approaching the tropics.  The weather is getting warmer each day.</p>
<p>Thur. Jan. 6th -16<br/>Start of heavyweight Boxing Tournament.  Pay Day.</p>
