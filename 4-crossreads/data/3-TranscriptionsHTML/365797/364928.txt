<p>He is attached to Dene's Battalion</p>
<p>Miss Coates has been taking Rollo's photo &amp; he is to print one himself for you. Miss Huntly has undertaken his music &amp; when she asked him to show her [indecipherable] he said "oh I can't, you have to go to Manly".</p>
<p>Dorothy is learning type-writing as she doesn't seem to be able to get constant work at illustrating. It is a pity as there is such good money to be earned &amp; she has put in a lot of time I only hope she will try to get some work done for the Bulletin as it pays so well.</p>
