Nest morning had a hurried breakfast   &  got to the boat at 8.30 a.m. It was cold, wet   &  windy. After struggling with baggage   &  making those wretched foreigners do what we wanted   &  waiting on the boat until after 10 Well it was simply raining cats   &  dogs - had to get back to our Hostel, dry our boots   &  clothing. Had a hurried luncheon   &  had to be on the boat by 1.30 p.m. She sailed at 2.30 p.m.   &  it was so rough that every body was sea-sick. Kelly   &  I stayed in Ladies Cabin. Kelly had never been sea-sick before. We though    Leave   was worth it. Arrived in London about 5 p.m.
