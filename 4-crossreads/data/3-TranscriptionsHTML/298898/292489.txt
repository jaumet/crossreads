<p>Amb. this evidently going to be our rest. 'Twas good to see our old friends again. The Albert Bapaume road was a great sight. The road itself was in a good state &amp; had thousands of men working on it, laying metal widening etc., it being the main road in the place. Saw large numbers of Indians coming in mostly cavalry who appeared to be a workmanlike crowd &amp; new artillery also on the march. Had several taubes over during the morning but they kept very high. Rode on the waggon down as I had hurt my foot on a nail. Received the long looked for mail today.</p>
<p>Saturday 7th April<br/>Discovered today that yesterday was "Good Friday". I had totally forgotten Easter but will try &amp; go to church tomorrow if I can locate a service. Have been</p>
