<p>27 - Looks very well. Is very interested in the girls. I think the work at the hospital has rejuvenated him. Work is good for all &ndash; it's wonderful it's effect - on the body &amp; mind but I wont add platitudes to this already copious letter.<br/>Devoted daughter<br/>Betha McMillan<br/>Please give this to Edith Wilson to read.
