<p>27th<br/>To prevent ourselves from going stone crazy the Doctor and I went for a bike ride into Daours passing through Fouilly &amp; Aubigny.  We called in to see a friend of the Doc's a Major Copplestone of the Sanitary Detachment, also a Medical Officer.  He invited us inside his luxurious billet &amp; played a piece of Schuman's to us on a very grand and beautifully toned grand piano.</p>
