<p>Monday 11/3/18&nbsp; Two Coys working two training, had a game of cricket- Two letters from Doff- Billie has been sick.</p>
<p>Tuesday 12/3/18. Robinson back from leave took over raiders. Going to musketry demonstration tomorrow. Wrote to Doff</p>
<p>Wednesday 13/3/18&nbsp; Left by car to Bailleul then by train St. Omer &amp; bus to Musketry School at Nordbecourt arrived about 5.30 p.m</p>
<p>Thursday 14/3/18&nbsp; Saw students at work and some demonstrations&nbsp; dined at night with Thelander who is at the school</p>
