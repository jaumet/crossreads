 October 1915 
 Friday 29 
 Tent pitching &amp; odd jobs. 
 Turkish fire very heavy in morning 
 Several shells fell in over our camp &amp; a great number just over the hill. 
 Shrapnel falling all over the place. 
 Roberts hit by spent pellet, but only bruised. 
 Jack [indecipherable] &amp; two others sent away on the sick list. 
 The number of sick sent away is alarming. 
 Heard that Allied troops are landing at Salonica to help Servians.
