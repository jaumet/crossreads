<p>Mrs Rice (with whom we are lodging) certainly knows the Art of Cooking &ndash; At 7 p.m. the curtain rises &amp; the Anzac Coves are tendered a wonderful reception by the Manchester people &ndash; Very difficult programme for few of us &ndash; Enthusiasm &amp; Appreciation of Audience.<br/>April 9th<br/>Mail from London &ndash; Read O.Henry &ndash; Visit to Food Controllers Office for purpose of procuring Pickels for meat, butter, &amp; sugar and tea etc. &ndash; See the matinee at Hippodrome &ndash; on the Bill are:&ndash; Ruth Vincent. Lupino Lane. Herbert Waring etc.</p>
