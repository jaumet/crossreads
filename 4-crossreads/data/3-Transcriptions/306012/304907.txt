(I visited this field some months ago.)<br/>The town of Corbie on the Somme was besieged by the Spaniards in 1636 in the 30 years war against Spain &amp; Austria.<br/>By the treaty of Utrecht in 1713 France had to give to England Newfoundland &amp; her Hudson Bay Territory.  By the same treaty England became the greatest naval power of Europe.  In 1761 Britain captured France's colonies in India (except 5 towns).
