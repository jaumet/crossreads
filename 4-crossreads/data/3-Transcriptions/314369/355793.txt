<p>6 March 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Cross Equator at 1 am this morning<br/>Father Neptune comes on board w/- his retinue   &amp;  court They start w/- the major then the rest of the officers But the fun started with the men Neptune's police had numerous fights with some of the victims</p>
<p>1915 March 7<br/>Sunday<br/>3rd in Lent</p>
<p>We get the first sight of Ceylon in the morning<br/>Keep just off the coast all day expect to make Colombo tomorrow.</p>
