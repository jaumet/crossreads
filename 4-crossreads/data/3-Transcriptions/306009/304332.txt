<p>1915<br/>May. 26 Wed.<br/>Not much doing earlier part of day so wrote several letters to Sydney.<br/>In afternoon received telephone message to be ready for patients to be transferred from No. 15 General Hospital, Alexandria &amp; about 6 p.m. 146 arrived in motor ambulances &amp; were admitted pretty quickly but smoothly.<br/>In the evening went for a stroll with Snow Freeman &ndash; How fine the old Nile looks in the moonlight.<br/>Although hot in the daytime a cool breeze springs up at night.<br/>Really must learn as much Arabic &amp; French as possible &ndash; they are so useful.</p>
