  
 Monday 23rd Hot again. Yesterday I skipped church and was told I'd be paraded this morning, but it happened I made the offence worse by not falling in this morning. I was helping the mess orderlies decorate their tables and so forgot all about parades. However the O.C. let me off as he wanted our men to win again.
