<p>It may be interesting to know that certain men were appointed from the Battalion to mend boots. Also we had a post-corporal whose duty it was to deliver the mail and the Parcels to us. There was the Butcher &ndash; in fact we were quite a community in ourselves.</p>
<p>Then the Battalion always had a chaplain who went by the name of "Padre" &ndash; to the "Father". As a rule we only had the Official Church Parade on fortunate occasions extra services were held the attend</p>
