<p>1916 August 13<br/>Sunday  <br/>Church parade at 9. 30 a.m. outside village. All 1st Brigade in hollow square. Gen. Birdwood attended &amp; presented military crosses to 9 men. Speech: @[?] stretcher bearers &amp; runners specially commended by Gen. <br/>Scene on hill side across valley. browns &amp; greys.<br/>View of firing line from windmill. 37 balloons  Service under trees. --- Fighting Mac.</p>
