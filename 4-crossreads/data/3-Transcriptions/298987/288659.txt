<p>Thursday 21st<br/>Finished guard at 9 aM Just the same old thing as at Liverpool only less style &amp; no arms to carry. There has been some mixup with the blankets &amp; hammocks. Two many are supposed to have been issued &amp; the Pursar was collecting spare one this morning &amp; got mine &amp; most of the guards while we were being relieved am without a bunk tonight</p>
<p>Friday 22nd<br/>Weather has improved greatly. <strike>Shoe</strike> Deck shoes were issued today. We do a little drill on deck now about &frac12; an hour morning &amp; afternoon After a good deal of trouble got a hammock &amp; blankets for my self &amp; any others of the Guard who were short.<br/>Reported we are to call at Fremantle</p>
<p>Saturday &amp; Sunday 23 &amp; 4<br/>Two nice days. Nothing out of the way has happened. Much letter</p>
