<p>1917 Monday 25 June</p>
<p>A fair night, Pittams quite recovered.  Skinner &amp; Medhurst almost out of wood, also Plant,  Farrow new admission bad case &amp; <u>KELLY</u>.  A typical Irishman, an awful worry, high querelous voice calls 'orderly' incessantly. Bunny hug.  <br/>Lena Ashwell gave concert but had to leave after first 2 items</p>
