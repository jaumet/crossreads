  
 Sept 30th :- Patrolled out to El Eras which is about 10 miles East of Bally Bunion. There is a very fine Hod here in which we had breakfast & many dates, & remained till mid-day. After lunch we patrolled to Hill 300 & then home. Oct 1st : O'Connor, Hind,  Martin  & myself went down to Ballah by the light railway &  had a couple of hours very fine swimming in the Suez Canal. Oct 2nd :- In the afternoon went swimming again in the Canal at Ballah. One Troop from the 2nd Brigade arrived to relieve us.
