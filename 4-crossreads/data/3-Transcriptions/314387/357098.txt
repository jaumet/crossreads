<p>Tuesday May 14th<br/>Same as usual<br/>Wednesday 15th<br/>Went for a bath in morning details went up to line night time went to pictures<br/>Thursday 16th<br/>Shooting in morning afternoon sports.<br/>Friday May 17th<br/>Same as previous day warned to go to line<br/>Saturday 18th<br/>Cooks fatigue morning afternoon went to Corps sports and to St Ouen.</p>
