<p>There was too a very steep hill in the vicinity. This place proved no exception to the rule.</p>
<p>This hill climbing at the end of the route-march was always a test for us with our packs. It is a saying of mine that I never fell out of a route march on the way. On one occasion I sat down &ndash; I could have finished with the rest.</p>
<p>After going through the manoeuvres we were marched back to our old "home" Buire.</p>
<p>Along the route near Frecourt we noticed the graves of some Germans who had been killed hereabouts in the fighting.</p>
