<p>Feb. Tuesday 20 1917</p>
<p>Put in a most rotten night &amp; find this morning myself very much out of sorts. Hot turns &amp; cold shivers vomiting &amp; pains all over body. Stay in bed all day in the beastly old dugout. How we're beginning to hate the stink of these foul holes. <br/>Two shrap bursts not far away late this evening get two men, killed one &amp; severely wound Sergt. Hennessy in head. <br/>Brown &amp; the M.O. tell each other off. Brown to be removed for insolence. Storm in tea cup. <br/>The boys have couple of carries in dark. I'm unable to lift my head. An awful journey over the mud &amp; shell holes in the black darkness. The game's "no bon."</p>
