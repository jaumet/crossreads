<p>12 Monday<br/>Arrived at camp about 5 am.  Very beautiful country all the way from plymouth.  Every <strike>thing </strike>where nice &amp; green.  Camp 4 miles from Railway station &amp; 75 miles from London.  Seems very little prospects of getting leave.  Issued with bed &amp; 2 extra blankets.  Living on Bully beef &amp; biscuits all day.  Very long days here, twilight lasts till about 11 pm &amp; daylight starts about 2 am.</p>
<p>13 Tuesday<br/>Orders as follows reveille 5.30 am, fall in 6 am 9.30 am &amp; 2.30.  Went on sick parade for tonsalitis and was given a gargle.  Saw Joe Todd on sick parade.  We are quartered in huts here, with electric lights &amp; hot water baths.  Every convenience specified in &amp; considered by all to be a real decent camp.  Went down Shipton village.</p>
<p>14 Wednesday<br/>The church in Shipton village dates back as far as 1560 &amp; graves (discernable) 1741.  Kit inspection held all the morning.  A very cold wind blowing nearly all day <strike>nearly</strike>.  Went for a walk into Tidworth in the evening &amp; took 4 spools of Films to be developed.  Sun set here is about 9 pm.  of course the daylight saving system puts it back one hour.  Daylight saving system is putting Greenwich time back 1 hour.</p>
