<p>the 20th Battalion crawled down the Sap called "Ellen Alley" , a distance of 1000 yards under machine gunfire and a shower of shells from the enemy's' Howitzer batteries. Not much cover was available, the sap was hardly 3 feet deep. Very soon the fight for the trench system East of Clery commenced.<br/>At 2 pm several companies of the 20th Battalion were bombing away into "Barge Alley"  until progress was arrested by heavy fire from some German Machine guns. A patrol from "A"  Company of the 20th Battalion went forward to reconnoitre. The enemy compelled this patrol to return to  " Ellen Alley" .
