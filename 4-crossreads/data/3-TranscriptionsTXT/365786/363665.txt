 And one does see some wonderful sights there &ndash; we saw  a home  one place, 4 houses of wives, the women are veiled some with fine muslin, others with coarse black cloth or lace on a thing like a cotton reel.  The girls are married from 12 to 15 as a rule, don't see husband till after the ceremony.  Native costume dirty &amp; unhygienic &ndash; flies, beggars, dirt, disease.
