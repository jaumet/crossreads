<p>awaiting the result of the application, which will be known on Friday &ndash; today is Wednesday 23rd Aug.<br/>X X X X X<br/>So ends my now second diary at a critical point, for any day I may be sent to France &amp; - things are far from healthy out there I believe.<br/>If I could look into the future what would I see?  Well here's for a dip into Diary No. 3 &amp; let's hope it will be the last one.</p>
