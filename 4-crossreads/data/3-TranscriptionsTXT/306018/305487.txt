
February 1918All the Australian papers are there & you can play cards at one of the many little tables if you please.  It is a good idea & keeps a good number of the fellows in.  An Australian soldiers club has been started close to our quarters & you can buy most of the necessary wants there at a reasonable rate.I went to the Races on Saturday afternoon with SQMS Halloran  There was a great crowd there & we had a good afternoons sport.  One meets so many people there, military & others.  It is really the only day of the week.  On Sunday Long & self went out to Briggs & stayed for afternoon tea & supper.  A Major Yuill was there of the AAMC & a Mr Ayres a local resident.  Major Yuill has just returned from England where he was married.  I met him just before he went to England at the Briggs.
