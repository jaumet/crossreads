 13 April, Sunday 
 April Showers &amp; windy. 
 I pack up &amp; get bus tat 11 am for Park House. Arrive on good order &amp; condition. After dinner I go to Cholderton with Ern &amp; another poacher. It rains like the deuce. I make a hit with my Red Cross Badge with some V.A.D.'s from the V.D. Hospital for W.A.A.C.'s. Come home for tea &amp; write all night. Recd letter from Jennie with note from Mamie Jeffs on back. 
 Wtote M.R.L , Home, D.J's. 
 14 April, Monday 
 W &amp; Windy 
 Dreadful night of wind &amp; rain. Recd letter &amp; films from Ellis. Go down Shipton for walk. It is flooded. We have a drink at the Boot Inn. I see Capt Orr. Read all afternoon &amp; evening. 
 Wrote Jennie, Roy &amp; Dick.
