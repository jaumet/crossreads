<p>4<br/>shell. Well they (the shells) found this poor chap &amp; several men with him some 100 yards from the entrance to our Camp &ndash; with the result I of our men Killed &ndash; another wounded (slightly) a friend of theirs lost his arm &amp; a tommy Killed by shock.<br/>Bad luck for the poor chaps &ndash; Pieces of shell used to fall near us as we played Cricket, but evidently "the numbers were not up" for the rest of us.<br/>Glad to say I am well &amp; hope Will &amp; the children are well also.<br/>In case you did not get my letter of about a fortnight ago &ndash; I again wish you many happy returns of your birthday.<br/>With very much love dear old girl to you all. Always your loving brother,<br/>Fred.</p>
