Packed up this morning &amp; reported at B.O.R. at 1300 &amp; walked down to the station at Amesbury where we caught the train, the country looks magnificent after yesterday's heavy rainstorms, all the hedges are snowy white &amp; the fields present a great contrast of colour with the daisies &amp; buttercups in full bloom &amp; horses &amp; cattle feeding contentedly.
