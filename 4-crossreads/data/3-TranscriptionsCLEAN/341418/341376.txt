  
 Romaneh - The district in which our troops have a standing camp. Extends over a large area consisting of a number of Palm grove Katieh - A well of good water  &  a few Palm groves A Regt. of Yeomanry were surrounded here in April 16 Er Arbah - Salt water marsh  &  grove. Oghratina - Single big Palm grove  &  wells backed by big sand dune. A whole Squadron of the Yeomanry wiped out here one man surviving same time as Katieh Dubarbos - Palm grove  &  wells in which we made our temporary base for the advance on  Birelabd - which is a small  grove with old well  &  full of some herb which when crushed gives out a strong musk like scent very noticeable in the dark.
