<p>always  find more. Nestles chocolates are a favourite item &amp; he has "sold out" many times. Films for a camera cost 1/- each (7d on land)  A reasonable profit is expected, but the prices are fabulous. Blankets are taken from us today &amp; only hammocks left to lie on. I hid mine until all was quiet &amp; then got it out &amp; it was quite acceptable. Hammocks are to go in today but I am hiding mine again. This is only done to save the stewards any trouble. Sunday today &amp; I start by having to appear before the doctors who have lost my medical papers - my luck again.</p>
<p>6/2/16  At lights out, the military police came round unexpectedly &amp; began searching for blankets, so had to be quiet. They passed me however &amp; I passed the night in warmth. 7/2/16 First thing to do was to dump the blanket out of the port &ndash; farewell to it. All day is</p>
