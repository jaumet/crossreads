<p>August1915</p>
<p>Thursday 12</p>
<p>Sick parade started at 6.45 AM for a change one or two minor accidents to deal with during the day chiefly a cut on the head from falling [indecipherable] Fire alarm in the afternoon, went well</p>
<p>Hunter refused to touch V.D. patients &amp; put in his place by the doctor.</p>
<p>Have only written one &amp; that letter to Rita, so far,</p>
