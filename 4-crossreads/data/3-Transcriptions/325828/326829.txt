<p>I think perhaps I had better come down home for a while, where I can get them right, i.e. if I may.  It would also allow of me seeing Walter &amp; Rid before they left, but I'll see how I get on with my legs.<br/>Very cold S.E winds been blowing the last few days &amp; it makes things very wintry.</p>
<p>Must close now, with love to all,<br/>Your loving son<br/>Leo.<br/>P.S. Thank you for your explanation of "efficacious".  I wondered what the meaning of it was.  There is just another word that is puzzling us, &amp; that is "phrenologist".  I've seen the advertisement in the "D.T." of a Henry Jones, phrenologist, Sydney.  Leo.</p>
