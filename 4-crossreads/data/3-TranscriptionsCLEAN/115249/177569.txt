  
 Ship of Origin - Flag 
 Date - 8 - 9 - 14 
 Time received or sent. 2. 5 
 RA to officer Commanding troops, CO Sydney, Commander D & Yarra, Information has been received as follows. At Kapanga about ten miles South of Cape Gazelle lives a Samoan woman named Gracie Schmidt who is known to be loyal to the British and who would if required send a message to her half brother Henry Cox who lives at Kapopo close to Kabakaul and would willingly provide at least forty native guides for the troops at Kabakaul i[f]
