<p>May, 1916<br/>Wednesday 17<br/>In the early morning parade we practiced standard tests.<br/>After breakfast the N.C.Os had their written Exam &amp; the Instructors corrected &amp; marked their books.<br/>A number of "Stokes Trench Howitzers" arrive<br/>Night Operations<br/>Temp&deg;</p>
<p>Thursday 18<br/>Put in all day at stoppages &amp; Standard tests.<br/>Weather much cooler only 99&deg;<br/>Am now going to the usual concert.</p>
