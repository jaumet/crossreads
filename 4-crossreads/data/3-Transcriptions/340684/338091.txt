<p>364<br/>It seems strange to be in England again &ndash; I'm afraid I wasn't a model child when I was here before and am further still from being a model now &ndash; However Auntie dear I'll try my best to be good &ndash;I'll write and let you know where they truck me off to on Saturday<br/>Much love<br/>From your affectionate nephew<br/>Gibson McMillan</p>
