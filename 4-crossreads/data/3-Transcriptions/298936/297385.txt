<p>July 1915</p>
<p>Friday 30</p>
<p>Arrived in Melbourne at 1.30 &amp; berthed at Port Melbourne 2.30.</p>
<p>Mails revealed nothing for me. No leave granted. Men annoyed at first. Talk of breaking leave &amp; at that Capt Hordern attempted to procure it from Commandant but got no reply.  Men broke out in a body at 4 p.m. but repulsed by guard.</p>
<p>Fresh guard brought up &amp; fixed bayonets ready.</p>
<p>Another attempt at dark ending in bloodshed</p>
