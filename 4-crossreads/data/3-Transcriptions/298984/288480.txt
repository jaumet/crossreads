<p>on Australia not very bright. Lost 4/6 at Poker first time I have played for money in the army. Have been playing a good deal of Crib lately. Our temperatures are taken daily &amp; we go through a fumigation</p>
<p>Friday 2nd<br/>Have sliped a day some of above should bee put in here. Each day is so much alike that one loses count.</p>
<p>Saturday 3rd<br/>Kangaroo Isle sighted about 10 &amp; we are now</p>
