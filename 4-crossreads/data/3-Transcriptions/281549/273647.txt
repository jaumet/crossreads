<p>When the old farmer came &amp; looked at it, he was very pleased because we had done the job so quick , but next winter he will get a big surprise when he sees them all grow up again, I hope he puts his cows into the field &amp; gets them all poisoned, It will make him wish that he had given us better food.</p>
<p>September 20th I had another fight with a chap belonging to the 16th Battallion . It only lasted a few minutes &amp; was another victory for yours truly.</p>
