<p>(exception of the time I rode from Poperinghe to Woodcote Farm, just outside Ypres.) that I had been on a bicycle since leaving Tasmania &amp; that's a fair while ago. The examination results came out this afternoon &amp; to my surprise I got 69 just 1 off "Very good or first class" there was no one more surprised than I am over it, I never expected it, for this is the first school you might say I have ever been to &amp; besides it is a very hard one to pass, some of the skites &amp; windbags have got a terrible shock I can assure you, I am 33 on the list out of over a hundred men, of which nearly every one of them</p>
