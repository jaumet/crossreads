<p>April Saturday 7 1917</p>
<p>No doubt some of the homefolk will be at the Show in Sydney today.   Wouldn't it be "bon" to be there instead of here at old Bazentin.   After a beautiful sunny day yesterday, rain set in again last evening.   Slight snow fell again this morning.<br/>A. &amp; B. Sec. bearers get orders to move on to Bapaume this morning.    I bade goodbye to the boys with the best of luck.   Severance.<br/>Pay this morning &ndash; good fortune.   I was reduced to 1 &frac12; d.</p>
