<p>in column of companies.  As we progressed toward the other end we saw the first troops begin to march past.  They were the Light Horse, and looked absolutely magnificent in their long lines.<br/>After marching quite a mile we marched past in fairly good order, the review was at an end.<br/>While the mass of troops was sitting down in the left corner of the field the King came along on his way out, &amp; one &amp; all left their food to help the parting cheers</p>
