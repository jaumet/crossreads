<p>31 Wednesday</p>
<p>Grey day.  After mid-day meal walked with Smith &amp; Mr. G. Brown up to the camp of M. Leon Treveau who gave us cakes.  I cd. not go quickly &amp; my leg hurt me right down to the sole of the foot.<br/>Matron went to Salonique to convalesce.</p>
