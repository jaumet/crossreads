  
 Thursday 18 November 1915  Went to Mena with Dr Henry (from Grafton ) & some others.  Climbed the 2 nd  pyramid & waved the Australian flag upon it - Kephren -  Left Henry & the other men to climb the first pyramid (Cheops) but when I met them again I was told that none of them had reached the top. Brought some pieces of Alabaster from base of 2 d  pyramid. 
 Friday 19 November 1915  Board on pyjamas at 12 noon.  Went with Major Brown for a trip past the Citadel, the Sulphur Springs to Old ( deserted ) Cairo, near to the Windmil Hills, & home via Gezeirah & Cairo.  Writing during evng. 
 Saturday 20 November 1915    The anniversary of my Fathers death in 1885 R.I.P. - Posted letters to Girls, Mollie, M. M. Knowles, Geo Varley, & Pharmtical [Pharmaceutical] Journal in Melbourne.  At Andebean's place 6 Shareh El Murah, Heliopolis.  Visit [Lieut.] Col. Nicholson at Zeitoun at 12-30  p.m. Saw L. Col Nicholson at Zeitoun. 
 Sunday 21 November 1915  Mass at 9am at Bassilica  Writing & reading all day. Visit [Lieut.] Col. Nicholson at Zeitoun at 6 p.m. At Convent of the Africian Mission at Zeitoun from 6-30 to 8-30 p.m. Vespers & Benediction.
