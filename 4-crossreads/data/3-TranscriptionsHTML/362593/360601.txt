<p>Sunday 15 September 1918</p>
<p>Monday 16 September 1918<br/>Recco to Deraa [also spelt Daraa].<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 3 h. 5 m.  Landed at Jerusalem and left from there with formation of 7 O.H. 9s who bombed.  1 crashed on way home.  Bombing good.  Many direct hits.  Interesting recco.</p>
