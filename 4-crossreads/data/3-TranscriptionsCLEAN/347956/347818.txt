The horses are all in good condition & were rather shy of the niggers. arrived at Maadi at 10.15 PM & I was told off for picquet duty till one o'clock.  most of the lads had leave the night before & I had not gone out so I did not make a fuss over picquet although we are exempt from it by right;  I was very tired when one oclock came & I took no rocking to sleep
