 When finishing, we hear of Zonnebaeke [also spelt Zonnebeke] taken by the British forces, and we expect more success tonight, because the guns have pretty well thundered last night & this morning.  Probably you have heard of it long before you will receive this letter, etc. etc. 
 Louis Casiez
