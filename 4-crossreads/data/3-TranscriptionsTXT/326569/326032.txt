 Tuesday March 27th, 1917 Passed Eddystone Lighthouse.  Arrived Plymouth about 1.45 a.m.  Left boat about 10 a.m. &ndash; entrained in 3rd class carriages at Devonport Dockyard for Dinton nr. Salisbury.  Arrived Dinton about 6 p.m. &amp; at Hurdcott Camp No.11 about 8 p.m.  Very cold, but carriages had steam heaters, made them very comfortable.  Supplied at Queen St., Exeter with Buns &amp; tea by Lady Mayoress &amp; helpers. 
 Wednesday 28/3/17 Very cold, frosty.  Inspection in full marching order &amp; dull during day. 
 Thursday 29/3/17 Placed in contact as later developed mumps.  Have drill, just the once during day though.
