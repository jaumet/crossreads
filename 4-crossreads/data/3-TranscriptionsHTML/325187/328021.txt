<p>[Thursday 5 October 1916]</p>
<p>Laura to Tea    Theatre for 3.<br/>Gladys .  Mrs Cotton came &amp; brought me Hay Graziers-<br/>The Doctor Jagger wants me to have an operation on my nose as he considers the bullet I got in SA is the cause of my constant Bronchitis.</p>
