<p>back. three cheers were given for the Prince &amp; a big hoot was given for the Brigadier Gen who was responsible for the conditions &amp; plans. It would have been a great success if we had marched at night &amp; rested by day We did not stay at Moascar long we were off the same day &amp; marched by the Prince &amp;&nbsp;Gen Birdwood the boys soon &nbsp;began to drop out again &amp; we picked them up &amp; carried their equipment. Their feet were very sore &amp; blistered, we then marched through Ismalia the pretty town I have already refered how lovely &amp; beautiful it seemed avenues of trees &amp; flowers &amp; two little flowing streams along each side of the road for irrigation purposes.</p>
