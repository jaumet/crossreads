<p>Jly 24 my dial snapped with several other boys. Either we sail shortly, or else go before another Board.</p>
<p>25 Fun galore until after midnight. Laugh! I've never laughed so much before. Some of the boys have been reboarded. Secured 11 oclock pass, so am down on the Prom amusing myself. Went to the pictures; Not a bad show. Pretty well dark now at 11 oclock. Bought Pittmans shorthand Teacher.</p>
<p>26 Several of the boys going to Dorchester.  40 chaps came down</p>
