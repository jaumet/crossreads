 24 October 1915 Sunday 21st After Trinity 
 After church parade go into Cairo with Dad   &amp;  visit the ancient   &amp;  Historical mosques of Sultan Hassan   &amp;  the Coronation Mosque Later we are shown the old Coptic church   &amp;  the Crypt in which the Holy family hid from Herod. it contains Pictures 900 yrs old. 
 1915 October 25 Monday 
 This afternoon all details in the Bn. were lined up to find out how many were fit   &amp;  ready to return to the front
