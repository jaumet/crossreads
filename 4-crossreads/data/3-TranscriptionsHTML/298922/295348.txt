<p>Monday 22 January 1917</p>
<p>I have about 160/-/- in Bank in England.<br/>I have about 293/-/- Anglo Egyptian Bank Cairo<br/>[Total] 453/-/-.<br/>My deferred pay 100 about<br/>[Total] &pound;555/-/-</p>
<p>How much is in Savings Bank in Deniliquin please!  Interest could be entered in Pass Bk. if you have it.</p>
<p>Khamsin blowing &ndash; odd Turkscome in they know we will treat them decently &amp; not feed the vultures on them like they do the Armenians, some terrible stories are told of what they have been doing to these unfortunate people.</p>
<p>One Turk had 2 bullets in him, shot by the Bedouins who got a lot of Rifles etc. from Rafa &amp; Magdaba.</p>
