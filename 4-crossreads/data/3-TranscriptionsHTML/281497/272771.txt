<p>We have men all over Ypres living in cellars &amp; the majority have electric lights it seems strange but true.   Ypres is like a dead city for there is not a sound as you march through her only your own echoing footsteps.   Who would have thought a few years back that this Belgian town would resound to the steps of Australian soldiers.</p>
