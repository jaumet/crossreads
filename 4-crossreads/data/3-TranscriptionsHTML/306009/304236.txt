<p>1915<br/>Feb. 19 Fri.<br/>850 in hospital.<br/>Worked until 4.30 p.m. &amp; then went off to Cairo with S.M. Joyner &ndash; had tea &amp; then went to 1st Cairo B-P Scout's clubroom where met several Territorials &amp; a New Zealander (mostly S.M.s} &ndash; about 22 boys present &ndash; some of their work was very good &ndash; must attend some more of their meetings on a Monday, Wednesday or a Friday night &ndash; Returned to the camp in a taxi which cost me 10 piastres.<br/>Note &ndash; My total wealth now amounts to 1 piastre (2&frac12;d).</p>
