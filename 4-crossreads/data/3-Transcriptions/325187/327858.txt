<p>[Easter Monday 24 April 1916]</p>
<p>Court all day today as <strike>all evidence </strike> Evidence is given through an Interpreter HINDUSTANI &amp; again sometimes in ARABIC with the Native witnesses it is a long tedious case.<br/>Did I tell you it was a case of a Sikh soldier murdering his Native Officer shot him in the night while asleep but evidence is difficult to get, &amp; the Witnesses swear Black is White &amp; vice versa.<br/>(Hear that Myra Wyse has gone to Marseilles) omit</p>
