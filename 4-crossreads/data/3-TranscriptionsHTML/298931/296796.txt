<p>Monday 25<br/>Three months for Xmas How time flies I have been 201 days, away from Australia<br/>Tuesday 26<br/>A very heavy artillery duel taking place and the shells are falling close I'm in my funk hole<br/>Wednesday 27<br/>To nights the night for our bretheren 19th are coming up to let us go back. We had a few casualtys<br/>Thursday 28<br/>Andy Fisher was up to see the Colonel and I but the old man was out, Andy looked well</p>
