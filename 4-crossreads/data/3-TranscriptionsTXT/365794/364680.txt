 Thursday 4 October 1917 Much cooler day.  Q. tournament still on.  "Patience" in both senses continues.  Methinks one needs to well observe the Chinese proverb.  God gave us one tongue &amp; 2 ears, therefore we should hear twice as much as we speak.  Apply this on board ship &amp; it worketh well.  The Padre continues to act the silly with N. Watson &ndash; why oh why was she ever allowed come out to disgrace her upbringing &amp; the A.A.N.S.  Again we visit the hold &amp; see our dear cabin trunks.
