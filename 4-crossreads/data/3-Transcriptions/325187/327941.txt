<p>[Monday 17 July 1916]</p>
<p>Battle Taube over this morning &amp; our Aeroplane chased him he went for his life followed by a few hundred rounds from our Machine Guns.<br/>Received Illustrated Papers from England, don't know who sent em but very acceptable.  Could not recognise writing   Our patrol is in touch with Turks today mounted on Camels.</p>
