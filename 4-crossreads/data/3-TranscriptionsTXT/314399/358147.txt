
Friday 30 May 1919Didn't sleep too well. Had a talk with McMillan about the house. Leave here next Friday. Got 3.17.0 refund. Gee he's scotch to the back bone. Bill had brekker at the flat & we had lunch at Arding & Hobbs at Clapham Junction. Came home early. Old lady gave me tea twice. did up my photos & went to bed.
Saturday 31 May 1919No Southend today as Bill sick again. Went to Eaton Place. Met Delepwawde & Gawsey. Went to Harry Brevitts place & then to lunch at Molinari's & then to lunch at Molinari's then to Marie's but she wasn't home so came back to flat. Read & wrote letters. Scrubbed my cap & boots.
