  June 15th  
 Sabbath. 
 Morning Church Service.  Afternoon Bible Study.  Third part of the "Elm Bank" letter is written. 
 Our vessel is now only 1,400 miles crom Cape Town.  Is the Southern Cross shining more brightly as we draw nearer Home - or does that fact make it appear so!
