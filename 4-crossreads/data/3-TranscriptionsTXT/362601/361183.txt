 3 April, Thursday 
 F. French draft in again. C.O. blows me up about Extension Report. Go to English &amp; Book-Keeping Class &amp; come down to write letters. 
 Afterwards, I go to bed at 10 pm &amp; do all night about W. at S. Hill. I am then fatigue. 
 Wrote Miss Scroggins, Dad &amp; Auntie Annie. Sent Brooches &amp; Coins to Auntie. 
 4 April, Friday 
 F. &amp; Hot 
 Go shopping &amp; to take my washing. Go to Dickens Lecture &amp; is very good. Meet Cal Slater, come back to office &amp; write &amp; study. Do some English later on. 
 Wrote M.R.L. &amp; Mais. Carter.
