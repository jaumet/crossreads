
7/7/18 Recd letters from Mother, Amanda & Mrs Brown. Still feeling very off & cannot eat.8/7/18 Feeling much better. Had first food for 60 hours. Very hot.9/7/18 Much cooler. Wrote home & to Uncle Fred10/7/18 Wrote to Mrs Brown.11/7/18 Up this afternoon for first time. Head still sore & a buzzing in the ears. last Friday met a Froggy up on top of hill at back of camp & he told me the three mounds are for French soldiers who died about 1839.  Wrote to Amanda.12/7/18 Met a Jock today who has just returned from Palestine. He told me our troops were severely dealt with & lost 9 guns when they crossed
