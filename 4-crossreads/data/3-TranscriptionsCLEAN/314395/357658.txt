No more parcels or papers have arrived since my last letter but, one or two might make their debut at Xmas which is not far off now.  Winter is staring us hard in the face & I tell you candidly it has us all frightened.  We know now what a winter in France in war-time means, and in Belgium, if we stay, it will be a d-sight worse.
