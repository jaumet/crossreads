<p>SUNDAY, AUGUST 24, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine but cloudy &amp; dull. Drove down</p>
<p>to Abbottslea in the morning with</p>
<p>some woolpacks ect. Linda came</p>
<p>with me. Tickle &amp; I put the 4D</p>
<p>sheep in the Abbottslea shed in the</p>
<p>afternoon ready for shearing</p>
<p>tomorrow. Al. drove Maisie, Myee</p>
<p>Jo, Jack Charley over to Coomoo Coomoo</p>
<p>in big car for lunch.</p>
