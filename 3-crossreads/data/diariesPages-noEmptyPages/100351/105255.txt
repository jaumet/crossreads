
Sun. 16-7-’16
Varennes 5 p.m.
Allotted huts ea platoon.
Mon. 17-7-16.
Drill. Rapid Loading etc. Horrible. Paid 20F after tea. Dir. Baths OK.
Tues. 18-7-’16
Packed ready for moving off 10 am. Remained so all day. Mac & self searched Town for du Pain without success. "Nm Faune Monsieur". Wained at 10 P.M. with Quarter Guard to morrow "C" Coy kept out of bed until 10 p.m. for some unknown reason. Loaned ex. Cpl Pearce 7.51
