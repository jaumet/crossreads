
18 - They get regular letters from Robert in trenches at Dardanelles. Archie is fretting to be off. Clapin rang up Aunt Isabel the day I arrived. She is married to Basil Greatrix - he is in the A.S.C. Lieutenant and goes Dardanelles this week too. I expect we'll all go together. I mean under the same escort.She is offering her services to Australian hospital in LondonClapin so have, her husband is a charming young man, full of grit - has been fighting in German East Africa.
