<p>North of France<br/>3 May 16<br/>My dearest Irene,<br/>I was very pleased to get another nice little letter from Margaret the other day. &ndash;How well she is getting on old Girl.<br/>We are at present living in a village about 4 miles from the trenches &amp; our other section are acting as stretcher bearers out at the trenches &amp; also runs a huge hot bath &amp; clean clothes concern in a village near us. Women wash the clothes there &amp; the men come in</p>
