
Wednesday July 4thWent to Abbeville for a day had a good time. Thursday 5 JulyFell in warned for guard following day.On guard all daySaturday 7thCame off guard went to Abbeville had a good daySunday 8thMoved out of Chest Camp morning left Faint Valery station half past two arrived Albert six o'clock marched out and joined Battalion again & Lenlist.
