The Rhinegau stretch of the Rhine is very lovely but I like it best above Rudesheim and Bingen because it is more wild. Passed the gigantic statue of Germania ? each nostril of her is said to be large enough to receive a man. Bingen ('sweet Bingen on the Rhine") is faced by a terraced bluff of vineyards and on the opposite side we passed Bishop Hatto's Mauseturm ? a low tower now used for navigation signals.
