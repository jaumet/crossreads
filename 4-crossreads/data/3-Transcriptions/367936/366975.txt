<p>Monday 8 May 1916<br/>Stables as usual all day.  In afternoon we were allotted to our boats and told to sleep with life-belts in our hammocks.<br/>Had big smoke after dinner which upset me &amp; cause loss of dinner.  On picquet at night.  Lovely calm day.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9 May 1916<br/>Was on picquet from 10 p.m. till 2 a.m. so did not get much sleep.  Carried on as usual, took sick horses up to stalls on top deck.<br/>Got sea kits up and put our warm clothes in them.  Marked identification disc in evening.<br/>Warm day, rain in evening.</p>
