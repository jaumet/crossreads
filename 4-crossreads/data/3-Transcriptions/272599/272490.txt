<p>1917 Tuesday 14 August</p>
<p>Afternoon off. Jock Mac &amp; self jaunt off.  <br/>Good concert on in evening. <br/>Received parcel from May containing tinned goods, biscuits, prunes cream etc. tres bon.  <br/>Attend practice of male choir at 8 p.m. Capt. Archdale conducts. <br/>Farrow sent to Blighty today. 7 weeks we had him. We're very proud of his "save". He should have been dead a dozen times, but he fought well &amp; finally pulled through.</p>
