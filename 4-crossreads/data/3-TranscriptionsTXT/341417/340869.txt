 Isle of Lemnos 31/9/15 
 Dear Uncle, Aunt &amp; Cousins 
 It is such a long time since I have written that I will commence with excuses. Since the end of last March we have had no opportunities. We began preparations for leaving Mena camp &amp; embarking then, &amp; were in Lemnos for three weeks on the transports before our landing. For at least two-months after landing I did not have the chance of writing a letter home &amp; for the rest of the time we averaged an issue of one piece of paper &amp; one envelope per man per fortnight.
