<p>14 December 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>76 of us detailed for evacuation.  Carry patients down to tram trucks, wheel them &frac34; mile, then carry &frac14; mile to waiting rooms. Not a great deal doing during the day, but all night we are kept going.</p>
