 Friday 14 December 1917. 
 In bivouac. Big Xmas Australian mail arrives. I got 27 letters &amp; cards. 
 The official announcement of the fall of Jerusalem. 
 Camels taken over from us by C.V.C. natives, so its &quot;finish camels&quot; with us fo a while. 
 My camel was broken up generally after the stunt and sent away from unit.
