
<p>246</p>
<p>1917&nbsp; January</p>
<p>12 FRIDAY</p>
<p>Very Cold, with rain, snow &amp; sleet all</p>
<p>day, but cleared up in evening.</p>
<p>I went for a bath in the morning</p>
<p>with the bath parade. Out</p>
<p>exercising horses in the afternoon</p>
<p>I got a horse &amp; went into</p>
<p>Bailieul in evening with</p>
<p>Charlie Pearce to get some</p>
<p>things for the canteen, &amp; some</p>
<p>gum boots. Heard my R.F.C.</p>
<p>application was not</p>
<p>forwarded [past? post?] D.H. Headquarters</p>
<p>at Larkhill.</p>
