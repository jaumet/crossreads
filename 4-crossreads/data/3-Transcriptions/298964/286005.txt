<p>22 Sun April<br/>moving again to day to a new camp  are amongst the Tommies don't find them so bad</p>
<p>23 Mon April<br/>Camped about 3 miles from Khan Yunus  watering there terrible dusty nearly chokes me  very quiet on the front  Gaza very hard to take</p>
<p>24 Tues April<br/>Buggar of a day blowing like Hell from the South.  dust to blind a chap.  Had a drop of J W. otherwise very quiet</p>
<p>25 Wed April<br/>Very hot &amp; dusty  first wash for Four days  it was great  would give a 100 for a long P. went to the beach about 9 miles swam horse  longing to have a spell   feel done</p>
