Their wounds are too awful for description and their suffering too morbid to dwell upon. There are 7 operations to day - 2 amputations. When one of the men was coming to after the op. he went through all the actions of a man charging, and certainly gave us a fine display of trench language. His idea of the Turk's pedigrees and social position though, were too much for the nurse, "who told an orderly to sit on his chest.
