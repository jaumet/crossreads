<p>orders have come through that all leave has been stopped to England &amp; Paris &amp; we have sent out orders recalling some of our officers &amp; men from Schools.   There is certainly something in the Air.</p>
<p>Sunday 24th March<br/>   Fine weather still.   Had a busy day with Sick getting them away in cars etc.   Our old camp shelled again, the salvage workers having a narrow escape.   Started making an inventory of Dispensary as we expect a sudden move.    At night had a mild hop over with music finishing up at 12 midnight.   Our piano I noticed has some shell splinters embedded in it from our late experience.</p>
