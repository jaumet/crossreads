
26 September 1915Sunday17th After Trinity
After church parade this morning I get a suit of Hosp uniform   &  make a trip out to Zeitoun via Cairo500 reinforcements for 6th Bde arrive at 6pm. very young looking  &  only partly trainedEverybody wondering what the Balkan row will bring forthAll Greeks in Egypt have been warned to return home
1915 September 27Monday
Have a quiet day at home to day. it does not take much to knock me up in my present conditionI make good use of the extensive library in the Hosp which is filled with real good reading matter.
