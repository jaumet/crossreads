 8-10-1918 Still in harbour, weather fine, escort waiting for us, Japanese Torpeodo boats. 
 9-10-1918 Under steam 7 a.m.  Left Taranto harbour escort of 2 Japenese. 
 10-10-1918 At sea, everything going smoothly.  Passed drifting boat, empty, ship's gunners shot practice.  Some good shooting.  Weather hot.  Strong head wind.  Our speed average 16 knots. 
 11-10-1918 In the real danger zone, 90% of the boats sunk happened about here.  Expect to reach port tomorrow morning.
