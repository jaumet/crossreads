February 1919.
21st. Close hospital wind up 1st Aust. Field Ambulance as a medical unit. Draft will now not leave before 5/3/19.
26th. Hand over all equipment of Ambulance, except things which are absolutely necessary to carrying on of unit. Doing nothing but wait orders for Australia. Weather unsettled.
28th. Dirty day and miserable but nevertheless went for a walk to Devant-les-Bois.
