<p>June 1918<br/>Thursday 27</p>
<p>In Billets. Erronville<br/>Weather fine and clear. Had the usual daily routine with horse and bycycles also wire and phones ,<br/>Drifted along to see my friend Suzanne on the Hellencount Road.<br/>Worked for awhile at the farm, had a beautiful spread of roast fowl peas and potatoes, also strawberries and cream.<br/>Fritz  bombed Abbeville.</p>
