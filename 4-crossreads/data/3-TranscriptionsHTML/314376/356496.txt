<p>185<br/>failed and from now till the sunken road we could see crowds limping along, most of them slightly wounded.</p>
<p>At the top of the rise the Ambulance was waiting. It was one of the old style vehicles drawn by horses with a big Red Cross in the centre. Similar to the ones used in the South African War.</p>
<p>When reached the sunken road we were ordered into the dugouts and found it a rather difficult matter to get accommodation owing to the large numbers of troops already quartered there.</p>
