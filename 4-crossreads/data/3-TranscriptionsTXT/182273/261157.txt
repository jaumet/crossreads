1918   August
22 THUR  Fine day very hot. rec.
letters from Gladys.
Else P.  & Mr W.T.B. final prepar-
ations for stunt tomorrow.
Quiet. resting in aft. Moved
forward. 9.PM & bivouacked near
Morecourt & Mericourt. very quiet
heavy bombing at night.
Thomas & Clingham went to Aussie
23 FRI  Reveille 2 am. Fine day. Warm
Stunt opened 4.40 am. very success-
ful. prisoners in galore. very quick
Objective reached & consolidated
Guns moved forward. Rec. letters from
May & Amy & watch. Heavy shelling
& bombs at night on both sides.
24 SAT  Fine day warm & cloudy.
Fairly quiet on both sides
Rec. PC. from Celie & letter
Amy Mc. Few small batches
of prisoners coming in.
Horses grazing. Heavy
bombing at night. & heavy
firing towards daybreak
(Sun) Fritz reported to be
evacuating. Writing.
