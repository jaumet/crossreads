 Tuesday 17 August, 1915 
 Yesterday &amp; today have been days of missery spent amongst the most apalling state of filth &amp; grime conceivable. 
 It turns out that the transport originally intended for us broke down &amp; we were sent off on this boat. On her last voyage she carried a cargo of coal, everything you touch is black. 
 Our rations have for the most part been dry biscuits &amp; bully beef with water. Anything would be an improvement on this.
