<p>Australian Div &amp; 5th Mounted Brigade are practically at the back of the Enemy.<br/>Sunday Sept 22<br/>Haifa was captured today also complete Aerodrome &amp; thousands of provisions.<br/>Brigade preparing to move out. We are moving at day break<br/>Monday Sept 23<br/>Moved out at 5 AM towards River crossing about 10 AM.<br/>We moved on to Shunet Nimrin about 7 miles across flat from Jordan River. Once again we are here but I think this time there will be no turning back. The 1ST Brigade are ahead somewhere and the Turks are retiring. We moved on again</p>
