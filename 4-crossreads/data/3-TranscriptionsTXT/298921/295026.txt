 February, 1915 Monday 22 Scouting &amp; advanced work Just received Days &amp; Mothers letter of the 10th January. Be sure &amp; address me in future 1st Light Horse Regiment 1st Light Horse Brigade New Zealand &amp; Australian Division Egypt. Was very glad to hear your [indecipherable] news &amp; to know all well &ndash; I am A.1 but lighter I am glad to say camped for dinner along side of a Harem I tried to see the inmates &ndash; but they are very shy, Donkeys, [indecipherable], &amp; a Camel appeared to be in the Hall.
