<p>Aout 8<br/>Duty Sec today. Not much doing. Had lecture by O.C. in afternoon on the new gas the Hun is using.</p>
<p>Aout 9<br/>Had 10 mile route march. Nothing else doing only a few Coy fatigues. No further progress reported from the Front except Russians made a slight advance in Bessarabia.</p>
