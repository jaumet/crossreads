LANDED AT MIDNIGHT MONDAY AND TOUCHED SOIL IN FIRST MOMENTS OF BIRTHDAY
September 1915
Tuesday 28
Rose at daybreak &amp; went with Hunter to Hqtr 3rd Field Ambulance situated on the beach &amp; then to camp in a large gully running up from the beach a mile inland. Reported &amp; received &frac12; doz letters including proofs of photos &amp; 2 letters from Rita which were welcome. Were welcomed with a few shrapnel shells one of which burst near enough to cause us to duck..Safely ensconced in a good dugout.The 3rd Field Hospital  &amp; stores are behind a hill in view of the sea &amp; of the corner swept by "Beachy Bill"
