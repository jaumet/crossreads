Sun. 26thWent for another walk to Durrington Bulford, two little villages about 2 miles. Saw a plane down went across a padd field had a good look at it. Also went to another village Ficheldene saw the old original chestnut tree blacksmiths shop. Got a couple of chestnuts a piece of stone off old church 800 years old. In all the villages very old churches grave-stones.Mon.Usual pictures.Tues. Wed.Thurs, 29th Jim came back after an absence in hos. of 2 months. Pleased to see him had a good long chat. Got a job as officers batman.Fri. Sat.Went for a walk with Jim Bert Wilson.Sun.Another walk forwarded papers at different intervals lately.Mon. 2nd AprilGot 5 letters from home.
