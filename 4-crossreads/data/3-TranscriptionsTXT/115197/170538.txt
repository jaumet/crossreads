  July 2nd  
 News of the Aeneas, Beltana &amp; Durham.&nbsp; These three vessels are ahead of us and are experiencing rough weather. 
 Conclude &quot;Our Boys Beyond the Shadow&quot; . 
 The growing popularity of our Wednesday evening hymn service is well marked on this day, when despite the bitter winds, rough seas and failing light a good number of the &quot;boys&quot; sit out the service on deck.
