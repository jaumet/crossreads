<p>We arrived at  Alexandria on 1st Feb 1915 we disembarked at 9AM &amp; caught 9.30PM train to Heliopolis which is the new town situated 7 miles from Cairo.</p>
<p>Heliopolis is a remarkable town being built within the space of 7 years. The place was a desert before that time. The houses &amp; public buildings are first class in design being a mixture of Byzantine &amp; Egyptian architecture.
