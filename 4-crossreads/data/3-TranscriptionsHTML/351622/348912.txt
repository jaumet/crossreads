<p>Arrived out of way Station before daylight marched over to Bouchon.</p>
<p>25 October 1916 Wed<br/>Griffin is turned down with the two franks. Appletrees Scotty &amp; McGee make themselves a nuisance. Issue of rum. Blacksmiths shop. Awful billet. A steep old village lying in the hills.</p>
<p>26 October 1916 Thur<br/>Inspection of helmet. Griff gets his water bottle full of cognac. Lettuces [eschats] [indecipherable] Walk over the fields finishing up in the church yard. All in bed 6 pm. Moving again tomorrow.</p>
