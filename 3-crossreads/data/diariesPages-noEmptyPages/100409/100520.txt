Left Paddington again for Weymouth at 12.15 pm passing through Westbourne Park, Acton, Ealing Broadway, Hanwell, Southall, Hayes, West Drayton, Slough, Taplow, Maidenhead, Twyford, Reading, Theale, Midgam, Thatcham, Newbury, Hungerford, Savenake, Pewsey, Edington, Westbury, Frome, Witham, Bruton, Castle Cary, Sparkford, Marston Magna, Yeovil, Yetminster, Evershot, Maiden Newton, Grimstone, Dorchester, Upwey Weymouth, arriving at 5 pm. Went to Motor Ambulances each given a cup of coffee then taken to the No 2 Command Depot. After having tea of sausage meat vegetable went looked at Canteen, Salvation Army Hut YMCA. Came back to Hut at 8 pm. On way down in train saw Horlicks Malted Milk factory at Slough, Huntley Palmers big biscuit factory at Reading at Newbury saw dozens of new "Tanks?, also large dumps of new shells. Raining in afternoon. Camp we are in is Westham Camp, No 2 Aust. Command Depot. It is about  miles from Weymouth Stn.
