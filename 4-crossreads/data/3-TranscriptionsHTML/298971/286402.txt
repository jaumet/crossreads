<p>on the incinerators.  The Scotch regiment consisted of Royal Scotch, Gordon H'landers, Seaforth H'landers, Highland Light Infantry, Scottish Rifles or (Cameronians) Black Watch etc.  Prisoners working at incinerators clearing the ashes away.  Coates Cotton Factory &amp; a Blue Factory on either side of us.  Troops have been shot on this spot &amp; I have heard tales of the disgraceful treatment of our prisoners.  Belgians hated at this part.  Belgium only across the bridge close to us.</p>
<p>25th Sept. Monday</p>
