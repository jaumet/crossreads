At 6.50p a big crowd of soldiers were aboard scattered about the decks talking, when we noticed an aeroplane approaching from the South flying at an enormous altitude; without a glass her distinguishing marks could not be seen; through the glass 4 black crosses could be seen; we all kept our eyes turned skywards, rather interested in her movements; slowing down as she approached, she flew over the top of us, as far forward as the bows, when she turned right around and flew Southwards.
