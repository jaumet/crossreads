<p>firing at them never got them  3AM writing a letter to aggie Powell and just finished it turning in now 10PM cold night<br/>Friday Jan 19.1.17. turned out 6AM had breakfast went round Battallion . had a letter from Josie and Walter [indecipherable]  Answered them. I am sure there is some sort of vermin on myself  I feel hitchy<br/>Saturday Jan 20.1.17. turned out 6AM cold morning had breakfast cleaned up very quiet afternoon turned in 10.30P.M.<br/>Sunday 21.1.17 turned out 6.30AM fine morning Breeze blowing had breakfast done some washing and mending taube in the distance went away again</p>
