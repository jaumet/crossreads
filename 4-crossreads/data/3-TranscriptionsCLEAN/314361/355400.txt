  
 It was a slow & dreary journey very cold all the while despite the fire. We arrived at Etaples about 4.30 pm yesterday & was marched up to our divisional lines. We will not be here long, may even have to go tomorrow, the food is very decent here, & plenty of it. Australians are always better fed than Tommies, that is when they are in their own camps or hospitals. The railways in France, I have always found to be very poor, slow & always go a round about way. Adelaide Camp Feb. 17 17 Rejoined the bn. on the 14th at  Bernafay the bn. did not come out until the 15th yesterday we came down to this camp. The journey from Etaples was a little faster than usual. I spent one night at the reinforcement camp at Albert. Feb 19th Bernafay Camp Returned to this camp this afternoon, will probably stay here for a couple of days. This camp is just behind [indecipherable] Wood. Feb 22nd 17 Needle Trench.
