 Wed Aug 16th Still on the same duties with a slight alteration, four of us were put to sort of undertakers &amp; buriel party at the morgue where there were generally 4 to 6 dead Soldiers laying &amp; our duty was to place them in coffins screw them down, load them into a motor lorry follow them to the cemetery unload &amp; return with the Union Jacks &amp; perhaps for another buriel. 
 At 2 pm the evacuation took place that is we closed up for receiving patients an hospital train arrived and we conveyed all the patients to &amp; loaded them on the train the work is hard and rapid as we had the train loaded in about four hours &amp; to make matters worse it rained
