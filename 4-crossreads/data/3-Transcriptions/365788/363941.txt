<p>2<br/>West Kensington &amp; seem to like it more than boarding houses.<br/>Kindly asked me to come &amp; see them often if I get leave. They have both had Influenza. Miss Norris goes to help at the Victoria League Club for overseas Soldiers 3 days a week. &ndash; She also tells me that her brother is Brigade Major of the 118th Brigade Infantry.<br/>I was working with some others several nights lately building a big dug-out as we expect some lively doings soon. It is to be gas-proof &amp; shell proof except for very large shells, &amp; is situated about a mile behind the front trenches.<br/>We used to start from here about 6 P.M. march out about 4&frac12; miles to our work &amp; work till about</p>
