 Saturday 7 April 1917. 
 On Easter Leave. 
 Met Joy in City. Out to D&#39;nong in afternoon. 7.30 from D&#39;nong to Lyndhurst. Home&nbsp;once more.&nbsp; 
 Father &amp; I went to Mr. Stephens for evening. Had games of billiards. Played fair game. 
 Handed V. P. K. A. K. to &quot;Kodaks&quot; for repair on Sat morng. 
 Raining.
