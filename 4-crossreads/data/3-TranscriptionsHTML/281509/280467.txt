<p>1 September 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Nearly missed parade tho' sleeping in barn. Dress in record time. Breakfast &amp; move off in forenoon. 3rd Field take our "possie". Arrive at Remi Siding &amp; take over "Rest Station" a good "possie" so outward seeming. Baths, washing places, mess room, library "tres bon". 4 Casualty Clearing hospitals adjacent. also Y.M.C.A Tent &amp; only 1 mile from Poperinge. Live in tent once more. Yankee sanitary method. Each man his own carrier. [indecipherable]ogh!</p>
