<p>28/<br/>Two ships passed, one was the Ballarat which had left before us &amp; had called into Aden.<br/>Two birds like Kesterels are perched upon the yards all day.<br/>We were paid the sum of 14/- so there will be a little game of two-up &amp; cards going again, about two men have got the last pay now. Won it at two-up etc.<br/>The final of the boxing tournament carried out. Some good fights occurred.<br/>I was picked out for Ships gaurd when we reach port.</p>
<p>30/<br/>Passed an island of rocks with a lighthouse upon it</p>
