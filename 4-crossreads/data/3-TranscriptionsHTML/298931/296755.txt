<p>Friday 5<br/>I have a letter from Elsie, poor little girl never mind old girl boys will be boys and go fighting<br/>Saturday 6<br/>10 years ago I arrived in Sydney for the 1st time and made my home at Randwick Jockey. Jack<br/>Sunday 7<br/>I have come to the conclusion that Egypt is the best washer-woman country in the globe dry in 2 hrs and the sun draws the dirt out<br/>A sunny Sunday in Sunland, with a merry mind memories of Manly and my maid ELSIE</p>
