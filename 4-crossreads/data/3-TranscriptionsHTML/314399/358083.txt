<p>Thursday 27 March 1919<br/>Back to camp at noon. Leg dressed &amp; lunch. Got put on duty for tomorrow. Am sick of staying here in Camp. Got a couple of letters &amp; wrote 3. Rained all the morning but quite sunny after lunch.</p>
<p>Friday 28 March 1919<br/>Rained all the morning but fine in the afternoon. Had a kick in the afternoon &amp;   hurt my leg a little. Was on train picquet. Things very quiet tho. Had a sing afterwards. Was goodo.</p>
