 May 1915 
 Sunday 16  Fine . Went into fire trench again last night &amp; stayed there till 6 PM tonight when we were shifted up to a supporting trench for a night's rest. We had to draw water for the cook's before turning in. Enemy very active of a night time. They have more machine guns very close to us &amp; a battery of big guns on our right. 
 Monday 17  Fine  Came back to reserve trench this morning about 5 AM. Enemy busy with new battery on our right. More arguing the point with them.
