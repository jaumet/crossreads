  
 & the Roll was called after Satisfying the Officers wot were all there were are marched over to the Hospital to have our Eyesight again tested & examined by the Medical Officer after satisfying the Officer we were taken down to the Quartermasters Stores for our first issue of Clothes, which was a Kakie suit & a like hat, then we were given 2 Blankets & a large bag to put Straw in to sleep on. Having a large number of Men in Camp & the Huts were full we had to sleep in Tents, which were Bell Shaped & held 7 men when Sleeping all their feet were in the Centre. My first Meal in Camp was of Bread & Jam which I was not to keen on after being used to the ordinary fare at home.
