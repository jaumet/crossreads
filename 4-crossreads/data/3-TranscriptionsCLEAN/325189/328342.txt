A few veiled & unveiled women are standing by.  After tea half of the section went ashore from 5-7 o' c.  The other half went from 7-9 o.c.  Bob Hutchings & I went together & had a good time.  It is a very big city and possesses some frightful smells.  I think the drainage must be bad.  There is no wonder at plagues sweeping thousands of these eastern folks off.
