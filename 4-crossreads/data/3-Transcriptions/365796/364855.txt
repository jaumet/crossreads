<p>So pleased to receive yr P.C. We had notice today to be on the "Kirarra" at 2 p.m. tomorrow. I think our destination is to be France". We do not know what our address will be. But if you write Rose, she will tell you. Mother &amp; R. are staying at Mrs. Colcrofts "Abbotsford" Darlinghurst Rd Darlinghurst. We have had such a wish.</p>
<p>Best love to you<br/>From [indecipherable]</p>
