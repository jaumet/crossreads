<p>25 September 1918 Wednesday<br/>Amman  5 1 99 114 + 10 {[indecipherable] Sigs AMC<br/>Moved on at 0600 with Goggly Wogs (British West Indians) &amp; Jew boys in support as infantry- former very pleased with themselves, they speak <u>good</u> English &amp; called themselves Black Anzacs-  The climate is beautiful in these Hills of MOAB- attacked AMMAN at 3 p.m. &amp; our Regiment captured <strike>4</strike> 3  6 inch Howitzers one 77" gun, much ammunition 2 Motor Lorrys 1 Motor Car 10 Wagons, 100 prisoners etc etc the Anzac Div getting many  Held outpost line &amp; every Bedouin proclaims himself one of the HEDJAZ  men, &amp; are having a great time among the loot</p>
