   The small birds have become quite noisy  Skylarks are sonstantly heard & in the areas between & behind the trenches our lads are shooting hare & partridge to supplement their rations; I am living in hopes of hearing the nightingale in the hedges at twilight but perhaps tis too early for that sweet singer.
   'Tis somewhat early for the trees but the buds swelled quite large seem to be only waiting for a signal to burst & clothe the naked limbs of the trees which have been indecently bare all the time that we have been in France.
