<p>Sat. June 23.  Anniversary of our arrival in France.  Had Stewed Rhubarb &amp; Custard for Dinner to celebrate the historic Landing.</p>
<p>Sunday June 24.  Church Parade at Y.M.C.A. Senlis.  Spent a busy day writing Letters &amp; making up stuff for "the  Tattoo".  Received parcel of socks &amp; eatables from Mrs Horobin.  Good old Lizzie.  Wrote her a Post Card.  Billsy May, Clarrie Blanton &amp; I sat up till about 11 pm. typing the "Tattoo".  Posted Letter 22 to Lill</p>
<p>Monday June 25.  Divisional Sports are coming off in about a week's time.  B" Section is entered for Stretcher Drill Competitions  &amp;  First Aid &amp; we put in a good mornings drill getting ready for it.  We went away down the village &amp; saw a 3 round Boxing Bout of the 8 Brigade Tourney.  This is the eve of my 2nd Wedding Anniversary.  Hope to the Lord I'm back with M.S. long before another year is gone.</p>
