<p>this evening.<br/>21st  Yesterday evening there was a cricket match between No 11. &amp; 10 platoon &amp; after a most exciting match No. 11 won by a run the scores were 28 &amp; 29 on the first innings.<br/>Len &amp; Holme's were up here last night Len brought me a batch of English paper's.<br/>We are having some great games of Bridge" here its wonderful what a few can play it.<br/>We had a church parade this morning held under the shade of some bonzer old trees &amp; the grass we were standing on was nearly knee high.<br/>Don Fraser another mate of mine left the Coy. this morning</p>
