<p>Sunday 3 September 1916<br/>Exciting chase of Taube by our plane in morning.<br/>West and I rode down to Mohomeda [possibly Mahemdia] to hold horses for chaps going on leave.  Had swim &ndash; naturally!<br/>Church parade in evening.</p>
<p>Monday 4 September 1916<br/>Small squad of us out on patrol the other side of Quatia [possibly Katia] looking for buried treasure but did not find any.<br/>Had a big feed of ripe dates also some nice figs.</p>
