  
 29 July, Tuesday 
 Mum & I go to Granma's & Aunt Fannies. Shopping at Grace Bros & to pictures & to Glebe. 
 Break up in general. We all strike same boat Home. Meet O.G.H.M. We all race to bed in screams of silent laughter. 
 Send [indecipherable] love & painting of Kookaburra [indecipherable]. 
 30 July, Wednesday 
 I go to Burwood. Watch tennis[?] at old Cernt & the Davey Boys. Meet Brigg's & Mrs Haigh. I have a very good time & sleep out on verandah. We write to Jessie. 
 Wrote M 17. Wrote Jessie.
