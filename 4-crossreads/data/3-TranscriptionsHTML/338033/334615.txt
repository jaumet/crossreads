<p>the 17th we were paid 1 our<br/>Extra duty pay which had been accumulating since the day we left Sydney. My cheque amounted to just over twenty pound, twelve of which I sent up to Briscoe and Co., to be drawn as required after going to France. I had a good time with the remainder and bought a few presents to send home, along with those I bought in Egypt. On Saturday the 21st five of us hired a motor and went for a trip to Southampton. We left at midday and carried the 34</p>
