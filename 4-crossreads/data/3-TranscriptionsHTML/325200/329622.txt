<p>until 1.30 am  &amp;  should reach there about 6 am.<br/>There is no water, I believe for our horses after Bir Salmana so from tonight till tomorrow night theye will get no water.<br/>Bir Salmana Sept 17<br/>I have just breakfasted  &amp;  feel as a result much better.<br/>Spent a miserable time last night trying to get to sleep. It was insufferably close  &amp;  one had to have one coat on to keep off the mosquitoes whose name was legion. And there I lay with Camel ticks crawling all over me from the ground and mosquitoes from above, sweating</p>
