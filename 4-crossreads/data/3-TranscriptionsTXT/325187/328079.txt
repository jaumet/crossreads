
[Saturday 2 December 1916]
Returned to Camp still minus Kit but got some more in Cairo, met many old friends there But very few soldiers now mostly TARBUSHESLeft leather Kit Bag & washing at Continental Hotel & got an old bag of Nowels.Got another batch of letters sent back by Laura Nov 4Jack Forth & Day Sept 9thJoe & Day Sept 17thMrs Strong Sept 2ndMiss Regus who is married!!!Aunt Louie- Sept 13thPapers from Mrs Cotton   Mothers parcel coming.Please thank those you can will write later but very busy I am so "bucked" to get all home news especially at present.
