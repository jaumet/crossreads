<p>THURSDAY, JULY 24, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Went into Quirindi</p>
<p>with Dad &amp; Mum. They got the</p>
<p>10.17 AM train to Sydney. Marj. &amp; Olga</p>
<p>went with them in an engaged</p>
<p>carriage. Got home for lunch. Peter</p>
<p>shifted horses from Seven Star</p>
<p>&amp; I picked out 5 &amp; put them in</p>
<p>Middle Paddock.</p>
<p>Darby down 42 ft in hard</p>
<p>sandstone. Tickle drawing</p>
<p>water to him all day.</p>
