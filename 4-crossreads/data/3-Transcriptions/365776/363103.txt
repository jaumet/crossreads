<p>25th Sept. arriving back early in Oct. to find the Battery back in action, on the same front as we left, only further advanced this time. we have had our own infantry in with us &amp; great work they have done, piling still greater honours, pushing the Hun well back off the high ground as far back as "Paschaedale" over the "Paschaedale" ridges, the heaviest fighting our fellows had was through Polyegood Wood. Our guns were in position on the left &amp; on the slopes of Anzac Ridge, here we remain till about the 10th  of Nov. The Cordoroy roads leading from Ypres, where our Waggon lines were.
