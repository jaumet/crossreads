<p>27 April, Sunday</p>
<p>W &amp; Snow</p>
<p>I go to church &amp; the parson talks at me &amp; tells me to get an idea. We all sir around fire all day &amp; I sort my bags over again. I play cards &amp; go to bed early.</p>
<p>We get no dinner.</p>
<p>28 April, Monday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I go to Bulford for Teeth. Tea at mess. Go to Sarum &amp; spend [?] Home at No 13. I run all the way to train have to wait 30 minutes. Recd letters from Dick, Doris &amp; Dad.</p>
