The Wind to-day is cruel, it is just like as if one was standing in the track of a bush fire.  We are all having to wear Goggles but the  rims & Glasses get red Hot & unbearable & all have to take them off. All the time we are working hard & I am afraid that we will be a very sorry Regiment if they dont soon hurry up & get us away. I would rather be shot than stay here another Month.  As long as I live I will never forget my stay here.  Sinaii will be written across my Brain for many the long Year to Come 
 17.5.16 Wednesday Another Hot & Windy day which absolutely stifled us.
