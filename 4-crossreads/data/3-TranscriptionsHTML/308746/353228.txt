<p>1917<br/>from Capt. Judge.</p>
<p>Nov. 29<br/>On leave in Boulogne.  Good food &amp; bad drink.</p>
<p>Nov. 30<br/>Pay a.m.  Repaid loan 100 frs.  Bn. guard at 1400.  Arranged with a Frenchwoman to buy 8 pork chops for the guard, <strike>not</strike> &amp; to cook them for supper.  Made me bilious.</p>
<p>Dec. 1<br/>Guard dismounted at 1430.  My digestion badly disarranged.</p>
<p>Dec. 2<br/>Communion.  Capt. Judge left as O.C. Brigade school, worse luck.  Ackerman to Corps Gas school.</p>
<p>Dec. 3<br/>Motor lorries to Etappes for Turkish bath.  Unwashed underclothing issued in return for our semi clean things.  Caught a chill on return journey.</p>
<p>Dec. 4<br/>Voted 'YES' on conscription referendum.  Temperature 99.8.</p>
<p>Dec. 5<br/>Very queer.  On picquet in Tingry wood.  Sun shining &amp; quite warm.</p>
<p>Dec. 6<br/>Musketry practise.  Shooting very bad as my cold &amp; eyes are bad.</p>
<p>Dec. 7<br/>On sick parade.  Light duty.</p>
<p>Dec. 8<br/>Bn. Sports.  A Coy won all events.</p>
