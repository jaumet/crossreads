<p>Hope everybody is well at Home and Father much better.</p>
<p>Expect to meet some of my big brothers here shortly.  Harry and myself both well and getting fit again.</p>
<p>Love to all,<br/>From<br/>Roy.</p>
<p>Last letter from Home was No. 22.</p>
