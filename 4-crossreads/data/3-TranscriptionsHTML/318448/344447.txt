<p>away below me; while far in the distance can be seen the Plains of Sharon &amp; the glittering Mediterranean. A soft &amp; cool breeze blows up the Hillside. One has the sensation of being on top of the world. On three sides one looks on &amp; on until sea, land &amp; sky merge in a delicate blue haze. Just now these hills are quite beautiful. The grey outcrops of stone contrasting with numerous little terraces of the yellowing corn while here &amp; there is the fresh green splash of a vineyard. And soon now we leave it <strike>all</strike></p>
