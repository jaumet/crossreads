 26.10.17 
 Dear Madam, 
 I have to acknowledge with many thanks receipt of your parcel, containing 48 pairs of socks &amp; 2 Balaclavas, knitted from Wah: Warrawee Wool.  Also sent in 5 prs war chest wool per Mrs. A. Reid. 
 Yours faithfully, "WAR CHEST" SUPPLY DEPOT, [Signed] J. M. Antill Hon. Superintendent A.D.M.
