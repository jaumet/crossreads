<p>Hope Wills Lumbago is better. Love to Dr &amp; Mrs R   Read</p>
<p>23 May 16<br/> Eric Greenwell is here &amp; is A.1.</p>
<p>My dearest Irene,<br/>How are you and the children. Fancy! Nearly 19 months since I left dear old Sydney.<br/>You can imagine how I would love to see you all you. We are at present about seven miles from the trenches having moved back from our last position for a rest. Thank you very much old girl for making me a present of   <strike>the</strike> what you fixed up for me at Harry</p>
