<p>July 1918<br/>Saturday 20</p>
<p>Frechencourt.<br/>Major asked for volunteers from gunners as spooks and received no reply to his most enticing question. He was slightly disgusted. <br/>Ran round to Bussey - la Daours  and saw friends had a bon spread in honor of my return.<br/>Weather very damp, paid 40 francs and had a little flutter on the cards. Received a new tunic from Q.M. and won a fine example of an Army bycyle.</p>
