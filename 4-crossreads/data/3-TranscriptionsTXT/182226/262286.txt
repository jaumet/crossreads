
Tuesday 21 September, 1915
Still off Duty.
Received a letter from the mater.
Artillery is very active lately. Fleet as well as land batteries.
Rum was issued.
The Island of Cinbros[?] is a beautiful sight just before dark. The sun goes down just to the right of it & it looks a very dark blue & wventually fades away, growin[g] the same colour as the sea.
