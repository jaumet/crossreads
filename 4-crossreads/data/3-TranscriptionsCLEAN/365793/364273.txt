  
 June 24 think ill get it again OK. Feel like doing a damn good swear. Have been to the Doc & he's going to do his best. Obtained a pass from 2 pm to 11 but will have to go easy as I only have 18 bob. Strolled round the older part of the town ran into sundry dead ends & narrow highwalled lanes, & unearthed a couple of Methodist Churches.
