
The 3 A.S. HospitalLemnos1 January 1916My beloved Father & MotherI think I was on night duty when I last wrote. I am becoming a very bad correspondent.I wonder if I told you of the very cold weather and wind we had - and of the wet & wind and the soldiers from Suvla with frost bite and exhaustion & starvation. Then we had fine warm sunshiney weather from about the 12 Dec till yesterday. The wind has come again & it blows cold but we are more acclimatized and do not feel the cold so much. I am writing in bed. The only warm spot for reading or writing. I have had very little time for either. The Psalm Daily Light, & now a Scotch hymn book kept from the books Aunt Lolah sent added to my daily reading stock. I have read Shelley's "Hellas" but I think I told you that - & the introduction so interesting - just at this time. Certainly - the highest has fallen to the lowest.
