 Friday 12/5/16 Went to the ordnance stores, across the canal from Ferry Post, on a waggon, while there received news to the effect that we are to be relieved by the 14th F. Amb to-morrow.  On my return to camp found all busy packing all equipment &amp; generally preparing to move.
