<p>hands now the Turks being too disorganised both literally &amp; morally to attempt anything.  We have captured at least 3000 prisoners out here amongst whom were 25 Italians who had been captured on the Italian front &amp; were working on the roads.  They were of course delighted.  Reveille tomorrow at 4.30 a.m. so more anon.</p>
<p>29.9.18<br/>Amman<br/>We have been here now since yesterday afternoon.</p>
<p>There is an enormous quantity of guns &amp; material at the station amongst which are some 8" howitzers.</p>
<p>After we had captured all the turks who had retreated from Shunet Nimrin, as far as I can gather we just sat down</p>
