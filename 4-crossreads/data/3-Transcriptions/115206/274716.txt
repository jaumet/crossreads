<p>May 1918<br/>Sunday 26</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines<br/>Weather glorious, nice and fair, no cold wind. <br/>Albert Garfott still down with Trench Fever.<br/>Fritz came across early with his bombs and shells and illuminated the countryside with their splashes.<br/>Finished the Y.M.C.A. dugout at 9.30 pm<br/>Went for a swim with Albert and Ernie to the pool.<br/>Fritz put wind up avec bombard avec aeroplane.<br/>C'est la Guerre.</p>
