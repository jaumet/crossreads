  
 26 April 1916 Wed Fatigue work the best part of the day painting tents & making them invisible from aircraft. Paid after dinner. Took a walk into Steenwerck with Marsh enjoying a good tea of eggs & chips. 7.30 p.m. the whole front is lit up with star shells & exceptionally heavy artillery duel. All the boys full 
 27 April 1916 Thur At 3.am this morning George Long & Nick Beattie leave for old England on furlough. They are the first to leave the 5th Field Ambulance. Solid work all day loading & unloading carts with manure. A rotten job. Went into Steenwerck to night adjourning to an estaminet where we met Sid Bragg & Johnny [indecipherable]. Plenty of noise & plenty of laughing
