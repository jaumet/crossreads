 Sydney, but even now in spite of the long time we have been away &amp; the trials &amp; hardships I have had to put up with I'm glad I was one of the first away, &amp; never regret joining up. Well it has turned out a wretched day it has rained steadily all the afternoon &amp; is still doing so. This morning we had Battalion drill I had charge of a platoon &amp; got on alright, in the afternoon we were in a hut the whole time for the men were receiving instruction on the Lewis Gun.
