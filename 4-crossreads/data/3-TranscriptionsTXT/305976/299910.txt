
Thursday Aug 23Rose at 6.45 AM. Breakfast 7 AM. On duty until 9 AM. This Camp will do me. Food is good. Duties light & plenty of surf bathing.Wednesday Friday Aug 24My first view of two German Taubes today. Flew over camp, very high in air. Heard Monitors shelling Gaza & could see flashes up coastSaturday Aug 25Cricket match today between staff & patients of rest camp. Patients won by four runs.Sunday Aug 26Two Taube overhead today. Flew much lower than on previous occasions. Wrote letters afternoon.
