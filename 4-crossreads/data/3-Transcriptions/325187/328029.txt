<p>[Friday 13 October 1916]</p>
<p>Gladys Best of Luck<br/>Had Gladys Riddler a nice girl friend of Dorothys to dinner at a fashionable restaurant &amp; then to see The Best of Luck at Drury Lane- good thing I had saved my pennies &amp; only have short time in London- couldn't keep going-<br/>This morning went to Regents Park for a Blow.<br/>Posting you some Cards of the Giants Causeway Mr Ferrar motored me there.  While in Ireland also Dorothys old school now an Hotel &amp; a jolly nice one too<br/>Going to see Mardi Ritchie tomorrow.  Expect to leave for Egypt end of this month.</p>
