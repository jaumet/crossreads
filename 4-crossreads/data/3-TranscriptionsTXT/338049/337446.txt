 Saturday 24 August Things as usual &ndash; busy cleaning saddlery &ndash; 
 Called out about 7 p.m. with wagon &ndash; Two wagons mine &ndash; Chancellors. Up along main road &amp; a fair way up one of the wheels of my wagon stuck &ndash; Could not repair. Shelled rather heavily. Horses withdrawn. Finally removed wagon to side of the road &amp; took stores on cable card. Laid line. 10th Bde &ndash; 291 QFA.
