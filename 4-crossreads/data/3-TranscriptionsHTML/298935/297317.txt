Finally after being a week in Monte Video Camp, Weymouth I sailed form Portland on the "Star of England" for Australia.<br/>On board were 273 wounded soldiers.  A week later we made a call at Dakar, West Africa, to put a gun ashore that had been carried in case a 'U' boat attacked us.  We very luckily missed the German raider "Moewe" by the small margin of six hours.<br/>Our food was no worse than that usually issued on transports but there was a good deal of grumbling and someone pinned up the following:</p>
