<p>dressing &amp; undressing &ndash; the 68 sisters without any convenience but a bed &ndash; no chairs or tables. We just have our suitcases with us.<br/>The officers and sisters mess together on this ship not as on the Grantala &ndash; and their quarters are very comfortable of course it is a beautiful ship. We anchored out in the outer harbour this morning and &ndash; what I saw was just a repetition of Simpsons Haven on a huge scale &ndash; and of course different back ground. The air is cool and fresh &ndash; we do not know when we shall be taken off the ship. All</p>
