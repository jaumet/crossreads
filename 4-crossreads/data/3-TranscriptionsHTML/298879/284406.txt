<p>July 1915<br/>Thursday 15<br/>Fine&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Several piers nearly completed. They are built mainly by Egyptian engineers</p>
<p>Friday 16<br/>Fine    Very dusty. Clouds of dust fly over the camp. The flies and heat are very trying. Have been acting brakes-man on a water cart to No1 Aust. Stn. Hospital.</p>
