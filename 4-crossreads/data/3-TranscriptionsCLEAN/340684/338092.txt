
365Epsom1st August 1915
Dearest MotherI know it is ages since I wrote to you but the letters I wrote to the Pater were for you both the same as this one - Last Tuesday all Australians and New Zealanders were transferred from Manchester to Epsom. This is a horrible place after the comfortable quarters and kind attention I had in Manchester at the Red Cross Hospital  also a few days before we were moved here the youngsters
