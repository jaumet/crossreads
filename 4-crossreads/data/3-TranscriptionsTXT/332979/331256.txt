 23/2/17. Friday When I awoke next Morning I did not feel to well, having a Head ache I remained on board  until  although the troops went for another Route March, &amp; after Dinner I dressed &amp; went ashore at 3 Pm &amp; caught a Train a Train &amp; went up towards the Mountains &amp; spent an hour looking down over the Town. On Returning I met a Chum Kept a precious appointment &amp; went out to Tea to a friend of his place,  I  a Mr Taylor who is  in the  a Black Smith in the Harbour Works.
