<p>13th  We went out to Hampton Court &amp; saw over the Palace and grounds, the <br/>Houseboats etc.  The 180 years old grape Vine.<br/>We saw the place where Henry VIII courted Anne Boleyn  The marvellous collection of <br/>paintings, the King &amp; Queens Bed &amp; Reception rooms.<br/>The Court is now devoted as apartments of the wives of notable men, granted rent free</p>
