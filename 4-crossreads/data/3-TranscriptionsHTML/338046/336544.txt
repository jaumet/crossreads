If anything went wrong, the correct thing to do was to blame the war bread.)<br/>July 20<br/>tom McCann returned from Tidworth School about 20th July. He had been made  a corporal and as soon as he hit the camp, was put on guard, much to his disgust. I had great pleasure in watching him mount guard (Signallers do not do guards). When I received the photos, I sent them to all the people I could think of. We attended a concert at Fovant the same night. One item that I thought rather clever was, bell ringing, with table bells. A girl was the performer. I had seen only one set in Australia. The 14th Reinforcement of 30th Battalion arrived one night about 9 pm between 15 &ndash; 23 July.
