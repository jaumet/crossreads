21/10/17 Sunday Up 6am cleaned up camp moved off about 8am. Marched 2 miles to a paddock after waiting 2 hrs got into motor buses. Left Ypres 2.30pm just as we were in outskirts of town a shell lobbed quite close. Travelled via Poperinghe, Abeele, Steenvoorde, Cassel, Arques Wizernes to Drionville where we left the bus then walked to our old billets at Wismes arriving 7.30pm. Very cold frosty night.
