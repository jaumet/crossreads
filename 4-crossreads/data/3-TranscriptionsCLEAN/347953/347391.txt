We had an excellent view from the mountain tops of all the surrounding    tops  country  & of the Turkish positions but on account of the open nature of the country we were frequently pepered with Schrapnell. May 21. Turks came forward with white flag at 5 p.m. and asked for armistice. General Godly objected  & asked them to come again in a proper manner.
