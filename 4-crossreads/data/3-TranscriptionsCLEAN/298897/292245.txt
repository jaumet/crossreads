Sunday 9th JanHaving had a severe pain in my right side ever since I left Lemnos which had given me three sleepless nights & which looked very like pleurisy I decided to break my record & go on sick parade.  The M.O. gave me a thorough sounding but found nothing wrong which left no doubt but that it was a muscular trouble & probably a strain through carrying the heavy tents & gear.  That relieved my mind & I have only to wait for time to affect a cure.  Went to church parade but struck a very poor chaplain.  It was a lovely day one of the best & I spent the most of the time reading my mail & answering the letters.  Start dispensing duties tomorrow.
