<p>13.8.18<br/>Usual work.  Did some roof work and completed the wood of roof.  In evening Bill came over and I went to his billets and spent evening with him.  Heard lecture by Chaplain Major on Palestine fighting.  Home at 10 and wrote diary &amp; went to bed.</p>
<p>14.2.18<br/>Didn't get up till late and wrote letters in intervals.  No. 2 on guard to-night.  Inoculated at 12.  Trip to Steenwerck to see Don but had gone to England.  Arm sore.  Cloudy.  Took some pills in even.</p>
<p>15.2.18<br/>Feeling ill to-day and diarrhoea very bad.  Could hardly work.  Some job.  Cloudy.  Ill all night.</p>
<p>16.2.18<br/>Went on sick parade so didn't get up till late and didn't do much all day.  Hayward gave a course of treatment for diarrhoea.  Spent a slightly better night.  Fine day with fair frost.</p>
<p>17.2.18<br/>Did nothing all day.  Still weak and inclined to be sick with cramps all over.  Can't eat at all.  Frost.</p>
<p>18.2.18<br/>Saw doctor again.  Slightly better.  Still can't eat.  More pills.  Didn't do much all day.  Frosty.</p>
<p>20.2.18<br/>Went out to work as usual &amp; went very gently and worked with <strike>Rosewarne</strike> Marchant on hut</p>
