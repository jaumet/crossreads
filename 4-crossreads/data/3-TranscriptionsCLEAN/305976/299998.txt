Course lasting 6 weeks Met several old pals  Tuesday Ap17  Wed April 17 Our instruction started this morning. Troop drill. Semaphore signalling. Visited Ismalia in evening. Rather pretty little town with trees planted in streets. There are some decent Houses & Streets & as usual some slums in Arab quarter. Thursday Ap 18 Semaphore Squad Drill. Troop Drill Concert in YMCA evening by Hot Totties. Moascar not much altered from when I landed.
