<p>1916 June 14<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Letter from Mary No 3. dated 16th Apr. giving later news.<br/>Go for route march thro' Etaples &amp;amp halfway to Paris Plage,  cold. Big Serg. &amp;amp quick walker. Inspection parade by O.C. Several chaps crimed for not shaving. <br/>Afternoons nothing doing. not usual. more at night Time put on hour. Summer time daylight saving<br/>Made marker. exempt from fatigues</p>
