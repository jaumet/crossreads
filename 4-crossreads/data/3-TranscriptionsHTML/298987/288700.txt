<p>Wednesday 19th<br/>Did 15 minutes PT then it rained &amp; kept on most of the day Concert in YM Quite good Payed 10/-</p>
<p>Thursday 20<br/>As usual. Never knew a Hut to be so quiet on a pay night must be saveing up for leave</p>
<p>Friday 21<br/>As usual Good Concert &amp; Aussie letters</p>
<p>Saturday 22<br/>Rought March. Started off over the Hill opposite the camp quite a stiff climb about 10 miles along open roads. Did not feel it at all today Concert in YM much higher class than any I have been to before</p>
<p>Sunday 23rd<br/>Open Air Service today. Went up hill opposite camp &amp; examined Rising Sun &amp; other Badges. They are made by cutting the turf off &amp; filling in the trench thus</p>
