  
 Egypt. 1916 Feb.13th. Last sight of good old Osterley   Dry rations- Poor train service from Suez- Pestering by natives begins- Indian sikh who admired Anzacs- Loss of cap.  Arrive Zeitoun 1 a.m.   Transport of kit to camp   Blanket issue- Bed. 2.30 am. Feb.14th. Parade 9 a. m - Address by O.C  Again meet Sgt Roberts & other Liverpool friends- Survey of camp- Easy day- First trip to Heliopolis & Cairo- Impression both good & bad- Slums- Natives- Y.M.C.A.
