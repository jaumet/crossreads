<p>July 1918<br/>Thursday 25</p>
<p>Frechencourt.<br/>Reeled and overhauled 3 miles of wire. Western, Drew and four others came on to the Spooks job, under compulsion.<br/>Weather showery<br/>Rode 24 kilos a very tiring ride too, but for a very happy reason <br/>Rode home my 24 kilos after having spent a rather pleasant afternoon.<br/>Weather simply rotten heavy rain and very little  [indecipherable] did not make the roads too good. Visited McSweeney</p>
