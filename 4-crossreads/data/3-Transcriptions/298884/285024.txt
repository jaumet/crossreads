<p>The coast is rugged but vegetation of a big variety grows right down to the waters edge. We saw very little of the dwelling places but a Sergt Major on one occasion drew a lot of attention by shouting out that he was able to see his home &amp; spent a great deal of time pointing it out to his mates.</p>
<p><u>17th</u> <strike>arrived at</strike>.Entered Port Philip Heads early this morning &amp; anchored off Point Nepean opposite the quarantine station. This station is the best situated of any we have seen yet.</p>
