<p>[Drawing]<br/>In addition to correct surroundings &amp; light I may mention the <strike>clothes &amp;</strike> equipment &ndash; webbing equipment.  In fact everything right.  A four hours stretch and worth it.</p>
<p>Tuesday 25 Feb. 1919</p>
<p>Dull &amp; rainy with fairly strong wind.  Painting again out of the question so with Spruce I hunted among the stunted bushes or small trees for pieces likely to make passable sticks or hunting crops which I hope to bring back as souvenirs of the peninsular.  We covered a lot of ground &amp; destroyed the original gloss of uniform &amp; general appearance by scrambling through thorny scrub in search of straight pieces.</p>
