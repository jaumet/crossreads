
Sunday 17 June 1917Hot & windy quiet day, church parade late did not go packed up for move onto beach.  Wrote No. [see images for possible Arabic number for 7].
Monday 18 June 1917Left Razar for the beach at 7.30 arrived early & put up lines & took horses for swim.  On picquet at night.
Tuesday 19 June 1917Cleaning up in morning, swim in afternoon.
Wednesday 20 June 1917Cleaning up in morning & swim in afternoon.
Thursday 21 June 1917Inspection by G.O.C. had great preparations.  All seemed to be quite satisfactory.  Concert at Y.M.C.A.
Friday 22 June 1917Quiet day, feeling a bit off after being caught in undertow.  Debate in Y.M.C.A.
Saturday 23 June 1917Concert by the Leisk Battery, quiet day, plenty swimming.
