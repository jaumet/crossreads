<p>"D" Company<br/>No. 6 Officer Cadet Battn.,<br/>Magdalen College<br/>Oxford.<br/>24TH Nov. 1918.</p>
<p>Dear Mother,</p>
<p>I have not written home for some time now and as an Australian mail is sailing next week I am taking the opportunity of sending you along a note to assure you all that I am O.K.  I'm afraid I have not kept up my usual home letters as frequently as I should have done, but once I came across to England I knew that you would be at ease and know that I was safe and out of danger. Since my last letter home the Armistice has been signed, and</p>
