<p>[Monday 20 March 1916]</p>
<p>Today shot 3 ducks 7 Quail 4 Curlew 4 Jack snipe 3 Plover &amp; some sort of Grouse.  The line of demarcation between Desert &amp; Valley of the Nile is like a fence, 20 yards between the two.  Out on the Desert, not even a bird is seen, only Lizards<br/>Inspected &amp; took photo of Native Incubator- 1000's of eggs, &amp; chicks falling out of them &amp; in adjoining mud hut the Chicks were fed leaf kept up by Maize stalks I told you before <u>Hens</u> never sit in Egypt.<br/>Now on last night in adjoining village [indecipherable] Natives killed, 20 wounded. Weapons used- old guns, reaping hooks, etc :- &amp; row over a piece of land, we had to turn out but only a local disturbance.<br/><strike>Letter from Mardi inclosing one from Kathleen &amp; one from you ex Katandra, glad to hear all's well</strike></p>
