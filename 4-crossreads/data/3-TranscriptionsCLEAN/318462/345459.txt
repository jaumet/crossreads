 Thursday 10/1/18 Snow melting fast tremendous thaw set in last night & all yesterdays snow has melted away But the ice underneath is still here & its the devil trying to walk. deferred Pay 40-14-6 Owe 8-7-0 Boys off to Blighty on leave every day don't expect I shall get away for another 12 months. Posted letter to Mrs Parsons. not so cold today all the designs gone off the windows. Sports on this afternoon & Tomorrow is Brigade sports. 
 Friday. 11/1/18 Snow nearly all gone, same old wind blowing & much warmer every where slush & mud for a change. Wrote to Nell last night. Sports to day. deferred Pay. 40-16-0 Owe 8-5-0 a little rain but not much still warm 8.30 pm 
 Saturday 12/1/18 Still keeping mild but I think going to rain. Sports next Saturday leave here about 27th for -------- Another letter from Nell also Jack, Carol, & Tony yesterday. will write to day. deferred Pay 40-17-6 Owe 8-3-0
