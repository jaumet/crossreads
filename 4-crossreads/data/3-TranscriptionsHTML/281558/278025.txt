<p>Friday Aug 17th</p>
<p>Monasterie : showery " reveille 6 am : parade for us : went for a walk down the stream &amp; got some lettuce " hired a punt in afternoon &amp; went down the canal to the junction : Morrie &amp; self caught 16 eels after tea</p>
<p>Saturday Aug 18th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 6.30 am : route march to Arneke &amp; back : not too bad : back about 4 pm : bath after tea</p>
<p>Sunday Aug 19th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 7 am : H. Com 9.30 : P.C. from Aunt Myra &amp; some wedding cake from Jess &amp; Harold : stroll after tea with Rock &amp; Alf to aerodrome : flying has certainly be mastered &amp; I would be delighted to go up in a plane</p>
<p>Monday Aug 20th</p>
<p>Monasterie : good day : reveille 6 am : we had to go on parade : fell in</p>
