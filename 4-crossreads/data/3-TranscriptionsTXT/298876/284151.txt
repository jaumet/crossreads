 Friday 10/3/16 
 Quite cool all day &ndash; Revalie at 6 am get on Parade at 6.30. Have a bit of a march till 7.30. Often go across to the ostrich Farms quite close to the Camp. Have had a Fatigue Party all day. Great Sensation in Cairo yesterday. An Australian was observed saluting an Officer. Another 17 A.M.C. men came in at 3 am from S. Australia. This is my opinion of Australians &ndash; They are seen &amp; heard at every corner of Cairo &ndash; in fact Australians predominate  in the life here today &ndash; They take up all the footpath &amp; most of the road.
