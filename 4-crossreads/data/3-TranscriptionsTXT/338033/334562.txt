
The sun. Good Friday was celebrated by service in the morning and a sacred lantern lecture in the evening. 23rd Easter Sunday a special service on the morning and a sacred concert at night. We crossed the line on the 24th but the occasion was not celebrated in the usual way. 25th being Anzac day, a dinner was given to returned men who were on their way back again. We were given a short lecture on Anzac day and then had a short concert.On the 27th we again caught sight of land early in the morning and by eight at night we were
