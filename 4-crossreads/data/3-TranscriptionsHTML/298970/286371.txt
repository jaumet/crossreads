<p>except for aeroplanes.  At night on returning to Wye Farm things were terrifying in the fields to our left.  Guns and bursting shells made a fierce chaos.  Looking out from the porthole in our building the scene was awful.  We had no sleep till 3 o'clock in the morning.</p>
<p>7th August (Monday)</p>
<p>Went to Elbow Farm for rations &amp; mail.  Fritz shelling the batteries behind us also Fleurbaix which is already in ruins.  Numbers wounded in the village.<br/>British squadron of planes passed over.  There were 8 altogether &amp; Fritz failed to hit any.  No doubt we have the superiority here in aeroplanes.</p>
