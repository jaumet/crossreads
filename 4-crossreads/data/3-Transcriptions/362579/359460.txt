<p>The patients here get a good time   &amp;  have lots of amusement   &amp;  invitations out to parties   &amp;  theatres.</p>
<p>I was helping in D &amp;  E Wards. They both looked very pretty, with Red, Pink   &amp;  White cherry blossoms. Shall tell you more about it when I return.</p>
<p>Plenty of Smokoes all day. An excellent dinner Roast Goose, cabbage, potatoes   &amp;  ham, cooked well &ndash; a lovely plum pudding with white sauce   &amp;  custard. Jellies   &amp;   tin fruits. A pint of Beer each. Sweets, apples nuts   &amp;  crackers.</p>
<p>Their tea was cold ham, beetroot, cakes   &amp;  jellies &ndash; bread   &amp;  tea.</p>
