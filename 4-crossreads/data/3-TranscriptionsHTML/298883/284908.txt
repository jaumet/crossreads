<p>Mademoiselle Clare should at an unlucky moment come into the barn looking for eggs as a hen raised such a cackling at sight of me and Mlle Clare always made a point of inspecting the nests when a hen cackled.  I finished and reached my room with my teeth chattering but thankful to have escaped without any embarrassment.  However to balance any of the little failings here stated I must say that Madame is a good motherly soul and her apple dumplings are the finest I have ever tasted.</p>
