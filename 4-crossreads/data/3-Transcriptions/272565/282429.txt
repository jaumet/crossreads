<p>Boxing Day<br/>No work again today.  High class and rich meals again were the order of the menu.  Strolled to Vignacourt &amp; Flesselles.  At 5 p.m., in response to a kind invitation from certain friends in the tent division 3 of us (bearers) were welcomed at a "banquet" in one of the Chateau rooms.
