  
 no one here but ourselves. We go to Jericho in three instalments, starting tomorrow at 4 ac emma we journey even unto Bethany which is distant three Kilos from Jerusalem along the via Doloroso - Tensan - Jerichosa. Tarrying there until the sun is low again we gird up our loins & journey forth to Talat Ed Durum  in the direction of the Moabites, & having eaten & drunken & (possibly) anointed ourselves on evening late of the third day
