 16 March 1918 Saturday Left Wadi  Auja  &amp; marched in the rain to  Rummon  drew rations &amp; on in the dark to Beitin 4 horses died of cold &amp; the infantry plodded along in the mud. We could light no fires &amp; had no tucker since yesterday mornings breakfast.  8 Nigger camel drivers died with the cold &amp; wet.  They cannot stand the cold like our men.
