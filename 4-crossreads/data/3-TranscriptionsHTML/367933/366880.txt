<p>I shld like to bring to yr notice a system which we have just inaugerated, by which, through the co-operation and courtesy of the Tobacco Co, we can now send parcels of Australian tobacco by post under bond &ndash; thus saving from 3/- to 4/- a lb. This is quite the most direct way of sending to Egypt and when you consider that you can send 40 tins of tobacco by post for &pound;1 you will realise how important it is to adopt this method and Australian tobacco is one of the greatest treats our men can have.</p>
