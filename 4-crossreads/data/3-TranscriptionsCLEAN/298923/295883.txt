
5 August 1918 MondayArrived midday & went to Anglo American Hospital where I found Dolly as lively as a cricket no trouble at all & both doing well  She is such a strong girl never felt anything much & never took any Chloroform-  I am sleeping at the Flat & go out each day twice to the Hospital.Cabled you & Mrs Ish this evening.  We are christening the Kiddy Kathleen Dorothy- the former after good old Day.
