  
 Sunday also, & the work of chaplains came under review & the question of Supernumeray levy (Current) was discussed and a unanimous vote that the Conference should be appealed to to pay it. On Friday it rained all day, & blew, the creeks and all flooded & in some parts damage has been done. 
 Saturday last I had a funeral at Salisbury, a death from Meningitis. 
 Sunday I had 2 services one at 9.15 & the other at 10.30 the latter was a very fine service. On Sunday night I got instructions to be ready
