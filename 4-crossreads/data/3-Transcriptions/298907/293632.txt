<p>29th. Have been out again this afternoon strolling in the sun &amp; yarning to all &amp; sundry otherwise it has been a very quiet &amp; uneventful day, there are some very fine nurses here &amp; it is quite a pleasure to speak with them, though I am afraid they have very funny ideas of us &amp; Australia' heavens only knows what their ideas where previous to the war breaking out.</p>
