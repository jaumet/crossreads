<p>22/3/18  Red Lodge</p>
<p>lead to trouble and there is the making of a great Paper War regarding the  casualties of the 24th in their little gas operation. Sutherland had a great plaster of papers and documents for me up to an hour as late as 11o'c. There is an immense amount of writing involved in this job of ours.</p>
