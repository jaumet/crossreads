Sighted small school of whales in the distance, during morning and have been accompanied by a great number of porpoises who are always playing in front of the ship.
Several cases of mumps reported.
Monday 18thStill good weather. Only a bit of life boat drill done this morning. Haven't seen any passing ships as yet and are 400 mls. from land.
