<p>Jan 19th<br/>Party at Mannings tonight.  Harry Fletcher and Mac from 24th there.  Kiss in the ring and goodness knows what  a bonzer night.</p>
<p>Jan 21st<br/>Inspected at Tower by Gen. Sir William Robertson.  A bunch of Russian officers on parade.  (Russia wins). He gave us an address in which he said "I am pleased to see you Gentlemen, for before me I see the cream of the British Army (B.S).  Good luck and God speed.</p>
<p>Went to Maid of the Mountains.</p>
