<p>Monday 9th July 1917</p>
<p>Orville<br/>Reveille   3am.<br/>Stables 3.30 breakfast 4am and ready to move out by 5am. Did not move out till near 6am. Going north. Arrived Orville about 12 noon. Harness off and watered mules. Good stables. After billeted in large brick building. Had a good feed of fried eggs, bread and butter. Got to bed about 9pm.</p>
