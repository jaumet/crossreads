Her father is an insignificant little man but a domestic tyrant who objects to her fiance a young French soldier &ndash; the latter was home on leave a fortnight ago and it was amusing how he hung about until the old mans back was turned &ndash; and at night when the old man was snoring below she would be leaning out of her attic window whispering to 'Romeo' below with all the neighbours for an audience.</p>
