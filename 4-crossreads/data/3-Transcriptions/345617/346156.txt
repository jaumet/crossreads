<p>Aout 22<br/>Coy marched to Blaringham where troops from each Brigade were reviewed by General Godley. Reached camp again about 2p.m. every-body feeling rather tired. The sun was very hot &amp; the steel helmets were very trying to wear.<br/>Went with O.C. to Hazebrouke to see a building which we are going to demolish &amp; bring down &amp; re erect at Ebblingham.</p>
<p>Aout 23<br/>Cpl Morrison &amp; I went to Hazebrouke to supervise dismantling of building which we are going to rebuild at Ebblingham. The building is to be used in connection with schooling &amp; housing of refugee children gathered in from villages that have been destroyed during the war.</p>
