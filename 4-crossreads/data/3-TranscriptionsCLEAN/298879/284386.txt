  
 June 1915 Saturday 5 Fine.   On water fatigue. We have scarcely have any rest now. We have to go over in "Shrapnel Gully" for the water so named because of the deadly effect of the Turk's shrapnel shells there. Great artillery duel this morning. Water fatigue  again  this afternoon. "A tot of rum issued to every  man " 
 Sunday 6  Fine . Our platoon in the firing line again. No news on account of our "Peninsular Press" not being published. quiet day
