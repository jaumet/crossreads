 June 12th Having a free day S. M. Carlisle, Jack Davey &amp; myself take a run out to the 7th F.Amb. where I meet most of my "Qld Pals", all of whom are very well &ndash; Harry at present in England &ndash; Interesting conversations &ndash; pleasing "reminiscences &amp; anecdotes" &ndash; A long talk with Reg Dunn about dear old "Elm Bank" &ndash; Entertained in Sergeants Mess &ndash; Proud possessor of many snap shots &ndash; Return to Bertangles in Motor Ambulance 11 p.m. &ndash; Leslie Hanson
