<p>The 5th B'de fell back from Bazincourt  Wood &amp; Ommiecourt on 30th, &amp; deployed across Somme Bridge at Feuilleres, &amp; occupied Elew [?] Trench system,   Remained there until 8 P.M. on 30th Aug.</p>
<p>There was a barrage from zero (hopping off time) plus 30.</p>
<p>They occupied zero plus 40, the intermediate objective.</p>
<p>By that time barrage had lifted from 5.30 am till 6 AM on the ultimate objective.  The 17th Batt was directly in front of Mt St Q, the 20th on the left extended to Feuillacourt</p>
