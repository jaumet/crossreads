<p>been pounding shell upon shells into this area, about two miles across. first there was the support trenches, then the front line, then across no mans land, then the enemys front line then his support trenches, then and only then does one begin to get into decent country again.</p>
<p>Well, as I said on the 17th we receive our orders to go forward. hooking eight horses into each gun limber we move up in the early morning to where our guns are, being in the side of a sunken road, hook them on &amp; away we start, a thick slimy mud up to the horses belleys. we go along this road</p>
