<p>47<br/>very little tea is drunk, may be in the big cities.</p>
<p>One picture was the crowding into carriages. Of a night it was not possible to stretch out and go to sleep, it was a matter of just sitting in the corner and dozing.</p>
<p>Whilst in the journey news came of the battle of Pozieres, the place where the Australians fought their first big stunt.</p>
<p>On the journey we passed a train load of "jocks", this being our first experience of British troops, excepting a few we saw on the wharf at Port Suez.</p>
<p>We eventually reached our destination, Havre, the only feature worth recording was the eagerness displayed by the French "garcon" after a tea of "Bully Beef". The journey</p>
