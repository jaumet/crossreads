
Billie is in fine spirets lately, He goes down visiting George & Ruth Sippi.
I am continuing this scribble tomorrow because there has been nothing doing for a couple of days.
Friday March 24thBillie is plagueing me to take him to a Bazaar to morrow, I suppose I will have to, it is Budden's up at Killara It is for some funds.
