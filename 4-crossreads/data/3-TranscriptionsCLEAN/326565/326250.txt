Thursday 25/11/15Very warm, nearing the line must expect hot weather.  Very large sick parade.  Getting a nuisance coming after sick parade hours, practically attending to someone all day.  Started to treat some of their corns, had a crowd around before I knew where I was, soon cut it out.
