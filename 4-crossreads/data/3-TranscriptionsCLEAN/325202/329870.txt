July 26/17Cycled over yesterday afternoon to Neuve Eglise & saw both Colling & Jimmy Shaw. Coming back at dusk I saw a procession of many tanks moving up nearer the line, today there has been heavy artillery all day & the air alive with lanes The offensive is evidently not far off.
