
Feb. 28thStill enjoying Peace & Quietness - Purchases of many Books for my next soujourn in France - Being pressed into a game of snooker by an Edinburgh gentleman in our Hotel - a New Zealand Corporal & myself have two easy victories - I surprise myself by doing all the scoring - Entertained by Miss Hater. - At night I witness my first Opera, which is "Tales of Hoffmann" by Carl Rosa Grand Opera Company.
