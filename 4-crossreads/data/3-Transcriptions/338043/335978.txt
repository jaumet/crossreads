<p>Sept. 25th<br/>Having a free evening owing to Engine troubles, - I take the opportunity, &amp; write new and original sketch entitled "&pound;500" &ndash; Up to date this is certainly my best effort.<br/>- Morning rehearsal.</p>
<p>Sept. 26th<br/>Letters from London &ndash; complete a new poem &ndash;<br/>Rehearsal of "Eastern Romance" &amp; "The Burglar".  Show resumed. &ndash; Crowded house &ndash; Cold &amp; wet weather.</p>
