<p>20th March 1917. Woke at 8 for breakfast cleaned up &amp; fooled around during the morning  Went on duty from 12 till 4. Went on again at 8 till 12. Another very quiet day. Wrote to Millie</p>
<p>21st march 1917. Woke at 8 for duty till 12. Came off &amp; fooled around during afternoon. Came on again at 6 till 8. Very quiet day. Went to bed at 9. Going on duty at 4 till 8.</p>
<p>22nd March 1917. Came off at 8 &amp; fooled around during the morning. Left at 12 for baths. Had bath &amp; had dinner in Armentieres. Came back at 4.30 &amp; went on duty at 6 till 8. Went to bed ready for duty at 12 till 4.</p>
<p>23rd March 1917. Woke at 8 for breakfast. Went on duty at 10 till 4 oclock. Came off &amp; had tea. Afterwards went to estaminet &amp; had a few "wads". Came back &amp; went on duty 8 till 12.</p>
<p>24th March 1917. Went on duty at 8 till 12. Fooled around during the afternoon. Went on again at 6 till 8. Very quiet day.</p>
<p>25th March 1917. Got up at 4 for duty till 8. Fooled around all day till 4 when I went on duty till 6. Had some tea at estaminet. Went to bed early. On duty at 12 till 4</p>
