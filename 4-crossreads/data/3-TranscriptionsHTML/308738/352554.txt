<p>Nov. 20 Saturday 1915<br/>Witnesses out at Mustapha all day<br/>Case concluded returned 3. 30 PM</p>
<p>Nov 21 Sunday 1915<br/>Church morning &amp; night. went to Noosa Garden in afternoon</p>
<p>Nov. 22 Monday 1915<br/>Worked till 12. 30 PM today<br/>Orders came to leave PO &amp; proceed with Pethard, Morgan, Rogers, Stewart to Over Seas Base Cairo. Reason unknown<br/>Left Alex &ndash; 4. 20 train. Arrived Base Cairo 8. 30 PM</p>
