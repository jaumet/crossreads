  
 December  1918. Sunday 1 
 Wembley. On furlough. Slept until noon. Had a glorious hot tub, and spent a pleasant afternoon. Went to Church and held hands after service. Mrs Jones is picking up very rapidly indeed and I sincerely hope she will soon be her old self. Made arrangements to take Eve to theatre on Tuesday night. Cnr of Oxford St and Ttham Ct Road at 6.30 pm.  Wrote E and Mother, also old "Doc" Sharpe. Made arrangements for a party on Wed next also.
