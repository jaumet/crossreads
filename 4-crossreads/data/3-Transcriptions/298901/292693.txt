<p>Was kept fairly busy during the afternoon &amp; began to feel better towards evening.  Turned in about 10 P.M. into the best bed I have had in the village am now in a cottage with the W.O. &amp; 3 other Sgts &amp; have it all to ourselves just as the people left it.  Each night the others cook a substantial meal for supper, there being potatoes &amp; onions in the house, other things being obtained by a little fossicking.  Of course coal &amp; fuel is plentiful &amp; the fire is a good one.  Rhubarb &amp; a few such things are also plentiful but they are not for me as my condition will not allow it.</p>
<p>Thursday 18th April<br/>Spent a fair night being up once at 2.30 AM but several times after 6, slept in all the morning without breakfast.  Got up for dinner feeling weak &amp; ill.  Found things rather mixed up at the Dressing room so had to set to. Made out a requisition for drugs &amp; sent off for them also issued some drugs.<br/>The Major approached me again about going off duty &amp; will have  a spell tomorrow at least as I am afraid of getting weak &amp; having to be</p>
