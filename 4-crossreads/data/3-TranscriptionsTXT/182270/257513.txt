 SUNDAY, JULY 20, 1919. 
 Fine. Peter &amp; I &amp; Ham went over to 
 Coomoo Coomoo in big car. Called 
 in at Merrilong on the way &amp; picked 
 up old Billy Dick. Played tennis 
 all the afternoon. The Trails from 
 Colly Blue were there too. We 
 stayed at Coomoo for dinner &amp; 
 came back after.
