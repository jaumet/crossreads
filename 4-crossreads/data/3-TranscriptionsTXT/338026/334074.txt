 Southall. 
 [17 Sun] London Hyde Park. 
 [18 Mon] Remained in hospital for a change. 
 [19 Tues] London Had a look at King George, Queen Mary, Prince of Wales &amp; Princess Mary driving along the Mall. Met W. Beaton in YMCA 
 [20 Wed] Spent the afternoon in London but went home early as it was very foggy. Got a lift from E. Broadway to Hospital by one of our cars. Would never have got home otherwise at  cars  traffic at stand still
