 19th. Sunday. March Reading &amp; Cards morning, ditto afternoon. Weather calm &amp; clear. Writing evening. 
 20th. Monday. Weather continues good. parade for innoculations &amp; reading. Afternoon Rifle cleaning &amp; Seniority lists gone into. Recommended for 3rd Stripe. Writing eve. Passed Malta 5.30Pm. Looked fine Rugged &amp; strong &amp; large. 
 21st. Tuesday. Weather fair but cold. Good Hot Bath after lunch [Indecipherable] rooms.  Finished up letter writing for mail. Sea rising &amp; heavy wind towards evening 
 22nd. Wednesday. Mail closed 9am. Weather fair again. S.A. Inspection. reading &amp; washing. 
 23rd.  Orderly Cpl. Dissembarked about 10.30 Lunch on wharf dealing with French people
