<p>22nd<br/>Off Guard 4.30.  Tea then to Canteen but could not get to counter before closing.</p>
<p>23rd<br/>Parade all day 9.15 a.m. to 11, 1.30 to 4 p.m.  Weather Cloudy &amp; cold.  Parcel Miss Purcell.</p>
<p>24th<br/>Parades as yesterday, N.C.O.s special parade during afternoons drill.  Y.M.C.A. Canteen but could not get served before closing.  Weather still dull &amp; damp.</p>
<p>25th<br/>Weather Beautifull, Bright &amp; warm.  On Water Fatigue all day.  Canteen after tea, most of which closed.  (Xmas)</p>
