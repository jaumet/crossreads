 Arrived at Dijon at 11.30 where some ladies gave us cold tea to drink.  Dijon is a large &amp; busy town, mainly remarkable for its beautiful Notre Dame Church &amp; Cathedral. The fine black spire can be seen miles away. Leaving Dijon the country changes from flat to hilly &amp; wooded slopes. Tea at Les Laumes.  During night passed Paris &amp; arrived at Mantes Gissecourt for breakfast. From there onwards the scenery was of an agricultural description, rather drab
