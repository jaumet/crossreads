 [Friday 29 September 1916] 
 Went to Lunch at Brother of Mrs Ferrars a lovely place on Belfast lock then afternoon tea in Belfast &amp; on to a Sir- Brett an  cousin  uncle Lawyer &amp; home to a Champagne Dinner. Mr Ferrar is alive &amp; well &amp; 80 years of age. Recd. Australian Mail from Laura. up to July 27 2 from Mother 1 from Day 1 from Joe but NO parcel or fountain pen these may come from Egypt. Letter from Kathleen &amp; May &amp; many others.  Glad all is well.  11.15 on Monday will see me a married man- Love to you all.
