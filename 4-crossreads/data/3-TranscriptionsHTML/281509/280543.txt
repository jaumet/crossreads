<p>16 November 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Do nothing much all day except try to keep warm &ndash; a hopeless task in this God forsaken country  Ice &amp; frost everywhere. mud frozen hard. Bob Johno &amp; self over to village &amp; canteen.</p>
<p>I have an acute attack of indigestion &amp; suffer some all afternoon &amp; evening</p>
