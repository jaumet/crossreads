<p>1917 FEBRUARY</p>
<p>21 WEDNESDAY</p>
<p>Fine but dull &amp; misty. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness &amp; horses ect all morning</p>
<p>&amp; the battery had to turn out</p>
<p>about 4 pm to get ammunition</p>
<p>&amp; take it up to the pits. We</p>
<p>sent 4 horse teams &amp; I did</p>
<p>not have to go. Got parcels</p>
<p>from C.S. Stores &amp; Mother.</p>
