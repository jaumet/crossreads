 June 1915 Monday 21  Hot  Gen Sir Ian Hamilton looked the hospital today. He seemed a very genial old chap sitting on the side of the beds inquiring as to the nature of each patients illness, myself being one of them. One point about him is that his teeth are  his own  
 Tuesday 22 Very hot. I with many others came over here to the English Convalescent Hospital Tents. The red Cross staff here are slightly undermanned here at present but they do their work well.
