<p>In fact any man with a reasonably good excuse may appeal &amp; he'll be given exemption &amp; married men will not be called up before Sept. 1917.  For the sake of our honour, to keep our promises &amp; to send reinforcements to those men, so dear to us now fighting for us in Egypt &amp; in France, let us each do our best to win votes in favour of yes!</p>
