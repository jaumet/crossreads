  
 25 January, Saturday 
 F & ice We go to Dance but I am not in mood neither is Mifo[?]. Moove some sit out & tell stories. I play for Schottische[Scottish Country Dance]. Go to Top's flat & talk until bed time. I sleep better in fact too well & can't get up next morning. 
 26 January, Sunday 
 W Tommy W & I go to Durrington & to church. I scale Miss M to go to church. Tea & supper at YMCA Florrie & I meet in the aisle & she is very conscious of her wrong doings. I hurry Home with Allam Waterman & Tom & write letter in triplicate. Wrote MRL Home & Mary.
