 Well old chap, I hope you are doing well.  Remember me to old Jim, Jack Fraser, &amp; any of the old hands that I knew. 
 I am afraid this has been rather an egotistical letter, but I will give you the general news when I get back to where the Sun shines every day.  It is deucedly cold &amp; foggy here.
