<p>Monday 25th<br/>Sea still very calm, which is getting pretty monotonous The steadiness of the boat is giving us all the blues.</p>
<p>Tuesday 26th<br/>The mumps are spreading pretty quickly, and there is a danger of being quarantined at Durban which is our first port of call. All our belongings fumigated and decks disinfected.</p>
<p>Wednesday 27th<br/>Weather still calm, and getting walmer. On picquet again. Saw about 20 flying</p>
