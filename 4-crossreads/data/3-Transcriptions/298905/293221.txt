<p>legs spreadout, they blow their trenches to pieces, they are shotting their small mortars &amp; Minnewerfers at us in dozen's there are a few chaps getting hit.<br/>A squadron of our aeroplanes about 7 in number flew straight over the German trenches this morning they must be going on a raid somewhere.<br/>The artillery have stopped firing now but we still have the bombs coming we now have watchers for the bombs, &amp; they sing out when they see them coming , in this way we dodge a lot of them, nice isnt it &amp; me just back from England."<br/>Len &amp; his party are going out tonight armed as a fighting patrol, they never had a single weapon on them last night, except a few bombs, he &amp; a few more are going out each night to see if they can come across them</p>
