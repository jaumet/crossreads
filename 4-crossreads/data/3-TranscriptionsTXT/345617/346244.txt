 Novembre 18 Due to sail for France today but am not leaving until tomorrow. Jack came down today to lunch with us &amp; afterward we went to Kensington to see his friends Mr &amp; Mrs Yeates &amp; friends. Came back to Mabel House about 6.45 pm. &amp; spent the evening here. 
 Novembre 19 Up at 5 a.m. &amp; left Victoria Station at about 7.30. Got to Folkestone about 10 left about 11. Boulogne about 12.45 marched to next camp &amp; had Reveille for homeward journey at midnight.
