29th August:- TuesdayThe greatest day for months  The news in this morning's "Daily Mail" is a repetition of "La Presse" d'hier soir. By jove, we shall see something moving amongst those Austrians & Bulgarians soon. The enthusiasm of the French is remarkle but quietly suppressed.. One needs to talk with them, as I have done to find out their feelings & they are highly excited over Rumania's declaration of war, but, & greatly to their credit, do not show the least sign of it. Our boys also & it's good enough to give one good health again   It is at least a great turning point
