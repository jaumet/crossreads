<p>Monday 13 May</p>
<p>Service yesterday morning by Major Makeham chaplain of 3rd Brigade . Curiously enough he was the man to whom a note of recommendation by Bishop Long of Bathurst had been sent on my behalf.</p>
<p>An oldish man (56 years) he is an old naval chaplain.  Shortly going to return to England to take over Chaplain in Chief at Tidworth and later</p>
