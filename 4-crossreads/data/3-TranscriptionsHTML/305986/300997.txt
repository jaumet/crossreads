<p>happened, couldn't get any wood for a fire just had to stand there and shiver, we got out of the train at Ribencourt and marched to another village where we turned in, we got a fire and went to sleep, Today we have been parading and mucking about, got a card from Lill, heard about the I W.W stunt in Sydney you ought to hear the crowd, I wish those mongrels were over here rather guess they'ed get a rough passage the swine. Think we will be in The line</p>
