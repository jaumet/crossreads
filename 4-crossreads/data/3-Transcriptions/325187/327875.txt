<p>[Friday 12 May 1916]</p>
<p><strike>40</strike> 20 miles East of Kantara.<br/>Very Hot &amp; camped on the desert where the Yeomanry were cut up recently-<br/>Flies bad, but after my spell at Court Martial &amp; Cairo I am quite satisfied to buck in [indecipherable] assistant   DAQME if you know what that is- A at Divisional H.Q. Deputy Assistant Quarter Master General- Temporarily Sounds important ??<br/>Search lights from ships passing along Suez Canal beam across the Desert &amp; a Military Train carries a search light also.<br/>Nothing but Sand dunes &amp; desolation, no cultivation no Egyptians only Arabs</p>
