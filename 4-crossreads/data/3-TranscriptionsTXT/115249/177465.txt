 Naval Signal 
 From - Flag 
 To - Berrima 
 Date - 13-9-14 
 Time - 9-0 
 Following received from Encounter following received from shore. &nbsp;Pass to Berrima. &nbsp;I have received no copy of proclomation and should be glad to receive one. &nbsp;Delay in informing you of this was owing to this afternoon operations my reports have also been delayed for same reasons. &nbsp;I  would &nbsp;will endeavour to send them in the morning. &nbsp;1855
