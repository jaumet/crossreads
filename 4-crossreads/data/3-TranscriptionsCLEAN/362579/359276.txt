
Kelly   &  I are going to Scotland    for a few days - perhaps leaving on Thursday. The former has some friends whom so wants me to meet. I cannot be bothered telling my friends I'm here, - they all asked so many questions   &  I can't be bothered - it is finally ended that little affair of mine   &  I'm jolly glad now I know. Received a letter from his O.C. saying he is quite well   &  in the Battalion. Why he has behaved in this manner I cannot understand - but I shall never trouble him again   &  am done with the whole d-Lot.
Anyhow I'm very happy   &  have plenty of interest in life to keep me busy. Hope to write a long newsy letter in a few days time. Hope I wrote to dear old Annie Thomas, thanking her for the soap, if not tell her I'm
