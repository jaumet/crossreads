<p>May 15 At 2 am our sqdn takes Turks trenches, but party detailed to fill them in cant get over, so we return at dawn.  Knocked over by a bomb in this event but nothing serious.   Very few answer roll call.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    16 Spend day in dug out.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    17 Quiet day.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    18 Trench digging.   New kind of shell arrives &amp; tears the scenery about.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    19 Turks attack Popes, Quinns &amp; Courtneys at daybreak.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Repulsed everywhere.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    20 Fifth attack within 2 &frac12; hours occurs at 12.15 am.  Hell of a row but no luck for the Unspeakable.   Armistice for about 1 hour in the afternoon.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    21 Very heavy firing in the morning.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    22 &amp; 23 Quiet.   Rain (23)<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    24 Armistice from 7.30 am till 4.30 pm.   Turkish losses in last attacks estimated at 10,000.</p>
