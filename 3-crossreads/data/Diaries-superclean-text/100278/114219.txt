Capt Price came on the bounce again this evening, he has got a nice set on us, I know what it is all over "the tucker" for Mr Champion and myself kicked up a row over it the other day and he is going to make our stay as uncomfortable as possible. 
There has been a most terrific bombardment all day long on our right the "Frenchies" have been tearing it into them and tonight I hear that the French have taken Deromse with a lot of prisoners I hope its true.
