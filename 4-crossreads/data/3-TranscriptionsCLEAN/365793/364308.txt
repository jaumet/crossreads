  
 Jly 27 Service, so p'raps they'll give me a job too 
 28 Going before Morseley & Ryan this m'ng for the Verdict; nearly 300 here now. "Later" Not going before the Board, or at least I haven't been warned. Buksheesh entertainment at the Jubilee Hall this afternoon, & it was a jolly decent show too. Came down in a Charabanc but it had insheed after the show. Had Photo taken, doz pcs - Also met the girl in the - coat, & had a nice long chat. 
 29 Clothing requisitions being filled  in  this morning, so things look promising.
