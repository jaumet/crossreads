Fri. 3rd March 1916.Sandstorm yesterday  & today. Could not leave the tents.Not enough water & a shortage of tucker.The "Gyppo" engine drivers are selling us the water out of their engine boilers.
Sunday. 19th .March.The Prince of Wales was to inspect us today but a soon as he arrived with Birdwood the boys broke from their lines & flocked around him & his horse.I suppose this is a prelude to our embarkation for France.Lt. Col. Macnaughton left Bn. yesterday for hospital.
Wed. March 22nd.Struck camp this afternoon  & crossed Canal to Serapeum East Railway siding.Bivouaced for night & entrained early next morning for Alexandria.
Thurs. Mar. 23rd.Embarked on the SS. "Simla" & left Alexandria tonight for France.
