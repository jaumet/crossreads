<p>30 Sun September<br/>Pay day also Prayers</p>
<p>1 Mon October<br/>C Squadron played G Sqd Football  a scoreless draw  very dusty</p>
<p>2 Tues<br/>Very dusty   a couple of Taubes over talk of a big move</p>
<p>3 Wed<br/>A &amp; B Sqd Played a draw  Four Taubes over in the morning   still very dusty and rain seems near</p>
