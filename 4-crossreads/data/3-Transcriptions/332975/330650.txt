<p>11-30 till 4 P.M. &ndash; Bombardment from 11-00 till 12-30 &ndash; about a dozen Casualties &ndash; advance on the left 7th Brigade.<br/>June 7th &ndash; Standing to all night &ndash; Turned in 5-30 &ndash; till 12-00 &ndash; dinner &ndash; On Fatigue at 2 o'clock till 4-30 &ndash; Tea 5-00 &ndash; Packed up after doing 12 days in the trenches &ndash; On fatigue from 8-30 till 12-00 &ndash; Marched to Billets Just &frac12; mile from firing line in Support Trenches.<br/>June 8th &ndash; Slept till 10-00 &ndash; breakfast &ndash; Rest all day &ndash; Went for stroll to Th-- [Thiepval?] &ndash; Turned in 10-00.<br/>June 9th &ndash; on Fatigue 5-30 till 12-00 on Fire trench &ndash; Aerial activity around Bois Grenier &ndash; Paid 20 Francs.<br/>June 10th &ndash; Breakfast 9-00 &ndash; Went for walk to Chappel Darmentiers lost the road &ndash; Came out at N.Z. Firing line then came to Armentiers and met with terrible wreckage churches etc.  On Fatigue 8-30 till 2 o'clock raining.<br/>June 11th &ndash; Turned in 9-00 &ndash; On fatigue 8-30 till 2-00.<br/>June 12th &ndash; innoculated &ndash; cold &amp; raining &ndash; no duties 24 hrs &ndash; Bombardment Ypres &amp; Bois Grenier</p>
