<p>[June 1918]<br/>23 Sun<br/>Been to church<br/>24 Mon<br/>Another full moon but the hun as been very quite with his aeroplanes<br/>25 Tues<br/>He threatened to have the town of Rouen in flames this moonshine but so far so good<br/>26 Wed<br/>I have, at this place, as at all others made good but I daresay that I will soon get Marching orders</p>
