
Sunday 25 February 1917Left Kantarah by the 5.45 train & came back to Bir-el-Abd & then into Bayud.
Monday 26 February 1917On day stableman.
Tuesday 27 February 1917Played N.Z. football & won played a draw, hard match.
Wednesday 28 February 1917Nothing doing in particular, had to get my flipper dressed.
Thursday 1 March 1917Quiet day:  Billy Ahearn got his com.
Friday 2 March 1917Billy A'hearn left for Bir-el-Abd.  Very windy day.  M.G. & H.Q. v B. Sq. -  won 9-3.
Saturday 3 March 1917Got my hand bandaged in morning.  A Sq. v M.G. & Q.V.
