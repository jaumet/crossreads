<p>Then all along the front arose the most dreadful din I ever heard hundreds of horns were blowing and bells ringing, other noise making instruments going strong. I turned the screw on my Strombos Horn; and it immediately set up a frightful bellow. Then I rushed along the shelters waking the chaps by shouting; "Gas! Put your helmets on!" I was a false alarm however; someone got the wind up about the rising mist I suppose.<br/>27/9/16.<br/>Working, fixing up the train line that runs along this road.
