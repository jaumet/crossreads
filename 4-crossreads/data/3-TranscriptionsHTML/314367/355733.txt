<p>Saturday Jan 30-15 &ndash;  Swim at 6.15 am &ndash; I am Medical Guard to-day &ndash; This means this ship flies a signal &amp; any ship in harbor who wants a doctor sends off for me &ndash; I have to remain inboard 7 Bells to 7 Bells &ndash; i.e. 7.30 am to 7.30 am &amp; do what comes along &ndash; There's a little innovation started which I have not previously mentioned &ndash; We found we had a bugler on board &amp; that he got 3d per diem for bugling &ndash; He now has to start the revelly at 5.30 &amp; right thro the day till 7 pm you hear the calls going out &ndash; Each evolution has its call Divisions &ndash; General quarters &ndash; Fire &ndash; Gunnery &ndash; Evening quarters at 7 pm &amp; so on &ndash;<br/>All hands are piped to dinner &amp; to fold 'ammicks by the same style of pipe as in Nelson's day &ndash; a pipe much like a pipe but the stem ends at the top of the bowl &ndash; [Sketch].  It takes time &amp; practice to use the pipe efficiently &ndash; They are made of Silver &amp; only issued by the Navy &ndash; [Sketch]<br/>The day was hot &amp; heavy &amp; the Ward room disgusted me by its talk about Sydney &ndash; mails &amp; girls &ndash; I wanted volunteers to climb Die Mutter 3300 feet &amp; got no encouragement &ndash; I cursed them for soft girls &amp; townies.</p>
