<p>19 May 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>2 Bottles of Beer per man at 4 PT = 1/-s bottle issue  Does the men a world of good &amp; keeps them from drinking local Koniac.<br/>Church parade as usual<br/>X Letter from Dolly including Mothers to Mrs Isherwood which the latter had sent Dolly. X<br/>Yes we will both walk in at the garden gate before many months now I hope.<br/>X My pen sometimes works &amp; other times I have to use pencil   Do hope you can read. X<br/>Painful trying to get men to sing at a Church Parade better cut Hymns out if no music -  X Stayed for communion-X</p>
