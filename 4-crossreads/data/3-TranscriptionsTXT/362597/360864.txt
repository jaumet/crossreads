 evening we had a mock trial of the Editor of our paper, a matter of libel. It was the best &amp; longest laugh I have had for months. Ellis &amp; Billy were witnesses for the plaintiff. The plaintiff gained a verdict of 1/4  damages &amp; costs. 
 I once said that I could go without meat. It is now over a week &amp; I have not had a taste of meat. 
 We are to have "High Jinks" tomorrow as we cross the line early in the morning. At 5 o/c this afternoon we were exactly half-way between Fremantle &amp; Suez. 
 I have physical drill &amp; stretcher drill every morning &amp; French lessons twice a day &amp; bandaging at night between 7 &amp; 8.
