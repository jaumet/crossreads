<p>Sunday 28 April 1918<br/>Arrived in London at 8.30 p.m. &amp; went to 3? Lon. Gen. Hosp.</p>
<p>Monday 29 April 1918<br/>Find good many of our Brigade are in the same ward.  Feel very crook after the journey over.</p>
<p>Tuesday 30 April 1918<br/>Ede came out to see me.  This ward I am in is long &amp; narrow &amp; very cold.  I sleep most of day &amp; night!!</p>
<p>Wednesday 1 May 1918<br/>Rup came up from Fovant Hospital to see me.  My eyes are still very groggy.</p>
<p>Thursday 2 May 1918<br/>The nurses here are not so careful as the V.A.Ds in France.  The Sister put the solution in my mouth instead of my eyes!!!</p>
<p>Friday 3 May 1918<br/>Feeling hungry.  Food here is not up to much.  One of the nurses bought me some cakes.</p>
<p>Saturday 4 May 1918<br/>My burns are getting better.  Half our Brigade seems to be gassed in this hospital.</p>
