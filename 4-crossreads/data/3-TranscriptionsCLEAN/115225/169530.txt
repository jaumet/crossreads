Feb. 9th  We spent a good part of the morning & afternoon with Suzanne. Lucienne came home from Neuve Eglise in the afternoon. Fritz has been shelling Neuve Eglise a bit lately, he chased a company of R. engineers of out of their camp there & also put a few into the 106th lines - I received my first lessons in the Pelman Course & we spent the evening comfortably reading & discussing the merrits of it.
 
Memo.  The weather for the past week has kept warm with wind from NW - W & SW winds & showers at intervals , atmosphere dull & damp - roads mostly drying - trees in good bud - crops coming up -
 
Feb.10th  We spent an hour up at Suzannes in the morning (Sunday)  In the afternoon we went for a walk & applied the first lesson of Pelmans. In the evening we studdied French & Pelmans -
