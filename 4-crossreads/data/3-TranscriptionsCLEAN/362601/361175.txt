
18 March, Tuesday
W.We have music & cards in the front Room. We are all very happy & don't want to go to bed. Ian H. Dring & make Supper. Another night until 'heart am.
19 March, Wednesday
W.We read about the Soldier Pacts. We have dinner very late & M & I play cards until tea. More cards after tea but I only score. A & M do not K---- & poor M is very lachrymose at night.
