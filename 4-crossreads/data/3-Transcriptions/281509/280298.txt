<p>1916 March 16<br/>Thursday<br/>We do nothing much all day. Tony &amp; self slip off to town in afternoon despite warning that no leave would be granted. Bob followed later.  We take tea at Mac's &amp; spend evening. Tony &mp; self take coffee at majestic earlier in evening. Dad &amp; T's fun.</p>
