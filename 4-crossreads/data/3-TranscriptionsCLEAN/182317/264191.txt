
I am writing to you sitting here in the ward room in the full blast of an electric fan and two portholes and yet I am bathed in perspiration. Truly this heat is of the hottest, the band is making sweet music without.It is doing very well and the young band signet is giving a good account of himself. He is most keen on his work both for the band and the stretcher bearing. The band will be easily the best band in the battalions when it comes to definite comparisons.The amount of exercise we take is small but steady. Arthur boxes with the Doc and blue with me. Unfortunately I sprained my thumb on his ribs at the beginning of our series of spars and have knocked it every time I have spared since. It is however getting better.
