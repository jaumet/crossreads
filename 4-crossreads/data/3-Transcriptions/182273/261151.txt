
<p>1918 &nbsp; August</p>
<p>1 THUR &nbsp;Fine day. one section</p>
<p>pulled out. relieved by&nbsp;</p>
<p>29<sup>th</sup> Division</p>
<p>Posted &#39;Blighty&#39; to May. quiet.</p>
<p>Bombs at night. very</p>
<p>close. Tom Magee arrived back.</p>
<p>2 FRI &nbsp;Raining hard all day.</p>
<p>remaining guns pulled out</p>
<p>Moved off 9 P.M. for Pont [Anspin / Auspin ?]</p>
<p>arr: midnight. rough accomodation</p>
<p>Rec. letter from Amy. Finished&nbsp;</p>
<p>4.30 am.</p>
<p>3 SAT &nbsp;Raining on &amp; off all day</p>
<p>Preparing to entrain tomorrow.</p>
<p>Walked to Comapgne at</p>
<p>night with J.W.C. &nbsp;JW &amp; WJC.</p>
<p>had a pleasant evening.</p>
<p>Rec. letter &amp; photo from</p>
<p>Amy. letters from Celie &amp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>J.M.S.</p>
