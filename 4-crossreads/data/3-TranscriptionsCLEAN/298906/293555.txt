Early breakfast this morning for the Duke of Connaught was going to review the troops on Salisbury Plain, every possible man was turned out, it was all over by 11.30, & in honor of the occasion the bugles blew "no parade today" this made the boys cheer.Had a big list of offences to be dealt with at the orderly Room this afternoon, & nearly all were these new draft of men, they want a bit of shaking up.There was a shocking number of men drunk last night
