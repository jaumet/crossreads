<p>Dec 3/<br/>Route march through Alexandria. The whole Brigade turned out .<br/>We rode in half sections &amp; made it look as if there were double the number.<br/>It was a ruse to let the niggers see that their was still plenty of troops left in Egypt. The niggers have been playing up with Turks urging them on</p>
<p>Dec 7/<br/>Left Mex camp at 11.30 pm My old horse Kaisa Bill gave me a good fly for freedom but I managed to sit him.<br/>We entrained at Gabarry Station for some unknown</p>
