<p>Steenvorde &amp; discovered the stoves had at last been exchanged for the Malthoid.  The work is now progressing &amp; I am taking possession in a few days.</p>
<p>Afternoon to Renescure, where the 7th Field Engineers are quartered &amp; there found my dearest old chum Azzie Webb.  What hand shakes &amp; heart throbs. Azzie looks very thin, but is still that same indefatigable &amp; admirable old character. Never have I met a more solid, capable and excellent fellow than Webb. Also met another friend from Wellington. I dined at Webb's mess with his coterie of enthusiastic officers who loudly applauded my mimic effects and the "fistaxophone Solo". Azzie had often spoken of me to them, so I was more or less an identity. The surrounding country is in glorious condition, the harvesting is nearly at an end, &amp; the golden sheaves are being built</p>
