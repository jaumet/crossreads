No showers on board except a "private Showers", he's a &ndash; poor  substitute. Rotten dinner. Greasy soup, rotten spuds &amp; 33rd class meat.<br/>3 Slept boat deck, rather breezy. Have to hang on to blankets. Sighted land 6 am on port side. Appears barren &amp; rocky. Fair dinkum this is a cow of a ship. Chatty all over, rotten accommodation, crook tucker - But then this is a British ship not an Australian Ship. The English Tommies call us Andy Fishers 6 bob a day Tourist Army &ndash; Dinner time Tucker rotten. Tied meat</p>
