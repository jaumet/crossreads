
<p>954</p>
<p>FRIDAY, JANUARY 3, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, &amp; very cold. Went down</p>
<p>&amp; fixed up with Sir Charles Wade</p>
<p>about getting back &amp; then to</p>
<p>the Bank &amp; sent a cable.</p>
<p>Knocking round town the</p>
<p>rest of the day. All had dinner at</p>
<p>the Pic at night, &amp; we had a</p>
<p>bit of a party at the flat at</p>
<p>night.</p>
