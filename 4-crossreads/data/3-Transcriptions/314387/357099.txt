<p>Sunday 19th<br/>Left Pernos ten o'clock arrived near aerodrome two very hot went to Bertangles<br/>Monday 20th<br/>Continued march through Cardonette. Allonville arrived Buzzy about two bombed &amp; night. Tuesday 21st<br/>Left Buzzy in morning arrived &amp; House line stayed till six moved on and joined unit that night in front line<br/>Wednesday 22nd<br/>In front line</p>
