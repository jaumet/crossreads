<p>to night it has been raining off &amp; on all day  Co Orderly Room</p>
<p>29/6/16<br/>Hell of a stunt on our right last night our  -    arrived safe &amp; sound.  thing rather warm all along the line.  shells fell here like hells. Aeroplanes out 11 all told our [indecipherable] &amp; fly over to the Boch line. plenty of shots fired at them  Rain again.  we are at.</p>
<p>30/6/16<br/>6 Batt or 7<br/>I ration party last night rough passage rain again heavy firing all along the line  there must be something doing somewhere close because we got a good reception here. C Co getting a rough time today. we got a good share late this afternoon.  [indecipherable] beautes into the bargain. no casualties but it demoralises the men needless to say I am shakey [indecipherable] it is a rotten place the trenches are up to putty but I think I can see through the whole affair ?</p>
<p>1/7/16<br/>Hellish bombardment last night all along the line things quietened about 9 A.M. both sides  we got a good shelling by the enemy this evening about 6 oclock our aeroplanes were over enemy line</p>
