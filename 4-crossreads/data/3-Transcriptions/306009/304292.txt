<p>1915<br/>Apr. 16 Fri.<br/>Plenty of work again to-day.<br/>Have been sleeping for the past few nights in a room that used to be part of B  Block Ward but as it is used as Sisters' quarters have had to move. To-night up into A Block where I spent the night.</p>
