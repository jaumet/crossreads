  
 This evening I am about to attempt the answering of "twenty-seven" letters which came to hand yesterday. 
 Oct.21st A "heavy" day - Dress Rehearsal - Preparation of stage & properties - Booking - In the evening "Saa - eeda Digger!" (A Romance of the East) is played for the first time to a very small audience - I am neither satisfied or disappointed _ I must wait & see. - Our manager (?)
