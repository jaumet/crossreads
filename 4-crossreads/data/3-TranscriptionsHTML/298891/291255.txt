<p>1917 June</p>
<p>21 Thursday</p>
<p>Feeling fine. Typed in room. Phil [indecipherable] came bringing strawberries &amp; we had tea together in my room. Colder day. Showers. Got two telegrams from Edinburgh hurrying me about papers. Slept very tired for 3 hours in afternoon.</p>
