<p>Aug 1917<br/>the water, &amp; just baby wavelets. We are not allowed bellow in the day time now except for meals. It would be more dangerous if we were torpeoded</p>
<p>Monday 20th<br/>In spite of all the talk we got 8/- today I payed the Mess Orderlies in full 7/- &amp; 1/- for 2 copies of the Beltana Bugle just cut it out. They say a Cruser passed early this morning &amp; a steam boat passed a long way off this evening. Sports on deck. The sea is calmer <strike>than</strike> today than I thought possible</p>
