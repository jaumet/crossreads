 1917 Saturday 31 March 
 Put up bell tents this morning &amp; shift into them. Jock Heylin leaves for 2nd A.G.H. tomorrow morning.  He takes Underwood &amp; self down village &amp; treats us to --- Walk on to Ville.  Back to camp &amp; bed. Put in applic. for exchange to 2nd A.G.H. Field work's got too strenuous for me.
