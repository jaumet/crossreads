<p>I have not heard nor seen Charlie for some time now, trust he is alrioght.</p>
<p>Sister Kelly got the Military Medal, only one to the Sisters   &amp;  one orderly got it. (Pete Thomas). We were all pleased Kelly got it but think it should be Military Cross.</p>
<p>I bought her the ribbon yesterday   &amp;  she was delighted but wished I had got it too. Anyhow the other sisters deserved it just as much,   &amp;  I think Annie Bell should have got it too.</p>
