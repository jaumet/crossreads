<p>Apl 6<sup><u>th</u></sup> I had to act as escort for Sid on his charge, he asked for a court martial &amp; was remanded (asking for a court martial was only bluff &amp; remanding was also bluff)&nbsp;&nbsp;Frank went out on Bud&#39;s lorry - we got the gear box finished &amp; are mobile if wanted &nbsp;it was fine in the morning but rained all the evening -</p>
<p><u>Mem</u>o. &nbsp;With the arrival of our 4<sup><u>th</u></sup> Division on the Somme the Germans seem to have brought to a halt&nbsp;at Albert, it seems that we &amp; the French will make a combined counter offensive all along the line -</p>
<p>Apl 7<sup><u>th</u></sup> &nbsp;We <s>drew</s>&nbsp;went out on supplies for the brigade as they are moving down South. We drew at Steenwerck &amp; hauled to Strazeele &amp; delivered to the individual batteriies - We parked in Strazeele for the night - fine day -</p>
<p>Apl 8<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp; We started up at 10.30 A.M. &amp; drew at Steenwerck &nbsp;We made two trips as the brigade are entraining to-night.</p>
