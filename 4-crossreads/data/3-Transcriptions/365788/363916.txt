<p>6<br/>the second deck &amp; there are places or rather hooks on which to sling hammocks but think I will sleep on deck.<br/>24 Mch 16 10.30 A.M.<br/>Slept very well on deck last night<br/>Have just finished cleaning up the Knives spoons &amp; forks, tin plates pannikins &amp; tin ware &ndash; most of which were in a filthy condition.</p>
