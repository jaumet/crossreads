<p>[Transcriber's note:  Sapper Vasco of the Field Company Engineers joined the Army on 11 May 1916 and embarked on the Suevic on 11 November 1916.  He was a Caricature Artist and Draftsman and there are many of his drawings in the following letters to his wife from England and from France.  He died of illness on 3 August 1918 and is buried in Hatfield Cemetery, St. Albans, United Kingdom.]</p>
<p>Australian Red Cross</p>
<p>"This side of the fence is out of bounds to all ranks and patients."</p>
<p>"A study in Blues"</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>"Vasco on the Rocks"<br/>47th General Hospital<br/>France<br/>June 20th 1918</p>
