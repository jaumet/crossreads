<p>Yesterday Fritz increased his shelling a great deal, rain notwithstanding, &amp; before long was dropping grass cutters all round &amp; in the camp. Luckily nobody was hit, but one exploded right on the side of Tom Pollack's dug-out, while he &amp; Roberts were lying in it. Some dropped round our dug out also, &amp; Ted Purchase (Staff Serg on Gallipoli), heard pieces dropping on the roof. He, being on night duty was sleeping there.</p>
<p>Just at dusk Fritz put up a barrage on a position a couple of hundred yards from our camp.</p>
