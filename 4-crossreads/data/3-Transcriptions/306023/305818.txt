<p>Monday 27th.<br/>Up fairly early and went back to the Butchers. My coming had so relieved them that they had all had a fine sleep &ndash; the first to wake up got up at 8.30 am, which shows that it had eased their worries a great deal.</p>
<p>With Mr Butcher I went up to London again and had a stroll around. We didn't see much that was new. it was interesting to stand outside St Paul's and see the pigeons perched on people's shoulders and arms eating breadcrumbs; it was a very pretty sight.</p>
