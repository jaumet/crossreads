<p>1915<br/>Jan. 23 Sat.<br/>Arose at 8 p.m.(special concession).<br/>Lieut. Col. Nash's clerk all day at Mena House until about 10 p.m.<br/>Men very busy getting camp hospital ready for patients to be brought in tomorrow by the 1st  2nd  &amp; 3rd Field Ambulances.<br/>Accomodation available for about 300 patients.<br/>Convalescents &amp; non-serious cases to be brought here while bad cases &amp; operations at Mena House where there is room for 120 patients.</p>
