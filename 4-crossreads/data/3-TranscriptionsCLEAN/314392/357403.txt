  
 To move about the yards of the station till about 4pm when the Powers that be decided we should move to Cimino about 4 miles distant round the harbour. Here we detrained at a Tommy rest camp. It appears no arrangements had been made to receive us and consequently there was nothing for us to eat except in a few isolated cases where men had saved a little of their rations from the train. But who ever heard of these things that are supposed to govern men being able to do anything properly or on time/
