<p>Thursday 20&nbsp;September 1917.</p>
<p>An uneventful day with the exception of 2 Taubes over and a row I had with the Orderly Room people on account of being crook. They tried to make out that I was malingeering. Thats is all the thanks I got for carrying out my duties satisfactorily.</p>
