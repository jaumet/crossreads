<p>1915<br/>May. 27 Thu.<br/>About our warmest day to-day so far &ndash; everyone getting into thinnest possible togs.<br/>At 6 p.m. admitted another 60 patients transferred from Alexandria.<br/>Work in the Administration office progressing smoothly with the 2 of us at it.</p>
