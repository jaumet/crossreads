 They leave Nubia for Egypt for employment, but only live for the time when they will return home with the money they have acquired, which will enable them to purchase a little land &amp; a couple of wives to cultivate it &amp; then they can settle down in comfort for the rest of their lives. The negro class are mostly slaves &amp; employed in the most menial offices.  Some however who are keepers of the harems of wealthy Arabs are very intelligent &amp; trustworthy.
