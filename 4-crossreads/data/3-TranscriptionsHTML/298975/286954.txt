<p>Sept 1917<br/>Saturday 8th<br/>Was put on the Sanitary staff today with 2 others Started digging a pit for rubbish Rambled down the country lanes in the afternoon &amp; bought about 18 very nice plums for 6d</p>
<p>Sunday 9th<br/>Rained gently most of the day Went for a walk in the evening</p>
<p>Monday 10th<br/>A very fine warm day. On the sanitary job all day.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11th<br/>Quite hot today Things as usual</p>
<p>Wednesday 12th<br/>Gave up the Sanitary Job &amp; went on pariade. Went through Miniture Range Score 38 Went over to next camp</p>
