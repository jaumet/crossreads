
June, 1915Saturday 26Letters from Laura & Jennie [indecipherable] from England today - former says all is well - Feast Fast days commence for a month for the native population they eat at night only now, & then in a month have a great spree.Wounded continue to come in, 2 more of my men (I mean 1st Reinf) I found last night in No.1 A.G. Hospital. Send with this some snapshots of our men at Gallipoli (& also one of myself & V.O. 1st L.H.)
