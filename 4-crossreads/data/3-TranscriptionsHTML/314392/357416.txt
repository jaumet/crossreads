<p>Be aboard the boat by Monday- very hot and dusty.<br/>Sunday20th left Camp for H.M.S Port Sydney and embarked about 4.40pm we pulled outside the breakwater and at 9pm headed for the Red Sea.Hurrah- we are on our way at last.<br/>21st Very hot. Progress is very slow but still we are on our way and the lord is good.<br/>23rd Ran into miniature cyclone which made the old Port Sydney look sick for a while.<br/>Nov.3 arrived in Colombo and was granted leave from 10am till 11pm. Had a gay time with several English tea planters. Our</p>
