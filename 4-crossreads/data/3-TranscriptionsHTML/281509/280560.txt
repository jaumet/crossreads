<p>1916 December 3<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>To work as usual  Nothing doing up line , not many cases.  We have our marching orders for up the line tomorrow. Fix up a couple of small parcels of souvenirs one for Cis &amp; Mary other for May &amp; Eva.  Heard Frank Cartwright got transferred to No 1 A.G.H.</p>
