<p>20.2.15</p>
<p>Sat 20th<br/>No parade to-day &amp; put the morning in cleaning saddles rifles etc.  Fresh remounts arrived to-day to fill the vacancies cause by horses lost on the journey coming from Australia;  doing nothing but writing this afternoon;  cannot afford to go to Cairo before next pay day &amp; am not anxious to go in, as I have seen all I want to of the city; The Kursaal being the only attraction for me;<br/>Col. Merrington paid all the tents a visit in C. Sqd this morning to have a chat with the Presbyterian lads he is a fine fellow &amp; well</p>
