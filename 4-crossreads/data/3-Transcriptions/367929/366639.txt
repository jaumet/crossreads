<p>June 1915<br/>16 Wed<br/>In valley bivouac.<br/>Capt Lawries &ndash; A Sqn went on to right of Popes Hill in lieu of a squ of 2nd LH. While 2nd relieved 3rd LH.<br/>SS Sicilin left Anzac. Shelling beach.</p>
<p>1 Thursday<br/>In valley bivouac.<br/>Shelling beach again some swimmers being nearly caught.</p>
