 Thursday 20 February 1915 Col. Fulton told us the following that Colombo was now closed to all troops as the Infantry had stolen or broken 3000/-/- worth of jewellery, English in fact all country Sailors are on strike &amp; it is indeffinite when we go home, but I will cable. It is not known for certain whether the Troopship will go strait to Sydney or if we disembark in Melbourne &amp; go by Train &ndash; No boat for Dolly yet.  I hope to be in Melbourne first to meet her &amp; so save anyone else the trouble &amp; expense.
