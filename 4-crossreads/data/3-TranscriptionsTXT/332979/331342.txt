Friday 13/4/17.As I was Orderly Corporal for the Day I was kept busy with the work attached to that position. I had to parade the Sick to the Doctor also march the Mess Orderlies to the Cook House at the Meal times. I had to stop in the Orderly Room after Tea until Bed time.
