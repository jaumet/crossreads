
August 1917
Thursday 2ndThe heads still have the wind up over the shelling so we are receiving no patientsFriday 3rdAnother board today I got [indecipherable] again some talk of shifting the HospitalSaturday 4thStill hanging on here waiting for Fritz to shell againSunday 5thPay day & the usual birthdaysMonday 6thSome talk of stopping here as Fritz as not shelled againTuesday 7thMore rain got a good dosing.  Met some Aus. this eveningWednesday 8thFritz gave us a good sally up with bombs all night
