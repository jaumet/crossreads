<p>Spt 3  Both wind &amp; sea are behind us now, &amp; we're travelling well. Its great sport standing on the Port Saloon deck waiting for the big waves, &amp; then ducking for cover. Just spotted a Cape pigeon; it's a devil of a long way out for a small bird (900 miles)<br/>Church Mafeesh, so have to be satisfied with a cigar &amp; biscuits.</p>
<p>4 Believe one of the propeller shafts is smashed, but luckily we are going with the sea &amp; wind. Rec'd the usual <u>fag</u> issue of 2 packets.<br/>Everywhere &ndash; foam crested</p>
