<p>Saturday 22 March 1919<br/>Very hot.<br/>Sent Dolly a wireless to say alls well &amp; that the S.S. Ulimaroa would be in Colombo on Tuesday &amp; received a reply in an hour's time saying all was well &amp; that the Derbyshire would also reach Colombo that day, her ship is about 190 miles behind.</p>
