<p>Sunday 26 November 1916<br/>Rifle inspection then on fatigue at Brigade putting up tents, etc.<br/>In afternoon got pair of clippers and T. Moffit clipped my horse.<br/>On telephone from 2 a.m. till 6.<br/>Tommies let off flare at 2-30.  I thought Turks were coming.</p>
<p>Monday 27 November 1916<br/>Burns removed shoes on my mare.<br/>Harry Jackob and I practiced signalling with buzzer.<br/>Wrote to Father (26).<br/>On telephone from 6 p.m. till 10.</p>
