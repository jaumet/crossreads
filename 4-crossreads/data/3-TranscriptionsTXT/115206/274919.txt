
December  1918.Tuesday 17
At Sea. H.S."Karoola."Weather fine and clear averaged 14.2 miles and for 24 hours had travelled 335 miles.Played Auction Bridge and one.Met Bill Hart and Don Cameron.Passed through the Straights of Gibraltar at 8 pm.The town and rock were a blaze of light as also was Algiers.Wrote to Ken Gunn. Climbed up to the yard arm of the mainmast.
