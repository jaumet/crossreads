 17 Sept 17 At sea doing fleet manoeuvres. It is a grand sight to see so many fast ships steaming at sea, with the airships overhead &amp; also sea planes. Altogether there must be a hundred ships out. 
 18 Sept 17 Arrived in Rosyth &amp; anchored at 4 am, &amp; coaled ship. 
 19 Sept 17 In Rosyth. Weather fine. 
 20th to 30th Sept Anchored in Rosyth. Dublin in dock. 
 1 Oct 17 Steamed down the Firth &amp; did sub-calibre firing, returning to anchor at 2.30 pm. 
 2 Oct 17 In Rosyth. Weather very cold. 
 3rd Oct 17 Anchored in Rosyth.
