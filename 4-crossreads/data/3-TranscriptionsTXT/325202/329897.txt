
This has been the mildest February for Europe that I have ever remembered.
Horwood has left us for a furlough to Australia The first man to go from D.H.Q. We are all hoping that it is the commencement of the promised furlough to Australia for the original men.
March 6 La Clyttewe pulled in here on March 2 - (it is a sector of the Ypres front) it was one of the bitterest days I have spent on the road & glad it was only some 16 kilos journey.
