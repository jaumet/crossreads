<p>Saturday 20th April<br/>Quite a bussy day went to the Church to the christening of Christopher Powel &ndash; Cotton came back Had cake &amp; champain &amp; planted a tree. Evening the Major showed us some of his stuffed beasts &amp; later lantern slides of one of his African trips giving a lecture each time all very interesting</p>
<p>Monday 22nd April<br/>A very fine day wheeled one of the lads about the grounds for a time</p>
