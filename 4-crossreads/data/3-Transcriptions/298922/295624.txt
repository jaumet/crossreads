<p>Wednesday 21st November 1917</p>
<p>Anchored off Portland or Weymouth since early morning &ndash; the same spot the HOS: ship S.S. "LETITIA: anchored 1 year ago &ndash; she is now down in Halifax - [indecipherable]. We have passengers for Mesopotamia, Bombay, China, Colombo &amp; Port Said &ndash; 7 women all told mostly nurses.</p>
<p>Got a Paper today &amp; I see our men are close to Jerusalem.</p>
<p><strike>Well I'm out of it for the present anyway.</strike></p>
