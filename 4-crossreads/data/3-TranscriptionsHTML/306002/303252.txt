<p>21.8.15<br/>Sat. 21st.  I believe the 17th, 18th &amp; 19th &amp; 20th battalions of Infantry was landed yesterday afternoon &amp; last night some were landed here on the main beach &amp; some on the left.  We went on duty on the 'phone at 12 o'clock today &amp; soon found things moving some, our rations were given us at 2 o'clock instead of 5 as usual &amp; at 3 oclock the warboats &amp; artillery opened up a very heavy fire on the left flank.  I walked to the top of Walkers Ridge &amp; had a grand view of the fighting in the low country below us, but there was very little one could see only clouds of dust &amp; smoke &amp; the flash as the shells exploded, the bombardment was terrific.
