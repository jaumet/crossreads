An argument in the tent probably defers departure till about 6 or 6.30, &amp; then one drinks coffee at the favourite place already described; chats to madamoiselle if she can tear herself away from fiance, until about 8, when one walks home arguing some ethical point.<br/>To night the four of us came in by motor bus (one of the London ones but submerged under a grey exterior but <strike>strik</strike> strikingly familiar), &amp; thereby gained great amusement by facetiously enquiring of the driver whether he stopped at Trafalgar square.</p>
