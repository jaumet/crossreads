 13 September, Saturday 
 Dinner at Esplanade Caf&eacute;. Go to Sports which are great. Aeroplane &amp; motor stunts. Go to Ellis' with crowd for Tea &amp; meet Billy. Meet Amy Bert &amp; Myra Brown. Go to Pictures see "Woman". 
 Recd letter from Lodge &amp; Doris. 
 14 September, Sunday 
 Go to Dee Why. Fix Linos &amp; clean up outside. An awful windy day &amp; it is a terrible drive Home. Sit &amp; talk after chopping wood &amp; cleaning up etc. Go to bed after a bath. 
 Wrote Doris.
