  
 1915 July 12 Mon. Went into Cairo in the afternoon & made some purchases - walked back to Gezira through Kasr-el-Nil. Mena House is to be opened up as a Convalescent Depot to-morrow & to-day 3 Medical Officers, 5 Nurses, a dozen orderlies & a clerk were detailed from our Unit to staff the place - Major Grey in charge (N.B. Not one seems to be terribly anxious to get back to Mena).
