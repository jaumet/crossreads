 May 1918 Saturday 25 
 Battery W.L. Fritz put a lucky one into some Labor Corps and accounted for 35 killed, 60 casualties altogether. Weather still dull but an improvement on yesterday. Knipe quite well today. Received a mag from Eileen. Wrote Mother and E.  Commenced a recreation dug out under surveillance of Y.M.C.A. representative.  Cleaned two saddles and received a visit from Andrews, also Tich Rice. Worked on dug out.
