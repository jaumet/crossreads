July 1917Sat 21stPay day so we are financial again.Sunday 22ndReceived a lot of cases today otherwise nothing happened to brake this monotony.Monday 23Our friend disturbed the peace again with the aeroplanes &amp; of course our guns opened up savagely so that ended sleep for a timeTuesday 4th&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; [this should be 24th.]Fine weather again we received more cases again.Wednesday 25Another visit from Fritz he dropped a few bombs but did no damage.Thursday 26thStill keeping fine &amp; nothing much doing
