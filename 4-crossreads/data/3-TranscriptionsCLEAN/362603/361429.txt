We saw all that was to be seen & returned for afternoon Tea.  We then sat on the Pier & talked to a Jewish Lady & her Husband for an Hour till the Ferry came in.  We arrived Home at 6.30 & then went to the Theatre to see a play Diana of Dobson's.  Next morning we did nothing & Jennie came to see us off by the 12.5 midday Train.
