<p>trust that it is largely the effect of a cold which I have suffered from the last few days &amp; also the aftermath of Picket last night.<br/>I am going out now to watch the moon rise.  It is a wonderful sight some times on the desert.<br/>When it rises &amp; shines coldly down on this apparently dead world of giant sandhills &amp; gullies of fantastic shapes one thinks in terms of H.G. Wells.</p>
<p>Dec. 16th<br/>Hostile 'planes over every day except the 13th when they took a day off.<br/>All three regiments have been treated to a salvo of three bombs each so far;  but, as yet, thank goodness, none have come near enough to us to justify our doing the ostrich act.
