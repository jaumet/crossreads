<p>March 1918<br/>Saturday 23</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Weather beautiful and warm.<br/>Had a swim in mine crater &ndash; small one, quite close to our home.<br/>Smithy trotted into Dranoutie with Laurence. <br/>Went for a stroll during afternoon with Ken and visited canteen, also wireless Boe's.<br/>Very little shelling all day ie. From Fritz, our hows and 60s did some splendid work.</p>
<p>We had a little snipe at a few Fritz officers.<br/>All leave cancelled also. All schools &ndash; looks like a stunt</p>
