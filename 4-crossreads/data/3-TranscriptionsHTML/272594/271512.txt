<p>trenches with 2 men &amp; myself the portion of trench I was in was mostly 5th Bde &amp; they had the wind up properly as they had lost heavily as the numerous dead lying about showed all too plainly, when we were taking over old Fritz made a counter attack but he was beaten off. I'm hanged if I know where were until day broke for shells seemed to be coming from all sides &amp; flares were going up practically all round us it seemed very queer.</p>
