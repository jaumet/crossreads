<p>2<br/>15.7.16.</p>
<p>Am glad you liked the photo<strike>s</strike> I sent you. Since writing the first half we have moved again &amp; are near ----- further south but we will I think march another 25 miles or so in a day or two. We left Belgium marched about 6 miles &amp; entrained at ----- at about 1 P.M. went towards the South &amp; arrived at ----- at about 6 P.M. then started to march for  the first place that we put up for a rest arriving about 3 A.M. (some 15 miles or more with one blanket &amp; our packs on our shoulders) very feeble - From here we set out at about 12 noon &amp; "foot-sacked" another 10 miles arriving at a Country Chateau at about 4 P.M. The scenery on the whole road being very lovely. &ndash; Next day we left at about 3 P.M. marched some 10 miles to this village. &ndash; The Poppies (had) &amp; corn Flowers &ndash; Daisies &amp; some yellow &amp; Heliotrope flowers looked lovely in the fields. The owner of the Chateau was (we are told) Governor of the French Congo &amp; is now</p>
