Wednesday 6.3.18 to Saturday 6.4.18
Very quiet peaceful month. Drafted into Quartermasters Store of Group Clearing Hospital day after returning from leave have now a fairly comfortable position. Have sole control of messing rations in connection with hospital.
During the month have spent a considerable amount of my spare time in at the YMCA at Warminster with Frank Grose have had a fairly good time. Billiards
