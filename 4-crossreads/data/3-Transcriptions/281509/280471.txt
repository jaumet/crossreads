<p>5 September 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Nothing doing just dawdle round pretending to carpenter. Over to Y M.CA for morning tea. after tea C &amp; Self go over to C of E service rain only a nurse &amp; parson beside ourselves present. Short. Yarn to parson. Canadian  Fysh's feet. Pong.</p>
