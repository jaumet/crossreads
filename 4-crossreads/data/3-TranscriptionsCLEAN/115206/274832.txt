
September 1918.Thursday 19
Rgt of Bernes. Bty Position.1 am sitting [indecipherable] the rain for five hours, drenched to skin, and blankets in a similar condition. All of Br in the same pickle. Moved to a new position i.e. our spooks dugout. Weather still about the same . Had a letter from Ken Gunn who has had is Rgt leg amputated at the knee and I'm very sorry hear the news.Nicely settled in new dugout which we have cleaned up. Talbot proceeded on leave and dropped his dixies.
