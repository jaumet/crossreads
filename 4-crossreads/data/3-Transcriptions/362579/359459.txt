<p>No 2 Aust. Aux. Hosp.<br/>Southall.<br/>Middlesex.<br/>England.<br/>26.12.17</p>
<p>My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>It is a very long time since I missed writing to you on a Saturday or Sunday but this time I could not manage it.</p>
<p>We were very busy preparing for Xmas   &amp;  I seemed to have so many changes from one ward to another that I was really too tired to write at night, but now the eventful  day is over   &amp;  things have settled down again.</p>
<p>Well, the patients had a very happy time. Plenty of good things to eat   &amp;  drink. The wards were all decorated beautifully. The Red Cross gave each patient a box with cigarettes, tobacco</p>
