
F [4/11/17 Sun] I spend all afternoon with my Washing. Dream of M.R.L. and all the DobsonsWrote M.R.L.
F [5/11/17 Mon] Canteen Corporal. Spent time reading & tidying up Room. Darn all my socks in afternoon. Dram that I natter with Mrs Stephen [Indecipherable] drowning
F [6/11/17 Tues] Catch 8.45 Train. Leave Coat at Hotel and Go to Court. Fined 2/6. Poachers & Scorchers in Jail too. Go to Close. Ride to Downton. C very upset. We play Darts but things are [Indecipherable]. Sleep at Y.M.C.A.
F [7/11/17 Wed] Catch 7.45 train to Fovant. Cycle to H'cott. Reach Salisbury at 12.45pm. Meet Bob. Cowan at Hurdcott. Go to Mrs E. Walk to Sarum with Kiddie & 3 ladies. Meet Mich. P go to Search Me Bring home the Bike.Could not sleep.
W [8/11/17 Thurs] Am Ord. Corp. but do not get up for 7 parade. Recd. Parcel from Lotte. letter & Photos from Jess about Alan's death. letter from George & Esme & Paper from Ame. Letter from M.R.L. & Roy.Wrote to M.R.L.
W&F [9/11/17 Fri] Recd. letters from Nette. Bess, Gran. Home (6) Marg. [Indecipherable]. Tidied up Room again.Wrote Aunty. Annie.
F [10/11/17 Sat] Don't go to S'bury. Play footy & get horribly Knocked about. Get my pants all ripped about in front of the Wac-acks. Ellis comes Home. Very sore & tired.Wrote to M.R.L.
