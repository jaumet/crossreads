  
 10 August, Sunday 
 Chop wood in morning. Geo & I go to Narrabeen see Livo. Come Home & stay. Moonlight & I think of Island. I play piano until Dad & Mum go to bed. Mum is melancholy. 
 M 19 
 11 August, Monday 
 Mum & I go to Manly shopping. Visit Mrs Stephen, Dobbies & Whiteheads & Creightons. We buy Ellis's presents & soda syphon. 
 Wrote to Mr Paul.
