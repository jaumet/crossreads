<p>Thursday 1 February</p>
<p>Not doing anything in particular today.  One section is required to assist back at Bellevue Farm Rest Camp, just a few hundred yards this side of Albert.  Our unit is taking the place over.  "A" section are therefore to go back to this Rest Camp in the morning.</p>
