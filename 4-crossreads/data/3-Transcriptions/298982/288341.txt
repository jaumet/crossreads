<p>Sun 22 Bathe noon below Snipers Peak Mac T unwell &amp; off duty. Jock came to tea.</p>
<p>Mon 23rd Fatigue of Leinsters at 8 am took down rifles &amp; ammtn &amp; equipment from Popes Cross roads. Turks snipers been busy &ndash; caught two. Had burn at incinerator in morn.</p>
