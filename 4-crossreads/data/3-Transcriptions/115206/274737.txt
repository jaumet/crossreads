<p>June 1918<br/>Sunday 16</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles<br/>Very peculiar weather prevails at present. Our <br/>experience here today was quite a typical tropical one.<br/>Did usual shift on phone. 6-8 &ndash; and then<br/>noon, thoroughly cleaned my saddle etc.<br/>During afternoon Battery sports was held, my sub carried<br/>the highest points for the day, winning the tug-o-war on<br/>foot and horseback. I was in both teams. <br/>They also won the Rescue race, high jump, and Bending<br/>race. Received a few letters one from Home.</p>
