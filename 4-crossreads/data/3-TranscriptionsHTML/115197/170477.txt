<p>Charleroi - a journey which knows no joys.&nbsp; Night on the train.</p>
<p><u>Feb. 19th</u></p>
<p>Pass through Bailleul.&nbsp; Steenwench.&nbsp; Armentieres.&nbsp; Lille.&nbsp; Tournai &amp; Charleroi.&nbsp; Walk with pack from Charleroi to Beaumont - a distance of 23 kilo-metres (15 miles).</p>
<p>Billet for night at Beaumont.</p>
<p><u>Feb. 20th</u></p>
<p>Set out from Beaumont and arrive at Jeumont.</p>
