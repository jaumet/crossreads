<p>1915<br/>Oct. 30 Sat.<br/>Getting in some conversation in French now so should make better progress.<br/>To-day is "Our Day" in Egypt when the British Community are doing their best for the Red Cross Society &amp; Order of St. John.<br/>My weight in uniform is 10 st. 6&frac12; lbs.</p>
