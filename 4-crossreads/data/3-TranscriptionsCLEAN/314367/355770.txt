  
 Your latest is Feb 14 - you'd got my wire - All seems well with you - I should think if your Mother just goes slow she will be all right - A glass of Port wine might help her - We are going alongside the wharf at Garden Isld - tomorrow at 9 am - The 3 Destroyers are here - Transports are all gone & the whole aspect of the place different to what it was in November - Well I'll end my Diary - I have had a very good trip - seen & done a great deal & got a good deal of fresh experience - Last word - We pay off - All guns are to be put ashore - This means a week's work. I'm ashore - Going to see Hedley - & take him & some Mollusca Finns collected at the Matusi hot springs -
