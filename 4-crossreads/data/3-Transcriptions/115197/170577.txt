<p><u>April 5th</u></p>
<p>Arrive Southampton 1 a.m. - Night passed on deck owing to the overcrowding of sleeping apartments down below.</p>
<p>Anchored in Southampton Harbour till 6 a.m. when we steam into the wharf and there immediately disembark.&nbsp; A free breakfast and a train ration.&nbsp; Entrain and leave Southampton at 10 a.m.</p>
<p>The pleasant land of England in Spring -</p>
