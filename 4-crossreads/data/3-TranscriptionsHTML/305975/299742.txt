<p>Sunday Dec. 1.12.18.<br/>am feeling well rested all day  nothing he[re] to see  wrote to maud Josie Bell Dorrie alma Mick  went down town to night  nothing doing turned in 9.P.M.<br/>Monday Dec. 2.12.18.<br/>am feeling o.k.  nice warm day  nothing doing  resting all day.<br/>Tuesday Dec 3.12.18.<br/>feeling well  had a look around  very cold to day  went to Pictures  turned in 9.30.</p>
