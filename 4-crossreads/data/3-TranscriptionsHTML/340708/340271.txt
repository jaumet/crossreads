<p>July 1917<br/>Sat 21st<br/>Pay day so we are financial again.<br/>Sunday 22nd<br/>Received a lot of cases today otherwise nothing happened to brake this monotony.<br/>Monday 23<br/>Our friend disturbed the peace again with the aeroplanes &amp; of course our guns opened up savagely so that ended sleep for a time<br/>Tuesday 4th&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; [this should be 24th.]<br/>Fine weather again we received more cases again.<br/>Wednesday 25<br/>Another visit from Fritz he dropped a few bombs but did no damage.<br/>Thursday 26th<br/>Still keeping fine &amp; nothing much doing</p>
