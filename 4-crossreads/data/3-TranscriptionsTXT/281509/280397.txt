
23 June 1916Friday
Dispensary again. Taken off all parades form henceforth. & put on permanent staff.Nettleton & Cartwright endeavour to get into Aerial Corps. prospects good.Talk of former patients of Harve hosp. 4,000 men. 200 officers. 4 parsons. Man & his sister.Camp hospital started at Ditails Camp. Tony, Bob & I chosen by Staff Smith as orderlies.
