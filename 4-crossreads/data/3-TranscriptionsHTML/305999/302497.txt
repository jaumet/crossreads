<p>9/9/15<br/>at 10 PM we went down to the beach and embarked on trawlers and went to lemnos ireland again   it was our first spell and we where very glad of it   a lot of men went to the Hospitalls  it was quite a treat to be able to by a few things again such as fruit</p>
<p>11/11/15<br/>we where mustared up in reviue order and inspected by Lord</p>
