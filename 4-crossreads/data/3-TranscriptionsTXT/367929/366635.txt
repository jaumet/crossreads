 June 1915 8 Tuesday On Popes Hill. Went to see G. Randell on Sicilia. Had lunch. Found the Cpl &ndash; R Skone &ndash; a cousin. Very pleasant and clean on boat. Came back with Gen.Godley. 
 9 Wed Relieved at 9 am by 3rd Regt LH. Retired to Valley on West of Popes and organized bivouac areas etc. Water arrived from ships and is stored in tanks. No casualties in Monash Valley owing to enemy snipers marked by our own. No casualties.
