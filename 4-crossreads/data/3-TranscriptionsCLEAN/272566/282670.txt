  
 Tuesday 9 October 
 To "K" Supply Column & 5th D.S.C. Workshops To A.D.M.S. Scottish Lines Busseboom To Do. and thence to all the Battalions of the 8th Brigade up near Ypres. Coming home tonight I met with a slight accident due to a loaded Ambulance Car stopping in front of me.  Resulted in smashing of the front wheel of my bike.
