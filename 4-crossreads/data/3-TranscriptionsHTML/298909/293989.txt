<p>4/6/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Very hot &ndash; about <u>110</u> in the coolest tent, the wind blowing about 30 miles an hour &amp; the dust would blind you &ndash; Two fall ins &ndash; done a little fatigue &amp; tried to keep cool the rest of the day. No church parade.</p>
<p>5/6/16  Monday</p>
<p>Very hot &ndash; even <strike>hoter</strike> hotter than yesterday &ndash; hot winds &amp; sand storm still raging &ndash; hot enough outside, but the bell tents are just like ovens &ndash; Had some heavy drill under Capt Coppleson &ndash; also some lectures &amp; drill on loading a new style of Ambulance wagons &ndash; Went for a march over to the best water offering &ndash; which is back water from the Canal &ndash; it was a compulsory swim &amp; all the division were there, it was a great sight &amp; just man everywhere.</p>
<p>6/6/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Very hot &ndash; Sand storm not quite so wild. Went for a swim after the morning drill. Had some heavy &amp; swift drill, our Major says he wants us to make a name for ourselves in France.</p>
