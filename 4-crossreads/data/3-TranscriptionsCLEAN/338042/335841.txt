  
 April 1918 April 1st Rehearsal at the Olympia Theatre, where we are to show for a week - We are wonderfully well advertised - (The long poster) - American Y.M.C.A. and its system - Both houses receive the Anzac Coves with the utmost enthusiasm & appreciation - During the evening we play (2 shows) to 9.000 people April 2nd Reading - Writing and a walk around town - Two shows.
