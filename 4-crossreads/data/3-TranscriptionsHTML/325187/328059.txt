<p>[Sunday 12 November 1916]</p>
<p>Staying here for 2 more days so am sending this as Registered letter let me know if these notes arrive.<br/>Have been made a member of the Mediteranean Club during stay- wrote Laura &amp; the little wife<br/>Beautiful weather but day &amp; night &ndash; the Searchlights from [indecipherable]  light up all the bay- the moon is full at present.<br/><strike>Love to you all little Mother, I never forget you each day when I scribble these notes.</strike></p>
