<p>not sorry either, none to pleasant trudging along the sandy beats during the heat of the day.<br/>Each day becoming more &amp; more monotonous, even the officers very discontented &amp; anxious to leave this country.</p>
<p>Thursday 11/5/16<br/>Working on mess shed which is being constructed for the use of our unit.  Afternoon half holiday.   First time on sick parade since joining the 8th F. Amb; received a bump during the obstacle race on Anzac day &amp; as in dozens of other cases here will not heal &amp; has become septic.</p>
