Friday 4 October
At Bolougne in rest camp after a decent days journey, En route for my leave to England. Quite a decent day evening in Bolougne. Go to Calais to-morrow embark there.
Saturday 5 October
Crossing Calais to Dover rough. Dover to Victoria St. get a glimpse of Canterbury Cathedral. Charing Cross Hotel. Accommodation is hard to get, the Strand Palace Hassells Hotels being crowded.
