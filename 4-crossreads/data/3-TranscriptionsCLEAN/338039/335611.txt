  
 June. 4th Sabbath- Mass 7.30 am- news of naval engagement- Reported German Victory Easy day. June 5th. British Victory off Jutland- Wet & miserable weather- I take part in concert at Y.M.C.A. "Australian Corroboree"- June 6th. Pay-day- Another journey to Bois-Grenier for a week- D.R. duties- Fierce & prolonged British bombardment- Aerial activity.
