 Nothing would stay on my stomach &amp; I had windy spasms. In the evening our chaps got some whisky &amp; this made me feel a little better. 
 Sunday 26th Dec Boxing Day passed quietly the 10th Battalion went away from the wharf near where they were camped. Young Lieut. Gray (9th Battalion) came down to see me &ndash; Brother of Alick Gray of 7th Bty &ndash; Saw Olly Gunderson at Ordnance when happened to visit there to get a tunic for one of our chaps who had ruined his. Curly McIntire was in charge of the Ordnance but was out.
