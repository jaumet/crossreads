 [Reverse of the above postcard. This is to Burrowes' mother] Mrs Burrowes Upperby Rooty Hill N.S.W. 
 5/5/18 Stayed one short night in Plymouth leaving early next morning, Saw this beautiful old church from the outside, but was not long enough in the place to see inside. Am having a very nice time on my four days leave in St Austell, but it is going all too fast. Will write later from Fovant. love from Arthur
