<p>Wednesday July 4th<br/>Went to Abbeville for a day had a good time. Thursday 5 July<br/>Fell in warned for guard following day.<br/><uFriday 6th<br/>On guard all day<br/>Saturday 7th<br/>Came off guard went to Abbeville had a good day<br/>Sunday 8th<br/>Moved out of Chest Camp morning left Faint Valery station half past two arrived Albert six o'clock marched out and joined Battalion again &amp; Lenlist.</p>
