<p>7 a.m., 17th April<br/>6 monitors off the coast here creating hell started at about 5.45.</p>
<p>8.30 p.m.<br/>As yet no call on us.  Rumour current that the Infantry have taken by 7 a.m. this morning all positions which they reckoned upon taking the day to occupy.<br/>Saw Capt. Macarthy &amp; first impressions certainly confirm the idea that his is a blithering idiot.<br/>On the face of it everything seems to have gone very smoothly, no-one has been galoping up to demand reserves of this &amp; that in a frenzied manner as is too often the case, but of course we don't see much.</p>
<p>19th April<br/>Still here, though we are told that there are great possibilities of us moving</p>
