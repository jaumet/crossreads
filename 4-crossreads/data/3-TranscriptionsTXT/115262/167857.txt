 Saturday 31 March 1917. 
 In camp. Duty again. This time Sanitary fatigue. Did washing in afternoon. Detailed to give A.M.C. a hand in the ward. Everyone going crook at this caper. Trust me I did very little work in the ward. 
 After 7 p.m. turned in early. 
 (Met Corp. Gray (cook) at Gar r isson in evening.) 
 L. J Laversha tentmate transferred to Base for treatment.
