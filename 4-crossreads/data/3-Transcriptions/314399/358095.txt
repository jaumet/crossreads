<p>Tuesday 8 April 1919<br/>Missed parade again this morning. Am on special patrol today &amp; no job tomorrow. Met Garry &amp; took him round to see different places. Bill came in &amp; we had dinner at a little french cafe. Had lobster. Goodo.</p>
<p>Wednesday 9 April 1919<br/>Bill &amp; I visited St Addresse [Adresse]. Got the train home OK. Got my negs &amp; prints. Quite good.</p>
