<p>it in  am getting ready to go away with horses  had tea  left camp and caught train 10.P.M. from Raffa  arrived Kantara monday morning 5.30.<br/>Monday feb. 17 <strike>17.2.19</strike> 17.2.19  arrived kantara 5.30   Put my Bed down and had a sleep  had a Rough trip down in train  a man is Pushed about in trucks like Pigs   had breakfast  horses arrived 12.AM  saddled up and left for moascar  arrived there 8.30.P.M.  had a good trip with them  had a good tea and went to bed.</p>
