<p>We, (Sisters) left Bapaume on "Monday-afternoon &ndash; Luggage   &amp;  thirteen Sisters   &amp;  one Officer came by car (Red +) to No6. Stat. Hosp. Frevent (British). We passed through most interesting   &amp;  beautiful country. Arras   &amp;  Doullens to No.6. Stat.H. That Hospital is beautifully laid out. It belonged to the French but the British have run it for nearly 2 years. Three Sisters, who came out with us on "Kyarra" have been there over 12 mths.</p>
