<p>Saturday 9 March<br/>Left Arques about 9.30 &amp; proceeded via Setques Lumbres to Nielles-'lez-Blequin &amp; then to Vaudrighem [Vaudringhem]. Stabled in old farm house. Chancellor &amp; I billeted in officers billet at same place as Sergts billet. Extremely nice.</p>
