
Oct. 19thMotor trip to Poperinge (Car orderly). Wet & cold weather.
Oct 20thAustralian Mail - Route march to village of Eecke - outgoing letters still prohibited.
Oct 21stOur Ambulance leaves "Steenworde" & after passing through Cassel & other villages we finish our days march at Noordpeene still in doubt as to our final destination - Our billets the worst we have had in France - Weather very cold & unsettled - Farmhouse - Concert
Oct. 22ndResume march from Noordpeene via the large town of St Omer and we are billeted at Pilques - Australian Mail arrives.
Oct 23rdFootball etc - further mail
