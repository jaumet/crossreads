<p>Renowned Beach Thomas, the Bulsh King.  The Headline of most importance is &ndash; "Wilson leaves it to Foch", this in connection with the recent peace talk.  This will most probably mean another twelve months of war at least.  Sir Eric Geddes in a speech recently is alleged to have said that we want to be prepared for another two years of war at least.  He is probably right but I hope he gets wet in the next storm that's all.</p>
