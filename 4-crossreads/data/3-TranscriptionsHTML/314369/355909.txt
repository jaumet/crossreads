<p>24 October 1915<br/>Sunday<br/>21st After Trinity</p>
<p>After church parade go into Cairo with Dad   &amp;  visit the ancient   &amp;  Historical mosques of Sultan Hassan   &amp;  the Coronation Mosque<br/>Later we are shown the old Coptic church   &amp;  the Crypt in which the Holy family hid from Herod. it contains Pictures 900 yrs old.</p>
<p>1915 October 25<br/>Monday</p>
<p>This afternoon all details in the Bn. were lined up to find out how many were fit   &amp;  ready to return to the front</p>
