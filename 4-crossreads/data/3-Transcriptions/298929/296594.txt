<p>10th May.  Shifted to Rivery a suburb of Amiens.<br/>The Cathedral which is one of the largest and most beautiful in the world is about a kilo distant.</p>
<p>13th  German planes came over in relays bombing Amiens several of which landed very close to our billets which is an old hospital.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   We have souvenired a mattress from a bris&eacute; house and are very comfortable.</p>
