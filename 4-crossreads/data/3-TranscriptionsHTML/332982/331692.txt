<p>No parades so have just been walking about Camp. Snow on ground this morning.<br/>Thursday 11th Jan 17<br/>After dinner walked to the village of Shipton, it is the right old English style. Thatched roofs and a creek running alongside the main street.<br/>Friday 12 Jan 17<br/>Route march this morning and after dinner snow started</p>
