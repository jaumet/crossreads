19   Raining all night. Got up late this m'ng, & bought sundry choc's etc; Leaving for the trenches this afternoon. Went to Estaires with Harry & had a good time; bought sundry articles & had dinner Fair sized town this & plenty of girls about. Spotted one spy first go but he imshied. Several buildings are shell marked. Met a real nice old F - lady, who took us into her house & entertained us with wine, champagne & biscuits. She said the Australians were so delightfully free & easy.Left for the trenches at 4.45 & arrived about 8.45 after travelling for miles through mud & over duck boards. Shells
