<p>At last, I might mention the tucker is decent and there is sufficient.</p>
<p>Wednesday 22nd<br/>Very heavy frost, which made the mud more sloppy when the sun came up. It was pretty warm after 9 am.</p>
<p>We have our special syllabus now and do an hour of each subject every day. The bayonet fighting is the best. The gas helmet practice is rotten. However I suppose they are nescessary evils.</p>
