 Friday 30 August Detachment called out in morning 11th Bde to 58 Div. Ran line in no time to map position. Souvenired two bales hay returning. Afternoon easy. 
 Evening to report to C.O. with car. Went to 58 Div. fixing up about the line, then to Bray, Cappy and finally home. Great ride again.
