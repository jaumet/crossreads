 Saturday June 8th Lovely day again. Rode into Bethlehem to see some of the boys. Mail arrived. Sunday June 9 Rode out towards Hebron on message to Wellington &amp; Canterbury Regs. Very windy day. Good news arrived from French front Monday June 10th Looking like rain today. Rode out to Hebron. Usual type of Palestine City. Tuesday June 11 Fresh issue steel helmets issued to day Received mail &amp; papers from home. We were inspected today by Gen Clayton. This means an early move I suppose.
