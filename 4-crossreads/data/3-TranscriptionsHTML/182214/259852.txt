<p>since leaving Australia, we also had sports, after sports the dinner &amp; after dinner a concert. we had so much dinner, fruit &amp; jellies left over that we were able to give 40 patients who were in our hospital a good feed. I might add here that only A Section were here &amp; B Section at Hoggs Back about four miles away. We still carried on out here until rumours got around that the turks were really getting ready for a fight &amp; our transports arrived. but the Turks were further south &amp; did not trouble us at all. We got one fearful sand storm while we were here sand was everywhere in our food bread &amp; jam it lasted two days &amp; I was not sorry when it was all</p>
