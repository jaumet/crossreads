  
 4 Sun March Very cloudy  little rain  Taube over & brought down at Rafa  Patrols in action  General left for Rafa with armoured cars  very cold & rain  Looking forward to a move 
 6th Tues March Very slow day  very lazy and sleeping throughout the day.  Turks returning  Rum Issue 
 7 Wed March Had a trip to Rafa with General returning in the evening  Inspected two aeroplanes  Niggers very scared of them  The airmen were throwing signal bombs at them and they were going like Hell
