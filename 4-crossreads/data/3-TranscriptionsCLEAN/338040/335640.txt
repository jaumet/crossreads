  
 Diary while in France March 19th Arrival at Marseilles in A.41 (Minnapoles). Impression of Marseilles Harbour and township. March 20th Left vessel and marched to special encampment site of Indian G.H. originally German residence- fine grounds with abundance of fruit trees and luxuriant vegetation- great contrast with Egypt. March 21st Bully beef and biscuits. Inspection by a French General. Break camp at Marseilles - Baggage party leave by train for unknown
