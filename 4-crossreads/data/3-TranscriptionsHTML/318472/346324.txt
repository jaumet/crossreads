<p>9 April 1918<br/>Tuesday<br/>Nothing special doing. Went to Concert at night given by No. 4 Command Concert party calling themselves the owls. It was very good indeed I have been specially recommended for discharge</p>
<p>1918 April 10<br/>Wednesday<br/>Just the same old camp routine. Went to camp pictures at night &amp; that finished an uneventful day. Beautiful warm weather.</p>
