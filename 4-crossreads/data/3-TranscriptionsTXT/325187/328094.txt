 Decber. 17th   [-This page is written in a different hand] Ten of our planes came along today so things should be exciting in the Air- Turks dropped a message saying "Thank you for building the Railway we will take delivery of it on Janry 6th" No service today- Decber 18th [Monday] I am always afraid something will crop up to make me lose these notes so will post today- Alls well-
