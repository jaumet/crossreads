  
 Octobre 1 Coy are resting today. Reported that Germans have made heavy attacks on English troops who relieved us. Supposed to have taken a small section of trenches from Tommies on the S.E. corner of Polygon Wood. Artillery still very heavy on both sides. 
 Octobre 2 Marched out from camp today leaving about 3.30 p.m. and reaching billets about 7 p.m. near Reningheltz. About 9 p.m. or 10 a formation of enemy planes came ever & bombed round about here - but do not know what damage has been done.
