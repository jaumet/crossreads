<p>Saturday 4 May 1918<br/>The L.H. who were in a bad position at Es Salt have withdrawn safely.<br/>H.A. Bombing and machine gunning.<br/>Bombs were not very successful but fired 800 rounds at troops and did good work.  Pilot Lt. Cameron, in air 2 h. 35 m.<br/>Could not get Cameron down low enough, fired 800 rounds.</p>
<p>Sunday 5 May 1918<br/>Pilot Lt. Watt, in air 35 m., test engine, test camera and two guns.</p>
