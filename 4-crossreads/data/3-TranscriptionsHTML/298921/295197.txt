<p>August, 1915<br/>Saturday 14<br/>Letters from Nowell Forth &amp; Lorna &ndash;<br/>Nowell may be up from the Sudan next month - Harry Phillips came in to see me, he is not so well as when I left got a cold &ndash; I have many visitors all want me to tell them a story of the fighting &ndash; I can shoot a man but have never used a bayonet yet I like a Revolver for Trench work.</p>
