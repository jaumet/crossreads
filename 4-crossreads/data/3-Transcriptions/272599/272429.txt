<p>June Friday 8 1917</p>
<p>Coal transport today. 4 of us shift 20 tons, - a new role. coal lumper <br/>Egyptian &amp; Chinese coolies. <br/>Finish 3.45 p.m.  Shower, tea &amp; down to Wimereux blowing.  <br/>Great British offensive being carried on near Ypres, Wycheate &amp; Missines.  Wounded beginning to come in, in large convoys operations in the line are always reflected here</p>
