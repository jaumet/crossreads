  
  Thursday   4/1/18   awarded bar to D.S.O. Bas & D.S.O Martin [Carly?] 
 Friday 4/1/18. Birdwood visited line & congratulated me on D.S.O 
 Saturday 5/1/18  New Huns opposite getting very active  had good old artillery duel- no casualties. 
 Sunday 6/1/18. More good patrol work- also good wiring work done 
 Monday 7/1/18  Quiet Day. releived by Currie- moved by light Railway to [Romarin?].
