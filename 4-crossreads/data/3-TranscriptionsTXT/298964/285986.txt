
15 Th FebruaryVery cold day little rain  10 Aeroplanes went out to bomb Jacko.  Pay day.  Wrote to G. & N. also sent P.C.  Sky very black thoughts of another wet skin
16 Fri FebruaryVery cold day showery  Peter & Andy gone to join 2nd M. Div  Rum Issue  Had a easy day mostly reading and day dreaming of home and one I.L
17 Sat FebruaryVery fine day  match between Australia & New Zealand  6 to NilSaw all old boys in Squadron
