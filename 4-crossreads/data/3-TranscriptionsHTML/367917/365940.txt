<p>Belgium. 26.12.18.</p>
<p>Dear Judge Ferguson &ndash;<br/>Thank you very much indeed for the parcel you were so good as to send me for Xmas &ndash; the cigarettes and sweets were most acceptable.</p>
<p>We are at present in a small village called Chastres about 14 Kilos South of Charleroi &ndash; that is B Company are here &ndash; the remaining three companies are in Pry about two Kilos from here &ndash; we are very comfortable and had a very good Xmas &ndash; much different to the past three &ndash; the first of which was on Lemnos Island &ndash; the next at Fleurs and the last one in the line near Armentieres.</p>
