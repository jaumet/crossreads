
Friday 3.8.17Losing our new Sgt who is going to Officers school, am again only N.C.O. left on bearer section, any amount of work  Walked to Recquotoile at night where a number of the Portuguese are billeted, met one who could speak English well, was very interesting to talk to.
Saturday 4.8.17Sent home full volume (8 copies) of "Australia in the great war"
Sunday 5.8.17Went to Church service in a little barn at night.  Very little doing during the day.
