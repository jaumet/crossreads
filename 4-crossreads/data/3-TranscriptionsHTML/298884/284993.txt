<p>The best bit of excitement we have had occurred about 6.30 p.m.</p>
<p>We had just finished a bun-fighting competition on the forward well deck &amp; were looking forward to a concert at 8 P.M. when the cry of man overboard was heard. Lt Ahern rushed straight up to the bridge where he found the Captain &amp; told him that the man had fallen over from the starboard side at same time throwing a life buoy overboard.
