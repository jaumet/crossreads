<p>Monday 16th August Boarded the S.S. Saturnia <strike>for</strike> at Alexand for Lemnos other troopships accompanied us.</p>
<p>Wed 18th owing to the prevelance of submarines we zigzagged all the way to Lemnos.<br/>Thursday 19th arrived Lemnos safely early morn.</p>
<p>Friday 20th Boarded SS Osmanieh at 7 pm &amp; sailed for the Dardanelles.</p>
<p>Landed at day break on Saturday 21st &amp; had my first experience of warfare Stretcher-bearing that night</p>
<p>Sunday 22 August the greatest day of my life poor Bill Ramage killed 18th Battalion cut up sustaining about 500 casualities</p>
