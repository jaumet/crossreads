<p>Into a lift and then to the 1st floor (one could almost imagine oneself being shown to one's room in an ordinary hotel.) but the room was a cubicle of about 6' x 12' and containing two iron camp-beds with straw mattresses, a deal table and frosted windows.</p>
<p>Quite a correct cinema setting for an officer-prisoner;  according to illustrated fiction dealing with this matter.</p>
<p>A moment later and the door again opened;  a Dazzling Personage was shown in.</p>
<p>The D.P. was attired in a pale blue uniform of miraculous cut and fit decorated with silver wings and buttons.   A row of decoration-ribbons stretched across his tunic, conspicuous being the scarlet of the Legion d'Honneur.   Blue puttees to match &amp; a jaunty brown-cloth aviator's cap completed Monsieur's toilet.</p>
