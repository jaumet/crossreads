  
  16th  Mrs. Pember took us for a ramble through the woods on their property. She has a great eye for landscape gardening and proved a very interesting hostess-companion. The tremendous energy displayed by all the occupants of this grand old house of Fair oak  astoni  is astonishing. It is perfectly bewildering.
