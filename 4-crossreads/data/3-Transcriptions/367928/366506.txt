<p>6th Nov 1916</p>
<p>To Lieut Col Vernon<br/>Commanding<br/>4th DAC</p>
<p>Dear Sir<br/>I wish to thank you for the parcel which you sent me. I was getting short of sox as where some of my mates they came at a very good time. The socks and mittens I shared out among them but the balance I kept for myself<br/>Again thanking you sir on behalf of my unit and myself.</p>
<p>I am yours obediently<br/>Drv Oswald Allsopp</p>
<p>[added in different handwriting]<br/>1st L.H.C.D.<br/>Mostly socks from the girls of An Electric Light [indecipherable]</p>
