<p>5th/<br/>Melbourne Cup run.<br/>The 10/6 10/1  6/12 Reinforcements arrived by the Moltdavia<br/>The tucker is a little better each man allowed 8&frac12; worth of rations from the canteen it being given by the Egyptian Government.<br/>Harry Milner a mate of mine taken to Hospital with mumps he being the tenth to leave us with the same complaint</p>
<p>No6/<br/>Mounted main gaurd.<br/>Heard that C. Thurlow from Scone who was with the 5th of the 6th is still here in camp as a farrier &ndash;Plenty of upstanding horses</p>
