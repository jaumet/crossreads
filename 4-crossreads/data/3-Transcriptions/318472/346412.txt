<p>6 July 1918<br/>Saturday<br/>Things as usual in the Depot. In the afternoon Sgt Connell &amp; I rode bikes into Colchester &amp; had a nice afternoon. A fete in aid of the Lord Roberts Memorial Fund was the chief attraction &amp; it was well patronized Arrived back in Depot about midnight</p>
<p>1918 July 7<br/>Sunday<br/>6 After Trinity</p>
<p>The usual quiet day in camp. The camp is open on Sunday afternoons &amp; a good many townspeople pay us a visit &amp; listen to the Band. Expect to go to Weymouth tomorrow.</p>
