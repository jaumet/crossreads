<p>Capt Munro of No 2 Coy <strike>has ju</strike> arrived here at day break <strike>with a</strike> from Matrugh with a flying column of 10 waggons, with 8 horses in <strike>apiece</strike> each, rested 4 hours to water &amp; feed &amp; has set off to <strike>the water</strike> our last nights waterhole 17 miles back to withdraw the small Sikh garrison, he has taken half (30) of our Yeomanry escort back with him.</p>
