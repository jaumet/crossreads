The wounded had had a very rough time &amp; had been out 3 days before we got them.  The Turks had bound their wounds up &amp; left them food &amp; water, which the wandering Arabs had taken off them.  They tried to strangle one man &amp; cut another about with a knife.  They were brought in on camels &amp; must have had a rough ride as they had to be brought in 6 miles.  Our Squadron was on outpost at night, &amp; next morning</p>
<p>Thursday 27th<br/>we came in at 5 oclock, had a quick breakfast bully &amp; biscuits &amp; got away at 6.15 with stripped saddles.  We had several shovels.  Both Chaplains came with us &amp; about 8.30 am we came upon the scene of the fight.
