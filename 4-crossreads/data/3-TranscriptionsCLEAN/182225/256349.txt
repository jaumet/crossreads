 AUGUST   FRIDAY 18   1916 
 Fine, but cloudy all day. Out signalling all day with rest of headquarters. M r  Rowe (Capt) took over the 27 th  Bty from Capt Playfair in morning. I went out with the 26 th  Bty headquarters in evening after tea & got some practice with the director.
