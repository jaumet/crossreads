  
 Wednesday 12 March 1919  Embarked men at Kantara & sailed at 3 p.m.  Arrived at Port Said with wife last night - had a big job to get accommodation.  Hired a launch & visited the Ulimaroa & arranged for Stores etc. to be loaded, also an increase in the meat ration from 13 ozs. to 20 ozs. Returning to Hotel & stayed the night but we had little sleep owing to 3 drunken Officers singing next door till early morning. Went by train to Kantara.
