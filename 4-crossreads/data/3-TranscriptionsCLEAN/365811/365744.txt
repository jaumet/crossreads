  
 France 10th April 1918 
 My dear Mr. Justice Ferguson, It is some considerable time I think since I last wrote and all sorts of things have happened since. 
 I suppose Caesar keeps pretty constantly in touch with you. I have not seen him for nearly twelve months but I think he is still going strong. 
 Just now of course we are at a critical stage in the war - I wish I could tell you all about it for I suppose you do not get many reliable facts on the situation away out there. 
 Needless to say the Australians are doing better than ever and we are all feeling quite cheerful. One thing that helps very much
