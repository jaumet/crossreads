 Oct. 3rd Re-opening night &ndash; Small but enthusiastic audience  - Continued allied successes &ndash; Are we approaching the end faster than we anticipated? Study my parts in "The Monkey's Paw" &ndash; 
 Oct. 4th Still in Corbie &amp; feeling well satisfied &ndash; In less than three weeks I have written two sketches, two poems, one monologue and four concerted themes. (The latter for the show) &ndash; Further British &amp; French successes.
