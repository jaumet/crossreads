<p>May 5th<br/>We are supposed to be moving shortly &ndash; I hope to goodness that it comes off &ndash; and mean while I am   <strike>supposed</strike>  somewhat exercised as to how I am going to carry all my stuff.<br/>We are supposed to be travelling light i.e. one is permitted to carry what one can get on one's horse  &amp;  a few things rolled up in ones blankets.
