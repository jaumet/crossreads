<p>9.30 same evening, went to the refreshments room on the station which was a very large one and had a drink or two.  They told us to go to a hotel just over the bridge which crosses the Ness River to obtain lodgings.  It was the Glenalban Hotel in a very pretty part of the town facing the river.  Whilst there visited various places which and beautiful parts.  castle hill prince charles stewarts castle, lots of other places with rather funny names all scotch and very beautiful places one in particular called Tomnahuricsh Hill we visited this on the Sunday 30th/1/16.  away on top of the hill is a cemetery and from the top could see the whole of Inverness it was a magnificent sight.</p>
