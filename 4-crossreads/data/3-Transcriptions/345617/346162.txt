<p>Aout 28<br/>Started out for rehearsal for big review tomorrow but had to return owing to state of weather. All the "Heads" appear to be very excited by the expected big review.</p>
<p>Aout 29<br/>Was reviewed today by Sir Douglas Haig near Ponte [indecipherable]. The troops  taking part were composite 15th Brde under Brigdr. Elliot including 14th 4 Coy Eng's and 14th 7 [indecipherable] 8th Brgde. The day has been very disagreeable with heavy wind &amp; squalls of rain.</p>
