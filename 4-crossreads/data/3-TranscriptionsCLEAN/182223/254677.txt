  
 Monday 5 July 1915 35 patients from Alexandria   Busy about hospital all day  Posted letters to The Girls Mrs Knowles, & C P Hyman of London  Letters to 31 st  May from Girls Buddie [pet name for Mollie] & others 
 Tuesday 6 July 1915  In Cairo during morning Letter s  posted to Mollie.  Major Bagshaw, Col. Manifold, & Lieut. Col. Barrett here between 5-30 & 7 p.m.  Writing letters to Joseph after evg meal 
 Wednesday 7 July 1915 In Cairo twice today  Cable from and to Mr (The Hon) Meeks of Sydney, about his son Victor.  Close. But all going well.  Wrote to Glen Sparks Goomerri Queensland.
