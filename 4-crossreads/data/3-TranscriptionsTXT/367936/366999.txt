 Sunday 25 June 1916 In afternoon Rostron, Nelson &amp; I went up to Alex and  caught  took a Garry out to Nougha Gardens.  Most beautiful gardens &amp; pretty drive out.  Most of shops open in Alex.  No Sunday kept.  Service in Y.M.C.A. in evening. 
 Monday 26 June 1916 Wrote to Father No. (6).  Went into Alexandria on my own.  Got two developed films &amp; left four more.  Photos of Pyramids came out very well. In morning I took a film of Rostron &amp; Nelson acting the goat. (No. 6)
