
1915Sep. 28 Tue.Met Mildred Brown at 3.15 p.m. & after calls at a photo. shop & post office, went off to the Native Bazaars where all kinds of shoes, mats, rugs, jewelry, tinware, etc, etc, can be seen in the making & all sorts of curios & valuables are on sale.Finished up with a visit to No. 1 Gen. Hospital, Heliopolis.
