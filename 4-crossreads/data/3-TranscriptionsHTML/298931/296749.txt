<p>Friday 14<br/>Life here not to bad but the dust or sand storm was a buggar<br/>Saturday 15<br/>Taking the place of horses 10 of us hooked up to a water waggon dragging it, 10 miles<br/>Sunday 16<br/>Sighing and almost Crying, cursing and then smiling for I took this job on, with my eyes open<br/>Tel El Keber the scene of a famous battle Britons v Egyptians trenches &amp; bones still visible</p>
