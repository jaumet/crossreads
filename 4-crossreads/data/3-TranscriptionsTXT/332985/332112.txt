 30-3-1917 Raining again, nothing doing.  Lecture tonight by O.C. on ammunition. 
 31-3-1917 Showery;  Half holiday.  Pay day.  Troops still passing through the village. 
 1-4-1917, Palm Sunday A dismounted route march, 6 miles this morning, in lieu of Church parad;  The O.C. liver must be out of order, he is leading the column, he is also acting as orderly officer.  Stirring things up.  I am ordered to make 100 hoof pick for drivers. 
 2-4-1917 Snowing, very cold before it started.  Bty. shooting this morning, instructional purposes.  An order I recieved today.
