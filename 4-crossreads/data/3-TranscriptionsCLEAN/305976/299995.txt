
Monday April 8The Jordan Valley is some 14 miles across from one range of hills to the other. On one side the hills of Moab on the other the Judean hills. The Jordan River practically flows down the Centre.Tuesday April 9Butler evacuated Hospital Davidson taking his place. Posted parcel home I received from Jerusalem to day. The horses here feel the heat as well as us. What a boon it is to have a decent Horse. Very hot & dusty. Jacko shelling.Wednesday Ap 10Lost one of our St. Sgt to day. If things go on like this there
