 This proved to be a very quiet day.  There was no Church Service as we are the only Unit in this vicinity &amp; we do not carry a Chaplain on our Strength.  The weather is fearfully hot &amp; we are utterly prostrated in the middle of the day. 
 17/4/16 Monday 2/3rds of the Company went on a route march over the Desert to-day &amp; as we were not told where we were going we did not take our Water Bottles, consequently we suffered very much from thirst. I was riding in rear of the Column for a while.  My mouth was parched &amp; tongue &amp; lips were swollen, it is perfectly marvellous how it affects one.  When I did eventually get a drink it made me sick. This is the one Curse here &ndash; no decent drinking water.
