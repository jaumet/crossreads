<p>The instructors were mostly chaps who had come over with the draft reinforcements being taken out and going to schools &ndash; had qualified as Instructional Sergeants. Mixed amongst them were a leavening of Imperial (Sergts) instructors who taught us Physical culture and Bayonet exercises. After about 3 weeks here I was drafted for overseas.</p>
<p>After examination as regards my physical condition following and being passed, was with others marched to the Station one morning headed by the depot band and there started new experience.</p>
<p>Entrained we started off for Folkestone which we reached about 1 o'clock in the afternoon.</p>
