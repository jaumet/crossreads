17/9/17 Reveille 5am left 9am in 3 ton motor lorries for the coast. Travelled via Drionville, Senlecques, Desvres, Wirwignes, Cremarest, Marquise to Tardinghen near Cape Gris-Nez arriving 12.30pm. After lunch walked to Wissant saw remains of German submarine recently stranded there.
