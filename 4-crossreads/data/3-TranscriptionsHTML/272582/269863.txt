<p>Date<br/>Aug 24th<br/>at 8.30am.  70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 38,120 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 25th<br/>4 men proceeded on leave and 2 rejoined from leave.<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 31,260 rations</p>
<p>Aug 26th<br/>Strength:  1 Off 87 O.Ranks not including 4 in hospital and 8 on leave.<br/>47 N.C.O.'s and men baked 20930 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 27th<br/>1 Rejoined from hospital<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 37,782 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 28th <br/>1 Rejoined from leave to U.K.  1 rejoined from hospital<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 39,040 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 29th<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 38,418 rations.</p>
<p>Aug 30th<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 38,460 rations</p>
