<p>Wednesday 19 December</p>
<p>Snowy &ndash; ground Frozen.  Beautiful sunny day followed by bright starry night.<br/>A busy day on the bike.  Visited La Capelle, Bellebrune &amp; Cremarest and ran along practically all the roads within a radius of 10 kilos.<br/>Trip to A.D.M.S. Sigs. etc. at Samer.<br/>Then after tea I had to run back to Desvres to look for our horse transport which is coming here by road</p>
