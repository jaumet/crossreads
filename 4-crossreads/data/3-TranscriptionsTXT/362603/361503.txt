
8.  a fellow gets Homesickness very bad.  Auntie & Marie are absolutely lovely in everything they do & say.  Uncle tells Her that   she   anyone would think it was her own son she was pining for & Auntie could not love a son very much more than her goodness to me indicate.Now Dad, Mum, & George, I must toddle off to bed.  Best of Love & Good wishes to you all.  Keep well & cheery & don't think because You get no letters that we have grown callous & do not write every week but lots of mail gets lost.Regards to our enquiring Friends too.Lots of Love & KissesFrom Your own BoyHarry X XRegards to "Good Old Bess"   I will write to Her soon.
