<p>conscription. The Kings Rules &amp; Regulations which were framed for the guidance of a small professional army are not suited for the mentality of a nation in arms &amp; the freest of all nations at that &ndash; Australia</p>
<p>Dec 26/16<br/>Pulled back to Pommiers over a fortnight ago but was at once taken sick with an influenza chill &ndash; was relieved of my team &amp; have been 'laid up' back with the Company at Becondel in a 'Bivvy' &ndash; a pretty miserable time &ndash; couldn't eat the army rations &amp; have been dieting myself as best I can on</p>
