 Lewis Gun all morning.  Afternoon had Extended Order &amp; digging trenches.  After tea walked to No. 11 Hurdcott Camp &amp; saw L.J. Smith &amp; Guild arriving back 9.30 p.m. 
 Thursday, 28/6/17 Ordinary drill in morning.  Rained dinner time, heavily so had rapid loading in a hut.  After tea had Lewis gun lecture.  Rained very heavily after tea. 
 Friday, 29/6/17 Rained all day so had no parade &ndash; only a hut inspection &amp; bombing lecture, was also very cold all day.  Paid after tea. 
 Saturday, 30/6/17 Musketry all morning.  Afternoon went to Hurdcott but Reg was out also Sowter &amp; Guild.  Had a washing day after dinner.  Stopped home in evening.
