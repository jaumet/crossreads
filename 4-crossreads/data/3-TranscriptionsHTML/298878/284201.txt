<p>and the barbers performed his operation of scraping part of the black off with a huge wooden weapon shaped like a razor; he then signalled to two of his varlets who tipped the seat up and precipitated the candidate into the tank.  Two assts clothed in oilskins then fell on him and administered a dipping.  After this he was allowed to clamber out of the tank and consider himself a duly qualified member of the order.  The roars of the crowd as each one was put through the ordeal are better imagined than described.  All the Military officers excepting the Major and Doctor, crowds of soldiers, and myself among the Ships company were put through.  Most of us acted wisely in wearing only our pyjamas.
