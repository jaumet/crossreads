  
 8 April 1918 Monday  Bethlehem     Posted you 3 Easter eggs one each carved in  Bethlehem  hard boiled real eggs- Still awaiting orders- to join up or get leave have  150  men here in the Monastery still   I enclose French Sergeants letter or certificate that the monastery in his care has been left in good order by me-   Among a lot of old Books here I notice a Girls Chatterbox 1882!!! written in English at that date never published for a Monastery in  Beth  I have heard Nuns behind spiked grills but never saw one, although a  Cake  arrived one day for me from their hands- what a quaint thing some people make of religion-
