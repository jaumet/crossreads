<p>5 5 January 1916<br/>Wednesday<br/> <br/>Sleep till 12 noon.  Shower. very hot &amp; sultry. Key stone. stretcher drill in afternoon.</p>
<p>Wait for Janet &amp; party to arrive.  Don't turn up.  Bob &amp; self go down line &amp; have tea.  Meet Cis &amp; Essie Hewlett at station, just arrived, delayed through waiting for friend.  Take them back to Sydney &amp; "My Old Dutch" at Crystal Palace. Refreshments &amp; back to camp.  Take second night shift. 2 hours sleep.</p>
