Durant's post is on top of a Steep Hill with Hill 971 in front of us looking from here you can See tents out at Suvla Bay They are hospitals and no Doubt they have a lot of patients in them. Then again there are Hospital Ships and warships  this ground was in the turks hands up till august the 6th when we had a big advance on our left and cleared our Enemy right out of it and gave them the fright of their lives no Doubt.
