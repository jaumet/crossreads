<p>Monday Aug 13th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 6 am : boys on route march : sent P.C.s to all at home &amp; all the family, also to Spooner St : stroll to aerodrome in afternoon &amp; had a good look round : about fifteen 'planes were loaded up with bombs ready for a raid so Fritz doesn't have things all his own way : very interesting to us : went to "our house" &amp; had a good time : it makes one wish to be home once more : the girls remind me much of Elsie &amp; Mell : she sat on my knee &amp; I taught her how to count in English :</p>
<p>Tuesday Aug 14th</p>
<p>Monasterie : reveille 6 am : boys on a stunt but had to return as it rained : swim parade in afternoon to Arques &amp; it was very good : Morrie &amp; self</p>
