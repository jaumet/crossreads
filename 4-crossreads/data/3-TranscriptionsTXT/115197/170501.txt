 April - 1919 
 &nbsp; 
  April 1st  
 Light hearts &amp; good fellowship.&nbsp; Glorious weather which improves as we go South. 
 A long day in the train - Incident concerning a Chinamen - Arrive at Harfleur (Le Havre) at midnight.&nbsp; Sleep in the train. 
  April 2nd  
 Disentrain at 6 a.m.&nbsp; March to A.G.B.D.&nbsp; Meet old friends - Norman Frettell.&nbsp; Billy Dick.&nbsp; Disappointment through not seeing Herb
