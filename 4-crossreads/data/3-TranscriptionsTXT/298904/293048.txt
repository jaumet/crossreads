English speaking people were pretty rare (French Italian, Greek, & Arabic were the most popular languages) but when we left Cairo in 1916 practically everyone could speak a little English, its marvellous how it spread so quickly, due no doubt to the thousands of Australian NZ & English troops who thronged the streets, there are 2 very fine hotels there the "Continental" & "Shepherds" both cater for the wealthy
