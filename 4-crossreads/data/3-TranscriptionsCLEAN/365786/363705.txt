Long ago water was only supplied to the land by the inundation - now however thanks to the huge dam at Assuan & the barrages at Assiut & the Delta, perennial irrigation is possible & no water escapes to the sea unless by wish of those in charge of the irrigation works.  Long ago the ancients speak of 7 mouths to the Nile, but now only the East & West branches are used, viz, the Rosetta & Damietta branches.
