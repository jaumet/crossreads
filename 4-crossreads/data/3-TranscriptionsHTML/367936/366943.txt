<p>Sunday 5 March 1916<br/>Father drew off honey then all hands took lunch over to Lease.<br/>Tried to rain but could not.</p>
<p>Monday 6 March 1916<br/>Dick &amp; Geoff plowing.  Bill Large came down and agreed to sucker Castle Rock.<br/>I raked down Straw Stack.  Father started fence round it.</p>
