
18.4.18Normal.The following targets were engaged throughout the night & morning, 2500 rounds being expended:-1.  Streets, Ville-Sur-Ancre.  (2)  Cemetery.  (3)  Sunken Road K.1.b. & d.  (4)  Area E. of Ville-Sur-Ancre E.25.d. & 26.c.  (5)  Hollow K.2.c.05.70.  (6)  Battery Positions K.7.b.7.7.Enemy artillery except for normal fire on roads about Treux, Buire & Marrett Wood has been quiet.  A few bursts of M.G. fire during the night.  Enemy Aircraft, Nil.
