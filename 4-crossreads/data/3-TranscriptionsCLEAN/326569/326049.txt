  
 Friday, 11/5/17 Much warmer today.  Afternoon went for short route march in morning saw two aeroplanes manoeuvring at battle practice.  After tea went to Perham with Meldrum for bath. 
 Saturday 12/5/17 Nice fine day, threatening rain in afternoon.  After dinner went to Tidworth & after tea to Salv. Army Institute & Regt. Inst. at Perham Downs with Andy Ken. 
 Sunday, 13/5/17 Reveille at 7 a.m.  After dinner washed my laundry & after tea went to Y.M.C.A. to post letters to Aust.
