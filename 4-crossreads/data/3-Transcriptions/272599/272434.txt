<p>1917 Wednesday 13 June</p>
<p>Turn night into day &amp; vice versa &ndash; <br/>After breakfast go to bed &amp; stay there till 4.30 p.m.  Sleep indifferently due to all the different noises &amp; distractions. After tea go out for a blow on cliffs.  <br/>Duty again at 8 p.m.</p>
