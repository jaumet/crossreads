
Australian Red Cross letterheadBulford28.4.17Dear Dad,I returned last night from Grimsby after having spent 3 1/2 days with Jennie & Her Father & Uncle.  We, Dave & I, arrived at their Home at 5.30 am on Tuesday last & arrived back in Daylight at 9.15 pm on Friday night.  On the first morning Dave & I went down to see the Fisherman unloading the fish.  We met Tommy Sergeant who was an ex-engineer on a Trawler & He knew everything, so we saw all that was to be seen even to the engine room on a Trawler.
