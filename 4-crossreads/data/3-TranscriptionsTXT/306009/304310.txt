 1915 May. 4 Tue. The little Chapel at the House has been converted into a ward with 25 beds in it &ndash; men are also on mattresses in all passages &amp; on stair-landings throughout the house &ndash; Our daily returns to Headquarters are shewing "No empty beds" At 8 p.m. sent into Ghezireh Palace to assist with rush of wounded expected. They commenced to arrive about 10.30 p.m. &amp; as there was no clerical work doing  leant  lent a hand to get them into wards &ndash; about 300 altogether- returned in Motor Ambulance to Mena arriving at 1.15 a.m. About 230 admitted.
