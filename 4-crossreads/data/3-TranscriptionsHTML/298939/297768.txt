<p><strike>Friday 4th May</strike><br/>17 miles, but I was in no way tired by it.  In fact I went to a concert with Aleck Nunan in the evening.</p>
<p>Friday 4th May<br/>Today was spent in lectures to give us an easy time after yesterday.<br/>The morning lecture by Cpl Merrilees dealt with the circulation, &amp; the afternoon by the same, dealt with the digestion.</p>
