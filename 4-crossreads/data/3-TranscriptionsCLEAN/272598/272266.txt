  
 Mentioned in despatches  December 6th, 16 
 Extract from General Staff orders by General McKay, G.O.C.  6.12.16 
 L/Cpl Berry H.V. On three separate occasions L/Cpl Berry, who was in charge of a party of bearers for more than three weeks, displayed conspicuous bravery, handling his men with ability & inspiring them throughout.
