<p>April 1915<br/>21 Wed<br/>Route march of the Bde through Heliopolis, Abassia, Railway Sq, cut off a corner along Boulac, to Kasr-el-Emi, Vieux Caire, Babs-el-Youn to Meadi, Dined with Geo Onslow. Boxing at 2nd Bde Stadium</p>
<p>22 Thursday<br/>Left Bivouac at Meadi about 7.30 am and rode to Helwan. March discipline much better. Native children fairly cheeky. Saw some of the Turk prisoners at Toura. Rode past San Giovanni up to Helouan. A bivouached behind the Tewfik Palace Hotel.</p>
