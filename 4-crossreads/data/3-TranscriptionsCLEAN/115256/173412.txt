  
 Tuesday 8 June 1915 
 March 30 - Inspection by H.R.H. the Prince of Wales. 
 March 27 - Left Tel-el-Kebir at 1600.  Bivouac 5 to 6 miles out. 
 28th - Starting at 0500 reach Mahsamah [also spelt Mahsama] at 0800.  Leave at 1500 and arrive at Moascar at 0900 on 29th.  All troops very much fatigued. 
 30th - Leave for Serapeum & cross canal from Africa to Asia. 
 1st to 3rd April - Recuperation after long march.  Bathing in the Canal.  On 3rd we leave for advance trenches & reach "Railhead". 
 April 4th - In the desert.  Post No. 3 occupied by B Coy, 45th Batt., part of sub-section commanded by Gen. Cox.
