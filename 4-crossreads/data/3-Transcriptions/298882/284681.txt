<p>10 AM Wed. 26 10.10 AM</p>
<p>Our Division attacked this morning at day break after a heavy bombardment by our heavy guns, field guns and Machine guns.  First objective gained 7/45 AM attack is still progressing favourably.  Our casualties fairly heavy up to date.  Many prisoners coming in, helping our wounded along.  A number of tanks participated in the attack.
