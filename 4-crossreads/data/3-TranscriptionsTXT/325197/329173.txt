 Wed. 30th Oct. Went to see "The Law Divine" at Wyndham Theatre. After the show taken to the Victoria League for tea. All expenses paid by the Australian Red Cross. 
 Thur. 31 Oct. Went to harry lauder's concert at the Pavilion 
 Sat 2nd November. Spent the afternoon in London with Miss Golding and Miss Prestwick. 
 Sun 3rd November Miss Prestwick carting me all over London. 
 Mon. 4th Nov. Had a change today by staying in the Hospital 
 Tues 5th Nov. Went with party to Windsor Castle and after going through all the Sate apartments and stables, etc. we had tea, and on by Princess Alice and a few ladies
