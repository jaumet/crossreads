<p>Saturday 7 August, 1915</p>
<p>Visited Gezeirch Hospital saw Lindsay Waugh &amp; Nurse Chisholm.</p>
<p>Went to see the &quot;Kursale&quot; a big music hall at Cairo. It was a magnificent programme.&nbsp;You are able to smoke &amp; have drinks brought to you while you sit listerning to the performance.</p>
