A lot of details have joined since I saw the Coy. last including, Bellchambers Freddie Turner, both of these are excellent chaps are "origionals". Had the first good square meal I have had for some time today, a bit of a contrast to D. Coy.
24th. Very windy last night it nearly blew our little shanty down, old Fritz kept us thinking for he was dropping an odd shell or two nearly the whole night through.
