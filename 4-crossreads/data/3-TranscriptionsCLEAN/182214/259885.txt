when we got off & marched to a place called Dernancourt its true we stayed here only one night but that night it was so cold & frosty we hardly slept & I was now sorry to light the fire & start cooking breakfast my feet were wet & I can safely say that during the next three weeks they were hardly dry except at night when I had my boots off. We left Dernancourt Oct 21st  & arrived at Mametz Wood after a rough journey through mud and slush shell holes etc. We started to make a stew under trying conditions it was still raining & the boys were very hungry.
