<p>After an eventful ride over the shell torn road in the pitch dark we arrived at Villers Brettoneaux and soon learnt that Fritz was still going back at the "tout" and also that 4 of our boys had captured the big gun &amp; crew that had been shelling Amiens</p>
<p>This villiage as may be expected has been somewhat shell torn, with not a building standing. A piece of wall 3 ft square is all that remains of the church.</p>
