
<p>SATURDAY, JULY 19, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Had a fox hunt in the Hill</p>
<p>paddock in morning and never</p>
<p>got any. Took the ferrets up the</p>
<p>Strand scrub in afternoon &amp;</p>
<p>got a few rabbits.</p>
<p>Girls &amp; Edwards went down to</p>
<p>Peace celebrations at Walhallow</p>
<p>in big car.</p>
