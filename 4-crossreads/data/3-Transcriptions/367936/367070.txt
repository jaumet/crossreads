<p>Tuesday 14 November 1916<br/>Flag drill in morning.  Got direction of C Troop (8 miles away) on map, shown how to sight with compass then fixed heliograph and Jim flashed message through.<br/>Got two parcels;  one from Mother &amp; one from Aunt Katie.</p>
<p>Wednesday 15 November 1916<br/>Telephone from 2 a.m. till 6 a.m.  Then signalling instruction as per usual.</p>
