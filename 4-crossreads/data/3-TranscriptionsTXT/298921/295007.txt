 February, 1915 Wednesday 3 Got home to A42 at 3.30 A.M. we had a rough passage back to ships in a "Felucca" manned by Arabs Head wind &amp; rough sea we tacked backwards &amp; forwards for 2 &frac12;  hours &amp; got wet through &amp; chilled to the bones. Alexandria a great city of learning in the past is now very squalid in places &amp; cosmopolitan Many Refugees from Jerusalem are here &amp; wounded soldiers are brought from the Continent.
