<p>Sunday 23 January 1916<br/>Monday 24 January 1916</p>
<p>Tuesday 25 January 1916<br/>Went alongside Wharf.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26 January 1916<br/>Left Suez at 7 a.m. for Cairo.  Arrived at Abbassia at 4 p.m.</p>
<p>Thursday 27 January 1916<br/>Watched Dressing in Morning &amp; started work at 2 p.m.</p>
<p>Friday 28 January 1916<br/>Bubs Dressings all day.</p>
<p>Saturday 29 January 1916<br/>Went to Cairo for first time.  Met C. Board &amp; McDonnell.  Came home in Chariot with H. O'Keefe &amp; Roy Farnsworth, Geoff Clarke.  A Gallop all the way.</p>
