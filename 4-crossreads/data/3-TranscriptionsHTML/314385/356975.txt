<p>1916</p>
<p>Fri Sep 1st<br/>To day we are releived by the Canadian A.M.C. and are preparing to take our departure. We handed in all blankets &amp; are paraded in marching order and are awaiting orders to move off.</p>
<p>I have a gathering in my ear through having a cold, weather is again fine we slept under the trees at back of chateau, we are warned to move off in the morning.</p>
<p>Saturday Sep 2nd<br/>Did not move off to day filling in the time cleaning up the camp &amp; billets.</p>
<p>Sun Sep 3rd<br/>Paraded at 6 a.m.</p>
<p>After breakfast marched off at 10 beginning with a number of haults through the congestion of traffic on the road.</p>
