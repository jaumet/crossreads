 Apl 20  th    We had a hurry call at noon for three lorries & we went up to S  t   Omer - We arrived there at 7.30 P.M. & were parking for the night when Frank who was at the wheel, hit the lorry in front, & broke the top of the radiator. We parked up, & Frank & I went to find the casualty park which we knew was in the town. I interviewed the Colonel in Charge, & he gave me a chit for a top off one of the radiators in the pack of disabled lorries, we went round & borrowed tools & set to work & dismantled one. I worked on the radiator, whilst Frank went round the other cars taking anything that was useful e.g. sparkplugs etc which we found hard to obtain honestly - we secured the top & carried it to our lorry - 
   
 Apl 21  st    We got up early & fixed the radiator & were finished in time to load up (Armouries workshop equipment) & we did'nt lose a minute.
