 Monday 1 February&nbsp;1915 Six minor operations today. Varicocele. Axillary Abscess. Circumcissions (2), tonsils. Stricture. Dined with Col. Braund &ndash;&nbsp;2 nd  Infty Battallion at 6pm. Walked to pyramids &amp; Sphynx with&nbsp;Sister&nbsp;?&nbsp;between 9 &amp; 10-15 pm. Moon  not  behind the clouds. Sat under jaw of Sphynx&nbsp;for ten minutes. Posted letters home &amp; many postcards. 
 Tuesday 2 February 1915 Some operations.&nbsp; Reading &amp; writing all day.&nbsp; Some reading  
 Wednesday 3 February 1915 Operations this morning. 2 hernias. 1 appendix. 1 Mastoid 1 Empyema. Asked Dr&nbsp;Bird to be present, he came.&nbsp; At 4-15 pm. set out for the great Pyramid. Climbed it down at 5-45 p.m. Name carved in the Stone as usual.&nbsp; Visited Dr&nbsp;Adam Scheuber, of Cairo, at his Chalet above Mena House, at 6 p.m.
