<p>[Monday 19 June 1916]</p>
<p><strike>If Day wants to know what I would like a Khaki Knitted Silk Tie would be very acceptable but I fancy she must be too busy at Home &amp; Red +.  Hope this arrives safely Love to all.</strike><br/>Over 6 months since we left Heliopolis with our Horses.</p>
