The carry was one of about a mile, up hill, &amp; in most parts knee deep in sticky mud, making walking an effort without having to carry a heavy man on a stretcher.  It was about four in the morning, &amp; we had been carrying constantly from about five in the afternoon, I came in &amp; threw my-self on the sodden floor of my dug-out, wet through, &amp; totally exhusted.
