<p>Sunday 6th Feb.</p>
<p>Paid on Friday last at Intermediate Base &amp; met Land in evening, &amp; spent happy time at pictures.  Saw others of 3rd Field Ambulance &amp; had good chat with Kennedy who is coming out to see me.<br/>Last <strike>might</strike> night Jack Buckle came to see me &amp; we had a cup of tea in the hospital.<br/>The dispensary is now reorganised</p>
