 Thursday 30th We got our saddlery  out & inspected the horses & allotted one to each man the old hands being allowed their own horses back again if they wanted them.  I took mine.  The saddlery was not in the best of condition so we could not have a mounted parade.  In the afternoon we went out on troop drill & were also allotted tents 3 to each troop.  Squadron Sergeants also had a tent.  A lot of parcels were distributed today.  We have plenty
