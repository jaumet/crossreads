<p>Saturday Dec 1<br/>Returned to Jaffa Found the boys standing to owing to Jacko shelling Some spies were caught signalling &amp; directing artillery fire from housetops. Taubes came over at night<br/>Sunday Dec 2<br/>Visited Jaffa &amp; visited ruins near the shore. Saw remains of House where Simon Peter supposed to have stayed<br/>Monday Dec 3<br/>Looking like rain today<br/>Received some parcels. Anzac Divisional <strike>Post Cards</strike> Christmas Cards came along today. Posted some to friends. In afternoon visited foundry</p>
