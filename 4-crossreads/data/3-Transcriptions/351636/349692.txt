<p>The Centre for Soldiers' Wives &amp; Mothers,<br/>92B. Pitt Street, Sydney.<br/>7-3-16</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton<br/>Could you assist Mrs. Saunders in the payment of debts. She is owing rent &amp; grocer's bill. She was left in debt when her husband sailed &amp; has been paying off gradually &ndash; Having an invalid son &amp; delicate daughter she finds it difficult to pay up in full</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>B. Booth</p>
