 St Patricks Hospital 
 Malta 
 27/12/15 
 We came into this hospital last night from mudros wich we left on Thursday December the 23 rd  and arrived in Malta harbour on Saturday the 25 th  about 2pm. Christmas Day we spent on Board Ship (Soudan)&nbsp; for Dinner we had roast Beef potatoes - pumpkin - and Plum Pudding and turkey. (Oh turkey) But we we were Sick of turkey as we were having lumps of it Since april the 26 th&nbsp;  So we had been haveing it for Eight months and then we had to withdraw&nbsp; Because Some turkeys are to hard to pluck&nbsp; So lets have Chicken next Xmas
