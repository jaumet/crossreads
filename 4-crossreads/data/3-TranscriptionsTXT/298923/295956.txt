17 October 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out)On Ambulance Train again &amp; in carriage 3 sick HUN officers who fare the same as we do &amp; get a square deal- in fact X  8 of our Officers sit while 2 Huns occupy lying down cots X  I asked them what they thought of the Australian Cavalry?  they replied "the same as the Turks do"  We gave them illustrated papers  with some good Cartoons   Arrived Kantara [indecipherable] Stationary Hospital at 1500.  Some of the Orderlies here are half witted untrained men owing to man shortage-
