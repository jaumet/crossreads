
Lord's PlaceOrandeSunday [Dec 1901]
Dear FatherWe are all very well only Hazel Is sick we dont know what she has got. Uncle Fred is verry well but Alan has a bit of a cold we are all settled down and Grand-father has such a lot of beans and cabbages nearly every davy we have some beans and cabbages we are having a very nice time. The people nels door their names are wests.
