<p>May 13th<br/>Some of our boys returned from the trenches. Weather very wet. Quiet day along the line.<br/>May 14th (Sabbath)<br/>Another quiet day artillery inactive- today is "Mother's" Day. Church in the morning pleasure walk in afternoon, hospital duties during the night. Two letters arrived.<br/>May 15th<br/>Cool and wet weather 14 hours sleep. Usual routine</p>
