<p>May 1918<br/>Saturday 4</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daoures<br/>Fred Aldred was over in morning and I paid him 20. Avec mercies.<br/>Weather glorious<br/>Had the usual day on duty at Generals office and spent the usual uninteresting time.<br/>One Fritzer brought down at La Neuville.<br/>Rode across to Bonnay  with a despatch to 14th and 150 Bdes.<br/>Had a run into [indecipherable] line near [indecipherable]  - horse cut a little about legs.<br/>Arrived home 12.30am <br/>A few [indecipherable]</p>
