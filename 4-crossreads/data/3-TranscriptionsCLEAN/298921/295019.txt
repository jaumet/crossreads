
February, 1915Monday 15Moved out at 7 am for Heliopolis to join the regiment, leading out Horses from Remount Depot - Said Goodbye to Abassia still no Mail -The men gave me a good Welcome & I am once more beded down in a fresh Camp,Horse feed is bad. Rice Straw a sort of Alfafa & Maize & the horses are getting badly sanded.Hope you can read this scribble.
