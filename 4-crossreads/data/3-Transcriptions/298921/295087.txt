<p>April, 1915<br/>Saturday 24<br/>Exercise today around hills &amp; Quarries, some of which would hold a Squadron of Cavalry. Went to the Hot Sulphur Baths &amp; lay in them for 20 minutes, like all Turkish or other Baths, Sandglass, nigger, blanket, etc. etc:-<br/>My watch changed color studs etc from the Sulphur. After the sand of the Desert it was like making [indecipherable] north of Bretonia were every Tommy got into a Bath some 3 deep, &amp; washed their clothes &ndash; I will post this here &amp; chance you getting it.</p>
