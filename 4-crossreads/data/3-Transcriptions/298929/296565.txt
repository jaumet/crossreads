<p>25th  Then we left Eblington and marched to Steenbecque and entrained to doullens on our way to the Somme</p>
<p>27th  Bivouaced for the night.<br/>Here the densities of the traffic rivals anything I have seen in London.  The thousands and thousands of ex-buses and motor transports, at times <strike>in</strike> three abreast in such narrow streets is simply marvellous.<br/>We boarded some of these buses and went to Franvillers.  All of our 3rd division</p>
