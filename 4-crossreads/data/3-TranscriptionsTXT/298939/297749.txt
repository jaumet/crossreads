  Act 4 Scene 1   Parkhouse Camp  
 Preparing for a second spell of active service. 
 March 27th 1917 Arrived here from Weymouth exactly a week ago During the ensuing time I was put in a draft for France, issued with kit for service gave in all extra kit not required so that I was left with what I stood up in &amp; a change of underclothes.
