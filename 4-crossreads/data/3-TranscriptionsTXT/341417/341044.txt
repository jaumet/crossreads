 Belgium Apl 16. 1917 
 Dear Norman Just to say that "All's Well" with me, and like "Johnnie Walker" am "going strong". Weather has been intensely cold, &ndash; 30&deg; of frost but Spring is trying hard to pop its nose above ground. Kind regards to Mrs. Norman &amp; all your folk. Sincerely yours J A Reid.
