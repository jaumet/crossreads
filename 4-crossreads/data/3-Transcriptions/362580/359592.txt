<p>a very out of the way place but we are happy &amp; well &amp; that is really everything Edith looks very well indeed I have never seen her look so well &ndash; of course all our work is out door &amp; that very possibly makes us very fit.  I expect I shall go on night duty very soon it is nearly my turn again &ndash; We do a month each &amp; as there are only five us to do night &amp; day duty it comes fairly often &ndash;</p>
