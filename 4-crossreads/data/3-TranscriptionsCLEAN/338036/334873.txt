
[16th Feb. contd]NZ officers came to look over Baths prior to occupation.  However at night young [?] Donovan (Capt Vickers batman) cooked us a bonny tea of pork chops eggs & chips.  All purchase locally.
Saturday 17/2/17Cleaning up etc.  All frozen pools & river have quite thawed & enjoyable.  Forgot to mention that yesterday saw one of our planes brought down from a great height.  Did not see what caused it but immediately afterwards saw two Germans making back as a great pace & concluded they were the cause.  The falling plane did not come down as quickly as I imagined it would.
Sunday 18/2/17Recd instructions to report to Erquinghem tonight so with full pack I in company with about 8 others set out along river bank.   And by gee talk about mud & heavy walking and pack seemed about a ton.  On arrival at Erquinghem I found good quarters & convenient surroundings & was well pleased with the place.
Monday 19/2/17Heavy day.  Letters from Miss Gregory & Ernie.
Tuesday 20/2/17Ernies Birthday (21)
