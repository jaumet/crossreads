  
 31/1/17  Wednesday 
 Snowing - very cold - Very quiet except for Artillery duels. Our Bearers went out. 
 1/2/17  Thursday 
 Frosty - About 6 pm 4th Division hopped over & took easily a very important trench, they had few casualtys & came back with some 50 prisoners. Heavy bombardment all night - All pay books called in. Got a fine parcel from Frank. 
 2/2/17  Friday 
 Frosty - Heavy bombardments, several small successful raids - a number of prisoners came through. Dr Helstrom got badly wounded at one of the posts - he was a great patient & said the - thing burst 6 yards away & this is what I got - is it good enough for Blighty - several bearers got slight wounds. 
 3/2/17  Saturday 
 Frosty & bitterly cold. Very few casualtys although two lines of captured trenches were retaken four times, finally our boys bombed them out & made good. A few more prisoners came through.
