 26 September 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out) Amman  Day Post Good grazing country &amp; Oleanders grow &amp; are in flower all along the Wadi-  blew up line &amp; captured one train  Lt Morrice wounded  Got some fresh mutton for men-  Have not seen any of the beautiful CIRCASIAN women yet, but Bedouins here are nearly white &amp; have curly  hair  Hair all carry Rifles &amp; daggers &amp; are a picturesque crowd- Snipers still firing, captured another 100-   We are among a treacherous lot of Bedouins  X who killed all the New Zealand rear guard the first time we came to Amman months ago.X
