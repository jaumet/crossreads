 [Tuesday 7 March 1916 ] 
 I had also sent some money to  Strange   for Opal but he did not receive it either, I mean at Dungalear.  Some awful fake photos are being published  in Australian papers   The  Sun  16/1/16 has one sent by Capt  Reynolds  of A.A. Service Corps shewing 4 wheeled G.S. Wagon &amp; horses &amp;  Gippies  loading  Furniture !!!! &amp; called the evacuation of ANZAC. It is so absurd, one wonders at a Paper printing such a thing. Killed a snake near Tent today, after centipede &amp; Scorpions, but no one seems to get bitten
