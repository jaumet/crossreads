<p>Friday 10 November 1916<br/>Flag wagging and sending on buzzer.</p>
<p>Saturday 11 November 1916<br/>Lot of our planes went out towards El Arish.  Suppose the Turks are catching it.  Taube came over but shrapnel hunted it.<br/>Shown how to rig telephone and send messages to stations.  Did four hour shift on telephone in orderly room.<br/>Wrote to Mother but did not number it, some photos in it.</p>
