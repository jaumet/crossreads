<p>July 1 are fairly numerous, &amp; I have spotted at least 2 women who drive their own cars. Of messenger boys one, walking, carried a fat brown paper parcel; &amp; the other sported a bike, &amp; a basket in which reposed a well-plucked fowl, - its head dangling absurdly over the side. (The rest we will reserve for some other fine day).</p>
<p>2 Officers going crook about Mess Orderlies &amp; state of hut, so we told them straight that all the permanent Orderlies were put on guard.<br/>Leaving for<br/>[in margin] Sunday</p>
