<p>Feb 10</p>
<p>Of course Alan &amp; I were quite at sea when we arrived at B'ham station &amp; as the place was in almost total darkness (for Zepps.) we found it rather difficult to improve upon our ignorance of the ins &amp; outs of the place. However, after wandering about for a while, we came upon an English "Tommy", who kindly took us to the Sailors &amp; Soldiers Institute &amp; there for the large sum of 9d we got a bed for the rest of the night.
