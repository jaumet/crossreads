 Monday Sept 21 We are alongside the Australia our flag ship filling our water tanks &ndash; good business for we'v been short  -  We lag on her starboard side till 3 pm  -  I got in board &amp; called on the PMO Dr Caw  -  He has 3 men under him  -  Ramsay Smith of Adelaide &ndash; Hornibrook of Melbourne &amp; and an Edinburgh man who was keen on aboriginal skulls  -  I told him the yarn of McGregh &amp; the German chap in New Guinea
