<p>Sunday 19 March 1916<br/>Fixed up show sheep.  Heard that one of the Cassilas horses had fallen on a stump and killed itself.  Father &amp; I rode up and look at it.<br/>Mr. &amp; Mrs. Dick Blackman came over in afternoon.</p>
<p>Monday 20 March 1916<br/>Helped Father make frame for lorrie, got show sheep in and then started Dick off for Mudgee with sheep.<br/>Father &amp; I went up and got 11 horses and took them down to Wonga.</p>
