<p>Tuesday 3/4/17<br/>Drilled in the slush &amp; snow on parade ground making feet very cold.  In afternoon route march through Compton Chamberlain, Favant, Dinton &amp; Barford back to camp;  a distance of 9&frac12; miles with full packs up.</p>
<p>Wednesday, 4/4/17<br/>Still cold but drilled outside all day.</p>
<p>Thursday, 5/4/17<br/>Had Battalion drill as supposed to be reviewed by King at Bulford on Saturday.</p>
<p>Friday, 6/4/17<br/>Good Friday &amp; had a holiday.</p>
