<p>Wednesday August 2. 1916</p>
<p>A nice cool morning. I had a very late breakfast. I took the bus to the Tower of London and crossed over the Tower Bridge which is a wonderful structure lifting up in the middle to let ships pass under.</p>
<p>Tower of London is a very ancient structure &amp; is a very wonderful place bring used at different times as a palace a prison and a fortress. It was commissioned by William the Conqueror, and now covers 18 acres. there are many Towers. After passing under the bloody Tower one passes into the armoury. The Jewel</p>
