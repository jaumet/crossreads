<p>previous stint in fact Hill 60 - where the tremendous mine exploded &ndash; lay near by to our right; no longer a hill but just an uncofimable mass. The ground is cut about by shell holes to a tremendous &amp; unimaginable extent, and old rifles and gear, together with all kinds of smashed up military equipment of <strike>of</strike> all kinds, lay everywhere.<br/>We arrived at the bearer post &amp; found Vic Hall, Harry Reynolds, Mc ilroy &amp; Strange installed therein. It was a German pill box &amp; evidently the scene of lot of fighting, and the centre of a hot barrage, for confusion reigned all around.</p>
