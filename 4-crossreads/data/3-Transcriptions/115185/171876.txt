<p>Kind to me.<br/>Col Fiaschi is too but has so much else to do that Col Dick looks after the patients more.<br/>Col Jamieson is exceedingly well never seen him so fit. Sister Lowe is in Charge of his medical ward and understands him &amp; met Mrs Pollock &amp; Mrs Gill in London and is very keen to help him &amp; save him any duties or anxiety she can. He is the best in condition of any of the surgeons &ndash; even Col McCormick has bad diarrhoea. I am moving this morning my 17 patients (officers) into a ward of 40 beds</p>
