<p>My wound was very sore this morning and the "doc" reckons I should still be in hospital, so do I, but there are such a lot of wounded coming in that we had to get out, but it feels better now that it has been dressed. Some of the wounded that come in bear some awful wounds, and the poor beggars suffer agony, but it all in war. 20.1.17. Getting better Quickly, they took us to Rouen to the pictures yesterday the town looked good to me so I handed</p>
