  
 Saturday 6 April Parade 7.45 & marched off to [East?] Camp. Issued dinner & tea tickets. 
 Marched Fontinettes Station drew dinner & tea after great push boarded train thro. window. Left about 12 & went via Boulogne Abbeville to Canaples. 6.30 p.m. Marched in rain to Corps H.Q. well lost & stop in duty stable wet through.
