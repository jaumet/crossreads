<p>Wed 16<br/>good news to impart soon Wish they'd issue rum again Heard from M.T.L. Men are mostly fed up with this eternal fatigue racket &amp; stationary posts. More disheartening than fighting. Most men lousy.</p>
<p>Thur 17 Quiet Usual little firing. Aeroplane now &amp; again drops bombs during this period. Reported &ndash; first two trenches at Gaba Teke taken. Reported troops landed to North. &ndash; Bulgaria has come in also Greece. Cheval ill &amp; away also Johnson. Tom Called morn</p>
<p>Fri 18 Swim</p>
<p>Sat 19th Swim</p>
<p>Tea with Tite.</p>
<p>Sun 20th Heavy bombardment at Cape Helles all day &amp; evening Went down &amp; had yarn</p>
