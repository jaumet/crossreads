<p>exploded, you should have seen the rush hardly one of them thought about putting their helmets on, it was a catch alright &amp; caused a great laugh, you should have seen our officers run it was dead funny.<br/>To-day has been a great one amongst the French people, all the women &amp; girls have been out dressed in their best &amp; the little girls were all dressed in white just like a bride going to her wedding &amp; the small boys were all dressed in black suits like a man &amp; wore white ribbons and bows on their arm &amp; bowler hats on their heads, it must be some religious ceremony.<br/>Mr Hughes &amp; Fisher General Birdwood &amp; staff reviewed the Brigade to-day &amp; we also had Battalion Sports. So the boys have had a pretty lively day.</p>
