<p>Sunday 27 May</p>
<p>Fine and cloudless day.<br/>Church parades in the Church Army Hut two doors away.<br/>Despatch Riding duties continue smoothly.<br/>Leave to Amiens has reopened &ndash; parties of 8 may go daily.<br/>No word yet about leaving this part of the front.</p>
