<p>18 October 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Kantara <strike>W</strike> East.<br/>Remained here all today &amp; believe go Port Said tomorrow to No.14 AGH   I would of course prefer Cairo &amp; X to the wee wife but must go where I am sent- X<br/>This place is overun with German &amp; Turk prisoners dying like flies from Pneumonia Malaria etc:- they get the same attention as we do-<br/>All sorts of Rumours reach us-  Kaiser abdicated etc etc-<br/>X The cutting I asked for was <u>not</u> Diary but my first impressions of Jerusalem I think printed in Pastoral Times.X</p>
