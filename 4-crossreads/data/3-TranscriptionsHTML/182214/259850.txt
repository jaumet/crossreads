<p>We left Rail head &nbsp;at least A Section did &amp; went three miles further out in the desert to Duntroon Plateau. we were now about ten miles out in the desert from the canal &amp; seven miles as a crow flies, over soft sand &nbsp;we always prefered the road. Water was scarce out here &amp; we led a very simple life indeed we ran a small hospital &amp; most of us got sick in one way &amp;&nbsp;another &nbsp;Water was pumped out from near the Canal &amp; a guard with fixed bayonet was placed over the storage tanks sometimes the tanks were filled to overflowing then we used to rush over &amp; get a bath how lovely one felt afterwards, but we only got such luxeries once or twice while we were here</p>
