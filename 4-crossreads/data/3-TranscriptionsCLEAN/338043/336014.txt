  
 Nov. 11th As it is decreed that we must rise at 5 a.m., we do so, and are fortunate enough to see a wonderful & inspiring "red dawn". Could there be a more fitting & majestic herald of /to the joyous tidings which we receive a little later. At 11 a.m. hostilities cease, the Armistice has to be signed, and after four years of War & bloodshed, during which the Divine Laws of the Almighty have been/are ruthlessly corrupted, "Right prevails over Might - and - Honour, Truth, [added in a different ink] MISERY & DISTRESS
