 427 New Zealand Hospital Walton on Thames 11.11.15 Dear Father Thanks very much for your kind letter &ndash; I can't make out how you hadn't received one from me written from Epsom about August 6th. Still it may have turned up later &ndash; From this on I am going to make an effort to write to you once a week &ndash; I don't know why I can't write a decent letter &ndash; I read it over after I have written it and it doesn't seem to be worth posting.
