<p>9th M.G. Coy<br/>5-12.16<br/>Brigade Major<br/>9th Inf Bdge</p>
<p>I Re form 42/2 dated 4-12.16. The instructions contained in Brigade memo of 30th Nov were read and initialled by the Section Officers of this Company. Further instruction have been forwarded to the Officer concerned.<br/>II The officer in charge Left Sector.- Lieut Graham- explained to me last night when I visited him that the reason his report was late in reaching me was that his runner lost his way. This report reached me at 3.pm yesterday.</p>
<p>B.J Carey Capt<br/>9th M.G. Coy.</p>
