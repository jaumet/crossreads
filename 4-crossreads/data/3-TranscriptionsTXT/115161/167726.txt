 Absent-mindedness I suffered from to a stage at times bordering on imbecility.&nbsp; On many occasions I have got to the ticket office of a station and forgotten the name of the place to which I wish to go. 
 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ------------------------------------------ 
 On one occasion I remember instead of handing the conductress one penny for my fare which I had in my hand in readiness, I dropped in her palm the cigarette which I was smoking.&nbsp; I apologised but I am firmly convinced that she was under the impression that I did it on purpose. 
 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ---------------------------------------------- 
 Many a time have I arranged to meet someone at a certain place and when the time comes have wandered about in some other locality, blaming them the whole time for not keeping their appointment. 
 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ---------------------------------------------- 
 Times out of number have I left my latch key in my other tunic and either had to waken the land lady or risk a policeman seeing me crawling through a window, at dead of night.
