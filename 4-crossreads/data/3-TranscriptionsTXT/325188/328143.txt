 ALLAN LINE R.M.S. 
 On Route Egypt December 23rd 1915 At Sea 
 Mother Dear You see I got through alright &amp; enclose the last few days of my diary. These have been very fine days indeed &amp; you will see I have Faith enough to be thankful to the Almighty for a calm sea the night we left. I had for 7 hours at [indecipherable] about 600 yards of Trench from Camel Hump to No 1 out post &amp; 14 men to hold it with without any relief &ndash; Posted at intervals
