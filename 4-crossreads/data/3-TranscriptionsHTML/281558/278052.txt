<p>they are alright : went to a picture show &amp; it was pretty fair</p>
<p>Saturday Oct 20th</p>
<p>Westham : parade &amp; V.D. inspection in morn : went on leave 2 pm &amp; met Ern : walked to the "White horse" &amp; then to his Aunt's for tea : Ern tells me that I have been recommended but I suppose that will be my share of it</p>
<p>Sunday Oct 21st</p>
<p>Westham : fair day : church parade in morn &amp; nice too : leave 2 pm : stroll along promenade in afternoon : nice service in Parish Church after tea : rained as I came home</p>
<p>Monday Oct 22nd</p>
<p>Westham : parade in morn : classified in afternoon &amp; got marked for furlough : stroll to town after tea with Tom &amp; went to the Pavilion : revue on &amp; a</p>
