<p>the &quot;Rio Pardo&quot; hits Australia.&nbsp; The Sth Australians &amp; Victorians land and others of us <s>goe</s> go ashore to stretch our legs.&nbsp; Roy, as usual, provides &quot;home comforts&quot; and after supper we turn in and sleep nearer to Home than we have been for a long, long time.</p>
