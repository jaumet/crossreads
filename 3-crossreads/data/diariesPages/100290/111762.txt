1 January 1916
Saturday
What will the new Year bring forth?
 
Today marks the 5 month less 3 days since coming into camp. General leave today. Left camp at 10 am for Sydney en route to Master Sergeant Meadows self dined at Marine Leaf Drive. Met Robbo at Quay. Journeyed furiously. Went on Motor Boat trip with Misses Ferguson amp. Astbury amp. Mrs. Lureford also Harold Whittey Ryan struck an upturned boat off the Heads scarcely a smash. Landed for tea above . Home again 8 pm. Sing song walk around Fairy Bower. Ice cream see Bob off bunk at Millers for night.
