<p>At 5 o'clock all the men were "fallen in" "roll called" &amp; huts all left clean &amp; tidy for the next lot coming in, after the inspection was over, we marched off to Poperinge Siding, where we entrained, the boys were all as merry as could be &amp; the night was nice &amp; fine, just after we reached the Siding", the 4th Bn. came along &amp; its band played them to the train After about an hours delay our train an armoured one came in &amp; we got on her, &amp; as we were pulling out the band played "Auld Lang Syne" &amp; very nice it sounded in the warm twilight.</p>
