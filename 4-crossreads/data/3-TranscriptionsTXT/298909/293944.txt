 8/4/16  Saturday 
 The O.C. gave us a hint, that the leave embargo may be lifted, but we were all disappointed as we were all refused passes. Just tried to keep cool &ndash; have been lucky enough to get hold of a few books, to keep me from going ratty. After Tea went down to Bill Bedsors dug out &amp; swopped yarns &amp; memories with him till 11 pm.
