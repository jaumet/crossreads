<p>Shortly after daybreak we were at the rest camp at Hurdcott, where we were given hot coffee &amp; curry for breakfast in a long mess room. Then we were packed off to a hut for every 30 men. blankets &amp; straw filled mattress were placed on a three plank trestle bedstead about five inches above the floor. We were soon in slumberland &amp; remained there till close on dinner time after which we formed up for review before Colonel Abbott. Who approved us as a likely looking lot of men.<br/>January 2nd<br/>We were marched further</p>
