Everyone is aiming at a trip to Paris or Nice, but I think our sojourn in Hunland has been abandoned.  Charleroi is not more than 25 kilos from here, and everyone has a good word for its gaiety. Mauberge too is not far (22 km) & we cart our rations every day from here to there.
