<p>1v.</p>
<p>W.E.Gillett enlisted in the Australian Imperial Forces 6 Mar. 1916, at Perth, Western Australia, was promoted to lance-corporal Feb. 1917 and later to sergeant. He served in France from Dec.1917, probably with D Company, 51&nbsp;<strike>21</strike>st Battalion. He transferred to a clerical position in 1917 and was awarded the Meritorious Service Medal in 1919. He served at the A.I.F. Headquarters, London from Feb. 1919 to May 1920, returning to Australia 30 July 1920</p>
