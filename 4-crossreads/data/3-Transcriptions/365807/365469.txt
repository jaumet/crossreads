<p>Mrs Leibius wrote to Lieut. Col. Ralston on Feb. 27th as follows:</p>
<p>"We have not yet heard any definite news of the arrival of a great quantity of Xmas parcels we sent as well as 2000 Xmas puddings, &pound;50 worth tobacco and over 1000 tins of sweets.</p>
<p>M.D.<br/>for Mrs Leibius</p>
