 22 Th February Moved to Sheikh Zawaied  as we were going along beach a Taube was over observing  travelled all day reached Sheikh about 4 pm tired &amp; dusty very fine country en route was warned for picquet as soon as we reached camp 2nd B &amp; NZ. B. moved to Rafa where they engaged the enemy gave horses feed of green barley [See also p 187] 
 23 Fri February Fixing a temporary camp in Hod  2nd NZ Bgde arrived back from Rafa they drove Turks out of their front line trenches our casualties were very slight 53 inf arrived expect a big go in a few weeks our camp is crawling with Camel ticks  I have been scratching like Hell [in margin] Rum Issue 
 24 Sat February Taubs over observing  no bombs  had Mike out grazing  Turks occupied Rafa  2nd Bgd moving out to keep in touch
