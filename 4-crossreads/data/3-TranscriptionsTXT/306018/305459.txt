 September 1917 Thursday 20th Went to Kafa in the morning to get some canteen stuff.  It was nearly dark when I got back.  The Sergeants mess got in a supply of stuff. 
 Friday 21st It is still very windy &amp; dusty.  There is nothing much to do here excepting feeding grooming &amp; watering.  I took over SQMS in the afternoon.   We are expecting a turn out  at night to see how long it takes to form up in full marching order with 48 hrs feed &amp; rations limbers &amp; waggons loaded. 
 Saturday 22nd The dust was not so bad today. 
 Sunday 23rd Went over to the 7th in the morning  Got some mail English.  Wrote a letter home.  There is a Brigade band now &amp; they entertain the various camps here.  The weather is fairly warm but there is always dust in the afternoon. 
 Monday 24th Usual sort of day.  Nothing doing.
