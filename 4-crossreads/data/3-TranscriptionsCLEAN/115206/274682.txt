  
 April 1918 Monday 22 
 Bussy - la - Daours   D.R. Went out on reconnaissance with Russell and shewed him plenty of the back areas, - second positions. Weather beautiful, fine and clear. Fritz dropped his advance  bombs for supper last night. Fritz shelling lightly back areas. Cleaned up all my saddle appointments. Wrote seven letters. Bomb went up to Aubigny on Buzzer Unit.
