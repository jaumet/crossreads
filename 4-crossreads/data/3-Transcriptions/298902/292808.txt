<p>Orders out for us to move a distance of 35 miles in 3 stages &amp; the Colonel has given me absolute freedom to make what arrangements I think best for the whole move, could anyone do otherwise than his best when so treated?</p>
<p>Monday 16th Dec.  Orders through for our move to take place in 3 stages starting from the 18th &amp; to end at Sivry in Belgium which is to be our winter quarters.  Had an easy day, just making arrangements for the packup tomorrow.  Wrote some letters thus making a start on my back correspondence.</p>
<p>Tuesday 17th Dec  The 4th Anniversary of my enlistment in the Army.  Spent the day collecting all our gear from the different departments such as dentists, dispensary etc etc &amp; taking it to the wagons for loading.  Completed all loading by dark ready for the march tomorrow.</p>
