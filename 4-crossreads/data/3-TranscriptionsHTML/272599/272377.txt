<p>1917 Monday 16 April</p>
<p>Duties as usual.  Roadmaking round Dressing Station. bricks from fallen walls  <br/>Finer, today  <br/>Active cannonade all along front.  We miss the "Daily Mail" up here feel quite lost without it. Hear scraps of news from one source or another.  <br/>Get more letters from Mary 18th Feb. Margot. Wall &amp; Jessie, Nurse Murell. A Comfort Depot &amp; Y.M.C.A. open up in the village. Both most opportune as rations very scarce &amp; often one wishes for something to chew between times.  If one can get in early he is fortunate to get chocolate, biscuits &amp; tinned fruit.</p>
