<p>13.1.15</p>
<p>Wed 13th<br/>Were signalling in the usual spot all day, aligning the helio on to station.<br/>an officer from Brde. H.Q. was with us &amp; had the watch on us, 90 seconds is the time allotted to set a helio for a test &amp; I smashed all records here by doing it in 50 seconds.  I had a good deal of luck &amp; could not do it again in six months.</p>
<p>about ten o'clock this morning the funeral of the lad who died yesterday passed by us.  we stood to the salute as the funeral passed by; the coffin was covered with the Union Jack &amp; his hat on top.  he was taken to the Citadel &amp; buried there. of course he had a proper military</p>
