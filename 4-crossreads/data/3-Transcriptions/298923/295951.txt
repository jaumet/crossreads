<p>12 October 1918 Saturday<br/>Move on to <strike>Es Salt</strike> Ain Hennar today feeling better but weak-<br/>Moved on by Motor Ambulance- the driver on arriving here whispered in my ear<br/>"Any chance of giving"<br/>"me your Revolver" ?  It shows what chance an insensible patient would have to keep his Kit-<br/>I am not condeming all-<br/>Muscular injection by Hyperdermic syringe of Quinine well pushed in is the thing-</p>
