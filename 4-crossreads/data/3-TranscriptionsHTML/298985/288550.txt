<p>Wednesday 11th<br/>Moved of at 9.15 &amp; marched through Perone &amp; one or two other smaller places ariveing no where in particular at 12 only had 2 short spells. It was raining slightly most of the time rather an improvement than otherwise. Plenty of iron to be had <strike>at</strike> close where a <strike>dressing station</strike> Hospital had been burnt. Made quite a good <strike>one</strike> home</p>
