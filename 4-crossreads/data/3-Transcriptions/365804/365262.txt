I found mine yesterday hidden in a pair of socks at the bottom of my trunk, so have just hastened to return his. It was very decent of him and he did it in such a way that I couldn't refuse.</p>
<p>Jack Mant is in a signalling school at present and won't get ver to France for a month or two yet if all goes well. He should get an N.C.O.s job out of it.</p>
<p>I haven't got the faintest idea where we will be posted, but think somewhere about the Ypres salient. Anyway I will be able to find out about Little and where Arthur's belongings have gone.</p>
<p>I have written to May Scott-Skirving, Mr.Pring and Miss Miles (Mr O'Reilly's cousin)</p>
