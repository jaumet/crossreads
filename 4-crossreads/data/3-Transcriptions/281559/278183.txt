<p>Wednesday June 25th at Sea</p>
<p>Quiet time : arrived off Adelaide about midnight</p>
<p>Thursday June 26th Adelaide</p>
<p>Dr aboard about 10 am &amp; had our temperatures taken again : we have had them taken each morn since leaving Albany : drew in to the wharf about 2 pm : good crowd : got a few snaps : after our "S.A.s" had disembarked we were given leave till midnight : Gordon &amp; self went up to town &amp; it seems a decent city : met Gibs &amp; he took us round to his people's place &amp; we had an enjoyable couple of hours :</p>
