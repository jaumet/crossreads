<p>Friday 2/2/17.<br/>same old thing snow still here &amp; more coming<br/>53rd raided Fritz last night.<br/>letter from Maude last night &amp; Papers from Mrs Parsons<br/>Pay &pound;2.12.9 &pound;20.6.0</p>
<p>Saturday 3/2/17<br/>9. am expecting to get away to day some time painful operation of shaving to be gone through<br/>heavy frost, big crowd back from blighty last night.<br/>Pay 2-13-9. &pound;20.7.0</p>
<p>Sunday 4/2/17.<br/>Terrific Bombardment last night went on for hours, must have been big advance. Still going strong 10. a.m.<br/>Expect to leave for Blighty today.<br/>Pay 2.14.9. &pound;20.8.0</p>
<p>Monday 5/2/17<br/>still here,<br/>last night an attack was made &amp; a 2 mile front taken Guns Gee Whiz I guess there were some<br/>Poor Lieutenant Vogan died from his wounds one of the best fellows I have met over here a perfect gentleman God take him. A lot of boys getting wounded.<br/>Pay 2-15-9 20-9-0</p>
<p>Tuesday 6/2/17<br/>600 Prisoners brought in yesterday, giving Fritz hell lately, going strong this morning (the Artillery).<br/>Pay 2-16-9. 20.10.9</p>
<p>Wednesday 7/2/17<br/>been bitterly cold these last 3 days North wind blowing<br/>nothing important<br/>Pay 2-17-9 &pound;20.11-0</p>
