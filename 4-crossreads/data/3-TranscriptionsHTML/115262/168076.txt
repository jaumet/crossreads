<p>Monday 5 November 1917.</p>
<p>Brigade Orderly despatch till 10.30 am.</p>
<p>At 2.30 pm A.C.F.A moved to main road &amp; relieved N.Z&nbsp;Mtd Fd Amb at 4.30 pm when A Sec. moved out at dusk to the front line for active service. I was in the group as advanced dressing station for the 2<sup>nd</sup> Battn I.C.C. Bgde.</p>
<p>The whole Bgde was completely lost &amp; never got into communication before midnight tonight when N.Z.&#39;s were relieved.</p>
<p>El Jabri<br />El Hawi<br />Ras-el&nbsp;Nag<br />Kuteelfah</p>
