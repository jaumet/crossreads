
November, 1915
Monday 29
After a bitterly cold wind last night, woke up to find snow this morning.
Bitterly cold wind blowing all day: simply impossible to keep feet & hands warm.
It is miserable working all Several water tanks frozen & pipes burst so that [indecipherable] us [indecipherable] to quarter rations of water or just enough for a couple of small drinks a day as tea.
