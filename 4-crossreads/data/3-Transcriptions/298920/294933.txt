Strange to say, the day has been the coldest we have had since leaving England.  No training, being Sunday.<br/><u>Monday 30th August 1915.</u>  Owing to diplomatic relations between the Purser &amp; 3rd Mate being broken there will not in future be any wheezing and crooning antics in concert.  About 9am we sighted a big school of porpises a few hundred yards away, sporting themselves in the sun.  There must have been 40 or 50</p>
