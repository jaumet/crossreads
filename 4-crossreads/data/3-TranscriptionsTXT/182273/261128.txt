May   1918
12 SUN  Dull day. Washing
3 Sergts came
down from guns. Fairly 
quiet a few bombs at
night
13 MON  Raining on & off all
day  Rec. letters from Tom
& Peggy. Writing at night
Capt. MalleyM.C. paid us a
visit. Very quiet. 
14 TUES  Posted letter & photo
to Peggy. Fine day. Very
quiet. Planes active
Rumour out re pulling
out on 20th. Bombs at night
15 WED  Fine hot day. Very
warm. Guns very active
Amm: going up.
Few bombs at night.
Quiet. Played 3rd Bty.
Cricket Match.
