<p>at 2.30 p.m.  The behaviour of the men throughout was extremely good.  They are quietly determined to carry on with their present attitude.  They say that they are willing to go into <strike>the</strike> a fight tomorrow if they are allowed to go as the 54th Bn. but they absolutely refuse to <strike>go</strike> fight with another unit.</p>
