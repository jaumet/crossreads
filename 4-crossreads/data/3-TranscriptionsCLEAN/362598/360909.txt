
Sunday 23 January 1916Monday 24 January 1916
Tuesday 25 January 1916Went alongside Wharf.
Wednesday 26 January 1916Left Suez at 7 a.m. for Cairo.  Arrived at Abbassia at 4 p.m.
Thursday 27 January 1916Watched Dressing in Morning & started work at 2 p.m.
Friday 28 January 1916Bubs Dressings all day.
Saturday 29 January 1916Went to Cairo for first time.  Met C. Board & McDonnell.  Came home in Chariot with H. O'Keefe & Roy Farnsworth, Geoff Clarke.  A Gallop all the way.
