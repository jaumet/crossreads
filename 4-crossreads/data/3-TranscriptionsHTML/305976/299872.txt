Novelty to us<br/>Wednesday June 20<br/>Disembarked at daybreak. Our first introduction to the Egyptian who put the hard word on us for Cigarettes &amp; Backsheesh  as he called it. Boarded some cattle trucks &amp; away we went. Imagine our condition, a boiling hot day, full dress, an open truck and travelling up till 1 O,clock practically through sand.
