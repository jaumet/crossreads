At 10.30 we tasted &amp; paid for, the Institute's eggs &amp; toast and then proceeded along Corporation St in search of the premises of Messrs Sames, piano makers &amp; salesmen, to whom Alan had a card of introduction from Palings.</p>
<p>We found the retail city shop &amp; thence were directed to the factory &amp; arrived at the latter at 12.15 &amp; were cordially welcomed by Mr Sames Sen. &amp; his two sons &amp; after being shown over the premises, were taken into town again in Mr Leonard Sames' car &amp; had dinner (a little turkey etc) at the "Midland's" Hotel.</p>
