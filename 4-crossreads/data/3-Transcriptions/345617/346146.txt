<p>French cathedrals the internal fittings are very elaborate &amp; beautiful. Had lunch at the E. Canteen &amp; dinner about 5p.m. at a French caf&eacute;. The Royal Fusiliers were playing good music in the park during the afternoon. The appear to be a good many Australians in the Military Hospital at St Omer.</p>
<p>Aout 13<br/>Had an easy day today. General clean up &amp; overhaul of equipment. Inspection of Coy by O.C. in the afternoon.</p>
