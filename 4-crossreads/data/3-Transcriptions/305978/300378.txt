<p>&amp; some 500 sheep. behind all the latter were brought into camp. We killed ten sheep for the squadron &amp; kidded we would have a feed of fresh meat &amp; make up for Xmas Day.<br/>The cooks boiled them with the bully beef &amp; the meat was so rank that it upset the stew. no more fresh meat on the desert in future.<br/>Some of the boys from NSWs received parcels so had cake &amp; plum pudding for a change. There was one thing wrong &amp; that was that there was not enough of it.</p>
