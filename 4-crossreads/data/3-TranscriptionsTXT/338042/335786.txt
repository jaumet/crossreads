 Jan. 8th. Early rise &ndash; Pack up &amp; Load Lorries &ndash; leave Sheenwerck, which proved to be safest town in which we have had the pleasure of showing &ndash; Arrive at Bailleul 11 a.m. (last in this town in July 1916) Afternoon spent in preparing Hall &amp; Stage for Panto. &ndash; For first time in France I become the proud possessor of a Private Billet.
