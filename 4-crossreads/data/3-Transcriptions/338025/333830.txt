<p>Saturday 27th October 1017<br/>Reninghelst<br/>Grooming and harness cleaning.</p>
<p>Sunday 28th<br/>4.30am Fed my two mules. Breakfast, harnessed up and moved out with wagon at 6am. One load of duck boards from R.E.Dump to Dickebusch to 4th bde. H.L ne Howe dump arr home 9pm.</p>
