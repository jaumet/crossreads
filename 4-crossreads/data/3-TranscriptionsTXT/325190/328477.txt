 Owing to his peregrinations in leading me to Poperinge, Eva is now the "surveyor" or the "guide", &amp; we laugh a good deal over such quips.  While Gray &amp; I are in the refreshment room, talking war to a semi-religious French Canadian with no great insight into the ways of nations; Pinky &amp; Eva are playing "house" in an estaminet.
