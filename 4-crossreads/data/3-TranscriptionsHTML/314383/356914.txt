<p>Everything appears to be working satisfactorily.</p>
<p>Later the Sidar just came across and said that he has got definite &amp; accurate news that our friends are at Sain Kala.  Got a special runner to ride back for Reid.</p>
<p>Aug 1st<br/>News confirmed by a traveller from the North.  Scotty, who is acting as Q.M. buying rice dried fruit tea &amp; sugar all day.</p>
