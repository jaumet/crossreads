<p>Sunday 26 August 1917<br/>Swimming horses, very bad headache from inoculation.  Picquet at night.</p>
<p>Monday 27 August 1917<br/>Swimming horses in morning.  Pictures at Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Tuesday 28 August 1917<br/>Swimming horses &amp; a good surf:  thumb pretty sore.</p>
<p>Wednesday 29 August 1917<br/>Exercising horses.  Got paid 30/-.</p>
<p>Thursday 30 August 1917<br/>Exercising horses in morning:  swimming horses afterwards.</p>
<p>Friday 31 August 1917<br/>Firing practice in morning.  Exercise in evening.</p>
<p>Saturday 1 September 1917<br/>Firing practice in morning and exercise in evening.  Picquet at night.</p>
