 January, 1916 Friday 14 Work as usual during the day Go to Helmia camp pictures at night w/- Dad. Nights very cold &amp; it is hard getting up at 5.30 am 
 Saturday 15 Exam in Description takes up best part of the morning My class do rather will. After lunch I go into Cairo have dinner &amp; go to Pictures Turn in about 11 pm
