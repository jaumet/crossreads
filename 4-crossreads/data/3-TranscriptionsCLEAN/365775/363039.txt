  
 through pretty little villages & now through green fields and waving crops, many of them falling to the reaper & binder. one thing most conspicuous was the absence of man labour, all the harvesting is done by the women & children, they seem to me to be doing all the work in the fields & ably doing it too. however shall tell you more of the work done by the French women later. shall endeavour just now to stick to my trip across.
