<p>Fritz, hammering sawing &amp; hacking   sometimes a shovel swings into view This happens too often sometimes &amp; our guns put a few rapid salvos into him bursting the shells all round the busy part, but again the situation is often reversed &amp; we get them at us, but one thing is absolutely certain which ever side starts it the other is bound to retaliate &amp; we knock off work for an hour or so &amp; let the guns fight it out. I suppose the gunners of both sides reckon they are chastising one another by shelling the trenches of the unfortunate infantry who have no say in the quarrel at all<br/>Next stop dinner, &amp; after this as a rule all hands have a camp for we get very little sleep at night but often our rest is broken for</p>
