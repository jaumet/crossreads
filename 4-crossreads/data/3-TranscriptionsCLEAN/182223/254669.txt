  
 Monday 7 June 1915 Usual routine work today  260 patients in hospital  Supper to Captain Williams at night in Heliopolis. Met Dr Brennan who has come from the Dardanelles, he told us something about the fighting, but he appeared to me to be himself mentally upset.  Posted letters to several people at home. Tomlins & others 
 Tuesday 8 June 1915 Letters posted to Bridge, Nan Johnnstone, Jas Roach, Dr Paton, Matron Sherwood  Busy all day admitting & discharging patients. Wrote to Mrs Thomson (Cooma), Kathleen Bryan, & Sister Blanch Sutcliffe. 
 Wednesday 9 June 1915 103 men sent to Australia by the Kyarra this morning.  Office & 4  ae  anaesthetics filled up the day.  Went Heliopolis for Supper. Brasserie de l'avenir. Fish  bread and lemonade  Home about 12 midnight.
