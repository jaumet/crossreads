<p>now &amp; we have lots of French Soldiers billetted in neighbouring farms &ndash; tough hardy looking men they are I have no idea how one got the idea that Frenchmen are small men &ndash; They are every bit as big as the Britisher</p>
<p>Their infantry on the march do not seem to keep any regular step &ndash; each to his own stride in a sort of shuffle, but they get along at a great pace There transport seems very rough &amp; ready &ndash; vehicles of all descriptions &ndash; not <strike>two</strike></p>
