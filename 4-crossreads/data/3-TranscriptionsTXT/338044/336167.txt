13th May:- SaturdayHot, I should not like to describe it otherwise. The wind is about two blasts from hell combined & one is afraid of his hair being burnt. Only 110  in the shade, but awful. Those fellows who got away in time to avoid this heat are more than lucky. This afternoon I took some photos out at Giza    &  tonight attended a picture show (the usual Egyptian production)
