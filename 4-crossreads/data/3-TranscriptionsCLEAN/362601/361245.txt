
31 July, Thursday
Meet H Deigre.
Catch 9.30 to Rockdale. M & I got to get M's skirt. It is a failure & we take it back. We have a very nice time. See Winnie at night. I introduce W. to Mrs Nelson. I go to Cuneo's & meet Ida Mul. We talk so we sit on the mat until 1 am. I sleep in dining room.
1 August, Friday
I catch 8.50 with Laura. Go shopping. Meet Dave. Visit Dulcie. Meet Roy & Jack. Go to Charlie Jones & Marlborough St. We play snooker & I win well. We meet Massie.
I go Home very tired. Knocked out.
