 May 1917 20th.   To-day the section was working about &frac12; a mile this side of Noreuil at the advance dressing station, putting in cupola dugouts in place of rough shelters  the  which they have had of late.   No 2 section have been working on them for the last three days with the help of a few of our section.   Fritz shelled us during the afternoon but no casualties resulted. The weather continues fine &amp; the aeroplanes have been very active. 21st.
