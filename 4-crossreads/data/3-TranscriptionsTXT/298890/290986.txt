 14. Sunday Very cold &amp;  grey.  Went to hear Rev. Campbell Morgan at 11.a.m and Usshers  at 4.  Leaving them at 9 p.m. 
 15. Monday Wet dull day.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10 &amp; home at 6. 
 16. Tuesday Agnes Nestor rang up at 9.30.  Went to Miss Newcomb &amp; left late at 5.45.  Snow on housetops &amp; slushy in streets. 
 17. Wednesday. Cold showery day.  Went to Miss N. at 10. Down to Bond St. for her after lunch &amp;  home &ndash; tired &amp; depressed.
