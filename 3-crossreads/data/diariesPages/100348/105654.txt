22/12/16.
Went into support. A good duckboard track all the way now. We have shelter in large tunnels excavated in the chalk. We are crowded into a sitting position all the time but it is dry, thank Heaven! There is no ventilation and the shortage of fresh air is very noticeable; no wonder, with all the steaming humanity that is packed into the place. Did fatigue half the night. The downpour of rain is increasing.
