<p>January, 1916<br/>Saturday 8<br/>The 9/13 march out at 7 am &amp; I do likewise shortly after.  Take my baggage down to school in the afternoon.  Dad's Bn. remove back to Zeitoun from Aerodrome today.</p>
<p>Sunday 9<br/>Write letter home.  Spend the day reading up work for next week.  Meet several old friends back from the front, one is now Sgt. in my place &amp; is one of three old hands left in the Platoon</p>
