
Tuesday 6 May 1919 Went out & arranged to start class tomorrow. Got Motor drivers license. Took Janet, Marie & Violet out to Wandsworth to see Bill. He looks very seedy. Went back to the hospital with them & had tea there. Matron called us in at 10 pm & we had a talk & then she took us down & showed us how to make coffee It was goodo too. Drew  20.
Wednesday 7 May 1919Started the school today & got on very well. Had my first driving lesson & did OK. Went out & saw Bill & met his girl for him at 7.30. Came home & wrote letters.
