<p>[16 Th]<br/>Mounted piquet at 6 pm.</p>
<p>[17 Fri]<br/>Grooming, and fixing the jumps etc. for our sports tomorrow.</p>
<p>[18 Sat]<br/>Sports started at 1pm but had to prospond them on account of rain  3.30 pm harnessed mules into mess cart and took Lieut Mar's [kit?] to rest camp near Longchamps. Arr there just on sunset. Tied mules to trees and slept under [cart?] as it was a lovely night</p>
