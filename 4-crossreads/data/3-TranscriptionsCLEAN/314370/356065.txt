  
 August, 1916 Saturday 5 same as yesterday read a good deal.  have got hold of a book worth reading "Valley of the Moon" Jack London 
 Sunday 6 Reached Malta about 6 am & spent about 7 hours in Masa Scirocco harbour 8 pm just come off signal bridge 16 knots. Anniversary of our attack on Suvla Bay & the Lone Pine charge
