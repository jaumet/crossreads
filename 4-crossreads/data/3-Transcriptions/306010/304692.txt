<p>two of used to join every night in the "promenade" which is such a feature of French seaside resorts. To do this properly meant staying out after hours (9 pm) &amp; we used ti sally forth togged up like officers with cap &amp; stick and wearing British warm. I found out later that the OC could issue late passes so we became dinkum Australians again.<br/>I discovered a tennis court on the other side of the village &amp; one of our NCOs introduced me to a family of French refugees from Lille who showed me how to set about joining.
