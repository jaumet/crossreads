
<p>288</p>
<p>1917 FEBRUARY</p>
<p>23 FRIDAY</p>
<p>Fine but dull &amp; misty. We</p>
<p>were called out at 1 AM</p>
<p>to take ammunition up to</p>
<p>the left section down in</p>
<p>Belgium, got back &amp; loaded</p>
<p>at the Croix du Bac dump</p>
<p>&amp; home about 8 AM. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness &amp; horses all day. Was</p>
<p>dead tired at night &amp; turened</p>
<p>straight in. I got my other</p>
<p>boot from Poss &amp; a parcel</p>
<p>of Champion from the Civil</p>
<p>Service.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
