 Thursday 5 Dec. Flew about fixing up pass etc until 10.30 when I hurriedly left the old boys in the box car. 
 Arrived Abbeville R.S. &amp; caught civilian train to Boulogne arriving there about 2.30 p.m. Had dinner booked a bed at the Grand Louvre. Strolled about in afternoon visited the pictures &amp; after more strolling finished up with a dinner. 
 Got to bed about 10 pm but slept a restless night.
