<p>3.<br/>a language which I claim to be the only Volap&uuml;k &amp; Esperanto will be right off the market within six months. It bears no resemblance whatever to any human speech, &amp; has no foolish prejudices about grammar or pronunciation. The chief rule is to talk very loud, &amp; pay no attention whatever to anything the other man is saying, but unfortunately I can't copyright that part of it &ndash; it is too fragrant an invitation of J.L.</p>
