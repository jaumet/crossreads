<p>January, 1917. Monday 22<br/>Usual routine, one truck. Home at 2.30. Wrote letters and had a bath after tea in dugout. Move in a day or two for sure. Very cold and more snow to-day &ndash; Artillery active on front to-night. No 3 Section got shelled coming from work the other night &ndash; Murray shell shock.</p>
