
Altogether Ferry post is a wonderfully busy spot a perfect hive of industry.
Sunday 9th April.More pit digging before breakfast.   Attended church parade during the morning.  In the afternoon we had a game of cricket but the sun is too hot for play so strenuous.
Monday10thSpent the day assisting dentist as he was short handed.  Only made a few fillings and did some clerical work.
Tuesday 11thMore pit digging in morning & swim in afternoon.
Wednesday 12thOn guard for the day.  At night a strong wind storm blew up with a little rain & all hands were into securing the tents several of which collapsed.
Thursday 13thA lull in the morning during which time I took on bricklaying & built a range & chimney for the cooks.  The wind blew up again about 10 A.M. & continued all day.
