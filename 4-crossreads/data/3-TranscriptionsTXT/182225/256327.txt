JULY   THURSDAY 27   1916
A nice hot day. Alan brought car round in morning & we went & had our photos taken Then went on and picked up Mrs Angus & we all went down to Bletchinly tosee Dr Fred Robertson he has a great we place down there. When we got back we picked Auntie Flo up & we all went down to Hampton Court & had tea & went for a row on the river afterwards. We got home & dropped mother & then took the others home & we were yarning there till nearly 2 pm. Its a bonsar car, but it was not running to well today.
