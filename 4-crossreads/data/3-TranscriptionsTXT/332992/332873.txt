They borrowed a piano from the Y.C.A. in Steinwerck.  The programme consisted mostly of comic songs [indecipherable]The doctors were present &amp; the Colonel presided.Sunday papers to hand are mostly a rehash of the weeks news.  There is another raider on the Atlantic.  She is a sailing ship with some form of combustion engines.  She carries the Norwegian flag &amp; shows signals of distress to attract her victims.  A French barque has landed at Rio de Janeiro 200 survivors from sunken vessels off Trinidad.
