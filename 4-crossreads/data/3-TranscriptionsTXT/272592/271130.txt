
to ride bicycles as far as Lille Gate which is at the other end of Ypres right on the edge of the moat, the party all told consisted of 4 bicyclists & 4 horsemen for the officers rode their horse's it was a very trying ride for the roads were very heavy & in some places muddy, we had to take our dinner with us mine consisted of a piece of dry bread & cheese, the horse's started off in front of us but we caught them just as we reached the "Cloth Hall" in Ypres & both pulled up at "Lille Gate after putting our horses & bicycles in a safe position for the Germans still shell all round
