<p>December  1918.<br/>Sunday 1</p>
<p>Wembley. On furlough. Slept until noon. Had a glorious hot tub, and spent a pleasant afternoon.<br/>Went to Church and held hands after service.<br/>Mrs Jones is picking up very rapidly indeed and I sincerely hope she will soon be her old self.<br/>Made arrangements to take Eve to theatre on Tuesday night. Cnr of Oxford St and Ttham Ct Road at 6.30 pm. <br/>Wrote E and Mother, also old "Doc" Sharpe.<br/>Made arrangements for a party on Wed next also.</p>
