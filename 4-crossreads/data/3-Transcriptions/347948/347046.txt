<p>2nd  A new term has come  to light Viz. "Windbag" this expression has become popular and refers to persons who have more to say than to do.</p>
<p>3rd  The Y.M.C.A. phonograph on deck is a great boon.  Concert on deck.  Often of a night the boys have what they call a menagerie and as every body thinks he is a good imitator the noise is deafening.</p>
<p>4th  Another amusement is to tie a lashing rope on a hammock and swing a chap backwards and forwards in the middle of the night, or, if he is sleeping on the floor</p>
