  
 [Friday 22 September 1916] 
 Saw Vicar of St Anne re Special Licence Bought Ring etc!!! arranged wedding for Oct 2nd only parents to be there- Grey day & drizzling-   Went to Blackpool to Theatre there saw " Tonights the night " 1000s of people a very gay place- not so nice as St Annes on Sea- Had Dinner in Blackpool Dolly & I spent our day together & are very happy- but I do  not  like crowds.
