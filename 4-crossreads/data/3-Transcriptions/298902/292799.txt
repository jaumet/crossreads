<p>Mr Harper had gone to meet me.  Had breakfast then a sleep until lunch, after which went out for a stroll round the town to get some rubbers for my boots.  Bought a Christmas number of the Graphic for Mum, so that I have now sent them each a Christmas number.  Streets very busy &amp; packed the Glasgow Gals throwing glad eyes ad lib to the Soldiers as usual.  Back again for dinner &amp; had a good old yarn, turning in about 11.</p>
<p>Saturday 7th Dec  Had breakfast about 9.35 after which we went for a walk with Mr Harper into the country round Glasgow.  Went around the town with Will in the afternoon &amp; at night tried to get into one of the numerous picture shows but they were all packed with queues lined up outside.  Every house of amusement was the same.  The streets were packed with people &amp;</p>
