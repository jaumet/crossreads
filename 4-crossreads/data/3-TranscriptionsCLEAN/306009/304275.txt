  
 1915 Mar. 30 Tue. Went into Cairo in the afternoon & got from Post Office a parcel containing Two novels Two prs. socks 1 diary toothbrush Cake chocolate 
 How glad I was to get hold of it & how decent of those at home to think of me. 
 Also had a haircut & went for a walk through Esbekieh Gardens. Beautiful moonlight night again.
