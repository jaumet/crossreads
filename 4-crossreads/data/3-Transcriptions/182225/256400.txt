<p>1916 &nbsp; SUNDAY 8 &nbsp; OCTOBER</p>
<p>Fine again. One of the fellows in the hut got the measles in the morning &amp; we were all put in quarintine, but like most other military things its a farce. The 7<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;Brigade played the [?] 3<sup>rd&nbsp;</sup>Infantry officers &amp; we beat them very easily. Poss Nivison &amp; self &amp; Jack went the pictures in evening &amp; they were rotten.</p>
