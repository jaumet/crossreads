<p>30th Went on 4 days leave to London.</p>
<p>Arrived at Waterloo 11.30 am.  I only had &pound;1 to keep me for the 4 days so I had <br/>to go very light.  On every big station in <strike>Waterloo</strike> London is a buffet for <br/>soldiers where a cup of tea or coffee and cakes &amp; bread &amp; butter may be had <br/>free  So I went down and had some refreshments then made off for Buckingham Gate <br/>Soldiers home where I booked my rooms and left my coat. I then went to the Anzac Club <br/>where free dinners</p>
