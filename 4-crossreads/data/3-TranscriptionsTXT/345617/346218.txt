
Octobre 23Sec. out laying sleeper Rd at Birr X Rds. Very nasty weather. Good news tonight about French advance on our Left.
Octobre 24Sec. out laying sleeper Rd at Birr X Rds. Shelling normal. Reported French have captured 6,000 prisoners & 60 guns. Somewhere near Graon. Also all their objectives. French have brought 4 Zeppelins & 4 more are wandering about in a crippled condition. They were returning from a raid on England.
