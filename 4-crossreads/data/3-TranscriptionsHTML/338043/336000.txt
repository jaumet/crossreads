<p>Oct. 26th<br/>Rehearsal of "Monkey's Paw" etc. In this town of Abbeville my time is well occupied &ndash; but I manage to join Y.M.C.A. Library, my first book being "Wisdom &amp; Destiny" by M. Maeterlinck.<br/>To-night's performance the first of the week &ndash;</p>
