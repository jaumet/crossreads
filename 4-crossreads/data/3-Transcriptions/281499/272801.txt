<p>&amp; he could not say anything so he took it lying down &amp; walked on, when we got a bit away we had a great laugh over it.<br/>I have been busy writing letters all day today for one of my mates is going to England on leave in a day or so &amp; I am getting some ready for him.<br/>Where we are camped now is right amongst the gun's &amp; tonight they had a bombardment &amp; they were roaring all round us 75s 18lbs 6 inchers &amp; all the rest of them one of our aeroplanes was directing the fire, &amp; Fritz was firing at him like mad with his machine guns but the plane took no notice of them but kept on flying close in &amp; signalling the effects</p>
