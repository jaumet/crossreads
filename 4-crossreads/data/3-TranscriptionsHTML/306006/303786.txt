<p>22-3-17<br/>came in &amp; said Jessie was waiting in the car, so we went around to the R.S.M. &amp; I told him I wanted to leave then &amp; would meet the train in Cairo he agreed; said Goodby to Ben &amp; Jessie Mathews &amp; I went to the Hospital as I thought Hills was coming to Zeitoun station to see me off, but we did not meet her, but was told she had left twenty minutes earlier, so we went back to Zeitoun station but she was not there, so we went into the Cairo Station through Matarieh &amp; found Hills on the station, Jessie had to be back on duty at 5 oclock so we parted at the station Mathews taking her back &amp; Hills &amp; I drove to Groppis' had afternoon</p>
