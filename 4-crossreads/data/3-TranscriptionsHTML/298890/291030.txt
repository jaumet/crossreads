<p>15 Sunday<br/>Lovely day.  Sunny and soft.  Wrote &amp; went to odious church in morning.  Afternoon listened to the band with Bessie Allin &amp; Nurse Grey &amp; read an E.M. Dell book.</p>
<p>16 Monday<br/>Grey day.  Finished novel</p>
<p>17 Tuesday<br/>Fine day.  Went to Felpham such a lovely little church  &amp; thatched cottages</p>
<p>18 Wednesday<br/>Fine day.  Went for walk with Miss Woledge before dinner.  Feeling very depressed &amp; week.</p>
