Sunday 15 December
Move this morning at 11 o'clock arrive at Solre St. Gery this afternoon.
Solre St. Gery is just out of Beaumont is a very quaint old village. We move on again to-morrow at 8 a.m. The country is fine, undulating seems to be of great agricultural wealth well inhabited. We are now out of France in Belgium which is a different Belgium to Ypres!!
Monday 16 December
Move again to-day to Gourdinne on a very circuitous route via Walcourt, which is a decent small town. Wet. The country we are passing through is magnificent very fertile closely inhabited cultivated.
