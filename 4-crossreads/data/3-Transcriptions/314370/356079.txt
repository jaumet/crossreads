<p>September, 1916<br/>Saturday 2<br/>Route march for troops. but I am detailed for other work &amp; miss it.<br/>After dinner went into Grantham.  evening to pictures, returning to camp at 11 pm are startled by loud hooter, all lights doused &amp; traffic stopped.  "ZEP. RAID"<br/>But it never came near us.  English people panic stricken at the very thought of it.</p>
<p>Sunday 3<br/>No report out yet about ZEP.  Morning cold &amp; dark.</p>
