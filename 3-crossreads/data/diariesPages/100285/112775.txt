29th. Thank Heaven I feel a tiny bit better today but my cough is still very bad, at present I have very little interest in life, I know I would make a very bad patient if it should ever be my misfortune to have a long illness stopping in bed breaks my heart, oh the horrible rotten medicines we have to swallow, almost as soon drink poison
30th. A little better again today but still have a headache feel weak its spreading like wildfire all over this part of the world according to the papers
1st July. Frightfully hot weather everyone nearly dead myself included sweat all day night, soon will be like a matchstick.
A big batch of men left here for Australia last night, nearly all had lost limbs or something
