In it will be an article on 'Ptomaine poisoning on a war ship' my article - I'm not taking the BMT or MJA  this year - Keep Spec & Sphere going for ourselves - Thank Mab for the handkerchief - & her nice letter - also for yours - This is Jan 10 - We've got news up to Dec 19 - not a word after that - we know absolutely nothing - No press news thro- We hear that the Censorship is off - letters here - Fitz - after divisions told the S.C that the  'Dresden'  was adrift & might come this way - If ashore & a gun fired - Commandeer any boat, any how & get inboard instantly - We're ready for a scrap but of course are utterly outclassed & haven't a hope. I think none of us save Fitz went to the beach to-day - It rained torrents - We played both varieties of Bridge & jumped from the rail into the ditch & I went to bed at 9 pm -
