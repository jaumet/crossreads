  
 1915 Jan. 14 Thu. Mess orderly Arrived at Alexandria about 9.30 a.m. Very fine harbour.- complete coaling facilities. No shore leave granted. A Lance-Cpl. from the First Expeditionary Force came on board and was entertained at dinner. Came from Sydney and belonged to 3rd Battalion. Infantry at Cairo. Several R.A.M.C. [Royal Army Medical Corps] men came on board during afternoon. There are 600 of them stationed at Alexandria.
