<p>Sunday March 3<br/>New Zealand band at Richon. Only band in Richon division which sticks to Division<br/>Monday March 4<br/>Rode over 1ST Brigade to see Sgt Beny[?]. found he had gone to hospital with bad knee. Received paper &amp; mail from Home<br/>Tuesday March 5<br/>1ST Brigade moved out to day. Supposed to be returning in few days. Will be glad when we move for a change.<br/>Wednesday March 6<br/>Very windy to day. Rumours of moving in few days.</p>
