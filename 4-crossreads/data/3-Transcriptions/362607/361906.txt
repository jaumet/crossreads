<p>18/7/16</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>This is the junction where trains branch off to go to Port Said. You will notice how these horses are all shot in a heap. All villages are the same &amp; the smell, ugh! it is frightful. These places are all on the skew &amp; one side lower than the other, like Dad's fowl houses before he got his glasses. Some are a foot out of the square yet they seem to hold together. I do hope that you will not be sick anymore. Hoping to Home again at X'mas  &amp; Remain Your's always Harry<br/>Make Dad try bee stings for Rheumatism</p>
