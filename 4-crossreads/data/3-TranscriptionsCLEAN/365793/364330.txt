  
 Aug 18 rigged Schooner on the Starboard Bow so cut in behind her & left her to Port. She passed very close & dipped the salute in passing. Counted 27 sails in all & could distinguish several figures on deck (12 Noon) averaging 300 a day & just 3,170 off Capetown. Every night the piano is in full request & the voices of "the boys" float out over the Atlantic, and it is the  good old  songs they love the best of all.
