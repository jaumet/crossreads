<p>We had a news paper cutting sent to us today from Adelaide, about Wilfred Snow (the writer of those letters you saw some months ago) (airman) I think I told you he won the M.C. &amp; had been promoted to Captaincy; since then he has been wounded; the cutting was to the effect that he has received further promotion, &amp; is now the major, in charge of a Squadron in France, he is young to have such responsibility.</p>
<p>As Flight Commander  before he had charge of six airships, 15 officers, &amp; 50 men, I don't know what a 'Squadron' represents.</p>
