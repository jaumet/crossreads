 from Bulford &amp; immediately went over to Tidworth to see Col. McWhae, A.D.M.S. I had some trouble with Mr Craig &amp; also with Major Johnson, O.C. Parkhouse, but we got over that &amp; here I am. The King's Review was a days enjoyment.
