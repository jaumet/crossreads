On Lewis gun in morning coming back 11.30am. Btn drill in afternoon back 4.20pm. Blackberrying after tea.21/9/17 Much cooler though fine. Out all day on Battalion drill & a stunt. Fairly cold night.22/9/17 Reveille 6.15am & breakfast 7am. Marched 2 miles & whole division was reviewed by Sir Douglas Haig near Drionville. After dinner four of us went blackberrying also had a feed raw turnips.23/9/17 Sunday. Guns audible in distance last 2 days. Numbers of aeroplanes flying about.
