 dinner. Finished there 9pm. 19/4/18 Recd parcel from Muriel posted Jany 30th. Very cold. 20/4/18 Heavy white frost &amp; very cold again.  21/4/18 Sunday Frosty morning but fine sunshine all day. Fritz aeroplanes over. Sick of job already. 22/4/18 Not so cold. Tried to change to Officers mess. No success so far.   24/4/18 Started in Officers Mess.  26/4/18 Commenced receiving wounded today. They include Fritzs &amp; French. 28/4/18 Sunday Temperature mild &amp; misty. Raining during night. Sent service card to Uncle Fred &amp; wrote home. 5/5/18 Sunday After Back being painful for 5 or 6 days got real bad today so had to go sick &amp; sent into hospital again.
