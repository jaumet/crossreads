  
 On arriving at our destination we marched about a mile & pulled up in a rough little Camp, there was a bit of excitement while we were getting the men in to their quarters 3 of Fritzs planes came over quite low I made sure they would empty a belt or two into us for we must have been a tempting target, however machine gun fire drove him off, then all of a sudden there was a stir in the heavens & three of our planes came tearing up & by a pretty piece of manouvering they cut one of them off & drove him down almost over our heads, there was great excitement as we watched our planes driving him down, once he looked as if he was going to get away, but one of our small fighting machines got into position above the Fritz
