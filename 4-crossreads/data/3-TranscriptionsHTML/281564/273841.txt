<p>21st August Friday<br/>1 AM Enter Moreton Bay &amp; lie all day<br/>Receive lectures on the habits of the Papuans &amp; also on the mode of the advance of an army</p>
<p>10.30 Again set sail after receiving sundry rumours that we were sunk in mid ocean by the German Fleet. Brisbane "Courier" confirms the above rumour. Sad scenes witnessed on deck by corpses of the soldiers &amp; sailors dancing bunny hugs, rags &amp; etc</p>
