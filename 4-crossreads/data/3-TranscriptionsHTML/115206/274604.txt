<p>February 1918<br/>Sunday 3</p>
<p>On the road<br/>Reveille at 6.30 am no parade until Boot and Saddle.<br/>Left at 11am. passed through Hazebrouck at 12.30<br/>Arrived at billets, three kilos from Bailleul, at 5.30pm<br/>Am feeling in very high spirits, received letter from Mrs. Adam.<br/>Warned off as horseholder for party going to the guns tomorrow morning<br/>Had a feed of eggs and chips at  a little Estaminet facing the billet.</p>
