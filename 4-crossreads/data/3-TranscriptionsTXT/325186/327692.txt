 of it. On the morning of 23rd Nov we marched to the train &amp; were put into cattle trucks holding 32 &amp; 35 men with our rations &amp; all gear.  It took us about thirty-eight hours to get to our destination  We arrived at night time &amp; then got out of the trucks all of us as stiff as crutches &amp; marched about seven miles to Billets in a village named  Outsetan   We had a couple of days spell &amp; on 27th Nov we moved on in Motor Lorries right through Armentiers to the mouth of the communication trenches  Taking with us full pack &amp; blankets &amp; we just got settled in our fire-bays
