
leaving for Australia
Dec. 25th 18On board SS Takada. Yesterday afternoon I  got embarked at Devonport  on the SS Takada, we left port for Australia at 10 AM this morning.
SS Takada Dec. 28th 18Fourth day out.Sea is beautifully calm we are now nearing Gibralter, will pass it either tonight or in the morning.The first day there was a heavy swell on & as the ship practically empty, she rolled a great deal. We had Xmas dinner on board, but most of us gave it up to the fishes, since then have been OK, eating plenty.
Jan 2nd 19. SS Takada.Yesterday being New Year's Day we had sports on board, & there was a very fine turn out of fancy dress costumes.
Jan 7th 19  SS Takada.In the Red SeaWe are making great progress. Early Sunday morning we arrived at Port Said, there we took in Water & provisions, & left about 6pm the same day.
