 "Charlerio" on Boxing Day &amp; things are very dear there Boots are 300 Francs per Pair (&pound;11) Meat 6/= per Pound &amp; evan a reel of Cotton is 5/6 a reel &amp; the Town is full of Belgian Refugees coming from England &amp; France. 
 The money used in Belgium now is German Marks The Huns Printed them. there it is all paper money am Enclosing one as a  Souvenir ". The Belgians are very good to us &amp; give us plenty of Coffee
