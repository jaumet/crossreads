<p><u>June 7th</u></p>
<p>Trouble brewing!&nbsp; The fireman are apparently shorthanded and are being over-worked.&nbsp; In the forenoon we pass Sierra Leone.</p>
<p>The weather grows more oppressive daily - and we are not yet to the &quot;Line&quot;.</p>
<p>Commence &quot;Society &amp; Solitude&quot; (Emerson).</p>
<p>Acquatic sports. More dolphins and a shark.</p>
