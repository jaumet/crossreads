 365 
 MAY 1917 
 11 FRIDAY 
 Warm &amp; Sunny. Just a year since 
 we left Sydney. Cleaning harness 
 &amp; I washed my horse in afternoon, 
 We had to turn out in evening. 
 &amp; shift the guns down to the pits 
 our fellows built in Ploegaert 
 We are to be an S.O.S. battery 
 there &amp; attached to the 8 th  Brigade 
 Got home at 2 AM.
