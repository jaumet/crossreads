<p>4 January 1918 Friday<br/>Arrived Mejoel 0900<br/>Once more all ranks would like to let the people of Australia know the good work done by Mrs Chisholm &amp; Miss McPhillamy at Kantara Soldiers home it puts a man fit for a long cold journey in an open truck in the rain<br/>Past Romani Gaza &amp; other places of interest, changed trucks at a place near Gath &amp; arrived 10 am on 5th Jany at Regt.  Raining hard.</p>
