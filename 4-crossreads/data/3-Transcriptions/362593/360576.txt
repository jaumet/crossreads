<p>Thursday 11 July 1918<br/>Escort to bomb raid on Upper Wady Fara, bombing indifferent.  Came down to 2000 ft. and gave the Jackos Hell.  Fired 800 rounds at transport, camps, camels and cavalry.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 2 h. 5 m.</p>
<p>Friday 12 July 1918</p>
