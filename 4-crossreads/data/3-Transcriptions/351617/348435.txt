<p>The Barman George Badcock from Junee was dressed up in a white apron and handled things as if he had been on the game all his life. After the sing-song we cleared away the things and had a dance only without girls worse luck.</p>
<p>Considering, things went off very well except for Eddie Boyce wanting to fight the crowd and Jimmy Caher falling half way down the stairs but we got them to bed and things quietened down. I think this was one of the funniest Christmas nights I ever spent and one Im not likely to see again.</p>
