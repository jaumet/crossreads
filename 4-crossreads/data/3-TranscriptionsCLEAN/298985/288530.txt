  
 This camp was near Arbinins &   Rosiers. Did not feel the march so much to day.  Left a flannel shirt & pr of sox behind to lighten the load. 
 Monday 12th Were told to make our dug outs splinter proof & be ready to move off at 10 minutes notice. We found ours was much to narrow to be cumfy so have widened it at one end & can now sleep side by side. Did not get much sleep last night Our guns did a lot of firing & the hum of the planes was almost contineous. Jerry dropped plenty of bombs the closest about 500 yds away.
