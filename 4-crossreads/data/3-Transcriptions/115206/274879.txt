<p>November 1918.<br/>Tuesday 5</p>
<p>Hosptl. Birmingham<br/>300 to 50, with Da Costa settling day today. So I've won my bet eh! Well that is not so bad altho I thought I'd surely lose it. <br/>Weather brilliant. Letter from ).J. very pleased to receive same.</p>
