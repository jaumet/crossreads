<p>Prussians &amp; Wurtenbergers.  One officer had been through Oxford, spoke perfect English &amp; on being questioned displayed little knowledge of hospital ship outrages &amp; thought there were 400,000 Americans over here whereas there are over 2 million our brigade went 5 kilos without many casualties.  The 4th  Div. leap frogged the 2nd and that day we advanced 10 miles &amp; captured 17,000 prisoners 400 guns &amp; countless machine guns.  We took We took Warfusie, Sailly, Laurette, Harbonnieres, La Motte on our sector and at present are on the outskirts of Proyat.  The officers (artillery)</p>
