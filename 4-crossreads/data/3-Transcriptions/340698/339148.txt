<p>including myself paid a visit to St. Omer in a dog cart. All went well until the return journey at about midnight. About 3 miles from home the tyre came off the wheel and we had to get out and hump shankus. To make matters worse it came on a blizzard and we crawled into bed a sorry crowd.</p>
<p>20/10/16<br/>Left Nortleulinghem</p>
