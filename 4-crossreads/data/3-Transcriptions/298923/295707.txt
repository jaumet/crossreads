<p>10 February 1918 Sunday (X to X crossed out)<br/>STILL RAINING.<br/>The Mediteranean is not supposed to be rough but above the roar of the Naval Guns we can hear the breakers on the Beach.<br/>We have postponed our Sports from day to day.  Just got word to be ready to move in 12 hours<br/>X All sports cancelled so post this with love to all<br/>Your son.   X</p>
