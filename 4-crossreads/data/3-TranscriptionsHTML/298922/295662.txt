<p>Saturday 29 December 1917</p>
<p>President of Court Martial usual thing Drunk &amp; stoning the Military Police. Still no word of train. My KIT all packed up &amp; standing by for Palestine. This Camp trains been in every branch of the service - &amp; is a good refresher, but I am a Soldier now.</p>
<p>Mail arrived from Australia, MOTHER/DAISY Nov. 11 &amp; Nov. 18 &amp; one from Mrs. Angus Robertson, good of her to write. Loud Cheers just got leave to go to Cairo again tonight &amp; see the wife returning tomorrow.</p>
