 Saturday 6 July 1917 [1918] Sister Nichol with us again and improves with time, off hand but genuine.  Off 2-5, rest &amp; sleep a trifle.  7.30 p.m. Miss Gemmell does rounds &ndash; in a rash moment I ask permission to wear white, long list of wrongs poured out " at " me &amp; am found fault with in strong force.  Great surprise on my part &amp; evidently mischief is being made &amp; listened to. Letter with May Witrels, son wounded &amp; in Citadel.  Fight in the "Bull Ring" results in an Aust. being jailed.
