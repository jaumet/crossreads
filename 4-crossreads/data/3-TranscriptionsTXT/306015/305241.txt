 Alan persuaded me to go &amp; see Charlie Chaplin in the Bapaume pictures in the evening.   On the way home we revisited the demolished church &amp; after 40 minutes hard work succeeded in dragging down an old torn paining from one of the walls.  This we tore into sections &amp; washed &amp;  are  we are going to try &amp; post the best parts of it home as souvenirs. 
 July 3.   A gas-alarm was sounded sometime in the middle of last night &amp; we all scrambled in the dark for our P.H. Helmets &amp; wore them 'at the alert' for the rest of the night.    In the morning we watched a demonstration 'stunt' by one of our platoons.
