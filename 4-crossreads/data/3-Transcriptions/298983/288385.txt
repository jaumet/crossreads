<p>Sunday 24th February<br/>As usual. Fritz sent us more stuff than usual today. Did no harm that I know of. His machines did not seem to be able to get up for observation</p>
<p>Monday 25th February<br/>As usual A nasty cold wet morning</p>
<p>Tuesday 26th February<br/>Finished the Observation Post. A beautifull day</p>
