Todays march was over the country from Cartignies to Beugnies which is nine kilos beyond Avesnes.  This country showed itself as picturesque, despite the weather, which, after two days of clear sunshine 2 frost, was muggy &amp; raining. The pure green of the grass, the pretty cottages which had a peace time neatness, &amp; the woods, even in their bareness retained a dull tinge of gold, were together pleasing to the eye.
