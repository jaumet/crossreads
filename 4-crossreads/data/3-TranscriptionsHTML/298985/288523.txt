<p>Came through Abbie Ville Fontain &amp; Longfray on the trip. We are to move on per motor for the time presently. Left camp at 6 &amp; marched about 5 or 6 miles when the Lorries caught us up. Believe we should have got them near the camp  Some of our Officers were drunk which probably accounts for mismanagement. Came through Felixcourt &amp; Doulins fair sized places.
