<p>Feb 3.<br/>On once more, much refreshed after a good bath and rest in the camp.  The journey along the valley of the Rhone simply magnificent as spring is just making itself felt.</p>
<p>Feb 5th<br/>Run thru Monte Carlo, Nice &amp; Mentone, unfortunately it was dark, still the beautiful moonlight allowed us to see to a certain extent.</p>
