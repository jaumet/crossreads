<p>"Souviner Hunters" and looked most desolate. We went to a range just fixed up temporarily and really in what appeared to be the moat, under the walls of an old fortress, The walls are 60, or 70 ft high, &amp; built of small bricks, about half the size of ours, and large stones on the corners &amp; here &amp; there in the wall, I went through one of the gates, and found it was a small tunnel with a strong door at both ends. The tunnel is the width or thickness of the wall, and I measured that &amp; found it just over 100 feet. On top of the wall were trees, &amp; shrubs, the flowers of one I sent home, also some beautiful roses</p>
