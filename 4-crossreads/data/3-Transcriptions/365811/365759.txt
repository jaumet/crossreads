<p>We have not had much news through for the last few days &ndash; mostly hot air in the cable news. From what is in them, we seem to be holding on pretty well now, but to think of the casualties that must have been sustained since March 21st makes one shudder.</p>
<p>I had a letter today from the D.A.D.M.S. Aust Corps over there, written on March 30th but not one word about what they were doing or about the stunt at all.</p>
<p>Some little time ago I heard from Walter MacCallum. He seemed in very excellent form &amp; was apparently on the best of terms with himself &amp; everyone else as a Brigade Major.</p>
<p>My last letter from home was dated in January but a later one from Dorothy tells me my Mother was over to see Mrs. Ferguson &ndash; I am very glad &ndash; I was afraid they would have returned to Forbes before you got back from your Melbourne trip.</p>
