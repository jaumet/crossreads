The native rising turned out to be a mob of niggers, infuriated by the dastardly conduct of Australian soldier;   who  they were after his blood.  The first shot came from a blank fired by native policeman at the mob, &amp; the second was an accident. An A.M.C. man on guard fired accidentally while loading.
