<p>Australia 22nd March 1916.<br/>I started my final leave to day.  I have seven days &amp; return to camp at Moore Park on Wednesday 29th &amp; sail, probably by the "Nestor" on Saturday 1st April.<br/>Eight other members of the school (Engr. Officers School) are leaving with me &amp; we expect to sail straight for Egypt.<br/>1st April (Saturday)<br/>We left by the Tp. Ship. "Makarini" at about 2 PM to-day.  She is a fine ship &amp; we have about 1000 troops aboard Inf-.A.M.C- A.S.C. &amp; Engrs (160)  With Lt. Nangle, Sgts Hart, Smith, &amp; myself are in charge of the three Engr. Reinf. of 1st-2nd-3rd.</p>
