<p>Wednesday 25 April 1917</p>
<p>ABUSSITTA</p>
<p>Snakes, Scorpions etc. more plentiful here &amp; much more insect life of every description, reason I suppose bigger population than the Desert.</p>
<p>Managed to get some tinned Fruit sent by Pack Horse from Canteen at Railhead this morning.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]  Australian NEWS SHEET cabled to the Troops consists of "Egg Laying Competition", "Plague of 'Possums"!!  "Dinner to Holman"!! etc. !!!  What we would like to know is "Seasons" &ndash; Repatriation, and other items of interest.</p>
<p>Very Hot about 110&deg;.</p>
