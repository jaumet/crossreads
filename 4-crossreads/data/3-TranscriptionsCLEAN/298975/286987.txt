
Jan 1918
to the Butts with a markinh party
Thursday  3Still at Butts Have Hegarty for a mate. Hall on next target. Backed our score against his for suppers. We won
Friday 4thGot another cheap supper
SaturdayOn the Butts Won again Went to Warminster in afternoon, Banked  5 Saw the Pictures & had 2 fair meals
