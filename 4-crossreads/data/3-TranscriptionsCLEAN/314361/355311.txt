Yesterday we had our weekly test, my results were [indecipherable] flag 99, Semaphore 100, Buzzer 100 Lamp 100. Have not got much to put down this time, do not know how long I shall be in Egypt, May be called out any time, but do not expect it until about a months time. Hope to pay another visit to the Museum for the afternoon I did not see it all, & it is well worth paying another 2 Pts. to look again. Zeitoun Sunday 19th Dec. 15 This afternoon I visited the Citadel. The Citadel is  a  very ancient, in the centre is a beautiful mosque. The tram fare to this place is two miliemes, The mosque is on the summit of a hill , & overlooks the city of Cairo.
