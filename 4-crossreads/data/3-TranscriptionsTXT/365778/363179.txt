
Tuesday 6/3/17Fixed things OK, our shelling a bit short, handed line over to Norrie & move into Support at Le Sars - Letter from the General.
Wednesday 7/3/17
Moved back to Reserve in 26th Avenue - men looking well. Letters from Doff.
Thursday 8/3/17
Move back to Acid Drop for a rest, relieved by Reid.
Friday 9/3/17
Went to Division, had visit from Whiteman of R.F.C. wrote to General & Doff.
