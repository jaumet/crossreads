<p>Dec. 7 Tuesday 1915<br/>In bed all day<br/>More letters from Home It is good to know that the loved ones are well &amp; thinking of you</p>
<p>Dec. 8 Wednesday 1915<br/>In bed all day<br/>All patients able to get up are given a suit of loose kharki clothes with a blue tie &amp; kharki hat. They can go out to Cairo in these togs.</p>
<p>Dec. 9 Thursday 1915<br/>In bed all day<br/>One patient in this Hospital got out to the canteen &amp; had a good feed &amp; was dead in a few hours poor chap. He thought the Dr was too hard on him but found out the mistake too late.</p>
<p>Dec. 10 Friday 1915<br/>In bed all day<br/>Yes, separation is a big word these days. I fear many in Qland today are thinking of the long separation of their loved ones &amp; many will never rejoin their friends this side of the grave</p>
