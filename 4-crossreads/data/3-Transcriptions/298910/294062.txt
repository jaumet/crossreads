<p>21/9/16  Thursday</p>
<p>Nice day. Got a parcel from Mrs O'Kelly. Got letters from Ammi, Eileen, Frank, Aunty Lyons, Mrs O'Kelly &amp; J. Hagan. On Gas Picquet night &amp; day.</p>
<p>22/9/16  Friday</p>
<p>Nice day. Went down for some ashes for filling up around the Camp. Very Cold. Went to evening devotions at the Church.</p>
<p>23/9/16  Saturday</p>
<p>Nice day. Got orders to pack up &amp; be ready &ndash; Finished up by taking over Aid Post at "Rifle Villa" &ndash; at last some of us are with our own brigade &ndash; few calls during the night. Most comfortable post we have been in yet.</p>
<p>Sunday  24/9/16</p>
<p>Nice day. Lively trench warfare &ndash; Fritz putting them in freely to V.V. Sap. Very few casualtys.</p>
<p>Monday  25/9/16</p>
<p>Nice day &ndash; Fairly quiet &ndash; Very little sleep last night. Aeroplanes busy.</p>
<p>26/9/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Nice day. Have a nasty cut in my hand, two stiches. Very quiet.</p>
