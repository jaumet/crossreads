<p>February, 1915<br/>Tuesday 2<br/>Arrived Alexandria 10 a.m. &amp; waited all day for orders to go along side wharf &ndash;<br/>Night came &amp; still here Very many captured Turkish German boats are here &amp; the American Battleship Tennessee sailed out as we entered; our men fell foul of their Sailors had a fight.<br/>[indecipherable] O.B. Rylie met me on board, from the Reg gave all the news was glad to see him &amp; went ashore.</p>
