 Monday 29/1/17

 Walked around visited Bastille and Hotel de Ville, Law Courts etc went to Madame Butterfly. 
 Wednesday 31/1/17 
 Left Paris at 9.45 am &amp; arrived at Amiens at 3 pm, reached Battalion at midnight.
