<p>At 9 p.m. on the 16th March orders came to hand, twenty bearers were to be ready to move off at 7 in the morning. A nice prospect full marching order again, blankets to be carried as well.</p>
<p>March 17th 1917.</p>
<p>The 20 Bearers leave the camp at 7 a.m. &amp; march about a mile &amp; a half to &hellip;&hellip; Railway Station &amp; board the train. Arrived at the old headquarters where we were last January at 11 o'clock. But what a difference we found there the place was almost deserted as Fritz had retired at last. At 1 o'clock we received orders to move on &amp; report to the nearest R.A.P. What a difference in the landscape we found since our last visit here in January.
