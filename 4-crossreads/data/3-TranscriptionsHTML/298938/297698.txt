<p>In the evening Lily Hammond Frances Vi &amp; Ernest, came &amp; we spent a pleasant evening<br/>Had to see Dad back to Caterham unfortunately at about 9, but hope to see him again soon.<br/>On Monday early I saw Mr Hammond Senr. &amp; spent the rest of the day in the city with Lily, Ernest, Frances &amp; Violet.<br/>Caught the 10.43 pm back, Lily &amp; Mrs Elerage, seeing me off.  It was a very short time to have at home, &amp; I was very miserable when I got back, but it was a very pleasant memory.</p>
