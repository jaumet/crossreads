 August,1918 Saturday 31 
 Bourcy or Barleau Bty Position Weather fine with a slight drizzle. Fritz shelling roads with 5.9s got the following Battery chaps. Sgt Milner killed, Gunnr Dickson, Voisey, Bdr Spooner, Tommy Main, Drv Hackett. Wounded Anderson, McDonald and Jack Gillam killed. All very fine fellows too. McCartney and young Clive left with Mr Mann for F.O.O. line with Mr Mays and Scottie Malcolm, Relaid wire to same.  Turned in at 11.10pm. for a brief rest.  Stunt to come off in the morning. Enemy shelling very consistently
