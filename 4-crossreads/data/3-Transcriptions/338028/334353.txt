<p>Thursday 7th Aug [Sept]<br/>Good news on all fronts, everyone is optimistic, things are very quiet in camp.</p>
<p>Wrote home and sent photos. Leave has been stopped, likelihood of us moving off.</p>
<p>Friday 8th Aug [Sept]<br/>Judging by paper the whole world seems marvelling as the recent light horse work. Am in a horribly unsettled state of mind, my finend [?] generally sits and writes poetry all day, I only wish the muse would haunt me as it does him.</p>
<p>Saturday 9th Aug [Sept]<br/>There is to be a competition between troops of the squadrons in a drill and shooting competition. H.Qrs are supplying a troop and I am coaching them. We were firing the range all day, I shot well myself.</p>
<p>One of our aeroplanes came down last night near our patrol, engine trouble, we had to guard it. The pilots walked in.</p>
<p>Sunday 10th May [Sept]<br/>Rest of mail in. I rec a parcel of socks from my sister for my birthday. No word from my brother, am terribly amxious about them.</p>
<p>C. Parade at night.</p>
<p>Monday 11th May [Sept]<br/>A party left for rest camp 1 off and 30o at Alex, for 7 days, I expect I will go in next. Rec 50 reinforcement horses from Remount depot.</p>
