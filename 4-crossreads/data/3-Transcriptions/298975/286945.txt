<p>Aug 1917<br/>After many false alarms the <strike>Torpedo</strike> Destroyers 8 of them &ndash; turned up about 2 PM today causeing quite a flutter of excitement We should certainly be safe now allthough I have never felt otherwise No Paraides today. Have just heard that we disimbark at 2 PM tomorrow</p>
<p>Friday 24th<br/>The above was quite out as it is now dark &amp; no land in sight We have put our blanket &amp; hammocks away &amp; will have to scratch for a bed tonight. Have our Kit bags &amp; equipment out all ready for landing Fair sea running today Passed 2 or 3 small boats</p>
