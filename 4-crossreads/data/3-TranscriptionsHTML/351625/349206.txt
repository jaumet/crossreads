<p>Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>C Coy, 2 Batt.<br/>France</p>
<p>10 Jan. 1918</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>Just a few lines while I am lying in my bunk in a pilbox.  We have a great deal of night work so that we sleep all day.  I am afraid I have not written to you lately but I have rearly not had the chance.  You will understand why but I can't</p>
