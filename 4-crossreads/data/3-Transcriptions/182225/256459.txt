<p>DEC. &nbsp; WEDNESDAY 6 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; frosty. Exercising horses &amp; cleaning harness in the morning &amp; cleaning harness all the afternoon. Went down &amp; saw Sam &amp; Geoff Smith ect in evening. Heard Alan was instructing in flying at Turnhouse.</p>
