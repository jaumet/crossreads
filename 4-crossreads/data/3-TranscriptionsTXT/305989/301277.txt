Sunday 7 October 1917Scored some Arrowroot from some of the lads at Camp.  Wrote No. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 7].
Monday 8 October 1917Changed guard, got a few letters, very little doing.
Tuesday 9 October 1917Quiet but dusty day, went over to Y.M.C.A. &amp; pinched some boxes.
Wednesday 10 October 1917Quiet day spent most of the afternoon preparing for a Big feed up with Sgts. of the 10th Irish Division real good night.
Thursday 11 October 1917Very dusty &amp; windy, got a couple of parcels.  B.W.I. band played at Y.M.C.A. quite a good turnout.
Friday 12 October 1917Nothing Doing.
Saturday 13 October 1917Nothing Doing, band played near our dump.
