<p>Sunday 19th<br/>Church Parade in morning. Lectures Singing Etc in afternoon. Mr Treevers of the YMCA has gave us chocolates at tea time &amp; has just delivered a short sermon &amp; given out Testements. I think his talking has had some affect on the swearing.</p>
