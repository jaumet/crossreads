 Monday 12/2/17 cloudy &amp; a little sleet this morning, leave nearly here now. am getting very impatient Quietest night I remember on the Somme our People must have been moving up the guns even now 10. a.m. theres hardly a gun fired. Pay 2.8.5. &pound;20.16.0 
 Tuesday 13/2/17 A thaw has set in &amp; its getting like the old Somme we knew, mud &amp; mud, nothing of importance all day, Gas alarms at night Fritz put it over but it did not come our way. Pay 2.9.5 &pound;20.17.0 Sent to Brigade Head Quarters at 4 PM for special duty am quartered near Sugar Refinery controlling the Troops going in to the line. Bitterly cold wind blowing went on duty 4.30 to 7. nearly frozen 
 Wednesday 14/2/17. Put in an awful night had two blankets &amp; coat but shivered all night the coldest I have felt yet, heavy frost. Pay 2.10.5. &pound;20.18.0 Just been into what is left of a wood to get some for a fire. Plenty of shelling going on on both sides. Eskimo Walk. 
 Guillemont on the Right &amp; Delville Wood left. nearly blown out about 3 oclock shell 6" lodged about 10 yds from dug-out sent mud etc flying all over us, close touch. still very cold Frosts &amp; winds. 
 Thursday 15/2/17 Slept better last night got a lot of Bags to sleep on. Going out for wood this morning Pay due &pound;2.11.5 deferred &pound;20.19.0 have no idea when going Blighty now, cant get any letters away either. Ice 3 ft. thick in shell Holes
