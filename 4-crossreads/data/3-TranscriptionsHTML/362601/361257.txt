<p>26 August, Tuesday</p>
<p>Dave's Place. We walk to P'sham [Petersham] &amp; I go to Trukdale[?] but all are out. I catch 10 pm boat, meet Norrie Dempsey.</p>
<p>27 August, Wednesday</p>
<p>Go to Bexley. Meet Clare. I just catch last boat. Go up to Darley Rd in Motor with 'am Bone &amp; arrive Home at 1 am.</p>
