
February 1918Saturday 16
MessinesWent to F.O.O.with Garfott with Mackensen's lunch. Battery fired a few rounds at a Fritzer plane. Great deal of aeriel activity throughout the day and night.Weather beautiful, a little frosty, shell holes covered with ice.Visibility very good.Had a rather disastrous evening at cards.Wrote to Doug Fraser and Ike Steinthal.Had the 10 to 12 watch last night.
