<p>I was translating the conversation to my wife.</p>
<p>When they left, my wife gave to them a miraculous medal blessed at the Mother house of the Sisters of Charity in Paris, they, directly, put it round their neck, we were deeply edified.</p>
<p>Just finishing this letter George is back in his capacity of Corporal with his friend for some military mission about the heights round here.</p>
