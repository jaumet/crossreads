<p>17-3-16<br/>Fri 17th<br/>Was out on the desert again today. but Minia was the only station we could get into communication with; The Colonel came down today &amp; the 3 men who were the cause of the disturbance in the village a few nights ago were up before him but discharged;</p>
<p>Had tea with our native friends at the village on the edge of the desert when coming back to camp; 3 camels were sighted on the desert West of Nazlet-El-Abid this afternoon but they made off back into the desert when the patrol tried to approach them, &amp; special patrols are being sent out from all villages where troops are stationed tonight in the hopes of catching them;<br/>Boxing still booms every evening after tea.</p>
