 Borre, Hazebrock to Zuytpeene &amp; billeted for night. 
 Thurs. Nov. 15th 1917: - Left Zuytpeene by motor &amp; billeted for night a Wardrecques. 
 Fri. Nov. 16th: - Left Wardrecques &amp; marched through Bilques, Helfaut, Bientques, &amp; billeted at Ramilly-Wirquin. 
 Sun. Nov. 18th: - Marched through Wirquin, Ouve-Wirquin, &amp; Drionville &amp; billeted at Blequin. 
 Mon. Nov. 19th: - Marched from Blequin through La Caligue, Desvres &amp; Samer &amp; are now billeted about 2 K. out near Tingry Only a short train ride from Boulogne now. This is our "resting" area. 
 Sat. Dec. 1st 1917. My second "Blighty" Furlough has come along. Left  Bn. at Tingry &amp; went by train to Boulogne. 
 Mon. Dec. 3rd: - Had a few days in Boulogne as the Banks in London would have been closed for weekend. Crossed Channel to Folkestone today &amp; thence by train to Victoria Station. Reached "Goatferry Road" Hqrs about 8.30 P.M.
