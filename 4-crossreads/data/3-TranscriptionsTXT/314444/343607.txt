 [Private James Patrick Godfrey (Jim) Griffin, No 7247, a labourer from Cathcart, NSW, enlisted on 6 February 1917 aged 26, and embarked from Sydney, on board HMAT A18 Wiltshire, 7 February 1917 with the 13th Battalion, 24th Reinforcement. He served in France and died of wounds on 31 August 1917 at Steenwerck, France.] Includes a number of post cards written home by Griffin and letters notifying his family of his death and burial place.]  Photo  sent by father Thos.
