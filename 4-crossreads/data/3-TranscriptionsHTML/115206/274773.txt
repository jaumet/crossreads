<p>July 1918<br/>Monday 22</p>
<p>Frechencourt.<br/>Weather fine; received two letters from home, one from E.A. new mail.<br/>Overhauled two miles of D.3. and mended all  bares and breaks.<br/>Walked along to a concert with blue and Jim, and enjoyed same immensely.<br/>Watched the Yanks at indoor baseball, and enjoyed the game almost as much as they did.<br/>Weather at night very wet 114th How pulled in late at night.</p>
