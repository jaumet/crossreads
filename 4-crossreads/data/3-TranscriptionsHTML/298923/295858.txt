<p>11 July 1918 Thursday<br/>So Joe has sailed!!  Never mind Mother dear we will  all come back soon-  Received letters from you April 28 May 5 &amp; May 12 May 19th- &amp; note all your news.  Also from May &amp; Joe &amp; Mrs Strange.  Note all you say re House   Sir Sam etc, it is brave of you little Mother &amp; I cannot advise so far away   One thing certain Joe will profit by it.  His job is there for him &amp; he needed a change badly will write c/- Laura.</p>
