<p>17/8/16.<br/>It continues to be fairly quiet   Fritz pays more attention to the communications in the rear, of the part we occupy. The trenches are of a rough and ready character here, they have been knocked about by the artillery of both sides; still they serve; although obliterated in places. We get much more duty now being so much under strength.<br/>18/8/16.<br/>Had rather a trying time last night.  Was lying out on the top of the parapet observing, under heavy fire for four hours; it gave me the "Willies" properly. One of his "Pip Squeak" guns was playing right at me for half an hour and gave me a bad attack of "Wind Up". Never got a scratch however. "B" Company has been</p>
