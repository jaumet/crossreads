 Saturday 29. Nothing of importance. 
 Sunday 30th September We are under orders to leave this afternoon We had a service this morning I spoke on "A Man shall be as the Shadow of a Great rock in a weary land". At I oclock we moved off to catch busses on the main road. After a wait of 2 hours the busses came. Owing to the dry weather the roads were terribly dusty & we were smothered in dust.
