<p>1915 Thursday December 23<br/>Got a bad cold no matter soon OK again as we sail South to a warmer clime &ndash;<br/>Left outer Harbour of Lemnos at midnight, convoyed by Battleships &amp; Torpedoe boat destroyers &ndash; 11 Hospital Boats including the Acquittania were in the Harbour.<br/>This boat is a big Atlantic Liner belonging to Allen Line &amp; had taken troops to Salonika &ndash;<br/>By wireless we hear that the Constantinople report is that we are driven into the Sea of Anzac. We are to hold on to Helles &amp; winter there. Am detailed as Capt. of the day -</p>
