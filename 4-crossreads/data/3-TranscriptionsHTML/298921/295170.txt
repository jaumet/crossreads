<p>July, 1915<br/>Sunday 18<br/>So many Egyptians are Xeyed, I find owing to conscription for Egyptian Army, the Mothers injure their male Babies eyes with fingers so as to be exempt &ndash;<br/>Jack Mack of "Yarto" I hear from [indecipherable] Mack is over here, they are all wanted.<br/><strike>took Lady Oakes to see Brigadiers</strike> off by train tonight in Brigade Car, I think I told you young Cutler is driving it; a good boy.</p>
