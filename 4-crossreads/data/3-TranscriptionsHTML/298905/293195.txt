<p>through Westminster Abbey it is a lovely building rich with <strike>l</strike>  statues of all the famous men of olden times, from there we walked over London Bridge &amp; doubled back to the Museum, it is a wonderful show <strike>wh</strike> we spent a couple of hour's in there, after that we had dinner, &amp; then went to a picture show &amp; saw the Williard -Moran fight, from there we pushed on up towards St Pauls for the great Memorial Service was being held there for Kitchener &amp; the King &amp; Queen were there we saw them as they drove through the streets they were in a carriage &amp; were escorted by all sorts of flunkeys &amp; soldiers the King was dressed in a kakhi suit.</p>
