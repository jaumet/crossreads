 1 September, Monday 
 Engaged at Walter Watrons. 
 We have a nice time &amp; music. I miss train but arrive at Tiksdale in time for telling of fortune. 
 2 September, Tuesday 
 Go to Town with B &amp; Hanna. I go Home to House of Girl. I nearly knock down on the wharf with Ellis &amp; Etta. We go to flat &amp; lay linos. Terrific thunderstorms &amp; a water sprout off the Heads.
