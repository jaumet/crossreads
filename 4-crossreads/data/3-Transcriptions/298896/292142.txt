<p>Friday 16th August<br/>At 8 A.M. we marched off for Hamel where we took over the M.D.S. from the 53rd Fld Amb. 17th Division. They had only been in the line 4 days but had been so severely gassed that they had to be withdrawn, report states that he hit one of his own gas dumps which we had captured. The march was hot &amp; tiring, 9 miles in all, but very interesting as we passed over many of the huns defences. Trains are already right forward &amp; we are using his light railway system &amp; the valley road which we used was already being improved.
