 Tuesday 23th October 1917 Disvres. Rev 6am fed up 6.30am and moved off 9.30am Our next stopping place is to be Therouanne which is 21 miles from Disvres About 5.30pm. Fed horses then dismissed had good huts to sleep in close to stable. John and I went into village which was full of Portuguese.
