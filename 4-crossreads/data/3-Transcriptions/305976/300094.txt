<p>Cairo. All the riders in the various races wore racing jackets with their own colours. It was a sight worth seeing.<br/>The New Zealand Brigade Band rendered popular music &amp; the Tote was going in full swing. Most of the boys rode over and there was one long string of Horses just outside the course. There was a good number of Civilians from the various villages principally Troops.
