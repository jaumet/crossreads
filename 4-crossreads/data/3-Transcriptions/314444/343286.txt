<p>This letter is from No. 169. Trooper Watson F Adamson. A. Co.  D. Troop 1st A.L.H. and is written from Gallipoli.  He was wounded at Lone Pine 7/8/15 and sent to London.  He is <strike>sill</strike> still away 9.8.19. &amp; is Sergt at H. Quarters.  London.<br/>T G Adamson. father. Nemingha<br/>Tamworth N.S.W.</p>
