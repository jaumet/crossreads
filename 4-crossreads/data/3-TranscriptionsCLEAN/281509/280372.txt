
1916 May 29Monday
Reveille' 5.30 a.m. Fine.  Trio had bath in stream. washed clothes. In afternoon, Raf. No. 843. our draft of A.M C. marched to No 1.T.C. Stadium, kerosene tins & sand Lecture by Lieut. on Gas & Gas helmets.  Practice trying them on turned over to Sergt. demonstration of sandbag building. Back to Stadium instructed by a Sergt. in first aid bandaging. Home to 6 o'clock tea. Stroll to house with tower among hills  Back to biscuits & apricots. Bed. Letters by Sergt.  Sky overcast not so cold.
