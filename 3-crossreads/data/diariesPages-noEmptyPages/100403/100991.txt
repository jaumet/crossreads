May 1.
15th. All day the Arabian African Coasts have been visible seeing one Arabian town at 5 p.m. we passed Perim into the Gulf of Aden. Saw first flying fish to-day.
17th. Passed Socrota to-day very closely it is very barren indeed.
20th. Detailed to work with a Sister to-day had a very amusing time, especially in carrying a patient along the deck. Frightfully hot enervating.
