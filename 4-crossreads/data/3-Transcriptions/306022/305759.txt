<p>I set out to do some "rubbernecking". We saw several old churches which are very well preserved My chum disbelieved that the mortar could have held for 1100 yrs, but when I introduced him to a bun at a cake shop made by the present inhabitants who are descendants of the Saxons he changed his opinion. I have never yet struck anything so tough.</p>
