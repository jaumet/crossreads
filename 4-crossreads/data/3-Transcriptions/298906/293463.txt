<p>soldier he is in the 53rd &amp; was badly wounded at Fleurbaix.<br/>6th. What a glorious morning we have had, the whole platoon went out to a spot pointed out to us on our maps which we found easily, had to work out a bivouac scheme which did not take us long, &amp; after this we went cycling all over the place visited a lot of little quiet peaceful villages, where we startled the people as we raced through the streets" hell for leather" you might guess what it would be like 40 odd young &amp; strong chaps in the pink of condition all racing one another for their lives, it was a beautiful day, &amp; the old English lanes looked almost perfect</p>
