<p>1/4/16  Saturday</p>
<p>Very hot all day &ndash; Cleaned up the lines till 11 am. No passes granted till 3 pm. Went into Cairo &amp; was going out to <u>Gizeh</u> &ndash; none of the party had passes, so the picket, would not let us over the bridge, in fact we were lucky not to be detained. Went to a variety show at night &ndash; after making a good inspection of the fashionable parts of Cairo &ndash; round about "Shephard's Continental etc.</p>
<p>2/4/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Very cold last night &ndash; Orderly Corporal &ndash; never said a word of course as Sunday is the best day to be on tours etc &ndash; Didn't mind so much when news came through that general leave of all Camps was stopped till further</p>
