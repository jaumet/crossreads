 Monday 28 October Very fine day. 
 As usual in morning Major inspected the crowd. 
 Afternoon journeyed to [indecipherable] &amp; saw Geoff Stillman. Then to Marieul saw Bill Morgan &amp; Korah Wills. Had tea with Bill &amp; a long yarn with Korah. 
 Came home in the dark but managed well.
