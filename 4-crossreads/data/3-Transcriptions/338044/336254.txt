<p>have lunch, then home for a wash &amp; shave. Return to town &amp; have photo taken to send home. It is just raining like one thing &amp; very nice we find it. Rather cold, too  Take a tram to Boisguillaine, but decide to return on same tram   Meet two Australian nurses. These French woods are all beautifully kept, even near the towns. As I have tea in our Cafe, right opposite the Railway gates, through which I shall have to pass next week for the front, it seems at least novel, in this old French Cafe, at present but always on the move. A soldier's life is strange</p>
<p>28th August:- Monday:-<br/>The usual amount of work &amp; to-morrow again until 10 pm. A French military aeroplane passed overhead at 5 pm going at great speed. She was a beauty.  The best news for months. Roumania</p>
