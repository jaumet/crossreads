15.10.16  Sunday
I went to Church Service today in Roningholst the first time since landing in France. On coming back I found that 1400 men had suddenly been attached to our dump which caused plenty of work.
I rode into Poperinghe in the afternoon with S Hayne & after a good look round cme back to camp very tired. Whilst there the English Division that took Le Sais marched in & they brought loads of trophies back with them.
There are persistent rumours about that we are going to Salonika, but whether it is true or not I cannot say.
The weather is bitterly cold & I simply cannot get warm. Every little shower of rain contains its share of hail & when one touches you it is like a knife going through you. Twice this morning it has hailed & at the present time they are coming down like peas.
16.10.16 Monday.
