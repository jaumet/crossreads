<p>One fine day about 8 o'clock at night I formed up in lines of platoons in fours at ----- paces interval, &amp; explained in about 20 words that we were about to do night operations &amp; not a man was to speak, or to give any officer cause to speak by shuffling. It was a very dark night &amp; I decided to give them a severe test. We marched for three quarters of an hour without a word being spoken even by myself, &amp; although it was too dark for me</p>
