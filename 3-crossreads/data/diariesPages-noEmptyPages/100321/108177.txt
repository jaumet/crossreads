12/2/19 Marcinelle
we often ploughed through the desert sands of Egypt. The roads were so slippery that one's thoughts had always to be of one's feet. Lunch on the road ? we went into an estaminet and ate bread and jam with cheese and coffee to the tune of a musical box. With band at head we entered Marcinelle at about 2.30 p.m. and passed D.H.Q in style with flags flying. Major General Sir C Rosenthal took the salute. Found our billets all right. I am in a good house where there is a little girl called Marie Antoinette about four years old. The house is electrically lit and has a central heating system with radiators.
