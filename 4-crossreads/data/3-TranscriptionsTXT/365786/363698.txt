 Their complexions are fairer than that of the Fellahin.  They are a Christian people, they believe in the divine nature of the Saviour but do not allow Him any human attributes.  to him  Their places of worship were very dirty &amp; evil smelling -  not  &amp; compared most unfavourably with the large &amp; spacious  clean  mosques. They hold to Old Testament institutions &amp; behave as if they lived in early Biblical times. The Bedouins are the nomadic Arabs &amp; there are about 600,000 of these.  They are generally gentle &amp; inoffensive, tho those in the north have inherited the fiery blood of the desert tribes.
