Enjoyed the run, country beginning to look very nice now everything is looking green, very pleasing to the eye after the long spell of winter.  Farming is in full swing everywhere.
Thursday 3.5.17Quite warm now, beginning to discard some of the superfluous clothing. Cost 5 francs 80 to register helmet which I posted a day or so back.
Friday 4.5.27First day of the Divisional Sports.  Our lads did very well in the open races & pulled off a few of the events.
