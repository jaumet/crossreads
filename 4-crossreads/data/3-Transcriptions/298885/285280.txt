<p>Well on our arrival here we heard we are to take root for a couple of months with the result that 4 of us, have secured beds, dinkum beds, <u>real</u> good respectable beds too.  I mean civilised sleeping apparatus which I can assure you is quite an appreciative novelty for us. All the boys are billeted likewise throughout the town.</p>
