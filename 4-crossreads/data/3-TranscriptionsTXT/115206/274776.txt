 July 1918 Thursday 25 
 Frechencourt. Reeled and overhauled 3 miles of wire. Western, Drew and four others came on to the Spooks job, under compulsion. Weather showery Rode 24 kilos a very tiring ride too, but for a very happy reason  Rode home my 24 kilos after having spent a rather pleasant afternoon. Weather simply rotten heavy rain and very little  [indecipherable] did not make the roads too good. Visited McSweeney
