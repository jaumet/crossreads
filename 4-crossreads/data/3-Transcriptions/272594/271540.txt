<p>Just before we left the Huns done a dirty trick on 2 of our stretcher bearers, they went out with a white flag to pick up a wounded man who was laying in front just as they bent over him one of them got shot in the body &amp; he collapsed his mate then picked him up &amp; started to carry him back to the trenches he got nearly there when a German shot him stone dead</p>
