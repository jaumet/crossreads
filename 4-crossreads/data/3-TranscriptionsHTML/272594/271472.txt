<p>here I am in an old Y.M.C.A. with plenty of shell holes in her, scribbling away as if my life depended on it.  I went to bed very late last night Stan  &amp; I talked nearly all the  night &amp; you can bet we had a lot to talk about, seeing as how long it is since I have been home.</p>
<p>Just close to our camp are 30 6 inch guns drawn up along the road they have brought them down from Vimy Ridge to be overhauled</p>
