<p>At 6 am this Sunday Feb 16th we were called and my relief took over and prepared the engine and left at 8.0 am with the living van and went over a R.O.D. line to Achet le Grand passing through no mans land for miles and miles, which was an eyeopener to us all, the country is cruelly knocked about, and not a tree is left standing neither a house of any sort, which proves there has been bitter fighting all over this area. In places hardly a piece of ground measuring 12 square feet is left untouched.</p>
<p>Trenches and old dugouts blown in everywhere and pieces of material of every description laying about. Likewise graves with their white crosses all over the place.</p>
