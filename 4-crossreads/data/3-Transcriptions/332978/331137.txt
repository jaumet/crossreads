<p>58 September 10th<br/>As can be imagined the result would have been disastrous &amp; our evacuation would have been necessary. He is a very decent officer &ndash;</p>
<p>Bought pair slippers from canteen (16 marks) they fit me very well.</p>
<p>September 11th<br/>Good sleep &ndash; up early this mng &amp; sat in Garden &ndash;</p>
<p>Half of our room challenged other half today at Rounders &ndash; we had two cripples on our side &amp; won by 36 points, much to the great astonishment &amp; excitement of the numerous spectators.</p>
<p>Played Bridge &ndash; received letter from Gertie</p>
<p>September 12th<br/>Raining heavily all day today slept in till 2 pm &ndash;</p>
<p>Visited by a General today (apparently on the retired list) &ndash; He informed us that he was doing his best to increase our walks &amp; provide a football ground. He said that it was impossible to enlarge this building it was originally intended to house 250 officers, &amp; at present there are only 200 &ndash; He also hoped that we would not be here much longer &ndash;</p>
