<p>May 23rd<br/>A week yesterday since leaving Blighty &ndash; Memories of that day &ndash; First Mail from London &ndash; Mr. &amp; Mrs. Scales at Essex &ndash; Command performance to General Birwood and staff &ndash; The General is most congratulatory &amp; very pleased with his "Coves" &amp; signifies his appreciation by hearty handshakes with all the boys &ndash; Back to camp &amp; bed.<br/>May 24th<br/>Rehearsal &ndash;<br/>The warm &amp; perhaps oppressive weather gives place to a cold change.</p>
