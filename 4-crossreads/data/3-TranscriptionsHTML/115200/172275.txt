<p>62</p>
<p>5/12/15</p>
<p>Gallipoli peninsula</p>
<p>on the above Date I was in the [16<sup>th </sup>Clearing?] Station at anzac. All that morning the turks were sending schrapnel over and some of it was coming close to the above stationary but it was not Done purposly for we had tons of ammunition close to the Hospital and not only that But [General?] Headquarters and also a wireless Station and a Battery of guns Close By. and this was the turk objective in the afternoon</p>
