The Turks who happened our way at the start were Regulars, fine big fellows, that I would say averaged at least fourteen &amp; a half stone in weight. Some judge to of weight, from the number of wounded we've fortunately had to collect. The slaughter on both sides has been terrible from the start &amp; our old First division of Infantry is merely a remnant. The third contingent, who relieved us, will have better time than we had as our Boys, by solid work, have wiped out most of the initial difficulties.
