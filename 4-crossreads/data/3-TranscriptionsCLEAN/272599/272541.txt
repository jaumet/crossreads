  
 October Saturday 20 1917 
 Catch train for Newcastle at 9.50.a.m. Change there into "Flying Scotsman" in half hour. slipped out & saw a bit of Newcastle. Stephensons statue. Rocket. Tyne Bridge.  Via Berwick. (border) Tweed River. Dunbar. Bass Rock mouth of Firth of Forth.  Arrive Edinburgh 2 p.m. Fine day. Scotch laddie takes me in tow & conducts to Overseas Club. Warm welcome by ladies Mrs Jackson. Dinner & yarn. Tour round the City. Edinburgh Castle. Holyrood. Scott Memorial etc. Tea. Amusement. Bed noise.
