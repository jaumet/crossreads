<p>outside cutting their barb wire, they promptly turned the machine guns onto them, &amp; then charged them with bayonet &amp; bomb, to such good effect did our boys go for them that they turned &amp; ran, &amp; the boy's chased them back into their own trenches, here they tried to make a stand, but the boy's went for them in such a determined way, that they completely routed them, it was a most daring affair &amp; from all accounts accounted for quite a number of German's, they brought 7 prisoners back with them including 2 officers, our reputation is bounding up here &amp; lets hope it continues to do so  In this case it was the raiders that was raided, there have been raids now every night for the last 10 days &amp; some nights there have been two</p>
