
<p>101</p>
<p>1916&nbsp;&nbsp; SATURDAY 19&nbsp;&nbsp; AUGUST</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Out signalling in the morning with the Head quarters. I got leave in afternoon &amp; went in to Salisbury&nbsp; I had dinner at the Country. Walked round town after dinner &amp; found Harry Armstrong &amp; we went over the Cathedral together after. Had tea at Country with Pat Rutledge - Vern Brown &amp; Watt they have their commissions now &amp; are same class. Armstrong &amp; Self came out in a car in evening. The 27<sup>th</sup> Bat on duty at night</p>
