 sleep towards morning.  Went on duty in dispensary &amp; also sick parade altho' I now knew more what the trouble was, first a strain &amp; getting a cold on top of it through the draughty train.  Managed to keep going all day although marked L.D. &amp; in the afternoon strolled out to see the trenches or embankments used in the battle of Tel el Kebir 1882.  They still stand &amp; are mounds of sand about 7 ft high in front &amp; 10 ft behind.  More later about this scrap. 
 Tuesday 11th Jan '16 Had an off day Wednesday &amp; Thursday nothing much doing only I felt very off colour &amp; unable to work.  Violent headaches &amp; severe pain in lung.  I decided to hang out as long as possible.  Went from duty. 
 Friday  19th  14th In the dispensary but had a very off day indeed. 
 Saturday 15th Jan. Went sick &amp; was immediately sent into hospital with pleurisy. 
 Written up 25th February Sunday 16th Was carried away to the No 1 Clearing Hospital &amp; from thence the same day by Hospital Train to Cairo &amp; so to Shezirch Hospital arriving about 7. P.M.
