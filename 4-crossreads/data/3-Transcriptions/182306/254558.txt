
<p>60</p>
<p>Feb. 7th Friday</p>
<p>Very hot to day all lying on deck to cool off. Passed a few ships also a small Island</p>
<p>Feb 8th Sat.</p>
<p>Big swell on to day. Held sports on the boat deck which were fairly lively. Just passed a big Island Had boxing to night which gaveus a couple of hour of excitement. All the boys scrambling for possies to sleep on deck.</p>
<p>Feb 9th Sunday</p>
<p>Last night we passed the Twelve Apostles</p>
