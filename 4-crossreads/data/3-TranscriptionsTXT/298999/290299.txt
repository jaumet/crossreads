Friday June 15.6.17turned out 5.30 had shower had Breakfast cleaned up they are very slow getting the new roofs on rested till 10 A.M.  went for a swim.  had dinner 12.30.  very hot to day rested till 3 P.M. went for a swim had tea 5 P.M. turned in 7 P.M.
