 Aug 16 Sunday Arrived Pt. Moresby. Coaled &amp; oiled at once. Stanforth Smith &amp;  Miss Beatrice Grimshaw, the novelist, shinned up Jacobs ladder to come aboard. Niggers. Great interest. Saw houses built on piles in the sea Aug 17. Left for Noumea. Nothing interesting on the way. Aug 20 Joined up with Melbourne. Aug 21 arrived Noumea. Here were French flagship "Montcalm" (Amiral Huguet), Pyramus, Psyche, Philomel, with 1400 N.Z. troops for Samoa. Must have been glad to see us come in. Coaled of course. Departure delayed by Monowai, transport touch'g the only available mudspot delayed us 24 hours. French people came off in crowds Shouting Viva (anything) and "Keep the Flag flying" Aug 23 left for Suva in follg. order by day Psyche 3 miles ahead    Australia, Melbourne, Psyche  Pyramus, troopships Philomel, Melbourne, Montcalm, Australia Different order at night.
