<p>Monday 26th March<br/>Got to work &amp; fixed up a dispensary, utilising the morgue for the purpose. By nightfall had a very presentable show &amp; had issued several battalions with stuff besides providing solutions for operating etc. Also spent a little time on fixing up my abode &amp; closed up the windows as a very cold wind was blowing through. We now have proper chairs, a table, fireplace &amp; even a bed gradually collecting odds &amp; ends from the ruins &amp; pressing them into use.</p>
