<p>Tuesday 19th Sept.</p>
<p>Raining all day.  The Major again hurried us on with our work because of patients.   He is very impatient.  The building is now ready all but the windows.  These have been made but not yet put together.  Aeroplanes busy today.  Intense bombardment of Fritz's line, taking advantage of the rain.  Dreamt that we would be called in tomorrow.</p>
