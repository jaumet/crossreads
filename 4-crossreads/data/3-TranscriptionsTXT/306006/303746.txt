
22-2-17Thurs 22ndJack Wright came over today & by all accounts he had a great time while in at the School . Heard today that we go in to the school next Saturday morning.
23-2-17Fri 23rdWord came through this afternoon that men going to the school must catch the 8.20 train at Abd tomorrow morning, busy tonight handing over to Elliott & straightening things up as we have to get away from here at 5.30: There are a good many going in from this Regt for different courses.
