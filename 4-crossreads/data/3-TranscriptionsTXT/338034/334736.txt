The whole of the mounted troops &amp; artillery shortly after daybreak attacked the Turkish flank, the engagement lasted all day &amp; at times was pretty severe. The Third Brigade had a fairly large number of casualties &amp;  a  two companies of the Camel Corps were badly cut up.  our casualties however on the whole were light.   We captured a number of Turkish positions &amp; our artillery must have inflicted severe losses on the enemy, it completely outclassing the enemy batteries.
