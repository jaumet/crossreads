<p>1915<br/>Mar. 13 Sat.<br/>Went on leave at 10 a.m. &amp; visited (1) Ghizeh Gardens, (2) Gardens just this side of Kasr-el-Nil Bridge, (3) Kasr-el-nil Barracks (4) Egyptian Museum (5) Bazaar &ndash; all of which very interesting.<br/>Back to Mena at 7.45 p.m.</p>
