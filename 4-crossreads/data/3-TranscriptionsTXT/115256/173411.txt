 Monday 7 June 1915 
 Xmas Eve &ndash; everything very quiet &ndash; no excitement &ndash; time camp fire concert. 
 Xmas Day &ndash; Holy Communion &ndash; prayers for my dear ones at home &ndash; very quiet Christmas &ndash; all our Xmas presents turn up at 7.30 in the evening. 
 29th &ndash; Embark on "Tunisian" for Egypt.  Hurrah! 
 New Year's Eve &ndash; everything quiet,  New Year's Day, everything quite. Ismalia &ndash; Moascar. 
 Jan. 16 &ndash; Review by Gen. Sir Archibald Murray.
