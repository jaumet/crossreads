<p><u>Monday 8th April 1918</u><br/>Am writing this in the Church Hut in the Australian Base at Harfleur: the same hut in which I attended a rather inspiring service last October, as mentioned in Diary No 6.<br/>As a result of being in the Boxing Club at Ecault I got a pass in common with the other members to visit a certain boxing tournament held at Ostrohove camp near the town of Boulogne.<br/>We had a man entered &amp;  although he by no means had the best of the past exchanges he was lucky enough to win</p>
