<p>Saturday 28 Sept.<br/>Chaps breakfasted in bed. Things at ease in morning.</p>
<p>Out to 27th American Div, 9th Bde, 10 Bde. Some trouble with horses in front of heavy guns. Line was laid right beneath the muzzles.</p>
<p>Peaceful night after visit of a Fritz plane with bombs (very low).</p>
