<p>However long Germany is able to hold out, we must fight on till she is beaten to her knees. We owe this to ourselves; we owe this to our fighting men, the living and the dead; we owe it to our children and our children's children. We do not seek to repay foulness by foulness, but Germany shall have justice, and nothing less than justice.</p>
<p>Sunday Nov 10th 1918</p>
