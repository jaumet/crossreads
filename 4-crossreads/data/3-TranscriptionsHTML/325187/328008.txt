<p>[Friday 22 September 1916]</p>
<p>Saw Vicar of St Anne re Special Licence Bought Ring etc!!! arranged wedding for Oct 2nd only parents to be there-<br/>Grey day &amp; drizzling-   Went to Blackpool to Theatre there saw "<u>Tonights the night</u>"<br/>1000s of people a very gay place- not so nice as St Annes on Sea- Had Dinner in Blackpool<br/>Dolly &amp; I spent our day together &amp; are very happy- but I do <u>not</u> like crowds.</p>
