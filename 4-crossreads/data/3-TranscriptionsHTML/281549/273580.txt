<p>The European population of the island is mostly portugese, How they all get a living I cannot say, the island on what I can see of it from the ship seems to be a very barren place &amp; absolutely free from vegetation.</p>
<p>One battleship of which I do not know the name &amp; H.M.Auxiliary Cruiser Ophir are in harbour here. The Cruiser Ophir was at one time the King's yacht, but since the outbreak of war she has been turned into an Auxiliary cruiser. She carries 8 guns, 2 on each side, 2 fore &amp; 2 aft.</p>
<p>We weighed anchor &amp; left again at 6.0 o'clock after a brief stay of 4 hours. I could not go ashore as no leave was granted. The reason for stopping here was to take a Sailor from the Cruiser - Ophir on board, He is being invalided home to England suffering from some sickness or disease.</p>
