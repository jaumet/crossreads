Wednesday May 15th
Dunkirk : on duty till 6 am : round to my friends place after tea another air raid : heavy couter barrage : book of views (Aust) & letter to J B J :
Thursday May 16th
Dunkirk : on duty till 6 am : yarn to the little girl in the shop in afternoon : nice stroll to Rosandal after tea & seen the largiest & prettiest pansies I have ever come across : Madam Avery nice & asked us to look all over the large gardens : very nice : three air raid alarms at night :
Friday May 17th
Dunkirk : on duty till 6 am : stroll after tea & had a look round
