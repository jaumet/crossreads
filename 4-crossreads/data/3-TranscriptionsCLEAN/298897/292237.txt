 Saturday Christmas Day No parades so had a very easy day.  The other troops had a Church parade so I went along to it & took a few photos.  A couple of Generals were present & they had a march past after the service & the men looked splendid.  Having plenty of good things to eat I had a good time in this respect.  The rest of the day I spent writing letters.  After tea I decided to walk round to the Hospitals for something to do & dropped into a concert given by some Royal Engineers.  It was rather good & as soon as I showed myself one of the Tommies handed me his mug of toddy & insisted on me drinking it as he had spent some time in Sydney.  I returned to camp about 11.30 after spending a very respectable Christmas.
