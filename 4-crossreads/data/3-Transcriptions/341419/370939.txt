<p>And anyway it has not been offered me yet!</p>
<p>I should be sorry to miss seeing Damascus which I hear fell on the 3rd Oct. yielding 7000 prisoners which must be just about all now.  I don't see what is to stop them going right ahead until Turkey <strike>throughs</strike> throws it in.</p>
<p>Sept. 10th<br/>Still here &amp; from all accounts &amp; appearances likely to remain here for some two or three weeks.</p>
<p>We have moved camp up to the station about a mile east of Ammon.</p>
<p>This is an awful place.  It has been the Turkish camp, there are several dead turks buried &ndash; or I should say covered</p>
