<p>May, 1916<br/>Friday 19<br/>Exam "Standard Tests" before breakfast &amp; "Stoppages" after.<br/>Weather positively cool only 91&deg;<br/>Go for a walk into Heliopolis returning at 10 pm</p>
<p>Saturday 20<br/>Early this morning the men started to march out &amp; we were very glad to see their backs<br/>Read pretty well all day &amp; go into Heliopolis again on the evening</p>
