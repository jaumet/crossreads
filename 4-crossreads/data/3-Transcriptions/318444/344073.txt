<p>New Year's Day 1920:-<br/>Landed at Woolloomooloo &amp; the usual fuss at the Anzac Buffet</p>
<p>Mon Jan 5th:-<br/>Have to go to Randwick Hospital today owing to nose trouble.</p>
<p>Mon Jan 12th:-<br/>Operated on today at the No. 4 A G H Randwick by Major Whiteman.</p>
<p>Mon March 29th 1920:-<br/>Discharge takes effect today &ndash; The finish of my 60 days furlough &amp; the finish of any more "Form Fours" as far as I am concerned. Amen.</p>
