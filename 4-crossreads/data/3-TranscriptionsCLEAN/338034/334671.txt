
The main Turkish position at Bir-el-Mazar was not attacked, but the 2nd & 3rd L.H. Brigades in conjunction with the Camel Corps reconnoitered all the country & gained the information required. They also succeeded in capturing two Turkish outposts (about 30 men). The Turks brought up some artillery & shelled our fellows fairly heavily for awhile, however we had three Barr Batteries of artillery there & they silenced the enemy. We had very few casualties on the stunt. The 1st Brigade acted as[Bir-el-Mazar is now Bi'r al Maza]
