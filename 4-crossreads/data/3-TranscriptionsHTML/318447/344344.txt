<p>my special cross, got lost (as they tell me is the usual thing) &amp; did not turn up until two days afterwards you would have laughed if you had seen me taking sick parade. I made it a point to give them all something anyway &amp; then trusted in their faith.</p>
<p>Well old 1917 goes out in a few hours &amp; he has not been such a bad year either for me at any rate. Battle of Rafa on Jan 9. then out of it &amp; on the beach most of the summer until the hard work in this advance.</p>
