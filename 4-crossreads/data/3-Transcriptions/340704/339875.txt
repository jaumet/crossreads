<p>November 1917<br/>Tuesday 27<br/>Got orders to move back to Latron with 5th Mtd Bgd. I and 3 others detailed as DRLs or mtd orderlies and attached to 60th Inf Div. Reported at Enab Hqrs 8.30 am. Cushy job so far</p>
<p>Wednesday 28<br/>Still at Enab with 60th Div. 10th ALH to be only Aust troops to go thro Jerusalem.<br/>Andrews and Anderson left .<br/>Very cold</p>
