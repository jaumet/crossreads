  
 [Saturday 2 December 1916] 
 Returned to Camp still minus Kit but got some more in Cairo, met many old friends there But very few soldiers now mostly TARBUSHES Left leather Kit Bag & washing at Continental Hotel & got an old bag of Nowels. Got another batch of letters sent back by Laura Nov 4  Jack Forth & Day Sept 9th Joe & Day Sept 17th Mrs Strong Sept 2nd Miss Regus who is married!!! Aunt Louie- Sept 13th Papers from Mrs Cotton   Mothers parcel coming.  Please thank those you can will write later but very busy I am so "bucked" to get all home news especially at present.
