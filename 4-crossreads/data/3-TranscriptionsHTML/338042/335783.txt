<p>January 1918</p>
<p>1st: New Year's Day &ndash;<br/>A Happy, Bright &amp; Peaceful New Year  to friends &amp; enemies &ndash; Our Hall graced by the Gen. Sir Wm Birdwood who shakes with one &amp; all after the show and is mighty pleased with his Concert Party &ndash; General Coxon also present.<br/>2nd<br/>Two Australian Letters received. A strange coincidence concerning one &ndash; Our Panto has a most disastrous ending this evening &ndash; A fire takes place in the engine room &amp; two engineers are badly burned</p>
