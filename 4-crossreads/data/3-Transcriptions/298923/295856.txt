<p>9 July 1918 Tuesday<br/>Out wiring last night Jacko shelled the adjoining hill pretty badly &amp; made good fireworks, we dont get home <strike>to</strike> until midnight or 1 0'clock &amp; stand to arms again at half past 3, so can do a sleep<br/><strike>[indecipherable] gone "phut" again</strike>-  Reinforcements come, stop a week or 10 days &amp; go, evacuated sick, only a few of the hard old survivors hang on &amp; make the back-bone of each troop- <strike> permanently my nerves are rotten</strike><br/>Anyway the type being sent <strike>from Australia now are a poor lot</strike> &amp; don't seem to last more than a few nights wiring etc:-</p>
