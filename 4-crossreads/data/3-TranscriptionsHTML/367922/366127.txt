<p>Recd. 23/2/17</p>
<p>Dec 31 '16<br/>Have seen All these sights. Edinburgh is a very fine city, very wide streets, open places &amp; very clean. Plenty of churches about &amp; is frightfully religious. Absolutely every shop of every kind is closed on Sunday here.<br/>(Reg Fry)</p>
