We now have a great number of guns on this front, both large &amp; small howitzers &amp; field guns. We now have an army of something like 100,000 men here in Palestine composed of three divisions of British &amp; Scottish infantry, four Brigades of Australian Light Horse, one Brigade of New Zealand Mounted Rifles, two Brigades of English Yeomanry, &amp;  a great deal of Artillery.</p>
