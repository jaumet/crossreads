<p>Thursday 19th</p>
<p>Bookkeeping.</p>
<p>Friday 20th</p>
<p>A lot of us went out to get green stuff for Xmas decorations.  It was cold &amp; wet even some hale a rotten turn out.  Motor Mecanac class reopened this evening.</p>
<p>Friday 28th</p>
<p>Have had a very busy week, decorating the Hall etc.  A very good dinner on Xmas day Beer instead of tea also a box from the Red + tob. lollies</p>
