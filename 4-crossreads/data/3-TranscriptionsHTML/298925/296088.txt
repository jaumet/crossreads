<p>1st August  Have been informed we are beginning our final course of training and as a start go for a 8 mile route march with full pack up.</p>
<p>Sugar is 5d &amp; 6d a pound and in Birmingham will not be sold at all without tea.</p>
<p>2nd  Went to Figheldean and saw the old chestnut tree which inspired Longfellow to write "the village Black Smith"  The old shop is still used as a blacksmiths.</p>
<p>Across the way is the old church mentioned in the poem.</p>
