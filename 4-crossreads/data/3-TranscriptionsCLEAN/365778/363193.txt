
Friday 4/5/17Relieved & came out to Corps line, re organised 200 men rejoined.Saturday 5/5/17Things still pretty warm, sent 2 Coys up to dig, moved to Faureuil.Sunday 6/5/17Men rested in morning, training during afternoon, saw Bas - rang up the General. Casualties 8 Off 126 O.R.Monday 7/5/17Went by car with Martin & Byrne to see the General. 3 Coys ordered into the line. Robinson in Charge, saw Wisdom at Noveuil.Tuesday 8/5/17Had cake from Doff (rather mouldy). Orders received to move tomorrow.
