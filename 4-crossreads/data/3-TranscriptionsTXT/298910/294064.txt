3/10/16  Tuesday
Raining &ndash; On Picquet &ndash; very dull &amp; quiet. Wrote to G.W. Hegerty &amp; G. French.
4/10/16  Wednesday
Raining &ndash; Off half day. Heavy firing both sides &ndash; A few casualties.
5/10/16  Thursday
Nice day &ndash; On duty to Estaire &ndash; Market day there &amp; it was a great sight &ndash; Most large Towns have a big square in the very heart, here the country folk gather from daylight &amp; take possessions of side walks etc &amp; erect awnings etc to sell their products. Fowls &ndash; Fruit &ndash; vegetables &ndash; meats &ndash; alsoGeo Kingsley &amp; I stopped a runaway horsemake sausages &ndash; sweets &ndash; etc. Had a look thro the Town Hall &ndash; part of it is alotted YMCA, this is a very old building &ndash; the records of Mayors shows from 1789 &ndash; A fine big Church here must date back early into 1700.
6/10/16  Friday
All hands &amp; some infantry men helping to build a stable for the poor horses, who have been weathering it &ndash; it will hold some 100 horses. Got a letter from Jim Hagan. Fritz shelling Levantie.
