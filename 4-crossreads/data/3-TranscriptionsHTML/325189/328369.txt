<p>look too modern.  Only one corner has therefore been done just to show visitors what it was like originally.  There is a door here of bronze inlaid with gold &amp; silver.  This is only a duplicate &amp; cost &pound;14,000.  The original was pinched by Napoleon.  Half way up the wall outside is a cannon ball fired by Naps. soldiers.
