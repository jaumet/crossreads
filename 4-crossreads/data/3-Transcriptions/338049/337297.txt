<p>Thursday 28 March<br/>Up early &ndash; caught 7 am train to Aberdeen via Sterling, Perth. Saw Wallace Monument. Arrived about 12-30.</p>
<p>Fixed up at Hotel &amp; then to Beach &amp; Don Bridge. [Note at bottom of page] (Photo taken &amp; spend a pleasant &frac12; hour there)</p>
<p>Evening along <strike>Princes</strike> Union St &amp; visited Beach again.</p>
