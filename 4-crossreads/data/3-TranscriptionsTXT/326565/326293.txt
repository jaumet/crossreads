Friday 28/1/16Rainy &amp; cold weather still continues.  Piquet duty; first for some considerable time; drew 12-30 AM &ndash; 4-30 AM shift.
Saturday 29/1/16Received letters &amp; cards from home  Celebrated the anniversary of my birthday on piquet duty.Relieved from piquet 4.30 pm; Walked to Serapeum Station about 1&frac12; miles across desert &amp; caught 7.30 pm train for Ismailia, arrived back in camp 12.30 AM.  About 70 Officers &amp; men of No 2 Casualty Clearing Hospital
