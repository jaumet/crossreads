<p>March, 1916<br/>Saturday 18<br/>As soon as dinner was over, at the invitation of several Scottish Horse Sgts, I went to the Army International Football match on the E.S.R.I. ground<br/>After a pleasant convivial evening we turned in about 11.30 pm.<br/>Shortly after 1 am it started raining &amp; came through our roof wetting us unpleasantly</p>
<p>Sunday 19<br/>Spent best part of the morning drying clothes &amp; blankets &amp; mating a permanent shelter out of some timber we pinched for the job &amp; a wp sheet.<br/>Have a high tea w/- dad &amp; go to the pictures.</p>
