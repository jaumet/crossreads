<p>Thursday [2] 3 November 1916<br/>Rode on all day.<br/>Camped just past Romani.<br/>Slight storm with a little rain in evening.</p>
<p>Friday [3] 4 November 1916<br/>On again.<br/>Camped in hod North of Ogratina, [Oghratina] near where we camped after the Bir-el-abd scrap.<br/>Storm at night with heavy shower which wet all blankets.<br/>Had to carry overcoats on horses as rain made blankets too heavy for camels.</p>
