
Monday 8 April 1918Cloudy, no photos.  Letters from Home.  Wrote Mother, Mrs. Howey, Mrs. G. Lee.
Tuesday 9 April 1918Photos.  Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 3 h. 35 m.  A good got a good lot of photos, very successfull.  They were very important as they were wanted to complete map.News from Hun prisoner that Oliver Lee is a prisoner and safe.
