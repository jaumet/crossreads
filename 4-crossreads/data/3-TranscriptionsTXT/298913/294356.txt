Cairo is a very large city & the first thing noticed is the traffic, To explain this concisely, one needs an eye in each elbow &  one at the back of the head to dodge the garries & trams. The trams are electric & of French design & make. They are painfully slow however. The tram conductor blows a small brass trumpet instead of a whistle. The stops are very few indeed & very far between. Air brakes are not used.The other means of conveyance are garries or cabs
