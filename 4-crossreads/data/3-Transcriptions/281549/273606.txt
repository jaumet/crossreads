<p>They possess very old Historical Churches and are in many respects like the other villages that I have described earlier.</p>
<p>I returned back to Camp footsore &amp; weary, Having walked about 18 miles in brand new boots.</p>
<p>Till the 12th of January it was just the regular Routine of the camp, vis Guards, Drill, Picquet &amp; Fatigues, On the 12th I took up new duties of Mess-Orderly,</p>
<p>On the 14th instant I had to Parade to orderly room for not having my work finished before 12 o'clock . I got punishment of 7 days Confinement to Camp.</p>
<p>January the 17th I was selected to accompany the Draft that is leaving for France shortly, I was examined by the Doctor &amp; Dentist &amp; passed as fit.</p>
<p>Some English aeroplanes were flying above the camp today.</p>
