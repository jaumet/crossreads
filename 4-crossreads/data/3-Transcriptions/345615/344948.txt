<p>We are in touch with civilization again as at this camp there is a telephone <strike>connected</strike> line to Matrugh &amp; I <strike>have</strike> am at present on duty at it (time 8.30 a.m) just learnt that our No 4 Coy has had a small scrap a few miles the other side of Matrugh with one man killed &amp; one officer wounded.</p>
<p>The roads were very bad for the last five mile yesterday &ndash; &amp; 2 of our horses were played out, hadn't a pull left in them, so had to hitch on a spare pair of mules from the Middlesex Regt Transport who is along with us They gave us a pull into camp through the thick dust.</p>
<p>Foster gave the 'Tommy' driving</p>
