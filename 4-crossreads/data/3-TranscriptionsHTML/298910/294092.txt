<p>Feeling a bit off myself with Influenza. 5th Division went into the trenches this evening.</p>
<p>20th Monday &ndash; Tuesday 21st &ndash; Wednesday 22nd &ndash; Thursday 23rd &ndash; Friday 24th</p>
<p>In the Field Hospital pretty bad with Trench Fever.</p>
<p>Saturday  25-1916</p>
<p>Drizzling rain &ndash; Knocking about a bit but not feeling too good. Our Ambulance went into the trenches on Tuesday, so I have missed them. Dr. Beard &amp; his staff from the 60th Batt. Aid Post came in suffering from Bronchitis.</p>
<p>Sunday 26 &ndash; Monday 27 &ndash; Tuesday 28th &ndash; Wednesday 29th &ndash; Thursday 30th</p>
<p>Very sick &ndash; taking things easy. Very cold.</p>
<p>Dec 1st 1916  Friday</p>
<p>Feeling no good again.</p>
<p>Saturday 2nd</p>
