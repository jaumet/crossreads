  
 9.9.15 Thurs 9th Stan succeeded in buying a couple of tins of Salmon & 2 tins of milk last evening for 4/- so he struck a mine & we had Salmon for breakfast today; Les has taken a bad turn again today & is far from well; Bill Nelson has recovered & is well enough again to do duty; Our rations have improved considerably during the last week & we even had a small issue of bread to-day, we have plenty of jam & a fair issue of rice & & have even had 2 eggs each since we arrived here; When down for a dip today I gave my clothes a treat also; Scott had a row with the Dr to-day and got transferred to A Sqd. The nights are beginning to get a bit chilly but the days are still warm & the weather is perfect; Everything quiet.
