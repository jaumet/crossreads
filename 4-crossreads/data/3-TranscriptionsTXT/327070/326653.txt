 Thursday 9 March 1916 
 Mother's Birthday. 
 A bright sunny day with a cool breeze making it very pleasant. 
 Continued the training for the week, the battalion again marched out into the desert (taking lunch) &amp; spent the day at battle work. During the morning went off some distance with Prescott &amp; 1 or 2 others &amp; had some revolver practice with my Colt automatic, in the end being quite satisfied up to 75 paces although there is a distinct throw to the left (2 ft at 75 paces). Prescott also did some good shooting with his. 
 We marched back to camp about 3 o'clock &amp; after a bath &amp; a change I walked off to look for Jocelyn at B Details, Feitoun. After some trouble I found the place &amp; from the orderly room learned that he with the 2 friends who had come away together again as they had done last year before Gallipoli had only that morning been sent down to Ismailia. 
 Had a lo9ng tramp back &amp; after dinner ran into Cairo to tell Oll that it was my intention to try &amp; get the next day off so as to have a run round with her. 
 Back in camp by 9 o'c. 
 Friday 10 March 1916 
 Again a beautiful day. 
 After breakfast obtained leave for the day from the adjutant &amp; abt 9.30 left for Cairo to meet Oll. Found she had lunch packed &amp; after getting one or two more necessities we had just time to get to the Sultan's palace in order to see his guard mount.
