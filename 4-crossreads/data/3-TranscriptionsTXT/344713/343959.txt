Fri. July. 13th: -Left Buire & marched through Dernancourt, Meaulte, Becoadel on to outside of Bray (side of Bronfay Farm) about a 10 mile journey to go 5 miles - Full pack again.We are a couple of miles from Cappy, Suzanne, Carnoy & Maricourt.
Tues, July 24th: - Left Bronfay Farm Camp & marched past Fricouat & Becordel through Meaulte & Dernancourt to Buire again to our old Billets.We must be a merry-go-round.
Fri. July. 27th: -Left Buire, entraining at Edge Hill & passed through Amiens, Bethune, St Pol, Hazebrouck to Cassel station. Marched through Bavinchove & billeted at Zuytpeene.
Sun. Aug 5th 1917: -Yesterday the finish of 3rd year of war; Tomorrow the anniversary of Lone Pine.Today went to church at Bavinchove at foot of Cassel hill.
Mon. aug. 6th.At La Nieppe as witness in Moran's F.G.C.M. at 3rd Bn. Headquarters.
