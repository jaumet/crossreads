<p>Christmas Day 1918</p>
<p>My Dear old Mother</p>
<p>I wonder what you are all doing at home today. Is it not lovely having a Christmas with &quot;Peace on Earth&quot; and the thoughts that next one we will all spend in the dear old home together.
