<p>but the majority had no time for anything like that, they only thought of the cold.<br/>22nd.   Very very cold last night, &amp; a sharp frost this morning, made one pull some funny faces while shaving before sunrise in the open.<br/>Had a parade this morning &amp; the R.C's were marched off to church in one of the little Catholic churches round here while the rest of us went out on parade for our padre is away crook.  Most of the morning was put in on physical jerks &amp; running about to keep the men warm for it was very cold out in the air, had a little Coy. drill &amp; finished up on a route march.<br/>On getting back they held an Orderly Room" &amp; dealt with a few cases &amp; some of those who played up the other</p>
