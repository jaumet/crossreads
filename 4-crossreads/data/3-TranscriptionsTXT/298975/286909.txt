 for King or country As night fell the sea got worse also the sickness I faced going below about 7 PM &amp; managed to swing a hammock &amp; hop into it. It proved more comfortable than it looked &amp; I slepped without a break untill 6 in the morning. 
 We sleep &amp; eat in the same place &amp; it is very stuffy in rough weather when the port holes are closed. Otherwise things are good
