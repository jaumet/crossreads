It was situated about 10 miles from Cairo and quite close to the Pyramids.  Our training was resumed in the shape of route marching over the desert, and what with the heat, sand and flies these were by no means pleasure trips.  Some days a heavy wind would fill the air with swirling sand and life under canvas was horrible.  On these occasions only the absolutely necessary work was done as everyone tried to keep within a tightly laced-up tent.
