<p>And passing through Picquigny. Amiens &ndash; and between Cambrae and St. Quentin we arrive at Le Cateau at 9 p.m.<br/>During the journey  evidence of the good news is shown by the display of flags &amp; bunting &ndash; and the hope &ndash;<br/>Last of all are the German prisoners working on the roads, who wave and smile as we pass &ndash; Our own lorry is well decorated and attracts considerable attention &ndash;<br/>Spend night in shell shattered &amp; deserted shop &ndash;</p>
