<p>Jan 1918</p>
<p>Monday<br/>Got on a Bayonet Fighting compition squad</p>
<p>Tuesday 15<br/>Practicing Bayonet Fighting<br/>Snowed all Sunday night about 8 in of snow on the ground in the morning. Lots of snowballing. An organised fight between 2nd &amp; 4th &amp; other Co</p>
<p>Wednesday 16<br/>Compition came off today No 2 Co won. Our Guard</p>
