  
 445 7 as a bit of a waster  - no doublt I was but don't think I am now - Dr Munro kindly sent me a lot of dates which were jolly good - I hope next week I will be able to tell you I am  up and the week after that something definite - Sister Holmes said good-bye today - she goes in the same ship as this letter - she will be in Sydney about Christmas - I hope she has a chance of seeing you but am a little afraid you will be at the [indecipherable] Fondest love to Mother Billy  and self and Constance and Ernest and the kids. Hoping you all have a jolly Christmas Your very affectionate son Gibson
