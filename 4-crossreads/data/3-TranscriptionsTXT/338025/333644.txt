Monday 26th MarchEtaplesFinal medical examination before going to the firing line. Concert at the Y.M.C.A Hut in the evening.Met Len. Georges of Malvern on Saturday evening (24th) at a concert in the Y.M.C.A. Hut. Greatly surprised to see John and I, came round to our tent the next evening Sunday 2/3/17.
