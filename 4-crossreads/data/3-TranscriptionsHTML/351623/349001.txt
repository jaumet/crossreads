<p>23 Nov 16<br/>Returned to Rosyth &amp; coaled ship.</p>
<p>24 Nov 16 )<br/>25 Nov 16 )  Anchored at Rosyth<br/>26 Nov 16 )</p>
<p>27 Nov 16<br/>Our Rugby football team played the H.M.S. Yarmouth. Our team won.</p>
<p>28 Nov 16 )  Anchored at Rosyth.<br/>29 Nov 16 )  Admiral Beatty has taken over the sole command of Fleet &amp; Admiral Jellicoe has gone to the Admiralty.</p>
<p>30 Nov 16<br/>Saw two British Airships.<br/>Proceeded to sea at 1 pm. At 7 pm we were recalled &amp; anchored at Rosyth again at midnight.</p>
<p>1 Dec 16<br/>Coaled ship at 2 am in morning watch.</p>
