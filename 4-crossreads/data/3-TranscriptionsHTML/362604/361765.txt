<p>Billy in Manly although that is very unlikely.  After dinner I went up to D.J's &amp; saw Percy Devereaux &amp; Mr. Charles.  I put the acid on C.L. for &amp;pound;5 which you might pay.  I expect to go to Havre soon &amp; I could not wait for Pay-Day &amp; would not borrow.  I expected to be going to Glasgow &amp; Edinburgh this week but I am undecided yet as I have no idea when I might be wanted to march out.  After</p>
