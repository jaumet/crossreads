<p>in Egypt &ndash; 3 oclock would see me again  going into the sea. Afternoon tea at 5.<br/>Shop, stroll around or read until about 7.30 then another excellent meal at the Caf&eacute; du Nil &ndash; where they have a French chef -  &amp;  so to bed generally not later than 10.That was my usual programme for the day &ndash; and very nice too.<br/>There seems to be a scheme for an attack on a large Turkish Outpost camp near El Arish shortly. A risky proceeding I think</p>
