<p>February 1918<br/>Wednesday 6</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Weather fine and clear<br/>Had a look around the vicinity and went across to Anti Tank position and saw Gunn and Russell.<br/>Garford on O.P.<br/>Received small amount of mail.<br/>Had the 10 to 12 watch.<br/>Played cards until ten</p>
