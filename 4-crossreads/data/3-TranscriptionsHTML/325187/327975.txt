<p>[Sunday 20 August 1916]</p>
<p>All the boys in the Hospital come &amp; see me &amp; we fight Romani over again, <u>they</u> know who squibs it &amp; who plays the game.<br/>All are getting over their wounds well- but are indignant at the idea of going to a RAMC Convalescent Hospital instead of Australian.<br/><strike>All the East of Suez Canal is quarantined for CHLORA,[cholera] so no one can get leave from there.</strike><br/>Went to Church in Pyjamas &ndash; as before a Chapel is part of these Barracks.</p>
