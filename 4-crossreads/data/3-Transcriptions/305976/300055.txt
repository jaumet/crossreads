<p>Monday Sept 30<br/>We are expecting to move in a few days to a better camping ground. It is impossible to stay here for long or all hands will be in Hospital. The weather at present is beautiful &amp; large numbers of Turkish prisoners are coming in daily.<br/>Teusday Oct 1<br/>We rode down to the Water troughs about one mile from station with a number of prisoners to bury the dead Horses.
