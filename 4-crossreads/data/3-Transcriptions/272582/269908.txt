<p>Committee was formed in the Camp for purpose of celebrating Anzac Day. This was done, and the evenings entertainment took the form of a social evening, participated in by members of the 4th   &amp; 5th A.I.F. bakeries, and members of the W.A.A.C. of the bakery, and a pleasant evening was spent by all.</p>
<p>1 man rejoined unit from hospital.</p>
<p>26/4/18.<br/>39 N.C.O.'s and men baked 23,568 lb rations. Owing to a slight fall in the issue, the 10 pm to 6 a.m. shift did no baking.</p>
<p>27/4/18.<br/>58 N.C.O's and men baked 39,600 lb rations. 1 man rejoined unit from A.A.S.C. Reinf Depot. Ex Inv to Eng.</p>
<p>28/4/18. <br/>39 N.C.O's and men baked 23,240 lb rations  <u>Sunday.</u>  The usual change was effected in shifts, there being no work done by the 2 pm to 10 pm shift.</p>
