
<p>Parody . Do they want us</p>
<p>to Lose the War.</p>
<p>&mdash;&nbsp;&nbsp; &mdash;&nbsp;&nbsp; &mdash;</p>
<p>The 4<sup><u>th</u></sup> command depot</p>
<p>is one of the best</p>
<p>Where war weary soldiers</p>
<p>get a much needed rest</p>
<p>And plump Canteen Corporals</p>
<p>all hearty &amp; hale</p>
<p>Laugh &amp; grow fat while</p>
<p>they guzzle brown Ale</p>
<p>Chorus</p>
<p>Do they want us to lose the War</p>
<p>To spread the sad tidings</p>
<p>to Camps near and far</p>
<p>Up soldiers together give</p>
<p>one hearty cheer</p>
<p>Shout death to the traitors</p>
<p>who water our beer</p>
