<p>March 8th</p>
<p>Arived at Port Phillip about 7 am and Anchored just near the Quarentine Stn The boys were told they to to go into Quarentine and refused to leave the boat. We were there all day when word came they would only be there two day. They decided to go and went ashore in punts We left the outer harbour</p>
