<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - R.A.</p>
<p>To - Brigadier</p>
<p>Date - 13/9/14</p>
<p>Time - 9.35</p>
<p>Lieut McKenzie will be sent onboard Berrima for duty with troops I have received no report from Comdr. Beresford I thought you would have had one. &nbsp;Should I get it before you I will inform you of fact.</p>
