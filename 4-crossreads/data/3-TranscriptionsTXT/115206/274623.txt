 February 1918 Friday 22 
 Messines Walked down to F.O.O. and "took" Jones dinner. Jones is going to another Battery. Had a great feed last night and tonight. I was chef and dished up, Soup-a la Oxe-des Ra, Turnips, Mashed &ndash;a la Bully Beef, and Xmas Pudding and choice almond flavoured custard. Had the 10-12 and 6-8, so had my share of "Yakker". Fritz very quiet. Wrote to London for Blue,bhewars
