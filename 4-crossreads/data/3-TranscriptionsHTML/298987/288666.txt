<p>Saturday 14<br/>Being the only N C.O on the Guard had no sleep last night. It seemed strange haveing the land close again. The new Guard came on at 9 a m as usual &amp; about midday we pulled in to the wharf for coaling All troops were taken ashore &amp; marched to the Town Hall about 2 miles on the way it rained the real stuff streams of it. In 2 minutes we were soaked But what did that matter we were on land miles of it to move about on after <strike>the</strike> a month on the limited space of the old boat<br/>Went by tram to Toll Gate quite a pretty suburb. Got a good deal of fun out of the Ricksha Men &amp; had several rides in them. Would not like to use them as a steady thing even allowing that the men are</p>
