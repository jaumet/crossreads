 14 February 1916 Monday 
 A day of surprises &amp; unfortunate happenings. Strike by soldiers at Casula &amp; Liverpool for shorter hours of drill. Procession from Casula  after breakfast to Liverpool. Broke up hotels &amp; cleared out the drink. Lighthorsemen came out after dinner. Grave position &amp; [indecipherable] disgrace to the Army. Thousands go to Sydeny &amp; play havoc there.  Tony, Bob &amp; self into Liverpool to view damage.  Meet Bob Abernethy. Concert at Methodist Tent. Hotels wrecked &amp; refreshment [indecipherable]
