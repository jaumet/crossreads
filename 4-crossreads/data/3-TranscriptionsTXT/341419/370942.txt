 20.10.18 Suweileh [Suweile] The last week or so we have been scratching ourselves &amp; swearing at the fleas in a camp near the Station at [indecipherable].  Now we are taking captured stuff back to Es Salt from Ahiman &ndash; the most antiquated collection of tinware called guns that you could imagine.  Two guns are smooth bore &amp; bear the inscription "Essen 1874".  We have halted here for the night &amp; I am writing this by the light of a gorgeous full moon.
