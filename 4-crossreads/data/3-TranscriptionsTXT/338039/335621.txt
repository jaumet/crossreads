 Strazeele &amp; then on to Boire, where we spend night in Barn.  Fine country (hops, wheat &amp; vineyards)- Church (1626)-  Our billet originally in hands of British in a skirmish with Germans who occupied a farm house 700 yards away- Germans driven out- Farmhouse shows signs of the frogs.
