<p>population came out &amp; gave us a hand to extricate the ford car from the mud, they pushed kneed deep in mud our skidding wheels sending mud flying like a Catherine wheels. The gipos made neat fun of it &amp; put us on the road to Esdus wher[e] we attived half an hour later. After exiting our tent we had a good hot tea &amp; laid down in our blankets talking over the trials we had had getting our equipment about the country.</p>
