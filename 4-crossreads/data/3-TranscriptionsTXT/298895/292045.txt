Wednesday 11th AprilWeather cold with plenty of snow.  Had our dinner &amp; concert which had been postponed &amp; which now turned out a huge success.  The Dinner was soup, meat &amp; vegetables, fruit salad custard, pudding, with biscuits chocolates, nuts etc beer &amp; cigars plenty of everything &amp; the best feed I have seen in the army.  Then the concert went very well everyone being in good spirits, the Colonel was in the chair &amp; did very well, altogether the celebration being a huge success.  General Howse  was round today &amp; inspected the whole concern a job he does not waste time about as many do &amp; seemed well satisfiedThursday 12th April.Everyone feeling heady today, not at all brisk.
