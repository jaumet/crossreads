<p>Division under orders to move out at 1 hrs notice.<br/>Monday Oct 8<br/>Usual routine<br/>Tuesday Oct 9<br/>Saw fine scrap in air to day. Two Taubes came over &amp; one of our planes gave them a go. Brought one Taube down near Somerset Battery lines &amp; drove other back to Turkish lines.<br/>Wednesday Oct 10<br/>Mail arrived today after a long break. All well at home. Heavy sand storm in afternoon. Column moving up closer to Gaza<br/>Thursday Oct 11<br/>Heavy bombardment in progress. Jacko over observing in planes all day.</p>
