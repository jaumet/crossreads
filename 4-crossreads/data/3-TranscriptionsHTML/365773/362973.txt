<p>Xmas card of Arras - 1915 - 14.10.1918 To my dear niece 'Beres'<br/>Arras is a big French town ruined by Hun shell fire &amp; bombs. The beautiful Cathedral being specially selected for the enemy fire.<br/>This city was gallantly defended by the Canadians in March 1918.<br/>Ken "Uncle"</p>
