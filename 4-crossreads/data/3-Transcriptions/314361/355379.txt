<p>If we could get the same pair back it would be OK. Every man has to change his socks once a day.<br/>We are having a very quiet time since we arrived back in trenches, though I can hear heavy bombardments on each side of us. There is a lot of rumours about of us making a shift away from this sector all together &amp; also of more raids &amp; stunts impending.<br/>I am well off for clothes just now, have had a new issue &amp; have managed to secure two pairs of trousers slacks &amp; [cycling?]<br/>Received letter from Matt.<br/>Received another photo from Lou the other day, she is looking fine, the real lady that she is.<br/>Friday 13 th 16<br/>There was a raid last night made by the 54th As usual we had a terrible bombardment. The din was awfull, &amp; flashes of light like lightning everywhere &amp; streams of bullets whizing through the air. Do not know how they got on, as good as we did I hope. I believe that we are leaving today for some unknown destiny, the Somme eventually I believe.</p>
