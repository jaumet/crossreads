
Sunday 14 April 1918Feelg. very dirty & unshaven.  Reminds me of Gallipoli.  D. Corp captured 2 prisoners last night.  One died on the way down.
Monday 15 April 1918Came down from the Bn. to the horse lines in Aubigny & had a good bath & the first shave for nearly two weeks!!
Tuesday 16 April 1918We move up & relieve the 53rd Battalion at Villers Bret.  The village reeks of gas & we wear our masks all the time.  Some of my sigs. go out to it.
Wednesday 17 April 1918Gassed - well & truly!!! at Villers Bretonneux!!  Don't know much what happened after 8 a.m.
Thursday 18 April 1918I was unconscious most of this day but remember being bandaged by a nurse in the C.C. Station.
Friday 19 April 1918Admitted into No. 3 General - Le Treport.
Saturday 20 April 1918For the next few days I lost all interest in life being blind & a lot more hopeless things.
