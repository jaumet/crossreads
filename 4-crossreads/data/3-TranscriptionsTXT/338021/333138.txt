
To O.C. 13th M.G.Coy
Have personally visited guns & crews. Everything alright. Cannot find any good material at Right position.
Can manage with present s.a.a. getting supplies from Infantry Casualties, but shall need more sent up tonight.
Casualties up to presentHinton, Hunter woundedMaples killedCpl Hamper missingSome of Battalion men are missing but expect they have reported their [Indecipherable]Condition of crews: in excellent state especially men who were sent up to line first & are asking for spell.CHP Weston Lt
