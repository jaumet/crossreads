 Sunday, 29/4/17 Reveille at 7 a.m. & breakfast about 8 a.m.  Had church parade at 9 a.m. & while it was on several aeroplanes were flying about.  Afternoon went to Ludgershall to Soldiers Rest & sent p/cards to Australia.  After tea went with Ned to Y.M.C.A. at Perham Downs about 11/2 miles across the valley. 
 Monday, 30/4/17 Reveille 6.45 a.m.  Breakfast about 7.30.  Had little of everything in drill & lecture by Medical Officer.  Afternoon dismissed about 3.45 p.m.  Seven aeroplanes flew over all together & numbers of others about during the day.  Went after tea to Ludgershall Y.M.C.A. & wrote a letter to find out Roy's whereabouts.
