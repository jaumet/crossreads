 Oct 12 It is just getting dusk as we are running into Port Said having had a  smooth  calm &amp; uneventful journey 
 Oct 13 We did not disembark last night as expected, but this morning the British Troops have been getting off &amp; the Ship is we believe taking us on to Kantara this afternoon (kantara = E1Qantara on Suez Canal) 
 We shall be glad to get off for she is a  Hungry  Ship. We have sent in complaints &amp; the only effect was to have some rigmarole read out about being on "Lines of Communication Scale
