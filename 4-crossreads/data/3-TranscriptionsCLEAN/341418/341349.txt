  
 "Hill 40" May 19 Now we are quite the other side of Egypt altogether, but I had better record how we got here first. We managed to get all our horses  &  stuff on board the train at Sohag by about 9.30 on Sunday night. I provided a very fitting climax to my unpleasant stay at Sohag by losing my money belt with a fiver in it just before we left. We spent an uncomfortable night in the train.
