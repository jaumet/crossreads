 Thursday 10 April 1919 Orderly officer today. Did very little. Rained all day. Bill got word of going to Blighty for non military employment. 
 Friday 11 April 1919 Pass came thro &amp; Bill managed to get a duty pass for Paris. Rushed around a bit &amp; we managed to catch the 5 pm train. Got to Paris &amp; we went to the Aussie Y.M. &amp; got a room (Hotel [indecipherable]). Took Bill &amp; Sgt. Kerr to the Boulevard for a walk &amp; showed them a few things.
