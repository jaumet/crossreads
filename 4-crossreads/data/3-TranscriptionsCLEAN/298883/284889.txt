  
 10th I went for a pleasant bike ride through Rogeant and Moyenville this afternoon.  The country round about is extremely pretty and peaceful.  France must have been a happy land in pre-war days. 
 11th In accordance with Corps Orders received at Le Mesnil re the re  direction  organising of each brigade  of  into only three battalions instead of four, the 54th Bn. were amalgamated  to  with the 56th Bn this afternoon.  The 56th Bn. marched over to our village here and both Bns. formed up on the one parade ground as one Battalion.
