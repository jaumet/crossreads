<p>April 1918<br/>Monday 1</p>
<p>In Billets- Orville<br/>Went down to Santos last night , with boys, had a great time, had quiet a good series of manoeuvres during morning and afternoon. <br/>Weather glorious<br/>At 2 am. this morning Fritz paid us a visit and dropped quite a considerable number of bombs around the district.<br/>After tea walked to Santos and had a feed with Ken Gunn.</p>
