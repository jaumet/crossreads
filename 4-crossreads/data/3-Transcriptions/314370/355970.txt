<p>January, 1916<br/>Saturday 22<br/>Exam "Mechanism" this morning<br/>Spend afternoon w/- dad &amp; make arrangements for going out tomorrow<br/>Get a letter from Aunty Emily</p>
<p>Sunday 23<br/>Go into Cairo w/- dad &amp; have a long walk through the Native Bazaars &amp; watch the Arabs making rope<br/>Have dinner in town &amp; turn in about 10 pm.</p>
