<p>September 1918.<br/>Saturday 14</p>
<p>In Reserve. Hebecourt<br/>Weather showery, and very unpleasant.<br/>News to hand tells old Ped wounded in foot, leg, thigh and shoulder very sorry &ndash; one of the best.<br/>A few Fritzs across tonight. <br/>Overhauled a fair amount of wire.<br/>Rumor going into line again</p>
