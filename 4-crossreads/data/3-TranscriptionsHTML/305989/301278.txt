<p>Sunday 14 October 1917<br/>Quite day, hands very sore.</p>
<p>Monday 15 October 1917<br/>Had snap taken of the Dumpites:  nothing doing.</p>
<p>Tuesday 16 October 1917<br/>Taube over:  one brought down in Wadi, nothing doing in particular.</p>
<p>Wednesday 17 October 1917<br/>Got two parcels from Aunt Rose &amp; Dorothy.  Saw a good concert given by The Tanks at Y.M.C.A. had good supper.</p>
<p>Thursday 18 October 1917<br/>Quiet day, got a lot of new gramophone records, quiet night, read after supper.</p>
<p>Friday 19 October 1917<br/>1st Anzac Mtd. Dump moving into Shellal Crossing;  helping the lads to pack up.</p>
<p>Saturday 20 October 1917<br/>Quiet day.  Matt Ward came back from Port Said.</p>
