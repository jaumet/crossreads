Now we are into it again close to a Jewish settlement which is here, it is a pretty little town quite clean & English in appearance full of Russian French & English Jews, who were delighted to see us & gave us oranges & bread etc etc, very acceptable.
We had our battery on a hill just above the town & it was very funny to see the womenfolk - which are very pretty
