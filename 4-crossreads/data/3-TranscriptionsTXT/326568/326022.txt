 June. 1st.  After a run of varied weather across Indian Ocean, we reached Fremantle this morning &amp; experienced (some on board) the welcome home. After disembarking W.A. troops, we had leave &amp; I went up to Perth. Fine Sunday afternoon. 6th.  Arrive off Outer Harbour, Adelaide at 6 a.m. &amp; disembarked troops at 1 p.m. &amp; left at same hour.  Glorious day.
