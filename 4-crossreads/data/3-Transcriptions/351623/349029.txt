<p>13th March 18<br/>Arrived Rosyth &amp; coaled &amp; oiled.</p>
<p>14 March 18<br/>Anchored at Rosyth.</p>
<p>15 March 18<br/>Went into 14 days cells on HMS Crescent. Had a good time on a few biscuits a day &amp; picking oakum. I also had 45 days pay stopped, dipped a badge &amp; 6 months leave stopped, for forgetting there was a Navy for 6 days.</p>
<p>29th Mar 18<br/>Released from cells. Had to wait on board Crescent till 1st April till ship returned.</p>
<p>1st April 18<br/>Went on board.</p>
<p>2nd April 18 )<br/>3rd April 18 ) Anchored at Rosyth</p>
<p>4th April 18<br/>Went out &amp; done firing</p>
<p>5th April 18<br/>to 10th April 18 Anchored at Rosyth</p>
