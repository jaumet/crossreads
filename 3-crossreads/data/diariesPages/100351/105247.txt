
Tues. 4-7-’16.
9th Ord. Platoon to-day. Fatigue work making Sap to new Coy. HQrs. Posted letter to P’down & to Dan Toohey 59th. S.M. re my mail. Told off to take charge of Guides who will lead Eng. Regt. ("B" Coy West Kents) into Saps we are evacuating t-night. This Job took all evg. up 8.45-11 P.M.
Left "A" Sector Subsidary Line 12 midnight
Gordon Anderson off to Etaples.
Wed. 5-7-’16.
Arrived in Huts in Wood 2 a.m. ½ mile back from Firing line. Good Sleep. Bk’fast 10 am. Rest of day ours.
