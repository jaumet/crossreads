
Tuesday Sept 18thRained a little this morning First rain I have seen since landing.Heard Anzac Div band this afternoon. Nothing much doing while in this camp so Tom & I having a good time..Wednesday Sept 19Moving tomorrow to Abbasia. Australian Division taking over place here for a spell. Allotted Horse. Seems a decent old Neddy. Jet black Star on forehead. Wrote mail.Thursday Sept 20Moved to day. Very windy day so had a most unpleasant trip across the sand. Arrived at new Possy about 4 pm. Tom gone to Rafa on duty.
