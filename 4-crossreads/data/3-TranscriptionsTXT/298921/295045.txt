
March, 1915Saturday 13my a/c No 10850 with the Commonwealth BK of Australia London making 200/-/- there to my Credit.We spend a lot here, an officer is expected to go to all the most expensive parts of every place & every one is out to rob & get Backsheesh.We only take 35 lbs luggage from here & our Blankets, & expect to leave on Tuesday the 16th Mar probably Alexandretta, but will let you know later meantime I post you this with love to you all.
