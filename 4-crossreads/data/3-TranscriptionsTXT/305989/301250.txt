 Sunday 1 April 1917 Church parade in morning.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic number for 2 and 5]. 
 Monday 2 April 1917 1st Regt. v F.A. &amp; M.G. combined draw no score. 
 Tuesday 3 April 1917 Played 1st Field Troop lost 3-0, two other games in progress. 
 Wednesday 4 April 1917 Brigade Races in the Wadi did not go:  on picquet at night. 
 Thursday 5 April 1917 Clarrie came back off leave.  4th Brigade came through:  saw all the boys:  getting ready to move eastwards. 
 Friday 6 April 1917 Left El Arish in morning coming through El Arish &amp; passing 4th Brigade, proceeded to Shiek Zowaiid, camped the night. 
 Saturday 7 April 1917 Left Shiek Zowaiid early &amp; got to Rafa about dinnertime &amp; saw places familiar in the scrap, arrived at Khan Yunis about 4 o'clock:  &amp; camped after watering up.
