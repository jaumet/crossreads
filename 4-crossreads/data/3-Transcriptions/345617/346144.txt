<p>Aout 10<br/>Not much doing today. A little Sec. drill &amp; overhaul of equipment &amp; a little marching. Nothing of importance reported from the Fronts.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Had Coy route march today, distance about 12 miles. The roads are very hard &amp; are very tiring for the feet.</p>
<p>12 Aout<br/>Had easy time today. Cpl. Morrison &amp; I went to St Omer &amp; had an enjoyable day.  Had a look at 3 old cathedrals. One is a mere ruin only the tower &amp; one wall standing. It was destroyed in one of the Revolutions. One large cathedral now in use is apparently very old &ndash; but I could not ascertain the actual age. Like most other</p>
