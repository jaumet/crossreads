 1917 Diary August 20th Hospital &ndash; Have applied for leave but do not think I will get it 21st Went for a [indecipherable] to "Agony Island" as those who are on deck call it &ndash; it is one of the old Forts blown up by bombardment I feel fitter but "short of a gallop" Still packing up &amp; leave for the Desert in the morning
