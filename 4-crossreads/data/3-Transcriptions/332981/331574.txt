<p>The four engines were ready with their crews, with the kits in a van behind and we left the Depot at 12 noon arriving at Conchil  le Temple at 12.15 were we had to wait until the French Railway officials were ready to let us on the Nord Line.</p>
<p>My relief driver worked the engine &amp; I rode in the van. For some reason or other we did not leave Conchil until 6 pm and after a lot of delays we arrived in Calais at 8.30 pm were the engines were stabled and all hands made themselves comfortable for the night, half the number on men slept in a hut &amp; the remainder in the van.</p>
