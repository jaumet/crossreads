<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&amp;  got back about 10 p.m. just beginning to get dark then. The sunsets are beautiful but the other morning (about 2 am.) Do   &amp;  I were simply spell-bound watching the moon rise.</p>
<p>Have not seen nor heard of Charlie this week, suppose he is busy at the Training- School.</p>
<p>Fred has not been along, they are a long way from here &ndash; takes over 3 hrs to ride. Rumour has it they are moving North. We may follow.</p>
