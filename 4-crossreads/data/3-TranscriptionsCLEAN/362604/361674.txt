  
 Australian Red Cross Bulford 2/6/18 
 Dear Mum, 
 Here is a lovely Sunday afternoon but very hot.  I am staying on Duty till half-past three & then I'll be finished for the day.  We both had a parcel from you this week.  The Jam in mine was O.K. & Ellis' cake lovely.  Do you know that every cake we have received from you has been glorious & fresh as a daisy, no matter how battered the tin.
