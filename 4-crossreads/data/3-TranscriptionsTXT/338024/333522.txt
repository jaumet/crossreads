 Mon. 13 Nov. 1916 Sighted lights of Cape Town at 7. 30pm.  Lay in Bay till next morning (arr in bay 9. 30pm) 
 Wed.  15 Nov. 1916 Route march in the morning.  Sport in oval in the afternoon, concert and tea in the evening at Feather Market. 
 Thurs. 16 Nov. 1916 Tram ride round lions head. Left Cape Town 6 am.
