<p>There will not be any mail for me for a considerable time I imagine for when I do move off, it will probably be in a new unit.<br/>"Sniper" Welchman of the 3rd is here &amp; also Brokhoff of the Queensland mob.  He transferred to Base detail from No. 2 Stationary.<br/>Today we have had a bad sandstorm (or "bad" to us).  The hot wind blows the sand all over one &amp; the flies are simply cruel.<br/>At night one is plagued by mosquitoes.<br/>Its all in the game however.</p>
