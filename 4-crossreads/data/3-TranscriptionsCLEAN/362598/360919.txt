  
 Sunday 2 April 1916 Went to Kasr-el-Nil [also spelt Qasr al-Nil] Gardens & Bridge.  Met Yankee girls.  Tea at Brit. Sold. Club at Esbekiah.  Met Said up the Waazaa.  Billy Bagnall & all watched the Dance, Vin, Ellis, Bill & I. 
 Monday 3 April 1916 Nancy Cohen, H. Hey, Nettie, Home, Doris. Joined the Cricket Club & started Hot Plasters. 
 Tuesday 4 April 1916 L. Porman, Home.  2 m. issued 4 pkts. Cigarettes.  Dave Niness gave me 4 pkts.  Billy Bagnall & I played Euchre, Crib & Casino while Ellis, Billy & Dave played Draughts. 
 Wednesday 5 April 1916 Went for a tour round the Mouski.  Ellis buys Blouse, I buy Gold Ash Tray.  Eric & I buy oranges & dates. 
 Thursday 6 April 1916 Ellis, Billy & I played draughts.  Night Wardmaster.  Shifted man from Boob to 4 Sydney 12 midnuit, much language. 
 Friday 7 April 1916 Morris' rabbit drive.  Tried to learn French verbs but am still trying.  Dreamt of J. Mather again, 3rd time. 
 Saturday 8 April 1916 More Smallpox, more rabbit drives.  Bread throwing stops.  No leave.  All Patients vaccinated.
