<p>July, 1917. 5th after Trinity. Sunday 8<br/>Another thunderstorm this morning and I had to move again and got wet. Very severe. Didn't attend first parade and Parade at 9.30 and were dismissed. Wrote letters all the morning. Fell in at 2 &amp; marched C.Cs. for burial of Cockburn. Home &amp; finished letters.</p>
