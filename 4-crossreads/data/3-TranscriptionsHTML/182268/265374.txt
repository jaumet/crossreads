<p>Monday 10/12/17. Training generally. &quot;C&quot; Coy Concert &amp; Supper Party.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11/12/17&nbsp; Feeling a bit off color&nbsp; quiet day, meeting of C.O&#39;s.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12/12/17. Battalion Scheme afternoon f/ball match against 5<sup>th</sup> Bde. Had dinner with C.n. Smith with 10<sup>th</sup> F. amb.</p>
<p>Thursday 13/12/17&nbsp; Reconnoitred front Line with Lloyd, going in front of [Warneton?]</p>
<p>Friday 14/12/17. Another trip to Front line, returned by The Shrine where the General was killed</p>
