In one portion of the trenches are the remains of what had once been a human being - every time I go through that section of the trenches, which are very narrow, I have to climb over this maimed corpse - there is not the time to bury him. We are putting up wire netting as protection from the bombs - we have very few bombs with which to return the enemy's continued onslaughts with this hideous weapon. The low dull thud of their explosion fills me with horror, as I can always picture the result; though God knows I am seeing quite enough horrors without the addition of my terrible imagination.
