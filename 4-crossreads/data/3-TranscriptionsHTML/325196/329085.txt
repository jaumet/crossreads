<p>12-1-16<br/>Wed 12th<br/>This morning started Les &amp; Reidy on sig. practice &amp; then went with Peters to the billiard room &amp; played billiards till dinner time arrived back to find Stan here, &amp; put the afternoon in visiting Browne (2nd Regt.) &amp; looking for Major Smith to see if Stan could draw money here. But could not find him, Stan went back to No.2 Auxiliary Hospital, &amp; I went to the A.V.C. to see Arthur, but could not find him, concluded he had gone to Cairo &amp; decided to follow, failed to meet him in Cairo, &amp; came back to Heliopolis, &amp; went into a cake shop for tea on my way to camp. &amp; found both Arthur &amp; Morgan there, had supper together &amp; then walked to Morgan's camp with him &amp; returned home shortly after 10 oclock; We were supposed to go away today, but put off till Friday; collected &pound;1 the Adj (White) owed me for a wager</p>
