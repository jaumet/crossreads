<p>We had a good trip down, although it was windy and dusty and fairly cold.</p>
<p>I see you have sent another cake. &nbsp;I hope it arrives in better condition than the last. &nbsp;Cpl Craddock received one a few days ago. &nbsp;It was iced and kept very well.</p>
<p>Later.</p>
<p>I will close this for the present &amp; drop a few lines later. &nbsp;Owing to some new arrangements I am off with a few more back the way we came. &nbsp;</p>
<p>I hope you are in the best of nick, and also, all at home.</p>
<p>Fondest love</p>
<p>Your loving son</p>
