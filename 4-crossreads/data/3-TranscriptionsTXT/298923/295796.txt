 10 May 1918 Friday Leave.  Spent the night on the Nile, cooler &amp; today did some more business for Dolly- an expensive luxury Babies!!! We go to Dinner tonight at Continental Hotel it is a change for Dolly from Pension &amp; she loves going out. The "Trumpeter" is well &amp; I'm sure you will be pleased to hear about the event! How about a name?  Ive got real Jordan Water for the Christening!!
