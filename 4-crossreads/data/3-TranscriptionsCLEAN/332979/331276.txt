Our Ship is darker than ever now & it is very awkward to move about up on deck & as the lights down below are lit until 10 pm all hands remained down until Bed Time. 
 Tuesday 13/3/17. Things all day were very quiet except  f when  that we were paid in the Afternoon, the last pay until we reach our destination, As there is only an amount of  2. placed on board for each man we had drawn 1/10/- previously so there were only a balance of 10/- coming to us.
