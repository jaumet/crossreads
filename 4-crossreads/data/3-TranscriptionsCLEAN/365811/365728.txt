  
 12.3.18 
 My dear Judge, I was talking of you last night to Pitt, he has not long returned from the U.K. & as I have a few minutes to spare think Ill send you a few lines. 
 I hear Keith is back in Australia, I hope he is getting on well, I was so sorry I missed him when in London. I trust Mrs. Ferguson & Dorothy are well, I hope the latter will forgive my speaking of her so familiarly, she must be quite grown up by now. 
 I receive Sydney papers regularly & am kept fairly well up in legal talk, there does not seem to be much new about it, I fear I have forgotten much that I knew in 1915 & I feel as if I would have to start reading on my return.
