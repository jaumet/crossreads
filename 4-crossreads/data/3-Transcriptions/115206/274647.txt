<p>March 1918<br/>Monday 18</p>
<p>Messines<br/>2 am. Fritz throwing them about rather liberally, very few in our direction.<br/>Another glorious day.<br/>Washed all the towels in the dugout.<br/>O.B.W., returned to W.L. Laurence arrived up at position.<br/>Fritz did some good ranging at 53rd and roadway, with H.E.s and 4.2 air bursts.<br/>11pm. Fritz shelled road with gas, and 5.9 H.E.s.<br/>Grayling out at O.P. <br/>Had a F.P.C. from A.D.S. who imparts information that he is well.<br/>Wrote R.L. and Mum</p>
