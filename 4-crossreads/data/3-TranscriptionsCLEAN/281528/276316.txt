  
 14/4/18 - Lavieville Some shells were put round our headquarters before dark but did no damage.  The Transport took back all our blankets and soiled clothes to the Transport Lines.  It was a windy and bitterly cold night.  The light continued good until about 8 p.m. and it was after that hour before any forward movement of our relief commenced.
