<p>nearly so warm as last year this time, also we are in a delightful spot  &amp; live under different conditions &ndash; Only I do want to get nearer my <u>beloveded</u>. It is a month to-morrow since I've heard from him but suppose , their letters are not allowed to come through yet. It is a very anxious time &amp;  at times I feel very miserable but must live in hopes &amp;  be patient.</p>
