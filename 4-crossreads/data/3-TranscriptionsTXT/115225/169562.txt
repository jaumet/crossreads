Memo  Fritz came over at 3.A.M. last Sunday. 5th with several machines & dropped bombs very close to us. we got the debris which they raised - The weather has been mostly wet this week.
 
May 12th  Fritz came over in the early morning with several planes dropping bombs in Amiens. We went out at 6.A.M. to Ailly-S.S. for supplies to Frechencourt brought back a load of wool from "Pont Noyelles to Ailly-S.S. in the afternoon returned to Amiens & took a load of wine to Belloy.Sur.Somme for a Froggy for a consideration - wet day -
 
May 13th  Fritz came over again early this morning, there are three machine guns mounted round the park & worked by some infantry billeted here ( we are parked in Hennesy's Farm yard - (of Hennesy's Brandy) three star cogniac) We went out on Supplies at 6.A.M. Ailly-S.S. to Frechencourt got home at 3.30 P.M.
 
May 14th  We left park at 5.A.M. & picked up a load of dry goods from a store in Amiens & took it to Ailly-S.S. for the Froggies - got to the railhead a bit
