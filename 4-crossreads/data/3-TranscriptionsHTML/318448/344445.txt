<p>&amp; we converse every day to her huge amusement.<br/>I got her sewing the other day &amp; she went through my entire wardrobe &amp; sewed buttons &amp; darned etc until everything was complete. A most useful girl. We make a beautiful picture of domesticity. I smoking my pipe at the table after lunch.  Miriam sitting at the tent door sewing in the sunshine. Henry placiding munching the contents of his feed bag &amp; the pup playing on</p>
