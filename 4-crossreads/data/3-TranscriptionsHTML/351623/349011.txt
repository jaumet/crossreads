<p>27 June 17<br/>Left Liverpool at 6 pm for sea. Weather cold &amp; misty.</p>
<p>28 June 17<br/>At sea steaming along west coast of Scotland. Did steam trial today average 25.7 knots.</p>
<p>29 June 17<br/>Arrived at Scapa Flow at 1 am this morning. Coaled ship at 9 am took in 380 tons.</p>
<p>30 June 17<br/>In Scapa Flow</p>
<p>1 July 17<br/>In Scapa Flow with Grand fleet.</p>
<p>2 July 17<br/>In Scapa Flow doing firing</p>
<p>3 July to 9th<br/>In Scapa Flow</p>
