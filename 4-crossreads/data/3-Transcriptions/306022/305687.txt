<p>Saw the last of NSW about 6 o'clock am.</p>
<p>Saturday 16th<br/>Didn't call at Melbourne, which was a bit of a disappointment.</p>
<p>The coolies provide plenty of amusement with their  habits etc. Washing seems to be a part of their religion, though whether it is to wash, or not to, I don't know. They wash in about 1 pint of water; first their hands, and then splash their face and run a wet finger round their ears</p>
