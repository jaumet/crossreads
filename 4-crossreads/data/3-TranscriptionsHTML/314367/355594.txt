<p>We are very cramped for room in the Fantome &ndash; Where they find room for 109 of us I don't know yet &ndash; We were off Ballina &amp; then Tweed Heads, driving North as hard as we could &ndash; We don't want to miss anything at Rabaul &amp; therefore we are doing our best to get up &ndash; The sea was smooth &amp; the weather glorious. So  far I much prefer this boat to the Upolu &ndash; all round &ndash; There is discipline &amp; an ordered life &ndash; The officers are all right &ndash; We have 6 who can play Bridge &ndash;</p>
<p>The morning is work &ndash; The aftn &amp; eveg have certain duties to be seen to but there is leisure &amp; talk &ndash; With this war on I shouldn't care to settle down to ordinary civilian life &ndash; You are in the game &amp; doing your little stunt with the best of the crowd &ndash; Stir up all the young men between 19 &amp; 35 to enlist is the game for all of us &amp; also for us senior men to take on whatever job offers &ndash; afloat or ashore &ndash; I'm making my S.B.A. get out the stretchers &amp; our haversack &amp; having everything put in order for instant service &ndash; He's slowly grasping that I'm boss, with a very strong will &amp; with a certain power of vocal expression &ndash; He's mostly Ass &ndash;</p>
