1918   March
21 THUR  Fine day. Battery in
action on & off day &
night. Fritz very active
H.E. & gas. Writing letters.
Posted one to W. Williams
Quiet night.
22 FRI  Fine day. Quiet. Posted
letter to Amy & encs. Mrs Mooney
Amy Mc & Celia Halsall & letter to Julie.
Quiet night.
23 SAT  Fine day. feeling very
sick from gas. stayed
in bed all day.
Very quiet. Heard about big german
offensive on Somme.
Leave to Blighty stopped
boys sent back from
Calais.
