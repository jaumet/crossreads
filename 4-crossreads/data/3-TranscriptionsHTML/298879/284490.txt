<p>December 1915<br/>Thursday 30<br/>Fine Arrived at Tel el kebir about 5.30 AM and marched out to our camping ground. One of our Sigs. did not turn up and had evidently either got off or fell off the train.</p>
<p>Friday 31<br/>Fine Our missing Sig. turned up safely.</p>
