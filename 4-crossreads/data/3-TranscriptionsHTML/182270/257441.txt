<p>FRIDAY, MAY 9, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Got into Sydney at 7 AM</p>
<p>&amp; got down to the Metropole</p>
<p>&amp; had a bath &amp; some breakfast</p>
<p>&amp; buzzed round town. Saw</p>
<p>Kieth Moses &amp; Linda came in to</p>
<p>lunch. Got a taxi home</p>
<p>for afternoon tea.</p>
