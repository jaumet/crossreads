 April 1915 Sunday 18 Our section had to mount guard at 9.30 AM. Church Parade  C. of E. . Weather dull but very calm. Projected boat trip to Queen Elizabeth with our Platoon Commander (Lieut Dawson) abandoned 
 Monday 19 Beautiful fine weather. Scarcely a ripple on the water. Had a good swim overboard. Water lovely. Another Aust Mail in. Had a grand little tour around the Queen Elizabeth &amp; other warships. We went on board the Prince of Wales &amp; were shown the works of the 12" guns. The charge is fired by a small cap or cartridge. A sailor put one in &amp; I fired it.   The sailors on the ships are real sports and very obliging
