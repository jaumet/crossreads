<p>Did I tell you she could not go on duty until last Friday because her eyes were sore.  They are better now &amp; she is happy once more.</p>
<p>I'm so glad that the wedding went off so well &amp; that you &amp; Belle enjoyed yourselves.  I suppose Archie will be leaving shortly.  It will be very hard for poor old Flo.  It must have been a pretty wedding &amp; they did things properly.</p>
<p>Suppose Belle has got over Annie's visit, hope they had a good time.</p>
<p>Sorry Edmund did not come here.  Is he on Military work?</p>
<p>Hope your "At Home" day is a success &amp; that you have plenty of visitors.</p>
