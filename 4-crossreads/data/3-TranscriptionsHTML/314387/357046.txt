<p>Monday 17<br/>Marched out of <strike>Ranqueham</strike> Raquiniem full marching order arreived Stienvoordie sixteen miles &amp; sunset<br/>Tuesday 18th<br/>Left Steenvourde morning marched to camp near Piperinghe Belgium<br/>Wednesday 19th<br/>Usual drill that night went for a walk to Abeele<br/>Thursday 20th<br/>Went up to have a look at a model of ridge to be attacked shortly nightime went to Poperingtle</p>
