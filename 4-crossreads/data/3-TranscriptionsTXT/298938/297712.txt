 Wednesday 20th Sept Received today a batch of letters including 2 from dear Rita. One was written in April, the 24th &amp; the other on June 23rd In the former Rita refers to having seen Mrs Fothergill that week. In another letter from Greta Mattlas  of a later date I learnt that poor old Fothergill was killed in action in Egypt on April 20th   just the time Rita wrote to me.
