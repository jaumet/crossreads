Monday 13.11.16
More road work, becoming quite an expert ganger. Taken to Fricourt baths in afternoon for a bath, first for a month. Met Roy McPhee Johnstone from Hospital. Having rather a rough time with 15 F.Amb; they are giving us a very poor deal.
Tuesday 14.11.16
Working in a quarry. No time whatever to ourselves. 18th 19th 20th Btns hopped over parapet took ridge before Baupaume. Subsequently there was a rush of wounded; had to work all night.
