 I got notified of my appointment as a Naval Surgeon from Aug 22 by the Naval Board at Melbourne &ndash; temporary service &ndash; 6 mo certain  -  You know this game suits me splendidly  -  I am absolutely at home in it &amp; the whole lot of us officers get on splendidly together  -  Not a single cross word have I heard on the rotten old sink of a ship called the  'Upolu'  since we left Sydney   -  That's something I often think how Rawdon wd  -  love to see all this - our diver &ndash; our motor launches &ndash; our jolly men   -  the natives &amp; their funny hair selling coco nuts  -  I got a Betel nut for R from one to day  -  I'm off the the shore &ndash; 2nd trip  - 
 I've returned &ndash; not much to be seen of men save pickets  -  I walked about &amp; noted the Bird life &amp; was pleased to see the black and white Fantail &ndash; our old familiar  -  Also a Kingfisher  -  Bird life in the harbor does not exist &ndash; not a gull or tern  -  Birdless  -  Its dry &ndash; very dry &ndash; no springs nor water supply &ndash; tanker only -  We are on an allowance per diem &ndash; 2 gallons for everything  -  Trees have been planted along the avenues &ndash; Casuarina avenue &ndash; Poinciana avenue I've christened them  -  A white Cedar &ndash; Mangos &ndash; Hibiscus &ndash; Croton &ndash; a Polypodium fern &ndash; a composite new to me was very common  -  Papaws do well &amp; I saw an Orange tree  -  No gardens anywhere as we understand the word garden  -  No [indecipherable] beds  -  Shrubs like Bougainvilleas
