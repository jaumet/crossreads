 No of A/c 69892 Com. Bank D Howes &ndash; Sandgate 
 are in waiting &ndash; also have a lot of German &amp; French prisoners working. It is really cruel to see the train loads of wounded coming in &amp; their broken limbs etc. 
 Nearly every woman here is in mourning for a relative of some sort &ndash; The women here are bricks, cleaning engines, working on the railway Good sheds &ndash; Farming etc. A Good many men are allowed to return to assist harvesting &ndash; Such a treat to find everything eatable etc scrupulously sweet &amp; clean after the cussed Gypos &ndash; Arrived at our destination about 4 pm &amp; marched about 4 miles from the siding Thienes to the village of Steenbecque, where we are temporarily billetted in stables &amp; barn's &ndash; rather rough but better than roughing it in all sorts of weather. It had been raining off &amp; on &amp; bitterly cold &ndash; We were all knocked up after the march, after being cramped in the train for three days &amp; nights &amp; 10 days on the Boat.
