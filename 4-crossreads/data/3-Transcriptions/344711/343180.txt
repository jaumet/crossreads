<p>2nd<br/>Good Friday.  On guard.  Everyone else having a holiday.</p>
<p>4th<br/>Easter Sunday.  Mess orderly.</p>
<p>5th<br/>A holiday in afternoon.  A real sandy, dusty beast of a day.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Had a big sham fight near Helouan.  Picquet at night.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Outpost work.</p>
<p>8th<br/>Had a big sham fight with A Squadron, and knocked spots off them.  Complimented in afternoon by Colonel Cox, who said "If you do as well at the Front, I will be satisfied."</p>
<p>9th<br/>Digging trenches all day, &amp; enjoyed a bit of hard work.</p>
<p>10th<br/>Easy morning's work, striking tents and cleaning saddlery.  In afternoon went to Cairo on leave, and went through the native bazaar.
