He sent me yesterday a newspaper cutting announcing the death of John D'Arcy, with his own comment that no doubt we should miss many of the old faces when we get back. This struck me as comic, unless he means the men who won't go back. The old familiar faces which did not shift out of Sydney town will still be there &ndash; pushed well in front of the struggle for life.</p>
