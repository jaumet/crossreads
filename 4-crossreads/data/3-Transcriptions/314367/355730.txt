<p>There's a lot of wasters about like that in all expeditions &ndash; However they generally get a very rotten time &amp; often wind up in gaol &ndash; I blame Johnson for a good deal &ndash; His absolute want of character makes him a cats paw among the men &amp; he glides from lie to lie &amp; plausible ones too like a snake from hole to hole &ndash; I had him up before an Officer &amp; J remarked that I didn't seem to believe him &amp; I said 'No I don't &ndash; I believe very little that you tell me &ndash; the greater part may be true or it may not' &ndash; Why a bad character like this goes with any expedition I know not &ndash; I took the man up to the Hosp from the Una as as Skeete &amp; the military don't let it &ndash; This time I had my men well in hand &ndash; "Shore party will stand by Stretcher until ordered to proceed". That kept them together &ndash; I got the man shifted &ndash; "Shore party will now man stretcher &amp; return to the whaler &ndash; Quick march"  I kept them all moving &amp; shoved off &amp; the job was done &ndash;</p>
