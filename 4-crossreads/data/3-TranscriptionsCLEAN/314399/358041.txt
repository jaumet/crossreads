
Thursday 13 February 1919Leave came thro for Blighty. Have to move some to get on to the boat but once on had to wait about 6 hrs. When we started tho it was O.K.  Had dinner on board.
Friday 14 February 1919Awakened at 5.30 & we had stopped.  Had a look & found we had stopped on account of fog. Made us very late. Didn't land till 1 pm. Had to collect the diggers & then got away. Wired Marie. Got train to London[Following 5 lines not transcribed]Went out to dinner with them and then to Brixton Theatre & saw Babes in the Wood. Was fair. Went to supper after & got home at 2.30 am.
