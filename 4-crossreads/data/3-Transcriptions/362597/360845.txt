<p>and saw the last of Australia at 7.30 pm.</p>
<p>Saturday New Year's Day (10)<br/>No parade in the morning. Sea a bit choppy &amp; one wave came into the Port Hole &amp; wet some of the chaps beds.</p>
<p>Held a boxing match at 2.30 pm. Rigged up a bonza ring on No 1 Hatch. Ropes &amp; corner posts &amp; all things stadium like. Spent a nice afternoon. Nearly all the boxers have bruized arms or fingers or cut lips &amp; black eyes.</p>
<p>Ship's staff gave another concert in F ward. Our nurses seated near us provided chocolates. We had sent them New Year Greetings early in the day.</p>
