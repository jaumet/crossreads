After having Breakfast at 10 am we were paraded for a roll & then dismissed until after Lunch when at 1-45 Pm we had a full dress parade & were inspected by the Colonel of the Camp who spoke very feelingly in welcoming us to Bordon & congratulated us on our successful although dangerous voyage from Australia. After giving us a few words of advice we were all Medically examined & then given some drill to liven us up, as the Weather is very cold & all of us feeling the change we welcomed the Marching Exercise.
