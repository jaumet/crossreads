 January, 1916 Thursday 20 Work as usual Semaphore Exam after dinner I made a little table &amp; book shelf this evening &amp; it looks O.K. with a white cover on top &amp; some red stuff on the sides &amp; front. 
 Friday 21 Work as usual in the morning &amp; after dinner march to Abbassia Range to fire Grouping &amp; Traversing practice return in time for tea
