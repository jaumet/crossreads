<p>November 1918.<br/>Friday 29</p>
<p>Wembley. On Furlough<br/>Went up to town at 11am. Met Smithy and Percy Grayling in the Aldwych Y.M.<br/>Went out to Lyons for lunch and then booked seats for "You never know Yknow" at the Criterion Theatre.<br/>Made up a neat little party and had a very enjoyable evening.<br/>Expect to leave tomorrow for Bristol to see Ken Gunn.</p>
