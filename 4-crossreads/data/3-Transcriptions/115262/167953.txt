<p>Thursday 5 July 1917.</p>
<p>On duty on open rifle range as A.M.C. detail in case of accidents Had an easy day.</p>
<p>1<sup>st</sup>&nbsp;mail from Australia. I got 7 letters. (3 from home, 1 from Toorak &amp; 1 from Burnley &amp; 2 from Joy. Wrote 1 to Dandenong &amp; posted it).</p>
