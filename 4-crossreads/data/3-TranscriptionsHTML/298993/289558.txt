<p>Friday Nov 10.11.16 returned from outpost stayed in camp H.Q camel picquet to night.<br/>Saturday 11.11.16 returned from picquet in camp taube came over air craft gun firing never got him taube very fast<br/>Sunday 12.11.16 stayed in camp washed clothes had a bath.<br/>Monday Nov 13.11.16 stand to 5 AM packed up and left mosefig 8 AM Bin Mazar arrived 1PM had dinner watered camels.<br/>Tuesday Nov 14.11.16. stand to 5.AM.  Packed up for a three days stunt left camp 8AM. Trecked all day country very rough very hot day camped at foot of stone hills.<br/>Wednesday Nov 15.11.16 Stand to 5.AM saddled up and left camp 7.AM trecked</p>
