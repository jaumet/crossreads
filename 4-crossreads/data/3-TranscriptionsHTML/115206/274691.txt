<p>May 1918<br/>Wednesday 1<br/>Bussy La Daours.<br/>Bill Aldred died of wounds at 11th C.C.S.<br/>Sympathised with Fred.<br/>Weather fine.<br/>A great amount of air fighting one of us was dropped handily, and another of Fritz came a wallop.<br/>Two more Fritzs dropped.<br/>Received 13 letters from home and elsewhere in Aust very welcome they were . one from Roy very cheerful . Going to Southall Hospital <br/>Rained at 10 pm.</p>
