<p>Harefield 1916</p>
<p>June 19 We draw our kit tonight &amp; go to Weymouth tomorrow. Rang up Com' Bank London but theres nothing there for me. "Rotten luck" ! Unable to get artificial eye until we reach Australia.</p>
<p>20 Up fairly early. 60 of us going today. Believe 300 wounded are waiting to come in from Plymouth &amp; Wandsworth. Nice fine day so will have a good trip. Left London at 12.25 &amp; arrived Westham Camp Weymouth about 5, after a nice trip through beautiful Country. There are about 25 or 30 of us<br/>(Dorset) [in margin] Weymouth (Dorset)</p>
