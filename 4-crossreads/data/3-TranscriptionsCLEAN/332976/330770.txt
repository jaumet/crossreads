  
 December 1916 19th (Continued)  We heard to-day that the French a few days ago advanced two miles on a six mile front & captured  of  over 8000 prisoners. To-day we had to made 50 "Hop over" ladders to be taken out to the trenches this afternoon for a raid which anticipated for to-night. We have been taking a few prisoners here lately, two & three a day.  They have evidently given themselves up & seem to be very pleased about it. 20th   The weather has been extremely cold all day & the ground has been covered with snow & frozen hard.
