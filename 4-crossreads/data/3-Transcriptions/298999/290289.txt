<p>Saturday June 2.6.17<br/>My Birthday to day.</p>
<p>Stand to 3 A.M. very foggy morning Blankets all wet fed up had cup of tea saddled up and moved off 5 A.M. the fog is very thick it is also cold I had to keep my great coat on trecked over some rough country went about 12 miles halted 11 A.M. unloaded had dinner.  it is very hot now.  rested till 3 P.M.  Packed up again and moved off 3.30 we went over some rough country saw a hare went about 10 mile and <strike>halted crosse</strike> crossed a stoney waddy halted and camped 6.30.  saw some Bedouins fed up.</p>
