 28 December Tuesday 1916 working hard reorganizing a lot to be done &ndash; Recd cards from Mrs Cotton &amp; others Please Mother write to D. Ish.  I send you some of her letters she is dear kid &amp; thinks or may I say loves me I know. I told her to send you her Photo. Her address Brooklands  Hotel Brooklands [indecipherable] but they are talking a house again. More letters delivered of Mothers &amp; Day Oct 10th &ndash; Aunt Louise Oct 16 everything is turning up from the 4 corners of the Mediterranean Top hole waistcoat but lovely weather here after Anzac
