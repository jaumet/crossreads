  
 28 January 1918 Monday (X to X crossed out)  Thomson -canteen- examine [indecipherable]  Heavy rain last night, the Bedouins  told us several days ago  that it would rain! My chest stands much better than I expected really, I weigh 13 stone 7 lbs-   X  Drink is the trouble in this "wine" country- the Blaspheming at night-time worst I ever listened to.  An Officers "Tunic" I gave our QM on the Beach I understand is assisting men to impersonate Officers & obtain grog. I believe in the RUM issue under supervision-  Just found old Day's message pinned in the Soks- Oct 2nd- Soks OK many thanks Day.X
