<p>18 April 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Entered Red Sea thro' St. Babel Mandeb &amp; Gate of Tears at sunrise. Perim over to the right, a British Naval Station guarding the southern entrance to the Red Sea. A most welcome sight after so many weeks of sea. Passed some remarkable volcanic islands &amp; Abu Ali Lighthouse at midday. In evening passed Twelve Apostles islands &anp; another lighthouse &amp; about 11p.m. another lighthouse. Received late news from passing steamer. the first since leaving Sydney.</p>
