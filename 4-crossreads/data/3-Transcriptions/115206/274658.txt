<p>March 1918<br/>Friday 29</p>
<p>Daylight Corner<br/>Weather wet. Getting ready for the road. Fritz as far back as one third of the way between the towns of Albert and Amiens.<br/>Major gave us the dinkum oil of what we are to expect shortly, and we are to leave at 2.30am tomorrow.<br/>Walked to Neuve Eglise and had a feed in the company of Adey, Da Costa.<br/>Weather still dull. Raining heavily.<br/>Fritz shelling Bailleul with H.O.</p>
