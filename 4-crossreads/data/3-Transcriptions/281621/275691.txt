<p>Sunday 25<br/>Beautiful day. Like last Sunday only spent under different circumstances.</p>
<p>Monday 26<br/>Raining, snowing &amp; windy. In village of Buire met a Parisian girl who knew Brighton &amp; Hove well. There on holidays.</p>
<p>Wednesday 28<br/>Anniversary of our arrival in France. Celebrate it with a bottle of Haumont &amp; drink to "next year at home". Unsettled weather.</p>
<p>Friday 30<br/>Very unsettled weather. A strong rumour hath that "B" Section is to go to form part of a new ambulance in the new 6th Division but no confirmation.</p>
<p>Saturday 31<br/>The advance generally continues &amp; now the line must be 20 miles away.</p>
