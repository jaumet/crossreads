Thursday 30 September 1915&nbsp;About Hospital all day&nbsp;Captain Bourke left hospital, as he was ordered for Alexandria.&nbsp;He was insulting when going away.&nbsp;
Friday 1 October 1915&nbsp;Notification received that I was to hand over hospital to Captain Bourke &amp; that I was to go to Alexandria on Monday the 4th inst.&nbsp;General Ford called with Major Bagshaw. Had a talk with the General&nbsp;
Saturday 2&nbsp;October 1915&nbsp;In Cairo during morning. Called on General Ford.&nbsp;He took me to General Maxwell.Had&nbsp;brief chat with the latter.&nbsp;Settled up accounts.&nbsp;Handed over to Captain Bourke at 1 pm.&nbsp;At Confession in Heliopolis at 8 p.m.&nbsp;
Sunday 3&nbsp;October 1915&nbsp;Rosary Sunday. Mollie&nbsp;Communion 7 am at Bassilica Heliopolis.&nbsp;Packing all day.&nbsp;At Zeitoun Convt between 6-30 &amp; 8-30 pm.&nbsp;Clearing up everything.&nbsp;
