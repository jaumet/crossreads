<p>Stringent measures are being taken to keep the number of British reinforcements up to the mark, and now even soldiers discharged physically unfit are being called up again for medical examination.  It is said that the number of men short of requirements is 100,000.  By this mail I am sending to the Wallad, a few hundred cigarette cards to play with so he won't have to ask fellows for them.  I don't know whether he is old enough yet to appreciate cards, but I am getting in early.</p>
