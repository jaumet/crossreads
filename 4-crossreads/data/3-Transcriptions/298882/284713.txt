<p>Left Romescamp by the 7.27 p.m. train on 3rd and arrived at Albert about 11 p.m. Stayed in Albert for remainder of last night and came on again this morning to the 1st Anzac Corps Sig. School where I am now comfortably installed.  Since leaving Poperinghe we passed through Calais, Bologne Etape&eacute;s Romescamp Amiens and Albert</p>
