
Naval Signal
From - Flag
To - Berrima
Date - 13-9-14
Time - 9-0
Following received from Encounter following received from shore.  Pass to Berrima.  I have received no copy of proclomation and should be glad to receive one.  Delay in informing you of this was owing to this afternoon operations my reports have also been delayed for same reasons.  I would will endeavour to send them in the morning.  1855
