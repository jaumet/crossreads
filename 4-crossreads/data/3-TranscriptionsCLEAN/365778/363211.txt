
Tuesday 7/8/17Coy training & route march, weather now good - ground drying up.Wednesday 8/8/17Wrote to Doff, rode down to see Corlette & Slane.Thursday 9/8/17Took the Battalion for a 15 mile route march, not a single man fell out of the march.Friday 10/8/17Seconded from Battalion now to command 2nd Aust Div Reinf Camp (a Dud).Saturday 11/8/17Went to St Omer, also Renescure, had a bad headache all day.Sunday 12/8/17Inspected site of proposed Camp at Bandinghem with Corlette inspected Bn Billets during evening.
