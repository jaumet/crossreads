
<p>June &nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>9 SUN &nbsp;Fine day. Went into</p>
<p>St. Omer on byke. called at</p>
<p>Aerodrome saw Capt. Malley.</p>
<p>Went on to town. Had glorious</p>
<p>time with Sgt. Savage &amp; girls.</p>
<p>Stayed overnight. Posted</p>
<p>letter to Julie.</p>
<p>10 MON &nbsp;Fine day. Heavy storm</p>
<p>at noon. Moved camp. Amm</p>
<p>going up. Quiet. Arr: home</p>
<p>7.15 am. Quiet night</p>
<p>Lt. Donoghue arr. back.</p>
<p>11 TUES &nbsp;Fine day. Rec. letter</p>
<p>from Amy. Amm: going</p>
<p>up. Fairly quiet. RS.Clarke</p>
<p>&amp; Brown went before C.R.A. re</p>
<p>commissions. Writing. <sup>Courtney &amp;</sup> Neville</p>
<p>Wdd.</p>
<p>12 WED &nbsp;Posted letter to Amy.</p>
<p>Fine day. Amm. going up.</p>
<p>Quiet day &amp; night.</p>
