<p>7th January 1917<br/>Still on Road Supervising.  Cold &amp; wet.  Things going well.  Nothing fresh going.</p>
<p>8th<br/>Things going well on Road work.  A good deal of shelling going on.</p>
<p>9th<br/>Transferred from 14th F.C. Engrs. to 1st Anzac Light Railway Operation Corps.</p>
<p>10th<br/>Took up duty with C.L.R.  Things very much mixed up at present.  Expect to go better before long.</p>
<p>11th<br/>Been having a very strenuous time.  Things are very much mixed up owing to overlapping of the old &amp; new formations.</p>
<p>12th<br/>Things are not working at all smoothly. Having a rough time trying to cope with work.</p>
<p>13th<br/>Still doing plenty of work trying to get things going on the Railway work.</p>
