
Monday 10.6.18  All ready for stunt  4 officers wounded  fight commenced at 9.45 p.m.
Tuesday 11.6.18  All objectives taken  300 prisoners, M.G. & mortars- own casualties very small  Hun shelling all day.
Wednesday 12.6.18.  feeling off color  sent for Robinson  Wrote to Doff, went to Transport lines
Thursday 13.6.18  Shelled all night, went by car to Div with Paton, then on to Beateaucourt [Berteaucourt] saw Wynter at [Coy?]
