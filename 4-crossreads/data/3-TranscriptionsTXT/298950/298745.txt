The Company Captain will report any irregularity to the Camp Comandant or Officer in charge &amp; will parade before him any Pris. of War who is guilty of disobidience to orders or who desires to make a complaint or Request. A Camp Committee to deal with the General wellfare of the Camp, will be sellected by the Prisoners.  The Committee will appoint a President &amp; Secretary &amp; they will comunicate with the Camp Comandant or Officer in Charge on behalf of the Committee. The Committee will appoint Camp Police Men who will be responsible to the Camp Comandant or Officers in Charge in all matters, pertaining to the law &amp; order of the Comand. Routine Reveile will be at 6.30 a.m.  Roll call 7.45 a.m. Breakfast 8 o'clock  Work parade 9 a.m. Inspection of Camp 10 a.m.  Dinner 1 p.m. Work Parade 1.45 p.m.  Roll Call 5 p.m. Tea 5.30 p.m.  Enquirey Parade 7.30 p.m.
