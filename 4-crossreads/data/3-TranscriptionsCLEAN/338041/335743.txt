  
 Oct 24th We leave Pilques in very bad weather and after a tiresome march we entrain at 7pm at town of Arques - spend night in train. 
 Oct 25th & 26th End our journey & leave train at 5a.m. in the village of St. Riquier - Breakfast on station and march to Ergines. Billeted in Farmhouse barn - "Apples to burn" - Episode of the Two Fowls". 
 Oct 27th Reveille at 4.30a.m. Another miserable march - We motor in French Transports for about 80 kilos, passing through Flixecourt & Amiens & completing our journey in the vicinity of Albert. Frightful Billets - Winter with a vengeance. 
 Oct. 28th. Signs of recent destruction in our village - Respirator drill - Referendum in Australia
