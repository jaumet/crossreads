<p>I am keeping well, &amp; of course am watching myself in the way of the slightest sign of constipation etc.<br/>The tucker is very fair here considering where we are &amp; how we are situated.<br/>I must close now, Father dearest, with love to all at home, &amp; my best love &amp; birthday wishes for yourself,<br/>Your loving son,<br/>Leo.</p>
