
16 February 1916Wednesday
Physical drill. Move into fresh reinforcement tent. Sent Power of Attorney back to Marriot & Oliver also wrote letter to Cis in Gospel tent. Called out on afternoon parade before O.C. & have to report at orderly room Headquarters at 4.30 pm. Re [indecipherable]. -Latter a mistake - Met by railway official re travelling on warrant. Paid fare under protest.Had tea down lines. Wrote letters to J.M. Watt.Concert at Rec. Tent. Bob & take walk down to canteen. Rumours of another riot. All quiet.- Bed at 10 pm.
