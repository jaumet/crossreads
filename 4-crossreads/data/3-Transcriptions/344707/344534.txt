<p>ALLAN LINE<br/>R.M.S.</p>
<p>On Route Egypt<br/>December 23rd 1915<br/>At Sea</p>
<p>Mother Dear<br/>You see I got through alright &amp; enclose the last few days of my diary. These have been very fine days indeed &amp; you will see I have Faith enough to be thankful to the Almighty for a calm sea the night we left.<br/>I had for 7 hours at [indecipherable] about 600 yards of Trench from Camel Hump to No 1 out post &amp; 14 men to hold it with without any relief &ndash; Posted at intervals</p>
