  
 Palestine  11-9-17 
 1st L H Comforts Depot Many thanks for the parcel of shirts which we received allright also tobacco and cigarettes. The boys appreciate them very much it is hard to get good cigarettes over here. Thanking you and wishing you succes on behalf of B Troop C Squadron . 1st ALHR I am yours sincerely no 426 Sgt Peters. WH
