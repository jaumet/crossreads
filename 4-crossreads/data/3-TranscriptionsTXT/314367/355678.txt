 I saw a similar one at Suva &amp; on a smaller scale at Lake Eacham in N Q-land &ndash; The S. American Caesalpuna pulcherrima was a mass of yellow &amp; red flame -  That &amp; the Poinciana regia stand out easily first for color effect &ndash; All vegetation is lush &amp; luxuriant &ndash; Breadfruit trees to Coco palms &amp; giant Pandani &amp; Rubber trees &amp; chocolate trees &amp; Castor oil Shrubs &ndash; I gathered some ferns but time is necessary for collecting &amp; one cant spare much &ndash; It was a very fine walk indeed &ndash; the best I've had so far &ndash; Inboard by 6.45 &amp; dinner Lambton was a guest &ndash; He returns to Sydney in 3 weeks he says &ndash; Wants to run a Naval Brigade to Europe &ndash; I've asked him to take me as medico &ndash; Probably that idea has been mooted &amp; is turned down by the powers that be &ndash; Beer flowed rather freely till 10 pm &ndash; Bridge &amp; Gramophone &amp; talk &amp; dancing &ndash; all at the same time &ndash; No wonder at times you forget you held the 13th card or mused as to what trumps were &amp; what the devil your partner was doing &ndash; Life after dinner flows very merrily in a naval mess &ndash; Bed &ndash; 10.30. very much rain I alone slept top side &ndash;
