<p>Thursday 29 March 1917.</p>
<p>More drill in morning &amp; A.S.C. fatigue in afternoon. Lumping 80 lbs. cases of &quot;bully beef&quot;. 50 lbs tins &quot;dog&quot; biscuits, bags of chaff, bran, &amp; oats.</p>
<p>Announced on parade&nbsp;by O.C. that we are sailing next week. Thurs probably.</p>
