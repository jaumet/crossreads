 3rd A.C.C.S. A.I.H. France 18.4.17 
 My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters, 
 Have a few minutes so trying to make the most of it. Sorry I cannot even send P.C. to my friends. We are not near anywhere to but cards   &amp;  have had very little time to write, Fortunately on Monday-night, two more sisters arrived, so we are 14 now   &amp;  really could do with heaps more.
