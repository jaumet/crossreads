  
 Sunday 10 Aug 1918 Marched out of L'Etoile, near where we detrained, & in which we spent a very comfortable day, to a point along the road where the infantry of our brigade were ahead already resting by the road side. After about an hour's wait a pack of motor char-a-bancs arrived, & the whole collection of us boarded them. By now news had arrived of the commencement by our boys, the Canadians, Tommies
