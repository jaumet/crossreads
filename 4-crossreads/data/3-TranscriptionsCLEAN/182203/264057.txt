  
 At Sea. Aug. 20th 1916. My dearest Mother & Sisters, I last wrote to you I think the day before we left Cairo. Well on Saturday Aug 12th we left Cairo & arrived in Alexandria that day about 2. p.m. - went on board the hospital ship "Kanowna".
