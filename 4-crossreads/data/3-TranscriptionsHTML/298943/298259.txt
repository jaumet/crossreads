<p>Result of study circle discussion was that we commenced a series of classes a couple of days ago after wards: English, &amp; Arithmetic (which I am taking); Shorthand &amp; French. Regular attendance &amp; good earnest spirit on the part of the dozen or so in each class.<br/>Plane flights in two sessions lately, near balloon.<br/>The last one was rather amusing. Several squadrons of our bombing planes had been going over, when, during a time of overhead quiet, a Hun plane suddenly swooped down from above &amp; attacked our balloon quite near.
