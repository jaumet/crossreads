<p>Thursday 23 September, 1915</p>
<p>Leon Ferguson wounded.</p>
<p>The big shed caught fire, several chaps were burnt out of house &amp; home i.e. their rifles, equipment &amp; overcoats went.</p>
<p>The ships were very active during the night &amp; sent a number of heavy shells into the Turks.</p>
<p>We are building a new cook house for the cook to keep out the weather.</p>
<p>It was very cold during the night &amp; windy in the evening.</p>
