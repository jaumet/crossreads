<p>March 3rd<br/>Harry Elliot &amp; self have a pleasant morning walk down &amp; under the Dean Bridge &amp; Gardens &ndash; In the afternoon an Australian (in company with a Lassie) set out for Crammond &ndash; a grand walk in which much picturesque scenery is encountered &ndash; The [indecipherable] &amp; the Ocean.<br/>Returned Edinburgh.<br/>March 4th<br/>Letters written &amp; received &ndash; "Poems of Sentiment" (Wilcox) &ndash; O. Henry's &ndash; Last day in Edinburgh &ndash; Evening spent at "59".</p>
