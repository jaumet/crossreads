<p>Wednesday Dec. 4.12.18<br/>am feeling well again  very cold and windy to day  went around Port said  nothing doing.<br/>Thursday Dec. 5.12.18<br/>feeling well  very cold to day  had tea 4.PM and left Port said 7.   was put in <strike>b</strike> Pig trucks and went to moascar  arrived there 11.P.M. nearly Perished with the cold  issued with two blankets  they would not keep a sparrow warm  turned in.</p>
