<p>&amp; if it rains (as it has very slightly) The leagon says it will rain for 40 days They say it was so last year. The Tale is that St Whitins who was Bishop of <strike>of</strike> Whinchester was buried out side the Church &amp; it rained for 40 days until they took the old chap inside &amp; then it stopped.</p>
