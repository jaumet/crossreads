<p>the Australian's on their fine trenches most of our dugouts also were very good, some of them were got up like a room, especially those who were not being moved about such as the artillery, A.S.C. Q.M.C. &amp; various other units, the infantry of course did not have such elaborate dugouts for we were often on the move one fellow even had a window in his &amp; a pot of flowers, &amp; at night time he would draw a curtain across his window, he was a machine &ndash; gunner &amp; his dug out was about the best there<br/>Looking at Anzac" from the sea it resembled, a huge warren, with the dugouts dotted all over it, there were tier upon tier of them</p>
