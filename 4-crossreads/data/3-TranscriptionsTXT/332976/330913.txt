 August 1917 21st   Drilling all day-(squad &amp; musketry drill)   We had another raid from Fritz to-night and a few bombs were dropped near Aire. 22nd   We did another route march this morning for about 12 miles.   It has been raining practically all day &amp; the ground is getting very sloppy. 23rd   Squad-drill all day-  Rain has ceased &amp; the weather is very fine. 24th   We had a 6 mile route march this morning and drill in the afternoon. 25th   We had a bath this morning in the new divisional baths near Blaringhem, &amp; in the afternoon, instruction on "Strong Points" 26th   (Sunday)   Company Drill this morning &amp; the afternoon off.
