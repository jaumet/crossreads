<p>Arrived at Dijon at 11.30 where some ladies gave us cold tea to drink.  Dijon is a large &amp; busy town, mainly remarkable for its beautiful Notre Dame Church &amp; Cathedral.<br/>The fine black spire can be seen miles away.<br/>Leaving Dijon the country changes from flat to hilly &amp; wooded slopes.<br/>Tea at Les Laumes.  During night passed Paris &amp; arrived at Mantes Gissecourt for breakfast.<br/>From there onwards the scenery was of an agricultural description, rather drab</p>
