<p>It blew a lovely cool breeze all day &amp; we saw dozens of sharks, noticeable by the fins. We had great fun sometimes watching the porpoises playing round the nose of the boat.</p>
<p>Thursday 20.1.16 (29)<br/>A beautiful cool day &amp; we are making very good progress. It is so hot generally in this part that the ship only travels about 5 or 6 knots an hour. The stokers can't stay down too long. As many as 6 are brought up together  sometimes, right out to it. We are travelling faster than we have done all</p>
