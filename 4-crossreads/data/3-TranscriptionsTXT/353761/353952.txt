 (21) "Good" and the major passes on continuing his enquiries down the long line of resting troops. Ten minutes has gone (It seems but five) "Fall in" The order is well-nigh hateful for the moment. The troops stagger up and shuffle to their places in fours on the road. "At ease; Quick march" We are moving forward once more with that everlasting tread tread tread. The boys are soon singing a song that tells of home, or whistling the tune of it. &nbsp;A glow now faintly glows with rose lights in the East.
