<p>Arrived at Suez at 5.30 p.m., and were taken on board the Hospital ship "Kanowna" (A.U.S.N. line, a sister ship to the Kyarra).</p>
<p>20th<br/>Left Suez at 7 a.m., passing on our way out the Canadian Hospital ship Loyalty lying at anchor.  At 9.30 a.m. met a French passenger steamer.</p>
<p>21st<br/>Burial at sea of a patient who died of brain fever.</p>
<p>22nd<br/>Fearfully hot, right out in the Red Sea.  The ship is vibrating like a milk shake.</p>
<p>23rd<br/>Still very hot.  Thousands of porpoises chasing shoals of flying fish all round the ship.</p>
<p>24th<br/>Sunday &ndash; Passed by Aden at 8 p.m., and met an Indian Hospital ship on her way back to the Dardanelles from India. Out of the Red Sea now, thank goodness.</p>
