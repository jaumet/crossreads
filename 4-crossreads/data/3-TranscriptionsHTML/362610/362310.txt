<p>Tuesday 4 April 1916<br/>Just outside Marseilles harbour at daylight &amp; alongside by breakfast. All hurry &amp; bustle then until disembarkation at 1.30. Train left at 2.30. Thro' Arles, Orange &amp; Avignon which we reached abt. 8 pm &amp; stopped at for tea.</p>
