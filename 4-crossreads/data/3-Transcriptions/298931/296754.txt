<p>[May, 1916.]<br/>Monday 1<br/>10th Reinf 17th Batt Ellis Skinner the first of above company to depart to the great unknown R.I.P<br/>Tuesday 2<br/>I hardly know what to jot down, for today I am here right in the mess officers mess writing letters<br/>Wednesday 3<br/>Strolling across the desert with Letty good company for Letty is a Terrier<br/>Thursday 4<br/>I wonder, yes, I wonder where she is who she's with and what they are doing, good thoughts</p>
