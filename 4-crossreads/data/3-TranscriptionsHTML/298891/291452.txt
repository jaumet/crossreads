<p>Friday 18 Dr De Garis returned from Salonique bringing Mrs McHardy White. Lovely mild bright day.</p>
<p>Sat.19th</p>
<p>Mrs Green went to Salonique &amp; took Mrs McH.W. Home.</p>
<p>Sunday 20</p>
<p>Lovely day &ndash; perfect frosty mornig no wind. Walked with Tubby &amp; saw bulbs opening all over hillsides Radomir's slava.</p>
<p>Monday 21</p>
<p>Deep fog made day cold. No incident.</p>
<p>Tues 22</p>
<p>Fog in morning &ndash; glorious afternoon.</p>
<p>E. C. Brown &amp; I went to Serb mess for music Rugitich a nuisance.</p>
