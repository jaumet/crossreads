We arrived at Capetown on Thursday about 11 oclock. We went ashore at 2.30. Joe Tarn &amp; I &amp; Riley YMCA took a car to Grooteschurr, Cecil Rhodes house, &amp; to his monument. It was a most enjoyable drive  Then we called on some Friends &amp; found them out, &amp; we met a couple of our officers who had a car &amp; invited us to go for a Ride which we did to Campa Bay on the seaside
