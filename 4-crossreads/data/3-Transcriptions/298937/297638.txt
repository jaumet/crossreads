<p><strike>Saturday</strike> Friday 4th Aug.</p>
<p>Commencing the 3rd year of the war.<br/>Service on board today on that account.<br/>In the afternoon a band concert including some gems from Sullivan's operas.<br/>Passing Crete for the best part of the day.<br/>Destroyers all round us.  One in front &amp; one behind.  Whale at times two ran parallel with us &amp; others hovered on the horizon.  Passed an oil ship &amp; a small wind jammer.  Lifeboat drill as usual, but</p>
