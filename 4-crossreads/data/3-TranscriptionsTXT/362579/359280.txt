
3rd A.C.C.S.A.I.H.France18.4.17
My dearest Mother   &  Sisters,
Have a few minutes so trying to make the most of it. Sorry I cannot even send P.C. to my friends. We are not near anywhere to but cards   &  have had very little time to write, Fortunately on Monday-night, two more sisters arrived, so we are 14 now   &  really could do with heaps more. The work here is very heavy   &  trying but I'm glad that I've been able to do it so long. We get all
