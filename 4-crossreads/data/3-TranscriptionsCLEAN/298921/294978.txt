  
 Dec 21 Revielle 4 & Marched to ships after handing in all - Saddlery to ordinance - in addition to own Horses had to take 281 from [indecipherable] Depot unbroken a lot of them some strangles and ringworm. Raining all hands wet I went ashore to Wilkinson &  Lavenders for S.D. returned per Motor Launch - sailed at 4 p.m. a tough night & raining
