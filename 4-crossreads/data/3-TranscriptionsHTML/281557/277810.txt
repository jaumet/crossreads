<p>every few hundred yards &amp; seemed like grass hoppers ; in fact some shoals were no bigger in size individually &amp; must have been young ones. The larger ones seemed able to fly a hundred yards comfortably ; providing they did not bump a wave. Slept on deck but the weather was too oppressive for sleep. No land or ships sighted.<br/>Friday 31st Dec<br/>A very calm day &amp; hot as per usual. Revellie 6am : no drill as I was on cooksfatigue : but I did not do a thing all day</p>
