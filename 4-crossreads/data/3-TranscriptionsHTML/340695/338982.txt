<p>Fritz started shelling like h&mdash;l this morning &amp; kept it up intermittently throughout the day. They were of large calibre &amp; made a sickening noise singing through the air. They lobbed as close as 100 yards away making this old place rattle again. It is funny to see all hands duck the feeling is one not easily described. The majority of the shells were duds but those that did explode tore things about.<br/>A good many German prisoners went through to-day escorted by our lads also quite a number of our chaps went past wounded.
