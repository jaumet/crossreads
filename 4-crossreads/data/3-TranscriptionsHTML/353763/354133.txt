<p>We baited a hook with a big bit of pork &amp; soon the shark took pork hook &amp; all  -  we hadn't a real good shark hook  -  One of the ratings got his rifle &amp; when he came right up on the surface let drive  -  He hit him fair &amp; square &amp; off old shark went &amp; we saw him no more  -  It was a sight for a naturalist to see a shark turn right over &amp; show the full white belly when he bit  -  I'd never seen this  -  They seem to turn right over slowly &amp; then a sudden rush &amp; snap  -  We made some good shark hooks yesterday &amp; we shall go at it again &amp; Rawdon &amp; you shall have a tooth each  -<br/>Then we saw two Bonito or Dolphins round the ship  -  The point to me was their brilliant purple color  -  The front half, including fins seemed to me a wonderful color scheme in purple  -  Then the ship went on &ndash; roll, pitch toss in a lubberly sea all Sunday  -  All Monday &amp; some of Tuesday  -  I got control of myself fairly soon &amp; by keeping on top side &amp; starving didn't do so bad  -  Tuesday aftn  -  We got word by signal that Simpson Haven was our bourne  -  Now</p>
