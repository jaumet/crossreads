
<p>Jan 6<sup><u>th</u></sup>.&nbsp; We pulled into Durban first thing in the morning &amp; were welcomed by a lady whom we saw signalling to us with flags - We had a route march through Durban in the morning &amp; <s>at</s> were then given leave from 2 till 10.30 P.M. The trams were free to troops &amp; we had a ride round. The day was a little bit showery but the sun was very hot. Had a hot bath &amp; went to the Y.M.C.A. concert in the evening. Like Durban very much. <s>Had</s> Had a ride in a ricksha</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Jan 7<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp; Sunday &amp; we had a church parade ashore. it was raining all day, &amp; <sup>we</sup> were given leave 11.30 A.M. till 10 P.M.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Jan 8<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp; Went for a five mile route march in the morning. lovely day - given leave from 2 P.M. till 10.P.M. Went to museum &amp; art gallery. didn&#39;t think much of them - Had a swim at the Marine Parade baths, &amp; had tea on the Parade - Went to Y.M.C.A. concert in the evening.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Jan 9<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp; We put out into the bay &amp; left Durban at 12.30 mid-day three hours behind the &quot;Berimar&quot;. we were in sight of land during the rest of the day - lovely day &amp; smooth sea.</p>
