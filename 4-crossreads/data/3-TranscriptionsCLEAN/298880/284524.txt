Here am I in a comfortable French train travelling through beautiful France on my way to an imperial school of instruction for one month.Sunday 7 AugustWe reached Cassel station about 1.30 PM yesterday and then walked to the school a distance of about 2 1/2 kilo. We were told off to a room in a large white house. The house is built on old fashioned lines. A moat runs almost right around it. It is three stories high with large rooms. A good view can be o[b]tained from its upper windows. There are no verandahs on these houses even the most modern built houses are built minus a verandah and giving them a most forbidding aspect.
