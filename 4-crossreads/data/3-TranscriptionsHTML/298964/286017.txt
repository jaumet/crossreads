<p>3 Sun June<br/>Taube forced down on the beach  very hot  feeling below zero</p>
<p>4 Mon June<br/>Jacko dropping bombs  very little damage also great air duel.<br/>[indecipherable] from Ada [?] also L. thinks the world of it  Very quiet on the front</p>
<p>5 Tues June<br/>Going like hell not a moment to spare  Very quiet in trenches  very heavy bomb  Taubes very active dropping bombs.</p>
<p>6 Wed June<br/>Still travelling some early morning until dark cannot find time to write a line  Getting very warm  usual activity in the air  Three enemy machines brought down in one week</p>
