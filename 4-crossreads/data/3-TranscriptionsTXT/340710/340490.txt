 Warloy 26.7.16  Well  It is over for us for the time being.  Posieres today stands within our lines or rather lies I should say for the village is but a thing of past glories, here and there a cellar is intact, everywhere bricks and pieces of tin are scattered about.  It was hard fighting, the hardest we have ever known.  Picture for yourself rows and rows of guns standing wheel to wheel and stretching back to a depth of three miles firing spontaneously, picture those shells bursting, picture the enemy replying not in kind certainly but as near as he can to it.
