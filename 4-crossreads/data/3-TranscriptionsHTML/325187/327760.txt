<p>[Saturday  January 15 1916]</p>
<p>Arrived <strike>Wardan</strike> &amp; one troop marched on 35 miles.  Of course it rained just enough to wet us through it always does this when shifting Camp.<br/>South of us is a Petrified Forest evidence of different climatic &amp; physical conditions in the past   The only roads in the Desert are Camel Tracks &amp; they represent the shortest distances between Water.</p>
