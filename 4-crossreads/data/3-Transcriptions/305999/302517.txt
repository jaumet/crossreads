<p>18/6/16<br/>then next morning I was examined by our Bt doctor and he said I had a touch of bronchitis  I had been very bad for the parss week or more</p>
<p>18/6/16<br/>I was sent to the second Fld Ambulance and was taken in with above</p>
<p>19/6/16<br/>I was sent from above to Balleul to the divisional rest camp  we where well looked after</p>
