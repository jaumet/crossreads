25th TuesdayPut tents up & had drill all day;  several heavy rain storms;  slept in tents.
26th WednesdayStruck tents about 7 a.m. and with full pack etc. set off for 1st Line of Defence some 8 miles east of Canal.  On journey many dropped out so had to remain with them & followed later as able on Donkeys commandeered [See Page 32 for continuation of this entry.]
