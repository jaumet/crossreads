<p>Sunday 23 September 1917<br/>Church parade, played bridge.  Wrote Nos. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 4].</p>
<p>Monday 24 September 1917<br/>Drill in early morning, dusty day.  Picquet at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 25 September 1917<br/>Drill in early morning.  Inoculated for Cholera, marched to F.A.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26 September 1917<br/>Good concert at Y.M.C.A Divisional Band played several selections.</p>
<p>Thursday 27 September 1917<br/>Drill in morning, dusty &amp; windy day.  On sanitary Fatigue.</p>
<p>Friday 28 September 1917</p>
<p>Drill in morning, quiet day, dusty.  Played 1st Reg. lost 8-3, real good game, heard Div. Band.</p>
<p>Saturday 29 September 1917<br/>Drill in morning.  Usual routine.</p>
