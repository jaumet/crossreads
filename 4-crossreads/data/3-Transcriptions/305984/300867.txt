<p>22 / 26/18<br/>nothing of note here plenty of lies about the war</p>
<p>27/3/18<br/>Plenty of news about the German offensive it simply staggered me to think they could advance the distance they did in 8 days but they must have suffered for it in casualties of course we only hear of their side of the whole affair.  received money order dated 16/3/18 today</p>
