
Thursday 21 January 1915At Mena HouseEveryone working hard getting hospitals into working order
Friday 22 January 1915Same as ThursdayTwo operations, inguial hernias
Saturday 23 January 1915Camp being erected rapidly. Officers & men working very well, especially Major Read, Captain Mackenzie, and Captain Clayton. Col. Springthorpe officious & boyrish [boorish?] as usual. Young officers slow & not punctual
Sunday 24 January 1915Camp ready for inspection. Major General Bridges inspected the camp at 11-15 am. Said "excellent".   Not able to be at Mass. - Operation.
