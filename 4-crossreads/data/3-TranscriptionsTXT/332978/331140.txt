
61 Sept 14thThey have let the allies down on several occasions, so I think it would be better for them to conclude a separate peace - The situation may change
Played Bridge
Sept 15thSelpt Slept well - Recd my money that I requisitioned for on 10th Sept - 1621/2 marks - Recd letter from Bay no 7. Very dull day today Recd 2 Parcels
Concert tonight half French & half English. Did not go - Played Bridge
September 16thSlept well - Episode with S Rafe & French last evening - Church service this morning - Had my foot bandaged. Confined to room for 2 days for being riotious after 10 pm - air raid early this mng. Could hear bombs falling but could no machines visible -
September 17th6 Officers escaped early this mng - Capt Robinson, Baerline, McIntosh Rees Craig. They hid in wood heap till midnight & then made a bid for it -
