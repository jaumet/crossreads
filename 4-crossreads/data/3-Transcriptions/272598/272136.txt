<p>Sunday 3.12.16<br/>Off duty to-day, sick of this locality but no news of moving.</p>
<p>Monday 4.12.16<br/>40 of our lads had to reinforce 15 F.Amb.  Dozens being evacuated sick.  In charge of bearers at A.D.S.  Large mail to-day; received 25 letters &amp; parcel from Lambton.</p>
<p>Tuesday 5.12.16<br/>Raining &amp; cold.  Again put in charge of A.D.S. bearers.  Relieved at lunch time &amp; spent evening in the endeavour to make myself a bit respectable</p>
<p>Wednesday 6.12.16<br/>Now doing 3 days in &amp; 3 days out on line owing to number of men who have been evacuated sick.  Off duty for the day, wrote home.</p>
<p>Thursday 7.12.16<br/>Leave list altered, original men go</p>
