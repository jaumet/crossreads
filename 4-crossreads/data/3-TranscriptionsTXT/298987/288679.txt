
The awning is up over the forward well deck - great improvement Boxing contest on hatch - makes a good ring Also lecture by one of the Pardres on aft well deck. A very good speaker. He has just come from Palestine. Did not mention before we picked up several Pardries & Officers at Capetown coming home for leave also about 100 Tommies YM gave chocolates for tea. Getting quite warm
Saturday 4Yesterday the Corned beef at dinner was very hard to take so we got extra cheese - it was uneatable. Today the Dr condemed the pudding so it went overboard & was nothing to replace it - true economy. Fire & Boat drill Ballmoral castle had gun practice could hear the shells burst distintly A great many flying fish about. 1st day in the 4 year of War also my birthday
Sunday 5th
