<p>Thursday 5 April 1917.</p>
<p>Drill as usual. Field Work.</p>
<p>Easter leave to be from Fri evening until 9.30. a.m. Tues. morning.</p>
<p>Everbody indignant as we have to drill rmorrow&nbsp;&ndash; Good Friday.</p>
<p>Rained &amp; our kit got wet.</p>
<p>Poor tents (bell)</p>
<p>12 men (C.B.F.A.) detailed for work at No 5 A.G.H. for 10 or 14 days.</p>
