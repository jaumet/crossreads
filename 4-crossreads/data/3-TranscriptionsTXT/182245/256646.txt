MAY 1917
11 FRIDAY
Warm & Sunny. Just a year since
we left Sydney. Cleaning harness
& I washed my horse in afternoon,
We had to turn out in evening.
& shift the guns down to the pits
our fellows built in Ploegaert
We are to be an S.O.S. battery
there & attached to the 8th Brigade
Got home at 2 AM.
