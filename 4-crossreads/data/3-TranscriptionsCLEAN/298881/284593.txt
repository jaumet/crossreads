
Beaulencourt 12/6/17Heavy German attack launched against portion of Hindenburg Line (held by the 64th Battalion) on 14th/15th May.  Attack a complete failure.  Our men did magnificent work.
Beaulencourt 14/6/17General John Pershing, Commander In Chief of U.S.A. Forces in Europe arrived in Paris yesterday evening.  Given a great reception by the French people.
Warloy 15/6/17Our Brigade was relieved from Beaulencourt by the 2nd Div. yesterday.  We had a very pleasant train journey down from Bapaume Station in the cool of the evening.  The country everywhere looks beautiful.
