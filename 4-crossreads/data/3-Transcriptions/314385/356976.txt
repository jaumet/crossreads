<p>There is also a village adjoining this one called ["Ampeliers"?] &amp; we are five kilos from a large town called Doullins.</p>
<p>Mon Sep 4th<br/>To day we are resting but a few break into parties &amp; do a bit of exploring in the village &amp; find something good to eat &amp; drink.</p>
<p>We are standing by to continue our march.</p>
<p>Tues Sept 5th<br/>We still camp here &amp; fill in the time visiting the people &amp; making things agreeable.</p>
<p>We very likely move off at midnight.</p>
<p>Fri Sep 5th<br/>Still waiting orders to move.</p>
<p>I went with others to get a bath but we were unfortunate as they were not ready.</p>
<p>The guns are very active here &amp; can also be heard at Yepres.</p>
<p>I in Waratah camp I omitted to write that on Wednesday.</p>
<p>We left Orville at 2 in the morning &amp; moved off in full marching order and reached Doullins R.S. after a five kilo march we had hot coffee at 5 am awaited &amp; boarded a train 12 m [a bon?] we moved off arriving</p>
