<p>10th  The tanks have let our boys down.  They were to have led our attack &amp; put the MG's out of action. Their commander was killed &amp; they turned back our boys carried on with the result that 2 batt's of the 10th were cut to pieces  Our attack has been held up for a day or two owing to the failure of the tommies to reach their objective.  Blast the Tommies.  Anyhow we put some of our 1st Div &amp; Yanks in &amp; they got their objective Chipilly &amp; are now on the outskirts of Bray</p>
