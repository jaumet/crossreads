<p>August Wednesday 1 1917</p>
<p>Pay day &amp; medical exam for classification. <br/>Still raining. Push proceeding  Fritz evacuating in places: but heavy casualties are being inflicted by him.  <br/>Col. Powell examines my heart.  Slight something or other. <br/>Mac B &amp; I into Boulogne. after 4 p.m. Cinema. Miss tram. I come an awful spill in dropping off travelling motor. splosh into mud.</p>
