  
 28/4/19 Wiesbaden little "grelons" fell. Continued along the crest by a delightful path through the green sweet smelling woods and noticed a squirrel tamely playing. Reached another look out which gave yet another panoramic view of the country and the absolute beauty of the scene made me nearly say that it was the loveliest I had ever seen. For half an hour stayed here and then wended a way down through the terraces of vines to Rudesheim station and took a train which after an hour landed me in Mayence.
