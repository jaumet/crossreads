 27 December 1918 Friday Dining with the CO of Australian [indecipherable] of General tonight Last night the New Zealanders came to us- about 24 Officers &amp; a lot of Horseplay was gone in for-  The men were firing Verey lights about &amp; it was wonderful how the Horses stood the racket- Bertie Austin wrote me &amp; said he was sorry he missed me in Cairo- &amp; said his wife often spoke of you-   I enclose his letter-
