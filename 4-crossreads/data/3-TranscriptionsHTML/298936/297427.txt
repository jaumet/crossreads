<p>LANDED AT MIDNIGHT MONDAY AND TOUCHED SOIL IN FIRST MOMENTS OF BIRTHDAY</p>
<p>September 1915</p>
<p>Tuesday 28</p>
<p>Rose at daybreak &amp; went with Hunter to Hqtr 3rd Field Ambulance situated on the beach &amp; then to camp in a large gully running up from the beach a mile inland. Reported &amp; received &frac12; doz letters including proofs of photos &amp; 2 letters from Rita which were welcome. Were welcomed with a few shrapnel shells one of which burst near enough to cause us to duck..<br/>Safely ensconced in a good dugout.<br/>The 3rd Field Hospital  &amp; stores are behind a hill in view of the sea &amp; of the corner swept by "Beachy Bill"</p>
