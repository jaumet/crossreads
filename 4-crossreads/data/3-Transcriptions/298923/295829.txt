<p>12 June 1918 Wednesday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Like much Trench life, the front line is the Easiest on the men when not fighting but anyone in responsible positions has a good deal of worry.  However alls well-<br/>X Just posted you some of Dolly &amp; her Mothers letters as I know will interest you X-  Jacko is using a new shell to try &amp; reach us &ndash; bursts in the air &amp; then on reaching ground bursts again- so getting behind our Rocks but "Malesh" as Gyppy says, he is doing no harm-  Very hot about 115&deg; in shade.</p>
