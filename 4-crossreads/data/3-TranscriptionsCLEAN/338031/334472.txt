Two local hotels were closed as is the custom when visiting troops are in the town. but plenty of grog was available in the houses of ill fame. Even rabid teetotallers would rather agree to the hotels or temporary canteens under strict military supervision being accessible to the troops instead of the abodes of vice and disease.
