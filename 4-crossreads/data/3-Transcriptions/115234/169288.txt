
<p><u>8th</u>, took by storm the famous Fritz Stronghold of &quot;<u>Polygon Wood</u>&quot; on the &quot;Passchendale Ridge&quot;&nbsp; It was there that the 31<sup>st</sup> Batt <sub>^</sub><sup>were</sup> driven mad by the sight of their best officers being treach-erously shot by wounded Boches, after the latter had put up their hands &amp; yelled &quot;Mercy Kamerad&quot;:&nbsp; The boys caught the Boches at Breakfast &amp; used to Bayonet em &amp; then toss them on the burning</p>
