
13 April 1918SaturdayA very Cool Change after bright warm weather. In the evening R.S.M. Smith & I went into Salisbury We went to see a couple of Shows which were fairly good & altogether had a fairly good time.
1918 April 14Sunday2 after Easter
Same old Stuff In the afternoon Smith & I went into Dinton a very small village on the South Western Line. Had tea there & afterward went to Church (C.E) in a quaint old Church a mile or so out of the town. A number of the boys who have died at Fovant Hospital are buried in the Church yard.
