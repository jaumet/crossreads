 Several of our waggons were up moving the baggage &amp; our Company suffered its first Casualty in France Bill Southam coming back with a Shrapnel Bullet in his leg &ndash; he was taken off to hospital this morning &amp; I think most of us envied him &ndash; I know I did &ndash; a few  good  weeks good rest &amp; change with the probability of being sent to England.
