<p>to the village of Montrelet hidden away among the woods in a deep valley</p>
<p>Aug 10<br/>After 2 days at Bonneville we moved back 4 miles to a fresh 'Bivvy' handy to water at Pernois &amp; have been here since.</p>
<p>The Division is on its way in again. The Infantry Brigade passed out yesterday &amp; the Divisional Troops expect to move out today.</p>
