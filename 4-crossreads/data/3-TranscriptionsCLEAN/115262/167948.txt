
Saturday 30  June 1917.
Parades during morning and none in afternoon. Did some washing after 4 p.m. 1st since leaving boat. An easy day in this respect.
At 10 pm.[a.m.?] detailed with 5 others for a stretcher squad to hospital depot 1/2 way to meet Amb. from Ismailia, with 5 patients. Amb. late - waited 3 hrs in sun with patients and got back to camp at 1.30 pm. & missed dinner. Another stretcher party at 7 p.m. to part of the isolation area.
