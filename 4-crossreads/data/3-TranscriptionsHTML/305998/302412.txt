<p>We found them minding herds of sheep &amp; cattle, they were mostly old men &amp; their families.</p>
<p>Arrived in camp at sun-down, water terrible scarce &amp; horses thirsty we travelled about 30 miles.</p>
<p>16th/ Up again at 5.30 am. left camp at 7.30 am. a much better hour, travelled 22 miles to El Dabla arrived at 3.30 pm &amp; camped for night, I had a shave a much needed refresher &amp; went off to the canteen for eatables, Put away a good tea &amp; felt satisfied to go to sleep.</p>
