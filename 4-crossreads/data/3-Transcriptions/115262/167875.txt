<p>Wednesday 18 April 1917.</p>
<p>Tent pitching during morning.</p>
<p>1st fog of the season this morning</p>
<p>Swim in Goulburn in afternoon, arrange tents &amp; lecture.</p>
<p>Water very cold. Nobody stayed in.</p>
<p>Reported to Camp Hd. Qrs for duty for tomorrow.</p>
<p>Wrote 4 letters.</p>
