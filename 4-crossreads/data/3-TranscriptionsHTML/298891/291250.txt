<p>1917 June</p>
<p>16 Saturday</p>
<p>Arose at 8 feeling very weak &amp; went to Shoolbreds to buy boots &amp; get Wellingtons. Home. Rested during afternoon &amp; typed "On D Street" &amp; went to bed at 10. Street children annoying. Warm day. Thunderstorm during late afternoon with hail &amp; much thunder.</p>
