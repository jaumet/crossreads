<p>that we were not knocked out altogether. I have never experienced such accurate shooting. These 75s" commonplace were not to use a common phase bursting <u>around</u> us they were bursting <u>on</u> us! Shell after shell would burst right on top of us but strange to say there were few casualties. Up to this time we had sustained something like 50 casualties so the Brigadier decided to send for several companies of the 15 Battallon to come back again and reinforce us. We left our C Squadron behind for duty there.</p>
