<p>There were now about 30 ships in the bay including two French cruisers.<br/>As the "Borda" has not coaled since leaving Capetown her supply is getting very low, and as labor if scarce we have all been detailed to assist in the coaling of the ship, when the collier comes alongside.<br/>Thursday 14th Dec.<br/>Five or six Troopships left the bay this</p>
