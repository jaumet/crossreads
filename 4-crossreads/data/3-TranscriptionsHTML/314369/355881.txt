<p>29 August 1815<br/>Sunday<br/>13th After Trinity</p>
<p>Out at sea which is beautifully blue but a bit choppy   &amp; a cold breeze blowing<br/>Pass an Island on starboard side about 10 am<br/>Have a rather restless night on deck my foot was troubling me</p>
<p>1915 August 30<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Soon after breakfast this morning we started to look for land<br/>Get into Alexandria about 1 pm   &amp;  tie up at 2 pm.<br/>All Australians  &amp;  +<strike>Z</strike> N.Z.'s are sent by train to Cairo. Tea   &amp;  Bread Butter   &amp;  Cheese are served on train at 6 pm<br/>On arrival at Cairo we are taken direct to No 2 A.G.H. &amp;  installed in wards according to our various complaints<br/>Have a hot bath  &amp;  get into a clean bed</p>
