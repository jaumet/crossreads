<p>British arrived &amp; drove them back. One can still see signs of fighting particularly one brick cottage whose walls are a mass of bullet splashes.</p>
<p>I don't know whether this is Flanders but it seems so by the name of the place &amp; the country people amongst themselves speak Flemish. The names on the tombstones also read more like Dutch than French.
