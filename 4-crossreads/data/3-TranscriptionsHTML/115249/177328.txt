<p>W.T.Signal</p>
<p>Receiving ship - &nbsp;Flag</p>
<p>Transmitting ship - &nbsp;Warrego</p>
<p>Date - 11.9.4</p>
<p>Time of Despatch - 7.40 pm</p>
<p>Comdr Beresford to Brigadier and Flag. &nbsp;I have no details yet of enemys losses I am sending reinforcements to the front which is about five miles from the pier. &nbsp;I have just received at six p.m a message from Lieut Bond he is again being engaged but is [indecipherable]?</p>
