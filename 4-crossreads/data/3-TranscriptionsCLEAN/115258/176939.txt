  
 I'll have to knock off sending these C.O.D.s if I  p  want Father to have any money to use for me - but I dont expect any leave for ages, after the next Port Said trip, so I wont do it again. I hope Father is able to buy James' next year. I suppose he will if the season holds good. We are still back in the peace camp and living pretty high; but it is getting a bit of a bore dont you know - they get us horribly regimental back here. A whole lot of remounts have been coming lately, but so far I have not got a new horse. They will see I have one before we move out - and in the  meen time its one less to groom and take to water.
