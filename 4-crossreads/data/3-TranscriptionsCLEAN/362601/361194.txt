  
 25 April, Friday 
 F. We march out at 9 am. I take the party to Bulford. I see Florrie & take bag to Hobdens. We catch 12.27 & I go to Mrs Edmonds & meet Marion's Bloke. We arrive at Front. I meet dozens of LH acquaintances. Billy & Les Nettleton. I go to No 13 Hut. I go to Pictures & all a man pestering a girl to get married & she wishes him dead. I am quite happy until then. 
 Wrote M.R.L. 
 26 April, Saturday 
 F. Walk to P.O. to post letter. 
 I go to Y.M.C.A. We play Billiards. I play for Bucks dance & it is OK.
