<p>LONDON<br/>Monday 12<br/>Swanking around the Strand Picadilly &amp; Leict. Square Leaving for Ireland tonight Via Scotland by the G.N.W Railway<br/>Tuesday 13<br/>Arrived at Strangaer (Scotland) at daybreak boarded the S.S Princess Maud, arriving at Larnde (Ireland) for dinner<br/>Wednesday 14<br/>Now in Coleraine ah it is good and very nice to hear the Irish Colleens say "Jackie"<br/>Thursday 15<br/>At an Irish part "och sure it was grand pretty wee lassie och I am in love getting</p>
