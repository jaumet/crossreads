 30 December, Tuesday 
 Home again. I amuse D &amp; Mum with piano &amp; get to bed at 9. Go to 430A at 1 pm. 
 31 December, Wednesday 
 Go to Top's. We wait &amp; watch for Slaper. Music &amp; [indecipherable] until the Boy Come Home. 
 Get to bed at 12.30 am. I am again at 430A. Dine with Ellis who cls the bike over.
