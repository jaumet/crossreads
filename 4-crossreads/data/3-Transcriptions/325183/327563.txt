<p>However I went to the little village church this morning with Mr. &amp; Mrs. Pember and Geldard and tried hard to feel good about it. The service was the prettiest I have ever attended. After church Mr. Pember took Geldard &amp; I for a stroll round his property which covers practically the whole of the town of Fair Oak &amp; a bit more.
