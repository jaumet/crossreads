<p>27th  Moved back to the tents again this morning &amp; by a much shorter route.</p>
<p>Am on guard this evening &amp; have 8 men in the clink nearly all deserters.</p>
<p>Looks like rain tonight so I got the men digging little trenches round the tents.</p>
