<p>Gallipoli Peninsula<br/>24.7.1915</p>
<p>My dear Father<br/>Since last writing home a fortnight ago I have received your letters of 17th 25th and 30th May and 8th June, for which many thanks. I can assure you Father, there is no letter more eagerly watched for by me than your budget which always seems to contain the sort of news a fellow likes to get. The one dated 17th May was rather lucky to reach me, evidently having met with some adventure on the voyage as it arrived with no cover. Some good Samaritan in Div HQ Post office must have known me and sent the letter on with enclosed slip.<br/>There is very little news since last I wrote. I am now officially transferred to the 3rd Field Artillery Brigade and posted to 3rd   [indecipherable] Column but</p>
