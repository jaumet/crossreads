
In the churchyard of Figheldean there are  many graves of Sheppards in the name of the blacksmith in Longfellows poem. The oldest stone I saw bore the date 1724 and was inscribedHere lieth ye lady of William SheppardDied October1724Aged 49 years
Diary Of Pte John D Wilson No 46.37. BarralionPioneers.Lark Hill CampSalisbury PlainsEngland
