<p>19.2.15</p>
<p>get back to camp till late went nowhere after tea but stayed in the tent &amp; spinning yards</p>
<p>Fri 19th<br/>Was sent to A Sqd. again this morning as the whole division was out on a scheme all day &amp; were handed out dummy cartridges to fight the New Zealanders with;  we had dinner about two oclock &amp; had the battle shortly afterwards.  It was nearly dark when we returned to camp; &amp; was rather weary, as we had a lot of running during the afternoon<br/>went nowhere after tea &amp; went to bed early</p>
