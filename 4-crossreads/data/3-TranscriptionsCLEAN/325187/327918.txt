  
 [Saturday 24 June 1916] 
 You might order from Chorley Military Tailor Sydney a Light Horse Puggaree for Felt Hat they know size of Hat tell them to post to my address Egypt Australasian from Joe 2/5/16 Letter from Mother 7/5/16 & Joe 7/5/16   "     "       Da  May 7/5/16 Also D'quin Papers- Please thank all Sleeping mostly today 25% of Horses on the sick list. Our Interpreter says "We shoot men like birds" (ie [indecipherable]) We travel in the night like Robbers.  We stand to arms the best part of the night & live on Bully & Weevil Biscuits both sides of my stomach & marmalade I'm sick with Brackish Water - I want to go home I & my donkey are tired.
