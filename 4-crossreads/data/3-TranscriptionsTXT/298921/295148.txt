 June, 1915 Saturday 26  Letters from Laura &amp; Jennie [indecipherable] from England today &ndash; former says all is well -    Feast  Fast days commence for a month for the native population they eat at night only now, &amp; then in a month have a great spree. Wounded continue to come in, 2 more of my men (I mean 1st Reinf) I found last night in No.1 A.G. Hospital. Send with this some snapshots of our men at Gallipoli (&amp; also one of myself &amp; V.O. 1st L.H.)
