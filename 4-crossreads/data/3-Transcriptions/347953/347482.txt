<p>Blind grenades should always be buried.</p>
<p>In handling detonators charge does not need much to cause explosion.</p>
<p>Fuses</p>
<p>1.  Ordinary live fuse, recognised by its colour black.  Delivered in fathoms of 8.</p>
<p>The centre consists of gun powder of salt petre.  The more salt petre the slower the fuse.</p>
<p>Salt petre enclosed in a rubber tube.  Around rubber tube is placed flax bound by wire insulation tape.</p>
