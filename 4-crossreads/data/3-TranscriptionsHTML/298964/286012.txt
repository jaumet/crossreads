<p>17 Th May<br/>Aeroplanes and Artillery very busy.  Went for a ride in the [indecipherable]<br/>Had a few apricots  very hot and dusty &amp;  about fed up</p>
<p>18 Fri M ay<br/>Heat terrible and flies &ndash; very quiet day.  Laying about eating flies  Mackenson no sign of a move reported here</p>
<p>19 Sat May<br/>A terrible day.  Dust a treat all day.  Could not see 50 yds and not a drop of water to have a wash  I got the horse brush and brushed myself<br/>All appearances of keeping up all night</p>
