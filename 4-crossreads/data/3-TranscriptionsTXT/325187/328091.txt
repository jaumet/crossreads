 [Thursday 14 December 1916] 
  Today the Infantry are practising their attack on El Arish  Aeroplanes dropping Smoke balls &amp; spotting for the guns   Enemy bombed 2 L H   I rode over &amp; had Tea there they showed me a 20 lb bomb that had not exploded [illustration ] Vane, Powder Cap &ndash; (Smaller than the Romani ones) than most   Later  4 of these were dropped on our Brig but did no damage- I watched with my glasses &amp; altho' the sound is very clear the bomb is not seen till it bursts- before this happens the horses &amp; men have scattered.
