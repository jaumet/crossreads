<p>16 February 1916<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Physical drill. Move into fresh reinforcement tent. Sent Power of Attorney back to Marriot &amp; Oliver also wrote letter to Cis in Gospel tent. Called out on afternoon parade before O.C. &amp; have to report at orderly room Headquarters at 4.30 pm. Re [indecipherable]. &ndash;Latter a mistake &ndash; Met by railway official re travelling on warrant. Paid fare under protest.<br/>Had tea down lines. Wrote letters to J.M. Watt.<br/>Concert at Rec. Tent. Bob &amp; take walk down to canteen. Rumours of another riot. All quiet.- Bed at 10 pm.</p>
