
Monday 25 June 1917.
4 a.m. reveille. Drill till 7.30 a.m.
Practice for coming N.C.O's exam.
At 10.30 a.m. full dress parade & were officially handed over to D.G.M.S. by Lt Colonel Croll. DIsmissed & went to our tents at 11.30 a.m.
Another batch of troops left for firing line last night at 12 mnght.
Hospital train passed through to Suez. Train of wagons arrives at main camp. Moved kits & tents during afternoon to new sight [site].
[The following sentence is crossed out:] In the morning for a time was on fatigue in charge of party.
