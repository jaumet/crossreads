<p>Somme<br/>[September 1918]<br/>12 Th<br/>Now in the vicinity of Peronne, the country is bare but it as not suffered much from shell fire<br/>13 Fri<br/>Nothing much doing on this front, 2 hun planes brought down in flames.Brigade sports held<br/>14 Sat<br/>Out on this sector we seem to be cut off from the world no news of anything and not even a cigarette</p>
