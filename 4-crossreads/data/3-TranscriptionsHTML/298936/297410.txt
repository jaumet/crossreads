<p>September 1915</p>
<p>Saturday 11</p>
<p>Got pay from paymaster in town &amp; visited dentist at Raz &ndash; el  his hospital to get that offending molar out.</p>
<p>It was rather sore but it has caused me a lot of trouble.</p>
<p>Any amount of French people in town.</p>
<p>French &amp; Italian soldiers</p>
<p>Cafes after French style, &amp; Cafes Chantant or music halls open all days [indecipherable] for payment of stiff prices for drinks.</p>
