
At Broadmeadows we had an exam to finish up, & obtained a certificateBuzzer 100 Semaphore 100 mores Flag 95.Oct 21stLiverpool camp once again. Arrived in Sydney Sat. last. returned to camp 18th. Expect to leave next week.Nov. 2ndAm waiting on No1 Wharf Wooloomooloo to embark on SS Empire.Left Liverpool about 4.30 Am arrived in Sydney about 6 Am, walked through crowds to wharf. We started off in fours, but had to form into single file to get through A crowd of peopleNov. 2nd 5.25 pmOn boat, & moving slowly down harbour.Things are unsettled onboard, but expect that it will all straighten out very soonNov. 4th Laying in Port Melbourne. fine day.There have been a good few sick, & I have had my share 3.40 pm is the time. Do not know how long we shall be here & we are most of us looking forward to a few hours on shore, but do not think that we will get it.There is a canteen on board open only from 11 till 3. There is also a great rush during these hours.
