<p>September, 1915<br/>Wednesday 29<br/>Just heard a Mail was going so post this. <strike>2 letters from Kath &amp; one Aunt Louis mentioning Marjorie coming have not seen her yet.<br/>Love to all, tell Joe he cannot dream of joining the fun, no chance my going home for a holiday &ndash; </strike> Probably go Dardanelles 10 days or so. Did my medal arrive? Photo goes later.</p>
