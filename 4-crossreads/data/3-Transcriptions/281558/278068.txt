<p>Friday Dec 7th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : mild &amp; inclined to rain : before the S.M.O. in morn &amp; marked for another weeks m'ssge : concert at night in Y.M.C.A. &amp; was very nice indeed</p>
<p>Saturday Dec 8th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : fatigue at Y.M.C.A. : Reg &amp; self went for a stroll after dinner : concert at Y.M.C.A. after tea &amp; very good indeed</p>
<p>Sunday Dec 9th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : stroll in afternoon : service at Y.M.C.A. after tea &amp; it was very good indeed</p>
<p>Monday Dec 10th</p>
<p>Sutton Veny : kit inspection in morn : classification &amp; V.D. inspection in afternoon</p>
