Of course I would love you and Father to be home on this side &ndash; but I might never see you.<br/>I shall not write more in this letter dearest as I must write a copy one to send the girls too. I received a very sweet but sad letter from Mrs Scot Skirving this mail smudged with tears. I am so glad she can cry. How terrible it must be to loose a son like that.
