<p>Wednesday 11 July 1917.</p>
<p>Discharged from hospital at 10 am. No duty today &amp; go back to join my unit tomorrow. &nbsp;The boys in A.C.B.F.A. lines had practice at&nbsp;riding mules.</p>
<p>Paid&nbsp;this afternoon 3 Egyptian&nbsp;&pound; notes (In English money&nbsp;&pound;3/1s/7d.)</p>
<p>Wrote 1 letter</p>
<p>10 aeroplanes up this morning.</p>
