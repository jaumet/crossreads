<p>just close handy we are expecting the Coy in about 12 tonight &amp; I expect they will be done right up for they have had a rough time.  It has been a most glorious day &amp; the sun was quite hot. Got 2 parcels tonight.</p>
<p>25th 'Anzac Day" broke dull &amp; cloudy, &amp; we have moved off further back very slow for the men are very sore &amp; tired for they had a rough time while in the trenches this time &amp; the march was more in the shape of a procession, the Battn</p>
