<p>Sunday 9 December 1917<br/>Usual routine:  clear days, rain finished for time.  Wrote home &amp; Jess.</p>
<p>Monday 10 December 1917<br/>Usual routine.  Grazing [indecipherable] during day.  Wrote Jess.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11 December 1917<br/>Grazing morning &amp; evening.  On picquet.  Very cold &amp; windy.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12 December 1917<br/>Grazing in morning &amp; evening &amp; went to Brig. Ryrie's house for Reunion of old T.K.S boys, had very enjoyable night.  (29)</p>
<p>Thursday 13 December 1917<br/>Grazing in morning, more during afternoon &amp; cleaning up gear for inspection.  Orderly Corporal today.</p>
<p>Friday 14 December 1917<br/>Muster parade of Brigade before Gen. Chauval to congratulate us on work done during "Big Push".</p>
<p>Saturday 15 December 1917<br/>Quiet day.  Usual routine, wrote Nos. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 9 and 4].</p>
