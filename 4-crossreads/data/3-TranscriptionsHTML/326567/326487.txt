<p>out to write up my Diary, while I am here in the Dug Out I can hear the shells whistling over head &amp; exploding some distance away.<br/>Friday 29th<br/>Things all day were quiet except that the continuous Road of the Big Guns, a Big Gun close by seem to be playing Lawn Tennis with Fritz as the Gun would send over a shell to Fritz &amp; in a few minutes Fritz would send another back &amp; that's how they amuse themselves. After Tea I went along to our O.C. quarters for my Pass to go &amp; see my brother over at Steinvercke after obtaining the Pass I met a Company Sergeant Major who was after a Pass also &amp; waited for him &amp; when he received his pass we both came back to our Camp &amp; I drew two days rations consisting of 1 Tin of Meat &amp; vegetables 1 of Bully Beef, 1 Loaf of Bread, Tea &amp; Sugar, Cheese, some Butter &amp; a Tin of Jam. We left word for the Guard to call us at 4.45 am &amp; the Night Cook promised to have something</p>
