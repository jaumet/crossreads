  
 Nov. 8. Another visit by German Aircraft - no damage - We lose Capt Hamilton - Despatch of Parcel (cards) - Quiet night 
 Nov. 9. Day duty - Acquaintances renewed - 8th Fld. - Aust Mail. 
 Nov. 10th Another quiet day - Rumours of Peronne - Still another visit by Taube - 37 patients killed or wounded in the Rest Station - No bon! 
 Nov. 11th Quiet day (for us) - fine night unfortunately - German armoured train shells our camp without doing damage - lively time while it lasts. 
 Nov. 12th News of the Arabia - Reuben Lovelock - Our Camp No mans land prior to offensive! Our tent originally German Officer's
