<p>Tuesday 12 Sept. 1916<br/>A dull day with drizzling rain at intervals. In the morning the 3rd Bde. Gas Officer &amp; Gas N.C.O.s came out to take over gas appliances &amp; I was kept busy taking them round showing the area, positions of gas alarms &amp;c. &amp;c.</p>
