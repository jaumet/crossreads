Dec 6th  Still very cold, but bright & no wind. frozen under foot all day - we made our trip for suppllies as usual - I wrote several letters in the evening. The Primus was'nt going too well, I had no prickers -
 
Dec 7th  Weather quite mild today  we got home from supplies at 2.30 P.M. as usual & worked on the lorry till 4.P.M. - Fred (came over with me on the "Medic") from 5th D.S.C. came in & spent the evening with me & we partook of some delicasies that I had received that day in a parcel from my Aunt in Kingston -
 
Dec 8th  Very warm to-day & a little wet & muddy underfoot. Yank got back from leave (8 days late) & was put on 38316 lorry - We made our trip for supplies as usual.
 
Dec 9th  Sunday - It rained all last night & all day to-day - We did our usual trip for suppllies
 
Dec 10th  The weather cleared & remained fair but it was very raw cold - Joe & Jim came home together well inked & I had to put them both to bed & give them something to eat - Usual trip on supplies
