 Sunday 29 October 1916 B. meets Alick Bax.  Receive Parcel from Bess.  Very wet &amp; rainy.  I didn't go to church.  Wrote to Dad (3 cards), Mum (Xmas card), Mum.  Sent view to Marie. 
 Monday 30 October 1916 Recd. letter from Marie.  Wrote Dad, Geo., Jennie &amp; P.C. to Bess.  I am Canteen Corp. &amp; don't go on Duty till 8.40 p.m.  Marg. &amp; Dulce &amp; Marie 2 P.C's. 
 Tuesday 31 October 1916 Wrote to Marie.  Major Raffan visits us.  Ellis mags all night so that I can't write.  I finish up about 10.30 p.m. 
 Wednesday 1 November 1916 We fix the Electric Light on properly.  Wrote to Maynie &amp; Queenie.  Dave comes over for supper. 
 Thursday 2 November 1916 Receive letters from Roy &amp; I go out for my washing &amp; send films to be developed.  Wrote to Frances, Nettie, Jack Preddey &amp; Ada Cohen.  Ellis &amp; I go to the Pictures. 
 Friday 3 November 1916 Recd. letter from Marie.  Wrote to H. Hey, Dad, Roy &amp; Marie. 
 Saturday 4 November 1916 Ellis goes to Amesbury with W. Ham.  Nin &amp; I go to Tuti-Fruiti.  We take Photos round the Ground.  Gen. Moore comes around.  I get a new pair of Boots.  Staff issued with Cigarettes. 
 Ellis meets Leo. Baker in Amesbury.  I dream about M. &amp; apricots.
