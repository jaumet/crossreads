<p>12 July 1915<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Piquet again<br/>About 3 am we are awakened by the arrival of our 5th Reinfs<br/>Great disappointment I get five letters from Sydney but none from home   &amp;  can't make it out why it can have missed</p>
<p>1915 July 13<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Officially take over the new men   &amp;  set them making homes for themselves<br/>Are called away without any warning to do a four hour job without any tea<br/>return   &amp;  have a good old supper at 9 pm.</p>
