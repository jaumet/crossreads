
Wednesday 14 November 1917.
At 10 am. moved to the foot of the hill on which is the village of Yebna. Given an hrs. leave in the village. At our Collecting Station wounded Turks are coming in being brought in.
Took snap of 91 Turkish prisoners and a couple of the village.
The oranges we got here are good oh and are a change needed in the diet of bully & biscuits ration. 
Got a little rest tonight apart from 40 mins picquet from 11.20 pm to midnight.
