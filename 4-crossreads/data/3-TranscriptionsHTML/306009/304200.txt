<p>1915<br/>Jan. 14 Thu.<br/>Mess orderly<br/>Arrived at Alexandria about 9.30 a.m. Very fine harbour.- complete coaling facilities.<br/>No shore leave granted.<br/>A Lance-Cpl. from the First Expeditionary Force came on board and was entertained at dinner. Came from Sydney and belonged to 3rd Battalion. Infantry at Cairo.<br/>Several R.A.M.C. [Royal Army Medical Corps] men came on board during afternoon. There are 600 of them stationed at Alexandria.</p>
