<p>fit to eat &amp; all had to be thrown away.  We must have luck or it is your choice of cakes.  I had a letter from Jennie last week in which she stated that Her Sailor Boy, Jack, a <strike>submarine</strike> mine-sweeper officer had asked Her to marry Him.  She has consented but will have to wait until after the war.  Marie &amp; Auntie have both written also &amp; they have both been a bit off colour but are improving now.  The heat has been very pronounced lately &amp; they have been busy spring cleaning.</p>
<p>I went to Sarum by train on Tuesday &amp; went for a row on the river.  It was very nice.  It is a long time since I went on the river.  I may go in on the bike on Tuesday.</p>
