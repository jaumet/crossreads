  
 September, 1917. Saturday 22 Up at 9 and waited about till 10.30 Day fine and cloudy. On pontoons on to job with Price on mono-rail - worked till 6 & home at 8. Williams missing. German heavies active & shelled all round us & we had to retire to dugout dressing stn. Numerous splinters flying about. One big shell landed right on our road and we had to repair road. German planes always active and shot down balloon  this  last evening at nightfall. Walked home via Ypres.
