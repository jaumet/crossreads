<p>France<br/>3 Mch. 18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>I am writing this in a large underground tunnel where I cant say.  We have left our comfortable billets where we have just had a good months spell, if we get another spell like it again soon it will do us.  We are both keeping well &amp; feeling none the worse for the winter although it has been pretty cold, the last couple of days a cold</p>
