
Fri. Feb. 4th -16Horses on our guns shied at camels and bolted across desert.
Sat. Feb. 5th -16Visited Cairo Zoo a very pretty and well laid out.  Animals and birds of every description from an elephant to a flea.
Sun. Feb. 6th -16Church Service 8.15 A.M.
Mon. Feb. 7th -168 men from 13/1st F.A.B. sent to Tel-el-kiber for patrol duty.
Tues. Feb. 8th -16On guard, weather very cold, wet and windy.
Wed. Feb. 9th -16Still raining very hard everyone and everything wet.
