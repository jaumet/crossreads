 Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers, 31/12/15 
 Dear Mr. Layton, Could you assist Mrs. Langhorne who brings this note to you with a little money until her pay comes, she lives with another soldiers wife who has 4 children the youngest a fortnight old &amp; is unable to go out &amp; earn anything for either of them as she has been attended to the mother &amp; the house 
 Mrs. Bright is the other womans name, I enclose a note from Mr. Alex Sadler who knows the case 
 Yours truly E. H. Bennie
