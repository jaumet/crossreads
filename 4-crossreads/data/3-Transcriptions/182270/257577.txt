<p>MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 22, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Started in Car with</p>
<p>Al, Barry, and Jo for Sydney.</p>
<p>Had lunch in Murrurindi</p>
<p>and afternoon tea at Singleton</p>
<p>I left them there; they went to</p>
<p>Wisemans Ferry. I had tea</p>
<p>in Singleton &amp; got a car on</p>
<p>to Maitland &amp; caught the 11.30 pm</p>
<p>train to Taree.</p>
