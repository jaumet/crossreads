 Col Cade came up &amp; spoke a few kindly words of encouragement &amp; appreciation when I arrived there, &amp; after a dressing of the eyes, a cup of soup, &amp; a chat with several of the boys, away the motor went to Main D.S. at Kemmel.  A little formality there &amp; then on  th  to C.C.S. (No. 2 British) at Ostersteene. We spent the night at that hospital, &amp; the following morning were put on an ambulance train &amp; sped along to Bologne which we reached at dusk.
