<p>I was in the front line a couple of days ago &amp; saw a man <u>reading</u> his shirt (comprenez?), I said good day, he looked up &amp; said "calling the role Sir".  But I wish I could tell you the really witty ones.</p>
<p>Humphrey Scott is just to our left.  I saw him in a neighbouring township a few days ago, he seemed in very good spirits.</p>
<p>Wish you could see my present abode in an old ruined farmhouse, evidently a flourishing farm before the war but we get our meals regularly and are only occasionally shelled so what is there to grumble at?  I was never fitter &amp; being busy haven't time for complaining, it is inactivity not work that makes a chap miserable.</p>
