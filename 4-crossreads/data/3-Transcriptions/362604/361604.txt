<p>But this makes me sick &amp; would make you sick too.  He's got it now &amp; all the papers are lawfully His &ndash; He's seen to that &ndash; so it is best left alone.  The Lawtons are well.  The Uncles, Jennie's Father &amp; His Brother are two dull slow Yorkshiremen &amp; they are nearly as bad as old Harry Mildner (Bertie's Husband) to converse with.  They are more genuine of course but oh, so slow &amp; dull.  Ben &amp; Col are quiet but good Lads.  John is with the R.E. in Mesopotamia.  He is the bonniest of all I believe.  He is getting His commission.  Jennie is nice &amp; bright too.  But I must have written strangely.  I may have given one thought to bringing Her out to Australia.
