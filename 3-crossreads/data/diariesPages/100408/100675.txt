30/3/18 Arrived at Etaples during night stopped till 1pm. While there saw some Australians on a hospital train they had been in the big stunt. Told me we had retaken Albert that Australians Canadians New Zealanders were holding the Fritzs about Albert Doullens. Said that 40th Aust Btn has been wiped out.
