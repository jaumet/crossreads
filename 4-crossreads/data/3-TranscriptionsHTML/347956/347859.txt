<p>16.1.15</p>
<p>signalling as usual in the same old spot near the camp.  lamp practice after tea &amp; it was rather cold to be signalling at present &amp; did not stay out overtime on that account</p>
<p>Sat 16th<br/>Signalling until dinner time &amp; then received a pass out from 1.30 till 11.30 PM.  Scotty Hamilton &amp; I went by tram to the Citadel &amp; went through several mosques &amp; also through the hospital;  a Regt. of Ghurkas arrived at the Citadel from the front.  they have been either sick or wounded &amp; have been sent here for a recovery on account of the cold weather.  we went back to Cairo &amp; had</p>
