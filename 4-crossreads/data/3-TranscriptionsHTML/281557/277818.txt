<p>up on &amp; off during the night : attended drill class after tea : no land or ships sighted : one bird followed for a few hours only. slept on deck but got a bit damp with rain.</p>
<p>Wednesday Jan 5th<br/>A fairly calm day : cloudy &amp; cool after yesterday's storm : Revellie 6 am : had my wrist painted : mess orderly all day : attended bible class at 1.15 pm : boxing tournament started &amp; some willing bouts were witnessed : slept on deck no land or ships sighted.</p>
