<p>Tuesday 2 October</p>
<p>At 7.45 a.m. set off for trip to A.D.M.S. on the Menin Road about a mile further out than Ypres.  Returned by 9 a.m., travelling via Poperinghe both ways.<br/>Todays runs also include 14th Fld. Ambce. Busseboom &ndash; Poperinghe and the 6th Brigade Artillery at Ouderdom.<br/>To "K" Supply Column at Abeele.<br/>The weather has been hot and tiring denoting rain although there are no signs of clouds about.<br/>The moon being still bright "Fritz" came over again on his bombing expedition (or egg-laying competition, as the boys term it)</p>
