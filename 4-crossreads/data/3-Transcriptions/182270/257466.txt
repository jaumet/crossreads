
<p>TUESDAY, JUNE 3, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Carting hay all</p>
<p>the morning for chaff &amp; for the</p>
<p>horses in Hill paddock. Drove</p>
<p>round Boundary &amp; Mountain</p>
<p>in afternoon with Linda.</p>
<p>Linda got a fox terrier pup up</p>
<p>from Sydney &amp; gave him to me as</p>
<p>a birthday present.</p>
