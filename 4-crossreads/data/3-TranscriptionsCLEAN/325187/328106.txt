  
 [Saturday 30 December 1916] 
 Rain again.  Parcels arrived per Camels Cigars from Father in law Tucker- Miss Pagors now Mrs Stoyles & some from Mother sent back from England by Laura. Papers too but we are off  in  this morning on another 2 days stunt I hope more Captives.  Some of the parcels have been following me since April. The Native Camels are being shorn with big rough scissors about 4 inches of Hair comes off & is made into very durable Tent Coverings Rope, & mats by the natives- Small Pox has broken out in El Arish but we are camped on the Hills.
