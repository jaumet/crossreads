<p>[Roumanall] killed &ndash; midnight Charge</p>
<p>2 February 1916 Wed<br/>Route march before breakfast. Lecture by the Colonel on treatment of wounds on the desert at 10 a.m. Afternoon off &amp; Ken &amp; myself went to the the historic graveyard of Tel-el Kebir. Coldstream-Guards, Life, Dragoon, [indecipherable] buried. Met [Anthony] canteen. [age]</p>
<p>3 February 1916 Thur<br/>Signalling before breakfast. Lecture on Sanitation by Major Phipps at 10 a.m. A lot of Kid given by Major Moseley who dismisses us a quarter of an hour early. After dinner lecture on the contents of an ambulance waggon followed by loading of wounded into waggon.</p>
