 June Thursday 14 1917 
 Busy night. Patients need a lot of attention. Have one ward surgical &amp; other medical. Former takes up all our time. They are all chest wounds &amp; in almost all cases pneumonia follows as in a  [indecipherable] sequence. Medhurst, Pittams, Skinner &amp; Williams all very bad.  Rather like work of attending tho tis very constant. Sister Wood a good sister &amp; considerate.
