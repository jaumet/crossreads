<p>Thursday Dec 26<br/>Boxing day. They held a race meeting which provided a good days sport. Old Tom left us for home to day.<br/>Friday Dec 27<br/>Farewell inspection by General Chauvel to day. He complimented the Division &amp; stated he was proud to have them under his command.<br/>Saturday Dec 28<br/>Another Race meeting to day Preparing for a days sports' meeting.</p>
