<p>February 1915<br/>Thursday 25<br/>Feeling better. Temperature normal 98 &deg;. Eat a little breakfast also some dinner</p>
<p>Friday 26<br/>Woke up feeling better. Doctor Storey said I could get up. I did so but had to return to my bed with bad pains in my stomach.</p>
