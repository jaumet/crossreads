
Thursday 27 June 1918Photos.  Pilot Ltd. Headlam.  In air 1 h. 45 m., successful.Allan Brown & Fin get a Hun.  Oxenham & Smith get a Hun but crash alongside at Katiani.  Excellent work by them all.  Feared Oxenham & Smith killed.
Friday 28 June 1918Photos.  Pilot Capt. Addison, in air 1 h. 20 m., returned owing to clouds, in air 1 h. 50 m., successful.
