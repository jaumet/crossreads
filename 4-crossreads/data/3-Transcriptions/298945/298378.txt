<p>Apparently he went first up to the left part of the battle front, thence to Corbie &amp; back to Amiens through Villers Bretoneux.</p>
<p>He looked a little worried, but very dignified</p>
<p>The Somme battle has developed to quite a decent offensive. The German reserves have been dropped into the battle, yet he is still retreating. At the presen moment we are fairly near the original 1915 battle line. Up in the north a little good has been done.</p>
<p>There have been thirteen casualties among our bearers, mostly from gas. Cooke, who got the military medal up at Hill 60 after I had been gassed, has been killed; George Walton, the most enthusiastic of the Christians (if it is possible to pick one of the circle out) has been wounded rather badly</p>
