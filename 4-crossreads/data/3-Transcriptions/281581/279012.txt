I was much interested however, in the landing of railway materials which is at present going on. The cargo vessels lie out to sea, and the stuffs are ferried in by surf boats. The port is extremely treacherous, as the boats have to pass through a narrow waterway between rocks of Andromeda, and even when inside the reefs there is barely enough water in which to swing them.</p>
