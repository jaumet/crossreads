
Fares are very cheap indeed & in some cases run into a few millemes only. Cairo Railway station is very large considering the traffic
(27/2/16)  Take another trip, to the Zoo this time. The  Zoo is on the other side of the Nile & is not very much to see. There are not the same amount; nor variation of animals as in Sydney, but in some respects, the outlay is superior. Soldiers get admission for one disaster but the public must pay 5 "disasters". An amusing sight there however is an Egyptian boys band of bagpipes, big drum & kettle drum. The music is passable for bagpipes.
