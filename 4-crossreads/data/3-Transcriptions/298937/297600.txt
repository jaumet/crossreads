<p>Friday 16th June</p>
<p>Yesterday I was appointed dispenser for A.M.C. details.  Took sick parade &amp; fixed up medicines.<br/>Later in the day we moved back &amp; pitched tents in Miscellaneous Details again.<br/>Took sick parade again this morning.<br/>Told about a dispenser wanted in the 6th Light Horse, &amp; am debating on it now.<br/>Moved back to Miscell. Details.</p>
