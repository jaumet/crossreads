After about 6 days sail passed through Gibralter. One of prettiest coast sights I ever saw was Range of Sierra Nevada running along S. Coast of Spain seeming to rise from the sea making a big bluff of about 8000 feet. All the top was glistening with snow or sand (arguments) amp with near dark in the foreground it looked tremendous as I remarked previously. Usual routine of troop ship life only no parades. 
Breakfast 8 lay on decks reading or cards, most of my time at chess. A sing-song at piano every night once a week a concert. Passed by Gib Malta about a week from
