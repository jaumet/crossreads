  
 Thursday June 13.6.18 feeling much better am still at Kantara  had a good nights sleep. Friday June 14.6.18 am feeling a bit Better left Kantara by train to day arrived Cairo 4.P.M got in car was taken to 31.g H abbassia dont feel to well after the trip  had a bath and went to bed.  very hot night. Saturday June 15.6.18 am not feeling to good feel very giddy  lying down all day the heat is awful  am not well to night Sunday June 16.6.18 am feeling a bit better this morning went to church at hospital very hot Monday June 17.6.18 feeling Better to day it is very hot
