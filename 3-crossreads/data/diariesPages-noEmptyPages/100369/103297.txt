We were ashore for a few hours two days in succession. It seems nothing will grow in the Soil. Some attempt has been made to grow some climbing plants along the fortway on the Crescent, and the plants are in barrels in soil evidently brought from other parts, and even then they present a very sickly appearance.
The unfavourable impression received of the Port from the Ship was more than confirmed on going ashore. Dirt, vile smells, and unsightliness prevail everywhere. The natives seem rather more energetic than Colombo, but not quite so persistent in their endeavours to force their wares on one.
