Saturday 14thGot orders to draw our Packs from Q.M. & be ready to move straight after dinner Came back to where we left 2 days ago. It is a good camp no guns about & only an odd shell comes back this way. It is more cumfie in the dug out tonight than falling over loose wire & dodging about wet trenches has been the last 2 nights.
