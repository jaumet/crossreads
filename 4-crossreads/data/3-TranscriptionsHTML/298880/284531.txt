<p>30th August</p>
<p>A good deal of rain has fallen and seems inclined to keep on falling. We are having a very easy time now. Sgt Tillin who is now in charge of us is a real sport. He was a signal Sgt in the 6th Dragoons and took part in the retreat from Mons.</p>
<p>1st September</p>
<p>We had 98 letters to send today <strike>on</strike> in 3 1/3 minutes on the large flag. It was very <strike>to</strike> stiff work. We also had 20 questions to answer in our A1 paper.</p>
