  
 Lemnos 12.10.15 My dear Sister, Your letter of 31.8.15 & Beres also Mrs Pollock to hand today. I get one & sometimes two from you every mail now, all mail is sent to the unit to be marked first & if you are with your unit you get your mail straightaway, all mail is marked by Coy Quartermaster Sergeants from the record books & sent to base & it is at the said base that a man loses run of his mail when he is unfortunate enough to be wounded
