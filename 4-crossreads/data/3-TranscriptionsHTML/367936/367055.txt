<p>Sunday 15 October 1916<br/>Swim in morning.<br/>Went into Alexandria in afternoon;  took tram down to Ras-el-tin (School now) then walked back through native quarter.<br/>Went to the Cursol [also spelt Kursaal], home about 12-30.</p>
<p>Monday 16 October 1916<br/>Wrote letters in morning.  Dick, Hunt, Hargrave and I went into Alex, saw Pompeys Pillar.<br/>Left shell bands at tin smith to be made into rings.<br/>Had dinner at Stella d'Italy.  Went to Cursol, also drove round Anfouchy in Garry.</p>
