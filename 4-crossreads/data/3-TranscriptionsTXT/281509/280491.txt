
25 September 1916Monday
Cart, Bob, self, Jockey, Mac, Tony & Bill Parsons get our phizogs taken in group in Pop. Tea after, & get it cooked by waitress. Cart, Bob & self back early carrying sponge cake for trifle for banquet tomorrow night.Glee Club practice. Soldiers' Chorus, Little Tommy went a-fish & Dear little Shamrock.Views of 2 Zepps destroyed in England.
