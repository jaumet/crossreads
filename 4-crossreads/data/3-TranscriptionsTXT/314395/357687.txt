 Australia.  I fancy these six month base jobs are a compromise. 
 About 12 months ago I asked you for another photo of yourself, as I had lost the other one, perhaps you never got the letter, as the photo has never come along.  My  one  own have not yet come from Paris, but I will send one as soon as they come. 
 Russia having thrown in the marble will make things extremely awkward for us, on the west, but the Yanks may make a slight difference when they kick off. 
 Some months ago I sent home my medal.  Did you ever get it? 
 Best wishes, Your affect. Son, George.
