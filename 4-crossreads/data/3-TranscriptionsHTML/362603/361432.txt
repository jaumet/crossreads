<p>6.  Georgies good luck.  I hope that His good luck sticks.  So the old Peach Tree turned trumps after all.  We miss the fruit over here very much.  Pears we paid 9d a dozen for in Sydney are 9d each in London &amp; ordinary 4d a dozen apples are 8d per lb here, about 4 to the pound.<br/>We will get Your Parcel alright but I expect it will be some time coming.  It may get Torpedoed too.  the submarines of the Germans have got us bluffed &amp; we can do nothing.  They must have an enormous number of them.  Fancy poor Billy Mac missing.  I suppose He is a goner.  Buried by a</p>
