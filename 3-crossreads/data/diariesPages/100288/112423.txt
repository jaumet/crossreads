Sunday 27/2/16
Windy dusty. No reveille; very late breakfast. Tom Martin came to camp, had a long yarn with him. During the day also met E. Hawkless, G. Earl, V Solomon, S. Hawkless of B Coy 56 Btn H O?Donnell
