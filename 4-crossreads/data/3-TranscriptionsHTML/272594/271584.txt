<p>18th.  Heavens what a rotten day I have had, the "Orderly Sergts had to account for every man in their Coy, what they were on, &amp; what they were doing, just fancy me just arrived from France &amp; not knowing a soul in the Coy. On my morning "state" I was 11 out B. Coy (that is the 2nd Batt) were 15 off C. 9 &amp; D. 1. well I had to go back to our office &amp; find those missing men I found them but it took me till 11 oclock &amp; nearly turned</p>
