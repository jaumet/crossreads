<p>Monday 3 March 1919<br/>Busy all day. Rushed about looking for work but crashed. finished up by getting a letter from Harry. Took Marie to dinner at the Gobelen. Janet couldn't come cos the Princess was inspecting their Hospital. Took Janet &amp; Marie to see Babes in the Wood at Drury Lane. It was excellent. Both acting &amp; staging.</p>
<p>Tuesday 4 March 1919<br/>Got train to Southampton at 11.35 &amp; arrived Southampton 2 pm. Had lunch &amp; found the boat. It was a Yank named Yale &amp; wasn't going till 10 pm. We went into town &amp; had tea. Booked seats for a show &amp; wandered about. Went to the sho &amp; met two Naval people also 2 girls. Took the girls home &amp; then back to the boat. It wasn't leaving till 5.30 am &amp; as we hadn't had any dinner we blew back to a fish &amp; chips joint &amp; had a feed. Getting on to the boat at 1 am.</p>
