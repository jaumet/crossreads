<p>February 1918<br/>Monday 11</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Raid last night on Fritz's strong post. Captured 25 Fritzies. Casual. 8 killed and 15 wounded. Some fellows of the 31st Batt took part. We supported with the Barrage.<br/>Walked across to Lamp Post Corner to see Gun, and brought back water.<br/>Da Costa arrived at S.O.S. gun. I am for O.P. tomorrow. Mills on laison. Alby on F.O.O.today. <br/>Weather unsettled</p>
