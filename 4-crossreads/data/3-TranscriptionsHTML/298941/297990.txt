<p>Just passed Margny a small village.<br/>Thursday 18th April<br/>Passed a very comfortable time last night although we were packed like sardines.<br/>Present moment the train is stopped Boulogne side of Wimereux station which makes us exactly opposite 53rd Hospital.<br/>We can see away on opposite  hill ([indecipherable] hospital) the inscription<br/>"Glorire a' Jesus Christ".</p>
