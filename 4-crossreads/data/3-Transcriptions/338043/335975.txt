<p>Sept. 19th<br/>Rehearsal &amp; Show &ndash; Derive much pleasure from reading short biography of R.B. Sheridan &amp; his play "The rivals" &ndash; A visit by General Carruthers.</p>
<p>Sept. 20th<br/>Further British Advance in which Australians greatly distinguished themselves &ndash; Commence reading "The Bancrofts" &ndash; a book of delightful Theatrical Reminiscences &ndash; and I also read for the first time the "Monkey's Paw".</p>
