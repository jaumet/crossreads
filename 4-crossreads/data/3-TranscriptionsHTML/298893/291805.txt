<p>19th Batt<br/>Popes Post<br/>30-11-15</p>
<p>Capt Hale,<br/>Can you let us have a telescope or telescopic Rifle for No 1 Snipers post.<br/>The telescope on the post was broken by a piece of schrapnel yesterday &amp; I tried to get another one to replace it from ordnance but was unsuccessful.<br/>A Gray RSM</p>
