<p>March 1918<br/>Sunday 10</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Had a holiday today, and spent the forenoon in sleep, eventually drifting down to the W.L. and camp of the 30th Infantry Batt, at Wulverchem.<br/>Met Donellan, Richards Bob Court, and Sid Morell. <br/>Weather clear and warm.<br/>Fritz distributed some rather good iron rations during day.<br/>Am down for Forward Observation Post tomorrow with Mr. Kerr.<br/>10.30pm. Fritz shelling pretty consistently, Hdqs right Batt and just a few on the left.</p>
