<p>The assault was made before the break of day<br/>But many difficulties turned their way<br/>And not a man did flinch that fight<br/>But charged the enemy till put to flight<br/>We said well done &ndash; They were heroes every one</p>
<p>In the years to come history will tell<br/>Of the great charge and how the brave Australian fell<br/>And as we ponder a deep prayer we breathe<br/>For the many heroes who now lay a-sleep<br/>In a place well known as Gaba-Tepe (Teep)</p>
