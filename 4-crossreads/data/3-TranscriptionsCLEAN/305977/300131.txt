  
 Novr 1914 Thursday Novr 12th Inspection by Govr Genl in the afternoon.  Still muddy but firming up again.  Bayonet drill & instructions in guard & sentry duties in the afternoon.  McDermott turned up again.  Ashworth & Pickering had a scrap. 
 Friday 13th  Mounted parade morning & part of afternoon 2 or 3 miles along the road.  Bayonet drill.  On fatigue duty in the afternoon on forage.  5 of the troop had uniforms issued.  Fairly warm day. 
 Saturday 14th  Extremely hot muggy day.  Mounted squadron drill in the morning B Squadron had a wild gallop.  No damage beyond a couple of spills.  On fatigue duty in the afternoon putting up a shelter for blacksmiths.  Regiment got their uniforms.
