<p>1915<br/>Aug. 30 Mon.<br/>Have developed a sore throat but am gargling it daily.<br/>Troops are now in Egypt from many parts of the British Empire:-<br/>English, Scotch, Irish, Australian, New Zealand, Indian, Ceylon, Canadian, Maori, R. Naval Division, Welsh.</p>
