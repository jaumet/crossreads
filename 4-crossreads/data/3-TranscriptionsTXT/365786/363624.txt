A staff of doctors, nurses & orderlies was on each train & saw to the needs of our men.On arrival in Cairo the trains were met by members of the British & Australian Red Cross who supplied cool drinks, refreshments & cigarettes to the men while they waited their turn for the motor ambulances which carried them to the hospitals.  The big No. 1 (one) General Hospital is situated at Heliopolis - it is a magnificent palace-like building & the men were well looked after there as at all our hospitals.  At The No. 2 Aust.
