<p>Monday 31.7.16<br/>About 60 of us have to toe the Carpet this morning for having in our possession, government property &ndash; to wit, blanket (spare) which should have been handed into the quarter store.  Gave a satisfactory explanation &amp; my charge was withdrawn.</p>
<p>Tuesday 1.8.16<br/>Weather has been rather warm for the past few days.  Advance party left for Divisional Rest Station at Doulieu.</p>
