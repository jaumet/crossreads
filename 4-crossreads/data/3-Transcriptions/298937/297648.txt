<p>I saw some soldiers who had 3 strips of gold braid.<br/>Another outstanding feature is the number of women doing men's work, - sweeping streets, driving carts etc. etc.<br/>The town is splendid.<br/>High up on a hill to the East of it is Notre Dame Cathedral which I noticed when we were steaming in.<br/>Surmounting the steeple is a beautiful gilt statue of great size.<br/>On the lowland east of the town &amp; I think on a branch of the Rhone delta is a large construction resembling a suspension bridge.
