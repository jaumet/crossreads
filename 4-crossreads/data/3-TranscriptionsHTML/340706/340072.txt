<p>It is surprising how many nooks &amp; corners this little village possesses. All mills here are run by water wheels &amp; every mill is lighted by electricity but the town &amp; private houses are on a contract with gas Co &amp; there is no gas since 1914 on account of shortage of coal &amp; so if you are out late at night you are not troubled with the glare of street lights but you way is lighted by the dim religious light of the stars.</p>
<p>18-4-19 (Fri)<br/>This is Good Friday but here it is the same as any other day. There is a voluntary church parade &amp; for the remainder there is a voluntary church<br/>parade so for those who would have clean souls their bodies must remain unclean.</p>
<p>For myself I set off on my billeting &amp; inspecting job. This morning while I was away there has been a big rumpus. Some days ago I opened the door leading out of my room &amp; finding no one there appropriated a carpet, a chair &amp; a rug.</p>
