
1915July 16 Fri.Nearly all the special duty contingent selected but not the Clerk. There are 8 candidates for the job:- 1 St. Sgt., 1 Sgt., 1 L.Cpl & 5 ptes. Competition is very keen.Later: Competition is still keener - there are now only 1 Sgt. 1 L.Cpl & myself in the running.Latest: Defeat! The lucky man is the Lance Corporal.Attended Scout meeting - the boys played games some of which were terrifically funny - I don't think I have laughed harder since I've been in Egypt.
