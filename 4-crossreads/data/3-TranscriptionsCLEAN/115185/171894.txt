Two opportunities of helping I had to miss - being unable to walk far enough - one was to see Gibson's Batt, which has been here 6 weeks and have tea with a N.Z. officer a Lieut Carr. They have gone back today to the [indecipherable] - I saw them march past our hospital - down our main street. I was just going to bed it was 10 am. I'm on night duty, and heard the band and ran along to cheer them. One man in the ranks shouted - "Goodbye Sister McMillan" - I think he must have been a man I found in a tent near my ward in the first rush of bringing the wounded in after Suvla Bay action - & noticing his badge told him I had a brother in his regiment.
