<p>July 1915<br/>Tuesday 27<br/>Same old dust. Still building horse troughs. No hay issue. Pretty rotten</p>
<p>Wednesday 28<br/>Same<br/>A number of our camp paraded before the doctor to see who was to go back to the Peninsular. I am fit alright, he said.</p>
