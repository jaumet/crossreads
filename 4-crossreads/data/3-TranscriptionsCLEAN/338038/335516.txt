 3rd Squadron A.F.C. France  4.9.18 
 My Dear Cousin Willie Your very welcome letter of 18.6.18 reached me a few days ago and I hasten to send my very sincere thanks for the same.  You were quite right in your letter in saying that I had not heard of poor old Terry's death by the 22nd April, in fact I think it was about the very end of that month that Aunt Bee wrote & told me the sad news.
