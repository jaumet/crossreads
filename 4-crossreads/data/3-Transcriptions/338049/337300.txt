<p>Sunday 31 March<br/>To Central Hall. Meth. Church. Anniversary service but not much different from the ordinary.<br/>Met Frank Rogerson. Had great sing.</p>
<p>Afternoon visited Braid Hills again. Very misty.</p>
<p>Evening strolled about &amp; finally turned up at Station in time for 10 p.m. train. Left Will who could not travel until tomorrow. Farewelled Edinburgh people in Sure Scotch style.</p>
<p>Frank Rogerson in same carriage. Good sleep.</p>
