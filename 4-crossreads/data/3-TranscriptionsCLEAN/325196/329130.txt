  
 5-3-16 Sun 5th It is reported that we leave here (Bahnasa) next Tuesday or Wed. & are to go to Minia & that English troops are coming here to take our place, nothing out of the ordinary happens here the days & [are] rather warm to be comfortable but the nights are quite chilly, I brought the set of boxing gloves & we make use of them pretty well every evening
