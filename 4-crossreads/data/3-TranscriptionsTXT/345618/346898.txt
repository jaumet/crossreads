 My past &amp; future are dragged out of me with a directness of attack of which only the inhabitants of  the  Australia (&amp; New Zealand) are capable and for the rest of our weary voyage to the Holy Land via Pagan Egypt all matters  concerning  pertaining to higher Ethics especially in  relation to the  their bearing on the relations of the sexes, are referred to me. 
 The Third occupant myself I need not mention.  If the reader has sufficient patience to wade through this little book of light &amp; personal vulgarities he will assuredly achieve  an appreciation  an insight into my doubtful character which no amount of  cunningly  skilled &amp; cunning journalism could conceal.
