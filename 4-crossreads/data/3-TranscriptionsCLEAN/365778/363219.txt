  
 Saturday 15/9/17 Rode to Winnezeele via Steenvorde[Steenvoorde] had lunch returned 6.30 pm letter from Doff. Sunday 16/9/17 Visit from Ralph, went around camp things not yet up to mark. Monday 17/9/17 Route marches etc letters from Doff, Rene & Sherbon. Tuesday 18/9/17 Wrote to Doff, men coming and going in large numbers. Wednesday 19/9/17 Orders received for big stunt which comes off tomorrow, finished letter to Doff.
