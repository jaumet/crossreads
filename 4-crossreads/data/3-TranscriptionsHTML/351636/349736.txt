<p>15-4-17.<br/>Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers</p>
<p>Dear Sir<br/>Can you give Mrs. Hendry temporary assistance till daughters who go to work but at present are ill can go back again. She has had some assistance before from you &pound;5 for glasses for her daughter &amp; herself, but in need at present</p>
<p>Yours sincerely<br/>E. H. Bennie</p>
