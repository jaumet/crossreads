<p>The boat began to move from the wharf. Women and children then began weeping and I felt very glad that I stopped my loved ones from coming thus far. As it was I had to choke down a lump that came in my throat.</p>
<p>To make things worse the band played Auld Lang Syne. As we drew further away the wind caught the streamers, making a very pretty spectacle. We remained in mid stream until after dinner passing through the heads at 1 PM on the 9th of April.</p>
<p>Although I became seasick right away, I had a long fond look at our well beloved shores.</p>
