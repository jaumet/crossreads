<p>A man who was near told me the pilots corpse hit the ground head first and went <strike>nea</strike> about 2 feet in <strike>off of</strike> with the rest of his body sticking out &ndash; of course it was just a pulp.</p>
<p>Nov 5/17<br/>Still with D.H.Q on the Ypres Front &ndash; am at present taking rations up to advanced Headquarters at Lille Gate each day from Dickebush</p>
<p>Fritz has been very busy 'bombing' each night &amp; pretty well everyone even back here is 'dug in' even the horse lines have ramparts of ear[t]h build behind the horses.</p>
