
7 November 1915Sunday23rd After Trinity
Dad   &  I go into Cairo after dinner   &  have a very pleasant afternoon in the Sgts mess belonging to the Ordnance Dept of Kasr-el-nil Barracks   &   Also  go through the Cairo Museum.
1915 November 8Monday
Spend most of the day pottering about in Dad's work-shop. having nothing to do in my own Coy.I have applied to attend a School of instruction in Zeitoun.
