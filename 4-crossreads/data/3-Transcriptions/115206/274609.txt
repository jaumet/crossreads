<p>February 1918<br/>Friday 8</p>
<p>Messines<br/>There is a scene of desolation round these parts; on our left and in front of the old pill box, is a mass of ruined houses once a small village. It is known as Guy Farm<br/>On clear days one can see old Fritzies villages, Comines. And Werweick, as marked by a cluster of steeples and church spires.<br/>Weather unsettled<br/>Was on 12 to 2 last night.</p>
