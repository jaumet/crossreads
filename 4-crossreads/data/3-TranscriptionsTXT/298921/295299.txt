 6 December 1916  Monday Cheer Oh a Mail &ndash; Joes of 29/8/15 &amp; Mothers Daisy 27/9/15 Laura 31/10/15  &amp; papers, probably more letters when sorted. The Ghoorkas suffered thro the frost, half the Battalion was frost bitten &ndash; The Ghoorkas get on well with our men who try to teach them our ways &ndash; Watched them playing CARDS, to shuffle the Pack then throw them into the air &amp; then collect. More letters &amp; Papers today &amp; good news re Russians coming to our help through Roumania. Will post this as one never knows when a boat will be sunk &amp; better chance a little than the lot.
