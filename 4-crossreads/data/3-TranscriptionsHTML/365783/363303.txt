<p>(No.8.)<br/>Troopship "Kyarra"<br/>Nearing Fremantle<br/>11th December 1914<br/>Dearest old Mrs,<br/>Well here we are nearing old Mother earth once more and it is pleasant to see the hills &amp; dales after five monotonous days of winds &amp; waves. But we have not had too bad a time, it was a bit unpleasant for the first day out from Melbourne but since then it has been all right. We expect to reach Fremantle late this afternoon &amp; will probably be there till Monday. We have not yet heard what our destination is to be but will probably know after we get away from Fremantle.   A graduation list of officers was</p>
