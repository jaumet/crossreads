
doing my best to fall in with my daily routine but not without thinking every now and then of my 14 day's leave.
Sept 9th - 18 [this date is wrong, it is either the 10th or the 11th]Yesterday we moved to this new position nearer the line a small village called Halle all that remains of the village is the name
Sept 12th - 18Off again at daybreak and found our way through torrents of rain to Lincourt Wood where we are now occupy huts which only a few days ago had german tenants
We are likely to stay here for a couple of weeks.
