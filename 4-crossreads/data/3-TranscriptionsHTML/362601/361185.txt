<p>7 April, Monday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I go the classes other to dance. I play for 3 &amp; dance 3 &amp; see Miss Dick home &amp; go home to bed very crook. I read awhile until Bill comes home.</p>
<p>Lovely hot day.</p>
<p>Wrote Ellis &amp; M.R.L.</p>
<p>8 April, Tuesday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I go to Sarum &amp; meet Ellis, we talk &amp; sup at Osmonds. I change hats &amp; see him off. Send wire to I.O.M. &amp; Glasgow. I go to the Close &amp; also No 13 where I stay. I am very early &amp; entertain everyone all night with funny experiences.</p>
<p>Recd P.C. from Ellis. I dream about Bun, S. of Arts &amp; falling off pile of boxes also M. will go to hit with me inspite of all 'cos it is all night.</p>
