<p>(49)<br/>It means the title of a tragedy played unseen to the outer world<br/>Even in places of peace it means all this<br/>But here in France, what does it mean?<br/>For Home is not, but was.<br/>And the was of it is more than tragedy.<br/>Our march is still through the town.<br/>The day has almost fully come. The sky in the East has cleared. The last veil of night has been torn and cast aside.<br/>The purity of beauty which is never so manifest in things of muddy earth, is there in that broad field of sky.</p>
