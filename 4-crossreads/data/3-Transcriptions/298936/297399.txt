<p>August 1915</p>
<p>Tuesday 31</p>
<p>Arrived opposite Suez at about 6 a.m. &amp; [indecipherable] over until 11 oclock when we moved into the wharf.</p>
<p>Terribly busy having all medical stores packed ready for disembarkation.</p>
<p>Got kits together &amp; moved our men off into a special carriage reserved for A.M.C.</p>
<p>Train left for Cairo at 2.20 &amp; arrived at 8 or 9. Very interesting country at parts.  Goats &amp; camels tended by picturesque Arabs in oases'</p>
<p>Date palms &amp; various plants under cultivation sand for the rest.</p>
<p>Arrived 1st Hosp. at 10 supper &amp; tent.</p>
