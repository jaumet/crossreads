<p>been reconnoitring over Gallipoli alighted on the water about a mile down the bay, and motored to a buoy between us &amp; the "Ark Royal".  We are not afraid of being torpedoed here, besides having to pass the T.B.D's patrolling the entrance, there is only 7 feet of water under us which would prove rather shallow for submarines to manouvre in.  Anyhow even if we were torpedoed the ship couldn't sink very far.  A couple of destroyers, colliers and the baloon ship arrived during the afternoon &amp; took up anchorages.  At 7.30pm 3 French</p>
