The entire Bdge left El Burg at noon &amp; moved of in section column the great serpent, over rocky defiles &amp; plain made a very impressive spectacle. The distant travelled to our nearest camp was twelve miles &ndash; a long track of slipping, slimy mud, the Bdge drew into camp just before dark &amp; are to move pff again tomorrow at 9 am.</p>
<p>Jan 3rd</p>
<p>We left the 3rd Bdge camp at 11 am. With a lumber for our baggage with horses for Capt H &amp; myself &amp; after a ten mile ride we arrived back at Dieran where we again took up our residence for a few days.</p>
