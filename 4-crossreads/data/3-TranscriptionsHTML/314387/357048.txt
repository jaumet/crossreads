<p>Rested during day left about ten &amp; night for front line for a hop [indecipherable]. Wednesday 26th<br/>Attacked the German trenches Polygon Wood about day light gained all objectivities enemy counter attacked on the right late in afternoon he got driven back with heavy casualities aeroplane brought down by our infantry late in afternoon <strike>afternoon</strike> fairly quiet during night time.<br/>Thursday 27th<br/>Dug in and laid low all day very heavy bombardment &amp; dusk got relieved by 32nd battalion eight o'clock</p>
