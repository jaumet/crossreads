<p>(four), going by fields &amp; roads, but mostly roads.  Shelling ahead on the way up, &amp; not comfortably far away either.  On the return he started shelling behind us at a cross roads we had left, &amp; following the other &amp; not our road thank goodness.  Then some fell ahead but it was erratic. Still <strike>they all fl</strike> a few fell far too near for comfort.  One man killed &amp; a couple wounded on the track ahead of us.</p>
