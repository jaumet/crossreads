1917 Monday 5 March
Furphies are flying round that we are to be relieved in a day or two. 
The good news is confirmed. The 2nd division is taking over tomorrow, we should all be out in a day or two. The 2nd 3rd Field Bearers are all being withdrawn from the relays going out tomorrow. 1st Field bearers are to carry on by themselves till relieved. Hayman, Maloney self 3 dressers at Edward's Post had to go up to Factory Corner, after tea for duty. Hayman self put on Gas Guard. I doing from 10 p.m. to 2 a.m. H. 2 to 6 a.m. Up in a possie in the sap not much protection. 18 pounder battery barks just a few yards away now then enough to give anyone shell shock. Wrote home letter.
