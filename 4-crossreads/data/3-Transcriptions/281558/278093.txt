<p>&amp; them 6 pm &ndash; midnight so as to change our shifts : Summer time came in today &amp; all time us put one hour ahead :</p>
<p>Monday March 11th  Tuesday 12th &amp; Wednesday March 13th</p>
<p>Misery : usual routine : stroll each morn round about : fair bit of gunfire going on : glorious weather still</p>
<p>Thursday March 14th</p>
<p>Misery : stroll round in morn &amp; amused myself watching the Africans working on the canal :</p>
<p>Friday March 15th</p>
<p>Misery : stroll in morn : on at noon : plenty of gunfire lately :</p>
<p>Misery Saturday March 16th</p>
<p>Stroll round in morn &amp; watched them building bridges over the Somme :</p>
<p>Sunday March 17th</p>
<p>Misery : stroll to Brie &amp; had a look</p>
