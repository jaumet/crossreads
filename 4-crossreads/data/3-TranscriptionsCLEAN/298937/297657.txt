Here the scenery is flat relieved by beautiful groupings of trees, cottages & avenues, while again it is hilly with an old stone bridge here & there & lichen covered rocks & boulders strewn everywhere.  What a relief after Egypt.  After Avignon we stopped at Orange for tea - one hour. Cheers, hand waves & smiles wherever we pass, either station or lonely country crossing.
