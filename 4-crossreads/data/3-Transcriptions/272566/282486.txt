<p>Monday 12 February</p>
<p>In morning went to Whiteleys and had a few Bus rides round Paddington.  Ride in tube from Charing Cross to Kings Cross.  In afternoon went to a performance at The Colliseum.  This is a magnificent Theatre of marble and about 24 <strike>dozen</strike> boxes.  At 9.45 p.m. caught train to Berwick from Kings Cross.<br/>Passed York &amp; Newcastle arrived Berwick just over the border at 8.30 a.m. (over).</p>
