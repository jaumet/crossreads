  
 Saturday 11 May Up as usual packed up and luggage dumped. 
 Out at 8-30 to Lahoussye with wagon to reel up two spare lines to Behrencourt - Pont Noyelles Road. Had dinner. Picked up drum cable at 2nd Div. 
 Arrived home 3 p.m. at Allonville. Evening to Pierrots with Hoppy. Really enjoyable. Salved good book of Aussie. 
 Mod off evening hay.
