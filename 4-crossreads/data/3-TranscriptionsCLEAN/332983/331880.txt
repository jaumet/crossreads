Instruction in box gas helmet 26-5-1916 Rained like fury all night, cloudy today. 109 rounds ammunition to Bty. 7,000 rounds of S.A. drawn from D.A.C. 27-5-1916 Warned that the wagon line would be inspected by General Birdwood and Hobbs. 148 rounds of shrap sent to guns. Enemy very quiet. Some big move coming off. Weather fine 28-5-1916 Our Artillery very active. 121 rounds sent to Bty last night. A German cook house was spotted, six Batteries opened fire on it. Finished cook house. One of our aeroplanes had a rough time last night at 8 oclock, About 250 shells fired at him by Fritz.
