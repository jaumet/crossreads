  
 Under cover of darkness all the mounted troops & artillery retired to Tel el Gemme where we arrived just after mid-night & camped till morning. Our casualties (the mounted troops operating on the flank) for the day would amount to something like 450 in killed & wounded, but our attack was considered quite successful in its object of helping in the main attack at Gaza.
