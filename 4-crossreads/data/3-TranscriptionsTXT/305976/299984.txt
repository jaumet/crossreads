Had a few hours sleep near Telat El Doon[Talat ed Dumm] on side of road. Thursday March 21 Plague of Caterpillars &amp; very hot here Remarkable views of hills &amp;  villages  valleys. We had great difficulty in watering having to go right down to Wadi [indecipherable] over rough narrow uneven track Friday March 22 Movement cancelled until bridge complete over River Jordan. Our boys having difficulty with bridge owing to Turks shelling &amp; bombing
