  
 [Report from Lt. Col. J. Paton] 
 S.S. 'Komet' at Sea 13th Oct 1914 
 The Administrator Rabaul 
 Sir I have the honor to report that pursuant to your instructions I proceeded in the 'Nusa' with Lt Comdr Jackson and a party of Naval & Military members of the Expeditionary Force at 4 am on the 9th inst for the purpose of (1) clearing up the situation in regard to the Wireless Station working on the Islands (2) Effecting the capture of the K.G.S. 'Komet' - The 'Komet' has been captured undamaged and the Wireless plant is on board and in working order.
