  
 January  1918 
 Sunday 6 Went to Church & a walk in afternoon came on to rain the first for some time Have had 18 Australian letters through the week 
 Monday 7 Marking on Range Hall won the Suppers today 
 Tuesday 8 Range again also another win for Hall - Rought march at night 
 Wednesday 9 Same as yesterday finished today Drill in afternoon. A cold day
