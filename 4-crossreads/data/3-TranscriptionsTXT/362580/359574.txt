 hospital ships &ndash; one The Kanwana which was supposed to sail that night for Australia with invalided &amp; wounded. 
 Two standing out at anchor waiting to go to the Dardanelles.  A number of large troopships.  Nearly every day boats are leaving for the front. 
 Lord Brassey's "Sunbeam" is lying at anchor, fitted up as a hospital boat &ndash; mostly for convalescing officers &ndash; she looks very clean &amp; smart. 
 It was a perfect day &amp; the sea is a beautiful blue here &ndash; It is the Mediterranean and is just as blue as we read about.  The sunset is simply too wonderful to describe. 
 Sunday morning two of us went to see Sister Nott &amp; she looked much better but is still running a high Temp.  Seems very comfortable &amp; contented.
