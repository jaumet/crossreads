 seems a different class of men than we have be used to, they are very strict so I can see some fun with the breaking in of the mob. 
 27th Had us up early &amp; out marching the sand &amp; learning their foot drill, the hours are long here from 5 am till 8 pm.  we have lectures five times a week at 7 till 8 pm. 
 March 30th Went over the Engineers camp to look up Harry but found he had left two days before for annother camp so my luck is out as far as meeting him.
