<p>6th October (Friday)</p>
<p>Buried a horse at Pont de Neippe in morning.  Ten more shells thrown over into the town.</p>
<p>7th October (Saturday)</p>
<p>On incinerator all day.  8th Brigade put gas over but proved disastrous to our own side as also did the raid last night.  No more shells today from Fritz.</p>
<p>8th October (Sunday)</p>
<p>Quiet day &ndash; light rain.  No work and peace from Fritz.  Estaminets close</p>
