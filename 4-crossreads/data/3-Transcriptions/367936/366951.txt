<p>Tuesday 21 March 1916<br/>Drove Father into Mudgee to the Land board and then went up to Show ground.  We won, Fat whrs., Fat ewes, Fat lambs, and Corriedale ram &amp; ewe &amp; 2 Corriedale fleeces.  Drove out with Dick &amp; brought 2 draught horses.</p>
<p>Wednesday 22 March 1916<br/>Father in Mudgee at Show.  Dick and I mustered up horses in Long pdk. and took them to Wonga.<br/>After dinner nailed up cases of honey, shod Galina on front feet, then rode round looking for horses but did not find them.</p>
