<p>1917 Thursday 7 June</p>
<p>Transport duty, 7 a.m. Walk into B. after breakfast.  Everything fresh &amp; beautiful after the wash of last evening. <br/>Two trips in &amp; out before dinner. One long trip 4 miles out of B to Mont something for kerosene. Pretty scenery. Late for dinner, cold. <br/>Away again first to Wim - &amp; then to B &ndash; again. fill up with clothing etc. finish about 5.30 after heavy day. Mac &amp; self out on to cliffs for brief stroll.  <br/>Letters from Frank M &amp; Tony.</p>
