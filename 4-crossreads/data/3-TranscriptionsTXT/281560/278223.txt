 8/3/16. Went onto musketry today all the old stunts we did at Liverpool, except that they have a few pet flourishes here. Went down to Abassia and found Sep's unit. Yarned with him a while, inspected his kit, camel, etc. then we went and had a beano at a dinkum eat-up joint in Heliopolis; struck Arch McIlveen there. 9/3/16. Woke up this morning to find that the company had been awarded 7 days pack drill! It appears that 3 chaps had a row with a Military Policeman; and the men got into our hut and he could not find them. Then he ran to the adjutant, with a cock and bull story, and he awarded us this punishment for something we know nothing of
