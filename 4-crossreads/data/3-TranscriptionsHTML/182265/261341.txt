<p>I met Lieut Goninon of 15th. who is convalescing after being wounded, in Bristol. I had met him previously at 4th Divisional School at La Chaus&eacute;e in France. After booking my room at the Royal Hotel, College Green, Goninon &amp; myself went to the Zoo to the UK Commercial Travellers&#39; Sports. The Sports were simply like Sunday School Picnic sports, there were dozens of side shows &amp; a fine merry-go-round, all of which took pennies, twopences etc, tickets being pressed upon us by dozens of girls who would not be denied. The proceeds were for War Invalids.</p>
<p>Leaving here Goninon &amp; I then taxied to the Centre, had afternoon tea - Raspberries &amp; cream, cake &amp; bread &amp; butter &amp; a pot of tea for 1/- each - really reasonable. Then we went to the Pictures for an hour, &amp; afterwards had dinner at St Stephen&#39;s Hotel for 4/9 each.
