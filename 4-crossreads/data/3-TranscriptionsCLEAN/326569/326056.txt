  
 Had tea at Y.M.C.A. Kings Cross & took bus to Tottenham Court Road & booked bed at Y.M.C.A. there.  Then walked about streets till bedtime. 
 Sunday, 20/5/17 Up at 7.45 a.m. & after breakfast walked to Trafalgar Sq. via Charing Cross Rd. & met Uncle Charlie at 10 a.m.  Went via Whitehall to Buckingham Palace & on way saw guard changed at Horse Guards & the guard changed at the Palace.  While there saw some U.S.A. officers in khaki.  Saw Victoria monument & went by St. James Palace to Pall Mall & along it to Trafalgar Square
