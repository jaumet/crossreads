
Tuesday 25 June 1918Total Hours flown,Over lines - 305 h. 17 m.Tests, etc. - 11 h. 35 m.Total - 316 h. 52 m.
Wednesday 26 June 1918H.A.P.  Pilot Capt. Addison.  In air 2 h. 50 m., no excitement.Murphy & Farquhar scrap a Hun over aerodrome.  Hun escaped but was on the point of landing.  Farquhar killed.  A very fine chap and a good sport.
