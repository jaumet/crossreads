<p>Friday 28th July</p>
<p>Capt. Earle Page of Grafton came here &amp; took charge this morning.<br/>He is a good sort &amp; had quite a long chat with us.<br/>He has gone to Alexandria on a few hours leave so Dr. Van Epen has not left us altogether yet.<br/>Today we had a big riot in the Camp.<br/>All the 1st Division, 14th T.B. &amp; some of ours &amp; A.M.C. details are leaving &amp; there was a lot of pay about.
