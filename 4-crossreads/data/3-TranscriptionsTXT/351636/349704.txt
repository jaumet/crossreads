 Centre for soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers &ndash; 9.3.16 
 Dear Mr. Layton Could you assist Mrs. Duck until her military payments will be settled her husband discharged &amp; now recruiting &ndash; She has struggled along always until now &amp; will be alright when payments are sent from Melbourne, but in difficulties until then 
 Yours respectfully E. H. Bennie
