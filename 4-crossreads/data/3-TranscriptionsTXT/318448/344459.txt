27:7:18Arrived at our old camp at Solomons Ponds after an exhausting &amp; inexcuseably  long trek of 16 hours. Beautiful climate here now. Perfect. I had a bath this morning. &ndash; not that a bath in itself is so unusual as to call for comment, far from it &ndash; but all of a sudden when bathing one of those fair damsels that infest the camp came round the end of the tent &amp; found me clothed in soapsuds &amp; chastity.
