Len tells me this morning that his groin is getting sore again that he is going to see the Dr. in a couple of days.
26th. Last night was a stormy one the wind howled raged like fury now again heavy storms of rain would come down, but luckily we are camped on sandy ground that saved us from being flooded out though we had gutters dug round our tent. Heavy firing could be heard yesterday evening I think it was Verdun way for it was East from here.
