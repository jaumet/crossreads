<p>Reached Paris. Marched to the Caserne Pepeniere Barracks. Crowds of people lined the streets.<br/>July 14th<br/>Left barracks at 7 AM and marched to the Esplanade Des Invalides where all the allied troops were drawn up. From here the procession began headed by the Belgians and ended at the Place de La Republique. We got back to barracks about 1 PM. Leave was granted from 2.30 PM to 8 PM. An English lady showed several of us a few of the fine sights of Paris. Great British advance on the River Somme. Weighed 11s 7</p>
<p>July 15th</p>
<p>Leave granted to all hands. I had a good stroll round Paris Streets. British still advancing.</p>
