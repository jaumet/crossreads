<p>October, 1915<br/>Thursday 21<br/>A beautiful calm morning the sea cobalt blue, &amp; not a ripple &ndash; We give the men boat drill each officer having charge of a boat.<br/>In the Gymnasium there are electrically propelled riding Saddles, for exercising the bloated hypochondriac on his voyage for health. I am luxuriating in the hot bath s etc:- knowing what is ahead.<br/>Will give these notes to Steward to post when the boat returns to Alexandria as no one knows how the Mails will be delivered in the future.</p>
