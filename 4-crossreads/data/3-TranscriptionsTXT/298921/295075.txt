
April, 1915Monday 12Received papers from Ferrar & have just read that Heliopolis is known in the Bible as "ON" see Genesis Xll 45. re Pharoah & Joseph.Many troops have left us. Papers from Joe. Grazier Pastoral Times -Sorry drought still so bad note HLA may buy property near Hay - I expect I shall be back some day & might be glad of a job if he wants a man.
