<p>In order to celebrate Anzac Day etc, we C. Section brought out a paper, with the title of "The Somme Chat" a real live paper, it consisted of some very fine Articles &amp; puns on Officers &amp; men &amp; things in general. We expect other Sections to come out with one now &amp; hit us off.</p>
<p>Thursday  26/4/17</p>
<p>Very nice day &ndash; Drilling all day &ndash; Good war news coming thro to us &ndash; some 80 Aeroplanes have been brought down this week &ndash; Very heavy bombardments on our front &ndash; Our Boy's 4th Division got as far as the Hindenburg line, but were driven back not being strong enough. Several pitched Battles in the open.</p>
<p>Friday  27/4/17</p>
<p>Very nice day &ndash; Section went for a route march to Meaulte. Good news still comes in from our front, of more Aeroplanes &amp; Prisoners.</p>
<p>Saturday  28/4/17</p>
<p>Very nice day. On Picquet &ndash; Complemented on the way we turned out. General Hough gave us a surprise visit. Wrote to Mrs O'Kelly, M Single &amp; Eileen. Big mail in but never got any.</p>
