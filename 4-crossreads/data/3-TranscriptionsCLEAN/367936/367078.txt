  
 Thursday 30 November 1916 Washed clothes and self in morning. On telephone at out post at night. Exciting aeroplane chase but Taube got away. 
 Friday 1 December 1916 H. Jacob & I practiced signalling in morning. Went out with M.G's range finding with mychrometer also box & shroud which Germans left behind at Romani & is worth [indecipherable].
