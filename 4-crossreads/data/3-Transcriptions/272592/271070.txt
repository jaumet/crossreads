<p>hate for you have to have everything so spick &amp; span or else look out for trouble.</p>
<p>There are some large cemetries around this part &amp; they appear to be well looked after by the Belgian's or our authorities I don't know which</p>
<p>As I write this the Germans are shelling 2 of our aeroplanes for all they are worth but they don't appear to take much notice of the shells that are bursting like white wooly fleeces all round them.</p>
<p>All vegetation especially trees &amp; hedges show the effects of the gas upon them, a lot are dead &amp; the majority are very seedy looking on it.</p>
