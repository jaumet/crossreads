<p>11.  as good a time as we can.<br/>Best of Love to You all<br/>From Harry<br/>Billy was over last Saturday but I was out.  Ellis saw Him.  Oh &amp; Reg. Keirle was over for a couple of Hours on Duty.  He is well &amp; looks fine.  That was yesterday.  We are just as busy as ever but we are old</p>
