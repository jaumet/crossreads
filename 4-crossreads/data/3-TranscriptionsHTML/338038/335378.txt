<p>I don't know whether I told you in my last that I saw Lt Col Lucas in London. He is going back to Aust for 6 months &amp; promised me he would telephone you first opportunity &amp; try &amp; make an appointment for a yarn with Father &ndash;</p>
<p>Saw Billy Watson yesteday &amp; all the H.Q. of the 45th Batn except Varley who is away at a school. Billy is in the same boat as I am about letters. He has not had any from home since April</p>
