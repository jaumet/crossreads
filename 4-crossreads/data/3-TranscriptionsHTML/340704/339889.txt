<p>December 1917<br/>Tuesday 25<br/>Xmas day  wet and cold<br/>Had good Xmas dinner. Only on e in our troop to have a fire.<br/>These olive trees burn well. Has the only wood here &ndash; so most have fires.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26<br/>Fine day in patches. Dried some clothes.<br/>Moved out at 8 pm to attack at daybreak. Awful journey rough. Very cold on outpost at night.</p>
