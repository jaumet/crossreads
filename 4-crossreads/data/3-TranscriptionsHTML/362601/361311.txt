<p>12 December, Friday</p>
<p>We see Motor Bike &amp; pay deposit. I eventually buy it on the 15/12/19</p>
<p>13 December, Saturday</p>
<p>Catch 1.5 train &amp; arrive Wyong 3.35 but no one to meet. They turn up at 4.15 [?] We get Home before 5 &amp; tea. Allie comes over go to mouth for prawns .Just get out of Church before dusk. We have a nice outing.</p>
