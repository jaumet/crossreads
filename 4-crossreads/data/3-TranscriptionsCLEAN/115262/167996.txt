  
 Friday 17 August 1917. 
 On no duty & treatment again today. 
 Received marching orders for the firing line. All kits for base packed & on the wagon by 10 a.m. & taken away to Base Depot. 
 Went to 14 A.G.H. & said goodbye to Ted Holden & Tom de Gruchy. Met Ern Harris in Hosp. again 2nd time down from 3rd L.H.F.A. (In going through kit thought I had lost  your  some photos. Found them later). Went to bed at 9 am. & ready for TOMORROW.
