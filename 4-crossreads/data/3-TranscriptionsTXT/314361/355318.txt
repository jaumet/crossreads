 now. The company is now back at Heliopolis near where we where the first few days in Egypt. Did not go anywhere this afternoon, money short. have only three &amp; a half piastres. I think that we will be paid again Tuesday. Thursday. I received two pairs of socks from Louie, they are very nice Heliopolis Jan 9th. Sunday Today I &amp; two friends went through the dead city. Started from Abbassia &amp; went through to the Citadel Cairo. We had a very enjoyable day. Before coming back we went to the Sultans palace, it looks very well with the Guards standing out side. Thursday 13th 16 I have now been put on elementary musketry, everyone has to go through musketry before he can go to the front, it generally takes about four days.
