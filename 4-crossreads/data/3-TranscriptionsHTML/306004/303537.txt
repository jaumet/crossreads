<p>27.9.16<br/>told us were leaving Rom,ani for Kantara tomorrow morning &amp; gave us some good advice  Three 2nd L. Horsemen were then decorated for conspicuous service on the Peninsula, one Military Cross and 2 DCM's;<br/>We have been kept very busy today preparing to move   Chauvel said we were going to Kantara as our horses needed a spell; Camel transports have been busy all the afternoon taking equipment to <strike>the</strike> Railway head.</p>
<p>28.9.16<br/>Thurs 28th<br/>Reveille this morning at 3 oclock, fed our horses and saddled up, had breakfast &amp; filed off the lines at 4.30 &amp; formed up West of the Camp, stopped for breakfast.</p>
