  
 Sunday 22 October 1916 Waiting about  camp all day.  Heavy frost & very cold all day. 
 Monday 23 October 1916 Working about camp - heavy rain & very cold.  Terribly muddy. 
 Tuesday 24 October 1916 Marched to High Wood & carried sand bags forward.  4 men killed, 3 wounded. 
 Wednesday 25 October 1916 No. 2 & 4 took up camp near "High Wood".  1 man killed No. 3.  Heavy shelling. 
 Thursday 26 October 1916 Heavy shelling both sides.  Sapr. Rickard killed.  No. 2 pushing stores forward.  Very cold & wet.  Things going well. 
 Friday 27 October 1916 Nos. 1 & 2 work & pushing stores forward.  Very wet, cold & muddy.  Working at high pressure.  No. 1 & 4 out at trenches.  Expect to attack shortly. 
 Saturday 28 October 1916 Putting stores up to front line, also other fatigues.  No. 1 & 4 at Trenches. 
 Sunday 29 October 1916 No. 2 proceed to trenches & took over from No. 1 & 4.  Working in trenches during afternoon & all night - very muddy & wet. 
 Monday 30 October 1916 Trenches again during afternoon & night.  Heavy rain, trenches waist deep in mud & water.  Men very exhausted. 
 Tuesday 31 October 1916 Still very wet & muddy.  Cleared up at night.  Digging trenches with large Infantry party during night.  Feel very exhausted. 
 Wednesday 1 November 1916 Relieved by No. 3 Sec. & proceeded back to H.Q.  Expecting to attack any time.  Still wet & muddy.  Large waste of men. 
 Thursday 2 November 1916 Resting today.  Very tired after strenuous times in trenches.  Still wet & muddy. 
 Friday 3 November 1916 Nos. 1 & 2 Sec. were out at "Crest Trench" forming road.  Usual bombardment going on.  Still showery & muddy. 
 Saturday 4 November 1916 Excepting few fatigues, all resting.  Expect to attack in front Bapaume tomorrow.  Tommies attack as "Left".
