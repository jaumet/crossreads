<p>Monday 18th March<br/>A day off to recuperate</p>
<p>Tuesday 19th March<br/>The Platoon or what is left of it went on Bath Paraide. Had an exelent bath &amp; a good change. Rained nearly all day, the wettest I have had in this country</p>
