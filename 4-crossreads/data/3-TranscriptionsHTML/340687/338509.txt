<p>July, 1917. Tuesday 24<br/>Sultry. Gas lecture by N.C.O. at 8.45 and worked on Froissy ground till 11.15. 5th div came at 2. only teams. 2. Soccer won 3-2. 2nd Aust won 10-12 to 6-6. 3rd Rugby won 29 pts to nil. Late home to tea. bed early &amp; read.</p>
