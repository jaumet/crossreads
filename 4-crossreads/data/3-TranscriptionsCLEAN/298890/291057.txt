  
 19 Thursday Dark foggy day.  Eye tiresome bathed it in 
 20. Friday Met Ethel & Claude at 7.30 & saw 'Man from Toronto' at Duke of York's  Tully & Iris Hoey, principals.  Dull dark day.  Fred called up about 6 & had a yarn.  Mrs. Aldridge & I had lunch at Minerva 
 21. Saturday Worked in office till 3.  & got home & wrote Xmas notes & got my few parcels ready.  Dull day.
