 Jan 27  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;Raining all the morning. we have our second daily issue of lime juice. 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 28  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;I was chased off deck by the rain at 3.A.M. Jack &amp; Fred took on mess orderly duties for the week - The sea is like a lake in smoothness _ 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 29  th  &nbsp;&nbsp;We have been travelling at half speed for the past three days on account of submarines. We sighted land first thing&nbsp; ^  this morning &nbsp;&amp;&nbsp; got &nbsp;went into &quot;Freetown&quot; Sierra Leone, which was crowded with all kinds of shipping, including cruisers also a French warship - we bought fruit &amp; cocoanuts from the natives. Bananas 1/- per doz Paw-Paws 3 d &nbsp;each Oranges 6 d &nbsp;per doz &amp; Cocoanuts 3 d &nbsp;each Nobody was allowed ashore as we had up the yellow flag for quarantine. Very warm day - We collected the fruit by letting down the money in a bucket on a rope &amp; hauling the fruit up. from the natives in their little canoes alongside. we each had to wait our turn. for the buckets
