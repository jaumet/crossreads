<p>Australian Red Cross<br/>Bulford<br/>8/9/18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>I am answering your letter of 30/6/18, although we have received one from Dad Dated 14/7/18.  Epidemics &amp; the like don't worry us, Mum.  We are inoculated &amp; as soon as these things come along all sorts of preventative measures are taken that the diseases do not spread.  It is part of my job to see that they don't.  I got your Birthday greetings on 4/9/18.  I do not know when you'll get yours.  I must wish you many happy returns even now for this is your Day.  I am almost</p>
