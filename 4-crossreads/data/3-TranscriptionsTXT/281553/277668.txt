
14th OrderOrders issued midday to pack & brand valises & blankets.  Weather fair.
15th Wed.Alloted to specially divided & equal strength companies;  no movement yet made.  Silent stunt from 2 to 6 a.m.
16th ThursdayVery cold.  Day quiet, nothing doing.  Beautiful sunshine and much milder.  Silent 1-4 a.m.
17th FridayDay quiet & beautifully bright.  Orders restricted re evacuation.  Mines connected with wires to No. 2 tunnels.  Tea the only cooked rations for evening meal.  Big fire at Anzac from midnight.  Weather Cloudy & Foggy.
