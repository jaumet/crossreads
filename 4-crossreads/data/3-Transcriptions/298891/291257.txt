<p>1917 June</p>
<p>23 Saturday</p>
<p>Bought suitcase on way to meet Miss Hodge at Minerva for lunch. Then she took me to "Inside the Lives" &amp; we went to call on Mrs Holman at Ruben's Hotel. Not in so we came back to Minerva to pick up things &amp; home tired.</p>
