<p>1915<br/>May. 15 Sat.<br/>Am living very comfortably for active service at present:- share a very comfortable room with Sgt. Clerk; nice bed with mosquito nets, sheets, etc. &amp; hot baths, up to date lavatories; occasional plum puddings purloined from doubtful sources; puddings, tea, cocoa etc. from the Arab waiters.<br/>Lady Maxwell (wife of General Officer Commanding the Forces in Egypt) paid a visit during the afternoon. The patients gave her a great welcome &amp; we finished up with a big group photo. in front of Mena House.<br/>Later in the afternoon received orders to proceed to Gezireh on the morrow.</p>
