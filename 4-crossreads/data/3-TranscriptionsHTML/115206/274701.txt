<p>May 1918<br/>Saturday 11</p>
<p>Bussy-la-Daours<br/>Paid a visit to Eglise with bob.<br/>During afternoon acted as a guide to an American Sergeant and rode across to Glisy with him.<br/>Weather wet. On arrival home found all the boys including Kenn Gunn, and Albie Garford, the former with my mail.<br/>One parcel from Eva one from Roy and one from Toots. Also a few letters.</p>
