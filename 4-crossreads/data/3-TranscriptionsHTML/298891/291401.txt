<p>14 Wednesday</p>
<p>Kaimacktchalan still clear &ndash; came up dull after lunch.  Leaves coming down in dead earnest now.  Nothing to report.  Dick, Saunders, Davis &amp; I went into Ostrovo to dine with some Serbs and Bogaljub &amp; Milan brought the camion for us at 9.15.  Dick quite drunk.<br/>Menu:  Slaw, tinned fish, sheeps' tongues &amp; paprikas;  pungent soup;  cabbage &amp; meat, chicken &amp; fried pot.;  slaw &amp; pastry;  Turkish coffee;  pankakes, Malta &amp; Turkish coffee wine.</p>
