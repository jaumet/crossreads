 I Egypt land of sin and sunsets, where my footsteps pause a while. Where the dusty sore - eyed Arab Barters fruit with native guile. Half the world we've crossed to reach you. Hearts were light and hopes were high Thinking that we came for fighting But we're only "standing by 
 II May be when our limbs are feeble And our heads are bending low We shall tell our children's children Of the days of long ago How, with patriotic fervour Men came in from far away Answering the call of freedom This includes the "standing by"
