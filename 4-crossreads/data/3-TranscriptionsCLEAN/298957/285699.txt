Lying down most of the day in the big Hospital tent where we have been sleeping for the past few days. 30 men and our Headquarters leaving tonight for France by the "Tunician". We follow them in a couple of days time on the "Transylvania". The Divisional Headquarters Staff and 3000 men will be leaving per this boat. Red Cross "Brassards" issued tonight and are to be sewn on the left arm sleeve.
