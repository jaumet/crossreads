<p>we arose at 6. am..passed an Island at 9am supposed to be Point Hells; a few houses on it &amp; wireless station, light house. we saw a shark that looked quite 20 ft long.<br/>The sea is lovely &amp; calm here. We had to fall in at 10am. to be inspected by our OC passed a chalk Island with a lovely looking lighthouse upon it.<br/>Two small steamers passed us at this point .<br/>A Boxing contest passed the evening away &amp; we gave a few good coo-ees to a passing ship.</p>
<p>27/<br/>Nice day. Three boats passed.</p>
