<p>3.  meet Her.  I am surprised &amp; glad to hear that the Lucas Girls are married.  You know what a shock we had once about one of them.  I Hope George worked His nut re the Dr's orderly Job.  It is the best in the Army.  Batman or Drs Orderly will always do for me.<br/>Give My regards to Nurse Lascelles &amp; also to Isa Webber.  Isa lives in Manly I think.  How is Jess Walker getting on &amp; also Her Husband?<br/>You are not exactly right about the money for Cyril.  When I first met Him, He was too independent to take it although I pressed Him hard.  He, like Myself, was rather shy about borrowing.  But I told Him not to want as I had plenty, &amp; to write immediately that He was broke.  Certainly I said Keep it.  We share until we are all finished.  Give to our Cobbers while we've got anything to give &amp; they are the same with us.  D.J's have not sent me any money this time   <strike>so far.</strike>  I am stoney, but I'll shake 'em up again.<br/>Well, Mum, I've to write to Dad &amp; George yet, so I'll have to wind up.  You forgot to mention Mrs Fremantle &amp; her Sister (who is an old maid &amp; lives with Her) in Your letter.  There is Dad, Mrs Fremantle, her sister Miss Salter, &amp; the two daughters, Elsie who is about Bertie Wrights age 31 or 32 I should guess, &amp; the younger daughter Constance who is about 30.  Don't mention their ages.  They don't know</p>
