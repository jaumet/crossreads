<p>WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 20, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Lopped in 4D in</p>
<p>morning then came back &amp; had</p>
<p>a yarn with Eric Cadell re Abbotslea</p>
<p>Round bottom end in afternoon</p>
<p>Tickle started poisoning but the</p>
<p>axle would not fit poison cart.</p>
<p>Jack Fitzpatrick came out and</p>
<p>started trapping.</p>
<p>Dad &amp; Jo &amp; Graham went into</p>
<p>Quirindi in little car.</p>
<p>The Beauly crowd came over at</p>
<p>night &amp; danced the floor in.</p>
