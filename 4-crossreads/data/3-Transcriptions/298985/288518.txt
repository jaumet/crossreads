<p>Thursday 1st Aug<br/>Loafed about the camp all day. Packed up &amp; sent our packs &amp; blankets away this evening Played cards untill about 1 AM &amp; then stretched out for a while but got no sleep.</p>
<p>Friday 2nd<br/>Turned out at 4 had breck right away &amp; then the usual bustle to fall in. Once you fall in it is the dead end. Apparently there is nothing more to be</p>
