<p>December, 1915</p>
<p>Thursday 2</p>
<p>earth &amp; explodes.</p>
<p>A sheet of flame goes up into the air, there is a deafening explosion and earth sandbags &amp; <strike>all</strike> huge lumps of shell fly in all directions.</p>
<p>The worst of these shells is that a man stands little chance of getting out alive except with the loss of a limb or a terrible wound.</p>
