<p>I still have two here, they do the clerical work.  Just now I am charging round the country all day &amp; enjoying it hugely after being cooped up in dug outs for a fortnight.  I come home tired and footsore &amp; hungry but am picking up wonderfully &amp; feel splendid.</p>
<p>I like walking better than riding especially this weather, its so gloriously fresh.  The very cold weather has finished thank God, it nearly killed us.  All the snow has vanished &amp; the ice.  We are having a little cold snap just now, a white frost &amp; very thin ice of a morning but nothing like what it was &amp; the sun has got some warmth in him.</p>
<p>Day before yesterday I was all round the batteries.  Very noisy.  Today I had a glorious walk to the new HQ position &amp; back.
