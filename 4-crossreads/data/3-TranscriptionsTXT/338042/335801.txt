 Friday Feb. 9th Final preparations &ndash; Bid Madame adieu, and in company with Jack Shaw, I entrain at Bailleul Station, loaded with personal &amp; theatrical goods &ndash; arrive Calais 2.30 p.m. but  fail  to board "packet" at 5.15 p.m. for Dover. Pass night in Calais. 
 Saturday. Feb.10th. Embark at 8.30 a.m. &ndash; a rough trip across the Channel. Arrive at Dover &amp; entrain for London &ndash; Arrive Victoria &amp; taxi to War Chest &ndash; thence to Court Theatre &amp; then back to War Chest &ndash; At night.
