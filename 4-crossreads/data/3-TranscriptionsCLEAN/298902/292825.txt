At night the Q.M. store celebrated the fact that 3 of them were returning to Australia & they made it willing.  Kept my end up & retired "Allthere" at midnight  Mj Wesley over during the night for a long yarn. 
 7th January '19  up feeling fairly bright & at 10 was on my way to Charleroi per car with C Section medical gear to hand over to medical stores also Mj Birch & two females who were getting a buckshee trip there.  Had two punctures on the road & arrived about 1.  Had a dinner of Beef (horse) & chips coffee & bread for 41/2 francs.  Then visited an Anatomy museum open to both sexes over 18.
