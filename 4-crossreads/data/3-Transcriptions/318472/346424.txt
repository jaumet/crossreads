<p>18 July 1918<br/>Thursday<br/>Just the same old Camp Routine The one absorbing topic is "Who will be on Carrier D.18. final Roll. Nasty wet weather.</p>
<p>1918 July 19<br/>Friday<br/>Left Westham this afternoon &amp; went to Monte Video Expect to be on D18 Carrier Supplementary Roll. Nasty wet weather Reported French &amp; American troops doing very well.</p>
