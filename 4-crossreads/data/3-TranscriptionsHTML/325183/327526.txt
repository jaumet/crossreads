<p>deserted. After banging on the door for a little while however, a beery eyed individual appeared to whom we appealed to have our luggage handed out. He shook his head and told us that the place did not open until 6 A.M. the following morning. Our position was a rather desperate one as we were anxious to quit Brussells for several reasons as soon as possible. We were determined to have our own kits out and shook the door and strongly stamped and swore and even threatened to take the fellows life all to no purpose though</p>
