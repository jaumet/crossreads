<p>A Rumour is running rife (if they do run like that &ndash; I forget)  concerning a stunt tonight at midnight However as Bernard is still undisposed it is unlikely that I shall go out.<br/>June 21st<br/>I was right, our Intelligence Bureau was correct even if its methods were somewhat devious. The  Stunters started at 3 oclock this morning, I, I am thankful to say, not among the number.<br/>Iron mongery in the Beef!!<br/>A certain hesitation  &amp; circumspection</p>
