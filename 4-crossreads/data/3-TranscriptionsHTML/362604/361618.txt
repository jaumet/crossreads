<p>Bulford<br/>24/3/18</p>
<p>Dear Dad,</p>
<p>I have three letters from you dated 13.1.18, 20.1.18 &amp; 27.1.18.  I was pleased to read of your having had a nice Holiday.  We are absolutely satisfied with that which you have done at Dee Why.  If you want to enlarge the place, we are also quite willing.  When we return the Paltry &amp;pound;200 or &amp;pound;300 will not be enough to set us up in business.  We intend to borrow &amp;pound;1,000 or &amp;pound;2,000 &amp; have a fair start.
