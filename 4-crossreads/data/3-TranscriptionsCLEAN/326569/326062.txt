 Tuesday, 29/5/17 Fine morning but rained after dinner so dismissed from parade.  After tea take stroll beyond Rail Stn. towards Poole. 
 Wednesday 30/5/17 Fine today - practising with gas helmets now.  After tea went to town & Y.M.C.A. for supper.  They are having boxing contests in the hut tonight.
