<p>225<br/>There was running races and tug-o-war. Some of the men also dressed up in finery parading round the ring. There was an exhibit of Charlie Chaplin as well.</p>
<p>We spent a happy afternoon together, marching back through Lavi&eacute;ville a place where the 1st Battn was quartered.</p>
<p>We attended moral lectures at another little village where the building was a huge place used at ordinary times for billeting the troops. The whole village was built in a hollow.</p>
<p>One day we went to a specially prepared range, fitted out with targets at different ranges representing enemy masses &amp;c.</p>
