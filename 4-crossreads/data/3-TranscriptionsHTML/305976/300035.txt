<p>Friday Aug 23<br/>The weather is exceptionally hot &amp; one feels fit for nothing here. Things are quiet but it is rumoured an attack is expected at any time.<br/>Saturday Aug 24<br/>Jacko shelling to day<br/>Everything ready for expected attack.<br/>Sunday Aug 25<br/>Went out in Sand Cart to 2ND Reg. to day. We had great difficulty finding them owing to number of attacks.<br/>Monday Aug 26<br/>Usual routine to day. Weather still very hot</p>
