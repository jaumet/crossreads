
5-12/16. Had a days leave in Amiens yesterday it was great. Left here about 8-30 am. in the Napier Car with Lt Hade & picked up two motor lorries at Ribenont in which we journeyed to the old city before mentioned. After briefing & loading the lorries with oil cake we parked them in a quiet street, & left orders to be ready to move at 5 pm. It was then about dinner time so we went in search of a grub house.
