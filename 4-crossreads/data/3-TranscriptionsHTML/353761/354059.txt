<p>(3)<br/>and always something to balance a seeming loss<br/>It is Autumn<br/>The world is a gallery of the seasons pictures<br/>And wonderful pictures they are too<br/>This part of the old globe has an advantage over our Australia in the definite change of its seasons.<br/>How delightfully unlike, are these four children of the year<br/>Time, after all, is a merciful old God.<br/>Always he blesses each year with the four beautiful seasons<br/>That they are so distinctly different seems wonderful.</p>
