
Small Christmas gifts from Australia given out during afternoon.
Wednesday 12/1/16Received further news re evacuation of Dardanelles; very successfully carried out with only slight losses.  Practically all Australian troops withdrawn from Gallipoli camping in vicinity of Tel-el-Kabir; all looking much the worse for wear.
Thursday 13/1/16Rained slightly early morning.  Very quiet day.
Friday 14/1/16Cold morning.  Received mail from home; first for nearly 3 weeks
