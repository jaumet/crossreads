<p>As I write this the boy's are kicking the football about &amp; enjoying themselves in the nice sun.<br/>You should see the old French people collect round our incinerator when we are moving they generally get some good articles for there is always a lot of  good stuff wasted when we are shifting about from place to place.<br/>Last night there was a fight between our aircraft &amp; the Germans I don't know how it ended up, but our fellows chased them till they were out of sight.<br/>I hear Captain Price is getting the D.S.O &amp; Lieut Moffatt, the M.C. I dont know what for something to do with the raid I suppose<br/>Left our parade ground this afternoon at 4'oclock &amp; marched to Bailleul, she is a nice little town, &amp; there are some solid buildings in her streets</p>
