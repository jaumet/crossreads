  
 Jan.  Thursday 11 1917 
 Rob. Ridg. & self get leave to Amiens. Up at daylight, walk to Ribemont 3 miles catch car on way. Get off at Lahoussye. cup cafe & biscuits. On to Querrieux . breakfast of eggs & cafe.  On to Amiens per motor trolly at 11 a.m. Board tram into centre of city raining & cold. Dinner, poke round, cathedral. Pastry tea, & other things. Miss train. Bob & the Banque Nationale.  Leave about 5 p m. tram to Barrier. No lorries, long wait in cold. about 30 gather. Rush for car. Talkative 2nd. Lieut. shikered. Home & dry 8.30
