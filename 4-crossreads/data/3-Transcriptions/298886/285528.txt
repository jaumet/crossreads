<p>15th MAY 1915 Gallipoli Peninsular</p>
<p>Everyone had a rest today C Squadron especially 3 days on Gallipoli and already had 30 men killed and more than that wounded From now onwards for about 17 weeks we took turn about in the Pope's Post trenches opposite Quinn's Post The 1st 2nd and 3rd Light Horse Regt held Pope's Post up till the beginning of August then we went into Quinn's post again and got cut up the second time.</p>
