 June 1918 Thursday 27 
 In Billets. Erronville Weather fine and clear. Had the usual daily routine with horse and bycycles also wire and phones , Drifted along to see my friend Suzanne on the Hellencount Road. Worked for awhile at the farm, had a beautiful spread of roast fowl peas and potatoes, also strawberries and cream. Fritz  bombed Abbeville.
