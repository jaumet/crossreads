<p>"Emden?"  "attention" &ndash; Steamer can do 9 &frac12; to 10 knots. "Emden &ndash; attention" "Whats the challenge to be"- "Lauterbach?" &ndash; "attention" "Challenge shall be Hans Caiser" (repeated).<br/>Markomania is now along side the steamer and the crew is taken on board. In the meantime I descent from the searchlight and am called from the bridge by the watch officer :- "Applicant!"  attention" &ndash; "prepare yourself to take charge of the next prize crew."</p>
<p>I provide myself with prize-papers and writing material, sidearm and pistols.I also put a few dollars in my pocket to buy cigarettes in the mess in case it should be a neutral steamer.</p>
<p>It was a Dutch steamer on the way from Rotterdam to Calcutta. She had no contraband on board with the help of my broken Dutch mixed with a little Low german, I carry out my orders and we understand each other quite well.</p>
