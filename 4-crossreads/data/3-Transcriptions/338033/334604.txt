<p>Victoria memorial now stands.<br/>I went in and had dinner at Lyons returning through the park to the Albert memorial, a large spire with industries depicted by carvings, round the base. Right Opposite, is the Albert hall, where all the great concerts are held. It is a circular hall, with rows of chairs right round and the platform in the centre at the bottom. I left there and went round where all the museums were, but time would only permit me to take a hurried</p>
