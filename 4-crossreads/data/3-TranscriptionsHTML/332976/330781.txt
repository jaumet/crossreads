<p>January 1917<br/>1st   Monday   We have had another free day to-day after the first parade, the same as yesterday.  I have caught another cold &amp; am not feeling too well.<br/>2nd   We started work again this morning but at lunch time we were told to get our gear packed <strike>to</strike> ready to change billets to make room for the 4th Division which is coming into Ribemont in place of the 5th Divn. which moved out yesterday en route to their new billets a few miles from Amiens.<br/>During the afternoon we changed over to new billets &amp; the 4th Div.
