  
 Saturday 6/10/17 Reorganised at Bellevaarde and went again to relieve Bas in same position. Sunday 7/10/17 Relief by 18th and moved back to Westhoek relief not complete until early on 8th . Letter from Doff. Monday 8/10/17 Again reorganised and reconnoitred new ground as support to 6th Bde, very muddy & got wet through. Tuesday 9/10/17 Stunt by 5th & 6th Brigades not altogether successful reinforced 23rd, 21st & 24th Bns. Relieved by 49th Bn. Wednesday 10/10/17 Spent day at Ypres Barracks very tired. Letter from Doff, moved by bus to Steenvoorde Area.
