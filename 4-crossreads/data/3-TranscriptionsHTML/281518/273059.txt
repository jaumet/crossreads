<p>11/9/16<br/>To Horseferry Road  and was there boarded and found fit for general service and report to Perham Downs to join a draft on Thursday Spent the afternoon at Wandsworth with wounded and returned to town late for dinner at Cock Tavern. Old fashioned waiter etc.</p>
