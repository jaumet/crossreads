 Wednesday 25/4/17- This being Anzac Day a half holiday was granted to the Railway Corps No 1 &amp; 3 Sections so it was decided to hold Sports on the Bordon Recreation Ground. So a large crowd made their way &amp; the Afternoon passed off pleasantly. The Sports held were fairly Interesting After Tea a Concert was held in the Wesley Hall by Australians &amp; there was some good talent.
