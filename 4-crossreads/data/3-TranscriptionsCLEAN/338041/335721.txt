  
 Australians still attacking & holding their gains. Gen Birdwood daily visits the firing line. Our chief is very pleased. 
 July 26th After a good day we depart from Vadencourt & proceed towards the line of battle (14 miles away). On our way we pass vast numbers of troops, munitions etc. We end our march & bivouac on the outskirts of Albert. 
 July 27th Shortly after rising we meet & converse with many of the "first Brigade" who left the trenches the previous night after doing such wonderful fighting. - I again meet Billy Hain & Roger Morgan of the 2nd Batt. A.M.C. - In the afternoon I part from Harry & Rog who are in the Stretcher Party for trenches - Good luck be with them. 
 July 28th Our tent division return to Vadencourt & take over from R.A.M.C. a D.R.S - Plenty of work. (Night Duty)
