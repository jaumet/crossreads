<p>[Reverse of the above post card]<br/>Master Frank Burrowes<br/>Franurra<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>N.S.W.<br/>Australia</p>
<p>26.5.18<br/>In with this are the two colours off my coat that I have been wearing one is for you if you would like it, perhaps Mum might sew it on your hat or something like that. Goodbye little boy until I send you the next card, lots of love from Dad.</p>
