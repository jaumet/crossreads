<p>Thursday 8 March 1917.</p>
<p>Still in camp.</p>
<p><u>Rumored</u> that S.S. Ballarat has been sunk and only 600 troops saved.</p>
<p>Lt Horse (interstate) march in off &quot;Itria (A53) to Seyr. [Seymour] Camp.</p>
<p>All day on the horses in the hills <u>for Dvrs</u>. Interesting but tiring work.</p>
<p>Light Horse Fd Amb bivouac being prepared by rest of unit.</p>
