<p>10 August 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Quiet on front.<br/>Started to rain this morning first for 3 weeks. Dust has been terrific. Go to Albert on service waggon for coal. Very misty. not much doing on front. Pass through Henencourt &amp; Millencourt</p>
<p>The King, Prince Wales, Generals Haig &amp; Birdwwod visit us. Nearly missed them, thro' having shave. Caught them up at Hospital  Good view.</p>
