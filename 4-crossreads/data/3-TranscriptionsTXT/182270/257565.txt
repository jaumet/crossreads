 WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 10, 1919. 
 Fine &amp; cold. Round Boundary &amp; 
 Mountain in morning and 
 Dick Tickle &amp; I shifted ewes &amp; 
 lambs from 4D to Boundary 
 in afternoon. 
 Tickle lopping in Top paddock 
 &amp; 4D in morning and went 
 down to the farm to try &amp; borrow 
 a dray in afternoon.
