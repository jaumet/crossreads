<p>Stayed in dug out all day relieved &amp; night by 53rd Batt. No casualities in A Coy. marched back to Bancourt arrived about two in morning dead tired<br/>29th Thursday<br/>Spent a miserable day &amp; Bancourt.<br/>30th<br/>Moved back to Bapaume to billets.<br/>31st<br/>Street fatigue had a bath<br/>Sunday April 1st<br/>Moved up to front line again and relieved the 53rd Batt.</p>
