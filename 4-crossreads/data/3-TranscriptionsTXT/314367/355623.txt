 HMAS Fantome Rabaul Dec 21 &ndash; 14 &ndash; 
 Dear girl The Matunga came in yesterday &amp; brought your note of Oct &ndash; You'd got Hordern's bills &ndash; Well I hope you like the things &amp; that the King Lawn mower now moves in stately fashion over our small lawns I hope you've got my back pay &amp; my present pay &ndash; Stir up Rawdon to see the N B in Melbourne if you don't &ndash; Love to the children &ndash; Let them order their young lives &ndash; We've set their feet on the road &amp; they must go on &ndash; no slackness &ndash; Early bed, early rise &ndash; To each his or her duty &amp; then play &amp; pleasure &ndash; I think of you all daily but so far I've had no means of communicating &ndash; You cant send messages from Rabaul &ndash; we came straight - even by wireless &ndash; Against orders &ndash; I doubt if you'll get this before the 2nd week in January &ndash; Our trip up to date sees us all right but I am  very  dissatisfied with the food problem &ndash; Ten days out &ndash; We were minus fresh meat, bread, rice, potatoes &ndash; no water save distilled &ndash; The whole arrangements are exceedingly bad &ndash; We have no cold storage, no refrigerator &ndash; At Rabaul we got some ice &amp; a little frozen meat &ndash; I've condemned the frozen!!!
