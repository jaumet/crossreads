 Visited Dudley Road Section Hospital &amp; saw Reg &amp; his mate Moroney. They were in the same tent in Kiama, came together, in action together &amp; both now have right wrist severely wounded. One holds his meat down with a fork while the other cuts, one pulls one side of a knot in a bootlace, while the other pulls other side.&nbsp;
