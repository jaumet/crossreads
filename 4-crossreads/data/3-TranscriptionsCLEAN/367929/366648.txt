  
 July 1915 4 Sun Walked with Capt Williams and Lane out to a Greek village 21/2 m. out. Villages and houses poor. Lunched on hopbeer sardines and bread. At 3.30 went on b Arragon and later on to Prince Abbas with 4 others for Anzac 
 5 Mon At 11am getting in to Lock Esk from Pr. Abbas an enemy submarine appeared about 5o yds off and the two boats parted hurriedly - Major Rutledge having Knox's box - went to Imbros and lost it there - I went to [indecipherable] till 4.30 when got on Lock Esk again - ashore 11.30 pm and thence to bivouac.
