<p>3rd A.C.C.S.<br/>A.I.H.<br/>France<br/>18.4.17</p>
<p>My dearest Mother   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>Have a few minutes so trying to make the most of it. Sorry I cannot even send P.C. to my friends. We are not near anywhere to but cards   &amp;  have had very little time to write, Fortunately on Monday-night, two more sisters arrived, so we are 14 now   &amp;  really could do with heaps more. The work here is very heavy   &amp;  trying but I'm glad that I've been able to do it so long. We get all</p>
