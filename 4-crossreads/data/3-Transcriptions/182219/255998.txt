<p>Dec 23 1917 Xmas Eve</p>
<p>Started on our journey once again had a very unpleasant ride last night on the goods as we were compelled to camp under a number of water carts &amp; the weather blew up very cold &amp; windy, we had alternate snatches of sleep &amp; nightmares owing to the innumerable bumpings buffeting &amp; stoppages. We finally succeeded in reaching Dier Sienade about 2 am &amp; were bumped into a siding to await the pleasure of the unloading gangs, this enabled us to get a little sleep but it was bitterly cold</p>
