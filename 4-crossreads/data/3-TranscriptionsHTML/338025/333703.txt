<p>Saturday 2nd June 1917<br/>Aveluy nr. Bouzincourt<br/>Reveille 5.30am.<br/>First parade 6am and water mules. Breakfast 7 till 9am then cleaning harness and water mules. Dinner 12 till 2pm. At 2pm we marched to the baths at Bouzincourt, where we had a good hot bath and a change of underclothing.</p>
<p>Sunday 3rd<br/>Rev. 5am<br/>2nd Div. church parade at 8.30am and inspection by General Johnson.<br/>Dinner 12 till 3.30pm</p>
