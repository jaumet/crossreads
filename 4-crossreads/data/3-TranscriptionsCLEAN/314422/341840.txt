After viewing the stables I went by electric tram on the Metropolition line to Euston had tea and caught the 7.10 train to Holyhead travelling via Crewe. After some delay at the pier I was put on board the mail steamer for Kingston. We left Holyhead about 2.30 and arrived at Kingston about 7.30. Caught a train for Dublin a journey of about 6 miles.
