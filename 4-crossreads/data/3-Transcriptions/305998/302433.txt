<p>Received three letters today no more letters accepted for outward post.</p>
<p>13/6/16 Kit inspections by the dozens &amp; getting ready for France.</p>
<p>14/  Packed most of the harness except the gun teams harness which will be used to pull the guns &amp; waggon to the station.</p>
<p>15/ Drawing guns etc to station, came out in Brigade orders gazetted as Driver so a extra 1/- a day now on. Put on B.H.L. gaurd &amp; marched prisoners down to train &amp; took them on boat at Alex.</p>
<p>17/ Sailed from Alex. at 8 am, we saw four</p>
