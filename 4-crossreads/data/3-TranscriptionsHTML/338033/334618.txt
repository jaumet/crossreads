<p>Norman the conqueror.<br/>After seeing all we desired we returned with our friends for tea, taking them to the theatre a little later. It was really a vaudeville show still we enjoyed ourselves very well. On see the girls home we saw to it that we obtained supper to eat on the way home. As we were about to start home one English dense fogs decended, and although we had an experienced driver, we were lost for a while.
