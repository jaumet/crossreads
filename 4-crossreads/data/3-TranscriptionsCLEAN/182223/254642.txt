Thursday 4 March 1915About hospital all day.Very seedy.  
Made a mistake in a day JBN 6-3-15  9 a.m.
About hospital all day Letter from Joseph. Posted in Sydney on 15-1-15 dated 15-1-15. Answered at once.Major Gray at Sakkara.  
[The following two sentences crossed through:]Not able to Eat.Interior upset, gaseous, making me miserable  
Friday 5 March 1915Made a mistake in a day JBN 6-3-15  9. am
Aet my first good breakfast for two weeks, porridge & sausages (vinegar & mustard) bread, tea.Posted letters to Girls & Mollie.Colonel away during the morning.
[Handwritten date:] 6. Saturday (65-300) [March 1915]Morning, as others here "perfect in atmospheric conditions.Writing & reading & hospital work all day
Sunday 7 March 1915Wrote Mrs Reardon & Jessie Kennedy.Not a Mass today, had to attend a parade at 9 am.Reading & writing all day
