<p>Sunday 16 January 1916<br/>Church Parade in forenoon.  A little light fatigues otherwise nothing doing.  Things going as usual.</p>
<p>Monday 17 January 1916<br/>Working in C.R.E. yards &amp; on water troughs on Canal.  Nothing of importance to report.</p>
<p>Tuesday 18 January 1916<br/>Working C.R.E. yard.  Large numbers troops still going down Canal.  Large number Camels.</p>
<p>Wednesday 19 January 1916<br/>Working in R.E. yard.  Nothing fresh of importance going.  Large number troops passing through each day.</p>
<p>Thursday 20 January 1916<br/>Working R.E. yard.  Usual Company routine.  Transports &amp; troops passing through daily.</p>
<p>Friday 21 January 1916<br/>Still working in R.E. yard.  Beautiful weather.  Nothing fresh going.  Troops still passing through.</p>
<p>Saturday 22 January 1916<br/>Exempt duty today sore feet.  Promoted L.Cp. this morning.</p>
<p>Sunday 23 January 1916<br/>Exempt duty.  Harry came to see me today.  Nothing fresh going.  Attended Divine Service in Y.M.C.A. at night.</p>
<p>Monday 24 January 1916<br/>Exempt duty on account of having sore feet.  No. 2 Coy. left Camp for presumably the Canal.  Nothing else going of importance.</p>
<p>Tuesday 25 January 1916<br/>Cpl. of the Guard today.  Very heavy wind, a little rain &amp; very cold.  Nothing of importance going.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26 January 1916<br/>Had easy day after coming off Guard.  R.E. Engrs. occupied 2nd Coy's old lines.  Nothing importance.</p>
<p>Thursday 27 January 1916<br/>Shifted from T.E.K. to * Advance party.  Lft. Keast, Cpl. Dobbie, Cpl. Faine, Cpl. Murray.  Was in charge baggage party.  (Serapeum *)</p>
<p>Friday 28 January 1916<br/>Had lazy time today.  Nothing doing.  A lot of work going on here preparing for supposed attack coming.  Caught in very heavy rain yesterday.  Showers since.</p>
<p>Saturday 29 January 1916<br/>Took charge "Pontoon" bridge today.  Much stores &amp; traffic crossing Canal.</p>
