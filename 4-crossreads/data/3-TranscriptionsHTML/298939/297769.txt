<p>Wool where I had tea walked back<br/>It was hot walking there but very cool coming back for a chilly wind sprang up.  Accomplished quite a record in walking back in 50 minutes &ndash; without a rest &amp; an average of a mile for ten minutes.</p>
<p>Sunday 6th May<br/>Brigade church parade in the morning attended by the General.<br/>Slept during the afternoon &amp; went to the pictures with Alex. Nunan in the evening<br/>He told me various</p>
