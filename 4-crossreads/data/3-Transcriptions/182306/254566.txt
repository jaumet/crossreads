
<p>Feb. 16th Sat</p>
<p>Entered Colombo about 6 am. A very pretty place. Got leave about 11 oclock and wnt ashore in lighters. And had a good ride all round town in a rikshaw and went out to the Cinamon Gardens and had a good look at the native quarters which are very funny The little black kiddies are playing about in the mud with nothing on. We made the best of our only</p>
