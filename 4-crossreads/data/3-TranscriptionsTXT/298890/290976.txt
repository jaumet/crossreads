
10. SundayIn House. Audrey came up from down stairs & Miss Smith for dinner. Read Miss H. "By Far Kaimacktchalan" at night.
11. MondayWent to meet Miss Hodge at Minerva & helped her buy a hat at Maxim. Almond trees almost out.
12. TuesdayCame back to Miss Smith. Nell came at night.
13. WednesdayMiss S. & I went to Suffrage celebration in Queen's Hall Fawcett in chair. Haydon  Henderson & Zylton  speakers
