<p>iron ones off some agricultural implement about 18 inches high tyres 1&frac12;  in wide. We unloaded at Rosile &amp; had a Mule to draw each Tank to the front line about 3 Kilos One Mule bolted &amp; I believe broke his tank another lost a wheel &amp; had to be left  I was with No 1 &amp; we got through allright  There were 6 all told. Don't know how the others got on. Pulled ours up past our outposts &amp; came back to a bit of a trench to wait for our barrage. Rain started about 11 &amp; we were in it untill morning.
