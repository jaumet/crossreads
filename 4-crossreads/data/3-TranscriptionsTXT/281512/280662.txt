His Spotting balloons could be very clearly observed &amp; doubtless his remarkable precision was due to their observation. The trees along the Menin Road are avenues of shot away stumps &amp; the surrounding lands, ploughed up with Shells like a sieve. The stench is frightful, &amp; even the old stagers dodge this charnel like thoroughfare. I saw the boch put out of action one of our batteries, &amp; explode the ammunition dump by a direct hit; I had to seek shelter in an adjacent dugout owing to his barraging the road, shells dropping along it in a long trail like succession.
