<p>Thursday 18th Oct.<br/>Last night rain set in &amp; fell steadily all night making everything awfully muddy This morning was bright &amp; clear I went to see the 35th Bn They have been badly cut up They went "over the Top" reached their objective but had to come back. Couldn't hold it, &amp; suffered Terribly. Poor old Cadell, Amphlett were killed Arnie I am told was wounded on the 12th. All the 9th Bde. suffered terribly</p>
<p>Friday 19th Oct.<br/>Nothing of importance today. The usual bombing raid was over tonight but we escaped.</p>
