<p>QM fatigue Sadlier gave me a fine tot of rum on coming off &amp; I got Kenyons rag out real well.</p>
<p>Saw Hudson in the morning on way back Slept all morn with Butch in a dug out at hill base</p>
<p>A silly fatigue at noon which I passively resisted Cleaning the gully of tins. Into the trenches again at 4 pm. Self firing line at old loop hole same place I had near shave at former night. Letters from Tassy KMW &amp; KW</p>
