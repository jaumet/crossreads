<p>April 1918<br/>Friday 5<br/>Mid to 8 am. On Road   ---houssoye? <br/>Arrived at Billet at 7.30 am after all night on the road. &ndash; very rough trip too. Rained throughout the night fairly heavily.<br/>Watered horses at river. A few shells  - high bursts of shrapnel over, Albert Amiens road.<br/>Seager and Piper returned from Doullens.<br/>Boys fairly bright under circumstances. <br/>.Awfully sorry I had to miss the company of Adianne.</p>
