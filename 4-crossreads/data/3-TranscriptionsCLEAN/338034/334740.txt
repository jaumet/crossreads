I think we are waiting for fresh Infantry Divisions to arrive. The Turkish fortifications around the hills near Gaza are apparently of great strength & will take a great deal of winning. We certainly have great superiority in artillery here, & warships lying off the coast assist in the bombardments. We have great stacks of gas shells here, & lately we have all been given gas helmets, so possibly we shall employ gas to eject the enemy if it cannot be done otherwise. They
