<p>Monday 20 November 1916<br/>On helio look out station from 10 till 2 p.m.  Had good practice with helio also flag &amp; buzzer.<br/>Heard that Mr. G. Edwards has died from absysis on the ear<br/>Tuesday 21 November 1916<br/>Wrote to Mother (25).<br/>Practiced signalling most of day.</p>
