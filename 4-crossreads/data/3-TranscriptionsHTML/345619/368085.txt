<p>Dearest Ruth<br/>Here I am nearly back to Australia again, and hope soon to be able to tell my nice family all about my holiday. I am only sending you one card I got at Colombo, but am bringing home more<br/>Lots of love &amp; kisses from Dad.</p>
<p>H.M.T. Takada 31-1-19</p>
