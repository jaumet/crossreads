<p>at both institutions altogether "Biljim" [average digger] of moderate means had good quarters to put up at and if he lost his bearings in any part of the metropolis the policeman was his cheerful guiding star and friend.<br/>The people of London are kind &amp; courteous &amp; the Aussies are quite at home with them at short notice. The non-shouting regulation in hotels was somewhat embarrassing to the natural propensity of Australians. In a number of the back streets it was not observed very strictly but on the main thoroughfares a man had to drink on his lonesome or distribute the coins to the "cobbers" he was treating.
