<p>Le Bizet<br/>Anti-aircraft duty were not sufficiently familiar with the use of the sight.  Orders were issued for additional instruction in the use of same.<br/>Work on the construction of fresh dugouts at "B" Battery was inspected.  The position chosen appears to be the only suitable one within reasonable distance of the battery.  As No. 3 gun had a direct line of fire immediately over these dugouts, this gun was "washed out" and a fresh emplacement constructed on the right of No. 1 gun.<br/>"B" Battery director was found to be unsatisfactory.  Instructions for the construction of a fresh director are now in hand.</p>
