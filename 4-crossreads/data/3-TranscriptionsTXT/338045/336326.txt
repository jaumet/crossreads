 leave for the front to-night, receive full equipment, rifles, gas helmets etc. &amp; report at 8 p.m., to march out &amp; join their Battalions in the trenches.  Some excitement &amp; now no doubt, the remainder of us will be off shortly to the front at last.  Most important &amp; eventful day so far.  Serves to show that we shall get short notice.  Cold day &amp; to bed early.  The boys leave for the front to-morrow morning 3.30 a.m. &amp; shall be there same night. 
 15th October &ndash; Sunday Reveille as usual.  Cold &amp; drizzling rain &amp; it rained heavily during the night.  The boys departed at 3 a.m.
