<p>1915<br/>Dec. 31 Fri.<br/>New Year's Eve.<br/>Cairo very quiet although precautions had been taken in event of any disturbance, by bringing in large numbers of Mil. Police &ndash; the Town Picquets, both mounted &amp; foot, being considerably strengthened.<br/>Art. Felton came over to see me this afternoon from Overseas Base, Giza. He was invalided from the Peninsula about 2 months ago.<br/>Mail arrived from Australia  Hooray! (13 months since we left Sydney &ndash; The "Hooray" has nothing to do with the 13 months etc).</p>
