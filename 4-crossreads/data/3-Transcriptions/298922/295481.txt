<p>Thursday 14 June 1917</p>
<p>Getting warmer each week &amp; the men are in SHORTS again, they were issued with Helmets last year but so many floated down the Canal we have been allowed to keep our HATS this year.</p>
<p>Just recd. word of Father-in-law's death, enclose cutting he left everything in = Shares to Dolly &amp; his son Capt. ISH. His wife NIL, nor does he mention me, but the Will was made in March 1916, I have a Copy &ndash; don't I know total but he was a wealthy man so Dolly is well provided for.</p>
<p>Will post this at once. Love to all.</p>
