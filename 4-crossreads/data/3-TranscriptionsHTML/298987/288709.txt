<p>October<br/>Drill morning lecture by Archdeacon &ndash; afternoon very good</p>
<p>Thursday 18th<br/>Unloading coal all day consequently our Hut is not short of fuel tonight</p>
<p>Friday 19th<br/>Working party again building a Clink at No 13 camp Mostely barb wire. Am wire expert with 2 assistants  Payed &amp; went to Warminster</p>
<p>Saturday 20<br/>Working party Warminster went to a very poor play would have seen much better in camp free</p>
<p>Sunday 21<br/>As usual Explored Sutten Verry a nice little place Had some very good stout</p>
<p>Monday 22nd<br/>Still Wire expert on Clink</p>
<p>Saturday 27th<br/>Have been on the working party all week about 50 of us. It is less irksome</p>
