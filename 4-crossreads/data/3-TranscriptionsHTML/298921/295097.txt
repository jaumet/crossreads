<p>May, 1915<br/>Tuesday 4<br/>Regimental training Ismalian Canal, Mulberry trees &amp; Lebbak a species of Acacia line the road here. This is the Harvest season &amp; a good many quail are about but I have not shot any yet.<br/>All the Hospitals are now full, quaint "bunks" made from the ribs of Palm trees at 5.P.T. each, &amp; a sad sight to see row upon row - &amp; some good yarns are spun re the fight</p>
