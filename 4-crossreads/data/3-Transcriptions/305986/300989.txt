<p>head don't like the idea of wintering in the trenches, Our "lost division" (the 3rd) is coming across shortly I believe, well the sooner the better they may relieve some of our chap's. 23.11.16 Here we are at Albert waiting for a train that's supposed to start &#64; 4  but that will mean about 4 tomorrow morning.</p>
<p>We got into a place with a name like a sneeze at 7 this morning and after waiting for two hours in the wind the RTO told us he didn't know where our unit was</p>
