<p>Date<br/>13/9/17<br/>1 Corp. rejoined unit ex "Invalided to England" and was taken on strength.  Supernumary to establishment.<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 38,728 rations.</p>
<p>14/9/17<br/>1 man evacuated to hospital  70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 39,100 rations</p>
<p>15/9/17<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 32,300 rations.</p>
<p>16/9/17<br/>Strength:  1 officer 95 O.Ranks not including 2 in hpl and 1 attachee in hospital.  47 N.C.O.'s and men baked 25,880 rations</p>
<p>17/9/17<br/>1 N.C.O. and 1 man evacuated to hospital.  70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 31,600 rations</p>
<p>18/9/17<br/>1 Attachee (P.B. personnel) rejoined unit from hpl.  70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 31,500 rations.</p>
<p>19/9/17<br/>70 N.C.O.'s and men baked 38,960 rations.</p>
