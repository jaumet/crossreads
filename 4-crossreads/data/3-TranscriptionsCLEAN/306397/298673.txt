30th October 1914The transfer from St John's Is to Tanglin Barracks took place today. A steam launch took us to Singapore and from there by auto to Tanglin. We could already at a distance, see the wire fence. Behind this fence were four big barracks - Y,W & X. I am placed with the 4th Engineer, the telegraphist and several sailors of the "Markomania."

1st - 2nd Nov. 1914Nothing unusual
3rd Nov 1914This morning 28 more German P o W arrived from Penang. They are mostly business men, four of whom are to be placed in our camp. There is a rumour that all conscripts are to be sent to a village in India for the purpose of building roads. 20% of the colliers working there are said to be dieing of fever
