<p>SYDNEY, 29th June 1917.<br/>P.O. No. A. 17-3987</p>
<p>Dear Judge Ferguson,</p>
<p>I have to inform you that the following cablegram respecting your son has been received from the Premier (now in London), dated 26th instant.-</p>
<p>"Please inform Judge Ferguson Premier has seen his son before he left for Scotland. Greatly improved, no visible effect of shock, speech apparently quite restored; am writing fully."</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>F.C.G. Tremlett<br/>Acting Secretary.<br/>per J.N.G.</p>
<p>The Honorable Mr. Justice Ferguson,<br/>Supreme Court,<br/>SYDNEY</p>
