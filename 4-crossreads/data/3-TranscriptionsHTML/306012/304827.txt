The rounds were done alright although many snowed up lorries &amp; staff cars were passed.  We took some patients from one of our own ambulances that was in a "bogged" condition, and started off to take them to hospital.  However between Neufchatel &amp; Dannes a three ton lorry ran into the Ambulance buckling a splashboard &amp; both front wheels as well as bending the axel &ndash; fortunately no-one was hurt.  We pushed the damaged Ambulance clear of the road &amp; left it there with the driver.  Soon afterwards I secured a Red Cross car from Etaples and dropped the patients at Camiers (20th Brit. Gen.
