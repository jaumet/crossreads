
I love the rain when I am well wrapped up so that it cannot harm me of course.
When at Warminster the other day, I met a Padre & got into conversation with Him.  Of course, the usual question, what State are you from.  He said He was a South Sea Island Missionary.  I said did you ever get to Epi [Vanuatu].  He said I have lived there with Mina, who is married to a surveyor, & Mr. Roxburgh.  Oh, what a small place the world is again.
