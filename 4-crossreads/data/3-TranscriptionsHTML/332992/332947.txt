<p>Wednesday May 9.1917<br/>A fine morning. The sun is shining brightly.  Last night was very quiet, scarcely any straffing.<br/>[Shorthand entry not transcribed]<br/>There have been parcels and letters coming in almost every day lately, but I have got none so far.  I may get some to day.<br/>I had a letter from my wife today dated March 10. No 54. and also one from B.C.Miller of March 11.<br/>I saw in the "Daily News" this evening that the Ministerialists Mr Hughes party, have had sweeping majorities both in the Senate &amp; House of Representatives.  This is good news and the boys are glad.  One Ministerialist, Mr Glyn is probably beaten.</p>
