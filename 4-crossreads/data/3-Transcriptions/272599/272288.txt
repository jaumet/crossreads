<p>Jan.    Monday 15 1917</p>
<p>Bob meets with accident, run down by motor ambulance on road. He &amp; Jock Heylin walking to Vaudeville, very dark, car not observed, woosh! He received a nasty cut on head &amp; much bruised arm &amp; chest. Ridgeway, Reid &amp; self had gone on before &amp; knew nothing of the mishap till our return. by then Bob had been evacuated to C.C.S.</p>
