<p>November 1917<br/>Thursday 29<br/>At Enab with 60th Div on hill over 2000 ft above sea level. Quiet day.<br/>Very cold night. Dull morning Taube flew over and sent our observation balloon down with machine gun fire. Observers landed in parachutes.</p>
<p>Friday 30<br/>Went into village Enab bought grape jam. Village fairly decent different from Egyptian villages.<br/>Quiet day.</p>
