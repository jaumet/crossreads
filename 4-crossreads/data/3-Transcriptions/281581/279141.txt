<p>4th August</p>
<p>We dropped anchor yesterday evening off Deal and since then have been at rest. Miserable weaither making one feel in harmony with it. We are one of a considerable fleet of vessels, all weirdly painted in camouflage and expect to proceed on our way sometime tomorrow.  Feeling fed up generally.</p>
