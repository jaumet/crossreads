<p>19/1/19 Gourdinne<br/>fittingly responded and tears rolled down the cheeks of his housekeeper. Altogether the function was a distinct success but marred by nonproduction of a bottle by the Maire!. Ration strength of battalion is now 367 only if Hughes' figures are right.</p>
<p>20/1/19<br/>Fine and uneventful day. I walked over to Bde.H.Q. in the evening with Gorman and had afternoon tea there. No fresh news. Came back through the Woods over boggy paths which today are frozen hard. In the twilight the many lights of surrounding villages are twinkling. Mess was a merry meal by reason of fellows back from leave and the presence as guest of our old friend Col. (Dr) Simmonds. Cold and frosty night.</p>
