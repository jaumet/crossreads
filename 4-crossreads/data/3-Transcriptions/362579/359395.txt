<p>She   &amp;  three other sisters from 3rd A.C.C.S. went together but expect they are back in  France to-day.</p>
<p>It is over 12 months since I came to this place (13th Sept 1916) but the weather is perfect. Can hardly think it is the same spot. Everything was so cold, wet   &amp;  miserable. The country is beautiful   &amp;  the weather excellent, one could not feel  <u>blue</u>  under these conditions also the patients all doing well   &amp;  therefore very bright.</p>
