<p>Tuesday 1 May 1917.</p>
<p>Up at 5 a.m. &amp; caught 6.15 pm. [a.m.] train from Spencer St. &nbsp;Got to camp at 9.30 a.m. &amp; stayed in tent till 12 noon. &nbsp;</p>
<p>On a big march tomorrow. &nbsp;</p>
<p>Nothing much doing.&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>Rumors are that we sail on 10<sup>th&nbsp;</sup>inst.</p>
