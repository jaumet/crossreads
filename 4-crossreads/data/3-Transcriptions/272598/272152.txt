<p>Monday 12.2.17 to Wednesday 14.2.17<br/>Weather getting warmer, snow &amp; ice beginning to thaw.  Several heavy bombardments have taken place.  Received parcel from home containing putties.  Rations very poor &amp; scanty.  Reg evacuated to C.C.S. Lads again went into line for a week.  Walked to Albert to try &amp; find 3rd Div Artillery, but was misinformed for they are still up North.</p>
<p>Thursday 15.2.17<br/>Hospital full.  Ground getting very muddy owing to thaw setting in.</p>
<p>Friday 16.2.17 &amp; Saturday 17.2.17<br/>Rained first time for a couple</p>
