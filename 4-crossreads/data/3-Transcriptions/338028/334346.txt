<p>Friday 11th Aug<br/>Did my six miles rounds of outposts, attending sick and looking after casualties, spent the day at Duntroon with Capt F. party. They were waiting for me with a big number of sick and dressings, it was a compliment to me for they had more confidence in me than an English Doctor close handy.</p>
<p>I carry some powerful fly poisons and around there camp in water, I must have killed millions. Then I rode to H.Qrs and paraded to Staff Captain, who obtained for me disinfectant, one R.A.M.C. [Royal Army Medical Corp} refused.</p>
<p>My gallant little mare never turned a hair although I rode her 12 miles through hravy sand. Could hear the guns all day from Oghratinia, they are still pushing the enemy back, they took more prisoners to-day. They are a motley lot.</p>
<p>Saturday 12th Aug<br/>Stayed Hagley Park fixing up a little hospital of three beds. I got some more stuff out and now have a fine little surgery on my own.</p>
<p>Turks are putting up a stand the other side of Bir el Abd.</p>
