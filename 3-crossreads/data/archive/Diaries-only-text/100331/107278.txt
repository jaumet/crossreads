if any unusual sound occurred I would be all of a tremble, I turned in at 1 am although during my absence, 2 trucks with bunks arrived had been shunted in the Siding my mates had transhipped all of our Things into one of the trucks had put the Stretchers on top of the Roof, what with the evening's experience finding my things thrown down anywhere, it was the limit I could have jumped on the people who ordered to change in the dark instead of letting all change the trucks next day. Anyhow I got a bottom bunk soon was snug asleep. 
Saturday 2/11/17.
I again signed at 16 oclock during the evening things were very quiet only now again a shell would go right over towards Epres.
