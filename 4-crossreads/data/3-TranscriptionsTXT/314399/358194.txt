
Wednesday 16 July 1919Rushed around to make sure I was on the quota but found that I had missed.  Saw orderly & rushed my leave thro' & got the 11.30 am to Newport & from there to Brecon. Arrived at Brecon at 5.30. Went to Gamitige Place & had dinner. We talked till 11 & then went to bed. I was tired too.
