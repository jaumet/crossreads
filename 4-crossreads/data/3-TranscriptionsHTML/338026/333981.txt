<p>[3 Th]<br/>Painting waggons.</p>
<p>[4 Fri]<br/>9-30am. Left lines G.S. Waggon &amp; 4 mules. Reported to T.M. at Neippe. Went to Steinwerck R.E dump for sandbags, then D.A. for a mile of telephone wire  Back to Neippe. Left 3pm. for Gun Pits. Got home about 8 pm.</p>
<p>[5 Sat]<br/>Grooming and Harness cleaning.</p>
