
[Friday 28 April 1916]
Court again.  From 9.AM to 6pm. it drags on, wasting time.  We have 3 Barristers among the Officers sitting 2 Scotchmen 1 Englishman & they are having the time of their lives quite enjoying it all.  I am quite satisfied as to the  lying powers of both Sikh & Fellaheen.Further Mail today Mother 3/3/16 Mrs Strange  Day 3/3/16 Laura 13/3/16 & of course ISH & Punches from Mr Ferrer-  From [indecipherable] the letters arrived a week later
