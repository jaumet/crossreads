 28 May 1918 Tuesday (X to X crossed out) Days pass quickly &amp; the Gyppy Labour Corps sing "How many days &amp; How many" "nights must pass ere" "I see my loved ones again" Am afraid you often have difficulty in reading my notes but pens &amp; ink are all bad in this country. X I wrote Aunt Louie this morning X   The cake from Boomerang Stall arrived safely &amp; in good condition thank you very much for it.
