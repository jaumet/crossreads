<p>Tuesday 12 February<br/>Weather fine &amp; warm.  The raid by the 3rd Division was very successful, 37 prisoners, 100 killed by raiders &amp; many by artillery preparation.  Our casualties 22.</p>
<p>Wednesday 13 February<br/>Weather wet. Our unit weekly made its debut to-day and is very decent.<br/>A fancy dress carnival is to come off on Friday.</p>
