Breakfast fell in 8.30 went for water Packed up had dinner 12.  done a lot of messing about saddled up full marching order left raffa 1.30 on a five days stunt went out 7 miles camped near trenches unpacked cooked tea and turned in 7.P.M. nice green country here.
