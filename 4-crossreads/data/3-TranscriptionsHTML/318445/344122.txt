Machine gun fire, all this time, came thick and heavy, and snipers kept firing with deadly <strike>precision</strike> accuracy. At this time it was estimated the 5th Brigade had almost crossed Mount St Quentin, and preparations were made to establish a rough defensive line. But enemy machine guns still continued to deplete our ranks,<strike>but</strike> yet the three Battalions, now considerably mixed up, stuck to their ground, and taking what cover was available, awaited the coming of the counter-attack.</p>
