 30 December 1916 Saturday 
 Easy night, few casualties  Nothing doing specially. 
 Sunday     Dec. 31 
 The last day of the old year. Go on duty at 8 p.m. We see the old year out &amp; new year in in rather pensive mood. Gathered round a smokey brazier. At midnight we drink each others health in Port &amp; whack up a couple of Xmas parcels. Continue duties till morning. No fusilade of shells to welcome in the New Year. Just the usual desultory firing
