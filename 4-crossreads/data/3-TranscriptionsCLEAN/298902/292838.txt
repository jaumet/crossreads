We sat here until midnight engaging in conversation with a well dressed civilian who worked at the Ministry of Justice.  He spoke English & was very interested on learning that I had been to Gallipoli saying - then you must be an Anzac.  I found generally that that our reputation was of the best, all the news apart from what the Germans gave them, coming from Dutch sources.  All Colonial & Scottish troops were firm favourites opinion being divided concerning the English proper, many expressing the view that they were all for business, money first & were too insular.
