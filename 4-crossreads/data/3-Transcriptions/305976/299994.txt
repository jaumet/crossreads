<p>Saturday Ap 6<br/>Visited Greek Monastry near camp today. It is built on Mt Forty[?] also Mt Temptation next to it. This monastry is built in the face of the cliff of stone &amp; inhabited by a few monks. Practically a impregnable position<br/>Sunday Ap 7<br/>An absolute roaster to day Temp 120 degrees had to go across River on message While over, Jacko started leaving some coal boxes. Was night before I could get back.</p>
