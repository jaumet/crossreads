<p>1917 Thursday 10 May</p>
<p>Duties as usual, my day in. Harry &amp; Jerry have a row. Concert in Y.M.C.A. after tea. Not bad but insufferably hot inside. Jock &amp;amp I over to canteen for cooler &amp; then on to cliffs for a blow for half hour.  Post card from Bob.</p>
