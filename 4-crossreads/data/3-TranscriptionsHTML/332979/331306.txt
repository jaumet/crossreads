<p>little Civilian population as nearly every house is occupied by the Authorities. On returning I adjourned into the Salvation Army Rest Room &amp; enjoyed some Music &amp; a Read. At 9 Pm I made my way back to my quarters, &amp; Bed.</p>
<p>Friday 30th 1917.<br/>Up again at 7 am &amp; paraded at 9 am &amp; <strike>was</strike> all was marched up to the Parade Ground &amp; had Company Drill until 12.15 Pm when we were dismissed for Dinner at 2 Pm we were again paraded for Roll Call &amp; then dismissed for the Day. I took things easy during the Afternoon, reading &amp; Smoking until Tea Time &amp; afterwards I went for another walk in another direction for about 4 miles over</p>
