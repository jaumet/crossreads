<p>Wednesday 15 March 1916<br/>Dick finished 40 acres, Geoff still sowing there.  Father &amp; I mustered Lease and weaned Lambs.  Thunder storm but no rain.</p>
<p>Thursday 16 March 1916<br/>Dick drove up to the Ryans and cut 49 gate rails in Castle Rock.  Father &amp; I rode up and looked round horses in Long pdk;  they are getting short of water, will have to shift them.  Geoff finished 40 acres.</p>
