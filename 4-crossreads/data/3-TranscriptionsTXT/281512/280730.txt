At Folkestone we caught the usual waiting Train &amp; arrived at Charring Cross 7.15 pm Colonel Wynter came with me &amp; we put up at the Imperial Hotel, Russel Square. It is quite like home being here again. I have my same room, &amp; as most of the same staff are here still, things are more pleasant. 
 14th October 17 Sunday Oh!  the delights &amp;  Comforts of a warm bath &amp;  comfortable bed. So much so that I slept through until 9 a.m. Breakfast with Col Wynter at 10 am.  After which I went for a stroll in Fleet St, up to Trafalgar Square, Pall Mall, &amp; then back in time for lunch.  Afternoon out to Enfield to visit friends whom I found out.
