 And make preparations at the Grand Theatre where we are to show for two nights &ndash; The House is a very good one indeed and its intercom is a most imposing structure &ndash; 
 Fine stage with good settings &ndash; First show goes remarkably well &ndash; audience comprises Americans. Australians Pommies and French people &ndash; Wordy warfare in which I am not defeated &ndash; Kindness of YMCA Ladies.
