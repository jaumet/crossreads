<p>October 1916<br/>4th   Left for leave in London &amp; returned on the 6th at Midday.<br/>This was my final leave before proceeding to France.<br/>6th.   We were supposed to leave for France to night but it was postponed.<br/>Since I have been in England I have had an interesting &amp; most enjoyable time &amp; I like the country very much.
