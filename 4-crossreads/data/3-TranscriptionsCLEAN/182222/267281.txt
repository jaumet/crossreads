Seemingly we are out of the war & I didn't start this diary to record a picnic, hence neglect.                                       8/6/17 Recalled to unit. This is how it happened. One a.m. we laid a wire near an Aussie Camp & in Crossing a road  we had to wait for a car to pass. It contd. the boss cocky Aussie M G man who stopped & asked all about us & our home secrets. Therefore we are in the active list again, all 1st class                                                                              Sigs--                                       ordered back to Perham Down and Andover, told Coy at Park House.
