
December 1917Friday 21Suffra.  Went out at 8 am as covering party to infantry reconnaissance. Showering all the time. Returned to camp 1 pm. Showering all afternoon. Went on outpost. Wet cold blankets. Fine night.
Saturday 22Suffra.  Fine day at last. Dying blankets. Cabled home for Xmas  more mail I got 13 letters and a parcel from home.
