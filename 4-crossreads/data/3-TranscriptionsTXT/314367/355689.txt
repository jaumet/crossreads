 Ajax told me it was no surrender &ndash; If a German came up he was to run &amp; if overhauled to burn his ship so Ajax had a tub of Benzine fore &amp; aft to turn over &amp; put a match to &amp; then tumble into the boats but said he &ndash; I've fixed the firehoses up so that if the blighters cleared out we could tumble inboard again &amp; put &ndash; these  self created  fires! out &amp; off we'd go &ndash; He was great fun &ndash; He'd tell wonderful tales 'what King George said' what Earl Roberts had said &amp; so on &ndash; Give him enough Beer &amp; he'd go on &amp; on &amp; never repeat himself &ndash; 
 Saty Jan 16-14 &ndash;  Cocoa at 6 am &ndash; Back to Rabaul &ndash; I'm off to the Yarra &ndash; My patients better but they'll be bad again tomorrow &ndash; 3 malaria &amp; one ? rheumatism &ndash; Then off to the Warrego &amp; saw Commander &amp; - Cumberledge &ndash; He's been 430 miles up the Kaiserin Augusta River &ndash; in G. N Guinea &ndash; He'd been in touch with tribes that had never seen a white man before.
