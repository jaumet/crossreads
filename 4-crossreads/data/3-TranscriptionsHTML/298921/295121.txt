<p>May, 1915<br/>Sunday 30<br/>I will enclose a letter from one of our Sergeants &ndash; Poor old Man a D.C.M. man was one of my best reinforcements &amp; gave me his medals &amp; papers the night he left &ndash; I hope you are all well &amp; this goes along safely<br/>Love to all<br/>Frank<br/>As Sgt Lovetts letter has passed Censor it should go on to you it &amp; I cannot be blamed for writing War news "comprenez"</p>
