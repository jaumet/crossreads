 Wednesday 31st  Oct, raining. O boat went this morning, concert tonight, I have to take seats over there. (Hall) 
 Thursday raining P boat went this morning. Q boat called out this afternoon, I am on it. I went to Weymouth &amp; bought some presents to take home, it was funny at times, sent parcel to Fred. Seen Mr Antill 
 Friday warned to leave Saturday morning &amp; then everything cancelled, very rainy looking but keeping off. 
 Saturday 3rd Nov. warned to leave at 8.15PM busy getting everything ready, threatening to rain. not leaving now, the boat is cancelled again. Sunday rainy looking but cleared up after dinner, went for a walk, fell in 4.30PM &amp; had to fall in 9.30PM to move off. Had to wait about 2 hours, being issued with biscuits roll call etc. Marched to Upwey waited an hour entrained about 1PM, Red Cross chap gave us lollies &amp; smokes.
