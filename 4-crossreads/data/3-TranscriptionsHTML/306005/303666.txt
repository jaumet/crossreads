<p>24-12-16<br/>seven miles from El Arish we met these &amp; were issued with 6 handfulls of oats per horse for a feed when we got to camp; we then continued our journey &amp; it was daylight when we pulled up at the troughs to water our horses &amp; sunrise when we arrived in camp; had breakfast &amp; all slept till dinner time. I then went to the wells had a bath &amp; took a ride to the beach only a few hundred yards away to find the pier run out &amp; boats unloading &amp; a good many tommies in swimming so the minesweepers must have cleared all the mines out &amp; horse feed &amp; rations now come by boat &amp; saves a long camel train across the desert; 2 Taubes came over at dinner time dropping</p>
