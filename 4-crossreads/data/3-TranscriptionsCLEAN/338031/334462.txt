A good few whales were sighted & some of them presented the appearance of a submarine emerging from the vasty deep.November 17thAt daybreak the ship was stationary in Table Bay. The previous day we had sighted the brown & somewhat dismal coast of South Africa. In many respects it is like the Australian coast as a monotonously sad and desolate landscape.
