This might be the Englishmans idea of showing what his manners are to Australians for some knew Isaackson but I am inclined to think it is just their natural character &amp; lack of courtesy &amp; knowledge of the elementery rules of politness so unnecessary to selfish natures. 
 After this we met the one decent Englishman in the room Twigg &amp; told him he was too good to be an Englishman &amp; we were taking out papers to make him a Naturalised Englishman. 
 3-4-19 (Thur) Attended a Masonic meeting in Room 13 Hotel du Nord. Method of examination is slipshop &amp; embarassing. Richards is with me but Isaackson has gone to Paris. Dined with Richards &amp; then went to Brasserie de l'Opera when he left me &amp; I came home.
