<p>7th April 1915 Wednesday</p>
<p>The harbour is full of transports &amp; there is also a battleship 'Tenessee'  &ndash; obsolete American boat.  The 'Teesta'  is next us full of Indians.  The 'F16'  went out yesterday.  The French soldiers have very peculiar uniforms.  Some have red baggy trousers &amp; others wear a bluey colored dress.  We are very uncomfortable here.
