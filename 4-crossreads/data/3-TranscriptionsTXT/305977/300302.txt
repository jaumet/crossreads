
December 1915of cake, lollies, chocolate & tobacco.  We have had so much good stuff given to us lately that I can hardly bear the sight of sweets.  Clothes are what I principally want as my kit bag was practically empty (ie the black kit bag I left behind when I went away  Got 5 letters today 2 from home & 1 from Mrs Vale & Doug & 1 from Meg & a parcel of chocolates from Kate.  Went to the pictures in Maadi at night.
Friday 31stWas pretty busy in the morning making up nominal roles duty roster & requirements for each man & horse so that we will be properly equipped.  More parcels from the Regimental Comfort Fund arrived today.  It is a pity we didnt get them at Gallipoli.  We are fairly inundated
