<p>List &amp; rec'd about 40 reinforcements to the Coy. Moved at 1pm. for Warloy which we reached &amp; were billeted in about 5pm. I was on the Billeting party and went through on Transport motors.</p>
<p>7th. Wednesday.<br/>Up 7am muster parade later. Recd mail.</p>
<p>Requisitions made out, complete<strike>ly</strike> Nominal rolls also completed &amp; nominations for promotions rec'd. Shoping &amp; to bed early.</p>
<p>8th. Tuesday.<br/>Orders for moving at 9am. Left on bike 8.30</p>
