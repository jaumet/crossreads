  
 "Ilkley"                                                                                                                                                                               Orrong & Alma Rds.                                                                                                                                                                  East St. Kilda.                                                                                                                                                                        9.5.22 To the Secretary of Baby Health Centre. Dear Madam, I am quite willing to take a course of 3 months training in Infant Welfare Work at South Melbourne Town Hall.     I  also agree to the conditions:-   of a   The 1st Month, no salary, &  the remaining 2 months at the rate of  2.2.0 per week. I should be pleased &  at  quite ready to commence the work  in July  any time after June.
