
<p>FRIDAY, AUGUST 22, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Lopping in 4D in</p>
<p>morning and measuring out</p>
<p>ground for Quainton in Wheelers.</p>
<p>About the house all the afternoon</p>
<p>Had a dance at night &amp; it was</p>
<p>a great success. About 36 people</p>
<p>to supper &amp; kept going till 5 AM</p>
