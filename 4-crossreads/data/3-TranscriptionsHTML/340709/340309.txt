<p>11<br/>I see by the roster that I am O.O.D. tomorrow, so now to bed.<br/>Wed 26th : There were only two events worthy of record today. A boat race &amp; the first issue of the "Expeditioner". The race took place this afternoon at 4 o'c. The Naval Reserve were challenged by the military for a sprint around the three ships, about 1 &frac12; mile. The military were out-classed from the start and the Naval Reserve won by several lengths. A fair amount of money changed hands. Vol 1 of the "Expeditioner" consists of three pages of satire and wit, with a few facts thrown in here &amp; there. The following are a few extracts:-<br/>Hatched:- Lies &amp; ideas only.
