<p>My finger is quite well now but minus a nail therefore looks very ugly.  I have not posted my parcel of souvenirs yet but hope to this week as I am expecting some money from Ken.  I had a nice little outing here yesterday with a cobber at the hospital here, we went to Redditch about 8 miles from here &amp; spent the afternoon at the pictures with a couple of nice &amp; very pretty girls that I have met since being here.
