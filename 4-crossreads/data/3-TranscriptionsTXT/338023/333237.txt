 Tomorrow we get leave to the town, but all pubs will be closed to Khaki, so the boys will not be likely to get lively. It's a good precaution as we are not the only crowd by a long chalk. 
 13th Just heard that all letters from D. are to be censored so am entrusting this to a returning wounded man who will post it in Aus. Will describe the marvels of this wonderful (?) city later on. 
 Bearer waiting. Thus for present will say so long.  Harry 
 Sending to Betty small packet contg shells from Bluff Beach &ndash; not much chop.
