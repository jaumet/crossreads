 17 March 1916 Friday 
 Tattoo this morning for all who was absent from parade last evening about 150 guilty. Some were fined 10/- including self. Bob Tony &amp; self go off to town on leave &amp; call at Comforts Depot &amp; receive handkerchiefs &amp; fly nets. Then we go round to Woolloomooloo  Bay &amp; view the Armadale. No one preventing us we go on board &amp; see what we have to make our home on for 38 days. Ye Gods! What a tub &amp; dirty. I go home with Bob to tea &amp; stay the night whilst Tony goes to Neutral Bay. We go to pictures at the Junction after tea.
