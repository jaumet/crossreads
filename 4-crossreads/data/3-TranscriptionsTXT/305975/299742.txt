 Sunday Dec. 1.12.18. am feeling well rested all day  nothing he[re] to see  wrote to maud Josie Bell Dorrie alma Mick  went down town to night  nothing doing turned in 9.P.M. Monday Dec. 2.12.18. am feeling o.k.  nice warm day  nothing doing  resting all day. Tuesday Dec 3.12.18. feeling well  had a look around  very cold to day  went to Pictures  turned in 9.30.
