<p>1.9.16<br/>Fri 1st<br/>Lindsell, Elliott, Wride &amp; Ingham went into Port Said on the 8 oclock train this morning on leave; Nothing of any consequence happened today. I have all the Sqdn Sig working on the phone now;  Bassans Court Martial came off today &amp; has been postponed till tomorrow. I heard today that Sergt Sutherland (Bde H.Q.) has also applied for Sig Officer to this Regt. &amp; as Col Meredith left yesterday &amp; is on his way to Australia on leave I am not too confident of getting it although Major Granville is doing all he can to get me the Commission<br/>We are pretty busy here as we have lamp practice every night. 5 Enemy planes bombed Port Said yesterday &amp; I believe a couple of them were brought down</p>
