<p>9 September 1918 Monday<br/>Went Jerusalem to sit on a Board of Enquiry <strike>into the loss of 400/-/- Cash stolen from AIF Canteen-</strike><br/>Road very dusty &amp; "Jericho Jane" amused herself by firing long range shots with her naval gun into Jericho-  Camping near Railway Station at Reinforcement Camp was like a Miller on arrival from the Dust-  Will be up here for 3 days I expect a nice cool change-</p>
