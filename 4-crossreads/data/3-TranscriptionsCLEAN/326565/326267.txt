
very dirty mixed race, you never see two dressed alike they wear anything.  The chap in charge carries a stick & whacks any of them that try to loaf.Egyptian police, fine men, carry a long cane & whack any of the men who they have occasion to speak to, strange to see them trying to duck away from the stick like a schoolboy.  Interesting to watch them diving for money; hold what they get in their mouth till it is full then fill their hands.  A couple of Indian Stretcher bearers came on board & took off men for hospital.
