<p>nothing to write about  Matches have been terribly scarce.  The Mess Sergeant came back today with <strike>out</strike> a box of cigarettes for us.</p>
<p>19th<br/>I went forward again this morning and after climbing the hill opposite Jerry's position pulled out my glasses and had a good look round the country.  Nothing was discernible over Jerry's way however except a few of our shells bursting in a wood.  Instead, a grand panorama of majestic hills behind rewarded me.  These hills overlook the Somme valley for miles.  Away in the distance the battered tower of Corbie Church</p>
