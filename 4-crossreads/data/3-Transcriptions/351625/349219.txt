<p>Somewhere in Belgium</p>
<p>Dear Mother,</p>
<p>I will start your weekly letters &amp; will try &amp; write a little more frequently than I have been the last month or so but you will understand that it is very hard to keep up correspondence when the Battalion is in the line &amp; we have just done 24 days out of which I did 8 days in the front outposts the</p>
