 survived the early washing &amp; after 11 days there had a week in the Con. Camp &amp; have been back in Base Details about 4 days waiting for another draft. 
 April 16/17 This morning on early morning parade my name was called out amongst others mostly old 1st Division men for the next draft up the line &amp; I am not sorry to be off again as we are all bored with the monotony of this camp life &ndash; the little we do has no interest in it &amp; we are all prepared to put up with the mud &amp; hard work in return for the comparative freedom of action up the line where one gets about ones work without eternally 'shunning' &amp; 'Stan at easing'
