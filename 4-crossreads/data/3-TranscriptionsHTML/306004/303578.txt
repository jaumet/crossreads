<p>29.10.16<br/>Sun 29th<br/>The 3rd Regt had a sports meeting today which opened with a swimming race from the other side of the Canal across to this a distance of 198 yards it was a good race with 9 competitors; then 3 heats for the 100 yards running Championship for the Bde then horse jumping &amp; some good jumping was witnessed with several falls to break the monotony but no damage done, then the final of the running Championship which was won in fine style by a Queenslander &amp; a lad from this Regt ran second; Then the sports were wound up with a long</p>
