<p>13.11.16<br/>Mon 13th<br/>Had a Regimental parade this morning, there was a good deal of amusement caused at dinner time by the despatch rider (Dean) trying to ride my pony down to water bareback, Dean was thrown 4 times, then two other lads decided to ride him but shared the same fate, &amp; Dean walked &amp; led him down. a number of men &amp; horses from details arrived this afternoon. The Artillery a mile or two to the East of us had some practise this morning;<br/>I received a parcel from home today; 8 or 9 aeroplanes coming from Kantara way flew over after sundown tonight &amp; landed at Salmana, Cpl Cabban sent away with rheumatics today</p>
