
May, 1917. Wednesday 9Fine day. Up at 7 and after wash had breakfast and paraded at 8. and was asked if we wanted to return to companies. I said "yes" and went away about 11. to Bapaume. 1st Divn. seems to be going out 2nd Divn. out already & I couldn't trace company so returned to [indecipherable] water point & slept the night there Only a little water came through & saw Bolton & he told me to come over to [Bicourt] [indecipherable] on next day. Bed at 12 after reading.
