
Monday 19 July 1915 Working hard all day at the hospital  Arranging for Englishmen to get away to England.
Tuesday 20 July 1915 Got 141 Englishmen away today, 7-45 am Entrained & all correct Very hot Letters from home
Wednesday 21 July 1915 In Cairo to see the D.M.S. this morning, in consultation about the O.Cs [?] & other hospital matters Sleep during the afternoon Letter writing to Girls & MacNamara at night
