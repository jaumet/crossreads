<p>This morning at dawn I was awoken by two shots from the big gun &ndash; shrapnel over Jericho &amp; then he shut up &amp; fired no more until this afternoon when they tell me that he fired six more up the river but I was up at the 1st Bgde. &amp; heard nothing so I hae me doots.</p>
<p>The weather has improved tremendously the last few days being much cooler.  The nights now are just lovely.</p>
