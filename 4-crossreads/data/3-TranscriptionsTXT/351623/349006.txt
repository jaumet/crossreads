
26 Dec 16Anchored at Rosyth. We had a concert tonight among the ships company.
27 Dec 16Got under weigh at 1.30 pm & proceeded to sea. Turned round & returned to Rosyth in First watch & coaled ship.
28 Dec 16Anchored at Rosyth
29 Dec 16Got under weigh at 3.30 pm & proceeded to sea in company with HMAS Sydney & between 10 & 20 Destroyers.
30 Dec 16Patrolling in North Sea
31 Dec 16[another year of war over]Returned with Sydney & Destroyers & anchored at Rosyth. Coaled & oiled ship.
