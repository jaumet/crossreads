 Novembre 20 Arrived back with the Company today from leave to Blighty. Found the Company had moved from Ypres to the Messines Front &amp; are camped about 4 k. from Dramontre. Left Boulogne about 4.30 am &amp; arrived Bailleul about 12.15. Very slow journey. 
 Novembre 21 Section working at Donegal Camp about 2k. from Dramontre &ndash; also at Dramontre. I have been having a look around to get used to the new localities.
