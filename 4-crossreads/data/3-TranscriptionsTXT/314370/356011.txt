 April, 1916 Thursday 13 The dreaded wind commenced to blow just after sunrise gaining strength all day until about 3 pm it was not possible to open ones eyes. The sand drifts into every crack &amp; hole making it impossible to keep it out. 
 Friday 14 After blowing all night the  Camseen  x abates after sunrise   x Khamsin Altogether it has done a whole heap of damage  it will take us a week to get clear of the dust
