<p>Friday Aug 12, 1916.</p>
<p>Cloudy all day with a few drops of rain in the afternoon. After dinner &amp; with  Handcock, Matthews &amp; N.Jorgensur&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;, went to Figeldean to have a look at the Village and the Spreading chestnut Tree. The river Avon runs through the village. There are some very old houses with thatched roofs. One house had a date 1666 on it. It was 2 stories high. The shop was open. There are two forges.
