<p>21 May, Wednesday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I just miss fatigue work. Sent photos to MRL. So to Lecture on "Birth of C'wealth" &amp; Boomerang Concert. Constituting began May 9th. Concert rather smutty. Play Billy Billiards on No 4 Crook Table. He beats me by flukes.</p>
<p>Wrote Maureen, C., Ruby Watson. M.R.L.</p>
<p>22 May, Thursday</p>
<p>F.<br/>I am No 13 in the Section &amp; come out on Boat roll of "Beltana". Need inspection &amp; write letters. Read all afternoon. We go to Torant after tea to get washing. Go to pictures afterwards with Billy.</p>
<p>Wrote Auntie Annie, Dad, Banks to Dad, Mag to M.R.L. &amp; M.R.L.</p>
