<p>June 20 in this hut &amp; we sleep on straw mattresses on the floor.<br/>Just been down town &amp; made a few purchases for the voyage<br/>Feeling goodo. Was paid &pound; 1 at Harefield so am fairly financial yet.</p>
<p>21 Rotten night last night , fellows coming in all night variously shickered &amp; confoundedly talkative and I was scratching some too &ndash; must have been the rough blankets. We are not allowed in the main part of the town without a pass, but can ramble about elsewhere. Pretty rotten camp &amp; not a very</p>
