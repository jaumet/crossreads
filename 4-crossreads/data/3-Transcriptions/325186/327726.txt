The first day in the Quarantine the troops kicked up a noise about the state of the Camp but conditions improved &amp; we left Quarantine on 3rd March for home.  I was given a leave pass for two days &amp; five pound  Then I reported again to Victoria Barracks received my discharge dated for 17th April 1919  I was glad to get back into Civilian clothes &amp; not sorry to be out of the Military although I have some</p>
