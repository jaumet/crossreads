  
 Monday 26 August Up as usual but suddenly advised 8 am. that we were moving immediately. 
 Packed & moved out in no time via Sailly-le-Sec - Laurete, Chipilly to Etinehem. One Detachment left  at  behind. 
 Pulled into Div H.Q. three wagons ordered immediately. Was able to have dinner first. Ran line from 11 Bde to 10 Bde roads harassingly shelled so had to try a different route. Got to 10th alright at Rly Stn Bray. Line not O.K. Returned to 11th repaired incorrect joint. 
 Home to horselines on Chipilly Rd. Well tired. Got knocked on back of knucles only scratched.
