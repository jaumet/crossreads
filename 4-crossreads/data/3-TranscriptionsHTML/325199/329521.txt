<p>Captain Davidson conducted divine service on the well deck and Captain McGregor in the fore part of the ship.</p>
<p>I finished reading a novel "The Spectre of the Past" by Winifred Graham. It is a very interesting story &amp; well worth reading.</p>
<p>Monday July 10. 1916<br/>It is a dull cloudy morning and much cooler than yesterday. The wind is from the N.W. The course is 3 points North of West.</p>
<p>I finished reading Part 16 Vol 2. chapter XXXIII of the "Times History of the War" dealing with the Defence and Fall of Antwerp. I have started reading the next number.</p>
<p>We were drilled this morning in Semaphore signalling.</p>
<p>I saw a lot of flying fish today. After dinner a large one funnelled steamer passed us on the Port side. [Shorthand not transcribed.] Peter Silver was discharged from the Hospital today. He had been laid up with the measles.</p>
<p>Tuesday July 11. 1916.</p>
