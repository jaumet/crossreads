Our friend Barnes of the original 3rd Fld  Amb., in  a state of intoxication, was a frequent demander of "Spells"; but the boys themselves silenced him Clough got a ride in a waggon looking after officers kit, &amp; Eva, Gray &amp; I did the remainder with many a cheerful jest &amp; without turning a hair. Packed into trucks at the Docks station &amp; our truck, capable of holding 10 comfortably, received forty &amp; so it was a night of misery.
