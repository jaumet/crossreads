 March, 1917. Monday 5 No fires allowed in day time so had breakfast at 6. Misty &amp; later sunny. Work at 10 cleaning up and dinner at 2 (hot) as it was misty. Carried on till 6 and tea at 7. Rations to come to-night. &amp; more men to-morrow. Bed at 9 after reading letters. To go out at 9 to-morrow. Division going out rumoured to Armentiers. Snowed fairly heavily.
