<p>A &amp; B sections play football- both codes- A section successful- Soccer 3-2, Rugby 9-6  Great day- good sport-  Fresh milk- fresh air fresh everything.<br/>Jul. 11th.<br/>Reveille 4 a. m.  Ambulance moves off at 6 a. m. for Wizernes (8 m) &amp; then we entrain.  We pass through Calais &amp; Boulogne.  Cattle-truck- High expectations in passing through B'ne.  Journey ends at 9.p.m. at Saleux   Night march which proves to be most strenuous experienced to date.  We lose our bearings-   Many exhausted.  Sleep on road.</p>
