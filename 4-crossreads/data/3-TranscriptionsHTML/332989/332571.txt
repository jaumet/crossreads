<p>in South Africa,   Also warm bath every week.    A big wooden tub (1/2 a large barrel full of hot water)    A Company goes down at one time &amp; there are about fourty tubs &amp; of [here inserted "C. Coy 11th Battalion 3rd Brigade"] course each man gets clean water  When we strip we take our dirty clothes to a window &amp; get checks for clean ones that are procured at another window, where we can always get clean towel, shirt underclothes &amp; sox (clean &amp; darned) &amp; once every three or four weeks a new pair of sox is issued to us from the Quartermasters Stores.   Fondest love to Girls &amp; Self    From Ever your Loving Son George</p>
