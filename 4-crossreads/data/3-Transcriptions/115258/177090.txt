When you find how many of them are half bred Greeks you begin to wonder still more. I know that often before I have cursed the Egyptian and told people wha a crawling blackguard he was, but I must withdraw and appologise. I was blaming him for the sins of his parasites - all the different nationalities, Greeks, Italians, Jews Spaniards and half breds who prey on him, whilst I in my ignorance was not able to realize the great difference between the true Egyptian and would-be Egyptian Greek parasites.</p>
