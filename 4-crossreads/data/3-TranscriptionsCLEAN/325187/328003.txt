
[Sunday 17 September 1916]
St AnnesRaining hard stayed in bed for Breakfast this is colder than London I think.  They have rooms here a gentlewoman runs the house with her 17 year old daughter but I believe the Isherwoods are fairly well to do anyway Dolly wants to marry me & come to Australia.  Probably go to Ireland at end of this week & then return here.Love to you all & some from Dolly.
