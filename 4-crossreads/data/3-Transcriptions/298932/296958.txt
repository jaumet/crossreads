<p>CAFE BELGE<br/>Monday 8<br/>The best bad day of my career, stranded with four jars of RUM at ZOWNEBEKE standing in mud, that covered my bosom,<br/>Tuesday 9<br/>but despite the severe trial, I cursed as a bush bred boy can curse, and <u>then</u> I laughed like hell<br/>Wednesday 10<br/>The 17th &amp; 20th Batts charged the huns yesterday, but after getting their object they retired Releived by the 45th Batt<br/>Thursday 11<br/>A lot of good men gone. Jack Lyons Mr New Andy Kear, and many more. Will it never end</p>
