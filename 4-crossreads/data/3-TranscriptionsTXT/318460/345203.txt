Sunday 18/2/17dense fog all day yesterday could not see 50 yds away, ended in rain last night, found our dug-out flooded &amp; had to pack up &amp; shift at 11 PM. no sleep all night. Blankets &amp; Waterproof sheet wetheavy firing all night.fog again this morning same as yesterday snow all gone &amp; nearly all ice but mud, a regular sea of it.Pay 2.14.5 &pound;21.2.0
56th came out tonight dead beat had a good raid 2 killed. took Prisoners &pound; killed a lot of huns
Monday 19/2/17Rained a lot during the night foggy again this morning. The thaw [indecipherable] in Properly nothing but mud now till springFritz seems very jumpy so the boys say, food very poor all through the Army Aus &amp; English.Ought to get out of this front in 11 dayssent Nell's &amp; Maudes letters yesterday.
Tuesday 20/2/17Raining &amp; miserable water dripping in dug-out, also cold wind blowing,same old routine nothing much in Artillery line last night too foggy that dense you could cut it with a knife.
Wednesday 21/2/17Still fog &amp; slight Rain, not much artillery fighting, can't see 50 yds away.54th going in tonight.Pay. 2.17.5. &pound;21.5.0
Thursday 22/1/17Gas alert again last night, same every night.55th went in last night to supports.fog &amp; Rain today.Artillery busy.Pay 2.18.5 &pound;21.6.0
Friday 23/2/17same as yesterday fog. very little gun firingPay 2.19.5 &pound;21.7.0Cold &amp; miserable.
