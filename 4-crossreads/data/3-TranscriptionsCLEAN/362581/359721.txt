  
 O.A.S. A.I.F. Passed Censor 55. 
 Master Edward Farmer Ilkley C/r. Orrong   &  Alma Rds East St Kilda Victoria Australia 
 Dearest Eddy, We had a fine time in Colombo [crossed out by Censor]. So sorry I could not send you a card from there but I had been sea-sick, felt too tired to write. Hope you are all well   &  happy. I shall send you some more cards later on. Fondest love   &  kisses. From Auntie Ede.
