<p>April 18th<br/>Same as usual sleep till mid-day. Gas picket at night.<br/>April 19th<br/>Picked in party of 33 to take up duties at Bois Grenier (advanced dressing station). To leave for destination in Cars at 8pm. Rough ride (no lights)<br/>April 20th<br/>First day in action stretcher bearing in support trenches. Our dug-out very comfortable "Jenolan</p>
