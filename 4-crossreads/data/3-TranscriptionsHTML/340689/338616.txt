<p>cut and general clean up and then found an old guide who took us round Paris for the day for 5F we paying all expenses. First to A.P.M. then on to Tuilerie  Eiffel and Hotel Invalid where we saw the tomb of Napoleon. Dinner in an arcade. Tea at Club and went with 1st Batt chap to Folies. Episode of the "femme." Folies very good but too much French  Bed late.</p>
<p>9.11.17. Walk before dinner and breakfast  &amp;  dinner close to Club. Versailles in aftn with New Zealanders. Paris by night in evng.</p>
<p>10.11.17. Promenade  <strike>to Sacre Coeur</strike>  &amp; in aftn to Malmaison guide from Blighty Corner. Alhambra in evening.</p>
<p>11.11.17 Sacre Coeur morning. Aftn  Wheel  Musee Grevin. Olympia at night.</p>
<p>12.11.17. Guide all day to Notre Dame &amp; churches  &amp;   Madeleine did nothing in even and caught train leaving about 12</p>
