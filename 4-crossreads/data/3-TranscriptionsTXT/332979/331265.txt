Saturday 3/3/17.It being very hot & close we all were feeling the heat so we had no drill to day.
Sunday 4/3/17.I was Corporal of the Guard all day from 10 am to 10 am next day & did practically the same as the previous Sunday.  We had to supply a Submarine Guard of 50 Men also their duty is to be posted on different parts of the Ship with rifles & watch for Submarines they do duty for 2 hours on & 4 off, as we are now daily getting nearer our destination the danger of Enemy Submarine is Greater so of course the number of Men will increase.
