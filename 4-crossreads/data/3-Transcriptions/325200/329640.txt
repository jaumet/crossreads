<p>emerges out of the blankets only to be hastily withdrawn on striking the cold morning air. Next the blankets are pushed down and a semi naked form emerges usually partly covered by a little short shirt  &amp;  stands up shivering in the cold blast  &amp;  yawning  &amp;  blinking the eyes. Then comes the performance of entering a pair of britches, an awkward business this for one half asleep, necessitating standing on one leg  &amp;  waving the other in erratic circles.<br/>Collapse is the almost inevitable result followed by a drowsy oath</p>
