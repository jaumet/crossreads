<p>Sunday 15 April 1917<br/>Went to church in morning, a Taube came over halfway through service.  Saw Andy Cunningham, pretty warm.  P.C. [see images for possible Arabic number for 6].</p>
<p>Monday 16 April 1917<br/>On Day Stableman:  busy day packing up for big stunt.  Left Khan Yunus at 8.30 &amp; travelled all night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 17 April 1917<br/>Arrived at Selal at sunrise, watered up at the Wadi Guzze, camped the day.  Taubes over all day they bombed but did little damage.  Outpost at night.</p>
<p>Wednesday 18 April 1917<br/>Left outpost early and came back to camp &amp; left there at 10.30 for supports about 5 miles east, came back &amp; packed up for a big night's stunt, left at 8.30 &amp; travelled all night.</p>
<p>Thursday 19 April 1917<br/>Arrived at Hamerea at daylight &amp; took up positions &amp; were shelled heavily.  Turks attacked about 4, heavy firing all day, left possy and came back to Tel-el-Junmel.</p>
<p>Friday 20 April 1917<br/>Arrived at Tel-el-Junmel stayed till 9.30 then made 5 hrs. to Guz-el-Selub where we were heavily bombed by 3 Taubes about 14 bombs did damage to the horses  inpaticular, watered at Wadi Guzzi.</p>
<p>Saturday 21 April 1917<br/>Stayed out on outpost all day &amp; were relieved at 4.30, proceeded to Wadi Guzzi watered up then proceeded on to Khan Yunus arriving at 9.30, on picquet at night.</p>
<p>Abassan el Kebir.</p>
