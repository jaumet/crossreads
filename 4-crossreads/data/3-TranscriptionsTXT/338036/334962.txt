
[1st Oct. contd]Fritz balloons were up & still the work behind the lines continued.  Awoke again at 5 pm & back to Lone Wood in time to get a harvest of cases.  At 7.10 pm an SOS went up & our barrage was turned on again.  Another counter attack.  Sam told the story of finding as dugout with 2 Fritzs & 2 Tommies bodies in & the top blown in.  At 8.30 we gave him another exhibition barrage & such a sight.  Mac & I sat there & watched the flashes & it was indescribable.  Before it finished a case came in & we had the roughest trip I ever experienced.
When half way a 5.9 bomb on our track in front & cracked us with the earth - hurting Jack Driver slightly.  A couple of seconds later one burst on our right & almost blew us over.  Pushing on two more burst uncomfortably close & how we escaped I do not know.  When we returned to dugout I opened a parcel which had just arrived from MB & for a few minutes had some feast.
