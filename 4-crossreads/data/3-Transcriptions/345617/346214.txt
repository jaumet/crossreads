<p>Octobre 19<br/>Working on drainage again between West Hoek &amp; Anzac Ridge. Shelling very heavy. Reported some of Infty party killed &amp; wounded. Lft Fullerton 54th Batt attached to our Coy killed. Sap's Ross &amp; Bernard killed. Cpl Furnell wounded. Reported heavy enemy shelling on three Comps Front.</p>
<p>Octobre 20<br/>Working on Rd. ditching again today. Fairly heavy shelling again. Some of our Infty party wounded. Nasty weather.</p>
