<p>353<br/>5<br/>P.S. Sergeant Braga has been transferred to the Hospital next door to this. I saw him at our gate for a few minutes this morning. He has to undergo a months electrical treatment &ndash; He was very charmed to get a letter from Mrs Bob Owen &ndash; I am going to try and see him again before I go &ndash;</p>
