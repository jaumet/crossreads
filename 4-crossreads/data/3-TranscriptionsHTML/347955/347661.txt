<p>there is a concert on here tonight I never went to it they have a Band Playing turned in 7.30<br/>Thursday Jan 31.1.18.<br/>turned out 6.15 phisical jerks on grasing guard saw a camel chaste Bedouins Brought camels in 2.P.M. had a look at football very rough game tea 5.P.M turned in concert on again to night I never went.  they have a wet canteen here now<br/>Friday Feb.1.2.18<br/>turned out 6.15 Phisical Jerks went to Raffa station with Pack camels loaded two bales tibbin for no 3 company they are in Contact dinner 12 looked at football match  tea turned in wrote to walter<br/>Saturday Feb.2.2.18<br/>turned out 6.15 fed up Phisical Jerks Breakfast on fatigue digging a dug out for a</p>
