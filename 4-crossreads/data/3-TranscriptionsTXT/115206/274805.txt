 August,1918 Friday 23 
 1&frac12;kilos from Morcourt. Bty. Pos. The stunt opened up at 4.20 pm. And we were supposed to move forward, but did not do so until early in the evening when we took up a position in the right rear of Proyart. The weather was very good, nice and warm with a fair breeze. Up till 5 pm we had captured our objectives and 1900 prisoners. Laid a line to our new possie from Bdge. Slept under the stars last night.
