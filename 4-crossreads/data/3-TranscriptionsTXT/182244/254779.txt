 Anecdote 
 A priest on his rambles round Galipoli. Well know as one of the best. Coming up to a sentry he said:/ 
 Are you an R.C.? 
 No but I am a bloody sentry sor,&nbsp;&amp; if you dont hurry away them Turks over there will shoot you sure. 
 Told me by Jerrom on S.S. Demosthenes going south in the Aegean Sea. 25th October 1915,&nbsp;St Crispin&#39;s Day&nbsp;Agincourt 1915
