
18. Thursday.Went to Minerva & waited at table. Home about 4 & did some washing & typing. Fine day but showers in evening.
19. Friday.
Typed all day at home. Heavy showers all day. A.A,S. came home in evening. Depressed & worried - money running out.
20. Saturday.Rain all day again. Went to Lady Byles for lunch met there Mrs Brook & Mrs D
