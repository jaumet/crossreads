<p>a promenade built against the sea shore, and then went to he Royal Hotel for tea.  After this we handed a report of our doings into the leading newspaper office, and then after having a walk around some of the streets went to the Town Hall where an organ recital was held at 8.30pm.  The interior of the hall is beautifully decorated and the organ is one of the biggest in the world, a splendid recital was given and lasted an hour.  Concluding we had seen enough we tramed to the pier and launched to the ship.  The tramcars of the City are the property of the Municipal Council</p>
