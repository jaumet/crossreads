<p>It may yet be cancelled however.  The 180 were put thro' an embarkation medical parade this afternoon &amp; most of my staff is packed up, so all we can do is hang on &amp; see what happens.<br/>It is no use writing letters for they will not be sent, but I would like to let Rita have the news.<br/>I am thinking of her more &amp; more every day, &amp; wishing I could only be back again.</p>
