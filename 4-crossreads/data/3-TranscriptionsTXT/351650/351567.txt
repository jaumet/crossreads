South Australian HotelNorth Terrace,Adelaide
1 October 1915
Dear Mrs John
Thankyou for the pen and clinical thermometer you sent me &ndash; they will see pretty continuous use after they arrive in England with me.
I should be honored to write you a letter occasionally, and will do so if you do not object.
