They are a Christian people, they believe in the divine nature of the Saviour but do not allow Him any human attributes.  to him  Their places of worship were very dirty & evil smelling -  not  & compared most unfavourably with the large & spacious  clean  mosques. They hold to Old Testament institutions & behave as if they lived in early Biblical times. The Bedouins are the nomadic Arabs & there are about 600,000 of these.  They are generally gentle & inoffensive, tho those in the north have inherited the fiery blood of the desert tribes.
