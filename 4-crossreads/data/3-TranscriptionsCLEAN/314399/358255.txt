  
 Monday 15 September 1919 The boys changed round this afternoon into different corps according to their states. No trouble at all. Gave the QM a hand in the morning. 
 Tuesday 16 September 1919 Boat drill at 9.30. Had an inspection of kits at 2 pm for duteable goods. Had some good boxing in the afternoon & in the evening a fancy dress. Some of the costumes were miles ahead of the last issue. Snow Sharland had a nasty fit this morning & is now in hospital.
