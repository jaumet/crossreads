<p>Kelly   &amp;  I are going to Scotland    for a few days &ndash; perhaps leaving on Thursday. The former has some friends whom so wants me to meet. I cannot be bothered telling my friends I'm here, - they all asked so many questions   &amp;  I can't be bothered &ndash; it is finally ended that little affair of mine   &amp;  I'm jolly glad now I know. Received a letter from his O.C. saying he is quite well   &amp;  in the Battalion. Why he has behaved in this manner I cannot understand &ndash; but I shall never trouble him again   &amp;  am done with the whole d-<br/>Lot.</p>
