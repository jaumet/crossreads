
Left London on April 30 at 10.30 a.m. from Euston for Liverpool & Birkenhead arriving 3 p.m.  Left at 9 p.m. in dense fog & had horn going for 2 nights & a day every 3 minutes.Boats slung out & provisioned 15 passengers 14 adult passengers, my 3 children & a little boy.Held up at Marseilles for 5 days.  Buying of a mans shirt (chemise).Steamers 2 & 3 deep at Quay.Got passports with photos.German prisoners at work 150 or more.3 transports off to the Dardanelles with Fr. troops
