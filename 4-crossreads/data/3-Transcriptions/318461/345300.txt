<p>Saturday 30/6/17<br/>This day last year was in Railway carriage on our way up from Marsailles<br/>Cold &amp; miserable, scotch mist &amp; windy<br/>Premier Holman reviewed Boys yesterday. Told them they would have to do Plenty of fighting yet.<br/>Pay. 31/9. &pound;27.16.0<br/>Paid 22/- 30 francs Bal 9/9.</p>
<p>Sunday1/7/17<br/>Still cold &amp; rain about. Rained hard yesterday &amp; last night<br/>Boys off to Amiens on leave not taking any myself saving for Blighty.<br/>More Petty theifts last night. A Coy lost 70 francs.<br/>Pay. 10.9 &pound;27.17.0.<br/>letter from Maude &amp; Percy Peacy.</p>
<p>Monday 2/7/17<br/>getting warm again, had to send 3 men back to Coys drunk.<br/>big Battle at Leus, Can hear guns here 25 miles away<br/>expect my leave next week. <u>Perhaps</u><br/>Pay. 11/9 &pound;27.18.0.</p>
<p>Tuesday 3/7/17<br/>Hot &amp; sultry more rain about, Boys Broke pretty quiet at night now.<br/>C. Coy lost competition, D Coy's stunt Tomorrow<br/>going into line on Saturday might have known it as its my leave next week.<br/>Pay. 12/9. &pound;27.19.0</p>
<p>Wednesday 4/7/17/<br/>Thunderstorm about 3 this morning Cool &amp; like more rain.<br/>D. Coy 's stunt this Afternoon hope they win.<br/>Pay. 13/9. &pound;28-0-0<br/>wrote to Viv, Maude, &amp; Nell Monday.<br/>D.Coy. won competition great excitement C. Coy 2nd.<br/>rain to night</p>
<p>Thursday 5/7/17<br/>Cool &amp; fine &amp; cloudy.<br/>Major Woods went to Blighty last night.<br/>Pay 14/9. &pound;28.1.0</p>
