Dec 8/158.30 am writing this by aid of an inch of candle in an old mess hut.
We are to move off in a couple of hours to the Railway. There will be no sleep tonight as by the time we get to the station it will & load up horses & waggons on the train it will be 4 a.m.
We have a great camp fire roaring outside built up of old boxes & the accumulation of months of camp. The men are all sat round
