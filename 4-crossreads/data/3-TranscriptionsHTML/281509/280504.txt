<p>1916 October 8<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Usual morning service at 10 a.m. wrote letters in afternoon. &amp; after tea went to hear Rev. Guthrie good service.  Tony not turned up for some time. Rumour hath it we are to move on in a few days.  Sorry to leave this possie</p>
