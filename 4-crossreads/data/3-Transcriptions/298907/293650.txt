<p>&amp; called out she was going to send me down some cigarettes, I signalled all clear to her &amp; she came over to the brick wall which divides us &amp; let a big silken scarf down, I was too cunning to go &amp; get it myself for perhaps the old nark was watching our little by play so I sent a boy who is in the civilian hospital &amp; comes on to the green with us for it, on undoing the scarf I found it contained some packets of cigarettes &amp; a note, I took the note out &amp; scribbled a few lines on the back &amp; got old Beggs to throw it over later on which he did, so I am expecting to hear something tomorrow, all going well</p>
<p>Len &amp; I were out again this afternoon &amp; met a girl or two not bad sorts I think Len was</p>
