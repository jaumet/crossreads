<p><u>9th. Aust. Mach. Gun Corp</u> #60<br/>Intelligence report from 6am. 23/2/17 to 6a.m. 24/2/17.<br/>Indirect fire was carried out last night<br/>Gun at C16d 15/40 on <u>X ROADS</u> C18 d [6/5?] 2) <u>ROAD</u> C16 c 10/40 to C18 b 65/60 500 Rds 7 to 8pm.<br/>Gun at C 22 a [75/10?] on <u>BRUNE RUE</u> C 29 c 80/60 2) <u>X ROADS</u> C30a 10/80 3) <u>LA VENTURE RD</u> C 23d 50/10 to C 30 c 90/90<br/>Gun at C 28 c [61/46?] on <u>X ROADS</u> C 30 a 60/18 2) <u>ROAD</u> C23a 75/13 to C 23 d 40/45 3) <u>COMM TRENCH</u> C 23 a 30/06 to C 24 c 24/60. 2000 Rds 7 to 10.30<br/>Gun [firing?] from J Gap on <u>ENEMY WIRE</u> immediately in front 5250 Rds throughout night C29a 45/40 [to?] C29a 60/50<br/>Gun fired from <u>ENEMY WIRE</u> C 23 c 80/60 to C 23 c 90/00 throughout night 3000 Rds.<br/>Gun fired from Locality 13 on <u>ENEMY WIRE</u> &amp; <u>PARAPET</u> throughout night 1000 Rds. C 23 b 1/8<br/>Quiet during the day except for intermittent shelling in the Right &amp; occasional M.G. bursts on tram track &amp; roads<br/>Heavy bombardment by our Artillery at 10.30 pm. Enemy retaliation very small and was confined [briefly?] to front line. Enemy sent up 40 Red very lights &amp; 8 [puw?] - the latter bursting into 3.<br/>Light E. breeze- misty throughout day &amp; night.<br/>Visibility poor</p>
<p>D. Whitehead Lt.<br/>O.C. Adv Hqtrs<br/>9th A.M.G.</p>
