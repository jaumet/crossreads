<p>28 August 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Reveille 7 a.m. Gas helmet parade. Do a little fatigue. More mail, letters from G. Rob. &amp; paper slips from Janet Mac. Bob over for yarn.<br/>In afternoon Mac, Bob, Frank C &amp; self go into Poperinge 2 miles ride on motor trolley. Hop picking by convent women. Poperinge a big  town, semi-deserted by inhabitants, bombarded by Germans, St Kilco fromYpres. Visit shops lace, postcards, churches, market squares, Cinema, orchestra. Cartwright &amp; wedding presents. Enjoy outing. Home betimes.</p>
