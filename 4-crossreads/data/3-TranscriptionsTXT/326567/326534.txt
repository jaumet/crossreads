 placed in the Centre after they had been sewn on my Tunic I went along &amp; had Tea &amp; then into the Cinema Theatre to see the Pictures which were splendid &amp; was packed with Soldiers. When I arrived back at 9 Pm in Camp, my colours caused a stir &amp; I was  criticised  critisecized anyhow several wanted me to get them theirs. I turned into bed at 9.30 &amp; was reading when Fritz started sending over High Explosive shells close to the Camp, the first shell did not do any damage but the second shell hit a Motor  Transport  Wagon standing  on  near the Road &amp; set fire to the Petrol there was a great flare up &amp; the Petrol in the Tanks &amp; Supply Tins as it exploded it made the flames &amp; smoke shoot up in the Air; the Motor Wagon contained Cartridges &amp; as they exploded they made a loud report this kept up for about &frac12; an hour then all was quiet again altogether there were 2 wagons &amp; 1 Traction Engine destroyed, one man killed 4 seriously wounded
