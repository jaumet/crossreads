<p>and after our hard training we must have appeared very fit.  The following day an event took place which will long be remembered by the men of the 1st and 2nd Contingents. This was the burning of the Wazir a very evil neighbourhood in Cairo.  Another incident which the survivors of the original 6th  Battalion will chuckle over for a long time also <strike>b</strike> occurred at this period.  Our colonel, "Pompey" Elliott gave us a severe lecturing for appearing on parade some wearing hats, others with caps.  We were told to all appear in caps for the future and if we did not possess one we had to beg, borrow or steal one.  Accordingly next day we paraded in caps with the exception of "Pompey".
