<p>battleships steamed in, and 30 minutes later 5 T.B.D's arrived; these fresh arrivals rather congested the small bay.  All the ships showing no lights, only black masses were observable in the gloom.<br/><u>Saturday 15th May.</u>  One of the wounded mules died during last night, and as it was necessary that it should be buried a message was sent to the "Blenheim" asking instructions as to its disposal; the Commanding officer there replied that we should proceed to sea and bury it; the anchor was picked up and we steamed a couple of miles</p>
