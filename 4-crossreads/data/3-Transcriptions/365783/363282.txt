<p>Charlie promised to advance me any money I wanted but I do not think I will want any. I am sending by train to-day a parcel of books that I brought with me but will not require. I am going on to the physician side of the hospital, it is so long since I have done any surgery &amp; there are so many good surgeons with us that I thought I would be more fitted for the medical work. They are dividing the whole hospital staff just as in our city hospitals into physicians and surgeons.
