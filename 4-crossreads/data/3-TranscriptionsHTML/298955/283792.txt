<p>32 &ndash; E deM<br/>before the boat got where he was last seen<br/>We arrived at Gage Roads, Fremantle Western Australia at 2.30pm Monday 11th November 1918.<br/>The Armistice was signed that day &amp; it was flashed to us by lamp about 10.45 pm.<br/>Tuesday 12th All day not a soul came near the ship. At noon the men lowered the boats &amp; went ashore. All boats with the exception of two being taken, &amp; over 400 men went ashore.<br/>Wednesday 13th taken to Woodmans Point Quarantine Station  remained till Monday the 21st arrived back Fremantle Midday same day,.</p>
