
Thursday 6 May 1915Report re officers today At Abassia all day. Two patients admitted. Wrote letters Robt. Paton & Mrs Knowles & Mrs Fraser.Had meals with Captain Williams.
Friday 7 May 1915Christian Brothers school at Koranfish today 3 or 3-30p.m. At Abbassia D. B Hospital nearly all day waiting for patients.Meals with Captain Williams.
Saturday 8 May 1915
at A. D. B. Hospital all day Inspection by General [blank], Duke Prince of Battenburg, Marquis of Anglesey, Surgeon Gen. Ford, and others.In town at night. Club. Ruisall. and home.
Sunday 9 May 1915Mass at HeliopolisAt Mena House during afternoon.
