 I am very pleased & proud to learn that D.J's are doing the right thing by the Boys.  Of course after a while they will have to get rid of some of 'em because so many have gone to the bow-wows & will not leave drink alone.  Well, no Firm could carry on when so many of their staff failed to put in an appearance because of a night out.  We did not have any cases of the disease you wrote about.  I was gadding about the Country in the usual way all the time.
