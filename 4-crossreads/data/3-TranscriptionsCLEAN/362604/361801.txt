
8.12.18
Dear Dad, Mum & George,
I'm glad to hear that Mum was up & about again on 8/9/18.  One is lucky to get over that Influenza.  They have been dying like flies over here.  It is taking off as many as all the other ailments put together.  Ellis had a slight attack & I made Him go to Hospital also Ernie Thomas but Ellis is back again now.  E. Thomas will be back tomorrow.  I suppose Len Val. is correct when He says you want more money as a Sergeant.  You have
