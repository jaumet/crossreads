<p>From - Captain Travers. &nbsp;&nbsp;To - Brigadier &amp; Rear-Admiral C.</p>
<p><u>12th Septr. &#39;14:</u></p>
<p>Have just returned on board &quot;Warrego&quot;. Have brought ten white prisoners and comandeered motor lorry. Lieut. Bond has taken up strong defensive position&nbsp;at Wireless. I understand he has been ordered to withdraw, and motor lorry is returning for his stores, etc.</p>
<p>(1300)</p>
