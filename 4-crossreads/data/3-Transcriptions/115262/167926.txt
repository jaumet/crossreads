<p>Friday 8 June 1917.</p>
<p>Weather moderate &amp; a decent day at sea in a few ways. Duties on Isolation do not allow of parade etc. with rest of the unit.</p>
<p>Still unescorted. Attended N.C.O&#39;s lecture parade at 4 pm on boat deck. Had a look at negatives of one of the Ship&#39;s engineers,&nbsp;&ndash; negs of snaps taken on last trip to Engd &amp; air raid on Engsh. coast.</p>
