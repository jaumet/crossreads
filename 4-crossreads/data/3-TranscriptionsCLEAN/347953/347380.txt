The work is tiresome & very fatiguing & demands great strength and endurance. The heat of the sun is terrific &at times almost unbearable but as usual the night are delightful, and freshy. Light horse camps are situated at different points quite close to the firing line & so are always handy in case of an attack. The horses look well & fresh & seem to bear the weather admirably.
