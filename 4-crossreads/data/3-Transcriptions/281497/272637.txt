<p>There was an issue of clean shirts &amp; socks this evening, &amp; they were badly wanted for we were all as lousy as could be &amp; filthy dirty, for we had not had a change of clothing for nearly a month, &amp; our boots had not been off our feet for nearly 10 days</p>
