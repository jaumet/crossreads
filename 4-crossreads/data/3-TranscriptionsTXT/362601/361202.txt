 11 May, Sunday 
 F. We sit about all day &amp; look at Books all afternoon. I play Hymns all morning &amp; Auntie sees Septers tail go up. M &amp; I have to rush to stop her talking because the V. has come in quietly. 
 12 May, Monday 
 F. We all walk around lane by rush cutter's place &amp; round one of the hedge again. No good night &amp; I sleep ever sowell &amp; wake up very late, &amp; then, addition to the family &ndash; Septer's foal.
