<p>ashore for a few hours &ndash; Went ashore in lighters &amp; then marched up to the Barracks two or 3 hundred yards from the wharf where the YMCA had some dinner ready for us &ndash; a <strike>ramb</strike> rickshaw ride &amp; ramble round the shops in the afternoon &amp; the ladies of Colombo gave us all tea at the YM in in afternoon &ndash; I had to be back on board by 5 p.m. as I was on guard that night.</p>
<p>Coaling is nearly over &amp; I expect we will leave here some time this evening</p>
