 Monday 29/10/17&nbsp; Dressed in kilts, went shooting grouse, not easy to hit only got one 
 Tuesday 30/10/17. More shooting, left by car for Dunkeld then to Perth and got sleeper for London 
 Wednesday 31/10/17&nbsp; Arrived at Euston at 8.45 a.m&nbsp; caught train for Folkestone from Charing Cross at 11 am &amp; boat to [Bolongne?] [Boulogne] arriving at 4 p.m.&nbsp; met Gale. stayed night at Folkestone Hotel. 
 Thursday 1/11/17. Left Bologne [Boulogne] at 9.30 arrived at Ypres at 11 p.m. tired stayed night with Div.
