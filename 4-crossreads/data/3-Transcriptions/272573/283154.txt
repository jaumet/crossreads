<p>If the patient survives for 48 hours after he has partaken of this gas he usually recovers. To my mind the use of this gas in warfare is a cowardly game.</p>

<p>Feb 8th. Thurs.<br/>Great activity in the air on both sides at present.</p>
<p>Feb 9 Fri.<br/>Two Taubes which were up very high had a very rough reception. Hundreds of Anti-Air Craft Shells were sent up in the air after them</p>
<p>One of them seemed to be brought down but judging from his position he would probably drop into his own lines.</p>
<p>Rumour has it that all the Sisters attached to us are to go away.</p>
<p>Went out to Merville with W. I. As we were walking along the road we noticed a cat hanging up in a tree. It looked as if it had been after a bird but getting intangled amongst the branches it had practically hanged itself.</p>
