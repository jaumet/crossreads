  
 29 Sun April Rum Issue With General on joy ride  beautiful weather  Albo over about Sundown shot fired at him.  [Albo or Alba = Albatros] 
 30 Mon April Very quiet & beautiful weather light rain also Canteen grub.  An Albo. over with two Bristols in pursuit  Bridge on to night 
 1 Tues May Had a trip to Beach  gave horses a dip  very rough  Artillery active on both sides  also Albos. 
 2 Wed May Big guns going some  also Albos  very warm and Flies would carry a chap away
