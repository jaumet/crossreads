<p>a jerry to some of these gentlemen.<br/>Left Folkestone this afternoon &amp; marched through the streets to the wharf, the streets were very lively &amp; full of girls &amp; women down for the holidays I expect.<br/>Had to wait on the boat for a couple of hours while the rest of the drafts were embarking, we had quite a small fleet going over with us &amp; a small fleet of destroyers as escort, it was a very fine sight &amp; to make it better a big airship cruised nearly all the way over with us, she used to almost come within touching distance &amp; we could see everything as plain as anything, I wish I had my Kodak what a snap one could have got, an officer who was next to me had his but</p>
