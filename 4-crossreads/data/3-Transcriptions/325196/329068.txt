<p>27.12.15<br/>our old camp "the" Aerodrome" here tea was ready for us; Reid &amp; Francis raided one of the tucker boxes &amp; we had a snack &amp; turned in; It was sunrise this morning before we got up &amp; met plenty of old Regts lads, also Ally Hargreaves from N.mine &amp; heard from him that Geo.Young Roy Forsyth &amp; Jack Dugan are at Abbassiah with the infantry; It is also said that a Regt of Light Horse made up of Reinforcements left here mounted for the front a fortnight ago; Had a parade this morning only to be told by the Colonel that we had played the game in Gallipoli &amp; he hoped we would play it here &amp; said a percentage of us could get leave after dinner. I decided to visit the A.V.C &amp; see Arthur Welch; hunted him up. Had dinner with him &amp; then went to</p>
