<p>23<br/>to make ourselves at home here &amp; we have to move on. The little kiddies are a bosker lot lovely rosy cheeks.</p>
<p>Rode over &amp; saw Eric to-night the weather is glorious. I'm sitting on the window sill writing this by daylight 10 to 10 p.m. Our landlady gave us a bottle of Cider this morning it is A.1. Received 4 letters to-day 2 from home &amp; 1 Dorie 1 Trish. The home letters were lovely &amp; long.</p>
<p>5/7/16.<br/>Arrived safely here (Sailly) yesterday about dinner time. Are billeted in a bosker cottage &amp; have a long room with folding doors, reminded me of home somewhat. The folk are very nice &amp; seem fairly well to do. Sailly is a very dirty place &amp; shows signs of bombardment. Received 13 letters yesterday</p>
