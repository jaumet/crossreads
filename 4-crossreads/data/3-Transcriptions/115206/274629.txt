<p>February 1918<br/>Thursday 28</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Walked across to Tank &ndash;pos and spent day with Gun. Received a quantity of mail including the remains of a parcel passed through customs.<br/>Weather delightful. Great scare here, O.P. manned at 4<br/>am. Fritz had a stunt<br/>near Ypres.- no results to hand yet. Fritz bracketed our<br/>position <br/>with.5.9, and two 8 inch. He also bracketed the 53rd Bty.<br/>He also executed quite a considerable amount of<br/>registering on the road and <br/>Wewique. 1 Fritz plane down</p>
