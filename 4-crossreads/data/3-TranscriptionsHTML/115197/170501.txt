<p>April - 1919</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><u>April 1st</u></p>
<p>Light hearts &amp; good fellowship.&nbsp; Glorious weather which improves as we go South.</p>
<p>A long day in the train - Incident concerning a Chinamen - Arrive at Harfleur (Le Havre) at midnight.&nbsp; Sleep in the train.</p>
<p><u>April 2nd</u></p>
<p>Disentrain at 6 a.m.&nbsp; March to A.G.B.D.&nbsp; Meet old friends - Norman Frettell.&nbsp; Billy Dick.&nbsp; Disappointment through not seeing Herb</p>
