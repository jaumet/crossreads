  
 Thursday 28/2/18 heavy rain & wind all night, fine but cloudy to day sleet fell this morning much colder, Amos gone on leave deferred pay 44-9-0. Owe 3-18-11. letters from Viv & Syd wrote & posted one to Viv he is off to Aussie. Snow storm 2 pm fine & warm 4 pm what a climate. heavy Bombardment 8 pm. 
 Friday 1/3/18 Heavy firing all night. up at 5 to get packs for men going to school. Heavy frost last night fine to day. deferred pay. 44-10-6 Owe 3-16-11 Wrote to Mrs Parsons two days ago, heavy sleet at 12 now sun shining 12.30. getting very cold 
 Saturday 2/1/18 Bitterly cold just like Xmas time all water ice & it has been freezing all day 3 p.m. last night the huns came over 3 companies in 3 places but got blown to ribbons 59th took 6 prisoners 53rd one prisoner The Barrage was terrific & lasted an hour, have not heard anything like it since the Somme. deferred pay 44-12-0 owe 3-14-11. Just commencing to snow all white again  10th Bat. fell in lost 30 Prisoners R.S.M. & a Major. 
 Sunday 3/3/18 Horibly cold, ground white, wind from N.E not much doing last night. our chaps raiding tonight. Plenty going on leave asked if I wanted to go this morning but of course no money. deferred Pay. 44-13-6 Owe 3-12-11. Very dark & cold night. 
 Monday 4/3/18 Very cold again this morning more snow coming Raid successful took 3 Prisoners 2 of whom were killed on their way in by the huns one of ours killed
