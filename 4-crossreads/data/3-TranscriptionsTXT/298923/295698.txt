 1 February 1918 Friday (Whole page crossed out) Rode to Deiran &amp; had a hot bath &amp; attended Gas Lecture.  This country is so much better than Jerusalem was-   One here rides for miles between Orange &amp; Lemon groves. 3 Letters from Laura &amp; Papers oldest letter dated Dec 19 &amp; Jan 2nd /18  Also letter from May redirected from England &amp; addressed to  CAPTAIN  Weir dated 5/11/17 please thank her for me- One of Lauras was undated.  May has never addressed me as Major yet!!
