<p>Norman too and Basil would be fighters I am sure, and as for Jack, well he would just like to have a go at the Turks.</p>
<p>At present this so called active service seems to me a farce in the Training Battalion but a certain (to be welcomed) change may take place very soon</p>
<p>Mother send me a cutting about a great man which shows that the hour always brings the man.</p>
<p>I would like to have that mans chance, and may get it yet, who knows</p>
<p>There is life to me in their doing something which adds in, even a small way to the conduct of war against our enemy, much more so will it be when we are in action</p>
<p>You dear must keep well and dont worry, for our children are with you and I will come back full of life and merry as a sand boy</p>
