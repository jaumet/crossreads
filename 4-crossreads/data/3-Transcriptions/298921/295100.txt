<p>May, 1915<br/>Friday 7<br/>Still very sore at being left &ndash; I had actually stored my kit &amp; made every arrangement, now the Regiment is broken up &amp; the Light Horse Brigade also &amp; the Division too - <u>men are wanted badly</u>; but I cannot give details or this would never reach you.<br/>You ask re Adjutant's work, it is the mouth-piece of the Colonel, either Lt or Capt can do it generally Lieutenant in peace Capt in War. One does not get much promotion when left behind.</p>
