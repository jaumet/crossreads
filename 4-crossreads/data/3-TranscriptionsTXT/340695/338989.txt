
9-5-17 Had a glorious swim in the River Arvere this afternoon it was a treat. The part where we (Charlie Ferguson & another Sgt) went in ran through the grounds of an old Chateau it was beautifully picturesque, the fruit trees were all in bloom & the garden trees looked grand, an ideal spot.Received a letter from Eric saying he was booked for Australia lucky beggar.10-5-17. Left Albert for the North by Motor Lorry at 7 am & arrived here (Vieux Berguia) att 5 pm. A very pretty place, & hosts of nice girls & estaminets.16-5-17 Last night we (Coogan & myself) secured a tip top billet it was great between the sheets.
