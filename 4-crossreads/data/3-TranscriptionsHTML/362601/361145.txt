<p>17 January, Friday</p>
<p>F<br/>Go to Bulford by Car &amp; go to Dick's Room all night. We yarn &amp; sup &amp; go to mess. Afterwards I play Billiards a bit yarn with all the old Boys. We have a lovely walk Home in the moonlight. Recd letter from Manly Predday.</p>
<p>Send telegram to Auntie.</p>
<p>18 January Saturday</p>
<p>F<br/>Lovely day, we got to Bulford by Car so to mess &amp; Dick's room. E goes to Gibbys with Tap. I go to YWCA &amp; Post Office. So to meet Hilda &amp; Corrie &amp; get scaled. So back to Dance &amp; Dance with Dick Dolly Moore who I squeez &amp; also Dorrie Webster. We set out &amp; walk home. I laugh at Billy &amp; Ellis sliding on the frosty hill side.</p>
