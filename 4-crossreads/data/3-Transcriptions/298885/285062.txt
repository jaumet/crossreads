<p>Easter Mon. Apr. 24th -16<br/>German guns firing very heavy and set fire to a cottage &amp; causing a quantity of ammunition to explode.  Also air craft are taking advantage of first class weather and both sides having dinkum duels all day.</p>
<p>Sniper missed me again.</p>
<p>Tue. Apr. 25th -16<br/>Relieved from this post and proceeded back to battery and came straight on duty for 24 hours.  Anniversary of Gallipoli Landing.</p>
<p>Wed. Apr. 26th -16<br/>Enemy's guns very active</p>
