<p>Lt Moores Court Martial was also continued today; Jock Elliott (Head Qr Sig) was told to report to Major Lawry this morning to take over from Cpl Bassan; this morning the Sigs had station work &amp; this afternoon I gave them a lecture; A fatigue party has been busy today erecting a Sergeants Mess out of horse shades (canvas) &amp; a meeting of the Committee was held tonight to arrange rules etc.</p>
