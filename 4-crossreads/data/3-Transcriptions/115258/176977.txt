<p>On account of having no very strenuous work lately there have been some great old arguments going on in the troop - at breakfast the other morning there was a most heated discussion as to whether five past twelve (midnight) was tonight or <s>tomorrow</s> the morning. The Knut (butting in) &quot;But you said you were stripped&quot;. Teh Tar (decisively) &quot;What you want young feller-me-lad aint a yarn but a bloomin hargument&quot;!
