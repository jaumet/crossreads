
12.  SundayShowers in the morning.  Mrs. Nelson Smith & I went to hear Dr. Walter Walsh in Steinway Hall at 11.  Afternoon with Lady Byles & [indecipherable] peopleRang Agnes up at night
13. MondayHeavy showers.  Went to lunch at Grosvenor with Agnes & Melinda.  Then to Liberty's, Strand & Censor's office with Melinda.  Home to tea with Mrs. French
14 TuesdayWent to Dr. Spindlo at 11.30  Read "Loom of Youth" at Selfridges & at 2 went to lunch with Mrs Fisher.  She took Miss Mitchell & I to tea at Allan's
15. WednesdayFair day.  Went to meet Mrs. Meggitt but got in wrong train so returned & went to bed thoroughly exhausted.  Sun in afternoon.
