<p>B Section moved from Popes post to Mule Gully. Ken entering our dugout is hit by cap of 75</p>
<p>Thursday 18 bearing Russels Top Received orders to move off the peninsular<br/>Arrived Lemnos early morning &amp; found our camping ground at West Mudros late that afternoon. Stones in galore</p>
<p>Saturday 25th Christmas day received billies</p>
<p>Sat Jan 17 Visited [Forbianos] room of skeletons<br/>Monday 3rd Left Lemnos for Alexandria by S.S. Manitou<br/>Saturday 8th January arrived Alexandria.</p>
