Many were killed while going for water or even food &amp; I have known as many as forty to have been killed or wounded in one day. 
 Sat 29 May.1915.Quinns post blown up at 3 A.m. Our artillery did good work here &amp; together with our effective hand grenade attack the Turks were repulsed and 19 prisoners captured.
