<p>[Sunday after Ascension 4 June 1916]</p>
<p>Posted Notes today.  As Adjutant gone away sick have taken on Adjutant's job for the Reg. again<br/>What I would like now is 3 months leave &amp; go home but the War does not seem to change things much .<br/>[The rest of the page is crossed out but is totally illegible]</p>
