
27 April, Sunday
W & Snow
I go to church & the parson talks at me & tells me to get an idea. We all sir around fire all day & I sort my bags over again. I play cards & go to bed early.
We get no dinner.
28 April, Monday
F.I go to Bulford for Teeth. Tea at mess. Go to Sarum & spend [?] Home at No 13. I run all the way to train have to wait 30 minutes. Recd letters from Dick, Doris & Dad.
