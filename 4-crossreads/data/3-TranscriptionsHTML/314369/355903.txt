<p>12 October 1915<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Very quiet  &amp;  restful<br/>receive two parcels of chocolate through the post<br/>Interview Dental Surgeon to have my teeth repaired  &amp;  put in order while I have so good a chance.</p>
<p>1915 October 13<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Not much doing lie  &amp;  sit about all day   &amp;  read<br/>Write a couple of letters in evening.<br/>I am now practically right again my foot is completely healed  &amp;  I have no inconvenience from it at all</p>
<p>expect go back to duty shortly</p>
