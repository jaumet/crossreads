<p>Elizabeth St as I call it &amp; raised great clouds of muck. Butch and I went up to a little "possie" on the hill side to see it It then ranged the hill side &amp; gradually worked along to the big bluff where it put in it's last shot before it knocked off about 7 pm</p>
<p>18th We all slept in our bandoliers ready.</p>
<p>At about 1 am or moon set a terrific fire burst out on all fronts &amp; the din was awful This was kept up till near dawn &amp; in fact after twice we were marched up the road to the barricades &amp; back again. Saw many</p>
