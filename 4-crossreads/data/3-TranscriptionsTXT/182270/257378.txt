FRIDAY, MARCH, 1919.
7      1017
Fine & warm. With a following breeze
Played games & read all day. The
hold was open mos in the morning
& I got some cool clothes out.
Got didinfected in the afternoon.
Won the first round of deck quoits
& tennis on a forfeit. Lt Binnington
is my partner.
Days run 345 miles
