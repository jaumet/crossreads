<p>The menu was as follows 10 (five each) Fried Eggs &ndash; salad &ndash; bread &amp; butter tin of Herrings I had left over from my last parcel &ndash; Coffee &amp; then afterwards a bottle of champagne consumed while smoking some 10 for 2d cigarettes! Champagne &amp; Woodbines &amp; yet such is active service.</p>
<p>He is billetted at 'Jesus Farm' near 'Nieppe' about 5 miles on our left so yesterday afternoon got a permit &amp; rode over to see him &amp; after another tea &ndash; eggs as usual only boiled this time &ndash; we laid in a grass paddock &amp; smoked &amp; yarned of old times &amp; watched the aeroplanes &amp; shrapnel</p>
