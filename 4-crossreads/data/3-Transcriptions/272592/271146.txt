<p>I sent away &amp; registered today a parcel of curio's which I collected round "Pozerie's" the time of our terrific charge for it.</p>
<p>Some of our Machine gunner's are leaving for the trenches tonight</p>
<p>From where I am writing this I can see one of our aeroplanes thousands of feet high &amp; now &amp; again she presents a beautiful sight for she strikes the rays of the setting sun &amp; shines &amp; dazzles like a piece of mirror away up in the clouds, &amp; how gracefully she flits about, with a soft droning sound from her throttled</p>
