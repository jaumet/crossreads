<p>Octobre 7</p>
<p>Weather continues to be very cold &amp; wet. We went for a short route march this morning full marching order &ndash; otherwise resting. Expect to move up toward the Lines tomorrow.</p>
<p>Octobre 8<br/>Left camp at 10 a.m. &amp; marched to a camp near Onderdom where we are staying for the night. Expect to leave here in the morning. It is raining very heavily tonight.</p>
