
<p>MONDAY, JULY 7, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Drove down to Boundary</p>
<p>well with old Cy &amp; Tickle &amp; I were</p>
<p>trenching there till 3 pm. Then we</p>
<p>went up to Mountain &amp; shot some</p>
<p>parrots &amp; laid a trail for foxes. Percy</p>
<p>George came out in his car &amp; brought</p>
<p>my things. Mum &amp; Jo went over</p>
<p>to Coomoo Coomoo in the car.</p>
