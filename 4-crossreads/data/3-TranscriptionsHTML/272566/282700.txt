<p>Thursday 8 November</p>
<p>Fine all day &ndash; Showery towards evening.<br/>Run to A.D.M.S. at Scottish Lines Busseboom &ndash; calling in Poperinghe to book seats as I did yesterday.<br/>The D.D.M.S. Col. Manifold inspected our camp today accompanied by Sir Alex McCormack who is now Consulting Surgeon to A.I.F.  Previously he was Consulting Surgeon to the British Army.</p>
