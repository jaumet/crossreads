<p>untill 4.20 &amp; were told to make our dugouts  - in the side of the R-way cutting. Just got a start on them &amp; were stopped as a better place had been found. It was evidently where our chaps dug in the first night of the advance  Morrison &amp; I got a bit of trench about 10 feet long &amp; 1 foot wide cleaned it up &amp; lengthened it a bit &amp; covered it with iron Passed through 2 little villages today all battened up &amp; a railway dump where the Hun has left a lot of stuff. Saw 3 of our planes down also a damaged tank.</p>
