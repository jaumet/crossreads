<p>They used to hire donkeys &amp; organize races thro the streets.  They visited the Citadel &amp; exclaimed over the view.<br/>Wonderful things to see.  One place always intrigued our men on the way to Mena.<br/>4 wives &amp; 4 houses.  Many of the women still veiled &ndash; girls marry from 12 to 15, flies beggars, dirt, disease &amp; smell.<br/>But soon the delight in something strange dies out &amp; they realize that they are fighting to preserve their liberty &amp; our happiness &amp; well being &ndash; officers &amp; men soon spent a good deal of time studying books &amp; military methods.</p>
