<p>C/o Matron-in-Chief<br/>A.A.N.S.<br/>Horseferry Rd,<br/>Westminster<br/>London<br/>18.2.17</p>
<p>My dear Mother   &amp;  Sisters,</p>
<p>I'm in London once more, - We arrived here on Friday-evening after travelling about three days   &amp;  nights. Shall be able to tell you all about our journey better when I see you but goodness known when I shall be in Aust. again.</p>
<p>It is much warmer here   &amp;  we are staying at a very comfortable hotel. Three of us got our Leave, it came through in a hurry. Sorry I did not have more time to w rite to friends   &amp;  let them know I was getting over here.</p>
