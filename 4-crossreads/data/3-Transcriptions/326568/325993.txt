<p>January 9th  In Brussels to-day &amp; to-night &amp; have a good look round the city which is very fine "Thiar" at the opera was very nice.  Leave to-morrow afternoon.<br/>January 10th.  In Brussels unit 2 p.m. when leave for Charleroi arriving after a most awful Journey of six hours<br/>January 11th.  At Beisme again  More rumours about return to Australia on 27th.</p>
