<p>Thursday 30 November 1916<br/>Col. Fuller &amp; Major Cross over to Geclia to see Capt. of Camel Corps returned 5 p.m.<br/>I went to Hasinya to see Lieu. Britton, &amp; Col. Croll.  Returned with medical requirements for Capt. Beith.<br/>Taube over our Camp &amp; dropped 5 or 6 Bombs but did no damage.<br/>All forage &amp; Rations now arrive at midday instead of night time.</p>
<p>Friday 1 December 1916<br/>Col. Fuller rode over to Brigade.<br/>Major Cross on Court Martial at 7th R.<br/>Capt. Chisholm &amp; Beith to Mageibra.<br/>Weather getting very cold.<br/>All Horses in Regiments being clipped.<br/>Col. Fuller returned from Brigade after having Dinner with 7th Reg.<br/>Lieu. McKenzie went early this morning to Kantara to arrange about Remounts.</p>
<p>Saturday 2 December 1916<br/>Col. Fuller, Capts. Chisholm &amp; Beith rode over to Gamel to interview Major Wright of 5th Regt. who has a Squadron on outpost there.<br/>Fatigue parties are out collecting empty Bags lying about desert as Reg. is responsible for every Sack sent to them.<br/>Photos of S.M. Johnson's Grave in Cairo to hand showing Tombstone costing &amp;pound;625.<br/>Col. Fuller returned from Gamel at 12-30 midday.<br/>Infantry are coming through in large numbers &amp; concentrating at Maazar.<br/>Major Cross returned from 7th Regt. to-day from two Court Martial Cases.<br/>Lieu. McKenzie returned from Kantara &amp; arrangements are complete to send out 30 Remounts &amp; men.</p>
