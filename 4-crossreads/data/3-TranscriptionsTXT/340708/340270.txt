
July 1917Tuesday 17thA draft of P.B's arrived from England today an old cobber amongst them.  They will soon be running this Hospital with P.B.'s.   Another big Evacuation today they have turned this hospital into a C.C.S. nowWednesday 18thFritz paid us another visit but they only got to the outskirts of the town & our guns brought one down & the rest turned tail & went home.   Another big lot of wounded admitted today.Thursday 19th.Another miserable day this is a great country for quick changesFriday 20thMy hopes are dashed again   The C men were evacuated to England today & I am still left   The sore part is they are from 14th to 24th Reinfts that went so it does'nt seem fair
