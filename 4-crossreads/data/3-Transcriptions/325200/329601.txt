<p>- The anniversary of Threlfalls death by the way &ndash; the brigade went out accompanied by the Second Brigade.<br/>We reached Katia &ndash; by then occupied by our infantry at about 7 oclock waited there until 3 am &amp; then proceeded to Dubarbos direct where we left our base.<br/>Coming out we had occasion to notice the Turkish fortifications of Oghratina, also we could see the tracks of their howitzer battery which they brought to Katia  &amp; the manner in which they managed this  stupendous business.</p>
