 Tuesday 7 March 1916 Little rain in morning.  We made clothes to cover Drill &amp; chaff cutter out of wheat bags.  Cut 25 bags of chaff. 
 Wednesday 8 March 1916 Father went to Mudgee.  Mother &amp;  Geoff  I rode round Wonga &amp; School pdks.  Dick &amp; Geoff plowing.  I got Galina in from Castle Rock.
