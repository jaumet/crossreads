  
 F 5 Sun &Wet. Starts to rain at 5 pm. T Williams & I ride to Uparon. pass Enford  &  Dog & Gun [Ima?]. Visit Major Gib & his wife son in law Tea at Dunington & W. J porter visits us. Come home & clean up all &  Write to Mrs E. Con. Dad  &  Mum. 
 W 6 Mon Hard work fixing up B2 & clean up for my holiday. Ellis comes home. Tap   & I play cricket others have bath. Bert Jones comes down. We have big supper. We all talk about Bournemouth. 
 W 7 Tues. Ellis & I have a big talk. Go on with B2 ward. Pack bag while Tap writes others go to bed a little after 9 pm. 
 F 8 Wed Catch 8 am Train  & arrive London at Noon. Dine on Marylebone Station &  go on to Denham by 12.58 Train. Back to London at 4. See   [indecipherable] C.L. Jones. Look at old photo in office. Stay at 48A Lime St. 
 F 9 Th Arise at 6.30 am. Walk round town & go to Paradise St School of Anatomy at 10 am. Send writing [Tabadiary?] to N.Upton Have chase after Warrant. Meet two nice Ladies on "Tynwald" Calm Sea. Arrive at 4 pm. Meet Kenuish. M.H.K. Meet Marie & Aunt jennie in Dongles. Go up for Tea see Aunt Hannah & Young George Send letter to Ellis. 
 F 10 Fri Up early. We all go to Bally Kelly for Tea & see Mrs Brooks & Mrs Goldsmith. Go home round "The Len"  & Andreas' Music at night 
 F 11 Sat M & I go to Ramsay. Have afternoon Tea. Riding Home we have great fun chattering as we go along. I do not lose my tongue on this occasion. We have music & sit & talk at night.
