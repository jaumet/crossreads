  
 Wednesday 17 January 1917 [1918] 1/2 day & went with S. Steel to Mosque & Dead City.  Both places very impressive.  Mahomid [Muhammad or Mohammad] Ali was 2nd Khedive & was the one who gave a dinner to the ... & then slew all save one who sprang on his horse & jumped a great height to the open space below.  All this was 120 years ago in 1811.  Mahomid Ali then built for 25,000-0-0 this beautiful mosque - 2000 electric lights put by Kitchener, splendid carpets, & lovely alabaster pillars & concave nitche in wall.
