<p>Wednesday 13 September 1916<br/>Judging distance practice in <strike>afternoon</strike> morning, were payed after dinner.<br/>Bought some good photos of camp.</p>
<p>Thursday 14 September 1916<br/>Guard and out post training in morning.  In afternoon had inspection of saddles to see if all were fixed up for stunt.<br/>Wrote to Mother (18) &amp; sent home some photo cards.</p>
