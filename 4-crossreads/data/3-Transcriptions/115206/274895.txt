<p>November 1918.<br/>Thursday 21</p>
<p>Southall. Hospital<br/>Weather wet and miserable. Spent day at workshops on little jobs. Made a nice lighter.<br/>Met a parcel of letters and papers from home.<br/>Ran across to Wembley and saw O.J. surprising her very much. Roy accompanied me much to Mrs Jones pleasure. Olive look very queer and thin and I'm sure</p>
