<p>could see it quite plainly it looked like a bluish white cloud of smoke that kept on coming in billows some thing like waves roll in on a beach I just got my helmet on in time for I was beginning to stagger, my lungs seemed fit to burst &amp; they were beginning to burn like anything while my throat had a choking sensation in it just as if some one were throttling me I reckon a couple more mouthfuls &amp; I would have been a stiff one.<br/>2 chaps went out to it, I helped one chap to put his helmet on for he was nearly done, I think one chap will die alright from the effects of the rotten stuff, we were extraordinary lucky to get out alive, only presence of mind saved us for all concerned took it very cool indeed</p>
