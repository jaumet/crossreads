<p>November 1918.<br/>Monday 18</p>
<p>Hospital. Birmingham<br/>Weather fine and clear.<br/>Went up town with Sgt and had a look in at [indecipherable] photoplay. <br/>Had afternoon tea performed several shopping manouvres [indecipherable] not feeling too bright.<br/>That evening in bed and had a slight increase in temperature.</p>
