<p>1914<br/>Dec 31 Th<br/>Mess orderly for Guard<br/>Passed Laccadive Islands at 3 pm.<br/>Wrote letters in evening<br/>Officers &amp; nurses had fancy dress ball.<br/>End of the old year</p>
<p>1915<br/>Jan 1 Fri<br/>New Years Day<br/>Worked in office<br/>Whale sighted in distance<br/>Wrote several letters<br/>Feeling a bit off</p>
