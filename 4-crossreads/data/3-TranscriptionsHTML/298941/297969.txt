<p>Saturday 13th April 1918<br/>My teeth <strike>have been</strike> are supposed to have been fixed up, &amp; so any day may find me off. As a matter of fact Walter &amp; I were both put on draft a couple of days ago, &amp; after we had been paid on the strength of it, the discovery was made that no 1st Division men were needed.<br/>Whilst waiting for the train at Boulogne, we saw the transport of the 1st Field Ambulance go by</p>
