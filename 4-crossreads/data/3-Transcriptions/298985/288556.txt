<p>&amp; tracer bullets made a great display. It looked as if the Plain came down but we were not sure. Got home about 3.30 dead tired  A drink of tea was turned on</p>
<p>Saturday 14th<br/>Got orders to draw our Packs from Q.M. &amp; be ready to move straight after dinner Came back to where we left 2 days ago. It is a good camp no guns about &amp; only an odd shell comes back this way. It is more cumfie in the dug out tonight than falling over loose wire &amp; dodging about wet trenches has been the last 2 nights. Have quite</p>
