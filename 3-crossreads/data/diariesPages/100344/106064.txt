16th. Tuesday.
Glorious day. up 4am. caught train 5am arriving Bologne 11am. stayed till 7Pm at Rest camp (Wireless Stn) embarked 8.30 leaving about 9.15pm entrained at Folkestone 11pm. reaching Victoria Stn. about 1am.
17th. Wednesday.
Beautiful day. Stayed at Abbotsford Hotel, after visiting Horseferry Rd. where we were very well treated and advised. Bath got to bed 3am. Up at 10.30 busy with Photograph's shop all following 3 hrs caught 5pm. train for Eastleigh. 2 hrs run. stayed night.
