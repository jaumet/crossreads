<p>I sent my pay book away this morning to England to have it audited so as I can see how I stand.</p>
<p>I have just been thinking of the last artillery duel I saw &amp; that was the other day, the Germans began it on a lovely morning &amp; lobbed the first shells on to our trenches &amp; did not stop till they had got rid of 4 or 5000, &amp; I don't suppose 1% of them done any damage to either life or limb, in spite of the skill of the German gunners &amp; the accuracy of their guns, but the guns on both sides put you in mind of a choir, where one sings the other answer's &amp; away they go for all they are worth.</p>
