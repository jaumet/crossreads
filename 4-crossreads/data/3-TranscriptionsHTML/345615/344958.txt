<p>&amp; barb wire entanglements One of our guns let the enemy have a couple of dozen rounds this morning of course everyone turned out to watch the first few rounds &amp; the third shot hit the ground <strike>evidently</strike> about 200 yards in front of the gun mouth &amp; went ricocheting off into the distance.</p>
<p>Evidently a defective shell but it must have given some Indians camped close by a surprise.</p>
