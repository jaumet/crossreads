<p>At 8pm Gib. was obscured by the high hills on the South Coast of Spain past which we were then travelling.  Before leaving port the Skipper was provided with a secret code for <strike>use in</strike> wireless work, to be used in any communication reporting enemy war vessels or in case of distress.<br/><u>Tuesday 29th June 1915.</u>  Today we are wallowing in the long roll of the Atlantic.  About 5am we passed a hospital ship bound towards Gib.  During the day parts of the coast of Portugal could be seen.<br/><u>Wednesday 30th June 1915.</u>  Ship rolling very badly at times made me feel very ill at times especially after tea.<br/><u>Thursday 1st July 1915.</u>  About 9am we heard a Japanese steamer which was a couple of hundred miles ahead of us reporting by wireless</p>
