<p>I'm not a bit sorry because we are very quiet &amp; now that I have an orderly on at night, it is not so lonely. Also my best <u>beloved</u> is in Ismailia, since Friday, so I can go out with him every afternoon. Oh, I'm in love this time &amp; of course he says he is but it is all so sudden that I shall make it a little longer so that we may get to know each other better before I become engaged.</p>
