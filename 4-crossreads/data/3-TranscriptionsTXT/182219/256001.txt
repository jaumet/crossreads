I awoke with the din of falling rain &amp; was thankful for the shelter of above us but putting my hand out I discovered that the water had overflowed the trench &amp; was coming inside. I gathered up my ground sheet &amp; blankets &amp; awakened my mate, lighting up we found the water rapidly rising so I pitched camp on the table.
