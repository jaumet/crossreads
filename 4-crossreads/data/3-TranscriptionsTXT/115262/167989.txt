 Friday 10 August 1917. 
 Usual routine before breakfast &amp; after breakfast sadde cleaning &amp; watering camels. 
 Detailed for picquet for 24 hrs from 6 p.m. tonight. 
 Printed photos. 
 Posted as 2nd relief on guard from 8 till 10 pm. but managed to work it so that I could see the concert, which was a success too.
