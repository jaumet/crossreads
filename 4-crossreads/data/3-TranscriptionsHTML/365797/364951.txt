<p>We got your cable from France on the 29 July, &amp; last week your first letter from France arrived, dated 12 June &amp; postmarked "base" 19th, &amp; most welcome it was. We understand there may be more difficulty in the way of your writing now than there was in Egypt, but so long as we dont see the Rev. Campbell solemnly entering the gate we wont mind.</p>
<p>You are right up to the real thing now, after nearly 2 years trying for it, &amp; we know you will not be found wanting. Only dont give too many chances without reason.</p>
