<p>January, 1916<br/>Tuesday 4<br/>No news yet as to our probable movements.  Put in the afternoon repairing a watch in dad's workshop.  It rains all afternoon &amp; night &amp; the water penetrates through our flimsey wooden houses &amp; makes things very unpleasant.</p>
<p>Wednesday 5<br/>Write a letter home &amp; generally waste the morning.  Go on  a short route march with the Coy in the afternoon.  March through Matariah &amp; see one of the famous obelisks a small replica of Cleopatra's Needle.</p>
