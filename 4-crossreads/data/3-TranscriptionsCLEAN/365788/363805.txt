This afternoon I go & pay him a visit to get 2 teeth stopped with Amalgam They have broken away a lot, & are not front teeth. Did you know that Major Scobie had been killed? His men all thought very well of him. We have had some very cherry news from the Russian Front & from France lately. Very much love to you all Ever your loving brother Fred
