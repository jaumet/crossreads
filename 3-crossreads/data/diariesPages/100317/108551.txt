6/6/18
Early the Germans attempted a raid with 30 men against our post at the 28th Battn. front. Our people got right into them with Lewis Guns and left a few of the Huns lying dead upon the wire. Our loss was 3 slightly wounded and the Hun party was dispersed. On our right parapet lie the dead bodies of some of them ? one an officer wearing spurs and who by looks received nearly a whole bomb to himself. The object of the Hun is to find out who is opposite him. On the disastrous results he ought not to have many doubts! Some of our men when they first came to this area were lousy and unable to obtain clean clothes. So they put on the chemises and knickers they found in the women's rooms of deserted houses.
