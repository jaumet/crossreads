  
 France 15.4.17 
 Dear Mother and Father It is now some time since I sent my last letter home and I hope you have not been anxious about me as I have been fairly well all through this trip. Since coming back from hospital I have been in the line pretty constant and could not keep up my usual correspondence. I am pleased to say that lately I have been getting my letters regularly and so have been in touch with home. There are so many and so much news that I hardly know how to begin to answer them.
