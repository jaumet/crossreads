<p>A decent change here. No horses to look after and no pickets . A decent rest<br/>Friday Ap 19<br/>Stretcher drill. Words of command Lecture on Clerk's duties Discipline Etc.<br/>Saturday Ap 20<br/>Went for swim over to Bitter lakes. Water very salt but enjoyed ourselves. Evening pictures Ismalia.<br/>Sunday Ap 21<br/>Saw Stone to day who used to work at same shop as I.<br/>No parade after a church parade<br/>Monday Ap 22<br/>Words of command lecture on reading &amp; setting maps.</p>
