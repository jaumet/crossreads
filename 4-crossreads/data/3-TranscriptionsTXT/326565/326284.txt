 Monday 3/1/16 Very cold &amp; windy. Not feeling well enough to go on parade.  Ordered to hospital No 2 ward.  Small steamer with a number of C. Coy 29th Btn on board, sank in canal; equipment &amp; all lost, beside machine guns.  Skirmish at outpost a few hours after we were relieved 
 Tuesday 4/1/16 Very windy &amp; cold. Great amount of work being carried out on all parts of canal.  Boat which sank yesterday, raised &amp; taken away to-day 
 Wednesday 5/1/16 Very wild &amp; cold day.  Rained again
