The "Hymettus" which was hard aground when we left Mudros, was towed off soon after we left and brought her troops on here.<br/><u>Tuesday 4th May.</u><br/>The salvo of foreign shells was so warm this morning that the water tank, and ourselves were compelled to shift our anchorage; soon after we left a shell fell into the water at the spot we had been anchored.
