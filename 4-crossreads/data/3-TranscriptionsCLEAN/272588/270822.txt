
Wednesday 25 DecemberXmas day passed quietly.  We had much to eat & a little to drink, but everything was very orderly. As a matter of act, I have a touch of flu.
Thursday 26 DecemberJoy! A return has been asked of all men who left Australia in 1915 & C.O.'s have been warned to be prepared to lose these men at any moment. Admitted to Rest Station with flu.
