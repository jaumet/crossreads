<p>1917 FEBRUARY</p>
<p>3 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; Sunny. Had a full dress</p>
<p>parade (dismounted) in morning</p>
<p>also inspection, horses, harness ect</p>
<p>&amp; exercising horses in afternoon</p>
<p>Capt Playfair inspected all</p>
<p>over again in afternoon. I did</p>
<p>a shift of horse picket for Bill</p>
<p>Constable at night. I got letters</p>
<p>from home &amp; Linda, also</p>
<p>parcels from Mother, Aunty</p>
<p>Florence &amp; Linda.</p>
