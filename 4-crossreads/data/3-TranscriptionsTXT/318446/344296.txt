
8th MayThey did bomb us last night & made a very protracted affair of it too, dividing their attention between us & Khan Yunis & coming over first at about 9 p.m. & then about 2 or 3 a.m.  Now we lie in bed in the spirit of Kismet & hope that they won't lob one too near.
9th MayMist last night & the taubes did the same.  Oooooh.
Deir el Bellah15th MayNothing at all has been doing of late.  Guns are heard of course at all hours of the day & night but lately they have slackened off a great deal.The Taubes have not been over again.We call our home here "Avilion".  Certainly it lacks the three Queens with crowns of gold & also fortunately we are not here for the specific purpose of healing a grievous wound.  The "Bowery hollows crowned with summer sea" [Morte d'Arthur]
