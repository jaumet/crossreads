<p>Thursday Feb 14<br/>Jacko over today Football match between Auckland v Div Team also 1st Reg v 6th Reg Inspected as usual.<br/>Friday Feb 15<br/>Sent to orange orchards in district to find number of wells &amp; condition same. Rest NZ Brigade moving tomorrow<br/>Saturday Feb 16<br/>Sent some Maltese Lace home to day. Fine day NZ. Moved out for Jericho 1st Brigade moving tomorrow. Reported Australian Mail sunk. NZ Band played at Richon. Harrison transferred 2ND  L.H.F.A.</p>
