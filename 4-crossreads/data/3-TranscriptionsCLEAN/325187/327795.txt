
[Saturday 19 February 1916]
Posted yesterday- Today Recd Mothers Joes & Daisys Jan 16. [indecipherable] Seidleitz Powders & Soks please thank him- Also letter from Maurie Phillips.  Thought old "TESS" would fail some day, a good lucerne paddock is what she wants now. Glad German measles finish.Smithy is at Helman Zeitoun, he wrote me.Dined with Mohammed  Pasha Abdullah beautiful carpets, China, ( 9 courses,)  He owns many wives & much land.  Pictures of the Koran worked in silk with immense Gilt frames, also an atrocious silk Cat, done by one of the Harem.Many Bells, & Mirrors, fans & much finery- but we never mention the wives or even see them
