
4 February, Tuesday
F & WGo to Dance & have a low time. We have supper all to-gether & New Zealander invites himself in [indecipherable] game to invite Him out. I butt in & take Dolly Home. She is very nice & invites me down next week. I get pushed by Judy, the 'orrid tabby that I don't like.
5 February, Wednesday
W & Snow all morning.
Go to B'ford in motor with Fitz Hill. Buy dinner rice cakes at Ashornes, sugar etc supplied. Go to close stay till 7 pm. Go to No 13 & have nice musical evening. Miss the Ball but not Army. Have a bed mate but don't see Him. Marion is engaged. I take Winnie Home & get roused on for being late.
Recd letter from Auntie Annie.
