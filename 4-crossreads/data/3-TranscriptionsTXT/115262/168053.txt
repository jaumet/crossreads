
Saturday 13 October 1917.
Full dress parade with camels & ourselves in full marching order. Inspection by O.C.
"Jacko" has not been over for a day or so.
Had a narrow escape from being killed tonight at the aerodrome after having seen through the R.F.C. section of it.
A.C.F.A Football team played A.F.C on Aerodrome football ground & were beaten in the last quarter.
