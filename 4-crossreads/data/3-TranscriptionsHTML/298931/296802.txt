<p>Monday 16<br/>Arrived at St Omer last night and marched to a village named Whisque, and made our home at a monastary<br/>Tuesday 17<br/>The monastry is a fine building. 4 stories and situated in a nice park, bands ect very pleasent<br/>Wednesday 18<br/>12 months ago today I joined the army. 365 days of pleasent cruel wierd &amp; wonderful experiences. Still going strong<br/>Thursday 19<br/>Sunshine &amp; shadows the way of life, yesterday a day of sore feet, to day OK [indecipherable]</p>
