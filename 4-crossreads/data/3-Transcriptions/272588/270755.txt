<p>Saturday 17 August<br/>Indulging in early morning swims in the Somme. A walk along the river to Sailly le Sec which is badly battered about. Fine &amp; warm.</p>
<p>Sunday 18 August<br/>Gloriously fine weather continues but everything is very quiet. News is scarce.</p>
