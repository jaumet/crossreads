<p>Mrs. Sanders, Eileen, very good gifts including sweets, books, underwear, soap &amp; hdkfs.  Action quiet all day &amp; night.</p>
<p>Nov. 8th Monday<br/>Very much colder &amp; duller.  Front quiet.</p>
<p>Nov. 9th Tuesday<br/>Weather fair.  Front quiet.  Naval small craft active especially about midday.  Cruisers firing heavy broadsides during afternoon onto Gun ridge.</p>
<p>Nov. 10th Wednesday<br/>Rain threatening.  Turks busy with hand bombs about 10 a.m. on Posts 3 &amp; 6, quietened by Garland &amp; Catapult.</p>
