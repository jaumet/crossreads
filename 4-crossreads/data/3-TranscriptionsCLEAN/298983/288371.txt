  
 4 P.M. Did not have our packs on. Am in A Co & had to carry our packs about a mile to there lines It was the last straw Got a plentifull issue of good tea & made an execelent meal Did not feel the march much to day Crossed into Belgum about 5 miles back Came through one village that has been completely smashed up. The weather is beautifull & quite warm
