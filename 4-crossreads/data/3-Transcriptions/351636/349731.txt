<p>Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers<br/>2-7.16</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton &ndash;<br/>Can you give Mrs. Meagher some temporary assistance her case is known to you as you formerly assisted her, &amp; when she thought she could do without it, gave up the 6/6 per month you gave her, but at present is in great difficulties which she can explain</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>E. H. Bennie.</p>
