9 th. This morning we marched to Heilly had a beautiful hot bath ? what a relief it was to get our chatty clothes off into new ones we felt like new men once more, the NCOs had a separate place set apart for them here we had our bath in big wooden tubs, afterwards we all got a complete change of clothes socks underpants shirts.
