<p>went to bed early next morning got up about 10.30 had breakfast and went over the Horseferry Rd but could see nobody there I knew so after having some coffee and sandwiches at the Anzac buffet there all free of charge to Aust Soldiers I got another ticket for a matinee same afternoon to see Oscar Asche and Lily Brayton at the Appolo theatre.  And stayed at Peel House for the rest of me furlough had a good time .  visiting theatres in stalls all free of charge.  went skating at brixton often.  Also the Strand, Piccadilly, Trafalgar Square, Leicster square.  up every morning about 10 and was quite handy to the Anzac Buffet used to go there every morning first.  it was a great meeting place for Australians marvellous who</p>
