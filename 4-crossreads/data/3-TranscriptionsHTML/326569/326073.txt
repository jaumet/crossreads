<p>Bargate &amp; on way called at a caf&eacute; &amp; had coffee &amp; cake &ndash; the coffee was beautiful.  After looking round S'Hmpton [Southampton] caught boat there at 2.10 p.m. for Cowes, arriving 3.5 p.m.  Caught train 3.45 p.m. &amp; arrived Newport about 4 p.m. thence walked with prisoners to the military gaol at Parkhurst about 1&frac12; miles.  After handing over hurried back but just missed train connecting with last boat back, so had good tea in Newport &amp; a shave then caught 6.45 p.m. train to Cowes &amp; put up at George Hotel for the night.  Walked around promenade before going to bed &amp; enjoyed ourselves pretty well.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 26/6/17<br/>Up 6.30 a.m. after real good night's sleep &amp; caught 7.15 a.m.</p>
