<p>60 Jack tars time expired sailors of the navel station</p>
<p>the cheese went Bad so their was a mock funeral formed up with the cheese carried on a dish covered with the union Jack the dead march played on an accordion marched 2 deep through the meddle of the ship along the top Back again was</p>
<p>slided into the atlantic with the last post on the Bugle</p>
