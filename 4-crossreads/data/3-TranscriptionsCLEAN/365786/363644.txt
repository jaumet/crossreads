When a man is wounded he is first attended to on the spot by a doctor if one is available or the A.M.C. orderlies or his own mates & hurried back as soon as possible to the nearest clearing station or Field Ambulance.  These are like small base hospitals & there urgent work is done.  Limbs needing immediate amputation are removed etc. & from there the men are were transferred to the Hospital ships & removed to the Base General Hospitals at the Base
