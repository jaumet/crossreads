<p>Bgd's Ambs.  &amp;  brought them on here to Nequilliet or Kilo 60, a distance of about  14-15 miles reaching here about 6 am. Rather an unpleasant ride owing to weariness; for the first time I was unable to stop falling asleep in the saddle. The first time  &amp;  fell asleep I landed up against a wagon wheel, the second I got nearly kicked by a draught horse. The third time I was soundly wrapped in pleasant little dreams when my cussed little pony took me right ahead alongside of one worthy Colonel  &amp;  Major  &amp;  my innocent slumber was broken by bitter words from the latter.</p>
