<p>13 January 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Work as usual.<br/>Go down to Fairfield to meet Mrs. Coleman. Have tea at Bloomfields.  Back to camp early.  Lecture by Capt. Willis on Preparing patient for Operation.</p>
<p>Received letter from Jack Martin from the Front.</p>
