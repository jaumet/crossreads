<p>Sunday 2 December 1917.</p>
<p>Shelled again today but only for 1/2 hr. [Rumored that our 60 pounders got 3 direct hits on one of Jacko&#39;s long range guns] &nbsp;These big guns of the enemy are run on rails backwards &amp; forwards thus making it impossible for artillery fire to be effective on them, So the report goes?</p>
