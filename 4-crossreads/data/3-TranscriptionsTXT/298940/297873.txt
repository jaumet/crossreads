 I went ahead to pick out the bad points of the track through the first part; which is a dip with duckboards raised a foot above the bottom, leaving a space at the sides. It was marvellous to me that I should be able to go ahead and pick out the way, while Ash &amp; Farred were floundering along behind, treading over the edge of the duckboards as often as on it, &amp; causing Stafford &amp; Kollosche, who were bearing, to laugh aloud by their stumbling &amp; smothered exclamations.
