 1917 FEBRUARY 
 3 SATURDAY 
 Fine & Sunny. Had a full dress 
 parade (dismounted) in morning 
 also inspection, horses, harness ect 
 & exercising horses in afternoon 
 Capt Playfair inspected all 
 over again in afternoon. I did 
 a shift of horse picket for Bill 
 Constable at night. I got letters 
 from home & Linda, also 
 parcels from Mother, Aunty 
 Florence & Linda.
