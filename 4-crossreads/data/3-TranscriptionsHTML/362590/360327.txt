<p>4.<br/>his first pay, &amp; 6d each pay after, so we ought to start with about &pound;15 in hand. But somebody else will have the running of it now, just when everything is nicely started, &amp; running so smoothly.</p>
<p>Since I have been in camp this last six months I have learnt two things :- firstly that it seems to be the duty of all military authorities to put a damper on keenness &amp; enthusiasm, &amp; secondly that if you have the misfortune to fall in love with a company, the eagle eyes of the authorities will spy you out, &amp; refuse to allow you have anything more to do with it.</p>
<p>The fact that I have already passed two schools of instruction seems something more to my detriment than in my favour.</p>
