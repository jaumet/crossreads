 1916&nbsp;&nbsp; MONDAY 21&nbsp;&nbsp; AUGUST 
 Fine &amp; warm. We were all innoculated for para-typhoid in the morning &amp; out signalling in afternoon&nbsp; We should have had a lamp parade in evening but the fellows were pretty bad from the innoculation so we did not have it. There was three batteries of 6 in howitzers firing just below our huts all day
