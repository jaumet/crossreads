<p>Monday 15 July<br/>Up at good time. Camp dismantled &amp; packed. Limber returned by D.A. (went forward early) 9 a.m. packed also.</p>
<p>Swim after hot work of cleaning up. Light lunch about 11-15 finished &amp; moved off from Glisy about 12.10 p.m. Arrived Buzzy 1-15 p.m. Arranged stables etc.</p>
<p>With the Coy again.</p>
