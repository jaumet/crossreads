In fact he's just a marvellous case &ndash; Meningitis is a fearful thing and very infectious. They have had a good many deaths here and are very strictly isolating cases.<br/>Palmer did not show any infectious germs in the serum we took from his spine at Lemnos. He was an interesting case being sent into my ward as a contact more than anything else &ndash; and I saw the early stage of the disease. I had just nursed a very bad case for three days and he had been transfered <br/>to an isolation ward &ndash; so my ward was full of what are called contacts. But the disease did not develop there.
