<p>31-3-17<br/>who won with Wheeler (Field Amb) second there were 18 starters; The wrestling on horse back &amp; obstacle race was very good, also the high jump, but only members of the Field Ambulance were allowed to compete in these. I have been very stiff &amp; sore today, &amp; fearing I have rheumatism I have been doping myself up with Sodical today</p>
<p>1-4-17<br/>Sun 1st<br/>Church parade this morning but I did not attend; &amp; an inspection of the lines by the C.O.; This afternoon we played B Sqds (No.2 team) football &amp; scored a win 5nil; No 1 team of A Sqdn defeated No 2 of C Sqdn</p>
