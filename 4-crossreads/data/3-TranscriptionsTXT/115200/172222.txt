
9)     Dead mans ridge
see one chap in particular crawling up to our trenches with Bombs. I jumped into the trench and fired. I Don't think that chap will tell any more tales he was no more than 10 yards from our trench. I reported to the Seargent that The Enemy were coming up  But he would have that they were our own men. any how it was not long before every rifle in that trench was [Speaking?] The attack had Been repelled.
