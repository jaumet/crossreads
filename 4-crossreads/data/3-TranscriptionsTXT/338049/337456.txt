 Tuesday 3 Sept. Things easy &ndash; Afternoon suddenly recalled to Suzanne. So packed up &amp; off. 
 Arrived, fixed tent, cleaned busied to show opposite D.HQ. Saw Press Representatives, German Prisoners goose-stepped past HQ. Visited vault desecrated by Fritz. 
 Then to Cooees 7.30. Prisoners Pioneers busy all day on building.
