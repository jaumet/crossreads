On Xmas eve we left Boulogne by train. The day was cold so weas there were only three of us we constructed a stove in the carriage. We arrived at Cambrai early on Xmas morning breakfasted at the club. After this we set out for Catellon, were lucky enough to secure a French lorry to Caudry. It was a beautiful morning, bright frosty. Our next left was to Le Cateau here we had our Xmas dinner of bread butter at the corner of the street on the road to Cantillon.
