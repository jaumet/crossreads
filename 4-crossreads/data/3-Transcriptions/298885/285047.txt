<p>Thur. Feb. 10th -16<br/>Made permanent brakesman to C.I. team.</p>
<p>Fri. Feb. 11th -16<br/>Accompanied C.I. team to Abbehsiah, Waila, and Slave markets for break.</p>
<p>Sat. Feb. 12th -16<br/>First lesson at riding school.  "Some Circus".</p>
<p>Sun. Feb. 13th -16<br/>Church Service 7.45 A.M.  Received first mail since leaving Fremantle and feel quite excited.</p>
<p>Mon. Feb. 14th -16<br/>Inspection of D.A.C. by Colonel Hobbs.  Weather very cold and windy with clouds of dust.</p>
<p>Tues. Feb. 15th -16<br/>Received first instruction</p>
