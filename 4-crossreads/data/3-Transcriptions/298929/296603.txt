<p>The Germans have taken all our old fronts, Armentieres, Neuve Chapelle, Estairs, Fleurbais, Jesus Farm, Rossignol, Canteen Corner, Steenwerck.<br/>Went into Gentilles and dodged round the shells looking for pomme-de-terres but got none<br/>Saw 2 planes brought down.</p>
<p>14th  Shifted to woods nr Boves, late in the evening and erected some sort of a shelter in the dark and rain.  It sufficed</p>
