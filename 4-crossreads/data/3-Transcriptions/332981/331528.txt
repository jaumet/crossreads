<p>The picture was a noble one as the great vessels ploughed their way to take part in the surrender of the Hun boats, and soon after they started paravanes were dropped overboard. These devices are shaped like tops and divert any mines which may be encountered, for we were now entering a minefield. Almost everyone on board donned a lifebelt, and just as the red sun appeared above the horizon the first U Boast appeared in sight.</p>
