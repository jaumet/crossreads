<p>34<br/>the day in the garden it was great.</p>
<p>Last night we sat out in front with the Adjutant, Doctor &amp; Vet &amp; had a real good yarn. They are 3 of the best especially the Adjutant (Capt Glasgow). The Dr shouted two bottles of beer. It is going to be another fine day to-day.</p>
<p>5/8/16.<br/>Still at Rubempr&eacute; but expect to move in a day or so. Our Division is about to take a place in the front line of trenches, we've just received word from the General.</p>
<p>I saw in orders the other night the sad fate of Claud Boone my old Rowing Club skipper. He had just joined his Battalion as a second Lieut. He was one of the finest chaps it has been</p>
