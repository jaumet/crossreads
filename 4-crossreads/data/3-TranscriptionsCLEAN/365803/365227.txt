  
 At 7, Herbert Crescent London S.W. 1.7.16 
 Dear Judge Ferguson, 
 Nothing I can  say will be of any comfort to you but I must write to tell you what we have lost in Arthur - if we had only had more officers like him things would be much brighter and better than they are now.
