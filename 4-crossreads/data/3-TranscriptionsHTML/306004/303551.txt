<p>5.10.16<br/>about 8 miles out of Alexandria.  went to a French Restaurant for dinner &amp; put in the evening looking around Alexandria in a carriage. Lindsell &amp; Laney were with me. we arrived back in Camp at 10 PM</p>
<p>6.10.16<br/>Fri 6th<br/>Reveille at 6.30 had a shave, breakfast at 7 oclock then a swim in the surf. were warned not to go in the surf when black flags are flying as the&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   surf is then considered dangerous. the black flags were flying this morning as the sea was a little rough &amp; its was lovely on the surf we were not long in before an English Officer</p>
