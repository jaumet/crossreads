 30 July 1915 Friday 
 Not much better this morning but get over the issue of rations all right Lie down nearly all day &amp; feel better in the evening Get orders to supply several Guards &amp; fatigue parties for tomorrow. "C" coy finding   Bgde   duties Rum 6 pm. 
 1915 July 31 
 Guards go on at 8 am &amp;  so relieve me of my worry for the day. I go  &amp;  have a look at the new 60 pounder field guns landed during the night  &amp;  apparently intended for the big general advance to take place shortly
