<p>15th Dec.<br/>5AM. Captured French Barque "Marshall Daveout of Nantes sailing to Dacar with wheat. Wireless &amp; 2 3in guns aboard. Took off crew &amp; provisions. 1 pm sank her &amp; proceeded.<br/>16th Dec<br/>Seaplane taken on deck again &amp; made flight at 5 pm.<br/>17th Dec<br/>Seaplane up twice, morning &amp; evening</p>
