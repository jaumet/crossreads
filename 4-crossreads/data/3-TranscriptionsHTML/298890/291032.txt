<p>22 Sunday<br/>At 13 Temple Fortune Court.  Went to Mrs Aldridge to tea.  Met Mrs. Burnham  Took on  a job with Mr. Aldridge.  Got back at 8.30</p>
<p>23 Monday<br/>Went to work at 41 Russell Sq.  Lunch at Minerva.</p>
<p>24 Tuesday<br/>Same as above but went to meeting of teachers in Memorial Hall for Nina Boyle</p>
<p>25 Wednesday<br/>Same as above  home rather late</p>
