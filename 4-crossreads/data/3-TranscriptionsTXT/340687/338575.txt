 September, 1917.  Friday 28  27 for scabies &amp; vermin &ndash; and came back and got our passes. Supposed to get train at 1.30 at Poperinghe. Decided to wait till morning as not much chance of catching train. Train at 10.30 to Boulogne. Bed about 11. 
 Friday 28  Saturday  Got up at 4 with rest and after breakfast shaved and got kit together. Went out with Sharp on limber at 7 and had a pleasant ride on a beer barrel. Tried to go by 10.30 but nothing doing. Went to Talbot house and had a look round Poperinghe. Went to a concert troupe Wanny Wans at 6 and enjoyed show.
