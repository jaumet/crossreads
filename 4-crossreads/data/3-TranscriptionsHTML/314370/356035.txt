<p>May, 1916<br/>Wednesday 31<br/>Half holiday today<br/>Too hot &amp; lazy to do anything except try to sleep which is almost impossible.<br/>After tea I went to Cairo for a change of air but came home early.</p>
<p>June, 1916<br/>Thursday 1<br/>Weather continues stifling &amp; oppressive &amp; would bring a thunder storm every day under the same conditions in Aust.  But it never thunders here.<br/>Rumours are rife about all Australians leaving at the end of the course</p>
