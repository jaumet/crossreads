<p>July 18th &ndash; 17<br/>From Staples the signallers had to leave at 2AM and proceed to our destination while the remainder were making a couple more stops</p>
<p>We eventually arrived at the end of our trip landing in Reninghelst at 9AM. This trip was very nice and gave us a splendid chance to see some of the country. The reason for so many stops was for the horses to have plenty of rest.
