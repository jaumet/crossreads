<p>7 October 1916 Saturday<br/>Theatre Laura<br/>Brighter &amp; warmer today-   Rupert Mathews now a Captain   Connie Tyron, Dorothy myself &amp; Laura all going to a "Professor's Romance" tonight-<br/>Last night Dolly &amp; I dined with W. <strike>Fer</strike> Neill &amp; he gave me back the medals I won at the Hay Show with the Mulberrygong Sheep.</p>
