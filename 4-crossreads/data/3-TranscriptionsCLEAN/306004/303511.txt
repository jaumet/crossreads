  
 4.9.16 time as he came down today, we had our photos taken separately & then in a group; Then we drove down to the Beach & put in an hour or two there, snapped a Soudanese Coast guard, had refreshments & then back for tea; I forgot to say yesterday afternoon we put in a hour looking over the Jupiter (warboat);  After dinner tonight we took a walk around the town & bought a melon (sugar) & at 9.30 went to the Cinema, & the  pictures were very good & were going till 11.30, we then made back to out room made a hole in the melon & were ready for bed by midnight; Everything is very dear here 3/- for bed & 3/- for each meal
