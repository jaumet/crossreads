<p>Aug 31 forth from scores of funnels &amp; chimney stacks &ndash; hiding the greater part of the city. Our decks are literally smothered in coal dust, the result of coaling by the <u>Kaffirs</u>. They're K'Nuts too some of these blacks, &amp; dearly love anything gaudy or jingly.<br/>Recd a telewire from Hilda at 10 am, &amp; we sailed at 12.30. It's a glorious sight, leaving the harbour as peak after peak &amp; scene upon scene break into view. Too many niggers in this country. As we passed through the breakwaters there were hundreds of em' lined up</p>
