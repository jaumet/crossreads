 playing billiards we went to the Empire Club once more &amp; had tea &amp; out on to the breakwater again, met the old gentleman fishing again &amp; came back to town with him, &amp; had a drink of coffee, arrived at the Continental at 7 oclock met Morgan &amp; his friends &amp; put in till 9.30 walking about the town seeing where the bombs were dropped during the air raid on Port Said &amp; eating fruit which is very cheap at the markets; at 9.30 Mills &amp; Morgans friends decided to see the late picture show as a new programme was on &amp; Morgan &amp; I had a cup of coffee &amp; then went back to barracks where he is stationed. Morgan then came back to the Continental with me; Morgan looks better than I have ever seen him looking &amp; is trying to get in a L.H. Regt.
