 [This page appears to contain rough notes relating to the respective dates.  They have been noted in date order &ndash; not quite as they appear on the page] Sun. 9.   Writing in morning.   Walk with Alan afternoon &amp; evening along canal &amp; round.   tea at St. Omer. 
 Mon. 10 Dental pde.   Pde 11.30 A.M. &amp; Stunt shortly to come on explained to us.   Details  for not wri  lost not written. 
 Tues  11. Up at 2 A.M. to go through the stages entailed in the stunt.   General rise of spirit in anticipation of coming event, which will be a big thing. 
 Wed.  12.   Pack up &amp; march Arques to Steenvoorde &amp; sleep in barn on hay, walk in evening. 
 Thur.  13.   up at 5.30.   march in battle order  leave pack &amp; march two kilos past Dickebusch in  Belgium  &amp; abt 6 miles from line   Sleep in building, no room in tents. 
 Fri.  14. 
 House too far from rest of Coy. canvas humpy [diagram] to big  wire stilts &amp; canvas  few shells about 
 Sat. 15 Nothing to do all day.   About 15  Fritz planes  lively time, M.G.'s &amp; antis heavy bombardment all night. 
 Sun.  16. 
 very cold last night guns going all day long, heavy artillery duel for few hours in morning - 14 friz planes over, I gather dozen of planes, both sides, everywhere in clouds etc.  &amp; direct a  pay of 40 Fs.
