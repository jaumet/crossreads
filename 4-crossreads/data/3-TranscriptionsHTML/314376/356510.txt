<p>We were set to work digging the trench deeper.</p>
<p>Parts were very shallow and with the German snipers opposite ready at a moments notice to shoot, it is easy for anybody to see the necessity of the work. The time we started was 2 o'clock in the morning. There was an heavy bombardment going on all the time, but all fears were kept away by the work.</p>
<p>Pieces of shell were whizzing about us without however doing much damage.</p>
