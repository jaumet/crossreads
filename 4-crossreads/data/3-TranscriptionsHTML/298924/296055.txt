<p>Friday 14 March 1919<br/>Red Sea<br/>All ranks settling down, anchored off Suez to take in Water &amp; then pushed on.<br/>The Tucker is good &amp; the men are given all the Deck space there is.  I opened a Library &amp; issued canvas shoes to all ranks.<br/>The men have been issued with 200 Packs of Cards, draughts, Punching Balls, Boxing Gloves &amp; literature, &amp; only have the general inspection once a day.</p>
