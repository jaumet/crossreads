I think I will be dining with him tonight at Broadmeadows. He says that he will not be returning to Newcastle &amp; in this I think he is quite right. I hope to write again before leaving Melbourne. Our next call will be Fremantle. Goodbye dearest Mrs &amp; God bless you all. Let Father &amp; Mother see this letter. Your loving husband Will. Haven't seen Fred since Sunday.  W.H.R.
