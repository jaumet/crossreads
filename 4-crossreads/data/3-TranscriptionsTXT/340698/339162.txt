 18/11/16 Marched from Frimont to St Saveur. This is the best town we have ever been billetted in. 
 1/12/16 Left St Saveur at 2 a.m. and marched to Vignacourt. Entrained here and proceeded to Buire. Marched back to Ribemont about four kilos. We expect to remain here several days.
