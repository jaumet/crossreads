<p>22 June 1915<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Nothing to report<br/>Several men hit by shrapnel at ASC Deport in Rest Gully.<br/>A battery of the City of Glasgow Territorial Artillery land with 5 inch Howitzers</p>
<p>1915 June 23<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>Very easy day hardly any firing both parties seem to be resting on their laurels   &amp;  waiting for the other to make the next move<br/>Enemy fire a few shells over but do no damage</p>
