
June 1918Thursday 27
In Billets. ErronvilleWeather fine and clear. Had the usual daily routine with horse and bycycles also wire and phones ,Drifted along to see my friend Suzanne on the Hellencount Road.Worked for awhile at the farm, had a beautiful spread of roast fowl peas and potatoes, also strawberries and cream.Fritz  bombed Abbeville.
