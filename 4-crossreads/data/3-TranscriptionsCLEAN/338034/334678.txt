  
 Oct 16th :- In the afternoon Hind, O'Connor & Self went to Alexandria. Did some business in the afternoon & later on had a very good dinner & evening at the Windsor Hotel. Oct 22nd : Arrived back at Kantara after spending a very pleasant week at Alexandria Sidi Bishr. Nov 2nd :- The whole of the 1st A.L.H.
