<p>3.<br/>men regardless of danger (I know the sort of effect which it has upon me to see such men) &amp; you can be altogether too careful about shell fire. That's not a disease most of our officers suffer from; &amp; if I were asked to balance the loss with the gain I should say the balance was on the right side.</p>
<p>I saw Maughan once or twice (he was at Quinn's); also Manning who was buried by a shell in Lone Pine &amp; was at Malta when I saw him afterwards; he had a very bad go of jaundice thrown</p>
