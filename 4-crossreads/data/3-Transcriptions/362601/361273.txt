<p>27 September, Saturday</p>
<p>Miss boat by seconds. We start to paint. I go in for a swim &amp; the cold nearly freezes me but lovely afterwards. Terrific hot day &amp; we all sleep out. M &amp; I on floor &amp; we get no sleep. Until southerly at minuit.</p>
<p>Recd letters from Dot &amp; Ida.</p>
<p>28 September, Sunday</p>
<p>I am tres malade. We paint House. Pony goes mad wants to sit down. Mum is scared. E &amp; Pad drive Home &amp; we come Home by tram. I with Sandy in the back. Ring Doris, feeling lonely. Go to bed at 6.30 pm.</p>
