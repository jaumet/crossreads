  
 Lunatic forced with Bayonet to work (Beyer) Provision stealing by Soldiers. Luggage robbed, luggage not delivered. No Straw delivered for Bedding Pris being arrested without course & kept under arrest without trial. Letter containing complaints made by Pris of War addresses to U.S.A Consul confiscated & not delivered. Complaint to Hawkes & authority impossible. Treatment of sick Pris while in hospital And what is our main and mos prominent complaint, and wich we look upon as a disgrace on our persones and an insult to our Nation is the Flogging Incident of one of our fellow Pris, wich we demand to be investigatet
