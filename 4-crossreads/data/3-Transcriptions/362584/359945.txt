<p>I am the possessor, amongst many other things of a small purse made by the niggers of Porto Grande from some kind of seed &ndash; I am going to send it out to you so you will be able to see it for yourself. But talking about Porto Grande, it is very interesting, instructive &amp; disgusting to see the niggers fish. Interesting because of the number they catch; instructive owing to its evident simplicity; &amp; disgusting because of the methods employed.</p>
<p>The paraphernalia is simple cheap &amp; easily procurable. It consists of a boat, a stick of bamboo two feet six inches in length, a piece of line three feet long attached to one end of the bamboo, &amp; a jag hook tied to the other end of the line. Being in possession of the necessary articles the nigger will row out into the harbour, spit into the water, &amp; wait for a fish.</p>
