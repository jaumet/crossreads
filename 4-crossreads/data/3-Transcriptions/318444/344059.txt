<p>Sun Nov 9th:-<br/>At midday we were 272 miles out &amp; just past Ushant into the Bay of Biscay. Average speed 12.5 knots.</p>
<p>Mon Nov 10th:-<br/>Rough night but sunny morning. Another 276 miles up till midday &amp; we passed Finnisterre this afternoon.</p>
<p>Tues Nov 11th:-<br/>Good weather &amp; 292 miles completed. Armistice day 1st anniversary. 6009 miles to Durban.</p>
<p>Wed Nov 12th:-<br/>287 miles by midday</p>
<p>Thurs Nov 13th:-<br/>266 miles today's count. A strong headwind blowing &amp; heavy seas running.</p>
<p>Fri Nov 14th:-<br/>270 miles up today. Passed Teneriffe at 11.30 last night. Sea has calmed down</p>
<p>Sat Nov 15th:-<br/>273 miles &ndash; just on 11.3 knots nearing Cape Blanc &amp; just getting in the tropics.</p>
<p>Sun Nov 16th:-<br/>289 miles &ndash; 12 knots. Getting sultry.</p>
