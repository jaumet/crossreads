<p>1917 Thursday 15 Feb.</p>
<p>Not much sleep last night  Make up for it today.<br/>Gas alarm blown tonight but only a little lachrymatory noticed. <br/>Received late letters from base from Evelyn Emery, Rube, Bert Edwards, Miss Bullen &amp; Arch. <br/>A few extra cases thro' during night. Fritz is towelling up our front lines mostly with pineapple bombs. Our artillery also pretty active.</p>
