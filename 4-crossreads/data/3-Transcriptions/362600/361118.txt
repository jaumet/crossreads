<p>W 30 Mon<br/>Recd letter from French Shop Go downtown &amp; received parcel. Go YMCA to Carnival but get fed up &amp; come Home via long route. Go straight to bed. I am dopey &amp; do not go over [indecipherable] the Girls</p>
<p>W 31 Tues.<br/>Send parcel &amp; 3 A. Bull. To [indecipherable].  Get pass tell 00.30  &amp; W. [Hamah?]  &amp; we go down   &amp; fight our way into [indecipherable] Tournament. Play as Girls &amp; I get 12 7 but it showed have now on main card to have won Prize. We go round  &amp; Clarke Sybil  &amp; Saadine are at Home  &amp; we sing &amp; play until the diggers come at 11 pm. Meet Barsee an old [Abassia?] patient.</p>
