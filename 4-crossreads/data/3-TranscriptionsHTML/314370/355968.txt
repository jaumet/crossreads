<p>January, 1916<br/>Tuesday 18<br/>Exam. Care &amp; Cleaning.<br/>My Class do very well  average 98.2 &percnt;</p>
<p>Wednesday 19<br/>Work as usual no half holiday<br/>After Tea I went into Town &amp; put in a couple of hrs at the American Cosmograph to break the monotony.<br/>Night very cold</p>
