 Shells burst about two hundred yards away & then began to think we were not in such a safe place after all but when we saw the old French woman still go ahead with her work it restored confidence & at the sme time greater admiration for them. , a firework display could be seen every night if one looked toward the lines where the star shells we continually going up. Armentieres was only a mile or so away & we could see its ruined church which was only a week previously destroyed.
