 instuition it is, they have stuck to us through thick &amp; thin, at Lemnos they not only supplied us with pens ink writing paper &amp; etc, but run a piano along as well besides a gramophone, &amp; supplied us with cricket sets boxing gloves draughts, chess, cards, quoits footballs &amp; "goodness" only knows what else, the piano was a Godsend &amp; it used to make the place quite merry of a night. The Greek children would stand with their mouth wide open &amp; gaze at the gramophone while it was playing as if it was some marvellous thing. I suppose it was to them the first one they had ever seen or heard.
