<p>[Wednesday  29 March 1916]</p>
<p>Luxurious Bathroom &amp; cool Bedroom.<br/>My duty work seems to try &amp; settle claims for damage to crops, by Camps etc:-<br/>Using the water pumped at the Railways &amp; Town Council &amp; all the questions of a Town Council, Sanitation, Bounds Town Police etc:-<br/>How long will all this last!<br/>I forcaste a change back up North again <u>very</u> soon.<br/>Senussi are funished I think.  Gyppies are rogues if the British paid nothing, it would serve them right, they claim for all sorts of imaginary things &amp; lie thro' a 10" board.</p>
