<p>this morning &amp; he said that I was alright provided that I did not undergo any exposure. He said I wasn't fit for the front.<br/>This afternoon two of us went over to Souverain-Moulin, a pretty walk of about 6 kilometres from Wimereux. The scenery is picturesque and well wooded. Souverain-Moulin is a quaint little village renowned for its pancakes (cr&ecirc;pes). We each eat a dozen of these, saw an old castle with a moat around it and a river running underneath, and then walked on to Boulogne, about 7 kilom&egrave;tres, followed later by a walk from town out to Wimereux. (104)<br/>Saturday, 18 Nov.1916 (9.30 p.m):<br/>Getting rotten beastly cold weather now. Yesterday morning when I turned out here was ice an inch thick</p>
