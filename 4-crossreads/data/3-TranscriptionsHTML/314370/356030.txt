<p>May, 1916<br/>Sunday 21<br/>Got up when the breakfast bugle went.<br/>After breakfast I went for a walk to the old Helmia camp. It is like a city of the dead.<br/>After tea I went for a long walk round Matariah in the cool.<br/>Write letters home</p>
<p>Monday 22<br/>We were granted leave again today &amp; after dinner I went out to Patti's &amp; made the acquaintance of their son who returned from the Italian front on Thursday last</p>
