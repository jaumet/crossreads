<p>Septembre 9<br/>Worked half a day on hutting. A good many of the boys went to St Omer on leave but I did not take leave. Did some washing instead.</p>
<p>Septembre 10<br/>Working on Refugee Huts Ebblinghem. Nothing fresh to report. 105 of the Coy have gone to Aire for the purpose of training on Heavy Bridging &ndash; including all officers.</p>
