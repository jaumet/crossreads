<p>A Concert, arranged by 2nd-Lieut. J. Carey, R.G.A. (39 Course), will be held in the Y.M.C.A. Hut at 8 p.m. (doors open 7.30 p.m.).  Admission by Ticket only.</p>
<p>Programme</p>
<p>1.  Duet &ndash; "The Merry Vagabonds"<br/>2nd Lieut. J. Carey, R.G.A., and 2nd-Lieut J. Welch, R.G.A.</p>
<p>2.  Violin Solo &ndash; "L'Extase"<br/>Lieut. O.C. Cox, R.F.A.</p>
<p>3.  Humorous Songs at the Piano<br/>Bdr. Flint, R.G.A.</p>
<p>4.  Card Manipulating<br/>Lieut. C.H. Cohen, R.G.A.</p>
<p>5.  Song &ndash; Selected<br/>Sergt. Stevens, Australian Infantry</p>
<p>6.  Dialect Comedy, etc.<br/>2nd-Lieut. J. Carey, R.G.A.</p>
<p>7.  Piccolo Solo &ndash; "Danse de Satyrs"<br/>Lieut. Marshall, M.G.C.</p>
<p>8.  Song &ndash; "Come to the Fair"<br/>2nd-Lieut. J. Welch, R.G.A.</p>
<p>9.  Song Sketch &ndash; "Water Scenes"<br/>Bdr. Flint, R.G.A.</p>
<p>10.  Dramatic Story &ndash; "The Shooting of Dan McGrew"<br/>2nd-Lieut. J. Carey, R.G.A.</p>
<p>11.  Song at the Piano &ndash; "Take a Pair of Sparkling Eyes"<br/>2nd-Lieut. J. Aitken, R.F.A.</p>
<p>12.  "Some" Comedy &ndash; by "The Jerkers" (Pip Tockers)<br/>Sergts. Grantham and Reaney, R.C.</p>
<p>Accompanist &ndash; 2nd- Lieut. J. Aitken, R.F.A.</p>
