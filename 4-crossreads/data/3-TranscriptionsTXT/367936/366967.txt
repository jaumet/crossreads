
Saturday 22 April 1916Went into Sydney in morning.  Had another sitting for photo.  Then went out to Show.  Met Mother & Father & all Telarah people & Aunt Katie.  In evening went to "Twelph Night" at Adelphi.  Terrific crowds.
Sunday 23 April 1916All went to church at St. Chads.  Mother, Father, Alan & I stayed for Communion.  Took more photos.Aunt Min came up.  All went over to station.  Saw Father, Hal & Fred off to Mudgee.  Mother, Aunt Katie & Alan went back to Mosman.  I came up to camp.
