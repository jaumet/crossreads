Quentin thereupon went to Italy and returning to Antwerp, visited the home of the artist & looking into the studio saw that it was empty, with a partly finished picture on the easel, he thereupon painted a fly upon it & then hid behind some curtains.  Soon the artist returned & began on the picture & on noticing the fly, flicked at it with a cloth, discovering that it was not natural, calling his daughter he said - the man who painted that fly is a greater artist than I, and fit to be my son in law whereupon Quentin discovers himself and all ends happily.
