<p>A few miles before reaching here we saw several German dead, quite close to the road it was an awful spectacle.<br/>Fritz has a railway running parallel to the road &amp; our gunners must have got wind of a loaded train at a certain spot, they blew it to ---. Loading of numerous descriptions was scattered among the debris the whole train was a total wreck. We mined the road in several places causing huge mine craters but our Engineers soon had roads made round them.<br/>This is the roughest billet we've ever been in. The town is in a shocking state. He blew every house up before leaving &amp; left secret mines worked by</p>
