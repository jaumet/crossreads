<p>night of <strike>of</strike> 12th inst. we bivouaced at Tilul eight miles West of Mazar   &amp; the following night at Bir-el-abd which is sixteen miles West of Tilul. On the way out we dropped a Squadron of the Second Regt. at each of the following places :- Mazar, Gererat and Tilul. The Third Regt. are remaining for the present at Bir-el-abd while our Regt moved out this morning to Bayud, about 10 miles South of Bir-el-abd. This is the place where one day last Summer our Brigade</p>
