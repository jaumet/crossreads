<p>14 March, Friday</p>
<p>F.<br/>We go to Beach for a walk. Meet Uncle &amp; Pig Trough on way home. I learn more about my Fate &amp; I tin coiffeur. Play Rummy &amp; other games in Kitchen. Supper at a late Home.</p>
<p>15 March, Saturday</p>
<p>F.<br/>We go to Ramsey &amp; Pictures &amp; see the Deemster. I get puzzled in the Darkness. Tea at Cingalee &amp; Home via Lezayre road. We are both very tired. I turn hair-dresser &amp; I am made very happy. We top up til near 12 &amp; are so sleeply.</p>
