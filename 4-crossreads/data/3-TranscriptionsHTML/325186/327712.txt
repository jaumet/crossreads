<p>as good as it would be possible to get.  We had invitations out to peoples places &amp; were treated tip-top.  Every Friday we were given a free theatre show.<br/>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;    Taking Portsmouth all round I enjoyed myself during the six weeks I was there. I was sent from Portsmouth to Harefield on 12th June.  I went to Glasgow on 17th &amp; came back to London on 19th June.  I had a good time on leave &amp; reported back to No 1 Com Depot  I was classified B. I. A4. Which meant that I was medically fit but dentally unfit  Australia House</p>
