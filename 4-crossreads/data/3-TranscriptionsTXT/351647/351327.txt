 France 2 November 
 Dear Mrs. Jack, 
 I simply can't write a decent &amp; reasonable letter so you'll have to forgive me for this miserable little letter. 
 We have had rather a rotten time lately simply fiendish weather &amp; lots of travelling, practically no rest at all &amp; I have been very busy &amp; nearly dilly.  Moving always does upset me &amp; when we have to move in such vile weather its adding insult to injury.  If I started off to tell you all I have thought, I'd never be able to stop &amp; this paper would burn with the language on it.
