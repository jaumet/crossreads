<p>Wednesday 14 August 1918<br/>Recco Turkies aerodrome Nazareth, A'Fuleh aerodrome, Jenin aerodrome.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 3 h. 10 m.<br/>Gunned a train from 200 ft., within 5 miles of Jenin (26 carriages [?]), gave it hell.  Fired 550 rounds.  Met 6 Hun scouts over Jenin (2 of us).  We drove them all down and shot up two of them.</p>
<p>Thursday 15 August 1918</p>
