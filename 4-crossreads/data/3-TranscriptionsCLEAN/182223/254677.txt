
Monday 5 July 191535 patients from Alexandria  Busy about hospital all day Posted letters to The Girls Mrs Knowles, & C P Hyman of London Letters to 31st May from Girls Buddie [pet name for Mollie] & others
Tuesday 6 July 1915 In Cairo during morning Letters posted to Mollie. Major Bagshaw, Col. Manifold, & Lieut. Col. Barrett here between 5-30 & 7 p.m. Writing letters to Joseph after evg meal
Wednesday 7 July 1915In Cairo twice today Cable from and to Mr (The Hon) Meeks of Sydney, about his son Victor. Close. But all going well. Wrote to Glen Sparks Goomerri Queensland.
