<p>in the distance. Should have mentioned that on Sunday when were marching up <strike>to</strike> from Brettenux a Hun Plane got on to one of our Baloons &amp; set it on fire. The 2 Observers jumped out &amp; looked very quaint comeing slowly down in there white parachoots. The blazeing balloon seemed almost to touch them as it fell leaveing a great straight coloum of smoke</p>
<p>Wednesday 14th<br/>Moved of at 9.30 &amp; have camped in an open hill side near a small ruined town Hammill I believe it is or was.  There is a fine big water hole here sort of swamp. We had a fine swim this afternoon</p>
