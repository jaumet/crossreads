<p>October Sunday 28 1917</p>
<p>Back to duty again on old P. Ward. Things had not been going too smoothly &amp; they were glad to see me back. Squires not too well. Put into hospital today in lower ward. Run down. <br/>Jock Martin, Low &amp; Patterson still patients in ward. all doing well. Two deaths whilst I was away. Lightolber &amp; forget name of other.</p>
