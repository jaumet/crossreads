<p>5-2-17<br/>Mon 5th<br/>Took some horses down to M.V.S.to be clipped today &amp; managed to get hit on the mouth by my horses head breaking two front teeth; The Padre has had a lot of books sent to him from England &amp; is busy tonight distributing them; Sutherland told me this morning that he is going with the 4th Mtd Bde shortly which is forming up with Johnny Meredith as Brigadier. The 42nd Div (English) went back a few days ago &amp; it is rumoured they are going to England &amp; then to France we still have the 52nd (Scottish) with us &amp; glad they are left instead of the English Troops. Lindsell went in to School of Instruction at "Alex" tonight.</p>
