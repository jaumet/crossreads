  
 Tuesday 29th Cooks Fatigue all day. Wednesday 30th Fatigue all day went down to Bazentin on A.L.R.  for  went up on top of the Butte we Wartencourt loaded wire for Fremacourt finished & night came back to Bapaume and shifted out into tents. Thursday 31st Usual drill morning and afternoon Friday June 1st Morning Route march afternoon bath.
