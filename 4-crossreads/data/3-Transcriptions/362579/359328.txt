<p>Alice   &amp;   Eddy had a nice holiday with Annie. Hope Belle   &amp;  Ethel were able to go away too. Glad Mother is keeping so well   &amp;  enjoys visitors, it is a good thing she is so easily satisfied. Hope lots of friends to call in   &amp;  cheer you up. Tell Ethel to go out more   &amp;  not take life to seriously, there is plenty of joy in the world yet. Too much of one things is bad for people &ndash; must have variety. I've received a number of Australasians, Bulletins, Punch   &amp;  Table Talk, lately, dating</p>
