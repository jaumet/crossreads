 Thursday 26 August 1915&nbsp; Col. Manifold lunched with me today.&nbsp; About hospital during the remainder of the day.&nbsp; Wild rumours from Galipoli&nbsp; A paper from Mrs Reynolds&nbsp;Catholic Press.&nbsp; 
 Friday 27&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; About hospital all day.&nbsp; All going well.&nbsp; 
 Saturday 28&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; At the Citadel&nbsp;during the morning.&nbsp; Visited the Indian hospital and saw the asphalt that is laid upon the sand as flooring for the tents.&nbsp; 
 Sunday 29&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; Took Sister Draper, Captain Cameron, &amp; another officer to Matarieh this evening.&nbsp; Finished letters to Girls &amp; Mollie.&nbsp; Dr&nbsp;Hollywood partook of tea with me.&nbsp;
