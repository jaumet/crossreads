<p>Wed Oct 11th &ndash; 16<br/>I was told to report to 1st Div Hd Qtrs to undergo 4 weeks training as an operator. I arrived here after walking 10 miles and losing my way 4 times.</p>
<p>Fri Oct 13th &ndash; 16<br/>So far have not started with the instructors, have only been watching the switchboards. In fact they don't seem to know if they want me or not.</p>
<p>Sun Oct 16th &ndash; 16<br/>Still nothing doing</p>
