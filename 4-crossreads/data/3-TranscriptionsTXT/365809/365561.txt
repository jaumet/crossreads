 Wonder, but there is no fear. 
 The other day I received about two pounds of loaf sugar for my horse. It was sent over from Sydney by Mrs. Pope, Now it is in my suit case and am scared because the other day a woman was fired for hoarding sugar. 
 Best Love Yours very affectionately C.R.Lucas 
 Ps. Did I tell you that I got a very good report from the school inter alia.  "Fit to command a Bn in the field at once" This is of course strictly confidential
