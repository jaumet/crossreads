<p>April 1915<br/>Sunday 25<br/>Coming to Helowan we passed the Turkish prisoners, one interpreter says they are amazed at our good Horses &amp; the size of the men compared to the Territorials &amp; English Allies &ndash;<br/>Turks continue to be captured in small numbers along the Canal but I must not go into details &ndash;<br/>Will post you Phamplet re hot bath s here, which is rather interesting &ndash; I had every sort Electric, Spray, Sulphur etc etc &amp; have not been so clean for a long time.<br/>Met Major Midgley whom I had not seen since Piennars River S.A. he &amp; I did a patrol together once.</p>
