 15.3.16 
 My darling little Eddy. XOXOXOXO 
 I was so delighted to get your lovely letter   &amp;  kisses yesterday also your photo. I'm very proud of my little soldier Nephew. All the photos are splendid,   &amp;  Grandma looks very happy with her little pet. the family group is great   &amp;  I think Alice Peel is very clever. XXXX 
 I do hope you   &amp;  Auntie Ethel are quite well once more   &amp;  that you will all be able to go away   &amp;  have a good holiday.  Love   &amp;  kisses From Auntie Eadle XXXXXXXXXXX
