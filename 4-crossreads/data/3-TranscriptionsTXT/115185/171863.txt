The constant kneeling &amp; lifting from that position &ndash; no clean clothing, nursing them in their gore soaked clothes &ndash; add up a few of the difficulties against which we have been fighting. We have had plenty of wool &amp; antisceptic gauze &amp; bandages also antisceptics from the R.AMC stores. The wounds have done well remarkably well. Out of 44 badly wounded officers we have not lost one. They are well on the road to recovery.
