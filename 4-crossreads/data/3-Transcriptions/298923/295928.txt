<p>19 September 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Offensive started- Cables will tell you- a dog belonging to some Unit has made friends here-  I expect my dogs I left <strike>at Dungalear</strike> are dead or too old to work now  <strike>Am glad Day has her spaniel</strike>.   As we may move any day &amp; I never like to carry Diary Ill post tomorrow.<br/>X Don't worry about me your prayers have carried me through so far I feel I'll come through alright &amp; it won't be so very long now Mother dear, before I bring my wife &amp; Baby home to you-X</p>
<p>20 September 1918 Friday<br/>No entry therefore no image</p>
