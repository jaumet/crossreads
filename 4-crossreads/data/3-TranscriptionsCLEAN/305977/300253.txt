 Monday 23rd A beautiful clear cool  morning.  We got orders to go out with the squadron when they filed out of the trenches.  At 1.30 pm C Squadron relieved us & we occupied the back fire trench.  I was posted on the observation post with 2 others for 24 hours.  You can get a great view of the Turkish trenches & can see practically all the right & cemtre of our position.
