 Monday 18 June 1917. 
 Still in the Red Sea. A hot day but breeze makes the day a little pleasant. No boats seen today. 
 Universal kit bags brought up from &quot;hold&quot; &amp; during afternoon we were given access to them. 
 Special parade at 1.45 pm. on account of a letter written by E.P.O&#39;R. of A.C.B.F.A. I was exempt ed &nbsp;from parade and so arranged my kit ect [etc]&nbsp;prior to disembarkation which we expect will be Wed. morning. Reveille will be at 5 a.m. tomorrow morning. 
 Passed the &quot;Deathless&quot; lighthouse at 5 p.m.
