<p>Rained dinner time &amp; cold wind came up.  At 8 p.m. took laundry to Fovant &amp; then walked on to Fovant church.  After tea went to Y.M.C.A. &amp; heard service by E.A. Sellors from Chicago.</p>
<p>Monday, 16/7/17<br/>On range all day &ndash; only shot fairly well;  inclined to have headache.  Received Australian mail dated April.  Wrote to Aunt Sarah.</p>
<p>Tuesday, 17/7/17<br/>Row at breakfast time as food was spoilt, so in end served extra meal.  On range all day, finished musketry course.  Scored 123 out of possible 170.</p>
<p>Wednesday, 18/7/17<br/>Rained almost all night &amp; nearly all day so stopped in huts all the time working on Lewis Gun.  Wrote to Mr. Randell.  Went stroll through Fovant after tea.</p>
