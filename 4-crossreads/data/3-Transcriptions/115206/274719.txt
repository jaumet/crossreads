<p>May 1918<br/>Wednesday 29</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.<br/>Weather beautiful, very warm, had the usual dip in lake avec Ernest. Walked across to old billet and stayed with the boys.<br/>Purchased a dozen eggs at 4d each, broke nine as the result of a fall from a bicycle. Received four parcels. Of small dimensions. One from O.J. one from Mary, encore from L.O.L. and again from Miss Hawker. <br/>No bombs tonight; noticed a heavy fall on Amiens</p>
