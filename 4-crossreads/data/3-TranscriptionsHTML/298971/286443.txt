<p>on account of the beastly weather.  The mud in places is feet deep.  No sleep at all last night.  A new invention has been put into operation today in the shape of a sledge which holds one stretcher.  This is drawn by a horse and it slides over the mud.  It is hoped to use this method more extensively in the future and so save the stretcher-bearers laborious work.  Air fight today in which one of our planes came slowly to earth.
