<p>3 July, Thursday</p>
<p>I am outed at 3rd round of Crib Tourney.</p>
<p>F.<br/>A gale blowing &amp; sea is very angry. I nearly go without brekkie but I am too hungry.</p>
<p>Shave &amp; can't find a place to work. I go to English lesson on greatness of English Language. Walk on promenade with Jack Craven. Go to bed in hammock. Big swell on boat rolling fiercely. Write &amp; read &amp; go to bed next to table at 11 pm.</p>
<p>4 July, Friday</p>
<p>F,</p>
<p>Feeling very onkers until Tea-time. Pushed about all morning. Cadge at trousers for packs. No Book-keeping. Listen to lecture on Light Horse to Gaza. Play Bridge Tourney. <u>Win</u>. Go up starirs early. Cadge Trous, have supper. Wash tops in bath. Turn in about minuint.</p>
<p>Sea fairly smooth.</p>
