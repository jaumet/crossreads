Capt Hurley then went to the R.T.O to find out how to get our equipment moved to a place of safety, Dier Sienade is an important railhead & distributing point & there are immense stores of rations & fodder & army requirements. Egyptian labour is employed extensively in fact it would be impossible to do without, & they are very poor workers but the work goes on through sheer excess of numbers, their earning is 1/2 pt per day & rations.
