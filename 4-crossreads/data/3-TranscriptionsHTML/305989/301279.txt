<p>Sunday 21 October 1917<br/>Nothing Doing.  Wrote Nos. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 ?].</p>
<p>Monday 22 October 1917<br/>Sent 20 odd Christmas cards, concert at Y.M.C.A., nothing doing.</p>
<p>Tuesday 23 October 1917<br/>Packing up for move.  Fixing up dump for moving.</p>
<p>Wednesday 24 October 1917<br/>Left dump at 1.30 &amp; proceeded on guard to Fuchari, stayed the night.  Brigade arrived about 7.30.</p>
<p>Thursday 25 October 1917<br/>Stayed at Fuchari all day.  On pumping fatigue.</p>
<p>Friday 26 October 1917<br/>Left Fuchari about 2.30 and proceeded to form up and stunt to Hossarne arriving about 10.30.</p>
<p>Saturday 27 October 1917<br/>Stayed all day.  Rained during night.  Mess orderly.</p>
