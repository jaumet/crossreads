 Sunday 26 August 1917 Swimming horses, very bad headache from inoculation.  Picquet at night. 
 Monday 27 August 1917 Swimming horses in morning.  Pictures at Y.M.C.A. 
 Tuesday 28 August 1917 Swimming horses &amp; a good surf:  thumb pretty sore. 
 Wednesday 29 August 1917 Exercising horses.  Got paid 30/-. 
 Thursday 30 August 1917 Exercising horses in morning:  swimming horses afterwards. 
 Friday 31 August 1917 Firing practice in morning.  Exercise in evening. 
 Saturday 1 September 1917 Firing practice in morning and exercise in evening.  Picquet at night.
