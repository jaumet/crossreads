 1917 July 
 3 Tuesday 
 Arose betimes but was not let off boat in time to catch early train so put in day at Havre paddling &amp; lying on the beach. Lovely weather. Left at 5 for Paris. Belgian port - met with accident at Rouen. Thunder showers. Met at St Lazare by Red Cross, &amp; taken to St.James Hotel, Rue Honore. Saw an investiture at [indecipherable]
