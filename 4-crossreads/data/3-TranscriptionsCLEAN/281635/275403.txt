Warned for trip to coast tomorrow for surf bathing. Turned out very fine in afternoon. Saw Chinese Coolies & Portuguese troops in Lumbres 17/9/17 Reveille 5am & left 9am in 3 ton motor lorries for the coast. Travelled via Drionville, Senlecques, Desvres, Wirwignes, Cremarest, & Marquise to Tardinghen near Cape Gris-Nez arriving 12.30pm. After lunch walked to Wissant & saw remains of German submarine recently stranded there.
