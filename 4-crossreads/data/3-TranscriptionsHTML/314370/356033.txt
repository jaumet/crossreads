<p>May, 1916<br/>Saturday 27<br/>With the first streaks of dawn the new mob start to march in.<br/>Practically no Australians as these are all in France or under orders to go.</p>
<p>Sunday 28<br/>Still more men keep coming in all day.<br/>After tea I went in to the Ezbekiah Gardens &amp; listened to the Rifle Bde Band. returning to camp early &amp; getting ready for tomorrow morning</p>
