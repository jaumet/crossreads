<p>Mon May 1st &ndash; 16<br/>Everything quiet all day except for snipers who are continually on the strafe.</p>
<p>Tues May 2nd &ndash; 16<br/>Another gas attack reported but too far away to cause us any anxiety.</p>
<p>Wed May 3rd &ndash; 16<br/>Enemy trying to shell Sailly Station all day but with no result</p>
<p>Our 1st casualty in Battery A cpl killed through the explosion of an old german</p>
