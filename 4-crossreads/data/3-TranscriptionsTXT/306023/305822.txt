Well by 1.30 am we had just arrived in camp, the ground of everywhere covered very thickly with a hard white frost.
Tuesday 28th.This morning it is very cold and I can hear somebody banging at the taps to try and smash the ice in the pipe to let the water run, but the whole thing had frozen.
