<p>This was in Rue Peronne Albert have been here since the 14th of last month.  The tea was a success too. After tea Bill Ackland  &amp; Les Lee called &amp; a little later Horace turned in so we had quite a little gathering in the office here all the Officers being out. Acko turned up Horts days ago gone. I was glad to see him also Les they are billeted only about 100 yards from here.<br/>Received word from Eric the other day he is in Oxford England No 3 Southern Genl Hosp so spent Xmas in Blighty.<br/>No snow fell today was hoping it would.<br/>Mails disappointed us for Xmas may turn up before New Year.</p>
