
<p>313</p>
<p>MARCH 1917</p>
<p>20 TUESDAY</p>
<p>Raining most of the day</p>
<p>with a very strong, cold</p>
<p>wind blowing. Getting</p>
<p>our harness up ready for</p>
<p>inspection all morning &amp;</p>
<p>as it was passed all</p>
<p>right we got our half</p>
<p>holiday. Reading ect in</p>
<p>the hut all afternoon. I was</p>
<p>on stable picket at night.</p>
