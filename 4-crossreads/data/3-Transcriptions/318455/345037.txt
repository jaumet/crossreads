<p>Sept 24.<br/>We marched <strike>yest</strike> back yesterday to Bray-sur-Somme some 18 kilos where we are being assembled with the men from the other Divisions &ndash; We are beginning to feel that we are really on our way.</p>
<p>Sept 26<br/>Made a start at 9.30 this morning from Bray on our long train journey &ndash; <strike>30 of</strike> There are 30 of us in one horse truck. &ndash; There is a kitchen truck &ndash; one half of the train draws tea at midday &amp; the other half stew &ndash; then vice versa</p>
