
Set. 14th"Beau-coup" rain prevents anything unusual from happening
Sept. 15thThird journey to town at Poperinghe, where we are sent to purchase footballs - in the afternoon we again defeat the 6th Fld. Amb at Rugby 2-0.
Sept. 16thClose of mail. We won our first soccer match against Sixth by 5-1 - a good game - Harry & self represented our Unit - Issue & lecture on new type of Gas helmet.
Sept. 17th"Sports day" is abandoned owing to another move of camp - We leave Waratah for Reninghelst (which is on the road to Ypres). Visits to Y.M.C.A & village.
Sept 18thGuard duties - Signs of the approaching winter
