Expect to leave any day now. 9/11/17 Aches &amp; pains got worse today so transferred to No59 General Hospital, 3rd Section at Moulle almost 7 kilos from St Omer. 12/11/17 Heard tremendous gunfire all last night &amp; all today. Told by chap from 1st British Division that they have been in a secret camp at Dunkirk for 4 months practising landing from monitors. No one was allowed in the camps and they were not allowed out
