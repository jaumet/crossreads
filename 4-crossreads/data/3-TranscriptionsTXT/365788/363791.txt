She says Major Johnston has been retired to England & she is very glad for Hermoines sake.The Bank has sent me no word of the money you were so good as to say I was to have for I went & saw Mr Walton & he was very nice & said I would get it in a few days Ralph asked the me to pay the money you gave me for him(&pound:6) into his a/c. He left for the front on the Sunday after you left Cairo. Hope you & Will are happy at home again ere this reaches you. Withvery much love fromyour loving brotherFredTell Will I miss himvery very muchFOP
