<p>July, 1915<br/>Tuesday 20<br/>Cole Cotton has a job as Transport officer for a Manchester Regiment so she writes pity cannot utilize himself at such a time. Have sent Chocolate Bovril [indecipherable] Paper Insectibane etc to the trenches.<br/>I am satisfied so far as our men are concerned that the Turks are fighting like gentlemen compared to the Germans, so give the Devil his due.<br/>It is the KURDS who do the Atrocities a mountain tribe of wild Turks but our men will make them respect an Englishman.</p>
