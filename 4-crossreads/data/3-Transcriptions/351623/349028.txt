<p>Feb 18th<br/>Dep anchor &amp; went to sea to meet convoy.</p>
<p>19 Feb 18<br/>At sea screening convoy, weather bad.</p>
<p>20 Feb 18<br/>With convoy</p>
<p>21 Feb 18<br/>to 4th March Arrived Rosyth, coaled &amp; went alongside the dockyard pier to have aeroplane platform completed. Also camaflouged the ship all colours of the rainbow.</p>
<p>4th March 18<br/>Left dockyard &amp; anchored in stream off Charlestowe plenty of leave breaking.</p>
<p>6th March 18<br/>Went out past May Id to do firing &amp; returned to Rosyth.</p>
<p>8 March 18<br/>Went to sea to meet convoy.</p>
