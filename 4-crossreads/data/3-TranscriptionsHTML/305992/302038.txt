<p>Wed. 21.8.18<br/>Arrived at Taranto at 1 pm having encountered a mod. gale for the last 16 hours and anchored at the outer harbor Mar Piccolo. At 4 pm proceeded into the inner harbor &amp; passed 6 Italian dreadnoughts, several Brittish &amp; French ships of war.<br/>Moored at 6 pm &amp; disembarked 2300 troops by 8.30 pm into transport barges.
