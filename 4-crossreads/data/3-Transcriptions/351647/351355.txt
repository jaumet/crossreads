<p>[The beginning of this letter appears to be missing.]<br/>&amp;lies.</p>
<p>I had a hunt for Joe Kirby but didn't get him.  Poor Joe, I can't help liking him, faults &amp; all.  I suppose you are home at Tog <strike>by</strike> ages ago, hope you had a nice welcome &amp; the weather decent &amp; garden gorgeous.  I also trust you did <u>not</u> get Stella's cold.</p>
<p>I simply longed to be home too, just to get you &amp; have a real long talk &amp; cheer &amp; brace you up, because I felt you weren't A1.  Perhaps I'm wrong, but you sounded bored &amp; restless.</p>
<p>One of my pals went away sick yesterday much to my grief, results of gas.  Still he'll be out of things for a little while &amp; may chance a trip to Blighty.</p>
<p>I had a very charming letter from Miss Arn today, they're all very excited as the Colonel is on his way home.  They are very charming &amp; so jolly kind to me.</p>
<p>My brother is in Palestine.  I wonder if I have told you all this before.</p>
<p>How is your romance, you simply haven't mentioned it for ages.</p>
