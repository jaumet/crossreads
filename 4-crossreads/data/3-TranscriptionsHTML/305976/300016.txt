<p>little chance of getting them better while we are here<br/>Monday June 24<br/>Scorpions in the valley are very numerous. Several of the lads have been bitten. Snakes are also fairly plentiful. It is practically middle of Summer here &amp; the average Temp is anything about 120 &deg; .Here they were told it was impossible for White men to live in the summer.
