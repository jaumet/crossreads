
14-2-17Wed 14thHarris is OK again & the Dr. says both he & I can go on the 9 oclock train tomorrow morning owing to a breakdown on the line no trains are running today. A lad named Martin (2nd Regt.) is also going back with us Our planes went out again this afternoon loaded with bombs
15-2-17Thurs 15thLeft the Field Ambulance at 9 oclock this morning & at 10 was on our way to Bir-El-Abd where we left the train at 1 oclock (Martin (2nd Regt) left us at Nazar) Olson met me at Abd with my horse, collected some letters from Rich (the Postman) who
