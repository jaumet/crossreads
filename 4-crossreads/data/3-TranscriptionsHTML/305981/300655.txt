<p>(Pov. Hannover) Germany where we went to a Hospital but did not receive much treatment for our wound<br/>on the 1st May I was sent to a prisoners camp at Soltau where I was convalescent for 6 weeks then I was sent into the working camp. Here we started work at 6 a. m till 6 P.M our breakfast a drink of   <u> German </u>   coffee and a slice of black bread.</p>
