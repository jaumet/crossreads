Sharp frost this morning &amp; bright sun today.<br/> The brigade that relieved us had a terrible time getting into the line. They were 10 hours covering 8 miles owing to the rain &amp; the shelling of the tracks many of them got bogged &amp; some of them died in the mud, bogged &amp; exhausted.
