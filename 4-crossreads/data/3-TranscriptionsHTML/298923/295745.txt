<p>20 March 1918 Wednesday<br/>Move postponed owing to Jordan being in flood, anyway I'm staying here in Bethlehem with detail.  We have many sick &amp; the Regiment will be away 10 days still wet, hope you can read  will post with love to all.  Hope go Cairo or Port Said end of month.<br/>RUM saved the men a lot during the last fortnight.</p>
