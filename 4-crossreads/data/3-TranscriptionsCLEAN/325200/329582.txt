The Second Brigade went out last night  &  engaged about 3000 of them at Oghratina  &  a brigade of New Zealanders went out to Bir el Bayud  &  held 1500 there or so they say. the latest rumour is that we have fallen back on Katia but I don't know whether that is right. We shall probably go out this evening.22nd JulyThe alarm came at 1 o'clock am. on the 21st yesterday.
