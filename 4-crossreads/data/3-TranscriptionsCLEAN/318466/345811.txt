  
 Sunday 21 July 1918 Went to Salisbury & saw Major & Mrs Simpson.  The old C.O. is not looking too good. 
 Monday 22 July 1918 Plenty of rain these days.  Doing most of parades under cover. 
 Tuesday 23 July 1918 Expect I shall get my marchg. orders soon. 
 Wednesday 24 July 1918 Usual parades. 
 Thursday 25 July 1918 Revolver shootg. in afternoon. 
 Friday 26 July 1918 Saw the Eye Specialist at Sutton Veny Hosp.  Raining all day.  Did no work. 
 Saturday 27 July 1918 Major Lucas left the OTB for overseas.
