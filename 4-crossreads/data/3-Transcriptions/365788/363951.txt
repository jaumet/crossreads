<p>5<br/>Cadbury's England Some cakes of chocolate.<br/>Very good of her.<br/>At present I am one of the Sanitary staff here &amp; Duncan Robertson works with me.  &ndash; It is our duty to see that All about the kitchens &amp; mess rooms is clean, also one or two other places. &ndash; I think I told you about the big dugout some of us were building out about 1 mile behind the firing line.</p>
