 Sunday 2nd Hall &amp; I walked to Wylie a village about 5 miles away. Quite a nice walk &amp; the road was new to us. 
 Monday 3rd Just pick &amp; shovel &amp; drains again. The cold is starting Heavy frost last night. Ice in sheltered places all day
