<p>Thursday 27<br/>We are still here. This morning the Col &amp; I rode into Steinvoorde we passed the 3rd Division &amp; the N. Zealander going up.<br/>Tonight, the batt. went to Steinvoorde to a Concert. We took the hall &amp; the Kookaburras put on a very fine show. The weather continues fine with clear moonlight nights.</p>
<p>Friday 28<br/>A beautiful day again with a sharp tinge in the atmosphere</p>
