<p>Wolf's Cruise</p>
<p>Left Kiel on November 1916. Laid mines off the Cape of Good Hope Also at Colombo, Bombay, Cooks Strait &amp; Gabo Island, also between Three Kings &amp; North Island N.Z. At anchor[symbol] for 3 weeks at Sunday Island (Kermadec Group)</p>
