Nov. 4th Thursday
Weather continues good. Our artillery playing up with Turks during afternoon. Turks blew up mine at No. 6 Post 5-40 p.m., no damage to our firing line but support trenches collapsed with falling earth burying 2 men who died before able to get them out, 3 others were wounded. About 7 to 8 heavy rifle fire commenced near Lone Pine extending along our line by Turks. Garland was kept busy also several rockets fired by us. Appeared to be 3 attacks or rallies on our right, last ending about 10-30 with a few heavy shells
