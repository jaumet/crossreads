<p>14 May 1916<br/>Sunday</p>
<p>Sunday in  mid-Mediterranean  Think of it!!  <br/>Another night passed without incident.<br/>Food pretty rotten.  Indigestion rife among the troops &ndash; Bread poison.<br/>Church parade on promenade deck at 9. a.m.  Pres. Chap. Hutchison  No singing. Bob, Tony &amp; self take communion in company of about 30 others in Sergeants Mess Room at 10 a.m. Rev. Hutchison [indecipherable] officiating<br/>Cold wind rising. &amp; sea with it. Sailing ship at pitch &amp; toss.</p>
