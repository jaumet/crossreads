23 April, 1916Hill 70  KantaraWe packed up &amp; got a move on about 12 noon &amp; got down near Kantara at dark - here we had tea &amp; moved off again at 9.30 pm crossed the Suez Canal &amp; went along a good road to a camp 6 or 7 miles out &ndash; it was 2 oclock when we crawled into blankets spurs &amp; all, dead beat.
