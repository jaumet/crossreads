A Guard of the Dump told us the Machine was taken over to the Aerodrome about a mile away, so we made our way there after a few inquiries we was directed to where the Machine was being loaded onto a lorry to be sent away. It was terribly smashed up a complete wreck, I saw the Black Cross quite plain on the Side, it had been a fine Machine was of a light blue colour. We then went around saw the French Machine which came down also, it appears that the French Pilot who shot the German came down a bit to quick in his excitement could not recover himselfves in time crashed into the Trees the Machine fell down to the ground, the Pilot received a nasty shaking but was not seriously hurt.
