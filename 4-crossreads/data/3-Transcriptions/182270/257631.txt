<p>SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 15, 1919.</p>
<p>Had a storm in early morning &amp; had</p>
<p>14 pts rain out of it. Lopping in</p>
<p>Top &amp; Boundary paddocks. I painted</p>
<p>the Middle paddock Mill stand</p>
<p>with oil after dinner.</p>
<p>The rest went to Coomoo Coomoo</p>
<p>for the night.</p>
