<p>Maladive Islands &amp; sent seaplane in with message to "Hitachi Maru"<br/>530 pm. Seaplane returned &amp; "Wolf" steamed away to W.S.W.<br/>12th Oct.<br/>Having been steaming to W.S.W.8kts, but seen no more ships. 330pm hauled to SSE.<br/>13th Oct.<br/>10A.M. Met "Hitachi Maru" &amp; kept in company until evening of 14th then Hitachi steamed away to southrd.<br/>20th Oct.<br/>Since 13th having been dodging North &amp; south, East &amp; West trying to find another ship 8 A.M. met Hitachi again &amp; both ships steamed in under lee of the Gurgados Islands 230 pm made fast alongside "Hitatchi Maru" at anchor. Commenced shifting coal from our No 3 lower hold to bunkers. Also taking more coal from the Jap.<br/>7th Nov.<br/>Having embarked all passengers stores etc from the Jap, both ships proceeded to sea &amp; about</p>
