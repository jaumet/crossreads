<p>Friday 30th<br/>Got no day duty &amp; had a quiet time in camp.  The Tommies were packing up ready to go today &amp; they were quite excited.  Got the Wassah duty at night &amp; sleeping on a bare form all night on top of the previous night was a bit rough.  We were only called out once.</p>
