Wednesday Nov 6 We passed through Jerusalem about 7 AM bound for Latron at the foothills. It was a very windy day. Going through Jerusalem the people watched us quietly moving on. It seems to me as a whole the people of Palestine do not seem to have much time for Australians. Going down the hills we saw some beautiful Panoramic views
