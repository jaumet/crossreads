<p>4 P.M. Did not have our packs on. Am in A Co &amp; had to carry our packs about a mile to there lines It was the last straw Got a plentifull issue of good tea &amp; made an execelent meal<br/>Did not feel the march much to day Crossed into Belgum about 5 miles back Came through one village that has been completely smashed up. The weather is beautifull &amp; quite warm</p>
