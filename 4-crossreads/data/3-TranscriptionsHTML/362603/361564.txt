<p>4.  &amp; one or two other important letters so as I have given You all the oil I will close.<br/>Dave Ern Billy Reg. Keirle &amp; Ellis are all well, as soon as I get my money I am going down to see Reg. Keirle.<br/>Best wishes to All the neighbours to Lily Mrs P. Etta &amp; Dora &amp; Mrs Stephen, Annie Dobson &amp; all the Dobboes Mrs Bunting Creighton &amp; Ali, &amp; to Aunt Fanny, Aunt Grace &amp; the Boys, - I have never got Vic's address yet or Willie Davies' either- &amp; to the North Sydney Cousins &amp; our new found Cousins who I am very curious about.<br/>Now Best Love to You All<br/>From Harry</p>
