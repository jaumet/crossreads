The pink clover &amp; scarlet poppies are about the best, and in the gardens the only thing they beat us with is the Begonia &ndash; some of the imported French varieties, from the Rhine &amp; Rhone valleys, have blossoms as large as saucers &amp; are very showy. The roses are of course to be numbered in millions &ndash; many varieties &ndash; but the blooms don't develop so well as in Aussi &amp; are not as large on the whole.
