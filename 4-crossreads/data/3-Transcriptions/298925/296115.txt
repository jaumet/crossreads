<p>of about 15 rooms and in this we are comfortable while the troops are having a terrible time.  There being no one to disturb us, some played cricket with pieces of palings and jam tins while I with a couple of mates went for a stroll when we came across mushrooms in galore 5? across.  We collected 4 hatfulls and went down to the cook and got &frac14; lb of butter, a handful of salt and a dixie lid.  We then cooked them and there was enough for a good feed for the whole 28 of us.</p>
