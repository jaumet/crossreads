<p>Madame's sons returns to Bailleul on Furlough (10 days  every 4 months) &ndash; Letter written to the Boys.</p>
<p>Feb. 3rd.<br/>Sabbath &ndash; Letter writing &ndash; Pay day &ndash; In the Afternoon I meet Billy Stewart &ndash; At night we journey "per" motor lorry to St. Jans Coppell where we entertain the 9th Corp School of Tommies &ndash; Great appreciation of our efforts.</p>
