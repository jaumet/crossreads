<p>30 Sunday  [Dec]<br/>Leave into Boulogne to do some shopping.  Put in for Paris leave  Diary arrived from Rup.</p>
<p>31 Monday<br/>Here ended the happiest year of my life, in the Army.  A Good time all thro'</p>
<p>1918 1 Jan. Tuesday</p>
<p>On the day we 'it the transport<br/>there was a cheering on the pier,<br/>An' the girls was wavin' hankies<br/>as they dropped a partin' tear,<br/>An' we felt like little heroes as we<br/>watched the crowd recede,<br/>Fer we sailed to prove Australia<br/>an' our boasting of the breed.</p>
<p>"The young who avoid the region of romance escape the title of fool at the expense of a celestial crown"<br/>meredith</p>
<p>Hard to behave as though one never cared<br/>Hard to believe we may not meet again<br/>London ! recall the happiness we shared<br/>I have forgot the hurting and the pain.</p>
