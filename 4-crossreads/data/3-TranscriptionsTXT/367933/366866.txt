 POSTCARD  18/1/17 
 Am writing to acknowledge receipt of parcel sent through your Depot to me containing food stuffs, milk, cheese,  asparagies etc, which reached me safely and in good condition &ndash; I would also like to take the opportunity --- You all that is good in this New Year which I hope will see us home again 
 Yours sincerely Cpl A Metcalfe  1030 B Squadron 1st Regiment 1st A.L.H.Brigade Egypt
