<p>to my sleepless sorrow for she was bursting about a mile away &amp; at that distance with no hills in between it sounds very loud &amp; near.  800 German Shock Troops attacked early in the morning &amp; broke through the Second Bgde &amp; grabbed all their comforts &amp; rations, but the posts stayed out &amp; we put a barrage behind the 800. The two divisions of Turks who were to support the Germans thought better of it &amp; so when the Germans looked behind them for their supports they saw the 1st Bgde</p>
