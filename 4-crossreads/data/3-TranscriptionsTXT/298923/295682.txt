 16 January 1918 Wednesday (X to X crossed out) X This is Rail head at present &amp;  Hors  Camels are loaded every morning with Forage &amp; stores for the Trenches- Your letter Nov 25th &amp; Days &amp; one for Jim Ayre came today, am glad mine turned up safely-  I note all your news-  I do hope you are able to accept Mary Austins offer &amp; get a trip to Melbourne   it does the little Mother good  X This afternoon the Naval guns have been banging away at the Turks line from the sea-   In the distance the 800 lbs Shells can be seen bursting from a hill with glasses
