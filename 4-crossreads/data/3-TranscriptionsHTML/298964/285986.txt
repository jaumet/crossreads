<p>15 Th February<br/>Very cold day little rain  10 Aeroplanes went out to bomb Jacko.  Pay day.  Wrote to G. &amp; N. also sent P.C.  Sky very black thoughts of another wet skin</p>
<p>16 Fri February<br/>Very cold day showery  Peter &amp; Andy gone to join 2nd M. Div  Rum Issue  Had a easy day mostly reading and day dreaming of home and one I.L</p>
<p>17 Sat February<br/>Very fine day  match between Australia &amp; New Zealand  6 to Nil<br/>Saw all old boys in Squadron</p>
