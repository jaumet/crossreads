<p>Monday 10 April 1916<br/>Raining hard all day.  Stables in morning then guard instruction in Y.M.C.A. tent with Stables again in evening.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11 April 1916<br/>On Guard all day.  Five prisoners in guard tent.  Got a letter from Father saying they had had an inch of rain and got 24/3 for Store merino ewes.<br/>Was transfered to B Squadron (17th Reinforcements).</p>
