<p>Friday Nov 2nd</p>
<p>London : seen Griff off &amp; then to the Aldwych for dinner : the Aust Y.M.C.A. has it now &amp; it is a real home from home : caught 1 pm to Weston &amp; went to my lodgings : pictures at night</p>
<p>Saturday Nov 3rd</p>
<p>Westin-Super-Mare : roamed round morn &amp; afternoon &amp; play at night : very good</p>
<p>Sunday Nov 4th</p>
<p>Weston-Super-Mare : roam on Promenade morn &amp; afternoon &amp; plenty of people about : Church at night &amp; very nice</p>
<p>Monday Nov 5th</p>
<p>Weston-Super-Mare : stroll in morn : caught 2 pm train to Taunton &amp; had a stroll round the town :</p>
