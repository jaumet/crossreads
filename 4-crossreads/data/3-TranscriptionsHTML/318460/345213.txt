<p>Saturday 10/3/17<br/>did not snow last night, heavy fog, wind dropped much warmer this morning, snow thawing so mud again.<br/>Pay 3/9 &pound;22.2.0<br/>not too well.</p>
<p>Sunday 11/3/17<br/>fine at last, rained a little during the night.<br/>54th had a great number of casualties.<br/>Pay 4/9. &pound;22.3-0.<br/>looks like thunderstorm coming up, what price this climate 2 days ago Blizzard now spring weather</p>
<p>Monday 12/3/17<br/>letter &amp; parcel from Maude yesterday in Uncles Will after all<br/>Too warm to last as I thought &amp; it rained like hell during the night, was up with a Torch filling up holes in the roof with chunks of mud, got soaked.<br/>Guns still going all the same<br/>Pay 5/9. &pound;22.4.0.<br/>it's a lovely morning again, sun shining, stiff wind blowing but not cold, Spring is on us.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13/3/17<br/>No rain last night, foggy today but clearing,<br/>letters from Nell &amp; Jack 2. Tony 1. Viv 2. wrote to Maude.<br/>Capt Palmer killed yesterday dug-out blown in on him good soldier gone one of Australia's best.<br/>Viv tells me I got more than him, in legacy.<br/>Pay 6/9. &pound;22.5.0<br/>Capt Chapman also missing expect he is gone<br/>the 55 is losing most of their good officers.</p>
<p>Wednesday 14/3/17<br/>Rained again last night &amp; still raining.<br/>some new guns opened up near us last night &amp; kicked up a hell of a row. 6" naval I think.<br/>Pay 7/9 &pound;22.6.0.<br/>Wrote to Viv &amp; am writing to Nell<br/>Rained all the morning &amp; looks like plenty more<br/>guns going strong on both sides fritz sending them very close to here all day</p>
