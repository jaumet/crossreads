<p>Alan &amp; I visited the electrical power-house &amp; got the foreman to show us all around the plant, which was in many ways very up-to-date. Among others, there was a turbine power plant which generated an enormous quantity of current for its size &amp; registered 3000 revolutions per minute.</p>
<p>Dec 16. To-day is a very important public festival &amp; holiday here. We had a 'picnic' to Camp's Bay. We got our lunch ready from bread cheese &amp; biscuits &amp; marched out to the place in about 2 &frac12; hours. The day was very hot, in fact a gentleman was telling me in town that it was the hottest day they have had here for 3 years, &amp; Cape Town is by no means a cool place at ordinary times.
