<p>1917 June</p>
<p>10 Sunday</p>
<p>Stayed in room all day putting things in order preparatory to going abroad. Nell came in evening bringing a home made loaf &amp; looking so nice in her new W.R.A. Uniform. Began to type a few pages of revised copy of "On Dearborn St."</p>
