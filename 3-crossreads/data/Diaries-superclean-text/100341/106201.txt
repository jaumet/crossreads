May 26th N.N.W. M.300
1-30 a.m. awakened from Guard sleep on open deck aft, big rain for about  hour, swung hamock later. Up 5 a.m. call, relief, shave, etc. 10 a.m. Bath washing towel Hdkf. cleaning wht. shoes, then rested till dinner, study hour  after. Lecture from 2-30/4 p.m. on Military vocabulary. Study after tea till sunset which was one of the best seen. just lovely. all colours order reflected as glass like sea. Fish very interesting, flying jumping. Heavy showers early .
