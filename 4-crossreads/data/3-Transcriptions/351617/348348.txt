<p>On the 9th of November we received orders to get ready to march out at 11 am as we were going to go back to Ribemont for a day or two &amp; then further back for a spell, which had been well earned. The march to Ribemont was uneventful except that we had to leave our little home on Hungry Hill, but never-the-less we were glad to get away from those roaring, banging gungs and it was with cheerful hearts that we swung along the road that lead to peace &amp; quietness.</p>
<p>We stopped in billets at Ribemont 1 night &amp; next day marched about 2 kilos to where we caught motor lorries to take us to "Vignacourt" where we were to have our spell.
