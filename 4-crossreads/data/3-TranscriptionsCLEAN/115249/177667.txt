  
 From - Captain Travers.   To - Brigadier & Rear-Admiral C. 
  12th Septr. '14:  
 Have just returned on board "Warrego". Have brought ten white prisoners and comandeered motor lorry. Lieut. Bond has taken up strong defensive position at Wireless. I understand he has been ordered to withdraw, and motor lorry is returning for his stores, etc. 
 (1300)
