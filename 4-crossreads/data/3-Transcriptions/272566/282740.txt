<p>Wednesday 26 December</p>
<p>Off Duty today &ndash; loafing &amp; writing.<br/>Snow fell all day with occasional spells of sunshine.  The snow is nearly a foot deep in places.<br/>Bombardments with snowballs took place all day too &amp; I was in the thick of it.  The Officers were given their share</p>
