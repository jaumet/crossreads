<p>Do you think it Enough for a man on active Service. I Dont. I Know and I only wish Some of the heads had to live on it for a few weeks and I am Sure they would make more complaints than the men. This food ration-talk that you read about in the papers is all pure Bluff let me tell you the English Soldier Does not [get?] Enough food and this is the Cause that there is so much Sickness amongst them</p>
