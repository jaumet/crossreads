<p>Sunday Feb 10<br/>Reported German Division reinforcing Turks Washaway on Railway again railway up as far as Ramli. Divisional sports postponed indefinitely as we may be moving out at any time.<br/> Monday Feb 11<br/>The villages round here look lovely. All the fruit trees are in blossom and it is the commencement of spring.<br/>Tuesday Feb 12<br/>Football Match Auckland v 6th light Horse. Good match won by Auckland<br/>Wednesday  Feb 13<br/>Trains running again. Jews &amp; Natives very cruel to domestic animals. Reinforcements 6 arrived for unit today.</p>
