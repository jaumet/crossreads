
Charles Hargreaves was a Printer hence his interest in keeping these papers.
Jan 2nd 1921 Blake & Hargreaves Pty Ltd 47 Geo St Nth. began in business, later to 53/5 Geo. St. Nth. & 1964 to own premises 72/8 Addison Rd. Marrickville.
47 Geo. St. Nth. is in The Rocks Area & is one of the earliest Bond Stores, now restored as historical.
Charles Hargreaves died Aug. 25th 1953.
