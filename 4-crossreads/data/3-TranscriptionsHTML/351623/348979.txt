<p>22 April 16<br/>Received mail this morning.<br/>Left Trinidad &amp; proceeded to Scarborough, on the island of Tobagos.</p>
<p>23 April 16<br/>Easter Sunday<br/>Anchored at Scarborough Tobago</p>
<p>24 April 16<br/>East Mon<br/>Heaved anchor &amp; left for British Guiana</p>
<p>25 April 16<br/>At sea</p>
<p>26 April 16<br/>Arrived off Georgetown on the Demerara River in British Guiana.<br/>There is only leaf to Petty Officers &amp; Chief Petty Officers, so us common sailors could not go ashore.</p>
<p>27 April 16<br/>Anchored off Georgetown.</p>
<p>28 April 16<br/>Left British Guiana, bound for St Lucia.</p>
<p>29 April 16<br/>At sea</p>
