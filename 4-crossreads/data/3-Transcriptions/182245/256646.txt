
<p>365</p>
<p>MAY 1917</p>
<p>11 FRIDAY</p>
<p>Warm &amp; Sunny. Just a year since</p>
<p>we left Sydney. Cleaning harness</p>
<p>&amp; I washed my horse in afternoon,</p>
<p>We had to turn out in evening.</p>
<p>&amp; shift the guns down to the pits</p>
<p>our fellows built in Ploegaert</p>
<p>We are to be an S.O.S. battery</p>
<p>there &amp; attached to the 8<sup>th</sup> Brigade</p>
<p>Got home at 2 AM.</p>
