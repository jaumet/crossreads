<p>Lieu Robinson on French; first of a series of lessons for those who are desirous of taking up the subject.  Leave altered to 4 from each section per day, which brings my leave closer.</p>
<p>4/3/16  Saturday.<br/>Weather very changeable, quite a contrast to yesterday; windy &amp; cold today.  Spent morning signalling.  Two day cricket match arranged between A &amp; B Sections, commenced this afternoon.</p>
