  
 the worst part of it had to be contested. After leaving Cape Toun we headed for Fremantle, which took about 15 days to get there, and I besides others thought it was going to take a year as our eyes were aching to get a glimpse of our sunny shores, anyway in due time we arrived there. I may mention we had the misfortune to have to bury a chap just after leaving the Cape, but I suppose it had to be.
