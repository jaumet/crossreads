
Nov 8thAt Present I am sitting swathed in a greatcoat on the desert or rather open ground about halfway with a dash of north in it between Beersheba and Gaza.We went out on the evening of the 6th after standing to all day &   just about dusk travelling in a northwesterly direction from Beersheba carrying only the "unexpired" portion of that day's rations &   our one days emergency ration.
