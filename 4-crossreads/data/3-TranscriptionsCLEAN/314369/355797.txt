  
 14 March 1915 Sunday 4th in Lent 
 More rumors, the chaplain says it is the last Sunday we will be on the ship   &  he ought to know. A steamer shows up astern of us this afternoon but is too far off yet for us to make her out. Weather cool   &  pleasant steaming W.N.W. ever since leaving Colombo 
 1915 March 15 Monday 
 Wake up to find the Seang Bee   &  the S.S City of London quite close to us but travelling much faster City of London nearly out of sight ahead of us by 10 am   &  Bee slightly in the lead. At Sunset the C of L out of sight   &  the Bee on the horizon
