 May, 1915 Sunday 2 Went to church under the Bough Shed of VMCA &amp; could not but think of our dead mates I sent you a letter  saying flourishing  yesterday as you May not realize that the 1st L.H. were still here &amp; had  not  crossed the water.  Donald Campbell I hear is mortally wounded &ndash; Daisy letter 28th March Mothers  "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;4th April  &amp; papers arrived safely tonight so will post these notes now &ndash; Love to you all. I see Joe has gone to Melbourne I trust nothing seriously wrong with eyes &ndash;
