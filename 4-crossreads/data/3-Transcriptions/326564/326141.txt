<p>Tonight our Instructor gave us all some of the latest books on bombing I don't like his chance of getting them back again they are too valuable, &amp; crammed full of information.</p>
<p>19th   Started to rain this morning just as we finished our live bomb throwing, the whole School has been throwing today, each man threw 5 bombs at targets you could not see, but you were told what distance they were away &amp; what angle they laid from you, it was a fairly hard test &amp; I done much better than I expected to, got 2 direct hits &amp; 1 fair with my first test &amp; in the second one which was from the lying position I got 1 bull &amp; 1 fair, there were</p>
