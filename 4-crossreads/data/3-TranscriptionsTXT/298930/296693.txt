 [Notes on two postcards sent to Mrs C Benson from Cpt C.Benson.] 
 [First  postcard] 
 Mecklenburg (Head Camp) germany 
 Mrs C. Benson "Aberlay" Bellevue Street Kogarah NS.W. Australia 
 2332 Cpl Claude Benson 13th Batt A.I.F. 23/1/18 
 My Dear Wife &ndash; Hoping this card finds you enjoying the best of health.   We are enjoying a few days fine weather.    God be with you all.   Love to you, ever you affectionate Husband 
 [Second postcard] 
 [indecipherable] Germany 
 Mrs C. Benson "Aberlay" 11 Bellevue Street Kogarah N.S.W. Australia 
 2332 Cpl C. Benson 13th Batt A.I.F. 16/1/1918 
 My Dear Wife.   Your welcome letter and P.C. dated 10/10/17 glad to hear you are well.   I also had a letter from Lottie with order for 5/- and more to come when she knows I have received the first.    I would very much like you to meet her, I will ask her to call on you, plenty of ice &amp; snow about.    God be with you &amp; may your prayers be answered your Faithful Husband Claude.
