 1/4/18 &ndash; La Basse Ville My first visitor after awakening was the Colonel of the 10th Cheshires who relieve us tonight.  He had been an Instructor at the Senior Officers School Aldershot.  His battn. will be inspected by General Plumer this afternoon and bathed so they have a busy enough day.  Discussed dispositions and arranged details, he is much weaker than we are.
