The weather today has been rather stormy, and there have been two or three rather smart showers   I do hope it does not break alltogether, It has been such good Sunny weather for chasing fritz,  The Corporal has just come for the Letters So I will have to ring off with all good wishes to all at Overstrand from Chas G.
