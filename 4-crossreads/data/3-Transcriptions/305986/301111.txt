<p>old doc couldnt call em anything else, though the first time he noticed them he called em eczema and looked up to see if I noticed it, it must have broke his heart to have to finally admit they were boils, but they are and I know it, Im stiff its seems Im destined to stay here, and watch Tommies eat, they are beauties one chap eats his soup with a spoon and evidently relishes it by the row he makes, but gets tired after the first two or three spoonfuls, too slow, and puts the plate to his mouth</p>
