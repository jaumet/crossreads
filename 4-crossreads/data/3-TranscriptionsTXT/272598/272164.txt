 Monday 26.3.17 Terrific explosion at midnight last night;  Town Hall blown up by a mine laid by Fritz &amp; supposed to have been set by clockwork.  Many killed &amp; few taken from cellars alive.  Earth rocked like a boat at the time of the explosion.  Warned against sleeping in cellars. 
 Tuesday 27.3.17 Fatigues for the day: walked around to the scene of the explosion; hundreds digging to try &amp; release any who may be alive in cellars.
