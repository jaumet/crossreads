<p>outside Soldiers one met very few who spoke the English language. The time has arrived for us to saddle up so my further impressions must for the present be discontinued.<br/>Jan 31<br/>We left for Rafa last night in a crowded Troop train. The usual trucks, very uncomfortable &amp; not much room. However this we did not mind seeing that a few days we expect to be on board. We arrived at Kantara just on daybreak &amp; caught the Motor Lorrys to the other side of the Canal where we had breakfast &amp; resumed our journey</p>
