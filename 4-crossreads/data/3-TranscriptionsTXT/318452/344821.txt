 Sunday Feb 17th This is my first entry since I left England. With 8 vessels on Convoy &amp; 5 or 6 destroyers &amp; Cruisers, we left Plymouth at 12 Sunday 3rd. The sea was pretty lumpy for 3 days &amp; many were sick. Regulations were very strict as regards lights &amp; lifebelts. No lights on deck not even allowed to smoke, &amp; Port holes closed day &amp; night &amp; life belts to be worn all day.
