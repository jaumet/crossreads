<p>At 10.30 a.m. on April 30th we left Euston for Birkenhead arriving there at 3 p.m.  We were due to leave at 6 p.m. but the Bibby Liner Warwickshire on which we were travelling didn't get away till 9 owing to a dense fog.  <strike>For 2 nights &amp; a day</strike>  We had the fog horn going every 3 minutes for two nights &amp; a day.  <strike>but escaped</strike> We were lucky enough to escape the submarines altho' the Captain &amp; officers were ready for the 'Huns.  All the boats were slung out &amp; provisioned.  We only had <strike>14</strike> 18 passengers on board including my 3 children &amp; a little boy, but all the boats had baby food &amp; biscuits ready for the children as well as food for adults.<br/>Had an eventful trip to Marseilles where we were held up for 5 days as the Italians who do most of the deck work these had nearly all returned to Italy.</p>
