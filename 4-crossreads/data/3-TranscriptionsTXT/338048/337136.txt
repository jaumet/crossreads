 &amp; went home to dinner. 
 After dinner went in a carriage sort of affair around hop fields saw a baloon land got some hops &amp; plenty of plums &amp; came home. Visited Uncles Club &amp; the drill room (Market) &amp; went to Pictures with Him. 
 In bed again 11.30 pm. 
 Wed 13th Same pleasant pre-breakfast routine as yesterday. Afterwards visited Mrs Sparrow whom must visit again. 
 Caught 11.11 am train to London, visited Harold Porsford strayed about until 7.30 when I met Harold at the Abbey Caught 8.40 pm train at St Pancras Stn. just in time Harold seeing me off. 
 Arrived Bedford 9-50 pm &amp; set out for camp on foot &amp; got a slight lift on a bike. But mainly walking I got home 11-35 p.m. doing nearly 7 miles in 1 hr 45 mins 
 Very enjoyably surprised to find a bed already made for me so turned in straight away. 
 Thursday 14th Paraded sick &amp; awarded 3 days light duty Doctor very pleased with the way my toe is doing. Writing in afternoon  We held a concert in the Y.M in the evening
