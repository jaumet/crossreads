I can just imagine the task of the French &amp; Spanish fleets when they besieged Gibraltar for 3 years &amp; then failed to take it, its about the most disheartening thing to look at with the idea of capturing that I know of for I'm certain that no man would scale one side even if he tried for a life time for it's a precipice hes got to climb &amp; in places it actually leans towards the sea, seems to have been made by Providence.
