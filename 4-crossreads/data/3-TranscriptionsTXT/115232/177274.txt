 Wirelesss - From Naval Board Melbourne To Comg Off HMAS Berrima Date 21st Aug 1914 
 Message from Secretary of State for Colonies - In connection expedition against German possessions in Pacific - British flag should be hoisted in all Territory occupied with success by His Majestys Forces and suitable arrangements made for temporary administration.&nbsp; No formal proclamation of annexation should [indecipherable] be made without previous communication with His Majestys Government
