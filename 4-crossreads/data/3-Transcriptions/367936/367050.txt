<p>Thursday 5 October 1916<br/>Kit inspection and saddle cleaning.  Swam accross canal to Sini [Sinai] shore and back in evening.</p>
<p>Friday 6 October 1916<br/>Exercising horse;  just rode round to fresh water canal and back.  Football match in afternoon.  Payed in evening.</p>
