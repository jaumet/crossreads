
October 1916.  In one month the proffit October 1916 consisted of over 50.  These sume wher dividet middle Novemb 1916 under the Camp Invalides & Clubs.  Up till April 1916 the Prices wher extraordinary high about 75% of outside Retail prices than they fell to about 50% & after October 1916 they ranged mostly 25% above outside Retail prices.  Anything we wished to procure, from the outside, we only could procure same, through the Cantine.  Up till October we wher charged additional 10% of catalogue prices.  All order wher mostly send to one Warehous.  However after October only additional 5% of face value wher charged & we wher permitted to send Order anywher we desired.  The Pris. wher compelled to pay Cartage & freight on everything so ordered.  We could procure anything in the Cantine we desired.  Well in fact it resembled a country General Store.
