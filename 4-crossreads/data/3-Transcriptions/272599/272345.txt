<p>1917 Saturday 17 March</p>
<p>Mail closes today. put in morning writing to Rube, A.G. Mary &amp; Cis note. <2 indecipherable/> to May. Mrs Wheatley Post card to Bob.<br/>Go along to see Reg not at home, go on into Albert to get daily paper. T. Gill waiting too, go along to Church Army hut where a concert is in progress. St. Patrick's Day. Good band music &amp; fair comedian. <br/>Reported occupation of Bapaume. 2nd &amp; 5th Divs. of Australians with some Tommies enter at daybreak. Fritz sets fire to the place &amp; evacuates.</p>
