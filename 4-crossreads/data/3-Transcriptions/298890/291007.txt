<p>27 Thursday<br/>Slept &amp; rested &amp; read &amp; in the afternoon Maud &amp; I went for a nice walk. Nice day but cold.</p>
<p>28. Friday<br/>Same routine. Went to Bedales after tea to see Girlie Smith.</p>
<p>29. Saturday.<br/>Lovely day. Miss Whishaw Maud &amp; I walked  to Frox  [Froxfield?] in afternoon Girlie Smith &amp; a small companion came.</p>
