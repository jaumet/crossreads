<p>My latest ideas for pushing forward are two.<br/>One is to apply for a commission in the infantry &amp; the other, to take a course with the University Correspondence College, with a view to passing the London Matric.  Have applied for particulars of the latter &amp; am finding out about the former.<br/>The weather is colder, duller, windier &amp; rainier than a month ago.  It is seldom we have a <strike>sunshine</strike> sunny day, but I like it just the same, for it is England.</p>
