18thI was accused this morning by Madam and her daughter of being "zig-zag" last night.  Apparently this was the only way in which they could account for my late arrival "home".  I stoutly denied the charge hem!  but am afraid I did not convince mine host.  Today is the anniversary of the Embarkation of the 1st Bde. from, at Sydney.  I am therefore entitled to wear 1 red and 4 f blue service chevrons which the village tailleur very neatly stitched on for me.
