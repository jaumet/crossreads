 Just as we finished dinner, each Australian was given a tin of comforts sent by the Red Cross Society of Australia.&nbsp;At night an engaged party gave us a good concert.&nbsp; On the night of January&nbsp;second we had a great spread, given by the British Red Cross Society.&nbsp;Plenty of cakes, fruit, nuts and sweets.&nbsp;A concert was held afterwards which caused much merriment.&nbsp; On January 16th there was a very heavy fall of snow which continued next day.&nbsp;I left on the 17th
