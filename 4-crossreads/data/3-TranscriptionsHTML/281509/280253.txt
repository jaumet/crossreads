<p>31 January 1916<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Do no shift today.  Down town in evening. Call at Hewletts.  Then on to Palsers.  Take back portmanteau.  Served with dinner.  Come into town.  Fool round go to pictures.  Home camp at 10. 30pm, meet Tony on train.<br/>Bob sick with ptomaine poison. New rules &amp; regulations out. A.M.C.  Col. Holman up Corps. divided into.  3 section. A B &amp; C.  Tony, self &amp; Bob in B.</p>
