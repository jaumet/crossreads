
Saturday Friday 4th Aug.
Commencing the 3rd year of the war.Service on board today on that account.In the afternoon a band concert including some gems from Sullivan's operas.Passing Crete for the best part of the day.Destroyers all round us.  One in front & one behind.  Whale at times two ran parallel with us & others hovered on the horizon.  Passed an oil ship & a small wind jammer.  Lifeboat drill as usual, but
