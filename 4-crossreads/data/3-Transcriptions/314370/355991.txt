<p>March, 1916<br/>Saturday 4<br/>The men for next school march in all day.<br/>A large detachment of British West Indies Natives are here this time<br/>I have managed to get a beastly cold.</p>
<p>Sunday 5<br/>Muster Parade at 9.30 am<br/>Find again that we have a lot more men than we had arranged for, about 750 Machine Gunners<br/>Had tea w/- Dad &amp; went to Pictures at Helmia camp</p>
