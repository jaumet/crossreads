
Monday 9/7/17Cold, windy & cloudy, like rainhaving great spell here "Mailly Mailet" nothing to do all day.Wrote to Mrs Parsons, Loo, & Nell got letter from Viv.D. Coy. goes Tomorrow on Guard of Honour to the King.Pay 18/9 28.5.0
Tuesday 10.7.17.still cold & windy, scotch mist last nightBig guns booming all along the line must be big battle going on.Pay 19/9. 28.6.0.wrote to Percy Peacy.King going to interview us shortly. Hoorah do do dittocalled to orderly room about my leave expect to go next week.
Wednesday 11/7/17.D,Coy gone as Body guard to King Brigade Band with them.Lovely morning, fine & clear been very cold these last 3 days getting like winter again,Terrible air Raid on London on Saturday & Premier Holman nearly blown out the other day Major Gen Holmes who was with him killedMay go to Blighty TomorrowPay. 20/9 28.7.0.Brigade out on stunt to dayGot new shirt & chat shirt to day first for about 2 months. also Pr Braces first since leaving Ausy.
Thursday 12/7/17/All the Boys gone to see the King the whole Division & others besides to be there I & Pat Shelly left behind on guardPay. 21/9. 28.8.0.A Beautiful day Hot & sunnyExpect to go to Blighty Tomorrow.
Friday 13/7/17Boys disgusted with long march to see the King so were not in too good a humour when he arrived & the cheers not what one would read in the Papers they were.Another lovely day but it's getting colder of a night & one Blanket is not enough.Pay 22/9. 28.9.0Brigade stunt tonight taking Beamont Hamel.all leave stopped till the 19th could have bet on it.
