 15 October 1918 Tuesday (X to X crossed out) Jerusalem   X Temp normal.  Feel off-   Had a glass of  Champagne  this morning &amp; good food- only a bit weak now X Hear Kaiser has abdicated &amp; all German troops gone back to Rhine is it true? Hope you won't worry when you see or hear I'm ill  Really nothing  &amp; soon OK   May cable if I go as far as Cairo. Jerusalem is full of prisoners &amp; captured GUNS &amp; war material   I saw some labelled 1st L.H.
