
[Reverse of the postcard referred to on previous page.]On Service Abroad
[Addressed to]Miss Ruth BurrowesFranurraRooty HillN.S.W.Australia
[Typed letter with postage date stamp 26 January 1918]Dear RuthWe see lots of people like these and all sorts of funny things I saw two little girls leading a cow and a calf through the streets and when someone wants to buy some milk on girl mils the cow sells the milk and then moves on again. I will be glad when I can tell you all about everything. Goodbye little girl, lots of love fromDad.
