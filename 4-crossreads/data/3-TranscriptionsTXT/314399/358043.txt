
Saturday 15 February 1919Busy all the morning running to Horseferry Road. Met Dad Hunter & he, Harry & I went out to lunch, then Dad & I went out to Marie's & had tea. Dad left early to go to his aunts & Marie & I went to see the Lilac Domino. It was good. Touched Grand opera in places, singing tho was poor. Went back to the flat for Supper.
Sunday 16 FebruaryUp at 11 am. As usual had brekker in bed. Went to Maries. Took Dad. Janet turned up later on. Dad had to leave again so we three went for a walk & then came home to supper. Saw Janet on to her bus. It rained all day.
