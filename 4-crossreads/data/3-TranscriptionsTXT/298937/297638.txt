  Saturday  Friday 4th Aug. 
 Commencing the 3rd year of the war. Service on board today on that account. In the afternoon a band concert including some gems from Sullivan's operas. Passing Crete for the best part of the day. Destroyers all round us.  One in front &amp; one behind.  Whale at times two ran parallel with us &amp; others hovered on the horizon.  Passed an oil ship &amp; a small wind jammer.
