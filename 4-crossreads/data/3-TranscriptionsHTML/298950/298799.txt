<p>tin mug wher supplied, no matter who many Internees wher keept in their in the big Cell &amp; Confinement.<br/><u>Shooting into Camp</u>:  The shooting in to our Camp wher very often &amp; allmost a nightly occurence wich however luckily with the excemption of one incident allready stated went off without any results.  However one night a Bullet very near had a serious consequences.  A Soldier Guard seeing a Pris. going towards the Musick Pavillion as it turned out after, to try to get some sleep, owing the heat &amp; the atmosphere of the overcrowdet Barrack, had made this impossible for him.  The Guard seen him &amp; ordered him to halt.  Owing the "Pavillion" being inside of the Barb-Wire fence, <strike>not be</strike> he had taken no notice of the "Order" not knowing the Soldier had called out to him.  So the Soldier fired a shot.
