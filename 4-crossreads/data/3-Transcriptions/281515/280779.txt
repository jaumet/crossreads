<p>Nov 13<br/>We embark on another steamer called the Mecklong a bigger steamer tHan the other one &amp; made of Iron  we put to sea &amp; all goes well</p>
<p>Nov 14th<br/>at sea, all well</p>
<p>Nov 15<br/>we arrive at New Ireland  Germans call the place or rather the Island, New Meckleburg  the place we disembark at is called Namatinia the chief place in  New Ireland, it is more healthy living here, the major sends word to some of the Germans to come in, Mr Bruce &amp; party go to Bom.</p>
<p>Nov 16<br/>all the Kanakers come in for pipes Tobacco &amp; Lava Lavas.  Herr Brackner arrives with 13 Police who surrender to us</p>
<p>Nov 17<br/>we wait &amp; are ready for anything</p>
<p>Nov 18<br/>we go pigeon shooting the marys are quite sociable here (being shy in Rabaul)</p>
<p>Nov 19<br/>the major &amp; 6 men &amp; 10 native police go to arrest some germans who flogged Mr Cox  they go on steamer to Muliama [?]</p>
<p>Nov 20<br/>we arrive at Muliama [?] at dusk &amp; capture a nig who turns out to be a nigger employed by the Germans to let them know when we arrive, he turns guide to us</p>
