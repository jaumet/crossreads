From these heaps a kind of earth is dug which sometimes contains as much as 12% of potash nitrate of soda & other chemicals.  But even these mud heaps are being rapidly exhausted.  In many parts however it is usual to obtain 4 crops of lucern per ann. from this wonderfully fertile land.There are many trees planted in the streets & some wonderful gardens.
