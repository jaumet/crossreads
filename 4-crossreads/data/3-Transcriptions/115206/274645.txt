<p>March 1918<br/>Saturday 16</p>
<p>Messines<br/>2 am. Fritz still dropping them on road.<br/>Another glorious day, weather simply beautiful. Received letters from Roy, Mum, Miss Carmichael and "Toots L." <br/>Paid by Redmond at 5 pm. Went across to 53rd and delivered pigeon book.<br/>To- Rhodes. Had a yarn to him also Tommy Barr.<br/>9.10 pm. Fritz plastered roads and put a few gas shells over, sneezing gas.<br/>Ivan on O.P. all day, came in at 7pm. Russell on l'aison.</p>
