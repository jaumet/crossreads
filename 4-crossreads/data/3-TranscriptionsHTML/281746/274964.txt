<p>Oct. 28th<br/>Delightful Concerts are being provided by the Impromptu Concert Party.<br/>The climate is so hot here every one is perspiring freely.  Beautiful evenings and magnificent cloud effects.</p>
<p>Oct. 29th<br/>The ship seems to be putting on greater speed since we entered the Indian Ocean.  Not so many in hospital.  Flying fish are flying about in droves over the sea.  They do not fly for any great distance.  They kept up for a short distance then drop into the water with a splash.<br/>The crew of the ship are busily engaged painting it.<br/>This is a splendid sea going vessel.
