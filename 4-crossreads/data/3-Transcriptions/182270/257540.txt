<p>SATURDAY, AUGUST 16, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Car met us in Quirindi</p>
<p>&amp; we got home for breakfast.</p>
<p>I was round bottom end all</p>
<p>day. Tickle got a big white</p>
<p>goat in cultivation while I was</p>
<p>away. Doolan &amp; Wardell started</p>
<p>trapping in Wheelers &amp; got about</p>
<p>300 in two nights.</p>
