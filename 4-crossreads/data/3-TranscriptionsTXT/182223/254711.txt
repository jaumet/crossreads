
Monday 1 November 1915 In Cairo during the morning.Back at 1-45 p.m. Reading, writing, & sleeping during the afternoon.
Tuesday 2 November 1915 All souls day. Visited Old Cairo today with Drs Henry of Grafton & Cunningham of India. Coptic Churches, one Mosque & the Nilometer. Letter from Car Joseph. Wrote to them as also to Mrs Keohan & Mrs Ryan.
Wednesday 3 November 1915 Writing during morning.From 2 till 6-30 p.m. in Cairo most around the bazaars off the Moushky with Drs Henry, Hollywood and [blank]. Felt rather miserable, and about 6 p.m. wretched noises in ears, rushing in the head, heaviness in legs below the knees, miserable all over, too talkative words flowing correctly & with much facility; worse coming home in tram at dinner wretched, 8 p.m. same but becoming worse inclined to go to bed at 8 p.m., some visitors too talkative to them; during night no sleep a great battle in progress all over body between lucocytes and some invading organism. Passing much urine 6 times in 12 hours, large amount each time. Imagining all sorts of things. Not fit to get up for breakfast. Treat - Salacine & x grs,. Dormoporde grs. Phenactin [phenacetin] xx grs [indecipherable] Laxative 3 tabs.
