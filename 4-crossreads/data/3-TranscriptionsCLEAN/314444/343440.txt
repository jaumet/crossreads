  
 Syria December 24th / 18 
 Dear Auntie Sare 
 Just a few lines on the eve of Xmas to let you know I am well & having a pretty good time as far as comfort is concerned, which means a good deal to a fellow out here. I really do not remember when I last wrote to you, but I know this much. That it is my turn, as your letter dated Oct 17 reached me yesterday. However now censorship regulations are moderated a little, I will try & outline my movements the last few  weeks  months, which will make a letter a little more interesting than it was possible before & give you an idea of what is really going on over on this particular part of the world.
