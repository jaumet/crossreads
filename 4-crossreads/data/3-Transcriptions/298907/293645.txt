<p>by the windows</p>
<p>There's one thing that beats me over here &amp; that is the large number of strong young able bodied men running about belonging to the Flying Corp, they could use the comb with great &amp; good effect here if we are so short of men as they say we are, for most of the work they are doing could easily be done by older men</p>
<p>We are having another concert in our Ward this evening, a bit better than the last one too</p>
<p>4th Got a telegram from Len this morning saying he would be up tomorrow afternoon (Sunday).</p>
<p>Have been out dodging round the city this afternoon &amp; it is quite hot after the heavy fall of rain last night, it fair came down in torrents &amp; washed everything clean.</p>
