 The "Nurse" who is on our Coy. for once did not expect trouble,  but  &amp; before  we  he knew where he was, he was seized by five pairs of powerful hands and held whilst a couple of others dressed him up in a nurse's costume &amp; blackened his face. A notice "Native Ayah" was then pinned to his blouse &amp; he was carried around the boat deck to the delight of hosts of diggers &amp; the remainder of the officers. 
 It was a most humiliating affair but it was well merited.
