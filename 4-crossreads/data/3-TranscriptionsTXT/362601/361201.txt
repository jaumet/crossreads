 9 May, Friday 
 F. Auntie &amp; I beat carpets. Marie &amp; I clean room, polish &amp; pictures. I help hang pictures. I have a 2 year promise of consideration. We are very chummy &amp; speak our minds. S----- is very kind a Sore[?] &amp; hangs my clothes up. We have a little music &amp; read after tea. Elddoe. I go round top road to farm &amp; return same road. It is lovely day. 
 Nurse. 
 10 May, Saturday 
 F. I help auntie &amp; then uncle in garden. Recd &pound;10 from Prim. M &amp; I go down to Sulby P.O. around by old Picnic place, we sit on stone by River &amp; I make a pyramid of stones &amp; tell the Tale of Australia &amp; what I intend to do. We come home very tired &amp; one has to be carried upon hips. 
 Write in Cosy Room to D.J's &amp; D. Welten
