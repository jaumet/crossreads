 Monday 19 November 1917. 
 Smith inspected camels. Got rid of my 2 lead camels as result. Moved again at 1 pm. 5 miles to Surafend a . Passed through hundreds of acres of well kept orange orchards where hundreds of tons of oranges are going to waste on the trees. 
 28 days for anyone caught taking oranges &amp; lemons from these orchards. People in the villages use Eng h  money and speak Eng h  language. 
 A wet night &amp; on picquet. Got drenched.
