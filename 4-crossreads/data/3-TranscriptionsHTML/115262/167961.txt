<p>Friday 13&nbsp;July 1917.</p>
<p>Usual parades and drill etc.</p>
<p>Isolation believed to be lifted tomorrow at midday.</p>
<p>At 4 p.m. we handed in, to Camp Q.M. the following articles :-<br />1 pr. (mounted service) breeches, 1 tunic (service dress), 2 complete suits dungarees, 1 pr. boots.</p>
<p>Awaiting orders <s>of</s> for further movements from this camp.</p>
<p>Got particulars from Pte. J. A. Gould, C&nbsp;B. F.A. of &quot;Konigsberg&quot; action on East coast of Africa. He was on the Cruiser &quot;Pioneer&quot;. Entered these in front of diary.</p>
<p>(A Chmlion [chameleon]&nbsp;a beautiful specimen found. C.L.B. has it as a pet).</p>
