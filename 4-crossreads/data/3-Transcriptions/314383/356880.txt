<p>June 1st<br/>A march of 12 miles fairly good going.</p>
<p>June 2nd<br/>19 Miles today in the broiling sun, had good feed &amp; am turning in.  Its now a case of bed before dark &amp; out again before daylight &amp; plodding along all day.
