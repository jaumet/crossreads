<p>I had that from Naylor ex pupil of Aldenham now A.D.C. to the General comding. 52nd Division of Infantry so it should be right.<br/>There are strong rumours &amp; apparently they are correct too of eight tanks now at Romani.  Indeed two of our chaps declare they saw them on the train covered of course with tarpaulins.  If so these should be of value if we can get near enough to the turks to use them.</p>
<p>Feb. 8th<br/>El Arish<br/>Pity the poor soldier on the eve of a trek!  So many valued possessions have to be jettisoned.  We are moving back 60 miles on Saturday, the truth to go on lines of communication.  It is somewhere near Romani &amp; horribile diction, some seven miles from the railway.<br/>However it will be fairly peaceful there I expect</p>
