<p>Wednesday 5 December 1917</p>
<p>Another S.O.S. message came <strike>from our rear</strike> last night about 5 miles away, our BAR Steward is very nervous as he has been torpedoed twice <strike>in PERSIA &amp; MOOLTAN.</strike>  We arrived however in <strike>Malta</strike> at 6 p.m. after going 96 miles past &amp; then circling back again &ndash; so tonight <strike>Dolly &amp; I</strike> we came ashore &amp; had dinner, but the Hotel tucker is not as good as on the boat. <strike>The Papers I want to read.</strike></p>
