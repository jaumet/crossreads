<p><u>17th</u> Jay&#39;s birthday.&nbsp;Took Bn. for route march this morning. This afternoon&nbsp; our Brigade played 12th Bde Rugby and Australian Football. The former a draw we lost latter by 10 to 62 pts.</p>
<p>Entry <u>21/10/17</u></p>
<p><u>18th</u> This afternoon Geo. Marper &amp; myself visited our Transport Lines near Ypres to get any mail. We caught a motor transport from Fletre to Ballieul. Here we bought a serviette ring each &amp; some postcards. The next lorry took us to Locre where just as we were getting down, General Plumer passed us. A walk for a &frac12; mile and another lorry</p>
