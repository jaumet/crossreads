 11/6/16  Sunday 
 Very dusty &amp; fairly hot &ndash; Went 6 am Mass also went to Communion &ndash; We done very little all day. Went down to the 60th Battalion lines &amp; Father [blank] said the Rosary &amp; gave a very interesting address on the origin of same. 
 12/6/16  Monday 
 Fairly warm &ndash; Lectures &amp; Demonstrations by Drs Coppleson &amp; MacIntosh &ndash; Kit inspection &amp; a full marching order parade &ndash; Went down to the 60th Line &amp; attended rosary. 
 13/6/16  Tuesday 
 Hot &amp; heavy storm blowing.  Had a course of Semaphore Signalling &ndash; The 8th Battalion moved out &ndash; Had a general medical inspection. 14/6/16  Wednesday 
 G.O.C granted a full holiday &ndash; Most of the Division assembled &amp; had a splendid forenoon of sport, which was both lively &amp; very interesting. They started at 6 am, I stayed till 10 am, then it got too bally hot. I got leave
