
Saturday 11 August 1917.
On picquet 2 a.m. till 4 a.m, Sudanese on picquet also. Told me that the They were excited as raiding Bedouins had cut 1 camel loose & taken it out into the desert.
Next post 8 am. till 10 am.
Printed & fixed photos & wrote letters.
Next post 2 p.m. till 4 p.m.
Whole picquet relieved at 6 p.m.
Had a shower & turned in at 8 p.m.
 
Sunday 12th (continued)
Mail delivered today. I received 8 letters. Only 1 of my Colombo letters received.
Sunday 12th (continued)
shot as a spy 2 months ago. Bloodstains still on tree & ashes of burnt clothing nearby. Grave not far away.
Saw Marmaluke's leap and chipped pieces of Alabasta off back of Mohammed Ali Mosque.
