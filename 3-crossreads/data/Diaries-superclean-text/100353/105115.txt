Lemnos when we arrived seemed bleak miserable. But it was the middle of winter when the fields were bear bare, the few fruit trees surrounding the small villages were leafless. The island has a population of some 35,000 who are contained in Kastra the capital in 37 other villages. The island is rich wheat land as one stands on one of the basalt hills looks up the valleys which mark out into farm plots like a chess board, it forms a very pleasant picture. Small flocks of long-woolled sheep tended by a shepherd with his picturesque mountaineer's costume, feed on the hills; donkeys, pigs oxen browse or wallow in the marshes.
