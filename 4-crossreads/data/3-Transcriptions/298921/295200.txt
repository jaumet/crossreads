<p>August, 1915<br/>Tuesday 17<br/>The Routine here is as follows White Robes, Red fez Arab brings tea &amp; Biscuit at 6.30 &ndash;<br/>I then get my crutches &amp; proceed to Bathroom &amp; wash all but my Right thigh &ndash;<br/>Then a Tommy makes bed (3 officers one ward), another sweeps, a nigger swabs out with a Bucket of water. Then a R.A.M.C. orderly comes along with Thermo : counts pulse * finish this in  letter.<br/>Just heard Aust Mail closes So send love to all &ndash; Frank</p>
