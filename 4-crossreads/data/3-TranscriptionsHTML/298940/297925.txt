<p>Brought cake &amp; chocolate back to Walter.<br/>Charley Taylor of White 's gulley on the Peninsula is in <strike>here</strike> this camp. He went away from the 9th in the early morning of the 7th March with gas, &amp; had a bad dose. He would not be out now, but for his characteristic abuse of a doctor in hospital. The same old Charley Taylor, Lance corporal today &amp; Private tomorrow, hardened cosmopolitan abusive &amp; independent &ndash; cynical &ndash; brave as a lion &amp; large hearted.<br/>He, Splinter Christie</p>
