Perhaps more &ndash; yet hardly that, for a man's duty is to his ideals, and Army classifications should not <strike>so sway</strike> turn a man away from what he knows he can do. The hardest part of having ideals is the living up to them when only you &amp; God know what you are doing &amp; why. It is so easy to accept the world's verdict. How often has some sincere &amp; struggling soul been tortured with the worldly advice. "You've done your bit; so why worry."</p>
<p>Fritz shelled the ridge at the back of us last night, &amp; round about here during the night. The weather was far too dull for aeroplane work, thank goodness.</p>
