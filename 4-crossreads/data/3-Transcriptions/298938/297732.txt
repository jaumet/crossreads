<p>getting back to the 3rd &amp; incidentally the dispensary job, is not great.<br/>Never mind, keep a good heart &amp; all will be well.  I've left for the front again &amp; am quite prepared for any misfortune that may come my way.  My good luck in living so long counteracts any hard luck I have had in not getting promotion<br/>A la victoire.</p>
<p>23rd March<br/>Unexpectedly put on draft yesterday.<br/>Handed in all spare kit today.<br/>On guard from 5 p.m. yesterday till 5 p.m. today.</p>
