 26. Thursday Frosty morning but grey afterwards.  Miss H. &amp; I went down to Piccadilly to see President Wilson.  Great crowd so I missed Ethel &amp; had tea with Mrs. French &amp; the Sawyers in Club 
 27  Friday Worked so late had no dinner.  Went to see Ethel at Dysart Hotel at 8 &amp;  then for night with Miss H. &amp; Miss Newcomb 
 28 Saturday Worked all day &amp; got to Dysart Hotel just in time to have dinner with Ethel and went to bed early &amp; talked till past midnight.  E. had a cold &amp; snored so I cd. not sleep
