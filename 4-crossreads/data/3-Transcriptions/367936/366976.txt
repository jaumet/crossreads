<p>Wednesday 10 May 1916<br/>Worked same as usual.  [indecipherable] went to Dr. in morning and was found to have mumps.<br/>Cut legs off my pants in evening.</p>
<p>Thursday 11 May 1916<br/>Ordinary work.  Very calm day, getting hotter.<br/>Took my hammock up to the bows on top deck, nice &amp; cool up there.</p>
