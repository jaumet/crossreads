<p>20 October, 1916 Friday</p>
<p>Left Melb 1914</p>
<p>Spent night at theatre &amp; another of my old attacks came on only more severe if anything. Doc examined me &amp; pronounced appendicitis so intend reporting sick tomorrow.</p>
