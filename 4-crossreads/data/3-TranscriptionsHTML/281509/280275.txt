<p>22 February 1916<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Called out on tattoo this morn.  First time since being in camp.  Absent from parade on Sat &ndash; last. Let off.</p>
<p>Field Amb. Being picked today.<br/>Drill in afternoon &amp; hand seat drill.<br/>Trio go down to Deans to tea.  Mary arrives too from Manly &amp; Cis &amp; Ess Hewlett after tea. Nice evening.  Dorothy &ndash; Charlie recite.<br/>Rescue soldier from having been run over by train at Strathfield Station.</p>
