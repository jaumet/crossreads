<p>[Transcriber's note:<br/>In this diary Corporal Rhodes advises that he wrote another diary which was lost. He was wounded at Butte de Warlencourt in France on 25th February 1917. His left leg was amputated at a hospital in England and he returned home to Australia on board the hospital ship "Kanowna" on 16 December 1917 and was discharged on 18 August 1918. His army records show correspondence which indicates that he died in early 1936.]</p>
<p>My Service with the A.I.F.</p>
