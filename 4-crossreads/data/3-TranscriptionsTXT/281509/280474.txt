 1916 September 8 Friday 
 Back to school 9.30 a.m. Further demonstrations with the new pattern helmet. We traipse into Pop for dinner about 2 miles &amp; arrive back for the afternoon session when drill was the order of procedure. Call again for Tony &amp; into Pop again meet Bob &amp; tea of pork peas &amp; potatoes  Pictures after. Tony looking well &amp; in fine fettle.
