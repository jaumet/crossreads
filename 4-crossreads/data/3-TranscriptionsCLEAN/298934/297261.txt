20 May Thursday
Had side stiched up.
Friday 21st May
Had 1 hole in arm stiched up.  Told I was a serious case & should be tfd. to the proper hospital but told later I was to remain here.  Some nurses arrd. today.
22nd June 1915
Had a concert once a week & tobacco & cigs. given us but we are all tired of Malta.  It is an unlovely spot.  Walked to Citta Vechia one day.  Very quiet village with a nice cathedral.
