<p>II<br/>"prosperity" &ndash; However I hope to have things more comfortable in a week or so &ndash;<br/>All my old Officers have left the Battery, except Greene who still flourishes &ndash; Walker has his Majority and has been transferred to the 12th Army Brigade &ndash; as also had Col Williams &ndash; We are now commanded temporarily by Major R.M. Edwards D.S.O. &amp; I hear there is a likelihood of Col King coming back to us &ndash; Rabett has gone to Australia &amp; Rosenthal has the 1st  Division &ndash; The reason for Rabetts return I could not ascertain unless as you mentioned in a former letter home influence worked it &ndash; Anyhow he's a lucky dog to get the trip back &ndash; and I wouldn't</p>
