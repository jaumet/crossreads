<p>called out and informed that one of our members had been taken away with German Measles &amp; as a result we were to be "isolated" for an indefinite period. Thirty of us are thus confined to the hut our only liberty being a little outdoor exercise twice a day.</p>
<p>To minimise the likelihood of our escape a permanent guard has been placed over our hut and no-one is allowed to go outside the door without an escort.</p>
<p>However, to relieve the monotony four of us scaled out after tea. I dodged the escort &amp; Alan &amp; another climbed out the window. Such is life! Freedom is never so precious as when it is partially or wholly denied.</p>
