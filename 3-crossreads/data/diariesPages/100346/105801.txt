Tuesday Nov 27th
Sutton Veny : still cold damp : got on Y.M.C.A. fatigue it is a sweet job : a couple of hours work in morn the a cup of tea slice of cake packet of fags bucksheesh : hours work in afternoon another cup of tea cake thrown in for luck : Morrie self went to O.T.B. to see Fred together to the pictures : not too bad
Wednesday Nov 28th
Sutton Veny : fatigue at Y.M.C.A. during day : Fred looked us up after tea we went to concert at Y.M.C.A. very nice
Thursday Nov 29th
Sutton Veny : fatigue at Y.M.C.A. : met Reg Palmer during day also a few more of the 17th Batt boys :
