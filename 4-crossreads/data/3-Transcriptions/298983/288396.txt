<p>Shells some HE one of the later dropped about 10 yards from our front door. Had just made some cocoa &amp; started on a feed when we got orders to put on our masks &amp; go down the road. Most of the show was over by then but we had to hang about for fresh orders &amp; eventually had to get our blankets &amp; go about 1 mile to where the rest of the Co are camped getting there about 1.30 AM Was luckie enough to strike a hut</p>
