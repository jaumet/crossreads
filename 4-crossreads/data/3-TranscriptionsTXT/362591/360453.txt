France27/5/16
My dear Mother,
I see by a cable I just received that Keith has sailed.  Was he sorry he had to go with a cycle corps?  He has nothing to be sorry about, &amp; what is more you will have no reason to worry.  I was afraid the day would come when he would have to leave, but now my mind is quite at ease about him;  sometimes I almost envy him.  I hope I have will have the opportunity of seeing him when he reaches here, but of course that is very doubtful.
