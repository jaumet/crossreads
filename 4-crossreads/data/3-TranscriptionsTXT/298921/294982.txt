
Dec 25Xmas DayI know the little Mother will be at Church & the lines she has written me 'Till we meet again' I have just re-read. Capt. White is O/C & there are only the Q'land Reinforcements & my own on board.Our boat is a German Prize boat originally the Pfalz & had only made one voyage when we boarded her -Wool, Ores, frozen Meat Crew are all strangers to each other, the Skipper a good manconcert in the evening
