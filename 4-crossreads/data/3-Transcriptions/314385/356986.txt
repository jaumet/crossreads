<p>1916</p>
<p>then she was a mass of flames &amp; distruction and dropped lifeless to the earth. Our planes had destroyed it to prevent it falling into the hands of Fritz. That is the second event I've seen.</p>
<p>Thursday Sep 28th<br/>A quiet day weather still fine.</p>
<p>Went to the village of Reninghulst about 4 kilos S &amp; saw a picture show by the Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Friday Sep 29th<br/>Things normal, weather fine.</p>
<p>Patients getting benefit of their treatment &amp; rest mostly all from our (the 6th Brigade).</p>
<p>There is heavy gun work going on.</p>
<p>Saturday 30th Sep<br/>Nothing particular doing just the same routine work. Went into Pge on corp duties.</p>
<p>Weather still nice &amp; warm.</p>
<p>Sun 1 October<br/>Usual duties. B Sect went on a bathing parade.Things quiet at the front.</p>
<p>Mon Oct 2nd<br/>Weather breaking up towards winter. Everything very quiet not many planes about.</p>
