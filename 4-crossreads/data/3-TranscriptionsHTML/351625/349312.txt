<p>he was in hospital in England &amp; doing well, he was wounded by a Machine gun bullet in the thigh, lucky beggar.  You were saying you were sorry I was on a Lewis gun.  Well I am not now &amp; have not been for a long time but as for it being a risky job it is no more so than anything else, bombers, riflemen &amp; all are much of a muchness there being no difference as far as risk goes, a soldier now is one &amp; all combined or whatever the emergency requires.</p>
