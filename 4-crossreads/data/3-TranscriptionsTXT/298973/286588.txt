Victoria Barracks 58 4/6 r.p. 12.50Rev Edwards Lindfield.
Officially reported that Number 1340 Pte A E Fry 13th Battalion died of wounds14th August please inform Mr C.Fry Northcote Lindfield and convey deep regret and sympathy of their Majesties the King and Queen and Commonwealth Government in loss that she and army have sustained by death of soldier.
