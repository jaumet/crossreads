Last evening I went to a service in the Military Church Hut.  I don't know what the Military Church is for certain except that it is C. of E., but from all I saw it seems to me to be a very high church.Half of a large hut was furnished in a style as near the real thing as possible & a bell had even been hung over the adjoining reading room.The officiating clergyman was not by any means intelligent
