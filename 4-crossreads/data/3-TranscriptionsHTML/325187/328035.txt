<p>[Thursday 19 October 1916]</p>
<p>Wrote Mother yesterday packed up for Blackpool.<br/>Raining &amp; Cold<br/>Dolly well.<br/>Mrs Cotton here to Afternoon tea; took her to Trocadero good music, but London is cold &amp; I'll be glad to go to a warmer climate if possible.<br/>Address all letters as before<br/>1st Light Horse Reg<br/>Egypt.<br/>Laura comes back from Walsall on Tuesday 24th October so I hope to see here again-<br/>Enquired for Jones as requested by Joe &amp; enclose all information I can get here.</p>
