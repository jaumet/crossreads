<p>17 - the girls to see Cousin Marian and family tomorrow. They collected them all before &ndash; last Sunday to see me. The newly married Queneth etc but I was in Manchester.<br/>Ethel Stenger has been to see me twice and is so king and warm hearted. She is looking up Gibson.<br/>Mrs S. Skirving wrote me and asked me to dinner. I went and had a nice talk with her and saw Wyn Laidly &ndash; Archies's regiment goes soon may have gone. Mrs S &amp; Wyn went down to Basingstoke to see him off.<br/>He is a fine fellow &ndash; and ought to make a good officer.</p>
