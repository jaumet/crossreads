<p>leave for Edinburgh by 10.30 p.m. train &ndash; A dreary night.</p>
<p>Feb.26th<br/>Arrive Waverley Station Edinburgh &ndash; Old &amp; familiar scenes. Put up at Old Waverley Hotel. &ndash; At mid-day I meet my dear little Scotch friend &ndash; Afternoon spent in writing &ndash; shopping  etc &ndash; Movies at night (Mary Pickford)<br/>Feb.27th<br/>Diary &amp; letters &ndash; again meet Jack Carey. &ndash; A quiet but enjoyable evening spent at 59 Brunswick St &ndash; Home of my friend.</p>
