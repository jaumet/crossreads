  
 Monday Oct 15 Another dust storm. What a desolate God forsaken hole this part of Palestine is. Sand seems to work its way in everything. Tuesday Oct 16 Saw another air scrap to day. Hun brought down secured piece of wing as souvenir. Heavy bombardment. Seems general opinion we are likely to move any time now. Wednesday Oct 17 Hot day. Flies & Sand bad Mail in afternoon. Received two parcels so cheered up
