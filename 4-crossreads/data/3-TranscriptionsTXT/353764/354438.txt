3.4.16 MondayI had a hard days work & am feeling very tired.The 5th 7th & 8th Battalions had to be moved to Helmieh & also F.A. & Ambulances.You never saw such a scene of chaos in your life as Helmieh was to-day.  Imagine an armies Baggage all dumped within a Certain Area with Wagons going this way & that & absolutely choking up all available spaces.I had about 20 to 30 Wagons on the job & there were also 15 Motor Wagons there.The English Motor Transport is going to take over our work & all the Australians have to leave.30,000 Aust Sail in a fortnights time & English troops are going to Garrison the Country.There was an unfortunate accident here to-day in which a N.Z. lost his life.  The Horse he was riding bolted & collided with a Cart.
