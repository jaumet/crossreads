 January, 1916 Sunday 16 Make another move this morning. All the M.G. Instrs. are put into several new tents in another part of the camp Have tea &amp; play cards w/- Dad. 
 Monday 17 Morning, Instruction Care &amp; Cleaning. &amp; Afternoon Judging distance out on the desert. Very tired so turned in early Very windy all day
