  
 February 1918 Sunday 24 
 Messines Had a fairly quiet day, and visited Gunn, and Da Costa at Anti Tank position. Garfott on F.O.O. my turn tomorrow. Fritz shelled the Corner Dump like hell for an hour Received letters  from Mrs Ladd, M, Mother and Uncle Graham the former via Mr [indecipherable] Weather dull, but with clear patches. Great aeriel activity. Won five francs form Russell.  Laurence returned form Leave.
