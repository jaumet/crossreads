 A thick fog hung over the city &amp; the congregation was not very large. I enjoyed the service. Rev Dinsdale Young preached from 5.40 to 7.8.Then said he Lo I am come to the volume of the book it is written of me I delight to do thy will It was a good solid sermon  4 points were taken (1) The reminiscent sermon 2. The Annunciatory sermon (3) The prophetic sermon 4 The obedient sermon I thought the first point a little strained but with the 10th Chapter of Hebrews as a commentary on it.
