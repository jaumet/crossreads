<p>The Blue Tiled Mosque was the prettiest. It is electrically lighted by thousands of small lights &amp; we saw them switched on. The effect of these artificial lights on the glistening marble is a sight worth going along way to see. We then visited one of the turrets &amp; had a magnificent panoramic view of Cairo. From here on to the Citadel where I understand some of the long term prisoners are kept. We saw the spot where a German Spy was shot. Joseph's well &amp; returned to Town after a most pleasant afternoon. In the evening our Unit gave an open air concert which turned out successful.<br/>Thursday Aug 9th<br/>On <strike>Picquet</strike> Picket usual Routine to day.</p>
