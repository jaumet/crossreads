<p>Medical examination of 2nd Brig and 9th Wrote to Vi.</p>
<p>8th Medical examination for whole camp to select men to relieve infantry on lines of communication. Infantry to go forward. I am passed as Unfit.</p>
<p>9 Camp. Routine</p>
<p>10 Went for a walk to Mission Station in forenoon.</p>
<p>11.12 13-14 Ditto [Note in margin: 14th]</p>
<p>16 paid &pound;1-00 16-17-18 Ditto camp 19th Camp</p>
<p>20/12/16 30 men from our camp (unfit) for Dar es salaam en route for Union J Banks among the number</p>
<p>22-23 40 more men left for Dar Es Salaam en route for Union Xmas Comforts Tobacco Cigarettes Potted meat<br/>Raining</p>
<p>24 Left Morogoro at 8 pm. for D E Salaam. arrived 9 am 25/12/16 hell of a journey</p>
<p>25/12/16 drew Kit again for trip home expect to leave for Union to day or tomorrow. Banks, Street &amp; Co still in camp D E S probably leave together</p>
<p>26/12/16 Made a false start for ship at 5.30 am 6 am. order cancelled still waiting a possible chance of going to day &ndash;</p>
<p>27-12-16 Leaving to go aboard. 5.30. AM. Left Dar Es Salaam 8.45 am per lighter and tug got to within a mile of our ship and then turned back for some unexplained reason (The Tug too light popular rumour)</p>
<p>28/12/16 Marched down to wharf again, all sailing orders cancelled. our third attempt to get away</p>
