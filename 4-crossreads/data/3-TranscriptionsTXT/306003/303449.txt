 29-7-16 Barrow (B Troop C Sqd) was very unlucky he only returned from England 3 days ago, &amp; was shot through the neck this morning the bullet coming out before the temple.  Longworth was wounded through the hip &amp; Peters was wounded &amp; captured by the Turks.  our lads could see the Turks dressing his wounds before they took him away. 
 W. Long (B Sqd Sig) received a slight wound on the wrist &amp; his horse was shot in two places;  Rinalde [?] A Sqd was also slightly wounded &amp; Watkins (C Sqd) was wounded &amp; could not be brought in &amp; is reported missing.  We did not wait at Qatia on our way home but came straight through &amp; arrived in camp at 11 oclock  One of our 'planes was brought down over Oghratina today by an Avatik
