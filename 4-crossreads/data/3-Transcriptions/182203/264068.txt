<p>so you see we have to take every precaution. This is  a very steady boat &amp; comfortable also good food. We are all very happy. Had a concert on board last night but it was not very successful because so many were recovering from sea sickness. Sister Strickland is matron of this Ship &ndash; she trained with me at the Melb. Hosp so we have great yarns</p>
