 Thursday 2nd September I was bad during the night and had to go to the rear on several occasions. Although it was midnight &amp; later had company on nearly every occasion. Things were still a bit quiet during the day. 
 Friday 3rd September The weather nice &amp; crisp with a cool breeze. Getting the store rebuilt a little in case of Winter protection &ndash; a quiet day. Went to Ordnance for clothing etc.
