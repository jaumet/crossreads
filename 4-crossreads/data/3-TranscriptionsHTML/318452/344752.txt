<p>Sunday Sept 16th<br/>We left Ottowa Camp Ooderdene today at 8 oclock  &amp; marched to Dickebush huts, a couple of miles up. The traffic is tremendous on the main roads. We passed a tank on the way &amp; another was standing near our camp I had a good look at it <strike>and</strike> both inside &amp; outside. The effect of their first appearance mush have been a terrific surprise for the bosh.<br/>Our huts are in a clump of hills.
