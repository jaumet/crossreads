<p>Dec 17<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;The weather was wild &amp; snowing in the morning - I had to get a tow to start my engine again, but carried on with the Supplies - Fritz is bombing near here very heavily - our guns are bombarding continuously -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dec 18<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Fine day but a bit of wind made it very cold - I got a tow to start my engine again, after the engine is warm, I can start it by hand. Our guns are still hard at it - To-morrow we draw at Steenwerck railhead as the Brigade is moving towards De Suel - The 1<sup><u>st</u></sup>&nbsp;D.S.C. took the place of the 5<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;D.S.C. &amp; we remained attached to them.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dec 19<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Beautiful day but cold, freezing all day - We drew to-day at Steenwerck . the brigade moved to-day - 106<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&amp; B.A.C. up at Neuve Eglise &amp; 16<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;17<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&amp; 18<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;Batteries at De Seul &amp; on Bailleul.Armenti&egrave;res road near La Cr&egrave;che - We did&#39;nt get back to park till 5.P.M. The 12<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;Brigade moved into the waggon lines which the 6<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;vacated -</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Dec 20<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Very cold, ice bearing everywhere there was a thick rhyme on the trees all day. I went up to K column</p>
