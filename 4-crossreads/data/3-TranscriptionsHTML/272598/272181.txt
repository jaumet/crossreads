<p>Tuesday 8.5.17<br/>Being relieved by 15th F.Amb to-day, packing up to move out to take over A.D.S. &amp; M.D.S. to-morrow.  Rained very heavily this morning</p>
<p>Wednesday 9.5 17<br/>8.30 full dress parade.  Moved off at 12 noon in motor lorries.  Arrived at Vauex about 4.30 pm rather a hot spot from all accounts further out; will be working on the line in front of Bullecourt &amp; Queant, (Hindenberg Line)<br/>Took over M.D.S. from 6th F Amb</p>
