<p>were issued out &amp; then for learning the foot drill &amp; rifle. I was put in the training squad for a couple of weeks.</p>
<p>Feb. 1st/<br/>Fifty of us made into the 5th Reinft. of 6th Regt. The month was continued with  the usual: camp drill, gaurde &amp; range shooting.</p>
<p>March 3rd/<br/>Went on final leave to Aberdeen &amp; had a good time three days only were granted for final leave; came back &amp; we are expecting to sail any day.</p>
<p>April 27th<br/>Went to measles contact camp, when about to come out fell sick with influenza. The troop sailed on May 20th &amp; I missed them, it was hard luck.</p>
