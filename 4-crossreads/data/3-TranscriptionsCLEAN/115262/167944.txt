  
 Tuesday 26 June 1917. 
 The parades this morning were under Lt Col. Croll. our O.C. (orig 2nd L.H.F.A.) & he complimented us on our marching etc. He said it was best of any Fd Amb. that had come to Egypt. Allotted to diffeent  divizions  sections for active service in the field. I was allotted as Stretcher Bearer. George Cameron & I are now separated in this respect. 
 Issued with plate & pannikin & khaki shorts during afternoon. Up to this date since disembarkation we were compelled to use our mess tin for all meals. 
 Mess orderly for B mess.
