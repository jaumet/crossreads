<p>January 1918<br/>Saturday 12</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Still on Battery Runner, but have not been so busy today.<br/>Weather cold and wet, would rather be in by the fire than out. <br/>We played 49th battery football and beat them badly, by 35-13. Boys are all jubilant, as this victory puts us at the head of our class.</p>
