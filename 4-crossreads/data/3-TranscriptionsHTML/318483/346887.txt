This work then was proposed &amp; recommended <strike>to me by the</strike> by the G.O.C. to me as the representative of the Commonwealth pictorial records in Egypt &amp; Palestine in conformity with the mission or responsibility imposed upon me by the Commonwealth through Australia House.  There would be no necessity for G.O.C. <strike>to write you on this subject</strike> the plan being made &amp; carried out in accordance with the wording of the Contract "Past Present &amp; Future Operations" you will remember.</p>
