<p>The men too were worn out with lack of sleep, marching &amp; fighting day &amp; night, and their grit &amp; cheerfulness was marvellous, &amp; it says wonders for their doggedness &amp; staying powers, that they went through such an ordeal with a minimum of sickness. When all did so well it is invidious to pick out individuals, with the exception of those already mentioned. But the Regt may be proud of having come through such a trying time with flying colours.</p>
