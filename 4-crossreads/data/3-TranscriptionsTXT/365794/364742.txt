 Tuesday 4 December 1917 Miss Coffy off duty with great rash over body due to very large dose of anti tetanus serum, she having handled the pt. who just died of the awful disease.  4 ops. from Connaught today &ndash; minor. 5 p.m. Miss Taten &amp; I got to town, garry (pt. 15) &amp; shop then home &amp; walk across from 14 A.G.H. way. Wrote M. Crookey. Dressings - for dirty wound to clean up - Use peroxide pure, gauze wet, then foment until slough comes away. Healing wounds - Dress with saline &amp; vary with red lotion occasionally.
