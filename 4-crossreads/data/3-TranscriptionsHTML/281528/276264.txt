<p>31/3/18 &ndash; La Basse Ville<br/>Slept on until about 9 a.m. then confined to studying a bit of French and doing some writing.  During the morning his m.w's were active and took some silencing.  After noon about 3.30 walked down to Bde. H.Q. and there saw the General and the B.M. and got some information regarding our relief tomorrow night.
