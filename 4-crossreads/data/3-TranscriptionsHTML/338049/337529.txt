<p>Friday 15 Nov.<br/>Obtained passes for [Kinga?], Richy's &amp; Joe to go into Abbeville by bike. Got in about 12 noon. Had a great time until about 8 p.m. Returned in moonlight. Pretty cold.</p>
<p>Visited mess on arrival. Got to bed late. Had a glorious day.</p>
