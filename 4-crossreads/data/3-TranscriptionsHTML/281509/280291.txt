<p>9 March 1916<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>No duties. Bob, Tony, Mac &amp; self go to former's place to tea. On way down in train, girl newly married Charlie Chaplin &ndash; eyes &ndash; lice  Get off at Petersham to go to dentist. not at home. &ndash; call at Macs for few minutes. pick up boys out at Waverley. Fine tea of duck vegetables sweets etc. Enjoyable evening. Leave for camp at 10.15 pm.  Home without incident.  Miss Teisher, Mrs. Robbins <strike>&amp;</strike> slip of tongue.</p>
