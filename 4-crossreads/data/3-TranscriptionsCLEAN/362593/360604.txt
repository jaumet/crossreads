
Monday 23 September 1918Rounds fired 1700, bombs dropped 16, trips 2, 2 h. 5, 2 h. 0.
Tuesday 24 September 1918Recco, roads.  Pilots Lt. McGuinness & Tonkin.In air 3 h. 35 m.Landed at Um es Senab with Arab forces, had an interesting time.  Landed at A Fuleh, gone in report, then home.A Fuleh was old Hun aerodrome.4 machines intact there.
