<p>11/5/16  Thursday</p>
<p>Drilling &amp; Tent Pitching practice from 6 am till 10 am &ndash; Then had a bonsur swim, I swim across the channel twice each time I go down just for practice &ndash; Major Moseley said that as we worked so well in the morning, we could have the rest of the day off &ndash; A Big Australian Mail is supposed to be in &amp; we are all roaring at the way the Postal arrangements are run, some of us intend parading &ndash; some of the Boys have not had any mail for six months. A Postal Corps was sent from Australia to run things properly, but all they seem to do is play tennis with the Gypo's &amp; amuse themselves generally &ndash; The Anti Air Gun Corps have left for Rail head. Still have rumours of going to Mesopotamia &ndash; France &ndash; Tripoli -</p>
<p>12/5/16  Friday</p>
