<p>20 February 1916</p>
<p>Stay in bed till mid-day.  Mary brings me breakfast at 9. Stubby chin. Come into Ashfield in afternoon. Stay to tea.  water the garden. Church Baptist. Hot &amp; steamy. Catch train to Sydney.  Meet Rex Chapman on station. Home 11 pm.</p>
