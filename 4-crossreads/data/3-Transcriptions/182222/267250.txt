<p>6<sup>th</sup> Getting settled in new quarters all very broke. Officer put in charge of us (Lt Bruce) a plausible youngster with sandy hair &amp; a red complexion.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Up in Aureol I was just getting set. Had made several useful dark friends at the old stone clink./ <u>Timence </u>native troops Big people, well made &amp; not bad looking, less lip &amp; nose &amp; more forehead. very straight &amp; dignified. Quiet mannered &amp; hospitable. Great for a joke &amp; have a keen sense of humour. made excellent punch, women also superior type &amp; not scared stupid in presence of the men. Look upon all whites as amusing creatures to be politely entertained &amp;</p>
