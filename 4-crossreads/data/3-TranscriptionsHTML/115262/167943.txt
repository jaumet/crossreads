<p>Monday 25 June 1917.</p>
<p>4 a.m. reveille. Drill till 7.30 a.m.</p>
<p>Practice for coming N.C.O&#39;s exam.</p>
<p>At 10.30 a.m. full dress parade &amp; were officially handed over to D.G.M.S. by Lt Colonel Croll. DIsmissed &amp; went to our tents at 11.30 a.m.</p>
<p>Another batch of troops left for firing line last night at 12 mnght.</p>
<p>Hospital&nbsp;train passed through to Suez. Train of wagons arrives at main camp. Moved kits &amp; tents during afternoon to new sight [site].</p>
<p>[The following sentence is crossed out:] In the morning for a time was on fatigue in charge of party.</p>
