<p>30th 7p.m. Like hell in "the Bull Ring" (below)<br/>Everybody feeling kark<br/>Land appears on Port Bow Light House flashes momentarily every 6 seconds. Showers turned off.<br/>11pm Pass L House flashing 3 successive flashes every 10 seconds. Cool during night.<br/>31st Hot again. 11am pass Perim. Midnight Blowing &amp; slight swell on. Soldier's wives make a song over seasickness. Cool.<br/>1/8/19 Swell on again .Hot as hell again. Preparing for thick weather &ndash; sails furled Thick &amp; heavy weather 5pm.</p>
