<p>July 1915<br/>Sunday 11<br/>Fine. Drew rations for the Camp. Attended a church service conducted by Rev. Mackenzie on the hill near the wind mills. Had to pull a barge inshore to be cleaned out ready for storing water.</p>
<p>Monday 12<br/>Fine. Eight men of us went down and cleaned the barge out this morning. Drew rations again from the 29 Divisional Transport tent.</p>
