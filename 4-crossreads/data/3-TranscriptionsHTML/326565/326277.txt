<p>24/12/15 (Friday)<br/>Arose 7 AM.  Camp now 11 miles from canal, situated between Bitter Lakes &amp; TIMSAH Lake.  One loaf between 12 of us for dinner, rather short rations.  Commenced another dugout during afternoon, to act as a dressing station.  Shower of rain at night, rather unusual</p>
