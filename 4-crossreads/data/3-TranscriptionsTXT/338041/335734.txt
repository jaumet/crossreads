 Sept. 19th Neuralgia &ndash; visit to dentist. Continuation of good news &amp; bad weather. 
 Sept. 20th. "Our boys" beaten at Australian Rules. Mail from Home. 
 Sept 21st We win our second game of Soccer, beating 4th Field Amb by 4 goals to nil &ndash; I spent the night at Y.M.C.A with Asher Chowne &ndash; Glorious weather. 
 Sept 22nd Wet &amp; stormy weather prevents work or pleasure. 
 Sept. 23rd A route march gives us our first distant view of the town of Ypres &ndash; In the afternoon we move camp from Reninghelst to Poperinghe.
