<p>April, 1917. Monday 2<br/>Up at 8. Day strong S. wind and heavy snow storm at 5. Pumped in morning and a lot of water going out. Rations &amp; officer came at 9 &ndash; wrote letters in aftn. &amp; turned in early and continued at 7. &amp; read Surveying and French.</p>
