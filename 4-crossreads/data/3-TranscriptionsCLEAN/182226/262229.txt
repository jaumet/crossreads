  
 Monday 26 July, 1915 
 Work commences today, most of it is done in the cool & we are allowed to lie down & rest in the hot hours. 
 The drill hours are from 6 a.m. to 9, lecture in tent from 11 a.m. to 12. Dinner at 1 p.m. drill from 5 p.m. to 7. 
 We are allowed 8d per day by Egyptian Govt & it is given to us in the shape of tinned fish & fruit etc which we have at midday.
