
October 1918.Sunday 20
left of Ribeaville. Bty Pos.3 am Fritz put down a barrage of gas and H.E. very constant and close too. Knocked the runners mule. Walid came along and called out that he was off to Blighty. Major and Gill swimming about 4 am. Peter Redmond ,made a Major.Busy salvaging wire all day long, very heavy work lately and an tired out. So is old Tom Code. Pulled in about two miles raining all the while and I'm feeling just about done in.
