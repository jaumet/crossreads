great white clouds were drifting across the sky throwing fleeting shadows across the old beech trees, which are just beginning to get faintly stained with a green tinge which heralds the approaching Spring, but the most beautiful part of it all was the lovely carpet of early wild flowers for they were there in abundance in all colours violets anaemones, crocuses, daisies cowslips, primroses, buttercups lots of other pretty little flowers that I don't know the name of, the whole land was just a mass of color, it was delightful walking in such a bonza place, I picked up a number of flowers am going to try press them
We have had music nearly all the day for the bands of the 27th 25th our Battalion have been giving selections
