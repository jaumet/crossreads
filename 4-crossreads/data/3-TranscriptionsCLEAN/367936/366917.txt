
Thursday 13 January 19161st Parade, attended class for N.C.Os.2nd Fatigue duty in lines.3rd Little extended drill then more fatigue duty cleaning parade ground.  Took my boots to boot maker to have them stretched.  Concert in evening;  music supplied by the men.
Friday 14 January 19161st Parade, N.C.O's class.2nd Marched to trenched ground and practiced hand grenade throwing from trenches, with jam tins filled with sand.3rd Rifle and extended order drill.Slight rain during day which layed the dust beautifully.
