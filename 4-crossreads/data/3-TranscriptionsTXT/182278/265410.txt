
Monday 15/4/18. Intercompany relief  caught prisoner, visited posts
Tuesday 16/4/18  wired half front & commenced to connect posts. Wrote to Doff.
Wednesday 17/4/18. Finished wiring & connecting posts  dug a C.T. Herbert Killed.
Thursday 18/4/18 Relieved by Currie- moved back to Support
Friday 19/4/18 Remained in support  2 Coys digging in front area
Saturday 20/4/18. Relieved by Dawson  moved back to Reserve  one Coy bathed- Two letters from Doff.
