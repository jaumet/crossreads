
13 June 18Anchored at S. Flow. Blowing a gale all day, at its worst about 10 pm. Two observation baloons broke away & have not been seen or heard of since.
14 June 18Still at anchor
15 June 18Went to sea & done 6 inch firing
16 June 18At anchor
17 June 18Done firing in the Flow.
18 June 18Left Scapay Flow to pick up convoy.
19 June 18At sea. Convoy arrived in Bergen Norway safely & we then picked up another to bring back to England.
20 June 18Returning with convoy
21 June 18Dropped convoy at Lerwick Scotland
