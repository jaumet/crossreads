<p>1915<br/>Mar. 15 Mon.<br/>Wrote up a lot of Scout work during the day.<br/>Cloudy all the morning &amp; in the afternoon rain fell &ndash; first since arriving in Egypt.<br/>Attended a meeting of our Scouts Association in the evening, which was adjourned until to-morrow night.<br/>Capt. G.L.B. Concanon came into Hospital with Enteritis.</p>
