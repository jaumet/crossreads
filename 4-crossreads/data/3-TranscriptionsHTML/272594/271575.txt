<p>In the afternoon we took the camera out &amp; done some snapshooting, goodness only knows how they will come out for we are both novices, there are some very pretty little scenes around here.</p>
<p>We live here to some order. breakfast, porridge, bacon &amp; eggs, or chops, plenty of bread butter jam, sauces &amp; etc. dinner roast, soup, fish &amp; a sweet, tea cold ham or beef or something like that &amp; plenty of jam butter &amp; such things besides there is a great variety of dishes</p>
