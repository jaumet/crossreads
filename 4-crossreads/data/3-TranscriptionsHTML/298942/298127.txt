<p>While I was at [indecipherable] our division was on the way to the Somme where they stopped (Amiens) for a couple of days.</p>
<p>No sooner had they had left the northern sector (north of Messines), which was left very thinly held, than the Germans broke through the Portuguese &amp; made their rapid dash on Bailleul.</p>
<p>Neuve Eglise &amp; Bailleul proved to be slight checking places, but after the capture  of Ballleul things were very critical indeed</p>
