<p>Sept. Thursday 6 1917</p>
<p>Large air raids over Chatham &amp; London.   A good deal of damage having been caused.   'Tis very hard to prevent these moonlight raids.   The air's a big space &amp; night time helps.<br/>Our airmen also make similar raids over his country, but it is a rotten mode of warfare, but has been forced upon us.</p>
