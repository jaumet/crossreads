  
 Aug 10th More Reinforcements - familiar faces from Liverpool - The King passes through two villages but does not pay us the visit we expected - patients arrive in hundreds - Anzacs good work 
 Aug 11th. "Bon" news from all fronts. Continuation of night duties - A further advance by Anzacs. 
 Aug 12th. Return of boys from Puchevillers and formation of "C" section. 
 Aug 13th. Good news from both Russian and Balkan fronts - News of Eddie Cornish. 
 Aug. 14th M's Birthday - Rainy & miserable day - Fourth Division heavy losses.
