 Bologne way I think they must either be the 3rd Division or some of our new formations, &amp; New Zealanders are also pouring in. We have been practicing all the morning, &amp; again this afternoon we were out drilling one another &amp; detailing by turn, this evening I came out on my own to do a bit of studying. I had a couple of hours at it, &amp; then the pretty scene in front of me claimed my attention. I am sitting on a little grassy bank overlooking the sea for miles, &amp; it flows right at my feet at the present time for the tide is coming in, away to the West &amp; towards old England
