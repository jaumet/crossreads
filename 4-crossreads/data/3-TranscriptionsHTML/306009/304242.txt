<p>1915<br/>Feb. 25 Thu.<br/>Patients in Hospital reach record total of 956 &amp; it should not be more than 520, according to regulations.<br/>A Sergeant &amp; 2 Privates of No. 2 G.H. are to be court-martialled for stealing about &pound;7 from a patient's clothes.</p>
