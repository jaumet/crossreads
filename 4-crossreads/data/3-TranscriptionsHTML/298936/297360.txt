<p>June 1915</p>
<p>Wednesday 30</p>
<p>Met Fothergill on way to catch train &amp; delighted to find he would be a companion to Brisbane for he goes to enlist.</p>
<p>Would give anything to go to Rita again but it cannot be.</p>
<p>Rev. Ware up at station &amp; two acquaintances to say farewell.</p>
<p>Once again leave Grafton Station but on more serious business than ever before.</p>
<p>Rather inclined to be bluish although the stark fact of parting from Rita is deadening as [indecipherable] a numbing of feeling stayed [indecipherable].</p>
