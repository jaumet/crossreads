1917 MAY
20 SUNDAY
Fine & warm, I went down to
L'Eppinette dump in morning
with waggons for amunition
& we had a half holiday in
afternoon. Had a turn out
at night but got back about
eleven oclock. A lot of the
boys went for a bath in
the morning. Laurie Cadell
was here in afternoon
