<p>Novr 17th<br/>Personal<br/>Novr 18th</p>
<p>1916<br/>Off the coast of <strike>Algeria</strike> again quite hot , did some big gun practice [indecipherable] ours &ndash; Understand we are to go to &ndash; first this will be interesting Must be many letters waiting for me in<strike>Egypt</strike>. &ndash; We have the usual Gymnasium of mechanicals riding horses etc We have many different regiments on board &ndash; One Scotch officer at <strike>Gibraltar</strike> when I admired his kilts offered to lend them to me to be photographed in, he said they cost &pound; 5 in Scotland. He thought he was doing me a great favor</p>
