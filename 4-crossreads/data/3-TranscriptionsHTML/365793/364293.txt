<p>July 10 been picked for guard  Damn disgrace I reckon. Unable get leave for over a week.</p>
<p>11 Two of the cripples are doing a hop on the hut floor; in fact the one limbed, &amp; the lame are the jolliest boys in the crowd.<br/>Wandered away over the hills &amp; discovered the picturesque Village of Wyke with its fine old Church, neat little gardens, &amp; shady avenues.<br/>In the distance was Portland with its famous prison.</p>
<p>12 Cold, &amp; still more cold; &amp; now its raining a treat. My blankets</p>
