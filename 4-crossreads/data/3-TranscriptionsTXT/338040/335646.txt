 Mar 31st Early morning march- game of football- host of Aeroplanes "herring and pears" for lunch- stretcher drill. Apr 1st April Fools Day- no practical jokes or false alarms- Football and Boxing under Capt Brownell- Lecture Capt Hamilton "Veneral Disease". Continued Bombardment on British front heard throughout day. Half holiday in afternoon.
