<p>Arthur Fry Esq<br/>Mines Dept.<br/>21 Feb 1900</p>
<p>My dear Sir,<br/>Will you oblige by supplying a contribution on any subject in which you are interested for publication in the March number of the P. S. Journal? I am desiring that the paper should be both useful and attractive, and for that reason I want to get articles on a variety of subjects from Civil Servants who have thought power and literary skill.
