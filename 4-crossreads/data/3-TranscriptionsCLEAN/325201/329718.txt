
Gaza.A man dies in good company hereabouts, fabulous adventurers from Egypt under the Ptolemies, heroes with Alexander the great ,  grim legionaries of Antony &  Ceasar.All fought  &  died before Gaza. During the Crusades 30000 Knights Templars were slain in a battle which raged for 5 days in the Valley of Gaza. While later still Napoleons army toiled along these inhospitable shores.
