 16 2/6/16 Very quiet day to-day but to-morrow will see things busy again as the 13th Bde leave. We entrain on Sunday so our time in Egypt is limited now. I haven't received word from home for some time now I suppose it will pick us up in France. 
 13-6-16 Arrived Marseilles. 
 23/6/16. Quite a lot has happened between the 2nd &amp; present date but it is all so impressed on my memory that the need of a diary is out of the question.
