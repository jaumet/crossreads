
[Saturday 26 August 1916]
Calm.  Bound for Southampton [indecipherable] Blind & unfit to travel  100's of Stretcher cases be good to crippled men after this war.  I'm thankful I'm able to get about-Still we are all merry & bright.The boat is a blaze of light at night & so different to a Troop ship.Of course I'm going to Dotty but there's only one [indecipherable] & you Mother I hope you get my letter & photos from Alex.  Only 4 Australian officers on board so we must be careful per example
