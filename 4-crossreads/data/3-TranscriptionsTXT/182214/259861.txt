 Band swung along playing the Marseillase. all the windows opened &amp; heads of women &amp; men popped out cheering &amp; waving their handkerchiefs. It sounded well &amp; they cheered our men who looked fine in their Aussy uniforms &amp; Hats &amp; sunburned faces, as soon as they entered the station yard the gates were closed &amp; the people still stayed there for some time afterwards, about midnight we entrained our party were in cattle trucks which were better than the carriages as one could get a lay down to sleep. we started on our long journey up north &amp; we followed the river Rhone to Lyons.
