<p>5th News has just come through that the Allies have granted Austria her Armistice. Great excitement on board-Sea calm.<br/>6th Everything very quiet except for the West Australians who expect to land in a day or so.<br/>11th Wireless message has just been posted up saying Germany has been granted and Armistice. But no one seems to worry much. Most chaps are talking of what they will so when they are discharged, but nearly all of us are sorry that we were not in at the death. Still as we are all original 1914 men</p>
