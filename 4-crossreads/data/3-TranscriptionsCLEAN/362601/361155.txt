
6 February, Thursday
F & iceI arise at 6.30 am & breakfast. Catch 7.50 train [indecipherable] to B'mouth & arrive at Downton, wait until 9 pm [?am] & then walk until picked up by an ASC [Army Service Corps] wagon for 3 miles. Arr at Sarum & visit Winnie & Cis. Buy shoes for Dick & get to Sarum Pub by motor 4 a pint & walk back for dinner. Chat with Morris Gow & set out at 3 for Home. Catch Red X Amb to Ford & YMCA Car to B'ford arr 4.15 pm. Go to Pictures with T. D. R. F. & I see Hindle Wakes.
7 February, Friday
F & iceWe conut[?] beds again & I tidy up down in room.
We are all in a very gay mood especially when thinking about "Old Guard" we go up to the Flat & yarn & chat & have one or two hands at Bridge
Mote MRC & Home
