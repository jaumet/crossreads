 January, 1915 Saturday 16 We marched over the desert to the rifle range &amp; the remainder of the Batt fired their practice. We did not work hard &amp; then marched back about three o'clock. Wrote to Flo. Every man issued with a field service dressing containing fine gauze, bandage wool &amp; waterproof.
