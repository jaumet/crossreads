<p>Sunday 18 February 1917<br/>Quiet day:  wrote Mum &amp; Jess.  Clarrie got back from School.</p>
<p>Monday 19 February 1917<br/>On Engineers fatigue in morning, mounted parade after dinner &amp;</p>
<p>Tuesday 20 February 1917<br/>On exercise during day:  A &amp; B Sq.:  played footy:  A won 3-0.  On picquet at night.  Wrote Theo &amp; "Purpley".</p>
<p>Wednesday 21 February 1917<br/>Out on exercise in morning on "pumping party", did some washing:  got ready for leave.</p>
<p>Thursday 22 February 1917<br/>Left camp at 5.30:  caught the 8.30 from Bir-el-Abd &amp; the 1.10 from Kantarah, got to Cairo 5.3 - went round to Mona Piot's for evening, had a very enjoyable evening:  stayed at Anzac Hostel, got to bed about 1.30.</p>
<p>Friday 23 February 1917<br/>Got a few things for the boys in the morning:  then got photo taken at Zola:  took taxi to Mrs. Brand's where had lunch, afternoon tea at Mrs. Walkers:  &amp; dinner with Mrs. Piot &amp; pictures afterwards.</p>
<p>Saturday 24 February 1917<br/>Took Sister and Robbo &amp; Jack Long for a spin to the Pyramids in evening &amp; had tea at Anzac:  then to Zola for my proofs &amp; then caught the 6.15 for Kantarah arriving at 10.30 stayed at Empire Club till daylight.  Wrote Mother &amp; Jess.</p>
