<p>29.12.16</p>
<p>Many thanks for parcel of Xmas gifts received today which I fully appreciate. Owing to the engagement we had with our old enemies the gifts were not distributed until today but altho' a little late for Xmas  they arrived at the time they were most needed.</p>
<p>Yours gratefully<br/>No.53  W J Bell.  1st LHFA</p>
