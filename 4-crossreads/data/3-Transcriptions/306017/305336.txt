<p>&amp; are living fairly well.  The horses are looking fairly fit, but my own opinion is that a bit more feed wouldn't harm them any especially as they are on harder work here most of the country being drift sand &amp; up &amp; down hill it is solid work &amp; more than 3 miles an hour is impossible.  An Australian mail arrived on the 5th &amp; I got a couple of letters one from mother &amp; another from Doles &amp; I also received a fair number of papers which were very welcome as we had had nothing to read for days.</p>
<p>On the 5th my troop went out to Tina Bay, about 6 miles from here as an escort to a party of engineers.  It was alright being on the beach but it would have been better had we been able to have a swim, but then we were there for work.
