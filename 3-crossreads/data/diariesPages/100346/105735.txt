got to Rubempre about 7.30 pm was pretty tired : met all the old boys that are left so was O.K. : 
Monday June 11th
Rubempre : hot day : handed in my equipment rifle so am S.B. again : cleaned up round the billets : looked J Marsland up : 
Tuesday June 12th
Rubempre : hot day : reveille 6 am : walked into Amiens to get my films : got a lift out again : boxing at night :
Wednesday June 13th
Rubempre : reveille 6 am : early parade 6.30 am : wrote in afternoon :
Thursday June 14th
Rubempre : reveille 6 am : moved off after dinner to Puighvillers entrained : passing through the "Somme" battlefield was some sight : got out at Bapaume :
