<p>[Tuesday 9 May 1916]</p>
<p>re Rail warrant 54143<br/>Had a swim in the Canal yesterday I mean the SUEZ not the dirty freshwater Nile Canals that we have lived on in Upper Egypt for so long.<br/>Great Ships passed but no one takes any notice of the brown naked soldiers &amp; it keeps them so fit.<br/>Court Dissolved &amp; I'm waiting Cairo for my Horses &amp; Batman before going Kantara, you will see it on the Canal, north of Ismalia.  Our Camp is 20 mile to the East on a <strike>PIPELINE</strike> Military Railway which is being extended daily</p>
