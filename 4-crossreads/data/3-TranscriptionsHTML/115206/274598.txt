<p>January 1918<br/>Monday 28</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Owing to some mess up in the acquittance rolls we did not draw our money as expected.<br/>Weather very fine.<br/>Preparing for the road. <br/>I am once again Battery Runner.<br/>Football team played a match at Paris Plage but were beaten.<br/>Sgt Henderson arrived back and I is charge of "F" sub.</p>
