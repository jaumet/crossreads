<p>&amp;  having resigned to the inevitable. Talk about hot though &amp; to add our mess food of a tough material sarcastically called steak &amp; "rabbit's food"  (a mixture of herbs called salad day after day) makes a pleasant life.</p>
<p>13th May:- Saturday<br/>Hot, I should not like to describe it otherwise. The wind is about two blasts from hell combined &amp; one is afraid of his hair being burnt. Only 110 &deg; in the shade, but awful. Those fellows who got away in time to avoid this heat are more than lucky. This afternoon I took some photos out at Giza    &amp;  tonight attended a picture show (the usual Egyptian production)</p>
