 Monday 27th December Nothing doing, just standing by awaiting orders.  At night walked round to the Hospital to post some mail & see some newspapers.  Coming back it was very dark & the recent rains having made some swamps into one of which I had the misfortune to wander.  I escaped however without more harm than a fair sprinkling of mud. 
 Tuesday 28th Dec 
 General routine once more resumed.  Physical drill before breakfast, route march after, ditto Wednesday. 
 Thursday 30th Orders came to send our gear to the jetty in readiness to move off.  This we completed during the morning.  In the afternoon went across to the Hospital to take a few snaps & exchange a tunic for one which fitted me. 
 Also had a hair cut.  On guard at night. 
 Friday 31st December 1915 New Years Eve On guard & had a prisoner to escort before the Colonel.  Went through my kit again in order to lighten it but did not manage to throw much out as it was all valuable, weather beautiful & sunny.
