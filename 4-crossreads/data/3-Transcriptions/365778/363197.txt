<p>Thursday 24/5/17<br/>Robinson returned. Regimental dinner a huge success.<br/>Friday 25/5/17<br/>Had a game of cricket. Boxing competitions at night. Had a spar.<br/>Saturday 26/5/17<br/>Visited Wisdom at Senlis, played football 1st round of Competition.<br/>Sunday 27/5/17<br/>Brigade Church parade at Senlis &ndash; Genl Birdwood presented medals to Officers, N.C.O.s &amp; men.<br/>Monday 28/5/17<br/>Refereed football match for 7th Engineers, Lloyd to Rouen &amp; Robinson to England.</p>
