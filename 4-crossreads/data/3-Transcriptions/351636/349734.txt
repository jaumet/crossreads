<p>The Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers &ndash;<br/>August 18th 1916</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Raymond &ndash;<br/>The bearer Mrs. Hodge is in need of assistance for some necessary dental trouble &amp; is unable to manage the expense without help &ndash;</p>
<p>Could you assist her. She will tell you what it is she needs &ndash;</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>B. Booth. Hon. Sec.</p>
