<p>May 11 Tuesday 1915<br/>Mounted parade  this morning from 6 AM to 8 AM<br/>Strong winds blowing clouds of dust<br/>Orders just received 10. 30 AM from Brigade Headquarters for the 3<br/>Regiments to stand ready to move on short notice preparations pushed on all day</p>
<p>May 12 Wednesday 1915<br/>Exercising horses this morning<br/>3 Officers to stay behind with horses &amp; men. Lieu Chisholm, Lieu Deane, &amp; Cap O'Brien,<br/>sorting out gear &amp; preparing to pack up. Received letters from M. &amp; Mother dated 11th April</p>
<p>May 13 Thursday 1915<br/>Major Fuller Lieu Cross Lieu Cork left 5 AM for Rifle Range &amp; will put through all reinforcements in musketry<br/>Remainder of men packing up saddles &ndash; equippment to leave behind</p>
<p>May 14 Friday 1915<br/>Brigade packing up all day.<br/>I went into Cairo to secure necessary articles for Major Fuller including Rodger &amp; Galleys Soap, cough mixture, tobacco,&amp; fixing his electric torch.</p>
