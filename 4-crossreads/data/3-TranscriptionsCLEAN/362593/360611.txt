  
 Wednesday 9 October 1918 
 Thursday 10 October 1918 Recco returned.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 20 m. This is the last trip I had on active service & is the only one on which McGuinness & self have had to return through engine trouble, except on one occasion when a dressing plug blew out.  Trouble overheating, water trouble.
