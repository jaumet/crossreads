<p>Spent most of the day clearing up negatives and writing letters.<br/>5th Novr. 17</p>
<p>Weather dull and misty. Went to Ypres. Climbed to the top of the Cloth Hall also to the risky summit of a &frac12; cut in two telegraph pole for panoramas.</p>
<p>6th Nov<br/>Weather drizzly remained at Steenvoorde siding up affairs. Interviewed Gen. Birdwood during the morning and showed him my photographs which has pleased him.
