<p>Sunday 8 July 1917<br/>Revaille at 3 a.m. &amp; went on a stunt to El Bugga in support to 2nd Bgde. N.Zers.  Stayed all day returning home at 10 p.m., plane landed close.</p>
<p>Monday 9 July 1917<br/>On Pump fatigue, went to dentist at 3.30, saw Guth at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 10 July 1917<br/>General fatigue, 1st Reg. played 2nd football, lost 6-5.</p>
<p>Wednesday 11 July 1917<br/>Usual fatigue, on picquet at night.</p>
<p>Thursday 12 July 1917<br/>1st Played 2nd L.H. footy and lost 9-3.</p>
<p>Friday 13 July 1917<br/>On Q.M. fatigue in morning and some washing in evening.</p>
<p>Saturday 14 July 1917<br/>Usual Routine, took horses out for grazing all day, on pumping fatigue, got word of stunt at 10 o'clock at night.</p>
