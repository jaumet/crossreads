Sunday 5
 Beginning of 4th year of war still no prospect of "victorious" ending though prospects of a compromise
Monday 6
 Beautiful day. Walk to Appleshaw through most beautiful private grounds with Cotterell
Wednesday 8
 Move to another part of camp where after having my teeth attended to, I will be "fit for France".
Friday 10
 Twenty two to-day.
 In to-days paper I notice that Capt Aspinal has been killed in action. He was a fine chap his loss will be deeply felt.
