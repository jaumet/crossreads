 December 1917 Sunday 23 Suffra.   Cold and showery all day Went outpost. Rained all night. Got soaked. 12 MN sat up.under tree until daybreak. Letter 47 JAR received. 
 Monday 24 Suffra.  Xmas eve. Showery and cold. No chance of drying clothes Terrific gale and rainstorm during night. Got wetter. Xmas parcels arrived from [indecipherable]
