 Entry 
  11th. Sept. 1917  A beautiful calm day, sun shining brightly.  calm. &nbsp;Have just come in from giving instructions in firing Hales No 20 Rifle Grenades &amp; Mills No. 23 with a 10 inch rod. 
 Last night we marched a mile across country in our box respirators.&nbsp; 
  Entry 19/9/17  In tent at Staples. We arrived by motor bus yesterday. I am Battalion Orderley Officer today. 
 The people at L- treated us very well &amp; were really sorry when we were leaving. 
 We had a Bn. dinner on 13/9/17 at L-&nbsp;a very decent affair. 
 Wrote home to J today.&nbsp;Received letter from George. 
  20/9/17  In tent shelter at Steenvoorde, West of Ypres. A terrific cannonading is going on this evening west, north west &amp; South of us.
