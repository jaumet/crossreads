Battn., referred to throughout diary, also as the 'Nids']23rdBeautifull sunsets the last few nights.  Seen a shar [shark?] tonight that left a reflection in the water like the moon.  Crossed the Equator today nothing happened.  Saw the Southern cross shining bravely in the South.
24th 3.16  FridayLeft the Southern cross behind at last should soon pick up the Pole Star.  Another medical examination today, between Inoculations Vaccinations &amp; short arm parades a fellow has very little time to spare.  My Hammock &amp; Blanket has disappeared probably in the hold.
