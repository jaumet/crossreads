<p>On incinerator.  Just after 6 o'clock Fritz gave us the usual welcome he gives all new troops to this town viz. a few shells.  Awful crashes and explosions occurred just down the street from us at 11.30 "square" and shrapnel burst overhead sending the pellets rolling along the footpath.  A number of casualties.</p>
