 13th Paid 20 francs &ndash; cigarettes issued. 14th On Guard in Camp &ndash; 24 hrs. sent home card. 15th issued with necessaries for firing line. 16th left Etaples 4-00 and disentrained at Steenwerck &ndash; march 8 miles to Billet &ndash; Stayed in horse Box for the night. 17th Drafted in A Company 18 Battn. &ndash; resting.  Gas alarm. 18th Went to support Trenches at 4-00 A.M. &ndash; building dug outs. 19th Fatigue to Front line. 20th Fatigue to Front line. 21st innocculated no duties.  Bombardment of Equingham &ndash; 200 shells.  Cigarette issue. 22nd Quarter Guard 24 hours. 23rd Went to Equingham for bath &ndash; paid 20 francs &ndash; Fatigue all night building parapets. 24th Rest all day &ndash; Mess orderly. 25th Fatigue 4 till 1 o'clock &ndash; Dug outs &ndash; Moved to Supports 2 o'clock &ndash; Fatigue building parapets 8 o'clock till 2 o'clock. 26th Sleep till 12 o'clock, making ready for firing line &ndash; Relieved 20th Batt. 9 o'clock &ndash; Stand too 2 till 4.
