  
 That General was Irving but General Tibby had sense & save his men by having the train at his disposal. Well I saw the start of the 14th Brigade the sun was coming up warm as they passed through the camp. The distance is only 30 miles but they must have travelled three times that distance as their General on horseback soon lost his bearings & went, here, & there till men began to fall exhausted & other went their own way.
