 After a very pleasant voyage, we sighted land at 10.15am on the 9th of Nov. Always to the front the Y.M.C.A. welcomed us   at Port O'Call No1 (Durban) even before the ship was made fast. From far out on the Jetty a young lady Semaphored the greetings of the ubiquitous and ever helpful institution. Come to the Y.M.C.A. Hut close to the Town Hall.
