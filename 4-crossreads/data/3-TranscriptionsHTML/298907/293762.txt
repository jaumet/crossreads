<p>girls over here are not thought the same of as they are in Australia as a stroll down any of the streets or a ride in the bus or tram will show, I suppose it is on account of them being so numerous, in one way I will be glad to get on leave &amp; get away from the Tommies I never saw such gluttons &amp; muldoon's  in my life if they think there is any chance of getting in for a second helping they will stuff &amp; gorge themselves like pigs racing one against the other to finish their first plateful, makes me feel as if I could knock their heads off, I wouldn't live among these people one day longer than I could possible help, if they are spoken to they won't say a word in self defence but take it all lying down, this is how for one reason the Colonials</p>
