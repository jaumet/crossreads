11.9.17Hired a carriage at 9.30 a.m. and went to Studland again where we had a short and very select bathe, which made us all tired and sleepy.  Whilst there I saw the prettiest pair of female legs it has ever been my fortune to gaze upon: short plump; with the most delightful turn inwards of the knees.
