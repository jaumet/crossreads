 27 June 17 Left Liverpool at 6 pm for sea. Weather cold &amp; misty. 
 28 June 17 At sea steaming along west coast of Scotland. Did steam trial today average 25.7 knots. 
 29 June 17 Arrived at Scapa Flow at 1 am this morning. Coaled ship at 9 am took in 380 tons.
