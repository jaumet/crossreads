 Wednesday 16.8.16 Quiet as usual, rained slightly,  Paid 40 Francs, &pound;1.8.8 
 Thursday 17.8.16 Still raining, roads very muddy. Received letters &amp; parcels from home.  Had a look over a fine old church in Doulieu a few days back, which was completely burnt out by the Germans, with the use of petroleum. 
 Friday 18.8.16 A &amp; B Sec came here route marching this morning, appear to be getting a rough time with squad drill again. 
 Saturday 19.8.16 Rained hard during morning.  Dozens of Tommies come here direct from Somme &amp; look much the worse for their experience; they tell some very appaling tales of the Slaughter
