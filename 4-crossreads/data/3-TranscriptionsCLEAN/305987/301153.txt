  
 Wed 15th:  Relieved by 20th. Came back to gully near Vaux-sur-Somme: Reserves. Sat 18th:  18th Bn hopped over this morning at 10 a.m. & captured Hun outpost without a casualty Sun 19th:  6th Brigade & 18th Bn hopped over at 2 a.m. & took every objective with very few casualties Tues 21st:  Advance party to front-line Wed 22nd:  Bn moved up to-night & relieved 20th Bn Thurs 23rd:  Lt Doig wounded & missing this morning. Presumed prisoner of war  ( Killed ) Tues 28th:  Relieved by 20th. Came back to reserve gully. Tues June 4th:  Fritz cam over 250 strong on the 19th Bn this morning.  He penetrated beyond front line, but got chopped to pieces. Not many getting back & [indecipherable] of 19th taken prisoner. Casualties very light
