Saturday 16thDidn't call at Melbourne, which was a bit of a disappointment.
The coolies provide plenty of amusement with their  habits etc. Washing seems to be a part of their religion, though whether it is to wash, or not to, I don't know. They wash in about 1 pint of water; first their hands, and then splash their face and run a wet finger round their ears
