<p>Centre for Soldiers Wives &amp; Mothers<br/>13-7.16.</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton,<br/>Mrs. Maxwell who I am sending up has filled in a form &amp; her case is being considered by you. but she is in very distressed circumstances &amp; has no money for herself &amp; child.</p>
<p>Can you kindly give her some immediate assistance till things can be arranged for her</p>
<p>Yours truly<br/>E. H. Bennie.</p>
