 Anyhow I have been given a medical outfit and have been put in charge of a party. 
 4th May (Thursday) Hot. In charge of party to clean the camp up &ndash; Then we were all marched out to dig a big sandbag dug-out a couple of miles off to be used as an advanced dressing station. We worked till late at night and were even told that we might have to work all night, but every time a wall was nearly built up the thing would collapse.
