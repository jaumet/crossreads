<p>18/3/17<br/>about 14 miles march Fritz brought two of our balloons down in flames this afternoon.</p>
<p>4/4/17<br/>Marched from Mountaban (Adelaide Camp) to Haplincourt about eleven miles. It snowed all the way and made things generally miserable. This village has had no shell fire in it but "Old Fritz"</p>
