<p>Thursday 30th<br/>Had a look round city afternoon went to Palladium Theatre<br/>Friday 31st<br/>Missed train for France afterwards saw medals distributed by General French afternoon visited St Pauls Cathedral and East End of London<br/>Saturday Sept 1st<br/>Left London by eight o'clock train arrived Folkestone ten o'clock left for Boulogne twelve had an awful rough trip sea sick all the way</p>
