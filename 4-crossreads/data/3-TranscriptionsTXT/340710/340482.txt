 Le Nouveau Monde 28.4.16 And so at last we are in the line opposite the Hun at last we did our last part of the journey a little over a week ago and now we are settled for our preliminary canter in a spot which has been most aptly termed "the Nursery"   It is here that all new Divisions in France have their first introduction to trench warfare.  The war here is still a thing of wonder to us, it is all so utterly unlike Gallipoli and what I pictured it would be, flares of all the colors of the rainbows appear several times nightly from the trenches opposite; here and there the "phut-phut-phut" of a machine gun; a dull booming ever and anon from behind and before denote the presence of big guns
