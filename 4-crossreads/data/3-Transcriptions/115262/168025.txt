<p>Saturday 15 September 1917.</p>
<p>On QM&#39;s fatigue today &amp; missed the watering trip to the Waddy. Not sorry either.&nbsp;(One camel having died is struck off the strength.) &quot;Orders&quot;. (What next!!)</p>
<p>One taube over this morning.</p>
<p>Boys returned from <s>Marakeb</s> Malaleh Beach. Went short way &amp; had a good time. Had 24 hrs. on the beach.</p>
<p>No Australian mail yet.</p>
<p>Wrote 1 letter.</p>
