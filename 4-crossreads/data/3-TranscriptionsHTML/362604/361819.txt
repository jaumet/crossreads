<p>Home for Supper at 9 p.m.  Well, my Dear ones, there can never be much news from here.  As long as you get a short letter to tell you that we are all well, I think you will know that it is hard to write about nothing.</p>
<p>Best Love to you all,<br/>From Harry</p>
