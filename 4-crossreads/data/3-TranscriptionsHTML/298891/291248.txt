<p>1917 June</p>
<p>14 Thursday</p>
<p>Waited &amp; cooked at Minerva &amp; at 4.30 was inoculated at St.Mary's Paddington &amp; went to Miss Hodge &amp; went to bed. Ned.Raine there. Lovely warm day. Miss Hodge gave me her bed &amp; took a room out. The Dr inoculating me had lost both legs in the war.</p>
