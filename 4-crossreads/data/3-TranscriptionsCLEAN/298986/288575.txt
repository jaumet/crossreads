  
 to the Tivoli, a good show.  There are Pictures, Concerts & Lectures in the different Y.Ms & Halls every night.  Sports one day, Football & Baseball at other times.  There are Yanks as well as all sorts of English & Oversee Troops here, other camps over the road with W.A.A.Cs, Indian, & Black troops, a horses convalese home not far away.  We are high up & look over the town with higher hills all round.  Have been marked out to day. 
 Wednesday 2nd
