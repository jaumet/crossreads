<p>The very worse cases are left here, the others sent further back if possible.</p>
<p>The Stunt has not come off yet, hope to be in our own C.C.S. in a couple of days. Our tents have all been re-built, made very safe   &amp;  comfy. So you see we are well cared for.</p>
<p>Some of our Surgeons   &amp;  orderlies are working here too. Which is very nice for us.</p>
<p>The sisters here are very nice   &amp;  they have been fearfully busy but not so bad now, still we have plenty to do   &amp;  the poo patients need plenty of nursing. I'm kept</p>
