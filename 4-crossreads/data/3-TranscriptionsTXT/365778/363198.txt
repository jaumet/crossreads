Tuesday 29/5/17Wrote to Doff. Poisoned leg very sore.Wednesday 30/5/17Remained in bed all day &ndash; final of boxing 26th won heavyweight.Thursday 31/5/17Went to 27th dinner &amp; stayed the night at Bde.Friday 1/6/17Judged cookers for 27th Bn, moved to get band back.Saturday 2/6/17Brigade Sports very good indeed &ndash; got 2nd place in best platoon.Sunday 3/6/17Church Serive &ndash; Bde dinner at night.Monday 4/6/17Horse show &ndash; won best transport &ndash; very good. 3 firsts &ndash; 4 seconds, went to Amien.
