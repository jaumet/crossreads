<p>[Saturday 4 November 1916]</p>
<p>A Hospital Boat has just passed us, with wounded &amp; I'm going back "cured" what a funny way it is to see the world!!!<br/>We never are to take our life Belts off, eat in them &amp; sleep in them-<br/>A Destroyer accompanies us<br/><strike>The troops on board are for Salonika</strike> &amp; we may go round that way &amp; so be 3 weeks on board<br/>We may also go Via <strike>Malta</strike> which place I have not yet seen.<br/>We get wireless news of the war every day.<br/>Weather rough &amp; nearly all troops are sick!</p>
