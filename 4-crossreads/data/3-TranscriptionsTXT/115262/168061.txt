 Sunday 21 October 1917. 
 &nbsp; 
 Fell in at 2 pm &amp; saddled up with all our gear, equipment etc &amp; 5 days rations. Thousands of troops are on the move. 
 The whole of the Imperial Camel Brigade in Palestine and the A.C.Fd Amb. are on the move where we do not know but only have an idea. 
 Halted &amp; formed up at 4.30 p.m 5 miles from Shiekh Nuran. Moved off at 5 p.m. and travelled right through  the  till midnight. No smoking or lights of any description and no noise whatever allowed.
