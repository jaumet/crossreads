<p>Saturday 8 January, 1916</p>
<p>The sea is calming down a great deal &amp; things are at most normal again.</p>
<p>Our escort, the little trawler is having engine trouble &amp; this is keeping us back.</p>
<p>We play a lot of bridge it is about the only way of putting in the time.</p>
