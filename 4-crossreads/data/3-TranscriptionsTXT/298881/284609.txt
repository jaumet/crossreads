We have had 3 weeks in Warloy-Baillon and 3 very happy weeks too for me at any rate, but like all good times it passed far too quickly.  Another tragedy enacted and already nearly forgotten. The tragedy which perpetually haunts a soldier's life.  A little love, a big kiss and parting with all its sorrow and fleeting memories of happy hours, gone, perhaps never to be rehearsed.
