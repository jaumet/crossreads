  
 May 1915 19 Wed I turned Regt out About 12.30 the enemy's fright died down a little but an attack had been prepared for at 3.50 am with cries of Allah the whole A and NZ posts were rushed. We drove 
 off some 50, killing many some in our trenches, our 1st hand to hand fight. Nichols arm hurt. They tried in front of Quinn's and Courtenays posts till 10am and gave up. Officially we are told they had 2000 dead and 5000 wd. Relieved during a heavy fire fight at 3 pm by 3rd LH. I am hit in the calf but by richochet only and cary on. Today killed 11, Wd 17 (taken away) Lined inner defence trenches at night.
