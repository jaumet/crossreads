<p>6-3-17<br/>put in the evening writing up notes..</p>
<p>7-3-17<br/>Wed 7th<br/>Reveille at 4.15 for some reason, most of the lads took no notice of it, but I got up had a wash &amp; steak &amp; eggs at the Canteen &amp; wrote a letter before the 6.15 parade;</p>
<p>Snowy Wride came out this morning, he is down on leave I gave him a letter of introduction to Jess &amp; told him we would be at the National at 8 oclock tonight &amp; have dinner with him &amp; Matheson, We had an extra hour on helio work this afternoon &amp; the lamps parade was cut out so, I got away early, reported</p>
