<p>Monday 6 August 1917.</p>
<p>[Bank Holiday.] No holiday here though.</p>
<p>On the camels once more. We are getting a little used to them by now.</p>
<p>During afternoon went to 14 A.G.H. to see Ted Holden (Sgt) &amp; Tom de Gruchy. While there saw several of the other boys on the Staff &amp; as patients there.</p>
<p>After tea &amp; feeding parade went to Cairo with H. Flavell.</p>
<p>Back for &quot;tattoo&quot; roll call at 9.30 pm at camp.</p>
<p>Made a few purchases in <s>the</s> part of the &quot;Mouske&quot;</p>
