 Friday Sept 21 Tom arrived from Rafa so we are sharing bivvy again Jacko came overhead to day. Put wind up us but dropped no pills. Saw Scott, Bailey &amp; Cameron on way to Marakat for spell. My neddy on seeing Camels, bolted bolted &amp; threw me. Fortunately no damage. Saturday Sept 22 Inspecting Batterys attached to Division, Inverness, Ayreshire &amp; Somerset. On way to Batterys passed deserted Bedouin village. Anti Air Craft guns hidden here for Jacko's benefit. Sunday Sept 23 Camp situated next 1st Light Horse Field Amb &amp; about 2 miles from Aerodrome which lies at a place called [indecipherable] O.C. granted leave.
