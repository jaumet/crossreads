<p>Sunday Sept 30th</p>
<p>Dartford Hosp : transferred to ward A.15 : Holy Comm at 10 am in the Chapel &amp; very nice ; church at 10.45 am &amp; also nice : band for orchestra &amp; fair few nurses &amp; W.L. [Women's Legion] girls there : another air raid at night</p>
<p>Monday Oct 1st</p>
<p>Dartford Hosp : inspected bt Dr in morn : wound nearly healed but thigh still stiff : said he would leave the two pieces of shell in so long as they didn't interfere with my movements : sent a Hun book &amp; a few clippings to Mum : Wilkie &amp; self strolled to Dartford : fair sized place : rested in Y.M.C.A. &amp; then</p>
