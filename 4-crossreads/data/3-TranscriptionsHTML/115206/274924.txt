<p>December  1918.<br/>Sunday 22</p>
<p>At Sea. H.S."Karoola."</p>
<p>Went to church service, ship rolled a considerable amount in doing her 325 miles. We have still a matter of 437 miles to go .<br/>Weather becoming warmer and there was a very fine sun shining yesterday.<br/>Lost &pound;2 again after a game of nap.</p>
