<p>Wednesday 4/7/17<br/>Went through General's effects with Bas, left by car for Bapaume via Amiens, saw General Birdwood and fixed Butlers return to Sydney.<br/>Thursday 5/7/17<br/>Arrived home at 2 am, made a big reconnaissance for scheme.<br/>Friday 6/7/17<br/>Appointed an umpire for scheme went over ground, saw Bas.<br/>Saturday 7/7/17<br/>Wrote to Doff also to General Birdwood etc. Played cricket against 27th.<br/>Sunday 8/7/17<br/>Raining heavily went to Amiens.</p>
