<p>Prof. Welsh and "Chappie" seem to have been enjoying themselves immensely over the inoculation for influenza question. The letters are very characteristic &ndash; Chappy essentially scientific and precise and talking shorthand with Taffy wearing his perpetual smile and getting in <strike>quite</strike> quiet well camouflaged thrusts in his gentle voice. Apparently Taffy thinks the inoculation must do some good &ndash; cant at any rate do any harm &ndash; and wants to break down the popular prejudice against it while Chappy takes exception to some scientifically inaccurate assertion in connection with it.</p>
<p>I see from a paper I received only today that Bert Evatt has left the C.J. and is starting on his own &ndash; also that he has announced his engagement. I hope he does well &ndash; he is a brilliant chap &ndash; but he should confine himself to that portion, if there is one, of a barristers work that does not require a sense of humour.</p>
