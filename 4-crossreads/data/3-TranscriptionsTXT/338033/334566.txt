
In Sydney. The native quarters are, as suits them, very filthy and evil smelling. All the Engineering works and big stores are owned by Europeans who live out near the Cinamen gardens in cool looking bungalows. Outside the shops are long pieces of native material which smoulders away. These are for the purpose of lighting pipes and cigarettes. The Buddist Priests wear a yellow robe and hold a huge bowl in their right arm across the chest.
