 3 May Saturday 
 F &amp; Showery 
 I take Marie to Ramsay. We go to Picture &amp; shopping. I lose her &amp; get wind up about coming home alone. Arrive home &amp; very tired. I am off my tucker &amp; quiet. All others play cards while I watch puzzle. We go to bed &amp; I get my face painted &amp; tucked in. a lovely good night by -----, while all others look for Venus &amp; the moon. 
 4 May, Sunday 
 F. We work at Puzzle especially Marie. I go up to make my bed &amp; M. comes to fix my blind. In afternoon M. sews while I puzzle. 
 We all sit &amp; try puzzle at night. I on a buffet in front of fire. 
 Auntie ticks me in this night, elddoc.
