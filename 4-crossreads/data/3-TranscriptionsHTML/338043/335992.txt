<p>Oct. 14th<br/>Nothing unusual happens during the day which is a wet and dismal one.</p>
<p>Oct. 15th<br/>Load  lorries and leave "Eu" at 10 a.m. A journey of one hour and half bring us to Abbeville a town of a fair size, which was heavily bombed before the enemy were forced back.<br/>We take over the Municipal Theatre (a fine building) and give opening show the same evening.</p>
