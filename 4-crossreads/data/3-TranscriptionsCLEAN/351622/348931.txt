  
 2 December 1916 Sat 6 chaps going on Furlough to England were killed to-night by big shell whilst waiting for train on Abert Station 
 3 December 1916 Sun Suffering with influenza very very cold. 1st Division going into the trenches. Issue of rum 7.30 pm but I am right off it. Rubbed Tom down with eucalyptus. Word that Bede Carrol has died of wounds.
