<p>1915<br/>June 21 Mon.<br/>Hospital inspected in the afternoon by Surg. Gen. W.D. Williams, Director Medical Services, Aust. Imp. Force &ndash; a big fat josser.<br/>Lumsdaine &amp; I have started having cold showers before going to bed as well as first thing in the morning. We also go for walks round Giza before breakfast.</p>
