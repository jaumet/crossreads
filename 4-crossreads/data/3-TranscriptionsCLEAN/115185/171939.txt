  
 Alma Dowe & Patricia Blundell - both very nice girls. There were two green painted side board affairs in the room - and I got them placed so they make a screen between Patricia's end and Almas & mine. There are three large windows with shutters and a large two pannel door. The height of the room is enormous - quite as high as two stories in an ordinary house. The length of the room is 15 of my strides and 8 across.
