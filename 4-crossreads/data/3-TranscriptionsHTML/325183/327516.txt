<p>In our house a fine old couple are living alone. They were very good to us and helped us in the way of obtaining a few chairs and a table to eat on as well as fossicking around for a fire-wood for us. What changes this old couple have seen! What troubles have they not had!<br/>They had little enough to give us and we did not ask for anything but we had arrived there hungry tired <strike>and</strike> wet and cold (as it had rained heavily during the greater part of the march) and these people did what they could for us and we appreciated it.
