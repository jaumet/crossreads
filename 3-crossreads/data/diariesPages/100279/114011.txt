Old Fritz threw a lot of bombs at the 8th Battalion but no result I believe Last night we were relieved by the 3rd Batt. they made a proper mess of it, sent a big fatigue party up with the relieving men you could see nothing but men for miles all making for the trenches in one long string nothing but good fortune saved them from being wiped out, to make matters worse they brought a fair number of half drunken men with them, one or two of them kicked up a great stink an officer threatened to shoot one of them if he did not hold his tongue but he took no notice of him the officer placed him under arrest a pretty sentence he'll get for drunkeness in the firing line is a
