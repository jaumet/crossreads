<p>the family, three spanking girls which meant that we missed tea &amp; spent 4 hours there. Had sundry bottles of champaygne, old English 1904 at 18 francs 50 a bottle which meant that we had a most enjoyable time. Drove home at a great pace &amp; finished celebrating the New Year at our billet.</p>
<p>Tuesday 2nd Jan.</p>
<p>Had leave granted to England today &amp; have just completed packing. My first leave for over two years so am looking forward to it greatly. Went by car today to various battalions to evacuate sick &amp; had a good run round.</p>
<p>Have just returned from leave &amp; will try &amp; write up my diary for that period. I found it utterly impossible to do so when away, my time was too much occupied, spare time all too little was spent in sleep.</p>
