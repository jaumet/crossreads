<p>8 March 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out)<br/>X Bethania see St John  St Luke chap XXIV X<br/>Field Officer today at above named place-<br/>Dead Turks, &amp; shells abandoned by them are plentiful. X Strange to relate West, poor Bluey Liddle's man is here in my Regiment and a good man he is too.  Can you read this "BLUE" LIDDLE. X<br/>Moved on through very rough country to <strike>Rummon</strike> bivouaced at midnight &amp; moved again at 2 AM escorting a Mountain Battery- to <strike>Taiyebeh</strike></p>
