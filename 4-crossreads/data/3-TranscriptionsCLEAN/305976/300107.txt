  
 the 14th  General Hospital. I expect some to be going home Jan 29 Great excitement today about 200 men belonging to the Division are going home. I believe I am among the lucky number to go Transport duty. We are eagerly waiting for the news to be confirmed. To night we are having a little celebration & farewell party to some of our pals. Jan 30 Last night was a wild one Tom & I are leaving by the Train to night to report at Moascar
