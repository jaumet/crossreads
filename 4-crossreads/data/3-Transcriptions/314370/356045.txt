<p>June, 1916<br/>Monday 26<br/>Route march again<br/>We returned terribly hungry &amp; tired.<br/>No parade till evening</p>
<p>Tuesday 27<br/>parades as usual<br/>The heat is terrific in our bell tents &amp; the dust is fearful.<br/>But we seem to get on alright, nobody is dying.</p>
