 24 January 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out) X  Boils  under arms and groin  strange no sooner than I get out I suffer these things-X Divisional Sports are coming along soon &amp; football to keep the men fit-  Birds here are Crow, Blackbird, Ducks, plover, &amp; Grouse,  Gazelle in the Judean Hills, but I have no shotgun now- Every Evening the hawks chase the Starlings home to roost- the latter fly in large mobs- X Did I mention seeing General Allenby in Judea- he is not like some Gs who remained in Cairo X
