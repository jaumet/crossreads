<p>339<br/>He may turn up later in the afternoon &ndash; The House Surgeon sent for me this morning and told me a Proffessor Dickson was coming to see me, He said the Prof. knew the pater very well &ndash; I think I shall be here for sometime but cant get anything definite, out of the Doctor in to what he is going to do &ndash; It is a beautiful day here and it is really a crime</p>
