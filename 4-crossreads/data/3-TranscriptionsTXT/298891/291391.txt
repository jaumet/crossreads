 4 Sunday 
 Grey day &ndash; Kaimacktchalan in clouds.  Great day in dump &ndash; 50 bales.  Sister Saunders &amp; I worked like horses till 4 p.m. with no cessation.  Great surprise to see Kath who came up with 50 horses from Salonique &amp; was with me from 12 to 2 p.m.  Little walk with Kath then with Dr. Rose then with Stephie.  Olding &amp; Le Blanc with Sheila for tea.
