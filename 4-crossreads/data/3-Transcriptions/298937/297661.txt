<p>fell in to march out.<br/>Embarked after about 7 miles march, on the "King Edward", a small Channel boat.<br/>Some wounded Tommies &amp; Australians on the wharf.  They say that a man does not last long on the Somme without getting wounded.<br/>Slept as best I could on deck &amp; in the early hours got up to see the lights of the Guard ships outside Portsmouth.  As the light got better we slowly steamed up the Solent &amp; passing Netley Hospital &amp; finally berthed in Southampton Docks.</p>
