 On board we have Headquarters 150 A Details 5002, 5th  1500 Division, 2nd 600 Division &amp; 1st Aux. 300 Hosp. &amp; a few Tommies who have been on Anti Aircraft duty at Imbros, Ternnados [?], Lemnos &amp; other islands. Left Alexandria at 11 &ndash; a.m. today with escort of one destroyer in front &amp; one behind.  The "Franconia" is a very fast boat of about 24 Knots per hr. capacity.
