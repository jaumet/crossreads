<p>NOV. &nbsp; WEDNESDAY 22 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. I was very crook all day &amp; stayed about the hut. Saw Campbell about getting week end leave &amp; got it &amp; wired for an appointment &nbsp;Poss Nivison saw the General about his commission but did not get any definate reply. He went on 4 days leave to London at midday</p>
