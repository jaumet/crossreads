<p>Monday 5 August 1917 [1918]<br/>4 p.m. S. Musgrave &amp; I grab a gharry &amp; drive to A.H.Q., draw &pound;5-0-0, remaining &pound;11 odd.  Then tea at Liptons with Musgrave &amp; her friend, Mr. Broughton.<br/>Shopping &amp; so back to 31st G.H.<br/>Ematine being given intra muscularly for doses.  By mouth in B.G.I. pdrs., i.e., Bismuth Emetine &amp; Iodide.</p>
