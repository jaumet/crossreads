<p>Dec 28th/<br/>Woke up with terrible pains in the stomach I put it down to bad drinking water; I went &amp; saw the Dr. but castor oil was the result.<br/>I must not forget to say that the day after Xmas we were given a small tin of chocolate from the Australian War Contiggent in London also a small issue of rum.<br/>We are all looking forward</p>
