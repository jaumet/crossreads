<p>Mar 20th &ndash; 18<br/>Just a word or two on Fritzs bad behaviour which is growing worse daily. He has suddenly taken great delight in seeing how many large shells he can put over to do damage in the back areas also round about our possie</p>
<p>Of course at such times our guns seem to develop a terrific cold and cough very bad, intensifying Fritz's annoyance. Also his bombs of late have been supplied to us in good issues but our planes have also recognised the beautiful Spring</p>
