<p>January 1918<br/>Thursday 31</p>
<p>On the road<br/>I awoke at 1 am to see old Dick standing over me and enquiring as to where he was to sleep.<br/>Reveille was at 3.30 am today.<br/>We have not come so very far about 25 kilos.<br/>Went for a walk this evening with Jamie and scraped a feed of eggs and bread.</p>
