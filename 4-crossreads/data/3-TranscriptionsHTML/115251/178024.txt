<p>I would point out the following additional points for your consideration:-<br />(1)&nbsp; Mr Komine (Japanese) of Rabaul was on board &quot;Nusa&quot; and thanks to his help &amp; knowledge of Natives, &quot;Komet&quot; was <u>exactly </u>located. Mr Komine showed great enterprise &amp; an absolute indifference to the probability of &quot;Nusa&quot; receiving &quot;Komet&#39;s&quot; fire; he also showed his great anxiety to help us by abandoning his occupation of salving a wreck off the Talele Islands in order to accompany the expedition.</p>
