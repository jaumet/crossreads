 October 1915 
 Friday 8 
 Fatigue duties in camp. 
 Dissolved partnership with Hunter &amp; got a dug out for myself, to be independent &amp; save a lot of trouble. 
 A fair amount of free gift supplies ( chocolate etc.) arrived &amp; were brought up with rations in afternoon. 
 First rain since landing came after dark with a brisk wind  right  causing alterations to be necessary for the dugout.
