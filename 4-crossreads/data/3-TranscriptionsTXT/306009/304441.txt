
1915Sep.16 Thu.Up at 6 a.m. & had a swim before breakfast.The rest of the day was devoted to 2 walks round the coast, reading, another swim, smoking & a yarn to 2 Scouts of 1st Alexandria B-P Scouts who are acting as orderlies.My uniform consists of rag hat, light blue coat with sleeves rolled, short pants tucked up as far as they will go & a pr. of white shoes.Weather - Snifter.
