<p>1916 &nbsp; SUNDAY 24 &nbsp; SEPT.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Went to the YMCA to church in morning &amp; Poss &amp; self got a car into Salisbury for dinner &amp; then got a car down to Bridport &amp; saw M<sup>rs</sup>&nbsp;Suttil &amp; had afternoon tea there &amp; we got back to Dorchester for tea. Had it at the &quot;Kings Arms&quot; hotel &amp; got back here about 11 pm. It was a great run down &amp; very pretty down there. Got news that two more Zeps&nbsp;had been brought down in England.</p>
