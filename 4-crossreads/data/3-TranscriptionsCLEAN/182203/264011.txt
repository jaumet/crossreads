Now that the weather is cooler  & that I shall not have so many temptations to go out, I shall be able to write more.I have not kept my diary - so suppose my friends of Terang will be disgusted with me. I have not bought a helmet yet, have not been able to get into uniform yet - My abscesses are clearing up beautifully - thought another one would have to be operated upon but thank goodness the foments are dispersing it. Have not gone to Alexandria yet, hope Major will say this morning when I can go. I'm really splendid now, getting quite fat. Still taking stout  & am going out every afternoon now. Last Wednesday a party of us when to see "The Scared Carpet," it is supposed to travel to Mecca, & stay covered over some wonderful tomb. Then the next year cut up into small pieces & given to the faithful. There is a new one  each year - It is a most beautiful piece of work - Looks like black marie! silk worked with beautiful Egyptian designs of gold, red & green - It is open
