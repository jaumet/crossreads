Jan 29/18Just returned from my second leave to Blighty & expect to pull out any day for a rest The weather is quite mild & we are experiencing a much better winter than last & also under much better conditions than on the Somme.
Feb 26 MerrisWe pulled out here about 3 weeks ago for a Rest & expect to be moving 'in' again in a few days time. This is a quiet little village between Bailleul & Hazebrouck - both two fair sized country towns.
