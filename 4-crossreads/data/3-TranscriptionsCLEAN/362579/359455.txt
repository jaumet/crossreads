
No. 2. Aust. Aux. Hosp.Southall.Middlesex.England.16.12.17.
My dearest Mother   &  Sisters.,
Saw Matron-in-Chief on Thursday morning   &  she told me I was not leaving until January. So perhaps it will be February   &  I shall get to Aust. about April. Anyhow 'm very comfortable at present   &  like the work here but it is a rest after the work I've had.
No letters this week from home, hope to get some before Xmas. Yesterday I had a Day-off. Stayed in bed until 11. a.m. dressed   &  had an early lunch, then caught the train for Paddington.
