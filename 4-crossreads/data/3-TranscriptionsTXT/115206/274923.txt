 December  1918. Saturday 21 
 At Sea. H.S."Karoola." 
 Weather fine and clear. Ship still keeps her speed pretty well. 345 miles is the distance travelled during the last 24 hours. Having a pretty good time. Wrote p.c. to Smithie, Percy G, Tom Code, Jim Sharp, Marg, Olive, Miss Brent, Eve, Mr Broone, Ken Gunn, Nurse Mac and Nurse Lottie, posted them all today.
