28th. Sunday MayLetter writing in paddock most of day with Ern &amp; Robo. Church service in barn after tea. 100 men from Coy. on engineer fatigue till 2am.
29th. MondayFritz shelled us out of our billet about 10am wrecked house much but our portion only got one or two shells. Very busy rescuing gear &amp; munitions from same between shots. Midday we took up our quarters in A.Coy. Redoubt few 100 yds along the road. Party sent to clean up billet after shelling also usual bathing parade. At dusk 100 went on fatigue to trenches, remainder back to billet where we slept. O.K. though expected more shells
