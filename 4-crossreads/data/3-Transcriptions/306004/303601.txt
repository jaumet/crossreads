<p>18.11.16<br/>Sat 18th<br/>Lasts night an order came out stating what Squadron was to water first, second &amp; last, nothing was said about Head Qrs, &amp; the R.S.M. told me to get in where I could &amp; if we were first down to the trough we water first; Reveille here at 5 oclock &amp; stables at 5.45, I got the H.Q men away early but they did not go far before Lieut Blackett halted them for a few minutes, &amp; then the Orderly Sergt. stopped them &amp; told them to water after B. Sqdn; Smelt an argument at mid-day stables &amp; decided to  go down myself;  We were first to water with B Sqdn right on us. the Orderly Sergt (Reid) pulled us up &amp; said we follow B Sqdn, I told him he was out of order &amp; had a very enjoyable</p>
