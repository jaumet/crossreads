
3.9.15Mills shortly after breakfast & did not return till nearly sundown, but he brought back a tin of fruit, 3 tins of milk & a tin of sardines, so he did not do too bad.  Smithers & I went for a swim this afternoon & gave all our spare clothing a good soaking.  Stan & Les have to go on duty on the 'phone from 4 till 8 oclock to-morrow morning.  Sergeant Nelson is pretty bad with dysentery or malaria & will probably go off duty tomorrow.  At last I am to the end of this book & I start the next on my 24th birthday.
