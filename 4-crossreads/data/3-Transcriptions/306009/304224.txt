<p>1915<br/>Feb. 7 Sun.<br/>Work the same as any other day till 10.15 p.m.<br/>An infantry brass band played at Hospital during the afternoon.<br/>About 3 p.m. went out with stretcher &amp; brought in a Territorial with <strike>compound</strike> simple fracture of tibia (leg) after fixing it up.<br/>Distance from Cairo to the Pyramids is about 9 miles.</p>
