<p>July 1916<br/>We rested in camp all day on the 23rd &amp; at 1 am on the 24th we set out for Katia.  'C' Sqdn went out to Oghratina &amp; we heard a fair bit of firing as they neared the Turks.</p>
<p>24th to 31st<br/>We have been going solid all the week.  Every alternate day we go out to meet Jacko &amp; have inflicted numerous casualties &amp; taken several prisoners.  The 1st Brigade had  the biggest scrap at Magoubra [Mageibra].  Our artillery &amp; machine guns have also done useful work.<br/>On the 28th Lieut Pearce Corpl Thorne &amp; 2 others stayed out on listening post.  The Turks surrounded them about midnight &amp; Mr Pearce was killed.  The others had a lucky escape &amp; were about 8 hrs in the enemy's lines.  They eventually got out &amp; reached Hill 70.  "B" Troop is without an officer now so also is "C".  On the 29th at midnight we set off again &amp; there was desultory fire all day.  The Turks are fairly active &amp; strongly entrenched.
