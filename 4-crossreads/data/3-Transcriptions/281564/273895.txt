<p>the scrub bound for the mission station on the fringe of never &ndash; never to get hold of an arsenal or something else. We continued to swear for an hour or so &amp; then as cannibals began to nose round we pulled out into the middle of the water &amp; anchored. The cannibals yelled &amp; the mosquitoes chewed big pieces out of out bodies &amp; treatened to sink the boat &amp; the mud hummed worse than a bone dust shop. We watched nearly all the night &amp; towards morning fell asleep for about half an hour.</p>
<p>Monday 19th Oct<br/>We woke at daybreak &amp; pulling in to the shore landed. The "cannibals" had gone &amp; in their place stood a peaceful looking mob of niggers with a glass of tea for each of us, a dish of fried bananas &amp; a bunch of others. We breakfasted &amp; then went to see our benefactor &ndash; a one-eyed Malay who braving the danger of the wilds had cleared a good patch of the forest &amp; grown cocoanuts, bananas &amp; vegetables. He treated us well &amp; gave us breakfast &amp; helped us to bail out our boat</p>
