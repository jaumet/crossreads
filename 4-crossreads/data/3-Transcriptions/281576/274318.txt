<p>What is your Favourite Flower?<br/>Oh: Give me the Rose every time, the emblem of true Love<br/>What is your Favourite Flower?<br/>Oh, mine's Hops. Hops the emblem of True Beer</p>
<p>Who do you think I met this morning?  Old Ben Fuller.<br/>Go on, what did he have to say?<br/>He says I'm getting fat.  No wonder.<br/>Why? How do you make that out?<br/>Well, you were looking Fuller in the Face.</p>
