<p><strike>33</strike> (34) July 26th (Thursday)<br/>place we can move about in. It is about 50 yards square, no scope for any outdoor games at all excepting "Rounders".</p>
<p>July 27th<br/>Cannot sleep so well in this place too many in our room.</p>
<p>All officers who wished to give their Parole were allowed to for a swim this morning &amp; also for a walk round the hills afterwards. I did not go, though I would liked to have. If I could be taken in a cab, the outing would be very appreciable.</p>
<p>Recd 2 Pcls one of Bread biscuits from our Red Cross &amp; the other from Sir Henry Proctor Presd Y.M.C.A.</p>
<p>Played bridge tonight. Feeling very fed up with things in general, wish I was with Lanagan &amp; Gardiner &amp;c.</p>
<p>July 28th<br/>Good sleep. Played Bridge; Had my foot dressed by the Dr today, it is still discharging. Attended a Concert held tonight &ndash; it was an excellent show, violion solos <strike>vocal</strike> songs, stories &amp;c.</p>
<p>July 29th<br/>Foot dressed again today. Recd letters from Bert, Charlton, Aust red Cross (2) Emma, Eileen &amp; Gert &amp; Roy Gowing. Archie Gowing is posted as wounded &amp; missing. Roy is naturally very worried about Him. I feel</p>
