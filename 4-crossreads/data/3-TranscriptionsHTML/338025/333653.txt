<p>Thursday 5th April 1917<br/>La Boiselle<br/>Marched to Martinbuich in the morning, taking ammunition out of the dugout and running it down on a light railway to a station. Took piece of shrapnel out of cross in cemetry. Arr. back to camp about 5pm.</p>
