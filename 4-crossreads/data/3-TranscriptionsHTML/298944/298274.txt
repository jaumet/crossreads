<p>Argument as to whether he tried to bomb us &ndash; despite the red cross on the roof. This struck me as childish considering that there are artillery men in paddocks all round here, not to mention anti aircraft guns.</p>
<p>Freddy Smith &ndash; the Staff dispenser &ndash; has been learning French during the night, &amp; has amused me considerably by his quaint facial expressions,  &amp; his weird pronunciations. "Ils ont " he gave us: "eels zonk" &ndash; suggestive of bombs falling on us.</p>
