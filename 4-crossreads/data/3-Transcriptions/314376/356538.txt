<p>A little way away from Bray was Plateau Siding. Here was a Y.M.C.A. We spent pleasant and profitable times here. The "Y.M." catered for spiritual as well as material food and a little hut was set aside for this purpose.</p>
<p>Here would gather different men from the Battalions. Here would gather different men from the Battalions and presided over by the "Y.M." Leader, a Scotch minister. We also had his assistant as well.</p>
