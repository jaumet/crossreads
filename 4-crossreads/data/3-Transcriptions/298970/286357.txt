<p>22nd July (Saturday)</p>
<p>This morning a letter of congratulations from the A.D.M.S. (Asst. Director of Medical Services) was read to us, highly praising the work performed by our Ambulance in the recent engagement.  It appears that we put up a record by clearing 3,075 wounded between the hours of 2 a.m. &amp; 6 p.m. on Thursday last.  Unlucky again &ndash; this time I was included in another party of 10 and sent to the trenches at Wye Farm.</p>
