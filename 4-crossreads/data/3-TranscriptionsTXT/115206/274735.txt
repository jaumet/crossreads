 June 1918 Friday 14 
 Battery W.Lines.  Pont Noyelles Weather changing, laid up throughout day and last night. Feel very stiff and sore through being in bed. D.A.C. and H.A.C.sports commenced. Line went under pressure of shell mended same Received no mail at all. Smithy and Ken Gunn still hors-de-combat at Guns. Mr Mann arrived from guns looking like a hero. Boils over his face and head caused the decoration. Mr Ken down for the afternoon.
