
April, 1916Thursday 13The dreaded wind commenced to blow just after sunrise gaining strength all day until about 3 pm it was not possible to open ones eyes.The sand drifts into every crack & hole making it impossible to keep it out.
Friday 14After blowing all night the Camseen x abates after sunrise   x KhamsinAltogether it has done a whole heap of damage  it will take us a week to get clear of the dust
