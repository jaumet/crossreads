<p>&amp; during the few days we have been here we have been busy swimming the Horses<br/>Thursday Nov29<br/>Working on water supply at Jaffa civilian hospital. Bombed again today. Jaffa particularly its outskirts is a pretty place with its red tiled houses, &amp; Orange Groves.<br/>Jacko received a surprise to day, Anti Air craft guns were brought up during the <strike>day</strike> night<br/>Friday Nov 30<br/>Sent to Ramley to day, road quiet until within 3 miles of Ramley where road was under shellfire. Got through safely</p>
