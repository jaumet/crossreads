<p>28.1.15</p>
<p>will probably be shifting to Heliopolas next Saturday.  there has been some fighting near the canal by to-nights paper reports, but none of any consequence.</p>
<p>Thur 28th<br/>This morning an advance guard to Heliopolas left here to get things in order for us there, I believe we shift on Saturday;  The A &amp; B Sqds returned at dinner time   Stan had to go on duty in the orderly room, as the other Sq. signallers were out on the march  had no lamp practice to-night as the lamps were sent away on the transport, with the advance guard.</p>
