Nov 1 (Wednesday) Pommies Redoubtarrived here on Monday afternoon after being 6 hours on the road doing 4 miles. The road being blocked with traffic as the 1st Aust Div was going in the & the 29th Div coming out.
Passed their Inf. on the road Munsters - R.IR. - Essex, Lancs Hants. They looked a pitiable sight - slathered in mud & shaken with the strain & hardships.
