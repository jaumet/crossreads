<p>Sylvan Fells<br/>Turramurra</p>
<p>26th March</p>
<p>Dear Miss Small</p>
<p>I have ready my contribution for the book.  It is rather a large page to send through the post.  Will you be good enough to call or send for it, to Mr. Smith's office, it will be there on Tuesday next &ndash;</p>
<p>Messers MacNamara &amp; Smith<br/>15 Belmont Buildings<br/>Castlereagh Street</p>
<p>(Next to Brook's the printer.)</p>
<p>Wishing you every success with the book,</p>
<p>Yours sincerely</p>
<p>G. Cossington-Smith</p>
<p>Please ask for Mr. Ernest Smith.</p>
