<p>&amp; gear loaded on the obstinate mules.  Many loads crashed before they were properly secured which brought forth "unfortunate" language.  My group is detailed for Advance Guard &amp; we traps a distance of 18 or 19 miles to Seri-pul.  The stream nearby is eagerly sought by all when camp is pitched when  refreshes one considerably.</p>
<p>The people are desperately poor being in most cases mere skeletons with only rags to cover themselves.</p>
<p>After tea we had a quick game of bridge then bed.</p>
<p>May 1st<br/>We pushed on to the foot of the Pia-Tak pass, the track was fairly good, much easier than yesterday, tho the heat in the deep valley is most oppressive.</p>
