<p>Saturday June 8th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : off duty 6 am : stroll after dinner &amp; pictures after tea : raid alarm at night :</p>
<p>Sunday June 9th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : changed shifts so did not go out</p>
<p>Monday June 10th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : stroll in morn &amp; look round : on duty till 10 pm : air raid alert at night :</p>
<p>Tuesday &amp; Wednesday June 11th &amp; 12th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : stroll round in morn &amp; on duty 2 pm &ndash; 10 pm : more raid</p>
<p>Thursday June 13th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : stroll in town in morning : on duty 2 pm &ndash; 10 pm : air raid &amp; a few bombs dropped : heavy barrage defence :</p>
<p>Friday June 14th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : stroll in morn : on duty 2 &ndash; 10 pm :</p>
<p>Saturday June 15th</p>
<p>Dunkirk : done my washing in morn : on duty 2 &ndash; 10 pm : got a few "Ausie" letters last night so feel satisfied :</p>
