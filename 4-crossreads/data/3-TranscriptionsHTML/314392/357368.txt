<p>poured shell after shell into the roads.<br/>4th  At 5am we left for the camp pursued by shrapnel and H.E. shells. It was indeed terrifying. Many wounded Major MacRae and Capt. Graham killed.<br/>6th This work still continues. It is raining and the roads are in an awful condition.<br/>6th Still raining-work continues. Our division has long since gone out of the line but we are still in and are likely to remain for some time.<br/>9th Fourth Div. are about to come into the line again. Gee! how much longer are we to stay in this living hell?<br/>10th Still raining. Job still progressing. Rotten.</p>
