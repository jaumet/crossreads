<p>[Transcriber's note:  Private Francis Joseph Brewer has taken down in shorthand the statement of Private William Miller concerning the Battle of Mont St. Quentin.  Private Miller from Wagga Wagga enlisted at the age of 18 on 4 April 1916.  He embarked in Sydney on the "Euripides" on 9 September 1916 and disembarked in England on 16 October 1916 and then went on to France.  He was wounded in action on 3 October 1918. ]</p>
