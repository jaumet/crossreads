 19th. Took my route march crowd as usual down to the little coffee shop in Harborne, we put most of the hour in there you know &amp; then march home the Commandant thinks I have them going all the time, yes I am the right sort for that sort of thing. 
 A big mob went away from here this morning to the various Camps I was expecting to go myself but I suppose it will be tomorrow now instead 
 Have been letter writing all the morning I must keep going while I have the fit on me I got quite a decent lot off too &amp; a lot of it had been lying about waiting to be answered for some time
