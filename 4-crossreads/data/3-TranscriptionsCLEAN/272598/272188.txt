Last of the boys came in from the posts taken over from us by the 'Tommies'. 13 of our lads have been wounded during the 14 days we have been in here; very fortunate taking everything into consideration for there has been over 300 casualties among the bearers of the different Amb's working on this part since the beginning of the months.
