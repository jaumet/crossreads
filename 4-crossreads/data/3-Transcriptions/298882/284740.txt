<p>16.12.17</p>
<p>Yesterday all the Aust. Base Camps were amalgamated and are now all camped on the opposite side of the valley <strike>just</strike> between Harfleur and Montvilliers.  We are well protected from the cold South Winds here by a long high range of hills.  The officers mess here is rather crowded as a result of the Amalgamation.  Last night was a lively one here.  A number of officers having been warned</p>
