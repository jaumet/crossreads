  No. 2.  Troopship Kyarra Victoria Dock Melbourne 1st December 1914. Dear old Mrs, Your letters of 25th &amp; 26th duly reached me also letters from Mother &amp; Margaret. I hope Margaret will continue to write, it is nice to get her letters more particularly when the thoughts expressed in the letter are her own. I was of course delighted to get your letters &amp; hope to hear from you again before we leave Melbourne. There was a great mix up in the storing of the cargo at Sydney &amp; we are at present engaged in getting it all out again &ndash; we will probably not be away from here till Friday.
