<p>Sunday 18<br/>Move from Ecuire to a farm house between Coursel &amp; Course near Desures, Sainer &amp; about 20 kilos from Bolougne. Weather grey</p>
<p>Monday 19<br/>Putting up tents etc &amp; other fatigues incident to making a permanent camp. <br/>Leave is open to Bolougne. Weather still grey.<br/>Mail</p>
<p>Tuesday 20<br/>Ordinary day in billet routine fatigues etc. <br/>Weather still bad. Pay day</p>
<p>Wednesday 21<br/>Bad weather still. Visit town of Desures.</p>
<p>Thursday 22<br/>Ordinary day except that Madame's fowls have suffered. The catching &amp; dispensing of them caused much amusement. Visit Bolougne on leave tomorrow.</p>
<p>Friday 23<br/>Leave to Bolougne. Travel from Coursel, via Desures &amp; Werevynes. The road is a succession of hills culminating in Mt Lambeth overlooking the town. L'Eyline Notre Dame &amp; Bolougne is on top, very prominent. Visit Weivereux &amp; have a look at fishermen &amp; women at work on quay. Arrive home at 11 pm.</p>
<p>Saturday 24<br/>Bath in open baths at Cement Works in warm water, 5 feet deep. Quite a decent swim.</p>
