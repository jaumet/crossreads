<p>Creating a cool draught. Our friend left us here, and we went along to the Royal Exchange, which is the greatest money market in the world. All round the inside walls are huge paintings by great and well- known artists, mostly depicting battles of the early days. From here we visited the Guild Hall which is very similar to a town hall with a large public library. Mr Rowe had to leave me here, after directing me to St Pauls Cathedral. This is a beautiful building with a great dome on<br/>Top after looking round this and seeing the famous painting.</p>
