<p>Monday 10/8/14<br/>At 3 o'clock alarm is given, a ship in sight in the fore. When nearer it was found to be a Japanese steamer. Otherwise nothing unusual. We are in the Great Ocean (Pacific Ocean) on the way to the squadron. Heavy seas towards dusk.</p>
