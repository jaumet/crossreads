 "A" Squadron 1st. Light Horse Training Regt. Egypt 26th Oct.1916 
 Mrs. Vernon Secretary 1st A.L.H. Comforts fund Sydney 
 Dear Madam, By latest mail two parcels of Champion tobacco came to hand. On behalf of the men of our Regiment I desire to thank you for the above, which are still another proof of the kind thought of the people at home and of the benefit of your fund. thanking you and wishing you every success Yours faithfully J Norman MacMillan
