 Mr. Hughes has returned to Australia & we are all waiting to hear if conscription is to come in straight away. It is time it did & I expect a great many who have not yet donned khaki will be glad to have onus of deciding removed from them. At all events all eyes are on Mr. Hughes as he has been so boomed by a certain section of the press in England; we are wondering if he just knows "where he are".
