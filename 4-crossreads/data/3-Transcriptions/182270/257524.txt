
<p>THURSDAY, JULY 31, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Came into town with Linda</p>
<p>&amp; fooled round for a bit. Saw</p>
<p>the Charley&#39;s &amp; went up &amp; bought</p>
<p>some pictures. Had lunch at</p>
<p>the Wentworth and got home after.</p>
<p>Caught the 8.30 train home</p>
<p>Mum, Dad, Al, Graham, Jessie</p>
<p>&amp; self &amp; we had an engaged carriage</p>
