Theres one thing which I find very peculiar & that is keeping to the left of the roads, I have been that long now following the right of the road both in Egypt & in France that it seems the correct side to me & I always find myself walking on the wrong side over here. Have decided to buy a little developing plant for my Kodak as it is much cheaper, & there is a chap in here who is going to teach me all he knows about the game, I think it is much more interesting when you can do your own films besides much cheaper. 22nd.
