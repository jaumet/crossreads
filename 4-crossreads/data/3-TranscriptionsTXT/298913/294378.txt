 21/3/16 Dream am alive with Chats &amp; awake to find that two drunken sergts are crawling around outside looking for diamonds with matches. Hell of a day 
 22/3/16 Payday &ndash; as usual with a great deal of messing about. Try to write letters at night but Bon Gow's voice too continuous. 
 23/3/16 Pay at last  Have a bit of a splash in Cairo after tea using blue band of signal service as a pass. Pay a visit to the Y.M.C.A. &amp; have a good feed.
