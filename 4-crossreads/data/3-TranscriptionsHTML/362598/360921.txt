<p>Sunday 16 April 1916<br/>Reinforcements 2 a.m.  Wrote Dad, Geo. &amp; Mum in Hut.  Moved my Dressing Hut.  Issue summer clothes.  L. McLean out to see us.</p>
<p>Monday 17 April 1916<br/>Night Ward Master.  Met Vic Digre.</p>
<p>Tuesday 18 April 1916<br/>Started my Hut at full strength.  Went to Cairo with Billy, Earn, Dave, Ellis &amp; met Les McLean.  Met Victoria.</p>
<p>Wednesday 19 April 1916<br/>Went to Cairo with Dave Niness.  Eric bought roses.  Met Tommy Soldat &amp; met Italian Girl.  Received mits from Bessie.</p>
<p>Thursday 20 April 1916<br/>Wrote to Bessie.  Received letters from Home, Marge, Hilda, Bess, Nettie.  Went to see Harold &amp; Sarfry.</p>
<p>Friday 21 April 1916<br/>Went to Cairo with B. Bag., Earn, Dave, Billy &amp; Ellis &amp; met McLean.  Supper at Saults [?].  Wrote to Mr. Flaherty, Scotland.</p>
<p>Saturday 22 April 1916<br/>Ida, H. Hey, Anne Methven.  Received letter from Marie &amp; parcels from Home.  Wrote 7 pages to Marge.  Played cricket against No. 4 Aux.  Dave &amp; I put on 30 runs for last wicket.  Dave got 20 &amp; I got 7 not out &amp; caught one man.  We won the match by an innings &amp; 45 runs.</p>
