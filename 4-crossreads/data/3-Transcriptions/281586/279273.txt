<p>1<br/>Let others tell of Eastern Climes<br/>The joys which they possess<br/>Experience has never made<br/>Me love my own the less</p>
<p>2<br/>The magic lure of Egypts night<br/>Ive sung with fervent zest<br/>I've lived beneath them many a moon<br/>Yet think my own are best</p>
<p>3<br/>The North Star's but a cold dead thing<br/>Indeed, I'm at a loss<br/>To find a single group of Stars<br/>To match the Southern Cross.</p>
<p>4<br/>Australian nights are unsurpassed <br/>Australian days are fair<br/>And this Australian's heart is stirred<br/>To feel his home is there</p>
<p>5<br/>Oh leave  me but in life the Joy<br/>Of giving her my love<br/>In death a home beneath the sod<br/>With her blue skies above<br/>L M   28-6-17<br/>Beni Selah</p>
