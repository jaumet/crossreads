Nice fine day, cool early in morning. 13/6/18 Received acknowledgement of my letter from Australian Base Post Office.14/6/18 After tea went to aerodrome & saw observation & fighting planes. One observation plane (2 seater) started its flight when I was there. In the shed saw a Bristol 2 seater fighting plane with 1 machine gun & 2 Lewis guns. It had  264 horse power Rolls-Royce engine & capable of speed about 120 miles per hr. Several trainloads of Americans have passed through last 3 days.16/6/18 Sunday. Cold change.
