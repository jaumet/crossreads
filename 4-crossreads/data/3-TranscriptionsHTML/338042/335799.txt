<p>Performance (in which I do well with "Sari Bair") we are tendered a farewell Evening by Officers &amp; Men of 3rd A.S.C. - Nero once again has a downfall &ndash; likewise a fall down &ndash; Bed at 2 a.m. (5th) Major McKenzie, Lt. Rowe,. Major Parmer. Capt Ramsay A.P.M. etc.</p>
<p>Feb.5th<br/>Packing up for London &ndash; Last night of the "Anzac Coves" in Bailleul &ndash; A fitting conclusion to a most successful run &ndash; Meet Jack Neal.  With George Martin once again.</p>
