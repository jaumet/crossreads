 we are off (I &amp;  thirteen  sixteen others) to the Base Details Etaples.  The excitement &amp; noise of the rest of the morning prevents work &amp; we get pay.  The afternoon off to report in full equipment at 9 a.m. to-morrow, ready to leave.  Until 5-30 p.m. I am taken up with making all my arrangements &amp; sending home by post my parcels.  Meet the Belge &amp; with him &amp; Legge we have a fine little parting dinner in a Restuarant in Rue Lafayette &amp; I take my parting from our mutual little friend Belge (Marcel Doutreloux).
