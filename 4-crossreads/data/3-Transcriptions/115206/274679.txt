<p>April 1918<br/>Friday 19</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours.   D.R.<br/>Very quiet day. Groomed old Mick and had a go at harness &ndash; looks better.<br/>Went down to river and had a swim<br/>During afternoon had a swim.<br/>Sleet fell altho the day was a bright one.<br/>Scottie fell into the mud pond.<br/>Inspection by Lt Booth on the staff.<br/>Satisfactory.</p>
