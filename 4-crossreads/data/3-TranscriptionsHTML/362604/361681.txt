<p>We are both well &amp; having as good a time as we can.</p>
<p>Give my Best Wishes to all the neighbours.  I hope that you are all keeping well &amp; Happy.  What did Nettie do when she watered the Garden.  Did she splash Herself.  She says that she had a feed of guavas.  I did not think there would be enough for anyone to have a feed.</p>
<p>Fond Love from Harry.</p>
