<p>Left Vignacourt at noon and pushed on 11 &frac12; miles to Allonville 13.7.16.</p>
<p>Went back Vignacourt with despatch on cycle 14.7.16</p>
<p>Left Allonville this morning and arrived at Warloy Baillon at about 2 p.m. We expect to discard packs here and proceed to firing line 16.7.16</p>
<p>Left Warloy Baillon. Marched through Albert and proceeded to firing line. 19.7.16</p>
