<p>everything with us including blankets, arrived at the platform we formed up &amp; were then dismissed for an hour or so for the train did not leave till 12 oclock that night we lit a big fire, &amp; soon a concert was in full swing, the band also helped to keep things going,  about 10' oclock that night a big passenger ship came through the Canal bound for Australia, as soon as she was spotted the whole mob rushed over &amp; cheered her like anything &amp; they cheered back again we could see the people quite plainly on the deck, we coo'eed to her till she was out of sight.</p>
