 dotted here &amp; there with little villages of white cottages with red roofs nestling against a spur in the midst of a cluster of orchards &amp; vineyards. The orange orchards are a mass of golden fruit &amp; every lane or road is hedged with wattle or prickly pear on either side. The farther north you go the prettier &amp;  more thickly populated the country becomes. 
 At Tel esh Sharia where the "fertile plains of Philistia" start the country is rolling flat &amp; monotonous.
