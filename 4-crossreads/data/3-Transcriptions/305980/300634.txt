Our final course was rice, flavoured with honey &amp; milk, the most delicious sweets I have ever tasted.  We ate this with the spoon we used for the soup. As the meal progressed, we were served with iced water in porous jars, the water flavoured with rose &amp; verbena leaves.  Coffee was then served &amp; after a chat we left, convinced that the wealthy native knows how to entertain his guests in a delightful manner.  We were sorry to leave this camp, as we were always well supplied with eggs &amp; milk whilst camped in this ancient village of Abid.</p>
