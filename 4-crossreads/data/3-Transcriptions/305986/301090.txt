The worst of it its so cold, the huts have no fires in the, and if you want to get into the YMCA, where there is a fire, and opens at 11 you have to line up at 10.30, in fact you have tom line up for everything here, dinner doctor wash and canteen, They have not paid any of us while in Hospital but I believe you get 5 francs after you have been here a week.</p>
