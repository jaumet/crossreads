<p>I'm afraid he will be a long time getting away with a Commission. There is an arrangement now, that says only &frac12; the required Officers for the A.I.F. are to come from Australia, the other half are made here from men who have been over to the front, so you will see that in future it will be harder &amp; will take more time to get away with a Com. from Australia.</p>
