  
 13 September, Saturday 
 Dinner at Esplanade Cafe. Go to Sports which are great. Aeroplane & motor stunts. Go to Ellis' with crowd for Tea & meet Billy. Meet Amy Bert & Myra Brown. Go to Pictures see "Woman". 
 Recd letter from Lodge & Doris. 
 14 September, Sunday 
 Go to Dee Why. Fix Linos & clean up outside. An awful windy day & it is a terrible drive Home. Sit & talk after chopping wood & cleaning up etc. Go to bed after a bath. 
 Wrote Doris.
