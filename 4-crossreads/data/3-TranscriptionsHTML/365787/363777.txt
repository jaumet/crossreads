<p>[Card]<br/>Dymock's Building<br/>68 Elizabeth Street<br/>SYDNEY</p>
<p>11.12.17</p>
<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>I have to acknowledge with many thanks receipt of your parcel, containing 42 pairs of socks and 7 Balaclavas from the Wahroonga-Warrawee War Chest Branch.</p>
<p>This Depot will be closed from Dec. 22nd to Jan 14th 1918.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully,<br/>"WAR CHEST" SUPPLY DEPOT,<br/>[Signed] J. M. Antill<br/>Hon. Superintendent<br/>Per N.H.M.</p>
