<p>Saturday 29 December</p>
<p>Cold &ndash; Snow on the ground.<br/>One run today &ndash; To Samer visiting A.D.M.S. Paymaster &amp; Signallers<br/>After Tea went for a stroll as usual<br/>As a result of the Field Bakery near Boulogne having been bombed the other night there is now a shortage in bread, the issue being one loaf to six men</p>
