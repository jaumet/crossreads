<p>1.2.15</p>
<p>Thur 11th<br/>Stan &amp; I both received 7 letters last night.  Stan paraded sick as his ankles is weak &amp; has been put on light duties;  We were signalling close to camp this morning &amp; this afternoon was sent out to the Tram Station &amp; were signalling back to Head Quarters;  Yule &amp; I went into Heliopolis to post some letters &amp; had a fairly decent feed for 6 piastres each.  we were paid this afternoon &pound; 1.8.0 &amp; was very acceptable as I have been stony for the last day or so.  I also bought a pipe to night.</p>
