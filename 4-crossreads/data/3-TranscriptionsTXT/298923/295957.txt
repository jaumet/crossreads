 18 October 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out) Kantara  W  East. Remained here all today &amp; believe go Port Said tomorrow to No.14 AGH   I would of course prefer Cairo &amp; X to the wee wife but must go where I am sent- X This place is overun with German &amp; Turk prisoners dying like flies from Pneumonia Malaria etc:- they get the same attention as we do- All sorts of Rumours reach us-  Kaiser abdicated etc etc- X The cutting I asked for was  not  Diary but my first impressions of Jerusalem I think printed in Pastoral Times.X
