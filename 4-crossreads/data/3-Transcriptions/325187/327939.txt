<p>[Saturday 15 July 1916]</p>
<p>Very hot &amp; no breeze today<br/>Some talk of a Rest Camp being formed at Port Said &amp; a percentage of men going for a Spell at a time.  [Following paragraph crossed out]<br/>The Tommies get a lot of leave, but of course the Australians are so undisciplined!!! They cannot be trusted etc. Letter from May dated 28/6/15- please thank her.<br/>We have plenty of Bandages with the Red +  tell May not to bother to send [indecipherable].<br/> We use Adhesive Plaster to cover our Watches just leaving the glass uncovered as the sand is so bad it gets into everything &amp; a Watch soon stops.</p>
