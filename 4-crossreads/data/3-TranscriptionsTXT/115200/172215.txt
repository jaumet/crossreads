 22nd/9/16 
 Cairo 
 to Day I am going to the zooligical gardens measeum and citadal 
 23/9/16/ 
 Yesterday Seargent Major Roy(?) and myself went out to the Cairo Zooligical gardens and we had a most interesting time 
 These is in this zoo. Various Virities of Both animals and Birds. in one place there are about Eight Kangaroos, Presented By the 3 rd  A.L.H regiment and a monkey presented By Pte Warren all there were OK. The grounds are most Beautyful.
