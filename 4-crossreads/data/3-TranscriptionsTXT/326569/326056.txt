 Had tea at Y.M.C.A. Kings Cross &amp; took bus to Tottenham Court Road &amp; booked bed at Y.M.C.A. there.  Then walked about streets till bedtime. 
 Sunday, 20/5/17 Up at 7.45 a.m. &amp; after breakfast walked to Trafalgar Sq. via Charing Cross Rd. &amp; met Uncle Charlie at 10 a.m.  Went via Whitehall to Buckingham Palace &amp; on way saw guard changed at Horse Guards &amp; the guard changed at the Palace.  While there saw some U.S.A. officers in khaki.  Saw Victoria monument &amp; went by St. James Palace to Pall Mall &amp; along it to Trafalgar Square
