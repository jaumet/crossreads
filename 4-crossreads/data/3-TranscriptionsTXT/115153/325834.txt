59 Sycamore Grove East St KildaVic. 1/12/19
Mr H. Wright Mitchell Librarian
Dear Sir,Under separate cover I am sending my war diary by registered packet post, which I hope you shall receive in good order.You will note that I have had my manuscript typewritten and that it now comprised 41 pages. Hoping to receive a favourable reply from you in due course.
I remainYours in anticipation
James Irwin
