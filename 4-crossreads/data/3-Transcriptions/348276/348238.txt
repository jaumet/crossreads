<p>War Diary<br/>1915</p>
<p>[Sketch of a typical pattern of support and fire trenches]</p>
<p>Showing useful type where men have to occupy &ndash; self contained &ndash; strength weak.</p>
<p>No two patterns of trenches are the same &ndash; no one pattern is possible &ndash; Accommodate Resting men near their firing positions &ndash; Sqn. cook house handy &ndash; Recesses here and there for everything.  In Paradoss such as  spare ammo., Bombs; Flares.</p>
<p>Label everything and every place name Roads and alleys.</p>
<p>[Sketch]<br/>Biscuit Box partitioned for various types of Bombs:  They should be replenished and cleaned daily.</p>
