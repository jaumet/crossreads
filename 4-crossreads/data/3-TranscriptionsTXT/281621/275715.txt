 Monday 10 The equipping &amp; examinations of the draft have been completed &amp; we expect to move any moment. We have parades every few hours to assure out not being out of camp. 
 Tuesday 11 The weather is beautiful but the pack is heavy. 
 Thursday 13 Still training for draft to France &amp; dancing about the country with packs. 
 Saturday 15 Another military funeral at Tidworth, 2nd in my army career. We are under marching orders &amp; tomorrow we move over to France. The O.C. is giving a supper tonight. Meet Major Wilson on the road.
