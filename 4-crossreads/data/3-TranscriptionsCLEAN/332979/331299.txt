  
 The Little Boys & Girls waved their hats & shouted out to us. We in turn gave them the (Australian Cooee.) The Seenery through the City was beautiful the Long Rows of Houses & Narrow Streets & the people at the Windows gave us a Welcome by waving  their  all sorts of Articles. As we left Devenport at 6 Pm & the Sun  was at mo  had almost disappeared, we could only get a partial glimpse of the Country en route, but still what we did see it looked beautiful & Green, the Ivy growing all along the Embankment & up the buildings.
