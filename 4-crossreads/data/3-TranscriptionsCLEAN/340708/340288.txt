
Oct 1917Thursday 11.Went to the University this morning & the art Gallery in the afternoon.   Went to a Revue called Goodbye this eveningFriday 12thVisited a munitions worksSaturday 13thWent to a Soccer matchSunday 14thVisited Lock Lomond   I would very much like to stop here.  The top of ald Ben Lomond as just got his winter garb onMonday 15thWent to Beardmores ship yards seen what will be the largest submarine afloat & a lot of other interesting This this place covers a mile & half on the bank of the Clyde.
