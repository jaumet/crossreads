
Government House,Rabaul.4th Oct. 1914.
Sir,I have the honour to forward herewith list of German Government Officials and their Wives, who are being deported this day by H.M.A.S. "Berrima".The Officials have not taken the Oath of Neutrality/
I have the honor to be,Sir,Your obedient servant,
Colonel
Administrator.
To:- The Military Commandant,Sydney.
