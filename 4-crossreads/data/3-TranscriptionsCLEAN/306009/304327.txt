
1915May. 21 Fri.Busy all the morning getting out Returns etc.  Lance Cpl. Hill came out of Hospital (influenza) to-day so that now there are two of us in the Admin. Office.Went into Town (Cairo) in the afternoon & was measured for an issue of khaki drill coat & long trousers.During the summer which has now commenced, the shops are closed from noon to 3 p.m. - the hottest part of the day.Saw a very pretty grotto in the Esbekieh Gardens to-day. We also have a very interesting grotto in the grounds of Gezira Palace.
