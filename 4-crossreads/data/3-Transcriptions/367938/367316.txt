<p>After &frac12; hr of chat we slid down a couple of hundred feet of ropes very comfortably, with niches cut as steps &amp; crawled into this comfortable den &ndash; the floor is all stone lumps wh. are hard to sleep on &ndash; but the jam spills etc of the 8 of us make it a little softer.</p>
