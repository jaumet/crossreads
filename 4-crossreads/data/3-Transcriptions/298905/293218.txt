There has also been a very heavy artillery duel in progress most of the afternoon, the heavy guns have been very active &amp; I daresay damaging we had a few casualties today from snipers Captain McKenzie goes away on leave tonight I hear. It looks pretty dull this evening &amp; feels something like rain, I hope it keeps fine, the companies are all on short rations today I believe.<br/>18th.
