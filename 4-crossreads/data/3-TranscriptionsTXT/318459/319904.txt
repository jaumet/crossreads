[1916 entries]July 22/16 Saturdaywhen getting through. discovered that the colours on my right shoulder are nearly torn off either by a piece of shell or shrapnel bullet.6. p.m. off out to Trenches again but not to the hell we were in the other night thank goodness hope to be back again in few days.
July 23/16, Sundayspending quiet day in Trenches, huns &amp; ourselves tired &amp; I slept nearly all day, was on all night till 4 this morning &amp; shall be again to night.The guns however never seem to stop &amp; are sending shells over us.
Monday July 24/16all quiet to day did some sand bagging or I should say mud &amp; had an excellent sleep am alright again, dont expect to be here much longer but it will do mejust going on duty 9.30 p.m.
Tuesday July 25/16After stand to 2.45 a.m. did a bit of washing, Boiled some water &amp; had Tea dont go on till 10.Took our Pay Books last night so must be going to pay us &amp; dam near time to &pound;2-17-1 they owe me.might leave here tomorrow.
I think the name of that fight of ours was Devenent. We are just beginning to realize what we came through.The name given by Germans to our fight was Fromelles.
Paid to day &pound;1-8-8 leaves me a Balance of &pound;1-8-7 &amp; deferred pay &pound;10.16-0 in all &pound;12-4.7 to date.The shells have started again &amp; are knocking down the Trenches to our right.
Thursday 26/16nothing of any moment up to 2 p.m. todayword passed along to get under cover as Trench mortars are going to commence at 4.
