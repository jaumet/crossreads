<p>My dearest Father,<br/>I am posting these developed negatives from London under Registered Postage.  Don't bother about sending any prints back, Dad dear, but there are some interesting ones among them &amp; I have explained them all.  There are none that the Military would prohibit.<br/>Love from Leo<br/>R  24.7.16</p>
