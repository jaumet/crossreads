
Thursday Oct 5.10.16Stuffing saddles to dayFriday Oct 6.10.16Branding camels to day number of my camel 2824.Saturday Oct 7.10.16Preparing to leave for the desert to day went into heilopolis to night returned to camp and packed up.Sunday Oct 8.10.16Revallie 4 o'clock had Breakfast 5. saddled up and rode to olbassia left about 8 o'clock arrived at Alfadan 5.p.m unloaded saddled up crossed canal and camped five miles out.
