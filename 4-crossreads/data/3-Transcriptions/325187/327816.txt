<p>[Saturday 11 March 1916]</p>
<p>Exchanged <u>warm</u> Warm clothing for summer things at J Cooks &amp; Son &amp; bought 500 Cartridges for my gun at <strike>75</strike> 100 P.T. per 100-<br/>Went Heliopolis saw old Camp met Miss Myra Wyse &amp; took her to Tea.  <strike>Mardi Ritchie was out.</strike>  The old Saturday night Dances are on again, at the Principal Hotels but I did not join in.  Got some boots &amp; Trousers for the men.  Had several Hot baths &amp; took back Mosquitoe Nets for all Officers-</p>
