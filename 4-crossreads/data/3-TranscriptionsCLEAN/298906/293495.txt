The 1st Battalion reinforcements came in last night about, 150 strong got here a t 11.30. Have had a stiff mornings work. Battalion drill & old Tubby kept us going all the morning, very funny at times to see the mix's up's & hear the arguments, some more "Fragments" came across last night, Taplin brought me a batch of letters from Len.Pay day round once, & I needed mine for it was a fair while since I drew my last money.This afternoon 2 other Sergts
