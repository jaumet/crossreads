 Dec. 3rd Pack up, load lorries and leave Le Cateau at 9 a.m. &ndash; During our journey (by road) we halt at one point to allow the King &amp; Prince of Wales to pass. The Royal party are at present visiting French Towns. - Arrive 2 p.m. at Avesnes. A good but crowded billet.
