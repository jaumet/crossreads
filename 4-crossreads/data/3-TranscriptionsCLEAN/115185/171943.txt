  
 Mrs Steward - the nice N.Z. woman who came to Lemnos to see her husband Col. Steward die - is managing the tea room with her sister. They have fixed up a writing room and reading room as well and the boys were thoroughly enjoying the homeliness of it all. Such nice well groomed boys. I wish you could see them. Their clothes are old & worn from Gallipoli, but they've brushed them up and get their boots & leggings cleaned and hair well brushed & shaven faces - so full of fun and health. It's a great work these women are doing for these men - and I often think how grateful the mothers at home would be to see that place with their boys happy and being cared for in an atmosphere of home.
