  
 (But Emmott came & told me that the raid had been a complete failure, & that Merris Ridge or Mount Merris had been captured) I strolled over to the point of the dressing hut, & saw the light motors unloading  the  our later wounded cases, & the Fritzes who had had to wait their turn behind our chaps, they not, of course being serious cases.
