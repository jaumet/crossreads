<p>1916 &nbsp; SATURDAY 14 &nbsp; OCTOBER</p>
<p>Cloudy with a very cold gale blowing. I was a mess orderly so did not go out on parade. Lay in the hut all the afternoon &amp; read. Had a book called the Mischief Maker&quot; by Oppenheim &amp; its very interesting.</p>
