<p>31-3-17<br/>Sat 31st<br/>This morning we had a kit inspection by the C.O.&amp; afterwards all the Sigs had our photos taken which we intend to have enlarged &amp; presented to Jimmy, Jimmy left at 11 AM on his way to Romani . No football today on account of sports meeting run by the 1st L.H.F.A. We had 5 men in the Sigs who entered (besides FitzMaurice of H.Q.)  Locke, Mann, MacNamara, Upham &amp; Hargrave, this was for the 100 yards Bde Championship, but only Locke &amp; Upham started, Locke won his heat easily, &amp; Upham run a dead heat in his, both running in the final; Locke finished third being beaten by Schooler 2nd Regt</p>
