May.
12th. With the "Karagola" just astern of us, we passed out of the Canal at 5.30, covering 87 miles in 11  hours.
However the "Karagola" left two hours or more ahead of us but we caught her at 8 p.m., passing her to strains of "Good Byee!!"
Country of Sinai that of Egypt along Gulf of Suez is the most inhospitable country I have seen. Now in Red Sea.
14th. Very hot two days a few patients stricken down.
Southern Cross visible to-night!!!
