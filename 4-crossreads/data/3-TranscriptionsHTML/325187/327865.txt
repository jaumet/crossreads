<p>[Monday 1 May 1916]</p>
<p>Court again.  Hear that the 2nd L.H. Brigade are fighting on the Canal, I would have been in this but for the Trial  Hear that Brigadier Ryan is shot through the stomach &amp; another 124 other Casualties.<br/>Glad to hear young Charlie got off with a slight wound.<br/>Mosquitoes bad-<br/>My horses are in good order the Gov. are supposed to Clip; Shoe; etc, but I find it costs a lot the upkeep in these small ways. when away from the Regiment.</p>
