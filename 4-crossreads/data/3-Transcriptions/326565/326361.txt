<p>Friday 12/5/16<br/>Went to the ordnance stores, across the canal from Ferry Post, on a waggon, while there received news to the effect that we are to be relieved by the 14th F. Amb to-morrow.  On my return to camp found all busy packing all equipment &amp; generally preparing to move.</p>
<p>Saturday 13/5/16<br/>Turned out at 4 AM to prepare to move off.  Breakfast 4.30 AM.  Lucky enough to be detailed for baggage guard, which enabled me to travel</p>
