<p>Tuesday 18 September 1917</p>
<p>Alex:<br/>Am posting you the figure of a Native, drawn by a Cotton Merchant friend of mine, from life. <strike>I wish I could send you some for Servants. I am sure poor old Day is weary of House keeping without proper help. She has done her BIT.</strike></p>
<p>The horses here are terrible crocks &amp; are cruelly used. You hear the story of putting a small eel down a horse's throat to make him lively well all this is <strike>down</strike> done by the Jelins here &amp; many worse things besides.</p>
