 Arrived at Steenvorde 7 p.m. after a slow passage &amp; bumping a number of midroad walkers our head lights having failed. Sighs of relief &amp; a good meal. 
 13th Saturday.  It rained like hell &amp; blew a full gale all night &amp; damned cold in the bargain. Thank heavens we are not in the trenches - it must be frightful.  To-day I am setting out on an unpleasant task. Shackleton has not met his obligation to me for &pound;530, an amount he promised faithfully to meet already several times.
