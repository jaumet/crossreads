<p>Dec. 2nd Thursday<br/>Glorious day.  Tinned meat &amp; vegetable ration issued just lovely cold.  Rice &amp; tea breakfast, Tea evening.  Letter writing &amp; improving bunk afternoon.  Bomb burst within 5 ft. of 3 of us morning fortunately no injuries.  No. 2 Northern.</p>
<p>Dec. 3rd Friday<br/>Lovely day, mild night, rations still very low, &frac14; water.</p>
<p>Dec. 4th Sat.<br/>Day fine, evening not too cold.  Line quiet.</p>
<p>Dec. 5th Sunday<br/>Weather continues mild.</p>
<p>Dec. 6th Monday<br/>Weather very nice.  Mail in.  Parcel from Eileen.  Day off duty</p>
