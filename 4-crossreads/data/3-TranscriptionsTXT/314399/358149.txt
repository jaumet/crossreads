 Sunday 1 June 1919 Shaved &amp; washed some clothes. After lunch went out to the hospital to see Bill. He had been sent back to bed on a/c of having a temp. Still was far from well. Marie rang up &amp; then came out. Had tea at the hospital. Took her home &amp; then came home myself. 
 Monday 2 June 1919 Went to see people at Grosvenor Place about Polytechnic. No good tho. Had driving lesson &amp; lecture. Came home &amp; wrote, read &amp; washed a pair of slacks. Looks very much like rain.
