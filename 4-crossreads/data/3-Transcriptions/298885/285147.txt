<p>eventually, after many enquiries found Stan and we were like a pair of old women trying to talk about everything together and at the same time</p>
<p>Left again for London by 7.40PM train as Stan was going out all next day shooting and it wasn't worth my while staying.</p>
<p>Arrive London 10PM and then found my bed, much contented with my day's lot.</p>
<p>June 12th &ndash; 17<br/>Amused myself walking around London getting lost etc</p>
<p>Norrie left for France much to my disappointment</p>
<p>After dinner found my way out to Bayswater</p>
