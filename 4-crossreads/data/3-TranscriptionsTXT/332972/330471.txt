 The Sun, Tuesday, April 15, 1919. Huns To Go 4000 Aliens to be Packed Off Commonwealth Takes Action Ships Now On The Way Melbourne, Tuesday. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It is understood that vessels for the deportation of 4000 Germans, Bulgarians, and Turks from Australia and 300 enemy aliens from New Zealand have been arranged for by the Commonwealth Government. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Two of the vessels to be employed will leave Italy shortly, and several are now on their way from Great Britain, carrying troops on board. Among these is the Wyreema. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The departure of the aliens from Australia should take place in about eight weeks time.
