 F &amp; W 23 Sun 1258 letters to 20 June Recd letter from Norm Upton. Pull bike to pieces &amp; have an awful job   [indecipherable] back tire  &amp; back wheel. Sit  &amp; yarn all time afterwards with Dick about  [indecipherable] France expectations.   Go to see .  'Our Miss Gibbs' 
 F 24 Mon Lovely Day. Recd letter from Roy. Influenza very bad  &amp;  we have to open Special Wards. Siddie has to work back all night. I don't. Wrote to Dad. Mum. George  &amp;  Aunt Annie. 
 F 25 Tues. Recd letter from Mum. Go for a run  &amp; do much better. Dick goes to bed ill. Buy paper &amp; print Photos also alter Breeches. Meet Jim Coylie again. Very busy day &ndash; 
 F 26 Wed Recd letters from Jennie  &amp;  Maisie. Mend Puncture &amp; generally clean up bike. Run around oval with my shirt all out &amp; the Lady looks on. Have supper read &amp;  go to bed to [indecipherable] Wrote to M.R.L. 
 F 27 Th Go for a run. Have a general tidy up. Sew Breeches legs, brettons etc. Deam of Saving K 90/M. &amp; in room opp. Summerhill. See in through top window looking for something. See [indecipherable] on [indecipherable] Prom. 
 W &amp; F 28 Fri All afternoon. Go to Sarum with Thomas. Buy lots of things &amp; return 7.10 Train. Parcel from Bess with lots of groceries etc. Met Hilda's double &amp; Mabel P. &amp; Lancashire lass. G.E.G. comes back from Leave 
 F 29 Sat Very busy day. Go out at 6pm &amp; field for our team who get badly beaten by 1 30 odd runs. Have a general tidy up later on &amp; talk until bed time. Top installs a Hot water (Electric) system &amp; one switches on the Hot water before he gets up in the morning. We [indecipherable] Biscuits &amp; they are lovely. Nether haven. Shorts.
