
This silence lasted until midnight on the 3rd day, & the instant time was up talk about a rifle fire I think nearly everyone had a go & relieved his feelings, it had been a most trying time for all concerned, & every one was glad when they could fire again, I expect the old Turks chuckled to themselves when we started to fire again, but the silence had served its purpose, & little did they know it.
