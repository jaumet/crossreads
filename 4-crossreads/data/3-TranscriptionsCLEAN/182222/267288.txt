 Aug 13/ Become 23 rd  A M G Coy att H Q  3 rd  Div.             Under orders France 25. Handed in Kit & standing to to march out. No Beer to be left in Park house, by order.         on (19)   Got another two days leave at last moment on nod. took spare kit to M rs  H.   Put a red edging on City of Westminster & turned Waterloo Sta round a couple of times with the old school mob.                                                   Saw the big raid from a distance.
