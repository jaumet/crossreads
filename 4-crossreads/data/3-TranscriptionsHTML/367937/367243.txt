<p>so that is the finish of them I expect.  We are only supposed to take what we stand up in and one pair of short trousers but the pockets of my overcoat are pretty big ones you know so am stacking in frannels &amp; socks.  We will require a good bit of training yet I expect as none of us know much about machine guns. It has been terrible dusty here the last two days but they say there is no dust out there.  I think we go to Romani away out from the Canal near Katia. I think I saw it mentioned in the paper the other day so suppose the censor will not stop this.</p>
