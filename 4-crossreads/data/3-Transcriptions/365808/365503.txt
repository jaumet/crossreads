<p>Craignish<br/>185 Macquarie St.<br/>July 14th</p>
<p>Regarding the case of Lt Ferguson I enclose a letter from the surgeon who treated him at the time of the injury. I saw him about 3 weeks later in the Empire Military Hospital. I confirm the report of Capt. Maybury.<br/>Lt Ferguson improved satisfactorily &ndash; and I saw him when I returned to Sydney 3 months ago.
