<p>14 November, Friday</p>
<p>Work get 10/- rise.</p>
<p>15 November, Saturday</p>
<p>Dinner at Esp Caf&eacute;. Tea with Muriel &amp; Eunice. Watch sports in Bay &amp; go down Corso at night. Meet Stan, Bruce, Dobboes, Sherriffs &amp; Bat Cocks.</p>
<p>Play Billiards at M's place.</p>
<p>Hear about Percy [?] farming.</p>
