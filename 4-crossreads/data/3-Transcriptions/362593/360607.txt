<p>Sunday 29 September 1918<br/>Orderly Officer carrying despatches to Prince Feisel head of the Hejaz which is co-operating with.  Dropped despatches on him North of Deraa.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 3 h. 0 m.  A good trip.</p>
<p>Monday 30 September 1918<br/>Road recco.<br/>Pilot Lt. McGuinness.  In air 4h.<br/>A long trip.<br/>Beirut &amp; Rayak.</p>
