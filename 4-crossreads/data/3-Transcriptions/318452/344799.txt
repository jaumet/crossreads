<p>General Walker was present. We had communion to follow in the School house on Monday evening. I distributed some cigarettes &amp; writing paper to the men of the 2nd Bn and gave them an address on Egypt. They took a keen interest in it and listened attentively &amp; one man moved a vote of thanks &amp; it was carried by acclamation.<br/>I received word this week that I could return to Australia &amp; that Pitendrigh would take my place with the first Brigade<br/>I shall be sorry to leave<br/>\</p>
