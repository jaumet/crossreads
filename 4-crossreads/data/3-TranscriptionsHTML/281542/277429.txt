<p>Wednesday 2nd Feb 1916</p>
<p>Could not visit Ishmailia.  <strike>Returned to Ghizeh with Sgt Hales,</strike></p>
<p>Thursday 3rd Feb 1916</p>
<p>Usual Ghizeh routine.  In Cairo.  At "Majestic" Pictures at night.  "Amour", "Amour" "Amour" - <strike>not sentimental stuff</strike>.</p>
<p>Friday 4th Feb 1916</p>
<p>Zoological Gardens Picquet.  Interesting job.  Magnificent palatial gardens &amp; a great variety of animals, birds etc.  Monkeys, Giraffes, &amp; lions most interesting.  Egyptian band, composed of reformatory boys, discorded music during afternoon.</p>
<p>Saturday 5th Feb 1916</p>
<p>Marched to Zeitoun, full marching order, with about 500 others.  About 10 miles.  Ended up with blistered toe &amp; sore feet generally.  Placed in "A" details.</p>
<p>Sunday 6th Feb 1916</p>
<p>Quiet day.  Early morning parade 7 o'clock.  Later than Ghizeh.  10 o'clock parade to Church.  No afternoon parade.  Attended Church <strike>parade during afternoon</strike> at Y.M.C.A. at night which I thoroughly enjoyed.</p>
<p>Monday 7th Feb 1916</p>
<p>Nothing unusual.  Route march during morning.</p>
<p><strike>Wrote Mab a letter at YMCA hut.</strike></p>
<p>Tuesday 8th Feb. 1916</p>
<p>Quiet day.</p>
<p>Wednesday 9th Feb 1916</p>
<p>Paid 30/-.</p>
<p>Thursday 10th Feb. 1916</p>
<p></strike>Posted letters to Mab, Grace &amp; Mum.</strike></p>
<p>Friday 11th Feb 1916</p>
<p>Usual routine.  Paraded sick.  Back again troubling me.  Dr. orders iodine painting.</p>
<p>Saturday 12th Feb 1916</p>
<p>Quiet day.  Not feeling well.</p>
