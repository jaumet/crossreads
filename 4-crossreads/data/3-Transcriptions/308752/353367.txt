<p>Whilst in the Trenches I received the following letters, ask my friends to read this &amp; accept it an answer.   We can safely call this the 4th Edition of my Diary.   Kindly type it.</p>
<p>Mother &amp; Father Sept 3rd, 34th, 10th 18th August 13th Date of letter.   28th</p>
<p>Vera &ndash; Sept 4th &amp; 12th &amp; pr socks.<br/>Dolly Levy &ndash; Sept. 4th &amp; August 29th.<br/>Dorothy Loder &ndash; Sept. 3rd<br/>Rose Cohen &ndash; August 30th<br/>Glady Herford &ndash; August 31st<br/>Berla Gall&amp; &ndash; Sept. 3rd &amp; 11th.<br/>Bene &ndash; Sept. 3rd &amp; 11th.<br/>My two cousins.  Daisy G. Colley-Priest<br/>    Lily B. Colley-Priest<br/>Fathers sister (Mrs Beaus)</p>
<p>I suppose a good mail &amp; parcels for me have gone down in the Arabia.<br/>Fondest Love&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Hard Luck from your only son   Langford</p>
