<p>11<br/>we added more milk to it from a tin I had.</p>
<p>18/5/16<br/>A little cooler to-day no hot wind. Rode up to YMCA hut on orderlies' horse to get a couple of tins of peaches to give the lads a return treat for cocoa last night.</p>
<p>Just before tea the "Mooltan" went through &amp; looked great. There ought to be more mail in a day or so.</p>
<p>Received a few papers yesterday amongst them was a Bulletin from Ruth with note from Cecil.</p>
<p>The lads came across &amp; we had quite a party on the chaff stacks of the Depot (2 tins peaches &amp; tin of milk).</p>
<p>19/5/16.<br/>Did some washing this morning in the same water</p>
