<p>[AUGUST &ndash; SEPTEMBER]<br/>Friday 31<br/>Nothing much doing just fooling round, the birds practising wood fighting ect, it is not such a bad war when back here<br/>SEPTEMBER Saturday 1st<br/>Pay day, 20 F a fortnight a promenade a drop of fire water tin of fags and a post card or 2 and then "finee" &ndash; broke<br/>Sunday 2<br/>A beautiful moonlight night but yonder sky is ablaze with gun fire and the roar of the guns is awful<br/>Overhead planes of the hun may be seen in the flashes of the searchlights and the explosion of their bombs is hellish, the place is shaking like a jelly</p>
