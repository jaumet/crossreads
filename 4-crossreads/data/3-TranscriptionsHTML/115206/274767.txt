<p>July 1918<br/>Tuesday 16</p>
<p>Bresle. F.O.P. and Bty Position.<br/>Weather perfect though a little warm if anything. <br/>Went on duty from 2 am till 8 am. Very light shelling of Ribemont by the Bosche.<br/>Parson's arrived on scene demanding water but I made him some tea. About 2 hours later he wanted more tea &ndash; he did not get it.<br/>Arrived home at 7pm with Percy. Old Mick Sheedy came up to Battery. Had a heavy thunderstorm in early part of evening drenched everything.<br/>Fritz attack commenced on a 40 mile front</p>
