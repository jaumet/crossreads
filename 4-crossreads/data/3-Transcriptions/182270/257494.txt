
<p>TUESDAY, JULY 1, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; cold wind. Burning off in</p>
<p>Middle till dinner time then</p>
<p>round the paddocks. I took</p>
<p>Tickle over today &amp; pay him</p>
<p>35/- per week, &amp; start to run the</p>
<p>place on its own. George finished</p>
<p>concreeting the troughs at Boundary</p>
<p>&amp; has to wait a few days for them</p>
<p>to dry.</p>
