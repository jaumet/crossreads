 June 1915 28 Mon On Popes 
 29 Tuesday Bourke and Monaghan killed. Turks rushed down gully after reverse on Walkers Top &ndash; about 200 &ndash; 250 of them. Broadbent carried 1 gun to left and we drove them back easily with many casualties about 10.30 Pm on. 4 wounded. Nash of 2nd regt K.
