
Friday 27/7/17Muster parade and general clean up for move.Saturday 28.7.17Marched to Miramont in intense heat, entrained at 4.45 pm for Cassell.Sunday 29/7/17Reached Cassell 3 hours late marched in a terrible thunderstorm to Woesterne.Monday 30/7/17Battalion in Camp H.Q.s at Billets in large farm, looked for training ground, wrote to Doff.Tuesday 31/7/17Visited by Generals irdwood, Smythe, & Wisdom, had a sleep during afternoon.
