
move to morrowWednesday Dec 26It was raining & cold & the roads were in an awful state. We were bogged several times crossing a flat near Yebnah. Yebnah is the usual type of filthy village. Our Horses were absolutely bogged at times & there was nothing else for it but to get off & sink in the mud ourselves. A few Horses were drowned .
