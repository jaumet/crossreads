  
 16th May  122 Miles Up 6 am as usual to find place awash.  Washing house smashed in by seas, speed reduced to nearly a standstill, sea still high, high wind on port bow & cold with rain on & off. spray often over bow.  No parades.   Sausages  Chops etc; breakfast, no duty all day reading most of morning C.S. & Magazine.  Dinner roast meet, vegetables currant pudding, Beeftea.  Showery all day, very little sun at all.  Many off their food.  Reading, Writing & sleeping till teatime.  Singing & reading till 8 pm then bed.  Showery all day & cold wind sea.
