<p>May 11th.<br/>Y.M.C.A &amp; Duties.<br/>May 12th.<br/>I witness my first Post Mortem Examination- Capt Culpin performs same.  Most interesting operation.  Y.M.C.A in Evening<br/>May 13th.<br/>Weather wet &amp; miserable- Artillery silent- Return of some of our "boys" from Bois-Grenier.<br/>May. 14th.<br/>Sabbath- Also "Mother's Day"- Attend Mass- Night duties.  Mail- Walk to Croix-du-Bac.  Quiet day.</p>
