<p>W.T. Signal</p>
<p>Receiving Ship - Flag</p>
<p>Transmitting Ship - Protector</p>
<p>Date - 13.9.14</p>
<p>Time of Despatch or receipt - 4.11 pm</p>
<p>Pass to first ship arriving Herbertshohe &nbsp;Tell O.C. troops Protector abreast Road between Kaba Kaul&nbsp;&amp; Herbertshohe as directed just west.</p>
<p>Beresford only asked for maxim&nbsp;&amp; field stand&nbsp;&amp; for Protector to proceed to&nbsp;position overlooking Road between KabaKaul&nbsp;&amp; Herbertshohe. &nbsp;Shall I stay here heavy firing going on round Herbertshohe. &nbsp;1605</p>
