 Decembre 14 Coy resting today after tiring day yesterday coming from Hanebeek. Had 10 hours travelling in train to do a distance of about 60 miles &amp; then marched about 7K from Devres. 
 Decembre 15 Coy engaged on fatigues making themselves comfortable. Went into Samer in the afternoon. A very poor class town. Coy transport arrived after four days travelling.
