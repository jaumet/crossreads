Packed up this morning & reported at B.O.R. at 1300 & walked down to the station at Amesbury where we caught the train, the country looks magnificent after yesterday's heavy rainstorms, all the hedges are snowy white & the fields present a great contrast of colour with the daisies & buttercups in full bloom & horses & cattle feeding contentedly.
