 BRISTOL. FARM Monday 17 If the rain keeps off this week will be one of the wildest weeks in history. Guns and munitions haunt me Tuesday 18 Three oclock in the morn I am on picket, horses very quiet but the front line one blaze of fire and the fight as just began Wednesday 19 The Batt. go into the fray tonight which will  be the greatest battle of the war God be with them and lead them to "Victory" Thursday 20 It would have broken Germanys heart, if they'd have seen our boys marching into action singing &amp; laughing like school boy's
