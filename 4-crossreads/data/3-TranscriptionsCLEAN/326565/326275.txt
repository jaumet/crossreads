
Very dreary marching across sand.  Accompanied by camel train of 70 camels with provisions.  Had tea 5 pm, bully beef & biscuits.
Monday 20/12/15In the desert, not a tree or growth of any description to be seen.  Infantry trench digging & constructing wire entanglements.  Meals still of bully beef & biscuits.  Easy day, lying about the most of the day.  Few dressings to do. caused while opening tins of bully beef.
Tuesday 21/12/15Arose 6.30 A.M. breakfast 8.30 AM.  Very busy day, making dug out & communication
