I may say it was freezing cold the morning we reached here & it has been so all along up till a few days ago we were bivouaced in the open & it rained for two days & nights so you can imagine I can fully realise what it is to sleep in wet blankets & wet clothes. When one got up in the morning he was like a piece of frozen mutton & had to wait for the sun to thaw him (Say a feather bed would be just alright now eh what!)Up to know we have had not much trouble only a few skirmishes, with no casualties The Terriers wiped out a tribe of Bedouins & had a few wounded.
