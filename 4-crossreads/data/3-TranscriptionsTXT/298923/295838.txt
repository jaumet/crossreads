 21 June 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out) Cannot use Clinical Thermometer in the day time- the more it is shaken the higher it goes till it BUSTS X You mention Fetherstonehaughs Book, I should love to have it but better wait I think will try &amp; borrow from Mrs Fetherstonehaugh later on- if it is not  repulsive Dolly might read it but she is not a very serious minded young lady in the book line. X Australasian Feb 2 says "Rabbits within  42  miles of" "PERTH are causing a" " great scare "    Germans  within 42 miles of  English  Channel result in 1 Recruit at Show. My Belt is going up hole by hole- I wanted it &amp; may not be so portly after another 3 weeks.
