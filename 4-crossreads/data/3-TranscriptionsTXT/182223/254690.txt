 Thursday 19&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; Major Purdy of Sydney. Here for lunch with me.&nbsp; Korno taken by the Germans&nbsp; Teutons victorious all along the Eastern front&nbsp; 
 Friday 20&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; About hospital all day.&nbsp; Young Coppin from Elsternwick Victoria took tea with me at 8 p.m.&nbsp; 
 Saturday 21&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; In Cairo during the morning&nbsp; Heard that Ramsay Smith had left No 1 A.G.H., and that Barrett had been deprived of his posts except the Red Cross business. 
 Sunday 22&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; In Cairo dng mng, saw Ramsay Smith at Sheppheards Hotel.&nbsp;Mass 7 a.m.&nbsp; Purdy to lunch  &amp; tea . Purdy &amp; Paton to tea. Drive round Matereh [Matarieh]&nbsp;between 6 &amp; 8 pm..
