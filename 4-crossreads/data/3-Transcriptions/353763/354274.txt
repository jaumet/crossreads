<p>The row the natives make is astonishing  -  They lie around the Atria -  a fleet of barges, laden with bananas for the Sydney market  -  We are all quite well &ndash; Officers &amp; men  -  We haven't any hardships though moans are heard now &amp; again but that's normal among any body of men &amp; women  -</p>
<p>I'm learning all I can about the Navy &amp; navy ways &ndash; I like the N.O. &amp;  their ways - &amp; I think I fit into this position all right  -  If this war lasts as there is evy indication of its doing one is bound if I follow up the Submarine to see Something &ndash; sooner or later  -  We drink too  -  To the day &ndash; ['Hock &ndash; Der Tag'] !!! -  Vale  - Fred</p>
