 Jan 13th The rain came down in torrents all night &amp; has continued to day accompanied by heavy lightening The Bdge in Bivouacs (Bivvys) were washed out &amp; are having a miserble time. We are now attached to desert Coys again 
 Jan 14th Heavy rains &amp; thunderstorms continued through the night but cleared into glorious sun shine during the morning Cpt H___&nbsp;secured two rooms to day for  the use of del &nbsp;his photographic requirements close to hand &amp;&nbsp;we spent part of the day fitting them up The new workrooms are part of a house occupied by a Yiddish family&nbsp;&nbsp;
