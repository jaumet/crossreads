<p>3.12.17. Usual parades. Cold.</p>
<p>4.12.17. March &amp;  gas lecture in morning. March under Fischer un afternoon. Usual parades. Football. Sunny but cold.</p>
<p>5.12.17. Chaff cutting  Sunny but cold. Voted in aftn &amp;  wrote letters.</p>
<p>6.12.17. Fine &amp;  Cloudy &amp;  not so cold  Usual parades. Dismissed in morning  &amp;  read  &amp;  wrote. Promenade in aftn  parade</p>
<p>7.12.17. Raining slightly &amp; warm  rumours of moving shortly  Usual parades. Boots  in morning and dismissed in aftn. Played bridge &amp;  read.</p>
<p>8.12.17 Cloudy usual parades  Dismissed in aftn.</p>
<p>9.12.17. Raining a bit. Guard at 9. Usual one prisoner. Clark</p>
<p>10.12.17. Boulogne at 10 and caught lorry right into town. Had a good look round and feeling tired came back at 6. Interesting trip</p>
<p>11.12. Footballers went to Boulogne  Bath in aftn  Fine.</p>
<p>12.12. Fine usual parades. Concert of 1st Div Engrs in evening. Rotten.</p>
<p>13.12.17. Moved off at 9. and marched without packs to Blequin  most of way Billets very crowded. Arrived about 3.30    Transport marching separately</p>
