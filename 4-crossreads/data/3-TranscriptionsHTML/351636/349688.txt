<p>The Centre for Soldiers' Wives &amp; Mothers,<br/>92B. Pitt Street, Sydney.<br/>February 1st 1916</p>
<p>Dear Mr. Layton &ndash;<br/>Could you give Mrs. Brown a little immediate assistance. She is entirely without funds until she can draw her husband's pay on the 16t inst. He went <strike>enlisted yesterday</strike> into camp yesterday &amp; she has two little children with her. It is a case of real necessity &amp; I hope you will be able to help.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>B. Booth.</p>
