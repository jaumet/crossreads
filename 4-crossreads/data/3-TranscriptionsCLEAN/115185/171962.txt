He also had great difficulty being accepted - though very large and healthy about Gibson's stature - he has very weak eyes and could not get a commission but managed things for the Veterinary Corp. somehow or other. He's a real country man. Armidale school - simple and straightforward - but should think [indecipherable] the land - has 5 brothers all - but the younger at Armidale School - on their own - and between them 9!! sisters. All married but three. His father has two stations - and this lad has had full running of them - but I think they have good over-seers there - he is however very anxious as he expects to hear of his Father's death any day - and his Mother is very dependent on this son. Guy Harden is a nice boy in the middle of his engineering course at Sydney University. We had lunch with them at the National Hotel.
