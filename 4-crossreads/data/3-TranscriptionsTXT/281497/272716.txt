After a little while we marched down to the Convalescent part of the Camp, &amp; pitched a tent for ourselves, that being done Len &amp; I went for a walk over the sand here for the tide was right out &amp; you could almost walk across to Paris Plage, but a fast running little channel blocked things unless you wanted to swim it, the ground around is just alive with cockles &amp; crabs.
