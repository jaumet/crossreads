 18 Rain chased us in off the foc'stle last night, & by gee it did rattle down. Beautifully cool this m'ng but fairly rough sea. Went before the Quack today, & was asked quite seriously if I could see out of the left eye. And he didn't half like it when I told him it was a "glassy". Just finished one of Haggards novels. Have read two good books "Lytton", & "Dickens" works, but am afraid they're aren't too many others about . Overhauled A full
