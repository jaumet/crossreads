 "My Regiment" &amp; "Action Front" 
 13th  We went out to Hampton Court &amp; saw over the Palace and grounds, the  Houseboats etc.  The 180 years old grape Vine. We saw the place where Henry VIII courted Anne Boleyn  The marvellous collection of  paintings, the King &amp; Queens Bed &amp; Reception rooms. The Court is now devoted as apartments of the wives of notable men, granted rent free
