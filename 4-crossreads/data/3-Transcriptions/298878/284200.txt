<p>on hearing his name called, had to step forward, and be introduced to Neptune.  Dr Seaweed (physician to the Court), then felt his pulse, and if necessary took his temperature by inserting a thermometer in the throat, a pill was then forcibly inserted, and Neptune pronounced sentence.  This was usually a shave and a tubbing as each man was sentenced one of the barbers assistants stepped forward, and liberally washed the candidates face with a whitewash brush and clean water, it was then necessary to walk forward across a bridging arrangement to the edge of the canvas tank, step over a wire, and take a seat facing the audience with his back to the tank, simply balanced by the seat and legs; another of the barbers assistants then painted the face with blacklead &amp; tallow</p>
