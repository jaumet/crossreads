We got back at 4.P.M. & we went to tea at a nice little restaurant where they catered only for a few regular customers, they were very nice to us however & the meals were well served by a little demoiselle named Cecille - We went to the "Nouveau cirque" Rue  Rivo  S  t   Honore in the evening it is a permanent circus in a big building. it was pretty good - 
   
 Jan 24  th    We got up late in the morning & had breakfast at the hotel - we had dinner at Cecille's joint & in the afternoon went to Jardins des plantes. Gardens & zoo in one  then to the Bastille & went through Cathedral de Notre Dame & saw Palace de Justice - We had tea at the leave club & in the evening walked around the city - 
   
 Jan 25  th    We went along to the Louvre in the morning, but the Musee did'nt open till 1.P.M. so we went back & had dinner at Cecille's. we marked time in the afternoon & in the evening went to Casino de Paris & saw Gaby Desly & Harry Pilcer, in Suzette amongst other things. it was beautiful. stage effects were beautifully managed
