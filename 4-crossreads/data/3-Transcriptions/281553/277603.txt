<p>May 17th<br/>Physical drill at 9 <strike>p</strike> am.  Medical inspection at 10. and lecture on boat drill  Boat drill at 2 Pm. given positions only in case of alarm. games from 3 till 4 pm.  After tea singing and writing.  Bed 8.30 pm.  Weather still showery, wind &amp; sea steadier but still cold, full speed again, <strike>W.S.W.</strike> direction WNW 195 Miles.</p>
