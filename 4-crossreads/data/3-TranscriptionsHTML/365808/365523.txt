<p>BASE RECORDS OFFICE<br/>DEPARTMENT OF DEFENCE<br/>MELBOURNE, 6 th August, 1917.</p>
<p>Dear Sir,<br/>I am in receipt of cable advice to the effect Lieutenant K. A. Ferguson, 4 th Divisional Headquarters is returning to Australia <strike>and is due in about</strike> When the transport is nearing Australia a list of names will be published in the Press.<br/>It is regretted that the movements or the name of the transport cannot be disclosed. Beyond this no further information can be supplied.<br/>It is to be noted that owing to possible mutilations in the cabled advice and other causes this notice may not be correct pending verification from the roll on arrival of the Troopship.<br/>Yours faithfully.<br/>J.M. LEAN, Major,<br/>Officer in Charge, Base Records</p>
