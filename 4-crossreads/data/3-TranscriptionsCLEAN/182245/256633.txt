1917 APRIL
28 SATURDAY
Fine & beautifully warm & sunny
Cleaning harness, horses, 
stables ect ready for Capt
Campbell to inspect. He
said all were good though
not up to inspection standard
Mine was very good. Had a 
half holiday in afternoon & I
lay in sun & wrote letters
The 26th Bat played 107th Bat
football & beat them 6 - 0.
