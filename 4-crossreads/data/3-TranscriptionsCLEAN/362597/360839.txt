
Ward last night & it was very good. All the officers & nurses were present & some of the latter are very nice.
Sunday 26.12.15 (4)Church Parade in the concert Room. Everybody standing. Few hymns & prayers C.E. style, R.C's went somewhere else. Received a wireless from the Kind & Queen with Xmas greetings.
Sea still smooth & we are being fed well & do nothing but lie down & read.
Monday Boxing Day (5)Usual routine till 2 pm when we held sports. Ran 2nd in Potato race, 2nd in Carry Your Chum & was unable to run in final of Wheel-barrow race. Ellis [Henry Nicholl's 22 year old brother who enlisted at the same time] & Jimmy McGowen's son got 2nd.
