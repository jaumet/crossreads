 12 &ndash; from Archie S. Skirving since I arrived here &ndash; one from Rosamond &ndash; one from Father one from Constance one from Mrs Man &amp; one from Major Man who is invalided from the firing line to Alexandria &ndash; one from Aunt Maria. These letters have been with the view &amp; a few books &ndash; chiefly the Psalms in Modern Readers edition. My only comfort &amp; help. I must go back on duty now.
