To-day has been a great one amongst the French people, all the women &amp; girls have been out dressed in their best &amp; the little girls were all dressed in white just like a bride going to her wedding &amp; the small boys were all dressed in black suits like a man &amp; wore white ribbons and bows on their arm &amp; bowler hats on their heads, it must be some religious ceremony. Mr Hughes &amp; Fisher General Birdwood &amp; staff reviewed the Brigade to-day &amp; we also had Battalion Sports. So the boys have had a pretty lively day.
