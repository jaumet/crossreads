<p>Spt 15 night. &ndash;<br/>"What is Nature but "The living mantle of God"? Thus speaks Carlyle in his "Sartor Resartus".<br/>Splendid Concert tonite by the Stewards of the boat, - absolutely the best yet on the old boat. Its really a farewell to the boys several of whom get off at Fremantle tomorrow or the day after. The WA boys were paid &pound; 1 each today, so they are about the only financial chaps amongst us.</p>
