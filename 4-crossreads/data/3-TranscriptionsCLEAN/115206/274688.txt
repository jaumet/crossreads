  
 April 1918 Sunday 28 
 Bussy - la - Daours.    D.R. Weather wet throughout day. Rode across to La Neuville as guide to a new runner. Met wagon lines in new position out from Pont Noyette. Fritz shelling village with gas. Rode up to W.L. with wire and cigarettes, in the evening. Fairly heavy shelling in the region of Viller Bettnovino, numerous tanks passed through . Met Charlie.
