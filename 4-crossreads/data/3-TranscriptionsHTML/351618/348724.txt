<p>Monday 3rd Jan 1916<br/>It was about 9 oclock when Transport came for the stores at Tel-el-Kebir Station where our camp was situated. The place is on the Suez Line about 60 miles from Cairo &ndash; Quiet &amp; a lovely place. I arrived where our headquarters was situated and everyone was pleased to see that the records etc were safe etc.</p>
<p>It rained to-day - very welcome after the windy sandy blowey day. We are back once more amidst the sand &amp; desert etc.</p>
