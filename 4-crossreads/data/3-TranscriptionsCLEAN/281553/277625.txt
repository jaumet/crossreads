
Sunday 30thFinal alterations to Canberra Redoubt  thence to Lithgow Post.  Coy shifted camp N.W. some 300 yds. into deep hollow nearer Canal.
Monday 31stSiting different position for Lithgow Redoubt all day.  Very cold wind.
February commenced in check covered Book.
DiaryMay 11th 1915 - Jan. 31st /16
