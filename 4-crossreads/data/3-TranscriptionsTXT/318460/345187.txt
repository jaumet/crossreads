
Saturday 20/1/17freezing hard ground & Snow like ironhaving good spell these two days. The French are Pouring it in on our right. have been all day.Pay 1-19-9 19-13-0Viv still in hospital.Sent letters to Nell. Maude & Will.expect to get away soon for London 31 went to day.fearfully cold & snow everywhere.On duty till 8 P.M.had some Rolled oats today Bought it & made it.
Sunday 21/1/17still frost bound & Bitterly cold how they stand it in the Trenches, God knows, only an OvercoatViv being sent to a Hospital today very weak best thing for him.Pay 2-0-9 19-14-0.
Monday 22/1/17still frost bound & cold.Viv still in Rest Hospital. Moving tomorrow they say.Livingstone in one of his usual stinking tempers.Pay 2.1-9. 19-15-0Wrote to Carol & Jack, Syd, & Mrs Parsons.Viv gone to Hospital looking very bad.Name Taken for Blighty go this week hip-hip.2 P.M. snowing thick.
Tuesday 23/1/17very heavy frost last night, country still white & the coldest yet.Went about 3 miles to see Viv & take him his things found he had been sent on, dont know how he will get them now should have taken them with him.Going blighty on the 28thnot moving todaymore Reinforcements coming in must be a stunt on soon.Pay. 2-2-9. 19.16.01 P.M. The water Taps are still frozen, cant get any water
