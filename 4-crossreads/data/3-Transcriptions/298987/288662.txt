<p>Sunday July 1st<br/>Church parade in Morning &amp; Song Service afternoon &amp; evening. What an easy time Chaplins have About &frac12; an hours service on Sunday &amp; nothing every other day. YM gave <strike>Monday</strike> out lollies at tea time</p>
<p>Monday 2<br/>The finest day since we left home &amp; the sea is a most beautifull shade of blue different to any thing I have ever seen. Washed my clothes</p>
<p>Thursday 3<br/>A wireless that a raider is out no lights tonight as a precaution We are not takeing this seriously as it is probably only to get us used to things when we reach the danger zone<br/>A Court Martial today. 96 days for disobaying  an order</p>
