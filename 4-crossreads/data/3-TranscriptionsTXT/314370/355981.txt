 February, 1916 Sunday 13 Write letter home in the forenoon.  It is so long since I have heard from mum that I find it hard to put anything into letters. After dinner take dad to the Zoo, &amp; see the new wonders of a hippo &amp; several splendid Giraffes. Have dinner in Cairo &amp; return at 8.30 pm 
 Monday 14 Peg away at the care &amp; cleaning subject; the men a very keen to learn. Have great hopes for them.
