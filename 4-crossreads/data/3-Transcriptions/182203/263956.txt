We    <strike>we</strike> were nearly 4 hours at that place, the coast is very beautiful  &amp;  small islands all over the place. We are travelling very slowly &amp; have had a  <strike>Piolet</strike>  [censored] he leaves us a [censored] At night we anchor, because it is not safe travelling then, so you see we are well looked after.<br/>We have Physical drill at 7.30 a.m. it is great &amp; very funny, sorry had not tennis shoes but will get a pair at [censored]  We have 5 men each to teach bandaging to &amp;  4 of mine are Victorians the other a Queenslander. They are very interested in their work. We have also started hospital duty but only do 4 hours at a time. I go on at 8 p.m. &amp; come off at 12. mid.night  for my first hospital duty. Then 4 hrs each day this week. Our Matron &amp;  Major are very charming &amp; trying to make the trip as enjoyable as possible. To-night we are to have a concert but I shall be on duty.
