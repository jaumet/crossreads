<p>March 1918<br/>Tuesday 5</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Had an S.O.S. call at 2.7 am. Fritz put his barrage down for a fairly long stretch of time.<br/>Had the 4-6 and left for F.O.P. at 6.30 with Mr Kerr.<br/>Weather dull, with icy winds from W.S.W.<br/>Spent a chilly morning.<br/>Mr May the news officer from D.A.C.arrived out at O.P. with Charlie.<br/>Kerr although looking very seedy, registered the Lens lines Code F.M. and Square FM for the Officers benefit.<br/>Let the carrier pigeons go at 5 to 4.</p>
