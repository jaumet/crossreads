<p>Thursday 13 June<br/>Rifle inspection by Capt Buckland. Satisfactory.</p>
<p>With Spr Hewett told off to go to Corps. Field Punishment Compound near [Bourlainville?]. Went there with good ride picked up Dr. Huston &amp; escorted him back to Orderly Room. Very pleasant ride. Xmas book fixed in pay book.</p>
