<p>They also told us this morning that we would be leaving for Etaples most probably tomorrow &amp; the sooner the better Camped in the same tent as I are 2 Bantam Sergts from the battalion we relieved when we first went into the trenches at Fleurbaix &amp; Levantie</p>
<p>15th Woke up this morning to find it raining like blazes don't know whats come over the country at all, in the last two days we have had 2 snow storms, 3 heavy frosts, 2 fine</p>
