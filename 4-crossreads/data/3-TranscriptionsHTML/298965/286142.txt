The harbour here is a fair size [indecipherable] there were a lot of [indecipherable] transports &amp; cruisers &amp; ships of all descriptions both French &amp; English [indecipherable] inside the entrance. <p>March 19th.<br/>The whole Battalion was beginning to get Lousy &amp; they were marched down to a creek to wash their clothes &amp; rub kerosene over themselves I was mess orderlie &amp; had a day off as I washed my things on St Patrick's day.</p>
