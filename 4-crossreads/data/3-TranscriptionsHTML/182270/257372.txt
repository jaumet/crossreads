<p>SATURDAY, MARCH 1, 1919.</p>
<p>1</p>
<p>Rain &amp; misty all day. We lay</p>
<p>alongside the wharf till 4.30 &amp; then</p>
<p>pulled out. It was very cold and</p>
<p>choppy outside. I was one of the</p>
<p>officers of the guard for twenty</p>
<p>four hours.</p>
<p>Capt. Kane of the Imperial</p>
<p>Army came into my cabin.</p>
