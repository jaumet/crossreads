 STEENVOORDE Friday 19 I am lousey these billets are alive with vermin, just had a chat, caught fifty five Saturday 20 Good things come to those who wait a good job offered me on the Divisional staff but the O.C (Mj.H) wouldent hear of it Sunday 21 Heavy Artillery fire on the front evidently a barage being sent over prior to a big attack, it makes me sigh A bright blue Sunday "Ah" I wish it would end for I am sick homesick. AUSTRALIA
