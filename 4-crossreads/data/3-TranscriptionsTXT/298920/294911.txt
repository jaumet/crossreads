
As she steamed away the Purser and 4th Mate each took a snapshot of her.  In half an hour she was below the horizon, out of sight.  Now that we are getting into tropical regions darkness descends early.  As soon as the sun sets the sky begins to dim, and by 7 oclock night begins properly.  This however is "Sunset region", and tonights sunset was a very fair sample.Sunday 22nd August.  When the 4th Mate called me this morning he informed me that land was in sight.
