 19 September 1918 Thursday (X to X crossed out) Offensive started- Cables will tell you- a dog belonging to some Unit has made friends here-  I expect my dogs I left  at Dungalear  are dead or too old to work now   Am glad Day has her spaniel .   As we may move any day &amp; I never like to carry Diary Ill post tomorrow. X Don't worry about me your prayers have carried me through so far I feel I'll come through alright &amp; it won't be so very long now Mother dear, before I bring my wife &amp; Baby home to you-X
