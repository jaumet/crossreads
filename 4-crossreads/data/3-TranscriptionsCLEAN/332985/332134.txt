 26-4-1917 Battery out of action, with the exception of 2 guns.  Going back into action tomorrow night.  Ammunition going up to position.  A big stunt coming off.  4 casualties in 101st Bty. and 2 in 1st Bty. 
 27-4-1917, Fremicourt Cloudy and cold.  Great preparations being made for the offensive.  Hundreds of guns going into position.  Very busy corner, near our wagon lines.  Tons of shells in dump, heavy stuff.  Roads alive with motor traffic, Bombardment just started 7.30 p.m.
