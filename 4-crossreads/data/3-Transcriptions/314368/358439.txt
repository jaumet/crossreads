We had our first examination last Friday and I passed quite easily,  getting 72 per cent in marks. Of course the Exams' don't count much at least the written ones don't, it is the field work that counts most, and as I have had a good deal of practice at that, I haven't much to learn.  They are fairly strict about  leave here at the College, we only get about 7 days in three months, so we don't get far away from Oxford. The city itself</p>
