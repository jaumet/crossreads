<p>Sunday 4th to Tuesday 13th<br/>Had several more casualty lists through.  The 6th were very light 4 killed &amp; 5 wounded.  Corpl Suffolk was wounded again &amp; G Warren &amp; Prendergast who were in my troop when I had "C" were both killed.  Good news of the advance has been coming in continuously &amp; I believe right through our losses have been small.  We are making preparations to remove from here to Cairo &amp; everyone is very sorry especially the chaps who have made a wide circle of friends.  They don't like leaving them.  Cairo will be very strange for most.  Have been out several times.  Last Sunday Long &amp; I had quite a round of visits &amp; had quite an enjoyable time.  Was up at the hospital a couple of times to see Sister Barrow.  Last Friday I got some cards &amp; sent them to all my friends.  Mail was going to</p>
