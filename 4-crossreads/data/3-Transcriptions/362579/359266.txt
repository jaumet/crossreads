<p>&nbsp;<br/>Think it is best to address my letters as above &ndash; One never knows when a move shall come   &amp;  Miss C. should know of our movements. We are all very happy with our own Mess-room. It is so homely   &amp;  we are all so much freerer. The foot [food] is excellent   &amp;  have company so far for Morning-tea (11 a.m.)   &amp;  Afternoon-tea (4 p.m.). Our M.O's are like a lot of kiddies, such a great crowd!</p>
