We expected to move tomorrow but it has been put back for 24 hours owing to the 13th Brigade having gone back in close support to a contemplated advance by the Canadians &ndash; Tonight is Ricks Joy &ndash; Wet blankets &amp; a bitterly cold night &ndash; I expect the same trouble again tonight as last viz. owing to the terrific gunfire our horses are terrified &amp; all broke the lines &amp; of course stampeded. This meant us all going after them at 1 am in a cold rain after them. Such is life. 
 6.9.16 Wednesday - 7.9.16  Thursday 
 Both nights &amp; days have been something vile &amp; which will be remembered by me for some time to come. It proves to be further developments of the new Somme offensive &amp; I can tell you all previous fighting which was severe enough has been surpassed. We have done exceptionally well having taken several villages &amp; lots of prisoners &amp; guns.
