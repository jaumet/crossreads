<p>afraid of <strike>a little</strike> heat. The hotels are very large &amp; airy &amp; the services are all good. The tram service is not an extensive one so far as I could judge but it serves all that is required. The country round about is very pretty &amp; cool. It is well irrigated. The main streets of Colombo are wide and very clean. The natives are all exceedingly respectful to the white man &amp; are not afraid of work. They are extremely well built as a rule: lithe &amp; muscular. They wear as few clothes</p>
