The old lady here told me that she recieved 1,400 Francs for two cows<br/>18-4-1916<br/>Still raining, cold and windy, I don't like this country always raining 13 miles from the firing line<br/>19-4-1916<br/>Raining, cold, No excitement, only horse exercise, gun laying and fuze setting<br/>20-4-1916<br/>Continue raining, and very cold 14 men and three sgts crimed for overstaying leave.
