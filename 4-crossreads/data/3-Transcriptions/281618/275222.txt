<p>16. Proceeds to empty the garrison canteen &amp; gets beastly drunk in the process. After the canteen is closed against him roams round making unseemly noises &amp; using language , keeps the garrison awake all night with his howling.<br/>17. Gets out early in the morning &amp; raids the garrison vegetable gardens, lemon trees, paw paws, &amp; cocoa- nuts.<br/>18. The paw paws not being ripe , cuts up many in sampling &amp; pulls off many more to fling at the monkeys. Cuts down a few bunches of green bananas; Also a few cocoa nut palms, in order to see if the young nuts are 'nearly ripe' <br/>19. Finding that the only fruit ripe comes from up the country, proceeds to look for it, gets lost, &amp; is found again by pitying niggers.<br/>20. Pokes his nose into the married quarters &amp; makes a damn nuisance of himself. Is ejected by nigger Guards &amp; told to behave himself.<br/>21. Steals small "curios" such as items of kit, helmets' etc from the men of the garrison &ndash; ie (from their huts where they have made him welcome) and remarks that "the nigger troops dont seem to be afraid of losing their things &ndash; the way they keep them where anyone can get them if he wants to!"</p>
