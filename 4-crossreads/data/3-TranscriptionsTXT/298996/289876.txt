  Thursday  9.4.15 Friday Hot again today not a breath of air the sea is just like a sheet of Glass. A nice cool breeze strung up   to  early night which made things a little cooler. About 9 Oclock we passed a Mail Boat or it passed used it was a real sight to see same steaming along.
