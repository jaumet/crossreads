February   1918
3 SUN  Fine day.
Packing wagons ready for
moving. drew 4 guns. Orders
out re moving on Tuesday.
Writing at night.
4 MON  Posted letter to Amy 
Right Section moved off in aft
relieved by 113th Bty. Preparing
to Move out tomorrow. Rec.
letter from Tense & letter, photo & card
from Tom. U.S.A.
5 TUES  Moved off 1.P.M. went
on bicycle. called at Mme Duean's
etc. had a good aft. Au: Vieu Berquin
4.P.M. Went into Au Progres at 
night. Had a bonne time. called
at Mme Larinets' for J.
6 WED  Home 6.30. Fixing up
billet, Stables scattered - also
billets. fine day. 4 Passes
for Paris. Lively night
with boys.
