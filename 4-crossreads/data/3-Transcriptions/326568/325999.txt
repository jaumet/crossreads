<p>February 1919<br/>16th.   Leave Paris at 7.30 per Brussels express via Amiens Arras, St Pol &amp; Lille, arriving Brussels at 12.15 in night.<br/>17th.  Morning at Galleries in Brussels &amp; leave per 2 p.m. for Charleroi, arriving at unit at 8.30 p.m.<br/>18th.  Back at Biesmes to find I had missed a draft for Australia which left on 12th.  However am well up on the next, which rumour has will leave on 22nd.</p>
