<p>underneath &amp; greatcoat over me, Elsie's balaclava cap being also called into use for the first time. A cold night.<br/>Next morning, Thursday 27th July, had a shave &amp; loafed about. Once I was awakened by someone who turned out to be Jocelyn : he had dropped in just for a minute or two to see how I was. Fairly late in the afternoon we received orders to move &amp; over dusty roads marched back to the wood near Warloy-Baillon where Rid had camped. I was detailed in charge of rear-guard &amp; so rode in a motor-lorry all the way, for which I was thankful.<br/>On Friday we moved to La Vicogne &amp; next day to Bonneville, thence coming into our present rest billets in Pernois.</p>
