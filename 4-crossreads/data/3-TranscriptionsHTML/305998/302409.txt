<p>10/Mr. Henley M.L.A. of Burwood came over from Alex. with gifts from Aust: he issued them personaly &amp; every man got something. He then gave us a little lecture &amp; I can say the boys gave him some rousing cheers in not forgetting us so far out in the desert.</p>
<p>We are all busy in exchanging letters &amp; notes that were enclosed in the parcels &amp; writing thanking the senders.</p>
<p>11/Roumour  on foot that we are leaving for Alexandria. Received letters from Eng: &amp; Aust. Went down to Dorset Yeomanry to look</p>
