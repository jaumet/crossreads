<p>Left Barrow with H.M.S. Adamant at 10 p.m. on the 10th February 1914<br/>Met with very bad weather and put into Holyhead on the 11th<br/>Left again on the 12th had to put into Fishguard till the weather had moderated<br/>Left Fishguard at 9,a.m on the 15th arrived Portsmouth on the 17th went alongside Fort Block House to make preparations for Australia giving ten days leave<br/>Left Portsmouth at 8,30 a.m on the 2nd of March with A.E.1 and H.M.S. Eclipse as Escort, lost a Propeller Blade going through the Bay of Biscay had to be taken in tow by Escort arrived at Gibraltar on the 6th at 2 p.m<br/>Went into Dry Dock and replaced damaged Propeller on the 9th came out of Dock and left same night at 11,45 p.m<br/>Arrived at Malta on the 13th<br/>Left Malta on the 14th arrived at Port Said on the 20th<br/>Left Port Said on the 23rd lost another Propeller Blade just before arrival at Aden, arriving on the 29th<br/>Replaced damaged Propeller on the 1st April by Divers and left the same day<br/>Arrived at Colombo on the 9th turned over to H.M.S. Yarmouth as Escort</p>
