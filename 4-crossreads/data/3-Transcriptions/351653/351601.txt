<p>Egypt<br/>13.9.1916</p>
<p>Mrs Robertson</p>
<p>Dear Madam</p>
<p>Your welcome letter to hand the mail before last very pleasing.  All were well as this leaves me at present.</p>
<p>I am very pleased you are haveing such a good year as it is wanted badly.  We never see a bit of green grass about hear.  I have not seen a decent bit of green grass this last 4 months.  I had a letter From Tom last mail, he was telling me he had his old job Back again, he said he liked it well.</p>
