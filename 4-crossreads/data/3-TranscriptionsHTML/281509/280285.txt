<p>3 March 1916<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Much as usual.  Bob &amp; Tony go down to A.M.C. Comforts Committee &amp; get 2 bags of clothing, comprising pair sand-shoes, 2 shirts, 2 undervests, handkerchiefs &amp; fly veils.<br/>I go down by 2 pm train, fool round, go to pictures: home again 9.25 train</p>
