
There are very few cows in the island, goats supplying the milk.  It is risky however to drink this milk boiled or unboiled.  Meditteranean or Malta fever has been traced to it.  The goats are allowed to ramble along the streets & the owner calls one up to the customer's door & milks it into the basin.  I believe such customer has his own particular goat.
