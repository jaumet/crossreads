 17 August 1918 Saturday Nothing of special import. Beautiful weather &amp; everything going well. Expect to reach the Cape about Monday. A little war news but nothing of great importance We are making a little progress. 
 1918 August 18 Sunday 12 After Trinity 
 Nothing exciting happening. Usual Church Services in morning &amp; "sing song" Service at night &amp; afterward a discussion of ways &amp; means of getting a living after returning home. O.C. Major Ross prisided &amp; a few legislators on "would be s " spoke.
