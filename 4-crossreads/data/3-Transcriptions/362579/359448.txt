<p>Met my friend at Restaurant arranged  &amp;  four of us had a jolly dinner together, then to see the 13th Chair, a rather weird play but awfully well acted. We all enjoyed it immensely but had a bit of trouble getting back. Could not get a taxi, so took tube to Paddington   &amp;  it was crowded which made me miss the 11.10. p.m. train but caught the 11.45. p.m. My friends stayed with me until the train left   &amp;  I returned to Hospital quite safely but jolly tired.</p>
