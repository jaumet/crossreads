<p>23/10/16<br/>Went per motor lorry to a position about 8 miles from Fricourt and marched to that place.</p>
<p>24/10/16<br/>Camped in tents and [bivouacs?] about a mile from Fricourt last night and moved on to Hametz Woods today.</p>
<p>We expect to go into the line in a few days.</p>
<p>25/10/16<br/>We are camped in all kinds of rough biveys here.</p>
