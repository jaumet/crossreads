
<p>November&nbsp;&nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>3 SUN&nbsp; Dull day. Went up</p>
<p>the village at night.</p>
<p>Evening after</p>
<p>Rumours out re going into</p>
<p>action shortly. Quiet</p>
<p>4 MON&nbsp; Fine day. Preparing for</p>
<p>the revue. New pay books</p>
<p>issued. Right half bty dinner</p>
<p>at night. Tr&egrave;s bon evening</p>
<p>Out with J.</p>
<p>5 TUES&nbsp; Raining heavy. Review</p>
<p>cancelled. 4 men went on leave</p>
<p>Banthrop arr. back as Lt in AIF</p>
<p>Educ. scheme. Went up village</p>
<p>at night.</p>
<p>6 WED&nbsp; Raining heavy all day.</p>
<p>10. men went on leave. Caf&eacute;s</p>
<p>started 12-2. &amp; 6 to 8 PM. Went</p>
<p>up village at night.</p>
