We had the afternoon off but the Colonel has cut out all sports & concerts so I finished off nearly all my letters. The Boys held a bit of a concert on the fore-deck & two of our Captains & the Major sang a song each. A dirty rub to the Colonel that was.
I have to use my balaclava cap to sleep on the deck now it is so cold. We passed the "Karoola" this night bound for Australia again.
