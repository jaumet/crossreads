<p>No 4s medical panniers, chequed &amp; handed out in readiness for immediate use.  Infantry  camping near by, all out in the trenches ready for an attack. <br/>Received parcel from home.</p>
<p>Thursday 4/5/16<br/>All rush &amp; bustle.  Bearers rushed out towards the trenches, about 1&frac12; miles out from here, to construct a dug-out, to act as a dressing station, if the expected attack eventuates.  Field Medicine, &amp; Surgical panniers also two Sand Ambulance Carts, hurried out here by a number of the transport men who are to remain</p>
