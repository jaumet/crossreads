<p>You asked me in one of your letters some time back what were my plans in regard to a profession after the war - well my dear I am seriously thinking of joining the Indian army<br/>- which of course means living in India after this strife is over. I would go in as a Lieut &amp; get my Captaincy in about 2 years - the pay until you attain captains rank is not too good somewhere about &pound;25-30 a month - I have written Dave to find out how much ready cash<br/>1 have in hand &amp; what income per year I can depend on from Bexhill. The life in India as an officer is ideal with fine chances of promotion - several of our fellows have already transferred - I havnt</p>
