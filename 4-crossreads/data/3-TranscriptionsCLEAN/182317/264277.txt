
Department of the Attorney General and of JusticeSydney.14th March, 1941.GAC.WPA 41-1054 AGB.Telephone B06 Ext. 868.Dear Sir, I am directed by the Attorney General to inform you that His Excellency the Governor, with the advice of the Executive Council, has been pleased to appoint you to sit and act as a Judge of the Supreme Court of New South Wales, in all jurisdictions of the said Court, from the 17th March, 1941 to the 30th April, 1941, both dates inclusive. You will be paid salary at the rate of   2,600 per annum during  the period mentioned.Your Commission and Rail Pass are forwarded herewith.Yours faithfully,  M.C. Nott Under Secretary of Justice.K. A. Ferguson, Esquire, Barrister-at-Law, 142 Phillip Street, Sydney.
