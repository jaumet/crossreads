<p>APRIL 1917</p>
<p>9 MONDAY</p>
<p>Raining in the morning &amp;</p>
<p>blowing very cold in</p>
<p>evening. Cleaning harness</p>
<p>act all morning&nbsp;<sub>^</sub><sup>two teams</sup>&nbsp;&amp; went</p>
<p>up to the battery in evening</p>
<p>We have always to take</p>
<p>six horse teams now, so</p>
<p>one man does not get a</p>
<p>spell every now &amp; then.</p>
<p>We had a progress &quot;500&quot; evening</p>
<p>&amp; had some good games. Bill</p>
<p>Constable &amp; self were a tie</p>
<p>for first place with two other</p>
<p>couples &amp; got second place</p>
<p>in a show poker hand for</p>
<p>places.</p>
