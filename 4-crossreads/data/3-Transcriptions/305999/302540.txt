<p>We had 3 bombardments on the enemy using 60 powanders bombs and artillery  it was pritty hot and also a bomb raid on one of his strong points</p>
<p>26/9/16<br/>at early morning we where releaved from the firing line by the 2 XX Bt we marched a good way behind the line then we entrained we traviled for &frac14;  of an hour when we disembarked and had 4 miles march to huts near the town of Ouderdom where we remained 13 days</p>
