<p>Nevada.  Country all barren looking mountains.  A good many villages to be seen.</p>
<p>27th June Sunday 1915</p>
<p>At midnight last night we passed Gib.  It was a bright moonlight night so we were able to see it clearly.  A searchlight shone on us several times.  The Spanish coast was quite near &amp; Africa also showed up plainly.  It has grown much cooler.  Passed very close to Cape St. Vincent.</p>
<p>28th June Monday 1915</p>
<p>Passed the Finisterre light at 9 p.m. &amp; entered the Bay.  Very calm.</p>
<p>29th June Tuesday</p>
<p>Buried a sailor died of dysentery.</p>
<p>30th June Wed.</p>
