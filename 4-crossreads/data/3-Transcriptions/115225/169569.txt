
<p>looked perfectly natural &amp; created quite a comotion when we went through a village &amp; every one stared at it &amp; pointed &nbsp;I have never seen such a unique decoration on any lorry. as the lorry travelled over the road the wings flapped or quivered slightly which made it look more life like than ever - under the bird I had&nbsp;the inscription &quot;ON LES AURA&quot; which being interpreted means. &quot;we will get them&quot;, referring to the Germans of course. It is an historic phrase from General Petain at Verdun &amp; is used for advertising the French war loan (Comprunt)</p>
