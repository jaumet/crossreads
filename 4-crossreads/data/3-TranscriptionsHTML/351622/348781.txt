<p>14 February 1916 Mon<br/>A. Markey 1260<br/>D. Comp<br/>30 Battalion<br/>Tel el Kebir<br/>Written by Arnold in train from Cairo to Ismailia 16.2.16.</p>
<p>Herbie closes the day by referring to his boyhood days relating his tales of love up against the Poor House wall &ndash; ninety one [indecipherable]</p>
<p>15 February 1916 Tues<br/>Paraded orderly room before Colonel Phipps for leave to Cairo which was granted. Swimming parade to the canal before dinner. Zulu is put in command of our squad &amp; cant give orders for laughing. All hands indulge also. Received two letters &amp; photo from Thelmas Smith. Mail Steamer passes along canal looks as though she is homeward bound.</p>
