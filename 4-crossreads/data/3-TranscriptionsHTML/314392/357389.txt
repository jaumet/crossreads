<p>and finding a rather decent one put a sign up "mined'. This was done to keep others away and I guess it succeeded too. When we joined him he told us &ndash; Jim Vaulawick, Bill Reidy, F Baker and myself what he had dome to save the dug-out for us and we all walked down and dumped our kits. At nightfall Vau found a wire running along the wall and said Gee! we'd look funny if this place was mined.<br/> This put the "wind up" all of us and one by one we found some excuse to beat it. I went in with the cook under a very heavy tarpaulin.</p>
