<p>early days, but after having dug wells we struck we struck beautiful  &amp; clear water which was always plentiful.  We had an excellent view from the mountain tops of all the surrounding   <strike>tops</strike> country  &amp; of the Turkish positions but on account of the open nature of the country we were frequently pepered with Schrapnell.<br/>May 21.
