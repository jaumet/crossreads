 He, by a curious chance, had chosen me as dispenser.  The difficulty of Molloy remained for he had 3 men in view. At last however he consented to take Molloy too so we felt glad. It seems we are to be attached to the 2nd Div. as far as England. We may not leave here with them, but as we have been put on the strength it seems likely.
