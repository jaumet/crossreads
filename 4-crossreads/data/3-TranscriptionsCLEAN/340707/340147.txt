Here as at tram stop if there is a crowd you can always take a ticket thus ensuring your turn in proper order. This is especially useful in the case of trams while this strike continues. 
 At railway I have difficulty in getting a taxi & a YMCA Aussi] comes along & offers me his big motor lorry which takes me first to Windsor & then to hotel & for which I give then 10 f. It would probably have cost me 25 f in a taxi & ie if he would have taken me, for during strikes short runs mean more money. 
 I dine at Rabbie tonight & meet my friends but leave them to look after my baggage. 
 12-6-19 I visit Grand Magasins du Louvre & Samantaine looking for this book & walk along the Grand Boulevard when even in daylight the queens of love & powder are plying their trade. I take [indecipherable] in a modest place & get little for 7.50 & in the afternoon I attend my first lecture, & seem to be the only one who can follow his little jokes
