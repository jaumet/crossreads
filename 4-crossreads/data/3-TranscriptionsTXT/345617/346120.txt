
Juillet 17No 2 Duty Section. Other Sections on Drill & Route March. Showery weather. Nothing of note on our Front. Russians still doing well & French more than holding their own.Had swim in lake off Somme Canal this afternoon.8th Brigade camped in Corbie for the night, & a good many [indecipherable] among them making a general nuisance of themselves.
Juillet 1814th Brigade marched into Corbie today & will camp here tonight. A good many zig zagging about the streets. Nothing fresh to report. We are carrying on with general training.
