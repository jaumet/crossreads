
Liverpool.
8 Jan 19Arrived Liverpool & embarked on Orsova Orient Line. There are about 1300 soldiers, & some soldiers wives & passengers besides about 80 of us sailors. Had a few hours leave in the Pool before we left. Shifted out from wharf in afternoon & anchored out in stream. A Tommy band played us out.
9 Jan 19Heaved anchor & proceeded to sea we are going by Suez Canal route.
10 Jan 19There is a bit of a roll on as we are crossing the Bay of Biscay & good many of the soldiers are sick.
