 24th Tuesday 9-30 Changed Base to relieve 14 Batt., dug ourselves in &amp; all O.K. 1 p.m.  No duty again all day.  1 Platoon in trenches 48 hrs. from 6 p.m. 
 25th Wednesday Stand to Arms 4 a.m. from now on.  Had look into our own trenches.  Carried Despatch to H.Qrs. at No. 2 Outpost C &amp; D Coys front. 
 26th Thursday No work all day.  Swim at Beach 8 p.m., 1st bath since landing.  Mails closed 1 p.m. 
 27th Friday 24 hrs. rations &amp; 200 rounds am.  Moved off from Quarters 3 p.m. to 15th Quarters, waited here during Gun Bombardment, started 3-45 &amp; rifle fire &amp; inf. attack 5 p.m. lasting
