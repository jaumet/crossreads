<p>Tuesday 1 April 1919<br/>Was out at 0330 this morning being April Fools, Reveille blew at that hour.<br/>Sea a good deal rougher.<br/>Hope you can read this &ndash; we now get in to Freemantle by midday Monday 7th Mch., so will soon be home now, Mother dear.<br/>All my love.</p>
