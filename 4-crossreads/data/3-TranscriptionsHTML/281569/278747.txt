<p>25 October, 1916 Wednesday</p>
<p>Feeling very crook &amp; to make matters worse jaundice had broken out, vomiting still bad &amp; pain pretty severe, propped up in bed, the boys called round but were not allowed to see me.</p>
<p>26 October, 1916 Thursday</p>
<p>Feeling slightly better pain easier, had a fair night, wrote two letters, another Australian admitted with a bad foot.</p>
