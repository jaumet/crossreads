<p>Tuesday 27 June 1916<br/>Rostron &amp; I went into Alexandria.  I sent registered book to Sydney.  Bought a coat.<br/>Went to concert on Pier.</p>
<p>Wednesday 28 June 1916<br/>Rostron &amp; I took garry and drove out to Pompie's [Pompey's] Pillar.  Saw ruins of old Roman church 2000 B.C.  Underground tombs, tunnels cut through sandstone.  Pillar made 5 pieces.  Pillar itself granite brought from same quarrie as Pyramids.  Came back.  Had tea Y.M.C.A., went to concert on Mousthapa [possibly Moustapha or Mustafa] Pier.</p>
