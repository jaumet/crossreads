<p>7 November 1915<br/>Sunday<br/>23rd After Trinity</p>
<p>Dad   &amp;  I go into Cairo after dinner   &amp;  have a very pleasant afternoon in the Sgts mess belonging to the Ordnance Dept of Kasr-el-nil Barracks   &amp;   <u>Also</u>  go through the Cairo Museum.</p>
<p>1915 November 8<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Spend most of the day pottering about in Dad's work-shop. having nothing to do in my own Coy.<br/>I have applied to attend a School of instruction in Zeitoun.</p>
