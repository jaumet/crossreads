Arrived at Albert 10 P.M.<br/>20-7-1916<br/>Battery in action, a real hell hole. Everything blown to pieces. Dead men, horses and mules lying about all over the place. The town of Albert absolutely blown to pieces. The R.C.Church is one mass of ruins. The Virgin Mary with child in arms on the church tower, made of some gilded material, is hanging over the street, face down, The steeple is about 120 feet hight. Ones heart bleeds as such sights. I don't suppose,  that there are a dozen houses in town not damaged. Big motor factury laid in ruins. The French people say, when the statue fall the war will end. A G.S.wagon blown to pieces, man and horses killed. 1st and 2nd Battalions of our Infantry cut up last night going to trenches. We relieve B Bty 86th Brigade.
