Wednesday May 30. 1917Cloudy but warm.The Italians continue their great battle on the Corso &and; made further progress in the neighbourhood of Monte Hermada, the mountain fortress which bars the road to Trieste. Strongly fortified trenches were captured, a village near the coast occupied &and; 9.6 in guns in perfect order for recent [indecipherable] were taken. British monitors assisted.The British &and; French have succeeded in bringing down 39 German machines.The British Censor after all the world finding out from German sources, allows the press to state, that Folkestone was the town recently boombed by Hun aircraft. The "Express" reprints H. G.
