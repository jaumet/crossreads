<p>Near Bethlehem 22:5:18<br/>Solomon's Ponds<br/>The above mentioned are three enormous water reservoirs let into the ground each about 100yds x 80yds. Their history I do not know as yet.<br/>We have two new members on the strength now. One is a little pup, name undecided yet, the other is my horse which I got from J. [indecipherable].. backsheesh. Whether they will let me keep it I dont know yet but I see no reason why they should not as I had Bernard with me when here</p>
