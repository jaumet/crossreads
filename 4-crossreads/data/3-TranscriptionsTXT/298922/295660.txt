 Thursday 27 December 1917 
 BACK at Moascar owing to Railway line being washed away by rain have to wait another few days  &amp; could have been with my little wife all this time.   Having dinner with Col.  McLeish  tonight in his MESS. Did I mention seeing STANLEY BRAIM he has improved a lot &amp; is now a Cadet in the Flying Corps &amp; may get his commission, he is not drinking now. Letter from Laura &amp; redirected one from May, please I did not get photo of Jack &amp;  Douglas &amp; I am  a Major not a Captain now for a year.
