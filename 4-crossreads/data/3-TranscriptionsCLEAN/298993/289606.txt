
few battalions of tommy infintary trecked till 9.30 covered about 10 miles camped put down camel line fed camels had tea named for guard not on guard turned in 11PM cold night this place called.Wednesday Mar 21.3.17 stand to 5.30 went to look for fire wood got a little had breakfast went to canteen nothing at canteen to buy sold right out got some tin fish returned to camp had dinner went for water had tea on line guard to night on 4 post 1 till 3 cold nightThursday Mar 22.3.17 stand to 5.15 cold morning had breakfast
