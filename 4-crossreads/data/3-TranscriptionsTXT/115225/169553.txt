 Apl 15  th  &nbsp; We left park at 7.30 A.M. &amp; loaded ammo at Poulainville &amp; took it to corps reserve dump on the  allon  Amiens Contay road  near S t  Gratien . got back to park at 4.30 P.M. &amp; received some letters from home &amp; a parcel. Cold day but no rain &mdash; 
 &nbsp; 
 Apl 16  th  &nbsp; We went to Flesselles amunition dump at 8.15 A.M. &amp; hauled to Corps reserve dump. heavy mist &amp; cold wind all day. We heard that the Germans have captured Bailleul, &amp; Meteren is  the front  no mans land 
 &nbsp; 
 Apl 17  th  &nbsp; We stayed in to-day to work on the lorry - it was a dull rainy day - I lost 15 francs at a two up school in the evening there was a very big school &amp; up for hundreds of francs. 
 &nbsp; 
 Apl 18  th  &nbsp; Five of the lorries are out on supplies every day for the Brigade &amp; when they have finished they return to Amiens &amp; load up with furniture etc &amp; haul for the civies - We got ready for the road. Cold bleak day &amp; some rain.
