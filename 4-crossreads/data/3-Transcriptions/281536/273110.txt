<p>31/7/19 Cape Town<br/>shops are closed today (Saturday) and until Tuesday next for the Peace holidays. One can now see Table Mountain perfectly with its craggy sides partially tree covered. Directly behind the Town Hall embowered in trees are what appear to be the best and most chic local residences. This view is not unlike Australia. About 2 pm came off a barge of niggers and coal and commenced the slow business of coaling by means of handfilling sacks and then hoisting above by winches. The Capt. Expects to sail for Durban before noon tomorrow. On the opposite to the one on which we are being coaled there is a big launch busy pumping water into our tanks. The troops cooped up aboard</p>
