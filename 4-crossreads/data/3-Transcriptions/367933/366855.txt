<p>20.2.17</p>
<p>Lieut N McMillan has very generously forwarded parcel addressed to him on to me which I wish to acknowledge and thank on his and my own behalf. The luxuries these parcels contain are greatly appreciated by us.<br/>Again thanking you I beg to remain<br/>Yours gratefully<br/>E B Thomas. 1st LH Regt.</p>
