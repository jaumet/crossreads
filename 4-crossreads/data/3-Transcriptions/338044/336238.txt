<p>11th August:- Friday<br/>Awoke late &amp; having intended going to the doctor, as I feel right played out, was too late arriving to do so. Found plenty of work awaiting, as the casualties of 19th Batt have been very heavy &amp; I am on this work. (380 wd in 8 days) Manage to pull through the day well &amp; have lunch &amp; tea at Soldier's Home as usual &amp; a walk around town &amp; again seeing the monument of Joan of Arc &amp; site of her execution  Talk at home after our walk around the town &amp; go to bed late  We received 15 francs each for expenses.</p>
