<p>Friday 15th<br/>Work. Some gunners have their cookhouse beside our job &amp; we often get a drink of tea from the old cook. Poor old chap got badly burned today. He had some cordite for lighting his fire &amp; it caught alight. The place was full of flame for a few seconds &amp; he was in it. We also use cordite as kindling &amp; had several bags in the Dugout. We are keeping it outside now. A parcel from Aunt Nima more tucker &amp; a pair of sox</p>
