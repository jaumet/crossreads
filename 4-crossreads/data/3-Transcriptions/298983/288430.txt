<p>Wensday 5th June<br/>Cycled about 14 to Hatford up the River that runs between Harwich &amp; Shotley Took lunch with us &amp; got a boat Rowed a couple of miles this very pretty river Came back to Allens for tea &amp; stoped for Diner</p>
<p>Thursday 6th June<br/>Left Harwich about 10, Bussed to Bayswater quite a long trip from Liverpool St Station &amp; spent a few hours with Aunt S</p>
