  
 [Previous page is library information] July 1917 Monday 2nd I was put on nursing orderly today but I dont care for the job much, it appears to me that we are sent up here to do the Red Cross chaps work a lazier lot of chaps I never seen, they are worse than the shirkers at home & they are all big hefty chaps Tuesday 3rd Fritz paid the town a vist last night but droped no bombs.   I am still carrying on with the nursing.  I took temperatures to day in a sort of a way. Wednesday 4th.   We received a lot of patients from the Baillieul C.C.S. today.   Fritz has been bombing & shelling the Hospital.
