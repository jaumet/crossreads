
<p>18</p>
<p>back to Ypres. Were first in time for the 3rd Oct Stunt. which we had to support. We had to di in six times before we reached our position. Lots of dead lying about. our own and German. Just in front of us is a big hollow where the Germans made the Concrete Blocks for their Pill Boxes and it was full of German dead Great big fellows we could not see a small one among</p>
