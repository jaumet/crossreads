<p>&amp; that was why when 'Skying' in Norway or Switzerland no Englishman was so good as a native in the tremendous leaps taken in that game &ndash; When absolutely necessary an Englishman will take risks but not otherwise &ndash; I said I thought we elder men developed caution in ratio to our responsibility &ndash; especially where life was concerned &ndash; I left him after some more talk &amp; beer &amp; went to the Ward room &amp; played Auction &ndash; Life on a Destroyer is not so good as on a gunboat &ndash; It's very  hot in the rooms &amp; the decks are all iron &ndash; The decks have slats [Sketch] like this all over them to  prevent men slipping &ndash; Feet must be covered from the heat &ndash; men slide over the side &ndash; They picked up their last in 4 minutes D told me, which is very smart &ndash;<br/>By the way I talked about Commissariat to him.  The Navy <u>will be</u> all right &ndash; We manage better than the Army &ndash;</p>
