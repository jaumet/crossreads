 Braithwaite &amp; Bill May are both bonza bites, they swallow floater sinker &amp; all, I can get them going any old time 
 28th. Sunday again &amp; Church services as usual, it has been a lovely day nice &amp; sunny. 
 A big barracouta was caught off the stern of our ship this morning 
 Saw the "Marathon" &amp; a few more ships go out today. 
 Went through the usual inspections today &amp; the ship was pronounced clean, at dusk the pilot came aboard &amp; our flag was hauled down, we are leaving at midnight for Port Melbourne.
