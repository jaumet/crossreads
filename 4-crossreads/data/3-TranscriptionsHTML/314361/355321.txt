The purpose of this work is to hold back the water until it reaches a certain level for flowing into the large canals, which then flows northward &amp; so irrigates miles of fertile land. The Barrage was began by Mohammed Ali in 1835 but unfinished till 1890. At barrage are beautiful &amp; well laid out gardens, &amp; a delightfull place for a picnic.
