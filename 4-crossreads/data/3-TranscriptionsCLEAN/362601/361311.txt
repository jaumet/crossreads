  
 12 December, Friday 
 We see Motor Bike & pay deposit. I eventually buy it on the 15/12/19 
 13 December, Saturday 
 Catch 1.5 train & arrive Wyong 3.35 but no one to meet. They turn up at 4.15 [?] We get Home before 5 & tea. Allie comes over go to mouth for prawns .Just get out of Church before dusk. We have a nice outing.
