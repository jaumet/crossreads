<p>Monday September 4.9.16<br/>Still at moaskar<br/>Tuesday Sep 5.9.16<br/>Still at moakcar I am sick of the place<br/>Wednesday Sep 6.9.16<br/>Still at moaskar. Fixing bayonets by numbers<br/>Thursday Sept 7.9.16 Still at moascar<br/>Friday Sept 8.9.16 Still at moascar<br/>Saturday Sept 9.9.16<br/>left moascar to day and glad to get away nothing but work. Arrived Poly-gon Camp Abbassia midday met Jim Bradbury.<br/>Sunday Sept 10.9.16 Sleeping all day wrote home. A very nice camp.</p>
