<p>What does Conscription mean?<br/>The dictionary tells us that Conscription means "obtaining recruits by compulsion" &amp; this is what we are asked to vote "for or against" next Saturday.  <u>Now what is it that we are asked to decide.</u>  We are asked if we are willing to keep up our A.I.F. of 100,000 men to its full strength or no.  To do that we require 16500 men every month &amp; under the voluntary system at present in vogue we are only securing 6000 men per month.  We have given the voluntary system a fair trial &amp; it has failed;  <strike>so it</strike> so it remains for us to decide whether we shall honour our promise or let the name of A.I.F. be but a memory.
