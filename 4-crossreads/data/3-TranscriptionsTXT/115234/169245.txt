Thirty years ago, I was a favourite at the 'Vic'  A finished actor, not a cuff & collar shooting star stick.  I roused the house to laughter; or called forth the silent tear & I made the Gods vociferiously cheer.  Those were the days; the palmy days, of histrionic art  Without a moments notice I d go on for any part  I do not wish to gas I merely state in self -
