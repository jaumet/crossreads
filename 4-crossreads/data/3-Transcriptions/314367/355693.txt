<p>After a bit No 1 Pay &amp; self left for a walk &ndash; I was guide &amp; took them right round the Gardens &amp; showed them a lot &ndash; No 1 (Nelson) is &ndash; like me &ndash; from Norwich &ndash; Very good hearted &amp; queerly intelligent &ndash; you'd think he's a fool but he's keenly intelligent in a limited range &ndash; He wont try mental excursions &ndash; I handed him a Spectator &ndash; "A bit too much for me he said" &ndash; "You know I don't understand it" "I don't think I'll mind thank you" I'm not given much to heavy reading &amp; so on &ndash; Almost every naval man is the same &ndash; He was interested &ndash; Pay was frankly bored to death &ndash; However we blew in at the Officers mess &amp; had aft tea &amp; sat back &amp; talked &ndash; Why is Camaraderie so much more marked in the navy than the army? No 1 gave an efficient answer &ndash; Because a sailor don't care a dam for anybody or anything &amp; says what he thinks &ndash; Boat form Yarra for me &ndash; Went to the Destroyer &amp; shifted 4 men into their whaler &amp; off I went to the shore -</p>
