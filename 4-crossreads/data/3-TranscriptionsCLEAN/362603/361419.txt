  
 Red Cross letterhead. 5.  The Harbert Girls were the first on the Terrace this year.  The Youngest & Eldest are fat like Dot Nelson & would make the Toboggan fly.  They are all great sports & the two stout girls swim in a mill stream with the water like Ice during the summer months.  In the afternoon, I sat on the mat & talked till 4 pm & then had to run like Billy-Oh for my Train.  I just caught it & having an Hour to spare, I got the Tube to the Strand & had a walk around.
