 1916 February 7 Monday 
 Janet, Bob &amp; self take trip up river. in milk launch.  Beautiful calm morning &amp; a pleasant trip. Side show companions.  Land at Wogamia. have a snack &amp; re-embark for homeward trip take pot - shots at rabbits etc. Expect Tony by mid-day train does not arrive. Bob &amp; self drive out home to Mayfield. New family arrive for. the farm  Franklins. Take Bob to Morisons at night.
