  
 397 New Zealand Hospital Walton on Thames 6.9.15 
 Dearest Joker and Bobbie Many thanks for your cheery letters - I am jolly glad to hear you are enjoying yourselves and have two youngsters to amuse you, although Mr Watson is a host in himself - you are lucky to have such a jolly good sort for an Uncle - The letter from Joker arrived arrived in an awful state, the envelope had come [indecipherable]  All I can make out of the writing outside is "Does this state of envelope suggest anything to you?" - The initials at the bottom I take are Lea's - It suggests very much what happened to me on Sunday - I was asked to a canoing and punting party - I was given a canoe and a girl - all went  well till the girl tried to move her position - The result was disastrous - over we went in to the water - luckily we were close to the bank and
