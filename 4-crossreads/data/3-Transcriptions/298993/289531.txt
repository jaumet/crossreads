<p>Friday July  8.7.16<br/>Revallie 5am Left Menangle 8.30 raining arrived Sydney 10.00 am marched to wharf left Sydney by R.M. S. Mongolia to day at 12PM, a wet day am well saw gussie.<br/>Sunday July 9.7.16<br/>Saw a number of ships am feeling very well. Reading and smoking on hurricane deck<br/>Monday July 10.7.16<br/>Arrived in melbourne have leave to go ashore went into Melbourne had a good time saw cpl Forseythe. had some photos taken a failure wrote home am well</p>
