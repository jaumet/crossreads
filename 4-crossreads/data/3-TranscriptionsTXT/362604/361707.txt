
A cricket team came out from Hurdcott on Independence Day to play our boys & won easily.  They had an International & 2 Interstate men & the Pick of their Camp where our team was only from the Hospital & some C.I men amongst them.  They got 170 & we got 99.  I knew one of the Chaps, George Wilson who went to St. James in Burwood & played with them.  I went to Southampton again on Saturday to try & cycle to Portsmouth.
