<p>Australian Red Cross<br/>Bulford<br/>2/6/18</p>
<p>Dear Mum,</p>
<p>Here is a lovely Sunday afternoon but very hot.  I am staying on Duty till half-past three &amp; then I'll be finished for the day.  We both had a parcel from you this week.  The Jam in mine was O.K. &amp; Ellis' cake lovely.  Do you know that every cake we have received from you has been glorious &amp; fresh as a daisy, no matter how battered the tin.  I've seen dozens of other fellows, Dave Niness included, receive cakes &amp; they have been mildewed right through &amp; not</p>
