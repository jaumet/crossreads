Thou Lovest
Thou may'st vainly, idly seek
feeble words to speak;
 meekly, humbly there
silent hours of prayer
If uncounted wealth were thine ?
the riches of the mine ?
a gift so dear impart
loving heart.
It shall droop wane ere long
the pleasure ? loving throng;
