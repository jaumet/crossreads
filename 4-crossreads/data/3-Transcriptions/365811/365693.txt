<p>29.1.18</p>
<p>My dear Judge,<br/>A mail today brought me your letter of Nov. 30 with the newspaper cuttings of Hughes' treatment at [indecipherable], &amp; C.J. Brennan's letter about mannixism.</p>
<p>Those two cuttings were the only piece of journalistic news of Australian doings that I have had for a long while and were an exceedingly interesting peep into what was happening out with you before the recent referendum was taken.</p>
