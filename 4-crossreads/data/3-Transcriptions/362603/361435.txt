<p>Australian Red Cross letterhead<br/>28.4.17<br/>Bulford<br/>Dear George,<br/>It is answer to Your letter of the 28th Feb. that I pen these few lines.  You were very fortunate, Son, in getting Your chance so Young.  Stick to it &amp; never mind if You do have to work a bit of overtime &amp; have to miss some pleasures.  It will be a great treat for You to get some outdoor life.  Indoor business is up to putty &amp; I would not stay inside 10 minutes if I could get out.  I must write &amp; thank Mr Frost for His interest in You.  I knew Dick Guyot well.  Give Him My regards &amp; tell Him what ever He does to keep out of</p>
