
June 191727th   Whe We have made excelent progress with our pontooning since we came & can now form a bridge in record time.   The 8th & 14th companies have brought their gear down the river & have parked just below us.   A large lagoon just at the back of us affords extra scope for bridging & is being used by them more than the river.   We have been making great efforts for this day & this morning we paraded in "battle order" for inspection.   The whole of the Engineers formed up in fine style on the river bank with all the transport
