 February, 1917. Thursday 1 Did fatigue all morning carrying wood and after doing fatigue in afternoon cleaned up and in evening we had an impromptu concert party headed by Gadjet. Good fun &amp; he is very good entertainer. Heavy strafe on our right started at 7. Days apparently getting warmer. Heavy snow fall early this morning. Cloudy only a little. Major Dowling &amp; Kernot inspected work this morning. Mail in to-day.
