  
 Friday 11 July 1919 Nice & warm again today. Had a bonny cold shower. Do me. Nosed around & got on a quota (59).  They leave somewhere about the 20th. Do me. Wrote a few letters at the Y.M. Usual morning & afternoon tea. 
 Saturday 12 July 1919 Rain all the morning but fine again in the afternoon. Was orderly officer all day but only just saw the camp in the distance usual tea. Played patience nearly all day.
