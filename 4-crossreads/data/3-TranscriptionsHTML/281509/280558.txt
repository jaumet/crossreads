<p>1916 December 1<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Up betimes frost. frozen mud. To usual duties, fairly busy mostly trench feet.  Still the cold.  Secured good tunic from patient. Our search for clothing &amp; boots from patients continue. The military often fails to supply our needs when most wanted.</p>
