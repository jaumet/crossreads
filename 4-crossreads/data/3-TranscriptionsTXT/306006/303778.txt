He came out per car, Hills &amp; Jessie  &amp; 3 other sisters went to Cairo with us &amp; we left them at Opera Square, did some shopping &amp; then Jessie, Hills, Vern, Ben, &amp; I went for a spin to the pyramids, went through one of the tombs, Jessie &amp; Hills had their fortunes told came back to St James for dinner &amp; then to a concert at the Anzac Hostel which was very good. Hill had to be back at 6.30 so we took her back &amp; brought Parsons in her place to dinner, we drove back to Abbassia in gharries; I found out today &amp; Cobb &amp; I arrived back at the Hospital at 9 oclock instead of 10 last night, by mistake; Ben had a pass till 10.30 tonight but it was after eleven when we got back;
