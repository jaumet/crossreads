C Sqd was the advance party &amp; as the Bde started to head for home we scouted out to the north a mile or two;  Mick O'Donnell &amp; Lucas were scouts &amp; decided to have a spell at one point till the troop got closer.  Lucas dismounted &amp; a bullet went mighty close to O'Donnells head;</p>
