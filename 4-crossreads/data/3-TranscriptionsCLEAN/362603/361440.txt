We therefore saw all over the Town.  The Cathedral was great & when Cromwell sacked the Town the Cathedral was the only building left standing.  We were stared at like monsters.  One Girl told us that she thought us to be two of the first Australians to visit the Town.  We saw Ladies underclothing sold in the open Market Place.  All these Towns still have the weekly Market-day in the open square.  It is such a strange custom to us.  We arrived back at Jennie's Home at 6.15 pm after 3 1/2 Hours run.
