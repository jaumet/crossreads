<p>II<br/>that we always have to undertake in the line &ndash; I have a completely new set of Officers except Greene my senior subaltern who was with me before I went away &ndash; The others are Capt F McAdam, Lieuts Smith, Mossman &amp; Bussell.  My old Battery Captain, Syd Walker has got his majority &amp; command of another Battery, while the other old hands are still away &ndash;<br/>Am surprised Col Lucas didn't ring you up before &ndash; I don't hold any great brief for him, and think were I in his place I should try and discharge these little duties as soon as possible -</p>
