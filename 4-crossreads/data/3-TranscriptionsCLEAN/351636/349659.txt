  
 The Centre Soldiers Wives & Mothers 92B Pitt St. August 23rd 1915 
 Dear Mr. Layton We are sending to you an applicant in need of help until she receives her Military Pay - 
 Do you think she will be able to obtain assistance this week? as I am afraid she may be placed in awkard circumstances unless she has some help. 
 Yours sincerely Ethel M. Wallack
