<p>main guard at Raffo  had a good quiet day  no one come near us  had tea 5.P.M  turned in<br/>Thursday Jan. 2.1.19.<br/>turned out 6.30.  had Breakfast  cleaned up  was relieved from guard. 9.A.M.  returned to camp  had dinner. 12.  went to wells  had a Bath  done washing  tea  turned in 8.P.M.<br/>Friday Jan 3.1.19<br/>turned out 6.30  fed up  Breakfast  fell</p>
