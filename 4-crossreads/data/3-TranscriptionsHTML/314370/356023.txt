<p>May, 1916<br/>Sunday 7<br/>Got up late  After Church parade we had dinner &amp; a good sleep. about 5 pm I went into town &amp; had Tea &amp; went to the Ezbekieh Gardens to hear the R.W.F. Band.<br/>Monday 8<br/>Start work again preparing for the C &amp; C exam tomorrow.<br/>Weather still scorching.<br/>Get several letters &amp; answer them</p>
