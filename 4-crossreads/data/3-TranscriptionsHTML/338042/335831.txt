<p>Leave "Elm Bank" (most reluctantly) and take 4 p.m. train from Euston. Slow journey through fine country &ndash; Arrive Birmingham 8.15 p.m. &ndash; Quartered at Y.M.C.A. &ndash; Early to bed, early to rise (?)<br/>Mar.18th<br/>Rehearsal with Orchestra Preparations for Evening &ndash; to a house of 2.000 we open with great success &ndash; Topping the Bill &ndash; Others on programme include Broughton &amp; [indecipherable]  Daisy Taylor,  Empire Opera Singers &ndash; Shaw Daunton Cyclists &ndash; Second House (Two shows) a night</p>
