 4/12/17 Received letters from Ma, Muriel &amp; Uncle Charlie &amp; cards from Ivie, Uncle Harry, Aunt Sara, Aunt Jean, Amanda &amp; a letter from Ivie. Wrote to Ivie &amp; Uncle Charles. Also sent to Uncle Charlie letter authorising my share of Grandma's letter to be sent to Mother &amp; her receipt to do for same. 5/12/17 Wrote to Amanda &amp; sent card etc home. 7/12/17 Wrote home 10/12/17 Wrote Mr Southwell 11/12/17 Recd letter from Muriel. 14/12/17 Last Monday a Sgt Simmonds Bedford Regt came in &amp; he was in capture of Schwaben Redoubt, Thiepval, Sept 22 1916.
