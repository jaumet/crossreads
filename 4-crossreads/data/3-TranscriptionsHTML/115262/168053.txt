<p>Saturday 13 October 1917.</p>
<p>Full dress parade with camels &amp; ourselves in full marching order. Inspection by O.C.</p>
<p>&quot;Jacko&quot; has not been over for a day or so.</p>
<p>Had a narrow escape from being killed tonight at the aerodrome after having seen through the R.F.C. section of it.</p>
<p>A.C.F.A&nbsp;Football team played A.F.C&nbsp;on Aerodrome football ground &amp; were beaten in the last quarter.</p>
