<p>September 1915<br/>Tuesday 7<br/>Fine. Men of the 23rd Batt. dropping into the work nicely. 1500 Turks coming from Achi Baba got cut up by our warships fire. Our observation balloons spotted them. Losses are unknown.</p>
<p>Wednesday 8<br/>Fine We are out of the trenches for good and we go to Shrapnel Gully<br/>until tonight. We instructed the new hands in their new duties and then handed over to them. We went to Rest Gully about 11.30 PM and then our Batt and the FIRST BN came on board HMS Partridge</p>
