To ensure that everything is carried and no straw is used to fill the packs; Police search the huts and confisticate all stray gear. There are some quaint subjects of the King on this front, Chinamen, Kaffirs, Negros, etc; but some natives of Kasmir, India, that I saw today take the biscuit. They look as if they had been cannibals in civil life, and stretch the lobes of their areas by boring them and wearing brass ornaments in the holes.
