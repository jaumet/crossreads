
Tuesday.  April 22.4.19am well  had a good nights rest in my new home  tons of food here  nothing to do only sleep and eatWednesday April 23.4.19.am well  cloudy here this morning  nothing doing  went for a march.4.P.M. got orders to get ready to leave quarantine thursday tea 5.P.M.  turned in  was issued with clothes  11.P.M had to get out of bed  sent Josie a wire
