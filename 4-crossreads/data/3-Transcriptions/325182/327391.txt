<p>11th<br/>I went round with the C.O. to the Right Battalion Hqrs. (12 London Regt.) and had a talk with the old josser of a colonel who didn't know a damn thing about his outposts, although his officers <strike>officers</strike> and men always salute like machines and nearly faint when he speaks to them.  We left him and went for a walk <strike>d</strike> round our Coys.  All the boys are very happy and anxious for Fritz to come over here.  We have had several men wounded from machine gun fire mostly very light wounds.  Our men are trying to locate</p>
