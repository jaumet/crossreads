 Monday 23&nbsp;August 1915&nbsp; Ramsay Smith relieved of his command at No 1 A.G.H. Why?&nbsp;Barrett relieved of all his Military duties &amp; ordered to confine his energies to Red Cross work. Why? Miss Bell the Matron, formerly, of N o  1. A.G.H. is to return to Australia. Why?&nbsp; A real convulsion. I fear some dirty bad work.&nbsp; Saw Smith &amp; Barrett for a few minutes today.  Col. Paton &nbsp;
