
F 27 Sun
Nice Day. Recd. 2 papers from ETTA go to NZ Comp. & watch footy match till half Time. E goes to Sarum.  Dick & I have a lesson all night in English etc.
F 28 Mon
Recd. Letter from Jennie and mum. Sit darn socks nearly all night. We have Tattoo Roll Call.
Wrote M.R.L
F 29 Tues.
Recd. Letters from Norm. Dad. Play Hockey. Rotten exhibition. Do no reading or writing at Sgt. Pantier comes down  &  we talk till after light out Recd let
F 30 Wed
Glorious Day. Play Hockey against the Civis' & W.A.A.C'S. Strain my leg. An Ord. Corp. Sgt's have dance  w Coffee Bar.Recd. Letter from MRL & PC from Chris.Go rounds alone. Have rotten time. E comes in at 1.30 am   &  I don't sleep for Hours.Wrote Dad  &  Mum.
F 31 Thurs.Recd. Letters Tournd. Esme. Dulce. Martin.Recd. 2 Papers D.J. paper. Flowers: Edie TFoggy but turns out lovely Day. Go to Lewes with 10 Prisoners. Have hasty motor ride through London arrive Lewes 4 pm. Leave Brighton 6pm. Go see Hugh. Catch 10.40 pm from No8 to Eastleigh. Arr Sarum 3.45 am. Geo M. Edmunds. An. B'ford 9pm.
F 1 Feb. Fri
Lovely Day. Go to bed at 9.30 am. Up for Dinner. E  &  D go to Pictures Rotten show. We talk to Jim Robbie. No supper. Go to bed cold tired.
W 2 Sat
Recd. Letter from Laura. Dad  &  Mum. Parcel from Sunshine Girls per Dulce. Dave comes up &  we talk. E. goes to the Theatre "under Two flags". I write  &  go down to see Dave. Top   & Jim come down to talk. E  &  D have supper & I take over  No1 Hut
Wrote to Dad. Mum. George  &  Auntie Annie.
