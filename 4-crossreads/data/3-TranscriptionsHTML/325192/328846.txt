<p>These wher never answered, however it had the effect that 1 tent got a Wwooden Floor, also that we could send them some Reading Material &amp; some tobacco. During April 1916 we wher surprised to see the escapee Andree being brought back to Headquarters under strong Guard, handcuffed Hand behind his back &amp; Leg Irones on. Him hawing been recaptured near Bourke 800 miles away from hier right in the Hearth of Australior. A place more like a dessert than land fitt habituations. After being photographed with Leg Irones on, legs well apart he also wher put into the special compound. April 12 there wher a small tribunal sitting wich only dealt with the escapee H Z&ouml;ller, the one wich had pushed the Wheelbarrow. The next day our Notice Board</p>
