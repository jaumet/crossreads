<p>1916 January 12<br/>Wednesday</p>
<p>In charge of ward during Tony's absence.  Blood sample taken from Mr Boxald for exam. for typhoid germs.</p>
<p>Wagon drill in afternoon.  Go over to see Tony &amp; stay to tea &amp; supper. Better.  Underworld.</p>
