
So tomorrow there will be alarms  &  excursions I suppose  &  poor old Jackie - our 8 hr - won't know whether he is on his head or his heels and the hard workers will do all the work while the pointers take a holiday  &  do nothing; Oh! I could map out the programmes of the next few days as accurately as if they were already past! - But I had better not anticipate. Let it suffice to say that we are at least about to shake from off our perspirings feet the thrice accursed dust of Sohag.
