  
 Wednesday 17 October 
 Fine Runs to 5th Div. Workshops (Hillock) A.D.M.S. Ypres - through Busseboom & Ouderdom & Vlamertinghe 3rd Aust. Field Ambce 5th Div. Workshops again 1st Anzac Corps Head Quarters Sgt. Roberts & Briggs evacuated "gassed" Cards again tonight as usual. Saw two Fritz planes brought down.  Our young 19 year old pilot descended & took the hat and gloves from one Fritz.
