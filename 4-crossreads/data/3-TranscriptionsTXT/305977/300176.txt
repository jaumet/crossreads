 came out after dinner &amp; we went down to the 7th lines &amp; found Walter Kirton.  We then went for a walk through Maadi &amp; along the banks of the Nile.  Ted stayed to tea &amp; struck a lot of acquaintances in the 6th. 
 Monday 15th [Feb] Got back the the old troop drill on foot, sections right &amp; sections left.  Capt White gave us some instructions in the use of the rifle.  Got our saddles out after dinner &amp; cleaned our rifles.  Went on stable picket at 6pm.  Had a quiet night.  Horses gave no trouble which was just as well, as I wasn't feeling too good.  Had an attack of indigestion.
