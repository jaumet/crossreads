We got home about 9 o'clock having had nothing to eat or drink all the time.  Needless to say we were ready for our tea. Last Sunday I took over some washing to a French woman, and got it back yesterday.  There were two shirts a pair of drawers, a pair of socks, & a pocket handkerchief.  They were all very dirty.  She washed them beautifully clean and darned the socks, and for the lot she only charged 1 franc 10d.
