 53 54 Poperinghe this afternoon. It was A.1. 
 20/10/16 First frost this morning by jove it was cold. The duck-boards around the place looked lovely under a snow white crust. It has turned out a lovely day, beautiful in the sun. The Aeroplanes took advantage of the clear morning &amp; were fairly active. 
 21/10/16 Frost heavier this morning the house tops were snow white &amp; glistened in the early morning sun. 
 23-10/16 Am just about to turn in after a busy day. We leave to-morrow for Steenvoorde but only for a day or so from there we are making for the Somme
