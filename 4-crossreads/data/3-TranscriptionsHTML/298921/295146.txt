<p>June, 1915<br/>Thursday 24<br/>Posted Diary yesterday &amp; P.C. from the trenches &ndash; Court Martial over &ndash; <strike>The Artillery here have painted their guns primary colors Blue Red Yellow supposed to make more invisible</strike> &ndash; No rain for months, in this climate &ndash; most of the Houses are roofed with <u>wood</u> like weather boards. Norman Belcher from Geelong once from [indecipherable] camped here last night he had been to Gallipoli &amp; gave us news.<br/>Went Cairo on a Signalers Motor Byke 3 speed Hudson I wish I could drive a "car" every man should learn how. I could do such a lot of my work if did not always want a chauffer.</p>
