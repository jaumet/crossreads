<p>Tuesday 4.6.18&nbsp; Meeting of C.O&#39;s at Bde re stunt, taking over from Martin&#39;s tomorrow. Wrote to Doff</p>
<p>Wednesday 5.6.18. Took over from Basil&#39;s old Bn. no casualties- now in Reserve.</p>
<p>Thursday 6.6.18. Reconnoitring &amp; digging at night- cables</p>
<p>Friday 7.6.18. Gas shelling at night, digging again, otherwise quiet</p>
<p>Saturday 8/6/18. More digging&nbsp; orders received for Stunt- Bn in Reserve.</p>
<p>Sunday 9.6.18&nbsp; moved into position before daylight &amp; remained quiet</p>
