<p>TO OUR CRITICS<br/>From an Australian Soldier in Egypt<br/>Aint yer got no blanky savvy,<br/>Have yer got no better use,<br/>Than to fling back home yer inky<br/>Products of your pens abuse.</p>
<p>Do yer think we've all gone dippy,<br/>Since we landed over here,<br/>Is a soldier less a soldier!<br/>Cause he socks a pint of beer.</p>
<p>Have yer got no loving mother!<br/>Waiting for yer over 'Ome,<br/>Do you own no smiling sister!<br/>Over there across the foam.</p>
<p>Do yer thinks they likes yer better,<br/>For your tales of drink and shame,<br/>Do yer think they'll praise yer action,<br/>In defamin our fair name.</p>
<p>One swallow makes no summer,<br/>Three schickers not a force,<br/>Where the few makes it a welter<br/>You condems, the lot o'course.</p>
<p>Do you think yer God Almighty,<br/>Cos yer wears a captain's Stars.<br/>Think us blokes is dirt beneath yer,<br/>Men of low degree &amp; bars.</p>
<p>Say yer cannot be Australian,<br/>Let us say in our defence<br/>Yer can read it on your coinage<br/>Honny soit qui mal y pense.</p>
<p>Cease you wowseristic whining,<br/>Tell the truth &amp; play the game,<br/>And we only ask fair dinkum!<br/>How we keep Australia's name.</p>
<p>We're not out to fight the Devil,<br/>On a new Salvarmy stunt,<br/>To reform the Arabs morals,<br/>While we're waiting for the front.</p>
<p>Let me ask you Mr Critic<br/>Try to face things with a smile<br/>Don't be finding all the crook-uns,<br/>Studying them Hokes all the while.</p>
<p>Then write home nice and Proper,<br/>"Bout the boys thats all true blue,<br/>And they'll love you better mister,<br/>This is my advice to you</p>
<p>Tpt F.E. Westbrook<br/>4th Battery, A.F.A.<br/>1st A.I.F. MENA.</p>
