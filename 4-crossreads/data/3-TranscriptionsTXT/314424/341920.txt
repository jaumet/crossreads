 Tuesday March 12. 1918. 1317 A beautiful day. 
 I was again out on parade this morning. The drill was bayonett fighting. In the afternoon I was blacksmithing. 
 I received 5 francs from P.J. Regan for making him a souvenir. 
 I received a copy of the Portland Observer from my Father and The Leader. Nov 24/17 from K. Miller. 
 Wednesday March 13. 1918. 1318. A beautiful day, I was again working on the Mess cart. 
 I made a fridge out of 9 shell case for E. Thomas this evening. 
 I finished reading "Modern Geography" by Marion L. Newbigin a very interesting book of the home University Library.
