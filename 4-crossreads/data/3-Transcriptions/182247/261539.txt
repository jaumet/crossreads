<td style="text-align: right; vertical-align: top;">58</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 18</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Went to Q.M. store for kit at 7.15.&nbsp; Got ready to leave.&nbsp; 8.30.&nbsp; Wen/t<s>o</s> to Admission Hall ready to start out.&nbsp; 9.&nbsp; Left by motor for Denham.&nbsp; Caught train for Marylebone Station where I met Mr Brooke.&nbsp; Formed up and marched to tube which we took to St. James Park and Horseferry Road.&nbsp; Had some tea and cakes and got pay, pass, etc.&nbsp; Mr Brooke met me there and helped me through.&nbsp; Drew &pound;18 pay.&nbsp; Got 14 days leave instead of 7.&nbsp; The only thing I ever got out of the Army so far, for nothing.&nbsp; Had lunch at Lyons&#39; with Mr Brooke and went and sat in the Lounge of the Grosvenor Hotel with him and yarned for about an hour.&nbsp; 2.&nbsp; Left by motor bus from Victoria to Cricklewood.&nbsp; Met Mrs Linley there and, after saying good-bye to Mr. Brooke at station, left for Mrs Linley&#39;s home.&nbsp; Arrived there about 4.&nbsp; Felt very tired and fatigued.&nbsp; Played Pianola till dinner at 6.30.&nbsp; Turned in at 10.15 while a big air raid was in progress.&nbsp; Spent a good night.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 19</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Slept in till 10.&nbsp; Had headache and got up.&nbsp; Had lunch and then some aspirin.&nbsp; Head a little easier but not much.&nbsp; Wrote after lunch.&nbsp; Nasty foggy day outside.&nbsp; Turned in and slept well.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 20</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 9.&nbsp; Feel better.&nbsp; Nasty day again.&nbsp; Played Pianola and wrote in afternoon.&nbsp; Rang Highwood re mail.&nbsp; Rang Mr Brooke re coming out to us on Saturday.&nbsp; He promised to do so.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 21</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 9.&nbsp; Lovely day.&nbsp; Went up town to shop for Mrs Linley and had haircut.&nbsp; First time out since coming down this way.&nbsp; Ruth Shellshear rang me up.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 22</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feel great.&nbsp; Spent most of day writing letters.&nbsp; Day dull and wet.&nbsp; Big home mail in through Mr Highwood.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 23</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Slept fairly well.&nbsp; Rose 9.30.&nbsp; Went up town to shop for Mrs Linley and got back feeling knocked up.&nbsp; Wrote for some time and then went out to meet Mr Brooke and Ruth Shellshear who were coming to afternoon tea.&nbsp; Nell Shellshear also came with them.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 24</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 9.&nbsp; Left at 10.30 for London.&nbsp; Went to see Mr. Highwood.&nbsp; Got &pound;7.&nbsp; Went to lunch with him and then got boots at Dick&#39;s for 35/-.&nbsp; Also underclothing from Hope&#39;s in Holborn.&nbsp; Left London at 4.&nbsp; Made appoint-ment with dentist in Cricklewood for Tuesday at 9.30.&nbsp; Arrived home 5.&nbsp; Had some tea with Mrs Linley.&nbsp; Then Dinner and a good old yarn before the fire.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Feb 26</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Went to Dentist 9.30 and had tooth done.&nbsp; Returned home 11.&nbsp; Lunched.&nbsp; Wrote letters and then went up town with Mrs Linley.&nbsp; Got back 4.30 dead beat.&nbsp; Rang up</td>
</tr>
