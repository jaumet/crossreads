This camp is called the Areodrom.because it used to be the flying camp.Went for a route march across the desert.Paid 34 pisastres equal to 7/- It will not go far as things are so dear,Felt sick all day. Wrote to Mother May, Nellie & sent xmas cards.
Nov 7No parade. went & looked up C Thurlow, & also South
Nov 10/Sent parcels of silk away
13/Nothing unusual has happened lately. Receiving plenty of drill & bom throwing, 200 men left camp for the Dardanelles
