<p>After dinner I went for a stroll up the town and bought some cigars for my old French friend at St Omer at [and] came back to the depot and caught a traiun straight away and arrived in St Omer a little before 6 pm, and went along to the caf&eacute; and had a good tea and gave the cigars to the old grand dad who was pleased to receive them.</p>
<p>I spent the evening with the family and said goodbye at 9 pm and perhaps my last farewell and thanked them all for their kindness to me since I had the pleasure of knowing them. I then caught a French goods train right close to the depot and arrived in the wagon again at 11.30 pm.</p>
