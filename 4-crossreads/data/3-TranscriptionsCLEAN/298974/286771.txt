  
 3 we got out of the gates there were hundreds of women & children waiting, many babes in arms. The endeavours of these poor exposed people to find their husbands & lovrs were pitiful. It was very cold & pitch dark too, so you can imagine their difficulty when there were 1500 of us. As we marched down Bourke St & down to Woolloomoolo gases were lit & people scantily clad rushed onto balconies & into the street to cheer us on. It was a remarkable sight, but horribly dismal.
