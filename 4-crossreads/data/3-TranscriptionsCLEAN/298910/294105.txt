  
 Corporal Fooks & Pte Burns 59th Batt Medical details - died as the result of burns from petrol igniting - 3 others were charred beyond recognition. 
 Saturday  17/2/17 
 Quite warm - getting very muddy & dirty. Fairly busy again with more gas cases - Lieutenant Galway died during the night - I volunteered to go to Details in the 59th Batt - but the Colonel wouldn't let any old hands go. Corporal Loveridge and 2 Reinforcements went. 
 Sunday  18/2/17 
 Quite warm - Very muddy & dirty - Fairly busy again - a few more gas cases came thro. G O/C was supposed to inspect us - but he never turned up. 
 19/2/17  Monday 
 Quite warm - Slush everywhere - Fritz putting over a lot of Gas shells - Fairly busy. 
 20/2/17  Tuesday 
 Quite warm - Fairly busy - several Gas Patients through - Trench feet starting again.
