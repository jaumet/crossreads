<p><u>Jan. 10th</u></p>
<p>Letter writing and studying.&nbsp; In the afternoon the Camp Commandant arrives and after filling in our Demobilisation Papers we are told that for certain reasons &quot;The Anzac Coves&quot; are broken up &amp; no longer exist as a Concert Party.&nbsp; We are then given a choice as to our next individual move - Application for &quot;Corner of Blighty&quot; - The spell is at last broken.</p>
