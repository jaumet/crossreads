Unfortunately I found upon arrival that there were only two companies of the Battn there. The others so I was told were camped at Bernagay[?] Wood - a matter of about 7 kilos away. Off I went and walking briskly along the main road eventually reached my objective only to find that my brother Syd had got his leave 6 days previously! However it could not be helped, so I set off back to our own billets. My terrible[?]was the lack of de[?].
