<p>Saturday 15 March 1919<br/>On special duty at the Rouelles station today . Went down to see if the Nirvana was in but it wasn't so we blew out to Bill's cousins place. Had dinner &amp; taught them some more bridge. Got wet through coming home. Didn't have our coats &amp; of course it rained a soon as we got out of the train.</p>
<p>Sunday 16 March 1919<br/>Went to Bills cousins &amp; had the usual old sing song. Only saw a little of the sun.</p>
