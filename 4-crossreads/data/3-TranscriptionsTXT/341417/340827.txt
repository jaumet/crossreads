 This is the first big scrap I have had since I got my Com. One has too much to watch &amp; think of now to get the tit bits as when a ranker. The second day was interesting, but mostly a case of endurance for horses &amp; men. I will ma[il] a separate letter of it. I am writing to Father to ask him to send a cheque for &pound;7.10 to Mrs Kate F Stribley 99 Sheppard St Sydney. He had given this amount to mind in Egyptian notes. The O/C told me to arrange things by keeping the money &amp; paying him if possible from the other end. Poor chap was a married man &amp; gamer than
