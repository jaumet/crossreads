  
 I often times think of the balls & dances that Tamworth folk are having now June being the month.  [Deletion by censor] Am terrible short of envelopes please enclose a few in your next letter & continue on till further notice we may not be able to get any for a long time  p. cards will have to do in the future now unless of course I drop on a few now & again Jack Tory encloses a letter home please see that his people get it next letter you will very likley get from them change about like. Well with the best of love to all Your loving son Watson F Adamson 
 P.T.O.
