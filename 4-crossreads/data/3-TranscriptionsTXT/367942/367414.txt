 3rd Dec. Round the Cape &amp; proceeding about due West 6th Dec. Met the "Igotz &ndash; Mendi" again &amp; gave her some stores 7th Dec. Spaniard left us going to Westrd "Wolf" steering about W.N.W. average speed 9 knots, evidently making for Trinidad 14th Dec. Sighted Barque just before sundown &amp; followed her all night. 15th Dec. 5 A.M. Captured French Barque "Marshall Daveout" of Nantes, from Geelong to Dakar with Cargo of wheat. Wireless &amp; 2 3in guns. Took off crew &amp; provisions. 1 pm sunk her &amp; proceeded. 16th Dec. Seaplane assembled again after recovering &amp; repairs made flight at 5 pm.
