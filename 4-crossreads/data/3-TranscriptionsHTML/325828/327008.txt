This was on the Saturday before New Year's Day.  On New Years Day I was discharged from Hospital &amp; had to report to the H.Q  London   I was very uncertain whether they would put me in a convalescent camp or give me sick furlough (14 days)<br/>It took me <strike>till 3</strike> from 9 AM till 3 to get fixed up at A.I.F Offices &amp; they finally gave me the furlough.  This pleased me as I would far rather have furlough now, than convalescence as it gives me an opportunity of visiting Walter almost every day.  I am making the most of the opportunity &amp; go out to Wandsworth every other day.  I thought Wal looked very bad the first time I saw him, &amp; indeed he was, but every time since that I have been in he seems much brighter &amp; more like his old self.
