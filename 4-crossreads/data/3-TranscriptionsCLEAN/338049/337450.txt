  
 Wednesday 28 August [King?] back to finish Div Train line. Fixed up many matters and stood by. 
 Ordered out immediately about 5 pm just before tea. Out to 251 Bde R.F.A then to 10th Bde then to 7th A.F.A. & finally to Bray. Last section in pitch dark. Very hard. 
 Home about 12.30 a.m. Lines working well. Shelling heavy.
