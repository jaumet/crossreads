 about 14 kilometres distant.  Having met the S.C. we were ordered to push on  Foucaucourt where I was told to go further on still by one of the Bde. Staff as the huns were retreating at a great rate.  I had to select a position for my Bn. near the 'site' of the old town of Estrees which I did.  It rained like blazes and I found that pushing a bike along muddy roads and sticky fields was a great joke I dont think.  I had a look round and dashed back as hard as I could to meet the Bn. whom I found having tea just outside Proyart.
