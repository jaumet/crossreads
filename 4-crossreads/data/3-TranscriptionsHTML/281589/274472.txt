<p>C2 roll &amp; put on C1., which means home service, either in England or Australia. On guard today 4.30PM till 4.30PM. It is "Keystone"  handling a rifle when I have never done so before. Lovely day today.</p>
<p>Tuesday September 4th  lovely day guard duty is no bon. I put in for furlough Wednesday unsettled weather, done up last night after guard duty, doing casual fatigues today. It rained tonight &amp; ten <strike>collasped</strike> collapsed.</p>
<p>Thursday Sept.6th on cooks fatigue solid work 6.15 AM till 3PM dreary weather, Good lecture at the Y.M.C.A. entitled "The Tabby on the rocks".</p>
<p>Friday fine, I volunteered as Mess orderly it may effect me going Home. Saturday Sept 8th fine, put in for furlough.</p>
