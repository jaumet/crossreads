No land sighted neither any ships Wednesday 29th Dec A fairly calm day; though dull with a few light showers &amp; the boat still rolling a bit : Revellie 6 am parade for physical drill 10 am ; A Private out of the 13th of the 3rd Batt died during the morning &amp; was buried at 4 pm with full military honours It was the first burial I have ever seen &amp; was very impressive.
