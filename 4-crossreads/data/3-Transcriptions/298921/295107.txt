<p>May, 1915<br/>Friday 14<br/>Drew 500/-/- &amp; opened a/c to pay Details left behind. I have many duties to perform now as Brigade Major &amp; o/c 1st L.H.R. the Col being acting Brigadier so far as pay is concerned I get no more but I do not mind. Last night I rode over &amp; saw Ralph Arnold he is a big strong fellow of 26 &amp; is the same Brigade &amp; Camp &ndash; I was glad to hear news of May.<br/>Very hot midday now.</p>
