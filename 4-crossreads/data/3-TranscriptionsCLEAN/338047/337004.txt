  
 1919 Apl 15 was marked - engaged.  We all embarked six to a compartment in a comfortable Second Class German Carriage.  Miss Rout came in & kissed us all good bye & left us to our own devices.  The train did not leave till 10.40 pm.  We thought it  a long while to wait, but shortly afterwards, when the train was opened to the vulgar herd there was a great rush for seats, but not to our carriage.  Officers & all were chasing around looking for seats, but we just sat there & watched them.  We had a good trip to Charleroi where we arrived next morning at about 8.30 am.
