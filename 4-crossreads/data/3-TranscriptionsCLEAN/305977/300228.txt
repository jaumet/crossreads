 Saturday 19th Went into the firing line at 6 oclock & were relieved by the infantry at noon.  We all came out of the trenches & occupied dug-outs in the gully.  We are going to be brigaded together again & occupy a certain portion of the firing line.  We had a big lot of fatigues to do during the day & at night a big party went out trench digging.  They did very well & were complimented by Genl Walker on their work. 
 Sunday 20th Was on fatigue in the morning till 10.30am.  Was detailed for guard duty at Brigade HQrs.  I wasn't feeling too good but got much better after tea.  The day was very warm.
