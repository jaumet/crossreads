<p>"Argoeuves" about 9 miles off and were billeted about 8am. Very poorly stocked shops.</p>
<p>13th. Thursday.<br/>Town picquet all day till lights out. 10pm. Very cold at nights with greatcoat only.</p>
<p>14th. Friday.<br/>Drill all morning &amp; nice Rout march afternoon, very pretty surroundings, hilly country all in crops &amp; beautifull to see the wild poppies thick among the corn. gold &amp; red &amp; green a charming scene on such slopes with little woods here and there &amp; scattered windmills. Out one road &amp; returned by different course.</p>
