<p>May 1918<br/>Saturday 25</p>
<p>Battery W.L.<br/>Fritz put a lucky one into some Labor Corps and accounted for 35 killed, 60 casualties altogether.<br/>Weather still dull but an improvement on yesterday. Knipe quite well today. Received a mag from Eileen. Wrote Mother and E. <br/>Commenced a recreation dug out under surveillance of Y.M.C.A. representative. <br/>Cleaned two saddles and received a visit from Andrews, also Tich Rice. Worked on dug out.</p>
