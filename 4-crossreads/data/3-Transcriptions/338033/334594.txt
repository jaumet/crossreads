<p>To Billingsgate fish markets.<br/>These are the main fish markets, and at that hour of the morning business was very busk, all kinds of fish we being sold wholesale to the mongers who wear dungarees and wide brimmed straw hats. The English slang is supposed to originate from here, and I am quite prepared to believe it too. Right in the centre of the market is a large monument marking the spot where the great fire started which finished at St Pauls Cathedral.<br/>A short distance from here is the tower bridge, which is a wonderful piece of architecture.
