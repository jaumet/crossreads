<p>Friday 4 [3] May 1917 ]1918]<br/>Beautiful morning &amp; all things unite to return, thanks to the one who gives the glorious world for us to live in.<br/>O.C. 11 a.m., "you would like to go to a Con. Home?  Make arrangements in a day or so" &amp; goes.<br/>Inventory once again so another is here for me to see mugs, chairs, etc. carried out, lined up in the hall &amp; all inspected.</p>
