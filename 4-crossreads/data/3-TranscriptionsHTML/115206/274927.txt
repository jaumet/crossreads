<p>December  1918.<br/>Wednesday 25</p>
<p>Port Said. H.S."Karoola."<br/>Weather fairly decent. Berthed and coaling still. Had a fine xmas dinner, pork and Duck with plum duff to follow. For tea we had salmon, green peas, potatoes with jelly and tinned peaches.<br/>Left Port Said at 6.30 pm. Passed Kantara at 9.55 and Fedan at 11pm. Passed Ismailia at 11.30</p>
