 Monday 10 September 1917 Duty 7 a.m. E Wards 
 Monday 9th [September] 1918 One year ago &amp; yet how things have changed.  A year in Egypt has taught me much &amp; what we as a family have suffered through the fault of one.  Often I feel that it must all be a dream &amp; that the little girl could never behave as she has done.
