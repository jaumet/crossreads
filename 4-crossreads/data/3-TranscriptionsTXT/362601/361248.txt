 6 August, Wednesday 
 Stop Home. Gardening &amp; House maid. Ring Nette, Mrs Clarke. Speak Nette 35 minutes or more. Sandy nearly eats a child. 
 Do a few hours gardening &amp; read &amp; write at night. 
 Receive notification about deferred pay. 
 7 August, Thursday 
 Mum &amp; I sort out trophies &amp; boxes, clothes, etc nearly all day. Ring Mrs Clarke. Write at night. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie.
