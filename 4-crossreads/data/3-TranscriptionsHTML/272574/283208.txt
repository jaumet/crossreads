<p>9 Still unfit. batt went out but came back early<br/>10 Our chaps been digging trench working in the heat like the devil.<br/>was to crook to help them so stayed in camp <br/>11 still no good<br/>12 Went out to look at the trenches but did not do any digging. we returned to camp early.<br/>The Batt has occupied the trenches. 2nd Batt making attack some time in the night. did not go out myself as I considered myself unfit.<br/>13 holiday but did not go out.<br/>Went to the doctor this morning he told me I looked neat &amp; respectable. I am wondering how to apply the compliment.<br/>14 Co mounted Div guard this evening; I prefered not to do picket.<br/>15 got snapped up for fatigue work but did not do much.<br/>16 had morning off filled up trenches in the evening.<br/>17 Brigade movement in the desert came back &frac12; past 2</p>
