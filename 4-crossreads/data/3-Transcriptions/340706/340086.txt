<p>After Court finished I make arrangements to sleep in tomorrow morning &amp; then to bed.</p>
<p>2-5-19 (Frid)<br/>Arrangements for departure still hold good in spite of measles. Rain all day. Mention to household that I am not getting up &amp; so they send breakfast up to me in bed. Finally get up at 10.30 &amp; have several commissions to perform</p>
<p>I collect 600 franc off Hutching [indecipherable] &amp; finally reach camp in neighbourhood of noon.</p>
<p>In afternoon I say good bye or adieu to all my friends &ndash; a somewhat painful task only relieved by the excellent wine that is supplied in honour of our last meeting not as a sign of gladness at my departure.</p>
