<p>Our chaps have had a heavy time of it &ndash; the Roads were very soft &amp; the waggons getting bogged continually &ndash; so it was a case of pulling each other out by turns consequently there were G.S. Waggons all over the countryside, in addition to bogged Motor Cars &amp; other vehicles. If the roads had been better so as the armoured cars could have joined in, the engagement would have been more decisive</p>
<p>As it is <strike>there</strike> we have inflicted heavy casualties on the enemy &amp; driven them away from the wells, but as they can reoccupy them at any <strike>rate</strike> time &amp; also inflicted serious casualties on us it seems to be a stalemate</p>
<p>The weather <strike>is</strike> has been miserable today</p>
