Not bad night. Gramophone is counted out to no end. Rotten machine. YMCA few yards away very handy. Concert tonight. Songs, Comic & otherwise, some class too. Obtained chocolate at last9 Bit off. Stiff neck. Too much chocolate perhaps. No letters yet. Pcs handed round10 Feeling better. Heard 2 Batt be relieved shortly. Pest in next bed has gone to Palace Hosp "Poor Palace".
