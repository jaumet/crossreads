<p>His recumbent snoring form was surrounded by about 30 lighted candles &amp; the boys then started a "wailing" It really looked most gruesome. The noise awoke the 'corpse' who on first coming too am sure thought he was a corpse &ndash; He was too drunk to rise but gave vent to his feeling. This incident must now close on <strike>a/c</strike> account of the language of the body which was most fluent.</p>
