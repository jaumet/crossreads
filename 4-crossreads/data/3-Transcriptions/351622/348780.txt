<p>12 February 1916 Sat<br/>March early in the morning &amp; very cold but got extremely hot towards midday. What would I give for a glass of beer &amp; ginger on such a day as this. Swimming in the canal before dinner. Went to the two-up school in the afternoon &amp; won &pound;4. Lecture by Captain SaVage on artificial respiration. Ken &amp; myself are [text continues top of page]</p>
<p>13 February 1916 Sun<br/>A repetition of yesterday scorching hot. Church parade on the Sinai desert. Played two-up after &ndash; came away winning a couple of pounds. Twelve months to-day since I enlisted &amp; what a great experience the past six months have been. Made an attempt to keep the day up by trying to get drunk on Caf&eacute;-a-liat. No effect.</p>
