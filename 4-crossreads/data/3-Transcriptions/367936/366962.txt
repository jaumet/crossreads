<p>Wednesday 12 April 1916<br/>Relieved from guard at 8 o'clock.  Whole camp 1000 men on a route march to Camden about 7 miles away.  I managed to get into the mounted Squadron.<br/>Camden is a small town on a hill.  Had lunch in show ground.</p>
<p>Thursday 13 April 1916<br/>Fatigue duty cleaning up before breakfast.<br/>Guard instruction all the rest of day.<br/>Heard that the 17th Reinforcements are to sail on Good Friday.  Rushed round, put in application for final leave and made allotment papers.  Am taking 2/- a day.  Father to receive 3/- per day.</p>
