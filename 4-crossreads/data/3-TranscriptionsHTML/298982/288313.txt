<p>Mon 28 June Heavy firing towards Achi Baba by Battleships another landing effected. Counted 24 small mine trawlers &amp; torpedo destroyers lined between Imbros &amp; there. Firing all day Midday a fizzle on our front on right reported trenches taken from Turks Own men went too far &amp; fired on by own side. Some big old shells on our hill side Also shrapnel. Another display at night with fire works &amp; cheering (Eggs.)</p>
<p>Fine swim in the dusk with Arthur</p>
<p>Tues 29th June. Ctd A heavy fire opened up all along own front abt 9 45 pm as well as heavy bombardment down the coast. This with the cloudy night &amp; the flares made</p>
