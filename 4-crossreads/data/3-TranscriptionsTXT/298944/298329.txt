 That was the first time it had been bombed since we first formed it. 
 Landed at Hoer[indecipherable] at 1 A.M, waited interminably in a share for "comforts fund" cocoa &amp; Australian biscuits, then started on a 4 kilo march. I was so tired &amp; sleepy that I almost went to sleep  standing  as I was walking along. 
 Reached Questede (where we formed up the 2nd Field last Nov.)
