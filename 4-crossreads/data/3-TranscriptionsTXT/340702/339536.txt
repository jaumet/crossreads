 Friday 8/2   Both our heads &amp; the German heads are very windy just now. We have simply miles of wire everywhere &amp; are putting up more. Machine Guns are being put in as Gun Escorts. The Artillery are worrying a great deal over the fact that banks of barbed wire are being put in behind them. They reckon they'll never be able to get out before a rapid advance. Fritz is raiding all along the Front seeking prisoners &amp; information. Spring is nearly here &amp; both sides are feeling the suspense. 
 Thursday 14.2   With reference to tucker. Practically every Pill box our fellows captured at Ypres last time was full of Tucker.
