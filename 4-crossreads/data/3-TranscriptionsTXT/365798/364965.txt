Left Sydney 11th Febry 1915 per "Seang Bee" &amp; made for Brisbane. Arrived early Saturday 13th Feb. &amp; berthed at Pinkenba. Took on some 300 Q'landers making 1100 soldiers on board &ndash; Left Pinkenba 5 pm 13th.
We steamed northwards &amp; took Pilot on board early Monday morning at Townsville. The route was inside Barrier Reef &amp; sea perfectly calm with warm sunny weather. Was on guard all day Tuesday day &amp; night &amp; about 4.00 am on Wednesday we struck a reef. Got over safely, but it took all way off the ship. Certainly a very lucky escape.
