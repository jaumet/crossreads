<p>Friday 23 February 1917</p>
<p>Made out big list of promotions for N.C.O.s in my Squadron, the standard of the L.H. is so high that Officers &amp; N.C.O.s have a great responsibility &amp; need to be correspondingly good.</p>
<p>Getting warmer every week, tho' nights are still cold.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>ENGLISH mail in &ndash; letters from Laura &ndash; the wife, Mrs. Ferrar &amp; Pagan's &ndash; am sorry the Housewife Mother made me has not yet arrived, but I've not given up hope as I've only just received Mothers hand painted Hanky was so pleased with it.</p>
