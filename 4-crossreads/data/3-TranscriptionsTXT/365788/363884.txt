 3 the cable you sent me to Lemnos &amp; also for the good wishes it embodied. It is awfully hard work writing subject matter here which is not censorable. Did I tell you I saw Beatrice Russel Glasson? I am writing part of this as I wait outside the Dentist's tent here. His name is Lt. Amphlett. He really seems to take a lot of trouble with our teeth &amp; it is a God sent to be able to get them done. Very sorry to hear you had to go to Moxham &amp; hope he did not give you much pain. Please remember me to Frank &amp; Mrs Dight when you write. Very much love to you all Ever your loving brother Fred
