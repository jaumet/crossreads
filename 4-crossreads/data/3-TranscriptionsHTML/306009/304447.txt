<p>1915<br/>Sep. 22 Wed.<br/>Swimming , boating, walking &amp; reading as usual to-day.<br/>Up at 6 a.m. each morning, wake the 4 Scouts &amp; we go down for a swim before breakfast.<br/>A very good concert was held at 7.30 p.m. on the fine, wide, tiled verandah overlooking the ocean.</p>
