<p>Suva is small &ndash; smaller than Glenn Innes a lot &ndash; smaller than Cairns  -  The white population is say &ndash; 2000 but the native Fijians &amp; Samoans amount to 80,000 I am told  -  There are also some 50 000 coolies from Madras &amp; Calcutta  -  They are indentured by the Government &amp; hired by the planters  -  Fiji is a Crown colony  Brown predominates everywhere &ndash; numberless women &ndash; native &amp; Indian parade the streets the Indian women with ornaments in their noses &amp; ears &ndash; one ayah had 3 in her nose &amp; 5 in each ear   -  Flat ornaments &amp; a ring in her nose &amp; fancy your ear pierced in 5 places on both sides  -  The native men &amp; women are a fine lot very lazy I'm told but then most races are  -  Fighting &amp; man hunting is forbidden &amp; all else is tame compared to that  -  Well I walked about &amp; examined the shops Indian &ndash; Japanese &ndash; Chinese &ndash; Natives huts &ndash; Mean white places &amp; eventually got back</p>
