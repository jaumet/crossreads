<p>Casino de Paris. Cairo<br/>"Le Dans de l'Amour" from "Madame Sherry"<br/>Gorman to Sparrow:-<br/>"Hail to thee blithe spirit. Bird thou never werest.</p>
<p>Grim viaged War hath smoothed his wrinkled front<br/>And he that mounted barbered steeds<br/>To fright the souls of feared adversaries<br/>Now capers nimbly in a ladies chamber<br/>To the lascivious pleasing of a lute."</p>
<p>Letter from old Bob Ralston concluded:-<br/>Remember me to all my dear fellow officers!"</p>
<p>Transcriber's note:<br/>Generally, place names are in upper case.</p>
