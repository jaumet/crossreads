<p>Tuesday 30th July<br/>After two or three committee meetings the scheme was put into practice yesterday. My first English class was about 30 strong, &amp; the Arithmetic nearly as many. I went to the Psychology in the evening, and enjoyed a good lecture by Barlow.</p>
