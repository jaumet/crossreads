<p>1916 March 20<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Up betimes this morning &amp; get our kits out to time. Feeling somewhat excited. At 10.00 we fall in for medical inspection by Capt. Moir. That over after some amusement we had dinner &amp; afterwards were inspected by the O. <br/> C. then at 12.15 we march off for the last time from the Hospital.  thus commencing the first stage of our long journey to Egypt. Arrived in Sydney after a good send off all along the line &amp; were met by all our friends. March out to the Show Ground &amp; thence joining in with the rest of the reinforcements parade to the Domain  People line the route &amp; cheer us on. Beautiful afternoon &amp; parade very successful. After it was over</p>
