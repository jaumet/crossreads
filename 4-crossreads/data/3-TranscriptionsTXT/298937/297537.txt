 a great deal of trouble. Ali Ibrahim, the consumptive, has been court martialled for plotting, &amp; the result is not yet out, but both he &amp; the other two have been treated exceedingly leniently. Eggs &amp; delicacies have been forced on them, to be received with surliness. Now Enver Pasha's brother Jaffa Pasha has been captured on the Western frontier &amp; there was a tremendous excitement when he arrived. All the officers were dressed in their best &amp; they waited at the entrance as if for a visitor of high rank.
