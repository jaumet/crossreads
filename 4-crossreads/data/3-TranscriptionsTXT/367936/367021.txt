
Tuesday 8 August 1916In morning Dawson & I rode to Rail Head & bought some sardines.In afternoon fell in 2-30 3 brigades and rode to Katia, had tea & rode on all night Birelabd [Bir El Abd].
Wednesday 9 August 1916Arrived near Birelabd dawn & came under shrapnel.  Attacked Turks.  Ronald killed.  Machine gun fire.  Beaten back.  Awful shrapnel.  Fought rear guard action.  Very hard to get wounded away.  Retired about 5miles to Ogratina [also spelt Oghratina].
