<p>Oct<br/>Wednesday 3rd<br/>Medical inspection <strike>etc</strike> &amp; all the little things <strike>attended</strike> that happen on return from leave Today was longer than the last 6 days</p>
<p>Thursday  4th<br/>Wet so nothing was done Payed in afternoon Wrote quite a lot of letters Sold my fountain pen for 7/6 &ndash; 2/6 proffit.</p>
<p>Friday 5th<br/>Same old thing Wrote to Leo &amp; Ned today</p>
<p>Sat 6th<br/>Did about 1 hour PT then messed about No rifles to use as they have been packed for our move next week. Collier &amp; I walked to Lesbury a small</p>
