<p>7. Sunday<br/>Heavy showers.  Finished Macedonia sketches.  Sig. Siga. Signora Bambacionis called for tea &ndash;<br/>Read History.</p>
<p>8. Monday<br/>Sunshine now &amp; again.  Went to Miss Hodges Indian tea at Minerva.  Terribly boring.  Home &amp; washed hair.  Dr &amp; Mrs Clark called</p>
<p>9. Tuesday<br/>Such a dense fog, lights necessary at 10 a.m.  Wrote article for S.M.H.  Pottered had a snooze etc.</p>
<p>10. Wednesday<br/>Showery &amp;  grey.  Went to Miss Newcomb at 10 returned home at 4 &amp;  pottered on odds &amp;  ends.</p>
