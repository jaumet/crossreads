 25th Aug. Much has taken place since I last wrote in this diary.  Today nurse gave me this, &amp; asked me if I wanted it.  I am in Hospital (Brit.) No. 2 Stationary at Abbeville.  It's worse than in the line &ndash; for 3 days I've lain here to my recollection &amp; had not even a dressing on my wound.
