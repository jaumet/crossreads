<p>Tuesday 5/6/17<br/>Quiet day, feeling tired &ndash; musketry.<br/>Wednesday 6/6/17<br/>Field day towards Hamel very useful work done.<br/>Thursday 7/6/17<br/>Visited by Genl Smythe &amp; Wisdom &ndash; Won final of Rugby football against 25th.<br/>Friday 8/6/17<br/>Musketry in morning. Close order drill in afternoon &ndash; lecture by Close at Senlis, wrote to Doff and General.<br/>Saturday 9/6/17<br/>Went by car to Amiens with Lloyd and Close of the Field Artillery.</p>
