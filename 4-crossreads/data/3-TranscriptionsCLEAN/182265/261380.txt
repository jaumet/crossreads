16 15/10/17 In barn near Caestre & Fl&ecirc;tre. On Sunday 14th I conducted 50 men up to Ypres to join the battalion f before they went in. We left camp in two motor buses at 8.15 & reached Ypres about 10.30. Returning an hour later I took an English wagon from Ypres to Poperinghe. Stayed there an hour. Then took Australian wagon to Abeele on Belgian border then walked 2 miles, took another Australian Bus to Steenvoorde. Leaving Steenvoorde I took a Canadian bus going to Hazebrouke as far as a small village, then another Canadian lorry to Caestre. Very cold sleeping in a damp tent.
