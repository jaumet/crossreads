June 10th N.N.W. 320M.
Orderly Cpl. Company, reading novels most day, passed many ships several transports Hospital Ship towards sunset. Running up kind of narrowing channel from midday. Rec?d Identification disk Pay Book to make out Will Form. Beautiful weather cool, good breeze.
