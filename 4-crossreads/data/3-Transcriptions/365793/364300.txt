<p>Jly 19 corridors; &amp; the poets corner &ndash; where lie so many famous men sleeping "the sleep which knows no waking" until that "Great Day" when the myriad dead shall rise, &amp; this old Earth be no more.<br/>Unable to get in to see Parl't sitting, but went to the afternoon Service at the Abbey &amp; was well rewarded. The organ is magnificent, &amp; the choir sang beautifully.<br/>Secured a railway warrant for Portishead &amp; then collected &pound; 25 from the NSW Bank. Saw sundry Dookes &amp; Earls at the House of Commons &ndash; also Haldane. Gee! I nearly forgot</p>
