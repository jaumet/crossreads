 Tuesday 9th April So far I have only passed the doctor &amp; dentist, &amp; have still to be put through gas before being eligible to be put on draft. My teeth have yet to be fixed up, so probably I shall not leave for a week.  yet  The battle has not yet entered on its second stage. The fierce yet only intermediary fighting which is going on at present, has been taken part in by Australians, so probably I shall be in the thick of a stunt soon.
