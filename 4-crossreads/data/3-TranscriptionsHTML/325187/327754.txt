<p>[Sunday  January 9 1916]</p>
<p>Day hot again at No.1   Took Mardi &amp; Miss Cook for a ride out on the Desert as we do not now march for a day or two-<br/>Both girls are well &amp; take a great interest in their work.  They were lucky to get to Cairo before the embargo on Women folk.</p>
