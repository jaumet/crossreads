 28 Not landing at Toulon Left at 6.15 for Marseille Fairly rough sea & we're hugging the coast. Dull & cold with slight rain  Arrived Marseille at 10.45. Poor sort of a harbour, breakwaters must have cost a bit. Beautiful building standing on one of the highest peaks, & crowned by a (golden) figure. Miserable & cold so our impressions of the port are not of the best. Most of the buildings are roofed with tiles, & the private places remind one of Sydney. Toulon is a much prettier port, as there appears to be little greenery here.
