<p>4/5/17<br/>Company Pontooning &amp; general training.</p>
<p>5th<br/>Div. Sports today.  Horses were looking beautiful &amp; turnover was very good.</p>
<p>6th<br/>Church Parade this morning.  Coy. played 55th Soccer in afternoon &amp; won.</p>
<p>7th<br/>Company Pontooning &amp; other training.</p>
<p>8th<br/>Too wet for training.  Getting ready for entraining.</p>
<p>9th<br/>Left Becourt <strike>for</strike> and per foot &amp; train travelled to Vaulx &amp; relieved 7th F. Coy. Engrs.  Lively Artillery firing along Line.</p>
<p>9th<br/>Very heavy Artillery firing along this Front.  Enemy firing a lot of shells on positions right back here.</p>
<p>10th<br/>Coy. doing a little training &amp; a little Front line work.  12th, 15th Brigade &amp; a Tommy Brigade hopped over.</p>
<p>11-5-17<br/>Party from my Sec. out wiring tonight in Front of Line.  Very heavy bombardment by our Guns.  Expect hop over in morning</p>
