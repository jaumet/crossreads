
Monday 3rd [Dec]Very heavy frosts.  All pools frozen this morning &  trying to snow.  It did a little.
Tuesday 4thAnother heavy frost this morning.  At football the A Team defeated B Team by 16 - 0.  The B Team were outclassed   I played my first game today with B Team & didn't have anything to do.  Saw Les Powers this afternoon. He is with 15 [?] Fld Amb & also Sammy Doyle.
Wednesday 5thConcert by our own boys.  Very good.  First appearance of our orchestra.
Thursday 6thFootball practice & at night Cribbage tournament.  Weather very cold & everything frozen pools [indecipherable] etc.
BOMBING RAIDS BY GOTHAS
Friday 7th  Saturday 8thPlayed football this afternoon against ASC Team (D Cunningham was playing) & won by 6 - 5.  Two goals by Billy Brown against a converted try.  I played but did not shape at all well at full back.
Sunday 9th Monday 10thFritz was over us bombing with his Gothas this afternoon - five or six of them & he distributed a few papers.
Tuesday 11thPolling day.  Reinforcements Referendum.  I voted  "yes"
Wednesday 12thA bad day
