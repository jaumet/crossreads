<p>11 p.m. tired out. Of course I said nothing on earth would keep me out of my bed after 8 p.m. Well, ask me, life is short. Why not be merry.</p>
<p>Am off-duty for a few hours this afternoon so trying to scribble a few lines. Dorothy is sleeping. Our Colonel has been having a few days in Paris   &amp;  we expect him back any time now. Some of the Sisters will be going next.</p>
