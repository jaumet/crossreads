<p>Friday 21st<br/>Still frozen hard.</p>
<p>Saturday 22nd<br/>Concert by No 1 ACCS party.</p>
<p>Sunday 23rd<br/>Concert by Anzac Coves Concert Party.</p>
<p>Monday 24th<br/>(Xmas Eve)  Snowing &amp; typically mild for this time of year.  Sat up late &amp; completed costume with Dick Gates.  Not feeling at all well.</p>
<p>Tuesday (Xmas Day)  Did not feel at all well when I awoke &ndash; slight attack of influenza or the like.  So remained in bed all the morning.  It was snowing hard all the morning and when I made an appearance about noon the ground was covered by about 6" inches of it with prospects of more to come.  Then followed a rather scrumptious dinner.  Soup ham cold meat vegetables inc potatoes brussel sprouts baked potatoes seasoning cabbage etc. turkey with sauce  custard jelly pudding fruit (tinned &amp; fresh) brandy sauce nuts cigarettes ale &amp; other things.  I missed a few courses but did well.</p>
