<p>Monday Dec 4.12.16.<br/>Left Romani 12.30 trecked till 4PM camped at a place I do not know<br/>Tuesday Dec 5.12.16 Stand to 6AM had breakfast and moved off 8AM trecked till 12AM had a rest. had four letters from win,Josi Dorrie and Jess Wednesday Dec 6.12.16  stand to 6AM had breakfast and moved off 8AM trecked till 12 reached destination Bir el abd  about 12.30 unpacked and had dinner<br/>Thursday Dec7.12.16 still at Bir el abb</p>
