
Christmas card  dated 8.12.18GREETINGS AND BEST WISHES FROMHandwritten:-.  Clyde,  Beaumeuse, Belgian Border
Text:-  CHRISTMAS 1918We greet you friend, where'er your distant home,In Britain, France, 'neath Austral's sunny dome;Now there is peace, we'll see you all again-For soon we hope to leave this cold Bohain,To safely dodge the Hun slow-action mineAnd spend our last war Christmas on the Rhine
