<p>"Cooee's". Bully beef, which was greatly in demand by civilians, was forthcoming so that we really gave away most of our rations. French railways are very fine indeed. Huge engines of all types &amp; sizes, and the numerous big railway yards did not escape our notice. As regards the names of railway stations "en route", I am only mentioning the larger ones. Our first meal on the train was at Orange 1.30 am Saturday 24th June. Hot water had been previously arranged</p>
