<p>5 January, Sunday</p>
<p>W<br/>Go down Town &amp; to flat with Medicine. Go around to YMCA, see Chappie &amp; Sallie but only stop a few minutes. Go down Rue Paris twice &amp; then Home by long route. Try to write but too interested in Tales of Old Times at 39 Gen.</p>
<p>Wrote Lily.</p>
<p>6 January, Monday</p>
<p>F<br/>Recd letter from W.H. Hughes. We fill in all papers but pending move to Bulford they are not signed. Little "Bo Peep" at Cinema afterwards. I have trouble to get a dog for show. It is a great treat &amp; I fall in Love with Bo-Peep who is glorious.</p>
