<p>usual slow journey &amp; as we proceeded we noticed a large number of Chinese labourers on the line.   They had been imported by the French.  We camped a few miles out from Bapaume, being classed as reserves, but there was no active fighting going on.  During the period up to the middle of July we were trained at a new form of attack which was very trying.  There was a good picture show at Bapaume &amp;amp considering we were within range of Fritz's shells it is astounding that  the enemy did not make a target</p>
