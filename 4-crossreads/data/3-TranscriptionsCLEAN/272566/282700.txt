
Thursday 8 November
Fine all day - Showery towards evening.Run to A.D.M.S. at Scottish Lines Busseboom - calling in Poperinghe to book seats as I did yesterday.The D.D.M.S. Col. Manifold inspected our camp today accompanied by Sir Alex McCormack who is now Consulting Surgeon to A.I.F.  Previously he was Consulting Surgeon to the British Army.
