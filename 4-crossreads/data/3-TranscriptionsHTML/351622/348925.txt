<p>20 November 1916 Mon<br/>Influenza not feeling too good. Dirty work officers Kit boots, Puttees, cars, buses 25 patients took wounded &amp; bad cases C.C.S. slight C.R.S. Very cold 4 shirts on &amp; a sheepskin vest also British warm.</p>
<p>21 November 1916 Tues<br/>Sleep 11 &ndash; Bolten [indecipherable] woke me up to tell me he was leaving [19th] Batt to take Staff Captain job on 7th Brigade [indecipherable] very slack on Front no cases coming in. It is now midnight &amp; we are sitting by a big table playing jack-pot. Started at 8 p.m. Doug Hall &amp; Smith leave for London.</p>
