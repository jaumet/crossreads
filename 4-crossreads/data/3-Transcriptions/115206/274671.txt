<p>April 1918<br/>Thursday 11</p>
<p>Blaney Tronville   D.R.<br/>Weather fine. Went across country to Daours, La P--- Bussey and reconnoitred bridges and position for<br/>retreat if necessary. This was carried out under Cap Mellor.<br/>At 2 pm. We left Chateau for Bussey and stayed at new artillery Hdqs.<br/>Plenty of taubes Gothas and etc. flying around unloading .N.16.C.<br/>Heavy bombardment by Fritz.<br/>Feeling fairly fagged.</p>
