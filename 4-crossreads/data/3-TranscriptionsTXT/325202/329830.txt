 on Tuesday evening reported to D.E. billetted in a brewery at at little village of Tilques about 5 miles from St Omer 
 Too late to get anything hot to eat or drink in the village &amp; as we were cold &amp; famished I told Bob to look after my horses &amp; I would do some foraging. Appealed to a Mlle in the Brewery Yard about making us some hot coffee &ndash; She came back &amp; said yes come into the house
