<p>Don.  Mrs. Deane 30.5.19</p>
<p>Belgium<br/>June 5th 1917</p>
<p>Today is a scorching hot day, and we have just had orders that we are going up to the line to get ready for the big push, at 10 P.M. so I strole down to the village to get a good supply of cigarettes and tobacco for it is rotten to run out of these, after having a good feed of pork and eggs I get back a few minutes before we start on our march which is about five miles, after marching for a few howers steady</p>
