<p>12th May 16<br/>  France</p>
<p>My dearest Irene,<br/>It was very nice to get a letter from you yesterday &amp; also one from dear little Tinys<br/>What a little pet she is Irene! &ndash; Also thank you very much for   <strike>a</strike> nice parcel containing:- a very nice silk hankey, a novel. some cigarette papers, two cakes Cadbury's Chocolate &amp; a nice tin of sweets (from Farmers<br/>Very kind of you old girl I am most grateful.<br/>There is talk of us being granted 8 days leave to go to England &amp; I have written to the aunts &amp; said I would like to pay them a visit one day if I get to England. &ndash; Had a letter from Nancy Norris yesterday They are living in a flat in</p>
