  
 23-3-17 Fri 23rd Rose after 8 oclock this morning & found Dev hard at work on the type-writer had breakfast & put the morning in lying on the bed smoking & reading, had dinner at 12 oclock & then Dev came with me to the station at Kantara East & I just managed to catch the 12.40 train to El Arish, had a cruel journey the trucks bumping & jolting all the way, arrived at El Arish at midnight & got off the train only a few hundred yards from where the Regt is camped; 3 lads from the 3rd Regt came down with me & knew where we are camped
