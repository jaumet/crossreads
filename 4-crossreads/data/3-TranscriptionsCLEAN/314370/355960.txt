
January, 1916Sunday 2Went to church parade & then had dinner w/- dadWe went into Heliopolis Music HallHad supper at 9.30 & turned in about 10.30 pm.
Monday 3All men returned from M.E.F. are paraded with a view of returning to our battalion. But hear no further news except that the 4th Bde is at Tel-el-Kebir
