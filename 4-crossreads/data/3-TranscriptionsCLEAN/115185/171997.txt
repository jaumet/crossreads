They don't care about the individual if he is not a useful fighting man. His health & future does not concern the military, and it is waste of their time to ponder at all over his case. So in one way as far as I can see younger and less experienced & valuable physicians are just as good at this work - of deciding about sending men back to their units or to Australia - and I think that the place for the good man is at home - where the poor unfortunate arrives or even perhaps on the hospital ship - for 4 week voyage is different from the few days to England. Please don't tell anyone - all I say about these things. All eye cases who cannot see with both eyes & all deaf men who cannot hear surely enough to repeat orders are being sent home.
