Rabaul
Flogging of 4 Germans who Floged Mr Cox the Missionary in New Ireland
The sailors were brought from Herbertshohe to witness the flogging all Troops sailors in Rabaul were paraded on the square forming up on 3 sides. a Box had been placed in the middle a wooden stake had been driven into the ground with a pair of leg irons fastened to it. Colonel Holmes gave a speech stating all the facts of the Brutal assault by the four Germans on Mr Cox the English Missionary.
The Facts are as follows. Mr Cox was on a visit to the German Missionary in New Ireland, about Tea Time a note arrived calling the German Missionary away leaving Mr Cox the Germans wife alone presently they heard some one on the verandah outside on Mr Cox going out he met the Germans who accused him of giving information to the British
They took hold of him threw him down flogged him with a stick each of the four in turn giving him blows while he lay on the ground, they left him there took off to the bush where they were captured by G Coy; brought back to Rabaul 
They were tried each sentenced to be publicly flogged The doctor 30 strokes the others 25 strokes with a cane
