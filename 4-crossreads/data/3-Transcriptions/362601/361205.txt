<p>17 May, Saturday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Billy &amp; I walk to Hundcroft in morning &amp; with Reg to village with washing in afternoon. Stay in at night, play cards &amp; write. Go to bed early.</p>
<p>Wrote Dick, Jennie, M.R.L.</p>
<p>18 May, Sunday</p>
<p>F.<br/>Miss parade &amp; with L/C Dunn after waiting Compton-Chamberlain Cemetery we walk to sarum. It is a lovely walk through beech avenue.i arrive at 11 &amp; go walk with Percy &amp; Mr E. Music &amp; chatters after dinner. Marion is very lively &amp; I have never seen her so price of the devil before. She kisses me while I am packing book for Mell, Dol's hubby &amp; Harry Marshall "(Ir.2 a.a.c.). W &amp; F come to station with me. I slip through to train &amp; get Home OK with Vic Wright.</p>
