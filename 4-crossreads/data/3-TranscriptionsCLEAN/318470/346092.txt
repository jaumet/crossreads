
29/5/17Coy. played 20th Div. Gloucesters A.S.C. Rugby League & won 3 to nil.  Rough game.  3 of Gloucesters had to leave field.
30thSec. digging trenches near Villa de Flos.  Other Secs. doing the same.
31stStill on "Strong points".  Doing a little training.
1st 1/5 [17]Nothing fresh going.  Training & Strong.
2ndTraining & diging trenches but having easy time.
3rdSame as yesterday.
4thNothing fresh going.  Having easy time.
5thNo. 2 Sec. played No. 3 & won with two wickets.  Nothing fresh.
6thCoy. had route march today about 5 miles.  Played 8th F. Coy. Soccer & won.
7thWorking on Sports ground & doing camp work & a little training.  Played a Tommy A.S.C. & won easily.
9/6/17Had Div. sports today & 14th F. Coy. came out on top with 47 points to 15th Coys. 36.  8th F. got 23 & Sigs. 22, got Silver Cup.
10thA little squad drill & Coy. Fatigues.  Nothing of importance to note.
11thGood news from North  British - including our 3rd & 4th Divs. & N. Zealanders advanced on a 9 mile front taking a lot of prisoners & a good many guns.
12thStill good news from Front North of Armentiers.  All Enemy counter attacks beaten off.  Lost Soccer to 15th yesterday.
13thHaving general clean-up today preparatory to moving out.
14thGetting ready to move out tomorrow.  Played 15th Rugby today & won 14 to 9.  Some more ground won near Arras.
