<p>he had fought &amp; won, &amp; hanging from the walls are old torn &amp; tattered flags which have been in many a fierce battle, relics of Englands past glory &amp; brave men. I spent nearly 3 hours in St Pauls &amp; then never saw half of it, from there I wandered about the busy part of London, I dodged the heavy traffic alright but got bushed about a dozen times during the day, I had my photo taken yesterday also, don't know how it will come out I am going to have a look at the proof to-day, after wandering about till I was tired I came back to Peel House had my tea, &amp; then went down to Victoria Station to see the Leave train in &amp; to meet a mate of mine who I expected was coming in, a great crowd had collected there all</p>
