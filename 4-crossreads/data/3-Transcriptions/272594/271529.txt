<p>today is very severe &amp; as the day wore on it got worse and at 7 oclock it reached a climax I'm hanged if you could see 10 yds in front for the dust &amp; earth raised by the bursting shells &amp; this combined with the fumes, smoke, &amp; stinking irritating tear gas of which they put over huge quantities made life almost unbearable &amp; almost certainly very precious men were being killed all along the trench &amp; we were all "standing to" for we were</p>
