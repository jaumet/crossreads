<p>Mond. 10th June<br/>I have just been reading the "Gazette des Ardennes" of 1.4.16 which was dropped over our lines by an enemy 'plane.  The paper is printed in French &amp; illustrated and contains discouraging news &amp; articles written up by Germans for the French people who are still living in the invaded department of the Ardennes.  It is rather interesting to read news from the other side as well as your own.  Captured German newspapers &amp; documents sometimes fall into our hands but I don't enough about the language of the "boches" to be able to read them.</p>
