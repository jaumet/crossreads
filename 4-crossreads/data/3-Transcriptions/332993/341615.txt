<p>A Puzzle.  [Reading from the right hand side first]<br/>A man went into a bar &amp; said to the barman &amp; said "Give me as much as I have got &amp; I will spend 6d.   He had a drink &amp; went to another hotel with the same request.  He had a drink and went to the third hotel with the same request.  He had a drink and then had no money left.  How much did he have at the start?<br/>Answ 5 &frac14;  d.<br/>1. 5 &frac14;  double = 10 &frac12; - 6 = 4 &frac12;<br/>2. 4 &frac12;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;"&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   = 9- 6 = 3d<br/>3. 3d&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  "&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   = 6- 6 = 0</p>

<p>How far was it?<br/>The steamer was able to go 20 miles an hour downstream but could only do 15 miles an hour upstream.  So, of course, she took 5 hours longer in coming up than in going down.  What was the distance from point to point?<br/>Odd digits &amp; even.<br/>1 + 3 + 7 + 9/5 = 12.8 and 2 + 4 + 6+.8 = 12.8</p>
