<p>18.4.18<br/>Normal.<br/>The following targets were engaged throughout the night &amp; morning, 2500 rounds being expended:-<br/>1.  Streets, Ville-Sur-Ancre.  <br/>(2)  Cemetery.  <br/>(3)  Sunken Road K.1.b. &amp; d.  <br/>(4)  Area E. of Ville-Sur-Ancre E.25.d. &amp; 26.c.  <br/>(5)  Hollow K.2.c.05.70.  <br/>(6)  Battery Positions K.7.b.7.7.<br/>Enemy artillery except for normal fire on roads about Treux, Buire &amp; Marrett Wood has been quiet.  A few bursts of M.G. fire during the night.  Enemy Aircraft, Nil.</p>
<p>19.4.18<br/>Normal.<br/>The following targets were engaged at irregular intervals throughout night &amp; morning, 1500 rounds expended:-<br/>(1)  Streets, Ville-Sur-Ancre.  <br/>(2)  Area E. of Ville-Sur-Ancre E.25.d. &amp; 26c.  <br/>(3)  Battery Positions K.7.b.7.7.  <br/>(4)  Cemetery.  <br/>(5)  Sunken Road K.1.b. &amp; d.  <br/>(6)  Sunken Road K.1.a.4.1.<br/>Enemy attitude as regards artillery &amp; Machine gun fire has been very quiet.<br/>Aircraft.  A few enemy machines were seen over his own lines.</p>
<p>20.4.18<br/>Instructions received as to possibility of a general side-slip of the line.  New Brigade boundaries given as P.10, 11, and 12, central &amp; D.23 &amp; 24 central, and E.19 central.  This still leaves the defence</p>
