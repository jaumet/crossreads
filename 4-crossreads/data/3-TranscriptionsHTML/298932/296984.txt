<p>Rossignol<br/>Monday 7<br/>Snow and sleet, and other damn things I have not had a bath for seven weeks<br/>Tuesday 8<br/>Battalion taking over tonight from the 20th. Peace tearms floating about they make us anxious<br/>Wednesday 9<br/>A big rumor going round about a big naval battle, with the result in our favour<br/>Thursday 10<br/>Another diary finished and I am hoping to be home before another one is ended</p>
