<p>Tues. 21st Nov.</p>
<p>Mrs Duvas &amp; Miss Cummings the people with whom I have been staying at Hendon just outside London saw me off <strike>at</strike> to the train. Their kindness to me I shall never forget. It was like home. It's rotten leaving to go back to the mud &amp; hell again. However, I shall be alright when I get back with the boys again. I saw some splendid plays during my leave. The Bing Boys and High Jinks being lovely. I saw George Robert and several other accomplished and clever artists <strike>on</strike></p>
