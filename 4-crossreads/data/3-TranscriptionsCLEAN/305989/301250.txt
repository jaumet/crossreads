
Sunday 1 April 1917Church parade in morning.  Wrote [see images for possible Arabic number for 2 and 5].
Monday 2 April 19171st Regt. v F.A. & M.G. combined draw no score.
Tuesday 3 April 1917Played 1st Field Troop lost 3-0, two other games in progress.
Wednesday 4 April 1917Brigade Races in the Wadi did not go:  on picquet at night.
Thursday 5 April 1917Clarrie came back off leave.  4th Brigade came through:  saw all the boys:  getting ready to move eastwards.
Friday 6 April 1917Left El Arish in morning coming through El Arish & passing 4th Brigade, proceeded to Shiek Zowaiid, camped the night.
Saturday 7 April 1917Left Shiek Zowaiid early & got to Rafa about dinnertime & saw places familiar in the scrap, arrived at Khan Yunis about 4 o'clock:  & camped after watering up.
