After coming back from fatigue I went &amp; had a good bath in a pond quite close to our billet, for the day was very hot &amp; the sun bonzer 3 of us got into a row over the saluting yesterday I don't mind saluting a soldier, but hang these flash cold footed crowd that hang well behind the firing line &amp; have all the skite, we have some wasters among us. Early this morning an old pig came into our billets &amp; every one being asleep she grabbed a bloke by the ear talk about a row you ought to have seen the dust &amp; straw flying.
