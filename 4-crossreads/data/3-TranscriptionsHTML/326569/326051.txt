<p>having to report back at Wareham on Sunday.  After tea went Perham for bath.</p>
<p>Thursday, 17/5/17<br/>Caught 10 a.m. train from Ludgershall &amp; arrived Waterloo, London about 12 noon.  Went Kings Cross by Tube &amp; booked bed at Y.M.C.A.  Then wired to Uncle Charlie &amp; Uncle Will about the leave.  Walked right down Grays Inn Road to Holborn then took Tube to the Bank &amp; then went to Basinghall St. to Petersen Boesens but there were no parcels for me.  Then went along Cheapside to St. Pauls which I looked at &amp; inside for few minutes.  Then went</p>
