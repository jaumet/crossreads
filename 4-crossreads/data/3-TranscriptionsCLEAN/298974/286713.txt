We ate some ships biscuits and waited on the wharf station for the wharf gates to open at 9. All wharves & railway stations are carefully guarded in England. Then we set out on a 3 mile march for our destination, Southampton Rest Camp on the common. We all marched like vets for the blooming town turned out to see us. Everyone wanted to shake hands, so we were given the order to march easy.
