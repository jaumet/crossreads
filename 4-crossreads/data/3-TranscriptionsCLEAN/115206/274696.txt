  
 May 1918 Monday 6 
 Bussy-la-Daours Had a run out to 19th Bdges Hdqrs. also to 150th Bdges Hdqrs. Had a rough spin owing to condition of ground and horse. Rained very heavily all day Had Ginger dressed by V.serg. and he looks a little better Ken Gunn came across to Btry Hdqrs. He is looking very well. Looked them up whilst on a run to Villers Bretnoo. .
