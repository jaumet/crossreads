<p>Got roused down last night by a heavy storm of rain.</p>
<p>This evening just after sunset I went along "forrard" to have a look at things, there was a most beautiful sunset all purple gold red &amp; pink &amp; the reflection was shadowed on the still water, it was one of the prettiest things I have ever seen &amp; was a worthy rival of the Gallopoli sunsets, but they don't last long in these latitudes, darkness follows very fast after the sun disappears &amp; then all the pretty clouds turn into black ones   when darkness set in the water that was being churned up by the prow appeared to be a  pretty</p>
