Havre with Morison.  Tram to the Ocean Front, Quite a nice place.  Several very nice hotels now used as hospitals or for other military purposes.  The architecture quite reminded me of Mosman.  It is not quite so prudish.  We walked back to town &amp; polished off 2 meals.
Thursday 24
Was cook this afternoon as it was the regular mans days off only got through about 60 Galls of tea &amp; coffee.
