<p>On Tuesday 4th December we had about 12 inches of snow, It looks beautiful on the ground, but it makes the air very cold.</p>
<p>On the 7th I was sick with a sore throat &amp; Influenza, I went to the Doctor, he gave me some medicine and work.</p>
<p>The snow that fell on Tuesday has just dissapeared. The food is as usual, (bad.)</p>
<p>On Sunday December the 16th we had more snow which continued to lie on the ground till 31st of the month. On two or three days last week we had a very heavy frost, and the early morning of all day, we had thick fog. The food was bad as usual.</p>
