  
 September 1918. Wednesday 11 
 In Reserve and at Hebecourt Weather rotten. Heavy rains throughout day and during evening. Relieved by 42nd Battery  4th Div, battery proceeded via Cortigny, Le Mesnil, Barleaux and Flaucort  to a position near Hebecourt. Spent the night very comfortably. Fritz bombed as usual some fairly close.
