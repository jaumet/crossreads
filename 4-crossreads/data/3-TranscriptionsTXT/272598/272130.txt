Thursday 16.11.16Arrived back in Camp at 3 AM.  Turned in & slept till fairly late.  Sent out after lunch to M.M. dump to draw heaters; again very cold.
Friday 17.11.16No duty.  Troubled with Neuralgia of late; not feeling too well.
Saturday 18.11.16Surprised to see a very heavy fall of snow this morning; weather exceedingly cold of late.  On duty in quarry loading limbers with stone.  Rained all morning.
Sunday 19.11.16General duties about camp.  Expect to be going out again in a few days to do a turn in the line.
