<p>February 1918<br/>Wednesday 13</p>
<p>Messines<br/>Spent last night at l'aison with Mackenson a very decent fellow. I'm afraid he was not impressed with my manners. He heard me address an officer in 29th Battal as Jim. I remained in ignorance of "Jims" identity until closer observation revealed the fact that Jim was a major.<br/>Went to new O.P. with Gun as guide and brought back water.<br/>Pioneers planted a machine gun near our dugout. Precaution against hostile aircraft <br/>Weather wet.</p>
