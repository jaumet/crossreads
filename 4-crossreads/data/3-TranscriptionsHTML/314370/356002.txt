<p>March, 1916<br/>Sunday 26<br/>Last of 'em marched out &amp; we are glad to see their backs.<br/>spend afternoon &amp; evening up dad.  Go to Helmia Pictures</p>
<p>Monday 27<br/>All New Zealand Instructors marched out to rejoin their units.<br/>About 4 pm my chum &amp; I went into town to see some friends of his.  at 8pm we dined at the New Obelisk Restaurant.  went to Kursaal &amp; walked home getting into bed at 2.30 am</p>
