
8 - 20.7.1915All decks are used all day till about 5 pm to excise and parade the troops.Everything is very interesting that goes on - so different from any other ship I've been on - even different from a hospital ship. I did some sewing & reading & washing today and was inoculated for Cholera - in the chest wall. The spot is very painful now and one has been promised fever and discomfort for 36 hours. I wonder the various germs or antigerms or dead boddies or whatever they are that I've had injected into my body
