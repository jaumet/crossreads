<p>April 1915<br/>Thursday 23<br/><u>Weather</u> still <u>cool</u><br/>Several fresh transports and destroyers have arrived. One of the destroyers shows signs of a recent encounter. Many Australian troops <strike>are</strike> were on one destroyer. Evidently practicing getting on and off</p>
<p>Friday 23<br/><u>No</u> change in <u>weather</u>. Seaplane from one of the warships very busy. It has been flying about all day at a great pace. I with Nick Carter was mess orderly for the day.</p>
