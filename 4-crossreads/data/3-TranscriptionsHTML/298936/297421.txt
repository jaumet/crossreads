<p>September 1915</p>
<p>Wednesday 22</p>
<p>Supposed to land at 11 a.m. [indecipherable] Mudros which is on the eastern side of the island but got under way at about 4 oclock.</p>
<p>Had to search out the Aust. A.M.C. headquarters &amp; then reported the arrival of 16 men to the [indecipherable].</p>
<p>Temporarily attached the 1st Austral. [indecipherable] Hosp. for duty.</p>
<p>Miller Robson &amp; Morris got about 50 letters each which made me jealous.</p>
<p>Had a walk through the village (a few rough huts) in the evening.</p>
