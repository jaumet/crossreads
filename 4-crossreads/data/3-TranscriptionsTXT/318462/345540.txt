Thursday 23/5/18Heavy clouds & much cooler this morning trying to rain, the usual heavy Bombardment almost all night. Fritz over bombing Amiens & surroundings last night got a dump also This morning at 5 saw 11 of them but the anti aircraft guns soon sent them home they are only brave when no one can see them such as night time There were 6 of ours coming to meet them & the cowardly swine turned tale & ran  no relief yet have done 11 days here now fancy Pat shelley will be looking for another job soon. deferred Pay 50-15-0 owe me 52/611-30 pm just going on duty at 12 to 3 very cold wind blowing our guns playing up a hell of a row. We were shelled here this afternoon 3 or 4 times got quite unhealthy at times.
Friday 24/5/18Cold & wet, back to winter again leaving here tonight for another Posi a few hundred yds away, dangerous spot Cpl Rex & others killed there last timemy two windy's still out. deferred Pay. 50-16-6.
