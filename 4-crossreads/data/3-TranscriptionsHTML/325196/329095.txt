<p>23-1-16<br/>Sun 23rd<br/>Church parade this morning conducted by Sol Green &amp; I attended  I think the Reverend gentleman must have been sent to war by the Church of England on account of him not being a "wowser" as he is rather partial to a wee drop occasionally &amp; suits a certain part of the men.This afternoon we had nothing to do but kill time till stables; the Corporals of C.Sqd have a tent to ourselves; After tea most of the lads put the remainder of the evening in while the daylight lasts boxing &amp; wrestling received a parcel from home today in excellent order; We have been getting much better food the last couple of day so our complaints have done some good</p>
