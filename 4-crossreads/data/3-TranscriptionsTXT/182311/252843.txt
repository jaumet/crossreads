   There was a period of nearly four weeks, when snow lay on the ground continuously & when standing water in ditches, ponds & shell holes froze to a depth of fifteen inches & I have seen blocks nearly two feet through.
   The river (River -) froze right over & this fine flowing stream fifteen yards wide ceased to be an obstacle & could be crossed on foot at almost any spot thereby shortening many of our little trips which now involve considerable detour to cross bridges.
