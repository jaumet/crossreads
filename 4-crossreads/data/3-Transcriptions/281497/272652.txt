<p>The boys are all wondering what is the matter for I have been sent for 2 or 3 times to Hqrs to day, &amp; several signallers have been round to me with message's I do hope it gets through This morning we had physical exercise &amp; a short parade after it, &amp; then finish for the day. It is very hot this afternoon &amp; there is not a breeze blowing a leaf about</p>
<p>This afternoon I had charge of a fatigue party down at Headquarters I had to check all the wounded packs &amp; see that they were correct a pretty tiresome job to have anything to do with at all, for you have to be so careful about them &amp; see that no mistakes creep in.</p>
