The state of affairs at present existing proved conclusively that the methods used in the Valley & elsewhere viz Canilisation & oiling stagnant pools with Kerosene did to a very great extent keep it down. Tuesday Oct 8 We have received good news from all fronts & we all think the war will end this year. It is rumoured that all 3 yr & over service will shortly be going home.
