<p>Sunday 9 September 1917<br/>Sky Pilot parade:  swapped jobs &amp; "slipped";  wrote Nos. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 2].  Swimming a good part of the day.</p>
<p>Monday 10 September 1917<br/>Preparing for inspection by G.O.C., got all gear clean, on picquet at night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 11 September 1917<br/>New Zealand band gave a concert in evening at Y.M.C.A., very good.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12 September 1917<br/>Sports all day.  (Schoolers Benefit).  Saw Gudge Brown:  concert and prize giving at night.  Brig. in great form.</p>
<p>Thursday 13 September 1917<br/>2nd Brigade gave us a concert at night, very good.  On Sanitary fatigue.</p>
<p>Friday 14 September 1917<br/>On "Pump fatigue", exercising horses &amp; picquet at night.</p>
<p>Saturday 15 September 1917<br/>Concert by Pyjama Pianos very fine turnout.</p>
