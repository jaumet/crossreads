 Replied to 3/5/17 
 "Lyndhurst" Strathfield 1st May 17 
 Dear Mr. Fry We were much upet on opening the morning paper to read of your sad loss. 
 My family &amp; self deeply sympathise with you in your great sorrow &ndash; as you know Dene was a great chum of my only son Eldon when in Camp at Liverpool and right through to Salisbury plains, keeping together up to the last moment, when the separation came on departing for France.
