Several big mines were fired by our Engineers at the commencement of the action and the report of the explosion was tremendous. We heard that a number of Turks were buried beneath the fall of earth where the mines were fired. 
 Sunday 1st August 1915. This morning we had a fair idea of the casualties of the engagement and on our side about 20 were killed &amp; 80 wounded. In our own unit we were fortunate and escaped without a loss. How the enemy fared we could not say to any definite extent but their losses must have been considerable.
