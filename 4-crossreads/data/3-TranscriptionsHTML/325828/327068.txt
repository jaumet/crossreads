<p>Army forms, &amp; of course there is plenty to do.  Since we are  under the British Army, (the R.F.C Branch of it) one thing has struck me very forcibly &amp; that is the Red-Tape in connection with the Army.  I verily believe that in time the whole show will be choked with it &amp; perhaps then they will see the folly of their ways.  In what little time I have had to spare, I have been studying &amp; working at  my books on "Theory of Flight"   Last Monday with several others, I was posted up as passing my tests, &amp; consequently classed as 1st Class Air Mechanic:  My correct address now, is<br/>No 6359,<br/>1st A/M J L Waterhouse,<br/>29th Reserve Squadron,<br/>Australian Flying Corps<br/>Shawbury,<br/>Shropshire<br/>England<br/>Don,t forget   <u>"Australian"</u></p>
