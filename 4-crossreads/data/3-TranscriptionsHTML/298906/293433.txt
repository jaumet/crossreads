<p>7th Today we had a day out doing tactical work &amp; planning how to attack &amp; defend positions.<br/>8th. This morning all lectures &amp; in the afternoon we each fired 12 rounds per man from Lewis guns out on the range.<br/>Tonight we are going out on a bombing stunt, which will Keep us out till about 2 oclock. So you see they have a very practical way of teaching.<br/>9th. Well I never laughed so much as I did last night for a long long time.<br/>We moved off from our Barracks about 10 oclock carrying rifles 10 rounds blank, 2 small bombs each, the majority of the men</p>
