Was still able to sleep on deck as we were in the horse boxes and so sheltered. Inside on our deck however the water got in and wash about 2" deep before they got the drainage pipe clear of socks that had been sucked into it.
Thursday 21stWeather good again and Australia again sighted. We were all disappointed at not calling at Fremantle. The last of the land faded away in the late afternoon.
