 25 April, Friday 
 F. We march out at 9 am. I take the party to Bulford. I see Florrie &amp; take bag to Hobdens. We catch 12.27 &amp; I go to Mrs Edmonds &amp; meet Marion's Bloke. We arrive at Front. I meet dozens of LH acquaintances. Billy &amp; Les Nettleton. I go to No 13 Hut. I go to Pictures &amp; all a man pestering a girl to get married &amp; she wishes him dead. I am quite happy until then. 
 Wrote M.R.L. 
 26 April, Saturday 
 F. Walk to P.O. to post letter. 
 I go to Y.M.C.A. We play Billiards. I play for Bucks dance &amp; it is OK.
