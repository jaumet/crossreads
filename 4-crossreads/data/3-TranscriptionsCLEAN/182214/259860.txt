  
 The Colonel gave us permission to get food & not stay away long he was a sport indeed & we saw a little of Marseilles whereas the rest of the boys except the Tramsport section who stayed there four or five days saw nothing. I might add here that we were a full unit again as we had another C Section formed at Ferry post, They did their drill all in the blazing heat & they had my sympathy I did not see much of Marseilles to say how I liked it but it seemed to be a fine place Scotty & I had a good feed. & I started to put my best school French over on them & cannot say it was successful. at 9.30 we were  we  all ready to entrain when the 29 Batt headed by the
