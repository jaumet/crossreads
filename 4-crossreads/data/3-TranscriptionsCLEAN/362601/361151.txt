
29 January, Wednesday
F & iceRecd letter from MRL bad one. I did not sleep last night for thinking & all the pretty ones could not make me forget Her. recd letter from Hilda, Marion Edwards. I am dopey & sit by fire talking to A until 8.30 & then go to bed. E goes to Pictures. I receive my kit bag from Salisbury.
30 January, Thursday
F & iceI work hard all day & rush up to Pictures at night. Come Home to W.O's flat & chat until late. Recd letter from MRL good one.
