<p>Tuesday 3 October 1916<br/>Fixing up camp all day.  Going to be a very ceremonial place this I think.<br/>Canal jolly nice to swim in.</p>
<p>Wednesday 4 October 1916<br/>Exercising horses in morning;  cleaning saddles in afternoon.<br/>Parcel from home.<br/>Wrote to Mother.  Forgot to [indecipherable]</p>
