<p>warmer than usual.  Playing cards &amp; writing to pass the time away.  Very few patients coming through.  Hot drinks at No. 1 post.<br/>9th December (Saturday)<br/>In the early hours Fritz shelled the road behind Delville Wood pretty heavily &amp; set fire to an ammunition Dump.  The effect looked very fine as the atmosphere was thick with fog.  This Dump isn't very far from No. 1 post (The loading station).,  A raid took place during the night</p>
