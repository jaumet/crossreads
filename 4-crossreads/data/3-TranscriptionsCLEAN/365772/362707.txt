  
 "Egypt 7.2.16 My dearest Sister, Again our mail seems to have gone astray we havn't had a mail from Aust. for over 3 weeks, our Brigade is separated from the division for a time so conclude the mail has gone to the latter we will pick it up when we rejoin. I think I thanked you dear for your parcels & Xmas cheer which you sent along but in case the letter has gone astray I do so again. Our correspondence is still under a very strict censorship so you will have to be content dear with brief letters until the embargo is lifted.
