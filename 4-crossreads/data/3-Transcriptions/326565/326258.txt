<p>Tuesday 30/11/15<br/>Exceedingly hot &amp; Sultry.  All men paraded in full marching order.  Too busy to dress for parade.  Very quiet day otherwise.  At noon travelled 5939, leaving 2357 to go.</p>
<p>Wednesday 1/12/15<br/>Mess orderly for the day.  Discovered lock &amp; handle of my white kit bag had been stolen &amp; contents scattered</p>
