<p>1917 MARCH</p>
<p>9 FRIDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; Sunny in the morning</p>
<p>but snowing &amp; blowing</p>
<p>very cold in the evening.</p>
<p>Cleaning up round the stables</p>
<p>in morning &amp; exercising horses</p>
<p>in afternoon. There was a Hun</p>
<p>plane over us this morning at</p>
<p>only about 500 or 600 ft &amp; they</p>
<p>could not get him. Got long</p>
<p>letters from Linda, Marj, Jo,</p>
<p>Eileen &amp; Mother. Sorry to hear</p>
<p>from mother that Aunty</p>
<p>Florence had died.</p>
