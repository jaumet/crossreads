
June 1918Tuesday 11
Battery W.Lines.  near Pont NoyellesWeather fairly cool, not feeling exactly brilliant. Went for a swim with P.L.C. Charlie Adey got up, andseems to be a little Better. Went down with trench fever during the afternoon, - feel pretty rotten, Percy Gray being also effected, he has the samecomplaint.Saw John Harvey on the road to his home. Expect to go out for a spell on the 18th. only a rumor so far
