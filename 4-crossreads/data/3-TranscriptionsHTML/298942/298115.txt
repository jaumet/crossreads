<p>Artillery slack this morning.  Fritz has put one or two on the road between here &amp; the cottage.</p>
<p>Rogers just arrived back from taking orders about relief to the various posts.  He has brought back a rumour to the effect that Fritz is supposed to be putting over a barrage at 2.30.  It is now 2 p.m.</p>
