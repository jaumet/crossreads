<p>June 1918<br/>Friday 28</p>
<p>Erronville. &ndash; On the road<br/>Weather glorious , fine and clear. All kinds of rumours prevalent as to moving up again.<br/>Orders to prepare for evacuation in a half hours time have arrived.<br/>Packed Smithy's kit and my own and set off to bid my friend au Revoir.<br/>Left Battery W.L. at 8.45 pm and arrived at [indecipherable] just before midnight. At 12 midnight  we were still on the road near Longpre.</p>
