22 Dec 16Run out of the bad weather last night. Picked up with some Destroyers in forenoon & came in & anchored at Rosyth. We were the last cruiser to get to harbour. Coaled ship & oiled ship in afternoon.HMS Yarmouth reports losing 3 men & 3 boats in heavy seas yesterday. HMS Southhampton had part of her bridge swept away.

25 Dec 16Xmas DayAnchored at Rosyth. Spent a pretty quiet Xmas. We have a new Captain on board. He came on board yesterday.
