 May 1918 Sunday 5 
 Bussy-la-Daours Weather clear, but very muggy. Cleaned up all my gear for saddle and my equipment. Boys are all very well and Ginger looks a little better. Paraded to M.O. for his inspection and had his legs dressed. Paraded before Muller in evening and he dished out the usual yap. Received a letter from Cousin and certainly liked the news
