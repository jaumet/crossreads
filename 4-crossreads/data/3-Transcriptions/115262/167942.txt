<p>Sunday 24 June 1917.</p>
<p>Reveille 6 a.m. but the &quot;planes&quot; who were flying as early as 3.30 a.m. kept us awake.<br />Fatigue work nearly all day, preparing <s>for</s> the area where our complete unit is to be in training. Filling&nbsp;sandbags and erecting tents. Were kept away from church parade. Had to stand by as special fatigue but was not required.<br />NIggers who encroached on our parade grounds were chased by the police on horseback.<br />Wrote one letter 1st since arriving in Egypt</p>
