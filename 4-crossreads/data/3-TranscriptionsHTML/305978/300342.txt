<p>8th/<br/>Arrived at Railhead at 10-3 am after travelling all night. New Zealand soldiers camped here also Egyptian camel corps.<br/>Wattered horses at well the water was putrid.<br/>We then started to ride for our destination &amp; did about 25 miles &amp; camped near well gaurded by Indians.<br/>We watered horses &amp; had tea Up again next morning at 2am. Saddled up &amp; away upon the track again.<br/>I was in a party told off for the rear gaurd; wagons began to get stuck up &amp; delayed us from advancing for sometime, arrived at</p>
