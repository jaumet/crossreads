The fact well ever remain that though there may be men of the rottenest class, who are capable of anything at all, except, fair, clean, fighting, or straight honest work, still they are fighting for their country and, there are men fighting beside them, who are not only good soldiers, and Patriots, but they are men any way you like to take them .. and the German nation taken on the whole, has undoubtedly proved to the world that organisation, cohesion, and, a little of the Iron hand thrown in, will go a very great way, towards success.
