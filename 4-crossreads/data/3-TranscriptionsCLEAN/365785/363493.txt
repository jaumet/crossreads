 Australian Imperial Forces. No. 2 Hospital, Training Depot, Seymour, Victoria 21.viii.15 
 Dear Mrs. Read, Your very welcome letter containing picture of the youngsters and the kind congratulations arrived yesterday.  I did not lose much time in volunteering when I got through but I did a little hospital work and then volunteered here in Melbourne as nothing was doing in Brisbane in A.M.C. at the time.
