  
 45 August 25th The main argument brought forward was for an equitable redistribution of orderlies, as at present the distribution is most unfair, there being 1 ord to 3 officers & in our room 2 ord to 20 offs. There are other such cases. 
 Concert held tonight - mostly French actors. 
 Dr was summoned to our camp tonight, as one of the officers took a very bad turn - He was taken to Hospital in the town - 
 August 26th (Sunday) Spent morning in Garden reading in the glorious sun. Recd Books that Mary sent from Censor - they are novels &c. Played Bridge - 
 Heavy storm again tonight -
