<p>1915<br/>Dec. 21 Tue.<br/>Egypt? &ndash; the land of sunshine &ndash; the land of the Pharoahs &ndash; the land of hidden mysteries of long ago et. etc. is supposed to exercise a fascination over some people but I can't say that the said fascination has gripped me.</p>
