<p>The Craven Hotel<br/>Craven Street<br/>Strand<br/>23 Nov. 16<br/>My dear Sister,<br/>A letter from you to-day dated 9.9.16 best date so far.<br/>Very sorry indeed to hear of D. Irons sad end, especially following so closely on Ralphs death - I havnt been to see Mrs Pollock yet &amp; dont think I will now as she was frightfully cut up over</p>
