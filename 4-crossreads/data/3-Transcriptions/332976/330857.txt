<p>May 1917<br/>the remainder of the company are billited, (having move up there about four days ago) but after a short stop, returned to Faveruil, about 2 Km further back, to where the company are to come in the course of a day or two.   Faveruil is a small village badly knocked about by Fritz &amp; about 3 &frac12; miles from the front line.   We have a number of howitzer batteries just in front of us, but the majority of the artillery is further up.<br/>There was a "hop over" this morning whith successful results by the 15th Brigade, in which over 270 pisoners &amp; an enemy strong-point were captured yesterday, Vaux was shelled heavily <strike>in which</strike> &amp; a couple of our lads were hit but the pioneers suffered more heavily loosing about 40</p>
