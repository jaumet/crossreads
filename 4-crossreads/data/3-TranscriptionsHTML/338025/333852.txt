<p>Thursday 22nd November 1917</p>
<p>Stweenwerck<br/>All teams harnessed up and ready to move out at 9am.<br/>9.30 pulled out from camp with four mule team. Arrived at our destination Pont d'achelles near Steenwerch about 1pm. Good stables and good huts to sleep.</p>
