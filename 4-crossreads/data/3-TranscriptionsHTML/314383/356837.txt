<p>Feb 26<br/>Trip fairly monotonous.  Arrived  here 1am for coal apparantly stay here all night.</p>
<p>Feb 27<br/>Left 5pm.  All the coaling is done at sea some distance from the land.  Huge barges convey the coal to the ships side &amp; natives carry it aboard in baskets carried on their heads.</p>
<p>March 1st<br/>Apparantly well up the Persian Gulf, as the water is more greenish in color &amp; apparantly shallow.  Russian classes continue which develop</p>
