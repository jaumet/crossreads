<p>1915<br/>Oct. 10 Sun.<br/>Spent the afternoon instructing 2 Patrols of Armenian Scouts in first aid for a Display to be given by the Troop &ndash; The language problem presents a little difficulty.<br/>Went to the Kursaal (variety show like Tivoli) at 6 p.m. &ndash; Splendid programme.<br/>Had good dinner &amp; then train to Gezira.</p>
