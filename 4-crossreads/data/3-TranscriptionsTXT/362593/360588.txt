
Wednesday 14 August 1918Recco Turkies aerodrome Nazareth, A'Fuleh aerodrome, Jenin aerodrome.Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 3 h. 10 m.Gunned a train from 200 ft., within 5 miles of Jenin (26 carriages [?]), gave it hell.  Fired 550 rounds.  Met 6 Hun scouts over Jenin (2 of us).  We drove them all down and shot up two of them.
Thursday 15 August 1918
