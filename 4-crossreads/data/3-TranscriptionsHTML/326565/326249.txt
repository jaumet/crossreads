<p>Friday 26/11/15<br/>Rained fairly heavily during latter part of last night &amp; early this morning.  Rather busy morning, good number of lads suffering from sprains, &amp; cuts caused through bottles bursting in their hands. Large number of bottles containing soft drinks burst while being opened.<br/>Rumour spread that we would cross the line at 3.45 pm.
