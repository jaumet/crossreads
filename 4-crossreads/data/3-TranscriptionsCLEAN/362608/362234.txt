  
 Bulford  9/3/19 My dear All  I have arrived back from the Island and a glorious time, and had visited Grandmother, Aunt Jennie etc. who all send their fond love. Harry left today for 15 days also and will arrive tomorrow night, and I am quite sure he will have a good time.
