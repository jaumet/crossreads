<p>18 August 1916 Fri<br/>Did a lot of writing during the day. Jim Murphy one of the old originals evacuated sick. One of the best. Went to the Rest camp after tea &amp; met Len Pearce one of the boys from the old school. He looks well &amp; is in the artillery. Asked Mr. Minter to get me in the [text continued at top of page]</p>
<p>19 August 1916 Sat<br/>Saturday kept going all day. Spent several minutes in Madams estaminet &amp; after I had finished work I went there again &amp; enjoyed a few with Bell &amp; Sharpley. Went for a walk with Keith it was beautiful walking along there just before dark with all the harvest fields in full swing</p>
