<p>27th.   Dave Mason took a small patrol out last night but never saw any of the Hun's.</p>
<p>This morning I took an inventory of all our Posts &amp; bombs &amp; 303 ammunition.   Our mortar battery landed 2 bombs almost in our trench this morning.   I reported the matter to the Capt.</p>
<p>From where I am writing this I can see "Messines" hill quite plain it is a fine position for it overlooks the country for miles around.   I think the "London Scottish" took it last year, &amp; about 200 yards behind me is the famous International trench so called on account of its being taken &amp; lost so many times by the British</p>
