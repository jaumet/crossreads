<p>the storekeeper had been robbing them also passing bad coins upon them , which was soon stopped before it be came very serious</p>
<p>15.5.15<br/>Ted growling because they were not paid to day  the reason being they were stony broke, and could not raise enough money to go to Cairo</p>
<p>16.5.15<br/>Today I was Battalion Orderly Sgt &amp; when I was going on my rounds I saw a native jumping into a well, which holds all drainage from the showers &amp; wash drain so one can imagine what filthy people they are about the camp. Anoth thing which they do is picking out food</p>
