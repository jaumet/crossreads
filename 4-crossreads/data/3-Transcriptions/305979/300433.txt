<p>"What would we give to our beloved?<br/>"The heros heart, to be unmoved,<br/>"The poet's star turned harp, to sweep ,<br/>"The patriot's voice, to teach &amp; rouse,<br/>"The monarch's crown, to light the brows<br/>"He giveth His beloved, sleep,</p>
<p>"O earth, so full of dreary noises!<br/>"O men, with wailing in your voices!<br/>"O delved gold, the wailers weep!<br/>:O strife, O curse, that o'er it fall!<br/>"God makes a silence through you all<br/>"and giveth His beloved sleep"</p>
