11th. Went into the firing line last night relieved D. Coy. things are very, very quiet about here so far, such a difference to the "Somme" the only thing we have to contend with seem to be bombs a few shells sniper's.
I have charge of a post named "Tara's Hall" she is a bombing possy one in the event of an attack I have orders to hold at all cost
The Germans are about 75 yards away we can hear them working away of a night, driving stakes mixing mortar, etc. we are on the defensive here for the present, so as to allow us to get on with our work get things ship shape for the winter, the Huns seem quite
