<p>31st August (Thursday)</p>
<p>Couple of our planes brought down.  We are now using High Explosives in the air.</p>
<p>1st September (Friday)</p>
<p>Building.  In evening went into Estaires.</p>
<p>2nd September (Saturday)</p>
<p>Should have been sent to Baths at Sailly this afternoon but the 15th Colonel mixed things up &amp; we were therefore beaten for our usual bath.  Some of our unit are doing traffic police duty in Estaires.</p>
