<p><u>Jan. 13th</u></p>
<p>My 22nd Birthday - Our Party breaks up, some returning to their Units - others to 3rd Div. Artillery Concert Party while Harry, Frank, Les, Rupert &amp; self leave for Aust. R.E. Workshops - where we are to reinforce a small entertaining troupe.&nbsp; Daily, good news is being received in regard to Australian &amp; English Frulough.</p>
