<p>Wednesday Feb 7<br/>Same old Drill all hands anxious to get away. Boxing contests at Stadium in evening.<br/>Thursday Feb 8th<br/>Riding exercise again better display than previous day.<br/>Came a cropper over log fence. Feel like having meals off Mantelpiece.<br/>Friday Feb 9th<br/>Very enjoyable Route march to Trawool some eight miles from camp along a country road shaded by trees &amp; the Birds whistling merrily. Across the bridge at Goulbourn River and on arrival at Hotel had lunch &amp; impromptu Concert spending a very pleasant couple of hours.<br/>Arrived back at Camp about 6 pm.</p>
