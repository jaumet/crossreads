 July 21st Arrived Takan Tepe.  Rather a pretty place for Persia in that it has avenues of poplars leading to the town.  Derives its name from a knoll just outside the town 18 miles.  Major Chaildecott joins us from across country. 
 July 22 Arrived San Jud a long weary trek of 28 miles 
 July 23rd Arrived Sain Kala 17 miles, pitched camp on outskirts of town hear a large River. 
 July 24 No sign of our friends.  Buy over telegraph operator who agrees to bring us all telegrams to look thru'.  Gov visited camp today.
