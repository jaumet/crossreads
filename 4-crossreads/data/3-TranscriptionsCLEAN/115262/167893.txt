
Sunday 6 May 1917.
On leave. Home during day.
Writing wallet given me by Mother in evening before leaving, Wallace's from Bayswater over for dinner & left at 6.15 pm.
Caught 6.10 train from Lyndhurst. Stayed 11/2 hours in Dandenong & then got 8 pm train to Melbourne.
Wrote note & turned in 11 pm. at Fed.Coffee Palace.
