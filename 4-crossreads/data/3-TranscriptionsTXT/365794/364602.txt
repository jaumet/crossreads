 Friday 19th July 1917 [1918] Very friendly from Mrs. T.A. Price, also one from M. Crookey. Cairo 5 p.m. with S. Kinnaine.  Groppi's &amp; home in garry, lovely sunset &amp; "child" sings.  End of a perfect day on the picturesque road back.  Duty 8 p.m., these pts. are very nice and I find Major Mullens, an R.C. Padre, is nephew to old O'Shaughnessy &amp; on 21st April '18 buried poor Dick Rutledge.
