14 November 1916 TuesThis has been a bad day for my section. Poor old Johnny Wain 18 years old has been Killed also Jake Harrison. Three of my pals Geoff Wood, Ack Adams, & [Jenno] have also been badly wounded. Scotty also stopped one through the arm. Our Sgt Christmas, Cotter & Carrol have all stopped one too. All have been evacuated. Very [text continued at top of page]

It was Clarry Loten who had stopped one in the nut. He has now his commission. Captain Rush was killed to-day.
