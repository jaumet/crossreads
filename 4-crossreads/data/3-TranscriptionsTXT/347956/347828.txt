 17th.12.14 
 in good time &amp; arrived back in camp well satisfied with our night out (continued) Signalling close to camp all day, helio being the principal instrument used. a very quiet day, also a perfect one.  after tea Stan &amp; I came to the writing tent to scribble a letter or two, to put in time.  paper &amp; envelopes are give to us free gratis, also pens &amp; ink is supplied in any quantity &amp; a couple of natives to wait on us, so we are not doing too bad.
