
<p>285</p>
<p>FEBRUARY 1917</p>
<p>20 TUESDAY</p>
<p>Raining all day &amp; its very</p>
<p>muddy &amp; sloppy everywhere.</p>
<p>Cleaning harness ect all</p>
<p>day. Out exercising horses</p>
<p>before breakfast. Capt Campbell</p>
<p>inspected the stables in</p>
<p>evening &amp; everything passed</p>
<p>satisfactorily. We had</p>
<p>to turn out in evening &amp; take</p>
<p>ammunition up, being section</p>
<p>on duty. Got parcels from</p>
<p>C.S. Stores &amp; Mother &amp; Aunty Florence</p>
