26/8/15
Cultivation flocks among thick growth on irrigated land, palm trees occasionally. From canal masts of native boats just visible. "A louer" signs very frequent en route. Plenty of water wheels usually in small grove of large trees, with the unfortunate cow or bullock on its never ending journey 
Bab el Hadid barracks garrisoned by Australians, built by Kitchener to dominate Rly station fortified no windows loopholes in masonry always in state of siege. Reached Pay office and then walked back to Bank. En route tried at chemist for rubber sponge for Major Smith am told all run out. At French place bought aluminium pocket cup. Round to Opera square and got sponge at Italian drougherie. Passing the shop was an albino
