I like them both very much - especially Sister Twynam. Her sister Mrs Gordon Weshe has been allowed 14 days here to see her sister & brother. I do wish Mother & Father could come home and stay here a few days on their way to England - "Wouldn't it be a dream" quoting the chorus of a song that has been made up and sung at the theatres here - supposed to give the wishes of the Australian & English with regards manners & customs, in vogue here in Cairo amongst the military. I'm going to get the music & words for Jocelyn & Rosemary. I heard it sung in duet by two Australian privates - in the characteristic lean too attitude the piano being the wall or post.
