<p>always some Chap in each hut who is the special clown or in some way is more noticeable than the others In my present hut theres is a chap "Bluie" O,Brian who is very funnie especially when he comes in about 11 oclock at night He has been in some of the Concert Parties &amp; has a lot of funnie sayings &amp; can imitate a Gramaphone quite well</p>
<p>Wensday 12th June<br/>On Draft for the Oversea Training Brigade, Sutton Veny Medical inspection Etc</p>
