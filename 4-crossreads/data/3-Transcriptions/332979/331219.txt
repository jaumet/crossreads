<p>held in our Mess Room &amp; it was crowded with the boys, <strike>the Officer</strike> The Lieut Colonel, Chief in Command &amp; his Officers was present also two Red Cross Nurses which were the first time we had the pleasure of their company, &amp; they were two pleasant looking ladies &amp; caused quite a stir amongst the Boys. During the Church Service the Chaplain took for his lecture the beginning of things &amp; quoted the life of a Soldier &amp; asked us to think of our folks at home &amp; to play the game &amp; act up to our traditions, after the Service the Lieut Colonel gave us a lecture on Dicipline &amp; expressed his pleasure of our conduct &amp; trusted that we will carry on in the future as we have done since we have been on board.
