 The  weather  temperature has risen considerably & most of us are beginning to feel the heat pretty badly. 
 Very little of interest on board to break the monotony. The band always draws an appreciative audience. We have had one concert on board for the men so far but there is no indication of another one being promoted so far. 
 We have a Sports Officer & Sport Committee on board but they are apparently not a business firm.
