<p>writing is going on &amp; the chaplins are kept busy censoring<br/>The yarn now is that we have a case of Meningitis on board &amp; will be quarantiened at Fremantle A medical examination for sore throats yesterday YMCA distributed lolies &amp; cake today</p>
<p>Monday 25th<br/>Passed Albany this morning &amp; ran N. all day The lights of Freemantle showed up just after dark. We anchored quite a long way out one stretcher case &amp; the mails were taken off &amp; we left again about 10 oclock</p>
<p>Tuesday 26th<br/>All sight sound &amp; smell of Australia was gone this morning It has been a great disapointment to many not going ashore. I was very glad we got a mail off<br/>A bit rough again</p>
