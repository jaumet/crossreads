<p>17th First fall of snow, very light just like frozen drops of rain. Weather very cold.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;  <br/>Social evening in our hut for band members as a farewell evening also a recognition of <br/>"Martha" Jones' Birthday.<br/>Each man contributed 1/- towards the provender which was laid out &agrave; la banquet   <br/>Several Songs and speeches were given throughout the evening by W.O. Flynn, <br/>Sergeant Bignall &amp; members of the band.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<br/>The whole broke up at 10 o'clock after spending a very enjoyable evening.</p>
