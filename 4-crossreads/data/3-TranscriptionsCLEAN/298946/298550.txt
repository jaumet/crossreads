The dump itself was just a patch of black earth covered with blackened shell cases, across which [indecipherable] earth lay the twisted skeleton of the munition train.  The road we were following crossed the railway through the train so to speak  for a portion of it was on our right while the main part of the train & the dump were on our left. That part of the train which was on our right was only partly damaged; half of one truck was blown up & in the remaining corner unexploded ammunition was laid bare.
