<p>Sat Oct 28th<br/>Had a good nights rest the first for seven days &amp; we are just resting nothing more. There is nothing as to our movements.</p>
<p>Had another look at the mill today it is worked by a horizental Turbine with a six foot fall of water.</p>
<p>This country tho somewhat flat is picturesque.</p>
<p>We pass the time playing cards yarning etc.</p>
<p>I went to see my little French madame &amp; madamoselle &amp; wrote some letters there.</p>
<p>Our transport landed here last night the weather is wet &amp; cold.</p>
<p>Sun Oct 29th<br/>The day again broke rainy and watery.</p>
<p>We are paid to day the first for a month.</p>
<p>Am writing to catch X mail.</p>
<p>Mon Oct 30th<br/>Weather improving.</p>
