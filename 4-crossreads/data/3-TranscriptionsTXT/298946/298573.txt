The rest of the story up to my return from 2nd Field is of transition from the cold of the muddy road to the misery of the mess hall; &amp; thence to the warmth of the estaminet where Lt. Lowe had his office; from this warmth, helped by what Lt.Lowe called a spot &amp; what I called a drink, to the wonderful music &amp; food of the Sgts mess; thence from that happiness to the happiness of another form experienced in a brisk walk in a sate of exultation after the music, in the cold if bleak atmosphere.
