 Thur. Mar. 9th -16 Made Cook's Fatigue for the day.  Peeling spuds carrots etc. 
 Fri. Mar. 10th -16 1st Division Artillery assembled 4 P.M. on account of presentation of medals to a driver and a gunner in recognition of their bravery in saving an officer's wife at Alexandria. 
 Mon. Mar. 13th -16 All batteries of 2nd Bde. out for battery manouveres on Saharah desert.  Clouds of dust and boiling hot sun 
 [Page 22] 
 Tues. Mar. 14th -16 Gun-laying tests all day. 
 Thur. Mar. 16th -16 Warned to be ready to leave at any notice. 
 Fri. Mar. 17th -16 Commencement of Gun  firing practice.  Excellent results.  6th Bty. easy winners. 
 Sat. Mar. 18th -16 Going out for more practice and when about half way, were ordered back to pack up to leave same night for Alexandria. 
 Mon. Mar. 20th -16 Orders cancelled for leaving, everybody disappointed.
