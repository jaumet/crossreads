<p>very likely, our artillery accompanied the storm of rain with one of shells they vied with the Heaven's in the violence of their cannonade, &amp; between the two of them they made a very good showing, ours was the heaviest bombardment I have seen our guns do, they fairly cut loose &amp; went mad   Old Fritz never even replied I think he was dumbfounded with the violence of it, there are great breeches in his parapet everywhere you look, &amp; he got a very hot time behind the lines for our heavies were in action &amp; firing as fast as they could  he is beginning to find the Australian gunners pretty hard nuts to crack &amp; very constant &amp; they are not afraid to reply to their batteries no matter how solid they are,</p>
