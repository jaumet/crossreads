
<p>311</p>
<p>MARCH 1917</p>
<p>18 SUNDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; nice &amp; warm. In the</p>
<p>stables cleaning up all</p>
<p>day. I was stableman for</p>
<p>the day. There were dozens</p>
<p>of planes about mostly</p>
<p>ours, but a few Huns came</p>
<p>over flying very high, but</p>
<p>they were soon hunted off.</p>
