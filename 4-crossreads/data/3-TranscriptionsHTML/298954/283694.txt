<p>The M.C. announced that "that favourite dance of His Majesty's lower-deck, the Bum-Dance, will be added to the programme.</p>
<p>The B.D. consists of ragging vigorously for 4 bars &amp; then endeavouring to hurl one's partner across the room with one's posterior!</p>
<p>Alas!  We were all tempted and fell, &amp; amid hilarious shrieks one saw a whirling kaleidoscope of blue &amp; silver French uniforms, green Italian ditto, khaki, navy blue &amp; not a little gold braid thrown in here and there, all demonstrating with verve and abandon "that favourite dance of<u> His Majesty's lower deck</u>."</p>
<p>Fri. 22nd Nov<br/>A sad day for poor Harry.</p>
<p>Only 350 leave tomorrow, length of captivity replaced rank as regards passports, consequently all captured later than November 1917 are to remain.   "Our Mc", Captns Bruce, Burgess.   &ndash; Cave &ndash; are to "hier bleiben".</p>
