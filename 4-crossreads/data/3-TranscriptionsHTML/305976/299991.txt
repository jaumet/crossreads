We rode all night through mud &amp; water &amp; at day break Monday came across a patrol.<br/>Monday April 1<br/>The Turks at this time were practically on us in large numbers &amp; it was only the darkness which pulled us through. 3 of our boys were caught through going back for some gear.<br/>We rode on till we reach Esault situated half way in the hills of Moab &amp; had breakfast Bully &amp; Biscuit &amp; pushed on for Shunet Nimron arriving about 2 pm. We were so worn out that we slept all afternoon &amp; night without waking</p>
