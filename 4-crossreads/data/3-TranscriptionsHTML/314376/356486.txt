<p>175<br/>20 men as a rule. Scattered here and there were the dead bodies of the Germans.</p>
<p>We passed quite close to a body of Germans presumably who may have been a patrol. When we got back it was to be told we were to move back for what turned out to be 4 days rest in the sunken road.</p>
