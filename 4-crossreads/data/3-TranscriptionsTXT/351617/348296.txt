 By now we were all getting used to the climate &amp; as black as the niggers themselves, faces, arms, &amp; legs, as we only used to wear a short sleeved shirt &amp; short trousers nearly to our knees. The boys knew me as "Corporal Abdul" as I used to wear a small Army issue forage cap &amp; had gained the exalted position of Lance Corporal in my reinforcement. I drank more water than I ever did in my life before, but later on we were but on the allowance of 2 pints to wash shave &amp; drink &amp; I can tell you many a time 6 of us washed in 2 pints of water, to make it last.
