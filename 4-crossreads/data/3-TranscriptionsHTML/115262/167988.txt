<p>Thursday 9 August 1917.</p>
<p>Camels in marching order &amp; a trot round the hills near camp. Finished up early this morning at 10.45 a.m.</p>
<p>At 2 p.m. went to 14 A.G.H. to see the boys.</p>
<p>Went to Cairo on leave with Ted Holden &amp; Tom de Gruchy. Had tea in Cairo &amp; then a tram ride to the Citadel just about dusk. Came back to city &amp; went to Groppe&#39;s &amp; Liptons &amp; back to camp at 9.45 pm.</p>
<p>Saw where one hole was made &amp; damage done by aeroplane raid by Turks last November on Cairo. Since then the Turks have been driven back 125 miles.</p>
