We marched out of Boulogne on Tuesday the 12th & took train at 2.45 p.m. after a 31/2 mile march with those awful heavy packs.  Ellis & I got corner seats in a 3rd class carriage which was ever so much more comfortable than the cattle trucks that the rest of the Boys had.
