
Thursday 30 December 1915 Arrived off Adelaide about 5 am. Newspaper to hand. No special news.Some officers went on shore to the City Coal, 300 tons, being put on board. The S.A invalides disembarked 18 
Friday 31 December 1915 Left Outer harbour Adelaide about  10.4 am.12 noon 22 miles 1'- 56" - 0"'  Fine day. All going well Back Stairs passage and Bass Straits not boisterous, with a cold head wind.
