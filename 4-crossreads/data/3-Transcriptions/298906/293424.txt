<p>scent which came from the wild flowers &amp; trees was glorious, it made me think of home &amp; Spring time in Australia, &amp; from afar off came the notes of the bugles wafted gently to us by a gentle breeze which our- <strike>brows</strike> cooled our brows &amp; made us feel content, &amp; so we gradually reached the Camp thoroughly tired out but satisfied. After reaching Camp I went down &amp; had a good shower, wrote my diary up &amp; then went to bed.<br/>27th Have been real crook all day long, I don't know what is the cause of it perhaps the exertion of yesterday was too much, &amp; that combined with the thundery weather may have upset me, I felt weak all over had a rotten headache &amp; could not eat a single thing.</p>
