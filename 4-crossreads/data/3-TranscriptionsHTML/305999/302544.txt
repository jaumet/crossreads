<p>Eperlecques and marched 10 miles to Arques where we entrained  we where travelling 12 hours then we disembarked at St Requier  and marched 3 miles to [indecipherable] that was on 21/10/16</p>
<p>23/10/16<br/>We left above and marched 5 miles and then we got into busses driven by French soldiers  we did about 20 miles up to the somme  we billeted the night in a village called Dernan Curt[Dernancourt]</p>
