 Tuesday 7 September, 1915 
 Received letters from home &amp; papers. 
 Letters from Gibby, Mrs Parish (2), Jessie Chilsholm, Ollie Turner, Phyllis Floden, &amp; Mater &amp; Ida. 
 Went down &amp; had a yarn to Bob Dryden. Cheered his heart by giving Tobacco, Matches &amp; Cigarette Papers. His battalion are having a rest down Monash Gully. 
 The 24 th  Batt arrived last night as support to the 8 th &nbsp;who are so weak in numbers that no man has a chance of being out of the firing line day or night
