<p>The enemy have brought a very clever man to match our Commander-in-Chief (Allenby), news having just arrived that Falkenhayn has established his head quarters at Jerusalem. &nbsp;I am of the opinion that he will meet his match in General Allenby though.&nbsp; Buried all our killed in the Australian burial ground at Beersheeba.</p>
