1918   February
7 THUR  Little rain. Fixing up
billet. Went to Au Progres
at night. had a good
time. Rec. letter from Tense.
8 FRI  Raining on & off all day.
Home 6.30. Rec. pay 40. Francs
7. passes through for Blighty
Writing at night. Warm.
9 SAT  Fine day. Sports in
aft. Very quiet.
