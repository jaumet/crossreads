<p>New Zealand mounted men holding portions of it, &amp; men are being raked up from practically everywhere, they had a lot of gas this time in the line &amp; nearly all the lads are talking a little hoarse &amp; husky &amp; quite a number have been gassed &amp; are now safely in Blighty<br/>On arriving back this afternoon I found quite a pile of letters waiting for me<br/>McManus went to Australia today how lucky is he,<br/>It has been very cold all day tonight light rain has set in <br/>Major Jacobs came back from England with our draft today he has been away a long time now but he is a good fellow for all that &amp; very popular with the boys &amp; all the Battalion</p>
