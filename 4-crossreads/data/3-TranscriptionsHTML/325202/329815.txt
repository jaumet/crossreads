<p>the two Richardsons, Reg Rhodes &amp; Jack Eddison (now Captain) &amp; Paul Steinthal (now Petrie) Major O.C I stopped till dark &amp; did not get back to our lines till 11 p.m.</p>
<p>Aug 1 (Tuesday) Bonneville<br/>arrived here from Senlis yesterday afternoon about 15 miles back, a very hot day which the horses found trying, Passed through the villages of Contay &amp; Herissart en route. Various units of the 1st Division are here waiting for reinforcements to make up their strength again. We have to go 1&frac12; miles to water our horses (that means 3 miles each journey 3 times a day)</p>
