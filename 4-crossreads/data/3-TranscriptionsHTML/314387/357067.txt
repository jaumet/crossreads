<p>Sunday Dec 16th<br/>Went to Le Havre afternoon and night<br/>Monday Dec 17th<br/>Wen through gas in morning nightime went to Le Havre<br/>Tuesday Dec 18th<br/>Went to Bull ring in morning afternoon paid forty francs<br/>Wednesday 19th<br/>Did nothing<br/>Thursday 20th<br/>Left base two 'clock marched to Le Havre</p>
