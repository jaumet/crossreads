<p>Wednesday 9 October 1918</p>
<p>Thursday 10 October 1918<br/>Recco returned.  Pilot Lt. McGuinness, in air 20 m.<br/>This is the last trip I had on active service &amp; is the only one on which McGuinness &amp; self have had to return through engine trouble, except on one occasion when a dressing plug blew out.  Trouble overheating, water trouble.</p>
