<p>1917 Thursday 5 July</p>
<p>Queen Mary visits the hospital at mid-day stays 3 minutes. No fuss made. French hang out flags thats all.  <br/>Jock MacB &amp; self go for walk after tea to Happy Valley &amp; Hidden singer, hoeing cabbages. We have a little impromptu operatic duet. 3 in one refreshment. <br/>A beautiful calm evening &amp; a most enjoyable stroll.  Call at Mde Hunch at Wimille raspberries &amp; sugar.</p>
