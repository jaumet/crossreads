 Sir; 
 I have the honour to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of 26th September forwarding me a copy of your despatch to the Minister of Defence, dated 23rd September, on the subject of the terms of the surrender of German New Guinea. 
 2. I wish to thank you for your courtesy in forwarding the same for my perusal. 
 3. I have no doubt that the future will prove the correctness of your opinion. 
 I have the honour to be 
 &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Sir, 
 your obedient servant, 
 (Sgd.) George Patey,
