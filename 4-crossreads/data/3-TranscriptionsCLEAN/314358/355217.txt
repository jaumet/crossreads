As the party of sappers work the "Knoor-up" of shrapnel and burst of high explosive tare the sky to ribbands and pound and smash anew the soil already in blackened ragged holes and ridges.
The party is under a charm this day, which is unbroken by disaster, but almost there is a stream of wounded passing down the sap from the front where shells and shrapnel and machine guns make Hell on earth.  Streatcher bearers tend them.
