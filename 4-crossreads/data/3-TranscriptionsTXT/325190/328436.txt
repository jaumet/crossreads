 Saturday Oct 20th 1917 Landed at Le Havre yesterday morning &amp; marched some six miles to Harfleur where we pulled up at Rouelles camp. Passed doctor; issued with tin helmet &amp; two varieties of gas helmets; also had vapour bath, all before tea.  Several of last draft still here. "Trish" of Tisbury fame, &amp; Naim &amp; Dunstan are here, but Harry Millard has gone up the line to a C.C.S.
