<p>1915<br/>May. 1 Sat.<br/>This morning had my first swim in the Baths at Mena House followed by a sun bake &ndash; splendiferous!<br/>In the afternoon was lucky enough to get a few hours off for a run into Cairo &ndash; had haircut, watch mended etc.<br/>The rush of wounded patients was continued to-day &amp; motor ambulances brought in another 230.</p>
