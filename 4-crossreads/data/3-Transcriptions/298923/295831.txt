<p>14 June 1918 Friday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Very hot<br/>Our washing water is from a Malarial Swamp, sticky, brackish &amp; won't lather, &amp; scarce at that but we go back to our horses again in 5 days-<br/>I know my writing bad but you will know it is because my pen is so often bad and I scribble under many difficulties at times-<br/>X Joe is good to keep the old home in repair &amp; sorry Day has no one to keep her again in the kitchen I note all you say re old Sir Sam but would not like to take Dolly away till end of August now-<br/>Love from your son <u>FW</u>X</p>
