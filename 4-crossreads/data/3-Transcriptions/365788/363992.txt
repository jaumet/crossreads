<p>16 Sept 16<br/>My dearest Irene<br/>Just a hurried scrawl to say I am well &amp; we are out of the Trenches again. Thank you for two nice parcels you sent Chocolate, Books, Washer, Tooth Brush in one &amp; Choc Hankeys Books in other.<br/>You are very good to me old girl. I am kept pretty busy in my new role but find it interesting.<br/>How are you? Got my letter I hope. How well the Button Day did! Am sorry to hear Mrs Yead is so poorly. Will you give her &amp; the Dr my love. Capt Embleton was M.P. in</p>
