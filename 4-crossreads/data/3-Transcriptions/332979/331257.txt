<p>having 2 Rooms down Stairs &amp; 2 Rooms up Stairs they were very sociable People &amp; made us quite at home. After a Splendid Tea, 2 more Soldiers came around also 3 Young Ladies &amp; Mrs Taylor having a Splendid Piano we had a lot of Music. Two of the Ladies played the Piano also Miss Bella Taylor had a good voice &amp; Sang Several Songs, during the Evening we cleared the Dining Room &amp; had several Dances. These Ladies all had some of their People in the Firing Line. Mrs Glover has her Husband up <strike>at</strike> in German East &amp; Mrs Wilkins has 2 Brothers one has been Killed &amp; the other wounded in France &amp; Miss Roberts has her intended Wounded &amp; in Hospital in England. After Supper we all had several Games &amp; at 12.20 am the Evening was brought to a close &amp; After Saying Au Reivior &amp; Thanking</p>
