<p>Monday 25<br/>A daylight ride to "Achiet Le Grand" with Lieut Small returned via "Bihucourt".<br/>Tuesday 26<br/>Won a fight last night and then went out on a midnight mission with the two horses, got bushed<br/>Wednesday 27<br/>As it is now sure tis a grand old war, nothing what so ever doing on this front<br/>Thursday 28<br/>Fighting again put up a great go the cheers and laughter of 2000 men was good to hear I won</p>
