<p>Mitchells Creek Station<br/>Jany 2nd 1868</p>
<p>Mr. Arthur Fry</p>
<p>Dear Sir<br/>I am in receipt of yours of the 29th Decr.</p>
<p>I will look out for your horse should be come here and send you word.</p>
<p>I sent your Boxes to Guilds some time ago, Hinchills has forwarded them to you no doubt.</p>
<p>I have sent a reference to your father by this post for you it ought to have gone sooner but I have been kept very busy.</p>
<p>I am yours faithfully,<br/>Robt. Kerr</p>
