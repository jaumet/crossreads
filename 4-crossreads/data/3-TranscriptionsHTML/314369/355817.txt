<p>23 April 1915<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Weather getting hotter<br/>We do very little drill now just passing away the time</p>
<p>1915 April 24<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Work   &amp;  play as usual<br/>Half Holiday but no money so the men stop   &amp;  have tea in camp and then go and see the free pictures in town.</p>
