 April 2, 1916 Sunday 
 Sohag 
 Revielle as usual. 
 Nothing doing today, cleaned camp &amp; had rest of the day to ourselves, an aeroplane came down to us today &amp; when she alighted at the aerodrome every nigger for miles went down causing a bit of a disturbance with their eagerness to see more of it, so they had to be drive away. 
 Chas Cox &amp; myself went for our usual bus ride (Sunday) &amp; went out towards Girga at a 30 ml lat, beautiful riding &amp; a glorious evening. 
 Onions are being gathered, this years crops are enormous, they grow them in amongst their cotton &amp; thus secure a double crop. 
 Wrote letters home, Nell, Dunc  &amp; then played cards. 
 Intend making trip to Makum tomorrow if possible. 
 Bed 9.30. 
 April 3, 1916 Monday 
 77 wks from home. 
 Revielle as usual. 
 Horses &amp; men went out as usual. I went out at lunch time. 
 Sgt Weir died this morning &amp; is to be buried at the monastery this afternoon at 3, he died with enteric &amp; double pneumonia, they intend holding an auction sale of his effects shortly. 
 Bobby left us tonight for Seraphim so now we can expect Smith down any day. I put in my application in Bde H Q's to join School of Inst at Zietim but think I will have to wait till the next &amp; vacancies are filled. 
 Perritt gave a great demonstration of his powers as an officers standing a warship was going down the canal &amp; his men were at attention he still left them there &amp; a staff Sergt pointed it out to him: Oh wait till I ask my uncle whether I can dismiss them or not.
