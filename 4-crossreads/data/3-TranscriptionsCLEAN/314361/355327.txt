The following are a few facts taken from the book "Cairo and its Environs" Cairo is a 11 1/4 miles, as the crow flies from the fork of the Nile. Situated 300 3' N lat. & 310 21' E long, in the plains below the right bank of the Nile & the western slope of the Mokattam hills. The town is rectangular in form running from NE to SW & measures over 4 m by nearly 2 m in width. The population of Cairo before 1908 may be estimated at more than 600000 550000 of which are natives, & 50000 Europeans.
