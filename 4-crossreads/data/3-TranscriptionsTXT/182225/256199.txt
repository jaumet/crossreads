 1916 &nbsp; SUNDAY 13 &nbsp; FEB. 
 Fine. We all went out for a launch picnic in afternoon up the Lane Cove river. M r &nbsp;Lloyd hired a launch &amp; we left about 2 oclock, picking up about a dozen more people between here &amp; Milson&#39;s Point. We had tea just above Fig Tree bridge &amp; &nbsp;went up about a couple of miles further after tea. Linda, Jo, Miss Nichol &amp; Self sat&nbsp;on roof coming home. Went back to camp at night.
