<p>5<br/>I saw in the papers that our boys had done good work at Rafa; it is rotton luck me missing out like that.<br/>On the 6th I was sent to Field Ambulance and that night five parcels came to the regt. for me; I was feeling pretty bad so I sent over and got Bill West to draw them for me and I told him to open them and take any fruit out as they wanted it so and I knew I would get good tucker in hospital; Bill did this and brought me all the notes in them; so I will be able to write and thank</p>
