
Regis. Had some afternoon tea in there, then caught 4.30 pm train to Melcombe Regis Stn, Weymouth. Went to YMCA on the promenade & sat in front of fire for time then walked back to camp, arriving about 6.30 pm. Very Cold. On way to Portland saw a torpedo factory of Whiteheads, & on way back to Weymouth saw old ruins of a castle or residence of Henry 8th. At Portland also saw over a dozen large tanks, like gasometers, containing oil fuel for the Navy.
18/11/18Recd letter from Uncle Charlie.
19/11/18Recd letter from Dick Lusc, also 2 parcels from Muriel, 1 containing 1 pr Sox, Housewife [sewing kit], and 2 bxs Old Gold Chocolate, & other contg Cake, Fruit, Sweets, Sugar, T.Paste, Soap, Coffee & Chocolate. Not so cold.
20/11/18After tea strolled into Weymouth.
21/11/18Changed some of my French money at YMCA tonight.
22/11/18After tea went to pictures in YMCA at Westham Camp. A Charlie Chaplin picture was shown. Beautiful sunshine day, not nearly so cold.
23/11/18Wrote to Uncle Charlie. Put in application to be deleted from D 35 list. After tea went into Weymouth.
24/11/18 SundayWas paraded to camp commandant re deletion from roll of D35 - he is recommending same. Put in another application to return via America at own expense, as last application was lost in Orderly Room. Raining this evening. Wrote home XX
25/11/18Not nearly so cold. After tea went to Westham YMCA.
26/11/18After tea saw a conjuring show at Westham YMCA. Raining today.
