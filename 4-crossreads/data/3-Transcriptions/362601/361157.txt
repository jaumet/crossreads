<p>10 February, Monday</p>
<p>F &amp; ice<br/>We stay in until 7.30 &amp; then go up for bath &amp; play cards with Dick &amp; Reg until late. I sleep in pyjamas first time since leaving Bulford. We have supper at the Flat.</p>
<p>11February, Tuesday</p>
<p>F &amp; ice<br/>I go to Sarum by 2 pm Train. Do shopping &amp; go to No 13 after 3 vain attempts. Me &amp; Albert &amp; Miss Farris got to Dance. Ian very lively &amp; entertain quite a lot. All Brummy dancers but I have a good time although I am sick. I play, dance, start games, wait &amp; clean up cups etc at Dance.</p>
