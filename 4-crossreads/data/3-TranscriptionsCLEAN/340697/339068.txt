In building a pier in the harbour here a small steamer was sunk to make a breakwater 21.6.15
Left Embros on El Hihira and took up our position in firing line the following day 7.7.15
The turks dropped about 40 8 inch howitzer shells in 2nd Bn trenches today causing about 10 casualties.
H.Q. sigs made full strength today 3 B. Coy. sigs joined including myself 3.7.15
