<p>Wednesday 30 August 1916<br/>Nothing doing all day.<br/>Asked at Canteen re camera but nothing heard of it.</p>
<p>Thursday 31 August 1916<br/>All of us down swimming again, had pretty good time.  Taube poking round again.<br/>Jolly good concert in Y.M.C.A. in evening.<br/>Tons of papers from home in evening.</p>
