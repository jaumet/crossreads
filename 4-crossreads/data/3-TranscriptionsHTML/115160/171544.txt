<p>First the young men defiled, then the Allied Soldiers: English, American, Italian, Polish, Slavonie - they played national airs.</p>
<p>Behind the Allied soldiers and the young men, there were little tanks which provoked the applauses and the admiration of the crowd.</p>
<p>Though there was a rainy weather the boulevards and the streets, where the filing off must passed, were crowded.</p>
