<p>To Mrs GO Hawkins<br/>10 Norman Av<br/>Hawkburn Australia &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No1<br/>Victoria General France<br/>Australia</p>
<p>1654 Sapper Hawkins G.O.<br/>7th Field Cop<br/>Aust Engineers</p>
<p>[In right margin] Much love to all</p>
<p>Dear Beatrice</p>
<p>Am in one of our own hospitals near or rather at [sensored]<br/>Left the trenches, two days ago. Nothing serious only 'trench feet'</p>
<p>Am booked for Blighty I believe</p>
<p>Military movement are so uncertain that postal arrangements keep astray as usual, preventing me getting letters</p>
