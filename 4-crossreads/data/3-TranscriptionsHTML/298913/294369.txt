<p>After our little jaunt round the citadel we went to the Gizeh Gardens over the other side of the Nile, &amp; from there we went to the Y.M.C.A. for tea.<br/>After tea we strolled around Cairo as usual until time to go back to Ma'adi,</p>
<p>5/3/16  Sunday  Spent in glorious loafing in &amp; around the tent.</p>
<p>6/3/16 &amp; 7/3/16   Signalling all day with lamp practice at night.</p>
<p>9/3/16   Go for a walk with Eric through native villages to avoid Fat Wild whom Eric owes some dough.</p>
<p>10/3/16   Fat Wild turns up but gets no money</p>
<p>8/3/16   Pay day &ndash; silver runs short first &amp; then it takes 2 days to pay a few hundred men. Glorious disorder &amp; confusion</p>
