<p>The incessant rain we are having made the soil very loose. But before long we examined the remaining part and being satisfied it was alright turned in again</p>
<p>Sun Dec 3rd &ndash; 16<br/>We all set to work this morning and made a first class staircase (with the aid of sandbags) where it had fallen in night before.</p>
<p>Wed Dec 6th &ndash; 16<br/>An event happened today which must have its place in this book:- The SUN shone for 3 hours.</p>
