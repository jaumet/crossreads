<p>March, 1916<br/>Tuesday 28<br/>Got up fresh as daiseys, had a wash &amp; breakfast then went up to see dad.<br/>After dinner went out w/- an old Pal from Liverpool Harry Grove<br/>sent &pound;10 home &amp; had a real good time<br/>turned in 11.30 pm.</p>
<p>Wednesday 29<br/>Very quiet all day reading &amp; poking about<br/>Very hot &amp; sultry after sundown cools off<br/>Play cards w/- dad &amp; the boys<br/>Write letter &amp; up my Diary<br/>Turn in at 11 pm.</p>
