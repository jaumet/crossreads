<p>24<br/>4 were from home, most of the others from unexpected sources. For breakfast I bought &frac12; lb steak &amp; took it across the road to be cooked.</p>
<p>6/7/16<br/>Had a good fly round in a car to-day looking for a place that was hard to find. Called &amp; had a yarn to Charlie Ferguson, gave me a tin of tobacco. Saw Eric this morning at the Coy &amp; had a yarn.</p>
<p>There was a gas alarm last night at &frac12; past 12 but it didn't reach us. I had my helmet handy in case it was a dinkum alarm.</p>
<p>8/7/16<br/>Have had a couple of good spins in a car these last couple of days it was great. Saw Norm Janson yesterday had a great yarn.</p>
