<p>August,1918<br/>Tuesday 6</p>
<p>Villers Brettoneaux. Bty Position<br/>At 4.30 am Fritz commenced a duty shoot with gas and 4.2s also 5.9s. Major Dugan 114th How was killed.<br/>After dinner Fritz settled down a little but shelled spasmodically with a H.V.4.2 gun. I hope to have the pleasure of viewing same one of these near days. <br/>Fritz bombed us with no detrimental effect during the evening he shelled us fairly constantly. Our guns "duty shooting"all night</p>
