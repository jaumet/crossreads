  
 6 July 1915 Tuesday 
 Inlying piquet. Our new commanding officer takes over this morning he is a 16th Bat officer by name Col. Tilney V.D. Stand to arms 3 am to 4.30  &  then sleep till about 8 am (Wednesday) 
 1915 July 7 Wednesday 
 Write a letter home Very quiet all morning but get a few shells over during the afternoon. Very hot. Hear that 16th lost four killed   & about 30 wounded in crossing on Monday.
