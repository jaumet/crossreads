22nd. There was a rather amusing little incident occurred during the progress of the concert last night, about half way through the major gets on the platform starts going very crook about a remark he overheard some fellow pass about him while the concert was on, he challenged him to come forward prove his cowardly lying statement, he said he would have a go at him in the Ring also if the chap was game enough, he was in a tearing rage could scarce contain himself, after he got down the R.S.M of the 12th Battn got on the platform added his testimony about the major being a soldier etc, he appealed
