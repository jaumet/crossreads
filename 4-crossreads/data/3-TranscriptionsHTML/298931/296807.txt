<p>[November, 1916.]<br/>Friday 3<br/>The Duke of Connought was here today along with a lot of WHISKY complexioned generals<br/>Saturday 4<br/>The flow of wine gin whisky ect is immence if the huns drink more well they are alway drunk<br/>Sunday 5<br/>Been to church, and now in an estaminat wondering how long and what the future as in store for me<br/>Muriel a letter<br/>Elsie likewise<br/>Mary a cable<br/>Oh dearie me</p>
