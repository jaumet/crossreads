 1915 June 21 Mon. Hospital inspected in the afternoon by Surg. Gen. W.D. Williams, Director Medical Services, Aust. Imp. Force &ndash; a big fat josser. Lumsdaine &amp; I have started having cold showers before going to bed as well as first thing in the morning. We also go for walks round Giza before breakfast.
