They are wonderful things there's no doubt, they have a crew of 8, an officer, N.C.O & 6 men, they are proof against bullets, but a direct hit from a shell knocks them out, & as they can only travel so slowly over the rough, shell-torn ground, this often happens. Well, it started off with a jerk, came to a shell-hole, quietly went into it, & up again the other side, then crossed a trench, & came to a steep bank & stopped. It then simply raised its nose, climbed the bank & went on, travelled right along a trench, & could have poured fire into any Huns there. It came to some small trees & just knocked them down & went on & knocked out an imaginary enemy strong post with its 6 pounder guns. On the level ground they can go pretty fast, look just like huge caterpillars plodding along.
