<p>Letter writing &ndash; A stroll along the Rue do Rivoli and into the Gardens of the Tuileries (originally the site of "tileries" &ndash; hence the name) &ndash; Beautiful lawns gardens &amp; walks. Also fountains and statues &ndash; Must be seen in Summer to be fully appreciated &ndash;<br/>In the Evening (by request), a few of our "Coves" give a short entertainment at the Corner of Blighty &ndash; and then accompanying Fred Reade I visit Monsieur &amp; Madame Griffie</p>
