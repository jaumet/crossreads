We still continued our simple life all this time the weather was getting hotter & the canal saw us very often having a bathe in its cool waters, all good things come to an end so we said good bye to the canal & Indians on Feb 26th after spending 2 1/2 months in that quiet but pleasant spot. We entrained on the light railway & later on to the larger trains & arrived at Tel-el-Kebir 7.30 same day.
