  
 B Section moved from Popes post to Mule Gully. Ken entering our dugout is hit by cap of 75 
 Thursday 18 bearing Russels Top Received orders to move off the peninsular Arrived Lemnos early morning & found our camping ground at West Mudros late that afternoon. Stones in galore 
 Saturday 25th Christmas day received billies 
 Sat Jan 17 Visited [Forbianos] room of skeletons Monday 3rd Left Lemnos for Alexandria by S.S. Manitou Saturday 8th January arrived Alexandria.
