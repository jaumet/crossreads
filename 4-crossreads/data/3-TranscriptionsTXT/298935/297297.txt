and there was plenty of joking and fun going on.  Returning to the deck we found that the new moon had set and the sea appeared like a sheet of oil.  These conditions were ideal for our purpose.  The ship was slowly moving ahead all the time and now and again the dim shape of a destroyer would loom up for a second.  Not a sound or a light was to be noticed anywhere.  Soon dawn appeared and we were able to make out the coast of Gallipoli.Around us were many transports shepherded by warships.  Then came the rattle of musketry ashore and we knew that the show had commenced.  Every minute more rifles swelled the strain and then the heavy guns of the battleships chimed in.
