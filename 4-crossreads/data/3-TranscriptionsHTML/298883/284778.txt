<p>nearly 12 miles a long way ahead of our own infantry and entering a town found a Corps Hqrs. Staff at <strike>work</strike> Mess in a House on the main street.  Our armoured car adventurers machine-gunned them through the open windows killing all the occupants.  Spying round for fresh victims they came across a transport column and slaughtered the lot, then tore off at about 40 miles per hour <strike>for</strike> to rejoin our own advancing infantry and to report their news of the enemy.
