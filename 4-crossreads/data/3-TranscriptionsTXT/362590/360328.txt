 5. The other day, somebody blew into the orderly room, &amp; asked to be allowed join the 12th Reins, whereupon I commenced to inform him that we were not a haven to shelter any wanderers that might happen along. In the course of the ensuing conversation. I learnt that he was one of uncle's rifle-club men &amp; wished to attend a depot school. He said he had a letter from uncle, so I took him on straight away with the intention of giving him an examination to see what he knew, &amp; making him a sergeant. 
 I will strongly recommend him to the new O.C. 
 Remember me to everyone 
 Your loving son 
 Keith
