<p>August,1918<br/>Tuesday 20</p>
<p>In front of Morcourt, Bty Pos<br/>Weather glorious, fine and clear, plenty of balloons about. Bty did very little shooting 112th shooting all day long.<br/>Walked across to Bdge with Smithy and repaired Q.A. line en route. Purchased supply of canteen stuff, pretty good to the palate.<br/>Received a letter from R. Ladd.<br/>Weather towards evening splendid.<br/>Bty shooting pretty frequently gas shells.</p>
