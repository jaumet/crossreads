<p>We repulsed him with heavy loss, the whole ridge to our right being covered with Germans in disorder. The 17th &amp; 19 then held the front line while the 20th  fell back into a communication trench to meet another counter attack. He did not force an assault &amp; that finished the contact:<br/>We bobbed off about 4 a.m. &amp; won our objective at 10 a.m. on the 31st, we remained there all day, &amp; all night, &amp; withdrew on 1st Sept.</p>
