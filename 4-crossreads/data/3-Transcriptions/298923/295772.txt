<p>16 April 1918 Tuesday<br/>Still Bethlehem<br/>No- Dolly's sister-in-law is in England; likes Mrs Cathy F.  Only week end Cables bad-<br/>Full rates go, so will know next time, sorry.<br/>Dolly is now in a Pension <strike>Rosemore</strike> Rossmore House Cairo<br/>Colonel McLaren's wife (a Doctor man) is with her &amp; also expecting a baby about same time so she has company &amp; they get on well together.<br/>Paid all hands today &amp; went Jerusalem in Governor's car 1000's of old muskets &amp; pistols are being handed in by the population being disarmed.</p>
