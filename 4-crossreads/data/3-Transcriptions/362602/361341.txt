<p>Tuesday 19.10.15?</p>
<p>A.M.C.<br/>Military Hospital<br/>Milson's Island<br/>Hawkesbury R.</p>
<p>Dear Dad,</p>
<p>Just a line to let you know that I hope to be home late on Friday night &amp; I will be able to go to Jack's send-off on Saturday night.  We have not received Mrs. Howard's letter yet but as I am home this weekend probably something will be done then.  You can come up to Hawkesbury &amp; we could get a day's leave to run down &amp; see you there but I would not advise coming up here, some of the patients are very rough &amp; would be ducking in for a swim naked.  We are getting on first rate with our work &amp; as there is plenty of it we will soon be good enough to go away.  Well as I have other letters to write I will keep the news till I come home.  We are in spanking health &amp; trusting you are all the same.  Will close with Love from</p>
<p>Harry, Ellis &amp; Bill</p>
<p>I think you had better make enquiries at the Tourist Bureau, Challis House about the times that the Launch leaves Newport &amp; returns as we do not get down to the town to make proper enquiries.  Since writing the above I have been paraded before the Doctor for leave to be</p>
