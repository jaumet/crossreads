<p>4th June (Sunday)<br/>Doing nothing all day. Everything is ready to leave on Wednesday. Writing letters.</p>
<p>5th June (Monday) On various fatigue parties all day. Took number of patients across to the "Tommies" Stationary Hospital opposite us. Preparing for tomorrow's inspection by Gen McKay. We were cleaning harness and oiling waggons till late at night.</p>
<p>6th June (Tuesday) On Cooks fatigue. Inspection by Gen. McKay in morning. He says our turnout was the best</p>
