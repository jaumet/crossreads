 Tuesday 28/12/15 All morning trench digging; afternoon half holiday.  First aid Lecture at night by Capt North, subject &ndash; Arresting Hemorrhage &ndash; Exceptionally cold night. 
 Wednesday  29/12/15 False alarm at 10 AM; quite a stir in camp; all tents dropped &amp; men rushed to their respective trenches.  Col Tibbey visited camp 12 noon.  Enemy scouts again seen in close proximity to camp.
