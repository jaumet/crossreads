<p>1915<br/>Nov. 7 Sun.<br/>Got the afternoon off &amp; paid my weekly visit to the Armenian Scouts.<br/>No. 2 G.H. received a great batch of reinforcements yesterday:- no less than 8 Officers &amp; 97 men. Since leaving Sydney we have doubled our strength &amp; added about 60 more although there are nearly 200 away from the Unit on duty.</p>
