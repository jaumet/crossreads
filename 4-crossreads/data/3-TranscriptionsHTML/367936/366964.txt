<p>Sunday 16 April 1916<br/>Beautiful day.<br/>Father, Mother and I drove over to Wonga and took 4 Rams.  Mustered sheep into bottom paddock.<br/>Went over to Linburn in evening to "send off".  Was presented with note book &amp; pen.  Large crowd present.</p>
<p>Monday 17 April 1916<br/>Dick and I cut chaff.  Father rode round.  Mother &amp; Miss Stirling went for clothes.<br/>Tellegram came from Bill Graham saying not sailing till Wednesday week.<br/>Father drew off honey in evening.</p>
