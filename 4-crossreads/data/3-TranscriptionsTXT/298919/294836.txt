
and went to  the Hippodrome where Variety was mingled with Pictures.  One of the attractions was an address by Mr Ben Tallett the famous agitator and transport workers leader.  He has had 3 weeks travelling through the country where the British & French troops are, and he appealed to all classes who were staying at home to give their all to ensure a tremendous supply of war requisites for the soldiers.  In fact he appealed to the masses not to on any account strike or do anything that would for one moment hamper our fighting men.  He spoke feelingly of the sights he had seen & the barbarities of the Huns.
