 Aug 31 along the wharf, fishing for their tea, &amp; they seemed to be catching some too. Lost sight of Table Mtn 5 hrs out (60 miles). &ndash; Rec'd 2 to 4 oranges each which means finish buksheesh I suppose. Still in sight of land at 10 oclock Several Hospital cases came aboard this mn'g - chaps who, enroute to England were put off at the Cape with Meninghitis, brain-fever etc.
