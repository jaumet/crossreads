<p>On the night in question I was sitting at the instrument about 11.30 pm listening to a german station in the distance sending cipher to another German Station further back. The night itself was very cold &amp; cloudy and as dark as pitch and everything was as quiet as death. All of a sudden I fancied I heard a faint footfall outside but of course thinking it was only one of the boys</p>
