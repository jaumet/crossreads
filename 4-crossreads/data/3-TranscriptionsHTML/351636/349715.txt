<p>Dear Mr. Horsefield &ndash;<br/>I am sending Mrs. Thorne who has had some reduction in pay this month &amp; her grocer &amp; rent bills due</p>
<p>Her allowance for the future will be 35/- p w &amp; having two children she is a little under scale</p>
<p>I am enquiring into the matter of pay &amp; let you know later on.</p>
<p>Yours faithfully<br/>B. Booth &ndash; Hon Sec</p>
