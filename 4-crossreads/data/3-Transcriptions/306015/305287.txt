<p>Mon 17.  stand by all day &amp; go into reserves in evening.  past guns etc. hell-fire corner gas-alarm (false), bags of tucker.</p>
<p>Reserve trench very rough.   plenty of shelling all the time, day &amp; night.   very little sleep.</p>
<p>Tues.  18   Artillery <strike>bombardment</strike> duel for about 1 &frac12; hours continuous.   shells everywhere, planes directly fire   men very cool, walk about in open.   member of one party reading paper on the way up, dirt &amp; water flying <strike>three</strike> three of our boys buried in dirt.</p>
