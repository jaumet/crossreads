
Tues Aug 8th - 16Spent all day getting everything ready, cleaning harness and waggons etc as we may have to return to Pozieres at an hour's notice
Wed Aug 9th - 16Continued cleaning and at 4PM we are to move out at 10Am following morning.
Norrie wounded in knee
Thur Aug 10th - 16Instead of 10AM we were pulled out at 3AM and it was some hurry up too as we had to get away by 4AM and after a wet trip we arrived at Bouzincourt again but in a different camp.
