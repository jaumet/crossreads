<p>[December 1918]<br/> 12 Th<br/>The Educational scheme is in its infancy, but the boys seem to be out to learn and refresh their minds on all subjects<br/>13 Fri<br/>Good Bye Nutty good bye the 17th Batt for I am off to England today to take up a job.A long March to Vaux Andrecie where we caught a sleep in a warm stable.<br/>14 Sat<br/>Boarded the leave train at 4.30 this morning at V-A proceeded to St Orente thence [indecipherable] Amiens and crossed the battle field of V.Brettonuex</p>
