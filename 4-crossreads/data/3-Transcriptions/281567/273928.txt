<p>Beaumetz to a sunken road outside Doynies. At the latter place we received a lively reception from Fritz's artillery  &amp;  M.G.s but no one was rendered a casualty.</p>
<p>At 3.30 on the morning of the 9th we left the sunken Road  &amp;  passing over a cultivated field, fresh  &amp;  free from craters we formed our line in the hollow in front of the German outposts. The attack by the 3rd Brigade on the Boursies began while we were in this position  &amp;  the numerous flares sent up by Fritz showed us up to our immediate enemy who further apprised of our advance by  <strike>on</strike></p>
