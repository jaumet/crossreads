<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Drill in morning.&nbsp; Parade 2.&nbsp; Pay &pound;1/9/4.&nbsp; Spent afternoon washing dirty clothes.&nbsp; Tea.&nbsp; At 5 attended concert.&nbsp; Repeat show almost, but good.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 1</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Parade 8.30.&nbsp; Nice morning.&nbsp; Went out trench digging behind the town.&nbsp; Sports parade at 3.&nbsp; Tea.&nbsp; At 5.30 saw football match and went for stroll.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 2</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Parade 8.30.&nbsp; Did not go.&nbsp; Had boots mended by company cobbler.&nbsp; Parade at 3 for sports.&nbsp; Got mail and relished it greatly.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 3</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Parade at 9.&nbsp; Full marching order.&nbsp; Dis-missed for the day.&nbsp; Watched cricket matches.&nbsp; Spent evening writing and wandering among the Mory ruins with Herb in search of materials for souvenirs.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 4</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.&nbsp; Parade 8.30.&nbsp; Went to do more trench work.&nbsp; Returned 12.30 for dinner.&nbsp; Spent rest of day washing.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 5</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Route march to Bancourt and back, six miles in full kit.&nbsp; Very hot and tiring.&nbsp; Dismissed for rest of day.&nbsp; Spent it resting after morning&#39;s march.&nbsp; Concert after tea.&nbsp; Fritz put a few bombs down about 9.30.&nbsp; No damage.&nbsp; Helped to fix Motabike.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 6</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Parade 8.30.&nbsp; Worked on barbed wire out near trenches.&nbsp; Easy morning.&nbsp; Spent afternoon riding round on section&#39;s bicycle looking for eats.&nbsp; Had rest.&nbsp; Herb started to make dinner-gong from material found.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 7</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Parade 8.15.&nbsp; Marched to Bancourt and did work preparing Sports Grounds for coming meeting.&nbsp; Hard work and such a hot day.&nbsp; Saw Walter Willey there.&nbsp; Spent afternoon at sports practice.&nbsp; Things fairly quiet.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 8</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 8.&nbsp; Parade 7.45.&nbsp; Four mile route march.&nbsp; Bath at 11.&nbsp; Parade 2.&nbsp; Fatigues cleaning up round billet.&nbsp; Went to Bapaume on bike and returned to billet.&nbsp; Spent rest of day resting and lolling about.&nbsp; News of Ypres battle.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">June 9</td>
<td style="text-align: left; vertical-align: top;">Rose 6.30.&nbsp; Parade 9.&nbsp; Lecture on Lewis Gun.&nbsp; Sports in afternoon.&nbsp; Our company did well.&nbsp; Did not go.&nbsp; Stayed behind to help Herb with souvenirs.&nbsp; Went out after tea in search for more souvenir material and watching rifle shooting at range.&nbsp; Drivers returned about 11.&nbsp; Kicked up awful row with result that a fight neatryy took place between a couple of them and Molyneux.</td>
