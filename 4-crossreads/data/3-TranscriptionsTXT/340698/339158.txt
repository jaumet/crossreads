 Some very big shells dropping around. We expect to be relieved this afternoon. 
 7/11/16 We were releived at 6 p.m. and marched to a camp on the Bazentin-Flers road. We moved on to Pommiers Camp. 
 It has been very wet all day and we had a miserable time.
