  
 arrived from Headquarters to tell us to get out at Daybreak Impossible as there is 250 wounded to get away. Sunday March 31 Still raining & very cold Artillery cannot get up owing state of roads. Our boys evacuating positions Camel Field Amb arrived to help get wounded away 10th Division evacuated midday. We got away at dark after 2ND Brigade & all troops retired Chances of getting out were not too bright. It was very dark so much so that you could not see a foot in front of you. The Camels with medical supplies kept falling in the soft going
