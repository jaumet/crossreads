  
 Monday 7 June 1915 
 Xmas Eve - everything very quiet - no excitement - time camp fire concert. 
 Xmas Day - Holy Communion - prayers for my dear ones at home - very quiet Christmas - all our Xmas presents turn up at 7.30 in the evening. 
 29th - Embark on "Tunisian" for Egypt.  Hurrah! 
 New Year's Eve - everything quiet,  New Year's Day, everything quite. Ismalia - Moascar. 
 Jan. 16 - Review by Gen. Sir Archibald Murray.
