<p>26th  Another burial to-day  Service as before except a short drum roll between volleys. at conclusion played 10 minutes of marches.  Death from pneumonia after measles.</p>
<p>27th  Sports on deck.  The watch method of telling direction used ie. 12 o'clock pointed at the sun and distance between hour hand and 12 bisected gives due north.</p>
<p>28th  Another man gone.  Buried at 11 o'clock (Pte Love)</p>
<p>29th  Miserable wet day.  Kit bags brought up from hold for first and probably only time</p>
