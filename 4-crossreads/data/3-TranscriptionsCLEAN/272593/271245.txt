Pretty busy today for I am Batt. Orderly Sergt, & in a camp there are so many things for one to look after. Had another meeting of the Sergts last night & the Mess is to be opened tonight & a good job too  Most of the boys were pretty full last night. Heavy frost this morning but the sun is struggling to show itself for a while hope he gets out Very heavy sick parade some of them with nothing at all the matter with them at the present time we have a terrible lot of malingerers
