
<p>TUESDAY, OCTOBER 7, 1919.</p>
<p>Came on to rain about 8 AM &amp; rained</p>
<p>till midday. Had 33 points.</p>
<p>Lopped in Top paddock in&nbsp;<s>afternoon</s></p>
<p>morning &amp; cutting cotton bush in</p>
<p>Wheelers in afternoon.</p>
<p>Tickle lopped in morning &amp; drew</p>
<p>water to Darby in afternoon</p>
<p>Darby got water about 300 gal</p>
<p>per hour at 125 ft. He is going a</p>
<p>bit deeper &amp; he thinks he will get</p>
<p>more.</p>
