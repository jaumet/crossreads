 10th Jan 1916 
 [Note: Date should be 1917] My Dear Mother Here I am again right back at the canal in a Tommy hospital under observation for diphtheria which I dont beleive a bit I've got. It really is rotten luck as the day I left I think the regt went out on a sort of raid again and there was likely something doing I think.
