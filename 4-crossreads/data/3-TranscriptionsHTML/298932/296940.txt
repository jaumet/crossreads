<p>RENESQUE<br/>Monday 6<br/>I have no shirt on cause I washed it this morning, I use to have a lot of shirts once, only got one now<br/>Tuesday 7<br/>Full marching order with a long road in front of us, Westbecourt was a good home. After a long trip we get to "Renesqure"<br/>Wednesday 8<br/>Riding a good horse on beautiful road's, through magnificent country with "Major Holmes" to the old town of "Steenbecque<br/>Thursday 9<br/>Stormy weather, rain in torrents but thank goodness old Nutty &amp; I are under cover</p>
