<p>Wednesday 6/3/18. Went by car to Boulogne had dinner &amp; returned about 11 p.m.</p>
<p>Thursday 7/3/18&nbsp; Orders to move forward received, Bn cleaning up.</p>
<p>Friday 8/3/18&nbsp; Left Henneveaux at 5Am and entrained at Desvres arrived at Steenwerck at mid-day, took over from Goddard at Canteen Corner&nbsp; very Dirty camp.</p>
<p>Saturday 9/3/18&nbsp; Cleaning up- men resting officers reconnoitred <s>for </s>forward areas</p>
<p>Sunday 10/3/18. Formed Bn Mess again Reconnoitred area around Nieppe.</p>
