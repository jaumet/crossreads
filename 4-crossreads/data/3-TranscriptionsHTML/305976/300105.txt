<p>Wednesday Jan 22<br/>Left Rafa in the usual old Trucks about 5pm last night. It was a cold trip down &amp; we arrived at Kantara about 6 AM. We walked across to the other side of the Canal &amp; had breakfast at Mrs Chisholms at the Canteen then catching the 8.30 AM train to Cairo.<br/>We arrived in Cairo about 1pm and stayed at Soldiers Club.<br/>Tuesday Jan 28<br/>Arrived back at Rafa about 6 AM. I had a fine weeks spell. During my stay I visited all the old sights probably for the last time. I visited</p>
