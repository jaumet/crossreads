<p>Bombing Lecture &ndash; Mills 23 Mark 2 hand grenade Hales Rifle Grenade Etc</p>
<p>Tuesday 18th<br/>More Bombing. Threw 2 line Bombs race &amp; used to get splashed with mud &amp; ice if they were not thrown far enough</p>
<p>Wed 19th<br/>Gass Lecture Flitted with Box Helmets</p>
<p>Thursday 20<br/>More Gass. Went through chamber to test helmets for both Tear &amp; Chlorine Gass. A smellie business. This Gas is not the dinkum stuff allthough quite a lot of the lads think it is.</p>
<p>Friday 21st<br/>G.S.O. (Group Staff Officer) Inspection A few minutes each at Squad Drill Bayonet Fighting Bombing &amp; Gass. Musketery Phyisacal Jerks whilst a few Officers walk around &amp; ask questions One emerges the finished article all ready for France It is not at all difficult to get through although a few failed today.</p>
