<p>Dear Ruth<br/>There are lots of these little carts and donkeys here and they trot along quite well, in the morning they come in loaded with melons and vegetables from the gardens away out over the Desert, we see camels coming in with haystacks on their backs, too.</p>
<p>With love from Don</p>
