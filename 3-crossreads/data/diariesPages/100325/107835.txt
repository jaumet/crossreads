26/8/15
apparently a European. Pure white hair and eyelids . Hat covering upper portion of face eyes closed. Saw very old Arab and his wife, poor old folk quite out of place among bustle and although so filthy apparently helping each other along. Old woman sat down in middle of street. 
The street sellers busy this early morning, crying their wares the baskets carried on their heads. Fruit vegetable (small marrows) c and the fruit of the prickly pear. Some of the cries very musical. To newsagents before reaching Continental and a chat to Mimi. No Times London until tomorrow.
The shiek's tomb mosque at entrance to lower part of square seems quite out of place among all the modern buildings and bustle round about
