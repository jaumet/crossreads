
27.5.16There was a Muster Call this afternoon, and we doubled up thinking a move was right. Nothing doing, it was apparently a practical joke. By the movement on the railway that runs by the camp we will be busy enough before long. Saw a regiment of French soldiers they are fine amiable fellows. Of a good stamp physically and nearly all cultivate fierce beards; they are called "les piolu" for that reason I am told. We were amazed at the clean shaven faces of our chaps.
