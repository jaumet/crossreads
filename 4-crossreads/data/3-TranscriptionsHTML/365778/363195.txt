<p>Monday 14/5/17<br/>Training commenced in earnest, had letter from Fred.<br/>Tuesday 15/5/17<br/>Visited by General Gough who came to watch the training. Saw Bas; took men for stiff march.<br/>Wednesday 16/5/17<br/>Raining, lectures in huts, had a game of Bridge.<br/>Thursday 17/6/17<br/>Musketry, Battalion shot well, raining.<br/>Friday 18/5/17<br/>Marched at 10.30 am for Bouzincourt, had midday meal on road &ndash; now in camp near Martinsart.</p>
