
February, 1916Sunday 27After breakfast I wrote a letter home but forgot to post itAfter Dinner Dad & I & Dad's boy went for a run into Cairo & out to Rod-el- farag the Nile docks.After Tea we went to the American Cosmograph
Monday 28Monday morning was spent making the dugout more like a home & less like a dump.I also received a welcome surprise got a parcel from home sox etc.  God knows how long it has been coming.Spent evening w/- dad
