
Sunday 30 January 1916Ward work & no spell even for a shower.
Monday 31 January 1916Continue in ward work & Operation Theatre
Tuesday 1 February 1916Still continue.  Wrote to Marg Millar.
Wednesday 2 February 1916Wrote Home & sent a few cards.  Wrote to Cohen's.
Thursday 3 February 1916Ellis & I with Staff Moore, L.C. Winn & Sergt. McIvor went to Pyramids followed by tea at Y.M.C.A.
Friday 4 February 1916Harold Filshie, Roland Hale & Eric Davis came up to see us.
Saturday 4 February 1916Had a letter from Roy Tredrea.
