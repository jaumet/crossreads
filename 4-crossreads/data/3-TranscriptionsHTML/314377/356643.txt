<p>I became attached to the Congregational Church here. Took a class also in the Salvation Army. Spoke on the Open Air.</p>
<p>During the week-days I would go and see the Boy-Scouts drills The Girl Guides also had a troup Upon leaving Southall I was given a photo of the Girl Guides and the boy scouts. I visited different people in the locality and was warmly welcomed.</p>
