<p>The end of a world wide war, devastation, starvation, crimes and all of the horrors such as have come about during the last 4 years</p>
<p>Well we wait with what patience we have for the appointed hour</p>
<p>Nov 10th &ndash; 18<br/>Paid another visit to Norrie and stayed overnight.</p>
<p>Nov 11th 18<br/>Have just returned from Halincourt and it is 10-45AM and another &frac14; of an hour to go before the decisive answer is given.</p>
<p>Excitement reigns supreme</p>
