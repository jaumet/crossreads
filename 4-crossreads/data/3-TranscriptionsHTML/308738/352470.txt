<p>June 29 Tuesday 1915<br/>Feeling little better. Met Young &amp; Walters of B Squadron both doing well<br/>Dr. gave me some medicine &amp; to drink plenty of water.</p>
<p>June 30 Wednesday 1915<br/>Feeling worse again very little to see or hear. Men are continually coming in &amp; out.<br/>Lieu Drummond came in to see me gave me 20 piastres.</p>
<p>July 1 Thursday 1915<br/>Feeling very little better &ndash; got up after tea &amp; got dressed cleared out of the Hospital. Took tram to Cairo bought one or two dainties to eat- returned 8. 30 PM</p>
<p>July 2 Friday 1915<br/>Feeling bit better again<br/>V. Convas came in to see me this afternoon</p>
