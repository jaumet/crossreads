<p>Have sent to England for more warm clothing. My money goes in buying clothes for comfort.</p>
<p>Sad to say your parcel has not arrived yet, think it must have gone down in the "Arabia".</p>
<p>The poor boys in the trenches much be having a fearful time   &amp;  this week some firing has been on the go. It is an awful noise, makes me feel quite ill at times, does not do to think   &amp;  best to be kept busy. Now it is going   <u>some</u></p>
