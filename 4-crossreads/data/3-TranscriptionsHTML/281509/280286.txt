<p>1916 March 4<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Cruise around all morning &amp; get ready for weekend leave &amp; Parade at 11.30.  Order of dress no collars or ties &amp; no cap covers.  Parade at 12.5 again before O.C. who inspects.  Wild rush for coaches &amp; squeeze on to trains.  Arrive at Hewletts for dinner at 1.45 pm.  Go to Macs for afternoon tea. Thence down to Manly arrive there about 6.45 pm have tea &amp; take M &amp; J to pictures. sleep in till next morning at 8.30 pm.</p>
