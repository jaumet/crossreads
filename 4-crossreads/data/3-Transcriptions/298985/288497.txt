<p>Tuesday July 9th 18<br/>Have mucked about all day being on draft. There was a free Picture show for us in the Afternoon Did not go as I wanted to do some writing. Fell is just before 7 P.M Role Calls Etc but just before we were to march off 3 Pioneers were taken of I am one of them Have no idea why it was done. But as it turned out turned out a very wet night &amp; I felt rather seedy with a cold was not all together sorry But it will mean getting all ready again in a day or so</p>
