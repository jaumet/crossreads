I tell you I'm not sorry. No one came in to relieve him so I have been kept going. Two chaps are digging outside & I just heard one say "here's a shank bone" what callous hearted wretches soldiers are. 30-3-17. An observation balloon escaped today & an anti-aircraft gun about 100 yards away opened fire but it was soon lost in the clouds. There is plenty of rain about & no billets for the place is in a mess, one  without receiving  could not imagine the destruction.
