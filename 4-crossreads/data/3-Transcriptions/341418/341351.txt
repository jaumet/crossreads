<p>God ever made, one simply dried up it was impossible to sweat &ndash; but it defies description.<br/>Then yesterday morning at 2 oclock we crossed the canal by the pontoon bridge  &amp;  rode out to this spot &ndash; "Hill 40" it is seven miles from the canal. There is a limestone road running out here also a railway which is nearly standard   <strike>gage</strike>  guage  &amp;  there is a meagre supply of water laid on: so all things considered we are very well off.
