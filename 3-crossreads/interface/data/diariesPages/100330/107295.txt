As this was my second Dock I had seen naturally I was interested. Parade was called for at 5 Pm after falling in the Roll called we were marched off at 5.40 Pm out of the Shed across the Dry Dock along the Wharf to our Boat arriving there at 5.50. The name of the Boat was ?Mona's Queen" a Paddle Steamer of 20 Knots. We were marched on board down below to the Second Deck placed our Kit Bags other effects on the Sides in the Centre then up again on deck to receive our Life Belts all the other units doing the same. At 7.50 Pm the Boat left the Wharf proceeded down Southampton Waters, the Sights passing down the Waters were very interesting to us all. Netley Hospital was passed on our left, a Magnificent Building with spacious Lawn's running down to the Waters edge.
