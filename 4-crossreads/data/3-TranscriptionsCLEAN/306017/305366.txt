28th SeptB Troop was detailed for camel escort to Oghratina.  We got there at 10 It is a tiresome job as the camels travel slow.  We had lunch fed & water our horses & brought back an English Yeomanry Captain who is taking over the camp for his Brigade when we leave.  On the 29th at 7 am the Brigade marched out of Hassaniya for Romani at 3 pm & occupied the old ground we first camped on last April.
