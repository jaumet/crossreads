<p>Monday 9 September<br/>Move this afternoon to Buire where we are to establish a M.D.S. in bow huts which our people built in 1917 &amp; had to evacuate again. About nine kilos from the line.</p>
<p>Tuesday 10 September<br/>Working about M.D.S. fixing things up generally for work. The division takes over to-night at midnight &amp; our bearers are already up, with the battalions.</p>
