 Had to make out my first "Parade State" this morning. 
 Jack Price, Digby &amp; Kendrick left for the trenches at midnight last night. 
 This morning we went for a bath it is a fair step to the joint but well worth the walk, they have a pretty good system of dealing with the men, this is how they do it
