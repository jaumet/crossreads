<p>the section of retaliation.<br/>In the midst of the Bombardment a big fire occurred in Town &amp; we could see the flames rising higher &amp; higher reddening the murky night.  A fine drizzling rain was falling.  I do not think the fire had any connection with enemy bombardment (9" F---).  I got to bed at 4 oclock &amp; got up at 10.<br/><strike>Thursday</strike><br/>We had 5 lads killed last night &amp; another today.  The enemy has been very bitter all day &amp; has put over a lot of shells.  We have had several wounded.<br/>I have been up to Armentiers today.<br/>Weather not very cold, showery.</p>
<p>Thursday 15th<br/>Fairly quiet.  He put over several shells just behind us.  Had another trip up to town to the cemetry yesterday.  Saw quite an exciting bit of anti aircraft shooting.
