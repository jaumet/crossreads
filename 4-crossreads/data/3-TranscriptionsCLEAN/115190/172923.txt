 through Afula.  Afula with its large aerodrome was has been captured by the British & Indian Cavalry on the 21 st .  The moon rises about 10 pm & we halt for an hour to let a wagon transport get ahead of us. 
 We now commence to climb, & continue climbing for hours.  About midnight we pass Nazareth.  It lies on our left & looks calm & beautiful in the moonlight.  Another hour brings us to Kaffr Kenna (Cana of Galilee) & here we halt amongst a grove of trees.  We off saddle & snatch a few hours rest. 
 6 th  Sept.  8 am.  We are jogging along again through the hills hour after hour.  I am in my shirt sleeves as usual with tunic strapped in front of me.  My water bottle is full & I have breakfasted heavily off bully & B as usual.  I have my old pipe & plenty of Tobacco
