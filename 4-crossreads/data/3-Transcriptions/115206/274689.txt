<p>April 1918<br/>Monday 29</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Daours   D.R.<br/>Weather wet<br/>4am. Fritz made a very heavy attack at Villers Bretnovino. Gained 100 yds.<br/>5th Mobile Vet section moved out.<br/>At 5 pm an enemy aeroplane was brought down intact and the pilot and observer captured.<br/>The machine was intact and I believe belonged to the circus of the German Flying Corps.<br/>Heavy stunt commenced but ended early.</p>
