<p>your eyes start to water &amp; smart like anything but still they are a very necessary thing for without them a man would be dead in a few minutes so we take it as a matter of course &amp; put up with it as best we can.<br/>This afternoon we finished our cricket match in fine weather it was an easy win for us for they only scored 63 in 2 innings. I kept wickets the whole time, which made my hands very sore for we have no gloves or pads as yet. out of our collection we bought a football but the cricket set has not arrived as yet.
