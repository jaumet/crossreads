 29 July, Tuesday 
 Mum &amp; I go to Granma's &amp; Aunt Fannies. Shopping at Grace Bros &amp; to pictures &amp; to Glebe. 
 Break up in general. We all strike same boat Home. Meet O.G.H.M. We all race to bed in screams of silent laughter. 
 Send [indecipherable] love &amp; painting of Kookaburra [indecipherable]. 
 30 July, Wednesday 
 I go to Burwood. Watch tennis[?] at old Cernt &amp; the Davey Boys. Meet Brigg's &amp; Mrs Haigh. I have a very good time &amp; sleep out on verandah. We write to Jessie. 
 Wrote M 17. Wrote Jessie.
