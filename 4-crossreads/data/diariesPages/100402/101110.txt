Saturday 27 July
Last night the battalion here was relieved changed over without a casualty. These men seem a better kind of men. North Staffs. relieve South Lancs. The doctor here R.A.M.C. is from Chatswood, Giblin. Very quiet on the front.
Sunday 28 July
Rotten weather raining stormy, but very quiet on the front. A game of bridge with the English padre to-night was very enjoyable. The ambulance is to be relieved in a day or so.
