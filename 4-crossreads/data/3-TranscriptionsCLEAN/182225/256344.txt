 1916   SUNDAY 13   AUGUST 
 Very windy & fairly warm. Went with the Presbeterians to Church at No 3 YMCA hut in morning & then wrote some letters. Wrote some letters in afternoon and had a sleep till tea. Went up to the Artillary School of Instruction to see Warren with Bill & Jack Hunter. We went for a walk round Durrington Camp ect after & Warren came back with us. 
 Wrote Linda, Jo, Jessie, Nan (Coomoo Coomoo) M rs  Mac Father and Alan
