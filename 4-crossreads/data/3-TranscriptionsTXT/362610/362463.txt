 Monday 4 Sept. 1916 A dull miserable day with a fair amount of rain in light showers. After breakfast went up to the line where the right battalion is, a very historic piece just in front of Hill 60. Things very quiet, fortunately, &amp; was able to get the alarms &amp;c. fixed up. In the afternoon attending to gas-proofing of Bde. H.Q. dug-outs, rigging wind-vanes, &amp;c.
