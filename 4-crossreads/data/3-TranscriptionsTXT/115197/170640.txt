  June 7th  
 Trouble brewing!&nbsp; The fireman are apparently shorthanded and are being over-worked.&nbsp; In the forenoon we pass Sierra Leone. 
 The weather grows more oppressive daily - and we are not yet to the &quot;Line&quot;. 
 Commence &quot;Society &amp; Solitude&quot; (Emerson). 
 Acquatic sports. More dolphins and a shark.
