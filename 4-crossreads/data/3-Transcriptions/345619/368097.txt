<p>[Reverse of the above postcard. This is to Burrowes' mother]<br/>Mrs Burrowes<br/>Upperby<br/>Rooty Hill<br/>N.S.W.</p>
<p>18/4/18<br/>It is not quite as bad as this picture, here, but it is quite enough of a rush. Have sent you a good few cards &amp; a couple of letters, hope you got them. Hope you are well &amp; will be glad when I get a letter.<br/>Love from Arthur</p>
