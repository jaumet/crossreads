<p>26 June 1918 Wednesday (X to X crossed out)<br/>Horses sent out &amp; [indecipherable] in to a court-Martial, got a splitting headache &amp; 103&deg; Temperature, back tonight again, hope I'm not getting Malarial  Jacko has a big gun he gets all over us at present.  Dust very bad, men cover their faces &amp; everything is white dust.  However all over some day-X [indecipherable] to wait till end of August [indecipherable] but we hardly expect such luck as to go home &ndash; yet.X</p>
