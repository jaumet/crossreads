 May 1 Saturday 1915 A Squadron left this morning for "Barrage"under the command  of  Major Charley Over 1000 wounded returned from Dardinelles, &amp; are in Heolopilos Hospital.  More fighting on the Canal. 
 May 2 Sunday 1915 Church Parade this morning Chaplain Robertson preached Col Cox went to Heoleon with Brigadier Ryrie returned 6 PM. Service  tonight   Chaplain Miller preached. Sent letter to England re UC 
 May 3 Monday 1915 Revierlle at 4. 30 AM. C. Squadron left 7. 15 AM for "Barrage"under the command  Major White. Col Cox visiting "Barrage"to inspect camp, returned 4 PM Posted letter Home to M. B Squadron returned from Barrage. Town Guard under Capt White returned to camp being away a fortnight. Australian mail arrived in camp. No letter from Home one from Clem Taylor
