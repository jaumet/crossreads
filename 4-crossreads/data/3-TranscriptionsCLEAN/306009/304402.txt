  
 1915 Aug. 6 Fri. Have during the last few days come across a rather decent Egyptian (a Copt). His father is the Govt. Minister for Finance. The Egyptians are of 2 religions - Coptic & Mahommedan. The Copts are Christians, few in number, but staunch upholders of the British.  The majority of the people are Mahommedans - same religion as the Turks - and only respecting the British because of our superior strength.
