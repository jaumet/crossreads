<p>We parted with our natives at the hotel for a very small amount about half a rupee each (ninepence in English money). We had a cool drink each a splendid cup of tea with cakes<strike>and some</strike> bananas &amp; paw-paws. This latter fruit I had never tasted before &amp; I must say that with the lime &amp; a little sugar they were very nice indeed.</p>
<p>We then wandered into a silk shop in the hotel premises. After being pestered with business cards &amp; natives trying to cajole us into buying some</p>
