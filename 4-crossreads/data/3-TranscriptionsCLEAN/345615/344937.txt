
struck & returned to Ordnance so we shall be once more bivouacing in the open. It will come a bit hard again - after tents & the homemade bunks we had all made during our 4 5 months back at the base - I have had a 'sweet' billet during that time as the companies 'postman' in the F.P.O, not too much work & plenty of liberty.
But now The Company is on the move at last I want to get back amongst them to see what's doing. So spoke to my C.O. & am when we reach the new Base, shall be back with the horses on convoy work & my pla & a 'B' Class man from hospital will take my take over the F.P.O work
