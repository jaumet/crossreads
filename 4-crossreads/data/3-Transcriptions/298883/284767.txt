<p>He spent some little time in the training camp in Bathurst and joined the 54th Battalion in France in time to go through the dreaded winter on the Somme.  Many younger and stronger men broke up during the hard long months of cold but Bill stuck it out and is still as energetic and active as a two year old.  As a batman he is absolutely invaluable.</p>
