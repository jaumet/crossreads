 Thursday 23rd Sept Went again to Ordnance. Had busy day getting goods and etc and later on when mules brought parcels up I issued to those required 
 Friday 24th Weather still keen. There is an absence of the short Pants now. Everyone is commencing to cover themselves up a little more. The wind is the worst part of the weather &ndash; it is so cutting just now. I made an appeal to Supply O.C. about bread &amp; in future our Battery is to be treated similar to the Infantry Brigades etc. 
 Saturday 25th A nice day. Just a little cool Zephyr blowing. We have a demonstration against the Turkish positions late in the evening. There was a tremendous fusilade of bullets &amp; shells for about 2 hours etc.
