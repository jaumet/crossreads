<p>Sunday 6 May 1917.</p>
<p>On leave. Home during day.</p>
<p>Writing wallet&nbsp;given me by Mother in evening before leaving,&nbsp;Wallace&#39;s from Bayswater over for dinner &amp; left at 6.<s>15&nbsp;</s>pm.</p>
<p>Caught 6.10 train from Lyndhurst. Stayed 1&frac12; hours in Dandenong &amp; then got 8 pm train to Melbourne.</p>
<p>Wrote note &amp; turned in 11 pm. at Fed.Coffee Palace.</p>
