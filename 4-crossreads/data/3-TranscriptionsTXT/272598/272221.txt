Monday 20.8.17Had a fairly comfortable ride, railways certainly fine &amp; good to travel by.  Arrived Glascow 10 Am,  Engaged room at Central Hotel, hurried &amp; caught boat at 10.40 Am for the trip up the Clyde, enjoyed trip thoroughly &amp; arrived back at Glascow about 8.30 pm.  Took a stroll around the town to fill in the remainder of the night.
