<p>but luckily no one was hurt, the bullet went right through a fair sized tree, from now on 8 N C O.'s from each Coy. have to have an hours instruction each evening on the guns.</p>
<p>This afternoon we all went for a wash, there were no showers so all the water was pumped up into buckets &amp; we washed in them the water was very cold.</p>
<p>I believe our Battalion is specially mentioned in Army Corp Orders for fine work done at Pozeries" &amp; thats a fine honour for any Battalion to have</p>
<p>A fair sized mail came in to night I got a few letters out</p>
