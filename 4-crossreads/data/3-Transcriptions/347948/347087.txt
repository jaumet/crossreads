<p>530 quick firers<br/>This however may prove as true as the other report.  At sunset we weighed anchor and left Dakar.  The "Kent" going on ahead  The "Swiftsure" going next and the 4 transports following in the same order as before.</p>
<p>1st July  H.M.S. "Kent" seems to have left us.  Boxing &amp; Wrestling Tourney continued this afternoon on forward well deck.</p>
<p>2nd  Received 12 cigarettes &amp; 1 pocket handkerchief from comforts fund.<br/>Church Parade</p>
