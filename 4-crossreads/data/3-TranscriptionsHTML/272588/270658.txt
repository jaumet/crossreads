<p>Sunday 10 February<br/>Still at Ravelsberg D.R.'s in the pack store. Expect leave to Paris shortly.<br/>1st Australian mail for ages.</p>
<p>Monday 11 February<br/>Football match 1st F. Amb. v. 1st F. Co. Engineers.  Victory for Ambulance 16-3.<br/>About 25 German prisoners passed along the road this morning.  Captured last night in raid.</p>
