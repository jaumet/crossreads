<p>There has been a debate on the deck this afternoon the subject was White Australia, I don't know if the speakers threw any fresh light on the subject but they caused an amount of fun</p>
<p>21st Been very hot &amp; cloudy all day long, they have erected canvas baths on the deck &amp; before turning in last night I had a dip &amp; this morning put my shorts on A fellow wants as little as possible on this weather</p>
<p>Have been watching the antics of a thumping big porpoise for a good &frac12; hour this evening, there seems to be a certain amount of fascination for people following their rapid &amp; graceful movements</p>
