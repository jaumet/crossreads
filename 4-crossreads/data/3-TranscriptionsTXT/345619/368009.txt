 [Reverse of the above post card] Master Frank Burrowes Franurra Rooty Hill N.S.W. Australia 
 26.5.18 In with this are the two colours off my coat that I have been wearing one is for you if you would like it, perhaps Mum might sew it on your hat or something like that. Goodbye little boy until I send you the next card, lots of love from Dad.
