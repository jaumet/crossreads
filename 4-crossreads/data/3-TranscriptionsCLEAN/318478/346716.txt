 We have two more days here and I really believe we may have sun, but there is too much to do in the time as usual. [Drawing] Wilkins is sick but will I think be alright tomorrow.  He works too hard at night in his cold dark room. 
 I rode today a horse that is nearly blind but we managed to get along.  Although he comes from an English unit he bears an Australian brand and is quite a gentleman. 
 Yours Ever G.W. Lambert War Records Section Capt. A.I.F. C/o H.Q. Cairo [Drawing]
