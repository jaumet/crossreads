<p>25-6-16<br/>Sun 25th<br/>Church Service again this morning at 6.30, had nothing to do all day but the general routine of stables etc;  A good many lads are getting sick &amp; going to the Field Hospital, but most of the lads are looking well on it;  Caban HQ Sig went into the School of Instruction at Zeitoun today, also 8 men for machine gun instruction.</p>
<p>26-6-16<br/>Mon 26th<br/>Some fine rumors have been started here during the last day or two, the best one came out today saying it is rumored that the Kaiser has committed suicide &amp; we shall get news within 24 hours that will startle the world.  Nothing out of the ordinary has happened today &amp; we are having a fairly easy time at present but the weather is rather warm</p>
