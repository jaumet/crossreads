<p>Saturday 24.3.17<br/>Made more patrolling and further preparations for the attack, visit from Osborne.<br/>Sunday 25/3/17<br/>Attack tomorrow at dawn, everything should go well.<br/>Monday 26/3/17<br/>Attack a great success but fierce opposition met. Cherry &amp; Corner killed. Enemy made a big counter attack which we beat off.<br/>Tuesday 27/3/17<br/>Relieved by Pye of 19th at 3 am, very tired indeed. Met Gen Gough who congratulated me most heartily on work of Battery.</p>
