<p>7th &amp; 8th Battalions arrived here &amp; camped on opposite bank of canal.  At night went to camp of 7th Aust Engineers; met the Neal brothers also Oscar Clarke.</p>
<p>Wednesday 2/2/16<br/>Morning route march to Signal Station Tussoux Second Field Ambulance arrived early this morning.</p>
<p>Thursday 3/2/16<br/>All preparations being made to move off at very short notice.  Piquet duty 8.30 pm till 12.30 am.</p>
<p>Friday 4/2/16<br/>Relieved from piquet 4.30 pm All tents struck &amp; now camping</p>
