<p>Continuation Diary<br/>R Harris</p>
<p>On April 4th we left Montauban Camp  &amp;  marched to Haplinct one of the newly captured villages about four miles from Bapaume. The track lay over the duck-board walk past Geudect  &amp;  in the snow storm everything in that region was desolation  itself. It is impossible to conceive a strip of country</p>
