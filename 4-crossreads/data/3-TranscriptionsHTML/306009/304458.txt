<p>1915<br/>Oct. 3 Sun.<br/>At 3 p.m. went to a Display by the Cairo Armenian Gymnastic Society in aid of Armenian Red Cross funds. Display lasted until 8 p.m. &amp; included marching, high &amp; broad jumping, wrestling, boxing, physical exercises, club swimming, horizontal bar feats et. etc. The Armenian Scouts, about 40 in number, assisted at the performance.</p>
