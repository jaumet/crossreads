 Jan.  Wednesday 31 1917 
 My squad go to Bazentin for rations   Exciting trip.  Fritz chases us with H.E's.  Receive latest letters &amp; 2 parcels Mary Mitchell's &amp; Miss Wilsons.  Return trip meet Tony brief yarn. Get spell off truck, jumped points, get bang in small of back which lays me out.  very sore.  Hobble back to post M.O. paints spot &amp; reports no bones broken. Reynolds squad back.
