<p>&amp;the guns roar about us &amp; Fritz drops a shell or two about at irregular intervals but he's not a bit startling.  The batteries are still in the line, they have a livelier time as they are working.</p>
<p>One of my pals in my old batt. was wounded this morning.  I am awfully sorry but glad it is not serious.  This is the second time.  He's not among the ones you know.</p>
