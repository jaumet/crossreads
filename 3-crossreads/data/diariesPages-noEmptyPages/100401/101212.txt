Sunday 24
 Afternoon at Kingston on Thames.
Monday 25
 I have come to the conclusion that this alleged hospital is at the lowest ebb as regards the conditions of food general affairs. The district is filthy I will be pleased to get out of it.
Wednesday 27
 I have asked to be discharged on Friday.
Friday 29
 Discharged from Hospital receive pass for 14 days furlough.
 "Maid of the Mountains" at Dalys. Weather wet generally miserable but things are OK. It is good to have meals at your own time etc.
Saturday 30
 Richmond this afternoon after going to the Bank this morning
