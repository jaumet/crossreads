Sunday. 13.10.18Arrived at Edinburgh about 8am. Took a taxi to the Caledonian Hotel but we slipped some & didn't click so we came back to the Royal. Got a room there alright. Went to the castle after brekker & wandered around generally after lunch I took Bill Cowper out to Comely Bank & uncle took us out to the Forth Bridge. We stayed there for tea & supper. Went to bed dead tired.
Monday 14.10.18Spent the morning shopping & sightseeing. Went out to both the Coistorphine & Comely Bank in the afternoon & went to The Theatre Royal
