Friday Feb 2.2.17. stand to 5.30AM had breakfast and cleaned up went to wells to do washing had a bath done neither no water the wind would blow one away everything covered in sand received 5 letters from maud .win josie walter aggie.Saturday feb 3.2.17  stand to 5.30AM had breakfast went to wells for water taube over to day.Sunday 4.2.17. stand to 5.30AM had breakfast and moved camp made a dug out on 1/4 guard to night in the desert a cold nightMonday 5.2.17. stand to 7.AM still on 1/4 guard fine morning wrote to Josie Walter aggie mick relieved from 1/4 guard 5.PM had tea
