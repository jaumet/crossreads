  
 Wednesday 28th:  In supports Thursday March 1st:  In front line 5 a.m. at 10 p.m we "went over & bombed Huns out & occupied trench Friday 2nd:  Barrage of intense Artillery fire on the trench.  Huns counter attacked. Shortage of bombs we had to retire:  7 am bombed him out again. 9 a.m retired again having run out of bombs. 12 noon advanced again. Huns retired, leaving trench in our hands.  Relieved at mid-night. Came back to Shelter Wood. Wednesday 7th:  Moved up to Bazentin-le-Petit. 
 Tuesday 13th:  Huns retired. We advanced & occupied the villages Biefvillers & Grevillers
