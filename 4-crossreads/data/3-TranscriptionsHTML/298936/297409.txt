<p>September 1915</p>
<p>Friday 10</p>
<p>Nothing to do all day but early parade.  The camp on the beach is simply lovely &amp; a bath [indecipherable] handy. Any amount of Tommies about &ndash; Dublin Fusiliers, [indecipherable] &amp; various[indecipherable].</p>
<p>Met a Dulwich College old boy who remembered Clifford Hill but not my old friend Leslie Hills of Sr. Olams.</p>
<p>The [indecipherable] of the bay is simply lovely.</p>
