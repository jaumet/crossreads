<p>6 August, Wednesday</p>
<p>Stop Home. Gardening &amp; House maid. Ring Nette, Mrs Clarke. Speak Nette 35 minutes or more. Sandy nearly eats a child.</p>
<p>Do a few hours gardening &amp; read &amp; write at night.</p>
<p>Receive notification about deferred pay.</p>
<p>7 August, Thursday</p>
<p>Mum &amp; I sort out trophies &amp; boxes, clothes, etc nearly all day. Ring Mrs Clarke. Write at night.</p>
<p>Wrote Auntie Annie.</p>
