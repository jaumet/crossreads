Sunday 17.6.17
No parade, dont take a fancy to the position where we are camping. Walked to Henencourt Millencourt at night. Mail Day.
Monday 18.6.17
Capt Irving left us to take up a position in "Blighty". All sorry to lose him, has kept the unit together made it what it is to-day. Very heavy hail storm.
Tuesday 19.6.17
Raining hard, nothing at all doing, very quite here, right out in the rural districts where all the old "cockies" are hard at work on their farms.
