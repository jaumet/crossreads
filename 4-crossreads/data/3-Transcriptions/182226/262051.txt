<p>Saturday 29 January, 1916</p>
<p>Am crook with Dysentery.</p>
<p>Our extra tucker came today so everybody is happy.</p>
<p>Some of the chaps say you are better off in gaol as you do get bread there &amp; they are not far out; it is worse here than in the Peninsula both for water &amp; food.</p>
