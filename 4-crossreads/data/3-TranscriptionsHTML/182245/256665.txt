<p>1917 MAY</p>
<p>30 WEDNESDAY</p>
<p>Fine &amp; warm. Building the pit</p>
<p>up all day &amp; making it</p>
<p>shipshape. Bill Hunter &amp; I</p>
<p>started a dug out of our</p>
<p>own in evening &amp; got it</p>
<p>going all right. We were</p>
<p>paid in afternoon. Old Fritz</p>
<p>sent a lot over in morning</p>
<p>behind us, but nobody</p>
<p>hurt much at all. We sent</p>
<p>over a lot all day</p>
