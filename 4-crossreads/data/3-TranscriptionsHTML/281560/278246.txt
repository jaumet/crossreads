<p>10/5/16.<br/>Went to the training ground and did some Battalion drill under young English officers. Most of these English officers are very young and generally are not popular with our fellows; we call them "War Babies". The one that had my platoon today wore a "window in his eye"; but he seemed a decent sort of chap. It poured rain all day.<br/>13/5/16.<br/>We are all sick and tired of this ceaseless training, and there is no indication of a move to the trenches as yet, though all the fellows are in the pink of condition. There is a detention camp near here; where I saw men undergoing the punishment known as "Crucifiction". They are strapped to a beam with their arms stretched</p>
