Possibly the <strike>solidarity of the</strike> permanent nature of our debating society gave him a favourable impression, but it was the tactful presence of Barlow which brought him whole heartedly into it, as the result of some friendly debating they had in C Section billet.</p>
<p>At present we are preparing a timetable for, &amp; discussing our intentions with regard to our stay in the next location, which will be Chateleine near Charleroi.</p>
<p>All ideas of going to Germany are, I believe, thoroughly exterminated.  Instead I understand that we are to stay in Charleroi for a couple of months, after which the demobilization plan will come into force.  They have sent the Jan &amp; Feb 1915 men back</p>
