<p>June 1918<br/>Saturday 29</p>
<p>Bourdon. On the road<br/>Pulled into our W. Lines at 2.30 am, feeling pretty tired too. <br/>Ken Gunn not looking very well and does not profess to be too well. Billeting party left at 11.30 am.<br/>Weather splendid walked into Hanzest in the evening with Ton Code, had a chat with one or two folk there and a snack.<br/>Pulled out at 9 pm for [indecipherable]. Had a spendid trip all night and arrived at Poulonville at 3.50 am. On Sunday morning.<br/>French advanced 7 kilos at Montdider.</p>
