 Jan 24  th  &nbsp; Had some more exercise with the gloves with Hec &amp; again went down for an hour, trimming &amp; firing. had a hot bath when finished, &amp; felt fit. We played cards in the evening - 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 25  th  &nbsp; We had &quot;Neptune&quot; sports in the afternoon as we crossed the line then, the canvas bath was fixed up &amp; filled &amp; several of the officers were caught &amp; took their turn being lathered with flour paste, shaven with a large wooden razor &amp; pushed of backwards into the bath. lance corporal Mason of &quot;Furphy&quot; -(a  paper  bulletin published daily &amp; pinned up in a conspicuous place for the perusal &amp; amusement of the troops)- fame also was immersed - I was one of Neptune&#39;s police - there was water flying everywhere. &amp; there was plenty of good-natured fun - In the evening a concert was held in honor of Rob  t   Burns aniversary - 
 &nbsp; 
 Jan 26  th  &nbsp; A concert was held in the afternoon - a great naval victory is reported by wireless - Also the &quot;Afric&quot; is reported sunk off Sierra Leone. about 300 miles away from us - I slept below on account of rain _
