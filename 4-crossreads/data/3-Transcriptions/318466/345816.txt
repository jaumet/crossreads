<p>Sunday 11 August 1918<br/>Met Mrs. H. in morng.  Went for a stroll.  Very warm in the Valley.  Hear that we are not to go back until enquiry is over.</p>
<p>Monday 12 August 1918<br/>Still hanging about.  Went for a walk in morng. with Mrs. Hendry.  Very hot in the Valley.  Bridge &amp; Billiards all day.</p>
<p>Tuesday 13 August 1918<br/>Usual work in the morning.  Concert in eveng. at Hospital &amp; anor. one at the Y.M.C.A.  Reced. my orders for returning to Blighty.</p>
<p>Wednesday 14 August 1918<br/>Embarked at 9 a.m. &amp; laid in the harbour at Havre all day.  Left at 9.30 &amp; made a good crossing.</p>
<p>Thursday 15 August 1918<br/>Reached S'hampton at 4 a.m. &amp; landed at 7.30.  Train to Warminster at 9.10 &amp; reached home at 11.30.  Rup was out but came in for lunch.</p>
<p>Friday 16 August 1918<br/>Met Johnny Watt at OTB &amp; he came along for tea.  Nothing doing &amp; no work if I can help it.  Tennis in afrnoon.</p>
<p>Saturday 17 August 1918<br/>Into Warminster to get my new glasses repaired.  Went in and back by 'bus.</p>
