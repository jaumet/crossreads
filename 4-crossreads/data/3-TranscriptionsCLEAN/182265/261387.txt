  
 We have a very comfortable & well managed Bn. Mess in a fine large chateau. Major Murray V.C. D.S.O. is President & Lt  East  Secretary. Joe Westwood received his M.C. ribbon from Gen Birdwood today. 
 A few days ago I received a fine box of chocolates from Reg in London. I have applied for Paris leave & am hoping to get it. 
  Entry 5/11/17  - Yesterday (Sunday) was my 31st birthday. An Ausralian mail was delivered but unfortunately for me I received none. There is another batch of Australian letters in today & so I still have a chance. I received a Deniliquin paper from my mother. After Church Parade in the field yesterday, we had a Bn. Football match, A & B Coys versus C & D. Neither team scored. Then after afternoon tea Joe Westwood & I walked across to Lisbourg to visit our old friends there.
