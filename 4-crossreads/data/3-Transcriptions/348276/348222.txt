<p>War Diary<br/>1915  26th Sept.<br/>400 bayonets, no supports or Reserves: -  6 old M. Guns and only 1 M.G. Officer left:  Bombed last evening by enemy &ndash; evidently with a long range, mortar --  Three long 4 feet bomb tails or round sticks found where bombs fell &ndash; Two men hit last night &ndash; working very hard to put trenches safe &ndash;</p>
<p>27th Sept.<br/>Gen. Godley (G.O.C. Div) inspected Area and Trenches and was very pleased with progress being made &ndash; saw men recommended for commissions &ndash; 8th L.H.  S.S.M. McGrath, Trooper C.S. Austin: 9th L.H. RSM Slattery (to be W.O.) 10th L.H. R.S.M. Rosevear, S,SM MacCallum, Sergt. Crowther &ndash;<br/>Very heavy shelling heard N around Sufla Bay from 1800 for an hour and unusual stir about Amafarta --  Star shells and small arms fire &ndash; Lieut. Fisher C.O. Engineers away enteric, also 8 men from usual sickness &ndash; Lieut. Oliver sent to 7th brigade to instruct in Mortar and Bombs &ndash; Patrols sent out to try and locate Turkish watering places and Bivouacs no success &ndash; news last night that our army in France making a push.  Sent list of officers and men away from Brigade &ndash; they number 55 officers a 1209 others &ndash;</p>
<p>28th Sept.<br/>13 to Hospital (1W 9th L.H.):  Enemy now active in Bombs which are fired some 400 yds. or more from a mortar with a long stick 4' 6" by 2".  These are thrown with great accuracy &ndash; Last night Pte. Friday and two others under Sergt. Linacre patrolled to the bottom of donga to try and ascertain watering places of Turks came across two patrols &ndash; did not fire in accordance with my instructions &ndash; Approval at last received &ndash; send an officer to Egypt to purchase stores for Regts. for winter &ndash; sending Lt. Oliver to-morrow &ndash;</p>
<p>29th Sept.<br/>Visited 3rd L.H. with Lt. Col. Abbott and saw Genl. Chauvel and Lt. Col. Meredith &ndash; Installed M.G. on Table Top and did good work in rear and enfilade of Snipers Nest and comn. trenches &ndash; 4 sick and 2w</p>
