<p>&amp;are now on our way to Rosyth. Arrived Rosyth in dogwatches &amp; started to coal ship.</p>
<p>22 June 18<br/>Finished coaling about 1.30 am this morning. Oiled ship this afternoon. No leave given.</p>
<p>23 June 18 )<br/>24 June 18 )<br/>25 June 18 ) Anchored at Rosyth<br/>26 June 18 )</p>
<p>27 June 18<br/>Finished my five years in Navy today, but will have to carry on now till the war is over.</p>
<p>28 June 18 )<br/>29 June 18 ) At Rosyth</p>
<p>30 June 18<br/>Sunday. Admiral of our Squadron came aboard today &amp; inspected ship &amp; ships company</p>
<p>1 July 18<br/>Anchored at Rosyth.</p>
<p>2 July 18<br/>Steamed outside boom defence with squadron &amp; done torpedo running in the morning watch</p>
