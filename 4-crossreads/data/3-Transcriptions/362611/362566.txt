<p>"Eccleburne" [Ecclesbourne]<br/>Blackheath<br/>9 April 1917</p>
<p>My dear John,</p>
<p>My dear John<br/>Your letter of the 29 well at hand- I am sorry  the pressure of business last week prevented my reply before.<br/>I am sorry to hear of the pain &amp; annoyance your son has suffered but trust that by this time things have been straightforward and that he is now improving favourably &amp; that his arm is comfortable. Of course [indecipherable] there are always unavoidable</p>
