
This proved to be a very quiet day.  There was no Church Service as we are the only Unit in this vicinity & we do not carry a Chaplain on our Strength. The weather is fearfully hot & we are utterly prostrated in the middle of the day.
17/4/16 Monday2/3rds of the Company went on a route march over the Desert to-day & as we were not told where we were going we did not take our Water Bottles, consequently we suffered very much from thirst. I was riding in rear of the Column for a while.  My mouth was parched & tongue & lips were swollen, it is perfectly marvellous how it affects one.  When I did eventually get a drink it made me sick.This is the one Curse here - no decent drinking water.
