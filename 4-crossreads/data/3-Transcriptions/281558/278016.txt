<p>the 18th : the day was too hot for marching especially with full pack up : another swim in afternoon :</p>
<p>Beaucourt : July 29th Sunday</p>
<p>Up at 5 am : didn'r sleep much as the "skeetas" were too vicious : on piquet in morning : packed up &amp; boarded train at 12 noon : moved off at 1 pm through Albert, Amiens &amp; Doullens to St Omer arriving at midnight : "bucksheesh" cocoa &amp; put our pack on a transport : march along canals &amp; through a decent sized town to camp arriving 2.30 am.</p>
<p>Monday July 30th</p>
<p>Arques : arrived 2.30 am : into tents &amp; slept till 9 am : drissly rain : walked round the town after dinner with Alf &amp; movies &amp; it is a nice place : got my photos</p>
