 From - Brigadier, &quot;Berrima&quot;. To - Flagship. 
  12th Septr. &#39;14  
 (6-0pm) 
 Commander Beresford was directed when retiring from Wireless Station to remove instruments. Motor lorry evidently acquired to facilitate this duty. Do not apprehend any difficulty, although I have had no report yet of his arrival at Herbertshohe. Naval guard at pier will be relieved immediately.
