
Monday 9 August 1915 Ted Norrie & Brucle [Bruce] McLachlan here to tea. Wrote to the Girls, Nel Johnson, Pat Watt, Muriel Bridge & others 
Tuesday 10 August 1915 In Cairo during the morning, at Chemical laboratory and at Antirabic institute. About hospital rest of the day. Writing letters at night Mrs Norrie. Travers. Paton. 
Wednesday 11 August 1915 Took Father Vrigille S.J. & some of his colleagues to the Pyramids at Mena this afternoon. Working in hospital during the day
