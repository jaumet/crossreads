  
 Monday 15 February 1915 Two appendix operations & one hernia this morning.  About hospital all day. Posted letters to the Girls, Matron Sherwood, and Doffie Williams. Walk about 6 pm. 
 Tuesday 16 February 1915 One appendix operation Busy all day.  Atmospheric conditions ideal. Walk around Native village during afternoon. Dirt in excelsis. 
 Wednesday 17 February 1915 3 operations - 2 Haemorrhoids & 1 fistula in ano.  In Cairo and at Heliopolis with Colonel Martin. At a conference re Neisserian affection at The Palace, Heliopolis; General Williams presided.  Col. Braund, Major Scobie, Father McAuliffe, dined with us at Mena House.  Ordinary hospital work.
