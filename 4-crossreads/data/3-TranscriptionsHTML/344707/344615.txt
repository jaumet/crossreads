<p>III<br/>Uniform and web equipment,<br/>Rifle pullthrough haversack.<br/>Epsom, One Tree Hill &amp; Penrose<br/>Marching out, &amp; marching back.<br/>Company- platoon &amp; section.<br/>Always willing wet or dry,<br/>Till they made the little Army<br/>Suitable for  "standing by"</p>
<p>IV<br/>Trains, transport, field &ndash; manoeuvres<br/>Tropic heat &amp; horsey smell<br/>Like the incidents of travel<br/>Though we stood them rather well<br/>Seasickness, inoculation<br/>Bivouac beneath the sky.<br/>Fitting us for active service<br/>Also 'praps for "standing by"</p>
