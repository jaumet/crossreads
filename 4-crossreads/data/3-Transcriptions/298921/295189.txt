<p>August, 1915<br/>Friday 6<br/>We Tranship into row boats from the Destroyer, under shell fire but at night so no damage is done &ndash; we then March 1 &frac12; miles up a Gully to [indecipherable] &amp; join the Regiment &ndash; It is hard work to get the c/s of Groceries along over by <u>Tips</u> of good gold can be done but I have it still including the insecticide &amp; Sand soap.<br/>All my love to you little mother.<br/>Boats here now, I write this from the Knight Templar.</p>
