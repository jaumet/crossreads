 6 December, Saturday 
 Carnival. I am Seating Steward. I turn in, Hood down. Good sports &amp; swimming events. We come Home for Tea. Mum, Hanna, Bess &amp; I go to Manly for a walk after Tea. B &amp; I miss Mum &amp; L in Political Meeting. 
 7 December, Sunday 
 Bess, Laura &amp; I do gardening. We go to Ellan Vannin after dinner. Have a very cold swim &amp; get weather bound. Home for tea. I see 'em off on boat &amp; meet Dave &amp; Misses Jones at Ellis' place. We laugh &amp; sing &amp; play.
