<p>our boat is docked, amongst the collection.  Tea in town, returned to Ship &amp; then went to Confession.<br/><u>Sunday 11th July 1915.</u>  Went to 9am Mass &amp; Comm.  Went to town at noon with Purser.  He took some of his belongings with him &amp; intends staying in town during our stay here.  Taxi'd from Fenchurch Street to Imperial Hotel Russel Sq where he engaged a room.  Washed &amp; then had lunch.  Taxi'd to the Zoo at Regents Park.  People only admitted on Fellows tickets on Sundays.  Fr Davidson having given me 2 tickets we were alright.  Gardens very nice.  Some very interesting specimens.  Walked about for a couple of hours, had afternoon tea &amp; motored back to Imperial.  Had dinner &amp; strolled down to Leicester Square.</p>
