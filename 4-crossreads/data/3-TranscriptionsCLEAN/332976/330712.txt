  
 August 1916 6th   (Sunday)   First draft of Australian Engineers left for France with Thompson & conducting officer. 10th   Left for London on weekend leave & returned on the 12th at midnight. 13th   Left Christchurch for the Engineering School of Instruction at Esher, a small town  about 15 miles out of London.  We were billeted in Wolsey Rd with Mrs Capel & settled down comfortably.  CS.M Stevens & Sgt Hutton were with me & we had a very enjoyable time
