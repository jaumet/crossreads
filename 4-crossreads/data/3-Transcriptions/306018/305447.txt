<p>July 1917<br/>Boxing Tournament.  There were some very good fights</p>
<p>Thursday 19th<br/>Took over SQM Sergt from Halloran who is going Sergeant Major.  The job is fairly easy &amp; I for one am not going to make it hard.</p>
<p>Friday 20th<br/>This Quarter Master's job is too easy.  There is absolutely nothing to do for most of the day &amp; one doesn't know what to do with oneself.  Besides the weather is too  hot to sleep or read.  In the cool of the evening went for a walk along the canal &amp; on our return went to the pictures.</p>
<p>Saturday 21st<br/>Got some more men into camp, some from hospital &amp; others from the School of Instruction.  It will make the duties much lighter.  Today was very hot fully 108&deg; in the shade.  Applications were called for the Flying Corps.  I put in one, but don't expect anything will come of it.</p>
