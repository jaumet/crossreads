<p>27/9/16  Wednesday</p>
<p>Nice day &ndash; Witnessed an escaped observation Balloon from over Fritzs lines, the wind was blowing over our way &ndash; so it went as far as Estaire &amp; was then shot down by our anti aircraft guns. Very quiet in the trenches.</p>
<p>28/9/16  Thursday</p>
<p>Dull day &ndash; Very quiet &ndash; not disturbed at all last night. Got the news that Combles &amp; Thiepval had fallen &ndash; Tanks doing great work.</p>
<p>29/9/16  Friday</p>
<p>Raining all day. Very quiet in our line. Fritz very busy putting over Minniewurzen &amp; Shrapnel on Supports &amp; entrance to V.C.S ops.</p>
<p>30/9/16  Saturday</p>
<p>Raining &ndash; Heavy bombardment &ndash; Made raids on Fritz's trench at night time, very successful &ndash; very few calls &ndash; on N.Z. buried in his dugout.</p>
<p>1/10/16  Sunday</p>
<p>Nice day &ndash; very quiet.</p>
<p>2/10/16  Monday</p>
<p>Nice day. Got relieved about 10 am, returned to Nouvea Monde, had the day off.</p>
