<p>14th Field Ambulance,<br/>A.I.F.<br/>Egypt<br/>24.4.16</p>
<p>My dear Mrs. Jack,</p>
<p>From the desert, greeting.  I have been here just a fortnight after having had on arrival 4 days off in Cairo to see the sights, they were perhaps the 4 days of my life most crammed full of experiences.  Cairo is some city, before the war it was reputed to be next to Paris the gayest city in the world, at the beginning of the war it was more so, now however it is comparatively quiet, the military Police control everything, all bars</p>
