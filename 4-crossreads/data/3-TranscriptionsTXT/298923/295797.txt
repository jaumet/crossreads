 11 May 1918 Saturday    Not for printing  Said goodbye to Dolly &amp; caught the 6 o'clock train for Palestine once more, the little girl is wonderfully cheerful &amp; well I am glad to say. Wrote Mrs Ish, Laura &amp; fixed up Dolly's a/cs etc:- Address all letters to the wife to c/- Manager Anglo Egyptian Bk Cairo  as she changes her address.
