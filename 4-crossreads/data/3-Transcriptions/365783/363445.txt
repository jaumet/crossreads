<p>[Telegram date stamped 10 Aug 1915 Ezbekia Egyptian State Telegraphs</p>
<p>To Mrs Read Doctor Maddin Saint David Building Cairo.<br/>Origin of Telegram Suez Docks 9 pm<br/>Text of telegram<br/>Arrived ship lovely air very fresh Captain Collins says plenty room for you willingly take you provided you get authority ask Martin help you.<br/>Read.</p>
