 3rd A. Gen. H. B.E.F. France 7.10.17 My darling Eddy, 
 Got your lovely letters this week   &amp;  think you are getting on wonderfully. Ella wrote to me also   &amp;  told me you were very happy at School. Hope you will continue to like it   &amp;  be strong enough o to on. What a lovely time you had at Grandma's birthday. Fondest love   &amp;  kisses.  From  Auntie Eadle.
