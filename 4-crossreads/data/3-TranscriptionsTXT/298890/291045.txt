 7 Thursday Twilight day with a fog.  Very fatigued felt very tired &amp; cold.  Took double dose of cachets &amp; nearly died in night.  Not able to do anything on play. 
 8 Friday Grey day.  Went to bank &amp; fooled around on various office boy errands:  B. Kinred came at six:  we went to eat at Colonial restaurant thence to Coliseum &ndash; Very exhausted &amp; cold. 
 9. Saturday Grey day &amp; foggy.  Got home at 3.20  Had dinner &amp; went to bed till six.  When Mlle. Bloust &amp; Signoria Bambacioni came &amp; stayed to supper to share our already pitifully inadequate food.  Miss Smith tried her plancheth board.  Seems to me it is infliction of thoughts of one who operates it.
