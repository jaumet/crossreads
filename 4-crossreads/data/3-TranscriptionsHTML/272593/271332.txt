As usual we are following the French who have been using this with their latest mode of attack with great <strike>for</strike> success.<br/>Cpl Starkey &amp; I had to go down to Hqrs this afternoon &amp; make a statement as regards Woods case   he was accidentally hurt at "Yorks Dump" This afternoon Starkey &amp; I scaled parade &amp; to put a bit of our time in we went &amp; gave an old French couple a hand to clean their grain   after we had finished we weighed ourselves according to the scales   I weigh 11-11 but I am sure I am not that heavy, you would have laughed at the roundabout way I got the weights worked out on, on account of my limited French &amp; the Kilogrames, but it worked out correctly in Starkey's case</p>
