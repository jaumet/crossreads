
Thursday 20/9/17Stunt a great success am sorry to be out of it. Every one jubilant.Friday 21/9/17Casualties for Div only 2,000 mostly slight.Saturday 22/9/17Started a sports meeting for Monday got Francs (1200) from Officers.Sunday 23/9/17Orders received to rejoin units on Tuesday.Monday 24/9/17Sports meeting a great success, contests very keen indeed.Tuesday 25/9/17handed over to 5th Div (Denchy) and after lunch at Fletre came to Div - Renighelst - now living with 7th Bde HQrs.
