<p>Oct 26<br/>arrival of Mail steamer &amp; return of men who went as guard to Sydney with some more german prisoners sent from here</p>
<p>Oct 27<br/>arrival of Masina with stores</p>
<p>Oct 28<br/>drill &amp; marches are the order, getting stale</p>
<p>Oct 29<br/>nothing happens now only the usual Parades until about Nov 10th</p>
<p>Nov 10th<br/>when we hear that an English missionary has been flogged  by Germans in New Ireland G Coy are to provide 50 men to go &amp; arrest the 6 Germans who took part in the flogging we hear that they have 200 Native Police &amp; are ready for us</p>
<p>Nov 11th<br/>getting ready for the Trip which is about 100 miles away the Germans call it New Mecklenberg  we call it New Ireland</p>
<p>Nov 12th<br/>We embark on a small wooden steamer called the Medang bound for a place called Namatinia  all well until 9.30 pm when they discover that she is leaking badly, there is a rough sea on, we fire rockets &amp; burn Blue lights &amp; Turn back  she is making water fast, we have 50 native Police on board &amp; 7 maries [women?]  besides 52 whites, the niggers are calling out &amp; are very frightened as the water is still coming in, the Pumps are working hard some of the boys are sleeping through it all, some men are sick &amp; few realise the danger we are in, our signals</p>
