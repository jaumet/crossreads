<p>Brawl between Portuguese &amp; Australian troops last night in a village near by, rumors say 5 Portuguese killed, several of our boys badly handled.  Met Fletcher, friend of Spie's</p>
<p>Wednesday 5.9.17<br/>In charge of a party doing a bit of road repair work, have to turn your hand at anything in this life.  Went to church meeting in the evening.</p>
<p>Thursday 6.9.17<br/>Rained heavily during the early hours of the morning  Days very long with nothing to do or at any rate very little.  Pay for it all shortly no doubt</p>
<p>Friday 7.9.17</p>
