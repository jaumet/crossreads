 [Monday 9 October 1916] 
 Examined 14 days extension sick leave. Laura here to Tea- Met Harold Ryrie.  Had breakfast in bed- Am not returning to Ireland it is hardly worth it. Went &amp; saw the "Bing Boys" at the Alhambra last night. To  day  morrow we go to Hendon Hall where Dorothy was at shool for some years. I am taking Laura &amp; Connie to something next Saturday night. Had afternoon Tea with Laura at Trocadero
