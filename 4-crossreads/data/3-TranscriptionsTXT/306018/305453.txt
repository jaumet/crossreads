 good week end.  On Saturday afternoon we got a car &amp; went out to Helouan, called in at San Giovanni cafe on the way back &amp; had afternoon tea.  Got back to town &amp; ran on out to the Pyramids &amp; saw them &amp; the Sphinx by moonlight.  Had tea in a cafe in Mena &amp; went back to Cairo by train.  Had dinner at York House on the roof.  It was beautiful &amp; cool there after the hot day.  Stayed till 11. 
 Sunday morning Rifle inspection &amp; Church parade at 7 am.  Went into town early had lunch at York House  &amp; spent most of the afternoon there.  When it got cool we went into the Esbekiah gardens &amp; listened to the band playing.  Afterwards had some refreshments at Saults &amp; then went out to Gezira.  It was the 11.30 train I got back to camp again.
