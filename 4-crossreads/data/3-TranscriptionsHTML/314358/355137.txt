<p>6<br/>control their mules at times<br/>Restive houses [indecipherable] till passing bodies bump<br/>Wheels almost lock<br/>Men lose their places<br/>Officers ride up and down sometimes on the footways. Each column loses its pace wrinkles up and becomes all halts and starts.<br/>All this for a little while then a melting seems to take place as though the two columns of shadows become one with a strange double movement like opposite flowing rivers joined and still maintaining their courses.<br/>For uttered curses and shouts and commands now die away<br/>Each dragon and the many weird members of each now understands<br/>Each is a counterpart of each<br/>Hoof clomps echoe hoof clomp<br/>Chain clink echoes chain clink<br/>The tread of feet echoe the tread of feet<br/>All sounds are doubled</p>
