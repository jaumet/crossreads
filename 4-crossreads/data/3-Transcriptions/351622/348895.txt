<p>25 September 1916 Mon<br/>A quiet day save a game of football &amp; visiting the 29th Division canteen. Sugar's penny spent on a glass Belgium beer.</p>
<p>26 September 1916 Tues<br/>Spent the morning with Jack Mowbray. At the estaminet [indecipherable] football lost 2 to 1. Aidy comes to have his teeth attended to. Things are very quiet on the front.</p>
