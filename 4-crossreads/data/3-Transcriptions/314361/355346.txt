<p>This time I went through the largest. It is all dark, slippery stone passages, &amp; chambers. Had our fortunes told in the Queen's chamber the usual Tommy rot. We had a good look round &amp; took a few more photos.<br/>I have not had word from home for a month now, so expect a good few letters when I reach my battalion.<br/>Do not know how I got on with the exam, did well with the practical part, but not too good with the theory.<br/>They stopped the Wednesday afternoon holiday.<br/>Am writing in YMCA here. YMCAs in Egypt are made of rush matting, which is cooler than canvas.<br/>Ferry Post, June 2nd 16<br/>Back again with the Battalion. Secured a 1st class certificate from the school.<br/>We are leaving for France I think it is next week some time. We have been fitted out with new gear &amp; clothing.
