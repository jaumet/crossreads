
<p>SATURDAY, MAY 24, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine &amp; Sunny. Round Cow &amp;</p>
<p>Boundary paddocks in morning</p>
<p>then Jo brought Linda</p>
<p>&amp; Sister &amp; the lunch down &amp; we</p>
<p>had it in corner of scrub in</p>
<p>Mountain. They went out</p>
<p>to the look out afterwards.</p>
