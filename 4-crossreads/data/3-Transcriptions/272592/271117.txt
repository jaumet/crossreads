<p>I have seen quite a number of Belgian officers about this part &amp; some of them look very smart in their brown coloured uniforms, the rank &amp; files outfit is not unlike the New Zealander's for it has red facings</p>
<p>This time last year we were on Lemnos Island having our spell after our hard times on the Penisular, &amp; if I am not back in Australia by the time September comes round again I will eat my hat</p>
<p>There are a few aeroplane's flying around here to day.</p>
