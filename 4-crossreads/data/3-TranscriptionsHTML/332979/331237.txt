<p>up for a run in the Hills getting back in time to fall in. This over I had a dip in the Briney which was A.1. After Dressing &amp; a Drink in a Cool Drink Shop my Mate wanted to remain there so I left him &amp; took a Tram to the Zoo. On my journey a Lady spoke to me &amp; invited me to her home for Tea &amp; spend the Evening which I duly accepted. On arrival at the Zoo I took leave of my Lady Friend &amp; had an Interesting Stroll for 1 hour viewing the Animals &amp; Birds, the Zoo is small but is laid out beautiful on a sloping hill facing the Ocean. Every thing being green &amp; well attended to. A large number of Natives are duployed as Gardeners &amp; Attendants &amp; they all do their work well. After satisfying my Thirst I came</p>
