<p>Friday 3 March 1916<br/>Dick &amp; Geoff plowing.  Father and I fixed up studs.  Father took honey from bees.  Beautiful day.</p>
<p>Saturday 4 March 1916<br/>All hands cutting chaff.  Carrier came and took another 75 bags of wheat.  Bill Large came down to look round suckering.  Laid posts round stack.  Hot day.</p>
