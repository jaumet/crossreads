 359 
 MAY 1917 
 6 SATURDAY 
 Fine & hot. Cleaning harness 
 ect all morning & we had 
 a football match in evening 
 just a scratch game. There was 
 no parade in afternoon 
 till stables was called. 
 I got a letter from mother 
 & some tobacco from the 
 Civil Service. I did one 
 shift of Bill Constable's picket 
 at night.
