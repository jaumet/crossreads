Will go up and occupy the front line on the Guedecourt sector tonight; orders were gravely read, that there is to be no fraternisation with the enemy on Christmas Day. I suppose the "heads" think we will want to kiss Fritz under the mistletoe or something.25/12/16.Last night was the worst I ever "put in" in my life. Had some causualties in the company during the releif. Volunteered as a stretcher bearer. Six of us had to carry a wounded man on a stretcher, through the mud, waist deep in places.
