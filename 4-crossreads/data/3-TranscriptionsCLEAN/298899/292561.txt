
Lecture to tent subdivision during the afternoon.Wednesday 15th. Aug.  Heavy rain during the morning the usual route march however, a short distance only.  In the afternoon received more gas lectures from our officers, one in particular being painful to listen to, he had not the faintest idea of his subject.Thursday 16th. Aug.  Route march in the morning about 7 miles passing through some beautiful country quite the best we have yet experienced in France.  During the afternoon we had the Company drill with the Officers.  The Sgts & officers together practised giving orders, each in turn yelling out the orders, later we drilled with ropes, the results at times being funny.  The band visited us later & so finished an event-ful day.Friday 17th. Aug.  Gas drill all the morning getting very monotonous altho' very necessary
