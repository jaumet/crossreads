Got an old one at 7 Con Camp & then got  British Warmer for 15 francs & the old coat. 1/2/18 After tea went with Roelig & chap from 31st btn to estaminet & had steak chips & beetroot & coffee costing 2fr 21/2 each. Then walked half way to Boulogne to fill in the evening.  2/2/18 Saturday Warned for base today. Leaving about 4.30pm. Been buying salmon sandwiches to eat on the way. Bought 1 doz Boulogne post cards for 141/2. Left camp 4.30pm & caught train at Boulogne - Tintelleries at 6.30pm
