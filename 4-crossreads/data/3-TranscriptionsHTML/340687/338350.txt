<p>February, 1917. Thursday 15<br/>Sunny and thawed a bit. Up at 3. Work at 5. Everyone had to go out and rumours of going up line shortly to put in strong points &amp; in a stunt. Worked solidly all night until 11 when we came home. Two gas alarms and we got tear gas fairly strong.</p>
