21/9/15
Cairo
Yesterday I had leave and I went to Cairo and The Pyrimids and Sphinx  Left Cairo By motor car after lunch time. we arrived at our Destination we had a Drink and then we hired a guide and he explained everything to us. we went under the mids and also through the Temples and the Buried City.
Things are very interesting here  There was three of us and we had a Splendid time on Donkeys motor cars and Carriages and Trams.
