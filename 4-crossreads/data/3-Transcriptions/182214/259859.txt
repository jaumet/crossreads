<p>the Medeterranean&nbsp;Sea fairly sparkled in the sun. I often wondered what Gallopoli&nbsp;looked like &amp; here we were within two or three days run from that famous place. our trip proved to be a quiet uninteresting one &amp; rather warm&nbsp;It was not a large boat &amp; we were well packed. We were the first to leave Egypt &amp; the last but one to reach Marseilles where our band played the Marseillase&nbsp;&amp; other tunes as we entered the port we got no reception we arrived in port about 8 AM June 23rd &amp; went ashore about 4.30 PM marched to the trains we saw german Prisoners working in the goods yard. they look very well &amp; fit &amp; one felt as if he hated them all ready</p>
