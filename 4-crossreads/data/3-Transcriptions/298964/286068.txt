<p>29 Th<br/>Took one line of trenches at dusk.  Very [indecipherable] Jacko about 400 yds on our front  Horses left in rear with spare man  Trenches are awful  too narrow we dare not move in case of shell and sniper  We do 24 hours in trenches</p>
<p>30 Fri<br/>We and Cavalry [?] took a line of trenches but could not hold them  enemy too strong.  He shelled us for hours and they were simply raining.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;   We withdrew about 600 yds still in trenches  cannot move or show ourselves we are isolated until dark</p>
<p>1 Sat  Dec<br/>In support at night  digging at 4 am.  7th Regt captured 147 men 2 MG  200 Rifles  Jacko tried to dig in too close  Heavy shell fire all day  We had  9 casualties trying to raid a Jacko post</p>
