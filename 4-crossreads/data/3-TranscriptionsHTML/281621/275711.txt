<p>Sunday 12<br/>Walk round a few villages about made up of thatched cottages etc. &amp; pretty garden</p>
<p>Tuesday 14<br/>At dentists one tooth out &amp; three filled which finishes the contract.</p>
<p>Wednesday 15<br/>Have come the proverbial "gutser" &amp; am now marked as A3 meaning medically &amp; dentally fit for France.<br/>Move to another camp</p>
<p>Thursday 16<br/>Move from command depot to Overseas Training Brigade from where infantry go on draft to France.<br/>We still have to go to our depot at Park House before crossing over.</p>
<p>Friday 17<br/>Rehearsal for big review on Monday next</p>
<p>Saturday 18<br/>Mess Orderly for guard &amp; detention a rotten job</p>
