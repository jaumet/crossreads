If the meal set before us was any criterion Mrs & Miss Newton were certainly cooks of the very best order.  After dinner we three males went for a walk along the main street.  Though only a village Sidcup appeared to be a fairly large one with several large and and well stocked shops.  On our return we yarned with the ladies till bedtime.  My bed proved vastly different to ship board bunk.
