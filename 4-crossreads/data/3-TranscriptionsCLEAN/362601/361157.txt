  
 10 February, Monday 
 F & ice We stay in until 7.30 & then go up for bath & play cards with Dick & Reg until late. I sleep in pyjamas first time since leaving Bulford. We have supper at the Flat. 
 11February, Tuesday 
 F & ice I go to Sarum by 2 pm Train. Do shopping & go to No 13 after 3 vain attempts. Me & Albert & Miss Farris got to Dance. Ian very lively & entertain quite a lot. All Brummy dancers but I have a good time although I am sick. I play, dance, start games, wait & clean up cups etc at Dance.
