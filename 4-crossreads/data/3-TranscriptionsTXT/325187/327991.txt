[Tuesday 5 September 1916]
Off Channel IslandsWireless instructions to go to Portland not Southampton(Cold better) Reason given submarines had missed course- many sweepers &amp; Torpedoe boats are ahead of us-   Left Portland at 3.30 pm with a White Star boat for Southampton so appears we will not now disembark.[indecipherable]We all expected to land at Portland .
