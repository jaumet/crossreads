<p>January 1918<br/>Tuesday 29</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Paid forty francs at 9.30 am.<br/>Inspection of gas masks at 11 am.<br/>Cleaned the leatherwork of my saddle before lunch, and the steel portion after I had finished lunch.<br/>Thompson rode to Etaples on business for Smithy.<br/>&nbsp;Cinema left Attin. For Dranoutie, I suppose Dick has gone with it.</p>
