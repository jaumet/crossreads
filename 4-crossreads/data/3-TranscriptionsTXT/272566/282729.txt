 Wednesday 12 December 
 Cloudy &amp; Sunny &amp; Showery alternately.  Very cold in early morning &amp; at 4 p.m. One trip today &ndash; to our Horse Transport at Daylight Corner through Lindenhoek About 20 Gotha Planes flew over this afternoon &ndash; Huge planes, the iron cross being easily recognised.  They didn't do any damage.  Saw one of these planes hit but it descended in Fritz's lines. I took the 6 a.m. &amp; 5 p.m. Casualty Wires to 15th Brigade Signallers. Trench Mortar accident at 14th Brigade &ndash; premature explosion &ndash; 6 killed &amp; an officer badly wounded.
