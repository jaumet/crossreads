<p>29th June (Thursday)<br/>Route march in morning over muddy roads. In orders this morning reference was made to the breaking of a shop window by members of the 8th Brigade. A repetition of this offence is threatened with a Court Martial &ndash; So we haven't been long in France before the rough element gets to work.
