<p>12th Tuesday<br/>New Spring Bomb thrower to hand &amp; tried with much success.  Mail in.  Weather Bright but cold.</p>
<p>13th Wednesday<br/>Weather very cold &amp; breezy.  Artillery duels during afternoon.  Land Batteries only.  Big Mail in.</p>
<p>14th Thursday<br/>All very quiet all day.  Turks using explosive shells attached to rod for firing during morning against our Post from top of No Man's Ridge, 2 passed right over our front &amp; Reserves only 1 exploding &amp; without</p>
