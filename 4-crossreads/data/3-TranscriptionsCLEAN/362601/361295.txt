  
 10 November, Monday 
 Go up on boat with pel & Lighthouse. Come Home. Played cards with Dad till 9 pm & wrote to M. Mum comes into the Shop. Sent paper (Smith's) & almanac to Auntie. 
 Wrote M 30 
 11 November, Tuesday 
 Recd letter from Auntie & Card of wedding from Marion. Go to Smiths & meet lots who know me. Tea at Railway with O & B. act guide to Smiths. Nice evening. Played the empty stockin' game.
