
<p>&nbsp;Feb.16<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;Last night was very cold. it froze for the first time since a long time - to-day was a lovely day - a Fritz Aeroplane was&nbsp;<s>brought</s><sup>forced</sup>&nbsp;down between Bailleul &amp; Meteren without a shot being fired. by one of the new Dolphin machines. the Fritz tried later to make his escape but was caught before he got to the line - We walked into Bailleul for some photographique material in the afternoon</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Feb.17<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp;- Sunday - .We developed the photos of Suzanne&#39;s outfit last night in the lorry &amp; printed them to-day - they were&#39;nt too bad. it was cold last night but a lovely day to-day - We went up to Suzanne&#39;s in the afternoon for a coffee &amp; to show them the photos.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Feb. 18<sup><u>th</u></sup>&nbsp;&nbsp; Fritz was over here last night dropping bombs, but did&#39;nt drop any near here - We were called at 5.A.M. &amp; left the park at 6.15 A.M. &amp; carried the Chinese labour corps from their camp at Mont Noir to their work at La Flettre &amp; back in the afternoon. they pinched our soap &nbsp;we got home&nbsp;at 5.30 P.M. &nbsp;lovely day &nbsp;We had a look over the Bailleul Aerodrome during the day as we came back to Bailleul &amp; parked there whilst the Chinese worked -</p>
