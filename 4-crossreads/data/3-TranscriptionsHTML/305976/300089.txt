Gen Chaytor is in command of the Australian &amp; New Zealand Mounted Troops on this Front<br/>Tuesday Dec 3<br/>We had a Donkey Polo match to day. It was the funniest thing I have taken part in for a long time. The little Grey Donks seemed to enter into the fun &amp; when the feed up whistle blew they all cleared out for home &amp; ended the game. Auckland Reg are holding a race meeting tomorrow to pick out the best for the Div Races.</p>
