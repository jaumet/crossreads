<p>Sunday 26 March 1916<br/>Adieu to Roy &amp; Jack &amp; T. Master.  Sick in bed all day.  Wrote Aunt Alice, Amy, Bess, Jess Mather, Dobbo's, Home.</p>
<p>Monday 27 March 1916<br/>Mrs. Merrett, Home.<br/>Ellis &amp; Billy went to Cairo after tea to get films &amp; paper.  Last lot of lettres posted at Esbekiah Gardens.  Still in Bed.</p>
<p>Tuesday 28 March 1916<br/>Got up at 2 p.m. &amp; went to Ward.  Inspection.  Bust up.  Kicked out to Treatment Hut.</p>
<p>Wednesday 29 March 1916<br/>Sick Parade.  Irrigation Tent all day.  Ward Master.  4 cases.  1st time for any cases at all.  Watered floor with medicine.</p>
<p>Thursday 30 March 1916<br/>Took charge of Bubo's in Ellis' Hut.  Night on Irrig. Patients.  Roll Call 9.30.  Reinforcements arrived (15).</p>
<p>Friday 31 March 1916<br/>Dulce, Ivy, Otto, Bess.<br/>Billy came to live with us again.  Hot baths for Bubo's started.</p>
<p>Saturday 1 April 1916<br/>Boys played Cricket at Boolak.  Dave Niness represents Annie's Room.  Won easily.  Billy &amp; I went through Waazaa.  Met Pte. Tailly whose Frere lives in Henry St. West Kogarah.</p>
