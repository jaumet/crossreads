<p>Buschy<br/>[September 1918]<br/>1 Sun<br/>Just doing<br/>2 Mon<br/>I've had a lovely rest, that will soon end, "ah" well never mind<br/>3 Tues<br/>5 beautiful weeks of all that could be desired tomorrow I leave for the WAR steadfast &amp; confident hopeful and happy<br/>4 Wed<br/>Dawn at Buchy Station Au Revior to a fine home Now in the train bound for Harfluer, with a load of hun prisoners</p>
