<p>III<br/>mind if one were offered me &ndash; Worst of it is I'm pretty fit and a Medical Board would think me a "comedian" if I asked for 6 months leave on health reasons! &ndash;<br/>Last mail also brought a letter from Mr Broughton &ndash; He is jolly good to keep up his interesting letters as I'm afraid my replies are few &amp; far between &amp; even then must seem dismal affairs.<br/>Must close this now Father as several matters have to be fixed up before I turn in &ndash;<br/>Hoping you are feeling much better than when you wrote &amp; continue to do so &amp; with love<br/>I am your affectionate Son<br/>Terence</p>
