 Some of the Boys had falls. Day passed quietly, settling down to new quarters. Still anchored Saturday May 12th Spent morning on Deck games, Weather very cold &amp; boisterous. Dropped anchor about 5pm Passed through rip &amp; heads of Pt Lonsdale &amp; Nepean. Sunday May 13 Very rough to day, too rough for me, "Seasick." Feel as if I do not care what happens. Monday May 14 Feel a bit better to day, still rough &amp; cold. What a night. Soldiers lying everywhere. Tuesday May 15th Not quite so bad today. Transports keeping close together escorted by Destroyers Brisbane &amp; Melbourne. Boat Drill all
