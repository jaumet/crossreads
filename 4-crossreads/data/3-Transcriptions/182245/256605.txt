<p>1917 MARCH</p>
<p>31 SATURDAY</p>
<p>Fine, but dull. Cleaning</p>
<p>harness ect all morning &amp;</p>
<p>I went for a bath in afternoon</p>
<p>&nbsp;&amp; it was pretty dirty. Had</p>
<p>a trip up to the Epinette</p>
<p>dump for ammunition</p>
<p>in evening. We had</p>
<p>our first casualty today</p>
<p>a whizz bang blew the&nbsp;</p>
<p>wheel off B Sub gun.</p>
