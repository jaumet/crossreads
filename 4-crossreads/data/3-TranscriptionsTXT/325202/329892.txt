 Nov 13/17 Renescure (W. St Omer) arrived here on the 11th from Reninghelst some 20 mile trek &ndash; billetted in the barn of a farm &ndash; &amp; last night Gosling, Adeney, Gaynor &amp; myself got the Madame of the farm to cook us a dinner of Soup, Chicken, Chipped Potatoes &amp; White Sauce, with a couple of bottles of Vin Blanc &ndash;  afterwards  Adeney had brought his violin &amp; when he started playing they invited us all into the kitchen to sit round the stove 'en famille'
