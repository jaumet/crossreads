<p>March, 1917. Saturday 3<br/>Frosty and dull all day. Up at 7 parade and out to work at 8. Went to Turks Dump and carried duck boards up track until 4.30. Home at 5.45. Guns howitzers near Flers &amp; 18 prs well up in front of us. Got orders to go out to dugouts in Le Barque 6 of us &amp; prepare position for rest of section at 8.30 in morning.</p>
