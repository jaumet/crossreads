<p>Monday 26th November 1917</p>
<p>Calmer &amp; wife happy &amp; we are now moving a bit south &ndash; to a warmer climate I hope. This is an old boat 27 years &amp; a lucky one so we hope for the best though our loading is all ammunition, so if we are hit, up goes the balloon.</p>
<p>[The remainder of the page crossed through.]<br/>The little wife is better but the world has been very black the last few days. I hope my bad writing will sicken the Censor when he gets as far as this.</p>
