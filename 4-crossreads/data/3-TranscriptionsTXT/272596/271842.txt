 I saw a peculiar &amp; pretty cloud just after tea last night, it was a bright green one &amp; both sides were salmon colored, it remained in the sky for a good 1/2 hour. 
 The bread we have been getting for the past week has been simply shocking being both sour &amp; only half cooked   we are making great noises over it but little improvement has been shown so far,  we took a lot of bananas &amp; cocoa nuts on board at Ceylon &amp; we get a certain amount each day 
 Soap has run out on the ship our canteen hasn't got a scrap so we are forced to fall back on the ships stores, they are taking advantage of this &amp; are charging 2/- a bar for common soap, &amp; we have to give it for soap we cannot go without
