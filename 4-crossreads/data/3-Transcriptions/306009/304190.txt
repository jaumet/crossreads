<p>1915<br/>4 Jan. Mon.<br/>Mess orderly for Nos. 23 and 25 mess.<br/>The Kyarra's run from 12 a.m. yesterday to 12 a.m. day is 283 <strike>days</strike> miles.<br/>It is alright sleeping up on deck but the crew washing down the decks with the hose causes an early disturbance.<br/>Since leaving Colombo we pass shipping daily.<br/>Passed Island of Socatra during the afternoon and Cape Guardafui (eastern most point of Africa about 8 pm.</p>
