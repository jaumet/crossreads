<p>28 December 1918 Saturday<br/>Gen Chauvel inspected us today &amp; said he hoped we would commence being demobilised soon-   He avoided the BEDOUIN massacre- it was wonderful how well the men turned out considering their spree-<br/>The Band had no Band Master &amp; let themselves go the tune was awful-<br/>Alls well, but I am hungering to get home<br/>With love to you all<br/>Frank</p>
