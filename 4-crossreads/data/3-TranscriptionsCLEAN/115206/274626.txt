
February 1918Monday 25
MessinesRained like the very deuce this morning. Left home at 6 am, with Kerr for F.O.P.Spent a rather chilly forenoon and registered Baab FarmIn afternoon, whilst registering Code F, saw a Fritz arise like a spirit under the persistence of an 18 pounder H.E.Arrived home, 6.15 pm. Glorious moonlight night .Fritz dropped a few eggs on supports.Received letters from Doug H, and Mother
