
31 SunW & Fine. Recd letters from Jennie & Marie. Arr ord. Corp.Wrote letters all night.
Wrote Mum. Edie T. Mr Horner.  &  Edie Tdais. Francais.F 1 Apr. MonG  & I go walk round Block & I came Home  &  read all night. Home good supper with Mat [Deldindelles?] who knows a Waac Sgt Cook. Cake very nice.
W 2 Tues.E & I go to Salisbury. Stay at close till 8pm. & then go up to Mrs E. We get caught in the rain round [Hashon?].They all say we will not allow to touch. I find out very different. I am very sick but manage to get Home Ok
W3 WedAm not well. Go to bed at 9pm. D & E talk about Scotland. Top has just returned from here. Recd. Letters from Marion & Maisie H.
Go to see "Facing the Music" with [Pene?] & George Slatter. Very funny.
W 4 ThNett. Comes up for a yarn.
I stay a bed until 9am. Recd. Letter from Dad [reake?] from Marion &  letter. Views of mountain & Sydney come to hand, also paper. Wrote Masie Hudson Wrote MRL. Marion Edwards. Marion Clarke
F 5 FriI print photos with Top. Write to Jennie & PC.of Hockey match to DAD. D Wall & Nick talk all about Nancy [Meron?] Jane
F 6 SatArr ord. Corp. Measles break out  &  I have H of time. Work till nearly 8pm. Then go to my room to read. D & E out all night.
Nothing doing at all.
