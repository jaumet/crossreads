<p>May 1st  at 6 AM we weighed anchor and proceeded to New York on patrol duties, to relieve HMS "Glory" a Battleship, who was ordered to Mediterranean</p>
<p>May 3rd  at 12.15 PM after a pleasant voyage we arrived off Sandy Hook; and drew up alongside of "Glory" &ndash; our captain thereupon paid a visit to Captain of "Glory" immediately after which "Glory" took her departure, and we commenced to patrol.<br/>At 5.15 PM HMCS 'Niobe' arrive abreast of us conveying confidential letters for our captain, our whaler was despatched to bring across letters and then "Niobe" departed at 5.45 PM.</p>
<p>May 4th  at 9.30 AM "Niobe" again arrived alongside and after exchanging signals she again departed.</p>
<p>May 6th  being a suitable day, sub calibre firing exercises were carried out by us.</p>
