<p>Thurs. 23rd:  Crossed Equator.<br/>Sat 1st Feb:  Reached Fremantle 9 a.m:  Went ashore after dinner. Sailed again at 7 p.m.<br/>Thurs 6th:  Arrived Adelaide. Went ashore after dinner<br/>Frid 7th:  Reception in the town<br/>Sat 8th:  Picnic at National Park.<br/>Sun 9th:  Sailed 6 a.m.<br/>Mon 10th:  Arrived at Port Melbourne<br/>Tues 11th:  Pulled in to pier 9 a.m. Went ashore until<br/>Wed 12th:  Sailed 4 p.m.</p>
