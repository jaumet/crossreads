<p>Sun 11th<br/>Rained last night &amp; cloudy today; I woke up during the night with pains in the foot &amp; find it is sore &amp; swollen again today. so the hot fomentations continue . I am on full diet &amp; can find no faults with anything; We put the days in spinning yarns &amp; reading; An enemy plane came in sight this afternoon &amp; the Anti Air Craft guns fired a few shots but she did not come in close.</p>
