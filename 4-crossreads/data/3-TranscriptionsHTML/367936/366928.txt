<p>Friday 4 February 1916<br/>N.C.O's class;  had a go at drilling the troup and got on pretty well.  After breakfast we were drilled by a Sergt. who did not know drill but was most careful we knew it, gave me the blues.  Extended order drill with Squadron.</p>
<p>Saturday 5 February 1916<br/>N.C.O's class then ordinary drill till dinner time.  Came to Sydney had dinner at Sargents then over to Mosman.<br/>Found Hal &amp; Freddie &amp; Edward here and decided to go to White City.<br/>Party of ten of us went and had great time, all went twice on Scenic Railway, got home about 12 o'clock.</p>
