 You at your age will feel the cold It's trying too, when you are old, To have your movements all controlled By Tom or Dick And have to do just what you're told, And do it quick. 
 In summer you will find the heat Terrific &ndash; when with blistered feet And choked with dust, and all but beat You have to march In shifting sand &ndash; not Regent Street Or Marble Arch. The flies too are a perfect pest That all your food &amp; drink infest. You swallow Johnny Walker's best To cool your throttle &ndash; And feel a buzzing in your chest &ndash; A d-d blue bottle.
