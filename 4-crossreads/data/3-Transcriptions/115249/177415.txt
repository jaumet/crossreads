<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Berrima</p>
<p>To - Flag</p>
<p>Date - 13 9 4</p>
<p>Time - 10 30 am</p>
<p>From Brigadier - I was informed yesterday that a British General named Wylde was in Rabaul I understand he was travelling in these parts at outbreak of war&nbsp;and was taken prisoner<strike>s</strike> by Germans and released on parole - I expect him to call at ship today and will advise you later</p>
