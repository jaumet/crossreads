<p>Wednesday 14th June 1916</p>
<p><strike>To</strike> Yesterday we were paraded in front of a clothing board &amp; there a note was made of any kit required.<br/>This morning early we went on the usual route march into the desert, &amp; were inspected there by the camp commandant.  He told us that we should be moving off this week.<br/>Last Sunday a row occurred in a gambling school on account of the use of a double headed penny.
