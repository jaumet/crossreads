<p>They bowled like Spofforth &ndash; at his worst &amp; they hit like Bonnors  &amp; they cheered everything alike &ndash; good &ndash; bad or indifferent &ndash; with running comment on style &ndash; I was 1 Umpire &amp; a man had to be bowled or caught &ndash; I gave them &ndash; well in &ndash; not out &ndash; under any other circumstances &ndash;<br/>Then I blew in on the Una &amp; had drinks &amp; talked &amp; saw a paper of Dec 12 with some account of Sturdee's job of work &ndash; We don't believe in that Submarine attack on Dover &ndash; nothing doing &ndash; We all came off at 6.30 &ndash; Gramophone in the eveg &amp; bed &ndash;</p>
<p>Tuesday<br/>22-12-14  I am going to end this section &ndash; Posting is out of the question &ndash;  The censorship is very strong &ndash; It is impossible to say why &ndash; of course there are troops &amp; sailors &amp; they are indiscreet without intention very often &ndash; I was ashore &amp; in the Gardens in the aftn &ndash; Everything is growing like mad &amp; reeks with heat &amp; damp &ndash; The wet bulb here is equal to &amp; often a degree above the dry bulb &ndash; Tell Rawdon that sailor men call gas, electricity 'Juice' They sing out 'Turn the juice on' 'Turn the juice off' &amp; so on &ndash; It's expressive &ndash; The 'juice' is off for 2 hours &amp; the fans wont work &amp; its very hot &ndash; Commander F gave a dinner party to Holmes &ndash; Jackson, Lambton last eveg on the Fantome &ndash; We go to New Ireland to-morrow on an expedition &ndash;</p>
