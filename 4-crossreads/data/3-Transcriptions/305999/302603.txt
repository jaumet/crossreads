<p>My Father and I went strait out to Crofton to Mrs Newmans where they had the place deccorated with flags and a lot of friends to welcome me home we had a great spread and a dance  I met a lot of my old friends</p>
<p>22/3/18<br/>I went out to south Melbourne to see my grandmother and a few more friends  there was</p>
