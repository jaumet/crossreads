This Comittee wher choosen by the Internees & at the rate of 2 Represantives for each Barrack.  Out of those so sellected, they choosed a President wich had the asistance of a Secretary.  The President acted as spokesman & done all dealings with the Military Authoritys.  At the comencement this proved a Boon to the Camp under the able Leadership of Merchant Burkhart.  We lived in Camp as a great big happy family.  This however fate decidet not to exist for long
