<p>the business centre with the tramway junction quite handy.<br/>Port Said is a very busy port being the shipping entrance to the canal. As soon as the boat is berthed numbers of dealers in oriental goods and fruit row round in pulling boats calling your attention and throwing up ropes with baskets attached. By this means you haul up your purchases.<br/>Numbers of boys are in the water calling out di-di-di and When you throw money into the water they drive after it bringing it to the top and putting it in their mouths.</p>
