 Monday 22 May 1916 Have two machines going clipping the horses.  Lucerne feed in evening.  Heavy swell &amp; head wind. 
 Tuesday 23 May 1916 Hot misty day, sea as calm as glass.  Sighted Cape Guadafui [Guardafui] and came past pretty close.  High cliffs &amp; sandy beaches &amp; a sandy headland.  Passed two villages which we could see with glasses.  Sea teeming with fish, saw 2 sharks, turtles, sword fish pourpoise etc.  Passed 2 dohws.  Turned round headland "The Elephant" in evening.
