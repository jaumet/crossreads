One can warm his Machonichies [Maconochies - a brand name of tinned] rations of meat and vegetables, dry his socks, tunic, greatcoat and blankets and keep warm. Then there is the bread he can toast, make a Dixie of tea. Of course owing to the reflections it casts upon the side of the trench and the fact that the Artillery observers are on the lookout for marks like this knowing there are troops in the vicinity the orders are very strict about them.
