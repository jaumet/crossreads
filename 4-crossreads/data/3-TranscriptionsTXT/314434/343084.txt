I found the railway line, the best track to follow, but even here the enemy kept his machine guns sweeping the road."We had no tanks (on 31st Aug), and no barrage. In the daylight we found ourselves well up to our objective."The 5th Brigade has, in my opinion, only 1000 strong (taking into consideration the previous fighting since 8th August 1918), and I estimate we took double* our own strength prisoners. The Fritzes who opposed us were volunteers to keep the Aussies from Capturing Peronne."The 5th Brigade was mixed up, (18th being in supports), swung to the left, and there left an opening for a successful attack by Fritz, but he neglected it+"What I think is that we had been forcing him to retire, and he had practically completed organizing himself in a position of defence in this very strong position.
