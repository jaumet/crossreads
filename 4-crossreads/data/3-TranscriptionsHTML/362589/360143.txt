The Turks have played the game right through &ndash; Yes you are right &ndash; the 4th were the first ashore after the 3rd Brigade on April 25, but we were kept in reserve all day till 4 p.m. &amp; most unpleasant it was being shelled without an opportunity of hitting back. Soon after 4 p.m. we were rushed up to the right &amp; attached to the 2nd Brigade &ndash; We took up our position between the 6th &amp; 8th Bns. &amp; that first night things were very ticklish.
