Other pictures commemorated matters of history, occupation of Spanish, & formation of Kingdom etc.  In the Grand Place which the building fronts stands a statute commemorating a deed from which the town is said to gain its name.  A giant living in a castle enacted toll from every ship entering, cutting off the hand of any objecting, till a last one gave fight & defeated the giant & the statue shows him hurling the hand of the giant from a ship, the word Antwerpen meaning - hand thrown.  Near the Cathedral is some ironwork, forms, being constructed by on Quentin Massey, a blacksmith, his figure being carved in stone on the Cathedral.
