 Jan 27  th    Raining all the morning. we have our second daily issue of lime juice. 
   
 Jan 28  th    I was chased off deck by the rain at 3.A.M. Jack & Fred took on mess orderly duties for the week - The sea is like a lake in smoothness _ 
   
 Jan 29  th    We have been travelling at half speed for the past three days on account of submarines. We sighted land first thing  ^  this morning  &  got  went into "Freetown" Sierra Leone, which was crowded with all kinds of shipping, including cruisers also a French warship - we bought fruit & cocoanuts from the natives. Bananas 1/- per doz Paw-Paws 3 d  each Oranges 6 d  per doz & Cocoanuts 3 d  each Nobody was allowed ashore as we had up the yellow flag for quarantine. Very warm day - We collected the fruit by letting down the money in a bucket on a rope & hauling the fruit up. from the natives in their little canoes alongside. we each had to wait our turn. for the buckets
