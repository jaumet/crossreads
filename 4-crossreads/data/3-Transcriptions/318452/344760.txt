<p>Tuesday 24th<br/>we left this morning by Motor Busses. It took about 100 Busses (Motor lorries) to take the Brigade &amp; beyond being smothered in dust &amp; a tiresome ride (which even then was much better than walking) we reached our billets in the vicinity of Steenvoorde &amp; here we are tonight as I enter up these records.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26<br/>The weather remains fine. Last night the Bosh counterattacked our new positions heavily.
