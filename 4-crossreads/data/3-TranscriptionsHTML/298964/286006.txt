<p>26 Th April<br/>Terrible hot and dusty  wind enough to burn a chap   having a fairly easy time on D Column.  Chums don't seem too bad.  Very quiet on the front</p>
<p>27 Fri April<br/>Very cool day wish for drops of rain had a drop of SJ  Fokkers very busy otherwise very quiet with heavy Guns in action</p>
<p>28 Sat April<br/>Had a few drops of rain, also very cool otherwise very quiet  A few Fokkers over</p>
