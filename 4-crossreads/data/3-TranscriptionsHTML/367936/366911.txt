<p>Saturday 1 January 1916</p>
<p>Mother, Father, and all of us went over to the Lease for a picnic.  Spent the afternoon burning hollow logs and shooting rabbits.</p>
<p>Sunday 2 January 1916</p>
<p>Played tennis nearly all day.<br/>Malone came and got Dick's dog "Jack".  All hands went training up tomatoes in the vegitable garden in the evening.<br/>Had some hymns after tea.</p>
