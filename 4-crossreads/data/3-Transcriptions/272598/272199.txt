<p>Quite a treat to get somewhere where it is a bit quiet</p>
<p>Wednesday 20.6.17<br/>Slight disturbance between a couple of our Sergeants.  Parade preparatory to inspection by G.O.C. to-morrow.  Two N.C.Os &amp; 22 men went on leave to Amiens.  One of our men placed under mental observation; general opinion is that he is working his ticket.</p>
<p>Thursday 21.6.17<br/>Inspection postponed owing to rain till to-morrow.  Acting postman for the day.  Played Baseball in the evening.</p>
<p>Friday 22.6.17<br/>Camp all in a state owing to the inspection by General Hobbs which took place at 11.15 Am.  Again played the role of postman.  Received</p>
