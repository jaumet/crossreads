<p>How is the allotment?</p>
<p>Cape Town 21/9/16</p>
<p>Dear Mother<br/>We had a few hours leave here but it was at night &amp; we could not see the town. We just strolled about &amp; had two big feeds. Some of the buildings are very fine &amp; the table Mountain is wonderful. Am feeling very well.<br/>Love to all<br/>Dene</p>
