<p>France  2.6.17<br/>[Transcriber's note:The following words have been added in another hand: Belgium &ndash; see page 3]<br/>My dear Father,</p>
<p>Your postcards of 23rd &amp; letter of 30th March duly reached me &amp; for which many many thanks  Your letters make me really ashamed of the miserable attempts I send you in return, but know you will understand the difficulty one finds in writing anything like a collected letter these times, and also the scarcity of news. First let me say how glad I am that Pat is still with you &amp; hope he will remain so a long time. There is nothing much to be gained in rushing across especially  if he has a chance of promotion on that side. I saw Les a few days ago. He had just returned from Leave &amp; was down to have a yarn. He</p>
