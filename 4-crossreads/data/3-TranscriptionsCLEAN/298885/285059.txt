
Sun. Apr. 9th -16I was made Brigade Orderly and not a bad job either.
Mon. Apr. 10th -16Witnessed first air-duel between balloon and 2 aeroplanes about 4 miles away.
Tues. Apr. 11th -16Horses excercised in morning and inoculated during afternoon.
Thur. Apr. 13th -16Heavy Snow storm.  SOME cold believe me.
Fri. Apr. 14th -16Still snowing on and off.  Groomed horses in a heavy fall.
Sat. Apr. 15th -16Warned to be ready to leave
