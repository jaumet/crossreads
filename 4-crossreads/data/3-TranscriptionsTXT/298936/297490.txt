 December, 1915 
 Monday 6 
 Morphia last night whilst away on the right is a case where the lower part of the jaw has been blown away. 
 He has to write on paper for what he wants. 
 One small hospital ship in but that utilized for walking cases, having no accommodation for beds. 
 Heavy shelling going on all the time around Hospital. 
 No. 16 Hospital bombarded &amp; one tent smashed to pieces.  About 20 casualties. Poor fellows.
