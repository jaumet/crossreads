<p>Sunday 28 July 1918<br/>Played tennis &amp; read a good deal.</p>
<p>Monday 29 July 1918<br/>Dance at the OTB.  Took Rup &amp; reached home at 3 a.m. on Tuesday.</p>
<p>Tuesday 30 July 1918<br/>Was warned for overseas &ndash; first warning.</p>
<p>Wednesday 31 July 1918<br/>Saw the S.M.O. Sutton Veny but don't know what his verdict was.</p>
<p>Thursday 1 August 1918<br/>Into Warminster with the draft.  Back at 11.30.</p>
<p>Friday 2 August 1918<br/>Read "Winged Warfare" by Bishop, V.C., D.S.O. &amp; M.C.  <strike>Went into Warminster with</strike>  Hear that I am to have 2 weeks more H.S.</p>
<p>Saturday 3 August 1918<br/>Drove Rup into Warminster to do some shopping.  Was caught in the rain.  Wrote Robinson &amp; Mrs. Cleaver for collars.</p>
