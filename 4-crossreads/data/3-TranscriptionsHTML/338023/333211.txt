<p>You must be having a darned rotten time in the office &ndash; but stick to it; I've got a 'kinder hunch' you'll get out on top in the end.</p>
<p>Tell old Jim I'm sorry to hear that Resch's have gone off &amp; hope they'll recover their quality by the time I get back to Maroubra. Had a letter from Brookie's cobber &ndash; you'll know who I mean &ndash; but no parcel has arrived yet. Brook sent his kind regards etc, so don't forget to return same from me next time you see him.</p>
<p>I hope to have more news before long &ndash; when we get on the guns in earnest, in the meantime must ring off as its <strike>nearly</strike> near to Last Post &amp; I'm afraid to go home in the dark on a/c of the flood.</p>
<p>By Gum, if old Noah was here in Seymour, he'd have a lively time sending out doves!</p>
<p>Yrs to a 'D.A.'<br/>Harry</p>
