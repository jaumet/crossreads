  
 Blangy Tronville 
 [7 Sun] Went to Boves for S.A.A. 
 [8 Mon] Cleaning rifles Mounted Piquet 6 pm. 
 [9 Tues] Grooming and loading waggons 
 [10 Wed] Left Blangy Tronville 12 noon. Arr. At our next stopping place (about a Kilo outside Boves) about 2 pm. Fixed up lines then build our dugouts. Camped in an orchard (About seven kilos from Amiens.)
