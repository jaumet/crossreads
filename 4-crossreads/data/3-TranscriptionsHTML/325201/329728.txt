<p>No, this summer I have always or almost always had enough water, &amp;   practically no stunts <br/>From April to June I was with the CTC near the sea<br/>June in reserve with the Unit At Abasan el Kebir, July <strike> wi</strike> at Beui Sela with the Clearing Station doing in a trip to the sea every day<br/>August &amp;  September on the Beach with the Unit resting. Then a week only in the dust at Kilo 9 &amp;  now on the beach again<br/>How long we are here for I don't know,  Public opinion</p>
