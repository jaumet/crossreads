<p>swirling round like large hornets.  Once this morning a flight of Bosch planes came over and shot down one of our planes.  The ground for miles round here is torn up badly by shellfire.  At one time a fine wood and the town of Hooge existed here but not a vestige of the village remains and only a few dead stumps can be seen to mark the one time wood.  Timber lies every where.</p>
