<p>April, 1916<br/>Tuesday 11<br/>Today when I got to the hosp I was plainly told that the men for the Karoola had left at 6 am.<br/>He had telephoned to the school as soon as he knew but no message had been given to me<br/>As soon as I got back I wrote him a letter to Suez.</p>
<p>Wednesday 12<br/>Work as usual<br/>At dusk sky looks very over-cast &amp; we expect the awful sand winds known as the "Camseen" (phonetic spelling) so called because they continue for a period of 50 days on &amp; off.<br/>Khamsin</p>
