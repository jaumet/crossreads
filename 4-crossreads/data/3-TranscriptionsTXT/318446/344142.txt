Nov. 10th 1916My mind is fast giving way I am afraid, for now I am quite content to lie flat on my back & do nothing, idly speculating & indulging in wholly fantastic suppositions, for hours at a time.And this was a thing I was physically incapable of a year ago, then I was acutely discontent when I was occupied with a book or something.
