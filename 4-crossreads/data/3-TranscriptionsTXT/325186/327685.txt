 that of the Hororata  We lived mostly from the canteens on both boats.  We steamed out of Alexandria on Tuesday 13th June with a gunboat as escort. We kept close to the African Coast  pretty well all the way to Gibraltar when we were about three days out from Alexandria our escort changed from a gunboat to two destroyers.  We pulled in to Gibraltar on Tuesday 19th June &amp; C &amp; D Coys of 34th Btn were doing submarine guard when we came to Gibraltar they dismounted us  We only stayed two hours &amp; steamed out &amp; we had to mount guard again.
