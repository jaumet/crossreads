Friday 8 November
March from Villers to Hangest where we entrain move off en route to Epehy, just north of Roisel.
Saturday 9 November
Ye Gods! we have only reached Epehy this evening after thirty hours to do 90 miles. In fact one stop lasted at Roisel for 7 hours. Epeny is of course just West of the Hindenburg System the country is devastated.
