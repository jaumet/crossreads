<p>when the water is frozen day &amp; night.  All day the bombardment by our guns has been nerve-racking.  Fritz has been dropping big shells all round our track and in places the earth is all churned up in a frightful manner right where we have to cross.  Luckily enough our cases came just at the right time so that none of us were touched.  This afternoon things got busy again.  Fritz caught the 29th Batt.</p>
