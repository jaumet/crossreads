 16th Down for swim.  More shelling 17th 0400 on watch. 0545 plane goes up &amp; sends Jobb 6.  Spotting for M21 X M 31. standing off Bela.  Heavy bombardment begins.  W18 relaying the spotting from the plane.  Go for swim &amp; watch the monitors shelling.  Go for another swim during the afternoon with Missen &amp; watch the cruisers &amp; monitors.  Shell the redoubt.  Salvos end after tea. 18/4/17 Heavy shelling all day. 19-4-17 Very heavy bombarding.  Continual roll like thunder. 20/4/17 War still continues pack up &amp; leave for Squadron leaving W2.  Hun submarine fires two torpedoes at cruiser one of which lands high &amp; dry on beach. 21-4-17 Up 4 am &amp; down for the mules 136 arriving.  Great fun on the way back to camp. 22-4-17 Heavy sea running.  Get thrown 4 times. 23 &ndash; Bombardment continues. Mules give trouble by breaking away. 24-25-26-27 [indecipherable]  Go to Khan Yunus for 9 reinforcements and W1.  A dirty hot day.  Taubes cause great trouble over bombs.  Two brought down. 28/4/17 Go on cooking in place of mules.  17 reinforcements arrive. 29/4/17 Heavy seas wet night. 1/5/17 Jacko expected to attack 2/5/17 No attack. 3/4/5th 10 PM another night air raid causes great excitement. 4th 21 reinforcements to arrive.
