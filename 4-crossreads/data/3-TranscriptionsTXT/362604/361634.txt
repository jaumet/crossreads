
Mess.  I finished my business at 6 p.m.  I booked my Room in the Strand at my usual place & then had Tea at the Aldwych Theatre which has been taken over as a Y.M.C.A. establishment by the A.I.F. Y.M. Association.
I went to Drury Lane Theatre at night to see "Samson & Delilah".  It was grand opera & pretty heavy.  The stage is such a long way from the seats in the circle that I could hardly keep my eyes open in the second Part.  But the orchestra is wonderful.  It is supposed to be the best in the world.
