<p>These portions have hardly know that a war was on, excepting of course the privations they have suffered. &nbsp;Still, they have had very little of their places knocked about, like we&#39;ve seen further back.</p>
<p>We have filled in our Demobilisation Paper, but I can&#39;t get to know what is doing at all in that matter. &nbsp;I hear that they are clearing home all the men who are in England, and the Times stated on Dec 24 that between January and May there would be over 50 transports</p>
