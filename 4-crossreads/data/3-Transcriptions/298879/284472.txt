<p>November 1915<br/>Wednesday 24<br/>Cold wind. Turkish gun shelling beach. Beachy Bill I suppose, he is very busy lately</p>
<p>Thursday 25<br/>Cold. Trenches wet and slippery. Turks hit a trawler with a howitzer but did very little damage apparently.</p>
