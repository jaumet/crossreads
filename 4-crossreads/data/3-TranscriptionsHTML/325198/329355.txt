<p>2 Sept, 1916<br/>Montazah<br/>The world has got me snowed just a treat;<br/>Crool Forchin's dirty left 'as smote my soul;<br/>An all them joys o life I 'eld so sweet<br/>Is up the pole;<br/>Fer 'as the poit sez, me 'eart 'as got<br/>The pip wiv yearnin' fer ---- I dunno wot.</p>
