TUESDAY, OCTOBER 7, 1919.
Came on to rain about 8 AM & rained
till midday. Had 33 points.
Lopped in Top paddock in afternoon
morning & cutting cotton bush in
Wheelers in afternoon.
Tickle lopped in morning & drew
water to Darby in afternoon
Darby got water about 300 gal
per hour at 125 ft. He is going a
bit deeper & he thinks he will get
more.
