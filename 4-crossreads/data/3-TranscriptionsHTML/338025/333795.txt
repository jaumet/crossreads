<p>Monday 17th September 1917<br/>Nr Reninghelst<br/>2.15am harnessed up and moved out of the camp by 3am (6 teams).<br/>Went through Dickebusch and Ypres. Fritz shelling Ypres, not a building has escaped the terrible shell fire. The Cathedral and clotlehall are in ruins. Filled our wagons with sleepers steel girders etc. For 10th Battery gun pits about &frac12; mile passed Hell fire corner. Dead horses, mules,metal wagons and timbers lying all over the place. Fritz sending over coal boxes, and old rubber gut into Ypres. Arr. back I camp about 11.45am</p>
