 6 January 1918 Epifhany Sunday Jack Mack came into my Bivy &amp; had a yarn just received PLUM PUDDING from  May  please thank her for me. Special Sunday service today   Am Field Officer.  Raining hard &amp; Church Parade cancelled -  The country is bare having been eaten out by the Turks, but grass should grow soon-  We get 28 inches in 12 weeks- Jany &amp; Feb wettest month of the year Letter from wife
