<p>2.6.15<br/>Wed. 2nd.  I believe the blowing up at Quinns Post was quite a success last night &amp; things were fairly lively for a while.  At 9.30 a.m. the 3rd L.H. came in to take our place &amp; we were moved further up Popes Hill.  One of the 3rd L.H. officers was shot dead (hit through the eye) while taking over the position.  Last night just before dark a New Zealand <strike>er</strike> Engineer, who has done good work here &amp; received a D.C.M. a couple of days ago, was sitting by our trenches smoking when one of the Japanese bombs was fired on Quinns Post &amp; a piece of the shell came across cutting the Engineers throat &amp; he bled to death in a few moments.
