<p>25-2-17<br/>have to wait till 3 oclock fo it. So I did not wait &amp; went to Heliopolis at 1.30 had a general clean up &amp; a shave &amp; drove to Abbassia arriving there at 3 oclock put in till 4 yarning with some of the boys in Hospital &amp; then took Hills for a garry drive around Heliopolis &amp; Zeitoun arriving back at 5.30; I then drove back to Heliopolis for dinner &amp; met Jessie at the Hospital at 8 oclock &amp; drove into Cairo with her, arriving back at Abbassia at 10 P.M. I then drove back to Camp &amp; after a good deal of hunting in the dark I managed to find the right hut &amp; turned in</p>
