<p>26/7/16<br/>being releved to night  shelling not so bad but still plenty of it feel fairly tired no sleep etc.<br/>began to move out at 11.30 PM<br/>Fritz is still shelling the road but I think we will get through.</p>
<p>27.7.16<br/>Got out alright marched althrough the night to Albert got there at 5.30 AM had a drink of tea and tried to sleep but was too tired</p>
<p>28.7.16<br/>moved back another 9 miles slept in</p>
