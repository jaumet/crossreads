<p>January, 1915<br/>Wednesday 13<br/>Went ashore &amp; had dinner at the Grand Oriental Hotel then a car to Mrs [indecipherable] &amp; the [indecipherable] Gardens. Finishing up with a box at Grace Palottas show.<br/>Washing sent ashore came back beaten to pieces &amp; yellow.<br/>The currency here is Rupees &amp; cents. But English gold is always acceptable.<br/>Soldiering is an expensive luxury.</p>
