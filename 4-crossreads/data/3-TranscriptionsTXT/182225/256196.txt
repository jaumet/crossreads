FEB. &nbsp; THURSDAY 10 &nbsp; 1916
Showery all day. Put up a tent for the Sargent &nbsp;Farrier, &amp; lay in it &amp; played cards all day &nbsp;Nicked away early &amp; met Linda &amp; Jo on wharf &amp; came over with them. Mr&nbsp;&amp; Mrs&nbsp;Norman Murray came over after tea but I had to go just as they arrived.
