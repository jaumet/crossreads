
At 11A.M. the rain fell in sheets.
25th  We reached Colombo & dropped anchor in the harbour about 3.30p.m. Much speculation as to whether we will be allowed ashore or not.
6 P.M. Instructions received from the shore authorities to the effect that all troops may go off our boat but that the town must be strongly picketed.
26th  All Companies fell in on their Parade decks at 7.30 A.M. Picket parties were told off & we went filed on to barges.  At 9.15 A.M. a small tug pulled the whole bulk of the 6 huge barges
