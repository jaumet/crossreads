<p>raining hard last night &ndash; an extraordinary thing here. "Ascanius" passed in Suez direction. Sheepskin vests issued. They were presented by a person in Australia. Bitterly <strike>clol</strike> cold day. Thousands more Australian troops arriving. A pontoon bridge  was towed down. This is to be the means of crossing the Canal at Serapeum.</p>
<p>26th Jan (Wednesday)<br/>Bridge now completed over the Canal and transport going across. Shipping is stopped between certain hours in the daytime. Bitterly cold. Kit inspection.</p>
