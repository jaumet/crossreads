 Thursday Oct 5.10.16 Stuffing saddles to day Friday Oct 6.10.16 Branding camels to day number of my camel 2824. Saturday Oct 7.10.16 Preparing to leave for the desert to day went into heilopolis to night returned to camp and packed up. Sunday Oct 8.10.16 Revallie 4 o'clock had Breakfast 5. saddled up and rode to olbassia left about 8 o'clock arrived at Alfadan 5.p.m unloaded saddled up crossed canal and camped five miles out.
