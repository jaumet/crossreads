
June
June 21st(Longest Day (Daylight at 10pm and 2.30am.More German destruction at Armentieres - I need a couple of Sydney Boys (H.H.) - Gas Helmet inspection - German shells land all round our building but fail to hit it. In steel helmets all but four of us evacuate to the trench.
June 22ndNight Duty in D.R - Gas alert - We are warned of the approaching danger of gas, just as the alarms in the trenches ring out. - False alarm. Correspondence
June 23rdFritz quieter - Thunder storm. - soldiers from Balmain - Food Tres bon as is usual at Bois Grenier.
June 24thCribbage - We return from Bois Grenier to Ft Rompu - A new "soccer" ball gives us plenty of sport.
