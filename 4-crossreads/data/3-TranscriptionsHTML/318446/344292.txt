<p>I could stay quiet &amp; comfortable in our dugout &amp; felt more or less safe but still they were very unpleasant.  None dropped very close to us, though I was surprised that they didn't have a go at the camels.</p>
<p>10 p.m., 5th May<br/>Tonight of course our 'planes have been on the rampage.  We suspected that they were our 'planes but still we felt impelled to blow the light out &amp; give little apprehensive shivers as they droned their way overhead.  Queer how nervous this sort of thing makes one.</p>
<p>10 p.m., 6th May<br/>For the second time in three days our nightly game of crib has been interrupted.<br/>Those Taubes have been at it again!<br/>Dropped about 20 bombs &amp; set fire to our supply dump at railhead but fortunately none came near us.  Our A.A. guns replied</p>
