There were 12 young men who signified their determination to serve Christ.  It was a splendid time. Wrote a letter home at night. 
 Monday Capt. McCook &amp; I went to Houplines to the Canteen &amp; I went on down to the Trenches.  Yesterday a shell killed the Colonel of the 36th &amp; another officer.  They were both buried this afternoon.  At the Funeral were many officers &amp; while there a messenger came to tell all officers to report to their Commanders at once &amp; the guns began to boom all round.
