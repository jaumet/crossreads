<p>but it was not your fault the moths ate it.<br/>Thank him very much all the same for me.<br/>I think I will wear navy blue next time I get a suit. &ndash; I think I shall send you a cable wire from W.A.to let you know when I am coming &amp; you might order me a suit then. If they have no serge you like I will like anything you choose, - but really I think I should pay for it myself &ndash; don't you?</p>
<p>FJP.</p>
