<p>8.2.15</p>
<p>Mon 8th<br/>Signalitis, Nelson, Mills, Farrell. Hobson, Cobcroft left here this morning on bikes &amp; had to be a Mena by 10 oclock,  they had twenty rounds of ammunition each &amp; their rifles &amp; blankets besides, the signalling equipment to carry, so they are loaded up &amp; expect to get back tomorrow night.<br/>we were signalling under Cpl. Howarth this morning; after dinner the Brigade went out sham fighting till dark, &amp; then dismounted, fed our horses &amp; had one piece of bread &amp; cheese &amp; one piece of bread &amp; jam for tea &amp; then set to work digging trenches &amp; did not finish</p>
