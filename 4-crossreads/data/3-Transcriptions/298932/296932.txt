<p>Monday 9<br/>It is cold &amp; miserable<br/>The Division are out in the field on a big dress rehersal affair<br/>Tuesday 10<br/>On the road to Beulancourt having a yarn to my old friend Snarky<br/>Wednesday 11<br/>A hopeful dawn a cheerful morn<br/>We will, I think be on the long long trail to Aus before Xmas<br/>Thursday 12<br/>Old Nutty looked as proud as a new papa when decorated yesterday by the "General" with the BLUE RIBBON</p>
