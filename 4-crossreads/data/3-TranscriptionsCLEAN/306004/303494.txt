
21.8.16Mon 21stHad a bad time myself last night from diarrhoea was on the go all night & and there is a large parade (sick) this morning, I have been feeling very sick all day & have done nothing by lay about doing nothing; Our horses are being inoculated against glanders today; The troops around Bir-El-Abd have been busy lately digging up all the Turkish graves & have recovered it is asserted over 1,000,000 rounds. The Turks dumped the ammunition in a  hole & then put a layer of sand then a dead body & then filled it in.A few more Turks came in today in an exhausted condition they arrived in camp tonight.B Sqdn went on swimming parade this morning
