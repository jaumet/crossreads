Saw some fine paintings &amp; some that would not be hung in Australia without a curtain in front.  One room of statuary very fine indeed.  Returned to starting place &amp; had a cheaper dinner than usual (funds be perilously low) for 5 francs then returned to hotel for our gear &amp; to settle up, then to the station to catch the 2 P.M for Charleroi.
