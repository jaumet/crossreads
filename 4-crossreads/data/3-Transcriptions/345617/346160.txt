<p>Aout 26<br/>Slack day today. A little Coy work. A lot of the boys away on leave to St Omer, Arques &amp; other villages.</p>
<p>Aout 27<br/>Very busy getting things ready for big review by General [blank] on 29th. Weather  very nasty &ndash; windy &amp; heavy showers.</p>
