Tuesday 17thPlanes over again no harm done. Saw one in the Secret light. A very rough little thunder storm early in the morning. Had to make a little dam inside our door way to stop the water running in.  Going out with some Dummie Tanks tonight for a big attack in the morning. Left camp about 8 & loaded the Tanks on the light railway at Tenecourt. They are excellent imitation. Wooden frame covered with canvas. They run on 3 wheels.
