  
 SWAN CHATEAU Monday 10 Getting ready to go into action a big hop over, I beleive is our mission The battalion is out on its final practice. Tuesday 11 Reville this morning at three oclock and then a starlight march to the parade ground for field manoeuvre's Wednesday 12 The way was long, we left Claumarais at 8, passed through Staple Hardajem & Castra arriving at Winnzelle just at nightfall. Going into action Thursday 13 Aroused at 4 this morning fell in a 5,30 marched through Steenvoorde & Arbelle to Renilhest. 50 KILO'S in 2 days and we are loaded like camel's
