 We marched out of Dernancourt along a congested road, each mile bringing us more & more into the realization of things.  At first we began to pass our reserve lines of trenches & wire entanglments (destined now never to be used)  Then such things a little wooden crosses; shell battered walls, & now & again demolished buildings presented themselves.
