Alexandria EgyptMay 22Wed. Finished loading gun carriages field kitchens etc. &amp; awaiting the arrival of troops for embarking.
23 The 1st wooden standard ship arrived here at Alexandria from the U.S.A. Very poor specimen of a steamer. The K.I.Hind, Malwa &amp; Leasowe Castle embarking troops. Rumour of the new Orient 14,500 ton steamer the Orland going to replace the Omrah in this convoy. News through this day another 2 German subs sunk by French distroyers yesterday.
24This day embarked 2000 troops from Palastine &amp; 30 sisters for the Western front of France. Fine hardy chaps. Sunburnt &amp; the majority been away here for the last 3 years &amp; over.5 pm left the wharf &amp; all leave stopped for anchorage.
25Still at anchor. All the troopships full. 20,000 awaiting the escort to arrive. All troops told off to boat stations &amp; raft &amp; lifebelt drill. As per usual the deck swarmed with troops.
26Sunday. 10 am boat drill11 am religious services of different denominations
