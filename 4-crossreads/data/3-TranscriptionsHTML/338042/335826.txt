<p>-Night Also.<br/>Mar. 10th<br/>On Sunday morning Mr Wm Scales &amp;amp self go strolling &ndash; "Parliament Hill &ndash; Hampstead Heath etc.  &ndash; The strange but common sight of "Garden Plots" amp; "their workers".<br/>Return to my home at "Elm Bank &ndash; Murray Sinclair visits us in afternoon &ndash; Evening spent with Mr. &amp; Mrs. Harold Scales &ndash; Their two "sweet &amp; pretty children". Return Elm Bank 11 p.m.</p>
