The Medical officer shall visit the patient while under restraint in the jacket, at least twice in every 24 hours & oftener if required.  He also will keep a Record of the day & hour it was finaly discontinued & the reason for its employment.  The restraint shall not be continued longer than it is necessary & never for more than 24 hours conseclutively.  If after that period the Medical officer deems it necessary again to order this jacket, 1 hour at least must elapse before it is reapplied, & he must make a record of the fact as before & continue his visits, as specified above & the same report shall be made as on first ocasion.  When the Restraint is discontinued, the medical officer shall inform the Camp Comandant in writing.  Leg Chains or Cross Irones :  are to be used only by the Order of the Visitor when a pris. under sentence has atempted to escape or to commit an asult
