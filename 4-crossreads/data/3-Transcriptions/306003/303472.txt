<p>11-8-16<br/>Fri 11th<br/>Had a good rest last night &amp; were not called till 5.30 this morning &amp; the sun was up when we awoke;  Watered our horses at the same Hod as yesterday &amp; had a very quiet time today resting &amp; picking dates that are ripe enough to eat.  these are very bitter with the skin on but very good fruit something like apples to taste when peeled</p>
<p>This morning we could hear the artillery to the east &amp; we received word this evening saying the infantry advanced to Bir-El-Abd, &amp; the Turks left early this morning with very little fighting<br/>Every man in camp that could be mounted on any sort of a horse was sent out &amp; joined us today, we are getting plenty of water &amp; rations here</p>
