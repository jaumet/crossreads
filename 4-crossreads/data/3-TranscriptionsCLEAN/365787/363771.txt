
[Card]8.10.17
Dymock's Building68 Elizabeth StreetSYDNEY
Dear Madam,
I have to acknowledge with many thanks receipt of your parcel, containing 37 pairs of socks & 4 knitted Balaclavas.
Yours faithfully,"WAR CHEST" SUPPLY DEPOT,[Signed] J. M. AntillHon. Superintendentper M.A.
2 of these socks were knitted from Wahroonga-Warrawee wool.  M.M.J.
