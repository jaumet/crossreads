<p>2 limbers Northern Sector, 2 limbers Southern Sector, 2 limbers Headquarters, 7 limbers &amp; 1 G.S. Wagon waited in Ribemont to convey troops to Franvillers.<br/>Relief reported complete at 11 p.m.<br/>Troops given hot meal on arrival at Franvillers.  Battalion Order for march to Corps Reserve received.  Instructions based on same issued.  Rear Coy. Hdqrs. moved from Franvillers to Querrieu.</p>
<p>11.5.18 &ndash; Franvillers<br/>Reveille 5 a.m.  Breakfast 5.30 a.m.  Men paraded at 7.45 a.m.  Transport reported 7.30 a.m. and fighting stores &amp; officers kits loaded.  Company marched to Querrieu, passing starting point at 8.10 a.m. via La Houssoyle [La Houssoye] and Pont Noyelles, arriving at camp in Chateau grounds at 10.15 a.m.</p>
<p>Querrieu<br/>The 22nd Aust. M.G. Coy. still occupied lines and did not move out till 2 p.m.  Camp taken over and men allotted to tents.  A guard consisting of 1 N.C.O. and 3 men mounted on limbers at 6 p.m.</p>
