
Wednesday 24 October
Cold winds - dull and showeryTo 1st Anzac Corps. Hd. Q. SigsTo A.D.M.S. 5th Aust. Div. Hd. Qd. at Ypres via Poperinghe and VlamertinghePlaying cards after teaOver Ypres the aeroplanes were very busy about midday.  The air was thick with them.  A few German new planes of the huge variety were over and ours tried unsuccessfully to attack them.  Men came down in paraschutes from their balloons.
