 Jan. 2 Saturday 1915 Troopship Suevic changed position falling in rear of Thermosticles 
 Submarine cast off from Berrima accompanying the Squadron as protector. Orders issued that all lights out as far as possible. 
 Jan. 3 Sunday 1915 2 Horses died. Weather getting warmer smooth sea. Averaging over 11 knots per hour. "Ayrshire"falling back. Examination of rifles Fire drill &amp; boat parade. Berrima Hauled to for the purpose of replenishing submarine with oil. 
 Jan. 4 Monday 1915 Weather calm &amp; fine 9th day vaccanated, feverish, off tucker, went to bed early. Temperature 101 Washing day
