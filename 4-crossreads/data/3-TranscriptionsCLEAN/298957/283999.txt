  
 23rd April (Easter Sunday) Fairly hot. No Church Parade Sports meeting held to make arrangements for a good time on Anzac Day. 
 24th April (Easter Monday) Hot. Carpentering and Lecture in morning. Enemy aeroplane flew over the other day dropping a few bombs on Serapeum. It is believed that a few were killed. 
 25th April (Tuesday - Anzac Day) After carrying an appendicitis case to Road Head North where he was met by an Ambulance Waggon, we came back to a fine dinner of Pineapples,
