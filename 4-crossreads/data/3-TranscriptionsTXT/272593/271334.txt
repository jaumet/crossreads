In this billet we have a great big open fireplace &amp; it takes our resources to keep it going, yesterday afternoon Jack Price &amp; a few mates chopped a good lump of a green tree down &amp; just as it fell, down rushed a Frenchie in a fit he danced round &amp; round the tree jabbering &amp; chattering &amp; throwing his hands about.
