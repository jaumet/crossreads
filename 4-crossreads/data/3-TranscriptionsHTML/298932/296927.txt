<p>Friday 22<br/>Boxing to day as usual I made the crowd laugh with my antic's<br/>Saturday 23<br/>The hun got lively last night our boys were standing to all night, but was'ent needed<br/>Sunday 24<br/>We get up at 4 oclock in the morning which is really 3 AM owing to the daylight saving and as we go to bed at nightfall 11 PM tis a long long day, the front is very very quite</p>
