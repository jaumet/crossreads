 Back to Presles early, and a haircut and a bath. 
 Waiting now till 9, when a celebrating to midnight commences in a room in the villiage.  In the house the Engrs. E.O. is billetted:  our point is the billet is unofficial!  By his manner a while ago &ndash; requesting quietness! he's a Tommy - or of close extraction. 
 Things 'swim'! 
 January 1st, 1919 17. A good evening:  and by a lucky chance, Racy as Drinking Partner:  he, Laurie and self saw each other home as far as where the roads cross the stream by the Chateau Gateway. 
 Morning and a heavy waking:  afternoon alone to Gaugnies, mostly by the fields.  The day evenly grey and a thin sun behind woven clouds.
