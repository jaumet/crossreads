<p>January 1918<br/>Thursday 3</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Worked during morning in accordance with our Routine which is Reveille, 7.15. Stables 7.30, Breakfast 8, Parade at Gun Park, 9.30am and detailed for duties. Stables 11am. Dinner 12.30 pm. Stables 4 pm. Dismiss 4.30 pm.</p>
<p>There has been a very severe frost and the result has left us with ice bound roads. Mac, proceeded to Boulogne to see his Sister who is nursing in the 29th General.<br/>Soccer team had a victory, 2 to nil . Numerous members of W.A.A.C. watched the game in the company of the fellows of our battery.</p>
