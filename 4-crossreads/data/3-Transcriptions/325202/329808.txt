<p>July 18 (Tuesday) Varennes<br/>arrived here this afternoon at 4 p.m having come today from Val de Maison a distance of 11 miles where we had stopped 2 days when it rained the whole time. Our waggons are pulled in alongside the village street &ndash; The horses in a paddock</p>
