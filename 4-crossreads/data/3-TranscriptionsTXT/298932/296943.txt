 "OCHTEZEELE" Friday 17 Peace floating about but I think it will float over the bulldog as his teeth well in and is not going to let go now Saturday 18 Harvest time in Flander's Ripe is the golden grain, And within my heart a feeling To be back home again A long route march to Arniques Sunday 19 An Australian mail in 2 letters for me, Els &amp; Mue I wrote a little article of prose and sent it to the editor of "Blighty" Down on the farm making butter and doing a bit of haymaking
