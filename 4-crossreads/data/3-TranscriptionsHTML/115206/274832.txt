<p>September 1918.<br/>Thursday 19</p>
<p>Rgt of Bernes. Bty Position.<br/>1 am sitting [indecipherable] the rain for five hours, drenched to skin, and blankets in a similar condition. All of Br in the same pickle. Moved to a new position i.e. our spooks dugout. <br/>Weather still about the same . <br/>Had a letter from Ken Gunn who has had is Rgt leg amputated at the knee and I'm very sorry hear the news.<br/>Nicely settled in new dugout which we have cleaned up. Talbot proceeded on leave and dropped his dixies.</p>
