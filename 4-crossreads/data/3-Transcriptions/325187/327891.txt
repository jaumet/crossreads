<p>[Sunday 28 May 1916]</p>
<p>Letter found these come by degrees from Divisional Hd Qts- Sunday<br/>I have some unexploded Aeroplane Bombs &amp; Turkish Field Artillery shells, but cannot carry along.  Curios lose interest when a man is hungry.<br/><strike>I send the notes on to ISH in England she likes to have them after you send them to me.</strike><br/>Sticky clothes again like the Salt Water washing on the Peninsular</p>
