
We went close in past Wrossel Island - It lay on our Port side - about half a mile off - Green & lovely & is said to have Head hunters living on it - About 2-4 miles out from it you notice - on your attention being called to them - Solitary trees, standing up out of the water - Really they are trees (?Coco palms) growing on a coral fringing reef - Between this reef & the island is a vast lagoon - Gordon was in there with the Warrego he tells me - Our rolling ended when once we got close in - We put in a true naval eveng - cards - beer - talk - Talk of a ribald kind but some of it was very funny - Walker & Nelson have been most everywhere & in all sorts of jobs - got into trouble - N. I fancy has done time in a foreign gaol but you cant believe half they say - Still when it came to having a good time - they had it - anywhere - anyhow - About 10.30 we all turned in & slept well -Friday
