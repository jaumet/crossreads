<p>Lieut. F. Thompson proceeded on leave.<br/>Weekly inspection of Transport took place on the 3rd.  Report received today stating that everything was correct.<br/>Concentration of fire, Nil.  Two thousand rounds fired on the following targets:-<br/>Ceylon Farm, C.6.c.90.05., Traversing &amp; Searching of Road, C.5.d.77.38 to 86.60., Junction of Trenches, C.11.d.65.56., Traversing &amp; Searching Road to Deulemont C.12.c.00.40 to 37.30., Working Party C.11.d.30.50.  Observed from O.P. flash lights.  No movement seen after fire was directed against them.<br/>Both enemy's Artillery and Machine guns were very quiet throughout day and night.  Weather dull, slight rain, but observation good.<br/>Concentration of fire during day, Nil.</p>
