<p>Sept<br/>Tuesday 18<br/>Have been B.O.M today not a bad job but you are kept moveing</p>
<p>Wednesday 19<br/>Did 15 minutes PT for the days work Rained most of the day. Concert in evening quite a good one. Payed 10/-</p>
<p>Thursday  20th<br/>As usual. The hut was very quiet last night considering it was pay day.</p>
<p>Thursday  21st<br/>As usual. Good concert in evening. Had 2 Aussie letters today</p>
<p>Saturday 22nd<br/>Went over the hill in front of the Camp as a start for the march did about 10</p>
