 Monday 8 November 1915&nbsp; Air Temperature ideal this moment 7 a.m. Left hospital ward at 4 pm. Nearly O.K.&nbsp; At dinner Mess meeting after dinner&nbsp; Walk with Henry Dr after dinner. 
 Tuesday 9 November 1915&nbsp; As I look out of my window at the central dome of this&nbsp;great building &ndash; Palace Hotel &ndash; &amp; note its surroundings of architecture &amp; atmosphere, my mind thinks:- How much a good Architect can do to fit puny art into great Nature?&nbsp;&ndash; 
 Wednesday 10 November 1915&nbsp; Met [Lieut.]&nbsp;Col. Nicholson in Col. Newmarch&#39;s office this morning:&nbsp;He has just come from Sydney, in charge of troops.&nbsp; In Cairo for a little during the afternoon
