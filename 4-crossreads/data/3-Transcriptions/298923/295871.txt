<p>24 July 1918 Wednesday<br/><strike>La Troun.</strike>   Marched all night again to <strike>La Troun,</strike> bright moonlight &amp; we could hear the guns in the distance Johnnie has a big one now can throw a shell 12 miles-<br/>I had a letter from the Priests in Bethlehem saying "goodbye"   I was sorry not to see them.<br/>As you may be anxious Ill post these notes on arrival at Coast.<br/>Thank you for "Australasians" I get them if pink wrapper is taken off first.   Am posting an old Nominal Roll of B Squadron it may arrive &amp; will be glad [indecipherable] to read</p>
