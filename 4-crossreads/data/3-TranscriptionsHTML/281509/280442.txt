<p>1916 August 7<br/>Monday Bank Holiday</p>
<p>Resume duty at 8 a.m. Help carry patients from dressing room to ward all day.<br/>Lieut. Jacka thro' wounded. Another Lieut's steel helmet pierced by bullet. narrow shave. Stream of wounded continue all day. What a price we pay for a few yards of ground. The way to Berlin is literally paved with the bodies of our gallant dead. The horror &amp; awfulness of it all.<br/></p>
