Very dull weather lately. 26th July Very dull weather. The sun has scarcely showed himself since our arrival here Village of Pozieres has been captured by Australians on the Somme. also two guns. 27th July A committee formed of officers of this Bn has been appointed to enquire into the loss of signalling gear of this Bde. We had an enquiry over ours  Ben  today and I as senior soldier in the section and in the absence of responsible NCOs had to make a statement concerning our loss or destroyed sig gear.
