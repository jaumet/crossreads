<p>We remained in Huts till Sunday morning.  On Saturday night I had a voluntary service in the Ch. Army hut &amp; had a good time.</p>
<p>Sunday Apl. 28th<br/>Up at a quarter to five &amp; we got away about 7 oclock.  We marched to La Bizet through Armentiers &amp; the Batt. is billeted in old houses.  We are now in Belgium territory.  I got a shake down for the night at Pont Nieppe.</p>
<p>Monday<br/>Morning early there was a heavy bombardment on our left.  The 36 lost 5 men killed.  The weather has been fine &amp; warm today.  A Bosh plane was brought down here today.  I did not go far today as I wanted to rest.</p>
<p>Tuesday<br/>Weather fine &amp; clear.  Spring is upon us now &amp; all the trees are coming into leaf.  I went up to the subsidiary lines today, our men have strong points &amp; are billeted in old ruins.</p>
