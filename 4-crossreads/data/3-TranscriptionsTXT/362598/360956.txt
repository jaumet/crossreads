
Sunday 17 December 1916Ice.  No church.  I write P.C. Maud Catell.  P.C. to Lily & George, letter to Roy & George started.
Monday 18 December 1916Ice.  Recd. card from Home & Book from D.Js.  I go on with M's Photo album.  Make good progress.  Go to bed late.
Tuesday 19 December 1916Recd. letter & Photo from Marg. & letter from Marie.  I'm Canteen Corporal but write to M.R.L. & send cards to Gran, Auntie & Jennie.  Ellis goes out for a walk & has Photo taken.  Meet Bill Drummond.
Wednesday 20 December 1916Raining like the deuce.  Footy match off.  I go on with my Photo Book after Wellard has gone.  Haughton & Goode are discharged.
Thursday 21 December 1916I go to the Pictures on my own.  Dave arrives back after a Tour of the Southern Provinces.
Friday 22 December 1916I write to Jennie, George, Roy & M.R.L.  Dave comes over for a yarn.  Ellis goes in to the Theatre for operations.
Saturday 23 December 1916Vin comes to Theatre with Ellis & I.  I send Photos to M.R.L.  Sapho at Theatre no good.  Rotten show.  New Padre arrives.  I practice Hymns for Church.
