<p>At 9 PM we went to the firing line again Butcher &amp; self on observation post on extreme left in small sap. Might hot corner &amp; I had to occasionally pop my head up to see what was doing on our front.<br/>Hell of fire all night which petered out at dawn &amp; we were relieved at 10 pm Hand grenades given us with most amusing instructions at 12 m n.</p>
