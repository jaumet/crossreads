<p>Monday 13 [12] August 1917 [1918]<br/>My pts. I find are very fond of me (S. Walker's statement).  Well I love the dear boys myself &amp; if a word or smile can help them let us give it surely.  Brave Aust. boys.  One tells of a duel he had with a German officer, both met suddenly.</p>
