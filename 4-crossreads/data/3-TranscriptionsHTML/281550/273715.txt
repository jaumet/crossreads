<p>Commandant of a large Prison Lager.</p>
<p>The Government are going to issue it in circular form to every English and American Prisoner-of-War leaving the country.</p>
<p>Gentlemen<br/>The war ended for you as soon as you became Prisoner-of War. But now it has ended for the whole world. A little longer and you will see your native land again, Your homes, your loved ones, your friends. You will once more take up your accustomed work.</p>
<p>The fortunes of war brought you as prisoners into our hands. You were freed even against your will from fighting, from danger, from death.</p>
