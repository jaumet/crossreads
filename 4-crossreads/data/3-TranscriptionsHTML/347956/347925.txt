<p>16.3.15</p>
<p>Tue 16th<br/>Was showery when we awoke this morning, we had porridge for breakfast this morning, for the first time since leaving Sydney &amp; it was very good;<br/>at 9 oclock we out on mounted parade, but as soon as we were mounted, it commenced to rain &amp; we were sent back;  The morning was rather cold &amp; the horses frisky, we were not on them for long, but long enough to see one or two of the boys take somersaults off their horses;  We were no sooner on the lines than it poured down &amp; we made a rush for the tent;  after dinner we had helio practice</p>
