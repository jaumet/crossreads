  
 of Saxon interest. 19th.  Motor to Salisbury this afternoon & look over the town Cathedral & along the Avon.  Walk Home via Wilton, where we go  th  to-morrow to see the Earl of Pembroke's house & Garden. 24th.  I have now been moved from Hurdcott to Fovant and have immediately been put on a boat roll, "China", to leave Devonport on 1st prox.  We will go to join her at London on 28th.
