<p>It is the 1st Div Reinforcement Camp. A very pretty place at the back of a Chateau About 3 or 4 Ac with very fine trees &amp; a hedge around it. The tents are pitched between the trees &amp; there is nice short grass in the centre Have passed through a lot of cultivated <strike>grass</strike> land to day very good crops. Can hear the guns in the line tonight<br/>A great number of our planes keep going up &amp; down to the line. Had a blanket issued this evening</p>
<p>Tuesday 30th</p>
