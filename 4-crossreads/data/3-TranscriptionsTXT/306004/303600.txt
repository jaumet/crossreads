
17.11.16Fri 17thHad to bustle around today to get the lines cleaned up & ready to move off at 9 oclock arrived at Willegha at 9.30 & found A squadron has a very comfortable Camp; the Hod is only big enough to hold one Sqdn & C Sqdn struck Camp to the East of the Hod & B to the West, The only drawback is, we have to go about half a mile to water our horses; Put this afternoon in erecting shelters & making ourselves comfortable.  A Troop of Anzac Mounted Police came along this evening for experience in patrol & Outpost work & they are likely to get it; Harold Leo Peake is with them, & had a long yarn with him tonight.
