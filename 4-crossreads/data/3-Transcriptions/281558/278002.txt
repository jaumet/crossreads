<p>Loupart wood in afternoon :</p>
<p>Wednesday June 20th</p>
<p>Bapaume : showers &amp; thunder : cleaned up &amp; roam around : "P" of Bieuvillers church &amp; yard : bath in afternoon &amp; disinfected our clothing : got a few letters also a parcel from JBJ (rolls)</p>
<p>Thursday June 21st</p>
<p>Bapaume : dull : reveille 4 am : cleaned up &amp; wrote during morn : Alf &amp; self roamed down to Le Sars &amp; had a look at the "Butte" &amp; malf trench : ride home in motor waggon :</p>
<p>Friday June 22nd</p>
<p>Bapaume : reveille 4 am : early parade 5 am : parade 8 am : sports in afternoon : I got 2nd in my heat of 100 yds but was "crossed" in the final &amp; cut off : ran 4th in the 200 yds : heavy bombardment after we went</p>
