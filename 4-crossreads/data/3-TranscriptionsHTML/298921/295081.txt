<p>April, 1915<br/>Sunday 18<br/>Attended Church Parade &amp; walked around Heliopolis a new suburb built by the Belgians to rival Monte Carlo &ndash; Casino Dancing etc. "Kamseen" or Sirocco [indecipherable] all day, sand inches deep in blankets &amp; in the Tucker, it is the desert sand that gives the men Pneumonia both men &amp; horses are getting sanded-up &amp; "fed-up" at wasting so many weeks in Egypt.<br/>Will look up Ralph Lorna's fianc&eacute;e but I do not know his name, also others who are coming &ndash; Sunday is like any week day. Casino etc all open</p>
