<p>Sunday 7 October 1917<br/>Scored some Arrowroot from some of the lads at Camp.  Wrote No. [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 8 and 7].</p>
<p>Monday 8 October 1917<br/>Changed guard, got a few letters, very little doing.</p>
<p>Tuesday 9 October 1917<br/>Quiet but dusty day, went over to Y.M.C.A. &amp; pinched some boxes.</p>
<p>Wednesday 10 October 1917<br/>Quiet day spent most of the afternoon preparing for a Big feed up with Sgts. of the 10th Irish Division real good night.</p>
<p>Thursday 11 October 1917<br/>Very dusty &amp; windy, got a couple of parcels.  B.W.I. band played at Y.M.C.A. quite a good turnout.</p>
<p>Friday 12 October 1917<br/>Nothing Doing.</p>
<p>Saturday 13 October 1917<br/>Nothing Doing, band played near our dump.</p>
