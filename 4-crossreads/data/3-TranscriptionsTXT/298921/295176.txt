 July, 1915 Saturday 24 Found Harry Phillips this morning he is camped about 1 mile from me is very fit, looks well &amp; happy &amp; has been made a Sergeant. I gave him a plan to find my Tent &amp; he's coming up this afternoon; If I can do anything for him I will of course do it &amp; have advised him to fill in a form for a Commission &amp; I will send it on &ndash; He has grown into a fine man &ndash; very like  Harry  Arthur Phillips, with blue eyes, &amp; all the characteristics of the Phillips. Tell  Marion  I will do all I can for him.
