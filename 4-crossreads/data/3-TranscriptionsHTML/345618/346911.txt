<p>Though fighting's not your special work,<br/>We know you're not the sort to shirk<br/>A rough &amp; tumble with the Turk.<br/>You will not spare 'em,<br/>And you will prove that you're as stirk<br/>Where there's a harem.</p>
<p>You'll find promotion rather slow<br/>Unless you blow up Jericho,<br/>Or do some deed of dering do<br/>And win the V.C.</p>
<p>If that should happen let us know &ndash;<br/>Drop us a p.c.<br/>At times you may be short of grub,<br/>For Palestine is mostly scrub.<br/>You'll think of us chaps at The Club,<br/>While you're away.<br/>Conder will dun you for your sub,<br/>We hope you'll pay!<br/>Now &ndash; whether you be pictures [indecipherable] &ndash;<br/>Or in the ice-cold ocean swimming &ndash;<br/>Or killing Turks or kissing women<br/>I know one who's a limb<br/>Of Satan &ndash; so &ndash; in bumper brimming<br/>Here's to Jerusalem!</p>
