<p>on either sides of me are lovely crops stretching right away to the horizion &amp; broken here &amp; there by little clumps of trees &amp; great long avenues showing where the roads are running &amp; above all the birds are singing for all they are worth, &amp; the sun shines brightly over all, as I write here I can see the French women &amp; girls at work in the fields some reaping, mowing, hoeing, some at work with the teams helping cart the hay &amp; clover in, others leading their cows home to be milked, its wonderful how quiet the stock are here, I suppose they have kept the same breed of cattle for so many years, that quietness is bred in them.
