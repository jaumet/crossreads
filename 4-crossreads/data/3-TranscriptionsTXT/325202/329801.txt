 We understood that we were to go a few miles back for our Divisional Rest but now no one of us knows where we are bound. Dame Rumour has it that we have a train journey in front of us; we have had an overhauling of our 'gas bags', field dressings etc so I think we are bound for the Somme where our present offensive is.
