
<p>SATURDAY, AUGUST 2, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine. Round the bottom end with</p>
<p>Alan in morning. We lifted the pipe</p>
<p>in Middle paddock bore with a jack.</p>
<p>Darby down about 125 ft in blue</p>
<p>rock.</p>
<p>Al &amp; I &amp; Pete went over to</p>
<p>Coomoo Coomoo in evening. The</p>
<p>employees gave Clyde a bit of</p>
<p>a Social evening there &amp; we had a</p>
<p>fine time.</p>
