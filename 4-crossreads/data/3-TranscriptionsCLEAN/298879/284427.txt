  
 August 1915 Thursday 26 Cool. A heavy bombard preceded on attack by our chaps on the left. Results of the attack have not come through officially. The capture of a couple of howitzers by our lads is reported. The enemy shelled our trenches considerably about 5 PM 
 Friday 27 Cool. Cloudy. A couple of Taube aeroplanes were seen above our trenches yesterday evening. They did not stay long though.
