<p>Mon Ap 22ND :-<br/>Relieved at 9.30pm by the 5th BN "Fritz" gave us a few salvos of "Whizz bangs" at parting. Marched out to Le Peupliel railway siding &amp; billeted in huts.</p>
<p>Tues Apr 23rd :-<br/>Everything quiet here though a few hostile aircraft came over on the lookout.</p>
<p>Wed Apr 24th :-<br/>Camp bombed by aeroplanes early this morning. Only one wounded .Made T/Cpl Vice Cpl J Honan wounded 17/4/18</p>
<p>Thurs Apr 25th:-</p>
<p>"Anzac Day" spent on rest. 25th RFTS joined us up. Two new sigs:- Rose &amp; Towner.</p>
<p>Fri Apr 26th :-<br/>All the Bn on fatigue tonight digging trenches on left of  Meteren. Worked short time as "Jerry" set alight a farmhouse behind us with a shell &amp; gave us unwanted illumination for the job.</p>
<p>Sat Apr 27th :-<br/>Still at camp at Le Peupliel: near the Borre North Railway</p>
