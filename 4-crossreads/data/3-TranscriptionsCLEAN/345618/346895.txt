  
 A.  Personal Account - G.W.L. 
 Waterloo Station Christmas morn cold & damp.  St  Officers train about to leave for Southampton.  Pathetic & very disturbing farewells exchanged between  men wom  men women boys girls.  Why do they let their women folk see them off at the Station [indecipherable]  I having got over  it my parting with my little lot two hours previously without unnecessary fuss.  Then she starts & from that moment England seems left behind & we are in the battle zone.  Our compartment contains officers, B.
