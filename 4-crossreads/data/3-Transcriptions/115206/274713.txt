<p>May 1918<br/>Thursday 23</p>
<p>Battery W.L.<br/>Weather dull and windy. Wind from N .W. with plenty of bite in it.<br/>Fritz put a few into Querrie, Received a letter from Aunt Ellie, an Aunt I did not know I possessed.<br/>Gunn down for a run. Knipe still indisposed think he's swinging the lad.<br/>Had a run across to Bussy, and interviewed Simonde and Solange.<br/>More bombs tonight.</p>
