Strayable the same day & got a good billet in a farm. We had great feeds of Blackberries which were very plentiful, everyday found Scotty & I having our usual tuck of them we even made puddings we also found out where spuds were plentiful the result was that we had potatoes for breakfast and dinner we did not stay here long & we left Strayable for Bailleul Oct 17th. Where we entrained for the Somme. it was raining hard all the time & we got wet through [?] about we travelled in the usual cattle trucks & I slept well in spite of the crowded way we were packed it rained all night next day found us at long[?] the rain had stopped we got tea at the Y.M.C.A. then
