<p><u>Nov 12th</u>:- Went into action with Scottish Infantry, attacked an entrenched ridge with redoubts. The action was fairly hot, we having to advance over a thousand yards across flat country absolutely without cover. Our Regt. withdrew after dark leaving the Infantry dug in their positions.</p>
