<p>Sept 7th  Warned again, but the blessed rain came down <strike>which</strike> and welcomed.</p>
<p>The English papers today are full of air raid.  The following is a copy of what Beach Thomas writes.  The enemy for example attacked our Hospitals with bombs holding more than 250lbs of explosive apiece and a few were heavier still.  The enemy is the apostle of night bombing, which is semi-blind even during the harvest moon now gibbons &ndash; but the general activity has increased and he attempts to secure observation of gun fire by Sending out a little fleet of machines to report for a Single Gun.  I don't know what to think of the Belgiums if they are with us, or them.  When we first came to England I was surprised that they were not enthused over by the E people.
