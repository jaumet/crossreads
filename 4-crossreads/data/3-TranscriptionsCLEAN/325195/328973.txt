
15.10.15Fri 15thReceived the canteen stores today & we got 2 tins Tomatoe, 3 bottles Sauce 2 large tins of Currie 1 Pkt Herbs 1 tin margarine; Nelson came back from Hospital yesterday & took charge again; We have a dirigible airship that appears to do a good deal of work about Imbros apparently trying to locate submarines, she is very seldom over this way & I have only seen her flying at a distance aeroplanes are always flying over us here, but they are taken no notice of now except when they drop an egg or two; It is rumoured that we are to attack the Turks tomorrow around at Lone pine & there is to be a 'Stand to Arms' at 3.45 tomorrow morning to make a demonstration.
