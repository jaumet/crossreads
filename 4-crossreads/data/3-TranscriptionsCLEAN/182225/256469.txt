 DEC.   SATURDAY 16   1916 
 Fine & frosty. Cleaning harness all the morning & we had the usual inspection. Saw Hughie McMaster, Reg Watt & Ophir Taylor in the afternoon. Ophir is in the officers school at Larkhill & doing well. Frank Payne from Wambramurra came over in afternoon & he looks very well. Charlie Scott & self went & had tea with Major Lloyd & his wife - Carline Rodd that was from Parramatta at Amesbury. We had a real good little time. I saw mother at the Abbey also.
