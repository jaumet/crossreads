<p>1917 October</p>
<p>14 Sunday</p>
<p>Fine day after rain.  Sewed until dinner.  Wrote a little.  Had a lesson with Milesov.  Then Tubb &amp; I went for quite a decent walk towards Kajmackalon.  Guys called after dinner.  Matron in bed all day.  Silly guns thudding all day.</p>
