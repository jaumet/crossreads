<p>28/10/17 Esani<br/>1st LH Bde ascertain if enemy still holding posts E of Abu Shar and NE of 960. 2 &frac12; miles east of 840.<br/>1212 1st LH Bde outpost reports enemy movement at 1170 N of 2nd A in G El Naam and enemy cavalry about Beersheba.<br/>1315 Water situation at Asluj now as follows &ndash; In reserve 13000 gallons. Developed wells make 2000 gallons an hour. Two wells still being developed. Supply will give by tomorrow morning one drink per day per horse for the whole division.<br/>1415 orders received from Des Corps for Divn. less two Bdes to move to Asluj tonight. 1st and NZ Bdes to move to Khalasa. Orders in accordance with above were issued.<br/>1445 Divisional Commander left for Asluj<br/>1510 Hostile aeroplane reconnoitred Esani and flew off NE<br/>[Transcriber's note: shows arrows to insert entries in correct time order]<br/>1430 Report from RFC &ndash; No movement or concentration<br/>1531 5th Mtd Bde reports from Ibn SAIID states redoubts E of Abu SHAR occupied. Odd Turks on trench line about 1070 and squadron moving N near there. Tents seen at Ras Hablein<br/>Asluj<br/>1705 Div. Commander arrived and DHQ opened &frac34; mile W of Asluj mosque.<br/>1825 Report recd from 2nd Bde that reconnaissance of W.
