 Relations with Cpl Merrilees &amp; Major Strachan have a little improved lately. Fancy latter is inclined to have favourites &amp; does not care a rap about the rest as individuals.  If he gets a dislike for a chap he works off his feelings by mean actions. Gertie has developed his eccentric mannerisms &amp; is a little less popular than before  In some ways has improved &amp; in others not Generally less noisy. Means well but fails lamentably.
