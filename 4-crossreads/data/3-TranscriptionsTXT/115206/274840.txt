
September 1918.Friday 27
Tincourt and we are still here.Weather wet during the morning, having a farily busy time with wire and etc.spent a fair amount of time on different work.Georgie Baker returned from hospitalAm inclined to believe that the big stunt comes off on Sunday morning, but could not say with any reliabilityMet Les Fuller, and one or two other boys.Spent a good night under the new blankets and dugouts.
