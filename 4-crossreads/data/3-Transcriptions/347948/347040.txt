<p>from after middle deck  Sighted land at 11 o'clock and travelling along coast.  Don't know where it is.  Passed S.S."Moira"</p>
<p>22nd  Passed Fremantle this morning.  Practice falling in to alarm posts.</p>
<p>23rd  Practice falling into alarm posts.  Practice in the morning, concert on deck at night.  Sang all the songs that have been murdered years ago.  Sgt Bignall and Powter played a cornet duet.  Duel would have described it better.  Tolerated it for about  &frac12;  an hour and then went and buried my head under the blanket-</p>
