
August,1918Monday 26
Hamel to Bty Pos. at 2 kilos ahead of Poyart.Weather still very showery. Left our spelling possie and journeyed via Cevisy and Morcourt and Proyart to a Bty Position selected by the skipper this morning about two kilos ahead of the latter village.Arrived Bty possie at 9 pm messed about by Makinson who would not allow us to have our pit where we wanted to have it. Communication established with Bdge at 10.30 pm. Laid our lines.No news of Ken Gunn at all. Mac, Walker, Scottie and myself up this time. Tom having a spell.
