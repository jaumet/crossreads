<p>Charles Hargreaves was a Printer hence his interest in keeping these papers.</p>
<p>Jan 2nd 1921 Blake &amp; Hargreaves Pty Ltd 47 Geo St Nth. began in business, later to 53/5 Geo. St. Nth. &amp; 1964 to own premises 72/8 Addison Rd. Marrickville.</p>
<p>47 Geo. St. Nth. is in The Rocks Area &amp; is one of the earliest Bond Stores, now restored as historical.</p>
<p>Charles Hargreaves died Aug. 25th 1953.</p>
