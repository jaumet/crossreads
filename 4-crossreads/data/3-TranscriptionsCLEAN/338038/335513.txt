  
 [Note added later] From Lieut Col Williams C.O. 10th Brigade AFA - AIF 4th  Division 
 France Aug 30/18 
 Dear Mr Garling Re your P.S. As you say your son undoubtedly earned decorations (according to what people have told you) more than once, and I can give you my word that he really earnt them.
