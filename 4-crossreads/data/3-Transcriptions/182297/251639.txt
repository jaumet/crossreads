<p>[Rear of postcard]</p>
<p>&quot;Kinnoul&quot;</p>
<p>Belmore</p>
<p>1.2.1915</p>
<p>Every thing getting on smothly in the Area been a felp. Changes in regards to Officers &amp; Staff. Olds gone over to Milita,&nbsp;I hope to be their shortly. Draper is going. And a S.S.M. Coleman been attached not the first one we had.</p>
<p>Every body O.k. here at home, hope when you write again that you are &quot;At Em!</p>
<p>&quot;Seasons Greetings to you all</p>
<p>L.E.Y.</p>
<p>Major J.G. Tedder</p>
<p>Transport Division</p>
<p>Australian Expeditionary Force</p>
<p>Cairo</p>
<p>Egypt.</p>
