<p>Left London on April 30 at 10.30 a.m. from Euston for Liverpool &amp; Birkenhead arriving 3 p.m.  Left at 9 p.m. in dense fog &amp; had horn going for 2 nights &amp; a day every 3 minutes.<br/>Boats slung out &amp; provisioned <strike>15 passengers</strike> 14 adult passengers, my 3 children &amp; a little boy.<br/>Held up at Marseilles for 5 days.  Buying of a mans shirt (chemise).<br/>Steamers 2 &amp; 3 deep at Quay.<br/>Got passports with photos.<br/>German prisoners at work 150 or more.<br/>3 transports off to the Dardanelles with Fr. troops</p>
