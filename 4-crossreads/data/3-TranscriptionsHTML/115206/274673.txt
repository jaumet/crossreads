<p>April 1918<br/>Saturday 13</p>
<p>Bussy &ndash; la &ndash; Dours.  D.R.<br/>Spent a farily quiet day.  Heard the bombardment for a Fritz hop over, and witnessed the usual fireworks.<br/>Weather wet and miserable.<br/>Fritz did a fair amount of bombing on Daours.<br/>He also shelled that region fairly heavily.<br/>Inspection and instruction on map reading.<br/>Reconnoitred bridges during afternoon and instructed the Bombadier in his alternative routes for necessitity</p>
