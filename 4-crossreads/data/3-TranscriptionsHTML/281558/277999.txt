<p>got to Rubempre about 7.30 pm &amp; was pretty tired : met all the old boys that are left so was O.K. :</p>
<p>Monday June 11th</p>
<p>Rubempre : hot day : handed in my equipment &amp; rifle so am S.B. [Stretcher Bearer] again : cleaned up round the billets : looked J Marsland up :</p>
<p>Tuesday June 12th</p>
<p>Rubempre : hot day : reveille 6 am : walked into Amiens to get my films : got a lift out again : boxing at night :</p>
<p>Wednesday June 13th</p>
<p>Rubempre : reveille 6 am : early parade 6.30 am : wrote in afternoon :</p>
<p>Thursday June 14th</p>
<p>Rubempre : reveille 6 am : moved off after dinner to Puighvillers &amp; entrained : passing through the "Somme" battlefield was some sight : got out at Bapaume :</p>
