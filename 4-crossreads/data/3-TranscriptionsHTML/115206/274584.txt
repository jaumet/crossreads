<p>January 1918<br/>Monday 14</p>
<p>At Beutin<br/>Paid forty francs today by Redmond, in the Local Estaminet Du Gare, the old Madame [indecipherable] is rubbing her hands and waiting till six pm when she will gather numerous francs in return for beer and etc.<br/>James was just a little "oiled" not too bad. <br/>Weather cold but fine. Went to rendezvous and played cards in the evening.</p>
