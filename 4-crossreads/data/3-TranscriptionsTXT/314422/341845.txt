Sunday Jan. 20 1918.1266 day of the war and the 8 day of my leave. The snow is all gone this morning and I filled in the time this morning writing letters[Shorthand symbols] 108.As it was showery I stayed in the house all day. The Gibsons came for dinner & spent the afternoon.
