
Wednesday 19thDid 15 minutes PT then it rained & kept on most of the day Concert in YM Quite good Payed 10/-
Thursday 20As usual. Never knew a Hut to be so quiet on a pay night must be saveing up for leave
Friday 21As usual Good Concert & Aussie letters
Saturday 22Rought March. Started off over the Hill opposite the camp quite a stiff climb about 10 miles along open roads. Did not feel it at all today Concert in YM much higher class than any I have been to before
Sunday 23rdOpen Air Service today. Went up hill opposite camp & examined Rising Sun & other Badges. They are made by cutting the turf off & filling in the trench thus
