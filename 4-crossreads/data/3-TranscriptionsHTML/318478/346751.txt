<p>26/7/19</p>
<p>To my Wife</p>
<p>My pocket book, left hand breast pocket of my tunic contains &pound;50 English (Bradburys) also please find Draft &amp;pound:50 Anglo Egyptian Bank.  This latter being deposit on commission Field Ambulance picture to be painted after Govt. contract leaves me free.</p>
<p>In the event of my not being able to complete this picture the &pound;50 (fifty pounds) must be returned to the Field Ambulance Office, Ref. Col. Croal [Croll], Romani Sand Cast Incident.</p>
<p>G.W. Lambert</p>
