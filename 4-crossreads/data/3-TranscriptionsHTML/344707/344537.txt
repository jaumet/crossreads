<p>Year<br/>Laura writes all's well with Auntie. I hope you are all the same at home<br/>Young Allanson of the Post Office spoke to me a few days ere we left Gallipoli<br/>Love to All<br/>Your fond son<br/>Frank<br/>26/12/15</p>
<p>Well Mother back again<br/>Here  we take our Horses &amp; leave in a week after the revolting Arabs Tripoli way "Senousis" or something like that they call them &ndash; Some of our men have gone already &ndash; Love Frank<br/>only Lieut in Camp</p>
