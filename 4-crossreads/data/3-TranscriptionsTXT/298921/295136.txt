 June, 1915 Monday 14 Sand lice are now plentiful on the Desert &amp; the Tents are in want of Keatings Powder. Sent 100 Pipes to the Reg at Dardanelles &ndash; Posted Diary yesterday Not much news these days only Sand &amp; Heat. But it agrees with me! &amp; I am very fit &ndash; no colds which since Africa have always been my trouble! 
 I send 2 Silver Piastres other  nickel  coins of same value make the coinage hard to follow, hope the Censor does not [indecipherable] to them
