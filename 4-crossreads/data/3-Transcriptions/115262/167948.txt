<p>Saturday 30&nbsp;&nbsp;June 1917.</p>
<p>Parades during morning and none in afternoon. Did some washing after 4 p.m. 1<sup>st</sup>&nbsp;since leaving boat. An easy day in this respect.</p>
<p>At 10 pm.[a.m.?] detailed with 5 others for a stretcher squad to hospital depot&nbsp;&frac12; way to meet Amb. from Ismailia, with 5 patients. Amb. late - waited 3 hrs in sun with patients and got back to camp at 1.30 pm. &amp; missed dinner. Another stretcher party at 7 p.m. to part of the isolation area.&nbsp;</p>
