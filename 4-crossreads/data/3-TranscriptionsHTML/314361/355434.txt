<p>Monday 25/6/17.<br/>Left Bradford 2.30 went by car to Salsaire &amp; walked over Shipley Glen, turned up over the Moors, &amp; whilst walking over there heard the Cuckoo in the woods, we walked over the Golf links to a little farm house &amp; had tea in the front garden, after tea we went out across the fields to Houndsworth &amp; went through the village on to Grimsby, from Grimsby we walked to Esholt on to Rawdon &amp; through Rawdon Woods &amp; along the river side &amp; up to Greengates &amp; took the car to Bradford arriving home about 10.30.<br/>Tuesday 26/6/17<br/>Went to Aunties to dinner &amp; after dinner went to Bolling Hall, it is a most interesting place, there are pictures of Old Bradford.<br/>The Hall itself is a very old building dating back to 1165, we had a delightful time, roaming about the rooms which are full of fine specimens of antique furniture. Coming home we called into Woodroyd Mills to see Florrie &amp; the manager of the Weaving Department showed us around, then we went &amp; had tea at Aunties.<br/>Thursday 28/6/17<br/>We went into town &amp; had a look at the shops, &amp; did a little shopping, then we call at home &amp; afterwards went across to Aunties for dinner, after dinner we went to Madge's house at Wibsey, had a look round &amp; then went</p>
