<p>3 March 1916 Fri<br/>Murphy at last owns up that that he is full of the whole affair reciting pieces from [indecipherable].</p>
<p>A very funny think happened this afternoon. A squatter Lance Corporal wandered into our camp with a throat as dry as a camel &amp; asked for a Stimulant of some sort. The Q.M. sent him to me who came &amp; saluted me thinking me an officer &amp; didn't he put the kid in for a whiskey [text continues at top of page]</p>
<p>4 March 1916 Sat<br/>Kit &ndash; inspection a man standing at the head of all his clothes etc laying out on the ground resembles a licensed dealer in old wares especially after having twelve months wear.</p>
<p>Took the mules &amp; the sand cart out to Lithgoro Post for malaria case. I was only thinking trudging through the sand nearly blinded with sand &amp; thirsty trying to realize it was a Saturday afternoon as compared with those of old</p>
