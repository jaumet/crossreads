24th. Sunday today we held a Brigade Church parade, we had to march about a mile along a very pretty little road, lined on either side by green hop-fields young growing crops, we formed up in a square in a nice little grass paddock where we held our sports the other day, the 3rd Bn. band was there played for the service which was conducted by old Mac, we had quite an important morning, for General Birdwood Walker were there also Andy Fisher someone else in civies
