<p>Wednesday<br/>23/2/16</p>
<p>A fine day &amp; sea calm.</p>
<p>A Cruiser appeared on the horizon at daylight &amp; was conversing by morse code &ndash; that is flash lights &ndash; some of the Boys deciphered it as &ndash; give us particulars &ndash; your name &amp; business etc. Another Cruiser also appeared later, so have no doubt they are keeping in touch with us. Passed a lot of sea snakes. Big sick parades after effects of Colombo.<br/>Deserters fined on an average 2 days pay &amp; 5 days on Fatigue. C.B. (Confined to Boat).</p>
