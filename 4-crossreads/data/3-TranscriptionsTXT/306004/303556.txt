 7.10.16 Restaurant.  arranged for a car to meet us at the base at 10.30, then went to the Kursaal but found it was about to come out, then went to the Cinema &amp; it finished a few minutes after we arrived, then went into "The Pops" till 10.30 it was 10.40 when we got to the base &amp; found Wright &amp; Todhunter had gone out in the car so we had to hire another &amp; got into Camp at 11.30 
 8.10.16 Sun 8th The black flag was flying again this morning so we did not go down for a dip; Our car  met us at 1 oclock this afternoon &amp; we went to Montasa
