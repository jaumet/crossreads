<p>Naval Signal</p>
<p>From - Encounter</p>
<p>To - Berrima</p>
<p>Date - 15 9 4</p>
<p>Time -&nbsp;</p>
<p>The following from Colonel Watson to Brigadier was made by WT. but is now repeated to ensure accuracy Colonel Watson to Brigadier Berrima. acting under your instructions from Berrima at 1 34 this morning I occupied Toma today at 3 PM.
