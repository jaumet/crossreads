  Feb. 10th  
 Miss Doris Scales (Niece of Mr &amp; Mrs) arrives from Cambridge to spend week at &quot;Elm Bank&quot;. 
 Morning and afternoon in London.&nbsp; Booking for Theatres etc. 
 Visi residence of Miss Fitzsimmons but have no luck as she is not at home. 
 Reading &amp; writing. 
  Feb. 11th  
 Another walk to &quot;Parliament in the Fields&quot; which was the original meeting place of the Wilena(?).
