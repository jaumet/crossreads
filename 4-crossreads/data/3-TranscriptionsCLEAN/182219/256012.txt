31st Dec
An so on the last day of the year I find myself way out on the hills of Judea taking part in the campaign against the turks at K - I - H. A is a time when all past memories crowd on me, thoughts of home & dear ones & of when I shall see them again. Have been out most of the day & as I am dead tired am now going to turn in for the night.
