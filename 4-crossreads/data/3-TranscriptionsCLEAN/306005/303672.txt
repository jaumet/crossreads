
26-12-16fall & we got the order to double & the strongest runner got home first & there was no dismiss; it was a rough ending to a rough parade: Found out this morning that C Sqdn Sigs brought back a Turkish Field 'Phone & 5 reels of light wire with them on the pack horse. Made some improvements on our "Bivy" this afternoon as it has been showery & looks like heavy rain tonight.
27-12-16Wed 27thHad some heavy rain last night & early this morning but managed to keep fairly dry. Went for a ride down to the pier this afternoon with Lindsell to give our horses some exercise
