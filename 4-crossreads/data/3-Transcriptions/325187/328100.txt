<p>[Sunday 24 December 1916]<br/>Magdhaba<br/>Xmas eve &amp; our job is to help clean up the Battlefield collect rifles &amp; ammunition Bury the dead, succour the wounded &amp; burn what we cannot take away- Any amount of loot- Oil engine pumps ordnance.<br/>Two Armenian Doctors help our own-<br/>We go back to El Arish today-<br/>This fight about finishes the Turks in SINIA for some time anyway-<br/>I just got back in time to help capture the guns that wounded our men in August.<br/>3rd night without a Blanket freezing cold but all hands cheerful at result of Battle.</p>
