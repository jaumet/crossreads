There are two New Zealand Hospitals in the town & I believe six of the fellows are married to French women.  At the sports we scored a win & one second.Saturday 5th May   Word through that about 16 of our bearers have been knocked, two being killed.  Came as a bit of a shock as they only went up to help the stunt being a second divisional affair- although our div. had to go up as supports.  From all accounts the ambulances suffered rather severely.  Morris will be well in it altho' I have an idea he is O.K.  Horse show today our Amb. wagon coming second.
