<p>21 April 1918 Sunday<br/>Australasians &amp; Parcels from Mutual Store Feby 5th from Mother arrived safely thank you so much; also Ligmolene from Joe &amp; Mother 2 pots, so am set up now.<br/>Some of our Officers are just back from Australia, they say they were often insulted by loafers, one had a fight on G.P.O. steps &amp; was taken to Military Barracks Paddington, the other a Colonel had a fight in the bar of the "Australia" Hotel Sydney evidently Triumphal Arches are things of the past as I foresaw</p>
