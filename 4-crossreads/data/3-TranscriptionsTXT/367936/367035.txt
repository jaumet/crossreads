 Tuesday 5 September 1916 All hands down swimming bar Bill West, he on day stableman. 
 Wednesday 5 September 1916 Wrote to Mother (15). Got a parcel from Telarah.  Back scratcher in it caused great excitement. Went over to 6th in evening to see Colin but he was at Cantarah;  heard that Leycester's wound was alright.
