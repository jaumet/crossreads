  
 Anti-conscriptionists are trying to persuade the farmers that if they vote Yes it will be impossible for any farming operations to continue.  The Prime Minister distinctly states that no necessary labour will be withdrawn till the crops are harvested & the wool clip fully taken off.  Before next harvest steps will be taken as in Great Britain & France to facilitate the proper distribution of labour among all essential industries including that of farming.  Moreover the production of food presents the best possible case for exemption & the Prime Minister has given his assurance of preferential treatment of farmers.
