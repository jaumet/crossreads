20 1917My Dear Sister,At last I have heard the good news. Laurel told me all about it. I hope you have completely recovered that you baby will be in the best of health when you receive this letter. I thought I would never hear how you had been faring, that is the main reason why I have not been writing you letters. I thought perhaps that things might not be going too well with you. Say, where did you get the name from; I don't ever remember hearing it before. Tell Vol I congratulate him for getting his new job, and hope he goes ahead. Well Sis I cannot send you any more news as this letter has to be posted within ten minutes as we are moving tomorrow the
