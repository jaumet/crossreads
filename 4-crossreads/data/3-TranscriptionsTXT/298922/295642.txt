 Sunday 9 December 1917 
 [The following paragraph crossed through.] Malta Posted notes yesterday at Malta &amp; cabled Laura we had got this far safely &ndash; still at Malta. Ask JOE to post me a small tin of LIGMOline, I still  have the rash &ndash; at intervals. 
 We are waiting for a convoy to escort us as a large boat was struck this morning  from British W. India. Wife &amp; I  We went for a long tram ride to see an old submarine on the shore near here. A 3 Card man tried to get me to play on the top of the tram, but I am not a tenderfoot.
