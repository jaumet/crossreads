<p>Arriving at our destination at 10.40 AM. The villiage of Vieulaine. A one horse villiage consisting 1 house (our billit) a church, 2 haystacks, and a dog.</p>
<p>It is a terrible slow place and quiet. Our liveliest and only occupation is to go down and watch the goods train buzz through.</p>
<p>But we have passes issued now and again for leave to Abbeville and Amiens where we can spend a day when desired</p>
