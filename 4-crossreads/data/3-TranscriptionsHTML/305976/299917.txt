<p>Tuesday Sept 18th<br/>Rained a little this morning First rain I have seen since landing.<br/>Heard Anzac Div band this afternoon. Nothing much doing while in this camp so Tom &amp; I having a good time..<br/>Wednesday Sept 19<br/>Moving tomorrow to Abbasia. Australian Division taking over place here for a spell. Allotted Horse. Seems a decent old Neddy. Jet black Star on forehead. Wrote mail.<br/>Thursday Sept 20<br/>Moved to day. Very windy day so had a most unpleasant trip across the sand. Arrived at new Possy about 4 pm. Tom gone to Rafa on duty.</p>
