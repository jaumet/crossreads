<p>August Friday 31 1917</p>
<p>MacB &amp; I into B ---- take my tunic in to get fitted. Refreshments at C. W. L.  Back to hosp for tea. Jock &amp; self down to W to meet ---- come a big "gutser" no appearance of the fair ones. To Y M after consoling ourselves with Dux Porto.</p>
