6 Missed Church parade, had face dressed, & helped fix up tent.Rain all gone. Lovely day. Ginza finger just healedBelieve some silly coots (newcomers) shot 3 bullocks from the troop train.D-m them, they only give us a bad name. Blisters all over dial. Went Pictures. Not bad programme. Good Comic[in margin] Sunday, AD, HG
7 Off parade today. Cleaned up tent etc. Feeling dead Crook. Most of boys gone to pictures. Mess Orderly
