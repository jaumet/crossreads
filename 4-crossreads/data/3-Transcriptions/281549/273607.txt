<p>was inspected by the Officer Commanding.</p>
<p>On Sunday 21st Jan I went to church and afterwards went for a walk to some of the surrounding villages.</p>
<p>Monday I was drilling all day long. At night I wrote letters to various people.</p>
<p>I received a registered letter from Alma containing a <strike>Regist</strike> Allotment Papers. I made a new allotment payable to Alma, Captain Henry forwarded the Papers to Headquarters for me.</p>
<p>On the 26th &amp; 27th I had practice at throwing Hand Grenades &amp; also sent all valuables that I prized to Uncle to take care of for me.</p>
<p>On Monday 29th I Contracted Influenza, I stopped in bed &amp; sent for the Doctor to come down to see me, He gave me some medicine &amp; told me to stay in bed. The following day he came to see me again &amp; the day after he allowed me to get up. At night I went to a lecture on India at the Y.M.C.A. Afterwards I wrote a few letters &amp; then turned in.</p>
