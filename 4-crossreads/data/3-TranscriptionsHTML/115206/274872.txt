<p>October 1918.<br/>Tuesday 29</p>
<p>Hosp. Birmingham<br/>War news pretty good today.<br/>Heavy rain all day.<br/>Have come to the conclusion that of all the day sisters and nurses there is no one to touch Sister Barron for a thorough sport. Petite.<br/>Bought a bon little watch from the yank.</p>
