<p>Transcribers note: Typed on Letterhead of Australian imperial Force, Base Records Office Victoria Barracks</p>
<p>Dear Madam,</p>
<p>Forwarded herewith is one of the late No. 3770 Private T. Bell, 20th Battn. received  ex "Barambah"<br/>I shall be much delighted if you will kindly let me know whether same (Enclosure.) comes safely to hand, by signing and returning the enclosed printed slip.</p>
