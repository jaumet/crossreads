Sunday 24Afternoon at Kingston on Thames.
Monday 25I have come to the conclusion that this alleged hospital is at the lowest ebb as regards the conditions of food &amp; general affairs. The district is filthy &amp; I will be pleased to get out of it.
Wednesday 27I have asked to be discharged on Friday.
Friday 29Discharged from Hospital &amp; receive pass for 14 days furlough."Maid of the Mountains" at Dalys. Weather wet &amp; generally miserable but things are OK. It is good to have meals at your own time etc.
Saturday 30Richmond this afternoon after going to the Bank this morning
