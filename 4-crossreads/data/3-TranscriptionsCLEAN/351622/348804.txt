 31 March 1916 Fri Every second house in this village is an inn & they are open selling beer & wine only from 11 till 1 pm. & from 6 till 8 p.m. They are well patronised & are always full. We have two tommies attached to us who are in charge of the motor. Fine coves & we get on well together. Very sick to-night [text continued at top of page] 
 1 April 1916 Sat There is a beautiful church here & very old, but inside the work of art is magnificent. The cave with Christ's body after being crucified. The big guns are booming some to-day. 39 motor ambulance in one line passed through this morning. Everyone is doing his best to pick up French & the expressions at times are very queer. Toby, Arthur & Norm come to see us & stay for tea in the barn 
 [Note in margin] Nearly every in black
