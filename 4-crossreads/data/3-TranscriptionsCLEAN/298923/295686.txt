  
 20 January 1918 Sunday second after Epiphany  (X to X crossed out) Went to Church Parade Sermon Text  "I AM THE DOOR"  Here the sheep are put in a corral, but no  door  is used, a Bedouin sleeps on guard, hence simile-  A Taube flew over during service- This afternoon rode into Richon-Le-Zion & had a  hot  bath at a Jews house for  10  P.T.  5  Majors  Ryrie  &  Irwin  came with me. X More letters Dec 2nd & 13th & cutting Dairy from Mother also 3 Australasians 1 snapshot of Jack Mathews playing polo- & my usual letter from the little wife, alls well.X
