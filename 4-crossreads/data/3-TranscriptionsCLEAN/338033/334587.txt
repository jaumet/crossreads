We went along the Ludgershall and Tidworth railway, arriving at the latter place just after nine at night. To our amazement the sun was still high in the sky and everyone asked when lights - out would be blown and if so, who was to shade the sun that we might sleep in darkness. Tidworth is a barrack town, and is surprisingly well built and clean. We did not quarter here, but marched a short distance to Perham Down camp.
