  
 8 February 1916 Tuesday 
 After breakfast Bob & self drive down to Appletree.  Find all O.K. stay to dinner. Back home at  4 pm call en route at Alec's, Violet Bank & Orange Grove. Mary very sick with bad knee.  Mary Jane coming out from Nowra to look after them. We go to Robson's after tea & tea & talk fill in the evening.
