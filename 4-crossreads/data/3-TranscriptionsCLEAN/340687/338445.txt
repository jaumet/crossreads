  
 May, 1917. Monday 21 Day cloudy & very sultry. Reveille at 5 & breakfast at 5.30. Fell in at 6 and after cleaning billets moved out at 7.15. Arrived at Ovillers at 12.45. Very tired but visited Becourt saw-mills and got my pack and to chateau for canteen stores. Had a good bath in evening & wrote this up & read papers until dark. Moving Henincourt in morning.
