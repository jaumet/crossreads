July 31st Saturday
Barracks at 7 o'clock. Reach, Liverpool at 3 o'clock. Housed with rest of R3 Coy on Villa No. 21. Issued with four new blankets, great coat, waterproof tick. Supper 5.30 ? Bread marmalade. Concert in Methodist Hall during evening.
A week's training drill we succeeded in getting a transfer into the 8th Rein. of the 2nd Batt. After some trouble received uniform rifle at 5.45 Monday we entrained for Sydney by 9 o'clock were embarked on HMAS Runic. Sailed at 3 oclock. Seas very calm until Wednesday morning when rounding Wilson's Promontory we struck a heavy head wind. 5.30 arrived off Melbourne
