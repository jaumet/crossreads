  
 Saturday 2/2/18. Went for Pattie took her to lunch & then to Queen's Hall Concert Dance at A/asian Club Piccadilly at night. 
 Sunday 3/2/18. Went down to Bamfords for a couple of days. 
 Monday 4/2/18. Received cable from Doff [sent?] on 12/1/18. Got a new pr of Alpine boots. Wrote Doff 
 Tuesday 5/2/18  Still at Bamfords  Went to a picture Show "The Greatest Wish in the World".
