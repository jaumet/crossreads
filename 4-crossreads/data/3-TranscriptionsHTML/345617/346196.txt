<p>Octobre 1<br/>Coy are resting today. Reported that Germans have made heavy attacks on English troops who relieved us. Supposed to have taken a small section of trenches from Tommies on the S.E. corner of Polygon Wood.<br/>Artillery still very heavy on both sides.</p>
<p>Octobre 2<br/>Marched out from camp today leaving about 3.30 p.m. and reaching billets about 7 p.m. near Reningheltz. About 9 p.m. or 10 a formation of enemy planes came ever &amp; bombed round about here &ndash; but do not know what damage has been done.</p>
