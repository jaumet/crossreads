<p>Monday. 26/3/17<br/>Raining like hell, everything on the move expect we go this afternoon, Place Beaulancourt about 7 or 8 miles from here, no more time for writing,<br/>Packed up ready to start. just heard we don't go till tomorrow<br/>leave starts on the 31st latest.<br/>Pay 5/7 &pound;22-18.0.<br/>We are going to Villers au Flos about 10 miles away.</p>
<p>Tuesday. 27 3 17<br/>Up at 7. BK 7.30 off at 10. all details go with us first Delville Wood then Needle Trench, Rise Trench, Millers Son, Millers Daughter. Then Le Transloy &amp; near here Villers au Flos.<br/>Talk about a walk. it was a beaut. 5 or 6 miles on duck-Boards then through mud for another 4 miles, feet &amp; shoulders nearly made me give in.<br/>Saw 3 dead Germans alongside the track also some skulls. But shell holes, I've seen some before but never anything like this, not a yard between them. Huns must have got hell here in these Trenches. Le Transloy &amp; this place burnt or smashed to Pieces. No civilians, nothing but desolation.<br/>Snowed nearly all the way here &amp; cold wind blowing, am camped by myself in Ruined house Just a bit of roof left all else open so will be cold tonight.<br/>At 6 P.M. it is snowing thick &amp; fast My feet acheing terribly don't know how I'll stand another march tomorrow.<br/>Got a good fire going am sleeping under a Partly fallen wall. hope it does not fall in on me, won't matter much if it does, pretty full up of it.<br/>Houses have all been blown down &amp; even fruit Trees cut down<br/>Pay 6/7 def &pound;22-19-0</p>
<p>Wednesday. 28/3/17<br/>12 months ago today march from Tel-de-Kebir to Moaska, lot died from thirst through getting lost thanks to Genrl. Irving<br/>My left foot gave me fits last night &amp; is not much better this morning, no sleep don't know how I will do this march,<br/>Guns Banging away quite near us.<br/>Tin [of] sardines and Biscuit for BKs same for Tea last night. No dinner yesterday yet I carried 2 Blankets in my Pack, overcoat and Cape, Bag with rations Rifle &amp; Equipment.<br/>Relieve the 15th Brigade to day</p>
