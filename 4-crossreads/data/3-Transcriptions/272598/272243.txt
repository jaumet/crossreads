<p>had to wait till 1.30 pm before we entrained. Train left about 2 pm for Cheltenham we were told but were eventually landed at Gloucester &amp; were taken to the Palace Hospital where we were given a fine meal &amp; put to bed.</p>
<p>Thursday 18.10.17<br/>Compelled to stay in bed for the day, passed my time letter writing.  Very pleased with this place, being treated like kings.</p>
<p>Friday 19.10.17<br/>Given my blues &amp; allowed to get up, some swank in my new rig out; -  Blues, pair old civil boots, Tommy cap, red tie, &amp; stick</p>
