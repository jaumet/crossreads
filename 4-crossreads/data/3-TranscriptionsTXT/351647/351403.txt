 Isn't this simply absurd paper &amp; I have simply nothing to write about. 
 Still plenty of noise about &amp; still plenty of good news trickling in.  Hope things continue to go well. 
 Suppose you are back home again.  I suppose the weather is just glorious again.  How is Grahame &amp; the Boss &amp; old Mac.  Most insulting to call him old. 
 Did I tell you I missed seeing Stuart in England.  I had lunch with his Father &amp; Mother.  Stuart wrote me a most violently insulting letter giving me hell.  I deserved it, I did rather mull things.
