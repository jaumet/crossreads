
Northern France Disgraceful with their Dirty work it made our Blood Boil when we passed Through the Villages, they left some Villages & Towns in a shocking condition Some Towns had Certain Desease which I cannot tell you & the military put them out of Bounds to all Troops
The Civilians were left Starving they stole all their Cattle & when we passed Through their Cry was "Mungie" (Bread), we gave them our Rations Poor Devils
