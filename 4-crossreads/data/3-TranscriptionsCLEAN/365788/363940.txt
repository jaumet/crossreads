
12th May 16  France
My dearest Irene,It was very nice to get a letter from you yesterday & also one from dear little TinysWhat a little pet she is Irene! - Also thank you very much for   a nice parcel containing:- a very nice silk hankey, a novel. some cigarette papers, two cakes Cadbury's Chocolate & a nice tin of sweets (from FarmersVery kind of you old girl I am most grateful.There is talk of us being granted 8 days leave to go to England & I have written to the aunts & said I would like to pay them a visit one day if I get to England. - Had a letter from Nancy Norris yesterday They are living in a flat in
