<p>The attack all along very successful.  All objectives gained and held.  Shelling terrific up to about 12 <strike>pm</strike> midnight last night.  One machine (enemy) brought down close by, by machine gun fire and two near the front line.  Gun fire on both sides practically ceased by 4 AM this morning.</p>
