<p>[Monday 3 July 1916]</p>
<p>Many calls for officers for Camel Corps &amp; Artillery    Many of our best Rankers are getting commissions good luck to them-<br/>We carry on much as usual when not out on a <strike>Stroll</strike> STUNT, we are digging trenches in the sand, which promptly fill up again before the morning-<br/>Gen <strike>Cox</strike> our Brigadier has gone to England for 6 weeks so Col <strike>J B Meredith</strike> is our Brigadier again-<br/>Clive Weston a sergeant with me in Africa &amp; a brother of Mr Blake's was in my Tent half an hour ago.<br/>We hope to go back to the Reserve within the next 3 weeks about 7 miles from the Canal</p>
