<p>July, 1915<br/>Tuesday 27<br/>Standing waiting at Anglo Egyptian Bank this morning an Australian ambulance pulled up with a plate showing - <u>Presented by Sir Sam McCarthy</u><br/>Harry Phillips came up again today, he has grown into a fine chap &amp; is giving me an Infantry Mans PACK to carry my load.<br/><strike>I gave him a form re Commission but he does not think he will follow it up just  now.</strike></p>
