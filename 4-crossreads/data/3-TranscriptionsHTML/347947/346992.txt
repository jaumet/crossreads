<p>very busy Electric trams running and there were large buildings and Shops plenty of people. the place was crowded almost like London and they were very Scotch and amusing to us to hear them talk they were very interested in us also and the same evening visited Her Majesties theatre where Tonights the Night was played. It was very good a crowded house, afterwards came out and it was very cold we stayed at Balmoral Hotel  Market Street that night a very big place next morning got up about 10.30 had breakfast there and went out. our Slouch hat was very uncommon and people used look at us, and then at our badges we were a kind of unknown here.</p>
