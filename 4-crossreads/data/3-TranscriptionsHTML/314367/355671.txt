<p>can be said or done &ndash; For absolute freedom of discourse &amp; comment give me a naval mess &ndash; We wind up at 11 pm &ndash; Lights out &amp; bed &ndash; Fitz let all the Ship's company over the side yesty &ndash; This is very good for the men &ndash; I have just sent to Sydney for 6 lbs of Quinine. Fitz thinks we ought to have a good bit &amp; so do I especially if we go up Rivers &ndash; I was a the Hospital yesty &amp; saw Strangman &amp; Fitzherbert again &amp; talked Malaria &ndash; I've never had any dealings with Malaria &ndash; I got some good hints &ndash; of course here I cant do any blood work &ndash; mine's simply clinical observation &amp; treatment &ndash;<br/>The S.S. Sumatra has had queer treatment &ndash; The naval brigade officers had <strike>it</strike> her as barracks &ndash; They've been put out in the stream &amp; some 120 malarial patients shoved inboard with them &ndash; Lambton is furious &amp; I'm with him. Fancy sleeping close to 120 malarial cases &amp; a ship full of mosquitoes who bite &amp; transmit &ndash; Fitzherbert has already had an attack of malaria &ndash;</p>
