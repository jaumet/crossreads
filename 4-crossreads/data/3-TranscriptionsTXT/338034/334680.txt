We are now doing patrol duties &amp; are getting into close touch with the enemy. Taubes were overhead today &amp; bombed Bir-el-abd, one plane was very nearly brought down by our anti-aircraft guns. The railway line crossing the Desert has been hurried along lately &amp; is now about 15 miles East of Bir-el-abd, duplication work is proceeding apace and water from the Nile is being brought out  in through two large pipes, one about 8 inches in diameter &amp; the other about 12 inches.
