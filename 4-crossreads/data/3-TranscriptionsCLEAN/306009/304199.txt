  
 1915 Jan. 13 Wed. Arrived at Port Said at 1 a.m. Up early and kept busy in office early part of morning. 2 sugar bags of mail sent ashore . - Natives very smart at coaling - French hydroplane flight watched with interest - one battleship, cruiser and gunboat and several destroyers in harbour. Went ashore on leave at 11 a.m. and had a good look round returning to ship at 4 p.m. Supposed to be 10 Air-planes stationed at Port Said - 3 seen in flight in evening on the lookout for hostile vessels on sea and Troops on land. Weighed anchor & left for Alexandria at 5.30 p.m. Egyptian coinage:  Milliemes & Piastres. 10 mms. to a piastre. A piastre equals 21/2d.  Postage to Australia is: Letters 6 Mms. ; Postcards 5 Mms.
