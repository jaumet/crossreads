<p>Friday Sept 28th</p>
<p>Napsbury Hosp : dressed in my kahki &amp; went to the hall : left in motors about 11 am : nice drive all the way &amp; passed through London : one motor punctured &amp; while waiting a fruit shop owner gave us a fine feed of fruit : got to Dartford about 1.30 pm &amp; were put in our wards : I did not go to bed : seems a good place though out of the way of towns etc : air raid about 9 pm</p>
<p>Saturday Sept 29th</p>
<p>Dartford Hosp : wound examined &amp; dressed : the inside of my thigh &amp; under the knee is all purple with the effects of the bruised blood showing out : went to "Red Cross" &amp; got some small kit : handed in our khaki : splendid tucker here : air raid about 7 pm &amp; again at 9 pm : in the later one the</p>
