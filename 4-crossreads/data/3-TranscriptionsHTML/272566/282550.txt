<p>Sunday 15 April</p>
<p>Gas helmet parade before breakfast in lieu of route march.<br/>Orders have come through from the A.D.M.S. saying that all bearers are to be held in readiness for the line at a few minutes notice.  The Australians have been pushed back at Lagnicourt but after a fierce counter-attack, retook the positions, inflicting a heavy hiding on Fritz.<br/>Still on Incinerator.<br/>Put through tear gas to test our box respirators.<br/>Showery all day.</p>
