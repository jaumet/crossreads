 6.9.16 Wed 6th Yesterday morning a Sqdn of our aeroplanes dropped 96 bombs on Maghara where the Turks now have their Head Quarters; Palm bushes have been placed around the tops of all the Infantry tents here as our aeronauts say tents with a few bushes placed on them are invisible &amp; cannot be picked up from the air; I went to the YMCA tonight to have a yarn with the Sec (Mr Velvin);
