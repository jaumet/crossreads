  
 Thursday 15/2/17 Continued have to get water, that is ice out of shell Holes, farther up, the other day Boys were getting water this way & Boiling it for their Tea, one of Fritz's shells lobbed right in Shell Hole & unearthed 2 dead Tommies & one German. Hows that for a horror just imagine drinking that water. Sun nice & warm today & the frozen ground is thawing so of course Plenty of mud. 57th had a raid last night 2 killed & 1/2 doz wounded got 9 german Prisoners do not know how many dead. Keeping fritz on the jump. Heavy gun fire all day to day. 
 Friday 16/2/17 Terrific explosion this morning one of our ammunition dumps blown up sounded like a thousand guns going off at once dont know how many killed & wounded Our Planes in great Nos the most I have seen this trip, time too, fritz had it all his own way, expect to be here till the 26th March God knows when I will get leave now. Getting good food here & a comfortable dug-out so do not mind. To day the big bombardment is supposed to start 4 days of it, so far there has been a lively time of it all the morning & fritz is blowing up our duck-boards on the way to the line Pay 2.12.5. 21.0.0. 
 Saturday 17/2/17. Foggy & misty rain, good bye snow, sooner have the snow, the ground is mud again & getting like it was for our 1st & 2nd Trip here. Hope we soon get out of it now. The weather don't stop the guns & they are busy as usual Our Fellows are Bombing & Raiding fritz's Trenches every night now & has him Jumpy Pay 2.13.5. 21.1.0 wrote to Maude & Nell  but not posted yet  forgot to say do my own cooking here been at it 5 days now
