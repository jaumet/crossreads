 September, 1915 Wednesday 29 Just heard a Mail was going so post this.  2 letters from Kath &amp; one Aunt Louis mentioning Marjorie coming have not seen her yet. Love to all, tell Joe he cannot dream of joining the fun, no chance my going home for a holiday &ndash;   Probably go Dardanelles 10 days or so. Did my medal arrive? Photo goes later.
