<p>[Tuesday 7 November 1916]</p>
<p>Our Skipper has just received a wireless message from the Admiralty thanking him for his Seamanship in bringing us through the storm at one time we were drifting very near to the French Coast-<br/>It is calmer today but a very high sea running.<br/>The Ship's order O.C. Troops thanked the men who worked in the stoke hold baleing all night &amp; then Captain of the day.  The Tommies are a big hearted crowd &amp; <strike>the life in the troop deck is HELL, they have no room for exercise.</strike></p>
