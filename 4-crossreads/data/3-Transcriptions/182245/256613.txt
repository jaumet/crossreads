
<p>332</p>
<p>1917 APRIL</p>
<p>8 SUNDAY</p>
<p>A beautiful day &amp; very warm</p>
<p>I was stableman for the day,</p>
<p>cleaning up stables ect.</p>
<p>Got up on the stable roof</p>
<p>in the afternoon, &amp; had a</p>
<p>real good old sun bake.</p>
<p>Fritz shell battery again</p>
<p>I believe.</p>
