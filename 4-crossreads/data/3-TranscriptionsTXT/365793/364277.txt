 June 27  Messed about at Monte for about 4 hours &amp; have to go to Eye Hosp! tomorrow. Got towel sox Shirt &amp; tooth brush. Was coming out of the YM &amp; fell head over heels into a nice heap of stones. Recd a letter from Uncle, &amp; one from the NSW Bank re &pound; 25 cabled to me. Written a long letter home. This eye socket gives me jip at times, &amp; the glare of the sun &amp; much walking or reading makes me feel dizzy. Packs are at Plymouth so have written for them to be sent on here [in margin] M
