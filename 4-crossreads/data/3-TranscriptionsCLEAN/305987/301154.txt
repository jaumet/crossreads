Frid 9th:  Moved up to 5 Div "objective" & hopped-off with the tanks at 4.20 p.m. Advanced 3000 yds, captured Framerville & dug-in 300 yds on other side (9000 prisoners) Sat10th:  Bombarded by our own heavy artillery for [indecipherable] hours Sun 11th:  19th  & 20th moved through our position at 4 a.m. & advanced 1000 yds. 150 prisoners. Total advance about 10 miles. Total of prisoners for 2nd Div 9000. Relieved by 10th Bgde. came back to reserve trench. Sat 17th:  Relieved by "Tommies". Came back, per motor, to Fouilloy near Corbie.
