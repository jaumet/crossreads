 March, 1916 Friday 10 Work as usual during the day After tea several of us went for a walk to Zeitoun &amp; had coffee &amp; cakes Wrote a letter &amp; sent some stamps to Amy Bryant &amp; finished up by giving one of my pupils an extra hours work. 
 Saturday 11 Exam on Description takes up all morning. After dinner went along &amp; had tea w/- Dad &amp; go to pictures Make arrangements about going out tomorrow
