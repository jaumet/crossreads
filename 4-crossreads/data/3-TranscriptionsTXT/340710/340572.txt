   The Camel Corps.  Once upon a time moved by the mystic wand of romance, I had aspirations for a post in the Camel Corps and pictured myself in the role of a bold roamer of the desert scouring its fastnesses for the elusive Turk and eating a humble crust in the shade of my humble steed, who would watch my every movement with dog-like devotion despite the fact that it had neither meat not drink for many days. But all these wild fancies had being before I had the fortune to see the aforesaid Camel Corps and now having seen it in full swing my fancies have dwindled day by day.
