  
 31 Th May Nothing doing on the front.  Aerial activity  no sign of a move  must be waiting for Jacko to attack 
 1 Fri June General visited line 15 South of Fara in sight of Beersheba  great activity in air one Taube forced down 
 2 Sat June Artillery duel through out the day.  Taube forced down at Rafa  machine intact also pilot & observer  our chaps after Jacko when he shows out
