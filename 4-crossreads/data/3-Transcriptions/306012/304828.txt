<p>result of the "NO" vote at the referendum.  From the 1st January 1st Anzac Corps, to which we belong (1st, 2nd &amp; 5th Divs.) is to be known as the Australian Corps.  Evidently the 3rd &amp; 4th Aust. Divs. are being transferred out of 2nd Anzac Corps to us and the New Zealand Div. will probably go to a "Tommy" Corps.<br/>A notification has just appeared in A.I.F. Orders of the award of one Military Cross, one Meritorious Service Medal &amp; 4 mentions in despatches to Officers &amp; N.C.Os. of 14th Field Ambulance.</p>
<p>Mond. 14th Jan. 1918<br/>Last night the Sergts. &amp; a few others held a Dinner at a Tearoom in Etaples in order to congratulate the N.C.Os. who received M.S.M. or were "mentioned".  The affair commenced at 5 p.m. and finished at 9.  There were 24 present.</p>
