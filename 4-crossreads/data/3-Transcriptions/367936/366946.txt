<p>Saturday 11 March 1916<br/>Dick discing.<br/>I took drill down and started sowing.<br/>Putting 7 lbs of lucerne to acre.  Father &amp; Geoff working at sheep.</p>
<p>Sunday 12 March 1916<br/>Mr. Chriss Heath and his Mother &amp; wife came out and looked at show sheep.  Beautiful warm day.</p>
