<p>18 March 1915<br/>Thursday</p>
<p>Land on both sides of ship this morning<br/>See several craft in the distance<br/>Reports by wireless that a ship ahead of us has been fired on from coast.<br/>2 p.m. Round bend of coast line we come up w/- the Bee   &amp;  another large dark three funnel steamer but are not close enough to make out what she is.<br/>Pass one of the Empire Boats   &amp;  small tug or trawler.</p>
<p>1915 March 19<br/>Friday</p>
<p>Plan of defence for the ship is being prepared<br/>Rather heavy weather during the night<br/>Ship rolls a good bit<br/>Weather very pleasant for the Red Sea<br/>We practice fixing bayonets this afternoon</p>
