<p>They made the audience. The Sisters &amp; officers are not allowed to mingle with the "men". If a sergeant makes too free he is spoken to about it. But that is all one man's fault &amp; although we have to take notice of Him now, He will find out his mistake when we get ashore.</p>
<p>If my diary appear abrupt or empty on other days just imagine all these parades as still going on.</p>
<p>We cannot get our piano. It is packed away behind 500 tons of cargo so we will have to wait till we get to the other side.</p>
