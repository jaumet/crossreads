  
 1919 May 28 2/6 per time & were very slow.  Seeing that the dinner consisted of a cup of  tea, bread, meat, & lettuce, I think they made good thing out of it.  It was the first time though that I had seen a clergyman increase his stipend by such means.  We landed at Hampton Court & looked through the grounds, entered some of the rooms, & saw the grape vine 100 or 1000 years old - I forget which, the tennis court in a room & the Maze.  We reached the centre of this easily.  Some of [Two photographs of the group, on the boat & at Hampton Court]
