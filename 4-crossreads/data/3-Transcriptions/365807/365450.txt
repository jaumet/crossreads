<p>&amp; has an occasional hesitation at the commencement of some words. He said he still had some loss of "confissence" when speaking, but that was one of the few flaws I noticed &amp; I should say he will in all likelihood have made a complete recovery in another two months.<br/>I must say his recovery so far appears to have been exceptionally good &amp; it was a great relief to me to find him so wonderfully well. You will have heard he is boarded for Australia so I hope you wont have long</p>
