 In the evening they poured several shrapnel &amp; grape into the bushes about 20 yds on the beach side of our dug outs Booty Butler was hit by a bullett in the leg (in the knee) &amp; had to go to hospital. 
 Wright went to beach with dysentery &amp; Johnson with a boil The latter I can't stick. Dave Downey &amp; Con &ndash; Cunime came along in the evening &amp; stayed for a yarn. It was good to see them again.
