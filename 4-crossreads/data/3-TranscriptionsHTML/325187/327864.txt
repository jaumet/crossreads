<p>[Sunday 30 April 1916]</p>
<p>Went to 3 Churches, the Coptic, Armenian &amp; Missionary &amp; the Arabic Christians, the middle one made the most noise &amp; I should think was furthest away.  the first, burnt incense &amp; Candles etc:- the latter the dirtiest- truly there are many ways to Heaven &amp; only one God.<br/>Got a wire asking how long I shall be here on the Trial as I'm wanted on the Canal, where there is trouble again &amp; our whole 1st L.H. Brigade are to join again with the ANZAC Light Horse Division. composed of Australian &amp; N Zealands</p>
