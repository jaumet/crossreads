THURSDAY, JANUARY 16, 1919.
Fine. I went down to Horseferry
Road in the morning & got
extended leave till 23rd Jan.
About the flat all the afternoon.
Poss & I had dinner at Hatchetts
in the afternoon evening and went to
"Box of Tricks" at the Hippodrome
afterwards. Clyde went back
to Heytesbury.
