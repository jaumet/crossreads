
September 1915
Wednesday 15
Friday 17
Paraded at 7.30 & off at
Embarked about dinner time and after being well settled down, pulled out mid stream about sundown.
Mostly Tommies on board a good many inexperienced officers.
Felt rather lonely at night & went to sleep on deck while listening to an impromptu concert by some sailors. Still in midstream when went to sleep.
