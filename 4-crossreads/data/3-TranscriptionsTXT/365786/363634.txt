Europeans of course use wood &amp; coal which are imported.  The land is wonderfully fertile &amp; in many places will give four crops of lucerne in a year. As we went along fields of cotton plants &amp; clover looked flourishing &amp; the ripe barley quite golden in the bright Egyptian  light  atmosphere was a lovely sight.
