  
 Monday 27 December 1915  302 miles. 23' - 35" - 32"'  Adelaide 738 miles. A strong wind from the south. Cold  Not much sea.   All doing well.  Wind continued all day & into the night   Concert by crew. 
 Tuesday 28 December 1915   Miles 275. 23' - 37" - 32"'   463 miles to Adelaide.  A moderate Cold wind from the South . Clouds. 
 Wednesday 29 December 1915 Miles 273   23' - 37" - 44"' 190 Miles to Adelaide  Cold windy day Islands (Neptune) 5 to 8 p.m. Letters posted to Girls & others Telegram for the girls
