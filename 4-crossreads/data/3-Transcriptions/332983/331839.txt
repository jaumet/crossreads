We are now awaiting further developments<br/>15/11/1915<br/>Started to rain at 4.30 A.M. stopped at 7.30 A.M. Very sloppy underfoot. The French 9.2 are putting a few shots over on the Asiatic shore. A warship is also putting a few over. The Saddler Sgt and a man going over to Tenedos to buy some fruit and vegetables. A few shells over this morning. Inspection of horse lines and shoeing by Vet Officer at 10.30 A.M. He was pleased with the condition of horses and lines. This is the first time he was ever so.
