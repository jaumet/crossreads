<p>130<br/>"roaring forties" &amp; we struck boisterous weather for a week including a Snow Storm. Had to be screwed down so things weren't too comfortable.</p>
<p>Plenty of entertainment on board orchestra &amp; local concert talent also a miniature Picture Show which drew large "houses" &amp; the remarks from the Diggers was always the Star turn.</p>
<p>Spent 2 &frac12; days in Cape Town &amp; saw the place- very nice too. Secured a car one day &amp; drove round Table Mountain a gorgeous trip. Plenty of Fruit</p>
<p>Touched Albany- more fruit. Touched Adelaide- more fruit &amp; said goodbye to our S.A. pals. Great excitement on the Wharf.</p>
<p>Arrived Melbourne 20th Aug 1919</p>
