  
 Thursday  5th 1917 
 Relieved at all Posts by 1st Division & their Ambulance. We marched back to Bapaume & stopped the night at the Main Dressing Station. Had a good nights sleep - also hot tea & stew. Dull day, but fine. Saw a Taut brought down. 
 Friday  6/4/1917 
 Dull day but fine - Packed up all our Amb stuff & it nearly broke our hearts to learn that instead of going for the Divisional spell which has long been promised us - we have to set to and build a rest station between Beaulencourt & Bapaume. Had rather a heavy day. 
 Saturday  7/4/17 
 Raining & snowing. Rigging tents etc, more horrors etc. At 6.30 pm a mail was announced - what a difference it makes to a blokes spirits - I got letters from Annie - Eileen - Jess Hagan - Lill - Kit & Gert - Maud Single & papers & letters from Mabel Evans. As our mail is still closed cannot write at present.
