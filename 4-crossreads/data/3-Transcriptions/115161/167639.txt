<p>CHAPTER XXV11.</p>
<p><u>AGAIN - WE MOVE &quot;BACK&quot;</u>.</p>
<p>About an hour later we are marching away from our rotten experiences and are informed that we are now really going out for a decent &quot;spell&quot;.&nbsp; This puts more spring into our step and we feel that really it is not too bad after all.&nbsp; We always feel like that when we are going &quot;back&quot;.&nbsp; The misery is very soon forgotten.</p>
<p>What a wretched place the world would be if there was nothing to look forward to, no future, no castles to build.&nbsp; We all do the latter.&nbsp; The &quot;money grabber&quot; in his &quot;castle&quot;, sees a way of amassing greater wealth, and doesn&#39;t care a hang who suffers in the process.&nbsp; His one idea, his one &quot;castle&quot; is to make more money.&nbsp; His only enjoyment in life is money-making.&nbsp; He does not enjoy what money brings.&nbsp; He does not spend his wealth by helping others, unless it&nbsp;benifits&nbsp;him materially.&nbsp; If he does an apparently kind action, or helps anyone in distress, or gives to charities, or entertains, it is always for some reason or motive. You can bet your life that he will benifit&nbsp;by such actions.&nbsp; It will bring &quot;grist to his mill&quot;.&nbsp; He will use anything or anyone as a stepping stone to acquire his end.</p>
