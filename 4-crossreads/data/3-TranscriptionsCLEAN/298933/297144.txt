  
 Boves [April 1918] 18 Th It is miserable, rain and cold mists, the Brigade is being releived tonight by the Tommys 19 Fri Left Boves after dinner and after a March of 12 Kilos we stayed at Querriu in a fine mansion 20 Sat Once upon a time the  home  of  Count Alcantara .On the move again.A beautiful March to Bihencourt living in a lovely chateau
