<p><u>24th</u> After a hot night we steamed into a strong cool breeze and about 10 A.M. passed Minny Coy Island.</p>
<p>At 10.30 A.M.  The breeze freshened into a gale though the sea still remained fairly smooth. Ahead of us the sky is black with stratus &amp; cumulus clouds.</p>
