 Dec 14  th    Very warm to-day. but a fine misty rain was falling all day - went on supplies as usual. I swopped batteries with one of the other lorries, I now serve 18  th   batt. instead of 17  th   batt. Fred left for Boulogne with his lorry. Fritz shelled Hazebrouck heavily - 
   
 Dec 15  th    Beautiful day. but still wet underfoot - We did our supply trip - Jim had a liberal lot of rum up at the battery & kept it up in the evening - The 5  A.S.P.  D.S.C.  have had several lorries catch fire during this month. some of them burnt pretty badly - 
   
 Dec 16  th    Cold & windy. I had trouble starting my engine & had to get a tow to start, the magneto is crook (Dixie)  I carried on with the supplies the rest of the 5  th   D.S.C moved off to Boulogne to-day -
