
Wednesday 20/6/17Wrote to the General, raining Field day postponed.Thursday 21/6/17Raining, went to see Corps Line, had dinner with 17th then visited the nurses at 3rd C.C.S. [Casualty Clearing Station]Friday 22/6/17Field Day carried out, raining heavily.Saturday 23/6/17Remained in bed all day with a touch of Malaria. Runner shot at night.Sunday 24/6/17Church Parade, played Officers match against 28th cricket, we won, made a score.
