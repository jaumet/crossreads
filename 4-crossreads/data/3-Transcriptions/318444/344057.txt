<p>Oct 5th Sunday:-<br/>London railway strike settled</p>
<p>Sat Oct 18th:-<br/>Notice today that we sail for "Aussie" by the SS Marathon on Nov 4th</p>
<p>Oct 27th Mon:-<br/>Alf Felton won the world's sculling championship today on the Thames from Barry.</p>
<p>Tues Nov 4th:-<br/>The "Marathon's" sailing date has been put back for a few days</p>
<p>Fri Nov 7th:-<br/>My last look at London for some years at least. Left from Waterloo Stn by 11.55am. Train to Southampton &amp; embarked on the "Marathon".<br/>Drew outside of docks &amp; anchored</p>
<p>Sat Nov 8th:-<br/>Our delay in sailing is due to some of the firemen deserting. They have managed to get some more aboard &amp;   we sailed at 1.30 pm passing by the "Needles".</p>
