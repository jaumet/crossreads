<p>Diary<br/>S.B.Young<br/>36th Battalion Band</p>
<p>13th <strike>june</strike>May 1916.  Sailed through heads at 3 pm. On Transport A 72 S.S."Beltana" from Woolloomooloo Bay.</p>
<p>14th  Ran into a gale. Nearly all the boys were sick, myself included.  The band have been separated.  The 16 headquarters Stretcher Bearers (to which I am attached) are together on "B" Deck.  The others are distributed among the different companies. Our deck is Forward.</p>
<p>15th  Still sick.  Tucker good but can't eat it.</p>
