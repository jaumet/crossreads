Graham killed. 6th This work still continues. It is raining and the roads are in an awful condition. 6th Still raining-work continues. Our division has long since gone out of the line but we are still in and are likely to remain for some time. 9th Fourth Div. are about to come into the line again. Gee! how much longer are we to stay in this living hell? 10th Still raining. Job still progressing. Rotten.
