<p>4.<br/>in. He was lucky to get off with his life. Willy Mackenzie I haven't yet seen tho' I have several times been though his territory.</p>
<p>You will probably have heard the rumour that we bribed the Turks to let us go. There is not a fraction or shadow of truth in it, though it is getting round very fast. Think for a moment of the cabinet minister or ambassador who would dare to take the risk of informing any Turk, however confidentially, that we were really going. If the news had been betrayed &amp; the Turks had cut us up the man who had informed them (whatever his motive or precautions) would have been hanged. I saw the whole thing from first to last &amp; there is not a fraction of truth in this rumour.</p>
<p>With kind regards</p>
<p>Yrs</p>
<p>C.E.W. Bean</p>
