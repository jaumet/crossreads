<p>Blangy Tronville</p>
<p>[7 Sun]<br/>Went to Boves for S.A.A.</p>
<p>[8 Mon]<br/>Cleaning rifles<br/>Mounted Piquet 6 pm.</p>
<p>[9 Tues]<br/>Grooming and loading waggons</p>
<p>[10 Wed]<br/>Left Blangy Tronville 12 noon. Arr. At our next stopping place (about a Kilo outside Boves) about 2 pm. Fixed up lines then build our dugouts. Camped in an orchard (About seven kilos from Amiens.)</p>
