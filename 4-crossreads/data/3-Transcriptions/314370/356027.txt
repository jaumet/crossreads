<p>May, 1916<br/>Monday 15<br/>5 am to 9.30 am Tactical scheme.<br/>When they return a lot of the men fell out sick on account of the heat.<br/>After breakfast Lecture. After dinner we had to demonstrate use of Graticules for overhead &amp; indirect fire<br/>Temp 112&deg;</p>
<p>Tuesday 16<br/>Early morning. Tactical handling &amp; gun mounting.<br/>After breakfast Stoppages &amp; S.T.<br/>After dinner we had a lecture &amp; stoppages.<br/>Temp 113&deg; at 2.30 pm</p>
