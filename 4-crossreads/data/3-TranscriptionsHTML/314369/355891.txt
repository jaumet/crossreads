<p>18 September 1915<br/>Saturday</p>
<p>Still going strong temperature normal   &amp;  eating like a horse.<br/>Put hot foment on foot to try  &amp;  soften it a bit<br/>Expect to be allowed up soon.</p>
<p>1915 September 19<br/>Sunday<br/>16th After Trinity</p>
<p>Feeling a heap better to day  &amp;  am allowed to get out of bed for which I am truly thankful.<br/>I was getting pretty sick of lying down after 20 days</p>
