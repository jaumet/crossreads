
Aug 1st
The following are speeds up to date.  We started from Devonport at 12 noon roughly on 29th July.
Tuesday 29th to Weed 30th  276 m               30th to 31st            272 m               31st to 1st             279 m
The boat has a fair amount of deck space, but this is taken up by fixtures, and in any case is insufficient for the numbers on board.
We have a promenade deck on both sides amidships open to troops and families alike.  Above that is the boat deck forward half of which is for officers 2& first class passengers ( who are few), and the aft half except on isolation sections for troops above.  The aft half deck is for families & troops; the forward one for troops.  Aft for steerage passengers (wives etc)  & forward
