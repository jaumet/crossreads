<p>Sunday 23 June 1918<br/>Had some Tennis at the <strike>Knights</strike> Sewards.  Cycled there.  Very windy.</p>
<p>Monday 24 June 1918<br/>Came up to Town in afternoon &amp; stayed at the Imperial Hotel, Russell Sq. on my own.</p>
<p>Tuesday 25 June 1918<br/>Up for anor. Board.  Got 2 weeks sick furlough.  Helped to move some of the things at No. 10 to Perivale, Broadwater.</p>
<p>Wednesday 26 June 1918<br/>Lunched with Rup &amp; Mrs. Hendry at Victoria.  Down to Worthing &amp; stayed at the Beach Hotel.</p>
<p>Thursday 27 June 1918<br/>Hay making at Muriel's farm.  Plenty of wounded chaps in hospital down here.</p>
<p>Friday 28 June 1918<br/>Yankee airmen are out at Shoreham &amp; fly over the beach every day.</p>
<p>Saturday 29 June 1918<br/>Went out to Shoreham but could not go over the aerodrome as they had 'flu there.</p>
