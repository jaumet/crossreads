<p>December 1916<br/>Wednesday 20<br/>Left Mazar 2.30pm arrived Kilo (?) 7 pm had tea and pushed on thro the night. Awful journey (rough) fell down sandhill with camel.</p>
<p>Thursday 21<br/>Arrived El Arish 8.30 am camped outside the town about 5 miles away. L.H. captured the town.</p>
