 LILLE GATE [OCTOBER] Monday 1st  The fighting is harder than ever, every gun in the army's seems to be on this front, the casualtys are few consd. Tuesday 2 I feel well &amp; strong but the least shock or excitement makes me tremble, "ah" well a chap cant go on forever Wednesday 3 Tomorrow at dawn the 1st 2nd &amp; 3rd Div's of Australians go over the parapet, may good luck go with them  Thursday 4 Over they went and met with great sucess 4000 prisoner's and much booty, good work
