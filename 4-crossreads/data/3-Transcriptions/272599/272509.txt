<p>Sept. Sunday 2 1917</p>
<p>Fairly easy night, most of the bad cases either dead or evacuated.   Sister Stiggins &ndash; Carty &amp; inquisition   Beautiful moonlight night  Conway of 3 in at 3 a.m.<br/>Sleep soundly from 8 a.m. to 2 pm.   Up &amp; go to church at W.  Good old Pres. service. &amp; communion.    Arrange walk with Miss S. tomorrow.   Home to the  [indecipherable] .   Walk down village with Frank C. &amp; Lyle &ndash; Malaga &amp; Porto.   5.25 francs.</p>
