
I'm jolly glad I've got plenty of warm underclothing - what we shall so about our washing I do not know. Cannot dry it here   &  no place near to have it done - Bapaume in ruins, so no women about. My last lot is in Amiens   &  another lot at Abbeville, so you see I have to keep a big stock going. Wear grey aprons, wish I had more, shall send to England for some.
