
Thursday  8/3/17
Strong winds blowing - bitterly cold, snow fell at intervals during night & day. Light artillery very busy. Very few casualtys through.
Friday  9/3/17
Snowing - foggy etc - Very quiet. A few trench feet came through.
Saturday  10/3/17
Dirty foggy day - Snow thawing. Excepting for local artillery duels very quiet. Very few casualtys. Wrote to Mrs O'Kelly giving more particulars of Hubert.
Sunday  11/3/17
Miserable sort of a dull day. Busy ay night. Fritz's artillery giving us extra special attention - also throwing over great numbers of Minniewwurfers etc.
Monday  12/3/17
Dull to drizzling rain - Got letters from G. Nock & C. Hagan. Very busy all night, nearly all cases, were very badly wounded. Performed several urgent operations at M.D.S.
