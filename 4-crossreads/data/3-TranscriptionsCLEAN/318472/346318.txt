  
 3 April 1918 Wednesday Left Edinburgh about 1. P.M. & went to Glasgow In the afternoon Dr Eddy & I went to the Municipal Building to have a look at them. It is a beautiful Building. Saw my friend Marie Cameron there. In the evening went went to Govan to see some people named Boyd. They proved to be very nice Sociable & homely people. Left Glasgow about 10. P.M. 
 1918 April 4 Thursday Arrived Euston about 10. a.m. Went to Leytonstone about mid day. Left Waterloo about 6. P.M. Arrived Hurdcott about 9.30 P.M. The end of a very pleasant Furlough especially as regards my visit to Scotland.
