<p>Sister Martin &amp; she was better, did not stay long, so are both busy writing our letters. I received a letter from Mr. C. from Alexandria &amp; he sent me information of Keith. He is in the 5th London General Hospital Lambeth, so I shall send a p.card to his mother in case she does not know. Give my love to all my friends &amp; tell tem I shall write as often as I can. Fondest love &amp; kisses to you all &amp; trust you are all well. From your loving sister Edith</p>
