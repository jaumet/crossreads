 Wednesday 5 September 1917. 
 Sports today at Shiekh&nbsp; Narang  Nuran&nbsp;and to be concluded&nbsp; tomorrow &nbsp;Friday. A Taube came over about 11 am. &amp; although shelled from all directions escaped unharmed to his own lines. Wrote letters in time between reliefs on the picquet. Did first lot of cooking&nbsp;&ndash; a success too. 
 Boys returned from Marakeb Beach, &amp; another detachment went to take their place (Tent Division.)
