
April, 1915Monday 5Hamaseen = "Sirocco" the start of warm weather & usually blows at Easter certainly the worst sand storm I was ever in, poor Horses on Lines must have suffered -You will smile at my remarks re Rabbits, the men make pets, buy young ones in the Bazaars & in the moonlight they gambol among the Tents -Just rec letter Joe 28/2/15 & very sorry to hear no letters have been recd since a P.C. to Diary at Ceylon, I have written every 10 days at least & sent Diary - I'll send no more till I hear you have rec..d Home letters have gone to 6 Reg no doubt' & I'll get them in few days
