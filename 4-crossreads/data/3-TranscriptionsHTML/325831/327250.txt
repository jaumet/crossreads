<p>Jan 22, 1917</p>
<p>Dear Colonel Warren</p>
<p>I often get letters asking from whose memorial Newbolt's 'scd miles scd propatris' [but the soldier but for the country] comes. But there is no memorial in our chapel upon which these lines can be seen by a visitor. We only put up the names &amp; dates. The words are Newbolt's own &amp; represent the spirit of the bare notices. I once referred the question to Newbolt but he only wrote 'Sat that I know the lines are in</p>
