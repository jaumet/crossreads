 [Drawing of Lamp in Wilton Church.] The church inside smelt very mouldy and was badly lit.   The lamps were on the same pattern as those in the temple at Jerusalem and hung along each side.   Small gas flares poked out all round them but I thought that a few electric lights would have lit the place up twice as well.  You had to strain your eyes to read.
