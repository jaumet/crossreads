August 27thHad bath today &ndash; foot bandaged. It is just the same as it was 2 months ago &ndash;
I was cook today, preparing my favorite menu once again the dishes meeting with the approval of all &ndash;
Made my pay Book up today &ndash; I have a credit of &pound;400, this includes everything also allowing for debits that costs of cakes &amp;c also money that has been sent me from Australia &ndash; Capt Robinson &amp; Lt Baerline tried for their offence today August 28
August 28thRecd letter from Bert &ndash; also 2 Parcels from A.R.C. &amp; Gertie &amp; Eileen over 300 parcels in today &ndash;
Spent morning in the sun reading &ndash; Played Bridge &ndash; Debate tonight Subject being that all land &amp;c be under state control the motion was lost. Burke is now President of the Debating Soc.
August 29thSlept in till 11 am &ndash; Raining
