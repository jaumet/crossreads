<p>Whether he's been having a fast time in Paris I don't know.</p>
<p>I am leaving this place, no nothing wrong but it rather got on my nerves, especially the continual dragging about.</p>
<p>I tried to manoeuvre a move to London but it was beyond me &amp; I seem to have succeeded in manoeuvring myself figuratively speaking into the mud.  I expect I'll land back in the D.A.C. a Corporal &amp; with a new Boss &amp; possibly a new job &amp; (more than likely an outside &amp; more strenuous one) I don't foresee a pleasant near future.  However I can but go on till I drop.  War is hell my dear.</p>
<p>I haven't seen anything of Duncan or Kirby but they are not far off.</p>
