<p>had up to the present. Received all the letters safely thanks. Also one written to the 7th of the 3rd Batt from you. How is the cold better I hope. Any word from Bert as to when he is likely to be back. I would like to see him before I go. I believe we are to get our uniforms shortly. Don't think we shall be here very much longer hope not at all events. Will close now hoping to see all on Sat night. love to all<br/>Your sincere son Clyde</p>
