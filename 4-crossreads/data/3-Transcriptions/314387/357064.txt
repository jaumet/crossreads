<p>Wednesday 5th<br/>Went to Doctor in morning got another three days different treatment<br/>Thursday 6th<br/>Usual day doing nothing<br/>Friday 7th<br/>Same again<br/>Saturday Dec 8<br/>Marked out by Colonel got uniform again left in Ambulance afternoon for Marlbourough Details camp arrived there dusk went down to Boulogne &amp; night.</p>
