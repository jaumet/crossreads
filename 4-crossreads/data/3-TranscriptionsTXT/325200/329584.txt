Six of the 2nd Reg. were caught by 30 turks when patrolling yesterday afternoon this fellow was hit  &  they brought him under cover over their saddles.We pumped 10 rounds from a battery of 18 pdrs onto Oghratina  &  then came home reaching here about 12 oclock. Stand to again at 3 - I shall sleep well today.24th JulyWent out to Katia again at 2 am yesterday morning  &  came back again at midnight. We all lay on our backs for about half an hour in the morning watching
