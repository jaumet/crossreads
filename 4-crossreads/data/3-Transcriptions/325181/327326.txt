<p>25 Spt 1915<br/>Another cool morning. Large percentage of eggs for breakfast are rotten (Not too good.) Several operations lately. Rupture &amp; appendicitis all patients doing well.<br/>26 New Orderlies on now so tucker slow in coming &ndash; Colonel told me have good spell, &amp; take things easy when I return to Office &ndash; May not return to Office at all &ndash; all depends; &ndash; Glorious music this afternoon, piano, 2 Violins &amp; flute - Rec'd letter from Mrs Robson dated 19 July &ndash; 1st since l'vg Gallipoli. [in margin] <u>Sunday</u><br/>27 Had good night. Didn't go Church yest'y &ndash; Felt not well enough.<br/>Spending about 1/6 per day. Must go easier.<br/>28 Nurse wont let me go out for meals, altho' I feel OK. Getting colder every day. Cool inside</p>
