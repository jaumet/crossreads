 Harefield. 
 1st A.A.H. Hospital 
 [25 Sun] Leave into Brighton. 
 [26 Mon] Left Brighton in the morning.  Arr London about 11-30 am. Had an hour in London. Arr Harefield about 2pm. Put into bed Trench fever 
 [27 Tues] In Ward 2 1st A.A. Hospital Transferred to Ward 3. 
 [28 Wed] Feel better so got up. X-Ray on arm  Dr. Broadhead in next bed.
