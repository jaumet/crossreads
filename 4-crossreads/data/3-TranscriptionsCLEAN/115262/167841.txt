
Thursday 15 March 1917.
1st Sham fight that we (A.C.F.A) took part in. 
The "white hats" were forced to retreat over the hills & across the "Trawool" bridge. Our No1 dressing station had to be disbanded in the retreat.
After dinner on the banks of Goulburn River we were marched in a round-a-bout way to the camp. Dist. 20 miles.
No lecture. "Furphies" still going strg.
