 June 23  rd    Sunday - We went to Frechencourt with supplies, we stop at La Chaussee every morning on our way to Ailly-S.S- & pick up Gisele & her mother drop them at Ailly-S.S- go to railhead for supplies & pick them up again on our way through at a cafe by the railroad crossing & take them on to Montieres where they stay for the day & we call for them on our way home & take them back to La Chaussee  It is forbidden to take Civies to-wards Amiens, but always passing the sentries we put the storm sheet up & hide them. I got a lovely bunch of roses & other flowers at the big gardens by the Canal - In the evening Jno & I went to Church with  June 24  th    We we  Zita & Marie. going home with them afterwards - 
   
 June 24  th    We went to Frechencourt with supplies. we stay out at Frechencourt & have a comfortable dinner & go home at our leisure, when it is warm enough we stay awhile on the boulevardes of Amiens. we always spend some time in the flower & fruit gardens & parking out at Belloy-S.S. amongst the civilians we almost forget there is a war on & we very much enjoy our privilages. not granted but taken nevertheless
