<p>28.11.15<br/>Cancel all orders for winter clothing asked for in previous page. A big snow storm set in last night &amp; is going strong now everything is covered in about 3 inches of the pure article, it is the prettiest sight I have seen in my life looking through our loopholes towards the Turks trenches, every hill gully, tree &amp; bush covered in snow &amp; "Abduls" trenches barely distinguisable &amp; by hell it is cold, the men go on post &amp; come off after an hour white from heels to head, &amp; we havnt had an issue of winter clothing yet, our cooks have an open cookhouse, so things are</p>
