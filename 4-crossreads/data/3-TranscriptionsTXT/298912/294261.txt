"Ashes to ashes & dust to dust!The officers wont eat you but the privates must"
The last service was held on the poop deck & on the last count out, it was gradually lowered & on "TEN" it was dropped plate & all into the sea where it sank amid howls & groans. Not an officer showed up during the disturbance & the only thing done was to tip it out on deck & shovel it over the side. The Chief Steward gave this order, his knees knocking together with fear.
