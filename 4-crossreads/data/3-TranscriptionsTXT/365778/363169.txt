 Sunday 21/1/17 
 Depot closes on 28th went to Corps and also to Ribermont re same. First Draft leaves tomorrow. Wrote to Doff. 
 Monday 22/1/17 
 First draft left &amp; were very good. Had a visit from Somerville. 
 Tuesday 23/1/17 
 Visited by Birdwood &amp; White &ndash; gave lecture on Map Reading &amp; Field Sketching &ndash; bitterly cold return home from Divisional School. 
 Wednesday 24/1/17 
 Second Draft away. School closes on Saturday &ndash; am removing all equipment etc.
