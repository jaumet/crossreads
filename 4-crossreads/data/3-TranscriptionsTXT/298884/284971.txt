 The sky is dotted with Cirrus &amp; Cumulus clouds. 
  24th  After a hot night we steamed into a strong cool breeze and about 10 A.M. passed Minny Coy Island. 
 At 10.30 A.M.  The breeze freshened into a gale though the sea still remained fairly smooth. Ahead of us the sky is black with stratus &amp; cumulus clouds.
