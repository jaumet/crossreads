<p>Monday 9th<br/>Another ship was sighted early, which kept close to us all day. It is very funny to see it seemingly steaming all over the place, for sometimes she is astern and others ahead. It was caused by the zig-zag movement of our ship.</p>
<p>By dinner time we were out of sight of land and were rolling a good bit which was very inconvenient at meal times.</p>
