<p>Tuesday April 9th - 18<br/>A quiet day getting used to things Think we have struck oil</p>
<p>Wednesday 10th<br/>Motored about 1 mile to see a chap decorated with MM. Another VAD place like this one only not so large</p>
<p>Thursday 11th<br/>Went for a walk in Village Buckingham Cabled home &ndash; (Word very slight cabel &pound; 5)</p>
<p>Friday 12<br/>Another walk got as far as the beach The first sunnie day since coming here</p>
<p>Saturday 13<br/>Usual thing</p>
<p>Sunday 14th<br/>Explored the Grounds Found a Bell Tower but could not get in to ring the Bells</p>
<p>Monday 15th<br/>Started out for Margate without asking the way &amp; landed in Westgate. Quite a large place The sea front is protected with barb wire entanglements</p>
