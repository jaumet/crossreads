St Patricks Hospital
Malta
27/12/15
We came into this hospital last night from mudros wich we left on Thursday December the 23rd and arrived in Malta harbour on Saturday the 25th about 2pm. Christmas Day we spent on Board Ship (Soudan)  for Dinner we had roast Beef potatoes - pumpkin - and Plum Pudding and turkey. (Oh turkey) But we we were Sick of turkey as we were having lumps of it Since april the 26th  So we had been haveing it for Eight months and then we had to withdraw  Because Some turkeys are to hard to pluck  So lets have Chicken next Xmas
