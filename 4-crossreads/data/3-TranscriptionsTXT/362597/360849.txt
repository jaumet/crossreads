
They made the audience. The Sisters & officers are not allowed to mingle with the "men". If a sergeant makes too free he is spoken to about it. But that is all one man's fault & although we have to take notice of Him now, He will find out his mistake when we get ashore.
If my diary appear abrupt or empty on other days just imagine all these parades as still going on.
We cannot get our piano. It is packed away behind 500 tons of cargo so we will have to wait till we get to the other side.
