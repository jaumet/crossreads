<p>22/8/16  Tuesday</p>
<p>Dull &amp; raining &ndash; Lively trench warfare &ndash; slight casualtys &ndash; Snipers very busy with enfilade fire on our roads.</p>
<p>23/8/16  Wednesday</p>
<p>Dull &amp; raining &ndash; Fairly quiet in the Saps. Fritz very quiet &ndash; Got a letter from Jen Hagen &amp; Mrs O'Kelly.</p>
<p>24/8/16  Thursday</p>
<p>Mysely &amp; 5 others were ordered in to "Nouvea Monde" for a blow as we had the longest spell in the trenches &ndash; Will be pleased to toget away from the shells for a while.</p>
<p>25/8/16  Friday</p>
<p>Dull day &ndash; Did a few odd jobs about the Dressing Station &ndash; Very few cases from the trenches &ndash; Have a lot of Tommys &amp; Scottys with us in the Billets.</p>
<p>26/8/16  Saturday</p>
<p>Very heavy wind all day &ndash; Doing some odd jobs all day &ndash; Went up to Bac St Maur in the afternoon.</p>
