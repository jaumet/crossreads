<p>July 1918<br/>Monday 1</p>
<p>Vaux sur Somme.1st Bty Posit.<br/>Arrived with guns at 2.30 am, after a fairly long march.<br/>There seems to be a big concentration of artillery heavy and otherwise all along the front . Rumored a big stunt to come off on the 4th.<br/>Weather very warm and fine.<br/>Walked to W.L. with despatch and walked home again &ndash; 18 kilos altogether. Ken and I are making dugout for two &ndash; rotten position.</p>
