<p>February, 1915<br/>Sunday 7<br/>Straightening up Cargo &amp; held Church parade all the Reinforcements are here together -  arrived crowd of infantry, artillery, &amp; Light  Horse, Camels, Guns, Wagons Remounts, - Hospitals, Veterinary Sections etc:-<br/>Gravelly pebbly sand the Desert has no vestige of grass or herbage &amp; "boots" wear out very quickly.</p>
