<p>Am really knocked right up through troubling about it all &amp; these miserable officers will not give one a chance. I'll go to the front if I have to clear out. After work spend the evening at home, after visiting the tailor</p>
<p>8th September:- Friday<br/>A fine day &amp; warm. Still formulating schemes to get out to the front    &amp; and <strike>I</strike> if I know much, at least one will be successful    &amp; quickly. Work as usual &amp; nothing out of the ordinary routine. At least 500 German prisoners arrive.</p>
