
Saturday 24 April, 1915
The 5th Brigade went on route march 18 miles.
The Brigade consisted of 17 - 18 - 19 Battalions of Infantry.
The 5th Brigade with Light Horse, Artillery, A.M.C. and French Sailors march through Sydney on a fare well send off.
Total number of Troops present 5,000.
25,000 people to view us, we marched part of the way, From Victoria Markets to Station through the rain.
