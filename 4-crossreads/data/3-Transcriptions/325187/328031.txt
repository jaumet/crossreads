<p>[Sunday 15 October 1916]</p>
<p>Delaney comes to town.<br/>Dined with Mrs Cotton No 17 Bus get out at Windsor Road &amp; Parkers to Tea 11 <strike>Cof</strike> Corfton Road only 10 minutes walk<br/>Met Laura &amp; Connie at the Parkers<br/>In the morning went to Regents Palace Hotel to meet Doctor Eastwood &amp; his wife who go out to Australia this week &amp; may see some of my friends out there.  I am introducing Dolly to all I can.</p>
