<p>Saturday 26 May</p>
<p>Weather continues fine.<br/>The premises we are now occupying are the cause of much comment.  Gardens etc. are looking first class.<br/>Exp. Force Canteen next door to us &ndash; Comes in handy for morning and afternoon tea.<br/>Fritz threw shells round our end of the town and inflicted many casualties on the 29th Batt.  Stray pieces of steel flew into our yard.</p>
