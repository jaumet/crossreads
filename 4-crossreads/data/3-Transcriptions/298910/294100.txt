<p>31/1/17  Wednesday</p>
<p>Snowing &ndash; very cold &ndash; Very quiet except for Artillery duels. Our Bearers went out.</p>
<p>1/2/17  Thursday</p>
<p>Frosty &ndash; About 6 pm 4th Division hopped over &amp; took easily a very important trench, they had few casualtys &amp; came back with some 50 prisoners. Heavy bombardment all night &ndash; All pay books called in. Got a fine parcel from Frank.</p>
<p>2/2/17  Friday</p>
<p>Frosty &ndash; Heavy bombardments, several small successful raids &ndash; a number of prisoners came through. Dr Helstrom got badly wounded at one of the posts &ndash; he was a great patient &amp; said the &ndash; thing burst 6 yards away &amp; this is what I got - is it good enough for Blighty &ndash; several bearers got slight wounds.</p>
<p>3/2/17  Saturday</p>
<p>Frosty &amp; bitterly cold. Very few casualtys although two lines of captured trenches were retaken four times, finally our boys bombed them out &amp; made good. A few more prisoners came through.</p>
