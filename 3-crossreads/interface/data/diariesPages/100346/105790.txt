Friday Oct 26th
Sutton Veny : showery : classification in morn furlough parade in afternoon : sent a dozen views of Weymouth to Mum Aunt Myra :
Saturday Oct 27th
Sutton Veny : showery : cookhouse fatigue in morn : stroll to Warminster in afternoon met Amos Miller Vic Berghoffer : wrote in army hut after tea 
Sunday Oct 28th
Sutton Veny : frost cold day : fatigue in morn : wrote in afternoon : song service in Army hut after tea
Monday Oct 29th
Sutton Veny : frost : showery day : fatigue in morn : parade for furlough particulars in afternoon : read in the army hut after tea
