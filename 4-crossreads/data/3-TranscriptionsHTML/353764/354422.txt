<p>though &amp; I am hopeful of turning it out a first class charger<br/>I am very tired after my long ride &amp; also feeling very dirty, but I do not get time to have a bath.<br/> The boys are not having so good a time now, as they are all up at all times of the night.<br/>The Strange Crowd we took in are proving themselves to be very troublesome &amp; I intend to start on them properly in the morning.<br/> I have not heard from Jack for a long while, so I presume he is now down at Tel-el-Kebir.</p>
<p>13.3.16 Monday<br/>Another day passed with a good deal of Convoy work over.<br/>I got the surprise of my life to-day caused by a Cable sent by Nina.  It was keenly appreciated &amp; I don't think I ever thought as much of anything as I did of that Scrap of paper with a few words on it.<br/>Expecting another batch of horses from Maadi to-day 90 all told.</p>
