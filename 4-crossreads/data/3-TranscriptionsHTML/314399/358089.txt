<p>Wednesday 2 April 1919<br/>Lovely day once more. Was dusty in town tho. didn't hit camp till 11 pm. Bill &amp;   I went out to St.Addresse [Adresse] &amp; sang a bit. Had a very quiet night altogether.</p>
<p>Tuesday 3 April 1919<br/>Lovely day again. Met Moffitt, Carmichael, Bruce Oliver &amp; Bruce Sloan. Went &amp; had a look at a game of soccer which was cruel. Was in charge of trains again. Things were very quiet tho. Had to meet a V.A.D. but she didn't turn up but her pal did &amp; explained matters.</p>
