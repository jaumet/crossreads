 Friday July 13.7.17 
 Turned out 5.30 felt very dry had a mug of Beer fed up had Breakfast.  got very sick.  I heared all over the Place it was the Beer that made me sick.  cleaned up the camp rested till 12.30 had dinner feel a little Better rested till 4.30 P.M. fed up had tea saddled camel and went on an out Post 3 mile away nothing doing turned in feel O.K. now.
