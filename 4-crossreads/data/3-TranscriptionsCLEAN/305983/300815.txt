  
 24/9/17 Beautiful day.  nothing of interest other than that we are to have a football match today.  yesterday  potatoes & peas today & yesterday Football match took place but was stopped  on account of so many men stopping in camp.  We had read out to us that a Russian was shot for try to escape also one for murder & one English shot for trying to escape.  Football match will be continued  today  tomorrow on these conditions all go out or all stop in. 
 25/9/17 Close day. no wind at all  Zeppelin flew past our camp today it was miles away but it was quite distinct. could see the gondola swung under. Football match took place fine game considering.  Turnips & spuds for dinner. 
 26/9/17 Nothing of interest.  beautiful day  Tired feeling today usual occourance
