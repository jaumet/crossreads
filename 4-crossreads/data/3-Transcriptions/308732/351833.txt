<p>The Bombardment kept up about two more hours in case Fritz made a counter attack, but nothing happened, so all our guns stopped, and after a while, Fritz started to pay no a little attention &amp; he pasted our position with shells, big &amp; little, but never did the slightest damage, as we kept close in our little dugouts, so he would have had to lob one right on to a dug-out, which would be very difficult as they are just behind a high bank, but dirt, stones, &amp;  bits of shell were rattling down on top of them, &amp;  the concussion shook the whole earth, I lit a candle to read, but although closed</p>
