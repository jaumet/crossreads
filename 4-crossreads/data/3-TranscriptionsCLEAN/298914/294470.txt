  
 and at the top - in full moon - pass part of the 60th Div who are singing "When v come to the end" etc.  They give 3 cheers for "the dinkums" and swarm over to us as we wind upwards - a long stream of horsemen reaching for miles.  Very cold here and misty. 24th 1am draw up in valley and draw rations.  2am proceed and arrive Jerus as the bells strike 3 am.  Bitterly cold wet & muddy.  Everyone frozen stiff.  Making the pace - a welter passing.  Calvary Damascus Gate & thence via Jaffa gate.  Arrive Bethlehem 5 am wet and muddy.  Have a sup of tea and at 8 am turn in. 25th Pack up ready to leave and at last min orders cancelled. 26th leave ( Jerusalem via ) Bethlehem 10 am and take a stony road via Hebron arriving Zacharias 4 PM Camp overnight. 27th Leave 9 am and continue past Junction Station and through AKIR and on to Deiran. 5/3/18 JC Tetson to Cairo on leave. 8/3/18 Practice stunt to Yebra, all aboard the carts.  A bum turn out. 11/3/18 9 CS backsheesh arrive.  W5 move out to NZMR.  HQ & I move over to AIF cable. 12/3/18 Move out and camp at Lavron. 3/3/18 On to Jerusalem arriving 1700 and camp in Section Camp. 14/3/18 Cold bleak day. 15/3/18 Blowing like hell and cold.
