Salonica, the Imperial Yeomanry Division.  There has been a considerable amount of sickness amongst our men lately & a great number of our horses are weak & in very poor condition & badly need a spell.
Sept 18th 17:  The Anzac Division moced out to the Second Line of defence today our month's rest on the beach having expired.  We relieved the Australian Division, which moved out to the Front Line to relieve the Yeomanry Division which moves into the beach
