
October 191618th Continued.  A band Concert was held in the mess hut to-night & proved a great success.19th   Heavy rain has fallen since last night.  I had to go out to the Kangaroo Rifle range this morning with reference number 104 to go through another musketry test.  Kangaroo Range is about 4 miles from here & on the other side of the Bull Ring21st   The weather has been very cold to day & yesterday with thick frosts in the morning.  I have had a very bad cold for the last two or three days & am not feeling up to the mark
