Sept.  Monday 24 1917
A note arrives from Bob.   Sure enough he was among the 20 &amp; got 8 wounds in leg arm &amp; bum.   all slight fortunately.   In Etaples hospital.   Expects to go Blighty.   He is a lucky dog, but it's a great relief to know he's out of it again.   Fighting very heavy &amp; hosp filling rapidly
