<p>to catch a Tube to Waterloo from Cambden Hill, where I left the Car, but my luck was out as the last Train had left, I then had to wait for another Car which only took me to the Depot, which was half way, in this Car I met an Australian Captain also my Chum &amp; we waited for a half an hour &amp; along came the last Car to Tottencourt Court Road &amp; in we got &amp; in a little while arrived at the Terminus, where my Chum left me, &amp; the Captain &amp; myself had to walk along Oxford St &amp; passed <strike>Liester</strike> Leicester Square then Trafalgar Square &amp; around into the Strand, where I said Good Night to the Officer, &amp; continued on &amp; over the Waterloo Bridge &amp; arrived at the Club at 1.30 am, where I turned in &amp; was called at 5 am, &amp; after Breakfast caught a Tube &amp; soon was at Victoria where I had to report for France. There were a large number of Boys returning also several Drafts &amp; at 7.30 we all were entrained, the first Trains lef at 7.50 containing Officers who travelled in Dining Cars, &amp; at 8.10 am we were off &amp; after a through run without a stop arrived at Folkstone at 10 am where we were marched</p>
