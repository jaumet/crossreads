<p>Arrived at Melbourne (Spencer St) 3.30 pm 9/10/15.  <br/>Marched to Albert Park &amp; camped near S. Melbourne Cricket Ground.<br/>General leave granted at night.  Visited city. Returned to camp 10.15 pm.</p>
<p>Sunday 10/10/15.<br/>Reveille 6 am  No early morning parade; church parade 10.30. went St Silas     Church, Albert Park.  General leave granted in afternoon, visited Domain &amp; Gardens, very enjoyable afternoon, gardens very nice.</p>
<p>Monday 11/10/15<br/>Parades, 6.30 am.  9.30 AM &amp;and: 2 pm.  Remained in Camp at night.</p>
