 27 July, Sunday 
 Drive to Dee Why. Go walk on hill. Duck for dinner. George to Marathon at Long Reef. Cold &amp; windy. 
 I look out all letters &amp; P.C's &amp; we sort them up &amp; sit to talk after tea. 
 Wrote Auntie Annie 
 28 July, Monday 
 Mum &amp; I go out in rain to H'ville, meet Dick's people &amp; then go to Nelsons. Arrive Home at 9 pm.
