<p>(Received parcel from Jess)<br/>Mon.16 cont.<br/>night. Picked up a piece of the shells that fell near wagons. Boys reckon that it is 9.2 that Fritz is sending over. None injured. Boys quiet pleased that they have not got to shift their huts that night.</p>
<p>Tuesday 17th<br/>Clearing everything away from old camp, as no one allowed to sleep there that night. Mule lines, huts etc. fixed by night.</p>
