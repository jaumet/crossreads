<p>21st.   This morning we had a Battalion parade &amp; for the first time in my life had charge of a Platoon on such, after this was over we had the usual drill &amp; a rifle team was picked out for the competition", they wanted me to take charge of it but I did not want the job so another Corporal took it on</p>
