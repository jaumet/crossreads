7th Nov:-We have been awaiting orders of removal &  wonder if we are to be sent on to the front, so the suspense is annoying. Detailed for inlaying picket to-day, & manage an accident to my shoulder. Very painful. After first parade, a surprise awaits me. We are to pack up kit & go to No 1 Hospital at once. After breakfast we set off and are duly sworn in & detailed to various duties in the hospital. So far, so good. Sort of "Do as you are told & ask no questions" business.
