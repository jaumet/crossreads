  
 Nov. 3 - 4 - 5 
 Trop ships from Freemantle joined us,  also other boats. two of our escorts returned to Australia  a mistake.  a concert after tea (37 troop ships now & 4 cruisers  & a battleship  are with us. 
 Wed 4th Had a very rough sea last night. the mugs & dishes were racing each other up & down the table all night; I was on duty from 4 AM. till 8 AM. on the bridge  Semaphore practice morning.  saw flying fish by the dozen yesterday another horse died.  teaching troops semaphore afternoon.  had a lecture on scouting after tea
