<p>In India &ndash; in the rice fields with a child on their back guiding them  -  Great lead colored -  unwieldy brutes &ndash; bulls horizontally horned  -  They scratch their sides in the most delicate manner with just the tip of their horns  -  Then we struck what I was sure of interest &amp; up I went &ndash; a lot of bee hive &ndash; circular huts &ndash; built of straw &ndash; raised 5 to 8 feet from the ground &amp; with a deck floor &amp; steps up  -  It was a native compound &amp; in we went &amp; explored  -  Lots of natives &ndash; males mostly some children  -  I saw no women  -  They were all very friendly &amp; evidently were connected with the gardens  -  They had a lot of mongrel dogs &ndash; like dingoes  -  Iron pots  -  They smoked &amp; yabbered away  -  The huts are circular</p>
<p>[drawing of hut]<br/>  -  straw for sides straw for roof  -  They'll burn fast enough &amp; must be hot shops</p>
