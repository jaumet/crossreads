<p>7 January, Tuesday</p>
<p>F Lovely Day</p>
<p>I have to return all gear to Q.M. &amp; send Patients to H &amp; I lines. I lose half my Bolsters &amp; lots of gear 'cos it is too much to do in one afternoon. I am ord S/M. write all night.</p>
<p>Wrote Lottie, OS, Jennie. Mum.</p>
<p>8 January, Wednesday</p>
<p>F<br/>Go down Town to see Dick Whittington &amp; His Cat with Ellis &amp; S/S Lilley. It is very long up to 11.15 pm. Very good in parts. I meet Natt, Roy's Brother. He, with whom I was nearly drowned. There is lots of sentimental rubbish shewn &amp; I am very scornful of it all. We have to walk all way Home via steps route.</p>
