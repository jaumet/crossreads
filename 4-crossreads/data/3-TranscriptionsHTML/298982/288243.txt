<p>23rd Mar Tuesday<br/>Left for Meadi Sham fight en route arr about 4.30 Spent evening at Mr Heaths till 12 am</p>
<p>24th Went on by road to Helouan beside river Nile arr about 2 45.<br/>Usual mess up over watering.<br/>The General Godley came &amp; inspected the lines as soon as all was settled</p>
<p>25 Left Helouan Reached Meadi<br/>Six inches between Viney horses!</p>
<p>26 Left Meadi<br/>Fight did not come off or we so nix</p>
