<p>March Friday 16 1917</p>
<p>Busy all morning putting down floor in tent.<br/>Reg brings along horse in afternoon. Have a ride, first since leaving Australia. Good &amp; willing cob. mouth as hard as iron. <br/>McBurney over to see me. Cartwright's post card. Write to same tonight.<br/>Send T. cheque.<br/>Ern. Murray up after tea. Going back to Company in morn. Eyes better &amp; M.O. would not send him, on. He works hard &amp; conscientiously important &amp; responsible position  Interesting to talk to about Egypt.</p>
