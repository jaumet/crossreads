
Bunkum.  There are hundreds of nice girls around here but I never go out.  I have been to Dances & so has Ellis & Dick, but the Dance over we come back & hide ourselves from them as from shells.  Not that there is any reason for this, but we have too much reading & writing & general tidying up & sewing & washing & shaving & boot cleaning etc.  Of course we don't have to do these things every night but the Girls take a fellow out every night & I'm sure I would have a bust up with one of 'em.  I've always said that they are good pastime & when one can pick 'em up & set 'em
