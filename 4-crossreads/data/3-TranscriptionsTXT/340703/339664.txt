 November 1916 Friday 24 Bought 100 oranges for trip. Breakfasted CTC. Train with Senussi prisoners arrived from Dahkla 10.30am. Took snaps. Visited village in afternoon. Very interesting cultivation prolific. Mud houses mostly underground. Filthy hovels .Visited the Omla who gave us afternoon tea English style. Had a swim in big fresh pool of unknown depth. We leave tomorrow. 
 Saturday 25 Left 7.30 am with the convoy of 200 camels and about 80 natives.
