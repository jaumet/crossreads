 Sunday 21/4/18&nbsp; Birthday- cable from Doff- Had a good dinner in a cellar- Wrote Doff 
 Monday 22/4/18&nbsp; Orders to move into line &amp; take over all Bde front. 
 Tuesday 23/4/18. Took over from Currie &amp; Chalmers. Went around line. 
 Wednesday 24/4/18&nbsp; Quiet Day- usual Shelling- men working well. 
 Thursday 25/4/18&nbsp; Heavy shelling on front- few casualties &amp; a bit of gas. 
 Friday 26/4/18. Letter from Doff- [Later?] Coy relief- Hun keeping low.
