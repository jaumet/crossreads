<p>Friday 29&nbsp;June 1917.</p>
<p>On no special duty today. Drill during morning and at 1.30 p.m. fell in <s>at</s> dressed in shorts, shirts &amp; carring our towel<s>s</s> &nbsp;waterbottle<s>s</s>, we were taken to one of the Bitter Lakes at Ismalia for a swim.</p>
<p>Crossed a pontoon bridge over a fresh water canal. Went within a mile &amp; &frac12; of Ferry&#39;s Port, Ismailia. Saw 2 native girls. They are very pretty &amp;&nbsp;<s>nice</s>&nbsp;peculiar mannered. Had a conversatn. with some natives. Natives came out on the sands while we were dressing &amp; tired to sell their wares but most of us hung shy6 of them. (Latest) (Aust. mail in Egypt for our unit gone astray).</p>
<p>(Found later that it went to Abbassia 1<sup>st</sup>)</p>
