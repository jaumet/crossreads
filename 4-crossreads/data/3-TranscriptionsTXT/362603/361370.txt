 I have a letter of Georgies to answer   &amp;   I must keep some news for Him.  We are both Tip-Top now   &amp;   I am quite used to Night work   &amp;   will be sorry to go off it. Give My Love to Mrs Stephens   &amp;   Mrs Whitehead   &amp;   Allison   &amp;   to the Parks   &amp;   Mac's give my Best Regards also, So with Love to Yourself, Dad   &amp;   George I remain Your own dear Boy Harry.
