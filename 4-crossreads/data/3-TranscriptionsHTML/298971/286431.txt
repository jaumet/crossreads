<p>Four fights in the air took place today.  Our antiaircraft guns spoilt one engagement by unavoidably <strike>bringing one</strike> forcing our plane to land.  It got in the fire zone of the land guns.  However we brought down 3 German planes this afternoon.  Relieved by the 14th Field Amb. at 12 o'clock midnight.  15th Field Amb. relieve the 14th at 8 am in the morning.</p>
