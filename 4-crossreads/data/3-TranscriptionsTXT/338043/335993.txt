 Oct. 16th Rehearsal &amp; Show. Good house. Splendid reception. Commence the preparation of the "Monkey's Paw". 
 Oct. 17th Excellent War News. &ndash; Important towns recaptured. Brilliant achievements of General Foch &ndash; Rehearsal Shows and study.
