<p>the rush slackened off.  Huge shells burst round Needle Trench (our post) all day but nobody was hit notwithstanding that infantry were standing there in large numbers prior to moving forward to the firing line.  A "dud" fell into the trench where men were drying their feet but nobody got hit.  The 58th M.O. put us to unnecessary trouble once by sending us in for 2 stretchers and again when he wanted a stretcher case lifted</p>
