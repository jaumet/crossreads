  
 183 Belvedere St. San Francisco 6. July 1916 
 My dear Dave & Alice, 
 I cannot comfort you, I can only send you our love & deepest sympathy. We still feel very sad about Arthur, & wish we could help you to bear this trial. I did hope you would be spared this blow - Strange that his call should come in France!
