 June 1915 fairly hot &amp; the water too tempting.  It is a pity that the water is so exposed to snipers.  Was on Q'Masters fatigue at 8pm. 
 Friday 25th Put in some pretty hard pick &amp; shovel work at the new communication trenches. 
 Saturday 26th Had a fairly easy day only 4 hrs on the pick &amp; shovel. 
 Sunday 27th Was on water fatigue.  It is a fairly strenuous job this hot weather as we have a full mile to go for it &amp; a good portion of the track is through sand.  Got letters from mother &amp; cousin Meg, also from Frank &amp; Doug. 
 Monday 28th Very hot day. We had to stand to arms at 12 o'clock.  About 12.30 portion of our Brigade attacked the Turks on the right &amp; occupied the Balkan Gun Pits.  It was fairly lively.  The 5th had a good few casualties mostly wounded slightly.  However
