28th Nov.We reached this place "Bir Arnusi" at about 7 p.m. Saturday night after a very boring day.  We stopped at some wells about 5 miles from here at 11.30 a.m. to water our horses & we did not get to the troughs until after 4 p.m.  Five mortal hours to water a brigade!!Now we are within about 11 miles of El Arish.
