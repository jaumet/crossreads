<p>10 October 1915<br/>Sunday<br/>19th After Trinity</p>
<p>Great joy I get a packet of letters  &amp;  parcel of cigarettes after nearly three months of waiting<br/>Church services at 10am<br/>Go for a tour round Helouan with a couple of old friends  &amp;  Dad.<br/>See dad off  &amp;  then go to church at 7pm</p>
<p>1915 October 11<br/>Monday</p>
<p>Very warm   &amp;  sultry during the morning but cool wind from the Nile in evening<br/>Pictures at night</p>
