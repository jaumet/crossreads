<p>some of the natives were fighting for some lemenade bottles with their tongues. The Officer was just going to speak to us when they started <strike>speaking</strike> their jabber &amp; he said go away you jabbering buggers &amp; of course the lads set up a laugh</p>
<p>22.4.15<br/>Hot again to day I went about without my hat off to day and the sun must have got at me anyhow my nose bled like a fountain I was reading at the time and the blood spouted all over the paper I was reading . I was temporary silly four <strike>out</strike> abouit two hours. I repaired to made a certain report</p>
