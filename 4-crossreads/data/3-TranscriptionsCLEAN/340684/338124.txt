 I saw a great deal of Isoult White Wallis while I was at Harefield - They live about 3 miles from the Hospital - she is quite different to what she was like in Sydney - I admire her tremendously - she has been awfully good to me - coming all the way here to see me and writing and sending things to me - I will be glad when I get Sir G.Reids letter and know what is going to happen to me when I do get out of this - I lay in bed day after day thinking about it and its getting on my nerves-
