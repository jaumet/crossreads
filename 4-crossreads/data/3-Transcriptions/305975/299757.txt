<p>Thursday. Dec. 26.12.18<br/>turned out 6.30  cold morning  groomed and fed up  Plenty of Black eyes to be seen and Bruised faces and Bleeding noses about  had dinner 12- looks like rain  very cold.<br/>Friday Dec. 27.12.18<br/>turned out 6.30. AM.  cold morning  fed and groomed  full dress Parade 9.AM was inspected by general Chauvel  gave us a lecture about going home  dinner 12.   rested all evening</p>
