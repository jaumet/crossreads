<p><u>Feb. 10th</u></p>
<p>Miss Doris Scales (Niece of Mr &amp; Mrs) arrives from Cambridge to spend week at &quot;Elm Bank&quot;.</p>
<p>Morning and afternoon in London.&nbsp; Booking for Theatres etc.</p>
<p>Visi residence of Miss Fitzsimmons but have no luck as she is not at home.</p>
<p>Reading &amp; writing.</p>
<p><u>Feb. 11th</u></p>
<p>Another walk to &quot;Parliament in the Fields&quot; which was the original meeting place of the Wilena(?).</p>
