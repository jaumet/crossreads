 5 April, Saturday 
 F. Go &amp; play Hockey. Bonnie game I score 2 &amp; miss 6 goals. Bath &amp; go to Dance. Play &amp; dance with some girls whom I have never danced with. I am very dopey especially between 8.45 pm &amp; 10 pm. 
 6 April, Sunday 
 F. Recd P.C. from Ellis. I go to Hop over &amp; get annoyed over Snooker. Tea with Doris &amp; Dick's (2). Go for promenade after &amp; meet Dick 3 times. Painful walk to B'ford Bridge &amp; return to Supper &amp; snooker. 
 Very tame affair all through &amp; see D.O.R.A. again.
