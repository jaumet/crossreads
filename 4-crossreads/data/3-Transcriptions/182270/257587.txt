
<p>THURSDAY, OCTOBER 2, 1919.</p>
<p>Fine, but cold &amp; cloudy. Lopped in</p>
<p>Top paddock in the morning and</p>
<p>ringing in Middle in afternoon</p>
<p>Darby down 70 odd ft with the</p>
<p>bore in sandstone from 60 ft, with</p>
<p>a small soakage at 50 ft.</p>
<p>Tickle drawing water to him.</p>
<p>Broke a small bone in Digger&#39;s</p>
<p>leg coming home.</p>
