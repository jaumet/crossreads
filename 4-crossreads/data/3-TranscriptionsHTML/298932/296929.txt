<p>[JUNE &ndash; JULY]<br/>Friday 29<br/>An awful storm last night, the worst I have ever witnessed the camp was like an inland sea, tents blown to pieces<br/>Saturday 30<br/>Won another fight beat an English tommy. Raining like blazes and our tent is like a seive. "Steele Rudd's platoon"<br/>[JULY] Sunday 1st<br/>At order and discipline mock not whether that of the army or push<br/>For brumbies well broken are better than thorough breds bred in the bush<br/>Another hopeless dawn</p>
