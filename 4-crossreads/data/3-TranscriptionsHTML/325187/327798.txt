<p>[Tuesday 22 February 1916]</p>
<p>Patrolling<br/>60% of the children die in the native villages so any that live are mostly immune &amp; can live in the most unsanitary places in the world- great place for sore eyes-"Jupiter"&amp; another star or planet is very bright as the sun goes down- some of the Natives tell us it never was so bright, &amp; must be an English light from Balloon.  Shifted to Exbid-SIDHOLM the place of Sef Bays- as Head Quarters thought the risk of plague too great-  We commandeered Felucca Boats &amp; floated the Baggage down the <strike>South</strike> JOSEPH Canal- Called Joseph's Canal from time immemorial but spelt in Arabic JOWSEF</p>
