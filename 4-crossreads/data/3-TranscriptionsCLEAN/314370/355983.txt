  
 February, 1916 Thursday 17 Instruction as usual, Semaphore exam & get guns ready for range practice tomorrow. Go & have a chat w/- Dad & play a couple of games of Bridge. 
 Friday 18 A most distressing accident occurred this morning.  An Instructor was demonstrating w/- a Garland Grenade Mortar & live grenade, when by some means the grenade exploded in the gun C.S.M Kirton was killed & 5 others more or less severly wounded.
