<p>While in London I had a couple of dinners at a place called "Ye Olde Cheshire Cheese" in Fleet St.  A most interesting old place dating back to the 16th century.  Everything is just the same today as when the place first opened.  The same tables, the same seats etc., and the autographs in the visitors books include those of Charles Dickens, Dr. Johnson, and many other celebrities, also the foremost men and women from all parts of the world, right up to the present time.
