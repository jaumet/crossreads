Coy's patrol ran across a German party last night &amp; there was a scrap right away, old Fritz turned his machine guns on to our patrol but they were to close to the German trenches to be hit so all the bullets went above their head's, our chaps had the best of the argument &amp; forced the squarehead's to do a get.<br/>Len went away to hospital last night</p>
