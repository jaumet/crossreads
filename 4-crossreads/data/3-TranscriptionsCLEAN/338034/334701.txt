Our howitzer & field gun batteries kept up a heavy bombardment of the enemy earthworks all day, & did excellent work.  Unfortunately  Our heaviest assaults were made between 1.30 p.m. & 4.30 p.m. but up to this time only a small part of the enemy defences had been taken.
