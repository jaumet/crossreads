<p>Following precedent I have been neglecting the homefolk again, but we have been resting for sometime now &amp; working very hard, indeed I hardly have a moment to myself. We drill etc. all the forenoon &amp; in the afternoon have recreational training:- inter-company running boxing, swimming etc.<br/>The weather is now delightfully hot we are all sunburnt &amp; browning</p>
