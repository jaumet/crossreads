Sunday 9
 Wet miserable.
Monday 10
 Beautiful weather
Tuesday 11
 Beautiful day. A dinner tonight was very decent.
 Rumours are about that we move back to the line at Messines in a couple of days
Thursday 13
 Weather is still fine. We perhaps move on Saturday morning to somewhere in the vicinity of Loere but the journey is to be accomplished in stages. 
Saturday 15
 Move to-day at sunrise to Senleques via Samer for Reserves. A beautiful day a decent march
