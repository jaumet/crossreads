The destruction is awful, on the side of the canal  is a large cement wharf with large portions blown about in all directions and barges riddled and lying on the bed of the canal which is empty of water, there are signs of many brave man's life being given for the cause, but in the distance one can see destroyed aeroplanes, tanks, guns and wagons lying about everywhere.</p>
