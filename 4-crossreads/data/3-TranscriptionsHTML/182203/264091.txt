<p>beautiful trip &ndash; on top of Bus, through most lovely country; the Parks &amp; fields with Deer, sheep and healthy looking cows &amp; cattle, also crops being rathered. It took about 1 hr. 40 mins to get there. <br/>We went through the Castle, saw the wonderful pictures and the <u>Vine</u>, which is in a glass house &amp; is covered with bunches of black grapes. I think I have sent you a picture of it, so you will see for yourself.  The grounds are laid out beautifully. The paths form the Letters &ndash; "M." &amp; "W." (Mary &amp; Williams Reign). The fountains in the gardens are simply grand. We had a very good day, left the Castle at 6. p.m. then at a very dainty Caf&eacute; opposite we had a good tea, reach Paddington about 8. p.m. Went to a Restaurant near Station &amp; had Sausages &amp; Mashed Potatoes. Things we could not eat in Egypt. Caught 9.7 p.m. train back to Southall.<br/><u>Tuesday</u> a party of us went to Windsor  Could not go into the Castle because</p>
