We are living pretty well just at present plum puddings cake & etc being the order of the day for we have a bonzer canteen & they supply us with plenty of money. It has been windy & dull all day but rain is hanging off well so far Some of the 13th Battalion were round here today having a look at our billets they are coming here I believe. This evening we had a nice game of football officers & all had a go funny you know football about 3 miles behind the lines it was in a nice little grass paddock all among the guns & howitzers.
