We were out to-day route marching and drilling and in the afternoon we had a lecture by our officers. 
15th This morning we had a Batt parade and on it the Colonel gave us a good lecture, he went through all the causes that led up to the failure a little time back, he gave us a lot of advice about certain military matters he is a fine chap is our Colonel.
After this we had some Battalion drill and then marched in, in the afternoon the Coy were out skirmishing.
