16 March, Sunday.
Dull
I write all morning. M &amp; I go ride to Sulby Glen Port &amp; Claddagh where we were caught once before in the rain. It rains again but we do not get wet. After Tea we sit by the Fire &amp; read until bed time. Inhes Poems accepted.
Wrote Mum, Miss Peck, Miss Daye, Dulce, Lottie, Jessie, Laura.
17 March, Monday
F.I fly off to Ramsey via Sulby Glen. I do some shopping &amp; visit Mont &amp; Mrs Sayle who lived at Peel whose friend &amp; cousin is Mrs Davies of Australian Paper Comp. Sydney. I have hard ride Home against wind. We sit by fire &amp; I write &amp; then we go to post. Tea &amp; Rummy until bed-time. Recd letter from Ellis about His return &amp; I send wire to go.
Wrote Ellis
