 Thursday 2 September, 1915 
 17 th  &amp; 18 th  Batts came to Monash Gully to relieve some of the Regiments who have been here for a long time. 
 Turkish aeroplane flew over our trenches reconnoitering in the evening. 
 The enemy kept up a heavy rifle &amp; shell fire all night knocking our parapet about in places. 
 Wrote to Mater &amp; Mignonette.
