<p>13 Sun May<br/>Keeping very quiet no sign of a move</p>
<p>14 Mon May<br/>Getting very monotonous  nothing but fight flies and eat dust  Batt of Indians came in for a rest</p>
<p>15 Tues May<br/>Very quiet no sign of a move  feeling very queer  had a great night JWs<br/>Very cold evenings</p>
<p>16 Wed May<br/>As usual Alba over dropping small bombs  still visiting Hospital  chance of a trip  tip in camp war over by June 30</p>
