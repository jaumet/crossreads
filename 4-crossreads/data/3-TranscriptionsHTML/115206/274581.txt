<p>January 1918<br/>Friday 11</p>
<p>At Beutin <br/>Have been battery runner today, relieving Gunn who is off to Bolougne. <br/>Plenty of despatches. Weather fine, but very cold.<br/>Played 10th A.A.S.C. and won by 55-23<br/>James on Stable Picquet. Weather changed to rain and wind at 6 pm. Wrote to Roy.</p>
