  
 12 July 1918 Friday Went up for Medical Colonel Newmarch presiding. Do not know the result yet. Things going along in the same camp-like way. 
 1918 July 13 Saturday Light factiques - Battn parade & games for the Troops in the morning. Sgt Clark & I went to Easton & Portland in the afternoon & had a very pleasant time. Went to Pictures at night which were only middling.
