<p>Trenches &amp; elaborate M.G. emplacements all facing the sea.</p>
<p>[The following paragraph crossed through.]<br/>The railway the Turks built out from Jaffa ends about 4 miles to our front.  Thus by laying about 15 miles more (our line is about 10 miles from El Arish) the Egypt to Palestine railway will be complete.</p>
<p>It is extraordinary when one thinks of the vagaries of fortune.  Today two men from the 1st Reg. after we had entered the place &amp; everything was quite safe, went bathing &amp; somehow managed to fall foul of a floating mine which had been driven ashore, &amp; were blown to atoms literally.  They were both 1st Conting. men.<br/>I believe outside the sea is sown with mines to keep off the monitors who used to come along occasionally &amp; blow hell out of the place.</p>
