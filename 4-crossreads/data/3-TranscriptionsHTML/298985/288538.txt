<p>Wednesday 21st<br/>Did some practice with rifle grenades this morning. Went on Guard in evening. But the Battn moved towards the line so had to march a bit over 8 Killos &amp; camped in a big valley near Merecourt still close to the Somme. Did a 3 hours stretch utill 4 as Gass guard.<br/>It is a very noisy place lot of guns about &amp; the echoes are wonderfull. The march last evening (got here about 9) was hot &amp; dusty we walked more quickly than usual &amp; had <strike>f</strike> only 2 spells of about 3 or 4 minutes each  Had some home letters of May. The moon was so bright. I read by it for a time No Planes troubled us</p>
<p>Thursday 22nd<br/>Still on guard. It is to be a 4 day</p>
