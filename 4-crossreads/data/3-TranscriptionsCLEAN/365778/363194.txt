  
 Wednesday 9/5/17 Moved by decauville [narrow gauge light railway] from Bapaume to Shelter Wood, arriving at 8.30 pm. Thursday 10/5/17 Very hot day visited Heritage, went to bed at 8 pm tired. Friday 11/5/17 Wrote to Doff, saw The General who is going north on Wednesday to 2nd Anzac. Saturday 12/5/17 Went to Amiens and then to Bazuux and saw frinals of 4th Div Boxing, stayed the night with the General. Sunday 13/5/17 Returned on horseback, had luncheon at Officers Club, arrived home about 6 pm.
