<p>Friday 14 December 1917.</p>
<p>In bivouac. Big Xmas Australian mail arrives. I got 27 letters &amp; cards.</p>
<p>The official announcement of the fall of Jerusalem.</p>
<p>Camels taken over from us by C.V.C. natives, so its &quot;finish camels&quot; with us fo a while.</p>
<p>My camel was broken up generally after the stunt and sent away from unit.</p>
