<p>Civilian No 7.  Saw 4 men presumably scouts of the force we are to meet going south their horses knocked up &amp; no feed last night.<br/>No 6 Saw 4 lots of men going south took 1&frac12;  mins to pass presumably a squadron at 8.30 am.<br/>Saw 60 or 70 pass at 7.30 am going south of Cairo.<br/>Saw 4 men at 8 am going south in scouting formation.<br/>Civilian No 8 saw in Cairo a force of mounted men 1 mile ling with carts</p>
