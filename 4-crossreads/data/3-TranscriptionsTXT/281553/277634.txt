
June 23Tried all town for Frame, got one & did some printing late evening, all well.  Writing night.
June 24Printing negatives, etc., took several photos.  Went for watch after tea.  Writing afternoon.  Washing Hdkfs.
June 25Photography most of day, out for while in evening back about 9 p.m.
June 26Letter writing & reading, out 2 hrs. after dinner.
June 27Relieved 10-30 a.m., marched back leaving kits for wagon following.
