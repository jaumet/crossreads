 Sunday 25 March 1917. 
 At home on week-end leave. Went on the &quot;Indian&quot; in the morning to McLellands to take snaps.&nbsp; 
 Camera went crook&nbsp;&ndash; no results&nbsp;expected 8 exposures. 
 Stayed at home in afternoon &amp; evening. Bed 9 pm (New time.) (Clocks altered.)
