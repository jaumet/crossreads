<p>1916 June 6<br/>Tuesday</p>
<p>Raining &amp; very cold.  Went for a walk after tea to hospital ostensibly to see Nestle but more for a stroll.  Sisters. Incident of the lady chased by sergeant of guard.<br/>News of great sea battle off Jutland came through.  Conflicting reports.  Heavy losses on both sides.</p>
