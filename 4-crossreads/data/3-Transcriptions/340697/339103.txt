<p>Received 7 Cycles for section 13.4.16</p>
<p>Taube dropped a couple of bombs at Baileull railway station last night 16.4.16</p>
<p>Left Outtersteene and marched 6 miles to Sailly. Expect to go to firing line tomorrow 17.4.16</p>
<p>We went up to firing line today. The name of this sector is Rue-De-Petillion. Batallion H.Q. is about half a mile behind front line trenches 18.4.16</p>
<p>Detailed for linesman</p>
