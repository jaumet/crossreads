
24. SundayProfitless day. Fiddled round neither worked nor rested dull grey & cold. Very tired.
25. MondayFog all day. Got home to prepare meal. Ethel out. Peter misbehaved in Wardrobe & Ethel acted ass.
26. TuesdayGot home & tried to find E.C. Brown then. Did not get to bed till late & Ethel slept in  room & disturbed me as I cd. hear again. Dense fog. Had to prepare meal in evening.
27. Wednesday.Brownie came with me to office & left. Office routine. Disturbed by Ethel at night.
