<p>11 January 1918 Friday<br/>Pow wow by General <strike>Chaytor</strike> this morning had a game of Bridge at B.H.Q. last night after a bath in my oil sheet.<br/>Enemy planes bombed a town not far here &amp; some of our anti air craft shells hit a man playing football-<br/>WINE is plentiful here &amp; oranges &amp; the Turks did not behave half as badly as some of the Papers state.  I hope to go to Jerusalem in a couple of days after we shift Camp, it is only 24 miles away.</p>
