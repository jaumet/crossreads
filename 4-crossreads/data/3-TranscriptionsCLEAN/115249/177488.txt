
Naval Signal
To - Flag 
From - Captain Travers
Date - 24 9 14
Capt Travers has returned with the Chief Official of the place with his staff.  The governor is inland & cannot be communicated with at present.  This chief official assures the Staff Officer that the place is undefended & there are no mines.  He wishes to see the Administrator and original of terms of capitulation.  I am sending him to Berrima now.
