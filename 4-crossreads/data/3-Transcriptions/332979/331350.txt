<p>Wednesday 25/4/17-<br/>This being Anzac Day a half holiday was granted to the Railway Corps No 1 &amp; 3 Sections so it was decided to hold Sports on the Bordon Recreation Ground. So a large crowd made their way &amp; the Afternoon passed off pleasantly. The Sports held were fairly Interesting After Tea a Concert was held in the Wesley Hall by Australians &amp; there was some good talent. The Longmoor Brass Band played several selections &amp; no doubt they are a first class lot they received a Grand reception one artist <strike>[indecipherable]</strike> Private Hook of the Dental Corp he gave several turns &amp; his patter was very laughable he was encored several times, the Concert terminated at 10 Pm &amp; the large audience went</p>
