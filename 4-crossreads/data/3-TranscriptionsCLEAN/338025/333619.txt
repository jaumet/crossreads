  
 Thursday 22nd February 1917 LONDON Wareham riot Piquet Arr.London 8am and as the Amesbury train did not leave Waterloo till 5.50pm we spent the time seeing the sights of London. Arrived Amesbury about 8.30pm, then had a three or four mile march back to Larkhill camp, which we reached at 9.45 pm.Glad to get into bed as we had been walking all day.
