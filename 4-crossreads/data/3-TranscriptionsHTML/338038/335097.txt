<p>5th June 1915</p>
<p>My dear Father<br/>I was just on the point of sitting down to write you when your letter of the 3rd April was handed me &ndash; over 2 months on the road, but none the less welcome, especially as I have had no letters at all for 6 weeks.<br/>There is not much news I can give you owing to censorship. I am temporarily in a Battery, for how long I don't know. Won't be sorry if it is for good as the other job was too slow for words. The Major is stripped of all his original officers now Richards &amp; Toomey being also in Batteries here.<br/>Parker had rather a painful accident some little time ago which necessitated his staying in Hospital at Alex.
