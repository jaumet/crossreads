12/3/17Rather a long gap since I last wrote in here, &amp; plenty to write too.The sisters arrived &amp; turned out to be splendid.Work increased &amp; the dispensary hut was cleaned out to be renovated in common with some dozen others; the others to form a new hospital here
A partitioned off dispensary was to form part of the improvementWhen work had just started Sgt Clay returned to the 3rd, &amp; on leaving promised to see about what chances I would have of getting job of dispenser with them
