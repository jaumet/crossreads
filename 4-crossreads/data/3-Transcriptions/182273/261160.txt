<p>September&nbsp;&nbsp; 1918</p>
<p>1 SUN&nbsp; Fine day. fixing up camp.</p>
<p>Rec. pay 40 Francs. paid</p>
<p>J.W.C. 25. Rec. letter</p>
<p>from Zelia. Things going well</p>
<p>Inf still advancing. also</p>
<p>up North. Quiet. writing at</p>
<p>night.</p>
<p>2 MON&nbsp; Fine day. Fixing up camp</p>
<p>Posted letter to Amy&nbsp; Swimming</p>
<p>sports in aft. Happy time</p>
<p>at night.</p>
<p>Fresh successes all along line.</p>
<p>3 TUES&nbsp; Fine day. Concert in</p>
<p>aft. Writing at night</p>
<p>Fresh advance near Arras.</p>
<p>Quiet. Writing at night</p>
<p>W. Craig went on leave <sup>send German</sup> towel to Amy.</p>
<p>4 WED&nbsp; Fine day. very warm</p>
<p>Posted letters to Gladys &amp; Else P.</p>
<p>Rode over to Beh&eacute;ncourt in aft</p>
<p>saw Tense. had a bonne time</p>
<p>Stayed overnight. Started</p>
<p>leave to Amiens.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &middot;X&middot;</p>
