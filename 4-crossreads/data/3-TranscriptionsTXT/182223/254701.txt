 Monday 27&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; Col. Featherstone. P.M.O. for Australia visited the Hospital and made an inspection.&nbsp; Dined with Mrs Newmarch at the Occidental Hotel.&nbsp; Spoke with General Ford and some others.&nbsp; 
 Tuesday 28&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; In town this morning called at Chem Laboratory, Med. School, and other places.&nbsp; Writing, reading &amp; sleeping for the remainder of the day.&nbsp; 
 Wednesday 29&nbsp;September 1915&nbsp; About hospital all day.&nbsp;
