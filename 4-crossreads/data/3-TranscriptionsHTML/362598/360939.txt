<p>Sunday 20 August 1916<br/>Play for Church.  No sleep.  D., E. &amp; I go to old Cairo to see cutting of Khalig.  We meet two Gentlemen in the Royal Salon.  I come home for Duty &amp; E. &amp; D. go Feasting.</p>
<p>Monday 21 August 1916<br/>I play Tennis &amp; play the Piano.  Ellis goes to Cairo for Films &amp; comes Home in a side car.  Wrote to Marg. &amp; Maisie Carter.</p>
<p>Tuesday 22 August 1916<br/>Mail Day.  E.T. gets 4 letters.  Letters from D. Vernon, Dad, Bess, Otto, H. Hey, Mum &amp; George.</p>
<p>Wednesday 23 August 1916<br/>Wrote to H. Hey &amp; D.C. Vernon, Otto &amp; Annie (5) &amp; Jess Mather, Bess.  Ellis plays tennis &amp; hurts his foot.  The mob have Physical Jerks.</p>
<p>Thursday 24 August 1916<br/>Wrote Home (65).  Very windy, dusty day.  E.T. Condon &amp; I dine in Chanchie [?] Alley on Tomatoes, apricots, Bread, butter &amp; coffee.</p>
<p>Friday 25 August 1916<br/>Ellis goes to No. 3.  Dave &amp; I go to Cairo &amp; Old Cairo Cemetery.  I take Photo &amp; Beat D. at Billiards.  I still go on Night Duty yet no sleep.  Hot Fom. from 3 to 6.30 on one chap.</p>
<p>Saturday 26 August 1916<br/>Leave all day starts from 9 a.m. to 9.30 p.m.  I go to Cairo for Film.  I go off night Duty &amp; have no Duty at all.  I receive letters from Dad, Mum, Maisie Carter, Julie, Nettie.</p>
