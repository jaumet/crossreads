<p>6/1/16  Thursday<br/>Very monotonous in hospital.  Very windy &amp; cold again.  English artillery moved camp.</p>
<p>Friday 7/1/16<br/>Dull &amp; cold morning, windy during afternoon.  Great rush of mumps patients into hospital.  Seventh Australian Engineers arrived here &amp; pitched camp.  All Australian troops being brought on to canal.</p>
<p>Saturday 8/1/16<br/>Rained heavily during early morning.  Stretcher squads rearranged; appointed No 4 in charge of squad.  A few large cruisers passed along canal.</p>
