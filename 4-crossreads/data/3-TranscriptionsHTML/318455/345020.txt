<p>three days &ndash; Fritz has failed to attack up here as expected &ndash; He has been making a further bold bid for Paris but his attack as broken down again &amp; Foch's counter offensive has compelled him to retreat from the Marne as he did almost 4 years ago. &ndash; Speculation as usual is very rife as to where we are bound for &ndash; perhaps out for a rest or to another sector of the front</p>
<p>Aug 5 (Monday) Wardrecques<br/>we arrived here on Saturday &ndash; it is a pretty little village some 8 kilos from St Omer where we are to entrain tomorrow morning We are all packed up ready</p>
