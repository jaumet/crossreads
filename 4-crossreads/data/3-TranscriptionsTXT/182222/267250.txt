 6 th  Getting settled in new quarters all very broke. Had made several useful dark friends at the old stone clink./  Timence  native troops Big people, well made &amp; not bad looking, less lip &amp; nose &amp; more forehead. very straight &amp; dignified. Quiet mannered &amp; hospitable. Great for a joke &amp; have a keen sense of humour. made excellent punch, women also superior type &amp; not scared stupid in presence of the men.
