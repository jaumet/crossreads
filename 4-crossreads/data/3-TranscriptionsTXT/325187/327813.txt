 [Ash Wednesday 8 March 1916] 
 This Canal must not be confused with SUEZ We were startled today by seeing the body of a dead Soldier floating down the Canal-   He turned out to be a Queenslander 2nd L.H. drowned 3 days before higher up the Canal. A sad end for a fighting man &ndash; it appears his [horse?] fell off one of the Ferries &amp; being weighted with Rifle &amp; ammunition had no chance- We wired for Motor Ambulance &amp; sent him to Minna for decent burial.
