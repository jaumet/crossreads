<p>CHAPTER XL1.</p>
<p><u>EMBARK FOR AUSTRALIA.</u></p>
<p>My fourth year in khaki had commenced well.&nbsp; I had made friends, at whose house I was ever welcome.&nbsp; They would never think of making any arrangements without including their &quot;Aussie&quot; friend, and it is to them my thanks are due for the remainder of my stay in London being so enjoyable.</p>
