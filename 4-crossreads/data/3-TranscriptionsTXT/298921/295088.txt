 April 1915 Sunday 25 Coming to Helowan we passed the Turkish prisoners, one interpreter says they are amazed at our good Horses &amp; the size of the men compared to the Territorials &amp; English Allies &ndash; Turks continue to be captured in small numbers along the Canal but I must not go into details &ndash; Will post you Phamplet re hot bath s here, which is rather interesting &ndash; I had every sort Electric, Spray, Sulphur etc etc &amp; have not been so clean for a long time. Met Major Midgley whom I had not seen since Piennars River S.A. he &amp; I did a patrol together once.
