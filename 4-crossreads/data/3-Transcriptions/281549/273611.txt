<p>It was bitterely cold, I had 8 Blankets &amp; could not keep warm. In the morning it was reported that two men were found Frozen to Death there.</p>
<p>I entrained at Boulogne railway Station at about 11 am on the morning of the 9th Feburary &amp; went by train to the town of Etaples which is situated on the coast &amp; is the Base of the Australian Forces in France.</p>
<p>On arrival I found the ground covered thickly with snow &amp; the weather very cold generally.</p>
<p>I was issued with Rifle and Bayonet, a gas-helmet, steel helmet &amp; 250 rounds of ball-ammunition.</p>
