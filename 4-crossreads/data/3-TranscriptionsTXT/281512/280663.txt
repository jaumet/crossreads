I visited one of our batteries, the 37th, where I had numerous friends &amp; discovered two had been killed &amp; the Major wounded, the previous evening, the battery had been "straffed" &amp; it is imperative that its position be changed. 
 Owing to the Menin road being bombarded I had to return via Zillebeck Lake; &amp; jolly pleased I was to arrive safely at Ypres.   Arrived at Steenvorde  at 10 pm. 
 15th Septr Saturday Suitable weather prompted me to again visit our 54th Battery of 8 in howitzers, which lies in front of lock 8, between Zillebeck &amp; Vormezelle.
