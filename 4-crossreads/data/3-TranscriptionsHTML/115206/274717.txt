<p>May 1918<br/>Monday 27</p>
<p>Battery W.Lines.<br/>Weather pretty decent.<br/>Wrote Stell.<br/>Went during afternoon for the usual swim. <br/>Albie a little better.<br/>Knipe professes to be ill.<br/>Percy Grayling visited the lines at 4.30 pm. <br/>Heard today that Archie Innes was wounded recently.<br/>"Gibbie" is also in hospital <br/>The Y.M.C.A. hut or rather dugout opened with much entertaining speechmaking and singing. Fritz planes avc bombs as usual;.<br/>Fritz shelling Pont Nooyelle and Querrie fairly regularly.</p>
