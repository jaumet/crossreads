<p>a different "basin" as they found it did not pay to have the Regts' too close together when attacked by aeroplanes, previously the 2nd &amp; 3rd Regts were in the same basin &amp; made a good target for bomb dropping &amp; that was the reason the 1st L.H. had no casualties a few days ago<br/>Sand flies are very bad &amp; give both horses &amp; men a rough time here</p>
<p>5-6-16<br/>Mon 5th<br/>This morning at 4 oclock A Troop went to Hill 110 &amp; I also went &amp; Les &amp; Reidy had to work telephone station from above water troughs, had a good bit of work to do  this morning as I had 3 stations going pretty regular &amp; came in at one oclock;  Les &amp; Reid are on till dark;  Bob Butter was transferred from the 6th to this Regt. &amp; arrived today;  Divisional Head Quarters also came here today &amp; a fatigue party from this Regt had to go &amp; pitch their tents;<br/>Received an account of battle in North Sea today saying 14 English &amp; 13</p>
<p>[Battle of Jutland, 31 May -1 June 1916]</p>
