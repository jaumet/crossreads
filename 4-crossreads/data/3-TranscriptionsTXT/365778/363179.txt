 Tuesday 6/3/17 Fixed things OK, our shelling a bit short, handed line over to Norrie &amp; move into Support at Le Sars &ndash; Letter from the General. 
 Wednesday 7/3/17 
 Moved back to Reserve in 26th Avenue &ndash; men looking well. Letters from Doff. 
 Thursday 8/3/17 
 Move back to Acid Drop for a rest, relieved by Reid. 
 Friday 9/3/17 
 Went to Division, had visit from Whiteman of R.F.C. wrote to General &amp; Doff.
