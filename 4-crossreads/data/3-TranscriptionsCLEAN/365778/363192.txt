  
 &wishing me many happy returns. Monday 30/4/17 Received pair of sox from Doff. Relief orders received. Tuesday 1/5/17 Relieved by True and Forbes came out to Favreuil, relief completed at dawn, had dinner at Division with Heritage. Wednesday 2/5/17 Men bathed and fitted out again, going in tonight for stunt. Saw Bas, wrote to Doff. Thursday 3/5/17 Things very warm, 5th Bde did not get in, 6th OK. My Battn switched in carrying bombing etc. Came back at night after being relieved by Norrie.
