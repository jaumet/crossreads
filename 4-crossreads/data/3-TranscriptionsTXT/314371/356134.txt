 Sun. 20th Beautiful day.  It is an awful pity to be shut up in Hosp. Doc. not satisfied with my head yet. 
 Mon. 21st The M.O. operated on my head this morning &amp; removed a piece of steel helmet. * 
 *Unfortunately lost during Ypres offensive, Sept. 1917.  J.C.L. 
 Tues. 11th Making very satisfactory progress. 
 Wed. 23rd Still doing O.K. expect to get out in a couple of day. Another man died of wds. in the ward this morning. 
 Thur. 24th Discharged from Hosp. to No. 7 Con. Camp.
