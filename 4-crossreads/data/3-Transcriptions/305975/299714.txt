<p>46 in one truck  not room to Breathe  left Lud 9 AM for Jerusalem which Place we reached 2.30  I feel very tired  had no dinner  had to walk a mile to a camp  asked the cook for some tea or hot water  was refused  had to wait till they made tea for us  6 P.M. had tea and turned in<br/>Sunday Sept.29.9.18<br/>turned out 6. AM had Breakfast  very cool here to day  wrote to</p>
