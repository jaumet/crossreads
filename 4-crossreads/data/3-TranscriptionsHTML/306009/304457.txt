<p>1915<br/>Oct. 2 Sat.<br/>Still at the French with which I seem to make little progress. Am also endeavouring to pick up a little Arabic.<br/>Went to a meeting of the French Scouts (Les Eclaireurs Fran&ccedil;ais) &ndash; They do far too much drill.</p>
