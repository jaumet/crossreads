Monday 25th[26th] July 1915.   Arose about 6.30 & went down to church to say goodbye to Fr Davidson.  Since meeting this dear good man I have taken a great liking to him & felt very sorry at parting.  He was sorry I had to go and assured me he would not forget me.  The North Sea pilot has been aboard since Saturday & a river pilot came aboard about 7.30.  Two tugs were alongside at 8 and after breakfast at 8.30 the ship backed out from the dock.
