 27.12.15 Abbasiah to see Morgan who appears to have a good job amongst the Red Caps drilling prisoners; made arrangements to meet Morgan in Cairo for tea; Arthur &amp; I put in the afternoon seeing the sights &amp; playing draughts in the Soldiers Home. Met Morgan at tea time &amp; then went to the pictures &amp; returned to camp about 10 oclock.I guess the Colonel would not have taken me for one of the Ki-wi Reg if he had seen me as I had on a pair of slacks, dirty boots, the same tunic I took to Gallipoli minus one or two buttons, &amp; an old hat that has seen a good deal of rough usage on the Peninsula, still I had a very good day &amp; arrived back in camp with no one the wiser for my absence
