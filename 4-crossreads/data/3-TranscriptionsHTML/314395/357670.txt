<p>also shake hands same with me once more, George promised me to come back again at his first leisure.  I read this letter to him, for which he was much pleased &amp; was rather touched for the part refering to him.</p>
<p>When finishing, we hear of Zonnebaeke [also spelt Zonnebeke] taken by the British forces, and we expect more success tonight, because the guns have pretty well thundered last night &amp; this morning.  Probably you have heard of it long before you will receive this letter, etc. etc.</p>
<p>Louis Casiez</p>
