<p>May 1918<br/>May 1st</p>
<p>Matinee &amp; Night show &ndash; Heavy work &ndash; Bouquets, or as one might better say congratulations from the Conductor of Orchestra &ndash; Comparison of "Coves" with Pellisiers "famous" Follies &ndash; letters received &amp; post &ndash;<br/>Hammersmith Bridge.<br/>May 2nd<br/>Two shows again today &ndash; I read "Ghosts" (a much discussed play by Ibsen) &ndash; Also selections from Byron &ndash; Much time spent in travelling from Highgate to Hammersmith.</p>
