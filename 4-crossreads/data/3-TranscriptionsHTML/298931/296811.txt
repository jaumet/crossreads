<p>Friday 17<br/>Falling shells are making things very uncomfortable, still on the station<br/>Saturday 18<br/>Snowing and tis very cold, but thank the Lord, I am warm &amp; under shelter<br/>Sunday 19<br/>Making the best of a bleak time still waiting for the transport at Albert<br/>The Battle front is very quite to day not a sound to be heard</p>
