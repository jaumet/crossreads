<p>8.30 pm [?] for fatigue parade. I have just to walk around the sleeping quarters till 9.30 when they have finished cleaning. Then another Roll Call at 10.30.</p>
<p>I am learning French &amp; the small class meets on the Fore Hatch after this parade till 12 noon dinner time. A lecture parade at 2.45 pm till 3.45 pm &amp; then finish for the day.
