<p>Tuesday 8 June 1915</p>
<p>March 30 &ndash; Inspection by H.R.H. the Prince of Wales.</p>
<p>March 27 &ndash; Left Tel-el-Kebir at 1600.  Bivouac 5 to 6 miles out.</p>
<p>28th &ndash; Starting at 0500 reach Mahsamah [also spelt Mahsama] at 0800.  Leave at 1500 and arrive at Moascar at 0900 on 29th.  All troops very much fatigued.</p>
<p>30th &ndash; Leave for Serapeum &amp; cross canal from Africa to Asia.</p>
<p>1st to 3rd April &ndash; Recuperation after long march.  Bathing in the Canal.  On 3rd we leave for advance trenches &amp; reach "Railhead".</p>
<p>April 4th &ndash; In the desert.  Post No. 3 occupied by B Coy, 45th Batt., part of sub-section commanded by Gen. Cox.</p>
