
<p>TUESDAY, JUNE 24, 1919.</p>
<p>Raining off &amp; on all day. I went into</p>
<p>town early &amp; buzzed round</p>
<p>Had lunch with Phil &amp; Jack Charley</p>
<p>Met Linda &amp; we went back to</p>
<p>Lisheen for afternoon tea. I left</p>
<p>after dinner &amp; got the 8.30 train</p>
<p><s>got</s>&nbsp;home.</p>
