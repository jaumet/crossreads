<p>August, 1915<br/>Monday 2<br/><strike>We are more settled today but the ship is very dirty &amp; we have 200 more men than room for.</strike><br/>We change into destroyers &amp; mine sweepers tomorrow night &ndash;<br/>Just washed a shirt so as to be clean. What men want training in in Australia is Rapid fire Musketry &amp; Trench work.<br/>I was sorry to leave my horses but glad to get away from the Desert &amp; into the firing line.</p>
