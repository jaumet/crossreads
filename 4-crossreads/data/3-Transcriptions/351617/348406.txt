Water came from shell holes and nobody ever looked at the bottom of them or goodness only know what we would have found. Never the less we existed those 3 days and then Morris, Thomas, and myself went back to half way house again.</p>
<p>It was just getting dark when we arrived and we proceeded at once to have a hard earnt wash, a good feed, and above all a sleep, as our eyes were just open and that's all. As soon as we landed in the Sergt called us over and gave us a big rum issue and after a feed, we were soon fast asleep.</p>
