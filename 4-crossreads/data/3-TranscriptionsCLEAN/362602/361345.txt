I have not been sick yet but I feel funny in the stomach.  A life on the ocean wave is no good to me.  She is a very steady old boat & is making good pace now.  The waves to-day are huge but it is only a long swell & she glides over them like a duck.  We have lost our piano somewhere so we only have a little organ & a flute & violin for our own private concerts.
