<p>Friday 15 September 1916<br/>On Q.M. fatigue in morning carrying cases amunition.  Each man to have 200 rounds.  Spent day getting things ready.<br/>Leave at 6.15 tonight to go out after Jhonnie.</p>
<p>Saturday 16 September 1916<br/>Rode all night and camped all day at Bir-el-abd.<br/>Good aeroplane dual in the morning but our plane had to imsky.</p>
