<p>"Il Balen del suo Sorriso"<br/>Il Trovatore</p>
<p>All is hushed save night winds stealing<br/>O'er the lime trees and the river<br/>Sounds that called up gentlest feeling<br/>Now have lost their charm for ever<br/>The loved haunts then fraught with gladness<br/>Can to me no joy impart<br/>Memory brings alas but madness<br/>In the tempest of the heart</p>
<p>Oh could I behold those glances<br/>Brighter than the stars above thee<br/>Hear the voice whose sound entrances<br/>Breathing forth the words "I Love thee"</p>
