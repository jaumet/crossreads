
Monday 28 June 1915Letters posted to Girls. Mollie. Strickland. Carruthers. Maxwell.  Working in office. Reading & writing all day
Tuesday 29 June 1915In Cairo this morning. Bank. Med. School. Dep. Public Health. Posted letters to Dr Paton. To Girls Cable from MacNamara at 6 p.m. 365 patients in hospital.
Wednesday 30 June 1915Cabled Joseph at 6 p.m. for her birth anniversary tomorrow. A hot day. Busy.- 470 odd patients Ten letters of Feby 1915 date to hand today.
