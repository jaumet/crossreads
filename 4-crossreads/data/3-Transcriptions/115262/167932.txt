<p>Thursday 14 June 1917.</p>
<p>A very hot morning and a good breeze. Slept on deck&nbsp;<u>last night</u>&nbsp;but on account of wind had to come below at 3 a.m. while crew removed awning. Dolphins seen again today.</p>
<p>Tonight we pass Perrim [Perim] &amp; Appaur [Afar?] and enter the Red Sea. Slept below.</p>
