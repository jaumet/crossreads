<p>Sunday 4 March 1917<br/>Quiet day:  Col. Granville inspected us:  &amp; wrote No. 4 [see images for possible Arabic numbers for 5 and 6].</p>
<p>Monday 5 March 1917<br/>Out on exercise in morning, played the N.Z.rs football won 3-0.</p>
<p>Tuesday 6 March 1917<br/>Out on exercise:  had a fine ride.</p>
<p>Wednesday 7 March 1917<br/>Not feeling too well;  went &amp; got physic from doctor.</p>
<p>Thursday 8 March 1917<br/>Went to Bir-el-Abd for a ride with Clarrie:  saw all the boys.</p>
<p>Friday 9 March 1917<br/>Not feeling too well:  bad cold &amp; sore neck:  quiet day.</p>
<p>Saturday 10 March 1917<br/>Quiet day:  played football:  M.G.s against A &amp; C troop A Sq., draw 0-0.</p>
