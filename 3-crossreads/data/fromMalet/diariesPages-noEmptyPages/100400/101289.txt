Febry 26th. Last night the long expected attack by the 9th eventuated and wild rumours are current. The objective was gained without personel opposition and it is mooted that Fritz has detered on a good long front. Many casualties are coming through as a result of machine gun fire shelling.
Feby 27th The boys are still going and doing well but suffering a fair number of casualties. Duncan Robertson was wounded in the shoulder last night I have been ill again for the last two nights and so have not been able to go up, genuinely to my disappointment.
Feb 28th The papers now confirm the above report an advance of 2 miles on an Eleven mile front. Le Barque, Varlancourt Liquy Thilloy fell to Australians who hold from Guidecourt to Le Sars Casualties have slackened off now Eighteen hundred yards from Baupaume.
Mar 1st The Colonel went up to the line this morning was with Shergold wounded severely in the chest. It is touch go for his life. Reg's leg was broken Nothing fresh about the advance.
Mar 2nd News of the death of the Colonel. March winds in full swing.
Mar 4th Move to Fricourt Farm as an advance party. The Doc. says I have trench fever has put me on quinine daily
Mar 5th. Balance of unit move back to the Farm bearers excepted who have been up in the line since Jany 25th.
Mar 8th. Usual March winds with snowstorms. At 4 pm. Sgt Wooster asked me if I would go on leave this evening Result I am in Amiens awaiting the 12.16 am to Bolougne. Leave is special two to a division daily.
