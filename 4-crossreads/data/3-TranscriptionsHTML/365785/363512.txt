<p>Dardanelles,<br/>Sep. 9/1915</p>
<p>Dear Mrs. Reid,<br/>Just a line to let you know I am doing well, having a fairly good time, plenty of work.  I hear we are likely to be sent away for a spell shortly, it will do us good.  This trench life gets very tiring, the Turks are getting a very rough time this last few weeks.  It is a beautiful sight to watch our Navy bombard the Turks trenches of a night, the flashes from the guns light up everything.  The Navy this morning dropped several fifteen inch shells over into the Narrows.  I think before long now the Turks will be cut off.  Our boys have</p>
