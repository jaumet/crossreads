  Diary while in France.  
 Mar. 20th. Left vessel &amp; marched to our encampment- Site of Indian G.H- originally a German residence- fine grounds with abundance of fruit trees &amp; luxuriant vegetation- great contrast with Egypt. Mar.21st. Bully-beef &amp; biscuits- Inspection by a French General- Break camp at Marseilles- Baggage guard.  Leave by train for unknown destination.  Sleep. (or at least try to sleep) on train.
