<p>we again pull out, for a few days, when we again receive orders that we are to go into action, almost in the same place, as we originally took up when first coming to the "Somme", it is then we got an inkling of what was doing. we are to advance, taking Bapaume which is on our left front &amp; we are to be one of the first batteries to cross. I assure you the news was great, after months of solid drudgery in the mud &amp; snow to know that we are soon to leave it all behind for fresh fields &amp; open country without bogs, mires and shell holes was great news indeed, of course</p>
