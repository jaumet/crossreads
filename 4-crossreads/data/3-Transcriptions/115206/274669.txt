<p>April 1918<br/>Tuesday 9</p>
<p>Blaney Tronville   D.R.  [Despatch Rider]<br/>Weather much about the same, fine during the morning &ndash; rained all the afternoon.<br/>Fritz shelled fairly lightly, roads and villages.<br/>Have so far not had a run to do today.<br/>Wrote a few whzz bangs.<br/>Fritz planes flew over chateau and home again attended by machine gun and anti fire 19th Batt captured Decaguaville</p>
