<p>&amp; Costumes &amp; in a few minutes we were enjoying a swim. The Water is kept warm by inflowing hot water at the Top &amp; cold water going out at the bottom. The baths were soon filled with Soldiers &amp; all seemed to be having a grand time. After a Swim we were directed to the Free Tea Rooms where Tea &amp; Cake was supplied to the Boys by the People which was highly appreciated after I had a cup of Tea &amp; some cake &amp; thanking the Ladies I caught a Car &amp; continued on my Journey around Lions Head Mountain &amp; after passing through several beautiful scenes made up of houses &amp; gardens we arrived back in Town at 5 Pm after enjoyable Afternoon. After having Tea at the Railway Institute I had to report myself at the Drill Hall for Picket Duty for the Evening walking up &amp; down</p>
