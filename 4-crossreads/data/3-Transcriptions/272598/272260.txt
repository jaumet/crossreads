<p>Tuesday 5.3.18  Classified again &amp; marked B2 A which means 6 months service in England.</p>
<p>Thursday 28.2.18<br/>Pay parade in connection with leave.  Purchased railway ticket.  Went to Warminster at night.  Transferred to different section of camp.</p>
<p>Friday 1.3.18<br/>Paraded at 2 pm for pass &amp; sugar card.  Caught 5.40 pm train at Warminster after a very amusing trip arrived at Bristol about 10 pm where we remained for the night.</p>
<p>Saturday 2.3.18<br/>Caught 9.10 Am train Bristol to Gloucester, arrived at Gloucester 10.5 Am.  Found accommodation</p>
