<p>[Fevrier 21 Vendredi]<br/>Have done very well on Nieuport did 7 landings today all good.  Graduated "C" today, so am now a "Scout pilot" &amp; wear wings at last.</p>
<p>[22 Samedi]<br/>Flew D.H.9 dual.  Went to [indecipherable] won &pound;4.10 so make up some of losses which amounted to &pound;14.</p>
<p>Don't feel too bright worst luck wont thorough rest.</p>
