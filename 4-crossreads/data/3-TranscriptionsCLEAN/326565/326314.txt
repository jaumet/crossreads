  
 Walked out about 11/2 miles to old field of Tel-el-Kebir; country very flat for miles. 
 Monday 28/2/16 Morning Squad drill, Afternoon Stretcher drill.  Sgt Ruming transferred to Casualty Clearing Hospital, ill. Went to 30th Btn lines at night & met a number of the Balmain boys.  Meeting of Officers & N.C.O.s in view of organising sports in connection with 8th F Amb. 
 Tuesday 29/2/16 Tea served out before early morning parade.  Forbidden to drink camp water, owing to outbreak of Enteric.
