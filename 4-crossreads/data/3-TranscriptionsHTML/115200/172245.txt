<p>32) Conclusion</p>
<p>Something [terriable?] to connect these trenches&nbsp; We had to lay on our Backs and dig for all we were worth&nbsp; all this time we had no water Since monday april 26 and we were nearly Dead for the want of water&nbsp; Sometimes one or two Bottles of water would be Passed along for Some one further up the trench&nbsp; A Sergeant and his men were in one Section of the trench from Sunday till Wenesday with very little water&nbsp; So you can just imagine for yourself what sort of time we had</p>
