
Rossmore HouseChareh El MadabeghCairo, Egypt3.11.15
My dear LadyFor weeks past I've intended posting you a few lines, but it is so late when I get home & I have so little time that my letter-writing gets behind.By now I suppose you are happily settled at home and feel as if you had hardly been away.  Mer. & I are wondering what your future plans are, if you will settle permanently now or you and the Major be returning?  I often look up to "The Flat" when passing & think of our pleasant visits there.The personnel of the Hospital has changed much, only a very few
