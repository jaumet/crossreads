<p>1915<br/>Oct. 25 Mon.<br/>In the afternoon went for a walk, round Gezira &ndash; through the leafy lanes of the English quarter, past creaking shadoufs worked tirelessly by muscular fellaheen, through the tall dhoura &amp; along the edge of the Nile &ndash;<br/>A chap wants to see all he can of Egyptian life while he is here.</p>
