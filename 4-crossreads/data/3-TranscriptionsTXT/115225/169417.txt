Jan 24th  Had some more exercise with the gloves with Hec & again went down for an hour, trimming & firing. had a hot bath when finished, & felt fit. We played cards in the evening -
 
Jan 25th  We had "Neptune" sports in the afternoon as we crossed the line then, the canvas bath was fixed up & filled & several of the officers were caught & took their turn being lathered with flour paste, shaven with a large wooden razor & pushed of backwards into the bath. lance corporal Mason of "Furphy" -(a paper bulletin published daily & pinned up in a conspicuous place for the perusal & amusement of the troops)- fame also was immersed - I was one of Neptune's police - there was water flying everywhere. & there was plenty of good-natured fun - In the evening a concert was held in honor of Robt Burns aniversary -
 
Jan 26th  A concert was held in the afternoon - a great naval victory is reported by wireless - Also the "Afric" is reported sunk off Sierra Leone. about 300 miles away from us - I slept below on account of rain _
