
March 1918Friday 29
Daylight CornerWeather wet. Getting ready for the road. Fritz as far back as one third of the way between the towns of Albert and Amiens.Major gave us the dinkum oil of what we are to expect shortly, and we are to leave at 2.30am tomorrow.Walked to Neuve Eglise and had a feed in the company of Adey, Da Costa.Weather still dull. Raining heavily.Fritz shelling Bailleul with H.O.
