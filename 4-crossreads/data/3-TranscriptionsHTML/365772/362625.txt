<p>Mena Camp<br/>11.3.15<br/>My dear sister<br/>Your letter to hand yesterday also a Morning Herald with the swimming news in it, the Duke is a fine swimmer and it too hot for our champions his companion is a warm swimmer too. We have a chap called Murray from Manly with us he is a crack swimmer does 100 yards in 59 secs. I suppose you have been reading Captain Beans comments on the behaviour of the troops, he caused quite a sensation didn't he, what</p>
