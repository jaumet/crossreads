 A lady took  two New Zealanders and myself over to Ivy Bridge in a car yesterday &ndash; we had lunch at her house and then went on to Walton on Thames to the opening of the N.Z. hospital there. It is a  beautiful place a magnificent mansion with grounds sloping down to the Thames River &ndash; we went for a row while we were there and are all hoping to be transferred to there from this wretched place
