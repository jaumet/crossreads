
13 August 1918TuesdayThe same old Boat routine - 3 meals per day - ships Inspection and put in the remainder of the day as best one can.
1918 August 14Wednesday"Concert at night"
A very considerable swell developing & a rough choppy sea running. The weather is now very much cooler than in the early part of the voyage & one can do comfortably with a blanket at night.
