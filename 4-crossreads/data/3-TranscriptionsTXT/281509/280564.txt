 1916 December 7 Thursday 
 No carries thro the night.  Do not get up till 10 a.m. as there is nothing to do &amp; we cannot roam about outside for Fritz can spot us &amp; send over a few coal boxes to nab the unwary. Another squad on cooking today,  No tea for breakfast  A carry just at dark. rather welcome the chance to get out. Receive papers &amp; parcel form Mrs. Gelling  -- pad &amp; handkerchiefs.
