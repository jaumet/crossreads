<p>Tuesday 19/6/17<br/>Wether still bad. This Boat is very fond of shipping water in a small way &amp; many have got good wettings to day Canteen opened</p>
<p>Wed 20<br/>The rougest night we have had so far waves comeing right over the bows. I am on guard today</p>
<p>Thursday  21<br/>Came of guard this morning. Just the same old thing as Liverpool. Only not quite as much style. No arms are carried &amp; the relief cannot march very well up &amp; down gang ways</p>
