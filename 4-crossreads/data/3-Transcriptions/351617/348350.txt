<p>fact. I had a clean change in my pack and after a rough bath &amp; my Clean Clothes on &amp; a shave, I was more like myself again. Next day we went on Fatigue building cook-houses and getting everything in order for our stay.</p>
<p>That night Nugget came round to my billet and we went round to have a look at the town and see what we could buy in the way of soap Etc. We managed to get a few things we wanted and above all a big feed of Steak &amp; Chipped potatoes which we thoroughly enjoyed. I think we visited that place every night of our stay in Vignacourt as we were fairly well off for money at this time owing to my having a big win at "Poker" while on "Hungry Hill".</p>
