<p>361<br/>Manchester Royal Eye Hospital<br/>Manchester<br/>8th July 1915</p>
<p>My dear Aunt<br/>Many thanks for your kind letter &ndash; I am sorry I have been so bad over writing to you when you wrote to me but for some reason or other I find it awfully hard to write to any one &ndash; I never know what to say &ndash; I do hope you will forgive me &ndash; I will ask you to when I see you  which I hope will be very</p>
