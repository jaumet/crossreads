 16 March 1915 Tuesday 
 Nothing in sight today We are nearing Aden  &amp;  the straits  Weather very warm   &amp;  sultry. An Irishman's Gale blowing that is straight up   &amp;  down the funnel. 
 1915 March 17 Wednesday Bank Holiday, Ireland 
 Making better speed again, we can just see the Bee ahead so we must be gaining on her Pass Aden   &amp; enter the red Sea Start the big fans in our end of the ship today Our company on duty Irish concert in the evening
