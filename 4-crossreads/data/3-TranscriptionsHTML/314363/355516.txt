<p>leaving for Australia</p>
<p>Dec. 25th 18<br/>On board SS Takada. Yesterday afternoon I  <strike>got</strike> embarked at Devonport  on the SS Takada, we left port for Australia at 10 AM this morning.</p>
<p>SS Takada Dec. 28th 18<br/>Fourth day out.<br/>Sea is beautifully calm we are now nearing Gibralter, will pass it either tonight or in the morning.<br/>The first day there was a heavy swell on &amp; as the ship practically empty, she rolled a great deal. We had Xmas dinner on board, but most of us gave it up to the fishes, since then have been OK, eating plenty.</p>
<p>Jan 2nd 19. SS Takada.<br/>Yesterday being New Year's Day we had sports on board, &amp; there was a very fine turn out of fancy dress costumes.</p>
<p>Jan 7th 19  SS Takada.<br/>In the Red Sea<br/>We are making great progress. Early Sunday morning we arrived at Port Said, there we took in Water &amp; provisions, &amp; left about 6pm the same day.</p>
