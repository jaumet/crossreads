 Tuesday 8 May 1917. 
 Very little drill doing but things busy about embarkation. 
 Returned stores &amp; struck some tents during morning. 
 Afternoon ―&nbsp;drill ―&nbsp;packed kit bag (universal) and placed in &quot;store&quot; for A.S.C. &nbsp;At 8pm. placed on G. S. Wagon for removal tomorrow morning at 4 a.m. 
 Advance guard for A.C. B .F d .A mb .&nbsp;detailed. 
 Wrote 10 notes&nbsp;tonight &amp; posted same. 
 A cold as result of Monday. 1st cold since last October. 
 A.M.C. (2 lots) leave for various transports.
