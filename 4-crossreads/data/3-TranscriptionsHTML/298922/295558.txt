<p>Wednesday 5 September 1917</p>
<p>Still HOT in Cairo .</p>
<p><strike>I am quite busy deciding what KIT to take &amp; what to leave behind. The days are Hot here but cold in England I expect.</strike></p>
<p>Had lunch with Nowell at Camel Corps Mess &ndash; he is better &amp; goes out to his Regiment tomorrow.</p>
<p>We looked at many old photos of "Carrakoorte" &amp; other places. Nowell gave me photo of 2 young Lion Cubs he <strike>is taking</strike> took to England from Abyssinia the last time he went HOME.</p>
