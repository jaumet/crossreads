 August, 1915 Sunday 29 Another week of bed I did expect to be out of this ere now, however, wound healing up well &ndash; but I'm full up of Hospitals &amp; smell of Chloroform &amp; the coughing &amp; dilerium of sick men etc:- Here's a yarn re a 1st L.H. trooper &ndash; "all hands were having a bathe on Beach at Anzac, when the Turks commenced shelling them &ndash; all made for shelter, one dripping naked Trooper dived into  Gen  [indecipherable] dug-out &ndash; exclaiming "I've had my ---------- swim any how." much to the astonishment of some English Staff Officers, pouring over Maps. Can hear Church service in the Distance, would go if I could.
