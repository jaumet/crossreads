 Had my usual trip to the beach stores in the evening. In the evening I also went down to Ordnance to see if Canteen Ship in and to obtain stores if possible. After taking party down I found we could only see a list of goods &amp; prices and articles would be distributed to various Divisional Headquarters etc. It suddenly turned very cool to-night and it made it much more pleasant. I trust that we shall have a continuance of these conditions for a time. 
 Friday 20th August It was nice &amp; cool again this morning. I had a trip to Ordnance in the afternoon to buy &amp; obtain boots but I was disappointed as the sizes required had not arrived.
