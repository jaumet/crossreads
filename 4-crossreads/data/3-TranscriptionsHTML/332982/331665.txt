<p>Nov 28th (Tuesday)</p>
<p>About 10 am we went ashore in the ships boats, pulled by a small steam tug.<br/>Granted leave till 3pm.<br/>Had a good ramble round town visited Victoria Park, and the only White people to be seen about the place is the Government officials, and a few store keepers.<br/>The rest are all blacks.</p>
