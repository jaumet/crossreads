<p>Wednesday feb 12.2.19<br/>turned out 6.30 run  fed up  Breakfast  went to water  very cold raining  started shooting horses to day.  nothing doing  turned in  caught a few lice<br/>Thursday. Feb 13.2.19.<br/>turned out 6.30  went for a run  fed up breakfast  on a fatigue  cleand up cleand and Put in Bandoliers.   went for a wash  washed clothes  had tea  on Picquet to night  on 2nd Post.   there is a roaring sgt in charge too  concert on to night- never went to it</p>
