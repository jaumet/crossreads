The Committee will appoint a President &amp; Secretary &amp; they will comunicate with the Camp Comandant or Officer in Charge on behalf of the Committee.<br/>The Committee will appoint Camp Police Men who will be responsible to the Camp Comandant or Officers in Charge in all matters, pertaining to the law &amp; order of the Comand.<br/>Routine<br/>Reveile will be at 6.30 a.m.  Roll call 7.45 a.m.<br/>Breakfast 8 o'clock  Work parade 9 a.m.<br/>Inspection of Camp 10 a.m.  Dinner 1 p.m.<br/>Work Parade 1.45 p.m.  Roll Call 5 p.m.<br/>Tea 5.30 p.m.  Enquirey Parade 7.30 p.m.</p>
