 July 3 &amp; the City of Weymouth. The background of cliffs stands out clear &amp; distinct against the deep blue of the Harbour. "And it is a  perfect  day"  From the trees &amp; the hedgerows come the songs of the birds, &amp; some happy youngsters are playing among the heather. Thank God that the children at least no little of the horrors of war!
