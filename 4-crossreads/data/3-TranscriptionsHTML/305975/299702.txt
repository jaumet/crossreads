<p>Thursday Aug 29.8.18<br/>am feeling well today sent dad a Paper.<br/>Friday Aug. 30.8.18<br/>feeling well to day went for a route march  had dinner  fell in 2 pm to hear a Court martial read a  tommy got 21 days no 2 field Punishment.<br/>Saturday Aug 31.8.18<br/>am feeling well  sent dad some papers  was up Before the doctor  had a lot of messing about too  had a Beer  dinner 12.30  tea 4 PM turned in</p>
