 July 1917 Camp Bay, 11 Miles the round trip. The line runs out along the coast at a fair height at times &amp; comes back around Lions Head over The Kloof 700 ft heigh  A splendid view of the town &amp; bay. The people here treat us much better than they did in Durban. The Rest Hut supplies Tea, Coffee Cakes etc for 1d each item &amp; there are good reading &amp; writing rooms. There is a very good tram service. The side streets are very narrow. But well made
