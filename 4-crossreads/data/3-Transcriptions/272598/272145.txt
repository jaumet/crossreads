<p>Friday 5.1.17<br/>Relieved from piquet at 2 pm; feet very troublesome, turned in early.</p>
<p>Saturday 6.1.17 to Saturday 13.1.17<br/>Have been off duty practically the whole of the week.  It has been exceptionally cold &amp; wet during the time &amp; very quiet about the billet; went into hospital again on the 11th with sore feet (trench feet).  G.O.C. General Hobbs, paid a visit here &amp; conversed with a number of the boys.  The unit concert party, known as the ("Nightbirds"), have been touring the district entertaining the infantry Battalions &amp; have proved highly successful.  There has been increased activity on the line in the vicinity of Baupaume &amp; Arras</p>
