 Thursday, 9/8/17 In bed all day with gastritis.  Tmpture. in evening 100.4.  Dr. undecided whether to send me to hospital or not. 
 Friday, 10/8/17 Feeling slightly better but stopped in bed all day.  So far have only had pint of milk & few biscuits since tea time Wednesday. 
 Saturday, 11/8/17 Feeling much better but got "No Duty".  Weekend leave was granted so caught 1.12 p.m. train from Dinton.  Had to stay in Salisbury 2 hrs. then caught 3.30 p.m. train to Bournemouth arriving there 5.15 p.m.  Looked about main street, promenade & pier & all about the place.
