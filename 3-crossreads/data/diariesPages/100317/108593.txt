28/6/18
Everyone early astir being moving day. A fine warm sunshiny day. Great bustle always when there is a move on. Batmen all going with their heads down to get their officers attended to and valises packed and stacked ready for transport. It wants a move to show up many little overlooked points. Tents and bivouacs that have been standing for a long time are struck and their accumulated rubbish from the term of occupation is exposed to view. People who ordinarily do not wear equipment are expected to turn out smart as those who do. One discovers just as we move off that the \band has dirty instruments or that there are many men carrying bags and other unmilitary an ragged looking paraphernalia. Just in front of us is a large swimming pond which has been used for sports and by other units. One
