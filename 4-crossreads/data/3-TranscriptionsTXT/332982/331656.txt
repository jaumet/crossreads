 View of Capetown and the Suburbs. On approaching the Nek the "Devils Peak" may be seen on the left above Groote Schuwr and the Lions Head on the right. Over the Nek, on the shoulder of the "Lions Head" are the famous Silver Trees, while the Apostles extend on the left. Camp's bay is said to be the prettiest Watering Place in the Cape Peninsular.
