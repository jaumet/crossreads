<p>1915<br/>Dec. 4 Sat.<br/>Feeling pretty fair to-day &ndash; Put the hard word on the Old Man for a few days at Ras-el-Tin Convalescent Depot, Alexandria.<br/>He said YES so left Cairo by 4.30 p.m. train &amp; arrived safely at Ras-el-Tin about 4 hours later.<br/>Given a bed in Ward 12 &amp; turned in.<br/>Ought to have a rather decent time here. Leave everyday from 1.30 p.m.<br/>The tucker is good &amp; everyone left pretty much to himself.</p>
