<p>was away at Luxor I stayed in or just went for little walks with some of the sisters. Since his returned have spent my spare time with him. This morning (Easter Sunday) I went to 7.30 a.m. Com. Service at St. John's in the Wilderness Church. It was a beautiful morning &amp; a second service, yet the church was rather full. I was the only Sister but a few other women were there. Matron &amp;  the Day Staff went to 6.30 a.m. When I returned, I did not take long to get to bed</p>
