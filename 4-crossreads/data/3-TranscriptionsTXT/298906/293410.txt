Theres one thing which I find very peculiar &amp; that is keeping to the left of the roads, I have been that long now following the right of the road both in Egypt &amp; in France that it seems the correct side to me &amp; I always find myself walking on the wrong side over here. Have decided to buy a little developing plant for my Kodak as it is much cheaper, &amp; there is a chap in here who is going to teach me all he knows about the game, I think it is much more interesting when you can do your own films besides much cheaper. 22nd.
