 Fonts one very ancient and one modern, made respectively of stone and marble. On the wall inscribed in marble are also copies of 4 wills of ancient beneficiaries to the church, also a list of Vicars which dates from 1491.  The floor is made of big stone slabs which are inscribed the same as tombstones which denotes an ancient custom of burying under the floors. The church is lit by suspended kerosene lamps.
