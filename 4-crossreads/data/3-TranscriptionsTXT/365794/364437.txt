 Tuesday 6 February 1917 [5 February 1918] On duty on again &amp; Connaught the same as ever.  S. Piele[Peile] has a day off.  C. Grant comes at 9 a.m. to see me &amp; laughs about my black eye but is very sympathetic nevertheless.  Quiet day &amp; peaceful, only 38 [?] pts. in &amp; none very ill.
