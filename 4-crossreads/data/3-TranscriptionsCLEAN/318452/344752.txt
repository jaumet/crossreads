  
 Sunday Sept 16th We left Ottowa Camp Ooderdene today at 8 oclock  & marched to Dickebush huts, a couple of miles up. The traffic is tremendous on the main roads. We passed a tank on the way & another was standing near our camp I had a good look at it  and  both inside & outside. The effect of their first appearance mush have been a terrific surprise for the bosh. Our huts are in a clump of hills.
