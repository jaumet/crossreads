
Sunday 4 June 1916Church Parade.  Hot & windy.  Ellis & I go to Helio.
Monday 5 June 1916Wrote to Marg., Home (38).  Nice Day again.  We have only had 3 hot days so far.
Tuesday 6 June 1916Billy, Dave, Ellis & I go sailing on the Nile.  Ice Cream Ghezireh Gardens.  Tea at Anzac & Billiards at Y.M.C.A.  Billy beat Dave 50, 48.  I beat Ellis 50, 48.  I beat Billy 50, 34.  Dave beat Ellis.  Had French Lecon & drinks at Saults.  Letters from H. Hey, Home, Mrs. Stephen, Roy, Wal Fisher.
Wednesday 7 June 1916Wrote Roy.  Finish No. 2 Hut.  Received Photos of Marjorie Melville & letter from Nettie.  Last night we met Ken Prior, Capt. of the 51st Artill. Battery at Groppi's.
Thursday 8 June 1916Start No. 2 Ward, Sydney.  Went & met McCoogi.  Bought 2 bottles Scent 3 P.T.  Met Billy Reid & saw Jack Mitchell in a Gharry.
Friday 9 June 1916Kit inspection by Major Morris & Lieut. Teece.  We pack parcel to send Home.  Wrote Marjorie Melville & George (41).
Saturday 10 June 1916George's Birthday.  Went to Cairo for French Lesson.
