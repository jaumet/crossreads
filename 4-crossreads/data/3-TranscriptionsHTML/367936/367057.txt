<p>Thursday 19 October 1916<br/>Wrote to Mother (21).<br/>Got parcel censored to send home.<br/>Went into town with Aire and West and out to Mex in tram for a drive.  Belview in evening.  Spoke to British Consul.</p>
<p>Friday 20 October 1916<br/>Fell in early and left Sidi Bishr for Kantara.<br/>Corn crops look lovely all the way.<br/>Got to camp about 6 o'clock in evening.</p>
