 611 bags of mail and a large number of sheepskin vests. News received 25.11.15 
 We have been working a bit of a ruse this last few days trying to make Abdul beleive we are going to evacuate. The turks sent out a feeler last night. About 200 of them attacked on our Bn and 7th L.H. fronts but were easily driven off leaving a number of dead on the field 27.11.15 
 The first snow storm last night snow about 3 inches deep this morning 28.11.15
