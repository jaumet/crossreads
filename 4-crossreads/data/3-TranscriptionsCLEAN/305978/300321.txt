 Feb. 1st/ Fifty of us made into the 5th Reinft. of 6th Regt. The month was continued with  the usual: camp drill, gaurde & range shooting. 
 March 3rd/ Went on final leave to Aberdeen & had a good time three days only were granted for final leave; came back & we are expecting to sail any day. 
 April 27th Went to measles contact camp, when about to come out fell sick with influenza. The troop sailed on May 20th & I missed them, it was hard luck.
