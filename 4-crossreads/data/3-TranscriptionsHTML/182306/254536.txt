<p>the boys lying in the sun all over the decks Passing a few boats. I saw part of the Spanish coast.</p>
<p>Tuesday 28th Jan.</p>
<p>Near the Spanish Coast expect to pass through Gibralta straits to day having lovely weather Passed Gibralta at 1-30. the country is very hilly on the left side is Spain and on the right Morroco. Africa.</p>
<p>Wed 29th Jan</p>
<p>Had to close all the ports expecting rough weather. which did not come off</p>
