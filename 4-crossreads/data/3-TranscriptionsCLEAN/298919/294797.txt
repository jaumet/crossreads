We then went to the War Office in Whitehall where we saw a Mr Pletts a prominent Marconi man who is attached to the War Office.  After having tea we returned to the Ship.  Weather threatening rain.  Thursday 8th July 1915.   Day dismal & threatening.  Cleared up towards nightfall, went for a stroll around the district.  Called in at RC Presby where I was made very welcome by the Priest.  After a while we went over to the Church.  Standing at the door was a soldier & his wife.  The Priest exclaimed "How are you Johnny.  I am very pleased.
