<p>Thursday 8th of June</p>
<p>Last night Dr. Shahbender &amp; I had a very interesting discussion, lasting till far into the night, &amp; embracing Sociology, Ethics, Religion &amp; diseases.<br/>It was a perfect brain stimulant.  His adventures on the way from Bierut to Egypt via Mesopotamia &amp; India were of a exciting nature &amp; there are several points about them that make vivid description.<br/>He had powerful friends in Bierut but powerful only on the Turkish side.<br/>Once he was invited to see the execution of a brigand.</p>
