<p>79<br/>added to the bill as a supplement. I visited Nostre Logis but found no one there whom I knew.</p>
<p>28-7-19<br/>Went to visit Harrison &amp; then to Canteen where I made purchase amount to 27 fr then to see Shackleton but he will not be there an address is given 13 Rue Marche du St Honore &amp; I pick up driver looking for him &amp; ride along with him.</p>
<p>Meet Shackleton &amp; arrange to see him to morrow at Pepiniere Barracks Place St Augustin at 10 am rather early for me. Then dine with Brise &amp; take her to No 2 Rue Brown Sequard &amp; we pass a pleasant evening with the Gamels &amp; the Huesson who drops in.</p>
<p>29-7-19<br/>Reach home at midnight &amp; find parcel Brise has left for me contains sweetmeats as a birthday present.</p>
<p>Yesterday I got a packet from Australia containing my pyjama 2 handkerchief &amp; some chocolates &amp; so the first thing I did after reaching home was to eat some of the two chocolates &amp; sleep in pyjama &amp; think of home Was it not lucky to arrive at such</p>
