<p>The fleet is sunk again;<br/>The Australians all for India,<br/>German Troops in Spain,<br/>Darcy's training now in camp;<br/>The Japs have got conscription<br/>Our battles with the MMP's<br/>Surpass all mans description.</p>
<p>Greene is climbing off the fence,<br/>Smuts taken "German West",<br/>Turks are in the Whassa;<br/>Austrians much distressed,<br/>Billy Hughes has died of gas<br/>From Politicians speeches;<br/>Ghidleys clothes are soon to be<br/>The fashion on the beaches</p>
<p>Bush fires burn Canberra out;<br/>The Murrumbidgee's dry;<br/>The Wowsers have another win</p>
