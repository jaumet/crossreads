<p>Theory of Grenades</p>
<p>All encased explosives thrown by hand are to be called grenades. Two kinds of grenades</p>
<p>[A] Shrapnell Burst</p>
<p>[B] Fragmental Burst</p>
<p>Heavy grenades weigh about 2 lbs. Light grenades weigh about 1 lb</p>
<p>Affected area of light grenades is roughly 6 yds in diameter while a heavy grenade is dangerous at a diameter of 30 yds.</p>
<p>There are two types of   <strike>grenades</strike> fuses [A] Percussion &amp; [B] [Fuse].<br/>All dummy grenades have a yellow band around the outside.  Live grenades have a red band painted on the outside</p>
