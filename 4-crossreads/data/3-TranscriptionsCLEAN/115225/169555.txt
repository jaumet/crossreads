We then had to rewire the engine & were fortunate enough to get some high tension cable from the loads we were carryinng - meantime Eddie's lorry was way ahead & we crawled in at 9 P.M. towing Sid's lorry as he had not been able to start up - We threw off our loads had supper & went to bed - 
   
  Mem o  The weather has been cold bleak & windy, regular March weather - The war prospects dont seem to improve  Fritz has got Meteren & Neuve Eglise 
   
 Apl 22  nd    We had a good nights sleep & got up at 10 A.M. I rewired the engine as I had obtained some cable from the Corps Armouries work shops which we brought down from St Omer. We got back to Park at 4.30 P.M. to-day has been fine, but it clouded up again in the evening -
