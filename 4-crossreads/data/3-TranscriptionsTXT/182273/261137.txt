1918   June
13 THUR  Fine day. Warm.
Amm. going up
Fairly quiet. Writing
at night. No bombs.
Major Selmes took over
tem: command of 1st F.A.B.
14 FRI  Dull day. Windy.
Amm: going up. Capt.
Masters arr: back. Very
quiet. Writing.
15 SAT  Posted letters to
Fine day. Rec: pay 40 Francs
paid same to E.Sav: Amm: going
up. 'Heads' got wind up
rumour 'Fritz' was hopping
over tonight. everybody
excited. Some guns with-
drawn to battle position.
Men carting amm etc. all night
Rec. letter from May. Webb.
Very quiet day & night.
