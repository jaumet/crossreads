<p>At Amiens we found the civilian railway people working at full speed, &amp; noticed two or three trains full of civilians, &amp; some trains full of French soldiers. I spoke to one French soldier who had just arrived from Soissons. What struck him most about that front, &amp; what aroused his displeasure was the number machine guns Fritz was using.
