<p>Read 9a to noon, at which hour, if the weather is not too cold, a swim.  Dinner 1pm.  Pace deck yarn etc 1.30 &ndash; 2pm afternoon siesta till tea at 5.30pm.  Pace deck yarn etc 6pm 7pm.  Listen in for press 7pm till 8.30 when retire for night.  Thus is good time being wasted; but of course the Transport officials will wake up some day, &amp; remember they have no account of the old "Hessen" for a long while, &amp; and will shift us on to somewhere else for a while.<br/>Everything quiet today.  Nil to relate.<br/> [Mitchell Library stamp]</p>
