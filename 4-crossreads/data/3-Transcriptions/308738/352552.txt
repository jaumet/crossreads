<p>Nov. 16 Tuesday 1915<br/>Went to work at 6 AM. After breakfast went out with other Witnesses to Mustapha. The case started at 10 AM. The "papers"occupied most of the day. Stewart &amp; Playdell &amp; myself were called in &amp; the case was adjourned till 10 AM tomorrow.</p>
<p>Nov. 17 Wednesday 1915<br/>Went to work 6 Am. Went out to Mustapha with other Witnesses  Case on all day. Adjourned till 11 AM tomorrow.<br/>Only two charges so far been brought against him 4 more to follow.<br/>Paid tonight 7/-<br/>Went to prayer meeting (spoke)</p>
<p>Nov. 18 Thursday 1915<br/>Went to Mustapha again with other witnesses &ndash; all day sitting returned 3. 30 PM. Dick &amp; I worked till 8. 30 PM</p>
<p>Nov. 19 Friday 1915<br/>Out again to Mustapha all day.</p>
