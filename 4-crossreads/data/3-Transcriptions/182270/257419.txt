<p>THURSDAY, APRIL 17, 1919.</p>
<p>48</p>
<p>Fine &amp; hot. Lay in Quarintine Bay</p>
<p>all day. On deck all day yarning</p>
<p>ect. &amp; we had an impromptu</p>
<p>concert at night. I got several</p>
<p>letters ect from Mum &amp; Linda.</p>
<p>This is our last night on</p>
<p>board as we disembark at 9.30 AM</p>
<p>TOMORROW.</p>
