<p>85<br/>from other side of Parc. Thence home to Mde Brisson where I dined &amp; then sat up all night with a sick friend</p>
<p>4-8-19<br/>[indecipherable] I am ill &amp; cant walk &ndash; lay up &amp; do nothing</p>
<p>5-8-19)<br/>6-8-19)<br/>Still the same &ndash; am attended on almost night &amp; day with an attention that is beyond comprehension till secret is revealed</p>
<p>7-8-19<br/>Still the same &ndash; state &ndash; if matters do not improve I shall have to call in a Doctor.</p>
<p>To night my little friend who has wished to give me a substantial birthday present has invited me to Opera Comique where one plays what is known [indecipherable] as Grand Opera which here is reserved for the Opera. The seats are excellent &amp; cost only 10 fr but there is a box for poor, a war tax &ndash; a supplement &ndash; 2 fr for booking &amp; in sum one pays 15 francs.</p>
