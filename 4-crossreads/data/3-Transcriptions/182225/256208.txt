
<p>FEB. &nbsp; TUESDAY 22 &nbsp; 1916</p>
<p>Fine &amp; very hot. Foot slogging in Park all morning. Got the last of my issue in afternoon &amp; got the early train to Sydney. Haardy Bros did not have any colours finished, so they are posting them. Linda &amp; M<sup>rs</sup>&nbsp;Lloyd in town all afternoon so are pretty done up.</p>
<p>Got an early boat back &amp; got the 9.45 train back to camp.</p>
