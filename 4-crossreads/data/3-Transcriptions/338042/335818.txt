<p>Feb. 28th<br/>Still enjoying Peace &amp; Quietness &ndash; Purchases of many Books for my next soujourn in France &ndash; Being pressed into a game of snooker by an Edinburgh gentleman in our Hotel &ndash; a New Zealand Corporal &amp; myself have two easy victories - I surprise myself by doing all the scoring &ndash; Entertained by Miss Hater. &ndash; At night I witness my first Opera, which is "Tales of Hoffmann" by Carl Rosa Grand Opera Company.</p>
