<p>No. 17836 Pte E. L. Troughton<br/>4th Australian Field-Ambulance A.I.F.<br/>France</p>
<p>France<br/>In the Field<br/>16th June 1918.<br/>(Ansd. 25 Oct)</p>
<p>Dear Mr Fry,<br/>Oh! My dear friend what will you think of me for leaving you so long without an answer to your gentle &amp; understanding letter. Still I feel you will have understood that my grief &amp; unhappiness make writing very difficult. I was so thankful or your letter &amp; though I cried over it quietly to myself, found great comfort <strike>it</strike> in it, &amp; keep it amongst my few little treasures, reading it</p>
