
18 January 1918 FridaySpeaking about Venus I was watching our Shells bursting away two Taubes when the star caught my eye- at midday!!! I hope you are all in Melbourne now at Mrs Jack AustinsMoved on to RICHON-LE-ZION which means the new Zion- not far from Jaffa.This town the Jews are shorter for food- NO SUGAR, TEA or COFFEE- Tea is made out of dried figs & sweetened with JAM-   Coffee-burnt MAIZE.
