22nd - Saturday.
Parades till noon. Orders to move off about 2pm. but did not leave till 6pm. then made for Albert about 7 miles off, where we bivouced about 9pm. for the night, on slopes just outside the town. Big attack on Pozieres.
23rd. ? Sunday.
Church Parade very impressive, held in quarry very deep closeby.
