<p>Sunday 12th March<br/>Had leave &amp; went to Cairo. I went straight out to see Will D. &amp; we spent the day together.  Tram rides viewing the place, stroll through gardens etc. I managed to see a section of the populace not seen in the city itself.  Spent a very decent quiet day.</p>
<p>Monday 13th March</p>
<p>Started on helping to build a cook house. Have joined myself to the Carpenters squad two of us &amp; the work is to build mess huts etc, anything for a change.  Was inoculated during the afternoon for para-typhoid which took effect immediately &amp; rather spoilt my night's rest.  Weather warm, cool nights.</p>
<p>Wednesday 29th March.<br/>Having quite got out of the habit of writing diary up daily I find great difficulty in remembering it but from this out will attempt to do so.  The weather has now changed to very hot &amp; the flies are becoming very troublesome our only peace being after dark when the beggars apparently go to sleep.
