<p>only a Mobilisation Test &amp; even the "Heads" did not know it till message was brought enroute. We continued the march &amp; returned by new course about 5pm after covering about 8 miles.</p>
<p>22nd - Saturday.<br/>Parades till noon. Orders to move off about 2pm. but did not leave till 6pm. then made for Albert about 7 miles off, where we bivouced about 9pm. for the night, on slopes just outside the town. Big attack on Pozieres.</p>
<p>23rd. &ndash; Sunday.<br/>Church Parade very impressive, held in quarry very deep closeby.</p>
