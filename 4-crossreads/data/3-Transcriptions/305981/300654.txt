<p>on the 12/4/17 we were taken in ammunition wagons to a small village a few miles further on where the wounded were kept in a Red cross tent until the train came to take us into Germany.<br/>On the 13th April we got the train to Germany.<br/>On the 16th April we arrived at our destination at Munster</p>
