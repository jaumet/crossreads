 The engineers sleeping in a tunnel next to ours &amp; leading off the same house like part of the hop factory, arose at about 7.30, so I turned in then &amp; slept till breakfast time at 1130, when Walter came &amp; woke me. 
 News greeted me that we were to be  be  relieved tonight by 1st Field bearers.
