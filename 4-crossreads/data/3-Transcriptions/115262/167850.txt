<p>Saturday 24 March 1917.</p>
<p>Up at 5.15. a.m. Caught 7. a.m. train from Seyr. Got to &quot;<u>the dear old Lewis &amp; Whitty&quot; once more</u> at 10 a.m. Spent morning in city &amp; in afternoon had a good time. Caught 6.30 pm tram to Lyndhurst.</p>
<p>8.30 home once more. Bed 10.35&nbsp;p.m.</p>
