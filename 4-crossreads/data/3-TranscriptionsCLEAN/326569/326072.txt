  
 Sunday, 24/6/17 Reveille 6.30 a.m. but did not get up till 7.  Started having meals in dining hall.  On duty all day as Btn. runner finishing 7.30 p.m.  While on job as runner got job as Escort to Isle of Wight, going tomorrow. 
 Monday, 25/6/17 Up 5.30 a.m., early breakfast 6.15 a.m. & reported with Mooney at B.O.R. 6.45 a.m. to escort 2 prisoners to Isle of Wight. Kept waiting till 8 a.m. & took them to Dinton, thence 9.18 train to Salisbury.  Went down Street in Salisbury to the Cathedral & had feed strawberries costing 7d. lb. - also had glass milk.
