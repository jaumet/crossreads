<p>down below in hammocks. No land or ships sighted. The last few days birds (large &amp; small) have appeared now &amp; again. Fresh water has also been scarce for the last three or four days &amp; we have had to wash in salt water. &amp; it leaves one pretty clammy. We have the salt water shower often: sometimes twice daily &amp; am in splendid health &amp; pretty fair nick:</p>
<p>Friday Jan 7th<br/>A very calm day :though cloudy</p>
