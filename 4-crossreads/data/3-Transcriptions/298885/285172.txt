<p>Oct 14th - 17<br/>Dad's Birthday.<br/>Met my brother Stan for the first time in France. Also met Harold &amp; Norrie</p>
<p>Oct 27th &ndash; 17<br/>After a month of uncertainty as to whether one of us would get a "Blighty" as a result of bombs we came into action at Ypres and are once more doing the "rabbit-act" living in the bowels of terra-firma.</p>
<p>This dug out has been constructed in the Ramparts to a depth of 60 feet, a width of 10 feet and highth of 6 feet.</p>
