 awakened at 7am the ship was anchored.  Of course there was a great deal of excitement aboard, &amp; everybody was anxious to get ashore.  At about 8.30am together with the Skipper &amp; Purser I went ashore in the launch that was sent for the use of the ship to Port Melbourne.  Here we entrained for the City.  Arriving there half an hour later I left the others and went to the Navy Office in Lonsdale Street.
