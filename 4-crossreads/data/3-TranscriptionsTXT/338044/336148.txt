
7th to 12th Feb:-Have had a bad cold for a few weeks past  & it worse this week. To add now I have found a big boil or abscess on my right jaw &  oh, we do look pretty. The doctor sends me off to the hospital to have it treated for a few days &  to day I reach No 2 Gen Hospital Ghezinek, after spending some time in the morning in the company of Madame Alice, &  her friends.
